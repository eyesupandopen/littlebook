Abajulirwa Abali Babiri: Yisirayiri
Ekiwoomerera Ekikaawa
Gatonnya 06, 2025

Enteeko ya Merukizeddeeki: Olubaale Lwakyusibwa, Lwayimirizibwa, ate ne Luddizibwawo

Okuva eri Yakuwa, NZE NDI Omukulu:

Muwulire Ebigambo bya Yakuwa Katonda ow’emirembe n’emirembe era munkkirize mbagabireko amagezi eri omusajja ow’amagezi ali mu mmwe. N’olwekyo, mbategeeza Enteeko ya Merukizeddeeki, y’enteeko eyo Kristo Yesu mwe yalonderwa okuba Kabona Asinga Obukulu, ddala ng’enteeko ya Merukizeddeeki emirembe gyonna! Naye bwe kityo nti olw'ebibi by'abantu, amakubo gange geerabiddwa, n'endagaano nnyingi zirekeddwa, n'ekizikiza kingi n'amakubo agatamanyiddwa bisaasaanidde ensi olw'obulimba bw'abo abaagwa. Era kino kibaddewo olw’obulimba obw’omugenderere n'abo abanoonya okuziyiza amazima mu makubo gaabwe agatalina butuukirivu ne mu bulimba bwabwe obubi ensi kw'esibiddwa mu bukuusa bwabwe obunene obujjidde ku nsi yonna. Era obukuusa obumu bulowoozesa nti obulamu bwonna bwavva mu kubwatuka okunene okw’omwanguka okwateekebwawo okusomesa abantu obulimba bw'oyo eyagwa, agezaako okukweka Yakuwa, NZE NDI Omukulu, mu nsi gye natonda n'omukono gwange.

Era NZE Mukama w'emyoyo, era NZE Omukadde w'Ennaku oli ddala, ddala eyaliwo ng’ennaku tezinnakolebwa era NZE eyatonda bamalayika abatukuvu aboogerwako nti: Afuula emyoyo ababaka be, n'abaweereza be olulimi lw'omuliro, ne mu kutonda ebitonde ne biggibwa mu by’obugagga by’omutima gwange ogw’emirembe n’emirembe ogukola byonna ng’okuteesa kw’okwagala kwange bwe kuli. Kubanga mazima namanya nti Adamu yali ajja kwonoona kyeyagalire n’okwonoona nga tannayonoona n'obuvundu buligwa okuva mu ggulu mu bantu b'abo abaagwa. Kyennava nteekateeka enteekateeka yange ey'emirembe n'emirembe ey'okununula Adamu okujjuvu eteekeddwa mu maaso ga Yesu Kristo, ye Yeshua HaMashiach mu Ndagaano ye Empya ey’obutuukirivu n’amazima!

Era mu kifaananyi n’ensuususo ya Adamu ow'oluvannyuma abantu balinunulibwa okuyingira mu ssanyu n’emirembe ebitaggwaawo, mwe balituukiririza mu kwagala ebigendererwa eby’emirembe n’emirembe ebya Yakuwa, NZE NDI Omukulu. Mazima ddala, ekitonde kyange kyatondebwa era ne kikolebwa mu kwagala ebitonde byange bisobole okujja gye ndi mu kwagala olw’ebyo byonna bye mbawadde mu kwagala kwe mbalinaako. N'ekitonde kyange kisobole okutambulira awamu nange mu bigendererwa byange eby'emirembe n'emirembe nga bituukiriza okwagala kwange okw'emirembe n'emirembe, era nabo baliba n'essanyu erituukiridde era erijjuvu, emirembe, n'obulamu obutaggwaawo nga bakulemberwa mu mirembe n'omukono gw'Omusumba wange Omukulu, Yesu Omunazaaleeti, oyo ataliiko muntu yenna asobola kusemberera Yakuwa, NZE NDI Omukulu, kubanga bwe ntyo bwe nnangiridde.

N'abo abaagwa basaasaanyizza obulimba bwabwe ku maaso g'ekitonde kyange era ekivuddemu nsi kati ejjudde obukuusa bwabwe, mwe beekweka okulimba. Obukuusa obwajja ng’ekivuddemu eky’ekyama eky’obubi ekyawonawo amataba ga Nuuwa nga kiyita mu kumanya okubi okw’oyo eyagwa kwe baawa omuntu ow’obutonde okumuzikiriza ddala. Naye ekyo tekikola makulu, kubanga nsindika obulimba obw'amaanyi eri abo bonna abasanyukira obutali butuukirivu bonna basobole okusalirwa omusango abo abagaana okumanya amazima ne balonda obulimba obubi obw'abo abaagwa. Naye bwe kityo nti, omuntu ow’obutonde mu butamanya agezaako okwewozaako mu butuukirivu bwe yeekoledde mu maaso g’okutunula kwange okw’emirembe n’emirembe era okw’obutuukirivu, n’olwekyo, tegeera nti tewali mubiri gutuukirizibwa mu maaso gange olw’obuvundu obuli mu musaayi gwo olw’ekibi ekiri mu mubiri. Kubanga obulamu bw'omubiri buli mu musaayi!

Olw'ensonga eno Omwana wa Yakuwa yalabikira abali mu nsi, nti olw'ekitambo ekinunula eky'omwana gw'endiga gwa Katonda, Adamu anunulibwe mu bulokozi obujjuvu okuva mu musango gwe ogwali gugenda okumutuukako eri abo bonna abawulize eri endagaano yange ey'emirembe wakati wange n'abantu, ye Yesu Omunazaaleeti afuuliddwa omwoyo oguwa obulamu olw'amaanyi g'omukono gwange. Era n'olw’Empologoma ey'omu kika kya Yuda, gye ntuma ku nsi mu butuukirivu okusala omusango n'okulwana era bwe kityo ekitonde kiriggibwawo n’amaanyi mu mikono gy’abamenyi b’amateeka abajeemu ennaku zonna era abanoonya okuziyiza okwagala kwange okw’emirembe n’emirembe, okuziyizibwa okwo tekusoboka, kubanga ani ayinza okuyimirira okuziyiza omukono gwange ogw'amaanyi? Ne mu muwendo gwabwe, ne mu bubi bwabwe, ne mu nkwe zaabwe, ne mu bukuusa bwabwe, ne mu tekinologiya waabwe atalina mugaso (gwe beenyumiririzaamu) abo abaagwa bawangulwa ddala olwa ssaddaaka y'omwana gw'endiga gwa Katonda, eyawaayo omusaayi gwe ogutalimu kibi nga Kabona Asinga Obukulu emirembe gyonna ng'Enteeko ya Merukizeddeeki bw'eri mu kuwuliza kwonna eri oyo eyamutuma, ye Yakuwa, NZE NDI Omukulu ow'Emirembe n'Emirembe!

N'obwakabaka bwe tebuliba nkomerero, kubanga mu butuukirivu bwonna n'obutukuvu obw'amazima aguze ensi mu musaayi gwe ogununula okufuga okw'obutuukirivu kusobole okujja ku nsi mu kiseera kyakyo n'okwewummuliza olunaku olw'okuwummula (kubanga emyaka olukumi eri Yakuwa giringa olunaku n’olunaku gulinga emyaka olukumi mu maaso gange), kwe kuwummula kw’olunaku lwa ssabbiiti ng’omusota ogwo omukadde tegunnasuulibwa mu nnimi z'omuliro mu kubonaabona okujjuvu okw'emirembe n'emirembe nga tegujjukirwa nate era nga n’entebe ey’olukalala omusango tegunnateekebwawo. Era ku lw’omulimu gwa kabona asinga obukulu ow’Enteeko ya Merukizeddeeki Adamu n’anunulibwa okuva mu kuvunda okuyita mu Yesu Omunazaaleeti eyawaayo ssaddaaka ye ey’emirembe n’emirembe mu kubonaabona okw’entiisa okusasula ekibonerezo kya Musa amateeka gange ag’emirembe n’emirembe gasobole okutuukiririzibwa ekitonde kyange eky’emirembe n’emirembe kwe kifugirwa mu bujjuvu mu kununulibwa okw’obutuukirivu.

Era mazima kiribaawo nti nditonda eggulu eriggya n’ensi empya kubanga eggulu ery’olubereberye n’ensi ey’olubereberye biriggwaawo, so tekulibaawo nnyanja nate; so tekulibaawo muntu yenna alibonyaabonya emirembe n’emirembe. Era kiribaawo mangu nnyo nti abawanguzi bange balisikira byonna, nange ndiba Katonda waabwe, nabo baliba baana bange. Naye abatya n’abatakkiriza, n’ab’emizizo n’abassi, n’abenzi, n’abalogo, n’abasinza ebifaananyi, n’abalimba bonna baliba n’omugabo gwabwe mu nnyanja eyaka omuliro n’ekibiriiti, kwe kufa okw’okubiri.

Kale, ani gwe musaanye okutya abaana bange, kubanga mufuuliddwa baana ba Katonda omulamu olw’ebikolwa bya Yeshua HaMashiach, ye Yesu Kristo, oyo gwe nnawa amaanyi okusinga byonna mu kitonde kyange, okuggyako mu ntebe yange y’obwakabaka gye nzigumya obuyinza bwange, kale ani ayinza okuyimirira okuziyiza omukono ogw'amaanyi ogwa Kristo Yesu, Mukama eyazuukira n’alinnya mu ggulu? Mazima ddala, nga bwe kyali, era nga bwe kiri, era bwe kiriba emirembe n’emirembe nti Yakuwa ne Yeshua HaMashiach bali bumu mu bumu n’ekigendererwa era mmuwadde amaanyi mu bujjuvu mu butuukirivu bwonna era ye gwe nnawa obuyinza ku ggye lyonna ery’omu ggulu, era afuga mu kuwuliza okutuukiridde eri okwagala kwange okw’emirembe n’emirembe. Kale mbategeeza obukumpanya obujjidde ku nsi, ye mirimba gy’abo abaagwa abandyagadde okukweka amazima g'obulokozi bwammwe n’amazima ga Mukama wammwe ow’emirembe n’emirembe, Kristo Yesu!

N’olwekyo, mutegeere nti abasomesi bammwe Abayesuwiiti baleetedde emitima gyammwe okuba emizibe bwe bakweka amazima g'ekitonde kyange n’okukweka ebyafaayo byammwe eby'amazima, mulyoke mutambule mu kizikiza nga mutuukiriza okwegomba kwabwe okubi, era n’amayiba gange geegatte nabo mu busika bwabwe obw'emirembe n'emirembe obw'okwokya n'okwetooloola ekibiriiti kubanga ekyo kye kigendererwa kyabwe. Kale bwe kityo nti Yakuwa, NZE NDI Omukulu, sirangako kulekawo mujulirwa. Era ku buli ndagaano gye nnawa, ne ku buli ndagaano gye nnawa n'okukkiriza, sirangako kulekawo bujulirwa bwa mawulire obutegeerekeka n'okulagirirwa okuweereddwa eddoboozi ly'abannabbi bange ab'edda, be nnalonda. Era okuweebwa eddoboozi ly'abannabbi ab'omu Ndagaano Empya Yeshua HaMashiach b'alonda abalimuweereza nga bakabona be ab'amaanyi n'abakabaka!

Kubanga bw’etyo bw’eri Enteeko ya Merukizeddeeki, era enteeko ey’emirembe n’emirembe eweereddwa abantu, okufuga Adamu kwe yalina okukozesa ku nsi kwali kwa kufugibwa oyo alondeddwa kabona asinga obukulu, kabaka, ne nnabbi! Kubanga bw’etyo Enteeko ya Merukizeddeeki bwe yali (era bw'eri) eweereddwa abantu, n’abantu balyoke babe n’okufuga okw’obutuukirivu n’omuntu oyo omukadde eyalimba Kaawa mu bukuusa bwe. Era n’Enteeko ya Merukizeddeeki n’eyongera okubeerawo, okuva ku lubereberye n’ewummulira ku Adamu gwe yaweebwa, naye mu kufa ne bafa n’enteeko ya Merukizeddeeki n’eyitawo okumala akaseera, mu nnaku okuva ku Adamu okutuuka ku Yakobo.

Era mu nsi eyaliwo ng'amataba amangi tegannabaawo, Enteeko ya Merukizeddeeki yayita okuva ku Adamu, n'etuuka ku Kayinani, n'etuuka ku Enosi, n'etuuka ku Enoka, n'etuuka ku Metusula, n'etuuka ku Nuuwa era Enteeko ya Merukizeddeeki bwe yaty o yayita mu nnaku ez'amataba amangi nga tegannabaawo. Naye, abasomesi bo Abayesuwiiti bawandiise obuwandiike buto, era bakoze ebikolwa by'amaanyi bingi okusobola okukweka amazima g'ebyafaayo bya Adamu eby'amazima nga bwe bagezaako okufuula abantu akatundu akatono ak'enfuufu mu bwengula bwabwe obulimba obwava mu kubwatuka okunene awali mu biseera byabwe ebyayita bye balowooza.

Kubanga Abayesuwiiti babuulira Yesu omulala, ye Yesu eyakolebwa mu kifaananyi kya Nimuloodi okuva e Babulooni, eyali omufuzi ow’olubereberye ow’ekika kye oluvannyuma lw’amataba. Era wadde abafuzi bange abatuukirivu abalondeddwa edda wansi w’Enteeko ya Merukizeddeeki baawa abantu obutuukirivu mu kifo ky’ekibi, okubonaabona, okufa, n’ekibonerezo eky’emirembe n’emirembe. Naye omuntu ow'obutonde asinga kulonda kibi, kubonaabona, kufa, na kibonerezo kya lubeerera. Wadde kiri kityo, bonna abali mu kitonde kyange abaweebwa eddembe ery’okweyagalira balina eddembe okwerondera oba balibeerawo eri Yakuwa nga bayita mu Kristo Yesu, oba nabo balironderera ekibonerezo kyabwe eky’emirembe n’emirembe olw’essanyu ly’ekibi okumala akaseera katono. Bwe kityo bwe kyawandiikibwa nti bonna ab’omu nsi eyaliwo okuggyako emyoyo munaana gyokka gye gyawonyezebwa nga giyita ku Nuuwa, eyali omusajja omutuukirivu eyafuga ensi ng'Enteeko ya Merukizeddeeki bw'eri okumala akaseera kubanga mu kufa yafa.

N'olwekyo, tegeera amakubo g'abo abaagwa, nti bakola n'emitima gyabwe gyonna okukyusa, okulimba, n'okukweka amazima okuva ku mayiba gange, be nnalondera obulamu obutaggwaawo. Kale bwe kityo nti ng’enteekateeka yange ey'okulokola Adamu bwe yagenda ebikkulwa, natuma ebiwandiiko ebitukuvu omwoyo gwa bunnabbi ogwabawumulirangako, era n'omwoyo gwa bunnabbi ogwazaalibwa mu baana ba Katonda olw'okuzaalibwa okupya okuweereddwa mu Ndagaano Empya eya Yeshua HaMashiach, mu oyo gwe nsiima bulijjo. Era bwe nnagenda mbikkula enteekateeka yange, nange nnagenda mpa ebiwandiiko ebitukuvu, ne Kristo Yesu awadde ebiwandiiko ebitukuvu bye nnafuna okuva mu mukono gwange, ye akola byonna ng’okuteesa kw’okwagala kwange bwe kuli.

N’abo abaagwa bwe batyo bwe bakola okwonoona amazima, nga bwe beeyonoonera emirembe n’emirembe. Kubanga bawandiise ebyawandiikibwa obupya, bawadde Baibuli ez’okuyiga ezitali za mazima, bawadde enkwatassenge n’enkolagana ezitali za mazima abantu bangi mwe balimbibwa n’ebigambo ebitasaana, nga Yakuwa tasobola kuwa bavvuunuzi basobola kuvvuunula Kigambo kyange kubanga Ekigambo kyange tekiyinza kumenyeka wadde okukwekebwa, wadde nga bagezaako. Mazima ddala, kitiibwa kya Yakuwa okukweka ekigambo n’ekitiibwa kya Bakabaka okukinoonya! N’olwekyo, mbalagira okunoonyereza ku mazima ga Yakuwa mu maaso ga Yeshua HaMashiach!

Kale tegeera nti abo abaweereza omwenzi omukulu oyo, be basomesi bo Abayesuwiiti, balonze olutimba olulondeddwa obulungi olw'obulimba obukwatagana obutono mwe beekweka okulimba. Bakwese obujulirwa obutukuvu bungi era bawandiise Baibuli nnyingi nga basinziira ku mawulire ag’obulimba era bakoze obulimba bungi obubi ku mazima g’ekitonde kyange. Ne mu kumanya kwange n’emisango gyange nakkiriza oyo eyagwa okukola bw’atyo, kubanga singa ebiwandiiko byange ebitukuvu byakuumibwa era ne bikuumibwa nga bwe nalagira era ne mbikwasa, obusirusiru obw’ekika ekyo tebwandisobose kutuukiririzibwa. Naye bwe gatyo amakubo g’omuntu omwonoonyi era bwe gatyo amakubo g’abo abaagwa abakyamya abantu mu kwegomba kwe okubi okwaka bulijjo. Naye nate, Ekigambo kyange tekiyinza kumenyeka era kirikola ekyo kye kitumiddwa okukola.

Era bwe kityo bwe kyawandiikibwa mu Yasheri nti oluvannyuma lw'amataba amangi olubaale lwa Merukizeddeeki lwasigala ku Nuuwa era oluvannyuma ne lukyusibwa ne lutwalibwa eri Seemu eyali ow'omukaaga mu kusikira okwambala olubaale era mu kiseera ekyo enteeko ya Merukizeddeeki n'ekyusibwa n'etwalibwa eri Abulamu eyasooka okufuuka Kitaawe w'amawanga amangi, era eri abasika be ab’ekisuubizo ebisuubizo by’obusika bye bigenda okutuukirizibwa kubanga omwana w'omukazi omuzaana talisikira bisuubizo bya Bwakabaka bwa Katonda wabula abatuukirivu abalina okukkiriza, nga bwe yali Ibulayimu omuwulize mu nnaku ez'edda.

N'olwekyo, mbategeeza amakulu ga Melukizeddeeki agategeeza Kabaka w'Obutuukirivu. Era bwe kityo enjawulo y'ebigambo ebyo ewandiikibwa okuva mu Luwebbulaniya okudda mu Lungereeza, Melek ng’etegeeza Kabaka ne Zedeki ng’etegeeza obutuukirivu, ng'amakulu agagendereddwa gali nti Kabaka w'Obutuukirivu. Era bwe batyo Bakabaka abo abatuukirivu bwe bafuze ekitonde kyange, be bantu abatuukirivu abaalindirira Masiya waabwe, oyo eyandizaaliddwa mu kika kya Yuda.

Era nga bwe kyawandiikibwa mu Baebbulaniya n'omukono gwa Pawulo (eyali omusigire w'ebyama eri Kristo Yesu) n'awandiika nti: Kubanga Merukizeddeeki ono yali Kabaka w'e Salemu, Kabona wa Katonda Ali Waggulu Ennyo, eyasisinkana Ibulayimu, ng'akomawo okuva mu kutta Bakabaka, n'amuwa omukisa: Era Ibulayimu gwe yawa ekitundu eky'ekkumi eky'ebintu byonna: oyo asooka okuvvuunulibwa Kabaka w'Obutuukirivu: oluvannyuma, naye ye Kabaka w'e Salemu, kwe kugamba, Kabaka w'emirembe, atalina kitaawe, atalina nnyina, atalina luganda, so talina ntandikwa ya nnaku ze, so talina nkomerero ya bulamu bwe: naye afaananyizibwa Omwana wa Katonda, era asigala Kabona emirembe gyonna. Bwe kityo olubaale lwa Merukizeddeeki bwe lwayisibwa eri Ibulayimu omuwulize mu kukuumibwa kwalwo, ddala enteeko eyaweebwa Yesu Kristo, ye Yeshua HaMashiach.

Naye, bangi ku mmwe mwakkiriza obulimba bw'abayigiriza bammwe Abayesuwiiti ababazibye amaaso n'okuyiga okubalemesesa okuyiga, nti ennyiriri zino zoogera ku Yesu Kristo yennyini so ssi ku Kitebe kya Melikizeddeeki. Kubanga mu butuufu, Yesu Omunazaaleesi alina Kitaawe, ye Yahweh NZE NDI Omukulu, era mu butuufu Yesu Omunazaaleesi alina nnyina, ye Maliyamu eyava e Nazaaleesi eky'e Ggaliraaya eyagamba nti kibeerenga gyendi ng'ekigambo kyo bwe kiri, kye kyokulabirako eky'olubeerera eky'okukkiriza ekiweereddwa abakkiriza balyoke bamanye amakubo g'okukkiriza mwe batambulira. Kubanga mu butuufu Yesu Omunazaaleesi alina ab'oluganda lwe b'ali nabo mu lununulo, lwaki temussa kitiibwa mu Bigambo byange eby'enkukunala ebyaweebwa mu byawandiikibwa okubakulembera obulungi okubaggya mu bulimba bwabwe obw'obukumpanya? Naye ate bangi ku mmwe mukkiriza obulimba buno okusinga obulambulukufu n'obwenkukunala bw'Ebigambo byange.

Naye, bwe kityo bwe kyawandiikibwa nti Seemu yasigala nga mwesigwa eri Nuuwa, kubanga Kaamu yasuulawo ddala endagaano ya Nuuwa n'erabira amakubo ga Yahweh era Kaamu n'abazzukulu ba Nuuwa bangi baagezaako okuzimba ekibuga kyabwe ekinene n'omunaala gwakyo ng'obwakabaka bwa Nimuloodi obutali butuukirivu bugolokose okufuga ensi mu nnaku ezo mu butali butuukirivu, okusinziira ku makubo g'abo abaagwa be yawerezanga, wadde nga nnakola emirundi mingi okuwa Nimuloodi omukisa okudda gyendi ng'eyenenyezza, omukisa gwe yagaana olubeerera n'emirembe gyonna.

Naye bwe kityo bwe kyawandiikibwa obulungi mu Yaseri ebikwata ku Abulaamu ne Seemu eyawa Abulaamu obuvunaanyizibwa bwe yamala okutta Kedorolaomeeri n'eggye lye: Era bwe yakomawo okuva mu kutta bakabaka abo, ye n'abasajja be ne bayita mu kiwonvu ky'e Sidimu awaaliwo entalo wakati wa bakabaka. Era Beera kabaka w'e Sodomu, n'abasajja be abalala abaali naye, ne bava mu bunnya bw'ettosi mwe baali bagudde, okusisinkana Abulaamu n'abasajja be. Era Adonizeddeeki (oba Melikizeddeeki) kabaka w'e Yerusaalemi, ye yali Seemu, n'afuluma n'abasajja be okusisinkana Abulaamu n'abantu be, ng'alina emigaati n'omwenge, ne babeera wamu mu kiwonvu ky'e Meleki. Era Adonizeddeeki (oba Melikizeddeeki) n'asabira Abulaamu omukisa, era Abulaamu n'amuwa kimu kya kkumi ku byonna bye yali aleese okuva mu munyago gw'abalabe be, kubanga Adonizeddeeki (oba Melikizeddeeki) yali kabona mu maaso ga Katonda.

Naye bwe kityo bwe kyawandiikibwa mu Yaseri nti: N'ennaku za Saala zaali emyaka kikumi mu abiri mu musanvu n'afa, era Ibulayimu n'amukungubagira nnyo nnyini, n'akola emikolo gy'okukungubaga okumala ennaku musanvu. N'abantu bonna ab'omu nsi ne babudaabuda Ibulayimu ne Isaaka mutabani we ku lwa Saala. N'ennaku z'okukungubaga kwabwe bwe zaayitawo Ibulayimu n'asindika mutabani we Isaaka, n'agenda mu nnyumba ya Seemu ne Eberi, okuyiga amakubo ga Mukama n'ebiragiro bye, era Ibulayimu n'abeera eyo emyaka esatu. Bwe kityo obuvunaanyizibwa bwa Melikizeddeeki ne buweebwa Isaaka, era ye n'awa obusika bw'omwana omubereberye ow'obuggulanda eri muto we, kubanga Esawu yanyooma ebintu bya Katonda n'atunda obuggulanda bwe olw'ebbenseni y'obwita: Kwe kusalawo Yahweh kw'akkiriza olubeerera.

Naye bwe kityo bwe kyawandiikibwa mu Yaseri ebikwata ku Kitebe kya Melikizeddeeki nti: Oluvannyuma lwa kino Sela mutabani wa Alupakusaadi n'afa mu mwaka ogwo, ogwali omwaka ogw'ekkumi n'omunaana ogw'obulamu bwa Yakobo ne Esawu; n'ennaku zonna Sela ze yawangaala zaali emyaka ebikumi bina mu asatu mu esatu n'afa. Mu kiseera ekyo Isaaka n'atuma mutabani we omuto Yakobo mu nnyumba ya Seemu ne Eberi, n'ayiga ebiragiro bya Mukama, era Yakobo n'abeera mu nnyumba ya Seemu ne Eberi emyaka asatu mu ebiri, era Esawu muganda we n'atagenda, kubanga teyayagala kugenda, n'asigala mu nnyumba ya kitaawe mu nsi y'e Kanani.

Naye bwe kityo bwe kyawandiikibwa mu Yaseri ebikwata ku Kitebe kya Melikizeddeeki, nti mu kiseera ky'ekimu Nze, Yahweh NZE NDI Omukulu, nabikkulira Yakobo nti ekitebe kya Melikizeddeeki kyali kigenda kwawulwamu ebifo bisatu nga bwe kiri mu Bigambo ebyawandiikibwa mu Yaseri mulyoke mumanye amazima g'Ebigambo byange: Era Lewubeeni n'akwatirwa nnyina Leeya obuggya olw'ekyo, n'ajjula obusungu, n'agolokoka mu busungu bwe n'agenda n'ayingira mu weema ya Bira era eyo n'aggyawo ekitanda kya kitaawe. Mu kiseera ekyo omugabo gw'obuggulanda, awamu n'ebifo by'obwakabaka n'eby'obwakabona, byaggyibwa ku batabani ba Lewubeeni, kubanga yali ayonoonye ekitanda kya kitaawe, n'obuggulanda ne buweebwa Yusufu, obwakabaka ne buweebwa Yuda, n'obwakabona ne buweebwa Leevi, kubanga Lewubeeni yali ayonoonye ekitanda kya kitaawe.

Bwe kityo bwe kyali era bwe kyawandiikibwa nti obuvunaanyizibwa obwali mu Kitebe kya Melikizeddeeki bwateekebwa ku bbali okusobozesa okukulembera kw'Endagaano ya Musa, nga mu Ndagaano ye eya Kale mwateekebwamu ebifo bisatu eby'enjawulo era eby'okwawukanako olw'emirimu egyo emitukuvu, bwe kityo Isiraeri yalina olunyiriri lw'Abakabaka, n'alina Ekifo ky'obwakabona obw'Abaleevi, era n'alina essomero ly'abannabbi, ye essomero eryagoberera essomero ly'abannabbi abaasooka, okuva mu nnaku ezaasooka amataba.

Bwe kityo obuvunaanyizibwa n'ebitebe bya Kabona, Kabaka, ne Nnabbi byonna byayita mu Isiraeri okusinziira ku nzaalwa zaabwe eziri mu biwandiiko ebitukuvu era mu kiseera ky'ekimu, ne wajja omusajja erinnya lye Yokaana eyazaalibwa Zekkaliya eyali ow'omu lunyiriri lw'obwakabona olwa Zadooki. Bwe kityo ne kigwanira Yokaana Omubatiza okutuukiriza obutuukirivu bwonna bwe yabatiza omwana gw'endiga gwa Katonda mu mugga Yoludaani Yesu gye yabatizibwa, n'ava amangu mu mazzi. Era laba, eggulu ne libikkuka gy'ali, era Yokaana n'alaba omwoyo gwa Katonda ng'akka ng'ejjiba, n'amutuulako. Era laba, eddoboozi ne liva mu ggulu nga ligamba nti, Ono ye Mwana wange omwagalwa, gwe nsanyukira ennyo.

Bwe kityo Yesu Omunazaaleesi n'alondebwa okuba kabona olubeerera n'emirembe gyonna okusinziira ku Kitebe kya Melikizeddeeki era ebifo byonna bizziddwawo wansi w'obuvunaanyizibwa bw'obwakabona buno obw'olubeerera, kaakano n'emirembe gyonna.

Kubanga Ekitebe kya Melikizeddeeki kitebe kya bukabona, era obuvunaanyizibwa bwakyo tebujje kuva ku Yesua HaMashiach, kubanga yazuukira obutaddamu kufa!

Kubanga Ekitebe kya Melikizeddeeki kitebe kya Bannabbi aboogera era abayigiriza ebyafaayo, amakubo, n'ebiseera eby'omu maaso eby'entegeka za Yahweh ez'obutuukirivu eziweereddwa mu maaso ga Yesu Kristo.

NZE NDI YHVH, ye NZE NDI Omukulu era ntumye Ebigambo bino okuyita mu mubaka wange era omuweereza wange era okuyita mu mubaka wange era omuweereza wange Ebigambo bino biweerezeddwa. Mu mazima, eri omubaka wange n'omuweereza wange aweereza wansi wa Kristo Yesu Mukama we ow'olubeerera era okuyita mu mwoyo gw'obunnabbi Yahweh ne Kristo Yesu babikkulidde omubaka waffe era omuweereza waffe Enjiri Ey'olubeerera olw'obuwulize n'obulokozi bw'abo bonna Ebigambo bino be bitumirwa. N'ebigambo by'Enjiri Ey'olubeerera bijja kutuuka ku maaso, ku matu, ne ku mitima gy'abo bonna be bitumirwa.

NZE NDI YHVH, ye oyo eyatonda, eyakola, n'atuma Omwana we omu yekka gw'eyazaala, ye Yesua HaMashiach ye Yesu Masiya olw'obulokozi bw'abo bonna abazaalibwa Adamu ne bakkiriza okununnulibwa okujjuvu singa abo abayitibwa baddamu omulokozi waabwe bw'abayita. N'olwekyo, tegeera nti Nze, YHVH, NZE NDI Omukulu abikkulira Yesu Omunazaaleesi abo ababuze, bwe kityo Omusumba waabwe Omukulu alyoke asobole okulonda era okuzzaayo endiga zange zonna ezibuze mu mawanika gange ag'obukuumi n'obubudamo olubeerera n'emirembe gyonna.

NZE NDI YHVH, ye oyo eyayawula obwakabona obw'olubeerera obwa Melikizeddeeki mu bifo bisatu eby'enjawulo era eby'okwawukanako ebya Kabaka, Kabona, ne Nnabbi wansi wa Yakobo mu biseera bye.

NZE NDI YHVH, ye oyo azzizzaawo Obwakabona obw'olubeerera obwa Melikizeddeeki mu Kristo Yesu, oyo awandiikibwako nti: Kubanga kirabika lwatu nti Mukama waffe yava mu Yuda, ekika Musa ky'ataayogerako kintu kyonna ekikwata ku Bwakabona. Era kirabika nnyo nnyini, kubanga okusinziira ku kifaananyi kya Melikizeddeeki, wagolokoseewo Kabona omulala, atafuulibwa kabona ng'amateeka g'ekiragiro eky'omubiri eky'edda bwe gali, naye ng'amaanyi g'obulamu obutaggwaawo bwe gali. Kubanga ategeeza bw'ati nti, Oli kabona emirembe gyonna, ng'engeri ya Melikizeddeeki bw'eri. Kubanga ekiragiro ekyasookawo, kyagobebwa, olw'obunafu bwakyo, n'obutagasa bwakyo.

Kubanga Amateeka tewaali kye gaatuukiriza, naye okuleeta essuubi eddungi lyatuukiriza, mwe tusemberera Yahweh. Era nga bwe kitakyalimu kulayira (kubanga abo bafuulibwa Bakabona awatali kulayira: Naye ono yafuulibwa n'okulayira okw'oyo eyamugamba nti, Yahweh alayidde, so talyenenya, nti Oli kabona emirembe gyonna, ng'engeri ya Melikizeddeeki bw'eri) Yesu kye yava afuuka omweteefu w'Endagaano esinga obulungi. Era mu bo bangi baafuulibwa Bakabona, kubanga tebaasobola kugenda mu maaso, olw'okufa kwabwe. Naye omusajja ono, kubanga agenda mu maaso emirembe gyonna, alina Obwakabona, obutasobola kuva ku omu kudda ku mulala. Kyava asobola n'okulokola ddala abo abajja eri Katonda okuyita mu ye, kubanga mulamu emirembe gyonna, okubawolereza.

N'olwekyo, mbawa okutegeera Ekitebe ky'Olubeerera ekya Melikizeddeeki n'amazima ga Yesua HaMashiach, era ye Yesu Kristo kubanga wansi w'obuvunaanyizibwa bwe obw'olubeerera obwa kabona asinga obukulu okusinziira ku Kitebe kya Melikizeddeeki, Kristo Yesu ajja kutikkira era n'okwatula eggye eddene ery'abakabaka n'abakabona wansi w'Ekitebe kya Melikizeddeeki era bwe kityo Obwakabaka bw'Eggulu bujja kufugibwa omukono gw'olubeerera ogwa Mukama waabwe ow'Obutuukirivu ow'Olubeerera, gwe bajja okuweereza mu butuukirivu obutuukiridde n'obutukuvu obw'amazima emirembe gyonna, kubanga ku bikwata ku Bwakabaka bwa Kristo Yesu, tewali nkomerero.
