Okunnyonnyola kw'Okubwatuka Omusanvu

Ekyama ky'Akatabo Akatono

Okuva eri Yeshua HaMashiach:

Laba, ekyama ky'akatabo akatono! Kubanga bamalayika musanvu batuma okubwatuka kwabwe, era okubwatuka ne kutegeeza amaloboozi gaakwo. Gano ge maama g'akatabo akatono, kubanga akatabo kaali kakasibweko akabonero. Kubanga eddoboozi lyava mu ggulu, ne ligamba Yokaana Omubikkulirwa nti: Teekako akabonero ku ebyo okubwatuka omusanvu bye kwogedde, era tobiwandiika. Laba, walya akatabo akatono, kubanga kabikkuliddwa, era kaali kawoomu ng'omubisi gw'enjuki mu matu go bwe wali weebase, naye kaali kakaawa mu lubuto lwo olwakuzuukusa mu kwolesebwa; era laba, wazuukuka mu tulo two! Laba, ekyama ky'akatabo akatono kibikkuliddwa mu biseera bino, kubanga kye kyama ekisembayo okubikkulirwa mu mirembe gyange egy'ekisa, kaakano akatabo akatono ak'akawoowo akakaawa. Kubanga amaloboozi g'okubwatuka galina okubikkulwa, era buno bwe butuufu obw'okubikkulirwa, kubanga ekyama ky'akatabo akatono kinene, newankubadde nga ke katabo akatono. Kubanga akatabo ako akatono kaabikkulirwa Yokaana Omubikkulirwa, era kaakano nja kukakubikkulira ggwe, kubanga okubikkulwa kwako kuli kumpi. Wulira eddoboozi lyange, omugole wange, era otege okutu kwo gye ndi, Kristo Yesu, kubanga obudde butuuse. Kubanga tewakyali budde.

Nze mwana wa Katonda eyajja mu mubiri, era nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, ntumye ebigambo bino.

Okuva eri Yahweh, Katonda ataggwaawo:

Eddoboozi ly'okubwatuka omusanvu LYOGERA! Mweteekereteekere, mmwe amawanga, kubanga omusango omunene gubaliiridde, n'ebigambo by'okubwatuka omusanvu bibikkuliddwa. Mukyuse amatu gammwe muwulire ebyogererwa by'ekyama ky'Akatabo Akatono. Kubanga emizingo gya kusumululwa, n'okusumululwa kw'obubonero kujja ku nsi yonna, omusango omunene n'akatyabaga. Kubanga ebibi by'ensi kivundu mu nnyindo zange, era nze, kaakano Yahweh, nja kulongoosa ensi, era nja kumenya buli kintu ekibi; era omwana wange gwe ntuma, alifuga mu butuukirivu ku nsi, era emirembe eminene giriba gya beesigwa be nkuumye okuva ku lunaku olubi. Era emirembe eminene nja kugiwa abo bonna abakoowoola erinnya lya Yesu Kristo, ye Yeshua HaMashiach, kubanga buli akoowoola erinnya lya Mukama alirokolebwa. Era mu mirembe n'obutebenkevu nja kubakulemberamu, era mu butuukirivu nja kubambaza, era mu butukuvu obw'amazima balimpitirizaamu ekitiibwa; kubanga mbatiikidde ekitiibwa, era obulamu obuggya gye baliba nabwo.

Kale ndeeta obwenkanya bwange n'obusungu bwange ndyoke nsasule abo abakola ebikolwa ebibi n'amakubo gaabwe amacaafu, kale buli eyeeng'aana nange n'alondawo obubi, aleke abe mubi, era obusungu bwa Katonda bumubeereko! Era buli aliba omutuukirivu n'abo bonna abava mu bubi nga bakoowoola erinnya lya Mukama baliba n'empeera nnene; kale babeere n'ebibala eby'emirembe eby'obutuukirivu, era baliba n'obusika obunene mu mwana wange, Kristo Yesu, alina empeera yaabwe mu ngalo ze. Kale buli anaaba omubi, aleke abe mubi. Era buli akyukira obutuukirivu n'akoowoola erinnya lya Mukama, aleke abe mutuukirivu, kubanga olunaku lulikiranga. Laba, ekintu kye nja okubategeeza nga tekinnabaawo, kubanga obunnabbi buli kumpi n'okutuukirira, era tewaliba kulwawo nate. Kubanga ebiseera by'ekisa n'okusaasira biri ku nkomerero, era ekiseera ky'obusungu obungi kinaatera okuteebwa ku nsi, era tewabangawo kiseera nga kino ku nsi, n'omu nnaku za Nuuwa, era tewalibaawo kiseera kingi bwetyo nate. Kubanga nze, kaakano nze, ndayidde erinnya lyange era ntegeezezza ebintu bino. Kyonna nze, kaakano Yahweh Katonda ataggwaawo, kye ntegeeza tekiyinza kukyusibwa era kiribaawo. Kubanga mu nnaku za malayika ow'omusanvu, bw'alitandika okufuuwa, n'ekyama kya Katonda kiriggwaawo.

Nze NDI GWENDI, kale bagambe nti NDI OMUNENE abatumye ebigambo bino, ye Yahweh, Katonda omukulu era ataggwaawo!

Obujulizi n'Ebyafaayo, nga bya William Brooks

Era kaakano okulaba okusingawo ku kitundu ky'obubaka buno Yeshua HaMashiach we yagambira nti, "Laba, walya akatabo akatono, kubanga kabikkuliddwa, era kaali kawoomu ng'omubisi gw'enjuki mu matu go bwe wali weebase, naye kaali kakaawa mu lubuto lwo olwakuzuukusa mu kwolesebwa; era laba, wazuukuka mu tulo two." Ekiseera ekimu emabega mu mwezi oguwedde oba gubiri, simanyi lunaku lwennyini, naye nnali neebase mu ttumbi, ne nzuukukira mu kwolesebwa okuva mu tulo (oba yali ndooto ey'amaanyi ne nzuukuka ku ntandikwa). Eddoboozi lyali lisoma ebigambo mu birowoozo byange n'omwoyo gwange, ne mmanya nti ddoboozi lya Mukama, era lyali lisoma mu matu gange ebigambo bye saasobola kutegeera kubanga ebigambo byali bikulukuta butereevu. Naye n'ebigambo mwajja emirembe eminene egyantuulako ddala. Ebigambo byali biwoomu nnyo mu matu gange, era nnali muwummule. Awo amangwago, ne nva mu mbeera eyo ng'olubuto luntamidde nnyo, olubuto lwange lwali lwetolodde, ne nsituka butereevu ku buliri, nga nsekula era nga nkolola. Ne nserengeta wansi ne nnywa amazzi amangi, agaayambako katono naye tegaatebenkeza lubuto lwange. Nnali mutunzi okumala akaseera oluvannyuma lwa kino, naye saamanya byonna bino kye byali bitegeeza.

Era ku bujulizi, Yahweh w'agambira nti, "Eddoboozi ly'okubwatuka omusanvu LYOGERA!" Nga Meeyi 7, 2024, nnalina olunaku olwali oluzibu ennyo, ne nkomawo eka ne ŋŋenda okwebaka ku ssaawa za mu ngulu. Nnali nkedde nnyo ekiro eky'olunaku olwasookawo kubanga saasobola kwebaka, ekintu ekimbeerawo enfunda nnyingi. Waaliwo embuyaga ey'amaanyi n'enkuba ng'ebikka bwe nnagenda okugalamira, naye okumyansa n'okubwatuka byonna byali wala. Nnagalamira okwebaka ku ssaawa nga kkumi na ssatu ez'ekiro ekyo, ezo ziri ku ssaawa zange ez'omu ngulu, ne nsinzira mu kye nnalowooza nti tulo. Simanyi oba nnali ddala neebase, naye nnayingira mu kwolesebwa oba mu ndooto ey'amaanyi ennyo, era nnali ntegeera bulungi embuyaga eyaliwo. Ebigambo n'amaloboozi byali binjijira okuva mu byerekezo eby'enjawulo, era byali buli wamu. Sijjukira bigambo bya maloboozi ago ag'enjawulo, okuggyako eddoboozi limu. Lyali ddoboozi erijjawo, era amaloboozi amalala gonna ne gasirika. Eddoboozi ne ligamba nti, "Eddoboozi ly'okubwatuka omusanvu LYOGERA!" Ebigambo ebyo bwe byayogerwa, amangwago nnazuukusibwa okubwatuka okusinga obunene kwe nnali mpulidde. Ne ntuula awo ku buliri bwange, nga nneewuunya ddala.

Bwe nnagabana bino ne mukyala wange oluvannyuma lw'olunaku lumu oba bbiri, twali ku bbalaza yaffe ey'omu maaso. N'ankwata ku mukono n'ankulembera okwetooloola ennyumba okudda emabega. N'aŋŋamba nti ye n'omwana waffe baali mu ddiiniro okubwatuka okwo bwe kwakuba. Okumyansa kwali kumulisa era ne kukankanya eddiiniro. Okutwaliza awamu, kyali kikubye mu kifo ekiriraanyewo eky'oluggya lwaffe olw'emabega, olutonotono, kubanga tubeera mu kibuga. Naye tetwalaba kwononeka kulala kwonna. Nnali ndabye engeri okumyansa gye kukuba ensi oba ekintu ku nsi n'obukambwe, era ne kirekawo okusaanawo ku mutendera guno na guli. Ye yalaba okumyansa, era ffenna twawulira okubwatuka okw'amaanyi ennyo, naye tewali kusaanira kwonna kwa maanyi.


Juuni 12, 2024

Mukama annyoleserezza ddala omulimu guno kye guli, era gutuumiddwa bwe guti. Nfunye ebirimu Akatabo Akatono akali mu mikono gya Malayika ow'amaanyi mu Kubikkulirwa 10, eyali ayambadde ekire era omusoke gwali ku mutwe gwe n'amaaso ge gaali ng'enjuba n'ebigere bye ng'empagi z'omuliro. Bino bikiikirira emirembe n'endagaano ezigendana nabyo, byonna ebikoma ku maaso ga Yesu Kristo, omu yekka eyazaalibwa Yahweh, n'ekifaananyi kya Katonda ataggwaawo.

Omusoke gwe kabonero akaaweebwa ku ndagaano ya Nuuwa Yahweh gye yakola ne Nuuwa oluvannyuma lw'amataba. Ekire n'empagi z'omuliro bikiikirira endagaano ya Katonda ne Musa. Amaaso ga Yeshua HaMashiach gaaka ng'enjuba era ye ndagaano empya. Obubaka buno omusanvu bulimu omusango ku abo abaagwa n'abalala okuva emabega w'amataba ga Nuuwa okutuuka ku nkomerero, mu ekyo ekikyali mu biseera by'omu maaso okusinziira ku lunaku lwa leero.
