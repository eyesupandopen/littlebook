Omujulirwa Omulamba: Ekkanisa
Mu Biwandiiko by'Omutume Pawulo
Meeyi 13, 2024

Eri Ekkanisa Yange, Omubiri Gwange, Era Omugole Wange: Muwulire Ebigambo bya Mukama Wammwe!

Eri Ekkanisa yange, Omubiri gwange, era Omugole wange: Nsaba mutege amatu gammwe eri amagezi gange era muwulire eddoboozi lyange, kubanga kaakano njogera nammwe. Mumpuliriza? Mute ebirowoozo byammwe ku njigiriza ez'enkyerema ezaakolebwa abantu n'ebiyitibwa eby'eddiini, era muteeke omutima gwammwe ku Byawandiikibwa Ebitukuvu, kwe kugamba ekigambo kyange ekirongoofu. Mwekebejje enjigiriza zammwe ezirimu ekizimbulukusa, kubanga okutabula kwonna okw'Ebigambo bya Katonda, newankubadde Ekigambo kya Katonda ekirongoofu, kuzimbulukusa obuwunga bwonna era temuba mukyalina byawandiikibwa. Kubanga Nze Kigambo kya Katonda ekyefuula omubiri, era mbeera munda mu mmwe, era mbayita okujja mu maka gammwe ag'omu ggulu mu bbanga eritali wala.

Mute ebbali ebiwandiiko byammwe ebinnyonnyola, era mulekere awo okuwuliriza amaloboozi amangi agatalina mazima, era amaloboozi agatali gange; wabula, mukuumire Ebigambo byange mu mitima gyammwe era mubigerageranye n'ebyawandiikibwa mulyoke mube n'okukkiriza mu Bigambo byange era n'okukkiriza mu nze, Kristo Yesu. Kubanga omuntu bw'ajja gye muli ng'atayigiriza njigiriza ya Mukama, era ng'ebyo by'ayogera si Bigambo birongoofu bya Katonda, temumulamusanga n'okumusuubiza omukisa. Kubanga nja kubafuula bagezigezi mu Byawandiikibwa byange Ebitukuvu, kwe kugamba Ekigambo eky'obulamu, era amagezi gange tegoonoona. Kale mwekuŋŋaanyize eby'obugagga byammwe mu ggulu, awalitali kutuunya newankubadde nnyenje ebyonoona, era ababbi gye batasobola kumenyawo kubba. Kubanga yonna eby'obugagga byo gye biri, n'emitima gyo gye giriba. Baana bange, nze by'obugagga byammwe, kale ebirowoozo byammwe n'emitima gyammwe mubitunuulize nze yekka n'omutima gumu.

Temusomangako Bigambo ebibikkula ekyama kino ekinene ekya Katonda? Kubanga tulina eby'obugagga bino mu bibya by'ebbumba obuyinza obungi obubirimu bulyoke bulabike nga bwa Katonda, so si bwaffe. Katonda gwe yayagala okumanyisa kye kiri obugagga obw'ekitiibwa eky'ekyama kino mu Bamawanga, ye Kristo mu mmwe, essuubi ery'ekitiibwa. Kale temutya, kubanga mwazaalibwa omulundi ogw'okubiri mu mwoyo gwange omutukuvu; kyemunaava mutegeera Ebigambo by'Ekigambo kyange. Kubanga nja kubabuulira amagezi aganaabakubagiza era aganaabakuuma okuva ku bulimba obw'amaanyi obuwambye ensi; kubanga ensi esibiddwa okusalirwa omusango era eri mu kutya okungi n'okubonaabona.

Kyennavudde nzija okwogera nammwe ndyoke ntegeeze abagezigezi mu mmwe ebikwata ku bakulembeze b'ensi ababiri abalabika kati ng'abasumba abalungi, era endiga ezirondeddwa okuva mu kisibo okuweereza mu buweerereza bwa bantu, abalabika ng'abazze okusumba endiga ezaabula eza Babulooni w'Ebyama okuzituusa mu bulamu. Naye abasumba bano ababiri ab'enkukunyi si ndiga, wabula nsolo nkambwe ezeefudde endiga era nga za mu kisibo kirala, ekya dragoni omusumba mwennyini. Dragoni omusumba si musumba mutuufu wa ndiga, kubanga ekisibo kye si kisibo wabula kibiina ky'abaagwa abaagwa okuva mu kitiibwa era ne bafuuka ebintu byonna ebitasaana ebigwanidde okuzikirizibwa. Njogerera ku bulimba obuliwo kati obukozesebwa okuteekateeka okuyimuka kw'abasatu abatali batukuvu. Kubanga waliwo abasatu abajulira mu ggulu, Kitaffe, Ekigambo, n'Omwoyo Omutukuvu: era bano abasatu bali bumu. Era waliwo n'abasatu abatali batukuvu abajja (era be bafaanana n'abasatu abajulira mu ggulu): dragoni, ensolo, n'omunnabbi ow'obulimba (naye nga naye nsolo). Era ensolo zino ezitaina magezi zakolebwa kuzikirizibwa, kubanga ebirindiridde byazo muliro ogwaka n'ekibiriiti mu nnyanja ennene ey'omuliro eyaka ennimi enjeru ez'ebbulu mu kizikiza ekikutte akalawali emirembe gyonna.

Njogera ku mbeera yaabwe ey'omu kiseera kino n'obufuzi bwaabwe obw'omulembe guno, kubanga tebannabikkulwa mu bujjuvu, kubanga aziyizaakyaliwo; naye nja kubiraga abeesigwa bange, be abo abalina amaaso okulaba n'amatu okuwulira. Mutegeere nti abalimbye bano bakola obulimba obw'amaanyi ne nga tebannabikkulwa n'okuweebwa obuyinza obutuufu, kubanga bajja kuweebwa obuyinza okuva eri dragoni; kubanga obwo abuweereddwa okumala akaseera katono era mu ngeri ey'ekkomo okukema n'okugezesa ensi, n'okulwanyisa abatukuvu n'okubawangula. Era wadde ng'ekyama eky'obujeemu kikola nnyo olw'ebigendererwa bino, okubikkulwa kwabyo tekunnatuuka naye kuli kumpi.

Kuba na kaakano balabika ng'abasajja abakulu, abasajja ab'ekisa, abasajja abazira n'abakola eby'obulungi, ababeera obulamu obw'okwerekereza. Naye temulimbibwa basumba ba nkukunyi, kubanga balabika ng'abalongoofu era abatukuvu mu maaso g'ekibiina ekinene ekijja okubewuunya era mu bbanga tono bagambe nti: Ani afaanana n'ensolo? (Kubanga na kaakano bakyamya ababuze, abasikiriziddwa obulogo bwaabwe.) Naye abo abalina amaaso okulaba n'amatu okuwulira, balabika nga bwe bali ddala: kubanga basumba ba nkukunyi abaggya amaanyi gaabwe ku dragoni omunene, ye Sitaani yennyini. Kubanga omu ajja kuva mu nnyanja era kati akolera mu kyama (emabega w'omukadde omugonvu eyeerimbise) olw'okuzikiriza Babulooni w'Ebyama; ate omulala ajja kuva mu nsi era atumiddwa mu bulimba eri abo abali mu Nsi Ensuubize era ajja kuba nnabbi w'obulimba ow'ekkuŋŋaaniro lya Sitaani, ye Moshiach waabwe ow'obulimba.

Kubanga nnabbi ow'obulimba ow'ensolo akolagana n'ensolo, efuga mu kyama, era bombi bakolera wamu okusitula mu bujjuvu ekyama eky'obujeemu, balyoke babikkulwe mangu; kubanga ebbanga lyabwe lituuse, wadde nga tebalina bwakabaka bwonnabwonna. Temugwa mu bulimba, kubanga omusumba omu ow'enkukunyi afuga mu kyama ye muddugavu w'omukadde omugonvu, ate omusumba omulala ow'enkukunyi alabika ng'ayigganyizibwa omukadde omugonvu akolagana n'omusumba ow'enkukunyi afuga mu kyama. Kubanga mu kiseera kino beefuula balabe, naye bonna ba mu kisibo kimu eky'enkukunyi, nga batuukiriza ebigendererwa bya dragoni.

Eky'ennaku, bangi abalowooza nti balokoka olw'okwatula Yesu nga Mukama n'omutima ogutakkiriza ku mimwa emitakkiriza (kubanga n'omutima omuntu akkiriza okutuuka ku butuukirivu) basikiriziddwa dayimooni zino, era bajja kulimbya ne ku balonde bange ddala bwe kisoboka, ne abo ababagoberera mu butamanya. Kubanga abasumba bano si balongoofu, n'ebyambalo byaabwe bikolebwa mu byoya by'endiga ebicaafu era ebiri mu ndwadde, kubanga basumba ba nkukunyi abalunda ekisibo eky'enkukunyi, era ku bibala byaabwe bye mulibategeerera.

Tebali balongoofu era beeru ng'omuzira, ng'endiga zange; kubanga Nze Mwana gw'Endiga atalina bbala newankubadde enkovu, eyalondebwa okuva mu kisibo okuba Okuyitako okutuukiridde okwa bulijjo. Nnaaza endiga zange mu musaayi gwange ogutangirira, era temuli nkukunyi zisangibwamu. Wadde nga beeyita nti batumiddwa Katonda Omuyinza w'ebintu byonna, Katonda Omuyinza w'ebintu byonna teyabatuma. Wadde nga bannabbi ab'obulimba bangi bamutegeeza mu bulimba, nze saamutuma, wabula dragoni. Kubanga nze kabona asinga obukulu emirembe gyonna okusinziira ku nteekateeka ya Merukizeddeeki, era tewali baana ba ndiga balala wabula nze, Kristo Yesu.

Obulokozi buli mu linnya lyange lyokka, kubanga tewali linnya lirala eriweereddwa mu bantu abantu lye bayinza okulokokeramu wabula eryange, Mukama waffe Yesu. Kubanga tewali muntu n'omu gwe ntegeka okufuna Ebigambo byange ataabiwulire. Bwe bafuna ebigambo byange mu kukkiriza oba bwe babaagaana mu butakkiriza lwe lunyiriri Kitaffe lw'asobozesezza buli omu okukola, kubanga Katonda takaka muntu yenna newankubadde okwagala kwe. Era nzija okusalira bonna omusango n'okunenya bonna abatali batukuvu mu bo, olw'ebikolwa byaabwe byonna ebitasaana bye baakola mu butasaana, n'olw'ebigambo byaabwe byonna ebikakali, aboonoonyi abatasaana bye baayogera okumuvvoola.

Njogedde nammwe mu ngeri nnyingi, era mu mirimu gyange emingi ntumye bannabbi bange n'abaweereza bange, kubanga obudde buweddewo. Era n'olwekyo nja kwogera ku bintu ebitakakasibwanga mu mirembe egyayita naye nga kati bibikkuddwa, kubanga ebiseera by'okubikkulwa kwange bituuse. Kubanga nnaatera okubikkulwa okuva mu ggulu, era nze, kubanga nze Masiya ow'amazima, ye Yeshua HaMashiach. Era nja kwogerera mu ngero eri abo abali ebweru, naye abo abalina amaaso okulaba n'amatu okuwulira bajja kutegeera, kubanga nja kukikakasa mu mwoyo gwange ogutondebwa mu bo.

Nzize okwogera ku bigambo Yokaana Omubikkulirwa bye yaweebwa, era nja kubalaga engeri ennyangu bwe munaakkiriza Ebigambo byange era ne muwombeeka emitima gyammwe eri eddoboozi lyange.

Laba! Njogera ku bafuzi babiri abajja abakolera awamu, era bajja kulimbalimba Ensi yonna ne batwala bangi awamu nabo mu muliro gw'obunnya. N'abo amannya gaabwe abatagawandiikibwa mu Kitabo ky'Obulamu bajja kubagoberera era bajja kukubibwa okulumwa kw'omusota, ensi kw'eyalabula, era akabonero k'omusota ako omuntu yenna taawonyezebwe; kubanga okulabula kuno kwa lubeerera, era ekyo Katonda ky'alangiridde tekiyinza kukyusibwa.

Abakulembeze bano, era abasumba ab'enkukunyi, kati beefuula abaweereza ba gavumenti aba Babulooni w'Ebyama. Kubanga wansi w'ebyambalo by'abasumba ab'enkukunyi bye bambala (kubanga buli omu yeerimbise mu ngeri ye), be nsolo ezirulira ez'okwegomba kwazo tekuyinza kukkusibwa, era bajja kwagala bonna babasinze n'ekifaananyi ky'ensolo ne dragoni ng'abali Katonda; kubanga banaatera okusumululwa aziyiza bw'aliggibwawo.

Kubanga mu bbanga ttono era amangu ago, bwe nnaakuŋŋaanya Ekkanisa yange eri nze mu bire, okuzikirira okw'amangu ago kujja kutuuka ku nsi; kubanga bajja kuzikiriza Babulooni ow'Ekyama, era olutalo olunene lujja kusaasaanira mu nsi yonna n'okuzikirira okw'amangu ago kujja kutuuka ku nsi. Abasatu abatali batukuvu bajja kuva mu vvu ly'amatongo n'akasasamalo ebirekeddwa omwebagazi w'embalaasi, abanaatera okusumululwa Omugole wange ng'amaze okuggibwayo n'atwalibwa gyendi mu ggulu. (Kubanga olwo lwokka lwe nja okuggulawo obubonero bw'endagaano yange kubanga Ekkanisa yange erokolebwa okuva ku busungu obujja, era nja kubanunula.) Bajja kuva mu vvu ly'olutalo, kawumpuli, enjala, n'obutali bwenkanya obungi, ne mu kufa kw'ebibiina by'abantu bingi nnyo.

Bajja kusuubiza emirembe n'obutebenkevu, era bajja kwogera eby'obulimba bingi bye bajja okuyisa ng'amagezi ga Katonda, era bajja kwogera ebintu bingi eby'okuwoolerezebwa era eby'okusalirwa omusango; era omu ajja kulaga omulala, era mu mukono gwabwe muliba ekigonjoolo kyaabwe, era obusagwa bwabwe obutayinza kuggyibwawo wadde okusonyiyibwa. Kubanga buli eyeewaayo okulumwa omusota, ajja kusigala ng'alina akabonero ako emirembe gyonna. Naye ogwo gwe gujja okuba omuwendo gw'okuyingira mu nsi yaabwe empya, era entegeka yaabwe empya eyakolebwa Sitaani ne bamalayika be gye bakola mu busirusiru olw'ezzadde lyabwe ery'omuzizo.

Bajja kusuubiza embeera empya etaliimu kufa na kuzikirira okunaatera okujja ku nsi yonna, okuzikirira okw'amangu ago bwe kujja ng'Ekkanisa yange ekuŋŋaanyizibwa. (Banoonya okuzzaawo omulembe gwaabwe ogwa zaabu Katonda gwe yazikiriza mu mataba ga Nuuwa.) Bajja kusuubiza emirembe, obulamu obuwanvu, eddembe okuva mu ndwadde, obulamu obulungi n'obutebenkevu, era bajja kusuubiza olunaku olupya. Naye abasumba bano ababiri ab'enkukunyi (abafaanana ng'abava mu kisibo) bajja kwogera nti Katonda Omuyinza w'ebintu byonna ye yabatumye, era ensi yonna ejja kubewuunya, naye tebali basumba. Nsaba mmwe, dragoni ayinza okulunda ekisibo? Ddi dragoni lyeyalabirirako endiga, okuggyako okukakasa okuzikirira kwazo okw'olubeerera n'okwawukana ne Patri ow'Ebitangaala, ye Yawe? Kubanga ensolo ne nnabbi ow'obulimba bajja kufuna amaanyi gaabwe okuva eri dragoni, era baddu b'okwegomba kwa dragoni n'okwagala kwe.

Mu kiseera ky'okubikkulwa kwabwe, bajja kwambula obufaananyi bwabwe obw'abaweereza ba gavumenti ab'omuwendo omutono; n'ekitiibwa kyonna, era n'ebyambalo byabwe eby'enkukunyi eby'amaliba g'endiga ag'enkukunyi n'embeera yaabwe ey'amazima, amaanyi, n'ebigendererwa bijja kubikkulwa. Ensi ejja kubewuunya n'amakubo gaabwe, era n'okubasinza okutuusa ku kuzikirira kwabwe. Bajja kutuuka ku siteegi y'ensi n'amaanyi mangi, era ekibiina ky'abakabaka ekkumi kijja kufuga. Ne kaakano, ekibiina kino kitegese dda eggye lyakyo mu kyama okulumba Babulooni w'Ekyama mu kyama. Beeteeseteese mu kyama, era bategekedde ddala okulumba, era balumbye Babulooni w'Ekyama mu lwatu okusinziira ku musango Katonda gw'alangiridde ogunaatera okumugwako, era tagenda kuddayo kugolokoka.

Kubanga obutali butuukirivu bwe butuuse mu maaso g'Oyo Asinga Obukulu, n'ebibi byabwe mu maaso g'entebe ey'obwakabaka ennene ey'omu ggulu, era mu maaso ga Katonda omunene ow'emirembe gyonna, kubanga tewali asinga; era omusango gumusomeddwa. Kubanga El Shaddai akozesa n'emikono gy'abalabe be okutuukiriza entegeka ze era n'abalabe be bagondera eddoboozi lye; kubanga ani ayinza okuziyiza Katonda Omuyinza w'ebintu byonna? Temukola nsobi, abasumba ababiri ab'enkukunyi kati bali mu lukwe emabega w'ekifaananyi ky'omuddugavu. Tebannabikkulwa eri Ensi, era bwe banaabikkulwa, ensi ejja kubewuunya n'ebagoberera okutuuka ku kufa kwaabwe. Naye tebali ndiga n'akatono, wabula nsolo nkambwe munda ezijja okuweebwa amaanyi amangi omuliro okuva eri dragoni, kubanga ago gaweereddwa okumala akaseera katono. Kubanga bajja kweteekawo okuziyiza, era mu kifo kya, omusumba omulungi; wabula abo abalina okwawula okw'eby'omwoyo bajja kulaba enkukunyi, era obubi n'obwonoonefu obw'olubeerera obuli mu bo, kubanga balina endowooza etali nnongoofu era babi okutuuka ku buziba bw'obulamu bwabwe. Mumanyi dda gwe beefuula, naye temumanyi gwe bali ddala, era beewadde amannya ag'obulimba, n'olwekyo omuwendo teguyinza kubalibwa.

Kubanga ensolo ejja okugolokoka okuva mu nnyanja okufuga amawanga yabaddewo dda, era erabika nga kati teriwo naye mu mazima ekyaliwo, era ekulembera emikono gy'omulundi omukadde ogutalina magezi. Ono ye oyo ayitibwa mu Lwebbulaniya ng'akozesa ebigambo bye nnayogera bwe nnalaba Sitaani ng'olumyansi lugwa okuva mu ggulu. Olumyansi lugwa okuva mu ggulu: soma oyige. Kubanga tayagala bakazi, so tayagala katonda yenna: kubanga ajja kwekuluntaza okusinga byonna. Kati mbabuuliza mulabe oba omusajja alumwa n'azaala omwana? Naye mukyala we alabika ng'omukazi alina abaana, wadde nga si baana be.

Ensolo ejja okugolokoka okuva mu nsi mugimanyi dda, era bangi ku mmwe mumugoberera mu butamanya, era nga mulimbye dda so nga mwandyagambye nti mbuulirira okuziyiza obulimba. Kubanga ava mu nsi, naye mu kika kye temuli muntu n'omu akubiddwako akabonero ku 144,000. Era ku Ddaani yagamba nti, Ddaani mwana gw'empologoma: ajja kubuuka okuva e Basani, era ava mu kika kya Ddaani, era yeenyumiriza mu lunyiriri lwe, kubanga omusaayi omubisi gukulukuta mu misuwa gye. Ayogerwako ng'omulembe guno Kuulo era ajja kuba muzimbi wa yeekaalu alyoke aleete eky'omuzizo eky'okuzikiriza mu kiseera kye, era kati azimba dda mu Nsi Ensuubize olw'ekkuŋŋaaniro lya Sitaani.

Baana bange abato, mbalekera amagezi okufumiitirizaako, era bino tebikyali byama wabula bibikkuddwa mu kiseera kyabyo. Ebbanga lyabyo lyokka teriribikkulwa eri ensi okutuusa aziyiza lw'aliggibwawo, era kijja kubawo mangu nnyo okusinga bwe mulowooza. Kubanga ebintu bino binaatera okutuukirira. era tewakyali kiseera, kubanga ennamba zigudde okuva ku ssaawa. Musigale nga muli beesigwa mu nze, era bwe nnaaba nkakasizza mu mwoyo gwammwe obuntu bwabwe, mukyuse ab'oluganda lwammwe. Laba, okwabika kwange kunaatera okutuukirira.

Nze Kigambo kya Katonda ekyefuula omubiri, era mbeera munda mu mmwe.

Mbayita okujja mu maka gammwe ag'omu ggulu mu bbanga eritali wala.
