Kubanga mazima ddala, tewali bitonde byonna ebifaanana nammwe ebigenda okukolebwa amangu ago era mu ngeri etasuubirwa kaakati, nedda si mu kifo kyonna eky'omu bitonde bya El Shaddai byonna, mazima ddala muli ba njawulo.

Kubanga nalagira Biblaridion:144 okugolola emikono gye ng'alangirira era ng'ategeeza nti obudde tebulibaawo nate, era bwe kityo KAATI mu maanyi amangi n'obuyinza obwaweebwa era obwasumululwa omubaka wange ow'amaanyi era omuweereza. Seera.

N’olwekyo, mwetegeke, era mwekweke mu madoboozi ge mbategekedde, kubanga essaawa emu n’ensolo kati eteekeddwa okugwa ku nsi mangu nnyo era mu ngeri etasuubirwa kati ejja okusaanyaawo ddala Babulooni ow’Ekyama. N’olwekyo, mwesaleeyo ddala, mu kusuubira okujjuvu kubanga nja kubanyagula gyendi, kubanga Harpazo oyo Omukulu atuuse!

Mwekweke abatali batuukirivu n’abo ab’omu Babulooni ow’Ekyama abali wansi w’omusango, kubanga omusango ogwo gubajjira, baagalwa bange. Mwekweke muleme okugabana ku musango ogutali gwammwe!

Laba! Abeesigwa mu Kristo Yesu, ye abo bonna be nterezezza kati ku ddoboozi ery’enjawulo ery’ekkondeere eryo eryatondebwa mu ngeri ey’enjawulo era erimasiddwa obulungi eryaweebwa mu mukono gwange Adonai-YHVH yennyini.
