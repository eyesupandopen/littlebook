Agusito 03, 2024

Mukube Ekikung'uujulu olwa Babulooni Ekyama!

Muwulire ebigambo byange era muwulire eddoboozi ly'oyo akaabira mu ddungu! Nkoowoola, nkoowoola, mujja kunnwulira? Musirise n'ennimi zammwe ezijjudde ebibi era mufukamire mu kwenenya muwulire eddoboozi lyange etaggwaawo bwe mukyayinza, kubanga okwagala kwange kwe nnina gye muli kungi, era nze oyo ayiwa amaziga amangi mu nnaku enkulu olw'ebyo ebijja okutuuka ku abo abanyooma eddoboozi lyange. Kubanga ne mu kaseera kano akasembayo, bonna abaddamu bayitibwa okuyingira mu bulamu obutaggwaawo. Kale mun मानने nga Mukama wammwe, kubanga nze Yesu Omunazaaleeti, era nze Katonda ow'olubeerera gwe yatuma okubataasa nga akatyabaga n'omusango tebinnatuuka. Lwaki mwerowooza nga abasaana okufiirwa obulamu obutaggwaawo? Mujja kulonda nnimi za muliro ez'okuzikirira okusinga ennimiro ez'emirembe ez'obuyinike gye mujja okufunira eddundiro etaggwaawo?

Kuba nga n'omumenyi w'amateeka eyakomererwa nange ku Gologoosa bw'anaaba nange mu lusuku lwa Katonda, bwe ntyo bwe nnaabawonya ddala okutuuka ku nkomerero bwe munnafuula Mukama wammwe kaakano! TEWALIIWO BBDDE LYA KWONOONA. Kuba nnafira ebweru w'enzigi za Yerusaalemi n'aboonoonyi bwe nnatwala ekifo kyammwe mu musango, era mujja kulokolebwa bwe munneekyukirako mu kwenenya kaakano. Mbagamba mazima ddala, nti: Ebigambo bino tebyatuuse ku matu gammwe lwa butanwa, era mangu ddala wajja kubaawo okuzikirizibwa okw'amaanyi era bangi tebajja kusimattuka era okugaana kwabwe kujja kuvugaanyizibwa emirembe gyonna mu muliro ogutazikira ogwategekerwa bonna abalonda okunywerera ku bibi byabwe n'okusigala nga bali wamu n'abo abaagwa.

Okwegatta ku bujeemu bwa Setaani, ne bwe kiba mu butamanya obw'ekizikiza, kwe kwetaba n'obwegendereza mu bibi byabwe, era bwe kityo bonna abakikola bajja kusitula omusango gwabwe emirembe gyonna. Mujja kukkiriza okuyitibwa kwange, nga mumanyi nti Katonda yanzuukiza mu bafu nneme kufa nate era nga mumanyi nti nze Mukama w'abakama era Kabaka wa Bakabaka? Mwenenye bwe mukyayinza, kubanga Yakuwa mwetegefu okuggalawo oluggi lw'eryato, era tewali bbanga era mu kaseera konna nja kunyakula abeesigwa okubatwala mu ggulu, ye bonna abalokolebwa okuva mu busungu mu ssaddaaka yange erokola bulijjo era etaggwaawo. Kale mulangirire nze, Yesu Omunazaaleeti nga Mukama wammwe era mumanye nti ndi mulamu emirembe gyonna kubanga Katonda yanzuukiza mu bafu nneme kufa nate.

Baana bange, muwulirize era mweyongere okufukamira mu kwenenya okutaggwaawo era muwulire eddoboozi lya Mukama wammwe Yesu Kristo, ye oyo Yakuwa gwe yatuma ng'omwana gw'endiga gwa Katonda. Muwulire Ebigambo byange, mmwe Ekkanisa yange era Omugole wange, Kuba nga Kabona wammwe Omukulu ow'olubeerera ow'ennono ya Merukizeddeeki nneewaddeyo nga Paasika yammwe etuukiridde, era nnawaayo omusaayi gwange ogutalimu kibi okutuuka ku ttondo erisembayo okubununula kwammwe okujjuvu okuva mu mbeera yammwe ey'okugwa kati era okununnulibwa kwammwe okujjuvu kutuuse, kale musanyuke!

Era bangi abanneeyanirizza n'okwatula erinnya lyange ery'olubeerera nga Mukama waabwe bajja kuba n'obulamu obutaggwaawo. Kubanga nze Yesu Omunazaaleeti Mukama wammwe ow'olubeerera, era mfuulye bonna abaatula erinnya lyange mu biseera bino eby'ekisa n'okusaasira abaana ba Katonda ab'olubeerera. Laba, okwagala okungi Kitaffe kw'abawadde, okuyitibwa abaana ba Katonda: olw'ensonga eno ensi tebamanyi, kubanga temumanyi nze, Kristo Yesu Mukama w'abakama era Kabaka wa Bakabaka. Abaagalwa ennyo, kaakano muli baana ba Katonda, naye tekinnaba kulabisibwa kye mujja okuba: era mumanyi nti bwe nnaalabisibwa, mujja kuba nga nze: kubanga mujja kundaba nga bwe ndi.

Baana bange abato, mutegeere nti nja kulabisibwa mu kaseera konna kati ng'okulabika kwange mu bire kutuuse. Kubanga nze, Kristo Yesu, nja kuserengeta okuva mu ggulu n'oluyoogaano, n'eddoboozi lya malayika omukulu, n'ekkondeere lya Katonda: n'abafu mu Kristo be bajja okusooka okuzuukira. Oluvannyuma mmwe abali abalamu, ne musigalawo, mujja kunyakulibwa wamu nabo mu bire, okusisinkana Mukama wammwe ow'olubeerera mu bbanga: era bwe mutyo bwe mujja okubeeranga ne Mukama wammwe. Mujja kukkiriza ebigambo bino okubakubagiza, baana bange? Mukkirizza Setaani okubaleetera okwegaana ebigambo bino ebyawandiikibwa obulungi bonna balabe? Mujja kulonda okusigala mu abo abasalirwa omusango mu bujeemu obw'obukakanyavu? Okwegaana ekikolwa kino ekyawandiikibwa obulungi mu byawandiikibwa n'omukono gw'omutume wange Pawulo kwe kunneegaana kubanga nzija okulokola abeesigwa okuva mu busungu kubanga bwe zityo ensuubizo za Yakuwa bwe zaabikkulirwa mu kyama ekinene kya Katonda ekyawandiikibwa Pawulo emyaka emingi egiyise.

Mbawabula: temunneegaana, era mulinnye eryato nga terinnaba kuziba. Kuba bonna abalonda okubeera mu misango egijja bajja kujja gye ndi nga bakoowoola erinnya lyange okutuukira ddala ku nkomerero, era bangi bajja kubonaabona okutuuka ku mukka gwabwe ogusembayo. Naye era bajja kuzuukira mu bulamu obutaggwaawo n'empeera enkulu n'essanyu eritagambika! Era bwe kityo bonna abayitirwa mu bulamu obutaggwaawo bajja kutuuka ku busika bwabwe obutaggwaawo, kubanga tewali n'omu ayinza kunyakula n'ow'okuntikko mu mmwe okuva mu mikono gyange egy'olubeerera. Kale muwulire ebigambo byange era musigale mu ndowooza emu eri mu nze, Kristo Yesu, kubanga nze Kigambo kya Katonda ekyefuula omubiri era bulijjo njogera mazima. Mulekere awo okuwuliriza amaloboozi amangi agatalina kukkiriza agagaana Ebigambo byange oba mujja kwesanga nga musigaddewo nago so nga ekyo tekyetaagisa.

Kuba mmwe abalokole bange, era mmwe kaakano muli Baana ba Katonda Omuwangaazi, mmwe ab'a Yakuwa yennyini okuyita mu era mu kyamagero eky'okuzaalibwa obuggya mwalokolebwa emirembe gyonna era mwazaalibwa omulundi ogw'okubiri okuva mu nsigo etevunda. Naye, muli bagenyi era batambuze mu nsi ez'amawanga, era bangi ku mmwe muli munda mu nsalo z'omwenzi oyo omukulu, ye ensi eziri mu njegere nga zilindirira omusango gwazo omujjuvu n'okuzikirizibwa: Kuba omugabo gw'omusango ogw'emirundi ebiri gulangiriddwa ku Babulooni Ekyama era bwe kityo bwe kiriba. Kuba mu kiseera ki Yakuwa ky'ataalokolangamu batuukirivu mu Musango, kubanga tewali kitonde kiyinza kugerekera busiru ku Yakuwa, n'obujulirwa bwa Lutti bujulira okutuusa leero. Ne mu nsi ezo ez'omuzizo eza Sodoma ne Ggomola, Yakuwa yatuma Bamalayika be okulokola omusajja ono omu omutuukirivu eyali atudde mu miryango gy'ekibuga ky'omulabe.

Era bwe ntyo bwe nnaalokola Ekkanisa yange eyeesigwa, be bonna be nnateekako akabonero n'omwoyo oyo omutukuvu ow'ensuubizo okuva ku lunaku lwa Pentekoote ku kuzaalibwa kw'Ekkanisa yange okwawandiikibwa mu bujulirwa bwa Ebikolwa! (Temusomanga?) Era bonna abaafa nga beesigwa mu Kristo okuva ku ntandikwa y'emirembe gy'Ekkanisa yange ne bonna abali abalamu ne basigalawo bajja kukungaanyizibwa wamu mu kusemba kw'eriiso era emirembe gyonna bajja kuba nange! Kuba nze oyo ajja olw'abeesigwa bange, abayite, n'abalonde, ye Omubiri gwange n'Ekkanisa yange era n'Omugole wange. Nja kununula abeesigwa, be abo abatambudde okusinziira ku mwoyo gwange ne bonna abeenenyezza ebibi byabwe ne bamfuula Mukama okuva mu mutima ogujjudde okukkiriza kubanga be Isiraeri wa Katonda! Era ekikolwa kino ekijja kijja kuba kinene era okukankana kujja kuba kunene era n'obubonero bungi okusembera kwange kwarangirirwa era kukyategeezebwa mu ggulu n'obubonero waggulu n'obubonero mu nsi wansi. Era bwe kityo okukung'aanya ebibala ebibereberye bwe kuliba bonna balabe era batunule mu kwewuunya!

Era bwe kityo eddoboozi lyange likyavugaanyizibwa mu nsi eno eyagwa, nga lituuka ku maaso, amatu, n'emitima gya bonna be litumiddwa. Owulira eddoboozi lya Yesuwa HaMashiach, otegeera eddoboozi lyange? Ojja kwaniriza ebigambo byange? Ompulira nga njogera n'omutima gwo? Kuba nga bwe kyawandiikibwa: emitima gy'abantu bano gigezze olw'okulya ennyo, n'amatu gaabwe ga kiggala okuwulira, n'amaaso gaabwe baagaziba, si kulwawo mu kiseera kyonna ne balaba n'amaaso gaabwe, ne bawulira n'amatu gaabwe, ne bategeera n'omutima gwabwe, ne bakyusibwa, nange ne mbawonya. Bangi ku bano babasenseredde okwebaka n'ebigambo eby'omuzizo naye ebiwoomerera ebya Yesu omulala, naye kakasa nti tewali mulala.

Kuba mu lulimi lwonna erinnya lyange lwe liyitibwa, nze Yesu Omunazaaleeti, ye Yesuwa HaMashiach, nja kuwulira era nja kulokola okutuukira ddala bonna abakoowoola erinnya lyange okutuusa ku nkomerero. Kuba ebigambo byange eby'amagezi, ye enjiri etaggwaawo byabikkuliddwa era bivugaanyizibwa mu nsi yonna. Era ebigambo byange bituuka ku abo be bitumiddwa, kuba bwe gaty'o ge Bigambo bya Katonda Asinga Obukulu, ye Yakuwa, kubanga tewali Kigambo kya Katonda na kimu ekiddaayo nga kikalu, era bijja kutuukiriza ekyo kye bigendereddwamu era lissibwa omukisa amaaso gammwe, kubanga galaba: n'amatu gammwe, kubanga gawulira n'emitima gyammwe gijjudde okukkiriza, era nsazise okwagala kwa Katonda mu mitima gyammwe mwennyini mwe mukaabira nti Abba Kitaffe! Muli baana ba Katonda era tekirabika kye mujja okuba, naye bwe nnaalabika mujja kulaba mu bujjuvu era obulamu bwammwe bukwekeddwa mu nze, Kristo Yesu, era obulamu bwammwe bunaatera okubikkulibwa mu bujjuvu era kati kye kiseera eky'okubikkula.

Laba! Babulooni Ekyama etudde mu njegere ng'amayembe ekkumi gamaliriza okwetegekera okuzikirizibwa kwayo okw'olubeerera. Mazima ddala, eri Babulooni Ekyama ne mu myaka mingi kaakano nnatuma abatume bange, nnatuma bannabbi bange, nnatuma ababuulizi b'enjiri bange, nnatuma abasumba bange, nnatuma abayigiriza bange, nnatuma abayigirizwa bange abeesigwa n'abooluganda era nnatuma ne bannamwandu mu bwavu bwabwe, era nfuse omwoyo gwange mu bujjuvu mu nnaku zino ezisembayo ez'ekisa n'okusaasira!

Naye mu kifo ky'amawanga okutuula mu bibukutu n'evvu, basanyusibwa obubonero n'okulabulibwa okw'omusango ogunaatera okujja nga bakuba ebifaananyi ne vidiyo okwesanyusa. Bagaanyi ababaka bange abaaleeta omusana gw'enjiri yange ey'okulokoka, basekereranga ne banyooma bannabbi bange, balumbye ababuulizi b'enjiri bange, boonoonye abasumba bange, era bayigganyizza n'okukosa abayigiriza bange.

Amagye g'ogusota omumyufu omunene gakwetoolodde ggwe Babulooni ekyama era gali ddala munda mu nsalo zo. Kuba n'abakulembeze bo abalyolyomi Abaziyoni bakutunze olw'akaserekale k'omugaati eri abalabe bo era n'okutwala mu maaso enkwe zaabwe eza Setaani. Era El Shaddai akkirizza abasajja bano abalalu okweyongerayo, era n'okukiteeka mu mitima gyabwe okutuukiriza ebigendererwa bye. El Shaddai alina amaanyi akusala omusango, ggwe malaaya omumyufu atuula n'amalala mu kusanyuka ne bw'oba nga wambuddwa ekitiibwa kyonna, wambuddwa eby'okulya byonna ebiwooma, wambuddwa n'amaka go kubanga mpaddeyo amaka go n'ensi zo mu mikono gy'abalabe bo. Ne mu mikono gy'ensolo n'omunnabbi ow'obulimba, kubanga bakolera wamu okukuzikiriza okw'olubeerera emabega w'ekifaananyi ky'omukadde omusirusiru. Kuba ensolo enaatera okusuula wansi omwenzi omukulu ensolo gw'ekooye okusitula, era ebigendererwa bye bituukiriddwa. Ky'eva ejja kwokebwa ddala, n'okuzikirizibwa ddala mu ssaawa emu era okuzikirizibwa kwayo tekulwawo.

Kuba ensalosalo zaabwe ez'olutalo, n'ensalosalo zaabwe emmyufu ze baawa mu bukuusa obutayisibwa, ziteereddwawo era ne zisalibwa emirundi n'emirundi n'obwegendereza. Temukkiririza bulimba bwabwe, newaakubadde okwewolereza kwabwe okw'obulimba kubanga banoonya okulabika ng'abatuukirivu mu lutalo lwabwe olujja era ensolo n'omunnabbi ow'obulimba abatannayimuka mu bujjuvu bajja kuyimuka mu bujjuvu okuva mu vvu ne beewaana nti omusango gwa Katonda gwagwa okuweereza ebigendererwa byabwe, kale temukkiririza na kamu ku bulimba bwabwe obutono era n'omusango gwabwe ogusembayo tegulwawo.

Kuba bajja kweyongerayo era n'okukola ebibi ebisingawo obubi okusinga Babulooni Ekyama, era balina omusango mu kukulembera Babulooni Ekyama mu bujjuvu okubuuka n'okutuuka ku kuzikirizibwa kwayo okw'olubeerera, kubanga Katonda akiteese mu mitima gyabwe okutuukiriza by'ayagala. Bakolera wamu mu nkukutu era bonna abalabika ng'abalabe bakolera wamu mu kyama nga bazannya okusinziira ku mpandiika yaabwe era ne bazannya ebitundu byabwe ku siteegi y'ensi, era bajja kutemula bonna abali mu bibiina byabwe abalonda obutayiga na kuzannya bitundu byabwe mu ngeri etuukira ddala okutuukiriza ebigendererwa by'ogusota. Kuba ogusota guwa ensolo amaanyi gaagwo n'entebe yaagwo ey'obwakabaka, n'obuyinza obungi, N'amayembe ekkumi gafuna obuyinza nga bakabaka essaawa emu n'ensolo era bano balina endowooza emu, era bajja kuwa amaanyi gaabwe eri ensolo kubanga bwe gityo bwe gabaweereddwa Yakuwa okutuukiriza. Mwekebejje baana bange era muyigire ku nze era mugoberere amakubo gange, kubanga amakubo gange ge makubo ga Yakuwa, Katonda ow'olubeerera omukulu!

Mutegeere era mweteeke mu, kubanga ensolo zino ez'obulyake n'abakabaka ababi ba mu bibiina by'Ogusota abafuze Babulooni Ekyama okuva mu bibiina byabwe eby'ekyama okuva lwe yatandika. Omusajja eyeefuula n'okulimba, n'omusajja omubi, atambula n'akamwa akakyamu. Yee beefuula kuba balala kubanga bonna beefuulifu, era nga bakwekeddwa mu mawanga gonna omuwemba gwasigibwa. Bakola obubonero n'amaaso gaabwe: bategeeza n'ebigere byabwe: balagira n'engalo zaabwe. Ebintu eby'obukaba biri mu mitima gyabwe: balowooza obubi buli kiseera, era batabula ennyombo. Ky'ekiriva kireeta okuzikirizibwa kwabwe amangu: bajja kuzikirizibwa mangu nga tewali kudda buto. Ebintu bino omukaaga Mukama by'akyawa: yee, omwoyo gwe gunyooma musanvu: Amaaso ag'amalala, olulimi olulimba, n'emikono egiyiwa omusaayi ogutalina musango, omutima ogulowooza enkwe enkyamu, ebigere ebyanguya okuddukira mu bubi, Omujulirwa ow'obulimba ayogera obulimba, n'oyo atabula ennyombo mu booluganda.

Muli bazibe b'amaaso? Mugaana okutegeera obubonero bwabwe musobole okweyongera okutuula mu butamanya obw'okwegomba nti abakulembeze bammwe ba mu abo abaagwa? Temutegeera nti abasanyusa bammwe ba mu abo abaagwa? Temutegeera nti tebali ba buzaale bwe bumu na mmwe, kubanga bangi beekyusizza emirembe gyonna mu bulalu bwabwe era abamu bali muwemba okuva mu kuzaalibwa era Omutonzi ye Kitaabwe? Naye mulonda okutuula mu kizikiza n'okwegomba, ne musanyukira era ne mwota mu bibi byammwe nga mwekulukuunya mu busa n'embizzi ezibakulembedde okutuuka ku miryango gyennyini egy'omuzimu gye bangi gye beeyolekera mu burofa ne mu malala. Kye kyandeetera okuwandiika mu bbaluwa yange eri Abefeso gye nnawa Pawulo nti: Zuukuka ggwe eyeebase, oyimuke ove mu kufa, nange, Kristo Yesu, nja kukuwa omusana! Mwekuume nno mutambulenga n'amagezi: si ng'abasiru, naye ng'abagezigezi. Era bwe ntyo bwe mbikkula abakulembeze bano ababi aba Babulooni Ekyama eri abayite bange abalonde n'abeesigwa muleme okugwa mu bulyolyomi bwabwe era musobole okuva mu ye mu bujjuvu ne mweyawula mu ngeri zammwe zonna era muleme okugabana ku bibonoobono bye.

Muwulire ebigambo byange era mutegeere ekigenda mu maaso kaakano, kubanga okulumba kwabwe okw'ekibwatukira kutuuse. Kaakano njogera ne Babulooni ow'Ekyama wadde nga muggavu wa matu, kasiru, era muzibe w'amaaso, era nga musibe mu njegere ddala olw'omusango gwe. Nga bwe bamaze emyaka emingi nga bateekateeka okulumba kwabwe okw'ekibwatukira, era nga bakakasizza okuzikirizibwa kwa Babulooni ow'Ekyama okuyita mu kuwola ku magoba amangi, n'enkola endala ez'obulimba, kubanga n'ennyingo ze ez'ensimbi si kirala wabula kisuubizo kikalu kya mabanjja agaaleetebwawo abakulembeze bammwe abaweereza abavunja b'ensimbi, era Babulooni ow'Ekyama atundiddwa mu mikono gy'abalabe baabwe okuyita mu nkola zino ez'amagezi. Temutegeera nkola ya bbanka ey'okukuuma ekitundu ky'ensimbi n'ensimbi etesigamye ku kintu kya muwendo? Musome muyige.

Saabawa mmwe kyakulabirako bwe nnagoba abavunja b'ensimbi mu Yeekaalu? Naye, mu kifo ky'okubagoba ebweru, mubayita okujja okwegabula nammwe ku kyeggulo n'amaka gammwe, era mwawongera abaana bammwe n'abazzukulu bammwe ng'omusingo okutuukiriza okwegomba kwammwe okw'omululu, era bwe kityo ezzadde lyammwe lisuubuddwa okukakasa okuzikirira kwammwe mulyoke mubeere mu ssanyu ly'ekibi. Ggwe, Babulooni ow'Ekyama, otta abaana bo abatanazaalibwa mu ssanyu lingi n'amalala, era abassi b'abataliiko musango balina omusango gwabwe ogwateekebwateekebwa era omusango gwo tegulwawo. Wadde nga waliwo abasigaddewo abatono abaliko abalinnya olukubo okuyingira mu lyato nga El Shaddai tannaggalawo luggi, abasinga obungi balisanga nga basigaddewo eri omusango kubanga balironzeewo okusigala ebweru nga basekerera era nga beeyagala mu bulamu.

Amangu ago, mu ssaawa emu okuzikirizibwa kwayo kuligwawo mbagirawo era tebaliwonawo. Okuva lw'eyeewaayo mu buweereza eri abalabe be ab'olubeerera, kaakano balireetawo okuzikirizibwa kwe okw'olubeerera kubanga Babulooni ow'Ekyama taliddayo kuggumuka. Ggwe Babulooni ow'Ekyama, okuva lw'awatunda abaana bo ng'omwazima gw'amabanjja go olyoke weeyongere okuba mu ssanyu ly'ekibi (kubanga n'ensimbi zo za magoba), okuva lw'watta abataliiko musango, n'ebirala bingi by'okoze. Kaakano nja kukuwaayo ddala mu mikono gyabwe era Yahweh alangiridde omugabo gw'omusango ogw'emirundi ebiri ku ggwe Babulooni ow'Ekyama. Era bangi ku baana bo baliweerezebwa mu nkambi z'emirimu n'okubakyusa endowooza, era bangi balitwalibwa mu nsi engwira kubanga ogusota guganyiziddwa okulya ensi n'abantu abanjeemye, nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, ne balondawo Barabba Masiya waabwe omulimba. Era bangi baliteekebwako akabonero kaako ak'ensolo ne batunda emyoyo gyabwe n'ezzadde lyabwe eri ekibonerezo eky'olubeerera okuva mu maaso ga Katonda ataggwaawo era ow'olubeerera olw'essanyu ly'ekibi ery'akaseera akatono.

Mukube ebiwoobe olw'abantu ba Babulooni ow'Ekyama, na mmwe abatudde mu kizikiza n'obutamanya obutakoma obw'okuzikirira kwammwe okujja. Mu maaso gammwe ddala era n'olukusa lw'akalulu kammwe n'olw'obugayaavu bwammwe obw'okutafaayo, eggwanga lyammwe lyonna lisuuliddwa mu maaso gammwe. Temukyalina busobozi oba nsawo kulwana ntalo, kubanga Nze, ye Kristo Yesu, ngyejjeeko amaanyi gammwe ag'amagye ne ngawaayo mu mikono gy'abalabe bammwe. Ebibuga byammwe ebyali ebikulu biriko mu matongo g'obumenyi bw'amateeka n'obwavu, bye mwembi abazannyi bammwe na mmwe bye mwenyumiririzaamu mu malala amangi. Abaedomu baaggyawo dda amakolero gaabwe mu nsi zammwe, era temulina maanyi ga kukola bintu ge mwalina edda wadde n'ebikozesebwa ebitannakolebwako okukola bye mwetaaga okulwana entalo zammwe ezitakoma.

Kubanga muva mu lutalo okudda mu lulala, era muzikiririzza ddala ensi, ez'ebweru n'ez'awaka. Kubanga okuva mu ggwe, Babulooni ow'Ekyama, mwe mwavudde ekibonyoobonyo ky'Amagye Amamyuufu, nga kisaasaanya mu nsi yonna ekizimbulukusa eky'omusaala ky'Ogusota omumyufu omunene, kubanga mwawerereza abagudde mu nkukutu okuva ku lubereberye n'okutuusa leero. Ggwe Babulooni ow'Ekyama, owaayo olukusa lwo n'okusiima eri abafuzi bo Abasitaani, abamanyi obulungi enteekateeka zaabwe era abamanyi obulungi nti bali mu kugezaako okuzikiriza ddala eggwanga lyo edda eryali eddene na ggwe. Kubanga mulina omusango gw'omusaayi gw'abatukuvu, era omusaayi gw'abajulizi gukaabirira okuva ku nsi okusaba omusango gwammwe era singa Yahweh tabasala musango yandyetondedde amawanga g'azze asalira omusango mu biseera ebyayita, n'ago agawerereza ng'okulabula ku musango gwammwe ogujja gw'omulindaalindira.

Okuva mu ggwe, Babulooni ow'Ekyama, mwe mwava okuzikirira okwaleetebwa ku nsi engwira Amagye Amamyuufu, Abaedomu bwe beeyanza eri abakozi n'ekizimbulukusa kyabwe eky'obutwa, ye ntegeka yaabwe ey'okuvvoola Katonda eyaweebwa ensimbi Engabo Emmyuufu. Kubanga abanyazi ab'edda, ye bagagga b'amakolero ab'emirembe egyayita, (n'abo be mwatundako ezzadde lyammwe) be bavunaanyizibwa ku kibonyoobonyo ky'amagye amamyuufu. Babulooni ow'Ekyama n'olutimbe lwe olw'amabanjja yawa ensimbi n'awagira Amagye Amamyuufu mu nteekateeka zaago okuva ku lubereberye, era Babulooni ow'Ekyama abadde kitundu ku mukutu gwago okuva ku lubereberye Engabo Emmyuufu bwe yeeteekawo mu nsi yonna. N'omukka ogw'omuliro ogw'ogusota ogwava mu Ngabo Emmyuufu, kubanga be bagudde abatambulira mu mmwe.

Okuva mu Babulooni ow'Ekyama abakulembeze baabwe "ab'enkyukakyuka" mwe baatendekerwa mu nkukutu kubanga atudde ku mazzi amangi, era baaweerezebwa mu nkukutu mu nsi engwira, ne mu Gogi ne Magogi era enkyukakyuka zaabwe zaali nnene, era za maanyi, era nkambwe era kati zibisse ku nsi. Baasaasaanya ekizimbulukusa kyabwe eky'obussitaani mu nsi z'ebuvanjuba ne bamalawo ddala byonna ebyali mu kkubo lyabwe. Baava mu Babulooni ow'Ekyama ne baba kibonyoobonyo ku nsi, era mu kiseera ekyo kyonna Babulooni ow'Ekyama yalimbye n'omwenzi omukazi n'agamba nti ayogerera nze n'eyeeyita Omukristaayo. Okuggyawo kuno okw'amaanyi okw'entegeka enkadde kutambudde mu nsi yonna era kati kutuuse ku mutendera gwakwo ogusembayo, era balikkirizibwa entegeka yaabwe, naye okugoberera ebigendererwa bya Yahweh eby'olubeerera, kubanga mu mazima abagudde bakankana era entiisa ekwata emyoyo gyabwe emibi kubanga bamanyi nti omusango gwabwe oguludde nga gujja gutuuse ku bo. Kubanga ggwe Babulooni ow'Ekyama mu bwenzi bwo n'obwamalaaya bwo, osula n'omulabe.

Kati omusango gwo gujja mu bujjuvu Babulooni ow'Ekyama era oli kyakulabirako eri bonna okulaba, naye weeyongera okuba mu butamanya n'obuzibe bw'amaaso ng'oyogera mu mitima gyo nti akabi tekalikujjirako, wadde nga wajaganyizza abakulembeze bo bwe baaleeta akabi ku mbalama z'amawanga agatabalika mu ntalo zaabwe ezitali za ntuukirivu ze wasanyukira ng'olaba ku ttivi.
