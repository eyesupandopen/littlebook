Jjulaayi 13, 2024

Okuyimuka kw'Abalambya Abasatu Abatali Batukuvu!

Noolwekyo obudde busembedde okuggwaawo n'okulabika kwange kunaatera! Kaakano ng'enda kubategeeza entegeka y'ebintu ebinaatera okubaawo, n'olwekyo mutege amatama muwulire eddoboozi lya Masiya wammwe, ye Yeshua HaMashiach, mazima ddala ebigambo bya Mukama wa bakama, Kabaka wa bakabaka, Masiya, era kabona asinga obukulu emirembe n'emirembe ng'engeri ya Merukizeddeeki bwe yali. Oboolyawo amalala gammwe ag'eggwanga gajja kuziba emyoyo gyammwe ne mutandaba, ggwe Babulooni ow'Ekyama: mazima ddala Amawanga ga Amerika Amagatte. Kubanga olowooza nti otudde nga Nnabagereka so tewali kabi kajja kukugwako, era weeyogerera nti, Nze siri nnamwandu! Naye okuzikirira kwo kujja kujja mu ssaawa emu era toliddayo kuyimuka wadde obukulembeze bwo okubonyaabonya ensi ya Yakuwa, kubanga omugabo omulundi ebiri ogw'omusango gwategeezebwa Yakuwa, era ekyo Yakuwa ky'ategeeza tewali muntu ayinza kukikyusa.

Lowoozaako, bwe kiba kisoboka, ensi etaliimu Bukristaayo na mawanga ga Kikristaayo. Tewalibaawo Kanisa ndala era Obukristaayo bujja kuba butali mu mateeka mu nsi yonna, n'ekiseera ky'Ekkanisa kijja kuba kiweddeko, era n'ekiseera kyange eky'ekisa ekyalangirirwa abatume bange n'abannabbi mu kyasa ekyasooka, era baasiga ensigo gye muyinza okufuniramu obubudamo mu biseera bino eby'okunakuwala, mazima ddala Ekigambo kyange ekitukuvu n'ebyama bya Katonda! Kikulu nnyo okutegeera ebintu bino nga tukozesa olulimi lwennyini olwakozesebwa Yokaana Omubikkulirwa, kubanga yagamba nti: Ne nnyimirira ku musenyu gw'ennyanja: ne ndaba ensolo eva mu nnyanja, ng'erina emitwe musanvu n'amayembe kkumi, ne ku mayembe gaayo engule kkumi, ne ku mitwe gyayo erinnya ery'okuvuma. Era nate: Ne ndaba ensolo endala eva mu nsi, n'erina amayembe abiri agafaanana ag'omwana gw'endiga, n'eyogera ng'ogusota. Era nate: ogusota ne gugiwa amaanyi gaagwo n'entebe yaagwo ey'obwakabaka, n'obuyinza bungi. Era bwe kityo bwe kuliba okuyimuka kw'abalambya abasatu abatali batukuvu, ensolo evudde mu nnyanja, ensolo evudde mu nsi era ye nnabbi ow'obulimba era Masiya ow'obulimba, n'ogusota ogubawa obuyinza obungi.

Tewali muntu omu akulira okulwanyisa Kristo, wadde ng'emyoyo gy'abalwanyisa Kristo gyayiting'ana mu nsi era ne girimbye abangi, wabula waliwo ensolo bbiri n'ogusota. Emu okufuga ennyanja, oba amawanga g'Abamawanga, n'endala okufuga ensi entukuvu, ey'omu kika kya Ddaani era nga muzimbi wa yeekaalu ow'ensolo. Ensolo evudde mu nnyanja ejja kufuga amawanga, oba abagoyimu ng'eyita mu mateeka ga Nowa, era amateeka ago gaateekebwawo dda, era gombi galwanyisa Kristo mu mbeera yaago. Omulwanyisa Kristo ye oyo awakanya nze, Kristo Yesu, era ng'asikira ekifo kya Kristo Yesu. Kubanga laba! Ekyama ky'obutali butuukirivu kikulidde ddala era Sitaani alindiridde okugenda kwammwe, ggwe Ekkanisa yange, mazima ddala Omugole wange. Ajja kufugira mu ddiini ey'omukago, ey'okutabikatabika n'alyeyita Mahdi waabwe. Omukago gw'amaddiini gujja kubagatta, wansi w'obulimba obugamba nti amakubo gonna gatuuka eri katonda omu. Okuggyako nze Yesu Kristo Masiya ow'amazima siriikikkirizibwa, era sirina mugabo gwonna mu bo era okuzikirizibwa kwabwe tekulwawo. Kale omukago gwabwe gw'amaddiini gujja kubaleetera entabaganya yaabwe ey'ensolo, akabonero gye kalikakkirizibwa ku mawanga okusobola okwetaba mu ntabaganya yaabwe. Temutwalanga kabonero, kubanga buli muntu alikattwala aligabana nnyanja y'omuliro n'ensolo ne nnabbi ow'obulimba. Era bonna abalittwala akabonero bajja kukyusibwa mu buzaale bwabwe emirembe gyonna. Temwasomako? Era gye walabira ekyuma ekitabuddwamu ebbumba ettereevu, bwe batyo bwe balyetabula n'ezzadde ly'abantu: naye tebaligattibwa wamu, nga n'ekyuma bwe kitagattibwa na bbumba. Abo abalittwala akabonero bajja kuba baddu b'entegeka eno empya ejja okuyimuka era bajja kuba baddu b'ebirowoozo eby'ekigambo ekimu ebinaava mu kutabika omubiri n'ebyuma bikali.

Ennyanja ekiikirira amawanga g'Abamawanga. Amateeka g'obulimba aga Nowa gagamba dda nti Obukristaayo businza bikalimagezi era galagira okutemako omutwe ng'ekibonerezo. Amateeka gano galiwo era gajja kukolebwako n'obukambwe. N'abo abalikoowoola erinnya lya Mukama okutuusa ku nkomerero balilokolebwa, kubanga kyawandiikibwa nti: Era Katonda alisiimuula amaziga gonna mu maaso gaabwe; so tewalibaawo kufa nate, newakubadde okunakuwala, newakubadde okukaaba, so tewalibaawo nate kulumwa: kubanga eby'olubereberye biweddewo. N'oli eyali atudde ku ntebe n'agamba nti, Laba, nkola byonna buba bipya. N'aŋŋamba nti, Wandiika: kubanga ebigambo bino bya mazima era bya kwesigwa. Era bwe kityo bwe kiriba ebiro byammwe eby'omu maaso, mmwe abatukuvu bange ab'omu kibonyoobonyo, kubanga nsaaliridde era ndirokola Yakobo na buli alikoowoola erinnya lyange ery'amaanyi mu lulimi lwonna lwe bamukoowoolamu.

Ensolo evudde mu nsi, era ye nnabbi ow'obulimba, ejja kufuga Isiraeri, era ejja kufuga Ensi yonna ng'esinziira mu nsi ensuubize, naye si bantu ba Katonda, si Isiraeri wa Katonda kubanga mmwe muli Isiraeri wa Katonda, mazima ddala abaana b'Oyo Asinga Obukulu abateekeddwako akabonero ka Katonda Asinga Obukulu, era akabonero ka Yakuwa tekayinza kumenyeka. Era ekibiina ekinene kijja kuva mu Kunakuwala kwa Yakobo ne kitwala ebifo byakyo ebituufu mu Isiraeri wa Katonda, kubanga Omuyudaaya n'Omumawanga balilokolebwa era baliba muntu omu omupya kubanga ndibatonda buto mu nze ku kuzuukira kw'abatuukirivu.

Baana bange, Ekkuŋŋaaniro lya Sitaani litegesetegefuga kwalyo era tebalina kusaasira, mmwe muli nsolo za magulu abiri gye bali, kubanga bagoberera Talimudi y'e Babulooni evuma Katonda, ekitabo ekiva eri Sitaani so si Yakuwa, kubanga Isiraeri yakyuka dda okuva ku Yakuwa bwe banjeemera nze, Yeshua Omunazaaleeti, era naye nnaatera okulokola abantu bange. Bano be omu omu ku ngano, abajjajja baabwe be nnagambira obweru nti: Mmwe muli ba kitammwe omulyolyomi, era okwegomba kwa kitammwe kwe mwagala okukola: oyo yali mutemu okuva ku lubereberye, n'atayimirira mu mazima, kubanga amazima tegali mu ye. Bw'ayogera eby'obulimba, aba ayogera ebibye ye: kubanga mulimba, era kitaawe w'obulimba. Mutegeere baana bange nti Ddaani mwana gwa mpologoma era Ddaani talina mukiise mu 144,000. Engano zino zirindiridde n'obunyiikivu omuzimbi wa yeekaalu yaabwe, era bamututte dda nga kabaka waabwe. Ensolo evudde mu nsi ye ereeta akabonero k'ensolo. Mazima ddala enkola ey'ensigo y'olutalo, kati egezeseddwa n'etuukirizibwa okugabibwa n'okukakibwa n'okufa kw'abo abagaana. Ensi ekiikirira ensi ensuubize.

Baana bange, mulekere awo okukkiriza obulimba bw'abo abatayogera ku lwange, kubanga saabatuma. Abannabbi bange ab'amazima boogera mu ddoboozi limu, era bangi, era tebeewakanya, wadde ebyawandiikibwa, kubanga nze Kigambo eyafuuka omubiri. Ensolo zino ebbiri embi teziweebwa maanyi okuva eri kitange w'ekkanisa omwononefu, wabula Omusota yennyini, kubanga Omusota ye mulyolyomi. Mutegeere nti Obukristaayo tebuliba na kyakukola kyonna kubanga amakanisa gonna ag'obulimba gasaliddwa omusango era gazikirizidde kubanga omusango gutandikira mu nnyumba ya Katonda, n'Ekkanisa yange ey'amazima, mazima ddala abange abayite, Abeesigwa n'abalonde bajja kutwalibwa mu ggulu okufuna empeera yaabwe n'okutandika obulamu bwabwe obutaggwaawo. Era kiweereddwa ogusota okufuga, era aliyeni balirabika, naye si baliyeni wabula bamalayika abagwa era Omusota ye mukulu waabwe. Abangi ku bano baali basibiddwa mu njegere okuva mu nnaku za Nowa naye kati bateereddwa okusalirwa omusango gwabwe ogw'enkomerero ogutunula.

Ensolo evudde mu nnyanja, mazima ddala oli erinnya lye bwe livvuunulibwa mu Lungereza okuva mu Lwebbulaniya: Okumemyuka kuva mu ggulu: yiga oyige. Kubanga tayagala bakazi, so tassa kitiibwa mu katonda yenna: kubanga alyegulumiza okusinga byonna. Ensolo ey'ogusota okuva mu nnyanja, n'ensolo okuva mu nsi bijja kuyimuka okuva mu vvu ng'abazira olw'okuzikiriza Babulooni ow'Ekyama, malaaya omukulu, era bonna bakolera wamu. Bakyayidde ddala Amerika n'ekintu kyonna ekigenderako erinnya lya Yesu Kristo, naye balifukamira amaviivi mu ngeri y'emu mu musango gwabwe, mwe batagenda kuddayo kuwona. Noolwekyo, ebyawandiikibwa tebiyinza kutegeerekeka mu ntegeka y'amawanga n'ensi yennyini eya kaakano. Kubanga Yakuwa yeeteekateeka okuleeta okukankana okunene ku nsi mu musango era ensalo zijja kukubibwa buto. Luno lwe lutalo lwabwe olw'enkomerero okuzikiriza entegeka enkadde, n'okuleeta entegeka ya Sitaani ejja okufuga obuntu ng'eyita mu nsolo evudde mu nnyanja n'ensolo evudde mu nsi era ye nnabbi ow'obulimba. Amayembe kkumi, ge bakabaka kkumi abaliweebwa obuyinza okufuga okumala essaawa emu, kubanga bwe kityo bwe kyagabibwa, baligabira amaanyi gaabwe n'okukkiriza kwabwe eri abasatu bano ab'ensolo.

Laba! Nja kubawa entegeka ey'ebintu eby'omuggundu mulyoke mumanye ebiseera mwe muli. Ebimu ku bino bibaawo nga bigobereragana, ate ebimu bibaawo mu kiseera kye kimu, era biri bwe biti!

  • Babulooni ow'Ekyama azikirizibwa ng'olutalo lw'ensi yonna lubaluseewo ku nsi, era ne kaakano bategeka amagye gaabwe era bateekateeka okulumba Babulooni ow'Ekyama mu ngeri ennungi ennyo. Temukkirizanga bakulembeze bammwe, wadde amawulire, kubanga katuti ka balimba abali mu kitundu ky'abalumba.
  • Ensolo evudde mu nnyanja, n'ensolo evudde mu nsi, n'ogusota biriyimusibwa mu vvu ly'olutalo.
  • Ensolo zino ebbiri embi ziryeyimirira okuwakanya abajulirwa 2 abaliragula okumala emyezi 42, mu kiseera ekyo lwe balittibwa ne balinnya mu ggulu mu nnaku ssatu n'ekitundu era bwe kityo okuzuukira kwabwe bwe kuliba.
  • Nga yeekaalu ezimbibwa, olutalo ku batukuvu lugenda mu maaso emyezi 42 era akabonero k'ensolo ne kateekebwawo, ne kakolebwako, ne kakakkirizibwa wansi w'obubalagaze bw'okufa. Oluvannyuma enkyukakyuka, ng'omuzizo ogw'okuzikiriza gukoleddwa era ensolo evudde mu nsi erinoonya okutta bonna abajja gye ndi, ye Yeshua HaMashiach mu kiseera kino.
  • Ensolo evudde mu nsi ne yeerangirira nga Katonda era ne ekyukira abantu okubatta, kubanga eryazaamaanya abantu mu ngeri y'emu nga Antiyokasi Epifane.
  • Ne ntunula, ne laba, Omwana gw'endiga ng'ayimiridde ku lusozi Sayuuni, wamu naye kasiriivu mu emitwalo kkumi n'ena mu enkumi nnya, nga balina Erinnya lya Kitaawe nga liwandiikiddwa ku byenyi byabwe. (Balina omukisa abafu abafiira mu Mukama okuva kaakano, weewaawo, Omwoyo bw'ayogera, balyoke bawummule emirimu gyabwe, n'ebikolwa byabwe bibagoberera.)
  • Abange 144,000 balisenga abantu bange ne babatwala mu bifo eby'obukuumi okugumira omuyaga ng'ensi erongoosebwa okuva mu butali butuukirivu bwonna, mazima ddala engano embi. Kubanga singa saafunza nnaku ezo tewali muntu yandiwonye, era bwe ntyo ndisenga abangi mu ddungu. Kino kiribaawo mu kitundu olwo ekisigalira ky'abantu kiwonyezebwe.
  • Abange 144,000 bajja ku nsi nga bambadde ebyambalo bye ndibakolera nze kennyini, era tebirimenyeka era biriba bya maanyi era bya buyinza era tewaliba kintu kyonna kya mulabe kirifuna buyinza, era bwe kityo ebigendererwa bya Yakuwa birituukirira.

Nze Yeshua HaMashiach era ntumye ebigambo bino ng'abiyisa mu muweereza wange, era ng'abiyisa mu muweereza wange ebigambo byange bitumiddwa.

Mutunule mu ggulu, mmwe Ekkanisa yange abeesigwa kubanga nsembera era ndibatwala mu nnyumba ya Kitange gye mbategekedde ekifo era mufugire wamu nange nga Bakabaka n'Abakabona, era Obwakabaka bwange tebulina nkomerero, kale mwebuumeeko amaanyi n'Ebigambo bino.
