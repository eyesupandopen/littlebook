Jjuulayi 23, 2024

Okukaaba kw’Abalina Obutaba na Musango!

Mutandike okukungubaga, mukaabe era muwowoggane olw’okuttibwa kw’abalina obutaba na musango. Kubanga tebalina kibi kye baakola, tebaalaba ku bulamu, wadde okukola ekibi kyonna, kubanga okuva kwabwe okw’amangu kwali kuva mu mbuto za bannyina ez’obukuumi, era nga tebannafuna mukisa gwa kussa mukka gwabwe ogusooka, ne baba nga tebaliiwo!

Mutandike okukungubaga, mukaabe era muwowoggane olw’obulamu obw’abalina obutaba na musango obwasaanawo n’omusaayi gwabwe ogwayiika, ogukaabira mu ggulu nga gusaba obwenkanya. Laba! Ensi eyeesuulira eruuyi n’eruuyi era enyeenyezebwa ng’obujjaazi bweyongera, kubanga obuzito bw'okuttibwa kw'abalina obutaba na musango bunene nnyo!

Era mutandike okukungubaga n’olw’abaana ba Laakeeri kubanga bw’ati bw’ayogera Mukama; Eddoboozi lyawulirwa e Laama, nga lya kukungubaga na kukaaba okw’omunaku: Laakeeri ng’akaabira abaana be, n’agaana okubudaabudibwa olw’abaana be, kubanga tebaaliwo. Newakubadde mulowooza nti kino kintu kitono, mutegeere nti ebibi bino tebisaanidde kunyoomwa era obuzito bw’ekibonerezo kyabyo bunene nnyo ddala era n’omusango ddala guli ku abo bonna abakoze ebibi bino era ne bagaana ekyapa kyange eky’emirembe!

Muwulire ebigambo byange, mmwe abantu bange, era mutege emitima gyammwe ku bigambo byange, muwulire amagezi gange kubanga omusango si gwammwe. Mutegeere ennaku enzito n’amaziga agakaawa ag’omulokozi wammwe, Omulokozi wammwe, ye Yesu Omunazaaleeti! Kubanga amaziga mangi nnyo, ge nkaabye enfunda n’enfunda olw’okuttibwa kw’abalina obutaba na musango. Mufulume mu bo mmwe abantu bange era munzikirize nsaanyeewo ddala emirimu gya Sitaani mu bulamu bwammwe mu nsonga eno, era ne mu nsonga endala zonna abaana bange. Kubanga omwoyo gwange tegukyajja kubanenya lwa kibi, kubanga mwafuulibwa obutuukirivu bwa Katonda mu nze, kubanga mwakkiriza ekirabo kyange eky’obulamu obutaggwaawo n’ekirabo kyange eky’obutuukirivu, mwe mwafuulirwa abatuukirivu, ye obutuukirivu bwa Katonda mu nze olw’omusaayi gwange ogutangirira n’olw’omwoyo gwa Katonda omulamu, ye omwoyo gw’obwana mwe mwateekerwa akabonero olunaku lw’okununulibwa, era muli bitonde bipya mu nze. Kale temutya!

Mwatondebwa mu butuukirivu n’obulongoofu obw’amazima era omwoyo gwange oguli mu mmwe bwe gubanenya, nze Kristo Yesu abanenya olw’obutuukirivu. Weewaawo, mbanenya lwa butuukirivu mulyoke mubeerewo mu butuukirivu kubanga muli butuukirivu bwa Katonda era muteekwa okweyongera okutta omubiri, ne mutambulira mu mwoyo gwange kubanga n’olwekyo kaakano tewali kusinza abo abali mu Kristo Yesu, abatambulira mu mubiri, wabula mu mwoyo! Kale mutegeere nti omusango si gwammwe, kubanga buli mmeeme Yakuwa gye yasonyiwa mu nze Kristo Yesu tegenda kujja mu musango lwa bibi wabula ejja kuva mu kufa okudda mu bulamu obutaggwaawo kubanga mwakkiriza Yesu Omunazaaleeti, ye endagaano ya Yakuwa ey’emirembe eri abantu bonna era emirembe gyonna eri abo bonna abakkiriza ssaddaaka yange!

Mujje gye ndi mwature n’ebibi bye mwekalabiza era mudduukirire obutuukirivu muleme okwonoona. Mujje gye ndi mwature ebyonoono byammwe, nsobole okubanaazaako ebbala lyonna mu musaayi gwange ogutangirira, n’emirimu gya Sitaani gisangulibwe ddala emirembe gyonna mu bulamu bwammwe. Lwaki munywerera ku bibi byammwe n’obukakanyavu? Lwaki munoonya ebirowoozo okufuna okuwummula? Muyinza okwekweka Katonda omulamu bwe muziba amaaso gammwe? Munywerera ku lugero lwa maaya ne mugezaako okugoberera olugero luno? Saabakoowoola kubeerawo mu lugero lwa maaya wadde okuziika emitwe gyammwe mu musenyu wadde okubikka ebibi byammwe mu ssanyu n’okwerabira. Kubanga maaya ateeka omutwe gwe mu ttaka okulabirira amagi g’abaana baayo abatannazaalibwa, so si kwekweka mazima. Naye nnabayita okwolekagana n’ebibi by’emabega yammwe ne mubyatura gye ndi nsobole okubawonya n’okusaanyaawo emirimu gya Sitaani mu bulamu bwammwe kubanga anoonya okubasiba ku bibi bye era emirembe gyonna abalumirize olw’obusiru!

Kyemunaava mweyongera okwatulanga ebibi byammwe n’ebyayonoono byammwe mwekka na mwekka era mutegeere nti bwe mukola mutyo abalabe bammwe bakankana kubanga balaba obujulizi obutagaanyiika obw’okulokolebwa kwammwe okweyongerayo n’obujulizi obutagaanyiika obw’okuzikirizibwa kwabwe okusembedde n’omusango ogw’emirembe mu nnyanja ennene eyo ennabirabira omuliro omulongoofu ennyo ogw’ebbulu gye gujja okwakanga emirembe n’emirembe era n’ekiwuka kyabwe tekifa! Kale mufulume mu bo mwawukane mutegeere nti mulina okusonyiyibwa ebibi. Bwe mweyongera mu nkolagana yammwe ey’obulokozi nange, Kristo Yesu, nja kubayonja ddala ebibi byammwe byonna nsobole okubeefuulira Ekkanisa ey’ekitiibwa eterina bbala wadde enkunyinyi wadde ekintu kyonna ekifaanana bwe kityo, naye mubeere batukuvu era abataliiko kya kunenyezebwa mu maaso gange mu kwagala. Era mulyoke mwambazibwe bafuta kubanga bwe gityo bwe guli omulembe gwammwe ogusembedde. Kale mutegeere nti mu nze, Kristo Yesu, temuli kusinza wadde omusango gye muli abaana bange n’ebigambo byange ebikakali si byammwe naye mbateesa muyige ku nze mutegeere obuzito bw’ekyo kye mbawonyezza kubanga mwanunulibwa okuva mu musango.

Era mumanye nti buli kikolwa kiwandiikibwa mu ggulu eri buli kitonde wansi w’eggulu ekiri wansi w’omusango omunene. Era ndabye ne mpandiika ebigambo n’ebikolwa bya bannabbi ab’obulimba, n’abo abeefuula abanjogerera ne bakwatizaako okuttibwa kw’abalina obutaba na musango olw’amakubo gaabwe ag’okwonona. Kubanga bano be nzizi ezitalina mazzi, n’abo abobwenzi bwabwe bwe bwavirako okuttibwa kw’abalina obutaba na musango. Abo, mu kifo ky’okwenenya obwenzi bwabwe n’obwenzi bwabwe baatta abaana baabwe abatannazaalibwa balyoke beeyongere okulya ku kisibo kyange mu kifo ky’okuliisa ekisibo kyange, era bajja kwolekagana n’obusungu obungi olw’ebikolwa byabwe eby’obumenyi bw’amateeka kubanga tekiyinzika emisege egy’ekyejo okwekweka mu katuti okuva mu kutunuulira kwange okw’emirembe, era bajja kwetikka omusango gwabwe bonna abataneenenya bikolwa bino!

Era n’olwekyo njogera naawe omuntu ow’obutonde, era njogera naawe Babulooni ow’Ekyama. Kubanga olina omusango gw’omusaayi gw’abalina obutaba na musango, n’abo be waggye mu mbuto za bannyina ez’obukuumi. Ozingizizza okukuba omwana ali mu lubuto mu kwewolereza okw’obulimba n’okunoonya ensonga ebigufuula ekikemo kyo eky’okwegulumiza eky’okutta abalina obutaba na musango okuba eky’olutentezi ng’okugen da kwa musawo, n’amalwaliro go ag’obutemu gali kivve mu nnyindo za Kitange, era kivve mu nnyindo zange. Kubanga omusaayi gw’abalina obutaba na musango gukaabira okuva mu nsi okutuuka ddala ku ntebe ya Yakuwa ey’obwakabaka mu ggulu ly’eggulu alaba byonna, n’abo bonna abanoonya okwekweka okuva mu kutunuulira kwe okutaggwaawo. Era nze Yeshua HaMashiach ntumiddwa Kitange mu musango, era nze Mpologoma ey’Ekika kya Yuda, kubanga mwagaanyi Omwana gw’endiga gwa Katonda era kaakano omusango gwammwe gujja. Era nja kulongoosa ddala ensi okuva mu makubo gammwe ag’obutemu era mujja kulya ku kazambi k’amakubo gammwe amabi, kubanga Yakuwa alangiridde omugabo gw’omusango omulundi ebiri ku ggwe, ggwe Babulooni ow’Ekyama.

Kubanga mwatta abalina obutaba na musango, nga mulowooza mu butamanya nti ekikolwa kino eky’omuzizo kintu kitono. Mwatunda abaana bammwe abatannazaalibwa olw’emiganyulo emicaafu mulyoke mutereeze emirimu gyammwe, n’emirimu gyammwe egy’obwereereere ddala kubanga gyonna gijja kuggwaawo era n’ebikolwa byammwe eby’ensi eby’omuwendo omungi eby’obwereereere byonna bijja kubula mu musango ne bitajjukirwa nate. Kubanga mwatta kyeyagalire abaana bammwe abatannazaalibwa mu ssaddaaka etamanyiddwa eri Moloki, era mutta abaana bammwe abatannazaalibwa n’amalala amangi. Era bwe kityo bwe kiriba, kubanga amalala gakulembera okugwa n'omwoyo ogwegulumiza gukulembera okuzikirira era omusango gwammwe n'okuzikirizibwa kwammwe tekulwa wabula kuli ku mmwe era kutandise dda era n'obulumi obw'okuzaala tebukoma kaakano.

Naye mu kifo ky’okwenenya, mu kifo ky’okujja gye ndi, Yesu Omunazaaleeti mu bwetoowaze bwe nnabawa obulamu obutaggwaawo n’okusoniyibwa ebibi n’ebiva mu musango ogugendana nakyo mwalonzeewo ebibi byammwe n’ekibonerezo kyammwe eky’emirembe. Mweyawaddeyo era ne mulondawo mu kifo kyange Yesu Omunazaaleeti, Balaba, Moshiach wammwe ow’obulimba. Mu kifo kya Yakuwa Katonda omunene ow’emirembe, mulonzeewo ogusota okuba katonda wammwe era kaakano nja kubawaayo gye bali kubanga mwegasse ku makubo gaabwe mu malala gammwe n’amalala gammwe era kaakano mujja kulya ku kazambi k’amakubo gammwe.

Muwaddeyo omusaayi gw’abalina obutaba na musango ku byoto by’okwemalira, ne muyiwa abaana bammwe abakubiddwa ng’ebiwero ebitaba birongoofu ebisuuliddwa mu bintu by’ekisawo eby’obucaafu, era mubaze omwoyo gw’obulamu oguli mu musaayi ekintu ekitatukuvu n’omusango gwammwe guli ku mmwe. Naye abammwe bennyini, n’abaana bammwe abatannazaalibwa, baali tebagendana na misono gyammwe egiggwaawo, era obulamu bwabwe bwali buzibu gye muli okwebikka ddala mu ssanyu ly’ebibi.

Mwenenya era musaba omusaayi gw’abalina obutaba na musango mu ndowooza zammwe ez’eby’obufuzi ez’obutemu, era mwesibye ku abo abagwa mu bujeemu bwabwe, kubanga ba Sitaani be baayigiriza okukuba omwana mu lubuto, n’enkola zammwe ezo ez’obutemu ez’omuwendo mwe musanyukira ennyo okukuuma n’okweraga. Era n’olwekyo omusaayi gw’abalina obutaba na musango gukaabira okuva mu nsi n’obuzito bw’ebibi byammwe buleetedde ensi okunyeenya ng’omutamiivu, era n’okunyeenya kujja kuba kunene n’okugwa kujja kuba kunene! Babulooni Omunene agudde, agudde!

Ggwe omuntu ow’obutonde, ggwe Babulooni ow’Ekyama, nga wagudde okuva mu kisa n’okusaasira kwe nnabawa mu ngeri etagwanidde. Era newakubadde ekisa kyange n’okusaasira kwange si bya bwereere, olw’ebigendererwa bya Yakuwa, n’ebigendererwa bye ebyakwekebwa mu kyama ekinene ekya Katonda bituukiririziddwa mu mawanga g’Abamawanga n’ebiseera byabwe biweddeko. Era nze oyo ajja kaakano okukyuka okulokola Yakobo okuva mu kuzikirizibwa, kubanga bajja kunkyukira okuba Masiya waabwe, n’abo bonna Yakuwa yennyini be yayita okuva emirembe n’emirembe okutambulira mu maaso ge ng’omuntu omu omuggya mu nze Kristo Yesu, n’eggwanga erimu eriggya era tewalibaawo nate Isirayiri wa Katonda okwawulibwamu era tewalibaawo nnyanja. Naye okusooka ensi ziteekwa okulongoosebwa n’obwakabaka bwa Sitaani bujja kuzikirizibwa ddala, ne butaddamu kugoloka. Ggwe omusiru, omubi era omwononefu omuntu ow’obutonde, n’oyo atalina mwoyo gwange n’oyo eyagaana obulamu obutaggwaawo mu nze Kristo Yesu.

Mutegeere nti abaana busika bwa Mukama, era kyandibadde kirungi omuntu obutazaalibwa okusinga okulumya abo abatalina musango nga tebannassa mukka gwabwe ogusooka; okulumya n’omu ku bato bange kibi nnyo era kireeta ekibonerezo eky’emirembe. Naye newankubadde mulina okweyongerayo. Newankubadde nnakubiriza abangi okuyimirira ebweru w’amalwaliro gammwe okugezaako okubaziyiza okusiba emikisa gyammwe. Naye temwandibadde na kimu ku byo, era mwagaanyi okulabula kwange era kaakano mujja kubonerezebwa omusango gwammwe.

Mwabula ne muyingira mu makubo ga Baali era mufuuse nga Sitaani bw’abakulembedde, kubanga musukkiridde mu kunyoomoola mu makubo gammwe ag’obutayo. Kola ky’oyagala lye tteeka lyabwe lyonna lye mwefuulidde eryammwe. Mwabuzaabuziddwa ne muyingira mu makubo g’Omukanani, kubanga nabo baawaayo abaana baabwe ssaddaaka, n’amalala amangi n’essanyu lingi, nga nammwe bwe mweyongera okukola! Naye mu malala gammwe mulowooza nti temuli nga abo abasiru n’abatayiga ab’edda n’amakubo gammwe gasinza ku b’edda bano, naye mukola ebintu bye bimu. Naye mulowooza mu busiru nti muli ba njawulo, era munoonya okukozesa obutamanya obw’okwefuula ng’ekyambalo, era munoonya okuggya ensonga n’okukweka ebibi byammwe mu misomo gyammwe egy’obulimba egibatuusizza ku musango. Kubanga obuzito bw’omusaayi gw’abataliiko musango bukaabira mu ggulu na kati era busaba obwenkanya, era Yakuwa awulidde okukaaba kwabwe!

Muwaddeyo abaana bammwe abatannazaalibwa mu butamanya okweyongerayo mu nkola y’ekinyama, n’abo abagwa bakkirizza ssaddaaka zammwe. Bakozesezza omusaayi gw’abalina obutaba na musango nga bwe bakola bulijjo mu kukola emizizo gyabwe, era bwe kityo bwe kiriba mu nnaku za Nuuwa. Kubanga omuziyiza bw’aliggibwawo, olwo mulitegeera obunene n’omuwendo gw’emizizo gyabwe, kubanga bajja kusumululwa nga tebalina kuziiziza.

Muwaddeyo omusaayi gw’abalina obutaba na musango n’emizizo gye bakoze gijja kubayigga era gibagoberere okutuuka ku nkomerero y’ensi kubanga balidde bulijjo abantu abali mu nsi n’omusiri omubi ogwakoleddwa emikono gy’abo abagwa tebalina kusonyiyibwa bibi byabwe n’ebikolwa byabwe eby’obumenyi bw’amateeka eri Yakuwa, kubanga omuntu yakolebwa mu kifaananyi kya Yakuwa era okwonoona obulamu bw’omubiri obuli mu musaayi kibi kinene era tekijja kusooka kulabibwa ku abo abagaanyi obulokozi bwange mu biseera byange eby’ekisa n’okusaasira. Laba! Nsindika ebikojjo okutuukiriza obusungu bwange!

Kaakano njakwogera n’abo abagwa, era bawulira era bakankana! Amakubo gammwe gavuddeko okuzikirira kwammwe okw’emirembe n’emirembe kubanga okwammwe kwe kuzikirira okw’emirembe n’emirembe mwe mujja okuddizibwa buli kibi emirundi mingi. Buli bulamu bwe mwaggyawo mu makubo gammwe amabi agajjudde omululu n’okwegomba kujja kubasasulwa emirundi kikumi. Kubanga mwakozesa omusaayi gw’abalina obutaba na musango mu mikolo gyammwe, n’ebikolwa, n’emikolo n’emizizo mingi gye mwakola nga muzannya katonda! Naye temuli bakatonda era temuyinza kweyawula mu mukono gwange, kubanga nze Kristo Yesu nze nsigo eyasuubizibwa ey’omukazi ajja okubetenta ddala omutwe gwammwe!

Kaakano gujja omusango gwammwe n’okuzikirizibwa okw’amangu. Kubanga temwayiye musaayi gwa muntu atalina musango gwokka, nze Yesu Omunazaaleeti, naye mulina omusango gw’omusaayi gw’abalina obutaba na musango okuva ku kusooka okutuuka ku kusembayo kubanga mmwe mwayigiriza okukuba omwana alyoke aggweewo. Amakubo gammwe ag’emizizo n’ebikolwa byammwe bijja kusasulwa ddala gye muli emirembe gyonna era temulidduka wadde okufuna akaseera ak’okuwummula okuva mu kibonerezo kyammwe eky’emirembe. Kubanga mmwe muvunaanyizibwa ku kufa n'okuzikirira kw'abalina obutaba na musango era bonna abagoberedde amakubo gammwe agazikiriza mu bukyayi obutenenya bajja kugabana ku busika bwammwe obw'omuliro ogutazikira nga musasula buli kikolwa eky'omuzizo kye mwakola ku kitonde kya Yakuwa. Kubanga ebintu bye bimu bye mwakolera abalala bijja kuweebwa mu bifuba byammwe emirundi mingi n’emirembe n’emirembe era temulidduka.

Kubanga mwagezaako okumbba obusika bwange, okuggyako nti mwagaana kubanga mwagezaako ekitasoboka: Mwagezaako okutta omusaayi gw’omuntu atalina musango, nze Yesu Omunazaaleeti, kubanga tekiyinzika kufa kunkuuma. Kubanga nze nnawaayo obulamu bwange kyeyagalire so tewali n'omu eyabuggyawo, naye nze nnateeka obulamu bwange wansi kyeyagalire ng’Okuyitako okutuukiridde okw’ebiseera byonna. Era nze, ye Kristo Yesu, nja kusaanyaawo emirimu gyammwe gyonna emibi mu bulamu bw’abo be mwalumya, n’abo abajja gye ndi nga benenyezza amakubo gammwe amabi n’abo abajja okukoowoola erinnya lyange okutuusa ku nkomerero, kubanga ekibiina KINENE kijja kulokolebwa. N’emmeeme yonna Yakuwa gye yalagira okufuna obulamu obutaggwaawo okuva ku ntandikwa y’ensi era temulikutulamu n’akatono ku bino okuva mu mikono gyange egy’emirembe, ggwe ogusota ogwagaana. Kubanga abalina obutaba na musango be mwatta bajja kuzuukira nga balamu mu bupya obw’obulamu obutaggwaawo n’essanyu ery’emirembe mu maaso ga Yakuwa era emirembe gyonna bajja kubeerawo mu ssanyu eritaggwaawo era bajja kunyumirwa amasavu g’ensi! Kubanga Yakuwa yasuubiza ki?

Muwulire! Bw'ati bw'ayogera Mukama, Eddoboozi lyawulirwa waggulu, ery'okukungubaga n'okukaaba okw'omunaku. Laakeeri ng'akaabira abaana be, n'agaana okubudaabudibwa olw'abaana be, kubanga tebaaliwo. Bw'ati bw'ayogera Mukama, Ziyiza eddoboozi lyo obutakaaba, n'amaaso go obutavaamu maziga: kubanga omulimu gwo guliba n'empeera, bw'ayogera Mukama, era balidda okuva mu nsi y'omulabe: Era waliwo essuubi mu nkomerero yo, bw'ayogera Mukama, nti abaana bo balidda mu nsalo zaabwe.

Laba! Nze, ye Yeshua HaMashiach, nja kuzuukiza abalina obutaba na musango bonna ababuze era nja kuwoolera ddala eggwanga ku musaayi gwabwe ku balina omusango! Nja kubazuukiza okufuna obulamu obutaggwaawo n’obulamu bw’abatannazaalibwa bujja kuzuukira mu bulamu obutaggwaawo n’essanyu ery’emirembe era nja kubagatta ku banunule, n’abo be nnalaga okusaasira abajja gye ndi nga benenyezza era nja kubazzaawo ekyo kye mwaggyawo, mmwe abagwa. Era okwabwe kujja kuba ssanyu litaggwaawo era nja kuzzaawo abalina obutaba na musango abattibwa eri amaka gaabwe era Yakuwa yennyini ajja kuba Kitabwe era bajja kutwala ebifo byabwe mu bika ebitaggwaawo era bajja kuwoolerezebwa eggwanga ne ku mitwe gyammwe egy’obwereere!

Laba! Bajja kufuulibwa ab’amaanyi, n’ab’ekitiibwa, era abalina obutaba na musango balina ekifo eky’enjawulo mu mutima gwa Yakuwa n’ebigendererwa bye bijja kutuukirizibwa mu bulamu bwabwe obw’emirembe era emirembe gyonna bajja kumasamasa mu maaso ga Kitabwe ne Katonda waabwe era bajja kufuulibwa ab’ekitiibwa emirembe gyonna mu nze Kristo Yesu ajja okubazuukiza n’okuzzaawo ddala obulamu bwabwe obwabbibwa nga tebannafuna na mukisa gwa kussa mukka.

Kale budaabudibwa Laakeeri, kubanga abaana bo bajja kuzizibwawo era bawummule wansi w’omukono gwange ogw’amaanyi ogw’okubudaabuda ng’omanyi nti abato bo bajja kuba balamu emirembe gyonna so tebalizikirira, kubanga nze Yeshua HaMashiach bwe ntyo bwe nnangiridde so tewali kiyinza kukyusa kulangirira kwange. Era Yakuwa Katonda omunene ow’emirembe agolokoka mu busungu n’omusango era n’omusango kaakano gujja kugwa!

Lwaki munoonya okuddayo okwebaka mmwe abayite n’abalonde bange? Kye nnasuubiza nja kukituukiriza kubanga njija mu maanyi n’ekitiibwa kya Yakuwa Katonda omunene ow’emirembe era ndi omu mu bumu n’ekigendererwa ne Kitange, ye El Shaddai Katonda omunene ow’emirembe kubanga tewali amufaanana! Mutegeere nti okulabika kwange kusembedde era kaakano kuli ku luggi lwammwe! Obudde buweddewo Babulooni ow’Ekyama okwenenya era omusango gwe gutandise dda. Amangu ago omuziyiza bw’aliggibwawo n’abantu bange ne basituka ne bakyusibwa okunsisinkana mu bbanga, okuzikirizibwa okw’amangu kujja kugwa ku abatali batukuvu era tebaliwona era n’omusaayi gw’abalina obutaba na musango ogw’ebiseera byonna gujja kuwoolerezebwa ddala emirembe n’emirembe ku lunaku lw’omusango omunene, era n’olwekyo kaakano omusango omunene gujja kugwa.
