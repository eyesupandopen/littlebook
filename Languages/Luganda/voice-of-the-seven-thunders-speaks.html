Eri Abagwa: Kati Okusalirwa Kwammwe Omusango Kutuuse!Okubwatuka 1 & 2 (Ssebo Aseka 6, 2024)

1Okuva eri Yeshua HaMashiach: Okubwatuka Okusooka!

Mmwe abayite bange, abalonde, era abeesigwa, muwulire Ebigambo bya Mukama wammwe omulamu era omulokozi, ye Yeshua Omuyisiraamu! Amawanga mwe mubeera n'ebyafaayo bye mwayigirizibwa abayigiriza bammwe Abayesuwiiti bye bintu ebikola olukalala lw'obulimba obunene, okubikkulirwa kwakwo okujjuvu okuli okumpi era okuliwo, omugwa abagwa mwe balirabika ng'abagenyi ab'ekisa naye mu mazima bali bamalayika abagwa ababi. Kiki ekyabangawo? Kye kiribeerawo: era kiki ekyakolebwa? Kye kirikolebwa: so tewali kigambo kipya wansi w'enjuba.

Sitaani yawaddeyo okumanya kwe okuyitibwa okwa waggulu okuva ku lubereberye, era n'okumanya kwe okw'obulungi n'obubi. Ye mukyusakyusa omukugu era omulimba eyayigiriza abantu ebintu bingi Yahweh bye yali tataddeewo, kubanga zino ze nkola ez'okufa. Ekitonde kya Yahweh tekyakolebwa kunyigirizibwa lwa maanyi ga muntu na magoba oba ng'engeri ey'okwewala okusalirwa omusango kwa Katonda, wadde okuteekawo amakubo n'entegeka ye. Kubanga Sitaani yayonoona abantu okuva ku lubereberye, era yayonoona abakuumi abagwa abeegatta ku bujeemu bwabwe.

Ayi ggwe omulabe omunene ow'obutuukirivu bwonna, era mmwe ba Sitaani: ebibi byammwe ebyasooka byawandiikibwa Enoka omuwandiisi emyaka gyonna gino egiyise, era biyimiridde na leero, kubanga ebibi byammwe byawandiikibwa bwe bityo. Era bwe ntyo nja kubifunza, kubanga mwakola bino, era mwakola bingi nnyo: Mwakyusa batabani ba Katonda bonna, ne mubassa wansi ku nsi, ne mubakyusa okuyita mu bawala b'abantu. Mwagaba eri batabani ba Katonda abatukuvu amagezi amabi, ne mubakyusa ne babafula abantu aboonoona emibiri gyabwe n'abawala b'abantu. Mwalaga abaana b'abantu ebikonde byonna eby'okufa, ne mukyusa Kaawa, ne mulaga ebyokulwanyisa eby'okufa eri batabani b'abantu: engabo n'ekyambalo eky'ebyuma, n'ekitala eky'olutalo, n'ebyokulwanyisa byonna eby'okufa eri abaana b'abantu. Era okuva mu mukono gwammwe byagenda ku abo ababeera ku nsi okuva ku olwo n'emirembe gyonna.

Mmwe ba Sitaani mwayigiriza abaana b'abantu ebikaawa n'ebinyuma, era ne mubayigiriza ebyama byonna eby'amagezi gaabwe. Mwalagira abantu okuwandiika nga bakozesa bwino n'empapula, era bwe kityo bangi baayonoona okuva emirembe n'emirembe okutuusa leero. Kubanga abantu tebaatondebwa lwa kigendererwa ekyo, okukakasa okukkiriza kwabwe okulungi n'ekkalaamu ne bwino. Kubanga abantu baatondebwa ddala nga bamalayika, n'ekigendererwa nti basigale balongoofu era batuukirivu; n'okufa, okuzikiriza byonna, tekwandibakutte, naye okuyita mu kumanya kuno bazikirira, era okuyita mu maanyi gano kukosa abantu.

Mmwe ba Sitaani mwalaga abaana b'abantu okukuba kwonna okubi okw'emyoyo n'abadayimooni, n'okukuba kw'omwana ali mu lubuto, asobole okuggyibwamu, n'okukuba kw'omwoyo, okulumwa kw'omusota, n'okukuba okujja mu bbugumu ery'omu ttuntu, omwana w'omusota ayitibwa Tabââ'ĕt. Kubanga mwasaba ne Mikayiri okubalaga erinnya erikwekeddwa, mulyogere mu kirayiro, abo basobole okukankana mu maaso g'erinnya eryo n'ekirayiro abo abaabikkula byonna ebyali mu kyama eri abaana b'abantu.

Kubanga mwaleetera abakuumi okwegatta ku bujeemu bwammwe bwe mweyongera okwonoona abantu. Era n'ebibi by'abakuumi ne biwandiikibwa Enoka omuwandiisi, era nja kubifunza bwe nti: Baayigiriza abantu okukola ebitala, n'ebiso, n'engabo, n'eby'omu kifuba, ne babategeeza ebyuma eby'oku nsi n'omulimu gw'okubikolako, n'ebikomo, n'eby'okwewunda, n'okukozesa antimon, n'okulongoosa ebikowe, n'amayinja ag'omuwendo ag'engeri zonna, n'amabala gonna ag'okusiiga. Baayigiriza eddogo, n'okusala emilandira, n'okusaasaanya eddogo, n'okulaguzisa emmunyeenye, n'ebiriporipooze, n'okumanya ebire, n'obubonero bw'ensi, n'obubonero bw'enjuba, n'entambula y'omwezi.

Era bwe kityo okuyita mu kwonoona kwammwe n'obulimba bwammwe, mujjuzizza ekitonde kya Yahweh emize gyammwe emibi n'emizizo gyammwe egivvoola. Mukyusizza abantu ne muyonoona Yahweh, era mukola abantu bibi, era mwayonoona ne binyonyi, n'ensolo, n'ebyewalula, n'ebyennyanja, era ebibi byammwe bireeta okusalirwa omusango omunene kwe mutajja kuwonera. Mujjuzizza ekitonde kya Yahweh tekinologiya wammwe n'okukikyusa, era mwaleetera abantu okwewuunya amagezi gammwe n'obulimba. Kubanga Yahweh yabagaanuulira bya maama byereere eby'ebitonde bye n'abakweka eby'omuwendo ennyo ebyali mu mutima gwe abyesonyiwe okubaliga kwammwe.

Mmwe ababi mwayigiriza amakubo gammwe agatalina mugaso eri abantu bwe baayala ku nsi, era ne muleeta okubonaabona okungi n'okuzikirira okuyita mu nkola zammwe embi ne tekinologiya wammwe. Kubanga temujja okuggyako okubba, n'okutta, n'okuzikiriza. Mwayigiriza okukyusa empewo, kubanga mmwe muli munnabyangwe ow'obuyinza bw'empewo, kubanga mukyusa aithēr. Kubanga Kayini bwe yayonoona, n'azimba ekibuga; era muyigirizza abantu ebyama byammwe ebitagasa n'okubikyusa. Ebibuga bino byakozesebwanga okufuga n'okukyusa n'okuleeta okuzikirizibwa kw'abantu, bwe mwabikozesa okukola emizizo gyammwe egy'okuzikiriza.

Muleetedde eggwanga okugolokokerera eggwanga mu ntalo n'okuyomba okutakoma, era mwaleetera abantu bange okwonoona, era Yahweh asaasaanyizza Ebika bya Isirayiri mu mpewo ennya. Tekijja nsonga, ekigendererwa kyammwe ekibi kyongedde ku bigendererwa bya Yahweh, kubanga okusaasaana kwabwe kwe kulituukiririza okukuŋŋaanyizibwa kw'endiga ezisigaddewo. Era nja kutuma abajulirwa bange ababiri okulagulira ennaku 1260 nga bambadde ebibukutu. Mu kutebenkera kwammwe kwonna okuzikiriza abaana ba Yahweh mwagaanye nnyo, era temwasobola kulaba nti okutebenkera kwammwe kwayongera ku bigendererwa bya Yahweh, kubanga n'abalabe be bawulira eddoboozi lye.

Kubanga mwayigganya ne muteeka ennaku ku bika 12 ku nsi yonna bwe mwanoonya okubizikiriza. Mwabba abaana okuva ku bamaama, ne muggya abaana mu maka ne mubateeka mu bifo ebirala ku nsi, ate ne mubalimba ne mubaleetera okukkiriza engero z'Abayesuwiiti ezikwata ku byafaayo byabwe eby'amazima, ebigendererwa, n'ensibuko yaabwe. Tekijja nsonga, mwagaanye, kubanga nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, nja kubagatta ddala n'ebiseera byabwe ebyayita, eggwanga n'obusika bwabwe obw'amazima, era nja kufuula bonna abasika b'obulokozi omuntu omu omuggya, mu nze, Kristo Yesu.

Nga mukozesa amagezi amakujjukujju n'okuzikiriza okungi, mukwekedde ddala tekinologiya wammwe, era ne mulimba nti abantu ab'edda baali bajinga era tebaalina tekinologiya. Kubanga mwalaga ab'edda ebyama byammwe ebitagasa, era tewali kigambo kipya wansi w'enjuba. Era bwe mutyo mukweka amazima agakwata ku kukyusa kwammwe okw'ekitonde, era ne mubikweka abo be mwandisendasenda n'obulimba bwammwe obunene. Muzzeemu okukola amagezi gammwe ag'obulogo, bwe muteesezzawo olukalala musobole okweyita abagenyi ab'omu bbanga. Era okuyita mu kulumba kwammwe okw'obulimba okw'abagenyi ab'omu bbanga mujja kuleetera bonna okwewuunya obubonero bwammwe obulimba n'ebyamagero, era n'abo amannya gaabwe ge gatawandiikibwa mu Kitabo ky'Obulamu okuva ku kutondebwa kw'ensi. Naye nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, nja kubabikkula ddala, na buli kyama ekikwekeddwa kijja kulabika.

Laba! Nja kusitula abeesigwa bange mu ggulu, kubanga njija okutwala Omugole wange era sijja kuziyizibwa. Era nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, njija okusala omusango n'okulwana n'okuleeta okusalirwa omusango omunene ku nsi, era temujja kuwona.

Nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, Mukama wa bakama ne Kabaka wa bakabaka, ntumye ebigambo bino omuyita mu muweereza wange, era omuyita mu muweereza wange ebigambo byange bitumiddwa, era byonna birituukirira.

2Okuva eri Yahweh, Katonda Omukulu Ataggwaawo: Okubwatuka Okw'okubiri!

Ayi mmwe ba Sitaani, mulimbye ekitonde kyange okugoberera obubonero bwammwe obulimba n'ebyamagero, era obusungu bwange bubabuubuukiddeko. Abantu bange bajja kwawulibwa ddala n'omuddo gwammwe gwe mwasiga mu nnyumba yange ey'ekisa; era Omwana wange, Kristo Yesu, ajja kukuŋŋaanya eŋŋaano yange mu tterekero lyange, era bwe kityo Omwami w'amakungula ajja kukuŋŋaanya, okusiba n'okwokya omuddo gwammwe. Okusalirwa omusango omunene kuli ku mmwe, era temujja kuwona mukono gwange. Okuva ku lubereberye, mwekululanga ne mwekweka mu baana bange, era ne mwonoona abaana bange abato okuva ku lubereberye n'obulimba bwammwe n'obukumpanya.

Mmwe temuli bakatonda, n'abo abaagoberera obulimba bwammwe n'endowooza zammwe ez'obuwanguzi nabo si bakatonda. Temusobola kutondebwa, kubanga temulina maanyi, era saabatonda na busobozi bwe butyo kubanga nnali mmanyi nti mwandijeemye nga temunnatondebwa; era mu butuukirivu bwange n'okwagala kwange nabatonda mu butuukirivu, era mwali mutuukiridde ku lunaku lwe mwatondebwa. Mu malala gammwe agatalina mugaso mwekuluntaza olw'obukulu bwammwe, naye temulina bukulu, era nja kubabikkula ddala mmwe abakumpanya. Nja kubateeka mu maaso ga Bakabaka, era balibategeera n'engeri gye muli abatalina maanyi. Omwana wange, Yeshua HaMashiach, yabawangula ddala e Kaluvaaliyo, era temulina ssuubi.

Mukkirizibwa okukola ekyo kyokka kye nkkiriza, kubanga muli mulabe eyawangulwa. Mwabba olukusa lw'ensi mu bukumpanya bwammwe n'obulimba eri Adamu, era kati omwana wange omu yekka, ye Yeshua HaMashiach, abawangudde mu kwagala kwe eri nze n'ekitonde kyange; era ensi yiye, era ye Mukama wa bakama ne Kabaka wa bakabaka, kubanga nkirangiridde. Kubanga yagula ensi n'abantu n'omusaayi gwe ogutalina kibi, n'abo abalonda obulamu balinunulibwa. Emirimu gyammwe gyonna egitali na mugaso gijja kuggibwawo, n'abo bonna abalonda obulamu baliva mu bulimba bwammwe n'obukumpanya ne bayingira mu bulamu obuggya, era nja kufuula abeesigwa okuba ab'ekitiibwa okusinga bwe mwali. Kubanga mwagaanye, ayi baami abanafu era abasiru ab'ensi, era buli mwami mu ntegeka yammwe agaanye awamu nammwe, ayi omusota ogwekulula, omulimba.

Kati obudde butuuse obw'okusalirwa kwammwe Omusango n'okuzikirizibwa okw'olubeerera okw'obwakabaka bwammwe, era n'obwakabaka bwe mwazimba mu kutebenkera okwagezaako okunneekwesa, nze Yahweh, okuva ku kitonde kyange. Temujja kuwona. Temuliddayo nate kuyingira mu ggulu, kubanga musuuliddwa wansi ku nsi obutaddayo, era ekifo kyammwe tekikyali mu ggulu. Musibiddwa ku nsi gye mwalulunkana, etabadde yammwe okugitwala. Buli mulimu gwammwe gujja kuggibwawo mu bulamu bw'abo be mwagezaako okuzikiriza (ne ku nsi yennyini); balibeera mu mirembe n'obutebenkevu emirembe gyonna, era baliba n'essanyu erituukiridde, era nja kubeera mu bantu era nja kubeera Katonda waabwe.

Naye ggwe, ayi omwekulula, tolina maanyi mu maaso gange, era tolina ky'oyinza kukola kunziza. Mu nnaku omukaaga okuva ku kugwa kw'omuntu, kubanga olunaku lumu lwe lukumi lw'emyaka mu maaso gange, tewali ky'otukirizza okuggyako okusalirwa kwo omusango, okusingisibwa, okuzikirizibwa, n'okubonyaabonyezebwa eri ggwe na bonna abakugoberera. Kubanga ojja kusibibwa n'osuulibwa mu nnyanja eyo ennene ey'omuliro gye nakukolera ennyiriri ezisinga okwaka, era ennyiriri ennamu eza bbulu, era ojja kwaka emirembe n'emirembe, era nja kukusasula buli mulimu omubi gwe wakola, emirundi mingi. Wasse ebibiina n'ebibiina, era ojja kusasulira buli mwoyo ogwabulira, era toliwonera. Ojja kumanya ebibi byonna bye wakola, era toliwonera musango, era ojja kubonyaabonyezebwa emirembe gyonna olw'ebibi bye wakola. Ojja kumanya ddala ebibi byo n'ebikolwa byo ebibi mu kubonyaabonyezebwa kwo, n'abo b'okyaye bajja kufuna obuwanguzi obusembayo ku ggwe. Era n'abo bonna abatali batuukirivu be wasendasenda okwonoona ne beewaayo okwegatta ku bujeemu bwo, era n'abo amannya gaabwe ge gatawandiikibwa mu Kitabo ky'Obulamu, baligabana mu kubonyaabonyezebwa kwo. Mwena mujja kusasulira buli kibi kye mwakola, kubanga mwayonoona Yahweh ne kitonde kyange, era nja kusasula. Kubanga mu butuukirivu bwange nja kusasula ebibi byammwe, ebikolwa byammwe, n'emize gyammwe emibi ddala mu bifuba byammwe emirundi mingi n'emirembe gyonna. Era nze, Yahweh, era Katonda omukulu ataggwaawo, ndayira bino mu linnya lyange, kubanga siyinza kulayira mu linnya lisingawo.

Mujja kuweereza ebigendererwa byange, era mu bukyayi bwammwe n'okuzikiriza mujja kumpondera abalonde bange; kubanga nja kubakulembera n'omukono gwa Yeshua HaMashiach n'eggye lye ery'abasajja ab'amaanyi, kubanga balikuŋŋaanya endiga zange ezisigaddewo. (Laba! Abo 144,000 abatumiddwa Yeshua HaMashiach balikoal emirimu gy'amaanyi mu maaso g'obubi bwammwe obw'omuziro.) So tewali n'omu ku abo be nalagira okufuna obulamu obutaggwaawo aligwa mu nkwe zammwe, kubanga tebayinza kusikulwa mu mukono gwange, so tewali n'omu ayinza kubasikula mu mikono gy'Omwana wange: Kubanga namuwa erinnya erisinga amannya gonna, era ajja mu maanyi ga Yahweh yennyini, kubanga nagabana naye entebe yange ey'obwakabaka. Era ajja kukumenya omutwe gwo ddala. Ogaanye, omulangira omuto, n'ebiseera byo bituuse.

Nze, Yahweh, Katonda omukulu ataggwaawo, ntumye ebigambo bino omuyita mu muweereza wange, era omuyita mu muweereza wange ebigambo byange bitumiddwa, era birituukirira. Kubanga Yahweh, Katonda omukulu ataggwaawo, bw'atyo alangiridde, so tewali kiyinza kumenya birango byange. Nze El Shaddai!


Obudde ButuuseOkubwatuka 3 ne 4 (Ogwokutaano 16, 2024)

3Okuva eri Yeshua HaMashiach: Okubwatuka Okw’okusatu

Laba! Obudde bw’okwawulibwa bunaatera okuggwaawo, n'okulabika kwange kaakano kutuuse okumpi. Kuli kumpi nnyo era ne bamalayika n'eggye ly'omu ggulu bali ku mulanga gwa kakondeere. Nagolodde emikono gyange eri ensi efa okuva emyaka mingi, era bangi bakkirizza endagaano ey'emirembe Kitange, Yahweh, gye yatumira abantu, ye nze, Kristo Yesu, kubanga nze mirembe gyammwe. Naye ate abasinga obungi bagaanyi ekirabo kyange n'ekisa kya Kitange n'okusaasira kwe ebifukiddwa ku nsi.

Abo abalambibwa n'abanunulibwa beebo abaalondebwa okuva ku ntandikwa y’ensi. Kubanga Kitange yabamanyanga okuva ku mirembe gyonna n'emirembe gyonna, era mubadde bulijjo mu mutima gwe wakati, nga nange bwe mbadde mu mutima gwe wakati, kubanga nze, Yesu Omunazaaleeti, ndi mwana wa Yahweh omu yekka eyazaalibwa. Naye olw’ebikolwa byange ebyatuukirira ng’Olusuku olutuukiridde era n'olw'obuwulize bw'oyo omu yekka eyazaalibwa, abaana ba Katonda bangi nnyo bazaaliddwa omulundi ogw'okubiri okutuusa emirembe n'emirembe, nga baggyiddwa ne mu baana b'abantu, kubanga nze ndi Mwana w'omuntu!

Obudde buno obw’ekisa bwali kyama ekikwekeddwa mu mutima gwa Yahweh kye yali tatabulirangako muntu yenna okutuusa lwe yakimbuulirira, era nange nkibikkulidde abatume bange abatukuvu n'abannabbi era nkuumidde Ekigambo kyange ekitukuvu eri amatu n’emitima gy'abalonde n'abeesigwa. Kubanga ggwe, Omugole wange, oli kitundu ku kyama ekinene ekya Katonda, n'obulamu bwammwe (obukwekeddwa mu nze) bunaatera okubikkulibwa. Era nja kuwa empeera abeesigwa olw'ebikolwa byabwe byonna eby'obwesigwa bye bakoze mu kukkiriza okungi gyendi, Yesu Kristo.

Era bwe ntyo nalokola ebibiina ebinene n'omusaayi gwange ogutangirira, kubanga omusaayi gwange gwali tegwaliiko kibi era n'okutuusa leero siriiko kibi, kubanga siryonoona. Abo abaamala obulamu bwabwe mu bwesigwa mu Kristo bajja kufuna empeera nnene, era bajja kumasamasa ng'emmunyeenye ez'omu ggulu, era ekitiibwa ekinene kya kubeerako, kubanga tebeegulumizanga ng'abakulu. Naye bagulumizizza nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, era nja kubaanjula eri Kitange nga tebalina bbala newaakubadde enkanyanya, n'ebyambalo byabwe bijja kuba byeru ng'omuzira era tebiricaafuwazibwe nate. Era bonna bajja kubeera balamu era tebaddemu kwonoona, oba nga bayita mu butatya Katonda oba mu malala. Era bajja kugulumizibwa mu nze.

Kubanga nze ndi musumba mukulu, era ndongoose ekisibo kyange mu malundiro amagimu okutuusa mu biseera by'okutuukiriza, ebinaatera okujja ku nsi, kubanga tewakyali musenyu gusigadde okuyita mu kirawuli. Era nja kuwa bonna empeera, okuva ku muto okutuuka ku mukulu, mu ngeri gye mutasobola kutegeera mu kiseera kino; naye okutegeera okwo kuli ku mulyango gwammwe, kubanga mujja kutwalibwa mu ggulu mangu nnyo. Era bwe kityo Omugole wange ajja kutwalibwa mu kitiibwa, gye nja okusalira emisango gy'ebikolwa bye n'okununula ddala buli musajja, omukazi, n'omwana ow'enkomerero. Nja kubawa empeera nnyingi ne ku bintu ebitono ennyo ebikoleddwa mu kunweereza. Kubanga Katonda si mutali mutuukirivu, okwerabira omulimu gwammwe n'okutegana kw'okwagala, kwe mwalaga ku Linnya lye, mu kuweereza kwammwe Abatukuvu, era muweereza.

Totya, ggwe Omugole wange, kubanga Katonda tasala musango nga omuntu bw'asala, kubanga omuntu si mutuukirivu era yeeryako mu maaso. Omuntu agulumiza abo omuntu b'alowooza nti bakulu, n'omuntu ow'obutonde emirundi egisinga obungi anyooma obuwombeefu n'empisa ennungi n'asiima ab'amalala n'abeetuukirizizza. Kubanga omuntu ow'obutonde akaabirira okubalibwa ng'omutuukirivu mu maaso ga Katonda, naye omuntu ow'obutonde tasobola kuwangula obuzaale bwe obw'ekibi era agaana okukkiriza nti ayonooneddwa, n'okwegaana Mukama wa bakama, eyandimulokodde.

Era bwe kityo amalala gakulembera okuzikirira, n'omwoyo ogw'amalala nga okugwa tekunnabaawo. Mu kifo ky'okukkakkanya emitima gyabwe eri ebigambo byange n'amagezi n'okwagala kwe nnyiye ku nsi, omuntu ow'obutonde mu kifo ky'ekyo alondawo okugoberera okwegomba kwe n'ennyonta ze, kubanga amakubo g'ensi gakolebwa Sitaani okukema n'okukuma ennyonta ezo, n'empeera z'obutali butuukirivu. Era bwe kityo obudde bw'Abamawanga bunaatera okuggwaawo, era budde bwa nnaku ya Yakobo; kubanga nja kununula Isirayiri, n'abalonde bange abeesigwa, abajja okujja gye ndi nga benenyezza ebibi byabwe n'ebibi bya bajjajjaabwe abaangaana. Era nga bwe kyawandiikibwa, bwe kityo bwe kirikolebwa.

Ayi ggwe omuntu ow'obutonde, lwaki okyalonda ebibi byo n'onyooma ekisa kyange n'obulokozi ng'ekyo kintu ekyangu? Yahweh newaakubadde nze, Kristo Yesu, tetulina ssanyu lyonna mu misango egijja n'entiisa ey'amaanyi eneetera okusumululwa. Naye Katonda talonze kitundu kino kya ntegeka ye alyoke abe mukambwe eri ekitonde kye: kubanga Katonda kwagala, era Katonda musana, era mu ye temuli kizikiza kyonna. Okubonaabona okungi okujja kutegekeddwa okuwa abantu omukisa ogw'enkomerero nga tebunnaziba. Ekibiina ekinene kijja kulokolebwa okuva mu kubonaabona, n'abo abakwata erinnya lyange okutuusa ku mukka gwabwe ogw'enkomerero era ne bataagala bulamu bwabwe okutuusa ku nkomerero.

Mwagoberera Basitaani abaagezaako okuzikiriza ekitonde kya Yahweh, okutuusa ku kutta nze, Yesu Kristo, bwe nneewaayo nzekka mu mikono gye nga Paasika eyatuukirira ku lw'ensi, bwe ntyo ne nnyweza okunnuŋŋutibwa kw'abantu era ne nnyweza empeera yammwe mu muliro omunene ogutayinza kuzikizibwa emirembe gyonna! Mujja kusasulira ddala ebibi byammwe byonna n'eby'omuzizo, na buli kikolwa ekibi kye mwakola kiribakyukira emirundi kikumi, kubanga bonna balisasulwa okusinziira ku bikolwa byabwe. Era amakubo gammwe gonna amabi galisasulwa ku mitwe gyammwe emirundi mingi, kubanga mwalowooza nti muyinza okunwangula, era n'amalala mukyalowooza nti muyinza okuwangula so nga mwawangulwa ddala.

Muwulire ebigambo byange, ggwe omusota ogw'edda, kubanga omusango gwo guli ku ggwe era ndaba okutya kwo, kubanga otya eby'omu maaso go okusinga ekintu kyonna, ggwe empologoma etalina mannyo eteriiko mugaso! Okuwoolera eggwanga kwange, era nze ndisasula, era ebiro byo bituuse. Obusungu bwa Katonda n'obusungu bw'Omwana gw'endiga binaatera okusumululwa nga bwe ndimenya obubonero omusanvu, ng'abamalayika omusanvu batumiddwa okufuuwa amakondeere ag'emisango, n'abamalayika omusanvu abatwalira ebibonyoobonyo omusanvu ebisembayo batumiddwa, era bijja kusumululwa, kubanga mu byo obusungu bwa Katonda mujjudde. Ennaku nnyingi zijja kubatuukako mwenna, nga mbabonereza ddala era ne nsaanyaawo obwakabaka bwammwe. Kabaka wammwe omukulu ndimuwangula ne nnabbi wammwe ow'obulimba ndimuwangula, era balisuulibwa nga balamu mu nnyanja y'omuliro eyaka n'ekibiriiti; era eyo gye balibalindirira okubegattako, era eyo gye mulibeerera nga muzingiddwa mu muliro n'ekibiriiti, era omusango gwammwe gunaatera okutandika!

Mugole wange, totya! Ebigambo byange eby'omusango si biriyo naye bya kukubudaabuda, kubanga nkuloppyeko mu busungu obunaatera okusumululwa, era ndikukweka mu bulamu obulungi n'empeera ennene, era olibeera nange emirembe gyonna. Kubanga nakulonda, kubanga nakuyita, era ggwe ggugu lyange erisinga obunene, era ggwe ggugu lya Yahweh erisinga obunene; era ggwe luulu olw'omuwendo omungi, kubanga nkuwaddeyo byonna byange. Nkuwa amagezi Mugole wange okunoonya okwawula era ojje gyendi ng'oyagala amagezi, kubanga ndikuyigiriza. Kubanga abamu ku mmwe mugoberedde amakubo gamu g'abannabbi bano ab'obulimba; mukyuke mweteeke mu Mukama wammwe, nange ndibakkiriza.

Kubanga babakemye n'obulogo obw'Ekikristaayo, era mukkirizza obulimba bungi bwe baabatunda, era ne babugamba nti bwa mazima, naye bwa muzizo. Enziro zino ezitalimu mazzi zigezezzaako okubaggya ku nze, era ekyo tekiyinzika, n'olwekyo temutya, kubanga sijja kugaana! Nja kweyongera okubasumulula mubeere baddembe ddala, era sijja kusirisibwa! Nkuwanirira mu ngeri nnyingi nnyo ze mutamanyi. Kubanga mwenna mwalambikibwa nga temunnazaalibwa, kubanga Kitange yali abamanyi ne mu nda ya bannyammwe, era yalindirira buli omu ku mmwe ekiseera kye mutasobola kutegeera, era bulijjo muli mu mutima gwe. Mwalambikibwa okuva lwe mwazaalibwa, era bamalayika bange baabakuuma ku kiragiro kyange era babalondodde era babakuumye, kubanga bwe batyo bwe batumibwa. Kino kikwewuunyisa? Mujja kuwuliriza Abayebbulaniya kye balina okwogera? Naye ani ku Bamalayika gwe yali agambye mu kiseera kyonna nti, tuula ku mukono gwange ogwa ddyo, okutuusa lwe ndifuula abalabe bo entebbe y'ebigere byo? Si bonna myoyo egiweereza, egitumibwa okuweereza abo abaliba abasika b'obulokozi?

Mugole wange, mmwe muli basika ba bulokozi mu nze, Yesu Kristo. Mwayitibwa mu nze; mwakuumibwa mu nze era nze; mwaweebwa omukisa okuwulira okubuulirwa okw'okukkiriza okukwata ku nze musobole okunfuula Omukama wammwe, n'olwekyo nabateekako akabonero n'omwoyo oyo omutukuvu ow'ekisuubizo. Mutegeere nti akabonero ako tekayinza kumenyeka, era munaatera okunnuŋŋutibwa mu bugenyi obutaggwaawo, kubanga temumanyi kujjula kw'omulimu gw'obutonzi gwe nnaatera okutuukiriza mu mmwe, Mugole wange! Mutegeere nti abagwa n'omuddo gwabwe babankwatirwa obuggya, era bakyawa kyonna Yahweh ky'ayagala, era ndibawalanirako eggwanga ddala ku mitwe gyabwe, kubanga nze ajja okubetenta omutwe gw'omusota ogwo ogw'edda, kubanga kyategeezebwa, n'ekyo Yahweh ky'ategeeza tekiyinza kukyusibwa.

Tewali muntu ayinza kubawula ku nze; tewali bubi bwonna buyinza kumenya kabonero ako; muli bange era muli ba Kitange, kubanga byonna Kitange by'ampa ndibizzaayo eri Katonda ffenna tubeere byonna mu byonna! Era yampa ebintu byonna mu ggulu ne mu nsi, okuggyako mu ntebe ye ey'obwakabaka ye mukulu okunsinga!

Era nze, Kristo Yesu, mbalangirira ebigambo bino, era nze, Yesu Kristo, asala omusango ku balabe bammwe, era ndigutuukiriza okutuuka ku kasente akasembayo. Mugole wange, okwagala kwange gy'oli tekuzikira, era nkutegekedde ekifo, kubanga mu nnyumba ya Kitange mulimu amayumba mangi! Kale, mangu nnyo mujja kufumbiriganwa nange emirembe gyonna, era bwe mutyo bwe mulibeera nange ennaku zonna, era mulisembayo okutegeera obunene bw'ekigendererwa kyammwe mu nze kye mwatonderwa okutuukiriza!

Kubanga mu nze, Yahweh yabawa eby'omu maaso n'ekigendererwa, era ekigendererwa eky'emirembe gyonna kinaatera okubikkulirwa mmwe, kubanga njija! Era sijja kugaana. Mulindirire okulabika kwange. Mulindirire okumasamasa kw'okujja kwange, kubanga kunaatera okubaluka ku nsi nga ndabika mu bire n'okwogerera waggulu okw'amaanyi, n'eddoboozi lya malayika omukulu, n'ekkondeere lya Katonda, kubanga mbagamba mazima nti ne bamalayika balindirira ekkondeere eryo erinaatera okuvuuga, era beeteeseteese! Muntegekere era mulabe, mukenneenye, mukette nga bwe kyalagirwa; kubanga munaatera okundaba n'ekitiibwa kyange Kitange ky'ayambazizza, kubanga bwe ntyo bwe ndibayambaza, kubanga muliba nga nze, era mulindaba nga bwe ndi.
