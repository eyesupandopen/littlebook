Omujulirwa Ow'okubiri: Ekkanisa
Mu Biwandiiko by'omutume Pawulo
May 08, 2024

Muwulire Ebigambo Byange Eby'okubudaabuda Era Muwulire Ebigambo Byange Eby'amaanyi!

Muwulire ebigambo bya Mukama, ddala ebigambo bya Yeshua HaMashiach. Mujje gyendi, Omugole wange; mujje gyendi nga mweteeseteese, kubanga nzija okubaggyawo. Muwummulire mu nze, mmwe abaana bange abato, era mutuule ku bigere byange. Muwulire ebigambo byange eby'okubudaabuda, era muwulire ebigambo byange eby'amaanyi, kubanga nabawa Omuwu̸muza era mu Muwu̸muza oyo mulimu amaanyi amangi. Mumpulirize era mukkirize ebigambo byange, mwetoowaze olw'okwetakabya kwammwe muwulire amaloboozi g'ababaka bange, kubanga beetoowaza mu mutima. Saabatuma mmwe ab'amaanyi ab'omu nsi eno; saabatuma mmwe abalina obugagga obungi n'ettutumu (newankubadde beeyita bwe batyo). Nedda, nabatuma abo ensi be yasuulawo n'okubanyooma kubanga boogera ku lwange era Omwoyo gwange guli mu bo era tegusobola kuggyibwamu.

Temwewuunya nti ensi ebakyawa, kubanga ensi yansooka kukyawa, era omuweereza tasinga mukama we. Bwe baŋŋondanga nze, bwe batyo bwe bajja okubakola nammwe. Naye mmwe muli baani okutya ekyo omuntu ky'ayinza okubakola? Mmwe muli baani okutuula mu kizikiza n'okutya so nga mbatutte eri Kitange era Kitammwe, ddala Katonda omukulu atalina nkomerero, kubanga tewali mulala! Nze, nze kennyini, Kristo Yesu, mbatuzizza mu bifo eby'omu ggulu, era bonna abali mu ggulu balindiridde n'essuubi eddene okutuuka kwammwe okuli okumpi, Omugole wange.

Kubanga ne Katonda omukulu atalina nkomerero, ddala Kitaffe, ddala Yahweh yennyini (abaddewo okuva emirembe n'emirembe) abalindiridde n'essuubi eddene! Kubanga mangu nnyo mujja kulabika mu maaso g'entebe ye ey'obwakabaka n'ekitiibwa kingi, era mu kisenge kye eky'entebe y'obwakabaka mwe mujja okutuuzibwa, kubanga mujja kugabana ku ntebe yange. Ne bamalayika babalindiridde n'essanyu lingi, baana bange, kubanga wabaawo essanyu mu maaso gaabwe ne bw'aba omu ku boonoonyi y'omu yeenenyezza. Bwe kityo bwe kiri: Naye mmwe muzze ku lusozi Sayuuni, ne ku kibuga kya Katonda omulamu, Yerusaalemi eky'omu ggulu, n'eri ekibiina ky'abamalayika abatabalika. N'abamu ku bamalayika abo bennyini be baweereza b'abo abajja okusikira obulokozi, era ekyo kibakwatako, Omugole wange. Era Nze, ddala Kristo Yesu, nze kennyini nneegomba okubeera mu maaso gange. Kubanga mbaagala era mbeegomba, era mangu nnyo tujja kubeera wamu lubeerera mu mbaga y'Omwana gw'endiga. Nze Wasseng'omugole wammwe eyeegomba Omugole we, era nzija okubaggyawo! Mbategekedde ebintu bingi, era ebitone bingi n'empeera z'obwesigwa bibalindiridde. Mangu nnyo mujja kutuuka ku busika bwammwe obujjuvu mu nze, era mujja kubeera n'obulamu obutaggwaawo okutegeera obukulu n'ekitiibwa ky'ebyo bye nkuŋŋaanyiza gy'oli okutuukiriza, n'ekyo kye nja okubafuula, kubanga muli bitonde bipya, omuntu omupya, eyatondebwa mu nze! Era mujja kunkolera ebintu bingi nnyo, si mu Bwakabaka bwange obw'obutuukirivu mw'onofugira ensi mu butuukirivu okumala olunaku lumu! Naye naddala mu ggulu eriggya n'ensi empya obukulu bwammwe bwe bujja okwaka, era mujja kubeera nange emirembe gyonna. Kubanga olunaku lumu eri Mukama lulinga emyaka lukumi, n'emyaka lukumi giringa olunaku lumu. Nnina ekibuuzo kye mbuuza, mmwe abaagalwa bange, nze kennyini, Yesu Kristo, Mukama wa bakama era Kabaka wa bakabaka! Mumpulirize era mufumiitirize ku bigambo byange, kubanga ebigambo bya Mukama bigambo birongoofu: ng'effeeza egezeseddwa mu kikoomi eky'ettaka, eyalongoosebwa emirundi musanvu. Lwaki mweteebereza nti temuli ba mugaso? Ntegeera nti muli bawombeefu era beetoowaza mu mutima, era ekyo kiva mu buzaale obuggya bwe nnatonda mu mmwe, kubanga omuntu ow'obutonde abeera wa malala bulijjo. Naye lwaki mwerowoozaako ng'abatalina mugaso, nga mwebuuza lwaki Katonda yandifuddeeyo okukola nammwe, era lwaki nnabawa obulokozi so nga waliwo abasingako? Lwaki mutuula awo nga mwennyamivu, nga mwejjerezera bwe mwonoona? Lwaki temweggyako nfuufu? (Enfuufu ejja kuba mmere ya musota.) Mugolokoke! Musituke! Muzuukuke! Yee, mwenna mujja kwonoona okuva ku makya okutuuka akawungeezi. Kino kintu Yahweh kye yasuulirira mu kumanya kwe okw'olubeerera? Kale kiki? Katonda yeerabira nti mwandyonoonye oluvannyuma lw'obulokozi? Kibeere wala: yee, Katonda abeere wa mazima, naye buli muntu mulimba! Kubanga mu nteekateeka ye ey'okununula, ne mu nteekateeka ze ezinkwatako, n'ekyama ekikulu ekya Katonda, byonna bino byakolebwa wamu mu kisa n'okusaasira; mukkirize kisa kyange n'okusaasira kwange mwokka, kubanga bangi ku mmwe mwekubagira.

Baana bange, nnasasula omuwendo gw'ebibi byammwe. Bwe mutambulira mu butuukirivu n'obwesigwa ne mwonoona kubanga omubiri gwammwe gwonoonese, mwatulirenga ekibi ekyo nze ne mwerongoosa mu musaayi gwange ogununula, kubanga nze kununulibwa kwammwe era mbavumuludde. Abavumululwa ba Mukama boogere bwe batyo: abo be yavumulula okuva mu mukono gw'omulabe. Tewali kintu kyonna kye muyinza okwongera ku mirimu gyange egyatuukirizibwa, kubanga emirimu egituukiridde mu bujjuvu gyetaaga ki ekirala? Bwe nnagamba ku musaalaba mu kussa kwange okwasembayo mu nnyumba eyo ey'ettaka eddungi, nnali njambadde mu kiseera ekyo nti: Kiwedde, ssaalekawo kintu kyonna nga sikikoze. Era Katonda bw'akkiriza ssaddaaka etuukiridde, etuukiridde, waleme kubaawo muntu yenna agezaako okwongerako ekintu kyonna; kubanga egyo mirimu gya bwereere, ddala amalala gammwe amakusike n'obwereere bwammwe, kubanga ku nsi kuliko obwereere bungi. Obwereere obw'obwereere, bw'atyo bw'ayogera Omubuulizi: obwereere obw'obwereere, byonna bwereere. Kubanga obwereere bwogera, era bubalimba, nga bugamba nti temumala era ssaddaaka yange si ntuukirivu.

N'olwekyo, mulina okukola ekintu ekirala okukakasa obugwanivu bwammwe bwe mwenenya mu maaso gange. Mazima mbagamba, ebibala ebisaanira okwenenya bikkirizibwa, naye si kwejjerezera, si kwekyawa, si ndowooza za kwemalako ssuubi ez'okwennyamira. Kubanga bwe mwenyigira mu bikolwa bino eby'omubiri ebitagasa, muba muyita amazima obulimba nga temumanyi era muba mugezaako okutabaganya omubiri gwammwe n'omwoyo. Baana bange, ekizaalibwa mu mubiri guba mubiri, n'ekizaalibwa mu mwoyo guba mwoyo. Kubanga omubiri gwegomba okuwakana n'omwoyo, n'omwoyo okuwakana n'omubiri: era bino byawukana; muleme okukola ebyo bye mwandikoze. Kubanga olwa ssaddaaka yange mwattabaganyizibwa nange, Mukama wammwe Yesu Kristo, era nabatabaganya ne Katonda, kubanga bwe kityo bwe kiri ekyama ekikulu ekya Katonda kye muli. Lwaki mubuusabuusa ebigambo byange? Mugambe omubiri gwammwe gusirike, era musirise amaloboozi agawakana n'okuyigiriza kwange okw'amazima! Kubanga mbategekedde obukulu. Mbawadde amaanyi mu mwoyo gwange ogw'obwana mwe mukaabira nti, ABBA, Kitange. Mbawadde amaanyi era mbawadde obusobozi okunkolera ebintu ebikulu; era okusinga byonna, mbawadde obubaka mu nze, ne mu bbaluwa zange ez'okwagalana eri Omugole wange. N'ebbaluwa ze nnawandiisa Pawulo eri Ekkanisa yange zinnyonnyola kino mu njogera ennyangu mmwe okutegeera n'okukkiriza. Kubanga nze nnagamba ki Ekkanisa yange ey'e Kkolinso ku mukono gwa Pawulo? N’olwekyo, omuntu yenna bw'aba mu Kristo, aba kitonde kipya: eby'edda biweddewo; laba, byonna bifuuse bipya. Era byonna biva eri Katonda eyatutabaganya naye ku bwa Yesu Kristo, n'atuwa obuweereza obw'okutabaganya. Baana bange, bwe mutambulira mu Mwoyo gwange ne muba beesigwa eri amateeka gange, mba ntandika okubatonda obupya mu nze.

Kubanga omuntu omukadde ow'ettaka eddungi azikirira, era Omuyudaaya n'Omu̸mahanga bombi bajja kufuulibwa omuntu omu omupya mu nze, ddala olw'omwoyo gwange ogw'obwana, kubanga nja kulokola abamu ku bonna. Kale, omuntu yenna bw'aba talina Mwoyo gwa Kristo, oyo si wuwe. Mu oyo era bwe mwamala okukkiriza, mwasibwako akabonero ak'omwoyo oyo omutukuvu ow'ekisuubizo, Oyo gw'omusingo gw'obusika bwammwe, okutuusa ku kununulibwa kw'obugagga obwagulibwa, okutenderezebwa kw'ekitiibwa kye. Kale mutambulire mu bupya obw'obulamu era mutegeere nti mangu nnyo obugagga bwange obwagulibwa bujja kufuna mu bujjuvu (mulina olukusa olusooka olw'omwoyo gwange), era mujja kwambazibwa emibiri empya, emibiri egitali mivundu, emibiri egirina amaanyi mu bugazi n'obusobozi, ddala ennyumba ezisaanira kabaka! Kubanga mujja kuba bakabaka ne bakabona mu maaso ga Katonda waffe. Kale mbatabaganyizza ne Katonda, era tewakyali bulabe, era mbawadde obuweereza obw'okutabaganya. O, naye lwaki nnakikoze, muyinza okubuuza? Muwulirize mbabuulire.

N'olwekyo Abakkolinso beeyongera okuwa obujulizi bwabwe: Okutegeeza nti, Katonda yali mu Kristo ng'atabaganya ensi naye yennyini, nga tababalira byonoono byabwe, era ng'atukwasizza ekigambo eky'okutabaganya. Kale ffe tuli babaka ba Kristo, ng'Katonda bw'abeegayirira ku bwaffe; tubegayirira mu kifo kya Kristo, mutabaganyizibwe ne Katonda. Baana bange, mmwe muli babaka bange, era mulina ekifo kya kitiibwa nnyo, kubanga munkiikirira nze. Kubanga okubeera omubaka wange kwe kukiikirira Kabaka wa bakabaka ne Mukama wa bakama eri ensi efa, era nze ndi kabona asinga obukulu emirembe gyonna ng'engeri ya Merukizeddeeki bw'eri. Kale, mbegayirira muwulire: mbafudde babaka bange. Mbatabaganyizza ne Katonda Omuyinza w'ebintu byonna, ddala El Shaddai, kubanga ebigendererwa bye bijja kukolebwa! Mbawadde ekigambo eky'okutabaganya, ddala ebigambo eby'amagezi ebisaanira ababaka bange, kubanga Ekigambo kya Katonda kya lubeerera. Era buli omu ku mmwe mbawadde obuweereza obw'okutabaganya. Kino kitegeeza nti buli mmeeme ezaalibwa obuggya olw'omwoyo gwange ogw'amaanyi ngiwadde obuyinza ng'abaweereza bange ab'amaanyi, era omulabe abatyayo nnyo era ajja kubaddukako bwe munaatambuliranga mu maanyi gange, newankubadde ajja kugezaako okubaweweenyuliza ebigambo eby'obulimba n'okuwuluguma ng'empologoma. Naye akamwa ke akavundu tekalina mannyo, kubanga gaamenyebwa amaanyi g'okuzuukira kwange, era tajja kuwona. Temufaayo ku mpologoma eyo etalina mannyo, era musse ebirowoozo ku ddoboozi lyange, ddala ku Bigambo by'Ekigambo kyange.

Mu kifo kino ekikulu eky'obuyinza n'amaanyi (kubanga mutambulira mu linnya lyange n'erinnya lyange lisinga amannya gonna obukulu, era Yahweh yekka y'asinga obukulu!) mbatadde ng'abaweereza bange n'ababaka, era mbategekedde ne mbateeka mu bwambazi obw'amaanyi amangi! Baana bange, temutegeera mateeka ga Bigambo byange, kubanga abasumba abajeemu baagula abaweereza Ab’e Lawodikiya okubalimba ku by'amaanyi n'ekirabo ebyatondebwa mu mmwe mu kuzaalibwa okupya. Kubanga Pawulo yannyonnyola obukulu bw'amaanyi agaaweebwa mu mwoyo gw'obwana ogwatondebwa mu mmwe. Kubanga yagamba Abakkolinso nti: Ku by'omwoyo, abooluganda, saagala mubeere batamanyi. Ate nate: Waliwo enjawulo mu birabo, mazima, naye mwoyo gumu gwokka. Ate nate: Naye okulabisibwa kw'Omwoyo kuweereddwa buli muntu, okugasa. Temuba batamanyi bya mwoyo; mutegeere enjawulo wakati w'ekirabo n'okulabisibwa, kubanga si bigambo bye bimu mu kyawandiikibwa.

Mpawadde ekirabo eky'omwoyo omutukuvu, era mu kirabo ekyo mulimu okulabisibwa. Kubanga Ekigambo kyange kigamba ki: Naye okulabisibwa kw'omwoyo kuweereddwa buli muntu okugasa.

Abaweereza bammwe Ab’e Lawodikiya ab'ebbugumu balikozezza obulimba Setaani bwe yayogera okuyita mu mirembe, obulimba obw'okubalimba nti temulina maanyi, n'obulimba obw'okugezaako okukweka amaanyi gammwe amatuufu mu nze, kubanga omwoyo gwammwe gwe Kristo ali mu mmwe, essuubi ery'ekitiibwa! Kale okulabisibwa kwange okw'omwoyo kulambikiddwa bwe kuti: ekigambo eky'amagezi, ekigambo eky'okumanya, okukkiriza, ebirabo eby'okuwonya, okukola eby'amagero, obunnabbi, okwawula emyoyo, ebika eby'enjawulo eby'ennimi, okuvvuunula ennimi. Nga tetunnagenda mu maaso, muteeke wansi ebinnyonnyozo byammwe eby'ensi n'enkamusi zammwe ez'ensi n'ebikwatagana byammwe eby'ensi, era mulekere awo okubakkiriza okubalimba okukyusa ebyawandiikibwa okutuukana n'ebigendererwa bya Setaani.

Mu buli mwoyo oguli mu buli muntu mulimu obusobozi buno okweyoleka amaanyi agava waggulu! Naye byonna bino bikolerwa omwoyo ogwo omu, ng'agabira buli muntu kinnoomu nga bw'ayagala. Baana bange, lwaki mulowooza nti bino birabo so nga ekyawandiikibwa kigamba nti bye byeyoleko? Babalimbidde; musome ebigambo byange era mube n'okukkiriza mu bigambo abavvuunuzi bange bye baalonda, kubanga nnali nabo. Mulina obusobozi buno bwonna, era bwe muba mwagala okubweyoleka, nja kubukolera mu mmwe okununula abasibe okuva mu lukwata lwa Setaani okubayingiza mu kutangaala okw'ekitiibwa okw'olubeerera. Temufaayo ku muntu yenna ayogera ebiwakana n'ebigambo bino, era mweyongere okumanya mu Kigambo kyange. Mulekere awo okweraliikirira engeri ebintu bino gye bikolamu, kubanga tebikola ku bw'ebigambo eby'amagezi n'ebirowoozo ebisanyufu. Bikola okusinziira ku bwetaavu, olw'okwagala kw'omuntu alina okuba n'okwagala okukola ebyo bye njagala, naddala olw'okukkiriza mu nze. Kubanga nja kukola emirimu egy'amaanyi ng'eyita mu mikono gyammwe, era mbatumye, omugole wange, kubanga kuno kwe kulagira okusembayo.

Mpawadde ekirabo eky'omwoyo omutukuvu, era mu kirabo ekyo mulimu ebirabo birala bingi, kubanga Abeefeeso bagamba ki: Naye buli omu ku ffe yaweebwa ekisa, ng'ekigera eky'ekirabo kya Kristo bwe kiri. Kyeyava ayogera nti: Bwe yalinnya waggulu, n'atwala obusibe nga busibe, n'awa abantu ebirabo. Ate era okuva mu Abaruumi: Kubanga njogera, olw'ekisa kye nnaweebwa, eri buli muntu ali mu mmwe, aleme okwerowoozaako okusinga bwe kimugwanidde okwerowoozaako; naye alowooze n'amagezi, nga Katonda bw'agabidde buli muntu ekigera eky'okukkiriza. Kale mutegeere nti mwandyeroowoozezzaako nnyo kubanga mwatongebwa obupya mu butuukirivu n'obutukuvu obw'amazima okutuusa ku kulaga ekitiibwa kya Katonda yennyini, ddala Kitaffe w'ebitangaala, kubanga mmwe muli kitangaala ky'ensi. Naye mulowooze n'amagezi, kubanga temusinga baganda bammwe ne bannyinammwe abazaaliddwa mu mwoyo gwe gumu nga mmwe: Kubanga mwenna mulina obusobozi buno, era mwenna mulina ebirabo bino.

Kubanga mpawadde abantu ebirabo: Era nawa abamu okuba abatume: n'abamu bannabbi: n'abamu ababuulizi b'enjiri: n'abamu abasumba, n'abayigiriza: Okutuukiriza abatukuvu, olw'omulimu gw'obuweereza, olw'okuzimba omubiri gwa Kristo. Waliwo obuwerereza bungi bwe mpawadde Omugole wange, era bwa mmwe bwe muba mubyagala. Munsabe nja kukola obuwerereza buno mu mmwe, naye mulina okuba n'okwagala. Kale mwegombe nnyo ebirabo ebisinga obulungi: Naye mbalage ekkubo erisinga obulungi. Ekkubo eryo erisinga obulungi, baana bange, lwe kwagala kwa Katonda okutawangulwa. Mwandyegombye nnyo ebirabo ebisinga obulungi, kubanga mbawadde amaanyi. Naye okuweereza okuva mu kwagala kwa Katonda, okuwaayo ssaddaaka z'emimwa gyammwe n'okutendereza kw'omutima gwammwe omwesigwa mu kwagala, kye kisinga. Muweere nga bwe mwaweebwa ku bwereere. Mutambulire mu maanyi gano kubanga mwagala Katonda era munjagala nze, Yesu Kristo, era muweere byonna ebibaweereddwa, era mugende nga temubuusabuusa kintu kyonna.

Omugole wange, bwe ntyo bwe mbatumye okumaliriza emirimu gino, era mangu nnyo emisinde gyammwe gijja kuggwa, era bwe nja okubakuŋŋaanyiza gyendi mu bire, mujja kumanya, nga nze bwe nnamanya ku musaalaba, era mujja kusobola okugamba (nga nze bwe nnayogerako): Kiwedde. Mangu nnyo mujja kuyingira mu ssanyu lya Mukama wammwe!

Nzija okubaggyawo mangu nnyo.

Nneegomba okubeera mu maaso gange.

Nze Kristo Yesu era mbawadde obulamu obupya mu nze.

Nze kabona asinga obukulu emirembe gyonna ng'engeri ya Merukizeddeeki bw'eri. Nze oyo eyawa abantu ebirabo.

Nze oyo eyabawa obuweereza n'ebigambo eby'okutabaganya.

Nze oyo eyabatuma ng'ababaka bange abeesigwa.

Nze Ndi nga bwe Ndi, kale mubagambe nti Ndi y'abatumye.

NDI y'antumye gye muli.

Nze oyo abatumye mu kiseera kino eky'enkomerero.
