Eri Abantu 144,000 n'Abatukuvu ab'omu Kibonyoobonyo eky'Ekiseera ky'Okubonaabona kwa Yakobo

Eri abantu 144,000 n'abatukuvu ab'omu kibonyoobonyo eky'ekiseera ky'okubonaabona kwa Yakobo: Mulabe! Mbabuulira ebintu bingi nga tebinnabaawo, omuntu ow'amagezi mu mmwe awulira eddoboozi lyange alyongere okuba ow'amagezi, era mulyoke mumanye obukakafu bw'ebigambo byange. Kubanga Setaani ajja kugezaako okubikka ku kugenda kw'abeesigwa mu Kkanisa yange (kubanga nja kubanyakula mu kugenda kwabwe okuva ku nsi okudda mu ggulu) n'olutalo n'okulabika okulimba okw'abagwira abayitibwa 'aliens' abatalina kye bali okuggyako abagwa abalyerula eri ensi mu butali butuukirivu obusukkiridde nga basimbye amakanda mu bulimba bwabwe. Kubanga bo bennyini bwe bulimba.

Balimbye ku ebyo ebyogerwa ku bwengula; kubanga tewali bwengula obugaziwa obutakoma obw'obwereere, naye waggulu w'ensi waliyo eggulu ery'omu ggulu, ddala (eggulu erisinga okuba waggulu) Yakuwa Katonda ali waggulu ennyo gy'atuula ku ntebe ye ey'obwakabaka, era n'ekirala ajja kusenga wamu n'abantu. Naye era abeera buli wamu era ye byonna mu byonna munda mu Mubiri gwa Kristo. Ku nsi kuliko luulu we ow'omuwendo omungi ennyo, abo abaalondebwa mu biro eby'emirembe n'emirembe emabega mu mutima gwa Yakuwa yennyini. N'olwekyo ensi ya Mukama n'okujjuulira kwayo; kubanga nze, Yesu Omunazaaleeti, nagula ensi n'omusaayi gwange ogununula.

Muyige ebikwata ku ntegeka y'ensi ey'amazima (muyige okusoma) era mukkirize ennyonnyola n'ebigambo ebiri mu Byawandiikibwa Ebitukuvu ebikwata ku kutondebwa, kubanga kiri nga Yakuwa bwe yakinyonnyola mu Byawandiikibwa, era Yakuwa tayinza kulimba. Oba bwe mbabuulira eby'oku nsi ne mutakkiriza, munaakkiriza mutya bwe nnaababuulira eby'omu ggulu? Nedda, tewali bagwira nga bwe babinnyonnyola mu bulimba bwabwe, wabula malayika abagwa, abajeemu, ye Setaani n'obwakabaka bwe abalimbye era abakyamizza ensi bonna balyoke basalirwe omusango abasanyukira obutali butuukirivu.

Baluse enfumo nnyingi ezalongoosebwa n'amagezi ne bazifuula olugoye olw'obulimba, era bafuze enjuyi zombi ez'ennyingo zaabwe okuva ku lubereberye nga bakyagenda mu maaso n'enteekateeka zaabwe. Kubanga bafuga ekirowoozo ekisookerwako era bafuga n'ekyakyo ekikontana, era ne bagattika emirimu gyabwe emibi ddaala ku ddaala, n'obulimba bwabwe bunene nnyo! Naye omusana gw'Ebyawandiikibwa Ebitukuvu gwolesa obukumpanya, naye omuntu n'agaana obuyambi bwa Katonda okutuusa omusango lwe gulangirirwa n'akabi ne kagwawo. Olwo abantu ne banoonya okwenenya ng'ebiseera biweddewo oluvannyuma lw'omusango okusalibwa, n'abo ababeera mu ngeri eyo tebaliwona musango gwabwe. Kubanga kyonna ekirangirirwa Yakuwa tewali ayinza kukikyusa!

Bangi bajja kugwa mu bulimba obw'amaanyi obujja okujja olw'olutalo lw'ensi yonna n'akabi akanaatera okugwira ensi. Kubanga bajja kwolesa eri abantu ng'ebitonde eby'ekisa ebyavudde mu nsi enkulaakulanye okugabana amagezi gaabyo ag'ekisa. Naye lye ge ge amagezi g'obulungi n'obubi agaddiddwaamu mu ngeri endala okutuukana n'ebigendererwa byabwe. Bajja kuba n'ebyuma ebikozesebwa ebisingawo era balirabika ng'abakulaakulanye nnyo, okusinga ennyo omuntu owa bulijjo. Naye okukulaakulana kwabwe kuli mu bubi bwokka, era si ba ku nsi kuno wabula baatondebwa Yakuwa ku lubereberye lw'okutonda mu ggulu. Baaliwo Yakuwa bwe yatonda ensi. Kitaffe mu kumanya kwe okw'emabega yamanya nti balirya enkwe n'olwekyo n'akweka ekyama kye eky'omuwendo eky'omu mutima gwe eri abo be yamanya nti bandireese okuzikiriza okw'obubi ku kitonde kye.

Nkyakowoola omuntu owa bulijjo eri obutuukirivu, naye omuntu owa bulijjo mu kifo ky'ekyo asinga kulonda bulwadde, kibi, na kizikiza; n'olwekyo nja kuwa abantu kye beegomba ennyo. Okuva omuntu owa bulijjo bw'akola obubi era bw'ayagala ekizikiza, nja kumuwa ekizikiza ekitagambika.

Kubanga mu kiseera kino, omuntu owa bulijjo ayimiridde mu kisa kyange n'amalala ng'akozesa okusaasira ng'ensonga y'okussa ekibi ku kibi, mu kifo ky'okukkiriza endagaano y'emirembe eya Yakuwa, ye nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, eyawaayo omusaayi gwange ogutalimu kibi mu kuwulira Yakuwa okuba omutango gw'ebibi byonna eby'abantu eby'ebiseera byonna. Temukola nsobi: Nze ye Masiya ow'amazima, n'abalala bonna bakopi ba wansi abatalina muwendo.

Setaani yajeema oluvannyuma lw'okutondebwa n'ayonoona ekitonde kya Kitaffe mu ngeri etalina nsonyi, ey'obubi, ey'obulabe, n'ey'okuzikiriza; naye kyakolebwa mu bukumpanya obungi n'obubonero n'ebyamagero eby'obulimba, nga mw'otwalidde n'ebyuma ebikozesebwa ebikulaakulanye ebyewuunyisa amaaso. Ate bo ne basaba okusinzibwa n'okugondera okw'amaanyi ennyo amakubo gaabwe, naye amakubo gaabwe bulijjo galeeta kufa n'okuzikirira kw'abantu, kubanga bwe gityo bwe gyategekebwa.

Tewali kipya wansi w'enjuba, baana bange. Abo abayitibwa bakatonda b'ebyafaayo eby'edda n'ezzadde lyabwe eddalu eriyitibwa 'demigods' balidda, era nga bwe kyali mu nnaku za Nuuwa, bwe kityo bwe kiriba. Kubanga basumululwa okuva mu njegere zaabwe n'okubonyaabonyezebwa kwabwe balyoke baweebwe omusango gwabwe omujjuvu, era nze nja kusasula, bw'ayogera Mukama waffe Yesu Kristo, era Yakuwa alibasasula ddala okutuuka ku nkomerero.

Setaani n'abanaabe abagwa baagaana okufukamira entegeka ya Yakuwa era mu kifo ky'ekyo ne bagezaako okubba ensi okuva ku bantu okuyita mu bulimba obungi. Setaani agezezzaako n'okubba omugabo gwange okuva eri Kitaffe w'Ebitangaala! Naye tewali ayinza kukwakula n'omu ku mmwe asinga obutono mu mikono gyange, era tewali ayinza kunzigyako mugabo gwange; kubanga gwampebwa Yakuwa yennyini, era buli agaana okufukamira nze, Yesu Kristo, ajeemera entegeka ya Yakuwa, n'ayegatta n'abagwa emirembe gyonna.

Kale mbabuulira, temukkiririza mu bulimba bwa Copernicus, eyali Omuyesuwiita era Omufirimasoni eyawerezanga Setaani. Yaddamu buyisi kuyigiriza bigambo bya bafirosoofa b'Abayonaani ab'edda abaali bakaafiiri. N'olwekyo engeri gye mutegeeramu ensi yalowoozebwa abasinza enjuba n'abakaafiiri nga basinziira ku bulimba bwa Setaani, nga mw'otwalidde n'Abayesuwiita abavvoola aboonoonese enkola zammwe ez'ebyenjigiriza n'okuyiga okukontana n'okuyiga, n'ekigendererwa ekijjuvu eky'okuba nti ensi yaabwe ey'obulimba esobole okuteekebwa mu birowoozo by'abantu okuyita mu bulimba obwakolebwa okufaanana ng'ebyenjigiriza. Mu kifo ky'ekyo, okutendekebwa obutendekebwa okutalimu kulowooza kwe kwabaddewo, n'ensi nga bw'eri ddala teyetegereise eri abo abagibeerako.

Kati olutimba lutegekeddwa okw'okulimba okw'amaanyi ennyo, n'obulimba buzimbiddwa dda, kubanga emisingi gyabwo butali butuukirivu n'obulimba obungi. Obulimba buno busibye amakanda mu byenjigiriza byammwe eby'Abayesuwiita. Kubanga ensi yonna ekyamiziddwa obulimba bwabwe; n'abalonde bennyini bandikyamiziddwa singa kiyinzika. N'olwekyo omutego gweteeseteese okutega, n'ekintu kyokka ekiguziyiza ye muziyiza, aliggibwawo amangu ddala era mu bwangu. Olwo olutalo olunene ne lubalukawo okwetooloola ensi yonna ng'abeesigwa bange bakuŋŋaanyizibwa gyendi mu ggulu. Olwo okuva mu vvu kulivaamu ensolo n'omunnabbi ow'obulimba, abaliba ab'enkizo mu bulimba obujja.

Kale temukkiririza bulimba bwabwe, mmwe ab'amaanyi bange, kubanga muli bantu ba njawulo era nabayita ne mbateekako akabonero ak'okulaga nti muli ba Katonda omulamu, obusungu bwa Katonda buleme okubakwatako. Nja kubatuma nga muli b'amaanyi bange. Muliba maloboozi gange amangi agakaabira mu ddungu wonna w'omulitumibwa, era mbatuma okukuŋŋaanya endiga zange ezisigaddewo ezibuze.

Kubanga ekibiina ekinene kijja kuva mu kiseera ky'okubonaabona kwa Yakobo, era mujja kukola ebikolwa eby'amaanyi n'okulagira ebitasoboka, kubanga nja kuwa obubonero bungi obw'amaanyi, ebyamagero, n'ebikolwa eby'ekyewuunyo bikolebwe emikono gyammwe. Mujja kukola ebyamagero eby'amaanyi eby'edda ebyawandiikibwa mu Byawandiikibwa Ebitukuvu, ddala mu maanyi ga Eriya, wabula emirimu egy'amaanyi egisingawo n'egisingawo gye mujja okukola okusinga egyawandiikibwa ku ye ow'edda.

Kubanga Ebyawandiikibwa bigamba nti oyo akkiririza mu nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, tajja kukoma ku kukola mirimu gye nnakola ku nsi mu buweereza bwange e Buyudaaya ng'omwana gw'endiga gwa Katonda, naye emirimu egy'amaanyi okusinga n'egyo gye nnakola gye mujja okukola, kubanga nja kuwa obubonero obw'amaanyi, ebyamagero, n'ebikolwa eby'ekyewuunyo mu mikono gyammwe nga mulagira ebitasoboka olw'okukkiriza mu nze. Kale mutunuulirenga nze bulijjo. Era amaso gammwe mugatereke ku nze, kubanga nja kubakulembera ddaala ku ddaala. Okutambula kwammwe kuliba kwa magezi, era muliba n'olulimi lw'ab'amagezi, era nze nja kuba amaanyi gammwe n'obulokozi bwammwe, kubanga mu nze, Kristo Yesu, mwe muli okujjuulira kwonna.

Era mbawabula mmwe mwenna nti mu bbanga ttono nnyo ssente zijja kufuuka ezitaliiko mugaso, era ssente zokka ezirikkirizibwa zijja kwetaagisa akabonero okusobola okutuuka ku bintu by'ensi. Temutwala kabonero, kubanga kyandisinzeeko obulungi okufa mu bulamu buno (nga temwagala bulamu bwammwe okutuuka ku nkomerero) okusinga okutwala akabonero ne musuulibwa mu nnyanja y'omuliro etaggwaawo eyateekerwateekerwa Omulyolyomi ne bamalayika be, kubanga ebiseera byabwe bituuse; kyokka temubegattako. Kubanga balisuubiza ebyo ebirabika ng'ebisuubizo ebinene era eby'omuwendo, naye ebigambo byabwe bya bulimba, n'obusagwa buli mu bigambo byabwe, n'ebisuubizo byabwe bya bulimba era bijjudde ebintu eby'omuwendo omubi byonna. Tebalimala gasobola kutuukiriza bisuubizo byabwe, kubanga omusango gwabwe gujja; tebalina mirembe, n'emirembe giriggibwawo ddala ku nsi.

Obubi bw'abantu bujja kuba butalina nkomerero ddala; era bangi, omuziyiza bw'aliggibwawo, balyewaayo emirembe gyonna eri eddalu n'obubi. Okuggyako nti oyo abeerawo ku kitala ateekwa okuttibwa n'ekitala, era ddala baligwa ku kkono ne ku ddyo mu muwendo munene, naye tekiribatuukako nga muntambulira; kyokka temwagala bulamu bwammwe okutuusa ku nkomerero era mugoberere ekyokulabirako kyange eky'obuweereza n'okwewaayo awatali kwerowoozaako. Kubanga empeera nnene ejja kuba yammwe olw'obuwulize n'obwesigwa.

Kale okwegomba kw'abantu n'obubi bujja kuba butalina nkomerero ddala, n'Omulyolyomi ne bamalayika be baliba tebalina nkomerero, n'emizizo gyabwe gyonna egitali mirungi giriba gitalina nkomerero, era balijja ng'abayambi b'edda. Obulimba bwabwe bunene nnyo era bulina amakanda ag'ebuziba aganaatera okusikukulwa ddala. Si bakatonda, era si bitonde bikulaakulanye bya kisa, wabula balimba, era beeyonoonyeyo mu ngeri zonna. Kale baliba tebalina nkomerero ddala, n'okujjuulira kw'obwakabaka bwa Setaani kulisumululwa okuva mu bunnya, kubanga kuliggulwawo.

Kino kijja kukolebwa okulaga omuntu owa bulijjo ky'alonze n'okuwa omukisa ogusembayo ogw'okwenenya; kubanga obubi tebulikyusibwa nate era tebulikwekeddwa, na buli kyama kirijja mu musana. N'obulimba bw'obukumpanya bwabwe obunene n'obulimba bwabwe obw'amaanyi biryanjulwa byonna birabe, kubanga Yakuwa yakirangirira mu Byawandiikibwa Ebitukuvu, era ekyo Yakuwa ky'alangirira tewali ayinza kukikyusa.

Kale okwawulibwa okusembayo kulibaawo, n'abangi ku beemalira be njagala balirekebwa eri ebigezo n'okukemebwa okujja, n'olwekyo balyoke balokolebwe. Kubanga tewakyali biseera, n'ekisa kyange n'okusaasira kwange tebirikyagabibwa mu ngeri y'emu, era tewakyali biseera bya makubo ag'ekivuguumize. Buli omu alina okukola ekiragiro; n'abatya n'abatakkiriza baliba balonze Setaani n'obwakabaka bwe bwe bandibadde basobola okunnonako nze, ye Yesu Kristo, omwana wa Katonda eyajja mu mubiri era ne nneewaayo ng'eky'Okuyitako ekituukiridde olw'ebibi byonna eby'abantu omulundi gumu okutuusa ku nkomerero. Abangi bajja kusigala nga bakakanyavu ne bagaana omukono gwange ogw'obuyambi mu kiseera eky'obwetaavu obunene, kubanga obuyambi bwange bwa lubeerera era bwa muyaga ogutaggwaawo ogw'empeera ennene, okusaasira, ekisa, n'okwagala.

Naye, abasinga obungi mu nsi balikkiririza mu bulimba era balyewuunya ensolo, nga bagamba nti, ani afaanana ng'ensolo? Bajja kwesiga okulumwa omusota oguluma ogw'obusagwa obutayinza kuwonyezebwa, kubanga gwa lubeerera. Omuntu okulonda obubi mu biseera eby'okulonda kijja kuba kiragiro kya lubeerera, n'abo abakola ekiragiro ekyo balisalirwa omusango omunene.

Abo abalonda obubi ku bwabwe balyananizibwa ku buli kirowoozo ekibi, buli kigambo ekibi, na buli kikolwa ekibi kye bakola mu bubi ku nsi, kubanga ebitabo biriggulwawo era teribaawo kusaasira. Era tewali muntu aliyimirirawo mu butuukirivu bwe, kubanga omuntu talina butuukirivu bwe; kubanga omusota gwaboonoona okuva ku lubereberye, n'ennaku zaagwo ez'okwonoona zinaatera okukoma. Bwe kityo Yakuwa n'antuma nze, omwana we omu yekka, ng'endagaano y'emirembe eri omuntu owa bulijjo, n'omuntu owa bulijjo agaanyi emirembe egitaggwaawo kubanga omuntu owa bulijjo ayagala obubi.

Omusango omunene gunaatera okuteebwa ku bwakabaka bwa Setaani. Omusango guno gujja kweyongera okuba omubi ennyo, n'ebikolwa ebikolebwa mu musango bijja kweyongera okuba ebibi ennyo. Olubereberye luligattibwako olubereberye n'okubonyaabonyezebwa kuligattibwako okubonyaabonyezebwa eri bonna abagaana okwenenya ebikolwa byabwe ebibi. Kubanga abangi tebalireka kukola mirimu gya mikono gyabwe, nga tebasinzanga bidayimooni, ne bifaananyi bya zzaabu, na ffeeza, na kikomo, na mayinja, na miti: ebitayinza kulaba, wadde okuwulira, wadde okutambula: So tebalireka kutta kwabwe, newakubadde obulogo bwabwe, newakubadde obwenzi bwabwe, newakubadde obubbi bwabwe.

Kubanga abagwa si bakatonda, wabula balimba, abeefuula, abazannyi, n'abakumpanya. Baleeta kufa n'okuzikirira kwokka eri abo abagoberera, n'abo bonna abakyamiziddwa baligondererwa ddala, kubanga bye basuubira birizikirira mu nnyanja ey'omuliro omunene omutali ssuubi. Era nze, Kristo Yesu, nja kusattulula ddala obwakabaka bwa Setaani mu musango omunene, kubanga omusango omunene guliteebwa ku bo bonna, era nze ndi Mpologoma ey'omu kika kya Yuda.

Bwe kityo buli kabonero kaligattako omusango ku musango, na buli kkondeere lirigattako omusango ku musango, buli nnaku erigattako nnaku ku nnaku, na buli kibya kijja n'obusungu bwa Katonda obujjuvu, ddala obusungu ku busungu. Obwakabaka bwa Setaani bujja kubonaabona nnyo, n'obwakabaka bwe bujja kuzikirizibwa ddala ne busaanyizibwawo ddala.

Kubanga balyekuŋŋaanya era balyeteeka mu ntegeka okulwanyisa okujja kwa Mukama, kubanga ddala nja kujja. Okuva ku ntandikwa y'okubonaabona kwa Yakobo, walibaawo ennaku musanvu ez'emyaka, kubanga ssabbiiti ya Danyeri ey'amakumi musanvu erituukirizibwa okuzikiriza obutali butuukirivu n'okulokola Isiraeri. Kubanga nja kulongoosa Isiraeri okuva mu bubi obuwambye ensi.

Kubanga Yakuwa oba nze kennyini, Yeshua HaMashiach, saatuma wadde okuteekawo abafuzi baabwe, kubanga bafugira mu butali butuukirivu n'okutta abantu kwabwe kwa muzizo, n'abantu bange basasula omuwendo gw'entalo zaabwe. Naye bangi nnyo mu Kkanisa yange bakkiririza mu bulimba bwabwe obw'Abaziyoni, kale mutegeere nti nze siri Muziyoni, kubanga bo balowooza nti Abayudaaya be basinga, era bali masinde, ezzadde lya Setaani yennyini; era nja kubasimbula mu nsi, kubanga beeyita bannannyini mugabo gwange, naye njija kutwala ekyange. Kubanga nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, ddala nze, nnina ekiwandiiko ky'ensi; kubanga nakigula n'omusaayi gwange ogutalimu kibi, era nnina ebisumuluzo by'okufa n'emagombe. Era nsasulidde ebibi by'abantu, na buli alikoowoola erinnya lya Mukama alirokolebwa.

Kale ensi yange n'okujjuulira kwayo, era nja kulongoosa ensi okuva mu bubi bwabwe, n'ekkuŋŋaaniro lya Setaani lirizikirizibwa ddala, kale nja kuleeta endagaano empya ku nsi ne nfugira mu butuukirivu. Kubanga ng'ensi bw'eyongera okuba enzikivu era embi buli sekonda, okujja kwange okw'okubiri kusembera buli sekonda.

Mubeere n'essuubi ddene ery'okujja kwange; kubanga nja kujja ne nnyasa eggulu n'eggye ly'omu ggulu, ddala eggye eritaliiko nfaananyi, kubanga eggye nga lyange teryaliwo, eggye eritasobola kuwangulwa eririmu abo abalamu emirembe ne batayinza kuttibwa wadde okuwangulwa. Kubanga Yakuwa yampa amaanyi olw'ekigendererwa ekyo, era nja kuba nabawa amaanyi ne mbawa empeera olw'obwesigwa bwabwe n'okunjagala kwabwe n'erinnya lyange, ye Yesu Kristo! Kubanga buli ayagala era aweereza Yeshua HaMashiach ayagala era aweereza ne Yakuwa, kubanga tewali asinga Katonda omunene ow'emirembe n'emirembe owa Isiraeri! Buli awakana Omwana, oyo talina Kitaffe; era nze ndi Yesu Kristo omu yekka eyazaalibwa Kitaffe eyeewaayo olw'ebibi bya bonna, era ndi kabona asinga obukulu emirembe gyonna okusinziira ku ntegeka ya Merukizeddeeki.

Era nja kuwangula ddala ogusota, ensolo, n'omunnabbi ow'obulimba. Kubanga ensolo n'omunnabbi ow'obulimba balisuulibwa nga balamu mu nnyanja eyo eyaka eyaka ennimi z'omuliro ez'ebbululu ezitasobola kuzikirizibwa era omutalibaawo na sekonda emu ey'okuwummula. Naye ebikolwa byabwe byonna ebibi biribaddira emirembe gyonna. Okubonaabona kwonna kwe baaleeta kulisasulwa emirembe gyonna, kubanga Yakuwa alisasula buli kikolwa ekibi okutuuka ku kasente akasembayo mu bifuba byabwe ebibi, era balisasulira emirembe gyonna olw'emisango gyabwe.

Kale Setaani alisibibwa mu bunnya okumala emyaka lukumi, kubanga bwe kityo bwe kyalangirirwa Yakuwa. Nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, nja kufuga ensi mu butuukirivu okumala olunaku lumu. Kubanga eri Yakuwa olunaku lulinga emyaka lukumi n'emyaka lukumi ng'olunaku lumu. Era nja kufugira ku ntebe ya Dawudi, n'obufuzi bwange bulibeera mu Isiraeri okutuukiriza ebisuubizo ebyakolebwa Yakuwa eri Bakadde, n'olunaku lwange luli kumpi.

Naye okusooka omusango guteekwa okujja ku nsi n'ensi erirongoosebwa mu buli kintu ekibi. Kubanga tewali kintu kibi kirikkirizibwa mu bwakabaka bwange, omuliba emirembe n'obutebenkevu nga bwe byasuubizibwa Yakuwa yennyini. N'empologoma erigalamira wamu n'omwana gw'endiga. Nze, ddala nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, ntegeeza ebigambo nga tebinnabaawo, mulyoke mumanye nti Katonda owa Isiraeri azze okukuŋŋaanya endiga ze ezibuze ez'omu nnyumba ya Isiraeri eri nze, Omusumba Omukulu, era nja kukuba amawanga n'omuggo ogw'ekyuma, era nja kujeemula ddala ekitonde kya Kitaffe mu butuukirivu n'obutukuvu obw'amazima. Tewakyali biseera.

Nze Yeshua HaMashiach, Masiya ow'amazima eri abantu bange Isiraeri.

Nze oyo alifuga ensi mu butuukirivu okuva ku Ntebe ya Dawudi.

Nze oyo alirabikira mu bire n'eggye ly'omu ggulu, kubanga nze ndiriragira.

Nze oyo alikuŋŋaanya abasigaddewo bange ng'ekibiina ekinene okuva mu kibonyoobonyo ekijja.

Nze oyo akema emitima gy'abantu, era nja kwawula endiga ku mbuzi.

Nze Mukama w'amakungula, n'ebiseera by'amakungula bituuse.

Nze Mukama wa Bakama ne Kabaka wa Bakabaka, era ani ayinza okuyimirirawo?

Laba olunaku lwa Mukama lunaatera okubalukawo ku nsi mu kabi n'omusango. Kubanga olunaku olunene olw'obusungu bwe lutuuse, era ani alisobola okuyimirirawo?
