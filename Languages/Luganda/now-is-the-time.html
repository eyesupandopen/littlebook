Obujulirwa obubiri: Isirayiri
Ekiwoomerera Ekikaawa
Agusito 24, 2024

Obudde bwe Buno

Obudde butuuse, baana bange, obudde butuuse. Obudde butuuse obw'okudda eka, ekkondeere bwe ririvuga n'ekkanisa yange n'ekuŋŋaanyizibwa, Yakuwa gy'alindiridde n'essanyu mu mutima gwe olw'okutuuka kwammwe okw'amangu. Obudde butuuse obw'okudda eka, eddoboozi ly'ekkondeere bwe ririyita abaana b'Oyo Ali Waggulu Ennyo okugenda awali bamalayika we babeera era we batambulira. Obudde butuuse, baana bange, okubakuŋŋaanya okuva ku nsonda ennya z'ensi, na ddala n'abo bonna abaafa nga bali mu Kristo be balisooka okuzuukira, nammwe abakyali abalamu era abalisigalawo mulitwalibwa wamu nabo mu bire. Kubanga nze Kristo Yesu ndi Mukama wammwe era Omulokozi omulamu, era mbayita okujja mu maka gammwe ag'omu ggulu awaalindiridde empeera zammwe, gye mbategekererezza ekifo mu nnyumba ya Kitange, kubanga mu nnyumba ya Kitange mulimu ebifo bingi. Mbayita okujja eka okuyimirira mu maaso ga Mukama wammwe, bonna balyoke baweebwe ebintu ebyakolebwa nga bali mu mubiri, oba birungi oba bibi, bonna baweebwe empeera yaabwe okuva eri Mukama, era bonna batwale ebifo byabwe mu nteeko ez'omu ggulu ezibategekerwa buli omu.

Laba! Nze Yeshua HaMashiach, era nze omugole aliko ajja okukuŋŋaanya omugole wange kubanga obudde bw'embaga yaffe butuuse, lwe ndiragaana endagaano eteriggwaawo n'abalonde bange, beebo abetegekedde, era be nnalongoosezza mu musaayi gwange ogutangirira kubanga bajja gye ndi n'omutima gwabwe gwonna ne bagumpa mu kwagala n'okwewaayo. Newankubadde tebaatambulira mu butuukirivu, era bangi ne bawangula ebibi eby'entiisa, beenaaza ddala mu musaayi gw'Omwana gw'endiga, era kaakano bayimiridde mu maaso gange nga balongoosebwa ddala okuva mu bibi byabwe era bali balongoofu ng'abawala abatamanyi musajja era abeezibidde omubi. Laba! Abawanguzi bange be nnasonyiwa ebibi byonna era be nnafuka amafuta mu ttaala zaabwe kubanga bajja gye ndi mu biseera byabwe eby'okwetaaga nange ne mbalaga engeri y'okusalako entambi zaabwe era ne mbawonya ne mbeeyawulira ddala, beebo bonna Yakuwa be yalonda okuva ensi nga tenassibwako misingi okuba omugole wange n'okubeera nange emirembe gyonna, era balifugira wamu nange nga Bakabaka era Bakabaka-bakabona emirembe gyonna kubanga bwe batyo bwe bayitibwa, abatukuzibwa era abalonde mu nze, Kristo Yesu Mukama waabwe eyazuukira.

N'abange emitwalo kkumi n'ena mu enkumi nnya (144,000) balikyusibwa ne bateekebwako akabonero ne batondebwa buto ddala okutuukiriza ebigendererwa bye baayitirwa era bye bategekerwa. Mazima, mazima, buli mmeeme eyakkiriza erinnya lyange ery'amaanyi n'enfuula Mukama waayo ow'emirembe n'emirembe erikyusibwa ddala n'etondebwa obuggya ng'ekitonde kipya, era balifaananyizibwa nga bwe nfaanana. So tebaliddayo kwonoona, oba lwa malala oba lwa butatya Katonda so tebaliddayo kukolimirwa, so n'obulwadde oba obulumi, oba okufa oba okunakuwala tebiriddayo kubakwatako kubanga eby'olubereberye biweddewo era omusana ogw'amazima kaakano gwaka. Era nze Kristo Yesu, kaakano eyagenda okweyanjulira Yakobo na buli alikoowoola erinnya lyange ery'amaanyi okutuusa ku nkomerero alirokolebwa, na ddala abatuuze bonna ab'oku nsi abalonderwa obulamu obutaggwaawo era walibaawo ekibiina kinene ekirinkkiriza nga Messiya waabwe era ndiba mbawonyezza ddala.

Nzija okubaggyayo, baagalwa bange, era obudde bwe buno. Nnindiridde ekiseera kyonna eky'ekisa era nnokodde bangi, era bangi balizuukizibwa mu bulamu obuggya mu nze, Kristo Yesu Mukama waabwe ow'emirembe, n'abafu mu Kristo be balisooka okuzuukira, n'ekkondeere erisembayo liri butegefu kaakano. Obubonero obw'amagero n'ebyamawnge bingi bibisse ku nsi mu kitiibwa kyabyo era obubonero tebukoma okwetooloola ensi yonna kaakano obulangirira okulabika kwange, obulangirira embaga y'Omwana gw'endiga n'omugole we omwesigwa, n'ebisenge by'embaga mu ggulu bitegekeddwa era biri butegefu.

N'olwekyo, waliwo embaga ey'omu ggulu erindiridde abeesigwa bange, abayite, n'abalonde, era kaakano bwe budde obw'okuwummuliramu ddala emirimu gyabwe gye baakolera mu nsi mu nnyumba zaabwe ez'ebbumba, era bakoleredde mu mubiri ng'abakozi bannange era bakuŋŋaanyizza ne baleeta bangi gye ndi, Mukama wange Yesu Kristo balyoke bafune obulokozi obutaggwaawo emirembe gyonna. Kubanga newankubadde emibiri gyabwe gyayonoononwa mu kibi Setaani, nnabawa ekigendererwa ne mbayita okuvaamu ddala obutaliimu bwe baasalirwa olw'ekibi.

N'olwekyo balina obugagga buno mu bibya by'ebbumba nti amaanyi ga Katonda gabeeramu ddala era gatuulamu, era baakola emirimu mingi egy'amaanyi mu linnya lyange, era nze eyabawa amaanyi okufuuka abasika b'obulokozi n'abasika b'obulamu obutaggwaawo era mbanunudde era mbawadde omusingo ogwo ogw'emirembe mu kuzaalibwa obuggya era mbataddeko akabonero n'omwoyo oyo omutukuvu ow'ekisuubizo, na ddala akabonero ako akatemenyeka ak'ekisuubizo. Era kaakano bwe budde obw'okununula ddala omugule, beebo bonna abamfuula Mukama okuva mu mutima ogujjudde okukkiriza nga bamanyi nti Yakuwa yanzuukiza mu bafu obutaddayo kufa. Kaakano bwe budde obw'okununulibwa kwabwe okujjuvu lwe baliweebwa ddala omusingo gwe baafuna okuva gye ndi mu kuzaalibwa obuggya okwa Kristo mu mmwe, essuubi ery'ekitiibwa!

Laba! Wulira eddoboozi lyange otegeere ebigambo byange era omanye ng'ebiseera byo eby'omu maaso eby'ekitiibwa tebiri wala myaka, so tebitegekeddwa kujja mu mayubiri agajja, naye biri ku ggwe, baagalwa bange, era mangu ago mu kaseera akataggwaawo mwenna mulikyusibwa era obudde obw'enkyukakyuka bwe buno. Mulisanga nga mukyusiddwa mangu ago ne mufuuka ab'ekitiibwa eky'emirembe, era ndibambaza ebyambalo ebitayonooneka ebitayinza kucankalanako. Mulisanga nga muyimiridde mangu ago mu kisenge kya Yakuwa eky'obwakabaka, na ddala mu kitiibwa n'obulungi bwa Patri w'Omusana obutaggwaawo, era muliryako emirembe gyonna ku buzaale bwe obutaggwaawo kubanga muli baana ba Katonda omulamu, na ddala mwenna abaagibwa mu Adamu ne mununulibwa okuva mu ttaka eddungi.

Era gwali musaayi gwange ogutangirira ogwayiibwa ku nsi e Gologoosa ogwakakasa obulokozi bwammwe omulundi gumu, emirembe n'emirembe bwe nnawaayo obulamu bwange n'ebigambo nti "Kiwedde!" Era nze Kristo Yesu, ndi mirembe gyonna eyazuukizibwa era n'alinnyisibwa Mukama wa bakama era Kabaka wa bakabaka kubanga Yakuwa yanzuukiza mu bafu obutaddayo kufa! Nze Adamu ow'oluvannyuma, era nze omwana w'omuntu eyayanunula abaana b'abantu abaakoowoola erinnya lyange n'abafuula abaana ba Katonda ab'emirembe abalibeera emirembe gyonna mu kitiibwa ekitaggwaawo n'obulungi obw'emirembe era emirembe gyonna Yakuwa aligulumizibwa mu bo, nabo mu Yakuwa kubanga mu nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, mwe mutuula okujjudde kwonna emirembe gyonna, era Yakuwa aliba byonna mu byonna era emirembe gyonna tulitambulira wamu ng'ekitonde kimu kipya n'omusajja omu omupya. Nammwe, abayite bange abalonde era abeesigwa, mununuddwa emirembe gyonna, era muli kumpi n'okutegeera obulamu bwammwe obw'amazima kubanga obulamu bwammwe bwa mirembe na mirembe. N'olwekyo tegeera nti mwafa, n'obulamu bwammwe bukwekeddwa nange, Kristo Yesu, mu Yakuwa. Nze Kristo Yesu, obulamu bwammwe, bwe ndirabika, n'olwo nammwe mulirabikira wamu nange mu kitiibwa. Temumanyi ng'obuntu bwammwe obw'amazima bukwekeddwa mu nze, Kristo Yesu?

Kati lwaki munakuwadde, mmwe abalonde bange, abeesigwa bange, Ekkanisa yange era n'Omugole wange? Nzija okubaggyayo era laba, nsemberera okubaggyayo. Era ndibakuŋŋaanya mangu ago gye ndi. Ensi bw'eneeyiringitira mu musango n'entalo, eggulu lirijaguza olw'okubeerawo kwammwe era walibaawo okujaguza okunene abaana ba Katonda lwe balibeera emirembe gyonna nga bagattiddwa ne Mukama wa bakama era Kabaka wa bakabaka waabwe mu kitiibwa ekitaggwaawo n'obulamu obuggya bwe bulibeerako bonna. So tewalibaawo nate kwawukana wakati w'abo abanunulibwa n'omusaayi gw'Omwana gw'endiga ne Kitabwe ne Katonda waabwe.

Kubanga kaakano bwe budde Yakuwa bw'alonze okutandika okweyanjulira ddala ebitonde bye era okweyanjulira kwe okujjuvu kutandikira ku nnyumba ya Katonda, era ali kumpi okweyanjulira ddala abalonde be n'abeesigwa be, na ddala ebibala ebibereberye, kubanga abo be nkuŋŋaanya gye ndi ng'ekkondeere erisembayo livuze be bibala ebibereberye okuva mu bafu. Era kaakano bwe budde obw'okumanya kwabwe okujjuvu, na ddala nga mwenna mumanyiddwa ddala nze ne Kitange. N'olwekyo ntegeeza okulabika kwange era mbakubiriza mmwe abeesigwa bange okweyongera okwetegekera okulabika kwange, kubanga kaakano bwe budde ng'omusango gutandikira ku nnyumba ya Katonda. Era mangu ago, gulibaawo.

Laba! Temutya mbuyaga ezijja bangi ku mmwe ze mulaba nga zikuŋŋaana: embuyaga z'entalo, embuyaga z'akabi akasemberedde, embuyaga z'obutwaavu obw'obutonde ebigenda okubaawo ddala. Tegeerera nti tewali kintu kyonna kiribakola kabi, newankubadde bangi babeera mu bikemo ne mu ntalo ez'eby'omwoyo eziwangulirwa ddala mu nze, Kristo Yesu. Laba! Obudde bw'okulabika kwange eri abeesigwa bange butuuse era ng'obulumi bw'okuzaala tebukoma era mangu ago balikuŋŋaanyizibwa ne banyagibwa okuva mu musango ogutali gwabwe.

Mbayita okujja mu maka gammwe ag'omu ggulu awaalindiridde empeera zammwe.

Mbayita okudda eka okuyimirira mu maaso ga Mukama wammwe, mulyoke muweebwe ebintu ebyakolebwa nga muli mu mubiri.

Nze Yeshua HaMashiach.

Nze omugole aliko ajja okukuŋŋaanya omugole wange kubanga olunaku lw'embaga lusembedde.

Nze eyabawa amaanyi okufuuka abasika b'obulokozi n'abasika b'obulamu obutaggwaawo.

Nzija okubaggyayo okubanyagira mu bulinzi bw'omu ggulu era obudde bwe buno.

Nze Yeshua HaMashiach ntumye ebigambo bino okuyitira mu muweereza wange, era okuyitira mu muweereza wange ebigambo bino bitumiddwa.

Musigale nga mwetegefu era temuleka bifo byammwe era mweyongere okuntunuulira. Temuba batakkiriza, naye mutunule era mutegeere nti obubonero bw'okulabika kwange buli ku nsi yonna, n'olwo mangu ago muliyimirira mu kubeerawo okutaggwaawo okw'OWOOMPONNA NZE NDI!
