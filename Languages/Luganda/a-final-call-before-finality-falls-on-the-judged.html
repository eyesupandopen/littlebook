Okuyita Okusembayo Ng'enkomerero Tenagwa ku Basaliddwa Omusango

Laba! NZE YAHWEH, NZE ALIWO OW'AMAANYI, era nzize okuweereza ebigambo byange eby'okwagala n'okuyita okusembayo eri abo abakwatiddwa enkomerero y'omusango bwe baba nga bagaanyi okuyita kwange okusembayo. Newankubadde, bonna abakoowoola erinnya lya Yesu okutuusa ku nkomerero balifuna obulokozi obw'olubeerera n'empeera enkulu, abasajja bangi nnyo baligwa mu musango olw'ebibi byabwe n'obukakanyavu bw'emitima gyabwe. Kubanga, NZE nze anenya abatamaliirivu okwenenya, era abo abagaana okwenenya, ŋŋenda mu maaso okubanenya okutuusa emitima gyabwe lwe gikaluba ddala ne batwalibwa mu musango.

Kyenvudde nzija okuwa obubaka buno mu kwagala kwange eri mmwe, ne mpeereza ekisa kyange ekitagwanidde eri ensi esaliddwa omusango nate mu kugezaako kwange okugonza emitima gyammwe emijeemu okukkiriza obulokozi; oba nti mu bukyayi bwammwe n'obujeemu mulekebwe mu mbeera ey'okugaana okusembayo, nga mwengeredde era nga mwetegefu okusalirwa omusango ogw'enkomerero. Mutegeere, tewakyali kiseera mu biseera byange eby'ekisa n'okusaasira eby'okwenenya mu bulamu bwonna olw'entegeka zange ez'obulokozi eri abantu bonna abalijja okufuna obulokozi obujjuvu mu biseera byabwe n'entegeka zaabwe eri abo abalikkiriza okwagala kwange eri bo. Kyenvudde, ebiseera byammwe biri wano era okuyita okusembayo kati kusindikiddwa. Selah.

Muwulire, O' Muwulire eddoboozi lya Katonda Omukulu Ow'olubeerera Ow'obutuukirivu, ye Yahweh, NZE ALIWO OW'AMAANYI, era ali Katonda Ow'olubeerera ow'Obutuukirivu era mutegeere amakulu g'ebyo bye njogera, era mumanye nti eddoboozi lyange liringa eddoboozi ly'amazzi amangi agkulukuta era ag'obulamu, era mutegeere nti eddoboozi lyange liwuuma ng'eddoboozi ly'okubwatuka okungi, (n'okubwatuka kw'okubwatuka) mu busungu bwange obw'obutuukirivu eri abatuula ku nsi abagibeeramu mu kutawulira okungi n'obutali butuukirivu n'ekibi ekingi.

Laba! NZE ALIWO OW'AMAANYI, era nnatonda eggulu n'ensi n'ebigirimu byonna, era mu ntegeka yange ey'obutuukirivu nakola eggulu n'ensi ebituukiridde mu makubo gange ag'obutuukirivu, ne mbitonda mu butuukirivu obw'emirembe era obw'obutuukirivu ebitonde byange byonna mwe byali birina okubeerera mu butuukirivu obw'emirembe n'okwagala n'okuweerezanga mu kwagalana. Era mu kwagala kwabwe eri Kitabwe era omutonzi waabwe, bandigonze eddoboozi lyange n'ebiragiro byange eri obulamu bwabwe ddala balyoke beetegeere nga bwe baatondebwa era n'okutegeera amakubo gange. Naye, obutali butuukirivu bwalabika mu Heylel, ye yagwa okuva mu butuukirivu obw'ekitiibwa, ye eyatondebwa mu kitiibwa eky'ekitalo era eky'entiisa, n'alyoka agwa ku nsi, ng'akyeseddwamu ekibi n'ekizikiza okubeera mu kifaananyi ky'omusota abakolimirwa olw'obutawulira bwa Serafimu. Selah.

Olw'amazima ag'olubeerera, amakubo gange gali waggulu okusinga amakubo g'omuntu, era NZE nze eyeetoolooza entebe yange ey'obwakabaka n'enkumi kkumi emirundi enkumi kkumi, era omuwendo gwa bamalayika ogutabalika abasinga mu magezi, mu maanyi, ne mu bukulu, era abagala Yahweh era abagala okuyiga amakubo ga Yahweh, NZE ALIWO OW'AMAANYI, mu kuwulira okw'amaanyi era okw'obwesigwa obw'olubeerera: Selah.

Mazima, mu mazima amakulu era ag'olubeerera, nabatonda nga ba maanyi era ba kitiibwa ne nbambaza okumasamasa okwaka, n'obutuukirivu obw'olubeerera, n'ekitiibwa eky'olubeerera. Mazima, mu mazima amakulu era ag'olubeerera, tebaaliiko kye beetaaga wadde mu nsonga entonotono; era bampereza n'ebitonde byange mu bujjuvu mu mitima gyabwe egy'okwagala eri Kitabwe era omutonzi waabwe, ye Elowimu, mu kuwulira kwabwe eri Kristo Yesu.

(Ye oyo, Yahweh, NZE ALIWO Omukulu era ow'olubeerera gw'atadde ebintu byonna mu ggulu ne ku nsi mu mikono gye egy'amaanyi era egy'olubeerera era egy'obuwulize.)

Kyova omanya amazima ag'olubeerera nti Kristo Yesu ye Mukama wa bakama era Kabaka wa bakabaka era ye alireeta emirembe mu ggulu ne ku nsi era ye alidda mu mukono gwange ogw'ekitiibwa (mu kwagala kwe eri Kitaawe era Omutonzi we, Elowimu, ye NZE ALIWO OW'AMAANYI) Obwakabaka obugonzeddwa ku nkomerero ya byonna emikono gye egy'amaanyi bwe giriba gimalirizza ebintu byonna ebyawandiikibwa era ebyalangirirwa ebikwata ku Mukama ow'Obutuukirivu ow'olubeerera! N'omwana w'omuntu oyo bw'aliba ng'amalirizza ebintu byonna mu maanyi amakulu, ne mu kwagala okusingawo. Era ku kiseera ekyo, nange ndiba mmalirizza mu maanyi gange okuyita mu mikono gye egy'amaanyi n'essaddaaka ebintu byonna ebyaweebwa mu ntegeka zange ez'olubeerera n'ebyama bye yalina okutuukiriza.

Noolwekyo, nkuwa okutegeera, nti NZE nze alina amaanyi n'obuyinza okutegeera ddala, okutuukiriza, okumanya mu bujjuvu, n'okufuga buli kamu ku buli kitundu kyonna eky'ekitonde kyange k'ebe kiŋŋana kitya oba nga kirabika kya maanyi kitya okutuuka ne ku katono ennyo, era tewali kiyinza kukwekebwa mu maaso gange! NZE nze ayinza okutuukiriza ebintu byonna amangu ago mu maanyi gange n'amaanyi gange ag'olubeerera. Naye, era nze nkola ebintu byonna ng'okuteesa okw'okwagala kwange bwe kuli era ateetaaga muwi wa magezi, kyenvudde, entegeka zange n'ebyama byange birituukirizibwa mu bujjuvu mu biseera bino eby'okutuukirizibwa kwabyo, ye kiseera kya KAATI.

Wabula, mu kwagala kwange eri ekitonde kyange n'okwegomba kwange okuba n'abaana n'amaka, ntuma abantu n'ebiramu eby'enjawulo (nga balina eddembe ly'okweloboza) olw'ebigendererwa byange eby'enjawulo eby'olubeerera ebiweebwa abawulize mu buweereza obukulu n'ekitiibwa, era ne mbawa amaanyi okusinziira ku ekyo. Kyenvudde, nkuwa amagezi okuyiga amakubo gange mu bulokozi oba okulonda ennyanja y'omuliro ezikiriza emirembe gyonna. Naye manya, Ggwe omuntu agudde, olina okulonda okwo okugenda okukyuka amangu ago ne kufuuka nti walina okulonda, era bonna abalironda obubi mu nkomerero enkomerero bw'erijja, tebalyewona misango gyabwe emirembe n'emirembe. Selah.

Ggwe ani, Ggwe omuntu agudde, ggwe eyeegatta ku bujeemu obutaliiko ntegeerera era obusiru era obw'edalu obw'abagudde mu butali butuukirivu bwonna, mu mululu, mu kwegomba, ne mu bukyamu okwegulumiza mu malala okulwanyisa Katonda Omukulu era ow'olubeerera eyakutonda olw'ebigendererwa byange n'essanyu lyange?

Ggwe ani, Ggwe omuntu agudde, nti mu butamanya bwo n'okwegomba kwo oyinza otya okusitula ekisinziiro kyo eri Katonda omukulu ow'olubeerera ow'Eggulu ly'Amaggulu (YE eyakuwa obulamu) mu makubo ga Basitaani? Kyova otegeera ebigendererwa byange n'ekiruubirirwa kyange kubanga NZE nkuweereza ebigambo bino eri GGWE ng'okuyita kwange okusembayo eri abangi ku mmwe abalowooza mu busiru nti mulina obudde bwe mutalina, era NZE nkawa n'ebigambo by'okulabula mu kwagala kwange okutaggwawo eri mmwe nga mbajjukiza nate ebyalangirirwa by'omusango n'akabi ebimaze okuweebwa bye mwanyooma mu bujjuvu ebyakolebwa Kristo Yesu ku ntebe y'obwakabaka n'amaanyi ga Yahweh, era n'ebyalangirirwa Yahweh yennyini, ye NZE ALIWO OW'AMAANYI!

(Totegeera nti ebyalangirirwa byange tebiyinza kukyusibwa?)

Naye, munyooma n'ebyo ebyayogerwa okubwatuka omusanvu bye nabikkulira, ne ndagira Yokaana Omubikkulirwa okubiteeka akabonero mu kiseera kye okutuusa ebiseera ebituufu eby'okubikkulwa era nga by'ebiseera bya kaakati. (Era birigenda mu maaso okutuusa obujulizi bw'abajulizi bange ababiri lwe buliggwaako mu musango ogw'enkomerero, nga bukyali ekibonyoobonyo ekisinga obunene kigwe mu bujjuvu ku batali batuukirivu, Selah.) Okubwatuka okwo okwayogerwa Yeshua HaMashiach ne Yahweh, NZE ALIWO OW'AMAANYI, okuva ku ntebe yange ey'amaanyi ey'okulangirira n'omusango, era okuleeteddwa kati n'omukono gw'omubaka wange ow'amaanyi gwe nsinzidde okuweereza ebigambo bino eby'okwagala n'okulabula okw'amaanyi ennyo era okw'entiisa, n'okutuusa nti bonna abayinza okujja bajje, omusango nga tegunnagwa mu bujjuvu era nga tewakyali kiseera!

Kyenvudde, bw'oba oyagala okwewonya omusango gwo n'okuzikirizibwa okw'olubeerera, nkulagira okufuula Yesu Mukama wo mu bujjuvu era awatali kulonzalonza. Kubanga emisango gyange gigwa, era girigwa ku yonna gye giweerezeddwa, ye misango egyo gyennyini egy'okusalirwa olubeerera mu nkomerero yaagyo emirembe n'emirembe. Kyenvudde, mpa amagezi bonna abatannayatura Yesu Kristo nga Mukama waabwe okuva mu mutima ogujjudde okukkiriza okukikola kati, nga bamanyi nti NZE nze eyali amuzukizza mu bafu mu maanyi amangi ennyo obutaddamu kufa n'okugenda mu maaso mu busika bwe emirembe n'emirembe. Naye, abasinga obungi baligwa mu musango omunene ogw'olubeerera nga bwe kyawandiikibwa mu biwandiiko ebitukuvu eby'edda n'ebiwandiiko ebitukuvu ebya kaakati.

Naye nzize okuwa okulabula kwange, n'oluvannyuma lw'ebirango by'omusango gwammwe ogw'enkomerero nga bimaze okulabula era nga bitandise dda okugwa. Kubanga amakubo gange ge makubo g'ekisa n'okusaasira n'okwagala era mu mazima ag'olubeerera, njagala waleme kubaawo n'omu azikirira naye abantu bonna bajje eri obulokozi obw'olubeerera n'okuzzibwa obuggya, newankubadde abasinga obungi baligaana eky'okulonda kyange ne beeronderawo okubonyaabonyezebwa kwabwe okw'olubeerera n'omusango gwabwe. Newankubadde njija okulabula abantu, tewali kulabula kuweereddwa okunaakyusa emisango gyange, newankubadde ekiseera kyagyo, newankubadde eby'okuvaamu, newankubadde entegeka zange ez'olubeerera okununula ebibala byange ebisooka, ne mbabaggyawo okutuukiriza obusika bwabwe obw'olubeerera akabi nga tekannagwa. Selah.

Era nze nsinzidde okuweereza obubaka buno obw'okwagala n'okulabula mu kwagala kwange eri omuntu ow'obutonde omubi, mu kwagala kwennyini kwe mpeerezesaamu obubaka obw'okwagala n'okulabula eri abaana bange bennyini abakkirizza Yesu Kristo mu buwulize; kubanga nze ntonnyesa enkuba ku batuukirivu n'abatali batuukirivu. Olw'okwagala kwange eri mmwe, nzize okuwa okuyita kwange okusembayo akabi nga tekannatuuka okuva ku ntebe yange ey'obwakabaka ey'amaanyi ag'olubeerera, omusango, n'okulangirira eddoboozi lyange eddene gye likyabwatukira mu busungu obungi era obw'obutuukirivu. Naye, kubanga MMWE Mugaana okuwulira newankubadde ndabudde emirundi mingi okumala jjubiri nnyingi nnyo kati! Mumpulire era mumpulire bulungi kubanga ebiseera byammwe eby'okuzikirizibwa okw'olubeerera birisindikibwa okusiba mu musango bonna abaagala era abakola obulimba, n'abo abasanyukira obutali butuukirivu, era bwe munnaŋŋaana mu biseera bino eby'ekisa, muligwa mu musango ogw'olubeerera kubanga tewali kulonda kulala.

Naye, mu malala gammwe ag'omuzibe ow'emitima emikakanyavu, mugaana omuntu omu yekka gwe nnatuma olw'obulokozi bwammwe obujjuvu, ye Yesu Omunnazaaleeti, ye Mwana omuwombeefu era omuweereza wa Yahweh gwe nnafuula omwoyo oguwa obulamu, era gwe nakola okuba wansi katono ku Yahweh yennyini mu kuzukiza kwange okuva mu bafu n'okugulumiza Kristo Yesu! Kubanga NZE ali waggulu era omufuzi ku bitonde byange byonna era mu mazima ag'olubeerera era agatakoma, NZE nze ntonnyesa enkuba ku batuukirivu n'abatali batuukirivu mu kwagala kwange eri ekitonde kyange, era mu makubo gange temuli kusosola mu misango gyange egy'obutuukirivu ku kitonde kyange.

Kyova otegeera okuba omusosoze w'abantu ge gamu ku makubo amangi amakyafu ag'abantu abagudde, kyenvudde nzize okulabula n'omwonoonyi asinga obubi mu bantu abali ku nsi omusango gwabwe nga tegunnabakuba mu bujjuvu, balyoke bawone mu busekendule obusembayo obukyaliwo era obuyitawo obw'ekisa akabi nga tekannatuuka. N'olwekyo mpa amagezi bonna abawulira oba abasoma ebigambo bino okuddukira eri Yesu Omunnazaaleeti amangu ago, nga tewali kubuusabuusa kubanga eri ekibiina ekinene tewalibaawo mukisa mulala, kubanga mu bibi byabwe eby'omuwano n'okunyooma ennyo ebintu byonna ebitukuvu, baligwa mu musango ogw'olubeerera, ne babakuumira mu kubonyaabonyezebwa gye balirindirira omusango ogw'entebe ey'obwakabaka enjeru, gye bataliyimirira naye balisibibwa ne baweebwayo eri ennyanja y'omuliro ey'okubonerezebwa ebibi byabwe gye biriweebwayo eri omuliro okusasulirwa mu bujjuvu obwenkanya emirembe n'emirembe.

Naye ggwe, Ggwe omuntu agudde, otuula mu makubo go ag'obutali butuukirivu, nga wagiddwa ddala abagudde mu butamanya bwo n'obuzibe bw'amaaso obwaleetebwawo obulimba bwabwe, era obulimba bw'oyagala kubanga oyagala amakubo g'obulimba bwo. Kyenvudde, nze mpeerezza ku nsi okubuzaabuzibwa okw'amaanyi nnyo mulyoke musalirwe omusango mwenna abasanyukira obutali butuukirivu ne mugaana Yesu Omunnazaaleeti, ye Yeshua HaMashiach. Era bwe muwammanta mu butamanya mu makubo gammwe ag'obuzibe bw'amaaso n'emitima emikakanyavu, bwe mutuula mu kizikiza mu butafaayo n'ekibi ekinene, bwe mutambula mu butaliimu n'okwekuŋŋaanyiza obusungu obungi n'ebizibu eby'olubeerera mu butawulira bwammwe, nja kusitula nate eddoboozi lyange, ne mpeereza ebigambo bino ng'obujulizi eri abo abaligaana, n'essuubi nti muyinza okuddukira eri Kristo Yesu, nsobole okubalokola okuva mu musango gw'abagudde: Selah.

Ggwe omuntu ow'obutonde, mmwe mwenna abaana ba Adamu abatanunulibwa era abatannenyisa, mmwe mwenna be nkangavvula ku nsi mu kwagala kwange eri mmwe! Naye mukkiriza obulimba obusinga obusirusiru obubawereddwa ng'emisomo mu kuyigirizibwa kwammwe abagudde. Era omusomo gw'okufa oguweereddwa mu matendekero gammwe amabi era agonoona ag'okufa amakubo g'omusodoomu gye gafuga kati nga bateeka akabonero era nga batendeka abaana bammwe bennyini balyoke balye ku bataliiko musango mu kwegomba kwabwe okuvunze. Naye mwerumbye nnyo ebintu ebireeta essanyu ery'ekibi, era mulemereddwa nnyo omululu gwammwe ogw'okwekuŋŋaanyiza eby'obugagga ebisingawo, n'okutuuka n'okuwaayo abaana bammwe mu makubo ga Baali olw'akacecce k'emmere enkyamu. Kyenvudde, nze nzikirizza abagudde okussa obutwa mu mmere yammwe, era mbawadde obungi bwammwe bwonna n'ogusota, n'ensolo eva mu nnyanja, n'ensolo eva mu ttaka biryoke bituukirize ebigendererwa byange, kubanga n'abalabe bange bawulira eddoboozi lyange.

Kubanga ebyo bye muyita emisomo gyammwe bya bukyayi era byonoonefu nga kyagendererwa era kino kiri kityo kubanga mugaana okumanya okw'amazima n'amakubo ga Elowimu omutonzi wammwe, ne musalawo okusasamazibwa endabirwamu zammwe enzirugavu ze mukutte mu ngeri ey'obwesigwa ennyo nga muva ku lutimbe okudda ku lulala mu butamanya obw'ekizikiza ekikutte. Naye muwanguddwa ddala era muzibiddwa amaaso mu ssanyu ery'omuwendo omutono era eriggwaawo amangu ng'ebirowoozo byammwe bikyusibwa n'eddagala ery'obuzaliranwa nga kigendererwa okuyita mu byuma bye mweesiba mu kibi. Kale okumanya okw'obukyayi kuleeta amalala era amalala gakulembera okuzikirira okungi. Tegeera, Ggwe Omuntu, nti okumanya kwo tekuyinza kukulokola ku misango gyange, era n'omusango gw'abagudde kati guteekeddwa okugwa. Naye, ggwe, Ggwe omuntu ow'obutonde oli musiru nnyo obutategeera nti abagudde babayigirizza obulimba bwabwe, balyoke baleete abantu abangi nga bwe kisoboka okwegatta nabo mu busika bwabwe obw'okwokebwa, okuvunda, n'amayinja ag'omuliro agali mu kuyiringisibwa. Era ekibonerezo ekikakali ekyakolebwa eri sitaani ne bamalayika be, so si Adamu. Selah.

Naye, mu kukkiriza kwammwe okw'okwagala mweraga amalala amangi mu ebyo bye mwatuukako mu nsi ebitagasa mwe mukuŋŋaanyiza mu mululu empapula zammwe ezigambibwa okuba ezitagasa n'ennamba zammwe ezigambibwa okuba ezitagasa mu akawunti ezigambibwa okuba ezitagasa eziribaggibwako amangu ago mu musango kati, kubanga ago ge makubo ga Esawu eyatunda obusika bwe olw'akacecce k'emmere. Naye, mu kukkiriza kwammwe okw'okwagala mukkiriza abagudde okubaggyako ssente n'okutiisibwatiisibwa okusasulira okuyigirizibwa kw'abaana bammwe mu makubo g'okufa. N'okutuusa nti bonna abayigirizibwa mu butamanya n'obuzibe bw'amaaso obwo bandiwerezza abagudde nga balowooza nti bakolera Katonda yennyini ekisa ekinene mu bibi byabwe, nga Yahweh yandibadde yeeggyawo ku kitonde kye n'obuyinza bwe olw'amalala gaabwe ag'ekibi agatalina mugaso. Era ekyo tekisoboka era tekiyinza kubaawo, kubanga NZE ALIWO.

Era mu makubo gammwe ag'obutali butuukirivu ag'omutima omukakanyavu mwafiirwa n'amatu gammwe ne muyimirira nga muli bakiggala, abasiru, era abazibe b'amaaso mu makubo gammwe ag'obutali butuukirivu agabakendeezezza mu buweereza ng'omuddu w'ekibi okukkakkanya okwegomba kw'abagudde, be muweereza mu makubo gammwe agatalina kukkiriza. Naye, omuweereza tasigala mu nnyumba emirembe gyonna bw'aba nga mu buwulize asazeewo okuyingira mu luggi olumu lwokka olw'eggulu lwe nja okuggulirawo abantu: Yesu Kristo era ali, Yesu Masiya, erinnya lye era ye Yeshua HaMashiach, ye luggi olwo olunene era oluggule lwokka olw'obulokozi olw'okuggulibwawo n'omukono gwange eri abeesigwa mu butuukirivu mu Kristo Yesu. Selah!

NZE ALIWO, era mpeerezza ebigambo bino eby'okwagala eri abasaliddwa omusango okuyita mu mubaka wange era omuweereza wange, era okuyita mu mubaka wange n'omuweereza wange mpeerezza ebigambo bino eri abo abagenda okusalirwa omusango mu bbanga ttono.

Mutegeere mwenna abawulira ebigambo bino, nti kino si kirangiriro kya nkomerero kya musango, ekireeta enkomerero eri omuntu ow'obutonde. Okwawukana ku ekyo, obubaka buno buweereza okulabula abatali bawulize n'abatali batuukirivu ab'omu Babulooni Ekikwekeddwa era n'abo bonna ebigambo bino bye biweerezeddwa, (era abamu balijja mu bulokozi mu busekendule buno obusembayo) naye era ng'obujulizi obusembayo obw'okulabula obuweereddwa omusango nga tegunnagwa mu bujjuvu ku Babulooni Ekikwekeddwa.

Mutegeere, nti ebigambo byange birizuula amaaso, amatu, n'emitima gy'abo be biweerezeddwa, era tewali kufuba kuva eri abagudde, oba emizizo gyabwe, newankubadde tekinologiya waabwe atagasa era atamanyi kiyinza kulemesa ekyo NZE ALIWO OW'AMAANYI ky'agenderera okutuukiriza.

Era ntyo nteeka omukono ku bbaluwa yange ey'okwagala eri abasaliddwa omusango nga nsuubira nti mu kuwulira ebigambo byange ebiweerezeddwa mu kwagala kwange eri bo bandiwonye emisango gyabwe omukono ogwo ogw'enkomerero nga tegunnakuba akasekendule ako akasembayo.

Era ntyo nteeka omukono ku bbaluwa yange ey'okwagala mu Kwagala okungi n'Obuyinza Obw'olubeerera!
