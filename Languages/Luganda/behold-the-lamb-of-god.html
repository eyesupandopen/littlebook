Abajulirwa Abali Ababiri: Isirayiri
Okukaawa n’Okuwooma
Novemba 16, 2024

Laba! Omwana gw'Endiga gwa Katonda!

Muwulire Ebigambo bya Yeshua HaMashiach, mutege amatu eri eddoboozi ly'Ebigambo byange era mukomole emyoyo gyammwe okukkiriza Masiya wammwe ow'amazima, Ai Isirayiri, era mutegeere enjogera yange mulyoke mube bagezigezi mu makubo gammwe, era mulyoke munzikirize okuluŋŋamya ebigere byammwe n'okukyusa amakubo gammwe okuva ku kkubo eryo eggazi ery'okuzikirira kwe mugoberera, okudda ku kkubo effunda ery'obulokozi agali amakubo gange era gwe nze. Kubanga nze oli gwe Yahweh yatuma olw'obulokozi bwammwe, era nze ezzadde essuubize ery'omukazi eriribetenta omutwe gw'omusota, era nkuŋŋaanya eri nze mwennyini ab'oluganda lwange, ddala ab'ekika kyange be ndi omununuzi waabwe ow'oluganda. Noolwekyo mbalagira okukomola emyoyo gyammwe muleme ebirowoozo byammwe ebitabala, mulyoke mubale ebibala mu Masiya wammwe ow'amazima, kale muwulire amateeka gange agaweereddwa olw'obuwulize bwammwe mulyoke mube balamu mu maaso ga Yahweh Omunene NZE NZE, ddala nga munkkiriza nga Masiya wammwe ow'emirembe gyonna, era ddala ddala: Nze wuuyo.

Era nga Yahweh bwe yatumira Bajjajjammwe amateeka ga Musa (amateeka ago ge gali omusomesa wammwe agenderedde okubaleeta eri Kristo), era bwe kityo Yahweh yantuma nga bwe yasuubiza edda bwe yasuubiza obulokozi Bajjajja b'abaana be ntuukiridde kaakano, ddala abaana ba Isirayiri be njagala n'okwagala okutazikira. Naye mu butamanya n'obutakkiriza obutalina kukkiriza, Bajjajjammwe banzira ne mpeebwayo mu mikono gy'abasajja aboonoonyi okunkola n'obukambwe n'obukyayi ebingi ebyo amateeka bye gaabagaana okukola; era n'olw'okulabika nga batuukiridde eri abantu b'omu nsi bwe batta omwana gw'endiga gwa Katonda nga Bajjajjammwe babikkirizza, ddala omusango guno abangi ku mmwe nammwe gwe mukyakkiririzamu mu busirusiru.

Naye, tegeera Isirayiri nti saanyagibwa lwa maanyi abalabe bange era abalabe bammwe. Tegeera nti saanyagibwa lwa maanyi, so lwa nkwe era tewali kyokulwanyisa kyonna ekinkoleddwako ekiyinza okumpanguza, kubanga Yahweh bw'atyo bwe yalangirira, era Yahweh ky'alangirira tewali ayinza kukikyusa! Mazima, Isirayiri, nze oli eyagamba Yuda Isukalyota bwe nalyebulwa omukono gwe olw'ebitundu bya ffeeza amakumi asatu nti, kino ky'okola, kola mangu! Kubanga omuwendo ogwali gusaana ow'omuddu eyalina omuddu we eyalumbibwa ente ennume, obulamu bwange bwatundibwa mu mikono gy'abo abamfumuula, nga buweereddwayo ddala abakulu b'abakabona ne Sanhedrin be nnanenya olw'amakubo gaabwe amabi n'obunnanfuusi. Noolwekyo Yuda n'alyebulira mu mikono gya Esawu, ddala oyo agamba nti alina omugabo ku nsi ey'obusuubize ate omulundi omulala n'amaanyi, ddala omugabo Esawu gwe yatunda olw'akamere akatono. Naye nze, nneeyagalira okukkiriza okutwalibwa abalabe bange so nga nnyinza okubadduka, era ddala ddala, singa nasaba Yahweh, yandimpadde obuyinza ku bitundu by'eggye bya bamalayika okusinga ekkumi n'ebibiri, naye olwo Ebyawandiikibwa byandituukiridde bitya, era ne kiweddizibwa.

Naye, n'olwekyo, ebigendererwa byange n'amakubo gange tebyategeerekeka eri ab'ekika kyange n'abo ab'eggwanga lyange be njagala emirembe gyonna n'okwagala okungi era okwaka okutayinza kuzikizibwa newaakubadde okuwangulwa, kubanga nze Lwazi lwammwe olw'Omusingi okutali muzimbi ayinza kuzimbako, era kwe abatalina kukkiriza kwesittalira. Kubanga mu kwagala kwange eri mmwe, na Adamu yenna awanguddwa abagwa, nze neeyagalira okwewaayo mu mikono gy'abalabe bange okubununula okuva mu kikolimo ky'amateeka.

N'olwekyo lino lye tteeka lyange ery'emirembe gyonna, nti mwagalane mwekka na mwekka, nga nze bwe nnabagala. Tewali kwagala kusinga kuno muntu yenna kw'alina, omuntu yenna bw'awaayo obulamu bwe nga ssaddaaka ku lwa mikwano gye, kye nnakola nga neeyagalira nga mmanyi nti awatali kuwulira kwange eri Kitaange by'ayagala, abantu bandisuuliddwawo emirembe gyonna. Era nze omulokozi wa Adamu, ddala oyo eyayanunula abantu okuva mu mbeera ye ey'okugwa, omuntu yenna bw'ajja gye ndi ng'afukamye era n'ampaatula nga Mukama waabwe, era ddala nze Masiya wammwe ow'amazima, Ai Isirayiri.

Mutegeere ab'oluganda lwange, nti saayogera kigambo kyonna ku nze kyokka nga tekyawandiikibwa na lugalo lwa Yahweh ku bipande by'amayinja era nga tekyayogerebwa mu kamwa k'abaweereza be bannabbi okuteekebwa mu buwandiike olw'okukkiriza n'obuwulize by'abasika b'essuubi, be muli mmwe Ai Isirayiri, singa mulema okukakanyaza emyoyo gyammwe nga Bajjajjammwe bwe baakola mu ddungu bwe baasoomooza Yahweh, ne mu ddungu obulamu bwabwe obutakkiriza ne bukomerera mu bwereere, era bo bennyini ne batasikira bisuubizo wabula abaana b'okukkiriza!

Naye mu buzibe bwammwe obw'ekitundu mukkiririza mu bulimba bwa Esawu nti nze ndi mulimba, era mazima Esawu yanzira kubanga Esawu anoonya kugamba nti alina obusikize bwe yatunda eri omuto, era tewali kkubo lya kwenenya eri Esawu, newaakubadde nga yalinunoonya n'amaziga. Era Bajjajjammwe tebaategeera bigendererwa byange by'amazima kubanga baali banoonya omukulembeze ow'ensi aggyewo abo be beeyama okugondera, bulyoke basobole okusigaza ebifo byabwe mu bantu. Naye, nnatumibwa okununula abantu okuva mu mukono gw'abagwa, Adamu gwe yeegattako mu busirusiru mu butawulira bwe eri etteeka lya Yahweh olw'enkwe z'omusota ezaamulimba, ne nnyina w'abalamu bonna bwe baayonoona mu Edeni ku lubereberye. Era newaakubadde amawanga mangi gaagalamidde era ne gagwa, n'obwakabaka bungi bwagalamidde era ne bugwa okuva mu biro bya ssaddaaka yange, Isirayiri akyali mu busibe eri abalabe be okutuusa leero, era aweereza ebigendererwa bya Esawu anoonya okubazikiririza ddala.

Weewaawo, Isirayiri asigadde mu busibe kubanga eggwanga lyammwe lyabaggyibwako ng'ekibonerezo olw'okutta omusaayi ogutalina musango, ddala okutta n'obweyagalire n'obukyayi omusajja atalina kibi yennyini eyatumibwa Yahweh olw'okununulibwa kwammwe. Ddala nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, obudde bwe bwatuukirira, Katonda n'atuma Omwana we, eyazaalibwa omukazi, era n'abeera wansi w'amateeka, alyoke anunudde abo abaali wansi w'amateeka. Era mu bulamu bwange obutalina kibi n'obuwulize ne nneewaayo ng'omwana gw'endiga gwa Katonda mu ssaddaaka ey'obulumi n'obukambwe obungi bwe nneewaayo nga kabona asinga obukulu emirembe gyonna okusinziira ku mutindo gwa Melkizeddeeki.

Era omulimu guno guwedde era ntuula ku mukono gwa ddyo gwa Kitange, ddala Yahweh, Omunene NZE NZE. Era nsikidde ebintu byonna, ne mu mikono gyange emiwanvu muweereddwayo ebintu byonna mu ggulu ne ku nsi okutuusa abalabe bange lwe balifuulibwa entebbe y'ebigere byange, naye Obwakabaka bwange tebuliggwaawo! Obwakabaka obwo Kabaka wammwe w'alifugira ddala busembera ku nkomerero y'okubonaabona kwa Yakobo, we ndizikiririza obwakabaka bw'omulabe ne ndeeta omusango omunene n'ebibonerezo ku bagwa era mu kubonerezebwa mwe baliyingira olw'ebibi byabwe ebiri ebingi era n'omusango gwabwe gwa mirembe gyonna ogutalibaawo kawummulo newaakubadde akaseera. Era abamu ku bo baliyingira mu nnyanja ey'omuliro ennene n'ekibiriiti ekyaka ku kujja kwange okw'okubiri okufuga ensi, ensolo n'omunnabbi ow'obulimba bwe balisuulibwamu nga balamu. Era Basetaani balisibibwa olujegere olwo olunene mwe balisibirwa mu kubonyaabonyezebwa n'entiisa ey'amaanyi nga basibiddwa mu kutyemuka okulindirira ekyo ekibalindiridde mu busika bwabwe obw'emirembe gyonna obw'okubonerezebwa emirembe n'emirembe. Ddala omusango ogw'entiisa Setaani gw'atayinza kudduka, mwe basibirwa ne basuulibwa mu muliro ogusinga okwaka emirembe n'emirembe ku nkomerero y'akaseera ke akatono.

Kubanga, nze oli eyaweebwayo olw'obulokozi bwa Yakobo, ddala ezzadde essuubize ery'omukazi Adamu lye yakkiririzaamu n'essuubi, era alizuukizibwa mu bulamu obuggya emirembe gyonna. Kubanga nze oli eyatumibwa olw'okununulibwa kwa Adamu, era nawaayo ettone lyange ly'omusaayi eryasembayo eryayiika ne linywebwa ettaka eddemyu e Gologosa, ddala we njogeredde ebigambo ebyo eby'olubeerera nti Kiwedde. N'olwekyo, nze oli mu kwagala kwange eri Yahweh n'okwagala kwange eri muntu munange eyewaayo mu ssaddaaka mu bulumi obungi ennyo, ensonyi, n'okubonyaabonyezebwa era n'awangula ekibi n'okufa, era olunaku lusembera ekibi kyonna lwe kitalibaawo nate.

Era nnakikola n'obweyagalire, ddala ku bwange olw'ekitiibwa ekyateekebwa mu maaso gange, era nnakikola nga nnyooma ensonyi zonna ezitaali zange okwetikka. Naye mu kwagala kwange eri mmwe, nnatwala ensonyi zammwe n'ekibonerezo kyammwe ku mugongo gwange, ddala bwe baayuza ennyama yange okuva ku magumba gange n'embooko zaabwe. Weewaawo, nnatwala ensonyi zammwe n'ekibonerezo kyammwe mu kunyoomoola n'obukyayi by'abantu aboonoonyi abaakozesebwa abagwa bennyini bwe bantira mu butamanya mu kifo kyammwe, nga tebategeera mu makubo gaabwe amabisi nti okutta omwana gw'endiga gwa Katonda baali beeteerawo emikka ku miriro emirembe gyonna, kubanga tekiyinzika okufa okunkwata, kubanga sirina kibi kye nnakola n'omulundi gumu, nedda, era ddala sirikikola!

Weewaawo, Isirayiri, nnatwala ekifo kyammwe mu kibonerezo n'ekifo kya Bajjajjammwe abanzira mu butamanya olw'okusendasenda kwa Esawu! Naye lwo mwandyayanunuddwa mutya awatali kulyebulira kwabwe okutalina kukkiriza, kubanga okununula Adamu omusaayi gwokka ogutalina kibi gwe gwandituukirizza ebisaanyizo by'ekibonerezo ekyetaagibwa amateeka ga Yahweh amatuukirivu ag'emirembe gyonna, kubanga awatali kuyiwa musaayi tewabaawo kusonyiyibwa kwa bibi. Era nga Adamu bwe yatondebwa nga talina kibi era nga mutuukirivu ku lunaku lwe yatonderwa, bwe ntyo nange, Yeshua HaMashiach (ali omwana gw'endiga gwa Katonda) natondebwa nga ndi mutuukirivu era nga sirina kibi olw'okununulibwa okujjuvu okw'abantu. Kubanga oba ng'omusaayi gw'ennume z'ente n'embuzi, n'evvu ly'ente enduusi, nga limansibwa ku abo abatali balongoofu, litukuza mu kifo ky'okulongoosa omubiri, omusaayi gwa Kristo, ogwaweebwayo olw'Omwoyo ow'emirembe gyonna nga yeewaayo eri Katonda awatali nsobi, tegulisinga nnyo kulongoosa omwoyo gwammwe okuva mu bikolwa ebifu, okuweereza Katonda omulamu?

Noolwekyo mbawa okutegeera nti nnatwala ekifo kyammwe mu kusalirwa omusango n'ekibonerezo, era nnakikola n'obweyagalire awatali kukakibwa; newaakubadde nga nnasaba mu nnimiro y'e Gesusemane nti oba nga waliwo ekkubo eddala, naye si nze by'njagala wabula ebya Yahweh bikolebwe. Noolwekyo mu kunyoomooka kwange, nnatwalibwa ebweru w'emiryango gy'ekibuga ekyo ekinene, (ddala Yerusaalemi atta bannabbi) gye nnatumirwa eri abange, era eyo ne bagabana ebyambalo byange bwe baabikubira akalulu! Weewaawo, eyo e Gologosa, oluvannyuma lw'okumala essaawa nnyingi mu kibonerezo ekitakoma so nga sirina musango, eyo nnakomererwa emikono gy'abantu aboonoonyi, be sirina mugabo ku bibi byabwe newaakubadde katono. Naye nze n'obweyagalire nneeyagalira okusibibwa n'okukomererwa enninga mu mikono gyange n'ebigere byange okuwanikibwa ku muti ogwo omugonvu mu kifo kyammwe, ddala ekibonerezo ekitaali kyange okwetikka. Kubanga ddi lwe kyaweebwako atalina musango okweyagalira okwambala n'ensonyi ku muti ogw'okunyoomoola mu kunyoomooka n'obulumi obungi olw'obulokozi bw'ab'oluganda lwe abatali batuukirivu okubonaabonera mu kifo kyaabwe, naye mu kwagala kwange eri mmwe nnakikola ddala ekyo. Tegeera nti mu kiseera kyonna nnyinza okubakomya nga nsaba Kitange n'ampa ebitundu by'eggye bya bamalayika okusinga 12 mu buyinza bwange, naye, sirisaba wabula ne nsalawo okugumiikiriza okutuukirira era n'obweyagalire ekibonerezo kyammwe ekitaali kyange okwetikka. N'olwekyo mu kwagala kwange nneewaayo ku lwammwe, era nandikiddamu, newaakubadde nga ekyo tekyetaagisa era Yahweh akkirizza ssaddaaka yange omulundi gumu, byonna, era emirembe gyonna, olw'ekyo tewakyaliyo ssaddaaka ya bibi.

Kale, ggwe Isirayiri, tegeera okwagala, okugumiikiriza, ekisa n'obusaasizi bye nkulaga mu mbeera yo ey'obujeemu n'okugaana Masiya wo ow'amazima, era nzijira gy'oli mu kiseera kino eky'enkomerero mu kwagala kwange eri ggwe okukyali okutaggwaawo era okutawangulwa, singa wonna oyinza okukkiriza Masiya wo ow'amazima. Kubanga Nze ndi oli eyakyetwala mu kifo kyo okusalirwa omusango, okubonerezebwa, n'okuttibwa kw'aboonoonyi kubanga empeera y'ekibi ye kufa, naye Nze sirina kibi emirembe gyonna, era bulijjo nkola bya Kitange, kye kyokulabirako kye mugaana mu butawulize bwammwe obw'obugenderere eri Yakuwa, ne mu kugaana kwammwe Masiya wammwe ow'amazima.

Era lwaki nagaananwa? Ne Pontiyo Piraato, eyali Omutongole wa Buyudaaya mu kiseera ekyo, yali amaliridde okunta n'ayogera amazima ku bampangiriza, be abo bennyini ab'omu Esawu abaakungaanya bajjajjammwe ne bamuleekaanira nti tusumululire Balaba! Nga bakyaleekaanira mu mwoyo gwe gumu nti, "Mukomerere!" bwe baali boogera ku Piraato okubasumululira Masiya waabwe ow'amazima n'omusaayi ogutaliiko musango gwe baagaana. Kubanga yamanya bulungi nti baali bampeerezzaayo lwa buggya. Ate era bwe yali atudde ku ntebe ey'okulamulirako, mukyala we n'amutumira n'amugamba nti, Tobaako nsonga na musajja oyo omutuukirivu: kubanga leero mbonyeenyezeddwa nnyo mu kirooto ku lulwe, era bwe kyali bwe kityo era bwe kyawandiikibwa mu biwandiiko ebitukuvu singa munaazuula okukkiriza okukkiriza obujulizi bwange.

Era byonna byali lwa kuba nti bajjajjammwe beegombanga ebintu by'ensi eno era baayagalanga ebifo byabwe mu bantu gye baasoboleranga okubeerawo ku bitundu eby'ekkumi n'ebiweebwayo by'abantu ne beeyimirirawo ng'abafumu ab'obulimba abeeziririsa gye baayogereranga amagezi ga Esawu mu matu g'abantu: amagezi amakyamu ag'obulimba bajjajjaabwe be baasinganga okutwala ng'ag'omuwendo okusinga eddoboozi n'amagezi ga Yakuwa, Nze Ndi ow'amaanyi, gwe baagaana bwe baagaana ne bakomerera Yesu Omunazaaleeti.

Era bwe kityo, mu kugaana kwammwe Nze, ye Yesu Omunazaaleeti, nakyukira Abamawanga, ne bawulira eddoboozi lyange okumala ekiseera era bangi, bangi abataalina ssuubi, kati bajja mu bulokozi mu Mukama waabwe era kibiina kinene kye nja okuzuukiza nga balamu eri Nze okubanjulira Yakuwa mu lukuŋŋaana olunene olw'abatukuvu olugenda okubaawo. Era bangi ab'e Buyudaaya n'abangi abaasaasaana mu nsi zonna baateekebwako akabonero k'obulokozi obujjuvu. Era ku baganda bammwe, abo ab'omu bwakabaka obw'obukiikakkono abalondeddwa okufuna obulokozi obw'olubeerera, bangi ku abo kati bateekeddwaako akabonero emirembe gyonna era bajja kuzuukizibwa mu bulamu obuggya. Mazima, mazima, bonna abo balese ekyokulabirako eky'okwenenya mmwe okukigoberera, singa mugondera eddoboozi lya Masiya omu yekka ow'amazima Yakuwa gw'ajja okutuma, ye Yesu Omunazaaleeti Masiya wammwe ow'amazima!

Kubanga olw'ebibi byabwe ebika eby'obukiikakkono byasaasaanyizibwa mu mawanga, gye byakuumirwa ne biteresebwa olw'obulokozi, n'omukono gwa Yakuwa ogw'amaanyi. Naye bwe kyawandiikibwa n'omukono gw'Omutume wange Pawulo bwe yawandiikira Abaruumi emyaka mingi egiyise: Tumanyi ng'ebintu byonna bikolera wamu olw'okulaba obulungi abo abaagala Katonda, abo abayitebwa olw'ekigendererwa kye. Kubanga abo be yategeererawo, era be yalondereratu okufaananyizibwa n'ekifaananyi ky'Omwana we, abeere omubereberye mu baganda bangi. Era abo be yalondereratu, abo era be yayita, n'abo be yayita, abo era be yatuukiririza, n'abo be yatuukiririza, abo era be yagulumiza. Bwe kityo mu Isirayiri wa Katonda okukuŋŋaanya ebika ebyabula kujja kutuukirira, era Abaisirayiri bangi baafuna obulokozi bwe banjatula okuba Mukama waabwe, ye Yesu Omunazaaleeti, mu mirembe gyange egy'ekisa n'obulokozi bwe baafuna.

Wadde nga mu kiseera kino munyooma emirimu gyange, n'emirimu gyange egy'obulokozi gye natukiriza mu mawanga ku kiragiro kya Yakuwa mu mirembe gyange egy'ekisa egikomekkerezeddwa; kale mutegeere ekyo Yakuwa kye yalongosa okuyita mu musaayi gwa Yesuwa Amasiya, temukitwala nga kya bulijjo oba nga si kirongoofu! Era Nze, Yesuwa Amasiya, mbeegayirira mugoberere amakubo ga baganda bammwe oba nga bali kumpi oba wala abaajja gye ndi nga beenenyezza ddala ndyoke mbasembeze era mbazzeewo eri Yakuwa, kubanga alina Omwana alina ne Kitaffe naye atalina Mwana talina na Kitaffe, era bwe kityo Yakuwa bwe yalangirira!

Kale bwe kityo bwe nkyukira nate ab'oluganda lwange nga bwe nva ku mawanga mu kiseera kino eky'enkomerero era okwawukana okw'enkomerero kugenda kubaawo mu wiiki ya Danyeri ey'amakumi asatu, etuuse okulongoosa ensi mu muliro gwange ogulongoosa era Esawu ajja kwawulibwa ku Yakobo emirembe gyonna. Kubanga Esawu yatunda obusika bwe, era Yakuwa akkirizza emirembe gyonna okusalawo kwe kusobole okuba okw'olubeerera, kubanga abaana b'ekisuubizo be basika b'ebisuubizo so si baana ba mubiri; oba nga muli ba Kristo, kale muli zzadde lya Ibulayimu, era basika olw'ekisuubizo. Bwe kityo ebisuubizo by'obusika ebyakolebwa Yakuwa yennyini byakolebwa mu kukkiriza eri abo be byakolerwa, kubanga omutuukirivu ajja kubeera mulamu olw'okukkiriza! Era oluvannyuma lw'ebisuubizo ebyesigwa okuweebwa, endagaano zaakolebwa n'obubonero bw'ekisuubizo ne buweebwa era endagaano ne zifuuka endagaano empya olw'okuyiibwa kw'omusaayi era endagaano zonna enkulu n'ebisuubizo bya Yakuwa byonna okuva ku bitono okutuuka ku binene byonna biteereddwa mu Ndagaano yange Empya ey'ekisuubizo, mwe etonde lijja kufuulibwa ettuukirivu emirembe gyonna mu kuzzaawo okw'olubeerera eri Patri ow'Ebitangaala, atalina nkyukakyuka newaakubadde ekisiikirize eky'okukyuka.

Kale njija eri ab'oluganda lwange mu kwagala n'obuwombeefu obw'omwoyo, era njija gye muli mu kwagala kwange nga saagala muntu yenna ku mmwe abule olw'obutakkiriza obutalimu ssuubi. Era mazima ddala, ensigalira ejja kulokolebwa nga nnamu okuva mu Yakobo ne mu kiseera eky'okubonaabona kwa Yakobo, ye wiiki ya Danyeri ey'amakumi asatu. N'olwekyo, mbeegayirira ab'oluganda, muwulire eddoboozi lyange kubanga kino kye kiseera eky'enkomerero era kuno kwe kwawukana okusembayo ng'obusungu tebunnafukibwa mu bujjuvu era nga sinnajja kuzikiriza bwakabaka bw'omulabe mu bujjuvu, kubanga mu butuukirivu njija ng'Empologoma ey'omu kika kya Yuda era njija kulamula n'okulwana!

N'abo bonna abajja okunkyukira mu kiseera kino, Ai Isirayiri, n'abo bonna be ndagira abazaalibwa bajjajja ab'edda okuwulira eddoboozi lyange, era n'abo bonna abajja okukoowoola erinnya lyange ery'amaanyi okutuusa ku nkomerero okuva mu mawanga bajja kulokolebwa era balokolebwe mu mitendera gyabwe. Amawanga gajja kufuna ekitiibwa ekifaananako n'ekyange bwe nneewaayo olw'ebibi by'omuntu, era bajja kuttibwa nga bayitirira Katonda bwe bakoowoola erinnya lyange erirokola okutuusa ku nkomerero. Era bajja kugenda mu kitiibwa ekinene emirembe gyonna n'empeera yaabwe ya lubeerera olw'okunywerera kwabwe, era olw'obuwulize bwabwe bajja kuba bawangudde embeera yaabwe ey'obutakkiriza n'ey'ekibuguumirize, era nga bayise mu muliro.

Ne ku Isirayiri, ab'oluganda lwange, nja kukomawo okubalokola era nja kubayamba n'abange emitwalo kkumi n'ena mu enkumi nnya, abo abeezizza obulungi ng'abawanguzi bange mu musaayi gwange ogutangirira era ogununula ku lunaku lwabwe olw'obulokozi era bajja kukuŋŋaanyizibwa gye ndi mu lukuŋŋaana olunene olugenda okubaawo amangu ago ku nsi. Kubanga nja kukulembera abange emitwalo kkumi n'ena mu enkumi nnya okukka ku Lusozi Sayuuni, era bajja kungoberera buli ntambula nga bakulembera ensigalira yange eya Yakobo okugituusa mu ddungu gye wali ekifo ekyategekebwa Yakuwa olw'obukuumi bwabwe, era bajja kuliisibwa n'okulabirirwa era ogusota, ensolo, ne nnabbi ow'obulimba tebajja kubakwatako, wadde okubazuula, ensigalira eyo ye mmwe Isirayiri. Naye Balaba ajja kuleeta omuzizo oguzikiriza n'omuddo mungi gujja kulongoosebwa mu nsi, era nja kukweka ensigalira yange ewala okuva mu lutalo lw'olunaku olwo olunene, ye lunaku lw'okujja kwange okw'okubiri olunaku olwo Yakuwa lwe yateekawo mu mirembe egiyise era luteekeddwawo formal mu nteekateeka ye ey'obulokozi era olunaku olwo ddala lusemberedde, kubanga bwe kyawandiikibwa era bwe kirikolebwa.

N'olwekyo, ab'oluganda lwange be njagala emirembe n'emirembe, naye era nkyassa ekitiibwa mu ddembe lyammwe Katonda lye yabawa okweronderawo ekkubo lye mujja okutambuliramu. N'olwekyo mbabuuza, mujja kusigala mu kkubo eggazi ery'okuzikirira erigenda mu nnimi z'omuliro ezitazikira ezategekerwa abagwa? Oba, mujja kugondera eddoboozi lya Masiya wammwe ow'amazima, oli eyawa buli ttondo ly'omusaayi gwe ogutalimu kibi mu kubonaabona n'obulumi obw'amaanyi ennyo mmwe musobole okuba abalamu mu maaso ga Katonda wammwe, ye Yakuwa NZE NDI ow'amaanyi? Yee, Yakuwa eyakulukusa amaziga mangi mu mutima gwe ogwamenyeka olw'endiga ezabula ez'omu nnyumba ya Isirayiri, abo bonna abagaana eddoboozi lye n'eddoboozi lyange. Mujja kwegatta ku Nze olw'obuwulize bwammwe eri Masiya wammwe ow'amazima? Oba mujja kwegatta ku Balaba mu bugenderere Esawu gwe yatumira okubazikiriza?

Kale muwulire Ebigambo byange Ai Isirayiri mutegeere n'eky'okusalawo kye mulina okukola era mutegeere nti mbayisa gye ndi era nja kutuma abajulizi bange ababiri ab'amaanyi era mbalagira mubawulire musobole okukkiriza obujulizi bwabwe kubanga bajja kutegeeza ebintu bingi ebyakwekebwa mu mitima gy'abantu naye bye bajja okubikkula n'obujulizi bwabwe bujja kunywerera emirembe n'emirembe. N'olwekyo, muwulire eddoboozi ly'omwana gw'endiga owa Katonda ow'amazima, Ai Isirayiri, kubanga tewali bulokozi na kununulibwa mu linnya eddala lyonna eryali liweereddwa, era mazima ddala, tewali linnya ddala lijja kuweebwa lwa kigendererwa kino, okuggyako Yesuwa Amasiya oyo era ye Yesu Omunazaaleeti wadde nga mu mubiri sikyamanyibwa era ndi Kristo Yesu, ye Mukama w'abakama era Kabaka w'abakabaka.

Nze ndi Empologoma ey'omu kika kya Yuda era mu butuukirivu njija kulamula na kulwana.

Nze ndi oli eyatumye Ebigambo bino okuyita mu muweereza wange era okuyita mu muweereza wange Ebigambo bino kwe byatumiddwa, era ntegeeza ne nkakasa nti Ebigambo by'Enjiri ey'Olubeerera ebibikkuliddwa n'omukono gwange, era ddala Yakuwa yennyini, bya mazima, era bya lubeerera, n'omuweereza wange ayogedde mu bwesigwa Ebigambo ebibikkuliddwa gy'ali mu kiseera kyabyo era bijja kukuumibwa mu mitawaana egijja era bijja kutuuka ku maaso, amatu n'emitima gy'abo be bitumiddwa.

Nze ndi Masiya wammwe ow'olubeerera era ow'emirembe eyeegomba obulokozi n'okununulibwa kwammwe Isirayiri, era mazima ddala ntegekedde okusembeza gyendi bonna abanaddamu eddoboozi lyange era mujja kuyingira mu Bwakabaka obw'emyaka olukumi era mujja kuba ggwanga lya njawulo era mujja kubeera n'obulamu obutaggwaawo era mujja kufuna eddundiro eritaggawo mu Isirayiri wa Katonda, era temuliddamu kwawulibwa na bufuzi butuukirivu bwa Kabaka wammwe, Mukama, Kabona Asinga Obukulu, ne Masiya era emirembe gyonna mujja kukulemberwa mu mirembe n'obutuukirivu era mujja kukula ng'ennyana ez'omu kisibo.

Nze ndi Yesuwa Amasiya era ndi ekyo NZE NDI ow'amaanyi kye yanfuula era ndi omwana gw'endiga owa Katonda eyaweebwa okuba paasika yammwe etuukiridde era etaliiko kibi emirembe gyonna n'ebiro byonna.
