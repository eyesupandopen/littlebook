Okukuŋŋaana okw'Amaanyi okw'Abakkiriza mu Kristo

Muwulire ebigambo bya Mukama wammwe ow'Emirembe n'Emirembe, ggwe ekkanisa yange, omubiri gwange, era n'omugole wange, era mutege amatu muwulire mu buwulize Ebigambo bya Yeshua HaMashiach. Muwulire eddoboozi lyange era mujje eri Mukama wammwe n'emitima emyetegefu okukkiriza Ebigambo byange, n'okussa ekitiibwa mu byo, kubanga ebiseera eby'okubudaabudibwa n'okununulibwa okujjuvu bituuse ku mmwe abaagalwa bange, kale mwetegeke!

Nga bwe kiri, Nze Yesu Omunazaaleeti, era ye Yeshua HaMashiach mu lulimi Olwebbulaniya, njija kubategekera ddala abaagalwa bange kubanga tewakyali kiseera era okukuŋŋaanyizibwa kw'abakkiriza mu Kristo kwakutandikawo mangu ddala mu ngeri etasuubirwa. Kale nno temutya, abaagalwa bange, era mutegeere nti emisango gy'abo abagaanye okulabula kwange si gya mmwe, era mulirokolebwa ddala mu busungu obujja! Mazima, mazima, kubanga mwakkiriza ne mutoola obulokozi n'okuggyibwako ebibi mu kuzaalibwa okupya okusinziira ku Ndagaano Yange Empya mu biseera eby'ekisa n'okusaasira, muli balokole ddala mu busungu obujja!

Naye, bwe kityo bwe kiri, nti emmeeme nnyingi ezitatereera era ezitali ntuukirivu ezoogera obulimba mu bunnanfuusi ne zikyunya Ebigambo byange okuleetawo omusango n'okuzikirira kwabyo, abakozesa obukujjukujju n'okwekuluntaza okw'omubiri ne beeyita amaanyi amakulu aga Yahweh, era ne babuzaabuza bonna abawulira era abagondera obulimba bwabwe obwogerwa okuva mu mitima egirimbaganya. N'olwekyo, mutegeere ebisuubizo byange bye nabikkulira Pawulo bwe nnamuyigiriza Ekyama Ekikulu ekya Katonda ekibikkuliddwa mu bujjuvu mu mabaluwa ge, era ekyama bangi ku mmwe kye mutakyategeera. Naye, yalaba n'ategeeza ebikwata ku busungu bwa Yahweh: Naye Katonda alaga okwagala kwe gye tuli, kubanga bwe twali tukyali boonoonyi, Kristo n'atufiirira. Kale nno, bwe tumaze okutukuzibwa n'omusaayi gwe, tulirokolebwa mu busungu ku bubwe. Kubanga bwe twali abalabe, twatabaganyizibwa ne Katonda olw'okufa kw'Omwana we, kale nno bwe tumaze okutabaganyizibwa, tulirokolebwa olw'obulamu bwe!

Mmwe ab'okukkiriza okutono, ndibagumiikiriza kutuusa ddi, abaagalwa bange? Kale njija kubawa kubudaabuda kubanga siribavaako so siribasuula, mmwe mwenna abali mu Kristo, era mazima ndibeera nammwe emirembe n'emirembe kubanga mulibeera nange ennaku zonna era mulibeera nga nze. Selah.

N'olwekyo, mutegeere nti bonna abawangaala mu ngeri ey'okutya Katonda balibonabona mu ngeri emu oba endala, era empologoma eyo etalina mannyo ekyatambulatambula enoonya gw'eneemira, kale mugiziyize ddala era eneebadduka. Naye, emiryango gya magombe tegirisinga Kkanisa yange newakubadde abakkiriza mu Kkanisa yange abagondera eddoboozi lyange era be mpawa amaanyi mangi n'omwoyo gwange ogw'Obwana obw'obuweereza ogutondebwa mu bonna abamfuula Mukama wange okuva mu mutima ogujjudde okukkiriza, era muli bateekeddwako akabonero ka Katonda Ataggwaawo ow'Obutuukirivu, ye Yahweh Nze Ani Mukulu, era akabonero ako tekamenyeka so temusobola kukwakulibwa mu mikono gyange, newakubadde nga balimba!

N'olwekyo, mutegeere nti muli balokole mu busungu, era muli balokole mu busungu bwonna. Kale mutegeere nti obusungu bw'Omwana gw'endiga tebulina kugendererwako bawulize, wabula okutandika omusango ogusembayo ku abo abagudde ne bonna abeegatta nabo oba abasigala nabo, n'okuleeta obulokozi obusembayo eri abo abatagondera era abakakanyavu abaligonondera Enjiri Ey'olubeerera mu biseera byabwe eby'okubonaabona n'akabi kubanga baasuulirira okulabula kwange mu biseera eby'okulabula ebikomekkerezeddwa kati, era mu kifo ky'okulabula, ebirango kati by'ebiyimiriddewo!! Naye, bonna abakkiriza mu Kristo, ye bonna abo abafunye obuyinza obujjuvu ng'Abaana ba Yahweh ab'emirembe era kati abasibiddwa ku muti gw'omuzeyituuni ogw'obutonde ogwa Isirayiri ya Katonda awalabikira okujulira kwonna tebali wansi w'obusungu bw'Omwana gw'Endiga, newakubadde obusungu bwa Yahweh, Selah!

Kubanga obusungu bw'Omwana gw'endiga bujja kusooka era bujja kugwa n'amaanyi mangi ku batagondera era abatali beesigwa ab'omu nnyumba ya Katonda, ka babe ba Isirayiri oba ab'amawanga. Kubanga kino kisa ekiweereddwa mu kisuubizo okuva mu ndagaano ez'edda ekikyasuubiziddwa era ekiteekeddwa okutuukirizibwa ebiseera byonna n'emirembe gyonna mu Ndagaano Yange Empya ey'okutuukirizibwa okujjuvu! Obusungu bw'Omwana gw'endiga bujja bwe ndisumulula obubonero ku muzingo gwange ogw'obusika, ye kye kiwandiiko ky'ensi era n'obusika bwange obw'amaanyi obuwereddwa mu mikono gyange egy'amaanyi Yahweh yennyini okugabana n'abawanguzi bange kubanga mu byonna nawangula! Abajulirwa ababiri baligolokoka mu kiseera kyabwe era balijja okukola eby'amagero ebikulu era eby'amaanyi, era balibikkula okubikkulirwa kungi, era balitegeeza obujulizi bungi obw'edda mu biseera byabwe eby'okubikkulirwa ebyalagirwa, era baliragira empiiso nnyingi, eby'amagero, obubonero n'eby'ewuunyo ng'oggyeko ebigambo n'ebiragiro by'emitima gyabwe egijjudde okukkiriza n'emimwa gyabwe egijjudde amaanyi! Selah.

Era, mu kiseera kino kiweereddwa ogusota, ensolo eva mu nnyanja, n'omunnabbi ow'obulimba okulwanyisa abatukuvu n'okubawangula. Naye, abo abataganya bulamu bwabwe okutuuka ku nkomerero n'abo bonna abakaabirira erinnya lya Yesu okutuusa ku nkomerero yaabwe balifuna obulokozi obw'emirembe n'obusika obw'amaanyi obw'emirembe obutageraageranyizibwa na kubonaabona kwe balibonaabona ku lw'erinnya lyange. Era Nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, ndikulembera 144,000 bange ab'amaanyi okulokola Yakobo, ye ensigalira eyo ey'amaanyi erinkkiriza nga Masiya waabwe, ye bonna abava mu Isirayiri ab'omu nsibuko y'omusaayi ogw'ekisuubizo era abaliyawulibwa ne batwalibwa nga balamu mu ddungu mu kifo ekibategekerwa Yahweh we balisigala nga bakwekeddwa bulungi okuva mu kibonyoobonyo ekisinga obunene ekyali kibuutikidde ensi, era mazima ddala ebiseera eby'okunakuwala tebiriddayo kubuutikira nsi, kubanga bw'atyo Yahweh, Nze Ani Mukulu, bw'alangiridde!

N'olwekyo, mutegeere Ebigambo byange era muwulire eddoboozi lyange abaagalwa bange, era mutegeere nti muli balokole mu busungu obujja, wadde nga mwenna mukyali ku nsi era mukyalwana entalo nnyingi eziriwo mu biseera bino eby'enkomerero y'olutalo lw'ensigo olwa Olubereberye 3:15 olukyagenda mu maaso n'okutuusa leero wakati wa Yahweh, n'eggulu, ne basitaani abawambye abantu ababi ku nsi okukola bye baagala olw'okwagala ssente n'obulimba bungi obulimbaganya era obusuubiza obuleetera mu nnyanja y'omuliro awali envunyu yaabwe etafa. Naye, nabawangula era entalo zonna zirikoma ku maanyi amakulu n'omukono ogw'amaanyi ogw'Empologoma ey'omu kika kya Yuda, bwe ndijja mu butuukirivu okusala omusango n'okulwana! Selah.

Weewaawo, muli balokole mu busungu era emiryango gya magombe tegiribawangula! N'olwekyo, mweyoleke eri Kristo Yesu Mukama wammwe ow'emirembe, ye oyo eyabakula mu mukono gw'omulabe, weewaawo mweyoleke eri Mukama wammwe kubanga ebiseera eby'okweyoleka kwammwe okw'omu ggulu bituuse. Era ekiseera eky'okusuubira okw'emirembe okukwekeddwa mu mutima gwa Yahweh kati ekibikkuliddwa mu bujjuvu, era ekiseera ekisuubizo eky'emirembe n'okusuubira lwe kisisinkana okutuukirizibwa n'okweyoleka kubanga, Nze, Kristo Yesu, ye Mukama wammwe ow'Emirembe n'Emirembe ow'obutuukirivu ndyolesa abakkiriza mu Kristo eri Yahweh, Nze Ani Mukulu, mu kisenge kye eky'obwakabaka mu ggulu ery'omu ggulu.

ERA NDYOLESA abawulize, nga tebalina bbala newakubadde kamogo, newakubadde ekko, newakubadde ekintu kyonna ekifaanana bwe kityo naye ndibambaza mwenna mu bujjuvu olugoye olweru olutukuvu, olutaliiko bbala era bwe zityo ennyumba zammwe bwe ziribeerera emirembe n'emirembe, kubanga ebyambalo, engoye, n'ennyumba byonna biraga emibiri gyammwe egizuukidde era egyatuukirira mu kanyaanya k'eriiso mu kanyaanya k'eriiso mu kanyaanya k'eriiso mu kanyaanya k'eriiso mu kanyaanya k'eriiso mu kanyaanya k'eriiso bwe nditonda abakkiriza mu Kristo mu kanyaanya k'eriiso mu kwogerera okwo okw'amaanyi okw'omukulu w'abamalayika n'akagombe ako akalungi ak'okutuukirizibwa bwe kalivuga, ne kakomekkereza mu bujjuvu ebiseera byange eby'Ekisa n'okusaasira, ekiraga enkomerero enjjuvu ey'ebiseera by'ab'amawanga, wadde nga, bonna abakaabirira erinnya lya Yesu Omunazaaleeti okutuusa ku nkomerero balilokolebwa mu bujjuvu.

N'olwekyo njija kwolesa abo abalibatyoboola, era bonna abo bangi ku mmwe abakyassaayo omwoyo ku bigambo byabwe ebivundu, era ebigambo eby'obulimba ebiweereddwa mu bulimba okuva mu mitima emibi egifugibwa okwegomba kw'omubiri gwabwe. N'abo abakyunya ebigambo ebitukuvu eby'enjiri yange n'enfumo n'ennyumya ezitali ntukuvu eziri obulimba, ezigendereddwamu mu bujjuvu okuzikiriza bonna abalikkiriza ebigambo bino ebitayinza kukkirizibwa. Baagalwa bange, muteeke essira lyammwe lyonna ku nze n'Ekigambo kyange kubanga nze nnywevu y'ebiseera byammwe n'amaanyi g'obulokozi, era ani ayinza okuyimirira ng'awakanya Empologoma ey'omu kika kya Yuda!?

Laba! Nkuumye ebigambo byange ebitukuvu mu luwalo lw'ebiwandiiko ebya Byzantine era Nze Yeshua HaMashiach, ssaako ne Adonai Yahweh, tubikkudde ebigambo byaffe mu Njiri Ey'olubeerera, era ne mu Katabo ako Akatono ak'obutuukirivu akazizzaawo amazima mangi mu bakkiriza mu Kristo, n'olwekyo, mugeraageranye n'obwegendereza buli kigambo okuva mu kintu kyonna ekigamba nti kyogera ku lwa Yeshua HaMashiach, oba Yahweh, Nze Ani Mukulu okutuuka ku bigambo byange ebitukuvu ebiri mu byawandiikibwa era mwekenneenye okulaga nti mukkirizibwa!

Kubanga obunnabbi bwonna obwali buweereddwa Yeshua HaMashiach oba Adonai Yahweh, tebuyinza kwawukana na mazima amanywevu agaabikkulirwa mu biseera eby'edda newakubadde ebiseera bya kaakano. Mwegaane obulimba bwabwe era mutegeere nti abakkiriza mu Kristo tebaliyita mu kizikiza ekikutte ng'ebibonyoobonyo by'e Misiri, so tebalibeera ku nsi ng'obunnya buggulwawo, so n'ekkanisa yange teriwangulwa miryango gya magombe, nti beekweke mu mayumba gaabwe ng'abatiiru abatayinza kuwangula bubi! Mutegeere, okuvuma kw'abo bonna abogera bino tekulirema kuganyula bonna abagaana okwenenya obusirusiru obw'ekika ekyo.

Mutegeere nate nti, 144,000 balondebwa Yahweh emisingi gy'ensi nga tenateekebwawo okw'ekiseera ekyo. Bakolebwa mu kubonaabona era bonna balina obusobozi n'amaanyi ag'enjawulo. Balitwalibwa mu kunyagululwa kw'abakkiriza naye bateekeddwawo ku lwa Yeshua HaMashiach era baliteekebwako akabonero mu byenyi byabwe mu ggulu. Bajja oluvannyuma ku nsi mu kiseera eky'okubonaabona okukulu okukulembera Isirayiri okutuuka mu bulamu. Naye okusooka batwalibwa okuwasa Omusajja waabwe kubanga be Mugole wange, abayite, abalonde, abeesigwa, era ab'amazima. N'abo be nze, Kristo Yesu, be nfuulye abatukuvu ng'omuwala mu musaayi gwange ogutangirira. N'olwekyo, yenna ayogera ebyawukana n'ebigambo bino tamanyi, so tayogerera nze! Selah!

N'olwekyo mutegeere nti emiryango gya magombe tegirisinga bakkiriza mu Kristo abali Ekkanisa yange ey'amazima kubanga bwe kyawandiikibwa era ne kisuubizibwa edda, era bwe kikyawandiikiddwa era ne kisuubizibwa mu biseera bya kaakano, kubanga Ebigambo bya Yahweh ne Yeshua HaMashiach bigambo bitukuvu nga ffeeza, ebigezeseddwa mu kikoomi ky'ensi, birongooseemu emirundi musanvu. Weewaawo, abagudde tebalina buyinza ku bakkiriza mu Kristo era ndireetera abakkiriza bange okulinnyirira wansi w'ebigere byabwe buli ngeri yonna ey'obwakabaka bw'omulabe, n'okulinnyirira abo abalinoonya okuziyiza ekitaziyizika.

N'olwekyo, mbalagira okubeera n'okukkiriza mu Mukama wammwe ow'Emirembe n'Emirembe era mukkirize Katonda Ataggwaawo ow'obutuukirivu abajjuzemu amaanyi ag'omunda mu maaso ga Mukama wammwe, Kristo Yesu! Mutambule n'okukkiriza okungi mu Yeshua HaMashiach, ye Yesu Omunazaaleeti kubanga mbawadde amaanyi musobole okulinnyirira wansi w'ebigere byammwe enjuyi zonna ez'obwakabaka bw'omulabe. N'olwekyo, mutegeere, nti ne bamalayika abagudde bali wansi w'ebigere byange nga bawanguddwa ddala, era singa musisinkana Sitaani yennyini mwandyagadde kuddamu nga Mikayiri bwe yaddamu bwe yali awakana n'omubiri gwa Musa, ng'ayogera n'okukkiriza okw'amaanyi nti, Mukama akubuulirire, ggwe Sitaani! Era nze ndinenya, era ndisiba omuntu yenna ku bagudde aligezaako okubakolako akabi oba okubaziyiza okukola by'oyo eyabatuma, singa mweyongera okutambulira mu kukkiriza nga mwogera Ebigambo bye ndibawa mu kukkiriza.

N'olwekyo mbalagira mmwe abaagalwa bange okwetegeka, kubanga okugenda kwammwe kuli kumpi! Era njija kubalabula okusigala nga muli beeyawudde ku Babulooni ow'Ekyama mu bintu byonna ne mu ngeri nnyingi nga bwe ndibakulemberamu okw'okutukuzibwa n'okwawulibwa kwammwe. Mugondere Ebigambo by'Enjiri Ey'olubeerera era mukkirize amazima gange gonna agabikkuliddwa mu biseera bino eby'okubikkulirwa okuba ettaala ku bigere byammwe era n'ekkubo lyammwe limulise, kubanga eriyo ekkubo limu lyokka, ye ngeri eyo engolokofu era emfunda etwala mu bulamu obutaggwaawo mu Kristo Yesu Mukama wammwe ow'emirembe!

Mwetegeke, ggwe omugole wange mugule amafuta okuva gye ndi ettaala zammwe zijjule! Amafuta gange gasangibwa mu bigambo byange okwagala kwange, era ne mu bigambo byennyini bye musoma kati era mbawadde ebigambo bingi okubabudaabuda, okubakuuma, n'okubakulembera mu butuukirivu bwonna omubi w'atabakwatako!

Mwetegeke, abaagalwa bange, kubanga bulijjo buba kiseera kya kulongoosa ntambi zammwe, era mulirongoosa entambi zammwe okwenenya ebibi n'okubala ebibala by'omwoyo nga muwangula emirimu gy'omubiri ku lw'omwoyo gwange omutukuvu oguzaalibwa mu mmwe mu bujjuvu, era ogutayinza kuggyibwamu nga gumaze okwesimba mu mitima gy'abantu mu butukuvu bwonna n'obutuukirivu. Mukuume emitima gyammwe bulijjo nga migonvu eri okwagala kwange n'ebiragiro byange, era n'okutuusa lwe munaakuuma nga muli balamu era nga mukuŋŋaanyiziddwa mu kabi konna mu kibatu ky'emikono gyange egy'emirembe.

Mwetegeke, Mmwe Abakkiriza mu Kristo, era mutegeere okuba okumpi kw'essaawa esembayo okukuŋŋaanyizibwa kw'abakkiriza mu Kristo lwe kuba, ne kubaggyawo mwenna ne kubayingiza mu bulamu obujjuvu n'empeera ey'omu ggulu kati eteekeddwa okubikka ensi mu kulabika kwange okw'ekitiibwa. Ekitiibwa kyange bwe kirimasamasa ng'okumyansa okuva ebuvanjuba okutuuka ebugwanjuba, bangi ku mmwe baliraba okumasamasa kw'okujja kwange.

Kale, mazima ddala, omukulu wa bamalayika bw'aliyogerera n'amaanyi mangi mu nsi yonna, ng'akagombe ako ak'amaanyi bwe kalivuga okwetooloola ensi, abafu mu Kristo balizaalibwa okuva mu nsi n'amaanyi amangi ennyo; olwo mmwe abali abalamu era abalisigalawo mulisisinkanira wamu nabo okusisinkana Mukama wammwe ow'Emirembe n'Emirembe ow'obutuukirivu mu bbanga, era amangu ago mulisanga nga mwambadde ekitiibwa ekijjuvu n'obulungi obw'ekitiibwa ennyo, nga muyimiridde mu ggulu ery'omu ggulu. Muliraba mu bujjuvu, muliwulira mu bujjuvu, mulimanya nga bwe mumanyiddwa, era bangi ku mmwe muliddamu okubeera awamu n'abaagalwa bammwe era bonna balisanyuka!

Mmwe abali abalamu era abalisigalawo mulitegeera ekintu ekyo kye mulisanga nga kibuutikiddemu era muliwulira ebigambo nti "Mujje Wano waggulu!" era muliwulira nga musikulwa n'amaanyi mangi era amangu waggulu mu kumasamasa kw'ekitiibwa kyange n'obulumi bw'okuzaala bulikoma ng'abaana ba Yahweh ab'emirembe bazaalibwa mu ggulu gye baliyimirira mu kitiibwa ekinene n'empeera ey'emirembe ng'okukkiriza kwabwe bwe kwali.

Nze, Kristo Yesu, Mukama ow'Emirembe n'Emirembe ow'obutuukirivu eyasindise Ebigambo bino ng'ayita mu mubaka wange ow'amaanyi era omuweereza, era ng'ayita mu mubaka wange ow'amaanyi era omuweereza Ebigambo byange bisindisiddwa, era abakkiriza mu Kristo babeere nga bategekeddwa ddala era nga balindirira okusemberera kw'Omusajja waabwe, kati asembereze omugole we! Selah.

Mwetegeke, Mmwe Abakkiriza mu Kristo, kubanga essaawa y'okununulibwa kwammwe okujjuvu eri kumpi okubaggyawo mu ngeri etasuubirwa okuva mu bizibu byonna ebirigwa ku nsi mu musango omunene era ogw'entiisa kubanga ebiseera by'omusango bituuse era birina okutuukirizibwa, mwawukane kwokka mu byonna mu kigambo ne mu kikolwa n'abatali bakkiriza muleme okugabana mu misango gyabwe, newakubadde okugabana mu bibonyoobonyo byabwe.

Mwetegeke, ggwe omugole wange, mujje gye ndi mu kwenenya okujjuvu era muve mu bubi bwonna ettaala zammwe zijjule n'entambi zammwe zirongoosebwe era mube nga mwetegekedde Omusajja wammwe asemberera ddala n'essaawa y'olugendo lwange esembera okuggwa! Selah!

Eri ekkanisa yange, omubiri gwange, era n'omugole wange, njogera nti ebiseera byammwe eby'okununulibwa okujjuvu bituuse, n'olwekyo, munindirire era mube beetegefu era munywerere ku bujulizi bwe mbawadde kubanga obujulizi buno bulibayimirizaawo era bubakuume okuva ku mubi asemberera ddala ng'alina obukyayi obungi n'obusungu obutali bwa mmwe. Mutegeere nti nnenya era nziyiza abagudde okusinga yenna ku mmwe bw'amanyi era baziyizibwa okutuusa omuziyiza wange lw'aliggibwawo. N'olwekyo, mwekuume nga muli beeyawudde ku bibiina ebisalirwa omusango era temwetaba mu nkuŋŋaana zaabwe kubanga zireese dda akavuyo n'okuzikiriza eri abo abaliweebwa omusango mu bujjuvu nga bwe gutyo omuziyiza wange bw'aliggibwawo. N'olwekyo, temutya bintu bye boogera, newakubadde okutya bintu bye bakola newakubadde okutya bintu bye bateekateeka era bye beekuluntaza nti ddala balibikola, kubanga bino si bya bakkiriza mu Kristo, n'olwekyo, temubatya.

Ani ayinza okuyimirira ng'awakanya Empologoma ey'omu kika kya Yuda n'obusungu obujjuvu n'amaanyi g'amaanyi gange!? Muwulire Ebigambo byange era mumuwulire bulungi, era mmwe mwenna abagudde okuva ku muto okutuuka ku mukulu. Nze, Yesu Kristo, era Kristo Yesu, nze oyo alangirira mu ntebe ey'obwakabaka n'obuyinza bwa Yahweh, Nze Ani Mukulu, nti nze oyo ajja, era ddala nditwala obwannannyini obujjuvu bwa bonna abanunulibwa mu bujjuvu n'omusaayi gwange ogutangirira era abassibwako akabonero ka Katonda omulamu, ye Yahweh, Nze Ani Mukulu, era ebiseera byabwe bituuse. N'olwekyo, sirigaanibwa era amaanyi gammwe ag'okunziyiza galigwira ddala ku mitwe gy'abo be mutuma okugezaako. Ani ali mu mmwe ayinza okuyimirira ng'awakanya amaanyi gange n'amaanyi gange!? Ani?!

Ebyo Yahweh, Nze Ani Mukulu by'ayagala bikolebwe emirembe n'emirembe, omulundi gumu, byonna n'emirembe n'emirembe! Laba, nsembereze, era bonna abanoonya okuziyiza baliremererwa ddala mu kuwangulwa okujjuvu n'omusango gwe bataliyinza kuwona, emirembe n'emirembe. Selah!
