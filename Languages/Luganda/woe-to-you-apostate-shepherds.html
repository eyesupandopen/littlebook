Omujulirwa Omulamba: Ekkanisa
Mu Biwandiiko by'Omutume Pawulo
May 04, 2024

Zibasanze Mmwe Abasumba Abajeemu!

Zibasanze mmwe abasumba abajeemu, kubanga musimbye amakanisa gammwe am'obulimba okubuna ensi zonna, era temunjogerera, so ssaalagiranga mmwe mu mirimu gye mwetwalira. Era mufuudde ensimbi katonda wammwe, kubanga mwesiga eby'amaguzi eby'obugwagwa, era muyigirizza abantu bange nti okugaggawala mu by'ensi ye kusaasira. Zibasanze mmwe abasumba abajeemu, kubanga ebigambo bya Yeremiya bibanunula bulungi: Endogoyi ey'omu ddungu eyateera, efuuyirira empewo mu kwagala kwayo; mu kiseera kyayo ani ayinza okugikyusa? Kubanga obwenzi n'obwamalaaya bwammwe buli wamu n'ekibi, kubanga mulonze Basetaani okuba katonda wammwe, era abo abalangira abataliiko mugaso si bakatonda. Muli baweereza ba bulimba abeefuula abaweereza b'obutuukirivu, era tekyewuunyisa, kubanga Setaani yeefuula malayika ow'ekitangaala.

Zibasanze mmwe abasumba abajeemu, kubanga musimbye amakanisa agafuudde amazzi gange amangi ag'obulamu okuba ng'omusuusa ogukaawa, ge muweereza abo be nnayita, amazzi ag'ekivuguto amacaafu agakaawa mu lubuto. N'amazzi gammwe agakaawa gaweerezebwa mu ndobo ezibobye, era temulina maanyi, wadde nga mwekuluntaza olw'amaanyi amangi. Mwonoonye ensi n'enjigiriza zammwe ez'obulimba ne muzimba amakanisa gammwe am'obulimba ng'amakomera ag'eby'omwoyo agonooneddwa gavumenti yammwe, kubanga mutunze ekisibo kyange mu mukono gw'omulabe; kyemunaava mufuluma omwo kaakano, mmwe abantu bange! Amakanisa gammwe am'obujeemu agatalina mugaso gakozesebwa ng'ebifo eby'okufugirako mwe mulireetera okufa n'okuzikirira abo abalisigalawo, era mujja kusasula.

Mufuudde Lemufani Katonda wammwe. Muweereza Baali. Muwaayo abaana eri Moloki. Mwononefu ddala. Muli nzizzi ezitaliimu mazzi, bintu ebitaliimu omwoyo ebisaanira okuzikirizibwa, kubanga omugabo gwammwe ye sulufeeti ow'omuliro, era mujja kuba mu nnyanja eyo ennene ne kitammwe, Omulyolyomi. Muli batume ba bulimba, bannabbi ba bulimba, ababuulizi b'enjiri ba bulimba, abasumba ba bulimba, n'abayigiriza ba bulimba; era abo be mukyusa okudda mu makubo gammwe amabi bafuuka emirundi ebiri abaana b'e Geyeena okusinga mmwe, kubanga muli ba kuvunda okwonoonera ddala. Kubanga Sitefano yategeeza ebikwata ku makubo gammwe bwe yayogera ku bajjajjammwe, eyo emiddo emibi egyatigomya Isirayiri, kubanga yagamba nti: Yee, mwasitula Eweema ya Moloki, n'emmunyeenye ya katonda wammwe Lemufani, ebifaananyi bye mwakola, okubisinza. Mmwe abasumba abajeemu muteeka n'emmunyeenye ya Lemufani awatali nsonyi gye muyita ya Dawudi, jjajjange; kubanga nze, Yesu Kristo, nva mu lulyo olwo, era ndifugira ku ntebe ya Dawudi mu butuukirivu. Kubanga nze y’oyo eyaweebwa okufuga okwenkanidde awo, kubanga ndifuga ensi mu butuukirivu!

Zibasanze mmwe abasumba abajeemu, ndibasasulira ddala ku bwammwe olw'ebigambo byammwe byonna ebibi, n'amakubo gammwe gonna amabi galibakomererwa ku mitwe gyammwe emirembe gyonna. Kubanga mujja kwegatta ku Mulyolyomi ne bamalayika be mu mugabo gwabwe okuva eri Katonda Ali Waggulu Ennyo; era Yahweh abategekedde ennyanja ennene ey'omuliro ogutazikira. Mugaanyi obulokozi abo abaanoonyeza, naye mumanye nti ndibalokola era mmwe mujja kusasula. Mugaanyi omwoyo gwange abo abandyatutte erinnya lyange ng'abawa ebigambo eby'obulimba okwogera n'okusaba okw'obuwala, okw'obwereere, n'okw'okuddiŋŋana okutalokola. Mumpulirize, mmwe abantu bange: obulokozi bujja lwa kwatula Yesu nga ye Mukama wo n'okukkiriza nti Katonda yanzuukiza mu bafu, era mu nkolagana yokka ey'obulokozi nange mwe muyinza okulokokerera; kubanga nsiba akabonero ka Katonda ku abo abaatula erinnya lyange n'omutima ogutalina kukkiriza, kubanga nnoonya mu mitima ne nkema ensigo.

Mulowooza n'amalala nti kubanga munnyita Mukama wange mu maaso g'ekibiina kyammwe kye mwawamba nti mmanyi mmwe? Ndibagamba ku lunaku olwo nti, munveeko, mmwe abakozi b'ebibi, kubanga ssaabamanya! Muyigiriza obulimba mu kifo ky'amazima, era abo abanoonya nze, Mukama wange Yesu Kristo, mubakyamya ne mubayingiza mu njiri eyononefu, ey'obusiru, kubanga tewali njiri ndala: Kubanga amawulire amalungi gali mu nze, Kristo Yesu! Okwogera kwammwe okw'obwereere kunaatera okuggwaawo, n'obusungu bwange bubaliiridde, era temujja kuwonawo. Ndaba buli kikolwa eky'obuvumi kye mukola; mpulira buli kigambo eky'obuvumi kye mwogera mu linnya lyange, kubanga muli befuulifuula. Mwefuula nti mwogera ebigambo byange, nga bwe mwefuula nti nze nabatuma n'emboozi zammwe ez'okwefuulifuula n'ebyamagero byammwe eby'okwefuulifuula n'okuwonya kwammwe okw'okuteekateeka, kubanga muli befuulifuula!

Zibasanze mmwe abasumba abajeemu, kubanga mubba ku abo abandyajjiddeko, ne mubalimba nti Katonda abalagidde okubawa ssente. Munyoolanyoola Ebyawandiikibwa Byange Ebitukuvu mu butamanya, ne mugamba abo be mwakyamya nti balina okubawa ekitundu eky'ekkumi. Kubanga kiki kye nnagamba Pawulo bwe yawandiikira abo ab'e Kkolinso: Naye kino nkyogera, Asiga obuke, era alikungula buke: n'oyo asiga obungi, era alikungula bungi. Buli muntu nga bw'amala mu mutima gwe, bw'atyo agabenga; si lwa kulumwa, newakubadde lwa kuwalirizibwa: kubanga Katonda ayagala agaba n'essanyu. Naye nedda, mmwe muyigiriza obulimba mu kifo ky'amazima ne mubba ku bantu bange, era n'ensimbi ze mukung'aanya muziweereza Setaani okutuukiriza ebigendererwa bye, era mujja kusasula, kubanga obusungu bwange bubookya nnyo kubanga mukulembera abantu abanoonya nze, Yesu Kristo, ne mubaggya ku bulokozi ne mubaleetera okuzikirira. Naye kino mukimanye, mmwe abasumba abajeemu, temujja kusikula n'omu ku bano mu ngalo zange, kubanga ku bonna Kitange be yampa, nnasubwa omu yekka, omwana w'okuzikirira, ye Yuda, kubanga yeeronderawo okuzikirira okusinga obulamu mu nze! Emirimu gyammwe n'okutegana kwammwe bya bwereere, mmwe abasumba abajeemu; mweterekedde okusalirwa omusango omunene n'obusungu. Kubanga nnayogera ku makubo ga bajjajjammwe, era mmwe be muli abalya ennyumba za bannamwandu era ku lw'okwefuula musaba essaala empanvu: MMWE mujja kufuna okusalirwa omusango okusingawo!

Zibasanze mmwe abasumba abajeemu, kubanga mukulembedde abantu bange okuwagira gavumenti z'ensi ezitali zange, wadde ng'ensi yange. O, mmwe abasumba abajeemu, mulaga abantu bange abakulembeze b'ensi ab'Omulyolyomi, ne mubagamba nti batumiddwa Katonda. Mukyamya abantu ne balowooza nti sijja kubawa mukisa okuggyako nga bawa omukisa abo abatta abatalina musango. Naye ki kye nnagamba mu byawandiikibwa ku mmwe, kubanga kino si kipya, era bangi baabakulembera mu bulimba, wadde nga muli basiru okumala okulowooza nti muli ba njawulo. Mumpulirize, mmwe abaana bange, kubanga mbikkula ekibi kino ku lwammwe. Kubanga ekyawandiikibwa kyange kigamba nti: Mmwe muli ba Katonda, baana bato, era mubawangudde: kubanga ali mu mmwe ye mukulu okusinga ali mu nsi. Abo ba mu nsi: kyebava boogera eby'ensi, n'ensi ebewulira. Okubuulira kwammwe okw'ensi, enkola zammwe ez'ensi, olukwe lwammwe olw'ensi ne Setaani, byonna bijja kukoma mangu ddala, era temulizuukira nate. Naye ggwe, Omugole wange, oli wange, era ebigambo bino si bibyo, kubanga ojja kuyingira mu ssanyu lya Mukama wo, ye nze, Kristo Yesu.

Njogera kaakano eri ebibiina by'amakanisa am'obujeemu agakulemberwa abasumba abajeemu, era nawe, ekibiina kyange eky'e Lawodikiya. Mmanyi ebikolwa byo, nti toli munnyogovu so toli mwokye; nandiyagadde nti obeere munnyogovu oba mwokye. Kubanga Pawulo yawandiika Timoseewo ku ggwe n'abalala abali nga ggwe: Kubanga ekiseera kirijja lwe bataligumiikiriza njigiriza nnungi, naye olw'okwegomba kwabwe kwennyini balyefunira abayigiriza, nga balina amatu agasiwa: Era balikyusa amatu gaabwe okuva mu mazima, ne bakyamira mu ngero. Bannabyo bangi nnyo beeyita mu makanisa gano am'obujeemu olw'okutya abantu n'okulaga nti bagezaako okunoonya nze, Kristo Yesu, ekintu kye batakola ddala.

Mwagala kusanyusibwa, kubanga okusanyusibwa kye kwegomba kwammwe, era okusanyusibwa kufuuse ekifaananyi kyammwe! Era temujja kumpulira, nze Yesu Kristo, so temujja kuwuliriza na n'omu ku babaka bange okuggyako ng'amatu gammwe gasiisirwa, naye ebigambo byange tebisiisira matu! Mmwe ebibiina eby'abajeemu: Mumpulire era mumpulire bulungi, kubanga nnagamba mu kitabo ekinene eky'Okubikkulirwa: Ne mpulira eddoboozi eddala eriva mu ggulu, nga ligamba nti, Mufulume omwo, mmwe abantu bange, muleme okuba n'ebibi bye, era muleme okufuna ku bibonyoobonyo bye: Kubanga ebibi bye bituuse mu ggulu, ne Katonda ajjukidde obutali butuukirivu bwe. Mufulume omwo, oba muligabana ebibonobono bye n'omusango gwe, kubanga ekiseera kituuse. Eri mmwe ab'e Lawodikiya: Muleke amakanisa gammwe am'obujeemu kaakano, kubanga ekiseera kiri kumpi, era mbalabula, wadde nga bangi ku mmwe bagayaavu nnyo okukola ku bigambo byange.

Temulonzalonza ku kulabula kwange, kubanga kiki kye nnawa Yokaana okuwandiika mu bbaluwa ye ey'okubiri? Buli muntu asukka n'atabeera mu njigiriza ya Kristo, talina Katonda: oyo abeera mu njigiriza ya Kristo, alina Kitaffe n'Omwana. Bw'abaako ajja gye muli, n'ataleeta njigiriza eno, temumwaniriza mu nnyumba yammwe, so temumusuubiza mirembe. Kubanga oyo amusuubiza emirembe, aba mugabi w'ebikolwa bye ebibi. Naye mmwe ab'e Lawodikiya temuwulira nnyo n'okutuusa lw'obusuula ebigambo bino era mu kifo ky'okubasuubiza emirembe, ekibi kyokka, mugenda wala mu kibi ne mubayita mu nnyumba zammwe ne mubawa omukisa okutuuka ku baana bammwe, era mulina omusango gwonna mu maaso gange, kale MWENENYE!

Kubanga mazima, mwaggulira ensi n'amakubo gaayo enzigi; mwaleka emisege, n'abasinga obubi, okuliisa abaana bange, era okuggyako nga mwenenyezza, mujja kusasula. Mwenenye oba ndijja okulwana nammwe, kubanga muli bagabi b'ebikolwa byabwe ebibi, era temuwulira nze, Kristo Yesu, ye wuuyo alibaggyako empeera yammwe n'agiwa omulala singa mweyongera okunnemesa. Kubanga ekisa kyange n'okusaasira byange binaatera okuggwaawo, n'obusungu bulidda mu kifo kyabwo, wadde ng'ekisa n'okusaasira biri nange bulijjo, era buli muntu alikoowoola erinnya lya Mukama okutuusa ku nkomerero alirokolebwa. Kubanga temuyinza kuba na nkolagana na makubo ag'ekizikiza agatalina bibala, wabula mulina okubanenya.

Naye muli basiru nnyo okulowooza nti kubanga omuntu alimba n'akozesa erinnya lyange ettukuvu mu buvumi ku mirimu gye egitali mitukuvu nti atumiddwa nze n'ayogera ebigambo byange. Muwulire ebigambo byange, kubanga ku bibala byabwe kwe mulibamanyira! Mudde eri Ekigambo kyange eky'amazima ekya Katonda muyige ku nze, kubanga temummanyi era eddoboozi lyange ligenyi gye muli, era temulina Kitaffe kubanga temulina Mwana. Mukyukire gye ndi nga mwenenyezza, nange ndibaaniriza. Mutuule mu bibukutu n'evvu mu mitima gyammwe era mwetoowaze nsobole okubagulumiza! Nnabayita okuwangula, era kati ge mukisa gwammwe ogusembayo okuwangula abasumba bano abajeemu n'amakubo gaabwe amabi; kubanga njija, era ndeeta obusungu bungi ku Babulooni Ekikwekwe.

Obudde bwe bumu ku bintu bye mutakyalina. Temuyinza kugayaala nate, oba mulisanga nga muli mu kutegana kwa Yakobo, lwe nandibateereddewo ekifo ku mmeeza yange ne mbawasa ng'omugole wange, naye temuli beesigwa muli mu mikono gy'omulala, ye Setaani! Kale mujje gye ndi nsobole okubaddiza empeera yammwe. Tekimala kufuluma n'ekinyegenyege; mulina okwenenya obubi buno ne mukyusa amakubo gammwe n'okwatula ebibi bino gye ndi, nange ndibayongereza mu musaayi gwange ogutangirira ne mbasiimula amabala ku mmwe mu buyonjo bwange obutuukirivu, kubanga nze ndi muyonjo n'ebyambalo byange byeru ng'omuzira omupya. Mudde gye ndi era mujja kubeera nange emirembe gyonna; mufulume mujje mu mikono gyange emigoloddwa, kubanga ndi wano okubagamba nti temulina budde bwa kusubwa.

Eri Omugole wange omwesigwa, eyawuliriza eddoboozi lyange n'agoberera amakubo gange: Ggwe nkugamba, weeyongere okuba omwesigwa gye ndi, era bwe wabaawo n'omu ku mmwe alina ekigere mu luggi lw'ekkanisa y'e Lawodikiya, nkulagira okukiggyayo mu mulyango oluggi luggalwe! Kubanga toyinza kukola ekyo Mukama wo ky'atakola! Temusomanga bbaluwa yange eri ekkanisa y'e Lawodikiya mu Kubikkulirwa? Laba, nnyimiridde ku luggi, ne nkonkona: omuntu yenna bw'awulira eddoboozi lyange, n'aggulawo oluggi, ndiyingira gy'ali, ne ndya naye, naye nange. Weetegereze, saagambye nti ndiyingira mu Kkanisa y'e Lawodikiya, naye omuntu yenna ali mu kkanisa y'e Lawodikiya bw'awulira eddoboozi lyange n'aggulawo oluggi, ndiyingira ne ndya naye, si kkanisa ye ey'edda ey'obujeemu, wadde nga nabo mbayita bwe baba bawuliriza. Mugole wange, omuweereza si mukulu okusinga mukama we, kale goberera amakubo gange.

Mugole wange, nsembere ddala okubakwata okubatwala mu ggulu, kale mweyongere okwetegeka era mweyongere okulangirira okujja kwange. Kubanga ntumye bangi ku mmwe mu mulimu gwa Yokaana Omubatiza okulanga okujja kwange n'okusaba okwenenya. Ntumye bangi ku mmwe mu mulimu gwa Peetero okulunda n'okuliisa ekisibo. Ntumye abamu ku mmwe ng'abawanika b'ekyama ekinene ekya Katonda, mu mulimu gw'Omutume Pawulo (n'abawanika abalala ab'ekyama). Ntumye bangi ku mmwe mu mulimu gwa Firipo Omubuulizi w'Enjiri, kale buulira ekigambo; beera mwangu mu biseera n'ebitali biseera; nenya, kangavvula, buulirira n'okugumiikiriza kwonna n'enjigiriza. Ntumye abamu ku mmwe mu mulimu gw'Omutume Yokaana, eyali n'omusumba n'alabirira ekisibo n'okwagala, obuwombeefu, n'okusaasira, n'okuwonya ebiwundu ebyateekebwa ku ndiga zange omubi. Temukibuusabuusa, kubanga nabatuma mu mirimu gyabwe; kubanga ekiseera kituuse, kubanga nnaatera okuwasa Omugole wange, kubanga njija ku lwammwe, mmwe ab'omukwano gwange! Mumaleko emisinde gyammwe, kubanga ndi kumpi. Obudde buweddeyo.

Nze Yesu Omunazaaleeti.

Nze wuuyo alifuga okuva ku ntebe ya Dawudi, kubanga bwe ntyo bwe nsibuka.

Mbikkula ekibi kino, wadde nga Setaani ayagala kisigale nga kya kyama.

Mbalabula, wadde nga bangi ku mmwe bagayaavu okukola ku bigambo byange.

Njija, era ndeeta obusungu bungi ku Babulooni Ekikwekwe.

Ndi muyonjo n'ebyambalo byange byeru ng'omuzira omupya.

Ndi wano okubagamba nti temulina budde bwa kusubwa.

Nkoowoola, naddala ggwe, Ekkanisa yange ey'e Lawodikiya.

Nsembere ddala okubakwata okubatwala mu ggulu.

Nnaatera okuwasa Omugole wange.

Njija ku lwammwe, mmwe ab'omukwano gwange!

Ndi kumpi era mbagala nnyo ddala; muli mu masekkati g'omutima gwange.

Nze ssuubi lyammwe n'obulokozi, ye Mukama wa bakama ne Kabaka wa bakabaka.

Nze Yeshua HaMashiach.
