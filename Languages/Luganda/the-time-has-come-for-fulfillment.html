Ekikaawa Ekirimu Obuwoomu
Okitobba 29, 2024

Ekiseera kituuse eky'Okutuukirizibwa: Weewaawo era Amiina

Okuva eri Yeshua HaMashiach:

Oyo Musa gwe yalagira Isiraeri okuwulirizanga n'okugonderanga.

Mutegere amatu muwulire ebigambo bya Yeshua HaMashiach mu kiseera kino eky’enkomerero, era mukotamye emitima gyammwe mu kugonda era mukkirize emitima gyammwe okukkiriza okuyigirizibwa kwange. Kubanga ebigambo byange bwe bulamu eri abo ababizuula era buzima biweesa obulamu obulungi n’okusuubiza, ne mu biseera eby'okubonaabona okungi. Kale mutegeere nti okubonaabona okungi kutuuse era akabi kasembedde, n'ebiseera bya kaakano. Mazima, mazima mu mazima ag'emirembe nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, yye Empologoma ey'omu kika kya Yuda ajja okusala omusango n'okulwana, era mutegeere nti tewali kintu na kimu ekinkisiddwa, so tewali kintu na kimu ekikisiddwa mu maaso ga Yakuwa era ndaba obutali bwenkanya n'okuttibwa kw'abantu ab'omu nsi okukolebwa abalumbaganyi abava mu Ssinagoga ya Sitaani era ndisasula, bw'ayogera Omukama Omutuukirivu!

Kubanga ddala, okumala emyaka mingi egy'ebijaguzo abagudde bayongedde okutumbula ekyama kyabwe eky'obutali butuukirivu, ne mu biseera bya kaakano awa entegeka zaabwe we ziri okutuukirira mu bujjuvu. Naye mutegeere nti bakkirizibwa kutuukiriza bigendererwa bya Yakuwa byokka mu ntegeka ze ez'emirembe n'emirembe ez'okulokola abantu okutuuka ku kununulibwa kwabwe okujjuvu. Kubanga omuntu ow'obutonde asiiriziddwa mu tulo mu bulimba obungi n’obwegendereza obungi.

Era bwe batyo abagudde bakozesa abantu okutumbula okuzikirizibwa kw'abantu kwennyini nga mu kiseera kye kimu bayita omusana ekizikiza n'ekizikiza omusana. Era bwe kityo ne Ssinagoga ya Sitaani eyalumba ensi ez'obusuubize era nja kugisimbulawo ddala. Kubanga Ssinagoga ya Sitaani enoonya okwezza ebisuubizo bya Yakuwa ebyawandiikibwa era ne bikakasibwa eri Bakitaffe ab'edda. Ssinagoga ya Sitaani ejjudde omujiijo omubi ogwasimbibwa abagudde mu ngano yange era ekiseera eky'okugusimbulawo kituuse era bwe kityo bwe kyawandiikibwa era bwe kityo bwe kirikolebwa: Kubanga, laba, olunaku lujja, oluliyaka ng'ekikoomi; n'abamalala bonna, weewaawo, na bonna abakola ebibi, baliba bisasiro: n'olunaku olujja lulibookya, bw'ayogera Mukama ow'eggye, so telulibalekera muzi wadde ettabi.

Naye, bwe kityo bwe kyatuukirizibwa abagudde nti nnabbubi aluse olutimba lwe mu nsi yonna kaakano, era mu bulimba balwana. Era kati kye kiseera kyabwe eky'okugolokokamu n'obufuzi bwabwe obutali butukuvu obw'ekiseera ekitono buli kumpi. Kubanga Yakuwa mu butuukirivu bwe ajja kuwa omuntu ow'obutonde ekyo kyennyini ky'ayagala mu busirusiru bwe mu kunoonya kwe okw'essanyu ly'ekibi. Era bwe kityo ekizikiza n'obubi omuntu ow'obutonde by'ayagala omuntu ow'obutonde kati ajja kubifuna. Naye, Enjiri yange bw'eba nga ekisiddwa, ekisiddwa eri abo ababula. Abo katonda w'ensi eno be yaziba amaaso g'emitima gyabwe abatakkiriza, omusana gw'enjiri ey'ekitiibwa eya Kristo, ye kabonero ka Katonda, guleme okubakaayakira. Era bwe kityo omuntu ow'obutonde mu butamanya bwe yeegasse ne nnabbubi mu bulimba bwe, kubanga mu bulimba balwana.

Kwe kuva El Shaddai n'abatumira obulimba obw'amaanyi, bakkirize obulimba obukoleddwa abagudde, bonna balyoke basalirwe omusango abataakkiriza mazima, naye abaasanyukira obutali butuukirivu. Naye mu musango gwa Yakuwa n’okusaasira kwe okutaggwaawo, essanyu kaakano liggyibwa ku nsi n'ebintu ebirungi omuntu ow'obutonde by'alulunkanira ennyo okukung'aanya bijja kuggyibwa ku nsi. Era bwe kityo n'essanyu ly'ekibi lijja kuggyibwa ku nsi, n'okutuusa abantu lwe balituuka ku ddalala nga banoonya okumatiza okwegomba kw'emitima gyabwe. Kyokka, Yakuwa talonda bikolwa bino kuba mukambwe eri ekitonde kye naye kubonereza abakola ebibi n'okuzuukusa abo abandyaganye okuzuukuka mu tulo twabwe kubanga okuyita mu ssanyu ly'ekibi n'empeera y'obutali butuukirivu bafuuse bagevvu era bazibe b'amaaso. Mu kifo ky'omusango omujjuvu ogw'amangu ago omuntu ow'obutonde aweebwa ekisa kubanga omuntu ow'obutonde awanguddwa abagudde.

Yee, mu kisa n’okusaasira kwa Yakuwa ne mu ntegeka ye ey’emirembe n’emirembe Yakuwa ddala anoonya okuzuukusa omuntu ow'obutonde okuva mu kibi n'okuyita omuntu ow'obutonde okujja mu butuukirivu. Ku kuyooleka okwo abangi beeyanirizza kyeyagalire obulokozi bwange mu biseera byange eby'ekisa okuyita mu Njiri ey'Obulokozi. Mu biseera ebyo, obulokozi bwali kirabo kya bwereere omuntu w'asobolera okunfuula Omukama we n'afuna obudde okukolera obulokozi bwe ye kennyini ng'atya era ng'akankana nga bwe bajja gyendi mu bujjuvu n'okutuuka ne bava mu kibi ddala. Era bwe kityo bwe kiri nti ebiseera byange eby'ekisa bituuse ku nkomerero yaabyo, wadde nga ekisa n'okusaasira biri nange bulijjo. Era mu bbanga ttono kijja kutuuka nti ebiseera by'okulinda tebijja kubaawo nate n'obwakabaka bw'ensi eno bujja kufuuka obwakabaka bw'Omukama wammwe, n'obwa Kristo wammwe, era nja kufuga emirembe n'emirembe n'abeesigwa mu busika bwaffe obw'emirembe n'emirembe bwe nja okugabana mu bujjuvu n'abawangula bange.

Era ekibiina kinene kijja kujja gyendi mu kiseera ky'okubonaabona kwa Yakobo okubalokola emirembe gyonna. Era buli muntu alikoowoola erinnya lya Mukama okutuusa ku nkomerero alirokolebwa, ka bibe lulimi ki oba ggwanga ki mwe bakoowoolera. Era nnyimirirawo okulokola Isiraeri, era mpita ab'oluganda lwange ab'amazima abaasendebwasendebwa amadiini ag'obulimba era abaajoogebwanga Ssinagoga ya Sitaani, era bajja kufuna obulokozi obw'emirembe n'emirembe era nja kulokola Yakobo obulokozi bwe bujja okutuukirizibwa n'omuliro ogulongoosa. Naye mutegeere nti ebiseera eby'obulamu obuwanvu n'obugagga obw'ebintu ku nsi bituuse ku nkomerero okutuusa Obwakabaka bwange lwe bunaaba bunywezeddwa mu bbanga ttono, era ensi ekwatiddwa mu njegere ng'erindiridde emisango gyayo egy'enkomerero okugifukirwako mu busungu obungi. Laba! Obusungu bw'Omwana gw'Endiga bujja kusooka, n'ebizibu ebinene bijja kusaasaanira ensi ng'omwegulo gw'okuzikiriza okutaliiko kkomo, era abangi balizikirira.

Naye, ebizibu ebinene n’okubonaabona bijja kusumululwa obubonero bwe bulimenyebwa n’obusungu obungi, ne kawumpuli n’endwadde, n’enjala, n’ekyeya, n’olutalo, n’obutali bwenkanya, n’okufa n’ebirala bingi bijja kujja mu bujjuvu ku nsi; naye ekibiina ky'ebibiina kijja kuzuukuka okuva mu mbeera yaabyo ey'ekivuvu. Era baliba mu nnaku nnyingi n'okulumwa kw'emmeeme bwe balitegeera amazima agatasobola kugaanyizibwa ag'ebyawandiikibwa n'engeri gye baasubwa mu bukakanyavu omukisa gwabwe ogw'okulokolebwa n'okuwona obusungu n'omusango ku kukuŋŋaana kw'Ekkanisa yange ennesigwa gyendi. Naye mu kubonaabona kwabwe okukaawa n'okulumwa kwabwe nja kubassaamu obumalirivu obutasobola kuwangulwa era nja kubasika okujja gyendi mu bujjuvu, abo bonna abajja gyendi nga babonaabona era nga balumwa n'okuyita mu kugezesebwa kw'okufiirira okukkiriza. Era balifuna empeera nnene, empeera nnene nnyo n'okutuusa ebizibu byabwe ebijja lwe bitasaana kugeraageranyizibwa na kitiibwa kya lubeerera ekigenda okubikkulirwa mu bujjuvu mu bo.

Bwe kityo bwe kyawandiikibwa era bwe kityo bwe kirimalirizibwa okutuuka ku kkomo: Bano be bava mu kubonaabona okungi, ne booleza ebyambalo byabwe, ne babyeruza mu musaayi gw'Omwana gw'endiga. Kyebava babeera mu maaso g'entebe ya Katonda, ne bamuweereza emisana n'ekiro mu yeekaalu ye: n'oyo atuula ku ntebe alituula mu bo. Tebaliddamu kulumwa njala, so tebaliddamu kulumwa nnyonta; so n'enjuba teribookya, newakubadde ebbugumu lyonna. Kubanga Omwana gw'endiga ali wakati mu ntebe, alibaliisa, n'abatwala eri enzizi z'amazzi amalamu: ne Katonda alibasiimula amaziga gonna mu maaso gaabwe.

Era bwe kityo nnabbubi aluse olutimba lwe okwetooloola ensi yonna mu bulimba obungi, okukanga, okunyaga era mu ngeri nnyingi obusagwa bwa nnabbubi bukola obubi bwabwo okuyita mu ngeri zabwo ez'obulimba n'obusagwa. Kubanga aluse olutimba lw'obulimba era aluse olutimba lw'amabanja n'okutuusa ensi lw'ekwatiddwa mu bujjuvu mu kuwamba kwa nnabbubi era ekalidde olw'obusagwa bwayo. Nnabbubi atendense n'obukujjukujju obungi n'obulimba okuyita mu kyama ky'obutali butuukirivu, era ensi kati eri mu lutimba lwayo mu bujjuvu. Era mu bulimba obungi abefuula bano (abakolebwa abagudde) balumbye ensi ng'enzige. Mu ngeri y'emu nnabbubi mw'abumbako amagi gaayo okutuusa ekibiina ky'abaana baayo lwe kyaluluma ne kiddukira okweyongerayo okuluka entimba zaakyo wamu nayo. Era bwe kityo essinagoga ya sitaani litutte ensi yonna n'amaanyi era n'obukujjukujju.

Era bwe wayiseewo ekiseera era okuyita mu nkwe nnyingi n’entegeka befuulidde ddala ab'amaanyi, kubanga bwe batyo bwe baweebwa Yakuwa mu ntegeka ze ez’emirembe n’emirembe ez’okulokola okutuukiriza. Kubanga omuntu ow'obutonde azibiddwa amaaso n'obulimba bwabwe era abagoberera mu butamanya okutuuka ku kuzikirira kwe ye kennyini. Ku ntegeka ezo ze njogerako kwe kulikomya okwawula okw’enkomerero wakati w'engano n'omujiijo n'awakati w'abo abeewaayo mu bujjuvu n'abo abagala ebibi byabwe era abaalonze n'obwegendereza ennimi z'omuliro mu kwagala kwabwe okw'ekivve okw'okuzikirizibwa kwabwe kwennyini, era balyeggyaamu emirembe n'emirembe olw'okulonda kwe baalonze okwenkana n'ebigendererwa bya nnabbubi. Era bwe kityo ebibya by'obusungu obw'okunyiiga kwa Katonda birifukibwa mu bujjuvu.

Mutegeere nti nnabbubi ye Ssinagoga ya Sitaani kati eyeteeseteese okutwala ensi mu nkola yaayo ey'enkomerero ey'ensolo obufuzi bwayo bwe ndikomya emirembe n'emirembe era okuzikirizibwa kwayo n'omusango gwayo tebigenda kulwa. Mutegeere, baagalwa bange, nti Ssinagoga ya Sitaani bonna beefuula abefuula. Bali mujiijo gwa Bedomu bakitaabwe be nnasanga ng'omwana gw'endiga owa Katonda bwe nneewaayo kyeyagalire okusasulira ebibi by'abantu. Era bwe ntyo ne mbabikkula, era bwe ntyo ne mbagamba butereevu mu maaso gaabwe amabi: Mmwe muli ba kitammwe omulyolyomi, n'okwegomba kwa kitammwe kwe mwagala okukola: yali mutemu okuva ku lubereberye, n'atayimirira mu mazima, kubanga amazima tegali mu ye. Bw'ayogera obulimba, anogera ku bibye ye: kubanga mulimba, era kitaawe w'obulimba.

Era bwe kityo okuva lwe baasooka okulumba ensi ensuubize etali yaabwe kwezza, bayigganyizza, ne babonyaabonya, era ne bakola okutta abantu ku butereevu ku batuuze ab'omu nsi ensuubize. Beefuula nti be basika abatuufu ab'obulokozi, n'okukozesa obubi ebitundu by'amateeka ga Musa mu ddiini yaabwe ey'obulimba eya Talmud. Era mu ngeri ng'ezo bazibye amaaso g'abangi abalowooza nti bagoberera okwagala kwange nga balowooza nti nja kuwa omukisa buli awa omukisa omujiijo guno era nja kukolima buli akolima omujiijo guno. Era bwe batyo abantu bange bangi mu butamanya beegatta ku Ssinagoga ya Sitaani olw'okukkiriza era ddala mbalagira okwenenya mu bujjuvu obulimba bwabwe bwe mweronze olw'okulimbibwa kwammwe mwennyini.

Baanange abato mutegeere nti nze, Kristo Yesu, ssawangako Baibuli zonna eza kuyiga naye zino zikozesebwa ng'ebikozesebwa eby'obulimba n'obulimba obuweebwa ku mabbali okutegeera amazima. Era abagudde bakyusizza ebigambo byange mu Baibuli ez'obulimba, kyova olaba, tunula mu lulimi olwa Byzantine, ne Textus Receptus mwe nnakuumira Ebigambo byange. Kyemunaava mujja gyendi baagalwa bange muyige ku nze era ndibatwala mu bujjuvu mu mazima g'obulimba bwabwe obuliwo bwe munaatoowaza emitima gyammwe eri eddoboozi lyange eriweebwa abo abanjogerera.

Mutegeere nti nze, Yesu Omunazaaleeti, eyawakanya abefuula b'ekika kye kimu olw'ennono zaabwe ez'omu kamwa ze baagulumiza okusinga Ebigambo ebirongoofu ebya Yakuwa ebyaweebwa Musa. Kubanga Amateeka ga Musa matukuvu, era matuukirivu, era magolokofu emirembe n'emirembe era omuntu yenna bw'akolima Amateeka ga Musa kale omuntu oyo ye kennyini alikolimwa. Kubanga nze, Yesu Omunazaaleeti, nnatuukiriza Amateeka ga Musa bwe nnawaayo omusaayi gwange ogutaliiko kibi ku Kaluvaali olw'ebibi bya Adamu (n'abazzukulu be bonna) era Amateeka ga Musa gakwatibwa mu bujjuvu abagalwa bange abatambulira mu kukkiriza mu nze, Kristo Yesu, Omukama waabwe eyalinnya mu ggulu era ow'emirembe n'emirembe.

Kale mutegeere nti tekisoboka kumenya Mateeka ga Musa omuntu bw'atambulira mu kukkiriza nga bwe mmukulembera n'omwoyo gwange gwe nnatonda mu ye emirembe n'emirembe. Newakubadde, omuntu yenna atambulira mu mubiri aba asobya kubanga Musa yajja kuvumirira kibi mu mubiri ng'awa etteeka eryo ery'emirembe ery'essanyu eryawandiikibwa olugalo lwa Yakuwa yennyini. So tewali mubiri mutuukirivu mu maaso gange so ne mu maaso ga Katonda ow'Emirembe n'Emirembe ow'Obutuukirivu. N’olwekyo, mwatulirenga ebibi byammwe n’obunafu bwammwe gyendi, omwana gw’endiga owa Katonda, kabona wammwe omukulu ow’emirembe n’emirembe oluvannyuma lw’Entegeka ya Merukizeddeeki era nja kubalongoosa mu bujjuvu mu musaayi gwange ogutangirira ogwayiibwa okulongooza bonna abajja gyendi okulokolebwa.

Ani yawulirako ekintu ng’ekyo? Ani eyalabako ebintu ng'ebyo? Ensi erizaalibwa mu lunaku lumu? Oba eggwanga liri zaalibwa omulundi gumu? Naye bwe kityo bwe kiri nti eggwanga lya Isiraeri lyaddamu okubaawo ng'eggwanga olw'okulangirira kw'omujiijo, era ye Katonda yennyini eyakkiriza abalabe be okwefuula mu butamanya mu bulimba bwabwe nti be Yakobo so si Esawu, kubanga ye Esawu eyatunda obusika bwe eri omuto. Era bwe kityo nnabbubi mu butamanya era n'amalala alowooza nti alireetawo emyaka gya zaabu egy'emirembe n'emirembe ku nsi kubanga bwe kutyo bwe kuli okwegomba kwa Bakitaabwe kwe balikozeera era abagudde be Bakitaabwe be Basitaani ab'obulamu bwe bagabana.

Naye balumbye ensi n'obukambwe obungi kubanga bwe batyo bwe baweebwa okutuukiriza okuzuukusa abo abebase, abo bonna abali mu buzibe bw'amaaso obw'ekitundu abalyanukula okuyita kwa Masiya waabwe, ye Yeshua Masiya ow'Amazima. Era mutegeere nti ndaba buli kikolwa ekibi kye bakola, era ndaba buli butali bwenkanya bwe balowoozesa ku bataliiko musango, era ndisasula, bw'ayogera Omukama ow'emirembe n'emirembe Yakuwa gw'afudde Masiya, Kabaka, Kabona asinga obukulu, era Omukama! Buli kikolwa ekibi kiwandiikibwa mu bw'obutuukirivu mu ggulu, era balisasulwa emirembe n'emirembe n'okutuuka ku nkomerero mu busika bwabwe obw'emirembe n'emirembe mu nnimi z'omuliro ezisinga okwaka eza kobalti eza bbulu mu kizikiza ekiddugavu emirembe n'emirembe.

Era ndaba Laakeeri ng'akaabira abaana be nga babonaabona olw'okuttibwa n'okubonyaabonyesebwa obutali bwa nsonga okwa Ssinagoga ya Sitaani era ndisasula, bw'ayogera Omukama w'abakama ne Kabaka w'abakabaka era ndirokola ensigalira ya Isiraeri nga nnamu okuyita mu wiiki ya Danyeri ey'amakumi musanvu, ye nnaku ya Yakobo. Mu ntegeka ya Yakuwa ey'emirembe n'emirembe, ategeera nti abangi tebaliva mu butuukirivu okuva mu ssanyu n'obulimba bw'ekibi. Kale mu kifo ky'okuleeta omusango ogw'amangu, Yakuwa alonzeewo okuleeta omusango ku musango n'ebigenda mu maaso ne bigattikana ne bweyongera okuba ebibi. Era omusango gujja kwegattikana mu ngeri ng'eyo bonna abajja okusalawo okunkoowoola mu nnaku zaabwe n'akabi be abo be ndirokola okutuuka ku nkomerero, era balitwalibwa mu mirembe.

Bwe kityo, ebigendererwa byabwe kati bijja kubikkulibwa. Kubanga nja kukulembera abange emitwalo ekkumi n'ena mu enkumi nnya okukka ku Lusozi Sayuuni, era bajja kungoberera buli ntambula yange nga bakulembera ensigalira yange eya Yakobo okubatwala mu ddungu awategekeddwa Katonda okubakuuma, era baliriisibwa n'okulabirirwa, era ogusota, n'ensolo, n'omunnabbi ow'obulimba tebijja kubakomako, wadde okubazuula. Naye omunnabbi ow'obulimba ajja kuleeta eky'omuzizo eky'okuzikiriza, n'essubi eddene lijja kulongoosebwa okuva mu nsi, nange nja kukweka ensigalira yange eri olutalo lw'olunaku olwo olukulu, ye, olunaku lw'okujja kwange okw'okubiri, olunaku Yahweh lwe yalaga mu by'emirembe gyonna egiyise era olwateekebwawo emirembe gyonna mu nteekateeka ye ey'obulokozi, era olunaku olwo ddala lusemberedde kubanga kyawandiikibwa.

Ne ndaba eggulu nga libikkuse, era laba, embalaasi enjeru; n'oyo eyali agituddeko ayitibwa Mwesigwa era ow'Amazima, era mu butuukirivu asala omusango era alwana. Amaaso ge gaali ng'olulimi lw'omuliro, ne ku mutwe gwe kwaliko engule nnyingi; era yalina erinnya eriwandiikidde, omuntu yenna ly'atamanyi, wabula ye yekka. Era yali ayambadde olugoye olunnyikiddwa mu musaayi: n'erinnya lye liyitibwa, Kigambo kya Katonda. N'eggye ery'omu ggulu ne limugoberera ku mbalaasi enjeru, nga bambadde bafuta enjeru ennungi. Ne mu kamwa ke ne muva ekitala eky'obwogi, akisale ensi: era alizifuga n'omuggo ogw'ekyuma: era alinnyirira essogolero ly'obukambwe n'obusungu bwa Katonda Omuyinza w'ebintu byonna. Era ku lugoye lwe ne ku kisambi kye alina erinnya eriwandiikiddwa, KABAKA WA BAKABAKA, ERA MUKAMA WA BAKAMA.

Kale bwe kirituuka nga nzija okutwala obusika bwange n'amaanyi okuva mu mikono gy'abanyazi n'abatta-nsi ab'ebiseera byabwe biweddewo emirembe gyonna, kubanga obwakabaka bwa Setaani tebujja kuddawo nate mu ngeri eyo. Nange nja kulongoosa n'okutukuza ensi okuva mu butali butuukirivu bwonna, era Setaani ajja kusibwa okumala emyaka lukumi ng'ali mu kubonyaabonyezebwa n'okutya okungi olw'obusika bwe obw'emirembe obusemberera mu kibiriitokye ekyo ekyaka ekiwunya obubi ekijja okwokya enjasi ezisinga okubuguma ez'ekibonerezo.

Era enjasi ezo zaatondebwa okusasula buli kikolwa ekibi kye baakola okuva lwe baatondebwa ne balonda obutali butuukirivu. Na buli kibi kyonna eky'abo abaagwa kijja kusasulwa mu bujjuvu eri buli omu ku bo, era buli kibi kye baayonoona n'obukyayi obungi eri Yahweh yennyini n'ebitonde bye bye baayonoona. Bwe kityo nga basibiddwa era nga basuuliddwa nga balamu mu njasi ezisinga okubuguma n'obulongoofu ez'ekibonerezo, omuliro we gujja okwaka ogwa bbulu asinga n'emitendera gy'obweru obwaka ennyo. Era mu njasi ezo ez'ekibonerezo bajja kusasulwa mu bujjuvu olw'ebikolwa byabwe byonna ebitagasa n'eby'omuzizo ebijja okuteekebwa mu bujjuvu mu bifuba byabwe emirundi mingi emirembe gyonna.

Kubanga mu musango gwabwe temuli kusaasira!

Kubanga mu musango gwabwe temuli mirembe!

Kubanga mu musango gwabwe tebajja kufuna kuwummula!

Kubanga baaggyawo okuwummula kw'abataliiko musango!

Kubanga emirembe tebagimanyi!

Kubanga tebaalaga kusaasira!
