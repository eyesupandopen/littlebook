Omujulirwa Omulamba: Ekkanisa
Mu Biwandiiko by'Omutume Pawulo
May 15, 2024

Laba, Empeera Yange Ndi Nayo!

Eri omwagalwa wange, Omugole wange omwesigwa, ansanze n'omutima gwo gwonna. Ggwe mbonerere y'eriiso lyange, era nzijja mangu okukudda, mangu okusinga bw'olowooza. Olw'obwesigwa bwo, waliwo empeera ennene mu ggulu eyakuteekerwateekerwa. Laba! Eriiso teriribanga kulaba, n'okutu tekuriwuliranga, so tekyayingiranga mu mutima gw'omuntu, ebyo Katonda bye yateekerateekera abamwagala. Bwe gityo n'amazima amakulu agakukwatako, ggwe omwagalwa wange, kubanga olijja kugabana ku busika bwange ng'obwesigwa bwo bwe bunaategeeza ku Ntebe yange ey'Okusalira Emisango, ye Bema eyo enkulu, gye ndisalira emirimu gya buli omu ku bayite bange, abalonde, era abeesigwa.

Abo emirimu gyabwe egiriggyawo, balifuna okufiirwa, naye bo bennyini balirokokebwa nga bayita mu muliro; ate abo emirimu gyabwe egirisigalawo, balifuna eby'obugagga bingi, kubanga olunaku olwo lujja kikyoleka mangu. Kubanga kino si kintu kiggya gy'oli, wabula kye wayiga mu bujulizi bw'Abakkolinso mu bbaluwa ya Pawulo ey'okubiri: Kubanga ffenna kitugwanira okulabisibwa mu maaso g'entebe ya Kristo ey'emisango; buli muntu aweebwe ebyo bye yakola ng'ali mu mubiri, oba kirungi oba kibi. Bwe tumanya bwe tutyo entiisa ya Mukama waffe, tuboolesa abantu; naye ffe tumanyiddwa eri Katonda, era nsuubira nti tumanyiddwa ne mu mwoyo gwammwe.

Kubanga omwoyo gwo gulijja kutegeeza wamu n'ebigambo byo bw'oliyimirira mu maaso g'Omulamuzi Omutuukirivu, ye nze, Yesu Kristo, okuwa ebyawe. Abalala baliyimirira mu maaso gange ne baleeta emirimu gyabwe egy'okwokebwa omuliro gw'omulongooseza, ekirivaamu okuswala n'okufiirwa. Abalala baliyimirira mu maaso gange nga beesimbye, nga benaazizza mu musaayi gwange ogutangirira, gye mulindeetera emirimu gye nabawa okukola. Kubanga okuyingira mu kuwummula kwange kwe kuwummula emirimu gyo. N'olwekyo abo abawummudde mu mirimu gyange balina empeera ennene ku bw'obwesigwa. Naye mwenna mulirokoka, so tewali n'omu ku mmwe eyatambulira mu butuukirivu obutuukiridde mu maaso gange, n'olwekyo olunaku lulikyoleka. Totya, ndikuwa empeera mu bujjuvu, kubanga emirimu gyonna egy'amaanyi gye wankolera mu kyama giribikkulwa, n'olwekyo olunaku lulikyoleka.

Lwaki emitima gyammwe gibeeraliikiriza olw'ebigambo bino? Temusomanga mu bbaluwa ya Yokaana eyasooka, gye namuwandiisa? Kubanga oba ng'omutima gwaffe gutusalira omusango, Katonda mukulu okusinga omutima gwaffe, era amanyi byonna. Abaagalwa, oba ng'omutima gwaffe tegu tusalira musango, tuba n'obuvumu eri Katonda. Era nate: Bwe twogera nti tetulina kibi, twerimba, n'amazima tegaali mu ffe. Bwe twatula ebibi byaffe, ye mwesigwa era mutuukirivu okutusonyiwa ebibi byaffe, n'okutunaazaako obutali butuukirivu bwonna. N'olwekyo, yatulanga ebibi byo gye ndi buli lunaku era buli lwe wandyagalidde, era weenaazenga mu musaayi gwange ogutangirira.

Kubanga nze Muwolereza wammwe, era mwagulibwa n'ekiweebwayo kyange ng'Omwana gw'endiga gw'Okuyitako omutuukiridde okukomya ebibi, n'okutabaganya olw'obutali butuukirivu, n'okuleeta obutuukirivu obutaggwaawo. Laba, ggwe Omugole wange! Ebiseera eby'okuddizibwawo biranze, kubanga Peetero yalagula ku biseera bino bwe yawonya omusajja ku Mulyango gw'Ekifo Ekitukuvu omulungi: Era alituma Yesu Kristo, eyabuulirwa gye muli okuva edda. Oyo eggulu gwe kigwanira okufuna, okutuusa ebiseera eby'okuddamu okutereeza byonna. Era kaakano wazze ebiseera eby'okuddizibwawo, ggwe Omugole wange omulungi, kubanga oli mulongoofu mu maaso gange era onaaziddwaako obutali butuukirivu bwonna mu musaayi gw'Omwana gw'endiga, kubanga nze Mwana gw'endiga owa Katonda! N'olwekyo, yatulanga ebibi byo n'obwesigwa.

Kale n'olwekyo omusango, okuddizibwawo, okufiirwa, n'empeera ennene byonna bitandikira ku nnyumba ya Katonda. Kubanga mu bbanga ttono, mulikuŋŋaanyizibwa wamu mu bire okunsisinkana mu bbanga, kubanga ndikka okuva mu ggulu n'okwogerera waggulu, n'eddoboozi lya malayika omukulu, n'ekkondeere erisembayo lirivuga! Mugende mu maaso n'okutunula mu ggulu n'okwetegereza, kubanga Katonda akoze obubonero bungi obw'amaanyi mu ggulu, nga mulimu n'okulangirira obufumbo bw'omugole omusajja n'omugole we omukazi, ye ndagaano eteriggwaawo wakati wange n'omugole wange, ekiri okubaawo mu bbanga ttono. Kale yatuulanga ebibi byo era onsabenga nkulage ebibi by'owerabidde olyoke naabyo obyatule; era okusinga byonna, nywereranga mu kwagala kwa Katonda, kubanga okwagala kwa Katonda kubikka ku bibi bingi. Kale totya, kubanga tekisoboka gy'oli okubyatula byonna.

Eby'omusango: kubanga muvudde mu kufa ne muyingira mu bulamu mu nze, Mukama wammwe Yesu Kristo eyazuukira. Kubanga nze nnawaayo obulamu bwange; tewali yabuntwalako. Nabuwaddeyo kyeyagalire era nandyongedde okukikola singa kyetaagisa. Naye tewali nsonga ya kintu ng'ekyo, kubanga kiki kye nnagamba Abebbulaniya: Kubanga tekiyinzika musaayi gwa nte ne gwa mbuzi kujjawo bibi. Naye omusajja oyo, bwe yamala okuweerayo ssaddaaka emu olw'ebibi emirembe gyonna, n'atuula ku mukono ogwa ddyo ogwa Katonda. Ggwe Omugole wange, sijja kukusalira musango ng'okusinziira ku bibi byo; kubanga bonna abali mu Kristo baaweebwa obutuukirivu, weewaawo baatukuzibwa, era baafuulibwa abatuukirivu mu nze, Kristo Yesu, aliba omulamu era alifuga emirembe gyonna, kubanga obwakabaka bwange tebulina nkomerero. N'olwekyo osonyiyiddwa ebibi byo; olw'ekyo toliba mu musango gw'aboonoonyi wabula mu musango gw'abatuukirivu, kubanga omusango gutandikira ku nnyumba ya Katonda.

Eby'okuddizibwawo: kubanga nze ndisasula, bw'ayogera Mukama. Bangi ku mmwe mwava mu mayumba, mu nsi, mu maka ne muweerayo ebintu bingi nnyo; era bangi mu Bagole bange baawaayo n'obulamu bwabwe, abo be Bajulirwa bange. Kale walibaawo okuddizibwawo kw'ebintu ebyaweebwayo era n'ebyo ebyasaanawo. Kubanga enkomerero ya Yobu yali ntya: Era Mukama yakyusa obusibe bwa Yobu, bwe yasabira mikwano gye: era Mukama n'awa Yobu emirundi ebiri okusinga ebyo bye yalina edda. N'eddoboozi lya Yakobo muganda wange nalyo ligamba nti: Laba, abo abagumiikiriza tubayita ba mukisa. Mwawulira eby'okugumiikiriza kwa Yobu, ne mulaba enkomerero ya Mukama: nti Mukama wa kisa kingi era wa ssaasi. Era bw'atyo Kitammwe bw'ali, era nange bwe ndi, kubanga ndi wa oyo NZE NDI!

Eby'okufiirwa: kubanga bangi balifiirwa, nga bwe nnagamba ku ntebe yange ey'emisango eri Abakkolinso: Kubanga ffenna kitugwanira okulabisibwa mu maaso g'entebe ya Kristo ey'emisango; buli muntu aweebwe ebyo bye yakola ng'ali mu mubiri, oba kirungi oba kibi. Era nate Abakkolinso bategeeza: Omulimu gwa buli muntu gulirabisibwa. Kubanga olunaku luligukyoleka, kubanga gulibikkulwa na muliro, era omuliro gulikema omulimu gwa buli muntu bw'enti bwe guli. Omulimu gwa muntu gwonna bwe guliggyawo, alifiirwa: naye ye yennyini alirokolebwa: naye ng'ayitira mu muliro.

Eby'empeera ennene: kubanga Abakkolinso boogera nate: Eriiso teriribanga kulaba, n'okutu tekuriwuliranga, so tekyayingiranga mu mutima gw'omuntu, ebyo Katonda bye yateekerateekera abamwagala. Mmwe abeesigwa era abalonde bange, ddala temuyinza kutegeera mbaga nkulu na bijaguzo bye mwateekerwateekerwa Kitange mu ggulu; kubanga mujja mu kujaguza, era mubikiiriddwa eby'obugagga bingi mu ggulu. (Kubanga mukomawo eka ewange, Kristo Yesu, ne Kitaffe, Yahweh, Katonda ataggwaawo.) Era nate: omulimu gwa muntu gwonna bwe gulisigalawo, gw'ayimbye ku gwo, aliweebwa empeera. Nina empeera nnyingi nnyo ez'okugaba, n'obusika bwange mbuli nabwo; kale temutya, kubanga muligabana ku busika bwange; era gye ndi, nammwe gye muliba, kubanga ebiseera eby'okwawukana biweddeyo.

Engule ez'empeera n'ez'obuyinza zibalindiridde. Ezimu nzigambyeko mu byawandiikibwa: engule etaboleka, engule ey'essanyu, engule ey'obutuukirivu, engule ey'ekitiibwa, n'engule ey'obulamu.

Abo abadduse embiro, ye mirimu gye nnabateerawo, baliweebwa engule etaboleka: kubanga muliba mwegadde wansi w'omwoyo gwange, era muliba mwekute mu ngeri yonna omuddusi w'embiro gy'ayinza okwekutama. Abo abawangudde embiro mu mateeka bagabirwa engule etaboleka.

Waliwo engule ey'essanyu. Kubanga ssuubi lyaffe, oba ssanyu lyaffe, oba ngule yaffe ey'okwesanyusa lye ly'ani? Si ye mmwe abali mu maaso ga Mukama waffe Yesu Kristo ku kujja kwe? Kubanga, mmwe kitiibwa kyaffe era ssanyu lyaffe. Era bwe tutyo tuliba n'essanyu lingi, era mwenna muliba mu kitiibwa era muligulumizibwa mu nze! Abo abakoze n'obwesigwa okukyusa n'okukuza baganda baabwe be balina engule ey'essanyu.

Waliwo engule ey'obutuukirivu eri abo abamaliriza emirimu gyabwe n'eri abo abaagala okujja kwange. Kale mugende mu maaso n'okutunula waggulu, kubanga ndi kumpi era nnina essuubi ddene ery'okulabika kwange. Kubanga n'essuubi ddene ery'okulabika kwange nalyo lireeta empeera ennene. Abo abalina essuubi ddene ery'okulabika kwange be balina engule ey'obutuukirivu.

Waliwo engule ey'ekitiibwa eri abo abaliisizza ekisibo kya Katonda. Kubanga Peetero yawandiika: muliisenga ekisibo kya Katonda ekiri mu mmwe, nga mukirabirira, si lwa kuwalirizibwa, wabula lwa kweyagalira: si lwa magoba manafu, wabula lwa mutima mugabi. Abo abateefuulanga bakama ku busika bwa Katonda era abataakubirizibwanga magoba manafu n'abo abampeerezza n'obwesigwa mu bwetoowaze n'omutima omutuufu ogw'okwagala be balina engule ey'ekitiibwa.

Waliwo engule ey'obulamu gye ndigabira abajulirwa bange, eri abo ababonaabona mu bibonoobono n'okuyigganyizibwa ku lw'erinnya lyange n'eri abo abagumiikiririzza n'obugumiikiriza amagezi n'enkwe za Setaani. Abo abawangudde ebintu bino n'obwesigwa be balina engule ey'obulamu.

Era mulifaananyizibwa nze, Adamu ow'oluvannyuma era muliba nga nze, era mulindaba nga bwe ndi, era nammwe bwe mutyo bwe muliba. Laba! Nja kubatonderamu emibiri emipya egitaliiko kye gifaananya kyonna kye muyinza okulowooza. Laba ebyayogerwa mu byawandiikibwa bwe nnalabikira abeesigwa bange nga sinnagenda mu ggulu. Nja kukukolera omubiri guno n'obwegendereza, era gulibaamu obusobozi bwo, bye wakola, bye watuukako; era ojja kwewuunya nnyo.

Temutegeera kye nnawandiisa Pawulo eri Abakkolinso? Era waliwo emibiri egy'omu ggulu, n'emibiri egy'oku nsi: Naye ekitiibwa eky'omu ggulu kirala, n'ekitiibwa eky'oku nsi kirala. Waliwo ekitiibwa kimu eky'enjuba, n'ekirala eky'omwezi, n'ekirala eky'emmunyeenye: kubanga emmunyeenye emu yawukana ku mmunyeenye endala mu kitiibwa. Bwe kityo n'okuzuukira kw'abafu bwe kuli: gusigibwa mu kuvunda, guzuukizibwa mu butavunda. Baana bange, muliba n'obusobozi bungi nnyo bwe mutalootangako. Newankubadde nga buli omu ku mmwe aliba wa njawulo ku munne, mwenna mulitambulira mu nze mu butuukirivu, kubanga ndibafuula bakabaka era bakabona mu maaso ga Katonda waffe!

Walibaawo ebintu bingi nnyo bye mutaliba nakyegolola. Tewaliba ndwadde nate, tewaliba kibi nate gye muli, tewaliba kufa nate gye muli, tewaliba kutya, tewaliba kweraliikirira; kubanga mwenna mulitondebwa buto mu bujjuvu mu nze, Kristo Yesu Mukama waffe. N'olwekyo nkugamba ggwe, Omugole wange, beeranga muwanguzi, kubanga empeera z'omuwanguzi ze ndigaba, era ziri wala nnyo okusinga by'olowooza oba by'oloota. Kubanga omuti ogw'obulamu, ogwabazanga ebibala bya ngeri kkumi na bbiri, ne gubalanga ebibala byagwo buli mwezi: n'amalagala g'omuti gaali ga kuwonya mawanga. So tewaliba kukolimirwa nate, wabula entebe ya Katonda n'ey'Omwana gw'endiga eriba mu kyo, n'abaweereza be balimuweereza. Nabo baliraba amaaso ge, n'erinnya lye liriba ku byenyi byabwe. So tewaliba kiro eyo, so tebalyetaaga ttaala, newakubadde omusana, kubanga Mukama Katonda abamulisa, nabo balifuga emirembe n'emirembe. Kale totya, Omugole wange omwesigwa, empeera ennene eri kumpi nnyo okutuukirizibwa gy'oli. Kubanga Katonda si mutuukirivu, okwerabira omulimu gwammwe n'okutegana kw'okwagala kwammwe, kwe mwalaga ku lw'Erinnya lye, bwe mwaweerezanga Abatukuvu, era bwe muweereza.

Nzijja okukudda, mangu okusinga bw'olowooza.

Nze Muwolereza wammwe.

Nze ndi era nammwe bwe muliba, kubanga mulindaba nga bwe ndi.

Nzijja, n'empeera yange ndi nayo eri abeesigwa.

Nze oyo alibawa obusika bwammwe mu bujjuvu, kubanga bwe butyo bwe bwampeebwa.

Nze oyo alibawa emibiri emipya okudda mu kifo ky'omubiri gw'ekibi.

Nze ndi, kubanga ndi wa oyo NZE NDI!

Noonyanga okulabika kwange mu bire, kubanga obubonero bulindanga nga okumasamasa kwange tekunnatanda mu ggulu. Nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, ntumye Ebigambo bino.
