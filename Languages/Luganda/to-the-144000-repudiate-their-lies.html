Eri Aba 144,000: Mwegaane Obulimba Bwabwe

Mutege amatu gammwe eri eddoboozi lyange muwulire amagezi gange, kubanga amagezi gakaabirira waggulu mu nguudo: munaawulira eddoboozi lyange? Nze Yeshua HaMashiach, ye Mwana wa Yahweh, eyajja mu mubiri era ndi Mwana gw'endiga gwa Katonda aggyawo ekibi ky'ensi. Mutegeere nti ensi mwe muli si kifaananyi kya bulimba abasomesa bammwe Abayesuwiiti kye baakuba okubabuza. Abangi ku mmwe mwayigirizibwa mu bulimba bw'Abayesuwiiti, nga n'okuyiga kwennyini mukuyiga kuwugamu. Olutalo luno ku mazima lwakolebwa okubumba ebirowoozo by'abantu okugondera Setaani era lufuba okuggyawo n'okunyaga eddembe ly'omuntu ery'okwesalirawo, omuntu agoberere obutayimirira bye yayigirizibwa.

Ebyafaayo eby'amazima tebisobola kutegeerekeka okuva mu bulimba bw'abafirosoofo Abayesuwiiti, kubanga abo balimba abasaasaanya obutali butuufu mu bukuusa obungi. Ebyawandiikibwa birambulukufu ku nsonga eno, era Abaruumi balangirira amazima nga gategeerekeka bulungi: Kubanga obusungu bwa Katonda bubikkulibwa okuva mu ggulu ku butatya Katonda bwonna n'obutali butuukirivu bw'abantu abaziyiza amazima mu butali butuukirivu.

Ebigambo ebyo mwabikkirizza okutuuka ku mitima gyammwe? Abantu bakisa amazima ku bubi n'obutali butuukirivu, era obusungu bwa Yahweh buli ku abo abakola bwe batyo. N'olwekyo okusoma kwammwe kufuusiddwa okw'obulimba olw'obulimba bwabwe.

Kino baakikola okuteekateeka ekkubo ly'obukuusa obunene obugenda okujja, era obwakabaka bwa Setaani busobole okusumululwa okufuga mu bukuusa obunene n'obulimba. Obulimba bwe baabategeeza mu by'enjigiriza bukozesebwa okuziba amaaso g'ekibiina ky'abantu ku mazima: Tewali bagenyi, wabula bamalayika abagwa abalaga mu nnyanja y'omuliro okusibwa n'okwokebwa emirembe n'emirembe. Temukkiririza mu bulimba bwabwe muddukire gye ndi, Kristo Yesu, kubanga mu nze mwe muli amazima gonna aganaabafuula ab'eddembe. Bino mbibagamba muleme okubuusibwabuusibwa wamu n'abo abalegatta ne Setaani ne bakkiriza akabonero k'ensolo akajja.

Ebyafaayo bitegeerekeka ng'ogoberera amazima aganywezebwa mu Byawandiikibwa n'ebitabo ebiri ebweru wa Bayibuli nga 1 Enoka, Yaseri, Yubiri, n'ebirala, okuva ku lubereberye. Kubanga abantu bano abatatya Katonda n'obulimba bwe bakozesa okuziba amazima bya kitundu ky'olutalo olunene, ye lutalo lw'ensigo wakati wa Yahweh ne Setaani olukyaliwo n'okutuusa ku ddakiika eno, naye nga luvuddeko okuwangula emirembe n'emirembe olw'emirimu gyange egy'okutangirira. Kubanga olutalo lwalangirirwa Yahweh ku musota ogwo omukadde olw'obujeemu bwe, okuva ku ntandikwa y'okutondebwa kw'omuntu Setaani bwe yagezaako okuzikiriza Adamu ne Kaawa. Yahweh yagamba omusota mu kulangirira kwe nti: Nja kuteeka obulabe wakati wo n'omukazi, na wakati w'ezzadde lyo n'ezzadde lye: liribeta omutwe gwo, naawe olimuluma ekisinziiro kye. Era n'olwekyo entalo z'ensigo zibaddewo okuva olwo.

Mu nnimiro y'Adeni, Setaani yasobola okwonoonna ensigo ya Adamu, n'asalira Adamu omusango wansi w'amaanyi n'okuvunda kw'ekibi. (Mu kino Mwana wa Katonda mwe yalabisibwa, amenye emirimu gya Setaani.) Kubanga Setaani yakozesa okumanya kwe okwonoonefu okw'obulungi n'obubi okwonoonera ddala abantu, era eri abo abagondera eddoboozi lyange, okwonooneka okwo kumenyebwa mu nze, Kristo Yesu, mw'oli ekitonde ekiggya ekisobola obutayonooneka. Ensigo yange tesobola kwonooneka, kubanga Yahweh yagifuula eteyonooneka, era okuyita mu kuzaalibwa okw'omulundi ogw'okubiri nateekako akabonero ku bange abalonde n'abeesigwa era n'omwoyo ogwo omutukuvu ogw'ekisuubizo!

Yokaana mu bbaluwa ye eyasooka alangirira amazima ag'emirembe n'emirembe: Buli muntu eyazaalibwa Katonda takola kibi: kubanga ensigo ye ebeera mu ye, so tayinza kukola kibi, kubanga yazaalibwa Katonda.

Mu kaseera katono oluvannyuma lw'okugwa kw'Adeni, Adamu we yayonoonera n'ava mu butuukirivu n'ayingira mu kuvunda, abakuumi baaserengesa ku Lusozi Kerumooni mu nnaku za Yeredi. Era bonna beesiba n'ebikolimo eby'awamu, ye kirayiro kyabwe ekitatukuvu mwe beesibira emirembe n'emirembe mu kibi n'ekibonerezo eky'emirembe n'emirembe. Bwe batyo baakikola bonna beesalirewo abakazi ku abo bonna be baalonda, bwe batyo ne bakyusa entegeka y'obulamu bwabwe.

Baajja eri abantu nga babawa okumanya kwabwe okw'abamalayika abagwa okuvundu, kubanga beegatta ku bujeemu bwa Setaani.

Baayigiriza abantu okukola ebitala, n'obwambe, n'engabo, n'eby'omu kifuba, ne babamanyisa ebyuma by'oku nsi n'obukodyo bw'okubikolako, n'ebikomo, n'eby'okwewunda, n'okukozesa sirimu, n'okulongoosa ebikowe, n'amayinja ag'omuwendo ag'engeri zonna, n'ebirungo by'amabala gonna. Baayigiriza eddogo, n'okusala emilandira, okumenya eddogo, okulaguzisa emmunyeenye, ebibinja by'emmunyeenye, okumanya ebire, obubonero bw'ensi, obubonero bw'enjuba, n'entambula y'omwezi.

Awo abakuumi abagwa ne batwala abakazi baabwe ne beeyama nabo, ne baba embuto ne babazaalira abazise ebire. Ekiseera bwe kyatuuka, ensigo eno etali ntukuvu n'emalawo byonna ebyali ku nsi, n'ebyo byonna ebyaweebwa abantu, era oluvannyuma n'emalawo abantu bangi nga bwe kyasoboka. Kubanga baali banywi ba musaayi era balya bantu, era baakola ebibi binene mu maaso ga Yahweh, kubanga obulamu bw'omubiri buli mu musaayi era omuntu yakolebwa mu kifaananyi kya Yahweh. Baakula ne baala era oluvannyuma ne bassaawo obwakabaka bwabwe ku nsi yonna. Laba! Amatongo g'ebizimbe byabwe ebinene ennyo gakyasaasaanye ku nsi ng'obujulizi ku bo. Baafuga ensi mu biseera ebyo, era baakolebwa okuzikiriza abantu. Bwe kityo emize gya buli ngeri ne gituukirizibwa, kubanga ebiwandiiko ebitukuvu birangirira engeri gye kyakolebwamu.

Nga tekimala abantu okwegomba emibiri emirala, okwey أما n'abamalayika abagwa, n'okuzaala abaana abazise, olw'abakuumi bonna baayonoona ku nnyonyi, ne ku nsolo, ne ku byewalula, ne ku byennyanja. Okuva ku ntandikwa y'okutonda, Yahweh yalagira buli kiramu okuba ng'ekika kyakyo bwe kiri. Naye abakuumi ababi baatabula ebitonde, ne bagatta ebiramu eby'ekika ekimu n'ebiramu eby'ekika ekirala ekifaanagana okuleeta obutali butebenkevu mu nsigo n'okukyusa ekitonde kya Yahweh eky'entandikwa eky'obutuukirivu obuwulikana. Omulimu guno gwaddibwangamu, nga bagatta ebiramu ebifaanagana eby'ekika ekimu n'ebifaanagana eby'ekika ekirala okutuusa lwe baafuna kye baali baagala. Bwe kityo ensi eyaliwo edda n'eyonooneka n'ebizira by'obubi bya buli ngeri ebyayonoona ennyo eri Yahweh, abantu, n'ebitonde byonna. Tegeera nti abagwa baagala ensi yaabwe, ye nsi ey'ekizikiza eyawukanye ddala ne Yahweh, era eyo ge ganaasikira mu nnyanja y'omuliro eyabategekerwa Yahweh yennyini.

Bwe kityo ennaku za Nuuwa ne zituuka n'ensi eyaliwo edda, eyali eva mu mazzi era nga eri mu mazzi, n'esaanawo n'amataba. Kubanga enzizi z'obuziba obunene zaabika, ne zireetawo ettemu eringi ng'ensi emenyekamenyekamu n'amaanyi g'amazzi amakambwe, n'amadirisa g'eggulu ne maggulwawo, ne gakkiriza amazzi okuva waggulu w'olubaawo (n'okuva wansi w'ensi) okubikka n'okuzikiriza ensi yonna.

Bwe kityo Enoka omuwandiisi annyonnyola enkomerero y'abazise bano bwe yati, kubanga Yahweh bwe yatyo yalangirira: Era kaakano, abazise, abava mu myoyo n'omubiri, baliyitibwa emyoyo emibi ku nsi, era ku nsi we wanaaba okubeera kwabwe. Emyoyo emibi givudde mu mibiri gyabwe; kubanga bazaalibwa mu bantu, okuva mu bakuumi abatukuvu ye ntandikwa yaabwe n'ensibuko yaabwe ey'olubereberye; baliyitibwa emyoyo emibi ku nsi, era baliyitibwa emyoyo emibi. Ate emyoyo gy'omu ggulu, mu ggulu mwe muliba okubeera kwagyo, naye emyoyo gy'ensi egyazaalibwa ku nsi, ku nsi kwe kuliba okubeera kwagyo. Era emyoyo gy'abazise gibonyaabonya, gijooja, gizikiriza, girumba, girwana, era gikola okuzikiriza ku nsi, era gireetawo emitawaana: tegirya mmere, naye n'okuluma enjala n'ennyonta, era gireetawo okwesittaza. Era emyoyo gino girigolokoka ku baana b'abantu ne ku bakazi, kubanga gyava mu bo. Tegeera nti ebitundu omwenda ku kumi eby'emyoyo gino emibi biri mu bunnya olw'okusaba kwa Nuuwa, era abantu babadde balwana na kitundu kimu kya kkumi kyokka okuva olwo. Ekyo kinaatera okukyuka ng'obunnya bugenda kuggulwawo byonna ne bisumululwa.

Bwe kityo ensi, n'ebizira byonna eby'abakuumi, byazikirizibwa. Abakuumi bennyini baasibibwa mu misango gyabwe egy'enjawulo okumala emirembe nsanvu, okusumululwa okusalirwa omusango mu kiseera ky'omusango omunene. (Kubanga omusango omunene gunaatera okutandika.) Naye Nuuwa yalaba ekisa mu maaso ga Yahweh kubanga yali musajja mutuukirivu, n'ab'omu maka ge Yahweh n'abawonya nga balamu emyoyo munaana gyokka, awamu n'ebiramu ebirala bingi ku lyato. Nuuwa yeekuuma ye n'ab'omu maka ge obutatataganyizibwa mu nsigo amataba nga tegannajja. Naye mutabani we Kaamu n'ezzadde lye ebbi baagaana Nuuwa n'okusingira ddala Yahweh yennyini (eyali abatondye n'abawonya), ne beegatta ku bujeemu bwa Setaani.

Ebigambo by'Olubereberye 6 byatuukirizibwa olw'ebibi by'ezzadde lya Nuuwa, ye luuyi lwa Kaamu. Waaliwo abazise ku nsi mu nnaku ezo: era ne mu nnaku ezaaddirira. "Ennaku ezo" ze nnaku okuva ku Yeredi okutuuka ku Nuuwa, era "abazise ku nsi" be baali ezzadde ly'abakuumi n'abawala b'abantu amataba nga tegannajja. "Oluvannyuma lwa ekyo" lwe luvannyuma lw'ennaku za Nuuwa, ebisoko by'abantu bwe byaddamu okwonooneka n'okumanya kw'abamalayika abagwa. Nkusaba okyuse okukkiririza mu bukuusa bw'ekiteeso kya Sesayiti ekikyamu okkirize ebigambo by'ekiwandiiko ekitukuvu, kubanga ebigambo bino ge mazima.

Ensi bwe yagabanyizibwamu emigabo mu baana ba Nuuwa (Seemu, Kaamu, ne Yafeesi), omugabo gwa Kaamu gwali gwa kusikira nsi za ku ddyo, bwe zityo ne ziba zize. Naye, Kanani yeegomba ekitundu ky'obusika bwa Seemu n'asengukira mu nsi ezitaali zize kutuulamu. Kubanga Kanani Nuuwa gwe yakolima. Kubanga Kanani yava mu busika bwe n'azimba ekibuga Ttuulo ku lubalama lwa Foyiniikiya era n'atuuka e Sidoni, Yezebyeri mwe yava. Kino yakikola asobole okuba okumpi n'Olusozi Kerumooni, abakuumi we baaserengesa era nze we nnafuulirwa ekifaananyi.

Kanani yazuula abakuumi kye baalekawo amataba ga Nuuwa nga tegannabaawo, kubanga baanoonya okukuuma obujeemu bwabwe n'oluvannyuma lw'amataba, kubanga baajja bingi ku mayinja ne ku njazi. Kanani yakozesa obumanyi bwabwe obw'abamalayika abagwa mu bulogo obw'amaanyi, era emikolo emibi mingi gyakolebwa Omukanani, kubanga baawangayo n'abaana baabwe bennyini. Bwe batyo baali babi nnyo era baasaasaana nnyo n'asobola okusiiga ensi ya Kanani n'ebizira bye ebiggya bye yakola, Setaani mwe yakolera olw'ebyonoono bye.

Bwe kityo ebika by'abazise ab'oluvannyuma lw'amataba, ye bika by'Abakanani eby'omuzizo, byagolokoka okuva mu byonoono bya Kanani n'obutali butuukirivu n'obulogo. Mu nsi Kanani gye yabba okuva eri Seemu mwajja Abakiiti, n'Abakiivi, n'Abaperizi, n'Abagirugaasi, n'Abamoli, n'Abayebusi. Be baali ab'entiisa, ye Refayimu. Waaliwo ebika ebisinga ku ebyo ebyalagulwa, naye abo ab'entiisa baali balindiridde mu nsi ey'ekisuubizo nga bagezaako okutta abo abaali bakuuma olunyiriri lwa Kristo, be bajjajjammwe Abayisirayiri n'abakadde bammwe abaali Abebbulaniya.

Yoswa yayingira mu nsi eyasuubizibwa okutwala ekyo ekyali kiweereddwa Seemu mu ngeri entuufu olw'akalulu n'ensi eyali ya Isirayiri mu ngeri entuufu olw'ekisuubizo kya Yahweh yennyini. Naye abaana ba Isirayiri tebaatuukiriza biragiro bya Yahweh mu bujjuvu okuleka nga tewali n'omu ku ngano eyalimba asigaddewo nga mulamu, kubanga baali ba kabi era si bantu, era empisa zaabwe zaali mbi, era baakola ebibi bingi mu maaso ga Yahweh, kubanga obuzaale bwabwe bwe bwabanga bwe butyo. Bwe kityo Yahweh yagamba Isirayiri nga bwe kyawandiikibwa mu byawandiikibwa: Naye bwe mutaligoba bantu ba mu nsi mu maaso gammwe, kale kiribaawo nti abo be mulireka ku bo, baliba maggwa mu maaso gammwe, n'amayinja mu mbiriizi zammwe, era balibajooganga mu nsi mwe mutuula. Era bwe kityo bwe kyali.

Ebyafaayo mu kitundu kiraga okusaasaana n'okutambula kw'ebizira bino, ye ngano eyalimba eyasimbibwa Setaani yennyini, nga bwe babeeredde mu ngano okuva mu nnaku ez'edda. Baateekawo empucankuba nnyingi nga bakozesa tekinologiya zaabwe n'amagezi g'abamalayika abagwa; be bazimbi abakugu, be balogo, bangi ku bo balina obusobozi obutali bwa bulijjo, era bali wamu ne kitaabwe Omulyolyomi, nga balina n'engeri ze n'okwegomba kwe. Ensi eno ey'obulimba yeenyize mu buli kitundu kya bantu, era si bonna bazise. Abangi balabika ng'abantu, wabula be bitonde ebyatabulwa ebyakyusibwa emirembe n'emirembe okuva mu nteekateeka za Yahweh ez'okutonda era tebirinunulibwa. Temubatya, kubanga ali mu mmwe ye mukulu okusinga ali mu nsi.

Engano eno ey'obulimba enoonya okutwala eby'obusika n'ekisuubizo eri abaana ba Katonda. Tewali nkolagana na bo, kubanga ba kitaabwe Omulyolyomi, era okwegomba kwa kitaabwe kwe balikoza. Yali mutemu okuva ku lubereberye, era bw'ayogera obulimba ayogera bibye yekka, kubanga ye mulimba era kitaawo. Mazima ddala balijeemera, balyonoona, balizikiriza, balitta, balinyaga, balinyaga, era balikoza ekisinga obubi ku kintu kyonna ekya Katonda kye beegomba. Tebalina mirembe, era obuzaale bwabwe bwonoonefu ddala. Baavumaganyizza ensi, era baliteekawo obwakabaka bwabwe okumala essaawa emu ng'ogusota gulina obufuzi bwagwo ku nsi. Naye banoonya okuzzaawo emirembe gyaabwe egya zaabu, naye tewaliba mirembe gya zaabu gye baliba nagyo wabula omusango omukakali, so tebaliwoona.

Ebizira bino ebibi byonoonye amagezi, okuyiga, ebyafaayo, eddiini, ssaayansi, ne birala ne birala bye byonoonye. Basaasaanizza tekinologiya w'abamalayika abagwa era tewali kiggya wansi w'enjuba. Ng'ebiseera bituukiridde, babisse ku nsi yonna obulimba, era ensi yonna eri mu bubi. Olw'okumanyisibwa kwabwe, ebikoosi by'Abayesuwiiti byonoonye ddala embeera z'abantu era bitutte abantu mu nnyanja y'obulimba, awaayinza okubeera okudduka kwokka nga bayita mu kwenenya n'okwegaana ebibi n'obulimba byonna. (Kubanga Rooma n'Obusiraamu yabukola; soma oyige.) Amagezi gaabwe ag'abamalayika abagwa n'obulimba byawakana n'ebyafaayo eby'omu byawandiikibwa era mu kifo kyabyo biwaayo enfumo enkalu eziziba amaaso n'emitima gy'abantu. N'olwekyo, muve mu bo mweyawule.

Ensi leero erimu abantu bangi ddala abalina obwerabize obw'awamu olw'okuyigirizibwa kw'Abayesuwiiti. Abangi ku mmwe mwerabidde si ndagaano n'amakubo ga Yahweh byokka, naye mwerabidde ddala ggwe ani n'abajjajja bo be baani ddala. Kino kyakolebwa ku bugenderere okuyita mu ntalo, obulimba, n'okuteeka abantu mu bifo ebipya okumala emyaka egyayisa abangi ennyo mu nsonda ennya ez'ensi. Kino ku bugenderere tebakimanyi nti tekiyinzika kukisa kintu kyonna mu maaso ga Yahweh; era nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, mmanyi abange era be mpita okununulibwa.

Abamu ku abo abaasaasaanyizibwa baali bamulekwa era baaggyibwa mu maka gaabwe oba mu ntalo oba mu kuwambibwa obuwambi. Emyoyo gino gyakula ku byafaayo eby'obulimba, ne balimbibwa n'ebirowoozo ebirungi eby'ebiseera eby'edda eby'obulimba, bwe kityo obulimba ne busimba amakanda mangi mu mitima gy'abantu, era abangi bazibirizibwa emirembe n'emirembe n'obulimba obukkirizibwa ng'amazima. Naye eri abo abankyukira, Yeshua HaMashiach, mulimanya amazima n'amazima galibafuula ab'eddembe. Embeera yammwe ey'omulembe guno ezimbiddwa ku matongo n'evvu ly'ebika by'abantu abagwa eby'edda, era amaanyi mangi gateekeddwa mu kukisa amazima. Ekyo ekyaliwo kiriddawo, era tewali kiggya wansi w'enjuba.

Awataali ssaddaaka yange ey'okutangirira n'ekirabo kyange eky'obulokozi, mwenna mwandibadde mubuuze. N'olwekyo mujje gye ndi, Yeshua HaMashiach, nange ndibakulembera mu mazima gonna era ndibayigiriza ebyafaayo byammwe eby'amazima. Ndibaddiza abajjajja bammwe ab'amazima, kubanga abangi ku mmwe muva mu nsi, wadde ng'obulokozi buli bwa mawanga gonna. Ndibayigiriza mu makubo g'obutukuvu, era mulibeerawo mu butuukirivu olw'okukkiriza mu nze, Yesu Kristo, era mulibeerawo emirembe n'emirembe. Tewali kyama ekitalijja mu lujjudde, era byonna biribikkulwa mu bujjuvu, amazima gasobole okumanyibwa banna.

N'olwekyo, mulekere awo okwesiima mu bulimba nga mweziika mu bwerembu, kubanga ebiseera by'obwerembu biweddeyo. Ebiseera by'okubikkulwa biri kumpi. Kubanga laba! Nga bwe kyali mu nnaku za Nuuwa, bwe kityo bwe kiriba. Setaani akozesezza olugoye lwe olw'obulimba okuteekawo ekisaawe ky'obukuusa bwe obunene obw'enkomerero, era abasinga obungi mu nsi baligoberera obulimba bwe. Kubanga obwakabaka bwa Setaani bwonna bulisumululwa okuva mu bunnya, n'abazise baliddamu okubeera mu bantu, kubanga bajja kutuukiriza obusungu bwa Yahweh.

Setaani ayogera obulimba obunene, era abo abagwa baligamba nti bava mu nsi ndala era nti bakulaakulanye okusinga abantu emyaka enkumi n'enkumi. Balijja nga bawaayo amagezi agalabika ng'amalungi, era balyebikka mu bulungi obw'obulimba. Naye bano ba mu bwakabaka bwa Setaani, n'olwekyo temugwa mu bukuusa. Temugoberera makubo ga nsi, kubanga ensi eneewuunya olw'obwakabaka bwa Setaani, nga n'okugamba egamba nti, ani afaanana ensolo?

Ndibakweka, ndibakuuma, ndibazzaamu amaanyi, ndibakulembera ntuuko ku ntuuko, era ndimenyaamenya ddala olutimbe lw'obulimba olubisse ku nsi mu bukuusa. Nze, ye Yeshua HaMashiach, ndibawa amaanyi era ndibaleetera okukola eby'amagero bingi eby'amaanyi mu kiseera kino eky'okubonaabona kwa Yakobo. Bwe ntyo bino mbibagamba, amazima gabafuule ab'eddembe, era mubeere ddala ab'eddembe muleme okugwa mu bukuusa bwabwe obunene. Kubanga emisingi gyabwo bulimba, bukuusa, n'obutali butuufu obusaasaanyizibwa ensigo y'omusota.

Nze kkubo, n'amazima, n'obulamu; tewali ajja eri Kitange wabula ng'ayita mu nze.

Nze eyabaleetera ekitiibwa n'obulungi.

Nze alifuga emirembe n'emirembe nga Mukama w'abakama ne Kabaka w'abakabaka.

Nze Mpologoma ey'omu kika kya Yuda.

Nze alagira amagye g'omu ggulu.

Nze alirabikira mu bire okukuŋŋaanya Ekkanisa yange.

Nze nnina ebisumuluzo by'okufa n'amagombe, era ndisala omusango mu butuukirivu.

Nze buli vviivi gwe lirifukamira na buli lulimi lwe liryatula.

Nze Oyo GWE NDI OMUNENE gw'antuma, n'olwekyo muwulire ebigambo byange.

Laba! Bino mbibagamba si lwa kubawa byafaayo bya nkomeredde naye okubakulembera ekigere kimu okudda eri amazima. Mu kiseera kyakyo, byonna birijja mu lujjudde n'obwakabaka bw'ogusota bulisaasaanizibwa, kubanga nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, nkitegeezezza, era nze oyo Yahweh gw'antuma.
