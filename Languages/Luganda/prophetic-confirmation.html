Ogw’ekkumi 28, 2024

Okukakasibwa okw’obunnabbi okuva eri J. Leland Earls

Muwulire ebigambo byange, mmwe abantu bange; muwulirize ekyo kye mbategeeza mu ssaawa eno. Kubanga si nze ajja gye muli kaakano ng’omubaka ow’amaanyi okubabikkulira ebyo ebyakwekebwa okuva emabega mu biro n’emirembe? Kubanga ne kaakano njagala kubikkulira abo abalina amatu okuwulira ebyo bye ntegekedde omubiri gwange mu nnaku zino ez’enkomerero. Mulowooza nti nja kubaleka awatali kulungamizibwa na kumanya bigendererwa byange gye muli mu nnaku zino ez’enkomerero? Mulowooza nti sisobola kulonda babaka ba njawulo be mpitamu okubabikkulira obubaka bwange n’ekigendererwa kyange gye muli ku nkomerero ya byonna? Muwulire ebigambo byange: Nkomyeewo, nti, muwulirize ekyo kye njogera nammwe mu ssaawa eno. Kubanga kaakano nkutte akatabo akatono mu mikono gyange, era ntegeeza abaweereza bange be nnalonda ennyo ebikakung’aanyiziddwamu.

Era kagamba ki? Kagamba bwe kati. Bantu bange, njija gye muli okuyita mu bubaka bwange n’ebigendererwa byange eby’ennaku ez’enkomerero, ebijja okuba okutuukirizibwa kwa byonna bye nnabategekera okuva edda emabega mu mirembe n’ebiro ebingi. Era nja kubaleetera okuyingira mu kujjula kw’ebyo bye nategeka, ddala okujjula kw’ekyo kye natuukiriza ku musaalaba bwe nnayiwa omusaayi gw’obulamu bwange ku lwammwe, okukakasa Endagaano etaggwaawo gye muli, ddala endagaano ey’okutuukirira kw’abantu bange; endagaano erimu okujjula kwonna okw’ekitiibwa kyange n’amaanyi gange eri abo abateegekeddwa ennyo okukiwaanyisa.

Kubanga mbagamba nti batono, yee, ensigalira, be bajja okutegekebwa okukkiriza okubikkulirwa n’ekigendererwa kino eky’ennaku ez’enkomerero, kye ntegeeza kaakano. Kubanga kirina amaanyi mangi okukkirizibwa okuggyako ekibinja ekitegekeddwa, ddala "ekisibo ekitono," ekirikkiriza okujjula kw’endagaano. Kubanga mujja kutambulira mu nnaku zino ez’enkomerero mu kujjula kw’ebyo bye nnabategekera. Kubanga ne kaakano mbawa okubikkulirwa kw’ekyo ekyassibwako akabonero, ne kikakasibwa, ne kiteekebwateekebwa bwe nnayiwa omusaayi gw’obulamu bwange e Kalivaali. Kubanga tekyawandiikibwa nti nnayogera ebigambo, "Kiwedde"? Kiki ekyaggwa? Okujjula kw’endagaano yange kwe kwaggwa; ne mu kiseera ekyo ne kutuukirizibwa ddala okununulibwa okujjuvu okuva mu maanyi gonna ag’omulabe, n’okuyingira okujjuvu mu byonna bye nneetegekedde era bye nsobola okugaba, nga mwotadde n’okuggyawo okufa. Yee, amaanyi amatuukirivu okuwangula emirimu gyonna egy’omulabe, n’ekitiibwa ekituukiridde ekyategekebwa okuva ku kutondebwa kw’ensi eri abo abeetegefu okukkiriza n’okuyingira.

Naye kaakano tekyawandiikibwa nti baaliko omupaka ku Mutukuvu wa Isirayiri? Baana bange, NZE Mutukuvu wa Isirayiri! Naye abantu bankomezza. Bakakanyazizza emitima gyabwe ne bakkiririza mu bulimba era ne balemwa okutwala mu bujjuvu byonna bye nnategeka ne nssaako akabonero, ne mbikakasa, ku musaalaba gwa Kalivaali. Yee, mbagamba nti byonna byali bitegefu mu kiseera ekyo. Naye wa we nnandisangidde abantu abaali abeetegefu—abaali bategekeddwa okukkiriza mu bujjuvu? Yee, tewaaliwo n’omu, okuggyako abo nga Yosuwa ne Kalebu. Alina amatu, awulire! Kubanga ne wano ntegeera nti amatu gammwe mazito, era sisobola kubabikkulira mu bujjuvu mu kiseera kino ekyo kye kitegeeza, naye mujja kukitegeera nga tennayitawo nnaku nnyingi.

Naye kaakano nja kubikkula ekyo kyokka kye mwetegekedde okukkiriza. Kubanga akatabo akatono kalimu bingi eby’olunaku luno n’ekiseera kino. Naye nteekwa okukabikkula mpolampola, kubanga ne kaakano bangi ku mmwe muli ng’abaana abato abakaggyibwa ku mabeere era nga temwetegefu kulya nnyama. Abalala bajja kutegeera ebisingawo ku ekyo kye njogera. Kale nno mundeeke mbategeke era nkomole emitima gyammwe mu bujjuvu okukkiriza obubaka buno obw’ennaku ez’enkomerero. Kubanga oyo aweebwa ebingi, naye yeetaagibwako bingi. Mwagala okumanya ebiri mu katabo kano akatono? Kale mutegeke emitima gyammwe okukkiriza. Kubanga mpolampola kajja kubabikkulirwa nga mwetegefu okukkiriza.

Kubanga ne kaakano ntegeka abantu abajja okutwala ensi etegekeddwa mu bujjuvu—abajja okusomoka amazzi ga Yoludaani—abajja okuseka amaanyi g’ogusota—ddala oyo ajja n’obubonero obw’obulimba n’eby’amagero era ng’okukola kwe kuli ng’amazzi ag’omugga Yoludaani agasenkenya. Kino mukifumiitirizeeko, bantu bange! Kubanga tekyawandiikibwa nti ogusota gujja ng’omujjuliro ogw’amaanyi ogw’okufa n’okuzikiriza? Era ogusota ogwo tegujja kulumba bantu bange n’omujjuliro gw’obulimba n’obukuusa mu nnaku zino?

Naye temujja kuwangulwa, kubanga nja kuleetera amazzi okuzibikira nga mmwe musomoka ku lw’omukono gwange ogw’amaanyi okutwala ensi gye mwategekerwa. Kubanga mazima, eno nsi nnungi. Era abantu bange babungiridde mu ddungu ery’omwoyo ekimala. Mbagamba nti ‘ekyasa eky’okugezesebwa’ kituukiridde, era omulundi ogwokubiri abantu bange bajja mu Nsi Ensuubize. Ne kaakano, mbategeka okusomoka mu maanyi g’omwoyo. Muwulirize era mufumiitirize ku ekyo kye mbagamba. Kubanga akatabo akatono ke nkutte mu mikono gyange kalimu ebyama byonna bye njagala okumanya mu nnaku zino.

O bantu bange, sirindiridde na kwegomba kussooka kuno lwe nnandyabibikkulidde ebyakwekebwa? Ssaawa eno si ye yonna ebyafaayo bye birindiridde? Kino si kye kyayogerwako bannabbi, wadde nga bo bennyini tebaakitegeera? O bantu bange, mufumiitirizza ku kitiibwa n’ettendo ebibaweereddwa okubeerawo mu ssaawa eno n’okwenyigira mu buweereza buno obw’ennaku ez’enkomerero? O, singa musisumuka okuva mu bunafu bwammwe okutunuulira ekitiibwa ekyategekerwa essaawa eno. Lwaki mwogera mu mutima gwammwe nti ekiseera tekinnatuuka? Lwaki mulindirira ne mutayingira n’omutima gwammwe gwonna n’emmeeme yammwe yonna? Mbagamba nti, KAΑKANO KE KASEERA OKUNNOONYA N’OMUTIMA GWAMMWE GWONNA, ndyoke njogerere nammwe mu kisenge eky’omunda eky’omutima gwammwe era mbategeeze okuteesa kwange okw’essaawa eno. Temuwuliriza abo abanoonya okubaggwaamu amaanyi n’okubaziba amaaso ku ekyo kye njogera.

Bali ludda wa abo abalina obuvumu OKUVAAYO ku mazzi ag’obuzibu n’okugezesebwa ne BANTAMBULIRAKO N’OKUKKIRIZA, nga bamanyi nti nsobola okubanyweza n’okubawanirira mu ssaawa eno ne lubeerera, mulyoke mutambulire mu buwanguzi waggulu w’amazzi g’okubonyaabonyezebwa n’okutabanguka, agajja okugezesa abantu bonna mu nnaku zino? Mutambule, mbagamba, nga mutadde amaaso gammwe ku nze bunywanywanywa, nga temutunula ku ddyo newaakubadde ku kkono; nga temutunula ku abo ababeetoolodde newaakubadde ku mazzi agatabanguse agandyagadde okubamira.

Kubanga mbagamba nti temujja kutuuka ku kuyitibwa n’okuzuukuka okw’ennaku ez’enkomerero okutuusa lwe muba mwetegefu olw’okukkiriza okuva mu lyato ly’obwangu n’obutebenkevu bwe mulimu kaakano ne muyingira mu MUKUTU GW’EKIGENDERERWA KYANGE EKY’OBWAKATONDA, nga temukkiriza mayengo ga kubuusabuusa na kutya kubawangula, newaakubadde okukkiriza abo abasalawo okusigala mu lyato okubalemesa. Naye MUJJE BANTU BANGE, kubanga nja kuba n’abo abeesunga okutambulira ku mazzi g’ekyo ekitali kikolebwangawo, ne bansisinkana nga njija gye muli mu maanyi n’ekitiibwa mu nnaku zino.

Tebaalaba nti yali mubiri ogutambulira ku mazzi so si muzimu nga bwe baali balowoozezza? Era Peetero, mu mubiri gwe, teyatambulira ku mazzi? Bwe kityo, bantu bange, si mwoyo gwokka naye OMUBIRI GW’ENNYAMA ogujja okundeeterwa mu nnaku zino okukola eby’obuzira waggulu w’amazzi g’okutabuka n’okulumwa kw’abantu. Mmwe si mmwe OMUBIRI gwa Kristo; si mmwe nnyama yange n’amagumba gange? Bwe mutyo mujja kuba omu nange mu nnaku zino; bwe tutyo tujja kugattibwa wamu KU mazzi okugenda mu maaso mu nnaku zino, bw’ayogera Mukama. Era bwe mutyo mujja okuba mu nnaku ezijja, bw’ayogera Mukama, OMUBIRI GW’ABAWANGUZI—abo abawangudde obugwagwa bwonna obw’emitima obw’ebifo eby’omulabe eby’amazzi era abatambulira mu buwanguzi mu maanyi agawangula.

Bantu bange, muwulire ebigambo byange! Asiidi eziri mu lubuto si kifaananyi ky’emirimu gyange wakati mu mmwe? Muyinza okufuna ekiriisa okuva mu ekyo ekitali mmere n’okuva mu ekyo ekitali ggwanvu? Mbagamba nti, emirimu gy’Omwoyo gwange gyetaagisa mmwe okukkiriza ekyo ekiwa obulamu. Kubanga sisobola kutoola ekyo eky’omugaso okuva mu kigambo kyange ne nkireetera okuyingizibwa mu mubiri gwammwe mulyoke mufune ekiriisa kye mwetaaga? Naye lwaki muliira ku ekyo ekitali kigambo kyange? Lwaki munoonya ekyo kye mumanyi nti tekijja kuggwaza munda mu mitima gyammwe?

Mbagamba nti, bwe mutanoonye n’omutima gwammwe gwonna ne muleka Omwoyo gwange okufuula ekyo kye muyingiza mu mibiri gyammwe ekiramu, mujja kwesanga nga muli mu mbeera ey’omwoyo efanaganako n’eyo ennyonnyoddwa wano. Emirimu gy’omwoyo gwange gyetaagisa. Mu ngeri endala, muba mutoola ekyo kyokka ekifu era ekitajja kukola kintu kyonna kuleeta bumalamu bw’omwoyo munda mu mitima gyammwe. N’olwekyo, mwesseeyo omwoyo ku ekyo kye musoma, kubanga kya mazima eri ekifaananyi ky’ekyo ekigenda mu maaso n’abantu bange leero. Kubanga tekiyinzika, mu mbeera eriwo kaakano ey’ekkanisa, nze okukolera mu Mwoyo gwange mu ngeri ng’eyo nti Ekigambo kyange kiwe obulamu n’obulamu obulungi n’okununulibwa eri bonna abeetaaga. N’olwekyo, nkomyeewo, nti, munnoonye mbalyoke mbawe ekyo ekyetaagisa mmwe okuba n’obulamu obw’omwoyo obulina amakulu era obujjudde, bw’ayogera Mukama waffe Yesu Kristo.

Obubaka obw’obunnabbi obwaweebwa J. Leland Earls 1923-1991, mu kiseera ky’olutalo lw’obutiti olwali wakati mu myaka gya 1962. Buggiddwa ku mutimbagano guno: Omubaka ow'Amaanyi n'Akatabo Akatono


Ebirowoozo bya William Brooks:

Emirundi ebiri kaakano, ntuukiridde omukuumi w'omutimbagano guno agulabirira emirimu gya Mwami Earls naye nga tewali kya kuvaamu, n’olwekyo ku ssaawa eno nsobola okuteebereza nti tajja kuddamu nate. Okuggyako ekyo, sirina kye njogera ku bantu bonna abeetisse obuvunaanyizibwa okukuuma emirimu gye, era kino ddala tekitunuulira mirimu gya bunnabbi Mwami Earls gye yatuukiriza era ng'ebigambo bye bikyaliwo n'okutuusa leero. Mukama ye mulamuzi waffe ffenna, era n'olwekyo eri Kristo gye tuyimirira oba gye tugwa, era Kristo asobola okutuyimirizaawo!

Kyokka, okuba omwerufu, omutimbagano gwe nnagasseko guliko enjigiriza n’ennyimirira nnyingi ze saagala era ze siwagira, era sisikiriziganya na byawandiikibwa ebimu ku byawandiikiddwa. Ebisinga ku bino bye njogeddeko tebyawandiikibwa Mwami Earls, naye newaakubadde ani awandiika ki, weekenneenye weeyanjulire Katonda ng’osiimibwa, omukozi ateeswalira, ng’awawula obulungi ekigambo eky’amazima.

Era nja kukozesa omukisa guno okuddamu okukigamba. Kyonna omuntu ky'ayogera bw'agamba nti ayogerera Katonda, mu bunnabbi oba mu ngeri endala yonna, twala ebigambo ebyo mu byawandiikibwa era naddala eri Mukama waffe okubikakasa n'okubitegeera. Tewali mugaso gwa kumbuuza butuufu bwa nnabbi omu oba omulala. Siriddamu kubanga sijja kugwa mu mutego gw'okwogera ekibi ku nnabbi yenna ow'amazima owa Katonda, era sirina budde bwa kutabulatabula magezi gange kukakasa mirimu gya muntu mulala. Ffe baani okusala omusango gw’omuweereza w’omuntu omulala? Eri mukama waabwe gye bayimirira oba gye bagwa, era bwe tutyo tuweereza Mukama waffe Yesu Kristo. N’olwekyo, Mukama ye wuuyo akakasa oba okugaana obutuufu bwa buli muntu agamba nti ayogera mu linnya lya Mukama. Mukama bw’asalawo okuyogera okuyita mu muntu, nkakasa nti balina enkolagana ey’amaanyi ne Kristo, era abamanyi okusinga bwe beemanyi. Kitwale eri Mukama!

Oboolyawo oyinza okugamba nti, William, nsonga ki gy’olina mu bino byonna? Kye nnandyagambye nti Mukama waffe Yesu Kristo yatutegekera ekkubo okufuna Akatabo Akatono oba Enjiri etaggwaawo, si mu byawandiikibwa byokka, ekiri eky’omugaso ennyo, naye era emyaka nga 60 egiyise mu kamwa ka J. Leland Earls. Ku lwange, nneebaza olw’obwesigwa bwe, era naddala neebaza Kristo Yesu, Mukama waffe omuzuukivu, olw’okukolera mu musajja ono okumuwa ebigambo bino ebituwa omukisa mu maanyi mangi leero. Neebaza Mukama waffe Yesu Kristo olw’okumbikkulira, okugabana nammwe mwenna, Akatabo Akatono ak’Okubikkulirwa Essuula 10/Enjiri etaggwaawo ey’Okubikkulirwa Essuula 14. Shalom Shalom!
