Omujulirwa Ow'okubiri: Isirayiri
Ekikaawa Ekirimu Obuwoomu
Agusito 09, 2024

Amangu Ago Essuubi Lyo Lirabise!

Mukuŋŋaane muwulire eddoboozi lya Mukama wammwe, eddoboozi lya Yesu Omunazaaleesi, kubanga nze eyeyawaayo omulundi gumu, kwonna, n'emirembe gyonna ng'Omwana gw'endiga ow'Okuyitako atuukiridde eri Yakuwa olw'ebibi byammwe, ye Katonda omunene ow'emirembe n'emirembe, nga kabona wammwe omukulu ow'emirembe gyonna mu ntegeka ya Merukizeddeeki mulyoke musonyiyibwe ebibi byammwe. Mujje gyendi, muleete amatu gammwe n'emitima gyammwe muwulirize n'okukkiriza okujjuvu ebigambo byange eby'okukkiriza! Weewaawo mbajjukiza okuwuliriza, si kuwulira buwulizi maloboozi ga bigambo byange, wabula muwulirize n'emitima gyammwe mulyoke mutegeere ekigendererwa kyange, mufune okubudaabudibwa mu kwagala kwe mbagala, era mulyoke mukkirize n'obuwombeefu ekigambo ekisimbibwa mu mmwe, ekiyinza okulokola emmeeme zammwe.

Kubanga njogera n'abeesigwa mu Kkanisa yange, omubiri gwange, omugole wange, bonna be ŋŋenda okukuŋŋaanya gyendi mu kifo eky'obulokozi mu ggulu mu ntebe y'obwakabaka eya Yakuwa. Kubanga nze alabirira emmeeme zammwe, era alabirira okukula kwammwe okw'omu maaso mulyoke mukule ng'ekimera ekituukiridde mu maaso gange; kubanga nze Yakuwa gw'antaddewo okuba omulimi n'omulabirizi w'olubimbi lwe olutukuvu ennyo olw'emmeeme z'abanunule. Kubanga mmwe ye luulu ow'omuwendo omunene, era mu mafaali ga Yakuwa amangi, muli mu b'omuwendo omuwambe ennyo, abataalekerwako jjinja lyonna nga terikyusiddwa mu bulokozi bwammwe, so n'amaanyi gonna gaaterekebwa okubasalira, okubayamba, n'okubaleeta eri Yakuwa, ye Kitammwe Omuyinza w'ebintu byonna, ng'omuwala atamanyanga musajja, atalina bbala, oba mukunya, oba kintu kyonna ekifaanana bwe kityo. Kale temutyanga, so temukkirizanga kubuusabuusa kwonna ku katundu konna akasembayo mu Bigambo byange!

Era nno mbabuuza, abaagalwa bange, lwaki emitima gyammwe gibeeraliikiriza? Lwaki munyeenya emitwe gyammwe ng'abalina ekivume? Mukkirizza Setaani okweyulula mu mitima gyammwe n'abongoota ebigambo eby'obulimba eby'okubudaabuda, ebitaleeta kintu kirala wabula obutakkiriza, ng'anoonya okuyuzaayuza n'okutagulaatagula byonna ebitukuvu? Mulitwala omusota ogw'edda ogwo mu bukujjukujju bwe obw'okulumbalumba obutayimirira n'obulimba bwe obweyongera buli kiseera, ogulyererya ne guyingira mu matu gammwe ne gubateekamu ebigambo by'obulimba ebyennyini ebinaasuula okukkiriza kwammwe, nga bwe kyatuuka ku bangi abakkirizza ebirowoozo byabwe okwononebwa okuva ku kussa ebirowoozo ku kigendererwa kimu kyokka, nze Kristo Yesu Mukama wammwe?

Kubanga tewali mulala alibafaako, era abalala bonna befuulifuula, era tewali Yesu mulala wabula ebifaananyi ebikoppoddwa ebitaliiko mugaso n'ebijingirire ebyakolebwa okubalimba. Kale mbajjukiza obutalekerera kutegeera kuno kwe njagala okubawa mulyoke musobole okweyongera okuba eky'okulabirako eky'omusaana, ne mu kiseera kino eky'enkomerero, ensi gye mumanyi nga tennaggwaawo, n'ekyusibwa emirembe gyonna okuva mu butatya Katonda bwayo obwa kaakano ne yeeyongera mu musango ogw'emirembe, ogunaagwa kati ng'ekibonerezo eky'emirembe ku batatya Katonda abanoonya okwetwalira obusika bwange. Kubanga ensi yange, n'okujjuza kwayo, era nze ndi Mukama ow'emirembe omunene owa bakama, era Kabaka wa bakabaka, ye Mukama wammwe omutuukirivu, era nze aliggya obusika bwange mu mikono gy'abatamiivu abatabulinaako buyinza, so si bakabaka, so si bakatonda, so si bannaŋŋanda okuva mu nsi endala, wabula abagwa abefuulifuula, kubanga bonna befuulifuula. Era ennaku zaabwe ziriggibwawo lubeerera mu busika bwabwe obw'emirembe mu nnimi z'omuliro ezisinga okwokya ez'ebbulu.

Kubanga obulumi bw'okuzaala kati tebukoma, era waliwo obubonero bungi waggulu, n'obubonero bungi mu nsi obulabika buli kiseera era obutakoma. Era bino biweereddwa ng'ekirangiriro mulyoke mube n'essuubi, kale temubigayanga ne mubyefuulira ensonga ng'abasekerera bwe bakola! Omulimu gw'emikono gya Yakuwa oguweddwako emikono gya bamalayika be ab'amaanyi, era ddala ogukubiddwa n'olunwe lwa Katonda yennyini oluusi, ku kiragiro kye eky'emirembe, birangirira ebikolwa bino eby'omu ggulu ebinaatera okutuuka; kubanga olunaku lutuuse era lubaliiko, abaagalwa bange, kale temutyanga! Ebiseera bituuse eby'okukuŋŋaanya abeesigwa bange okunsisinkana mu bire, nga nzikirira okuva mu ggulu n'eddoboozi lya malayika omukulu n'ekkondeere lya Katonda ery'amaanyi, kale mukkirize Ebigambo byange kubanga bye Weewaawo era Amiina!

Ani oba kiki ekiribatyemula, mmwe abaana bange? Mutya nti awasizza omugole talijja kutwala mukono gwa mugole we mu ndagaano ey'emirembe nga bwe kyasuubizibwa edda? Ekigambo kya Katonda kifuuse kya bwereere? Katonda akiziyize! Nga bwe kyawandiikibwa era ne kirangirirwa, bwe kityo ddala bwe kiriba nti amangu ago ndibaggyawo mu busungu ne mbateeka mu kifo eky'obulokozi mu ggulu gye ngenze okubateekererateekera ekifo, kubanga mu nnyumba ya Kitange mulimu amayumba mangi. Era bwe mutyo mulibeeranga nange emirembe gyonna, era bwe mutyo muliba bakabaka ne bakabona mu maaso ga Yakuwa Katonda omunene ow'emirembe; kubanga bwe kityo bw'ateeseteese era bw'ategese mu bulamu bwammwe, era ani ayinza okuziyiza ebigendererwa n'entegeka ya Katonda ow'emirembe?

Mbagamba amazima nti tewali kintu era tewali muntu ayinza okuziyiza n'akatono ebigendererwa bya El Shaddai, so tewali ayinza kuyimirira (n'okuwakanya okusingayo obutono) nga tasirisiddwa mu kutya olw'amaanyi, ekitiibwa, n'okukankana okuvawo, Yakuwa bw'atambuza n'akatono okuyimirira okuva ku ntebe ye ey'obwakabaka ey'emirembe, era eggulu lyonna n'ensi bikankana olw'okubeerawo kwe okw'amaanyi. Kubanga newankubadde Yakuwa yeekwese mu bugonvu, mu kwagala, ne mu kusaasira okumala ennaku nnyingi kati (kubanga olunaku lumu luli ng'emyaka lukumi mu maaso ga Katonda ow'emirembe), kati kye kiseera Yakuwa ky'alonze mwe yeebikkulira eri ekitonde kye, lwe balimanya bonna nti waliwo Katonda mu ggulu, era ddala balitegeera nti nga waggulu bwe kiri, bwe kityo bwe kiriba wansi ne ku nsi, kubanga Yakuwa ye Katonda w'eggulu n'ensi, era afuga byonna ku kitonde kye, so tewali mulala wabula ye yekka mu maanyi, mu kitiibwa, ne mu buyinza. (Era abagwa bakwatiddwa okutya okw'emirembe.) Kale temutyanga, mmwe Abaana b'Oyo Asinga Obukulu, kubanga Katonda omunene ow'emirembe, ye Yakuwa yennyini, y'abalwanirira, era y'alibawonya, alibanunula, era alikyusa obulamu bwammwe ne bufuuka kitiibwa kya lubeerera mu kanyaanya ka liso.

Kale lwaki mufuuka abatagumiikiriza? Muwulira ng'omwami wammwe ow'emirembe era awasizza omugole abasuuliridde, mmwe mugole wange? Omuwonya wammwe ow'emirembe asudde omubiri gwe, n'aguleka nga tegulina mutwe? Katonda akiziyize n'ekintu ekyo okukirowoozaako. Kintu ki Yakuwa ky'alangiridde eri ekitonde kye nti kinaatera okubaawo, ne kitabaawo oluvannyuma? Ebigambo bya Nuuwa byagwa ku ttaka ekkalu ng'ebisuubizo eby'obwereere, bwe yali omubuulizi w'obutuukirivu okumala emyaka kikumi mu abiri mu nsi eyaliwo mu biro ebyo? Nedda! Wabula amangu ago era n'amaanyi mangi amazzi gaasumululwa mu kaseera kamu ne gasukka ensozi ezisinga obuwanvu ku nsi mu nnaku ezaaddirira.

Era nno mbakubiriza obutaddamu nate okwegatta ku abo abakyayira mu butamanya. Balonda obutamanya kyeyagalire baleme kutegeera nti olw'ekigambo kya Katonda eggulu lyabangawo okuva edda, n'ensi ng'eyimiridde okuva mu mazzi era mu mazzi: Ensi eyaliwo mu biro ebyo, bwe yayanjaalirwa amazzi, n'esaanikira: Naye eggulu n'ensi, ebiriwo kaakano, olw'ekigambo kye kimu biterekerwa omuliro okutuusa ku lunaku lw'omusango n'okuzikirira kw'abantu abatya Katonda. Era nno ndangirira nate nti olunaku olw'omuliro n'omusango n'okuzikirira kw'abantu abatya Katonda lutuuse, nga bwe kyasuubizibwa edda, era bwe kityo bwe kiriba amangu ago.

Era nno mbabuuza nate, mmwe baana bange abato, Katonda muntu n'alyoka alimba? Nze, Yesu Kristo omutuukirivu, siri mwesigwa ne nneerabira mmwe, ate ne mbawa essuubi ery'obulimba? Mbeerabidde ne ŋŋenda kuvuba? Mbabuuza, abaagalwa bange, nnawaayo ettondo lyange ery'omusaayi erisembayo mu bulumi obw'amaanyi ku musaalaba, ne ndyoka mbasuulawo, so nga mbawadde ebisuubizo ebikulu ennyo era eby'omuwendo, ddala ebisuubizo ebyaweebwa Yakuwa yennyini mu kyama kye eky'emirembe, ye ekyama ekinene ekya Katonda?

Katonda akiziyize ebintu ebyo n'okubirowoozaako, era nno mutegeere ebirowoozo ebibi bya Setaani by'abakasukira, mubigaane mubikangavvule, era mu kifo ky'okulowooza okwo okutaliimu nsa ne kubi, muteekewo ebyawandiikibwa ebitukuvu, ddala ebisuubizo bya Yakuwa ebiri Weewaawo era Amiina. Naye mu biseera byonna era naddala mu biseera ng'ebino kyawandiikibwa, era Pawulo bwe yawandiikira Abakkolinso ku lwange nti: Naye ntya, omusota nga bwe gwalimba Kaawa olw'obukujjukujju bwagwo, ebirowoozo byammwe bwe bityo bireme kwononebwa okuva ku kussa ebirowoozo ku kigendererwa kimu ekiri mu Kristo. Era nno mbakubiriza, mmwe baana bange, ddala abaagalwa bange be nneegomba ennyo, okusigala nga mussa ebirowoozo byammwe byonna ku nze ne ku bigambo byange, kubanga nze ndi Kigambo kya Katonda ekyefuula omubiri, era buli Kigambo kya Katonda ddala kiribaawo, so tewaliba n'akatono ku Bigambo byange akalemererwa, ebyayogerwa era ne bisikira Omwoyo Omutukuvu oyo omunene ow'emirembe, ye Yakuwa, Katonda omunene ow'emirembe!

Era nno mbakubiriza, mmwe baana bange, ddala abaagalwa bange be nneegomba ennyo, mmwe ssanyu lyange era engule yange: muwummulire mu nze, muwummulire mu mirimu gyange, muleme okukola egyammwe. Kubanga kyawandiikibwa: Kale kisigalidde okuwummula eri abantu ba Katonda. Kubanga oyo ayingidde mu kuwummula kwe, naye awummudde emirimu gye, nga Katonda bwe yawummula egye. Kale mufube okuyingira mu kuwummula kwange, omuntu yenna aleme okugwa ng'ekyokulabirako kye kimu eky'obutakkiriza. Mmwe abaana bange mufube okuleka emirimu gyammwe, mukole emirimu gye mbawa okutuukiriza, so temwesigamanga ku kutegeera kwammwe, naye mu makubo gammwe gonna munkkirizenga nze, Kristo Yesu Mukama wammwe eyalinnya mu ggulu, era mulifuna okuwummula mu nsozi ez'amalundiro ag'omuddo omuto n'obulokozi, naddala wansi w'okulabirira n'okukuuma kw'omukono gwange ogw'amaanyi, kubanga nze Yeshua HaMashiach, era omusumba wammwe omukulu anaatera okulabika!

Era nno mbabuuza, abaagalwa bange, muliddayo okubeera mu basekerera abasirusiru abakudaala era abanoonya okubakema okuyingira mu makubo gaabwe ag'obutalina kukkiriza? Mulireka ebigambo byange n'obubonero obungi, eby'amagero, n'ebyamatendo ebibeetoolodde? Mu bujjulujjulujju mulowooza nti muyinza okweyongera okubeera ng'abo abatategeera akabi n'omusango ebigenda okujja? Muliddayo okubeera mu bantu abasomeddwa omusango, abagaana okwenenya ne balondawo omusango ogw'ekikambwe n'ekibonerezo? Mbagamba lwatu nti bwe muligezaako, mulisanga nga muli ng'abatalina magezi abayita ku kulabula kwange ne babonerezebwa awatali nsonga, era nno mulabuddwa.

Mutegeere nti ensi mwe muli kati si ye yaliwo wadde wiiki ntono eziyise, era yeeyongera okukyuka ku sipiidi eyeyongera. Mutegeere nti tewali n'omu ku mmwe eyali abadde mu nsi eriwo kati, kubanga ensi mwe muli kati esibiddwa mu musango, naye ekkiriziddwa okuwa ebirangiriro eby'ekitiibwa eby'embaga ey'omu ggulu enejja, nze Kristo Yesu gye ndiwasa omugole wange omwesigwa emirembe gyonna, era bwe batyo balibeeranga nange. Era omugole wange n'Ekkanisa yange bwe balitwalibwa amangu ago, olwo okuzikirira okw'amangu ago kuligwa ku basekerera bonna n'abantu ababi abaabasekereranga ne babakudaala, nga bagamba nti: Enkya eriba nga leero, ne leero liri nga jjo, so siriraba kabi, so siri nnamwandu wabula ndi nnabagereka afuga ebintu byonna! Okuggyako nti okuzikirira kwabwe kulibatuukako amangu ago ng'omukazi alumwa ebisa, so tebaliwona.

Kale muve mu bo, mweyawule mu bintu BYONNA ne mu makubo gammwe gonna. Temuyinza kugabana nabo mu kintu kyonna, oba muligabana nabo mu bibonyoobonyo byabwe. Mutegeere nti ebikolwa bitandise dda, ddala ebikolwa ebirikwata ku boonoonyi, naye nze nditeekawo obulokozi eri abeesigwa bange, ebikangabjulo bino bireme okubalumya mmwe baana bange. Kubanga obulumi buno obutakoma obw'okuzaala bugendereddwamu okuzuukusa abasembayo ku basula, n'okwawula abasembayo ku balinnya mu lyato, akabi nga tekannagwawo amangu ago n'ebiseera ne biggwaawo. Temukyayinza kubeera na bulamu bwa kulagajjalira, era muteekwa okutambulira mu magezi amangi, kale mukwate amakubo gange, era mutegeere nti mbawadde amagezi amangi n'okutegeera mulyoke musobole okwawula ebiseera mwe muli, ne mu bantu abali mu tulo otungi ennyo, abatategeera kabi akanaatera okutuuka, era abalibonaabona olw'ekyo.

Kale mutegeere nti ebiseera ebisigaddeyo tebiriiko mugaso, era biweereza bigendererwa bya Yakuwa byokka, omusango nga tegunnaba kugwawo amangu ago. Weewaawo, omulabe aweereddwa omukisa okwetegekera okulumba kwe ku Babulooni Ekyama, kubanga bwe kityo bw'aweereddwa ye okuva eri Yakuwa okukituukiriza. Naye mutegeere ddala nti Setaani mulabe eyawangulwa, asibiddwa ku nsi kulindirira musango, era akkirizibwa kyokka okutuukiriza ebigendererwa bya Yakuwa, era alituula asirise alindirire omuzibi okuggibwawo, so tayinza n'akatono kukola kisingawo ku ky'akkiriziddwa, kale temutyanga kuwuluguma kwa mpologoma eyo etalina mannyo, kubanga omulangira omuto asibiddwa mu kuwangulwa! Kubanga ddala, abagwa babatya, era bategeera era bamanyi nti abo be baayigganyanga edda be balireeta omusango n'ekibonerezo ekituukiridde mu bifuba byabwe emirembe n'emirembe. Kiki? Temumanyi nti muliramula bamalayika?

Era nno mbabuuza, abaagalwa bange, kati temulikkiriza kisa kyange n'okusaasira kwange okugololerwa ababuze, ne bwe kiri nti omusango n'akabi bitegekeddwa okugwa amangu ago? Mwerabidde emyaka emingi egy'ekisa n'okusaasira gye nabawa awatali kubagwanira mulyoke mutuukirize ebibi byammwe nga temunnakyukira butuukirivu? Muligaana abo abanoonya okulinnya mu lyato tulyoke tube wamu akaseera katono emabegako? Nzikiririza ebisingawo obulungi ku mmwe, mmwe baana bange, era nno mbajjukiza okugumiikiriza n'okubabirira, ne bwe kiba nti akabi kanaatera okugwa amangu ago.

Bw'eba nga waliwo mu mmwe alowooza nti alina ensonga okuleeta obwenkanya ku Setaani n'obwakabaka bwe, nze nnina ensonga zisinga ezaali ziyingidde mu mitima gyammwe. Kubanga ani ku mmwe eyalwana ne Setaani, okutuusa ku ttondo ly'omusaayi lye erisembayo, nga kyeyagalire mwawanikiddwa ku muti ogwo omukakanyavu mu bulumi obw'entiisa? Kubanga nze kyeyagalire neetikka omusaalaba gwange ne nneewaayo mu buwanguzi obw'emirembe, bwe nnayisibwa obubi mu mikono gy'abakozi b'ebibi, bwe nnatwala ekifo kyammwe mu musango mu bulumi obungi ebweru w'enzigi mu boonoonyi abasinga obubi. Omusaayi gwange gwali mutukuvu, so tewaali kibi kyayinza kusimbibwa ku kiwandiiko kyange mu butuukirivu. Naye mu kwagala kwange eri Kitange n'okwagala kwange eri mmwe, kyeyagalire nnawaayo obulamu bwange, era nandyongedde okukikola mu kanyaanya ka liso, newankubadde ekintu ekyo tekyetaagibwa. Kale ne mu kubonaabona kwammwe okwa kaakano, mugoberere ekyokulabirako kyange eky'okugumiikiriza, nga mumanyi nti amangu ago muliyingira mu kuwonyezebwa okw'emirembe!

Era nno mbajjukiza okweyongera okunywera okutuusa ku nkomerero, ddala enkomerero lwe mutwalibwa amangu ago mu kanyaanya ka liso okuyingira mu ggulu mu mpeera ey'emirembe n'ekigendererwa eky'emirembe, naye abafu mu Kristo be balisooka okuzuukira, nammwe mulibegattako amangu ago mu kujaguza okw'emirembe. Kubanga ebigendererwa eby'emirembe bye mwayitirwa, ne mulondebwa, ne muteekebwako akabonero, ne mukuumibwa, birituukiriza ebigendererwa eby'emirembe ebya Katonda asinga obukulu, era byonna binaatera okubikkulibwa mu mmwe baana bange, kale munyirire ku lwange ng'ettaala zange kaakano mu kuwummulamu kuno okusembayo, akabi ak'amangu ago nga tekannatuuka ku nsi. Era ndibaleetera mmwe mulyoke mubaleete gyendi, kubanga mukyali babaka bange okutuusa lwe mutwalibwa amangu ago okuyingira mu ggulu. Era amangu ago akabi akanene kaligwa.

Mutegeere nti ebiseera biggw_eddeko, so tewali kiyinza kukyusa bikolwa ebiri mu kujja era ebirinnya ku basenze b'ensi, kubanga musango munene ogunaatera okujja ku boonoonyi. Era nno mbajjukiza era mbakaka olw'okusaasira, ekisa, n'okusaasira okwo kwennyini Yakuwa kwe yabooleseza mu maaso ga Yesu Kristo olw'obulokozi bwammwe. Muyinza okusigala nga muli beesigwa okutuusa ku nkomerero? Mulikkiriza okuyingira mu kuwummula kwange okw'emirembe awatali kuggwaamu maanyi ku ddakiika esembayo? Mulitunuulira n'omutima gwammwe gwonna n'okussaayo omwoyo ku bigambo byange, ne mutegeera nti amangu ago kirikyuka emirembe gyonna, era amangu ago ye kaakano.

Tewakyali biseera bisigaddeyo Babulooni Ekyama okwenenya, so tewakyali biseera mu mirembe gyange egy'ekisa n'okusaasira, kubanga ebiseera biggw_eddeko. Era buli kiseera kirina ekiseera kyakyo n'ekigendererwa kyakyo, era mu kutuukirizibwa kw'ebiseera, okununnulibwa okw'amangu ago kuligwa ku banunule, kale abanunule b'ensi bagambe nti be yagula okuva mu mukono gw'omulabe. So temukkirizanga mubbi kubabbako ssanyu lyammwe, kubanga mugole ki akungubagira olunaku lw'embaga ye, ddala olunaku lw'embaga olw'ekitiibwa, olutukuvu, olutayinza kwononebwa oba kutyemulwa?

Kale mubudaabudibwe mu Bigambo byange eby'okubudaabuda, muleme okubeera ng'abo abasuuliddwa wansi mu nsonyi ne mu musango. Kubanga mmwe mugole wange, muli abo be nfuulidde abataliiko bbala era abataliiko nsobi mu maaso g'entebe ya Yakuwa yennyini, era muli bibala ebibereberye okuva mu bafu. Newankubadde abo ku mmwe abakyali abalamu era abasigaddewo tebalirega ku kufa, mwazaalibwa nga mufiiridde mu kusobya ne mu bibi. Era muliba muyise butereevu okuva mu kufa (mwe mwandyandifiiridde), ne muyingira butereevu mu bulamu obw'emirembe obwo bwe mulimu dda omutemwa, ddala akabonero ako akanene aka Yakuwa ke mwateekebwako olw'olunaku olwo olw'amangu ago olw'okununulibwa. Kubanga mwazaalibwa nate okuva mu nsigo etevunda, era muli baana ba Katonda asinga obukulu, ddala baana ba Katonda Omuyinza w'ebintu byonna!

N'olwekyo, mubudaabudibwe, muwummule, era mukolere wamu nange mu mpaka zino ez'obuwanguzi ez'ebiro byonna. Kubanga tewali biseera byali bibaddewo ku nsi ng'ebino, so tewaliba biseera ng'ebino nate. Ddala ebiseera abaana ba Katonda ab'emirembe lwe balindirira mu butanwa okussa omukka gwabwe ogusooka ogw'obulamu obw'emirembe ng'abaana abato ab'emirembe, okuyingira mu kubeerawo okw'emirembe okwa Katonda ow'emirembe, gye baliwasa Mukama waabwe ow'emirembe mu ndagaano ey'emirembe, era... amangu ago, bwe kiriba!

Nze eyeyawaayo omulundi gumu, kwonna, n'emirembe gyonna ng'Omwana gw'endiga ow'Okuyitako atuukiridde.

Nnaatera okukuŋŋaanya abeesigwa gyendi mu kifo eky'obulokozi mu ggulu mu ntebe y'obwakabaka eya Yakuwa.

Nze alabirira emmeeme zammwe, era alabirira okukula kwammwe okw'omu maaso mulyoke mukule ng'ekimera ekituukiridde.

Nze Yakuwa gw'antaddewo okuba omulimi n'omulabirizi w'olubimbi lwe olutukuvu ennyo olw'emmeeme z'abanunule.

Nze ndi Mukama ow'emirembe omunene owa bakama, era Kabaka wa bakabaka, ye Mukama wammwe omutuukirivu.

Nze aliggya obusika bwange mu mikono gy'abatamiivu abatabulinaako buyinza.

Nze ndi Kigambo kya Katonda ekyefuula omubiri, era buli Kigambo kya Katonda ddala kiribaawo.

Nze Yeshua HaMashiach, era omusumba wammwe omukulu anaatera okulabika!

Nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, ntumye Ebigambo bino ng'abiyisa mu muweereza wange, era okuyita mu muweereza wange Ebigambo bino bitumiddwa!

Era nno mbakubiriza, abaagalwa bange, obutaggwaamu maanyi, era musigale nga munywera okutuusa ku nkomerero ddala ey'amangu ago, eneekomya emirembe gyonna ebiseera byammwe ku nsi mu mbeera yammwe eya kaakano. Amangu ago, era mu kanyaanya ka liso, mulisanga nga muyimiridde mu ntebe y'obwakabaka eya Yakuwa mu ssanyu ery'emirembe, era mulitegeera ddala era mulimanya ddala nga nammwe bwe mutegeerwa era bwe mumanyiddwa. Era bwe kityo ekisuubizo kirisisinkana okutuukirizibwa mu ssanyu ery'emirembe n'ekitiibwa eky'emirembe ekibalindiridde, era byonna birituukirizibwa mu mmwe mu kanyaanya ka liso, era amangu ago bwe kiriba. Musigale mu nze, mmwe baana bange, kubanga mbagala nnyo, so sikwatibwa nsonyi kubayita baganda bange.
