Obujulirwa obw'emirundi ebiri: Ekkanisa
Mu Biwandiiko by'Omutume Pawulo
Meeyi 01, 2024
Okubwatuka okw'omusanvu

Mumpulirize era Muntegeere Ddala!

Mumpulirize era muntegeere ddala, mmwe bannabbi ab'obulimba abakyusa Ekigambo kyange ekitukuvu, kubanga temuli bange, so temulina mwoyo gwange omutukuvu, era mulumbye Omugole wange, ye Kkanisa yange gye nnayita! Muwulire era mugondere okulabula kwange, ggwe ggwanga etakkiririza, ekkozi era ekyamye, kubanga mujja kwanukula olw'ebikolwa byammwe byonna ebibi! Muwulire ebigambo byange eby'okulabula, mmwe abakulembera Ekkanisa yange mu kukyama, kubanga obusungu bwange bubabuubuukiridde, kubanga nze Mwana gw'Endiga owa Katonda! Kubanga bangi ku mmwe mujja kwekwesa mu mpuku, ne mu njazi z'ensozi. Era mujja kugamba ensozi n'enjazi nti, Mutugweko, mutukweke eri amaaso g'oyo atuula ku ntebe, n'eri obusungu bw'Omwana gw'Endiga: Kubanga olunaku olukulu olw'obusungu bwe lutuuse, era ani ayinza okuyimirira?

Kubanga ensi ejja kulaba okwabika kwange, era okukankana okunene kujja kujja ku bonna, era okuzikirira okw'amangu kujja kutuuka ku nsi ng'Omugole wange anyagulibwa n'atwalibwa mu kifo eky'emirembe. Kubanga njija kuggyawo omuzibi, n'obubi bwonna bujja kuba nga tebukomekkerezebwa, era omusango omunene gujja kusuulibwa okuva mu ggulu, nga bwe kyawandiikibwa mu kitabo ekyo ekinene eky'okubikkulirwa kwange, ye nkitabo eky'Okubikkulirwa! N'olwekyo, nkusalirawo omusango, mmwe mwenna abajja okwonoona Ekkanisa yange: ennaku zammwe zibalibwa, era emitima gyammwe gijja kubagwamu amangu ddala ng'obusungu bwange bubikkuliddwa. Mmwe bannabbi ab'obulimba abataliiko kye mugasa abaayingirira Ekkanisa yange, muyigirizza eddogo ery'okutabikatabika n'eby'emizizo n'Ekigambo kyange ekitukuvu n'amakubo gange amatukuvu, era mujja kusasula.

Muleetedde abaana bange abato okwonoona n'okugwa mu bizibu ebinene, ebyo bye njija okubakomezawo emirundi kikumi mu musango gwange ku mmwe. Mukyusizza amakulu g'ebigambo mu biwandiiko byange ebitukuvu, mukoppye ebiwandiiko byange ebitukuvu nga mubiddamu okuwandiika n'okukyusa ebigambo bya Katonda Ataggwaawo, ye Yahweh, era mujja kusasulira emirembe n'emirembe. Muyingizza ebiwandiiko ebyonooneddwa mu Kkanisa yange mu ngeri y'emu bakadde bammwe abataliiko kye baamala bwe baawa obutwa enzizi ne bawa obutwa emmere yennyini eyaweebwa okubezaawo abantu, era mujja kusasulira emirembe n'emirembe. Buli kigambo ekitaliiko nsa kyonna kye mwogedde, buli ssente ey'ennyuma ennafu gyonna gye muterekera okwezikiriza, buli kirowoozo ekibi kyonna kye mwali mulowoozezza, buli kigendererwa ekibi kyonna kye mwagoberera kijja kubaleeterwa, era mujja kusasula, okutuusa ku kasente akasembayo; era temujja kusobola n'akatono okuyimirira mu maaso gange, era mujja kutwala ekifo kyammwe mu nnimi z'omuliro Ssebo gye yateekerateekera Sitaani ne bamalayika be.

Naye mutegeere nti temuli kya kitalo era temuli bya kwewuunya, kubanga muli batume ab'obulimba, abakozi abalimbya, nga mwefuula abatume ba Kristo. So si kya kitalo, kubanga Sitaani yennyini yeefuula malayika w'omusana. N'olwekyo, si kintu kinene abaweereza be bwe beeyinulamu nga be baweereza b'obutuukirivu, enkomerero yaabwe bweba eneeberanga ng'ebikolwa byabwe bwe biri. Kubanga bwe ntyo bwe nnawandiikira Abakkolinso!

Mujja kweyongera okukola emirimu gyammwe nga mujjuza ebibi byammwe okutuuka ku ddaala erisinga n'okujjuza emirembe n'emirembe n'ekibonerezo n'okubonyaabonyezebwa kwammwe kwennyini, kubanga obusungu bwange buli ku mmwe nnyo, era mujja kwegatta ku bakadde bammwe mu muliro. Kubanga muli ngano mu ŋŋaano, era njija kubasimbulawo ddala; kubanga Kitange, ye Yahweh, alangiridde nti temuliba na mmizi newaakubadde obusika, kubanga ensigo yammwe ya wa mubi, era mwenna muli batemu era balimba. Kubanga nze nagamba ki bakadde bammwe mu Buyudaaya emyaka gino emingi egiyise? Mmwe muli ba kitammwe Setaani, era okwegomba kwa kitammwe kwe mwagala okukola: yali mutemu okuva ku lubereberye, n'atayimirira mu mazima, kubanga mu ye temuli mazima. Bw'ayogera obulimba, ayogera ebibye: kubanga mulimba, era kitaawo w'obulimba.

Mutegeere kino, mmwe mwenna abazaalibwa mu kuvvoola, ye nkuvoola okw'okuvvoola Yahweh nga bakadde bammwe abataliiko kye baamala bwe baagezaako okukyusa ekifaananyi kya Katonda, Adamu kye yatondebwa mu kyo, ne bakifuula emizizo egyakolebwa okuzikiriza abantu, ennaku zammwe ziri kumpi okuggwaako. Kubanga ani ku bannabbi bakadde bammwe gwe bataayigganya? Era basse abo abaabuulirira edda okujja kw'Oyo Omutuukirivu; mmwe abamulyamu olukwe kati n'abamutta. Temujja kuwona, ka mube nga mwagenda wala mu nsi okwekwesa obusungu bwange; obusungu bwange bujja kubazuula, kubanga tewali kiyinza kukwekebwa mu maaso gange, era ensi yange n'okujjudde kwayo, kubanga nnagigula mu musaayi gwange gwennyini bakadde bammwe gwe baayiwa mu buvvoomi, ne mukakasa ddala ekifo kyammwe mu muliro. Ye nze, Kristo Yesu, eyalinnyisibwa n'agulumizibwa, njija gye muli, so temujja kuwona.

Mumpulirize, mmwe abagwa, ye mwenna abaanfumita, kubanga mweegamba nti, tuwangudde Yahweh ne tumutta omwana we gwe yatumira okutuwangula ensi, era tuli bawanguzi emirembe gyonna, kubanga Yeshua afudde, ne musanyuka mu busirusiru! Mmwe abasiru abasanyaladde abatategeera! Temwategeera nti Katonda yandinzukizza, ng'asumuludde obulumi bw'okufa: kubanga tekyasobokanga okuba nti nze nkusibwa bwo. Mwamponya akaserengeto kange koka, era temulina maanyi ku nze, so temwategeera nti oyo ali waggulu ennyo, ye Mutonzi wammwe, Yahweh, yababalaga bya kyama bitagasa byokka!

Kubanga yamanya nti mwandimujeemudde n'ekitonde kye ne mugezaako okutwala ebyo bye yanteekerateekera nze, Mukama waffe Yesu Kristo. Abaavu bange bajja kusikira ensi, kubanga ensi yange n'okujjudde kwayo. Kubanga mwayigiriza abantu amagezi agagaanyizibwa; mwayigiriza entalo; mwakyamya abakazi mu kulongoosa amaaso gaabwe n'obuso obusiigeko okutuukiriza okwegomba kwammwe ku bo; mwayonoona olusuku lwa Katonda nga musiga engano mu ŋŋaano, era buli ngano gye mwasiga mu biseera byonna ejja kusimbulibwawo, n'esibibwa, n'esuulibwa mu muliro nammwe! Kubanga mwawangulwa bwe nneewaayo kyereere olw'okulokolebwa kw'abantu, era Katonda n'anzukiza mu bafu obutaddamu kufa!

Naye temwalina magezi okutegeera nti nze nandiwaddeyo obulamu bwange olw'okwagala kwa Katonda kwe yantonda mu nze, kubanga nze mulokozi w'ensi! Okwagala kwa Yahweh tekuyinza kuwangulwa, kubanga Yahweh tawangulwa, era tewali kitonde kyonna mu bitonde ekimufaanana, kubanga ye ali waggulu ennyo, era okumasamasa kwe n'ekitiibwa kye birina amaanyi gonna okuva emirembe n'emirembe! Kubanga nabagamba nti, "kino ky'okola kikole mangu!" Mmwe mu butamanya n'amalala temwategeera nti nze nneesonyiwa okukwatibwa abapangisibwa bammwe abataliiko kye baamala abaakola omulimu omubi ogw'okutta omusaayi ogutalina musango! Mu kutamanya kwammwe okw'ekivve, mwagaana okutegeera nti nnalina ebibinja bya bamalayika omulundi gumu n'emitwalo ebiri nga mbiragira, kubanga wonna we nnandyagalidde nnandibaziyizza. Naye okwagala kwa Yahweh kwandituukiridde kutya, okuggyako olw'okwagala kwange n'obuwulize bwange eri Kitange? Kubanga okwagala kwa Yahweh tewali ayinza kukuvvuunuka! Nedda, si mu ggulu so si mu nsi, mu ngeri yonna so si lubeerera!

Mmwe abagwa, ye mmwe abalangira b'ensi eno egudde, mmwe ba Sitaani, mwenna mujja kwokebwa mu muliro ogutazikira olw'obukyayi bwammwe obutali butukuvu n'amakubo gammwe amabi ennyo ag'obutawulize n'okuzikiriza. Nga bwe mwakikola omu ku basembayo ku bano, baganda bange, mwakikola nze. O mmwe abakuumi abagwa, ye mmwe bamalayika abatagondera abaava mu kifo kyammwe eky'olubereberye, temulina mirembe! Mujja kusuulibwa mu muliro mwe mutaliva, era mujja kwokya ddala. Engano zammwe zonna ez'emizizo ezitali ntukuvu ze mwasiga nga musukka ensalo za Yahweh mu nkola zammwe ez'okukyusa endagabutonde wakati w'omuntu ne malayika, wakati w'omuntu n'ensolo, wakati w'omuntu n'ebitonde by'omu nnyanja, wakati w'ensolo ez'engeri ez'enjawulo, ye buli kintu eky'omuzizo kyonna kye mwakola kijja kusuulibwa mu muliro nammwe, kubanga temulina mirembe newaakubadde okusonyiyibwa!

Mwagoberera ba Sitaani abaagezaako okuzikiriza ekitonde kya Yahweh, okutuusa n'okumbulagiza, Yesu Kristo, bwe nneewaayo kyeyagalire mu mikono gye ng'ekiweebwayo eky'Okuyitako ekituukiridde eky'ensi, bwe ntyo ne nkakasa okunnuŋŋutibwa kw'abantu n'okukakasa ekifo kyammwe emirembe n'emirembe mu muliro omunene ogutayinza kuzikizibwa! Mujja kusasulira ddala ebibi byammwe byonna n'emizizo, era buli kikolwa ekibi kyonna kye mwakola kijja kubakomezebwawo emirundi kikumi, kubanga bonna bajja kusasulibwa okusinziira ku bikolwa byabwe. N'amakubo gammwe gonna amabi gajja kusasulibwa ku mitwe gyammwe emirundi mingi okusingawo, kubanga mwalowooza nti muyinza okumpanguza, era n'amalala mukyalowooza nti muyinza okuwangula so nga mwawangulwa ddala.

Muwulire ebigambo byange, ggwe omusota ogw'edda, kubanga omusango gwo guli ku ggwe era ndaba okutya kwo, kubanga otya eby'omu maaso go okusinga byonna, ggwe empologoma etalina mannyo! Okuwoolera eggwanga kwange, era nze nze ndisasula, n'ebiseera byammwe bituuse. Obusungu bwa Katonda n'obusungu bw'omwana gw'endiga binaatera okusumululwa nga njija okumenya obubonero omusanvu, nga bamalayika omusanvu batumiddwa okufuuwa amakondeere g'emisango, era bamalayika omusanvu abatwaliddwa abalina ebibonyoobonyo omusanvu ebisembayo ne batumibwa, era bijja kusumululwa, kubanga mu byo obusungu bwa Katonda mujjuzibwamu. Obubalagaze bungi bujja kubatuukako mwenna, nga mbonereza ddala era ne nsaanyaawo obwakabaka bwammwe. Kabaka wammwe omukulu ndimuwangula ne nnabbi wammwe ow'obulimba ndimuwangula, era bajja kusuulibwa nga balamu mu nnyanja y'omuliro eyaka n'ekibiriiti; era eyo gye bajja kubalindirira okwegatta ku bo, era eyo gye mujja okubanga mwetooloddwa omuliro n'ekibiriiti, era omusango gwammwe gunaatera okutandika!

Omugole wange, totya! Ebigambo byange eby'omusango si biriyo naye bya kukubudaabuda, kubanga nkuloppyeko mu busungu obunaatera okusumululwa, era njija kukukweka mu kifo eky'emirembe n'empeera ennene, era emirembe gyonna ojja kubeeranga nange. Kubanga nze nakulonda, kubanga nze nakuyita, era ggwe ggugu lyange erisinga obunene, era ggwe ggugu lya Yahweh erisinga obunene; ye ggwe luulu olw'omuwendo omunene, kubanga nze nkuwadde byonna byange. Nkuwa amagezi Omugole wange okunoonya okwawula n'okujja gyendi okufuna amagezi, kubanga njija kukuyigiriza. Kubanga abamu ku mmwe mugoberedde amakubo gamu g'abannabbi bano ab'obulimba; mukyuke mwetegefu eri Mukama wammwe, nange ndibakkiriza.

Kubanga babasendasenze n'eddogo ly'Ekikristaayo, era mukkirizza obulimba bungi bwe babatunze, ne babagamba nti mazima, naye bulimba bwa muzizo. Enzizi zino ezitasiima maaso zigezezzaako okubaggya ku nze, era ekyo tekiyinzika, n'olwekyo temutya, kubanga sijja kugaana! Nja kweyongera okubasumulula mubeere ab'eddembe ddala, so sijja kusirika! Nze mbalwanirira mu ngeri nnyingi nnyingi ze mutamanyi. Kubanga mwenna mwateekebwako akabonero nga temunnazaalibwa, kubanga Kitange yabamanya nga mukyali mu nda za bammwe, era yabalindirira buli omu ku mmwe okumala ekiseera kye mutayinza kutegeera, era bulijjo mubadde wakati mu mutima gwe. Mwateekebwako akabonero bwe mwazaalibwa, era bamalayika bange baabakuuma ku kiragiro kyange ne babakuuma ne babalwanirira, kubanga bwe batyo bwe batumibwa. Kino kikwewuunyisa? Ojja kuwuliriza Abebbulaniya kye balina okugamba? Naye ani ku Bamalayika gwe yali agambye ebbanga lyonna nti, Tuula ku mukono gwange ogwa ddyo, okutuusa lwe ndifuula abalabe bo entebbe y'ebigere byo? Si bonna myoyo egiweereza, egitumibwa okuweereza abo, abalisikira obulokozi?

Mugole wange, muli basika b'obulokozi mu nze, Yesu Kristo. Mwayitibwa mu nze; mwakuumibwa mu nze era nange; mwaweebwa omukisa okuwulira okubuulirwa kw'okukkiriza okukwata ku nze musobole okumfuula Mukama wammwe, era n'olwekyo nabateekako akabonero n'omwoyo oyo omutukuvu ow'ekisuubizo. Mutegeere nti akabonero ako tekayinza kumenyeka, era munaatera okununulibwa ddala okuyingira mu butaggwaawo, kubanga temulina kye mumanyi ku kujjula kw'omulimu gw'okutonda gwe nnaatera okutuukiriza mu mmwe, Mugole wange! Mutegeere nti abagwa n'engano zaabwe babakwatirwa obuggya, era bakyawa kyonna Yahweh ky'ayagala, era ndibawalanirako eggwanga ddala ku mitwe gyabwe, kubanga nze ndi oyo ajja okufumita omutwe gw'omusota ogwo ogw'edda, kubanga kyategeezebwa, era ekyo Yahweh ky'ategeeza tekiyinza kukyusibwa.

Tewali ayinza kubawula ku nze; tewali bubi buyinza kumenya kabonero ako; muli bange era muli ba Kitange, kubanga byonna Kitange by'ampa ndibizzaayo eri Katonda tubeere ffenna mu byonna! Era yampa byonna mu ggulu ne mu nsi, okuggyako mu ntebe ye y'obwakabaka ye mukulu okusinga nze!

Nange, Kristo Yesu, ntegeeza ebigambo bino gy'oli, era nze, Yesu Kristo, asala omusango ku balabe bo, era ndigutuukiriza okutuuka ku kasente akasembayo. Omugole wange, okwagala kwange gy'oli tekuzikizibwa, era nkutegedde ekifo, kubanga mu nnyumba ya Kitange mulimu amayumba mangi! N'olwekyo mangu nnyo ojja kumpasibwa emirembe n'emirembe, era bw'otyo bw'ojja okubeeranga nange, era olisembayo okutegeera obunene bw'ekigendererwa kyo mu nze kye watondebwa okutuukiriza!

Kubanga mu nze, Yahweh akuwadde eby'omu maaso n'ekigendererwa, ye kigendererwa ky'emirembe n'emirembe kinaatera okubikkulirwa gy'oli, kubanga njija! Era sijja kugaanyizibwa. Noonya okwabika kwange. Noonya okumasamasa kw'okujja kwange, kubanga kunaatera okumenyeka ku nsi nga ndabika mu bire n'okwogerera waggulu okw'amaanyi, n'eddoboozi lya malayika omukulu, n'ekkondeere lya Katonda, kubanga mbagamba mazima nti ne bamalayika balindirira ekkondeere eryo erinaatera okufuuwa, era bali beetegefu! Mubeere beetegefu gye ndi era mulabe, mulabirire, mulinde nga bwe mwalagirwa; kubanga munaatera okundaba n'ekitiibwa kyange Kitange ky'anjambazza, kubanga bwe ntyo bwe ndibayambaza, kubanga mujja kuba nga nze, era mujja kundaba nga bwe ndi.

Nze Mwana gw'Endiga owa Katonda!

Njija okutuukiriza obusungu ku bwakabaka bwa Sitaani bwonna, era tebajja kuwona.

Nze mulokozi w'ensi!

Nze Kristo Yesu era okuwoolera eggwanga kwange era nze ndisasula!

Nze ndi oyo ajja okufumita omutwe gw'omusota, kubanga Katonda Omuyinza w'ebintu byonna yakitegeeza! Nnaatera okubikkulirwa mu bire eri Omugole wange, gwe ndinyaga.

Nze oyo ajja ng'atudde ku mbalaasi enjeru.

Nze ayitibwa Omwesigwa n'Ow'amazima, era mu butuukirivu ndisalira omusango ne ndwana olutalo.

Njija!
