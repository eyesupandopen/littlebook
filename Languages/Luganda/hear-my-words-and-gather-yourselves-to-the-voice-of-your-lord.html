Ogwekumineebiri 28, 2024

Muwulire ebigambo byange era mwekuŋŋaanye eri eddoboozi lya Mukama wammwe!

Muwulire ebigambo byange era mwekuŋŋaanye eri eddoboozi lya Mukama wammwe, Kabaka, Masiya, era Kabona Asinga Obukulu, ye Yeshua HaMashiach. Lwaki mubuusabuusa ebigambo byange n'entegeka y'ebigenda okubaawo, era ne mu bujulizi bw'ebigambo byange? Kubanga nategeeza lwatu ku Babulooni eky'ekyama n'enkomerero ye eneetera okutuuka:

Okubikkulirwa 17

[17:13] Abo balina endowooza emu, era baligabira ensolo amaanyi gaabwe n'obuyinza bwabwe.

[17:16] Era amayembe ekkumi ge walabye ku nsolo, ago gakyawa omwenzi, ne gamuzikiriza ne gamwambula, ne galya ennyama ye, ne gamwokya n'omuliro.

[17:17] Kubanga Katonda yateeka mu mitima gyabwe okutuukiriza okwagala kwe, ne bakkaanya, era ne bagabira ensolo obwakabaka bwabwe, okutuusa ebigambo bya Katonda lwe birituukirira.

Naye bangi ku mmwe temulina kutegeera kwa byawandiikibwa okutuufu era bangi ku mmwe mukyalina okukkiriza mu muntu wa kamere. Mulina okukkiriza mu busobozi bw'omuntu wa kamere, amaanyi g'omuntu wa kamere, eby'okukola by'omuntu wa kamere, n'obulimba bw'omuntu wa kamere. Temusomangako nti abantu bonna balimba, baana bange? Mumpulire era mutegeere: Tewali kikolebwa omuntu wa kamere ekitali kuza eri ogusota, era ogusota gulifuga ensi okumala akaseera katono, omuzibi bw'aliggibwawo, era ne kaakano ye katonda w'ensi eno. Kino tekyawandiikibwa lwatu mu byawandiikibwa?

Lwaki mukyabuusabuusa ebigambo byange, abaagalwa bange n'abo be nneegomba? Mukkirize ebigambo byange era mutegeere nti omuntu wa kamere talina maanyi, omuntu wa kamere talina busobozi bwennyini bwa kulwanyisa Setaani, eby'okukola by'omuntu wa kamere bikoma mu butaliimu era biweereza bigendererwa bya Setaani, era tewali mazima mu muntu wa kamere n'abakulembeze b'ensi yammwe. Kubanga omuntu wa kamere yayonoonwa okuva ku lubereberye mu nnimiro y'e Edeni olw'omusota era akyama okuva mu lubuto, nga n'okutuusa azaalibwa afudde mu byonoono n'ebibi, era olw'ekigendererwa ekyo, Nze, Yesu Omunazaaleeti, nnabikkulirwa nsobole okumenya emirimu gya Setaani mu abo abakkiriza ekirabo kyange eky'emirembe n'obulokozi.

Mulabe! Abakulembeze b'ensi bonna bakolera wamu mu kyama, era bonna baweereza Setaani. Temulina bakulembeze be mulonda, wabula mubaweebwa okulonda okw'obulimba kubanga temulondangako abo be mulowooza nti mubalonda ku kalulu, wabula babaleetera okubalonda musobole okubasiima era okusiima kwammwe kwe kibi kubanga temuyinza kusalawo okukkiriza ekibi ekitono ne munywerera ku Nze mu kaseera ke kamu, okwo kwe kulimbibwalimbibwa. Mwenenye! Mufulume mu bo era mwawukane! Nze, ye Mukama Yesu Kristo mu lulimi Olungereza ne Yeshua HaMashiach mu Luwebbulaniya, saaletawo kikolebwa kya byabufuzi era tewali mukulembeze wa nsi ajja kuyimuka kubanga saawaayo n'omu, era abo abakkiriza obulala balimbibwa nnyo. Abakulembeze b'ensi bonna bali mu lukwe, era tebajja kumenya nkolagana yaabwe kubanga abakulembeze bammwe bonna baweereza Setaani oba mu kyama, oba mu lwatu. Mukyagala ebigambo ebirungi n'emboozi ennungi? Temulimbibwanga kimala, nga muludde nga mubeera mu bulamu obw'obulimba bwa Setaani okuva lwe mwazaalibwa? Lwaki mukyanoonya abakulembeze b'ensi mu kifo kyange, Nze Kristo Yesu? Kubanga Nze ndi Mukama wammwe, Kabaka, Masiya, era Kabona Asinga Obukulu Yakuwa gwe yateekateeka, era nagula ensi n'obulamu bw'abanunulibwa mu musaayi gwange ogw'okutangirira ogutalimu kibi. Kubanga nga Kabona wammwe Asinga Obukulu, nneewaayo nga Ppaasika yammwe etaliiko bbala era etuukiridde. Era Ndi Mukama wa bakama ne Kabaka wa bakabaka, era njija nga Mpologoma ey'omu kika kya Yuda era ndifugira ku ntebe ya Dawudi ku nsi, era ebigendererwa bya Yakuwa eby'olubeerera eby'obulokozi n'okununulibwa birituukiririzibwa mu Nze, Mpologoma bw'eneegalamira wamu n'omwana gw'endiga mu Bwakabaka bwange obujja n'obufuzi obutaliggwaawo.

Mulabe! Mbagamba ekintu nga tekinnabaawo. Babulooni eky'ekyama, ye Amerika eno eyali edda ng'ettaala eri ensi, eri mu njegere nga n'okutuusa etegekeddwa okusalirwa omusango gw'omuliro. Omusango gwe n'okuzikirizibwa bijja kujja mangu era mu ssaawa emu aliyokebwa n'omuliro, mu ssaawa emu aliyulikamu, mu ssaawa emu engoye ze ziribikkibwako amazzi okuva ebuvanjuba n'ebu запад. Amagye amamyuufu ag'ensolo gamaze dda okuyingira ensalo ze era gamaze okwetegeka nga galindirira ebiragiro, era gajja kutta buli musajja, mukazi, na mwana atali ku lwabwe. Kubanga ensolo n'omunnabbi ow'obulimba abaatera okubikkulibwa bakolera wamu kaakano okuzikiriza Babulooni eky'ekyama, era bali dda mu nsalo zaayo.

Bombi baali bakulembeze ba Babulooni eky'ekyama. Omu alabika nga si y'aliwo era n'afuga akasajja akakadde akatama nga ke kaliwo, ate omulala alabika ng'ayigganyizibwa akasajja ako akakadde, naye abo ab'omu nsi baamulangirira nga Kuulo waabwe era baagala abeere kabaka waabwe, agamba nti ye mulonde kubanga ogusota gwamulonda okusobola okulimba abo ab'omu nsi ey'obusuubize, era ajja kuzimba yeekaalu yaabwe, wadde nga tewali nsonga ya kintu ekyo kubanga Nze Yeshua HaMashiach ndi Ppaasika etuukiridde era mmwe muli yeekaalu ya Katonda omulamu, ye yeekaalu etaazimbisibwa mikono gya bantu wabula n'amaanyi ga Yakuwa olw'ekitiibwa kye era ajja kubagulumiza mu Nze Kristo Yesu Mukama wammwe!

Mulabe! Ensolo zino embi n'omukago gwazo ogw'amayembe ekkumi oguli mu kutegekebwa byamala dda okutegeka okuzikirizibwa kwa Babulooni eky'ekyama. Kubanga byeteeseteese ku nsi yonna, era byetegekedde ku nsi yonna okusuula ensi nga bwe mugimanyi, era mu vvu mulijjamu entegeka yaabyo empya, mu kavuyo mulijjamu entegeka yaabyo eya Setaani era akabonero kaliteekebwawo okusobola okuyingira mu ntegeka yaabwe era abantu ba Katonda bajja kubeera mu ddungu nga mu nnaku za Nuuwa era batono ddala be balisigalawo. Kubanga ensolo n'omunnabbi ow'obulimba balirwana n'abatukuvu ne babawangula. Abo be bajja okuva mu kibonyoobonyo ekinene, era nga bayoza ebyambalo byabwe, ne babiyeruza mu musaayi gw'Omwana gw'endiga. Kyebava babeera mu maaso g'entebe ya Katonda, ne bamuweereza emisana n'ekiro mu Yeekaalu ye: era oyo atuula ku Ntebe alibeera mu bo. Tebaliddamu kulumwa njala, newaakubadde ennyonta, newaakubadde enjuba okubakolako, newaakubadde ebbugumu lyonna. Kubanga Omwana gw'endiga, ali mu makkati g'entebe, alibaliisa, era alibakulembera eri enzizi ez'amazzi amalamu: era Katonda alisangula amaziga gonna mu maaso gaabwe.

Mulabe! Omulabe ajja n'entegeka y'ebigenda okubaawo bw'eti: Omuzibi aliziyiza okutuusa lwe nkuŋŋaanya abeesigwa mu Kkanisa yange okunsisinkana mu bire ku kulabika kwange eri Ekkanisa yange, ye Mugole wange. Wadde nga bangi ku mmwe mwegaana ekyawandiikibwa ekiraga lwatu ku kikolwa kino, kijja kubaawo, era kijja kumanyibwa ku nsi yonna. Tewali kunyagulwa kwa kyama, kubanga ago ge magambo g'abannabbi ab'obulimba ab'Abaziunisiti, era okwabika kwange tekulibaako kubuusabuusa ku nsi yonna. Baligezaako okubikka ku kugenda kwammwe n'olutalo n'okulumwa okw'obulimba okw'ebitonde ebiva mu bbanga, naye tewali bitonde ebyo wabula abagwa abeeyisa bwe batyo. Mugole wange bw'alitwalibwa mu ggulu n'omuzibi n'aggibwawo, okuzikirizibwa okw'amangu kuligwa amangu ku nsi yonna n'amawanga gonna agaali geefuula Amakristaayo gajja kuggwaawo nga bwe gali kaakano, naye walisigalawo abantu abawambiddwa era abasaliddwako. Babulooni eky'ekyama y'esooka okugwa era amangu ddala mu kiseera kye kimu n'okulumba kwabwe okw'ekibwatukira, naye tajja kuddamu kuyimuka nate, kubanga Yakuwa alangiridde omusango ogw'emirundi ebiri ku ye.

Kubanga Babulooni eky'ekyama yakulembera amawanga mu kibi ekinene n'obulimba ng'eyita mu bulogo bwayo n'obusamize, nga n'okusingira ddala okusaakiriza kwayo okw'emizannyo n'olw'obusamize bwayo obubi obwafuulibwa eddagala ate nga mu butuufu butwa obutta. Kale bw'etyo enkomerero ye erijja mangu era awatali kuwonyezebwa. Era walisigalawo matongo, wadde nga walisigalawo abasigaddewo abalirokolebwa, kubanga buli alisaba erinnya lya Mukama era n'atayagala bulamu bwe okutuusa ku nkomerero alirokolebwa.

Kale mutegeere ebiseera mwe muli era mutegeere entegeka y'ebigenda okubaawo era mutegeere nti tewali kiyinza kukyusa bintu bino, kubanga Yakuwa ky'alangiridde tewali ayinza kukikyusa!

Mulabe! Nze Yeshua HaMashiach era ntumye Ebigambo bino ng'ebiyita mu muweereza wange, era okuyita mu muweereza wange Ebigambo bino biweerezeddwa.

Ndirabika amangu ddala, mweteeseteese Mugole wange?
