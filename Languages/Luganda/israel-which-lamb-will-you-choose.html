Mujulirwa Omubiri: Isiraeri
Ekikaawa Ekirimu Obuwoomu
Novema 13, 2024

Ggwe Isiraeri! Mwana gw'endiga ki gw'ogenda okulondawo?

Muwulire ebigambo bya Yakuwa NZE NDI ow'amaanyi era muddukire ku ddoboozi ly'ebigambo byange mulyoke mufune amagezi era n'omuntu ow'amagezi mu mmwe alyoke yeeyongere obukabakaba mu magezi.

Laba! Nzize okubategeeza ebinaabangawo mulyoke mumanye obukakafu bw'ebigambo byange era mulyoke mutuuke ku bulokozi bwammwe obujjuvu mu Yeshua HaMashiach, oyo ddala ye Messiya wammwe ow'amazima, Kabaka, Mukama wammwe, era Kabona Asinga Obukulu emirembe gyonna ng'engeri ya Merukizeddeeki bw'eri.

Kyenvudde ndagira nti mumuwulire n'obuntu bwammwe bwonna era mukomole emitima gyammwe mu buwulize! Kubanga ye Yeshua HaMashiach, ye Yesu Kristo mu lulimi Olungereza, ataaweebwayo kulokola Isiraeri yokka, naye n'okulokola ekika kyonna ekya Adamu kubanga bonna abalikoowoola erinnya lya Mukama okutuusa ku nkomerero balilokolebwa, k'abe Muisiraeri oba ow'amawanga, era Yeshua HaMashiach alirekawo abasigaddewo abalamu mu Isiraeri, nga bava ne mu kika kya Adamu abaliyita mu bufuzi bwe obw'emyaka olukumi.

Kale bwe kityo, nnateekerateekera ekika kya Adamu ekyo kye kyali tekisobola kweteekereteekera mu bulokozi bwakyo, kubanga ekika kya Adamu kyandisobodde kufuna okununulibwa okujjuvu mu musaayi gw'omwana gw'endiga gwokka. Kubanga nze, Yakuwa, NZE NDI ow'amaanyi, nze nnateekerateekera Okuyitako kwammwe okutuukiridde, omulundi gumu, kaakano, n'emirembe gyonna, era ekiweebwayo kye kyakkirizibwa emirembe gyonna, kyekiva mulema okwetaaga ekiweebwayo ekirala, ggwe Isiraeri.

Nga bwe kyawandiikibwa mu Abaebbulaniya, nate era nkigamba kaakano nti: Kubanga oba ng'omusaayi gw'ennume z'ente n'embuzi, n'evvu ly'ente ennume ento erimansibwa ku abo abatali balongoofu, litukuza okutuusa okulongoosa omubiri: Omusayi gwa Kristo gwasinga nnyo, eyali yeewaayo yekka eri Katonda ng'ayita mu Mwoyo ogw'emirembe, nga talina bbala, okunaaza omwoyo gwammwe okuva mu bikolwa ebifu, mulyoke muweereze Katonda omulamu? Kale nkuwa amagezi, ggwe Isiraeri, okkirize ssaddaaka yange kubanga tewakyaliyo ssaddaaka ndala ya kibi!

Kale tegeera, Isiraeri, nti tekisoboka muntu yenna kusemberera Katonda Ataggwaawo ow'Obutuukirivu ng'ayita mu Musa nate, kubanga nfuulidde Yeshua HaMashiach okuba ekkubo, amazima n'obulamu, omuntu yenna kyatayinza kujja eri Yakuwa wabula ng'ayita mu Yeshua HaMashiach!

Era nnagira obuwulize eri eddoboozi lye, nga liyita ne mu Tora, eddoboozi lya Musa lyennyini mwe likaabira okutuusa leero ng'omujulirwa okubalumiriza ne mu ngeri zammwe ez'obutamanya n'obutakkiriza, mu kifo ky'obuwulize mmwe mu butamanya munoonya okuzzaawo yeekaalu eyakolebwa omuntu, nga mukyamizibwa Esawu! Kubanga mwagaana yeekaalu eyazimbibwa Omutonzi wammwe, yeekaalu etaazimbibwa na mikono, yeekaalu yennyini eyakolebwa Yakuwa kennyini eri Isiraeri wa Katonda, Yesu Kristo gw'ali ejjinja essonderwako erikulu. Kale mulekere awo emirimu gyammwe era muwummulire mu mirimu gya Messiya wammwe ow'amazima nange naabawa Ssabbiiti.

Kale nkulagira, ggwe Isiraeri, era nze nnawa ebigambo ebyo Musa bye yayogera ne bakitammwe ku Sinai, ebigambo ebyo bye ndagira muwulirize bulijjo n'emirembe gyonna ne mu butawulize bwammwe obw'obukakanyavu. Kale Musa n'agamba olw'obuwulize bwa bonna: Mukama Katonda wammwe alibayimusiza Nnabbi ali nga nze, okuva mu mmwe, mu baganda bammwe: oyo ye munaawuliranga!

So ng'ate Musa ye yali omulamuzi w'endagaano esooka (eyawandiikibwa olunwe lwange ku Sinai) eyaweebwa okusalira omusango ekibi mu mubiri, endagaano eyo kaakano eyaka mu maaso ga Yesu Kristo, ye Yeshua HaMashiach ye Mukama wammwe era Messiya ow'amazima! Naye amateeka ga Musa kye kinyiriri ky'ebintu eby'okujja, ebintu ebyo ebyatuukirizibwa mu Kristo Yesu era mmufudde omwoyo oguwa obulamu ate Yesu Omunazaaleeti tamanyiddwa nate mu mubiri!

Era nga bwe kyatuukirizibwa omukono gwange ogw'amaanyi, n'olunwe lwa YHVH NZE NDI ow'amaanyi, bwe nnawandiika amateeka gange ku bipande eby'amayinja ku Sinai we nnawa Musa amateeka gange Isiraeri alyoke abeere omulamu mu maaso gange. Naye, eky'aweebwa Isiraeri ku Sinai kyali kusinga kuba kusalirwa musango gwa kibi mu mubiri, Isiraeri alyoke abe n'omusomesa okumuleeta eri Messiya we ow'amazima, ye Yeshua HaMashiach gwe nnatuma olw'obulokozi bwammwe obujjuvu.

Era bwe kityo, amateeka ga Musa malungi, matuufu, matuukiridde, era matukuvu era omuntu yenna bw'akolima amateeka ga Musa, asalirwe omusango mu bukambwe obujjuvu obulagirwa wansi w'ekikolimo n'ekibonerezo eky'amateeka ga Musa. Naye ekyo ekirungi, ekituufu, ekituukiridde era ekitukuvu kyaleeta okufa kw'abo bonna abaasalirwa omusango olw'ekibi mu mubiri, kubanga awali amateeka, ekibi tekyogerwako, amateeka ga Musa kyegava gaweebwa okulaga n'okusalira omusango ekibi mu mubiri.

Naye lwa mwoyo gwa mateeka so si lwa nnukuta omuntu lw'afunira okusaasira mu kwagala kwange kwe nnina gye bali, kubanga ennukuta y'amateeka ereeta obukambwe (Temusomanga nti njagala kusaasira okusinga ssaddaaka?) era mu kwagala kwange eri abaana ba Isiraeri be ŋŋenderera okununula bwe munaaba bawulize eri Yeshua HaMashiach, nnalagira ssaddaaka kubanga awatali kuyiwa musaayi tewaba kusonyiyibwa bibi. Era omukono gwa Musa ogulabirira gwe gwakuuma abantu bange okutuusa Kristo, newakubadde nga bangi mu mmwe mukyukidde bakatonda ab'obulimba era mwagala ebiyitikondo byammwe ebiddugavu, ebintu ebyo bya muzizo nnyo mu maaso gange, ggwe Isiraeri, kyemuvudde mubivaako amangu ago!

Kale tegeera obusungu bwange ku ggwe, ggwe Isiraeri, kubanga NZE YHVH, NZE NDI ow'amaanyi, ye Katonda Ayinza Byonna ow'emirembe gyonna ow'Obutuukirivu, atakoowa, so tewali kintu kizibu nnyo eri omukono gwange ogw'amaanyi okukituukiriza. Era nze El Shaddai, Katonda Ayinza Byonna, nze nnatonda obutonde mu magezi gange ag'emirembe, n'amaanyi amakulu ag'omwoyo gwange byonna biwaniriddwa wamu mu buwulize eri eddoboozi lyange.

NZE Elohim wammwe, enkumi n'enkumi zeetoolodde entebe yange ey'obwakabaka, eggye lya bamalayika ery'amaanyi ery'emyoyo egiweereza, abaminsani abo nga bwe lulimi lw'omuliro olusinga mu magezi era abatondebwa mu ngeri etaliiko kamogo era ennungi ennyo nga bwe nnabatonda mu kwagala kwange gye bali! Era mu kwagala kwabwe gye ndi, bawulize nnyo mu byannagamba eby'emirembe era mpadde Yeshua HaMashiach obufuzi ku byonna.

Naye, nze YHVH NZE NDI ow'amaanyi, ye oyo eyeetooloddwa eggye lya bamalayika eritaliiko muwendo, SEETAAGA MUWI WA MAGEZI mu magezi gange ag'emirembe, era ku butonde bwange nze nfaayo era nkola ebintu BYONNA olw'okuteesa okw'okwagala kwange, era ku basajja abasinga amagezi mu byonna eby'emirembe gyonna tewali asinga amagezi NZE NDI ow'amaanyi! Kale wulira eddoboozi lyange era osirise ennimi zo bw'onoosalawo okukkiriza obulamu obutaggwaawo okusinga ennimi z'omuliro ezitazikira ez'okubonerezebwa, ggwe Isiraeri kubanga ekyo kye kirondwa buli muntu ky'alina okukola.

Era mu magezi gange ag'emirembe nnatuma Yesu Omunazaaleeti e Isiraeri, ne Buyudaaya bwe yali efugibwa Pontiyo Piraato, ne bakabona abakulu ne Sanhedrin bwe baamulumiriza mu ngeri eteri ya bwenkanyikivu era omwana gw'endiga gwa Katonda ne guttibwa era ne gukomererwa emikono emibi, omubiri gw'ekibi ogusiba Adamu gulyoke guzikirizibwe mu abo bonna abawulize ababeerawo olw'okukkiriza, kubanga omutuukirivu anaabanga mulamu lwa kukkiriza! Era NZE ye, ye El Shaddai (Katonda Ayinza Byonna) eyateeka ebintu byonna mu ggulu ne ku nsi mu mikono gya Kristo Yesu egy'amaanyi, ye Yeshua HaMashiach era ye Mpologoma ey'Ekika kya Yuda gye NDI ntuma okusalira omusango n'okulwana n'okulokola abeesigwa n'abawulize. Kyemuvudde muwulira ebigambo byange mu buwulize eri Messiya wammwe ow'amazima, kubanga tewali mulala!

Kale tegeera obusungu bwange ku ggwe, ggwe Isiraeri, nti mu buzibe bwo obw'ekitundu bwe wasikira okuva eri bakitaawo ab'emitima emikakanyavu, eddoboozi lyabwe erya Talmud lye mussaamu ekitiibwa okusinga Elohim wammwe. Yee, mulina obulombolombo bw'abo be muyita abasajja ab'amagezi n'ab'amagezi mu kitiibwa ekinene okusinga ebigambo bya NZE NDI ow'amaanyi, ye Mutonzi ow'emirembe gyonna n'olutatadde ow'ebyo byonna ebyatondebwa! Kubanga byonna byatondebwa YHVH, ebiri mu ggulu, n'ebiri ku nsi, ebintu ebirabika n'ebitalabika: oba ntebe za bwakabaka oba bufuzi, oba bukulu, oba buyinza, byonna byatondebwa nze, era ku lwange, era NZE NDI mu maaso ga byonna, era mu YHVH byonna mwe biri.

Naye, olowooza nti oli muwi w'amagezi wange era mu busirusiru weeyita abantu bange abalonde ate ng'oganye ebigambo byange mu butakkiriza obutawulize? Ggwe Isiraeri okyuse n'odda ku bifaananyi ebikasa eby'omutima n'ebigambo by'obulimba ebya Esawu, amagezi ge ge musinga okwagala okusinga Katonda ow'emirembe ow'Obutuukirivu, ye Elohim wammwe! Era mwesittala mu buzibe obw'ekitundu kubanga mu Musa emitima gyammwe gibikkiddwako. Naye, omutima gwammwe bwe gulikyukira Messiya wammwe ow'amazima, ekibikka kiriggibwawo, era mu kifo ky'obuzibe obw'ekitundu muliraba!

Ggwe ani, ggwe Isiraeri, eyeeyimirizaawo okuba omuwi w'amagezi owa Yakuwa? Naye mu kifo ky'ebigambo bya Yakuwa ebyaweebwa mu maaso ga Yeshua HaMashiach, mukkirizza obulimba obwayisibwa mu bulombolombo bw'abakadde bammwe abaali mu busibe bwa Esawu, ebiwandiiko byabwe bye mweyasibira Esawu mu ngeri y'emu, era eri Esawu muli basibe! Era nnawandiika butereevu Musa okuwa bakitammwe ku Sinai nti: Ekintu kyonna kye mbalagira, mukikwate mukikole: temugattangako, so temukendeezangako. Era nate nakibawandiisa mu magezi ga Sulemaani, amagezi ago agayimirira emirembe gyonna: Buli kigambo kya Elohim kirongoofu: ye ngabo eri abo abamwesiga. Tewali ky'ogatta ku bigambo bye, aleme okukunenya, n'obeera mulimba.

Laba, ggwe Isirayiri, singa wabikkirizza emitima gyo mu kugondera Ebigambo ebyaweebwa okukuwa obulokozi n'essanyu eritali ggwawo, singa waddamu okuwuliriza Ebigambo by'Omutonzi wo! Era NZE oyo ayinza okukuzzaawo n'okukuleeta mu kisibo eky'olubeerera ekiri wansi w'omukono ogw'amaanyi gw'Omusumba wange Omukulu gwe nawa byonna! Ye Yeshua HaMashiach, asumba Isirayiri ow'amazima owa Katonda mu ngeri etuukiridde mu kwagala kwe okugondera eddoboozi lyange, nga kye kyokulabirako kyennyini kye munyooma. Kubanga Yeshua HaMashiach ye muntu yekka eyagondera ebiseera byonna mu bujjuvu okwagala kwa YHVH, NZE NDI ow'amaanyi era gwe nsiimira ennaku zonna, kyennava mmufuula omwoyo oguwa obulamu, kale mumuwulire oyo gwe nnatuma ku lwammwe kubanga tewali mulala.

Era mukakanyazizza emitima gyammwe nga Jjinja ly'Omusingi erisibiddwa Esinagogi ya Setaani kubanga mwagaana ebigambo by'okuteesa kwange ebyaweebwa mu maaso ga Kristo Yesu, gwe mwagaana mu butamanya bwammwe, bwe mutyo ne mwegaana essuubi lyammwe lyokka ery'okuzzibwawo n'obulokozi obw'olubeerera emirembe gyonna. N'olwekyo muyimiridde nga muzibe b'amaaso, nga muli bwereere era nga muswad_de mu kibi kyammwe, nga mulowooza mu butamanya nti mufuuse abawi b'amagezi eri YHVH, NZE NDI ow'amaanyi!

N'olwekyo mbawa okutegeera mu kuziba kwammwe okw'ekitundu nti mwesittadde ku jjinja lyange enkulu ery'oku nsonda abazimbi bammwe lye baagaana, ddala ejjinja lyange ery'okwesittaza. Kubanga Yeshua HaMashiach lye jjinja enkulu ery'oku nsonda, ddala omusingi gw'amazima, ye Yesu Omunazaaleeti gwe mwagaana mu butawulize obw'omugegerero wadde nga nnabalonda okuba abantu bange ab'enjawulo era omutima gwange ne gwatiika.

Wulira, wulira, ggwe Isirayiri, otegeere nti NZE ndi oyo ayoola amateeka gange ag'olubeerera, okwo kwe kulaga okwagala kwange okw'olubeerera mu nteekateeka yange ey'olubeerera ey'okununula gye nnakola oluvannyuma lw'okuteesa n'okwagala kwange okw'olubeerera era okutuukiridde ku bipande by'amayinja olw'okugondera kwa Isirayiri e Sinayi! N'olwekyo, NZE ndi oyo ajja okuwandiika amateeka gange ku bipande eby'omubiri eby'emitima gyammwe kubanga mu kwagala kwange eri mmwe, akatikitiki k'amaanyi gange kasobola okwoola amateeka gange ku bipande byammwe eby'amayinja era n'ekisa n'okusaasira kwange ebiri mu maaso ga Yeshua HaMashiach nja kumenyaamenya ebibajjo byammwe eby'amayinja ne mbitabula n'ebbumba ly'omubumbi mulyoke mutondwe buto emirembe gyonna mu butuukirivu n'obutukuvu obw'amazima nga bwe nnalayirira mu ndagaano zange eri bajjajjammwe nti bwe ntyo bwe nja okukola.

N'olwekyo, ggwe Isirayiri, nkulagira ogondere eddoboozi lya Yeshua HaMashiach kubanga tewali bulokozi mu linnya lya mulala lyonna erisukka erya Yesu Kristo Omutuukirivu, gwe muyita mu butamanya ekifaananyi eky'ab'amawanga kubanga muli mu busibe bwa Esawu!

Naye nga bwe kyawandiikibwa era bwe kyatuukirira: Okuva ku Ibulayimu okutuuka ku Isaaka, Yakobo ne Esawu bwe baamuzaalibwako, omukono gwa Yakobo gwasooka kukwata kisinziiro kya Esawu. Kubanga Esawu ye nkomerero y'ensi, ate Yakobo ye ntandikwa y'egiddirira. Omukono gw'omuntu guli wakati w'ekisinziiro n'omukono. Naye kaakano nja kutandika okwawula Esawu ku Yakobo kubanga Esawu ye Ssinagogi ya Setaani abali omuzinzi omubi oguyingiridde ensi y'ekisuubizo omulundi omulala, era Isirayiri addamu okujjula Abakanani.

Era nga bwe kyawandiikibwa, bwe kityo bwe kirikolebwa, bw'ayogera NZE NDI ow'amaanyi: Kubanga, laba, olunaku lujja, oluliba ng'ekyokero ekyaka, n'abo bonna ab'amalala, weewaawo, n'abo bonna abakola ebibi baliba bisasiro: n'olunaku olujja lulibookya, bw'ayogera Mukama ow'eggye, ne lutalekawo mmizi newakubadde ettabi. Naye mmwe abatya Erinnya lyange, Omwana w'obutuukirivu alibavaakira ng'alina okuwonya mu biwaawaatiro bye, era mulifuluma ne mukula ng'ennyana ez'omu kisibo.

Era nga Yeshua HaMashiach akuŋŋaanya abeesigwa mu kkanisa, ye mubiri gwe, n'omugole we, kaakano nja kukozesa Yeshua HaMashiach okwawula okw'enkomereredde wakati wa Esawu ne Yakobo, era Yakobo alirokolebwa wadde nga ayita mu bizibu bya ssabbiiti ya Danyeri ey'amakumi musanvu. N'olwekyo, wewale okubaawo omwenzi, oba omuntu atali mutukuvu nga Esawu, eyatunda obusika bwe olw'ekiccope ky'ennyama. Kubanga mumanyi nga bwe yamala n'ayagala okusikira omukisa, n'agaanibwa: kubanga teyalina bbanga lya kwenenya, wadde nga yanoonya omukisa ogwo n'amaziga.

Era n'olwekyo obuntu buli wakati w'omukono gwa Yakobo n'ekisinziiro kya Esawu, wabula nga Esawu anoonya kugumya Yakobo ekiwundu eky'okumutwe eky'enkomerero, kye sitaaganye. N'olwekyo, abantu bonna ab'oku nsi abalisigala mu kibonyoobonyo kya Yakobo okugumira omuliro ogulongoosa baliba ba Yakobo okuyita mu Yesu Omunazaaleeti, be baana b'obusika, oba baligaanibwa wamu ne Esawu eyatunda obusika bwe olw'akamere akatono. N'olwekyo, omuntu ow'obutonde alikoowoola erinnya lya Yesu Omunazaaleeti okutuusa ku nkomerero ye alyoke azuukizibwe mu Isirayiri wa Katonda oba naye alitunda obusika bwe obw'olubeerera olw'akamere akatono ak'omu mukono gwa Balaba, kubanga Balaba ye alikulembera Esinagogi ya Setaani era ye mwana gw'ogusota.

Era bwe kityo omutego bwe gulitegebwa mu nsi okusiba Yakobo emirembe gyonna era Balaba aleeta obulimba bungi n'obufuzi bwe era ebibi bingi by'alituukiriza ku lukalu ne ku nnyanja. Naye, bwe kiriba nti ng'omuliro gw'entalo gukutte era gwaka, Balaba aligolokoka n'okweyita nti azuukidde okutuukiriza ebigendererwa bya Katonda era eby'obuvumi n'ebibi bingi by'alirimba. Era alireeta ettabi lye ery'omuzeyituuni era alyeyita Nuuwa, n'okuba omulamu okulokola Ensi okuva ku musango ogwagendererwa abantu ababi.

Kubanga ensolo eva mu nsi era ye nnabbi waabwe ow'obulimba erireeta ekigonjoolo kyayo kubanga mwana gwa ndiga alina amayembe abiri ayogera ng'ogusota. Era obufuzi bwe buliba bwa mitawaana ebiri kubanga alinoonya okugatta abantu bonna wansi w'obufuzi bw'ogusota, ye Setaani omukadde yennyini. Kubanga Balaba tajja kufuga yekka wadde ng'alyeyita Katonda naye aleeta endagaano ey'okwawukana ng'amayembe abiri galifuga ennyanja n'ensi, era ku ababiri omu lye yyembe ettono lya Danyeri. Naye nasuubiza nti Isirayiri taliddamu kwawulwamu era ku nsi ze ebbiri tewalibaawo kigonjoolo naye Yeshua HaMashiach aligatta Isirayiri mu bujjuvu era emirembe gyonna.

(Naye bwe kiriba, baagalwa bange, n'olwekyo tegeera kye nja okutuukiriza okuyita mu Yeshua HaMashiach: Nja kuggya abaana ba Isirayiri mu mawanga, gye baagenda, ne mbakuŋŋaanyiza enjuyi zonna, ne mbazza mu nsi yaabwe. Era nja kubafuula eggwanga limu mu nsi, ku nsozi za Isirayiri, ne kabaka omu aliba kabaka waabwe bonna: so tebaliba nate mawanga abiri, so tebalyawulwamu nate oluvannyuma lwa leero okuba obwakabaka obubiri. So tebalyonoonebwa nate n'ebifaananyi byabwe, newakubadde n'emizizo gyabwe, newakubadde n'okwonoona kwabwe kwonna: naye nja kubalokola mu bifo byabwe byonna mwe bayonoonera, ne mbalongoosa: bwe batyo baliba bantu bange, nange naabanga Katonda waabwe.)

Omulala ye nsolo eva mu nnyanja eyawandiikibwako nti: Kubanga teyeegomba bakazi, so tassa kitiibwa mu katonda yenna: kubanga alyegulumiza okusinga byonna. Kaakano nkubuuza olabe oba omusajja alumwa okuzaala? Naye mukyala we alabika ng'omukazi alina abaana, wadde nga si baana be. Era ensolo eva mu nnyanja erigolokoka nga egattiddwa mu kigendererwa n'ensolo eva mu nsi era beefuula abalabe abalabika nga baawukanye mu ndowooza ne mu ddiini.

Naye balimba era beefuula era kitaabwe ye gusota gwe gumu eyakola ensolo zino ebbiri ez'obubi olw'ebigendererwa bye ebibi, okuzikiriza Adamu alyoke abbe ensi emirembe gyonna okuba eyiye era tekisoboka nti atuukirize enteekateeka ze ez'obubi kubanga ensi ya Mukama n'okujjula kwayo, bw'atyo bw'alangirira YHVH, NZE NDI ow'amaanyi!

Kubanga Balaba aleeta ensi ebbiri ezirigattibwa wansi w'ogusota era emirembe gy'obulimba gye baliba nagyo nga basuubiza obulamu obutaggwaawo n'obulamu obulungi n'emirembe singa mukkiriza okuteekebwako akabonero kaabwe ne mubegattako nga batabula ekyuma n'ebbumba naye tebirikwatagana. Tegeera, mmwe mwenna abalowooza okwegatta emirembe gyonna ku abo abaagwa okuyita mu kabonero kaabwe nti balirimba ebigambo bingi nga bagamba nti ddagala lya kyamagero eririwa obulamu n'obulamu obulungi era lya kitalo mu nkola yaalyo!

Naye bonna abakkiriza obutwa bwaabwe balyefuula emirembe gyonna era ne bakyuka mu ngeri eteriimu kuddamu. Kubanga okuvuma ekifaananyi kya YHVH Adamu kye yatondwamu kwe kwegatta ku bibi by'abo abaagwa abeefuula bakatonda so si bo, era bonna abakola ekyo tebalina kusonyiyibwa era tebalina mirembe era mu nnyanja ey'omuliro etazikira gye baligenda emirembe gyonna kubanga bwe batyo bwe baliba balondeddewo.

Naye, ng'abasatu abatali batukuvu bwe bagolokoka, n'abajulizi bange ababiri nabo baligolokoka, era baliragula ennaku 1,260 nga bambadde ebibukutu era obujulizi bungi bwe baliwa. Balijja e Isirayiri ne bategeeza ku Katonda Ataggwaawo ow'Obutuukirivu era balitegeeza n'amaanyi ku Yeshua HaMashiach, ye Yesu Omunazaaleeti, ataliiko muntu yenna ayinza kujja eri El Shaddai!
