Mujje Byonna eri Mukama Wammwe ow'Emirembe n'Emirembe!

Muwulire, muwulire! Muwulire eddoboozi lya Mukama wammwe era muwulire ebigambo bya Yeshua HaMashiach, era mukotamye emitima gyammwe mu buwulize eri okwagala kwa Mukama wammwe, era mukkirize ebigambo byange okubudaabuda emyoyo gyammwe. Noolwekyo mbabuuza ekibuuzo? Mumpulikira? Omwana w'Omuntu bw'alijja, nja kusanga okukkiriza ku nsi? Mazima, mazima, nja kusanga okukkiriza mu beesigwa bange be nja okuggyawo ne mbatwala mu bulamu obw'omu ggulu olw'obwesigwa bwabwe, naye abasinga obungi ku nsi tebalina kukkiriza. Noolwekyo nneeyongera okubalabula, baagalwa bange, okuva mu bibi by'aboonoonyi, n'okuva mu makubo gaabwe ne mweyawula, muleme okufuna ku bibonerezo byabwe.

Naye, bw'ekityo bwe kiri, nti abantu bangi bannyinira kumpi n'emimwa gyabwe mu bunnanfuusi so ng'emitima gyabwe giri wala nange. Ku lunaku olwo bangi balingamba nti Mukama Mukama, nange ndibagamba nti, Muve we ndi, saali mbamanyi! Mutegeere nti tekimala kwatula nti ndi Mukama okuva mu mutima ogutalina kukkiriza, naye abo abamfuula Mukama ow'amazima beebo abeenenya ne baleka ebibi byabwe era abanoonya okubeera abatuukirivu mu maaso gange. Temumanyi nti nze, Yesu Omunazaaleeti, singa nnyinza okulonda, nandisinze okulonda omutima gwammwe gwonna mu kwagala n'okweyama okusinga ssaddaaka z'emimwa gyammwe? Yee, njagala kusaasira so si ssaddaaka, kubanga mu kitundu okwagala kwange kulabikira mu kusaasira kwange, okuziyiza obusungu obubagwanidde, ndowooza okwagala kwa Yahweh, Katonda omunene ow'emirembe gyonna! Naye ebigambo byange eby'okulabula biwulirwa abo abanjagala era abaanukulidde mu kwenenya okwagala kwange gye bali nga bwe nneebikkulidde gye bali olw'obulokozi bwabwe. Bw'ekityo bwe kiri, okwagala kwange kukkirizibwa mu abo abalina emitima emigonvu eri eddoboozi lyange, era, mazima, eri abo bonna abanjagala era be nnalagidde okuyigira ku nze, be nnyini be nkulembera mu makubo gange ag'obulokozi.

Lwaki muleka emitima gyammwe okubatyemula, mmwe baana bange abato? Abatume bange n'abannabbi tebaalabula ku lunaku luno okuva ku lubereberye lw'emirembe? Abannabbi ab'edda tebaalagula ku wiiki ya Danyeri ey'amakumi musanvu okujja n'omusango omunene ku nsi? N'olwekyo, mutegeere, muli mu nnaku ezikulembera ettemu lya Yakobo okubalukawo mu nsi yonna, era ku nnaku zino wabaddewo ebizibu bingi, obubonero, eby'amagero, ebyewuunyo, n'emisango egyalangirirwa era kati giteereddwa, kale lwaki mwewuunya? Zino ze nkomerero z'okulabula ku mutendera omutono gw'ekyo ekigenda okujja ku nsi yonna, abeesigwa bange nga bamaze okugenda gye ndi mu ggulu okuva ku nsi, nga mbakuŋŋaanyizza ng'ennyana ez'omu kisibo, era balifuna obulamu obutakoma n'okutuukirizibwa nga bayimiridde mu ssa ery'olubereberye nga bazaaliddwa omulundi ogw'okubiri mu butayuza, gye bataliibeerako kuzikirira, wadde okubonyaabonyezebwa, wadde okulwala, naye baliba n'essanyu erituukiridde n'emirembe egiwera ne Yahweh, era nange Yeshua HaMashiach, era ndibawa empeera ennene n'obusika, kubanga empeera yaabwe ngirina mu mukono gwange ogw'amaanyi.

Bangi ku beesigwa bange bazimbulukusiddwa enjigiriza ez'obulimba ez'enjiri y'obugagga ebuulirwa mu bukyamu abannabbi ab'obulimba n'abasomesa abalimbalimba abantu bange, nga babagamba nti ebizibu tebiribatuukako ebbanga lyonna nga balowooza ebirungi. Oluzzi luno olutaliimu mazzi lubuulira Yesu omulala era tebammanyi, nze Yeshua HaMashiach, era nange saabatumanga. Mugeragerenganyenga bulijjo ebigambo by'abo abeeyita nti boogera ku lwange n'ebyawandiikibwa, era gye biwakana n'ebyawandiikibwa we boogerera okuva mu mitima gyabwe ku lw'amagezi gaabwe ag'omululu.

Mazima, mazima, waliwo abaweereza ab'obulimba bangi abasize obulimba mu bibiina byange, era enjiri ey'obulimba ey'obugagga etwala mu kkubo eggazi ery'okuzikirira, omulyango gwa ggeyeena mwe guliggulirwa bannalukalala eri abo abagoberera okutuuka ku nnyanja eyaka omuliro ogwaka n'ennimi ezisinga okwokya eza bbulu wa kobalti eyategekerwa abagwa n'abo ababagoberera, kale temugwa mu bulimba bwabwe! Soogera ku bannabbi bange abeesigwa abayinza okunyooma ekigambo ku lwange, newankubadde abo abasobya mu bwesimbu ne beenenya. Mutegeere nti tewali ku bannabbi bange alagula mu bujjuvu, naye bonna abannabbi bange balagula mu bitundu bitundu era tebayinza kuwakanya byawandiikibwa bwe boogera amazima ku lwange, Kristo Yesu, kubanga nze kigambo kya Katonda ekyefuula omubiri n'abeera mu mmwe, newankubadde nga ku mubiri simanyiddwa nate.

Mmwe baana bange abato, temulimbibwanga kubanga mu nsi eno mulimu okuyigganyizibwa n'okulumizibwa, naddala kati ku nkomerero y'obufuzi bwa Setaani ng'aboonoonyi bajja mu musango, era nga beetegekera okukola kye bakkirizibwa okutuukiriza mu musango gwabwe, kubanga n'abalabe ba Yahweh bawulira eddoboozi lye. Temusomanga mu Abaruumi we nnalagira Pawulo mu makubo gange? Ani alitwawula ku kwagala kwa Kristo? Okuyigganyizibwa, oba okulumizibwa, oba okuyigganyizibwa, oba enjala, oba obwereere, oba akabi, oba ekitala? (Nga bwe kyawandiikibwa nti, ku lwammwe tutibwa olunaku lwonna, tubalibwa ng'endiga ez'okuttibwa.) Nedda, mu bino byonna tusinga abawanguzi, ku bw'oyo eyatwagala. Kubanga nzikiriziddwa nti, newankubadde okufa, newankubadde obulamu, newankubadde bamalayika, newankubadde abakulu, newankubadde amaanyi, newankubadde eby'omu kiseera kino, newankubadde ebigenda okujja, newankubadde obugulumivu, newankubadde obuziba, newankubadde ekitonde ekirala kyonna, tekiyinza kutwawula ku kwagala kwa Katonda, okuli mu Kristo Yesu Mukama waffe.

N'olwekyo abalonde bange babonyeebonye nnyo olw'erinnya lyange, era wabaddewo ekibinja kinene ky'abajulizi mu mirembe gyange egy'ekisa abawaayo obulamu bwabwe mu kuweereza mu kusaasaanya enjiri y'obulokozi, ye nnyini enjiri eyalokola emyoyo gyammwe olw'okukkiriza mu Yesu Omunazaaleeti kubanga mu kitundu omulimu gw'abajulizi bange gwe gwasaasaanya enjiri y'obulokozi ku nsi. Naye, bangi ku mmwe bazimbulukusiddwa ekizimbulukusa eky'enjigiriza ez'obulimba ez'enjawulo ezigamba nti kirangirire kikubeerewo, ezaasaasaanidde Ekkanisa yange ey'e Lawodikiya.

Enjigiriza zino embi teziva gye ndi era ebirowoozo byammwe tebituukiriza kintu kyonna ebweru wammwe okuggyako nga mwogera ebigambo byange ku mimwa gy'okukkiriza bwe mpeebwa obubonero, eby'amagero, n'ebyewuunyo okukolebwa emikono gyammwe. N’olwekyo, temusobola kulowooza nti muliva mu mbeera enzibu n’okuyigganyizibwa, era abayite bange, abalonde, n’abeesigwa tebasonyiwe bibi. Wa mu Kigambo kya Katonda we kigamba nti buli muntu yenna anzikiririzaamu talibonyaabonyezebwa, oba okulumizibwa, oba okuyigganyizibwa, oba enjala, oba obwereere, oba akabi, oba ekitala? Ku luuyi olulala, ebimu ku bino oba byonna biteekwa okutuuka ku abo bonna abampeereza mu bwesigwa, n’olwekyo mbakubiriza okuwakanya ebigambo byabwe kubanga ebigambo byabwe bya lugero olukoleddwa mu bukuusa nga bitumiddwa aboonoonyi okusuula okukkiriza kwennyini okw'abalonde singa ekyo kyandisobose. Naye mutegeere nti abeesigwa bange bonna abangoberera n'omutima gwabwe gwonna balokolebwa okuva mu busungu bw'Omwana gw'endiga n'obusungu bwa Katonda era balitwalibwa mu kifo eky'obulamu mu ntebe ey'obwakabaka eya Yahweh ng'omusango tegunagwa ku nsi.

Noolwekyo, baana bange, bangi ku mmwe mulibonaabona olw'ebizibu eby'enjawulo n'okuyigganyizibwa ebireetebwa abagwa, era bangi balifiirwa amagoba g'ensi eno mu nkomerero eno nga sinaba kukuŋŋaanya beesigwa bange. Buli sekondi ng'eyitawo, okulabika kwange kuba kumpi nnyo. Noolwekyo mbakubiriza, mmwe baagalwa bange, okunyimirira ku lwange era mukwate nnyo ku kwagala kwange ng'amaanyi agabakubiriza okugumiikiriza ekyetaagisa mmwe okuleeta obulokozi eri ababuze nga mubakulembera okuyingira mu lyato ng'akabi tekannaba kutuuka.

Mutegeere kye nnawa Pawulo okuwandiikira Abakkolosaayi: Kaakano nsanyukira mu kubonaabona kwange kwe mbonyaabonyezebwa ku lwammwe, n'okutuukiriza ebyasigalawo mu kubonaabona kwa Kristo mu mubiri gwange ku lw'emibiri gye, ge makubo, mwe nnafuulibwa omuweereza okusinziira ku kiragiro kya Katonda, ekiragiro ekyo kye nnaweebwa nze gye muli, okutuukiriza ekigambo kya Katonda. Bw'ekityo bwe kiri, baagalwa bange, bwe mubonaabona olw'erinnya lyange, olwo mweyongerayo mu kubonaabona kwange n'okugabana kwe nnagumiikiriza ku Gologosa. So nga, ssirina kye nnazira mu bulokozi bwammwe, era nze Ppaasika atuukiridde era omulamba ow'ebiseera byonna, mbayise buli omu ku mmwe okugumiikiriza ebintu bingi olw'erinnya lyange, kale mukibalirenga byonna ssanyu! Era mumanye, baana bange, nti eriiso teryalabanga, newankubadde okutu tekuwuliranga, newankubadde tekyayingiranga mu mitima gy'abantu ebyo Katonda bye yategekera abo abamwagala. Kale mudduke, mudduke mulyoke mufune! Kubanga Katonda si mutakyamu, n'alyerabira omulimu gwammwe, n'okuteekawo okwagala, kwe mwalaga eri Erinnya lye, mu kuweereza Abalamazi, era mukyali muweereza.

Bangi ku mmwe abakyamyusiddwa mu bye musuubira temukyalaba nti emirundi mingi abayite bange, abalonde, n'abeesigwa bagumiikiriza n'obwegendereza ebizibu, okuswazibwa, okufiirwa, n'ebirala bingi nnyo era ekivuddemu ekisembayo mu kugumiikiriza kwabwe mu kubonaabona kwa Kristo kwe kulokolebwa kw'ababuze n'empeera ennene n'okusasulwa kw'abo abeewaayo mu ngeri eyo. Temusomanga mu Ebikolwa by'Abatume, Pawulo ne Siira bwe baagumiikiriza ebizibu bingi mu Masedoniya, nga bakubiddwa nnyo ne basuulibwa mu kkomera mu butali bwa nsonga ne basibibwa mu bisiba e Filippi?

Naye nnatuma musisi okubaggya mu njegere zaabwe, era omukuumi w'ekkomera Owmufiriipi n'ennyumba ye baafuna obulokozi olw'okugumiikiriza kwa Pawulo ne Siira. Era bangi ku abo abangoberera, baligumiikiriza ebizibu n'okuyigganyizibwa n'okubonaabona. Naye mu bino byonna muli basinga bawanguzi era mbalwanirira mmwe baana bange, singa muba bawulize eri eddoboozi lyange era ne mugoberera ebyo bye njagala n'omutima gwammwe gwonna era ndibaleetera okuwangula ebintu byonna singa muba beesigwa eri eddoboozi lyange era ne munkkiriza okuluŋŋamya amagenda gammwe, kubanga obulokozi bwammwe buli mu kuwuliriza omulokozi wammwe ow'emirembe n'emirembe.

Noolwekyo mbakubiriza, mmwe baagalwa bange, munyimirire ku lwange era muwe okwagala kwange ensi efiridde era esalidde omusango eyimiridde mu njegere ng'omusango gunaatera okugwa. Mulinzija okubayamba okuwangula okuggwaamu amaanyi mulyoke mukuumire essuubi lyammwe mu birowoozo? Mwerabidde ebigambo byange, mmwe baagalwa bange? Era kaakano kubeererawo okukkiriza, essuubi n’okwagala, naye ekisinga obukulu mu bino kwe kwagala. Noolwekyo mbatuma okwagala ababuze, kubanga emirundi mingi obulungi bwa Katonda bwe buleetera abantu okwenenya.

N’olwekyo, mumpeereze mu bitundu byammwe, musabire abo abafunye byonna kubanga bwe bakyassa omukka kiyinza okuba nti okufiirwa ensi eno kulibaleetera okufuna emirembe gyonna. Mubuulire aboonoonyi era muleete obulokozi eri ababuze era mujjukire nti muli babaka bange baagalwa bange era mutuukirize okuyitibwa kwammwe mu nze era mubeere banywevu mu nkomerero eno ey'ebiseera. Noolwekyo mbatuma okwagala baliraanwa bammwe n'okuwa obuyambi n'okuyamba abo abali mu bwetaavu. Newankubadde ekinywebwa eky'amazzi oba essowaani y'emmere, muwe buli kye musobola okuyamba abo ababonaabona nga temubuusabuusa kintu kyonna. Era bwe kibaawo nti mubonaabona wamu n'ababuze, olwo mubayagale nnyo era mulage obujulizi bwange n'amaanyi gange era mwogere ku lwange ku mimwa egy'okukkiriza okuva mu mutima ogujjudde okukkiriza, essuubi, n'okwagala!

Noolwekyo mbabuuza, mutya okuyamba aboonoonyi? Temutegeera nti bwe nnatumibwa okuggyako eri endiga ezabula ez'ennyumba ya Isirayiri nti nnamala ebiseera byange n'abawooza n'aboonoonyi? Mwerabidde nti mwali muli mu bulamu obw'ekibi eky'embiro? Mwerabidde nti nze nzekka omusajja atalina kibi newankubadde omwana gw'endiga owa Katonda? Kale abanunule ba Mukama boogere abo be nnanunula okuva mu mukono gw'omulabe. Mwagale, mwagale, mwagale okutuusa ku mukka gwammwe ogusembayo kubanga kisingako okufa ng’ompeereza mu kwagala kwa Kristo okusinga okukankana olw’amafuba ag’obwereere ag’ebintu ebinafu era eby’obwavu, bye nawangula ddala ne mpa obuwanguzi abo bonna abamfuula Mukama waabwe ow’emirembe gyonna. Noolwekyo temwagala bulamu bwammwe okutuusa ku nkomerero era mugoberere ekyokulabirako kyange eky’okuweereza awatali kutya mu maaso g’obubi n’okufa ebyawangulwa ddala olw’okwagala okutaggwaawo okwa Katonda omunene ow’emirembe gyonna, ye El Shaddai!

Totya, kubanga ndi naawe: totya, kubanga nze Katonda wo: ndikuwa amaanyi, era ndikuyamba, era ndikuwanirira n'omukono gwange ogwa ggwanga ogw'obwenkanya. Kale mujje gye ndi baana bange muwummulire mu kwagala kwange gye muli nange ndibudaabuda emyoyo gyammwe era ndiwonya ebiwundu byammwe era ndibawa amaanyi mu kumenyeka kwammwe mulyoke mube n'eky'okuwa abo ababeetaaga kubanga bwe mutyo bwe mutumibwa. Kubanga mmwe babaka bange abatayogeddwako n'okuyitibwa mu kitiibwa ky'Obwakabaka bwange, naye era okubonaabona olw'erinnya lyange.

Mazima, mazima, muli bagabana ku kubonaabona kwange, mugoberere kyokka ekyokulabirako kyange era temuzirika mu ssaawa yammwe ey'okuggwaamu amaanyi. Kubanga ndi nammwe buli ntambula, era nze, Mukama waffe Yesu Kristo alongoosa emyoyo gyammwe mu muliro gwange ogulongoosa, newankubadde nga muli bibya bya bbumba byokka mu mikono gy'omubumbi, omuliro ogulongoosa gulifuula amaaso gammwe okuba ng'ejjinja mulyoke mube abatayinzika kumenyeka mu maanyi gange naye nga mukyali mu bunafu bw'omubiri gwammwe. Naye, bw'ekityo bwe kiri nti mu bunafu bw'omubiri omwonoonyi mwe mukulaakulira okwagala kwange mu mmwe nga nkwesembereza gye muli era ne mbateekateeka n'okusingawo olw'ebigendererwa byammwe eby'emirembe n'emirembe. Noolwekyo, kya nsonga abanunule okubonaabona olw'erinnya lyange kubanga nnawaayo omubiri gwange gwonna mu ssaddaaka ey'okubonaabona mulyoke mubeerewo mu maaso ga Yahweh, ye Katonda omuyinza w'ebintu byonna. Mutegeere nti mu kugezesebwa kwammwe n’okubonaabona kwammwe temuleeta kisa kyange ekirokola eri abo abali mu bwetaavu naye era mukola obulokozi bwammwe mu kutya n’okukankana nsobole okubatuusa ku mpeera yammwe enzijuvu era n’olwekyo essuubi lyammwe linaatera okutuukirira!

Oboolyawo owulidde ku kugumiikiriza kwa Yobu? Nga Yobu yali musajja mwesigwa eyafunibwa mu biseera ebizibu era eyalumbibwa Setaani mu butali bwa nsonga. Oboolyawo owulidde ku nkomerero ya Yobu kubanga Yobu teyali musajja mutali mutuukirivu eyawangulwa obubi, naye yali musajja mutuukirivu eyasanga ekisa eyawangula obubi n'obulungi ng'agoberera ebigambo bya Yahweh n'asigala mu butuukirivu ne mu maaso g'okuyigganyizibwa okubi. Noolwekyo Yahweh yaddiza Yobu emirundi ebiri egy'ebyo byonna ebyatwalibwa mu butali bwa nsonga, era Yahweh n'asasula Yobu olw'ebizibu bye n'okuyigganyizibwa. Laba, tubala abo abalina omukisa abagumiikiriza. Muwulidde okugumiikiriza kwa Yobu, era mumanyi enkomerero Mukama gye yakola. Kubanga Mukama wa kisa kingi era wa kisa.

Bw’etyo esigaddewo okukkiriza, essuubi, n’okwagala, era ekisinga obukulu mu bino kwe kwagala. Kale mutegeere baagalwa bange nti eri abeesigwa essuubi linaatera okutuukirizibwa mu bujjuvu nga mukwatibwa okutuusibwa mu bulamu obw’omu ggulu okuva mu misango egigenda okujja. Muwulire eddoboozi lyange era mutegeere okwagala kwange gye muli mmwe abayite bange, abeesigwa, n'abalonde, omubiri gwange n'omugole wange! Noolwekyo, muwummulire mu kwagala kwange kubanga ne mu buzibu obusinga obunene okwagala kwange kusigala kwa maanyi era tekuyinza kuwangulwa kubanga nze omu mu bumu n'ekigendererwa ne Kitange, era bwe kwagala kwa Yahweh okutaggwaawo kwe mpa abo bonna abasikira obulamu obutaggwaawo n'abo bonna abajja eri Yesu Omunazaaleeti okulokolebwa.

Tegeera tewali muntu ayinza kujja eri Yahweh okuggyako nga ayitira mu nze, Kristo Yesu. Bw’etyo okwagala kwa Yahweh tekuyinza kuwangulwa kubanga Yahweh tayinza kuwangulwa, n’olwekyo nange siyinza kuwangulwa, kubanga nawangula. Era nze Mukama ow'obulokozi ow'emirembe n'emirembe, ye oyo Yahweh gwe yafuula Mukama wa bakama ne Kabaka wa bakabaka, era yampa erinnya erisinga erinnya lyonna erya Yesu Kristo buli vviivi lyonna eriri mu ggulu ne ku nsi ne wansi w'ensi lyonna okufukamira. Mutegeere nti abagwa babatya baagalwa bange era okutya okungi kukwata emitima gyabwe olw'omusango gwabwe ogugenda okujja ogutayimirira.

Mbalagira era mbakubiriza, mmwe baana bange, okutwala okwagala kwange eri ensi eno enwadde era efa era esaliddwa omusango mu kiseera kino ekisembayo nga akabi tekannaba kugwa. Mbagamba nti muwonye abalwadde, muzuukize abafu, mulongoose abagenge, mugobe emizimu kubanga oyo anzikiririzaamu emirimu gye nnakolanga ku nsi naye aligikola era emirimu gisingako obukulu ddala kubanga ngenze eri Kitaffe. Mutambulire mu kwagala okungi n'obuwombeefu, era musisinkane abo abali mu bwetaavu n'amaanyi ga Kristo ge nja okukozesa mu mmwe n'amaanyi. Kubanga mu mmwe, mmwe bibya byange eby'ettaka, mwe muli amaanyi g'obulungi bwa Katonda mu Kristo mu mmwe. Mwogere ku bizibu bye musanga mu kukkiriza. Mube n’okukkiriza mu Yahweh, n’okukkiriza mu Yeshua HaMashiach era mugambe olusozi olwo nti GGYAWO OSUULIBWE MU NNYANJA! Era bwe mulikikola mu bwesigwa, ndikuwa obubonero bungi eby'amagero n'ebyewuunyo okukolebwa emikono gy'abeesigwa.

Mube bavumu era bavumu, kubanga nze Mpologoma ey'omu kika kya Yuda abalwanirira. Mbasiima era mbaziyiza olw'okwagala kwa Katonda okuli mu Kristo Yesu Mukama wammwe okugumiikiriza n'obwegendereza olw'erinnya lyange era muleete ababuze eka eri Kitaffe ne mu kiseera kino ekisembayo. Mbasiima mmwe abalonde bange mubeere ekyokulabirako kubanga mmwe beesigwa bange n'abalonde bange be nnaweesa mu kubonaabona nti mu bunafu n'okumenyeka kw'omubiri gwammwe muyinze okwolesa amaanyi ga Katonda omunene ow'emirembe gyonna, ye Yahweh yennyini nga mugenda mu buweereza n'obuyinza obunene eri abo be mbatuma okuweereza.

Ndi nammwe buli ntambula.

Nze omu mu bumu n'ekigendererwa ne Kitange.

Nze Mukama ow'obulokozi ow'emirembe n'emirembe.

Nze oyo Yahweh gwe yafuula Mukama wa bakama ne Kabaka wa bakabaka.

Nze Mpologoma ey'omu kika kya Yuda abalwanirira.

Nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, ntumye ebigambo bino omuddu wange, era omuddu wange ebigambo byange bitumiddwa olw'obuwulize n'okuzzibwamu amaanyi kw'abo bonna be bisanga. Era Ebigambo byange birisanga amaaso, amatu n'emitima gy'abo be bitumiddwa. Muyimirire n'amaanyi baagalwa bange kubanga empeera yammwe eri ku mulyango gwammwe, kyokka temuzirika. Muwummulire mu kwagala kwange era muwummulire mu mirimu gyange, nga muwedde okukola egyammwe era kaakano ke kaseera k'okununulibwa kwammwe era kaakano ke kaseera k'obulokozi bwammwe obujjuvu, era njija ku lwammwe baagalwa bange. Mumpulire era muwulire ebigambo byange, siribaleka newankubadde okubasuula mmwe beesigwa bange mu Kristo, n'abo bonna abawulira eddoboozi lyange. Mutunule mu ggulu, kubanga okulabika kwange kunaatera okubalukawo mu bbanga mu kiseera n'essaawa bye mutasuubira. Njija ku lwammwe mmwe abeesigwa bange, mwetegefu?
