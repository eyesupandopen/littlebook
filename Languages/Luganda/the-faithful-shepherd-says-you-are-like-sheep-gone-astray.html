Omusumba Omutiibwa Agamba: Muli Ng’endiga Ezaabula!

Ayi ggwe bantu bange, mwenna muli ng’endiga ezaabula, era buli omu ku mmwe akyamye n’adda mu kkubo lye, amakubo ge saabalabira, naye mugoberera amakubo ga bannabbi ab’obulimba abalimba era abalayirira mu linnya lyange ettukuvu, era tebalirema kubonerezebwa. Mbalabula n’obukambwe, abantu bange, era nkoowoola eggulu n’ensi okutegeka ebigambo byange: TEMUBALANGA bigambo bya bunnabbi nze, Mukama wammwe Yesu Kristo, bye mbawa ng'ekintu ekitali kitukuvu; wabula mukeme buli kigambo kye mbawa. Bwe munaagaana, kiba kibi ku mutemwa gwammwe.

Mbagamba nga sinnaba kweyongerayo, bangi ku mmwe baliziba amatu gaabwe era baliziba amaaso gaabwe eri ebigambo bye nnaatera okwogera, era mbakubiriza obutatya. Abangi ku mmwe baligwa olw'okukakibwa ab'emikwano n’ab'omu maka gaabwe, okukakibwa amakanisa ag'obulimba n'ebitongole by'ensi, nga balowooza nti eby'obuyigirize by'ensi binkwatirako, so ng'ate eby'obuyigirize by'ensi ge magezi ga Sitaani yennyini ag'ekifere, n'amagezi ge ag'okwawulawo obulungi n'obubi bulijjo galeeta kufa, kubanga bw'atyo bwe yategeka ebigambo bye okutuukiriza. Temubeeranga mu abo abagaana ebigambo byange, naye muwulirize n'emmeeme yammwe yonna era mufumiitirize ku ekyo kye ndyogera, kubanga ebigambo byange bulamu, era birinyweza okuyitibwa kwammwe n'okulondebwa kwammwe.

Wa mu Kigambo kyange ekitukuvu we mulabira ekintu ekiyitibwa Obukristaayo bw’Obuzayuni? Temutegeera nti abakulembeze baabwe be ba ku sinagoogi ya Sitaani? Yee, waliwo abo abayingiridde Isiraeri abatali ba Isiraeri era nga ba mu kuŋŋaaniro lya Sitaani, era balimba, nga bagamba nti Bayudaaya so si be! Mmwe, Ekkanisa yange, ye Isiraeri wa Katonda, era temuli ba mu kifo kyaabwe, wadde okuba nti eno y'enjigiriza y'okudda mu kifo, wabula Kigambo kyange kitukuvu. Mutunuulire mwembi mulabe! Nedda! Soogera bubi ku bange, newankubadde nga waliwo mu mmwe abagezi mu kulowooza kwabwe abaligamba kyennyini ekyo.

Mwekebejje olulyo lwange, kubanga nazaalibwa mu bajjajja, n'obusika bwange butuufu; era singa wabaawo muntu yenna mu mmwe annyiikira Isiraeri, nze mmusinga, kubanga nawaayo obulamu bwange ku lwabwe! Mmange ne baganda bange be baani? Kubanga buli akola Katonda by'ayagala, ye mmange era be baganda bange, temusomanga? Mbasosolera: ekirungi n’ekibi, kubanga bangi ku mmwe babulwa okusosola okw’omwoyo okw’amazima era bazibiddwa amaaso olw’obulimba bw’ababi. Temusomanga nti Katonda emirundi egimu atuukiriza amakubo ge ng’ayita mu mukono gw’abalabe be, kubanga n’abalabe be bagondera eddoboozi lye? Yee, Isiraeri yaddizibwawo mu 1948, era njagala bangi mu nsi eyo era ndibawonya; naye Isiraeri yaddizibwawo abasajja ababi, n’abakulembeze baabwe balina ebigendererwa eby’ekyama ebibi. Tekibasaanira, baana bange, okuba n'omukono mu kitundu kino eky'emirimu gya Kitange mu kiseera kino, newankubadde nga muliyamba mu biseera eby'omu maaso mu Bwakabaka bwange; naye kiri ku nze okulokola Yakobo, si mmwe!

Mwakyamizibwa ebigambo ebirungi n'okwogera okusanyusa era mwagwa mu kabenje ak'amagezi amangi. Mwalogeddwa emyoyo gya Yezeberi, egikolera mu makubo ga Balaamu. Mmanye nti ndimenya obuyinza bwe babaliko era nzigule amaaso gammwe n’amatu gammwe singa munkyukira n'obuwombeefu n'okwagala okujja eri Ekigambo kyange ne muyiga ku nze nga temulina endowooza zammwe ezaasooka ze mwayiga okuva mu myoyo gino emilimba egiragulira mu bannabbi baagyo abalimba. Emyoyo gya Yezeberi egiyogera obulimba obunene mu bannabbi be ab'obulimba, n'okutuusa ku bubonero n'ebyamagero eby'obulimba, n'okulagula olw'okwegomba okw'obugagga mu mwoyo gwa Balaamu, gino giyingiridde amakanisa gammwe, era mulya obucaafu okuva mu mikono gyabwe.

Mwalimbibwa ekkuŋŋaaniro lya Sitaani okuyingira mu ndagaano ey'obulimba nabo, ekkuŋŋaaniro lya Sitaani. Abamu mu Kkanisa yange si beesigwa eri ebigambo byange n'ebiragiro byange kubanga bakkirizza obulimba obwogerwa mu linnya lyange, nga babaganyulwako mu buzibe bwabwe, ekintu ekisaanidde. Temusangibwanga mu bo bwe ndijja; ssigambanga nti mufulume mu ye mweyawule, kubanga Kristo alina kutabagana ki ne Beliyaali? Munaabe enziro mu maaso gammwe mu musaayi gwange ogutangirira era mugaziye mulabe omusana ogw'amazima gwe ndibawa, kubanga amaaso gammwe gasibiddwako ebbumba, era temusobola kugaziya nga siri wano.

Nga bwe kyawandiikibwa nti Katonda yabawa omwoyo ogw'otulo, amaaso baleme okulaba, n'amatu baleme okuwulira okutuusa leero, era olw'ebigambo ebirungi n'okwogera okusanyusa mwegasse mu buzibe bwabwe, nga temuli bazibe ba maaso ku bigendererwa byange eri Isiraeri kyokka, naye era muzibiddwa amaaso okuva ku kuyitibwa kwammwe okw'amazima mu nze, Kristo Yesu, Mukama wammwe! Bwe muba muzibe ba maaso, temuli ba ddembe kusoma na kutegeera bigambo byange, era temutambulira mu mwoyo. N’olwekyo, mugoberere ebigambo byange, abantu bange; kubanga newankubadde mwammanya olw’omubiri, olw’omubiri temukymmanyi nate. Kubanga nkyusiddwa okuba omwoyo oguwa obulamu, eyawa omwoyo gw'obulamu eri Isiraeri wa Katonda, ye mwoyo gw'obwana mwe mukaabira nti, ABBA, Kitange!

Kaakano Mukama ye Mwoyo ogwo: era Omwoyo gwa Mukama w'ali, w'ali eddembe; kale munywerere mu ddembe Kristo lye yabalekera, so temwesibanga nate mu kikoligo ky'obuddu. Mutege amatu gammwe era mukyuse emitima gyammwe muwulire Ekigambo kyange, so temukigaananga! Omwoyo guwa obulamu; omubiri tegugasa kantu. Ebigambo bye njogera nammwe mwoyo era bulamu. N'olwekyo, temwefaanaanyirizanga Isiraeri olw'omubiri, era mufulume mu bulimba bwe obw'ebyobufuzi, naye mufaananyizibwe n'omwoyo gwange ogukoleddwa mu kifaananyi kya Katonda!

Temutegeera? Obulimba bwabwe bukkirizibwa okuteekateeka olubaawo lw'omwana w'okubula, bonna basobole okusalirwa omusango abasanyukira obutali butuukirivu, amannya gaabwe gaatali mu Kitabo ky'Obulamu okuva ku ntandikwa y'ensi. Muwulire ebigambo byange eby'okunenya so temukakanyazanga mitima gyammwe, kubanga ebigambo byange biribawonya akabi akajja era biribayawula ku abo abalwana nange era abaanjeemulukuka okumala ebbanga lya kumpi emyaka 2000 kati. Era newankubadde amazima gaabula ku Isiraeri, ensigalira ya Yakobo erinkyukira era erikkiriza obutuukirivu obutajja na mateeka naye olw’okukkiriza mu nze. Mulongoose ebirowoozo byammwe era mwesunsule ebigambo eby'obulimba eby'emisege bye baboogerera mu matu, n'okwogera obulimba mu linnya lyange, kubanga saabatuma.

Waliwo ekibi ekinene ku nsi yange, era ndijja kununula. Waliwo ekibi ekinene ku Yakobo kubanga bakkirizza okwonoonwa abalimba b'omu Edomu. Temukkirizanga lipoota yange nti ekkuŋŋaaniro lya Sitaani libaawo era lya mazima? Mmanye nti batambulira mu mmwe! Temutegeera nti omuzibe w’amaaso tasobola kukulembera muzibe munne, era Isiraeri yazibibwa amaaso okutuusa okujja kw’Amawanga lwe kulituukirira? Ndinunula ensigalira ey’abeesigwa okuva mu Yakobo, era nteeka akabonero ku 144,000. Era balinkolera emirimu egy'amaanyi, era ndibatuma okudda n'okudda mu kiseera kya Yakobo eky'okubonaabona, era balinunula bangi, era emirimu mingi egy'amaanyi gye balikola, era ndibakulembera okukulembera abalala, kubanga tewali kkubo ddala erigenda eri Kitange wabula okuyita mu nze, Mukama wammwe Yesu Kristo, Masiya gwe baagaana.

Naye mmwe, ayi Omukristaayo ow'omulembe guno, mmwe baana bange ab'ekivve, mulowooza nti mumanyi bulungi kubanga musomye ebiwandiiko byabwe ebyaleetebwa mu nkukutu mu mmwe. Era mukkirizza lipoota yaabwe ey'obulimba, lipoota ey'obulimba okuva mu kkuŋŋaaniro lya Sitaani ery'omu Edomu, ekibiina eky'okuzimba yeekaalu, abazikiriza bennyini Sitaani be yatumye okuwamba ensi. Era bakulembera eggwanga lye mwandisinze mu kifo kya Kitange singa mugoberera engero zaabwe ezaayiiyizibwa n'amagezi, nga beeyita abantu ba Katonda abalonde era ne babakakasa okubawa omukisa so nga balibazikiriza ku mukisa ogusooka. Mmanye nti saatumawo nteekateeka ya byabufuzi, era gavumenti z'ensi ziwereza Sitaani, eyazigula n'empeera y'obutali butuukirivu.

Temusomanga era temutegeeranga nti nze nzekka nteekateeka, era nze Kabaka omulwanyi eyatumibwa Yahweh yennyini okuwamba obwakabaka mu butuukirivu n'okuggyawo obufuzi bwonna n'obuyinza bwa Sitaani? Yee, ndimenyaamenya obufuzi bwe, butalemenga kuddamu kussa, era ndiwunya omutwe gwe omulimba nga bwe kyalagulibwa, era alyaka emirembe n'emirembe mu nnimi z'omuliro ezisinga okwokya ez'ebbulu, era aliwuga mu sulufeero eyaka mu nnyanja eyo ennene ku lunaku olwo olunene, era taliddamu kussa naye alizikirizibwa era alyaka emirembe n'emirembe. Mmanye nti tewali bukambwe butali bwa nsonga mu buteebenkevu bwa Kitange, wabula empeera etuufu ey'ebikolwa bya Sitaani yennyini n'okwegomba kwe eri abaana ba Kitange n'ekitonde, n'olwekyo Kitange alimuwa ekyo ky'ayagaliza mmwe mu buteebenkevu bwe.

Temusomanga? Kabaka alyanukula n'abagamba nti Mazima mbagamba nti Bwe mwakikola omu ku abo abasinga obuto ku baganda bange bano, mwakikolera nze. Lwaki mugoberera abakulembeze b'ebyobufuzi ab'obulimba nga mulinga abangoberera? Ndibagamba lwaki mutegeere ddala: Abangi ku mmwe mugoberera obulimba obuli mu Baibuli zammwe ze saaterekawo ze saawandiika, era saalonda abo abaakungaanya ebiwandiiko ebyogerwako ng'eby'Obukristaayo bw’Obuzayuni. Tewali kintu ekiyitibwa Obukristaayo bw’Obuzayuni, kubanga siri muyima wa Bayudaaya. Singa nali Muzayuni, sandikyuse n'adda eri Amawanga, era amawanga gaŋŋaaniriza okumala akaseera. Nedda, bakozesa Obuzayuni okuleeta omwana w'okubula n'eyekaalu yaabwe gye balizimba mu butamanya mu linnya lya Kitange, era bawemula Kitange kubanga banjeemulukuka, Yesu Kristo. Si nti najja eri abange ne njeemulukukibwa abange kyokka, naye bangi ku bo bakyanjeemulukuka ne leero. Naye ggwe, ayi ekkanisa ey'e Lawodikiya, olowooza nti osobola okwanguyiza okutuukirizibwa kw'obunnabbi ng'ogoberera eddalu lyabwe; kubanga ekkuŋŋaaniro lya Sitaani lyagala ensi yaalyo, ensi eteriimu mmwe, baana bange. Sibawonyezza mu bo?

Mmanye nti Sitaani yagezaako okumbba obusika bwange, kubanga ensi ya Mukama n'okujjula kwayo. Sitaani ayagala ensi etalimu Katonda, ensi etalimu Katonda, ensi etalimu nze, ejjinja essona ery'oku nsonda, n'ensi omusinga amaanyi mw'awangaalira nga batta abantu ku nsi okuggyako ensigo ya Sitaani yennyini, gye bali. Isiraeri ayingiriddwa okumala enkumi n'enkumi z'emyaka kati olw'ebiwandiiko byabwe eby'e Babulooni (yee, baakola amateeka g'ennono ezaayisibwa mu bigambo) ebivvoola nze, Mukama wammwe Yesu Kristo, ekintu ekisoboka okufuna kubanga sirigaanibwa. Era ensi enaatera okutegeera obusungu bwange n'amaanyi gange, kubanga nze mukulu okusinga bonna okuggyako Kitange, era tekisoboka kunziza.

Nnindidde mu kugumiikiriza n’okubonyaabonwa okungi, ne nzikiriza erinnya lyange okuvumibwa n’ekirabo kyange eky’okutangirira okubalibwa ng’ekintu ekitali kitukuvu omugole wange asobole okulokolebwa, omugole yennyini eyali akwekeddwa mu biseera ebyayita mu kyama ekinene ekya Katonda, ekyama kye nnabikkula mu bujjuvu eri Omutume Pawulo. Era osobola okusoma ebiwandiiko bye okutegeera, kubanga teyeeyogerako, naye yalangirira enjiri y'Ekyama ekinene ekya Katonda kye yamuwa okumbikkulira. Tewali ajja kutwala ekyange, kubanga nnakisasulira n'omusaayi gwange ogutangirira, era ndisasula abakola ebibi.

Sitaani ayagala ensi eteriimu mmwe, baana bange, naye bangi nnyo ku mmwe mu busirusiru muwaayo ssente zammwe okuwagira ekkuŋŋaaniro lya Sitaani n'enteekateeka zaabwe ezitali ntukuvu mu nsi yange ne ku nsigalira y'eggwanga lya Isiraeri, eggwanga lye ndifuga ensi okuva ku ntebe ya Dawudi mu bwakabaka bwange obunaatera okubikkulwa. Muwagira okufa kwammwe, nga mulowooza nti mukulaakulanya enjiri. Lwaki temusobola kutegeera nti okufa n'okuzikiriza n'abafuzi abatta abantu si byange naye nsigo ya Sitaani yennyini; yee, kitaabwe ye Mulyolyomi, Kitaffe w'Obulimba, Sitaani yennyini. Mmanye nti ye mulabe eyawangulwa, era atya n'okumanya nti ennaku ze ntono, era atya ebiseera bye eby'omu maaso okusinga ekintu kyonna, kubanga amanyi nti tewalibaawo kukkakkana ku kibonerezo kye, kubanga Katonda yennyini akirangirira! Baana bange, temusobola kugoberera Sitaani ne mumpeereza. Kubanga tewali muntu asobola kuweereza bakama babiri: kubanga oba alikyawa omu, n’ayagala omulala; oba alinywerera ku omu, n’anyooma omulala. N’olwekyo, mulonde gwe mulisinza, kubanga ssiragirangako Kkanisa yange kuba n'akakwate konna n'ekiyitibwa Obukristaayo bw’Obuzayuni, era omugendo ogwo si gwange, wabula gwa Sitaani, era agukozesa okuleeta omwana w'okubula. Abangi ku mmwe mwabulwa amakulu g’ebigambo byange, nga mulowooza nti “okulwanyisa” kitegeeza kulwanyisa nnyo era tewali kirala. Nedda, omusajja ow’ekibi atumibwa mu magezi mu kifo kyange eri abantu abaanjeemulukuka, naye era ndirokola Yakobo ng’ayita mu kuzibu okunene, era sirirongoosa nsi yange ey’obusika yokka, naye ndirongoosa ensi yonna okuva mu kibi n’okwonoona. Tegeera nti ndirokola Yakobo olw'ebisuubizo Kitange bye yakola eri bajjajjaabwe, era si lwa buweerero wadde obutuukirivu bwabwe, kubanga tewali mubiri mutuukirivu mu maaso gange.

Mbakubiriza okussa wansi ebiwandiiko by'abo abatayogera ku lwange, era mube bagezi, baana bange; kubanga bangi ku mmwe mulina obutamanya nnyo nti temusobola kwawula bulimba na mazima. Kubanga temunkwata ng'omutwe gwammwe naye mukwata ebigambo ebyogerwa bannabbi ab'obulimba aba Yezeberi, abakolera mu makubo ga Balaamu, nga bagoberera empeera y'obutali butuukirivu. Kubanga Ekigambo kya Katonda kiramu era kya maanyi, era kisala okusinga ekitala kyonna ekiwabyawabyako ebbali zombiriri, nga kyawula n'emmeeme n'omwoyo, n'ennyingo n'omusokoolo, era kyawula ebirowoozo n'ebigendererwa by'omutima. Mudde eri Ekigambo kyange ekirongoofu ekya Katonda, kubanga Kitange akikuma okutuusa leero.

Mutegeere engeri yange, mmwe abantu bange, era muyige ku nze. Saalonda ddoboozi limu lyokka kutuusa njiri yange, so saalonda ddoboozi erisinga obukadde okufuga ebigambo byange n’okukakasa amazima gaabyo. Nedda, mu makubo ga Kitange (Kitange teyeetaaga muwi wa magezi mu nteekateeka ze) ne mu magezi ga Kitange yalonda amaloboozi mangi okuva mu biseera eby'enjawulo, ebifo, n'obuwangwa obutali bumu naye nga byonna bikwatagana. Mutunuulire ebya Byzantine, mmwe abaana bange, so temubuusabuusa bigambo byange, era mwekenneenye ebiwandiiko bye nkuumye, era mwetoowaze eri ebigambo byange. Temwalagirwa okusoma mwerage nti musaanidde? Kubanga abaana b'ensi eno mu mulembe gwabwe balina amagezi okusinga abaana b'omusana: Kubanga balina amagezi mu bubi n'obulimba, era tebakola kirala wabula okubba, okutta, n'okuzikiriza.

Kitange akuumye ekigambo kye ekituukiridde ekya Katonda, era kiri ddala wansi w'ennyindo zammwe, naye mukigaana musobole okumatiza okwegomba kwammwe okw'obunafu n'enkyusa ennyangu okusoma, enkyusa ezonooneddwa ze saatuma wadde okuwandiika. Lwaki mugenda kuva ku bigambo bino? Mu butamanya bwammwe obw'obujama, temutegeera nti Setaani naye akoppa Ebyawandiikibwa Ebitukuvu. Akikola mu ngeri y'emu omubbi omulimba gy'ayinza okubbiramu ng'aggyako enkooni emu ku mujiji omulundi gumu okufuuza omujiji nga tewali alabye. Ensalo tezisazibwa mpolampola? Kubanga omuyizzi ki atega omutego gwe ng'ennyonyi eraba? Akyusizza ebigambo, aggyeemu ebitundu n'ennyiriri enziramba, ayongeddeyo ebigambo, era abikoze byonna ng'ayagala okugatta eddiini zonna wansi w'enteekateeka emu ey'okugatta amakanisa okugondeza bonna eri Kristo we omulimba, atendera mu mmwe mu lwatu, era anaatera okubikkulibwa.

Nja kusuula ensolo n'omunnabbi omulimba nga balamu mu nnyanja eyaka omuliro n'ekibiriiti basobole okutuuka ku busika bwabwe, obusika Kitange bwe yategekera Omulyolyomi n'abamalayika be. Kubanga ajja kubawa ensi yaabwe n'ensi esaanira okwegomba kwabwe okubi kwe babagombeza, mmwe abaana bange. Temuyize oba mwerabidde nti ebibiina by'abanamatiga byakyusa ebbaluwa zange amangu ddala nga bazifunye? Era mulowooza nti tekiyinza kubaawo mu biseera byammwe, amagezi we geeyongedde?

Munoonye ekigambo kyange ekituukiridde, era mube n'okukkiriza nti nsobola okuwa abavvuunuzi abeesigwa. Mube n'okukkiriza mu bigambo bye mbawadde; mube n'okukkiriza mu bigambo by'Ekigambo kya Katonda. Temuba bajama nnyo okulowooza nti ekiwandiiko kyonna kituukiridde; ssaabalagira okusoma mwerage nti musaanidde? Naye mmwe abantu bange, bwe musoma, muleka abalabe b'omusalaba okutegeeza ebigambo byange mulyoke musendasendebwe okuwa olukusa eri ekkuŋŋaaniro lya Setaani. Mu butamanya muwa olukusa lwammwe n'okukkiriza okw'ekisirise eri Setaani n'abaana be abawambye ettaka lyange ettukuvu, naye okw'ekiseera kyokka, n'okubonaabona kwa Yakobo kujja kubirongoosa okuva mu nsi, n'abawombeefu balisikira ensi. Mube n'okukkiriza mu nze, Ekigambo eyafuuka omubiri, okubawa amazima; n'amazima gali mu Byawandiikibwa byange Ebitukuvu, kubanga byogera ku nze.

Mujja kugaana ebigambo byange, mmwe abantu bange? Mujja kusalawo okujooja ababaka be ntumye? Kubanga ntumye bangi! Abangi ku mmwe mujja kunyiikira mu bulalu bwammwe, nga temufuddeeyo na kufumiitiriza ku bigambo by'obunnabbi buno, era mujja kukikola okutuusa ku kabi kammwe n'okufiirwa empeera. Amangu ddala omuzibi agenda kuggibwawo n'Omugole wange n'atwalibwa, bajja kwanguwa nnyo okutta buli kintu na buli muntu Omukristaayo. Bajja kumalawo Obukristaayo ku nsi singa basobola, era bajja kuyigganya n'okuttira abatuukirivu bange mu ngeri nnyingi, na buli alikoowoola erinnya lya Mukama alirokolebwa.

Mukyukire nze kaakano, era mulongoose emyoyo gyammwe okuva mu bigambo byabwe eby'obulimba. Mwekuŋŋaanyirizza ebigambo by'okufa n'okuzikirira mu butamanya obujjuvu ku byafaayo byammwe. Mbasoomooza okusoma ebyafaayo by'ebitabo bye saawandiika bye mugulumiza okusinga ebigambo byange. Musome abawandiisi ab'amazima be baali, kubanga baawandiika obulimba obunene mu bitabo byabwe, kubanga mujja kubategeerera ku bibala byabwe. Ebingi ku bitabo bino bifuuliddwa eby'omutindo mu masomero gammwe ag'obusaaserdooti; mukinoonye, kubanga kya mazima! Bwe munaaba banyiikivu mu nsonga zino ne munywerera ku nze, kale nja kubakulembera mu mazima gonna, era mujja kutegeera ekyama ky'obwakatonda kye muli. Naye n'ekyama ekyo ekinene kinyoomeddwa mu birowoozo byammwe ne mu mitima gyammwe olw'obulimba bwabwe; ekyama ekyo ekinene ye muntu omu omuggya eyaggyibwa mu Muyudaaya ne mu Munnaggwanga, awatali bbugwe lwa kwawula, kubanga olubbugwe olwo lwaggibwawo mu nze.

Abangi ku mmwe munywerera ku lunyiriri lw'Endagaano Enkadde oluggyiddwa mu ngeri etali ntuufu: nga mulowooza mu butamanya nti mulina okuwagira abassi b'ekika okusobola okuweebwa omukisa nze. Mpa omukisa abo abayitiddwa mu linnya lyange, era newaakubadde ntonnyesa enkuba ku batuukirivu n'abatali batuukirivu, emikisa gyonna giri mu nze, Kristo Yesu Mukama wammwe, Mukama w'abakama ow'amazima era Kabaka w'abakabaka ow'amazima. N'olwekyo, mukyukire nze nga mwenenyezza, kubanga mututte obulimba bwa Yezeberi ng'amazima g'enjiri, era mwekuŋŋaanyirizza obusungu bwe mukola bwe mutyo; kubanga nja kuwa n'abaana bange okusinziira ku bikolwa byabwe, newaakubadde sijja kubasalira musango gw'ebibi byabwe.

Nze Mwana gw'Endiga owa Katonda eyalondebwa ensi nga tennassibwako musingi.

Nze Kabaka omulwanyi eyatumibwa Yahweh.

Nze oyo eggulu gwe liteekwa okukkiriza okutuusa ebiseera by'okuddawo kw'ebintu byonna.

Nze oyo eyabatizibwa Yokaana Omubatiza omwoyo bwe gwakka ng'ejjiba ku ddoboozi lya Kitange yennyini.

Nze oyo eyajja eri ababe n'agaanibwa, omusajja ow'ennaku eyasasula omuwendo ogusinga waggulu ku lwammwe mwenna.

Nze mukulu okusinga bonna okuggyako Kitange, Yahweh.

Nze Yeshua HaMashiach, amanyiddwa nga Yesu Kristo, ne ιησου χριστου mu Luyonaani ne mu nnimi endala nnyingi zonna ezintegeeramu nga Yesu Kristo oba Kristo Yesu, kubanga nze Mukama!

Mangu nnyo mujja kundaba amaaso n'amaaso, mmwe Omugole wange, era mujja kumanya nga bwe mumanyiddwa. N'olwekyo, mbayita okujja gye ndi; kale temugaana bigambo byange, era muwulire eddoboozi lyange, kubanga obulamu n'obulokozi bifunibwa mu bawulize.
