Omujulirwa Omulamba: Ekkanisa
Mu Bikoleddwa bya Yokaana Omutume Omubatiza
Gatonnya 01, 2024

Ssaakubatuukiriza?

Mulindire era mubeere n'emirembe, era mumanye nga nze Mukama eyabakowoola okuyimirira mu maaso gange nga muli batuukirivu mu makubo gammwe gonna. Ssaakubatuukiriza? Ssaakubafuula batuukirivu mu nze, Mukama wammwe Yezu Kristu? Temuli bumu nange mu kubonaabona kwange n'ekitiibwa kyange? Nze Mukama wa Bakama era Kabaka wa Bakabaka, era sirimba. Ebyawandiikibwa Ebitukuvu tebyakirangirira? Muyimirire nga muli beesigwa era abatuukirivu, era mubeere n'emirembe mu mitima gyammwe, era mumanye nga nze, Mukama wammwe Yezu Kristu, nabawa okusonyiyibwa okutuukiridde n'emirembe mu maaso gange bwe munaawuliriza ebigambo byange ne mukkiriza okuyigirizibwa kwange.

Amagezi kye kintu ekisinga obukulu; n'olwekyo, funa amagezi — si magezi ga nsi agaggwaawo, wabula amagezi ga Mukama. Era muwummulire wansi w'omukono gwange ogw'amaanyi. Kubanga okutya Mukama kwe kwewala ekibi n'okutambulira mu maaso gange nga oli mutuukirivu, bw'atyo bw'agamba Mukama ow'amaanyi, yee Mukama wammwe Yezu Kristu. Wuliriza eddoboozi lyange! Wuliriza eddoboozi lyange so towuliriza malala! Eddoboozi eddungi lisinziira ku Kigambo kyange ne libalimba, nga bwe linoonya okufuga emyoyo gyammwe ddala, balyoke babafuule omunyago bwe muligoberera. Temwagala nsi, baana bange, wadde ebintu ebiri mu nsi. Ensi eno embi eya kaakano eggwaawo, era nze, Mukama, nkigambye, era sirisirisibwa kulemererwa kwammwe okukkiriza, era sirisirisibwa kubulwa kwammwe kukkiriza. Nedda, nja kuleekaana n'amaanyi mangi nnyo mulyoke mumanye amazima g'ebigambo bye njogera. Mubigerageranye n'Ebyawandiikibwa Ebitukuvu. Nze si kigambo ekyufuuka omubiri ekibeera mu mmwe? Ssaabawa kusibwako mafuta na Mubeezi okutuusa lwe nja okujja?

Mumanye, baana bange, nti nabagula n'omusaayi gwange mwennyini, n'ensi yange, era mangu ddala obutuukirivu bujja kufuga ku nsi. Nkigambye. Nabawa obulamu mu nze; kyokka temugaana makubo gange. Nabawa ekigendererwa; kyokka temugaana bulamu bwe mbakoledde. Nabawa okuyitibwa, yee, okuyitibwa okwa waggulu okw'emirembe n'emirembe era okutalina nkomerero y'ennaku bwe munaakyukira nze. Munkyukire n'amaaso n'omutima omuggule, era munkkirize mbalongooseko ebibi byammwe n'obutali butuukirivu bwammwe mu musaayi gwange ogutangirira. Abo bonna be njagala, mbanenya, mulyoke muyimirire mu maaso gange nga muli batuukirivu mu bafuta embazzi enjeru entukuvu gye nja okubawa mu bbanga ttono.

Engoye zammwe zonna ze mwerondedde okwekwesanga nze tezirina mugaso wabula ebiwero eby'obugwagwa. Naye tewali muntu n'omu ayinza okwekwesa amaaso gange, era nkulaba nga bw'oli ddala, era nga bwe nakutonda nga mupya mu nze. Waliwo empeera nnene eterekeddwa abaana bange abeesigwa; naye abo abanjeegaana, nange nja kubajeegaana. Lwaki mukyakutte ensi eno efirayo n'ebizibu byayo n'obulumi bwayo? Mulina okulondawo mu nze, okulondawo emirembe emirala gye batabeerangako nga sinnafaayo ssaddaaka yange. N'olwekyo, buli anjeegaana mu biseera by'okuzza obuggya alina ekibi ekisinga obunene. Okwagala kwange gye muli kwa lubeerera era tekusobola kumenyeka. Kitange nange tuli bumu, era ebigendererwa byaffe n'enteekateeka zaffe tebyawukanika. Kitange yampa ebintu byonna ndyoke ndokole abamu.

Temutwala kifo kyammwe mu batakkiriza, kubanga nabawa ensonga nnyingi ez'okukkiriza okungi. Okukkiriza okw'akaweke ka kaladaali ke mwetaaga, okwesiga okutonotono. Mwambale engoye ze nabawa. Ssaabafukirako mwoyo gwange, mmwe ababuusabuusa? Temuba batakkiriza wabula mukkirize, kubanga ebiseera n'emyaka biri ku mmwe era tewali kudda nnyuma. Ensi gye mwamanyanga edda teridda, n'ensi gye mumanyi leero ya bukuusa bungi. Wadde ng'ebintu bikyali bya bulijjo gye muli, byonna bye mwali mukutte ennyo byatwalibwa omubi; kubanga alina essaawa eyalagirwa, essaawa singa saagikendeeza, tewandisigaddewo mubiri mulamu. Lwaki mwagala olunaku lwa Mukama olugenda okujja ku nsi okukema emitima gy'abantu? Lunaku lwa kizikiza awatali muntu n'omu ayinza okuyimirira. Lunaku lwa musango n'ebivaamu eby'entiisa, era ne mu nnaku ezisinga ekizikiza, buli muntu alikoowoola erinnya lya Mukama okutuusa ku nkomerero alirokoka.

Ebiseera n'emyaka bikyuka mu maaso gammwe, naye mukyalemererwa okukkiriza amaaso gammwe. Wadde nga nkola obubonero n'ebyamagero mu mmwe ne mu mmwe, mukyaziba amaaso gammwe ne muziba amatu gammwe mu kutegeera okutali kwa kukkiriza. Ekisa kyange n'okusaasira kwange bya mirembe na mirembe, naye ebiseera bikyuka, era omusango n'akabi bisemberera ensi gye mwagala ennyo, n'ebintu bye mukutte ennyo bijja kuyita mu ngalo zammwe ng'omusenyu. Mangu ddala ensi teriba na kuyitibwa kwa kisa na kusaasira, naye abo abalirokoka balina okugumiikiriza okutuusa ku mukka gwabwe ogusembayo okurokoka mu nze. Munkyukire mukkirize okuyita kwange, kubanga okubeera mu Kristo kwe kubeera n'enkolagana ey'obulokozi nange, Mukama wa Bakama era Kabaka wa Bakabaka. Ggwe wekka ayinza okuziyiza obulokozi bwo ng'onjeegaana mu kutakkiriza n'okugaana okuyita kwange. Lowooza ku Esawu, eyatunda obusika bwe ku lw'akamere akatonotono akaasuulibwa mu kalulu, n'awa muganda we obusika bwe mu kutakkiriza. Okunyooma ebigambo byange n'okutambulira mu kizikiza kibi, n'olwekyo temwonoona era muyimirire mu maaso gange.

Lwaki mukyeyesa abatasanyidde kisa kyange, so ng'ekyetaagisa kwe kukkiriza? Mukkirize emirimu gyange egy'okutangirira mu bulamu bwammwe era munkkirize nnyingire mbayambe mu kiseera kyammwe eky'okwetaaga, singa emitima gyammwe egitali na kukkiriza gyali ginkkiriza. Mulondeewo obulamu, kubanga leero lwe kiseera eky'okulondawo; wuliriza eddoboozi lyange so temukakanyaza mitima gyammwe mu ddungu nga bajjajja bwe baakola ne bagwa ku mabbali g'ekkubo. Temungwako; temugoberera kuzikirira na makubo amabi ag'okutakkiriza, wabula muntunuulire era mwesige eddoboozi lyange. Yee, endiga zange ziwulira eddoboozi lyange, n'olwekyo mungoberere mu bulamu. Temulowooza nti omutima ogutakkiriza guyinza okuyimirira nga mutuukirivu mu maaso gange, kubanga obutakkiriza kwe kugaana okukkiriza kwe nabawa mu nze.

Muntukuze, Mukama wammwe Yezu Kristu, mu mitima gyammwe era temutya, kubanga okulabika kwange kuli kumpi nnyo. Mwesige eddoboozi lyange era mutambulire mu bigere byange, kubanga ekkubo lye mbateereddewo litwala mu bulamu obuggya mu maaso ga Katonda. Kubanga nze yekka kkubo erigenda eri Katonda; nze kkubo, n'amazima, n'obulamu. Wadde ng'omubi asuubiza ebintu bingi, talina ky'ayinza kuwa, era tayinza kweyambula mu mukono gwange. Nedda, obusungu bungi buterekeddwa ku ye, era Sitaani alina obusika obugenda okujja obw'okwerondera kwe mu nnyanja eyaka omuliro. Abo bonna abamulondawo, bonna abakkiriza akabonero ke, bonna abasinza ensolo n'ekifaananyi kyayo balimugoberera mu busika bwe obw'obusungu bwa Katonda obutaggwaawo.

Kitange tali mutuukirivu mu kulwanirira ebitonde bye? Tali mutuukirivu mu misango gye n'amakubo ge yateeka mu maaso gammwe? N'olwekyo, mulondeewo obulamu era mulondeewo obusika bwe nabawa, kubanga nsuubiza eggulu eriggya n'ensi empya omuli obutuukirivu. Mbasuubiza obulamu obutaggwaawo, obulamu obw'obugagga bungi, emirembe n'essanyu. Naye, mu kubulwa kwammwe okukkiriza, mukyajeema. Nabakola ki okubanyiiza ne mugaana enkolagana nange, omwana wa Katonda, ne Kitange ow'ekitiibwa? Okulondawo ensi eno n'amakubo gaayo kwe kulondawo okufa, era mu nkomerero okulondawo kwa buli muntu kuliba kwa lubeerera, kubanga ebiseera by'okwawukana bituuse; n'abo abatagenda kusalawo, ensala ejja kubakolerwa.

Tewali sanyu mu kuzikirizibwa okugenda okujja okugwa ku nsi yammwe. Ebibaluko bijja kwala, n'ensi ejja kukankana, n'amayengo amanene gajja kukuba ku Babulooni ekyama; kubanga ensi ejja kuyulika mu bifo bingi. Omuliro munene gujja kwala ku nsi, n'abasuubuzi bajja kuyimirira wala olw'okutya okubonaabona kwayo. Okuzikirira okunene kuli ku malaaya, Babulooni omunene, eyatamidde ensi n'obwenzi bwe n'obukaba bwe, kubanga abadde n'abagalwa bangi. Abo abatagenda kweyawula ku ye baligabana ku musango gwe, n'olwekyo lwaki mukyalondawo okufa mu kifo ky'obulamu bwe nabawa, obwasasulirwa mu musaayi gwange ogutalina kibi? Olutalo lunaatera okujja mu nsi yammwe ne mu ggwanga lyammwe, era wadde nga lutandise dda — yee, olutalo olutalangirirwa lwe balwana nammwe — mangu ddala lujja kubuna ensi yonna ng'obubonero busumululwa.

Olutalo olunene n'okuyigganyizibwa okunene bijja kujja ku nsi nga bagezaako okuzikiriza ekintu kyonna ekya Katonda n'ekya Kristo we kubanga bajja kulangirira obulungi olutalo olutukuvu ku mmwe. Bajja kuwamba bangi, era bangi balitwalibwa, n'abo abalirokoka baliba n'okulondawo kumu: okugumiikiriza okutuusa ku nkomerero era batayagala bulamu bwabwe okutuusa ku nkomerero era bakoowoola erinnya lyange okutuusa ku nkomerero. Abakulembeze bammwe babalyamu olukwe era bajja kuwaayo bangi eri omubi mu ntalo ezijja okubalukawo. Eggwanga lyammwe lirirumbibwa okuva munda n'okuva ebweru. Amagye amamyuufu galiwo dda, era galindirira ebiragiro byago; kubanga gategeka okulumba okw'amangu, era bangi balitwalibwa. Amagye g'omubi galitwala buli kintu okuva mu nsi ku lwabwe era n'okuva mu abo abanjeegaana. Balibabba ettaka lyammwe, amayumba gammwe, n'ezzadde lyammwe; n'obulamu bungi buliggwaawo mu kutakkiriza. Olutalo lujja kuva bweru kubanga amawanga mangi gakolaganye nammwe; era balina olukusa lw'abakulembeze bammwe, n'abakulembeze bammwe bakolera kufa kwammwe. Baagala ensi yaabwe, ensi eweereddwayo eri Sitaani gye bayinza okugoberera mu bujjuvu ebibi ebibeeramu, era tewali kijja kuziyiza okwegomba kwabwe okubi, kubanga omuziyiza aliba agenze.

Bajja kwokya Babulooni ow'Ekyama n'omuliro, na buli atagenda kufa mu nnimi z'omuliro ajja kufa mu njala; buli atagenda kufa mu njala ajja kufa mu kawumpuli; buli atagenda kufa ku buwanga bw'ekitala ajja kuweebwayo eri akabaawo ku lw'erinnya lyange, era bangi balitemebwako emitwe nga Kigambo kyange Ekitukuvu bwe kirangirira. N'abo abafiira ku lw'erinnya lyange baliba n'empeera nnene, nange ndisangula amaziga gonna mu maaso gaabwe.

Mumanye, baana bange, nti eby'okulya eby'omuwendo n'amasanyu gonna ag'ekibi abantu ababi ge beeterekera bijja kubula mu bbanga, n'okubonaabona n'ennaku bijja kuyimirira mu bifo byabyo. Ekizikiza kijja kubikka ku nsi, era walibaawo enjala y'Ekigambo kyange, n'eddoboozi ly'omugole omusajja n'eddoboozi ly'omugole omukazi teririwulirwa mu ye nate.

N'olwekyo, ndituma bannabbi bange mu mmwe, kubanga nditeeka akabonero ku muwendo okuva mu bika ebisigaddewo okuweereza abo, era ebyamagero ebinene bye balijja okukola. Obubonero n'ebyamagero bingi birigabibwa mu nnaku zino ez'ekizikiza, n'abo abakoowoola erinnya lya Mukama okutuusa ku nkomerero balirokoka. Ebinyonyi byammwe birigwa. Entindo zammwe zirimmenyeka. Enjala erigwa ku nsi, era walibaawo obumenyi bw'amateeka obutagambika, era balijja kunoonya abo abayimirira ku lwange, era bangi balifiira ku lwange; naye empeera nnene etegekeddwa abo abayimirira okutuusa ku nkomerero. Lwaki mwagala olunaku lwa Mukama? Kubanga lwandizikirizza buli mubiri okuva ku nsi singa saagikendeeza ennaku. Olwo ensi erindaba nga njabika okuva mu ggulu n'amagye agali mu ggulu, kubanga ensi ya Mukama n'okujjuza kwayo.

Yee, mbagamba bino nga tebinnabaawo, si lwa kuyimirira awo nga temukkiriza, wabula lwa kuba n'okukkiriza ng'akaweke ka kaladaali, kubanga kajja kukula okusinga emiti emirala era kajja kufuuka ekitaganyulwa mu nze bwe munaŋŋanya mbalongooseko ebibi byammwe era bwe munaajja gye ndi okufuna okuwonyezebwa n'okuzzibwa buggya. Okufuuwa kwa kakondeere kuli kumpi nnyo era kunaatera okubatuukako, nange ndisika omugole wange okuva mu makkati. Nditeekawo ekifo ku mmeeza yange eri buli muntu alimfuula Mukama ng'ayanukula enzigi zaabwe; kubanga, laba, nnyimiridde ne nkonkona!

Nze Mwana wa Dawudi era ndifuga ensi okuva ku ntebe ye.

Nze kikolo kya Yese; wuliriza eddoboozi lyange!

Nze Mwana w'Omuntu; totya era ontunuulire.

Nze Mwana wa Katonda, yee, eyazaalibwa omu yekka owa Kitange.

Nze mulamuzi omutuukirivu alamula mu butukuvu n'obutuukirivu.

Nze mwana gw'endiga gwa Katonda eyalondebwa mu kisibo, atalina bbala wadde enkovu.

Nze luggi lw'endiga, era tewali misumaali giriwuuma egiyinza okuyingira.

Nze mugaati gw'obulamu; buli agulyako taliruma njala nate.

Nze mwana gw'endiga gwa Katonda asaanidde okusumulula obubonero obusiba omuzingo.
