Obujulirwa obw'okubiri: Ekkanisa
Mu biwandiiko bya Yokaana Omubikkulirwa
Ogwe Omukaaga 04, 2024

Laba! Omusango gujja n'ebiseera bituuse!

Ebiro bituuse okutuukirira, mmwe abaana bange abato, okutuukirira kw'ebintu byonna ebikwata ku sabiiti ya Daniyeri ey'amakumi musanvu. Kubanga sabiiti ya Daniyeri ey'amakumi musanvu ye sabiiti y'emyaka, nga buli lunaku luba mwaka. Sabiiti ya Daniyeri ey'amakumi musanvu eneetera okutuukirira era ejja kutandika ng'abeesigwa mu Kkanisa yange basitunulibwa okunsisinkana mu bire. Ekkondeere ery'enkomerero lijja kuvuga, era n'eddoboozi eddene erya malayika omukulu, abafu mu Kristo balizuukira olubereberye, nammwe abali abalamu abalisigalawo mulinyagulirwa mu bire awamu nabo, era bwe mutyo mulibeeranga nange ennaku zonna, ye nze Kristo Yesu, kubanga nze Omunazaaleeti eyazuukira, eyafiira ekibi omulundi gumu era atakyalwa kufa. Kubanga obwakabaka bwange bwa lubeerera, era obwakabaka bwange tebuliggwaawo, kubanga Yawe bw'atyo bwe yalangirira.

Abange abeesigwa bwe balitwalibwa mu ggulu, era n'omuziyiza aliggyibwawo, era sabiiti ya Daniyeri ey'amakumi musanvu n'etyo eriyita etandika. Bangi ku mmwe mutawaanyizibwa n'endowooza yange, Yesuwa Hamasiya, okunyagula abange abeesigwa okuva mu musango ogutabagwanidde, naye nzija kubategeeza, bwe munaaba mujjidde gye ndi n'obuwombeefu bw'omwana omuto, n'obutamanya kabi n'okwagala okumanya okw'omwana omuto. Kubanga mmwe muli baana bange abato, abaana bange abato abalaba mu ndabirwamu etalaga bulungi era abalagula ekitundu. Era bwe ntyo mbagamba nti, mwetoowaze mwenenye obumalaki bwammwe obutagasa, kubanga kibi, mujje gye ndi muyige era mukkirize ebyawandiikibwa ebitukuvu. Okugaana ekitundu kyonna eky'ebyawandiikibwa kibi, kubanga buli Kigambo kya Katonda kirongoofu era kirina okukkirizibwa n'ekitiibwa eky'obuwulize, era nze, Yesu Omunazaaleeti, nze kigambo ekyefuula omubiri.

Okukungaanyizibwa kw'abatukuvu bange gye ndi mu bire kyali kyama ekikwekeddwa munda mw'ekyama ekikulu ekya Katonda. N'olwekyo kyali kyama munda mwa kyama era tekyabikkulwa okutuusa lwe kyabikkulirwa Omutume Pawulo mu bbaluwa ze. Gy'ali ekyaama ekikulu ekya Katonda gye kyabikkulirwa, kubanga ne Pawulo yali musigire w'ebyama bya Katonda, kubanga nze bwe ntyo bwe nnamutuma. Kale mutegeere nti okwegaana ekyawandiikibwa ku kitundu kino kwe kwenneegaana nze era n'okuba mu bawala abatayina magezi.

N'olwekyo njakubannyonnyola ebintu ebingi ebinaatera okubaawo, nga n'okweyongerayo n'emirimu gye nnawa Yokaana Omubikkulirwa, kubanga obujulirwa bw'okubikkulwa kwange bwawandiikibwa okukisa nga bwe bubikkula. N'olwekyo kaakano nja kubabuulira ebintu nga tebinnabaawo mulyoke mweteekereteekere ennaku ezijja. Kubanga nze, Kristo Yesu, nja kuleetera abantu bange okuyimirira mu mirimu gye mbatuma mulyoke mutuukirize mu bujjuvu emirimu emingi egy'amaanyi gye mbateekeddeteekedde okukola.

Ng'okukuŋŋaanyizibwa kugenda mu maaso, omuziyiza aggyibwawo, era okuzikirizibwa okungi kuligwa ku nsi, amangu ago mu kaseera katono. Kubanga omuziyiza bw'aliggibwawo, tewaliba kisigaddewo okuziyiza obubi mu nsi n'okwegomba n'okwagala okubi okw'abantu. Olutalo olunene lulityemuka okwetooloola ensi, era ow'oluganda aliddayo okutta muganda we, n'okuzikirizibwa kwabwe kuliba kungi. Amawanga mangi galigwa nga Babulooni ow'Ekyama, ye malaaya omukulu atuula ku mazzi amangi, alifuulibwa matongo mu ssaawa emu.

Ekibuga ekyo ekikulu, ye ggwanga eryo eririmu Amawanga Amagatte amangi, kirizikirizibwa ddala; kubanga kisasanyizza omugabo gw'omusango ogw'emirundi ebiri, era kigugwanidde. Kubanga kyabeera mu kibi ekingi mu ngeri ey'okwesanyusa; kituula mu malala amangi nga kirowooza nti tekiyinza kugwa era ebintu biryeyongera, eggulo nga leero, n'enkya nga kye kimu era nga tebikyuka. Era malaaya omukulu ayonoonye ensi n'ekikopo ekya zaabu ky'alina mu ngalo, ekijjudde emizizo n'obugwagwa bw'obwenzi bwe. Kale n'omusango gwe guligwa mangu. Omuliro omungi gulitwala ebibuga bye ng'emizungirwa gigwa; walibaawo okukankana okw'amaanyi, n'eggwanga eryo liriyatikamu wakati. Ennyanja zaalyo zirijjula amazzi mangi, so teririddayo kugolokoka, kubanga ebibi byalyo bingi n'omusango gwalyo mungi.

Olutalo bwe lulityemuka mu nsi n'abeebagadde embalaasi ne batuukiriza emisango gyabwe ku nsi, walibaawo okubonaabona n'okuzikirira okungi mu nsi. Ensi eriwo kaakano teriddamu kubeerawo; omuziyiza bw'aliggibwawo, buli kintu kiriba kya njawulo. Walibaawo okufa okungi, kawumpuli, enjala ennyingi, n'obutali bwenkanya bungi; naye abange emitwalo kkumi n'ena mu enkumi nnya (144,000) balikuŋŋunizibwa okuva ku busungu n'omusango, kubanga baliba n'akabonero ka Katonda Omujololo, ye Yawe, mu byenyi byabwe; kubanga bwe batyo bwe baliteekebwako akabonero, era ntuma bamalayika bange okubateekako akabonero. N'olwekyo enkola ey'akavuyo n'obutawulira mateeka birifuga olunaku. Emyeezi 42 egisooka giriba biseera bya kuyigganyizibwa kungi n'okubonaabona okungi, naye ekibonyoobonyo ekinene kiribaawo mu myeezi 42 egy'okubiri: kubanga waliwo emyeezi 84 mu nnaku omusanvu ez'emyaka gy'okubonaabona kwa Yakobo. N'olwekyo ekitundu eky'okubiri kye kibonyoobonyo ekinene, naye okubonaabona n'okuyigganyizibwa mu nsi kuliba kwa ntiisa eri abagibeeramu mu kiseera ekyo kyonna.

Kubanga nze, Kristo Yesu Omukama eyalinnya mu ggulu, newakubadde Yawe, Katonda omukulu atalina kibi (kubanga tewali amufaanana), ffenna tetusanyukira misango na bizibu ebigenda okujja. Kitange teyatonda bantu mu kwagala asobole okubasalira omusango n'okubazikiriza. Naye, Sitaani ayonoonye olusuku lwa Katonda olw'ekisa, era kaakano obwonoonefu bujja kulongoosebwawo obutuukirivu bulyoke bufuge. N'olwekyo tewali kulondawo kulala eri abo abaalemwa okukkiriza okuyita kwange okw'obulokozi mu mulembe gw'ekisa. Abantu balina okuzuukuka mu tulo n'essanyu ly'ekibi, kubanga tewakyali kiseera era kaakano ke kaseera k'okulondawo okw'enkalakkalira, oba okusalawo kulikolebwa mu musango eri abo abanneegaana nga tebaagala kulondawo. Ebizibu ebingi n'emisango egijja birizuukusa bangi okufuna obulokozi obw'olubeerera, nga bibaggya ne mu muliro ogutazikira; kubanga bangi batamidde essanyu ly'ekibi, n'olwekyo essanyu liriggyibwawo. Tebaliba na ssanyu lya ngeri yonna mu nnaku ezo, era okuwonawo kuliba kwa buli ddakiika ebiseera ebimu, n'akabi kangi kalibaawo.

Bwe kityo kiriba nti abange abeesigwa era abalonde mu Kkanisa yange balikusibwa mu bbanga okuva mu biseera by'okubonaabona kwa Yakobo mu ggulu. N'emirimu gyabwe girisalirwa omusango n'omuliro, era oyo yenna emirimu gye gye girisigalawo alifuna empeera n'oyo yenna emirimu gye gye giriggya alifuna okufiirwa, naye bo bennyini balirokokka, bwe kityo nga bayise mu muliro. Balitwala ebifo byabwe mu nteekateeka eyabateekerwateekerwa mu ggulu. Kubanga nze, Yesuwa Hamasiya, nze nsaanidde okutwala ekitabo n'okumenya obubonero, era n'okumenyebwa kw'obubonero abeebagadde embalaasi ne basumululwa. Era baligenda mu nsi okuwamba, okulwana, okuleeta kawumpuli n'okufa okungi. Amakondeere omusanvu galivuga, ennaku zijja, n'ebibya omusanvu eby'obusungu bwa Katonda birifukibwa ku nsi mu bujjuvu.

Olutalo lw'ensi yonna olugenda okujja terulimala myaka mingi, kubanga Sitaani amanyi nti alina akaseera katono, naye olutalo lulibeerawo mu biseera bino eby'okubonaabona. Mu matongo n'entiisa y'olutalo muliva ensolo eva mu nnyanja, n'ensolo eva mu nsi, oyo ye nnabbi ow'obulimba. Bino byombi birikolera wamu, nga bwe bikolera wamu mu kyama okuzikiriza Babulooni ow'Ekyama. N'amayembe ekkumi, gano galikyawa malaaya, ne gamufuula matongo, era nga ali bwereere, ne galya ennyama ye, ne gamwokya omuliro. Kubanga Katonda yateeka mu mitima gyabwe okutuukiriza okwagala kwe, n'okukkiriza, n'okuwa ensolo obwakabaka bwabwe, okutuusa ebigambo bya Katonda lwe birituukiririzibwa. (Era bwe kityo abeebagadde embalaasi balyebagala.)

Baligolokoka nga baleeta emirembe, obutebenkevu, n'obutiibwa Katonda obw'obulimba. Baligaana buli kintu ekikwata ku nze, Yesu Kristo, okwetooloola ensi yonna, era balirwana n'abatukuvu ne babawangula, kubanga kiweereddwa mu mikono gyabwe okukikola. Abagenyi baabwe abayitibwa ab'oku bbanga balirabika ne bawa obuyambi bwabwe eri abantu, naye bano be bamalayika abagwa abaleeta obulimba obungi. Laba, ndeeta ebisolo ebikulu okutuukiriza obusungu bwange, era bw'atyo Yawe bw'alangiridde. Nga bwe kyali mu nnaku za Nuuwa, bwe kityo bwe kiriba eri bamalayika abagwa ab'edda; n'abo abayitibwa bakatonda ab'edda (si bakatonda) balinoonya okuddamu okuzimba omulembe gwabwe ogwa zaabu ku nsi, era basumululwa lwa musango, naye tebaliba na mulembe gwa zaabu. Emizizo gyabwe girisumululwa, kubanga omuziyiza aliba agenze era ng'aggyiddwawo ddala, so tewaliba kibaziyiza. Abo abaagala obulamu bwabwe balibufiirwa emirembe gyonna, kubanga balyetaaga akabonero okwetaba mu nsi yaabwe empya, ye ntegeka yaabwe empya. Naabo bonna abalitwala akabonero k'ensolo balifuna omugabo gwabwe mu nnyanja y'omuliro.

Abange 144,000 tebatumibwa kubeera mu bibuga byabwe, gye bandyagibiddwa okutwala akabonero okusobola okubeerawo. Naye balitumibwa okudda n'okudda era balikuŋŋaanya bangi gye bali ne balabirira bangi era baliyamba okuleeta ebibiina ebingi mu bulokozi gye ndi, Yesuwa Hamasiya (mu lulimi lwonna): bonna abalikoowoola erinnya lyange okutuusa ku nkomerero balirokokka. Katonda alisangula amaziga gonna mu maaso gaabwe; so tekulibaawo kufa nate, newakubadde okunakuwala, newakubadde okukaaba, so tekulibaawo kubalagalaba nate: kubanga eby'olubereberye biweddewo. Nga bwe kyali mu nnaku za Nuuwa, abo abatya Katonda balibeera bweru w'ensi yaabwe embi ey'abamalayika abagwa, kubanga eyabwe eriba nsi ya kibi etwala mu kuzikirira okw'emirembe n'emirembe mu muliro ogutazikira; kale temwegattanga nabo kubanga okusalawo kwa lubeerera. Kale abange 144,000 balireeta obulokozi n'obuyambi eri bangi.

Balikola obubonero obw'amaanyi, eby'amagero, n'ebyamawnunyi, era nja kubakulembera nnamu, era nja kulungamya amakubo gaabwe eri abo be mbatuma. Era nja kukuŋŋaanya endiga zange gye bali zisobole okukulemberwa mu butebenkevu. Na buli muntu atayagala bulamu bwe okutuusa ku nkomerero alirokolebwa. N'abo ab'amagezi balimasamasa ng'okumasamasa kw'eggulu; n'abo abakyusa abangi eri obutuukirivu ng'emmunyeenye emirembe n'emirembe.

Ensolo evva mu nnyanja erigolokoka era eriweereza ebigendererwa ebibi by'abo abafuga ensi ey'ekisuubizo mu kibi ekingi, ye sinagoga ya Sitaani. Erifuga amawanga era eyogere eby'okuvvoola bingi, era ekole obubonero obw'obulimba obw'amaanyi n'ebyamawnunyi, ne basinza ogusota ogwawa ensolo amaanyi, ne basinza ensolo, nga boogera nti, Ani afaanana ensolo? Ani ayinza okulwana nayo? Sinagoga ya Sitaani erikola byonna by'esobola okuzikiriza buli akoowoola erinnya lyange, n'ensolo ne nnabbi ow'obulimba baliteeka mu nkola n'obukambwe amateeka ga Nuuwa ku mawanga era balirabika ng'abalongoosa obubi okuva mu nsi. Naye mu kifo ky'ekyo balirwana n'abatukuvu ne babawangula, era kiweereddwa ensolo eva mu nnyanja okweyongerayo emyeezi 42.

Ensolo evva mu nsi nayo eriva mu vvu. Erina amaanyi gonna ag'ensolo eyasooka, era erikola obubonero obw'obulimba obungi n'ebyamawnunyi mu maaso g'ensolo eyasooka, era erikola ekifaananyi ky'ensolo, era omwoyo gulikkirizibwa okuleetera ekifaananyi ky'ensolo okwogera, nga kigamba nti buli atavunnama n'asinza ekifaananyi ky'ensolo eyasooka attebwe.

Kubanga ensolo eva mu nsi balimulongoosa kabaka w'ensi ez'ekisuubizo, era ye aliba Moshiach gwe baludde nga balindirira, era alizimba yeekaalu yaabwe (newankubadde Yawe teyamutuma), era bwe kityo bwe kiriba. Balimukkiriza ng'omufuzi omukulu, omulungi, nnabbi, era kabaka. N'ensolo evva mu nsi (ye nnabbi waabwe ow'obulimba) erireetera bonna, abato n'abakulu, abagagga n'abaavu, ab'eddembe n'abaddu, okufuna akabonero mu mukono gwabwe ogwa ddyo oba mu byenyi byabwe, Era nti tewali muntu ayinza kugula wadde okutunda, wabula oyo alina akabonero, oba erinnya ly'ensolo, oba omuwendo gw'erinnya lyayo. Baakayigiriza n'ensi okukkiriza akabonero, n'entegeka yakakasibwa mu kiseera ky'ekirwadde kyabwe ekiyitibwa.

(Kyawandiikibwa mu Kubikkulirwa nti: Malayika ow'okusatu n'abakoberera, ng'agamba n'eddoboozi ddene nti, Omuntu yenna bw'asinza ensolo n'ekifaananyi kyayo, n'ateekebwako akabonero kaayo ku kyenyi kye, oba ku mukono gwe, Oyo alinywera ddala ku vviini ey'obusungu bwa Katonda, efukiddwa awatali kutabulwa mu kikompe ky'obusungu bwe; era alibonyaabonyezebwa n'omuliro n'ekibiriiti mu maaso ga bamalayika abatukuvu, ne mu maaso g'Omwana gw'endiga: N'omukka gw'okubonyaabonyezebwa kwabwe ne gunyooka emirembe n'emirembe: so tebawummula emisana n'ekiro, abo abasinza ensolo n'ekifaananyi kyayo, na buli ateekebwako akabonero k'erinnya lyayo.)

Mu myeezi 42 egisooka, nja kuwa amaanyi abajulirwa bange ababiri, era baliragula ennaku lukumi mu bibiri mu nkaaga, nga bambadde ebibukutu. Bano be miti emizeyituuni ebiri, n'ebikondo by'ettaala ebibiri ebiyimiridde mu maaso ga Katonda w'ensi. Era omuntu yenna bw'ayagala okubaluma, omuliro guva mu kamwa kaabwe, ne gumalawo abalabe baabwe: era omuntu yenna bw'ayagala okubaluma, alina okuttibwa bw'atyo. Bano balina obuyinza okuggala eggulu, lireme okutonnya mu nnaku z'okulagula kwabwe: era balina obuyinza ku mazzi okugafuula omusaayi, n'okukuba ensi n'ebirwadde byonna, emirundi gyonna gye baliba baagadde. Era bwe balimala obujulirwa bwabwe, ensolo eva mu bunnya obutakoma erirwana nabo, n'ebawangula, n'ebatta.

Ensolo eva mu nsi, ye nnabbi waabwe ow'obulimba, mazima ddala erireeta omuzizo oguzikiriza. Eriziyiza ssaddaaka eya buli lunaku n'eteekawo omuzizo oguzikiriza mu kifo kyaazo. N'olwekyo nnagamba era Matayo n'awandiika nti: Bwe muliraba omuzizo oguzikiriza, ogwayogerwako nnabbi Daniyeri, nga guyimiridde mu kifo ekitukuvu (asoma ategeere), Kale abo abali mu Buyudaaya baddukire mu nsozi: Oyo ali ku nnyumba aleme okukka okuggyamu ekintu kyonna mu nnyumba ye: Newakubadde oyo ali mu nnimiro aleme okuddayo okudda okutwala engoye ze. Ne zibasanze abo abaliba n'embuto, n'abo abayonseza mu nnaku ezo! Naye musabe okudduka kwammwe kuleme okuba mu biseera by'obutiti, newakubadde ku ssabbiiti: Kubanga mu kiseera ekyo kuliba okubonaabona okungi, okutaliwo okuva ensi lwe yatondebwa okutuusa ku kiseera kino, nedda, so tekulibaawo nate.

(Balina omukisa abafu abafiira mu Mukama okuva leero: Weewaawo, bw'ayogera Omwoyo, bawummule emirimu gyabwe; n'emirimu gyabwe gibagoberera.)

N'olwekyo nja kukuuma bangi ne nkwega bangi mu ddungu okuva mu kibonyoobonyo ekinene, kubanga singa ennaku tezaali mpanvu, tewandibaddewo mubiri guwona. Awo ensolo ne nnabbi ow'obulimba balikungaanya bakabaka b'ensi na bonna abayimiridde nabo okuva mu bwakabaka bwa ggusagalira olunaku olwo olukulu olw'olutalo. Era bwe ntyo bwe nnagamba Yokaana Omubikkulirwa okuwandiika: Ne ndaba eggulu nga libikkuse, era laba, embalaasi enjeru; n'oyo eyali agituddeko yayitibwa Mwesigwa era ow'Amazima, era mu butuukirivu asala omusango era alwana. Amaaso ge gaali ng'olulimi lw'omuliro, ne ku mutwe gwe kwaliko engule nnyingi; era yalina erinnya eriwandiikiddwa, eritamanyiddwa muntu yenna, wabula ye yekka. Era yali ayambadde olugoye olunnyikiddwa mu musaayi: n'erinnya lye liyitibwa Kigambo kya Katonda. N'eggye eryali mu ggulu ne limugoberera ku mbalaasi enjeru, nga lyambadde bafuta enjeru ennungi. Ne mu kamwa ke ne muva ekitala eky'obwogi, alyoke akube amawanga: era alibafuga n'omuggo ogw'ekyuma: era alinnyirira essogolero ly'effujjo n'obusungu bwa Katonda Omuyinza w'ebintu byonna.

N'ensolo ne nnabbi ow'obulimba bombi balikwatibwa nga balamu ne basuulibwa mu nnyanja y'omuliro eyaka n'ekibiriiti. N'abantu abasigaddewo ne battibwa n'ekitala ky'oyo eyali atudde ku mbalaasi, ekyo ekitala ekyava mu kamwa ke: n'ennyonyi zonna ne zikkuta ennyama yaabwe. Ne ndaba entebe z'obwakabaka, ne batuula ku zo, ne baweebwa omusango: ne ndaba emyoyo gy'abo abaasalibwako emitwe olw'obujulirwa bwa Yesu, n'olw'ekigambo kya Katonda, era abataasinzanga nsolo, newakubadde ekifaananyi kyayo, newakubadde abaateekebwangako akabonero kaayo ku byenyi byabwe, oba mu ngalo zaabwe; ne baba balamu ne bafuga ne Kristo emyaka lukumi.

Awo malayika wange alikka okuva mu ggulu, ng'alina ekisumuluzo ky'obunnya obutakoma n'olujegere olunene lw'alisibisa Sitaani mu bunnya obutakoma okumala emyaka lukumi n'ateekawo akabonero aleme okukyusa amawanga emyaka lukumi. Ku nkomerero y'obufuzi bwange obw'emyaka lukumi, Sitaani aliyimbulwa okuva mu bunnya okumala akaseera katono.

Aligenda okukyusa amawanga mu nsonda ennya ez'ensi, Gogi ne Magogi okubakungaanya wamu ng'omusenyu gw'ennyanja, era alirumba olusiisira lw'abatukuvu n'azingiza ekibuga ekitukuvu, n'omuliro gulikka okuva eri Yawe ne gubookya ddala. N'omulyolyomi eyabakyusa alisibwa n'asuulibwa ng'omulamu mu nnyanja ey'omuliro, eyaka n'ekibiriiti mu nnimi ezisinga okwokya eza bbulu. Era walibaawo okusasulwa okungi okw'obubi n'obwenkanya obungi buliweebwa ku mutwe gwa Sitaani yennyini, bamalayika be abagwa, na bonna abeegatta ku bujeemu bwabwe; so tebaligolokoka nate, era balyerabirwa ddala; so tebalijjira nate mu birowoozo.

Awo Yawe alitonda eggulu eriggya n'ensi empya, era aliteeka ennyumba ye mu bantu, era mu mirembe n'obumu mulibeera emirembe gyonna, so tekulibaawo nate kiseera kya bizibu, so tebalyonoona nate, newakubadde olw'amalala newakubadde olw'obutatya Katonda, n'ennaku zaabwe zonna ziriba za mukisa.

Nze Omunazaaleeti eyazuukira, eyafiira ekibi omulundi gumu era atakyalwa kufa.

Nze Yesuwa, ye Masiya wammwe ow'amazima, Yawe gw'atutumye okutuukiriza ebisuubizo ebyakolebwa eri bajjajjaffe.

Nze oyo amaaso ge ge nnimi z'omuliro, ne ku mutwe gwange engule nnyingi.

Nze Mukama wa Bakama era Kabaka wa Bakabaka, era mu butuukirivu njija okusala omusango n'okulwana.

Nze oyo alirungamya n'akulembera abange 144,000, era baliyimba oluyimba oluggya olutayigirizika muntu yenna.

Nze oyo aliwangula obwakabaka bwa Sitaani, era nja kumubetenta omutwe.

Nze oyo Yawe gw'atutumye olw'obulokozi bwa bonna abakkiriza, n'ekiseera kyange kituuse. Laba! Omusango gujja n'ebiseera bituuse.
