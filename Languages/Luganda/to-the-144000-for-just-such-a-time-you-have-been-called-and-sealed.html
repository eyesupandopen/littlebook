Omujulirwa Ow'okubiri: Ekkanisa
Mu Biwandiiko bya Yokaana Omubikkulirwa
Juuni 01, 2024

Eri Abakakadde n'Enkumi Annya mu Bina: Kubanga ku Biseera ng'ebino mwe mwayitirwa ne muteekerwako akabonero

Muyimirire ku lwange era mugoberere ekyokulabirako kyange, kubanga Abaebbulaniya boogera ki ku kuyimirira kwange eri Yahweh? Oyo olw'essanyu eryateekebwa mu maaso ge, eyagumiikiriza omusalaba, n'anyooma ensonyi, era atuula ku mukono ogwa ddyo ogw'entebe ya Katonda. Noolwekyo mu kwagala kwange eri Kitange, okutayinza kumenyeka, ne mu kuwulira kwange olw'okwagala, ne mu kwagala kwange eri abantu n'okwegomba kwange okwewaayo okubalokola, nagumiikiriza ekibonerezo eky'entiisa, obulumi obungi, n'okubonaabona era nakikola kyeyagalire, n'essanyu mu mutima gwange. Namanya nti olw'okuwaayo kwange n'okufa kwange, bangi baalifuulibwa abatuukirivu era abalamu olw'amaanyi g'okuzuukira kwange kubanga Yahweh yennyini yanzuukiza mu bafu, kubanga tekyasoboka kufa kundema. Era n'amaanyi ago gennyini, nammwe bwe mutyo mulizuukizibwa mu bulamu obuggya, kubanga nasaasula omuwendo bwe nnawaayo omusaayi gwange ogutalina kibi ng'ekinunulo kya bonna abalikkiriza mu linnya lyange, na buli muntu alikoowoola erinnya lya Mukama alirokolebwa, abataagala bulamu bwabwe okutuusa ku nkomerero!

Era kati mbabuuza mmwe, abeesigwa bange era abalonde, n'oluganda lwange lwennyini, munyimirire ku lwange so temwesulanga ennyuma. Kubanga nnategeeza lwatu mu kiwandiiko kyange eri Abaebbulaniya nti: Naye omutuukirivu aliba mulamu olw'okukkiriza: naye omuntu yenna bw'aneesulanga ennyuma, emmeeme yange terisanyukira mu ye.

Ekyo mukitegeere, era mukisome nate, so temwesulanga ennyuma eri Tora, kubanga Tora ekwatibwa lwa kukkiriza mu nze; kyekiva kirema omutuukirivu aliba mulamu lwa kukkiriza. Muli wamu nange, era nze eyabatuukiriza mu bikolwa byange ebitangirira n'omusaayi gwange ogutalina kibi.

Bangi nnyo ku mmwe mulwana n'endowooza nti nnatwala ekifo kyammwe mu kusinziirwa ku muti olw'ebibi bye saakola, era nakikola kyeyagalire era n'essanyu okuleetera omuntu ow'obutonde eyayonooneka obulokozi, ndyoke mbakomyeewo eri Yahweh, ye Katonda wammwe era Kitammwe, ye Kitange ow'Ebitangaala atalina kukyuka newakubadde ekisiikirize ky'okukyama. Kubanga Yahweh takyuka; ye w'ejjo, ne leero, n'emirembe gyonna!

Ye Katonda yennyini bakitammwe gwe baakkiriziganya naye mu ndagaano, ku lusozi Sinaayi, Musa bwe yafuna amateeka okuva eri Yahweh yennyini. Era wadde amateeka matukuvu, n'etteeka ttukuvu, era ttuufu, era ddungi, omuntu teyasobola n'akamu kutuukiriza mitindo gyago kubanga amateeka gaweebwa okulumiriza ekibi mu mubiri, era mmwe mwenna muzaalibwa nga mufudde mu kusobya n'ebibi kubanga ekibi kiri mu mubiri gwammwe. Tora teyaweebwa bantu batuukirivu wabula abaali abatalina butuukirivu, ekibi kiryoke kirabike ng'ekibi mu mubiri. Tora yaweebwa ng'omusomesa wammwe okubaleeta gyendi, ye Kristo Yesu, Mukama wa bakama era Kabaka wa bakabaka eyalinnyisibwa!

Noolwekyo neetikka ebyava mu kibi kyeyagalire, kubanga bwe ntyo bwe nnazaalibwa okununula abantu okuva mu kikolimo ky'amateeka. Kubanga amateeka tegaakolimirwa; yee, amateeka gayimirire na buli muntu akolimirwe avuma amateeka ga Musa, kubanga sajja kumenya mateeka, wabula kuganyweza era n'okusingawo amateeka ga Musa. Oyogera nti kuvvoola?

Obujulizi bw'Abaebbulaniya buyimiridde wakati waffe nga butulamula: Kubanga oba ng'omusaayi gw'ente ennume n'embuzi ennume, n'evvu ly'ente embooko erimansibwa ku abo abatalina buyonjo, bitukuza okutuusa okulongoosa omubiri: Nga gulisinga nnyo omusaayi gwa Kristo, eyeyawaayo yekka awatali bbala eri Katonda ku bw'Omwoyo ogw'emirembe n'emirembe, okulongoosa omwoyo gwammwe okuva mu bikolwa ebifu okuba nga muweereza Katonda omulamu?

Olw'okuba mwagala Musa (nga nange bwe mmwagala), mbakubiriza okuwuliriza n'okwagala ebiragiro bya Musa; kubanga yagamba nti: Yahweh Elohim wammwe alibayimusiza Nnabbi okuva mu makkati gammwe, mu booluganda lwammwe, alifaanana nange; oyo mulimuwuliriza. Kale muwulire eddoboozi lya Yeshua HaMashiach! Mwalongoosebwa okuva mu kibi n'ebibi byonna bye mukola olw'omusaayi gwange ogutangirira ogutalina kibi gwe nnawaayo eri Yahweh ng'ekinunulo ky'obulamu bwammwe, era mbazizza n'okubaggya mu mukono gw'omulabe. Temwonoonanga! Era omuntu yenna bw'ayonoonanga, mwete ebiri byammwe gyendi, kubanga nze abalongoosa mu musaayi gwange ogutangirira.

Ssaatangirira bibi byammwe n'ebivaamu ebiri mu byo n'embuzi ey'okuweerera, so saawaayo mwana gwa ndiga gwa mwaka gumu; kubanga nze Mwana gw'Endiga gwa Katonda gw'omwaka ogusooka, era nze eyeyawaayo awatali bbala n'obutaliiko nkovu, n'okuba omutukuvu, omuyonjo, era eyawulibwa eri Yahweh yennyini (Kitange) olw'ebibi byammwe omulundi gumu emirembe gyonna. Nnatangirira ebibi byammwe mu musaayi gwange ogutalina kibi; kyekiva kirema tewakyali ssaddaaka ya bibi. Era okunjeemera, nze Yeshua HaMashiach, y'endagaano y'emirembe etuukiridde eya Yahweh eri abantu, kwe kwegaana Yahweh n'okwegatta ku bujeemu bwa Setaani, kubanga Setaani ajeemera enteekateeka ya Katonda okuva ku lubereberye.

Kubanga nagamba Abaebbulaniya nti: Kubanga bwe twonoonanga kyeyagalire nga tumaze okufuna okumanya amazima, tewakyali ssaddaaka ya bibi. Noolwekyo okwonoona kyeyagalire ng'ogaana omusaayi gwange ogutangirira, n'olwekyo okugaana endagaano y'emirembe eyaweerezebwa Yahweh, kibi nnyo, era tewakyali ssaddaaka. Kubanga kyawandiikibwa mu Abaebbulaniya nti: Oyo eyanyooma amateeka ga Musa yattibwa awatali kusaasirwa mu bujulizi bw'abantu babiri oba basatu: Nga mulowooza alisaanira kibonerezo kinene kitya, oyo eyalinnyirira Omwana wa Katonda wansi w'ebigere bye, n'abala omusaayi gw'endagaano, gwe yatukuzibwamu, okuba ekintu ekitali kitukuvu, n'avvoola Omwoyo ow'ekisa?

So mbakubiriza mwenna okukkiriza ssaddaaka yange era mutegeere nti tewakyali ssaddaaka zeetaagisa era waliwo Masiya omu yekka ow'amazima, ye nze, Yeshua HaMashiach. Era kyawandiikibwa ku nze nti: Naye omusajja ono, bwe yamala okuwaayo ssaddaaka emu olw'ebibi emirembe gyonna, n'atuula ku mukono ogwa ddyo ogwa Katonda; Okuva olwo ng'alindirira okutuusa abalabe be lwe balifuulibwa entebbe y'ebigere bye. Kubanga olw'okuwaayo okumu, yafuula abaatuukirizibwa okuba abatuukiridde emirembe gyonna.

N'olwekyo tegeera nti nkubalongoosezza mu byonna n'enkulaakulana zonna ezikwata ku kibi n'ebivaamu. Mwatukuzibwa mu nze; mwatuukirizibwa mu nze; mwafuulibwa obutuukirivu bwa Katonda mu nze; era mbazizza ddala gyendi, nze Yeshua HaMashiach, Yahweh gwe yatumye okuba Masiya wammwe. Noolwekyo tegeera nti tewakyaliwo Bakabona Abakulu Abalawi, era Obwakabona bwa Alooni bwajja ne buggwaawo, kubanga bwali kisiikirize ky'ebintu ebyali bigenda okujja. Kubanga nze kabona wammwe omukulu emirembe n'emirembe ng'engeri ya Merukizeddeeki bwe yali.

Kubanga amateeka tegaafuula kintu na kimu kutuukirira: naye gaali nnyanjula ya ssuubi eddungi erisingawo, essuubi lye tusemberera okumpi ne Yahweh. Era nate: Kabona Omukulu ng'oyo kyatusaanira okuba naye, omutukuvu, atalina kabi, atalina bbala, eyayawulibwa ku boonoonyi, era eyafuulibwa okuba waggulu w'eggulu. Era nate: Kubanga amateeka gafuula abantu okuba bakabona, abalina obunafu: naye ekigambo ky'ekirayiro ekyajja oluvannyuma lw'amateeka, kifuula omwana okuba kabona, oyo atuukiridde emirembe n'emirembe. Temusomangako ebyawandiikibwa bye byogera ku nze? Yahweh yalayira era talyejjusa: Ggwe oli Kabona emirembe gyonna ng'engeri ya Merukizeddeeki bwe yali.

Tegeera nti tewali kibula mu ssaddaaka yange, era muli banywevu mu nze, Kristo Yesu. Tora ekwatibwa lwa kukkiriza mu nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, era tekisoboka mmwe kumenya mateeka ga Katonda gonna nga mutambulira mu nze, Kristo Yesu. Kubanga mutambulira mu tteeka ly'Omwoyo ow'obulamu, era mbafudde ba ddembe okuva mu tteeka ly'ekibi n'okufa. Noolwekyo mube n'okukkiriza mu nze, kubanga omutuukirivu aliba mulamu olw'okukkiriza. Nnina emirimu mingi egy'amaanyi gye mbayitiye, era muliyamba okuleeta obulokozi eri abangi. Waliwo essuubi eddene n'empeera ebategekeddwa mu ggulu olw'okuyimirira kwammwe n'obwesigwa bwammwe.

Kubanga mbatuma, nga muteereddwako akabonero mu byenyi byammwe ak'akabonero ka Yahweh yennyini, mu nsi ejja okubakyawa ddala, era si kya kitalo, kubanga ensi yankyawa nnyo okusooka, era omuweereza tasinga mukama we. Mmwe bajulirwa bange era bannabbi bange n'abawonya bange n'abagabi bange. Mbatuma mu kizikiza ekisinga obuzikiza ekyali kibisse ku kitonde kya Yahweh. Kubanga ku kiseera ng'ekino mwayitirwa, ne mulondebwa, ne muteekerwako akabonero, era ebiseera bino bijja kuba bya bujeemu n'obubi ensi by'etannalaba, n'okusinga ennyo ennaku za Nuuwa. Mbatuma wakati mu bo, era mbagamba nti temutyanga! Temutyanga kutisibwatisibwa kwabwe; temutyanga ekyo omuntu ky'ayinza okubakola; temutyanga maaso gaabwe; temutyanga myoyo egibawa amaanyi; temutyanga nsolo na nnabbi ow'obulimba; era temutyanga Setaani yennyini: N'ogusota ogwo omunene. Kubanga mulirinnyirira buli kikwata ku bwakabaka bwa Setaani, so tewali kintu na kimu kiribalemesanga bwe munywera ku ddoboozi lyange ne munkkiriza okubalung'amya n'erinnya ku ddaala. Kubanga mu mazima, muli bakozi bannange mu kiseera kino eky'okunakuwala kwa Yakobo, era mbawadde amaanyi.

Muli ba maanyi era ba mpanvu, era mbatuma mu bwetoowaze n'obuwombeefu. Mbatuma okukwata amateeka olw'okukkiriza kwammwe mu nze, era mube n'okwagala Yahweh n'omutima gwammwe gwonna, n'obulamu bwammwe bwonna, n'amagezi gammwe gonna, n'amaanyi gammwe gonna, era mwagale muliraanwa wo nga bwe weeyagala wekka. Muliraanwa wo y'ani? Bonna abeetaaga obuyambi bwammwe be mbatumye, era sibakoma ku nsalo za mawanga. Mugende mulunde ekisibo kyange, kubanga nze Musumba Omukulu alina empeera yammwe mu ngalo zange olw'obwesigwa. Mugende mulabirire ekisibo kyange, muwonye ebiwundu byakyo, mukiriise, mukirongoose, mukikulembere, era okusinga byonna mukindeetere; kubanga mmwe mwekka temuli kintu, naye mu nze, muli basajja bange abaamaanyi, ab'amaanyi aba Katonda. Mubayigirize okukoowoola erinnya lyange okutuusa ku nkomerero, kubanga buli alikikola alirokolebwa.

Mutunuulire empeera n'essanyu ebiteekeddwa mu maaso gammwe, era mweyongereyo okutuuka ku kalu w'empeera ey'okuyitibwa okwa waggulu okwa Katonda mu Kristo Yesu. Kubanga empeera yammwe ejja kuba nnene, era mujja kwewuunya ebyo ebibategekeddwa mu ggulu. N'empeera yange si ya mu nsi muno wabula ya lubeerera, n'empeera zammwe teziriggwaawo naye ziriweereza ng'obusika obw'olubeerera okutuukiriza ebisuubizo Yahweh bye yakola eri bakadde; buli yod oba eriggwa lya yod birituukirizibwa. Temulitegeera bunene bwa mirimu gyammwe okutuusa ku lunaku olw'empeera, naye Ebyawandiikibwa biraga lwatu ebibala by'emirimu gyammwe.

Kubanga Yokaana Omubikkulirwa yawandiika n'obwesigwa bye nnamulaga nti: Oluvannyuma lw'ebyo ne ntunula, laba, ekibiina kinene, omuntu yenna ky'atayinza kubala, eky'amawanga gonna n'ebika, n'abantu, n'ennimi, nga bayimiridde mu maaso g'entebe y'obwakabaka, ne mu maaso g'Omwana gw'Endiga, nga bambadde ebyambalo ebyeru ebiwanvu, n'amatabi g'enkindu mu ngalo zaabwe. Ne boogerera waggulu n'eddoboozi ddene, nga bagamba nti, Obulokozi buva eri Katonda waffe, atuula ku ntebe, n'eri Omwana gw'Endiga. Kubanga muliyamba okulokola n'okuweereza ekibiina kinene, n'empeera zammwe ez'obwesigwa ziriba nnene.

Era mulinngoberera wonna w'endaga, era mmwe bibala ebibereberye ebyagulibwa okuva mu bantu, era muliyimba oluyimba oluggya mu maaso g'entebe y'obwakabaka mmwe mwekka lwe muyinza okuyiga. Era mulifugira wamu nange mu Bwakabaka bwange emyaka lukumi era bulijjo muliba nange mu butaliiko nkomerero. Kubanga essanyu lyammwe lya lubeerera, kubanga muliba nange mu Bwakabaka bwange ku nsi nga nfuga ensi mu butuukirivu okuva ku ntebe ya Dawudi, era Obwakabaka bwange tebuliggwaawo. So tewaliba kintu kibi kiriyingiramu, so tewaliba bizibu nate, era obwange bwakabaka bwa butuukirivu omwana gw'endiga n'empologoma mwe birigalamira awamu mu mirembe. Era ku nkomerero y'obufuzi bwange obw'emyaka olukumi, Setaani alisumululwa akaseera katono okukuŋŋaanya okulumba kwe okw'enkomerero, n'omuliro guliva eri Yahweh ne gubazikiriza ddala. Setaani alisuulibwa mu nnyanja ey'omuliro, n'atadda nate, era Yahweh alitonda eggulu eriggya n'ensi empya, era alibeera mu bantu, era bwe mutyo mulibeera mu Yerusaalemi Omusu, ekyo ekibuga ekitukuvu ekyatondebwa Yahweh yennyini. Era Yahweh yennyini yagamba nti: Oyo awangula alisikira byonna; nange ndiba Katonda we, naye aliba mwana wange.
