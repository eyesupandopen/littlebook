Munnindeeko Akaseera: Sirigenda Kulwa

Munnindeeko akaseera katono, ggwe abaana bange, era sirigenda kulwa. Siribireka so siribabuukako, kubanga muli mpeke ya liiso lya Kitange era mpeke ya liiso lyange ddala. Muli Mugole wange, era omugole ki aleka omugole we eri omusango gw'aboonoonyi?

Mutegeere nti Nze, Mukama wammwe Yesu Kristo, nja kununula abantu bange. Temusomanga mu Byawandiikibwa Ebitukuvu? Kitange teyaggya Enoka n'amutwala mu ggulu? Kitange teyaggya Eriya n'amutwala mu ggulu? Kitange teyawanula baana ba Isirayiri okuva mu Misiri? Teyawanula Lutti okuva mu Sodoma ng’omuliro tegugwa ku bibuga ebyo, ne gubizikiriza byonna? Kale nja kusinga nnyo okuwanula omugole wange gwe nnagula n’omusaayi gwange mwennyini okuva mu mikono gy’omusango ogugenda okuyita ku nsi eno? Mulituusa ddi okumpa obusiru, Nze Mukama wammwe, ne Kitange, Katonda wammwe?

Mbadde mbawa obulamu obutaggwaawo, era waliwo empeera nnene eterekeddwa abeesigwa mu ggulu, kale temutyanga, kubanga obusika bwammwe bunaatera okutuukirira. Nze Musumba omutuukirivu anunula ekisibo okuva mu misege egirulunkanira. Emisege gye gimu gino gijja kuwulira nti, “Muveewo, mmwe abakozi b'ebibi; Ssaabategeeranga, newankubadde mugamba nti, Mukama, Mukama!” Nze Musumba omwesigwa atafiirwanga ku muntu yenna Kitange gw’ampadde, wabula omwana w'okuzikirira, eyankulamu olw'ebicweka bya ffeeza amakumi asatu, era ekyawandiikibwa kyatuukirira ddala olw'okusobya kwe n'okugaana kwe. Masomo ki agalimu gye muli, mmwe abaana bange, okuggyako nti temuyinza kubula mu mukono gwange era tewali ayinza okubaggya mu nze?

Temutyanga musango gwa bakozi ba bibi, mmwe abeesigwa bange, era mumanye nti obusungu bwa Kitammwe tebujja kubakwatako. Kitaawe ki mu mmwe ayinza okuwaayo omu ku baana be mu mikono gy’omulabe? Newankubadde nga kino kibi kya bulijjo mu bantu b'oku nsi, tewali kisinziiro kya kukyuka eri Kitaffe ow'ebitangaala! Kale temutyanga era mubeere mirembe. Mulekere awo emirimu gyammwe era muwummulire mu nze. Mugoberere emirimu gyange era munyumirwe okuwummula mu nze, Mukama wammwe Yesu Kristo, eyeewaayo ng'ekiweebwayo olw'ebibi bya bonna omulundi gumu emirembe gyonna.

O mmwe abaana b'ensi, ebibi byammwe biri mu maaso gange, era mweterekedde obusungu n’omusango omungi! Mu kifo ky’okweterekera obusika bw’emikisa, mweterekedde obusungu ku busungu olw'olunaku olwo olunene olugenda okubatuukako. O, ebigambo byammwe eby'obwereere bye mwogedde, nga mugamba nti, “Ali ludda wa Mukama; ye lugero lwokka olutaliwo era olw'enkya lujja kuba lwe lumu n'olw'eggulo.” Omusango gwammwe gujja kubatuukako amangu ddala, n'ebigenda okubaawo bijja kwanguwa nnyo ne mubula mu kubonaabona. Ebigambo byammwe ebibi n’amakubo gammwe amabi bijja kubaleetera okuzikirira okungi mu kaseera. Byonna bye mukwata ng'ebyamuwendo bijja kubaggibwako, n'ebintu byammwe byonna eby'obugagga bijja kubula mu mikono gyammwe kubanga mbibawadde mu mikono gy'abo ababakyawa, kubanga mwankyawa awatali nsonga.

Obusungu bw'Omwana gw'endiga bunaatera okubatuusibwako, n’obulumi bw’okuzaala busembedde wamu kati, busembedde nnyo okutuusa lwe mubutabulatabula n'obusungu bw'Omwana gw'endiga. Tegeera, ggwe omuntu omusirusiru, nti mu kugumiikiriza kwange okungi nkulabula ku kuzikirira kwo, ate naawe okyasekerera mu butakkiriza. Mu bbanga ttono nja kuggyawo omuzibi wakati mu mmwe, era nja kuggyawo Omugole wange wakati mu mmwe, era okuzikirira okw'amaanyi kujja kubatuukako amangu ddala. Setaani atutte eggye lye n'ebitundu bye mu bifo byabyo, nga yeeteeseteese okubafukako obusungu bwe bwonna n’ekiruyi kye, era mbawaddeyo mu mikono gye. Eggwanga lyammwe eddene lisaziddwamu era liteekeddwako akabonero k'okuzikirizibwa ddala; singa mwategeera ebigambo, Babulooni eky'ama. Naye wakyaliwo ebbanga ery’okwenenya, naye abasinga obungi ku mmwe temujja kwenenya mu makubo gammwe amakakanyavu ag'obujeemu obw'ekivve.

Omusango gwammwe gujja kubajjira mu ngeri bbiri enkulu: okuva munda n'okuva wabweru. Omwenzi omukazi omukulu ajja kuyuzaayuzibwa; ajja kumalibwawo mu mazzi n’ayokebwa n'omuliro era abikkibweko kawumpuli n’enjala. Yee, ggwe omuntu w’oku nsi, ani ajja kukuwanula mu mukono gwange? Tewali ajja okununula. Okuzikirira kwammwe kuli kumpi. Amagye amamyuufu gaatambula ne gayingira mu ggwanga lyammwe wansi w'ennyindo zammwe, era temwafayo wadde katono, okutuusa lwe mwasanyusibwanga ne mugejja n’obugagga obw’ekiseera obutaali bwammwe, kubanga ensi ya Mukama n’okujjuza kwayo. Temukomye ku kweteerawo kuzikirira kwammwe, naye era mwatunda abaana bammwe n’abazzukulu bammwe eri okuzikirira okw'amaanyi, era mukyasekerera. Ezzadde lyammwe libuze. Temujja kusekerera bbanga ddene kubanga amakubo gammwe gonna amabi n'amawetame gajja kubazisa mu nnaku, mu kubonyaabonyezebwa, ne mu kuzikirira, newankubadde ekibiina ekinene kijja kulongoosebwa okuyita mu kibonyoobonyo.

Bonna abankyukira ne bakoowoola erinnya lyange era ne batayagala bulamu bwabwe okutuusa ku nkomerero, nja kubasasula empeera emirembe gyonna! Tebaagenda kuddamu kubonaabona, naye okubonaabona kwammwe tekujja kuggwaawo, mmwe ababi. Naye ggwe, ggwe omuntu ow'obutonde agaana Katonda, eyalanga olutalo ku Mutonzi wo n'oyegomba obujeemu ku nze ne ku makubo gange. Okuzikirira kwo kujja kujja mangu ddala era tewajja kubaawo kuwummula. Mufuuse eggwanga ly'abenzi, era nga kituukira ddala amawanga gammwe okuyitibwa omwenzi mu Byawandiikibwa Ebitukuvu. Amagye gajja kumalawo ekyasigalawo ku ggwanga lyammwe eryali eddene era ery'amaanyi, era okulongoosa okw'amaanyi kujja kujja ku nsi, n'ekibonerezo ekinene kijja kubatuukako olw'amakubo gammwe amabi.

Mbaggyeeko buli kintu olw'amakubo gammwe amabi era amakakanyavu; era newankubadde nnasasulira ebibi byammwe, mwagaana okukkiriza kye nnawa, ne musalawo okubyeyererera, era mujja kwejusa emirembe n'emirembe. Omuzibi bw'aliggibwawo, okuzikirira okw'amangu kujja kuba munno; era muweereddeyo abaana bammwe eri omukisa gwe gumu nga ggwe, newankubadde abangi bajja kulokolebwa. Ne bamu ku baana bammwe bajja kunkyukira, era nja kubaŋŋaanya mu kitiibwa, newankubadde mwabaziyiza okujja gyendi, nammwe mujja kugenda mu kuzikirira mu nnyanja ennene eyo erindiridde Omulyolyomi n'abamalayika be.

Mmwe abaana bange abato, temutyanga. Mweyongere okuba abeesigwa gyendi wano mu ddakiika eno esembayo, weewaawo, eddakiika yammwe esembayo mu nsi eno efa. Mbayise, mbalonze, mbalonze n'omukono ku lw'okunjagala kwammwe n'obwesigwa bwammwe, era eby'obugagga ebinene bibalindiridde mu nnyumba ya Kitange. Mujja kutwalibwa mu kifo eky'obukuumi n'empeera ennene; newankubadde abamu bajja kufiirwa, bonna abalonde bange bajja kulokolebwa ddala okutuukira ddala.

Munteekeko essira; mwogeragane nange; ndi kumpi okusinga okussa kwo okuddako. Mumanye, mmwe abalonde bange, nti mbalwanirira ddala; era newankubadde okuyigganyizibwa n'okubonyaabonyezebwa byeyongera, mujja kulokolebwa okuva mu busungu. N'olwekyo, mwenaaze nga muva mu nsi eno era nga mwegaana amakubo gaayo amabi. Munaabe emikono gyammwe ku bibi byayo era muve mu butali butuukirivu bwayo, kubanga buli muntu ali mu kitanda ky'omwenzi ajja kubonaabona. Mwenaaze mu musaayi gwange ogutangirira era mwenaaze ddala. Laba, mbateeseeko akabonero ka Kitange, era nja kubanunula ddala n'okubatonda obuggya okufaanana nange. Weewaawo, mujja kuba muntu mupya era muli kitonde kipya mu nze, era mangu ddala wajja kubaawo eggulu eriggya n'ensi empya, kubanga Kitange afuula ebintu byonna biggya. N'olwekyo munywere era mumanye nti okwagala kwange muliro ogwaka, era muli ba muwendo nnyo era mweyaayanirwa. Mangu ddala mujja kubeera nange.

Nze Mukama wammwe omulamu era omulokozi, Yesu Kristo.

Nze mulamuzi omutuukirivu apima byonna mu minzaani.

Nze kikolo kya Yesse agenda okutuula ku ntebe ya Dawudi mu Bwakabaka bwange obusemberedde.

Nze mwana wa Katonda eyajja mu mubiri n'akomererwa olw'ebibi byammwe, n'atwala ekifo kyammwe mu musango.

Nze mwana w'omuntu eyalangirirwa edda.

Nze oyo atambulira wakati mu bikondo by'ettaala omusanvu.

Nze musana gw'ensi.

Nze mugaati gw'obulamu.

Nze oyo ava mu lubuto lwe emigga gy'amazzi amalamu.

Nze oyo abafudde abaggya era abatonze obuggya.

Musigale nga munyweredde, mmwe abeesigwa bange, kubanga okununulibwa kwammwe kuli kumpi okusinga bwe mulowooza.
