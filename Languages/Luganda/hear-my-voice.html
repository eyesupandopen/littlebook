Obujulirwa obw'Omulundi ogw'Okubiri: Ekkanisa
Mu Mirimu gya Yokaana Omutume Omubatiza
Nakasenene 14, 2024

Muwulire Eddoboozi Lyange!

Ekigambo kya Mukama ne kinjijirako. Muwulire eddoboozi lyange, mmwe abaana bange abato, mujje mu mikono gyange egigooloddwa. Mumanye nti okwagala kwe njagala mmwe tekusobola kumenyeka wadde okuwangulwa, kubanga bwe kwagala kwa Kitange mu ngeri gye yagabamu ku buzaale bwe eri abaana be, era mu nze mwe muli okujjuva kwonna. Nkubayise amannya gammwe era mbayise okuyimirira mu maaso gange. Endiga zange ziwulira eddoboozi lyange, kubanga zimanyi eddoboozi ly'omusumba waazo. Temunnawulira ddoboozi lyange? Mwezibye amatu gammwe ku ddoboozi lyange? Nkayabira waggulu mu nguudo ne mu tuyumba; n'okutuusa ne nkyalira amakomera gammwe ne ndeetera abayozaayozebwa n'abeerabirwa obulokozi.

Woowe, ggwe omuntu, lwaki weegaana Mukama wo? Lwaki wakyukira okuva mu makubo gange? Naye ojja kugamba nti, Sikyuse kuva mu makubo go; nze wuuyo Mukama gwe yayita. Mazima, nnayita, era mwaddamu okumala ekiseera, ekiseera eky'obuvubuka n'okusanyuka okungi. Abamu ku mmwe mwatwalibwa awalabirira emisege, era bangi ku mmwe mwakyamizibwa okuyita mu bigambo ebirungi, emboozi ennungi, n'ennimi ezirimba eziri kumpi okuziyizibwa.

Jjukira okwagala kwo okwasooka, era weenenye; weyatule ebibi byo gye ndi, kubanga bulijjo nteekateeka okukunaazaako ebibi byo mu musaayi gwange ogutangirira.

Muwulire eddoboozi lyange, abaana bange, kubanga amaloboozi mangi gafaanana nalyo era Ba Kristo abalimba bangi bagezaako okukoppa eddoboozi lyange. Mazima, nfaayo era ndunda Ekkanisa yange n'omukono gwange nzennyini, nsobole okubaweerayo eri Katonda nga muli bayonjo ng'omuwala embeerera era nga mwambadde bafuta enjeru ennungi, ng'amabala g'ebibi byammwe gasonyiye, gaggyiddwawo, era geerabiddwa, kubanga ngasudde mu buziba obusingayo, ne gataddayo kusituka. Naye, bangi ku mmwe musobeddwamu era n'okulogebwa mulozeddwa ensi eno embi ey'omu kiseera kino. Balumbye n'obulogo bwabwe okuyita mu kulumwa kw'omusota, naye mutegeere nti newankubadde omusota gukubalumye, tewali kabi kajja kubatuukako, kubanga akabonero ka Kitange tekasobola kumenyebwa. Mutunuulire nze, Omwana we omu yekka gw'azaala, gwe yawaayo kyeyagalire okutangirira ebibi byammwe tusobole okuddamu okugattibwa, ne tutaddayo kwawukana. Munkirize mbawonye; munkirize mbawonye okutuuka ku nkomerero era mbaggyeko enkovu zammwe musobole okuba abalamu era abatuukiridde mu maaso gange. Mugoberere amakubo gange era mugoberere Ekigambo kyange, kubanga:

Nze Kigambo eyafuuka omubiri abeera mu mmwe.

Nze mugaati gw'obulamu, na buli agulyako taliddayo kulumwa njala.

Nze wuuyo Obwakabaka bwe butali bwa nsi eno, n'empeera yange eri nange eri abeesigwa bange n'abalonde.

Nze wuuyo atambulira wakati mu bikondo by'ettaala omusanvu era alagira bamalayika omusanvu ab'Ekkanisa.

Nze Mutukuvu wa Katonda.

Nze muwolereza wammwe.

Nze wuuyo akema emitima gy'abantu.

Bulijjo nteekateeka okunaazaako ebibi byammwe mu musaayi gwange ogutangirira.

Nze mulamuzi omutuukirivu.

Nze Kristo yennyini eyalangirirwa okuva ku lubereberye.

Nze mwana wa Katonda eyajja mu mubiri.

Nze wuuyo eyawaayo omusaayi gwe ogutaliiko kibi ku lw'aboonoonyi, basobole okusikira obulamu obutaggwaawo.

Nze mwana wa Katonda eyajja mu mubiri.

Nze musumba omulungi.

Nze wuuyo eyajja mu babe naye n'agaanibwa ababe.

Nze Kristo yennyini, eyalondebwa Kitange okuva ku ntandikwa y'ensi.

Nze Ndi nga bwe Ndi.

Abaana bange, mbagamba mazima nti ngolodde omukono gwange eri ensi efa okuva emyaka mingi egiyise, era omukono gwange tegujja kubeera mugoloddwa bulijjo mu kusaasira n'ekisa, wadde nga okusaasira n'ekisa biri nange bulijjo. Nnyise bangi, era bangi bagaanyi. Naye, abo be nnayita ne mbalonda ne mbatukuza ne mbanaaza ne mbalongoosa ne mbawonya, abo bennyini be bajja okuyimirira ku ntebe yange ey'omusango ne batwala empeera ennene olw'obwesigwa bwabwe.

Newankubadde ng'ennina emirimu mingi gye muteekwa okukola, era newankubadde nga munkoledde bingi n'erinnya lyange, nneegomba omutima gwammwe gwonna okusinga emirimu gyammwe. Nneegomba mukyukire gye ndi muve mu bibi byammwe. Mujje gye ndi nga muli bayonjo, kubanga Mukama wammwe ateekateeka n'omukono gwe oguwonya ogugoloddwa nsobole okubafuula abayonjo ng'omuzira omupya. Newankubadde emikono gyange gyafumuulwa, omusaayi gwange ogutangirira gwakulukuuta okuva mu gyo n'okuva mu mubiri gwange okutuuka ku ttondo erisembayo. Nnawaayo byonna byange musobole okuba abalamu mu maaso ga Kitaffe emirembe n'emirembe.

Mazima, saatangirira kibi kimu kyokka; mazima, saatangirira bibi bitono byokka; mazima, saatangirira bibi bingi, naye nnatangirira ebibi byonna eby'ebiseera byonna singa mwandianukudde bwe nkanga emitima gyammwe okuyita mu mwoyo gwange ogw'amaanyi. Temutegeera nti sirina kwagala kubasalira musango? Waliyo alina okwagala okwo era yeegomba okuzikirira kwammwe era abaloopera emisana n'ekiro mu maaso ga Kitange, newankubadde nze muwolereza wammwe. Yasuulibwa wansi era n'awangulwa; naye, lwaki mugulumiza omubbi mu kifo eky'oku mwanjo mu mitima gyammwe so nga nze nnina obuwanguzi obujjuvu era obw'amazima ku ye n'obwakabaka bwe obuli kumpi okuggwaawo? Musirike mube mirembe mujje gye ndi n'omutima omuwombeefu. Temumanyi nti singa mweesalira omusango, temwandisimbiddwa mu maaso g'omulamuzi? Munsabe mbalage obulamu bwammwe, kubanga nja kubabikkulira ebintu bingi musobole okweyatula ensobi zammwe ne mufuulibwa abatuukiridde.

Temutya, mmwe abaana bange abato, kubanga si nze abasalira omusango. Muwulirize omwoyo gwammwe ogubaluma era temugezaako kwemulula ku ddoboozi lyange. Ensi eno enaatera okusuulibwa wansi, era ng'ensi eno ekyusiddwa emirembe gyonna, mujja kusitulirwa waggulu mu ggulu, nsobole okubaweerayo eri Kitange, musobole okutegeera obusika bwammwe n'okufuna empeera zammwe ez'obwesigwa. Nneegombye olunaku lwe tulirabagana mu ggulu musobole okufuna obulamu bwammwe obw'amazima obubikkuliddwa gye muli kubanga mudduse obulamu buno obw'ekiseera obw'obubi, ekibi, n'obujeemu. Nze wuuyo akema emitima gy'abantu, si lwa kubasalira musango, naye nti okuyita mu muliro gw'omuweesi musobole okutukuzibwa okuva mu makubo gammwe amakadde ne mutegeera amakubo gange ag'okwagala, ekitangaala, essanyu, emirembe, okugumiikiriza, n'obuwombeefu.

Muwulire okunenya kwange era mukkirize okukangavvula kwange kubanga Kitammwe abakolako ng'abaana, era mwana ki Kitaawe gw'ayagala atanenyerwa era n'akangavvulwa? Okukangavvula kwange kuleeta ebibala eby'emirembe eby'obutuukirivu, kubanga okukangavvula kwange kwe kubalongoosa ddala ku bibi byammwe singa munaawulira ebigambo byange ne mugoberera eddoboozi lyange. Mugende, muyimirire, era mwogere ebigambo byonna eby'obulamu buno. Abamu bajja kukyajjira mu bulokozi mu ssaawa eno esembayo, era mu linnya lya Yesu Kristo lyokka, erinnya lyange ery'amaanyi awalimu obulokozi, kubanga tewali linnya lwa mulala eririmu obulokozi obwaweebwa. Erinnya lyange ery'amaanyi linyweevu, nga libalindiridde mubaleete gye ndi, nsobole okuweerayo abeenenya eri Kitange ng'omwana omuwere azaaliddwa obupya mu butaggwaawo. Mbalinaako okwagala okungi, era nga Kitange bwe yampa, nange bwe ntyo bwe nja okuwa abeesigwa bange n'abalonde.

Nze mwana wa Katonda eyajja mu mubiri, ataliiko kibi era omuyonjo emirembe n'emirembe. Mu ngeri y'emu, nze wuuyo eyawaayo omusaayi gwe ogutaliiko kibi ku lw'aboonoonyi basobole okusikira obulamu obutaggwaawo. Mmwe, abeesigwa bange n'abalonde, temuli mu basika b'ekitiibwa ekitaggwaawo? Lwaki mukyasirika ng'abalindiridde ebigambo ebituufu? Mwogere! Yasamye akamwa kammwe mwogere, kubanga nja kulung'amya okwogera kwammwe okwekebeza obulumi obwennyini obukyakwata abo be nnayita, era bangi bajja kukyanukula. Mwegatte ku nze mu mulimu gwange mu ssaawa eno esembayo nga sinnakankanya misingi gy'ensi ne ngiyuzamu ebifo bingi n'omusango omunene ne gubuna ensi. Nja kubaddizaawo ensi eno mu Bwakabaka bwange obujja, naye okusooka obwakabaka bw'ababi bujja kuzikirizibwa ne butwalibwa mu musango okusibibwa mu bunnya, era bamanyi nti ennyanja eyaka omuliro omweru gye bajja okumalamu emirembe n'emirembe ebalindiridde.

Nja kuzikiririza ddala entebe zaabwe ez'obuyinza; nja kuzikiririza ddala obufuzi bwabwe bwonna; nja kubookya ddala ne mbafuula vvu n'omuliro ogutasobola kuzikizibwa, nga nange, Mukama omwesigwa, bwe nnabagambye nga tekinnabaawo. Mu nnyumba ya Kitange mulimu amayumba mangi, era obukulu n'ekitiibwa ekinene bibalindiridde. N'olwekyo, waleme kubaawo muntu n'omu ababba empeera yammwe era mubeere beesigwa gye ndi. Mutunuulirewo nze omu yekka, Mukama ataggwaawo, ye Mukama ow'ekitiibwa. Nze musumba omulungi; Nze Kristo yennyini eyalondebwa Kitange okuva ku ntandikwa y'ensi. Nze wuuyo eyajja mu babe n'agaanibwa ababe, wadde nga okuyita mu kugezesebwa okungi Yakobo ajja kulokolebwa n'omukono gwange ogw'amaanyi. Enkomerero y'ebintu byonna erinaatera, n'enkomerero y'omulangira w'ensi eno eri kumpi. Newankubadde ajja kusumululwa okumala akaseera katono, omusango gwe ogw'olubeerera gugoberera, mwe tatasobole kulokolebwa era tewaliba ddagala. Mudde gye ndi, mwogere nange, muweereze ku lwange, kubanga Kitange ampadde ebintu byonna, n'empeera yange eri mmwe eterekeddwa mu ggulu eri abeesigwa bange n'abalonde era ntuufu ng'akabonero ka Kitange ke nnabateekako. Wasigaddeyo eddakiika ntono zokka ez'ekiseera eky'ekisa, n'omusango gwange gujja kugwa mangu ng'obusungu bw'Omwana gw'endiga bujja ku nsi. Mweteekereteekere emitima gyammwe era mweteeketeeke, kubanga ekiseera kituuse.
