Mukube Ekikungubago

Ebigambo Mukama by'ampadde, mbilangirira lwatu: Muwulire Ekigambo kya Mukama!

Mukube ekikungubago; ddala, mukube ekikungubago olw'abo abakyanywerera ku nsi eno egudde. Kubanga bagambye mu mitima gyabwe nti, "Kiki ekiyinza okuntusaako? Nga bwe kyabanga edda, bwe kityo bwe kirigenda mu maaso ebbanga ttono; era nze n'abange tuligenda mu maaso n'okwesiima, era tulikungaanya ebirungi by'ensi eno n'okutereka ssente, ezo ddala eziriruwonya mu kiseera ky'okwetaaga." Mugambye mu mitima gyabwe nti, "Tuli bagezi, era tuli balamu; akabi tekalikututuukako, nga bwe katatuukako, kubanga Katonda waffe tajja kuleka ggwanga lyaffe kugwa; kubanga tuli bagezi era ba maanyi." Okuggyako nti temuli bagezi, wabula mwerowooza mwekka okuba abagezi; era mpadde abalabe bammwe obuwanguzi ku mmwe.

Ayi ggwe omuntu, ani alikulokola mu ssaawa yo ey'omusango egisembezizza ensi yonna? Abakulembeze bo balikulokola? Mazima, mbagamba nti nedda, kubanga omwana w'okuzikirira ajja gy'oli era ng'atambula lwatu mu mmwe, ng'alimbalimba bangi n'ebigambo ebirungi n'emboozi ennungi. Sente zo zirikulokola? Nedda, eby'obugagga byo byonna birilangirirwa nga tebiriiko mugaso amangu ago, era tewalibaawo kusasulwa kw'emirimu gy'obulamu bwo gy'okung'aanya n'omululu. Ayi, singa tewaava mu makubo ga Mukama n'oyagala muliraanwa wo n'oyamba abaavu n'obuyinza bwo.

Naye nedda, mukweka obungi Katonda bw'abawadde okukkusa emmeeme yammwe. Mazima, mbawadde bangi ku mmwe okusingawo okumala mmwe n'okuwa omukisa muliraanwa wammwe; naye nedda, mwagambye nti, Nja kwekwasa obungi buno obungi kubanga Katonda ddala ampadde omukisa olw'obulungi bwange n'amaanyi gange. Laba, mbategeeza ekintu nga tekinnabaawo, n'olwekyo mu ssaawa yammwe ey'akabi munaatera okujjukira eddoboozi lyange n'ebigambo by'akamwa kange bye njogezza mu bannabbi bange. Kubanga sibatuma kwogera byabwe byokka, wabula mbatuma ng'okulabula mmwe, mulyoke mulokole emmeeme yammwe nga mukyukira gye ndi, Mukama wammwe Yesu Kristo, ayinza okubalokola okutuuka ku nkomerero. Naye mwogedde mu mitima gyammwe nti, Obulokozi? Seetaaga kulokolebwa, kubanga ndi mulungi nga bwe nazaalibwa. Naye ebibi byammwe bivundu mu nnyindo za Kitange, era ensi evunze olw'obutali butuukirivu bwammwe. Temumanyi nti mwazaalibwa nga mufiiridde mu kusobya n'ebibi, ne mwonoona obuzaale bwammwe?

Wadde nga nandyagadde okubawa obutonde obupya, obuyonjo, musuulawo ssaddaaka yange nga terina mugaso ne mukwatira ku mubiri gwammwe omuvundu.

Mukube ekikungubago olw'abantu b'eggwanga lyammwe eddene ennyo abagaana eddoboozi lyange. Kubanga ennaku n'okulumwa okungi biribatuukako amangu ago. Walibaawo okukankana okw'amaanyi, era eggye lyange ery'amaanyi ery'omu ggulu lirikutonnyesa okuzikiriza okungi ku malaya omukulu, era tajja kugolokokanso, newankubadde abasigaddewo balyesigamizibwa. Ne mu kabi kammwe nâ€™okuzikirizibwa okujja ddala, nja kutuma bannabbi bange gye muli. Mulibategeerera ku bibala byabwe, ebigambo byabwe, n'okuwonya; nja kukola ebyamagero bingi n'okuwonya okungi mu mikono gyabwe. Naye, n'olwekyo bangi balizikirira ne bafiira mu bibi byabwe, naye abawombeefu balisikira ensi; kubanga obusika bwange buli nange, bwesudde okugabira abeesigwa. Nnyinza kutandika omukono gwange emyaka emeka eri abantu abajeema abannyimirirawo n'amakubo gange ne mu bulimba bwabwe ne beerangirira nga batuukirivu mu maaso ga Katonda mu byambalo byabwe eby'effulungu?

Mwennenye! Mukaabe era muwowoggane olw'ebibi byammwe, era munkaabire nsobole okubalokola. Amakanisa gammwe mangi gayingiriddwa emisege egiruluma egibabuulira ebigambo ebiribatuusa ku nkomerero yammwe, ate nammwe temugikyukira gy'ali ne mukyukira gye ndi n'obwenyi obugazi n'omutima, nga bwe nandyagadde okubalokola mu nnaku zammwe n'okukuuma abaana bammwe nga balamu okuva mu magye g'omubi, abatambula lwatu mu nguudo zammwe era nga babba nnyo okuva mu mmwe. Okwegomba kwabwe tekulikkuta okutuusa lwe balibaggyako buli kimu ne balemaaza bangi. Naye abo abakoowoola erinnya lyange balilokolebwa bwe baligumiikiriza okutuusa ku nkomerero.

Lwaki mukakanyaza emitima gyammwe eri Mukama w'eggye? Lwaki mukyalimba emitima gyammwe, nga mugamba nti, "Tewali kabi; nga bwe kyali jjo, bwe kityo bwe kiriba leero, era enkya ereeta kye kimu?" Mmwe abanyooma mulikungula empeera y'amakubo gammwe agatali ga bukkiriza, era abo abanjeemera balituusibwa ku kuzikirira okutuukiridde n'ennaku ennyingi. Muwulire ebigambo byange, ayi mirembe gya bwenzi era emibi. Muwulire ebigambo byange era mutege amatu gammwe okubiwuliriza buli nnukuta gye nja kwogera. Okukankana okungi kuliyuzayuza eggwanga lyammwe lye mutenda ennyo, era okuva mu kukankana okungi kulivaamu okuzikirizibwa okungi. Nja kusaanyaawo ebibuga byammwe ebyali ebinene era nja kuleeta n'amazzi okubikka ennyanja zammwe ez'ebibi; kubanga temwagala Mukama naye muli baagazi ba ssanyu okusinga abagazi ba Katonda.

Siri musanyufu mu musango gwammwe, kubanga ekisa kyange n'okusaasira kwange kubundiridde gye muli okumala ekiseera kiwanvu kati. Bangi ku abo abaayonoonye endagaano ya Nuuwa nabo bennyini balibikkibwa mu mazzi olw'okwonoona omusoke omutukuvu ogw'amabala amangi Kitange gwe yawa ng'ekiragaano. Omuliro gulikutonnya ku mmwe n'omukono gw'abalabe bammwe, era bangi balifiira mu nnimi z'omuliro n'obusagwa obuliddirira. Ensi yammwe eriyuzibwa, era akabi akanene kaliba ekyemisana kyammwe, kubanga mwagaana Mukama wa bakama ne Kabaka wa bakabaka.

Omubi amaze okuyingiza Eggye lye Emyuufu mu bibuga byammwe, mu bibuga ebitonotono, ne mu byalo. Batambula mu mmwe, nga bategeka okuzikiriza kwammwe; era balindiridde ebiragiro byabwe n'okugenda kw'omuziyiza wange, gwe nja okusumulula mu bbanga ttono. Balimaliriza era bazikirize era bafuge ebisigaddewo. Si ekyo kyokka, naye balikyeyita kyabwe, era balitwala bangi ku mmwe mu nkambi zaabwe ze bamaze okutegeka. Bangi balifiirwa emitwe gyabwe ku lw'erinnya lyange, era nja kubasasula nnyo bwe banaasigala nga beesigwa okutuusa ku nkomerero. Tewaliba kutangaala; walibaawo olutindo olugudde n'enguudo eziguddemu. Ani alibawa emmere n'amazzi eggwanga lyammwe bwe liriba lisaanyiziddwawo ne litagolokoka nate? Naye nja kusigaza abasigaddewo, era abasigaddewo nja kusigaza, kubanga nkirangiridde!

Temutya, mmwe abantu bange n'abeesigwa bange. Nja kubaggyawo mu makkati mu kirenge ky'eriiso okubatwala mu nnyumba ya Kitange, gye mbategekedde ekifo n'emmeeza ffe tulyoke tusangire wamu, mmwe abaana bange. Nneegomba okubeerawo kwammwe, era mu bbanga ttono muliraba amaaso gange n'amaaso gammwe, era mulifuna empeera y'okukkiriza kwammwe mu nze kubanga mulina okukkiriza mu mitima gyammwe nga temunnalaba na maaso gammwe ekitiibwa kyange, wadde ekitiibwa kya Kitange, ansinga obukulu. Era mbayise era mbalonze okuba abange, era temuliva gye ndi, kubanga mulibeera ne Mukama wammwe n'omununuzi wammwe ennaku zonna. Mumanye nti nja kubasasula nnyo era mbafukirire emikisa gye mutayinza kutegeera mu kiseera kino. Musigale nga muli beesigwa okutuusa ku nkomerero, kubanga ekiseera kitono era bino birituukirira; mazima, obudde buyise, era ekiro kigudde ku nsi.

Nze Kabaka wa bakabaka era Mukama wa bakama, era mpeereddwa erinnya erisinga buli linnya, nti ku linnya lya Yesu Kristo buli kintu kyonna ekiri mu ggulu ne ku nsi kifukamire.

Nze Omulangira w'Emirembe, era ebweru wange tewali mirembe.

Nze oyo eyabawa okuwummula, mulyoke muwummulire mu nze era musange obubudamo mu kisa kyange.

Nze oyo atambulira mu birenge by'ebikondo by'ettabaaza omusanvu, alagira bamalayika ba Kitange, era ebintu byonna ebiri mu ggulu ne ku nsi biri mu mikono gyange, nga Kitange bwe yabimpa mu buwanguzi bwange ku mubi okutuusa abalabe bange lwe balifuulibwa entebbe y'ebigere byange.

Nze Mukama wammwe n'omununuzi wammwe, era nze omusumba omulungi eyatikkirwa ekitiibwa kyonna, era nkwatidde obulamu obutaggwaawo mu mikono gyange eri abo abanaatoowaza ne basaba.

Munangirire nga nze Mukama! Temutya, mmwe abaana bange abato, kubanga bino bye biro eby'enkomerero era ekibiina kinene kirirokolebwa okuva mu kibonyoobonyo ekigenda okujja. Buli muntu yenna alikoowoola erinnya lya Yesu Kristo alirokolebwa, era nja kubawonya ne mbawa empeera olw'obwesigwa bwabwe. Mazima, ntambula mu mmwe, era kiki ekinene Mukama ky'ategese ky'atategeezezza bannabbi be nga tekinnabaawo? Mbagamba lwatu, mutegereze eddoboozi lyange, kubanga ekiseera kitono nnyo kisigaddeyo nga tannavuga ekkondeere ery'enkomerero. Ne bamalayika bayimiridde mu ggulu waggulu, nga balindiridde okuvuga kw'ekkondeere! Okununulibwa kwammwe okujjuvu kuli ku mulyango gwammwe, n'olwekyo temutya era munywerere ku nze, kubanga obulamu bwammwe bukwekeddwa mu nze era bunaatera okubikkulibwa.
