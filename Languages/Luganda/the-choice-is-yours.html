Obujulirwa Obubiri: Isirayiri
Ekikaawa Ekirimu Obuwomi
Jjulaayi 15, 2024

Okulonda Kuli Mu Mikono Gyo!

Muwulirize ebigambo by’omulokozi wammwe, Omutonzi era Kabaka wammwe, ggwe abaana bange, era mutege amatu eri Ebigambo bya Mukama wammwe, ye Yesu Kristo omutuukirivu, kubanga nze mwana w’obutuukirivu era ngolokoka mu kitiibwa kyange, nammwe mukulidde ng’ennyana ez’omu kirugu mu mirembe n’obukuumi obulabika, era mbakuzizza olw'ekigendererwa eky'omu ggulu ekigenda okubikkulirwa mu bujjuvu mu mmwe, ggwe abeesigwa bange era abalonde. Laba! Nze ngezesa emitima gy’abantu nulyo mumanye n'obukakafu obanga ddala muli mu kukkiriza, oba nga mubuze era nga mutwaliddwa ebibi byammwe n'obulimba obw'amaanyi obuliwo kati obuwambye Ensi.

Kubanga nze oyo Yakuwa gwe yagabana naye entebe ye ey'obwakabaka, ye Kristo Yesu Omutonzi eyazuukira era alina obuyinza, era nze Mukama wa Bakama ne Kabaka wa Bakabaka era laba Empologoma y’Ekika kya Yuda ejja mu bire okwaniriza abeesigwa bange eri nze balyoke batwalibwe mu ggulu olw’embaga y’Omwana gw’endiga, kubanga Omugole wange yeeteeseteese. N’ettabaaza ze zijjudde, era alongoosezaako entambi zaazo, era alindiridde okwabika kwange ng’omuwala omugezi, be abo be nnalongoosa mu bujjuvu era abatalina musango mu maaso g'entebe ya Katonda. Be abo abatakyusibwanga nkwe za Balaba, kubanga Balaba aleeta okujeema okw’effujjo, entalo n’akatyabaga, kubanga mu kiseera kino ye mutwe gumu mu mitwe egiwerako egikolera awamu mu lukwe, naye ajja kugolokoka nga Moshiach ow’obulimba, era alondeddwa abantu b’ensi okufuga ekkuŋŋaaniro lya Sitaani. Kubanga ye musika w'oyo bakitaabwe gwe baalonda, era beeteesateesa okumwaniriza nga Kabaka waabwe.

Naye ggwe, Ayi Kanisa y’e Lawodikiya, kaakano weetuuse ekiseera kyo eky’okuzikirira n’omusango. Kubanga mwerondeddewo omulokozi omulala okuba Kabaka wammwe, ye oyo okujja kwe kulangirirwa obubonero n’ebyamagero eby’obulimba era akomyewo okubakulembera n’obulogo n’eby’ekusamize. Nze Yeshua HaMashiach era nabagula mu musaayi gwange ogw’omuwendo, ogutaliiko kibi, era nze asobola okubafuula abalamu obulungi, n’okubazza obuggya mu mwoyo gwange, kubanga nafuulibwa omwoyo oguwa obulamu kubanga bwe kityo Yakuwa bwe yalagira obukulu bwonna ku Mwana we, ye oyo ali okutuukirira kw’omwana gw’endiga owa Katonda n’Empologoma y’Ekika kya Yuda! Era mu butuukirivu nja kusala omusango n’okulwana olw’omwana gw’endiga owa Katonda n’Empologoma y’Ekika kya Yuda bituukiridde mu nze, Kristo Yesu, ddala Yeshua HaMashiach kubanga bwe ntyo bwe ndi!

Nja kumalayo obulokozi bwammwe, ggwe Ekanisa y'e Lawodikiya, naye temweteeseteese kuyingira mu Bwakabaka bwange mu kiseera kino kubanga mukyamye okuva ku kigendererwa kyammwe eky'omwoyo gumu mu Kristo! Nja kubalaga ensobi yammwe, era ddala mulina omukisa okwenenya ebibi byonna, naye temukyalinayo kiseera kya kuwakana na ddoboozi lya Mukama wammwe, kubanga bwe kityo ekibi kino eky'entiisa kye kimu ku bingi Sitaani by'akozesezza okubasiba obutajja gyendi mu kwenenya nulyo mbasobozese okubanaaza mu musaayi gwange ogutangirira okuva mu bibi byammwe.

Laba! Ebiseera by’Abamawanga bikomye era n'omuzeyituuni ogw'omu nsiko ogwasibibwa mu bo, era nabo bagabana ku kikolo n'amasavu g'Omuzeyituuni, kaakano gujja kutemebwa. Kubanga munyooma Ebigambo bya Kristo Yesu, kubanga nabagamba lwatu obutekwankwanisa ku matabi agaagwa mu butakkiriza: naye bw'okwankwanisa, tossanira kikolo, naye ekikolo ggwe. Nze Kikolo kya Yese, era nze ayimiridde ng'akabonero k'essuubi mu biseera byonna, ye oyo aleeta obulokozi n'amakubo ga Yakuwa ku nsi era nja kufuga obusika bwange okuva ku ntebe ya Dawudi mu butuukirivu era nagula ensi mu musaayi gwange ogutaliiko kibi era nja kutwala ekyo ekyange ku lwa mateeka n'amaanyi okuva mu mikono gy'abamenyi b'amateeka, be abo abaagwa.

Nze oyo eyawa ekisa n’okusaasira ku nsi, kubanga emyaka gyonna egy’ekisa Yakuwa yagimpa olw’obulokozi bw’Abamawanga. Kubanga ebigendererwa bya Yakuwa mu Isirayiri bulijjo byali nti Abamawanga baali ba kuweebwa omukisa era okujja eri obulokozi okuyita mu kugonda kwa Isirayiri. Naye oluvannyuma lw’ekiseera baafuuka ab’amalala, era ab’ekivve, era ne batemebwa okuva ku muzeyituuni ogw’obutonde mu butakkiriza. Naye ggwe, Ekanisa yange ey'e Lawodikiya, ddala ggwe kyennyini kifaananyi kya malala, obutakkiriza, n’obuzibe bw’amaaso obw’engeri y’emu obwalagibwa amatabi ag’obutonde agaasalibwako mu butakkiriza, era kaakano naawe ojja kusalibwako okuva ku muzeyituuni ogw’obutonde. Era kaakano nja kukyuka okumaliriza omulimu gwange ku muzeyituuni ogw’obutonde, ye Isirayiri ow’oluganda lwange n’abantu bange, be abo bonna be nnalonda okunkkiriza nga Mukama waabwe nsobole okubazza obuggya ng’omuntu omu omuppya era ekyama kya Katonda kimalirizibwe, ddala okutuukirira kw’emirimu gyange egy’okutangirira egy’obulokozi.

Naawe, Ekanisa yange ey'e Lawodikiya, olesse okwagala kwo okwasooka era oli mu mikono gy’omulimba gw’olayiridde obwesigwa. Mwerondeddewo Balaba omuppya ddala nga Kabaka wammwe era mwagaanyi Yesu Omunazaaleeti okuba Mukama wammwe era muli mu mikono gy’omulokozi omulala ow’obulimba. Ye oyo eyeefuula omulokozi wa Babulooni ow’ekyama, ayogera n’akola ng’omu ku mmwe, naye ng’ayogera ng’ogusota. Kubanga Babulooni ow’ekyama abeera mu bugagga obungi, n’essanyu n’obugagga obungi.

Era Babulooni ow'ekyama atamiize ensi yonna n’obugwagwa bw’obwenzi obuli mu kikompe kye ekya zzaabu, naye bwe zityo bwe ziri engeri z’abo abaagwa, abawaayo obugwagwa obusinga obubi, era ggwe okunywa kyeyagalire era n’essanyu lingi kubanga ozibye amaaso ekikompe kya zzaabu ku bugwagwa obusinga obubi bw'onywa mu bugwagwa. Kubanga n’Ekanisa yange ey’e Lawodikiya tekyakuŋŋaanira ku byawandiikibwa byange ebitukuvu wabula ku nkungaana za Balaba, gw'ossaamu obwesige. Era bwe mutyo mulonda omulokozi omuppya kubanga mwagala ensi n’ebintu by’ensi era temusobola kutegeera kirowoozo nti mwandifiiriddwa eby’okulya byammwe eby’omuwendo, okuggyako nja kubaggyako amasanyu gonna mu busungu bwange obusemberedde, era ne kaakano okufiirwa kwammwe kweyongera!

Kubanga muzibe ba maaso era mulimbibwalimbibwa mu malala gammwe nti Yakuwa yandibawadde omulokozi omulala okusinga nze, Yesu Omunazaaleeti, kubanga nze nsigo eyasuubizibwa ey’omukazi era Dani mwana wa mpologoma atalivaamu Masiya. Mumpulire era mumpulire bulungi, ggwe ekanisa ey’e Lawodikiya, kubanga kaakano kwe kwenenya kwammwe okuva mu kibi eky’emitima gyammwe oba kaakano kye kiseera lwe ndibaleka mu muliro ogulongoosa era bonna abakoowoola erinnya lya Mukama okutuusa ku nkomerero balilokolebwa era bangi abaayatula erinnya lyange mu butakkiriza baligwamu ne babula, nga bafusizza omukisa gwabwe ku ngeri y’okutya Katonda eyagaana amaanyi gaagyo: Mmwe Abalawodikiya, nze maanyi gaayo era mu nze Yakuwa mwe yateeka obukulu bwonna, nammwe mwanganze, Mukama wammwe era omulokozi, era nja kubasesema okuva mu kamwa kange okuggyako nga mwenenyezza kaakano!

Mu kiseera kyange eky’ekisa, obulokozi bwali busaasanye mu mawanga, era eggwanga n’eriddako ne ligabana ne ligabana ku kikolo ky’omuzeyituuni ogw’obutonde. Era kaakano, amawanga tegakyali kabonero ka bulokozi era ekiseera kyago mu Mwana kyajjawo olw'okugaanibwa kw'aboluganda lwange n'abantu bange kubanga baaleekaana mu butakkiriza nti mutusumululire Balaba! Naye ebyama bya Yakuwa bwe byabikkulwa abatume bange abatukuvu ne bannabbi, nziyiza Balaba ne simusumulula mu bujjuvu, naye nakkiriza kyokka ekyama ky’obubi okweyongerayo. Era kaakano ebiseera bituuse lwe ndisumulula Balaba mu bujjuvu ng’omuziyiza wange aggyiddwawo era ng’abeesigwa bange, abayite, n’abalonde batwaliddwa mu ggulu gye mbategekedde amaka gaabwe ag’enkalakkalira, era balyambazibwa bafuta ennungi etaliiko bbala etasobola kusiigibwako wadde okwononebwa.

Muli bazibe b'amaaso nnyo eri ensi yammwe, era muggavu wa matu nnyo n'obutakyawulira ddoboozi lyange ggwe Kanisa y'e Lawodikiya? Mukkirizza obulimba obwaleetebwa ekyama ky’obubi, era mukkirizza ekizimbulukusa eky’obutwa okuyingira mu Baibuli zammwe ezonooneddwa ezireese okwononeka okw’amazima mu Makanisa gammwe okonoonye emitima gyammwe n’enfumo mu butakkiriza obw’ensobi. Mukkirizza obulimba bw'abafumu ab'ebitundu okubaloga n'ebiwandiiko eby'obulimba n'ebigambo eby'obulimba. Temukyategeera byawandiikibwa, era mwerundidde ebizibu ebinene ebiribajjira amangu ago. Buli Kigambo kya Katonda kirongoofu era kigendereddwamu okugondera okukkiriza n'okuzimba abakiweebwa. Tegeera Ebigambo by'Ebigambo byange era otegeere ebiseera by'obeeramu.

Kubanga naleka Yokaana Omubikkulirwa n'awandiika nti: Ne ndaba ensolo eva mu nnyanja, erina emitwe musanvu, n'amayembe kkumi, ne ku mayembe gaayo amangule kkumi, ne ku mitwe gyayo erinnya ly'okuvvoola.

Era nate Yokaana Omubikkulirwa n’awandiika nti: Nalaba omukazi ng’atudde ku nsolo emmyuufu, ejjudde amannya g’okuvvoola, erina emitwe musanvu, n’amayembe kkumi. N’omukazi yali ayambadde olugoye olwa kakobe n’olumyufu, era nga yeerimbise ne zzaabu, n’amayinja ag’omuwendo ne luulu, ng’alina ekikompe ekya zzaabu mu ngalo ze, ekijjudde emizizo n’obugwagwa bw’obwenzi bwe.

N’ate: N’amayembe amakumi abiri ge walaba ku nsolo, gano gali kikyawa omwenzi, era galimufuula matongo, era bwereere, era galirya ennyama ye, era galimwokya omuliro. Kubanga Katonda yateeka mu mitima gyabwe okutuukiriza okwagala kwe, n’okukkiriza, n’okuwa obwakabaka bwabwe ensolo, okutuusa ebigambo bya Katonda lwe birituukiririra. N’omukazi, gw’olaba, kye Kibuga ekyo ekinene ekifuga bakabaka b’ensi.

Baana bange, mutegeere nti ensolo esobola okwogera ku bantu era ne ku bwakabaka, muyige okumanya. Ensolo esitula omwenzi ejja kuzikiriza omwenzi era teejja kubeera wansi w'omwenzi, kubanga afuze bakabaka b'ensi mu bwenzi bwe. Kale ensolo eneegolokoka okuva mu nnyanja ejja kufulumya Mahdi gwe basuubira, naye era ejja kuzikiriza ddala Babulooni w’Ekyama, kubanga Katonda awaddeyo omwenzi omukulu mu mikono gy’abalabe be abamukyawa ddala.

Ensolo ey’oku nnyanja n’ensolo ey’oku nsi nga tezinagolokoka mu bujjuvu mu Bwakabaka bw’ogusota, waliwo abafuzi bangi abali wamu mu kigendererwa okuwa amaanyi gaabwe n’obufuzi bwabwe eri ogusota, kubanga Katonda akiteese mu mitima gyabwe okukituukiriza. Era balizikiriza Babulooni w’ekyama. Olowooza nti ofunye obukugu mu byawandiikibwa byange ebitukuvu kubanga okkirizza abasomi b’eddiini bo ab’obulimba? Tegeera era owulire Ebigambo bya Mukama wo!

Nate, naleka Yokaana Omubikkulirwa n'awandiika nti: N'ensolo gye nalaba, yali ng'engo, n'ebigere byayo ng'ebya dduyiro, n'akamwa kaayo ng'akamwa k'empologoma: n'ogusota ne gumuwa amaanyi gaagwo n'enteben'obuyinza obungi. Ne ndaba omu ku mitwe gyayo nga gufudde, naye ekiwundu kyakyo eky’okufa ne kiwona, n’ensi yonna ne yeewuunya ne yeegatta ku nsolo.

Baana bange, ebigambo bya Yakuwa birimu obuzito obw’enjawulo, omuntu yenna by’atasobola kufuga. Ensolo ennyonnyoddwa bwakabaka obuligolokoka okuva mu kuzikirizibwa kw’omwenzi, era mu ntegeka enkadde muliva entegeka empya! Kikiikirira ensolo ey’oku nnyanja, kubanga ejja kugolokoka nga Mahdi waabwe, era kikiikirira obwakabaka bw'ajja okufugisa ennyanja. Lwaki tossa kitiibwa mu bigambo by’Ekigambo kyange? Tekigamba nti ensolo yennyini ey’oku nnyanja yafuna ekiwundu, kubanga muntu ki ddala alina emitwe musanvu? Naye omu ku mitwe gyayo nga gufudde. O, mmwe abasirusiru, abalina emitima egigenda mpola okukkiriza byonna Bannabbi bye baayogera! NGA KIRI nti yafuna ekiwundu eky’okufa. Ebigambo "nga bwe kyali" byandikukomezza mu makubo go, naye tokyakkiriza na katono ku bigambo byange, era nze Kigambo ekyefuula omubiri. Tekikweraliikiriza, NGA KIRI, lulimi lwa dda? Tekikweraliikiriza nti "byali" kya subjunctive? Tekikweraliikiriza nti kufuuze kwa, nga bwe kyandibadde?

Naye nedda, mukkiriza obulimba bw’ogusota, kubanga Balaba ayogera ng’ogusota, naye si ye Balaba, wabula ensolo eva ku ttaka. Era kaakano ensi yonna ejja kwewuunya olw'ensolo olw'obubonero bwe n'ebyamagero eby'obulimba, era ajja kukola obubonero obulala bingi eby'obulimba n'ebyamagero, era tagolokose mu bujjuvu kubanga omwenzi tannayokyebwa muliro, naye kaakano abikkuliddwa mu kiseera kye. Ayogera ng’ogusota, ng’agamba nti "Katonda yekka ye yamulokola okuva mu kiwundu kye eky’oku mutwe" era buno bulimba obuvvoola. Yakuwa, Katonda omukulu ataggwaawo, yantuma nze Yesu Omunazaaleeti okuba omulokozi w'ensi. Nze ne Kitange tuli bumu mu bumu n'ekigendererwa era Yakuwa teyalokola Balaba wadde nze, Kristo Yesu. Mubuze era mutwaliddwa era mutegekeddwa mu bulimba obunene, era mbagamba nti mwenenye muddeyo eri okwagala kwammwe okwasooka era mukikole kati, kubanga tewali kiseera!

Kubanga nze ngezesa emitima, era nze ngezesa bonna be mpita eri obulokozi, kubanga nlongoosa bonna mu muliro ogulongoosa! Laba! Nze ntudde ng’omulongoosa era omulongoosa ffeeza: era nlongoosa batabani ba Yakuwa, ne nbalongoosa nga zzaabu ne ffeeza, balyoke baweeyo eri Mukama ekiweebwayo mu butuukirivu. Temutegeera nti nja kubafuula bakabona ne bakabaka mu maaso ga Kitange? Newankubadde ekirabo kyange eky’omwoyo omutukuvu, mwe muzaalibwa obuggya n’ensigo etavunda kituukiridde era tekyetaaga kulongoosebwa, muva mu Adamu olw’omusaayi era mulina okukkiriza omulimu gwange okutuukirizibwa mu bulamu bwammwe, okuggyako nga ensigo yange egudde ku ttaka eddalu.

Kaakano weetuuse ebiseera by’omusango n’ebiseera by’ekisa biweddeko. Newankubadde abangi balinyooma ebigambo byange ne bakyuka mu butakkiriza, Yakuwa amaze okumanya ebigenda okuddirira. Kubanga Yakuwa bulijjo akakasa abantu amakubo ge agoonooneddwa era anenya abantu olw’ekibi, ng’amanyi mu kwagala kwe n’amagezi ge ag’emirembe n’emirembe nti alirokola abamu. Era nga Yakuwa bwe yakakanyaza omutima gwa Falaawo, bwe kityo bw'ajja okukakanyaza emitima gy’abangi abawulira naye ne batawuliriza. Yakuwa ajja kugezesa omuntu emirundi n’emirundi. Era abamu baliba nga Falaawo ne bakakanyaza emitima gyabwe nate mu butakkiriza naye abamu baliva mu makubo ga Falaawo ne bagolokoka okutuuka ku bulamu obutaggwaawo mu Kristo Yesu, naye okuyita mu muliro ogulongoosa.

Mutegeere nti abo abalonda Balaba balyefuula abagabana mu bizibu bya Balaba, n'abo abagaana okukoowoola erinnya lyange okutuusa ku nkomerero bonna balisalirwa omusango kubanga baasanyukira obutali butuukirivu. Kubanga omulokozi wammwe omuppya si mulokozi wabula nsolo ey’ettima. N’entegeka ye n’obwakabaka bwe bikolebwa abo abalumya abaana. Temutegeera makubo ga Gusota? Kubanga abo abagoberera amakubo g'ogusota banoonya abaana. Bawaayo abaana eri Moloki, banoonya oluzzi lw’obuvubuka mu musaayi gw’abaana ababonyebonyezebwa, balya abaana olw’okwemala gaabwe amalogo, era bakola omulimu gw’okukuba embuto mu nda okutta abato nga tebannazaalibwa, era babatutte mu nkukutu mu makubo gaabwe, kubanga bwe gityo bwe guli amakubo g’abakulembeze ba Babulooni w’Ekyama era n’obwakabaka bw’ogusota. Era kaakano weetuuse omusango gwa Katonda olw’ebintu bino n’ebirala bingi. Kubanga singa temwalaba bintu bino temwandibadde na kibi, naye kaakano mulaba era ekibi kyammwe kisigadde.

Temusomanga? Bw'ati bw'ayogera Mukama nti, Eddoboozi lyawulirwa waggulu, okukaaba n'okukaaba okw'entiisa. Rakeeri, ng'akaabira abaana be, yagaana okubudaabudibwa olw'abaana be, kubanga tebaaliwo. Kubanga mulemereddwa omulimu ogwabaweebwa, era mwateekebwako ebyama byange ne muyimirira ng’omusana gw’obulokozi era akabonero k’essuubi. Naye kaakano mwegasse ku abo be nnatuma okuyimirirawo okulwanyisa. Era bwe kityo kaakano weetuuse ebiseera lwe ndibaleka mu biseera byammwe eby’omu maaso.

Mumpulirize era mumpulirize bulungi mmwe mwenna abajja okugaana eddoboozi lya Yeshua HaMashiach, ddala eddoboozi lya Kristo Yesu: temulina kiseera! Mbasaba mumumpulirize mu kwagala kwange eri mmwe abaana bange era mwenenye kati! Kubanga omuliro ogulongoosa gwagala okubagwako mu bujjuvu, era gulirongoosa ensi okuva mu bwakabaka bwonna obw’ogusota n’ensi tejja kubeera wansi w’amakubo ga Sitaani nate era obwakabaka bwe tebuligolokoka nate, kubanga nze Mukama wa Bakama ne Kabaka wa Bakabaka, era nja kuzikiriza ddala abo abaagwa. Bonna abagaana okwenenya balifuna omugabo gwabwe mu nnyanja ey’omuliro era baligabana mu kibonerezo kya Setaani ne bamalayika be mu nnyanja eyo ennene ey’omuliro ogw’ekibiriiti. Mu ngeri y’emu, bonna abeenenya nga bava mu kibi ne bakoowoola erinnya lyange okutuusa ku nkomerero balilokolebwa era baliweebwa empeera ennene ey’emirembe n’emirembe mu nze.

Laba! Kaakano kye kiseera eky'okukuŋŋaanya abeesigwa, era kaakano ndireka abo abagaana eri Balaba, kubanga bakoze okulonda kwabwe.
