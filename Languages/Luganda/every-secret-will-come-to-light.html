Mu Biwandiiko bya Peetero
Ogwekkumi n'ebbiri, Ntenvu 2024

Buli Kyama Kirituuka mu Kitangaala!

Temusomanga? Buli kyama kiribikkulwa. Temusomanga? Buli kyakisiddwa kiryanjululwa. Temusomanga? Buli kigendererwa ky'omutima kirijja mu maaso gange. Buli lulimi lulyatula nti nze Mukama Omutuukirivu era Kabaka Omutuukirivu, afuga mu butuukirivu, bw'ayogera. Ai abantu bange, lwaki mulondawo ensobi ya Adamu ne Kaawa, abaatandikirawo okukweka ensoni zaabwe mu bikolwa byabwe, kubanga baatunga amakoola g'omutiini ne beekolera ebyambalo. Naye, Katonda yalaba ekyali kibaddewo era yali amanyi nti bajja kwesittala nga tannabatonda. Era Kitaffe yabakolera ki? YE YABAKOLERA ebyambalo by'amaliba n'abambika, awatali bikolwa byabwe bo.

Lwaki mulowooza nti muyinza okunkweka ebibi byammwe, abaanange? Nze mbalaba mwenna era mbamanyi okusinga bwe mwemanyi. Mulekere awo okuwuliriza abasumba abakyamu ababuulira Yesu omulala; mazima ddala, mu bidiba ebyatise, bawa amazzi ag'ekivuguto eri ekkanisa ey'ekivuguto esibiddwamu obwerufu obw'obulimba n'ekiyitibwa endowooza ennungi. Babalimba n'Ebukristaayo obw'okweyamba, nga babagamba amazima agatalina mukongo nga Katonda abagala nga bwe muli. Wadde ng'okwagala kwa Kitaffe tekuggwaawo era kwa muliro eri abaana be, Obukristaayo obw'amazima bubuulira okwenenya n'okusaasirwa ebibi, ebibi ebyatulwa mu mazima n'omutima omulongoofu ebireeta obulokozi n'obujuvu. Naye, bangi mu Kkanisa yange beekwasisa eby'obufirosoofo era ne beewolereza ebibi byabwe. Beewolereza mu ndowooza zaabwe ne banoonya ekifo eky'obulimba, nga batunuulira ab'oluganda lwabwe wansi mu kweraga okw'amalala nga basingirira obutali butuukirivu bwabwe mu kwewolereza kwabwe okw'obulimba. Okwo si kwe kumanya kwa Sitaani bwe yawa ekibala ekyagaananibwa eri nnyina w'abalamu bonna? Ebyo si bye bikolwa by'omubiri?

Mbabuuzizza ekibuuzo; mwenna mukimanyi ansa. Empeera y'ekibi y'eki? Ebyawandiikibwa Ebitukuvu bigamba nti empeera eyo y'eki? Si kufa? Naye, bangi ku mmwe muwulira nti ebibi byammwe birina ensonga era nti ky'ekimu ku ekyo ekibafuula abe'enjawulo, nga mukozesa bubi ekisa kyange. Mu kufa, mulifa ddala. Newankubadde mulina obulamu obutaggwaawo mu nze, era nja kubasonyiwa byonna era sijja kubasuula wabweru bwe munywerera mu nze, mu kufa mulifa ddala.

Okuva ku mukka gwammwe ogusooka, muzikirira olw'obuzaale bw'ekibi obubali mu mmwe.

Bwe mweyongera okukola ekibi n'okusiga mu mubiri, mujja kukungula kuvunda buli mulundi, kubanga mu kufa mulifa ddala. Ekibi kijja kutta buli kitundu ky'obulamu bwammwe n'omubiri gwammwe okutuuka ku mukka gwammwe ogusembayo bwe muneeyongera. Si ekyo kyokka, naye mujja kufiirwa empeera yammwe ne muyimirira n'ensoni mu ggulu ku ntebe yange ey'omusango ng'embaawo, essubi, n'ebisambu bye mwaterekanga byokya ne bifuuka kitaliiko mu maaso gammwe, era mujja kufiirwa nga ngyambula empeera yammwe ne ngiwa omulala, kubanga nze Mulamuzi omutuukirivu. Lwaki mujja gye ndi nga mwenenyezza ate ne muddayo ku bisesemye byammwe? Kiki kye mutuukako? Temutegeera nti temuyinza kuweereza nze ne Sitaani omulundi gumu? Bwe mwonoona, muba baddu ba kibi okutuusa lwe mujja gye ndi nga mweteefuteefu, era buli lulimi lulyatula! Lwaki mwegomba okuddayo eri ebintu ebinafu eby'obwavu? Ebyo byonna bye birikola kwe kunoonya okubatta nga bibatwala ng'omunyago.

Mumanye nti emyoyo emibi gyenyumiriza ku baana bange be gisobola okuwamba. Lwaki mulondawo okuleetera amannya gammwe ensoni ne mwefuula eky'okusekererwa? Mwerabidde okwagala kwammwe okusooka, era bangi ku mmwe mwetabye mu bulogo obw'Ekikristaayo n'Obukristaayo obw'okweyamba, engeri y'okukkiriza awatali nze, kubanga sijja kuyingira mu kkanisa y'e Lawodikiya wabula nja kuyimirira ku luggi nkonkone. Mbagambira lwatu nti endowooza yammwe ennungi tefuga kintu kyonna, era muli balimbe.

Okusanyuka mu kibi okw'akaseera obuseera kukulu nnyo gye muli? Temutegeera nti nja kubasasula nnyo n'ebintu eby'omu ggulu olw'ebizibu n'okugezesebwa kwammwe? Nja kubaliwa kye muweerayo ku lwange mu linnya lyange. Muddeyo eri okwagala kwammwe okusooka, abaanange, mujje gye ndi era mufulume mu Babulooni. Kubanga ennaku z'omwenzi omukulu zibalibwa, era tewaliba ddagala lyonna ku lulwe; kyemunaava mufuluma mu ye, abantu bange, mweyawule muleme okugabana ku bibonyoobonyo bye. Mwawukane n'okwegomba n'ebikolwa by'omubiri. Kubanga byonna ebiri mu nsi, okwegomba kw'omubiri, n'okwegomba kw'amaaso, n'amalala g'obulamu, tebiva eri Kitaffe, wabula biva mu nsi era ensi eggwaawo.

Mudduke obwenzi muleme okugabana mu nsoni n'ebivaamu. Baanange, kiringa mmwe bennyini abakola ekikolwa bwe musanyukira obujeemu n'obwononefu. Mwabulire engeri zammwe ez'omululu, kubanga nja kubonereza abo abeekunganiriza obugagga, era mu bbanga ttono ssente zammwe teziriba na mugaso. Mweyongere mu mwoyo gwange, kubanga omwoyo gwange bulamu era mirembe. Mwawukane n'obubi era temwonoonanga! Mukungule ekibala ky'omwoyo mu nze, kubanga mbawadde amaanyi ku bibi byammwe bwe munaajja gye ndi nga mweteefuteefu. Mulowooza nti muyinza okunkweka ekintu kyonna mu maaso gange? Nze mperebwa amaanyi okulaba, era nze ne Kitange tuli bumu mu bumu ne mu kigendererwa. Tekisoboka kukwekebwa, era tekisoboka kunkweka bibi byammwe. Bangi ku mmwe temulina kye mumanyi ku ngeri gye mulabikamu ey'ekifuulannenge bwe mwekolakolako ng'abataliiko kibi, ate nga amaaso gammwe gakaayana olw'okwegomba okukumisa ebiwato byammwe.

Mutegeere nti omubbi akozesa enkwe n'obukujjukujju bungi ku mmwe. Bwe mugoberera okwagala kwange okuziyiza ne mukyukira gye ndi, nze ndi muyambi aliwo buli kiseera mu kwetaaga era nja kubayamba, nja kubakuuma, nja kubalongoosa era nkolerewo engeri y'okuwonamu ebikemo ebingi bye mwolekagana nabyo, naye muteekwa okuziyiza n'amaanyi gammwe gonna. Tekitegeeza kintu kyonna bwe mugamba nti musonyiwe ne muddayo butereevu ku bisesemye byammwe, naye mwandyekanze okulaba embwa ng'ekola kye kimu, naye mu malala gammwe mulowooza nti muli ba njawulo, n'olwekyo temweyisanga ng'embwa ey'omu nsiko.

Ekibi kibi. Engeri yokka ey'okuwangula ekibi kwe kuyita mu musaayi gwange ogutangirira n'okuwummulira mu nze. Omwoyo gwe mbafukiddeko gujja kubawa amaanyi. Ntadde obuzaale obuggya mu mmwe, era bwe munaasaba obusabi, nja kutonda omutima omulongoofu mu mmwe.

Kale mulekere awo emimwa gyammwe emicaafu, muzibe amaaso gammwe ageegomba mu kusaba, era mwawukane n'obutali butuukirivu, nange nja kubalongoosa. Weewaawo, nja kubalongoosa ddala okuba abeeru ng'omuzira omupya bwe munaayatula era ne mukyuka ne muva ku makubo gammwe amacaafu. Abo bonna be njagala mbanenya; era abo bonna be njagala mbakangavvula. Taata ki atakangavvula mwana we? Wadde ng'abangi bakola ekyo, ezo si ze ngeri zange, era ezo si ze ngeri za Kitange. Okuva Katonda bw'abayisa ng'abaana, era ajja kubakangavvula ng'abaana; kubanga bw'atakangavvula, kale temuli baana be wabula muli baana ba ndese.

Naye muli baana be, n'olwekyo mutege amatugga eri ebigambo byange era mugumiikirize okukangavvulwa, era mugondere okweweerera, kubanga obulamu n'essanyu bibalindiridde. Tewali kukangavvula kulabika kwa ssanyu mu kiseera ekyo, naye kulireeta ebibala eby'emirembe eby'obutuukirivu. Mujje gye ndi, nange nja kubalongoosa okuva mu ngeri zammwe enkadde, era nja kubayigiriza engeri zange mulyoke mutambulire mu butuukirivu n'emirembe. Muyambe ab'oluganda lwammwe era mufuuke ekyokulabirako eky'okwenenya, kubanga mu kuyimirira kwammwe mujja kumenya enjegere nnyingi n'ebisiba bingi, n'ebisiba bye mukutte mu ngalo zammwe ne mubinyweza. Mbakubiriza mubireke byonna bigende. Ekibi kireeta busibe, era kisasula kufa; kyemunaava mufuluma mu ye, muleme okugabana ku misango gye.

Nze Mulamuzi omutuukirivu.

Obudde bufunda, abaanange, era embiro zammwe zijja kuggwaayo mangu nnyo. Temulowoozanga nti obutali butuukirivu bujja kuyingira mu bwakabaka bwange, oba nti mujja kuyimirira mu maaso gange mu bibi byammwe ne mutwala empeera. Abo bokka abawangula be bajja okuyimirira nange. Mujja kulokolebwa, naye lwaki mwandyagadde okufiirwa obukulu bwe mbategekedde, kubanga temuyinza na kutegeera kye muweerayo olw'okwegomba kwammwe.

Mujje gye ndi, kubanga ndi mugagga mu kusaasira.

Mujje gye ndi, kubanga ekisa kyange kijja kubayimirizaawo.

Mujje gye ndi, kubanga nze atambulira wakati mu bikondo by'ettaala omusanvu era nze ajja okukuuma empeera yammwe bwe mujja gye ndi mu bwetoowaze, nga mwabulira amalala gammwe agatalina mugaso. Mbagala nnyo, abaanange, era mu bbanga ttono mujja kuba nange. Laba! Nnyimiridde ku luggi era nkonkona akaseera katono kokka akakyasigaddeyo. Mbagambira bino si lwa kubanenya, naye mulyoke mube n'amafuta mu ttaala zammwe era mube beetegefu gye ndi bwe n'ajja. Nzijja mu kiseera kye mutasuubira, era nzijja mangu okusinga bwe muyinza okutebereza. Obudde busemberedde okuggwaawo.
