Obujulizi obubiri: Ekkanisa
Mu Bikorwa bya Yokaana Omutume Omubatiza
Gatonnya 17, 2024

Ensi ya Mukama!

Ensi ya Mukama n’okujjuza kwayo: Newankubadde omunyazi n’omubbi agiteereddeko obwannannyini bwe, asobola kukola ekyo kyokka Kitange ky’amukkiririzza mu nteekateeka Ye entukuvu ey’okununula abantu. Temumutya! Ateekwa okweyongerayo okumala ekiseera, kubanga ekiseera kye kituuse; naye temumutya, mmwe abatuukirivu bange, abeesigwa bange; temumutya! Ssibawanga kuwangula ye? Okubonaabona kwa Yakobo si kwa beesigwa bange, era abo abajja gyendi mu biseera eby’okutabangulwa baligulumizibwa nnyo mu kisenge ky’obwakabaka bwa Katonda. Yee, abantu bange abeesigwa, empeera ennene ebayitiridde olw’obwesigwa bwe mwandaze mu maaso gange.

Mbafudde bakabaka era bakabona gyendi mu Bwakabaka bwange obujja era ne mu maaso ga Kitaffe, kale munywere, baana bange, mu ddakiika zino ezisembayo ez’essaawa ey’enkomerero. Okulabika kwange kuli kumpi, n’okununulibwa kwammwe kuli kumpi ng’omukka gwammwe oguddako, kale mubeere beesigwa mu maaso gange, nga mumanyi nti nze maanyi gammwe n’obulokozi bwammwe era muli bateekebwako akabonero mu nze, kubanga nabagula n’omusaayi gwange bwe nawaayo obulamu bwange ku lwammwe mulyoke mutabaganyizibwe mu kwagala ne Kitaffe ali mu ggulu. Nneesunga olunaku olwo, ne Kitaffe yeesunga olunaku olwo, lwe muliyimirira mu maaso Ge mu mibiri gye mbalina mu kununulibwa kwammwe okujjuvu, so tewaabengawo nate kikolimo ku balonzi bange.

Ooh, ggwe omuntu, ggwe ani okuteeka obwannannyini ku busika bwange? Tomanyi nti nagula ensi n’ebirimu byonna n’omusaayi gwange ogutalina kibi? Ensi ya Mukama n’okujjuza kwayo, n’obutuukirivu bujja ku nsi! Newankubadde oyagala okuzikiriza ensi n’ogifuula ekifo eky’omuzizo eky’ebizira byo eby’omuzizo, newankubadde weeyama okugondera omulabe wange ow’emirembe n’emirembe n’okwenoonyeza okubonaabona okw’ekiseera okufuna amagoba amabi, totegeera nti olwana n’emyoyo gyo bwe weetereseza obusungu obw’okulunaku olw’obusungu, lwe ndisasula buli muntu okusinziira ku bikolwa bye.

Ooh, ggwe omusirusiru, weekaabira nti bakatonda bo balikulokola, so nga tebalina mpeera gyonna gye bakulinira, era olimbibwa ate ng’olimbye n’abalala mu bulimba bwo. Bakatonda bo ab’amayinja tebasobola kwogera; ebifaananyi byo ebiri mu mitima gyo tebisobola kuwulira; tebisobola kukulokola, gwe asinza ebifaananyi! Kyandibadde kirungi gy’oli singa tewazaalibwa, kubanga obusungu bwa Katonda bukubeerako olw’emitima gyo emikakanyavu. Newankubadde akamwa ko kafuuse entaana enzigule, nga kavuma ebikulu ebivvoola Mukama wa Bakama, ebigambo byo birikuddukako, n’okwegomba kwo okubi kulikyusibwa ne kukuddukako mu kubonerezebwa kwo okujja okw’emirembe n’emirembe. Ooh, singa wankyukira n’okkiriza obulokozi okuva mu mbeera yo ey’okugwa ey’omu kiseera kino. Ooh, singa wayatula erinnya lyange okuva ku mimwa gyo emirimbi. Naye nedda, oyagadde obulimba bwo, era oyagadde okulimbibwa kwo, era otunze emyoyo gyo eri okubonerezebwa okw’emirembe n’emirembe olw’omugaati ogutayinza kukukuuma; kubanga nze mugaati gw’obulamu! Okuzikirira kwo kulikutuukako amangu ddala era awatali dagala. Ndigabanya eggwanga lyo; ndizikiriza eby’obusuubuzi n’ebyenfuna byo; mu kaseera katono ndikunyaga obugagga bwo; mazima, ndikuggyako essanyu lyonna mu kaseera katono, n’obwavu bwo n’okubonaabona kwo birikusaanyaawo. Obusungu bw’Omwana gw’endiga busembera, obuwoolerwaggwanga bwange Kitange bwe yampa lwe bulituukirizibwa, kubanga nsaana okubikkula ebitabo n’okusumulula obubonero.

Osobedde n’olowooza nti okugumiikiriza kwange kunafunafu era tekweraliikiriza. Ogambye mu mitima gyo nti: Mukama ali ludda wa? Alinga lugero lwa dda. Tuyize ebisingawo okusinga amakubo ga Mukama agalokola; mu kumanya kwange, nsinga, kubanga tekinologiya wange wa maanyi okusinga ekitabo ekikadde eky’okweyawulayawula n’enfumo. Mazima, bangi ku mmwe temukkiriza nti najja mu kusooka ne nfuka omusaayi gwange ogununula olw’ebibi n’obulokozi bw’abantu. Kirungi singa tewazaalibwa, kubanga obusungu bwe weetereseza omwoyo gwo bulikutuukako awatali dagala, era olimanya bulungi ebibi byo, n’okubonaabona kwo okungi n’okweraliikirira bijja kuba bbaawo. Olijja kulya obubi bwo, n’amakubo go amabi gajja kubeeranga naawe, ng’oziikiddwa mu bibala by’obusungu mu bikolwa byo ebibi bye wakola mu bubi obungi ku linnya lyange. Nviira; sikumanyi!

Ani ku mmwe ayogera mu bukyamu mu linnya lyange? Mweyogera nti mummanyi, nti munkiikirira; abamu ku mmwe mulowooza nti musobola okulagira okukola kye mwagala n’okwefunira amagoba mu by’ensi olw’ebikolwa byammwe ebirungi, so nga tewali kigambo kirala wabula ebiwero eby’obugwagwa. Oyinza otya okunjogerera nga tonmanyi? Oyinza otya okunjogerera nga sikikuwadde bigambo? Oyinza otya okunjogerera eri abantu bange nga sikutumye? Nedda, mweetumye mwekka, nga mutunda ebyamaguzi byammwe ebyonoonedde olw’amagoba amabi ag’omululu n’olw’okutenderezebwa abantu kwe mwagala ennyo. Ggwe omusiru! Nviira, kubanga saakumanya!

Mmwe bannabbi ab’obulimba, abalangirira nze Mukama mu linnya lyokka; mmwe bannabbi ab’obulimba, abayogera obulimba obunene obuvvoola emyoyo gyammwe; mujja kuzikirira ddala, era ndibawanika okuva mu kamwa kange. N’olwekyo, mwenenye! Mudde gyendi nange ndidda gyemuli. Muve mu bifaananyi byammwe era muve mu bibiina byammwe ebikolimidde mujje gyendi munfuule Mukama wammwe! Ooh, singa mwakkiriza obulokozi bwange; naye musigala mu bibi byammwe ne mwekwasa obutaliiko musango bwe mujuliza Ekigambo kyange ekitukuvu okuva mu mutima omukalu, omutima ogujjudde obwenzi n’obwamalaya bwe muddukira eri ebifaananyi byammwe. Ani alibalokola okuva mu busungu bwange nga mubuzizza abantu bange olw’amagoba gammwe amabi? Singa mwanfuula Mukama ng’akamwa kammwe akalimbye bwe kalangirira, nandibalokodde okuva mu bibi byammwe ne mbafuula bazima mu nze. Nandisumuludde ebiwundu omubi by’abatuusizzaako. Nandibawadde ekkubo n’ekigendererwa ne mbatuma ku nsi n’emimwa egijjudde emikisa n’amazima nga mubadde mukulembedde abantu bange okujja gyendi.

Temulowooza nti nneetaaga obuyambi bwammwe, kubanga nze Mukama ow’amazima ow’eggulu n’ensi, era ndi Kabaka wa Bakabaka ne Mukama wa Bakama. Abo be nnalonda mu buweereza baweebwa ekitiibwa ekinene okunjogerera n’okulabirira ekisibo kyange, ekitiibwa kye ndisasula n’obugagga obw’emirembe n’emirembe; naye, mumpangisizza Kristo ow’obulimba. Yesu omulala gwe mwekoledde; mwagadde omwoyo gw’omulabe wa Kristo, era mwonoonese emirembe n’emirembe eri emyoyo gyammwe bwe mwalangirira nti siri mu Kkanisa yange gye nnagula n’omusaayi gwange, ekibi kye mutajja kuwonako. Mazima, bino byonna n’ebisingawo bijja kubatuukako mu busungu bwange okuggyako nga mweetoowazza mu maaso gange ne munfuula ddala Mukama wammwe bwe mwenenya mu bibukutu n’evvu. Temunoonya nsonga mu bigambo byange bwe muzannya n’amagezi mu kwogera kwange! Mwenenye! Mutuule mu bibukutu ne mu vvu mukaabire ebibi byammwe, nange ndibaaniriza gyendi ne mbazzaawo eri empeera yammwe gye nandisobodde okuwa omulala.

Nze Mukama omutuukirivu.

Nze mulokozi w’ensi, Kabaka wa Bakabaka era Mukama wa Bakama.

Nze oyo atambulira mu bikkondo by’ettaala omusanvu era alagira bamalayika omusanvu ab’ekkanisa omusanvu.

Nze mwana gw’endiga eyattibwa okuva ku ntandikwa y’ensi, era mu nze mulimu obulamu obutaggwaawo.

Nnina ebisumuluzo by’okufa n’emagombe, era Kitange agabanye nange entebe ye ey’obwakabaka, nsobole okugabana entebe yange ey’obwakabaka n’abeesigwa bange, abayite, n’abalonde. Ntambulira wamu n’abantu bange era njogera n’abantu bange, era nze musumba ow’amazima asumba ekisibo ekyo Kitange ky’ampadde. Mmwe mmunye y’eriiso lyange, mmwe abeesigwa bange, era mmwe mmunye y’eriiso lya Kitange, era olw’ekiseera kino n’essaawa eno muziziddwaamu amaanyi okutuuka ku butuukirivu n’obutuukirivu obw’amazima.

Essaawa esembera, era muteekwa okuleka emirimu gyammwe mulindirire okulabika kwange, kubanga kinaatera okujja ku nsi bwe mbakuŋŋaanyiza okuva ku nsonda ennya ez’ensi. Mazima, mazima, ndikuŋŋaanyiza endiga zange ez’amazima mu ggwanika lyange nga sembuyaga tannajja anaazikiriza ensi eno embi ey’omu kiseera kino, kubanga obwakabaka bwa Setaani bujja kutemebwatemebwa ne buzikirizibwa ddala okusobozesa Mukama omutuukirivu anaafuga ensi okuva ku ntebe ya Dawudi mu butuukirivu n’obutuukirivu obw’amazima, era ndigulumiza nnyo omugole wange mu bwakabaka obujja, kubanga be bakabaka bange era bakabona bange, era balifuga mu butuukirivu obw’amazima n’obutukuvu, kubanga empeera yange eri mu ngalo zange eri abeesigwa.

Nze maanyi gammwe n’obulokozi bwammwe, era muli bateekebwako akabonero mu nze.

Nze mugaati gw’obulamu!

Nsaana okubikkula ebitabo n’okusumulula obubonero.

Nze mukama ow’amazima ow’eggulu n’ensi.

Nze kabaka wa bakabaka.
