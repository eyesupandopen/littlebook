Omujulirwa Omulundi Ogw'okubiri: Isirayiri
Okukaawa Okw'obuwomi
Ntenvu 3, 2024

Omusaayi gw'Omwana gw'endiga owa Yakuwa

Mujje eri Mukama wammwe ow'emirembe n'emirembe muwulire ebigambo byange, mmwe ab'oluganda lwange, era mukotamye emitima gyammwe mu buwulize eri ebigambo bya Yeshua HaMashiach. Kubanga nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, ndifuula omugezi mu mmwe, ggwe Isirayiri, okuba omugezi n'okusingawo bw’onoogonderanga amateeka gange olyoke ofune amagezi n'okumanya ebirala, kubanga amagezi n'okumanya bye biriba obunywevu bw'ebiseera byo n'amaanyi g'obulokozi, era okutya Mukama bwe bugagga bwo.

N'olwekyo, mutegeere ebiseera mwe muli mulyoke mutambulirenga mu ntegeka yange era ebigendererwa byange eby'obulokozi bituukirire mu bujjuvu mu mmwe abayitebwa okusinziira ku linnya lyange, ee, ggwe Isirayiri, wadde nga mu kiseera kino temummanyi so temutambulira mu makubo gange kubanga Isirayiri afugibwa Esawu ne Asikenazi, ate nga kyategeezebwa lwatu nti omukulu anaweerezanga omuto era nga Esawu oba Asikenazi tebalina kye banunula ku buzaaliranwa bwa Yakobo obw'obusika. Naye ate, Yakobo yagaana Yeshua HaMashiach nange ne nkyukira amawanga okumala akaseera ntuukirize ekyo Isirayiri kye yali tagonderanga kutuukiriza mu bujjuvu, nti okuva mu Isirayiri amawanga gaali gaakuweebwa omukisa era nti ku bika ebyabula ensigalira yandirokoddwa.

Wabula, bwe mulikyusa emitima gyammwe mu buwulize eri eddoboozi ly’ebigambo bya Masiya wammwe ow'amazima eby'okuyigiriza, mulizzibwawo okuba abantu ab'enjawulo eri Yakuwa, wadde nga bajjajjammwe baava ku Yakuwa edda. Yee, baava ku Yakuwa bwe banjeemera, nze Yesu Omunazaaleeti, era ye Yeshua HaMashiach, ne mu nnaku Pontiyo Piraato lwe yafuga Buyudaaya bwe nnaleetebwa bakabona baabwe abakulu n'Olukiiko Olukulu mu mikono gy'abantu aboonoonyi okukola ebyo amateeka ge gaabagaana lwatu okukola, naye mu bukyayi bwabwe gye ndi ne ku bintu byonna ebya Yakuwa baabikola amangu n'amalala n'obukambwe obungi. Era bwe butyo obulokozi bwa Adamu ne butuukirizibwa n'emikono gy'abalabe be, buli muntu akkiriza n'akoowoola erinnya lya Yesu Omunazaaleeti era ye Yeshua HaMashiach alirokolebwa mu bujjuvu.

Era bwe kityo kubanga nze mwana gw'endiga owa Yakuwa, okutangirira ebibi by’omuntu kwandisobose kutya okutuukirizibwa okuggyako ng'omusaayi gwange ogutalina kibi guyiika nga ssaddaaka okusasula omuwendo ogutabalika. Mutegeere nti wadde omuntu ow'obutonde yeenyumiriza mu malala amangi nti yeekola era yemalira mu byonna, omuntu ow'obutonde yagwa mu mbeera ey'ekibi n'ey'okunenya ey'obutali butuukirivu n'okuvumaganyizibwa okumugwanidde.

Naye, mu kisa kyange n'okusaasira kwange n'okuweebwayo kwange ku Gologoosa bwe nneeyanjula eri Yakuwa ng'omwana gw'endiga owa Katonda bwe nneewaayo mu kubonaabona okungi ettondo lyange ly'omusaayi erisembayo bwe lyakulukuta ku Gologoosa, okutuusa ku mukka gwange ogwasembayo bwe nnayogera ebigambo nti kiwedde. Ne nziikibwa, ne nzuukira ku lunaku olw'okusatu okusinziira ku byawandiikibwa; nga Yakuwa bwe yasuubiza okukola, kubanga tekyasobokanga okufa okukwata omusaayi ogutalina kibi. Era Yakuwa n'anzuukiza, oluvannyuma lw'ekyo ne nnyingira mu kifo ekitukuvu ennyo mu ggulu mwennyini, gye nneeyanjulira nga kabona asinga obukulu emirembe gyonna eri Yakuwa, Nze Ndi aliwo, ssaddaaka ye gye yakkiriza emirembe gyonna. Kale bwe mulaba nti mulina kabona omukulu atyo, eyayingira mu ggulu, ye Yesu omwana wa Yakuwa, tunyweze okwatula kwaffe. Kubanga temulina kabona omukulu, ggwe Isirayiri, atasobola kukwatibwako kunakuwala kwammwe, naye eyakemebwa mu byonna nga ffe, kyokka n'atasobya.

Kale mbabuuza, ggwe Isirayiri, nti kiki ekyawedde? Kale nyanukula lwatu: Endagaano yange empya, ye ndagaano eteriggwaawo, era Endagaano yange Empya! Kubanga endagaano ekakasibwa abantu bwe bafa: kubanga bw'etyo teba na maanyi oyo eyagikola bw'abaakyali mulamu; era kumpi ebintu byonna birongoosebwa na musaayi okusinziira ku mateeka, era awatali kuyiwa musaayi tewaba kusonyiyibwa bibi. Mu ngeri y'emu ng'Endagaano Enkadde Musa bwe yamala okwogera buli kiragiro eri abantu, okusinziira ku Mateeka, n'addira omusaayi gw'ennyana n'embuzi, n'amazzi n'ebyoya eby'effulungu ne ezobu, n'amansira ekitabo n'abantu bonna, ng'agamba nti, Guno gwe musaayi gw'endagaano, Katonda gye yabalagira.

Naye, endagaano ey'edda, era endagaano ya Musa ey'emabega, teyali ya kugendererwako kukomekkereza nteekateeka ya Yakuwa ey'okununula abantu, so ne Isirayiri teyali wa kukuŋŋaana mu malala, ng'akola ebiwayi eby'enjawulo mu njawukana nnyingi ezitali za mugaso, era ez'obussi. Naye mu kifo ky'ekyo Isirayiri yalina okuba ng'ekyoto eky'obulokozi eri ab'amawanga amawanga balyoke balabe nti waliyo Katonda mu Isirayiri era amawanga galyoke gakulukutire mu Isirayiri okuyiga ebikwata ku Yakuwa nga bwe kyawandiikibwa mu biseera eby'edda.

N'olwekyo, mutegeere obwangu bw'amateeka ga Musa nga Isirayiri bwe yagakolangako mu biseera eby'emabega w'Endagaano yange Empya era mutegeere nti Musa abeeramu ddala mu Masiya wammwe ow'amazima, ye Mutukuvu wa Katonda eyatambulira mu bugonvu okusinziira ku Musa era eyawaayo obulamu bwe omulundi gumu, byonna, n'emirembe gyonna okusasulira ekibonerezo kya Musa ku lwammwe. Nga bwe kyawandiikibwa, n'olwekyo, mutegeere Endagaano yange Empya, ggwe Isirayiri: Kubanga Amateeka bwe galina ekisiikirize ky'ebirungi ebigenda okujja, so si kifaananyi ky'ebintu, tegayinza na ssaddaaka ezo, ze bawaayo buli mwaka bulijjo, okutukuza abajjawo. Kubanga tebyandirekeddwaawo kuwaayo, kubanga abajjawo, bwe bamala okulongoozebwa omulundi gumu, tebandibaddewo nate kweraliikirira bibi? Naye mu ssaddaaka ezo, mulimu okujjukira nate ebibi buli mwaka.

Kubanga tekiyinzika musaayi gwa nte ne mbuzi kuggyawo bibi. Era bwe kityo ab'e waffe, nti Musa yasobola kulongoosa bibi okumala akaseera, naye ebibi bingi wansi wa Musa ne bisigalawo abo abaakola ebintu ebyo gye baagobebwa mu bantu emirembe gyonna olw'ebibi byabwe. N'olwekyo, Musa yali kisiikirize ky'ebintu ebigenda okujja, ebintu ebyo bituukiririzibwa mu bujjuvu mu ssaddaaka yange nze kennyini, kubanga omusaayi gwange ogutalina kibi gutangirira ebibi byonna, omulundi gumu byonna n'emirembe gyonna, era n'abo bonna ab'omu Adamu basobole okufuna obulokozi. Naye abo abakkiriza akabonero k'ensolo, tebalifuna kisonyiwo.

Era bwe kityo bwe kyawandiikibwa ku Yeshua HaMashiach, nti olw'obulamu bwange n'obuwulize bwange Adamu yandisobodde okuzzibwa eri Elohim, Omutonzi we ow'emirembe n'emirembe era omutuukirivu. N'olwekyo, bw'ajja mu nsi, agamba nti, ssaddaaka n'ekiweebwayo tewabyagala: naye omubiri gw'onteekerateekera. Mu biweebwayo ebyokebwa, n'ebiweebwayo olw'ekibi, tewasanyukamu. Awo ne ŋŋamba nti, Laba, nzize (Mu ntandikwa y'ekitabo kyawandiikibwa ku nze) nti nkole by'oyagala, ai Katonda. Waggulu, bwe yagamba nti, Ssaddaaka n'ekiweebwayo, n'ebiweebwayo ebyokebwa, n'ebiweebwayo olw'ekibi tewabyagala, so tewasanyukamu (ebiweebwayo okusinziira ku Mateeka) Awo n'agamba nti, Laba, nzize okukola by'oyagala, ai Katonda,aggyawo eky'olubereberye, alyoke anyweze eky'okubiri. Olw'okwagala okwo kwe tutukuzibwa, kwe kuwaayo omubiri gwa Yesu Kristo omulundi gumu. Era buli Kabona wa Leevi ayimirira bulijjo ng'aweereza, era emirundi mingi awaayo ekiweebwayo ekya ngeri emu, ekisobola obutasobola kuggyawo bibi: Naye omusajja ono bwe yamala okuwaayo ssaddaaka emu olw'ebibi, n'atuula emirembe gyonna ku mukono ogwa ddyo gwa Katonda, Era okuva olwo alindirira, okutuusa abalabe be lwe balifuulibwa entebe y'ebigere bye. Kubanga n'ekiweebwayo kimu yatuukiriza emirembe gyonna abo abatukuzibwa.

Kubanga ne Yakuwa atutegeeza: kubanga bwe yamala okugamba nti, Eno y'Endagaano gye ndiragaana nabo oluvannyuma lw'ennaku ezo, bw'atyo bw'agamba Elohim, nditeeka amateeka gange mu mutima gwabwe, ne mu birowoozo byabwe ndigawandiika. N'ebibi byabwe n'obutali butuukirivu bwabwe siribijjukira nate. Kaakano awali okusonyiyibwa kw'ebintu bino, tewakyali kiweebwayo kya kibi. Nammwe muli mu kiseera ky'obutamanya ng'eggwanga bajjajjammwe gye baajeemera edda oyo ali Omutukuvu Omwana gw'Endiga owa Ppaasika owa Yakuwa yennyini, era Yakuwa bwe yayagala ennyo ensi, n'okuwaayo Omwana we omu yekka eyazaalibwa buli muntu akkiririza mu linnya lya Yeshua HaMashiach, ye Yesu Kristo omuntu oyo alirokolebwa mu bujjuvu. Kale mbayita okuva mu butamanya bwammwe n'obuzibe bw'amaaso obw'ekitundu, ggwe Isirayiri, nga mugaana era ne mwegaana ddala amagezi amavume ga Esawu agali mu Talmud ye embi era muddire ebigambo ebirongoofu ebya Yakuwa, era ebigambo ebyo bikuumiddwa mu luwandiiko lw'ebiwandiiko ebya Byzantine, ye Textus Receptus nga bwe yakuumibwa mu kiwandiiko kya Stephens.

N'olwekyo, mutegeere okugaana Yeshua HaMashiach kye kitegeeza gye muli emirembe gyonna mmwe Abaisirayiri mulyoke mutegeere obukulu bw'okugaana okwo. Kubanga bw'oyonoona kyeyagalire ng'omaze okufuna n'okukkiriza amazima ago, tewakyaliyo ssaddaaka ya bibi, wabula okulindirira okw'entiisa okw'omusango, n'omuliro ogw'effujjo, ogulirya abalabe. Oyo eyanyooma Amateeka ga Musa, yafa awatali kusaasira wansi w'abajulirwa babiri, oba basatu: Ku kibonerezo eky'entiisa bwe kiti mulowooza nti kye kigwanidde, alinyirira Omwana wa Katonda wansi w'ebigere bye, n'abala omusaayi gw'Endagaano Empya ng'ekintu ekitatukuvu. N'olwekyo, mukkirize ssaddaaka yange era mwatule ebibi byammwe gye ndi nsobole okubasonyiwa kubanga ndi mwesigwa era mutuukirivu okubasonyiwa ebibi byammwe mulyoke mulongoosebwe mu musaayi gwange ogutangirira era mwatulirenga ebisobyo byammwe.

N'olwekyo, njija gye muli mu kusaasira kwange n'ekisa kyange nsobole okubaggya mu buzibe bwammwe obw'ekitundu, ggwe Isirayiri, mulyoke mufune obulokozi olw'obuwulize bwammwe eri eddoboozi lyange era mulyoke muve ku kugaana kwa bajjajjammwe okwateekebwawo emirembe gyonna mu Talmud yammwe embi ne mu ddiini yammwe embi Esawu gy'abakolera okuzikirizibwa kwammwe, naye gye mwekwata nga nti amagezi ge gaweebwa Yakuwa yennyini, so si Setaani. Ggwe Isirayiri, wulira eddoboozi lyange erirokola okkirize okwagala kwange gye muli era mujje gye ndi n'emitima gyammwe gyonna nga miggule era myetegefu okukkiriza mulyoke mujjuzibwe ekitiibwa kyonna n'obulungi.

Mbawa okutegeera okusonyiyibwa okujjuvu n'okuzzibwa obuggya omusaayi gwange ogutangirira gye guleeta eri bonna abankkiriza nga Mukama waabwe alokola, era nga Masiya waabwe ow'amazima, kubanga bwe ntyo bwe ndi. Era nga Adamu bwe yatondebwa, n'abumbibwa, n'akolebwa nga talina kibi era mu kutuukirira okujjuvu okutuusa ku lunaku lwe yayonoona kyeyagalire, n'okusasulira ekibi kya Adamu kyetaaga omusaayi ogutalina kibi, era ekyo kyetaagisa ekitasoboka eri bonna abali ku nsi mu kwonooneka okukiwaayo. Era mu kwagala kwa Yakuwa yawaayo okutuukirira okwo n'omusaayi ogutalina kibi mu mubiri gw'omwana we omu yekka eyazaalibwa aweereddwayo nga ssaddaaka olw'ebibi bya Adamu, n'olwekyo tewakyali ssaddaaka ya kibi.

Nze oyo eyeewaayo mu bujjuvu olw'ebibi bya Adamu eyagwa.

Nze oyo eyeewaayo mu bujjuvu mu kutangirira okw'emirembe n'emirembe bonna abeewaayo eri obulokozi obw'emirembe n'emirembe balyoke balongoosebwe.

Nze Kabona wammwe Asinga Obukulu emirembe gyonna okusinziira ku nkola ya Merukizeddeeki, era oyo eyeewaayo ng'omwana gw'endiga owa Ppaasika omulundi gumu, byonna, n'emirembe gyonna.

Nze Yeshua HaMashiach, era oyo ye Masiya wammwe ow'amazima era obufuzi bwange tebuliggwaawo.

Nze Kristo Yesu, era ntumye Ebigambo byange ng'ebiyita mu mubaka wange era omuweereza wange era ng'ebiyita mu mubaka wange era omuweereza wange Ebigambo byange bitumiddwa.

N'olwekyo, mutegeere nti okutuukirizibwa kw'ebintu byonna kutuuse, ggwe Isirayiri, era mbayita okujja gye ndi nga muyita mu Njiri eteriggwaawo eweereddwa mu biseera ebigwanidde eby'okubikkulirwa era n'ebiseera eby'okubikkulirwa kaakano. Kubanga okuva ku lubereberye Yakuwa yagamba omusota mu lusuku mu biseera ebibi we byayingirira mu nsi nti, Nditeeka obulabe wakati wo n'omukazi n'ezzadde lyo n'ezzadde lye, olimuluma ekisinziiro, naye alikubetenta omutwe.

Era bwe waayitawo ekiseera Dawudi, bwe yali omulenzi omusumba, eyafuuka Kabaka wa Isirayiri obwakabaka nga tebunnaba kwawulibwamu; era eyafuuka omusajja ow'omutima gwa Yakuwa, n'alwanirira Isirayiri n'atta omuzimbi Omufirisuuti, ye Goliyaasi ow'e Gaasi eyali ensigo eya wuzi eyava mu Bafalimu! Awo Dawudi omulenzi omusumba n'agenda okulwana ne Goliyaasi ow'e Gaasi ng'omusumba, n'omuggo, n'ensawo n'envuumuulo, ng'agenda okulwana n'omuzimbi omulwanyi, naye Dawudi n'agenda okulwana mu linnya lya Yakuwa, Abafirisuuti gwe baanyooma, era Dawudi n'ayimirira okulwanyisa abalabe ba Isirayiri eggye lya Elohim lisobole okuwangula.

Ejjinja okuva mu nvuumuulo ya Dawudi bwe lyagwisa omuzimbi ono omutali mutukuvu ku ttaka ng'agaaliimbira, Dawudi n'asowola ekitala ky'omuzimbi oyo n'amusalako omutwe. Era nga bwe kyawandiikibwa edda nti: Dawudi n'addira omutwe gw'Omufirisuuti, n'aguleeta e Yerusaalemi, naye eby'okulwanyisa bye n'abiteeka mu weema ye. Awo Dawudi n'aziika ekiwanga kya Goliyaasi ow'e Gaasi ebweru w'ebisenge by'ekibuga Yerusaalemi. Naye, mu ssaddaaka yange nze kennyini ng'omwana gw'endiga owa Katonda, nnatwalibwa ebweru w'ebisenge by'ekibuga era eyo ku Gologoosa ne nkomererwa, era eyo ye w'ekiwanga. Era bwe nnakomererwa, bankomerera ku muti ogwo omukakanyavu gye baaluma ekisinziiro kyange n'emisumaali bwe bankomerera ku muti ogwo omukakanyavu. Era awo obuwanguzi ne bufuunibwa Adamu ekisinziiro kyange bwe kyalumizibwa eyo ku Kalevaali omutwe gwa Setaani gye gwabetentebwa era omusaayi gwange bwe gwakulukuuta ne guleeta obuwanguzi ku zzadde ly'omusota emirembe gyonna. N'olwekyo, mu ngeri y'emu omulenzi omusumba bwe yawangula ezzadde lya Setaani, n'omusumba omukulu bwe yamala n'awangula Setaani emirembe gyonna mu ssaddaaka yange ey'okwagala gye nnakola olw'ebibi bya Adamu.

Naye, bwe nnali nfa mu kubonaabona okungi ng'omumenyi w'amateeka owabulijjo (obumenyi bw'amateeka obwo sirina musango) ebweru w'emiryango gy'ekibuga ekyo ekitukuvu, omusaayi gwange ogutalina kibi ne gukulukuta okuva mu mubiri gwange mu kwolesa okujjuvu okw'omusajja omwononefu ow'ettaka eddungi ng'ajja mu kununulibwa okujjuvu. Era omusaayi gwange bwe gwanyikira ettaka eddungi mu kifo eky'ekiwanga eyo ku Kalevaali omutwe gw'omusota ne gubetentebwa emirembe gyonna, era mu kutuukirizibwa kw'ebintu byonna ogusota ogwo ogukadde gulissibwa ne gusuulibwa emirembe gyonna mu muliro gw'okubonerezebwa awalema kye kitafa, kubanga obwo bwe busika bwe.

Era nze oyo ajja okuzikiriza ddala obwakabaka bw'abalabe ogusota gusobole okusibibwa ne guteekebwako akabonero mu bunnya okumala emyaka lukumi okufuga kwange okw'obutuukirivu ku nsi kusobole okunywezebwa. Ku nkomerero yaayo alisumululibwa okumala akaseera ke akatono gy'alikuŋŋaanya okwewaggula kwe okusembayo. Era bwe balumba olusiisira lw'abatukuvu omuliro guliserengeta okuva eri Yakuwa yennyini ne gubookya ddala ku kiseera ekyo Setaani alisuulibwa mu muliro ogutazikira ogwaka emirembe gyonna n'ekibiriiti ekivunda.

N'olwekyo, mbawa okutegeera, ggwe Isirayiri, nti ekibonerezo kya Musa kisasulwa mu bujjuvu olwa ssaddaaka ya Yeshua HaMashiach ye Yesu Kristo mu lulimi Olungereza era mulyoke mutegeere obujjuvu bwa ssaddaaka yange n'okutuukirira okwatuukirizibwa n'omusaayi gwange ogutangirira ogwayiika ne guweebwayo olw'ebibi bya Adamu. Mutegeere mu bujjuvu, ggwe Isirayiri, nti singa abagwa baali bategeedde ekyama kya Yakuwa, tebandikomeredde Mukama wammwe ow'ekitiibwa! Kubanga olw'okukomererwa kwange omusota gye gwanduma ekisinziiro okumala akaseera, omutwe gw'omusota kaakano gubetentebwa emirembe gyonna, era ekyo kiwundu ky'atasuubira kuwona.

N'olwekyo, ggwe Isirayiri, wulira ebigambo byange otegeere okwagala kwange gye muli nti mbayita mu kiseera kino ekisembayo. Kale mutegeere ebigambo byange kubanga kyawandiikibwa nti: Katonda ow'emirembe eyazuukiza Yeshua HaMashiach, omusumba omukulu ow'endiga, ng'ayita mu musaayi gw'endagaano eteriggwaawo, abatuukirize mu bikolwa byonna ebirungi, okukola by'ayagala, ng'akolera mu mmwe ekyo ekimusiimisa nga kiyita mu Yesu Kristo, aweebwe ettendo emirembe n'emirembe, Amiina.
