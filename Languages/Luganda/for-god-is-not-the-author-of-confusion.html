Abajulirwa Ababiri: Ekkanisa
Mu Biwandiiko bya Peetero
Nakasenene 20, 2024

Kubanga Katonda si ye Nsibuko y’Akatabankanyo!

Kubanga Katonda si ye nsibuko ya katabanguko, naye ya mirembe, nga bwe kiri mu kkanisa zonna ez'abatukuvu! Musome ebigambo bino era mutege amatu eri eddoboozi lyange. Mukkirize okutu kwammwe, muwulire ebigambo by'ab'amagezi, era muteeke omwoyo gwammwe ku kumanya kwange. Oboolyawo mwandigoberedde ekyokulabirako kyange eky’omu nnimiro bwe nnasaba n'obulumi obungi bwe nnagwira ku ttaka mu maaso ga Kitange nga nsaba mu bwetoowaze, n'anzizaamu amaanyi okukkiriza ekikompe ky'obusungu olw'ebibi byammwe bye nneetikikka ku lwammwe, kubanga nze nsobola okubyetikka. Njogerera ab'amalala mu Kkanisa yange, era njogerera enjawukana ennyingi eziri mu mmwe. Naye ggwe lwaki osalira muganda wo omusango? Oba lwaki onyoomoola muganda wo? Kubanga ffenna tuliyimirira mu maaso g'entebe y'omusango gwa Kristo. Tekyawandiikibwa mu bbaluwa gye nnawandiikira abagoberezi b'Ekubo e Roomo? Naye Zekkaliya agamba atya ku nsonga eno: Naye ne bagaana okuwulira, ne baggyawo ekibegabega, ne baziba amatu gaabwe, baleme okuwulira. Temusomanga Isaaya, abange abato? Kubanga bannabbi ab'edda bagamba batya? Kubanga Isaaya ate agamba nti, Kuwulira muliwulira naye temulitegeera: na kulaba muliraba naye temulimanya.

Baana bange, mbalaze ekkubo erisinga obulungi, naye bangi ku mmwe mugudde okuva mu kisa era amalala gammwe amakakanyavu gabaleetedde okwegulumiza okusukka ekigero. Naye ebigambo byange bye nnawa Omutume Pawulo eri Abagalatiya bigamba butereevu nti: Kubanga omuntu bw'alowooza okuba ekintu, so nga si kintu, yeerimba yekka. Baana bange abato, mmanyi nti muli basanyufu mu mutima okumpeereza, era mmanyi nti munjagala, wabula oluusi si lwa kuwuliriza kumanya. Temutegeera nti Katonda aziyiza ab'amalala naye ab'obuwombeefu abawa ekisa? Kale mwetoowaze wansi w'omukono gwa Katonda ogw'amaanyi, alyoke abagulumize mu kiseera kyakyo. Bwe mutyo nammwe abavubuka, mugonderenga abakadde bammwe. Weewaawo, mwenna mugonderanenga, era mwambalenga obuwombeefu. Katonda aziyiza ab'amalala naye ab'obuwombeefu abawa ekisa. Temulowooza nti ebigambo byange mbiddaabiddamu, kubanga mu kamwa k'abajulirwa babiri oba basatu buli kigambo kirinywezebwa. Mutegeere, baana bange, temusobola kumpeereza n'omubiri gwammwe: Kubanga byonna ebiri mu nsi, okwegomba kw'omubiri, n'okwegomba kw'amaaso, n'amalala g'obulamu, si bya Kitange, wabula bya nsi. Mmwe Ekkanisa yange, mugonderanenga mwekka na mwekka mu ndowooza eyambadde obuwombeefu, kubanga nze nnyinza okuba nga mbadde mbawa ebigambo eby'okwogera n'okutiiririrwa okubayigiriza, newankubadde bangi ku mmwe muliggyawo eddoboozi ly'omuwombeefu ne musalawo okugoberera ettutumu n’ekitiibwa ky'omuntu. Mugobererenga Katonda, baana bange, kubanga mazima ddala Katonda tasosola mu bantu.

Laba! Kiteesa Katonda okukweka ekigambo: naye kitiibwa kya bakabaka okunoonya ekigambo. Mutegeere nti ndibafuula bakabaka era bakabona mu maaso ga Katonda waffe era Kitaffe, kale mutegeere ebigambo by'ab'amagezi mu mmwe: Katonda tabawanga matu abiri na kamwa kamu? Temusomanga? Buli omu ku mmwe alina zabbuli, alina enjigiriza, alina olulimi, alina okubikkulirwa, alina okutegeera: Byonna bikolebwenga okuzimba! Naye nedda, mulumagana era mulyanagana mu malala gammwe; mu malala gammwe mulumba abo abagezaako okubayigiriza, era mufuuse abatayigirizika. Muwakana, muyomba, muvuganya, era mulwanagana mu kifo ky'okufumiitiriza mu buwombeefu ku ekyo omulala ky'agamba. Ku ludda olulala, muli balalu nnyo okulowooza nti olw'okuba ebigambo biva mu mimwa gyammwe nti bituufu kubanga MMWE mubyogedde? Mugonderanenga mwekka na mwekka. Musirike era mutwale ebigambo by'abooluganda mu byawandiikibwa okukakasa byonna era munywerere ku birungi. Muzibule amatu gammwe muwulirize ddala era mufumiitirize, kubanga mu kukola mutyo ndibafuula ab'amagezi mu buwombeefu bwammwe, kale temuba b'amagezi mu kulowooza kwammwe. Mbuulirira buli omu mu mmwe, muwulire ebigambo byange. Era ebigambo ebyo bigamba bitya? Kubanga njogera, okuyitira mu kisa ekyampeebwa, eri buli muntu ali mu mmwe, aleme okwerowooza okusinga bwe kisaana, naye alowooze mu ngeri ey'okwegendereza, nga Katonda bwe yagabira buli muntu ekigera ky'okukkiriza.

Nedda, yimirira, fumiitiriza ku bigambo bye nkakakuwa, ggulawo Baibuli zo osome ebigambo bino nate. Biwulire obikkirize, kubanga kikugwanidde okwerowoozaako ebirungi, kubanga mbazukirizza wamu ne mbatuuza wamu mu bifo eby'omu ggulu mu nze, Kristo Yesu. Naye mu kiseera kye kimu mwandyagadde okulowooza mu kwegendereza, kubanga Katonda agabidde buli muntu mu mmwe ekigera kye kimu eky'okukkiriza, n’olwekyo omwoyo ogwamuweebwa si gusingako oba okutonera ku gw'abooluganda, naye buli omu ku mmwe aweereddwa ekisa, okusinziira ku kigera ky'ekirabo kya Kristo. Kyeyava agamba nti: Bwe yalinnya waggulu, yatwala obusibe nga musibe, n'awa abantu ebirabo. Mpayo abamu okuba abatume: n'abamu bannabbi: n'abamu ababuulizi b'enjiri: n'abamu abasumba, n'abayigiriza: Okutuukiriza abatukuvu, olw'omulimu gw'obuweereza, olw'okuzimba omubiri gwa Kristo. Mugonderanenga era muleke amalala gammwe agatalina mugaso kubanga galeeta enjawukana mu mmwe. Lwaki temutegeera nti tewandibaddewo njawukana mu mmwe, baana bange. Omubiri gwa Kristo gwawukanamu? Kiki kye nnagamba Abakkolinso?

Kale mbeegayirira, abooluganda, mu linnya lya Mukama waffe Yesu Kristo, mwenna mwogere ekigambo kye kimu, era muleme okubaamu njawukana: naye mutukirizibwe mu ndowooza emu, ne mu kusala omusango okumu. Si mwenna muli bumu mu nze, Mukama waffe Yesu Kristo? Naye mulina ebiwayi bingi nnyo mu mmwe, era mulumagana ne muliraganira obutakkaanya obutono ennyo. Mumanye nti mu biwayi byammwe n'okwogeragana obubi musanyusa Setaani, n'anyumirwa olw'obufu bwa maanyi gammwe agamalibwa mu kulwanagana kubanga bangi ku mmwe tebalina buwombeefu. N'amatu gammwe gazibikidde ne mutasobola kuwuliragana maddoboozi ga bannaabwe, era muzibye amaaso gammwe, era gasibiddwa wamu mu malala n'obutakkiriza, kubanga bangi nnyo ku mmwe mulowooza nti mulina amazima gonna. Era munyooma ebigambo by'abooluganda, era munyooma ebigambo byennyini bye mbawadde ku lwammwe, era mu malala gammwe amakakanyavu temulina buwombeefu bwa nsibo obw'okuwulira n'okufumiitiriza. Kubanga omuntu bw'atawulira kigambo, busirusiru era nsonyi gy'ali. Era nate, lwaki munyooma abooluganda, so nga ffenna tulina okuyimirira mu maaso g'entebe y'omusango gwa Kristo? Mbuulirira mmwe, Ekkanisa yange, okwegaana amalala gammwe n'empisa zammwe ez'okwegulumiza era mujje gye ndi ng'omuyizi w'Ekigambo ky'obulamu, kubanga nze Kigambo kya Katonda ekyefuula omubiri.

Kale muwulirize n'emmeeme yammwe yonna era muteeke omwoyo ku maloboozi g'amatu gammwe era mutegeere ebigambo byange. Kale mutegeere engeri y'ebintu ng' bwe yawandiikibwa mu Kigambo kyange, kubanga bangi ku mmwe balimbibwa emisege emirulajjana gye mugoberera mu butamanya, kubanga muwulirizza abasumba abakyamu. Mmwe baani okubuulira nti tewali kukuŋŋaana kwa Kkanisa yange gye ndi, nti sirisisinkana Kkanisa yange mu bbanga bwe ndijja n'omwanguka omunene n'eddoboozi ly'omutume omukulu n'ekkondeere lya Katonda? Kubanga ekkondeere lirivuga n'abafu mu Kristo balizuukira olubereberye nammwe abali abalamu abalisigalawo temulibasooka, era mwenna mulitwalibwa mu bire okunsisinkana era munaabeeranga nange emirembe gyonna. Ebigambo bino birungi nnyo okukkiriza? Oba muligenda kukkiriza ebigambo by'abasumba Abayudaaya abakyamu okusinga eddoboozi lya Mukama wammwe era omulokozi, Kristo Yesu? Lwaki temulina kukkiriza mu Kigambo kyange newakubadde mu bigambo byennyini ebikola Ekigambo kyange? Temutegeera kyama kya mu Baibuli kye kitegeeza oba ekigendererwa kyakyo?

Nja kubategeeza: Ekyama kintu ekikwekebwa naye nga kyalagulwa ekibikkulwa mu kiseera ekisaanidde. Era lwaki Kitange yakola ekintu ng'ekyo, mubuuza? Temusomanga alipoota yange? Naye twogera amagezi ga Katonda mu kyama, ge ago amagezi agakwekebwa Katonda ge yalagira edda ensi nga tennaabaawo, olw'ekitiibwa kyaffe. Ekyo tewaali n'omu ku bafuzi b'ensi eno eyakimanya: kubanga singa baakimanya, tebandikomereredde Mukama w'ekitiibwa. Mmwe baani okubuzaabuza enteekateeka n'amakubo ga Kitange? Kubanga yeetaaga muwi wa magezi, wadde ng'abali ku ntebe ye enkulu bali mitwalo emirundi eky'emitwalo.

Kale musanyusaganenga n'ebigambo byange, kubanga mulokolebwa okuva mu busungu mu nze. Ate olwo, mu makubo gammwe agatalina kukkiriza, muwakana ku kiseera ekyo lwe kiribaawo. Wadde ng'abamu ku mmwe mu bukyamu mugamba nti tewali kukuŋŋaana, bangi ku mmwe muwakana ku kiseera we kibaawo nga mukigerageranya n’ekibonyobonyo kya Yakobo. Nja kukitegeza bulungi bwe munaawuliriza, wadde ng'abamu ku mmwe munaakyeyimirira ne mwakana ekintu kino, ne mwakana nange ne musiba omukisa gwammwe okutwala ekifo kyammwe mu bawala abatali bagezi. Kubanga Ekigambo kyange kigamba ki?

Naye tubegayirira, abooluganda, ku lw'okujja kwa Mukama waffe Yesu Kristo, na ku lw'okukuŋŋaana kwaffe gy'ali, Muleme okuseseetuka amangu mu birowoozo byammwe, newakubadde okutegana, newakubadde olw'omwoyo, newakubadde olw'ekigambo, newakubadde olw'ebbaluwa, ng'evudde gye tuli, ng'olunaku lwa Kristo lutuuse. Omuntu yenna alemenga okubalimba mu ngeri yonna yonna: kubanga olunaku olwo terulituuka, okuggyako nga wazzeewo okuvaamu okusooka, n'omuntu oyo ow'ekibi abikkulwe, ye mwana w'okuzikirira. (Mutunuulire ebya Byzantine, baana bange, kubanga Baibuli nnyingi enkyamu zivudde mu biwandiiko by'Abagnostic, ebiwandiiko ebitali bituufu ebyaweebwa abantu aboonoonyi. Temutegeera njawulo wakati w'ekiwandiiko kya Baibuli n'enkyusa ya Baibuli?) Walibaawo okuva ku kukkiriza mu bujeemu, era abo abagaana okuva mu kukkiriza baliva ku nsi okunsisinkana mu bbanga.

Kubanga omwo mwe muli olugero lw'abawala ekkumi. Abawala ab'amagezi abataano baagula amafuta ne balongoosa entambi zaabwe ne baba beetegefu omugole bwe yajja, ate abataano tebaali beetegefu ne basuulirira okugula amafuta, ne bagezaako okuggya ku byetaago by'abalala. Abawala abataano abeeteeseteese ne bagenda n'omugole, ate abataano abatali bagezi ne basaba okuyingizibwa oluvannyuma ne bagambibwa nti, munveeko, sibamanyangako. Temuzikiza mwoyo gwange ali mu mmwe ogwandikakasizza ebigambo bino. Mutegeere nti omwana w'okuzikirira tasobola kujja mu buyinza n'obwakabaka bwa Setaani obujjuvu tebusobola kuva mu bunnya okutuusa ng'omuziyiza aggyiddwawo, era okuva bwe muli abalokole okuva mu busungu, omuziyiza aliggyibwawo bwe ndikuŋŋaanya Omugole wange gye ndi mu bire bw'alinsisinkana mu bbanga.

Oluvannyuma ku nkomerero y'emyaka omusanvu egy'okubonaabona kwa Yakobo wajja okuzuukira kw'abatuukirivu. Laba! Ne ndaba entebe z'obwakabaka, ne batuula ku zo, ne baweebwa okusala omusango: ne ndaba emmeeme z'abo abaasalibwako emitwe olw'obujulirwa bwa Yesu, n'olw'ekigambo kya Katonda, era abatavunnamiranga nsolo, newakubadde ekifaananyi kyayo, newakubadde okuteekebwako akabonero kaayo ku byenyi byabwe, oba mu ngalo zaabwe; ne baba balamu ne bafugira wamu ne Kristo emyaka lukumi. Naye abafu abalala tebaaba balamu nate okutuusa emyaka olukumi lwe gyaggwaako. Kuno kwe kuzuukira okw'olubereberye. Alina omukisa era omutukuvu oyo alina omugabo mu kuzuukira okw'olubereberye: ku abo okufa okw'okubiri tekulina buyinza, naye baliba Bakabona ba Katonda, n'aba Kristo, era balifugira wamu naye emyaka lukumi.

Mukyayinza obutakkiriza bigambo byange? Mazima, bangi ku mmwe mulinywerera n'obukakanyavu ku nsobi yammwe n'ebigambo by'abasumba abakyamu, era bangi ku mmwe abandyawonye obusungu bwa Katonda obujja okukema ensi mu kiseera eky'okubonaabona kwa Yakobo, ne musalawo okugumiikiriza obusungu obungi so nga nze maze okubakema n'okubagezesa, okuggyako nga muligaana n'obukakanyavu okujja kwange, era mulinywerera ku busungu obutabagendereddwamu. Saakubagamba nti mufulume mu ye muleme okusaasaana mu bibonyoobonyo bye?

Naye, walibaawo okuzuukira okulala kumpi. Ne ndaba entebe ey'obwakabaka enjeru ennyo, n'oyo eyali agituddeko, ensi n'eggulu ne bidduka okuva mu maaso ge, so tewaali bifo byabyo. Ne ndaba abafu, abato n'abakulu, nga bayimiridde mu maaso ga Katonda: n'ebitabo ne bibikkulwa: n'ekitabo ekirala ne kibikkulwa, ekyo kye Kitabo ky'Obulamu: n'abafu ne basalirwa omusango okuva mu ebyo ebyawandiikibwa mu bitabo, ng'emirimu gyabwe bwe gyali. N'ennyanja n'ewaayo abafu abaagirimu: n'okufa n'emagombe ne biwaayo abafu abaabirimu: ne basalirwa omusango buli muntu ng'emirimu gye bwe gyali, kubanga kuno kwe kuzuukira kw'abatali batuukirivu abantu bange.

Lwaki temutegeera nti okukuŋŋaanyizibwa kw'Ekkanisa yange okunsisinkana mu bire mu kujja kwange kwawukana ku kukomawo kwange n'Omugole wange n'eggye ly'omu ggulu? Lwaki temutegeera nti okukuŋŋaana kw'Ekkanisa yange kyama ekyabikkulwa Omutume Pawulo mu ngeri y'Ekyama Ekinene ekya Katonda. N'olwekyo kyama mu kyama, era tekyabikkulwa bulungi eri batabani b'abantu okutuusa lwe kyabikkulirwa Pawulo eyakiwandiika, era nkikuumidde ku lwammwe.

Naye, waliwo ebyokulabirako ebiwerako Katonda by'atwala abantu mu ggulu olw'ebigendererwa eby'enjawulo. Enoka yagenda. Eriya yagenda. Nze nnagenda mu ggulu ng'abatume bange batunuulira mu kwewuunya. Firipo yaggyibwa mu kifo kimu n'atwalibwa mu kirala nga Kitange amututte olw'ebigendererwa byange. N'omugole wange bwe kityo alikuŋŋaanyizibwa gye ndi mu bire okutwalibwa mu ggulu okuwasa nange n'okuweereza ebigendererwa byange ebinunula.

Temutegeera nti Omugole wange aliba bakabaka era bakabona mu maaso ga Katonda wange era Kitange era alifugira wamu nange mu bwakabaka bwange obugenda okujja era alibeera nange emirembe gyonna? Lwaki muli balonzalonza okukkiriza ebigambo byange? Mwalogeddwa ne mubuzibwa abasumba abakyamu? Naye, bangi ku mmwe mulifuuka obunnabbi obwetuukiriza era mulisiba omukisa gwammwe eri okuvaamu nga mwawukana n'ebiragiro byange, naddala enjigiriza y'Ekkanisa yange okukuŋŋaanyizibwa gye ndi. Ensonyi zibakwate mmwe abakwataganya enjigiriza eno ne bannabbi ab'obulimba mu byafaayo ebyakale, nga mulangirira okutwalibwa okw'ekyama okw'obulimba okukwataganyizibwa n'enjigiriza endala ez'obulimba ez'ebiseera eby'enjawulo.

Mutegeere nti waliwo ebiseera mu kiseera kya Katonda so si ebiseera by'okugaba ebirungi, era si nsonga ze zimu. Mwefubenga okweraga nga musiimibwa, era mulekere awo okuba abagayaavu mu magezi. Mulekere awo okwesigama n'okusoma okutegeera kwa buli muntu omulala ku ebyawandiikibwa, era mubeerewo nga kyawandiikibwa nti: Kubanga temwetaaga muntu yenna kubayigiriza. Naye, bangi ku mmwe balisekerera ne bagaana amagezi gange. Okuggyako ng'ekintu kino tekirikolebwa mu kyama era Setaani alitandikawo obulimba obunene okubikka ku kintu kino kubanga kirimanyibwa mu nsi yonna. Walibaawo okukankana okunene, n'ensi yonna erikyuka okuva ku nsi gye mumanyi, n'omuntu oyo ow'ekibi alibikkulwa n'agolokokera mu vvu erisigaddewo abagoba b'embalaasi abana. Kiseera kyeteekateeka, mmwe Omugole wange, era mukikole mangu, kubanga laba okujja kwange kuli kumpi!

Nze mwana wa Muntu eyalangirirwa Enoka, Katonda gwe yatwala mu ggulu.

Nze oyo eyafuulibwa ekifaananyi n'ayogera ne Musa ne Eriya ku kugenda kwange, era Katonda yatwala Eriya mu ggulu mu kiseera kye.

Nze oyo eyatwalibwa mu kire bwe nnaayanirizibwa mu ggulu okutuusa ebiseera by'okuzzaawo.

Nze oyo eyatuma Firipo eri omulaawe Omwesiyopiya nga Katonda tannamutwala mu kifo kirala ku nsi.

Nze oyo eyakwata Pawulo n'amutwala mu ggulu ery'okusatu n'amulaga ebintu ebitayogerekeka bye yali talina ddembe kugabana.

Nze oyo alikka okuva mu ggulu n'omwanguka omunene okukuŋŋaanya Omugole wange okunsisinkana mu bbanga.

Nze oyo alizuukiza abajulirwa bange ababiri okuba abalamu oluvannyuma lw'okufa ennaku ssatu n'ekitundu, era nze alibatwala mu ggulu.

Nze Alufa ne Omega, kubanga nze ntandikwa n'enkomerero, era laba! Njija mangu okusinga bwe mulowooza, kale mweteekereteeke okunsisinkana oba mugwe.

Buli muntu akoowoola erinnya lya Mukama okutuusa ku nkomerero alirokolebwa.
