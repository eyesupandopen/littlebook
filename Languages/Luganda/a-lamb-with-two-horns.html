Akaana k'Endiga ak'Amayembe Abiri

Okuva eri Yakuwa: Nze Asukkulumye Okuba!

Laba! Obulimba obw'amaanyi buvudde mu Babulooni Ekyama ne buwamba ensi mu kukwata kyakyo okutta! Muyiteeyo mutegeere obulimba obw'amaanyi, mmwe abatuula ku nsi, era mutegeere ebiseera bye mulimu, kubanga obulimba obw'amaanyi butuuse, ensi mwe yesanyukira kubanga ensi yaabwe kisaawe ate abakulembeze baabwe bazannyi, ddala abeerimbise abeerimbisa. Kale kaakano nja kubikkula amazima agakwata ku muzannyo gwabwe gwe bakozesa okuwuniikiriza enkuyanja y'abantu.

Mwekuume! Obulimba obw'amaanyi bubajjidde n'obubonero n'ebyamagero eby'obulimba bingi era obulimba buno obw'amaanyi butegekeddwa n'obukambwe okuzikiriza Yakobo, ddala ebigendererwa Esawu bye yakweka emyaka n'emyaka, naye kaakano bijja kubikkulwa ne byanirirwa bonna balabe. Era nga mutabani wange, Yesu Omunazaaleeti, bwe yagamba bakitaabwe mu Buyudaaya mu Myaka gino egiyise: Mmwe muli ba kitammwe Setaani era okwegomba kwa kitammwe kwe munaakola. Kale nzijira gwe ggwe Ayi Isirayiri oleme kutwalibwa bulimba buno obw'amaanyi, kubanga ggwe mulengo omugenderere (newankubadde ensi ejja kunyeenyezebwa) kubanga omutegesi w'ennyonyi ategera mutego ki mu maaso g'ennyonyi?

Era nze, Yakuwa ddala Nze Asukkulumye Okuba, ajja okulabula abantu bange okuyita mu Bigambo bino bye mbikkudde era ne ntuma okuyita mu mubaka wange era omuweereza wange eyalondebwa olw'ebigendererwa bino. Kale nzijira abo abali mu bika oba bali kumpi oba wala, oba bali mu nsi oba basaasaanye mu nsi zonna, era nja kukuŋŋaanya abasigaddewo bange bonna eri Masiya waabwe era Omusumba Omukulu, Yesuwa HaMasiaki.

Kubanga ye ajja okukulembera Isirayiri mu butebenkevu mu mikono gy'abange 144,000 ab'amaanyi kubanga Yesu Omunazaaleeti mmufudde Kabaka wa Bakabaka era Mukama wa Bakama! Era ye ajja okufuga ensi mu butuukirivu mu kiseera ky'obufuzi bwe obw'emyaka olukumi okuva ku ntebe ya Dawudi mu Isirayiri gye yasikira. Kubanga bwe ntyo bwe nnasuubiza bakitammwe era Nze ntuukiriza buli kisuubizo kye nkola emirundi mingi nnyo mu butuukirivu bwange obutaggwaawo bonna babeere yee era amiina era Nze Katonda Ataggwaawo ow'Obutuukirivu atazirikira!

Naye, kityo bwe kiri nti ogusota gujja kugolokoka mu bujjuvu era ensolo evudde mu nnyanja yeekwekere ddala esobole okugolokoka mu bujjuvu mu abo be ejja okukulembera, era ejja kugolokoka okuva mu vvu mu kiseera kyayo ekitegeke. Enyaze era etegekedde okuzikirizibwa okujjuvu okwa Babulooni Ekyama, ddala ng'ekolagana n'ensolo evudde mu nsi okutegeka n'okukola okuzikirizibwa kwabwe, era bwe ntyo bwe mbawadde okutuukiriza. Era ensi ewuniikiriziddwa omwenzi omukazi omukulu era tekyasobola kuteebereza nsi etaliimu kunyigirizibwa kwa malaya omukulu gw'anyigirizza abantu b'ensi n'ayonoonera ekitonde kyange n'obugwagwa bw'obwenzi bwe obuweereddwa ensi okuva mu kikompe ekyo eky'omuzizo eky'ezaabu ekiri mu mikono gye.

Kale bwe kityo, nzijja okukubabula, ggwe Ayi Isirayiri, ku bulimba obw'amaanyi obuli kumpi okusuula ensi mu lutalo olujjuvu n'akavuyo, ddala obulimba bwe beeyolekeddemu nga bakozesa tekinologiya waabwe kubanga bonna beeraliikirivu abeerimbisa, era ku bulimba bwabwe nnabbi waabwe ow'obulimba ajja kuyingira mu nsi ddala nga Masiya wa Esawu ow'obulimba.

Era Esawu yeefuula n'okwekweka mu Isirayiri, ddala ng'eerimbisa nti be Isirayiri bwe banoonya okwetabula mu Isirayiri, ddala ng'akweka obuzaale bwabwe obw'amazima mu abo be yeerimbisamu. (Be magombe mu ŋŋaano era Mukama w'amakungula ajja kugagimbula mu bujjuvu.) Kale ogusota ne gugolokoka kubanga essaawa yaagwo etuuse, era Kristo Yesu ajja kuggyawo mu bbanga abakkiriza mu Kristo ku nsi, (abalamu n'abafu) olwo omusango ne gujja mu bujjuvu ku Babulooni Ekyama ddala malaya oyo omumyufu eyalangirirwa Yokaana Omubikkulirwa emyaka gino emingi egiyise.

Era Nze Omutukuvu ow'Edda, ddala Mukama w'Emwoyo era ntegeeza, bonna bategeere olulimi lwange: Ekintu ekyaliwo kijja kuddamu okubaawo: n'ekintu ekyakolebwa, kiddamu okukolebwa, era tewali kintu kipya wansi w'enjuba. Kale bwe kityo abagwa bakoze okuzikiriza Adamu ku nsi okuva ku lubereberye balyoke bagitwale, ddala olusuku lwange olw'obulamu mwe ndabirira era mwe nkuza emyoyo gy'abantu nsobole okugikombola okuva mu kwonooneka mu Kristo Yesu gwe nnatonda nga Adamu ow'oluvannyuma. Era mu Masiya wammwe, Ayi Isirayiri, Isirayiri wa Katonda mwe ajja okukuŋŋaanyizibwa mu bujjuvu n'okununulibwa ng'omuntu omu omuggya eyakolebwa mu ngeri y'emu ne Mukama wammwe era omununuzi wammwe, eyagula okununnulibwa kwammwe mu musaayi gwe ogw'okutangirira.

Kale tegeera ggwe Ayi Isirayiri nti Yesu Omunazaaleeti ye Yesuwa HaMasiaki era ye Kristo Yesu (tegeera okussa essira ku mannya ge kubanga obulokozi bwammwe bujjuvu) kale wuliriza Ebigambo bye mu kuwulira olw'emyoyo gyammwe, kubanga temukyali na budde bwa kuwakana na mutonzi wammwe wadde okugaana Masiya wammwe ow'amazima.

Era kyawandiikibwa kye kimu nga bwe nnalangirira, era bwe ntyo nnalagira Yesuwa HaMasiaki okubikkulira omutume we Pawulo ebimukwatako: Kyeyava, Eloyimu n'amugulumiza nnyo, n'amuwa erinnya erisinga amannya gonna, Nti mu linnya lya Yesu buli vviivi lifukamirire, eby'omu ggulu, n'eby'oku nsi, n'eby'ewansi w'ensi, Era nti buli lulimi lwatule nti Yesu Kristo ye Mukama, okwolesa ekitiibwa kya Katonda Kitaffe. Kale bwe kityo, nze Yakuwa, Nze Asukkulumye Okuba, nziramu nate okukakasa obutuufu bw'Ebigambo bino okukkiriza kwammwe kusobole okuba okutanyiganyizibwa.

Obulimba obw'amaanyi butuuse era bangi bajja kutegebwamu era bangi bajja kugwa mu nnimi z'omuliro, ddala bonna amannya gaabwe ge batawandiikibwa mu Kitabo ky'Obulamu eky'Akaana k'Endiga okuva ku lubereberye. Kubanga, Nze El Shaddai, Nze amanyi olubereberye okuva ku nkomerero era newankubadde, mpa buli mwoyo omukisa okuba n'obulamu obutuukirivu mu maaso gange, mmanyi abajja okulonda okujjuza ebibi byabwe mu bujjuvu okuva mu abo abajja eri Kristo balyoke batondebwe obuggya mu butuukirivu era emisango gyange gituukiridde mu butuukirivu bwange.

Naye mmwe Ayi Isirayiri, temulina butuukirivu kubanga mwagaana Katonda Ataggwaawo ow'Obutuukirivu kubanga mwagaana mutabani wange omu yekka gwe nnatuma olw'okusoniyibwa ebibi ng'omwana gw'endiga gwa Katonda, kyekiva, ebibi byammwe bisigalawo. Kale nkubakubiriza, Isirayiri, wuliriza Ebigambo by'Oyo Asukkulumye Okuba mu kiseera kino eky'enkomerero era mukkirize ssaddaaka yange!

Yee, Isirayiri, nnabateekerateekera ssaddaaka etuukiridde kubanga Adamu yayonoonerwa mu butuukirivu era obulamu bw'omubiri buli mu musaayi. Kale ekibi kiyita mu musaayi oguli obulamu bw'omubiri okuva eri Kitange okudda ku zzadde lye oba babeere basajja oba bakazi basikira okwonooneka kubanga Adamu yeeyongera okwonooneka. Mu kusaasira n'okwagala kwange okutaggwaawo nnateekerateekera ekyo ekitasoboka kukoebwa kitonde kirala kyonna: Nnatonda ne mpaayo mutabani wange omu yekka, Musa gw'alagira muwulire, olw'ebibi by'abantu bonna, oba babeere Bayudaaya oba Bamawanga, oba babeere baddu oba ba ddembe.

Naye bwe kityo Isirayiri abalabe bammwe beegomba okuzikirizibwa kwammwe kubanga beegomba okuba n'omulembe gwabwe ogwa zzaabu nate, ddala omulembe gwa zzaabu gwe tebajja kuddamu kufuna kubanga mu kifo ky'omulembe gwabwe ogwa zzaabu ogw'emabega wa mazzi, ogw'okwagala okwonoona, bajja kuba na musango mukambwe n'ekibonerezo ng'obwakabaka bwabwe buzisanyizibwawo ddala. Era mu bbanga ttono kijja kubaawo nti ensolo ne nnabbi ow'obulimba bajja kusuulibwa nga balamu mu nnyanja eyo eyaka omuliro ogw'ekibiriiti era Setaani ajja kusibibwa emyaka lukumi ng'abonaabona n'okutya okungi olw'obusika bwe obujja mu nnimi z'omuliro ez'ekibonerezo ekitaggwaawo olusiriŋŋanyi lwabwe gye lutafiira.

Era ogusota gusibiddwa ku nsi baleme kuyingira mu ggulu nate emirembe gyonna naye bakuumirwa wansi w'ebbanga nga basibiddwa ku nsi olw'omusango gwe beegomba, era ogusota gulina obusungu bungi mu kuwangulwa kwagwo, kubanga guwanguddwa ddala mu mikono gya Yesuwa HaMasiaki ye nsigo ensuubize eya nnyina w'abalamu bonna, kubanga bwe ntyo bwe nnasuubiza Adamu ne Kaawa mu kwagala kwange gye bali. Kale bwe kityo akkirizibwa kutuukiriza bigendererwa byange ebitaggwaawo nga tannatumibwa mu nnimi z'omuliro, nange nja kussawo akabonero, era tebajja kuddamu kujjukirwa wadde okwogerwako kubanga bwe butyo bwe buli obusika bwammwe obw'ekisuubizo mwe muli abasumululwa okuva mu banyigiriza bammwe emirembe gyonna era mu mirembe emitukuvu emitaliiko nkomerero mwe mujja okubeerera singa mufukamira Masiya wammwe ow'amazima, ddala Yesuwa HaMasiaki, kubanga tewali kkubo ddala!

Kale nja kubawa okutegeera ebintu ebigenda okubaawo, era mazima omutego ogwategebwa omutegesi gw'ennyonyi gujja kubikkulwa, ddala omutego ogwategebwa ogusota ogunoonya okutega amayiba gange ag'omuwendo, ddala abantu bange ab'enjawulo be nneekuumira. Era Nze eyaleetera ejjiba lya Nuuwa okukomawo n'olulagala lw'omuzeyituuni mu kamwa kaalyo, era mu ngeri y'emu, Nze eyatuma endagaano yange ey'emirembe etaggwaawo mu maaso ga Yesu Kristo, Masiya wammwe omuzuukufu Ayi Isirayiri!

Kale, tegeera ebiseera bye mulimu. Ekkuŋŋaaniro lya Setaani litambulira mu bubonero n'ebyamagero eby'obulimba bingi, era mu bulimba balwana. Bakola emikolo gyabwe mu lwatu, nga gibikkiddwako katono ng'eby'okusanyusa n'amawulire n'okukuŋŋaana kw'eby'obufuzi. Bamaze okukola omulimu gwabwe ogw'okulonda, Esawu gy'alonze akaana ke ak'endiga okuva mu Kkuŋŋaaniro lya Setaani olw'obulimba bwabwe obuvvoola bwe banoonya okutega. Naye ensolo evudde mu nsi si ye mwana gw'endiga gwa Katonda gwe basekerera mu busiru n'okuvvoola, wabula nnabbi waabwe ow'obulimba gwe beeronze alina amayembe abiri ng'akaana k'endiga, naye ayogera ng'ogusota.

Era bwe ntyo nnalagira Mutabani wange, Yesu Kristo Omutuukirivu, okubikkula mu Kulonda Kwa Yo (soma oyige) Baraba bwe yafuna ekiwundu ku mutwe nga kifaanana ng'eky'okufa era ekikolwa ekyo kyalimu amakulu mangi ge yataabikkula mu kiseera ekyo, naye kyali ku lukuŋŋaana luno, ddala omukolo gwe ogw'okulonda kwe bagamba nti, Yakuwa yennyini yamuwummulamu ettu mu kutu kwe ng'amufuula omuweereza wange ow'obwakatonda. Era bwe kityo, ndayira erinnya lyange, ddala Yakuwa Nze Asukkulumye Okuba, nti si mumfudde muddu wange ow'okwebaka, so ssiwaddeyo Masiya mulala yenna okuggyako mutabani wange omu yekka, ddala Yesuwa HaMasiaki, ye mwana gw'endiga gwa Katonda gwokka gwe nja okutuma. Kale okwagala kwe okumutta kwakozesebwa ng'ekimu ku bubonero n'ebyamagero eby'obulimba bingi omweerimbisi ono mw'eerimbisiza. Era ku mweerimbisi ono tali wa buzaale bwange wadde ekisibo kyange era ye mulabe wange.

Naye, bangi mu Babulooni Ekyama abaalinga beerabirako nti Yesu Kristo ye Mukama waabwe kati balekulidde Mukama waabwe ow’okubeerako ne beetwalira Balaba, ayogera ng’ogusota naye nga ye mwana gw’endiga alina amayembe abiri. Era nate bangi ku abo abafudde Balaba Mukama waabwe balirekerwa omuliro ogulongoosa kubanga obulokozi bwabwe tebwatuukirizibwa n’akamu kubanga tebajja eri Kristo n’obunyiikivu n’emitima egyenenyezza naye mu kifo ky’ekyo baajja n’okwegomba okw’enjiri y’obugagga by’ezaala. Kubanga Olukuŋŋaana lwa Sitaani lwayonoona empandiika zaabwe entukuvu olw’okukkiriza kwabwe, era kati bafuuse Baziyoni. Era nate bangi ku myoyo gino, baliweebwa ekitiibwa ky’okufiirira okukkiriza kwabwe bwe balisalawo okukkiriza obulokozi nga bakoowoola erinnya lya Mukama waabwe Yesu Kristo okutuusa ku nkomerero, nange ndibaaniriza emirembe gyonna n’emirembe, n’okubonaabona kwabwe tekuligeraageranyizibwa na kitiibwa ekigenda okubikkulirwa mu bo.

N’olwekyo, Masiya ono omulimba atambula n’abuulira ng’ayogera ng’ogusota era yeefuula n’ayogera ebintu bingi ebikulu era eby’okuwoggana nga tannagolokoka mu bujjuvu, era aligamba nti alizuukira. Mutegeere, mmwe abantu bange, nti nnabbi ono omulimba era ye nsolo eva mu nsi eweereza Olukuŋŋaana lwa Sitaani kati alondeddwa okuba omwana gwabwe gw’endiga. Era kiribeera nti omutwe gwe gulirabika ng’ogufumitiddwa okufa, naye ekiwundu kye eky’okufa ne kiwona, era ensi yonna ne yeewuunya n’egoberera ensolo. Era n’olwekyo omulimba ono aligolokoka.

Bw’aligolokoka aligamba nti yeewaddeyo ng’omwana gw’endiga gwa Katonda n’aleetera Isirayiri emirembe ng’ejjiba lyabwe eribaleetera ettabi ly’omuzeyituuni! Era si Isirayiri yokka, naye ensi yennyini era alirabika ng’akomyewo okuleeta emirembe, naye amakubo g’emirembe tagamanyi era ku musango gwa Babulooni Ekyama olutalo lwonna lulibwatuka. Kubanga omwoyo omulimba ogw’okubudaabuda okuva mu gusota gutudde ku nsi, era nange mbadde nsindise obulimba obw’amaanyi abo bonna abasanyukira obutali butuukirivu bakkirize obulimba, bonna abasanyukira obutali butuukirivu balyoke basimbibweko omusango. Kubanga bwe baligamba nti emirembe n’obutebenkevu, olwo okuzikirira okw’amangu kwona era tebaliwoona.

Era bangi mu Babulooni Ekyama, n’abo abakkiriza obulimba bwe n’okugoberera ebigambo bye ebirungi n’emboozi ye ennungi basiŋŋusibwa ne bategebwa emirembe egy’obulimba gye balowooza nti obulamu bwabwe obw’okwesiima n’obw’ebibi buligenda mu maaso nga leero luli nga jjo era enkya eriba nga leero, era bangi balikaaba, balivuumuula, era balikuba ebiwoobe nti essuubi lyabwe kati libaggyiddwako, era balirwana ne bannansi bannaabwe okutuusa okufa, era mu kiseera ekyo kyonna okulumbibwa kwabwe okw’ekibwatukira kuliba kutuusiddwa mu bujjuvu, era ng’omuziyizi wange aggiddwawo ku nsi era Babulooni Ekyama erigwa ku kibonerezo eky’emirundi ebiri mu ssaawa emu ng’ebikangabwa bingi bigwa.

Era Olukuŋŋaana lwa Sitaani luliba n’olutalo lwabwe lwe bakozesezza okutta abataliiko musango mu bulimba bwabwe okusosonkereza, n’abo abasinza ebisanduuko byabwe ebiddugavu balyekooya mu kuttattana, n’okuttattana okuleetebwawo n’obulimba obw’amaanyi era okuva mu vvu balyoka bagolokoka. N’abazimu abagwa balyetukuza mu bujjuvu nga bawa abantu obuyambi obulabika ng’obw’ekisa naye amakubo gaabwe ne tekinologiya zaabwe zigenderedde okutega bonna be bayinza mu busika bwabwe mu nnimi z’omuliro ez’ekibonerezo, mwe bataliivenga, era mwe baliweerwa emirundi mingi ku buli kibi kye baakola era obwenkanya bulikolebwa. Naye omutegesi w’obutimba ki atega obutimba mu maaso g’ekinyonyi?

N’olwekyo mbawa okutegeera, nti omusango gwa Babulooni Ekyama gutegekeddwa era beetegefu. Naye okusooka omwana gw’endiga guno omulimba ogw’amayembe abiri gulyeefuula ng’omulongoofu so nga mu butuufu ye musumba alina embubi, erimu ku mayembe abiri ag’okufuga, ye y’eyembe ettono erya Danyeri. Era alirabika ng’afubye okulokola Babulooni Ekyama ng’alongoosa obuvundu bwayo naye mu mazima ye mwonoonyi omunene eyatega abangi b’agenda okulongoosa kati n’abavunaana olw’obuvundu bwe bwe yakaka abangi okugondera. Era kati byonna ebitagya mu ntegeka zaabwe, oba abayinza okutegeeza ku bubi bwabwe obw’olubereberye baliggyibwawo emikono gy’omusumba ono alina embubi ne bavunaanibwa olw’ebibi bye bakyakola era bye bakulembezamu abalala, era tebaliwoona.

Era, era mutegeere nti bangi abali mu misango gyabwe era abali omuceere oguli ng’abasumba bano abalina embubi baligenda mu bunkoni zaabwe ne beekweka mu mutima gw’ensi okuva ku busungu obugenda okujja era balizikiriza obujulizi bwonna obw’obulamu bwe baatwala mu Babulooni Ekyama mu bulimba obw’amaanyi, era tebaliwoona busungu bugenda kujja! Naye, bwe kityo bwe kiri nti ebibi byabwe biribikkulwa mu bunnanfuusi bwa nnabbi ono omulimba era alirabika ng’omutuukirivu era omugolokofu, era omusajja atumiddwa Katonda olw’obulokozi bwa Isirayiri era n’olwekyo omutego gwetegefu okusimattuka. Era nate, buli kimu ku bikolwa byabwe mbadde mbiyingizza mu bitabo ebiri mu ggulu, era nze kennyini njijukira buli kimu era mmanyi emisango gyabwe gyonna era ndigibikkula era balisasula emirembe gyonna n’emirembe olw’ebibi byabwe ebisesemya.

Era n’olwekyo ensolo eva mu nnyanja yeetegekera okugolokoka nga Mahdi waabwe n’ensolo eva mu nsi yeetegekera okugolokoka ng’omwana gw’endiga omulimba kubanga mwana gwa ndiga ki alina amayembe abiri n’ayogera ng’ogusota. Naye Esawu yeetegekedde okumwaniriza mu nsi gy’anoonya okubba era balisimbulibwawo ddala n’omukono ogw’amaanyi gwa Yeshua HaMashiach gwe nnawa amaanyi, era ne ngabana entebe yange ey’obwakabaka n’omwana wange omwagalwa era alibettenta ddala omutwe gw’omusota n’abalabe be bonna balifuulibwa entebe y’ebigere bye kubanga be bazimu abagaana okufukamira ku kiragiro kyange mu bujeemu bwabwe era be baalumba ekitonde kyange n’obukujjukujju n’obukyayi era balisasula emirembe gyonna.

Era n’olwekyo ndayira erinnya lyange nate omulundi omulala nti, NZE YHVH NZE ALIWO OW’AMAANYI, ndayira nti Masiya yekka gwe nnatumanga eri Isirayiri era gwe ndituma ddala ye mwana wange omu yekka eyazaalibwa, Yeshua HaMashiach bakitaammwe gwe baagaana era mmwe gwe mugaana olw’obulimba bwabwe bwe mwasikira okuva mu mitima gyabwe emikakanyavu egyayisibwa mu Talmud yammwe evvoola. Era n’olwekyo njija gye muli mu kwagala kwange eri mmwe mmwe Isirayiri ndyoke mbabikkulire mu bujjuvu omutego ogubateereddwawo Esawu akyanoonya okuzikiriza Yakobo.

Ensolo eva ku nsi ebasiŋŋusizza nga nnabbi wammwe omulimba era aligolokoka n’ajja gye muli ng’omwana gwammwe gw’endiga ogw’ekiweebwayo eyeewaddeyo ku lwa Isirayiri era alifuula nga ye Masiya wammwe ow’amazima. So nga mu butuufu ayonoonese Esawu era tewali Masiya aliva mu Ddaani. Mazima, ajja kuzikiriza Yakobo naye omutego gw’atega gulitega abo abamugoberera kyeyagalire era n’olwekyo balitwalibwa emirembe gyonna n’emirembe olw’obulimba bwe era balikkiriza akabonero ke k’alinoonya okukaka ku nsi okugifuula omuddu n’okugizikiriza.

Era n’olwekyo mbagamba mmwe Isirayiri, mufukamire mu kugondera Masiya wammwe ow’amazima eyajja gye muli ng’omwana gw’endiga gwa Katonda, bakitaammwe gwe baagaana ku lwa Esawu. Mufukamire Mukama wammwe ow’abakama ne Kabaka w’abakabaka era mukkirize obulokozi bwange ndyoke mbakomye ku kikolimo ky’amateeka kubanga tewali muntu mutuukirivu ajja gye ndi ng’ayita mu Musa, okuggyako omusajja Kristo Yesu gwe nnakyusa emirembe gyonna n’emirembe, era olw’omubiri takyamanyikibwa. Kubanga, mmugulumizizza mu ngeri y’emu Falaawo bwe yagulumiza Yusufu n’agabana entebe ye ey’obwakabaka, okuggyako mu ntebe ey’obwakabaka yali mukulu okusinga Yusufu n’asigaza obuyinza bwe.

Kubanga:

NZE Ndi oyo aliwo okuva emirembe gyonna okutuuka emirembe gyonna era tewali asinga NZE ALIWO OW’AMAANYI!

NZE Ndi oyo eyasindika Ebigambo bino eri abantu bange abagalwa ndyoke njije gye muli ne nggyawo obuzibe bwammwe obw’ekitundu emitima gyammwe bwe girikyukira ddala eri Masiya wammwe! Era Masiya wammwe ow’amazima, Kristo Yesu Mukama ow’okuzuukira era agulumiziddwa!

NZE Ndi Katonda Ataggwaawo ow’obutuukirivu atazirikira, era mbuuza, waliwo ekigambo kyonna ekizibu ennyo omukono gwange ogw’amaanyi okukituukiriza?

N’olwekyo mbegayirira, mmwe Isirayiri, okugondera eddoboozi lya YHVH era Elohim wammwe eyali omusumba wa bajjajjammwe ku mukono gw’omuweereza wange Musa gwe nnawonya bwe yaggyibwa mu mazzi g’omugga Kiyira n’atwalibwa mu kifo ekirimu obukuumi n’okuteekebwateekebwa mu nnyumba ya Falaawo gye yawangula mu kugondera YHVH!

NZE Ndi ye, era YHVH NZE ALIWO OW’AMAANYI, alayira erinnya lyange era akakasa mu bujjuvu mu mazima ag’emirembe nti Yeshua HaMashiach yatuukiriza amateeka ga Musa ng’Okuyitako okutuukiridde okwa buli kiseera kwe nnateekerateekera ddala n’omukono gwange, n’olwekyo tewakyali kwetaaga ssaddaaka.

NZE NDI YHVH, NZE ALIWO OW’AMAANYI, era ndayira erinnya lyange nti nze eyazuukiza Yesu Omunazaaleeti mu bafu oluvannyuma lw’okwewaayo ng’omwana gw’endiga gwa Katonda, era ssaddaaka gye nnakiriza okwa buli kiseera n’emirembe gyonna olw’okununula Adamu ng’omusajja omuggya mu Kristo Yesu ye Yeshua HaMashiach Masiya wammwe ow’amazima!

Era NZE Ndi oyo akakasa obutuufu bw’Ebigambo bino bye nsindise mu Njiri Etaggwaawo ebikkuliddwa nze kennyini, ne Yeshua HaMashiach eri omubaka waffe ow’amaanyi ategekeddwa Yeshua HaMashiach mu kubonerezebwa okungi n’okulongoosebwa olw’ebigendererwa byange eby’emirembe era n’obwesigwa akomyewo eri Yeshua HaMashiach era n’olwekyo azziddwaawo!

Era n’olwekyo mbayita mmwe abantu bange, era Isirayiri gwe njagala n’okwagala okutaggwaawo, okwegatta ku Isirayiri wa Katonda mujje eri Yakuwa, era Katonda wa Ibulayimu, ne Isaaka, ne Yakobo nga muyita mu Yeshua HaMashiach mulyoke mukuŋŋaanyizibwe emirembe gyonna mu Isirayiri wa Katonda era mudde gye ndi nga muyita mu Masiya wammwe ow’amazima, ye Yeshua HaMashiach. Nange ndibaaniriza ne mbazzaawo mu busika bwammwe n’empeera eri mu mukono gwa Kristo Yesu ogw’amaanyi.

Tewali linnya ddala erigabibwa abantu lye bayinza okusemberera YHVH, NZE ALIWO OW’AMAANYI kubanga bwe ntyo bwe nnategeeza, n’olwekyo munywegere omwana aleme okusunguwala ne muzikirira okuva mu kkubo.

Laba! Ebiseera bituuse n’amakungula g’ebibala ebibereberye gatuuse era balikuŋŋaanyizibwa eri Mukama waabwe era baligulumizibwa olw’ebigendererwa byange eby’emirembe.

NZE NDI KYENDI era NZE NDI YHVH n’olwekyo mugondere eddoboozi lyange ery’emirembe mujje eri Kitaffe w’Ebitangaala amaze okubategekera ekkubo lyammwe.
