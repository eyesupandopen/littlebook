Mu Bikoleddwa bya Yokaana Omutume
Ogwekusatu 25, 2024

Mube n'Okkukkiriza mu Nze

Muwulire eddoboozi lyange, mmwe abaana bange, era mube n'okukkiriza mu nze, Mukama wammwe Yezu Kristu. Mbayitidde mu kitiibwa n'obulungi era okuyimirira mu maaso gange nga mutukuziddwa okuva mu makubo gammwe mu musaayi gwange ogutangirira, kubanga omusaayi gw'omwana gw'endiga gunaazaako obutali bulongoofu bwonna. Mutukuziddwa mu nze. Mumanye, mmwe abaana bange, nti tewali kusalirwa musango kw'abo abali mu nze, kwe kugamba, mu Kristu. Okubeera obumu nange n'ebigendererwa byange kwe kujja mu kussekimu ne Kitange, Yakuwa, nange, omwana we Yesuwa. Nze Mukama eyabayita ku mannya n'abawa okuyitibwa mu bulamu obutaggwaawo awalitabeerawo kuzibu kwonna, newankubadde okunakuwala, newankubadde ennyiike; kubanga nja kusangula amaziga gonna mu maaso gammwe mu bulokozi bwange gye muli. Nja kutandika omulimu ne ssigumaliriza? Katonda w'eggulu n'ensi alireka ekintu nga tekiweddewo oba okusuubula mu bisuubizo bye n'ekigambo kye? Temusomanga nti tekisoboka Katonda okulimba? Mazima ddala, buli kisuubizo kijja kutuukirira era kijja kutuukirizibwa, kale mumanye nti okununuzibwa kwammwe kusembedde — si kununuzibwa kwa kitundu, wabula okununuzibwa okujjuvu nga tewali kisigaddewo era tewali kitali kituukirivu.

Ebintu bino byonna bituukiriziddwa, era ekitasoboka kibaddewo mu nze kubanga nawangula okufa ne ssiddamu kufa. Mumanye nti buli mulimu omubi Sitaani gw'agezezzaako okuzimba mu bulamu bwammwe nja kugumenyawo. Weewaawo, nja kuzikiriza emirimu gyonna gya Setaani; kubanga ekyo kye kyandeeta okweyoleka. Mbawadde obulamu obujjuvu, era nja kubawonya ddala okuva mu mibiri gyammwe egyonooneddwa, era mujja kufaanana nange. Keberanga Ekigambo kyange, muwulire Ebigambo byange, mubigerageranye n'Ebyawandiikibwa, era mumanye nti okununuzibwa kwammwe okujjuvu kuli kumpi ng'omukka gwammwe oguddako era kujja kubaawo mbagirawo. Munywerere ku lwange; mwogerere ku lwange. Temuba na kakwate n'abo abasinza ebifaananyi n'abenzi abayingiridde Ekkanisa yange era abandyagadde okubuza abaana bange abato. Obusungu bwange bubatuulako era na buli muntu yenna alumba abantu bange okubabuza.

Kubanga tekisoboka nti muyinza okusikukululwa okuva mu mukono gwange, era abo be nnalaga okusaasira beebo abamfuulidde ddala Mukama waabwe nga batula erinnya lyange ery'amaanyi, Yezu Kristu, nti ye Mukama. Sijja kubagwisaamu ssuubi, era tekiyingirangako mu mutima gwa muntu bintu ebibalindiridde, kubanga nja kubatwala mu bbanga mangu. Ebigambo ebibi bireme okutawanya emitima gyammwe, mmwe abaana bange, kubanga mulokolebwa okuva mu busungu bwa Katonda, era abeesigwa bange tebajja kugumira kiseera kya myaka musanvu eky'obusungu ekigenda okuteebwa. Emitima gyammwe gibeere mirembe era giwummulire mu nze, ne mu kubonaabona kwammwe n'okuyigganyizibwa kwammwe muwummulire mu nze, nange nja kubawa emirembe egitagambika. Okusaasira kwange n'ekisa kyange bya lubeerera, era nasasula omuwendo gw'ebibi byammwe, kale mweyongere okubivaamu. Ndaba akacankalano ke muyitamu. Mpulira ebigambo byonna ebikambwe ebiboogerwako era ebibakwatako. Ate era, mbazzaamu amaanyi mu bunafu bwammwe, era mbawadde ku mwoyo gwange, era muli ba maanyi nnyo mu nze. Mu maanyi gammwe mwennyini mujja kulemererwa, kale muwummulire mu maanyi gange, kubanga Kitange angulumizizza okusinga byonna.

Nja kuwalana eggwanga ku babi mangu nnyo, mmwe abaana bange, kale temutya. Okuwalana eggwanga kuno si kwammwe, era tekugendereddwamu baana ba Katonda wabula abalabe be abaayonoona abantu n'abantu ababi abatta era abanyaga baliraanwa baabwe ne baganda baabwe.

Weewaawo, abantu ababi bangi beegasse ne Sitaani olw'amagoba ag'omululu, era balina omugabo gwabwe n'empeera yaabwe mu nnyanja y'omuliro etegekeddwa Setaani ne bamalayika be. Mmwenna temuli ggwanga limu, eggwanga ly'abantu? Naye, mumanye nti obusungu obungi bugenda kuteebwa n'eggulu ligenda kubikkulirwa ensi. Buli kikolwa kiwandiikibwa. Buli kigambo eky'obwereere ekyogerwa kyawandiikibwa. Tewali kyama kiyinza kunkwekebwa, era tewali wakwekweka okuva mu kutunula kwange. Newankubadde ababi basima wansi mu ttaka okudduka obusungu bwange obusembera, bajja kusisinkana enkomerero yaabwe nga bagezaako okunkweka.

Nja kusasula, era nja kuwalana eggwanga n'okunyweza obwenkanya ku lw'abo abaayonoonewa. Nina ekitala eky'obwogi ekiwagadde enjuyi zombi ekiva mu kamwa kange, era tewali kintu era tewali muntu ayinza kunziiyiza, kubanga nze Kabaka wa Bakabaka era Mukama wa Bakama. Ani ayinza okubawula ku kwagala kwa Katonda okuli mu nze? Saasagulira bibi byammwe n'okuwa obulamu obutaggwaawo okubateerako obusungu n'okusalirwa omusango, mmwe abeesigwa bange. Tewali kintu era tewali muntu ayinza okutwawula, era mangu ddala mujja kutunula ku Kitange nange n'amaaso gammwe gennyini. Newankubadde muzimbye okwesiga okungi n'okukkiriza mu nze, nga temulabangako n'amaaso gammwe gennyini, mangu ddala mujja kulaba, era mujja kumanya nga bwe mumanyiddwa. Ani oyo abazibuwalira? Talifuuka kya bwereere n'ebikolwa bye ebibi ne bimuddira emirundi ebiri? Abantu ababi beeterekedde obusungu obungi, weewaawo, obusungu obungi olw'okukozesa obubi ebitonde bya Kitange, era nja kusasula, bw'ayogera Mukama.

Muyimirire mu maaso gange era mweyongere okweyawula okuva mu nsi eno embi, efa era mukkirize omulimu gwange ogutangirira okubayitamu, nga gubanaaza okutuukira ddala. Laba! Nfuula ebintu byonna bipya, era ebibi byammwe n'obutali butuukirivu bwammwe sijja kubijjukira nate. Kale muve mu mirimu gyammwe gyennyini mutwale emirimu gyange, kubanga bwe mukola bwe mutyo mujja kuleeta obulamu obujjuvu mu myoyo gyammwe gyennyini. Temutya babi! Kubanga mangu ddala emirimu gyabwe gijja kusaasaana ng'enfuufu mu maaso g'empewo ennyingi, era nja kufuula ebintu byonna birongoofu era bipya. Waliwo empeera nnene eri abeesigwa bange era abalonde; era mangu, weewaawo, mangu nnyo mujja kukungula empeera y'emirimu gyammwe bwe mutazimba.

Munnyimirirewo okutuusa ku nkomerero, kubanga oyo afiirwa obulamu bwe mu nze ajja kulaba obulamu era ajja kukungula essanyu eddene n'obulokozi n'okusanyuka emirembe n'emirembe mu maaso ga Kitange. Mbafudde bakabaka era bakabona mu maaso ga Kitange, kubanga nze kabona asinga obukulu owa lubeerera olubeerera ng'engeri ya Merukizeddeeki bw'eri. Munywerere mu kisa kyange era mumanye nti mangu nnyo oluggi lujja kuggalawo, nga Katonda bwe yaggalira Nuwa n'ebintu bye eby'omuwendo munda mu lyato. Nange bwe ntyo bwe nja okubakuŋŋaanyiza gyendi mu ggulu. Waliwo oluggi oluggulewo omuntu yenna lw'atayinza kuggulawo wadde okuggalawo, era nja kubaaniriza gyendi, mmwe Omugole wange. Kale mununule, kubanga obudde bunaatera okuggwaawo.

Nze Mukama omwesigwa.

Nze kkubo, n'amazima, n'obulamu. Nze mutwe gw'omubiri.

Nze musajja awasizza omugole, era ngamba nti jjangu!

Nze oyo ataddamu kufa, kubanga tekisoboka kufa kunkuuma.

Nze oyo eyabayitira mu kitiibwa, mu bulungi, ne mu mazima.

Nze Mukama Yezu Kristu, omwana wa Katonda eyeyolekera mu mubiri eyafiirira ebibi byammwe.

Nze oyo eyazuukizibwa ku lunaku olw'okusatu n'ataddamu kufa.

Nze oyo Katonda gwe yayaniriza mu ggulu nga abatume bange n'abayigirizwa bange bandaba nga ŋŋenda mu bire.

Nze kabona asinga obukulu owa lubeerera ng'engeri ya Merukizeddeeki bw'eri.
