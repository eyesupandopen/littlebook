Omujulirwa Omulundi Ogw’okubiri: Isiraeri
Ekikaawa Ekirimu Obuwoomu
Ogw’omunaana 18, 2024

Babulooni Ekikulu Kigudde, Kigudde!

Mutege emitima gyammwe eri ebigambo byange muwulire bye njogera, era mukkirize ebigambo byange okuyingira mu mitima gyammwe: kubanga n’abakkiriza mu Kkanisa yange era n’Omugole wange boolekedde ebikemo eby’okwekwasa ensonga n’obutakkiriza. Mumpulire bulungi era mutege amatu okutegeera ebiseera bye mulimu, kubanga tewali n’omu ku mmwe eyali alabye ku bintu ebiriwo kati ebitegekeddwa okusalira ensi omusango. Kubanga omusango n’akatyabaga bituuse era bitandise dda, newankubadde nga bino byonna bisa ng’obulumi obw’okuzaala, obutakoma kati, era nga bigendereddwamu okulabula abo abali mu butamanya. Naye, ne mu balonde bange abeesigwa mulimu abo abandyagadde okusalawo obutaba na kulaba mu kifo ky’okulaba eby’amazima era abandyagadde okusalawo okuwulira oluyogoyogo mu kifo ky’obulagirizi obutegeerekeka n’okubudaabuda ebigambo bya Mukama bye bireeta.

Kubanga Nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, oba Yesu Kristo mu lulimi Olungereza, Nze atuma ebigambo bino okuyita mu muweereza wange gwe nnayimusa ne mmuwa obubaka era ne mmukweka mu kyasi kyange olw’ekiseera nga kino. So tekineewuunyisa kubanga amakubo gange bwe gatyole, kubanga Nze Kristo Yesu, Nze asobola okuyimusa abatume, bannabbi, ababuulizi b’enjiri, abasumba, n’abayigiriza okuva mu bantu abangi abataliiko mpapula za nsi eno egudde wadde okuba mu abo abeegasse mu kukkiriziganya okumu n’abaweereza ba Sitaani bennyini abalabika ng’abaweereza b’obutuukirivu. Era tekineewuunyisa kubanga Sitaani yennyini yeefuula malayika ow’omusana, kale tekyewuunyisa nti n’abaweereza be beefuula ng’ab’obutuukirivu, kubanga ddala banoonya okugatta bonna wansi w’ekibatuukirizo kyabwe eky’ensi emu.

Era nga mukwano gwange Yokaana Omubatiza bwe yagamba emyaka mingi egiyise emabega bwe yali agolola amakubo ge nnanditambuliddemu: Temulowooza kwegamba munda mu mmwe nti, Tulina Ibulayimu nga kitaffe: kubanga mbagamba nti, Katonda asobola okuyimusa abaana ba Ibulayimu okuva mu mayinja gano. Era na kati embazzi eteekeddwa ku kikolo ky’emiti: buli muti ogutabala bibala birungi gutemebwa, ne gusuulibwa mu muliro.

Musome mutegeere era musabe amagezi, mmwe baana bange abato, era mukozese ebigambo bino ku biseera byammwe. Kubanga mbagamba lwatu nti ssintumye ab’amaanyi b’ensi eno ng’ababaka n’abannabbi bange, wadde okwetaaga empapula z’ensi, nga Dokita ono ne Ssaabasumba oli, era amannya abantu ge beeyita okulabika ng’abatya Katonda. Kubanga omuntu agudde bulijjo anoonya kugulumiza mubiri gwe mu ebyo by’atuukako mu nsi, nga gezaako n’okuyimirira ng’omutuukirivu mu maaso ga Katonda yennyini mu mubiri ogw’okuvunda ogutayinza kulongoosebwa muntu yenna, okuggyako okuyita mu Nze Yesu Omunazaaleeti. Kale mbabuuliriza, mpapula ki ez’ensi ze nnali nnina mu kiseera ky’obuweereza bwange mu Buyudaaya, bwe nnatumibwa eri endiga ennabuze ez’ennyumba ya Isiraeri zokka? Tekyali kisittaza eri abatakkiriza mu nsi yange bwe baabuuza nti amagezi gano n’ebikolwa bino eby’amaanyi biva wa eri omusajja ono? Ono si ye mwana w’omubazzi?

Era nga bwe kyawandiikibwa bwe kityo bwe kituukirizibwa: Naye Katonda yalonda ebintu ebisirusiru eby’ensi okuswaza ab’amagezi; era Katonda yalonda ebintu ebinafu eby’ensi okuswaza ebintu eby’amaanyi; Era ebintu ebitali bya kitiibwa eby’ensi, n’ebinyoomedwa, Katonda yabironze, weewaawo, n’ebintu ebitaliwo, okufuula ebintu ebiriwo nga bya bwereere: Waleme kubaawo mubiri gwonna okwegulumiza mu maaso ge. Kale muwulire Ebigambo byange eby’okukkiriza ebyogerwa ku mimwa gy’abeesigwa bange era nagamba dda, era kati ndabika nate nti bannabbi bange, abali abangi, tebawakana na byawandiikibwa bitukuvu, wadde okumpakanya Nze, Kristo Yesu Mukama eyaalinnya mu Ggulu!

Mutegeere, mmwe abalina okukkiriza okutono, nti bannabbi bange boogera mu bumu, era boogera Ebigambo bye mbawa. Temumanyi ddoboozi lya Mukama wammwe? Ebigambo byange bigenyi gye muli, mmwe baana bange abato? Mutya amazima? Kubanga Nze Kigambo kya Katonda eyafuuka omubiri n’abeera mu mmwe era Nze eyabawa ku Mwoyo gwange ow’amaanyi gwe nnagabira abantu bonna mu nnaku zino ez’enkomerero nga bwe nnasuubiza. Kubanga kyawandiikibwa nti: batabani bammwe ne bawala bammwe baliragula, n’abavubuka bammwe baliraba enjole, n’abakadde bammwe baliroota ebirooto: Era ku baddu bange n’abazaana bange ndigaba ku Mwoyo gwange mu nnaku ezo; era baliragula: Era ndiraga ebyamagero waggulu mu ggulu, n’obubonero wansi ku nsi; omusaayi, n’omuliro, n’omukka ogufuumuuka: Enjuba erifuuka ekizikiza, n’omwezi gufuuke omusaayi, olunaku lwa Mukama olukulu olw’entiisa nga terunnatuuka.

Kale muwulire ebigambo byange era mukkirize Ebigambo byange kubanga bya mazima era byonna birituukirira, ne yodi emu oba akasale ka yodi birituukirira kubanga buli Kigambo kya Katonda kiweebwa olw’obuwulize era buli Kigambo kya Katonda kirifuulibwa ekituukiridde awatali kubulako kantu, kubanga amakubo ga Yahweh matuukirivu era kyonna ky’assaako omukono okukola kivaamu obutuukirivu obw’ekitalo. Temumanyi makubo ga Kitammwe, mmwe baana bange abato?

Kale muwulire eddoboozi lyange, mmwe abeesigwa bange abayitiddwa era abalonde, n’Ekkanisa yange era n’Omugole wange! Mujja kweyongera kunnyooma bigambo byange ne mukyuka okuva ku ddoboozi lyange ne mukkiririza mu bulimba bw’ensi? Mujja kugoberera eddoboozi ly’abagudde bwe mumala obulamu bwammwe nga mutunudde butunuzi ku masasiro? Mbeegayiridde, muwulire eddoboozi lyange era mbagamba nti, lwaki emitima gyammwe gibateganira? Temutegeera nti Nze ntudde n’ekiwujjo mu ngalo nga nkuumirira ennyimiriro z’omuliro ogulongoosa ndyoke nsobole okuggyamu ebisasiro mu bintu bya Yahweh eby’omuwendo ebingi?

Era bwe kityo emirimu gy’emikono gyange bwe gizaala mu mitima gyammwe ne mu bulamu bwammwe, mmwe baagazi bange: kubanga Nze nziggyamu obutakkiriza, Nze nziggyamu amakubo gammwe agatalina kukkiriza, Nze mbalongoosa ddala ku buli ngeri yonna ey’okwononeka kw’omubiri n’obubi bwe mujja gyendi ne mwatula ebibi byammwe n’ensobi zammwe ndyoke nsobole okubalongoosa mu musaayi gwange ogutangirira musobole okuyimirira nga temuliiko nsobi mu maaso g’entebe ya Yahweh bwe ndibaanjula ddala eri Kitange mu maaso gammwe mu kiseera ekiri okumpi ennyo.

Naye, bangi ku mmwe banoonya okuddayo mu bulamu bwammwe obw’edda bwe mba sikoze bya Yahweh by’ayagala okusinziira ku biseera byammwe n’ebisuubizo byammwe. Kubanga bwe kityo bwe kiri, n’abayigirizwa bange abasinga okuba okumpi nange n’abatume baaddayo okuvuba oluvannyuma lw’okukomererwa kwange n’okuzuukira, naye nabafuula abavubi b’abantu. Era bwe ntyo nzijja okubakungaanya nate ku ddoboozi lyange, so temutegeera mazima kabi akabeetoolodde mu nsi eno embi. Era bwe ntyo mbayita okujja gyendi mu nnaku zino ez’enkomerero musobole okujja gyendi ddala era muve mu kibi n’obutakkiriza, kubanga Nze eyabayita okujja mu bugagga n’amazima, newankubadde nga bangi ku mmwe bakyasiima okuddayo e Misiri.

Era bwe ntyo mbategeeza okuyiga ebyayigirizibwa ebyawandiikibwa edda ebiwa okulabula n’amagezi eri ab’amagezi. Kubanga baagamba Musa nti: Olw’okubanga tewaali malaalo mu Misiri, otututte okufiira mu ddungu? Kiki ekyakukoza bwe tutyo, okutuggya mu Misiri? Lwaki mukyalulunkanira ennaku ez’edda? Mukyasiigiddwa okubeera mu ssanyu ly’ekibi? Lwaki munoonya okusigala mu bawala abasirusiru so nga nnyimiridde waggulu wammwe nga mbawa omukono okubayimusa, kubanga mugudde nga temumanyi era muli mu nfuufu y’ensi, kubanga enfuufu y’eriba emmere y’omusota. Kale ggwe eyeebase yimuka weekunkumuleko enfuufu y’ensi oyimuke mu kitiibwa eky’omu ggulu era mube n’okukkiriza mu bigambo byange eby’okukkiriza abeesigwa bye boogedde ku mimwa gy’okukkiriza!

Kale mutegeere ekigenda mu maaso kati era mutegeere ebiseera bye mulimu, kubanga bangi bakkirizza okwebaka era ne bakkiriza ebigambo bya Sitaani ebitalina kukkiriza bw’abakulumiriza obulimba obuwulirika obulungi obwandirimbye n’abalonde bennyini singa basalawo okukkiriza obulimba bwe. Mutegeere nti Sitaani, oba omulyolyomi alimba ensi yonna, kati agobeddwa okuva mu ggulu era asibiddwa ku nsi olw’omusango ogunaatera okujja ogutalwawo.

Era bwe ntyo waliwo akaseera akatonotono abagudde we bakkirizibwa okutambuza ebyabwe ku lubaawo lw’ensi olw’omuzannyo, era akaseera emyoyo egisigaddewo we giri kati nga gisaabala mu lyato ly’obulamu obutaggwaawo mu Nze Kristo Yesu Mukama waabwe eyazuukira era Omulokozi waabwe. Naye, bangi ku mmwe mu kutegeera kwammwe okutalina kukkiriza mugamba nti Ebigambo byange bya bulimba era mu buseegu mugamba nti mulina obudde okuddayo mu bulamu bwammwe obw’edda obw’ekibi n’essanyu. Okuggyako nti, mu kaseera katono, abeesigwa balitwalibwa mu bubudamo eri entebe ya Yahweh yennyini mu mpeera enkulu n’ekitiibwa ekitaggwaawo n’obusika obutaggwaawo, era abeesigwa bonna balikyusibwa emirembe gyonna.

Oli sigala mu beesigwa? Olina obwetoowaze okutegeera obutali bwesigwa bwo n’okwenenya osobole okwegatta ku mulongo gw’abeesigwa? Bw’osalawo okulaba, Nze Kristo Yesu, ndikulaga. Kale lwaki okoowa okukola obulungi? Osazeewo nti abo be nkuleetera n’abo be nkuleetera tebasaanira bulamu butaggwaawo? Kale nkuwa amagezi okulaga ekisa n’obusaasizi eri munno nga bwe nnakulaga mu kiseera kino eky’enkomerero nga okuzikirizibwa tekunnakka. Kale bwe mukola mutyo mulirokola bangi, kubanga mukyali babaka bange. Kale mwogere mu kukkiriza, nga temubuusabuusa, nange ndibawa ebigambo eby’okwogera.

Kale abaana bange abato era n’abeesigwa bange bafuuka abatalina mirembe era abatalina kugumiikiriza, ne mu kiseera kino eky’enkomerero nga Ekkanisa yange n’Omugole wange tebannatwalibwa mu ggulu. Naye, nga bwe kyali mu ddungu Musa bwe yasisinkana Yahweh ku lusozi Sinaayi abaana ne bayimuka okulya n’okunywa n’okuzannya, ne beekolera ennyana ya zaabu okusinza mu kifo kya Katonda omulamu, oyo gwe bataalina kukkirizibwa okusemberera wabula ne bayimirira kumpi ne balaba ekitiibwa ky’okubeerawo kwa Katonda omukulu ataggwaawo ku Sinaayi! Era nga Musa bwe yayimiririra abaana, nange bwe ntyo nnyimiririra bonna abasika b’obulokozi obutaggwaawo, bonna abayitiddwa bajje mu bulokozi obutuukiridde n’okwenenya okuva mu makubo gaabwe amabi era agatalina kukkiriza. Mudde ku ddoboozi lyange musigale mu byalo bye nabawa, nange ndibakuuma okuva ku musango ogutali gwammwe. Ssibagambye okuva mu bo ne mwawukana? Mugondere eddoboozi lyange!

Kale omusango kati gugwa ku nsi era omusango gwategekebwa dda eri abatakkiriza n’ab’ensingo enkakanyavu, n’abo abakkiriza oba abatamanyi abakutte ku bujeemu bwa Sitaani. Kubanga bangi nnyo, nnyo, nnyo abantu balisalawo ennimi z’omuliro ezaategekerwa omulyolyomi ne bamalayika be mu kifo ky’okuva mu kibi ne bajja mu bulamu obutaggwaawo nga bakoowoola Yesu Omunazaaleeti okubalokola. Era bwe kiriba bwe kityo, kubanga El Shaddai, oba Katonda Ayinzabyonna, yawa abantu eddembe ly’okweroboza basobole okusalawo okubeera abatuukirivu era ab’emirembe mu maaso ga Yahweh, oba okusalawo okuzikirira emirembe gyonna mu nnyanja eyo enkulu ey’omuliro ogwa bbulu eyakolerwa abagudde awali akawuka kaabwe akatafa. Era ebiseera by’omusango bye bino.

Kale mbuulira bonna abaliwulira ebintu ebiribaawo nga tebinnabaawo. Ebintu bino si bya kyama naye bibuuliddwa bannabbi bange n’abaweereza bange okumala emyaka mingi kati, n’abo bonna be nnatuma e Babulooni eky’Ekyama. Kubanga ebiseera by’Okusalirwa Omusango eri omwenzi oyo omukulu bituuse, era ensolo emusitula kati ejja kumuzikiriza ddala. Kubanga Katonda akiteese mu mitima gy’amayembe ekkumi okutuukiriza by’ayagala, era bakyawa omwenzi, era balyeyongera okumufuula amatongo n’okumwambula obwereere, era balirya ennyama ye, ne beeyongera okumwokya n’omuliro. Era bwe kityo Babulooni eky’ekyama kirifuulibwa amatongo ddala ne kyanikibwa mu nsonyi bonna balabe.

Ebibuga bye ebyali ebikulu ennyo ebyali eby’okulabirako by’okukola ennyo n’obungi bwa El Shaddai kati bitudde matongo n’ezzadde lye erinywa enjaga era eribeera mu kazambi ku nguudo zaakyo ez’omwono. Era afuuliddwa bwereere kubanga ebyokwerinda bye bitwaliddwa wala n’obugagga bwe n’amaanyi ge biweereddwa mu mikono gy’abalabe be Abayedomu abeefuula mikwano. Omwenzi omukulu eyalwana n’amawanga gonna ge yatumibwa okuwamba kati asibiddwa, tateesobola kwerwanako era yeetegefu okuwambibwa ddala. Era abantu bonna balizikirizibwa mu nnaku era bonna abaliwambibwa balittibwa era abo abasobola okuweereza ebigendererwa by’ensolo balitwalibwa mu busibe era abasigaddewo balittibwa, newankubadde nga abasigaddewo balisigalawo. Era akyaka era waliwo ennyimiriro nnyingi eziri mu nsalo ze ne mu nsi za baliraanwa be ab’obukiikakkono abagabana naye ku kawumpuli we. Naye bino bisa ng’obulumi obw’okuzaala era si misango gya nkomerero wabula kulabula bonna ku kuzikirira kwabwe okunaatera okujja. Kubanga kiribaawo mangu, era kiribaawo mangu. Kiribaawo kyokka era nga bwe nkwakula abeesigwa bange okuva ku nsi okubatwala mu kifo eky’obulokozi era ndikungaanya amangu eŋŋaano ya Yahweh mu tterekero lye etaggwaawo, kubanga ebiseera by’amakungula bituuse. Naye si makungula ga nkomerero, wabula ga kubiri mu amakungula agawerako, era bwe ntyo omusango gutandikira ku nnyumba ya Katonda.

Ggwe Babulooni ekyama, obudde bwo butuuse, n'ennaku zo zikomekkerezeddwa. Nze, Yesu Omunazaaleeti, nkaabye amaziga mangi olw'okuzikirizibwa kwo okuli okumpi, era mbadde nsindika obubonero waggulu, n'ebyamagero mu bbanga, n'obubonero ku nsi, era n'ebikangabwa bingi eby'amangu bigenda mu maaso mu kiseera kino ne mu makkati go, naye Babulooni ekyama aziba amatu ge mu bujeemu obw'amakaati, era kaakano agenda kulya ku bibala by'emize gye egy'obujeemu. N'olwekyo tegeera nti, nga bwe nkuŋŋaanya abeesigwa mu mangu okubatuusa mu bulamu n'okuweebwa empeera, bwe kityo n'okuzikirizibwa okw'amangu kuligwa ku Babulooni ekyama ng'omuziyiza aggiddwawo ddala. Wulira ebigambo byange era otegeere eddoboozi lyange kubanga omuziyiza ye yekka akoma okwegomba okuzikiriza okw'abo abaagwa okutuusa oyo aziyiza lw'ataziyiza n'aggibwa mu makkati.

Oluvannyuma okuzikirizibwa okw'amangu kulijja ku nsi yonna. Ensalo z'olutalo zitegekeddwa, era beetegekedde olutalo lwabwe olw'ensi yonna olw'okusatu, ye lutalo lwabwe olw'okuggyawo ddala engeri ez'edda n'entegeka ez'edda. Kale n'olwekyo omuliro guligwa mu mangu ku Babulooni ekyama n'ebibuga bye birizikirizibwa. Omuliro guligwa mu mangu ng'ebizungirizi bikuba ku nsi ye, ne kimuleka ng'ayokeddwa era ali bwereere. Naye ebibuga bye bingi ebiri ku mbalama ze ez'ebibi birigwa mu mazzi agalijja mu mangu ne gasensera ku mbalama ze ne gayanjaala mu nsi okumala mayiro nnyingi nnyingi, era kino kiribaawo okuva ebuvanjuba n'ebugwanjuba. Ensi erikankana mu mangu mu ngeri etefugika, n'okuzikirizibwa okunene kulijja ku nsi za Babulooni ekyama, n'ensi zirikolebwako enkyukakyuka ne zifuulibwa endala. Entindo ze zirigwa, n'amakubo ge galifuuka agatayitikamu, era eddungu n'amatongo bye birisigalawo. Evvumbe ly'okufa liribikka ku nsi ze n'akawumpuli n'endwadde birijjuza ensi ze, era aliba talina buyambi bwonna.

Era ng'okulumbibwa kuno okw'enkomerero kugenda mu maaso, eggye ddene ery'abambadde emmyuufu liriyimbulwa ddala ku abo abalisigalawo mu nsi. Balitta buli ajeema era balitta bangi n'abatali bajeemu. Kubanga abantu ba Babulooni ekyama batundiddwa mu buddu olw'amabanja amanene ge yeetisse, kubanga okusinziira ku kuwola okw'amagoba, abo abaagwa bakola okuzikiriza enkuyanja y'abantu. Era nga bwe baasuubiza era ne bassa emikono ku bulamu bwabwe olw'okufuna eby'obugagga n'essanyu ly'ekibi, bwe kityo abo be balonda okufuula abaddu balyoke baweereze abo be babanja mu mirimu egy'empaka era akabonero kalikakizibwa ku buli asigalawo, naye ggwe tokkirizanga kabonero.

N'olwekyo ensi eriwo kaakano, eriddirisibwa mu mangu evvu ly'amatongo n'ebisigaddewo ebinyooka eby'entabaganya eyaliko emalamu. Kale enjala erigwa ku nsi, kubanga Babulooni ekyama aleese enjala ku nsi nnyingi era kaakano enjala eriba mugenyi we ow'ekyeggulo, kubanga bw'atyo bwe yagaba era bwe kityo emirundi ebiri bw'agenda okufuna kaakano. Amazzi galiba kintu kinnoonyezebwa nnyo, n'amazzi mangi galifuulibwa amakaawa olw'okuzikirizibwa n'emiro gy'olutalo n'olw'ebiwuka by'abo abataliiziikibwa ebirichafuwaza ensi. Era bangi balibonaabona mu ngeri eteri ya mugaso ne bazikirira mu musango olw'ebibi byabwe, so ng'ekintu ekyo tekyali kya bweru ku bo, naye abo abalisigala mu balamusibwa omusango nabo bennyini balisalirwa omusango, naye omuntu yenna alikoowoola erinnya lya Mukama we okutuusa ku nkomerero alirokolebwa, naye balina okugumiikiriza okutuusa ku mukka gwabwe ogw'enkomerero. Kubanga obwesigwa okutuusa ku nkomerero ky'ekintu ekibadde kyetaagisa eri abeesigwa, era n'abaana ba Yahweh bennyini aba Ibulayimu abali abaana b'ekisuubizo, kubanga omutuukirivu aliba mulamu lwa kukkiriza.

Bwe kityo Babulooni ekyama aliyulikamu ddala wakati, n'amazzi galyera w'eyali ensi enkalu ennungi, era n'ensi ez'omu makkati ziriyanjalala okutuuka wakati. Kale n'olwekyo ekisa kya Katonda n'okusaasira kwe biriggibwawo ddala ku nsi ng'omuziyiza aggiddwawo mu mangu n'okuzikirizibwa kuliwasa okuzikirizibwa, kubanga bino bye biseera by'okuzikirizibwa omuli obwakabaka bwa Sitaani we bulisalirwa omusango ddala ne buzikirizibwa ddala, naye okusooka balyoke baweereze ebigendererwa bya Yahweh mu kumalawo ddala Babulooni ekyama era bw'atyo alyokebwa ddala n'amalibwawo mu miro mingi egiribumbujja ne gitandika okulongoosa ensi. Kale n'olwekyo okuzikirizibwa kwa Babulooni ekyama kulibaawo mu ssaawa emu, kubanga nga bwe kyawandiikibwa, bwe kityo bwe kirikolebwa, era alifuna omusango emirundi ebiri. Era nga bwe kyawandiikibwa, bwe kityo bwe kirikolebwa.

Kuba kyawandiikibwa edda Yokaana Omubikkulirwa nti: Nalaba Malayika omulala ng'akka okuva mu ggulu, ng'alina obuyinza bungi, ensi n'eyakira olw'ekitiibwa kye, N'ayogerera waggulu n'eddoboozi ddene, ng'agamba nti, Eguze, eguze Babulooni ekibuga ekyo ekinene, era kifuuse nnyumba ya dayimooni, n'ekifo eky'emyoyo gyonna emibi, n'ekiyumba ky'ebinyonyi byonna ebitali birongoofu era ebikyayibwa. Kubanga amawanga gonna ganywedde ku mwenge gw'obusungu bw'obwenzi bwakyo, era Bakabaka b'ensi bayenze nakyo, n'abasuubuzi b'ensi bagaggawadde olw'obungi bw'ebisanyusa byakyo.

Ne mpulira eddoboozi eddala eriva mu ggulu nga ligamba nti, Mufulume mu kyo, mmwe abantu bange, muleme okwenyigira mu bibi byakyo, era muleme kufuna ku bibonyoobonyo byakyo. Kubanga ebibi byakyo bituuse mu ggulu, era Katonda ajjukidde obutali butuukirivu bwakyo. Mumusasule, nga ye bwe yabawa empeera, era mumuwe emirundi ebiri okusinziira ku bikolwa bye: ne mu kikompe mwe yabajjuliza, mumujjirize emirundi ebiri. Nga bwe yeegulumiza, n'abeera mu ssanyu, bwe mutyo nammwe mumuwe okubonyaabonyezebwa n'ennaku: kubanga ayogera mu mutima gwe nti, Ntudde ndi Nnabagereka, so siri nnamwandu, era siriraba nnaku. Kyaliva kireeta ebibonyoobonyo bye ku lunaku olumu, okufa, n'ennaku, n'enjala, era aliyokebwa omuliro: kubanga Katonda amusalira omusango, ye Mukama ow'amaanyi!

Kale eggwanga lino eddene ery'ensi n'ebibuga bingi lirigwa mu mangu, ne malayika w'okuzikiriza kaakano ayimiridde ku kibuga kye ekisinga obunene era ekikulu ku mazzi g'ebuvanjuba n'amalala ge n'essanyu lye n'obuggya bw'ensi birizikirizibwa ddala ne bitaddamu kuzuukuka. Era n'okuzikirizibwa kwe kuligabanyizibwa n'abo bonna abeegasse naye, kubanga ye malaaya oyo omukulu atuula ku mazzi amangi agali abantu n'ebibiina n'amawanga n'ennimi.

Kubanga omusango gwabwe tegulwawo, era bakabaka bonna ab'ensi beegasse ku mayembe ekkumi mu kulumba kwabwe okw'ekibwatukira. Kubanga abakulembeze baabwe baweereza n'obwegendereza abo abaagwa, kubanga bwe batyo abafuzi bonna ab'ensi bwe bali. Tewali mulungi, nedda, wadde omu. Abakulembeze bano bakoze emyaka mingi okuleetawo okulumba kuno era balyemu olukwe amawanga gaabwe ge baatunulibwa okulimba. N'abantu abaabagoberera n'obwegendereza mu ssanyu baligwa mu miro gy'olutalo, akawumpuli, okufa n'okuzikirizibwa. Era ebintu bino byetegefu okuyimbulwa mu mangu ddala ku nsi. Kale amawanga gonna ageeyita Amakristaayo galigwa n'ekitala ng'omusango gutandikira ku nnyumba ya Katonda. Era nga bwe kyali mu nnaku ez'edda Isirayiri bwe yava ku Kitange w'ebitangaala n'aweereza bakatonda abalala, abo ku bwabwe abatari bakatonda, bwe kityo nabo baliweebwayo mu mikono gy'abalabe baabwe, era ng'ebika ekkumi n'ebibiri bwe byayawulwamu ne biwangulwa edda olw'okumenya endagaano zaabwe era ne kireetebwa ekikolimo ky'amateeka eri abatawulira n'ab'emitima emikakanyavu.

Era okuva mu vvu ly'olutalo olw'awamu n'okuzikirizibwa, olwo entegeka ey'ensolo ey'enkomerero erizuukuka, kubanga ogusota gwetegekera dda entegeka y'ensolo okuzuukuka, era n'abo abeegatta ku bakabaka ekkumi abaliwa amaanyi gaabwe gonna ensolo eva mu nnyanja n'ensolo eva ku nsi ye nnabbi waabwe ow'obulimba era Moshiach waabwe ow'obulimba. Ensolo eriva mu nnyanja erizuukuka nga Mahdi waabwe, era yye aweereddwa obuyinza okulwana n'abatukuvu n'okubawangula. Kale n'ensolo eriva ku nsi erina amaanyi ge gamu n'ensolo eriva mu nnyanja, era y'oyo Ejjembe ettono eryayogerwako edda Daniyeri.

Kale n'ensolo zino ebbiri zirifuna amaanyi mu bujjuvu okuva mu gusota ogwo omumyufu omunene ne zizuukuka mu bujjuvu ne ziwangula ne zigenda okuwangula, era zirigamba nti: Ani afaanana ensolo? Era ani ayinza okulwana n'ensolo? Kale bonna abalisalawo okwegatta nabo mu kibiina kyaabwe ekya sitaani balyetaagibwa okufuna akabonero kaabwe. Era eddalu liribikka ku nsi yonna, kubanga omuziyiza talibawo nate okunenya abantu olw'ekibi n'okuziyiza okwegomba kwe n'okwegomba kwe okubi. Kale n'olwekyo obubi obutaziyizibwa butegekeddwa okuyimbulwa ku nsi n'obunnya buliggulwawo ddala, kubanga abatuula ku nsi bagaanyi Yahweh, kubanga banzize, Yesu Omunazaaleeti eyatumibwa olw'okulokolebwa kwabwe. Naye beebaze nga tebasaana bulamu butaggwaawo era ne bagaana Yahweh, Yahweh bwe yajja gy'ebali mu kwagala, obuwombeefu, ekisa, n'okusaasira n'awa abantu endagaano y'emirembe eteriggwaawo. Endagaano y'emirembe gye bagaanye kaakano. Naye, omuntu yenna akoowoola erinnya lya Mukama okutuusa ku nkomerero alirokolebwa.

Era newankubadde ebiro by'omusango, okuzikirizibwa okungi, olutalo, enjala, kawumpuli n'ebirala biriyimbulwa ku nsi yonna, wiiki eno ey'enkomerero, ye wiiki eya Daniyeri ey'amakumi musanvu, eweereddwa olw'okulokolebwa kwa Yakobo, era nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, nja kununula abasigaddewo mu Isirayiri abalikkiriza nga Masiya waabwe ow'amazima era ow'obutuukirivu, kubanga nze bwe nnatondebwa, ne nkolwa, ne ntumibwa Katonda Omuyinza w'ebintu byonna mu kitiibwa ky'ebisuubizo ebyakolebwa mu ndagaano n'endagaano eri Bakitaabwe.

Kubanga Yahweh ye Katonda omunene ataggwaawo atuula waggulu era kaakano agenda okweyoleka eri ekitonde kye, era nze, Kristo Yesu, nze Mukama w'abakama ataggwaawo era Kabaka w'abakabaka obwakabaka bwe butalina nkomerero. Era ndituma bannabbi bange ababiri, era n'ebikondo byange eby'ettaala ebibiri ebiriragulwa, era ndituma abakumi n'enkumi n'ana mu bina okusigaza abalamu abo be batumibwa mu ddungu mu kifo ekyalondebwa Yahweh era Yahweh y'alibaliisa n'okubalabirira. Era Katonda ataggwaawo bw'atyo bwe yalangirira edda. Kubanga Yahweh y'ayogera ku bintu ebitaliwo ng'amaze okubaawo kubanga bwe zityo engeri za Katonda Ataggwaawo eyampa Enjiri Eteriggwaawo okulangirirwa ku nsi obulokozi obutaggwaawo bulyoke bujje eri bonna abalikoowoola erinnya lyange eddene, eritaggwaawo.

Nze Yeshua HaMashiach, ye Kristo Yesu, ntumye ebigambo bino ng'abiyisa mu muweereza wange era ebigambo bino bitumiddwa ng'abiyisa mu muweereza wange.

Nze Kigambo kya Katonda ekyefuula omubiri ekibeera mu mmwe.

Nze oyo atuula n'ekiwujjo mu ngalo ng'akoleeza ennimi z'omuliro ez'okulongoosa.

Nze oyo agggyawo obutakkiriza.

Nze oyo agggyawo engeri zammwe ezitali za bwesigwa.

Nze oyo abalongoosa ddala mu ngeri zonna ez'obwononefu bw'omubiri.

Nze oyo eyabayita okujja mu mpisa ennungi n'amazima.

Nnyimiridde waggulu wammwe, mbagololede omukono okubazza ku bigere byammwe. Mujja kukkiriza kye mbawa?

Era mumanye nti nze oyo Katonda omunene ataggwaawo era ow'emirembe n'emirembe gw'awadde ebintu byonna mu mikono gyange egy'amaanyi era n'ampa erinnya erisinga amannya gonna, nti mu linnya lya Yesu, amaviivi gonna galifukaamirira mu ggulu, ne mu nsi n'ewansi w'ensi. Era nze oyo ali Empologoma ey'ekika kya Yuda era mu butuukirivu nzijja okusala omusango n'okulwana n'okusala omusango ku abo abaagwa, era essaawa y'omusango kaakano etuuse. Kale n'olwekyo mpanirira abo abatuula ku nsi okugoberera ebigambo bya Yahweh n'okunywegera Omwana, nneme okusunguwala, ne muzikirira okuva mu kkubo, obusungu bwange bwe bukoleezebwa katono.

Balina omukisa abo bonna abanteekako obwesige bwabwe, ye Yeshua HaMashiach n'abo bonna abakoowoola erinnya lyange okutuusa ku nkomerero balirokolebwa, era n'abo bonna abasikira obulokozi nga emisingi gy'ensi tenateekebwawo.
