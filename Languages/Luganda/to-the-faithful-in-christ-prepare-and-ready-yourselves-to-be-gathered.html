Naye, bangi mu Kkanisa yange omubiri gwange era n'omugole wange bagenda mu maaso n'okukkiriza abasumba bano abalimba okusuula okusuubira kwabwe nti Omugole waabwe ajja ku lunaku lwe beemalirawo, obunnabbi obw'obulimba obwawukana n'ekyo ekyamala okulabikibwa YHVH, NZE NDI NZE ow'amaanyi, era ne Kristo Yesu ku kiragiro kya Adonai-YHVH kubanga bulijjo nkola by'oyo eyantuma. Naye, bwe kityo. Omugole wange kati atudde ng'anyize mu kweraliikirira, ng'akwatiddwa ebigambo by'abo abakuba obulaguzi era abavumaganya! Kale bwe kityo, Omugole ajja ku lwa mugole we era ensi amangu ago eribikkibwa ekizikiza ekikutte, kubanga ekitiibwa kyange sirikigabana na mulala, era ng'ekizikiza ekikutte bwe kiribikka ensi, ekitiibwa n'okumasamasa kw'okwabika kwange kulimulisa ensi nga laddu bwe yaka okuva ebuvanjuba n'egenda ebugwanjuba, era amangu ago awatali kulinda Ekkanisa yange, Omubiri gwange, n'Omugole wange balisanga nga bayimiridde mu ggulu ery'eggulu, nga bayimiridde mu ntebe y'obwakabaka eya YHVH, NZE NDI NZE ow'amaanyi, akaseera Adonai-YHVH k'alinze okuva emirembe egiyise, ekiseera eky'edda ennyo kye mutasobola kutegeera mu kiseera kino. Era mwonna mwakwekebwa mu maanyi ge n'omutima gwe ogw'ekitalo ogw'okwagala n'ekitangaala, gye yabakuumira nga mukuumibwa mu kyama kye ekisinga obukulu ddala eky'amaanyi, Ekyaama Ekikulu ekya Yahweh, NZE NDI NZE ow'amaanyi, era mazima ddala eggulu lyeteeseteese era lisanyuka kaakano olw'okutuuka kwammwe okw'amangu ago, nga n'okutuuka temunnatuuka. N'olwekyo, muwummuze ebirowoozo byammwe n'emitima gyammwe okujjuvu mu kwagala kwange gye muli abaana bange, era temukkiriza bigambo bya bulimba bya bannabbi balimba abakuba obulaguzi era abavumaganya era abassawo enaku z'embaga yange ey'e Ggaliraaya okusuula okukkiriza kwammwe, wadde okubaleetera ennaku mu mutima ng'alowooza nti nja kusuula Omugole wange omulungi eyeeteeseteese mu buwulize obw'okwagala eri eddoboozi ly'Omugole we. Kale mbalagira abanjagala okussa omutima gwammwe gwonna, obulamu bwammwe, amagezi gammwe, n'amaanyi gammwe ku Bigambo bya Mukama wammwe Omuteriggwaawo ow'Obutuukirivu, Ebigambo byange ebitukuvu eby'omu kyawandiikibwa bye nnabikkula mu katabo ako akaggule akaguddwa mu ngalo z'omubaka wange n'omuweereza wange, kubanga akatabo k'Okubikkulirwa essuula 10 kalimu okubwatuka kw'okuduumira omusanvu era n'enjiri eteriggwaawo mu kitundu, era bino bye Bigambo ebiweereddwa okukulembera abantu bange mu makungula g'ennaku ya Yakobo era ssabbiiti ya Danyeri ey'amakumi musanvu. Ebigambo ebyasibibwa era ne bikuŋŋaanyizibwa mu kyama ku kiragiro kya YHVH, NZE NDI NZE ow'amaanyi, kubanga mazima, mazima, okubwatuka n'eddoboozi ly'okuduumira okw'omusanvu byogera, era okuduumira kuno okw'omusanvu kwe kutandika ekitabo ky'okubikkulirwa, ssabbiiti ya Danyeri ey'amakumi musanvu ejja okuvaamu okuzikirizibwa okujjuvu era ddala okw'obwakabaka bwa Heylel Ben Shachar emirembe n'emirembe, era ensi erifugibwa mu Butuukirivu, obutuukirivu obutaliimu kintu kyonna kibi eky'okuyingira mu bwakabaka bwange obw'emyaka olukumi, kale muwummule n'essuubi eddene mu Bigambo bino eby'essuubi n'ekisuubizo ekitaggwaawo abanjagala.

Nze ndi Kristo Yesu, era nzize okuleeta Ebigambo bino eby'okukangavvula, okunenya, n'okwagala eri abawulize, n'abatawulize nti mazima, mazima, temugwa ku luuyi olutawulize olw'ekitala kyange eky'omwoyo, nti musaanire okubalibwa okukuŋŋaanyizibwa n'abeesigwa amangu ago awatali kulinda. Kubanga ne mu ddakiika zino ezisembayo nga olube olw'amaanyi terunnavuga, era nga n'eggwaanga ery'amaanyi terinnafuyibwa n'amaanyi mangi era awatali kulinda kaakano, nkyakankanya era ndeeta okukankana okw'amaanyi okujja ku bantu bange, nti nkankanye abatali beesigwa basobole okufuuka abeesigwa mu buwulize obw'obutuukirivu, oba okugwa mu muliro ogulongoosa mu butawulize bwabwe, nga bukyali ku kaseera ako aka kairos okusisinkana n'ebiseera eby'ebiseera ebigenda okubatuukako mwenna amangu ago awatali kulinda. Ng'ensi bw'ekaabira omulokozi waayo omulimba, omujulirwa omulimba gwe nnatuma, naye eyaleetebwa Balaba, era naye bangi kati bagoberera omujulirwa ono omulimba, nga babuusibwabuusibwa okuva ku ddoboozi lya Kristo Yesu era ye Yesuwa HaMashiach, Selah.

Kale bwe kityo, nteekateeka abo abalisigalawo ddala mu muliro ogulongoosa, bonna abaliweebwa omukisa okukoowoola erinnya lya Yesu Kristo okutuusa ku nkomerero yaabwe, kubanga bonna abataganza bulamu bwabwe okutuuka ku nkomerero mu ngeri eyo balifuna obulokozi obutaggwaawo, mazima ddala, obutagerageranyizibwa na kubonaabona kwe balibonaabona ku lw'erinnya lyange.

Mweteeketeeke mmwe Ekkanisa yange Omubiri gwange era n'Omugole wange, mweteeketeeke era mwetegeke okusinziira ku Bigambo byange ebitukuvu n'ebiwandiiko by'Enjiri Eteriggwaawo era mundindirire abanjagala kubanga njija ku lwa mmwe era temuliggwaamu maanyi mu kusuubira kwammwe. N'olwekyo, mukkirize okunenya kwange n'okukangavvula kwange era mugondere Ebigambo byange n'okulabula kwange kubanga njija ku lwa mmwe abanjagala, era sirireka bakkiriza mu Kristo mu muliro ogulongoosa, wadde mu musango gw'aboonoonyi ogutali gwa mmwe.

Mweteeketeeke abanjagala, mu buwulize bwonna n'okusuubira, era mwezingire mu Bigambo by'Enjiri Eteriggwaawo, era mubikuumire mu birowoozo byammwe n'emitima gyammwe era mutegeere obugagga bw'ekyawandiikibwa bwe nnawa mu nsi okubakuuma mu biseera bino eby'okubikkulwa, okubikkulwa kwe nnatandika n'amakonde amangi amategeke mu kisibo kyange ekitono agavudde mu bwesigwa, amakonde ago agaateekateeka ekkubo ly'omubaka wange n'omuweereza wange ali ekibya ekironde eri Mukama we Omuteriggwaawo, ye Kristo Yesu, era ye Yesuwa HaMashiach, we mbikkulira Ebigambo byange mu kibya kyange ekyawuliddwawo ekiri mu kukkiriziganya era nga yeewaddeyo ddala eri ebigendererwa bye nnamuwa, era lwa kiragiro kyange n'amaanyi gange engalo ze we zisangira ebinuunulo okubikkula Ebigambo bino ebitukuvu bye nnamuwa okusumulula mu nsi omulundi ogusooka, Ebigambo ebitonyezeddwa okusanga amaaso, amatu, n'emitima gy'abo bonna be bitumiddwa, Selah.
