Zibasanze Abo Abayita Ekizikiza Omusana n'Omusana Ekizikiza!

Muwulire ebigambo bya Mukama wammwe era Omulokozi wammwe, mmwe Ekkanisa yange, ye Omugole wange, kubanga nze agaba amagezi eri abagezigezi, abagezigezi mu mmwe balyoke beeyongere obugezi. Era nze muweesi akoleeza omuliro ogw'okutukuza, kale muwulire ebigambo byange mwenna, era mufukamire okuyitibwa kwange okujja mu bulamu obutaggwaawo era munoonye amagezi kubanga amagezi kye kintu ekisinga obukulu, kyemunaava munoonya amagezi. Kale mujje gye ndi n'omutima omweggule n'emikono emiggule era mufukamire era mweyongere okumfuula nze, Yesu Omunazaaleeti, Mukama wammwe ow'emirembe n'emirembe kubanga nkyayita abo bonna abasikira obulamu obutaggwaawo, wadde nga abamu bazitoowerera okuwulira.

Naye muganyenga amagezi g'ensi eno agajja okuggwaawo, kubanga amagezi g'ensi gava mu balangira b'ensi eno abajja okusibibwa bonna mu bunnya obutakoma okumala emyaka olukumi nga bali mu kubonyaabonyezebwa n'okubonerezebwa olw'amakubo gaabwe. Kubanga ne mu njegere enzito mwe bajja okusibibwa mu bbanga eritali lya wala bajja kutegeera ebiribaawo mu biseera byabwe eby'omu maaso, era bajja kukankana n'okutyemtyemuka okumala emyaka olukumi ng'ekiseera kyabwe eky'emirembe n'emirembe mu nnyanja eyo eyaka emirembe gyonna ey'ekibiriiti ekivunze kyeyongera okwaka mu nnimi z'omuliro ezisinga okwokya eza bbulu wa kobalti kubanga Yakuwa bw'atyo bwe yateekateeka era obusika bwabwe bwe butyo bwe bubalindiridde.

Kubanga bw'etyo okwegomba kw'omutima gwabwe eri abaana ba Katonda omulamu bwe kuli, Yakuwa alyoke akomyewo okwegomba kw'emitima gyabwe mu bifuba byabwe byennyini, era bwe batyo balyaka emirembe n'emirembe n'emirembe. Kubanga okusingira ddala abo abagwa balifuna okuva mu makubo gaabwe bo bennyini kubanga be bakitaabwe b'ekibi, okusobya, obutali butuukirivu, n'obujeemu era ebikolwa byabwe eby'omuzizo biribaddizibwa emirembe gyonna. Era balimanya era balisasula ddala buli kibi kye baakola emirundi mingi nate era nate okubonerezebwa kwabwe kuliba, kubanga bw'etyo bwe kwasangibwa mu mitima gyabwe n'ebikolwa byabwe bya muzizo emirembe n'emirembe. Kubanga bw'atyo amakubo ga Yakuwa bwe gali, nga omuntu bw'asala omusango gw'omulala, bw'atyo naye bw'alisalirwa omusango era bw'etyo n'ekirivaamu n'okubonerezebwa kwaabo bonna abalonda okuzikirira kwabwe bo bennyini mu kifo ky'obulamu obutaggwaawo mu nze Yesu Omunazaaleeti kubanga nze Mwana gw'endiga gwa Katonda.

Kubanga buli eyeesembye okubeera omusiru okutuuka ku kujeemera entegeka y'obutonde, ye n'entegeka z'obutonde bwa Yakuwa eyakola obulamu ng'olugoye oluluke obulungi, aligabana mu kubonerezebwa kwabwe. Okujeemera entegeka z'obulamu kwe kuleetera abalala okufa n'okuzikirira, n'obubi obungi n'akabi akanene bikolebwa abo bonna abajeema mu ngeri eyo era abagoberera okwegomba kwabwe bo bennyini okutuusa ku kulumya abalala. Okusanyukira mu bujeemu bw'oyo eyagwa kwe kwegatta ku makubo gaabwe era abo bonna abakola batyo baligabana emirembe n'emirembe mu busika bwabwe mu nnimi z'omuliro ezitazikira, era tewali kulondawo kulala.

Kubanga okuva edda n'okuva ku lubereberye omuntu yaweebwa eddembe ly'okulonda era Adamu n'asendebwasendebwa Kaawa eyaggya ku musota ogwamulimba mu bukujjukujju bwagwo. Bw'atyo Adamu ne Kaawa ne balonda okufa ng'ekyo ekiva mu kibi, kubanga abantu baatondebwa ddala nga bamalayika, n'ekigendererwa nti beeyongere okuba abalongoofu era abatuukirivu, n'okufa, okuzikiriza buli kintu, tekwandisobodde kubakwata.

Era bw'atyo okumanya obulungi n'obubi kwonoonye abo bonna abalidde okuva olwo. Era olutalo lw'ezzadde lwawuluguma ne lutabuka okuva mu Lubereberye n'ezzadde eryasuubizibwa ery'omukazi, mu kuzikirira okungi, era mu lutalo luno tewali kyawonye ku ebyo ebyavaamu. Okutuusa leero obutonde bwonna butaagana era businda mu bulumi n'okutuusa kaakano kubanga n'obutonde nabwo bulirokolebwa okuva mu busibe obw'okuvunda okuyingira mu ddembe ery'ekitiibwa ery'abaana ba Katonda, era okununulibwa tekulwawo! Naye mutegeere baana bange nti okutuusa leero olutalo lw'ezzadde luwanguddwa ddala mu nze, Kristo Yesu, Kubanga muli bazaalibwa omulundi ogw'okubiri mu nsigo etevunda era mu mwana gw'endiga gwa Katonda mwe mwakolebwa okutangirirwa ebibi by'abantu omulundi gumu emirembe n'emirembe. Era olw'amaanyi agatasobola kugerekebwa aga Yakuwa, okufa kwawangulwa emirembe gyonna mu kuzuukira kwange mu bafu, kubanga tekyasoboka kufa kundemesa kubanga sirina kibi era empeera y'ekibi kufa.

Naye naasasula omuwendo ogutasobola kutegeerekeka olw'okununulibwa kwammwe n'eddembe lyammwe, era abaana ba Katonda omulamu ekiseera kyonna balisanga nga bayimiridde mu kisenge ky'entebe y'obwakabaka ya Katonda nga bambadde emirembe n'emirembe obulamu obutaggwaawo. Bwe kityo bwe kiri era bwe kityo bwe kiriba, nti buli akoowoola erinnya lya Mukama alirokolebwa emirembe n'emirembe, bwe banaasigala nga beesigwa mu kukoowoola erinnya lyange okutuusa ku nkomerero. Kubanga abo abagwa be balabe abawanguddwa, era kaakano bituuse ebiseera eby'okuzikirizibwa okw'olubeerera ku bwakabaka bwabwe n'okubonerezebwa okujjuvu ng'obusungu bw'Omwana gw'endiga n'obusungu bwa Katonda bwe bufulumizibwa mu nnaku ezijja. Kubanga olunaku lwa Mukama lutuuse, kale muwulire eddoboozi lyange nga oluggi lw'elyato terunnaggalawo.

Muwulire ebigambo byange era mutegeere bye njogera. Kubanga njogera ku bintu ebiriwo kaakano era n'obulimba obunene obwandirimbye n'abalonde singa balonda okuggala amatu gaabwe ku ddoboozi lyange. Kaakano njogera ku mbizzi ez'omu nsiko ezinoonya okulabika ng'ate teziri mu biyumba mwe zikuumirwa omunziyiza wange. N'ekibiina n'entegeka z'embizzi ezireeta okuzikirira buli lunaku nga zireeta ensi okumpi n'olutalo olw'ensi yonna, n'akatyabaga ak'olubeerera, kubanga bwe batyo bwe baweereddwa okutuukiriza era Katonda yakiteeka mu mitima gyabwe okutuukiriza by'ayagala kubanga n'abalabe ba Yakuwa bagondera eddoboozi lye.

Embizziga zeekulukuunya mu kazambi waazo, nga zisekkula mu ttosi, era tezikula n'akamu, naye zirirya kyonna kye zisobola nga bwe zisekkula n'ennyindo zaazo ku ttaka kubanga za ku nsi, era zifaamye eri byonna wabula okwegomba kw'embuto zaazo n'okwegomba kwa kitaabwe bye zirigoberera. Kubanga bwe batyo abakulembeze b'ensi ab'omu kiseera kino bwe bali, era bakaaba mu bulumi nga bakuba abo be bafumita. Kubanga tewali kisobola kumatiza ddala mbizzi zino, era zirimu obusagwa obujjudde okuva munda olw'ebyo bye zirya, kubanga zonna si nnongoofu era ziri omuzing'onyo ddala era bw'etyo n'obulamu bwazo bwe buli.

Ensologoma zino embi miti mivunze era egitaliiko bibala, gifudde emirundi ebiri, era gisimbuddwa n'emiramwa. Teziriibe na bulamu butaggwaawo, era gaazo ge makubo g'okufa n'okuzikirira okw'emirembe, ye okuzikirira okw'olubeerera mu busika bwa Bakitaabwe mu nnyanja eyo ennene ey'omuliro ogwa bbulu. Mu butonde bwazo za mululu, era zeegatta ku balala ku luliba nga zinoonya obubudamo nga bwe zikaaba nga z'elumiziddwa, era nga zivumirira abalala olw'obukyayi bwe zireeta ku mawanga. Zirira mu bulumi nga zikuba abo be zandizikirizza balyoke balabike ng'abatuukirivu mu makubo gaabwe amabi.

Kubanga ensolo zino ez'omu nsiko tezisobola kusaasirwa kubanga gaazo ge makubo g'okuzikirira era zino zirigoberera okwegomba kwa Kitaabwe omulyolyomi kubanga okwegomba kwe kwe kusanyusa kwazo era bwe zityo bwe zituukirizibwa. Zibasanze abo abajaguza obubi era abayita omusana ekizikiza n'ekizikiza omusana. Kubanga gaabwe ge makubo g'okuzikirira, era okuva mu muliro ogutaggwaawo tebalyeyo. Mutegeere baana bange nti ekiseera kyonna kaakano embizzi ziriteebwa okuva mu biyumba mwe zibadde zikuumirwa omunziyiza. Kubanga ng'omunziyiza bw'aggyibwawo, embizzi zino ezirabika ng'ez'awaka era ezitali za kabi ezefuula okuba abalonde ba Katonda zirifuuka ez'omu nsiko amangu ago nga ziteereddwa okuva mu kukwata kw'omunziyiza okutayonooneka, kubanga bw'etyo obutonde bwazo obw'amazima bwe buli.

Zirirumba ensi nnyingi era zirirya byonna bye zirisanze. Ziririira ku kufa n'okuzikirira kw'abalala, kubanga bw'etyo obutonde bwazo bwe buli. Zibadde zitambulatambula okuva mu kifo ekimu okudda mu kirala, era zirina ebiragiro byazo n'entegeka z'embeera zaazo, kubanga Yakuwa n'awa embizzi omutindo gw'amagezi n'entegeka, kubanga amakubo ga Yakuwa matuukirivu era matuufu. Embizzi zino tezirina kwejjusa, tezirina nneewulira, era tezirina buzibu kulya bafu okumatiza emmere yaazo, era zirirya n'abalamu bwe kinaaba kisaanidde ebigendererwa byazo. Laba essaawa kaakano embizzi we zitaribeerere na nkomerero era bwe zityo bwe ziriba, zirirya ensi, zirirya amasavu g'ensi, zirizikiriza emirembe ddala okuva ku nsi era ziririira ne zeekulukuunya mu kuyiwa omusaayi kw'olutalo olw'ensi yonna. Kubanga n'abasanyusa bammwe ababi bayimba ku makubo gaabwe, era amakubo gaabwe si ga kyama.

Embizziga zino ez'omu nsiko zakolebwa olw'okuzikirizibwa n'okulya kazambi w'ensi okutukuza ensi olw'ebigendererwa bya Yakuwa, oluvannyuma ne zisibibwa ne zisuulibwa mu muliro ogutaggwaawo emirembe n'emirembe. Kubanga ebibi byabwe binene, n'ebibi byabwe bingi. Naye mutegeere nti embizzi zino zikuumiddwa mu biyumba byazo okutuusa ebiseera ebirangiridde era abangi beefuula abalangiridde ng'ebibya ebitukuvu olw'obwakabaka bwabwe obujja. Era kaakano kye kiseera ky'okuteebwa kwazo, era kaakano ziriraga obutonde bwazo obw'amazima bwe zeekwese mu byambalo ebirabika ng'eby'eddiini, ne kiyuubu enzirugavu eya Satani eri wakati mu mmunyeenye yaabwe era n'okusinga amaaso. Zinoonya okwawula kubanga zeerondedde okuba ab'obwanannyini bwa Katonda. Naye tewali muntu ajja eri Yakuwa wabula okuyita mu nze, Yesu Omunazaaleeti, oyo Bakitaabwe be baagaana era abo abamfudde Mukama be Isiraeri ow'amazima owa Katonda era balitondebwa omulundi ogw'okubiri mu nze ng'omuntu omu omupya, oba Muyudaaya oba Mgentile, oba muddu oba wa ddembe. Omuzing'onyo guno ogw'Abayedomu gulibikkulwa ddala olw'obulimba bwe guli era guliggibwawo ddala mu nsi ne mu nsi yonna mu musango omunene n'okubonerezebwa okunene.

Naye nga tebunnaggibwawo, balikkirizibwa okwegomba kwabwe okw'omusaayi ng'obutonde bwabwe obw'amazima bwe bubikkulwa bonna balabe, kubanga kaakano beekwese mu bintu ebirabika ng'eby'eddiini. Ziriira ku kasasiro, ziriira ku bafu, zirumba era zilya abalamu kubanga okwegomba kwazo okw'omusaayi kunene era tekusobola kumatizibwa! Era ebbanga lyonna beefuula nti batukuvu, beefuula nti ba mu nsi era basika abasaanidde, era beefuula nti si balumbaganyi kubanga ekifo kyabwe eky'obutonde lye ddungu so si nsi ya ssuubi. Era bw'etyo n'obutonde bw'embizzi bwe buli kubanga amangu ago nga zikomezebbwa mu nsiko obutonde bwazo obw'amazima obw'omu nsiko bubikkulwa ddala. Naye, beefuula okuba n'obulamu bw'abo be bandiwangudde era balisimbulibwa ddala mu nsi era tebalina kumpi na ttabi kubanga bw'etyo Yakuwa bwe yasuubiza. Kale mutegeere embizzi zino zirabika bakulembeze ba bantu mu nsi ensuubize so ng'ate mu butuufu nsolo za lukaka ezisanyukira okutta abantu era ezirinoonya okuzikiriza ekintu kyonna ekyange, Kristo Yesu, era ziriryamu olukwe n'abo ab'omu Nsi abagoberera ebiragiro byabwe ebya Talmud okubatuusa mu kuzikirira naye ekisigalira ky'ensi kirijja gye ndi, Yeshua HaMashiach, kubanga ndikyali nja kulokola Yakobo!

Zibasanze abo abayita ekizikiza omusana n'omusana ekizikiza! N'abo abalumba Yakuwa, ne Yeshua HaMashiach nga balumba abaana ba Katonda Omulamu. Kubanga mu ekyo bwe baakikola ow'oluganda lwange asinga obutono baakikola nze, era okuwoolera eggwanga kwange, bw'ayogera Mukama ow'emirembe n'emirembe, era ndisasula. Kale ggula amaaso go, baana bange, era mugaane abo abawuuta emmunyeenye ya Remphan ku bbendera yaabwe! Kubanga okwegatta nabo kwe kubeera mu weema ya Moloki n'okufukamira Baphomet waabwe! Saakubagamba okubala omuwendo gw'ensolo era omuwendo ogwo gulagibwa n'amalala mu kifaananyi ekyo kye beekolera okusinza katonda waabwe ow'emmunyeenye, kubanga wakati mu mmunyeenye yaabwe mwe muli kiyuubu yaabwe! Mutegeere olugero luno, kubanga obutonnyeze mukaaga butwala ku masaze mukaaga agakola enjuyi mukaaga era munda mutuuamu kiyuubu yaabwe, kale mubale omuwendo gw'ensolo era mulekere awo okukkiriza obulimba bw'Abayedomu kubanga basinza abo abagwa era katonda waabwe ye gwaayida.

Era okuva mu nnaku za Nuuwa, n'oluvannyuma lw'amataba, ebitundu mwenda ku kkumi eby'emyoyo egitaliiko mibiri egy'Abanefuli giziyiziddwa mu bunnya obutakoma, kubanga Nuuwa yasabira bazzukulu be abaali balumbiddwa bo era ne bafugibwa. Era bw'atyo Yakuwa n'assa ekitiibwa mu kusaba kwa Nuuwa n'asiba abasinga obubi n'omuzizo, era ebitundu mwenda ku kkumi eby'omuwendo gwabwe bikuuumiddwa mu bunnya obutakoma okuva olwo. Mumpulire era mumpulire bulungi, Baana bange, era mutegeere eddoboozi lya Mukama wammwe. Mu kukuŋŋaana kw'abeesigwa mu Kkanisa yange era n'Omugole wange gye ndi mu bire omunziyiza aliggibwawo, n'obunnya obutakoma butegekeddwa okuggulwawo ddala.

Obubi tebulikyayizibwa nate, kubanga Yakuwa ayongedde okuziyiza okujjuva kw'obwakabaka bwa Setaani abo abasikira obulokozi obw'emirembe n'emirembe balyoke balokolebwe, be abo abamfudde Mukama waabwe mu biseera byange eby'ekisa n'okusaasira. Okuva omuntu kati bw'anziridde, obubi tebulikyayizibwa nate ku muntu. Mutegeere nti byonna, n'ekibiina ekinene ennyo eky'emyoyo gino gyonna biriteebwa, n'omuwendo omunene ennyo ogulijjula ensi n'obubi obutayizibwa era obutayogerekeka. Kubanga Enoka omuwandiisi yawandiika ebikwata ku bino: N'emyoyo gy'ebisolo ebikulu gibonyaabonya, gijooga, gizikiriza, girumba, girwana, era gikola okuzikiriza ku nsi, era gireeta emitawaana: tegirya mmere, naye n'enjala n'ennyonta, era gireeta ebisobyo. Era emyoyo gino girigolokokera abaana b'abantu n'abakazi, kubanga gyava mu bo.

Temusomanga mu kigambo kyange ekitukuvu gye nnayita mu nsi y'Abagadareni? Kubanga nnagoba emyoyo mu bantu babiri abaali bagirimu, era bwe ntyo ne nkkiriza emyoyo gino emibi okuyingira mu kisibo ky'embizzi ekyali okumpi. Mutegeere nti okujjuva kw'obwakabaka bwa Gwaayida kunaatera okuteebwa okumala akaseera katono, okusobozesa abo abalagirwa obulamu obutaggwaawo okuyingira mu mikono gyange egirokozi! Kubanga emyoyo gino egisibiddwa giriteebwa okwegatta ku bwakabaka bwa Setaani obujjuvu ku maaso g'ensi. Era giriyingira mu mbizzi omulundi omulala. Kubanga zirwana n'abantu era zandiwangudde abantu kubanga okwegomba kwazo tekusobola kumatizibwa era gino gye myoyo egiwa embizzi amaanyi era egikolera awamu n'embizzi era embizzi zino ez'omu nsiko kye kidduka kyazo.

Si zo zokka naye naabo bonna abakkiriza akabonero kaazo ak'ensolo nabo balifuuka bidduka bya kwagala olw'emyoyo gino egirirwana n'abantu omulundi omulala. Kubanga zirirya omuntu ow'obutonde omulundi omulala mu biseera bino eby'enkomerero, kubanga bw'etyo bwe kwalagirwa okubaawo mu biseera bino eby'enkomerero. Era enkomerero etandise dda era ejja kujjula mangu nnyo ng'okubonaabona kwa Yakobo kutandika mu nsi. Obulumi bw'okuzaala buli kumpi nnyo kaakano tebuyimirira era Yakuwa akoze obubonero bungi ku nsi n'obubonero n'ebyamagero mu bbanga ne mu ggulu ne ku nsi, naye bangi nnyo bakyajeemera okulabula kuno mu kusinziira okutali kwa kukkiriza.

Laba! Mu kifo ky'okujja eri okwenenya, ebikuukuulu bisanyusibwa n'ebisera by'ebiwuka ebiwangudde abangi ne mu nsi nnyingi okwetooloola ensi. Nga baseka era nga banyoomoola okulabula okuweereddwa Yakuwa eri ensi kubanga Katonda ow'emirembe n'emirembe aligoloka okuva mu kifo kye eky'okubeeramu era alyeraga n'abo abagwa bakankana kubanga ebiseera byabwe bituuse, era bakwatiddwa entiisa ey'emirembe n'emirembe. Kubanga mmwe abagwa mwalowooza nti mwanditutte Katonda ow'emirembe n'emirembe n'amagezi, so ng'ekintu ekyo tekyali kya kusoboka. Kubanga ddi ebibya ebitassaamu kitiibwa lwe byagoloka okulwana n'okutwala omubumbi n'amagezi? Naye bwe butyo obuddugavu n'amalala g'abo abagwa bwe buli, era tebaliwona kiyungu kyabwe ekyaka emirembe n'emirembe.

Kale mutegeere ebiseera mwe muli era musigale mu nze, mmwe Ekkanisa yange, ye Omugole wange! Kubanga okununulibwa kwammwe okujjuvu n'obusika bwammwe obujjuvu tebulwawo, era temulisigala mu mbizzi kubanga mwakkiririza mu linnya lyange mu nnaku zino ez'ekisa n'okusaasira, era bwe mutyo ekisa n'okusaasira okw'emirembe n'emirembe bye muliba nabyo. N'ab'oluganda lwa mmwe balyegatta nammwe emirembe n'emirembe ne mu Bwakabaka bwange obujja obw'obutuukirivu, be abo bonna abatali beetegefu kulabika kwange.

Kubanga nze omulongooseza akoleeza omuliro ogw'okutukuza ndyoke ntukuze eby'obugagga bya Yakuwa, nga nzigyamu kazambi, era nze oyo atuula ng'omuweesi n'omuvuwo mu ngalo ng'akoleeza amanda era azinga n'aweesa ebyuma eby'omuwendo ebya Yakuwa okuleeta amaanyi ag'emirembe n'emirembe. Ekibiina kinene, n'ekibiina ky'ebibiina, kijja kujja gye ndi nga kiyita mu kubonaabona kwa Yakobo, era kirikungula empeera nnene, era be basika b'obulokozi obw'emirembe n'emirembe era mu ssanyu ery'emirembe n'emirembe mwe baligenda era Isiraeri alirokolebwa! Kale temutya baana bange, so temweraliikirira baagalwa bammwe era mube n'okukkiriza mu nze Kristo Yesu, kubanga nsobola okulokola ddala abo bonna abakoowoola erinnya lyange, so tewali n'omu ku abo abalagirwa obulamu obutaggwaawo asobola okusikulwa mu mukono gwange, era ndirokola bangi okuva mu muliro ogutaggwaawo, nga mbakaggyamu mu nnimi z'omuliro.

Nze Yeshua HaMashiach ntumye ebigambo bino okuyita mu muweereza wange, era okuyita mu muweereza wange ebigambo bino biweerezeddwa!

Nze agaba amagezi eri abagezigezi, omusajja omugezi alyoke abeere omugezi.

Nze Mwana gw'endiga gwa Katonda ajja ng'Empologoma ey'omu kika kya Yuda.

Sirina kibi, naye naasasula omuwendo ogutasobola kutegeerekeka olw'okununulibwa kwammwe n'eddembe lyammwe.

Nze oyo atuula ng'omuweesi azinga n'aweesa ebyuma eby'omuwendo eby'obugagga bwa Yakuwa.

Nsobola okulokola ddala abo bonna abakoowoola erinnya lyange, so tewali n'omu ku abo abalagirwa obulamu obutaggwaawo asobola okusikulwa mu mukono gwange.

Mulindirire okulabika kwange, kubanga ebiseera bituuse. Ekiseera ekisigaddeyo kiweereddwa okuweereza ebigendererwa bya Yakuwa byokka baana bange, kale temukkiriza kusiisibwa kwebaka nate, n'okunyooma ebigambo byange eby'amagezi n'okulabula okungi.

Omusajja omugezi alaba kawumpuli n'alyekweka, naye abasiru beeyongera kugenda, ne balumizibwa. Saakubayita eri amagezi ga Yakuwa ag'emirembe n'emirembe?

Era nze oyo alibakweka gye mutandisobodde kwekwemba, kubanga ndibakweka mu kisenge kya Yakuwa eky'entebe y'obwakabaka ne mu ggulu ly'amagulu gye muliyingirira mu kusanyuka kwa Mukama wammwe n'ebisenge by'ebijjulo eby'omu ggulu biribajaguliza mwenna, kubanga mangu okusinga bwe mulowooza muliraba n'amaaso gammwe obulungi obw'ekitiibwa obw'emirembe n'emirembe obubalindiridde mu busika bwammwe. Mweyongere okubeera mu kwagala kwange, kubanga mwagalwa nnyo Kitaffe era muli mbonerere y'eriiso lyange, abo be ntaswazizza kubayita baganda bange.

Nze Yeshua HaMashiach njogedde ne ntuma ebigambo bino era bwe bityo biwandiikiddwa n'Enjiri ey'Emirembe n'Emirembe ebikkuddwa mu kiseera kyayo.
