Ogwokuna 19, 2024
Okubwatuka 5 & 6

Abatali Batuukirivu Tebalisikira Bwakabaka bwa Katonda!

Okuva eri Yeshua HaMashiach:

Mwana wange, bw’onookkirizanga ebigambo byange, n’otereka amateeka gange gy’oli; n’oteganga okutu kwo eri amagezi, n’osinzikiza omutima gwo eri okutegeera. Temukakanyazanga mitima gyammwe, nga mu kutaamye okw’omu kusoomoozebwa, ku lunaku olw'okukemebwa mu ddungu: era mumanye nga nze, Mukama wammwe Yesu Kristo, bulijjo ndi nammwe era bulijjo ntera okubeera mu bulamu bwammwe bwe munywerera ku nze n’obulokozi bwange, kubanga nze Mutwe gw’Omubiri, era nze Mukama wammwe omwesigwa, ggwe Omugole wange omwagalwa. Kubanga mbategekedde ekifo n'ebiseera eby'omu maaso, era waliwo empeera nnene eri abo be nnayita, abeesigwa, era abalonde.

Saabayitira kufiirwa na kubonerezebwa so saabayitira nsonyi na kwenenya; temwasomanga nti tewali kusalirwa musango kw'abo abali mu Kristo Yesu, abatambulira mu mubiri wabula mu mwoyo? N’olwekyo mukkirize ebigambo byange! Saabayitira kubeera mu kisiikirize ky'ebibi byammwe eby'edda, wadde okubeera mu kisiikirize ky'ebikolwa byammwe eby'omu buvubuka, wabula mbayise okufiira engeri zammwe ez'edda ez'okwonoona ne mutwala obulamu obuggya mu nze bwe mwambula omubiri gwammwe, bwe mwambula ebibi byammwe ne mutambulira mu bulamu obuggya mu nze. Laba! Nfuula ebintu byonna biggya! Lino si lye ssuubi lyammwe?

Baana bange, mbaggye mu kwonooneka kw’ensi eno efa, era mbawonyezza mu maanyi g’ekizikiza ne mbazza mu bwakabaka bwange, obwakabaka bwa Katonda, obukulemberwa nze, Kabaka wa Bakabaka era Mukama wa Bakama, nga Yahweh ye Katonda, kubanga tewali mulala. Mbayitidde ekitiibwa n’obulungi mu nze, era mbanunudde mu bwakabaka bwa Sitaani, obwakabaka bw’ekizikiza, okubayingiza mu Bwakabaka bwange, awalimu omusana! Kale, zuukuka ggwe eyeebase, ogolokoke mu bafu, nange, Yesu Kristo, nnaakuwa omusana.

Temwasomanga mu mabaluwa ge nnawandiikira Omugole wange, ge nnabasindikira mmwe, Ekkanisa yange yennyini? Temumanyi ng'abatali batuukirivu tebalisikira bwakabaka bwa Katonda? Temulimbibwanga: abenzi, newakubadde abasinza ebifaananyi, newakubadde abasambazi, newakubadde abasiyazi, newakubadde abeegadanga n’abasajja bannaabwe, N’ababbi, newakubadde abeegombi, newakubadde abatamiivu, newakubadde abavumi, newakubadde abanyazi, tebalisikira bwakabaka bwa Katonda. Era abamu ku mmwe mwali bwe mutyo: naye mwanoozebwa, naye mwatukuzibwa, naye mwaweebwa obutuukirivu mu Linnya lya Mukama waffe Yesu, ne mu Mwoyo gwa Katonda waffe.

Temutegeera biragiro byange, ai, Ekkanisa ya Lawodikiya eya langilangi? Saabayitira kuddamu kwegatta na mubiri gwammwe, era saabayitira kweyongera mu makubo gammwe agatali matuukirivu. Lowooza, era wefumiitirize; oyo alowooza nti ayimiridde, yeegendereze aleme okugwa! Mwekuume mwekka, n’enjigiriza: mubeerenga mu ebyo: kubanga bw’onookolanga bw’otyo, olyerokola ggwe, n'abo abakuwulira. Tegeera, mmwe abato bange ab'akalanzi, nti temusobola kubeera na bulamu bwa kukkiriziganya wakati w'obulungi n'obubi, nga mulowooza nti muli batuukirivu mu maaso gange, bwe mulondawo ekibi ekisingako obutono mu bikolwa byammwe eby’okulonda bye siwagira wadde okukkiriza.

Mufulumemu, mmwe abantu bange, kubanga SSAATEGEKAWO nteekateeka ya byabufuzi, era sirina n'akatono ke nnabawa akakulembeze w’ensi okuzza ekintu kyonna nga kikulu. Mulimbibwa, era mutegekebwa okugoberera n'okukkiriza omwana w'okuzikirira atambula mu bbanga mu maaso gammwe, newankubadde tannabikkulibwa. Naye mmwe abasaba okutegeera mulimanya bw’ali, mulyoke mwewale obulimba bwe. Nedda, okukkiriziganya si kwa mmwe, kubanga muli baana bange na baganda bange, era saawangula bulokozi bwammwe nga nteesa ne Sitaani okuwaayo obulamu bwange ng’ekinunulo ky'abangi; kubanga okukkiriziganya kwe kwegatta ku bibiina byabwe. N’olwekyo temukola nsobi: Oba munyimirirawo ku lwange mu butuukirivu n'obutukuvu obw'amazima, oba mwegasse ku bibiina bya Sitaani mu bwakabaka bwe obufa, kale MWENENYE!

Mufunamu ki bwe muteesa n’obubi? Temutegeera nti muteekwa okuwangula obubi n’obulungi? Kale munywerere mu nze muleme kuddamu kujjulukuka ng’ekyennyanja ekiri ku lukalu. Abangi ku mmwe mukyapeepeetulwa empewo yonna ey’enjigiriza n’obukujjukujju, mwe beeraliikirira okubalimba. Nze, Kristo Yesu, njogedde, era sijja kusirika. Abangi ku mmwe mwefuze mwekka n’ekkanisa yange, era mbabuuza nti: mulisigala muwetenkanya wakati w'ebirowoozo ebibiri okutuusa ddi? Bwe kiba nti Mukama ye Katonda, mumugoberere: naye bwe kiba nti Baali, mumugoberere ye. Mu bufunze, temusobola kukkiriziganya ne mulondawo engeri yonna ey’obubi ne mulowooza nti munyimirirawo ku lwange; bwe mulondawo obubi, muba mulonze ensibuko y’obubi obwo okuba mukama wammwe era omukulu wammwe era mufuuka muddu w’ekibi. Temumanyi nti oyo gwe mweyongerayo okuba abaddu be okumugondera, abaddu be ye gwe mugondera: oba bwa kibi okutuusa okufa, oba bwa kugonda okutuusa obutuukirivu?

Mumpulire era mumpulirize bulungi, mmwe Ekkanisa yange; mumanye nti ntamiddwa nnyo olw’ebikolwa ebiri mu ggwanga lyammwe ne mu nsi yonna, bye mwateesa ne mubikkiriza mu Kkanisa yange okubonyaabonya abato bange. Nga bwe kyali mu nnaku za Nuuwa, bwe kityo bwe kiriba. Ennaku za Nuuwa zaali biseera nsi we yafugibwa emizizo emibi egy'abamalayika abagwa, ezzadde lyabwe lyennyini, ezzadde ly'obubi erikoleddwa okubazikiriza ddala: era balidda, kubanga laba, ndeeta ebiremu okutuukiriza obusungu bwange. Kino baakituukako nga baggyawo ne bamenya ensalo z'obutonde Kitange ze yateekawo obulungi n'obutuukirivu biryoke bibeerewo, naye olw'okuggyawo n'okusala mu ngeri eteri ya mpisa ensalo zenyini ezirabika mu butonde, Kitange ze yatandikawo, abangi ku mmwe mwetyabise olw'okukkiriza kwammwe obubi obusasanyuse mu biseera byammwe. Ekintu ekyabangawo, kye kiribeerawo: n'ekyo ekikolebwa, kye kirikolebwa; so tewali kigambo kipya wansi w'enjuba. Temwasomanga, mmwe abalina okukkiriza okutono? Okuggyawo ensalo za Katonda yennyini kireeta eddalu, n’endwadde, n’okutabukatabuka kw’ebintu byonna. Abangi ku mmwe mwesanyusa mu bulwadde bw'omu mutwe, nga mulowooza nti omusajja asobola okufuuka omukazi n'omukazi okufuuka omusajja, era mukulembera abaana bammwe bennyini okuyingira mu ndowooza zammwe ez’obulimba. Abangi ku mmwe mugenda mu makanisa agakulemberwa abaweereza ab'obulimba abasiyazi abali mu lwatu era abeewulira mu bulwadde bwabwe, era mulowooza nti boogera ku lwange? Mumanye kino: bw'oba ​​oli mu mbeera eyo ey'ekibi, nnaakuwaayo eri okwegomba kwe weegomba ennyo, era olikungula ebibala by'amakubo go amatali malongoofu.

Laba! Olw’ensonga eno, Katonda yabayabulira mu kwegomba okw'ensonyi: kubanga n'abakazi baabwe baakyusa enkozesa y'obutonde ne bakozesa ekitasaana: Era n'abasajja nabo bwe batyo, bwe baaleka enkozesa y'obutonde ey'omukazi, ne bakwatibwa okwegomba kwabwe okumu ku munne, abasajja n'abasajja nga bakola ekitasaana, ne bafuna mu bo empeera y'ensobi yaabwe eyali ebasaanira. Naye nedda, tekimala kukulemberwa abasajja abakyakazi n’obulimba bwabwe; mukkiriza abaana bammwe okuteesa era n'okutuusa okubatuusaako obulema mu maaso gange.

Waliwo ebibonerezo binene ebitegekeddwa olw’ebikolwa bino, era tebijja kulekebwa nga tebibonerezeddwa. Era n’okweyongerayo mu ddalu lyammwe, era bwe mutoonenye, nnaabawanzamu mu kamwa kange. Mujje gyendi nga mwenenyezza, mutuuze mu bibukutu ne mu vvu, ndyoke mmanye obunyiikivu bw’okwenenya kwammwe era mukaabe era muwowoggane olw’ebibi byammwe muleme okusasula omuwendo mu musango. Abangi ku mmwe mulina amalala mangi kubanga mugezaako okumpuula Mukama wammwe ate mukyalina omutima ogutakkiriza, kubanga omuntu n’omutima gwe akkiriza okutuusa obutuukirivu. Kale mube n’okukkiriza mulyoke mulokoke; okukkiriza kwammwe kuliragibwa eri bonna nga mwegaana ekibi ne muva mu makubo gammwe amakyafu. Ababi batambulira ku njuyi zonna, abantu abasinga obubi bwe bagulumizibwa. Naye mukyakkiriza amakubo g’omusodomiti, era muyamba okuleeta obubi obungi ku nsi, okutuuka n’okulonda abakwasi b’abaana okukulembera ensi zammwe. Temulina wadde eddaala erisookerwako ery’okutegeera, era temugoberera kkubo lyange; era bwe muneeyongerayo, nnaabawaayo ddala eri okwegomba kwammwe okw’ensonyi okw'okuzikiriza omubiri omwoyo gulyoke gulokoke.

Leka nzigule amaaso gammwe, mmwe abatamanyi era abazibe b’amaaso ku bugenderere, mmwe abatuula mu kazambi n’ekizikiza, nga mwetakulankula mu busa. Temusobola kukkiriza bubi ne mulowooza nti mumpereza, kubanga naziyiza obubi n’okukemebwa okutuusa ettosi ly’omusaayi erisembayo bwe lyankulukuta okuva mu nze okugwa ku ttaka eddungi, kubanga nze Adamu ow’oluvannyuma era nfuuse mwoyo oguwa obulamu. Abangi ku mmwe muzibye amaaso n’ebigambo ebirungi n’emboozi ennungi ne muwa olukusa olujjuvu abazzi b’emisango n’abantu ababi abatigomya abaana. Bakwata abaana, babatulugunya olw'okwegomba kwabwe okubi: Kubanga bw'etyo obusungu bwa Katonda bujja ku Babulooni ekyama, naye abangi ku mmwe beeyongerayo okuwa olukusa eri ensolo zino ez’effutwa eziteekeddwawo olw’emiro gy’omuliro ogutazikira. Mufulumemu, mmwe abantu bange, oba muligabana naye mu bibonerezo bye n’omusango ogw’entiisa ogumujjira; era mbalabudde. Kubanga Kristo alina nteesa ki ne Beliyaali? Bw’oligabana mu bulimba bwe, oligabana mu bibonerezo bye; kale mwawukane ddala mu kigambo ne mu kikolwa, so temukkiriziganya nabo n'akatono. Temubeera na bulamu butali bwa kifaayo, obw'okukkiriziganya; kubanga abo bonna abakola bwe batyo nnaababonereza. Mulowooza nti kubanga mulina obulamu obutaggwaawo temusobola kufuna kufiirwa kunene n’ebibonerezo? Nnaabaggyako empeera zammwe ne nzigaba, era muliba abasinga obutono mu bwakabaka obw’omu ggulu bwe muneeyongerayo, kubanga bw’atyo bw’ayogera Yeshua HaMashiach, Omugole omusajja, eri Omugole wange!

Okuva eri Yahweh, Katonda Omukulu Asemba byonna:

O mmwe abatalina mugaso abalumya ekitonde kyange, mmwe abatalina mugaso abeetunda eri Sitaani, mmwe abatalina mugaso abafudde Sitaani Katonda wammwe, mmwe abatalina mugaso abagaana omwana wange Yesu Kristo n’obulokozi bwe nateekateeka mu linnya lye, mmwe abatalina mugaso abakkirizza abaana bammwe okwokya mu muliro gwa Moloki. Nnaabawa ensi gye mwagala, era mbategekedde ensi etaliimu nze, ensi etaliimu mwana wange Kristo Yesu, ensi erimu ennyanja ennene ey’omuliro n’ekibiriiti eyammwe mwekka.

Nnaabawa ekizikiza kye mwagala ennyo, kubanga omusana gwange tegulibeerawo, era mulimala emirembe n'emirembe nga mwawuddwa ku nze, Yahweh, Katonda omukulu ow’ekitiibwa, ekitiibwa kye n’okumasamasa kwe tewali mubiri gusobola kulaba era aliwo okuva emirembe n’emirembe. Mmwe abajeemu, ababi abazikirizza ekitonde kyange okuva ku lubereberye mu kwegomba kwammwe n’obukyayi n’obuggya, mumanye nti mwekuŋŋaanyirizza ekibonerezo ekinene n’okubonyaabonyezebwa kwe mutajja kuwonyezebwa, n'eri bonna abakyamizibwa obulimba bwammwe n’okugaana Yesu Kristo, okugaana okuva mu bulimba bwammwe okudda eri amazima gange.

Mulowooza nti okwonoona amakubo gange n’okuggyawo ensalo zange kintu kitono, naye mulitegeera obusungu bwange amangu ddala ng’olulimi lw’omuliro olusooka lubakoonako, era mulizingizibwa omuliro n’ekibiriiti bye mutajja kuwona. Ate era, mwatta, mwakwata, mwanyaga, mwakwata, mwalumya, mwatta, mwazikiriza, era mwenyigira mu misango egisinga obubi, nga mwagala n’omubiri ogw’ekika ekirala; era mwegasse ku bamalayika abagwa, kale nnaabegattako ku nnyanja y'omuliro gye nategekera Omulyolyomi n'abamalayika be, so temuliwona. Temulina mirembe.

Nnaabasalira omusango ogw’ensonga, kubanga ndi Katonda ow’ensonga, era ndi Katonda ow’okuwalana eggwanga. Naye, mulinyimirirawo okunzira mu malala, nga mwegayirira ensonga yammwe, nga mulowooza nti nnaatya amakubo gammwe aga sitaani ag’okukola ky’oyagala, liribe lye tteeka lyonna. Temulina mirembe. Nnaasasula, nammwe mugamba nti ndi Katonda ow’okwagala (nga bwe ndi), naye ne musuubira nti sijja kusalira musango abo aboonoonye ekitonde kyange, abo abaatulugunya ne batta omwana wange omu yekka eyazaalibwa bwe yeewaayo okuba ekinunulo kya bonna; yee, musuubira nti sijja kubasalira musango olw’amakubo gammwe agatalina mugaso bwe mwatta, ne mutulugunya, ne mulemaza abataliiko musango era ne muviirako abato bange okufa enjala olw’enteekateeka zammwe.

Mazima, Sitaani yennyini yandizikirizza byonna ebyange olw'omululu gwe, n'oluvannyuma n'ogezaako okunnumiriza obusirusiru mu misango gyange emituukirivu bwe nzijja okukuuma abange. Ennaku zammwe ez’okukyamya abato bange ziri kumpi okukoma. Laba! Ensolo yammwe ne nnabbi w’obulimba balizikirizibwa ddala, era nnaabasuula nga balamu mu nnyanja ennene eyo eyaka omuliro n’ekibiriiti; balibalindirira okwegatta nabo mu busika bwammwe.

Omusango omunene guli ku ggwe, ai omulangira w'ekizikiza; obwakabaka bwo n'abo bonna abakugoberera bali kumpi okuzikirizibwa ddala. Kubanga mmwe si bakatonda, era temusobola kwewonya mu mukono gwange, era nnaabasiba mu kizikiza ekisinga obunzitovu kye mwagala ennyo, era n'obunnya obusinga obunzitovu nnaabasibiramu olujegere olunene lwe mutasobola kumenya. Yee, mu bunnya mulibeeramu olunaku lumu, kubanga olunaku lumu eri Mukama lulinga emyaka lukumi, n’emyaka lukumi lulinga olunaku lumu. Era n'oluvannyuma nnaabasumulula akaseera katono mulyoke mukeme era mukuŋŋaanye abasembayo ku abo ab'omuzizo abandyagoberedde n'amakubo go. Nange olwo nnaabamalawo mwenna n’omuliro, n’entebe ez’omusango ziriteekebwawo, nange nnaasalira omusango mmwe mwenna abakola obubi, era muligenda mu kubonerezebwa okutaggwaawo okuva mu maaso ga Mukama. Muliyokya mu maaso ga bamalayika ne mu maaso g'omwana gw'endiga, era balibeesiima mu kubonyaabonyezebwa ne mu kibonerezo, kubanga mubasobyeko bonna, era mulisasulira ddala ebibi byammwe emirembe n'emirembe.

Kale mulisibirwa mu kkomera lyammwe, era mulyerabirwa, so temulidda mu birowoozo, kubanga eby'edda biriggwaawo ne byerabirwa. Kubanga laba, ndikola eggulu eriggya n’ensi empya omubeera obutuukirivu; nange, Yahweh, Katonda omukulu ow’emirembe n’emirembe owa bakatonda bonna, kubanga tewali alinga nze, laba, ndifuula ebintu byonna biggya, kubanga nze, Yahweh, nkyogedde! Yahweh, Katonda omukulu ow'emirembe n'emirembe, aliteeka entebe ye ey'obwakabaka ku nsi, n'ekifo kyange eky'okubeeramu kiriba mu bantu, so temulidda kubaawo nate, ai mmwe abatalina mugaso, era muliyokya emirembe n'emirembe mu kkomera lyammwe eryerabirwa ery'okubonyaabonyezebwa lye mutasobola kuwona, kubanga nnaateekawo akabonero so tewali ayinza kukamenya.

Naye, bangi ku mmwe abasaasira Sitaani bannumiriza obusirusiru olw’okutwala obwenkanya n’okuwalana eggwanga mu mikono gyange nzekka okuwonya abo abeefuula abasaanira ekibonerezo ekitaggwaawo eri ennimiro z’omuliro. Wuliriza ebigambo byange, ai abatuula ku nsi: Omwana wange ajja, era ajja n’okuwalana eggwanga okungi, obusungu, n’ekiruyi olw’ebikolwa ebibi ebikoleddwa emikono gyammwe. Ebiro by’omusango, n’omusango gw’olunaku olukulu olwa Kristo gwali kumpi okubwatuka ku nsi, so tewaliba ddagala ly’ababi, tewaliba ddagala lya Sitaani, so tewaliba ddagala ly’abo bonna abamwegasseeko, kubanga bonna baligenda mu kuzikirira okutaggwaawo, era nnaabaddiza byonna bye baaleeta ku bataliiko musango.

Kyendiva mbasasula okwegomba kw'emitima gyabwe emibi n'ebikolwa byabwe byonna ebibi emirembe n'emirembe. Laba obusungu bwange nga bwesera era omanye nti ebiseera by'okuzzaawo ebintu byonna bituuse, so tewakyali nnamba ku ssaawa ez'omu ggulu, kubanga ennamba zonna zigudde ku maaso g'essaawa, kubanga obudde buweddeyo! Nkubiriza bonna abasoma ebigambo bino okuddukira eri omwana wange Yesu Kristo nga beenenyezza ne bamufuula Mukama waabwe; kubanga tewali ajja gyendi wabula ng’ayita mu Yesu Kristo, kubanga tewali linnya lya ngeri yonna lye nnawa lya bulokozi, n’ebifaananyi byammwe byonna biriyokya. Wakyaliwo ebbanga lya bulokozi, na buli muntu alikoowoola erinnya lya Mukama okutuusa ku nkomerero alirokolebwa. Kale muddukire eri Yesu Kristo nga mukyasobola, kubanga olunaku lwa Mukama lutuuse, era nnaasumulula Omwana wange, n’obusungu bw’Omwana gw’endiga bulikkusibwa. Munywegere Omwana aleme okusunguwala, ne muzikirira okuva mu kkubo, obusungu bwe bwe bubuubuuka akatono: Balina omukisa bonna abamwesiga.


Okujuliza: Isaaya 13:1 okuva mu Septuagint: Ndagira, ne mbaleeta: ebiremu bijja kutuukiriza busungu bwange, nga bisanyuka mu kiseera kye kimu era nga bivuma.
