Omujulirwa Omulamba: Ekkanisa
Mu Biwandiiko by'Omutume Pawulo
Meeyi 03, 2024

Mbawadde Essuubi Erinene!

Mujje gye ndi muwulire ebigambo byange, Omugole wange, kubanga ndi wano okubeebeeta emyoyo gyammwe, okubudaabuda emitima gyammwe, n'okubafuula abatuukiridde mu nze, Kristo Yesu. Temuggwaamu maanyi olw'ebigambo byange eby'okunenya, kubanga gwennjagala, ye gwempima, okuyitibwa kwammwe n'okulondebwa kwammwe bibeere ddala eby'amazima mu nze, Omugole Omwami, ye Kristo Yesu. Baana bange, mutege amatu gammwe eri eddoboozi lyange muwulire ebigambo byange, kubanga saabayita kulongoosebwa na kusalirwa musango, naye nabalangirira nga mutuukiridde mu nze, ye Mukama wammwe Yesu Kristo. Temwawulira Pawulo kye yagamba Abakkolinso mu bbaluwa ye eyasooka? Era kaakano wabeerawo okukkiriza, essuubi n'okwagala, ebyo byonsatule: naye ekisinga ebyo byonna kwe kwagala. Kale nno okwagala kye kisinga obunene mu byonsatule, kubanga okwagala kwa Yakuwa tekuyinza kuwangulwa. Okwagala kwa Yakuwa kye kiramu ekinyweza. Kye kireeta amaanyi amangi, era okuwa awatali kisasulwa nga nammwe bwe mwafuna awatali kusasula, kwe kubeera abakoppi ba Katonda!

Bangi ku mmwe mufiiriddwa essuubi, n'essuubi lyammwe mu nze, Yesu Kristo, kubanga mbawadde essuubi erinene, baana bange. Mutegeere nti omubbi ayinza okubatataganya n'okubakyamya, naye Katonda si ye mutandisi wa kutatagana, wabula owa mirembe nga bwe kiri mu kkanisa zonna ez'abatukuvu. Setaani afubye okulabisa obubi ebiragiro byange bye nnawa mu kubikkulirwa kwange okw'Ekyama Ekinene ekya Katonda eri Omutume Pawulo emyaka mingi egiyiseewo. Baana bange, essuubi lyammwe y'engeri ennyini ebayamba okugumiikiriza ebigezo n'ebibonyoobonyo ebingi bye musanga mu bulamu buno. Kubanga awatali ssuubi lyammwe, mwandyabaddewo nga Pawulo bwe yalangirira eri Abakkolinso: Bwe tuba n'essuubi mu Kristo mu bulamu buno bwokka, ffe abasinga bonna okusaasirwa. Naye kaakano Kristo azuukidde mu bafu, era afuuse ekibala ekisooka eky'abo abeebase. Baana bange, mbawadde essuubi ddene olw'essuubi lyammwe, kubanga nnagamba: mmwe abakyali abalamu era abasigaddewo mulinyukulibwa mu bire, nga abafu abali mu Kristo bwe balisooka okuzuukira, era mwenna mulifuulibwa abaggya, kubanga bwe mutyo bwe muliba nga nze emirembe gyonna era bwe gutyo Omugole wange bw'aliba nange emirembe n'emirembe.

Kubanga essuubi lyammwe mu Kristo kiki, baana bange? Si kye kisuubizo ky'okununulibwa okujjuvu n'okuzzibwa obuggya, bwe muliraba eggulu n'ekitiibwa n'obukulu bwa Yakuwa n'amaaso gammwe gennyini? Essuubi lyammwe si biseera bya mu maaso we mulimpeereza mu bwakabaka bwange obw'obutuukirivu, kubanga nga bwe mwabanga abeesigwa ku bintu ebitono, bwe ntyo bwe ndibafuula abafuzi b'ebingi. Essuubi lyammwe si kye kisuubizo ky'obulamu obutaggwaawo bwe mulirya ku muti gw'obulamu ne mutambulira mu nguudo za Yerusaalemi Omusya, omusana gwa Yakuwa n'omusana gwa Yeshua HaMashiach we guliyakira emirembe gyonna mu kwaka okw'ekitiibwa? Essuubi lyammwe si kitiibwa kya ngule nnyingi n'empeera olw'obwesigwa bwammwe n'okwagala kwammwe gye ndi, Kristo Yesu? Essuubi lyammwe si ssuubi lya bulamu bulungi ddala, nga tewakyali ndwadde newaakubadde obulwadde? Essuubi lyammwe si lye lya nze okuteeka abalabe bammwe wansi w'ebigere byammwe era n'okufuula ekuŋŋaaniro lya Setaani okuvuunama ku bigere byammwe? (Kubanga balimanya nga nabagala!) Essuubi lyammwe si lya ssanyu teriggwaawo mu maaso ga Yakuwa yennyini? Kubanga alissaawo ennyumba ye mu bantu, era tewalibaawo nate bwakabaka obwawuddwamu, wabula Obwakabaka bwa Katonda bwokka, kubanga tewali asinga Yakuwa; nedda, tewali ayinza kugeraageranyizibwa ku kitangaala kya Katonda omunene ow'emirembe, ye Kitaffe!

Musanyukire ebiseera byammwe eby'omu maaso, baana bange, kubanga mbawadde ebiseera eby'omu maaso n'ekigendererwa ekitukuvu mu nze, Mukama wammwe ayaka! Essuubi lyammwe si ssuubi ddene bwe lityo bwe mulokolebwa mu busungu mu nze? Kubanga eryammwe lye ssuubi erisinga obunene, essuubi eryogerwako omubaka wange omulonde Omutume Pawulo, ye musigire w'Ekyama Ekinene ekya Katonda? Kubanga nabikkulira Pawulo kye yawandiikira Abakkolosaayi: N'ekyama ekyakwekebwa okuva mu mirembe gyonna, n'emiryango, naye kaakano kibikkuliddwa eri abatukuvu be, Katonda be yayagala okumanyisa obugagga bw'ekitiibwa ky'ekyama kino bwe buli mu Bamawanga, ye Kristo mu mmwe, essuubi ly'ekitiibwa. Essuubi lyammwe lya kitiibwa ekisaanira kabaka, kubanga ndibafuula bakabaka ne bakabona mu maaso ga Katonda waffe; kubanga nze kabona asinga obukulu emirembe gyonna ng'engeri ya Melkizeddeeki bw'eri, era tewalibaawo mulala.

Baana bange abato, temutya bulimba bwa mulabe, kubanga yandyabakooyezza mu bukyamu singa yandisobodde; naye yansanga nze ne mmuwangula ddala, era njija okuzikiriza obwakabaka bwe, kubanga nnatumibwa okumenya emirimu gya Setaani, era bwe ntyo bwe nabatuma! Omusota ogw'edda n'obwakabaka bwe bwonna tebulina ssuubi. Bamanyi nti basaliddwa omusango gw'okuzikirizibwa n'okubonerezebwa okutaggwaawo, we baliyokebwa mu nnimi z'omuliro ezisinga okwokya eza bbulu nga bawuga mu sulufeeri ayaka emirembe gyonna. Babakwatirwa obuggya era bandiwaddeyo kyonna okubeera nga mmwe, kale temuwuliriza bigambo byabwe. Mumpulirize, Omugole wange; Setaani alikola kyonna okubaggya ku mpeera yammwe mu nze, Kristo Yesu. Alikola kyonna okukweka essuubi lyammwe lye mbawadde, kale nno engeri ze mu ngeri eno emirundi mingi kwe kussa wansi ekifaananyi nga akoppa amazima n'olw'olulimi lwe olulimba.

Temumutya, naye mwawukane n'obulimba bwe mukuumire essuubi lyammwe ery'amazima mu birowoozo, kubanga temwasoma? Omwoyo yennyini ategeeza wamu n'omwoyo gwaffe, nti tuli baana ba Katonda. Era bwe tuba abaana, n'olwekyo tuli basika, abasika ba Katonda, n'abasika awamu ne Kristo: bwe kiba nti tubonabona naye, tulyoke tugulumizibwe wamu. Kubanga ntegeera nti okubonaabona okw'omu kiseera kino tekusaana kugeraageranyizibwa n'ekitiibwa ekigenda okubikkulibwa mu ffe. Baana bange, singa mwategeera obunene bw'ekitegeeza okubeera omusika awamu ne Kristo, naye mu bbanga ttono mulimanya nga nammwe bwe mumanyiddwa. Temutegeera nti Katonda ampadde ebintu byonna mu ggulu ne ku nsi, era obusika bwange bwe busika obusinga obunene? Baana bange, temwetegeera mmwe bennyini ab'amazima, kubanga mmwe bennyini ab'amazima mukwekeddwa mu nze, era bwe ndirabika, era nammwe mulirabika nange mu kitiibwa!

Temutya, era munywere mu kwagala kwammwe n'okukkiriza, kubanga essuubi lyammwe liribaleetera okutambulira mu kugumiikiriza okungi; kubanga essuubi lyange n'essanyu Kitange lye yanteekera mu maaso byaleetera ki okutuukirizibwa? Kubanga Abebbulaniya kigamba nti: Nga tutunuulira Yesu Omukulembeze era Omutuukiriza w'okukkiriza kwaffe, olw'essanyu eryateekebwa mu maaso ge, yagumiikiriza omusalaba, ng'anyooma ensonyi, era atuula ku mukono ogwa ddyo ogw'entebe ya Katonda. Saabaleetera kyakulabirako, era ssaafuuka kika kye musaanidde okugoberera? Temutegeera? Nti oyo anzikiririza talikola mirimu gyokka gye nnakola mu kujja kwange okwasooka, naye emirimu egisinga obukulu egyo gye nnakola? Kubanga ngenze eri Kitange era mbawadde amaanyi era mbayimiriddeko n'amaanyi gange gonna, kubanga ndi wa maanyi, kubanga bwe ntyo Katonda bwe yanfuula mu kuwuliza kwange.

Bantu bange, ebigambo bino birina okubazzaamu amaanyi n'okubawa amaanyi okuyimirira, kubanga mbateereddewo essanyu lingi! Temukkiririza bulimba bwa mulabe ku ssuubi lyammwe, kubanga yandigezezzaako okubaleka nga temulina ssuubi era nga mulinnyirirwa wansi w'ebigere, era ne mu bunafu bwammwe mufuulibwa ab'amaanyi mu nze, Mukama wammwe Yesu Kristo! Kaakano ng'enda kubanyigiriza ebiseera byammwe eby'omu maaso, Omugole wange, kale mutege amatu gammwe eri ebigambo by'akamwa kange, kubanga bigambo bya kukkiriza, bigambo bya kwagala, era ebigambo byange birikomyawo essuubi lyammwe mu mazima, kubanga essuubi erinene liri mu mazima ge mbawadde!

Okusooka, mutegeere nti nabayita; nabalokola mu mwoyo gwange ogw'amaanyi n'obwana; nabafuula ekitonde ekipya mu nze, Kristo Yesu! Olw'okwewaayo kwange ng'Okuyitako okutuukiridde, bonna abanzikiririza mu kukkiriza okulokola okuva mu mitima gyabwe nabo balilokolebwa mu busungu mu nze. Baana bange, mutegeere nti ebigambo bino bya kitalo nnyo eri abantu abasinga obungi, kubanga ebibi byabwe bibakuba ne bibateeka mu musango, okusalirwa omusango, n'ensonyi, era kino kibaawo kubanga emirundi mingi bagaana okwetukuza mu musaayi gwange ogutangirira, era balekerera okwatula ebibi byabwe, era balekerera enkolagana yaabwe nange, omulangira ow'emirembe, kubanga obulokozi buva mu kukkiriza okw'enkolagana mu nze, Mukama omunene!

Bangi ku mmwe temutegeera nti nabatuukiriza, era olw'okutuukirizibwa okwo mu nze, mulokolebwa mu busungu, kubanga nagamba Abaruumi: Kale nno, nga kaakano tutuukirizibbwa olw'omusaayi gwe, tulirokolebwa mu busungu okuyita mu ye. Munzaniremu kino, Omugole wange gwe njija okujja, lwaki mwagala olunaku lwa Mukama? Lwaki muwulirizza abaweereza ba Setaani abeefuula abaweereza bange bwe bagamba nti mulina okuyita mu kibonyoobonyo kya Yakobo n'ekibonyoobonyo ekinene ekibaawo mu kitundu eky'enkomerero ekya ssabbiiti ya Danyeri ey'amakumi musanvu? Babalimbalimba nammwe ne mubakkiririza. Bajjukiza embeera yammwe ey'ekibi, nga babagamba nti temulina mugaso so nga mmwe luulu ey'omuwendo omungi; babagamba nti musaanidde obusungu obungi mu nze, era balimba! Babagamba nti temulina kusuubira kununulibwa mu kibonyoobonyo, era bakozesa bubi Ekigambo kyange ekitukuvu. Kubanga ddala nagamba Pawulo bino wammanga okuyigiriza Timoseewo: Okuyigganyizibwa, ebibonyoobonyo ebyantukako e Antiyokiya, e Ikoniya, e Lusitula, okuyigganyizibwa okungi kwe nnayita mu: naye mu byonna Mukama yannunula. Weewaawo, naabo bonna abaliyagala okubeeranga n'empisa ezisaana Katonda mu Kristo Yesu, baliyigganyizibwa. Era mazima, Setaani aliyigganya bonna ababeera n'obulamu obusaana Katonda, era mu kuyigganyizibwa kwe kwonna ndibanunula bwe mukyukira gye ndi mu bizibu byammwe ne musigala nga muli beesigwa mu ssuubi lyammwe. Mutegeere enjawulo, kubanga Setaani atataganyizza ensonga: Setaani aliyigganya bonna ababeera obulungi, era ekyo kwe kuyigganyizibwa; era emirundi mingi mulina okugumiikiriza obusungu bwe, kubanga mu kuwangulwa kwe alina obusungu bungi. Naye temutya kuwuluguma kwe, kubanga nnagamba Peetero nti: Mubeere beegendereza, mutunule: kubanga omulabe wammwe Setaani ng'empologoma ewuluguma atambulatambula, anoonya gw'anaalya: Oyo mumugumire nga munyweredde mu kukkiriza, nga mumanyi nti ebibonyoobonyo ebyo bye bimu bituukirizibwa mu baganda bammwe abali mu nsi.

Mutegeere, baana bange, nti obusungu obugenda okujja si busungu bwa Setaani wabula busungu bwa Katonda n'obusungu bw'Omwana gw'Endiga obutegekeddwa Setaani. Kubanga Setaani mutiitiizi eyeewalula mu kitonde kyonna okwegezaako okwekweka obusungu bwe yateresa, ng'alowooza nti yandiwangudde Yakuwa mu nsonga ezo; naye ebigendererwa bya Kitange bitegeezeddwa bwe biti: Owange, towasiga nsigo nnungi mu nnimiro yo? Naye omuddo guno guvudde wa? N'abagamba nti, Omulabe ye yakikoze. Abaddu ne bamugamba nti, Kale oyagala tugende tugukuŋŋaanye? Naye n'agamba nti, Nedda: bwe munaakuŋŋaanya omuddo, mulyoke musimbule n'eŋŋaano wamu nagwo. Muleke byombi bikulire wamu okutuusa ku makungula: era mu kiseera ky'amakungula, ndigamba abakunguzi nti, Musooke mukuŋŋaanye omuddo, mugusibe mu nnyanda okugwokya: naye eŋŋaano mugikuŋŋaanyize mu ggalaniro lyange. Bwe kityo eŋŋaano erikuŋŋaanyizibwa mu ggalaniro, naye omuddo naabo bonna abaagusimba n'okugufukirira balisuulibwa nga balamu mu nnyanja ey'omuliro ne sulufeeri Kitange gy'ategekedde Setaani ne bamalayika be, naye tewali kitundu kya busungu bwa Katonda ekiri ku mmwe, wadde nga Setaani aligezaako okwekwesa emabega wammwe era alinoonya okubatwala nga abasibe ng'omunyago. Temutya, kubanga obusungu si bwa mmwe. Ntegeera nti emyoyo mingi emitatagana ginyoola olugero luno, nga gabagamba nti muliyita mu busungu nabo, naye bakikola nga tebafaayo ku ntegeka y'ebyawandiikibwa ennyonjo ku nsonga eno, era bafuba okubaziba amaaso nga bakozesa bubi olugero lwange.

Mutegeere nti ndibaggyawo ne mbatwala mu ggulu, kubanga mbategekedde ekifo eky'obukuumi n'empeera eri Omugole wange; kubanga mu nnyumba ya Kitange mwe ndibakweka era mulimu amayumba mangi. Kale yee, ndibakuŋŋaanya wamu gye ndi ku kulabika kwange, okuli okumpi okubaawo. Kubanga nagamba Abasessaloniika nti: Kubanga Mukama waffe yennyini alikka okuva mu ggulu ng'ayogerera waggulu, n'eddoboozi lya malayika omukulu, n'ekkondeere lya Katonda: n'abafu abali mu Kristo balisooka okuzuukira. Naffe abali abalamu, abalisigalawo, tulitwalibwa wamu nabo mu bire, okusisinkana Mukama waffe mu bbanga: bwe tutyo bwe tulibeeranga ne Mukama waffe ennaku zonna. N'olwekyo, musanyusagane mwekka na mwekka n'ebigambo ebyo. Mbabuuza, Omugole wange eyalimbirwa, kaakano muligaana obulimba bwa Setaani ne munywerera ku bigambo byange ne mubifunamu okubudaabudibwa ne mubudaabuda ab'oluganda? Kubanga bwe kityo bwe kiriba mu bbanga ttono, era nneegomba okubeerawo kwammwe, kubanga mbagala nnyo, kubanga nze Mugole wammwe.

Bwe muliggibwa ku nsi, ekibonyoobonyo kya Yakobo kirityanka ku nsi yonna era okuzikirizibwa okungi kulijja mangu ago, naye si kwa mmwe, kubanga mulitwalibwa mu ggulu. N'olwekyo muliyimirira mu maaso g'entebe yange ey'omusango ndyoke mbasasule okusinziira ku bikolwa byammwe. Era eri abeesigwa kuliko empeera nnene n'engule nnyingi, era mulyambazibwa ennyumba gye mbakoleredde, yee ennyumba eribakolwako mmwe mwekka era eribaamu ebintu byammwe ebingi bye mwatuukako, gye nditeekamu obusobozi obungi, kubanga bwe gutyo omubiri omuggya gwe mbalindiridde bwe guli. Tewali ndwadde, tewali bulumi, tewali nnaku, so tewakyali kikolimo, baana bange, kubanga ekikolimo kirikyukira ku mitwe gy'abo abakolimirwa. Era bwe mutyo bwe mulibeera mu ggulu mu kiseera ekyo eky'emyaka omusanvu, yee ssabbiiti ya Danyeri ey'amakumi asatu. N'olwekyo, mulyeebagala nange nga n'eggye lyonna ery'omu ggulu bwe liriba, kubanga kyategeezebwa era bwe kityo bwe kiriba: Nange ne ndaba eggulu nga libikkuse, era laba embalaasi enjeru, n'oyo eyali agituddeko ayitibwa mwesigwa era ow'amazima, era mu butuukirivu asala emisango era alwana. Amaaso ge gaali ng'olulimi lw'omuliro, ne ku mutwe gwe kwaliko engule nnyingi, era yalina erinnya eriwandiikiddwa, eritatereerwa muntu yenna wabula ye yekka. Era yali ayambadde olugoye olunyikiddwa mu musaayi, n'erinnya lye liyitibwa, Kigambo kya Katonda. N'amagye ag'omu ggulu ne gamugoberera ku mbalaasi enjeru, nga bambadde bafuta enjeru ennungi.

Baana bange, ebiseera byammwe eby'omu maaso bya kitiibwa ekitapimika, era temuliba na kyetaago nate, kubanga mwenyumirizza gye ndi, era temwetaaga kugezesebwa kulala; kale lwaki mwandyetaaze okuyita mu kibonyoobonyo kya Yakobo? Kubanga ekigendererwa kyakyo kwe kulokola Yakobo, kubanga abasigaddewo balilokolebwa. Naye mukakase nti abo abawakanya okulabika kwange mu kufuba kwabwe okutaliimu nsa okusaanyaawo essuubi lyammwe ery'amazima, ye abasumba abo abakyamye, baliyita mu kibonyoobonyo kya Yakobo, era balilokolebwa bwe balikoowoola erinnya lyange ne babeera abeesigwa okutuusa ku nkomerero. Naye tebaliba na kifo ku mmeeza yange, kubanga okwegaana okulabika kwange kwe kwegaana nze, Omugole Omwami, bwe njija okujja Omugole wange, era ekyo kye nnaatera okukola.

Munoonye okwaka kw'okulabika kwange! Muwulirize, kubanga mulimpita eddoboozi lyange n'okuwuuba kw'ekkondeere okw'amaanyi okw'ekkondeere erisembayo; kubanga wadde ndi Mwana gw'Endiga gwa Katonda, njija ng'Empologoma ya Yuda, era obwakabaka bwa Setaani bwonna bukwatiddwa entiisa olwange, Kristo Yesu, kubanga ebiseera byabwe bituuse, era ennimiro z'omuliro zireeteddwa okubookya. Naye tekiribatuukako, kubanga mbategekedde empeera nnene n'obusika obunene, Omugole wange. Musome ebigambo byange mu kitabo ekinene eky'Okubikkulirwa, kubanga ndibalaga ekifaananyi eky'ebiseera byammwe eby'omu maaso: Era okwetooloola Entebe kwaliko entebe amakumi abiri mu nnya, ne ku ntebe ne ndaba Abakadde amakumi abiri mu bana nga batudde, nga bambadde engoye enjeru, ne ku mitwe gyabwe kwaliko engule eza zaabu. (ate nate) Era ebisolo ebyo bwe biwa ekitiibwa, n'ettendo, n'okwebaza oyo atudde ku Ntebe, abeerawo emirembe n'emirembe, Abakadde amakumi abiri mu bana bavuunama mu maaso g'oyo atudde ku Ntebe, ne basinza oyo abeerawo emirembe n'emirembe, ne basuula engule zaabwe mu maaso g'Entebe, nga bagamba nti, Osanidde, Ai Mukama waffe, okuweebwa ekitiibwa, n'ettendo, n'amaanyi: kubanga ggwe watonda ebintu byonna, era olw'okusanyuka kwo bye biri, era byatondebwa.

Baana bange, temutya biseera byammwe eby'omu maaso, naye muntunuulire mu kusuubira okungi, kubanga mu bbanga ttono nnyo muliba mu ggulu nga musuula engule zammwe mu maaso g'entebe ya Katonda asinga obukulu!

Nze mulamuzi omutuukirivu era mu bbanga ttono mulifuna empeera nnene ku Bema yange.

Nze Mugole Omwami eri omugole wange.

Njija kunyaga Omugole wange!

Nze Mpologoma y'Ekika kya Yuda era ensi eneetera okutegeera.

Nze oyo aweereddwa obukulu mu ggulu ne ku nsi, kubanga erinnya lyange lisinga amannya gonna okuggyako Yakuwa.

Nze Mwana wa Katonda eyajja mu mubiri olw'ebibi by'abantu, era nazuukizibwa ku lunaku olw'okusatu ne ntwalibwa mu ggulu.

Nze oyo alifuga mu butuukirivu ku nsi, n'Omugole wange alifuga nange.

Nze oyo eyabayita mu kitiibwa n'obulungi, kale temuggwaamu maanyi!

Nze ndi.
