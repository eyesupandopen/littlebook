Omujulirwa Omulamba Bbiri: Ekkanisa
Mu Biwandiiko bya Pawulo Omutume
May 02, 2024

Nja Kuwonya Okukkiriza Kwo, bw’atyo bw’ayogera Mukama Omukulu!

Laba! Nze, nze kennyini, Kristo Yesu, Mukama Omulokozi w'ensi eyamala okuzuukira, nnina ekibuuzo kye nkubuuza, ggwe omugole wange. Onoonziramu mu mazima? Onoosobola okumpa eky'okuddamu ekisaanira okuwandiikibwa mu kitabo ky'amazima? Kale, nja kubabuuza: Omwana w'omuntu bw'alijja, mulowooza, alissanga okukkiriza ku nsi? Mazima ddala kino kibuuzo kya njogerayanjule, naye singa wandiyimiridde ku kuyita kwange n'onziramu, eky'okuddamu kyo kyandibadde ki? Wandimpangiisizza ku kukkiriza kwo n'engeri gye kuli okw'ekitiibwa? Wandinnyumizza ku birowoozo byo ebirungi, bye mulowooza mu busiru nti mufuga ensi n'ebirowoozo byammwe? Temutegeera nti n'enjigiriza ezisinga obwangu mu kukkiriza kwange, ddala okukkiriza kwa Yesu Kristo, n'enjigiriza ezo ezisingayo obwangu zaakyusibwa ne zifuulibwa obulimba? Naye mmwe mu busiru mugamba nti: "O, Katonda teyandikkirizza ekyo kubaawo!" Kwe nkubuuza nti, omanyi Katonda kutuuka wa? Ggwe omuwi we w'amagezi? Onooyimirira n'olagira Yakuwa? Onooyimirira n'ondagira nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, okukola by'oyagala? Naye bangi ku mmwe mu busiru mugezaako okukola ekyo, kubanga mwalimbibwa mu ngeri ey'entiisa ennyo.

Mwakkiriza bannabbi ab'obulimba, ddala omuceere ogwo Setaani gwe yasiga mu kkanisa ze ez'obulimba (ezitali zange), bannabbi abo bennyini ab'obulimba muganda wange Yuda be yayogerako bwe naluŋŋamya ebigambo byange mu mutima gwe era bwe nnyeenyeenya omwoyo gwe munda mu ye mu kulungamizibwa okutukuvu nti: Zibasanze, kubanga baatambulira mu kkubo lya Kayini, ne baddukira n'omululu mu kusoba kwa Balamu olw'empeera, ne bazikirira mu kuwakana kwa Koola. Abo ab'obulimba abo batambulira mu maaso gammwe lwatu, era bangi ku mmwe mubagoberera, nga mulowooza nti mugoberera nze, kubanga bali ba kikumpanya, era bagezaako okukoppa eddoboozi lyange abataamanyi mu ndiga zange balyoke babagoberere okubayingiza mu kusoba okungi, mwe bali. Kubanga Yuda kiki ekirala kye yayogera ku abo abatagasa? Abo be bbala mu mbaga zammwe ez'okwagala, bwe baliira awamu nammwe, nga beeriisa awatali kutya: beebire ebitatonnyesa, ebitwalibwa empewo, emiti egibala byayo ebikaze, egitaliiko bibala, emife emirundi ebiri, egisimbuddwa n'emiramwa. Amayengo g'ennyanja agasattira, agavaamu ensonyi zaago zokka, emmunyeenye ezitambulatambula, abaterekerwa ekizikiza ekikutte ennyo ennaku zonna.

Nkimanyi nti ekikumpanya kisinga obunywamu eri abamu ku mmwe, kubanga abamu ku mmwe temummanyi bulungi naye mufuba okusinza Yakuwa n'okungoberera nze, Mukama wammwe Yesu Kristo, okuyita mu njigiriza z'enjiri y'obugagga. Temusobanga: Enjiri y'obugagga bulimba, era sseewaayo bwe ntyo ng'Omwana gw'endiga gwammwe ogwa Pasika mulyoke mwekuŋŋaanyize obugagga: Kubanga okwagala ssente kye kikolo ky'ebibi byonna: abamu bwe baakwegomba, ne bakyama okuva mu kukkiriza, ne beefumita n'ennaku nnyingi. Abangi ku mmwe mwagoberera enjiri emu ku nnyingi ez'obukumpanya, naye tewali njiri ndala. Abangi ku mmwe mwagoberera omu ku Bakristo ab'obukumpanya abangi, naye tewali Kristo mulala wabula nze, Yeshua HaMashiach. Mbagamba lwatu nti, mwalimbibwa, era bangi ku mmwe mwawaanyisizzaamu okukkiriza okw'amazima okwa Yesu Kristo n'okukkiriza okw'obulimba. Okukkiriza okw'obulimba n'okukkiriza okusiiwuula, ekigambo okukkiriza (saabawa Kigambo kya Kukkiriza eky'amazima?) nga tekyategeeza kwesiga, kukkiriza, na bunywevu eri Yakuwa, Kitammwe, nange Yesu Kristo Mukama wammwe, naye kyakyusibwa ne kifuulibwa eby'obwereere eby'olubatu n'enfumo n'ebirowoozo eby'obufumu. Mwaleka abatagasa ne babakyaza, era amakanisa gammwe gaayingirirwa abasatani, ddala omuceere omubi gwe yabasigamu.

Eby'olubatu bino byonna biva ku kulowooza ebirungi n'ebibi, era mu busiru mulowooza nti kyonna kye mufumiitirizaako ebbanga eddene kijja kutuukirira, nga Katonda yateeka amaanyi ge n'okuganja kwe ku abo abalina okukkiriza okusinga obulimba, mwe mulagira ebintu n'ebirowoozo byammwe. Temutegeera nti bonna abakola eby'obwereere ebyo bakwatibwa ne bategebwa ne basibibwa eri okwegomba kwabwe n'obulimba buno, ddala basibibwa ku kulemererwa, kubanga mu butamanya mugoberera amakubo ga Setaani? Tegeeranga nti bamalayika abagwa, yee, baSetaani n'abalabirizi abaabeegattako, be baayigiriza abantu obulogo, okusaawa emiramwa, eddogo, n'obusawo; era abasajja ababi bangi baayingirira ekkanisa yange ey'e Lawodikiya ne batwala bangi ng'omunyago abakkiriza obulimba bwabwe.

Kino kyaliwo kubanga bangi mu kkanisa yange ey'e Lawodikiya bagaana okuyimirira n'okulwana mu butuukirivu mulyoke muwangule obubi n'obulungi! Ka mbayambe okutegeera amazima. Tewali n'omu ku mmwe alina amaanyi n'obusobozi ku bubwe, kubanga mu bunafu bwammwe mwe mutegeerera ddala obutagasa bw'okufuba kwammwe mwekka. Olwo, emirundi mingi, mutegeera nti bwe mwesiga nze, Kristo Yesu, ne mugoberera ebiragiro byange mu kkubo lye mbalonzezza, mwesiga nze n'amaanyi gange era mulina okukkiriza okujjuvu mu nze. Kubanga nja kulaga mmwenna mu ngeri yange, bwe musigala nga muwombeefu eri eddoboozi lyange ne mukyuka bwe mbagamba, nti mu kufuba kwammwe nammwe n'amaanyi gammwe temuli kintu kyonna wabula musenyu mu ddungu, ogubuze n'embuyaga, era ssaafa kununula musenyu wabula ettaka eddemyu erya Adamu, kubanga nze Adamu ow'oluvannyuma, era ndi mwoyo oguwa obulamu. Naye, mu nze Kristo Yesu, mulina amaanyi era temuyinzika, kubanga mulina eby'obugagga bino mu bibya by'ebbumba, era nja kuwa eby'amagero bingi, obubonero, eby'ewuunyo n'ebiringa ebyo mu mikono gyammwe bwe muba n'okukkiriza okw'enkolagana mu nze, Yesu Kristo, mu kifo ky'okwekkiririzaamu mwekka. Mbasaba munkkirize okubakulembera, muleke obulimba bw'abo abagwa, kubanga olw'ebikolwa byabwe obusungu bungi buterekebwa ku bo ku lunaku olw'obusungu, okutuusa enkomerero yaabwe lw'eririirwa mu muliro n'ekibiriiti ekyaka.

Tegeeranga engeri obulimba buno bwe bwatuukirizibwamu, kubanga mu myaka gya Yubiri egiyise waaliwo okumanyisibwa okw'obulimba, abo ab'omu kitundu kyammwe be muyita abakulu ne bagaana Katonda ne beewaayo eri amagezi gaabwe agataagasa. Baalekaana ne bafuula amagezi ekifaananyi mu kiseera ky'oluyombo lwabwe; temumanyi byafaayo? Eby'obugaayu bino ebitagasa ebyalimu amagezi gaabwe agoonoonefu gaagaana Katonda ne byanjula obulimba nti Katonda mulabi obulabi ataliiko ky'akola atafaayo nnyo ku bantu naye agaba byonna ng'ali wala era akkiriza abantu okubyetegereza, ng'okuganja kwa Katonda kugenda ku mulogo asinga amaanyi mu bo. Oluvannyuma, emyoyo mingi egitanywera, ddala omuceere gwa Setaani, ne giyingirira ekkanisa yange ey'e Lawodikiya okutuukiriza obulimba bwago mu biseera ebigere era ebituufu. Baabayingiriramu n'ebigambo ebirungi n'emboozi ennungi ne bayigiriza amaanyi g'ebirowoozo eby'obufumu eri Omugole wange, era bangi mu Mubiri gwange ne babagoberera n'omululu, kubanga omululu gwabwe gwakumibwa.

Abaana bano ababi aba Setaani baatabulamu obulogo mu njigiriza zange mu nkukutu ne batuuka okusiikuula Ekigambo kyange ekitukuvu n'ekizimbulukusa, wabula Ekigambo kyange tekiyinza kusiikuulibwa nfumo zaabwe, kubanga obulimba bwabwe buyimiridde ku bulimba. Temutegeera nti ekizimbulukusa ekitono kizimbulukusa ekibumbe kyonna? Temutegeera nti ekizimbulukusa kya Kerode n'ekizimbulukusa ky'Abafalisaayo si ze zokka engeri z'okwonooneka? Kubanga mwalogebwa n'ekizimbulukusa ky'obulogo bwa Kikristo! N'olwekyo, MWENENYE! Kubanga ntya bye nnagamba Abakkolinso mu mukono gwa Pawulo Omutume? Kale, mweggyemu ekizimbulukusa ekikadde, mulyoke mubeere ekibumbe ekipya, nga bwe muli abatali bazimbulukuse. Kubanga ne Kristo Pasika waffe yaweebwayo. Wuliriza Ebigambo byange! Kale ensolo zino ez'omu nsiko, eby'obugaayu bino ebitagasa, ddala omuceere ogwo ogw'obutwa ne gubuulira ne gutandikawo obubondo bwabwo obw'obukristaayo obw'obulimba, ebidongolero ddala, ne bibba bangi ku abo abaali babagoberera okuva mu Kkanisa ey'amazima, gye nfaayo ku Mugole wange mu kwagala n'ekisa n'okusaasira, ne bibaggya ku nze ne bibatwala mu nnyumba ez'okusinzizaamu ez'obulimba eziweereddwayo eri Baali mu kyama, kubanga eggwanga lyammwe ye Babulooni Ey'ekyama!

Nga kino tekimala, abalogo bangi ab'amaanyi n'abagagga baasasaanya ssente nnyingi ne bafuba nnyo okussa obulimba bwabwe mu Bayibuli ez'obulimba. Baaleeta ebiwandiiko by'e Alexandria, eby'Abagnostiki, ne kireetera Ekkanisa yange okuba n'ebirowoozo bibiri okuva ku kigendererwa eky'omwoyo ogumu eky'ebiwandiiko bye nnakuumira okuva e Antiyokiya, ne Bayibuli ezimu ez'okwekebejjeramu obulimba buno mwe bwageragerwamu. Ebitabo bino oluvannyuma byayingizibwa mu masomero gammwe agoonoonese ne bifuuka Bayibuli ez'okuyigiramu kw'abo bannabbi ab'obulimba ababuulidde obulimba okuva olwo, era bajja kusasula mu bujjuvu. Okuva mu kiseera ekyo, ensi zammwe zijjudde amakanisa ag'obulimba agakulemberwa emisege egirulunkanira, era naye endiga zange nnyingi nnyo zikyasobya ne zilowooza nti ziyinza okubeera n'emisege. Amakanisa gammwe ag'obulimba nnyumba za kuvvoola; nnyumba za kusanyusa mwe bayimbira Setaani ennyimba z'okutendereza mu maaso gammwe, nammwe ne muba temulina kusosola kwa mwoyo kulaba. Waliwo bakama bangi mu bwakabaka bwa Setaani, era bamuyimbira nti Mukama, Mukama, wabula bwe balingamba nti Mukama, Mukama, ndigamba nti, Munveeko, mmwe abakozi b'obutali butuukirivu, kubanga ssaabamanya.

Okvongera okwonoona embeera, baatwala enjiri ey'obugagga ey'okuvvoola ne bagikyusa nate ne bayingizaamu sayikologiya w'okweyamba, era ki kye nnayogera ku nsonga ng'ezo: Mwekuume omuntu yenna aleme okubaggyako ng'abanyaze olw'amagezi g'ensi n'obulimba obulimbye, olw'obulombolombo bw'abantu n'amateeka ag'ensi, so si nga Kristo. Amakanisa gano ag'obulimba goonoonese, tegaliiko kigasa kigagasa, era mangi gazimbibwa abazimbi ababi abatali bange, nze ejjinja erisima essuubi, era ebizimbe byabwe byonna bijja kugwa mu musenyu ogukyukakyuka. Bano bazimbi bavvoola era babazzi ba mayinja ab'obulimba n'abakabi b'amataffaali ab'obulimba abagaanyi ejjinja erisima essuubi, so tebalina mazima mu bo! Baaniriza n'obubonero bwabwe mu ezo ze muyita ennyumba z'okusinzizaamu, mwe babaduulira lwatu. Wuliriza Olugero lwe nkulina, kubanga lwa mazima era lujja kuleeta okumanyisibwa okw'amazima mu myoyo gyammwe: Omusajja omubi, omuntu omukola ebibi, atambula n'akamwa akakyamu. Anyenya amaaso ge, ayogera n'ebigere bye, ayigiriza n'engalo ze.

Obukyamu buli mu mutima gwe, alowooza obubi bulijjo, asiga enjawukana. Bwe batyo bwe bali era bwe bakola mu maaso gammwe, naye temuyinza kulaba, kubanga muzibye amaaso mu njigiriza zaabwe ez'obulimba n'amakubo amakyamu.

Baana bange, muve mu bo mweyawule; kubanga Kristo alina nkolagana ki ne Beliyali. Temuba na nkolagana na bikolwa bya kizikiza ebitabala, era mwawukane ddala ku byo. Kubanga mmwe, ekkanisa yange ey'e Lawodikiya, nammwe muli ba luguudo era temulina nkizo, era ago si ge makubo gange n'akatono, kubanga nnagoba abawaanyisa ssente mu Yeekaalu mu kunyiikira kwange eri Yakuwa. Mudde eri nze, Yesu Kristo, omusumba ow'amazima era omusawo w'emyoyo gyammwe, muyige ku nze. Kubanga okwatula okulungi n'ebirowoozo ebirungi ebitagasa si byange, wabula bulimba obubaboggolerwa abatume ab'obulimba, ddala abaweereza ba Setaani abefuula abaweereza b'obutuukirivu. Mundabire, baana bange abato, kubanga obudde buweddeyo, nammwe mu butamanya mulowooza nti mulina obudde bwonna mu nsi, so mulina eddakiika ntono nnyo okwenenya n'okudda eri nze mu bwetoowaze ndyoke nteeketeeke ekifo gye muli ku mmeeza yange era ndyoke nzigulewo oluggi mu ggulu mulyoke muyingire mu ssanyu lya Mukama wammwe!

Kubanga bangi ku mmwe mulowooza nti mujja kukuŋŋaanyizibwa gye ndi bwe mubeera mu bulimba buno era bwe mulya obucaafu bannabbi ab'obulimba bwe babaliisa, mu kuvvoola mu linnya lyange. Mumanye nti ssaabatuma era ebigambo byabwe si byange, era bwe mutabavangako, mujja kulina okweyeraga gye ndi mu kugezesebwa n'okukemebwa okungi mu kiseera ky'okubonaabona kwa Yakobo, ekijjidde ensi yonna era ekiri kumpi okusumululwa! Muntunuulire mu mwoyo gwammwe; mwekwate, kubanga muyinza okuwulira obulumi bw'okuzaala mu mwoyo gwammwe, kubanga mbayita okudda eka era Omugole wange anaatera okuzaalibwa. Laba, mbalekera ebbaluwa gye nnabawandiikira, mmwe ab'e Lawodikiya, kubanga mu bigambo bino mulimu okumulisa ekkubo ly'okudda gye ndi; era bwe munaawuliriza, nja kubazzaawo empeera yammwe, era mujja kusula nange ku mmeeza yange mu ggulu gye ntegekedde Omugole wange.

Era ow'ekibiina eky'omu Lawodikiya omuwandiikire nti: Bw'atyo bw'ayogera (Amiina) omujulirwa omwesigwa ow'amazima, olubereberye lw'ebitonde bya Katonda. Mmanyi ebikolwa byo, nti toli munnyogovu so toli mwokye. Nandiyagadde obeere munnyogovu oba mwokye. Kale, kubanga oli wakati wa byombiriri, so toli munnyogovu so toli mwokye. Nja kukusesema mu kamwa kange: kubanga oli mugagga, era oyongeddwako eby'obugagga, so tewali ky'okwetaaga, so tomanyi ng'oli munaku era ow'okusaasirwa, omwavu, omuzibe w'amaaso era ali bwereere. Nkugamba ogule gye ndi zaabu eyakemebwa mu muliro, olyoke obeere omugagga, n'engoye enjeru, olyoke oyambale, obwereere bwo obw'ensonyi buleme okulabika, era osiige amaaso go eddagala ly'amaaso, olyoke olabe. Abo bonna be njagala, mbanenya era mbonereza. Kale, nyiikira, wenenye. Laba, nnyimiridde ku luggi era nkonkona. Omuntu yenna bw'awulira eddoboozi lyange n'aggulawo oluggi, ndiyingira gy'ali, era ndisula naye, naye nange. Oyo awangula ndimuwa okutuula nange ku ntebe yange, nga nange bwe nnawangula ne ntuula ne Kitange ku ntebe ye.

Laba!

Nze atambulira wakati mu bikkondo by'ettabaaza omusanvu, kale mudde gye ndi, nneme okuggyawo ekikondo kyo.

Nze musumba mulungi eyeewaayo ku lw'ekisibo.

Nze ajja okufuga ensi okuva ku ntebe ya Dawudi era n'akuba amawanga n'omuggo ogw'ekyuma.

Nze asaanira okutwala ekitabo n'okumenya obubonero.

Nze alagira malayika ayambuka okuva ebuvanjuba, ng'alina akabonero ka Katonda omulamu, kubanga bajja kuteekako akabonero ku 144,000 bange, ddala abaweereza ba Katonda omulamu.

Nze ajja okuyimirira ku lusozi Sayuuni n'abange 144,000, era bajja kuyimba oluyimba olupya olutayinzika kuyigibwa muntu yenna.

Nze Mukama wa bakama era Kabaka wa bakabaka, era njija kulwana ne bakabaka b'ensi, kubanga ekiseera kyabwe kituuse.

Nze ajja okujja okutwala Omugole wange, kubanga okulabika kwange kuli kumpi. Mwetegeke, ggwe Omugole wange!
