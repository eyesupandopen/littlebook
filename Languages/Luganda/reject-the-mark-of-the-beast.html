Omujulirwa Omulamba: Isirayiri
Okukaawa n'Okuwooma
Jjulaayi 27, 2024

Mugaane Akabonero k'Ensolo!

Muwulire eddoboozi ly'amagezi, mufukamire, era muwulire eddoboozi eddungi erya Yeshua HaMashiach, kubanga nze Mpologoma ey'omu kika kya Yuda, era nze Kabaka wa Bakabaka era Mukama wa Bakama! El Shaddai, ye Katonda Omuyinza w'ebintu byonna, yansitula nnyo n'ampa erinnya erisinga amannya gonna. Buli vviivi erinnyo lya Yesu lifukamireko, eby'omu ggulu, n'eby'oku nsi, n'ebiri wansi w'ensi: Era buli lulimi lwatule nti Yesu Kristo ye Mukama, olw'ekitiibwa kya Kitaffe w'ebitangaala, ye Yahweh, Katonda ataggwaawo era ow'emirembe n'emirembe, kubanga tewali afaanana nga Kitaffe!

Muwulire eddoboozi lya Mukama wammwe era mutegeere ebintu ebiriwo kati n'ebigenda okubaawo mu bbanga ttono, kubanga laba nze ayogera ku bintu ebigenda okujja nga bimaze okubaawo, kubanga ddala Mukama wammwe omukulu, ye nze Kristo Yesu asinga byonna, talikola kintu kyonna, okuggyako nga abikkulidde ebyama bye eri abaddu be Bannabbi. Lwaki muwuliriza amaloboozi amangi ag'enjawulo, era lwaki muwalira wakati w'ebilowoozo bibiri? Saagamba nti bannabbi bange boogera n'eddoboozi limu era tebakyukirire bigambo byange bitukuvu? Kubanga nze kigambo ekyufuuka omubiri era mbeera mu mmwe olw'omwoyo gwange omutukuvu oguzaalibwa mu mmwe emirembe n'emirembe, kubanga buli mmeeme ezaalibwa obuggya eteekebwako akabonero n'omwoyo omutukuvu ogw'ekisuubizo oguli ensigo yange etevunda era akabonero ka Katonda Omujololo tekisobola kumenyeka. Era olunaku lw'okununulibwa kw'abantu be yagula luli kumpi era okununulibwa kwammwe okujjuvu kuli wano.

Bw'atyo bw'agamba Mukama ow'Eggye, temuwuliranga bigambo bya bannabbi ababalagulira, ababayigiriza ebitaliimu: boogera okulaba okw'omutima gwabwe, so si okuva mu kamwa ka Mukama. Mujje gye ndi abaana bange kubanga okufukibwako amafuta kwe mwafuna okuva gye ndi kubeera mu mmwe: era temwetaaga muntu yenna kubayigiriza: Naye, nga okufukibwako amafuta okwo bwe kubayigiriza ku bintu byonna, era nga kwa mazima, so si kwa bulimba: era nga bwe kwabayigiriza, bwe mutyo mujja kubeera mu nze. Abaana bange mubeere mu nze era mugerageranye ebigambo by'omuntu yenna agamba nti anjogerera n'ebyawandiikibwa era munsabe mbikakase mu mwoyo gwammwe, oboolyawo mwawulira eddoboozi lyange kubanga abasinga ku mmwe muggala amatu, era abasinga ku mmwe mwagala ebigambo eby'obulimba ebisisimula amatu gammwe mulyoke mulabike ng'ab'amagezi naye mu kifo ky'ekyo mugoberera ebigambo eby'obulimba eby'abasirusiru ab'amalala.

Laba! Njogera ku biseera by'okubonaabona kwa Yakobo ebiteekateese okusaasaana mu nsi yonna. Kubanga omusango gutandise dda era tegusobola kuyimirizibwa, kubanga Yahweh ky'alangirira tewali asobola kukikyusa! Nakaabye amaziga mangi nnyo olw'omusango gwa Babulooni w'Ebyama era mbadde ntuma bannabbi bange okumala emyaka mingi nnyo kati era ntumye n'abagwira okuva mu nsi engwira okutegeeza n'okulagula ku misango egyali gijja naye nga kati gituuse.

Kubanga bwe kityo bwe kiriba bwe kityo bwe kyawandiikibwa: Amayembe ekkumi ge walabye ku nsolo, ago galikyawa omwenzi, era galimufuula matongo, era wa njuba, era galirya ennyama ye, ne gamwokya omuliro. Kubanga Katonda yateeka mu mitima gyabwe okutuukiriza okwagala kwe, n'okukkiriziganya, n'okuwa ensolo obwakabaka bwabwe, okutuusa ebigambo bya Katonda lwe birituukirizibwa. N'omukazi, gwe walabye, kye kibuga ekyo ekinene ekifuga bakabaka b'ensi.

Muwulire ebigambo byange, omusango gwa Babulooni ow'Ekyama gutandise dda. Amayembe ekkumi gateesezza ekirumbagano kyago eky'ekibwatukira era ekirumbagano kyago kiri kumpi era kiri ku luggi lwammwe. Kubanga n'amawulire gammwe geegomba okubasendasenda okwebaka era bangi batuula mu kizikiza nga tebalaba mazima nga bagamba nti: Ntuula nga Nnabagereka so siri nnamwandu era siriraba kukungubaga. Olunaku lw'enkya luliba nga luno, kubanga olwa leero luli nga lwa jjo. Nga bw'eyeegulumiza, n'abeera mu ssanyu, bw'atyo bwe mumuwe okubonyaabonyezebwa n'ennaku: Olw'ekyo ebibonoobono bye birijja mu lunaku lumu, okufa, n'ennaku, n'enjala, n'ayokebwa omuliro: kubanga Yahweh amusalira omusango, ye Katonda Omuyinza w'ebintu byonna so tewali asinga era tewali kiyinza kuyimiriza mukono gwa El Shaddai ogw'amaanyi!

N'olwekyo enjuba ejja kuseerera ku lunaku lw'akabi lwe mutasuubira, era olunaku olwo luli kumpi okusinga bwe mulowooza. Era olunaku olwo lujja kuleeta okuzikirizibwa okw'olubeerera ku Babulooni w'Ebyama, kubanga alina omugabo gw'omusango ogubiri ogulangiriddwa ku ye. Ebikolobero bye n'obwenzi bwe bingi era ekibonerezo kye tekirwawo n'ekibonerezo ekinene kirigwa ku abo abateeyongeddeyo okwonoona nga Ekkanisa yange ey'obwesigwa n'omugole wange ow'obwesigwa bwe balikung'aanyizibwa gye ndi mu bbanga mu bire era balokolebwe okuva mu busungu, kye kibonyoobonyo ekinene eky'esabbiiti ya Danyeri ey'amakumi musanvu. Babulooni Omunene Agudde, Agudde! Muwulire ebigambo byange kubanga njogera ng'ekimaze okubaawo kubanga tewali kijja kukyusa, kulwisa, kuziyiza, wadde okuyimiriza emisango gya Yahweh okugwa ku nsi.

Mu ssaawa emu alyokebwa omuliro, ebibuga bye ebimu biryaka, ebibuga bye ebimu biryonooneka n'amataba ag'amaanyi, eminaala gye girigwa ne gimenyekamenyeka, entindo ze zirigwa, n'okukankana okungi kulibaawo. Ensi eriyulikamu n'amaanyi n'ettaka lingi liribikkibwako amazzi ag'amaanyi. Engoye ze ziribikkibwa okuva ebuvanjuba n'ebugwanjuba mu mayengo amanene n'amayinja ag'amayinja ag'amayinja n'okukankana okw'amaanyi. Ekirisigalawo kiriba ddwaniro era taliddamu kukkirira, kubanga eggwanga lino eririmu amawanga n'ebibuga bingi teririddayo kukkirira n'okugwa kwe kuliba kunene! Kubanga ensi ya Mukama n'okujjuza kwayo, n'ebibi byonna ebiva mu butonde bwe ebibi biriggibwawo emirembe n'emirembe mu mawanga mu ssabbiiti ya Danyeri ey'amakumi abiri n'ebiri, kubanga bwe ntyo bwe ndirongoosa ensi mu busungu bwange. Nze, Kristo Yesu, Empologoma ey'omu kika kya Yuda, ntegeezezza ebigambo bino, n'okweteekerateekera okutuukirizibwa kwabyo kutandise dda era birituukirizibwa mangu mu ssaawa emu.

N'olwekyo olutalo lujja kubalukawo mu nsi yonna ng'ekimu ku kirumbagano eky'ekibwatukira ku Babulooni w'Ekyama, kubanga kino kitegekeddwa era kitegekeddwa okumala emyaka mingi kati, kubanga ekyama ky'obujeemu kibadde kikola okuva mu nnaku ez'edda era kirijja kufaayo ddala okutuuka obulungi singa omuzibi ajjibwawo ng'abeesigwa bange batwalibwa mu ggulu. Olutalo lujja kugwa ku nsi yonna era lujja kujja ku bantu bonna n'amawanga gonna, kubanga luno lwe lutalo olw'okusatu, ng'abagwi banoonya okuzikiriza entegeka z'ensi enkadde ne bazizzaawo entegeka yaabwe empya, ye ntegeka y'Omuzira. Ensolo evudde mu nnyanja n'ensolo evudde ku lukalu zirigolokokera mu busungu obungi, era zirirwana n'abatukuvu, kubanga zibaweereddwa okubawangula abatukuvu. Bwe kityo kiriba nti bonna abataganza bulamu bwabwe ne bakoowoola erinnya lya Mukama okutuusa ku nkomerero balilokolebwa mu ssanyu eritagambika okutuuka mu butayuza bwa butayuza. Era nze Yesu Omunazaaleesi naleka ekyokulabirako eky'obuwulize eky'olubeerera okutuusa ku kufa, kubanga obuwulize bwange eri Yahweh bwali kufa ku musaalaba nga mbonyaabonyezebwa era ne nsasulira ebibi by'abantu ng'omwana gw'endiga gwa Katonda era laba nzija ng'Empologoma ey'omu kika kya Yuda so siriyimirizibwa wadde okuziyizibwa!

N'olwekyo bannabbi bange ababiri abali emiti emizeyituuni ebiri n'ebikondo by'ettaala ebibiri abayimiridde mu maaso ga Katonda w'ensi baliragulira okumala emyezi 42 nga bambadde ebibukutu, kubanga baatwalibwa mu ggulu mu biseera by'edda. Era omuntu yenna bw'alibatuusaako akabi, omuliro guva mu kamwa kaabwe, ne gukozonga abalabe baabwe: era omuntu yenna bw'alibatuusaako akabi, ateekwa okuttibwa bw'atyo. Bano balina obuyinza okuggala eggulu, ne bayimiriza enkuba mu nnaku ez'obunnabbi bwabwe: era balina obuyinza ku mazzi okugafuula omusaayi, n'okukuba ensi n'ebibonoobono byonna, emirundi gyonna gye baliba baagala.

Obujulizi bwabwe bwa lubeerera, era balirangirira enjiri ey'olubeerera, era balyogera ku nteekateeka za Yahweh ez'ebiseera byonna, era balinjogerera nze Kristo Yesu, era ebyama bingi bye balibikkula era balikyuusa emyoyo gy'abantu bangi eri Kitaffe w'Ebitangaala atalina nkyukakyuka wadde ekisiikirize eky'okukyuka. Balimpuliriza nze Yeshua HaMashiach kubanga nze Yahweh gwe yantuma e Isirayiri okutuukiriza ebisuubizo bye yakola eri bajjajjaabwe era nze Masiya ow'amazima kaakano n'emirembe n'emirembe era obwakabaka bwange tebuliggwaawo! Kubanga biseera ki ebyaliwo Yahweh we yeerekera nga talina mujulirwa? Tewabangawo kiseera Yahweh ky'asigadde nga talina mujulirwa, naabo bonna abalondebwa okufuna obulokozi obw'olubeerera bajja kulokolebwa ne bayingira mu butayuza obw'olubeerera n'essanyu ery'olubeerera, kubanga okutuukirizibwa kw'ebintu byonna kutandise dda.

N'olwekyo ensolo evudde mu nnyanja erigolokokera wamu n'ensolo evudde ku lukalu, ye nnabbi ow'obulimba. Era alyasamya akamwa ke ng'ayogera ebivume ebinene ebivvoola Yahweh okuvuma erinnya lye n'eweema ye n'abo abali mu ggulu. Era kirimuweebwa okulwana n'abatukuvu n'okubawangula n'obuyinza bulimuweebwa okufuga buli kika, n'olulimi, n'eggwanga kubanga alikulembera ensi. Ensolo evudde ku lukalu nayo erifuga ettaka nga Moshiach waabwe ow'obulimba era eriba omuzimbi wa yeekaalu yaabwe. Ensolo evudde mu nnyanja erifuga okusinziira ku mateeka gaabwe amavume aga Noahide nga banoonya okumalawo ekintu kyonna ekikwatagana ne Katonda ow'amazima n'omwana we omu yekka Yesu Kristo okuva ku maaso g'ensi.

Ensolo evudde ku lukalu ereetera ensi okusinza ensolo, eyawona ekiwundu kye eky'okufa. Era alikola eby'amagero ebinene okulimba abo abali ku nsi, kubanga kirimukkirizibwa okukola obubonero bungi obw'obulimba n'ebyamagero, era alireeta omuliro okugwa ku nsi mu maaso g'abantu era alireetera abantu bonna okubewuunya. Naye mulabe, era yeegulumiza ku byonna ebiyitibwa Katonda, oba ebisinzibwa: n'okutuuka okutuula nga Katonda mu Yekaalu ya Katonda, ng'eyeeraga nga ye Katonda.

Era alikola ekifaananyi ky'ensolo era alireeta bonna okusinza ekifaananyi ky'alikola. Era kirimukkirizibwa okuwa omwoyo ekifaananyi ky'ensolo ekifaananyi ky'ensolo kiryoke kyogere era kirireeta bonna abatayagala kusinza kifaananyi kya nsolo battibwe. Era alikola bonna, abagagga n'abaavu, ab'eddembe n'abaddu, okufuna akabonero ke ku mukono gwabwe ogwa ddyo oba ku byenyi byabwe.

N'olwekyo ndiramu okugamba abo abali ku nsi bonna bawulire:

BW'ABA WOOLI OMUNTU ASINZA ENSOLO N'EKIFAANANYI KYAYO, N'AKKIRIZA AKABONERO KAYO KU KYENYI KYE, OBA MU NGALA ZE, OYO ALINYWA KU MVINYOOY'OBUSUNGU BWA KATONDA, EGABIDDWA NGA TEETABUDDWAMU MU KIKOMPE KY'OBUSUNGU BWE, ERA ALIBONYAABONYEZBWA N'OMULIRO N'EBBALUWE, MU MAASO G'ABAMALAYIKA ABATUKUVU, NE MU MAASO G'OMWANA GW'ENDIGA: N'OMUKKA GW'OKUBONYAABONYA KWABWE GUNYIIKA EMISANA N'EKIRO. SO TEBALINA KUWUMMULA MISANA NA KIRO, ABASINZA ENSOLO N'EKIFANANYI KYAYO, N'OMUNTU YENNA AKKIRIZA AKABONERO K'ERINNYA LYAYO.

Ensi eriwo kaakano teriddamu kubaawo, n'obwakabaka bw'omusota ogwaka terijja kusuulibwa ddala wansi, kubanga obwakabaka bwa Setaani butuuse ku nkomerero yaabwo, kubanga bali ng'abakozi ababi so si bakabaka. Era eno y'ensi abangi mwe balisanga, n'abo abaliwonawo mu kusaanawo okujja n'olutalo. Temutwala kabonero, abantu bange, kubanga balijja nga basuubiza emirembe, balijja nga basuubiza obukuumi, balibagamba nti byonna biri ku lw'obulungi bwammwe. Balitandikawo obulwadde bwabwe obuyitibwa obw'ekirwadde kubanga bategeese dda okukakasa ensi nti balwadde. Era buli kika kya kawumpuli n’endwadde n’ebiyiringiti biribuna kubanga bwe bityo ebibala by’omuvuzi w’embalaasi ajja era balyebagala!

Balibawa obulamu n'okuwonyezebwa okuva mu misango gya Katonda, so ng'ate omuntu yandisaanye okwenenya obubi bwe. Balibawa okuyingira mu nsi yaabwe okuva mu misango egy'entiisa eginaatera okumenyeka ku nsi. Kubanga nze Kristo Yesu ndibagamba nga tekinnabaawo mulyoke mumanye obwesimbu bw'ebigambo byange era mulyoke musobole okuyita mu mazzi ag'akabi abangi mwe balisanga, n'abo abalina obuvumu okukoowoola erinnya lyange okutuusa ku nkomerero, kubanga abo be basika b’obulokozi obutaggwaawo. Kubanga bonna abakoowoola Yesu Omunazaaleeti nga Mukama waabwe, n'abo bonna abakoowoola erinnya lyange ery'amaanyi ne bataganya bulamu bwabwe okutuusa ku nkomerero, balilokolebwa ne bafuna obulamu obutaggwaawo.

Temusomanga? Bano be abo abaava mu kibonyoobonyo ekinene, ne boza engoye zaabwe, ne bazeeza mu musaayi gw'Omwana gw'endiga. Kyebava babeera mu maaso g'entebe ya Katonda, ne bamuweereza emisana n'ekiro mu Yeekaalu ye: n'oyo atuula ku Ntebe alituula mu bo. Tebaliddayo kulumwa njala, so tebalirumwa nnyonta nate, so n'enjuba teribookya, newakubadde ebbugumu lyonna. Kubanga Omwana gw'endiga ali wakati mu ntebe, alibaliisa, era alibakulembera okubatwala eri enzizi ez'amazzi amalamu: ne Katonda alibasiimula amaziga gonna mu maaso gaabwe.

Naye, newankubadde ekibiina ky'ebibiina kirilokolebwa mu wiiki ya Danyeri ey'amakumi asatu era obulokozi bulijja eri Isiraeri kubanga ensigalira erimvunnamira nze Yeshua HaMashiach, (kubanga nze Masiya ow'amazima omwana gw'endiga wa Katonda mw'alireka Empologoma ey'Ekika kya Yuda, era byombi birifuuka kimu mu nze mu buwuliziganye obutaggwaawo, kubanga mu nze Kristo Yesu Empologoma erigalamira mu mirembe n'omwana gw'endiga.) mu nnaku, era mu ngeri eteri ya mugaso, ensi yonna eriwuniikirira era n'egoberera abafuzi bano ab'omu nsolo, kubanga baliragula abangi. N'obulogo bwabwe n'obulogo bwabwe, balisendasenda ensi okukkiriza obutwa bwabwe, era balitwa abagala ensi emirembe n'emirembe n'obulogo bwabwe, kubanga akabonero k'ensolo kalifaananyizibwa ng'eddagala. N’olulimi lw’omusota olulumwa sisitiimu yaabwe lwamala dda okutwalibwa ne lukeberebwa, era n’abantu bamaze dda okuteekebwa mu mbeera ey’okukkiriza akabonero kaabwe era n’okukola kwabwe okulwanyisa ensigo kwali kwa buwanguzi.

Era n'olwekyo sisitiimu yaabwe yeetegefu okukozesebwa, era bategeese obubonero bwabwe obw'embeera z'abantu era bonna abalibeera mu bibuga byabwe ne mu kitundu kyabwe balifugibwa. Kubanga balina okutwala akabonero k'ensolo okwetaba mu kibiina kyabwe ekya Setaani, n'obwakabaka bw'omusota. Abo abatwala akabonero balikyusibwa emirembe n'emirembe. Ensengekera zaabwe zennyini zirikyusibwa, kubanga balyetabula n'ensigo y'abantu: naye tebalikwatagana, nga bwe kiri nti ekyuma tekitabulwa na bumba. Akabonero kaabwe ak'ensolo, n'olulimi lw'omusota oluluma, kalizikiriza ddala bonna abatwala obutwa bwabwe era tewali kudduka.

N'abo bonna abatwala akabonero balikyusibwa emirembe n'emirembe, era balisanga nga bali kitundu kya sisitiimu yaabwe, era balisibibwa wamu n'ebikozesebwa mu tekinologiya era ebirowoozo byabwe tebirikyaba byabwe. Balifugibwa era baliba ng'abafu abatambula, baliba baddu ba lubeerera eri emmeeme y'omuzinga gwa sisitiimu yaabwe, n'eya tekinologiya waabwe kubanga balisibibwa wamu ne yintaneeti y'ebintu ebinaabaawo. Balifiirwa okwagala kwabwe, era tebaliba na nneewulira era ekintu kyonna ekya Katonda kirisangulibwawo mu bo emirembe n'emirembe kubanga balyekyusa ne babeera mu mbeera eteyinza kununulibwa, era balyegatta mu bujjuvu era emirembe n'emirembe eri abo abagwa era emirembe n'emirembe baliba ba musota era eri ennyanja ennene ey'omuliro gye baligenda emirembe n'emirembe. Era bwe bityo bwe buliba obusobozi bw'okulonda kwabwe.

Kubanga enkomerero y'okulonda kwabwe teyinza kusazibwamu. N’ebiyiringiti byabwe n’amabwa gaabwe n’endwadde zaabwe biriba bingi, era newankubadde balisuubizibwa obulamu n’emirembe, obulamu n’emirembe tebalibifuna. Kubanga Setaani yeemalira ku kukozesa obulogo bwe okulwanyisa obusungu n'emisango gya Katonda n'okutwa n'okutta abantu, naye tebaliba na kuwummula era abo abeenyigira mu bulogo bwe tebalidduka. Abo abatwala akabonero balimasamasa wansi w'amatala gaabwe amaddugavu, n'amatala gaabwe galirira mu nguudo z'ebibuga byabwe bye bategekedde abaddu baabwe. Tegeera nti bano be baliba abakolimiddwa abatambula abataliiko kisonyiwo era abatayinza kuzzaawo, kubanga bano be abo amannya gaabwe abatali mu Kitabo ky'Obulamu eky'Omwana gw'endiga, abattibwa okuva ku ntandikwa y'ensi. So tewaliba bulokozi mu bo newakubadde mu kibiina kyabwe, kubanga okutambulira mu bo n'okwegatta mu kibiina kyabwe kiryetaagisa ddala akabonero k'ensolo.

Abajulizi bange ababiri balitegeeza okutuusa ng’ebiseera by’okutegeeza kwabwe biweddeyo kubanga emyezi 42 giweereddwa. Era bwe kityo kirigabirwa ensolo eva mu bunnya obutagondamizika okulwana nabo, era eribawangula, n’ebatta, n’emiti gyange gy’emi Zeyituuni ebiri, n’ebikondo by’amatala ebibiri bwe birimala okutegeeza kwabyo. N’emirambo gyabwe girigalamira mu luguudo lw’ekibuga ekinene, ekiyitibwa mu by’omwoyo Sodomu ne Misiri, Mukama wammwe gye yakomererwa. N’ababi balijaguza nnyo ne bawaŋŋana ebirabo olw’okufa kwabwe. Era oluvannyuma lw’ennaku ssatu n’ekitundu Omwoyo gw’obulamu oguva eri Katonda gulibayingiramu: nabo balyoka bayimirira ku bigere byabwe, n’okutya kungi kuligwira bonna abaalaba. Ne bawulira eddoboozi eddene eriva mu ggulu, nga libagamba nti, mulinnye wano. Ne balinnya mu ggulu mu kire, n’abalabe baabwe balibalaba nga balinnya mu ggulu mu bulamu obutaggwaawo era emirembe n’emirembe balikyusibwa. Ne Malayika ow'omusanvu alifuuwa, ne wabaawo amaloboozi amanene mu ggulu, agagamba nti, Obwakabaka bw'ensi eno bufuuse obwakabaka bwa Mukama waffe, n'obwa Kristo we, era alifuga emirembe n'emirembe.

(Balina omukisa abafu abafiira mu Mukama, okuva leero, weewaawo, bw'ayogera Omwoyo, bawummule okukola kwabwe, n'ebikolwa byabwe bibagoberera.)

Era n'olwekyo okubonaabona okusinga obunene okusinga okuliwo kulitandika, newankubadde nga okubonaabona kwa lubeerera mu wiiki ya Danyeri ey'omusanvu. N'essabo lyabwe liriggwawo n'ekizikiza eky'okusaanawo kiriggwaawo olw'omulimba waabwe Moshiach. Naye alikakasa endagaano n’abangi okumala wiiki emu: naye wakati mu wiiki aliremesa ssaddaaka n’ekiweebwayo, n’olw’okusaasaana kw’emizizo, aligifuula matongo, okutuusa n’okuggwaawo okumalirirwa kulifukibwako abataliiko kantu.

Bino bye biriba ebiseera ebiziyiza ennyo baana bange, kubanga nze Yeshua HaMashiach mbagamba bino nga tebinnabaawo, era biwandiikiddwa ng'okulabula eri bonna. Era bwe kityo 144,000 bange balijja okuyamba abo abandyazikiridde era abakoowoola erinnya lyange. Ndibakulembera nnamu, era balinkolera ebikolwa bingi eby'amaanyi. 144,000 bange baliserengeta ku lusozi Sayuuni era ndibakulembera.
