Omujulirwa Omubiri: Isirayiri
Okukaawa n'Okuwooma
Ogw'ekkumi 25, 2024

Eri Ab'Amazima mu Kristo Yesu!

Muwulirize eddoboozi lya Mukama wammwe Ataggwaawo, ye Yeshua HaMashiach, kubanga Nze nzuuyo eyabayitira mu kitiibwa n'obulungi okuyitira mu kuzaalibwa okupya kwe mwateekerwako akabonero n'omwoyo gwange omutukuvu ow'ekisuubizo. Weewaawo, mbafudde baana ba Katonda omunene ow'emirembe n'emirembe, ye Yahweh yennyini, era tewali amufaanana! N'olwekyo, mutegeere, baagalwa bange, nti muli mu abo be nnateerako akabonero okulindirira olunaku olw'okununulibwa lwe muliyimirira mu bujjuvu mu butuukirivu bwe ndibambaza, era abangi balisitulira ddala empeera ennungi olw'obwesigwa bwabwe, newankubadde abamu balifuna okufiirwa.

Mweyongere okugoberera n'amaanyi ekiruubirirwa, olw'ekirabo eky'okuyitibwa okw' waggulu okwa Katonda mu Kristo Yesu, era n'okutuukiriza empeera yammwe ejjuvu olw'amakubo ge nnateeka mu bigere byammwe, ge makubo g'obuweereza we nnalagira ebigere byammwe mu kunnweereza ku nsi mu biseera byange eby'ekisa. Era muli mulimu gwa nkoze yange, ogwatondebwa mu Kristo Yesu olw'ebikolwa ebirungi, Katonda bye yateekateeka edda, mulyoke mubitambuliremu.

Era bwe kityo ddala, baagalwa bange, nti Nze nzuuyo eyabawa ekigendererwa eky'emirembe n'emirembe mu Nze, Mukama wammwe ow'emirembe gyonna. Nze nzuuyo ali mu bumu obutuukiridde n'ekigendererwa ne Yahweh, ye NZE NDI ow'amaanyi, n'olwekyo Katonda bw'aba ku ludda lwammwe, baagalwa bange, ani ayinza okubawakanya? Lwaki mugondera ne muteeka ebirowoozo byammwe ku bintu ebinafu eby'ensi ebinoonya okubasendasenda okuva mu mukono gwange ogw'amaanyi ogw'emirembe, okwo okusendebwasendebwa kwe kutasoboka singa musigaza amaaso gammwe ku kigendererwa kimu eky'okuntunuulira Nze, Kristo Yesu Mukama wammwe eyalinnya mu ggulu!

Era n'okutuusa leero nkyayigiriza olw'okugondera kw'abo bonna abalina amatu okuwulira, nti temweterekeranga bya bugagga ku nsi, ennyenje n'eryenvu gye byonoonera, n'ababbi gye bamenya ne babba. Naye mweterekerenga eby'obugagga mu ggulu, ennyenje n'eryenvu gye bitayonoonera, n'ababbi gye batamenya so tebabba. Kubanga eby'obugagga byo gye biri, n'omutima gwo gye guli. Ettabaaza y'omubiri ge maaso: Bwe gatyo amaaso go bwe galaba awamu, omubiri gwo gwonna gujja kujjula omusana.

Baagalwa bange, temutegeera lugero lwange? Eby'obugagga byammwe bye birowoozo byammwe, era n'olwekyo mbawa amagezi okussa ebirowoozo byammwe ku bintu eby'omu ggulu, n'okutya nti mu kutambula kwammwe mubale ebibala era mutambulire mu bikolwa ebirungi bye nnalagirira ebigere byammwe okugoberera n'omutima omulongoofu. N'olwekyo, nnawandiisiza Pawulo eri Abakkolinso nti: Naye ntya, nga omusota bwe gwalimba Kaawa n'obukujjukujju bwe, bwe gutyo ebirowoozo byammwe bireme okwononebwa okuva mu bulongoofu obuli mu Kristo.

N'olwekyo, muteeke okwagala kwammwe ku bintu ebiri waggulu, so si ku bintu ebiri ku nsi. Kubanga mwafa, n'obulamu bwammwe bukwekeddwa wamu nange mu Katonda. Bwe ndirabika Nze, Kristo Yesu, ye bulamu bwammwe, nammwe mulirabika wamu nange mu kitiibwa. Kyenva nkaliriza, baagalwa bange, okutegeera nti muli musana gwa nsi, be baana bonna aba Kitange w'Emisana! N'olwekyo, muteeke ebirowoozo byammwe byonna ku Nze, Mukama wammwe ow'emirembe gyonna Omukama w'abakama era Kabaka w'abakabaka, nange nnaakolera n'amaanyi mu mwoyo gwammwe omusana gwange ogw'emirembe gulyoke gwake okuva mu mubiri gwange nammwe kwe muli, era bwe kityo ebibya byange eby'ebbumba ne bimasamasa ne biraga ekitiibwa kya Katonda ataggwaawo mu maaso ga Yesu Kristo!

Era mbabuuza omugole wange, mbabuuza omubiri gwange, mbabuuza abeesigwa bange mu Kristo, lwaki mukkiriza abalabe bammwe okubaggya ku kigendererwa n'okubasaasaanya okuva ku kitiibwa kyange n'obulungi bwange? Mutegeere nti abagwa bategeera bulungi nti okununulibwa kwammwe okujjuvu kuli kumpi ng'omukka gwammwe oguddako, era n'olwekyo banoonya okubabbako essanyu lyammwe kubanga bamanyi nti tekiyinzika kubaggya mu mikono gyange egy'emirembe, newankubadde bagezaako.

N'olwekyo, temutyanga n'akatono ababaziyiza, ekyo gye bali kye kabonero akalaga okuzikirira, naye gye muli ka bulokozi, era ako kaava eri Katonda. Temukyusanga birowoozo byammwe eri oluyoogaano lw'abagwa, baagalwa bange, so temukyanga ku ddyo newankubadde ku kkono. Naye musigale mu kkubo eggolokofu era effunda, lye kkubo ly'obulokozi mwe muyigira ebinkwatako Nze Yesu Omunazaaleeti kubanga Nze nzuuyo abayigiriza amakubo g'obulokozi mwe mulokolebwa mu bujjuvu bwe munaazibwa mu musaayi gwange ogutangirira okuva mu kibi kyonna, mulyoke mubeere mu butuukirivu bwa Katonda mu Kristo ali mu mmwe essuubi ery'ekitiibwa!

Mutegeere nti bwe muntambuliramu, Sitaani abalumba ng'anoonya okuziyiza okufuba kwammwe n'okubakyusa okubayingiza mu mirimu gye egy'obwereere egitaliimu. Era n'enjiri ey'obulimba ey'abantu etaagala kwenenya na kusonyiyibwa bibi, wabula ebikolwa ebirungi. Naye bwe kityo ddala, okuliisa abalumwa enjala, okwambaza abali obwereere, okuzimbira abataliiko we babeera, n'ebirala bingi, gye mirimu emirungi egisaanira okutuukiriza ebyetaago ebiriwo.

Naye Nze, Omwana gw'endiga gwa Katonda, seeyawangayo omulundi gumu, kwonna, era emirembe gyonna ng'ekiweebwayo eky'enkomerero olw'ekibi ku muti ogwo omukakanyavu e Gologoosa okusasulira ebikolwa ebirungi, so ne Yahweh teyanzuukiza mu bafu okuba omugabi w'ebintu by'ensi eno eri abali mu bwetaavu, wabula nnawanikibwa ku muti ne nfuka ettosi lyange ery'omusaayi erisembayo mu bulumi obw'entiisa ng'omuwolereza era esasula olw'ekibi n'olw'ebiva mu bibi by'aboonoonyi.

Nze, Kristo Yesu, mbavunuddemu ekikolimo ky'Amateeka, bwe nnafuulibwa ekikolimo ku lwammwe, (kubanga kyawandiikibwa nti, Akolimidde buli muntu awanikibwa ku muti) ate nga ogwo gwali muwendo ogutali gwange kusasula, kubanga Nze, Yesu Omunazaaleeti, nze oyo obutayonoona bwe bukyayimiriddewo n'okutuusa leero! Era ndi mutukuvu emirembe gyonna kubanga bulijjo nkola byonna Kitange by'ayagala. N'olwekyo, muwummulire mu mirimu gye mbawadde okukola era mwetikke ekikoligo kyange mu bujjuvu, kubanga ekikoligo kyange kyangu, n'omugugu gwange muwewufu.

Ebiseera by'omusango omunene bituuse ku nsi yonna, ge biseera eby'okubonaabona kwa Yakobo. Naye okubonaabona kwa Yakobo nga tekunnaba kutuuka mu bujjuvu ku nsi, kigwanidde abange abeesigwa okusitulibwa okuva mu busungu obugenda okujja, era Nze, Kristo Yesu, nze oyo ajja mu bire n'eddoboozi lya malayika omukulu n'ekkondeere lya Katonda, n'abafu mu Kristo be balisooka okuzuukira, nammwe abali abalamu abalisigalawo mulisitulirwa mu bire wamu nabo, ne muyingira mu kisenge ky'Entebe ya Yahweh Kitammwe ow'emirembe gyonna.

Era olunaku lubatuuseeko, baana bange, be bantu bonna ab'amazima mu Kristo Yesu. N'olwekyo, muleke emize gy'ensi eno egudde emabega era muteeke ebirowoozo byammwe byonna ku Mukama wammwe ataggwaawo, ye Yeshua HaMashiach. Weewaawo, ebirowoozo byammwe byonna awatali kwabuluza kubanga omulyolyomi anoonya abo b'ayinza okumira, era ng'empologoma ewuluguma bw'atambulatambula n'okuwuluguma kwe okutaliimu, naye mutegeere nti mulabe awanguddwa, baagalwa bange, kubanga nnamuwangula mu bujjuvu n'obuwanguzi obujjuvu bwange okuva ku bigambo byange ebyasembayo bye nnayogera mu mubiri ku muti ogwo omukakanyavu: Kiwedde!

Era bwe kityo nno kaakano nkolera ddala ku kununulibwa kwammwe okujjuvu era nnaamaliriza omulimu ogwo ogutuukiridde munda mu buli omu ku mmwe, lwe mulitondebwawo amangu ago mu bujjuvu ng'ekitonde ekipya ekituukiridde era ekitaliiko kibi. Weewaawo, ekitonde ekituukiridde okuvunda kwe kutayinza kuvunza, ekitiibwa kyakyo tekiriggwaawo, n'okumasamasa kwakyo tekulizikira. Kubanga mulifaananyizibwa Nze, Kristo Yesu, kubanga nze Adamu ow'oluvannyuma, era ndi mwoyo oguwa obulamu era Omukulu w'obulokozi bwammwe.

N'olwekyo, mulowooze ku bintu eby'omwoyo! Temukkirizanga obusaale obw'omuliro obw'omubi okwokya eby'obugagga byammwe. Mutegeere nti Sitaani yandyagadde okuzikiriza ebirowoozo byammwe ebiri ku Mukama wammwe ataggwaawo, n'eby'obugagga bye ebitagasa era ebivundu ebiseera ebisinga ebifaanana n'eby'omuzannyo n'eby'amazima. N'olwekyo mugaane ebirowoozo bye n'ebiteeso bye era mweggyeko amakubo ge gonna ag'obujeemu kubanga age ge makubo g'okufa era mazima anoonya okugabana omugabo gwe mu nnimi z'omuliro ezo ezisinga okwaka eza sulufeeri omuvundu n'omuntu yenna alonda ennyanja ey'omuliro ey'emirembe n'emirembe, naye enkomerero eyo tesoboka gye muli baagalwa bange era mbateereddeko akabonero ak'emirembe n'emirembe ak'akabonero akalamu aka Katonda.

N'olwekyo musigale mu Nze ndyoke ntereeze amakubo gammwe, era ndyoke mbalage lwatu ebyambika Sitaani by'ayagala okutegeka okusiba abange abeesigwa. Musigale mu Nze, baagalwa bange, kubanga mu Kristo tewali kyambika kiyinza kutegebwa n'ekyambika ky'omuyizzi tekiyinza kutegebwa na bukodyo wadde na maanyi. Kyenva nkuuma ebirowoozo byammwe era mukuume emitwe gyammwe n'emitima gyammwe nga bijjudde Ebigambo byange ebitukuvu! (Kubanga ebyokulwanyisa by'entalo zaffe si bya mubiri, naye bya maanyi mu Katonda okusuula ebigo.) Nga tusuula ebirowoozo, na buli kintu ekigulumizibwa ekigulumiza okumanya Katonda, era nga tuwamba buli kirowoozo okugondera Kristo.

Kubanga tetulwana na musaayi na mubiri, naye tulwana n'abakulu, n'ab'obuyinza, n'abafuga ensi ab'ekizikiza ky'ensi eno, n'obubi obw'omwoyo obuli mu bifo eby'omu ggulu. N'olwekyo mwambalenga ebyokulwanyisa byonna ebya Katonda, mulyoke muyinzenga okuziyiza ku lunaku olubi, era bwe mulimala byonna, muyimirire nga munywevu.

Weewaawo, muyimirire nga munywevu mu Mukama wammwe ataggwaawo, baana bange abato, kubanga ebiseera bibatuuseeko. Newankubadde bangi ku mmwe muba mwagala okudda mu mbeera y'okwebaka, nga amawulire g'ensi eno agaboggolera mu bukyamu aganoonya okubasendasenda okuyingira mu bulimba obunene. Temugwanga mu kuloga kwabwe, era muve ku bulogo bwabwe n'amalogo gaabwe. Muve ku ebyo bye bayita okwesanyusa, kubanga tekyetaagibwa abaana abatukuvu aba Katonda Ali Waggulu Ennyo ab'a Kristo Yesu mu bujjuvu okusanyusibwa n'ekibi nate.

Nkakasizza mu bange abeesigwa okubeera okumpi n'obutuufu obutabuusibwa obw'okulabika kwange okugenda okujja okukuŋŋaanya abange abeesigwa mu bulamu obw'omu ggulu. N'okutuusa kati mpadde ebirooto, okwolesebwa, n'ebigambo bingi eri abaana abato abaagalwa ennyo. Baagalwa bange, ekintu kino kibatuukiridde mu bujjuvu era kibali kumpi nnyo okusinga bwe mulowooza. N'olwekyo mwenyumirize. Mweyongere okuva mu kibi kyonna era mwatule ebibi byammwe gye ndi mu bujjuvu era mwatulaganenga ensobi zammwe.

Nnateeka akabonero ku bange abeesigwa mu mwoyo ogwo omutukuvu ogw'ekisuubizo era nateekateeka amafuta g'ettaala zammwe, be bantu bonna abafunye okuva gye ndi ekirabo kyange eky'obulokozi n'obulamu obutaggwaawo n'omugabo ogutaggwaawo mu Kristo Yesu Omukama w'abakama ataggwaawo era Kabaka w'abakabaka! Mutunule mu ggulu baagalwa bange, n'abo abalindirira n'obuwulize n'obwesigwa okulabika kwange baliraba n'amaaso gaabwe ekitiibwa ky'okulabika kwange, kubanga ekintu kino tekirikolebwa mu kyama era Sitaani tayinza kukweka kitiibwa kya kulabika kwange, newankubadde alirimba era alinoonya okubikka ku kulabika kwange n'abagenyi ab'obulimba n'olutalo lw'ensi yonna.

N'olwekyo, munindirire n'obugumiikiriza era mutuukirize mu bujjuvu emirimu gye mbawadde okumaliriza mulyoke mumalirize mu bujjuvu emisinde gyammwe egy'obuweereza mu ntegeka yange ey'emirembe n'emirembe ey'obulokozi mu bulamu bwammwe. Mutegeere baagalwa bange nti obwenkanya bwa Yahweh n'empeera ye n'omugabo gwe n'empeera ye tebibalibwa ng'omuntu ow'obutonde bw'abala kubanga omuntu ow'obutonde si mutuukirivu era si mwenkanya. Buli omu ku mmwe aweereddwa emirimu okubala ebisaanyizo mulyoke muyongere okutuukiriza ebigendererwa byange eby'obulokozi era Nze, Kristo Yesu, nnaalamula emirimu gyammwe gye mwakoze mu buweereza n'obuweereza eri Mukama wammwe ataggwaawo.

Mutegeere nti mwenna muliweebwa empeera nnyingi olw'emirimu gyammwe egy'obwesigwa gye mwakola mu bwesigwa mu linnya lyange ettukuvu. Era, Nze, Kristo Yesu, nze oyo alaba buli mulimu ogukolebwa ab'amazima mu Kristo n'ekigendererwa ky'omutima era nnaabawa empeera okutuuka ku nkomerero. Bwe nnalaba abagagga nga basuula ebirabo byabwe mu ggwanika, nnalaba abagagga abasuula ebirabo byabwe mu ggwanika. Nnalaba ne nnamwandu omu omwavu eyasuulamu obusente obubiri: Ne ŋŋamba nti, Mazima mbagamba nti nnamwandu oyo omwavu asuddemu okusinga bonna.

Kale nzijja gye muli baagalwa bange, ne wano mu busaawa buno obusembayo nga sinnabakuŋŋaanya gye ndi ne mbakubiriza mwenna olw'okwagala okutaggwaawo okwa Yahweh yennyini okuyimirira nga munywevu era mutunule mu bbanga kubanga okununulibwa kwammwe kubatuuseeko. Era buli jjinja ery'akabi lijja kweyongera mu maanyi n'okuzikiriza kuliyongera nga buli jjinja bwe liseetula okuyita ku nsi. Obulumi bw'okuzaala kati tebukomawo n'ensi egenda okuzaala omwana waayo kubanga mu nsi mulimu abaana ba Adamu abafuuse abaana ba Katonda okuyita mu Adamu ow'oluvannyuma. Era amangu ago bonna balikyusibwa emirembe n'emirembe ne bayingira mu ddembe ery'ekitiibwa ery'obutuukirivu era balyambazibwa emirembe n'emirembe ekitiibwa eky'emirembe n'emirembe era balina ebiseera by'emirembe n'ekigendererwa eky'emirembe n'emirembe mu ntegeka za Yahweh ez'emirembe n'emirembe.

Mutegeere nti ogusota ogwo omumyufu omunene gukkiriziddwa okuteekateeka olutikko ne gutambuza ebitundu byagwo ku lubaawo lw'ensi. Mutegeere nti ebyo ebiriwo bitandise dda era nti enkola y'ensi eriwo kati teyinza kugumira era ejja kuvaamu akabi ak'amaanyi era ak'amangu era bingi ku bintu bino ebirimu okutegeka bijja kujja wamu mu ngeri etuukiridde.

Kubanga bwe butyo obudde bw'entegeka za Yahweh bwe buli, na buli Yod n'akaggwa ka Yod bijja kujja wamu mu ngeri etuukiridde era mu nkolagana, kubanga ekintu kyonna Yahweh ky'ateekako omukono gwe kivaamu obutuukirivu obw'emirembe n'emirembe n'obutuukirivu obw'emirembe n'emirembe kubanga n'abo abalonda okuvunda n'ennimi z'omuliro ez'emirembe n'emirembe ez'okubonerezebwa balamulibwa mu butuukirivu era mu ngeri etuukiridde era ne basalirwa omusango.

Era ngamba nti, baagalwa bange, mukuume entambi zammwe nga nsalire era mweteeke ku ddaala erisinga obuyonjo mu musaayi gwange ogw'obutuukirivu era ogunaaza. Mukuume entambi zammwe nga mweteeka mu buyonjo obw'ebirowoozo ebigambo byange eby'emirembe n'emirembe bye bireeta kubanga Katonda ow'emirembe n'emirembe, ye Yahweh yennyini, ye yatuma ekigambo kye n'abawonya.

Nze nzuuyo eyabayitira mu kitiibwa n'obulungi.

Nze nzuuyo eyabawa ekigendererwa ekitaggwaawo mu Nze, Mukama wammwe ow'emirembe gyonna.

Nze nzuuyo ali mu bumu obutuukiridde n'ekigendererwa ne Yahweh, ye NZE NDI ow'amaanyi.

Nze nzuuyo abayigiriza amakubo g'obulokozi mwe mulokolebwa mu bujjuvu.

Ndi mutukuvu emirembe gyonna kubanga bulijjo nkola byonna Kitange by'ayagala.

Nze Adamu ow'oluvannyuma.

Ndi mwoyo oguwa obulamu era Omukulu w'obulokozi bwammwe.

Era Nze nzuuyo atuma ebigambo by'enjiri yange etaggwaawo okuyitira mu mubaka wange gwe nnagolola ne mmuwa obubaka olw'ekigendererwa kino, era okuyitira mu mubaka wange ebigambo by'enjiri yange etaggwaawo bitumiddwa.

Mazima, mazima, Nze nzuuyo Yahweh gw'agabanye naye entebe ye okutuusa abalabe bange lwe balifuulibwa entebe y'ebigere byange, okutuusa lwe nnaakwasa ekitonde ekinunuddwa mu bujjuvu mu mikono gya Katonda ow'obutuukirivu ataggwaawo, Yahweh alyoke abe byonna mu byonna era byonna bibeere weewaawo ne Amiina.

Era Nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, nze nnatuma ebigambo by'enjiri etaggwaawo olw'okugondera kw'abo bonna abasikira obulamu obutaggwaawo era ebigambo bino bijja kukuumibwa okuyita mu bibonyoobonyo n'emisango egigenda okujja era ebigambo bino bijja kufuna amaaso, amatu, n'emitima gy'abo be bitumiddwa. Mazima, nzijja mangu. Amiina. Ekisa kya Mukama waffe Yesu Kristo ow'emirembe n'emirembe kifukiddwa nnyo ku bantu bonna ab'amazima mu Kristo, AMIINA ne Amiina.
