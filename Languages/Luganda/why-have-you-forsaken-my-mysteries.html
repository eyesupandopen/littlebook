Mu Biwandiiko bya Peetero
Nakasenene 15, 2024

Lwaki Mwassa Ebyama Byange ku Bbali?

Lwaki mwassa ku bbali ebyama byange, ggwe kkanisa y’e Lawodikiya ekkutte akaba? Lwaki mwaleka ekyama kya Katonda ekikulu ennyo? Mwawaanyisaamu ebyama bya Kitange ebitukuvu ennyo, ye Kristo ali mu mmwe, essuubi ery'ekitiibwa, ne mufunamu amagoba amakyafu, era olubuto lwammwe lwe lwafuuka katonda wammwe. Naye mujja kung'amba nti, "Sirina musango. Sinnoonya magoba makyafu, kubanga ndi mutuukirivu mu maaso go." Nange ne mbaddamu nti: Temugoba n'enjiri ey'obugagga ey'obulimba egigishirizibwa mu kkanisa zammwe ez’e Lawodikiya? Mwatunda amazima g'ebyama bya Katonda ebitukuvu ennyo okusukkiridde ne mufunamu amagoba amatono, era temukyali na ssuubi lya kitiibwa lye njagala ennyo mutegeere, wabula mwakyusa essuubi ery'ekitiibwa ne mulifuula essuubi ly'okufuna eby'obugagga bye munoonya okwekuŋŋaanyiza olw'ebirowoozo byammwe ebitatukuvu. Saabaitira kugagga na kunoonya bya nsi, wabula nnabyaitira butuukirivu na butukuvu obw'amazima obusangibwa mu nze.

Mukyuke muve mu nsobi zammwe mugule eddagala ly'amaaso okuva gye ndi, mulyesige ku maaso gammwe mulabe amazima agali mu Byawandiikibwa ge nnabawa olw'obulokozi bwammwe. Mwawubisibwa okuwagira gavumenti z'ensi, nga mulowooza nti bwe muziwagira muba muwagira abalonde ba Katonda. Mwandisaanye okukwatibwa ensonyi mu maaso gange kubanga mu butamanya bwammwe temutegeera ani Isiraeri owa Katonda ow'amazima, era temukyategeera kyama kya Katonda kikulu nti Abaamawanga baba basika wamu, era ba mu mubiri gumu, era abafuna ku kisuubizo kye mu Kristo olw'Enjiri. Kubanga nze mirembe gyammwe, eyafuula byombi okuba kimu, era n'emenya ekisenge eky'omukono ogw'omumakati ekyabawulamu.

Mukkirizza okuŋŋanga kwammwe okubbîbwa ekkuŋŋaaniro lya Sitaani, era ne mwebulira mu bigambo bya bannabbi ab'obulimba. Temutegeera nti mmwe Isiraeri wa Katonda? Bonna abatambulira mu tteeka lino, emirembe gibeere ku bo, n'okusaasira, ne ku Isiraeri wa Katonda. Ggwe kkanisa ekkutte akaba, ani ajja kukulokola mu bw'obwenzi bwo obukwonoonye okuva ku kulowooza okumu okuli mu nze, Kristo Yesu Mukama wo? Toyinza kunywerera ku nsi eno efa, ng'olowooza nti bw'okola bw'otyo ompereza; era wandisaanye okwenenya olw'okuwagira gavumenti z'ensi ez'ekivve, kubanga Kristo alina kutabagana ki ne Beliyaali, era teziŋŋereza nze wabula ziwereza Sitaani. Mugende mu Byawandiikibwa byange munoonyeeyo obuntu bwammwe obw'amazima bwe nnakweka mu mabaluwa gange; kubanga mwafa, n'obulamu bwammwe bwakwekebwa wamu ne Kristo mu Katonda. Era oyo eyafa, aba asumuluddwa okuva mu kibi, n'etteeka ly'Omwoyo aggya obulamu libasumuludde okuva mu tteeka ly'ekibi n'okufa.

Kale, omuntu yenna bw'aba mu Kristo, aba kitonde kipya: eby'edda biweddewo, laba, byonna bifuuse bipya. Nnabatonda mu nze ng'omwana omuwere, nga mussa omukka gwammwe ogusooka mu bulamu obutaggwaawo; kubanga obulamu obutaggwaawo bwabatandikira ekiseera we mwazaalirwa obw'okubiri mu mwoyo gwange, kubanga nnafuula ababiri okuba omuntu omu omupya, ne ndyoka nteekawo emirembe. Weewaawo, omuntu omu omupya mu nze eyakolebwa Omuyudaaya n'Owaamawanga bonna mu kkanisa ya Katonda. Abo be nnayita okujja mu bulamu obupya mu nze be bantu ba Katonda abalonde, n'abo abazaalibwa mu mwoyo gwange be baana ba Katonda ab'amazima, era omuntu yenna bw'aba talina mwoyo gwa Kristo, oyo si wuwe.

Lwaki mugaana ebitundu ebimu eby'Ebyawandiikibwa, nga mubiraba ng'ebitatukuvu olw'ekyo kye muyita okulowooza okulungi? Lwaki mwawulamu Ebyawandiikibwa, nga mulowooza nti Ekigambo kya Katonda kyonna si kya kuyigiriza kwammwe, kukola kwammwe, na kuyiga kwammwe? Okulowooza okulungi okw'obulimba okujjawo amazima kubaziba amaaso ne kutabaleetera kulaba makubo gange na bigendererwa byange bye nnateekateeka edda gye muli. Weewaawo, mwanditadde ebirowoozo byammwe ku bintu eby'omu ggulu, naye temuyitanga bitundu bya Byawandiikibwa byange nti bya kuzikiriza na kunakuwala kubanga temubiraba ng'okulowooza okulungi. Muli mu ntego y'obusamize obw'Ekikristaayo, nga mukozesa okumanya kwennyini okwa bamalayika abagwa abayigiriza abantu obulogo n'obusamize n'okusala ebisusunku okuva ku ntandikwa.

Okumanya kwammwe okw'abamalayika abagwa kulimba okwakolebwa n'amagezi amakujjukujju okutega emyoyo gyammwe n'ebigambo ebirungi n'emboozi ennungi. Mumanye nti abantu ababi bangi okumala emyaka mingi baleese obusamize mu Bukristaayo, nga babagamba nti bw'olowooza ekintu ekiseera ekiwanvu, kirina okubaawo nga bw'olowooza. Ggwe musiru! Katonda teyakutonda kuzimba nsi yo ey'obulimba okwetooloola Obukristaayo bwo obw'okweyamba n'ebirowoozo ebisanyufu. Mugoberera Yesu omulala era mwava mu kukkiriza okuli mu nze, era mwaleka ebigambo byange n'ebiragiro byange ne musinga okwagala ebigambo ebirungi n'emboozi ennungi, era mwetoolooleddwa emisege egy'omululu egibalimba.

Babalimba era bakozesa bubi amateeka ga Musa, nga bagamba Abakristaayo nti balina okuwa eky'ekkumi okuwagira ebibiina byabwe ebitazimbiddwa ku nze, ejjinja erisembayo ku nsonda. Keberanga Ebyawandiikibwa, mutegeere nti buli muntu nga bw'asalawo mu mutima gwe, bw'atyo awe n'okuwa awe n'essanyu, kubanga Katonda ayagala oyo awa n'essanyu. Era lino tteeka kkulu mu Ndagaano Empya eyakakasibwa mu musaayi gwange. Eky'ekkumi kyali kya kuwagira bakabona Abaleevi, era nabo baawagiranga kabona asinga obukulu n'ekiweebwayo ekiwungulwa. Kino kyaggibwawo mu nze, kubanga nze kabona asinga obukulu emirembe gyonna ng'engeri ya Merukizeddeeki bw'eri era mbafudde bakabona era bakabaka mu maaso gange. Naye mmwe, ggwe kkanisa ekkutte akaba, muzibye amaaso n'okwolesebwa okw'amaanyi n'obusobozi obw'obulimba, obusobozi obutasangibwa mu Kristo.

Tewali kye mufuga n'ebirowoozo byammwe byokka, era muzibye amaaso gye ndi ne ku bigendererwa byange bye mbateekeddeeteekedde. Muzzibye amatu gammwe obutawulira njigiriza nnanamu ne musinga okwagala enjigiriza ez'obulimba ezibasanyusa amatu. Si ekyo kyokka, naye mulumba abooluganda abafuuse abalabe bammwe kubanga bababuulira amazima mu nsonga zino, era mwagaanye endowooza y'Ababeriya ne musinga okwagala okugoberera ennyimirira z'emitima eyitibwa mu bukyamu Obukristaayo. Mutegeere kino, abasumba abakyamu: mujja kusasulwa ku lunaku lw'omusango olw'okubuza endiga zange, era okuwoolera eggwanga kwange era nze ndisasula.

Mutegeere kino, baana bange: tewali kye mufuga n'okulowooza kwammwe okulungi, era si mmwe abaleeta obugagga olw'ebirowoozo byammwe ebisanyufu. Mubuzeeko era mwakyamizibwa abalimba bangi. Ate era, tewali kye muyinza kukola nga siriiwo era nga nange bwe sirina kye nsobola kukola nga Kitange taliiwo; kubanga Katonda y'awa abatuukirivu amaanyi era Kitange y'atonda byonna, era buli kimu kya kugulumiza linnya lya Yakuwa ettuukirivu okusinga gonna!

Muve mu mululu gwammwe muleke okwagala ssente, kubanga okwagala ssente kye kikolo ky'obubi bwonna.

Bwe muziba amaaso gammwe obutalaba njiri yange yonna ne mulondawo kyokka kye mulaba nga "kirungi," muba mu bunnya n'abakulembeze abazibe b'amaaso abaabakulembera okutuuka eyo. Saabaitira kugwa mu bunnya baana bange, wabula nnabaitira okubeera mu maaso gange mu butuukirivu n'obutukuvu obw'amazima, kale muyimuke muve mu bitosi. Nnabaita okuleka ebibi byammwe n'okwenenya amakubo gammwe amatalina butuukirivu. Ku bw'omusaayi gwange ogutangirira gwokka kwe muyinza okulongoosebwa okuva mu bibi byammwe; ebirowoozo byammwe ebirungi tebireeta butuukirivu, wadde nga mubifudde ebifaananyi eby'emitima gyammwe.

Lwaki mukkiriza abooluganda ab'obulimba n'Abayudaaya okujja mu mmwe okuketta eddembe lye mulina mu nze, Omutuukirivu Mukama? Singa mwategeera ekyama kya Katonda ekikulu, mwandyategeedde nti Kristo ye nkomerero y'Amateeka olw'obutuukirivu eri buli muntu akkiriza, Kubanga Musa awandiika ku butuukirivu obuva mu Mateeka, nti omuntu akola ebyo aliba mulamu ku bwabyo. Kubanga ntegeeza nate buli muntu akomolebwa, nti alina ebbanja ly'okukola amateeka gonna. Ate amateeka ga Musa gaatyawa?

Nnatuukiriza ebyetaagibwa mu mateeka ne ntuukiriza omuntu olw'okukkiriza mu nze, kubanga nnawangula, era nnatuukiriza amateeka, ne nneewaayo ng'Okuyitako okutuukiridde omulundi gumu emirembe gyonna; olw'ekyo, omutuukirivu aliba mulamu lwa kukkiriza. Temusomanga nti sajja kuzikiriza mateeka oba bannabbi naye okutuukiriza amateeka n'okuwa abantu obutuukirivu olw'okwefuula omu nange, Mukama waabwe Yesu Kristo.

N'olwekyo njogera nti, mutambulirenga mu mwoyo, so temulituukirizanga okwegomba kw'omubiri, era tewali tteeka erirwana n'ekibala ky'omwoyo. Kale, mwaziikibwa nange olw'okubatizibwa mu kufa kwange, era nga Kristo bwe yazuukizibwa mu bafu olw'ekitiibwa kya Patri: era nammwe bwe mutyo mutambulirenga mu bulamu obupya. Mu mirimu gyange egy'okutangirira gyokka, ng'omwana gw'endiga gwa Katonda atalina kibi, omuntu yenna mw'ayinza okuwangulira ekibi n'okusumululwa okuva mu kikolimo ky'amateeka; kubanga nnafuulibwa ekikolimo ku lwammwe, kubanga Akolimirwa buli muntu awanikibwa ku muti.

Musa yawandiika ku butuukirivu obuva mu mateeka, Nti omuntu akola ebyo aliba mulamu ku bwabyo. Kubanga mu Kristo Yesu okukomolebwa tekugasa kintu, newakubadde obutakomolebwa, wabula ekitonde ekipya. Kristo ye nkomerero y'amateeka eri buli muntu akkiriza, naye bangi ku mmwe bajja kugaana ebigambo bino byennyini kubanga mussaamu ekitiibwa ebigambo by'abasumba bammwe, ababuulizi, n'abarabi okusinga ebigambo bya Mukama wammwe era Omulokozi, Kristo Yesu. Temutegeera nti mwazimbirwa ku musingi gw'abatume ne bannabbi, Yesu Kristo yennyini nga ye jjinja erisembayo ku nsonda? Kubanga tewali musingi mulala muntu yenna gw'ayinza okuteekawo, okuggyako ogwateekebwawo, ye Yesu Kristo; olw'ekyo, temuli wansi wa mateeka wabula wansi wa kisa.

Kubanga abo abakkiriza bayinza okuyingira mu kuwummula kwange, kubanga oyo ayingidde mu kuwummula kwange naye awummudde emirimu gye, nga Katonda bwe yakola ku gye ku lunaku olw'omusanvu. Temumanyi nti amateeka gaali musomesa wammwe okubaleeta gye ndi, Kristo Yesu? Naye bangi banziriridde nze n'ekisa kyange n'okusaasira kwange ne basinga okwagala emirimu gyabwe egy'obutuukirivu egitaliiko nsa. Obuzibe bw'amaaso mu kitundu kyabwo bujjeere Isiraeri okutuusa okujjudde kw'Abaamawanga lwe kulituukirira. Ggwe ttabi ly'omuzeyituuni ogw'omu nsiko, teweenyumiririzanga ku muzeyituuni gwe wasimbwako, so togolereranga buzibe bwabwe; kubanga nnyinza okulokola Yakobo era nja kukikola ddala kubanga ensigalira erirokolebwa.

Kale omuntu yenna alemenga okubasalira omusango mu kulya, oba mu kunywa, oba ku bikwata ku lunaku olutukuvu lwonna, oba ku mwezi ogw'okuboneka, oba ku nnaku za ssabbiiti. Lwaki muteeka omugugu ku baana bange, nga mubagamba nti balina okwatula erinnya lyange mu lulimi olutukuvu? Ntegeera ennimi zonna era ntunula ku mutima, n'olwekyo mmanya abaana bange bwe bakoowoola erinnya lyange mu lulimi lwonna lwe bakoowoolamu, ne mbateekako akabonero ka Kitange nga bwe kigwana, ne mbaleeta mu Kkanisa ya Katonda. Newakubadde bajjajjammwe newakubadde mmwe temusobola kukwata mateeka, naye amateeka gatuukiririzibwa mu mmwe mu butuukirivu bwange; kubanga amateeka gava ku biragiro bibiri ebikulu: Yagalanga Mukama Katonda wo n'omutima gwo gwonna, n'emmeeme yo yonna, n'amagezi go gonna, n'amaanyi go gonna, era yagalanga muliraanwa wo nga bwe weeyagala wekka.

Obutuukirivu tebujja lwa mateeka wabula lwa kukkiriza mu nze, Mukama Yesu Kristo. Kubanga nze kabona asinga obukulu emirembe gyonna ng'engeri ya Merukizeddeeki bw'eri, era olw'omusaayi gwange ogutangirira mwetengererezebwa, kubanga nze Okuyitako okutuukiridde, era nze mwana gw'endiga gwa Katonda. Omuntu yenna alemenga okubakyamya mu nsonga zino. Etteeka terikolerwa muntu mutuukirivu, wabula abajeemu n'abatagondera. Laba! Nnasangulawo oluwandiiko lw'obulombolombo olwali lubalanga omusango, olwali lubaziyiza, ne nduluggyaawo, ne ndukomerera ku musalaba gwange. Naye, mukyali mwagala kubeera mu buddu bw'ebintu ebinafu era eby'obwavu. Ebyawandiikibwa bigamba ki: Goba omuzaana n'omwana we, kubanga ffe si baana ba muzaana wabula ba muddembe.

Ssaayogera lwatu nti: Laba! Mbalaga ekyama, ffenna tetuliggalira tulo, naye ffenna tulifuulibwa, kubanga ndikka okuva mu ggulu n'oluyoogaano, n'eddoboozi lya malayika omukulu n'ekkondeere lya Katonda n'abafu mu Kristo be balisooka okuzuukira, mmwe abali abalamu abalisigalawo mulitwalibwa mu bire okusisinkana nze, Kristo Yesu mu bbanga, era bwe mutyo mulibeeranga nange emirembe gyonna. Ggwe kkanisa y'e Lawodikiya, ogaana n'ebigambo byange eby'olwatu ennyo gye oli. Kale, manya nti ndikugoba ku mmeeza yange, bwe mba njagala ennyo okukuteerawo ekifo mu ggulu.

Mulowooza nti kintu kitono okugaana ebigambo byange n'okukyamya abalala mu nsobi yammwe. Kale, okuva bwe mwagala olunaku lwa Mukama, ndibaleka okugumiikiriza olunaku lwa Mukama, kubanga mulina amalala nga mulowooza nti muyinza okuyimirira. Mugaana okwabika kwange ne mugulumiza ebigambo bya bannabbi ab'obulimba okusinga enjigiriza ennamu gye nnawa mu nze. Temusomanga olugero lw'abawala ekkumi? Mbakubiriza okuba n'okukkiriza mu bigambo byange so temunneegaananga, nneme okubalemerako okubeegaana. Mujja kuba baani, baana bange? Mujja kuba mu bawala ab'amagezi abeeteekateeka n'entambi ensalire n'amafuta mu ttaala zaabwe? Oba mujja kutwala ekifo kyammwe wamu n'abawala abasirusiru, nga mugamba nti, Mukama, Mukama tuggulirewo; nange ne mbagamba nti, Sibamanyi!

Temusomanga nti? Olw'ensonga eno omusajja anaalekanga kitaawe ne nnyina, n'atabulwa ne mukazi we, n'abo bombi banaabanga omubiri gumu. Kino kyama kikulu: naye njogera ebikwata ku Kristo n'Ekkanisa. Temutegeera bigambo by'omugole omusajja, ggwe mugole wange omukazi? Mulina okunnywererako ng'omutwe gwammwe mu ngeri yennyini omukazi gy'anywererako ku bba ng'omutwe gwe, kubanga Omugole ye Kkanisa yange era omubiri gwange, era musajja ki eyakyawa omubiri gwe? Naye, mukkiriza abasumba abakyama okubakyamya mu nsonga eno, era bangi ku mmwe mussaamu ekitiibwa ebigambo ebikyamye okuva mu bannabbi ab'obulimba abakyamye okusinga Ekigambo kyange Ekitukuvu.

Muteese okukkiriza kwammwe mu bantu ababi, abantu be saatuma; naye babalimba n'ebigambo ebirungi n'emboozi ennungi bye saabawa. Mubagoberera nga muli ng'abali ng'angoberera nze. Mwenenye! Bangi ku mmwe mugoberera abaweereza ab'obulimba abalabika ng'abaweereza b'obutuukirivu. Naye si kya kwewuunya, kubanga Sitaani yeefuula malayika ow'omusana, kale tekiteekwa kwewuunyisa nti n'abaweereza be bakola kye kimu. Bangi ku mmwe muzibiddwa amaaso omusana ogw'obulimba, okuva mu baweereza ab'obulimba be saatuma, era boogera ebigambo ebikulu eby'okwekuza ebikontana n'enjigiriza ennamu eri mu nze, Mukama Yesu Kristo.

Mweyimirirewo, mmwe Abakristaayo abatagonda, mufulume mu ye ne muva mu Babulooni Ekyama. Nandyagadde mubeere abookya oba abannyogoga so si abakutte akaba, era sirina kwagala kubawansula mu kamwa kange. Kale, mukomewo gye ndi, baana bange, mukomewo ne ku bigambo byange. Munnywerereko ng'omutwe gwammwe, kubanga nze Mukama eyabafiirira, era mwazuukira bwe nnazuukira. Lwaki mwesiga bannabbi ab'obulimba? Temwetaaga muntu yenna kubayigiriza, naye okufukibwako amafuta kwe mwafuna okuva gye ndi kubeera mu mmwe era kubayigiriza byonna, mmwe abalina okukkiriza okutono! Mukyukire gye ndi mukomewo ku kkubo effunda. Mukaabe, muwowoggane, era mwenenye, baana bange! Bonna be njagala, mbanenya, okuyitibwa kwammwe n'okulondebwa kwammwe bibeere bya mazima.

Mumanye nti mbagala nnyo era njagala omutima gwammwe gwonna okusinga emirimu gyammwe. Mujje gye ndi n'omutima omuggule munneeweeyo mwenna, nga nange bwe nneewaddeyo mwenna ku lwammwe. Temutyanga so temwesalira musango, kubanga tewali kusalirwa musango kw'abo abali mu Kristo Yesu. Mukomewo bugwi gye ndi mwatule ebibi byammwe, nange ndibasonyiwa ne mbazzaawo mu bigendererwa ne mu birabo bye nnabawa. Muyimirire nga munywevu era nga temunyiginyiga, kubanga nja kulabika mangu nnyo amangu ago, n'ensi ejja kumanya nti mbanyazizzaawo, kubanga ekintu kino tekirikolebwa mu kyama. Obudde bufunda, baana bange, era mangu nnyo obudde tebulibaawo.

Nnyinza okulokola Yakobo era nja kukikola ddala kubanga ensigalira erirokolebwa.

Nze kabona asinga obukulu emirembe gyonna ng'engeri ya Merukizeddeeki bw'eri.

Nze Okuyitako okutuukiridde.

Nze mwana gw'endiga gwa Katonda.

Nze Mukama eyabafiirira, ye Yeshua HaMashiach.
