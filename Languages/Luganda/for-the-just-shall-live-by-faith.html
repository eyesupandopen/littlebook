Obujulizi obwa Lweggala: Ekkanisa
Mu biwandiiko by'omutume Pawulo
Epreero 27, 2024

Kubanga Omutuukirivu aliiba mulamu olw'okukkiriza!

Kubanga omutuukirivu aliiba mulamu olw'okukkiriza! Mugolokoke, era mugolokose ab'oluganda lwammwe, ekkanisa yange ey'e Lawodikiya ebuguma bugumi, eyannoondera ebyama byange; kubanga essaawa ziyiseeko era obudde busemberedde okuggwaawo. Mujje gyendi, mmwe abaana bange, n'emikono gyanyu egy'eggule era n'omutima oguggule (kubanga emikono gyange n'omutima gwange bya ggule ku lwammwe); mujje gyendi n'omutima omugonvu era omwetegefu okukkiriza ebigambo byange. Kubanga muganda wange Yakobo yagamba atya? Bwe mutyo mweggyeko obugwagwa bwonna n'obubi obungi ennyo, mukkirize n'obuwombeefu ekigambo ekyasigibwa, ekiyinza okulokola emyoyo gyammwe. Baana bange, munzigobererenga, kubanga ndi muwombeefu era mukkakkamu mu mutima, era mukkirize n'obuwombeefu Ekigambo ekyasigibwa, kye nateeka wakati mu mmwe mulyoke mube n'amaaso agalaba n'amatu agawulira. Mudde eri nze, kubanga nze kkubo, n'amazima, n'obulamu, so tewali ajja eri Kitange wabula ng'ayita mu nze, kubanga ekyo kye kyayagala Kitange bwe kiri, era yakiteekawo bwe kityo.

Kiki ekibaletera okutegana mu mitima gyammwe, mmwe abaana bange? Lwaki mukyakyuka okuva ku bigambo byange ne mukyamya abalala mu nsobi yammwe? Kubanga mmwe, ekkanisa yange ey'e Lawodikiya, mwerabidde kye muli mu nze, Kristo Yesu, era mubuzizza ebyama byange, newaakubadde nga nnabibakwasa. Kubanga nze Mukama eyabagulisa omusaayi gwange mwennyini; nze Mukama w'abakama ne Kabaka w'abakabaka eyasikira ensi okuva eri Kitange, era ndigabana obusika bwange n'abeesigwa era abalonde; ndigabana obusika bwange nammwe, Mmwe Omugole wange! Kale temutya, kubanga newaakubadde nga muli baavu, muli bagagga mu nze, kubanga Kitange yakwasa byonna mu mikono gyange. Ayi, mmwe ekkanisa ey'e Lawodikiya, mufuuse omukazi atalina kukkiriza, ow'okwekkiriranya.

Nedda, temufaayo ku by'okulya eby'omwoyo eby'omu kigambo kyange, so temungoberera n'omutima gwammwe gwonna; temusomanga Lugero? Okutegeera okulungi kuleeta ekisa, naye ekkubo ly'abatali beesigwa lizibu. N'amakubo g'okwekkiriranya ge mwalonze mazibu; amakubo gammwe agatalina kukkiriza si gange, era mugezaako okutambulira mu kkubo lye nnabawa n'ekigere kimu mu kkubo ate ekirala mu lusalosalo, n'oluvannyuma ne muba n'obuvumu okunnunamu, nze Yesuwa HaMashiach, bwe mugwa mu lusalosalo nga bwe mwakulemberwa. Kubanga olugendo lwammwe lulimu okugwa okungi, era essuubi lyange gye muli liri nti okugwa kwammwe kube ng'okugwa kw'omutuukirivu: Kubanga omutuukirivu agwa emirundi musanvu n'ayimuka nate: naye ababi baligwa mu kabi.

Ssaabayitira kugwa mu kabi, mmwe abaana bange, newaakubadde mmwe, ekkanisa yange ey'e Lawodikiya, kubanga sinnaba kubaleka mu biseera by'okugezesebwa. Kubanga laba! Nkyayimiridde ku luggi ne nkonkona kubanga sirikola kintu kyonna ku ngeri yammwe ey'obugumi bugumi, ey'okwekkiriranya, naye mbayita okujja mu butuukirivu. Mbayita okuyimirirawo mu butuukirivu, kubanga abasumba abakyama babalimba, ne babakulembera mu makubo ga Balamu; era bangi ku mmwe mulagulira mu bukyamu olw'emyoyo gya Yezeberi, abandyonoonyeyo ebigambo bya bannabbi bange ab'amazima era ne beegomba okutta bannabbi be nnatukuza mu nze, kubanga nze eyawa abantu ebirabo. Kubanga nnawandiikira Abagalatiya bwe beesittala nga mmwe bwe mweesittala: Neewuunya, mmwe okubeera nga mwanguwa bwemutyo okuva ku oyo eyabayitira mu kisa kya Kristo, okudda eri enjiri endala: Etali ndala. Weewaawo, soma ekyo nate, kubanga tewali Njiri ndala so tewali linnya lya ngeri yonna erirala eryaweebwa abantu mwe banaalokokerera okuggyako eryange, Yesu Kristo. Lwaki mukyamizibwa okuva gyendi, mmwe abaana bange abato? Si nze mbalabirira era ne mbafaako, kubanga nze musumba omulungi eyawaayo obulamu bwe ku lw'endiga. Naye ebigambo bye saayogerera, bye mwegomba ennyo era bye mugoberera, bibasisimula amatu; kubanga okufuna obugyamu obwo kufuuse okwagala kwammwe okusooka mu kwekulumbya kwammwe; kubanga mukuuma obulimba mu mitima gyammwe nga buli mazima, era mubuzizza amagezi gonna ag'okwawula.

Mw'erabidde ebyafaayo ebya bulijjo eby'ekkanisa? Muli bagayaavu nnyo okuyiga? Omutwe guno tegubasisimula matu okuba nga mugawulira ebigambo byange ne mubikkiririzaamu? Kubanga omuntu taba mulamu na mmere yokka naye na buli kigambo ekiva mu kamwa ka Katonda! Ekkanisa yange yazaalibwa ku lunaku lwa Pentekooti era kiwandiikiddwa mu Bikolwa by'Abatume mulyoke mukebere. Kubanga ekkanisa yange yatandikira mu Isirayiri, mu Buyudaaya, newaakubadde tebaategeera bigambo bye nnayogera nga mpita mu Peetero ku lunaku olwo olw'ekitiibwa lwe nnafuka omwoyo omutukuvu ku balonde bange, bwe baayogeranga ennimi endala ng'omwoyo bwe gwabawanga okuvvuunula! Kubanga Peetero yagamba ki ekibiina eky'e Yerusalemu ku lunaku olwo bwe yanjogerera? Kubanga okusuubiza kuli gye muli, ne gye baana bammwe, ne gy'abo bonna abali ewala, abangi abo bonna Mukama Katonda waffe b'aliita. Baana bange, mmwe muli abo abali ewala, kubanga bangi ku mmwe si ba Isirayiri mu mubiri, naye nabaggya mu mawanga mu kisa kyange mulyoke muyimirire mu kusaasira kwange, kubanga mwayitibwa.

Temusomanga? Naye kaakano mu Kristo Yesu, mmwe abaali ewala edda, mwasembezeddwa olw'omusaayi gwa Kristo. Naye mu mayumba gammwe ag'okusinzizaamu ag'okwekkiriranya ge muwonga mu buvveenvu mu linnya lyange (newaakubadde nga siriyingiramu wabula nja kuyimirira ku luggi ne nkonkona), mulowooza nti mwafuna amateeka ga Musa okuva gye ndi, era mu malala gammwe temutegeera nti Musa yali wa Isirayiri, era Isirayiri ye yaweebwa Torah okugikwata, omulimu gwe baalemwa; kubanga tewali muntu ayinza kukwata mateeka ga Musa olw'okwononeka omusota kwe yaleeta mu mubiri gwammwe, kubanga okumanya kwe kulaga kwonoonanga, n'empeera y'ekibi kwe kufa. Kubanga Abakkolinso bajulira ebigambo byange: Era ye yatufuula abaweereza abatuukiridde ab'endagaano empya, si ba nnukuta, naye ba mwoyo: kubanga ennukuta etta, naye omwoyo guwa obulamu. Mwayitirwa okutambulira mu mwoyo, newaakubadde nga bangi ku mmwe mwegomba nnyo olw'ensobi ennukuta y'amateeka, etali ya mmwe, naye etuukiririzibwa mu nze.

Munnyanukulire kino: Omwoyo gwabajjira olw'okutuukiriza kwammwe mwennyini okwa Torah? Oba gwajja olw'okutuukiriza kwange okutuukiridde okwa Torah ng'Okwambuka kwammwe okutuukiridde? Kubanga obujulizi bw'Abagalatiya bukaaba ki? Kino kyokka kye njagala okuyigira ku mmwe, omwoyo mwagufuna olw'emirimu gy'Amateeka, oba olw'okuwulira kw'okukkiriza? N'ate Pawulo agamba Abagalatiya nti: Sigayaaliriza kisa kya Katonda: kubanga singa obutuukirivu bujjira mu Mateeka, kale Kristo yafa bufa. Temutegeera nti ku ntandikwa y'emirembe waaliwo ekkanisa y'Abayudaaya n'ekkanisa y'Abamawanga? Newaakubadde ng'ekkanisa yange yazaalirwa mu Buyudaaya, Pawulo yagamba Abagalatiya era n'abakuuma eyo: Kubanga oyo eyakolanga obulungi mu Peetero mu butume bw'abakomole, ye yali w'amaanyi ne mu nze eri Abamawanga. Kubanga omulundi ogusooka nnatuma Peetero eri Abamawanga, era kiwandiikiddwa mu Bikolwa bwe nnagamba Peetero nti: Ebyo Katonda by'alongosezza, ggwe tobyogerangako nti bya nziro. Kubanga bwe nnatuma Peetero eri Koneriyo, obujulizi obuddako bwafulumizibwa: N'abo ab'omu bakomole abaali bakkirizza, ne beewuunya, abo bonna abajja ne Peetero, kubanga ne ku Bamawanga nabo kwafukibwanga ekirabo eky'omwoyo omutukuvu. Mbabuuza kino, mmwe Ab'e Lawodikiya, Koneriyo yafuna obulokozi olw'okugoberera Torah (kubanga yali musajja wa ddiini)? Oba yafuna okusuubizibwa okuyita mu kukkiriza? Abakkolinso balina kye boogera ku nsonga eno Pawulo bwe yabawandiikira: Mmwe nammwe mwe mulokolebwa, bwe mukwata mu bwongo ekyo kye nnababuulira, okuggyako nga mwakkiririza bukiririza. Obulokozi bwajja olw'okubuulirwa okw'okukkiriza so si olw'ennukuta y'amateeka, kubanga okweteeka wansi w'amateeka ga Musa, n'okukkiriza kwammwe kuba kwa bwereere.

Bw'atyo Abaruumi bagamba nti: Naye kaakano obutuukirivu bwa Katonda obutali mu Mateeka bulabisiddwa, nga bujulirwa Amateeka ne Bannabbi. Obutuukirivu bwa Katonda, obuyita mu kukkiriza kwa Yesu Kristo eri bonna, ne ku bonna abakkiriza: kubanga tewali njawulo: Kubanga bonna baayonoona, ne batatuuka ku kitiibwa kya Katonda. Kubanga nnagamba ekkanisa yange ey'omu Galatiya Pawulo we yawandiikira n'obuwulize nti: Kubanga mu Yesu Kristo, okukomolebwa tekugasa kintu, newaakubadde obutakomolebwa, wabula okukkiriza okukolera mu kwagala. N'ate eri Abagalatiya Pawulo yawandiika nti: Kubanga mu Kristo Yesu okukomolebwa tekugasa kintu newaakubadde obutakomolebwa, wabula ekitonde ekiggya. Bw'atyo obulokozi tebuweebwa olw'emirimu gy'amateeka, so n'obulokozi bwammwe tebuweebwa era tebuweebwa mirimu gya mateeka. Kubanga eri Abaruumi kyawandiikibwa nti: Laba, nteeka mu Sayuuni ejjinja erisittaza, n'olwazi olw'ekinyigo: na buli amukkiririza, talikwatibwa nsonyi. Baana bange, nze liiso eryo erisittaza era olwazi olw'ekinyigo, kubanga nze Yesu Omunazaaleeti, omwana gw'endiga gwa Katonda, era ntukirizza amateeka.

Ayi, mmwe ab'e Lawodikiya, mulowooza mmwe mwekka abeetala ku nsonga eno? Ngamba nedda. Kubanga muyinza okukuba amabaluwa gange mu mitendera ebiri, okuggyako ng'omubiri gwombi guwandiikiddwa eri omuntu omu omuggya mu Kristo Yesu. Kubanga amabaluwa gange gawandiikibwa eri amakanisa g'Abamawanga, n'amalala gawandiikibwa eri amakanisa g'Abayudaaya, n'olwekyo Peetero yawandiika ku kubikkulirwa kw'Ekyama kye nnabikkulira Pawulo nti: Nga ne mu mabaluwa ge gonna bwe gali, ng'ayogera omwo ebintu bino, mwe muli ebimu ebizibu okutegeera, abo abatali bayivu era abatanywevu bye bakyama, nga bwe bakola ne ku Byawandiikibwa ebirala, okutuuka ku kuzikirira kwabwe kwennyini.

N'olwekyo okubonaabona kwammwe si kuggya, kubanga tewali kikemo kyabakwata wabula ekyo eky'abantu bonna: naye Katonda mwesigwa, atalibaganya kukemebwa okusinga bwe muyinza; naye wamu n'ekikemo ajja kukola n'ekkubo ly'okuwona, mulyoke musobole okukigumiikiriza. Munaatwala ekkubo ly'okuwona Katonda ly'abategekedde mu kisa kyange n'okusaasira okw'okudduka abo abandibaggyeeko empeera yammwe nga babaleetera okugwa okuva mu kisa? Munaagaana ebigambo byange ne mukulembeza Abayudaaya ababagamba n'obutamanya nti mulina okukwata Torah olw'obulokozi bwammwe? Muli basirusiru nnyo okuba nga mulowooza nti, bwe mwamala okutandika amateeka g'obulamu mu mwoyo gwa Yesu Kristo, kati muweereddwayo eri amateeka g'ekibi n'okufa? Kubanga amateeka gali gatya? Baana bange, nja kubagamba. Temumaliriranga mu busiru nti ndi ddoboozi lya bwereere eribakulembera mu nsobi, kubanga bangi ku mmwe temumanyi ddoboozi lyange so temummanyi, newaakubadde nga mbayita! Temulowooza nti nnajja okuzikiriza amateeka oba bannabbi. Saajja kuzikiriza, wabula okutuukiriza. Kubanga mazima mbagamba nti, Okutuusa eggulu n'ensi lwe biriggwaawo, ennukuta emu oba akabonero k'ennukuta, tekaliva mu mateeka, okutuusa byonna lwe birituukiririzibwa.

Nze oyo eyatuukiriza amateeka ga Musa, n'ebiragiro byago bikwatibwa mu buwulize eri ebigambo byange eby'ekisa n'okusaasira mu mabaluwa gange ne ng'eyita mu kukkiriza kwa Yesu Kristo. Mmwe mulowooza mu butamanya kubanga nze kutuukiririzibwa kw'amateeka nti nkwagala omuntu yenna okumenya amateeka ga Katonda, oba amateeka ga Musa oba amateeka ga Katonda ag'empisa. Kubanga nnagamba ki abo abaali babeera e Kkolinso? Ekkanisa yange ey'e Lawodikiya, gye njagala, si mmwe musooka okusanga ekikemo kino, era bwentyo nnawandiisa Pawulo nti: Era ye yatufuula abaweereza abatuukiridde ab'endagaano empya, si ba nnukuta, naye ba mwoyo: kubanga ennukuta etta, naye omwoyo guwa obulamu. Naye oba nga okuweereza okw'okufa okwawandiikibwa, ne kuwandiikibwa ku mayinja, kwali kwa kitiibwa, abaana ba Isirayiri ne batayinza kutunuulira maaso ga Musa, olw'ekitiibwa ky'amaaso ge, ekyali kiggwaawo: Okuweereza kw'omwoyo tekuliba kwa kitiibwa nnyo? Temukola nsobi, mmwe abaana bange, ennukuta etta olw'ekibi ekiri mu mubiri gwammwe ekisalirwa omusango olw'ennukuta eyo yonna, n'amateeka ga Musa: Bw'atyo amateeka matukuvu, n'ekiragiro kitukuvu era kituukirivu era kirungi. Olwo ekyo ekirungi, kyantonderwa okufa? Katonda akigaane. Naye ekibi, kilyoke kirabike ekibi, nga kikolera okufa mu nze olw'ekyo ekirungi: ekibi olw'Ekiragiro kilyoke kibeere ekibi ennyo. Bwe ntyo nnawandiisa Pawulo eri Abagalatiya nti: Sigayaaliriza kisa kya Katonda: kubanga singa obutuukirivu bujjira mu Mateeka, kale Kristo yafa bufa. Baana bange, mwatwalibwa bubi, era nabagamba nti omwenge omuggya guteekwa okuteekebwa mu nsawo empya ez'amaliba, kale munywerere mu kisa Kristo Yesu mwe yabafuulira ab'eddembe.

Baana bange, temuyinza kumenya mateeka ga Katonda bwe mutambulira mu mwoyo, kubanga tekiyinzika. Mwazimbibwa ku musingi gw'abatume ne bannabbi, Yesu Kristo ye yennyini nga ye jjinja ekkulu erisiba enjuyi. N'eri Abebbulaniya kyayogebwa nti: Kubanga ndisaasira obutali butuukirivu bwabwe, n'ebibi byabwe n'obutali butuukirivu bwabwe siribijjukira nate. Mu ekyo agamba, endagaano empya, afuuse eyasooka enkulu. Kaakano ekyo ekivunda era ekikaddi kyetegeka okuggwaawo. Baana bange, obutuukirivu tebujja olw'okukwata amateeka, kubanga kirabo ekifunibwa olw'okukkiriza mu nze, Kristo Yesu. Era olw'okuba mufuusiddwa abatuukirivu mu nze, musumululwa okuva mu mateeka g'ekibi n'okufa.

Kubanga kuvvoola okugamba nti okutambulira mu mwoyo gw'obulamu mu ngeri emu kuba kibi, kubanga buli akkiriza bw'atyo abala ssaddaaka yange ku lw'ebibi by'abantu ng'ekintu ekitali kitukuvu. Kubanga nze mukulu okusinga Musa, kubanga Musa yennyini agamba nti: Mukama Katonda wo alikuyimusiza nnabbi okuva mu makkati go, mu baganda bo, alifaanana nze, ye gwe muliwuliranga. Wuliriza eddoboozi lyange, mmwe abaana, era wuliriza ebigambo byange, kubanga sibafuulangako bataliiko mateeka, kubanga abaana ba Sitaani tebalina mateeka, so si baana ba Katonda! Kubanga mu Abaruumi Pawulo yawandiika lwatu nti: Tewali kusingisibwa nate ku abo abali mu Kristo Yesu, abatambulira mu mubiri, naye mu mwoyo. Kubanga amateeka g'omwoyo gw'obulamu, mu Kristo Yesu, ganninnyisa okuva mu mateeka g'ekibi n'okufa.

Kubanga ekyo amateeka kye gatayinza kukola, kubanga lyali linafu olw'omubiri, Katonda ng'atuma Omwana we, mu kifaananyi ky'omubiri ogw'ekibi, era olw'ekibi n'asingisa omusango ekibi mu mubiri: Obutuukirivu bw'amateeka bulyoke butuukiririzibwe mu ffe, abatambulira mu mubiri, naye mu mwoyo. N'ate Abaruumi bagamba nti: Ndigamba nti, mu kiseera kino obutuukirivu bwe: alyoke abeere omutuukirivu, era atuukirize oyo akkiririza mu Yesu. Olwo okwenyumiriza kuli ludda wa? Kuggiddwaawo. Mu Mateeka ki? Gya mirimu? Nedda: naye olw'Amateeka g'okukkiriza. N'olwekyo, tugamba nti, omuntu aweebwa obutuukirivu olw'okukkiriza, awatali bikolwa bya Mateeka. Kubanga nnagamba ki: Kubanga mu yo obutuukirivu bwa Katonda bubikkulirwa okuva mu kukkiriza okutuuka mu kukkiriza; nga bwe kyawandiikibwa nti, Omutuukirivu aliiba mulamu olw'okukkiriza.

Naye bangi ku mmwe mweteekako emigugu egitagyamu ku mulala nga mukkirizza bannabbi ba Sitaani abatumibwa okubakwasa mu ntego y'okukwata Torah mu bukyamu ng'ekyetaagisa mu bulokozi era n'okuwangaalira mu bulokozi; era abamu ku mmwe muvumaganya nga mugamba nti nnatuukiriza amateeka olwa ssaddaaka yange eyange nzekka mulyoke mukwate Torah. Ku ludda olumu, oli mutuufu, kubanga okutambulira mu mateeka g'omwoyo gw'obulamu kwe kukwata Torah mu bujjuvu olw'okweyitira mu nze; kubanga ekkanisa yange ezimbiddwa mu butuukirivu n'obutukuvu obw'amazima ku nze, Kristo Yesu, ejjinja ekkulu erisiba enjuyi. Mukwata Torah olw'obuwulize eri okukkiriza okuli mu nze. Lwaki mulonda eddoboozi ly'Abayudaaya okusinga eddoboozi lyange etukuvu? Lwaki muteeka emigugu egitasaana ku baganda bammwe, kubanga temusomanga? Kubanga nnagamba dda Abakkolosaayi nti: Kale omuntu yenna aleme kubasalira musango mu by'okulya, oba mu by'okunywa, oba ku bikwata ku lunaku olutukuvu lwonna, oba ku mwezi omuggya, oba ku nnaku za ssabbiiti Ezo kye kinyenya ky'ebintu ebigenda okujja, naye omubiri gwa Kristo.

Baana bange, sinenya mateeka; nnondawo eky'olwatu nti olw'ekibi ekiri mu mubiri gwammwe temuyinza kukwata Torah okuggyako olw'okukkiriza kwammwe mu nze, kubanga omutuukirivu aliiba mulamu olw'okukkiriza! Okukkiriza mu nze kiki, oyinza okubuuza? Okukkiriza mu nze kuzimbiddwa ku kwesiga ebigambo byange n'okutegeera nti muli wamu nange ku buli mutendera, kubanga mmwe muli kkanisa yange gye nnaazizza mu musaayi gwange ogutaliiko kibi; kubanga okusonyiyibwa ebibi tekiyinzika kufunika olwa ssaddaaka y'ebisolo. Mube n'okukkiriza mu nze, kubanga okuyita mu kukkiriza mu nze Torah ekwatibwa, okuyita mu kukkiriza mu nze amateeka gatuukiririzibwa mu mmwe, era muli batuukirivu, abatuukirivu, era abatukuvu! Bwe nnafuuka obulamu obuggya, nammwe mwazuukizibwa; era bwe nnafuuka ku mukono ogwa ddyo gwa Kitange, nammwe mwatudde mu ggulu. Kubanga Abeefeso bajulira nti: Naye Katonda omugagga mu kusaasira, olw'okwagala kwe okungi kwe yatwagala, N'edda bwe twali abafu mu bibi, yatulamuya wamu ne Kristo (olw'ekisa mwe mwalokolera), Era yatuzuukiza wamu, n'atutuuza wamu mu bifo by'omu ggulu mu Kristo Yesu. Muteekwa okutegeera nti amateeka ga Musa si ndagaano yammwe, kubanga eyammwe ye ndagaano empya eyakakasibwa mu musaayi gwange era n'esasulirwa okubonaabona kwange.

Kale muwulirenga Okukkiriza, mmwe abantu bange, era muleme kubeera ba bugumi bugumi. Mulonde obulamu mu nze, Yesu Kristo, kubanga bwe mwatyoolwa. Mukole emirimu emingi egy'amaanyi gye nabawa, ne mu ddakiika eno esembayo. Nneetaaga okutegeera nti awatali nze, temuyinza kukola kintu kyonna, era mukwata enjuyi zonna eza Torah olw'okukkiriza mu nze. Ate era, Abayudaaya ab'erinnya etukuvu bajjudde mu kkanisa yange, nga bateeka emigugu egitasaana ku abo abanoonya obulokozi n'abo abali bange. Kitange yampa erinnya erisinga amannya gonna, erinnya lya Yesu Kristo buli vviivi liryoke livuuname. Mulowooza nti nandyeteekako omugugu ogutali gwa nnono ku mawanga agayogera ennimi nnyingi okukozesa erinnya lyange, Yesuwa HaMashiach, mu Lwebbulaniya lwokka? Mumpulirize era mumpulirize bulungi, kubanga njija, era nja kukola ku batagondera n'abo abandibatawaanyizza.
