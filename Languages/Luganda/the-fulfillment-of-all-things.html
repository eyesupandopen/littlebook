Ogwomunaana 11, 2024

Okutuukirizibwa kw'Ebintu Byonna!

Muwulire Ebigambo bya Mukama wammwe omulamu era Omulokozi, ye Masiya wammwe ow'amazima, ye Yeshua HaMashiach. Mutegeere era muwulirize Ebigambo byange, abaana bange, kubanga kaakano ngenda kwogera ku kutuukirizibwa kw'ebintu byonna, kubanga okutuukirizibwa kwabyo kuli kumpi. Kale mutegeere, kubanga abantu kaakano bagaanye endagaano y'emirembe gya Yakuwa era ne bagaana Yesu Omunazaaleeti (kubanga nze ndagaano yammwe ey'emirembe), obudde ku nsi kaakano bujja kuva mu kisa okudda mu musango, bujja kuva mu kusaasira okudda mu kutya, bujja kuva mu mirembe okudda mu lutalo, bujja kuva mu buwombeefu okudda mu busungu, kaakano bujja kukyuka okudda mu kuwalana eggwanga okw'olubeerera n'okuzikirizibwa okw'olubeerera eri abo abali ku nsi. Kwe kugamba, bonna abagaanye okumfuula Mukama waabwe n'okukkiriza okwagala kwange n'okubasonyiwa kwange, bye nnandibazizzaamu eri Omutonzi waabwe ne mbawa obulamu obutaggwaawo, obutafa nate.

Naye mu kifo ky'omuntu ow'obutonde okujja gye ndi mu bujjuvu, bangi bagaanye mu malala ag'obujeemu ne basalawo okusigala ne Setaani n'obujeemu bwabwe, era olw'ekyo ne beegatta nabo oba mu butamanya, oba nga bamanyi bulungi ensala yaabwe. Kale abo abalonze mu busirusiru obwenkanidde awo, kaakano bajja kuba wansi w'obusungu bwa Katonda n'obusungu bw'Omwana gw'endiga obuli okumpi okuyiibwa mu bujjuvu. Era wadde nga bangi mu kiseera kino bagaanye, ekibiina kinene nnyo kijja kujja gye ndi ne kyatula nti Yesu ye Mukama era bajja kukoowoola erinnya lya Masiya waabwe okutuusa ku nkomerero. Kale ku bw'erinnya lya Yeshua HaMashiach, bajja kulokolebwa. Naye abasinga obungi bajja kuzikirira, kwe kugamba, bonna amannya gaabwe ge gataawandiikibwa mu Kitabo ky'Obulamu okuva ku kutondebwa kw'ensi, era ekyo kye bajja kuba balonzeewo.

Kale mu ngeri y'emu enkyukakyuka zino bwe zibaawo ku nsi mu musango, emirembe gituulidde mu ggulu, era olutalo kaakano lujja kusaasaanira ensi yonna. Waaliwo olutalo mu ggulu era omuwaabi w'abooluganda asuuliddwa wansi, asuuliddwa wansi, era olugwe olumyufu olwo olunene kaakano lusibiddwa ku nsi era lulindiridde omusango gwalyo, era omusango gwalyo tegulwawo. Lujja kuweereza ebigendererwa byalyo mu ntegeka za Yakuwa ezitaggwaawo nga liwanguddwa ddala, kubanga n'abalabe be bawulira eddoboozi lye, era bwe kityo liweereddwa mu ngeri ekomye ennyo era obwakabaka bwalyo bujja kuzikirizibwa ddala. Laba! Omusota ogwo omukadde, ye lugwe olumyufu olwo olunene, kaakano gusuuliddwa ku nsi, era gulina obusungu bungi kubanga gumanyi nti gulina akaseera katono! Kale nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, mbagabira amagezi mwenna mudduke nga muwulira eddoboozi lyange era mukoowoole erinnya lyange ery'amaanyi okutuusa ku nkomerero, kubanga abo abataagala bulamu bwabwe okutuusa ku nkomerero bajja kuzuukukira mu kitiibwa ekitaggwaawo, essanyu, n'empeera, era empeera yaabwe nnene! Kubanga bonna abakoowoola erinnya lyange okutuusa ku nkomerero bajja kulokolebwa, mu lulimi lwonna lwe balikoowoola, era nze Yesu Kristo, era ye Yeshua HaMashiach, ntegeeza ebigambo bino!

Kale endagaano n'ebisuubizo eby'edda bijja kutuukirizibwa, kwe kugamba, endagaano zonna ez'ebiseera byonna, kubanga enjiri etaggwaawo bw'etyo bw'eri. Era olutalo bwe lusaasaanira ensi, emirembe kaakano giteekeddwawo mu ggulu, era eggulu kaakano litegekeddwa okwaniriza abeesigwa mu Kristo, kwe kugamba abo abafudde nga beesigwa mu Kristo, n'abo abalamu abasigaddewo era ababeera mu bulamu bwabwe mu Kristo. Kubanga bonna abatambulira mu tteeka ly'omwoyo gw'obulamu mu Kristo Yesu mu mirembe gyange egy'ekisa bajja kukuŋŋaanyizibwa era baweebwe empeera yaabwe etaggwaawo, era bajja kutegeera era bateekebwe mu mitendera gyabwe okutuukiriza ekigendererwa kya Yakuwa ekitaggwaawo kye baatonderwa obuggya mu Kristo. Kale embaga y'omugole omusajja n'omugole omukazi kaakano ejja kubaawo, era wajja kubaawo okujaguza okunene mu ggulu mu kitiibwa n'ekitiibwa ebitaggwaawo!

Kubanga okuva ku lubereberye, Katonda yatonda Adamu, era okuva mu Adamu n'atonda Kaawa alyoke abeere munne ow'olubeerera era abeere nnyina w'abalamu bonna era babeere abaana era baale. Baalagibwa nti ku miti gyonna egiri mu lusuku lwe Edeni, balina olukusa okulyako ku muti gwonna okuggyako omuti ogw'okumanya obulungi n'obubi. Kale olw'okukemebwa okw'amagezi ag'omusota, Kaawa n'alimbibwa, n'alyako ku kibala ekyagaana, n'alya, n'awa bba Adamu, naye n'alya ng'amanyi, bwe kityo ekibi ne kyonoona abantu era mu kufa baafa okuva olwo. Kale Adamu ne Kaawa tebaatuukiriza kuyitibwa kwabwe okw'okufuga n'okujeema ensi mu butuukirivu, n'okuzaala n'okwala, n'okusaasaanya obutuukirivu ku nsi, naye mu kifo ky'ekyo ne boonoona ne babeera mu mpeera y'ekibi okutuusa empeera eyo lwe yatuuka ku kufa kwabwe era mu kufa abantu baafa okuva olwo. Era mu kuzuukira kw'abatuukirivu bajja kufuna empeera yaabwe n'omugabo gwabwe kubanga baali beesigwa okutuusa ku nkomerero.

Era bwe kityo Yakuwa, mu kumanya kwe okw'olubeerera, yamanya nga tekinnabaawo nti abantu bajja kwesalirawo ekibi n'okufa okuva ku lubereberye. Era El Shaddai, ne mu kwagala kwe okunene kw'ayagalamu abantu, (n'ebitonde bye byonna), yawa ekisa n'okusaasira ebitategeerekeka bwe yalangirira nti ajja kuteekawo obulabe wakati w'ezzadde lya Setaani n'ezzadde ly'omukazi. Ezzadde lya Setaani lyamenya ekisinziiro kyange ku Kalevaaliyo okutuukiriza Ebigambo bya Yakuwa, era kaakano ngenda kubetenta ddala omutwe gw'omusota kubanga eyo y'engeri yagwo ey'omu maaso n'abo bonna abagulondoola, era nga bwe kyawandiikibwa, bwe kityo bwe kijja okukolebwa okutuusa lwe kinaatuukirira.

Naye, Katonda alonze mu magezi ge agatanakoma okubikkula entegeka ze ez'ekisa, okusaasira, n'emirembe olw'okununulibwa okujjuvu okw'abantu mu bitundutundu, naye bulijjo awadde essuubi eddene eri abatuukirivu nti olunaku lumu mu bbanga ttono bajja kuzuukizibwa mu bulamu obuggya okuva mu bafu obutafa nate, era bajja kubeera mu bungi n'emirembe ennaku zonna ez'obulamu bwabwe era nga ku nnaku ezo tewaliba nkomerero. Okumala emyaka mingi abeesigwa bafudde n'okukkiriza okujjuvu mu Yakuwa okutuukiriza ebisuubizo bye era bafudde nga beesiga endagaano za Yakuwa ng'essuubi lyabwe ery'obulokozi era endagaano zonna eza Yakuwa zijja kutuukirizibwa mu bujjuvu mu maaso ga Kristo Yesu, ye nze, Mukama wammwe eyazuukira era Omulokozi. Kale ne bakkiriza Katonda era kyabalirwa okuba obutuukirivu, eri bonna abakkiriza ne baba n'essuubi mu zzadde eryasuubizibwa ery'omukazi.

Kale entegeka ya Yakuwa ey'ebiseera byonna ey'okununulibwa okujjuvu okw'abantu bulijjo ebadde etunuulidde nze, Kristo Yesu, ye Yesu Omunazaaleeti era ye Yeshua HaMashiach kubanga nze Mukama ow'Amaka agaalinnyisibwa mu ggulu, ye Mwana gw'endiga owa Katonda era eyaleetera Omuyudaaya n'Omunnaggwanga bombi obulokozi obutaggwaawo, era nja kulokola Yakobo okuva mu abo abaazingako ensi ey'ekisuubizo era okuzikirizibwa kwabwe tekulwawo. Nze oyo empologoma mw'eneegalamirira n'omwana gw'endiga kubanga mu nze empologoma ey'ekika kya Yuda n'omwana gw'endiga owa Katonda bijja kutuukirizibwa mu bujjuvu. Kubanga nze mpologoma ey'ekika kya Yuda era nze Mwana gw'endiga owa Katonda kubanga nze Kabaka wa Bakabaka era Mukama wa Bakama era nze Kabona Asinga Obukulu emirembe gyonna ng'engeri ya Merukizeddeeki bw'eri.

Kale Adamu ne Kaawa ne babeera beesigwa okuva olwo mu maaso ga Mukama Katonda waabwe, ne bazaala era ne baala, naye Setaani yanditegese omulumbaganyi omulala n'obujeemu bw'abalabirizi abagwa. Beegatta ku bujeemu bwa Setaani era okumatiza okwegomba kwabwe ne baleka ekitiibwa eky'olubeerera eky'embeera yaabwe eyasooka, gye baatonderwa, okweyonoona n'abakazi, n'omubiri n'omusaayi, kubanga bandisigadde mu kitiibwa eky'olubeerera naye mu kifo ky'ekyo ne beesalirawo okubonerezebwa kwabwe okw'olubeerera balyoke batuukirize okwegomba kwabwe ku lwawo era n'okufa kw'ekitonde ekyaliwo mu kiseera ekyo.

Kale bamalayika bano abagwa ne bayigiriza omuntu agudde engeri y'okwezikiriza ng'ayita mu lulyo lwa Kayini era n'okuyita mu kumanya n'enkwe ezaagaanibwa ze baayigiriza abantu. Beefuula bakatonda ne beetaaga okusinzibwa era ne BEETWALIRA abakazi ku mpaka okuva mu bonna be baalonda, era abakazi bangi ne beewaayo ku lulagiriro lwa Setaani balyoke bakeme era basendasende abalabirizi mu bibi byabwe eby'okwegomba bye beesibako ekikolimo kyabwe eky'olubeerera kubanga baakolagana ebibi byabwe.

Era abalabirizi n'ezzadde lyabwe ne bonoona ensi n'ebirimu byonna era enkyukakyuka ez'enjawulo eza jeneetiki ne zikolebwa ne zisaasaanira ensi kubanga baayonoona omubiri gwonna era okwegomba kwabwe tekwalina nkomerero, era beefuula bakatonda, ne bakatonda abatonda naye mu butuufu tebaliiko kye bali wabula beeyita abataliiko kye bali abaayetaba mu kuvumbaganya okw'ekivve wakati w'ebika. Kale ensi ne yonooneka ddala era n'efugibwa Banefuli, abaalyanga abantu n'eby'abantu. Era bonna abasigala nga tebonooneddwa mu biseera bya Nuuwa (kubanga Nuuwa yali mutuukirivu era mugolokofu era n'atambulanga ne Yakuwa era n'alaba ekisa mu maaso ga Yakuwa) baali emyoyo munaana egyalokolebwa nga miramu n'ensolo Yakuwa ze yaleeta mu lyato olw'obulokozi bw'ebika byazo. Kale Katonda n'asaanyaawo ensi n'obwonoonefu bwonna okuva mu nsi eyaakafa, era abalabirizi tebalina mirembe, wadde okusonyiyibwa era omusango gwabwe omukakali gujja kubeererawo emirembe gyonna kubanga bagusaanyira. Era si balabirizi bokka, naye bonna abeegatta ku bujeemu bwa Setaani bajja kubonerezebwa mu ngeri ng'eyo era babonerezebwe emirembe gyonna mu nnyanja ennene ey'omuliro ogw'ekibiriiti ogwaka ennyiriri ezisinga okwokya n'obulongoofu bw'okubonerezebwa.

Oluvannyuma lw'amataba, Katonda n'ateekawo ensi eriwo kaakano, era n'agiteekawo mu butuukirivu. Era Yakuwa n'alagaana ne Nuuwa endagaano, Yakuwa n'ayita Nuuwa okusitula obutuukirivu ku nsi n'okuzaala n'okwala ezzadde eryasuubizibwa ery'omukazi liryoke lijje. Yakuwa yasuubiza obutaddamu kusaanyaawo nsi, era newankubadde abantu baali babi, Katonda yakolera wamu n'abantu okukuuma n'okuwa omukisa emiryango egy'abatuukirivu ku nsi nze Yeshua HaMashiach mwe nnava.

Katonda n'abawa omukisa okuzaala n'okwala n'okujjuza ensi, era Katonda n'asuubiza omuntu n'ensolo nti tajja kuddamu kuzikiriza buli kintu kiramu ku nsi n'amazzi era El Shaddai tajja kuddamu kusaanyaawo nsi yonna naye ajja kukuumira ensi, n'okukuumira abantu n'ezzadde lye, era ensi eno ekumibwa okuva ku lunaku olwo olw'endagaano Kitaffe ow'Ebitangaala lwe yateeka omusoke gwe mu ggulu ng'akabonero era ekijjukizo ky'endagaano ye ne Nuuwa. Kubanga kino bakimanyi nga tebakyagala, nti ku kigambo kya Katonda eggulu lyaliwo okuva edda, n'ensi ng'eva mu mazzi era ng'eri mu mazzi: Ensi eyaliwo mu kiseera ekyo, bwe yasaanyizibwawo n'amazzi, n'ezikirira: Naye eggulu n'ensi, ebiriwo kaakano, ku kigambo kye kimu biterekebwa, ne biteresebwa omuliro okutuusa ku lunaku lw'omusango n'okuzikirira kw'abantu abatali ba Katonda.

Kale Yakuwa n'ayimusa Aburamu n'afuuka Ibulayimu, n'asuubiza Aburamu nti erinnya lye terikyaba Aburamu naye Ibulayimu era nti ajja kumufuula Kitaawe w'amawanga mangi era nti ajja kuwa Ibulayimu n'ezzadde lye ensi ya Kanani okuba obutaka obw'olubeerera era nti Yakuwa ajja kuba Katonda waabwe! Kale Ibulayimu n'afuuka Kitaawe w'amawanga mangi, Yakuwa ge yasuubiza mu ndagaano. Kaakano, ebisuubizo byakolebwa eri Ibulayimu n'ezzadde lye. Tagamba nti, Era eri enzigi, ng'ez'abangi; naye ng'ey'omu, Era eri ezzadde lyo, ye Kristo. Kale omutuukirivu ajja kubeera mulamu olw'okukkiriza!

Kale abaana ne bagwa mu buddu e Misiri oluvannyuma lw'abaana b'ekisuubizo okugendayo wansi w'obukuumi n'obuyambi bwa Yusufu kubanga Yusufu yafuga mu butuukirivu wansi wa Falaawo eyali omukulu okusinga Yusufu ku ntebe, naye Yusufu yaweereza NZE NDI! Ne wajjawo falaawo ataamanya Yusufu, era omu omuzibu guno ne gujooga era ne gugezaako okutta olunyiriri lwa Kristo nate, ye baana b'ekisuubizo. Era Yakuwa n'ayimusa omununuzi Omwebbulaniya okuva mu nnyumba ya Falaawo, ye oyo eyaggyibwa mu mazzi n'alokolebwa ng'omulamu olw'ekisa kya Yakuwa. Ye Musa yennyini eyandikulembedde abaana b'ekisuubizo okuva mu buddu okudda mu ddembe ery'ekitiibwa ery'ensi ey'ekisuubizo, kubanga be baana b'ekisuubizo era nja kubazuukiza okuva mu tulo twabwe era mpeere abeesigwa n'abatuukirivu empeera nnyingi, kwe kugamba abo abasigala nga beesigwa okutuusa ku nkomerero. Kale Musa n'anunula abaana ba Isirayiri okuva mu mukono gw'abajoozi baabwe era bonna abaabagoberera ne bafiira mu nnyanja, kubanga ani ayinza okuyimirira mu maaso ga El Shaddai?

Kale Yakuwa n'akulembera Musa n'abaana b'ekisuubizo okugenda ku lusozi Sinaayi Yakuwa n'awa Musa ebiragiro, amateeka n'ebiragiro ebiri mu mateeka abaana bonna bagondere. Era emikisa n'ebikolimo byateekebwawo olw'okugondera n'obutagondera. Era Yakuwa bwe yamala okuwa Musa endagaano n'amateeka agali mu biragiro okugondera, Musa n'alyoka akakasa endagaano n'agifuula Endagaano empya ng'ayiwa omusaayi. Era Musa n'addira ekitundu ky'omusaayi, n'aguteeka mu bibya, n'ekitundu ky'omusaayi n'agumansira ku Kyoto. N'addira ekitabo ky'endagaano, n'asoma mu maaso g'abantu: ne bagamba nti, Byonna Mukama by'ayogedde, tujja kubikola, era tujja kugonda. Era Musa n'addira omusaayi n'agumansira ku bantu, n'agamba nti, Laba omusaayi gw'Endagaano Mukama gy'alagaanye nammwe, ku bigambo bino byonna. Era, n'amansira n'omusaayi ku Weema, n'ebintu byonna eby'Obuweereza. Era kumpi ebintu byonna bitukuzibwa n'omusaayi mu tteeka: era awatali kuyiwa musaayi tewali kusonyiyibwa bibi.

Kale ebyafaayo ne bituukirira nga bwe byawandiikibwa, era mu kiseera kyakyo eggwanga lya Isirayiri ne liteekebwawo mu bujjuvu, naye waali tewannabaawo kifo kya Yakuwa kya kubeeramu mu bantu. Era Yakuwa n'ayimusa Kabaka omutuukirivu, ye musajja ow'omutima gwa Katonda! Mu kiseera kyakyo Yakuwa n'atuma Nasan Nabbi eri Dawudi n'amugamba nti, Ennaku zo bwe zirituukirira, n'oweebaka ne bajjajjaabo, ndiyimirizaawo ezzadde lyo eririkuddirira, eririva mu lubuto lwo, era ndinyweza obwakabaka bwe. Alinzimbira ennyumba erinnya lyange, era ndinyweza entebe ey'obwakabaka bwe emirembe gyonna. Nnaabeeranga kitaawe, naye anaabeeranga mwana wange. Era ennyumba yo n'obwakabaka bwo binaanywezebwanga emirembe gyonna mu maaso go: entebe yo eneenywezebwanga emirembe gyonna. Ng'ebigambo bino byonna bwe biri, era ng'okwolesebwa kuno kwonna bwe kuli, bwe kityo Nasan bwe yayogera ne Dawudi.

Bwe kityo abantu baagobereranga Yakuwa okuva mu ndagaano emu okudda mu ndagaano endala, nga bongerako ku ndagaano eyafuulibwa entuufu ng'eyita mu ssaddaaka y'ensolo era endagaano ne yongerwako ku biragiro. Naye, wadde abantu baafubanga nnyo, tebaasobolanga na maanyi gaabwe kuwenkana na butuukirivu bwa Katonda, wadde okutuukiriza amateeka ago amatuufu era amagolokofu ga Musa ge nze njagala era ge nnatuukiriza. Buli muntu yenna bw'anaakolimiranga amateeka ga Musa n'endagaano ennyingi eza Yakuwa, naye akolimirwe. Kubanga amateeka malungi, matuufu era matukuvu n'ebisuubizo byonna ebya Yakuwa bye yee era amiina. Naye, amateeka gaaleetebwa nga omusomesa okubaleeta eri Kristo. Kubanga nze, Yesu Omunazaaleeti, gwe Yakuwa yawa okutuukiriza ekyo abantu kye batasobola olw'ekibi ekyayisibwa okuva mu mirembe gyonna okuva mu musaayi gwa Adamu, ogwayononeka ennaku zonna. Kubanga amateeka kye gaalemwa okukola, kubanga gaali manafu olw'omubiri: ekyo Katonda yakikola, n'atuma Omwana we mu kifaananyi ky'omubiri ogw'ekibi.

Bwe kityo mu kutuuka kw'ebiseera, nze Yesu Omunazaaleeti, nnazaalibwa omukazi okusinziira ku byetaago by'amateeka ga Musa era nga okutuukirizibwa kw'endagaano, era nze ndi ezzadde essuubize ery'omukazi Yakuwa yennyini lye yategeeza edda emyaka emingi egiyise. Kubanga Katonda bwe yayagala ensi, n'awaayo Omwana we omu yekka, buli muntu yenna amukkiriza aleme okubula, wabula afune obulamu obutaggwaawo. Bwe ntyo nze ndi mwana omu yekka owa Katonda, ezzadde eryo essuubize bannabbi lye baalagula okuva edda okutuuka kaakano. Era nze oyo eyateekebwawo okutuukiriza ebintu byonna era mu nze, Kristo Yesu, ebintu byonna n'ebisuubizo byonna birituukiririzibwa so tewaliba kisuubizo na kimu kirireme kutuukirira, naye byonna birituukirizibwa lubeerera.

Kubanga kyawandiikibwa mu Abaebbulaniya gye nnawandiisiza Pawulo nti: Kubanga oba ng'omusaayi gw'ente ennume n'embuzi ennume, n'evvu ly'ente enduusi erimansibwa ku abo abatalongoofu, litukuza okutuusa ku kulongoosebwa kw'omubiri: Omusaayi gwa Kristo gw'asobola nnyo okusingawo, eyeyawaayo olw'Omwoyo ogw'emirembe gyonna, nga talina bbala, eri Katonda, okutukuza emyoyo gyammwe okuva mu bikolwa ebifu, okweteekateeka okuweereza Katonda omulamu? Era ku lw'ensonga eno ye Mutabaganya w'Endagaano Empya, nga bwe yafa, olw'okununula ebikolwa ebyasobya ebyali wansi w'Endagaano eyasooka, abo abayite, basobole okufuna ekisuubizo ky'obusika obutaggwaawo.

Kale mutegeere nti mu nze, Kristo Yesu, ebisuubizo eby'edda bikuumibwa, n'endagaano ez'edda zikuumibwa, n'endagaano ey'edda ekuumibwa era etuukirizibwa kubanga nze kutuukirizibwa kwabyo okutuukiridde. Era naye wakyaliwo ebisuubizo ebirina okutuukirizibwa era kati bye biseera by'okutuukirizibwa. Kubanga ebiseera we biri, lwe nnaazuukiza mu ngeri ey'ekikutuko abo abaalekaana mu Kristo, era nnaakungaanya mu ngeri ey'ekikutuko abo abakyali abalamu ne basigalawo okunsisinkana mu bire, era mu ngeri ey'ekikutuko tunaagattibwa mu kisenge ky'entebe ya Yakuwa yennyini. Kubanga mmwe Ekanisa yange era omugole wange nammwe muli kutuukirizibwa kwa kisuubizo kya ndagaano era mwazaalibwa omulundi ogw'okubiri okuva mu nsigo etevunda ne mununulibwa n'ebisuubizo ebingi ebiri mu Ndagaano yange Empya, ye ndagaano empya etwaliramu buli kisuubizo ky'endagaano ez'edda era n'eyongerako ebisuubizo ebikulu ennyo era eby'omuwendo omungi mwe mununulirwa okufuna obulamu obutaggwaawo n'empeera etaggwaawo era tulibeera ffembi lubeerera.

Mutegeere nti obulamu bwe mwabeeramu mu mubiri, era mmwe abeesigwa bange era abalonde, obulamu obwo bukomye ku kununulibwa kwammwe okujjuvu. Mutegeere nti mufaananyizibwa nange ddala, Kristo Yesu, ku buli mutendera. Bwe nnakomererwa olw'ebibi byammwe, nammwe mwakomererwa wamu nange. Bwe nnafiira ku muti ogwo omukakanyavu, nammwe mwafiira wamu nange. Bwe nnazuukizibwa mu balamu oluvannyuma lw'ennaku ssatu emisana n'ekiro mu mutima gw'ensi, bwe mutyo nammwe mwazuukizibwa okubeera mu bulamu obuggya, era bwe nnawanisibwa mu ggulu ery'eggulu, bwe mutyo nammwe mwawanisibwa wamu nange era mutudde mu bifo eby'omu ggulu. Kubanga nze nze Okuyitako kwammwe okutuukiridde, era nze eyabaddira mu kifo mu kusalirwa omusango n'ekibonerezo ekibagwanidde. Era nze, Yesu Omunazaaleeti, nnawanunula kyeyagalire okuva mu kikolimo ky'amateeka kubanga olw'omubiri gwammwe ogw'ekibi temusobola kutuukiriza mateeka na maanyi gammwe.

Mutegeere nti sajja kumenya mateeka, newakubadde endagaano, naye okutuukiriza byonna mu biseera by'okutuukirizibwa. Buli muntu yenna bw'anaakolimiranga amateeka ga Musa n'endagaano ennyingi eza Yakuwa, naye akolimirwe. Kubanga amateeka malungi, matuufu era matukuvu, n'ebisuubizo byonna ebya Yakuwa bye yee era amiina. Naye, amateeka gaaleetebwa nga omusomesa okubaleeta eri Kristo. Kubanga nze, Yesu Omunazaaleeti, gwe Yakuwa yawa okutuukiriza ekyo abantu kye batasobola olw'ekibi ekyayisibwa okuva mu mirembe gyonna okuva mu musaayi gwa Adamu, ogwayononeka ennaku zonna. Kubanga amateeka kye gaalemwa okukola, kubanga gaali manafu olw'omubiri: ekyo Katonda yakikola, n'atuma Omwana we mu kifaananyi ky'omubiri ogw'ekibi.

Buli kisuubizo kinaatera okutuukirizibwa ddala mu nze Kristo Yesu era nnaayingira mu ndagaano y'obufumbo n'Omugole wange era bonna wamu banaafugiranga nange mu busika bwange era, nga mbakulembedde era nga tuli wamu, banaawonyanga ensi ne bagenda ne bafuganga nga bakabona era nga bakabaka mu nsi era buli ndagaano erituukirizibwa okuva ku Adamu okutuuka ku Musa mu Maaso ga Yesu Kristo. Bino bye bisuubizo by'obulamu obutaggwaawo ebirituukirizibwa mu batuukirivu, be bonna abo abalifuulibwa omuntu omu omuggya mu Kristo Yesu. Kubanga bonna balitondebwa buto ddala ne baweebwa empeera etaggwaawo olw'omugabo gwabwe era byonna biriba yee era amiina. Kubanga buli yodi, na buli katuganti ka yodi birituukirizibwa ddala ennaku zonna.

Mutegeere nti mmwe muli yeekaalu ya Katonda omulamu, Ekanisa yange ey'obwesigwa era nnabatonda ng'ennyumba etazimbibwangako mikono mulyoke mubeere ennyumba eteriggwaawo eya Katonda ataggwaawo, ye Yakuwa Kitammwe. Era nnaawasa omugole wange ne mbeera naye lubeerera mu ndagaano eteriggwaawo, nti we ndi, nabo we balibeera, era ennaku zonna baliba nga nze, Kristo Yesu Mukama waabwe ataggwaawo. Era mutegeere abange abeesigwa nti ebiseera by'okwawukana biri ku nkomerero era tewalibaawo kwawukana nate wakati wa Yakuwa n'abaana be abaagalwa era tewalibaawo kwawukana nate wakati wa Kristo Yesu Mukama wammwe ataggwaawo n'abo be njagala ennyo. Mutegeere nti tulitambula ng'omuntu omu so tewalibaawo kibi newakubadde ekiziyiza. Mulikola ebikolwa ebingi eby'amaanyi ebitannayingira mu mitima gya bantu. Muliba bakabaka bange ab'amaanyi era bakabona bange be nze Kabaka wa Bakabaka era Mukama wa Bakama era tulifuga ensi mu butuukirivu.

Era abalala bangi balizuukizibwa mu balamu ku nkomerero ya ssabbiiti ya Danyeri ey'amakumi asatu era bonna baliweebwa empeera ng'obwesigwa bwabwe bwe bwabanga. Era bonna abafudde nga beesigwa nga basuubira nti Mukama waabwe ne Masiya talireka myoyo gyabwe mu ntaana balizuukukira mu bulamu obuggya n'okuddizibwawo okutaggwaawo. Kubanga nnaabagatta ku baagalwa baabwe era nnaabaddiza ebyaliirwa enzige era nnaabaddiza Sitani bye yababba mu biseera we baali abagwira ku nsi nga balindirira okununulibwa kwabwe okujjuvu okuva mu nsi eno egudde era embi.

Bwe gityo bwe guli Enjiri eteriggwaawo era ennaku zonna ebintu byonna birituukirizibwa mu butuukirivu, mu butukuvu, ne mu mazima. Kubanga tewali kintu na kimu kiriyingira mu miryango gy'obwakabaka bwange ekitali kirongoofu oba ekibi oba ekyagala n'okukola obulimba, naye obufuzi bwange buliba mu mazima n'obutuukirivu obutaggwaawo. Kale wuliriza eddoboozi lya Yeshua HaMashiach, mmwe mwenna abo be bigambo bino biweerezeddwa. Mukama wammwe ataggwaawo teyeerabidde bisuubizo by'Edda so tewali ndagaano yonna eyayonooneka. Naye byonna birituukirizibwa era mazima, ebisinga obungi byamala dda okutuukirizibwa kale mwetegereze ebyo mufune essuubi. Kubanga nga bwe kyawandiikibwa bwe kirikolebwa era bwe gityo bwe guli Enjiri eteriggwaawo mu biseera bino eby'okutuukirizibwa.

Nze Yeshua HaMashiach ntumye ebigambo bino ng'eyita mu muweereza wange era ng'eyita mu muweereza wange ebigambo bino bitumiddwa.

Nze gwe baligamba nti empologoma eneegalamiranga wamu n'omwana gw'endiga.

Nze ndi empologoma y'ekika kya Yuda.

Nze ndi Omwana gw'endiga owa Katonda.

Nze ndi Kabaka wa Bakabaka era Mukama wa Bakama.

Nze ndi Kabona Asinga Obukulu lubeerera ng'okulondebwa kwa Merukizeddeeki bwe kuli.

Kale mbawa okutegeera nti okutuukirizibwa kw'ebintu byonna kutuuse era kugenda mu maaso. Abo ab'omugole wange n'omubiri gwange balisinzanga era balibeera mu bulamu obuggya awamu n'abo abalizuukizibwa mu butuukirivu ku kuzuukira kw'abatuukirivu oluvannyuma lwa ssabbiiti ya Danyeri ey'amakumi asatu. Era mu butuukirivu n'emirembe tulibeera mu mirembe n'ebitonde bya Katonda era walibaawo emirembe emiwangaazi era eteriggwaawo eri abaana b'ekisuubizo, era baliweerezagananga, era balimpeereza nze, era baliweereza Yakuwa era mu mirembe n'obumu ebitonde bya Yakuwa biriddizibwaawo.

Kubanga wakyaliwo okuwummula eri abantu ba Katonda. Kale omulembe gwange ogw'ekisa bwe gunaatera okukoma, omulembe ogusembayo gusembedde, baana bange, era olunaku olwo olw'enkomerero lwe tulibaawo ffenna okuwummula okuva mu kutegana kwaffe n'ebizibu byaffe, era tulirya ebisava eby'ensi nga tuwummudde ku lunaku lw'okuwummula era walibaawo olunaku lwa Ssabbiiti olw'emirembe. Wabaddewo ennaku mukaaga ez'okukola, kubanga olunaku olumu eri Mukama lulinga emyaka lukumi n'emyaka lukumi ng'olunaku olumu. Bwe kityo, mu bufuzi bwange obw'emyaka olukumi abeesigwa balimala olunaku olw'okuwummula ne Mutonzi waabwe, Kitabwe, era Katonda ataggwaawo nga Yakuwa tannatonda ggulu ggympi na nsi mpya omulituula obutuukirivu obutaggwaawo bwokka.
