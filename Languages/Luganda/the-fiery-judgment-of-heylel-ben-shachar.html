Umujuli Omubiri: Isiraeri
Ekyongerezwako
Novemba 7, 2025

Omusango gw'Omuliro ogwa Heylel Ben Shachar

Muwulire, Muwulire Ebigambo bya Adonai-YHVH era mukung'aane okumpi n'eddoboozi lyange, mulyoke mutuuule ku bigere byange, mmwe abaana bange, era muwulire Ebigambo byange bye nfulumya okuva mu mwoyo gw'omubaka wange ow'amaanyi era omuweereza wange. Ebigambo ebyaweebwa n'obuyinza obungi mu Mwana wange omu yekka eyazaalibwa, eyateeka akabonero ku Bigambo bino eby'amaanyi mu mubaka waffe era omuweereza waffe gwe nnawa Kristo Yesu ng'ekibya ekironde, okutuula ku mukono gwe ogwa kkono mu kitiibwa eky'emirundi ebiri ng'omusumba ow'okwagala era omukuumi ajjudde ekisa kubanga yakuzibwa Omusumba Omukulu ye Yeshua HaMashiach era n'ajjuzibwa okwagala kwange mu kusaasira okutaggwaawo n'ekisa ebyaweebwa mu mwana gw'endiga owa Adonai-YHVH mu kwagala kwe mwe yagingamu n'aweesa Biblaridon:144 Yeenyini, nga bwe yamaliriza ddala mu bonna ab'amaanyi be 144,000 abali abaana ba Yeshua HaMashiach be yakuza ye yennyini n'omukono gwe, era tewali kusosola mu bantu mu makubo ga Adonai-YHVH era Yeshua HaMashiach ye Kigambo kya Adonai-YHVH ekyafuuka omubiri. N'olwekyo, mutegeere, mmwe mwenna 144,000: Muli baana ba Kitange Yeshua, era NZE ndi Adonai-YHVH wammwe ali Kitammwe mwenna mu Kristo Yesu. Selah.

N’olwekyo, mutegeere okuteekerateekera kwange okw’olubeerera n’obuyinza bwange obw’obwakabaka nti NZE NDI oyo asobola okugolola abalwanyi ab’amaanyi Yeshua HaMashiach balagire, oyo kati ayimiridde mu buyinza obujjuvu obuwulirwa era ng'aweereddwa amagezi mu bujjuvu era ng'atambuliziddwa okununula abeesigwa mu Kristo Yesu ng'Empologoma y'Ekika kya Yuda okuva wakati, ng’aleetawo okuzikirira okw’amangu bwe yakomyawo essaawa emu n’ensolo ku kugenda kwammwe (mu ngeri ey’amangu ennyo) mu kiseera ekyo ekyamuweebwa mu bujjuvu mu mikono gye okutuukiriza mu bugonvu bwonna obutuukirivu era obw'essanyu, era ye Yeshua HaMashiach akola bulijjo ebyo Mukama we Adonai-YHVH by'ayagala.

Kwe kugamba, Biblaridon:144 era kye kyokulwanyisa eky'amaanyi era ekirimu enjuyi nnyingi mu mukono gwa Yeshua HaMashiach ali mu nkolagana ennungi n'ebigendererwa bye nga bwe byabikkuliddwa okutuusa kati n'emirembe gyonna, oyo era ali omukozi w'omusango ogulangirirwa era oguterezebwa, okukozesebwa ku kiragiro okukuba, era n'okutumibwa okulangirira n'okutartbinkiriza ddala nga bwe yalangirirwa wansi w'obuyinza obujjuvu obw'Empologoma y'Ekika kya Yuda. Mazima, Biblaridon kye kitala ekiri mu kiraato ekikwatiddwa mu mukono gwa Yeshua HaMashiach okusowolwa mu bujjuvu mu nnaku musanvu eza Daniels, era n'olw'ebigendererwa byange eby'olubeerera ebitannabikkulwa bwe mulikung'aanyizibwa gyendi mu kutemyatemyatema kw'eriiso gye ndibabikkulira obuntu bwammwe obw'amazima n'ebigendererwa ebyakwekebwa kaakano mu Kristo Yesu, naye kati nga byoleseddwa kitundu.

Mutegeere abaana bange nti abeesigwa bonna mu Kristo Yesu abali ekkanisa ye, omubiri gwe, era n'omugole we, balondebwa mu ngeri ey'enjawulo mu Kristo Yesu emisingi gy'ensi nga tennaateekebwawo. Newankubadde abangi bavvuunula Ebigambo bino n'ebigambo ebisiru nga "eyasiimibwa ennyo era omukisa ddala" okuwa omubiri okubudaabudibwa bwe byogerwa awatali kukkiriza era awatali kutegeera mazima gange ag'olubeerera, ekyo si kye kigendererwa ekijjuvu naye mazima ddala eri abeesigwa mu Kristo olw'obuwulize bwabwe n'okwenenya kwabwe.

Mutegeere, ekigambo "okulondebwa mu ye emisingi gy'ensi nga tennaateekebwawo" kiraga nti Adonai-YHVH wammwe yabalonda Yeenyini n'abalung'amya mu maanyi g'okwagala amakwafukira ddala n'okutegeka okujjuvu okw'ekigendererwa, nga tayonoona kudda kwa muntu yenna na katono, ng'alonda, ng'akola, era ng'atonda obupya mu Kristo Yesu buli omu ku mmwe n'okutegeera okujjuvu n'obuyinza obujjuvu obw'obukugu n'okutegeka n'okufaayo okungi ennyo ku tunsela nsela mu buli omu ku mmwe, ekyetaaga emyaka gyonna okuva ku Adamu ne Kaawa okutuuka kati okutuukiriza mu butuukirivu bwange.

Muli bitonde byange eby'ekitalo ebyatondebwa mu Kristo!

Obukugu obwo, obutukuvu n’entegeka bisigibwa mu nsengekera yammwe gye nnazironda mu ngeri ey’olukujjukujju n’obukugu buli kanu na kali ku mmwe mwenna olw’ebigendererwa byange eby’olubeerera ebigenda okubikkulwa mu bujjuvu mu ntebe yange ey’obwakabaka, amangu ago era n’amaanyi ku kiragiro kyange. N’olwekyo, mutegeere nti nabumba, nnakola, era nnatonda buli omu ku mmwe mu ngeri ey’enjawulo era mu tunsela nsela nnyo ne mutayinza kutegeera kye mugenda okukyusibwamu okusinziira ku bigendererwa byange eby’enjawulo ddala era ebirondeddwa eri buli omu ku mmwe.

N’olwekyo, temutya, Adonai-YHVH wammwe takola muntu omu mu ngeri ey’enjawulo okusinga omulala nsobole okufuna omukisa ogulabika ne gujaguza mu kibi eky’amalala, wadde ekiremwa kimu kiryoke kifuge ekirala mu lukwe olw’ekibi n’obusirusiru obw’obukambwe, wabula ebigendererwa byange eby’olubeerera biryoke bituukirire mu mmwe mwenna. Ku mazima ag'olubeerera: Tekinnayingira mu mutima gw'omuntu mu kiseera ky'okuwandiika kuno kye mulibeera nga mukyusiddwa era n'ebigendererwa ki. Kyenva mbasaba mu kwagala kwange gye muli (mmwe be nnatonda era be nnalonda olw’okukkiriza kwammwe, mmwe abaana bange abeenenya mu bwesigwa) okuba n’okukkiriza kwokka ng’akaliga k’akaladaala ne mutegeera nti okuva ku muto okutuuka ku mukulu mu Isiraeri wa Adonai-YHVH mwenna muli batukuvu era muli ba kitalo era mwakolebwa n’amaanyi n’obuyinza ne kiba nti ye Isiraeri wa Adonai-YHVH ajja okwewuunyizibwa abantu bonna.

Emirembe gibeere gye muli mu bujjuvu.

Era kino nga tewali kusosola mu bantu, naye mu kifo ky'ekyo olw'obutuukirivu bwange n'okumanya kwange okw'olubeerera okw'obuyinza bwonna n'okufuga kwange okutereevu okw'ebintu byonna gye nnabalonda buli omu ku mmwe mu ngeri eyo olw'ebigendererwa byange eby'olubeerera mu Kristo Yesu. Kubanga nnamanya mu bujjuvu mu biseera eby’edda buli omu ku mmwe, ku bubwe, nti yandijja mu bujjuvu eri Kristo Yesu mu biseera byammwe, kyennava nteekateeka. N’olwekyo, mutegeere nti amakubo agabikkuddwa gabikkulwa olw’obujulizi n’ekyokulabirako eky’okuweereza mu Biblaridon:144, ebintu bino bikolebwa mulyoke mutandike okutegeera obunene bw’obusika bwammwe n’ebigendererwa eby’olubeerera mwe nnabatondera, ne nnabalonda, ne nnabakola mwenna olw’entegeka ey’amagezi okutuuka ku nsela ennyo. Musanyuke!

N'olwekyo, mujje mu bujjuvu eri Yeshua HaMashiach, newankubadde mmwe mwenna abeesigwa mu Kristo Yesu, mulyoke mufune empeera yammwe ejjuvu eya mirundi ebiri mu kitiibwa ekijjuvu, gy'alina mu mukono gwe gye nnawa eri bonna abawangula mu ngeri y'emu nga Kristo Yesu bwe yawangula. Muwulire ebigambo byange eby'okubazzaamu amaanyi, newankubadde mmwe mwenna 144,000, abali abaana abalondeddwa aba Kitammwe Yeshua, era ajja kugabana nammwe ebintu byonna abaana be, abali n'abawanguzi be abeesigwa, abawangudde ebintu byonna nga Yeshua HaMashiach bwe yawangula. Kyokka, bano si be bawanguzi bokka abajja okuba nga bawangudde ebintu byonna ebibalumba, kubanga bangi nnyo mu beesigwa mu Kristo Yesu abatali 144,000 bawangudde ebintu byonna mu ngeri y'emu nga Kristo Yesu, n'empeera yaabwe nnene!

Weewaawo! Waliwo abawanguzi bangi mu Isiraeri wa Adonai-YHVH era tewali kusosola mu bantu eri Adonai-YHVH, era naddala Yeshua HaMashiach akola bulijjo mu ngeri etuukiridde ebyo Adonai-YHVH we by'ayagala. N'olwekyo, temweraliikirira ne mutaawaanyizibwa bwe mufuba okutegeera ebyakwekebwa mu Ggulu ebitannayingira mu bujjuvu mu mutima gw'omuntu mu kiseera kino, era naddala mwewale okweragerageranya nga bwe kiri mu mbeera yammwe ey'okugwa kati, naye nga mutukuziddwa mu bujjuvu mu Kristo Yesu.

Kale mutegeere, mu Bwakabaka obw’omu Ggulu, omuntu yenna agulumizibwa okusinga omulala kikolebwa mu buyinza, n/oba empeera olw’ebigendererwa byange byokka, naye si lwa kumugulumiza nsobole okuleetera omulala mu kitonde kyange okuwulira ng’omunafu, oba abo abayinza okugulumizibwa balyoke beerarike mu kibi ku mulala. Naye mu kifo ky’ekyo, ekitiibwa n’okwagala bibaaweebwa mu bujjuvu. Kubanga nnabatonda mwenna, era naddala abeesigwa mu Kristo Yesu olw’obuwulize bwammwe mu Kristo Yesu nsobole okubatonza obupya n’ekitiibwa ekijjuvu. Mmwe, naddala, muli bitonde byange eby'ekitalo ebyatondebwa mu Kristo Yesu okutuuka ku bikorwa ebirungi mulyoke mutambulirenga mu byo mu maanyi amangi olw'ebigendererwa eby'olubeerera ebya Adonai-YHVH wammwe, ebyaweebwa mu Kristo Yesu. Musanyuke!

Era bwe kityo NZE NDI Omukulu, era Kitange w'omugole omusajja, ye Yeshua HaMashiach, amaze okukoowoola Omugole n'abagenyi be ku Ttumbi, era beeteeseteese ne bajjuza ettaala zaabwe amafuta g'omwoyo omutukuvu era n'entambi zammwe zisaziddwako ebitali birongoofu byonna n'ettaala zammwe zikoleezeddwa ennimiro ezo ennungi ezikoleddwa amafuta ago amalongoofu ag'omwoyo gwange ge nnawa mu Yeshua HaMashiach, era nti yakola mu ngeri ey'enjawulo munda mu buli omu ku mmwe, abeesigwa mu Kristo.

Era n’olwekyo, nkubiriza abeesigwa bonna mu Kristo Yesu okweyongera okugondera mu bujjuvu n’obwegendereza mu bintu byonna Omusumba waabwe Omukulu afaayo ku buli kimu eky’obulamu bwammwe mu nsela nsela n’obutuukirivu obusinga. Mujje mu bujjuvu eri Mukama wammwe ow'Obutuukirivu obw'olubeerera gwe nnabateerawo mwenna Yesu Omunazaaleeti bwe yeewaayo ng'ekiweebwayo kya Pasika ekituukiridde eky'ebiseera byonna mu mikono gy'omulangira oyo omuto eyawangulwa mu kugondera kwonna okw'ebyo Adonai-YHVH by'ayagala. Mujje kati mu bujjuvu, awatali kulonzalonza era mutegeere nti NZE NDI Omukulu mbayisa mu Bigambo bino ebitereezeddwa okuzuula amaaso, amatu, n'emitima gy'abo bonna be bitumiddwa.

Mutegeere ebiseera mwe muli, kubanga ekiseera kyange eky’ekisa kiggwaako, okukomekkerezebwa mu ngeri ey’amangu era etasuubirwa era mu bujjuvu ku kiragiro kya Yeshua HaMashiach ekiragiro bwe kiweebwa n’okwogerera waggulu okw’amaanyi!

Oluvannyuma, okufuuwa kw’akagombe ak’amaanyi era akalungi, amangu ago ku nsi nga temuli!

Bwe mulikkirizibwa mu kitiiibwa ekinene, obuwanguzi, amaanyi n’ekitiibwa mu busika bwammwe obw’emyaka egitegekeddwa okugabirwa abeesigwa mu Kristo Yesu. Mazima ddala ekiseera ekyali kisuubirwa eky’okununulibwa kwa Adamu eri abeesigwa mu Kristo Yesu kituuse mu Ggulu nga tewali kiseera kirala kyonna kyenkana na kiri, kubanga Abaana bange ab’olubeerera abalambikiddwa mu ngeri ey’enjawulo n’ebigambo bino ebyaweebwa mu Yokaana Omusooka: “Laba, okwagala okungi Kitange kw'atuwadde, nti tuyitibwe abaana ba Katonda: Olw'ensonga eno ensi tebamanyi, kubanga teyamumanya. Mmwe abaagalwa ennyo, kaakano tuli baana ba Katonda, naye, tekinnayolesebwa kye tuliba: era tumanyi nti bw'alyolesebwa: tuliba nga ye: kubanga tulimulaba nga bw'ali. Era buli muntu alina essuubi lino mu ye yeerongoosa, nga naye bw'ali omulongoofu", kati bagenda kuzaalibwa obupya nga balamu era nga batondeddwa obupya mu bujjuvu mu kitiibwa, ekitiibwa, n'ekitiibwa amangu ago ku kiragiro kya Yeshua HaMashiach kye nnamuwa mu ngeri ey'enjawulo era mujja KATI kuyimirira mu ntebe yange ey'obwakabaka n'abeesigwa bonna mu Kristo Yesu.

Okutwalibwa kwammwe kutuuse!

Era n’olwekyo, ndagira abeesigwa bonna mu Kristo Yesu okukwata Ebigambo bino mu kugondera okusinga obukakafu Kristo Yesu alyoke abalongoose mu bujjuvu wansi w’omusaayi gwe ogutangirira nga temunnatwalibwa ne mukyusibwa olubeerera n’olubeerera.

Mubeere beetegefu!

Kubanga mugenda kuzaalibwa mu kisenge kyange eky’obwakabaka, we ndindiridde n’essanyu eringi ng’Omugenyi Kitaffe gwe NDI, okulindirira okutuusibwa okujjuvu okwa Isiraeri wa Adonai-YHVH agenda okukung’aanyizibwa KA’KAANO.

Nga tulina endowooza eno, nzijja okuyamba buli omu ku mmwe okutegeera amaanyi n’obukakafu obwakozesebwa mu magezi amangi n’obukugu obw’amaanyi Adonai-YHVH wammwe bwe yakozesa okubakweka n’okubakuuma okuva eri Heylel Ben Shachar eyandizze ku nsi omwana wange yenna singa afuna omukisa, naye n’okubabikkula ekimala ne mweyongera okuweesebwa okusinziira ku bigendererwa byange eby’emirembe na mirembe mu Kristo Yesu.

Kwe kwava, mwaliko akabonero era ne mukuuumibwa, kyokka ne mukwekebwa era ne mukisibwa mu lwatu, naye ne mubikkulwa ekimala: Era byonna nga biyita mu mikono gya Kristo Yesu! Kubanga ye eyabaagala n’eewaayo yekka, n’abatukuza, n’abalongoosa n’abatuukiriza Ekkanisa ye ey’amaanyi erimu abeesigwa mu Kristo Yesu. Kubanga Mazima, emiryango gy’e Ggeyeena tegiriwangula n’akatono Ekkanisa ey’amazima mu kitiibwa kyayo kyonna n’amaanyi gaayo. Ekkanisa eno y’egenda okukyusibwa n’okufuulibwa obuggya kaakano, ebigendererwa byayo ebyali eby’amazima era ebikwekeddwa kaakano bifuulibwe eby’olwatu ddala bye mulimanya mu bujjuvu nga nammwe bwe mumanyiddwa.

Mugumikirize abaana bange, ebiseera byammwe bituuse KA’KAANO.

Wabula, nnawuddemu ekibinja ekimu eky’enjawulo okuva mu Kkanisa, nga mbalonze okuva edda olw’ebigendererwa byange (newankubadde mwenna mulina kye mwalonderwa) era ne mbawa Kristo Yesu. N’olwekyo, mutegeere enjawulo eno mmwe mwenna 144,000, era na buli asoma ebigambo bino.

Kristo Yesu mu kwagala kwe yalina eri mmwe mwenna, n’okwagala kwe n’obuwulize bwe eri Adonai-YHVH we, yayimusiza abaana Adonai-YHVH abeesigwa mu Kristo Yesu era abassibwako akabonero n’omwoyo omutukuvu ogw’ekisuubizo era ne bawuulibwa ne bawaayo emirembe gyonna okujjuza ebifo ebyalimu obweru mu Ggulu mu kitiibwa eky’emirundi ebiri, era n’okusikira ensi mu kitiibwa eky’emirundi ebiri kubanga mwalonda okuba n’okukkiriza mu bujulirwa bwa Yesu Omunazaaleesi mu lunaku luno olubi, ye mwana gw’endiga gwa Katonda bwe yababuulirwa Adonai-YHVH wammwe era bwe mwafuna omukisa okuba n’okukkiriza ng’obw’ensigo ya kaladaali, ne mukkiriza! Era ensigo eyo ey’amaanyi era ey’emirembe na mirembe yakula nnyo nnyini kaakano mu mmwe mwenna! Naye ebikkuliddwa mu bujjuvu mu kisenge kyange eky’obwakabaka mu ngeri ey’ekibwatukira era etasuubirwa kaakano.

MUBEERE BEETEGEFU!

Mutegeere ddala nti, abeesigwa mu Kristo Yesu be baana ba Adonai-YHVH, abajja okuweereza Kristo Yesu, era ye Yeshua HaMashiach, mu mirimu gyammwe ne mu bibinja byammwe bye ntegese mu butuukirivu era nga bigyaanira buli omu ku mmwe. Empeera yammwe egyaanira buli omu mu butuukirivu era ejja kuba nkulu okusinga endala, okusinziira ku buwesigwa bwammwe bwokka. Naye kino si kikugiro ku kugulumizibwa kwammwe olw’obusika, wabula kitiibwa ekiweebwa oyo akisaanira olw’ebintu ebingi bye mwakola mu kuweereza Kristo Yesu mmwe mwenna. Era mwenna muli ba njawulo nnyo okuva ku munno, naye nga mufaanagana mu bintu bye mwagumiikiriza ne muwangula n’omukono ogw’amaanyi ogwa Kristo Yesu abayambye mwenna.

Ate era, mu beesigwa mu Kristo Yesu mwe mwaggyiddwa 144,000 abawuddwawo mu ngeri ey’enjawulo ng’abaana abaloondobamu ba Yeshua HaMashiach, abalondeddwa mu Isiraeri wa Adonai-YHVH n’omukono gwange omulonde, n’okwagala kwange okw’emirembe na mirembe era okutalina kikugiro. Abalwanyi bano ab’omuliro baweesebwa mu kubonaabona okungi era okuyita mu kugezesebwa kungi ne mu bibonyoobonyo bingi bye baagumiikiriza byonna mu mikono gya Heylel Ben Shachar, era kati babumbiddwa ne baweesebwa mu maanyi amangi n’obuvumu emitima gyabwe gisobole obutalyalyamya n’akatono bigendererwa bya Adonai-YHVH ebiri mu Yeshua HaMashiach, era n’okukuumibwa mu kwagala kwa Agape okutayongayonga era okw’amaanyi gonna. Selah.

N’olwekyo, mutegeere mmwe abange 144,000, nti mwenna mwatondebwa mu maanyi amangi era mu butuukirivu mu ntegeka ey’amaanyi era ey’amagezi, era ku kiragiro kya Kristo Yesu, era ye Yeshua HaMashiach, mujja kutuukiriza ebigendererwa byaffe eby’emirembe na mirembe ku musota guno ogw’omuggwa ogw’ekuzimu, era abagwa bonna basobole okusalirwa omusango mu bujjuvu n’ekibonerezo ekijjuvu ekiweereddwa mu musango ogw’omuliro ogw’obutuukirivu ku mulangira ono omutono omusirusiru era omulemereddwa. N’olwekyo, mmwe mwenna 144,000 muli babaka b’ebikolwa bino ebiweereddwa nga bitunuuliddwa mu bujjuvu ebyawandiikibwa nga bwe biri: Okuwoolera eggwanga kwange: Nze n’ansasula, bw’ayogera Mukama. N’olwekyo, mmwe muli babaka abalondeddwa ab’okusasula kuno okw’obutuukirivu okw’okuwoolera eggwanga ku mitwe gy’abagwa bonna, naye naddala omulangira oyo omusirusiru, omulemereddwa era eyeewaananga bulijjo, Heylel Ben Shachar.

Ye mulangira oyo omulemereddwa eyassibwamu ekitiibwa okusinga banne olw’ebigendererwa bya Elohim eby’emirembe na mirembe, era mu kwegomba kwe n’amalala ge bwe yeegulumiza, ng’alowooza nti byonna bye nnali mmutondedde byali bya kitiibwa kye n’ebigendererwa bye okukozesebwa ku kiragiro kye n’okwagala kwe, n’agwa mu bujeemu obw’amaanyi n’obutawulize mu ngeri eswaza ennyo n’ensonyi nnyingi era n’amalala ge gaamenyekamenyeka ddala era n’akuumibwa mu mbeera ey’obubalagaze ennyo era etalongooseka gy’ataagenda kuddamu kuvaamu era egenda kweyongerayongera obubi emirembe gyonna.

Bwe kityo, Heylel Ben Shachar (n’abo bonna abaagoberera seraafi ono) bwe baagobebwa n’amaanyi, n’obukambwe, era n’obulumi obungi n’amaanyi gange amatonotono okuva mu Ggulu ery’eGgulu, bwe baayanjulwa n’amaanyi obuyinza bwonna olw’ebibi byabwe eby’entiisa era eby’ekigendererwa era eby’olukwe ebyakolebwa n’obunkyayi bwonna n’olukwe. Ebyo ebyakula okuva munda mu mitima gyabwe kubanga Heylel Ben Shachar yagaana okufuga ebirowoozo bye mu buwulize eri Elohim we, mu kifo ky’okussa essira ku kitiibwa n’amaanyi ga Elohim we, nze mmuyimusize mu kifo kye eky’emirembe na mirembe eky’obuweereza, obuyinza, amaanyi n’ekitiibwa, ye n’assa essira ku kitiibwa kye kye yeerengeramu kye yagezaako okutwala n’amaanyi n’obukambwe nga kikye.

Era mu kulimbibwa kwe okw’ekibi okwabaddewo bwe yeegulumiza mu kibi okusinga Elohim we eyamutonda n’amaanyi amangi era n’ekigendererwa eky’amaanyi n’amutikkira engule ey’ekitiibwa n’ettendo ku lunaku lwe yatondebwa okusinziira ku nteekateeka zange n’ebigendererwa byange, n’obukugu n’amaanyi, Heylel Ben Shachar n’abo bonna abaamugoberera baasuulibwa n’obukambwe okuva mu Ggulu nga tebannayimuka kujjuza bifo byabwe bya buweereza bya mirembe, obuyinza, amaanyi n’ekitiibwa kubanga beewaayo olw’ebirayiro by’obutawulize obw’obukyayi eri obujeemu obutagasa.

Heylel Ben Shachar oluvannyuma n’ekigendererwa era n’olukwe lwonna yasiga obujeemu bwe mu nkukutu mu banne, nga El Shaddai talaba bikolwa bye n’ebirowoozo bye n’ebigendererwa by’omutima gwe.
