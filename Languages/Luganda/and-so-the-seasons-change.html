Era Bwe Tyo Ebiseera Bikyuka!

Mwekuŋŋaanye, mmwe Ekkanisa yange, era mutege amatu eri amagezi. Mutege amatu eri eddoboozi lya Kabaka wammwe omulwanyi, ye oyo ayimiridde okubalwanirira, ye nze Empologoma ey'omu kika kya Yuda, Mukama wa bakama era Kabaka wa bakabaka. Nze oyo Yakuwa gwe yawa ebintu byonna mu ggulu ne ku nsi, mu mikono gyange egy'amaanyi kubanga ndi mulamu emirembe n'emirembe era nkutte ebisumuluzo by'okufa n'eky'emagombe, era ensi yange kubanga nagigula n'omusaayi gwange ogw'okutangirira. Era nnina erinnya erisinga amannya gonna mu kuba nti mu Linnya lya Yesu buli vviivi lirifukamira, ery'ebintu ebiri mu ggulu, n'ebintu ebiri mu nsi, n'ebintu ebiri wansi w'ensi. Era nti buli lulimi lulyatula nti Yesu Kristo ye Mukama, olw'ekitiibwa kya Kitaffe w'ebitangaala, atalina kukyuka kwonna newaakubadde ekisiikirize eky'okukyama. Era ani ayinza okuyimirira eri ekitala eky'obwogi obw'enjuyi zombiriri ekiva mu kamwa kange? Kubanga nakyo nnaakubisa amawanga: era nze oyo alibafuga n'omuggo ogw'ekyuma: era nze oyo alinnyirira mu busungu essogolero ly'obukambwe n'obusungu bwa El Shaddai, ye Katonda Omuyinza w'ebintu byonna. Era nze oyo alina ku lugoye lwe, ne ku kisambi kye erinnya eriwandiikiddwa, KABAKA WA BAKABAKA, ERA MUKAMA WA BAKAMA, era ekiseera kyange kituuse!

Kiki ekisobola okusangibwa mu kitonde kya Yakuwa kyonna, mmwe abaana bange, kye mwandyagadde okutya? Mumanye amazima, mmwe abaana bange abato, nti abo abagwa babatya. Kubanga bamanyi nti kaakano bali nga mmwe, era mulifunanyizibwa ekitiibwa ekingi okusinga bwe baali, abo bonna abaava mu kifo kyabwe eky'olubereberye. Okuva mu ntegeka Yakuwa z'ateekawo eri ekitonde kye kibi kinene kubanga ekitonde kya Yakuwa kigendereddwamu okukola mu kutuukirira okw'emirembe. Era Yakuwa awa eddembe ly'okweroboza, kale okulondawo okujeema mu kwegomba okw'obukyayi kireeta okufa n'okubonaabona mu lusenyi lw'obulamu. Kale ekibi ky'abo abagwa kikyavuga n'okutuusa leero, era ebiseera by'omusango gwabwe bituuse! Temumanyi, mmwe abaana bange, nti mulisalira bamalayika omusango?

Ani alibawula ku kwagala kwange? Okubonaabona oba ennaku, oba okuyigganyizibwa, oba enjala, oba obwereere, oba akabi, oba ekitala? Nedda! Mu bino byonna muli basinga kuwangula mu nze Kristo Yesu Mukama wammwe eyalinnya mu ggulu n'aweebwa obuyinza, kubanga Yakuwa agabanye nange entebe ye ey'obwakabaka era nange nja kugabana entebe yange n'abeesigwa, be abo abanjagala era be njagala. Mutegeere nti Yakuwa yennyini abalwanirira era Yakuwa yantuma, ye Mukama wammwe, Kabaka era omutaasa. Kubanga nze kigo kyammwe eky'amaanyi era olwazi lw'okwekwesamu, kubanga nze Lwazi lw'Emirembe, ye Mukama wammwe era omulokozi. Era nze omulongooseza atuula n'omuvubo n'ennyondo mu ngalo okukuma omuliro ogw'okulongoosa ng'aggyamu ebisasiro! Era nze oyo akuba ennyondo n'asondeka ebyuma eby'omuwendo eby'omu nnyumba y'obugagga bwa Yakuwa, omuli eby'obugagga bye ebingi bye bintu by'omuwendo ebitagambika bye batabani ba Katonda okubatuusa mu kutuukirira okw'ekitiibwa, era emirimu gyange ginaatera okutuukirizibwa mu mmwe baana bange, ye mmwe ebibala byange ebisooka era abayite n'abalonde era abeesigwa.

Laba! Ekiseera kituuse eky'okumalirizibwa n'okununulibwa okujjuvu okwammwe, mmwe abanjagalwa, era nja kumalayo okununulibwa kwammwe era mu butayuza bw'obutayuza kaakano munaagenda! Era mu kusekejjuuka kw'eriiso mulisanga nga muyimiridde mu kisenge kya Yakuwa eky'obwakabaka mu kwewuunya okw'omu ggulu n'essanyu eritali ggwaawo, kubanga kaakano muliyingira mu ssanyu lya Mukama wammwe. Era mulisanga nga muzingiddwa mu kitangaala eky'ekitiibwa ekitaggwaawo ekya Yakuwa, Katonda omunene ow'emirembe n'emirembe n'okumasamasa okutaggwaawo okw'Omwana w'Obutuukirivu era mulitondebwa buto, era mulyewuunya ekitiibwa kye muliba mwambaziddwa. Baana bange, buli mulimu gwe mwakolerapo mu linnya lyange mu kuweereza Kristo, mu kuweereza abatuukirivu n'okubweereza, guwandiikiddwa mu ggulu! Eriiso teryalaba, so n'okutu tekunawulira, so n'okuyingira mu mutima gw'omuntu tekunayita ebyo Yakuwa bye yategekera abo abamwagala era empeera yammwe eri mu mukono gwange, kubanga mmwe bawanguzi bange, era mbazizza buggya mu nze emirembe gyonna, era temulivunda era obusika bwammwe bwa lubeerera obutazikira n'ekitiibwa kyammwe kya lubeerera. Kubanga mu ekyo mwe mwabanga abeesigwa mu katono ennyo nja kubafuula abafuzi b'ebingi.

Mu kaseera katono, mu kakasa, mu kusekejjuuka kw'eriiso okununulibwa kwammwe okujjuvu kulibaawo n'ebizibu byammwe byonna biribulawo mbagirawo. Amangu ago tekulibaawo ndwadde, amangu ago tekulibaawo bulumi, amangu ago tekulibaawo kutya, amangu ago muliba mu bumu obutuukirivu ne Yakuwa, amangu ago muliba mu bumu obutuukirivu ne Yeshua HaMashiach, amangu ago mulisanga nga mujjudde essanyu, emirembe, n'okumasamasa okutaggwaawo kulibavaako emirembe gyonna. Kubanga mu ekyo mwe mwagulumiriza Omwana gw'Endiga, Empologoma y'omu Kika kya Yuda kaakano eribagulumiza, kubanga nze oyo gwe mwakkiririzaamu, era nze oyo omwesigwa era ow'amazima, eyakuuma ekyo kye mwassa gyendi okutuusa ku lunaku luno. Era mmwe be nnafuula ab'amagezi, mulimasamasa emirembe gyonna, nga okumasamasa kw'olubangirira: era mmwe abakyusizza abangi okudda ku butuukirivu, mulimasamasa ng'emmunyeenye, emirembe n'emirembe. Kale kaakano nga mukimanyi kino baana bange nti kaakano kiseera kya kuleka kweraliikirira kwa nsi eno ne muwummulira mu nze era musigale nga muli balongoofu ng'omuwala atamanyi musajja mu musaayi gwange ogutangirira.

Laba! Omuwaabi w'ab'oluganda asuuliddwa wansi asuuliddwa wansi, ye musota ogw'edda, ye gumisota omumyufu ogunene ogw'omukira gwe gwakwata ekitundu kimu kya kusatu eky'emmunyeenye n'azisuula ku nsi. Ne wabaawo olutalo mu ggulu, Mikayiri ne bamalayika be ne balwana ne gumisota, ne gumisota n'alwana ne bamalayika be. Naye ne batayisa, so n'ekifo kyabwe ne kitakyalabika nate mu ggulu. Ne gumisota omunene, omusota ogw'edda, ayitibwa Omulyolyomi ne Setaani, n'agobebwa, oyo alimbalimba ensi yonna: asuuliddwa ku nsi, ne bamalayika be ne basuulibwa wansi naye. Kubanga waaliwo okumyansa n'okubwatuka ne wabaawo okukankana n'akabonero k'okubeerawo kwe ne kawulirwa, n'obulimba bw'omulimba ow'olulimi olw'amawanga ne bubuna dda kubanga bulijjo baali balindiridde okujja kw'akabonero ke. Akabonero ke kaali waggulu w'ekibumbe kye eky'okuvvoola.

Zibasanze abatuula ku nsi kubanga omulyolyomi ne bamalayika be basuuliddwa wansi mu mmwe era basibiddwa ku nsi olw'omusango era tewali kifo mu ggulu gye bali emirembe n'emirembe era tebaliddamu kuyingira. Musanyuke mmwe ab'omu ggulu, kubanga omuwaabi w'ab'oluganda asuuliddwa wansi eyabawaabiranga emisana n'ekiro mu maaso ga Katonda waffe, n'ekifo kye tekikyali mu ggulu emirembe gyonna. Kaakano obulokozi bujja, kaakano ekitiibwa ky'abatuukirivu kijja, era baligulumizibwa kubanga baligulumiza Yakuwa mu nze Kristo Yesu nange ndibambaza obutuukirivu obutaggwaawo, ye bafuta enjeru ennamu etayonooneka etayinza kusiigibwako bbala, era ndibawa empeera etaggwaawo era ndibafuula Bakabaka ne Bakabona mu maaso ga Yakuwa era balifugira ku ntebe yange ey'obwakabaka ng'okwagala kwabwe n'obwesigwa bwabwe bwe byali. Eri abeesigwa waliwo empeera nnene n'eri abatali beesigwa waliwo okufiirwa, naye balilokolebwa, naye nga bayita mu muliro.

Mumpulire, mmwe mwenna abatuula ku nsi, newaakubadde nga muli bazibe b'amatu era mugaana obubonero bwange! Omulyolyomi kaakano asuuliddwa wansi mu mmwe, era amanyi nti alina akaseera katono. Alina obusungu n'okuwoolera eggwanga okungi, kubanga ye mulabe eyawangulwa asibiddwa olw'ebigendererwa bya Yakuwa ebirimalirizibwa mu nnyanja ey'omuliro eya kobalti eya bulu ey'okuzikiriza okutakoma. Era kubanga mmwe, ggwe omuntu ow'obutonde mwagala ekizikiza okusinga omusana, kubanga mmwe, ggwe omuntu ow'obutonde mwagala ebikolwa byammwe ebibi n'okunyoomoola omukono gwange ogulokola ogw'ekisa, (Kubanga buli muntu akola ebikolwa ebibi, akyawa omusana, so tajja eri omusana, ebikolwa bye bireme okunenyesebwa) kale kaakano nja kubawa ekibi kye mwegomba ennyo. Kubanga kaakano mubeera mu weema ya Moloki nga muwuuza emmunyeenye ya katonda wammwe Lemufani ku bendera yammwe, ebifaananyi bye mwakola okubisinza.

Okuva bwe muwaayo abaana eri Moloki, okuva bwe musinza Bafometi wammwe ow'omuzizo, laba! Gumisota wammwe kaakano ali mu mmwe. Era muliyiga mbagirawo nti omubbi tajja wabula okubba, okutta n'okuzikiriza. Okuggyako kaakano tekulibaawo kya kwebikkako, tekulibaawo muziyiza, tekulibaawo kusaasira na kisa okuva mu ggulu kubanga mulonzeewo gumisota okuba katonda wammwe, era mulonzeewo Balaba okuba omulokozi wammwe ne kabaka. Kale kaakano mbawa okubafuga ng'emitima gyammwe emibi bwe gyegomba. Laba! Wano we wali amagezi, oyo alina okutegeera abale omuwendo gw'ensolo kubanga guli mu mmunyeenye ya Lemufani gye batwala ku bendera yaabwe, kubanga be lisinzizo lya Setaani abanoonya okubba ettaka n'okutta abalituulamu ab'ensonga nange ndirokola Yakobo!

Laba! Setaani ayingira mu Balaba, era atwala entebbe, n'obubonero bwe obw'obulimba n'ebyamagero biribaleetera okuzikirira okw'olubeerera. Kubanga alikozesa ebyo by'agamba nti Katonda yekka ye yamulokola okugamba nti atumiddwa Katonda era nti ye Katonda, kubanga bwe batyo bakkiriza nti be Katonda mu mubiri n'abalala bonna nsolo. Alikozesa obubonero bwe obw'obulimba n'ebyamagero era awamu n'ensolo eva mu nnyanja balireeta okuzikirira okunene ku nsi, kubanga mulonzeewo gumisota, era kaakano mulirya nnyo ku kazambi ak'olubeerera gumisota gw'aleeta.

Mmwe, ggwe atuula ku nsi, muwandizza ekisa n'okusaasira bya Yakuwa bye yatumira mu nze, ne mu maaso ga Yesu Kristo, era okuva bwe mwagaana Omwana gw'Endiga, kaakano mujja kusisinkana Empologoma ey'omu kika kya Yuda. Kubanga najja mu mirembe n'okwagala n'obulungi n'ekisa n'okusaasira, era mwagaanye obulokozi mu busirusiru okusinga okufa kwammwe n'ekibonerezo ekitaggwaawo, kubanga tewali kulondawo kulala. Okuwandira Yakuwa ng'agolodde omukono gwe mu mirembe n'okwagala, n'okuwaayo obulamu bw'omwana we omu yekka gw'azaala, kibi kya lubeerera era tewali kudduka busungu okuggyako okukoowoola erinnya lya Yesu ery'amaanyi okutuusa ku nkomerero era oyo atayagala bulamu bwe okutuusa ku nkomerero alirokolebwa, n'ekibiina ekinene ky'ebibiina kirilokolebwa.

Naye ggwe, ggwe omuntu ow'obutonde, olonzeewo amasanyu g'ekibi ng'oweereddwa ekisa era n'oweebwa omwanya okwenenya. Kubanga nnakugololedeko omukono, n'agololera bakitaawo, n'agololera bakitaawo ba bakitaawo, n'okudda emabega emyaka mingi okuva lwe nnatuma abatume bange ne bannabbi n'ekyama ekinene ekya Katonda ne kibuulirwa n'okutuuka ku nkomerero z'ensi! Era kaakano okugaana kwammwe kutuukiridde, era nzikiriza okusalawo kwammwe okw'enkomerero, era nze Kristo Yesu, era nze oyo alangirira kaakano nti ebiseera by'Abamawanga biweddeko era kaakano omusango gujja nga gutandikira ku nnyumba ya Katonda. Mu ekyo emimwa gyammwe mwe gyegaanira Yakuwa, ye Mukama w'Emwoyo, obusika bwammwe obwalagirwa kaakano buli mu nnyanja eyo ennene ey'omuliro, ogwa sulufeeti ey'ebinirira, era Kyandibadde kirungi gy'oli singa tewazaalibwa. Mu busirusiru mulowooza nti mulina obudde bwonna mu nsi nti Yakuwa agenda kweyongera okulinda okutuusa lwe mulikyukira obutuukirivu okuva mu bibi byammwe eby'omuzizo. Era mbagamba kaakano lwatu nti, obudde buweddeko era bangi basubwa emirembe gyonna omukisa gwabwe ogw'obulamu obutaggwaawo n'omukisa gwabwe okubeera mu mugole wange alijja okwegombebwa bonna ku lunaku olwo.

Kubanga bwe gutyo gwe mulamwa gw'abo bonna abatwala akabonero. Kale ntegeeza amawanga nate nti:

BWANA WABA OMUNTU ASINZA ENSOLO N'EKIFAANANYI KYAYO, N'ATEEKERWA AKABONERO KAYO MU KYENYI KYE OBA MU MUKONO GWE, OUYO ALINYWA KU MVINYOO Y'OBUSUNGU BWA KATONDA, AFUKIDDWAMU AWATALI KUTABULWAMU MU KIKOMPE KY'OBUSUNGU BWE, ERA ALIBONYEBONYEBWA N'OMULIRO N'AMAZZI GA SULUFEETI, MU MAASO GA BAMALAYIKA ABATUKUVU, NE MU MAASO G'OMWANA GW'ENDIGA: N'OMUKKA GW'OKUBONYEBONYEBWA KWABWE GUNYooka EMIRIMBE N'EMIRIMBE. N'ABATALINA KUWUMMULA EMISANA NEWAKUBADDE EKIRO, ABASINZA ENSOLO N'EKIFANANYI KYAYO, NA BULI ATEEKERWA AKABONERO K'ERINNYA LYAYO.

Kubanga akabonero ddala kasemberedde. Kaliba mulyango gw'okuyingira mu kitundu kyabwe, era balibagamba nti lye ddagala. Balibagamba nti lirireeta obulamu obutaggwaawo, balibagamba nti temuliddamu kulwala, balibagamba obulimba bungi nnyo mulyoke mutwale eccumu lyabwe ery'ensolo, era balikakasa bangi nti liri lwa bulungi bwabwe. Laba! Abo abagwa bajja nga befuula abagwira n'obukodyo bwabwe mu ngalo zaabwe. Mutegeere nti akabonero kalibafuula abaddu b'obukodyo bwabwe, kubanga balyetabula n'ezzadde ly'abantu: naye tebaligattagana, era ng'ekyuma bwe kitatabulwa na bbumba. Akabonero kaabwe ak'ensolo, ye kulumwa kw'omusota, kalizikiriza ddala bonna abanywa obutwa bwabwe era tewali kudduka.

Laba! Ne mpulira eddoboozi ddene eriva mu Yeekaalu, nga ligamba Bamalayika omusanvu nti, Mugende, mufuke ebibya omusanvu eby'obusungu bwa Katonda ku nsi. Owoolubereberye n'agenda n'afuka ekibya kye ku nsi: ne kugwawo ejjute eddene eriruma ku bantu, abaalina akabonero k'ensolo, ne ku abo abaasinzanga ekifaananyi kyayo. Kale bwe gutyo bwe guli omulamwa ogulindiridde bonna abakkiriza n'obwegendereza okubonaabona kwabwe okutaggwaawo n'abo abalonzeewo okufa okw'okubiri. Byonna kubanga mwagaanye Yesu Omunazaaleeti, eyandibakozeeko eby'okulokoka mu byonna. Era bonna abatwala akabonero baliba n'amabwa gaabwe amaluma era bonna abatwala akabonero balibeera emirembe gyonna mu busika bwabwe mu nnimi z'omuliro eza bulu mu kizikiza ekikutte akawungeezi emirembe gyonna, naye omulamwa guno gulondeddwa n'obwegendereza abo be gugendereddwamu era bwe gutyo bwe guliba!

Kale okwawulibwa okw'enkomerero kaakano kutandika era nga bwe waaliwo olutalo mu ggulu kaakano kulibaawo olutalo ku nsi kubanga nja kuggyawo emirembe mbagirawo era mbagirawo omuzibi aliggyibwawo era eddalu eritaliiko nkomerero liribwatuka okubuna ensi yonna kubanga ekibi tekiriddamu kuziyizibwa, era nja kuwa buli muntu ng'ebikolwa bye bwe biri. Kale ebiseera by'Abamawanga biggaddwa era olutalo lunaatera okubalukawo okubuna ensi yonna, era baliddamu okutegeka ensalo zaabwe mu bwakabaka bw'ensolo obw'enkomerero omuliba gumisota, ensolo eva mu nnyanja n'ensolo eva mu nsi era ye Moshiach waabwe omulimba aligoloka n'okubonaabona kwa Yakobo kulibwatuka okubuna ensi yonna mu kaseera katono, era tebaliwona!

Laba! Ntumye enjiri yange eteriggwaawo, kubanga okugituma kwalangirirwa edda Yokaana Omubikkulirwa bwe namuwandiisa nti: Nalaba Malayika omulala ng'abuuka wakati mu ggulu, ng'alina Enjiri eteriggwaawo, okugibuulira abo abatuula ku nsi, n'eri buli ggwanga, n'ekika, n'olulimi, n'abantu. Laba! Okutegeeza kwa Yesu gwe mwoyo gw'obunnabbi era mumanye nti ebigambo byange birizuula amaaso, amatu, n'emitima gy'abo bonna be bitumirwa n'Ebigambo by'enjiri eteriggwaawo bibikkuliddwa mu kiseera kyabyo era birikuumibwa eri abo be bitumirwa, kale muwulire eddoboozi lya Moshiach wammwe ow'amazima! Kubanga nze Yeshua HaMashiach!

Kale Ebigambo bya Yokaana Omubikkulirwa biyimiridde ng'eky'okubudaabuda n'ettaala ey'essuubi eri abo abalifuna obulokozi:

Okubikkulirwa 21:1-7

Ne ndaba eggulu eriggya, n'ensi empya: kubanga eggulu ery'olubereberye, n'ensi ey'olubereberye byali biweddewo, so n'ennyanja tekyaliiwo. Nange Yokaana ne ndaba ekibuga ekitukuvu Yerusaalemi ekipya nga kikka okuva eri Katonda mu ggulu, nga kitegekeddwa ng'omugole ayonjereddwa bba. Ne mpulira eddoboozi ddene eriva mu ggulu, nga lyogera nti, Laba, Eweema ya Katonda eri wamu n'abantu, era alituula nabo: nabo baliba bantu be, ne Katonda yennyini aliba Katonda waabwe wamu nabo. Era Katonda alisiimula amaziga gonna mu maaso gaabwe: so n'okufa tekulibaawo nate, so n'okunakuwala, so n'okukaaba, so n'okulumwa tekulibaawo nate: kubanga eby'olubereberye biweddewo. N'oyo eyatudde ku ntebe ey'obwakabaka, n'ayogera nti, Laba, nfuula byonna bipya: n'aŋŋamba nti, Wandiika: kubanga ebigambo bino bya bwesigwa era bya mazima. N'aŋŋamba nti, Kiwedde, nze Alufa ne Omega, olubereberye n'enkomerero. Nnaamuwanga oyo alumwa ennyonta, ku luzzi olw'amazzi ag'obulamu buwa. Oyo awangula, alisitukira ebintu byonna, nange ndiba Katonda we, naye aliba mwana wange.

Nze oyo Yakuwa gwe yawa ebintu byonna mu ggulu ne ku nsi.

Nze oyo alibafuga n'omuggo ogw'ekyuma.

Nze oyo alinnyirira mu busungu essogolero ly'obukambwe n'obusungu bwa El Shaddai.

Nze oyo alina ku lugoye lwe, ne ku kisambi kye erinnya eriwandiikiddwa, KABAKA WA BAKABAKA, ERA MUKAMA WA BAKAMA, era ekiseera kyange kituuse.

Nze kigo kyammwe eky'amaanyi era olwazi lw'okwekwesamu, kubanga nze Lwazi lw'Emirembe, ye Mukama wammwe era omulokozi.

Nze omulongooseza atuula n'omuvubo n'ennyondo mu ngalo okukuma omuliro ogw'okulongoosa ng'aggyamu ebisasiro.

Nze oyo akuba ennyondo n'asondeka ebyuma eby'omuwendo ebya mu nnyumba y'obugagga bwa Yakuwa, omuli eby'obugagga bye ebingi bye bintu by'omuwendo ebitagambika bye batabani ba Katonda okubatuusa mu kutuukirira okw'ekitiibwa.

Nze oyo gwe mwakkiririzaamu.
