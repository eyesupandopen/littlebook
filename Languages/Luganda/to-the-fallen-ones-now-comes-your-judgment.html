Kale kitegeereke, nti emisango gino giwereddwa gisomebwe ku lunaku luno, ku nnaku z'omwezi ez'ekyenyinyalwa eza kalenda ya Girigori eya Jjulaayi 6, 2025.

Sela.

Ennyanjula

Ebirangiriro bino byonna biggiddwa mu Kubwatuka Omunsanvu era biweereddwa Jjulaayi 6, 2025, nga Yesua HaMashiach ne YHVH yennyini bwe yalagira.

Emisango gino giggiddwa mu Kubwatuka Omunsanvu okubikkuliddwa mu Katabo Akatono ak'Okubikkulirwa Essuula 10, era ge Mawaulire Amalungi Agataggwaawo ag'Okubikkulirwa essuula 14; okubikkulwa okuva Maaki 2, 2024, okutuuka Maaki 20, 2025. Emisango gino giweereddwa Yesua HaMashiach ne YHVH okuyita mu Mwoyo gw'Obunnabbi era bino bye Bigambo byabwe, bye nsiima era bye njagala emirembe n'emirembe. Ebirangiriro bino gwe musango oguwereddwa okusomebwa omusango ogujjuvu nga tegunagwa, oguligabirwa mu bifuba by'abawagguse emirembe n'emirembe kubanga mu kubonerezebwa kwabwe tebalina kuwummula, so tebalina mirembe, so tebalina kusaasira: kubanga okuwummula, emirembe, n'okusaasira tebabimanyi, tebaalaga, so tebaagaba.

Kitegeereke nti, mu ngeri eno, nkola ng'omubaka omulamuzi era omutumwa aweerezeddwa okuva ku ntebe ya Yave yennyini, wansi w'obuyinza bwa Kristo Yesu, alinga ye Yesua HaMashiach, gwe mpeereza.

Nze gwe Mukama wange gwe yanziggyamu, era nze Biblaridion:144 we, akatabo ke akatono, ye omubaka annyonnyolwa n'obubaka obukaawa obuwoomerera era mu mukono gwange mulimu akatabo akatono akabikkule Sela.
