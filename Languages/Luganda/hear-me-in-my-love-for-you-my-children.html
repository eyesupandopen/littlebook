Obujulizi obubiri: Ekkanisa
Mu Biwandiiko bya Pawulo Omutume
Nakasenene 21, 2024

Mumpulirize mu Kwagala kwe Nnina gye muli mmwe abaana bange!

(Mu ntegeera y'obubaka buno obw'obunnabbi, “Omwedomu” kitegeeza abo abaayaniriza enkola z'Abakaanani ez'okukyusa ensa, obulogo obw'ekizikiza, okuwaayo abaana ssaddaaka, okulya abantu, n'ebirala. Mazima, Esawu yawasa abakazi babiri Abakiiti, ab'omu Kaanani, era n'awasa n'omukazi ow'okusatu okuva mu nnyumba ya Isimaeri. Okuwasa kwa Esawu abakazi Abakaanani kulaga obujeemu n'okwetabanga awamu n'okusaasaanya okw'ensa okwali okw'obulabe, kubanga Kaanani yali akozeeko ku nsa mu ngeri emu oba endala. Mu ngeri eno, erinnya Omwedomu likozesebwa ku abo abakyakola emikolo gya sitaani gino, era erinnya lino liyinza okugwanira ekika kyonna oba eggwanga lyonna erikola bwe lityo. Kabaka Kerode yali Mwedomu, era n'abamu ku Bafalisaayo baali bwe batyo, era Yesu yabagamba nti baali baana ba Sitaani, ekiraga ensigo. Naye tegeera nti, erinnya Omwedomu nga bwe likozesebwa mu bubaka buno, terikwata ku muntu yenna olw'okuba w'eddya lye oba eggwanga lye. Luba lunyiriri olukozesebwa okwawula abo abakola eby'obulogo by'Abakaanani mu kyama oba abo abava mu lulyo olwo era ne basaasaana mu mawanga.)

— William Brooks


Muwulire ebigambo byange, mmwe Ekkanisa yange, era mutege amatu gammwe eri amagezi n’amazima, kubanga laba: Ebigambo byange ge magezi era ge mazima. Amagezi gayogerera waggulu mu nguudo, n’okutegeera kunoonya okwogera nammwe; kaakano mujja kumpa okutu kwammwe mu kugondera okw'okweyagalira? Kubanga Nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, nkaabirira waggulu okumpulirwa Omugole wange, ye mubiri gwonna ogwa Kristo. Muwulire ebigambo byange, bw'ayogera Mukama omukulu era omwesigwa, oyo obwakabaka bwe obutali bwa nsi eno, kubanga obwakabaka bwange bwe bwakabaka obw'omu ggulu. Nga bwe kiri mu bwakabaka bwange, nga bwe kiri mu ggulu, bwe kityo bwe kiriba ne ku nsi; kubanga ndireeta obutuukirivu ku nsi, so tewaliba kintu kyonna kibi ekiyinza okuyingira mu miryango gyange, kubanga obwakabaka bwange bwe bwakabaka obw'obutuukirivu, empologoma mwe neerumaalika wamu n'omwana gw'endiga mu mirembe n'obutebenkevu. Bangi ku mmwe, Omugole wange, mufuuse ba malala mu nnaku zino ez'okumanya okweyongedde, era kino si kya kitalo, kubanga Ekigambo kyange kigamba kitya: Kubanga okumanya kuleeta amalala! Naye okwagala kuzimba. Kaakano mumpulirize mu kwagala kwe nnina gye muli, abaana bange!

Muwulire ebigambo byange, kubanga njogera ku Bedomu abali mu mmwe, kubanga bangi ku mmwe temumanyi gwe njogerako. Njogera ku abo abambala ebyambalo by'endiga naye nga misumaali egirumya, kubanga beefuula ne batambulira mu mmwe. Njogera ku kkuŋŋaaniro lya Sitaani n'abo bonna abayingiridde ekitonde kya Kitange, nga batumiddwa Sitaani yennyini, kubanga be nsigo yennyini ey'omusota. O, linda, temuzibikira matu gammwe n'okubikka ku maaso gammwe kubanga temukkiriziganya n'ebigambo byange, O, mmwe abaana bange abato abazibe b'amaaso era abazibe b'amatu mu bweyagalire, naye mufukamye amaviivi gammwe mu buwombeefu era mutege okutu kwammwe eri ebigambo byange, kubanga amalala gakulembera okuzikirira n'omwoyo ogw'ekiboggwe gugwa.

Mumanye, mmwe abaana bange abato, nti ebigambo ebiri mu Kigambo kyange ekya Katonda bituufu, bituukiridde, era byesigika: ne bwe mutabitegeera ebigambo bino ne mugezaako okubyesonyiwa amazima gaabyo mu bigambo, nga mwegamba nti: O, ekyo si kya butereevu wabula kya lugero. O, ekyo tekyawandiikibwira nze wabula omulala. O, ezo ngero za buntu za mu biseera by'edda n'enkola z'okuwandiika. O, ezo ngero za mu biseera ebyayita era tezirina kutwalibwa nga bwe ziri. Ggwe omusiru! Okkiriza abasumba abakyamu okugamba nti Ekigambo kya Katonda kyennyini, ebigambo ebyo bye muyitamu okufuna obulokozi bwammwe, ebigambo bya Yakuwa byennyini si bya mazima naye biyinza okunnyonnyolwa obunnyonnyozi awatali kukkiriza? O, muve mu makubo gammwe agatalina kukkiriza, mmwe abantu bange, mujje gyendi n'omutima omugazi n'amagezi agambadde obuwombeefu.

Temusomangako? Olubereberye lugamba ki ku nsonga eno Yakuwa bwe yavuma omusota olw'obukujjukujju bwe bwakola mu Lusuku emyaka mingi egiyise? Era nditeeka obulabe wakati wo n'omukazi, n' wakati w'ezzadde lyo n'ezzadde lye: liribonyabonya omutwe gwo, naawe olimubonyabonya ekisinziiro. Mulowooza nti ebigambo bino bya kulimba era bya lugero, abaana bange? Mukkiriza ebigambo ebitakkiriza eby'okumanyisibwa ebimazibwa amaaso gammwe ku bigambo byange ebbanga lino lyonna? Obulimba obukkirizibwa mu sayansi wammwe, kubanga bufuuse butali butuufu olw'obulimba bwabwo. Obulimba obukkirizibwa mu ntegeera yammwe ey'obutonde bw'ensi, kubanga bufuuse butali butuufu olw'obulimba bwabwo. Obulimba obukkirizibwa mu byafaayo byammwe, kubanga mwerabidde kye muli, mmwe abaana bange abato, era mutabaganyiziddwa n'ebyafaayo byammwe eby'amazima ne mubeera mu bulimba bw'omulabe mu butamanya bw'ensi yennyini mwe muli.

Nga bwe kiri nti ddala nze nsigo eyasuubizibwa ey'omukazi, yee, omukazi oyo ow'ekitalo mu byawandiikibwa (abamu gwe basinza mu kibi) eyagamba nti, Kibeerenga gye ndi nga bw'oyagadde, gwe nnayitamu okujja mu nsi eno. Nazaalibwa mu butuufu okuva eri Maliyamu mu Besirekemu eky'e Buyudaaya ne nkulira ng'omwana w'omubazzi, n'akulizibwa Yusufu eyali ow'omu lulyo lwa Dawudi, bwe yali tayagala kumuswaza mu lwatu (kubanga yamwagala nnyo), kubanga yalabika ng'ali lubuto lwa Mwoyo Mutukuvu. Nga nze bwe ndi ensigo eyasuubizibwa eyatumibwa Katonda, ne Sitaani bw'atyo alina ensigo. Si lwakuba nti asobola okutondawo ekintu kyonna yekka, kubanga ye mulunyunyusi era mubbi, naye akoze eby'omuzizo olw'okuzikiriza kwammwe.

Lwaki kino kizibu nnyo gye muli okukikkiriza? Nga muwuliriza obulimba obutwalibwa ng'amazima, mwesonyiye ebintu eby'omusingi ennyo, kubanga mbabuulidde eby'ensi, ne mutakkiriza: mulikkiriza mutya bwe mbabuulira eby'omu ggulu? Temusomangako bigambo bya Yakuwa eri omuweereza we omwesigwa Yobu? Wali ludda wa bwe nnassaawo emisingi gy'ensi? Yogera, oba ng'olina okutegeera. Ani eyagipima, oba ng'omanyi? Oba ani eyagigololerako omugwa? Emisingi gyayo gyassibwa ku ki? Oba ani eyagiteekako ejjinja ery'oku nsonda? Emmunyeenye ez'enkya bwe zaayimbiranga wamu, n'abaana ba Katonda bonna bwe baayogeranga waggulu n'essanyu. Musome ekitundu ekyo eky'enkomerero nate, abaana bange,

"abaana ba Katonda baayogerera waggulu olw'essanyu" — yee, bamalayika baaliwo mu kutonda, era bamanyi mangi ku byama by'omu ggulu ebitasaanira muntu kumanya, kubanga omuntu anoonyezza emyaka mingi mu kwegomba kwe okw'amaanyi n'ekitiibwa.

Kuno kwe kumanya kwa bamalayika abagwa, abaana bange, temusomangako? Oba mukyakkiririza mu bulimba bw'Abedomu bwe baasiga mu mmwe? Naye Olubereberye lugamba ki ku nsonga eno? Waaliwo Abanefuli mu nsi mu nnaku ezo: era n'oluvannyuma lw'ebyo, abaana ba Katonda bwe baayingiranga eri abawala b'abantu, ne babazaalira abaana; abo ne bafuuka abasajja ab'amaanyi, abaaliwo edda, abasajja ab'ettutumu. Bangi ku mmwe munkooya n'okukkiririza mu lugero olukyamu nti abaana ba Katonda baava mu Sese, ne bawasa abawala b'abantu ababi ne bazaala abanefuli mu bubi. Abantu babadde babi okuva lwe bagwa, era ka zibe ngeri ki abantu ababi gye bazaalamu, ezzadde lyabwe terijja kuba banefuli banene nnyo. Nedda. Olwo lugero olw'okubuzaabuza, kubanga okumu ku kuwangula kwa Sitaani kuva ku kyama kye eky'obulimba, era awanguddwa, n'olwekyo okuwangula kwe okw'ekiseera kiwedde mu kulemererwa okw'amaanyi era tajja kuwona nnyanja ye. Temumutya, naye mutegeere ebigambo by'Ekigambo kyange mu ntegeera yaabyo ey'omu Bayibuli, so temuba bamusiru era abatamanyi mazima.

Abaana ba Katonda be balinzi mu ntegeera eno, bamalayika abaateekebwako okuweereza abantu naye mu kifo ky'ekyo ne baleka ekifo kyabwe eky'olubereberye, kubanga beegomba abawala b'abantu. Kubanga nagamba ki Yuda okulanga ku lwange? Ne Bamalayika abataakuuma kifo kyabwe eky'olubereberye, naye ne baleka amaka gaabwe, abakuumidde mu njegere ezitaggwaawo wansi w'ekizikiza, okutuusa ku musango gw'olunaku olukulu. Musome nate so temuba abatalina kukkiriza, naye mukkirize kyokka. Abaana ba Katonda be bamalayika abagwa abeeyonoonera n'abakazi; bangi ku bakazi bano baatwalibwa ne bayisibwa bubi, wadde ng'abamu baakikola ku bwabwe era balisalirwa omusango gwabwe.

Ezzadde lye baazaala mu kweyonoona kwabwe, yee, omukago guno omutali mutukuvu gwazaala abanefuli, ebitonde ebibi ebyakolebwa olw'okuzikiriza kwammwe. Mwekozesa bubi nnyo bwe mwesigama ku bitabo byammwe 66 byokka. Waliwo enyongeza nnyingi era enyongeza yammwe ey'ebitabo 66 eby'Abapolotesitanti terimu bunnabbi bwonna obwali buweereddwa naye erimu ebintu byonna ebikwata ku bulamu n'okutya Katonda mu nze Kristo Yesu. Musome ebitabo bya 1 Enoka ne Yasheri ne Yubiri. Ebyo si byawandiikibwa gye muli naye ddala bye bitabo ebisemba okusoma ebyogerwako era ebikopoolwa mu byawandiikibwa byennyini bye mwagala. Temufugibwa kulowooza nti ebitabo byokka ebikkirizibwa mu nnyiriri emu bye bunnabbi bwonna obwali buweereddwa. Muyigire ku nze.

Naye, Katonda yazikiriza ensi eyaliwo mu biro ebyo, kubanga mukwano gwange Peetero agamba nti: Kino bakireka okukimanya nga baagala, nti olw’ekigambo kya Katonda eggulu lyabangawo okuva edda, n’ensi ng’eyimiridde okuva mu mazzi, era nga eri mu mazzi, ensi eyaliwo mu biro ebyo lwe yazikirira, bwe yasaanyizibwawo n’amataba. Era mu nnaku za Nuuwa, Kitange Omutonzi yaleeta amataba amanene agaasaanyaawo ebyonoono byabwe byonna eby’omuzizo, kubanga baayonoona nnyo ebyaleeta okuzikirira okunene ku nsi. Baamenya entegeka ey'obutonde ey'obulamu ne baleeta obutali buteefu mu ndagabutonde z’omuntu zenyini, omuntu n’atabulwa n’ensolo, ensolo n’etabulwa n'ekyennyanja, era ekika ekimu eky’ensolo ne kizaalibwa n’ekirala okutuusa lwe baafuna eby’omuzizo bye baali baagala. Oluvannyuma eby’omuzizo ebyo ne bizikiriza abantu. Kitange n’abizikiriza, n’awonya emmeeme munaana zokka. Nuuwa yalaba ekisa mu maaso ga Katonda, era Nuuwa n’ab’omu nnyumba ye be bokka abaali tebonooneddwa ku nsi mu kutabula kwabwe okw’endagabutonde, kubanga Nuuwa yali musajja mutuukirivu.

Musome ebigambo byange mutegeere ensi yammwe, kubanga bangi ku mmwe mukkiririza mu bulimba bw’okulumirwa okw’obulimba obwagaana kyonna ekigendererwa mu by’omwoyo era ne kusobozesa okusiruwalaza abantu bangi mu bulimba bwabwe obutali bwa kukkiriza nti ebintu by’oku nsi byokka bye bikulu. Temutegeera nti, baagaana Katonda mu kwogera kwabwe okutaliiko kukkiriza? Baabalimba ku lw’obugenderere n’okuyiga okuggyawo okuyiga: muyige okutegeera enjigiriza eno ey’Abajesuiti.

Naye Olubereberye lugamba ki ku nsonga eno? Mu nnaku ezo waaliwo Abaneefiri mu nsi: era n’oluvannyuma lw’ebyo.

Mubuuza nti, oluvannyuma lw’ennaku ki? Oluvannyuma lw’ennaku za Nuuwa. Kubanga oluvannyuma lw’amataba ensi yagabanyizibwa n’akalulu k’obusika, era mu nnaku za Peregi ensi yagabanyizibwa mu busika wakati w’abaana ba Nuuwa, Seemu, Kaamu ne Yafeesi. Ku lw’ebigendererwa byaffe tujja kugoberera olunyiriri lwa Kaamu, kubanga okuva eri Kaamu mwe mwava olulyo olukolimidde, ne Nnimuloodi okuva e Kuusi n’e Misiri oba Mizulayimu, n’Abakanani ababi ne bava ku Kanani omubi. Kubanga Kanani Nuuwa gwe yakolima. Kubanga Kanani yaleka obusika bwe n’azimba ekibuga Ttuulo ku lubalama olwafuuka olw’Abafoyiniikiya ne kituuka e Sidoni Yezebyeri gye yava. Yakikola kino alyoke abeerenga okumpi n’Olusozi Kerumooni, abakuumi we baaserengetera era nange gye nnafuulirwa ekifaananyi.

Kanani yazuula ekyo abakuumi kye baaleka, n’akozesa amagezi gaabwe aga bamalayika abagwa mu kuloga okw’amaanyi, era obulombolombo bungi obw’omuzizo bwakolebwa Abakanani, kubanga baawangayo n’abaana baabwe. Baali babi nnyo era baasaasaana nnyo n’asobola okusiiga ensi ya Kanani eby’omuzizo bye yali yakola obupya, Setaani mwe yakoleranga olw’ebyonoono bye. Bwe kityo nga Kitange Omutonzi bw’aggya Abaebbulaniya mu busibe okuva e Misiri, bwe yamuwa n’omulimu gw’okuggya obusika bwabwe mu bika bino eby’omuzizo eby’abaneefiri. (Mukyayinza obutakkiriza Bigambo byange?) Temusomanga alipoota embi eyaleetebwa abakessi ekkumi ku kkumi n’ababiri Musa be yatuma mu nsi y’e Kanani okugiketta? Kubanga Yoswa ne Kalebu bokka be boogera ebigambo eby’okukkiriza, naye alipoota embi eyaleetebwa yali ya mazima ddala, okuggyako nti yali alipoota y’ekibi, etalina kukkiriza eyagaana ebigambo bya Kitange Omutonzi n’esinga okukkiriza kye baalaba n’amaaso gaabwe, kubanga Katonda munnene okusinga amaaso g’omuntu yenna, mmwe baana bange abato. Musome alipoota mu Ekyamateeka: Naye abasajja abaalinnyana naye ne bagamba nti, Tetuyinza kulumba bantu abo; kubanga batusinga amaanyi. Ne baleetera abaana ba Isiraeri alipoota embi ku nsi gye baali bakette, nga bagamba nti, Ensi gye twayitamu okugiketta, ye nsi erya abantu baayo; n’abantu bonna be twalaba omwo baali basajja ba kigere kinene. Era eyo gye twalaba abaneefiri, batabani ba Anaki, abava mu baneefiri: ne twefaananyiriza mu maaso gaffe ng’enzige, era bwe tutyo bwe twali mu maaso gaabwe.

Bwe kityo Yoswa n’alongooza ensi okuva mu by’omuzizo bino ebibi, era tebaali abo be muyinza kuyita bantu; byali bitonde ebivangamu eby’omuzizo ebyakolebwa okuzikiriza olunyiriri lwa Kristo, nga Setaani bwe yali ayagala okuziyiza okujja kwange okusooka. Naye abaana ba Isiraeri tebaatuukiriza n’obwesigwa omulimu Kitange Omutonzi gwe yabawa ogw’okubaggyako obusika bwabwe abanyazi abakambwe. Ebitonde bino bingi byali birya bantu; temusomanga nti ensi yalya abantu baayo? Kubanga Ekyabalamuzi kitugamba ki ku nsonga eno: Mukama n’abeera ne Yuda, n’agoba abantu b’oku lusozi, naye teyasobola kugoba bantu b’oku lusenyi, kubanga baalina amagaali ag’ebyuma.

Era Ekyabalamuzi kigamba nate nti: Newaakubadde Manase teyagoba bantu b’omu Besuseani, n’ebyalo byakyo, newaakubadde Taanaki n’ebyalo byakyo, newaakubadde abantu b’omu Doli, n’ebyalo byakyo, newaakubadde abantu b’omu Ibuleyamu, n’ebyalo byakyo, newaakubadde abantu b’omu Megido, n’ebyalo byakyo: naye Abakanani baali basalawo okubeera mu nsi eyo. Awo olwatuuka Isiraeri bwe yaba n’amaanyi, ne basoloozesa Abakanani omusolo, so tebaabagoba ddala. Kubanga Okuva lugamba ki ku nsonga eno: Naye bwe mutaligoba bantu ba mu nsi mu maaso gammwe, kale kiribaawo nti abo be mulireka ku bo, baliba nnyindo mu maaso gammwe, n’amaggwa mu mbiriizi zammwe, era balibonyaabonya mu nsi mwe munaatuulanga.

Bwe kityo ekyawandiikibwa ne kituukirira era ebika by’Abakanani bikyabonyaabonya abaana ba Katonda n’okutuusa leero. Beegattise mu bitonde bya Katonda ne beekweka mu lwatu, nga bakozesa n’obulombolombo n’empisa z’amawanga gaabwe. Naye enkomerero ye y’emu buli mulundi: Bazikiriza mu bugenderevu amawanga gaabwe (Katonda yabafuula abadduki n’abatambuze mu nsi). Boogedde obulimba obunene ne batunda obulimba obwo ng’amazima. Bamenye amawanga gammwe, era temubalaba, kubanga muzibye amaaso ku makubo gaabwe kubanga bangi ku mmwe mwakola nabo endagaano, ne lwe kuba okuŋŋaaniro lya Setaani.

Mudde eri Ekigambo kyange ekitukuvu era muleme kufaayo ku bigambo n’amakubo g’Abedomu. Mulekere awo okugulumiza ebigambo eby’ekibi okusinga Ekigambo kya Kitange Omutonzi Ekitukuvu Ennyo. Kubanga bangi ku Bakanani bano baafuuka Abaidume mu kiseera ekyo ne babeera kumpi ne Yuda. Yokaana Hyrcanus bwe yawamba Idumea, yakyusa Abaidume okudda mu Buyudaaya, Yudeya n’ezaalibwa okuva mu kugattibwa kwa Yuda ne Idumea. Era bwe ntyo ne mbawakanya mu Isiraeri emyaka nga 2000 egiyise, era okuyimirira kwange kwawandiikibwa mu njiri mulyoke mutambulirenga nga bwe nnatambulira. Ebigambo byabwe byakyusa Tora, era obulombolombo bwabwe obw’akamwa n’emikolo gyabwe egy’ekyama okuva e Babulooni ne byekwesa mu nsi yange, kubanga ensi yange yatwalibwa abalinzi, Okuŋŋaaniro lya Setaani ddala.

Okuva mu Olubereberye 3:15 wabaddewo olutalo lw’ensigo wakati wa Katonda ne Setaani era ensigo ya Setaani yennyini erwanira obukulu, so nga taliba nabwo. Nawangudde, mmwe baana bange, era mbawadde amaanyi, mmwe baana bange, okulwanyisa ebika byonna eby’obubi, so tewali kiribaziyiza n’akatono; kubanga nze, Yesu Kristo, nawangudde obwakabaka bw’omulabe. Mubayimirirewo era muleme kukuza makubo gaabwe amabi. Muve mu bo kubanga befuulidde ddala nga mmwe. Temukkiririza lugero lwange olw’eŋŋaano n’omuddo omubi? N’abagerera olugero olulala, ng’agamba nti; Obwakabaka bw’omu ggulu bufaananyizibwa omusajja eyasiga ensigo ennungi mu nnimiro ye: Naye abantu bwe baali beebase, omulabe we n’ajja n’asiga omuddo omubi mu ŋŋaano, n’agenda. Muve mu bo mweyawule okuva mu kuŋŋaaniro lya Setaani abagamba nti Bayudaaya so si balimba. Kubanga Isiraeri asaasaanyizibbwa mu Bamawanga, so nze saatumibwa wabula eri endiga ezabula ez’ennyumba ya Isiraeri; bwe kityo ebiseera by’Abamawanga binaatera okuggwaawo, era nfuulidde mu babiri, omuntu omu omuggya mu nze, bwe ntyo ne ndekawo emirembe.

Baanange, mbawa okwekenneenya okumpi ebyafaayo byammwe, kubanga ebyafaayo by’abantu biva ku Nuuwa okutuuka ku Adamu, era Katonda yakola amawanga gonna mu musaayi gumu. Baanange, obudde bunaatera okuggwaawo, naye ekyo tekitegeeza nti omulimu gwammwe guwedde. Nkyayita abaana bange okunnyimirira n’okumpeereza. Kubanga mbafudde abaweereza abasobola ab’Endagaano Empya mu musaayi gwange. Temusomanga we nnagambira abagoberezi bange nti bwe banaagamba olusozi luno nti luggyibweewo lusuulibwe mu nnyanja, era bwe batabuusabuusa, banaaba n’ebintu bye balagira? Mbabuulirira okuddayo emabega olunyiriri lumu, kubanga ekyo ekyogera kitandika n’okuba n’okukkiriza mu Katonda (kubanga eri Katonda tewali kiyinzika) era temuyinza kukola kintu kyonna nga tewali nze, mmwe baana bange abato.

Bangi ku mmwe mwayigirizibwa obulimba bw’Abedomu obubaleetera okuyingira mu makubo gaabwe. Naye bwe muba n’okukkiriza mu Katonda, muliragira ekitasoboka, era mulyetaaga okukola ekyo ddala mu mulimu gwe mbatuma. Mbatuma, mmwe baana bange, okukuŋŋaanya endiga ezisigaddewo, kubanga bangi balijja gyendi mu ddakiika zino ezisembayo so siribagaana, naye mbanoonya n’okwagala okungi. Mwogere n’okukkiriza munnyimirire era muweereze abaana bange.

Mbabuulidde engeri y’okukozesaamu amaanyi gange, naye mugaana okuyigirizibwa kwange, kale nja kugambira nate abo abeerabira mu mmwe. Lwaki mwesabira mu ngeri ey’obutiitiizi omwoyo okuva mu mutima gwagwo nga mwekwasa emimwa era nga mukozesa erinnya lyange, Yesu Kristo, mu ngeri etalimu kukkiriza? Temukozesanga erinnya lyange mu bukyamu! Mulagire emyoyo mu linnya lyange n’okukkiriza okungi era mube n’okukkiriza mu bigambo bye nja okubawa, era emyoyo emibi girigenda lwa maanyi gange okuyita mu mmwe!

Lwaki musaba obulwadde mu ngeri ey’obutiitiizi obulwadde buveewo so nga mwandibadde mwogera n’obulwadde obwo n’obwesige mu linnya lyange ne mulagira ekitasoboka mu linnya lyange ne muwonya abalwadde. Gamba olusozi olwo nti, luggyibweewo lusuulibwe mu nnyanja, muwonye abalwadde, mugobe emyoyo, muzuukize abafu era mulagire ekitasoboka, kubanga bwe ntyo bwe nsobozeserezza.

Temusomanga nti ow’olubereberye aliba ow’enkomerero, n’ow’enkomerero aliba ow’olubereberye? Mumanye, mmwe baana bange, nti ŋŋenda mu maanyi amanene okusinga bwe nnakola mu kyasa ekyasooka, era nnafuuye omwoyo gwange mu kigero ekijjuvu okwetooloola ensi. Ani ku mmwe alina obuvumu okumpanirira? Okunjogerera ebigambo bye mbawa? Ani ku mmwe alina okukkiriza obutadiba ku alipoota embi naye n’akuba omutwe omubi? Kubanga ali mu mmwe munene okusinga ali mu nsi. Temubanga bawulize eri Omukanani, kubanga bakolimidde okuba abawulize gye muli. Ate era temukwatibwanga ku bika by’amawanga, kubanga nnatumibwa mu mawanga gonna ndyoke ndokole abamu, era mu kukola bwe ntyo, nnunula Isiraeri ow’amazima, kubanga asuuliddwa ebweru mu mawanga, era mwenna muli Isiraeri ow’amazima owa Katonda!

Munnyimirire era munjogerere, kubanga obudde bunaatera okuggwaawo. Munaaba omu ku abo abalisemberera entebe yange ey’omusango nga mukuŋŋaanyizza enku, essubi, n’ebisasiro okulwanyisa olunaku olwo olunene olusemberera? Oba munaampa zaabu, ffeeza, n’amayinja ag’omuwendo omungi, kubanga abantu bange be ggwanika lyange. Musumulule abasibe mukolere wamu nange mu ddakiika zino ezisembayo. Era awatali kuwakana, ekyama ky’okutya Katonda kinene: Katonda yalabikira mu mubiri, n’atuukirizibwa mu Mwoyo, n’alabibwa Bamalayika, n’abuulirirwa Abamawanga, n’akkirizibwa mu nsi, n’atwalibwa mu kitiibwa.

Baanange, nze Kigambo eyafuuka omubiri, era nze ne Kitange tuli omu. Mu ngeri y’emu, mufuuke omu naffe olw’okumpulira kwammwe, kubanga mangu nnyo muliba nga nze era mulitwalibwa mu kitiibwa, kubanga nze bibala ebibereberye okuva mu bafu, era nja kubawa ekkanisa ey’ekitiibwa, etalina bbala, newaakubadde enkunyikunyi, newaakubadde ekintu kyonna ekifaanana bwe kityo; naye ereme kubeerenga ntuukirivu era etaliiko kamogo. Obudde bunaatera okuggwaawo, kale temulwawo. Mukolere wamu nange mu ddakiika zino ezisembayo, era temulwawo kwogera. Nja kubaleetera, kale mukole emirimu gyange muleme kufaayo ku gyammwe. Nja kukola emirimu mingi egy’amaanyi n’emikono gyammwe, mwemale okubuusabuusa!

Nze nsigo eyasuubizibwa ey’omukazi.

Nze nsigo eyasuubizibwa eyatumibwa Katonda.
