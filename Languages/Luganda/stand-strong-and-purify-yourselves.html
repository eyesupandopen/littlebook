Munywerekere Era Mwetukuze

Mukama omwesigwa agamba abalonde be era abeesigwa nti: Munywerekere era mwetukuze nga muteeka wansi ebibi byammwe ne mubyatula. Mufulume mu ye, abantu bange, era mweyawule ku bibi bye muleme okusasulira wamu naye mu kibonerezo kye n'ebikolimo bye. Mubuuza nti Ye ani? Si ye Babulooni Omukulu, oyo atuukiddwako okusalirwa omusango? Si ye malaaya omukulu atamidde omusaayi gw'abatukuvu? Nja kubawa obukuumi n'emirembe, era nja kubakulembera okubatuusa mu nnyumba ya Kitange mu buddukiro okutuusa ekiseera kino lwe kiriggwaawo.

Nze Mukama wammwe Yesu Kristo, eyabayiita okujja mu kitiibwa n'obulungi, kale temuggwaamu maanyi mu kukola obulungi. Nabawa eddoboozi ly'okunjogerera n'okulangirira okujja kwange okuli okumpi. Naye bangi mu Kkanisa yange eddoboozi lyabwe lyababulira era ne baggwaamu okukkiriza mu kusuubira Kitange kwe yabateekera mu Kigambo kye. Temuba batakkiriza, wabula mukkirize bwannamunigina. Munoonyeze obubonero obw'okujja kwange, nga Kitange bw'abyalangiridde mu ggulu era bimanyiddwa dda bulungi. Lwaki mukyaludde okukkiriza? Mubeere n'obwesige mu nze, abaana bange, kubanga mbayamba; weewaawo, buli ttabi eritaliibala bibala ndisalako. Mubeere n'obukakafu mu Bwami bwange ku lwammwe era mweyongereyo mu bulokozi bwange nga mukolera obulokozi bwammwe mwennyini n'okutya n'okukankana. Mukyukire nze mu kusaba era munsabe mbayolese ebibi byammwe mubyatule gye ndi era mubiwangule olw'omusaayi gwange. Kale temutya, kubanga obulokozi bwammwe buli kumpi; weewaawo, akakono ak'obusekkonda kanaatera okumalayo okwetooloola kwako okusembayo mu mulembe gwange ogw'ekisa.

Mukyukire nze mujje gye ndi kubanga mbateeseteesezza ekifo ku mmeeza yange era mbategekedde obuddukiro mu nnyumba ya Kitange okwewonya tsunami w'ebibonyoobonyo ebinaatera okujja. Bisemberera abo abali ku nsi, n'ekibonerezo ekinene kinaatera okugwa ku nsi yonna. Temwegaana linnya lyange! Mmanyi nti mukooye mu ssaawa eno esembayo, naye muwummulire mu nze era munfunemu amaanyi mulangirire okujja kwange okuli okumpi. Saabalagulira lunaku oba ssaawa gye mulindirire, kubanga mwandyesezze obulamu bwammwe bwonna ku kintu ekyo. Naye temulowooza nti tekiriiwo: olunaku n'essaawa bimanyiddwa mu ggulu mu kiseera kino, era bamalayika bange bali beetegefu, balindiridde okufuuwa kw'ekkondeere lwe nja okukuŋŋaanya abo abeebase mu ttaka okusooka, naabo abalamu abasigaddewo baliyungibwa nabo okunsisinkana mu bbanga, era nja kubatwala mu busika bwammwe Kitange bwe yakweka mu nze. Mukkirize bwannamunigina.

Kijja kubaawo mu kkubagiza kw'eriiso, bangi ku mmwe lwe muliba nga muli ku mirimu gyammwe egya bulijjo, era amangu ago muliyimirira mu maaso gange nga mbaleeta mu maaso ga Kitange; era ffenna twagala nnyo olunaku olwo nga bwe twagala okubeera nammwe. Mazima ddala nandyelabise ne mbuulira bangi, era ani ayinza okugamba nti ekyo si kye nkoze ebbanga? Mujja kulangirira ekyo Mukama ky'ayinza okukola? Oba mujja kukomya obusobozi bwange n'obwami bwange olw'obutakkiriza bwammwe? Tekisoboka! Kyokka, nabafuula bakozi bannange ne mbatwala mu mulimu Kitange gwe yampa okutuukiriza, era mumanye nti kitiibwa era ssanyu lye nabawa olw'obulokozi bwammwe, empeera yammwe, n'obulamu bwammwe obujjuvu.

Bwe muba nga muweerezza abalala mu linnya lyange, nange sibawerezza? Muweeyo kyereere so temukweka kumanya kwange okw'okujja, wadde nga bangi bajja kugaana era mujja kubalibwa ng'abalalufu. Obulimba bw'omulabe bujja kusaanuukira mu maaso g'ebigambo byammwe ebijjudde amaanyi nga mwogera ku lwange era nga mulangirira okulabika kwange era nga mulangirira ekibonerezo ekigenda okujja, kubanga ne mu busekkonda buno obusembayo abamu bajja kulokolebwa. Bangi tebeetegefu era bajja kugaana ebigambo byammwe; mubayagale n'ekisa, kubanga ekibiina ekinene kijja kulokolebwa mu bizibu ebinene n'okubonyaabonyezebwa, era mumanye nti ebigambo byammwe biyinza okubeera omumuli ogw'okumulisa n'essuubi mu bizibu bye bayinza okwolekagana nabyo.

O mmwe abatuula ku nsi abagaanye okuyitibwa kwange ne mwebala nti temusaana kirabo kyange eky'obulokozi n'ebirala byonna: Ekibonerezo ekinene kilangiriddwa ku nsi era kinaatera okubatuukako, wadde nga mwerimbisa nga mugamba nti, "Nnina entegeka nnene ku lwange era enkya eriba nga leero!" Ggwe omusirusiru! Totegeera na kukyuka kwa biseera, kubanga eggulu lirangirira embaga y'omugole wange gye ndi mu ggulu, era eggulu likyuka okuva mu kisa okudda mu kibonerezo mu maaso gammwe. Akatyabaga akanene kajja kubajjira mu bbanga ttono, era bangi bajja kufiira mu bibi byabwe. Si kwaagala kwange okuzikirira, naye mugaana ssaddaaka yange etalina kibi ku lwammwe eyandibaloKoledde ddala.

Ensi gy'omanyi leero ejja kubula mu maaso go, n'ebisanyusa byonna eby'ekibi mu bulamu buno tebijja kuba mikwano gyo; naye ojja kuyigganyizibwa era oyiggibwe eggye ly'abalabe bo abalulunkanira okuzikirira kwo era abalulunkanira eby'obugagga byo. Wajja kubaawo okukankana okw'amaanyi wansi w'ebigere byo, n'ensi ejja kweyabuluzaamu, era bangi bajja kugwa mu nsi. Abakulembeze bo baakulyamu dda olukwe, wadde nga mu busirusiru obwo mukyabatunuulira, nga mugezaako okusalawo ku kalulu ko. Ebigo byo bijja kugwa, n'entindo zo zijja kugwa, n'ebikolebwa byo bijja kubula mu maaso go, era tewali kijja kuba kye kimu nate.

Munaaddukira wa nga mwagaanye Mukama omutukuvu, nze, Mukama Yesu Kristo? Ani ajja kubalokola kaakano okuva mu kiseera kyammwe eky'ekibonerezo? O, naye mugamba nti, Sisaanira kibonerezo, kiki ky'otegeeza? Temwagaana Mutonzi wammwe bwe yantuma gye muli olw'ekisa kye ekirokola, ekisa ekirokola kye mwerowooza nti temusaana kufuna? Lwaki mwangaana? Kibi ki kye nnakola? Nawaayo ettone lyange essembayo ery'omusaayi mu kubonyaabonyezebwa okunene n'obulumi obw'amaanyi musobole okuba abalamu mu maaso gange, naye mukyagaana okukkiriza abajulirwa bange be nnatuma mu linnya lyange.

Ne bwe mbalabula ku katyabaga akammwe akanaatera okujja, nze newaakubadde Kitange tetulina ssanyu lyonna mu ekyo ekigenda okuteebwa. Naye nga bwe mwasanyusibwanga ekibi, kaakano mujja kumiribwa balamu ebyo ebiva mu kibi bye mweterekera. Nga bwe mwalulunkana ssente, eby'obugagga, n'ettutumu mu by'ensimbi, temwakola kirala wabula okwetuuma obusungu ku mmeeza yammwe ey'eky'eggulo, era mujja kulya ku kubonyaabonyezebwa n'obulumi obw'amaanyi. Nja kubawa ekizikiza kye mwagala ennyo. Ekizikiza kijja kugwa ku nsi yammwe era kijja kubuutikira ensi, ekizikiza ekikutte ennyo n'enjuba ey'omu ttuntu erirabika ng'ezikiziddwa. Mungamba nti, Kibi ki kye nnina ekireeta obusungu bwo; tetwakola mirimu mingi egy'amaanyi mu linnya lyo?

Mazima ddala, mwakola emirimu mingi, naye si gye mirimu gye nabawa; nedda, mufuuse bannabbi ab'obulimba, abalagula ebigambo ebiva mu mubiri gwammwe omukyankalamu. Nedda, mukola emirimu gyammwe olw'ekitiibwa kyammwe, kubanga mufuudde embuto zammwe katonda wammwe. Mukwata ebigambo ebirungi n'emboozi ennungi ez'abasajja ababi okusinga Ekigambo kyange ekitukuvu. Si nze Kigambo eyafuuka omubiri? Mwandiridde ku nze omugaati ogw'obulamu ne muwona okuzikirira, ne mutaddamu kulumwa njala. Mwandinywedde ku migga gy'amazzi amalamu egikulukuta okuva mu nze; weewaawo, mwandinywedde ku mwoyo gwange ne mutaddamu kulumwa nnyonta. Naye mu kungaanira kwammwe, mujja kugenda mu nnjala ennene n'okubonaabona, wadde nga okutuusa kati mukyalowooza nti tebijja kubajjira mu kaseera katono. Ebyenfuna byammwe bizikiriziddwa, n'obugagga bwe mwalulunkana bujja kuba bwa bwereere mu kaseera katono. Muteese obwesige bwammwe mu bafuzi b'ekizikiza kino era ne munywera ku bulimba bwa Setaani okusinga omusana gw'amazima gange agaliwo bulijjo.

Kyemunaava munoonya ekifo ku mmeeza yaabwe, ne mukizuula nti tewali kifo kye babategekedde. Bajja kuba n'ebingi bye beeterekera, era mmwe mujja kubifuna nga mututte akabonero k'ensolo yokka. Newankubadde ensolo eno tennabikkulibwa mu kiseera kyayo, nze nkyalabuddeko era ne ngiraga abeesigwa bange baleme okukyamizibwa ebigambo byayo ebirungi n'emboozi zaayo ennungi, wadde nga mu kiseera kino terina bwakabaka, oba yaakabufuna. Temutwala kabonero kaayo, kubanga bonna abakakwata bajja kufuna omugabo gwabwe awamu n'ensolo ne nnabbi ow'obulimba mu nnyanja ey'omuliro, era omukka gw'okubonyaabonyezebwa kwabwe gujja kunyooka emirembe gyonna.

Mulabuddwa, era wadde nga nkyanoonya okubawonya okuzikirira kwammwe, mukyagaana n'obukakanyavu okuyitibwa kwange. Kyennaava mbawa ebibala eby'amakubo gammwe.

Mujja kufuna enjala ennene, entalo ennene, okuzikirizibwa okungi. Amagye amamyuufu gajja kubayigganya, okubatulugunya, n'okuzikiriza bangi ku mmwe; era kiribaawo nti mujja kwetooloolwa akabi n'okuzikirira. Oyo atatwalibwa mu bunyage mu kutulugunyizibwa n'okufa oluvannyuma ajja kugwa n'ekitala; oyo atagwa na kitala ajja kusisinkana ensolo z'omu nsiko, ezijja kutta bangi; oyo atazikirizibwa nsolo za mu nsiko ajja kufa na njala, era engeri z'okufa zijja kwala ku nsi.

Okubonaabona okungi, akatyabaga, okuzikirizibwa bijja kubajjira mu kaseera katono, n'amayengo ag'amaanyi ag'ennyanja gajja kujja ku ggwanga lyammwe. Temujja kuba ggwanga lya maanyi nate, naye ensigalira ejja kulokolebwa. Buli muntu alikoowoola erinnya lya Mukama alirokolebwa, naye oyo ayagala obulamu bwe okutuusa ku nkomerero ajja kufuna omugabo gwe mu nnyanja ey'omuliro, era mujja kusasulirwa wamu mu kutulugunyizibwa kwa Setaani n'ekibonerezo kye kubanga mwakutte ku nsi ye efa okutuusa ku mukka gwammwe ogw'obukakanyavu ogusembayo, era mujja kuzikiririra ddala n'ensi eno efa. Mukyukire nze nga obudde bukyaliwo nssobole okubalokola n'amaka gammwe okuva mu katyabaga akajja. Nga bwe waliwo ekibiina ekyalokoka ku lunaku lwa Pentekooti emyaka mingi egiyise mu ddakiika ezisooka ez'omulembe guno, era bwe kityo ekibiina kijja kulokolebwa mu ddakiika ezisembayo ez'omulembe guno, era okuyita kwange kukyaliwo okw'ekiseera ekitono ddala.

Ekibi ekijja tekijja kussa kitiibwa mu bakazi oba abaana oba abakadde oba abalwadde. Nedda, ekibi ekyo kijja kumala byonna by'ekisobola mu kuzikirira n'obulumi obw'amaanyi. Mudddukire gye ndi ng'obudde bukyaliwo, kubanga essaawa enaatera okuyimirira, era tewaliba budde nate. Setaani ajja kusuulibwa ku nsi mu bbanga ttono, n'obwakabaka bwe bujja kuteebwa mu bujjuvu ku nsi yammwe, era alina obusungu bungi kubanga amanyi nti alina akaseera katono. Mukyukire nze nga mukyasobola okuwona ekibi ekigenda okujja.

Nze Mukama wa Bakama era Kabaka wa Bakabaka.

Nze oyo atambulira mu makkati g'ebikondo by'ettabaaza omusanvu era alagira bamalayika omusanvu ab'ekkanisa omusanvu.

Nze oyo alagira amagye g'omu ggulu, kubanga Kitange ateese obuyinza bwago mu mikono gyange.

Nze Kigambo eyafuuka omubiri eyabeera mu mmwe.

Nze mulandira gwa Yese, era obwakabaka bwange tebulina nkomerero.

Nze mwana wa Katonda eyajja mu mubiri okutangirira ebibi bya bonna, singa bonna bakkiriza.

Abo abakkirizza ekisa kyange era ababadde abeesigwa bajja kufuga nange mu bwakabaka bwange era bajja kugabana ku ntebe yange ey'obwakabaka, kubanga mbafudde bakabaka ne bakabona mu maaso ga Katonda waffe. Waliwo empeera ennene gye mwateekerwa mu ggulu; munywerere era mubeere beesigwa eddakiika ntono endala.
