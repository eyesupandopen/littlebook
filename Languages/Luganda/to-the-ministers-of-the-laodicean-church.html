Omujulirwa Omulundi Ogw'okubiri: Ekkanisa
Mu Biwandiiko bya Pawulo Omutume
May 06, 2024

Ggwe Abaweereza b'Ekkanisa y'e Lawodikiya: Muwulire Ebigambo Byange!

Mugole wange era Kkanisa yange, mpulire mu kwagala kwe nkwagala, mmwe baana bange, era mutege amatu eri ebigambo byange. Muwulire Ebigambo byange, mmwe abaweereza bange b'e Lawodikiya, mmwe be nnawa ebirabo. Kubanga nnagaba ebirabo eri abantu, era nnawa abamu okuba abatume, abalala bannabbi, abalala ababuulizi b'enjiri, abalala abasumba, n'abalala abayigiriza. Ebirabo bino nnabibawa olw'okutuukiriza abatukuvu, olw'omulimu gw'obuweereza, olw'okuzimba omubiri gwa Kristo. Kubanga kiki kye nnagamba abatume bange n'abayigirizwa bange mu biwandiiko by'enjiri ya Yokaana: Era laba, nja kubatumira ekisuubizo kya Kitange. Naye mubeere mu kibuga Yerusaalemi, okutuusa lwe munaayambazibwa amaanyi agava waggulu.

Ne mu Ebikolwa by'Abatume bange mwawandiikibwa okutuukirizibwa kw'ekisuubizo kino: Awo olunaku lwa Pentekoote bwe lwatuuka, bonna baali wamu n'omwoyo gumu mu kifo kimu. Amangwago ne wava eddoboozi mu ggulu ng'ery'embuyaga ey'amaanyi, ne lijjula ennyumba yonna mwe baali batudde. Ne walabikira gye bali ennimi ez'enjawulo, ng'ez'omuliro, ne zituula ku buli omu ku bo. Bonna ne bajjula Omwoyo Omutukuvu, ne batandika okwogera mu nnimi endala, nga Omwoyo bwe yabawa okwogera. Era okutandikibwa kw'Ekkanisa yange kwawandiikibwa bwetyo mu byawandiikibwa mulyoke mumanye okutuukirizibwa kw'ekisuubizo kino ekinene eky'enjiri ennungi, nti mbajjuzizza amaanyi agava waggulu, mu kuzaalibwa okw'omulundi ogw'okubiri, mu nze, Kristo Yesu Mukama wammwe. Era nze mutwe gw'omubiri, Ekkanisa: nze ntandikwa, omubereberye mu bafu, nsobole okuba owa waggulu mu byonna: Kubanga kyasanyusa Kitange nti mu NZE mubeeremu okujjuza kwonna.

Mmwe abaweereza bange b'e Lawodikiya, okujjuza kwange tekubeera mu kkanisa zammwe ze mwakola mu bifaananyi byammwe, kubanga nze nnyimiridde ku luggi era nkonkona! Mmwe abaweereza bange b'e Lawodikiya, saakekera mu bweyagalize okujjuza kwonna okwa Kitange okuli mu nze, naye nnafuka omwoyo gwange ne mbafuula ab'amaanyi, era mbateereddeko akabonero n'omwoyo ogwo omutukuvu ogw'ekisuubizo. Tewali kiyinza kumenya kabonero ako, newakubadde nga muyinza okusalawo okutambula okugwa mu bujeemu. Naye akabonero ka Kitange, ye Yahweh Katonda ataggwaawo, tekayinza kumenyeka; kubanga tekiyinzika kumenya kabonero ka Katonda, era muli banywevu mu nze bwe muba abeesigwa mu nze, kubanga ebisuubizo byange bya ye ne amiina!

Mmwe abaweereza bange b'e Lawodikiya, ebirabo byange bye nabawa mubisuuliridde olw'engeri zammwe ez'ekivuguto, era muzikirizza omwoyo gwe nabawa, era mugaana okuggulawo ebirabo bye nabawa, era muleka ebirabo byange nga tebigguddwaawo mu butafaayo n'obunafu. Kino si kintu kitono eky'okutwala nga ekyangu, kubanga kibi kinene ku mutemwa gwammwe, era temuwulira ddoboozi lyange. Temussa kitiibwa mu bigambo byange, era temussa kitiibwa mu makubo gange newakubadde okugayigiriza. Kubanga kiki kye nnawandiikisa Pawulo eri omukulembeze omuto ow'Ekkanisa yange eyali yaakatandikibwawo, ye Timoseewo: Tonyoomanga kirabo ekikuli munda, ekyakuweebwa ng'okuyita mu Bunnnabbi, n'okuteekebwako emikono gy'omukadde. Bino byegezesengamu, era weeweeremu ddala, kirabike ng'ogaggawala mu byonna.

Singa mwandisomye ebigambo byange n'okutegeera! Singa mwandijjukidde ebigambo byange mu kukkiriza, naye temutegeera era temulina kukkiriza! Singa mwandigondedde ebigambo byange, kubanga okungondera nze, Mukama wammwe Yesu Kristo, kugondera nsulo ya bulamu, era mu nda zammwe muliva emigga gy'amazzi amalamu, kubanga nabawa omwoyo gwange. Naye mu kifo ky'okusoma ebigambo byange, mutunuulira za kompyuta zammwe mu butawulize obutaliiko magezi; muva ku kompyuta emu ne mudda ku ndala, nga musanyusibwa kibi! Era muli basiru nnyo okulowooza nti obutawulize obutaliiko magezi bwe kukkiriza okukola mu nze, era mubuliriddwa. Musuuliridde ebirabo bye nabawa, omuntu omuggya, era mu kifo ky'ekyo mumaze ebbanga nga mwegezesa mu muntu wammwe omukadde mu butaliimu nga munoonya okumatiza omubiri gwammwe.

Mu kifo ky'okwegezesa mu by'omwoyo, mwegezesa mu bu-laalaala emibiri gyammwe egigenda givunda era gisaanawo, kubanga okwegezesa omubiri kugasa akaseera katono, era emibiri gyammwe gisaanawo. Kubanga eddoboozi lyange liwulirwa okuyita mu biseera okuva mu bbaluwa ya Pawulo eri Abefeeso: Bwe yamala okuggyawo mu mubiri gwe obulabe, ye etteeka ly'ebiragiro ebiri mu mateeka, alyoke afuule mu ye bombi omuntu omu omuggya, bw'atyo n'aleeta emirembe. Mumpulire, baana bange, kubanga nkwagala era nfaayo ku myoyo gyammwe, n'mmwe Ab'e Lawodikiya ab'ekivuguto. Kubanga musinga kutambula mu muntu wammwe omukadde n'amakubo ge, ye mubiri gwammwe omwononefu, kubanga gwe musinga okulaga okwagala. Mu kifo ky'ekyo, mwanditadde ebirowoozo byammwe n'emitima gyammwe ku nze na buli kigambo kyange: Kubanga omuntu taba mulamu na mmere yokka, wabula na buli kigambo ekiva mu kamwa ka Katonda. Kubanga okugondera ebigambo byange n'amaaso agali kungulu (ye Kigambo kya Katonda, kubanga nze Kigambo eyafuuka omubiri era mbeera mu mmwe era munda mu mmwe), mutambulira mu mwoyo so si mu mubiri.

Kubanga nagamba Ekkanisa y'e Kkolinso bino wammanga: Naye ffe fenna, nga tutunuulira mu ndabirwamu n'amaaso agali kungulu ekitiibwa kya Mukama waffe, tukyusibwa mu kifaananyi kye kimu, okuva mu kitiibwa okutuuka mu kitiibwa, nga bwe kiri olw'omwoyo gwa Mukama waffe. Baana bange, amabaluwa gange eri Ekkanisa yange (ye mabaluwa g'okwagala ge nnawandiikira Mugole wange) ge ndabirwamu y'engeri yammwe ey'amazima, kubanga engeri yammwe ey'amazima yakwekebwa mu kyama ekinene ekya Katonda. Olw'okugondera ebigambo byange n'ebiragiro byange mujja kutambulira mu mwoyo, so temujja kuwa mubiri gwammwe omwononefu kye gwagala. Ebyawandiikibwa byonna birina okussibwamu ekitiibwa n'okuyigibwa, naye muli kyama ekyakwekebwa mu Katonda era kati kibikkuliddwa, era mulina okuyiga kye nnabikkulira Pawulo okutegeera ekitonde ekipya kye mbafuulira mu nze, Kristo Yesu.

Mmwe abaweereza bange b'e Lawodikiya, mulimu ensobi ddala. Muggiddewo ekitiibwa kyonna eri ebigambo byange, era musuuliridde ebirabo bye nabawa okutuusa lwe mukyawona naye ne muwaayo ekibiina kyange eri eddagala n'akambe; kubanga singa mwandibadde bagonvu eri nze, nandikkirizza eby'amagero okukolebwa emikono gyammwe. Naye mussa ekitiibwa mu bigambo by'abasumba abajeemu okusinga ebigambo bya Yeshua HaMashiach, era ekibiina kyange eky'e Lawodikiya kisasula omuwendo gw'obutawulize bwammwe; kubanga temukyabafaako, naye mu kifo ky'ekyo mubaleka nga babonaabona mu kibi ekitaliimu maanyi. Abasumba abajeemu bategeerekeka n'ebigambo bye nnawa Yokaana mu bbaluwa ye eyasooka: Baana bato, buno bwe buseera obw'enkomerero: era nga bwe mwawulira nti Omulabe wa Kristo ajja, ne kaakano waliwo abalabe ba Kristo bangi, kye tuva tumanya nti buno bwe buseera obw'enkomerero. Baava mu ffe, naye tebaali ba ku ffe: kubanga singa baali ba ku ffe, tebandiremyewo kubeera naffe: naye baavaayo balyoke bamanyisibwe nti tebaali ffenna.

Temutegeera nti abo abafugibwa emyoyo egirwanyisa Kristo baleetawo Yesu omulala mu kifo ky'omusumba ow'amazima, nze Kristo Yesu? Kubanga abo be balabe b'omusalaba gwa Kristo, era bandwanyisa, era beeteeka mu kifo kyange, nga banoonya okunyaga obuyinza bwange, ekintu ekitasoboka. Bano beenyigidde mu kkanisa zammwe, mu masomero gammwe g'eddiini, mu bitongole byammwe eby'enjawulo, era bafuga amakkanisa gammwe olw'okukkiriza kwammwe okukolera wansi wa 501(c)(3), kubanga bonna abali wansi w'endagaano eyo basibiddwa emimwa gavumenti z'ensi. Za 501(c)(3) zammwe ziraga okwegomba kwammwe okw'empeera embi, kubanga mwetunze wansi w'amakubo ga Balaamu, era muwa ebigambo eby'obulimba olw'ensimbi mulyoke mwekwatire ensimbi zonna abaana bange abato ze babawa mu bwambazi bwammwe obusonyiyibwa omusolo! Mufuuse mukwano gw'ensi, era ensi ejja kujja okutwala kyonna ekigattiddwa ku linnya lyange omulemese bw'aliggibwawo. Temulina kusosola kwa mwoyo era muli bazibe ba maaso! Temulina kufaayo kukuuma kisibo ku misumaali gino egirya ennyama, kubanga mussa nnyo omukago ku mannya agagattiddwa ku mannya gammwe, amannya ge sitaabawa; naye mugakwata olugambo. Kubanga nabagamba okwegombanga ennyo ebirabo ebisinga obulungi: Era nate mbalaga ekkubo erisinga obulungi: ekkubo erisinga obulungi lye mugaana mu ngeri yammwe ey'obugayaavu, ey'ekivuguto.

Temukyabuulira kibi n'okwenenya okuva mu kibi, naye mubuulira enfumo ezisanyusa ezitava gye ndi, era enfumo zammwe zizimbiddwa ku bigambo bya bulimba era zireeta amakubo amakyamu; kubanga temuli kirala wabula omugani asanyusa amatu g'abagayaavu. Njogera nammwe, mmwe abatume bange: kubanga amaaso gammwe gazibye, era temukyalaba kitangaala kyange, era mulangirira bulimba! Njogera nammwe, mmwe bannabbi bange: kubanga mwogerera Yesu omulala, era musuuliridde ebigambo byange, era temummanyi, era mulagulira eby'omumaaso eby'obulimba! Njogera nammwe, mmwe ababuulizi bange b'enjiri: Kubanga mubuulira amawanga enjigiriza enkyamu ez'emizimu, era mubanywesa amazzi amacaafu, kubanga mubajjuzza emyoyo emitali mirongoofu! Njogera nammwe, mmwe abasumba bange: Kubanga mufaayo ku kwegomba kwammwe, era mufuusizza embuto zammwe katonda wammwe n'ebitagasa byammwe ebya filozofofiya w'okweyamba; ggwe musiru, toyinza kuwonya ndiga zange na lulimi olulimba! Njogera nammwe, mmwe abayigiriza bange: Kubanga temukyayigiriza bigambo birongoofu bya njiri yange, naye muyigiriza ebigambo by'okukkiriziganya okw'amadiini gonna, era mulimu ensobi ddala mmwenna, mmwe abaweereza bange b'e Lawodikiya. Mwenna mulina obulwadde bwe bumufaananya: Temukyalina kitiibwa mu biragiro ebiri mu Kigambo kyange, era temukyalina kitiibwa mu bigambo by'omu byawandiikibwa byange.

Mu malala mulowooza nti muli bavvuunuzi kubanga muyinza okulondamu amakulu mu nkuluze z'ensi n'ebitabo by'amakulu okutuukana n'ebigendererwa byammwe: Ebitabo bye sitaawandiika, kubanga nze mpandiisa ebyawandiikibwa okuyita mu bannabbi bange, kubanga omwoyo gw'obunnabbi bwe bujulirwa bwa Yesu! Abavvuunuzi aboogera olulimi lumu, era mufuuse kya kusekererwa! Mu kifo ky'ekyo, mussa ekitiibwa mu bigambo by'abasumba abajeemu, ye ssere we yeefuula. Beenyingiza mu bibiina byammwe balyoke beeyite mmwe (kubanga mmwe muli ŋŋaano gye nandyagadde okukung'aanyiza mu tterekero lyange singa mwakyuka ne mujja gye ndi). Naye bo bali ssere, ab'oluganda ab'obulimba abaafuuka abaweereza ab'obulimba ababatambuza bukyamu. Bali kimera kya bukyamu era kya butwa!

Ababi abo tebakyava mu Kkanisa yange okusobola okubikkulwa, naye mmwe mubayingiza ne mubakaka okusangira nammwe era n'okussa ekitiibwa mu bigambo byabwe eby'obulimba n'ennimi zaabwe ez'obulimba okusinga ebigambo bye nnandibawadde, ebigambo bya Katonda Omuzinzi, ebigambo bya Yakuwa! Mutunda ebigambo byange musobole okufuna ekitiibwa kyonna n'ettendo ebyange, Omukama wammwe Yesu Kristo. Mazima ddala mufunye empeera yammwe, era nja kuggyawo empeera yammwe ne ngiwa oyo eyali omwesigwa mu kifo kyammwe. Kubanga temukyayogera ku lwange, era kinneetaagisa okujja mu Kkanisa yange ng'eddoboozi ly'omuntu ayogerera mu ddungu, kubanga mwanggalidde oluggi mu maaso gange, wadde nga nkyayimiridde nkonkona! Ensonyi n'okunyoomebwa okw'amaanyi biri ku mmwe okuggyako nga mwenenyezza!

Bwe munyoomanga ebigambo byange ne mutawulira ddoboozi lyange, bwe muneeyongeranga okuba ab'ebbugumu, nja kubaleka mu nnaku ya Yakobo; kubanga nnagamba edda mmwe Abakyalawoodikia bange nti, nja kubawanika okuva mu kamwa kange okuggyako nga mwenenyezza! Ate era nagamba ku nnabbi omulimba oyo Yezeberi bwe nnawandiikira Tiyatiira, kubanga kiki kye nnagamba Yokaana Omubikkulirwa?

Wabula nnina ebintu bitono bye nkuvunaana, kubanga ogumiikiriza omukazi oyo Yezeberi, eyeeyita Nnabbi, okuyigiriza n'okusendasenda abaweereza bange okwenyigira mu bwenzi, n'okulya ebintu ebisaddakiddwa eri ebifaananyi. Era namuwa ekiseera okwenenya obwenzi bwe, n'ateenenya. Laba, nja kumusuula ku kitanda, n'abo abenyigira naye mu bwenzi, mu kubonaabona okungi, okuggyako nga beenenyezza ebikolwa byabwe.

Temusomanga bigambo bino? Muli bamalala okumala okuloowooza nti Yakuwa oba Yeshua HaMashiach basosola mu bantu? Mulowooza nti nssa ekitiibwa mu mannya ge mweewa okugatunga ku mitwe gy'abantu bange, nga mwefuula abakulu okusinga obusika bwa Katonda?

Reverend ono, Bishop oli; singa temwanyoomanga birabo byange, ekirabo kyammwe kyandibafunidde ekifo mu maaso g'abasajja abakulu era temwandyeetaaze mannya gammwe agatalina mugaso, kubanga nnandibawadde ekitiibwa nammwe nemunzikiriza, naye mmwe temunzisaamu kitiibwa! Waliwo nsobi nnene mu mmwe, n'obusungu bwange bubabuubuukiddeko, kubanga mulemereddwa emirimu gye nabawa n'okusingawo mufuuse abajeemu, era mukulembera abaana bange abato mu bunnya mwe mwasimba eweema zammwe. Musinza amazzi agataali malongoofu mu bunnya bwammwe okusinga amazzi gange amayonjo ag'omwoyo omutukuvu abo bonna abaganywa tebaddamu kulumwa nnyonta!

Muwa abaana bange abato amazzi agatali malongoofu, amacaafu, mu byalo ebyatise, n'obusungu bwange buli ku mmwe! Nja kuggyawo empeera yammwe ne ngiwa omulala, kubanga nga bwe nagamba ku mmwe nti: Mazima ddala, mufunye empeera yammwe. Mufunye empeera yammwe ey'ebifo byammwe eby'okwetiika eby'ekitiibwa, era mufunye empeera yammwe ey'amannya agatalina mugaso, era mufunye empeera yammwe ey'obugagga obucaafu, era mujja kufuna empeera y'obusungu bwange okuggyako nga mwenenyezza ne munkomberera mu buwombeefu. Kubanga empeera y'obusungu bwange bwe busungu, era bwe mulindetera embaawo zammwe, essubi, n'ebisasiro, biryaka ne bifuuka vvu mu maaso ga bonna, era mulizuulibwa nga muli baweereza balimba!

Waliwo ensobi nnene mu mmwe mwenna, era nammwe, abatume bange, bannabbi, ababuulizi b'enjiri, abasumba, n'abayigiriza! Temukyeyimirizaawo mu nkolagana ennungi nange, Yesu Kristo, naye mwandese; kubanga nze mutwe gw'omubiri, era mulina kunkwata waggulu wa byonna! TEMUSOBOLA kuweereza ku lwange nga temumanyi na ddoboozi lyange. Muli baggavu b'amatu, era mulina obuzibu bw'amatu obw'olubeerera okuggyako nga mwenenyezza, kubanga nja kuwonya amatu gammwe emyoyo gy'okutondowala musobole okuwulira eddoboozi lyange, kubanga njogera nammwe! Okuggyako nga munkutte ng'omutwe gwammwe n'okussa ekitiibwa mu Bigambo byange, nja kubaleka nga muli bazibe b'amaaso era mu bunnya n'abo abaabakulembera. Muli bazibe b'amaaso, era mulina obuzibu bw'amaaso obw'olubeerera okuggyako nga mwenenyezza, kubanga nnandiwonyezza amaaso gammwe emyoyo gy'okutondowala musobole okusoma ebigambo byange n'okulaba amaaso gange: naye okutondowala okutonotono, n'okuzinga emikono okutonotono.

Mufuuse ekisekererwa n'ekivume, kubanga enneeyisa yammwe ey'ebbugumu evoola erinnya lyange ettukuvu. Si nti temunkwata, Kristo Yesu, ng'omutwe gwammwe (kubanga singa mwakikola, mwandisobodde okunjogerera), naye mugenda wala mu bujeemu nga muvoola ebigambo byange ebitukuvu, kubanga nze Kigambo ekyefuula omubiri! Mbabuuza, temusomanga kulabula kwange? Kubanga ntegeeza buli muntu awulira ebigambo by'obunnabbi obw'ekitabo kino nti, Omuntu yenna bw'anaagattanga ku bino, Katonda alimugattako ebibonyoobonyo ebiwandiikiddwa mu kitabo kino. Era omuntu yenna bw'anaaggyanga ku bigambo by'ekitabo eky'obunnabbi buno, Katonda aliggyako omugabo gwe mu Kitabo ky'Obulamu, ne mu kibuga ekitukuvu, ne mu bintu ebiwandiikiddwa mu kitabo kino. Muli basirusiru nnyo, baggavu b'amatu, era bazibe b'amaaso ne mutategeera nti nagaba okulabula okwo si lwa kitabo kinene kyokka eky'okubikkulirwa kwange, Okubikkulirwa! Naye okulabula okwo kukwata ku biwandiiko ebitukuvu byonna! Kubanga Engero zange zikaabira n'eddoboozi eddene mu nguudo eri bonna abalina amatu okuwulira: Ebigambo bya Katonda byonna birongoofu era biyonjo; kubanga ye ngabo eri bonna abamwesiga. N'olwekyo togatako kigambo kyonna ku bigambo bye, aleme okukunenya n'obeera mulimba.

Baweereza bange Abakyalawoodikia, abaafuuka mikwano gy'ensi. Baweereza bange Abakyalawoodikia, abeefuula abakulu okusinga obusika bwa Katonda, nga mweraga nti muli bantu bakulu. Baweereza bange Abakyalawoodikia, abaanyooma omwoyo gwange gwe natonda mu mmwe. Baweereza bange Abakyalawoodikia, abeeha amannya agatalina mugaso n'obukodyo bw'okwegattako ku mannya gammwe. Baweereza bange Abakyalawoodikia: Muwulire ebigambo byange munziremu, kubanga buli lulimi lulirangirira!

Ani yabawa olukusa okukyusa ebigambo byange, n'ekigambo kya Mukama kye mpa okuyita mu babaka bange? Enneeyisa yammwe, mmwe baweereza abakyalawoodikia abaganzi, si nshya, kubanga amangu ddala ng'amabaluwa gange gaasaasanyizibwa mu Makkanisa gange amapya agawandiikibwa mu Ebikolwa n'awalala, ebika by'abanagositiko ebyakulemberwanga bannabbi ab'obulimba abanagositiko baakyusa ebigambo byange ne bawandiika ebyawandiikibwa obupya, kubanga baava mu Kkanisa yange bonna bamanye nti baali basumba bajeemu!

Mulikungula empeera y'enneeyisa yaabwe, kubanga mwegasse nabo, era mubeera mu nneeyisa yaabwe, era mwolesa emyoyo egitali mirongoofu gya Yezeberi, nnabbi omulimba gwe muyita omutukuvu! Ndaba ebikolwa byammwe ebibi, era biwandiikiddwa mu ggulu mu bujjuvu obutaliimu nsobi okubavunaana! Mulina okwogera ebigambo bye mbawa, era mulina okubyogera mu bwesigwa, mu bugolokofu, nga temukyusizza wadde ekigambo kimu kyonna eky'obunnabbi! Soogera kwekwakanya nzekka, kubanga nnagamba nti: Fuba okweraga nga osiimiddwa Katonda, omukozi ateeswala, ng'ogeraageranya ekigambo eky'amazima mu butuukirivu. Ebigambo bingi bikyusiddwa mu biwandiiko byange emyaka egiyise, era bwe mulisoma n'okussa ekitiibwa mu bigambo byange bye mbawadde, nja kubakulembera mu mazima gonna, kubanga bwe ntyo bwe nagaba omubeezi.

Nja kulaga, bwe muliwuliriza amaloboozi amangi ge nabalekerera. Mugoberere ebyawandiikibwa bye nagaba e Antiyokiya, kubanga ge maloboozi g'abajulirwa abangi, era abajulirwa bange tebalimba so tebakontana. Mutunuulire Textus Receptus era mwewale Baibuli z'abajeemu: musome mutegeere! Naye mmwe, baweereza bange abakyalawoodikia, temussa kitiibwa mu bigambo byange, era mukyusa ebigambo byange okutuukana n'ebigendererwa byammwe ebibi, nga mubojjera abantu bange basobole okweyongera mu kibi, era mubakulembera okugenda ewala mu nneeyisa yammwe embi! Waliwo ensobi nnene mu mmwe, era temugoberera nneeyisa yange, kubanga muli bamalala era muli ba mitima mikakanyavu, era nja kubaleka mu kuzikirizibwa kw'omubiri okuggyako nga mwenenyezza ne mukyusa enneeyisa yammwe. Kubanga nze, Yesu Kristo, nsize ebigambo bino! Temubinyoomanga, era mwenenyere mu bibukutu n'evvu. Mwenenyere mu lujjudde eri ebibiina byammwe era mwatule ebibi byammwe eri abo be mwakulembera obubi, kubanga nja kuggyako ekikondo kyammwe. Mwenenye era mutambulire mu bugolokofu, era nja kukomyawo ekikondo kyammwe n'olulimi lw'omuliro oluggya, kubanga nze alikoleeza omuliro gw'omwoyo gwange mu mmeeme yammwe.

Muddeyo eri okwagala kwammwe okwasooka, kubanga ddala munjagala, naye si lwa kumanya. Temukalambira ku bigambo byange, kubanga njija mangu okusinga bwe mulowooza, kubanga njija ng'omubbi ekiro. Ne mu nsobi yammwe, mulowooza nti omubbi ekiro ye muntu eyeenyoola mu ddirisa ng'omumenyi w'amateeka. Mmwe abasirusiru! Lwaki temutegeera nneeyisa ya Bahebulaniya. Omubbi ekiro yali kabona asinga obukulu eyajjanga nga tategeezezza okulaba nti bakabona be baali beesigwa mu mirimu gyabwe egy'omu yeekaalu, kubanga emirimu egimu gyagendanga ekiro kyonna okutuusa emirimu egyo lwe gyaggwa okusinziira ku mateeka. Kabona asinga obukulu bwe yandisanze kabona ali wansi we ng'asula ku mulimu, yandikasuse ejjinja ery'omuliro mu byambalo bye, kabona oyo n'akunkumula ebyambalo bye ebyaka ne yeddukira ebweru wa yeekaalu ali bwereere era nga muswadde. Temubeeranga nga mwebase bwe nlijja, oba mulisuula mu maaso gange muli bwereere era nga muswadde mu nsonyi ezisinga obubi, kubanga nja kubaswazibwa era mbaggyeko empeera yonna. Okukangavvula kwange kuli ku mmwe n'omukono gwange guzito ku mmwe, era okuggyako nga mwenenyezza, mulifuna ebizibu bingi ddala bye mwandisobodde okwewala. Mwenenye, nsobole okubateekerateekera ekifo ku mmeeza yange, era mulyegatta ku Mugole wange mu bufumbo nange, Kristo Yesu, Omugole Omulenzi.

Nze Kigambo ekyefuula omubiri abeera mu mmwe.

Nze musumba omulungi era endiga zange ziwulira eddoboozi lyange.

Nze Mwana gw'endiga owa Katonda eyatumibwa olw'ebibi by'ensi.

Nze kabona asinga obukulu emirembe gyonna okusinziira ku mutindo gwa Merukizeddeeki.

Njija era empeera yange eri nange.

Nze oyo atambulira mu makkati g'ebikondo omusanvu eby'ettaala ne bamalayika omusanvu.

Nze nnabbi eyagolokokose okuva mu b'oluganda Musa gwe yayogerako nti: Muwulire eddoboozi lyange mu buwulize.

Nze oyo eyawa abantu ebirabo, n'olwekyo temunyoomanga birabo bye nagaba.

Njija mangu okusinga bwe mulowooza; mwetegeke, mmwe Omugole wange!

Nze, Mukama Yesu Kristo, nsize ebigambo bino era nfuuse eddoboozi ly'omuntu ayogerera mu ddungu; kubanga Ekkanisa yange ey'eLawodikiya ya bbugumu era eneetera okuba obwereere n'okuswala okuggyako nga yeenenyezza. Nnyimiridde ku luggi nkonkona akaseera katono akakyasigaddeyo! Tewakyali nnamba ku ssaawa, kubanga nnamba zigudde; era tewakyali musenyu gwonna okuggyako ng'omusenyu ogusembayo guyise mu ssaawa y'omusenyu, era gunaatera okugwa.
