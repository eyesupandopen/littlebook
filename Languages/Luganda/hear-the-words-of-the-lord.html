Obujulizi obw'emirundi ebiri: Ekkanisa
Mu biwandiiko by'Omutume Pawulo
Nakasenene 24, 2024

Muwulire Ebigambo bya Mukama: Ekyama Ekinene Kibikkuddwa!

Yesu Kristo agamba Omugole we nti: Mutegere amatama muwulire ebigambo byange, mmwe abalonde bange, era mutegeere bye njogera, kubanga ekyama ky’okutya Katonda kinene nnyo! Abangi ku mmwe temulina kitegeera kyonna ku kyama kya Katonda ekinene, naye nga kino ky'ekiri ku mutima era omusingi eri abagoberezi b’Ekkubo, mu njigiriza ne mu bikorwa byombi: Kye kifo ekikulu eky'Obukristaayo mwe nnabatondera mu nze, era muli bitonde bipya mu Kristo Yesu, kubanga nze, Yesu Kristo, nabafuula bwentyo.

Eri abangi ku mmwe, ekyama kya Katonda ekinene kyasibira mu mirembe n’ennono n’obukujjukujju obw’akabi mwe beekweka okulimba. Temutya, baana bange, era muwulire ebigambo byange era muwulire bye njogera, kubanga nja kukibannyonnyola wano mu ddakiika zino ezisembayo ez’obudde buno. Mutegeere, baana bange, nti okutabukatabuka okungi n’obulimba bungi byaleetebwa mu kkanisa yange emisege emikambwe n’enjigiriza zaabyo ez’obulimba. Byagabanyizza mu bulimba Ekigambo kyange (n’Omubiri gwange) okuyita mu njigiriza y'ebiro eby'enjawulo (dispensationalism), era guno gwe bulimba obunene obwaleetebwa abo abaazimba ennyumba zaabwe ku musenyu, so si ku nze, ejjinja ly’oku nsonda enkulu, kubanga awatali nze tewali muntu ayinza kuzimba.

Omusenyu gwe baalonda okuba ejjinja ly’oku nsonda ery'ebizimbe byabwe gwe Bayibuli ezonooneddwa ezireeta obumu mu madiini gonna olw'ebigendererwa omuntu w’ekibi by'alina mu mutima gwe, ebigendererwa by'okufuga n'okufa n'okuzikiriza. Omusenyu gwe baakozesa okuzimba gukoleddwa mu biwandiiko ebyonooneddwa, ebiwandiiko by'Oluyonaani ebyayisibwa okuva e Alessandria. Mugoberere ebiwandiiko okuva mu lulimbe lw'e Antiyokiya, olwa Byzantine, era bino bituusa ku Textus Receptus, omulabe omukulu owa Rooma. Tewali biro bya njawulo (dispensations), era Katonda tagabanyiziddwamu, wadde nga nja kunnyonnyola eby'ebiro bino nga bwe birabikira mu mabaluwa gange. Katonda yakola n’abantu ng'ayita mu mirembe; muyige okumanya. Kale mutegeere, okwawukana kuno si kwange wabula kuteekebwateekebwa okwonoona Ekkanisa yange okuva ku lubereberye.

Ekyama ekinene kyabakwekebwa, baana bange, naye tekyabikkirirwa, kubanga nakibikkulira Omutume Pawulo emyaka mingi egiyise. Kikwekeddwa mu bulimba, mu bukuusa, mu njigiriza enkyamu ezitali zange; naye kikwekeddwa mu lwatu, era omubbi yandyagadde nnyo muleme kukiraba, naye ky'ekizibu eky'amaanyi ennyo. Kubanga ekisinga okutiisa Setaani ku Kkanisa yange kwe kumanya nti talina buyinza kubafuga bwe mutambulira mu maanyi gange amatuukiridde n'enkolagana nange ey'olwatu. N'olwekyo, kubanga tayinza kumenya kabonero ka Katonda ke nnateeka ku mmwe, era tewali ayinza kubaggya mu ngalo zange newaakubadde mu ngalo za Kitange, mu kifo ky'ekyo yandyagezezzaako okubazisa obusiiro. Si busiiro bwa bintu; Ekkanisa yange teyandibadde ebuulirira njiri ya kulakulaggana enkyamu kubanga si yange, newaakubadde nga bangi ku mmwe mukkiririza mu bulimba obwo. Setaani abagaliza obwavu obw'eby'omwoyo alyoke abakweke kimuli kyammwe eky'amazima era abasendasende mukkirize nti muli kalugu ka wansi atalina maanyi, abaliko okulindirira okuwaayo oluyi olulala n'okudduka ng'omutiitiizi, nga kuno kwe kukozesa obubi ebiragiro byange okuziyiza batabani bange okutambulira mu maanyi gaabwe ag'amazima. Nedda, saabatuma kuweeyo luyi lulala mulyoke mufugibwe era muwangulwe obubi; nnabatuma okuwangula obubi n'obulungi n'okusaanyaawo emirimu gya Setaani, kubanga nze bwe nnatumibwa, era nammwe bwe nnabatuma. Naye mutegeere nti: okuwoolera eggwanga kwange, kale temweerwanirangako, wabula muweeyo oluyi olulala bwe kiba kisaanidde olw’okulokoka kw’omulala n’okuwa ekiruyi ekifo, kubanga emmeeme nnyingi zirokose bwe zityo.

Mutegeere nti: baabalimba n’obulimba bwabwe, era baateeka obulimba buno obwakana n’amazima gange kumpi n’ebiwandiiko ebitukuvu. Baakikola ku mabbali g'empapula za Bayibuli ez’okuyiga; baakikola nga bakozesa ennyinnyonnyola enkyamu; baakikola n’obukujjukujju nga bakyamya amakulu g'ebigambo by'Oluyonaani ebimu agasibuka mu nnono z'edda ez'abakaafiiri so si mu mazima g'Ekigambo kya Katonda Ekitukuvu. Muyinza okukozesa ensibuko zino oluusi, naye mubeegendereza nnyo era mulekere awo okukkiriza omuntu ow’obutonde okubannyonnyolanga eby'omwoyo; kubanga omuntu ow’obutonde tayinza kutegeera bya mwoyo, kubanga gy'ali busirusiru. N'olwekyo, mumanye nti bangi ku bakujjukujju bano abacaakuna ebyawandiikibwa tebalina kukkiririza mu Kigambo kya Katonda era tebasaana mulimu gwe beewa kubanga tebalina mwoyo gwa Kristo era si bange; kubanga singa baali bange, nandibakulembedde mu mazima. Nze oyo eyabateekako akabonero okutuusa ku kununulibwa okujjuvu mu maanyi g'omwoyo gwange omutukuvu.

Mukama waffe Yesu Kristo agamba omugole we omwagalwa nti: Nneetaaga mutegeere ekigendererwa kyammwe eky'amazima, era bwe mutategere ekyama ekinene nga bwe kibakwatako, temujja kutambulira mu kujjula kw'amaanyi ge nnabayita okutambuliramu, wabula mujja kuba batonoowaziddwa mu kufuba kwammwe nga bangi ku mmwe bwe muli. Kinnuma era kinakuwaza omutima gwange okulaba nti abaana bange tebategeera kimuli kyabwe eky'amazima era tebalina kwagala kunoonya. Bulijjo musaanidde okunoonyereza mu bigambo byange n'obunyiikivu bwonna; naye enjigiriza ez’obulimba zireetera bangi obugayavu, kubanga temuli kiriisa. Kubanga bwe bulimba bwa Setaani, naye abasinga babeera ku mmere ey'amangu n'eby'okunywa ebya sukaali, era abasinga obungi tebalina maanyi ga kulya mmere gye nabawa na kunywa ky'okunywa kye nabawa, kubanga bwe mukola mutyo temuliddayo kulumwa njala newaakubadde ennyonta.

Kale muwulire ebigambo byange, ggwe Omugole wange omugayavu; kiseera kya kusitukira mu kuyitibwa kwo okw'amazima n'okukola emirimu gye nakuteekereteekera, era mumanye nti nja kukola eby'amagero bingi okuyita mu mikono gyammwe nga muntambulira mu kukkiririza n'okumanya okw'amazima. Kubanga kiki kye nnagamba mu buweereza bwange eri Isiraeri edda bwe nnatambuliranga mu Buyudaaya mu lwatu? Kubanga ki ekyawandiikibwa Lukka ku nsonga eno: N’abagamba nti Mmwe muweereddwa okumanya ekyama ky’obwakabaka bwa Katonda: naye abo abali ebweru, bino byonna bikolebwa mu ngero. Kale mutegeere, abatume bange baalondebwa okuweebwa ekyama kya Katonda ekinene kye nnababikkulira. Baakiwandiika, era nze nkuumye ebiwandiiko mulyoke mumanye amazima g'ensonga.

Bangi nnyo ku mmwe muwuliriza Abayudaaya abandyagadde okubazza wansi w'Amateeka ga Musa (Torah). Kyanjawulo okumanya nti singa si kyama kya Katonda ekinene, abasinga obungi ku mmwe temwandimanyidde ku Mateeka ga Musa; kubanga tegaaweebwa mawanga ga Bannaŋŋanda wabula Isiraeri ow'edda okugakwata, okuba omusomesi waabwe okubaleeta gy'endi. Kubanga nze kutuukirizibwa kw'amateeka, n'abo abanywerera n'obunyiikivu ku mateeka ga Musa banoonya kugulumiza omubiri gwabwe n'okutambulira mu butuukirivu bwabwe bo, era tebalina kutegeera nti Amateeka ga Musa gakwatibwa mu bujjuvu era ku nnukuta yonna olw'okwenkanankana kwammwe nange. Kubanga nze nzekka eyasobola okukwata amateeka, era nze, Yesu Omunazaaleeti ntuukirizza ebyetaago eby’obutuukirivu eby’amateeka. Nakakasa bye nnakola mu musaayi gwange nzekka, ate ne mbanaaza mu mazzi agakulukuta ag’omwoyo gwange nga mubikkiddwa mu nze.

Kubanga Pawulo namugambya ki eri Abakkolinso? Kubanga mu Mwoyo omu fenna twabatizibwa mu mubiri gumu, oba Bayudaaya oba Bannaŋŋanda, oba baddu oba ba ddembe; era fenna twanywesebwa Omwoyo omu. Bwe munaatambula okukwata amateeka, mujja kulemererwa era mujja kusubwa okuyitibwa kwammwe, kubanga nze nafuumuula amateeka mu biragiro ebibiri ebinene: Era onooyagalanga Mukama Katonda wo n'omutima gwo gwonna, n'emmeeme yo yonna, n'amagezi go gonna, n'amaanyi go gonna: kino kye kiragiro ekisooka. N'eky'okubiri kifaanana nakyo, kye kino nti, Onooyagalanga muliraanwa wo nga bwe weeyagala wekka. Mumanye, baana bange, nti tewali biragiro bikulu okusinga bino, kubanga mu bino ebiragiro bikwatibwa.

Mutegeere: Nnyinnyonnyola bino byonna mu mabaluwa ge nnaweereza amakanisa gange mu biseera ebyayita. Kale mutegeere nti mbikkudde ekyama kya Katonda ekinene, era bannabbi ab'edda baanoonyeereza n'obunyiikivu era baabuuliriza ku kyama kino kye baali bamanyi, naye tekyabaweebwa kukimanya. Nandyagadde mutegeere nti wadde engulu ya Bayibuli zammwe egamba nti “Ekitukuvu,” si tukuvu lwa bwatukuvu bwayo, kubanga ekyo kye kidiba ky'ekitabo kyokka. Ekifuula Bayibuli entukuvu kwe kuba nti ebitabo bye basiba birimu Ebyawandiikibwa Ebitukuvu ebyaweerezebwa omwoyo eri abaana b'Oyo Ali Waggulu Ennyo, ebyaweerezebwa Yakuwa, Katonda omunene ow'emirembe gyonna, kubanga tewali amufaanana. Kale muwulire ebigambo byange ebitukuvu n’emmeeme yammwe yonna! Kubanga Peetero yabagambiraki, baana bange? Obulokozi obwo Bannabbi bwe baabuuliriza ne banoonyeereza n'obunyiikivu, abaayogerera ddala eby'ekisa ekyandibajjidde, nga banoonyeereza nti mirembe ki, oba mirembe gya ngeri ki Omwoyo gwa Kristo ogwali mu bo gwe gwategeeza, bwe gwategeeza nga bukyali okubonaabona kwa Kristo, n'ekitiibwa ekyaddirira.

Baana bange, mutambulira mu kitansabwa ky'ekitiibwa ekyandibaddewo nga temumanyi, era obusika bwammwe obujjuvu bunaatera okubabikkulirwa mu nze, Kristo Yesu, kubanga munaatera okusitulibwa ne mugulumizibwa mu bujjuvu. Kale ekyama kino kiki? Era mukisanga wa? Nja kubagamba, kubanga nze Mukama wammwe Yesu Kristo, alunda ekisibo, era nze Omuwasi eri Omugole wange, era mutambulira mu kyama kya Katonda ekinene, wadde nga bangi ku mmwe mutambulira mu kizikiza mu nsonga eno. Temutya, nja kubawa okutegeera, n'abagezigezi n'abawombeefu nja kubawa amagezi. Naye abo mu mmwe abajja okugoba ebigambo byange, mukikola nga munvuganya; era oba muli ku lwange oba munvuganya, tewali wakati awo. Kubanga mbagoberera fenna okuyiga ku nze, muwulire ebigambo byange era temugoba ddoboozi lyange, kubanga mbawadde eby'omu maaso n'ekigendererwa mu nze. Nze Musumba omulungi: omusumba omulungi awaayo obulamu bwe olw'endiga, n'endiga zange ziwulira eddoboozi lyange, kale mulekere awo okugoberera abakozi ab'empeera abeefuula nze!

Laba! Omwoyo agamba nti: Waliwo ebyama ebimu munda mw'Ekyama kya Katonda Ekinene! Nja kutandika n'Ekyama Ekinene, oluvannyuma nnyogere ku byama ebirala ebikirimu. Nga tetunnagenda wala, sangula olufu lw’enjigiriza y’ebiro eby’enjawulo (dispensationalism) ku magalasi go era ozibule amaaso go olabe: Enjiri nga bwe muzimanyi, emirimu gya Matayo, Makko, Lukka, ne Yokaana, mikulu eri bonna. Ebitabo bino biraga okutuukiriza kwange okw'amateeka ku nnukuta, nga tewali kisigaddewo. Bimbikkula mu kujja kwange eri Isiraeri. Mu kugondera enjiri ennya nga bwe zimanyiddwa, muyiga okutambula nga bwe nnatambula, kubanga bwe mwalagirwa. Naye zzo, si ze njiri zokka; nedda, si ge mawulire amalungi gokka agankwatako.

Kubanga amawulire amalungi ag’Ekyama Ekikulu ekya Katonda nagawa Omutume Pawulo mu bbaluwa ze eri ekkanisa, nga nze namusindikira okubibikkulira. Mujja kunyooma endowooza eno? Mulowooza nti mbuulira njiri ndala era nti ndi ddoboozi ly’obulimba? Mumanyidde nnyo okugoberera amaloboozi g’abapakasi okutuusa lwe mutakyategeera ddoboozi lyange? Kubanga abo abajja okunyooma, mwandigonderedde eddoboozi ly’Abagalatiya Pawulo bwe yagamba nti: Naye mbategeeza, abooluganda, nti Enjiri gye nnabuulira si ya ngeri ya bantu. Kubanga saagiweebwa muntu, so saagiyigirizibwa, wabula kwe kubikkulirwa kwa Yesu Kristo. Pawulo yayogera n’obwesigwa amazima ge nnamuwa, naye bangi ku mmwe munyooma ebigambo bye nnamuwa, nga mukendeeza n’ebigambo n’ebigambo ng’ebiti: O, ebyo Pawulo byeyawandiika byokka, oba, ezo bbaluwa za Pawulo zokka; si bigambo bya Kristo kubanga tebiri mu bimyufu! Ggwe omusiru! Oli mu nsobi; zibula amaaso go eri omusana gwange n’amazima gange! Nnawa Pawulo enjiri y’ekyama. Muli basiru nnyo okukkiriza nti waliwo ebintu ebina eby’obwangu byokka ebirimu Enjiri yange?

Muwulire ebigambo bya Kristo Yesu, baana bange, kubanga nja kuyigiriza abagonda. Kubanga Ekyama ky’Okutya Katonda kinene n’enjiri erimu, kubanga nalangirira amawulire amalungi ag’ekyama. Naye sijja kubaleka na ddoboozi lya mujulirwa omu; buli kigambo kinywezebwe mu kamwa k’abajulirwa babiri oba basatu. Kubanga ebbaluwa gye nnawandiikira Abaruumi erina ky’eyagala okwogera nammwe: Kale eri oyo ayinza okubanyweza ng’enjiri yange bw’eri, n’okubuulirwa kwa Yesu Kristo, ng’okubikkulirwa kw’ekyama bwe kuli, ekyasirikirwa okuva ensi lwe yatondebwa. Amawulire amalungi ga Pawulo ge kubikkulirwa kw’ekyama ekyasirikirwa okuva ensi lwe yatondebwa, nga Matayo, Makko, Lukka, ne Yokaana bwe bali amawulire amalungi ag’okujja kwange okwasooka eri Isiraeri.

Nze Mukama O mwesigwa n’ebigambo byange bya mazima, n’olwekyo muntegerekere n’omutima gwammwe, kubanga n’omutima omuntu gw’akkiririzaamu okutuuka ku butuukirivu. Mbasaba mubeere n’okukkiriza okwenkana akaweke ka kaladaali, kubanga ensigo eyo ejja kukula n’amaanyi mu mmwe ng’efunye okulabirirwa okutuufu. Kubanga Pawulo yagamba ki Abakkolinso: Naye twogera amagezi ga Katonda mu kyama, ge magezi agakisibwa, Katonda ge yalagira edda ensi nga tennaabaawo, olw’ekitiibwa kyaffe. Abakulu b’ensi eno n’omu teyagamanya: kuba singa baagamanya, tebandikomerereredde Mukama w’ekitiibwa. Kyawandiikibwa lwatu, baana bange, era tekyetaaga kunnyonnyolwa, naye nja kunnyonnyolako katono.

Mutegeera ebigambo bino, baana bange? Mutegeera omuwendo gwammwe bwe guli nti Katonda abadde abassaako eriiso okuva ensi nga tennatondebwa, era nga bw’ali ow’emirembe gyonna, nammwe bwe muli, kubanga mubadde mu birowoozo bye eby’omu maaso okuva emirembe nga teginabaawo, era abawadde obulamu obutaggwaawo mu nze, Kristo Yesu? Ebiseera byammwe n'obulamu bwammwe biragiddwa Yakuwa, Katonda omukulu era ow'ekitiibwa, okumasamasa kwe tewali mubiri guyinga kuyimirirawo. Singa Setaani yali amanyi entegeka za Kitange, teyandinkomerereredde, kubanga mu kuwuliza kwange okutuusa ku kufa ne mu kuzuukira kwange mwamalirizibwa amaanyi agamuwangudde era azikiridde ddala. Teyandyagadde n’akatono kkanisa ya maanyi eyateekebwamu amaanyi n'omwoyo gwange era nga nze nkola ebikolwa eby'amaanyi n'emikono gyabwe.

Ekyo kibakwatako? Mulekere awo okutabuka mu birowoozo kubanga ababaka bange babagamba ekintu kye mutayagala kuwulira, era muwangule amalala gammwe, kubanga nja kukulembera abawombeefu bokka era nja kuziyiza ab’amalala. Muwulire ebigambo byange era mubireke bikkire mu buziba bw’emyoyo gyammwe; kubanga nabatonda mu nze, era muli ba maanyi, era muli ba kyamagero; kubanga mulongoosebbwa mu musaayi gwange nze, omusaayi gw'omwana gw'endiga. Kubanga nze mutwe gw'Omubiri gwange ogukola ebikolwa eby'amaanyi bingi eri abantu bange be nkyayita, mukwate bukwasi ku Mukama wammwe, Yeshua HaMashiach, n'omutima ogumu n'ekigendererwa kimu, kubanga nze Mukama wa bakama.

Omujulirwa omwesigwa, era omubereberye mu bafu agamba abeesigwa be era abalonde! Waaliwo ekkanisa ey'edda mu Efeso, era gy'ali obujjuvu bw'Ekyama Ekikulu ekya Katonda bwabikkulirwa mu bbaluwa eyasindikibwa Pawulo okuva gye ndi, kubanga teyayogerera ku lulwe. Nja kunnyonnyolako katono, naye mumanye nti nsuubira okuwuliza kwammwe mu kukkiriza eri nze, Yesu Omunazaaleeti, era oyo aweereddwa ebingi, ebingi bye bisuubirwa okuva gy'ali. Nga sinnagenda mu maaso, njagala mulowooze ku bino okuva e Kkolosaayi, kubanga nnasindika n’ebbaluwa eri Abakkolosaayi: Kubanga mwafa, n'obulamu bwammwe bukwekeddwa wamu ne Kristo mu Katonda. Kristo, bw'aliba obulamu bwaffe, bw'alirabika, n'olwo nammwe mulyoka mulabika awamu naye mu kitiibwa.

Baana bange, mwafa bwe nnafa, era mwefiirira buli lunaku nga mubala obuzaale obukadde nga bufiiridde; kubanga mwakomererwa nange, era tewali kifo kirala kyonna mu byawandiikibwa we mujja okusanga endabirwamu y’obuntu bwammwe obw’amazima, kubanga tewali kitundu kirala kya njiri kikibikkula okuggyako enjiri ey’Ekyama Ekikulu gye nnabikkulira Ekkanisa yange. Wadde nga mulabira mu ndabirwamu etalaga bulungi, ekyo kinaatera okukyuka, era mujja kumanya era nga nammwe bwe mumanyiddwa. Mwennyikire mu Njiri ey’Ekyama Ekikulu ekya Katonda, kubanga ekkanisa yange ey'e Lawodikiya ebuze ebyama byange, kubanga abasinga ku mmwe kitera okubaawo n’obutamusoma bbaluwa zange eri Ekkanisa kubanga abasumba abakyamu babatwala walala nga banoonya ebigambo ebiri mu bimyufu. Baana bange, mmanyi nti munjagala, kale munjagale ng'okumanya bwe kuli, era temutambuliranga mu bulimba.

Kale mbabuuza nti, ebbaluwa eri Abefeeso erina ki eky’okwogera? Kubanga, laba, ekyama ekikulu kiri eyo! Bwe yamala okutumanyisa ekyama ky’okwagala kwe, ng’okusiima kwe bwe kuli, kwe yeeteekerateekera yekka, Nti mu kuteekebwateekebwa kw'ebiro bwe kulituuka ajja kukungaanyiza mu Kristo byonna wamu, eby'omu ggulu n'ebyo ebiri ku nsi; mu ye. Mmanyisizza ekyama ky'okwagala kwa Yakuwa, kye yakweka mu ye yekka, kubanga teyeetaaga muwi wa magezi. Temulowooza ku nnyinnyonnyola ey’obulimba gye mwayigira okuva mu bannabbi ab’obulimba mu nnyinnyonnyola zaabwe ezitaliiko mugaso eza ku mabbali, kubanga Setaani akyusa mpola ennyinnyonnyola z’ebyawandiikibwa okutuukana n’okwagala kwe n’entegeka ze ezitaagasa.

Ekyama kino kyaweebwa Pawulo, era ye yali omusigire eyakwasibwa okukigabira Ekkanisa yange, na gye muli mmwe singa mulina amatu agawulira n’amaaso agalaba. Kubanga enjiri ey’Ekyama Ekikulu yaweebwa Pawulo, era ne mmutuma ku luguudo olugenda e Ddamasiko. Mu bujjuvu bw’ebiro, Kitange ajja kukungaanyiza mu nze, Kristo Yesu, ebintu byonna ebiri mu ggulu n’ebiri ku nsi. Tewalibaawo nate kwawukana, tewalibaawo nate kwawukana wakati wa Katonda n’abaana be, tewalibaawo nate bulabe wakati w’Omuyudaaya n’Omunnaggwanga, kubanga mmwenna muzaaliddwa omulundi ogw’okubiri era muli kitonde kipya mu nze, Kristo Yesu. Ebigambo bino bya kyamagero nnyo gyemuli? Ebigambo byange bibanyiiza kubanga mukkiririza mu kunnyonnyola kw’abasumba abakyamu?

Muyige ku nze mu buwombeefu mulyoke mutegeere. N’ate, mu Abeefeso Pawulo yawandiika ebigambo bye nnamuwa: Olw'ensonga eno nze Pawulo, omusibe wa Yesu Kristo ku lwammwe Abannaggwanga, Oba nga mwawulira eby'obuweereza obw'ekisa kya Katonda, bwe nnaweebwa ku lwammwe: Nga bwe yamanyisa ekyama olw'okubikkulirwa. N’ate, temwesittalanga ku bigambo bya bannabbi ab'obulimba, kubanga obuweereza obw'ekisa kya Katonda bwe buweereza bwe eri Ekkanisa, kubanga ye (n'abalala) baalondebwa okuba abasigire b’ebyama bya Katonda.

Era nate: Ekyali kitamanyisibbwa mu mirembe emirala eri abaana b’abantu, nga bwe kirabise kaakano eri abatume be abatukuvu ne bannabbi mu Mwoyo, Nti Abannaggwanga babeere basika bannaffe, era aba mubiri gumu, era abassa kimu mu kusuubiza kwe mu Kristo, olw’Enjiri. Yee, kisome bulungi era okisome nate: Abannaggwanga abakkiririza mu linnya lyange ettukuvu, Yesu Kristo, be basika bannaffe era ab'omubiri gumu n'abo ab'omu Isiraeri abaamfuula Mukama waabwe, kubanga tewali njawulo mu nze.

Era nate: Kyennyini ekyama ekyakwekebwa okuva emirembe n'emirembe, naye kaakano kirabisibwa eri abatukuvu be, Katonda be yayagala okumanyisa obugagga bw'ekitiibwa ky'ekyama kino mu Bannaggwanga bwe buli, ye Kristo ali mu mmwe, essuubi ery'ekitiibwa. Yee, muzaaliddwa omulundi ogw’okubiri mu mwoyo gwange, era omwoyo gwange gubeera mu baana bange emirembe gyonna, kubanga gwe mwoyo gw’obuzaale, omusingo gw’obusika bwammwe, era omwoyo ogwo gulimu obusobozi bwonna bwe mbawadde, kubanga mpawadde abantu ebirabo. Mwatondebwa mu kifaananyi kyange, era mwatondebwa obupya mu nze; kubanga Kitange abazadde mu nze, kubanga nze mwoyo ogulamuza.

Kristo Yesu eyalinnyisibwa agamba nti: Baana bange abato, mubeere n’okubudaabudibwa okungi mu nze, kubanga obulokozi bwange, bwe butuukibwako oluvannyuma lw’okunfuula Mukama wammwe mu kukkiriza n’okwenenya, tebuyinza kusasika oba okuggyibwawo; kubanga ani ku mmwe ayinza okuggyawo okutondebwa kw’obuzaale mu baana bennyini, newankubadde bayinza okufiirwa obusika singa mwabagaana. Obulokozi kirabo ekiweebwa Kitange okuyita mu nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, era ebirabo n’okuyita kwa Katonda tebyenenya. Kaakano, nja kubawa obukunkumuka obw’okugoberera ku lugendo lwammwe mu byama ebiri mu Kyama Ekikulu ekya Katonda, kale mwefubenga okweraga nga musiimibwa eri Katonda, omukozi w’Ekigambo, ng’awawula obulungi ekigambo eky’amazima, muleme okukwatibwa ensonyi mu maaso gange bwe nlijja.

Waliwo ekyama mu Abaruumi, nga tulowooza ku buzibe bw’amaaso bwa Isiraeri mu kitundu. Waliwo ekyama eky’okukung’aanyizibwa kw’omugole wange, abamu kye bayita okutwalibwa, era ekyo kiri mu Abakkolinso (n’awalala); kubanga mu kaseera, mu kubonereza kw’eriiso, ekkondeere erisembayo lijja kuvuga, era okuvuga kwalyo kuli ku mmwe. N’oluvannyuma waliwo ekyama ky’omusajja n’omukazi okufuuka omubiri gumu, era kino kyama kinene: naye njogera ebikwata ku Kristo n’ekkanisa, era ekyo muyinza okukisanga mu Abeefeso. Kubanga nja kuggyawo obuzibe bw’amaaso ku Isiraeri bwe balikyukiza emitima gyabwe gye ndi. Kubanga nja kukung’aanya Omugole wange gye ndi, kubanga nze Ssebo w’omugole. Nze oyo gwe mujja okuwasa, tulyoke tubeere omu emirembe gyonna mu nze ne mu Kitange, kubanga Katonda ye byonna mu byonna. Omutima gwange guwummulira ku mmwe, baana bange, era munaatera okundaba amaaso n'amaaso; era temukola nsobi, mbayita gye ndi, abawala bange ab’amagezi, era njija ku lwammwe mangu nnyo, era nneegomba okubalaba mwenna gye tuliba amaaso n'amaaso, era mujja kumanya, nga nammwe bwe mumanyiddwa.

Nze oyo eyabassa akabonero okutuusa ku kununulibwa okujjuvu mu maanyi g’omwoyo gwange omutukuvu. Nze kutuukirizibwa kw’amateeka.
