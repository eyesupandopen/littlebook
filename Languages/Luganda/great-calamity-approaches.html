Obujulizi obubiri: Ekkanisa
Mu Mirimu gya Yokaana Omutume Omubatiza
Ogw'okusatu 11, 2024

Akabi Akanene Kasembedde

Ekigambo kya Mukama Katonda kyanzijira; nkwegayirira wuliriza.

Muwulire eddoboozi lyange, bw'ayogera Mukama ow'Eggye! Bantu bange, munkomokere, kubanga akabi akanene kanaatera okutuuka ku ggwanga lyammwe. Akabi akanene kali ku mulyango gwammwe, era akabi akanene kajja kutuuka ku mbalama z'ensi yammwe. Munkomokere era muntye, bw'ayogera Mukama ow'Eggye. Katonda asalidde Amerika omusango ku njuyi bbiri. Wajja kubaawo omusango okuva munda era wajja kubaawo n'omusango okuva ebweru. Okuva munda wajja kubaawo okukankanyizibwa okunene, okukankanyizibwa okw'amaanyi, era Amerika ejja kugwa n'amaanyi mangi olw'okukankanyizibwa okwo. Okuva ebweru, amagye ge gazze okubba n'okutta n'okuzikiriza byonna ebiri mu makubo gaago. Mu mitima gyago temuli kusaasira. Balina endowooza emu. Era bajja kubaggyako obugagga bwammwe; bajja kubaggyako eby'obugagga byammwe; bajja kutwala ezzadde lyammwe; era enjala enkalu ejja kujja mu nsi yammwe. Oyo atasobole kuttibwa na ntalo alittibwa mutawaana; oyo atasobole kuttibwa mutawaana alittibwa nsolo za mu nsiko; oyo atasobole kuttibwa nsolo za mu nsiko alifa lwa njala. Naye ne mu kabi kammwe, buli muntu yenna alikoowoola erinnya lya Mukama alirokolebwa.

Era ndirokola abantu bange, n'okuyita mu kubonaabona okungi ne mu muliro ogulongoosa. Mujje gyendi, bantu bange, ng'ekiseera kikyaliwo. Mujje gyendi, nze Mukama wammwe Yesu Kristo, kubanga nze Mukama omutuukirivu. Nze Kabaka wa Bakabaka. Nze Omusumba Omulungi, era tewali ayinza kusikula ndiga zange mu mukono gwange. Mujje gyendi! Mujje gyendi ng'ekiseera kikyaliwo, mwennyeene ebibi byammwe; mwatule erinnya lyange ery'obuyinza nga ye Mukama, kubanga tewali bulokozi mu linnya ddala lyonna. Kubanga nze, Yesu Kristo, nateekebwawo olw'obulokozi bwammwe bwe nnagula n'omusaayi gwange mwennyini. Mujje gyendi, weewaawo, mujje gyendi mu kwagala kwange kwe mbakwatidde. Temunjeemerera, naye mujje gyendi, nsobole okubalokola ne mbawa obulamu obutaggwaawo era mpe omukisa ezzadde lyammwe. Mujje gyendi, bantu bange, so temukakanyaza mitima gyammwe, nga bajjajjammwe bwe baakola mu ddungu. Kubanga Mukama Katonda wammwe alangiridde omusango omunene ku ggwanga lyammwe, kubanga ebibi byalyo bingi era bya ntiisa.

Yee, nnasasulira ebibi ebyo n'ebibi by'abantu bonna, naye okweyanjula kwange n'emikono gyange tebijja kuba bigoloddwa lubeerera. Ku lunaku olw'akabi, obulokozi bujja kufunibwa nga buyitira mu kweraliikirira okungi n'okubonaabona okungi kwe ndireeta ku nsi okukema emitima gy'abantu, era oyo agumiikiriza okutuuka ku nkomerero alirokolebwa. Temugoberera muntu wa kibi. Temutwala kabonero ke so temutwala ddagala lye, kubanga omusulo gw'omusota guli omwo, era temujja kuwona busagwa bwagwo. Buli alitwala akabonero k'ensolo alifuna omugabo gwe mu nnyanja ey'omuliro awamu n'ensolo ne nnabbi ow'obulimba. Tekyawandiikibwa mu Kigambo kyange Ekitukuvu? Temusomangako? Temuwulirangako? Mukomewo mu kwagala kwange mujje gyendi, baana bange, kubanga mbayita mu ssaawa eno esembayo, kubanga akabi akanene kali ku mulyango gwammwe.

Mumpulire, bw'ayogera Mukama ow'Eggye:

Kubanga nze wuuyo, Omwana w'Omuntu eyakomererwa wansi wa Pontiyo Piraato, eyafiirira ebibi byammwe n'azuukizibwa ku lunaku olw'okusatu. Nnazuukizibwa ne ngulumizibwa.

Nze Mukama wa Bakama era Kabaka wa Bakabaka.

Nze Mwana w'Omuntu, Mukama omutuukirivu, Mukama ow'amazima, omwesigwa era ow'amazima, era tewali n'omu ku mmwe ayinza okunsikula mu mikono gyange.

Nze musumba mulungi, era tewali ayinza kusikula ndiga zange mu mukono gwange.

Abo bonna Kitange be yampa, ndibanjulira ye, era njagala okubanjulira Kitange nga temuli nsobi, oba bbala, oba kunenyezebwa, nga mwambadde bafuta enjeru etobye mu musaayi gwange. Engeri yokka omuntu yenna gy'ayinza okuyimirira mu maaso gange kwe kukkiriza ssaddaaka yange olw'ebibi byammwe. Temulwawo n'aboonoonyi! Temulwawo; temulwawo! Muve mu bo mweyawule ku bibi byabwe. Mwennyeenere ebibi byammwe mwennyini gyendi era mwennyeenere n'ebibi bya bajjajjammwe gyendi, kubanga bibasikira. Mubeere obulamu bwe mbawadde, era mutambulire mu kkubo lye mbateereddewo, nga bwe mbayise ku mannya. Temukyama ku kkono so temukyama ku ddyo, naye muteeke ebirowoozo byammwe n'endowooza emu ku bigambo byange. Naye, bangi bajja kugaana okuyitibwa kwange, era bangi bajja kuzikirira. Mube n'okukkiriza mu nze, kubanga okwagala kwa Katonda kujja kulokola bonna abakoowoola erinnya lya Mukama. Mube n'okukkiriza mu nze, era emigga gy'amazzi amalamu gijja kukulukuta okuva mu mbuto zammwe ng'ebibi byammwe binaaziddwa ne biyonjebwa n'omwoyo gwammwe ne gulamuwa. Mujje gyendi ng'ekiseera kikyaliwo; mwatule erinnya lyange; munfuule Mukama, kubanga tewali bulokozi mu linnya ddala lyonna eriweereddwa wansi w'eggulu abantu mwe bayinza okurokolebwa.
