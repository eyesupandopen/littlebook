Abajulirwa Ababiri: Isirayiri
Okukaawa n'Okuwooma
Ssebutemba 7, 2024

Nga Bwe Kyawandiikibwa, Bwe Kityo Bwe Kirikolebwa!

Mwekuŋŋaanye muwulire ebigambo bya Mukama wammwe, ye Yeshua HaMashiach, mmwe abaana bange, kubanga Nze Mukama wa Bakama era Kabaka wa Bakabaka, era mu butuukirivu n'endijja okusala omusango n'okulwana olutalo. Kale mutegeere ebiseera bye mulimu n'ebigenda okubaawo, era mutege amatu eri eddoboozi lyange, muwulire ebigambo bya Mukama wammwe ataggwaawo era ow'emirembe gyonna, ye Kristo Yesu eyazuukizibwa era n'agulumizibwa! Kaakano njogera ku bintu ebigenda okujja mu ssabbiiti ya Danyeri ey'omusanvu, era n'ankola amakubo gammwe matereevu, era n'angaziya ekisa kyange eky'obulokozi okuyita mu mikono gy'abange 144,000. Kubanga bajja kulokola abantu bange okuva mu kuzikirira, era bajja kukweka abantu bange okuva mu busungu, ye busungu bwa Katonda obutegekerwa abo abagwa n'abo abeegattako emirembe gyonna ku bo n'obwakabaka bwabwe nga batwala akabonero kaabwe, ge maluma g'omusota ogw'ensolo ogwo ogutalina ddagala.

Naye bwe kityo bwe kiri nti n'ansika Omugole wange okumutwala mu ggulu, era n'anteeka akabonero ku bange 144,000 okuva mu bika bino wammanga, kye kimu 12,000 okuva mu buli kimu ku bino: Yuda, Lewubeeni, Gaadi, Aseri, Nafutaali, Manaase, Simyoni, Leevi, Isakaali, Zebulooni, Yusufu, ne Benyamini. Kale muwulire eddoboozi lyange!

Era baliba ggye eritaliiko ayinza kuliziyiza, era baliba maanyi gatayinza kumenyeka. Kubanga obulamu bwabwe bwa lubeerera, obutaliiko kuvunda kuyinza kuvunza. Emibiri gyabwe tegirizikirizibwa, kubanga tewali kuzikirira kuyinza kukwata ku banunulibwa; kubanga obuwanguzi ku kufa n'okuzikirira babulina emirembe gyonna. Balitondebwa buto ng'abaana b'omusana, era balikwatibwa ekitiibwa ekitaggwaawo, era baliba n'obusobozi obw'enjawulo n'ebitono ebiwandiikiddwa mu buntu bwabwe ebiriraga bye baakola ebikola n'okulaga obusobozi bwe n'atonda mu bo. Kubanga Nze, Kristo Yesu, nze mulongoosa atuula n'omuvubo mu ngalo, nga nkoleeza ennimi z'omuliro ogulongoosa, ndyoke mbatonde mwenna obupya mu Nze, Kristo Yesu Mukama wammwe eyalinnya era Omulokozi; era buli muntu awangula, ye wuuyo aligabana byonna.

Era mbawa okutegeera ebigambo ebigenda okubaawo mu bbanga ttono, era ebimu bibaawo nga bigobereragana ate ebirala ne bibaawo omulundi gumu, naye n'abinyonnyola mulyoke musobole okwawula ebiseera bye mulimu, era mulyoke mmwe abatukuvu bange ab'omu kibonyoobonyo muleme okutwalibwa obulimba obw'amaanyi obusaanikidde ensi era obulimbalimba abangi era ne batwalibwa mu musango. Kubanga abagwa baliryeraga era balirimba emirundi mingi, era balisuubiza obulamu naye tebalina maanyi ga kuwa bulamu, so tebayinza kugaba bulamu; naye buli kigambo kye boogera kisibuka mu bulimba obutwala mu kufa okw'okubiri, era okuzikirira okw'emirembe gyonna kwe kulifunibwa abo abagoberera amakubo g'abagwa n'abo bonna abakkiriza obulimba bwabwe obubi ng'amazima. Naye okusalirwa omusango okwajja mu nsi bwe kuno, nti abantu baagala ekizikiza okusinga omusana, n'abantu baagala obulimba okusinga amazima, kubanga abantu banganze Nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, ye Yesu Kristo, ne basalawo omulokozi ow'obulimba; naye amazima gali mu Kristo yekka, era abalala bonna balimba era bafere.

Era ebintu ebigenda okujja bye bino, era ebimu bibaawo omulundi gumu ate ebirala ne bibaawo nga bigobereragana era byetaagisa ebirala okubaawo. Era bingi tebyogeddwako naye birijja mu kiseera kyabyo. Kale ekyo abamu kye bayita okusikulwa, oba okukuŋŋaanyizibwa kw'abo bonna abali mu Kristo eri Mukama waabwe, kye kigambo ekisooka ekigenda okutandika ebirala byonna mu kiseera ky'okubonaabona kwa Yakobo, era ekiyitibwa ssabbiiti ya Danyeri ey'omusanvu, kubanga eriba ssabbiiti ya myaka musanvu, buli lunaku nga lwe mwaka. Era abeesigwa mu Kkanisa yange, gwe Mubiri gwange era Omugole wange, balitwalibwa nga Nze, Kristo Yesu, nzikka okuva mu ggulu n'okwogerera waggulu, n'eddoboozi lya malayika omukulu n'ekkondeere lya Katonda. Kubanga ekkondeere erisembayo liriwulirwa, mu kiseera ekyo abafu abali mu Kristo balizuukizibwa mu bulamu obuggya; olwo mmwe abali abalamu ne musigalawo mulyambuka mu bire nabo, era kiribaawo nga bwe kyawandiikibwa, era mu ngeri gye kyagabibwamu kiribaawo mu kusoowagana kw'eriiso.

Kale mutegeere, mmwe abeesigwa bange, be njagala n'okwagala okutaggwaawo era okw'emirembe gyonna, nti mulisanga amangu ago nga muli mu ntebe ey'obwakabaka eya Yakuwa yennyini, gye n'abalaga nga temuli bbala wadde enkunyalala oba ekintu kyonna ekifaanana bwe kityo eri Kitange w'omusana, era mmwe musana gwa nsi. Era amangu ago mulimanya nga bwe mumanyiddwa, era mulindaba, Yeshua HaMashiach, era muliraba Yakuwa, Katonda Omukulu ow'Emirembe, n'amaaso gammwe ku mulundi ogusooka, era muliwuniikirira nnyo olw'ebyo bye muliraba n'ekitiibwa kye muliba mwambaddemu. Era emirembe gyonna muliba mu kwegatta okutaggwaawo ne Kitange, ne Mwana, era emirembe gyonna bwe tuliba.

Mu biseera eby'okukuŋŋaanya abatukuvu, omuziyizi aliggyibwawo, n'omusango guligwa ku nnyumba ya Katonda gwonna. Era abo abanzikirizza okubalongoosa mu muliro gw'omulongoosi balyemerera kubanga balongooseddwa mu musaayi gw'omwana gw'endiga era balyemerera mu butuukirivu bwe mbawadde, kubanga Nze Mukama Omutuukirivu ali mwoyo oguwa obulamu. Era abo abaagaana eddoboozi lyange ne basigala nga bakakanyavu eri by'njagala era ne bagaana okuva mu kibi okuyingira mu butuukirivu balirekebwa okweyongera okulongoosebwa mu kiseera ky'okubonaabona kwa Yakobo, era ebibiina binene birifuna obulokozi obw'emirembe gyonna, empeera, n'ekitiibwa, era balibeerawo emirembe gyonna n'ekitiibwa kinene kiriba kyabwe emirembe gyonna. Era amakanisa agasigaddewo ag'ekifere galizikirizibwa, kubanga Omuzira, ensolo evva mu nnyanja, n'ensolo evva mu ttaka biritambula mangu okulwana n'abatukuvu abasigaddewo era biribawangula. Era bwe kiriba bwe kityo.

Omuziyizi bw'aliggibwawo, Babulooni ey'ekyama erizikirizibwa nga bwe kyawandiikibwa. Mu ssaawa emu yokka enkomerero ye eriba ey'enkomeredde, era okubonaabona n'okuzikirira okungi kulirya ensi, n'okukankana kungi kulibaawo, era kiryeraga eri ensi nti olunaku lwa Mukama lutuuse, newankubadde ng'abagwa balirimba ne beeyolesa ng'abagenyi ab'ekisa, era balifuba okubikkawo okweraga kwange eri abeesigwa mu lutalo n'omulumbagano gw'abagenyi ogw'obulimba. Kale temukkiriza bulimba bwabwe, baana bange, kubanga obulimba bwabwe bugendereddwamu okubatwala mu kuzikirira okw'emirembe gyonna okuterekebwa abagwa, kale temubeegattako.

Era obubonero bulisumululwa n'abeebagadde embalaasi ennya baliteebwa ku nsi, era abantu bangi nnyo balifa olw'ekyo. Era obwakabaka bwa Babulooni ey'ekyama obutuula ku mazzi amangi, n'abano balizikirizibwa ne bakendeezebwa, n'omunyago guliweereza ensengeka y'ensolo esinga n'ey'enkomerero; era ye ya nkomerero kubanga Nze, Kristo Yesu, n'azikiriza ddala obwakabaka bwa Setaani, obutadda nate kudda. Era ensi eriwuniikirira ensolo, era abangi balyegatta ku bwakabaka bwabwe obwa Setaani. N'embeera embi mu bantu, eyaleetebwawo ekibi kya Adamu, Kaawa bwe yakka okuva ku musota n'alya, teribaako n'akatono akagiziyiza, era abantu baliba n'obuddugavu obujjudde mu mitima gyabwe, era abangi balyegattira ddala ku nsolo nga bakkiriza era batwala akabonero k'ensolo. Temutwala kabonero ke, kubanga bonna abakakola balisuulibwa nga balamu mu nnyanja ey'omuliro ey'ekibiriiti, eyaka ennimi ezisinga okwokya eza bbulu.

Mu vvunvu ly'olutalo obwakabaka bwabwe obw'enkomerero mulimulimuka, okuva mu vvunvu ly'enteekateeka enkadde, kubanga okuva mu kavuyo ke baleetawo muliva enteekateeka yaabwe empya, ye nteekateeka y'Omuzira. Era balinoonya okugatta ensi yonna wansi w'ebbendera yaabwe, era baligatta amadiini gonna ag'ensi era balinneega ddala Nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, ye Yesu Kristo, atalikkirizibwa mu bo. Era mazima ddala, sirina kye nnakoze nabo wadde okuba n'omugabo mu bo. Era balitambula n'embiro nnyingi n'obukambwe bungi mu lutalo lwabwe ku batukuvu, era ensi erikyukira ddala abatukuvu ab'omu kibonyoobonyo, era bonna abawangula nga bakoowoola erinnya lyange okutuusa ku nkomerero baligulumizibwa emirembe gyonna mu mpeera ey'emirembe n'ekitiibwa ekitaggwaawo, era empeera yaabwe nnene, n'abo abawangula baligabana byonna.

Nga embeera yaabwe empya ejja, bwe kityo n'emiti gyange ebiri egy'emi Zeyituuni girimulimuka, ne bino byange ebibiri eby'ettabaaza eby'emere mu maaso ga Katonda w'ensi. Amaanyi amangi galibaweebwa, era baliragulira emyezi 42 nga bambadde ebibukutu; era omuntu yenna bw'anaabakola obubi, omuliro guliva mu kamwa kaabwe, ne gumirira abalabe baabwe: era omuntu yenna bw'anaabakola obubi, ateekwa okuttibwa mu ngeri eno. Bano baliba n'amaanyi okuggala eggulu, lireme okutonnya mu nnaku z'obunnabbi bwabwe: era baliba n'obuyinza ku mazzi okugafuula omusaayi, n'okukuba ensi n'ebirwadde byonna, emirundi gyonna gye baliba baagala. N'okukyawa kw'ensi kuliba kungi nnyo ku bo. Era mu kuwakanya emiti gyange ebiri egy'emi Zeyituuni muliba ensolo n'omunnabbi ow'obulimba, era omu aliraga ku munne, era balyegulumiza, era ebigambo bingi eby'obuvumi balibyogera, n'obubonero n'ebyamagero bingi eby'obulimba balibikola mu maaso g'abantu.

Ensolo zino zirikola ebyamagero ebinene okulimba abo abali ku nsi, kubanga kirikkirizibwa bo okukola obubonero n'ebyamagero bingi eby'obulimba, era mu maanyi ge gamu bwe balifuga. Ensolo evva mu ttaka erireeta omuliro okugwa ku nsi mu maaso g'abantu, era erikulembera abantu bonna okuwuniikirira bo n'obubonero n'ebyamagero bingi eby'obulimba. Era erikola ekifaananyi ky'ensolo era erireetera bonna okusinza ekifaananyi kye erikola. Era kirikkirizibwa yo okuwa omwoyo ekifaananyi ky'ensolo ekifaananyi ky'ensolo kiryoke kyogere era kireetere bonna abatakkiriza kusinza kifaananyi kya nsolo okuttibwa. Era erikola bonna, abagagga n'abaavu, ab'eddembe n'abaddu, okufuna akabonero ke ku mukono gwabwe ogwa ddyo oba ku byenyi byabwe.

OMUNTU YENNA BW'ASINZA ENSOLO N'EKIFANANYI KYAYO, N'AFUNA AKABONERO KAYO MU KYENYI KYE, OBA MU NGALA YE, YE Y'ALINYWA KU MVUYO GW'OBUSUNGU BWA KATONDA, OGUTABUDDWAMU AWATALI KUTABULA MU KIKOMPE KY'OBUSUNGU BWE, ERA ALIBONYEBONYEBWA N'OMULIRO N'EKIBIRIITI, MU MAASO GA BAMALAYIKA ABATUKUVU, NE MU MAASO GA MWANA GW'ENDIGA: N'OMUKKA GW'OKUBONYEBONYEBWA KWABWE GULINNYUKA EMIRIMBE N'EMIREMBE. ERA TEBALINA KIWUMMULO EMISANA N'EKIRO, ABO ABASINZA ENSOLO N'EKIFANANYI KYAYO, NA BULI AFUNA AKABONERO K'ERINNYA LYAYO.

Era mu kiseera kino yeekaalu yaabwe erizimbibwa, ne bwe kiba nti ssaddaaka y’ebisolo tekyetaagisa kubanga Nze Mwana gw’Endiga era neeweerayo ssaddaaka olw’ebibi by’abantu ng’omukulu w’abakabona ow’olubeerera okusinziira ku ndagaano ya Merukizeddeeki. Okutangirirwa kw’ebibi by’abantu kwakolebwa omulundi gumu, kwonna, era emirembe gyonna mu musaayi gwange ogwatangirira, ogutalina kibi. Kale bwe kityo, tewakyali ssaddaaka ya kibi.

Bwe kityo emiti gyange ebiri egy’omuzeyituuni bwe girimala okutegeeza kwagyo ku nkomerero y’emyezi 42, ensolo eva mu bunnya obutakoma erirwana nabo n’etta ebikondo byange bibiri eby’ettaala, kubanga kyagiweebwa okukikola. Era emirambo gyabwe girigalamira mu nguudo okumala ennaku ssatu n’ekitundu, ng’ensi yonna ejaguza era ewaŋŋana ebirabo olw’okufa kwabyo. Era eddoboozi eddene liryava mu ggulu ne ligamba nti, Mwambuke wano! Era balizuukizibwa mu bulamu obuggya, era balitwalibwa mu ggulu nga balabibwa abalabe baabwe.

Era balimaliriza yeekaalu yaabwe era omutego gulitegebwa, kubanga omutegesi w’ennyonyi atega atya omutego ng’ennyonyi eraba? Kubanga ensolo eva mu nsi y’oyo atega omutego mu nsi, eriyingiza bangi mu mutembo gwayo ogw’obulimba, era eriryamu olukwe abantu b’ensi. Era omuntu oyo ow’ekibi alibikkulibwa, ye mwana w’okuzikirira, Omulabe, era alyegulumiza ku byonna ebiyitibwa Katonda, oba ebisingizibwa: n’okutuuka okutuula nga Katonda mu Yeekaalu ya Katonda, ng’eyeraga nti ye Katonda. Era yeekaalu yaabwe eriggwa, n’omuzizo oguzikiriza gulituukirizibwa Moshiach waabwe omulimba. Era alikakasa endagaano n’abangi okumala wiiki emu: era wakati mu wiiki aliremesa ssaddaaka n’ekiweebwayo, era olw’okusaasaana kw’emizizo; aligifuula matongo, okutuusa okuzikirira okwateekebwateekebwa lwe kulifukibwa ku matongo.

Era bwe kityo okubonaabona okusinga byonna kulibaawo, okubonaabona okutali kwati nga tekunabangawo ku nsi, era mazima, tekuliddamu kubaawo. Laba! Nze, ye Yeshuah HaMashiach, sirireka bantu bange kuzikirizibwa kwa mangu, era nja kulokola Isirayiri, ye ns remnant eyo ey’amaanyi erinkkiriza nga Masiya ow’amazima, kubanga Yahweh bwe yantonda okuba. Era bwe kityo abange 144,000 balijja okuyamba abo abandyazikiridde era abakoowoola erinnya lyange. Nja kubakulembera ntanvu ku ntanvu, era abange 144,000 balinkolera ebikolwa eby’amaanyi bingi.

Kubanga bano beebo abatwalibwa mu kukung'aanyizibwa kw’abatukuvu gye ndi, Kristo Yesu, mu bbanga ku ddoboozi ly’ekkondeere erisembayo, era ekkondeere liri kumpi kuvuga. Bano be beesigwa bange, beebo abataasangibwamu bukuusa, kubanga beeyonja ng’omuwala atamanyi musajja mu musaayi gw’Omwana gw’Endiga, gwe nnawaayo emirembe gyonna ng’omukulu w’abakabona baabwe, kubanga ndi mukulu wa bakabona ow’olubeerera okusinziira ku ndagaano ya Merukizeddeeki. Bano be Mugole wange, era balingoberera wonna we ndigenda. Laba! Nja kubafuula ba maanyi, era tewali kintu kyali kilabiddwako ku nsi ekigeraageranyizibwa n’obusobozi bwe ndibayambaza. Baliba bambadde bafuta enjeru ennungi, etaliiko bbala, era obusobozi bwabwe buliba kifaananyi kya bye baakola, era baliba n’obusobozi obw’amaanyi; kubanga beesigwa, abayite, era abalonde, okuva ne nga tannassaawo nsi Yahweh olw’ebigendererwa bino.

Abange 144,000 balikka ku Lusozi Sayuuni, nange nja kubakulembera. Balitwala abo be mbakulemberera mu ddungu okukwekebwayo ekizikiza ekikutte n’okuyigganyizibwa kw’obwakabaka bw’abalabe. Balikulembererwa mu kifo eky’obulokozi okuva mu bibonyoobonyo omusanvu, kubanga mu misango gino mwe muli obukambwe bw’obusungu bwa Katonda obulifukibwa ku ntebe y’ensolo n’okubonyaabonya obwakabaka bw’Oggwekkeredde, kubanga ekibonerezo kyabwe kinene. Era abantu 144,000 balibakulembera mu bulokozi; balibawonya ebiwundu byabwe; balibasanyusa ne babawa essuubi eddene okukoowoola erinnya lya Mukama, okutuusa ku nkomerero bwe kinaaba kyetaagisa. Kubanga singa saagendako ennaku, tewandisigaddewo muntu mulamu. Era bwe batyo balituukiriza ekyo mwe ndibakulemberera.

Era ng’abasatu abatali batukuvu bwe beetegeka okuzikiriza abo abankkiriza nga Masiya waabwe, n’obusungu bwa Katonda obujjuvu bulituukirizibwa ku mitwe gyabwe ne ku bwakabaka bwabwe bwonna; mazima ddala, ebibya omusanvu ebijjudde obusungu bwa Katonda; era omusango omunene n’ennaku enkulu biri mu bibya byabwe, era biribaawo nga bwe kyawandiikibwa:

Malayika ow’olubereberye alifuka ekibya kye ku nsi, n’abo abakkiriza akabonero kaabwe akatali katukuvu balirumbibwa amayute amabi n’agabanyiga okuva mu kibya malayika ow’olubereberye ky’alifuka.

Era malayika ow’okubiri alifuka ekibya kye, n’ennyanja zirifuuka ng’omusaayi gw’omuntu omufu, n’ebiramu byonna eby’omu nnyanja birifa.

Era malayika ow’okusatu alifuka ekibya kye, n’afuula emigga n’obugga omusaayi, kubanga baayiwa omusaayi gw’abatukuvu n’ogwa bannabbi, kale kibagwanira okuweebwa omusaayi okunywa.

Mazima ddala, malayika ow’okuna alifuka ekibya kye ku njuba, era aliweebwa okubonyaabonya abantu n’obwoki bw’omuliro, n’abantu balyokyezebwa n’obwoki ne boogera ebintu ebikulu eby’obuvumi eri Katonda w’eggulu.

Era malayika ow’okutaano alifuka ekibya kye ku ntebe y’ensolo, n’obwakabaka bwayo bulisiyagguka nnyo, n’amaanyi mangi, era balyeruma ennimi zaabwe olw’ennaku.

Era malayika ow’omukaaga alifuka ekibya kye ku mugga omunene Fulaati, n’amazzi gaagwo galikala, ekkubo lya Bakabaka b’ebuvanjuba litegekebwe.

Era malayika ow’omusanvu alifuka ekibya kye mu bbanga: ne wabaawo eddoboozi ddene eriva mu Yeekaalu y’eggulu ku ntebe y’obwakabaka, nga ligamba nti, Kiwedde. Ne wabaawo amaloboozi, n’okubwatuka, n’okumyansa, ne wabaawo musisi mungi nnyo, ogutali gwati okuva abantu lwe baali ku nsi, musisi gwa maanyi bwe gutyo. N’ekibuga ekinene kiryawulwamu ebitundu bisatu, n’ebibuga by’amawanga birigwa. Kale ebintu bino bwe biriba.

Kale mutegeere, baana bange, nti Yahweh alikweka ababe ne mu kiseera ky’okubonaabona kwa Yakobo, ne mu kiseera ky’okubonaabona okungi ens remnant erisigalawo nga nnamu era eribeera mu bulokozi mu ddungu, kifo ekyategekebwa Katonda yennyini, balyoke babaliisize eyo ennaku lukumi mu bibiri mu nkaaga. Tewandisigaddewo muntu mulamu singa ennaku tezaagendako. N’abo abagwa balyekuŋŋaanya okulwana ne Kabaka wa Bakabaka era Mukama wa Bakama, kale bwe kyawandiikibwa era bwe kirikolebwa.

Ne ndaba eggulu nga libikkuse, laba embalaasi enjeru, n’oyo eyali agituddeko yayitibwa Mwesigwa era ow’Amazima, era mu butuukirivu asala omusango era alwana. Amaaso ge gaali ng’olulimi lw’omuliro, ne ku mutwe gwe kwaliko engule nnyingi, era yalina erinnya eriwandiikiddwa, eritamanyiddwa muntu yenna wabula ye yekka. Era yali ayambadde olugoye olunyikiddwa mu musaayi, n’erinnya lye liyitibwa, Kigambo kya Katonda. N’eggye eryali mu ggulu lyamugoberera nga litudde ku mbalaasi enjeru, nga lyambadde bafuta ennungi, enjeru era ennyonjo. Ne mu kamwa ke mwava ekitala eky’obwogi, alyoke akitwale okukuba amawanga: era alibafuga n’omuggo ogw’ekyuma: era alinnyirira essogolero ly’obusungu n’ekiruyi kya Katonda Omuyinza w’ebintu byonna. Era alina ku lugoye lwe, ne ku kisambi kye erinnya eriwandiikiddwa, KABAKA WA BAKABAKA, ERA MUKAMA WA BAKAMA.

Era bwe kityo malayika wange alikka okuva mu ggulu, ng’alina ekisumuluzo ky’obunnya obutakoma n’olujegere olunene lw’alisibiramu Sitaani mu bunnya obutakoma okumala emyaka lukumi n’ateekawo akabonero aleme okukyusa amawanga emyaka lukumi. Ku nkomerero y’obufuzi bwange obw’emyaka olukumi, Sitaani alisumululwa okuva mu bunnya okumala akaseera katono.

Laba! Nga Yokaana Omubikkulirwa bwe yawandiika, bwe kiriba: Ne ndaba entebe z’obwakabaka, ne batuula ku zo, ne baweebwa okusala omusango: ne ndaba emyoyo gy’abo abaasalibwako emitwe olw’okutegeeza kwa Yesu, n’olw’ekigambo kya Katonda, era abatasaasinzanga nsolo, newaakubadde ekifaananyi kyayo, newaakubadde abataafunanga kabonero kaayo ku byenyi byabwe, oba mu ngalo zaabwe; ne baba balamu ne bafuga ne Kristo emyaka lukumi. Naye abalala ku bafu tebaaba balamu nate okutuusa emyaka olukumi lwe gyaggwaako. Kuno kwe kuzuukira okw’olubereberye. Wa mukisa era mutukuvu oyo alina omugabo mu kuzuukira okw’olubereberye: ku abo okufa okw’okubiri tekulina buyinza, naye baliba Bakabona ba Katonda, era ba Kristo, era balifuga naye emyaka lukumi.

Era ku nkomerero y’obufuzi bwange obw’emyaka olukumi Sitaani alisumululwa okuva mu kkomera lye okumala akaseera katono okubakuŋŋaanya ng’omusenyu gw’ennyanja. Era aligenda okukyusa amawanga agali mu nsonda ennya ez’ensi, Gogi ne Magogi, okubakuŋŋaanya okulwana: omuwendo gwabwe guli ng’omusenyu gw’ennyanja. Era balyambuka ku bugazi bw’ensi, ne bazingiza olusiisira lw’abatukuvu, n’ekibuga ekyagalwa: n’omuliro gulikka okuva eri Katonda mu ggulu, ne gubookya. N’omulyolyomi eyabakyusa alisuulibwa mu nnyanja ey’omuliro n’ekibiriiti, ensolo n’omunnabbi ow’obulimba we balibeera, era balibonyaabonyesebwa emisana n’ekiro emirembe n’emirembe.

Era bwe kityo kulijja okuzuukira kw’abatali batuukirivu, kubanga bwe kyawandiikibwa Yokaana Omubikkulirwa: Ne ndaba entebe ennene enjeru ey’obwakabaka, n’oyo eyali agitaddeko, ensi n’eggulu ne bidduka okuva mu maaso ge; so tewaafunika kifo kya byo. Ne ndaba abafu, abato n’abakulu, nga bayimiridde mu maaso ga Katonda; n’ebitabo ne bibikkulwa: n’ekitabo ekirala ne kibikkulwa, kye Kitabo ky’Obulamu: n’abafu ne basalirwa omusango okuva mu ebyo ebyali biwandiikiddwa mu bitabo, okusinziira ku bikolwa byabwe. N’ennyanja n’ewaayo abafu abaagirimu; n’okufa n’emagombe ne biwaayo abafu abaabirimu: ne basalirwa omusango buli muntu okusinziira ku bikolwa bye. N’okufa n’emagombe ne bisuulibwa mu nnyanja ey’omuliro. Kuno kwe kufa okw’okubiri. Na buli ataasangibwa ng’awandiikiddwa mu Kitabo ky’Obulamu n’asuulibwa mu nnyanja ey’omuliro.

Era bwe kyawandiikibwa era bwe kirikolebwa, nti waliwo okukuŋŋaana abafu mu Kristo we balizuukizibwa n’abo abalamu abasigaddewo balitwalibwa wamu nabo mu bire, era kino kiribaawo nga tebunnaba kutandika kubonaabona kwa Yakobo. Awo ku nkomerero y’okubonaabona kwa Yakobo kulibaawo okuzuukira kw’abatuukirivu, okukolebwa abatukuvu ab’omu kiseera ekyo n’abo ab’omu Ndagaano Enkadde abaafa nga balina okukkiriza, nga baali babeerawo okusinziira ku bisuubizo. Era nga bwe kyawandiikirwa Danyeri, bwe kiriba nti: Naye genda olinde enkomerero: kubanga oliwummula, era oliyimirira mu mugabo gwo ku nkomerero y’ennaku. Kale enkomerero y’ennaku ye ntikko ya wiiki ya Danyeri ey’amakumi asatu era ku ntandikwa y’obufuzi bwange obw’emyaka olukumi.

Era ku nkomerero y’obufuzi bwange obw’emyaka olukumi era oluvannyuma lwa Sitaani okusuulibwa mu busika bwe obw’enkomerero mu nnyanja eyo eyaka emirembe, kulibaawo okuzuukira kw’abatali batuukirivu, ye musango gw’oku ntebe ennene enjeru gulibaawo, na buli muntu erinnya lye eritaba liwandiikiddwa mu Kitabo ky’Obulamu alisuulibwa mu nnyanja ey’omuliro.

Era bwe kityo nze, ye Yesu Omunazaaleeti, ye Mwana gw’Endiga, ye Kristo Yesu, era ye Mpologoma y’omu kika kya Yuda, nja kujja kusala musango n’okulwanyisa Oggwekkeredde, n’obwakabaka bwe bwonna bulimenyekamenyeka ddala, ne butaddamu kugoloka. N’ensolo n’omunnabbi ow’obulimba balisuulibwa nga balamu mu nnyanja ey’omuliro, ne Sitaani alisibibwa n’olujegere olunene mu bunnya obutakoma okumala emyaka lukumi, era bwe kityo obufuzi bwange obw’emyaka olukumi bulitandika, era obufuzi bwange buliba bwa butuukirivu. Laba! Ndeeta Omugole wange n’ekibiina kya bakabaka n’abakabona be ndifugira nabo ensi mu butuukirivu, era ndifuga amawanga n’omuggo ogw’ekyuma. Kale mubeere n’essuubi eddene, baana bange, kubanga okutuukirizibwa kwa byonna kuli kumpi, n’ekiseera kino eky’emyaka omusanvu eky’okubonaabona okungi, ye nnaku ya Yakobo, kirigobererwa obufuzi bwange obw’obutuukirivu, era Yahweh aliba byonna mu byonna.

Era bwe ntyo mbawadde essuubi eddene, baana bange abato, mulyoke musimbeko ebirowoozo byammwe ku ssanyu eribateereddwa mu maaso kubanga okubonaabona kw’ennaku ya Yakobo tekugeraageranyizibwa newaakubadde tekusaana kwogerwako okugeraageranya n’essanyu ery’olubeerera eribateereddwa mu maaso. Laba! Kyawandiikibwa Yokaana Omubikkulirwa: Bano beebo abaava mu kubonaabona okungi, ne boza engoye zaabwe, ne bazeeza mu musaayi gw’Omwana gw’Endiga. Kyebava babeera mu maaso g’entebe ya Katonda, ne bamuweereza emisana n’ekiro mu Yeekaalu ye: n’oyo atuula ku Ntebe alibeera mu bo. Tebalifuna njala nate, newaakubadde ennyonta, n’enjuba teribookya, newaakubadde ebbugumu lyonna. Kubanga Omwana gw’Endiga, ali wakati mu ntebe, alibaliisa, era alibakulembera okutuuka ku nzizi z’amazzi amalamu: ne Katonda alisiimuula amaziga gonna mu maaso gaabwe. Baliba Bakabona ba Katonda, ne ba Kristo, era balifuga naye emyaka lukumi.

Era bwe lityo essuubi lyammwe, baagazi bange, mulyoke mugumiikirize okutuusa ku nkomerero, era essuubi lyammwe lya lubeerera, kubanga El Shaddai, Katonda Omuyinza w’ebintu byonna, alitonda eggulu eddene n’ensi empya omubeera obutuukirivu, era muliweebwa okuyingira mu kibuga ekyo ekitukuvu ekyategekebwa ne kiyondekebwa Yahweh yennyini ng’omugole ategekeddwa omugole omusajja, era ku muti gw’obulamu muliirya. Era nga bwe kyawandiikibwa nti: Asinga, alisikira byonna, nange ndiba Katonda we, naye aliba mwana wange.

Laba! Nze Yeshua HaMashiach, era ntumye ebigambo byange ng’abiyisa mu muweereza wange, era mu muweereza wange Ebigambo byange mwe byayise.

Laba! Enjiri ey’olubeerera ebikkuddwa era esindikiddwa mu kiseera kyayo ekigyamu, n’ebigambo bino birikuumibwa, era birituuka ku maaso, amatu n’emitima gy’abo bonna be bisindikiddwako.

Laba! Mwekuume era temuleka bifo byammwe, bantu bange, kubanga ebiseera bituuse.

Laba! Ebiseera bye bino!
