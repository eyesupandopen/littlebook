Ogw'ekkumi n'ebiri 5, 2024

Endabirwamu Elimba!

Ggwe endabirwamu, endabirwamu ku kisenge, mbuulira ku nsi etobye mu byuma ebisikiriza ebitegeero byonna! Nga biddibwamu obulimba obubulirirwa emirundi n'emirundi, n'ebifaananyi eby'obulimba ebitaliiko kkomo ne bikola ebirowoozo byabwe mu bukyamu obw'amaanyi n'okusanyusibwa okw'emikolo, bwe batyo endiga ne zibuuzibwa emabega w'ebigambo ebyali mu birowoozo byabwe! Newaakubadde bwe kityo, basikirizibwa okwegomba kw'omubiri, n'okwegomba kw'amaaso n'amalala g'obulamu agakolebwako ne gasakira mu kumanya okutali kutuufu okubweereddwa mu kiyitibwa okusoma wonna awalabikira obulimba bwabwe obukyamufu. Bwe batyo, amatu ne gasanyusibwa era ne gagondererwa amaloboozi agaddibwamu emirundi n'emirundi ab'amalala n'abasirusiru ne bafugibwa ne bakulirwa. Era mu bbanga erituuse baazudde nti okuyita mu nnyimba z'abaana ezifuga ebirowoozo, empisa z'omuntu zikyusibwa era n'asibibwa mu ddogo lyabwe!

Naye bwe ntyo ensi gy'obeeramu bw'eri, ggwe Isirayiri, era ensi etobye ddala mu bubi, era ensi esibiddwa era n'esikirizibwa ddala olulimbolimbo lw'ebigambo eby'obulimba ebikozesebwa okuteekateeka abantu eri enjigiriza zaabwe ez'okutta ezibweereddwa obutayosa kaakano olw'okuzikiriza Adamu. Endabirwamu, ezirabikira wonna, naye mu kutunuulira kw'endabirwamu amazima temuli, kubanga amazima tegalabibwa maaso agazitunuulira kubanga endabirwamu zaabwe za bukuusa okukola embeera etali ya ntuufu ekoleddwa tekinologiya w'ebitonde ebyagwa eyategekebwa okusiba abantu mu bulimba bwabyo Adamu afe emirembe gyonna.

Endabirwamu enzirugavu, ezirabikira wonna era nga ziteereddwa okulagibwa wonna amaaso gye gayinza okulaba, endabirwamu zibweere obulimba bwe bumu nga bukwasaganyiziddwa n'obukujjukujju obubi, omuntu ow'obutonde n'asanyusibwa ekibi n'okufa era n'asikirizibwa ebiraguzo ebingi era eby'amaanyi ebiteekeddwateekeddwa ng'amagezi n'obugagga naye nga bireeta kufa na kuzikirira kubanga temuli mazima mu bifaananyi eby'obulimba bye bawa. Endabirwamu enzirugavu, ezirabikira era ezikwatibwa mu ngalo, ebifaananyi nga biddiriŋŋana ne bizimba eddalu n'obulimba obw'amaanyi ku nsi, amazima gye gatayawuliddwa ku bulimba, bonna abasanyukira obutali butuukirivu tebaliwona naye balifa emirembe n'emirembe mu nnimi z'omuliro ogutazikira era ekibonerezo kye basibiddwa emirembe n'emirembe era tewali we bayinza kuddukira oba okwekweta awalina okwewala omusango.

Ai, wulira nga bayimba! Wuliriza owulire eddoboozi ly'ebinyonyi ebigezi ebingi nga biyimusa amaloboozi awamu, nga bituukagana n'ebifaananyi eby'obulimba ebikwatagana ebikompe byabwe ebiddugavu bye biwa obutayosa. Bayimba okumpi n'ennyimba z'ennyonyi z'amazima naye mu butuufu eyabwe luyimba lwa kikwangala olugamba obulimba amaloboozi gaabwe ne gakwatagana n'obwegendereza obw'obulimba mu bukuusa bwabwe bwe banoonya okukweka. Ate era bino ebinyonyi ebigezi eby'obulimba tebirina nnyimba zaabyo zokka naye bikoppa ne biddamu ennyimba z'ennyonyi z'amazima nga tebiwandiika oba okutegeera ebigambo ebiri mu nnyimba ze bayimba, era ebifaananyi ebisaasaanyizibwa ku ndabirwamu enzirugavu nga ze skrini bikwataganyizibwa obulimba ne buddaabuddwamu n'abantu ne balemala. Era okutegeera okw'obulimba n'embeera ey'obulimba ensi yonna mwe yatwalirwa mu bulimba bw'ebitonde ebyagwa, era embeera ey'obulimba ejjidde ku nsi okweyongera bw'o nga nziggyawo omuzibi wange bwe nkung'aanyiza abeesigwa mu Kristo mu kifo eky'obukuumi mu ggulu amangu ago era mu ngeri etasuubirwa kaakano.

N'olwekyo, wuliriza ebigambo bya Yeshua HaMashiach otegeere ensi gy'obeeramu n'obulimba obukkiriziddwa okukwata ensi mu mikono gyakyo egy'obulimba. Era obulimba obuweereddwa mu musango ebitonde ebyagwa gye bikkiriziddwa okuba n'obufuzi bwabyo, era ekyo kubanga omuntu ow'obutonde ayagala okwegatta ku Sitaani mu bujeemu bwabwe olw'empeera y'obutali butuukirivu, asobole okusanyukira obutali butuukirivu okutuusa lw'azikirira, era omuntu ow'obutonde kaakano agenda kuweebwayo mu mikono gy'abalabe be, naye era ekibiina kinene kigenda kulokolebwa bwe kityo n'omuliro gw'abayenguzi. Era okwawukana okusingawo era okujjuvu kujja kubaawo mu kiseera ky'okubonaabona kwa Yakobo, era okwawukana omulimu gwa Isirayiri gye guwonyezebwa okuva mu busibe bwa Esawu ne gufuulibwa gwa ddembe okuyingira mu bwakabaka bwange obw'emyaka olukumi obuli kumpi okuteekebwawo ku nsi!

Naye, omuntu ow'obutonde asinga kulonda embeera y'obulimba ey'ebitonde ebyagwa gye banoonya okukweka Yakuwa (kwe kutasoboka kukweka), naye bwe butyo obulimba obw'amaanyi bwe baasaasaanyizza ku nsi bonna abagaana Yesu Omunazaaleeti, era Yeshua HaMashiach, gye basibiddwa. N'olwekyo, wuliriza Ebigambo bya Yeshua HaMashiach, olyoke otegeere ensi gy'obeeramu n'obulimba obw'amaanyi obukutte ensi mu mukono gwabwo. Tegeera, ggwe Isirayiri, nti nze, Yeshua HaMashiach era ali Kristo Yesu nze oyo ajja okuggyawo obuzibe bwo obw'ekitundu bw'okyukiza emitima gyo mu kuwulira okujjuvu eri eddoboozi lyange, eriyogerwa ku mimwa gy'abeesigwa ababeerawo olw'okukkiriza mu Mwana wa Yakuwa, kubanga omutuukirivu ajja kubeerawo olw'okukkiriza!

Kiki ekyabangawo? Ekyo, ekyo kye kiriba: era kiki ekyakolebwa? Ekyo kye kirikolebwa nate: so tewali kigambo kipya wansi w'enjuba. Waliwo ekigambo kyonna, omuntu ky'ayinza okwogerako nti, Laba kino, kipya? Kyabangawo dda mu biseera eby'edda ebyatukulembera. Tewali kijjukizo kya kiseera kino eky'olubereberye, so tewaliba kijjukizo kya kiseera ekiriddawo ekiriba, eri abo abaliddawo. Mazima ddala, ekiseera kijja kutuuka ebitonde ebyagwa lwe bitajja kujjukirwa, so tebijja kujja mu birowoozo n'ebyama byabyo eby'obulimba bijja kuggwaawo emirembe n'emirembe bwe birisuulibwa mu nnimi z'omuliro.

N'olwekyo, tegeera obulimba obw'edda obwaweebwa ebitonde ebyagwa eri abantu mu nsi eyaliwo olubereberye amataba amangi aga Nuuwa nga teganajja, era obulimba buno bwe bumu budddamu okuteekebwateekebwa ne bukolebwa okuzikiriza abantu omulundi omulala. Kale tegeera, ebitonde ebyagwa bireeta tekinologiya waabyo n'obufuzzi bwabyo n'obubonero obw'obulimba n'ebyamagero bye baakozesa okuloga n'okuzikiriza abantu mu nnaku za Yaledi. Era ensi eridda nga bwe yali mu nnaku za Nuuwa era bwe kityo n'okubonaabona kwa Yakobo kuliba, era ekiseera ku nsi ekifugibwa abalabe ba Yakuwa. Era ogusota gunene ogw'edda ogusibiddwa kaakano ku nsi olw'omusango, n'emizizo mingi girisumululwa mu nsi y'abantu; era nga Yakuwa bwe yalangirira nti ajja kuleeta ebikuukuulu okutuukiriza obusungu bwe.

Kino bakimanyi mu bugenderevu nti olw'ekigambo kya Katonda eggulu lyabangawo edda, n'ensi nga yava mu mazzi, ne mu mazzi, ensi eyaliwo mu kiseera ekyo, bwe yasaanaayizibwa n'amataba amangi, n'ezikirira. Naye eggulu n'ensi ebiriwo kaakano, olw'Ekigambo kye kimu biterekebwa, ne biteresebwa omuliro okutuusa ku lunaku lw'omusango, n'okuzikirira kw'abantu abatali batukuvu.

Eky'ennaku, ebibiina bingi nnyo ku nsi bimanyi mu bugenderevu nti ensi eyaliwo mu kiseera ekyo Yakuwa yagisaanyaawo n'amataba mangi n'ezikirira olw'Abanefuli n'emizizo gyabwe emingi; era olw'ebitonde ebyagwa ebyefuula nga bakatonda bwe baayonoona amakubo g'abantu ku nsi n'ebyama byabyo ebitagasa ebyayigirizibwa abantu olw'okuzikirira kwabwe bo bennyini. Kale wulira amazima, otegeere Ebigambo byange, owulire eddoboozi lya Yeshua HaMashiach olyoke otegeere amazima era oleme okutegulwa tekinologiya waabyo era oleme okukyusibwa akabonero k'ensolo ofuuke omuzizo oguteresebwa enimi z'omuliro ez'okubonerezebwa emirembe gyonna.

Kubanga tekinologiya waabyo ye eyo eyakolebwa edda, era kaakano eriwo, era egenda kujja mu bujjuvu nate omulundi ogusembayo ogw'enkomerero. Ddala ddala, tekinologiya waabyo ajja kubikkulibwa mu bujjuvu nga beeyongera okuteekawo enkola yaabwe ey'ensolo esembayo, n'obubonero bwonna obw'obulimba n'ebyamagero ebiweereddwa abo abazikirira. Era ogusota gujja kugoloka okufuga ensi mu bulimba obungi okuyita mu mwana gw'endiga alina amayembe abiri.

Era ebitonde ebyagwa biribikkula nga bitonde birungi ebiva mu nsi ezetooloolatooloola mu ngeri ey'obulimba mu njigiriza yaabyo ey'obulimba ey'ensi ezetooloolatooloola obutayosa eyakozesebwa mu nnaku ez'edda. Tegeera nti waggulu w'ensi waliwo eggulu n'ebbanga era n'eggulu ly'eggulu Yakuwa gy'atudde ku ntebe ye ey'obwakabaka, era mazima ddala Yakuwa afuga byonna byonna by'atonda by'atonda ye yekka ku lubereberye olw'okuteesa kw'okwagala kwe ye yennyini era olw'ebigendererwa bye eby'emirembe n'emirembe.

N'olwekyo, somera okutegeera ensi yo nga bw'eri ddala era otegeere nti pulaneti ze mmunyeenye ezitambula eziweereddwa olw'obubonero n'ebiseera era si nsi ndala ezirina ebiramu ebirala, n'olwekyo, gaana obulimba bw'ebitonde ebyagwa. Bano abayitibwa abagwira be abo abaajeemera Yakuwa okuva ku lubereberye era bali b'ebweru w'ensi mu ngeri nti ensibuko yaabwe eri mu ggulu ku lunaku lwe baatondebwa mu butuukirivu obutuukiridde Yakuwa yennyini. Naye, mu bujeemu bwabwe baagwa okuva mu butuukirivu bwabwe obw'olubereberye, era beekyusizza emirembe n'emirembe, so tebaliwona. Naye omuntu ow'obutonde asinga kulonda obulimba buno alyoke atuukirize okwegomba kw'omubiri gwe omuvundu mu butali butuukirivu bwonna, na bonna abatambulira mu kkubo lino eggazi ery'okuzikirira baligwa mu nnimi z'omuliro ez'emirembe n'emirembe bwe batyo.

Era bwe batyo balirabika, nga befuula abafaayo ku nsonga z'abantu era balibikkula mu bujjuvu omuzibi bw'aliggibwawo Ekkanisa yange ng'ekung'aanyiziddwa gye ndi mu bire. Balirabika nga boogera nti bazze okukuuma abantu ku bulumbaganyi n'okulokola abantu mu lutalo era balijja n'obulimba bungi obuteekeddwateekeddwa obulungi era obuwulirika obulungi. Naye, amayembe ekkumi galina ekirowoozo kimu era galiwa ensolo amaanyi gaago n'obuyinza bwago! Era amayembe ekkumi ge galyokyanga omwenzi, ne gamufuula matongo era ne gamwambula, ne galya ennyama ye, ne gamwokya omuliro mu lutalo lwabwe olw'ensi yonna olw'okusatu oluli kumpi okusaasaanira ensi. Ate era balirabika ne tekinologiya waabyo mu ngalo, naye bireeta nsa eza kikwangala n'amakubo amakyamu ag'obulimba n'obukuusa ebivaamu omusango ogw'emirembe n'okuzikirira eri bonna abegatta ku bujeemu bwabyo.

Naye, bwe batyo baligoloka, era ensolo evaa mu nnyanja n'ensolo evaa mu ttaka, era baligoloka ng'omwana gw'endiga alina amayembe abiri ayogera ng'ogusota, era Omwana gw'endiga gw'ogusota Esawu gwe yeerondedde okufuga ensi mu butali butuukirivu. Era okufuga amawanga gye gafugibwa mu bukambwe Ashkenaz okuyita mu Noahide ensolo evaa mu nnyanja n'ensolo evaa mu ttaka zifuga ettaka n'ensi yonna okuyita mu Talmud ya Esawu mu bukuusa obungi ng'atega omutego gwe eri Yakobo.

Tegeera nti ensolo evaa mu nnyanja yeewagguddemu ku Babulooni ey'Ekyama alyoke yeewaggulemu ku misango gye egiri emingi, era balimusaaliza erinnya lye ne balabika ng'abamuvunaana. Naye tegeera nti ensolo evaa mu nnyanja n'ensolo evaa mu ttaka biva mu bika by'omusaayi eby'omuzizo bye bimu era bikolera wamu, era ekibiina eky'ensolo, okuweereza n'okumatiza ebigendererwa by'ogusota eby'okwegomba. Ensolo evaa mu nnyanja erigoloka mu kiseera kyayo era erigoloka okufuuka omulwanyi waabwe omusuubize, era Mahdi waabwe gwe balindirira kati. Era ajja kukweka obuzaale bwe obw'amazima n'ebyo ebyayita n'emisango gye, newaakubadde okufuba okwo tekuliiko mugaso kubanga buli kibi ky'akoze kyawandiikibwa mu bujjuvu mu ggulu. Newaakubadde, ajja kulimba abo abamugoberera olw'ebisuubizo bye ebitaliimu nsa.

Era bwe baligoloka okuva mu vvu ly'olutalo, balyegatta mu lwatu n'ogusota n'ebitonde ebyagwa bye bali, era balyanguwa okweteekawo mu bwakabaka bwabwe obusembayo, bwe baateekawo dda omusingi gwabwo, era nga Babulooni ey'Ekyama tennazikirizibwa okuyita mu kyama eky'obutali butuukirivu. Naye, balinoonya okuzimba ekyo ekyaliwo dda ebitonde ebyagwa bwe binoonya okuteekawo emirembe gyaabyo emipya egya zaabu, era emirembe gya zaabu gye batayinza kubeera nagyo naye omusango omukambwe. N'olwekyo, baligoloka mu maanyi amangi n'ensolo emu eraga ku nnyinaayo era balitegeeza eby'omu ku mulala n'omulala ku omu n'ebigambo bingi eby'amaanyi n'eby'okuvvoola bye baliyogera n'obubonero bungi obw'obulimba n'ebyamagero bye balireeta.

Naye era, bwe batyo bwe banaagolokoka nga bwe kyawandiikibwa. Era Omwana gw'Endiga alina amayembe abiri ajja kukolera wamu n'ogusota, era bijja kwegatta ne biba kimu mu ntegeka yaabwe empya. Bijja kugatta enkola yaabyo ey'eby'ensimbi, bijja kugatta gavumenti zaabyo, bijja kusiba ebintu byonna eby'omu ntegeka yaabyo empya awamu era ne byeyagalanya ne biba kimu mu nteeko yaabyo empya. Bijja kugatta eddiini zaabyo ez'ekyama ez'okuvvoola ez'edda ne zifuuka eddiini empya eneefaanana ng'eddiini ya bakitaabwe abaasinzanga obukookoolo bwabwe obuddugavu, naye bijja kugatta eddiini zaabyo ne ziba eddiini endala enkyamu bakitaabwe gye bataamanya, naye obukookoolo bwabwe bujja kusigalawo. (Naye era, tajja kussa kitiibwa mu Katonda wa bakitaawe, newaakubadde okwegomba kw'abakazi, so tajja kufaayo ku Katonda yenna: kubanga ajja kwekuluntaza yekka okusinga byonna).

Mutegeere, ggwe Isirayiri, kye bajja okukola, kubanga bajja kulwanyisa abatukuvu era bajja kubawangula, era bonna abanaakoowoola erinnya lya Yesu Omunazaaleeti, ye Yesua HaMasiya, okutuusa ku nkomerero balilokolebwa, kubanga bwe kityo bwe kyasuubizibwa era ne kiwandiikibwa. Balivumirira abo abakoowoola erinnya lya Yesu olw'ebikolwa byabwe ebibi, era Balaba ajja kulyamu abatukuvu b'e Babulooni eky'ekyama olukwe, kubanga bangi ku bo baawaayo Amakanisa gaabwe eri ensolo eva mu nsi nga tebamanyi, eyo etenajja mu kiseera kino eky'okuwandiika kuno. N'okwefuula Mukama waabwe era n'atunda Baibuli mu linnya lye, naye ye kennyini akkiriza nti si Mukristaayo, wadde nga bangi mu busirusiru bakkiriza nti ye ye.

Era ensi bw'eneeyiringitanga ng'evudde mu mbeera n'egwa mu ntalo ennene, ne mu kawumpuli, ne mu njala, ne mu butali bwenkanya, ne mu kufa, balivumirira ebintu byonna ebya Kristo olw'ebizibu byabwe bye beereetera, era baligamba nti abagwira be baatwala abakkiriza abaakungaanyizibwa mu Kristo, so nga mu mazima Nze nja kuba mbakungaanyirizza mu kisenge ky'Entebe y'Obwakabaka eya Yawe okw'embaga y'omugole omusajja n'omugole we omukazi, era balyoke baweebwe omugabo gwabwe era bagulumizibwe emirembe gyonna. Naye ku nsi balirimba ebigambo bingi, era balijja ne bategatega ebintu bingi nnyo ku ndabirwamu zaabwe enzirugavu nga bakozesa obukujjukujju n'obuyiiya bw'abo abaagwa, era balisaasaanya obubonero n'ebyamagero bingi eby'obulimba ku ntimbe zaabwe, abantu bangi bye bajja okukkiriza amangu ago.

Mutegeere nti abo ababeera mu bibuga byabwe n'abo abakkiriza akabonero k'ensolo balikyusibwa emirembe gyonna, era balitabula ekyuma n'ebbumba naye tebirikwatagana. Abo abakkiriza akabonero kaayo balikyusibwa era baliba baddu b'ebirowoozo byakyo eby'ekigambo kimu n'omutimbagano gw'ebintu ogugenda okubaawo, era balisibibwa okukola byonna ogusota bye gwagala, gwe banaaba beesibyeeko emirembe gyonna, kubanga banaaba beekyusizza okuva ku kifaananyi kya Yawe mwe baatonderwa. Era bonna abakola ebintu ng'ebyo tebalisonyiyibwa, kubanga ekintu ekikyamye tekiyinza kugololwa.

Kale mutegeere nti entegeka yaabwe empya ejja kubeera nga yonna ezimbiddwa ku bukuusa n'obulimba, era abantu ab'oku nsi balyawukanira ddala mu bulimba okuva ku nsi ey'amazima n'embeera ey'obutuufu mwe balibeera era mwe balituula. Ensi yonna erisibibwa mu bukuusa obunene, era abantu bangi balyewuunya ensolo nga bagamba nti, ani alinga ensolo era ani ayinza okulwana nazo? Era abantu bangi nnyo baligwa mu nnimi z'omuliro olw'okukkiriza obulimba bwazo, era abantu abo bennyini amannya gaabwe ge gataawandiikibwa mu kitabo ky'obulamu okuva ku kutondebwa kw'ensi, baligwa emirembe gyonna kubanga baliba bagaanye ddala Yawe olw'okugaana Yesu Kristo, ye Yesua HaMasiya.

Okugaana kuno kuliba kwa lubeerera era tebalisonyiyibwa kubanga baayisa Ebigambo by'obulokozi ebyandibalokodde ddala ng'ebitali bitukuvu ne basalawo nti amazima ga Katonda bulimba, era kibi kye batagenda kuwonako. Naye bwe kutyo kwe kuliba okwawukana okw'enkomerero okw'ebiseera, nti tewaliba kintu kyonna kibi kiyinza kuyingira mu Bwakabaka bwange obw'emyaka olukumi, wabula abo bokka abajja eri Yesua HaMasiya, ye Yesu Kristo, nga beenenyezza mu biseera by'obulokozi, kubanga tewali muntu ayinza kujja eri Yawe mu linnya eddala lyonna so tewali na kkubo ddala, kubanga Nze kkubo ery'amazima era eddamu.

Bino bye biseera mwe muli, ggwe Isirayiri, era toyinza kubeera mu bibuga byabwe so toyinza kwegatta ku ntegeka yaabwe, kubanga okwegatta nabo kyetaagisa akabonero, era bonna abakkiriza akabonero k'ensolo tebalisonyiyibwa. Naye era, omuntu ayigiriziddwa okuyita mu kusoma kwe okw'ekitiibwa okw'obulimba, okwo kwe kuyigirizibwa okutundibwa ng'okusoma, era abantu bangi bwe batyo bwe bateekebwateekebwa, era balikkiriza obulimba bwabwe, era ekitundu ekisinga obunene eky'omuntu ow'obutonde kiryegatta n'abo abaagwa emirembe gyonna.

Naye bwe kiriba bwe kityo, nti bonna abakkiriza obulimba ku bwabwe ne basanyukira obutali butuukirivu basobole okusibibwa okubonerezebwa okw'emirembe n'emirembe olw'okwegatta n'abo abaagwa, era bonna abeegatta n'abo abaagwa baligabana mu bujjuvu omugabo gwabwe ogugabibwa awatali kusaasira, kubanga tebaalaga kusaasira wabula obukyayi obw'effujjo eri ebintu byonna ebya Yawe.

Nze Kristo Yesu, era ye Yesu Kristo, era ye Yesua HaMasiya, era nzija ng'empologoma ey'omu kika kya Yuda, era mu butuukirivu mwe njijira okusala omusango n'okulwana olutalo.

Nze oyo ajja okuzikiriza obulimba obunene n'embeera ey'obulimba ey'abo abaagwa, era mu ddembe eddungi ery'amazima n'obutuukirivu ekitonde mwe kirinunulibwa okuyingira mu ddembe eddungi ery'abaana ba Yawe.

Nze oyo ayita abo ab'omu Isirayiri, era mu butuufu bonna ab'omu Adamu okujja mu bulokozi balyoke batabaganyizibwe mu bujjuvu ne Yawe okuyita mu musaayi gw'omwana gw'endiga, kubanga bwe ntyo bwe ndi.

Nze Kristo Yesu, Mukama wammwe eyalinnya mu ggulu era n'azuukira, era nfuuliddwa omwoyo oguwa obulamu, nti bonna abajja gye ndi n'omutima omugazi n'amaaso amagazi basobole okujja mu bujjuvu mu bulokozi mu Mukama waabwe ow'emirembe gyonna!

Nze Yesua HaMasiya, wamu ne Yawe Omukulu NZE NDI, gwe natuma Ebigambo by'Enjiri ey'Emirembe n'Emirembe okuyita mu mubaka wange ow'amaanyi era omuweereza wange eyawangula ebintu byonna ng'ajja mu bujjuvu eri Mukama we ow'emirembe n'emirembe era Omulokozi we mu bwetoowaze n'okwenenya okw'ennaku, era okuyita mu mubaka wange ow'amaanyi era omuweereza wange Ebigambo by'Enjiri ey'Emirembe n'Emirembe mwe bisindikiddwa okuwulirwa kw'abo bonna Ebigambo bino be bisindikiddwa.

N'olwekyo, mutegeere ebiseera eby'obulabe mwe muli, ensi yonna mw'eneetwalirwa mu ddalu n'obulimba, n'ekibi kirirekerwa ddala awatali kuziyizibwa, naye ekibiina ekinene ennyo kirifuna obulokozi obw'emirembe n'emirembe nga bikoowoola erinnya lya Mukama waabwe Yesu okutuusa ku nkomerero. Era mu ngeri y'emu, Nze Yesua HaMasiya, ye Masiya ow'amazima owa Yakobo, ndirokola ensigalira ey'amaanyi ey'omu Isirayiri, eneetwalibwa n'eweebwa okuyingira mu Bwakabaka bwange obw'emyaka olukumi, buli kisuubizo mwe kiriba nga kiriba bwe kityo era Amiina!

N'olwekyo, ggwe Isirayiri, gaana tekinologiya waabwe so togenda mu maaso ng'osikirizibwa endabirwamu zaabwe enzirugavu, kwe kugamba, entimbe zaabwe abantu gye basibiddwa kati nga bamala obulamu bwabwe mu bitaliimu ng'bava ku lutimbe okudda ku lulala nga banyulirwa era nga basanyusibwa ekibi. Kwe kugamba embeera ey'obulimba efaanana ng'ey'amazima, naye mu butuufu, bye bigambo bya ngero eby'obulimba era tewali kirala wabula enfumo ennungi ezirukiddwa awamu okusobozesa okuzikirizibwa kw'omuntu.

N'olwekyo, muve wakati mu bo mweyole mu bintu byonna okuva gye bali. Mwetukuze mu musaayi gwange ogutangirira nga mwatulira nze ebibi byammwe byonna, era mwetukuze mu kigambo kyange ekitukuvu, ye Ebigambo bya Yawe, kubanga Nze Kigambo eyafuuka omubiri abeera era n'atuula mu mmwe, era mazima, munda mu mmwe okuyita mu mwoyo gwange ow'ekisuubizo singa munaawulira Ebigambo byange.

Mbaagala nnyo mmwe abantu bange, era mazima mbalwanirira era ndibawonya ddala singa temuggwaamu maanyi mu bizibu byammwe, naye ne muwummulira mu maanyi gange n'obuyinza bwange, kubanga nzija n'obuyinza obujjuvu obwa Yawe Omukulu NZE NDI, eyagabana entebe ye ey'obwakabaka okutuusa abalabe bange lwe balifuulibwa entebbe y'ebigere byange, era okufuga kwange tekulina nkomerero, era ndirokola abawulize okutuukira ddala mu kwagala n'obulokozi obutaggwaawo, era muliba nate bantu ba njawulo, ggwe Isirayiri, Seera ne Amiina!
