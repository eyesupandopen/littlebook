Muwulire Ebigambo Byange era Mutegeere Ebiro mwe Muli!

Muwulire ebigambo byange, mmwe abantu bange, ye, mmwe mwenna be mpita, era mutegeere ebiro mwe muli. Kubanga ensi gye mumanyi kaakano ejja kuggwaawo, era mu kifo kyayo walisigalawo matongo mangi, ye, n'evvu n'ebifunfugu bya Babulooni eky'Ekyaama, kubanga olutalo lutuuse. Kubanga bwe nnaasikula Omugole wange okumutwala mu ggulu, akabi k'amaanyi kalizingirira Babulooni eky'Ekyaama, era ajja kuzikirizibwa mu ssaawa emu, era mu ssaawa emu mw'alizikirizibwira, so tewali kiyinza kukyusa kugerekwa kwe, kubanga Yakuwa bw'atyo bwe yalangirira.

Kubanga ggwe, Babulooni eky'Ekyaama, wagaana eddoboozi lyange n'onteeka ku bbali, nze Yesu Kristo Omutuukirivu, olw'essanyu ery'ekibi. Kubanga n'okulabula okw'amaanyi okwaweebwa ku ggwanga lyo mu ggulu kwakola ng'eky'okusanyusa emitima gyammwe egitali na kukkiriza n'amaaso gammwe ageegomba, era gye muli lwali lunaku lwa bulijjo bwe mweyuniranga mu bibi byammwe ebibatwala mu kuzikirira. Mwandibadde mwenenyezza!

Kubanga mufuuse eggwanga ery'abasinza ebifaananyi, eggwanga ery'abalwana entalo, eggwanga erirya obulimba era eriyagala okulimbibwalimbibwa kwalyo, eggwanga erijjuzza ensi n'ebizira byalyo; n'amawanga ganywera ku kikompe kyo ekya zzaabu ekijjudde ebizira n'obugwagwa bw'obwenzi bwo.

Kyenva nkuvuganya, era okuzikirira kwo kutuuse era kwatandika dda, kubanga wanyooma okuyita kwa Yakuwa okwayita mu nze, Yesuwa HaMasiiya, era nandibakulokodde. Era nkaabidde olw'emitima gyammwe emikakanyavu, naye era temukyuka kuleka bubi; kyenvudde mbavaako, era ebiro by'Abamawanga binaatera okutuukirizibwa mu bujjuvu bwe nnaakungaanya abeesigwa mu Kkanisa yange ku kuvuga kw'ekkondeere.

Naye oli ggwanga erisibiddwa mu njegere, era ojja kuzikirizibwa ddala, so toliddayo kugulumira. Omugole wange bw'aliggibwawo, ensi eneewuunya nnyo olw'ekyo kye banaaba balabye, kubanga ekikolwa kino tekijja kubaawo mu kyama, wabula mu lwatu, ensi yonna ereme kumanya nti waliyo Katonda mu ggulu era nti yantuma nze, Kristo Yesu, olw'ezzaabu, effeeza, n'amayinja ag'omuwendo ag'oku nsi. Kubanga eby'obugagga bya Kitange ebisingayo omuwendo, ye, ne luulu ez'omuwendo omungi, zijja kuggyibwawo okuwonyezebwa omusango n'okuzikiriza okugenda okujja.

Kubanga Omugole wange yawonyezebwa ekiruyi olw'essaddaaka yange, era nnaanunula abeesigwa n'abo abalisanibwa nga beesigwa mu Kristo mu kiseera eky'okulabika kwange n'abo bonna abaafa nga beesigwa mu Kristo, ye, nga bateekeddwako akabonero nze, Kristo Yesu, kubanga nnatuukibwako okuba omwoyo oguwa obulamu. Era nnaazuukiza abaafa mu Kristo, n'abo abali abalamu abalisigalawo banaatwalibwa wamu nabo okunsisinkana mu bire. Kale mutegeere nti ekikolwa kino kya kuweesa mpeera abeesigwa era n'okutuukiriza obufumbo bw'Omugole Omusajja n'Omugole wange, kubanga tujja kugattibwa olubeerera mu ndagaano era tebalimvaako. Era nze oyo alibafuula bakabaka ne bakabona mu maaso ga Kitange.

Era bwe batyo bwe baliva ku nsi, era ensi bw'etyo bw'eriraba, era bangi baneewuunya nnyo. Kubanga ekitundu eky'ensonga y'ekikolwa kino kwe kuleeta kwenenya mu abo abalisigalawo, ye, abo abaagaana okuyita kwange mu mulembe ogw'ekisa, era n'abangi abatawulirangako kimala okukkiriza abalirokolebwa mu kiseera eky'okubonaabona kwa Yakobo, naddala abo ab'omu nsi ey'ensuubizo.

Kubanga nkuyita, ggwe Isirayiri: weetegese okuwulira eddoboozi lya Masiya wo ow'amazima, Empologoma ey'omu kika kya Yuda? Kubanga eddoboozi lya Masiya wo ow'amazima liyita, era nze oyo alifugira ku ntebe ya Dawudi mu kibuga ekitukuvu, ye, nze, Yesuwa HaMasiiya; kyokka tonyoomanga bigambo bya kuyita kwange! Era n'olwekyo omulembe gw'ekisa, ye, ebiro eby'okuzza obuggya, kati biggwaawo mu nkomerero. Era enkomerero eyo ejja kutuuka mu bujjuvu bwe nnaakungaanyiza abeesigwa b'omu Kkanisa yange gyendi mu bire era n'okugenda kwabwe bwe kulikankanya ensi yonna.

Lujja kukankanya ensi ddala, n'okukankana okw'amaanyi kulirya Babulooni eky'Ekyaama, era ajja kwabulwamu wakati abatukuvu bange bwe baligenda. Ajja kwokebwa omuliro ddala okuva mu ggulu ebyasi bwe birigwa. Ebisambi bye biribikkibwako amazzi okuva ebuvanjuba n'ebugwanjuba! Kubanga malayika ow'amaanyi alisitula ejjinja eriringa olubengo olunene, n'alisuula mu nnyanja, ng'agamba nti, Bwe kityo n'amaanyi ekibuga ekyo ekinene Babulooni bwe kirisuulibwa wansi, so tekiriddayo kulabika. Kubanga bwe kityo bwe kyawandiikibwa era bwe kityo bwe kirikolebwa!

Kubanga omusango gwe munene, n'omusango gutandikidde ku nnyumba ya Katonda. Amakanisa ge amajeemu galizikirizibwa, kubanga ngasalidde omusango gonna agatamuli mu nze, ye, amakanisa gonna amakumpanya agayogera obulimba obw'amaanyi mu bunnanfuusi. Babulooni eky'Ekyaama, singa wandikyukidde nze, Yesu Kristo, n'oleka amakubo go amabi, kubanga nandikulokodde, era nze ne Yakuwa tetulina ssanyu lyonna mu misango eginaatera okuteebwa.

Naye n'ewakubadde, emisango gijja kugwa nga bwe gyalangirirwa, era omusango ogwo gujja kuba gwa maanyi era gwa lubeerera. Kubanga nnakyukira Abamawanga okumala akaseera, era kaakano nja kuva ku Bamawanga era nkyukire ekisibo mwe nnavumbukira, ye, Isirayiri. Newankubadde buli muntu alikoowoola erinnya lya Mukama okutuusa ku nkomerero aliwonyezebwa, k'abe Muyudaaya oba Mummwanga, kubanga obulokozi tebusibiddwa ku nsalo za mawanga.

Ggwe Babulooni eky'Ekyaama, entindo zo zijja kugwa; enguudo zo zijja kusendebwasendebwa, kumenyeka, era nga teziyitikamu; emigogo gyo egy'ebintu by'obwetaavu gitandise dda okusaanaawa era mu bbanga ttono giriba matongo. Tewaliba bintu bya kwetaaga birikuleeterwa, kubanga tewaliba ajja kukuyamba, era oliba wekka mu ssaawa yo ey'okulekebwa awo, ggwe malaaya omukulu. Toliba na ssanyu, n'abo abaliwonawo balisanga nga balwana okulwana olw'okufuna amazzi ag'okunywa. Enjala eriranda mu nsi. N'akawumpuli ak'amaanyi kalikukwata, era n'ensolo zenyini zirirumba, kubanga ebiro bituuse.

Amagye amamyuufu gavumenti yo ennakyemalira ge yakkiriza okuyingira mu nsalo zo galitta buli musajja, mukazi, na mwana abatagyamu migaso gyago. Kubanga balitta era balifuula abaddu bonna be balisanga. Ge magye g'ensolo agaakungaanyizibwa okuva mu nsonda ennya ez'ensi olw'ekigendererwa kino, n'ensolo ekeeseddwa n'omunnabbi ow'obulimba ow'ekiseera be gaakungaanyizza.

Tebalina kisa, tebalina kusaasira, tebalina kya kusonyiwa, tebalina kwenenya, era bakyawa ddala abantu ba Babulooni eky'Ekyaama olw'ebibi eby'entiisa abakulembeze bammwe bye baakola ku nsi zaabwe. Ate nga mu kiseera ekyo mmwe abasinga obungi mwajagulizanga entalo zammwe ezitali za nsonga bwe mwazirabangako ku ttivi zammwe nga musanyuka, bwe mwasanyukiranga ennyo ebibi by'abakulembeze bammwe.

Mwayaniriza abakulembeze bammwe be mwayanirira ebibi byabwe mu kulonda, era kyemuliva mugabana nabo ku musango gwabwe, kubanga mwefuulidde ddala abasaana okufuna omugabo gw'omusango ogw'emirundi ebiri, era gulibatuukako kibwatukira.

Era n'olwekyo Omugole wange alirabika ku ntebe yange ey'omusango, era abamu balifuna okufiirwa, abamu balitwala empeera ennene, era bonna balifuna mu bujjuvu ekyo kye baasooka okuweebwa: Kubanga nja kubawa emibiri emiggya, egitaliimu kibi egitalivundenga! Era n'olwekyo obubonero obw'empeera buliteebwa, kubanga newankubadde okubonaabona n'ennaku bibunye ku nsi okumala emyaka mingi kati, si busungu bwa Katonda newankubadde obusungu bw'omwana gw'endiga.

Abangi ku mmwe mu bujinga mulowooza nti mulina okugumiikiriza obusungu bwa Katonda, nga mulowooza n'amalala nti musobola okugumira ekigenda okujja. Mutegeere nti mponyezza Ekkanisa yange obusungu, kubanga nnajja eri abange n'eri Abamawanga mu mulembe gw'ekisa ng'endagaano ey'emirembe, nga mpa emirembe n'okutabaganyizibwa ne Kitange ow'Ebitangaala, ye, El Shaddai! N'abo be nnalokola baakkiriza ku lwabwe ekisa kyange ekitali kya nsa, kale mugoberere ekyokulabirako kyabwe!

Era nja kuteeka akabonero ku 144,000 bange okuva mu bika bino wammanga, ye, 12,000 okuva mu buli kimu ku bino wammanga: Yuda, Lewubeeni, Gaadi, Aseri, Nafutaali, Manaase, Simyoni, Leevi, Isakaali, Zebbulooni, Yusufu, ne Benyamini. Kale muwulire eddoboozi lyange!

Era mujja kunkolera ebikolwa eby'amaanyi bingi, era mujja kuleeta bangi mu bulokozi okuva mu nsi efiridde era ey'obukambwe. Kubanga Setaani ategesetezza dda obulimba obw'amaanyi era ajja kukola kyonna ky'asobola okulimba, okubikka, n'okukweka okukuŋŋaana okw'amaanyi mu bire okunaatera okubaawo. Obulimba bwe bumaze dda okusimba amakanda mu birowoozo byammwe ne mu mitima gyammwe okuyita mu nnyigiriza y'Abayesuwiti egoba Kitange era ereetebwa gye muli abagwi. Bafuulidde ebintu byonna eby'obukumpanya, nga n'okutuusa okuleeta abangi ku mmwe okukkiriza nti waggulu w'ensi waliwo ebbanga eritaliiko nkomerero ery'enjuba n'obulamu obulala, era abangi baliba bajama nnyo ne bamira obulimba buno. Era n'abo amannya gaabwe ge batawandiikangako mu Kitabo ky'Obulamu, kubanga balikkiriza akabonero kaabwe.

Mumanye, mmwe abantu bange, era muwulire okulabula kwange. Tewali bulamu ku njuba ndala! Musome muyige, kubanga enjuba ze mmunyeenye ezitambula ezaalagirwa okulangirira omulimu gw'engalo za Yakuwa mu bbanga wansi w'olubaawo lw'eggulu. Abo tebaliba bagwira okuva mu nsi endala ezikyukakyuka mu bulimba; naye, si ba ku nsi, kubanga be bamalayika abagwa abaava mu ggulu, era beekoledde ebizira bingi. Temubakkiririzanga!

Kale okuva mu vvu ly'omwebagazi w'embalaasi n'okuva mu kuzikirira okw'amaanyi omwo mwe mulivumbukira ensolo n'omunnabbi ow'obulimba, kubanga ekyama ky'obujeemu kikola dda. Kubanga Babulooni eky'Ekyaama aweereddwa mu mikono gyabwe n'omukago ogukolebwa, ye, amayembe ekkumi galimuzikiriza, kubanga be bakabaka ekkumi abawa byonna byabwe eri ogusota, ensolo, n'omunnabbi ow'obulimba. Kubanga omuzibi bw'aliggibwawo, baligolokoka ddala okusinziira ku Byawandiikibwa; kubanga mu kavuyo mwe muva enteekateeka yaabwe empya, ye, enteekateeka y'Ogusota, kubanga ye buyinza bwabwe n'obukulu bwabwe, kubanga bw'atyo bw'aweereddwa mu ngeri etali ya lubeerera okumala akaseera akatono ennyo.

Ogusota gulina obusungu bungi era gulitambulira mu mwoyo gwa Hamani okufuula ebintu byonna ebya Kristo ebitali mu mateeka, na buli muntu agattibwa ku linnya lyange, ye, Yesu Kristo, aliyigganyizibwa era attibwe. N'abo abataganya bulamu bwabwe okutuusa ku nkomerero baliwonyezebwa era balina empeera nnene. Kale mutere emitima gyammwe n'ebirowoozo byammwe ku ssanyu eribateereddwa mu maaso, kubanga mu ngeri eyo nnanyweza obulokozi bwammwe mu kubonaabona okw'entiisa okwali okwammwe, okuggyako nti nze nneetikka ekibonerezo kyammwe ne nsasulira mu bujjuvu ebibi by'abantu omulundi gumu emirembe gyonna, ye, emirembe gyonna.

Ensolo ejja kutambula mangu, kubanga ejja kweyongerayo okumala emyezi 42 ng'eyogera eby'okuwovoola eby'amaanyi. Erirwana n'abatukuvu, era eweereddwa okubawangula. Eritemako abangi emitwe. Erikola obubonero obw'obulimba bungi n'ebyamagero, era okuva mu vvu mulivumbukira enteekateeka yaayo empya. N'omunnabbi ow'obulimba aligiraga n'agitendereza era n'aleetera bonna okusinza ekifaananyi kyayo, kubanga ebintu bino byawandiikibwa butereevu mu Byawandiikibwa. Era n'olwekyo ensolo ejja kulongoosa ensi okuva ku kintu kyonna ekirungi, ekitukuvu, n'eky'omuwendo. Naye, nja kukweka abeesigwa bange, era ebyamagero bingi bye balikoIa, era abangi baliwonyezebwa.

Naye omunnabbi ow'obulimba, ye, ensolo eva mu nsi, alireetera bonna, abato n'abakulu, abagagga n'abaavu, ab'eddembe n'abaddu, okuteekebwako akabonero ku mukono gwabwe ogwa ddyo oba ku byenyi byabwe: Era nti tewaliba muntu ayinza kugula oba kutunda, wabula oyo alina akabonero, oba erinnya ly'ensolo, oba omuwendo gw'erinnya lyayo. Kale mutegeere nti okwenyigira mu kitundu kyabwe ekipya eky'obulimba kulikwetaagisa akabonero, era bonna abakkiriza akabonero tebalifuna kisonyiwo. Era balifuna omugabo gwabwe mu nnyanja eyo ennene eyaka omuliro n'ekibiriiti olubeerera n'olubeerera, era n'omukka gw'okubonaabona kwabwe bwe gutyo bwe gulitimba.

Abajulirwa bange ababiri baligolokoka, era baliragulanga nga bambadde ebibukutu! Bano be miti emizeyituuni ebiri, n'ebikondo by'ettaala ebibiri ebiyimiridde mu maaso ga Katonda w'ensi. So tewaliba n'omu ayinza kubayimirirawo, kubanga baliragulanga okumala emyezi 42. Era omuntu yenna bw'anaayagala okubakola obubi, omuliro ne guva mu kamwa kaabwe, ne gwokya abalabe baabwe: era omuntu yenna bw'anaayagala okubakola obubi, alina okuttibwa mu ngeri eno. Bano balina obuyinza okuggala eggulu, lireme okutonnya mu nnaku z'obunnabbi bwabwe: era balina obuyinza ku mazzi okugafuula omusaayi, n'okukuba ensi n'ebirwadde byonna, emirundi gyonna gye banaayagaliranga.

Awo bwe balimala okutegeeza obujulirwa bwabwe, ensolo erinnya okuva mu bunnya obutakoma erirwana nabo, era eribawangula, n'ebatta. Era balireka emirambo gyabwe mu nguudo okumala ennaku ssatu n'ekitundu, n'ensi yonna erijaguza, olwo ne bazuukizibwa ne batwalibwa mu ggulu.

Omunnabbi ow'obulimba amaze dda okwegwanyiza abo abali mu nsi okulowooza nti ye Masiya waabwe era kabaka waabwe. Muzimbi wa yeekaalu mukumpanya era ava mu kika kya Ddaani, era si Kkuulo. Ajja kuwa ensolo esooka erinnya okuva mu nnyanja obuwagizi bwonna. Kubanga ensolo esooka ejja kukakaatiriza mu ngeri ey'obukambwe amateeka ga Nuuwa ku mawanga mu bujeemu obw'amaanyi, n'omunnabbi ow'obulimba alifugira ku ntebe etali yiye, kubanga yange, ye, eya Yesuwa HaMasiiya. Era n'olwekyo yeekaalu ejja kuzimbibwa, n'omunnabbi ow'obulimba alikola obubonero bungi obw'obulimba n'ebyamagero, kubanga aweereddwa amaanyi ge gamu n'ensolo esooka, era n'olwekyo bombi balikkolera wamu n'ekirowoozo kimu n'ekigendererwa kimu, ye, n'ekirowoozo n'ebigendererwa by'ogusota ogwo omulimba omunene, naye talina mugaso era talina mannyo, kale temumutyanga!

Kubanga amanyi nti njija kuzikiriza obwakabaka bwe, kubanga si kabaka wabula mulabe awanguddwa ddala, atalina ssuubi lyonna. Kale temumutyanga era mubeere n'ekirowoozo kimu ku nze. Kubanga laba, mbategeeza ebintu bino nga tebinabaawo mulyoke mube n'okukkiriza mu nze era mube n'okukkiriza mu Kitange eyantuma, ye, Yakuwa Katonda ow'olubeerera omukulu, era nange omwana we, Yesuwa HaMasiiya, alituukiriza ebisuubizo ebyakolebwa eri bajjajja.

Era n'olwekyo ku nkomerero y'emyezi 42 egisooka, eky'omuziro eky'okuzikiriza kiribaawo ku mukono gw'omunnabbi ow'obulimba, ye, ensolo erigolokoka okuva mu nsi. Mu myezi 42 egy'okubiri, ye, mu kiseera ekyo mwe mulibeerera okubonaabona okusinga obunene okutabangako era n'eky'omuziro eky'okuzikiriza, era alyerangirira okuba Katonda! Abangi balibonerezebwa era abangi balizikirira, naye abo abawulize eddoboozi lyange balitwalibwa mu kifo mu ddungu gye balikwekebwa ne bakuumibwa obulungi okumala akaseera, kubanga bino bye biriba ebiro eby'obukambwe obusinga, n'ebiro bino eby'okugezesebwa n'okukemebwa bye birisinga obuzibu. Olwo muliraba eggulu nga libikkuse, era mulindaba nze, Yesu Kristo, nga nkulembera okuva mu ggulu eggye eryo eddene era ery'amaanyi ery'omu ggulu, era liriba ggye eritaliiko nsa, lyonna! Kubanga Omugole wange aliba nange. Era nja kubetenta ensolo n'omunnabbi ow'obulimba n'amagye gaabwe gonna n'ebizira byabwe byonna bye baakola mu ngeri ey'ekizira, era bwe batyo bwe balizikirizibwa n'okumasamasa kw'okujja kwange n'ekitala ekyo ekiwagyala ekiwagamivu ekiva mu kamwa kange, so tebaliyimirirawo wabula balizikirizibwa ddala. N'ensolo n'omunnabbi ow'obulimba balisuulibwa nga balamu mu nnyanja eyo eyaka omuliro n'ekibiriiti, so tebaliteebwa kuva mu kubonaabona kwabwe, kubanga beesaanyirizza.

Era olwo okuzuukira kw'abatuukirivu kulibaawo, abatukuvu ab'edda n'abatukuvu ab'omu biro eby'okubonaabona bwe balizuukizibwa okuva mu bafu okuyingira mu bulamu obuggya, so tebaliddayo kufa, era ndibawa empeera nnene, ey'engeri etannayingira mu mitima gy'abantu mu kiseera kino. Kubanga nze Mukama wa kisa era Kabaka wa kisa, era nze bujjuvu bwa Bwakatonda mu mubiri.

Era n'olwekyo okufuga kwange okw'emyaka olukumi ku nsi kulitandika, n'obutuukirivu bulifuga ku nsi, era ndifuga amawanga n'omuggo ogw'ekyuma, n'ensi erijjula amakubo ga Yakuwa. Kubanga njija n'ekibiina ky'abakabaka n'abakabona, era balifugira ku ntebe yange, era balifuga mu butuukirivu. N'empologoma erigalamira wamu n'omwana gw'endiga.

Mbayita okutegeera ebiro mwe muli.

Nze mwoyo oguwa obulamu, kubanga bwe ntyo bwe nnatuukibwako.

Nze oyo alibafuula bakabaka ne bakabona mu maaso ga Kitange.

Mbayita, ggwe Isirayiri.

Njija kuzikiriza obwakabaka bwa Setaani, kubanga si kabaka.

Nze Mukama wa kisa era Kabaka wa kisa.

Nze bujjuvu bwa Bwakatonda mu mubiri.

Njija n'ekibiina ky'abakabaka n'abakabona, era balifugira ku ntebe yange, era balifuga mu butuukirivu.

Mbayita; kyokka temunyooma kuyita kwange!
