Omujulirwa Omulundi Ogw’okubiri: Ekkanisa
Mu Biwandiiko by'Omutume Pawulo
May 10, 2024

Mmwe Abonoonyi! Mujje Gyendi mu Biseera Bino Eby'enkomerero!

Muwulire ebigambo byange, ebigambo bya Yesu Omunazaaleeti, kubanga nzija gye muli mu mwoyo gw'okwagala, okusaasira, obuwombeefu, ekisa, era naddala okusaasira okungi. Kubanga mangu ddala ekisa kyange n'okusaasira bijja kuddizibwa mu kifo kyabyo ennaku enkulu, ye nnaku ya Yakobo, era ekiseera kyonna kino eky’emyaka omusanvu kijja kuba nga tekyali kibaddewo ku nsi, okuva ku kutondebwa kwayo. Kijja kutandika n’okuzikirizibwa okw’amangu n'okuyigganyizibwa okujja okweyongera obukambwe. N'olwekyo, nzija omulundi guno ogw'enkomerero eri aboononyi abatannaba kwenenya mu kisa kyange n’okusaasira. Kubanga wakyaliwo akaseera katono ak’okwenenya kwammwe singa mujja gyendi, Yesu Kristo, ne munjatula nga Mukama wammwe era ne mukkiriza mu mitima gyammwe nti Katonda yanzuukiza mu bafu. Ngolodde emikono gyange mu kisa n'okusaasira eri ensi efiridde mu bbanga lyonna ery'ekisa, era bangi bamfuudde Mukama waabwe, bangi nga Mukama Katonda waffe be yayita era be yalonda okuva ensi lwe yatondebwa okubikkulirwa mu nze, Yesu Kristo Mukama ow'amaanyi: Kubanga nze Mukama wa Bakama era Kabaka wa Bakabaka.

Era njise abalonde, n'abo bonna abafuuse abeesigwa mu bulokozi bwange. Bangi balokose! Mulokose ki, bwe mubuuza? Mulokose musalira gwa nkomerero n’okuzikirizibwa okugenda okuteebwa ku nsi eno, bakatonda baayo, abafuzi baayo, obwakabaka bwayo obw'omwoyo, n'abo bonna abagoberera Setaani, kubanga ye katonda w'ensi eno era omusango gwe gutuuse. Mulokose ne ku musango gw'entebe y'obwakabaka enjeru enkulu n'ennyanja y'omuliro eyategekerwa Omulyolyomi n'abamalayika be. Omusango gusaliddwa ku bafuzi b’ekizikiza kino ekiriwo, era mangu ddala gujja kutuukirira era gutuukirizibwe, kubanga ensi ejja kufugibwa n’omukono gwange mu butuukirivu, kubanga ekiseera kyange kituuse okubikkulirwa, era ensi yange n'okujjuza kwayo, ye nze, Yeshua HaMashiach!

Omusango gujja era gutuuse naye tegunnatuukirira mu bujjuvu. Ate bangi ku mmwe mukyaliwo nga mukyalowooza nti mulina obudde bw'okweyagalira mu bibi byammwe n'okunoonya okumatiza okwegomba kwammwe okw'ekibi okuva mu mubiri gwammwe omwonoonefu. Mutegeere nti sirina kwagala kusala musango era kuvumirira muntu yenna ku mmwe, kubanga nawaayo obulamu bwange mu kwagala kwe nnina gye muli era nga nsasulira ebibi byammwe mu bujjuvu mu bulumi obw’amaanyi n’okubonyaabonyezebwa ku musalaba! Kubanga tewali kibi na kimu ekitasasulirwa mu bujjuvu ssaddaaka yange etangirira ng'Embaga y'Okuyitako etuukiridde. Nze Mwana gw’endiga gwa Katonda aggyawo ebibi by’ensi. Era sajja kukusalira musango, naye ndi wano okubalokola. Ensi nga bwe mugimanyi egenda kukyuka nnyo ne mutayinza kumanya kigenda kuva mu vvu, kubanga ebitundu bingi eby'ensi bigenda kusirikka mu nnimi z'omuliro era bizikirire mu ngeri nnyingi mu musango gwa Yakuwa omutuukirivu. Era entiisa enkulu ejja kuva mu vvu lino.

Kubanga omulangira w'ensi eno asalirwa omusango, era omusango gwe gujja kutuukirizibwa era gutuukirire mu bujjuvu. Kubanga Setaani akoze ebibi bingi n'amakubo ge ga muzizo, era ajja kubba, kutta, na kuzikiriza. Ayonoonye ekika ky’omuntu mu bujjuvu ne mu ngeri nnyingi, era anoonya okuzikiriza byonna Katonda by'ayagala. Kubanga Katonda bwe yayagala ensi, n'awaayo Omwana we omu yekka, buli muntu amukkiriza aleme okubula, naye afune obulamu obutaggwaawo. N’olwekyo mutegeere mu bujjuvu nti nze Mwana gw’endiga ow'Embaga y'Okuyitabo ow'enkomerero ow’ebiseera byonna (tewalibaawo mulala), era nsasulidde ebibi byonna. Setaani ayonoonye ensi okutandikira mu nnimiro y'omu Edeni, bwe yakema Adamu ne Kaawa okukkiriza ekibala kye ekivundu era n'akkiriza okumanya kwe okutaliiko mugaso, kubanga okumanya kwe kuleeta okufa eri bonna abakirya. Era olw'ensonga eno Katonda Omuyinza w'ebintu byonna awaddeyo endagaano y’emirembe eri ekitonde kye, ye nze, Yesu Kristo.

Kubanga eddoboozi ly’Abefeeso ligamba ki, Pawulo bwe yabawandiikira ku bikolwa byange ebyatuukirira (kubanga nagamba mu mukka gwange ogw’enkomerero ku musalaba nti, “Kiwedde”)! Naye kaakano mu Kristo Yesu, mmwe abaatanga baali wala, musemberezeddwa musaayi gwa Kristo.

Kubanga ye mirembe gyaffe, eyafuga byombiriri okuba kimu, era n’amenya ekisenge ekyali ekiziyiza wakati waffe. Wulira eddoboozi lyange. Nze mirembe gyammwe, kubanga amateeka gaasalira omusango ekibi mu mubiri; n'omubiri gwammwe n’omusaayi gwammwe byonooneddwa mu kibi, era ne byonoonebwa okuva mu Edeni. Nze, Yesu Kristo, mu ssaddaaka yange etangirira ku Kaluvaali, namenyawo ekisenge eky’omu makkati eky'okwawula omuyudaaya n’omunnaggwanga, ne mbafuula bombi omuntu omu omuggya, era ne mbatonda mu butuukirivu n'obutukuvu obw'amazima eri bonna abakkiriza emirembe nga banjatula nga Mukama waabwe eyazuukira!

Naye waliwo ebisingawo okuva mu Befeeso (nga kino tekimala kuba kirungi), n’olwekyo Abefeeso bagenda mu maaso: era n’aggyawo mu mubiri gwe ensonga y’obukyayi (okutegera amateeka ag’ebiragiro agali mu mateeka agawandiikiddwa) okusobola okufuula babiri omuntu omu omuggya mu ye, n'aleeta emirembe: n'okutabaganya bombi eri Katonda mu mubiri gumu okuyitira mu musalaba gwe, n'atta obukyayi. Wuliriza ggwe omuntu agaanye okuyitibwa kwange: Nkuyita kati mu ddakiika zino ezisembayo, era obudde busemberedde okuggwaawo! Nzikirize nga ssaddaaka yo ey’emirembe, kubanga nkuwa obulokozi n’okununulibwa ku bwereere okuva mu mibiri gyo emivundu, era n'obulamu obutaggwaawo mu nze, Kristo Yesu. Tolemwa: Buli kirabo kirungi, na buli kirabo kituukiridde kiva waggulu, era kikka okuva eri Kitange ow'ebitangaala, atalina nkyukakyuka, newankubadde ekisiikirize eky'okukyuka.

Wulira eddoboozi lyange era otegeere nti ekintu kyonna ekirungi mu bulamu bwo, ekintu kyonna eky’omuwendo n'empisa ennungi, kya Kitange era kyava eri Kitaffe ow'Ebitangaala olyoke ofune obulamu obujjuvu era ojje gyendi, Mukama wange Yesu, nga Omulokozi wo. Era olyoke omanye nti waliwo obulamu obutaggwaawo gy'oli singa okkiriza. Mwekyawa nnyo n’okutuuka ku ssa ly’okuwaayo buli kintu kye mwagala mu bulamu ekibadde kiweereddwa Kitange okumatiza okwegomba kwammwe okw'obuntu? Ebibi byammwe bikulu nnyo gye muli n’okutuuka ku ssa ly’okulonda okuvumirirwa okutaggwaawo ne Setaani okusinga obulungi bwa Katonda n’obuggya bw'obulamu mu nze, Kristo Yesu? Ebibi byammwe bikulu nnyo bwe bityo gye muli?

Nkuyita, ggwe omuntu ow'obutonde, era nkuyita mu mirembe n'obulamu obutaggwaawo, obulamu obutaliimu ndwadde na kibi, obulamu obutalimu ndwadde na mutima gumenyese, obulamu obutalimu kubonaabona nga toliraba nate abo b'oyagala nga babonaabona; kubanga ebintu eby'olubereberye bijja kuggwaawo mangu nnyo. Tewakyali budde bumatiza bibi byo. Kubanga bangi ku mmwe mukozesezza bubi ekisa kya Katonda ne mukkiriza obulimba bw'omubbi, ayagala okubabbako obulamu bwammwe mwennyini mulyoke mwegatte naye mu kibonerezo kye ekitaggwaawo. Tolemwa. Okunjoola nze, Yesu Omunazaaleeti (oyo eyawaayo obulamu bwe olw'ebibi byo n'obulokozi bwo), kwe kwegaana obulamu n'okulonda emirembe gyonna okufa okw'okubiri Setaani n'abamalayika be kwe baasalirwa omusango: Kubanga tebalina kisonyiwo, era tebaliwummula, era tebalina mirembe. Era ennyanja y’omuliro etegekeddwa Omulyolyomi n’abamalayika be.

Okugaana ssaddaaka yange kwe kwegaana obulamu obutaggwaawo n’okwegaana Katonda mu bujjuvu. N’olwekyo, tewalibaawo kusaasira, kisa, kwagala, kitangaala, newankubadde emirembe wabula okwejjusa, ekizikiza, n’okubonyaabonyezebwa kwokka we mulikungulira ebibala ebivundu eby’omubiri gwammwe emirembe gyonna. Tolaba ngeri kino gye kiri ekisirusiru? Nsasulidde ebibi byo mu musaayi gwange ogutangirira! Naye ogaana amakubo gange, newankubadde nkuyita kati era eri abasinga obungi omulundi ogw’enkomerero. Ogaana okwagala kwange, newankubadde omutima gwange gukyayulika ku lulwo ne ku maka go, era nandikulokodde okutuuka ku nkomerero singa weenenya n’oleka ebibi byo. Totegeera nti nnabonaabona ng'omwonoonyi asinga obubi mu ssaddaaka yange etangirira ndyoke nkuyimusize eri Katonda mu bulokozi? Kubanga nagulumizibwa ku musalaba okulokola emyoyo gyammwe n'okubatabaganya ne Kitange, era mbadde sirina kibi kyonna, kale mukkirize omusaayi gwange ogutalina kibi!

Kubanga Ebyawandiikibwa bigamba ki? Ddala wawulira nti Katonda bwe yayagala ensi, n’okutuusa n’okuwayo Omwana we eyazaalibwa omu yekka: buli amukkiriza aleme okubula, naye afune obulamu obutaggwaawo. Nkimanyi nti ebigambo bino bimanyiddwa gy’oli, kubanga n’abasanyusa bo abakukuumira mu busibe bwa Setaani bakozesezza bubi ebigambo byange mu kuvvoola. Naye wandikoze bulungi okusigala ng’osoma, kale nkwegayiridde wulira eddoboozi lyange! Kubanga Katonda teyatuma Mwana we mu nsi kusalira nsi musango: naye ensi erokolebwe okuyitira mu ye. Amukkiriza tasalirwa musango: naye atakkiriza asookedde dda okusalirwa omusango, kubanga takkiriza Linnya lya Mwana wa Katonda eyazaalibwa omu yekka.

Okwogera kwange tekuli lwatu kukimala? Ebigambo byange tebitegeerekeka mangu? Bw’onjoola nze, Mukama wange Yesu, Omwana gw’endiga gwa Katonda, ojja kwegatta bujja n’omulabe wange, n’abo abavunaanyizibwa ku kuyiwa omusaayi ogutalina musango: Kubanga okunjoola kwe kwegatta ku bujeemu bwa Setaani, kubanga Omulyolyomi yayonoona okuva ku lubereberye. Era kuno kwe kuvumirirwa, nti omusana gujje mu nsi, n’abantu ne baagala ekizikiza okusinga omusana, kubanga ebikolwa byabwe byali bibi. Bwe guli n’amazima agakukwatako, ggwe omuntu ow’obutonde, n’abo ku mmwe abandyegaanye omwoyo gwange ogw'obulamu, era ku lw’ebyo byonna nkyakuyita, newankubadde kuno kwe kuyita okw’enkomerero omusango nga tegunnatandika. Kubanga tewakyali budde. Ebibi byo bikulu nnyo n’okutuuka ku ssa ly’okumala emirembe gyonna mu kizikiza eky’ebweru n'ekibonerezo mu nnyanja ennene ey’omuliro eyategekerwa Omulyolyomi n’abamalayika be? Kubanga Katonda takwagala kibonerezo kino era yantuma nga ssaddaaka ye ey'emirembe okukutabaganya naye n'omusaayi gwange ogutangirira.

Wulira ebigambo byange okkirize n'emirembe gyange. Mmwe mwenna abawulira ebigambo bino, mumanye nti nze, Yesu Omunazaaleeti, mbayita; era sifaayo ku bibi ebibasiba kubanga mbyasasulidde byonna. Mutegeere nti okutwala akabonero k'ensolo tekirisoboka kusonyiyibwa, era ngezaako okubalokola mu kikemo kino (newankubadde akabonero tekannaba kutuuka); kubanga bangi bajja kufiirwa obulamu bwabwe mu kubonyaabonyezebwa olw’okugaana. Naye tegeera bino wammanga: Toyinza kugwa wansi nnyo ne nsiba okukutuukako; kyokka totwala kabonero. N'ab’amalala tebayinza kwegulumiza mu kibi okutuuka ku ssa eryo ne nsigala nga siyinza kubassa wansi mu ngeri ennyangu okubatuusa ku bulokozi n'okubateekako akabonero n'omwoyo gwange ndyoke mbagulumize! Kubanga nsasulidde ebibi byonna eby’ekika ky’omuntu mu biseera byonna, era sirinako kye nnaleka nga sikikoze. Waliwo ebibi bingi nnyo, naye nja kuyita abamu ku mmwe mu ngeri ey'enjawulo okusinziira ku bubi bwammwe. Mumanye nti nja kubasonyiwa ebibi byammwe byonna so si bino byokka, era nja kubanaaza mu bujjuvu mu musaayi gwange okuva mu bibi byammwe byonna.

Eri omutamiivu n'omukozesa w'ebiragalalagala, ngamba nti: Mukyukire gyendi kaakano mu kaseera ak'obutaggwaawo ak'okutegeera obulungi muwulire ebigambo byange, kubanga bw'omuwuliriza mu kukkiriza, ebigambo byange bijja kubaleetera omusana ogw'ekitiibwa ogw'obulokozi. Ebigambo byange bijja kusiba dayimooni ababaleetera okunywa n'okukozesa ebiragalalagala, newankubadde nga mwandyabafudde mikwano gyammwe. Mbawa okutegeera okw’ekyamagero musobole okusalawo kye mujja okuba nakyo emirembe gyonna: Obulamu obutaggwaawo nange, Yesu Kristo, mu kwegatta okutuukiridde ne Katonda yennyini? Oba okuvumirirwa okutaggwaawo mu muliro ogutazikira? Ekibi kye mukutte n’omululu mu mukono gwammwe, ye nno ennywa lyammwe n’obukozesa bw’ebiragalalagala, kikulu nnyo n’okutuuka ku ssa ly’okulonda okubula, n’okutuusa n'okuteeka omukisa gwammwe ku abo be mwagala ababagoberera mu kifo kyange?

Toli wansi nnyo ne nsiba kukuyimusa, era njagala nnyo okukusonyiwa, naye olina okwanukula eddoboozi lyange n’ova mu makubo go ng’owenenyezza. Yiwa omwenge gwo oleme kwefuula muto, ojje gyendi ng’owenenyezza, nange nja kukuwonya. Tewali kiseera kya kutaataaganya; tonjoola era todda mu butamiivu n’owerabira ebigambo byange, kubanga ebigambo byo byennyini eby’okugaana bijja kuwandiikibwa mu ggulu ng'omujulirwa akuluma ku lunaku lw’omusango, era toliwonye!

Ggwe omussi, nkugamba nti: Komekkereza amangu ddala ebikolwa byo eby’obussi n’obutemu ku abo abaakolebwa mu kifaananyi kya Katonda, era abantu banno, kubanga kino kibi kyamawaggali. Mazima ddala, era mazima g’amazima agatakyuka, mwetemudde mwekka buli bulamu bwe mwaggyeewo, era nga nze ndi awatali, tewali ddagala lya kibonerezo kyammwe ekijja. Naye n’okutta kwammwe kwasasulirwa mu bujjuvu mu ssaddaaka yange, kubanga saalekawo kintu kyonna nga sikikozeeko. Ndaze ekisa kyange mu bulamu bw’Omutume Pawulo omukulu ng’omujulirwa ow’okubazzaamu amaanyi, mmwe abassi.

Kubanga ne Sawulo yali alimbibwa era ng’alolekera okusalirwa omusango omunene ku kkubo ery’e Damasiko, gye nnamulokola okuva mu ye yennyini. Pawulo yayigganya, n’alumya, n’asiima era n’atta abantu b’Ekkanisa yange entukuvu! Kubanga ye kennyini abyatula mu Bikolwa by’Abatume: "Era kkubo lino nalyigganyanga okutuusa ku kufa, nga nsiba era nteeka mu makomera abasajja n’abakazi." Naye yakkiriza okusaba kwange okw’obulamu obutaggwaawo n’anfuula nze, Yesu Kristo, Mukama we era n’ampeereza n’obwesigwa ng’ekyokulabirako eri bonna eky’okwenenya, era nze namugulumiza nnyo olw’obuwulize bwe. Amakubo gammwe ag’obutemu nagasasulira bw’onoogeenenyako n’ojja gyendi ng’weenenyezza mu mazima, kubanga bw’okikola kati, nja kukwaniriza mu kisa n’okusaasira. Temulimbibwanga; tewali kiseera kya kunoonya.

Abo abali wansi ennyo, mbagamba nti: Nateeseteese n’ennyanjula y’okuva mu bulalu bwammwe n’okutabukatabuka, kubanga nabagabula mu kwegomba kwammwe okw’effujjo nga Abaruumi bwe kigamba: Olw’ensonga eyo Katonda n’abawaayo mu kwegomba okw’ensonyi: kubanga n’abakazi baabwe baakyusa enkola yaabwe ey’obutonde ne bakola ekitali kya butonde: Era n’abasajja nabo bwe batyo, ne baleka enkola ey’obutonde ey’omukazi, ne baakiraŋŋana mu kwegomba kwabwe, abasajja n’abasajja nga bakola ebitasaana, era nga bafuna mu bo empeera ey’okusobya kwabwe eyali esaana.

Muve mu butali bulongoofu bwammwe, muve mu malala gammwe agatalina mugaso, era mulekere awo okuvuma akabonero akaaweebwa Nuuwa na buli kitonde ekiramu, omusoke ogulabikira mu bire by’enkuba. Omusoke guliwo ensi yonna emanye nti Katonda taliddamu kuzikiriza nsi na mazzi, wadde ng’okusala omusango n’omuliro kuli kumpi. Mujje gyendi mwenenye, mukyuse amakubo gammwe era mukoowole erinnya lyange, munjatule nga Mukama okuva mu mutima ogujjudde okukkiriza mu kuzuukira kwange, nange nja kubaaniriza. Mwanguwe! Kubanga temulina kiseera kya kulowooza, kubanga okusala omusango kuli kumpi okuteebwa. Tewali kiseera kya kunoonya.

Muwulire ebigambo byange mujje gyendi, kubanga sisanyukira musango na kuzikirizibwa okujja, era ne Yakuwa bw’atyo, nedda, na Katonda Omuyinza w’ebintu byonna! Kubanga Adamu yatondebwa mu kwagala kwa Kitange n’obutuukirivu mu butuukirivu n’obutukuvu obw’amazima. Katonda yamulamuza obulamu obujjuvu nga tewali kimubulako. Setani n’alumba ekitonde n’ayonoona omubiri gwammwe ddala n’obutonde bwammwe ddala n’okutuuka lwe mukyama okuva mu lubuto, kubanga ekibi kiri mu musaayi gwammwe: Obulamu bw’omubiri buli mu musaayi. Naye Katonda, mu kusaasira kwe okungi, kubanga okusaasira kwe kubeerera emirembe gyonna, era Katonda Omuyinza w’ebintu byonna yawaayo nze, omwana we omu yekka, okuba ssaddaaka etuukiridde ejja okubalokola mu bujjuvu bwonna singa mujja gyendi nga mwenenyezza ebibi byammwe. Emirimu gya Setani mu bulamu bwammwe nja kugiggyaawo singa mujja gyendi. Mbasaba mujje, kubanga emikono gyange tegigenda kugololebwa ebbanga ddene!

t

Wasigadde akaseera katono nnyo, era ennaku ya Yakobo eri kumpi okubalukawo okwetooloola ensi yonna, era emitima gy’abantu gijja kubaggwaamu olw’obukambwe bw’ebyo ebijja. Setani ategese eby’omuzizo bingi by’ajja okuteebwa, kubanga okugaana okuyitibwa kwange kwe kulonda obwakabaka bwa Setani obw’okufa n’okuzikirira; nange nja kubaleka mu kulonda kwammwe, era nja kubawa ekizikiza n’ennaku bye mwagala ennyo, era mujja kulya ku bibala by’amakubo gammwe okuggyako nga mwenenyezza. Kubanga kino kye kiseera, kuno kwe kuyita okusembayo nga okusala omusango tekunnajja mangu. N’abaweereza bange n’abaminisita bange bakaabirira enkomerero z’ensi yonna mu kasikini kano, okuggyako nti bajja kukaabirira obulokozi bwammwe okutuusa lwe bajja okusitulibwa okunjija gyendi mu bire lwe ndijja okwaniriza omugole wange mu bbanga mu bbanga ttono, era kino kijja kubaawo nga tekisuubirwa, wadde nga mbabuulira kaakano nga tekinnabaawo. Olwo okuyita kwange okusembayo okw’ekisa n’okusaasira kujja kuba kuweddeyo. Era bwe ndisitula omugole wange okumuteeka mu kifo eky’obulamu, okuzikirira okw’amangu kujja kubalukawo okwetooloola ensi, era bangi ku mmwe abawulira ebigambo bino tebajja kuwona.

Abo abatattibwe kuzikirira kuno okw’amangu okugenda okuteebwa ku Babulooni Ekikyamu bajja kwesanga mu nsi gye bataalowoozangako n’akatono, ensi awalibaawo okufa okutaggwaawo n’okuzikirira, kubanga Setani ajja kuggyawo mu nkola buli kintu kyonna ekinkwatako, nze Yesu Kristo, era ajja kukikola awatali kusaasira. Walibaawo okubonyaabonyezebwa okwetooloola ensi n’obukambwe obw’effujjo, era okujja gyendi mu kiseera kino eky’okusala omusango n’okugezesebwa kijja kuba kizibu nnyo; naye buli alikoowoola erinnya lya Mukama alirokolebwa, era bajja kulina okugumiikiriza okutuusa ku nkomerero, era bangi bajja kuttibwako emitwe, era bangi bajja kufa nga babonyaabonyezebwa nnyo.

Naye ne mu kiseera ekyo, Katonda ajja kusangula amaziga gonna mu maaso gaabwe, kubanga Yokaana Omubikkulirwa yababuulirako nti: Bano be bavudde mu kibonyoobonyo ekinene, ne booleza engoye zaabwe, ne bazeezizza mu musaayi gw’Omwana gw’endiga. Kyebava babeera mu maaso g’entebe ya Katonda, era bamuweereza emisana n’ekiro mu Yeekaalu ye: n’oyo atuula ku ntebe alituula mu bo. Tebaliddamu kulumwa njala, so tebaliddamu kulumwa nnyonta, so n’enjuba teribookya, so n’okwokya kwonna. Kubanga Omwana gw’endiga ali wakati mu ntebe alibaliisa, era alibakulembera okubatuusa ku nsulo z’amazzi ezoolamu: era Katonda alibasiimulako amaziga gonna mu maaso gaabwe.

Nze, nze nzennyini, Kristo Yesu, Mukama eyazuukira n’alinnya mu ggulu, era Masiya ow’amazima: Mbakoowoola, era essuubi lyange n’okusuubira kwange kwe kuba nti mujja kukkiriza ekiweebwayo kyange eky’emirembe mujje mu bulokozi bwange mulyoke mubeere nange emirembe gyonna mu kifuba kya Kitange! Muleme kugaana kuyitibwa kwange kwennyini, kubanga sirikoowoola bbanga ddene nate, era sirikoowoola mu kisa n’okusaasira mu ngeri eno nate, kubanga obudde buweddewo (wadde ng’ekisa n’okusaasira biri nange emirembe gyonna). Mbasaba mujje gyendi ndyoke mbalokole mu busekendee buno obusembayo obw’ekisa. Kubanga temuliiko njawulo na abo abakkiriza ku ntandikwa y’emirembe gyange egy’ekisa, era obulokozi bwange n’empeera yange bijja kuba byammwe singa muwulira eddoboozi lyange kaakano. Kaakano ke kiseera eky’obulokozi! Mwenenye mujje gyendi mu bwetoowaze. Munfuule Mukama wammwe era mumanye nti ndi mulamu emirembe gyonna, kubanga Katonda yanzuukiza mu bafu obutaddamu kufa. Singa mukkiriza okusaba kwange, mujja kuba muwangudde, era mujja kuweebwa empeera z’omuwanguzi:

Laba, nnyimiridde ku luggi, era nkonkona: omuntu yenna bw’awulira eddoboozi lyange, n’aggulawo oluggi, ndiyingira gy’ali, era ndisangira naye, naye nange. Omuwanguzi, ndimuwa okutuula nange ku ntebe yange, nga nange bwe nnawangula, ne ntuuzibwa ne Kitange ku ntebe ye. Soma bino emirundi n’emirundi, kubanga nkuwa omukisa ogusembayo ogw’okulokolebwa n’ekisa. Kwokka togganga kuyitibwa kwange, kubanga tewakyali kiseera kya bintu ng’ebyo. Eri abangi ku mmwe, okunjeema kujja kuba kwa lubeerera, so temujja kuddamu kuwona newakubadde okwewala omusango ogujja, kubanga okuzikirira okw’amangu kubatuuseeko. Era Omwoyo n’Omugole bagamba nti, jjangu. N’oyo awulira agambe nti, Jjangu. N’oyo alumwa ennyonta, ajje. Na buli muntu ayagala, atwale amazzi ag’obulamu buwa.

Era ekisembayo: Oyo atali mutuukirivu, abeere ng’akyali mutuukirivu: n’oyo ali effulannenge, abeere ng’akyali effulannenge: n’oyo ali omutuukirivu, abeere ng’akyali mutuukirivu: n’oyo ali omutukuvu, abeere ng’akyali mutukuvu. Era laba, njija mangu, n’empeera yange eri nange, okuwa buli muntu ng’omulimu gwe bwe guliba. Kale kituusizibwe bwe kityo.

Nkoowoola aboonoonyi mu mwoyo gw’okwagala kwange.

Ndi Mukama wa bakama era Kabaka wa bakabaka.

Ndi Mwana gw’endiga gwa Katonda aggyawo ebibi by’ensi.

Ndi wano okubalokola.

Ndi mwana gw’endiga Omuyitirivu ow’enkomerero.

Ndi kiweebwayo kyammwe eky’emirembe.

Mbakoowoola kaakano mu kiseera kino ekisembayo.

Mbawa obulokozi n’okununulibwa.

Mbakoowoola kaakano okwenenya n’okuggibwako ebibi.

Mbayita okujja mu mirembe n’obulamu obutaggwaawo.

Nkyabakoowoola.

Ndi mulamu emirembe n’emirembe. Onooyagala okwegatta nange?
