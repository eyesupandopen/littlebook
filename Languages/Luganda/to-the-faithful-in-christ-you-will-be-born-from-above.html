Eri Abakkiriza mu Kristo: Mujja Kuzaalibwa Okuva Olw’Eggulu: Akaseera k’Okuzzaalibwa Kwammwe Okujjuvu Okuggya Katuuse

Muwulire, Muwulire, ebigambo bya Yeshua HaMashiach era mukotamye emitima gyammwe eri eddoboozi ly'Ebigambo byange Ebitukuvu era mutegeere ebiseera bye mulimu, baagalwa bange, kubanga tewali n'omu ku mmwe eyali abaddemu ebiseera bino, wadde ebyo ebigenda okubaawo kati ebitegekeddwa okusalirwa omusango n'okuzikirizibwa amangu ago ku Babulooni ow’Ekyama, ye Amerika eno, okusumululwa mu kumbeera era mu ngeri etasuubirwa kati ku kuvuga kw’akagombe ako akategekeddwa okuvuga mu kumbeera era mu ngeri etasuubirwa kati. Weewaawo, ku kuvuga kw’oluyoogaano olw’amaanyi kubanga ebiseera bituuse baagalwa bange, era ebiseera bino bibatuuseeko nga bwe kyalangirirwa era ne kisuubizibwa. Nja kubakuŋŋaanyiza gyendi mu kumbeera era mu ngeri etasuubirwa kati, okunsisinkana mu bbanga nga bwe kyasuubizibwa, kyenvudde mbalagira nti: MUBEERE BEETEGEFU!

Mwetegeke Omugole wange, kubanga essaawa y’okununulibwa kwammwe okujjuvu ebaleetedde n’amaanyi amangi n’okusuubira kutuuse! N’olwekyo, mujje mu bujjuvu eri Kristo Yesu era mweteendere mu bujjuvu eri Mukama wammwe ow’Obutuukirivu Ataggwaawo, nti mazima ddala, nkwoleze ddala ebibi byammwe byonna era mbafuule abawangula ebintu byonna mu buwanguzi obw’ekitiibwa mu nsi eno ejjudde ebibi, nga nange bwe nnawangula ebintu byonna eby’ensi eno ejjudde ebibi mu buwanguzi obw’ekitiibwa.

Era ng’obulumi buno obw’enkomerero obw’okuzaala bweyongera, mutegeere nti bunaatera okutuukirira, weewaawo ku kiseera ekyo ekituufu ekya Kairos ekya Harpazo lwe kinaakomekkerezebwa omwana omutukuvu bw'anaazaalibwa omulamu mu ggulu ery’omu ggulu, n’azaalibwa era n’azaalibwa omulamu emirembe gyonna era atayonooneka emirembe n’emirembe mu ntebe y’obwakabaka eya Adonai-YHVH, nga bwe kyalangirirwa era ne kisuubizibwa edda mu Bigambo by’omubaka wange ow’amaanyi era omuweereza wange, weewaawo, oyo Ebigambo bino gwe byaweebwa era ne bitumibwa mu mikono gye ng’ekirangiriro era olw’okwetegeka n’okuteekateeka abawala bange ab’amagezi, mmwe mwenna mulyoke mweyawule ku bawala abo abasirusiru abanyooma era abasuula ebigambo byange, era nsobole okubagatta mu bujjuvu n’empeera yammwe enjjuvu ebategekeddwa mu bujjuvu mu mikono gyange, ebategekeddwa okubaweebwa okuva gye ndi okutuukiriza ekisuubizo ky’omutemwa eri ekyaganyulwa.

Wabula, essaawa emu n’ensolo esembedde era etuuse era waliwo amaloboozi mangi agatatukuvu agakeka n’okwogera eby’obusiru eby’amaanyi! Mazima ddala, amaloboozi mangi amakyamu kaakano gakaaba nnyo, nga gawa ebigambo ebikyamu mu ddungu eri abo abalina amatu agasiwa. Mazima ddala Yezebbeeri bano bamanyidde eddungu ng’endogoyi enkazi ey’omu nsiko bw’eba mu kiseera ky’okwaka, ng’eyita abayise ab’emizimu, era mu kiseera kye ani ayinza okumuyitako obuyisi n’atabuukira mu nsuwa zino ezivuunise, ezisalirwa omusango ogutalwawo?

Naye era bwe batyo emyoyo gya Yezebbeeri abalagula ebigambo eby’obulimba eby’obulimba n’obulimba, weewaawo, ebigambo eby’ekifumo abasinga obungi bye basinga okwagala mu kifo ky’Ebigambo byange ebirongoofu eby’Obutuukirivu ebyaweebwa okuva ku mutima gwa Adonai-YHVH yennyini, mu maaso ga Kristo Yesu, era ye Yeshua HaMashiach! Naye era, okwawukana kuno kuleke kubeewo kubanga kireetebwa ekitala ky’omwoyo gwange, kyokka temukkiriza kugwa ku luuyi olwa kkono olw’ekitala kyange kubanga muligabana ku misango gyabwe, nga mwagaanye okweyawula mu bujjuvu ku kiragiro kyange!

MULABUDDWA MU BUJJUVU KATI!

Kyenvudde mbalagira mwenna, muve mu bo, abantu bange, MULEME okugabana ku misango gyabwe, era nnabalabula emirundi mingi nate nate kati okufuluma n’okweyawula mu bujjuvu ku Babulooni ow’Ekyama, kubanga omusango gw’omwenzi oyo omukulu kati gutegekeddwa okubwatuka okwetooloola ensi yonna ng’essaawa emu n’ensolo etegekeddwa okusumululwa n’obukambwe n’obusungu obw’amaanyi: Okuggyako, weewaawo, era ku kiseera ekyo ekituufu ekyateekebwawo mu mirembe egitali na nkomerero Adonai-YHVH, NZE ASINGA OBUNENE! N’olwekyo, temuzirika mu kusuubira kwammwe kubanga abasinga obungi mu mmwe mukooye mu kusuubira kwammwe, wadde nga nnabalagira okuba n’essanyu, era TEMUZIRIKA!

Kale mbuuza ekibuuzo kino ekisirusiru: Mugole ki atuula ng’annakuwadde ku mabbali g’ekkubo ng’alinga eyasuulibwawo, naddala ng’Owuwe asembera?

Naye bwe kityo bwe kiri, abasinga obungi mu mmwe abatuula ku mabbali g’ekkubo kati nga munakuwavu era nga mitima minene, muggyeewo okussa ebirowoozo byammwe byonna ku Kristo Yesu ne mubiteeka ku bigambo by’abo abakeka n’okwogera era abatayogera ku lwa Yeshua HaMashiach! Bwe kityo kubanga abasinga obungi mu mmwe abali okumpi nange mu nkolagana, era abali okumpi n’okununulibwa kwammwe okujjuvu mufuuse abalina endowooza ebbiri olw’ebigambo ebikyamu eby’abasirusiru abalimba.

Laba! Muwulire Ebigambo byange era mukkirize!

Laba! Mugenda kununulibwa n’okuwambibwa nga muli balamu okuva ku nsi era ebiseera bya KATI!

N’olwekyo, mwetegeke, era mwetegeke mu bujjuvu kubanga munaatera okulaba era ddala muliraba amaaso ag’ekitangaala ag’Omukama wammwe ow’Obutuukirivu Ataggwaawo, oyo eyabagula n’omuwendo ogutabalika era ogununula emirembe gyonna, weewaawo, omusaayi gwange omutali kibi era ogw’omuwendo ogutagambika era okununulibwa kwammwe okujjuvu kwa KATI!

Laba! Ensi ejja kuddugala mangu ago ku lunaku lwe mutasuubira! Era okuva mu kizikiza ekyo ekikutte, ekitangaala eky’emirembe n’emirembe eky’ekitiibwa kyange ekitaggwaawo kijja kwasa okuyita mu ggulu eddiguze era ensi erimanya era n’etegeera nti Kristo Yesu alabise eri abakkiriza be, era tekijja kugaanebwa, wadde nga, bajja kubunya ebigambo eby’obusiru ebingi nga bagamba nti abagwira batuuse ne bababba. Weewaawo, obulimba obuliggibwawo mu musango ogw’obukambwe era omukakali, Seera!

O' singa Omugole wange alowooza ng'ow'ebugwanjuba yakkiriza okusanyuka mu kiseera kino eky'okununulibwa okw'obutuukirivu okubatuuseeko!

(Sanyuka Omugole wange, kubanga Owuwo asembera, nga bwe kyasuubizibwa era ne kirangirirwa!)

O' singa Omugole wange alowooza ng'ow'ebugwanjuba yakkiriza okutegeera nti ekiseera ky'okununulibwa kwo okujjuvu kituuse, era tewali n'omu ku mmwe aliwona ekiseera kino nga tanunuddwa!

(Sanyuka Omugole wange, kubanga Owuwo asembera, nga bwe kyasuubizibwa era ne kirangirirwa!)

O' singa Omugole wange alowooza ng'ow'ebugwanjuba yakkiriza okutegeera nti tewali kiyinza kuyimiriza ekigenda mu maaso!

(Sanyuka Omugole wange, kubanga Owuwo asembera, nga bwe kyasuubizibwa era ne kirangirirwa!)

O' singa Omugole wange alowooza ng'ow'ebugwanjuba yakkiriza okusanyuka kubanga essaawa y'okununulibwa kwo n'okuwonyezebwa kwo okujjuvu ekutuuseeko, bw'onookkiriza okugondera Ebigambo byange n'eddoboozi lyange. Seera.

(Sanyuka Omugole wange, kubanga Owuwo asembera, nga bwe kyasuubizibwa era ne kirangirirwa!)

Naye, abasinga obungi mu mmwe mwessa omwoyo ku kiseera ekyo ekituufu eky’okutuukirira, ne mwezza essanyu lyammwe erijjuvu mu kusuubira okw’obutuukirivu, ne mukkiriza Heylel Ben Shachar okubabba okusuubira kwammwe okw’obutuukirivu olw’amaloboozi amakyamu amangi n’ebigambo by’abo abakeka n’okwogera n’okulangirira ebigambo eby’ekifumo eby’obutali butuukirivu! Seera.

Era kino kubanga abasinga obungi mu mmwe musinga kwagala maloboozi manji era agali mu kazambi era agazaawa akazambi agaweebwa bannabbi ab’obulimba abatumibwa okwonoona abawala bange ab’amagezi abakyanoonya okusigala mu bawala abasirusiru, wadde nga nnoonya okubawula mu bujjuvu ku bigambo byabwe eby’obulimba ebiweereddwa okubasuula n’okwawula ebirowoozo byammwe ku ddoboozi lyange eddongoofu n’Ebigambo byange ebirongoofu eby’Omukama wammwe ow’Obutuukirivu Ataggwaawo, era nsembera okubakuŋŋaanya Omugole wange n’abagenyi baffe, temuzirika, era mwesige Ebigambo byange Baagalwa bange era muwummule mu kwagala kwange kwe nnina gye muli kubanga omugugu gwange mwangu era ekikoligo kyange kyangu.

Mujje gyendi mu bujjuvu baagalwa bange, mujje gyendi mu bujjuvu awatali kutya era n'emitima gyammwe gyonna mu kusuubira okujjuvu era nga mugaziye amaaso gammwe gonna, nga temulina kye mukweka. Laba! Ndaba mu bujjuvu ebyama by’emitima gyammwe n’ebirowoozo byammwe, era ntegeera enkola y’ebirowoozo byammwe n’okutya kwammwe okungi okutali kwa magezi, era kyenvudde mbagamba nate nti temutya!

Era ani gwe mwandyatidde, baagalwa bange? Mazima ddala, mbakutte mwenna mu bukyakafu mu bibatu byange, nti tewali kintu na kimu mu bwakabaka bw’abo abagwa, (nedda, ne Heylel Ben Shachar yennyini, ogusota ogwo omunene ogw’edda), ayinza okusumulula ku kukwata kwange okw’emirembe n’emirembe kwe mbakuumiramu! Kubanga tewali muntu n’omu mu bitonde bya Adonai-YHVH byonna ayinza okubaggya mu mikono gyange egy’amaanyi n’obuyinza obw’emirembe n’emirembe.

Mazima ddala, tewali ayinza kubayimirirawo bwe musigala nga muli bawulize eri Omukama wammwe ow’Obutuukirivu Ataggwaawo, era kyenvudde mbabuuza nate nti, ani gwe mwandyatidde? NGAMBA ANI!

Mutegeere ddala gye ndi, baagalwa bange, kubanga nze mwana gw’endiga gwa Adonai-YHVH, weewaawo, omwana gw’endiga ataliiko kibi, ataliiko bbala wadde erinnyogo. Era mu maanyi gange n'okwagala kwange okutayonooneka gye muli, baagalwa bange abeesigwa, nnawaayo obulamu bwange mu kusaddaaka okw'obulumi olw'ebibi byammwe, bwe ntyo, nnunule abantu mu bujjuvu okuva mu mukono gwa Heylel Ben Shachar, omulangira oyo omutono eyalemwa. Era mu ngeri ye ey'ekisolo, Heylel Ben Shachar yawaayo omwana gw'endiga owa Adonai-YHVH mu bukyayi bwe obw'ekisolo era obutali bwa magezi era obw'okwegomba ebintu byonna ebirungi. Era mu kukola bw’atyo Adamu yanunulibwa mu bujjuvu n’omusaayi gwange ogutaliiko kibi, era Adonai-YHVH yanzuukiza mu bafu, emirembe gyonna nga siriddayo kufa kubanga tekyasoboka kufa kunkwata.

Naye bwe kityo bwe kiri, nti nneeyanjula eri Adonai-YHVH nga Kabona Asinga Obukulu emirembe gyonna ng’Enkola ya Merukizeddeeki bw’eri era Adonai-YHVH yakiriza nnyo ssaddaaka yange n’okutuusa lwe yakyusa Yesu Omunazaaleeti n’amufuula omwoyo oguwa obulamu ogulibakola obuggya mwenna mu kumbeera era mu ngeri etasuubirwa kati ku luyoogaano olwo olw’amaanyi n’okuvuga kw’akagombe ako ak’amaanyi akategekeddwa okuvuga mu kumbeera era mu ngeri etasuubirwa kati!

Gye ndibakolera omuntu omu omuggya, awatali Muyudaaya newakubadde Omunnamawanga: awatali muddu newakubadde ow'eddembe: awatali musajja newakubadde mukazi: kubanga mwenna muli bumu mu Kristo Yesu, era ye Yeshua HaMashiach! Era okuva bwe muli ba Kristo Yesu, kale muli zzadde lya Ibulayimu, era muli basika olw'ekisuubizo era abasumululwa mu butuukirivu emirembe gyonna okuva mu mikono gy'abalabe bammwe mu Kristo Yesu Omukama wammwe ow'emirembe gyonna, Kabaka, Kabona Asinga Obukulu, era Masiya, kyenvudde okununulibwa kwammwe kujjuvu, kujjuvu, kujjuvu ddala!

Weewaawo, okununulibwa kwammwe kujjuvu nnyo n’okutuusa lwe mutaba na kye mwetaga era mwenna mwagulibwa n’omuwendo ogutagambika ogw’emirembe n’emirembe! Kale mbuuza nti, ani ku mmwe ayinza okuwaayo omusaayi ogutaliiko kibi okutangirira ebibi bya Adamu, eyayonoona ng’akyali mutaliiko kibi? Naye Kitammwe ali mu ggulu, ye Adonai-YHVH, NZE ASINGA OBUNENE, yawaayo Omwana we omu yekka, omulundi gumu, bonna era emirembe gyonna okutangirira ebibi byonna eby’abantu!

Kyemuva mugulibwa n'omuwendo, kale mugulumize Adonai-YHVH baagalwa bange kubanga amaze okuwa ekiragiro okubakuŋŋaanya mu bujjuvu ku kiseera ekyo ekituufu ddala, era kyenvudde mbalagira nti: MUSANYUKE!

Naye, abasinga obungi mu mmwe temutegeera bunene bwa kikolwa kino, ne mulowooza nti okununulibwa kwammwe kye kintu kyokka eky’omugaso ekirina okukolebwa. Naye bwe kityo bwe kiri, nti abasinga obungi mu mmwe mwessa omwoyo ku kutuukirira kw'okukuŋŋaanyizibwa kwammwe kwokka gye muliyimirira nga mununuddwa mu bujjuvu era nga mukyusiddwa mu bujjuvu emirembe gyonna mu ggulu ery'omu ggulu akagombe ako lwe kalivuga, kubanga kanaatera okuvuga!

Era nga n’ebigambo bino biweereddwa ng’ekikubiriza, eddoboozi ly’Abaebbulaniya liyimiridde nga lijulira okulaga obujulizi ku basinga obungi mu mmwe: Kubanga bwe kyandibagwanidde okuba abayigiriza olw'ebiseera ebyayitawo, naye mwetaaga nate okuyigirizibwa eby'olubereberye eby'ekigambo kya Katonda; era mufuuse ng'abo abeetaaga amata, so si emmere enkalubo.


N’olwekyo, nkirigamba nate, nga bwe kyawandiikibwa, amagambo gange galyoke ganywezebwe:

Era newankubadde nga ebigambo bino biweereddwa ng’eky'okukubiriza, eddoboozi ly'Abebbulaniya lijulira okubanenya bangi ku mmwe: Kubanga bwe mwasaanye okubeera abayigiriza olw'ebiro ebyayita, naye ate mwetaaga omuntu abayigirize eby'olubereberye eby'ekigambo kya Katonda; era mufuuse ng'abo abeetaaga amata, so si mmere nkalu.

Bangi ku mmwe mwerabidde eby'olubereberye eby'obunnabbi bwa Katonda n'olwekyo mulagirwa okukwata ebigambo byange eby'ekisuubizo mu birowoozo byammwe era mutunuulire yekka Kristo Yesu, ye Yeshua HaMashiach, n'omutima omugumu n'ebirowoozo ebyetegefu. Nti mukyuse ebirowoozo n'emitima gyammwe era mukwate ebigambo byange eby'ekisuubizo n'obunyiikivu mu birowoozo n'emitima gyammwe bye nawa okuyita mu kisibo kyange ekitono abalangiridde n'okuteekateeka abantu bange eri akatabo kano akatono akayitibwa Enjiri ey’Emirembe n’Emirembe ebikkuddwa mu kitundu, ke nzigudde mu mukono gw'omubaka wange n'abaweereza bange, nga bwe kyasuubizibwa Yokaana Omubikkulirwa emyaka mingi emabega, newankubadde amawulire ag'okubwatuka omusanvu gaasibibwa ne gaziyizibwa mu Okubikkulirwa essuula 10. Naye, ne galangirirwa nate ne ganywezebwa ng'Enjiri ey’Emirembe n’Emirembe mu Okubikkulirwa essuula 14. Naye, bangi ku mmwe be njagala, musiruwalira ne mugaana ebigambo byange ne mukulembeza abasomesa b'eddiini ab'obulimba, ab'obulimba bwabwe bujja kukomezebwa mu musango ogw'amangu. N’olwekyo, muve wakati mu bo, mweyawule mu bintu byonna muleme kugabana ku musango gwabwe.

Era kino kibaddewo kubanga amawulire ag'okubwatuka omusanvu ge gannyonnyola ddala ebyo ebyalangirirwa mu Apokalupsis ya Yesu Kristo, ekitabo ekyo eky'edda ekyamaanyi ekyakwekebwa: Okubikkulirwa kwa Yesu Kristo; ekyawanikibwa n'ekigendererwa mu butuufu nga tekijjudde. N’olwekyo, abantu bajje eri Yesu Kristo Omulamuzi Omutuukirivu era Omuwolereza, nsobole okulamula obulamu bwammwe mu butuukirivu, nga nggyawo ebibi byammwe, singa munaabeera bawulize mu kukkiriza n'okwagala okugondera amateeka agaaweebwa eddoboozi lyange.

N’olwekyo, mutegeere nti okubwatuka omusanvu kubikkuddwa, kutandisiddwa, era kati kukola, era tekusobola kuyimirizibwa wadde okuziyizibwa. Era tekisoboka kukyusa nnyonnyoka z'okubwatuka omusanvu ezivudde ku ntebe ya Adonai-YHVH, ezaaweebwa mu ddoboozi lya El Shaddai era ne Yeshua HaMashiach eyasikira byonna okuva mu mikono gya Adonai-YHVH eg'amaanyi, bye nja okugabana n'abawanguzi bange abeesigwa.

Kale mutegeere nti Nze Mpologoma ey'omu kika kya Yuda kati eyimiridde ku lwa bantu bange! Era mu butuukirivu njija kulamula n'okulwana!

Naye nsooke nkumaanyize Omugole wange omulwanyi ne mmwaniriza gyendi, ye buli muntu eyeeteeseteese eri Omugole we agenda okutuuka, era ndaba ettaala zammwe zibumbujja ze mwateekateeka n'obuwulize ne muzijjuza n'omuliro gwange ku mabbali g'ekkubo mu kulindirira okw'obutuukirivu bwe nsembera!

Kale mutegeere bulungi kye kitegeeza kino, abaagalwa bange, kubanga tewakyali kulwa, era nze, Kristo Yesu, ndangirira mu muntu w'omubaka wange omukulu n'omuweereza wange, nti obudde tebujja kubeerawo nate!

Kubanga okuyita kwange kulangiriddwa okwetooloola ensi yonna mu mulembe gwonna ogw'ekisa, era bangi beeyanirizza, era bangi bajja kununulibwa mu bujjuvu mu ngeri ey'amangu era etasuubirwa. Era abawanguzi bange bajja kujjula ebitongole ebyalekebwa mu ggulu bya Heylel Ben Shachar, n'olwekyo nja kubikkula emitwe gino omusanvu, be basitaani omusanvu, abakulemberwa Heylel Ben Shachar. Newankubadde, mu musango gwabwe, baggyibwako obuntu bwonna ne bamanyibwa ng'abasitaani, era abaseeraafi bano ababi bajja kusibibwa ne basuulibwa mu nnimi z'omuliro ogw'ebweru, n'ekibonerezo kyabwe kijja kubeera ekisinga obukakali ku byonna eby'abo abasalirwa omusango, abajja kuweebwa mu nnimi z'omuliro ogw'ekibonerezo, abasibiddwa emirembe n'emirembe okufuna okuwoolera okujjuvu n'omusango gw'omuliro emirembe n'emirembe gye bataayinze kuwona, wadde okuwummulako akaseera.

Wabula, nja kubabikkula kubanga banoonya okwekweka, kubanga bonna beeyita abo be batali, so tewali kyama kyonna ekitajja kubikkulibwa.

Waliwo Heylel Ben Shachar, ggomasi oyo omukulu omujeemu ow'edda eyatandika olutalo mu ggulu n'alumba obusika bwa Adamu, ne kivaako ye n'ekitundu kimu kya kusatu ekya bamalayika be abaamugoberera mu bujeemu bwabwe obw'olubereberye mu biseera Elohim bwe yatonda eggulu n'ensi okugwa emirembe n'emirembe mu nnimi z'omuliro ez'ekibonerezo eky'olubeerera.

Waliwo Yequn: ono ye yayonoona abaana ba Bamalayika Abatukuvu, n'abassa ku lukalu, n'abayonoona okuyita mu bawala b'abantu;

Waliwo Asbeel: ono yawa amagezi amabi abaana ba Bamalayika Abatukuvu, n'abayonoona, ne bayonoona emibiri gyabwe n'abawala b'abantu;

Waliwo Gadreel: ono ye yalaga ebiwundu byonna eby'okutta abaana b'abantu, n'ayonoona Eva, Era yalaga eby'okulwanyisa eby'okutta eri abaana b'abantu, engabo n'eky'omukifuba, n'ekitala eky'okutta, n'eby'okulwanyisa byonna eby'okutta eri abaana b'abantu. Era okuva mu mukono gwe byavaayo ne bigenda ku abo abali ku lukalu okuva mu kiseera ekyo n'emirembe n'emirembe.

Waliwo Penemue; ono yalaga abaana b'abantu ebikaawa n'ebiwomerera n'abalaga ebyama byonna eby'amagezi gaabwe. Yayigiriza abantu omuzannyo gw'okuwandiisa bwino n'empapula, era okuyita mu kino bangi baayonooneka, okuva ku mirembe n'emirembe, okutuuka leero. Kubanga abantu tebaatondebwa lwa kino, nti bakakase okukkiriza kwabwe bwe batyo, ne kalamu ne bwino.
