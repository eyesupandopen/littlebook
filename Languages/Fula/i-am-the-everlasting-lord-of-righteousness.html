Seedeeji ɗiɗi: Isiraayiila
Ko Welɗi e ko Haɗɗi
Siilo 01, 2025

Min woni Joomiraawo Poofɗo Goongaajo!

Ndaaree! Nananee Konnguɗi Iisaa Almasiihu, pooɗtinee miijooji mon, sunninee kadi ɓerɗe mon e noppi mon e nder ɗooftaare Konnguɗi Yeesu HaMasiiha, mi rokkan annduɓe hikma, haa o anndudo e mon annda kulol Joomiraawo, on annda kadi diwnaade yeeso Joomiraawo Konuuji, sabu noon Alla mawɗo, Min Mawɗo on, waɗiri mi.

Hol an, an neɗɗo tagoowo ngam haɓaade Alla, Min Mawɗo on, kanko woni Kiiɗo jamaanuuji, kanko woni Joomiraawo Ruuhuuji? Hol an neɗɗo tagoowo ngam mawninkinaade e mantiro e dow Iisaa Nasarankeejo, mo woni kadi Almasiihu Iisaa, kanko mo innde mum ɓuri inɗe kala mawnude, kanko mo Alla, Min Mawɗo on, renndidi e mum jooɗorde laamu, kanko mo toownaa dow kala huunde so wonaa Alla e hoore mum haa ayɓe am laatoo teɓteɓel koyɗe am? E laamu am e njiimaandi am alaa fof timmoode, mi hollan Laamu ngoo Baaba annoore en, haa Joomiraawo Alla laatoo kala e kala!

E ko taganooɗo oo waawi wiyde tagoowo oo, hol ko waɗi a tagirii mi noon? E mbulmbuldi ndi waɗiraa haa laatii haako teddungal waawi wiyde tafɗo ndi, hol ko waɗi a waɗirii mi noon? Hol ko waɗi a rokki mi teddungal ngal fof? Miɗo yiɗi laataade goɗɗum, miɗo yiɗi heɓde teddungal maɓɓe! E on tafɗo waasataa halkude tagu bonngu hono nguu, ngu ummotoongu e dow junngo mum haa tawa nguɗo yiɗi halkude kanko e taguuji makko goɗɗi; waɗi noon ko hono ɗum waawataake waɗeede?

Kono noon laabi meereeji e fijirde bonɓe ɓe ngummii e dow tagoowo maɓɓe ɓee, ɓe acci kuule maɓɓe e golle maɓɓe laaɓɗe tedduɗe ɗe keɓnoo ngam timminde e nder peewal, hay sifaaji ɗi Alla e hoore mum rokki ñalnde o tagi ɓiɓɓe Alla. Hay malaa'ika'en laaɓɓe ɓe Alla tagnoo ko adii nde o tagata leydi ndi, ɗum winndaa e deftere Ayuuba nde o tagi leydi ndii: Koode subaka njimdunoo, ɓiɓɓe Alla fof njimii e weltaare! Ɓe njimii goonga e weltaare sabu kaawisaaji e kaawniiɗi ɗi ɓe njiynoo e juuɗe Alla nde o tagi leydi ndii. Ko noon kadi malaa'ika'en bonɓe ɓee, hay ɓiɓɓe Alla ɓee, puɗɗorinoo tagireede, ɓe laatii mawɓe e doole ngam sifaaji maɓɓe, Alla huɓɓirii ɓe teddungal, o waɗi ɓe ɓuraade e hikma ngam ɓe njiɗa Alla e nder ɗooftaare timmunde e daande makko, goonga ɓe keɓii darnde teddungal adannde ndi ɓe tagiraa, haa ɓe ngummii e yiɗde ɓerɗe maɓɓe, bonannde yiytaa e maɓɓe.

Ko noon Seytaaneeji ɗii ngummii gila e fuɗɗoode e dow tagoowo maɓɓe, hay ɓeen wonnooɓe sara jooɗorde laamu Alla, hay mo jooɗorde mum woni hannde sara mum ko 10 000 laabi 10 000, kono Alla ko jom baawɗe, o alaa haaju e wasiyaaji. Kono noon winndaama e dow ndee mbelu mawndu boɗeeru, jogiindu ko'e jeeɗiɗi, e kacce sappo, e kuronne jeeɗiɗi e dow ko'e mum, wonde laaci mum ɓoftii tataɓal koode asamaan, o werlii ɗe e leydi: ko noon kadi Seytaaneeji ɗii e hay tataɓal malaa'ika'en rewɓe e ummital maɓɓe njaɓɓaa e leydi, hay e ñalɗi ɗi bonannde malaa'ika'en yani gila e asamaan e nder hakke e ummital, ɓe laatii niɓɓere timmunde caggal hakkeeji maɓɓe, ko noon ɓe ngoniri haa hannde.

Ko noon mboddi ndii ɓadorii yumma woodɓe fof e nder Aljanna Aadama gila e duuɓi keewɗi, o ƴetti Awa ngam ñaamde huunde nde Alla haɗnoo ɓe ñaamde, sabu ñalnde ɓe ñaami lekki ganndal moƴƴere e bonannde, hay e oon ñalngu gooto maayata. Hay noon, Awa ƴetti e mboddi ndii no o feliraa e fenaande mum o ñaami, o rokki gorko makko ndee ɓiɓɓere haɗaande, Aadama ñaami e yiɗde mum, e maayde ɓe maayi no fodaama, neɗɗaagu woni e les maayde gila ndeen sabu hakke yiytaaɗo e ƴiiƴam mum. Sabu Aadama bonii haa abada e nder hakke, ko ngam ndee sabaabu Ɓii Neɗɗo feeñi haa mi halka golle seytaane, mi addana Aadama bonɗo oo kisal, sabu ko miin woni Aadama cakkitiiɗo mo Alla waɗi ruuhu hokkoowo nguurndam. Goonga, goonga, mi tagitan kala jaɓɗo noddaango am kisal laatoo tagu kesu, goongaajo, sabu ko ɗum Aadi am Keso oo addi.

Ko noon Aadama e Awa njoɗɗinaa e Aljanna Aadama, ɓe mbaylaa gila e sifaa maɓɓe jalboowo moƴƴo mo ɓe tagiraa e mawnikinaare timmunde e juuɗe Alla e hoore mum, e les njiimaandi hakke e bonannde ndi addata ɓe ndilli e leydi e nder lepte maɓɓe. E ñalɗi Yared, Riyooji ɗii njippii e dow tulde Hermon, hay ɓeen ɓe njiɗde ɓerɗe maɓɓe nanngi, sabu ɓe njiɗnoo ɓiɓɓe rewɓe yimɓe, hay ɓe njiɗnoo ɓiɓɓe e maɓɓe, e nder njiɗɗe ɓerɗe maɓɓe ɗe Seytaaneeji ɗii njiylorii ɓe, ɓe kaɓɓondiri e kuddiiji, hay e hunayeere maɓɓe laaɓaani ndee, nde laatii kuddi ndu alaa timmoode, ndu timmintee e nder ndiyam mawɗam ɗam yiite wulngo, ɗo ngilngu maɓɓe maayataa.

Ko noon ɓiɓɓe Alla naati e ɓiɓɓe rewɓe yimɓe, ɓe njiɗondiri e rewɓe e kala ɓe cuɓii. Rewɓe wonɓe njahii e yiɗde mum en, hay ɓe ndokki hoore maɓɓe fof hakkeeji bonɓe ɓee (ɓe keɓata ñaawoore mum en), wonɓe nannaa e doole, Riyooji ɗii torri ɓe e njiɗɗe maɓɓe bonɗe. Ko noon aduna oo wonnoo, hay aduna halkaaɗo e ilam mawɗam waɗnoonɗam e ñalɗi Nuuhu, halkaaɗo sabu bonanndeeji mawɗi ɗi riyooji ɗii mbaɗi, ɓe mbaɗanoo goonga ngam halkude neɗɗaagu haa neɗɗaagu heɓa leydi ndi bonɓe ɓee njiɗnoo, ɓe kaɓɓaa hannde e ñaawoore.

Sabu ɓe cuɓii halkere maɓɓe e torra poofɗo ngam heɓde ko ɓe njiɗi, ko wonaa kaɓɓe ɓe kaɓɓi, ko ɗum en ɓe mbaɗi, ɓe ndoondi haa jooni ngam heɓde e doole. Kono, golle maɓɓe e peeje maɓɓe fof maa laato meere, so ɓe keɓataa kadi duuɓi maɓɓe kaŋŋe ɗi mbiyno-ɗen, kono ñaawoore tiiɗnde, saɗtunde, poofnde, ko ɗum ɓe keɓata. Sabu goonga mo waylataa: Leydi ndii ko Joomiraawo e heewngo mum, miin woni Joomiraawo poofɗo goongaajo, soytitiiɗo leydi ndii e ƴiiƴam am kisnoojam, miɗo ara ngam heɓde e doole ko woni kañum am, hol baawɗo daraade e doole am mawɗe?

No winndiraa e dow am nii maa waɗee: Ndaaree, Mbaroodi leñol Yahuuda, ɗaɗol Daawuuda, foolii ngam udditde deftere ndee, e udditde kodde jeeɗiɗi ɗee. Ko ɗum waɗi, faamee no feewi wonde ko miin fotata udditde kodde ɗee sabu leydi ndii ko kañum am! E no winndiraa e dow am nii maa waɗee sabu miɗo foti ƴettude deftere ndee, e udditde kodde mum: sabu mi hirsanooma, mi soytitii kala arɓe e kisal poofngal to Alla e ƴiiƴam am laaɓɗam, mi soytitii on e kala leñol, e ɗemngal, e yimɓe, e leydi: alaa fof gooto baawɗo ɓoftude hay gooto e mon e juuɗe am poofɗe! Ko ɗum waɗi, hol mo pot-ɗon hulde?

Ko noon rewɓe ɓe ɓiɓɓe Alla (hay riyooji bonɗi) ƴetti, ɓe ndewi, ɓe ndimi maɓɓe yimɓe mawɓe bonɓe haa nder ɓerɗe maɓɓe. E mawneeki maɓɓe e doole maɓɓe ɓe koolii, ɓe manti, kono e ñaawoore Alla e maɓɓe sabu bonannde nde ɓe bonni neɗɗaagu, o haɓɓi ruuhuuji maɓɓe e leydi ngam lepte sabu ɓe mbaɗaama e leydi e nanndiral e ɓalli hakkeeji, ruuhuuji maɓɓe ko poofɗi, keɓaaɗi e baabiraaɓe maɓɓe ɓe njiɗɗe maɓɓe kaɓɓaa ngam timminde. Ko noon Nefiliim en maayiri e ñaawooje maɓɓe gila e Alla e hoore mum, ruuhuuji maɓɓe ceertinaa e maayɗeele maɓɓe, ɓe njokki, ɓe kaɓɓaa e leydi e lepte. Ko ɗum waɗi, ɓe ndolli, ɓe ndomɗi, kono ɓe mbaawaa ñaamde walla yara ndiyam. Nde ɓe njoginoo ɓalli mawɗi, doole, ɗi ɓe kuutorinoo ngam laamaade hoɗɓe e leydi, ɓe laatii baasɓe so wonaa nde ɓe mbaawi heɓde hoɗorde nde ɓe hoɗata hono mborosaaji seytaaneeji, sabu ko noon ɓe laatorii. Faamee, gila e ñalɗi Nuuhu sabu naatgol Nuuhu, sappo e jeenayi e maɓɓe ndesaa e luggeendi, kono ɓe ɓooyataa yaltineede e leydi e ñaawoore.

Kono, ko noon ummital bonɓe ɓee woniri gila e ñalnde bonannde yiytaa e ɓerɗe maɓɓe. Ndeen fijirde e haqqille hebbinii ɓerɗe bonɓe ɓee e ummital maɓɓe; wondude e neɗɗo tagoowo, mo naati e maɓɓe e salagol yaltude e ngonka mum bonka, hakkeeji: ɓe fof ɓe njiɗi laataade goɗɗum ko wonaa ko Alla anniyii e timmal goonga. E nokku ɗo ɓe ndoftata tagoowo maɓɓe, mo anndinta timmal maɓɓe timmungal, weltaare, welo-welo, e jam; mo rokkata ɓe haa heewa e yiɗde mum e tagu mum, ɓe njiɗi, ɓe njokki njiɗɗe ɓerɗe maɓɓe, ɓe njiɗi aduna maɓɓe tawa Alla alaa, ɗo ɓe mbaawi wuurdude njiɗɗe ɓerɗe maɓɓe, ɓe ngeloo e ummital maɓɓe e weltaare hakke e dow tagu Alla. Hay njiɗɗe hakkeeji e bonɗe ɗe ɓe keɓataa, ɓe timminataa.

Ko noon neɗɗo tagoowo naatiri e ummital maɓɓe, so wonaa neɗɗo tagoowo jibinaama e anndude e niɓɓere sifaaji mum goonga e mawnikinaare mum potnde, nde Alla fodani ɗoftooɓe, neɗɗo tagoowo rewi e yiɗde mum laabi bonɓe ɓee, hay so tawii bonɓe ɓee ndarniima e halkude Aadama, ko ɗum Aadama anndaa e njiɗɗe mum, hay e nder ɗum o haɓɓiri hoore mum e hoolaare meere e fenaanɗe maɓɓe keewɗe, bonɗe. Hay e laabi fenaanɗe, heewɓe maa njah to maayo yiite, nde tawnoo ndeen lepte wonaano ngam maɓɓe.

Kono, ndee ummital jokkii haa jooni e nder duuɓi, hay ummital waɗeteengal e wolde iwdi hakkunde taguuji goongaaji Alla e bonanndeeji bonɗi ɗi waylude tagu bonɓe mbaɗi, ɓe ngonaa allaaji hay so ɓe mbeltiima. Ko noon jamaanuuji mon ngoni, an neɗɗo, ummotooɗo e salaare bonnde e laabi tagoowo mum, hay e anndude naatde e ɓeen ndarniiɓe fof e maayɗeele mon e halkere mon poofnde nde ɓe njiɗi.

Ko noon mi hollirta ñalngu nguu, miin Yeesu HaMasiiha, mi hollirta ñalngu Joomiraawo arii, ina fotnoo feerde e dow leydi ndii e ñaawoore mawnde, bonnde, e musibbaaji keewɗi ndarnaama hannde ngam yaltineede e leydi, hay musibbaaji e ñaawooje cifaaɗe gila e jamaanu, ɗe waktuuji mum en njottii. Ko ɗum waɗi, faamee hol ko mi woni, miin waɗoowo ndeeɗoo wolde e doole e jooɗorde laamu Alla e hoore mum, sabu ko miin fotata ƴettude deftere ndee, mi uddita nde, mi uddita kodde mayre, mi waɗan ɗum tigi rigi, hol baawɗo haɗde junngo am doole?!

Faam, an neɗɗo tagoowo, hay an salotooɗo tagoowo maa, wonde jamaanuuji maa njottii. E yiɗde heewnde, e yurmeende, e yurmeende rokkaama e ɗiiɗoo jamaanuuji ñaawoore, sabu kala noddoowo innde Iisaa Nasarankeejo haa timmoode mum maa his, teddeendi njeenaari mum fotaani nanndireede e torraaji ɗi o muñata ngam innde am. Faam, an neɗɗo tagoowo, wonde mi ardoto 144 000 doole am ngam hisnude Yaaquuba, mi suuɗan kadi heddiiɓe doole Isiraayiila ɓe njokkata nguurndam e Laamu am duuɓi ujunnaaje e nokku mo Alla feewnani e ladde, hay ngam ɓe laatoo barkinɓe, ɓe keewa, ɓe ngeloo e mawnude leydi ndii e laamu am duuɓi ujunnaaje ɗo ɓooyataa ara e leydi ngam timminde fodoore, aadi, e wasiya.

Ko noon wonirta neɗɗo tagoowo, wonde ɓe njaɓan njawdi Alla wonde kisnoowo maɓɓe walla ɓe cuɓii ñaawoore maɓɓe poofnde e nder ndiyam yiite wulngo, sabu alaa cuɓal goɗngal, alaa kadi nokku goɗɗo ɗo ɓe njahata, sabu Alla jaɓataa fof bonannde foola, halka tagu mum, ñalɗi maɓɓe ummital e yiɗde mum en ina fotnoo timmude.

Miin woni njawdi Alla, rokkaandi ngam hakkeeji aduna.

Miin woni Mbaroodi leñol Yahuuda, e nder peewal miɗo ara ngam ñaawoyde e haɓde.

Miin woni mo njawdi Alla e Mbaroodi leñol Yahuuda kawri fof e sifaaji am poofɗi e laamu am hono Laamɗo laamɓe e Joomiraawo jomiraaɓe, hol woni baawɗo daraade e Yeesu HaMasiiha, laaɓɗo Joomiraawo, hay mo Alla neldi ngam waɗde ñaawoore poofnde e dow bonɓe e kala cuɓiiɓe rewrude e laabi maɓɓe.

Ko noon miɗo wasiyoo goonɗinɓe e Almasiihu ɓee yo ɓe keddo e goonɗinal maɓɓe haa jooni, sabu ñalawma ndimaagu mon arii, omo ɓadii sanne. Njehee tan e Joomiraaɗo mon duumiiɗo Gonɗo Goongaajo on, sabu tooñooɓe ɓee kebbinii aduna oo e keneeli mawɗi, fenaande e pewje ngam laamninde hay cuɓaaɓe ɓee so tawii ɗum ina waawi waɗde. Sabu ɗuum, paamee wonde annabaaɓe fenaande heewɓe nelaama, ɓe nganndinta ko e laabi Balaama, ɓe ruuhuuji Yezebel njogiima, ɓe kaaldata e ruuhuuji liddiiɓe Almasiihu, ɓe njannginta Iisaa goɗɗo, dariiɗo e fenaande e nokku Iisaa Almasiihu, hono Yahuuda Masiih e nder ɓerɗe laamnaaɓe ɓee. Kala mo laamnaa e laamnagal ngal fof maa heɓto ñaawoore mum so o salii tuubde e laabi makko nde Joomiraaɗo makko haaldata e makko e hunukooje goonɗinɓe Almasiihu. Goonga, goonga, won annabaaɓe fenaande laaɓɓe, laamnaaɓe, ɓe goonga waawi yettaade e dow ɗemɗe giɗli, kono ko goonga tan rimɗinta ɓe, ko goonga ceniiɗo kañum e annabaakuuji maɓɓe fenaande ngam ɓe mbaawa tuubde e tigi rigi.

Woodi kadi annabaaɓe mbaɗooɓe annabaaku ngam kaalis, e jawdi tunndi, e ngam heɓde teddungal e hakkillaaji e ngam weltoraade jawdi aduna oo bonndi. Hay so tawii ko hombo woni annabiijo fenaande oo, calanee ɗum, calanee konnguɗi makko, hay so tawii ko konnguɗi toownaaɓe peeñnirnoo ngam laamninde hay cuɓaaɓe ɓee so tawii ina waawi. Hol ko waɗi on ndogata njaha, on ngarta, Ekklessiya am goonɗinɗo? Mi yamiraani on, mi wasiyaaki on, mi semmbinaani on yo on laato foolooɓe am? Hay so tawii ko ɓeen foolɓe kala fannu e laamu añɓe hono no mi fooliri nii? Sabu ɗuum, aree e Almasiihu Iisaa, mi tabitina e nder ɓerɗe mon annabaaɓe am goonga, sabu annabaaɓe am goonga ina kaalda e ɓernde Joomiraaɗo mum en, hono Konngol ngol waɗi ɓanndu. Sabu ɗuum, Almasiihu Iisaa waawaa luulndaade binndi ceniiɗi, sabu ko miin woni ɗum!

E ɓeen toownotooɓe ɓe cikkata ina ndarii e am kono ina laamnaa e fenaande: Hol ko waɗi on ndogata njaha, on ngarta, ina njiɗa goɗɗo weltina noppi mon, ina njiɗa hay boknaaɓe ɓe cooyata, ɓe cukkata, ɓe kaalata e mon ko njiɗ-ɗon nande ngam wuurtinde giɗli bonɗi e nder ɓerɗe mon bonɗe nde ndogoton e tuubugol njahdon e juuɗe laamniiɓe nelaaɓe ngam laamninde. On ndogata njaha, on ngarta sabu on njaɓaani daande Yahuuda Masiih, sabu on calii goonga ka rimɗintunoo on, sabu on calii janngude e am e jogagol Konngol Allah ko ngol woni tolno mon ɓurɗo toowde e gooto. Sabu ɗuum, miɗo wasiyoo kala cikkiiɗo ina waɗa annabaaku ngam Almasiihu Iisaa, hay so tawii ko kala cikkiiɗo ina haalda e innde Allah, Mawɗo Miin Wonɗo on, yo reeno koye mum en woto njanngu e niɓre duumiinde e ñaawoore duumiinde, sabu kala nannduɗo e toownaaɓe e nder laabi mum en, o salii tuubde, innde mum maa momte e deftere nguurndam so o salii laɓɓingol ngol o accete so o jokkii e bakkaatuuji makko, sabu waktu alaa.

Ko noon miɗo wasiyoo e miɗo rentina ɓeen ɓe njiɗ-mi ngam ɓe ndutta laamnagal tiiɗngal ngal feewnaaka e maɓɓe so ɓe njahii e ɗooftaade Joomiraaɗo mum en, ko noon miɗo wasiyoo e miɗo rentina ɓeen ɓe njiɗ-mi ngam woto ɓe laamnire laamnagal mawngal ngal jooni ina heɓtina aduna oo. Miɗo wasiyoo kala nanɗo konnguɗi ɗii yo acca bonannde, acca boknaaɓe ɓe cooyata, ɓe cukkata, itta nokkuuji mon e taabal Yezebel, caloo njoɓdi Balaama. Paamee wonde kala mo annabaakuuji mum luulndii binndi ceniiɗi, ina haalda fenaande e naafikaare. Paamee wonde hay Allah, Mawɗo Miin Wonɗo on, hay Yahuuda Masiih (hono Almasiihu Iisaa) waawaa luulndaade binndi ceniiɗi. Sabu ɗuum, ɓeen kaaldooɓe e binndi ceniiɗi ina kaalda e Allah Mawɗo Miin Wonɗo on, ina kaalda kadi e Yahuuda Masiih, hono Almasiihu Iisaa.

Paamee no feewi hol ko miin woni e hol ko Allah tagiri mi. Ko miin woni Konngol Allah ngol waɗi ɓanndu, ngol hoɗi e mon, giɗaaɓe am, mi waawaa luulndaade hoore am, mi fenataa, sabu mi alaa bakkaat, mi heddoto mi alaa bakkaat haa abada. Paamee Baaba Annooraaji mo alaa waylo-waylo, alaa ɗowdi, hay so tawii ko kañum kaaldata e mon! Paamee wonde Adonay Allah waawaa luulndaade hoore mum sabu Adonay Allah waawaa fende. Kono, hol ko waɗi on njippinta noppi mon e nder majjere e ɓeen rewooɓe e jamma? On njahraton e jeeno, giɗaaɓe am, tawa ko yo on laato goonɗinɓe, ɗoftooɓe Joomiraaɗo gooto, hono Kisiyanke mon duumiiɗo, hono garoowo ngam haɓde goonɗinɓe e Almasiihu e nder asamaanuuji e kala saha?

Sabu ɗuum, paamee, wonde dame Jahannama foolataa goonɗinɓe e Almasiihu, hono Ekklessiya am goonga, sabu ko noon winndanoo, fodaama gila ko ɓooyi, ko noon haa jooni winndaa, fodaama e jamaanu hannde oo, sabu konnguɗi Allah e Yahuuda Masiih ko konnguɗi laaɓɗi no cardi nii, laɓɓinaaɗi e ngaska leydi, laɓɓinaaɗi laabi jeeɗiɗi. Eey, toownaaɓe ɓee alaa doole e goonɗinɓe e Almasiihu, mi waɗa goonɗinɓe am yo njaɓɓu kala fannu e laamu añɓe, hay njaɓɓu ɓeen njiɗooɓe darɗinde ko darɗintaake.

Ko miin woni Almasiihu Iisaa, hol baawɗo daraade e doole am e semmbe am ɗe keɓ-mi e Allah e hoore mum!

Paamee wonde miin e Baaba am ko en gootum e dental e anniya, miɗo waɗa sahaa kala ko Baaba am yiɗi! Sabu ɗuum, hay gooto waawaa daraade e am, alaa hay tagoodi e nder toownaaɓe ɓee baawndi foolde mi, hay e peeje, hay e doole, hay e keewal, hay e karallaagal mum en ngal alaa nafoore! Ko miin woni mo tabitini foolgol maɓɓe duumingol e Kalvaari nde kaal-mi konnguɗi ɗii, timmi. Paamee wonde on nanndinaama e Almasiihu Iisaa, mi waɗa on yo on ndaro e semmbe am e doole am! Sabu ɗuum, hol baawɗo haɓde e mon, giɗaaɓe am? Hol mo pot-ɗon hulde? Nanee konnguɗi am, sukaaɓe am, paamee wonde mi accataa on, mi woppataa on e juuɗe añɓe mon, sabu on foolii ɓe, on ɗoftiima jarribooji e ndeeñɗam keewɗam baɗaaɗam e juuɗe am kisinooje!

Hol e mon potɗo suuɗaade no kulooɓe e nder cuuɗi mon? Hol e mon potɗo deƴƴude e yeeso fenaande maɓɓe bonnde? Hol e mon potɗo ɗoftaade mbaroodi ndi alaa ñiiƴe ndi ñaawoore mum ɓooyataa? Nanee wullaango Mbaroodi leñol Yahuuda dariindi ngam haɓde e mon, paamee anniyaaji am e ɗiiɗoo sahaaji cakkitiiɗi hade caɗeele Yakuba arde! Hol ko waɗi on kuli añɓe mon foolaaɓe? Njehee e am e goonɗinal, mi hokkata on konnguɗi ɗi kaaloton e maɓɓe ngam haɓɓude ɓe, riiwde ɓe, deƴƴinde tooñooɓe mon ɓe njiɗatnoo haɓɓude on e ñaawoore maɓɓe e fenaande maɓɓe ndi njiɗata laamninde bipoole am.

Sabu ɗuum, miɗo yamira on yo on ngoonɗin Joomiraaɗo mon duumiiɗo, calanee Alla Gonɗo Goongaajo duumiiɗo on yo semmbine on e nder yeeso Joomiraaɗo mon, Almasiihu Iisaa! Njaltiree goonɗinal mawngal e Yahuuda Masiih, hono Iisaa Nazaret, sabu mi semmbinii on ngam on njaɓɓa kala fannu e laamu añɓe. Sabu ɗuum, paamee, hay malaa'ikaaji toownaaɓe ɓee ina ngondi e koyɗe am e foolgol timmungal, so tawii on kawrii e Seytaane e hoore mum, ko jaabaade tan pot-ɗon no Mika'el jaabirinoo nde o haɓatnoo e ɓanndu Muusaa, o wiyi e goonɗinal doole, Joomiraaɗo hure ma, Seytaane! Mi huri, mi haɓɓa kala toownaaɗo yiɗɗo lorlaade on walla darɗinde on e waɗde anniya on nelaaɗo, so on njokkii e yahde e goonɗinal nde kaaloton konnguɗi ɗi mi hokkata on e goonɗinal. Sabu ɗuum, ngoonɗinee e nder ɓerɗe mon, mbiyaa ndeeɗoo tufnde yo itte! Nde werlee e maayo e doole am mawɗe. Hol to ngonɗon ɓeen yahnooɓe e dow ndiyam no Piyeer nii tawa gite mum en ina njahdi e am? Hol to ngonɗon ɓeen jogiiɓe cuusal ngam daraade, mbiya goonga e yeeso caɗeele mawɗe? Eey! Hol e mon laatoto Yosuwa e Kaleb am? Daree, njaltinee e yeeso Joomiraaɗo mon yamirɗo on yo on ɗofto e golle!

Sabu ɗuum, paamee doole ɗe pot-ɗon yahde e mum, goonɗinɓe am e Almasiihu, kam e gardiiɗo am ina gardii haa goonɗinɓe e Almasiihu njetta, e oon sahaa gardiiɗo alaa ngam gardaade toownaaɓe ɓee. Nde njahraton e asamaanuuji e dow ballal, Ekklessiya am, ɓanndu am, hay debbo am; toownaaɓe ɓee maa ndimɗe timme alaa gardiiɗo e ñaawoore e Babilon sirlu, ɓooyataa, maarde maa uddite, buubaaji maa puɗɗe, e nder waktu, konkooje tikkere Allah maa njuute. Sabu ñalawma Joomiraaɗo arii e dow leydi, jamaanuuji caɗeele Yakuba njettiima, hol baawɗo daraade?

Sabu ɗuum, maa feccere sakkitiinde fuɗɗoo e dow ballal. Ɓeen yamiraaɓe kisal duumingal maa noddu innde Iisaa Nazaret haa laakara, e ɓeen Isra'ilnaaɓe jaɓɓuɓe Yahuuda Masiih ko Masiih maɓɓe goonga maa njetta e ladde, cuuɗee e tikkere Allah e hareeji ñalawma mawɗo oo e ndimoondi, e daabaaji, e annabiijo fenaande. E jam e kisal, ɓe ndeenetee e lorra, ɓe ñammintee, ɓe toppitee ɗo ɓe cuuɗaa, ɓe tawaaka.

Ko miin woni Iisaa Almasiihu, sahaa kala ina nodda goonɗinɓe e Almasiihu ina ɓada e jokkondiral kisal e Joomiraaɗo mum en e ndimaagu mum en, goonga, goonga, ina toppitii ruuhuuji mum en.

Ko miin woni Yahuuda Masiih, miɗo nodda Yakuba yo jaɓɓo mi ko mi Masiih mum goonga, sabu alaa Masiih goɗɗo mo Allah nelata.

Ko miin woni mo Joomiraaɗo e Mbaroodi leñol Yahuuda kawri haa abada, sabu ko miin woni Joomiraaɗo mon e ndimaagu mon, e goonga mi ari ngam ñaaawde e haɓde, hol baawɗo daraade e doole am!

Ko miin woni balloowo, semmbinoowo goonɗinɓe e Almasiihu, haa anniyaaji Allah e Yahuuda Masiih nguura e nder nguurndam maɓɓe e golle maɓɓe, haa ɓe njaɓɓa kala fannu e laamu añɓe so tawii ina haani!

Ko miin ñaaawata kala neɗɗo cikkiiɗo ina haalda e innde am ko annabaaɓe am ceniiɗi, e annabaaɓe am goonga, ruuhu annabaaku ina hoɗi e maɓɓe, ina kaalda e gootum, haa teeŋti e Joomiraaɗo mum en hokkuɗo ɓe konnguɗi ɗi kaalata, sabu ruuhu annabaaku ko seedaade Iisaa. Annabaaɓe am goonga maa keɓ njoɓdi duumiindi ndokkaandi annabaaɓe goonɗinɓe, goonga, mbaɗooɓe konnguɗi Joomiraaɗo mum en, ɓe ndokketee teddungal mawngal e golle mawɗe nde mi laamninta ɓe, mi laamna ɓe laamɓe e almuɓɓe ngam gollanaade Laamɗo maɓɓe Laamɓe e Joomiraaɗo Joomiraaɓe!

Ko miin waɗata annabaaɓe fenaande fof yo njanngu e deƴƴere timmunde e nder ñaawoore maɓɓe, ɓe njoɓa njoɓdi tiiɗndi sabu ɓe laamnii goonɗinɓe, ɓe naati e murto toownaaɓe, ɓe cikki ko huunde famɗunde haalde fenaande e naafikaare, ɓe cikki ko Konngol ngol waɗi ɓanndu rokki ɓe konnguɗi maɓɓe fenaande, sabu goonga ko miin woni ɗum! Kala kaalɗo fenaande e oon laawol maa heɓ ñaawoore duumiinde, majjere, e ndeeñɗam so o salii tuubde e laabi makko bonɗi. Ɓeen caliiɓe tuubde, njokkii e mum en, maa keɓ feccere mum en e ndeen maayo yiite ɓurngo wulde, ɓurngo wulde, ɗo ngilngu mum en maayataa.

Sabu ɗuum, daree, woto dille, goonɗinɓe am ɓe njiɗ-mi, ɓe mi rimɗinta e dow ballal! Paamee wonde mi waɗa on yo on poola timme so on keddii e ɗooftaade daande am, e ɓeen foolɓe, miɗo yamira on yo on njokku e golle ɗe ndokku-mi on yo on mbaɗ, reenoo koye mon e laaɓal timmungal, e sahaa kala ina njogii tuubugol ngam mi laɓɓina on timme e nder jinni am duumiiɗi.

Ko miin, kam e Allah, Mawɗo Miin Wonɗo on, peeñnini konnguɗi Linjiila duumiiɗo e nelaaɗo amen e maccuɗo amen, e nelaaɗo amen e maccuɗo amen, konnguɗi Linjiila duumiiɗo peeñnaama ngam ɗooftaade kala mo nelaa! Konnguɗi Linjiila duumiiɗo maa tawa gite, noppi, e ɓerɗe kala mo nelaa! Selah!

Miɗo ara e mon, giɗaaɓe am, e dow ballal, ndeen ko noon, njoɓee ɓerɗe mon fof e Almasiihu Iisaa Joomiraaɗo mon. Selah!

Ndaaree! Miɗo ara e mon, giɗaaɓe am, mi taga on e goonga e cenidoore goonga, ko miin ɓornata on laaɓal timmungal, mi artira on tagoodi laaɓndi kadi, ndi ɓurata teddude e nde Aadama taganoo e fuɗɗoode. Sabu ɗuum, toowne gite mon! Sabu mi ari e waktu mo cikkataano, hay waktu mo alaa sekondereeji keewɗi hakkunde nde ndimaagu mon fodanooɗo timmata e jamaanu hannde oo. On nanii? Buuba sakkitiika ina fadi fuɗde! Selah!
