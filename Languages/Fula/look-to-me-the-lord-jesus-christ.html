Seedeeji Ɗiɗi: Eklesiya
E nder Golle Yuhanna Jaltinoowo
28 mars 2024

Njeeɗee kam, Joomiraawo Iisaa Almasiihu!

Sukaaɓe am, udditee gite mon, njeeɗee kam, Joomiraawo Iisaa Almasiihu. Finnee e ɗoyngol, ngartee e am; ngartee e am, sabu juuɗe am ina peŋaa faade e mooɗon, onon cuɓaaɓe am. Hono seeɗa on ngonndata e am, sabu waktu oo yettiima e hojomaaji sakkitorɗi, kadi waktuuji yurmeende ɗii ɓooyataa timmude, mi addana ɗum timmoode kadi. Finnee e ɗoyngol, mi helicinan on gite mon e mbileewuuji aduna oo. Mbooyee, ngaree sabu gacce mon e salaare mon arde e am, nganndon miɗo nodda on e oo hojoma sakkitorɗo. Miɗo nodda on kadi faade e am, giɗo mon gadano, hay so tawii on naatinii woɗɓe e nder ɓerɗe mon.

Doggee ɓe fof, sabu ko ɓe fenaaɓe; ee, ko buubi nder comci baali, onon kadi on ɗaaniima e mbaɗɗudi maɓɓe bonndi ndii. Woto nanngee, sukkaaɓe am, kono finnee, ngenndee kam, sabu maandeeji am ngoodanii e dow kammuuji, miɗo ara no gujjo nii e jemma. Ko min tawata so mi artii ngam Eklesiya am? So tawii mi tawii goonga? Ee, eklesiya Laodisiya, onon ko on mofɓe, miɗo yiɗi kaari on nguli walla on peewi. Ngartanee kam kadi, mi laɓɓinan on e ɗoyngol mon, mi welnan on barmantaaji ɗi bonɓe ɓee mbaɗi on. Mi alaa yiɗde tuutde on e hunuko am, ngaree kadi e am, mi jaɓɓoto on. Nganndon yiɗde am e mon ndee meedaa maayde, ko ndee semmbe heewnde, meedaa wayleede.

Alaa goɗɗo so wonaa miin baawɗo hisnude on, alaa goɗɗo so wonaa miin baawɗo sellinde on, sellinde on, alaa goɗɗo so wonaa miin baawɗo reenude on, rokke on nguurndam duumotooɗam, alaa goɗɗo so wonaa miin baawɗo hisnude on e gacce mon, sabu alaa innde woɗnde rokkaande ɗo worɓe mbaawi hisnireede. Ko miin woni jikke mon duumotooɗo, mo tagi on kesum e nder jibinannde am hesere nde mi suɓi e dow mon. Nganndon onon ko on sukaaɓe Baaba am, on ngoodanii ndonu mawnungu sabu koolaare mon. Kono, hay so tawii mi ñaawoyan golle mon ngam njoddi walla mbuuteendi, on njawtii e bonannde faade e nguurndam duumotooɗam sabu goonga mon e am e Joomiraagal am duumotoongal. Henee! Mi waɗii on laamɓe e teddungal yeeso Baaba am, sabu miin ko mi tedduɗo haa abada e mbaadi Melkisedek.

Hol oo baawɗo haɓde e mon? Sabu haɓde e miñiraaɓe am ko haɓde e am, miin kadi mi yoɓtoto, wiyi Joomiraawo mawɗo duumotooɗo, jom teddungal, ummoraade e dow! Kala ko woni e dow kammu e e leydi rokkaama miin e juuɗe am, nganndon miɗo jogii doole dowdowɗe, alaa goɗɗo mawɗo e am so wonaa Baaba am, mo ɓamti kam dow kala. Hol oo baawɗo huleede? So torra e tampere ngaddanii on ɓeydaade semmbe e am? So wonaa so doole mon timmii, miin miɗo woni sara mon ngam semmbinde on e nder mbaadi mon ndii?

Ndaranee semmbe, keewee doole e am! Hol ko waɗi on cikki konnguɗi am? Kono, koolnee kam, kaalee konnguɗi ɗi mi rokkata on, sabu konnguɗi ɗii ko doole, semmbe so on kaalii ɗi e koolaare. Kaalee e koolaare e doole, njaɓanee geɗe tooke ɗee e innde Iisaa Almasiihu, innde am mawnde dow kala. Hono seeɗa on njiyrata kam e gite mon, kadi wonde mon e dow kammu ina yiɗaa no feewi e am e Baaba am, ee, hay e Yesuwa e Yahweh. Onon ko on coftal ɓurngal mawnude e Baaba am, on ngalaa njoɓdi. Sabu hol ɗo ƴiiƴam ɗam alaa gaace rokketee ngam gacce mon so wonaa e am?

Ee, onon bonɓe jooɗiiɓe, njaŋtooɓe, cikkotooɓe on keɓii nasaraaku mawngu. Mi bonnan on peeje mon bonɗe, on mbaawataa waɗde so wonaa ko Baaba am yamiri on mbaawi waɗde ngam yahrude yeeso peeje makko. Onon ko on tooke, tooke, on ndartinaama. Ee, neɗɗo tago, hol ko waɗi on cuɓotoo laabi bonɗi ɗi bonɓe ɓee njuɓɓinta on? So welooji gacce ɗii poti seernude haa abada e teddungal Alla duumotooɗo? Hol ɗo njiyraton nguurndam yaasi am? Hol oo hisnata on so wonaa oo mo ngonɗon salaade? So yurmeende am e yurmeende am ndartinaani junngo gaño?

Kala ko moƴƴi ko njogiiɗon ko ummorii e Baaba annoore, mo alaa waylo walla ɗowdi waylo. Kono, alaa, on saliima yurmeende am, on kuutorii yurmeende am ngam weltaade e wuurde e nder bonanndeeji mon. Henee! Mbonni mawnungu nanngoyan on. Ardiiɓe mon njaɓii on, ɓe njiɗi bonnude on, ɓe njaɓii konuuji boɗeeji naatde. Mbonni e bonannde mawnde ngaroyan e mon e dow laylaytol, ɗum wonta no mi nanngirta debbo am e nder laabi mon bonɗi ɗii. Ndeen mbonni ngaddoyan on, sabu hol oo foonduɗo foondu foondu nder gite foondu? Alaa, pitagol seertugol ina heɓii, ɗum wonta so mi itti jogaade am, on mbaawataa daɗde.

Ndeen ɓerɗe mon njoloyan e mon, doole mon ndogoya so on njiyii e gite mon tagoore nde ɓooyataa yaltude e nder leydi faade e mon. Mi udditan luggere, sabu miɗo jogii doole e mayre; sabu miɗo jogii coktirɗe maayde e jahannama, alaa baawɗo nanngude ɗe e am. Kaari on paamtuno on ngalaa ko njogiiɗon e dow leydi, kala ko woni ko dokkal yurmeende e yurmeende, sabu yiɗde Alla ina addana worɓe tuubde. Kono, alaa, on njaɓii moƴƴere Alla, on mbari koɗo mon e nokku ɗo potnoɗon yiɗde ɗum no hoore mon nii, no mi yamirunoo nii. Alla yiɗi aduna haa o rokki ɓiyiiko gooto, Iisaa Nasara, ngam hisnude on e makko e yiɗde rewrude e am.

Kono e nder mawnikinaare mon e laabi mon aduna, on tiiɗinii ɓerɗe mon e am e hisnugol am. Ko ɗuum waɗi, mbonni timmuɗi ngaroyan e leydi mon e nder waktu gooto, Babilon Mawɗo majjata. Ee, Babilon mawɗo, sirluuji mon peeñoyan ngam kala yiyde, kadi ɓeen nannguɓe on njaɓoyan ñaawoore wootere.

Ko ɗuum waɗi, ee, neɗɗo saliiɗo Alla, toownee hoore mon, ngaree e am jooni! Waktu mon haaju oo ko jooni. Ngartanee kam; miɗo ñaawoo on, miɗo nodda on e innde! Yaltu e oo aduna maayɗo, sabu hisnugol ina woodi e am so on njaɓii.

Mi alaa welo e ñaawoore mon, Baaba am ina yiɗi on nguura yeeso makko, kadi e nder yiɗde makko o rokki tagoore makko cuɓal: miɗo waɗa yeeso mon ñalnde hannde nguurndam e maayde, ko ɗuum waɗi, cuɓee nguurndam ngam onon e ɓiɓɓe mon nguuree! Ngaree e am hade waktu oo laataade, nganndon oo aduna bonɗo mo nannguɗon oo bonnoyte haa timma, ɗum wonta rewrude e tikkere Jibinaaɗo. So Alla ñaawoyta bonɓe warɓe sukaaɓe makko, ɓe mbari, ɓe torri laaɓɓe, ɓe mbari ɓiyiiko gooto Alla? Hay so tawii mi rokki nguurndam am ngam mon, alaa neɗɗo itti nguurndam am, ñaawoore mawnde e nuunɗal ina poti waɗeede e am. Sabu mi soodi leydi ndii e ƴiiƴam am e sadak am, ko e am woni. Mi sosan laamu nuunɗal e laaɓal goonga, ɗo liingu e jibinaaɗo njaɓɓondirta, ɗo alaa ko boni naatata. Ko ɗuum waɗi, yaltu e gacce mon, sabu miɗo yiɗi on, miɗo nodda e fooftere am sakkitiinde hade maaro sakkitoro oo fiyde. Kadi ɗum ɓooyataa.

Ruuhu e Debbo ina mbiya, ngaree!
