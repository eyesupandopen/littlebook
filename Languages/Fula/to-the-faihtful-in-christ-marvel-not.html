15 Desammbar 2024

Tilimɓe e nder Almasiihu: Woto on kawee!

Woto on kawee, yo sakiraaɓe am, woto on kawee, hay so tawii aduna oo ina añi on. Anndee aduna oo añinoo kam ko adii nde o añata on, yo yiɗɓe am, te jeyaaɗo mawnataa joom mum. Ko ɗum waɗi, paamee jamaanu mo nguɗon, ɓattoo e nder ɓerɗe mon to Yeshua HaMashiach, sabu miɗo nodda on ɓadtaade kam, yo eklesiya am, ɓanndu am, kadi e debbo am, te miɗo nodda on ɓadtaade kam e nder ɗiiɗoo sahaaji ko adii nde njahraton to dow kammuuli ngam mbaawon foolde kala huunde e nder Almasiihu.

E ko ɗum waɗi, e law e tawa on mbaɗaaka miijo, maa njahraton to dow kammuuli, sabu miɗo ara e waktu mo on mbaɗaani miijo. Gitee! Miɗo ara hono gujjo e jemma! Kono no ñalɗi Nuuhu ngonirnoo nii, non kadi garal Ɓii neɗɗo ngal wonirta. Sabu no e ñalɗi ɗi ilam ngaraani ɗii, hiɓe njaññunoo, hiɓe njarunoo, hiɓe resunoo, hiɓe ndesunoo, haa ñalnde Nuuhu naati e laana kaa, te ɓe anndaa hay huunde, haa ilam ngam ari, itti ɓe fof, non kadi garal Ɓii neɗɗo ngal wonirta.

Ko ɗum waɗi mi wasike on, yo yiɗɓe am, yo on ndutto e caɗeele aduna oo, oɗon anndi dartaagol mon e aduna oo bonɗo ina ɓadii. Kono non, oɗon njogii nguurndam ngu pot-ɗon wuurdude, te oɗon poti jokkude haa nde nguurndam mon ngam joofata, sabu so on njahraama to dow kammuuli tawa on mbaɗaaka miijo e juuɗe am cemmbinɗe kisal, golle mon maa timmude, te nguurndam mon e dow leydi maa joofoy e oon sahaa. Ko ɗum waɗi miɗo wasoo on, yo cukaloy am, nguuree nguurndam mon e nder ɗooftaare timmunde e daande Joomi mon e Kisnoowo mon, hono Almasiihu Yeesu Joomi mon jippinaaɗo to dow, te mi waɗan on foolde fof!

Ko ɗum waɗi, e nder nguurndam mon, ndokkiree kam fof e nder pellital ɓernde mon e anniya mon, sabu ko mi heewande mon e kala huunde. Woto mbi'ee e nder ɓerɗe mon wonde on keɓii nafoore e jawdi e semmbe juuɗe mon, malla e seedanteeje duɗe janngirɗe ɗe heewɓe e mon njogori e mbaadi majjere dow anndal Yahweh, hay e fenaande yeeyanaande on ko goonga e nder jaŋde mon bonnde, ndu fof mahiraa ko e fenaande, kono oɗon mawnintino e mbaadi majjere mbaawkaaji mon ɗi keɓ-ɗon heen ɗii. Ko ɗum waɗi miɗo wi'a on kadi, yo on njaltu e maɓɓe, ceertoron e kala huunde, malla maa on keɓdu e maɓɓe lepte e ñawuuji so ɗum waɗii, tawa noon ɗum fotaani hay dara, sabu e nder ɗiiɗoo sahaaji miɗo nodda on, miɗo ñaagoo on yo on ngar e am timma-timma e nder giɗli am e mon, yo ɓiɓɓe am. Nde nduttoɗon e mawninkinaare meere nde anndal addata ndee!

Ko ɗum waɗi, miɗo rokka on faamde kala ko keɓ-ɗon e nguuɗoo nguurndam ko fayti e jawdi aduna oo, ko e welwelo e moƴƴere e yurmeende Yahweh, miin Mawɗo oo, mo yiɗi ko ɓuri fof yo on njawdu, cellon, hono no ruuhu mon celliri nii. Kono, woto on won majjuɓe haa cikkoton ko on mbaɗi hoore mon, malla wonde e nder njeenaaje mon aduna oo, on ndaarii e yeeso Yahweh, miin Mawɗo oo, malla Yeesu Almasiihu, sabu alaa neɗɗo gooto fotaani e yeeso amen. Nde paamee, ɓeen ɓe mawnintini hoore mum en e mbaadi nii, maa njogoy torra mawka sabu mawninkinaare mum en, ndi laaɓi ndii adotoo ko halkaare mawnde.

Ngam nafoore mon, miɗo ñaagoo on yo on njaltu e aduna oo e kala jikkuuji mum bonɗi, jikkuuji neɗɗo waɗi hoore mum, daliiluuji bonɗi fenaande e mawninkinaare ɓanndu. Sabu, goonga goonga, so tawnoo yurmeende Yahweh, miin Mawɗo oo, weertaaka e giɗli makko duumotooɗi e mon, hay e ɗo giɗli makko e annoora makko duumotooɗi njilliri e nder ɓerɗe mon e nguurndam mon e yeeso Yeesu Almasiihu: on fof on njibbinaynoo ko Sodoma e Gomora, te on keɓataano hay reedu hay laalaagal, ko ɗum waɗi yo mawninkinaare mon won e Almasiihu. Kono, ndeen battane wonaa mon, yo yiɗɓe am, ko ɗum waɗi, e nder giɗli mon e am, ɗofto-ɗee daande am ngam mbaawon foolde kala huunde, sabu so on ɗoftike daande am, mi waɗan on foolde!

Non woni, te non wonirta, foolooɓe am ina njogii njoɓdi mawndi, haa teeŋti e ɓeen foolɓe hono no mi foolirnoo nii, te njoɓdi maɓɓe wonan ko ɓuri mawnude. Kono, heewɓe e tilimɓe am e nder Almasiihu ina njiiloo, ina nanngiree, hono no njahratnoo e maayɓe nii! Hay e yeeso yamirooje am ɗe ndokkunoo-mi Pool e sahaa Jiibileeji keewɗi ko ɓooyi e nder ɓataake makko to Efesiyankooɓe, o winndi, miɗo siftina on, yo yiɗɓe am: Ko ɗum waɗi o wiyi, fin, an ɗaaniiɗo, ummo e maayɓe, te Almasiihu maa rokku ma annoora. Nde keɓtinee no njahraton, woto wonon hono majjuɓe, kono wonon hono hakkilanteeɓe, coodton sahaa: sabu ñalɗi ɗii ko bonɗi. Ko ɗum waɗi, woto wonon majjuɓe, kono paamee ko woni anniya Joomiraaɗo.

Mbele on paamii anniya Joomi mon, yo cukaloy am? Mbele ko ɗum woni ko ɓuri teeŋtude e mon? Malla oɗon ngollande hoore mon e nder ɓerɗe mon, oɗon njiɗa ɓeydude jawdi e nder keesuuji mon ñalawma kala? Mbele on ndentinii ɓerɗe mon e ngollande fedde, malla renndooji worɓe bonɓe ngam kaalis bonɗo, hay e sahaa nde kaalis oo worɓe ndaññata, ndaññata e mbaadi bonnde to maɓɓe, maa wiyee alaa nafoore e yeeso maɓɓe? Malla on njokkondirii e ɗooftaare daande am, on njiytii e hakkille kala Konngol ngol ndokku-mi on ngam kisal mon? So on njokkondirii e ɗooftaare daande am, mi waɗan on foolde kala huunde, ko ɗum waɗi, woto on tampu!

Miɗo ñaagoo on haa jooni, on fof tilimɓe e nder Almasiihu e on fof ɓe ngontata tilimɓe e nder Almasiihu, yo on njokkondir e arde e am, sabu miɗo nodda on ɓadtaade kam haa ɓurti, ngam ɓadondiral timmungal giɗli am e mon semmbina on, walla on e kala huunde, ko ɗum waɗi, woto kulon, woto njeeyon waktu e nder hoore mon ñiŋde. Ko ɗum waɗi, njokkondiree e welwelo e nder giɗli mon e pellital ɓernde mon e Joomi mon Yeesu Almasiihu, yo cukaloy am, sabu alaa waktu goɗɗo potɗo yeeyeede! Te so buutolfooli fiyii, maa on ngon e nder sukaaɓe rewɓe majjuɓe ɓe ngoppete e yiite laɓɓinoowo, malla maa on tawa e nder sukaaɓe rewɓe hakkilanteeɓe ɓe njahratnoo to dow kammuuli e gorko maɓɓe kala saha. Hol mo ngonɗon e hol ɗo ndaaroton so mi feeñii e law kala saha?

Mbele maa ngoppam mi rokka nebam e lampaaji mon ngam mbaawon daraade e jooɗorde ñaawoore am? Mbele maa ngonon e laɓɓinde fitilaaji mon no haanirta nii e ɗooftaade yamirooje am? Malla maa cuɓoton e mbaadi majjere yo on ngon heewande hoore mon, oɗon ndaara e nuunɗal mon ngal keɓ-ɗon hoore mon, te oɗon ndaara e nder sukaaɓe rewɓe majjuɓe? Hay so mi feewnanii on ko ɓuri moƴƴude, so on ɗoftike daande am timma-timma. Ko ɗum waɗi, miɗo wasoo on, yo tilimɓe am, njokkondiree e arde e am e nder giɗli e ɗooftaare fof, ngam mbaawa mi mawninde on haa toowngo to dow kammuuli, ngam njilliron no koode nii e nder kammu, ngam geɗe ɗe mbaɗ-ɗon e ɗooftaare ngam ngollande Joomi mon duumotooɗo nuunɗal!

Ko ɗum waɗi, miɗo naamnoo on fof annduɓe daande am, hay onon noddaaɓe e innde, te noddoɓe e innde am: Mbele on fini timma-timma? Mbele gite mon ina udditii timma-timma e aduna oo no woniri hannde nii? Paamee aduna mo nguɗon hannde oo wonaa hono no wonirnoo hay e lebbi jeegom ko ɓooyi nii, te leydi ndii ina jokki e waylaade no feewi e yeeso mon, hay so tawii heewɓe e mon ina njiɗa daliiluuji e kaawniiɗi e nder keefeero mo alaa hoolaare, ina njiɗa ko artude e ɗoyngol. Ko ɗum waɗi, njiyton hoore mon e peeje mon e Joomi mon duumotooɗo, ngam mbaawa mi ardaade laabi mon, ndeenam on e bonannde e nder giɗli am e yurmeende am.

Non woni, fenaande mboddi ndii ina heewi e leydi ndii ko ɓuri no wonirnoo ko adii nde mboddi ndii werlee to leydi; sabu mboddi ndii werlaama, werlaama to leydi, te haɓɓaama les kammu ngam ñaawoore, te ina anndi ina jogii waktu seeɗa, te tikkere mum ina mawni! Te ina ara ngam haɓde, te hareeji mum maa mawnude, kono alaa mawnugol ngol ndeen mboddi ɓooyndi ndii jogorata so wonaa yiite e kaaƴe sulfur ɗe njiilotoo, ɗe ngulata e yiite ɓurɗo wulde e lepte.

Ko ɗum waɗi miɗo ara e mon, yo yiɗɓe am, ngam semmbinde on yo on njokkondir e ɓadtaade ɓernde am e ɗooftaare mon e Konnguɗi am, te e dow koɗorɗe ɓernde am, kala inɗe mon timmuɗe ina mbinndaa e nder giɗli am e mon, ɗi ngoni duumotooɗi e duumotooɗi. Eey, e nder giɗli am e mon, (mi anndi kala gooto e mon ko ɓuri no anndirton hoore mon nii) miɗo jogorii on no feewi e ɓernde am e kala sahaa, haa teeŋti e sahaaji mon caɗeele e jarribooji ɗi njahraton. (Mi moɓtii kala gonɗi mon e nder butel am gonɗi ngam mbaawa mi njoɓde on kala gooto) Te e nder hareeji ɗi njahratnoo e nder nduuɗoo hareeji aawdi, ɗi njahratnoo e Almasiihu, te ɗi njahratnoo e warɓe; paamee, hareeji mon njahratnoo ko timma-timma e Joomi mon duumotooɗo! Ko ɗum waɗi, nduttoɗon e Joomi mon duumotooɗo, hono Almasiihu Yeesu, te nduttoɗon e golle mon meere, te nduttoɗon timma-timma, te timminon golle nuunɗal ɗe ndokku-mi on yo mbaɗon, ngam mbaawon foolde añɓe mon, te mi rokkan on Sabba! Selah!

Ko ɗum waɗi, paamee ñalɗi ɗi nguɗon, sabu alaa gooto e mon wuuriino ɗiiɗoo ñalɗi garɗi e leydi ndii e nder nduuɗoo sahaa cakkitiiɗo, hay so tawii ina nanndi e ko anndunoo-ɗon. Sabu leydi ndii ina e nder waylo, te goonga leydi ndii ina waylee, te goonga goonga leydi ndii maa jokku e waylaade e semmbe no anniyaaji e peeje Yahweh ngoniri nii.

Paamee sahaa waylo mawngo arii e leydi ndii, hay sahaa nde leydi ndii feewnetee, feewnetee e kewuuji e musiibaaji e ñaawooje garooje haa artira leydi ndii no wonirnoo e ñalɗi Nuuhu nii! Te kewuuji e ñaawooje maa mbaɗoy no annabi'en ɓooyɓe mbi'irnoo nii, ɗi poti waɗde timma-timma e nder torra Yaaquuba. Ko ɗum waɗi, paamee, heewɓe e ñaawooje puɗɗiima haa jooni, hay e nder nduuɗoo sahaa waylo, sabu wonataano no geɗe mbaɗirta e laawol goɗngol nii.

Te non winndaa, te non waɗirtee, so mi moɓtii tilimɓe e nder Almasiihu e law e tawa on mbaɗaaka miijo jooni, so haɗoowo oo ittaama, te liɓiliɓi maa toɓoy, te kaayitaaji maa udditee, te buutol-fooli maa fiyee! Wonande seedeeji am ɗiɗo ummotooɓe, ɓe njamiran yiite toɓde e dow kammuuli, ɓe piyan leydi ndii e kala sifaa ñawuuji no njiɗiri nii, te ɓe mbaɗan toɓo toɓataa e leydi ndii e nder ñalɗi annabaagal maɓɓe e yamiroore maɓɓe, te heewɓe maa caloo tuubde! Paamee ɓe ndaaran e yeeso mboddi ndii, e mbeewa ndii, e annabi fenaande oo, te so tawii anniya neɗɗo ko lorlaade ɓe, ndeen neɗɗo maa lorlee no haanirta nii. So tawii anniya neɗɗo ko warkoyde ɓe, ndeen neɗɗo maa warkoyde no haanirta nii. Ɗii ko lampaaji am ɗiɗi e leɗɗe zaytuun am ɗiɗi, te heewɓe, heewɓe geɗe maa ɓe peeñnina e nder sahaaji peeñninooje maɓɓe, te seedanteeje maɓɓe maa mbinndee haa abada.

Ɗii ko sahaaji ɗi nguɗon, yo cukaloy am, te ɗii ko sahaaji ɗi pot-ɗon muñde e ɓadtaade anniya am e nguurndam mon, sabu ko heen woni kisal mon e jam mon. Paamee, on mawni ko hono nagge e nder sahaaji am yurmeende e yurmeende, ɗi ndokkaa ngam kisal leƴƴi majjuɗi, ɗi njaajii e nder leƴƴi, te ko ɗum waɗi, leƴƴi ndokkaama barke, njaraama e giɗli, e yurmeende, e yurmeende, te heewɓe keɓii heen kisal. Kono jooni sahaaji mbayliima no feewi, te miɗo yamira on yo on njahdu e HAKKILLE MAWNDE, te yo on won hakkilanteeɓe hono no mboddi nii, te yo on won ko aldaa e bonannde hono no colli nii, sabu ɗiiɗoo sahaaji ina mbaawi wonde bonɗi.

Sabu mboddi ndii haɓɓaama e leydi ndii, te warɓe ina ɓeydoo semmbe e kala sekonnd, kono ɓe ngalaa baawɗe daraade e daande am cemmbinnde e semmbe am timmuɗo e semmbe! Ko ɗum waɗi, miɗo fela ɓe, miɗo haɗa ɓe ngam mon ko ɓuri no mbaawirton faamde e nduuɗoo sahaa, te miɗo waɗi kala geɗe laamu añɓe les koyɗe mon, ngam mbaawon jaɓɓude ɓe so haaju wonii. Ko ɗum waɗi, woto kulon ɓe, sabu mi foolii kala huunde, hay e laamu Seytaane timmungu; te non kadi maa foolon e mbaadi nanndundi, so on njokkondirii e tilimɓe e Konnguɗi am yamirooje ɗe ndokku-mi, te miɗo rokka ngam ɗooftaare mon.

Woto on kawee, yo yiɗɓe am, hay so tawii warɓe ina peeñnina hoore mum en e leydi ndii, woto on kawee hay seeɗa! Sabu ñalɗi Nuuhu ngarii! Hay e ñalɗi ɗo Seytaane e malaa'ikaaji mum ngoni e werleede to leydi, ɓe ngartataa hay abada to kammuuli ɗo ɓe ngoppitaa haa abada e nder foolde timmunde. Hay e ñalɗi nde reenooɓe ɓee ngoppitaa e kasooji mum en ɗo ɓe haɓɓunoo, ceertuɓe e gootum e gootum e nder torra mawka ngam bonanndeeji maɓɓe bonɗi ɗi mbaɗnoo e Yahweh e tagoore makko e ñalɗi ɓooyɗi, te e nder foolde timmunde ɓe haɓɓunoo e murto maɓɓe e alkule ɗe njuurnoo hoore mum en yo timmin. Kono, alkule maɓɓe maa timmoy haa abada so ɓe haɓɓaama, njerlee e yiite lepte e nder ndeen maayo mawngo yiite sulfur ɗo ñorndi maɓɓe maayataa.

Hay e ñalɗi, kadi, artuɗi hono ñalɗi Nuuhu nde reenooɓe ɓee njippii e dow tulde Hermon e ñalɗi Jared. Hay e ñalɗi artuɗi ɗo warɓe ɓee ngollodirta e laamɓe leydi ndii e laamɓe maɓɓe aduna, ɓe cuuɗii caggal mum en, ɓe ngoni e peeje maɓɓe ɗe njiɗi addude halkaare mawnde e musiiba e hareeji e law no haɗoowo oo ittirtee, nde bonannde maɓɓe ndartete timma-timma. Eey, ɓe ngoppitaama e leydi ndii, te so haɗoowo oo ittaama, laamu Seytaane timmungu maa udditee so liɓiliɓi toɓii, te luggere ndee udditee, te damuɗe maɓɓe maa nguurto kadi. Gitee! Miɗo adda mawɓe ngam timminde tikkere am!

Woto on kawee, yo yiɗɓe am, hay so tawii warɓe ina njillina kammuuli les kammu e annooraaji mum en, e orbs, no ɓe peeñnirta hoore mum en e mbaadiiji mum en, te masiŋaaji maɓɓe ina mbaawi diwde, te heewɓe fenaande e jiiɓru ina ummoo ngam waɗde e yeeso leydi ndii e karallaagal mum en, hay e nder joofnirde muuseeji jibinannde, ɗi poti joofde e law e tawa on mbaɗaaka miijo e nder musiiba seeɗa jooni. Te neɗɗo tagoowo ina haawee, ina miijoo, ina waɗa daliiluuji e geɗe dow tagoore ɗe ngonti tagoore kadi.

Eey, neɗɗaagu ina waɗa daliiluuji e mbaadi keefeero e semmbe Yahweh kolliraaɗo e anniya wonde neɗɗaagu maa fin e nuunɗal, keɓa kisal. E nokku nde ndokkata Yahweh njeenaari ngam golle makko e maandeeji makko anniyaaji ngam reentaade ñaawoore garooru; neɗɗaagu ina rokka njeenaari neɗɗaagu e karallaagal mum meere ngal keɓi e warɓe: ko ɗum waɗi ñaawoore tiiɗnde e tiiɗnde ina ara! Woto on keɓdu hay feccere e daliiluuji maɓɓe keefeero, te njokkondiree e mawninde Yahweh ngam golle makko kisal ɗe ndokkaa e peeje makko duumotooɗe ngam kisal Aadama e yeeso Yeesu Almasiihu, mo woni Aadama cakkitiiɗo.

Miɗo anndina on non: hay si ko woni fuu waɗi mbayliigu ka kammu naa ka leydi, maa mbayliigu ka weeyo leydi, naa si neɗɗo fuu wayli tagu Yahwe no waawi wonirde fuu: Miɗo yamira on faamude no feewi wonde Yahwe ko Laamɗo timmuɗo dow tagu mum e kala ko waɗata fuu, ɗum waɗata tan ko e duŋaye makko, e ganndal makko timmungal, kadi fii hebbingo anniyaaji makko duumiiɗi ɗi hisingol ɓiyiiko majjuɗo, Aadama. Faamee, Yahwe ina acca bonɓe, ɓerɗe tiiɗuɗe, e luutiiɓe ɓe wuura nguurndam muɓɓen bonɗam faa ɓe kebbina aybeeji maɓɓe haa timma, ñaawoore maɓɓe aroore ndee, ɓe ndawataa nde, si wonaa si ɓe ngarii e Iisaa Almasiihu e tuubu timmungu so nde waktu heddii.

Kono non, Yahwe ina acca bonɓe ɓe mbaɗa bone faa ka ñaawoore, ɓe njoɓtiree no timmiri, ɓe keɓa njoɓdi timmundi golle maɓɓe bonɗe ɗe e ko'e maɓɓe haa abada abadin ka ɗemɗe lepte, hay so tawii ɗum fotaano, ina waawano hiseede e hisindam rewrude e ɗowtanaagol peewal. E moƴƴere Yahwe, e yurmeende makko e anniya makko hisinirɗo Aadama duumiiɗo; Yahwe yiɗaa hay neɗɗo gooto majja ka ɗemɗe, kono non, yimɓe fuu ndokkaama suɓagol mawnikinaangol fii suɓaade bone, naa suɓaade peewal e yeeso Iisaa Almasiihu, kañum tagitata kala garɗo e hisindam hono neɗɗo gooto keso, ɓe nguura haa abada abadin.

Nii woni, yankiniiɓe ɓe ndimaama timmunde fii ñaawoore dow hoɗɓe ka leydi luutiiɓe salagol innde Yahuusa Hamasiihu, hono Iisaa Almasiihu! Ko ɗum waɗi, faamee wonde kala saliiɗo Iisaa Almasiihu wonde Joomiraaɗo, naa wuurdiiɓe e ngoonga-anndaa e luutagol yamiraale am, maa accete e les njiimaandi maɓɓe tiiɗndi e laamu maɓɓe, ɗo ɓe niyetee momtude Aadama e yeeso leydi no feewi; e peeje maɓɓe ɓooyɗe ɗe ɓe niyetee wujjude leydi ndii e juuɗe am baawɗe, hay si ko peeje ɗe ɓe mbaawataa heɓde e majjere maɓɓe luutotoonde.

Kono non, neɗɗaagu ina yahda e anniya mum tawa ina naata e fannu maɓɓe, hay si omo firtude maale dokkaaɗe fii ñaawoore makko aroore, nde o salii haa jooni goongɗinde e miijooji makko ɗi ngoonga-anndaa, dokkaaɗi e jaŋde makko fenaande, ɗo hakkilantaagal makko wumnii timmunde e leydi goonga ndi o wuuri ndii, hay si ko leydi ndi Yahwe Mawɗo MI WONII tagi, kañum neɗɗaagu yeddude e jaŋde makko fenaande ndi Yesuit en peewni, jaŋde luulndiinde jaŋde. Si mi haaldii e mooɗon geɗe leydiiye on ngoongɗinaani, no ngoongɗinirton si mi haaldii e mooɗon geɗe kammuuyeeje? Sabu hay neɗɗo gooto yaltataa ka kammu, si wonaa on jippiiɗo ummaade ka kammu, hono Ɓii Neɗɗo on wonɗo ka kammu. On njettoto ɓerɗe mooɗon on ngoongɗina Konnguɗi Ɓii Neɗɗo on? Naa on kaɓɓotoo e tooruuji ɓerɗe mooɗon e fenaande wuyɓe jannguɓe haaldooɓe e luulndaare Konnguɗi dokkaaɗi e Yahwe e yeeso Iisaa Almasiihu?

E miijooji neɗɗaagu ɗi ngoonga-anndaa, omo fuyta omo ɗaɓɓita jaabawuuli fenaande fii maale e kaayɗe yankiniiɓe ɗe. Hay jaabawuuli dokkaaɗi e yankiniiɓe kadi sahaa e sahaa kala saliiɗo Konnguɗi Adonay Yahwe, ɗi Konnguɗi ɗi ndokkaa fii ɗowtanaagol e juuɗe laawol goonga ngol wuurngol, laawol ngol min, Almasiihu Iisaa, mi woni goonga. Ko ɗum waɗi, woto on naatdu e maɓɓe e miijooji maɓɓe ɗi ngoonga-anndaa hay seeɗa. Nii woni, miɗo anndina on, leydi ndii ko El Sadday jeyi, kañum rokki ɗum Yahuusa Hamasiihu, Laamɗo Laamɓe e Joomiraaɗo Joomiraaɓe, ko ɗum waɗi goonga, leydi ndii ko Joomiraaɗo jeyi e kebbudi mum! Alaa hay tagu gooto baawngu ittude ndonu am e juuɗe am baawɗe, miɗo ara fii heɓtude ndonu am e doole e juuɗe hoɗɓe ɓe ngalaa hakke, ɓe njahata timmunde e ñaawoore duumiinde nde ɓe ndawataa hay abada. Kono non, ɓe kaɓɓotoo e hoolaare maɓɓe fenaande wonde e limoore maɓɓe timmunde ndimaande, ɓe mbaawi foolude Yahuusa Hamasiihu, hay hoolaare nde mi ronkata timmunde e doole semmbe am dokkaaɗe e Adonay Yahwe e hoore mum, e jappeere laamu nde o renndini e Iisaa Almasiihu.

Woto on naate e njuumri maɓɓe, yiɗɓe am, woto on accite e majjere tiiɗnde e ñaawoore nde wonaa mooɗon. Sabu on mawni hono gaynaaɗi ka weendu e jamaanuuji deeƴere e kisal, ɗo gardiiɗo on gardinoo, moƴƴere e yurmeende keewi ka leydi hay si torra ina heewi. Jooni noon, deeƴere e kisal ustiima ka leydi, ina fotnoo lannude, hay si gardiiɗo on ina jogii haa jooni. Yankiniiɓe ɓe ina njokki e peeje maɓɓe e anniyaaji maɓɓe, ɗo ɓe ɗaɓɓirta tabitinde jamaanu maɓɓe kaŋŋeeji wuurtinaaɗo, kono tan ñaawoore tiiɗnde e mettuɗe ɓe keɓata.

Ko ɗum waɗi, on tawaaka ɗee jamaanuuji ko adii, yiɗɓe am, miɗo jokkata e ardude on yeeso e yeeso, faa ɓurton ɓadaade daande timminoore mooɗon, ɗo miɗo heppi, miɗo welta e mooɗon so on timminii golle mooɗon dokkaaɗe e juuɗe am fii teddungal mooɗon, hisindam mooɗon, e njoɓdi mooɗon! Ko ɗum waɗi, woto on kule ɓe, dewgal am, sabu on ko foolooɓe am, woto on kule, ɓanndu am, sabu njoɓdi mawndi ko mooɗon, e 144 000 am ɓe ngolata golle ɓurɗe mawnude ka leydi hay e ɗe ngolnoomi e jamaanuuji golle am ka leydi hono Jowgel Alla, nde Pontiyus Pilaatus wonnoo laamɗo dow Yahuuda!

Sabu hono no ñalɗi ko adii ilam, ɓe njaarno, ɓe njaruno, ɓe resuno, ɓe ndokkunoo e dewgal, haa ñalnde Nuuhu naati e laana ndiyam, ɓe nganndaa hay huunde, haa ilam ngam, heɓti ɓe fuu, non kadi garol Ɓii Neɗɗo ngol wonirta. Nden, ɗiɗo maa ngone e ngesa, gooto maa jaɓee, goɗɗo on salaama. Rewɓe ɗiɗo maa njiirde e niirude: gooto maa jaɓee, goɗɗo on salaama. Kakkilee non: sabu on nganndaa waktu mo joom mooɗon arata. Goongɗinee ɗum, so Joom Galle on annduno e waktu mo wuyɗo on arata, o kakkilano, o accataa galle makko feññe. Kakkilee non kadi: sabu e waktu mo on cikkaali, -le Ɓii Neɗɗo on arata.

Hol non woni maccuɗo koolaaɗo e jom hakkilantaagal, mo joom mum waɗi laamɗo dow galle mum, fii rokkude ɓe ñamri e sahaaji mum? Barke wonanii maccuɗo on, mo joom mum so ari, tawi omo waɗa noon. Goonga, miɗo wi'a on, o waɗan mo laamɗo dow jawdi makko fuu. Kono so maccuɗo bonɗo on wi'ii e ɓernde mum, joom am nennii garol mum, fuɗɗii fiyde wondiiɓe mum, ñaamda, yarda e sakramɓe, joom maccuɗo on maa ar ñalnde nde o heppaali, e waktu mo o anndaa, o taƴan mo, o rokka mo geɗal mum e naafiqeeɓe: ton wonan wullere, e ŋitgol ñiiƴe.

Nii woni, miɗo rentina on kadi, sabu kala mo mi yiɗi miɗo rentina, kala mo mi yiɗi miɗo felna, kala mo mi yiɗi miɗo laɓɓina, kala mo mi yiɗi miɗo fiyra laabi keewɗi sabu Yahwe ina jogorii on hono sukaaɓe, hol Baaba laɓɓintaa sukaaɓe mum, si wonaa ɗum, on ngonaa sukaaɓe kono ko on ñiiɓɓe e yeddaaɓe. Ko ɗum waɗi, muñee laɓɓingol am, ndenndinee Konnguɗi am felnooji e laɓɓinooji faa mbaawon foolude yankiniiɓe ɓe e kala geɗe e nguurndam ɗam, ɗo ɓe ɗaɓɓirta falnude on, hay faa woto on njottoo e ndonu mooɗon timmungu ngun mi joganii on e juuɗe am baawɗe. Hay ndonu ngun on mbaawataa majjinde so on ɗowtiima timmunde yamiraale am ɗe on poti reenude e yiɗde mooɗon Yahwe, e Yahuusa Hamasiihu kadi.

Faamee yiɗde am e mooɗon, eklesiya am, ɓanndu am, hay dewgal am, anndee miɗo nodda on faa ɓurton ɓadaade kam sabu ɓooyataa mi jippoo ummaade ka kammuuli e daande arkanjo, e buutital Yahwe, buutital ngal wullan, daande ndee wullan, ñalawma, waktu, e saha garol am anndinaama jooni ka kammuuli, ina ɓadii timmude ka leydi, hay si ko e tampere e nde heppaaka no fodaama. Kala yidduɗo e yiɗɗo garol am maa heɓ njoɓdi mawndi, hay heɓa kaɓirgal peewal fii hoolaare maɓɓe sabu jogiiɓe hoolaare hono ndee ko maayɓe timmunde e aduna bonɗo hannde oo, ɓe poolan timmunde e doole, semmbe, e peewal Joomiraaɗo maɓɓe duumiiɗo Teddungal!

Miin ko Iisaa Nasaraat, mo noddata on faa ɓurton ɓadaade kam, eklesiya am, ɓanndu am, hay dewgal am.

Miin ko Iisaa Almasiihu, mo noddata on ɓura ɓadaade e ɗee jamaanuuji ado on naatireede e njoɓdiiji ndonu mooɗon, ɗi mi rokkata on ɓooyataa ka kammuuli so on njettaama.

Miin ko Almasiihu Iisaa, mo goonga, goonga, woni heddere mooɗon e kala geɗe, ko ɗum waɗi, fooftoo e golle am e peewal am, naamndee kam e jettagol, ndenee kala laabi mawninkinaare heddere hoore mum, sabu on ɓadii hawtude timmunde e Jom Dewgal mooɗon, mo rokkata on haa abada heewre e jawdi kala geɗe e ndonu mooɗon e nder Almasiihu.

Miin ko Yahuusa Hamasiihu, mo noddata, mo ñaaagotoo on ngara timmunde e am e yiɗde am e mooɗon, sukaaɓe am, faa hisindam mooɗon timmungal timma e ɗee jamaanuuji ndimaagu timmungu fii koolaaɓe e nder Almasiihu, si ɓe fooftii e ɓernde leydi ɗo ɓe nani e jam e peewal, naa si ɓe nguuri, ɓe keddii e yeeso leydi so mi arii, mo waktu mum yottii.

Ko ɗum waɗi, faamee nafoore e jaawal jamaanuuji ɗii sabu alaa waktu heddiiɗo fii luutagol, goonga ina fotnoo timmude jooni, wonde so mi arii, koolaaɗo e nder Almasiihu maa nannge faa hisa ka kammuuli, ɗo njoɓdi mawndi wonan maɓɓe, ɓe ndawete e tikkere aroore ndee, e ñaawoore nde ɓadii yani e leydi ndii e musiiba mawɗo. Nii kadi, so mi arii, mo wonaa koolaaɗo, mo wonaa e Almasiihu, maa accete e yiite laɓɓinirde, ɗo ɓe hisintee so ɓe noddii innde Iisaa Joomiraaɗo maɓɓe haa e timmoode maɓɓe, alaa suɓagol goɗngol heddiiɓe e leyɗe saliiɓe hisindam maɓɓe timmungal e saha garol am.

Nii woni, yiɗɓe am, miɗo ara haalde on kadi, woto on ndillo e nano, naa e ñaamo, kono njogitee koyɗe mooɗon no moƴƴi e laawol ceeɗungol ngol, ɗo mi waɗata jom en ɓerɗe newiiɗe e jettuɓe foola timmunde so ɓe foolii yankiniiɓe ɓe e doole am baawɗe, e peewal am ngal mi rokkiri e dokkal ɗowtiiɓe ɓe. Ko ɗum waɗi, njogitee gite mooɗon e hakkilantaagal gootal e Joomiraaɗo mooɗon duumiiɗo Peewal, faa njottoɗon e njoɓdi mooɗon timmundi, faa pooftoɗon timmunde e jam e deƴƴere nde mi addata so miɗo saakta e mooɗon jam am e deƴƴere am e yiɗde am e mooɗon. Faa hay e yeeso caɗeele e jarribooji, mbaawon daroraade e semmbe duumiiɗo e taƴre mooɗon e torra mooɗon so on fooftii e semmbe am mo foolataake.

Miin ko Yahuusa Hamasiihu, kañum e Yahwe, Mawɗo MI WONII, neli Konnguɗi Linjiila Duumiiɗo e juuɗe nelaaɗo am baawɗo e maccuɗo am mo winndi, mo janngi ɗii Konnguɗi no koolaagol fii ɗowtanaagol e hisindam kala mo ɗii Konnguɗi neldaa. E juuɗe nelaaɗo am baawɗo e maccuɗo am Konnguɗi Linjiila Duumiiɗo neliraama. Goonga, goonga, Konnguɗi Linjiila Duumiiɗo maa ndesete rewrude e musiibaaji e ñaawooje garooje, ɗe ɓadii fuɗde e dow leydi ndii. Faamee wonde seedanteeje goɗɗe maa ndokke, mbinndee, ndeenee e defte ka kammuuli, Konnguɗi Adonay Yahwe maa kebbina leydi ndii e kala peewal e seninaare goonga!

Miɗo ara ɓooyataa fii koolaaɓe e nder Almasiihu, goonga, jamaanuuji maɓɓe njottiima! Jamaanuuji ɗo ɓiɓɓe leɗɗe arandeeje ndimri maa njottine fodde e njoɓdi e yeeso Yahwe e hoore mum (waktu mo Yahwe yiɗnoo gila e jamaanuuji ɓooyɗi), e Yahuusa Hamasiihu, e mboolaagu malaa'ikaaɓe keewngu, ɓe weltaare woni e yeeso maɓɓe baawngo so neɗɗo gooto heɓii hisindam duumiiɗam. Kono non, non wonirta so mi noddii koolaaɓe e nder Almasiihu faa galle maɓɓe fii njoɓdi maɓɓe duumiindi, ɓe njooɗotoo e nokkuuje maɓɓe hakkunde leƴƴi sabu ko ɓe Isra'iila Alla, ɓe kebbina darnde maɓɓe, mboolaagu laamɓe e almuɓɓe baawngu mi laɓɓinan mi senina fii golle duumiiɗe e laamu am duumiingu, ngun alaa timmoode.

Miin ko Yahuusa Hamasiihu, mo woni kadi Joomiraaɗo Ndimri, goonga, goonga, jamaanuuji ndimri ɓiɓɓe leɗɗe arandeeje njottiima, e saha ndimri ɓiɓɓe leɗɗe arandeeje fii njoɓdi, non kadi o yottii.

Jamaanuuji ɗii ko jooni. Selah!
