Juungo añɓe mooɗon ina woni e daande mooɗon Amerik! Tuubee! Nanee Konnguɗi am Babiilon Cirlol!

Kono on njiifama onon hoɗɓe e leydi, si on cikkii won neɗɗo fof potɗo e Almasiihu Yeesu Joomiraawo Dowrowo mo poofɗal, mo on anndaa, hay onon fof jooɗiiɓe e niɓre, yiɗɓe bone e nder mawnude yankinaare e daande Almasiihu Yeesu mo woni kadi Jibinaaɗo Alla, jooɗiiɗo e nyaamo Yaahwe Mawɗo MIIN WONƊO ƊO, mo mi renndi jooɗorde e laamu mum sabu mi waɗaama ruuhu hokkoowo nguurndam, mo jooni darii e doole mawɗe no Mbaroodi leñol Yahuuda, sabu alaa Ɓii Neɗɗo goɗɗo, Seelaa, paamee tikkere am e torra am ina heɓanii fawtaade e mbonniigu nguu on nji'ataa ina ara, nguu on ndoggataa.

Bone, BONE MAWƊO wonande mooɗon ɓiɓɓe yimɓe yankiniiɓe, ɓe jooni Torra Jibinaaɗo oo fawtoytoo e dow mooɗon e wakkati gooto, adda mbonniigu nguu on sikitaaki sabu laabi mooɗon toownooɓe tooruuji, onon jeeloowo mawɗo, on nanay wullaango am nde leydi niɓɓidata, sabu mi renndataa teddungal am e goɗɗo, Seelaa!

Nanee Konnguɗi am Heylel, hay aan laamɗo famaro foolaaɗo, jooɗo e nder foolaare e yankinaare, acca wullaango maa ngo aldaa e ñiiƴe, sabu a ko añamde foolaande, a heɓataa duuɓi kaŋŋe kono ñaawoore tiiɗnde, kono aran maa a gollo anniyaaji YHVH Mawɗo MIIN WONƊO ƊO, e nder yankinaare e laamu am e nder foolaare maa, (e nder hersa maa e foolaare maa) a waɗataa so wonaa ko mi yamiri e laamu am, sabu alaa nokku e tagu YHVH ɗo juungo am e laamu am timmungu heɓataa, miɗo yiya kulol maa, aan mboddi foolaandi! Jooɗo e deƴƴere aan malaa'ika buntiiɗo!

Jooɗo e deƴƴere laamɗo famaro sabu juungo am ina jogii daande maa no feewi, e ko inniraa AI maa ngoo ina woni e nder yankinaare e jooɗorde YHVH, Mawɗo, MIIN WONƊO ƊO e yamiroore am aan laamɗo famaro foolaaɗo! Hol woni baawɗo haɗde juungo doole, baawɗe, e laamu ngol rokkaa e Almasiihu Yeesu, Joomiraawo Dowrowo mo poofɗal, mo woni kadi Yeesu Almasiihu, Seelaa!

Onon fof foolaaɓe, on mbaɗataa so wonaa ko on njaɓaama waɗde e nder hersa mooɗon e foolaare mooɗon, kono waktu mooɗon yontiima laamɗo famaro, on nji'ii no laaɓiri, hay so tawii on njiifii no feewi, aan ñolliwɗo. Kono laabi mooɗon njoofii, Caɗeele Yaaquuba ɗe ngoni asaweere cappanɗe jeeɗiɗi Daniel ndee maa laɓɓin leydi ndii e laabi mooɗon haa abada: So wonaa e sahaa mooɗon cakkitiɗo, famaro, mo murtal, on mooftay ko heddii e comci ɗi puɗɗotoo e murtal e les laamu nuunɗungu Linjiila Duumotooɗo ngol feññinii jooni feccere e Deftere Famarde Peeñal Faandaare 10, hay deftere famarde udditiinde nde mi udditi e juungo maccuɗo am.

Kono noon woniri! Ko heddii e Alkawal Kesal ngal maa rokke Yaaquuba mo jooɗotoo e koyɗe seedeeji am ɗiɗo ɓe ngarata e leydi ndii e sahaa gooto e laawol ngol a waawaa sikitaade, aan laamɗo famaro foolaaɗo. E Lekkiiji am ɗiɗi oliiw, ɗi ngoni fitillaaji am ɗiɗi, maa njanngin e seernda hay heddiiɓe doole e alkama, ɓe sujjananta Yeesu Almasiihu, itta bumnaare feccere e yimɓe am goonga ɓe ceerndotoo e gawri maa! Kono galle heeferɓe maa ñiilire koyɗe e nder hare e seniiɓe nde dabba mbeela oo waɗi, mo inni hoore mum maayre ina fawa e asamaan e ɗemngal Ebere, hay sifaa no Barack Hussein Obama nii, gollodiiɗo e dabba leydi oo, mo tooke mum e nyallore mboddi mum e juungo, mo jillindata njamndi e loope kono ɗi njokkondirtaa.

E Enok Binndoowo e Eliya Tishbite maa njanngin alkama am ngam ittude, hay alkama am mo ndeeniraa haa abada e reentaare alkawal e fodoore e ɓiɓɓe yankinaare ɓe ngalaa nuunɗal haa abada ngam timminde Alkawal am Kesal ngal Nuunɗal, ngal Deftere am Ceniinde wonnoo tan ko sarans e jeyi soodaaɗo mo darii haa abada e goonga duumotooɗo, sabu miin ko mi Konngol Adonai-YHVH waɗiraaɗo ɓanndu, mo woni kadi fonsiyoŋ mooɗon duumotooɗo e kisal, hay kaaƴe mooɗon mawɗe, mo ɓooyaani jooni yaltina yamiroore: E wullaango doole! E luwal ngal maa yim e hollita e sahaa gooto, e doole, e ɗo on sikitaaki jooni e mawningol gomɗinɓe e Almasiihu, ko anndata ɗum tan ko ɓeen ɓe njanngaa e annoore mum e tuubugol nuunɗungol, ɓe fitillaaji mum laɓɓinaa e yankinaare e laabi am!

Yo ɓeen nanɓe: Paame sawtaaji ɗii ina njiima e gooŋɗinal e jam, sabu hol baaba hokkata mboddi ɓiɓɓe mum, so wonaa e les koyɗe mum'en, haɓɓaaɗo e deƴƴere foolaande ngam yaltineede ngam laamaade ɓe ngonaa gomɗinɓe, hay so tawii ko nde gomɗinɓe e Almasiihu njaltata e sahaa gooto jooni!

E nde teddungal jalɓi am feƴƴata e asamaan, e luwal doole ngal yimata no feewi e noddaango sikkaango ngam jom-suudu am e hoɓɓe amen, ngal yimata ngam gomɗinɓe e Almasiihu, hay onon fof ɓe mi njanngini jooni e annoore luwal doole Alla ngal, ngal woni luwal cakkitiingal e jamaanu am mo yurmeende, mo fotata timmude e sahaa gooto e ɗo on sikitaaki: Nanee Konnguɗi ɓesngu am, sabu reenɗo mooɗon e rimɗinoowo mooɗon ina darii jooni ngam rimɗinde on timmunde Eklesiya am, Ɓanndu am e hay jom-suudu am, Sewee!

Njannginee ko'e mooɗon jom-suudu am, jooɗee e sara laawol e lampaaji mooɗon keewɗi nebam am laaɓɗam, ngam woni maande Nuunɗal e ruuhu Ɓiɗɗaaku, ngam woni ruuhu ceniiɗo fodoore jibinaaɗo e nder kala ɓiɓɓe annoora, ɓe keɓi sarans timmuɗo e jeyi soodaaɗo oo, fotɗo rimɗineede timmunde jooni e juuɗe Almasiihu Yeesu, Joomiraawo maɓɓe Duumotooɗo e Konnguɗi laaɓɗi seedanfaagu am e ruuhu am sabu torra am wonaa ngam mooɗon.

Sabu ɗuum, accitee golle mooɗon, poftoree e yiɗde am ɓesngu am, kono daroree no tiiɗiri, njaɓee kaafa miñi am ruuhu am seernda comci cakkitiiji ɗii e nguurndam mooɗon yiɗɓe am.

Njannginee ko'e mooɗon yiɗɓe am sabu rimɗinde mooɗon yontiima, on ɓooyataa nanude wullaango doole, on njaltintee e sahaa gooto jooni haa e jooɗorde YHVH e hoore mum, sabu ɗuum njannginee ko'e mooɗon ɓesngu am sabu miɗo ara no gujjo e jemma, wonaa no kewu limtaaɗo, kono yo ceerndagol am daro, limiree limooje ɗee fawaade e Konnguɗi am Ceniɗi e laabi am; wonaa e annduɓe ɓe mbi'ata e ɓe mbi'ata, malla ɓe njokkondirta e ñalɗi ɗi ngonaa laaɓtuɗi e kalandiriye mooɗon Gregoire mo laaɓaani.

Nanee Konnguɗi am hoɗɓe e leydi, sabu miin ko mi Mbaroodi leñol Yahuuda, miɗo ara ngam ñaawoyde e haɓde!

Nanee daande hakkilantaagal, sujudee, nanee daande wuurdere Yeesu Almasiihu, sabu miin ko mi Mbaroodi leñol Yahuuda, miin ko mi Laamɗo Laamɓe e Joomiraawo Joomiraaɓe! Hay on mo El Shaddai, hay Alla Baawɗo oo, toowni no feewi, rokki innde dow kala innde. Hono e Innde Yeesu kala hoore sujjidata, e ko woni e asamaan, e ko woni e leydi, e ko woni e les leydi: E hono kala ɗemngal seedotoo, wonde Yeesu Almasiihu ko Joomiraawo, ngam teddungal Baaba Annooraaji, hay Yaahwe, Alla duumotooɗo e mo alaa gadanal, sabu alaa nannduɗo e Baaba amen!

Nanee daande Joomiraawo mooɗon, paame geɗe ɗe puɗɗiiɗe e geɗe ɗe fotata waɗde, sabu nji'ee miin ko mi haaloowo geɗe garooje no mbaɗiri nii, sabu goonga Joomiraawo mooɗon mawɗo, hay miin Almasiihu Yeesu ardiɗo oo, waɗataa hay huunde, so wonaa mi feññina sirruuji am e maccuɓe am Annabaaɓe. Hol no on njaɓirta daandeji luurondirɗi heewɗi, e hol no on njiilortoo hakkunde miijooji ɗiɗi? Malla mi wi'aani annabaaɓe am ina kaalda e daande wootere, ɓe luurondirtaa abada e Konnguɗi am ceniɗi? Sabu miin ko mi Konngol waɗiraaɗo ɓanndu, guurɗo e hakkunde mooɗon e ruuhu am ceniiɗo jibinaaɗo e nder mooɗon haa abada, sabu kala neɗɗo jibinaaɗo kadi ina maandiniree ruuhu ceniiɗo fodoore ngol, ngol woni aawdi am ndi bonnataa, e maande Alla Wuurdere ndee waawaa feƴƴeede. E ñalawma rimɗinde jeyi soodaaɗo oo ina ɓadii, e rimɗinde mooɗon timmunde ina ɗoo.

Ko noon Joomiraawo Konuuji wiyri: Nanee konnguɗi annabaaɓe ɓe nganndinta on, ɓe njannginta on meere: ɓe kaalata ko yi'annde ɓerɗe maɓɓe, wonaa ko yalti e hunuko Joomiraawo. Ngaroyee to am ɓiɓɓe am sabu teddungal ngal on keɓi e am ngal, ina woni e mooɗon: on njiɗaa hay neɗɗo janngina on: Kono, no teddungal ngal jannginirta on geɗe fof, ngal ko goonga, ngal wonaa fenaande: e no ngal jannginiri on, on nduurtoytoo e am. ɓiɓɓe am nduuree e am, pottinee konnguɗi kala neɗɗo wi'ɗo ina haalda e am e binndi ceniɗi, naamndee mi ngam mi tabbintina e ruuhu mooɗon, so tawii ma a taw on njaɓay daande am sabu heewɓe e mooɗon ina nanndi e noppi, heewɓe e mooɗon ina njiɗi konnguɗi fenaande ɗi njiɗata noppi mooɗon ngam on mbaawa yiyeede no annduɓe kono on njaɓay konnguɗi fenaande e majjuɓe.

Nji'ee! Miɗo haala jamaanuuji caɗeele Yaaquuba ɗe fotata feƴƴude e dow leydi ndii fof. Sabu ñaawoore ina fuɗɗii, waawaa haɗeede, sabu ko Yaahwe holliti alaa baawɗo waylude! Heewɓe, heewɓe gonɗi mi woyi e ñaawoore Babiilon Cirlol, mi neli annabaaɓe am duuɓi keewɗi, heewɗi jooni, mi neli hay hoɓɓe ummoriiɓe e leyɗeele keewɗe ngam seedaade e annabaade ñaawoore ndee ina ardi kono jooni ina ɗoo.

Sabu noon wonirta, sabu noon winndiraa: Luuɗe sappo ɗe nji'ɗon e dow dabba oo, ɗee maa añe jeeloowo oo, ɗe mbaɗa ɗum mo alaa ko woni, e mo alaa ko waɗi, ɗe nyaama ɓanndu mum, ɗe njula ɗum e yiite. Sabu Alla waɗii e ɓerɗe maɓɓe ngam timminde anniya mum, e jaɓondirde, e hokkude laamu maɓɓe dabba oo, haa konnguɗi Alla timma. E debbo oo, mo nji'ɗon oo, ko wuro mawngo ngo laamotoo laamɓe leydi ndii.

Nanee konnguɗi am, ñaawoore Babiilon Cirlol ina fuɗɗii. Luuɗe sappo ɗee njannginii hare maɓɓe nde ɓe mbaɗata e nder wakkati gooto, hare maɓɓe ina ardi, ina woni e damal mooɗon. Sabu hay jaayɗe mooɗon ina njiɗi welnude on haa on ɗaano, heewɓe ina njooɗii e niɓre, ɓe nji'ataa goonga, ina mbi'a: Miɗo jooɗii no Laamɗo debbo, mi wonataa jom-gorko maayɗo, mi yiyataa sunu. Janngo maa won no hannde nii, sabu hannde ko no keñaan nii. No o mawniniri hoore mum, o wuuri e welwelo, noon ndokkee mo torra e sunu: Sabu ɗuum, belaaji mum maa ngar ñalnde wootere, maayde, e sunu, e heege, o wuliree yiite: sabu Yaahwe ñaawoowo mo, ko Alla Baawɗo, alaa mawɗo, alaa ko haɗata juungo El Shaddai doole!

Ndeen naange maa fuɗ e ñalawma musiiba mo on sikitaaki, ñalawma oo ina ɓadii no cikkirɗon. Ñalawma oo maa addan mbonniigu timmungu e Babiilon Cirlol, sabu omo jogii feccere ɗiɗi ñaawoore hollitaande e makko. Bonanndeeji makko e jeelooji makko ina keewi, ñaawoore makko ina leeltata, e lepte mawɗe maa njah e dow ɓe tuubaaki, no Eklesiya am gomɗinɗo e hay jom-suudu am gomɗinɗo maa mooftire to am e sahaa gooto e duule, kisiree e torra, hay caɗeele mawɗe asaweere cappanɗe jeeɗiɗi Daniel. Babiilon Mawɗo oo yani, yani! Nanee konnguɗi am sabu miɗo haala no mbaɗiri nii, sabu alaa ko waylata, leeltinta, haɗata, malla darnata ñaawooje Yaahwe nde ɗe njahata e leydi ndii.

E nder waktu gooto o wuliree yiite, yoga e gure makko maa njula, yoga e gure makko maa njiilee e ilam, cuuɗi makko toowɗi maa njane, njiifoo e pecce keewɗe, ponji makko maa njane, e dillere heewnde maa waɗu. Leydi ndii maa feƴƴe no feewi, e leydi heewndi maa suuɗiree e ndiyam doole. Wutte makko maa suuɗiree ummoraade fuɗnaange e hirnaange e beeli mawɗi, e hakkunde beeli ɓuuɓɗi e dillere doole. Ko heddii heen maa won wuro meere, o ummataa kadi abada, sabu ndee ngenndi gure keewɗe e gure keewɗe ummataa kadi, yanugol makko maa mawnude! Sabu leydi ndii ko joomiraawo e timmal mum, e kala fannuuji bonanndeeji mum maa njuutiree e leyɗeele haa abada e nder asaweere cappanɗe jeeɗiɗi Daniel, sabu noon mi laɓɓinirta leydi ndii e torra am. Miin, Almasiihu Yeesu, Mbaroodi leñol Yahuuda, mi hollitii ɗee konnguɗi, e janngugol ngam timminde ɗi ina fuɗɗii, ɗi timmintee e sahaa gooto e nder waktu gooto.

Ndeen hare maa feƴƴude e leydi ndii fof no feccere e hare nde Babiilon Cirlol waɗirtee e wakkati gooto, sabu ɗum anniyanooma, yuɓɓinaama duuɓi keewɗi jooni, sabu sirru bonannde ina golli gila e jamaanuuji ɓooyɗi, o timmataa so wonaa nde haɗoowo oo ittaa, no gomɗinɓe am njaɓirtee e asamaan. Hare maa fawa e leydi ndii fof, o yettotoo yimɓe fof e leyɗeele fof, sabu ndee ko hare tataɓere, no foolaaɓe ɓee njiɗirta bonnude laabi ɓooyɗi aduna oo, njuɓɓina geɗe fof e laabi maɓɓe kesi, hay laawol Dragon oo. Dabba mbeela oo e dabba leydi oo maa ummo e tikkere mawnde, ɓe kaɓoytoo e seniiɓe, sabu ɗum rokkaama ɓe ngam foolde seniiɓe. Ndeen wonirta kala mo yiɗaa nguurndam mum, nodda innde Joomiraawo haa timma, maa hisine e welwelo duumotooɗo haa e duuɓi duuɓi. E miin Yeesu Nasaraat mi accii yeru duumotooɗo e yankinaare haa e maayde, sabu yankinaare am e Yaahwe wonnoo hay maayde e leggal gaafaangal no mi torririi, mi yoɓi boneeji yimɓe no jibinaaɗo Alla, nji'ee miɗo ara no Mbaroodi leñol Yahuuda, mi haɗataake malla mi leeltintaake!

Nii woni, annabaaɓe am ɗiɗo, wonɓe leɗɗe oliba ɗiɗi e lampaaji ɗiɗi dariiɗi yeeso Joomiraaɗo leydi ndii, maa kaaldu annabaaku duuɓi cappanɗe nay e ɗiɗi ɓorniiɓe saakuuji, sabi ɓe njettaama haa asamaanuuji e jamaanuuji ɓooyɗi. So neɗɗo goɗɗo anniyike waɗde ɓe bone, yiite yaltan e kunɗe maɓɓe, ngoon yiite wulan añɓe maɓɓe. So neɗɗo goɗɗo anniyike waɗde ɓe bone, o foti warireede noon. Ɓeen ina njogii baawɗe uddude asamaan, haa toɓo ronkat toɓde e ñalɗi annabaaku maɓɓe; ina njogii kadi baawɗe e dow ndiyam faa mbaɗta ɗam ƴiiƴam, ina njogii kadi baawɗe memtinde leydi ndii kala lorra, kala nde ɓe njiɗi.

Seedantaagal maɓɓe ko daymiingal, tee maa ɓe njottin linjiila daymiika, ɓe kaala kadi geɗe Yaweh gila e fuɗɗoode haa e laakara, ɓe kaala e innde am miin Almasiihu Iisaa, ɓe peeññinan geɗe keewɗe cuddiiɗe, ɓe ngartiran kadi ɓerɗe yimɓe heewɓe feewde e Baaba Annduule, mo alaa batte waylaare walla leeɓol waylaare. Maa ɓe ceedoo e dow am miin Yesuwa Hamasiyaak, sabi ko miin woni mo Yaweh neli to Isra'iila ngam timminde alkule baɗanooɗe maamiraaɓe maɓɓe, tee ko miin woni Almasiihu goongaajo jooni e haa abada, tee laamu am alaa timmoode! Sabi honɗum jamaanu woni ɗo Yaweh acci hoore mum tawa alaa seede? Hoto jamaanu wonaani ɗo Yaweh acci hoore mum tawa alaa seede, tee kala mo yamiranaa kisal daymiingal, maa heɓa hisneede haa e nguurndam daymiingam e weltaare daymiire, sabi timmoode kala huunde fuɗɗiima.

Nii woni, dabba mo yaltata e maayo oo maa ummo, kam e dabba mo yaltata e leydi ndii, woni kadi annabiijo fenaannde oo. O udditan kadi hunuko makko omo haala kufneeje mawɗe e Yaweh, ngam yenande innde mum e galle mum e hoɗɓe e asamaan. Maa rokkete mo waɗde hare e senaareɓe, o foola ɓe, maa rokkete mo kadi laamu e dow kala leñol, e ɗemngal, e ngenndi, sabi o ardoto aduna oo. Dabba leydi ndii kadi maa laamo leydi ndii, o wona Mosiyaak maɓɓe fenaannde, o wona kadi mahoowo suudu dewal maɓɓe. Dabba maayo oo maa laamoro no sariyaaji maɓɓe kufneeji Nuuhu mbiyri nii, nde tawnoo ɓe njiɗi ko momtude kala ko jokkondiri e Alla goongaajo oo e ɓiyum jeyaaɗo gooto oo, Iisaa Almasiihu, e yeeso leydi.
