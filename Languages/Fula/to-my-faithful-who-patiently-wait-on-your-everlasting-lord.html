Seedamaku Ɗiɗo: Isra'iila
Ko Welɗi e ko Haaɗi
Oktoobar 13, 2024

Tilay feewde e Goonɗinɓe am ɓe Muññii e Deƴƴere Joomiraawo mon duumotooɗo!

Nanee konguɗi Joomiraawo mon duumotooɗo, kanko woni Yeesu Almasiihu, ngare e am e ɓerɗe moƴƴe ngam heɓde e jikkude njoɓdi mon ummaade e juuɗe am, sabu goonga wakkati on jooni. Hay e ndeen sahaaji ɗi ndeenaten gila ndeen nde mi jippotoo e dow kammu e sawtu mawɗo, e daande maleyka mawɗo e kowgal Alla (sabu kowgal ngal fiyete), maayɓe nder Almasiihu ummoto e mbaadi nde bonnataa, onon wuuruɓe heddiiɓe on nanngete e duule ɗo potton mbaɗata mbaadi ndi maayataa, ko noon potton ngonndirta e, nanndon e, Joomiraawo mon duumotooɗo haa abada.

Min woni Joomiraawo Joomiraaɓe e Laamɗo Laamɓe, min woni o soodi leydi ndii e ƴiiƴam am kisinoojam. Goonga, goonga, mi yoɓii njoɓdi hakkeeji neɗɗaagu ngam neɗɗaagu ngartirana Yaahwe, sabu miin, Yeesu Nasaraat, ko mi nanondiral jam mon ngal yimɓe ngartirantee e Alla, ko ɗum waɗi mi waɗi Pool winndani Korintuɓe: Jooni non minen ko min nelaaɓe Almasiihu: hono no Alla waajortoo on e amen, min ñaawoo on e innde Almasiihu, ngartiree e Alla. Sabu o waɗii mo hakke ngam men, kanko mo anndaa hakke, ngam en mbaɗee peewal Alla e makko.

Ko ɗum waɗi, ɓiɓɓe am, mi waɗii on peewal Alla e laawol Almasiihu e mon, koolol teddungal, hay e oon ruuhu duumotooɗo ɓiyankaagal mo noddirton ABBA, Baaba, ko noon on njibinaaka kadi ko on ɓiɓɓe Alla duumotooɓe, on takkaama e oon ruuhu laaɓɗo alkawal. Faamee wonde takkol Yaahwe waawaa fusde, waawaa wayleede, e kala takkaaɓe e maggal ina njogii kisal duumotoongal, ko noon faamee ko Yaahwe winndi e anndini hay gooto waawaa waylude. Ko hommbo waawi ittude on e juuɗe am? Ko hommbo waawi foolde Alla mawɗo duumotooɗo, kanko woni Yaahwe, Baaba Annduure? Ko ɗum waɗi on cukkii hoore mon, on kulii mi riiwat on? Kanko, hol e mon riiwatnoo ɓiɓɓe mum, hono no ko huunde famɗunde yeeso Joomiraawo mon e Kisnoowo mon, kanko woni Yeesu Nasaraat.

Goonga, goonga, dokke e noddaango Alla ngalaa tuumeeki, e ɓe ndokkaa nguurndam duumotoongam e anndinde Yeesu ko Joomiraawo tawi ina koolii Alla umminii kam e maayɓe, ɓe ndokketee nguurndam duumotoongam, ɓe mbaɗee peewal Alla e Almasiihu Yeesu Joomiraawo maɓɓe. Jooni so neɗɗo waɗii ndeen seedamaku e ɓernde nde alaa goonɗinal, mi jogotoo takkol Alla guurɗo ngol ngam mi fawde ɓe e am haa timma, ngam ɓe ngara e tuumeeki timmuki e hakkeeji, mbele no abbaare am yanii e leydi kaaƴeeje. Jooni so neɗɗo alaa ruuhu Almasiihu, o jeyaaka e makko. Miin woni o etotooɗo, o jarribooɗo ɓerɗe yimɓe ngam ɓe laɓɓinee e yiite am laɓɓinooje, sabu miin woni o jooɗiiɗo e billo e juuɗe, ina njuppa yiite laɓɓinirɗe, sabu miin woni gaynaako njuppoowo kaaye ngam on keɓa ndonu mon timmungu e am, Almasiihu Yeesu, ko ɗum waɗi, ndewee e golle am, woto calagol njogorɗum ruuhu ɓiyankaagal jibinaaɗo e mon, addoowo peewal e laaɓal goonga. Ko ɗum waɗi mi ñaagii on ɓiɓɓe am, njahdee e rewde ruuhu ngam woto on timmin kare ɓanndu.

Kono heewɓe e mon ina njaɓa ɓerɗe mon njirbina on tawa alaa ko haani, wonde takkol Alla ngol waawi fusde e majjere neɗɗo. Mi naamndii on, yiɗɓe am? Mbele El Shaddai e anndal mum adanngal yejjitii wonde ina nelda mi, Yeesu Nasaraat, ngam hisnude hakke-luuɓe ɓe njahratnoo e hakke caggal kisal? Mbele Yaahwe, Alla mawɗo duumotooɗo, mo haajotaako wasiya hay so tawii ujunnaaje sappo laabi ujunnaaje sappo ina njuɓɓina jappeere mum, kanko gollirɗo kala huunde e wasiya yiɗde mum, faamaani timmaaki Aadama bonɗo, hay e kala yimɓe jibinaaɓe e leydi boɗeehi, ina keɓa ƴiiƴam Aadama?

Alaa ɓiɓɓe am, waawaa wonde Yaahwe faamataa ɗee geɗe, o moƴƴanii on e kala laawol, o rokki on kala laawol ngam kisal mon timmungal ngara e mon timma, yiɗɓe am. Ko ɗum waɗi njahdee e sariya goonɗinal e laawol ruuhu am jibinaaɗo e mon, njahdee e fawaade e ruuhu nguurndam e Almasiihu Yeesu. Sabu e Almasiihu Yeesu sunna waɗataa hay huunde, sunnaaki kadi waɗataa hay huunde, kono ko tagofeere hesere. E kala njahooɓe e fawaade e ndee sariya, jam wonan e maɓɓe, e yurmeende, e Isra'iila Alla. Mbele on faamaani peewɗo wuurtirta ko goonɗinal? Ko ɗum waɗi faamee wonde hay gooto e goonɗinɓe am timmaani e ndee saha, alaa, haa kisal maɓɓe timma. E kala hisnaaɓe takkaaɓe e oon ruuhu laaɓɗo alkawal, ɓe fof ɓe njahrat e hakke, tawa ina anndi walla ina anndaa, e nder ñalawma fof. Ko ɗum waɗi laɓɓinee koye mon e ƴiiƴam am kisinoojam ñalnde kala. Ngaree e am, ngartiree jokkondiral mon e am, ndeen mon ngal, yiɗɓe am. Anndinee hakkeeji mon ñalnde kala, keddee e teddinde daande am deƴƴunde so mi arii e mon ngam hollude on peewal. Mi arii e mon, mi darii yeeso mon ina njiɗa on, ina njiɗa laabi mon ngam on mbaawa yahde e laawol goongawal ngol on noddaaka yahde. Ndeen e nder yaakaare ñalawma kisal mon timmungal ɗo potton daraade timma yeeso Yaahwe e hoore mum ina kolli peewal ngal mi rokkata on timma, sabu miin, Almasiihu Yeesu, ko mi kolliroowo on comci laaɓɗi, daneeji laaɓɗi, tawa alaa taaɓre, alaa mboros, alaa nyiɓre walla hay huunde hono noon.

Ko ɗum waɗi, faamee cukalel am, ñaawoore arii e leydi ndii, hay e ndeen ñaawoore mawnde anndinaande gila ndeen e annabaaɓe ɓooyɓe, nde waktu mum timmungal jooni. Naawoore jibinannde ndee ina jokki e leydi ndii jooni, sabu ñaawoore ina ɗoo, hisnaaɓe am keɓat kisal maɓɓe timmungal. Ko noon geɗe garata, wempeere caggal wempeere. Kala wempeere ina ɓura mawde, ina ɓura tiiɗde bonnugol mum e nde adannde ndee haa nde ɗaɓɓirte-ɗon kisal, ɗaɓɓirte-ɗon Babilon sirlu alaa. Kala wempeere ina adda ñaawoore, kala wempeere ina ɓura tiiɗde e daande e nde adannde ndee. Ko noon ñaawooje am e jeertine am e leydi ndii, sabu mi arata ko no rawaandu leñol Yahuuda nii, e peewal mi arata ngam ñaawoore e haɓde.

Ko noon wonirta, hade musiiba cakkitiiɗo oo yani, e yamiroore am goonɗinɓe am ɓe ndaanii e Almasiihu ummoto e yanaande maɓɓe, goonɗinɓe am wuuruɓe heddiiɓe ina kawrondirta e maɓɓe, njahdon e kammu kammuuji, ɗo on fof potton daraade yeeso jappeere ñaawoore mawnde Almasiihu ngam on fof keɓon ko mbaɗɗon e ɓanndu. Kala ɓe njaɓɓaa ngam yawtude Bema am ngaraani toon ngam ñaaweede sabu hakkeeji maɓɓe, sabu on njaɓii yaafuya hakkeeji e ñalawma neɗɗo hakke-luulɗo hay e ndee ñalawma nde neɗɗo hakke-luulɗo laamotoo leydi ndii, laamu nguu ina joofi. Sabu leydi ndii ko am, Almasiihu Yeesu, e timmal mum, miɗo arta, mi ittat e doole ndonu am e juuɗe jooɗiiɓe, ñaawoore maɓɓe timmunde ina heddii. Yiɗɓe am, faamee wonde on njalti e maayde haa e nguurndam duumotoongam e am, Almasiihu Yeesu Joomiraawo mon. Miin woni o laɓɓinɗo on timma e hakke e ƴiiƴam am kisinoojam, sabu miin woni Paska mon timmuɗo, o sakkii hoore mum e Yaahwe, Alla mawɗo duumotooɗo, laawol gootol, fof, e haa abada ngam hakkeeji neɗɗaagu: ko ɗum waɗi, alaa sakkinde hakke heddiinde.

Ko ɗum waɗi, ɓiɓɓe Alla ina njaha e jappeere am ñaawoore ngam mi ñaawo golle maɓɓe ngam fof keɓa ko keɓi, tawa ko moƴƴi walla ko boni. Ko noon on fof potton addude sadakaaji nguurndam mon kisnaaɗam e leydi ɗi sakkiiɗon e golle Joomiraawo mon duumotooɗo. No winndiraa nii, ko noon waɗirtee, no winndiraa nii: Sabu fondasiyoŋ goɗɗo hay gooto waawaa mahde, so wonaa ko mahiraa, kanko woni Yeesu Almasiihu. So neɗɗo mahii e ndee fondasiyoŋ, kaŋŋe, kaalis, kaaƴe tedduɗe, leɗɗe, huɗo, walla leɗɗe, Golle kala neɗɗo ina peeñta: sabu ñalawma oo ina anndina ɗum, sabu ina feeñtinee e yiite: yiite ndee ina etoo golle kala neɗɗo no siforii. So golle neɗɗo, ɗe mahii e dow mum, ndurrii, o heɓat njoɓdi. So golle neɗɗo njani, o heɓat hasaare, kono o hisnat hoore mum: kono no e yiite nii.

Ko noon wonirta yiɗɓe am, mi njoɓat on timma ngam golle mon e am, geɗe ɗe njani no cukkiri nii e yiite am ñaawoore, on heɓat hasaare, e ɗeen geɗe ɗe ndartii e yiite am ñaawoore, hay e golle ɗe mi rokki on timminde, njoɓdi mawndi wonan on. Sabu ina winndaa: Gite njiyaani, noppi nananaani, hay e ɓernde neɗɗo naataani, geɗe ɗe Alla waɗani ɓe njiɗi mo. Ko noon sifaa ndonu mon e njoɓdi mon, wonde hay neɗɗo gooto e leydi ndii faamataa timma teddungal e maŋngu ndonu ɓiɓɓe Alla duumotooɓe jibinaaɓe e ruuhu Almasiihu, ɗo on jibinaaka kadi ɓiɓɓe Alla haa abada.

Ko ɗum waɗi faamee yiɗɓe am, ñalawma kisal mon timmungal ina ɓadii no miijortoo-ɗon nii, ina yaawi feewde e mon e ndee saha, ñalawma oo ina ɓadii timmude, ɓuri yaawde no miijortoo-ɗon nii. Faamee wonde alaa waktu heddii ngam jeertinde, alaa waktu heddii ngam ñaawoode, alaa waktu heddii ngam haalde e Babilon sirlu, sabu ñaawoore mum ko timmunde, waawaa wayleede. Alaa, wakkatiiji tuumeeki mum mbeltiima, o yejjitii timma daande annabaaɓe am e nelaaɓe am, ina wiya e ɓernde mum no o jaltirta konguɗi am "Mi jooɗii no laamɗo nii, mi wonaa jom suudu, jaŋngo wonat no hannde nii, hannde ko no heñun nii."

Kono e waktu gooto musiiba mum yanat e mum timma, o ummotoo kadi abada. Kono e nokku leydi mawndi nde teddunoo laabi Yaahwe, ɗo yimɓe njaɓunoo Yeesu Nasaraat, ɓe njaɓaani yiɗde hoɗdiiɓe maɓɓe, nokku ɓuuɓɗo heddotoo, ɗo Babilon sirlu laamoraa e waktu gooto, sabu Joomiraawo Alla ñaawooɗo mo ko tiiɗɗo. E no oon musiiba ɓadorii, yiɗɓe am, ko noon kadi kisal mon timmungal, njoɓdi mon, e weltaare mon duumotoonde, ko ɗum waɗi woto njiɗee ñaawoore hakke-luuɓe nde wonaa ngam mon so on njalti e hakkeeji mon timma.

Ko noon ɗaɓɓere gite ndee ɓadorii, ɗo e nder feccere sekonnde on tawata koye mon ina ndarii e suudu laamu Yaahwe e hoore mum, ɗo jappeere mon pawaa, ɗo on njooɗii no laamɓe duumotooɓe e almuɓɓe nii e ndonu Joomiraawo mon duumotooɗo, ɗo potton laamoraade leƴƴi e sawru njamndi, sabu ko noon mi rokkiraa, ko noon mi rokkata ɓe fooli. Ko hommbo woni foolɓe am? Hay e ɓe njalti e hakke timma e tuumeeki timmuki e calagol hakkeeji maɓɓe. Miin, Almasiihu Yeesu, ko mi foolɗo peeje Seytaane timma haa abada, mi acci mo no gaño laaɓɗo, foolaaɗo timma, mo yiite duumotooɗe ndartinaa, ina keɓa, sabu ko noon ndonu maɓɓe, ko noon ɓe poti.

Ko ɗum waɗi, mi waɗii laawol mawngol ngam kala garooɓe e am timma ngam foolde timma peeje Seytaane no mi waɗiri nii. Ko ɗum waɗi ngaree e am timma ngam mi ardora on e laawol goongawal ngol haa e kisal mon timmungal. Nanee daande am onon ɓe njippi, ɓe njani e laawol, sabu neɗɗo peewɗo ina yano laabi jeeɗiɗi, o ummoo kadi, ko ɗum waɗi ummee, ee yimɓe Alla, ngaree e am timma ngam mi jokkondira on e njoɓdi mon e laɓɓinde on e ƴiiƴam am kisinoojam ngam on mbaawa daraade timma ina kolli peewal am. Faamee wonde foolɓe am ina keɓa kala huunde, hay huunde moƴƴere woto rewee e maɓɓe. Ko noon foolɓe am, ina njuɓɓina haa abada, hay no laaɓal asamaan nii: onon ɓe ngartiri heewɓe e peewal, on njuɓɓinat no koode nii, haa abada. Jooni anndude ɗum ɓiɓɓe am, jooni ko wakkati ngam accude caɗeele ndee aduna, ndeen e am, keddee e laaɓal no debbo laaɓɗo nii e ƴiiƴam am kisinoojam.

Ko ɗum waɗi, yiɗɓe am, ndaree e yeeso musiiba garoowo, sabu on nji'ii ñalawma bonɗo oo ina arta, miin woni o udditɗo gite toowɓe e ɓernde ngam on mbaawa yiyde goonga aduna mon, woto on naata e majjere tiiɗnde nde wonaa ngam mon. Faamee wonde miin, Yeesu Almasiihu, ko mi waɗoowo bumɓe yiyde, ko ɗum waɗi, ngaree e am ngam mi wujja gite mon e safaare gite, ngam on mbaawa yiyde feccere ndee aduna bonɗo no siforii e nder mum. Ko ɗum waɗi woto kul ñaawoore hakke-luuɓe, sabu ndeen ñaawoore wonaa ngam mon yiɗɓe am, alaa ñaawoore wonaa ngam hisnaaɓe, sabu on hisnaama e tikkere Jibinaaɗo e tikkere Alla, ko ɗum waɗi ndaree e kisal mon, yiɗɓe am, sabu ñalawma oo ina ɓadii, ina yaawi feewde e mon e kala sekonnde.

Eey, oon sahaaji kisal mon timmungal arii e mon yiɗɓe am, oon sahaaji duumotooɗi ina ɓadii, ina ɓadorii on e kala sekonnde. Mi anndinii e ruuhuuji mon, mi hollii maandeeji dow e maandeeji e leydi, mi waɗii kaawisaaji e kammu, mi wurtinii musiibaaji e ñaawoore e hakke-luuɓe, ngam finndinde ɓe ndaanii ngam ɓe mbaawa faamde jooni ko wakkati ngam kisal timmungal ɓiɓɓe Alla Jom Baawɗe, hay e debbo am e ɓanndu am.

Woto njahdee e maɓɓe e laabi maɓɓe ɗi ngalaa goonɗinal, sabu ɓe njiɗi ko ƴettude, ɓe njiɗi ko anndinde maandeeji keewɗi e kaawisaaji ɗi njiytortee e kammu, ɗi heewɓe njiyti e junngo Yaahwe e hoore mum. Mbele on yejjitii Jenesis, yiɗɓe am? Sabu ina winndaa: Alla wiyi, yo annooraaji ngone e kammu asamaan, ngam seerndude ñalawma e jamma, yo ɗi ngone ngam maandeeji, e sahaaji, e ñalɗi e duuɓi. Yo ɗi ngone ngam annooraaji e kammu asamaan ngam rokkude annoora e leydi: ko noon waɗiri. Ko ɗum waɗi, faamee wonde Yaahwe ina laamii fof, alaa huunde jaɓaande e tagofeere mum nde o jaɓaani, nde o anndaa. Ko ɗum waɗi, woto njahdee e maɓɓe e miijooji maɓɓe ɗi ngalaa goonɗinal, wonde neɗɗaagu jooni ina jogii, ina wayla kammuuji, hay so tawii neɗɗaagu ina etoo foolde Alla, golle mum ina njoofna e majjere.

Kono, ɓe ndonki ɓeɗii ɓe etaade suuɗude maandeeji am e kala kunngi keewɗi ɗi Yaahwe waɗi e dow kammuuli ko fayti e dewgal jom-goro e jom-suudu mum. Nde wonnoo, mbelee e teddungal Alla, nganndee wonde kala ko ɓe ndonki ɓeɗii ɓe njaɓaa waɗde, ko ngam wallude anniyaaji Alla duumiiɗi tan, sabu laabi makko ina kollita hay añɓe makko ina ɗoftoo daande makko. Kono wota on njuum, Yaahwe laamiiɗo dow kala huunde, alaa ko waɗata so wonaa e yurmeende makko, e yurmeende makko, e ñaawoore makko duumiinde e dow yimɓe tuubaani e ɓe ndonki ɓeɗii. Hay ñaawoore mawnde ndee, ndi nganndiraa gila law, ndi jooni ina heddii seeɗa udditee e dow leydi ndii e mbaadi tiiɗndi.

Ñalnde ndee arii e mooɗon, yiɗɓe am, miɗo ari ngam mooɗon! Hay so tawii wonɓe e nokkuuji ɗi ngonaa mum en ɓee ina njiɗi suuɗude teddungal am, mi renndataa teddungal am e goɗɗo, ɗum waɗi tiiɗnaare geɗe lo’uɗe e miskineeɓe ɗee ko meere ngam suuɗude feeñde Almasiihu Iisaa ngam jom-suudu am e terɗe am. Alaa, miɗo haɗaa, miɗo suuɗee hay seeɗa, winndere ndee kala maa anndu feeñde am, hay so tawii wonɓe e laamuuji bonɗi ɗii ina njiɗi suuɗude feeñde am e hare e njiimaandi maɓɓe fenaande. Faamde wonde alaa hoɓɓe ummoriiɓe e adunaaji fenaande goɗɗi, kono ko bonɓe riiwaaɓe e asamaanuuji haa abada, ɓe cikkatnoo ko kam en ngoni fof, ɓe ngari ko ngam rewrude e laawol.

Faamde wonde feeñde am fotaani e garol am ɗiɗaɓol, ko sirlu suuɗiiɗo e nder sirlu mawɗo Alla mo miin, Yeesu Almasiihu, udditiri nelaaɗo am Pool. Garol am ɗiɗaɓol anndaama, ina heɗitoroo gila e jamaanuuji jooni, ina wadda ummital nuunɗuɓe. E feeñde am, miɗo ara ngam mooɓtude seniiɓe am e hoore am ngam yoɓde ɓe, mi resnda jom-suudu am, mi ñaamda e hoɓɓe am tedduɓe. Ko e feeñde am, mi nanngata e hoore am nuunɗuɓe ngari e am e tuubu timmuɗo, mi nawta ɓe e kisal asamaanuuji haa ñaawoore e dow leydi ndii heɓa, sabu waktuuji ñaawoore ngarii e winndere ndee kala, haa teeŋti noon e bonɓe.

Faamde wonde garol am ɗiɗaɓol ko ɗo mi arata e hisɓe am e 144 000 am, e konu asamaan kala ngam halkude laamu Seytaane fof, laamu makko meeɗataa artude. E garol am ɗiɗaɓol, kala neɗɗo ina yiyata, kala neɗɗo ina anndata, alaa fenaande waawnde rewde hay gooto e ndee hiirde. Seytaane wonaa laamɗo, bonɓe ɓee ngonaa allaaɓe kono ko fenaande. Seytaane maa haɓɓee, werlee e luggeende duuɓi ujunere ngam wota o rewru leƴƴi kadi, haa o yaltinee e sahaaji makko seeɗa, o wona toon o haɓɓaa e jolokere mawnde e nder torra e kulol ngol o heɓata e ndonu makko e nder ndiyam yiite e suluf, ɗo o meeɗataa daɗde. Caggal sahaaji makko seeɗa, o werletoo fof e nder ndiyam ɓuuɓɗam ɓuuɓɗam, ɓuuɓɗam ɓuuɓɗam, ɓuuɓɗam ɓuuɓɗam, ɓuuɓɗam ɓuuɓɗam. Ndeen ñaawoore jooɗorde laamɗo daneejo mawnde ndee maa waɗ, hay so tawii Yaahwe tagii asamaan keso e leydi kesere, o waɗa kala huunde hesere e nder timmal duumingal e timmal.

Ko noon winndiraa, ko noon kadi maa timminiree ɗoon e ɗoon, mi jippotoo mi ummoo e asamaanuuji e wullaango mawngo e daande malaa’ika mawɗo e buunndu Alla, ndeen buunndu mawndu nduu laaɓataa, ko ndu sakkitiinde e yonta am yurmeende no winndere ndee waylirta ngam hawrude e ñaawoore mum arande e tiiɗnde. Mi mooɓtata cuɓaaɓe am ummoraade e nokkuuji nayi leydi ndii, hay ɓeen fof nannguɓe e Almasiihu, ee, ɓeen fof nannguɓe e Almasiihu, ɓe njuɓɓinaama e ruuhu ceniiɗo oo. Ñaawooje jibinannde ɗe ngonaa daɗndooji ɗee maa ndokku leydi ndii jibinde ɓe nannguɓe ɓee, kono ɗum fotaani maayde ina jogii hay sekonde gooto. Ndeen onon wuuruɓe, heddiiɓe ɓee, on nanngete e weeyo e maɓɓe ngam kawrude e am e duule, e nder ŋakkere yiitere on nji’ata.

Ee, on nji’ata e gite mooɗon teddungal duumingal Alla mawɗo duumiiɗo, hay so tawii ko Baaba annoore yiɗɗo wonde e mooɗon, o waɗanii ndeeɗoo sahaaji haa abada sabu on ngonnoo e ɓernde Yaahwe haa abada. On nji’ata mi, Kisnotooɗo mooɗon duumiiɗo, mo njoɓɗo hoore am fof e mette no mi sadakiri hoore am ngam on nguura yeeso Baaba annoore, baaba annoore winndere ndee. Ee, on nji’ata mi e gite mooɗon, hay onon fof jogiiɓe goonɗinal e Joomiraaɗo mooɗon tedduɗo mo nganndaaka tawo e gite mooɗon.

On nji’ata e gite mooɗon teddungal am timmungal ngal Yaahwe hokki e ndonu am ngal mi renndata e foolɓe am, ngal mi yoɓata kala garɗo e am e golle daɗndooje e jarribo yiite. Goonga, goonga, on nji’ata e gite mooɗon konu asamaan, heewɓe e maɓɓe neldaama ngam gollanaade ronooɓe hisde duumingal. On ndarotoo e timmal timmungal, hay so tawii on tagaama e timmal asamaan, on ndarotoo, on kaawaa ko battane mooɗon duumiiɗe ngaddata e ko jawdi teddungal ndonu am e nder seniiɓe firti e mooɗon.

Faamde yiɗɓe am, (hay ɓeen fof yiɗɓe nguurndam mooɗon haa laakara) hay onon yaltuɓe e salaare mooɗon e tuubu timmuɗo: Battane mooɗon duumiiɗe ko fuɗɗoode mooɗon duumiinde nde njahraton e sekonde mooɗon gadane nguurndam duumingal, alaa ko bonnata on kadi hay seeɗa. Sabu on tagaama timmuɗo e timmal ceniiɗo, on meeɗataa bonde kadi sabu on tagaama mo bonnataa.

Haa abada on ngollanto Yaahwe, haa abada on ngonndata e am kala ɗo njahmi. Mi artiranta on fof ko mbuuba ñaami e fof ko Seytaane wujji e mooɗon. On njoɓete fof ngam golle mooɗon e golle yiɗde ɗe kolliɗon e innde am nde ngollaniɗon seniiɓe e golloraade. Men ñaamata ko ɓuri heewde e leydi ndii e laamu am duuɓi ujunere ɗo laamoto-ɗon e am hono laamɓe am e almuɓɓe am, laamu nuunɗungu ngoo wonata haa abada.

Ko miin laɓɓini on fof e salaare e nder ƴiiƴam am cafroowo.

Ko miin woni Paska mooɗon timmuɗo, mo njoɓɗo hoore am e Yaahwe Alla mawɗo duumiiɗo.

Ko miin udditi gite ɓe ɓerɗe mum en toowɗe.

Ko miin woni gaño ɓuuɓnoowo yiite ngam on njotta e ndonu mooɗon timmungu e nder am, Almasiihu Iisaa.

Mi neldaama e I AM mawɗo, miɗo ari ngam mooɗon!

Ko miin neldi konnguɗi am e maccuɗo am mo ummini mi gila e cukaagu mum, mo mi suɓii ngam anniyaaji am, mo mi suuɗii e kaɓirgal am ngam yonta hono oo, e maccuɗo am konnguɗi am duumiiɗi neldaama. Konnguɗi linjiila duumiiɗi ɗii maa ndesndee e masiibaaji garooji ɗii, maa keɓee e gite, e noppi, e ɓerɗe ɓe neldaa ɓee sabu miin, Yeesu Almasiihu, hay Almasiihu Iisaa, miɗo anndina ɗee geɗe e laamu timmuɗo e doole Yaahwe e hoore mum, o renndini jooɗorde makko e ɓiɗɗo makko gooto jibinaaɗo ngam e juuɗe am doole tagngo ngoo artiree haa abada e tagɗo ngoo, barkinaaɗo haa abada, ee e Aamiin.
