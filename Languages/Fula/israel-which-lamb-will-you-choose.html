Seedeeji ɗiɗo: Isra'iila
Ko Welɗi e ko Haɗɗi
Noowammbar 13, 2024

E an yo Isra'iila! Njawdiri ndi ndeen suɓotoɗaa?

Nanee Konnguɗi Yahweh, Miin Mawɗo oo, njehelee e daande Konnguɗi am ɗiin, mbele keɓon hakkilantaagal, mbele kadi neɗɗo hakkilante e mon oo ɓeydoo hakkilantaagal mum.

Ndaaree! Mi arii ngam haalande on ko arata, mbele anndon goonga Konnguɗi am, mbele kadi keɓon hisde timmunde e nder Yesuwa Almasiihu, on wonɗo goonga Almasiihu mon tigi, Laamɗo mon, Joomiraaɗo mon, e Hooreejo Limaamiiɓe mon haa abada e dow laawol Melkisedek.

Ko ɗum waɗi, mi yamirii on yo on neɗɗo mo e ɓerɗe mon fof, tee on laɓɓina ɓerɗe mon e nder ɗooftaare! Sabu, ko Yesuwa Almasiihu, hono Iisaa Almasiihu e ɗemngal Engele, on rokkaaka tan ngam hisnude Isra'iila, kono kadi ngam hisnude leñol Aadama ngol fof, sabu kala nodduɗo innde Joomiraaɗo haa timmoode hisay, tawa ko o Isra'iilanke walla o jeyaaɗo e leƴƴi goɗɗi, tee Yesuwa Almasiihu hisinay wuurɓe heddiiɓe e Isra'iila, hay e leñol Aadama, ɓe naatoyoo e laamu duuɓi ujunere nguu.

Nii woni, miin rokki ko leñol Aadama waawaano rokkude ngam hisnude hoore mum, sabu leñol Aadama waawataano heɓde sottorgal timmungal so wonaa e nder ƴiiƴam njawdiri ndiin. Sabu ko miin, Yahweh, Miin Mawɗo oo, rokki on Juulde Mooftorde mon timmunde ndee, laawol gootol, jooni, e haa abada, tee sadak makko oo jaɓaama haa pooma, ko ɗuum waɗi, sadak goɗɗo alaa ko heddii, e an yo Isra'iila.

No winndiraa e nder Ibraninko'en nii, nii mi haalanta on kadi jooni: Sabu so ƴiiƴam ga'i, e mboson, e ndoondi ga'al laɓɓinoowo neɗɗo tuunɗo, ina laɓɓina ɓanndu makko, haa teeŋti noon e ƴiiƴam Almasiihu, mo e dow Ruuhu duumotooɗo oo, rokki hoore mum tawa alaa gafe to Alla, hol no ɗam ɓurata laɓɓinirde hakkillaaji mon e golle maayɗe, mbele ndewon Alla guurɗo oo? Ko ɗuum waɗi mi wasiyoo on, e an yo Isra'iila, njaɓee sadak am oo, sabu sadak goɗɗo ngam bakkaatuuji alaa ko heddii!

Faamee noon Isra'iila, wonde hay gooto waawaa ɓadtaade Alla Duumotooɗo Jaafaaro oo rewrude e Muusaa kadi, sabu, mi waɗii Yesuwa Almasiihu laawol ngol, goonga on, e nguurndam ngam, tee hay gooto waawaa arde to Yahweh so wonaa rewrude e Yesuwa Almasiihu!

Mi yamirii on ɗooftaare e daande makko ndee, hay e nder Tawreeta, ɗo daande Muusaa e hoore mum wullotoo haa hannde, ko seede e dow mon, e laabi mon ɗi nganndaaka, ɗi ngalaa goonɗinal, ko wonaa ɗooftaare, on njiɗi ko mahde suudu dewal waɗaandu e juuɗe yimɓe, no Esawu majjiniri on nii! Sabu on njaarii suudu dewal mahndu ndu tagɗo on mahnoo nduun, hono suudu dewal waɗaandu tawa juuɗe alaa, hono suudu dewal ndi Yahweh e hoore mum mahani Isra'iila Alla, ndi Iisaa Almasiihu woni kaaƴe mum ɓurɗe teeŋtude. Ko ɗuum waɗi, ngoppee golle mon, fooftoɗon e golle Almasiihu mon tigi, mi rokka on Asabar.

Ko ɗum waɗi mi yamirii on, e an yo Isra'iila, tee ko miin rokki Konnguɗi ɗi Muusaa haalannoo baabiraaɓe mon to Sinay ɗiin, Konnguɗi ɗi mi yamirta on ɗooftaare mum en haa abada, hay e nder salaare mon e tiiɗɗe ɓerɗe mon. Nii Muusaa haaliri ngam ɗooftaare yimɓe fof: Joomiraaɗo Alla mon umminanay on Annabi hono am, ummoriiɗo e mon, hay e banndiraaɓe mon: ko kanko neɗotoɗon!

Sabu Muusaa ko cakaajo Aadi adanndi ndiin (ndi mbinndumi e hoore am to Sinay) rokkaandi ngam hollude bakkaat e nder ɓanndu, Aadi ndi jooni ina jalba e yeeso Iisaa Almasiihu, hono Yesuwa Almasiihu, on wonɗo Joomiraaɗo mon tigi e Almasiihu mon! Kono Sariya Muusaa ko ɗowdi geɗe garooje, geɗe timminaaɗe e nder Almasiihu Iisaa, tee mi waɗii mo ruuhu rokkowo nguurndam, tee Iisaa Nasaranke oo anndaaka kadi e dow ɓanndu!

No timminiraa e juuŋgo am doole, hono juuŋgo YHVH mawɗo oo e hoore mum, ni mi winndiri sariyaaji am e alluuje kaaƴe to Sinay, ɗo mi rokki Muusaa sariyaaji am, mbele Isra'iila wuura e yeeso am. Kono, ko rokkaanoo Isra'iila to Sinay ko feloore bakkaat e nder ɓanndu, mbele Isra'iila heɓa jannginoowo ardooɗo ɗum en to Almasiihu mum en tigi, hono Yesuwa Almasiihu mo neldimi ngam hisde mon timmunde.

Nii woni, sariya Muusaa ko moƴƴo, ko nuunɗuɗo, ko timmuɗo, ko laaɓɗo, tee kala neɗɗo huɗɗo sariya Muusaa, yo ñaawee heen e ñaawoore tiiɗnde wonnde e les kuddi e njoɓdi sariya Muusaa. Kono ko moƴƴi, ko nuunɗi, ko timmi, ko laaɓi koo, addanii maayde kala ñaawoowo heen e bakkaat e nder ɓanndu, sabu ɗo sariya alaa, bakkaat limaaka, ko ɗum waɗi sariya Muusaa rokkaama ngam hollude e felde bakkaat e nder ɓanndu.

Kono ko e ruuhu sariya oo, wonaa e alkulal ngal, neɗɗaagu heɓata yurmeende e nder yiɗde am e mum en, sabu alkulal sariya ngal ina adda tiiɗalla (Mbele on njanngaani miɗo yiɗi yurmeende ko ɓuri sadak?) ko ɗuum waɗi e nder yiɗde am e sukaaɓe Isra'iila ɓe njiɗmi hisnude so on ɗoftike Yesuwa Almasiihu, mi yamirii sadak sabu so ƴiiƴam woppaaka, alaa yaafuya bakkaatuuji. Nii yimɓe am ndeeniraa e juuŋgo Muusaa haa Almasiihu ari, hay so tawii heewɓe e mon ngartiima e allaaji fenaande, njiɗi kubbeeje mon ɓaleeje ɗee, geɗe bonɗe sanne e yeeso am, e an yo Isra'iila, ko ɗum waɗi ceertee heen jooni jooni!

Faamee noon tikkere am e dow mon, e an yo Isra'iila, sabu ko miin woni YHVH, miin Mawɗo oo, hay kaŋko woni Alla Duumotooɗo Nuunɗuɗo, mo tampataa, tee alaa ko saɗti e juuŋgo am doole ngam waɗde. Ko miin, El Shadday, Alla Jom Baawɗe oo, tagi tagoore ndee e hakkilantaagal am duumingal, tee ko e doole ruuhu am doole oo, geɗe fof njokkondiri e nder ɗooftaare e daande am.

Ko miin woni Elohim mon, mo ujunnaaje ujunnaaje capanɗe ujunnaaje ndarii e sara jappeere am, hono konu malaa'ika'en doole, gollotooɓe, ɓe golle mum en ngoni yiite jalbooje, ɓe nganndi hakkilantaagal, ɓe tagaama no feewi, no tagir-mi ɓe e yiɗde am e mum en nii! Tee e nder yiɗde maɓɓe e am, ɓe ɗooftii no feewi yiɗde am duumingal, tee mi rokki Yesuwa Almasiihu laamu e dow maɓɓe fof.

Kono, miin YHVH Mawɗo oo, hay kaŋko woni mo konu malaa'ika'en keewngu haa alaa keerol ndarii e sara mum, mi alaa haaju e wasiya e nder hakkilantaagal am duumingal, tee mi laamii tagoore am, mi gollat geɗe FOF e dow wasiya yiɗde am, tee e nder annduɓe ɓurɓe mawnude e duuniyaaru nduu fof, alaa ɓurɗo miin Mawɗo oo hakkilantaagal! Ko ɗum waɗi, nanee daande am, ndeeƴnine ɗemɗe mon so on cuɓiima nguurndam duumingam e dow yiite njoɓdi ndi nyifataa, e an yo Isra'iila, sabu ko ngol suɓngo kala neɗɗo foti waɗde.

E nder hakkilantaagal am duumingal, mi neldi Iisaa Nasaranke oo to Isra'iila, hay so tawii Yahuuda ina laaminoo e les njiimaandi Pontiyus Pilaat, hay so tawii mawɓe limaamiiɓe e Sanhedrin tuumi mo e dow fenaande, tee e juuɗe bonɓe njawdiri Alla ndiin waraa, fasaa, mbele ɓanndu bakkaat ndi haɓɓi Aadama ndiin halkee e kala ɓe nguurdata e goonɗinal, sabu nuunɗuɓe nguurday e goonɗinal! Ko miin, hay El Shadday (Alla Jom Baawɗe oo) waɗi geɗe fof to kammu e to leydi e juuɗe Almasiihu Iisaa doole, hono Yesuwa Almasiihu, tee ko kanko woni Muudo Leñol Yahuuda mo neldatmi ngam ñaawoyde e haɓde, e hisnude goonɗinɓe e ɗoftiiɓe. Ko ɗuum waɗi, nanee Konnguɗi am e nder ɗooftaare e Almasiihu mon tigi, sabu alaa goɗɗo!

Faamee noon tikkere am e dow mon, e an yo Isra'iila, wonde e nder wumre mon seeɗa ndi ndonduɗon e baabiraaɓe mon tiiɗɓe ɓerɗe, ɓe daande mum en Talmud teddinirton ko ɓuri Elohim mon. Eey, on teddinii aadaaji annduɓe mon e hakkillaaji mon ko ɓuri Konnguɗi Miin Mawɗo oo, on wonɗo tagoowo duumotooɗo, tagoowo kala ko tagaa! Sabu ko e YHVH geɗe fof tagaa, geɗe wonɗe to kammu, e geɗe wonɗe to leydi, geɗe yiyateeɗe e geɗe yiyataake: tawa ko jappeere laamu, walla laamuuji, walla laamɓe, walla baawɗe, geɗe fof ko miin tagi ɗe, e ngam am, tee miɗo adii geɗe fof, tee e nder YHVH geɗe fof njokkondiri.

Kono, on cikkat ko on wasiyaaji am, on noddirta koye mon e dow majjere yimɓe am cuɓaaɓe, tawa on njaarii Konnguɗi am e nder salaare nde alaa goonɗinal? Oo Isra'iila, on ngartii e tooruuji muumɗi ɓernde e konnguɗi fenaande Esawu, ɓe wasiyaaji mum en njiɗɗon ko ɓuri Alla Duumotooɗo Nuunɗuɗo, hono Elohim mon! On taccitii e nder wumre seeɗa sabu e nder Muusaa ɓerɗe mon ina cuurɗinaa. Kono, so ɓerɗe mon ngartii e Almasiihu mon tigi, cuurde ndee ittatee, ko wonaa wumre seeɗa, on njiyay!

Hol wonɗo on, e an yo Isra'iila, haa mbaɗton koye mon wasiyaaji Yahweh? Kono ko wonaa Konnguɗi Yahweh rokkaaɗi e yeeso Yesuwa Almasiihu, on ngoonɗinii fenaande ndokkaandi e aadaaji mawɓe mon, wonnooɓe e les njiimaandi Esawu, ɓe binndanɗe mum en kaɓɓirɗon koye mon e Esawu no nanndi nii, tee e Esawu on kaɓɓaa! Mi winndii no laaɓiri ngam Muusaa rokka baabiraaɓe mon to Sinay: Kala ko mi yamirii on, ndeeƴnee ngam waɗde ɗum: woto on ɓeydu heen, woto on ustu heen. Mi winndini kadi e hakkilantaagal Sulaymaana, hakkilantaagal dariingal haa abada: Kala konngol Elohim ko laaɓngol: ko kanko woni gaafaangal wonannde ɓe koolii mo. Woto ɓeydu heen hay huunde, mbele o felataa ma, tee keɓaaɗaa ko a fenoowo.

Oo Isra'iila, yo on tuubnu ɓerɗe mon e nder ɗooftaare e Konnguɗi rokkaaɗi ngam hisde mon e weltaare mon duumingal, so on ɗoftike kadi Konnguɗi tagɗo on! Ko miin artirta on, naatna on e nder galle duumiiɗo, wonɗo e les juuŋgo doole Gaynaako am Mawɗo mo rokku-mi geɗe fof! Hono Yesuwa Almasiihu, gaynotooɗo Isra'iila Alla tigi e nder yiɗde mum ɗooftaare e daande am, hono misaalu mo njidɗon. Sabu Yesuwa Almasiihu ko gorko gooto tan ɗoftuɗo no feewi yiɗde YHVH, miin Mawɗo oo, mo welnata mi e mum haa pooma, ko ɗuum waɗi mi waɗii mo ruuhu rokkowo nguurndam, ko ɗuum waɗi nanee on mo neldimi ngam mon, sabu alaa goɗɗo.

On tiiɗinii ɓerɗe mon haa tiiɗi no Kaaƴal Gonngal ngal Synagogue Setan haɓɓi ngal nii, sabu on njaarii konnguɗi wasiyaaji am rokkaaɗi e yeeso Almasiihu Iisaa, mo njahraton e dow anndude, ko noon njahraton e goonga mon gooto tan ngam artireede e hisde haa abada. Ko noon ndari-ɗon e wumre, e ɓoornaade, e hersude e bakkaat mon, on cikkat e dow anndude on ngonti wasiyaajo YHVH, Miin Mawɗo oo!

Ko ɗum waɗi miɗo rokka on faamde e nder wumre mon seeɗa ndee, wonde on taccitii e kaaƴe am ɓurɗe teeŋtude ɗe mahooɓe mon njarii, hono kaaƴe am taccitorde. Sabu Yesuwa Almasiihu ko kaaƴe ɓurɗe teeŋtude, hono kaaƴal gonngal tigi ngal, hono Iisaa Nasaranke mo njahraton e nder salaare, hay so tawii mi suɓiima on ngam hoore am, ko on yimɓe teeŋtuɓe, tee ɓernde am ina mettina.

Heɗee, heɗee, onon Isra'iila, paamee wonde ko Miin woni Jom senaare senuɗo sariya Am duumotooɗo, hono laaɓal anniya Am duumotooɗo e feere Am kisnugol duumotooɗo nde peewnumi e dow peewnugol anniya Am timmuɗo duumotooɗo, e dow alluhaaji kaaƴe, ngam ɗooftaare Isra'iila to Sinay! Sabu ɗum, ko Miin woni binndoowo sariya Am e dow alluhaaji ɓalli ɓerɗe mon sabu e nder yiɗde Am e mon, hay so tawii ko doole Am ɓurɗe famɗude ina mbaawi senaade sariya Am e dow alluhaaji mon kaaƴe, kadi e neema Am e yurmeende Am feeñnde e yeeso Yeesu Almasiihu, Mi woppat alluhaaji mon kaaƴe e nder loope lohnoowo, ngam on mbaawa tagireede haa abada e nder nuunɗal e senuyeendi goonga, hono no njanngorimi e alkule Am to Baabiraaɓe mon Mi waɗat nii.

Sabu ɗuum, onon Isra'iila, mi yamirii on yo on ɗofto daande Yeesu Almasiihu sabu alaa kisnugol e innde woɗnde so wonaa Yeesu Almasiihu Nuunɗuɗo, mo on noddata e majjere tooru goyim en sabu on ngonɗon e maccungaagu to Esawu!

Kono noon winndaa, noon kadi waɗi: Gila e Ibraahiima haa e Isaaka, nde Yaaquuba e Esawu ndañaa e mum, juuɗe Yaaquuba nanngi yeeso tenngu Esawu. Sabu Esawu ko joofnirde aduna, Yaaquuba ko fuɗɗoode mum rewtunde heen. Juuɗe neɗɗo ina hakkunde tenngu e juuɗe. Kono noon, jooni mi yaltinta Esawu e Yaaquuba, sabu Esawu ko Juulirde Seytaane, ɓe ngoni tooke bonɗe kebbini leydi alkule laabi keewɗi, Isra'iila kadi hebbini Kanaannaaɓe.

Hono no winndiraa nii waɗirtee, wiyi Jom mawɗo oo: Sabu ndeeɗoo ñalawma ina ara, ina huɓɓa no fonngo nii, e kala mawnikiniiɗo, e kala baɗoowo bonannde maa laato no ñommbi nii, ñalawma garoowo oo ina huɓɓa ɓe, wiyi Joomiraawo konuuji, alaa ko heddotoo e maɓɓe, so wonaa ɗaɗi e calɗi. Kono onon hulɓe Innde Am, Ɓiy Nuunɗal ina ummoo, ina woodi cellal e bileeje mum, ina yaha, ina mawna no ñalli jawdi nii.

Hono no Yeesu Almasiihu mooɓtiri nuunɗuɓe e nder eklesiya, hay so tawii ko ɓanndu mum, e jom suudu mum, mi yaltinta Yeesu Almasiihu ngam seerndude Esawu e Yaaquuba, Yaaquuba ina hisnee hay so tawii ko e jarribooji yontere cappanɗe jeeɗiɗi Daniyel. Sabu ɗuum, woto won jeenoowo, walla neɗɗo bonɗo no Esawu nii, mo yeeyi ndonu mum sabu geɓal gootal e teewu. Sabu on anndii no caggal ɗuum kadi nde o foti ronude barke, o salaa: sabu o heɓaani nokku ngam tuubde, hay so tawii o ɗaɓɓiri ndeen barke e gonɗi.

Nii woni neɗɗaagu ina hakkunde juuɗe Yaaquuba e tenngu Esawu, so wonaa Esawu ina ɗaɓɓa waɗde Yaaquuba gaññeeje timmuɗe, ɗe mi accataa. Nii woni, denndaangal hoɗɓe e leydi heddiiɓe e caɗeele Yaaquuba ngam muñde yiite laɓɓinoowo oo, maa laatoɓe e leñol Yaaquuba rewrude e Yeesu Nasaraatu, ɓe ngoni ɓiɓɓe ndonu, walla maa ɓe calete e nder Esawu yeeyi ndonu mum sabu mburu. Sabu ɗuum, neɗɗo tagoore maa noddu innde Yeesu Nasaraatu haa joofnirde mum ngam o ummitine e nder Isra'iila Alla, walla o yeeyata baawɗe mum e ndonu duumotooɗo sabu mburu dokkaaɗo e juuɗe Barabas, sabu Barabas ardototo Juulirde Seytaane, kanko woni gaaylorde mbeewa mawka.

Kono noon, pittaali ɗii maa mbaɗee e leydi ndii ngam nanngude Yaaquuba haa abada, Barabas ina adda fenaande mawnde e laamu mum, o waɗat bonanndeeji keewɗi e leydi e maayo. Kono noon, no yiite hare huɓɓiri, Barabas ina ummoo, ina wiyee o ummitinaama ngam timminde anniyaaji Alla, o wiyata geɗe keewɗe bonɗe e bonɗe. O addat calɗi oliiw mum, o wiya hoore mum ko Nuuhu, o wuuri ngam hisnude Aduna e ñaawoore anniyanoonde worɓe bonɓe.

Sabu kulle leydi ɗee, ɗe ngoni annabaaɓe mum en fenaande, ina adda feere mum en sabu ko gaaylorde jogiinde luuɗe ɗiɗi, ina haala no mbeewa mawka nii. Laamu mum maa won e sifaaji ɗiɗi, sabu omo ɗaɓɓa hawtude denndaangal neɗɗaagu e les laamu mbeewa mawka, hono ibliis goonga oo. Sabu Barabas arataa ngam laamaade tan, hay so tawii o wiyata ko kanko e hoore mum woni Alla, kono omo adda alkulal feccere nde luuɗe ɗiɗi laamotoo maayo e leydi, gooto e ɗiɗo ɓee ko luulndu tokosru Daniyel. Kono mi alkulanii Isra'iila so feccitotaako kadi, e dow laamuuji mum ɗiɗi alaa feere so wonaa Yeesu Almasiihu hawtinta Isra'iila timmuɗo haa abada.

(Kono noon waɗata, yiɗɓe am, paamee ko mi timminirta e Yeesu Almasiihu: Mi ittat ɓiɓɓe Isra'iila e nder heeferɓe, to ɓe njahi, mi mooɓta ɓe e kala bannge, mi adda ɓe e leydi mum en. Mi waɗta ɓe leñol gootol e leydi ndii, e dow pelle Isra'iila, laamɗo gooto laamotoo ɓe fof: so ɓe ngonataa kadi leñol ɗiɗol, so ɓe peccitotaako kadi e laamuuji ɗiɗi. So ɓe tuunotaako kadi e tooruuji mum en, walla e bonanndeeji mum en, walla e kala tooñanngeeji mum en: kono mi hisnat ɓe e denndaangal nokkuuji hoɗorde mum en ɗo ɓe mbaɗi bone, mi laɓɓina ɓe: nii ɓe laatoroo yimɓe am, mi laatoo Alla maɓɓe.)

Gooto oo ko kulle maayo ngoo winndaaɗe e mum: Sabu o yiɗaa rewɓe, o hormaaki hay alla gooto: sabu o mawnintoo hoore mum dow kala huunde. Mi naamndii on jooni, njeeɓee so tawii gorko ina reedu? Kono debbo makko ina yiyee no debbo jogiiɗo ɓiɓɓe nii, hay so tawii ɓe ngonaa ɓiɓɓe makko. Kulle maayo ɗee maa ummo, hawtii e anniya e kulle leydi ɗee, ɓe mbaɗata ko no ɓe ngoni añɓe nii, ɓe peeñata ko no ɓe ceertuɓe e miijo e diine nii.

Kono ɓe fena, ɓe mbaɗata ko no ɓe mbaɗata nii, baaba maɓɓe ko mbeewa mawka goota, tagɗo ɗee kulle ɗiɗi bonɗe ngam anniyaaji mum bonɗi, hay so tawii ko ngam halkude Aadama ngam o wujja leydi ndii haa abada ngam hoore mum, waawaa timminde peeje mum bonɗe sabu leydi ko Joomiraawo e timmal mum, wiyi YHVH, Jom mawɗo oo!

Sabu Barabas ina adda laamuuji ɗiɗi hawteteeɗi e les laamu mbeewa mawka, jam fenaande maa ɓe njogoo nde ɓe alkulanii nguurndam duumotooɗam e cellal e jam so tawii on njaɓii maande maɓɓe, on naati e maɓɓe nde ɓe njillondiri njamndi e loope kono ɓe takkondirtaa. Paamee, onon fof ɓe miijotoo hawtude haa abada e tooñooɓe ɓee rewrude e maande maɓɓe, ɓe mbiyata fenaande keewnde, ɓe mbiyata ko safaara kaawisa, dokkoowo nguurndam e cellal, ina haawnii e peewnugol mum!

Kono kala jaɓɗo tooke maɓɓe maa waylu hoore mum haa abada, hay so tawii ko waylo ngo alaa ko sellinta. Sabu tooñde natal YHVH ngo Aadama tagiraa ko hawtude hoore mum e bone tooñooɓe ɓe mbaɗata ko no ɓe ngoni allaaji nii, ɓe ngonaa, kala baɗɗo ɗum alaa yaafuya, alaa jam, e nder ndiyam yiite ngo alaa ko ñifata, ɓe naatata haa abada sabu ko ɗum ɓe cuɓii.

Kono noon, no tato bonɓe ɓee ummirii nii, seedeeɓe am ɗiɗo ɓee kadi, ɓe annaboytoo balɗe 1260, ɓe ɓoorii saak, ɓe ndokka seedeeji keewɗi. Ɓe ngarat to Isra'iila, ɓe ceedoo Alla Duumotooɗo Nuunɗal, ɓe ceedoo no feewi e Yeesu Almasiihu, hay so tawii ko Yeesu Nasaraatu, mo alaa neɗɗo waawi arde to El Shaddai so wonaa e mum! Kono tato bonɓe ɓee maa mbiy Yeesu Almasiihu ko gaño, ɓe kaɗa binndi ceniiɗi ɗi Yeesu woni Konngol ngol waɗi ɓanndu. Kono noon, ko ɓe ɗaɓɓata haɗde koo, maa firtu koye maɓɓe e foolde duumotoonde.

Sabu ɗuum, e nder balɗe seedeeji maɓɓe, so tawii anniya neɗɗo ko lorlaade ɓe, yiite ina yalta e kunuɗe maɓɓe ngam halkude añɓe maɓɓe: sabu so tawii anniya neɗɗo ko lorlaade ɓe, ina foti lorleede noon, so tawii anniya neɗɗo ko warde ɓe, ina foti warde noon. Ɓeeɗoo ina njogii baawɗe uddude asamaan, ngam toɓo waasa toɓde e balɗe annabaagal maɓɓe, ina njogii baawɗe e ndiyam ngam waylude ɗam e yiiƴam, e fiyde leydi e kala ñawanɗe, no ɓe njiɗiri fof. Nii woni balɗe seedeeji maɓɓe.

Ɓe kaalat geɗe keewɗe, ɓe njaltina Linjiila Duumotooɗo, hono no feññiniraa e jamaanu mum ngam timminde kala huunde e yeeso Yeesu Almasiihu. Seedeeji maɓɓe kadi ɓe ndokka, geɗe keewɗe ɓe peeñtina e balɗe seedeeji maɓɓe, fof maa winndee haa abada. Nde balɗe maɓɓe joofi, ina foti warde ɓe, ɓalli maɓɓe maa njooɗo e laawol wuro mawngo, ngo e ruuhu ina wiyee Sodoma e Misra, ɗo Joomiraawo mon kadi fawaa e leggal, ɓe njuulat no feewi, ɓe njoɓondira dokke sabu maayde maɓɓe. Nde balɗe tati e feccere joofi, ɓe nanat daande mawnde wiyeteende, "Ngaree ɗoo", ɓe ummitinee, ɓe nguurtoo haa abada, ɓe nawaa kadi e asamaan, nii seedeeji maɓɓe timmata.

Paamee ndee fenaande arii e dame mon Isra'iila, paamee cuɓagol ngol pot-ɗon waɗde. Maa on njaɓ Barabas ko Almasiihu mon fenaande, on njaɓa maande mum, on naata e Esawu, on yeeya ndonu mon sabu mburu haa abada, walla on ustoo ɓerɗe mon, on ɗoftoo daande Yahuwah, Alla mawɗo duumotooɗo, hay so tawii ko Jom mawɗo oo, e yeeso Yeesu Almasiihu, hono Almasiihu Yeesu mo mi ummini e maayɓe, mo mi nawti e asamaan, mo mi teddini e njiimaandi duumotoondi, laamu mum e laamu mum alaa joofnirde, alaa neɗɗo waawi ɓadtaade YHVH e innde woɗnde so wonaa e asamaan walla e leydi walla e les leydi haa abada. Sabu e nder Almasiihu fof maa wuurtine, maa mbaɗee haa abada no neɗɗo keso gooto nii, sabu Almasiihu Yeesu ko Aadama cakkitiiɗo.

Ɓe kaɓat e seniiɓe, ɓe poola ɓe, sabu baawɗe ndokkaama ɓe ngam timminde ɗum. Kono ɓe poolataa Isra'iila, alaa, heddiiɓe e Isra'iila ina poti hisneede nde ɓe ngartata e Yeesu Almasiihu e ɓerɗe maɓɓe fof.

Sabu ɗuum, heɗee Konnguɗi am, onon Isra'iila, ngam on mbaawa wuurdude yeeso am, mi mooɓta on e hoore am e les laamu tiiɗngu Almasiihu mon goonga, hono Yeesu Almasiihu. Winndaa: Kono waawaa wonde konngol Alla waasde waɗde huunde: sabu fof ɓe ngonaa Isra'iila, ɓe ngoni e Isra'iila: So ɓe ngonaa kadi fof ɓiɓɓe, sabu ɓe ngoni iwdi Ibraahiima: kono, E nder Isaaka iwdi maa noddirtee: Ko ɗum woni, ɓe ngoni ɓiɓɓe ɓanndu, ɓe ngonaa ɓiɓɓe Alla: kono ɓiɓɓe alkulal, ina limtee e iwdi.

Ko miin YHVH, Jom mawɗo, miɗo nodda on Isra'iila ngam heddiiɓe nuunɗuɓe ngara to Yeesu Almasiihu ngam o hisna on e pittaali ɗi Barabas gollotoo ngam waɗde e leydi ndii ngam halkude Yaaquuba haa laaɓi, hay so tawii ko halkere nde mi accataa.

Ko miin neldata ɗee Konnguɗi e daande gooto woyɗo e ladde, mo Almasiihu Yeesu soodti e heeferɓe, o senndaa ngam anniyaaji men duumotooɗi, to Yeesu Almasiihu o gollotoo YHVH, Jom mawɗo, sabu alaa neɗɗo waawi arde to am e innde woɗnde, hay so tawii ko e yiɗde am Muusaa tan, sabu Muusaa ina ɗaɓɓi ɗooftaare Yeesu Nasaraatu!

Ko miin ittoowo bumni feccere e Isra'iila, bumni e Alkawal Kesal sabu wuttere Muusaa ndee ina suddii haa jooni ɓerɗe maɓɓe e bumni feccere. Denndaangal Isra'iila nodduɓe Yeesu Almasiihu maa keɓtu yiiterde mum en, ɓe njiya timmuɗo, hay so tawii ko e yeeso Yeesu Almasiihu, Almasiihu mon duumotooɗo!

Ko miin Alla Jom baawɗe fof Ibraahiima, e Isaaka, e Yaaquuba, mi ari ngam timminde denndaangal alkule am laabi keewɗi, mi waɗat ɗum timmuɗo e golle duumotooɗe Ɓiɗɗo am giɗaaɗo, mo mi yamiri on hoolaare, goonɗinal, yiɗde, e ɗooftaare timmunde, onon Isra'iila, so wonaa on anniyii yeeyde ndonu mon, on naata e Esawu e dow laabi e nder yiite duumotooɗo ñaawoore e njoɓdi nuunɗal.

Paamee, onon Isra'iila, wonde leydi ndii maa anndu ina woodi Alla e nder Yaaquuba, Alla Yaaquuba ko haa abada duumotooɗo Yahuwah, hay so tawii ko Jom mawɗo, ko e nder Almasiihu Yeesu Isra'iila Alla mooɓtete, mi mooɓta ɓe e hoore am hono no alkuliraa. So tawii ɓe ɓadii walla ɓe woɗɗii, so tawii ɓe Yahuudiyankooɓe walla heeferɓe, fof ina mbaawi mooɓteede e nder Almasiihu, ɓe ngoni rontooɓe nguurndam duumotooɗam, ɓe ngoni ɓiɓɓe goonga alkulal, sabu ɓiɓɓe alkulal ko nuunɗuɓe wuurdunooɓe e goonɗinal.

Nii woni, YHVH wiyi noon, fof maa laato Ii e Aamiin e Aamiin e AAMIIN!

Ko miin Elohim wuuruɓe, YHVH konuuji Elohim mon.
