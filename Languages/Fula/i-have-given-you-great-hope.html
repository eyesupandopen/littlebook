Seedeeji Ɗiɗi: Eklesiya
E nder Golle Poola Nelaaɗo
03 Mee, 2024

Mi Rokkii On Tama Suurɗo!

Ngaroyee e am, nanee konnguɗi am, an yo Debbo am deweteeɗo, sabu miɗo woni ɗoo ngam deeƴnude ruuhuuji mooɗon, wellinde ɓerɗe mooɗon, e timmin'de on e nder am, Iisaa Almasiihu. Wota on tennito e konnguɗi am ɗi njoorndi, sabu mo njiɗ-mi, oo mi laɓɓinta, fii no noddaandu mooɗon e suɓagol mooɗon tabitira e nder am, Jom-debbo dewo, hono Iisaa Almasiihu. Onon ɓiɓɓe am, heɗitee daande am, nanee konnguɗi am, sabu mi nodaaki on fii ñaawoore e yerdugol, kono mi laɓɓinii on e nder am, Joomiraawo mooɗon Iisaa Almasiihu. Mbele on nanaali ko Poola wiyi Korentiyankooɓe e nder ɓataake mum arano oo? Jooni noon, ina heddii goongɗinal, e tama, e yiɗde, ɗeeɗoo geɗe tati: kono mawɗo e majje ko yiɗde. Ko ɗuum waɗi yiɗde woni mawɗo e ɗeeɗoo tati, sabu yiɗde Yaahwe hendaaka. Yiɗde Yaahwe ko kaɓɓirgal baɗngal. Ko ka sabaabu mawɗo, kadi rokkude e moƴƴere no on njoɓiraa e moƴƴere nii, ko wonde nannduɓe e Alla!

Heewɓe e mooɗon njoppiima tama, haa e tama mooɗon e am, Iisaa Almasiihu, sabu mi rokkii on tama suurɗo, onon ɓiɓɓe am. Faamee wonde gujjo oo ina yiɗi jiiɓde on, o fooɗa on, kono Alla wonaa Jom-jiiɓru, kono ko Jom-jam no woniri e eklesiyaaji seniiɓe ɗii fof nii. Sayɗaane etinooma niɓɓinde yamirooje am ɗe ndokkir-mi e nder wahayu am dow Sirlu Mawɗo Alla haa e Nelaaɗo Poola gila duuɓi keewɗi jooni. Onon ɓiɓɓe am, tama mooɗon ko ka miijo ngoo reeni on e muñde jarribooji e torraaji keewɗi ɗi keɓoton e nguurndam ɗam. Sabu so wonaa tama mooɗon, on mbaɗatno ko Poola wiyi Korentiyankooɓe nii: So tawii ko e nguurndam ɗam tan njogorino-ɗen tama e Almasiihu, ko enen ɓuri bonde kisal e yimɓe fof. Kono jooni Almasiihu ummiima e maayɓe, o wonti ɓiɓɓe adanɓe e maayɓe ɓee. Onon ɓiɓɓe am, mi rokkii on tama mawɗo fii tama mooɗon, sabu mi wiyiino: onon wuuruɓe heddiiɓe ɓee, on njettoyte e duule, no maayɓe e nder Almasiihu ɓee adortoo ummitaade nii, on fof on mbaɗtiloyte, sabu ko noon mbaɗirton nanndude e am haa abada, ko noon kadi Debbo am deweteeɗo wontidira e am haa pooma.

Sabu hol tama mooɗon e nder Almasiihu, onon ɓiɓɓe am? Mbele wonaa fodoore rimɗinegol e moƴƴitingol timmungol, ɗo njiyoton kammu e mawngu e teddungal Yaahwe e gite mooɗon? Mbele tama mooɗon wonaa ko yaawi ɗo ndewoton am e laamu am nuunɗungu, sabu no ngoonɗinir-ɗon e geɗe seeɗa, ko noon kadi mbaɗir-mi on laamiiɗo e dow geɗe keewɗe. Mbele tama mooɗon wonaa fodoore nguurndam duumotooɗam ɗo nyaamoton e leki nguurndam, njahdon e laabi Yerusalaam Keso, ɗo annoora Yaahwe e annoora Yeesu HaMasiyaak jalbirta haa abada e nder jalbugol teddungol? Mbele tama mooɗon wonaa teddungal kuɗeele keewɗe e njoɓɗeele fii goongɗinal mooɗon e yiɗde mooɗon e am, Iisaa Almasiihu? Mbele tama mooɗon wonaa tama cellal timmungal, alaa ñawu, alaa rafi? Mbele tama mooɗon wonaa ko mi lelnata ayɓe mooɗon les koyɗe mooɗon, mi waɗa haa suudu dewal Sayɗaane ndu sujjana koyɗe mooɗon? (Sabu ma ɓe anndu mi yiɗii on!) Mbele tama mooɗon wonaa weltaare duumotoonde yeeso Yaahwe e hoore mum? Sabu o waɗoyan galle makko e nder yimɓe, laamu feccaangu woodataa, kono ko Laamu Alla tan, sabu alaa ɓurɗo Yaahwe mawnude; alaa, hay gooto waawaa nanndineede e teddungal Alla duumotooɗo, hono Baaba men!

Welte e ko arata e mooɗon, onon ɓiɓɓe am, sabu mi rokkii on ko arata e faandaare seninaande e nder am, Joomiraawo mooɗon jalboowo! Mbele tama mooɗon wonaa tama mawɗo ɗo ndañoton e tikkere e nder am? Sabu mooɗon ko ka tama ɓurɗo mawnude, tama ka haali Nelaaɗo am cuɓaaɗo Poola, gonɗo reenoowo Sirlu mawɗo Alla oo? Sabu mi feeñanii Poola ko o winndi Kolosiyankooɓe: Hono sirlu mo suuɗanoo gila e jamaanuuji, e iwdiiji, kono jooni o feeñanii seniiɓe makko, To ɓe Alla yiɗi anndinde ko woni jawdi teddungal sirlu oo e nder Jentilankooɓe, hono Almasiihu e nder mooɗon, tama teddungal. Mooɗon ko tama tedduɗo fotanɗo laamɗo, sabu mi waɗoyan on laamɓe e almaamɓe yeeso Alla men; sabu ko miin woni hooreejo almaamɓe haa abada e dow limoore Melkisedek, goɗɗo woodataa.

Onon cukalel am, wota on kulo fenaande gaño oo, sabu o tampinayno on e nder meere so tawii o waawii; kono o hawrii e nanndoowo makko e am, mi foolii mo no feewi, miɗo ara ngam bonnude laamu makko, sabu mi nelaama ngam ittude golle Ibliis, ko noon kadi mi nelir-mi on! Mboɗi ndii e laamu mum fof ngalaa tama. Ɓe anndi ɓe ndaniima halkaare e leptingol duumotoongol, ɗo ɓe njalata e yiite ɓurnge wulde bulo ngoo, ɓe nduufoo e nder sulufara jalboowo haa abada. Ɓe ngañii on, ɓe njoɓayno kala huunde ngam wonde on, ndeke wota on njeso konnguɗi maɓɓe. Nanee am, onon Debbo am deweteeɗo; Sayɗaane waɗay kala huunde ngam fooɗde on e njoɓdi mooɗon e nder am, Iisaa Almasiihu. O waɗay kala huunde ngam suuɗde tama mooɗon mo ndokkir-mi on, ko ɗuum waɗi laabi makko e nder ndee fannu heewi wonde ko hoynude miijo ngoo rewrude e fenaande goonga e laawol ɗemngal mum pewal ngal.

Wota on kulo mo, kono ceerte e fenaande makko, ndeenon tama mooɗon goonga oo e hakkillaaji mooɗon, sabu mbele on njanngaali? Ruuhu e hoore mum ina seedtoo, e ruuhu men, wonde ko en ɓiɓɓe Alla. So tawii ko en ɓiɓɓe, ndeen ko en ronooɓe, ronooɓe Alla, e wondiiɓe ronooɓe e Almasiihu: so tawii en nimsidii e makko, fii no ndenndiren kadi teddineede. Sabu miɗo sikki, wonde torraaji jamaanu hannde oo, potaani nanndineede e teddungal ngo feeñoyta e men ngoo. Onon ɓiɓɓe am, so tawnoo on faamuno mawngu wonde wondiiɗo ronoowo e Almasiihu, kono ɓooyataa on annda no on anndiraa nii. Mbele on faamaani wonde Alla rokkii am kala huunde e kammu e leydi, kadi ndonu am ko ndonu ɓurngu mawnude? Onon ɓiɓɓe am, on anndaa tan ko on tigi-rigi, sabu ko on tigi-rigi suuɗii e nder am, so mi feeñii, ko noon kadi on feeñirta e am e nder teddungal!

Wota on kule, ndaaree e yiɗde e hoolaare mooɗon, sabu yaakaare mooɗon waɗan on mbaawka muñal mawngal; ko yaakaare e weltaare am nde Baaba'am waɗani mi yeeso am waɗi mi waawde timminde? Sabi deftere Hebre'en wi'i: Hiɓe ndaara Iisaa, Puɗɗoowo e Timmбіnoowo goonɗinal meeɗen, on mo ngam weltaare nde o waɗanaa yeeso mum, muñi e dow leggal kalasal, o yefii hersa, o jooɗii e nyaamo jooɗorgal laamu Alla. Mbi'a mi accanaali on misaalu, mbi'a mi laatanaaki on sifa mo oɗon poti rewde? On faamaali naa? Wonnde kala goonɗinɗo mi, wonaa tan golle ɗe mi golli e wakkati aral am gadanal o golla, kono o golla golle ɓurɗe ɗe mi golli mawde? Sabi mi yehi to Baaba'am, mi hokkii on baawɗe, mi daranii on e semmbe am fof, sabu mi semmbiɗɗo, sabu non Alla waɗiri mi e nder ɗoofanaare am.

Yimɓe am, konnguɗi ɗi poti semmbiɗinde on e hokkude on doole ngam darnude, sabu mi waɗanii on weltaare mawnde yeeso mooɗon! Wota on ngoonɗin fenaande gaño on ko fayti e yaakaare mooɗon, sabu omo yetto accude on tawa alaa yaakaare, oɗon nyaafee e koyɗe, hay e nder cellal mooɗon leefngal, oɗon mbaɗee semmbiɗɓe e nder am, miin Joomiraaɗo mooɗon Iisaa Almasiihu! Jooni mi famtinan on ko arata yeeso, Aan Dewgal am, ndeke nanee konnguɗi hunuko am, sabu ko konnguɗi goonɗinal, ko konnguɗi yiɗde, konnguɗi am kadi njahran yaakaare mooɗon e goonga, sabu yaakaare mawnde woodi e goonga ka mi hokkii on!

Aran, paamee wonnde mi noddii on; mi hisnii on e ruuhu am baawɗo e ruuhu ɓiyngu; mi waɗii on tagoore hesere e nder am, miin Almasiihu Iisaa! Ngam sadaka am wonde Jibingel Juulde laaɓngel, kala goonɗinɗo mi e goonɗinal hisinngal iwngal e ɓerɗe mum en kadi hisnan e tikkere e nder am. Sukaaɓe am, paamee wonnde konnguɗi ɗi no mawni haa heewɓe mbaawaa faamde, sabu hakkeeji maɓɓe no ndigga ɓe e aybe, e njooɓaari, e hersa, ɗum no waɗa sabu heewi no ɓe calii laɓɓinde ko'e maɓɓe e ƴiiƴam am kiimal, ɓe njaɓataa tuubde hakkeeji maɓɓe, ɓe ngoppa jokkondiral maɓɓe e am, miin laamɗo jam, sabu hisnugol no iwdi e goonɗinal jokkondirngal e am, miin Joomiraaɗo mawɗo!

Heewɓe e mooɗon paamaali wonnde mi fonndii on, e sabu fonndaare ndeen e nder am, on hisnaama e tikkere, sabu mi wi'iino yimɓe Roma: Ndeke jooni, nde tawnoo en laɓɓinaama e ƴiiƴam makko, en hisnan tikkere rewrude e makko. Njaaboo kam ɗum, aan Dewgal am ngo mi ardata, hol ko waɗi oɗon njiɗi ñalawma Joomiraaɗo? Hol ko waɗi on nanii jaagorde Seyɗaani ɗe njoorii hoore mum en no jaagorde am nii, nde ɓe mbi'i on poton muñde fitina Yakuba e hay fitina mawɗo o waɗata e feccere cakkitiinde e yontere cappanɗe jeeɗiɗi ɗe Daniyel haali ndee? Ɓe fenanii on, on ngoonɗini ɓe. Ɓe nootii sifaa mooɗon hakkeejo, ɓe mbi'i on on ngalaa nafoore tawi ko on luulu'u keewngu coggu; ɓe mbi'i on on potani tikkere mawnde e nder am, ɓe feni! Ɓe mbi'i on wota on njorto hisneede e fitina, ɓe wayli konngol am ceningol. Sabi goonga mi wi'iino Poolos ngam jannginde Timote: Torraaji, fitinaaji baɗɗi kam to Antiyos, to Ikoniyum, to Listra, hol fitinaaji ɗi mi muñnoo: kono e nder majji fof Joomiraaɗo hisni kam. Goonga, kala jooɗiiɗo e dewal e nder Almasiihu Iisaa, maa torro. Goonga, Seyɗaani maa jokku kala neɗɗo guurɗo e dewal, e nder torraaji mum fof mi hisnan on so on ngartii e am e nder caɗeele mooɗon, on keddii e hoolaare e yaakaare mooɗon. Paamee ceeral ngal, sabu Seyɗaani jillindiri haala ka: Seyɗaani maa torro kala guurɗo e dewal, ɗuum ko torra; heewi kadi oɗon poti muñde tikkere mum, sabu e nder foolgol mum omo jogii tikkere mawnde. Kono wota on kule gullaango makko, sabu mi yamiriino Piyeer, mi wi'i: Kakkilee, ndaaree: sabu gaño mooɗon julaaŋo no yaha no wa'i no rawaandu gullaandu, no ɗaɓɓita mo o waawi ñamde: Kaɓeɗon e makko, on ndarii e goonɗinal, oɗon nganndi wonnde banndiraaɓe mooɗon wonɓe e aduna no keɓa torraaji nannduɗi e ɗii.

Paamee, sukaaɓe am, wonnde tikkere garoore ndee wonaa tikkere Seyɗaani kono ko tikkere Alla e tikkere Jibingel ndee, ndi fawaa ko e Seyɗaani. Sabi Seyɗaani ko kulɗo, o ɓolii e nder tageefo ngoo fof ngam yettaade suuɗaade tikkere nde o resi ndee, o sikki o waawan foolde Yahweh e geɗe ɗee; kono anniyaaji Baaba'am no laaɓtinaa nii: Mawɗo, mbi'a a aawaano aawdi moƴƴiri e ngesa maa? Ndeke to hudo bonngo ngoo iwi? O wi'i ɓe, Gaño waɗii ɗum. Gollotooɓe ɓee mbi'i mo, Aɗa yiɗi ndeen min njeha min moɓta ɗum? Kono o wi'i, Alaa: woto tawa so on moɓtii hudo bonngo ngoo, on pooca kadi alkamaari ndii. Accee ɗi fof ɗi mawdida haa wakkati sooje: e wakkati sooje, mi wi'an sooje'en, Moɓtude aron hudo bonngo ngoo, kaɓɓon ɗum e baali ngam wulneede: kono moɓtee alkamaari ndii e gaggaal am. Nii woni alkamaari ndii moɓtete e gaggaal, kono hudo bonngo ngoo e kala aawɓe e yaruɓe ɗum fof maa njuɓɓe tawa ina nguuri e maayo yiite mawngo ngoo e sufar ko Baaba'am waɗani Iblisa e malaa'ikaaji mum, kono hay feccere e tikkere Alla wonanaa on, hay so tawii Seyɗaani yetto siiforaade on, omo ɗaɓɓita nawrude on e maccungaagu no koohoho. Wota on kule, sabu tikkere wonanaa on. Miɗo faami heewɓe ruuhuuji ɗi ngonaa tabituɗi no njirla misal ngal, ina mbi'a on maa on muñdu e maɓɓe tikkere ndee, kono ɓe mbaɗata ɗum ko e yejjitde laaɓal Binndi ɗii e haala kaa, ɓe no etoo wumnude on e waylude misal am.

Paamee wonde mi nanngoytowan on haa kammuuli, sabu mi heblanii jom suudu am nokku kisal e njoɓdi; sabu mi suuɗat on e nder galle Baaba am, ɗo cuuɗi mawɗi keewɗi ngoni. Ee, mi mooɓtindintin on e am nde mi feeñata, ɗum ɗoo feɓɓii waɗde. Sabu mi wiyinoo yimɓe Tesalonik: Sabu Joomiraaɗo e hoore mum ina jippoo e kammu e noddaango, e daande Malaa'ika mawɗo, e kural Alla: maayɓe e nder Almasiihu ummotoo artude. Nden menen wuurɓe heddiiɓe ɓen, en nanngondirtoyee e maɓɓe e nder duule, ngam hawritoyde e Joomiraaɗo e nder weeyo: noon kadi en ngonndirta e Joomiraaɗo haa pooma. Ngam maajum, ndewlondiree ɗii konnguɗi. Miɗo naamno on, onon jom suudu am fennanooɗo, mbeltotoɗon jooni ko njaɓataa fenaande Seytaane, njaɓon konnguɗi am, ndewloree ɗi, ndewliron banndiraaɓe mon? Sabu ɗum feɓɓiima waɗde, miɗo hiranii woodgol mon, sabu miɗo yiɗi on no feewi, sabu ko miin woni goron mon.

Nde on ittaaka e leydi ndii, ñawu Yaaquuba ina fuɗɗoo e dow leydi ndii, halkere mawnde ina arda e juhal, kono wonaa fii mon, sabu on njahroyte kammuuli. Nden on ndaroyto yeeso jooɗorgal kiita am, ngam mi yoɓa on no golle mon njahri. Wonanɓe hoolaare njoɓdi mawndi e kalaaji keewɗi, on koltintee galle mo mi mahanii on, galle mo maandanaa on tan, ina jogii golle mon keewɗe, mo mi hokkata baawɗe keewɗe, sabu noon woni ɓanndu hesru ndu mi heblanii on. Alaa ñawu, alaa muuseeki, alaa sunaare, alaa kadi kuddi, onon sukaaɓe am, sabu kuddi ndii ina firlitoo e ko'e kuddaaɓe ɓen. Noon kadi on ngoni e kammuuli duuɓi jeeɗiɗi ɗii, hay ñalɗi jeeɗiɗi Daniyel. Nden, on njahdoyay e am hono no konu kammu fof nii, sabu ɗum annabaama, kadi noon wonirta: Mi yiyi kammu ina udditii, ndaarii puccu danewu, kadi jooɗiiɗo e mum o ina wiyee Koolaaɗo e Goongɗinɗo, kadi e nuunɗal o ñaawoyta o haɓata. Gite makko ina nandi e yiite jeyngol, e dow hoore makko ina woodi kalaaji keewɗi, omo jogii innde winndaande, nde hay gooto anndaa so wonaa kanko e hoore makko. O koltinaama comci muccaaɗi e ƴiiƴam, innde makko ko Konngol Alla. Konuuji wonnooɗi e kammuuli ɗii njahdi e makko e dow pucci daneeji, ina koltii comci daneeji laaɓɗi.

Sukaaɓe am, ko fayti e mon ko teddungal ngal alaa keerol, on njiɗataa hay huunde kadi, sabu on kollitii ko'e mon e am, on njiɗaa ɓeydude jarribo; hol ko waɗi on muñoytoo ñawu Yaaquuba? Sabu faandaare mum ko hisnude Yaaquuba, sabu heddiiɓe ina hisnoyee. Kono kooloree, ɓeen saliiɓe feeñde am e tiiɗnaare mum en mbonnere ngam halkude yaakaare mon tigi-rigi, hay ɓeen aynooɓe majjuɓe, ina muñoyoo ñawu Yaaquuba, ina hisnoyee so ɓe noddii innde am, ɓe koolnii haa timma. Kono ɓe keɓataa nokku e taabal am, sabu salaade feeñde am ko salaade miin, Gorko oo, nde ngar-mi ngam jom suudu am, kadi miɗo feɓɓii waɗde ɗum.

Ƴeewee jalbugol feeñde am! Heɗee, sabu on nanay daande am e sawta kural mawngal kural gadiingal; sabu hay so tawii ko miin woni Jawgel Alla, miɗo ara no mbaaru Yahuuda nii, kadi laamu Seytaane fof ina hulii miin, Almasiihu Iisaa, sabu waktuuji maɓɓe njettiima, yiite ngol ina huɓɓanee ɓe. Kono ngol ɓadataako on, sabu mi heblanii on njoɓdi mawndi e ndonu mawngu, onon jom suudu am. Njannge konnguɗi am e nder deftere mawnde Wahyu, sabu mi holloyma on seeɗa e ko arata: Kadi sara jooɗorde laamu ndee ina woodi jooɗorɗe noogas e nayi, e dow jooɗorɗe ɗee mi yiyi mawɓe noogas e nayi ina njooɗii, ina koltii comci daneeji, ina njogii e ko'e maɓɓe kalaaji kaŋŋe. (kadi kadi) Nde ɗeen daabeeji ndokki teddungal, e teddungal, e yettoore jooɗiiɗo e jooɗorde laamu ndee, wuurɗo haa pooma, Mawɓe noogas e nayi ɓen ina njuula yeeso jooɗiiɗo e jooɗorde laamu ndee, ina ndewa wuurɗo haa pooma, ina mberloo kalaaji mum en yeeso jooɗorde laamu ndee, ina mbiya: Aan yo Joomiraaɗo, a heɓii teddungal, e teddungal, e baawɗe: sabu a tagii kala huunde, kadi ngam weltaare maa ɗe ngoodi, ɗe tagaa.

Sukaaɓe am, wulliree ko fayti e mon, kono njeeɓee mi e hirjinde mawnde, sabu ɓooyaani on ngoni e kammuuli ina mberloo kalaaji mon yeeso jooɗorde laamu Alla toowɗo!

Ko miin woni ñaawoowo nuunɗuɗo, ɓooyaani on keɓay njoɓdi mawndi to Bema am.

Ko miin woni gorko jom suudu am.

Miɗo ara ngam nanngoyde jom suudu am!

Ko miin woni mbaaru leñol Yahuuda, aduna oo ina feɓɓii faamde.

Ko miin woni dokkaaɗo mawngu e kammu e leydi, sabu innde am ina mawnina inɗe fof so wonaa Yaweh.

Ko miin woni Ɓiy Alla garɗo e ɓanndu ngam bonanndeeji neɗɗaŋke, mi umminaama ñalnde tataɓere, mi jaɓɓaama e kammuuli.

Ko miin laamoytoo e nuunɗal e dow leydi, jom suudu am laamodoytoo e am.

Ko miin noddi on e teddungal e moƴƴere, woto tampu!

Ko miin.
