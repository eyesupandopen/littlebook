Seedeeɓe ɗiɗo: Isra’iila
Ko Welɗi e ko Haɗɗi
17 Noowammbar, 2024

Ndaaree! Mbaroodi leñol Yahuuda!

Unnditee noppi mon, njaɓee mi udditande on gite mon, onon leñol Isra’iila, nfintee e umminaare mon seeɗa ndee, nanee daande Mesiya mon goongaajo, hono Mbaroodi leñol Yahuuda, sabu ko miin woni oon. Faamee noon konnguɗi Yeesu Almasiihu, kañum mo nganndu-ɗon kadi, onon leñol Isra’iila, sabu ko miin woni oon.

Gooto, gooto, nanee konnguɗi am, onon musiɓɓe am, mbele no faamdon sahaaji ɗi ngonɗon ɗii, mbele no faamdon yaakaare noddaango mon ngoo, ngo woni artude e leydi fodoore ndii, sabu ko miin woni yaakaare noddaango mon ngoo, te ko miin noddata on, onon leñol Isra’iila, mbele no faamdon ko arata e kadi tikkere ndi yaltintaande e ɗii sahaaji ɗi ngonɗon ɗii. Ko miin woni Mbaroodi leñol Yahuuda, mi arata ko e nuunɗal ngam ñaawoyde e haɓde, faamee noon tiiɗnaare konnguɗi am, konnguɗi on mo nganndu-ɗon oo, sabu noon Yahwe waɗiri mi.

Sabu miin woni kaaƴe faddorde ɗee, kaaƴe mawɗe ɗe kala mo alaa goonɗinal faddotoo e mum en ɗee, sabu nuunɗuɓe ɓee nguurdataa e Muusaa jooni, kono nuunɗuɓe ɓee nguurdirat ko e goonɗinal! Sabu Muusaa ina wuuri e Mesiya mon goongaajo oo, kañum rewnoowo sariya mo Isra’iila waawaa rewde oo haa laaɓi, hay gooto e iwdi Aadama waawaa rewde sabu hakke wonde e ɓalli mon bonɗi ɗii. Ko miin kadi yoɓi njoɓdi Muusaa ndii nde neldu-mi hoore am e Yahwe, hono Jawgel Alla ngel, e sadak kafara timmuɗo haa pooma, sabu njoɓdi hakkeeji wonaa miin foti yoɓde, kono e giɗli am e Yahwe, e giɗli am e aadee en, mi yoɓii njoɓdiiji mon ɗii fof. Hay njoɓdiiji torra mawka, satteende, e maayde mi yoɓii fof, Yahwe jaɓii sadak am oo haa abada.

Faamee noon, tikkere Jawgel ngel ina foti udditeede, buuneeje ɗee ina poti fuɗeede, ndaaree, piyanɗe tikkere Alla ndee ina ɓadii, ina woni e damal mon, ko ɗuum waɗi, nanee daande am mbele onon e sukaaɓe mon no nguura, ndogdon fenaande nde Barabas gollotoo ngam waɗande yimɓe am ɓee, hono jawgel pooli ngel Isawu suɓii ngam wonande ɓe Masiya mum en, kañum kadi woni mahoowo suudu dewal bonndu. O ummotoo ko no jawgel jogiingel galleeje ɗiɗi nii, o arat ko ngam senndude leyɗeele ɗee haa ɓura e joom en mum en goonga en, ɓe ndiiwaa e warngo maɓɓe ngam senndude e jippiiɓe iwɓe e Isawu, ɓe mbiylotonoo ko alaa e sago, e naatooɓe ɓe pooli ngii neli, ɓe ngoni iwdi Askenas, wonaa Yaquuba, kono ɓe kawritii e Isawu e laabi maɓɓe Talmuud.

Ko miin woni on mo Yahwe neldi hono jawgel Alla, hono nanondiral jam timmungal, hono Paska mon timmuɗo, e sadak cakkitiiɗo ngam hakkeeji aadee en, laawol gootol, fof, haa abada, sadak goɗɗo ngam hakkeeji alaa heen. Mi arii e yimɓe am e yiɗde e jam, hay so tawii ko ɓe njaɓaani sadak am oo, kono mi neliraama no Paska timmuɗo nii, e nder ɗooftaare e yiɗde Yahwe, ko miin woni jawgel Alla, ngel Yahwe rokki ngam hakkeeji mon haa laaɓi, nanee daande am, onon leñol Isra’iila, faamee mi artataa e leydi ndii kadi no jawgel Alla nii, jam ina ittaama e leydi ndii e ñaawoore ariinde. Hay so tawii ñaawoore ndee ina foti yaltineede e suudu Alla adan, sabu ñaawoore fuɗɗotoo ko e suudu Alla.

Sabu goonɗinɓe e Almasiihu ɓee nawete ko e kammuuli, ɓe ngalaa yurmeende ɓee accete e yiite laɓɓinoowo ngam halkude ɓalndu, mbele ruuhu ina hisna, kono no yaltirta e yiite nii. Heewɓe, heewɓe no feewi ummoriiɓe e leyɗeele kala, e leƴƴi kala, e ɗemɗe kala, arat e Yeesu Nasaraan oo, mi laɓɓinat ɓe e nuunɗal, mi jaɓɓotoo ɓe, mi tagat ɓe e nuunɗal e senuye goonga, njoɓdi maɓɓe ndii alaa ko fotaani e torraaji ɗi ɓe muñata ɗii, sabu teddeendi njoɓdi maɓɓe ndii ɓurat yaakaare yimɓe, ɓe nguura haa abada e jam timmuɗo, e senuye, e nuunɗal goonga.

Sabu winndaama e maɓɓe, noon kadi waɗata: Ɓee ko ɓe njaltunoo e torra mawka, ɓe lootii comci maɓɓe juutɗi, ɓe laɓɓinii comci maɓɓe juutɗi e ƴiiƴam Jawgel ngel. Ko ɗuum waɗi, ɓe ngoni ko e yeeso jappeere laamu Alla, ɓe ndewata mo ñalawma e jamma e suudu dewal makko, on jooɗiiɗo e jappeere laamu ndee hoɗdat e maɓɓe. Ɓe keɓataa rafi kadi, ɓe keɓataa domɗi kadi, naange alanaa ɓe nafoore, hay wulaango. Sabu Jawgel ngel, ngo woni hakkunde jappeere laamu ndee, ardotòo ɓe, ardoo ɓe haa e ɓulli ndiyam guurɗam, Alla ittanta ɓe gonɗi maɓɓe fof.

Faamee noon ko miin timminɗo golle am hakkunde heeferɓe, sahaaji am yurmeende e muñal timmiima, hay so tawii yurmeende e muñal ina njokkondiri e am haa abada, Yahwe e hoore mum wiyi maa o yurmo on mo o yurmini, yurmeende e muñal ina duumoo haa abada. Gooto, gooto, mi yahi e heeferɓe no egguɗo salaaɗo e yimɓe am, ɓe Isawu nanngi e maccungaagu aadaaji mum bonɗi ɗi ɓe njiɗi bonnude konnguɗi Yahwe duumiiɗi haa abada, ɗi mbonnataa. Kono ɓe mbaɗii goonɗinal maɓɓe bonngal e juuɗe winndooɓe maɓɓe, ɓe mbaɗi aadaaji worɓe bonɓe ɓee e deftere ceniinde, kono hol no deftere bonnde waɗirta ceniinde? Gooto, gooto, bone woodanii ɓe noddooɓe niɓɓere annoore, annoore niɓɓere, sabu ñaawoore maɓɓe neɓtaaki!

Sahaaji am hakkunde heeferɓe ɗii timmiima sabu ɓe natti e hakkeeji ɗi baabiraaɓe mon natti ɗii nde ɓe njaɓaani jawgel Alla ngel Yahwe neldi ɓe tawa ɓe njettaaki, no nanondiral jam duumiingal e leyɗeele ɗee nii, heeferɓe heewɓe keɓii hisde. Jooni noon, ɓeen potnooɓe wonde sukaaɓe fodoore hakkunde Isra’iila Alla njiytii hoore mum en e calɗi tawaaɗi ɗii, hay e lekki oliwi tawaaɗi, ɗo ɓe njaltinaa ɗoo, nde calɗi tawaaɗi ɗii taƴaa e nder goonɗinal ngal.

Kono ɓe ittaama e lekki oliwi ladde, hay lekki ngo alaa yaakaare, alaa fodoore, e ɓeen arɓe e am, mbaɗi mi Joomiraawo maɓɓe duumiiɗo e nuunɗal e sahaaji jam, yurmeende, e muñal, jooni ɓe naatnaama e Isra’iila Alla, ɓe naftoroo fodoore waɗanoonde baabiraaɓe ɓee, heewɓe e maɓɓe ndewat mi haa abada no ɓalli am nii, mi nawdat ɓe no resraaɗo am nii, mi maandinat 144,000 am ɓe laɓɓinnoo hoore mum en e ƴiiƴam am laɓɓinoojam no sukaaɓe rewɓe laaɓɓe ɓe debbo mawɗo oo bonnaani nii. E hisde heeferɓe ɓee leyɗeele barkinaama, goonga, ɓeen leƴƴi majjuɗi ɗooftiiɓe daande am keɓii hisde e Joomiraawo mum en, ɓe nguura haa abada hakkunde leƴƴi Isra’iila Alla! Faamee noon, mi hisnat yoga e Aadama fof, kala ɗo o yejjitii e leydi ndii e ñalɗi lepte makko.

Sabu Yahwe waɗii mi ruuhu hokkoowo nguurndam, mi anndiraaka e ɓalndu kadi. Gooto, gooto, ko miin taƴɗo lekki oliwi ladde ngii sabu goonɗinal mum en e sabu ɓe njuurngitii ahdi am, hay Alkawal am Kesal. Sabu ɓe poti jogaade Joomiraawo gooto, hono miin, Almasiihu Yeesu, Joomiraawo Joomiraaɓe e Laamɗo Laamɓe, e laamu am tan woni yaakaare maɓɓe. Kono ɓe njogii yaakaare fenaande e Barabas, ɓe mbaɗii yaakaare maɓɓe e joomiraawo fenaande, naatɗo e juulirɗe maɓɓe e yaakaare fenaande ngalu, hay Joomiraawo fenaande fodotooɗo hisnude Babilon Sirri, kono e suunde o gollotoo ko ngam halkude Babilon Sirri. Hay on fenaande wootere, hono kullel ngel ummorii e leydi, ngel ummotoo no Masiya fenaande Isawu nii, hono jawgel pooli ngel ummotoo jogiingel galleeje ɗiɗi. Nii woni Egliis am naatnaaɗo oo e Laodicea waɗii juumre, jooni ina jaɓa maayde mum en, sabu Barabas addataa so wonaa maayde, o halkat debbo mawɗo oo haa laaɓa, hay Babilon Sirri, mo ñaawoore mum ari sabu bonannde mum e bonannde mum nde o bonniri leydi ndii e jeenay mum.

Nii woni ɓe cuɓii e murtal maɓɓe hay on addanoowo ɓe halkaare no hisnoowo maɓɓe fenaande nii, sabu Juulirde Seytaane ko fenooɓe fof. Anndee noon, onon leñol Isra’iila, ko Barabas foti waɗde, sabu o yeeyii ɓe e juuɗe añɓe maɓɓe, o fenat gaññeeje hoore makko, o suuɗoo e leydi e caɗeele garooje. O ummotoo ko e fenaande mawnde no Masiya fenaande Isra’iila nii, sabu ɓe keɓat hare maɓɓe, hay hare maɓɓe tataɓere, nde ɓurat tati ɗee fof. Hay so tawii noon, no winndiraa nii, noon kadi waɗirtee: Mi yiyii kullel ina yalta e maayo, ina jogii ko’e jeeɗiɗi, e galleeje sappo, e dow galleeje mum sappo ina jogii laamuuji sappo, e dow hoore mum innde bonnde. Kullel ngel mi yiyii ngel ina nanndi e lewru, koyɗe mum no be’i nii, hunuko mum no hunuko mbaroodi nii: pooli ngii rokki mo doole mum e jappeere mum laamu, e laamu mawngu. Mi yiyii gooto e ko’e mum no barminaaɗo haa maayi nii, kono gaññeeje mum maayɗe cellii, aduna oo fof haawnii, rewi e kullel ngel.

Nii woni konnguɗi winndaaɗi ɗii ina kollita fenaande makko, hay konnguɗi ɗi ndokku-mi Yuhanna Wahyu oo duuɓi keewɗi ko adii ɗuum. Ina wayi no Barabas maa heɓ gaññeeje hoore, kono o fenat, o fowat aduna oo, mo goonɗintoo maande makko e kaawniiɗe makko fenaande, sabu noon o rokkaa ngam nawde kala jiɗɓe bonannde e hoore mum en e fenaande tiiɗnde, sabu ko kanko woni bonnoowo maɓɓe e halkoowo maɓɓe. E juuɗe makko o addata ko ñawu mboddi, hay ñawu kullel ngel, ɓe njillondirat njamndi e loope, hay so tawii ɓe njokkondirtaa, kono noon jawgel pooli ngel ummotoo. O ummotoo ko e dow laamu cakkitiiɗo kullel ngel, ngo wonata laamu maɓɓe cakkitiiɗo ngam laamaade leydi ndii e leydi fodoore ndii. Kono o ummataa tan, o ummodoo e kullel ngel ummorii e maayo, ɓe laamdoo kawral maɓɓe laamɓe, rokkuɓe doole maɓɓe fof e laamu maɓɓe pooli ngii, sabu ɓe fof ɓe ndewata ko pooli ngii, ɓe ndokkii ɗum fof, sabu ko kañum woni baaba maɓɓe, hay on aawniiɗo gese mum bonɗe hakkunde alkama Yahwe, mi ruttat ɓe fof, mi werloo ɓe e yiite.

Nii woni jawgel pooli ngel ummotoo e fenaande timmunde, maande fenaande keewɗe e kaawniiɗe o hollirta ngam hollude annabaaku makko fenaande, o waɗat konnguɗi mawɗi, bonɗi, hay so tawii o wiyi ko kanko woni Alla. Kullel gootel hollirta goɗngel, ɓe laamdoo e nanondiral, hay so tawii ko e peeje pooli ngii ɗe o laamotoo laamu makko bonngu nguu seeɗa. Ɓe mbaɗat kala ko yowitii e diine kerecee en ko haram, ɓe ndañat binndi ceniɗi, ɓe ndokkaama juuɗe ngam haɓde e seniiɓe, ndartina ɓe, noon kadi ɓe mbaɗata. Kono ɓe njillondirat diineeji aduna oo fof, mbele fof ndewata ko kaɓirɗe maɓɓe ɓaleeje, sabu e koyɗe Seytaane ɓe kawritii e murtal cakkitiiɗo, ɓe ndoggataa junngo am doole, wiyi Mesiya mon duumiiɗo, hono Yeesu Almasiihu!

Kono e añde maɓɓe kala ko yowitii e Almasiihu Yeesu, ɓe addanat hisde e yimɓe heewɓe ɓe ɓe mbardata, hay ɓeen noddooɓe innde Yeesu Nasaraan haa e fooftaare maɓɓe cakkitiinde, mi semmbintaa ɓe, mi rokka ɓe yaakaare mawnde, ɓe ngarat e am haa laaɓa, hay ɓeen fof ronooɓe hisde duumiinde, hay gooto waawaa ittude ɓe e juuɗe am, ɓeen fof ɓe inɗe mum en mbinndaa e deftere nguurndam jawgel ngel hisnat. Woto hul ngam yiɗɓe mon ɓe nanndi e majjuɓe, sabu heewɓe e maɓɓe ina njogii e juuɗe am doole, mi addanat ɓe hisde e Almasiihu Yeesu, hay e ɗemngal ngal ɓe noddirta innde am doole ndee mi nanat ɓe!

Kono noon, jawgel fenaande jogiingel galleeje ɗiɗi ummotoo ngam laamaade aduna oo e leydi fodoore ndii, nde Isawu yiɗi jogaade no mum nii, kono haala mum ko fenaande, ronkere Yaquuba ina tabitii haa abada, hay ronkere nde Isawu yeeyi ngam taƴre mbuuru. Sabu Isawu naatii e yimɓe laaɓɓe, o naatii e Askenas, ɓe njogii aadaaji e golle Kanan bonɗo, kono ɓe nfenata ɓe mbiya ko ɓe iwdi Isra’iila, tawi wonaa. Sabu ko Kanan woni giɗɗo laabi yimɓe laaɓɓe caggal ilam mawɗo oo, o wayli iwdi makko haa abada e halkaare duumiinde, sabu kala waylitooɗo e hoore mum e mbaadi Alla, mbaadi ndi ɓe tagaa ndii, alaa yaafuya, sabu ɗee ko laabi Seytaane ɗe Isawu winndi e feccere e Talmuud makko bonɗo, ɗo fenaande makko ummorii.

Nii woni Askenas, mo iwdi Yafet, naatii e Isawu e haala mum fenaande ronkere, ɗo ɓe njiɗi wujjude ronkere Yaquuba e sukaaɓe fodoore ɓee. Kono, golle ɗee ngalaa nafoore, ɓe ndarataaki, sabu ko miin woni on mo Yahwe rokki kala ko woni e juuɗe am doole e baawɗe. Faamee noon ko miin ronɗo kala ko fodaama Yaquuba, ko miin woni Mesiya mon goongaajo, e juuɗe am woni njoɓdi mon, onon leñol Isra’iila, e juuɗe Barabas halkaare mon duumiinde ina heddii, woto kawreede e makko!

Faamee noon njoɓdi fodoore waɗanoonde baabiraaɓe adanɓe ɓee ina fawii e juuɗe am, hay gooto waawaa wujjude ko woni hakke am, sabu ko noon doole ɗe mi rokkaa e I AM mawɗo oo, mi rokkat ndee ronkere sukaaɓe fodoore ɓee, hay nuunɗuɓe nguurdirɓe e goonɗinal e Ɓiɗɗo Yahwe, giɗɗo ɓe, rokki hoore mum ngam hisde duumiinde sukaaɓe fodoore ɓee, ko ɗuum waɗi, onon leñol Isra’iila, njaɓee mi no Mesiya mon nii, mbele onon e sukaaɓe mon no nguura, no keɓa barke e laawol ngol. Mi reentat on, mi reentat on, mbele no keɓon feccere mon timmunde e ronkere e laamu am duuɓi ujunnaaje, ngo woni e damal mon. Nanee noddaango am, onon leñol Isra’iila! Sabu miɗo nodda on mbele no ndokkiton hoore mon e junngo am doole, mbele kadi no ngonon sukaaɓe fodoore, sabu Muusaa ina rewee e goonɗinal e am, Yeesu Almasiihu, ko ɗuum waɗi, ndesee e golle am, njoppee golle mon.

Nii woni Askenas naatii e Isawu e haala mum fenaande, ɗo ɓe njiɗi wujjude ronkere Yaquuba e sukaaɓe fodoore ɓee. Kono, golle ɗee ngalaa nafoore, ɓe ndarataaki, sabu ko miin woni on mo Yahwe rokki kala ko woni e juuɗe am doole e baawɗe. Faamee noon ko miin ronɗo kala ko fodaama Yaquuba, ko miin woni Mesiya mon goongaajo, e juuɗe am woni njoɓdi mon, onon leñol Isra’iila, e juuɗe Barabas halkaare mon ina heddii. Ko ɗuum waɗi, faamee cuɓagol ngol potɗon waɗde, e on mo nganndu-ɗon kadi, sabu ko miin woni on neldaaɗo e Yahwe ngam hisde mon, hay on mo Muusaa yamiri ɗooftaare mon, onon musiɓɓe am saliiɓe.

Nii woni no winndiraa e maɓɓe, nii kadi ɗum timminirtee e juuɗe am doole, sabu Yaweh feññinanii annabaaɓe mum goonga Iisaawu e ñalɗi bonanndeeji mum ɗi keewi. Sabi no winndiraa gila law: Ngam bonannde maa e musiɗɗo maa Yaaquuba, hersa maa suuɗa, maa taƴee haa abada. Nde ndari-ɗaa bannge goɗɗo oo, ñalnde jananɓe njahri jawdi mum, jananɓe naati e dame mum, mbaɗi kuraa e dow Yerusalaam, hay an a jeyaa e maɓɓe.

Paamee ndeen, onon Isra'iila'en, ceergal cakkitiingal hakkunde Yaaquuba e Iisaawu arii e mooɗon, mi arii laɓɓinde leydi fodanoondi ndii e juuɗe laamɓe ɓe njibinaaɓe, Iisaawu maa rutte haa laaɓa, te Iisaawu noon no winndiraa e mum: Galle Yaaquuba maa won moto, galle Yuusuf wona jalɓe, galle Iisaawu wona koppi, ɓe njuula nder mum en, ɓe njoñca ɗum en: te heddotaako hay gooto e galle Iisaawu: sabu Joomiraaɗo haalii ɗum. Ndeen paamee onon iwɓe e Iisaawu jaŋgooɓe Konnguɗi am, ko Yaweh haali hay gooto waawaa waylude, te sakkitoode mooɗon maa won sakkitoode timmunde, on maa rutte haa laaɓa, on njibintaako kadi.

Sabu miin woni Raawaandu leñol Yahuuda, te e nuunɗal mi arii ñaawoyde e haɓoyde. Ndeen nanee Konnguɗi am onon Isra'iila'en, cuuɗee e ñalawma garɗo e mooɗon oo. Cuuɗee e neɗɗo bakkaat garɗo e mooɗon oo, sabu Barabas arii e juuɗe mum maayde ndu alaa keerol, ndi o yettiri no cellal kaawniingal fenaande, ndi o fodi rokkude nguurndam duumingam. Kono ɓe keɓataa nguurndam e maayde ɗiɗmere ndee, so wonaa halkere jokkere enɗam e nder yiite caɗtungo lepte, ɗo liɗɗi maɓɓe maayataa.

Miin woni cattoowo kode e dow deftere am ndonaandi, hay e dow seedee leydi ndi soodi-mi e yiiƴam am jamfaaɗam e juuɗe Yaweh e hoore mum. Te mi arii ruttaade jooɗiiɓe ɓee e fof ko laamɓe ɓe njibinaaɓe neli, hay e laamɓe ɓe njibinaaɓe e koye mum en, te waktuuji maɓɓe timmii. Ndeen paamee ko arata, Isra'iila, sabu ɓe ndarnat laamu maɓɓe, ɓe nji'ee no jaaliiɓe, te maantooje e kaawniiɗe fenaande keewɗe ɓe kollirta ngam ɓe njiifoo fof yimɓe welɓe e bonannde. Te ɓe nji'ay ɓe ndarnii fuɗɗoode jamaanu maɓɓe kaŋŋeewu ngu ɓe njiɗi darnude, sabu ɓe iwii e Baaba maɓɓe Ibliis, te Ibliis woni liingu mawngu nguu, ndeen, anniyaaji liingu nguu ɓe mbaɗata.

Kono, ɓe keɓataa jamaanu kaŋŋeewu so wonaa ñaawoore tiiɗnde e saɗtunde, nde ñaawoore ndee fuɗɗotoo yaawde no goonɗinɓe am e Almasiihu mooɓtorte e am e nder duule no mi feññinirta koye am e aduna oo fof. Kono, ɓe njenat, ɓe kaalan aduna oo wonde añɓe arooɓe e dow leydi nanngii heewɓe e dow leydi, te ɓe ngartat, te leydi ndii fotaa heblanaade haɓde e njiyaaɓe ɓee. Kono e goonga ɓe ngeblii koye maɓɓe ngam haɓde e hareeji ñalawma mawɗo oo, hay e ñalawma garol am ɗiɗmol, hay e ñalawma ɗo colli kammu njamɗata e teewu laamɓe maɓɓe laamɓe leydi, te noon wonirta.

Te no ñaawooje ɗee keewrata, no kode ɗee cattortee (pucciiɓe ɓee njahata) te buubaaje pooɗee, te ɗum waɗata hay no winndiraa e Yuhanna Feññinoowo e kadi e fawaade e Konnguɗi Linjiila duumingam ngam Isra'iila heblanaade ñalawma caɗeele mum en, heblanaade ñalawma kisal mum en, ngu leelataa. Te heewɓe maa njuurnoo Alla asama ngam ñaawooje garɗe e maɓɓe, te heewɓe tuubataa, te ɓurɓe heewde maa njaɓa torde maɓɓe ɓurnde bonde, ɓe njillondira e karallaagal ngal ɓe paamataa, sabu karallaagal maɓɓe woni ngam halkude Aadama, ndeen cuuɗee onon Isra'iila'en.

Te annabaaɓe am ɗiɗo, hay e lampaaji am ɗiɗi, maa ummoo, ɓe njanngana heewde geɗe cuuɗiiɗe e ɓerɗe yimɓe, te ɓe peeñnina heewde ko majjunoo e binndanɗe law, te goonga anndintee, te laamɓe ɓe njibinaaɓe peeñnintee haa laaɓa, te Edom laɓɓintee ɗo Iisaawu ummii, te fof yimɓe kawritiiɓe peeñnintee, te goonga anndintee. Te ɓe njettinay Linjiila duumingam, te seedanteeje maɓɓe ɓeydotoo e binndanɗe laaɓɗe haa abada, haa abada. Kono hay e jamaanu ñaawoore, yurmeende am laamii haa heewi, sabu e nokku ñaawoore yaawnde ndi muumtunoo leydi nguurndam, yurmeende e waktu tuubugol rokkete hade ñaawoore sakkitiinde waɗeede. Ndeen, heewɓe ngarat e Joomiraaɗo mum en nuunɗo duumingam, te mi jaɓɓotoo ɓe e koye am, mi reena ɓe haa abada ngam anniyaaji Yaweh duumingam ɗi ɓe kisnaa ngam timminde.

Te nde ɓe timminii seedanteeje maɓɓe, mbeewa iwka e lugge nde alaa les, maa haɓa e maɓɓe, foola ɓe, wara ɓe, te ɓalli maɓɓe maa leloo e laabi ñalɗi tati e feccere. E sakkitoode waktu o, ɓe ummotoo e nguurndam kesam no ɓe nanirta daande doole ndee ummoraade e asama, ndi wiyata e yeeso maɓɓe fof: “Ngaree ɗoo!” Te ɓe ummintee, ɓe nanngee e asama e yeeso maɓɓe fof.

Te no suudu dewal timminirtee, ɓiɗɗo liingu nguu maa yaha ngam laɓɓinde suudu dewal nduu, kono o waɗan bonannde halkere e nokku laaɓɗo oo, o waɗa sadak oo dartoo e caka yontere, ɗo o haalanta hoore makko wonde ko Alla, o firlitoo e yimɓe ɓee. Te yiiƴam maa ilnoo no feewi, te yimɓe saliiɓe Konnguɗi am, kawritiiɓe e Barabas, hay e heewɓe e maɓɓe, maa mbaree hay no winndiraa: Te Malaa'ika oo werlii sikkitoore mum tiiɗnde e dow leydi, taƴi wuro inabooje leydi ndii, werlii ɗum en e ñiɓirde mawnde lepte Alla. Te ñiɓirde ndee ñiɓiraa yaasi wuro ngo, te yiiƴam yalti e ñiɓirde ndee, haa yettoyii e laamuuji pucci, e njaajeendi ujunnaaje jeegom e jeegom furlong.

Ndeen paamee pitam ngam mooɗon, Isra'iila, te nceeree e laabi Iisaawu, naatanee e juuɗe Mesiya mooɗon goongaajo, te mi hisinan heddiiɓe ɓee ngam koye am. Te noon wonirta, mi arat e dow leydi e dow hoore Siyon e 144,000 am, te mi ardotoo on e nokku jeereende mo Yaweh heblani, ɗo on toppitete, on reenatee e lepte ñalawma mawɗo oo. Sabu ɗum waɗata hay no winndiraa: Maayɓe e nder Joomiraaɗo, ko barkinaaɓe sanne. Hay noon wiyi Ruuhu: sabu ɓe pooftii e golle maɓɓe, te golle maɓɓe ina njokki e maɓɓe.

Te no jawdi am nanngirtee e ladde mum kisal, lepte timmuɗe Alla maa njellee e dow laamu mbeewaaji ɗii, hay e fof yimɓe hoɗɓe e dow leydi e bonannde, te ɗum waɗata hay no winndiraa, te muuseendi mawndi, e batte bonɗe, maa njanoo e yimɓe ndarniiɓe koye mum en e Raawaandu leñol Yahuuda, te lepte mawɗe e torra ɓe ndañata sabu butelaaji lepte ɗi njelletee e dow leydi, te noon wonirta.

Ndeen paamee hol mi woni, onon Isra'iila'en, sabu on nganndaa Yaweh, on njokkataa laabi mum, kono on ndewii allaaji fenaande ɗi baabiraaɓe mooɗon nganndaa, hay e laabi Iisaawu kawritiiɗo e laamɓe ɓe njibinaaɓe e fedde kawritinde hoore mum en e deftere mum en fenaande nde ɓe mbinndi aadaaji yimɓe e dow goonga konnguɗi Yaweh duumingam, ɗum waɗi ɓe ndewii, ɓe njoganii tagooje e nokku tagoowo, mo barkinaa haa abada, Ee e Aamiin!

Sabu:

Miin woni garɗo e lepte timmuɗe ɓiɗɗo oo, te mi ñaawoyan fof yimɓe tuppunooɓe am, e fof yimɓe dariiɓe e am nde ngar-mi e konu asama, te ɓe halkirtee e laawol kaafaahi ɗiɗi keerol yaltungol e hunuko am.

Miin woni Yeshua HaMashiach garɗo ñaawoyde e haɓoyde, te e nuunɗal duumingam laamu am duumingam wonirta.

Miin woni cattoowo hoore liingu nguu, ko kañum woni mboddi ndii, hay e Seyɗaani e hoore mum, te e gaññeeje mum o sellataa, te laamu mum ummataako kadi, sabu o wonaa laamɗo, te lepte mum ko ɓurɗe tiiɗde, sabu e juuɗe mum tagooje bonniraa fuɗɗoode, te heewɓe nanngiraama e pewje mum.

Miin woni Joomiraaɗo Joomiraaɓe, Laamɗo Laamɓe, te lepte am ina tiiɗi, te ñaawoore am arii, te waktuuji yontere capanɗe jeeɗiɗi Daniyel ina arana yande e dow leydi ɗoon e ɗoon, hay no mi nanngirta goonɗinɓe e Almasiihu e hoore am e kisal jooɗorde Yaweh, ɗo ɓe nganndata hay no ɓe nganndiraa, te jom suudu oo maa hooy e hoore mum jom suudu mum heblaniiɗo jom suudu mum e laɓɓingol heewngol e jamfa e nder yiiƴam duumingam jamfa ɓiɗɗo oo.

Ko ɗuum waɗi, paamee wonde ko miin woni on mo fonnditata bonanndeeji keewɗi e nder ñaawoore, tee ko miin woni on mo winndaa e mum: « Joomiraaɗo wiyi yoɓo ko kañum jeyi, tee ko kañum yoɓtotoo ». E ma wonan, mi ittiray Esawu haa laaɓa e leydi fodanoondi ndii, miin woni kisnoowo ndonu Yaaquuba e juuɗe am doole ɗe mi halfini ɓiɓɓe fodanooɓe ɓee, ɗe ɓiɓɓe ɓanndu nduu njaafaaka sam e majje.

Ko ɗuum waɗi, paamee Konnguɗi am, onon yimɓe Israa’iila, paamee ko mi timminoyta, sabu ko nii winndaa e Esawu, tee ko nii maa waɗire: Kono a (Esawu) fotnooma yiirde ñalawma musiɗɗo maa, ñalnde o waɗaa jananɗo, fotaano a seynude e ɓiɓɓe Yahuuda, ñalnde halkaare mum en: fotaano a mawninkinaade e ñalnde fitina mum en. Fotaano a naatde e damal yimɓe am e ñalnde halkaare mum en, fotaano hay so tawii laar-ɗaa fitina mum en e ñalnde halkaare mum en, fotaano a fonndude juuɗe e jawdi mum en e ñalnde halkaare mum en. Fotaano a daraade e laabi ɗiɗi ngam taƴude ɓe, ɓe ndogata, fotaano a uddude heddiiɓe ɓee e ñalnde fitina. Sabu ñalawma Joomiraaɗo oo YAWIIMA, o arii e dow heeferɓe fof: no mbaɗir-ɗaa nii, maa mbaɗire: njoɓdi maa ndii artay e hoore maa.

Ko ɗuum waɗi:

Onon yimɓe Israa’iila, paamee tiiɗnaare jamaanuuji garanɗi ngam rimɗinde on timmunde, hay jamaanuuji ɗo leydi ndii laɓɓiniretee haa laaɓa e bonanndeeji, haa timma e garal am ɗiɗmal e leydi ɗo mi tabitinta Laamu Asamaan timmungu e leydi, ɗo leydi ndii laamirtee e nuunɗal Alkawal am Kesal ngal mi jaɓi, mi tappi e ƴiiƴam am kisnoojam.

Onon yimɓe Israa’iila, ceerte e Esawu, ngarte e Mesiya mon goonga oo, mi jaɓɓoto on e am, mi gaynoo on sabu ko miin woni Gaynaako Mawɗo gaynoowo jawdi Yaahwe e juuɗe am ɗe ngalaa ella.

Onon yimɓe Israa’iila, ceerte timmunde e laabi Esawu, ngarte e binndanɗe laaɓtuɗe ɗe Yaahwe, njalte timmunde e hakkunde maɓɓe e konngol, e golle, e kuugal, onon ɓiɓɓe am, mbele on ndañataa e ñaawooje maɓɓe, mbele on nattataa e caɓi Barabas, sabu hol fowru waɗoowo caɓi yeeso colli?

Onon yimɓe Israa’iila, cuuɗee e tikkere garoore ndee, ceerte e laawol tooñooɓe, mi reentoo on e laawol, mi hisna on e halkaare mon, hay so tawii halkaare ndee ummorii ko e juuɗe añɓe mon, hay maande maɓɓe mbeeyu ndee nde hay gooto hisnataa.

Ko miin, Yesuwa HaMasiya, tee ko miin neldi ɗee konnguɗi rewrude e nelaaɗo am e maccuɗo am, tee ko rewrude e nelaaɗo am e maccuɗo am ɗee Konnguɗi neldaa, Linjiila Duumotooɗo oo feeñnii e sahaa mum, tee Konnguɗi Linjiila Duumotooɗo oo keɓay gite, noppi, e ɓerɗe kala ɓe ɗi neldaa.

Ndaaree! Wakkati am arii, miin woni on garoowo tigi-rigi ngam ƴettude gooŋɗinɓe am e Almasiihu, tawa ko wuurɓe walla maayɓe, tee Aduna oo fof annday feeñde am, kono tan woto ngoonɗinee fenaande maɓɓe, onon yimɓe Israa’iila, sabu feeñde am ndee ko maande goonga e garanti garal am ɗiɗmal ngal waɗoyta tigi-rigi no fodorinoo gila adan nii, tee alaa ɗo añɓe am cuuɗotoo ɗo mi yiitataa hay gooto e maɓɓe sabu hay gooto waawaa daɗde e juuɗe am doole.

Ko miin woni on mo Yaahwe e hoore mum rokki doole ngam artirde tagu nguu e tagɗo nguu e nuunɗal timmungal e laaɓal goonga, tee mi waɗay tigi-rigi ko Yaahwe yamiri sabu miɗo ɗoftoo sahaa fof daande Baaba am e ɗooftaare timmunde mbele kala Yod, e kala gaska Yod, ina timmina e nuunɗal fof.

Ko miin woni Mbaroodi leñol Yahuuda, tee miɗo ara ngam wirnude haa abada hoore ndeya mbonon-waaldi mawndi, hay ndeya mboddi ɓooyndi ndi peeje mum bonnii ngesa Yaahwe gila e puɗɗoode, KO ƊUUM WAƊI, hol baawɗo daraade yeeso on mo MAWƊO MIIN oo semmbini, o renndi e mum jappeere laamu, sabu ko El Sadday gollirta doole e juuɗe am doole ɗe o semmbini, tee hay gooto waawaa seerndude mi e anniyaaji am duumotooɗi, sabu ko miin woni on mo jippinta bonannde fof, nde meeɗataa ummaade, nde laamoo kadi.

Ndaaree! Ko miin woni on mo hannde dilli ngam waɗde añɓe am tappeere koyɗe am, mi doolana ɓe fowde koppi maɓɓe murtuɗi ɗii e ñaawooje am, tee miɗo ara ngam halkude jooɗiiɓe ɓee, mi nela ɓe e lepte e ñaawooje ɗe ɓe meeɗataa daɗde.

Ko miin woni garoowo ngam yoɓtaade laatirooɓe tooñooɓe, mi hisna Yaaquuba haa abada e juuɗe maɓɓe.

Miin woni on mo artirta tagu nguu e jam caggal ñalawma fitina oo, mi njoɓa feewɓe e nuunɗuɓe fawaade e golle maɓɓe, tee haa abada en naatoyta e jam e deeƴre ɗo weltaare timmunde e weltaare laatoo e kala, tee Yaahwe waɗay hoɗorde mum e hakkunde yimɓe, hay e oon ñalawma mawɗo nde mi rokkata laamu gonngu e les am nguu e juuɗe makko doole mbele Yaahwe wontoo kala e kala, tee mbela ñalawma ngootaagu e timmunde oo maa wel.

Miɗo yiɗi on, onon leñol am, tee miɗo ara ngam haɓannde on, onon yiɗɓe am, ko ɗuum waɗi, ndenndee e daande Mesiya mon goonga oo, paamee konnguɗi Yesuwa HaMasiya, hay on mo on njogii kadi haala e mum, onon yimɓe Israa’iila, sabu ko nii mi woniri.

Ko miin woni Mbaroodi leñol Yahuuda!
