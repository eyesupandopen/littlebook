Seedeeji Ɗiɗi: Isra'iila
Ko Welɗi e ko Hawni
Noowammbar 25, 2024

Jowtere Arii

Nanɗee, nanɗee, Konnguɗi am onon Isra'iila, ɓiccon ɓerɗe mon e ɗowtaare to daande am, njaɓon e newaare Konnguɗi Yeesu Almasiihu, mbele e Konnguɗi Linjiila Duumotooɗo on keɓa kisal, on njottoo e nguurndam duumotooɗam nder Almasiihu mon goongaajo, mo haɓantoo on tigi-rigi e yiɗde. Nii woni mi arii ngam rentinde on, hay onon iwdi Yaquuba e kala janngooɓe njaɓa konnguɗi Linjiila Duumotooɗo e goonɗinal kisinoowal, mbele ɓe ngoonɗina Yeesu Almasiihu, mo woni kadi Yeesu Almasiihu, e mbele ɓe ngoonɗina Yaahwe Mawɗo MIIN WONI mo Linjiila Duumotooɗo suuɗinoo e nder ɓernde mum haa sahaaji peeñninooje mum. Ko noon Yuhanna Peeñninoowo oo heɓɓiri daandeeji diƴƴe jeeɗiɗi ɗe o nani ɗee. Nii woni mi arii ngam rentinde on e jowtere ndee, nde sofi gere mum, nde nangi aduna oo e ɓaŋe mum; hono no nde ñammirta ƴiiƴam nguurndam woɗɓe nii, e mbele on mbaawa daɗde e gereeji mum keewɗi, woto on nangiree e ɓaŋe mum warooje.

Ko ɗum waɗi, ɓiccon ɓerɗe mon e ɗowtaare, nanee konnguɗi Almasiihu mon goongaajo, hono Yeesu Almasiihu! Sabu mi arii tigi-rigi ngam waɗde neɗɗo hakkilante e mon ɓeydoo hakkilanaade, mbele on paama sahaaji ɗi ngonɗon ɗii, e mbele on paama ayɓe mon e sifaa mum en e laabi mum en fenaande; sabu ɓe ina njaha e suuraare mawnde e cuccu, e e fenaande ɓe ngollirta hareeji. Sabu kamɓe fof ko fenooɓe fennitooɓe, jowtere ndee ina yaha e suuraare mawnde e cuccu haa sahaa nde mbaroodi jowtere ndee nanngete e gere mum, nde jowtere ndee huutortoo sahaa fof ngam anniyaaji mum bonɗi.

Ko ɗum waɗi, mi arii ngam rentinde on e jowtere ndee, mi holla on laabi mum mbele on mbaawa daɗde e tooke mum, on ndeenira ko'e mon e ɓaŋe mum jeɗɗinooje, warooje. Kono hol jowtere waɗtunoo hoore mum laamu e leñol? Hol jowtere naatunoo e kawral mboninooɓe, hay e weltaare ina waɗa geɗal mum e mbonannde murtoore? Hol jowtere jaggitunoo inɗe nanngaaɓe e gere mum? Kono noon jowtere nde ngar-mi rentinde on ndee wayi, tigi-rigi, jowtere ndee ko gootal e geɗe keewɗe e laamu ayɓe, kadi ko jowtere tookeene, tookeene tigi-rigi.

Sabu jowtere ndee ina sofa gere mum e cuccu e fenaande. Hay gere fenaande e gere nyamaande jowtere ndee sofata, gere mum ndee jooni ina huɓɓi leydi ndii fof e ɓaŋe mum cuccuɗe. Miilo-ɗee jowtere e nokku mum tagoore, ina sofa gere mum e cuccu e nokkuuji ɗo mbaawata nangeede mbaroodi mum, hay so mbaroodi mum ndii jippii e gere mum tawa anndaa. Nde mbaroodi mum ndii haɓata ngam yaltude e gere nde nanngiraa tawa anndaa, nde ɓeyda tanngude hoore mum, jowtere ndee ummotoo e golle, tuppa tooke mum, huɓɓa mbaroodi mum e gere mum. Ndeen jowtere ndee reena mbaroodi mum ngam anniyaaji mum, ñamma seeɗa-seeɗa mbaroodi mum haa alaa ko heddii, acca mbaroodi mum e ngoƴa haa tigi mbaroodi mum wontaa.

Kono noon laabi jowtere tagoore taganaande anniyaaji Yaahwe wayi, so wonaa hol e mon yiyɓe jowtere ina golondira e laamu e leñol jowteeji goɗɗi; kono mboninooɓe ɓee kawritii jowteeji mum en e ɗiiɗoo sahaaji cakkitiiɗi. Nii woni mi arii ngam rentinde on e yimɓe iwdi Yafet, jibinɗo Gomer, Gomer jibini Ashkenaz, e Ashkenaz leñol sosaa sabu Ashkenaz ina wonnoo e doggol leƴƴi e yamirooje ɓooyɗe, hay e balɗe ɗo yimɓe ngonnoo e les ahdi Nuuhu, ahdi ndi yimɓe ngoppi; kono tawi ina woodi Ibriima koolaaɗo, ɗaɓɓuɗo laabi Yaahwe nde leydi ndii murtunoo kadi e Tagɗo mum en. Kono hay so tawii, neɗɗo tagoowo oo salii ahdi Yaahwe; Yaahwe ko koolaaɗo e alkule mum no woodiri fof. Alaa hay alkulal gootal e ahdi ndi Yaahwe waɗi e Nuuhu (mo wonnoo neɗɗo deeƴɗo e yeeso Yaahwe) taƴaani e leydi tawa timmaani, fof maa won ee e aamiin haa abada sabu Yaahwe ko koolaaɗo timmuɗo sahaa fof!

Caggal nde leydi ndii yooliraa e balɗe Nuuhu, leydi ndii fecciraa e keɓe ndonaandi hakkunde ɓiɓɓe Nuuhu e balɗe Peleg, nii woni Yafet naati e ndonaandi mum e leyɗe worgo. Yimɓe ɓee ko ɗemngal gootal, ɓiɓɓe Haam kawri e leydi ndi wonti Babilon. Laamɓe Nimrod njanngondiri e Fuut, e Misra, e Kuus, e Kanan e galleeji mum en ngam mahde wuro mum en, e nder mum galle toowɗo, hay galle toowɗo sanne, mbele ɓe mbaawa haɓde e kammuuli, e mbele ɓe kawra, mbaɗana hoore mum en innde e murtal yamiroore Yaahwe yo ɓe keew, ɓe kebbina leydi. Kono noon laabi neɗɗo tagoowo mo alaa goonɗinal, mo e yiɗde mum e mawnikinaare mum gollaa e hakke, o gollii Baaba hakke; hono seytaane, rewrude e ɗowtaare yimɓe e Yaahwe sabu hakke wonnde e ɓanndu neɗɗo tagoowo.

E iwdi Nimrod ɓeydiinde bonannde, ina wiyee ummaade e bonɗo yaltata bonannde mawnde. Ko noon Nimrod polliri leydi ndii e laabi mboninooɓe, e sihirɓe mum e kufeeɓe mum ngari duɗe sirri ɓooyɗe ɗo kufeeɓe mboninooɓe ɓee njanngini sirriiji mum en baasɗi ɗii to ɓe mbardete e yiite haa abada e hakkeeji seytaaneeji, ɓe naatondira e mum en e ndonaandi mum en duumotoondi. Nimrod ummii e laamu gadano caggal iyeende Nuuhu, hay ɗo Nimrod laamii leydi ndii e bonannde; hay laamu nguu mboninooɓe ɓee njiɗata yo ɓe keɓan hoore mum en timmunde e dow leydi, kono alaa duuɓi kaŋŋe keɓata so wonaa ñaawoore tiiɗnde.

Nii woni Yaahwe e ñaawoore mum waɗi leydi udditii, ñooli tataɓal galle mum en, yiite jippii ummoraade e kammu, ñooli tataɓal goɗngal galle mum en, tataɓal gootal accaa ngam wonde siftorde e dow mum en. Ko ɗum waɗi, innde mum inniraa Babel, sabu Yaahwe senndindini ɗoon ɗemngal leydi ndii fof: ummaade ɗoon Yaahwe saaktini ɓe e dow yeeso leydi ndii fof. Leƴƴi e yimɓe keewɗi calii, njijjiti Yaahwe mo naati e ahdi e Nuuhu, ko noon ɓe njijjitiri ahdi ndi Nuuhu waɗi e Yaahwe fawaade e ndonaandi mum en e fawaade e ɗemɗe ɗe ɓe kaalatnoo, ɓe njijjiti ahdi Yaahwe e Nuuhu, ɓe njuurnii laabi mboninooɓe sabu alaa cuɓal goɗngal. (Kono Ibriima wonti Ibraahiima, deeƴɗo oo maa wuuran e goonɗinal!)

To Babilon, Nimrod waɗi bonannde mawnde hay so o waɗi tooruuji keewɗi, o wiyi hoore makko ko reweteeɗo, o waɗi tatiire makko jeenoore, hay tatiire laaɓaande, anniya mum ko jaltude tatiire goongaare e aawdi neldaandi rewru nduu, e majjinde ɓiɓɓe Aadama e bonannde. Ko noon Nimrod sosi duɗe mum wi'aaɗe sirri, nde ganndal mboninooɓe ngal fuɗɗii saaktaade, huɓɓa leydi, yimɓe mbayli kadi goonga Yaahwe wonti fenaande, ɓe ndewi, ɓe ngollii tagoodi ɓurnde Tagɗo mo barkinaa haa abada, Aamiin!

Hono no yimɓe ɓee saaktirnoo ummoraade Babilon nii kadi ganndal fenaande e laabi mboninooɓe saaktirnoo e maɓɓe, hay e juulɗe mum en fof ñidduɗe, jeenooje, ɗe ɓe ñammata teewu e ƴiiƴam e ɓe njarata ƴiiƴam Aadama ɗo nguurndam ɓanndu woni; hay golle bonɗe, ñidduɗe sanne, ɗe Yaahwe haɗi laawol gootol, fof, haa abada, sabu Aadama tagaa ko e mbaadi Yaahwe, nguurndam ɓanndu ko e nder ƴiiƴam woni. Kono ɓe ina njokki e laabi mum en ñidduɗi, jeenooji haa hannde, golle mum en e juulɗe mum en ndeeniraama e nder rewɓe seytaaneeji e sujjotooɓe tooruuji muumɗi, ɗii ko laabi jowtere, kono jowtere ndee ina fenna wonde ko Yaquuba deeƴɗo, mo alaa heen geɗal, alaa heen feccere.

Nii woni laabi bonannde ɓitaama e laabi yimɓe leƴƴi kadi. E Gomer yalti Ashkenaz, e Ashkenaz e sahaaji yalti leñol mawngol yimɓe wontunooɓe egga-hoɗa, hoɗnooɓe, njahnooɓe e ladde Eurasi e saraaji mum, hay ɗo ɓe mbaɗi laamu mum en. Hay so tawii, laamu mum en mawnii e bonannde, ngo yani e ayɓe mum en no woodiri fof. Hay so tawii ayɓe mum en kaɓii e mum en sabu bonannde mum en, e nokku nde ɓe njaɓata Yeesu Nasaraat, ɓe ngoppa bonannde, ɓe nfenna ko ɓe Yaquuba, e nder Yaquuba alaa leñol Ashkenaz iwngol e Yafet. Ko noon ɓe caliri Yeesu Nasaraat sabu ɓe ngoni ko e baaba mum en seytaane, yiɗde mum ko ɓe kaɓɓaa haa abada ngam waɗde.

E nder yimɓe Ashkenaz ɓe laabi mum en ngonnoo bonɗi, fenaanɗe, yalti yimɓe, so wonaa e leñol Aadama ɓe timmaani, kono ko aawdiiji bonɗi aawaaɗi hakkunde alkama Yaahwe, mbonnii hoore mum en haa abada e bonannde. Hay ɓe jibinaa e baaba mum en seytaane sabu aawdi seytaane ina heddii haa jooni, hay so tawii mi ɗoocitay ɗi, mi warda ɗi e fornere hono no Yaahwe haaliri nii. E Ashkenaz yalti jowtere kono wonaa jowtere tagoore, kono ko leñol jowteeji ñidduɗi, hay so tawii mbaylaama e mbaadi Yaahwe Tagɗo mum en, kaɓɓaaɓe haa abada e yiɗde Baaba mum en seytaane, yiɗde mum en ɓe ngollata. Nii woni jowteeji ɗii saaktiraa, hay so tawii ɓe ndogii e juuɗe ayɓe bonannde daraniiɓe laabi jowtere ndee. Ko noon Ashkenaz saliri Yeesu Almasiihu, mo alaa heen geɗal, alaa heen feccere.

Nii woni e goonga, yimɓe majjinaama e mboninooɓe gila Aadama yani e ngonka mum alaa hakke, ngonka ka o taganoo e jarne Eden, bonannde saaktii e leydi rewrude e iwdi Kayinu. Bonannde ndee ndeenooɓe yaniiɓe ɓeydi bonnude nde ɓe njipii e dow tulde Hermon e balɗe Yared. Ko ɗum waɗi, Yaahwe laɓɓini bonannde mum en e yeeso leydi ndii e ndeen iyeende mawnde e balɗe Nuuhu, kono mboninooɓe ɓee cosi laabi mum en kadi caggal iyeende ndee, sabu ko noon Yaahwe acciri yimɓe suɓaade hoore mum en. Sabu tigi-rigi, Yaahwe hokkii kala ko o tagi e suɓngo e nder tagoodi mum ndimaagu suɓaade so ɓe nguuran laabi Yaahwe walla ɓe suɓoo gollaade seytaaneeji e murtal mum en, murtal ngal ɓadtiima timmude. Sabu miin, hay Yeesu Almasiihu, ko miin fiyoytooɗo hoore mboɗaari, hay mo bonnoyta laamu mum haa abada, ngo ummataa kadi.

Nii woni, ko nii goonga ka njanngin-mi ko fayti e laamu kammuuli nde ngar-mi to Isra'iila hono Baaƴel Alla e sahaa am e nder ɓanndu to Yahuuda. Sabu ko noon, winndaama: Laamu kammuuli ina nanndi e neɗɗo aawɗo aawdi moƴƴiri e ngesa mum. Kono nde yimɓe naniiɗi, ayɗo mum ari, aawi aawdiiji bonɗi hakkunde alkama, yahri laawol mum. Nde leɗɗe ɗee puɗi, njoɓi ɓiɓɓe, ndeen aawdiiji bonɗi ɗii kadi peeñi. Ndeen gollooɓe joom galle ngari, mbiyi mo, Joomiraawo, wota a aaw aawdi moƴƴiri e ngesa maa? Hol to aawdiiji bonɗi ɗii ngari? O wiyi ɓe, Won neɗɗo jom kañu waɗi ɗum. Ndeen gollooɓe ɓee mbiyi mo, Aɗa yiɗi ndeen min njehen min moofta ɗi? O wiyi, Alaa, so wonaa so on njahii mooftude aawdiiji bonɗi ɗii, on ɗoocitondira e mum en alkama. Accu ɗiɗo fof mawda haa wakkati soñño, e wakkati soñño mi wiyata soññooɓe ɓee, Mooftee gadane aawdiiji bonɗi ɗii, kaɓɓon ɗi e kalaaji ngam wuleede: kono mooftee alkama oo e ñibaare am.

Hay so tawii alkama ina sujjana e ɗowtaare tawi aawdiiji bonɗi ina ndarii e murtal bonngal. Selah!

Kono noon sahaaji mooftugol ngarii, mi mooftay alkama am e nder ñibaare Yaahwe sabu wakkati soññude ɓiɓɓe gadane arii. Nii woni maa wonta mi ɗoocitay aawdiiji bonɗi ɗii, mi haɓɓa ɗi, ɗi mbardete e yiite e ñaawoore mum en. Paamee leydi nde ngonɗon ndii, hay hareeji ɗi jibinaaɗon ɗii. Hay hareeji aawdi ɗi ngoni gila e jarne Eden, ɗo Yaahwe haalani mboɗaari e mboninooɓe hareeji sabu bonannde mum en e bonnude jarne Yaahwe. Hareeji ɗii timmata ko e fooloore timmunde wonande kala murtunooɓe e mbaadi nii e mboninooɓe, ndonaandi mum en yiite wirsu ina heddii.

Nii woni ko Yahweh yewti: Mi waɗan konnaagu hakkunde maa e debbo oo, e hakkunde iyal maa e iyal makko, aan a mursan teppere makko, kanko o mursan hoore maa. Nii teppere am mursiraa e maayde am dow gaafaangal nde ndokkirnoomi hoore am sadaka Paska timmuɗo hono Jawgel Alla e dow yiɗde am e juuɗe ayɓe am bonɓe. Kadi ko miin woni iyal debbo oo fodanooɗo, mo mursirta hoore Seytaane haa laaɓa, mi waɗan ayɓe am leeso koyɗe am, sabu ko miin woni Muudo leñol Yahuuda, e nder peewal miɗo ara ngam ñaawoore e haɓde!

Ko ɗum waɗi, paamee wonde hare iyal ndee foolaama haa laaɓi e nder Iisaa Almasiihu, hono no mbaɗirnoomi Petruus winndi duuɓi keewɗi ko ɓenni: jibinaaɗo kadi, wonaa e aawdi bonndi, kono e aawdi ndi bonnataa, e konngol Alla ngu wuuri, ngu heddoto haa abada. Kadi mi waɗii Yuhanna Cifotooɗo winndi e nder ɓataake mum: Kala jibinaaɗo e Alla, waɗataa anniya bonannde; sabu aawdi mum ina heddii e mum, o waawataa waɗde anniya bonannde, sabu ko e Alla o jibinaa. E nder ɗuum, sukaaɓe Alla ina peeñninaa, e sukaaɓe ibliis: kala mo gollaaki peewal, jeydaa e Alla, wanaa kadi mo yiɗaa musiɗɗo mum. Ko ɗum waɗi, kala mboɗɗinaaɗo e nder Almasiihu waawaa hay gooto bonnude ɗum en e sifaa oo, kadi ɓe mbaawaa waɗde anniya bonannde ɗo ɓe mbaylaaka e mboɗeendi Alla guurɗo oo, hono Yahweh mo ɓe njeyaa oo. Ko ɗum waɗi, woto accudee ko wayi no ndii baasal tampina on, wonde jibinaaɓe kadi e ruuhu am ceniiɗo baaɗeejo, mboɗɗinaaɓe e mboɗeendi Yahweh, mbaawaa hay gooto sooftude ɗum en e juuɗe am baawɗe ɗee hay dara. Ko ɗum waɗi, wonande heɓɓe kisal, kisal mon ina tabiti.

Nii mi feeñninta laabi mboddi ndii, mi feeñninta mboddi ndii haa laaɓa, hay so tawii mboddiiji ina njiɗi yahde e cuuraare mawnde, mi feeñninta laabi maɓɓe. Sabu ɗii mboddiiji baylaaɗi ko kamɓe keɓata jikkuuji ɓe njiɗi warde ɓee. Ɗee ko mboddiiji mawnintooɓe bonannde mawnde, njiɗɓe torraade poolgu mum en ngam waɗde tan ko Baaba mum en yiɗi, mo ɓe kaɓɓaa ngam rewnude, mo ɓe ngoni iyal mum. Ɗii mboddiiji baylaaɗi ɗi kaal-mi ɗii ko wuyɓe, e wuyɓe, e warooɓe, e fenaaɓe (e ko heewi goɗɗum ko ɓe mbaɗi bonannde) ɓe keɓataa hay dara, ɓe njiɗi maayde Aadama ngam heɓde duuɓi maɓɓe kaŋŋe haa abada wondude e laana-cerɗi ɗii. Nii mboddiiji ɗii njahri e aduna oo fof, ɓe pawi caalli maɓɓe e cuuraare e sirlu mawɗo.

Hono caalol nyamaande mawngol sabu mboddi ndii ina mawni, ina alɗini hoore mum e kaŋŋe nde heɓi e nder usura bonɗo. Mboddi ndii suuɗiima e nder Yaakubu, ina nanndi e mum, ɓe mbaɗii nanndingol laaɓngol. Sabu mboddi ndii waawaa hollirde hoore mum no mboddi bonndi, baylaandi nii; hol fowru waɗata pitirlooji e yeeso colli? Kono mboddi ndii ina suuɗoo, ina wayla mbaadi mum e jikkuuji mum ngam hawrude e ɓe njiɗi nyaamde ɓee. Nde mboddi ndii fuɗɗii fawde caalol mum e nder ɓe accunoo mboddi ndii nokku ngam fawde e nder leñol mum en; mboddi ndii ndeen addata mboddiiji goɗɗi, walla ɓe ngarata ngam tawtoreede mo e golle mum bonɗe, walla e laawol ɓoggi goɗɗi puccooɗi e boowal mboddi ndii, ɗi njahata ngam fawde caalli mum en, ɓe njokki e doggol maayde. Nii Aadama haɓɓiraa, cocconaa e ɗii araknid bonɗi. Seeɗa seeɗa e welwelo mawngo, ɓe njaɓata, ɓe nyaama Aadama, e bonannde mawnde e bonki, mboddi ndii ina ɗaɓɓita aduna oo fof ngam hoore mum e Baabaaji mum en, ɓe kaɓɓaa e yiɗde mum en.

Sabu e nder fenaande mum, mboddiiji Ashkenaz njokkondirii e Esau, e laawol Talmud, sabu duuɓi keewɗi ko ɓenni ɓe keɓi Talmud ko mawɗo maɓɓe e laamɗo maɓɓe, kono ɓe ngonaa janngooɓe Muusaa goonga, ɓe njahdaani e laawol Yeshua HaMashiach, kono ɓe mbeltortoo. E nder Esau ko warooɓe am ngoni, e laawol hakkilantaagal fenaande Esau, hay aadaaji maɓɓe bonɗi ɗi ɓe mawni dow laabi Yahweh, ɓe mbuli Yaakubu, ɓe njaɓi miñiiko Esau laabi keewɗi. Hono no mbiyrunoomi ɓe e nder wakkati am e nder ɓanndu hono Jawgel Alla: On njeyaa ko e baaba mon ibliis, on mbaɗan ko baaba mon yiɗi: ko o waroowo gila e fuɗɗoode, o heddaaki e goonga, sabu goonga alaa e mum. Nde o haalata fenaande, o haalata ko e mum: sabu ko o fenoowo, ko o baaba mum.

Kono mboddi ndii yaltinaa laabi laana-cerɗi ɗii, mboddi ndii ina jokki e tawtoreede, ina weltoo e golle mum bonɗe. Sabu mboddi ndii ina nyaama sukaaɓe ɓe cocconii ko poolgu mum, ina yara ƴiiƴam mum en e sukaaɓe ɓe torrata e nder ɓuuɓri mum. Sabu mboddi ndii ina timmina yiɗde mum bonnde e sukaaɓe ɓe njippinta e leñol mum en e cuuraare mawnde so sukaaɓe ɓee njaɓɓaama e caalol mum. Hol mboddi fawi caalol, kono kadi ina asa e leydi. Sabu caalol mboddi ndii ina yahra haa e les leydi, caalol mum ina yaaji, laamu aduna oo fof e laamu, mboddi ndii ina ɗaɓɓita. Nii mboddi ndii yahrata e fenaande mawnde, sabu e fenaande ɓe ngollata hare.

Kono ɗii fenaaɓe jokkondirɓe e laana-cerɗi ɗii haa abada, ɓe ngoni iyal mum en goonga; ina mbeltora wonde ko Yaakubu, ɓe mbiyi e fenaande wonde ko leydi alkawal ndii, nde ɓe ngalaa geɗal walla geɗal, kono ko fenaaɓe mbeltortooɓe. E laabi maɓɓe ummotoo ko pitirlooji ɗi kullel ummoraade e leydi ndii waɗanta Yaakubu, sabu e laawol Talmud Esau, Ashkenaz hawritii e Esau, ɓe kawritirta kadi e jawgel mbeewa ndee, hay jawgel maɓɓe fenaande ngel galluɗe ɗiɗi. Hay jawgel tawaa-ɗen ngel galluungal gootal laamii maayo ngoo fawaade e Nuh, goɗngal ngal laamii leydi alkawal ndii e nder bonannde mawnde e fenaande, sabu ko kaŋko woni galluungal famaroongal Daniyel, nii aduna oo laamiree laamu maɓɓe e nder wakkati seeɗa. Sabu ɓe kawritiran doole e pelle bonɗe fof e les laamu maɓɓe, ɓe ngartiran ɗum en ngam haɓde e ñalawma mawɗo oo, ɗo ɓe fof mbirsetee haa abada, ɓe naata e lepte e ñaawoore haa abada.

Kono laabi mboddi ndii ko laabi santaj, e nanngugol, e warngo, e bonannde, e laabi keewɗi bonɗi mboddi ndii jannginta, wuura, sabu mboddi ndii ina ɗaɓɓita waɗde mboddiiji goɗɗi ɗi ngoni sukaaɓe jahannama laabi ɗiɗi no kamɓe e koye maɓɓe. Nii mboddiiji ɗii njimata, ina njetta koye mum en e warngo maɓɓe e yimɓe leydi ndii, hay so tawii ina njima e juuɗe njokkondirɗe, ina njima e weltaare e maayde e mbonni poolgu mum en, heewɓe e maɓɓe ko rewɓe e sukaaɓe e bebunooɓe; nii mbonni maɓɓe heddotaako. Kono ɓe keɓii ardiiɓe aduna oo, ɓe njaɓi laamɓe aduna oo e dow yiɗde e e dow foolde, ɓe ɓadaaki foolde aduna oo fof e caalli maɓɓe keewɗi, e caalli maɓɓe aduna oo haɓɓiraa, rokkaa e juuɗe mbeewa ngam ñaawoore e mbonni ko ɓadaaki timmude. Kono mboddi ndii ina wulla e mette so ina nyaama poolgu mum, hay so tawii ina nanndi e waasɓe tooñaaɓe, kono e goonga ko mboddi ndii tooñata, bonnata Aadama.

Ko ɗum waɗi, ndenndee e daande am, leñol am, hay on fof janngooɓe ɗee konnguɗi, ceertindiree e mboddi ndii e caalli mum, sabu so neɗɗo wayliima e mbonni mum ngam noddude innde Joomiraawo, hono Iisaa Nasaraatjo, mo woni kadi Yeshua HaMashiach, mo woni Almasiihu goonga Israayiil; goonga, goonga, neɗɗo oo hisnan.

Goonga, goonga, miɗo yamira on fof yo on njaɓtu, on njaɓtu Talmud maɓɓe bonɗo, woni deftere bonannde mawnde, tuundi, e fenaande; hay deftere nde Esau winndi, nde Juulirde Seytaane jaɓi, nde bonannde e tuundi tan hoɗi. Sabu mboddi ndii ko Juulirde Seytaane, hay so tawii jibinaa ko e leñol Ashkenaz mo Yafet, mo alaa geɗal e ndonu Sem, teeŋti noon e ndonu Yaakubu peewɗo, mo mi hisnanta heddiiɓe baawɗe.

Ko ɗum waɗi, njalte e maɓɓe, ceertindiree, ngartiree e Yeshua HaMashiach ngam janngude e am, janngude nguurndam laabi peewɗi Yahweh, laabi ɗi mi woni, ɗi mi woni Konngol ngol waɗiraa ɓanndu, sabu Muusaa ina wuuri haa laaɓi e nder Yeshua HaMashiach, e nder Alkawal am Kesal ko alkule e alkule ɓooyɗe fof, alkule ɗe timminirtee haa laaɓi e nder laamu am duuɓi ujunere, Laamu nguu mi sincirta e peewal e aduna oo.

Ko miin woni Yeshua HaMashiach, Almasiihu goonga Israayiil, goonga ko miin woni Laamɗo mon, Joomiraawo mon, Hooreejo Alkawal mon, e Almasiihu mon haa abada, Laamu am e laamu am alaa ɗo haaɗi.

Ko miin ngar-mi ngam reentinde leñol am e ndii fenaande e mbonni mawɗo, mo haaɗi e aduna oo e nder caalol mum, sabu mboddiiji ɗii pawi caalli mum en e aduna oo fof jooni.

Ko miin woni Iisaa Almasiihu, mi neldii ɗee Konnguɗi e nelal am e maccuɗo am, e nelal am e maccuɗo am, ɗee konnguɗi neldaama.

Ko miin woni Iisaa Almasiihu, mo woni kadi Yeshua HaMashiach, mo feeñnini Konnguɗi Linjiila duumotooɗo wondude e Yahweh e hoore mum e nder wakkatiiji feeñninde jooni. Hay wakkatiiji feeñninde hade tampere Yaakubu fuɗɗaade haa laaɓa, ngam Yaakubu hisna.

Ko ɗum waɗi, paamee teddeendi Konnguɗi am, woto ngonon luutndiiɓe, kono njaɓee tan seedantaagal am, nanee Konnguɗi am, njalte e luutndagol e murtital, sabu jooni ko wakkatiiji ngartugol e Yahweh e laawol Yeshua HaMashiach, hay so tawii ko e wakkatiiji tampere, wakkatiiji ɗi ngari.

Kala garɗo e Almasiihu mum duumotooɗo, jaɓɗo mi e Alkawal am Kesal, heɓan kisal e nder tolnooji mum. Ɓe noddoowo innde Joomiraawo Iisaa Almasiihu e teddungal maayde, umminoytee e ummital peewɓe, teddeendi njeenaari maɓɓe duumotoondi ɓurtan caɗeele maɓɓe e tampereeji maɓɓe, ɗi mi semmbinirta ɓe ngam foolde, hono no Yahweh semmbinirnoomi ngam foolde.

E ɓe ummoriiɓe e Yaakubu ɓe mi suuɗata e jeereende gila e wakkatiiji tampere ɓurnde mawnude abada; caggal ɗuum ɓe keɓoytee e nguurndam e nder Laamu am duuɓi ujunere, hay so tawii ko ngam heɓde ɓiɓɓe, ɓe keewa, en nguurdan laabi Yahweh, sabu e peewal aduna oo laamiree, hay huunde bonnde naatataa heen.

Ko miin, Yeshua HaMashiach, kollitata timmal wakkatiiji ngam aduna oo jibina ɓe nani e nder Almasiihu, e hisnude ɓe wuuri, ɓe keddii e feeññinde am, ɓe tawetee ko goongɗinɓe e nder Almasiihu, sabu goonga, goonga, wakkatiiji ɗii ko jooni. Selah!

Miin, Yeshua HaMashiach, miɗo ara e mon, giɗiraaɓe am, miin ko mi ko Miin Mawɗo oo waɗi mi.

Nii paamee yiɗde nde njoganii-mi on, paamee yiɗde nde Yahweh joganii on, sabu goonga, goonga, yiɗde men ko yiite ɓuuɓɗo, yiɗde Yahweh waawaa foolde, sabu Yahweh waawaa foolde, Yahweh ko yiɗde, Yahweh ko annoora, miɗo nodda on e laamu yiɗde e annoora e peewal; hay so tawii ko ɗo teddungal hisnaaɓe ɓee jalbirta haa abada, tagoore ndee rimɗintee e rimɗinde teddunde sukaaɓe peewɓe Yahweh, ɓe mi ardotoo ko Laamɗo Laamɓe maɓɓe e Joomiraawo Joomiraaɓe maɓɓe.

Nii fof anndan on ko am, miin ko on, e yiɗde nde kollir-mi mi e nder ɗooftaare yamirɗe am, e yiɗde nde kollirton goɗɗo e goɗɗo so on njahata e nguurndam mon, hay so tawii ko e caɗeele. On njiɗan Yahweh e ɓernde mon fof, e ruuhu mon fof, e hakkilantaagal mon fof, e semmbe mon fof, goonga, goonga, on njiɗan koɗo mon hono no hoore mon nii, nii yamirɗe Yahweh ngoni.

Jokkee e muñal, jom suudu am, sabu wakkati rimɗinde mon timmunde arii. Nii miɗo siifoo innde am e seedantaagal Linjiila duumotooɗo, hay so tawii ko ngam waɗeede.

Siifaa e yiɗde Yahweh e nder ƴiiƴam am hisnugol haa abada!

Yahweh Mawɗo oo, e Yeshua HaMashiach mo woni kadi Iisaa Almasiihu, Aamiin.

Selah!
