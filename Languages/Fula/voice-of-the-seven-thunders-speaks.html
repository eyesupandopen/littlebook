1Gila e Yesu'a HaMashiach: Gimol Aranol!

Onon cuɓaaɓe am, suɓaaɓe e gooŋɗinɓe am, nanee Konnguɗi Joomi mon guurɗo e kisnoowo, kanko woni Yesu'a Masiihu! Leyɗe ɗe nguuroton e daartol ngol jannginooɓe mon Yesuwit en njannirinoo on ko ɗum woni caggal fenaande mawnde ndee, nde feeñnude mum ɓadti, nde jooni ndee ina yahra, ɗo ɓe njaɓɓuɓe ɓee peeñata hono no koñɗi moƴƴi nii, kono e goonga ko malaa'ika'en bonɓe njaɓɓuɓe. Hol ko wonnoo? Ko ɗuum wonata: hol kadi ko waɗanoo? Ko ɗuum waɗetee: alaa kadi huunde hesere les naange.

Seytaane hollitii ganndal mum ngal o inniri ngal ɓurngal yahrude yeeso gila e fuɗɗoode, haa e ganndal mum moƴƴere e bone. Kanko woni jiɓoowo e fenoowo mawɗo, o jannginii neɗɗaagu geɗe keewɗe ɗe Yahweh anniyaaki, sabu ɗee ko laabi maayde. Tagooje Yahweh tagaaka ngam jiɓeede ngam heɓde baawɗe e nafooje mum, walla ngam wonde laawol dogde e kiitaaji Alla, walla ngam sosde laabi e yuɓɓo mum. Sabu Seytaane bonnii neɗɗaagu gila e fuɗɗoode, o bonnii kadi reenooɓe njaɓɓuɓe ɓe naati e murto maɓɓe.

Oo, onon mawɓe gañooji nuunɗal fof, kanko Seytaane: bonanndeeji mon gadani ɗii ko Henuuk winndiyanke oo winndi ɗi gila e ndeen jamaanu, ɗi ndarii haa hannde, sabu bonanndeeji mon ɗii mbinndaama noon. Ko ɗum waɗi mi juɓɓitintoo, sabu on mbaɗii ɗee geɗe, kadi on mbaɗii geɗe keewɗe goɗɗe: On njiɓinii ɓiɓɓe Alla fof, on njippinii ɓe e leydi, on njiɓinii ɓe e juuɗe ɓiɓɓe rewɓe. On ndokkii ɓiɓɓe Alla ceniiɓe ɓee wasiyaaji bonɗi, on njiɓinii ɓe haa ɓe mbonni ɓalli maɓɓe e ɓiɓɓe rewɓe. On kollii ɓiɓɓe yimɓe fiyngooji maayde fof, on njiɓinii Hawa, on kollii kaɓirɗe maayde ɓiɓɓe yimɓe: tamre e wutte njamndi, e kaafa ngam haɓde, e kaɓirɗe maayde fof to ɓiɓɓe yimɓe. Gila e juuɗe mon ɗe njahrii e dow hoɗɓe e leydi gila ndeen ñalawma haa abada.

Onon Seytaane'en, on njannirii ɓiɓɓe yimɓe ko weli e ko ɓuuɓi, on njannirii ɓe sirluuji hakkilantaagal maɓɓe fof. On njamirii neɗɗaagu winndude e njaram e kaayitaaji, ko ɗum waɗi heewɓe mbaɗii bonannde gila e jamaanu haa e jamaanu haa hannde. Sabu yimɓe tagaaka ngam noon, ngam tabitinde goonɗinal maɓɓe moƴƴal e binndol e njaram. Sabu yimɓe tagaama no malaa'ika'en nii, ngam ɓe njokkoo e laaɓde e nuunɗude; maayde, ndi halkata kala huunde, waawaano nanngude ɓe, kono e nder ngal ganndal ɓe nattii, e nder ngal baawɗal ɗum nattini yimɓe.

Onon Seytaane'en, on kollii ɓiɓɓe yimɓe fiyngooji bonɗi fof ɗi ruuhuuji e jinneeji mbaɗata, e fiyngooji ɓiɗɗo e nder reedu, ngam o maaya, e fiyngooji ruuhu, e ɓoggi mboɗeeri, e fiyngooji baɗɗi e nguleeki hakkunde naange, ɓiɗɗo mboɗeeri ina wiyee Tabââ'ĕt. Sabu on naamndiima kadi Mika'il yo o hollu on innde suuɗiinde ndee, ngam on mbaawa wiide nde e nder hunoore ndee, ngam ɓeen njiɗɓe ndeen innde e ndeen hunoore njirloo, ɓeen peeñninɓe kala ko suuɗinoo e ɓiɓɓe yimɓe.

Sabu on mbaɗii reenooɓe ɓee naati e murto mon nde njokkuton e bonnude neɗɗaagu. Ko ɗum waɗi bonanndeeji reenooɓe ɓee mbinndaama e juuɗe Henuuk winndiyanke oo, mi juɓɓitintoo noon: Ɓe njannirii yimɓe waɗde kaafaaje, e laɓɓe, e tamre, e wutteeji njamndi, ɓe nganndini ɓe njamndiiji leydi e karallaagal golle mum, e jeewuɗe, e cuɗaari, e kuutoragol antimoine, e cuɗagol gite, e kala sifaa kaaƴe tedduɗe, e kala noonɗe nate. Ɓe njannirii dimgal, e taƴde ɗaɗi, e feewnude dimgal, e anndal koode, e koode, e anndal duule, e maandeeji leydi, e maandeeji naange, e yahdu lewru.

Ko ɗum waɗi e nder bonanndeeji mon e fenaandeeji mon, on mbonnii tagooje Yahweh e laabi mon bonɗi e mbonndeeji mon bonɗi. On njiɓinii neɗɗaagu, on mbaɗii bonannde e Yahweh, on tooñii neɗɗaagu, haa on mbaɗii bonannde e colli, e daabaaji, e kulle, e liɗɗi, bonanndeeji mon ngaddan on kiita mawɗo mo on kiitortaako. On mbonnii tagooje Yahweh e karallaagal mon e jiɓugol mon, on mbaɗii neɗɗaagu ina haawnii e fijooji mon e fenaandeeji mon. Sabu Yahweh hollitii on tan ko sirluuji tagooje mum ɗi ngalaa nafoore, o suuɗii sirluuji mum ɓurɗi teddude ɗii e nder ɓernde makko, o daɗndii ɗi e janfa mon.

Onon bonɓe, on njannirii laabi mon ɗi ngalaa nafoore ɗii e neɗɗaagu nde ɓe keewi e dow leydi, on ngaddanii ɓe musiiba mawɗo e halkere e nder peeje mon bonɗe e karallaagal mon. Sabu on ngaraani so wonaa ngam wujjde, warde, e halkude. On njannirii jiɓugol henndu, sabu on ko on laamɗo laamu henndu, sabu on njiɓi aithēr. Sabu nde Kayinu waɗi bonannde, o mahi wuro; on njannirii neɗɗaagu sirluuji mon e jiɓugol mon ɗi ngalaa nafoore. Ɗee gure kuutoraama ngam laamaade e jiɓde e addude halkere neɗɗaagu, no kuutorirno-ɗon ɗe ngam waɗde mbonndeeji mon halkooje.

On mbaɗii leñol ummanii leñol e wolde e luural ngal alaa ɗo haaɗi, on mbaɗii yimɓe am mbaɗii bonannde, Yahweh saaktii leƴƴi Isra'iila e hennduuji nay. Hay so tawii noon, anniya mon bonɗo oo ɓeydii faandaare Yahweh, sabu e saaktude maɓɓe, mooɓondiral baali heddiiɓe ɓee timmintee. Mi nelan kadi seedeeji am ɗiɗi ngam annabaade balɗe 1260 ɓoorniiɓe saakuuji. E nder golle mon fof ngam halkude ɓiɓɓe Yahweh on njaɓaani sanne, on mbaawaano yiyde wonde golle mon ɗee njahrii e faandaare Yahweh, sabu haa e gañooji mum ina nɗoftoo daande mum.

Sabu on torrii, on torrii leƴƴi 12 ɗii e nder leydi ndii fof nde njiɗɗon halkude ɓe. On wujjii sukaaɓe e juuɗe yumma'en, on njoɓtii sukaaɓe e nder galleeji, on njoɓtii ɓe e nder leydi ndii fof, ndeen on nfenanii ɓe, on mbaɗi ɓe ngooŋɗini taali Yesuwit en ko fayti e daartol maɓɓe goonga, e faandaare maɓɓe, e iwdi maɓɓe. Hay so tawii noon, on njaɓaani, sabu miin, Yesu'a HaMashiach, mi jokkondirta ɓe haa timma e daartol maɓɓe, e leñol maɓɓe, e ndonu maɓɓe goonga, mi waɗan fof ronooɓe kisal neɗɗo keso gooto, kanko woni miin, Almasiihu Yeesu.

E nder ñeeñal e halkere mawnde, on cuuɗiima karallaagal mon e nder ñeeñal, haa on nfenanii wonde yimɓe adanɓe ɓee ko majjuɓe, ɓe ngalaa karallaagal. Sabu on kollii ɓooyɓe ɓee sirluuji mon ɗi ngalaa nafoore, alaa kadi huunde hesere les naange. Ko ɗum waɗi on ncuuɗi goonga ko fayti e jiɓugol mon e tagooje, haa on ncuuɗi ɗum e juuɗe ɓeen ɓe njiɗɗon e fenaande mon mawnde. On ngartirii fijooji mon maagol mon kadi, no mbaɗirno-ɗon dingiral ngam on mbaawa feeñde hono no koñɗi nii. E nder fenaande mon nde njiɗɗon feeñde e koñɗi, on mbaɗan fof haawnii e maandeeji mon e kaawniiɗi mon fenaande, haa e ɓeen ɓe inɗe mum en mbinndaaka e Deftere Nguurndam gila e sosde winndere ndee. Kono miin, Yesu'a HaMashiach, mi feeñnan on haa timma, kala sirlu suuɗiiɗo feeñan.

Ndaaree! Mi nanngan gooŋɗinɓe am to asamaan, sabu miɗo ara ngam jommbaajo am, mi haɗataake. Kadi miin, Yesu'a HaMashiach, miɗo ara ngam ñaawoyde e haɓde e addude kiita mawɗo e dow leydi, on ndogataa.

Miin, Yesu'a HaMashiach, Joomiraawo Laamɓe e Laamɗo Laamɓe, mi nelii ɗee konnguɗi e juuɗe golloowo am, e juuɗe golloowo am konnguɗi am neliraa, fof timmintee.

2Gila e Yahweh, Alla Mawɗo Abada: Gimol Ɗiɗaɓol!

Oo, onon Seytaane'en, on njiɓinii tagooje am ngam rewrude e maandeeji mon e kaawniiɗi mon fenaande, tikre am ina wuli e dow mon. Yimɓe am serndete haa timma e geɗe mon ɗe aawɗon e nder alkama am e nder gese am łaska; Ɓiɗɗo am, Almasiihu Yeesu, mooɓan alkama am e nder suudu am, ko ɗum waɗi Joomiraawo ndema oo mooɓan, haɓɓa, wula geɗe mon. Kiita mawɗo ina woni e dow mon, on ndogataa e juuɗe am. Gila e fuɗɗoode, on njippiima, on cuuɗiima e nder ɓiɓɓe am, haa on mbonni cukalel am gila e fuɗɗoode e fenaandeeji mon e fenaandeeji mon.

On ngonaa allaaji, ɓeen rewnooɓe fenaande mon e miijooji mon keɓal ɓee kadi ngonaa allaaji. On mbaawaa tagde, sabu on ngalaa baawɗe, mi tagiraani on noon baawɗe sabu miɗo anndi on murtoto hade mon tagde; e nder nuunɗal am e yiɗde am mi tagirii on e nuunɗal, on timminoo e ñalawma mo tagir-ɗon. E nder mawnikinaare mon nde alaa nafoore ndee on njaari e mawnude mon, kono on ngalaa mawnude, mi feeñnan on haa timma e fenaande mon. Mi waɗan on yeeso Laamɓe, ɓe njiyan on, ɓe njiyan no on ngalaa baawɗe. Ɓiɗɗo am, Yesu'a HaMashiach, foolii on haa timma to Kalvaari, on ngalaa yaakaare.

On njaɓaama waɗde tan ko mi jaɓi, sabu on ko on gaño foolaaɗo. On wujjii deedal leydi e nder fenaande mon e fenaande mon to Aadama, jooni noon ɓiɗɗo am jibinaaɗo gooto oo, kanko woni Yesu'a HaMashiach, foolii on e nder yiɗde mum e tagooje am; leydi ndii ko makko, kanko woni Joomiraawo Laamɓe e Laamɗo Laamɓe, sabu mi bayyiniima ɗum. Sabu o soodi leydi ndii e neɗɗaagu e ƴiiƴam makko ɗam alaa bonannde, ɓeen cuɓiiɓe nguurndam kisintee. Golle mon fof ɗe ngalaa nafoore ndartintee, fof cuɓiiɓe nguurndam njaltan e fenaandeeji mon e fenaandeeji mon, njaha e nguurndam kesam, mi waɗan gooŋɗinɓe ɓee teddungal ɓurngal ngal ngonno-ɗon. Sabu on njaɓaani, oo laamɓe leydi ɓe ngalaa doole e miskineeɓe, haa e kala laamɗo e nder yuɓɓo mon jaɓaani e mon, oo mboɗeeri fenaande.

Jooni wakkati Kiita mon e halkere laamu mon timmunde arii, haa e laamu nguu mahiɗon e nder golle mon ngam etde suuɗde mi, kanko Yahweh, e tagooje am. On ndogataa. On naatataa kadi e asamaan, sabu on njaɓɓaama e leydi ngam on ngartataa kadi, nokku mon woodaani kadi e asamaan. On kaɓɓaama e leydi ndi njiɗno-ɗon, ndi wonaano ndi mon ngam heɓde. Kala golle mon ndartintee e nguurndam ɓeen ɓe njiɗno-ɗon halkude (e dow leydi ndii e hoore mum); ɓe nguuran haa abada e jam e kisal, ɓe keɓan weltaare timmunde, mi hoɗan e nder neɗɗaagu, mi wonan ɓe Alla maɓɓe.

Kono onon, oo mboɗeeri, on ngalaa baawɗe e yitere am, on mbaawaa waɗde hay huunde ngam haɗde mi. E nder balɗe jeegom gila e njaɓɓude neɗɗo, sabu ñalawma gooto ko duuɓi ujunnaaje e yitere am, on timminaani hay huunde so wonaa kiita mon, e ndonu mon, e halkere mon, e torra mon ngam mon e fof rewnooɓe. Sabu on kaɓɓete, on njaɓɓete e nder ndiyam mawɗam ngulɗam ɗam mbaɗ-mi ɗam ɓurɗam wulde, haa e yiite laaɓɗe bulo, on nulan haa abada, mi yoɓan on kala golle bonɗe ɗe mbaɗɗon, laabi keewɗi. On mbarii yimɓe heewɓe, on njoɓan kala ruuhu majjuɗo, on ndogataa haa abada. On nganndan bonanndeeji ɗi mbaɗɗon fof, on ndogataa e kiita, on torrentee haa abada ngam bonanndeeji ɗi mbaɗɗon. On nganndan no feewi bonanndeeji mon e bonanndeeji mon e nder torra mon, ɓeen ɓe njiɗɗon ɓee keɓan poolgu timmungu e dow mon. Kadi fof bonɓe ɓe njiɗno-ɗon waɗde bonannde, ɓe njaɓi naatde e murto mon, haa e ɓeen ɓe inɗe mum en mbinndaaka e Deftere Nguurndam, ɓe ndenndan e torra mon. On fof on njoɓan kala bonannde nde mbaɗɗon, sabu on mbaɗii bonannde e Yahweh e tagooje am, mi yoɓan. Sabu e nder nuunɗal am mi yoɓan bonanndeeji mon, e golle mon, e laabi mon bonɗi haa timma e nder ɓerɗe mon laabi keewɗi haa abada. Kadi miin, Yahweh, kanko woni Alla mawɗo abada, mi hunanii ɗee geɗe e innde am, sabu mi waawaa hunoraade goɗɗo ɓurɗo mawnude.

On ngollanan faandaare am, e nder yiɗde mon e halkere mon on ndoggan cuɓaaɓe am to am; sabu mi ardotoo ɓe e juuɗe Yesu'a HaMashiach e konu mum worɓe doole, sabu ɓe mooɓan baali am heddiiɓe ɓee. (Ndaaree! 144 000 neldaaɓe e Yesu'a HaMashiach mbaɗan golle doole yeeso bonannde mon mbonnde.) Kadi hay gooto e ɓeen ɓe mbaɗ-mi e nguurndam abada oo yaltataa e peeje mon, sabu ɓe mbaawaa yaltude e juuɗe am, hay gooto kadi waawaa yaltude e juuɗe Ɓiɗɗo am: Sabu mi rokki mo innde ɓurnde kala innde, o arata e baawɗe Yahweh e hoore mum, sabu mi renndinii jooɗorde am e makko. O firtan hoore mon haa timma. On njaɓaani, laamɗo tokooso, wakkati mon arii.

Miin, Yahweh, Alla mawɗo abada, mi nelii ɗee konnguɗi e juuɗe golloowo am, e juuɗe golloowo am konnguɗi am neliraa, ɗi ngarata. Sabu Yahweh, Alla mawɗo abada, bayyiniima noon, hay huunde waawaa feewnude bayyinaango am. Miin woni El Shaddai!

3Iwde e Yeshua HaMashiach: Gite Tataɓere

Ndaaree! Wakkatiiji senndindirgol ɗon ɓadoo timmude, yaltugol am ngol kadi heɓii jooni. Ɗum ɓadiima sanne haa e maleyka'en e mooliiɓe asama ɗon ndari ngam noddaango buutayru. Mi fontake junngooji am faade e aduna mo maayata duuɓi keewɗi jooni, woɓɓe keewɓe njaɓi nanondiral jam ngal Baaba am, Yahweh, neldi neɗɗaagu, hay miin, Almasiihu Iisaa, sabu ko miin woni jam mon. Kono woɓɓe ɓurɓe heewde njaɓaali sadaka am e yurmeende e moƴƴere Baaba am nde o loowi e dow leydi ndin.

Woɓɓe maantiniraaɓe e hisnaaɓe ɓe'e ko suɓaaɓe gila e fuɗɗoode aduna. Sabu Baaba am anndino on gila e neege haa e neege, on laatike hakkunde ɓernde makko, no min kadi mi laatiri hakkunde ɓernde makko, sabu miin, Iisaa Nasaranke oo, ko mi Ɓiɗɗo Yahweh bajjo. Kono e golle am timmuɗe ɗe mbaɗ-mi no mi woniri Paska timmuɗo, e ɗowtaare bajjo o, ɓiɓɓe Alla heewɓe, heewɓe, ndimaama kadi haa abada, hay ittaama e nder ɓiɓɓe Aadama, sabu ko miin woni Ɓii Aadama!

Zamanuuji yurmeende ɗi'i laatinoke siri suuɗiiɗo e nder ɓernde Yahweh mo o haalanaano hay gooto haa o feññinani mi, min kadi mi feññinanii nelaaɓe am laaɓɓe e annabaaɓe am, mi reeni Konngol am laaɓngol ngol ngam noppi e ɓerɗe suɓaaɓe e gooŋɗinɓe. Sabu onon, Jommbaajo am, on jeyaa ko e siri mawɗo Alla, nguurndam mon (ngam suuɗii e am) ina heɓii feññineede. Mi yoɓtan gooŋɗinɓe ɓe'e golle maɓɓe gooŋɗinɗe ɗe ɓe golli e am, Iisaa Almasiihu, e nder gomɗinal mawngal.

Ko noon mi hisniri jamaa mawɗo e ƴiiƴam am kiisneteeɗam, sabu ƴiiƴam am alaa e mum bakkaat, haa hannde mi alaa bakkaat, sabu mi waɗataa bakkaat abada. Woɓɓe wuurnooɓe nguurndam mum'en e gooŋɗinal e nder Almasiihu, ma ɓe keɓ njoɓdi mawndi, ma ɓe jalbu no koode e nder asama, teddungal mawngal ina jeyaa e maɓɓe, sabu ɓe teddiniraaki hoore maɓɓe no ko kamɓe ngoni mawɓe. Kono ɓe teddinii miin, Yeshua HaMashiach, mi hollan ɓe Baaba am tawa alaa e maɓɓe toɓɓere walla nyunye, comci maɓɓe maa laaɓ no mbuneeje nii, maa ɗi tuundo abada. Maɓe kala ɓe nguurda, ɓe mbaɗataa bakkaat kadi abada, woni e bonannde walla e mawnikinaare. Maɓe teddinire e am.

Sabu ko miin woni Gaynaako mawɗo, mi ardike gowal am faade e huɗo hecco haa wakkatiiji timmoode, ɗi njahdata e leydi ndin, sabu alaa kadi njaareendi heddiindi e nder werlaas o. Mi yoɓtan kala, gila e ɓurɗo famɗude haa e ɓurɗo mawnude, e laawol ngol mbaawaa faamde e oo wakkati; kono faamde nde'e ina woni e damal mon, sabu on naɓete asama ɓooyataa. Ko noon Jommbaajo am naɓirtee e nder teddungal, to mi ñaawan golle makko, mi hisna haa timma kala gorko, debbo, e cukalel. Mi yoɓtan ɓe no feewi hay e geɗe ɓurɗe famɗude ɗe ɓe mbaɗi ngam gollanaade mi. Sabu Alla wonaa tooñɗo, o yejjitataa golle mon e golle giɗli mon, ɗe kolluɗon Innde makko, nde njoginoɗon Laaɓɓe ɓe'e, on njokki e mum.

Hulee, Jommbaajo am, sabu Alla ñaawataa no neɗɗo ñaawirta nii, sabu neɗɗo ko tooñɗo, ko jiɗɗo yimɓe. Neɗɗo ina teddina ɓe o sikki ko kamɓe njogi nafoore, neɗɗo tagoowo oo ina heewi yeebde dewoowo e moƴƴere, o jogoo mawnikinaajo e timminɗo hoore mum. Alaa, wonaa e asamaan, wonaa e leydi, wonaa e laawol gootol, wonaa hay gooto!

Onon yaniɓe, hay onon laamɓe ndee aduna yaniinde, onon Seyɗaneeji, on fof on njoolat e yiite ngu nyifataa ngam konnaagu mon ngo alaa senuye e laabi mon ɗi ngoni bonɗi sanne ɗi ngoongaagu e mbonniaagu. No mbaɗirɗon gooto e ɓee ɓurɓe famɗude, banndiraaɓe am, ko miin mbaɗirɗon. Yoo, onon reenooɓe yaniɓe, hay onon malaa'ikaaɓe ɓe ngoongaaki ɓe acci darnde maɓɓe adannde, on ngalaa jam! On njettete e yiite ngu njaltataa haa abada, on njoolat haa laaɓa. Kala aawdi mon bonndi ndi aawɗon e feewnude keeri Yahwe e nder mbayliiguuji mon genetik hakkunde neɗɗo e malaa'ika, hakkunde neɗɗo e dabba, hakkunde neɗɗo e tagooje maayo, hakkunde dabbaaji kala sifaa, hay kala huunde bonnde nde mbaɗɗon, werletee e yiite wootere e mon, sabu on ngalaa jam, on ngalaa yaafuya!

On njuumnii Seyɗaneeji ɗi etinoo bonnude tagu Yahwe, haa yettii warngo am, miin Iisaa Almasiihu, nde mi rokki hoore am e juuɗe makko no Paska timmuɗo ngam aduna, haa noon mi siifii hisnugol neɗɗaagu e mi siifii battane mon e duumotooɗe e nder yiite mawka ka nyifataa! On njoɓat haa laaɓa kala tooñanngeeji mon e bonanndeeji mon, e kala golle bonɗe ɗe mbaɗɗon, waylitoyto e mon laabi teemedere, sabu kala gooto yoɓitirte ko fayi ar golle mum. E laabi mon bonɗi fof njoɓitirte e koye mon laabi keewɗi, sabu on cikkiino on mbaaway foolde mi, e mawnikinaare mon on cikkii haa jooni on mbaaway heɓde poolgu tawi on foolaama haa laaɓi.

Nanee konnguɗi am, yo mboddi ndiinoondi, sabu ñaawoore mon arii e mon, miɗo yi'a kulol mon, sabu on kuli ko arata ko ɓuri kala huunde, yo rawaandu ndi alaa ñiiƴe, ndi alaa nafoore! Njoɓdi ko miin jeyi, mi yoɓitoyto, e sahaaji mon njettii. Tikkere Alla e tikkere Jibraa'iilu ina ngondi e udditeede no mi udditirta siiltooji jeeɗiɗi, no malaa'ikaaɓe jeeɗiɗi njoñnirtee ngam fiyde buutule ñaawooje, e malaa'ikaaɓe jeeɗiɗi njoñnirtee jogiiɓe masiibooji jeeɗiɗi cakkitiiɗi, ɗi udditete, sabu e nder majji tikkere Alla heewi. Masiibooji keewɗi njettat on fof, no mi leptirta mi bonnirta laamu mon. Laamɗo mon mawɗo oo, mi foolat ɗum, e annabi mon fenaannde oo, mi foolat ɗum, ɓe njettete ɓe nguurti e weendu yiite njoolndu e sulufuur; toon ɓe ndeenata on njawtida e maɓɓe, toon on njoolat haa abada e yiite e sulufuur, e ñaawoore mon ina foti fuɗɗaade!

Bamdiiɗo am, woto kule! Konnguɗi am ñaawooje ngonaa ngam mon kono ko ngam weltinde on, sabu mi hisnii on e tikkere ndi foti udditeede ndee, mi suuɗat on e kisal e njoɓdi mawndi, e haa abada on ngonndat e am. Sabu mi suɓiima on, mi noddiima on, onon ko ngalu am ɓurngu teddude, onon ko ngalu Yahwe ɓurngu teddude; onon ko hay perel ngel njoɓdi mawndi, sabu mi rokki on fof ko njogii mi. Miɗo wasiyoo bamdiiɗo am yo ɗaɓɓu faamde e arde e am ngam heɓde ganndal, sabu mi jannginat on. Sabu yoga e mon njuumnii yoga e laabi ɗii annabaaɓe fenaande; tan ngartiree e tuubugol e Joomiraawo mon, mi jaɓɓotoo on.

Sabu ɓe njuurnii on e mbilewu Kerecee'en, on ngoonɗinii fenaande keewnde soodanaande on, hay wi'aande on ko goonga, kono ko fenaande bonnde. Ɗee ɓulli ɗi ngalaa ndiyam etinoo ittude on e am, ɗum waawaa waɗde, woto kule, sabu mi jaɓataa! Mi jokkat e rimɗinde on haa laaɓa, mi deƴƴataa! Mi haɓante on e laabi keewɗi ɗi on nganndaa. Sabu on fof on maantinanoo ko adii jibineede mon, sabu Baaba oo annduno on hay e nder reedu yumma mon, o deenuno kala gooto e mon e nder sahaaji ɗi mbaawaa faamde, on ngonnoo haa jooni e caka ɓernde makko. On maantinanoo e jibinannde, malaa'ikaaɓe am kadi ngenndi on e yamiroore am, ɓe ndeenii on, ɓe ndeenii on, sabu noon ɓe njoñnirtee. Ɗum haawnii on? On njananat ko Hebreeɓe mbi'ata? Kono hol e malaa'ikaaɓe o wi'i e kala sahaa, jooɗo e nyaamo am, haa mi waɗa añɓe maa tekke koyɗe maa? Wonaa ɓe fof ko ruuhuuji gollooji, njoñaaɗi ngam gollanaade ɓeen rontooɓe hisnugol?

Bamdiiɗo am, onon ko rontooɓe hisnugol e nder am, miin Iisaa Almasiihu. On noddaama e nder am; on ndeenama e nder am e juuɗe am; on ndokkaama fartaŋŋe nande waaju gooŋɗinal jowitiingu e am ngam mbaawa waɗde mi Joomiraawo mon, haa noon mi siiforii on ndeen ruuhu seniiɗo fodanaandi. Anndee wonde siilto ngo waawaa feƴƴeede, on ngondi e hisneede timmunde haa e duumotooɗe, sabu on nganndaa timmal golle tagooje ɗe poti-mi timminde e mon, bamdiiɗo am! Anndee wonde yaniɓe ɓee e aawdi maɓɓe ina njiɗi on, ɓe njiɗaa kala ko Yahwe yiɗi, mi yoɓitoyto on timmunde e koye maɓɓe, sabu ko miin woni barñooɗo hoore ndeen mboddi ndiinoondi, sabu ɗum anndinaama, e ko Yahwe anndini waawaa wayleede.

Alaa baawɗo senndude on e am; alaa bonannde baawnde feƴƴude ndeen siilto; onon ko miin jeyi, onon ko Baaba oo jeyi, sabu kala ko Baaba oo hokki mi, mi artiray Alla ngam en fof ngona e nder kala huunde! O hokkii mi kala huunde e asamaan e leydi, so wonaa e jooɗorde makko o ɓuri mi mawnude!

E miin, Almasiihu Iisaa, miɗo anndina on ɗee konnguɗi, ko miin, Iisaa Almasiihu, ñaawoowo añɓe mon, mi timminay ɗum haa e cakkitiiɗo. Bamdiiɗo am, yiɗde am e mon anndaaka nyifde, mi hadanii on nokku, sabu e galle Baaba am ina woodi cuuɗi keewɗi! Haa jooni on resete e am haa abada, haa noon on ngonndat e am, on faamoytoo timmal faandaare mon e nder am nde on tagaa ngam timminde!

Sabu e nder am, Yahwe hokkii on ko arata e faandaare, hay faandaare duuɓi ina foti udditeede e mon, sabu miɗo ara! Mi jaɓataa mi haɗee. Ndeenee peeñgol am. Ndeenee jalbugol arive am, sabu ina foti feeñde e leydi no mi feeñirta e duule e noddaango doole, e daande malaa'ika mawɗo, e buutu Alla, sabu miɗo wi'a on goonga wonde hay malaa'ikaaɓe ina ndeenoo ndeen buutu ndi foti fiyde, ɓe ngondi e heblude! Njeheenanee mi, ndeeƴee, njeeñee, ndeeƴee no yamiiraa; sabu ko ɓooyaani on njiyoyat mi e teddungal am ngal Baaba oo ɓornii mi, sabu noon mi ɓornirta on, sabu on nanndoyat e am, on njiyoyat mi no mi woniri.

Miin woni Jibraa'iilu Alla!

Miɗo ara ngam waɗde tikkere e laamu Seytaane fof, ɓe ndoggataa.

Miin woni Kisinoowo Aduna!

Miin woni Almasiihu Iisaa, njoɓdi ko miin jeyi, mi yoɓitoyto!

Miin woni barñooɗo hoore mboddi, sabu Alla Jom doole fof anndinii ɗum! Miɗo foti feeñde e duule ngam bamdiiɗo am, mo mi nanngoyta.

Miin woni garoowo e puccu daneewu.

Miin woni wi'eteeɗo Goongaajo e Goongaajo, e nder peewal mi ñaawoyat mi haɓa konu.

Miɗo ara!
