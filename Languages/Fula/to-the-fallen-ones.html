Seedeeji Ɗiɗo: Eklesiya
E nder Golle Yuhanna Wahyudanke
Yuni 06, 2024
Diɓɓi 1 e 2

Onon Tokaraaɓe: Jooni Ñaawoore Mon Arii!

Gila e Yeshua HaMashiach:

Onon noddaaɓe am, suɓaaɓe am, deeƴɓe am, nanee Konnguɗi Joomiraaɗo mon guurɗo, kisnoowo mon, hono Yeshua Almasiihu! Leƴƴi ɗi wuurdunooɗon ɗii e daartol ngol jannginooɓe mon Yesuit en janngini on ngol, ko ɗum woni caggal fenaande mawnde ndee, nde feeñnude mum ɓadake, nde yahrata kadi, ɗo tokaraaɓe ɓee peeñata hono malaa'ikaaɓe moƴƴuɓe, kono goonga mum ko malaa'ikaaɓe bonɓe rammuɓe. Ko wonnoo ko? Ko ɗum wonata: ko waɗanoo ko? Ko ɗum waɗetee: alaa ko hesɗi e les naange.

Gila e fuɗɗoode, Seyɗaani rokkii ganndal mum inniraangal ngal ɓurngal, haa arti noon e ganndal mum ko fayti e moƴƴere e bone. Ko o baawɗo fenaande e majjinde mawɗo, o jannginii neɗɗaagu geɗe keewɗe ɗe Yahwe anniyaaki, sabu ɗee ko laabi maayde. Tagu Yahwe nguu waɗaaka ngam huutoreede ngam heɓde baawɗe e nafooje keeriiɗe, walla ngam wonde laawol dogde e ñaawoore Alla, walla ngam sosde laabi e yuɓɓo mum keeriiɗo. Sabu Seyɗaani bonnii neɗɗaagu gila e fuɗɗoode, o bonnii kadi reenooɓe rammuɓe ɓe naati e murto makko ngoo.

Yo an, gaño mawɗo peewal fof, aan Seytaane: bonanndeeji maa gadani ɗii mbinndaama e juuɗe Anuhu binndoowo oo gila e oon sahaa, ɗi ngoni haa hannde, sabu bonanndeeji maa mbinndaama noon. Ko ɗum waɗi mi siftintoo, sabu a waɗii ɗee geɗe, a waɗii geɗe keewɗe goɗɗe: A majjinii ɓiɓɓe Alla fof, a jippinii ɓe e leydi, a majjinii ɓe rewrude e ɓiɓɓe rewɓe yimɓe. A naatnii e ɓiɓɓe Alla ceniiɓe ɓee wasiyaaji bonɗi, a majjinii ɓe haa ɓe tunwini ɓalli maɓɓe e ɓiɓɓe rewɓe yimɓe. A hollitii ɓiɓɓe yimɓe piiji maayde fof, a majjinii Hawa, a hollitii kaɓirɗe maayde ɓiɓɓe yimɓe: dariwol e wutte njamndi, e kaafa konu, e kaɓirɗe maayde fof wonande ɓiɓɓe yimɓe. Gila e juuɗe maa, ɗe njahii e hoɗɓe e leydi ndii gila ndeen ñalawma haa abada.

Onon Seytaane'en on jannginii ɓiɓɓe yimɓe ko kaɗɗi e ko weli, on jannginii ɓe gehe hikma maɓɓe fof. On jannginii neɗɗaagu windude e njaram e kaayitaaji, ɗum waɗi heewɓe mbaɗii hakke gila e fuɗɗoode haa hannde. Sabu yimɓe tagaaka ngam noon, ngam tabitinde goongɗingol mum en moƴƴol e mbinndi e njaram. Sabu yimɓe tagaama no malaa'ikaaɓe nii, ngam ɓe njokke laaɓde e peewde; maayde, hono ndee halkoore fof, waawaano nanngude ɓe, kono ko e ngal ganndal ɓe halkata, ko e ngal baawgal ɗum halkata yimɓe.

Onon Seytaane'en on kollitii ɓiɓɓe yimɓe piiji bonɗi fof ɗi ruuhuuji e jinneeji mbaɗata, e piiji mbusu e nder reedu, ngam waawa maayde, e piiji ruuhu, e ɗaɗɗude mboddi, e piiji baɗooji e nguleeki ñalawma, ɓiy mboddi ina wiyee Tabââ’ĕt. Sabu on naamndii hay Mika'ilu yo hollit on innde suuɗiinde ndee, ngam on mbaawa wolwude nde e nder hunayeere ndee, ngam ɓe njiɗi huccude e oon innde e hunayeere ɓe peeñnini kala ko suuɗinoo e ɓiɓɓe yimɓe.

Sabu on mbaɗii reenooɓe ɓee naata e murto mon nde njokku-ɗon bonnude neɗɗaagu. Ko ɗum waɗi bonanndeeji reenooɓe ɓee mbinndaama e juuɗe Anuhu binndoowo oo, mi siftintoo nii: Ɓe jannginii yimɓe waɗde kaafaaji, e laɓe, e dariiji, e wutteeji, ɓe anndini ɓe njamndiiji leydi ndii e karallaagal golle mum, e jawooji, e cuɗaari, e kuutoragol antimon, e cuɗagol yeeso, e kala sifaa kaaƴe tedduɗe, e kala mbaydiiji pentiir. Ɓe jannginii pirlitaaji, e taƴgol ɗaɗi, e firgol pirlitaaji, e ganndal koode, e koode, e ganndal duule, e maale leydi, e maale naange, e doggol lewru.

Ko ɗum waɗi, rewrude e bonanndeeji mon e fenaandeeji mon, on tunwini tagu Yahweh e laabi mon bonɗi e ngannuuji mon keefeeri. On majjinii neɗɗaagu, on mbaɗii hakke e Yahweh, on bonnii neɗɗaagu, hay e colli, e daabaaji, e kulle ladde, e liɗɗi, bonanndeeji mon ina ngadda ñaawoore mawnde nde njaltataa. On tunwini tagu Yahweh e karallaagal mon e jiɓgol mon, on mbaɗii neɗɗaagu heɓtinde pirlitaaji mon e fenaandeeji mon. Sabu Yahweh hollitii on tan ko gehe tagu mum ɗe ngalaa nafoore, o suuɗii gehe mum ɓurɗe teddude ɗee e nder ɓernde makko, o danndi ɗe e janfa mon.

Onon bonɓe, on jannginii laabi mon ɗi ngalaa nafoore ɗii neɗɗaagu nde ɓe keewi e dow leydi, on ngaddii musiiba mawɗo e halkere rewrude e peeje mon bonɗe e karallaagal mon. Sabu on ngaraani so wonaa ngam wujjude, warde, e halkude. On jannginii jiɓgol henndu, sabu on ngoni laamɗo baawɗo henndu, sabu on jiɓata aithēr. Sabu nde Kayinu waɗi hakke, o mahi wuro; on jannginii neɗɗaagu gehe mon ɗe ngalaa nafoore e jiɓgol mon. Ɗee gure kuutoraa ko ngam reende e jiɓgol e ngam addude halkere neɗɗaagu, no kuutorirnoo-ɗon ɗe ngam waɗde ngannuuji mon halkooje.

On mbaɗii leñol ummanoo leñol e nder hare e fitinaaji ɗi ngalaa keerol, on mbaɗii yimɓe am mbaɗa hakke, Yahweh saaktii Leƴƴi Isra'iila e nokkuuji nayi henndu. Kono noon, anniya mon bonɗo oo ɓeydii faandaare Yahweh, sabu e saaktude maɓɓe, mooɓtugol baali heddiiɓe ɓee timminetee. Mi nelan seedeeji am ɗiɗo ngam annabaade balɗe 1260 ɓoorniiɓe comci boɗeeji. E nder golle mon fof ngam halkude ɓiɓɓe Yahweh, on nattiima no feewi, on mbaawaano yiyde wonde golle mon ɓeydii faandaare Yahweh, sabu hay gañooji makko ina ɗoftoo daande makko.

Sabu on torrii, on torrii leƴƴi 12 ɗii e nder leydi ndii fof nde njiɗnoo-ɗon halkude ɓe. On wujjii ɓiɓɓe e juuɗe yumma mum en, on itti ɓiɓɓe e nder galleeji, on njuɓɓini ɓe e nder leydi ndii fof, ndeen noon on penanii ɓe, on mbaɗi ɓe ngoongɗina tinndi Yesuit en ko fayti e daartol maɓɓe goonga, e paandaale maɓɓe, e iwdi maɓɓe. Kono noon, on nattiima, sabu miin, Yeshua HaMashiach, mi jokkondirta ɓe haa laaɓa e daartol maɓɓe, e leñol maɓɓe, e ndonu maɓɓe goonga, mi waɗan fof ronooɓe kisal neɗɗo gooto keso, hono e am, Almasiihu Yeesu.

E pirlitaaji e halkere mawnde, on suuɗii no feewi karallaagal mon, on penii hay yimɓe zamanuuji ɓennuɗi ɓee ko majjuɓe, ɓe ngalaa karallaagal. Sabu on kollitii ɓooyɓe ɓee gehe mon ɗe ngalaa nafoore, alaa ko hesɗi e les naange. Ko ɗum waɗi on suuɗata goonga ko fayti e jiɓgol mon tagu, on suuɗata ɗum hay e ɓe njiɗnoo-ɗon ƴattaade e fenaande mon mawnde. On artirii pirlitaaji mon kadi, no njuɓɓinir-ɗon dingiral ngal ngam on mbaawa fenude hono malaa'ikaaɓe. Rewrude e fenugol mon e hare malaa'ikaaɓe, on mbaɗan fof heɓtinde maale mon fenaandeeje e kaawniiɗe mon, hay ɓe inɗe mum en mbinndaaka e Deftere Nguurndam gila e sosngo aduna. Kono miin, Yeshua HaMashiach, mi peeñnan on haa laaɓa, kala gehe suuɗiiɗe peeñan.

Ndaaree! Mi nanngan deeƴɓe am haa e asamaanuuji, sabu miɗo ara ngam Resndu am, mi dartinataake. Miin, Yeshua HaMashiach, miɗo ara ngam ñaaawde, haɓde, e addude ñaawoore mawnde e dow leydi, on ndaɗataa.

Miin, Yeshua HaMashiach, Joomiraaɗo Joomiraaɓe, Laamɗo Laamɓe, mi nelirii ɗee konnguɗi rewrude e golloowo am, rewrude e golloowo am konnguɗi am neliraama, fof timminetee.

Gila e Yahweh, Alla Mawɗo Abada:

Yo onon Seytaane'en, on njiɓii tagu am ngam rewrude e maale mon fenaandeeje e kaawniiɗe mon, tikare am ina wuli e mon. Yimɓe am njoortiree no feewi e ƴuccuuji mon ɗi aaw-ɗon e nder alkama am e nder gese am barke; Ɓiɗɗo am, Almasiihu Yeesu, maa mooɓtu alkama am e nder suudu am, ko ɗum waɗi Joomiraaɗo ndema maa mooɓtu, haɓɓa, wula ƴuccuuji mon. Ñaawoore mawnde ina woni e mon, on ndaɗataa e juuɗe am. Gila puɗɗam, on njippii, on suuɗii e nder ɓiɓɓe am, on bonnii hay sukaaɓe am tokosɓe gila e puɗɗam e fenaandeeji mon e njiɓudi mon.

On ngonaa allaaji, ɓe ndewi e njiɓudi mon e miijooji mon ɗi keɓal ɗii kadi ngonaa allaaji. On mbaawaa tagde, sabu on ngalaa baawɗe, mi tagiraani on e oon mbaawka sabu miɗo anndi on murtatno hade mon tagde; e nder nuunɗal am e yiɗde am, mi tagirii on e nuunɗal, on laaɓi ñalawma tagiraa-ɗon. E nder mawnikinaare mon nde ngalaa nafoore, on mawninta hoore mon, kono on ngalaa mawnude, mi peeñnan on haa laaɓa wonde ko on fenooɓe. Mi lelnan on yeeso Laamɓe, ɓe njiyan on, ɓe njiya no on ngaldiri baawɗe. Ɓiɗɗo am, Yeshua HaMashiach, foolii on no feewi to Kalvaari, on ngalaa yaakaare.

On mbaawaa waɗde so wonaa ko mi yamiri, sabu on ngoni gaño foolaaɗo. On wujjii tiitoonde leydi ndii e pirlitaaji mon e fenaandeeji mon to Aadama, jooni noon ɓiɗɗo am gooto, hono Yeshua HaMashiach, foolii on e yiɗde mum e tagu am; leydi ndii ko makko, hono Joomiraaɗo Joomiraaɓe, Laamɗo Laamɓe, sabu mi hollitii ɗum. Sabu o soodi leydi ndii e neɗɗaagu e ƴiiƴam makko laaɓɗam, ɓe suɓii nguurndam kisnetee. Golle mon fof ɗe ngalaa nafoore majjat, fof ɓe suɓii nguurndam njaltata e fenaandeeji mon e njiɓudi mon ngam naatde e nguurndam kesam, mi waɗan deeƴɓe ɓee ɓura teddude e mon no ngonnoo-ɗon. Sabu on nattiima, yo laamɓe leydi ɓaleeɓe, hay laamɗo kala e nder laamu mon nattiima e mon, yo mboddi fenaande.

Jooni wakkati Ñaawoore mon e halkere laamu mon timmunde arii, hay laamu nguu mahi-ɗon ngam etaade suuɗde mi, hono Yahweh, e tagu am. On ndaɗataa. On naatataa kadi e asamaanuuji, sabu on njaltinaama e leydi haa abada, nokku mon woodaani kadi e asamaanuuji. On kaɓɓaama e leydi ndi njiɗnoo-ɗon, ndi wonaano mon ngam nanngude. Kala golle mon majjat e nguurndam ɓe etinoo-ɗon halkude (e dow leydi ndii kadi); ɓe njoɗɗo haa abada e jam e kisal, welo-welo timmungo ɓe keɓat, mi hoɗdan e neɗɗaagu, mi wontan Alla maɓɓe.

Kono aan, yo mboddi, a alaa baawɗe e yeeso am, a waawaa darnde mi. E nder balɗe jeegom gila e njippudi neɗɗo, sabu ñalawma gooto ko duuɓi ujunere e yitere am, a waɗaani hay huunde so wonaa ñaawoore maa, torra maa, halkere maa, e torra maa e fof rewɓe e maa. Sabu a haɓɓetee, a werlee e nder maayo wulngo ngoo ɗo mi waɗiri yiite ɓurnge wulde ngam maa, hay yiite laaɓngo bulo, a wulan haa abada, mi yoɓan ma kala golle bonɗe ɗe mbaɗnoo-ɗaa, laabi keewɗi. A wardii ujunnaaje ujunnaaje, a yoɓan kala ruuhu majjuɗo, a daɗataa abada. A anndan bonanndeeji ɗi mbaɗnoo-ɗaa fof, a daɗataa e ñaawoore, a torrete haa abada ngam bonanndeeji ɗi mbaɗnoo-ɗaa. A anndan no feewi bonanndeeji maa e bonanndeeji maa e nder torra maa, ɓe njiɗaa ɓee keɓan poolgu timmungu e dow maa. Fof bonɓe ɓe njiɗnoo-ɗaa mbaɗa hakke, ɓe naati e murto maa, hay ɓe inɗe mum en mbinndaaka e Deftere Nguurndam, ina keɓa e torra maa. On fof on yoɓan kala hakke gooto mo mbaɗnoo-ɗon, sabu on mbaɗii hakke e Yahweh e tagu am, mi yoɓan. Sabu e nuunɗal am mi yoɓan bonanndeeji mon, golle mon, e laabi mon bonɗi haa timma e nder ɓerɗe mon laabi keewɗi haa abada. Miin, Yahweh, Alla mawɗo abada, miɗo hunorii ɗee geɗe e innde am, sabu mi waawaa hunoraade ko ɓuri mawnude.

A gollanto paandaale am, e nder njiɗdi maa e halkere maa, a doginan suɓaaɓe am haa e am; sabu mi ardan ɓe e juuɗe Yeshua HaMashiach e konu mum worɓe doole, sabu ɓe mooɓtan baali am heddiiɓe ɓee. (Ndaaree! 144,000 neldaaɓe e Yeshua HaMashiach maa mbaɗ golle doole yeeso bonanndeeji mon ngannuuji.) Hay gooto e ɓe mi yamiri ngam nguurndam abada, fotaani e peeje mon, sabu ɓe mbaawaa itteede e juuɗe am, hay gooto waawaa itteede e juuɗe Ɓiɗɗo am: Sabu mi rokki mo innde dow kala innde, o arata e baawɗe Yahweh e hoore mum, sabu mi renndini jappeere laamu am e makko. O mettinan hoore maa haa laaɓa. A nattiima, laamɗo tokoso, wakkati maa arii.

Miin, Yahweh, Alla mawɗo abada, mi nelirii ɗee konnguɗi rewrude e golloowo am, rewrude e golloowo am konnguɗi am neliraama, ɗi ngardat. Sabu Yahweh, Alla mawɗo abada, hollitii noon, hay huunde waawaa feccude bayyinaango am. Miin woni El Shaddai!
