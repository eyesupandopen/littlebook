Seedeeɓe ɗiɗo: Israa'iila
Ko weli e ko haaɗi
20 Noowammbar, 2024

Njipaari e Muusu Kesorii Laatii Ngootiri!

Ngaree e Yesuwa HaMasiyaak, onon leñol am, paamon golle daɗndoowo mon, sabu ko miin woni daɗndoowo on leñol. Sabu ko miin hisnata heddiiɓe e Yaakuuba ɓe nguurata yeeso mawɗo KAN MIIN, kanko YHVH, Alla duumotooɗo, mo alaa fuɗɗoode, mo alaa sakkitoode. Laamu am e laamuuli am alaa gasirde, ko ɗum waɗi, kettoree Konnguɗi Joomi mon duumotooɗo ahadi, kanko Yesuwa HaMasiyaak, mo nganndiraa kadi Iisaa Almasiihu, mo nganndiraa kadi Almasiihu Iisaa, sabu ko noon mawɗo KAN MIIN waɗiri mi haa pooma.

Gooto, Yaahwe hokkii mi innde ɓurnde inɗe fof mawnude, fii so innde Iisaa innamaa, kala koppi fof ŋonndoo, tawa ko e dow kammu e dow leydi, hay e ley leydi. Sabu ko miin Yaahwe halfini geɗe fof to kammu e to leydi e juuɗe am baawɗe fii timmingol geɗe fof. Ko ɗum waɗi, onon Israa'iila, kettoree daande am e ɗooftaare giɗliinde, ngam jaabaade giɗliinde nde kollu-mi on nde maay-mi e nokku mon e torraaji ɓuuɓɗi ɗi potaano mi muñde. Kono e giɗliinde am e mon, mi lomtii on fii daɗndude on e njoɓdi Muusaa, njoɓdi nde mbaawaa onon e ko'e mon yoɓde sabu bakkaat gonɗo e ɓalli mon. Gooto, ko fii daɗndude Aadama nelanoo-mi e aduna.

Sabu Muusaa ko goongaajo, laaɓɗo, pooccanooɗo, tee Muusaa ina wuuri tigi e nder Masiyaajo mon goongaajo, Joomi mon duumotooɗo peewal, sabu ko noon ngon-mi. Tee ko miin rewni sariya Muusaa haa laaɓi e giɗliinde am e ɗooftaare am e Yaahwe, gardinooɗo mi e giɗliinde mum ndu maayataa e leñol Aadama ngo tooñaa, ngo şeytaaneeɓe fooli, ko ɗum waɗi yurmeende e moƴƴere ndokkiraama no heewi fii dartinde ñaawoore e tikkere timmunde haa seernitaare timmunde waɗa hakkunde gawri Yaahwe e zawaaniiji şeytaaneeɓe, ko noon kadi ronooɓe nguurndam duumotooɗam e nder Almasiihu Iisaa fof ndaɗndirtee suɓaade jebbilaade Yesuwa HaMasiyaak, kanko Almasiihu Iisaa, kanko kadi Iisaa Annasaarankeejo.

Ko e leƴƴi ɗin Yaahwe neldi mi, tee ko e golle leƴƴi Israa'iila majjuɗi mi yahi, sabu mi salanooma e leñol am e nder añde diine, hay e juuɗe mawɓe almuɓɓe e Sanhedrin, wonɓe e gollande Wummbalde e gunndoo, sabu iwoɓe ko e Isawu, mo hawri e añde miñi mum Yaakuuba e Askenaaz mo Yaafet, fii no ɓe mooɓtira geɗe am, ɓe ndokka Yaakuuba mbuutu hoore sakkitiingu ngu Yaahwe jaɓataa, hay golle ɗe o haɗata e juuɗe am baawɗe, ɗooftooje duu.

Ko ɗum waɗi, onon leñol am, njebbilee ɗooftaare daande am fii no nguuroron yeeso am, e no nguurodon yeeso Yaahwe mo hay gooto mbaawaa ɓadtaade tawa Iisaa Almasiihu alaa, sabu ko noon Yaahwe suɓii e peeje mum duumotooɗe daɗndugol e hakkilantaagal. Ndeke paamon tawa Masiyaajo mon goongaajo, kanko Yesuwa HaMasiyaak, alaa, on njokkii e maayde e nder tooñanngeeji e bakkaatuuji ɗi pool-mi nde yoɓ-mi njoɓdi Muusaa timmundi haa abada e fii kala suɓiiɓe jaɓde sadak am.

Ko noon waɗi Muusaa wuuri e Yesuwa HaMasiyaak, tee ko miin Muusaa yamiri ɗooftaare mon timmunde, ko ɗum waɗi kettoree konnguɗi am, taaɗnon ɓerɗe mon e noppi mon fii nanngol haala am. Sabu ɓerɗe mon njippiima e nder añde ɗooftaare, on kebbinii noppi mon e ganndal Isawu, on tunwinii ɓerɗe mon e fenaaji mum en ɓuuɓɗi, Talmud. Paamon Muusaa ko timmuɗo, goongaajo, laaɓɗo, tee so neɗɗo huɗii sariya Muusaa, ndeen o huɗee sabu kuugal makko, hay e sariya Muusaa mo o huɗi oo. Ko ɗum waɗi, miɗo anndina on sariya oo rokkiraama fii hollude bakkaat e nder ɓalndu, sabu ɗo sariya alaa, bakkaat limetaake e fawaade e sariya, hay so tawii bakkaat ina woodi e nder ɓalndu noon e noon. Ko ɗum waɗi, e golle sariya Muusaa hay ɓalndu suutintaake yeeso Yaahwe, sabu ko e sariya Muusaa ganndal bakkaat iwata.

Kono, no nduin-mi Pool winndani Eklesiya am e yonta am moƴƴere, jooni peewal Alla feeñii tawa sariya Muusaa alaa, seedtinaama e sariya e Annabaaɓe. Onon haalooɓe am adinooɓe, wonɓe e yande e darnde mon adannde nde Yaahwe rokki leñol mon mawngol ngol, sabu oɗon potnoo wonde leñol keeriiŋol, ceertuŋol e leƴƴi goɗɗi ɗin! Kono on caliima mi no baabiraaɓe mon nii e nder murtital mon tiiɗngal ɓerɗe e peeje e anniyaaji Alla Baawɗo Fof, kanko El Sadday. Ko noon waɗi, e salagol mon Konnguɗi am dokkaaɗi e linjiila kisal nde peeñin-mi kuddi mawndi Alla ndin e nelaaɓe am laaɓɓe e annabaaɓe, on laatiima no jiɗɓe kosam nii, on mbaawaa ñaamde teewu tiiɗngu sabu on njokkii e wonde cukaloyeeɓe ɓe paamgol timmungol alaa, hay so tawii oɗon njiyra koye mon no annduɓe hakkilanteeɓe e lampaaji annoora, kono ko şeytaaneeɓe anndini on e fenaande fii majjinde yimɓe e laabi mon ɗi nganndaa ɗin.

Ko ɗum waɗi, paamon, onon leñol am, pooccanooɗo o wuuran e diine, tee e golle sariya hay ɓalndu suutintaake! Ina winndaa e binndi laaɓɗi dokkaaɗi e juuɗe Pool, mo tigi-rigi wonnoo Benyaminjo ɗooftotooɗo, mo adinoo ɗooftaaki, tiiɗnooɗo hoore e salagol Iisaa Annasaarankeejo, no onon nii: Ndeke ɓeen wonɓe e diine, mbarkaama e Ibraahiima diinɗo. Sabu yoga e wonɓe e golle sariya ɓee, ina ngoni e les kuugal sariya: sabu ina winndaa, huɗaaɗo ko kala neɗɗo jokkataa e geɗe fof, binndaaɗe e deftere Sariya, fii waɗde ɗum'en.

Kono, sabu bonannde, hiila, e pewje mboddi ñalnde Aadama yani gila toowɗo; bakkaat laamiima e nder ɓalndu gila ndeen, tee mbaydi Aadama mbonndi ndii wonaa galle mon, tee on mbaɗaaka fii waɗde duuɓi duumotooɗi e nder cuuɗi loope ɗi ngartata e mbulmbuldi leydi ɗo iwdi mon ittaa. Ko ɗum waɗi, Yaahwe waɗii mi ruuhu dokkoowo nguurndam, tee ko miin woni Aadama sakkitiiɗo. Tee ko miin waɗiraa no yeru nguurndam duumotooɗam nii, fii no on fof mbaɗiree e timmal e ngootaagu, hay no neɗɗo gooto keso nii. Sabu formeeji ɗiɗi neɗɗo ngoodataa haa abada, kono gooto, sabu mbaydi Aadama ndin halkete haa pooma wondude e bonannde wonnde e nder ɓalndu mbonndu.

Ko ɗum waɗi, paamon şeytaaneeɓe ɓee ina njiɗi suuɗde on goonga e nder wumre mon jooni e mawninkinaare mon, hay goonga ka rimɗinta on haa laaɓa e rimɗineende nde njiɗ-mi rimɗinde on ndee. Miɗo anndina on, onon Israa'iila, (ɓe njiɗ-mi) hay neɗɗo suutintaake e golle sariya Muusaa yeeso Yaahwe, ina laaɓi: sabu pooccanooɗo o wuuran e diine. Tee sariya oo wonaa diine: kono neɗɗo baɗɗo ɗeen geɗe, wuuran e majje. Tee miin, Yesuwa HaMasiyaak, mi daɗndii on e kuugal sariya, mi laatii kuɗaaɗo e mon, sabu ina winndaa, kuɗaaɗo ko kala baagiiɗo e lekki. Ko ɗum waɗi, onon Israa'iila, njaɓee sadak am fii no onon e iwdi mon nguuroton, no keɓiron naatde e laamu am duuɓi ujunere ngo rokkaa ngam fooftere ñalawma aset e nder peeje Yaahwe duumotooɗe daɗndugol neɗɗaagu, sabu duuɓi ujunere ko no ñalawma gooto nii, tee ñalawma gooto ko no duuɓi ujunere nii yeeso Yaahwe, mo nganndu-ɗon, onon Israa'iila.

Ko ɗum waɗi, ceertee e fenooɓe on ɓee, ceertee e ndeen deftere fenaande ɓuuɓnde, neldaande e şeytaaneeɓe fii laɓde on e haɓɓude on e majjere fii halkaare mon timmunde. Ceertee e fenaaji mboddi ndin, njaltuɗon e maɓɓe, ceertuɗon, onon Israa'iila, mi jaɓɓoroo on koye am, mi hisna on e halkaare yontere capanɗe jeeɗiɗi Daniyel, hay torra Yaakuuba ngarooja fii laɓɓinde leydi e juuɗe bonɓe e ñaawoore mawnde, peewnde.

Sabu sahaaji seernitaare ina ngara timma e dow leydi ɗo Yaakuuba, mo ndonu mum woni ahadi fii ɓiɓɓe ahadi wuurooɓe e diine, seertintinirtee haa abada e miñi mum ɗooftaaki, kanko Isawu, njiɗɗo wujjude ko o yeeyi e nder añde diine sabu o hormaani, o diinaani e ahadiiji diinɗi ɗi Yaahwe waɗani Ibraahiima diinɗo, kono o teskiima geɗe leydi ɓurɗe ɗum, o woni jeenotooɗo, neɗɗo meereejo yeeyɗo ndonu mum duumotooŋgu e jibinannde mum fii mburu gooto.

Miɗo anndina on ko arata ko noddumi on fii tawtoreede, mi hisna on e kisal semmbe am so on njebbiliima laamu am e baawɗe am, sabu so on mbaɗii noon, mi artirta on haa laaɓa e Yaahwe, kanko Baaba Annooraaji, mo waylotaako, mo alaa ɗowdi waylo. Ko noon winndiraa e ahadiiji ɓooyɗi, ko noon kadi wonirta, onon leñol am ɓe ngon-mi daɗndoowo leñol!

Ndeke so torra Yaakuuba gasii, mi tabintina laamu am e peewal e dow leydi, tee hay huunde bonnde naatataa heen. Paamon mi timminan laamu Seytaane haa abada, tee wummbalde ndee ummataako fii laamaade kadi, tee kullel ngel gila e maayo e kullel ngel gila e leydi, njanngete ina nguuri e nder weendu yiite mawndu ndu sulfura mboɗeerta ka ɓulata tumanɗe ɓurɗe wulde bulo, tee e nder ndeen weendu tordinooru ɓe njanngete haa pooma, ɗo liɗɗi maɓɓe maayataa, sabu ko ɗum poti.

Seytaane e hoore mum nanngete, haɓɓee e callalal torra e tordino mawngal, ɗo o heddotoo e kulol e mette halkaare makko garoore, ndonu ngu o renndata e kala rewɓe mo. Gooto, gooto, e nder luggeendam o heddotoo uddaaɗo, haɓɓaaɗo duuɓi ujunere, so ɗum gasii, o woppite fii mooɓtude ɓeen murtuɓe, naatɓe e laabi makko, hay murtital makko sakkitiingal fii no bone halkiree haa abada e timmal ñaawoore. Tee so sahaa makko seeɗa oo gasii, nde o mooɓti ɓe no njaareendi maayo nii e dow nokkuure laaɓɓe dow leydi, yiite yanan gila to Yaahwe toowɗo, ngo halka ɓe fof haa laaɓa, tee Seytaane haɓɓee haa abada, njanngee e ndonu mum duumotooŋgu e nder ɗeen tumanɗe duumotooɗe tordino, tee ndeen ñaawoore jooɗorde laamorde daneeje mawnde fuɗɗotoo.

Tee e nder ndeen weendu tordino duumotoondu, ɓulndu tumanɗe ɓurɗe wulde, hay e nder jaɓɓungal mayru duumotoongal, Seytaane yoɓan kala bakkaat mo o waɗi laabi keewɗi, tee kala golle bonɗe mbinndaama timmuɗe to kammu, ndokketee e tumanɗe tordino ngam wonde ndonu makko duumotooŋgu. Gooto, kala tagoodi rewnoodi, halfiniindi mo, dañan heen tordino ngootu, tee ɓe njuurmetaake, sabu ɓe njuurmaaki, ko ɗum waɗi yurmeende alaa haa pooma. Tee no ɓe mbaɗiri woɗɓe, no ɓe bonniri tagu Yaahwe, ko noon yoɓitortee e koye maɓɓe, no ahdiraa gila ɓooyii, sabu hay ahadi Yaahwe wootiri yanataa e leydi tawa timmaani. Gooto, gooto, kala saliiɓe noddaango am kisal, njanngetee e yiite, tee alaa teskagol yimɓe.

Kono, battane şeytaaneeɓe ɓe cuɓii, ɓe mbaɗani koye maɓɓe e murtital maɓɓe bonngal e Yaahwe e tagu mum, wonaa fii mon, onon leñol am, so on njaɓii mi no Masiyaajo mon nii. Alaa, ko luutndii ɗum, ko njeenaaje e barkeeji keewɗi ndesanaa on, ɗi nganndon timma e laamu am ngo laamoto-mi e peewal duumotoongal. Laamɗo laamɓe gooto e Joomi joomiraaɓe gooto laamotoo leydi e peewal duumotoongal, hay gila Yerusalaam, hay gila e jooɗorde laamorde Daawuuda nde ngon-mi ronooɗo duumotooɗo! Tee e resndaaɗo am diinɗo mo teeyoto-mi e ahadi duumotoondi, mi laamɗinan, mi laɓɓina dental mawngal Laamɓe e Almuɓɓe no diine maɓɓe woniri, tee ɓe laamotoo e peewal e nanondiral timmungal e Laamɗo maɓɓe Laamɓe e Joomi maɓɓe Joomiraaɓe, tee en laamotoo no gooto nii.

Tee yimɓe leydi ndii e yimɓe leƴƴi ɗin mbaɗtitan kaafaaje maɓɓe kaɓirɗe ndema, tee leƴƴi kaɓataa hakkunde majji kadi, tee yimɓe leydi ndii njanngataa haɓde e laamu am laaɓngu. Hay no winndiraa e annabaaɓe ɓooyɓe: Pooli kadi hoɗdoytoo e njipaari, tee gawjal fukkodoo e mbeewa, tee ñalel, e muusu kesorii, e ñalel newiiɗo fof, njirlodoo, tee cukalel tokosel ardoto ɗum'en. Tee nagge e daɓa fof ñaamdondirta: ɓiɓɓe majje pukkodoo: tee muusu kesorii ñaaman huɗo no ngaari nii. Tee cukalel muynowel fijirta dow wuddere mboddi, tee cukalel anngaaɗo waɗan juuɗe mum dow wuddere mboddi bonndi. Ndeen hay gooto bonnataa, halkataa e dow fello am laaɓngo fof: sabu leydi ndii heewan ganndal Joomiraawo, no ndiyam heewiri maayo.

Tee e ndeen ñalawma ndiyam nguurndam yaltan gila Yerusalaam, feccere majjam feewde maayo fuɗnaange, feccere majjam feewde maayo sakkitiiŋgo, tee wonan, e ndunngu e ndungu fof. Tee Joomiraawo laamotoo leydi ndii fof: e ndeen ñalawma, Joomiraawo gooto wonan, tee Innde makko wootere wonan. Leydi ndii fof waylotoo no fello gila Geba haa Rimmon, feewde worgo Yerusalaam, tee toownetee, hoɗee e nokku mum: gila e damal Benyamin haa nokku damal gadanal, haa damal koñngal, e gila e suudu toowndu Hananiel, haa nokkuuji ɓirduɗi inaboje Laamɗo. Tee yimɓe koɗan heen, tee halkaare alaa kadi, kono Yerusalaam hoɗee e kisal.

Ndeke paamon, onon Israa'iila, ko mbaɗan-mi kala yiɗɓe mi e rewrude e yamirooje am duumotooɗe nguurndam e jam ɗe njamir-mi on. Paamon yiɗde Yaahwe ko reende yamirooje mum, tee yamirooje Yaahwe ina njamira ɗooftaare Ɓiɗɗo Yaahwe, mo ngon-mi: ko ɗum waɗi, ñunnee Ɓiɗɗo o so wonaa mi tikka, on halka e laawol.

Sabu laamu am ko laamu ngu anniyaaji am duumotooɗi kawray haa timma, haa timmitinaa haa laaɓa, tuma nde Njamnaari ndii wonta gooto e Mbooju nduu.

Sabu miin woni Njamnaari Allah, ndi rokkiraa sabu hakkeeji aadee, ndi ƴiiƴam mum woni sadaka cokkitorɗo duumotooɗo, mo bonɓe laɓɓinee haa mbula no mbuloori e nder laaɓal ceniiŋgal.

Sabu miin woni Mbooju Yahuuda, ka haɓanantoo ma, aan Isiraayiila, ka bonnoya aybeeji maa fof no windiraa e am nii; yantagol ko kaŋge jogii, mi yantoyto, wiyi Joomiraawo Joomiraaɓe e Laamɗo Laamɓe!

Ko ɗum waɗi, paamee anniyaaji am e on mo Yahweh neldi ngam hisnude on, e won geɗe keewɗe e nder anniyaaji am hisnugol. Ko miin woni ardiiɗo oo, oo keɓɗo hisnude on haa abada so on cuɓiima jaɓde junngo am giɗliingo, on njaɓi alkawal am kesal ngal.

Sabu ko e alkawal am kesal ngal mi tabintiniri ƴiiƴam am, ngal wonti Alkawal am Kesal, ngal leydi ndii laamorte e peewal! Anndal Yahweh heɓɓoyay leydi ndii, teddungal am jalboyay gila Yerusalaam haa leƴƴi ɗii mooɓtoo, naata e damal ceniiŋgal wuro ngoo ceniiŋngo ngam ɓe anndina Yahweh, ɓe nguura e laabi makko e leydi ndii.

E nder laamu am, alaa heen cukalel balɗe seeɗa, alaa kadi mawɗo mo timminaani balɗe mum: sabu cukalel maayay ina yahra e duuɓi teemedere, kono hakke o yahratnooɗo e duuɓi teemedere, o huɗaama. Maa won ko mo wuurii haa yettii duuɓi teemedere, o waya no cukalel nii.

Hono non, leydi ndii artirtoyee e teddungal mum adanngal hade laɓɓinaaɓe ɓee bonnude leydi ndii e koɗɗe mum en nde ɓe njipii e dow Hoore Hermon ngam hunanaade hunannde maɓɓe huɗaande ndee, mbele ɓe nguura e nebbamji maɓɓe e nder hakke mawɗo e murto e yamirooje nguurndam ɗe ɓe tagiraa e timmal. Ɓe tawaaka e laamu am; leydi ndii kadi laamortaako e fitinaaji ɗi ɓurɗo semmbe wuurata, sabu hono ɗeen geɗe ko waylude sariyaaji Yahweh, ɗi Seytaane yiɗi waylude laabi Yahweh ngam mbonnaari laamoo.

Ko miin, Yesuwa HaMasiyaak, laamotooɗo leƴƴi ɗii e sawru njamndi e juuɗe Laamɓe e Almaamɓe heewɓe ɓe mi laɓɓintoo, mi toɗɗoo e golle ɓurɗe toowde, ɓe ngoni ɓiɓɓe adanɓe ummitaade e maayɓe, ɓe ngoni maayataaɓe haa abada, njoɓdi maɓɓe alaa keerol, sabu ɓe ngari e am e yiɗde maɓɓe e balɗe moƴƴere, mi mooɓtii ɓe e am e nder terɗe am ɗe tagiraa kadi no neɗɗo gooto keso nii, tawa ko ɓe Yahuudiyankooɓe walla Jentilnaaɓe, tawa ko ɓe jiyaaɓe walla ko ɓe rimɓe. Ɓe keɓoyay baawɗe e mbaawkaaji keewɗi rokkiraaɗi ɓe sabu goongɗingol maɓɓe, sabu ɓe ngoni foolɓe am ɓe fooliri kala huunde e ƴiiƴam Njamnaari ndii.

Sabu ko Njamnaari Allah fooli laɓɓinaaɓe ɓee to Kalvaari haa abada, ko Mbooju Yahuuda foolata tagoore ndee fof haa Yahweh, haa Yahweh wona fof e fof tuma nde mi hollirta mo tagoore foolaande ndee, sabu mi foti laamaade haa aybeeji am fof mbaɗee e les koyɗe am, ko Mbooju Yahuuda yiiɓata kala geɗel e laamu Seytaane e les koyɗe am baawɗe, duumotooɗe.

Ko ɗum waɗi, ko miin woni mo njamnaari ndii hawtidata e deeƴre timmunde e ngootaagu e mbooju nduu, sabu anniyaaji am duumotooɗi mbaɗoytee haa abada, alaa ko woni e alaa ko woni baawɗo daraade e Yesuwa HaMasiyaak, waktuuji am njettiima ɗo mi feeñtinta hoore am e hoɗɓe e leydi ndii. Ndaaree, mi aray e duule, kala yiitere yi’ay am: Ee, hay ɓe tuppi am ɓee: e leƴƴi leydi ndii fof mboyay e yeeso am, hono noon, Amiin.

Miin woni Yesuwa HaMasiyaak, mo fotnoo feeñtinde hoore mum e leydi ndii, aduna oo fof annday wonde Alla ina woodi e dow kammu, laabi Yahweh keɓɓoyay leydi ndii e peewal ceniiŋgal.

Miin woni Almasiihu Iisaa, Joomiraawo mon, Laamɗo mon, Almaami mawɗo mon, e Masiya mon mo umminaa, mo ummitinaa!

Ko miin neldi Konnguɗi am ceniiɗi rewrude e nelaaɗo am e maccuɗo am, e rewrude e nelaaɗo am e maccuɗo am, Konnguɗi am ceniiɗi nelaa! Sabu nelaaɗo am baawɗo oo artii e noddaango mum duumotoongo e muñal caggal njurum keewngal, tuubu, e laɓɓingol e nder ƴiiƴam am cokkitorɗam; mi jaɓɓii mo e am e nder giɗli am e tuubooɓe kala ɓe ngarata e am e ɓernde yankinaande. E nelaaɗo am oo mi feeñnani Deftere Pamare ndee nde Yuhanna Feeñnitiiɗo oo yamiraa suuɗde haa nde feeñtina e waktu mum, nde deftere ndee ina jogii daande diwli jeeɗiɗi ɗii fof, ɗi ngoni Linjiila Duumotooɗo oo, o anndini Konnguɗi am e goonga no o yamiraa nii, seede mum Yuhanna Feeñnitiiɗo oo winndi, o seedii.

Miin woni mo suɓaaki yimɓe, hay Yesuwa HaMasiyaak, e kala neɗɗo garɗo e am e leñol Aadama e oon laawol mi jaɓɓoto, so wonaa ɓe mbonnaaɓe ɓe nanngi maande daaba ngoo, walla ɓe mbayli hoore mum en e oon laawol. Goonga, goonga, kala garɗo e am e oon laawol, jirlitooɗo ngam wuurde nguurndam peewɗam, o njoɓdoyte haa abada, teddeendi njoɓdi maa ndii ko duumotoondi.

Ko noon woniri banndam, hisnugol ko dokkal nguurndam duumotoongam, ngal miin Yesuwa HaMasiyaak keɓi e rewrude e sariya Muusaa e goonga, e sadaka am duumotooɗo e hoore am no Njamnaari Allah ndi alaa hakke, ndi timmundi nii. Ko ɗum waɗi, kala garɗo e am e tuubu timmuɗo, e yankinaare, e ɗooftaare, mi artiray mo e am e Yahweh, mi rokka ɗum golle peewɗe keewɗe ɗe Yahweh yiɗnoo ɓe timmina e nder anniyaaji mum duumotooɗi. Ko ɗum waɗi, dokkal nguurndam duumotoongam ina woni e juuɗe am ngam kala naamndiiɗo e newaare e tuubu, e garɗo e am e waktuuji rokkiraaɗi ngam tuubu.

Miin woni Yesuwa HaMasiyaak, miɗo yiɗi on leñol am, miɗo ara ngam hisnude on, mi hisnoyay ɗoftiiɓe e mon ɓee haa timma, ko ɗum waɗi, ɗoftoɗee Konnguɗi mawɗo MIIN WONAA e nder ɗooftaare Masiya mon goonga oo, mo njamnaari ndii yahdata e mbooju nduu, sabu goonga, ko miin.

Ndaaree! Hisnugol Isiraayiila ina ɓadii, njamnaari ndii ina yima e ngootaagu e mbooju nduu ngu wulla e ndeenka tiiɗka ngam cukaloy mum, ko ɗum waɗi, ngero-ɗee, aan Isiraayiila, sabu hisnugol mon yettiima.

Nanee am e nder giɗli am e mon, leñol am, sabu Masiya mon goonga oo ina ɓadii!
