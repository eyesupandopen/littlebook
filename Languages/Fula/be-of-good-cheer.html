Seedeeji Ɗiɗi: Eklesiya
E nder Golle Piyeer
10 Abriil, 2024

Kooloree!

Onon cukalel am, kooloree; walla mi foolaani bonɗo on, mi soottori on ƴiiƴam am? Ko waɗi onon njuroo? Ko waɗi ɓerɗe mon teddi; on cikki mi yejjitii on? Alaa, e nokku nde ndaaroton faamu mon, ɗaɓɓee ñeeñal am, ngal ummorii dow; sabu ko e ñeeñal dewal on ardotoo, woto ngoppee ngal! Walla ñeeñal ena woya haa jooni e laabi mawɗi? Ndeen woto kulon, kooloree, sabu mi foolii, onon kadi on poolay, hono no mbaɗir-mi e ƴiiƴam am nii. Acciree jam am on deeƴna ɓerɗe mon, acciree konnguɗi am on welna ruuhu mon, sabu miɗo wonndi e mon.

Hol mo senndata on e giɗli am? Walla ko musiiba, koo kaafa, koo heege senndata on? Mi wiyi alaa, ndaaree anndon ko onon ndaɗii e am. Anndon mi maantinirii on maande Baaba am, ndeen maande waawaa fe'eede walla itteede e mon, ɗum non keddee e am, Joomiraawo mon, semmbe mon, e sottorɗo on. Ndaaree! Mi soodirii on ƴiiƴam am, mi innirii on, mi maantinirii on e fawaade e goonɗinal mon e innde am, kala goonɗinɗo ɗum hisiray goonɗinal, sabu peewɗo wuuran goonɗinal! Jokkee tama'u mon wuura, nde semmbina on, ɓeydon muñal e giɗli mon e goonɗinal mon, sabu on kisiniraama tama'u. Tama'u mawɗo e heɓtindaare mawnde ko mon, sabu mi bayyinii ɗum.

Ko waɗi on sunii, on njuroo? Ndaaree! Mi foolii, ɓooyataa on ngona e am, on nji'iran am gite mon, on nganndan hono no nganndiraa-ɗon nii.

Ndaaree! Wiyi Joomiraawo mawɗo, Joomiraawo doolnuɗo, Joomiraawo Joomiraaɓe. Ndaaree e am e nder sunaare mon e caɗeele mon. Mi anndi ɓerɗe mon, mi anndi no feewi on njiɗi kam, on njiɗi wonndude e am hono no njiɗir-mi wonndude e mon nii; tan woto njoƴƴee. Njaabee lam ɗoo, saanga seeɗa heddii e ndeenka mon — walla ɗum fotndorete e duumotooɗo e am, eehey teeyanam? Alaa, torraaji mon e fitinaaji mon e dow leydi potaani fotndoreede e teddungal ngal ɓooyataa mi feññina e mon, sabu on ngonay hono no ngonir-mi nii, on nji'an am hono no ngonir-mi nii, ɗum non woto acciree ɓerɗe mon jiiɓoo. Faamee jamaanuuji mon, laatoɗon heblaade law, sabu feeñde am waɗiray e jaawle, nde ɓuray ko njiɗnoo-ɗon fuu.

Ndaaree, onon sukaaɓe am! Aduna anndan miɗo yiɗi on, onon ko on am, Seytaane addan njoofka mawka ngam njoofde majjuɓe, kono ɗum ɓadaaki on. Alaa, ñaawoore wonaa mon, tikere am kadi wonaa mon. Faamee, onon sukaaɓe am, e nder am, onon ko on peewɓe, wonaa peewɓe e golle mon, kono ko peewɓe e peewal ngal mi ɓornii on. Woto mbedee comci ɗi mi ɓornii on, woto njoñon ruuhu am. Hay so tawii ko jamaanu sakkitiingo, mi wurtinan ruuhu am, ɓiɓɓe mon worɓe e rewɓe kaalan haala annabaaku, mawɓe mon ndootan, sukaaɓe mon nji'an ji'e. Golle ena keddii haa jooni, dille am cakkitiiɗe e ɗii jamaanuuji moƴƴere ɓuray ñalawma Pentekoste nde aran mi wurtini e suɓaaɓe am ruuhu mo keɓ-mi e Baaba am. Golle mawɗe ena njooɗii e mon, heewɓe keddii haa jooni hisnee, ɗum non ngollee golle am, ndewon sukaaɓe am, ɗaɓɓee baali am majjuɗi. Naamnee kam, mi nelon, hono no mbaɗir-mi on doole mawɗe, mi hoki worɓe dokke. Hol waawɗo daraade e mon, hay so tawii on njogii ngal jawdi e leeɗe ɓaleeje? Ko waɗi on cikkitorii kam haa jooni? E nder lo'eende mon semmbe am timminirtee e mon. Ndeen noon, keewnon e golle Joomiraawo, onon sukaaɓe am.

Ndaaree Deftere am Ceniinde, sabu hono no mbertinir-mi maayɓe nii, onon kadi on mbaawi waɗde noon. Wujee gite mon ngam on nji'a cellal mawngal keewngal ngal mbaɗ-mi ngam rimɗinde jeyaaɓe nde njah-mi to am'en; onon kadi on mbaawi waɗde noon. Walla mi wiyaani goonɗinɗo am, kanko o waɗan golle ɗeen, o waɗan golle ɓurɗe ɗeen mawnude sabu mi yahii to Baaba am e Baaba mon, to Alla am e Alla mon: Onon ko on laamɓe e cerniiɓe yeeso am, on njooɗii e nokkuuji toowɗi, ko waɗi on cikkitorii haa jooni? Jogoɗon tan goonɗinal hono gabbe mustard, sabu ko gabbe tokoose sanne jibinta lekki mawki, noon on ngonirta so on keddiima e am.

Ko waɗi on jiiɓata konnguɗi am, on njaasna sirluuji e haala am? Mi fenataa: on mbaawi waɗde ko nel-mi on so on keddiima e dewal. Kaalee Konngol am e teddungal mawngal e cuusal. Mbiyee ɗiin pelle, Njehee e maayo! Konnguɗi am ko goonga e nguurndam, ɗum non woto ngoonɗinee fenaande durooɓe majjuɓe ɓe mbi'ata on mbaawaa waɗde ko mbi'an-mi on. Keɓee jannde. On njiɗi sellinde ñawɓe? Yamiree ñawu ngu yalta e innde am, kaalee konnguɗi ɗii e goonɗinal e am e goonɗinal e konnguɗi ɗi ndokkat-mi on. Kaalee ngam ɗum laatoo, sabu nguurndam e maayde ena ngoni e doole ɗemngal; no ɗemɗe mon doolniri so on kaali doole Alla, on njamiri ko waawaa wonde.

Kaalee e cuusal. Kaalee e koolaare. Kaalee e goonɗinal, tawa on cikkitaaki hay huunde; sabu mi nelii on, mi hokkii on doole. Ko waɗi on naamnotoo ruuhu yalta e innde am tawa kulol ena woni e toni mon? Yamiree ɗum yalta e innde am e cuusal e koolaare, sabu mawɗo wonɗo e mon ɓuri mawde mo woni e aduna. Ndaaree! Ruuhuuji bonɗi kulii on, ena ndoga konnguɗi mon, ko waɗi on kulata geɗe lo'uɗe ɗe foolaa, ɗe ndeenndi yiite? Woto kulee, tan ngoonɗinee!

Njehee e innde am, ngollee golle am mawɗe. Ciftoree giɗli mon gadani, fooftoree e fooftere nde ndok-mi on. Accitee golle mon e teddeendi mon, njaɓee nawaale am; sabu nawaale am ina newii, donngal am ina hoyi. Ngollee golle ɗe mballan-mi on, njahdon e majje, sabu e waɗde noon on hisinan heewɓe e yiite, ceede keewe e konte mon. Wakkati ina ɓadii, teeyanam, e joofnirde laawol mon ina ɓadii, ɓuri ko cikkito-ɗon. Kono nanee kam, onon sukaaɓe am; nanee kam, teeyanam! Toranee so wakkati ena woodi haa jooni. Cellinee ñawɓe so Naange ena jalba e nguurndam mon, ena semmbina on e doole am.

Ɗaɓɓee baali am majjuɗi; noddee ɗi, sabu heewɓe keddii haa jooni hisnee e ɗii segonji sakkitiiji ɓuuɓri. Sabu ɓooyataa on keɓan ndonu mon e am, on nganndan nguurndam mon timmuɗam e am; sabu nguurndam mon suuɗii e am, so mi feeñii, noon kadi timmal nguurndam ngal Baaba hokki on e am feeñirta. Miɗo yiɗi on no feewi, onon cukalel am, ɗum non njiɗee majjuɓe noon. Woto njaawon ɓe, woto njaasnon ngonka maɓɓe, sabu Seytaane foolii ɓe hono no foolirnoo on nii; ɗum non ciftoree ndimaagu ngu mbaɗan-noo-mi on, ndokkee ɗum wonɓe e haaju mum. Hay so tawii heewɓe kaɓan e mon, woto ndaree haa baali am mooftee, sabu ɓooyataa alkama am moofetee e kisal e suudu am. Saliiɓe feeñde am saliikam, sali konngol am; ɗum non semmbiniree konngol am, ngoppee goonɗinde wuyɓe, sabu wuyɓe ko fenuɓe kadi.

Ko miin muñɗo haa e wuttudu am sakkitiindu, mi haɓi e greka e ƴiiƴam am ceniiɗam.

Ko miin umminaaɗo ñalawma tataɓo, mi ƴeeŋi to Baaba am.

Ko miin yahi e nder almuɓɓe am e nelaaɓe am balɗe capanɗe nayi, hay mi yiyanoo ko ɓuri yimɓe teemedde joy e saanga gooto, heewɓe nji'i kam, meemi kam, sabu ruuhu alaa terɗe e ƴiiƴam.

Ko miin ittaaɗo dow, tawi ena ndaara kam, e nder duule.

Ko miin mo jalbugol mum ɓuri naange jalbude, sabu miɗo hollita teddungal Baaba am.

Miɗo hebiloo feññinde teddungal mawngal e mon.

Miɗo wonndi e mon law, haa e joofnirde aduna.
