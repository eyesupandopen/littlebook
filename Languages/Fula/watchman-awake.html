Seedeeji Ɗiɗi: Eklesiya
E nder Golleeji Yuhanna Baptista
Abriil 05, 2024

Aan Wardiiɗo Fin!

Aan wardiiɗo, fin, taƴu cembol maa e goonga, njogorɗaa comcol nuunɗal!

Aan wardiiɗo, fin, njogorɗaa kufune kisndam e kaafa espri, ngal woni Konngol Allah!

Aan wardiiɗo, fin, njogorɗaa paɗe maa njoɗɗinaaɗe e sago Linjiila jam!

Tinndin yimɓe am, wata a deƴƴu, sabu mi udditii demngal maa! Haɓan yimɓe am, tinndin cuɓaaɓe am, sabu njoɓdi annabi'en a heɓay so a haalii konnguɗi ɗi mi hokkata ma tan. FIN! Malla mi wi'aani yimɓe am yo ɓe ndaro arde am? Malla wakkatiiji e sahaaji ɗii laaɓtaaki, ɗii ngonaa e mooɗon? E sabu ɗum, ndaree feeñde am, sabu ɗum ɓadiima. Ndaaree maale e kaawisaaji dow asama, sabu Baaba am feewnii laabi maɓɓe gila puɗɗam, ñalnde ndee maandinaama. Ndaaree jalbugol am dow asama nde buutayru ɓadii fiyeede; ndaree, mi anndinii ɗum.

Yimɓe am, njanngen Deftere am, ndaaree Binndi am Ceniɗi ngam faamde wonde mi ittay jommbaajo am e kiita bonɓe. Mi reenay jommbaajo am e terɗe am; sabu goɗɗo fuu wanyataa ɓanndu mum, kono o ñamminta ɗum o deena ɗum? Ndaaree, tokkee, ndaaree sahaaji, nganndee wakkatiiji ɗii njottake, subaka maa feeñu e dow maajum no naange mawngo fuɗirta e dow asamaan. Mi saleteeke, mi haɗeteeke. Hay so gujjo oo etotoo, o ubbay no rawaandu nii; kono rawaandu nde alaa ñiiƴe woni ko o woni. O haɗataa kam, mi saleteeke jawdi am, sabu mi soodii leydi ndii, teeŋti noon e yimɓe am, e ƴiiƴam am ɗam alaa ella. Innde am ndee ina toowi dow kala so wonaa Baaba am, mo ɓuri mi mawnude; mi saleteeke, mi haɗeteeke, mi dartineteeke.

Miɗo ara, mi deƴƴataa, tikkere am ndee ina wuli e dow Babilon Suudiiɗo, ee, Babilon Suudiiɗo yeeyoowo sukaaɓe tokosɓe no marsandiis e juuɗe worɓe bonɓe, ɓe ndartintee. Ko ɓuri ɓe jibineede ko ɓe jibinaaka sam, so wonaa ɓe njibine ɓe ngaɗa aybeeji bonɗi, mawɗi, e dow sukaaɓe. Ko ɓuri ko haayre niisirde fawaa e daaɗe maɓɓe, ɓe mberlee e geec; kono ɓe ndesanaama yiite duumotoongo, ñaawoore maa waɗee ɓooyataa, sabu tikkere Jawgel ngel ɓadii suddaade leydi ndii fof, torra Yaaquuba maa fuɗɗo so haɗoowo oo ittaama.

Tebitee, yimɓe am, ndaree tiiɗɗi e am e ɓernde wootere e anniya gooto. Accitee geɗe aduna ɗe njiɗno-ɗon, mbaɗee kam, Joomiraawo Iisa Almasiihu, yiɗde mooɗon; sabu ko miin woni suudu mooɗon tiindungal, kaaƴe ndeenka mooɗon, e reenooɓe mooɗon. Hol joganɗo mooɗon baawɗo daranaade juuɗe am semmbe? Cuɓaaɓe am e koolniiɓe am ina ngoni e juuɗe am udditiiɗe, hay gooto waawaa ittude mooɗon e am. Ko on lamɓe am, on kisnaama e am; nfooƴee e am e golle mooɗon, sabu wakkatiiji njoɓdi maa puɗɗo e dow maajum. Njoɓdi kiita bonɓe ndii ina ɗoo, njoɓdi koolniiɓe am ndii ina woni e dow cuɓaaɓe am, sabu mi njoɓitay ɓe e ndonu maɓɓe, sabu mi waɗii ɓe fotɓe.

Yo aan, mboddi nde alaa nafoore, nde alaa ñiiƴe; aan mboddi ɓooyndi: wakkati maa yottake, kiita ina dow maa. Hay so aɗa sikki e mawninkinaare maa a dañay nafoore, mi ittay kala ko mooftu-ɗaa e juuɗe maa gujjooje, e ɓeen rewɓe maa e njiɗɗaari maɓɓe njoɓdi maa njoɓdi ndonu maɓɓe e weendu yiite, ɗo kala njoɓitirtee golle mum laabi keewɗi. Tikkere Allah maa jooɗo e dow maa haa abada, a fooƴataa.

Lepte mawɗe maa won comfortooɗo maa, cuurka jalbooja ka sufre wulnde maa wona haa abada e kine maa, a njoɓay haa abada. Cuurka lepte maa maa ɓeydo haa abada, kala rewɓe maa gila e tokosɓe haa e mawɓe maa keɓ ndonu maɓɓe e njoɓdi maɓɓe e maa e leppi boɗeeji bulooji ɗi suddotoo maa haa abada so a haɓɓaama a werlaama e ngonka maa. Wonay woyde mawnde e ñitde ñiiƴe. Aɗa sikki aɗa waawi daranaade Yahweh e Almasiihu mum, kono a wontay huunde so wonaa jeertineede duumotoonde e lepte maa ɗe ngalaa keerol, a uddete haa abada. A bonnataa tagu am kadi, a yejjiteete haa laaɓa, a siftoreteeke.

Nii woni yimɓe am, jommbaajo am koolniiɗo mo njiɗ-mi no feewi, hol ko huloton? Tikkere am e tikkere Baaba mooɗon wonaa mooɗon, ndawrataa e dow mooɗon, on ngontataa e ngoƴa maayde ɗiɗmere. Mbele hulee no mi njoɓitirta gañooɓe mooɗon no fotiri, sabu njoɓdi ko e am, wiyi Joomiraawo! On nganndaa on kiitoyay malaa'ika'en? On nganndaa on kiitoyay aduna? Mbele laɓɓinee e am, sabu ko ƴiiƴam am sobtinɗam tan waɗata on fotɓe daraade yeeso am e teddungal. Haɓɓee konngol am e nder ɓerɗe mooɗon, ndesee binndi am e tiiɗe mooɗon, nfooƴee e konnguɗi am ɓooyɗi; sabu nguurndam ko ɗum woni e mooɗon, nguurndam mooɗon ina suuɗii e am, mi feeñinay ɗum!

Yo aan, Babilon Suudiiɗo, so a faamno sadaka jam mo Baaba am neldi ma, jooni a saliima kam, Iisa Nasarankeejo, ɗum waɗii no binndi ceniɗi ɗii nii, wonde worɓe bonɓe e jammotooɓe maa ɓeydoo bonde. Kono aan, yo Babilon Suudiiɗo mawɗo, a bonnii ɓoggol Baaba am ngol nooneeji keewɗi ngol o hokki Nuuhu e aadee en fof no fodoore nii wonde o halkataa leydi ndii fof e ndiyam kadi, o jogoray konngol makko. Kono aan, yo Babilon Asira, a halkete e waktu gooto.

Yiite mawngo maa ara e dow maa; dimmbere mawnde maa foondu ma; cuuɗi maa toowɗi maa njanu; gollorɗe maa maa majju e bonanndeeji, yoorɗereeji mawɗi maa njaltu e daande geec maa, mi njoɓitay ma no golle maa nii, kiita mawka ina e dow maa. Sabu aɗa wonnoo yitere yaakaare e jayngol aduna he sahaaji seeɗa, kono a suɓiima njiɗɗaari, neɗɗaagu, e bonannde ngam wonde wondiiɓe maa, yo debbo jangu mawɗo! Hol hisinɗo ma e am e tikkere am e tikkere am ɓadtiinde ndee, ndee wuli e dow maa? Aɗa darii e jolokooje, aɗa ndaara kiita maa cakkitiika, ka wonay laaɓtungal ngam aduna oo yiyde, kulol mawngol maa ara e heewɓe.

Ndaaree! Leyɗeele keewɗe Baaba am liɓii e kiita, sabu o toownata gooto o jippina goɗɗo, sabu Yahweh ko Jom Baawɗe, alaa ɓurɗo Alla mawɗo duumotooɗo daraniiɗo hisna hoore mum e yiite ngam bonɓe. Ndaaree! Leyɗeele ɗe laamlaamuuji mawɗi mbaɗi ɗii ina ngoodi haa jooni, kono e laawol ustiingol, kono ɗe njokkondiri. Kono aan, yo debbo jangu mawɗo, a yoolii e yoolooɓe heewɓe ngam njiɗɗaari bonndi e welwelooji greb. A fuɗɗiima yimɓe am e am e nder winndere ndee kala e tuundi ndi ndokku-ɗaa leƴƴi ɗii ngam nyaamde e yarde, a werlete haa laaɓa, a wontataa kadi. E dow ndoondi maa leñol mawngol ummataa, kono heddiiɓe maa hisnee. Juuɗe gaño ina ngoni e daaɗe mooɗon, mi addii leɗɗe e keeri mooɗon. Ɓe njaltata, ɓe njanngata, ɓe nanngata ko heddii. Hol walloowo ma?

Sabu a salii ɓadimaawo maa, kulol Joomiraawo alaa e ɓerɗe mooɗon. A wiyi, mi wonaa jom suudu, kono aɗa jom suudu, aɗa bumɗo, aɗa muumo yeeso am. Kisndam alaa e maa sabu jangu maa jeertingal ngal ngaɗ-ɗaa e aduna oo; e sabu ɗum, mi halkay ma haa laaɓa. Kewuuji garooji maa njaha ko ɓuri yaawde; ndeen ndiyam mawngam maa sudda comci maa. Yiite maa wula ma haa laaɓa, maa laɓɓina bonɓe e leydi ndii, jeyaaɓe am maa rimɗinee, hay huunde waawaa haɗde ko ɓadii waɗde. Ko miin woni Joomiraawo Iisa Almasiihu, mi haalani ma ko adii nde ɗum waɗata, mi anndinii ko ɓadii waɗde no waɗiri nii, sabu konnguɗi ɗii ko goonga, hay huunde waawaa waylude ɗi, sabu annabaakuuji maa timminee.

Ndaree tiiɗɗi, yimɓe am, sabu mi nanngay jommbaajo am; sabu miɗo jogii peeje e anniyaaji ngam makko e laamu am garoowu. O wonta e am haa abada, o yaltataa e am haa abada. Miɗo yiɗi mo, miɗo deena mo e yiɗde e yurmeende timmunde. Mi waɗay on semmbe e teddungal am, on ngaɗay golle mawɗe e ndonu mooɗon garoowu, mbele hulee. Anndee ɓernde am ina heli, miɗo sunii e yimɓe am ɓe nparnaaki hoore mum en. Woɓɓe maa accete sabu alla oo aduna oo wumnii hakkillaaji maɓɓe, heewɓe parnaaki. Kulee, hay so on muññay heewde ngam am, on fof on hisneeɓe jogiiɓe maande Allah guurɗo. Mi hokkay on so on saliima innde am, so, on ngollanii kam e golle ɗe ndokku-mi on.

On ngaɗay golle mawɗe so on naamndiima kam ngam mi toɗɗa on. Mi noddii on, miɗo yiɗi on no feewi, ɓeen noddooɓe innde am, muññooɓe haa laakara, maa hisnee, umminee e teddungal mawngal, njoɓee haa abada ngam darnde mooɗon, sabu miɗo yiɗi on, mi haalanii ɗum noppi mooɗon. Won gese keewɗe, kala e sahaaji mum, mbele hulee; sabu sahaaji tiiɗɗi ndokketee worɓe Allah mawɓe ngam ndeenka yimɓe, mi noddii on. Malaa'ika'en am maa maandin 144,000, woɓɓe e mooɗon maa mballu ɓe e golle maɓɓe e toɗɗagol ngol ndokku-mi ɓe. Kulee, sabu mi waɗay maale, kaawisaaji, e kaawisaaji keewɗi e juuɗe mooɗon, nfooƴee e yiɗde am, nfooƴee e semmbe am; sabu miɗo wondi e mooɗon, mi accataa on, mi woppataa on.

On ngannday mi nanngii jommbaajo am, mi mooftii koolniiɓe e am ngam jibinde tuubugol heewɓe e nder aduna maayɗo. Mbele aduna oo rewa, wata on tawtore musiiba e ngonka mum, sabu wonaa mooɗon so on cenanaa Joomiraawo e nder ɓerɗe mooɗon, on nguura ngam am. Sabu bonannde mawnde ina ɓadii, nde ummaaki e ndeeɗoo laawol, nde ummataako kadi, sukaaɓe am tokosɓe, sabu mi anndinii ɗum. Ndeenee e laawol ngol ndarni-mi ngam mooɗon, ndesee koyɗe mooɗon tiiɗɗe e laawol ngol. Mi wonta e mooɗon, mi hokkay on jam ɓurɗo faamde, on keɓay jam e nder aduna mo alaa deeƴre, jam mo reenata on so on njippii nano walla njaafii.

Wata on njaɓ maande ndee, wata on ngoongɗin fenaande ndee. Ɓe ndokkoyay jam, cellal, e kisal ngam ɓeen njaɓooɓe maande ndee, ɓe kaalay on on naati e aduna keso jam, jawdi, e kisal. Wata on ngoongɗin fenaande mboddi ndii, sabu laamɗo maɓɓe oo jogorataa konngol mum. Oon neɗɗo greb oo ina jogii anniya gooto, oon woni: halkude.

Goonga alaa e makko, Seytaane e hoore mum maa jogoo laabi makko. Jam alaa e makko, kono kala konngol maa won fenaande welnde, mbaɗaandi ngam ardinde aduna oo e halkaare timmunde. So mi ustiino ñalɗi ɗii, hay ɓanndu hisataano e lepte ɗe o addata e leydi ndii, sabu o rokketee baawɗe ngam timminde anniyaaji am sahaaji seeɗa. Ndeen o haɓɓetee e jolokooje niɓɓere tiiɗɗe e nder lugge ngam o yaltine seeɗa ngam jammude aduna oo laawol cakkitiingol. So o mooftii ɓe e hoore makko, ɓe mbaɗay njanngu e ndeen wuro mawngo, yiite ummoraade e Allah maa halka ɓe fof. Ndeen kiita cakkitiika, ee, kiita jappeere laamu boɗeejo mawngo, maa fuɗɗo e dow maɓɓe fof, ɓe keɓataa safaara. Mi haalanii on ɗee geɗe ngam hokkude on yaakaare e parnaade yimɓe am, sabu Asira Mawɗo Allah maa timmine.

Ko miin woni laawol, goonga, e nguurndam.

Ko miin woni Laamɗo Laamɓe e Jom Jomɓe.

Ko miin woni potɗo udditde maande, wakkatiiji ɗii njottake.

Ko miin woni etotooɗo laabi cuɓaaɓe am ngam mi laɓɓina on, mi laɓɓina on e ƴiiƴam am sobtinɗam.

Ko miin woni Ɓiy Allah garɗo e ɓanndu, fawaaɗo e leggal, umminaama ñalnde tataɓere, yiyraama e heewɓe, jaɓɓaama e dow asama yeeso gite maɓɓe.

Ko miin woni ɓiy Daawuuda, mi mooftay heddiiɓe Yaaquuba e les juuɗe am deeƴre e semmbe.

Ko miin woni timminoowo anniya Baaba am fof.

Miɗo ɓadii waɗde gañooɓe am teɓteɓel koyɗe am, bone wonande ɓeen saliiɓe am, sabu mawngu woni tikkere Jawgel ngel.

Ko miin woni jaltinoowo tagu Baaba am, mi rokkata geɗe fof e juuɗe makko, ngam Allah wona kala e kala.

Ko miin woni mo laamu mum woni haa abada haa abada, mi hokkay cuɓaaɓe am nyaamde e leɗɗe nguurndam e yarde e maayooji ndiyam guurɗam, on njuurnataako, on njoornataa kadi, mi foottay gonɗi fof e gite mooɗon.

Miin, Joomiraawo, mi haalii ɗum, mi waɗay ɗum. Wakkati oo yottake, ndaaree feeñde am, sabu hay huunde waawaa haɗde juuɗe am semmbe.
