28 Oktoobar 2024

Goongɗingol Annabaaku iwde e J. Leland Earls

Nanndee haala am, yimɓe am; kettinee ko mi anndinta on e oo sahaa. Tawde ko mi ari e mon jooni, mi woni nelaaɗo doole ngam udditande on ko suuɗanoo gila e jamaanuuji e jiidi? Ee, jooni miɗo faalaa udditande hettintooɓe ko mi halfinani fedde am e ɗii balɗe sakkitorɗe. Hiɗon miijo mi accat on tawa alaa peewal, tawa on anndaa anniyaaji am e mon e ɗii balɗe sakkitorɗe? Hiɗon miijo mi waawataa suɓaade nelaaɓe heertiiɓe ɓe mi hollirta nelal am e anniya am e mon tuma nde kala huunde timmata? Nanndee haala am: Miɗo artira, kettinee ko kaalanam mi on e oo sahaa. Sabu miɗo jogii jooni defterel e juuɗe am, miɗo anndina ko woni e maggel koo to maccuɓe am suɓaaɓe heertiiɓe.

Kadi hol ko winndaa heen? Ko ɗum ɗoo winndaa heen. Yimɓe am, miɗo ara e mon rewrude e nelal am e anniyaaji am ngam balɗe sakkitorɗe, ɗi ngoni timmoode kala ko mi halfinani on gila e jiidi e jamaanuuji keewɗi. Kadi mi waɗan on naatde e timmoode ko mi halfinani on koo, hay timmoode ko mi timmini dow gaafaangal tuma nde mi funti ƴiiƴam yontere am sabu mon, ngam goongɗinde e mon Ahdi Duumotooɗo oo, ahdi timmuɗo ngam yimɓe am; ahdi moƴƴinɗo timmoode teddungal am e doole am fof ngam ɓeen ɓe nganndinaa heertiiɓe ngam heɓde.

Sabu miɗo wiya on seeɗa, ee, keddiiɗo, maa halfine ngam heɓde ndee wahyu e oo anniya balɗe sakkitorɗe, ko ɗum miɗo anndina jooni. Sabu ɗum doolnu no feewi, waawaa heɓeede so wonaa fedde halfinaande, hay “girel famarel,” ngo heɓata timmoode ahdi oo. Sabu on njahat e ɗii balɗe sakkitorɗe e nder timmoode ko mi halfinani on koo. Sabu jooni miɗo rokka on wahyu ko uddanoo, nanondiraa, waɗaa, halfinaa tuma nde ƴiiƴam yontere am toɓi to Kalvaari. Tawde alaa ko winndaa mi wiyi: “Timmii”? Hol ko timmi? Timmoode ahdi am timminoo; hay e oon sahaa waɗanoo fof ngam daɗndude timmunde e kala doole gaño oo, e naatgol timmungol e kala ko miɗo halfini e mi waawi rokkude, haa arti noon e ittude maayde. Ee, doole timmuɗe ngam foolde kala golle gaño oo, e teddungal timmungal halfinaangal gila e fuɗɗoode aduna ngam ɓeen halfinaaɓe heɓde e naatde.

Kono jooni kadi alaa ko winndaa ɓe kaɗii Ceniido Isra'iila? Sukaaɓe am, KO MIIN woni ceniido Isra'iila! Kono yimɓe njoommimi. Ɓe tiiɗnii ɓerɗe maɓɓe ngam goongɗinde fenaande, ɗum addani ɓe waasde huutoraade timmunde kala ko mi halfini, mi nanondiri, mi nanondiri, to gaafaangal Kalvaari. Ee, miɗo wiya on kala huunde halfinanoo e oon sahaa. Kono hol to mi heɓata yimɓe halfinaaɓe—ɓe nganndinaama ngam goongɗinde timmunde? Ee, hay gooto heɓaaka, so wonaa Yosuwa e Kalepu. Kettintooɗo, yo hetto! Sabu hay ɗoo miɗo yiyri noppi mon teddi, mi waawataa udditande on timmunde e oo sahaa ko ɗum firti, kono on paamat hade balɗe keewɗe ɓennude.

Kono jooni mi udditan ko keɓɗon heɓde. Sabu defterel ngel ina jogii ko heewi ngam ndee ñalawma e oo sahaa. Kono mi foti udditande seeɗa seeɗa, sabu hay jooni heewɓe e mon ko hono cukaloy koy newaaɗi e kosam, on njaɓaani teew. Woɓɓe paamat ko ɓuri heewde e ko kaal-mi koo. Nii woni yo mi halfin on, mi juulna ɓerɗe mon timmunde ngam heɓde nelal balɗe sakkitorɗe ngoo. Sabu rokkiraaɗo ko heewi, ko heewi ina ɗaɓɓiree e mum. Hiɗon njiɗi anndude ko woni e ndee defterel? Ndeen halfinee ɓerɗe mon ngam heɓde. Sabu seeɗa seeɗa udditante on so on keɓii heɓde.

Sabu hay jooni miɗo halfina yimɓe ɓe njogoytoo leydi halfinaandi ndii fof—ɓe njaltata maayo Jordani—ɓe njalata doole liingu mawngu nguu—ee, kañum ardata e maale e kaawniiɗe fenaande, golle mum nanndi e ilam maayo Jordani. Miijitee ɗum, yimɓe am! Tawde alaa ko winndaa liingu mawngu nguu arata hono ilam maayde e halkaare mawɗo? Kadi tawde oon liingu mawngu arataa e yimɓe am e ilam fenaande e njofndi e ndee ñalawma?

Kono on foolataake, sabu mi waɗan ndiyam dam haɗee tawa on njalta e juuɗe am doole ngam jogaade leydi halfinaandi on ndii. Sabu goonga, ndee leydi ko leydi moƴƴiri. Yimɓe am njahii e ladde ruuhu mberlaandi duuɓi keewɗi. Miɗo wiya on ‘cappanɗe jeewte’ ɗee timminii, laawol ɗiɗaɓol yimɓe am ina ngara e Leydi Alkawal. Hay jooni, miɗo halfina on ngam jaltude e doole ruuhu. Kettinee, miijitoo ko kaal-mi on koo. Sabu defterel ngel njogii-mi e juuɗe am ngel ina jogii kala sirruuji e kaɓirɗe ɗe njiɗ-mi anndon e ɗii balɗe.

Ee yimɓe am, tawde mi heñaaki duuɓi keewɗi ngam oo sahaa ngam mi waawa udditande on ko suuɗanoo? Tawde wonaa oo sahaa woni ko daartol fof heñunoo? Tawde wonaa ɗum woni ko annabaaɓe kaali, hay so tawii kamɓe e koye maɓɓe ɓe paamaano ɗum? Ee yimɓe am, hiɗon miijii teddungal e teddungal rokkaaɗon ngam wuurde e oo sahaa e tawtoreede ndee golle sakkitorɗe? Ee, yo on finu e ɗowngo mon ngam yiytude teddungal halfinaangal ngam oo sahaa. Hol no njiyrorto-ɗon e ɓerɗe mon sahaa oo yettaaki? Hol no keñorto-ɗon, on naatataa e ɓerɗe mon fof e yontere mon fof? Miɗo wiya on, JOONI WONAA WAKKATI ƊAƁƁIRDE MI E ƁERNDE MON FOF, ngam mi waawa haalde e mon e nder suudu ndernderiiru ɓernde mon, mi anndina on wasiya am ngam oo sahaa. Woto kettinee ɓeen ɓe njiɗata saftinde on e wumtinde on e ko kaal-mi koo.

Hol to ngonnooɓe cuusal ngam YALTUDE e ndiyam caɗeele e jeewte, NJAHDA E AM E GOONƊINAL, tawa hiɗon anndi mi waawi wallude on e tabitinde on e oo sahaa e haa abada, ngam on njaha e foolde dow ndiyam jarribooji e fitinaaji, ɗi njarrabta yimɓe fof e ndee ñalawma? Njahee, mi wi'i, hunaade gite mon e am, woto ndaaree to bannge nyaamo walla to bannge nano; woto ndaaree to bannge yimɓe wonɓe e mon walla to ndiyam mbonɗam njiɗɗam muñde on.

Sabu miɗo wiya on on njottataa abada e noddaango sakkitoro e finnde haa on keɓa goongɗinde ngam yaltude e laana mon jooni ngam welde e kisal, on naata e NDOGU ANNIYA AM CENIIƊO, woto mbonkaaji sikkitaare e kulol foola on, woto kadi ɓeen cuɓiiɓe jooɗaade e laana ndee kaɗa on. Kono NGaree YIMƁE AM, sabu mi jogotooɓe cuusal yahde e ndiyam ko meeɗaani waɗeede, ɓe kawra e am so mi arii e mon e doole e teddungal e ndee ñalawma.

Tawde ɓe njiyaani ko ɓanndu yahata e dow ndiyam, wonaa ruuhu no ɓe cikkanoo? Kadi tawde Piyeer, e ɓanndu mum, yahaani e dow ndiyam? Nii woni, yimɓe am, wonaa tan ruuhu kono ko ƁANNDU TEEW ndu addante e am e ndee ñalawma ngam waɗde golle kaawniiɗe dow ndiyam jiiɓru e anksu aadee. Tawde wonaa on woni ƁANNDU Almasiihu; tawde wonaa on woni teew am e yi'e am? Nii woni on ngonata gootum e am e ndee ñalawma; nii woni en kawray ON THE ndiyam ngam yaltude e ndee ñalawma, Joomiraawo wiyi. Nii kadi on ngonirta e balɗe garooje, Joomiraawo wiyi, ƁANNDU FOOLOOƁE—ɓeen foolɓe kala pollugol ɓernde e ndiyam gaño oo, ɓe njahata e foolde e doole foolde.

Yimɓe am, nanndee haala am! Tawde asiidaji e nder reedu ko yeru golle am e nder mon? Hiɗon mbaawi heɓde ñamri e ko wonaa mburu e ko hebbintaaki? Miɗo wiya on, golle Ruuhu Am ina poti ngam on keɓa ko rokka nguurndam. Tawde mi waawataa heɓde ko weeɓi e haala am, mi waɗa ɗum naata e ngonka mon ngam on keɓa ñamri nde kaajorɗon ndee? Kono hol no njaŋngirto-ɗon ko wonaa haala am? Hol no njiylorto-ɗon ko anndu-ɗon hebbintaa ndernderi mon?

Miɗo wiya on, so on ɗaɓɓiraani mi timmunde, on accaani Ruuhu am semmbina ko naatnaton e nder njuɓɓudi mon, on taway koye mon e ngonka ruuhu nannduɗo e ko siforaa ɗoo. Golle ruuhu am ina poti. So wonaa ɗuum, ko ko maayngu tan njogoton, ko waɗataa hay huunde ngam addude semmbe ruuhu e ndernderi mon. Nii woni, kettinee ko njanngaton koo, sabu ko goonga e laawol ko woni e waɗde yimɓe am hannde. Sabu waawaa, e ngonka jooni ka eklesiya, ngam mi gollora e Ruuhu am haa haala am rokka nguurndam e cellal e kisal kala kaajorɗo. Nii woni, miɗo artira, mi wiya, ɗaɓɓee mi, yo mi rokku on ko poti ngam on nguura nguurndam ruuhu keewɗam semmbe, Joomiraawo Iisa Almasiihu wiyi.

Ƴeewte ummoraade e William Brooks:

Laabi ɗiɗi jooni, mi jokkondiri e jom ndee lowre, kalfinaaɗo golle Mr. Earls, kono hay huunde heɓaaka, ɗum addani mi miijaade jooni o jaabotaako kadi. Gaa gaa ɗuum, alaa ko mbiimi e hay gooto e yimɓe ɓe njogii golle mum, ɗum wonaa hay huunde e golle annabaaku ɗe Mr. Earls waɗi, haala mum darii haa hannde. Joomiraawo ko ñaawoowo en fof, ko to Almasiihu en ndarii walla en njani, kadi Almasiihu ina waawi darɗinde en!

Kono, ngam laaɓtinde, lowre nde njokkondir-mi ndee ina jogii jaŋde e darnde keewɗe ɗe mi renndaani, ɗe mi jaɓaani, kadi mi nanondiraani e Biibal e yoga e ko winndaa. Heewde e ɗeen geɗe ɗe njibini-mi, wonaa Mr. Earls winndi ɗe, kono hay so tawii hol winndi, janngu haa a laaɓa yeeso Alla, gollotooɗo mo hersataa, feccude haala goonga no haanirta nii.

Kadi mi heɓan fartaŋŋe mi wiya ɗum kadi. Kala ko neɗɗo wiyi so wiyi ina haalda e innde Alla, e annabaaku walla e ko wonaa ɗuum, njaɓee ɗeen konnguɗi to binndi ceniɗi, haa teeŋti to Joomiraawo ngam goongɗinde e faamde. Alaa nafoore naamnaade mi goonga annabi oo walla oo. Mi jaabotaako sabu mi yiɗaa naatde e piccal ngam haalde ko boni e hay annabi goongaajo Alla, kadi mi alaa wakkati ngam waylude hakkilantaagal am ngam hollude golle neɗɗo goɗɗo. Hol en ngoni ñaawooɓe maccuɗo neɗɗo goɗɗo? To joom mum en ɓe ndarii walla ɓe njani, nii en ngollortoo Joomiraawo men Iisa Almasiihu. Nii woni, ko Joomiraawo woni goongɗinoowo walla yeddoowo goonga kala neɗɗo wiyi ina haalda e innde Joomiraawo. So Joomiraawo suɓii haalde e neɗɗo, mi fodiima on ɓe njogii jokkondiral tiiɗngal e Almasiihu, o anndi ko ɓuri heewde e maɓɓe ko ɓe nganndi e koye maɓɓe. Njaɓee ɗum to Joomiraawo!

Ee, ma a taw a wi'an, Wiliyam, hol ko woni anniya maa e ɗuum fof? Mi jaabotoo ko Joomiraawo Iisa Almasiihu udditi laawol ngam en heɓa Defterel ngel anndiraangel Linjiila Duumotooɗo, wonaa tan e binndi ceniɗi, ko ɗum woni ko ɓuri teeŋtude, kono kadi duuɓi 60 jooni e hunduko J. Leland Earls. E hoore am, miɗo yetta goonga makko, haa teeŋti miɗo yetta Almasiihu Iisa, Joomiraawo men ummitiiɗo oo, sabu golle mum e oo neɗɗo ngam rokkude mo ɗee konnguɗi barkinɗi en no feewi hannde. Miɗo yetta Joomiraawo Iisa Almasiihu sabu udditande mi, ngam mi rennda e mon fof, Defterel Wahyu Faandaare 10/Linjiila Duumotooɗo Wahyu Faandaare 14. Jam e kisal!
