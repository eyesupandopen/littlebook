Seedamaku ɗiɗo: Seedaaku Eklesiya on
Nder Kuuɗe Poola Nelaaɗo on
24 Abriil, 2024

Nanee Konnguɗi Joomiraawo: Asiri Mawɗo on Feññinaama!

Yesu Almasiihu wi'i resraaɗo makko: Toɗɗitee, nanee konnguɗi am, onon suɓaaɓe am, faamee haala am, sabu asiri mawɗo woni dewal! Heewɓe e mooɗon paamaali asiri mawɗo Allah on, ammaa o jooɗii nder ɓernde e dow tagdi tokkooɓe Laawol ngol, haa to bannge diina e to bannge golle fof: O woni caka diina Masiihiŋko'en ka mi tagiri on nder am, on laatake tagaaɗo keso nder Iisaa Almasiihu, sabu miin, Iisaa Almasiihu, mi waɗirii on noon.

Heewɓe e mooɗon mbilinganake asiri mawɗo Allah on ngam wakkati e aadaaji e naalankaagal fenaande ka ɓe ndarii ngam ɓe njoola. Wota on kule, onon sukaaɓe am, nanee konnguɗi am e haala am, sabu miɗo firtan on ɗum ɗoo nder ɗii menenji seeɗa cakkitiiɗi jamaanu. Faamee, onon sukaaɓe am, wonde jiiɓru mawndu e fenaanɗe keewɗe naatinaama nder Eklesiya am diga uuruuji njoomɗi e jannde majje ceniiɗe. Ɓe senndiri Konngol am (e Ɓanndu am) e laawol fenaande rewrude e jannde jamaanuuji (dispensationalism), ndee ko fenaande mawnde nde addunooɓe ɓee mahi cuuɗi mum'en dow njaareendi, wanaa dow am, kaaƴel nyiɓirde mawngel ngel, sabu so wonaa miin, hay gooto waawaa mahde.

Njaareendi ndi ɓe suɓii ngam wonande ɓe kaaƴel nyiɓirde ko Bibiluuji bonɗi, ardinooji haa kawral diina ngam anniyaaji neɗɗo hakke oo jogii nder ɓernde mum, anniyaaji laamu e maayde e halkaare. Njaareendi ndi ɓe kuutori ngam mahde ko binndi bonɗi, binndi Gereknaaɓe ɗi njoɓaa diga Aleksandiriya. Tokkee binndi diga iwdi Antiyokiya, iwdi Bizantiin, ɗiɗi fof ina njahra haa Textus Receptus, gaño mawɗo Room. Alaa jamaanuuji senndaaɗi, Allah senndaaka, ammaa miɗo firtan jamaanu no o hollirtee nder ɓatakeeji am. Allah nanondirii e aadee rewrude e jamaanuuji; janngee faa paamon. Nde faamee, ɗee geɗe senndaaɗe iwdaa e am ammaa ko ɗe peñaa ngam bonnude Eklesiya am gila puɗɗam.

Asiri mawɗo on suuɗaama on, onon sukaaɓe am, ammaa suuɗaaka, sabu mi feññinanii ɗum Poola Nelaaɗo duuɓi keewɗi ko ɓenni. O suuɗiraama fenaanɗe, joolol, e jannde majje ɗe iwdaa e am; ammaa o suuɗii ɗo yimɓe fof njiyata ɗum, gujjo oo yiɗuno ko wota on tesko ɗum, ammaa ko huunde mawnde nde meeɗaa yaltude hakkunde. Sabu ko ɓuri hulɓinde Seyɗaane e Eklesiya am ko o anndi o alaa baawɗe e dow mooɗon so on njahii e baawɗe am timmuɗe e jokkondiral peewngal e am. Ngam maajum, nde tawno o waawataa fusde maande Allah nde mi waɗi e dow mooɗon, hay gooto waawataa jaɓtude on e juuɗe am malla juuɗe Baaba am, o yaltinan on, o etotoo ñiddinde on e baasal. Wanaa baasal jawdi; Eklesiya am fotaano hay wakkati gooto mawninde linjiila barke fenaande, sabu iwdaa e am, hay so tawii heewɓe e mooɗon ngoonɗinii ɗeen fenaanɗe. Seyɗaane yiɗani on ko baasal ruuhuyankeewal ngam o suuɗa on goonga mooɗon, o namma on ngoonɗinon onon ko on jattinaaɓe ɓe ngalaa baawɗe, laatiiɓe fof ko waylittooɓe bannge goɗɗo ngam hulɓere, ko ɗum naftoraango fenaango e jamirooje am ngam haɗde sukaaɓe am yahde e baawɗe mum'en goongaaje. Aa'a, mi neldaali on ngam waylittoɗon bannge goɗɗo ngam laatoɗon maccuɓe, bonannde foola on; mi neli on ngam fooliron bonannde e moƴƴere, mbonnonon golle Ibliisa, sabu noon mi neliraa, noon kadi mi neliri on. Ammaa faamee: yoɓto ko yam wonani, wota on yoɓtito, ammaa waylitee bannge goɗɗo so haananii ngam hisnude neɗɗo goɗɗo e accude tikkere waɗa golle mum, sabu nafsuuji keewɗi kisaama e ndee laawol.

Faamee: ɓe njoolii on e fenaanɗe majje, ɓe ndarii ɗee fenaanɗe luutndiiɗe goonga am sara binndi ceniiɗi. Ɓe mbaɗiri ɗum nder geɓe Bibiluuji janirɗi; ɓe mbaɗiri ɗum e firtooji fenaanɗi; ɓe mbaɗiri ɗum e jamba rewrude e waylude maanaaji won e konnguɗi Gereknaaɓe ɗi tuugnii e aadaaji heeferɓe arandeeji, wanaa e goonga Konngol Allah Ceniingol. On mbaawi huutoraade ɗii iwdiiji sahaa e sahaa, ammaa mbaɗee hakkilantaagal mawngal, accitee neɗɗo tageejo etaade firtande on geɗe ruuhuyankeeje; sabu neɗɗo tageejo waawataa faamde geɗe ruuhuyankeeje, sabu ko ɗe puyɗe e makko. Ngam maajum, anndee wonde heewɓe e ɗii mbondiiji ɗi taƴata binndi alaa goonɗinal e Konngol Allah, ɓe potaani golle ɗe ɓe ndokki hoore mum'en, sabu ɓe ngalaa ruuhu Almasiihu, ɓe ngonaa am; sabu so tawno ɓe ngonaano am, mi arduno ɓe e goonga. Ko miin woni kaŋko mo maantini on ngam rimɗineede timmunde e baawɗe ruuhu am ceniingu.

Joomiraawo Iisaa Almasiihu wi'i resraaɗo makko giɗaaɗo: miɗo haajani on faamde anniya mooɗon goonga, so on paamaali asiri mawɗo on e no o jokkondirta e mooɗon, on njahataa e timmal baawɗe ɗe mi noddiri on, ammaa on laatan ɓe golle mum'en ustii no heewɓe e mooɗon ngoniri. Ina metta kam, ina sunna ɓernde am yiyde sukaaɓe am paamaali goonga mum'en, ɓe ngalaa yiɗde yiylo. Fota ko on yiyloo konnguɗi am e hakkilantaagal fof; ammaa jannde fenaanɗe ina waɗa heewɓe weltoo, sabu alaa nafoore heen. Sabu ko fenaanɗe Seyɗaane, ammaa heewɓe ina nguuri e ñamri yaawndi e njaramji sukkara, heewɓe kadi ngalaa doole ñamde ñamri nde mi hokki e yarde njaram ka mi hokki, sabu so on mbaɗii noon, on njoomtaataa, on nɗomɗataa kadi haa abada.

Nde nanee konnguɗi am, onon resraaɓe am weltooɓe; wakkati yottake ummaade e noddaango mooɗon goonga, mbaɗon golle ɗe mi heblani on, anndon miɗo waɗan kaayeefiiji keewɗi e juuɗe mooɗon so on njahii ngam am e goonɗinal e anndal goonga. Sabu dume mi wi'unoo nder golle am to Isra'iila nde njahannoo-mi e yeeso yimɓe nder Yahuuda? Dume Luuka winndi e dow haala kaa: O wi'i ɓe, Onon, on ndokkaama anndude asiri laamu Allah: ammaa ɓe ngonaa nder mum, geɗe ɗee fof ina mbaɗee e misaaluuji. Nde faamee, nelaaɓe am cuɓaama ngam heɓde asiri mawɗo Allah on mo mi feññinani ɓe. Ɓe mbinndi ɗum, mi reenii binndi ɗi ngam on annda goonga haala kaa.

Heewɓe e mooɗon ina keɗoo Yahuudiyankeeɓe yiɗɓe rutttinde on les Tawreeta. Ina weli teskaade wonde so wanaano asiri mawɗo Allah on, heewɓe e mooɗon anndataano Tawreeta; sabu o rokkiraaka leƴƴi jeenti ammaa Isra'iila arandeere ngam ɓe ndeenoo ɗum, o laatoo jannginoowo maɓɓe ngam addude ɓe e am. Sabu miin woni timmoode sariya, ɓe njokkiri sariya Muusaa e weltaare ina njiɗi mawninde ɓanndu mum'en e yahde e peewal mum'en, ɓe paamaali wonde Tawreeta ina deenaa haa timma, haa e alkulal rewrude e kawral mooɗon e am. Sabu ko miin tan gooto waawi deenaade sariya, miin, Iisaa Nasarankeejo, mi timminii ko sariya yiɗi e peewal. Mi tabbintini golle am e ƴiiƴam am, caggal ɗuum mi looti on e ndiyam ruuhu am duurndam nde on lootiraa nder am.

Sabu dume mi wi'i Poola wi'a Korintiyankeeɓe? Sabu e Ruuhu gooto en fof lootiraama nder ɓanndu wooturu, ko en Yahuudiyankeeɓe malla jeenti, ko en maccuɓe malla rimɓe; en fof njariraama nder Ruuhu gooto. So on etake deenaade sariya, on pooccitan, on njuumtan noddaango mooɗon, sabu mi muusini sariya oo nder jamirooje ɗiɗi mawɗe: A yiɗiran Joomiraawo Allah maa e ɓernde maa fof, e ruuhu maa fof, e hakkilantaagal maa fof, e doole maa fof: ndee ko jamiroore arandeere. Nde ɗiɗaɓere ndee nanndi e mayre, hono ndee, A yiɗiran koohooɗo maa no yiɗirtaa hoore maa. Anndee, onon sukaaɓe am, wonde alaa jamirooje ɓurɗe ɗee mawnude, sabu nder ɗee jamirooje ɗee fof ina deenaa.

Faamee: miɗo firta ɗum fof nder ɓatakeeji ɗi mi neli Eklesiyaaji am nder wakkatiiji ɓennuɗi. Nde faamee wonde mi feññinii asiri mawɗo Allah on, annabaaɓe adinooɓe ɓee njiylodii, njiylii ndee asiri nde ɓe anndi ndee, ammaa rokkiraaka ɓe anndude. Mi yiɗuno ko on paama, hay so tawii e dow gal-galal Bibiluuji mooɗon ina winndaa "Ceniingol," cenidaa e hoore mum, sabu ko gal-galal deftere tan. Ko waɗata Bibilu ceniingol ko defte ɗe ɓe kaɓɓata ina njogii Binndi Ceniiɗi ɗi ruuhu neldi sukaaɓe Ɓurɗo Toowde oo, ɗi Yahwe, Allah mawɗo duumotooɗo oo neldi, sabu alaa nannduɗo e makko. Nde nanee konnguɗi am ceniiɗi e ruuhu mooɗon fof! Sabu dume Piyeer wi'i ngam mooɗon, onon sukaaɓe am? Kisndam kam, annabaaɓe njiylodii, njiylii haa laaɓi, ɓe annabii e dow moƴƴere nde foti arde e mooɗon, ina njiylina ko honno, malla wakkati honno Ruuhu Almasiihu wonɗo nder mum'en siftorii, nde o seedii ko adii e dow torra Almasiihu, e teddungal ngal foti rewrude heen.

Onon sukaaɓe am, onon tawa anndaa, on nguuri ko e roŋkuru teddungal ngal foti rewrude, ndonu mooɗon timmungu ina foti feññineede on nder am, Iisaa Almasiihu, sabu ɓooyataa on njaɓtete, on teddiniree haa timma. Nde dume woni ndee asiri? To ngonnduɗon ɗum? Miɗo wi'an on, sabu miin woni Joomiraawo Iisaa Almasiihu, duroowo durngol, miin woni jom resraaɗo am, onon nguuri ko e asiri mawɗo Allah on, hay so tawii heewɓe e mooɗon ina njaha e niɓre e dow ndee haala. Wota on kule, miɗo rokka on faamu, tedduɓe e yankinaaɓe miɗo rokka ɓe hakkilantaagal. Ammaa ɓe ngoni hakkunde mooɗon ɓe njaɓataa konnguɗi am, on mbaɗata ɗum ko luutndiiɗo am; on ngoni ko e am malla luutndiiɗo am, alaa hakkunde. Sabu miɗo wasiyoo on fof janngude e am, nanee konnguɗi am, wota on njaɓtu daande am, sabu mi rokkii on ko arata e anniya nder am. Miin woni Duroowo Moƴƴo oo: duroowo moƴƴo oo ina rokka nguurndam mum ngam baali, baali am ina nana daande am, ndee accitee tokkude gollotooɓe njoɓdi njumitiiɓe wonde ko miin!

Ndaa! Ruuhu on wi'i: Ina woodi asiraaji keewɗi nder Asiri Mawɗo Allah on! Miɗo fuɗɗora Asiri Mawɗo on, ndeen mi haala asiraaji goɗɗi heen. Ko adii nde njahaten yeeso, ittee niɓere jannde jamaanuuji e gite mooɗon, udditee gite mooɗon ngam yiyde: Linjiilaaji no anndirɗon ɗi, golle Matiyu, Markus, Luuka, e Yuhanna, ina njogii nafoore mawnde e yimɓe fof. Ɗee defte ina kolla timminannde am e sariya haa e alkulal, alaa ko heddii. Ina feññina mi nder arande am to Isra'iila. E ɗoftaare Linjiilaaji nayi ɗi no anndiraa, on janngan yahde no njahannoo-mi, sabu noon on njamiraama. Kono non, wanaa ɗi tan ngoni Linjiilaaji; aa'a, wanaa ɗi tan ngoni kabaaru moƴƴo dow am.

Sabu mi rokkii kabaaru moƴƴo Asiri Mawɗo Allah on Poola Nelaaɗo nder ɓatakeeji makko to Eklesiya, ɗi neliraa e am to makko ngam feññinde on. On njalnan ndee miijo? On miijii miɗo waajoo linjiila goɗɗo, miin ko mi daande fenaande? On aadii tokkude daande gollotooɓe njoɓdi haa on annditaali daande am? Ɓe njalnataa ɓee, fota ko on keɗoo daande Galatiyankeeɓe nde Poola wi'i: Ammaa miɗo seedanoo on, onon miñiraaɓe, wonde Linjiila ka mi waajii kaa, iwdaa e neɗɗo. Sabu mi heɓaali ka e neɗɗo, mi jannginaaka ka, ammaa ko e feññinande Iisaa Almasiihu. Poola haalii goonga mo mi rokki mo e koolondiral, ammaa heewɓe e mooɗon ina njaɓta konnguɗi ɗi mi rokki mo, haa e ustude konnguɗi ɗi e konnguɗi hono: Oo, ɗum ko ko Poola winndi tan, malla, ɗi ko ɓatakeeji Poola tan; wanaa konnguɗi Almasiihu sabu ɗi mboɗeewaa! Aan puuyɗo! A majjii; uddit gite maa e annoora am e goonga am! Mi rokki Poola linjiila asiri on. On puuyɗi haa ngoonɗinon wonde defte nay tan, pamare, njogii Linjiila am?

Njaafodii, nanee konnguɗi Iisaa Almasiihu, onon sukaaɓe am, sabu miɗo janngina yiɗɓe ɓee. Sabu Asiri Dewal on e linjiila wonɗo heen ina mawni, sabu mi anndinii kabaaru moƴƴo asiri on. Ammaa mi accataa on e daande seedo gooto; yo konngol kala tabit e hunuko seedeeɓe ɗiɗo malla tato. Sabu ɓataake mo mi neli Roomnaaɓe yiɗi haaldude e mooɗon: Jooni, kaŋko baawɗo tabitinde on e fawaade e linjiila am, e waaju Iisaa Almasiihu, e fawaade e feññinande asiri, ka suuɗanoo gila aduna fuɗɗii. Kabaaru moƴƴo Poola ko feññinande asiri ka suuɗanoo gila aduna fuɗɗii, no Matiyu, Markus, Luuka, e Yuhanna ngoni kabaaru moƴƴo arande am to Isra'iila.

Miin ko mi Joomiraawo koolaaɗo, konnguɗi am ko goonga, ndee hokkam noppi maa e teeŋti e ɓernde maa, sabu e ɓernde neɗɗo goonɗinta haa heɓa peewal. Miɗo ñaagoo on jogorɗon tan goonɗinal nandingal e gabayel mutar, sabu ngel gabayel mawnan sanne e mooɗon so on reentii no haanirta. Sabu dume Poola wi'i Korintiyankeeɓe: Ammaa en kaalata ko hakkilantaagal Allah e nder asiri, kadi hakkilantaagal suuɗaangal ngal Allah yamiri ko adii aduna, ngam teddungal meeɗen. Ngal hay laamɓe ndee aduna anndaa: sabu so ɓe annduno, ɓe ndonkano Joomiraawo teddungal. Ina winndaa laaɓɗum, onon sukaaɓe am, haanaani firto, ammaa miɗo firtan seeɗa.

On paamii ɗii konnguɗi, onon sukaaɓe am? On paamii no teddirɗon haa Allah jogii gite mum e dow mooɗon gila ko adii tagaa aduna, no o duumotoo, noon onon kadi on duumotoo, sabu on ngonii e hakkilantaagal makko gila ko adii wakkati, o rokkii on nguurndam duumotooɗam nder am, Iisaa Almasiihu? Wakkatiiji mooɗon e nguurndam mooɗon ko Yahwe yamiri ɗi, Allah mawɗo, kaayeefinaaɗo, mo hay ɓanndu waawataa yiyde annoora mum. So Seyɗaane annduno peeje Baaba oo, o donkano kam, sabu e ɗoftaare am haa maayde e ummital am timminiraa baawɗe foolɗe mo, o halkaama haa laaɓi. O yiɗataa hay wakkati gooto Eklesiya doolnuɗo, baawɗo e ruuhu am, mo mi waɗata golle mawɗe e juuɗe mum.

Ɗum ina naata on? Accitee jiiɓru hakkilantaagal mooɗon sabu nelaaɓe am ina mbi'a on huunde nde on njiɗaa nande, foolon mawnikinaare mooɗon, sabu mi ardotoo ko yankinaaɓe tan, mi haɓan e mawnikinaaɓe. Nanee konnguɗi am, accitee ɗi naata nder lugge ruuhuuji mooɗon; sabu mi tagirii on nder am, onon ko on doolnuɓe, onon ko on kaayeefiiji; sabu on laɓɓinaama e ƴiiƴam am, kadi ƴiiƴam baalel ngel. Sabu miin woni hoore Ɓanndu am waɗoowo golle mawɗe keewɗe ngam yimɓe am ɓe miɗo jokki noddude, njokkee tan Joomiraawo mooɗon, Yesu Almasiihu, e ɓernde wootere e anniya gooto, sabu miin ko mi Joomiraawo Joomiraaɓe.

Seediiɗo goongaajo on, arano jibinaaɗo e maayɓe on, ina haala e seediiɓe mum suɓaaɓe! Wonnoo ko eklesiya qaddiimijo to Efesus, kamɓe nde sirlle Mawɗe Alla feeñananoo e nder ɓataake mo Pool neldi ummoraade e am, sabu o haalaani ko e hoore makko. Miɗo faamnude seeɗa, kono anndee miɗo ñaaŋii ɗooftaare mon e gooŋɗinal e am, Iisaa Nasaraankeejo on, e mo keewal rokkaa, keewal ina ɗaɓɓiree e mum. Tawi mi yahraani yeeso, miɗo yiɗi miijitoɗon ko rewi heen ummoraade e Kolose, sabu mi neldii kadi ɓataake to Koloseyankooɓe: Sabu on maayii, nguurndam mon ina suuɗii e Almasiihu e nder Alla. Nde Almasiihu, woni nguurndam men, feeñoyi, onon kadi on peeñdoyat e makko e nder teddungal.

Ɓiɓɓe am, on maayii nde mi maayi ndee, onon kadi on maayay e ko'e mon ñalnde kala so on limo jikku mon kiɗɗo oo ko maayɗo; sabu onon e am fof ndigaama, alaa nokku goɗɗo e nder binndi mo njiytoton heen natal goongaagal mon, sabu alaa feccere Linjiila woɗnde feññini ɗum so wonaa Linjiila Siri Mawɗo mo mi feññinani Eklesiya am. Hay so tawii on njiyrataa ko e daandeere niɓɓunde, ɗum ɓooyataa wayleede, on nganndoyat no on nganndiraa kadi. Muccinee e Linjiila Siri Mawɗo Alla, sabu eklesiya am Lawdikiya oo majjinii sirlle am, sabu ko ɓuri heewde e mon meeɗaa janngude ɓataakeeji am neldaaɗi to Eklesiya sabu aynaaɓe woofaɓe njannginii on nokkuuji goɗɗi on na njiilotoo konnguɗi boɗeeji. Ɓiɓɓe am, miɗo anndi on njiɗii kam, njiɗee kam noon e anndal, wotee njaha e laawol njiilam.

Miɗo naamnoo on, hol ko ɓataake neldaaɗo to Efesusankooɓe oo haalata? Sabu ndarii, siri mawɗo oo woni toon! Gila o anndini en siri muuyɗe makko, e dow wellitaare makko, nde o anniyinoo e hoore makko ndee, Nde yontaaji timmaali ɗii njottotoo, o mooɓondira e gootal kala ko woni e nder Almasiihu, ko woni e asamaan e ko woni e leydi, hay e nder makko. Mi anndinii on siri muuyɗe Yahweh, mo o suuɗnoo e hoore makko, sabu o soahtaake wasiyee. Wotee miijo taƴre fenaande nde njannguɗon e annabaaɓe fenaande e nder binndanɗe mum'en ɗe ngalaa nafoore jowitiiɗe e yontaaji, sabu Seytaane ina wayla miijooji binndi e suura ngam hawrude e muuyɗe mum e peeje mum ɗe njahdataa.

Siri oo rokkaama Pool, ko kanko woni joganiiɗo halfinaaɗo sarde ɗum to Eklesiya am, hay e mon so on njogii noppi nanirɗi e gite jiyrɗi. Sabu Linjiila Siri Mawɗo oo rokkaama Pool, mi halfini mo e laawol Damas. Nde yontaaji timmaali ɗii njottotoo, Baaba oo ina mooɓondira kala ko woni e asamaan e ko woni e leydi e nder am, Almasiihu Iisaa. Bölöŋnge ronkat wonde, seerndere hakkunde Alla e ɓiɓɓe mum ronkat wonde, añorgal hakkunde Yahuudankeeɓe e Jentilnaaɓe ronkat wonde, sabu on fof on njibinaama kadi, on ngonti tagu hesu e nder am, Almasiihu Iisaa. Mbele konnguɗi ɗii ina njiɗɗii e mon? Mbele konnguɗi am ina njikki on e mum'en sabu on ngoonɗinii firo aynaaɓe woofaɓe e mum'en?

Njanngee e am e nder deeƴere mbele mbaawon faamde. Kadi, e nder Efesusankooɓe, Pool winndii konnguɗi ɗi mi rokkunoo mo: Sabu ngol dalil miin Pool, kaso Iisaa Almasiihu sabu mon Jentilnaaɓe, So on nanii e dokkal moƴƴere Alla, ngal mi rokkaa sabu mon: No o anndiniri mi siri oo e wahayu. Kadi, woto njaɓee konnguɗi annabaaɓe fenaande mbelsinde on, sabu dokkal moƴƴere Alla ko golle makko e Eklesiya, sabu kanko (e woɓɓe) cuɓaama ngam wonde joganiiɓe sirlle Alla.

E kadi: Ko e jamaanuuji goɗɗi anndinaaka ɓiɓɓe Aadama, no feññiniraa jooni Annabaaɓe makko ceniiɗi e Ruuhu, Yo Jentilnaaɓe ngontu ronooɓe wondude, e ɓanndu wooturu, e tawtoraaɓe fodoore makko e nder Almasiihu, e Linjiila. Eey, njanngee ɗum no haanirta nii, njanngitee kadi: Jentilnaaɓe goonɗinɓe innde am ceniiɗo, hay Iisaa Almasiihu, ko ronooɓe wondude e terɗe ɓanndu wooturu hono Isra'iilaaɓe mbaɗɓe mi Joomiraawo, sabu alaa ceeral e am.

E kadi: Hay siri suuɗanooɗo gila e jamaanuuji, e jiilaaji, kono jooni feññinaama ceniiɓe mum, To ɓe Alla yiɗnoo anndinde holi ngaluuji teddungal ngol siri oo e nder Jentilnaaɓe, woni Almasiihu e mon, koolaare teddungal. Eey, on njibinaama kadi e ruuhu am, ruuhu am ina hoɗi e ɓiɓɓe am haa abada, sabu ko ruuhu ɓiyankaagal, waɗoore ndonu mon, oo ruuhu ina jogii baawɗe ɗe mi rokkunoɗon fof, sabu mi rokkii dokke to yimɓe. On mbaɗaama e sifa am, on tagaama kesum e nder am; sabu Baaba oo jibinii on e am, sabu miin ko mi ruuhu wuurninɗo nguurndam.

Almasiihu Iisaa ɓamtaaɗo oo wiyi: Ɓiɓɓe am pamarɓe, keɓee deeƴere mawnde e am, sabu kisal am, so keɓaama e waɗde mi Joomiraawo mon e gooŋɗinal e tuubugol, waawaa majjeede walla itteede; hol e mon baawɗo ittude tagngo jikkuuji mon e ɓiɓɓe mon, hay so tawii ɓe mbaawi majjude ndonu so on ndiiwanike ɓe. Kisal ko dokkal dokkaangal e Baaba am rewrude e am, Yahuuda Almasiihu, dokke e noddaango Alla ngalaa tuubugol. Jooni, miɗo rokka on buudi ɗi njoɓɓoton e laawol mon faade e sirlle gonɗe e nder Siri Mawɗo Alla, njanngee haa mbantoton e Alla, golloowo Konngol, feccooɗo konngol goonga no haanirta nii, mbele njippotaako yeeso am e wiyngo am.

Woni siri e nder Roomankooɓe, jowitiiɗo e wumre Isra'iila feccere. Woni siri mooɓondiral jommbaajo am, mo won wiyooɓe naɓngo, oo woni e nder Korintiyankooɓe (e nokkuuji goɗɗi); sabu e nder jipyere gite, buutol cakkitol ngol foonetee, foonngo maggo ina woni e mon. Ndeen woni siri gorko e debbo laataade ɓanndu wooturu, oo ko siri mawɗo: kono miɗo haala ko fayti e Almasiihu e eklesiya, on mbaawi yiytude ɗum e Efesusankooɓe. Sabu mi ittay wumre e Isra'iila so ɓe ngartirii ɓerɗe maɓɓe e am. Sabu mi mooɓondiray Jommbaajo am e am, sabu ko miin woni Jom suudu. Ko miin woni mo reseteɗon, mbele en ngonta gootal haa abada e am e Baaba am, sabu Alla ko fof e nder fof. Ɓernde am ina weyyii e mon, ɓiɓɓe am, ɓooyataa on njiyat mi yeeso e yeeso; woto njuumee, miɗo nodda on e am, jiwiraaɓe am hakkilanteeɓe, miɗo ara e mon ko ɓooyaani, miɗo heñorii yiyde on fof ɗo ngonaten yeeso e yeeso, on ngannda, no on nganndiraa kadi.

Ko miin hatimnii on ngam hisnugol timmungol e doole ruuhu am ceniiɗo.
