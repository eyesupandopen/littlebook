Seedeeji Ɗiɗi: Ekklesiya
E nder Golleeji Yuhanna Baptista
17 Mars, 2024

Leynol ko Joomiraaɗo Woodani!

Leynol e heewngo mum fof ko Joomiraaɗo woodani: Hay so tawii gujjo e jattooɗo o hollitii jeyi mum, o waawataa waɗde so wonaa ko Baaba am hokki juuɗe makko ngam timminde e nder peeje makko laaɓtuɗe ngam hisnude aadee. Wata on hulu mo! O foti jokkude haa saanga gooto, sibu waktu makko arii; kono wata on hulu mo, onon goongɗinɓe am, gooŋɗinɓe; wata on hulu mo! Tawa mi hokkiraani on poolgu e dow makko? Torra Yakuuba wonaa ngam gooŋɗinɓe am, e ɓeen ngarɓe e am e sahaa weytungo maa toowee no feewi haa e nder jooɗorde laamu Alla. Ee, onon yimɓe am gooŋɗinɓe, njoɓdi mawndi mooftanaama on sabu koolniiɗo oo mo kollirduɗon mi e laawol peewal.

Mi waɗii on laamɓe e cernooɓe am e nder Laamu am garoowu nguu e yeeso Baaba amen, ko ɗuum waɗi, ndaree tiiɗɗi, onon ɓiɓɓe am, e nder ɗee simooje cakkitiiɗe e waktu cakkitiiɗo oo. Feeñnde am ina ɓadii, hisnugol mon ina ɓadii no foytugol mon garoowol ngol nii, ko ɗum waɗi ndaree koolniiɗi e yeeso am, anndee wonde ko miin woni semmbe mon e hisnugol mon, onon kadi on maŋŋaama e am, sibu mi soodirii on ƴiiƴam am nde mi hokki nguurndam am ngam mon, mbele on mbaawa renndinde e giɗli e Baaba amen to kammu. Miɗo yiɗi ñalnde, Baaba amen kadi ina yiɗi ñalnde, nde njooɗotoɗon e yeeso makko e nder ɓalli ɗi mi joganii on ɗii e nder hisnugol mon timmungal, kuddi alaa e dow cuɓaaɓe am.

Ee, aadee, hol an jom tiiɗal ngam wiyde aɗa jey e ndonu am? Anndaali a soodirii leydi ndii e ko woni e mayri fof ƴiiƴam am laaɓɗam ɗam alaa ella? Leydi ndii e heewngo mayri fof ko Joomiraaɗo woodani, peewal ina ara e leydi ndii! Hay so tawii aɗa yiɗi bonnude leydi ndii, a waɗa nde hoɗorde meere ngam fenaande mon anndeteende, hay so tawii aɗa woondoo wonde aɗa wonana gaño am duumotooɗo, aɗa ɗaɓɓa torra maa ngam heɓde nafa aduna, a faamaani wonde aɗa haɓa e ruuhuuji maa nde wonno aɗa moofta tikki ngam ñalawma tikki, ñalnde mi yoɓata neɗɗo kala no golle mum njiidirta.

Ee, aadee jiibiiɗo, aɗa wiya hoore maa wonde laamɓe maa maa hisnu ma, kono ɓe ngalaa njoɓdi ngam maa, aɗa woni e fenaande, aɗa woni e fenande woɗɓe e nder jiiɓru maa. Laamɓe maa kaaƴe ɗii njaawataa haalde; tooruuji maa e nder ɓerɗe maa njaawataa nande; ɓe njaawataa hisnude ma, an tooriiɗo! So a jibinaaka ko ɓuri ma, sabu tikki Alla ina heddii e maa sabu laabi maa tiiɗɗi ɗii. Hay so hunuko maa wonti yanaande udditiinde, ina tuuta geɗe mawɗe e Joomiraawo Joomiraaɓe, konnguɗi maa maa ngartu e maa, yiɗde maa bonnde maa waylitee e dow maa e nder torra maa duumotoorka garoorka. Ee, so a artuno e am, a jaɓuno hisnugol ummoraade e ngonka maa ngoo toɓɓere. Ee, so a tan sennduno innde am e dow leppi maa fenaande. Kono alaa, a yiɗii fenaande maa, a yiɗii jiiɓru maa, a yeeyii ruuhuuji maa to torra duumotooɗo ngam heɓde mburu mo waawaa rokkude ma; sabu ko miin woni mburu nguurndam! Musiiba maa maa ari e dow maa e tampere, tawa alaa safaara. Mi senndat leñol maa; mi bonnat luumo maa e faggudu maa; e nder waktu gooto mi ittat jawdi maa; goonga, mi ittat weltaare maa fof e nder waktu gooto, baasal maa e torra maa maa njaltu ma. Tikki Jippiiɗo ina ɓadii, ñalnde mbattoore am ndee, nde Baaba am hokki mi ndee, maa huutoree, sabu mi fotii udditde defte ɗee, mi itta maŋngooje ɗee.

On njiyrii e majjere munyal am e muñal am ko laafu e yejjiteede. On mbi’ii e ɓerɗe mon: Hol to Joomiraawo woni? O wonaa so wonaa taali ɓooyɗi. Min njanngii ko ɓuri laabi hisnugol Joomiraawo; e anndal am, mi ɓuri, sabu karallaagal am ina ɓuri semmbe deftere ɓooynde jiiɓru e taali. Goonga, heewɓe e mon ngoongɗinaani hay so tawii mi arii e fuɗɗoode, mi loowii ƴiiƴam am hisnugol ngam bakkaatuuji e hisnugol aadee. So on njibinaaka ko ɓuri on, sabu tikki ngol mooftuɗon e dow ruuhu mon maa ari e dow mon tawa alaa safaara, on anndat no feewi bakkaatuuji mon, torra mon e mbonniigu mon timmungu maa wonan on dewgal. On njaat e coondi mon, laabi mon coondi ɗii maa ngondu e mon haa abada, nde tawnoo on ubbiraama e leeɗe tikki e nder golle mon bonɗe ɗe mbaɗɗon e bonannde mawnde e innde am. Njaltiree e am; mi anndaa on!

Hol e mon mo haaldata e innde am e fenaande? On mbi’a on anndi mi, on ndariima e am; won e mon cikki hay so tawii on mbaawi yamirde mi waɗde ko njiɗɗon, on ngadda nafa kaɓirɗe e mon e golle mon moƴƴe, ɗe ngonaa so wonaa comci coondi. No mbaawirton haalde e innde am tawa on anndaa mi? No mbaawirton haalde e innde am tawa mi hokkiraani on konnguɗi? No mbaawirton haalde e yimɓe am e innde am tawa mi neldaani on? Alaa, on neldii koye mon, on yeeyata marsandiis mon bonɗo ngam heɓde nafa bonɗo, e ngam yettoore yimɓe nde njiɗɗon no feewi. An jiibiiɗo! Yaltu e am, sibu mi anndaano ma hay abada!

Onon annabaaɓe fenaande, ɓe mbiyata mi Joomiraawo e innde tan; onon annabaaɓe fenaande, ɓe kaalata fenaande mawnde e dow ruuhuuji mon; on njaltinay no feewi, mi tuutay on e hunuko am. Ko ɗuum waɗi, tuubee! Ngartiree e am, mi artay e mon. Njaltiree e tooruuji mon, njaltiree e diineeji mon njiɗaaɗi, ngaree e am, mbaɗee mi Joomiraawo mon! Ee, so on njaɓuno hisnugol am; kono on njokkii e bakkaatuuji mon, on njaɓii aybeeji mon nde wonnoo on njanngata Konngol am laaɓngol ummoraade e ɓernde meere, ɓernde heewnde jeeno e njiyaaagu nde ndogaton e tooruuji mon. Hol hisnata on e tikki am nde tawnoo on njiilanii yimɓe am ngam heɓde nafa mon bonɗo? So on mbaɗuno mi Joomiraawo no leppi mon fenaande mbiyri nii, mi hisnunoo on e bakkaatuuji mon, mi waɗa on timmuɓe e am. Mi reennoo torraaji ɗi bonɗo oo waɗata e mon. Mi hokkunoo on laawol e faandaare, mi nelda on e leydi ndii e hunduko heewngo barkeeji e goonga nde tawnoo on ardotoo yimɓe am haa e am.

Wata on cikki miɗo haajaa e ballal mon, sibu ko miin woni Joomiraawo goongaajo kammu e leydi, ko miin woni Laamɗo Laamɓe e Joomiraawo Joomiraaɓe. Ɓeen ɓe ndokki mi e golle ina njogii teddungal mawngal haalde e innde am, reena gese am, teddungal ngal mi yoɓrata e jawdi duumotoondi; kono, on mbaylitii mi e natal Almasiihu fenaande. On mbaɗanii koye mon Iisaa goɗɗo; on njiɗii ruuhu gaño Almasiihu, on mbaɗii bakkaat duumotooɗo e dow ruuhuuji mon nde mbiyɗon mi alaa e Eklesiya am mo soodirmi e ƴiiƴam am, bakkaat mo mbaawataa heɓtude. Goonga, kala ɗee geɗe e ko ɓuri ɗum maa ngar e dow mon e nder tikki am so wonaa on njuulnii hoore mon e yeeso am, on mbaɗa mi Joomiraawo goonga e tuubugol e comci coondi e nder ndoondi. Wata on nanndin konnguɗi am nde wonnoo on pija e haala am! Tuubee! Njooɗee e comci coondi e nder ndoondi, mboylee e dow bonanndeeji mon, mi jaɓan on e am, mi artiran on e njoɓdi mon nde mi rokkata goɗɗo.

Ko miin woni Joomiraawo peewɗo.

Ko miin woni hisnoowo aduna, hay Laamɗo Laamɓe e Joomiraawo Joomiraaɓe.

Ko miin woni mo yahrata e nder lampaaji jeeɗiɗi ɗii, mo yamirta malaa'ikaaji jeeɗiɗi eklesiyaaji jeeɗiɗi ɗii.

Ko miin woni jippiiɗo mo hirsanoo gila e sosngo aduna, e am woni nguurndam duumotooɗam.

Miɗo jogii coɓɓuli maayde e jahannama, Baaba am kadi renndinii jooɗorde mum e am, mbele miɗo waawa renndinde jooɗorde am e gooŋɗinɓe am, noddaaɓe, cuɓaaɓe. Miɗo yahda e yimɓe am, miɗo haalda e yimɓe am, ko miin woni gaynaako goongaajo, gaynoowo gese ɗe Baaba am hokki mi. Onon woni ɓiɗɗo yiitere am, onon gooŋɗinɓe am, onon kadi woni ɓiɗɗo yiitere Baaba am, ngam oo sahaa e oo waktu on ngartiraama e peewal e laaɓal goonga.

Waktu ina ɓadii, on poti woppude golle mon, on ndaara feeñnde am, sibu ina foti arde e aduna oo nde mooftumi on ummoraade e nokkuuji nayi leydi ndii. Goonga, goonga, mi mooftay baali am goongaaji e nder suudu am hade duule garooje bonnude oo aduna bonɗo, sabu laamu Seytaane maa sennde, bonnee no feewi ngam waɗde laawol Joomiraawo peewɗo mo laamotoo leydi ndii ummoraade e jooɗorde Daawuda e peewal e laaɓal goonga, mi semmbinay no feewi dewgal am e nder laamu garoowu nguu, sibu ko ɓe laamɓe am e cernooɓe am, ɓe laamotoo e peewal goonga e laaɓal, sibu njoɓdi am ina woni e juuɗe am ngam gooŋɗinɓe.

Ko miin woni semmbe mon e hisnugol mon, onon kadi on maŋŋaama e am.

Ko miin woni mburu nguurndam!

Mi fotii udditde defte ɗee, mi itta maŋngooje ɗee.

Ko miin woni joomiraawo goongaajo kammu e leydi.

Ko miin woni laamɗo laamɓe.

Ko miin woni Joomiraawo peewɗo.

Ko miin woni hisnoowo aduna, Laamɗo Laamɓe e Joomiraawo Joomiraaɓe.

Ko miin woni mo yahrata e nder lampaaji jeeɗiɗi ɗii, mo yamirta malaa'ikaaji jeeɗiɗi eklesiyaaji jeeɗiɗi ɗii.

Ko miin woni jippiiɗo mo hirsanoo gila e sosngo aduna.

Ko miin woni gaynaako goongaajo gaynoowo gese ɗe Baaba am hokki mi.
