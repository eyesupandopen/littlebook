Gaynaako Goongaajo oo Wiyi: On Nanndi e Baali Majjuɗi!

Onon yimɓe am, on fof on nanndi e baali majjuɗi, kala e mooɗon waylitii e laabi mum, laabi ɗi mi waɗanaani on, kono on rewata ko laabi annabaaɓe fenaaɓe ɓe fena, ɓe mbolta e innde am ceniinde, ɓe njaltataa tawa alaa ko heɓi ɓe. Mi habri on no feewi, yimɓe am, mi noddii kammu e leydi ngam seedaade konnguɗi am: Woto miijoree konnguɗi annabaagal ɗi miin, Joomiraaɗo Iisaa Almasiihu, hokki on, ko huunde hersiniinde; kono, laaɓtinon kala konngol ngol mi hokki. So on calii, ko bakkaat wonan on.

Mi haalan on gila mi yahdaani yeeso, heewɓe e mooɗon maa cuppu noppi mum en, maa cuɓɓita gite mum en e konnguɗi ɗi njogo-mi haalde ɗii, mi wasii on woto kulɗee. Heewɓe e mooɗon maa ndullu e tiiɗnaare sehilaaɓe e ɓesnguuji, tiiɗnaare eklesiyaaji fenaaɗi e juɓɓule aduna, on cikka jaŋde toownde ina lomtoo mi tawi jaŋde adunaare ko ganndal Sayɗaan fenaangal, ganndal makko moƴƴal e bonngal fof ina adda maayde, sabu noon o feewniri konnguɗi makko ngam waɗde ɗum. Woto jeyeɗee e ɓeen salotooɓe konnguɗi am, kono kettinoree ɓernde mooɗon fof, ciftoroo ko mi haalata, sabu konnguɗi am ko nguurndam, maa ɗi laaɓtin noddaango mooɗon e suɓaango mooɗon.

Hol to e nder Deftere am ceniinde njiy-ɗon wonde ina woodi huunde wiyeteende Siyonism Kerecee’en? On paamaani wonde ardiiɓe mum en ko suudu dewal Sayɗaan? Eey, ina woodi naatɓe e Isra'iila tawa wonaa Isra'iilanaaɓe, ɓe njeyaa ko e suudu dewal Sayɗaan, kamɓe ɓe fena, ɓe mbiya ko Yahuudiyankooɓe tawi wonaa! Onon, Eklesiya am, ko onon ngoni Isra'iila Alla, on wonaa lomto, ɗum wonaa diine lomto, kono ko Deftere am ceniinde. Ndaaree, njiyee e koye mooɗon! Alaa! Mbiya-mi bonannde e yimɓe am, hay so tawii noon ina woodi hakkilanteeɓe e koye mum en hakkunde mooɗon ɓe kaalata noon.

Laaɓtinee iwdi am, mi jibinaa ko e baabiraaɓe ɓee, iwdi am ko laaɓndi; so tawii hay gooto e mooɗon ina wondi e yiɗde Isra'iila, miin mi ɓuri ɗum, sabu mi hokkaama nguurndam am ngam maɓɓe! Hol ko woni yumma am e miñiraaɓe am? Kala baɗoowo faale Alla ko kañum woni yumma am e miñiraaɓe am, on njanngaani naa? Miɗo seerndan on: moƴƴal e bonngal, nde tawnoo heewɓe e mooɗon alaa hakkilantaagal ruuhuyankeewal goongaawal, on bumɗinaama e fenaande bonɓe ɓee. On njanngaani wonde Alla ina hebbina laabi mum sahaa e sahaa fof e juuɗe añɓe mum, sabu hay añɓe mum ina njaɓa daande mum? Eey, Isra'iila artiraama e hitaande 1948, miɗo yiɗi heewɓe e leydi ndii, mi hisna ɓe; kono Isra'iila artiraama e juuɗe worɓe bonɓe, ardiiɓe mum en ina njogii anniyaaji niɓɓuɗi. Wonaa onon, ɓiɓɓe am, jogaade juuɗe e ndee feccere e golle Baaba am e oo sahaa, hay so tawii noon maa ndeenɗon e laamu am garoowu; kono ko miin foti hisnude Yakuuba, wonaa onon!

On majjinaama e konnguɗi moƴƴi e haalaaji laaɓɗi, on naati e mboddi laaɓndi no feewi. On mboddaama e ruuhuuji Yezebel, gollotooɗi e laabi Balaam. Anndee mi fiyata nanngere maɓɓe e mooɗon, mi udditata gite mooɗon e noppi mooɗon so on ngartii e am e muñal e yiɗde arde e Deftere am, njanngon e am tawa alaa miijooji mooɗon ɗi njanngu-ɗon e ɗii ruuhuuji fenaaɗi, annabaaɗi rewrude e annabaaɓe mum en fenaaɓe. Ruuhuuji Yezebel ina kaala fenaande mawnde e nder annabaaɓe mum en fenaaɓe, hay maandeeji e kaawniiɗi fenaaɗi, ina annaboo ngam dañal e ruuhu Balaam, ɗiin naati e eklesiyaaji mooɗon, on nyaama tuundi ummoraade e juuɗe maɓɓe.

On njiilaama e suudu dewal Sayɗaan haa njahdondir-ɗon e mum en, suudu dewal Sayɗaan. Wonɓe e Eklesiya am ngoongaani konnguɗi am e sariyaaji am sabu ɓe ngoongɗinii fenaande haalaande e innde am, ɓe ndenndi e bumɗe maɓɓe, ɗum ko potngal. Woto tawaaɗee e maɓɓe so mi arii; mi wiyanaani on naa, njaltee e maɓɓe, ceertuɗon, hol ko hawtini Almasiihu e Beliyal? Lootee loope e gite mooɗon e ƴiiƴam am jamfoton, udditee ɗe ngam yiide annoora goonga mo mi hokkata on, sabu gite mooɗon ina keewi loope, on mbaawaa udditde ɗe tawa mi alaa.

No winndiraa nii, Alla hokkii ɓe ruuhu ɗoyngol, gite ɗe ɓe njiyataa, e noppi ɗe ɓe nanataa haa hannde, e konnguɗi moƴƴi e haalaaji laaɓɗi on naati e bumɗe maɓɓe, wonaa tan bumɗe e anniyaaji am ngam Isra'iila, kono kadi bumɗe e noddaango mooɗon goongaango e am, Almasiihu Iisaa, Joomiraaɗo mooɗon! So on bumɗii, on ndimaani janngude e faamde konnguɗi am, on njahaani e nder ruuhu. Ko ɗum waɗi, rewɗee konnguɗi am, yimɓe am; hay so tawii on anndinooma mi e ɓanndu, e ɓanndu on anndaa mi kadi. Sabu mi waylaama haa laatii ruuhu hokkoowo nguurndam, hokkuɗo ruuhu nguurndam Isra'iila Alla, hay ruuhu ɓiyankaagal baɗoowo on wullaade ABBA, Baaba!

Jooni noon, Joomiraaɗo oo ko oon Ruuhu: ɗo Ruuhu Joomiraaɗo woni, toon ndimaagu woni; ko ɗum waɗi, ndaroree e ndimaagu ngu Almasiihu rimɗini on, woto naatee kadi e joko njiyaagu. Cuftinee noppi mooɗon, ngartinee ɓerɗe mooɗon ngam nande Deftere am, woto calagol ngol! Ruuhu ina rokka nguurndam; ɓanndu alaa nafoore. Konnguɗi ɗi kaal-mi e mooɗon ko ruuhu, ko nguurndam. Ko ɗum waɗi, woto nanndee e Isra'iila ɓanndu, njaltee e fenaande mum politik, kono nanndee e ruuhu am baɗaaɗo e mbaadi Alla!

On paamaani naa? Fenaande maɓɓe ina accaa ngam waɗde nokku ngam ɓiy halkere oo, ngam fof maɓɓe ndaanee ɓe keɓata weltaare e tooñannge, ɓe inɗe mum en mbinndaaka e Deftere Nguurndam gila e fuɗɗoode aduna. Kettinee konnguɗi am cemtinɗi, woto tiiɗnine ɓerɗe mooɗon, sabu konnguɗi am maa kisnu on e musiiba garoowo, maa ceerndu on e haɓtooɓe am e saliiɓe am fotde duuɓi 2000 jooni. Hay so tawii noon bumɗe heɓii feccere e Isra'iila, heddiiɓe e Yakuuba maa ngartu e am, njaɓa nuunɗal ngal ummoraaki e sariya kono e goongɗingol am. Laɓɓinee miijooji mooɗon, njoftee konnguɗi fenaaɗi uulooɓe ɓe mbuuɓnii e noppi mooɗon, hay so tawii ɓe kaala fenaande e innde am, sabu mi neldaani ɓe.

Bonannde mawnde ina woodi e leydi am, mi arat mi hisna. Bonannde mawnde ina woni e Yakuuba sabu ɓe accii hoore maɓɓe mbayleede e fenaaɓe Edom. On ngoongɗinaani habaru am wonde suudu dewal Sayɗaan ina woodi, ko goonga? Anndee ɓe ina njaha hakkunde mooɗon! On paamaani wonde bumɗo waawaa ardude bumɗo, Isra'iila bumɗinaama feccere haa timmal Jentil en naata? Mi hisnat heddiiɓe e Yakuuba, miɗo siilnoo 144 000. Kamɓe ɓe mbaɗanat mi golle mawɗe, mi neldat ɓe ɗoo e ɗoon e nder caɗeele Yakuuba, ɓe kisnat heewɓe, ɓe mbaɗat golle mawɗe keewɗe, mi ardoo ɓe ngam ardude woɗɓe, sabu alaa laawol feewde e Baaba so wonaa rewrude e am, Joomiraaɗo Iisaa Almasiihu, Almasiihu mo ɓe calii oo.

Kono onon, Kerecee’en hannde, onon ɓiɓɓe am ɓuuɓɓe, on cikka on ɓuri anndude sabu on njanngii binndanɗe maɓɓe naatiraaɗe e mooɗon e niɓre. On ngoongɗinii habaru maɓɓe fenaaɗo, habaru fenaaɗo ummoraade e suudu dewal Sayɗaan Edom, diine mahooɓe suudu dewal, halkooɓe ɓe Sayɗaan neldi ngam jogaade aduna. Kamɓe ɓe ardoo leñol ngol ndewoton e nokku Baaba so on rewdii fenaande maɓɓe feewniraande e hakkilantaagal, ɓe noddira hoore maɓɓe yimɓe suɓaaɓe Alla, ɓe mbaɗa on mbarjikee ɓe tawi ɓe halkat on so ɓe keɓii fartaŋŋe. Anndee mi neldaani feere politik, laamuuji aduna ina ngolla ngam Sayɗaan, soodɗo ɗi e njoɓdi tooñannge.

On njanngaani, on paamaani wonde ko miin tan woni feere, miin ko mi laamɗo kaɓoowo, neldaaɗo e Yahweh e hoore mum ngam jogaade laamu e nuunɗal, ngam ittude laamu e doole Sayɗaan fof? Eey, mi fiyata laamu makko haa laaɓa, so tawii artataa, mi fiyata hoore makko fenaande no annabiranoo, o wulirta haa abada e yiite ɓaleeje ɓurɗe wulde, o wuufat e sulufuur wuloowo e nder maayo mawngo ngoo e ñalawma mawɗo oo, o artataa kono o halkete, o wulirta haa abada. Anndee alaa tooñannge e nuunɗal Baaba am, kono ko njoɓdi golle Sayɗaan e yiɗde mum ngam ɓiɓɓe Baaba e tagoore, ko ɗum waɗi Baaba am hokkata mo ko o yiɗi ngam mooɗon e nuunɗal mum.

On njanngaani? Laamɗo oo maa jaabo, wiyana ɓe, Goonga miɗo wiya on, No mbaɗir-ɗon gooto e ɓee miñiraaɓe am ɓurɓe famɗude, ko miin mbaɗir-ɗon. Ko waɗi on rewata ardiiɓe politik fenaaɓe no njahdirton e am? Mi haalan on ko waɗi ngam paamon no feewi: Heewɓe e mooɗon ina rewde fenaande e nder Biibul mooɗon ɗi mi winndaani, mi yamiraani ɓe mooɓti binndanɗe wi’eteeɗe Siyonism Kerecee’en. Alaa huunde wiyeteende Siyonism Kerecee’en, sabu mi wonaa yahuudiyanke ɓurɗo woɗɓe. So tawii ko mi Siyonist, mi waylittaano feewde e Jentil en, leƴƴi ɗii njaɓii mi e sahaa gooto. Alaa, ɓe kuutoroo Siyonism ngam addude ɓiy halkere oo e suudu dewal maɓɓe nde ɓe mahata e majjere e innde Baaba am, ɓe teddinataa Baaba am sabu ɓe calii miin, Iisaa Almasiihu. Wonaa tan mi arii e yimɓe am, mi salaa e yimɓe am, kono heewɓe e maɓɓe ina njaɓa calagol am haa hannde. Kono onon, eklesiya Lawodisiya, on cikka on mbaawa yaawnude timmal annabaagal rewrude e jinnaaɗe maɓɓe; sabu suudu dewal Sayɗaan ina yiɗi aduna mum, aduna mo on alaa, ɓiɓɓe am. Mi hisnaani on e maɓɓe naa?

Anndee Sayɗaan etinooma wujjude ndonu am e am, sabu leydi ko jeyaaɗo Joomiraaɗo e timmal mum. Sayɗaan ina yiɗi aduna mo Alla alaa, aduna rimɗuɗo e Alla, aduna rimɗuɗo e am, kaaƴe mawɗe, e aduna ɗo ɓurɓe semmbe nguurata so ɓe mbonnii leydi ndii haa heddii tan ko iwdi Sayɗaan, nde njeyaa. Isra'iila ina naatiraa gila e duuɓi ujunnaaje jooni e binndanɗe mum en Babilon (eey, ɓe kodifii aadaaji haalaaji) ɗe ñaawoowo am, Joomiraaɗo Iisaa Almasiihu, ɗum waawaa heɓde sabu mi jaɓataa mi saliree. Aduna oo ina heddii faamde tikkere am e doole am, sabu mi ɓuri fof so wonaa Baaba am, waawaa haɗde mi.

Miɗo ñiiɓi e muñal e juutgol ɓernde, mi accii innde am wiyee bonnde, sadaka am jamfoore limtiree ko huunde hersiniinde ngam Teegal am hisnee, Teegal ngal suuɗinoo e sahaa ɓennuɗo e sirlu mawɗo Alla, sirlu mo mi udditiri no feewi e Nelaaɗo Pool. On mbaawi janngude binndanɗe makko ngam faamde, sabu o haalaani e hoore makko, kono o habrii linjiila Sirlu mawɗo Alla mo mi hokki mo ngam udditde ngam am. Hay gooto heɓataa ko woni jeyi am, sabu mi yoɓii ɗum e ƴiiƴam am jamfoore, mi yoɓtat bonɓe ɓee.

Sayɗaan ina yiɗi aduna mo on alaa, ɓiɓɓe am, kono heewɓe e mooɗon ina ndokka jawdi mum en e majjere ngam wallitde suudu dewal Sayɗaan e anniyaaji mum en bonɗi e leydi am e heddiiɓe e leñol Isra'iila, leñol ngol mi laamotoo aduna e jappeere Daawuuda e laamu am garoowu. On mballitii maayde mooɗon, on cikka on ɓeydii linjiila. Ko waɗi on mbaawaa faamde wonde maayde e halkere e laamɓe bonɓe wonaa ummoraade e am kono ko iwdi Sayɗaan; eey, baaba maɓɓe ko Iblis, Baaba Fenaande, Sayɗaan e hoore mum. Anndee o ko gaño foolaaɗo, o huli o anndi balɗe makko ina pamɗi, o huli ko arata ko ɓuri kala, sabu o anndi o heɓataa jam e lepte makko, sabu Alla e hoore mum habrii ɗum! Ɓiɓɓe am, on mbaawaa rewde Sayɗaan e gollande mi. Sabu hay gooto waawaa gollande joomiraaɓe ɗiɗo: maa o wany gooto, o yiɗa goɗɗo; walla o jogoo gooto, o yawa goɗɗo. Ko ɗum waɗi, suɓee mo njahdaton, sabu mi yamiraani Eklesiya am jogaade jokkondiral e ko wiyetee Siyonism Kerecee’en, oon dille wonaa ummoraade e am, kono ko Sayɗaan, omo huutora ɗum ngam addude ɓiy halkere oo. Heewɓe e mooɗon mbelsii maanaa konnguɗi am, on cikka “anti” ina firti tan ko luulndaare tiiɗnde e hay gooto. Alaa, gorko bakkaat oo neldaama e hakkilantaagal e nokku am feewde e yimɓe saliiɓe am, kono mi hisnat Yakuuba e caɗeele mawɗe, mi laɓɓintaa tan leydi am ndonu, kono mi laɓɓintat aduna oo fof e bonannde e bakkaat. Faamee mi hisnat Yakuuba e alkule ɗe Baaba am waɗi e baabiraaɓe mum en, wonaa e teddungal walla e nuunɗal mum en, sabu hay ɓanndu ngoongaani e yeeso am.

Mi wasii on mboppee binndanɗe ɓe kaalataa e innde am, keɓee hakkilantaagal, ɓiɓɓe am; sabu heewɓe e mooɗon ko majjere haa mbaawaa seerndude fenaande e goonga. Sabu on njogoraani mi ko mi hooreejo mooɗon kono on njogorii konnguɗi kaalaaɗi e annabaaɓe fenaaɓe Yezebel, gollotooɓe e laabi Balaam, rewooɓe njoɓdi tooñannge. Sabu Deftere Alla ina yaawi, ina jogii doole, ina ɓuri kala kaafaahi bermbe ɗiɗi, ina naata haa fecca ruuhu e ruuhu, e jokkondire e ƴiye, ina laaɓtina miijooji e anniyaaji ɓernde. Ngartiree e Deftere am laaɓnde, sabu Baaba ina reena nde haa hannde.

Paamee laawol am, yimɓe am, anndee mi. Mi suɓaaki daande wootere ngam wonande dewtere am, mi suɓaaki kadi daande ɓurnde ɓooyde ngam laamaade konnguɗi am e tabitinde goonga majji. Alaa, e laabi Baaba am (Baaba am alaa ko haajani wasiya e peeje mum) e e hakkilantaagal Baaba am o suɓii daaɗe keewɗe ummoriiɗe e jamaanuuji, nokkuuji, e pinalji ceertuɗi, ɗe kawri hakkunde majji. Njeehee e laawol Bizantiin, onon ɓiɓɓe am, woto on cikka e konnguɗi am, cuɓɓitee binndi ɗi mi reeni ɗii, njeeƴee hoore mon e konnguɗi am. Wonaa yamiraaɗon njanngee haa njiyee ko on njaɓaaɓe? Sabu ɓiɓɓe aduna ɓee ina ɓuri ɓiɓɓe annoora hakkilantaagal e jamaanu mum en: Sabu kamɓe ko e bonannde e fenaande ɓe hakkilani, ɓe mbaɗataa goɗɗum so wonaa wujjude, warde, e halkude.

Baaba am reenii konngol mum laaɓngol, ngol ina woni yeeso mon, kono on njaɓataa ngol mbele caɗeele mon ɗe njiɗɗon ɗee ina njaɓee e firooji newiiɗi no feewi, firooji bonɗi ɗi mi nelaani, ɗi mi winndaani. Hol ko waɗi on nduuŋtotoo e ɗee konnguɗi? E nder majjere mon nde njahraton ndee, on paamaani wonde Seytaane ina waɗa ko nanndi e Binndi ceniɗi ɗii. Omo waɗa ɗum no fenaande fenaande nii, omo itta jokkondire e jolokoo gooto gooto haa jolokoo oo ina famɗi haa alaa ko anndi. Wonaa keeri ina njuɓɓinee seeɗa seeɗa? Holo fowler ina waɗa pittirgal e yeeso colli? O waylitii konnguɗi, o itti tonngooɗe e aayeeji timmuɗi, o ɓeydi heen konnguɗi, o waɗi ɗum fof ko e yiɗde hawrude e diineeji kala e nder renndo ngoo ngam hawrude e Almasiihu mum fenaande, mo woni e yaha e mon udditiiɗo, mo yaltata ɓooyataa.

Mi werlotoo kullel ngel e annabiijo fenaande oo ina wuurde e maayo ngo ina huɓɓa e yiite e mbarnaa haa ɓe njogoo ndonu mum en, ndonu ngu Baaba am halfini Ibliis e malaa'ikaaji mum. Sabu o rokkata ɓe aduna mum en e aduna baawɗo huutoraade giɗli mum en bonɗi ɗi ɓe njiɗi e mon, onon ɓiɓɓe am. Wonaa on njanngii walla on njippii wonde pelle gnostik en mbayliima epistolaaji am no ɓe keɓiri ɗum en nii? Aɗa sikka ɗum waawataa waɗde e jamaanu mon, nde ganndal ɓeydii?

Njiɗee konngol am laaɓngol, kooloɗon wonde miɗo waawi rokkude firooɓe nuunɗuɓe. Kooloɗon konnguɗi ɗi mi rokki ɗii; kooloɗon konnguɗi Konngol Alla. Woto on won majjuɓe haa cikkoton wonde kala binndol ina laaɓi; wonaa mi wiyi on njanngee haa njiyee ko on jaɓaaɓe? Kono yimɓe am, so on njanngii, on acca añɓe leggal ngal ina fira konnguɗi am mbele on njaɓataa wonde on njaɓii suudu dewal Seytaane. On njaɓii majjere mon, on njaɓii Seytaane e ɓiɓɓe mum ɓe njippini leyɗeele am ceniɗe, kono tan ko e dumunna gooto, caɗeele Yakuuba ina laɓɓina ɓe e leydi ndii, jeertuɓe ɓee ndonata leydi ndii. Kooloɗon mi, Konngol ngol wonti ɓanndu, ngam rokkude on goonga; goonga ina woni e Binndi am ceniɗi, sabu ko e am kaaldi.

Mbele on njaɓataa konnguɗi am, yimɓe am? Mbele on cuɓotoo ko bonnude nelaaɓe ɓe mi neli ɓee? Sabu mi neliino heewɓe! Heewɓe e mon ina njokki e majjere mum en, tawa hay so tawii ɓe njiɗaani tabitinde konnguɗi ndee annabaagal, ɓe mbaɗata ɗum ko e bonannde mum en e majjere njoɓdi. No ndimaagu nguu ittirtee, njuumri am ndii ina ittee, ɓe njahata e yaawre mawnde ngam warde kala ko woni Kerecee’en. ɓe laɓɓinay diina kerecee’en e dow leydi so ɓe mbaawii, ɓe torra, ɓe ndartina seniiɓe am e laabi keewɗi, ɓe nodda innde Joomiraawo ina hisna.

Ngartiree e am jooni, laɓɓinee ɓerɗe mon e konnguɗi maɓɓe fenaande. On kebbinii konnguɗi maayde e halkaare e hoore mon e nder majjere timmunde e daartol mon. Miɗo yetta on njanngee daartol defte ɗe mi winndaani, ɗe njiɗɗon dow konnguɗi am. Njanngoo hol winndooɓe ɓee tigi rigi, sabu kamɓe ɓe njaltinii fenaande mawnde e defte maɓɓe, sabu on anndirta ɓe ko e ɓiɓɓe maɓɓe. Heewɓe e ɗee defte mbaɗaama e duɗe jaaɓi haaɗtirde mon; njiɗee ɗum, sabu ko goonga! So on njahrii e ɗee geɗe, njogii mi, mi ardotoo on e goonga fof, on paamta haa jooni sirlu Alla mo ngonɗon oo. Kono hay sirlu mawɗo oo ina famɗi e miijooji mon e ɓerɗe mon e fenaande maɓɓe; oo sirlu mawɗo ko neɗɗo gooto keso, ittaaɗo e Yahuuda en e Jananɓe fof, ɗo alaa ko heddii e mahe ceernduɗe, sabu ndeen mahe ina momtaa e am.

Heewɓe e mon ina njokki e aayeeji Tawreeta ittaaɗi e nokkuuji mum en: majjere ina sikka ina foti wallitde warooɓe yimɓe ngam barkinde mi. Miɗo barkina ɓe noddirtee innde am, hay so tawii miɗo waɗa toɓo e dow nuunɗuɓe e bonɓe fof, barkeeji fof ina ngoni e am, Almasiihu Iisaa Joomiraawo mon, Joomiraawo Joomiraaɓe goonga e Laamɗo Laamɓe goonga. Ko ɗum waɗi, ngartee e am e tuubugol, sabu on njaɓii fenaande Yezebel ko goonga Linjiila, on kebbinii tikkere e hoore mon e waɗde ɗum; sabu miɗo rokka hay ɓiɓɓe am e golle mum en, hay so tawii mi ñaaawataa on e aybeeji mon.

Ko mi Jibinaaɗo Alla cuɓaaɗo hade winndere ndee fuɗɗaade.

Ko mi laamɗo konu nelaaɗo e Yahwe.

Ko miin kammuuji poti jaɓɓaade haa jamaanuuji mbayliigu kala.

Ko miin Yuuhanna Baabtisooɗo baabtiisnoo nde ruuhu oo jippii no foondu nii e daande Baaba am.

Ko miin gartuɗo e yimɓe am, jaɓaaka, neɗɗo mette, yoɓɗo njoɓdi ɓurndi toowde e mon fof.

Mi ɓuri kala so wonaa Baaba am, Yahweh.

Ko miin woni Yeshua HaMashiach, anndiraaɗo kadi Iisaa Almasiihu, e ɗemngal Gerek ιησου χριστου e ɗemɗe goɗɗe keewɗe, ɗe fof ina anndina mi ko mi Iisaa Almasiihu walla Almasiihu Iisaa, sabu ko mi Joomiraawo!

Ɓooyataa on njiyata mi yeeso e yeeso, jommbaajo am, on annda no on anndiraa nii. Ko ɗum waɗi miɗo nodda on e am; woto on ndarto e konnguɗi am, njeeɓee daande am, sabu nguurndam e hisde ina tawaa e ɗoftiiɓe.
