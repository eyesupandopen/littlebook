Seedeeji ɗiɗi: Eklesiya
E nder golle Poola Nelaaɗo
02 Mee, 2024

Mi sellinan Goongɗinal mon, wiyi Joomiraaɗo Mawɗo!

Ndaaree! Miin, aan am, Almasiihu Iisaa, Joomiraaɗo ummitiiɗo, kisnoowo aduna, miɗo jogii naamnal ngam mooɗon, jommbaajo am. Mbele a jaabanan am goonga? Mbele a waawan hokkude mi jaabowol fotowol e binndol goonga ngol? Nii woni, mi naamnoto on: So Ɓii neɗɗo arii, on cikki, mbele mi tawanan goongɗinal e leydi? Ina laaɓi, ɗum ko naamnal ngal jaabowol mum anndaa, kono so tawii ko e noddaango am njooɗotoɗon ngam jaabaade, hol ko wonnoo jaabowol mon? Mbele a haalananam goongɗinal maa e no ngal woniri kaawis? Mbele a haalananam miijooji maa moƴƴi ɗi njiɗirtaa e majjere maa laamaade aduna e miijooji maa? Mbele on paamaani wonde hay kuule ɓurɗe huuɓtodinde e goongɗinal am, hay goongɗinal Iisaa Almasiihu, hay kuule ɓurɗe huuɓtodinde ɗee fof mbayliima, ciforaama no fenannde? Kono onon e majjere mon on mbi’a: "Oo, Alla meeɗataa accude ɗum waɗa!" E ɗuum, miɗo naamno on, hol no nganndirɗon Alla? Mbele on woni wasiyaŋkooɓe makko? Mbele on njooɗoto ngam yamirde Yahwe? Mbele on njooɗoto ngam yamirde mi, Yeshua HaMashiach, waɗde ko njiɗɗon? Kono heewɓe e mooɗon e majjere mum en ina etoo waɗde noon, sabu on njiilaama e fenaande mawnde sanne.

On accii annabaaɓe fenaannde, hay huɗo bonngo ngo Seytaane aawi e eklesiyaaji mum fenaande (ɗi ngonaa e am), annabaaɓe fenaande ɓeen ɓe miñi am Yahuuda haali no mi welnirnoo konnguɗi am e nder ɓernde mum e no mi yiiwirnoo ruuhu mum e nder mum e welo welo ceniingo: Bone maɓɓe, sabu ɓe njaarii e laawol Kayiina, ɓe njaawi e dow yiɗde ngam heɓde njoɓdi Balaam, ɓe halki e salaare Korah. Ɓeen fenaannde ina njaha e yeeso mon, heewɓe e mooɗon ina njokki ɓe, ina cikkatnoo ko miin njokkoton, sabu ɓe ko fenaannde, ɓe njetoo kadi daande am mbele baali am ɗi nganndaa ɗii ina mbaawi rewrude e maɓɓe haa naata e majjere mawnde, hono no ɓe ngoriri nii. Hol ko Yahuuda haali kadi e ɓeen ɓe ngalaa nafoore? Ɓeen ko boddereeji e nder ñalɗi mon sadaka, so ɓe njuurdiima e mon, ina ñammbina koye mum en tawa kuleeki alaa: ko duule ɗe ndiyam alaa, ɗe henndu yahrata, leɗɗe ɗe ɓiɓɓe mum en njooli, tawa ɓiɓɓe alaa, ɗe maayi laabi ɗiɗi, ɗe pocii e ɗaɗi. Ko falooje maayo, ina njuppa koye mum en e hersa, ko koode njiilotooɗe, ɗe niɓere niɓere ndesndanaa haa abada.

Miɗo anndi fenaande ndee ina welna yoga e mooɗon, sabu yoga e mooɗon nganndaa mi goonga kono ina njiɗi rewde Yahwe e rewrude e am, Joomiraaɗo mon Iisaa Almasiihu, rewrude e kuule linjiila risku. Woto njiilno: Linjiila risku ko fenaande, mi meeɗaa hokkude hoore am no Jawmu mon Paska nii mbele on mbaawa renndinde jawdi mon: Sabu yiɗde kaalis ko ɗaɗol kala bone: ko yoga e mum en njiɗnoo, ɓe njiilii e goongɗinal, ɓe puji koye mum en e torraaji keewɗi. Heewɓe e mooɗon njokkii gooto e linjiilaaji fenaande keewɗi, kono linjiila goɗɗo alaa. Heewɓe e mooɗon njokkii gooto e Almasiihuuji fenaande keewɗi, kono Almasiihu goɗɗo alaa so wonaa miin, Yeshua HaMashiach. Miɗo wi’a on laaɓɗum, on njiilaama, heewɓe e mooɗon mbaylitii goongɗinal goonga ngal Iisaa Almasiihu e goongɗinal fenaande. Goongɗinal fenaande e goongɗinal fenaande ɗo konngol goongɗinal ngol (mbele mi hokkiraani on Konngol Goongɗinal goonga ngol?) wonaa hoolaare, goongɗingol, e darnde e nder Yahwe, Baaba mon, e am, Iisaa Almasiihu Joomiraaɗo mon, kono wayliima haa yettii ko ɓuri heewde e ko aldaa e nafoore, e daari, e miijooji maagiyankooɓe. On accii ɓe ngalaa nafoore ndee njiilna on, eklesiyaaji mon naati e seytaneeɓe, hay huɗo bonngo ngo bonɗo oo aawi e mooɗon.

Ɗee geɗe ɓurɗe tiiɗde ina ngarta e miijooji moƴƴi e bonɗi, onon e majjere mon on cikki wonde kala ko miijiiɗon haa juuti ina waɗata, hono no Alla waɗirnoo doole mum e moƴƴere mum e ɓeen ɓe ngalaa goongɗinal, ɗo njiɗirtaa geɗe ɗee e miijooji mon. Mbele on paamaani wonde kala gollotooɓe e ɗeen geɗe ɗe ngalaa nafoore ina nanngaa, ina njalta, ina njogii e njiɗɗe mum en e ɗeen fenanndeeji, hay so tawii ina njogii e majjere, sabu onon e majjere mon on njokki laabi Seytaane? Paamee ko malaa’ikaaji toɓɓuɗi, ee, Seytaaneeɓe e rewnooɓe ɓe njokkondiri e maɓɓe, ɓe njanngini neɗɗaagu maagi, taƴde ɗaɗi, bolle, e njuɓɓudi; e worɓe bonɓe heewɓe naati e eklesiya am Laodicea, njahdi e heewɓe no mbonam nii, ɓe ngoongɗini fenanndeeji mum en.

Ɗum waɗi ko sabu heewɓe e eklesiya am Laodicea ina calii daranaade e haɓde e nuunɗal mbele on mbaawa foolde bonannde e moƴƴere! Accu mi wallude on faamde goonga. Hay gooto e mooɗon alaa baawɗe e mbaawka e koye mum en, sabu ko e nder laaɓal mon so on njiytii nafoore golle mon. Ndeen, laabi keewɗi, on paamata wonde so on ndariima e am, Almasiihu Iisaa, on njokkii laabi am e laawol ngol cuɓii-mi ngam mon, on ndariima e am e baawɗe am, on njogii goongɗinal timmungal e am. Sabu mi hollan on fof e laawol am, so on njaɓii daande am, on ngartii so mi wi’ii on, wonde e golle mon e baawɗe mon on wonaa hay huunde so wonaa ɓuuɓri e nder jeereende, majjunde e henndu, mi maayaani ngam hisnude ɓuuɓri kono ko leydi boɗeeri Aadama, sabu miin woni Aadama cakkitiiɗo, miin ko ruuhu rokkowo nguurndam. Kono, e nder am Almasiihu Iisaa, on doolnuɓe, on mbaawaa haɗeede, sabu on njogii ndee jawdi e nder leydi, mi rokkete kaawniiɗe keewɗe, maande, kaawniiɗe e ko nanndi heen e juuɗe mon so on njogii goongɗinal jokkondirngal e am, Iisaa Almasiihu, wonaa goongɗinal e koye mon. Woto njaɓee mi ardude on, njaɓee fenanndeeji toɓɓuɓe, sabu e golle maɓɓe tikkere mawnde ina resndanaa ɓe e ñalawma tikkere, ɗo joofnirde maɓɓe ndee wonata e yiite e sulufur.

Paamee no ndee fenaande waɗirtee, sabu e nder Jubileeji cakkitiiɗi ina woodi anndinoore fenaande nde ɓeen ɓe renndo mon limtata mawɓe njaɓaani Alla sabu miijooji mum en ɗi ngalaa nafoore. Ɓe mbaɗi hay sanam miijo e nder njiimaandi mum en; mbele on nganndaa daartol? Ɗee ɓulli ɗi ndiyam alaa e filosofiiji mum en bonɗi e ɗi ngalaa nafoore, ɓe njaɓaani Alla, ɓe njanngini e majjere wonde Alla ko neɗɗo tan jooɗiiɗo, mo yiɗaa neɗɗaagu, kono ko o rokkata fof gila to woɗɗi, o acca neɗɗaagu faamde ɗum, tawa moƴƴere Alla ndee ina yahra e maagi ɓurɗo doole e maɓɓe. Ndeen, e nder waktuuji, nafseeji keewɗi ɗi ngalaa deeƴre, hay huɗo bonngo ngo Seytaane, naati e eklesiya am Laodicea ngam timminde fenanndeeji mum en e waktuuji e sahaaji potɗi. Ɓe naati e mooɗon e konnguɗi moƴƴi e konnguɗi laaɓtuɗi, ɓe njanngini baawɗe miijooji maagi e Jommbaajo am, heewɓe e nder ɓanndu am njokki ɓe e yiɗde, sabu yiɗde maɓɓe ndee ɓeydaama.

Ɓee sukaaɓe bonɓe ɓe Seytaane naati e mum en e nder suura, ɓe njanngini maagi e nder janngirɗe am, ɓe njottii haa ɓe mbonni Konngol am ceniingol e lewuur, so wonaa Konngol am ngol waawaa bonnude e fenanndeeji mum en, sabu fenanndeeji mum en ina ndarii e fenanndeeji. Mbele on paamaani wonde lewuur seeɗa ina lewina mburu oo fof? Mbele on paamaani wonde lewuur Heroda e lewuur Farisi’en ngonaa laabi bonannde tan? Sabu on njuumtaama e lewuur maagi kerecee’en! Nde wonnoo, TUUBEE! Hol ko mi wi’i Korintiyanaaɓe e juuɗe Nelaaɗo Poola? Ittee ndeen lewuur kiɗɗo oo, mbele on mbaawa wonde mburu keso, hono no on nguuriraa nii. Sabu hay Almasiihu Paska meeɗen sadakaama ngam meeɗen. Nanee Konnguɗi am! Nii woni, ɗii kulle ladde, ɗii ɓulli ɗi ndiyam alaa, hay huɗo bonngo ngo alaa nafoore ngo, njanngini, puɗɗii diineeji mum en fenaannde kerecee’en, diineeji hay, ina wujja heewɓe e ɓeen ɓe njoortoo e Eklesiya goonga, ɗo mi toppitotoo Jommbaajo am e yiɗde e yurmeende e yurmeende, ina arda ɓe gila e am haa e cuuɗi dewal fenaannde ɗi Baal woni e mum en e suura, sabu leydi mon ko Babilon Sirri!

Hono no ɗum bonirta nii, maagi’en doolnuɓe e alɗuɓe heewɓe njoɓii kaalis keewɗo, mbaɗi golle keewɗe ngam waɗde fenanndeeji mum en e nder Bibelji fenaannde. Ɓe njaltini binndi Iskandari, gnostik, ɓe mbaɗi Eklesiya am ina jogii miijooji ɗiɗi gila e binndi ɗi ndesndu-mi gila Antioche, hay so tawii ko Bibelji goɗɗi ɗo ɗeen fenanndeeji ɓeydaa e banngeeji. Ɗeeɗoo defte naatnaa e duɗe mon bonɗe, wonti Bibelji janngirɗe ngam ɓeen annabaaɓe fenaannde ɓe njanngini fenanndeeji gila ndeen, ɓe njoɓan haa timma. Gila ndeen, leyɗeele mon keewi eklesiyaaji fenaannde ɗi gilaaji njiɗɗi ardii, kono heewɓe e baali am ina cikka haa jooni ina mbaawi jooɗaade e gilaaji. Eklesiyaaji mon fenaannde ko cuuɗi hulɓiniiɗi; ko cuuɗi wellitaare ɗo ɓe njimrata Seytaane yeeso mon, on ngalaa paandaale ruuhu ngam yiyde. Joomiraaɓe keewɓe ina ngoodi e nder laamu Seytaane, ɓe njimrata Joomiraawo, Joomiraawo e maɓɓe, so wonaa so ɓe mbi’ii mi Joomiraawo, Joomiraawo, mi wi’an, Ittee e am, onon gollotooɓe e bonannde sabu mi meeɗaa anndude on.

Ngam bonnude geɗe ɗee, ɓe njaltini linjiila risku hulɓiniika, ɓe mbayli ɗum kadi e naatnude hakkilantaagal wallude hoore mum, hol ko mi haali e geɗe ɗee: Heddinee woto hay gooto ara bonna on e filosofi e majjere fenaande, e aadaaji yimɓe, e kuule aduna, wonaa e Almasiihu. Ɗee eklesiyaaji fenaannde ko bonɗi, alaa nafoore, heewɓe e mum en ko mahooɓe bonɓe ndarii, ɓe ngalaa e am, kaaƴe mawɗe ɗee, mahdiiji maɓɓe fof ina njaɓɓa e leydi mbayliindi. Ɓee ko mahooɓe hulɓiniiɓe, taƴooɓe kaaƴe fenaannde, e mahooɓe biriki fenaannde ɓe njaɓaani kaaƴe mawɗe ɗee, alaa goonga e maɓɓe! Ɓe kollirta hay maande maɓɓe e nder cuuɗi dewal mon, ɗo ɓe njalnata on yeeso mon. Nanee konngol ngol njogii-mi ngam mon, sabu ko ngoonga, ngo addan nafseele mon anndinoore goonga: Neɗɗo bonɗo, neɗɗo bonɗo, ina yaha e hunuko bonngo. Ina ñifta gite mum, ina haala e koyɗe mum, ina janngina e kolli mum.

Bonannde ina woni e ɓernde mum, ina miijoo bonannde wakkati fuu, ina aawa ceerundal. Nii ɓe ngoriri, nii ɓe mbaɗirta yeeso mon, kono on mbaawaa yiyde, sabu on wumɓii e janngirɗe maɓɓe fenaannde e laabi maɓɓe bonɗi.

Sukaaɓe am, njaltee e maɓɓe, ceerndee koye mon; hol ko Almasiihu nanndi e Beliyal. Woto njokkondiree e golle niɓere ɗe ngalaa ɓiɓɓe, ceerndee e maɓɓe haa laaɓa. Sabu onon, eklesiya am Laodicea, hay onon on nguli, on ngalaa ƴiye, ɗee ngonaa laabi am hay dara, sabu mi riiwi waylooɓe kaalis e nder suudu dewal e nder yiɗde am Yahwe. Ngartiree e am, Iisaa Almasiihu, pastoor goonga e safroowo nafseele mon, njanngee e am. Sabu konnguɗi moƴƴi e miijooji moƴƴi ɗi ngalaa nafoore ngonaa e am, kono ko fenanndeeji ɗi nelaaɓe fenaannde mbi’ata on, hay so tawii ko gollotooɓe e Seytaane ɓe mbayli hoore mum en wonti gollotooɓe e nuunɗal. Dogee e am, sukaaɓe am tokosɓe, sabu waktu timmi, onon e majjere mon on cikki on njogii waktuuji fof e aduna, on njogii tan ko minitaaji seeɗa ngam tuubde e hootde e am e nder deeƴre mbele miɗo waawi haa jooni waɗande on nokku e taabal am, mbele miɗo waawi haa jooni udditande on damal e nder asamaanuuji mbele on mbaawa naatde e welo-welo Joomiraaɗo mon!

Sabu heewɓe e mooɗon ina cikka maa on ndenndine e am so on nguuri ɗeen fenanndeeji, so on njaɓii ko annabaaɓe fenaannde ndokkata on, e innde am. Anndee wonde mi nelaani ɓe, konnguɗi maɓɓe ngonaa e am, so on njaɓaani e maɓɓe, maa njahdon e am e nder jarribooji e jarribooji keewɗi e nder caɗeele Yaakuuba, ɗe ngari e aduna oo fof, ɗe ngoni ko e waɗeede! Ndaaree mi e ruuhu mon; hakkilanee, sabu on mbaawi weltinde muuseeki jibinannde e nder ruuhu mon, sabu miɗo nodda on galle, jommbaajo am ina heddii e jibinde. Ndaaree, mi accii on e ɓataake mo neldu-mi on, Laodicea am, sabu e nder ɗeen konnguɗi laawol hootde e am ina anndinaa; so on nanii, mi artiran njoɓdi mon, on njuurdan e am e taabal am e nder asamaanuuji ɗi njiɗ-mi ngam jommbaajo am.

E nelaaɗo jumaa, woni e Laodise, winndee: Nii wiyi (Aamiin) seedeejo goonga e goonga, fuɗɗoode tagooje Alla. Mi anndi golle ma, a wulaani, a wulaani. Miɗo yiɗi a wula walla a wula. Nde wonnoo a woni hakkunde maɓɓe ɗiɗo, a wulaani, a wulaani. Mi wurtinan ma e hunuko am: sabu a alɗuɗo, a ɓeydii jawdi, a alaa ko njiɗɗaa, a anndaa no ngonirtaa bonannde, miskiino, wumɗo, ɓaleejo. Miɗo wasiyoo ma soodude e am kaŋŋe jarribaaɗo e yiite, mbele a alɗa, e comci daneeji, mbele a ɓoornoo, mbele comci maa ɗi ngalaa nafoore ɗii njiyaa, njuute gite maa e safaare gite, mbele a yiya. Heewɓe e ɓe njiɗmi, miɗo fada, miɗo jaɓɓa. Ngonnee e weltaare, tuubee. Ndaaree, miɗo darii e damal, miɗo fiyata. So tawii hay gooto nanii daande am, udditi damal, mi naatan e makko, mi njuurdan e makko, kanko e am. Mo fooli, mi rokkete jooɗaade e jooɗorde am, hono no mi fooliri nii, mi jooɗii e Baaba am, e jooɗorde makko.

Ndaaree!

Miin woni jahroowo hakkunde lampaaji jeeɗiɗi ɗii, ngartiree e am, woto mi itta lampa mon e mon.

Miin woni pastoor moƴƴo hokkuɗo hoore mum ngam jawdi.

Miin woni laamɗo leydi ndii gila e jappeere laamu Daawuuda, mi fiyan leƴƴi ɗii e sawru njamndi.

Miin woni mo fotata heɓde deftere ndee, mi fecca maande.

Miin woni yamirde malaa’ika ummotooɗo fuɗnaange, jogiiɗo maande Alla guurɗo, sabu ɓe maandan 144 000 am, hay so tawii ko maccuɓe Alla guurɗo.

Miin woni dariiɗo e dow tulde Siyon e 144 000 am, ɓe njiman yimre hesere nde hay gooto waawaa anndude.

Miin woni Joomiraaɗo Joomiraaɓe e Laamɗo Laamɓe, miɗo ara ngam haɓde e laamɓe leydi, sabu waktu maɓɓe arii.

Miin woni garoowo ngam jommbaajo am, sabu yiyngo am ina heddii. Njiɗee koye mon, jommbaajo am!
