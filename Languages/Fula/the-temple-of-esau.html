Seedeeji Ɗiɗo: Isra'iila
Ko Welɗi e ko Kaɗɗi
Nuwammbar 23, 2024

Haykaliiru Esawu

Nanɗee, nanɗee! Onon leñol Isra'iila, konngol Yesu'a HaMashiyak, kanko woni Ɓiɗɗo bajjo Joomiraawo Yahweh, Mawɗo MIIN WONI! Hino weli mi rentina on e geɗe garooje, mbele oɗon annda kadi paawngal ngal Barabbas feññinta ngam wota on naatondir e heeferɓe; geɗe ɗe mbiaama heen seeɗa gila adan e Linjiila duumotooɗo. Kono mi ari ngam mi yeetoo on paawngal ngal mboddi weernata e leydi alkawal, mi laɓɓina ngal ngam wota on kalkiree peeje maɓɓe. Miɗo yiɗi laɓɓinde laabi mon, mi jogoo on e laabi nuunɗuɗi, hono laabi Yahweh, ɗi wuuri haa laaɓi e nder Yesu'a HaMashiyak, Masiya mon goongaajo, mo jokki anniyee Yahweh haa timmi. Miɗo yiɗi kadi mbele oɗon mbaɗa koyɗe mon e laawol fongol ngol, ngol mi darnirta koyɗe mon so tawii on njokkii rewde mi e hoolaare nde ɗoftotoo. Sabu nuunɗuɓe ɓee, ko e hoolaare njooɗirta!

Nde anndee wakkati yottake, no kaaliraa, no anndinaa, ɗo mi mooftata he waktu gooto hoolaaɓe e Almasiihu fayde kammu, woni wuurɓe walla maayɓe; tuma seniiɓe hoolaaɓe ɓee njaɓɓetee he waktu gooto, halkaare wootere yani e Baabila Asiriijo jooɗiiɗo e ndiyamji keewɗi, woni leƴƴi, e jamaaji, e leyɗe, e ɗemɗe. Tuma ɗoondi konu nguu ɓuuɓata, yuɓɓo leyɗe ngo hannde ngoo halkee, ngo feewnee no haanirta anniyeeji maɓɓe, njuɓɓudi ndariindi cakkitiindi ndii wondugo nduu ummotoo haa laaɓa.

Kono, wondugo iwngo e maayo e wondugo iwngo e leydi fiyi fijirde aynaaɓe moƴƴuɓe, warɓe ngam aynude baali majjuɗi Baabila Asiriijo e alkule keewɗe meereeje; alkule fenaande ɗe njaltinta maayde e halkaare. Eɓe njuurnitoo koɓe ngayɓe sirliniiɓe caggal nate neɗɗo nayeejo demento, mo ɓe cuuɗii caggal mum, kanko neɗɗo oo woni ko ngam o fawaa aybeeji maɓɓe; eɓe ndennda kala ko o waɗata, eɓe mbinnda kala ko o haalata. Sabu ngol majjere mawnde ndee rokkiraama ngam ñaawoore Baabila Asiriijo; mbele majjere tiiɗnde nannga mo haa laaɓa, sabu o suddii leydi ndii e fenaande makko, omo jogii e junngo makko jarduɗum kaŋŋe, heewndum kooseeje e neesuuji jeenay makko.

Kono, ɗii wondugooji ɗiɗi bonɗi bonnii Baabila Asiriijo gila hade halkaare mum yettaade, eɓe keɓi doole mum konu e keewal mum ngam anniyeeji drakoŋ e nder njuɓɓudi mum cakkitiindi garoori. Sabu konu garoowu nguu ñamete Baabila Asiriijo, e goonga o halkete he waktu gooto, sabu noon Yahweh, Mawɗo MIIN WONI, haaliri. Kono, ɗum ko e peeje, kadi ko e peeje Yahweh kisndam (mbele ayɓe makko ɗoftoo konngol makko), ñaawoore Debbo Jerɗo Mawɗo ndee haalaama, windaama, noon wonirta haa abada, so o ummitaako kadi. Nonnon, ɓe peccirta leydi, dillere mawnde waɗata, eɓe peewni halkaare makko ngam ɓe moofta jawdi ndi ɓe njiɗi, ndi suuɗii e nder leydi. Kaa jawdi ndi ɓe kaajorii ngam semmbinde e yaajnude karallaagal wondugo nduu; karallaagal ngal aduna oo heɓtii, o majjinaa, omo jogii e nder majjere mum tiiɗnde, omo heddii e nder ɓuuɓri maɓɓe e waylude maɓɓe, ɗo ɓe njiirtee, ɓe laamiree.

E nder yimɓe heddiiɓe caggal halkaare garoore Baabila Asiriijo ndee; wondugo iwngo e leydi e wondugo iwngo e maayo naatni konu naatooɓe e warooɓe e keeri mum, limoore maɓɓe no wa'i no baasal mbuuɗu mawngu, ɓe accitete so maande maɓɓe rokkiraama! Sabu ɗii ko konuuji wondugo ngoo, mooftaaɗi ngam halkaare Baabila Asiriijo. Heewɓe e maɓɓe ko e leyɗe drakoŋ e leyɗe ursus, ɓe mbaɗata no Baabila Asiriijo waɗirnoo ɓe e sirlu e nder yubileyji ɓennuɗi: ɓe mbarata kala gorko, debbo, e suka tawa alaa yurmeende, mo fotaani e anniyeeji maɓɓe, e heewɓe e accaaɓe wuurde, ɓe nduwete e kampuuji maɓɓe maccuɓe ngam gollude e nder nokkuuji maɓɓe haa ɓe maaya. Eɓe ngollira, eɓe tampude haa ɓuuɓol maɓɓe cakkitiingol; sabu kala ko Baabila Asiriijo waɗi e sirlu e woɗɓe, jooni waɗete e makko e laawol peeñngol ngam ñaawoore e lepte. Sabu Baabila Asiriijo ina yardi ƴiiƴam seniiɓe, e ƴiiƴam seedeeji Iisaa ɗi o waɗi seedeeji e keewal mawngal e leyɗe janane; omo wiyana hoore makko omo gollande Iisaa Nazaareeno. Kono e goonga omo gollande wondugooji, anniyeeji makko timmi jooni, eɓe njiɗi halkaare makko ngam ɓe cudda aybeeji maɓɓe, mbele aynaaɓe maɓɓe ɓooyɓe mbaawa yaltude e laawol moƴƴere so ɓe ummii ngam ɓe cudda paawngal maɓɓe.

Kono, ɓe mbaawaa hay gooto suuɗde hoore maɓɓe e Yahweh, walla e Yesu'a HaMashiyak, aybeeji maɓɓe mbinndaama no haanirta e nder kammu, kala gootum e salaare mbinndaama no feewi, wondude e miijooji e anniyeeji ɓerɗe maɓɓe lo'tuɗe. Wondugo iwngo e maayo e wondugo iwngo e leydi ko kooseeje bonɗe, tagiraaɗe ngam halkude aadee. Ɗii kooseeje bonɗe ko bonnooɓe sukaaɓe, eɓe moofta, eɓe kuutora ɓalli sukaaɓe ɓe mbari ngam waɗde kooseeje maɓɓe, kadi kala ko ɓe kalki, rokkiree ngam sadaka alla maɓɓe. Sabu ɓe njogii Tabernakel Molok, e hoodere alla maɓɓe Remfan, nate ɗe ɓe mbaɗi, ngam dewal, e ko heewi ko ɓe mbaɗi, ko feeñete haa laaɓa e wakkatiiji mum peeñɗi, sabu kala sirlu yaltata e laaɓal.

Nii wonirta, wondugo iwngo e leydi ummotoo no wa'i no ummii e yanaande; omo wiyana hoore makko o ummiima e maayde, hono ɓernde leydi, sabu maayde waawaano jogaade mo! O heɓata ummital makko fenaande, o addana jam fenaande e leydi ngam o timmina konu maɓɓe, o yaltata no wa'i no o ari ngam o hisna aadee. Wondugooji ɗii peeñan e aadee no wallooɓe moƴƴuɓe, ɓurɓe toowde e hakkilantaagal, ɓe ɓamtiima e laabi karallaagal, safaara, e jam.

Eɓe keɓa maandeeji fenaande keewɗi, eɓe mbiya wonɓe mooɓtaaɓe e Almasiihu e mooɓgal mawngal, naatnaaɓe e kisal kammu hade musiiba mawɗo yani; wonde ɓeen tan naɓiraama doole e gaño leydi janannde. Kaa doole gaño leydi janannde garɗo ngam halkude aadee, ɓe ndawra neɗɗo tago ngam o hawta e maɓɓe ngam o hisna hoore makko e ngol bonannde nde ɓe miijii e nder aduna maɓɓe fenaande, mo meeɗaa darorde, mo meeɗaa dartude e nder meere meere.

Hono fenaande tagngo aduna nde wondugooji kuutorinoo gila e wakkati kaŋŋe mum en ɓooyɗo, wakkati kaŋŋe oo timminiraa e junngo Yahweh e ilam mawngam e wakkati Nuuhu, ko winndaa e binndanɗe ceniɗe ɓooyɗe. E ɗeen fenaande ndartete les, sabu majjere maɓɓe mawnde ndee peeñete haa laaɓa, mbele kala yimɓe njiya ɓeen fenuɓe no ɓe ngoniri, sabu ɓe ngoni tagoore toownde ummoraade e laamu kammu, ɓe bonnii hoore maɓɓe gila ko ɓooyi e nder ummital meere e nuunɗal Yahweh, mbele ɓe ndona halkaare maɓɓe nde ɓe mbaɗani hoore maɓɓe e nder ndonu maɓɓe mbuuɗu njareteengu, ngu ɓe njaltataa.

Nii wondugo iwngo e leydi ummotoo no wa'i no ummii e maayde, e maandeeji fenaande keewɗi o yaltata, omo jogii lekki oliwa e junngo makko. O ummotoo no baalel jogiingel galaaɗe ɗiɗi, o ummotoo e doole e laamu drakoŋ, wondude e wondugo iwngo e maayo, kanko suɓaaɗo e drakoŋ e wondugo iwngo e leydi ngam laamaade leyɗe, mbele o laamoo maayo leyɗe no haanirta sariyaaji maɓɓe Nuwaayid bonɗi, warooɓe yimɓe. Kono, leyɗe makko ɗiɗi ɗee ngonaatah safaara, sabu miin woni mo hawtinta Isra'iila haa abada, so o feccaaka kadi e laamuuji ɗiɗi.

Nii ɓe kuutorirta sariyaaji maɓɓe bonɗi e batte mum en e nder Nuwaayid ngam haɓde e seniiɓe, ngam foolde ɓe, so wonaa kala nodduɗo innde Joomiraawo haa timmal, hisintee, hono ɓeen nodduɓe Iisaa Nazaareeno hono Joomiraawo maɓɓe kisnoowo, mo hisnanta ɓe haa timmal so ɓe tampataa e caɗeele maɓɓe. Nii wondugo iwngo e maayo accitete e ko heddii e leyɗe ɗe ɓe kalki, ɗoondi mum en ko leyɗe ɗe ɓe laaminoo e majjere mawnde. Eɓe njiyee no ɓe ummii no feeniks mawɗo e ɗoondi e yiite konu nguu heddiiɓe e majjere maɓɓe. Kadi konu mawngu wonata, konu ɓurngu mawnude e konuuji tati ɗi ɓe ummini, ɓe ngollii; mbele drakoŋ oo ummoo haa laaɓa e wakkati mum, e wondugooji mum bonɗi, ɗi o heɓirta laamu makko seeɗa, so o laamaaki kadi.

Sabu sirlu bonannde ina golloo gila e jamaanuuji; ɗo debbo mawɗo jooɗiiɗo e kooseeje jeeɗiɗi, debbo boɗeejo bonɗo dewoowo laamɗo kammu, jannginoowo Iisaa goɗɗo e nder tatiire mum pagan. Hono leɗɗe mum ɗe leɗɗinaa e Baabila ɓooyɗo, ɗo Nimrood fuɗɗii haalaaji mum e laamɗo kammu. Hono ndonu mum, e almuudooji mum, e leƴƴi mum ina njokki e wondugooji laamɗo Baabila ɓooyɗo e tatiire mum bonnde, nde ɓe kuutortoo ngam jalnude Alla goonga, e aawdi alkawal debbo. Ɗee golle pagan e juulde njogii leydi e sirlu mawɗo, ɗo almuudooji wondugooji naatnaaɗi ina ndewa naange e sirlu, kono ina mbiya koɓe Dental Iisaa; noon eklesiya maɓɓe fenaande, bonɗo, bonnii, tooñii leydi e nder balɗe sirlu bonannde ina yaaji e sirlu. Ko ɗum waɗi, halkaare maɓɓe ina fadi, wakkati maɓɓe yottake!

Kono ɗii kooseeji ɗiɗi bonɗi, ɗi jooni ina ngollondira juuɗe e juuɗe ngam halkude Baabila asiriijo, hono wondugo iwngo e maayo e wondugo iwngo e leydi; ɗii ɗiɗo laamɓe Baabila asiriijo, laamɓe e alkule keewɗe meereeje, ɗiin aynaaɓe ɗiɗo ɓooyɓe ina njaɓɓi baali majjuɗi Baabila asiriijo, ina njaɓɓana jawdi e keewal fenaande. E nder juuɗe maɓɓe, e peeje maɓɓe, ɓe njuɓɓinii, ɓe kuutortoo ko rokkaa e juuɗe drakoŋ ngam timminde.

Sabu Baabila asiriijo, debbo mawɗo jooɗiiɗo e ndiyamji keewɗi, ko leydi waɗaandi ngam ɗiin kooseeji ɗiɗi bonɗi mbaawa ummaade e ɗoondi mum e wakkatiiji mum en peeñɗi. Kaa leydi jenyootoo, leydi jeenay, leydi debbo mawɗo, wiyata hoore mum ko almuudo nuunɗuɗo Iisaa Nazaareeno. Kono e goonga ko leydi mahooɓe mum ndimaani e Hayre Foundation goonga, hayre mawnde nde heeferɓe nduppata, hono Iisaa Nazaareeno, kanko woni Yesu'a HaMashiyak. Nii ɗiin mahooɓe bonɗe ndimaani e kisal, ɓe ndokki hoore maɓɓe e ndiyam yiite ɗam liɗɗi mum en njaltataa, sabu ɓe njaɓaani hayre mawnde, hayre tooñannge, so ɓe ndogataa!

Kono wondugooji ina njogii anniyeeji gooti, peeje maɓɓe e anniyeeji maɓɓe ina keddii gooti. Sabu ɓe anniyii halkude Aadama haa timmi e leydi, ɓe anniyii halkude Yaakuuba haa timmi e les naange, mbele ɓe keddoo no hoɗɓe, no keɓtiiɓe leydi bonɓe, mbele ɓe keɓa wakkati maɓɓe kaŋŋe ɗo ɓe njiɗi. Kono anndee wakkati maɓɓe kaŋŋe ko wakkati ɗo Aadama foolaa, laamiraa e laamu aduna bonngo, ngam halkude aadee haa timmi, kooseeje maɓɓe ngartata! Kono wakkati kaŋŋe ɓe keɓataa, so wonaa yidde bonnde e ñaawoore tiiɗnde, so laamu wondugooji halkiraama yeeso maɓɓe e juuɗe am doole!

E wakkati maɓɓe biyeteeɗo kaŋŋe, ko wakkati ɗo leñol nuunɗungol Set suuɗii hoore mum e ladde e renndo maɓɓe bonngo e aduna oo. Mbele leñol Set heddoo laaɓngol e pollugol leñol, e aawdi alkawal debbo (ko miin woni), mbaawa arde no alkawal Yahweh gila adan. Ndaa! Yahweh haalii o addan Mawɓe ngam timminde tikkere makko, noon wonirta. Kono, laamu maɓɓe bonngo ummotoo, laamoo leydi ndii fof, bonanndeeji keewɗi ɓe ngaddata, kooseeji keewɗi accitete caggal galaas, e nder ɓernde leydi, lugge ɗee udditete.

Bonannde mawnde wonata e leydi, hay so ɓe ummii ngam addude jam fenaande; konu laamotoo laamu maɓɓe sabu jam ittaama e leydi ngam ñaawoore so mi itti joom am. Jam ɓe keɓataa sabu Yahweh haalii gila adan wonde wondugooji alaa jam sabu ɓe tooñii aadee e nder yidde maɓɓe bonnde, e konnaagu maɓɓe e kala ko Yahweh tagi. Eɓe ndarna jam fenaande e leydi, wondugo iwngo e leydi naatata e doole mum haa timma, o laamotoo no baalel jogiingel galaaɗe ɗiɗi, haaldata no drakoŋ.

Sabu baalel Esawu, hono wondugo iwngo e leydi, kanko woni Barabbas, suɓaama ngam mahde haykaliiru maɓɓe bonndu, o rokkaama no baalel drakoŋ ngam Moshiach maɓɓe fenaande e laamɗo maɓɓe fenaande. E goonga, e goonga, o hawtinta Esawu e Ashkenaz e kala hawruɓe e maɓɓe e les bandera diine mo baabiraaɓe maɓɓe nganndaano, laabi maɓɓe Talmud laamotoo leydi. Eɓe nduwata leyɗe e les Nuwaayid e laawol bonngol, ɓe laamotoo leyɗe e laawol bonngol; wondugo iwngo e leydi addanta kala, woni pamaro e mawɗo, alɗuɗo e miskineejo, dimo e maccuɗo, heɓde maande mum e juuɗe mum en nyaamo walla e tiinde mum en, mbele hay gooto waawaa soodde walla soodde, so wonaa jogiiɗo maande ndee, walla innde wondugo ngoo, walla limoore innde mum. Sabu omo addana ɗakkere mboɗeeri nde hay gooto waawaa sellude, kala jeyaaɗo e mum waylotoo hoore mum haa abada, so o heɓaani yaafuya, ɓe kaɓɓondirta njamndi e loope, kono ɓe kaɓɓondirtaa. Ko wonaa nuunɗuɗum waawaa nuunɗineede, kala waɗɗo hoore mum no wonaa nuunɗuɗum, heɓata feccere mum e ndiyam yiite mbuuɗu njareteengu, ɗam meeɗaa ɓuuɓde, ɗo liɗɗi mum en njaltataa.

Nii woni, kuuyeejo ummoriiɗo e leydi, mo woni kadi annabiijo mum en fenaande e Mashiach mum en fenaande, maa hollit konnguɗi mum, o mahana suudu dewal mum, sabu o neliraama no mahoowo suudu dewal kuuyeejo, mo mahani hoore mum cuuɗi toowɗi keewɗi e nder laamu mum ngam teddinde innde mum, o yewta kadi hoore makko e mawninkinaare mawnde, haa o wonta o wi’a ko kanko woni Alla. Sabu o ari ko ngam waɗde fodoore mawnde heewnde nde o alaa baawɗe timminde, o ari kadi ko ngam haalde geɗe mawɗe, bonɗe, tee nii o waɗata sabu ko ɗum rokkaa mo ngam o timmina. Sabu ko o mahoowo e ƴellitoowo, mo waɗani hoore mum cuuɗi toowɗi e nokkuuji fooftere keewɗi, tee goonga, o jooɗii ko e dow laamu makko ngo o halfini ngam teddinde hoore makko; sabu omo yettude hoore makko sahaa kala, sabu ko nii woni laabi mbeewa, baaba makko goonga.

Nde suudu dewal makko ndu mahiraa, ndu timminaa e nder mawningol mawngol e fodoore fenaande heewnde, o waɗata kadi konnguɗi fenaande keewɗi e nder golle makko no annabiijo fenaande, laamu makko nguu wonata ko tiiɗngu, bonngu. Kono, o ñiɓata hettere makko, kono hol fowru ñiɓata hettere tawa colli ina nji’a? E suuraare mawnde o ñiɓata hettere makko, e fodoore mawnde o fodoto o halfinana suudu dewal makko yimɓe leydi ndii, e weltaare mawnde Esawu e Ashkenaz njaɓɓotooɓe hoore mum en e dow mbaala Esawu, ɓe mbaɗta hoore mum en no artirɓe laabi Tawreeta nii, kono ko laabi maayde ɓe ngaddata, ɗi mbinndaa e nder deftere maɓɓe maayde bonnde, hay Talmud maɓɓe. Ndaaree! Kuuyeejo ummoriiɗo e leydi ko mbardoowo, ardiiɗo fitinaaji bonɗi e Yahweh e laabi Barabas. O janfanto fof ɓe o fodorii fodoore mawnde, janfa makko o wonataa e nimsitaare, e añam mawɗam ɗam o roni e Baaba makko, seytaane mo o waɗi anniya timminde yiɗde mum e nder bonannde.

Kono nii woni sifaa ñabbuunde nde sofi aduna oo fof e nder mbuubaari mum. Ñabbuunde ndee ina jooɗii ina ndaara cooyngal mum ina naata e mbuubaari mum, ndeen noon nde ummotoo ngam nanngeede, parlitineede, e huccineede cooyngal mum ngam nde nyaamda seeɗa seeɗa e nguurndam nannge mum; nde ɓe njuutortee seeɗa seeɗa e bonannde nde ɓe nanngaa ndee. Kono jamaanuuji ɗii ummortoo ko e nokkuuji ɓadiiɗi e woɗɗuɗi so ɓe nanii noddaango makko; e noddaango halfingol e laɓɓingol suudu dewal makko ngam weltinde aduna oo fof, heewɓe e salaade Masiya mum en goonga nanngete e hettere makko, majja heen. Kono hettere ndee yaltataa e yeeso ɓe o siiri ɓee, kono ko ummital Muusaa ardii Sanhedrin mum en, laamii aadaaji e hakkilantaagal Esawu gonngal e deftere maɓɓe bonnde, nde alaa barke. E fedde almuɓɓe fenaande ɓe ngonaa e leñol Levi, sabu Levi ina heɓee fof e nder Hooreejo Alkawal Melkisedek, sabu miin, Yesuwa HaMasiya, ko mi Hooreejo Alkawal mon haa abada e dow limoore Melkisedek, sabu ko ɗum Yahweh holliti.

Kono no mi jannginiri goonga e nder waktu am nde ngar-mi e leñol am no Mbaala Alla nii; nde mi salaa e leñol am, kono mi jannginii goonga noon e noon, ko ɗum waɗi, an yo Israayiil, a alaa gaasirgal. Kono, e nder yiɗde am e mooɗon, e fawaade e fodoore e alkule ɓooyɗe, mi ari mi haalana on kadi haa Yakuba yani e Esawu.

Sabu winndaama e binndi laaɓtuɗi: Nde nji’on noon bonannde mbonnere, nde Annabi Daniyel haali ndee, ina darii e nokku laaɓɗo, (hol mo janngata, yo faam). Ndeen noon, ɓeen wonɓe e Yahuuda, yo ndog e kooseeje. Mo woni e dow suudu, yo woppu, yo ƴettu hay huunde e suudu mum: Kadi mo woni e ngesa, yo woppu ngam ƴettude comci mum. Bone mum en wonɓe e reedu, e ɓe muyninta e ɗeen ñalɗi. Kono nduwanee yo dogdu mon woto won e ndunngu, woto won kadi e ñalawma aset: Sabu ndeen wonan ñalɗi mawɗi, ɗi nji’aaka gila puɗɗagol aduna haa hannde, alaa, ɗi nji’ataake kadi haa abada. So tawii ñalɗi ɗii njaatnaaka, hay ɓalndu hisataa: kono sabu suɓaaɓe ɓee, ñalɗi ɗii njaatnetee.

No winndiraa nii, nii waɗirtee, Barabas maa uddit hettere mum nde o suuɗnoo e laaɓal. Sabu majjuɓe ɓee ina njiɗi halkude leñol Aadama fof e leydi. Ko ɗum waɗi, ɓe kaɓat e seniiɓe ɓe foola ɓe, Esawu e Ashkenaz ina njiɗi ñiɓde Yakuba, haa Yakuba yani e mboros hoore mo ɓe njiɗi halkude Yakuba oo. Faamee wonde so wonaano yurmeende e yurmeende Yahweh mo halfini hoore mum e nder yiɗde mum duumiinde e Israayiil e nder fodoore e alkule keewɗe ɓooyɗe ngam hisnude e hisnude Yakuba e juuɗe añɓe mum; on fof on majjat e juuɗe tato ɓe ngalaa barke ɓee! Kono Yahweh ina heddii e goonga haa abada e kala fodoore nde o waɗi! Miin, Yesuwa HaMasiya, kañum woni soodoowo kisal mon e sadak am mettuɗo, miin ko mi goongɗinɗo timminde kala fodoore kala alkulal ngal waɗaaka haa abada, tee goonga, kala fodoore nde waɗatee haa abada, tee hay fodoore wootere nde Yahweh walla Yesuwa HaMasiya waɗi, yantataa e leydi tawa timmaani, kono fof timminirtee, haa abada.

Ko ɗum waɗi, mi ari mi walloya Israayiil, hay ɓeen ummoriiɓe e Yakuba, ɓe njaɓata laamu am no Masiya mum en nii e nder feere hisnugol duumiinde Yahweh, nde Yahweh rokkata ngam hisnude Yakuba; sabu goonga, goonga, so ñalɗi ɗii njaatnaaka, hay ɓalndu wuurndu hisataa. Sabu ko nii majjuɓe ɓee njiɗiri, nde ɓe kalkata seniiɓe, ɓe kalka Yakuba, gila toon ɓe kalka fof heddiiɓe e leñol Aadama; haa leydi ndii fof wontana ɓe e nder peeje maɓɓe majjuɗe ɗe ngalaa nafoore.

Ndeen noon, ñalɗi mawɗi ɓurɗi mawnude mbaɗata e leydi, ƴiiƴam ilat e leydi heewɗam. Barabas maa war heewɓe yaasi wuro ngo o huɓɓiri konuuji mum ngam ɓe ndogataa; ko ɗum waɗi winndaama: Barke ngam maayɓe e nder Joomiraawo, gila jooni, eey, wiyi Ruuhu, ngam ɓe poofteree golle maɓɓe, golle maɓɓe kadi ina njokka ɓe.

Nii woni, ñalɗi mawɗi ɓurɗi mawnude mbaɗata, hay ñalɗi ɗi nji’aaka e leydi ko adii, tee goonga, ɗi mbaɗataa kadi. Ndaaree! Miin, hay Yesuwa HaMasiya, mi accataa Yakuba e halkaare nde alaa sabaabu, mi hisnan kadi Israayiil, hay heddiiɓe mawɓe ɓe njaɓata mi no Masiya mum en goonga nii, sabu ko ɗum Yahweh tagiri mi. Nii woni, 144 000 am ari walloya ɓeen ɓe majjatnoo, ɓe noddata innde am. Mi ardotoo ɓe seeɗa seeɗa, 144 000 am ɓee mbaɗanan mi golle mawɗe keewɗe, hay golle ɓurɗe mawnude ɗe mbaɗ-mi e nder waktu am e nder ɓalndu am nde alaa hakke no mbaala Alla nii.

Sabu ɓee ko ɓeen ƴettaaɓe e mooɓondiral seniiɓe haa e am, Almasiihu Iisaa, e nder weeyo so buubaaji cakkitiiji puɗii, buubaaji ɗii ina ngondi e puɗɗagol. Ɓee ko goongɗinɓe am, hay ɓeen ɓe alaa fenaande, sabu ɓe laɓɓinii hoore mum en no suka debbo laaɓɗo nii e nder ƴiiƴam Mbaala; ɗam mi rokki haa abada no hooreejo alkawal maɓɓe nii, sabu miin ko mi hooreejo alkawal haa abada e dow limoore Melkisedek. Ɓee ko Dewgal am, ɓe njokkat mi kala ɗo njah-mi. Ndaaree! Mi waɗan ɓe doole, tee hay huunde meeɗaani yiyeede e leydi nde fotaani e mbaawkaaji ɗi mi ɓornata ɓe ɗii. Ɓe ɓoornetee ko comci daneeji, laaɓtuɗi, ɗi alaa toɓɓe, mbaawkaaji maɓɓe kadi ina mbaɗdi golle maɓɓe, tee mbaawkaaji mawɗi ɓe njogotoo; sabu ko ɓe goongɗinɓe, noddaaɓe, suɓaaɓe, hay gila aduna oo sosaaka, e juuɗe Yahweh ngam ɗeen dalillaaji.

144 000 am ɓee njiippotoo e dow tufnde Siyon, mi ardotoo ɓe. Ɓe njahrat ɓeen ɓe njiɗ-mi ardude e ladde ngam suuɗeede e niɓere e torra laamu añɓe. Ɓe ndenndinee e kisal e yurmeende Alla timmunde wonnde e nder konkooje jeeɗiɗi; sabu e nder ɗeen konkooje woni tiiɗnaare tikkere Alla ndee yantete e jooɗorde kuuyeejo oo, torra laamu Mbeewa, sabu lepte maɓɓe ina mawni. 144 000 ɓee ndenndina Yakuba e kisal; ɓe cellina ɓe e mborosaaji maɓɓe; ɓe mballa ɓe, ɓe ndokka ɓe yaakaare mawnde ngam noddude innde Joomiraawo, haa e joofnirde so ina haani. Sabu so mi jaatnaaki ñalɗi ɗii, hay ɓalndu hisataa. Nii woni ɓe timminirta ko mi ardotoo ɓe. Yakuba suuɗetee e kisal e ñalawma ndeen wolde mawnde, hay ndeen wolde mawnde Armagedon nde ɓe ndogataa e halkaare maɓɓe.

Ko ɗum waɗi, faamee, banndiraaɓe am, jamaanuuji ɗi nguuroton e fenaande nde njahraton ndee, nde muumata aduna oo fof; fenaande ndee muumata e nguurndam e ñaawoore ɓeen añɓe, salaade Yahweh e batte e lepte mawɗe ngam laabi maɓɓe ɗi ngalaa goonga.

Miin woni Mbaala Alla, ngal rokkaama ngam ittude hakkeeji aduna oo e nuunɗal.

Miin woni Raawaandu leñol Yahuuda, e nuunɗal mi ari mi ñaawoya, mi waɗa wolde.

Miin woni mo hisnata heddiiɓe mawɓe e Yakuba, ɓe njokkata nguurndam haa e laamu am duuɓi ujunere, laamu ngo Laamɗo mon e Joomiraawo mon Joomiraaɓe sostat.

Miin woni Yesuwa HaMasiya, jiwo fodaango debbo, mo waɗaa e les njiimaandi sariya, rokkaama sadak ngam lepte Muusaa, ngam yoɓde hakkeeji e yaafuya timmunga e hisnude fof arɓe e am e nimsitaare timmunde.

Miin woni nelɗo ɗee konnguɗi e juuɗe nelaaɗo am e maccuɗo am, mo heɓi Linjiila duumiika, woni Deftere famarde ndee, seediinde e Yuhanna anndinoowo, mo haalanaama yo suuɗ konnguɗi jeeɗiɗi puɗɗiɗi ɗii, woto winndu ɗi, tee heen woni hisnugol Yakuba feeñninaama!

Goonga, goonga, miin, Yesuwa HaMasiya, mi seeditii wonde Konnguɗi Linjiila duumiika ɗii ndokkaama, peeñninaama e juuɗe am, e juuɗe Yahweh e hoore mum, hay Mawɗo Miin! Ɗee Konnguɗi Linjiila duumiika ɗii ko goonga, tee suuɗaama e nder ɓernde Yahweh e hoore mum haa waktu potɗo feeñninde mum; tee mbinndaama no fotiri nii no ndokkiraa nelaaɗo am doole, mo laɓɓinaa e torra heewka e nder ƴiiƴam am hisnoojam, o anndinii ɗee Konnguɗi no ndokkiraa nii, e goonga, ngam deeƴre ɗee konnguɗi fof ina njiɗaa. Konnguɗi Linjiila duumiika ɗii ndeenatee e nder musiibaaji e hareeji garooji, njiyata gite, noppi, e ɓerɗe ɓe neldaaɓe.

Ko ɗum waɗi, ɗoftoɗee Konnguɗi Masiya mon goonga, hay Yesuwa HaMasiya, an yo Israayiil, ngartiree e Yahweh Mawɗo Miin, e jaɓde am no Masiya mon goonga nii, sabu, goonga, goonga, on alaa waktu heddiiɗo ngam yeewtidde e tagɗo on walla Masiya on, mo rokkaama e yiɗde ngam hisnude on, tee goonga, hisnude fof ummoriiɓe e Aadama, jaɓɓe dokkal am nguurndam duumiingal.

Ndaaree! Waktuuji timminugol geɗe fof ngari, waktuuji am yurmeende e yurmeende timmii, e nokku mum en tikkere e ñaawoore ndarii jooni. Fof Israayiilnaaɓe jaɓɓe torra, ɗoftooɓe daande am, kamɓe tan hisnetee e nguurndam haa e laamu am duuɓi ujunere; ɗo ɓe ndarotoo no yimɓe keeriiɓe Yahweh nii, e faandaare goonga Israayiil no jawdi keeriindi e hoore mum nii dow yimɓe fof, tee ɗum waɗata no fodoranoo ko ɓooyi nii, mi addan fedde Laamɓe e Alkawalɓe, ɓe laamotoo e yamiroore am, hay e jappeere Daawuuda, e gila e ndeen wuro laaɓngo mawngo, aduna oo laamotoo e juuɗe Yesuwa HaMasiya e nuunɗal.

Ndaaree! Fof noddooɓe innde Joomiraawo, hay Iisaa Nasaraat haa e joofnirde, keɓat kisal, umminiree e njeenaari mawndi e teddungal e ummital ñaawooɓe e joofnirde yontere jeeɗiɗaɓere Daniyel, njeenaari maɓɓe ndee wonata ko mawnde!!

Ndaaree! Waktuuji ɗii ko jooni, kisal mon ina ɓadii.

Jam e mon! An yo Israayiil, jaɓɓo dokkal am jam ngal Yahweh rokki e yeeso Yesuwa HaMasiya, sabu alaa waktu goɗɗo, walla laawol goɗngol ngol ndokkaaka e feere hisnugol duumiinde Yahweh, ko ɗum waɗi, an yo Israayiil, jooni ko waktu hisnugol mon, jooni ko waktu hisnugol mon timmungol.

Miɗo yiɗi on, banndiraaɓe am, ko ɗum waɗi, poftoree e golle Yesuwa HaMasiya, mo Muusaa wuuri, mo cellal mum, haa poftoron e golle mon, njaɓɓoton aseteeji e juulɗe keewɗe e laamu am laaɓngu, nguu waktu mum ari. Goonga, goonga, waktuuji ɗii ko jooni!
