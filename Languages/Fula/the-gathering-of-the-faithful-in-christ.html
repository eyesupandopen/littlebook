Seedeeji ɗiɗo: Isra’iila
Ko Welɗi e ko Haɗɗi
10 jolal 2024

Haɓɓondiral Goonɗinɓe e Almasiihu!

Ndaaree, Ndaaree! Faamee ko woni e feɲɲineede e dow leydi.

Ndaaree, Ndaaree! Tigi rigi, tikkere Jawgel ngel arii e dow maa, an Babilon sirriijo. E no miin, Yeesu Almasiihu, mi rentiniri, non kadi ɗum waɗirtee!

Ndaaree! Ndaaree! Ñalawma oo arii e dow maa, an Babilon Suudiiɗo, ñalawma kiita maa arii, hay ñalawma mo maa feɲɲin ñalawma mawɗo oo, mo fodaano gila adan, hay ñalawma Joomiraaɗo oo! Bone mon, onon hoɗɓe e leydi, sabu tikkere mawnde no feewi woni e feɲɲineede e dow mon timmunde, on ndaɗataa, tigi rigi, yontere cappanɗe jeeɗiɗaɓere Daniyel ndee arii.

Ko ɗum waɗi miɗo wasiyoo eklesiya am, ɓanndu am, e deeyam boo, yo ɓe mbaɗtoo peewal am, ɓe njiɓinoo ngam daraade e peewal yeeso Joomiraaɗo mon, hay e nder jooɗorde laamu Yaawe, ɗo mi hollirta adanɓe coggu ɓee, hay maayɓe e Almasiihu ɓee fof, ɓe ndoomii ɓe ngoɗɗii noddaango am, e goonɗinɓe wuuruɓe e dow leydi e sahaa nde peeñan-mi ndee. E no nanndiri e no mi hollirinoo hoore am yeeso Yaawe, miin adiiɗo ummaade e maayɓe, non kadi on nanndintee e Joomiraaɗo mon duumiiɗo peewal oo. Sabu no mi yaltiniraa mi nawaa to dow kammuuli, non on nawatiree to dow kammuuli, ɗo mi hollirta adanɓe coggu am ɓee, tawa alaa toɓɓere, alaa ɲakkere, alaa hay huunde hono ɗum.

On fof on ndaroto yeeso jooɗorde kiita am, hay ndeen jooɗorde kiita mawnde nde golle mon kiitoytee, yoga e mon njobaama, yoga njootee, yoga kadi njootee njobe sabu leɗɗe, huɗo e gaɓɓule ɗee nulan, sibu hay gooto e mon yahraani timmunde yeeso am, kono e nder yiɗde Yaawe, on kaawtoroytoo mawngu ndonu mon. Annduɓe am, njobdi mon ina mawni, on ndonoyii kala huunde, no miin mi heɓiri nde njaafan-mi laamɓe bonɓe ɓee. Kono, fof hawrɓe e Joomiraaɗo mum en duumiiɗo oo, hay Yeesu Almasiihu, keɓan nguurndam kesam, nde ɓe tagoytee haa abada, on bonataa, on ngostataa timmal ngal heɗii on ngal, sabu Yaawe oo haalii ɗum!

E dow tampere on nanngete, aduna oo anndan mi peeñii, harkilo oo ittan! E nder ŋakkeende harkilo am oo, doole e semmbe bonannde fof feɲɲintee e dow leydi. Kanko woni miin, Yeesu Almasiihu, hay Almasiihu Yeesu, haaloowo Konnguɗi ɗii, ɗi nganndoytee e dow leydi, ɗi njottoyoo gite, noppi e ɓerɗe ɓe ɗi nelaa ɓee.

Ko noon woniri, ko noon wonirta, kono ko e sahaa seeɗa sanne, laamu Liingu nguu ummotoo haa timma, sabu ko ɗum rokkaa ɓe mbaɗa. Sabu nde mi salaa e yimɓe am, hay Isra'iila mo mi woni soklitɗo mum, mi waylitii to leƴƴi ngam yo leƴƴi barkinee, hay no Yaawe fodinoo gila adan. Konnguɗi am njaɓaama no feewi, hay e nder torra mawka e jarribooji keewɗi, baafe jahannama njaafaaki Eklesiya am. Kono, no Eklesiya am ittirtee nii, e harkilo am oo ittirtee, bonannde ndee heɓan leydi ndii fof, ɓe ndaɗataa.

Hay fof weltotooɓe e bonannde, saliiɓe Yeesu Nasarankeejo oo ngam suɓaade Barabas, so ɓe mbi'ii jam e kisal, ndeen halkere tampere ari, ɓe ndaɗataa. Kono Liingu nguu feɲɲintoo, o haɓɓaama e leydi, o riiwaama e kammuuli haa abada, o hootataa, omo jogii tikkere mawnde sabu o anndi waktu makko ina seeɗi, kono omo sikki e nder majjere makko omo waawi jaafde mi, miin Yeesu Almasiihu, hay so tawii mi jaafii mo jaafgol timmungol to Kalvaari e nder golle am kisinooje, joofirɗe “Gasii!”.

E won e laawol, laamɓe bonɓe ɓee cikkatnoo ina mbaawi daɗde kiita mum en fodaaka gila adan sabu ɓe kulii ko arata ɓurataa hay huunde; sabu ɓe paamii e nder bonannde maɓɓe, ɓe kollaani yurmeende, ɓe kollataa yurmeende. Sabu ɓe anndi ɓe kollaani yurmeende, ko ɗum waɗi yurmeende hollirtaake ɓe. Ɓe nganndi no feewi golle maɓɓe bonɗe e bonɗe ɗee fof, maa ndokke ɓe laabi keewɗi e nder ndiyamul jeeyngol ngol, ɗo yiite lepte ɗee ɲifataa haa abada. Hay yiite tagaaɗe ngam yoɓtude kala golle bonɗe ɗe ɓe mbaɗi, ɗe njoɓoyee e nder ɓerɗe maɓɓe, ɓe njoɓan haa timma, e nder mette mawɗe ɓe ngonoytoo haa abada.

Sabu ɓe njanii e Yaawe, ɓe njanii e gese Alla, hay no ɓe njirri wonde ko kamɓe ngoni allaaji, kiita maɓɓe arii. Kono ko noon woniri, laamɓe bonɓe ɓee naniini yimɓe ɓee e karallaagal mum en, e peeje mum en, e welwelooji bakkaat, e njoɓdi bonannde. Kono, ko yimɓe yiɗi niɓere ɓuri annoora, jooni, mi rokkoyan ɓe niɓere nde ɓe njiɗi ndee.

Sabu welwelo fof ittan e leydi, nguurndam bakkaat e nguurndam mawngu meeɗaa yiyeede e leydi hono no yimɓe mooftirta jawdi, ɓe acca banndiraaɓe maɓɓe e nder ngoƴa e bonannde maɓɓe, hay ɓe njiɗa ɓe njiɗata ɓe, ɓe taƴa ɓe e laawol maɓɓe ngam ɓeydude jawdi. Neɗɗo tagoore ina lutti e welwelooji bakkaat haa o ittataa hay hoore fowru ngam wallude miskineeɓe, ko ɗum waɗi mi ittataa hoore fowru ngam wallude mo daɗde kiita makko, sabu neɗɗo tagoore rokkaama jooni e juuɗe añɓe mum. Ko noon wonirta, geɗe ɗee njahataa e yontere cappanɗe jeeɗiɗaɓere Daniyel, meeɗaa waɗde kadi e laamu am peewal!

Kono so harkilo oo ittaama, bonannde suuɗiinde e nder aadee sabu peeje mbeewa ndee, jooni heɓan ndimaagu, jinnaaɓe keɓan leydi, musiɗɗo wara musiɗɗo, leñɗi kaɓa e leñɗi mum en, fitina mawka e wolde keɓan leydi. Kono ndee hiirde ko halkere e kiita Babilon sirriijo, maandeeji ɗii njiɗaa udditeede, pucci ɗii njahan!

Ko ɗum waɗi, ngoƴa mawka ina waɗa, no annabinaa. Sabu e sahaa nde peeñan-mi, nde heewɓe potnoo wonde e heɗaade e laɓɓinde hoore mum en e Konngol am e ruuhu am, e nokku ɗo ɓe timminata kisal maɓɓe e juuɗe am yiɗɗe, ɓe acci Joomiraaɗo maɓɓe, hay Almasiihu Yeesu, ngam Barabas mo ɓe ndokki ɓerɗe maɓɓe. E no Pons Pilat, mo fellitnoo yoɓde mi e fenaande maɓɓe farisiyankeeɓe e tuume maɓɓe, o suɓini yimɓe leydi ndii suɓngo. Pilat wiyi ɓe nde ɓe kawri ndee, hol mo njiɗɗon mi yooɓana on, Barabas, walla Yeesu biyeteeɗo Almasiihu? Ɓe mbiyi, Barabas. Pilat wiyi ɓe, hol ko mbaɗan-mi Yeesu biyeteeɗo Almasiihu? Ɓe fof ɓe mbiyi mo, yo o ferɓe.

Ɗum waɗi ko sabu Esawu huutorii semmbe mum bonɗo e dow yimɓe ngam yo ɓe njogoo waroowo e nokku mo nelanoo ngam kisal maɓɓe, hay Yeesu Nasarankeejo! Kono no lepte sariya Muusaa mbaɗirtee so wonaa e sadak am, e mette am ɓurɗe haaɗde, miin Jawgel Alla, ɗo mi rokkiri hoore am ngam bakkaatuuji aduna, miin mawɗo limanɓe haa abada e laawol Melkisedek. Kono, e nder ganndal Yaawe, o annduno no feewi Isra'iila maa salo Mesiya mum goonga oo, o huutorii añam-leñol e jalbugol laamɗo bonɗo oo e dow mum en, sabu so ɓe ngannduno sirru mawngu Alla, ɓe ferɓataa Joomiraaɗo mon duumiiɗo teddungal oo!

Kono ko noon woniri, Melkisedek Laamɗo Salem addi mburu e inaboojam: ko kanko woni Limanjo Alla Toowɗo oo. Abram waɗi sadakaaji mum no haanirta nii ngam teddinde Yaawe, ko noon Melkisedek woniri laawol goonɗinal, ngol wonnoo ko natal geɗe garooje. Sabu no winndiraa e Ibraahiima goonɗinɗo, mo wuuri e goonɗinal e Alla Toowɗo, e yeru makko, peewɓe ɓee maa nguure e goonɗinal haa abada! Ko noon kadi leƴƴi barkinaa, naatnaa e leƴƴi Isra'iila Alla, ɓe keɓii feccere timmunde e alkule baɗaaɗe e Baabiraaɓe, sabu ɓe njogaama e Yaawe e hoore mum, hay e juuɗe am doole, ko Alla laɓɓini, wotaa wi'ee ko huunde woore walla ko ko laaɓaani, an Isra'iila!

Kono ko ñalawma am arii. Mi renndataa teddungal am e goɗɗo, Alla neli majjere doole ngam yo ɓe ngoonɗin fenaande. Sabu rewɓe Barabas ɓee ko sabu caɗeele mum en, sabu ɓe cikkatnoo ko kamɓe ngoni feccere e sahaa kaŋŋe mum fodaaka, mo wonaa ngam maɓɓe, hay so o fenaani. Sabu sahaa kaŋŋe mo o fodi oo, tuugii ko e sahaa kaŋŋe laamɓe bonɓe ɓee e jamaanu Nuuhu, ɗo laamɓe bonɓe ɓee laaminoo leydi e bonannde! Ko ɗum waɗi, so Barabas haalii sahaa kaŋŋe, o haalii goonga e ɓerɗe maɓɓe, so wonaa ɓe njiɗi halkere timmunde Aadama ngam bonanndeeji maɓɓe e geɗe maɓɓe keɓa gese Alla haa abada. Ko noon woniri, jooni on paamii hol ko waɗi hay ɓanndu wuurndu daɗataa so Yaawe ɓuuɓnaaki ñalɗi ɗii.

Kono sahaa kaŋŋe alaa mo keɓan, sabu ko miin woni aawdi debbo fodaaka, mo maa hisin hoore mbeewa ndee haa abada, mi hisinan laamu makko fof, ɓe ndarotaako. Ko ɗum waɗi e nokku sahaa kaŋŋe mum en njiɗaaɗo oo, ɓe keɓan kiita tiiɗɗo, laamu maɓɓe halkee yeeso maɓɓe, meeɗaa ummaade kadi, maandeeji ɗii udditee, buubaaji ɗii puɗee, heddiiɓe am ɓee cuuɗoytoo, ndeen weenduuji tikkere timmunde Alla ɗii njimoyee, ɓe ngonoytoo e torra mawka e mette, hay so ɓe nji'ii foolde maɓɓe ina arda timmunde.

Ko ɗum waɗi, ɓeen rewɓe Barabas e nder majjere, hay so omo waɗa hoore makko no neliraaɗo Yaawe, o anndi no feewi ko o jeyaa e liingu, o suɓaama ngam ardude leydi ndii e aduna oo e daaba ladde ummoraade e maayo. Kono ko noon woniri, heewɓe naatɓe e juuɗe oo fenoowo, acci Almasiihu Yeesu, jooni maa ndokke e juuɗe makko so goonɗinɓe ɓee njaltinaama. Woodi ɓe tuumata mi accude Eklesiya am, kono ko ɓeen ngonaa e Almasiihu, meeɗaa heɓde kisal timmungal, acci mi, naati e Barabas, mi rentinnoo ɓe yo ɓe ngon e leɗɗel ngel, sabu so mi alaa, on mbaawataa hay huunde.

Hay so tawii heewɓe maa halke, ɓe inɗe mum en mbinndaaka e Deftere Nguurndam Jawgel ngel, ɓe halkan e leydi so kiita maɓɓe arii, e dow tampere, yaakaare maɓɓe fof halkee e nder seeɗa.

Sabu ɓe njogaaki yaakaare peewal!

Sabu ɓe njogaaki yaakaare nuunɗal!

Sabu ɓe njogaaki yaakaare senngo!

Sabu ɓe njoɓii yaakaare fof e hootde Joomiraaɗo maɓɓe, hay no mi fodinoo e Alkawal am Kesal, kala alkawal Yaawe e Yeesu Almasiihu maa timmin haa abada!

Sabu sahaa am moƴƴere e yurmeende arii haa joofii, hay so tawii moƴƴere e yurmeende ina njokki e am haa abada, e no winndiraa nii, maa waɗee, Yaawe e Yeesu Almasiihu fof maa njurmoo mo njiɗɗen njurmaade, hol baawɗo daraade yeeso jooɗorde laamu Yaawe, sabu ko kanko woni Alla Toowɗo, alaa nannduɗo e makko! Ko ɗum waɗi, faamee tikkere ndee no heɓii, no feɲɲinirtee e dow leydi, hay no jibinanɗe am njibini, annabinii, ñalawma oo arii!

Ko ɗum waɗi miɗo anndina peeñgol am, maa waɗee no winndiraa e binndi ceniiɗi, deeyam oo maa hawra e jom suudu mum haa abada, fof nanngeteeɓe to kammuuli e sahaa nde peeñan-mi, tawa ko wuuruɓe walla maayɓe, maa ndagee haa abada e nguurndam kesam duumiiɗam. Fof wonɓe e konnguɗi moƴƴi e haalaaji belɗi Barabas, maa njalto e hawrude am, no haakooji ɓuuɓɗi e bakkaat. Kono, heewɓe e ɓeen njaltuɓe maa ngar e am e nder jarribooji mawɗi e torra, heewɓe e maɓɓe maa noddu innde am haa joofnirde, yimɓe heewɓe maa njalto e yontere cappanɗe jeeɗiɗaɓere Daniyel ngam heɓde nguurndam duumiiɗam, hay so tawii ko ɓuri heewde maa njogoo maande daaba ladde, naata e yiite haa abada.

Ko ɗum waɗi, miin, Almasiihu Yeesu, hay Joomiraaɗo mon ummiiɗo, ummiiɗo, mi yamirii goonɗinɓe am yo njalto e maɓɓe, ɓe ceerta e maɓɓe haa timma. Njiɓinee, yiɗɓe am! Wota on naat e seppooji maɓɓe, ndaɗee e mooɓooje maɓɓe, e kala ko waawi wonde, ndaɗee e bonannde hawrude e ruuhu jam fenaande, sabu njanngu maɓɓe no heɓii. Faamee yiɗɓe am, so on naatii e maɓɓe, on naatii e pittirɗe maɓɓe. Yiɗɓe am, waktu oo arii yo on acca caɗeele aduna oo, sabu nguurndam mon ina suuɗii e am, miin Almasiihu Yeesu, ina feɲɲinee haa timma, ngam on ngannda no on nganndiraa.

Ko ɗum waɗi, faamee fenaande wonnde e dow mon, yiɗɓe am, cuuɗee, ngoɗɗee peeñgol am, sabu ina heɓii! Faamee fenaande maɓɓe, jawgel ngel jogiingel galaaɗi ɗiɗi ngel maa waɗa hoore mum gaññiiɗo hoore, o peeñan no ummiiɗo e maayɓe, o sellii e gaññiiɗo hoore makko, kono fof ko o hollirta ngam tabbıtinde fenaande makko wonde ko kanko woni Moshiya maɓɓe, ko maandeeji e kaawniiɗi fenaande, hay Yaawe mawɗo oo, miin, Yeesu Almasiihu, min nelaani oo fenoowo, hay so o accaama o arda e waktu makko ngam o laamdoo e daaba ladde ummoraade e maayo, e liingu nguu, ko ɓe ardiiɓe, ko ɗum rokkaa ɓe mbaɗa e peeje duumiiɗe Yaawe ngam soklitde Aadama!

Ko ɗum waɗi miɗo wi'a on, yiɗɓe am, yo on ngoɗɗo e muñal, wota on ngoonɗin fenaande maɓɓe, sabu alaa gooto, alaa hay huunde baawnde haɗde peeñgol am ngam goonɗinɓe Eklesiya am, hay so ɓe njiɗii suuɗde ndee hiirde e njanngu jananɓe fenaande e wolde aduna ndee, ndimaande fof wolde meeɗnde waɗde e leydi. Kono, wolde maɓɓe ndee wonataa hay huunde so yerondiraama e wolde ñalawma mawɗo oo, ɗo ɓe halkoytee haa timma so mi arii e leydi e konuuli kammu, sabu e peewal miɗo ara ngam hiitaade e waɗde wolde!

Miin, Yeesu Almasiihu, mi neli ɗee Konnguɗi e juuɗe jeyaaɗo am, mo woni nelaaɗo am goonɗinɗo, ko miin, Yeesu Almasiihu, tabbıtini seedeeji ɗii, rokkaaɗi e Linjiila Duumiiɗo, feɲɲinaaɗi e waktu mum.

Ko miin woni mo jooni waylitotoo to Isra'iila ngam soklitde ɓe e leydi, miin soklitɗo maɓɓe, miɗo nodda on, an Isra'iila, sabu waktu oo arii yo on njaɓ Mesiya mon goonga oo, sabu no oo fenoowo janfiri, o halkiri Babilon sirriijo, non o janfiri yimɓe leydi ndii e bonannde makko halkunde ndee, ndee no arda.

Ko ɗum waɗi, faamee ɓe naɓoytoo e buŋɗi maɓɓe ngam suuɗaade e kaaƴe leydi, heewɓe maayoytoo toon. Ko ɗum waɗi miɗo naamnoo, hol to neɗɗo waawi suuɗaade ngam daɗde gite am duumiiɗe, walla hol baawɗo daɗde gite Yaawe, sabu fof ina yiyee, ina anndaa, kala ko waɗata e leydi ina accaa waɗee, tawa ko e kiita walla ngam kisal ɓe noddata innde am haa joofnirde, walla ɓe njaɓata mi ko mi Mesiya maɓɓe, fof waɗɓe bonannde maa njoɓee haa timma.

Ko miin woni mo maa peeñu e dow tampere e kammuuli e teddungal mawngal, ɓe nganndan, heewɓe paaman ɓe njoɓii ndee hiirde gootel, ɓe cikkatnoo no feewi ɓe jeyaa, kono ɓe salanaama sabu nguurndam maɓɓe bakkaat, sabu bakkaat meeɗaa naatde e kammuuli, meeɗaa daraade yeeso am.

Ndaaree! Waktuuji ɗii arii, jooni miɗo yaha ngam hawrude goonɗinɓe.

Ndaaree! Ndeenee! Keɗee! On njiɓini, yiɗɓe am? Ñalawma oo arii e dow mon.
