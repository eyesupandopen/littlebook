Seedeeji ɗiɗi: Ekklesiya
E nder Golle Yuhanna Wahyudanke
23 Mee, 2024

Nanee Konnguɗi Am, paamon Duuɓi ɗi Wuurɗon!

Nanee Konnguɗi am, yimɓe am, hay on fuu ɓe noddatmi, paamon duuɓi ɗi nguurɗon. Sabu aduna mo annduɗon jooni oo maa fuɗɗo, e nokku mum, nokkuuji keewɗi ɗi ngalaa nguurndam maa heddoo, hay mbuuldi e bonanndeeji Babilon Sirri, sabu hare arii. No mi nanngirta Teegal am faade e asamaanuuji, masiibo mawngo maa muumo Babilon Sirri, o halkete e waktu gooto, e waktu gooto o halkete, alaa ko waylata daɗndal makko, sabu Yahweh hollitii ɗum.

Sabu aan, Babilon Sirri, a salii daande am, a woppii mi, Iisaa Almasiihu Nuunɗuɗo, ngam welwelooji hakke. Sabu hay jeertinaali mawɗi ɗi ndokkaa e leydi maa ndii e nder asamaanuuji, ko ngam weltinde ɓerɗe mooɗon ɗe ngalaa goonɗinal e gite mooɗon ɗe njulaare tan, wonande mooɗon ko ñalawma goɗɗo tan no nguurnirɗon e welwelo e hakkeeji mooɗon ardiiɗi on e halkere. Ko foti wonde ko on tuubunoo!

Sabu on laatiima leñol sanamuuji, leñol haɓooɓe, leñol nguurngotongol e fenaande, leñol yiɗngol delkooji mum, leñol hebbintinongol leydi ndii e bonanndeeji mooɗon; leƴƴi ɗii ina njara e kaakol mooɗon kaŋŋeewol heewungol bonanndeeji e tuundi fornication mooɗon.

Ko ɗum waɗi, miɗo haɓa e mooɗon, halkere mooɗon arii, fuɗɗiima kadi, sabu on mbaɗii hoore mooɗon ko on nganndaa noddaango Yahweh ngo mi hokkirani on e juuɗe am, Yeshua HaMasiihu, miɗo hisnannoo on. Mi wullanii laabi mooɗon ɗi ɓerɗe tiiɗɗe, kono haa jooni on njaɓaani woppude bone; ko ɗum waɗi mi huccitii e mooɗon, duuɓi Aljanna en ngari haa timma no feewi nde mi nanngata goonɗinɓe e nder Ekklesiya am e noddaango buuton.

Kono on ko leñol ngol jolɗinaama, on halkete haa laaɓa, on ummotaako haa abada. Nde Teegal am nanngaama, aduna oo maa haawnude no feewi e ko ɓe keɓi, sabu ndee hiirde waɗataa e cuuɗi, kono e peeñcu, haa aduna oo fof annda wonde Alla ina woodi e asamaan, o nelii mi, Iisaa Almasiihu, ngam kaŋŋe, kaalis, e kaaƴe tedduɗe leydi ndii. Sabu jawdi Baaba ɓurndi teddude, hay peerlaaji tedduɗi, maa itte e ñaawoore e halkere garoore ndee.

Sabu Teegal am hisnaama e tikkere sabu sadak am, mi hisnan goonɗinɓe e ɓe njiytetenooɓe e goonɗinal e nder Almasiihu e sahaa nde mi feeñata e denndaangal maayɓe e goonɗinal e nder Almasiihu, hay ɓe mi maantiniri, Iisaa Almasiihu, sabu mi waɗaama ruuhu hokkoowo nguurndam. Mi umminan maayɓe e nder Almasiihu, ɓeen wonɓe e nguurndam, heddiiɓe ɓee, maa ndennda e maɓɓe haa ɓe kawra e am e duule. Nii woni, paamon wonde ndee hiirde ko ngam yoɓde goonɗinɓe e timminde dewgal Teegal e Teegal am, sabu men kawray haa abada e alkule, ɓe ceertataa e am haa abada. Kadi ko miin woni baɗoowo ɓe laamɓe e almuɓɓe yeeso Baaba am.

Ko noon ɓe njaltiri leydi ndii, ko noon aduna oo yi’irta, heewɓe maa kaawnude no feewi. Sabu, feccere e dalillaaji ndee hiirde ko ngam addude tuubugol e nder heddiiɓe ɓee, hay ɓeen saliiɓe noddaango am e jamaanu moƴƴere, kadi heewɓe ɓe nannaani haa laaɓi ngam goonɗinde ɓe hisnatee e sahaa caɗeele Yakuuba, haa teeŋti noon e yimɓe leydi alkule.

Sabu miɗo nodda on, aan Isra'iila: on keɓii nande daande Masiihu mooɗon goonga, Liwru leñol Yahuuda? Sabu daande Masiihu mooɗon goonga ina nodda, ko miin woni laamɗo e jappeere laamu Daawuuda e nder wuro laaɓngo, hay miin, Yeshua HaMasiihu; woto ngoppu konnguɗi noddaango am! Ko noon jamaanu moƴƴere, hay jamaanuuji fooftere, ina udditoo haa laaɓa. Ko noon timmal ngal timmirta so mi mooɓii goonɗinɓe Ekklesiya am haa e am e nder duule, so yaltugol maɓɓe ngol dimmbii aduna oo fof.

Maa ɗum dimmbina leydi ndii tigi-rigi, dimmbungal mawngal maa muumo Babilon Sirri, o feccee hakkunde mum so seniiɓe am njaltii. O wuliree yiite ummoraade e asamaan so misilaaji ɗii njippii. Koode makko maa kebbina ndiyam ummoraade fuɗnaange e hirnaange! Sabu maleyka doole maa hooyu kaaƴel hono kaaƴel niisirde mawngel, werboo ɗum e maayo, o wiyee: Nii woni, Babilon wuro mawngo ngoo maa werbeede e doole, yiyeetaako haa abada. Sabu ko noon winndiraa, ko noon waɗirtee!

Sabu ñaawoore makko ina mawni, ñaawoore fuɗɗiima e galle Alla. Eklesiyaaji mum bonɗi maa calɗe, sabu mi ñaaawii ɗi fof ɗi ngonaani e am, hay eklesiyaaji fenaande ɗi kaalata fenaande mawnde e naafikaare. Babilon Sirri, ee, so a artunoo e am, Iisaa Almasiihu, e laabi maa bonɗi, mi hisnannoo ma, miin e Yahweh min ngalaa welwelo e ñaawoore nde arii ndee.

Kono non, ñaawoore maa jippo no holliraa, ñaawoore ndee maa tiiɗ, duumotoo haa abada. Sabu mi huccitii e Aljanna en fotde saha, jooni mi huccitan e Aljanna en, mi huccitan e durngol ngol mi ummii, hay Isra'iila. Kono kala nodduɗo innde Joomiraawo haa timma maa hisne, tawa ko Yahuudiyanke walla Aljanna, sabu hisnugol ngol haɗaaka e keeri leydi.

Ee, Babilon Sirri, ponndaaji maa maa njippoo; laabi maa maa mbayle, taƴe, njahataa; laabi mooɗon ina njirbii, ɓooyataa ɗi ngalaa hay huunde. Alaa kaɓirɗe keɓɗe on, sabu alaa garɗo wallude on, on njooɗodoo e weertare mooɗon, ee, debbo mawɗo jahoowo. On keɓataa weltaare, ɓeen wonɓe e nguurndam maa njiytu koye mum en ina kaɓa tan ngam heɓde ndiyam. Heege maa heɓto leydi ndii. Kadi ñawu mawngu maa heɓto on, hay daabaaji maa kaɓa, sabu duuɓi ɗii ngarii.

Golle boɗeeje ɗe laamu mooɗon mbonngu acci e keeri mooɗon maa mbardu kala gorko, debbo, e suka mo fotaani e anniyaaji mum en. Sabu ɓe mbardan, ɓe maccina kala mo ɓe njiyti. Ko ɓe konu daaba ngo mooɓaama gila e pelle nay aduna ngam ndee anniya, kadi daaba cuuɗiiɗo e annabi fenaande cuuɗiiɗo mooɓii ɓe.

Ɓe ngalaa yurmeende, ɓe ngalaa yurmeende, ɓe ngalaa moƴƴere, ɓe ngalaa nimsitaare, ɓe añii haa laaɓi hoɗɓe Babilon Sirri sabu bonanndeeji bonɗi ɗi ardiiɓe mooɗon mbaɗi e leyɗeele maɓɓe. Kadi e nder ɗuum fof, heewɓe e mooɗon njaɓii hareeji mooɗon ɗi ngonaa nuunɗuɗi no on nji’irta e weltaare e teleeji mooɗon, no on nji’irta e weltaare mawnde e hakkeeji ardiiɓe mooɗon.

On ndokkii ardiiɓe mooɗon jaɓgol mooɗon ɗi on njaɓi hakkeeji mum en e woote, ko ɗum waɗi on ndenndan e ñaawoore mum en, sabu on mbaɗii koye mooɗon ko on poti heɓde feccere ɗiɗi e ñaawoore, nde keɓtotoo on e dow ŋakkere.

Ko noon Teegal am feeñirta e jappeere am ñaawoore, yoga e maɓɓe maa ndañ bonannde, yoga maa ndañ njoɓdi mawndi, fof maa keɓa timmal njoɓdi nde ɓe keɓnoo ndee: Sabu mi rokkata ɓe ɓalli kesi, ɗi ngalaa hakke, ɗi bonnataa haa abada! Ko noon maantorɗe ɗee udditortee, sabu hay so tawii caɗeele e mette keewii e leydi ndii duuɓi keewɗi jooni, ɗe ngonaa tikkere Alla, ɗe ngonaa tikkere Jibraa’iil.

Heewɓe e mooɗon e nder majjere ina cikki ina poti muñde tikkere Alla, ina cikki e mawnikinaare ina mbaawi dañde ko ari. Paamon wonde mi hisnii Eklesiya am e tikkere, sabu mi arii e am e Aljanna en e sahaa moƴƴere hono nanondiral jam, miɗo rokka jam e nanondiral e Baaba Annoora, hay El Shaddai! Kadi ɓeen ɓe mi hisni ɓee, e dow yiɗde mum en, njaɓii moƴƴere am nde alaa njoɓdi, ko ɗum waɗi rewee e misaalu maɓɓe!

Mi maantinan 144 000 am e leƴƴi garooji ɗii, hay 12 000 e kala e ɗii ɗoo: Yahuuda, Ruuben, Gaad, Aser, Naftali, Manase, Simiyon, Lewi, Isakar, Zebulon, Yuusuf, e Benyamin. Nii woni, nanee daande am!

On mbaɗanan mi golle mawɗe keewɗe, on arditan heewɓe e hisnugol e nder aduna maayɗo, keewɗo fitina. Sabu Seytaane heɓiima delko mawngo, o waɗan ko o waawi fof ngam fenaade, suuɗde, e suuɗde mooɓgol mawngol ngol arii e nder duule. Fenaande makko ina rewi e hakkillaaji mooɗon e ɓerɗe mooɗon rewrude e jaŋde mooɗon Jesuit en saliiɓe Baaba am, nde ummii e juuɗe majjuɓe. Ɓe mbaɗii fof ko fenaande, hay ardude heewɓe e mooɗon e goonɗinde wonde dow leydi ndii ko weeyo ngo alaa keerol, ngo heewi palanneeji e nguurndam goɗɗam, heewɓe maa njahra e ŋakkeende haa ɓe njoppa ɗee fenaande. Hay ɓeen ɓe inɗe mum en winndaaka e Deftere Nguurndam, sabu ɓe njaɓan maande mum en.

Anndee, yimɓe am, nanee jeertinaango am. Alaa nguurndam e palanneeji goɗɗi! Janngude ngam anndude, sabu palanneeji ko koode eggooje ɗe Alla yamiri ngam hollitde golle juuɗe Yahweh e nder weeyo les firmament. Ɓee ngonaa jom en leyɗeele goɗɗe ɗe fenaande ina taaroo; kono, ɓe ngonaa e leydi, sabu ko ɓe maleykaaji majjuɗi ummoriiɓi e asamaanuuji, ɓe mbaɗii koye mum en bonanndeeji keewɗi. Woto goonɗinee ɓe!

Ko noon e nder mbuuldi puccu e halkere mawnde, daaba e annabi fenaande ummotoo, sabu sirlu bonannde ina golloo. Sabu Babilon Sirri rokkiraama e juuɗe maɓɓe e kawral ngal sosaa, hay luuɓe sappo ɗee maa calde ɗum, sabu ko ɓe laamɓe sappo rokkuɓe fof e mum en daaba, daaba, e annabi fenaande. Sabu so gardiiɗo oo ittaama, ɓe ummotoo haa laaɓa no winndiraa e Deftere; sabu e nder caɗeele, njuɓɓudi maɓɓe hesere ummotoo, hay njuɓɓudi Dragon, sabu ko kañum woni doole e laamu maɓɓe, sabu ko noon rokkaa ɗum e laawol keeriingol fotde waktuuji seeɗa.

Liyel ngel ina jogii tikkere mawnde, ngel yahrata e ruuhu Haamaan ngam haɗde kala ko jowiti e Almasiihu, e kala jokkondirɗo e innde am, hay Iisaa Almasiihu, o fawete, o ware. Eɓe ngoni hisɓe, ɓe keɓa njoɓdi mawndi, ɓeen ɓe njiɗaa nguurndam mum en haa laakara. Ndeke, toppitee weltaare wonnde yeeso mon, sabu ko nii mi heɓirnoo on hisnude e nder torra muusɗo mo ngonno-ɗon, so wonaa mi jaɓanii on kuugal mon, mi yoɓi bakkaatuuji aadee gooto fof laawol gootol, haa pooma.

Kullel ngel yahrat no yaawi, sabu ngel jokkat lebbi 42 ina haala haalaaji mawɗi tufooje. Ngel haɓata e seniiɓe, rokkaama kadi ngel foola ɓe. Ngel taƴat ko'e heewɓe. Ngel waɗat maandeeji e kaayɗeeɗi fenaande heewɗi, e nder ndoondi ndiin, laamu mum kesu ummortoo. Annabiijo fenaande oo hollirat mo, yetta mo, waɗa yimɓe fof mawnina natal makko, sabu ɗiin geɗe mbinndaama no laaɓiri e nder Deftere Alla. Nii woni, kullel ngel laɓɓinta aduna e kala ko moƴƴi, ko laaɓi, ko teddini. Kono, mi suuɗat goonɗinɓe am, ɓe mbaɗat kaayɗeeɗi heewɗi, heewɓe kadi hisat.

Kono annabiijo fenaande oo, hono kullel ngel ummotoongel e leydi ndii, waɗat yimɓe fof, mawɓe e famɓe, alɗuɓe e miskineeɓe, rimɓe e maccuɓe, yo ɓe njaɓ maande e juuɗe maɓɓe ñaame malla e tiiɗe maɓɓe: Mbele hay gooto waawa soodde malla yeeyde, so wonaa jogiiɗo maande ndee, malla innde kullel ngel, malla limoore innde maggel. Paamee non, wonde tawtoreede renndo maɓɓe keso fenaande ngoo ina foti maande, kala jaɓɗo maande ndee, yaafaaka abadan. Kamɓe kam, geɗal maɓɓe wonat ko e weendu mawndu ndu yiite e sufre wuldata haa abada, nii kadi curki torra maɓɓe ɓeydotoo.

Seedeeji am ɗiɗo ɓee ummoto, ɓe laatoo annabaaɓe ɓe ɓoornii saakuuji! Ɓeen ngoni leɗɗe oliiw ɗiɗi ɗee, e lampaaji ɗiɗi dariiɗi yeeso Joomiraaɗo leydi ndii. Hay gooto waawataa daranaade ɓe, sabu ɓe laato annabaaɓe lebbi 42. So neɗɗo yiɗi lorlaade ɓe, yiite yaltat e kunuɗe maɓɓe, ngoonda ayɓe maɓɓe: so neɗɗo yiɗi lorlaade ɓe, ko nii o foti wardeede. Ɓeen ina njogii baawɗe uddude kammu, mbele toɓo toɓataa e balɗe annabaaku maɓɓe: ina njogii kadi baawɗe e ndiyam ngam wattitinde ngam yiiƴam, e fiyde leydi ndii kala masiibooji, no ɓe njiɗiri fof.

Nde ɓe timmini seedanfaagu maɓɓe ndee, kullel ngel ƴeftoongel e ngaska lugguɗo ka haɓata e maɓɓe, foola ɓe, wara ɓe. Ɓe acca ɓalli maɓɓe e nder laabi balɗe tati e feccere, aduna oo fof weltoto, ndeen ɓe umminoyte, ɓe ƴettee to kammuuli.

Annabiijo fenaande oo feññinaniino hoore mum yimɓe leydi ndii haa ɓe cikkat ko kañum woni Almasiihu maɓɓe e laamɗo maɓɓe. Ko mahoowo suudu dewal fenaande, o ummorii ko e leñol Dan, wonaa o Sirus. O wallirat kullel gadano ngel ummotoongel e maayo ngoo. Sabu kullel gadano ngel doolnat sariyaaji Nuuhu e dow leyɗeele ɗee e nder bonannde mawnde, annabiijo fenaande oo laamoto e jooɗorgal ngal wonaa ngal mum, sabu ko ngal am, hono ngal Yeesuwaa HaMasiyaak. Nii woni, suudu dewal nduu mahirtee, annabiijo fenaande oo waɗat maandeeji e kaayɗeeɗi fenaande heewɗi, sabu o rokkaama doole no kullel gadano ngel nii, nii ɓe ɗiɗo fof mbaɗdata miijo gooto e anniya gooto, hay e miijo e anniyaaji liyel fenaande mawngel ngel, kono ngel alaa nafoore, ngel alaa ƴiiƴe, wota on kulu ngel!

Sabu ina anndi miɗo ara ngam halkude laamu mum, sabu wonaa o laamɗo kono ko gaño foolaaɗo haa laaɓi, mo alaa yaakaare sam. Wota on kulu mo, njokkee e am tan. Sabu ndaa, miɗo haalana on ɗiin geɗe hade mum en waɗde mbele on ngoonɗinan mi, on ngoonɗinan Baaba am nelɗo mi oo, hono Yahwe Joomiraaɗo mawɗo duumotooɗo oo, e miin ɓiyum, Yeesuwaa HaMasiyaak, timminoowo alkule baɗanooɗe maamiraaɓe ɓee.

Nii woni, e joofnirde lebbi 42 gadani ɗii, ko nyidduɗum halkoojum waɗata e juuɗe annabiijo fenaande oo, hono kullel ngel ummotoongel e leydi ndii. E nder lebbi 42 ɗiɗmi ɗii, e nder oon sahaa, fitina ɓurɗo mawnude abada waɗata, e ko nyidduɗum halkoojum, o wiyata ko kanko woni Alla! Heewɓe mursinte, heewɓe kadi halkoto, kono ɓeen ɗoftiiɓe daande am, nawete to nokku e jeereende ɗo ɓe suuɗetee, ɓe ndeeniree dumunna, sabu ɗiin ngoni sahaaji ɓurɗi saɗtude, ɗiin sahaaji jarribooji e ndaartagol ngoni ɓurɗi tiiɗde. Ndeen on njiyata kammuuli udditii, on njiyata miin, Iisaa Almasiihu, miɗo ardoo e kammuuli, konu mawngu teddungu kammu nguu, ngona konu ngu alaa yeru mum, abada! Sabu Be'am oo wonanndoo e am. Mi mursinat kullel ngel e annabiijo fenaande oo e konuuji maɓɓe fof e nyidduɗi maɓɓe fof ɗi ɓe mbaɗi, nii kadi ɓe halkirtee jalbugol arde am e kaafaahi ɓeyɗoongol gande ɗiɗi yaltowohi e hunuko am, ɓe ndarotaako kono ɓe halkete haa laaɓa. Kullel ngel e annabiijo fenaande oo, werletee e nguurndam e nder weendu yiite e sufre wulndu nduu, ɓe rimɗintaake e torra maɓɓe abada, sabu ko kamɓe mbaɗi hoore maɓɓe.

Ndeen ummital nuunɗuɓe waɗata, nde seniiɓe adanɓe ɓee e seniiɓe fitina ɓee ummintee e maayde ngam heɓde nguurndam kesam, ɓe maayataa kadi, mi rokkat ɓe njoɓdi mawndi, ndi hay yitere yiyaani, nofru nanaani, ndi naataani e ɓernde neɗɗo e oo sahaa. Sabu miin ko mi Joomiraaɗo yurmeende, mi Laamɗo yurmeende, miin woni keewal Allaagal e nder ɓanndu.

Nii woni, laamu am duuɓi ujunere e dow leydi fuɗɗotoo, nuunɗal laamoto e dow leydi, mi laamoto leƴƴi ɗii e sawru njamndi, leydi ndii heewat laabi Yahwe. Sabu miɗo ara e laamɓe e almaamɓe heewɓe, ɓe laamoto e jooɗorgal am, ɓe laamoto e nuunɗal. Gajal lelat e mbaala.

Miɗo nodda on yo on paam sahaaji ɗi ngonɗon ɗii.

Miin ko mi ruuhu hokkoowo nguurndam, sabu ko noon mi waɗiraa.

Ko miin waɗata ɓe laamɓe e almaamɓe yeeso Baaba am.

Miɗo nodda on, an yo Isra'iila.

Miɗo ara ngam halkude laamu Seyɗaani, sabu wonaa o laamɗo.

Miin ko mi Joomiraaɗo yurmeende, mi Laamɗo yurmeende.

Miin woni keewal Allaagal e nder ɓanndu.

Miɗo ara e laamɓe e almaamɓe heewɓe, ɓe laamoto e jooɗorgal am, ɓe laamoto e nuunɗal.

Miɗo nodda on; wota tan on ndutto e noddaango am!
