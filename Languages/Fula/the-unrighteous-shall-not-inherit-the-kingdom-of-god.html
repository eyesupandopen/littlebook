Seedeeji ɗiɗi: Ekklessiya
E nder Golle Piyeer
19 Abriil, 2024
Dille 5 & 6

Tooñooɓe Ndonataa Laamu Alla!

Gila e Yeshua HaMashiach:

Ɓiɗɗo am, so a jaɓii konnguɗi am, a suuɗii yamiraaɗe am e maa; haa a nanana ñiiɓal, a naattina ɓernde maa e faamu. Woto tiiɗnoo ɓerɗe mon, wano e ñalawma fitina e jarribo e jeereende: anndee miin, Joomiraawo Iisaa Almasiihu, miɗo wondi e mon faa abada, miɗo woodi e nguurndam mon so on njogitii kam e kisndam am, sabu ko miin woni hooreejo Ɓanndu ndu, miin woni Joomiraawo mon koolaaɗo, onon Giɗaaɓe am. Mi heblanii on nokku e ko arata, njoɓdi mawndi woodanii noddaaɓe am, koolaaɓe am, cuɓaaɓe am.

Mi nodaaki on ngam halkere e lepte, mi nodaaki on ngam hersa e tooñugol hoore mon; on njanngaali naa wonde alaa tooñannge e wonɓe e Iisaa Almasiihu, ɓe njahdataa e ko ɓanndu yiɗi kono e ko Ruuhu yiɗi? Ndeen, ngoonɗinee konnguɗi am! Mi nodaaki on ngam nguuroton e dow junuubaaji mon ɓooyɗi, walla e dow golle mon ɗe ngollunooɗon e cukaagu mon, kono mi noddi on ngam maayde e laabi mon bonɗi ɓooyɗi, njogitoo nguurndam kesam e am so on mberliima ɓanndu mon, so on mberliima junuubaaji mon, on njaha e nguurndam kesam e am. Gite! Miɗo waɗa kala huunde hesere! Wonaa ɗum naa woni yaakaare mon?

Ɓiɓɓe am, mi ittii on e bonannde aduna oo mo maayata, mi hisnii on e doole niɓɓere, mi naatinii on e laamu am, laamu Alla, ardinaangu e am, Laamɗo Laamɓe e Joom Joomiraaɓe, mo Yahweh woni Alla mum, sabu alaa goɗɗo. Mi noddii on e teddungal e moƴƴere e am, mi daɗndii on e laamu Seyɗaani, laamu niɓɓere, faade e Laamu am, ɗo annoora woni! Ndeen, ummo, an mo ɗaaniiɗo, ummo e maayɓe, miin Iisaa Almasiihu, mi okkay ma annoora.

On njanngaali naa e ɓataakeeji ɗi neldirnoomi Giɗaaɓe am, ɗi neldirnoomi on, Ekklessiya am? On anndaa naa wonde tooñooɓe ndonataa laamu Alla? Woto njiwee: wanaa jeenooɓe, wanaa rewooɓe tooruuji, wanaa jeenooɓe rewɓe worɓe woɗɓe, wanaa worɓe rewɓe, wanaa worɓe wonnduɓe e worɓe, wanaa wuyɓe, wanaa cimtooɓe, wanaa yaruɓe haa mbaalda, wanaa jattooɓe, wanaa hisnooɓe jawdi, ndonataa laamu Alla. E yoga e mon ko noon ngonnooɗon: kono on laɓɓinaama, on laɓɓintinaama, on fonnditaama e Innde Joomiraawo Iisaa, e Ruuhu Alla men.

On paamataa naa yamiraaɗe am, onon ekklessiya Lawdisiya mo ɓuuɓɗo? Mi nodaaki on ngam artude e ɓanndu mon, mi nodaaki on ngam jokkude e laabi mon ɗi ngonaa fonnduɗi. Miijee, ciftoree; kala jiɗɗo dariima, yo reen woto o feyyo! Reenee ko'e mon, e diine: njokkee e majjum: sabu so on mbaɗii ɗum, on hisnan hoore mon, e hisnan ɓe nanata on. Paamee, onon ɓiɓɓe am ɓuuɓɓe, wonde on mbaawaa wuurdude nguurndam kawral hakkunde moƴƴere e bone, hiɗon cikki on fonndii yeeso am, tawi hiɗon cuɓoo ko ɓuri hoyde e boneeji ɗiɗi ɗi e cuɓi mon ɗi mi jaɓaani, ɗi mi wallitaaki.

Yaltee e makko, yimɓe am, sabu mi roccaani safaara dawrugol, mi roccaani on ardiiɗo aduna ngam waɗde hay huunde mawnde kadi. On njiwaama, hiɗon heblanee ngam rewde e jaɓde ɓiyum halkere mo yahdata e mon udditii, hay so tawii o feeñnaaka tawo. Kono onon ɓe njiɗata faamu, on anndan mo o woni, ngam on ndoola njiyaagu makko. Alaa, kawral wonaa ngam mon, sabu onon ko on ɓiɓɓe am e miñiraaɓe am, mi heɓaani kisndam mon e rokkitde nguurndam am ngam hisnude heewɓe e waɗde kawral e Seyɗaani; sabu waɗde kawral firti ko naatde e fedde maɓɓe. Ndeen, woto njiwee: walla on ndaran kam e fonndugol e senuyeendi goonga, walla on naatii e fedde Seyɗaani e nder laamu mum maayngu, ndeen TUUBEE!

Hol ko keɓoton e waɗde kawral e bone? On paamataa naa wonde ko foti e mon ko foolirde bone e moƴƴere? Ndeen ndaree e am, accitee ɗannude no liingu yalti ndiyam. Heewɓe e mon ina njeftiree kala henndu jannginol e fenaande juuɗe, ɗo ɓe ndaarii ngam njiwde. Miin, Almasiihu Iisaa, mi haalii, mi deƴƴintetaake. Heewɓe e mon mbaɗii kawral e hoore mon e ekklessiya am, miɗo naamnoo on: haa honnde tiempo on cikkat hakkunde miijooji ɗiɗi? So tawii ko Joomiraawo woni Alla, rewdee mo: so tawii ko Ba'al, rewdee mo. E konngol gootol, on mbaawaa waɗde kawral e suɓaade kala sifaa bone, cikka hiɗon ndari e am; so on cuɓii bone, on cuɓii iwdiiji oo bone ngam wonde joomiraawo mon e mawɗo mon, laatoo maccuɗo junuuba. On anndaa naa wonde, kala mo ndokkuɗon hoore mon maccuɗo ngam ɗoftaade, ko on maccuɓe makko mo ɗoftoton: walla ko junuuba haa e maayde, walla ko ɗooftaare haa e fonndugol?

Nanaam, nanaam no feewi, Ekklessiya am; anndee miɗo darii e ñidde golle ɗe keewi e leydi mon e aduna oo, ɗe mbaɗɗon kawral e ɗe naatnuɗon e Ekklessiya am ngam torraade ɓiɓɓe am. Hono no ñalɗi Nuuhu nii, ko noon wonirta. Ñalɗi Nuuhu ko wakkati mo aduna oo laaminoo e bonanndeeji ñidduɗi baɗaaɗi e malaa'ikaaɓe feyyuɓe, iwdi maɓɓe, iwdi bone baɗaandi ngam halkere mon timmunde: ɓe ngartan, sabu gite, miɗo adda yimɓe mawɓe ngam timminde tikkere am. Ɓe mbaɗiri ɗum ko e ittude e luutndaade keerol ngol Baaba am waɗi ngam deeƴre e fonndugol mbaawa heɓeede, kono e ittude e taƴde e laabi bonɗi keerol ngol feeñi e tagooje e hoore mum, ngol Baaba am sosi, heewɓe e mon tuunniima e hoore mon e jaɓde bone keewɗo e jamaanu mon. Ko wonnoo, ko ɗum wonata; ko waɗaa, ko ɗum waɗatee; alaa huunde hesere les naange. On njanngaali naa, onon ɓe ngoonga mum en famɗi? Ittude keerol Alla fof ina addana jinnaaɓe, e ñawu, e cakkitagol kala huunde. Heewɓe e mon ina njiɗi ñawu hakkille, ina cikki gorko waawan laataade debbo, debbo waawan laataade gorko, hiɗon ardina ɓiɓɓe mon e miijooji mon. Heewɓe e mon ina njaha e ekklessiyaaji ardinaaɗi e almuudo'en fenaande rewɓe worɓe, ɓe udditii, ɓe njiɗi ñawu maɓɓe, hiɗon cikki ko kamɓe kaaldata e am? Anndee ɗum: so on ngoni e oon ngonka junuuba, mi rokkay on yiɗde nde njiɗɗon no feewi, on keɓan ñamri laabi mon tuunɗi.

Gite! Sabu ɗuum, Alla acci ɓe e yiɗde bonɗe: hay rewɓe maɓɓe mbayli kuutoragol tagoore faade e ko luutndii tagoore: Noon ne worɓe, acci kuutoragol tagoore debbo, njani e yiɗde maɓɓe hakkunde maɓɓe, worɓe e worɓe ina ngolla ko boni, ina keɓa e hoore maɓɓe njoɓdi luural maɓɓe potngal. Kono alaa, yettaaki ardineede e worɓe rewɓe e fenaande maɓɓe; on njaɓan ɓiɓɓe mon mbaɗee kawral haa taƴee yeeso am.

Golle ɗee ina njogii batte mawɗe, ɗe ceerataa e lepte. Kono hiɗon njokki e jinnaaɓe mon, so on tuubaani, mi tuutay on e hunuko am. Ngaree e am e tuubugol, njooɗee e gude e ndoondi, ngam mi annda goonga tuubugol mon, mboolee, njaree e junuubaaji mon ngam woto on njoɓ to ñaawoore. Heewɓe e mon ina mawnikini sabu hiɗon etoo waɗde mi Joomiraawo tawi hiɗon njogii ɓernde ngoonga alaa, sabu ko e ɓernde neɗɗo goonɗinta haa e fonndugol. Ndeen, njogoree ngoonga ngam kisnee; ngoonga mon feññan e yimɓe fof e salagol mon junuuba e yaltude e laabi mon tuunɗi. Bonɓe ina njaha e kala bannge, so yimɓe ɓurɓe bonde mawninaama. Kono hiɗon njokki e jaɓde laabi sodomankeeɓe, hiɗon mballita e addude bone mawɗo e leydi ndii, haa hiɗon cuɓoo worɓe torrotooɓe sukaaɓe ngam ardaade leyɗeele mon. On alaa hay faamu ɓurɗo famɗude, on rewataa laawol am; so on njokkii, mi rokkay on faa timma yiɗde mon bonnde ngam halkere ɓanndu ngam ruuhu hisee.

Accam mi uddita gite mon, onon ɓe cuɓii humaade e bumɓe, onon jooɗiiɓe e tuundi e niɓɓere, hiɗon taaroo e loopal. On mbaawaa jaɓde bone, cikka hiɗon ngollanam, sabu mi rewiima bone e jarribo haa ɓuuɓri ƴiiƴam sakkitiindi wulii e leydi boɗeeri, sabu ko miin woni Aadama sakkitiiɗo, mi laatiima ruuhu guurnuɗo. Heewɓe e mon bumɗinaama e konnguɗi moƴƴi e haalaaji belɗi, hiɗon ndokka jaɓgol mon timmungol e bonɓe e yimɓe bonɓe torrotooɓe sukaaɓe. Ɓe torroto sukaaɓe, ɓe torroto sukaaɓe ngam yiɗde maɓɓe bonnde: Sabu ko noon tikkere Alla ari e Babilon Sirri, kono heewɓe e mon ina njokki e jaɓde ɗeen daabaaji bonɗi ɗi ndesanaama yiite jahannama ngo meeɗataa ñifde. Yaltee e makko, yimɓe am, walla on kawray e masiibooji makko e ñaawoore bonnde nde ardata e makko; mi rentinii on. Hol kawral Almasiihu e Beliyal? So on kawrii e fenaande makko, on kawray e masiibooji makko; ndeen ceertiree faa timma e konngol e golle, woto njaɓdee e maɓɓe hay seeɗa. Woto nguuree nguurndam moƴƴaaki, kawral; sabu mi leptay kala baɗɗo ɗum. Hiɗon cikki sabu on njogii nguurndam daa'ima, on mbaawaa heɓde halkere mawnde e batte? Mi ittay njoɓdiiji mon, mi ndokka ɗi, on laatoo ɓurɓe famɗude e laamu asamaan so on njokkii, sabu ko noon Yeshua HaMashiach, Gorko Giɗo, wi'i Giɗaaɗo am!

Gila e Yahweh, Alla Mawɗo Daa'imaajo:

Onon ɓe ngalaa nafoore bonnooɓe tagu am, onon ɓe ngalaa nafoore yeeyuɓe hoore mon e Seyɗaani, onon ɓe ngalaa nafoore waɗɓe Seyɗaani Alla mon, onon ɓe ngalaa nafoore saliiɓe ɓiyam Iisaa Almasiihu e kisndam ngam mi hokki e innde mum, onon ɓe ngalaa nafoore accunooɓe ɓiɓɓe mon wulde e yiite Moloch. Mi rokkay on aduna mo njiɗɗon, mi heblanii on aduna mo mi alaa, aduna mo ɓiyam Almasiihu Iisaa alaa, aduna mo weendu yiite mawndu e sulufru heblanaa on tan.

Mi rokkay on niɓɓere nde njiɗɗon no feewi, sabu annoora am woodataa toon, on ndañan duuɓi daa'ima hiɗon ceertuɗi e am, Yahweh, Alla mawɗo jalboowo, mo teddungal e jalbugol mum alaa ɓanndu waawi yiyde, mo woni gila e fuɗɗoode haa e abada. Onon luutndiiɓe, bonɓe bonnuɓe tagu am gila e fuɗɗoode e yiɗde mon e konnaagu e hasida, anndee on kebbinii hoore mon lepte mawɗe e torra ɗe meeɗataa cellude, e kala ɓe njiwii e fenaande mon, ɓe saliima Iisaa Almasiihu, ɓe saliima yaltude e fenaande mon faade e goonga am.

Hiɗon cikki wonde bonnude laabi am e ittude keerol am ko huunde hoynde, kono on paamay tikkere am nde yiite gadano ngo memi on, on naatan e yiite e sulufru ɗe meeɗataa dartaade. Gaa gaa ɗum, on mbarii, on njaanii, on kuɓɓii, on torrii, on lorlii, on mbarii, on mbonnii, on naatii e bonanndeeji ɓurɗi bonde, haa on njiɗi ɓanndu hawniindu; on kawrii e malaa'ikaaɓe feyyuɓe, ndeen mi hawray on e weendu yiite nde keblan-mi Iblis e malaa'ikaaɓe mum, on ndaɗataa. On alaa jam.

Mi yoɓtoy on, sabu miin ko mi Alla ñaawoowo, miin ko mi Alla yoɓtotooɗo. Kono, on ndaran kam e mawnikinaare, hiɗon kaɓa e haala mon, hiɗon cikki mi ɗaccay laabi mon seyɗaaniiji ɗi: ko njiɗɗon, ko ɗum wonata sariya fof. On alaa jam. Mi yoɓtoyay, hiɗon mbiya miin ko mi Alla giɗo (ko mi oon), kono hiɗon tammoo mi yoɓtotaako ɓe tuunni tagu am, ɓe torri, ɓe mbari ɓiyam gooto mo ndañnoomi, nde o hokki hoore makko ngam hisnude fof; eey, hiɗon tammoo mi yoɓtotaako on ngam laabi mon ɗi ngalaa nafoore nde mbarduɗon, torruɗon, lornuɗon alaaɓe aybe, nde mbeeluɗon ɓiɓɓe am ngam anniyaaji mon.

Gooto, Seyɗaani e hoore mum ina foti bonnude kala ko woni e am ngam nafoore mum, hay so tawii hiɗon etoo tuumde mi e fenaande e ñaawooje am fonnduɗe nde ngarmi ngam hisnude yimɓe am. Ñalɗi mon ɗi njiwoton ɓiɓɓe am ina ɓadii timmude. Gite! Daaba mon e annabi mon fenaande halkete faa laaɓa, mi werlayan ɓe ɓe nguuri e nder ndeen weendu mawndu wulndu e yiite e sulufru; ɓe ndaaran on haa on naata e ndonu mon.

Ñaawoore mawnde ina woodi e maa, an laamɗo niɓɓere; laamu maa e kala rewɓe e maa ina ɓadii halkireede faa laaɓa. Sabu onon wonaa allaaji, on mbaawaa hisnude hoore mon e juuɗe am, mi haɓɓay on e niɓɓere ɓurnde luggiɗde nde njiɗɗon, hay e ɓurnde luggiɗde nde mi haɓɓirta on e jolokooje mawɗe ɗe mbaawaa feccude. Eey, e nder luggeere ndee on ngonay ñalnde wootere, sabu ñalnde wootere e Joomiraawo ko no duuɓi ujunere nii, duuɓi ujunere ko no ñalnde wootere nii. Hay so tawii mi yaltinan on seeɗa ngam on njarriba, on mooɓta sakkitiiɓe e ɓeen bonɓe rewɓe on e laabi mon. Ndeen mi halkay on fof e yiite, jooɗorɗe ñaawoore ndaree, mi ñaawoyan on fof bonɓe, on njahan e lepte daa'imaaje yeeso Joomiraawo. On ngulan yeeso malaa'ikaaɓe e yeeso Jawgel ngel, ɓe njiyay on e torra e lepte, sabu on tooñii ɓe fof, on njoɓan junuubaaji mon faa laaɓa duuɓi daa'ima.

Ndeen on maɓɓay e kasoo mon, on njippintee, on meeɗataa siftoreede kadi, sabu ko ɓennuɗo oo yahan, njippintee. Sabu gite, mi waɗan asamaan keso e leydi heseri ɗo fonndugol hoɗi; miin, Yahweh, Alla mawɗo daa'imaajo mo allaaji fof, sabu alaa nannduɗo e am, gite, mi waɗan kala huunde hesere, sabu miin, Yahweh, mi haalii ɗum! Yahweh, Alla mawɗo daa'imaajo, waɗan jooɗorde mum e leydi, nokku am hoɗorde wonan hakkunde yimɓe, on meeɗataa wonde kadi, onon ɓe ngalaa nafoore, on ngulan faa abada e kasoo mon njippinaaɗo e torra mo mbaawaa yaltude, sabu mi waɗan maɓɓere, alaa baawɗo feccude ɗum.

Kono, heewɓe e mon ɓe njiɗi Seyɗaani ina tuuma mi e fenaande ngam mi ƴetti ñaawoore e yoɓto e juuɗe am ngam hisnude ɓe mbaɗi hoore maɓɓe potɓe lepte daa'imaaje haa e yiite. Nanaa konnguɗi am, onon hoɗɓe e leydi: Ɓiyam ina ara, o arda e yoɓto mawngo, tikkere, e fitina ngam bonanndeeji baɗaaɗi e juuɗe mon. Waktuuji ñaawoore, hay ñaawoore ñalawma mawɗo Almasiihu, ina ɓadii feerde e aduna oo, alaa safaara wonan bonɓe, alaa safaara wonan Seyɗaani, alaa safaara wonan fof ɓe kawri e mum, sabu fof naatan e halkere daa'ima, mi artiray ɓe kala ko ɓe mbaɗi e alaaɓe aybe.

Sabu ɗuum, mi artiray ɓe yiɗde ɓerɗe maɓɓe bonɗe e golle maɓɓe bonɗe fof e dow maɓɓe duuɓi daa'ima. Njeeƴee tikkere am ina wula, anndee waktuuji artirgol kala huunde ina ɓadii, alaa limooje keddii e montorooje asamaan, sabu kala limooje njanii e montor, sabu waktu timmi! Miɗo wasiyoo kala janngoowo ɗee konnguɗi yo dogdu e ɓiyam Iisaa Almasiihu e tuubugol, waɗa mo Joomiraawo; sabu alaa gardo e am so wonaa e Iisaa Almasiihu, sabu alaa innde woɗnde ndokku-mi ngam kisndam, tooruuji mon fof ngulay. Waktu ina woodi tawo ngam kisndam, kala nodduɗo innde Joomiraawo haa e sakkitoore hisay. Ndeen dogdee e Iisaa Almasiihu tawi hiɗon mbaawi tawo, sabu ñalawma Joomiraawo ari, mi yaltinan Ɓiyam, tikkere Jawgel ngel heɓay. Sunnee Ɓiɗɗo o woto o tikka, on halka e laawol, so tikkere makko ummiima seeɗa: barke woodanii fof ɓe koolii e makko.


Ngam siftorde: Esayi 13:1 gila e Septuagint: Miɗo yamira, miɗo adda ɓe: yimɓe mawɓe ina ngara ngam timminde tikkere am, ina njiɗi, ina njaɓɓa.
