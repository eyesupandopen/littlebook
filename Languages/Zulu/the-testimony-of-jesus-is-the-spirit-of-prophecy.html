Ubufakazi BukaJesu Bungumoya Wesiprofetho

Bhekani, nginembulela imfihlakalo yencwajana. Ngoba incwadi isinikiwe, futhi umoya wesiprofetho ungubufakazi bukaJesu. Ubufakazi bami ngoJesu baqala empilweni yami lapho iNkosi uJesu Kristu ingibizela enkonzweni nasemsebenzini ngo-1996 ngesikhathi ngihlala eNew York City. Ngangisabelile obizweni lweNkosi lokuphenduka ngokuhambela iqembu lokufunda iBhayibheli ekhaya futhi ngangifunda kwelinye lamakilasi abo. Ngobunye ubusuku ngangihleli endaweni yokudlela eLong Island City nomngane wami omkhulu, wangicela ukuba ngivule iBhayibheli lami kuJohane 4, waqala ukufunda okulandelayo nami ngimlandela:

Johane 4:35-38

Anisho yini nina ukuthi: Kuseyizinyanga ezine, bese kufika ukuvuna? Bhekani, ngithi kini: Phakamisani amehlo enu, nibheke amasimu; ngokuba asemhlophe, alungele ukuvunwa.

Novunayo wamukela inkokhelo, abuthele izithelo zokuphila okuphakade, ukuze notshalayo kanye novunayo bathokoze kanyekanye.

Nalapha kunjalo ukuthi izwi liqinisile, elithi: Omunye uyahlwanyela, omunye avune.

Nganithuma ukuyovuna lokho eningakukhandlekelanga; abanye bakhandlekile, nina-ke ningene ekukhandlekeni kwabo.

Ngesikhathi umngane wami efunda la mavesi, amazwi abonakala egxuma ephuma ekhasini angena ekujuleni komphefumulo wami. Kwabhebhetheka umlilo enhliziyweni yami, kanjalo-ke iNkosi yangithuma enkonzweni yayo ngo-1996. Ngayilahla impilo yami yonke nakho konke engangikwenza ukuze ngingene enkonzweni. Ngaba umholi ebandleni elikhulu elalisakhula, kwathi kamuva, lapho ngithola ukuthi babonakele kangakanani ngempela, ngaphuma ngomnyango angabe ngisabuya ngo-2008. Ngadonsa kanzima iminyaka embalwa njengoba ngangizama ukuhamba noKristu, kodwa izono zami zazibuyisela emuva. Ngo-2013 impilo yami yawa yacitheka, ngazicekela phansi, futhi kwakuyingozi yesitimela esihamba kancane eyayisinesikhathi eside isenzeka. Kanjalo-ke iNkosi yangiyisa ekuyalweni okukhulu yangishaya kaningi kwaze kwaba ngibuyela ezingqondweni ngoJulayi 7, 2020, lapho ngaphenduka ngokugcwele ezonweni zami.

INkosi yangilungiselela lolu hambo ngokunginika imibono eminingi, izibonakaliso, izimangaliso, ukuphulukiswa okuningana, futhi ngikhiphe imimoya eminingi ngesiyalezo seNkosi, futhi isiqinisekiso esikhulu iNkosi enginike sona ngobuqotho bezinkonzo zami nobizo lwami. Kanjalo-ke sengibuyiselwe eNkosini yami, ngabuyiselwa kuYahweh, ngabuyiselwa kumkami nendodana yami nomvuzo wami waphakade.

Ngesinye isikhathi ngasekupheleni kuka-2023, kwenzeka into emangalisayo. Izikhathi eziningi ngilala kudlala umbhalo oNgcwele ku-TV yami nesipikha somsindo. Ngabo lobo busuku, ngalala kudlala iTestamente Elidala kancane kancane. Ngaphaphanyiswa ubuthongo phakathi nobusuku lapho isipikha sami somsindo, ebesithule kakhulu, siba nomsindo omkhulu kungazelele muntu. Akekho owayekhulise ivolumu, futhi isilawuli-kude sasilele singathintiwe etafuleni lami eliseceleni kombhede. Kwadlala amavesi alandelayo avela kuHezekeli ngesikhathi ngihleli lapho ngingakholwa:

Hezekeli 3:17-22

Ndodana yomuntu, ngikubeke waba ngumlindi wendlu ka-Israyeli; ngalokho yizwa izwi eliphuma emlonyeni wami, ubaxwayise ngami.

Lapho ngithi komubi: Uyakufa nokufa, kepha ungamxwayisi wena, ungakhulumi ukumxwayisa omubi ngendlela yakhe embi, ukuze umsindise, yena lowo-mubi uyakufa ebubini bakhe, kepha igazi lakhe ngiyakulibiza esandleni sakho.

Kepha uma umxwayisa omubi, angaphenduki ebubini bakhe nasendleleni yakhe embi, uyakufa yena ebubini bakhe, kodwa wena usindise umphefumulo wakho.

Futhi, lapho olungileyo ebuya ekulungeni kwakhe, enze ububi, ngibeke isikhubekiso phambi kwakhe, uyakufa; ngokuba ungamxwayisanga, uyakufa esonweni sakhe, nokulunga kwakhe akwenzileyo akuyikukhunjulwa, kepha igazi lakhe ngiyakulibiza esandleni sakho.

Nokho, uma umaxwayisa olungileyo ukuba olungileyo angoni, angoni, uyakuphila nokuhle, ngokuba exwayisiwe; nawe futhi usindise umphefumulo wakho.

Isandla sikaJehova sasiphezu kwami lapho.

Futhi kube njalo. Noma ngaleso sikhathi ngangingaqondi ukuthi iNkosi ingithuma njengomprofethi wezikhathi zokugcina, ngangizokuthola maduze.

NgoMashi 2, 2024, ngaqala ukubhala imiyalezo yesiprofetho kulokho iNkosi eyangichazela kona njengemisebenzi kaJohane uMbhapathizi. Lokhu kwaqhubeka kusukela ngoMashi 2 kuze kube ngu-Ephreli 5, 2024, futhi ngaleso sikhathi ngathola imiyalezo yesiprofetho engama-20. Yonke imiyalezo yayigxile ekwahlulelweni kweBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlakalo, ibizela abangcwele ekuphendukeni, futhi imemezela ukuvela okuseduze kukaJesu Kristu uqobo, njengoba ezovela emoyeni noma nini manje kwabathembekileyo eBandleni lakhe. Empeleni ngangicabanga ukuthi isiprofetho sango-Ephreli 5 kwakungesokugcina. Nganginephutha. Umyalezo wesiprofetho olandelayo wenzeka ngo-Ephreli 10, 2024, iNkosi yangitshela ukuthi ingithuma emsebenzini kaPetru ukuze ngondle futhi nginakekele umhlambi.

Isimo nokuqukethwe kwemiyalezo kwashintsha kusukela lapho, nakuba isaqukethe izinto ezivela emiyalezweni yokuqala engamashumi amabili, futhi eminingi yale miyalezo iyahlobana. Ngo-Ephreli 21, 2024, isimo nokuqukethwe kwemiyalezo kwaphinde kwashintsha, iNkosi yangitshela ukuthi ingithuma emsebenzini womPhostoli uPawulu njengomphathi wezimfihlakalo zikaNkulunkulu. Kanjalo-ke okuqukethwe yimiyalezo kwaphinde kwashintsha futhi kwakhelwe phezu kwemiyalezo yangaphambili futhi kuyahlobana. Le miyalezo yaqhubeka ngendlela efanayo kwaze kwaba nguMeyi 16, 2024, lapho okuqukethwe kwaphinde kwashintsha khona. Ngatshelwa ukuthi ngithunyelwa emisebenzini kaJohane uMambuli (igama engingakaze ngilizwe noma ngilisebenzise), kwathi ngoMeyi 16 zaqala lezo ziprofetho.

Futhi-ke umsebenzi wezandla zeNkosi ubonakaliswa obizweni enginike lona, futhi ingithumele lapho ngingakhandlekelanga khona; abanye abantu bakhandlekile, nami ngingene ekukhandlekeni kwabo. Akukhona ukuthi la madoda amakhulu kaNkulunkulu awaziqedanga izifundo zawo, kodwa ukuthi le misebenzi iqhubekile kusukela kuleso sikhathi eside lapho uJesu Kristu ebathuma emisebenzini abathume yona. Lezi zigaba zazingaziwa kimi lapho ngiqala ukuprofetha, futhi njengoba engitshelile, ngangena emisebenzini yomunye nomunye. Angizange ngikuhlanganise lokhu kwaze kwaba ngembulelwa nguYeshua.

Ubizo nezinkonzo empilweni yami kugcwalisekile. Abanye bakhandlekile, wangithumela emisebenzini yabo, nalapho ngikhandlekile khona. Ngithunyelwe emsebenzini kaJohane uMbhapathizi ukumemezela ukuza kweNkosi. Ngithunyelwe emsebenzini kaPetru ukwelusa nokondla umhlambi. Ngithunyelwe emsebenzini womPhostoli uPawulu njengomphathi Wezimfihlakalo zikaNkulunkulu. Ngithunyelwe emsebenzini kaJohane uMambuli ukubiza abayi-144,000 nokukhombisa izenzakalo ezizayo.

Ngokwami, angifanele ukuziqhathanisa nanoma yimaphi ala madoda amakhulu kaNkulunkulu futhi angifaneleki ngokwami ngisho nokukhuluma ukubingelela okulula kunoma ubani kini. Kodwa iNkosi ingenze ngafaneleka, futhi ngithunyelwe kule misebenzi ngokufanayo. Impilo yami yonke iholele kule miyalezo, futhi wonke amazwi azofinyelela emehlweni, ezindlebeni nasezinhliziyweni zalabo ethunyelwe kubo. Uma iNkosi idinga ukuba ngisho okwengeziwe, ngizokwenza. Kanjalo-ke ngizoqeda uhambo lwami nemisebenzi enginikezwe ukuba ngiyiqedele. Futhi ngale ncazelo, umsebenzi wezandla nesiginesha yeNkosi uJesu Kristu kwembulwa kule miyalezo yesiprofetho enginikezwe yona. Sengiyiqedile imisebenzi angithume yona. Kanjalo-ke ngilindele iNkosi.
