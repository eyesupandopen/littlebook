I-Operation War-seed / Ukuthuthukiswa Kophawu Lwesilo

  2. Qondani ukuthi i-Operation War-seed nokulunywa kwayo yinyoka kwakungelona uphawu lwesilo, kodwa uphawu lwesilo luyokwenziwa sengathi luwumuthi futhi luyisifungo esigcwele sokwethembeka ekwamukeleni ubuchwepheshe babo obuyosula unomphela igama likaYahweh kulabo abavumayo.
  3. Ngisho nokulunywa yinyoka okuhlelo lwabo oseluvele lusatshalalisiwe futhi lwahlolwa, futhi izixuku sezivele zilolongelwe ukwamukela uphawu lwabo futhi i-Operation War-seed yabo yaba impumelelo.
  4. Kuvela kuwe Bhabhiloni Eyimfihlo, lobu buchwepheshe besihogo futhi ngamanga akho nobuqili bakho izixuku zifakwe ushevu futhi ukwahlulelwa kwakho akulibali.

Imvume yobubi

  1. Abaningi benu banokuzidla okukhulu ngoba nizama ukungibeka iNkosi kuyilapho nisenenhliziyo engenakukholwa, ngokuba umuntu ukholwa ngenhliziyo kube ngukulunga. Kepha nina nisavumelana nezindlela zabesodom, futhi nisiza ekuletheni ububi obukhulu ezweni, kangangokuthi empeleni nivotela abahlukumezi bezingane ukuba bahole amazwe enu. Nintula ngisho nezinga eliphansi kakhulu lokuqonda, futhi anilandeli indlela yami; futhi uma niphikelela, ngiyoninikela ngokuphelele ezinkanukweni zenu ezimbi kube ngukubhujiswa kwenyama ukuze umoya usindiswe.
  3. Ake ngivule amehlo enu, nina eningafuni ukwazi futhi niyizimpumputhe ngokuzithandela, nina enihleli ekungcoleni nasebumnyameni, nizingiqika odakeni. Ningaphili izimpilo ezinganakekile, ezihlanekezelwe; ngokuba ngiyobajezisa bonke abenza kanjalo. Nicabanga ukuthi ngenxa yokuba ninokuphila okuphakade anikwazi ukulahlekelwa kakhulu nemiphumela?   6. Makwaziwe! Izindlela Zokuphila Ezinesono Ngokujabulisayo / Ukuzidla / Ubulukhuni Benhliziyo

  1. Ngokuba wahlala ngokujabulisayo esonweni esikhulu; uhleli ngokuzidla okukhulu ecabanga ukuthi ngeke awe futhi izinto zizoqhubeka, izolo njenganamuhla, nakusasa kuya kufana njalo kungaguquki.

  5. Bhabhiloni eyimfihlo, uyizwe lokwaneliseka okusheshayo, futhi awuhlakaniphile neze.

  10. Manje kufika ukwahlulelwa kwakho ngokugcwele, Bhabhiloni eyimfihlo, futhi usuyinto yokubukwa yiwo wonke umuntu, kodwa uyaqhubeka nokungazi nobumpumputhe uthi ezinhliziyweni zakho ukuthi inhlekelele ngeke ikwehlele.
