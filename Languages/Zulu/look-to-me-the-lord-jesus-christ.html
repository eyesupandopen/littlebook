Mashi 28, 2024

Bhekani kimi, iNkosi uJesu Kristu!

Bantwana bami, vulani amehlo enu nibheke kimi, iNkosi uJesu Kristu. Phaphamani ebuthongweni nibuyele kimi; buyelani kimi, ngoba izingalo zami zelulekele nina, bakhethwa bami. Masinyane nizoba nami, njengoba ihora selisemzuzwini wokugcina nezikhathi zomusa sezicishe zaphela, futhi ngizokulethela esiphethweni esiphelele. Phaphamani ebuthongweni, ngizokwephula imilingo yeziphonso zalo mhlaba emehlweni enu. Khalani nilile ngenxa yezono zenu nokwenqaba kwenu ukuza kimi, futhi nazisise ukuthi nginibiza kulo mzuzu wokugcina. Nginibizela emuva kimi, uthando lwenu lokuqala, nakuba namukele abanye ezinhliziyweni zenu.

Balekani kubo bonke, ngoba bangabakhohlisi; yebo, bayizimpisi ezembethe isikhumba semvu, futhi nilalele ezingalweni zabo ezimbi. Ningozeli, bantwana bami, kodwa phaphamani ningilinde, njengoba izibonakaliso zami sezisezulwini futhi ngiza njengesela ebusuku. Ngizotholani lapho ngibuya kwiBandla lami? Ngizothola nokholo yini? Oh, bandla laseLawodikeya, usivivi, ngifisa sengathi ngabe uyabanda noma uyashisa. Phendukela kimi futhi, ngizokuhlanza ebuthongweni bakho, futhi ngizokwelapha amanxeba owalinyazwe ababi. Anginaso isifiso sokukuphalaza emlonyeni wami, ngakho-ke buya kimi futhi, ngizokwamukela. Yazi ukuthi uthando lwami ngawe alufi futhi luhlala luqinile futhi aluguquki.

Akekho ngaphandle kwami onganisindisa, akekho ngaphandle kwami onganenza niphelele futhi aniphilise, akekho ngaphandle kwami onganivikela futhi aninike ukuphila okuphakade, akekho ngaphandle kwami onganisindisa ezonweni zenu, ngoba alikho elinye igama elinikiwe lapho abantu benganisindiswa ngalo. Ngiyithemba lenu laphakade elinidale kabusha ekuzalweni kwami okusha engikuthele phezu kwenu. Yazini ukuthi ningabantwana bakaBaba, futhi ninefa elikhulu ngokwethembeka kwenu. Nokho, nakuba ngizokwahlulela imisebenzi yenu ukuze nithole umvuzo noma ukulahlekelwa, nisukile ekungalungini nangena ekuphileni okuphakade ngokukholwa kwenu kimi naseBukhosini bami baphakade. Bhekani! Nginenze amakhosi nabapristi phambi kukaBaba, ngoba ngingumpristi ophakeme kuze kube phakade ngokohlelo lukaMelkisedeki.

Ngubani lowo ongamelana nani? Ngoba ukumelana nabafowethu kuwukumelana nami, futhi ngizophindisela, isho iNkosi enkulu yaphakade yenkazimulo esezulwini! Zonke izinto ezulwini nasemhlabeni zinikezwe ezandleni zami, ngakho-ke yazini ukuthi nginegunya eliphakeme, futhi akekho omkhulu kunami ngaphandle kukaBaba, ongiphakamise ngaphezu kwakho konke. Ngakho-ke ngubani lowo okufanele nimesabe? Ukushushiswa nokuhlupheka akunenzi yini niqine kakhulu kimi? Akunjalo yini ukuthi lapho amandla enu ephela, ngiba khona kanye ukuze nginiqinise ebuthakathakeni benu?

Ngakho-ke imani niqine nibe namandla kimi! Kungani ningabaza amazwi ami? Kunalokho, kholwani kimi nikhulume amazwi engininika wona, ngoba lawa mazwi anamandla amakhulu uma nizowakhuluma ngokukholwa. Khulumani ngokuzethemba nangegunya, niyale izinto ezibuthaka ngegama likaJesu Kristu, igama lami elinamandla ngaphezu kwakho konke. Masinyane nizongibona ngamehlo enu, futhi ubukhona benu ezulwini bufiswa kakhulu yimi noBaba, yebo, ngisho nanguYeshuwa noYahweh. Niyigugu elikhulu likaBaba, futhi anilinganiseki. Ngoba kuphi okunye lapho kwanikelwa khona ngegazi elingenasono ngenxa yezono zenu ngaphandle kwami?

Oh, nina enikhohlakele enihleli nizigabisa, nicabanga ukuthi ninokunqoba okukhulu. Ngizophazamisa amacebo enu obubi, futhi nizokwazi ukufeza kuphela lokho uBaba anqume ukuthi ningakwenza ngokuhambisana nokuqhubekisela phambili amacebo akhe. Nibuthaka, aninalutho futhi nehlulwe ngokuphelele. Oh, muntu wemvelo, kungani usakhetha izindlela ezimbi zabakhohlakele abanidukisa ngobuqili? Ingabe izinjabulo zesono zifanele ukuhlukaniswa phakade nenkazimulo kaNkulunkulu waphakade? Nizokutholaphi ukuphila ngaphandle kwami? Ngubani ozonikhulula ngaphandle kwalowo enisamlahlayo? Umusa wami nesihe sami akusibambanga yini isandla sesitha?

Noma yini enhle eninayo yehle ivela kuYise wezinkanyiso, kuye okungekho ukuguquguquka noma isithunzi sokuphenduka. Kodwa cha, niwulahlile umusa wami futhi nasebenzisa isihe sami ukuze nijabule futhi nizitike ebubini benu. Bhekani! Imbubhiso enkulu izonehlela. Nikhashelwe abaholi benu, abahlela ukubhujiswa kwenu futhi bavumele amabutho abomvu ukuba angene. Ukubhujiswa nobubi obukhulu kuzofika phezu kwenu ngokuzumayo, futhi kuzofika ngesikhathi ngihlwitha umakoti wami phakathi kwezindlela zenu ezimbi. Khona-ke ukubhujiswa okuzumayo kuzonibamba, ngoba yimuphi umthiyi obeka ugibe emehlweni enyoni? Cha, ugibe olusitsheke kancane selulungele ukugxuma, futhi kuzokwenzeka lapho umvimbeli wami esusiwe, futhi ngeke niphunyuke.

Khona-ke izinhliziyo zenu ziyoncibilika phakathi kwenu namandla enu ayobaleka njengoba nibona ngamehlo enu izidalwa esezizokhishwa ekujuleni komhlaba zize phezu kwenu. Ngizovula kwalasha, ngoba nginegunya ngisho naphezu kwawo; ngoba nginezikhiye zokufa nesihogo, futhi akekho ongazihlwitha kimi. Ukube beniqonda ukuthi aninanto emhlabeni, futhi konke kuyisipho somusa nesihe, ngoba uthando lukaNkulunkulu luholela abantu ekuphendukeni. Kodwa cha, nihahela ubuhle bukaNkulunkulu nina nodwa futhi nibulale umakhelwane wenu esikhundleni sokumthanda njengoba nizithanda nina, njengoba ngiyalile. UNkulunkulu walithanda izwe kangaka waze wanikela ngeNdodana yakhe ezelwe yodwa, uJesu waseNazaretha, ukuze anibuyisele kuye ngothando ngami.

Kodwa ngokuzidla kwenu nangezindlela zenu zasemhlabeni, nizenzile lukhuni izinhliziyo zenu kimi nasensindisweni yami. Ngakho-ke, ukubhujiswa okuphelele kuzofikela isizwe senu ngehora elilodwa, neBhabhiloni Eyinganekwane ngeke lisaba khona. Yebo, Bhabhiloni elikhulu, izimfihlo zakho zisazodalulwa ukuze wonke umuntu abone, nalabo ababambelele kuwe bayohlupheka ngokwahlulelwa okufanayo.

Ngakho-ke, oh, muntu olahla uNkulunkulu, zithobe uze kimi manje! Ihora lakho lesidingo yimanje. Phendukela kimi; ngiyakuncenga, futhi ngikubiza ngegama! Phuma kulo mhlaba ofayo, ngoba kukhona insindiso kimi uma nje uzokwamukela.

Angijabuli ekwahlulelweni kwenu, futhi uBaba ufisa ukuba niphile phambi kwakhe, futhi othandweni lwakhe unikeze izidalwa zakhe ukukhetha: Ngibeka phambi kwenu namuhla ukuphila nokufa, ngakho-ke, khethani ukuphila ukuze nina nenzalo yenu niphile! Ngakho-ke yizani kimi kungakephuzi kakhulu futhi nazisise ukuthi lo mhlaba omubi enibambelele kuwo uzobhujiswa ngokuphelele, futhi kuzokwenzeka ngolaka lweWundlu. UNkulunkulu angeke yini ahlulele abenzi bokubi ababulale abantwana bakhe, abalimaze futhi bahlukumeza abangenacala, ababulale iNdodana ezelwe yodwa kaNkulunkulu? Nakuba nganikela ngokuphila kwami ngokuzithandela ngenxa yenu futhi kungekho muntu owathatha ukuphila kwami, kukhona ukwahlulela okukhulu nobulungiswa okufanele kwenziwe yimi. Ngoba ngathenga umhlaba ngegazi lami nomhlatshelo wokuhlawulela, futhi ungowami. Ngizomisa umbuso wokulunga nobungcwele beqiniso, lapho ibhubesi newundlu kuyolala ndawonye, lapho kungekho nto embi eyongena khona. Ngakho-ke, phumani ezonweni zenu, njengoba nginithanda, futhi ngimemeza ngomoya wami wokugcina ngaphambi kokuba kuwa inhlamvu yokugcina yesihlabathi. Futhi sekuseduze.
