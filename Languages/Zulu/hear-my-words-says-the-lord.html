Yizwani Amazwi Ami, Isho INkosi!

Yizwani amazwi ami, isho iNkosi! Usuku lweNkosi seluzonishonela njengomlilo oqothulayo, futhi ngenxa yomlilo izono zabaningi zizohlanzwa, labo abayobiza igama lami futhi bangazithandi izimpilo zabo kuze kube sekupheleni. Izimvu zami ziyalizwa izwi lami, futhi ngizozilungiselela indlela, nakuba zizohlangabezana nezinkinga eziningi. Labo ababambelela egameni lami elinamandla bayosindiswa, noma kunjalo kube sengathi kungomlilo. Isitha siphezu kwenu, nina eniziqhenyayo enenqabile ukuthula ebenginipha khona. Izono zenu zifike phambi kwami futhi ukwahlulelwa okukhulu kuphezu kwenu. Nakuba umlilo wami ungeke ubaqede abancane bami, futhi izikhathi zizoba nzima kakhulu kunanini ngaphambili, abangcwele abaningi bayophunyuka ekufeni kwesibili ngokuphendukela egameni lami ngehora labo lokuhlupheka nangokuma baqine kuze kube sekugcineni. Mina, iNkosi uJesu Kristu, ngikukhulumile futhi ngithumele isixwayiso kulabo abanezindlebe zokuzwa namehlo okubona.

O nina zizwe, nina zizwe ezikhonza izithixo ezihweba ngezono ezinkulu. Yebo, seniphenduke abahwebi bobubi abadumisa omubi ngawo wonke amazwi enu amabi nezindlela zenu ezimbi enizifundele ukubhujiswa kwenu. Seniphenduke abahwebi benyama nabantu, ngoba ngisho nezingane seziphenduke impahla yenu yokuhweba. Ulaka lweWundlu, yebo, ulaka lwami luzoqeda umhlaba wenu, futhi umlilo wami uzohlanza izwe emisebenzini yabenzi bokubi. Ngaphambi kokuba umbuso wami ufike, ukuhlanjululwa okukhulu kuyofika emhlabeni, futhi umhlaba uyongcoliswa yigazi labenzi bokubi. Nokho, ngizowakha umbuso wami emhlabeni, umbuso wokulunga nokuthula lapho kungekho nto embi eyongenayo, futhi ngiyobusa izizwe ngenduku yensimbi.

Lokho okukhona manje kuzobhujiswa futhi ngeke kuphinde kwakhiwe, futhi ngizozidiliza ngokuphelele izithixo zenu, futhi izindawo eziphakeme zengqondo yenu ziyothotshiswa. Ngoba nishilo enhliziyweni yenu, "Angisiye umfelokazi!" Kodwa ukubhujiswa kwenu kuzonehlela ngehora elilodwa, futhi kuzogwinya ngokuphelele futhi kulolonge kabusha umhlaba njengoba beniwazi. Kungani nikhonza ukufa kwenu, nina bakhonzi bezithixo bomhlaba? Kungani nihlukanisa futhi ningcwelise isijeziso senu eniziqongelelele sona emiphefumulweni yenu? Anibonanga yini? Anizwanga yini? Anifundanga yini? Cha, niyizimpumputhe, futhi niyizithulu, futhi anikwazi ukuqonda amazwi ami ngoba nenqabile umoya wami ongcwele ebengizowuthumela phezu kwenu futhi nginihlambulule ngokuphelele phambi kwami ukube benizokwamukela isipho sami.

Ngizophindisela, isho iNkosi. Ngizophindisela kumuntu ngamunye ngokwemisebenzi yakhe, futhi ngizonikeza isabelo esiphindwe kabili sokwahlulela njengoba ngibuyisela imisebenzi yenu kube ngumonakalo emiphefumulweni yenu, futhi ngeke nilulame. Ukwahlulelwa sekuzoqala emhlabeni, futhi abaningi benu basazishaya indiva ubizo lwami futhi abawalaleli amazwi ami abengazonisindisa ngokuqinisekile enhlekeleleni yenu. Ihora lokwahlulelwa kwenu liyasondela, futhi abaningi bazothatha uphawu, becabanga ukuthi bangasindisa izimpilo zabo futhi baphile ezonweni abazithandayo isikhashana eside. Indodana yokulahlwa izothembisa izinto eziningi futhi izokhohlisa umhlaba wonke njengoba bemangala ngesilo, bethi, "Ngubani onjengesilo?"

Nokho, omubi ozayo, nezibonakaliso zakhe zamanga nezimangaliso, uzonikhaphela, futhi nizobhubha ngokuphelele. Nizofela ezonweni zenu bese nivukela ekwahlulelweni kwesihlalo sobukhosi esikhulu esimhlophe lapho nizolandisa khona ngaso sonke isono, ngawo wonke umcabango omubi, yebo, ngalo lonke izwi eliyize nizolandisa ngalo. Futhi ngezingubo zenu ezingcolile niyoficwa nintula, futhi ngeke nime niqonde, ngoba akukho nyama elungile emehlweni akhe, kodwa bonke baphambukile futhi bakhonza ukonakala kwabo. O muntu, noma ngubani ongilahlayo futhi olahla uMdali wakhe uyobhubha ngokuphelele engenakhambi. Manje yisikhathi sensindiso. Niyobalekela kubani lapho nithunjwa futhi niphonswa niphila echibini lomlilo? Umcamelo wenu uyoba isibabule esivuthayo, nezingubo zenu zokwembatha kuyoba amalangabi aphakade uBaba awahlungisele uDeveli nezingelosi zakhe. Kodwa, ngokungilahla, nizikhethele isiphetho senu futhi nahlanganyela nomubi, futhi niyohlanganyela ekwahlulelweni kwakhe.

Mina, iNkosi uJesu Kristu, owenziwa waba yinyama futhi wahlala phakathi kwenu, impela, ngizowubhubhisa ngokuphelele umbuso wesitha, futhi umhlaba uzoba inkundla yokugcina yempi. Balekani nisesenethuba ngoba isikhathi sesicishe siphelile! Kusele izinhlayiya ezimbalwa zesihlabathi ukuba ziwe kulesi sikali sesikhathi, bese kukhala icilongo. Lapho iBandla lami lihlwitshwa futhi umakoti wami eyiswa emakamelweni akhe, uSathane nempi yakhe bayophonswa emhlabeni, futhi uyolwa nokuqothula nabakhileyo bomhlaba. Kuzovulwa kwalasha, futhi izidalwa eziningi ezinengekayo ziyokwenyuka zifuna noma yini ezingayishwabadela. O muntu oyisiwula, awuqondi yini ukuthi ngomusa wami nangesihe sami ngiwuvimbile umbuso wesitha ekufikeni phezu kwenu ngobukhulu bawo?

Nokho, ngizoninika lokho enikulangazelelayo ezifisweni zenu eziyisono, futhi ngizowukhulula wonke umbuso wakhe phezu kwenu, futhi nizonikelwa ngokuphelele ezandleni zezitha zenu. Inhloso yakhe iwukubhubhisa sonke isintu kuze kube semphefumulweni wokugcina, ngoba ufuna umhlaba ongenankulunkulu wakhe, angeke awuthole nanini. Ukube bengingazifinyezanga lezo zinsuku, ubeyophumelela, futhi bekungeke kusale nyama iphila! Kodwa, nisakhetha izono zenu kunokukhululwa, uthando, nokukhanya enginiphathele khona ukube benizokhetha ukuphila futhi nivume igama lami njengeNkosi! Yebo, ngenzeni mina, uJesu Kristu, iNkosi yenu, njengoba ngiphila kuze kube phakade njengesibani kulabo abasezobhubha. Labo ababiza egameni lami bayosindiswa.

Bantwanyana bami, isikhathi sesicishe siphelile. Ngiyazi ukuthi abaningi benu bakhathele futhi bacindezelekile emiphefumulweni yenu ngenxa yobubi obuqhamuke nxazonke zenu. Lena yimizuzu yokugcina yemihelo yokubeletha, futhi abakhethiweyo bami bayokhululelwa emazulwini, lapho ngilungise indawo yokuphepha lapho bengaphephela khona. Umvuzo wami usesandleni sami owabathembekileyo ngisho nabangathembekile, abayolahlekelwa kodwa basindiswe baphilele ingunaphakade ngoba bangenzile iNkosi yabo. Ngakho-ke, sukani ezonweni zenu nibheke kimi, ukuze ngikhanyise ukukhanya kwami enhliziyweni yenu futhi nizihlambulule ngegazi lami eliyisihlawulelo. Ningesabi izikhathi futhi ningesabi umsebenzi osasele kini, ngoba isikhathi siseduze.

Njengoba izigameko zenzeka phambi kwamehlo enu, yazini ukuthi zona lezo zigameko zizoshesha futhi izinto zizokwenzeka ngokushesha okukhulu, ngoba ukwahlulela akulali. Izikhathi zomusa nezikhathi zokuqabuleka zeBandla lami sezisondele ekupheleni. Ngempela, angilibazisanga ngezithembiso kodwa ngibonise ukubekezela okukhulu nokucobeka ukuze wonke umphefumulo engiwukhethile uze kimi. Ukwahlulelwa kuphezu kwakho, O mhlaba, futhi kuphela abathembekileyo eBandleni lami abayosinda. Kungani nisavumela abakhohlisi nabaprofethi bamanga ukuba banikhathaze ngamazwi abo akhathazayo abawakhuluma bephikisana nobulula obutholakala kimi? Kungani ningabavumela ukuba baninyengele nisuke emshadweni wami nomakoti wami lapho benginganilungisela isihlalo etafuleni ukube benizophuma ezonweni zenu futhi nikholwe amazwi angokoqobo aseZwini lami Elingcwele?
