Zwanini Amazwi Ami Futhi Niqondisise Izikhathi Eniphila Kuzo!

Zwanini amazwi Ami, bantu bami, nani nonke enginibizayo, futhi niqondisise izikhathi eniphila kuzo. Ngoba izwe enilaziyo manje liyophela, futhi esikhundleni salo kuyosala amazwe amaningi ayincithakalo, ngisho umlotha namanxiwa eBhabhiloni eyimfihlakalo, ngokuba impi isifikile. Ngoba njengoba ngihlwitha uMlobokazi wami ngimyise emazulwini, usizi olukhulu luyogubuzela iBhabhiloni eyimfihlakalo, futhi iyobhujiswa ngehora elilodwa, futhi ngehora elilodwa iyodilizwa, futhi akukho okungashintsha isiphetho sayo, ngoba uYahweh ushilo kanjalo.

Ngoba wena, Bhabhiloni eyimfihlo, uliphikile izwi lami futhi wangibeka eceleni, mina Jesu Kristu Olungileyo, ngenxa yezinjabulo zesono. Ngoba ngisho nezixwayiso ezinkulu enazinikwa ezulwini ngesizwe senu zavele zaba ukuzijabulisa ezinhliziyweni zenu ezingakholwa nasemehlweni enu ankanuko, futhi kini kwaba olunye usuku nje njengoba nanizitika ngenjabulo ezonweni zenu eziniholela ekubhujisweni kwenu. Kwakufanele niphenduke!

Ngoba seniphenduke isizwe sabakhonza izithixo, isizwe sabashisekeli bezimpi, isizwe esizitika ngamanga nesithanda ukuzikhohlisa kwaso, isizwe esigcwalise umhlaba ngezinengiso zenu; futhi izizwe ziphuza enkomishini yenu yegolide egcwele izinengiso nokungcola kokuphinga kwenu.

Ngalokho-ke, ngimelene nani, futhi ukubhujiswa kwenu sekufikile futhi sekuqalile, ngoba anilunaki ubizo lukaYahweh engalunikeza ngami, uYeshua HaMashiach, kanti ngangizonisindisa. Futhi ngikhalile ngenxa yezindlela zenu ezinenhliziyo elukhuni, kodwa namanje anisuki ebubini; ngakho-ke senginifulathele, futhi izikhathi zabeZizwe sezizopheleliswa ngokugcwele lapho ngithatha abathembekileyo eBandleni lami ngokukhala kwecilongo.

Kodwa niyisizwe esiboshwe ngamaketanga, futhi niyobhujiswa ngokuphelele, futhi anisoze naphinde navuka. Lapho uMlobokazi wami ethathwa, umhlaba uyomangala ngokwethuka okukhulu ngalokho okuzobe kubonakele, ngoba lesi senzakalo ngeke senzeke emfihlakalweni, kodwa obala, ukuze umhlaba wonke wazi ukuthi kukhona uNkulunkulu ezulwini nokuthi ungithumile mina, Kristu Jesu, ngenxa yegolide, isiliva, namatshe ayigugu omhlaba. Ngoba amagugu amakhulu kaBaba, ngisho namaparele entengo enkulu, ayosuswa ekwahlulelweni nasekubhujisweni okuzayo.

Ngoba uMlobokazi wami usindisiwe olakeni ngenxa yomhlatshelo wami, futhi ngiyokhulula abathembekileyo nalabo abatholakala bethembekile kuKristu ngesikhathi sokubonakala kwami nabo bonke abafelayo bethembekile kuKristu, ngisho beqinisiwe yimina, Kristu Jesu, ngoba ngenziwe umoya onika ukuphila. Futhi ngiyovusa abafileyo bekuKristu, nalabo abaphilayo nabasalayo bayothathwa kanye nabo bayongihlangabeza emafwini. Ngakho-ke qondisisani ukuthi lesi senzakalo senzelwe ukuvuza abathembekileyo nokufeza umshado woMyeni noMlobokazi wami, ngoba siyohlanganiswa ingunaphakade esivumelwaneni futhi abasoze bahlukana nami. Futhi yimina engiyobenza amakhosi nabapristi phambi kukaBaba.

Kanjalo-ke bayoshiya umhlaba, kanjalo-ke umhlaba uyobona, futhi abaningi bayomangala ngokwethuka okukhulu. Ngoba ingxenye yesizathu salesi senzakalo ukudala ukuphenduka phakathi kwalabo abasala ngemuva, ngisho nalabo abenqaba ubizo lwami ngesikhathi somusa, kanye nabaningi abangakaze bezwe ngokwanele ukuze bakholwe abayosindiswa ngesikhathi sosizi lukaJakobe, ikakhulukazi labo bezwe lesithembiso.

Ngoba ngiyanibiza, O Israyeli: senilungele yini ukuzwa izwi likaMesiya wenu weqiniso, iNgonyama yesizwe sakwaJuda? Ngoba izwi likaMesiya wenu weqiniso liyabiza, futhi yimina engiyobusa esihlalweni sobukhosi sikaDavide emzini ongcwele, yebo mina, uYeshua HaMashiach; kuphela ningawanaki amazwi obizo lwami! Kanjalo-ke isikhathi somusa, ngisho nezikhathi zokuqabuleka, manje seziyavalwa ngokuphelele. Futhi lokho kuphelela kuyofika ngokugcwele lapho ngiqoqa abathembekileyo beBandla lami kimi emafwini nalapho ukuhamba kwabo kunyakazisa umhlaba wonke.

Kuyonyakazisa umhlaba ngokoqobo, futhi ukuzamazama okukhulu kuyodla iBhabhiloni eyimfihlo, futhi iyodabuka phakathi nendawo lapho abangcwele bami besuka. Iyoshiswa ngokuphelele ngomlilo ovela esibhakabhakeni njengoba imicibisholo izowa. Izingubo zayo ziyogutshuzelwa ngamanzi avela empumalanga nasentshonalanga! Ngoba ingelosi enamandla iyothatha itshe elinjengelitshe elikhulu lokusila, iliphonse olwandle, ithi, Kanjalo-ke ngodlame lowo muzi omkhulu iBhabhiloni uyophonswa phansi, futhi awusoze waphinde watholakala. Ngoba kulotshiwe kanjalo futhi kuyokwenziwa kanjalo!

Ngoba kukhulu ukwahlulelwa kwayo, futhi ukwahlulela sekuqalile endlini kaNkulunkulu. Amasonto ayo ahlubukayo ayobhujiswa, ngoba ngiwahlulele wonke angasiwo awami, ngisho nawo wonke amasonto omkokotelo akhuluma amanga amakhulu ngokuzenzisa. Bhabhiloni eyimfihlakalo, O, ukube waphendukela kimi, Jesu Kristu, washiya izindlela zakho ezimbi, ngoba ngangiyokusindisa, futhi akekho, kungaba yimina noma uYahweh, onenjabulo ekwahluleleni osekuzodedelwa.

Kodwa nokho, ukwahlulela kuyofika njengoba kumenyezelwe, futhi lokho kwahlulela kuyoba nzima kube phakade. Ngoba ngangiphendukele kwabeZizwe isikhashana, futhi manje sengizobashiya abeZizwe ngiphendukele emhlambini engavela kuwo, ngisho u-Israyeli. Nakuba noma ngubani obiza igama leNkosi kuze kube sekupheleni uyosindiswa, noma engumJuda noma oweZizwe, ngoba insindiso ayiboshwe yimincele yamazwe.

O, Bhabhiloni eyimfihlo, amabhuloho akho ayowa; imigwaqo yakho iyogumbuqelwa, iphuke, ingabe isahambeka; imigudu yakho yokuhlinzeka isivele iyabhidlika futhi maduze izoba incithakalo. Ngeke kulethwe miphako kuwe, ngoba akekho oyokuza azokulekelela, futhi uyoba wedwa ngehora lakho lokuchithakala, O sifesi esikhulu. Ngeke ube nayo injabulo, futhi labo abasindayo bayozithola belwela nje ukuphuza amanzi. Indlala iyogcwala ezweni. Futhi isifo esiwumshayabhuqe esikhulu siyokwehlela, ngisho nezilwane ziyohlasela, ngoba izikhathi sezifikile.

Amabutho abomvu uhulumeni wakho wamambuka owawavumela ukuba angene emincele yakho ayobulala yonke indoda, owesifazane, nengane abangazifezi izinjongo zawo. Ngoba ayobulala futhi agqilaze bonke abatholayo. Angamabutho esilo aqoqwe emagumbini omane omhlaba ngenxa yale njongo, futhi isilo esifihliwe nomprofethi wamanga ofihliwe bawahlanganisile.

Abanalo uzwelo, abanaso isihe, awukho umusa, abazisoli, futhi bayabazonda kakhulu abakhileyo eBhabhiloni eyimfihlakalo ngenxa yezono ezesabekayo abaholi benu abazenza emazweni abo. Futhi sonke lesi sikhathi abaningi benu babejabulela izimpi zenu ezingenabulungiswa njengoba nanibuka ngokuzijabulisa komabonakude benu, njengoba nanithokoza kakhulu ezonweni zabaholi benu.

Ninikeze imvume kubaholi benu enavumelana nezono zabo ngevoti, ngakho-ke niyohlanganyela nabo ekwahlulelweni, ngoba nizifanele isabelo esiphindwe kabili sokwahlulelwa, futhi kuyonehlela ngokuzumayo.

Kanjalo-ke uMlobokazi wami uyovela esihlalweni sami sokwahlulela, futhi abanye bayolahlekelwa, abanye bayothwala umvuzo omkhulu, futhi bonke bayokwamukela ngokugcwele isibambiso asebevele besamukele: Ngoba ngiyobanika imizimba emisha, engenasono, engasoze yonakala! Kanjalo-ke izimvu zamanothi ziyovulwa, ngoba nakuba usizi nokuhlupheka sekwandile emhlabeni iminyaka eminingi manje, akusilo ulaka lukaNkulunkulu noma ulaka lwewundlu.

Abaningi benu bacabanga ngobuwula ukuthi kufanele bakhuthazelele ulaka lukaNkulunkulu, becabanga ngokuzidla ukuthi bayakwazi ukuthwala lokho okuzayo. Qondisisani ukuthi ngilisindisile iBandla lami olakeni, ngoba ngeza kwabami nakwabeZizwe ngesikhathi somusa njengesivumelwano sokuthula, nginikela ukuthula nokubuyisana kuBaba wezibani, ngisho u-El Shaddai! Futhi labo engibasindisile, ngokwentando yabo ekhululekile bamukele umusa wami ongafanelwe, ngakho-ke landelani isibonelo sabo!

Futhi ngiyobeka uphawu lwami kwabayizi-144,000 bami ezizweni ezilandelayo, ngisho abayizi-12,000 esizweni ngasinye kwezilandelayo: uJuda, uRubeni, uGadi, u-Asheri, uNafetali, uManase, uSimeyoni, uLevi, u-Isakare, uZebuloni, uJosefa, noBenjamini. Ngakho zwanini izwi lami!

Futhi niyongenzela imisebenzi eminingi emikhulu, futhi niyohola abaningi ensindisweni ezweni elifayo nelinodlame. Ngoba uSathane usevele ulungiselele inkohliso enkulu futhi uyokwenza konke okusemandleni akhe ukuqamba amanga, ukufihla, nokufihla ukuhlangana okukhulu emafwini osekuzokwenzeka. Amanga akhe asevele egxilile ezingqondweni nasezinhliziyweni zenu ngemfundo yenu yamaJesuit ephika uBaba wami futhi elethwa kini ngabawileyo. Benze zonke izinto zibe mbumbulu, ngisho nokuholela abaningi benu ekukholweni ukuthi ngaphezu komhlaba kunesikhala esingapheli samaplanethi nokunye okuphilayo, futhi abaningi bayoba lula kangangokuthi bayogwinya la manga. Ngisho nalabo amagama abo angakaze abhalwe eNcwadini yokuPhila, ngoba bayokwamukela uphawu lwabo.

Yazini, bantu bami, futhi nizwe isixwayiso sami. Akukho kuphila kwamanye amaplanethi! Fundisisani ukuze nifunde, ngoba amaplanethi ayizinkanyezi ezizulazulayo ezimiselwe ukumemezela umsebenzi wezandla zikaYahweh emkhathini ngaphansi kwesibhakabhaka. Laba ngeke babe abafokazi abavela kwamanye amazwe azungezayo angamanga; nokho, abasiwo abomhlaba, ngoba bayizingelosi eziwileyo zasezulwini, futhi benze izinengiso eziningi. Ningabakholwa!

Ngakho-ke emlotheni womgibeli wehhashi nasekubhujisweni okukhulu kuyovela isilo nomprofethi wamanga, ngoba imfihlakalo yobubi isivele iyasebenza. Ngoba iBhabhiloni eyimfihlakalo inikelwe ezandleni zabo nomfelandawonye owakhekayo, ngisho nezimpondo eziyishumi ziyoyibhubhisa, ngoba zingamakhosi ayishumi anikela konke kwadrako, isilo, nomprofethi wamanga. Ngoba uma umvimbeli esusiwe, bayovela ngokugcwele ngokweMibhalo; ngoba esiphithiphithini kuphuma uhlelo lwabo olusha, ngisho uhlelo lukaDrako, ngoba ungamandla negunya labo, ngoba unikiwe kanjalo ngendlela elinganiselwe isikhashana esifushane kakhulu.

Udrako unolaka olukhulu futhi uyohamba ngomoya kaHamani enze zonke izinto zikaKristu zingekho emthethweni, futhi noma ngubani ohlotshaniswa negama lami, ngisho uJesu Kristu, uyozingelwa abulawe. Futhi labo abangathandi ukuphila kwabo kuze kube sekupheleni bayosindiswa babe nomvuzo omkhulu. Ngakho-ke gcina ingqondo nenhliziyo yakho enjabulweni ebekwe phambi kwakho, ngoba ngaleyo ndlela ngaqinisekisa insindiso yakho osizini olubuhlungu okwakungolwakho, ngaphandle kokuthi ngathatha ngokuzithandela isijeziso sakho ngakhokhela ngokugcwele izono zesintu kanye kuphela, ngisho naphakade.

Isilo siyohamba ngokushesha, ngoba siyoqhubeka izinyanga ezingama-42 sikhuluma izinto ezinkulu zokuhlambalaza. Siyolwa nabangcwele, futhi kunikiwe ukuba sibanqobe. Siyonquma amakhanda abaningi. Siyokwenza izibonakaliso nezimangaliso eziningi zamanga, futhi emlotheni kuyovela uhlelo lwaso olusha. Futhi umprofethi wamanga uyokhomba kuso asidumise futhi enze bonke bakhonze umfanekiso waso, ngoba lezi zinto zilotshwe ngokucacile eMibhalweni. Kanjalo-ke isilo siyohlanza umhlaba kunoma yini enhle, engcwele, neyigugu. Nokho, ngiyobafihla abathembekileyo bami, futhi bayokwenza izimangaliso eziningi, futhi abaningi bayosindiswa.

Kodwa umprofethi wamanga, ngisho isilo esivela emhlabeni, siyokwenza bonke, abancane nabakhulu, abacebile nabampofu, abakhululekile nabayizigqila, bamukele uphawu esandleni sabo sokunene noma emabunzini abo: Futhi ukuthi kungabikho muntu ongathenga noma athengise, ngaphandle kwalowo onophawu, noma igama lesilo, noma inombolo yegama laso. Ngakho-ke qondisisani ukuthi ukuhlanganyela emphakathini wabo omusha wamanga kuyodinga uphawu, futhi bonke abathatha uphawu abasoze bathethelelwa. Futhi bayoba nesabelo sabo kulelo chibi elikhulu elivutha umlilo nesibabule ingunaphakade, futhi kanjalo intuthu yokuhlushwa kwabo iyokhuphuka.

Ofakazi bami ababili bayovuka, futhi bayoprofetha bembethwe indwangu yamasaka! Lezi yizihlahla ezimbili zomnqumo, nezinti zezibani ezimbili ezimi phambi kukaNkulunkulu womhlaba. Futhi akekho oyokwazi ukumelana nabo, ngoba bayoprofetha izinyanga ezingama-42. Futhi uma kukhona umuntu ofuna ukubalimaza, umlilo uphuma emlonyeni wabo, ushwabadele izitha zabo: futhi uma kukhona umuntu ofuna ukubalimaza, kufanele abulawe ngale ndlela. Laba banamandla okuvala izulu, ukuze lingani ngezinsuku zokuprofetha kwabo: futhi banamandla phezu kwamanzi ukuwaphendula igazi, nokushaya umhlaba ngazo zonke izinhlupho, kaningi ngendlela abathanda ngayo.

Futhi lapho sebeqedile ubufakazi babo, isilo esikhuphuka emgodini ongenamkhawulo siyolwa nabo, sibanqobe, sibabulale. Futhi bayoshiya izidumbu zabo emigwaqweni izinsuku ezintathu nengxenye, futhi umhlaba wonke uyojabula, bese bevuswa bathathwe bayiswe emazulwini.

Umprofethi wamanga usevele wazithandekisa kulabo abasezweni ukuze bacabange ukuthi unguMesiya wabo nenkosi. Ungumakhi wethempeli wamanga futhi uvela esizweni sakwaDani, futhi akayena uKoresi. Uyosekela ngokugcwele isilo sokuqala esivela olwandle. Ngoba isilo sokuqala siyophoqelela ngonya iNoahide ezizweni ngobugebengu obukhulu, futhi umprofethi wamanga uyobusa esihlalweni sobukhosi okungesona esakhe, ngoba ngesami, ngisho esikaYeshua HaMashiach. Kanjalo-ke ithempeli liyokwakhiwa, futhi umprofethi wamanga uyokwenza izibonakaliso nezimangaliso eziningi zamanga, ngoba unikezwe amandla afanayo nesilo sokuqala, kanjalo-ke laba ababili bayosebenza ngenhloso eyodwa nomqondo owodwa, ngisho nangenhloso nezinjongo zalowo drako omkhulu wamanga, kodwa akalutho futhi akanamazinyo, ngakho-ke ningamesabi!

Ngoba uyazi ukuthi ngiyeza ukuzobhubhisa umbuso wakhe, ngoba akayona inkosi kodwa uyisitha esehlulwe ngokuphelele, esingenathemba neze. Ngakho-ke ningamesabi futhi nihlale nigxile kimi. Ngoba bhekani, nginitshela lezi zinto ngaphambi kokuba zenzeke ukuze nibe nokholo kimi futhi nibe nokholo kuBaba wami ongithumile, ngisho uYahweh uNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade, nami indodana yakhe, uYeshua HaMashiach, oyogcwalisa izithembiso ezenziwa kokhokho.

Kanjalo-ke ekupheleni kwezinyanga zokuqala ezingama-42 isinengiso sencithakalo siyokwenzeka ngesandla somprofethi wamanga, ngisho isilo esizovela emhlabeni. Phakathi nezinyanga zesibili ezingama-42, ngisho nangaleso sikhathi kuyokwenzeka usizi olukhulu kunawo wonke owake waba khona kanye nesinengiso sencithakalo, futhi uyozimemezela enguNkulunkulu! Abaningi bayocindezelwa futhi abaningi bayobhubha, kodwa labo abalalela izwi lami bayoyiswa endaweni ehlane lapho beyofihlwa khona bagcinwe bephephile isikhashana, ngoba lezi ziyoba izikhathi ezinonya kakhulu, futhi lezi zikhathi zokuvivinywa nokulingwa ziyoba nzima kakhulu. Khona-ke niyobona amazulu evulekile, futhi niyobona mina, Jesu Kristu, ngihola ngiphuma emazulwini leyo mpi yasezulwini enkulu nenamandla, futhi iyoba yibutho elingakaze libe khona, naphakade! Ngoba uMlobokazi wami uyoba nami. Futhi ngiyochoboza isilo nomprofethi wamanga nawo onke amabutho abo nazo zonke izinengiso zabo abazenze ngendlela enengekayo, kanjalo-ke bayobhujiswa ngisho nangokukhanya kokufika kwami nangaleyo nkemba ebukhali esika nhlangothi zombili ephuma emlonyeni wami, futhi ngeke bame kodwa bayobhujiswa ngokuphelele. Futhi isilo nomprofethi wamanga bayophonswa bephila kulelo chibi lomlilo nesibabule elivuthayo, futhi abasoze bakhululwa ekuhlushweni kwabo, ngoba bazenze bafaneleka.

Khona-ke kuyokwenzeka ukuvuka kwabalungileyo, lapho abangcwele basendulo nabangcwele bosizi beyovuswa kwabafileyo babe sekuphileni okusha, futhi ngeke besafa, futhi ngiyobanika umvuzo omkhulu, onjengalowo ongakaze ungene ezinhliziyweni zabantu ngalesi sikhathi. Ngoba ngiyiNkosi enesihe neNkosi enomusa, futhi ngiyikho konke kobuNkulunkulu ngokomzimba.

Kanjalo-ke ukubusa kwami kweminyaka eyinkulungwane emhlabeni kuzoqala, futhi ukulunga kuyobusa emhlabeni, futhi ngiyobusa izizwe ngentonga yensimbi, futhi umhlaba uyogcwala izindlela zikaYahweh. Ngoba ngiza nenqwaba yamakhosi nabapristi, futhi bayobusa esihlalweni sami sobukhosi, futhi bayobusa ngokulunga. Futhi ingonyama iyolala newundlu.
