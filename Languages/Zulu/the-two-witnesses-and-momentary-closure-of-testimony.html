Ubufakazi Obukabili: U-Israyeli
Okubabayo Nokumnandi
Januwari 13, 2025

Ofakazi Ababili Nokuvalwa Kwesikhashana Kobufakazi

Kusuka kuYahweh, uMina Omkhulu Enginguye:

Bhekani! Ngize ukunitshela ngofakazi bami ababili, izinti zami ezimbili zezibani, neminqumo yami emibili, nokunika abayizimpumputhe ukubona ngokwembula izimfihlo ebezigcinwe ziyimfihlo emhlabeni ngokuhlakanipha kwami, ukuze nazi isiqiniseko sezinto ezizayo nokuthi indlela yensindiso icaciswe kubo bonke abafundayo futhi abanokholo lokulalela la Mazwi Evangeli Laphakade.

Bhekani! NginguYahweh, yena owadala amazulu nomhlaba ngezinjongo zami zaphakade nenkazimulo, futhi nginguye oyobuyisela indalo ekonakaleni, ukuze izindlela zokulunga zikaYahweh zigcwale umhlaba ekulungeni ebusweni bukaJesu Kristu. Ngaleyo ndlela izindlela zami ziyogcwalisa umhlaba eMbusweni kaKristu Jesu Weminyaka Eyinkulungwane, umbuso lapho kungekho nto embi eyongenamo, umbuso lapho umhlaba ubuswa ngokulunga, nokuthula kweqiniso nokubuyiselwa kuyogcwala umhlaba, nabakholwayo bezizukulwane zonke bayohamba kuwo ngokuthula okuphelele nokuzola ngoba izindlela zikaYahweh ziyosibekela umhlaba ngokulunga. Isikhathi lapho uSathane eboshiwe ekuhlushweni nasekuthuthumeleni okukhulu ngesijeziso sakhe esizayo saphakade, lapho eyobona isilo nomprofethi wamanga bephonswa emalangabini esijeziso saphakade futhi beshiswa, ngoba bonke bakufanele lokho, kodwa uyogcwalisa izinhloso zami zaphakade zokulunga ngaphambi kokuba aboshwe futhi aphonswe emlilweni kuze kube phakade, angaphinde aphume, futhi ngiyobeka uphawu futhi ngeke baphinde bakhunjulwe.

Futhi ngiyonikeza (futhi ngijabulele) ukuphumula kosuku lweSabatha nabanqobi bami abalungileyo nabo bonke abavunyelwe ukungena eMbusweni kaKristu Jesu Weminyaka Eyinkulungwane! (Ngoba iminyaka eyinkulungwane injengosuku olulodwa emehlweni ami, nosuku olulodwa lunjengeminyaka eyinkulungwane, neminyaka eyinkulungwane iyiminyaka eyinkulungwane.) Bonke labo abayobe bebuthiwe futhi bevusiwe kuzo zonke izizukulwane, zamanje nezadlula, kuKristu Jesu; ngaphambi kokuba uSathane aphonswe emalangabini, ngaphambi kokuba kufike isizini yakhe encane yokugcina. Ekupheleni kwayo leyo nyoka endala iyoboshwa unomphela futhi iphonswe emlilweni, ingaphinde isuke esijezisweni sayo esingapheliyo emalangabini ashisayo kakhulu esibabule esivuthayo. Ngoba uSathane nabo bonke abalandela amanga akhe abanamandla ngempela emehlweni ami aphakade! Ubani ongahlula uYahweh, uMina Omkhulu Enginguye, noma beqamba amanga futhi bezigabisa? Noma ubani ongaguqula intando yami yaphakade nezimemezelo zezahlulelo ezimiselwe ukuwa ngokugcwele lapho umvimbeli wami esuswa ngokuzumayo manje ngokukhala kwecilongo elinikezwe ukuba likhale ngesikhathi esiqondile nesiphelele engisikhethile ohlelweni lwami lwaphakade kusukela emaphakadeni adlule. Umzuzwana esikhathini osuseduze ukuba wenzeke lapho isithembiso sokulunga sihlangana nokugcwaliseka kwaphakade ezikhathini zamanje.

Ngoba nginguye, uMina Omkhulu Enginguye, yena owazi isiphetho kusukela ekuqaleni nokuqala kusukela esiphethweni. Ngakho-ke, ngiza ukwembula izici zohlelo lwami lwaphakade lwensindiso obeluyimfihlakalo, izinto ezisafihliwe eMfihlakalweni kaNkulunkulu esezophela ekulungeni kwaphakade. Njengoba kwalotshwa nguJohane uMambuli njengoba wabhala ngokwethembeka amazwi awanikezwa nguKristu Jesu! Kanjalo kulotshiwe ukuthi ezinsukwini zezwi leNgelosi yesikhombisa, lapho izoqala ukushaya icilongo, ngisho nemfihlakalo kaNkulunkulu iyophela, njengoba ememezele ezincekwini zakhe abaprofethi.

Nokho, abaningi kakhulu baphunyukwe yiqiniso lala Mazwi ngabathakathi bamanga benkathi abakhokhelwa yisihlangu esibomvu ukuze bahlanekezele imibhalo yami engcwele ngamanga enkathi nawobuZiyoni namanga ezimfundiso zokushintshwa, amanga anikezwe ngobuqili yiziwiweyo ngabantu ababi ukuze bakhohlise izinhliziyo zabathobekileyo nabaphansi. Futhi bayokhohlisa abakhethiweyo bami uma abakhethiweyo bami bekhetha ukunganaki amazwi ami okulunga ngokuzidla becabanga ukuthi ngeke bakhohliswe ngenxa yokukhethwa kwabo. Kodwa bonke abadukayo ngaleyo ndlela ngokwenqaba iqiniso ngenxa yamanga abo bayokhohliswa. Ngakho-ke, qondani bantu bami ukuthi icilongo lesikhombisa alifani necilongo elinikezwe ukukhala ukuqeda izikhathi zami zomusa nokuqoqa abakholwayo kuKristu, ngakho-ke ningakhohliswa!

Noma kunjalo, iqiniso likaYahweh limi liqinile phakade futhi aliguquleki, nezwi lami alinakwephulwa, nezwi lami liyofeza lokho engikuhlosile kunoma ubani izwi lami lithunyelwe kuye. Futhi akekho ongamisa izinhlelo zami zaphakade! Futhi akekho ongamisa uKristu Jesu, okuhlala kuye ngokugcwele uhlelo lwami lwaphakade lwensindiso. Yena engimnike amandla phezu kwazo zonke izidalwa endalweni yami futhi ngamnika ukubusa okugcwele phezu kwendalo yami kuze kube yilapho izitha zakhe zenziwa isenabelo sezinyawo zakhe, lapho ezokwethula umbuso othotshisiwe ku-Adonai Yahweh.

Nokho, ngeqiniso laphakade, akukho ukuphela kokubusa okulungileyo kukaYeshua HaMashiach, oyobusa ngokulunga kuze kube phakade, nomakoti wakhe oyohlala naye kuze kube phakade nasemaphakadeni ezikhundleni zabo zegunya nasemiyalelweni yenkonzo. Futhi lokho kungenxa yokuthi bamukela uJesu waseNazaretha njengeNkosi yabo yaphakade ezikhathini zomusa nesihe, futhi bamthanda ngokugcwele ngokulalela amazwi akhe, okungamazwi abayonqoba ngawo ngokugcwele zonke izinto zombuso wesitha kuze kube phakade.

Ngeqiniso laphakade, ingubo kaMelkisedeki ihlezi iqinile emahlombe kaYeshua HaMashiach aphakade, ingasuki! Ngoba kuze kube phakade nasemaphakadeni onke iNkosi Yokulunga iyobusa kuze kube phakade ngohlelo lwayo lwabapristi, amakhosi, nabaprofethi abayocwalisa umhlaba ngezindlela zokulunga nolwazi lukaYahweh, kuze kube sezikhathini lapho iziprofetho ziyophela kuze kube phakade ngoba ngiyokwakha indawo yami yokuhlala nabantu ezulwini elisha nasemhlabeni omusha osuzokwakhiwa ngokulunga nobungcwele beqiniso! Indalo lapho ulwazi lokuhle nokubi luyophela phakade futhi kuyotholakala kuphela ukulunga nothando nokuthula kuze kube phakade, kungasuki noma kungancishiswa noma konakaliswe nakancane. Lokhu kungenxa yokuthi ukonakala kuyophonswa emalangabini kuze kube phakade.

Nokho, kunjalo ukuthi iziwiweyo zimbozile futhi zasakaza amanga azo phezu kwendalo yami, zisakaza ngokungaphezi ngobuchwepheshe bazo besihogo, ukuze umuntu wemvelo alawulwe futhi aphathwe ngamafrikhwensi azo nobuchwepheshe besihogo. Ngisho nezithombe ezilawula ingqondo neziguqula ukuziphatha, izindaba ezimbi, neziphonso ezisakazwa ezibukweni zabo ezimnyama lapho abaningi behlezi ebumnyameni beluthwe yizithombe namanga abo. Futhi ngeqiniso, iziwiweyo zithakathile izixuku ngobuthi ne-pharmakeia okunikezwa njengomuthi, ukuze zifakwe ubuthi futhi zibulawe ngakho. Nokho, ubuthi bazo nobuchwepheshe bazo nezibuko zazo kuyobhujiswa kuze kube phakade, nokusakaza kwazo kuyophela phakade, lapho ziphonsa iziphonso zazo futhi zivusa izinkanuko nezifiso zomuntu wemvelo esonweni esikhulu futhi nobuchwepheshe bazo obungenamsebenzi buyoba yize, bungaphinde bube khona.

Nokho, kunjalo, ngavumela iziwiweyo ukuba zimboze indalo yami ngamanga azo, ukuze bonke abalandela izindlela zazo ezinengekayo bakhethe ukuhlala efeni labo laphakade lesibabule esivuthayo ngoba babenamajabulo ekungalungini ngokona kuYahweh, uMina Omkhulu Enginguye, nakwabangenacala endalweni yami, ababenzele okubi. Ngokuhlakanipha kwami kwaphakade nothando endalweni yami, ngavumela ngamunye kusukela ku-Adamu ukuba akhethe ukuthi uzokhonza bani: kungaba yisono kuze kube sekufeni, noma ukulalela ukulunga kuze kube sekuphileni okuphakade.

Zinjalo izindlela zikaYahweh, ngoba izindlela zami ziyizindlela zokulunga, ukuthula, nothando! Uthando olungapheliyo lwaphakade, lapho yonke indalo yami, ehlanganiswe nezidalwa ezinhlobonhlobo ezakhiwe ngokumangalisayo zazo zonke izinhlobo nezinjongo, iyophila ngokuzwana okulungileyo nothando. Ngoba indalo yami ihloselwe ukuba ibe yindwangu yokuphila ehlobene ebuswa ngokulunga nothando ukuze kuzuze ngokugcwele futhi kube yinkazimulo yazo zonke izidalwa zami, lapho kungekho nesidalwa esisodwa esiyoke sizuze ngezindleko zokulahlekelwa komunye, kodwa bonke bayokwabelana ngokukhululeka ngobuningi obungapheliyo obusezinqolobaneni zami obuhloselwe ukuzuzisa nenkazimulo yalabo abalalela imiyalo yami elungileyo ngezinhliziyo zabo ngothando kuBaba wabo waphakade, enginguye ngempela!

Nokho, kunjalo, ukuthi NginguMina Omkhulu Enginguye futhi angiwavumeli futhi angivumelani namanga abo, futhi ngeke ngivumele amanga abo ukuba aqhubeke. Kunjalo, ngiyamemezela, ukuthi mina, Yahweh, uMina Omkhulu Enginguye onguNkulunkulu Waphakade Wokulunga ngiyodalula ngokugcwele zonke izici zombuso wesitha omiselwe ukuphakama isizini emfushane kakhulu enikezwe udrako ukuba abuse, futhi kuyoba ukubusa okulinganiselwe kakhulu. Ngokunjalo, ngiyodalula iziwiweyo ezizifihlile phakathi kwendalo yami, okufanele zisishulwe futhi zidalulwe ehlazweni lazo laphakade ngokugcwele ngaphambi kokuba ziphonswe emalangabini esijeziso kuze kube phakade, lapho impethu yazo ingafi. Kanjalo isimemezelo sami siyophuma, ngoba lokho okumenyezelwe enkazimulweni nasemandleni esihlalo sobukhosi sikaYahweh, uMina Omkhulu Enginguye, akunakuze kuguqulwe, futhi kuyokwenzeka ngqo njengoba kumenyezelwe. Selah!

Nokho, kunjalo, ukuthi angikaze ngizifihle ngokugcwele iziwiweyo, futhi sengivele ngiqalile ukuzidalula ngokugcwele futhi isisitho siyancipha. Leso sisitho sokufihla engizifihle ngaso kubantu, nomvimbeli uyosuswa ohlanganiswe ngamaphuzu ahlukahlukene okuzithiba ayosuswa ngokugcwele lapho uMikayeli enikezwa umyalo lapho icilongo lokugcina likhala ngokuzumayo manje. Kanjalo iqhwa liyowa, nomhlaba uyoguqulwa, futhi bayoba nempi yabo enkulu futhi abaningi bayobhubha ngayo, njengoba kulotshiwe futhi kwabikezelwa nguJohane uMambuli ebufakazini bakhe baphakade. Kanjalo ngininika ukuqonda ukuthi ngesikhathi sokubuthwa kwabakholwayo kuKristu ngiyosusisa leso sisitho sokufihla okuyingxenye ngokuzumayo nangamandla njengoba ngadabula isisitho sethempeli kabili kusuka phezulu kuze kube phansi okuphawula ukuphela kwenkonzo kaLevi nokubuyiselwa okuphelele kukaMelkisedeki. Ngale ndlela ngingasusa ubumpumputhe obuyingxenye kubantwana bakwa-Israyeli kuze kube phakade, kungaphinde kubuyele emajuba ami.

Futhi lokhu kuyokwenzeka ngokuzumayo nangokungalindelekile ngoba umhlaba awulindele ukuba uKristu Jesu akhanyise imikhawulo namazulu ngokugcwele ngenkazimulo yakhe yaphakade kulezi zikhathi zemihelo yokubeletha efinyelela umvuthwandaba, kunjalo, ngoba umhlaba awunakholo ngempela. Futhi kulabo abenqatshelwa ukungena emazulwini lapho leso sivalo esikhulu sivalwa, abaningi abayizoni ezimbi bayobhubha futhi bathathwe ekwahlulelweni kuze kube phakade, labo abamlahle ngokugcwele uJesu waseNazaretha ngokuhlambalaza okukhulu nokuzidla ngokumelene nomnsalo wami ongcwele, nabanye abaningi abayizoni ngokufanayo ekwenqabeni kwabo okunokuhlubuka abayowela phakade esahlulelweni esinzima ngenxa yobubi ababenzile. Ububi obufanayo abaqhubeka bebenza ngokuzinikela okukhulu, futhi ngeke baphunyuke.

Qondani ukuthi bonke abafuna ukwephula imingcele yemvelo nezimiso zendalo yami ngokweqa leyo mingcele nezimiso, befuna ukuziguqula babe yizinengiso (ngezindlela zeziwiweyo) bayowela ngokufanayo esahlulelweni saphakade: Ngoba lesi yisono esingenakuthethelelwa, ukuhlambalaza uYahweh onguMoya oNgcwele, befuna ukuzishintsha esithombeni sikaYahweh abadalwa ngaso ekuqaleni. Ngakho-ke, lahlanani nezinganekwane zabo zamaJesuit eziguqule ubunkulunkulu beqiniso baba yizinganekwane ezalethwa nguNimrode ngabathathu bakhe bokuqala abangcwele! Ngoba ubathathu obungcwele bukaBaba, iNdodana, nomoya ongcwele bonakalisiwe emqondweni nasehlosweni labo lokuqala, kodwa iqiniso likaYahweh alinakufihlwa yizinto ezibuthakathaka nezingelutho noma beqamba amanga! Noma kunjalo, uJesu waseNazaretha unguYahweh owabonakaliswa enyameni, nomoya wami ongcwele wobudodana ugcwalisa ngokugcwele abakholwayo kuKristu abayingxenye yobunkulunkulu ngempela ekubuseni kwabo noKristu Jesu ngaphansi koHlelo lukaMelkisedeki njengabaPristi namaKhosi kuze kube phakade! Ngakho-ke, qondani ukuthi ningobani kuKristu bantwana bami ngoba iziwiweyo zifuna ukufihla iqiniso ngani ngezinganekwane zazo ezingelona iqiniso.

Nokho, abakholwayo kuKristu baphilile futhi bafa futhi basahamba ebusweni bomhlaba ngokholo olukhulu, futhi lo mzuzwana omangalisayo futhi okhazimulayo ngeke ubafice bengazi ngoba bayokuqonda ukumemeza kwalelo khwelo elikhulu, futhi bayolizwa izwi lecilongo lokugcina oselivele linikeziwe esandleni nemiyalelo egcwele yokuba likhale lapho umyalo unikezwa nguKristu Jesu, ohlale eyenza ngokuphelele intando ka-Adonai Yahweh. Abakholwayo kuKristu bayovunyelwa ukungena kuleso sivalo esikhulu esiya emazulwini okungekho muntu emhlabeni ongavala noma avule, noma belele noma bephila ebusweni bomhlaba ngesikhathi sokubizwa kwabo!

Futhi ukuhlupheka okukhulu kuyofica abangakholwa abavuma uJesu waseNazaretha ngenhliziyo engakholwa, benqaba ukuphila impilo yokuphenduka ngoba babethanda izono zabo. Kuyokwenzeka ukuthi labo abakhetha ukuqhubeka esonweni, abathi ngezinhliziyo ezingalaleli nezinenkani bavuma uJesu njengeNkosi kodwa baqhubeka emisebenzini yenyama, bayoshiyelwa emlilweni wokuhlanza ukuze kubhujiswe inyama, ukuze baphunyuke ekujabuleni kwesono nokuthi baphunyuke enyameni yabo ebathumbile ezinkanukweni zayo. Futhi abaningi bayofinyelela ensindisweni ngokubiza uJesu waseNazaretha kuze kube sekuphefumuleni kwabo kokugcina, ngoba bekulokhu kuyimfuneko kwabakholwayo, ukuthi umuntu atholakale ethembekile kuze kube sekuphefumuleni kwakhe kokugcina, uma engeyena walabo abambalwa abathathwa bephila bayiswe ekuphepheni kwamazulu ezikhathini zabo zokwethembeka. Selah!

Futhi impi yabo iyovutha iqhubeke isikhathi sayo esimisiweyo, futhi bayovuka emlotheni wokubhujiswa kwabo. Udráko, isilo sasolwandle nesilo sasemhlabeni esingumprofethi wabo wamanga, bayovuka ngokuhlambalaza okukhulu ukuze babe nokubusa kwabo okunonya kodwa okufushane ngokomusa. Noma kunjalo, uYahweh, uMINA OMKHULU ENGINGUYE, akakaze azishiye engenabo ubufakazi, njengoba kufakazwa yisixuku sofakazi emibhalweni yami yaphakade nengcwele elondolozwe ngokupheleleyo emazulwini. Futhi njengoba kunjalo, ubufakazi bami obuningi bulahlekile ngenxa yesikhathi nokunganakwa yilabo ababenikwe umsebenzi wokuwanakekela. Qondani izikhathi, bantabami, futhi nilahle amanga abo enkohliso yezinkathi. Qondani ukuthi indalo ayinazo izigidi zeminyaka njengokwamanga alabo abawayo. Indalo iseduze neminyaka eyizinkulungwane eziyisithupha ubudala, futhi ngizihlukanisile lezi zikhathi zaba yizinkathi. Kwakukhona inkathi yesiphithiphithi esithile ngenxa yalabo abawayo abangcolisa izwe langaleso sikhathi ngezinengiso zabo, zona kanye lezo zinengiso ezabhujiswa ngokupheleleyo esahlulelweni esafinyelela kuvuthondaba ngozamcolo omkhulu ezinsukwini zikaNowa.

Nokho, uNowa waqhubeka ephila kuzamcolo, wasindiswa ephila emkhunjini kanye nenzalo yakhe (nezilwane nendalo) abanikezwa indawo yokuqhubeka ngoba uNowa wayeyindoda elungileyo nombusi olungileyo ngokohlelo lukaMelkisedeki, okwahlala phezu kwamahlombe akhe ingubo kwaze kwaba yilapho inceku yami iya ekuphumuleni kwayo kokuthula ize ivuswe maduzane kangaka manje ekulungeni. Ngokuhamba kwesikhathi uMose wakhishwa ephila emanzini aseNayile, wavuka ngamandla amakhulu, waqalisa inkathi yami yeTorah, ngisho nalapho u-Israyeli enziwa isizwe esikhulu nesinamandla esiyisithunzi sezinto ezizayo.

Futhi ngesikhathi esimisiweyo esifanele nesinembile uJesu waseNazaretha wazalwa yintombi, wavuka waba yiwundlu likaNkulunkulu, wazinikela waba yinhlawulo yaphakade ngenxa yesijeziso sikaMose, umhlatshelo engawamukela kuze kube phakade ukuze kuthethelelwe izono kuze kube phakade, kubo bonke abangcweliswayo ngawo, ngisho naseTestamenteni lakhe Elisha lokulunga uKristu Jesu aliqinisa walivala uphawu kuze kube phakade ngegazi lakhe elingenasono; ngakho-ke, asisekho isidingo somhlatshelo wezilwane. Ngeqiniso, uJesu waseNazaretha uyisisekelo sokulunga saso sonke isikhathi, isikhathi esafinyelela kuvuthondaba ngokunqotshwa kwalabo abawayo kuze kube phakade, isikhathi esibhalwe ngamazwi aphakade athi "SEKUPHELILE!"

Kanjalo-ke inkathi yami yomusa yaqaliswa ngalolo suku lwePhentekoste lapho nginika uKristu Jesu umoya wami ongcwele wobuNdodana awuthulula ngomyalo wami wokuhlenga bonke abakhetha ukuba ngamadodana kaNkulunkulu anikwe umoya wobuNdodana kulezi zikhathi. Kanjalo-ke kwaba wukuzalwa kweBandla likaKristu Jesu elanikezelwa umusa nesihe kwabezizwe, ngisho nangaphambi kokuba kwehle isahlulelo esinokhahlo nesokugcina. Izikhathi zomusa nesihe lapho izizwe ezilahlekileyo zendlu ka-Israyeli zazingabuthwa zibe ku-Israyeli kaNkulunkulu, kanye nanoma ubani ovela ezizweni owayebiziwe wasabela ngokwenza uJesu waseNazaretha iNkosi yakhe yaphakade. Nabantu bami abahlakazwa ngesijeziso bazulazula emhlabeni njengezihambi emazweni angaziwa, nabo bonke abavela ku-Adamu abamukela insindiso kulezi zikhathi bayoba yingxenye ka-Israyeli kaNkulunkulu kuze kube phakade, bangaphinde basuke ezimisweni zabo zaphakade zokukhonza uKristu Jesu iNkosi yabo eyenyukayo!

Ngakho qondani ukuthi izwe elikhona manje selifinyelele esiphelweni salo saphakade, futhi leli zwe elibi lamanje alisoze liphinde libe khona ukuze lihlupe labo engibathandayo kuze kube phakade. Futhi inkathi yami yomusa iyaqhubeka kuze kube ngumzuzwana wokugcina lapho kukhala khona lelo cilongo lokugcina lenkathi yomusa osekunikezwe umyalo wokuba likhaliswe. Ekuphethweni kosizi lukaJakobe, okuyiviki lamashumi ayisikhombisa likaDaniyeli, iviki leminyaka lapho usuku ngalunye luwumnyaka evikini lezinsuku eziyisikhombisa, uMbuso weMinyaka Eyinkulungwane kaYeshua HaMashiach uyomiswa ukuze kube wusuku lokuphumula, ekuphethweni kwalo okuyoba yiminyaka eyizinkulungwane eziyisikhombisa. Ngakho-ke, qondani ukuthi kuyobe kube nezinkathi ezintathu zeminyaka eyizinkulungwane ezimbili lilinye, nosuku lwesabatha lweminyaka eyinkulungwane oluzokwenzeka esiphethweni esingcwele nesilungileyo lapho indalo yami isihlengwe ngokupheleleyo esonweni, ingaphinde yone.

Ngakho qondani izikhathi eniphila kuzo, ngoba izimfihlakalo nezindlela zikaYahweh zilahleke kancane ngenxa yezono zabantu kulabo abahlala emhlabeni, zifihlwe ngisho nayizinkohliso zalabo abawayo.
