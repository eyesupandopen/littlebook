Okthoba 29, 2024

Sifikile Isikhathi Sokugcwaliseka: Yebo no-Ameni

Okuvela kuYeshua HaMashiach:

Lowo uMose ayala u-Israyeli ukuba amlalele futhi amhloniphe.

Thobelani izindlebe zenu ukuzwa amazwi kaYeshua HaMashiach kulesi sikhathi sokugcina, futhi nithobise izinhliziyo zenu ekulaleleni futhi nivumele izinhliziyo zenu zamukele imfundiso yami. Ngoba amazwi ami ayimpilo kulabo abawatholayo futhi aletha impilo nesithembiso, ngisho nasezikhathini zokuhlupheka okukhulu. Ngakho-ke, qondani ukuthi ukuhlupheka okukhulu sekufikile futhi inhlekelele iyasondela, nezikhathi sezikhona manje. Ngiqinisile, ngiqinisile, ngeqiniso laphakade yimina, uYeshua HaMashiach, iNgonyama yesizwe sakwaJuda ezayo ukwahlulela nokulwa, futhi qondani ukuthi akukho lutho olufihliwe emehlweni ami, futhi akukho lutho olufihliwe emehlweni kaYahweh, futhi ngiyakubona ukungabi nabulungiswa nokubulawa kwesizwe okwenziwa kubantu bezwe yizihambi ezivela kwiSinagoge likaSathane, futhi ngiyakuphindisela, isho iNkosi eLungileyo!

Ngoba ngeqiniso, iminyaka eminingi yamaJubili abawileyo bebelokhu bethuthukisa imfihlakalo yabo yobubi, kuze kube manje lapho amacebo abo eselungele ukufezeka ngokugcwele. Kodwa qondani ukuthi bavunyelwe kuphela ukufeza izinjongo zikaYahweh ezinhlelweni zakhe zaphakade zensindiso ukuze kube nokuhlengwa okuphelele kwesintu. Ngoba umuntu wemvelo ulaliswe ubuthongo ngokukhohliswa okukhulu nokunganaki okukhulu.

Ngakho-ke abawileyo basebenzisa isintu ukuqhubela phambili ukubhujiswa kwesintu, kuyilapho bebiza ukukhanya ngobumnyama nobumnyama ngokukhanya. Kunjalo-ke neSinagoge likaSathane elihlasela amazwe esithembiso, futhi ngiyakulisiphula ngokuphelele. Ngoba iSinagoge likaSathane lifuna ukuzithathela izithembiso zikaYahweh ezabhalwa zaqinisekiswa koKhokho basendulo. ISinagoge likaSathane ligcwele ukhula olubi abawileyo abalutshala phakathi kukakolweni wami, futhi isikhathi sokulusiphula sesifikile, futhi kulotshiwe kanjalo, futhi kuyokwenziwa kanjalo: Ngokuba bheka, usuku luyeza, luyavutha njengeziko; bonke abazidlayo, yebo, nabo bonke abenza okubi bayakuba yizinhlanga; usuku oluzayo luyakubashisa, isho iNkosi yamaBandla, lungabashiyeli mpande nagatsha.

Nokho, sekwenziwe ngabawileyo ukuthi isicabucabu sesiluke utando lwaso emhlabeni wonke, futhi ngokukhohlisa bayalwa. Manje yisikhathi sokuvuka kwabo futhi ukubusa kwabo okungcwele okufushane kuseduze. Ngoba uYahweh ebulungisweni bakhe uyonika umuntu wemvelo lokho kanye akufisa ngobuwula bakhe ekufuneni kwakhe injabulo yesono. Ngakho-ke ubumnyama nobubi umuntu wemvelo abufisayo, umuntu wemvelo manje uyokuba nakho. Nokho, uma iVangeli lami lifihliwe, lifihlelwe labo abadukileyo. Kubo unkulunkulu waleli zwe uphuphuthekise izingqondo zalabo abangakholwayo, ukuze ukukhanya kweVangeli elikhazimulayo likaKristu, ongumfanekiso kaNkulunkulu, kungakhanyi kubo. Kanjalo-ke umuntu wemvelo ekungazini kwakhe uhlangene nesicabucabu ekukhohliseni kwaso, ngoba ngokukhohlisa bayalwa.

Ngalokho, u-El Shaddai, ubathumela inkohliso enamandla ukuze bakholwe amanga enziwe ngabawileyo, ukuze bonke balahlwe abangakholwanga iqiniso, kodwa bajabulela ukungalungi. Nokho, ekwahluleleni kukaYahweh nomusa wakhe ohlala njalo, injabulo manje izosuswa emhlabeni, nezibiliboco umuntu wemvelo azilangazelela ngamandla kangaka ukuziqongelela zizosuswa emhlabeni. Kanjalo nenjabulo yesono iyosuswa emhlabeni, kangangokuthi abantu bayohlanya lapho befuna ukwanelisa izinkanuko zezinhliziyo zabo. Nokho, uYahweh akakhethi lezi zenzo ukuze abe nonya endalweni yakhe, kodwa ukuze ajezise abenzi bokubi futhi avuse labo ababeyokwenqaba ukuvuka ebuthongweni babo, ngoba ngenjabulo yesono nangenkokhelo yokungalungi benziwe bakhuluphala futhi baphubuka. Esikhundleni sokwahlulelwa okugcwele nokusheshayo, umuntu wemvelo unikezwa umusa ngoba umuntu wemvelo wehlulwe ngabawileyo.

Yebo, emuseni kaYahweh nesihe sakhe nasehlelweni lakhe laphakade, uYahweh ufuna ukuvusa umuntu wemvelo esonweni futhi abizele umuntu wemvelo ekulungeni. Kulolu bizo abaningi baye basabela ngokuzithandela ensindisweni yami ngezikhathi zami zomusa ngeVangeli leNsindiso. Kulezo zikhathi, insindiso yayiyisipho samahhala lapho umuntu ayengangenza iNkosi yakhe futhi abe nesikhathi sokusebenzela insindiso yakhe ngokwesaba nangokuthuthumela njengoba eza kimi ngokugcwele, ephuma ngokuphelele esonweni. Kanjalo-ke izikhathi zami zomusa sezifinyelele esiphelweni sazo, nakuba umusa nesihe kuhlala njalo kumi. Futhi maduze kuzokwenzeka ukuthi izikhathi zokulinda ngeke zisaba khona, nokuthi imibuso yaleli zwe iyoba imibuso yeNkosi yenu, nekaKristu wenu, futhi ngiyobusa kuze kube phakade nabathembekileyo efeni lethu laphakade engiyokwabelana ngalo ngokugcwele nabahluli bami.

Futhi isixuku esikhulu siyokuza kimi ngesikhathi sosizi lukaJakobe ukuze sisindiswe phakade. Bonke abakhuleka egameni leNkosi kuze kube sekupheleni bayosindiswa, kungakhathaliseki ulimi noma isizwe abakhuleka kuso. Futhi ngiyanyakaza ukusindisa u-Israyeli, futhi ngibiza izihlobo zami zeqiniso ezidukiswe yizinkolo zamanga futhi ezicindezelwe yiSinagoge likaSathane, futhi bayothola insindiso yaphakade, futhi ngiyosindisa uJakobe, onensindiso yakhe eyofezwa ngomlilo wokucwenga. Kodwa qondani ukuthi izikhathi zokuphila isikhathi eside nokuchuma kwezinto emhlabeni sezifike esiphelweni sazo kuze kube yilapho uMbuso wami usumiswa maduze, futhi umhlaba uboshwe ngamaketanga ulindele izahlulelo zawo zokugcina ukuba zithululwe ngolaka olukhulu. Bhekani! Ulaka lweWundlu luza kuqala, nezinhlupheko ezinkulu ziyokhukhula umhlaba njengesikhukhula sokubhujiswa okuphelele, futhi abaningi bayobhubha.

Nokho, izinhlupheko ezinkulu nosizi kuyokhululwa lapho izimvu kuzovulwa, nolaka olukhulu, nezinhlupho eziningi nezifo eziwumshayabhuqe, nendlala, nesomiso, nempi, nokungabi nabulungiswa, nokufa nokunye okuningi kuyofika ngokugcwele emhlabeni; nokho isixuku sezixuku siyovuka ezindleleni zaso zokuba mlomo-munandi. Futhi bayoba sebubini obukhulu nasekuhluphekeni komphefumulo lapho bebona iqiniso elingenakuphikwa lemibhalo nokuthi baphuthelwe kanjani ngenkani ithuba labo lensindiso nokubalekela ulaka nesahlulelo ekubuthaneni kweBandla lami elithembekileyo kimi. Nokho, ekuhluphekeni kwabo okubuhlungu nasekuhluphekeni, ngiyokhuthaza isinqumo esingenakunqotshwa ngaphakathi kwabo futhi ngiyobadonsela kimi ngokugcwele, bonke labo abeza kimi besekuhluphekeni nasebunzimeni nangezilingo zokufela ukholo. Futhi bayoba nomvuzo omkhulu, umvuzo omkhulu kangangokuthi izinhlupheko zabo ezizayo azifanele ukuqhathaniswa nenkazimulo yaphakade eyokwembulwa ngokugcwele kubo.

Kanjalo kulotshiwe, futhi kuyofezwa ngokuphelele: Laba yibo abaphuma osizini olukhulu, bahlanza izingubo zabo, bazenza zaba mhlophe egazini leWundlu. Ngalokho, baphambi kwesihlalo sobukhosi sikaNkulunkulu, bamkhonze imini nobusuku ethempelini lakhe; futhi lowo ohlezi esihlalweni sobukhosi uyohlala phakathi kwabo. Abayikulamba futhi, abayikoma futhi, nelanga aliyikushaya phezu kwabo, namanye amakhaza. Ngokuba iWundlu eliphakathi kwesihlalo sobukhosi liyakubelusa, libaholele emithonjeni ephilayo yamanzi; uNkulunkulu uyokwesula zonke izinyembezi emehlweni abo.

Kanjalo-ke isicabucabu sesiluke utando lwaso emhlabeni wonke ngokukhohlisa okukhulu, ngokuxhaphaza, nangokucindezela, futhi ngezindlela eziningi ubuthi besicabucabu busebenza ububi baso ngezindlela zaso zokukhohlisa nezinoshevu. Ngoba siluke utando lokukhohlisa kanye notando lwezikweletu kangangokuthi umhlaba manje usulukiwe ngokugcwele ekugonweni kwesicabucabu futhi ukhubazekile ngobuthi baso. Isicabucabu sihambe ngobuqili obukhulu nangokukhohlisa ngemfihlakalo yobubi, futhi umhlaba manje ususothandweni lwaso ngokugcwele. Futhi ngokukhohlisa okukhulu laba bazenzisi (abenziwe ngabawileyo) bahlasele amazwe njengezinkumbi. Ngendlela efanayo isicabucabu esisonga ngayo amaqanda aso kuze kube yilapho inqwaba yabancane baso bephuma bagijimele ukuyoluka izintando zabo eceleni kwaso. Kanjalo-ke isinagoge likaSathane selithathe umhlaba ngamandla nangobuqili.

Ngokuhamba kwesikhathi nangamaqhinga amaningi, bazenze baba namandla, ngoba banikwe lokho nguYahweh ezinhlelweni zakhe zaphakade zensindiso ukuze bafeze. Ngoba umuntu wemvelo uphuphuthekiswe ngamanga abo futhi ubalandela ngokungazi ekubhujisweni kwakhe. Ngalezo zinhlelo engikhuluma ngazo, zizofinyelela ekuhlukanisweni kokugcina phakathi kukakolweni nokhula naphakathi kwabazinikele ngokugcwele nalabo abathanda izono zabo futhi bazikhethela ngokuzithandela amalangabi othandweni lwabo olungcolile lokuzibhubhisa, futhi bayozisola phakade ngokukhetha kwabo ukuzihlanganisa nezinhloso zesicabucabu. Kanjalo-ke izitsha zentukuthelo yolaka lukaNkulunkulu ziyothululwa ngokugcwele.

Qondani ukuthi isicabucabu siyiSinagoge likaSathane manje oselungele ukunqoba umhlaba ohlelweni lwalo lokugcina lwesilo, okuyisibusiso engizosenza isiphetho saphakade, nokubhujiswa nesahlulelo saso esingalibali. Qondani, zithandwa zami, ukuthi iSinagoge likaSathane bonke bangabazenzisi abazenzisayo. Bangukhula lwama-Edomi okhokho babo engabhekana nabo njengewundlu likaNkulunkulu lapho ngazinikela ngokuzithandela ukukhokhela izono zesintu. Kanjalo ngabaveza, futhi ngabatshela ngqo ebusweni babo obubi: Nina ningabakayihlo udeveli, nezinkanuko zikayihlo niyakuzenza; wayengumbulali kwasekuqaleni, akemi eqinisweni, ngoba alikho iqiniso kuye. Lapho ekhuluma amanga, ukhuluma okwakhe; ngokuba ungumqambimanga, noyise wawo.

Kusukela ekuhlaseleni kwabo kokuqala izwe lesithembiso okungelona elabo ukuba balithathe, bahlukumezile, bahlukumeza, futhi babulala ngokuqondile izakhamuzi ezifanele zamazwe esithembiso. Bazenza izindlalifa ezifanele zensindiso, baze basebenzise kabi izingxenye zomthetho kaMose enkolweni yabo yamanga yobuTalmud. Ngaleyo ndlela, baphumphuthekisile abaningi abacabanga ukuthi banamathela entandweni yami, becabanga ukuthi ngiyobusisa noma ubani obusisa lolu khula futhi ngiqalekise noma ubani oqalekisa lolu khula. Ngakho abaningi babantu bami bazihlanganisa ngokungazi neSinagoge likaSathane ngokuvuma, futhi ngiyaninika umyalo wokuthi niphenduke ngokugcwele emangeni abo enizikhethele wona ukuze nikhohliseke.

Bancane bami, qondani ukuthi mina, uKristu Jesu, angikaze ngibhale amaBhayibheli okutadisha, kodwa lawa asetshenziswa njengamathuluzi okukhohlisa ngamanga anikezwe emaceleni ukuze kuhunyushwe iqiniso. Futhi abawileyo bashintshe amazwi ami emaBhayibhelini amanga; ngakho-ke, bhekani uhlu lwemibhalo yaseByzantium, ngisho neTextus Receptus lapho ngalondoloza khona amazwi ami. Ngakho-ke, yizani kimi zithandwa zami, nifunde kimi, futhi ngizoningenisa ngokugcwele eqinisweni lokukhohlisa kwabo kwamanje uma nizothobisa inhliziyo yenu ezwini lami elinikezwa yilabo abakhuluma egameni lami.

Qondani ukuthi yimina, uJesu waseNazaretha, owabhekana nalolu hlobo olufanayo lwabazenzisi ngenxa yamasiko abo omlomo ababewaphakamisa ngaphezu kwamazwi ahlanzekile kaYahweh anikezwe uMose. Ngoba uMthetho kaMose ungcwele, ulungile, futhi uqotho kuze kube phakade, futhi uma umuntu eqalekisa uMthetho kaMose, lowo muntu makabe oqalekisiwe. Ngoba mina, uJesu waseNazaretha, ngawugcwalisa uMthetho kaMose njengoba nganikela ngegazi lami elingenasono eKalvari ngezono zika-Adamu (nayo yonke inzalo yakhe), futhi uMthetho kaMose ugcinwa ngokugcwele yizithandwa zami ezihamba ngokholo kimi, uKristu Jesu, iNkosi yabo eyenyuka futhi engunaphakade.

Ngakho-ke qondani ukuthi akunakwenzeka ukwephula uMthetho kaMose lapho umuntu ehamba ngokholo njengoba ngimhola ngomoya wami engiwudalile kuye kuze kube phakade. Nokho, noma ubani ohamba enyameni wephula umthetho ngoba uMose weza ukuzolahla isono enyameni ngokunikeza lowo mthetho waphakade wenjabulo obhalwe ngomunwe kaYahweh uqobo. Futhi ayikho inyama elungile emehlweni ami noma emehlweni kaNkulunkulu waphakade wokulunga. Ngakho-ke, vumani izono zenu nokushiyeka kwenu kimi, iWundlu likaNkulunkulu elingumpristi wenu omkhulu waphakade ngokohlelo lukaMelkisedeki, futhi ngizonihlanza ngokugcwele egazini lami lokuhlawulela elachithelwa ukuhlanzwa kwabo bonke abeza kimi bezofuna insindiso.

Ngubani oke wezwa into enjalo? Ngubani oke wabona izinto ezinjalo? Umhlaba uyovezwa ngosuku olulodwa na? Noma isizwe siyozalwa ngesikhathi esisodwa na? Kodwa kunjalo-ke ukuthi isizwe sakwa-Israyeli sabuyela ebukhoneni baso njengesizwe ngesimemezelo sokhula, futhi kwakunguNkulunkulu uqobo owavumela izitha zakhe ukuba zizenze ngokungazi ekukhohliseni kwazo ukuthi zinguJakobe hhayi u-Esawu, ngoba kwakungu-Esawu owathengisa ubuzibulo bakhe komncane. Kanjalo-ke isicabucabu ngokungazi nangokuzidla sicabanga ukuthi sizoletha inkathi yegolide yaphakade emhlabeni, ngoba yizinkanuko zoYise abazokwenza, futhi abawileyo bangoYise babo, ngisho noSathane abanemvelo yakhe.

Nokho bahlasela amazwe ngobudlova obukhulu, ngoba banikwe lokho ukuze bafeze ukuvusa abaleleyo, ngisho nabo bonke abanobumpumputhe obuyingxenye abayosabela obizweni lukaMesiya wabo, uYeshua uMesiya weqiniso. Futhi qondani ukuthi ngiyabona zonke izenzo zabo ezimbi, futhi ngiyabona konke ukungabi nabulungiswa abakwenza kwabangenacala, futhi ngiyakubuyisela, isho iNkosi yaphakade uYahweh ayenze uMesiya, iNkosi, uMpristi Omkhulu, neNkosi! Yonke isenzo esibi silotshwe ngokuphelele emazulwini, futhi bayovuzwa phakade nangokuphelele efeni labo laphakade emalangabini ashisayo e-cobalt blue ebumnyameni obumnyama phakade.

Futhi ngiyambona uRaheli ekhalela abantwana bakhe njengoba behlushwa ukubulawa kwesizwe nokuhlushwa okungenabulungiswa yiSinagoge likaSathane, futhi ngiyakubuyisela, isho iNkosi yamakhosi neNkosi yamakhosi, futhi ngiyosindisa insali ka-Israyeli iphila ngeviki lamashumi ayisikhombisa likaDaniyeli, ngisho nosizi lukaJakobe. Ehlelweni laphakade likaYahweh, uyaqonda ukuthi abaningi ngeke basuke ekulungeni baye enjabulweni nasekukhohliseni kwesono. Ngakho esikhundleni sokuletha isahlulelo esisheshayo, uYahweh ukhethe ukuletha isahlulelo phezu kwesahlulelo ukuze izenzakalo zande futhi zibe zimbi kakhulu. Futhi isahlulelo siyokwanda ngaleyo ndlela kangangokuthi bonke abayokhetha ukubiza kimi ekuhluphekeni nasekuhluphekeni kwabo yilabo engiyobasindisa ngokuphelele, futhi bayoholwa baye ekuphepheni.

Kanjalo-ke izinhloso zabo manje ziyokwembulwa. Ngoba ngiyohola abangu-144,000 bami ukuba behlele eNtabeni iSiyoni, futhi bayongilandela isinyathelo ngesinyathelo njengoba behola insali yami kaJakobe baye ekuphepheni ehlane lapho uNkulunkulu alungise khona indawo yokuphepha kwabo, futhi bayondliwa banakekelwe, futhi udrako, isilo, nomprofethi wamanga ngeke babathinte, noma babathole. Kodwa umprofethi wamanga uyoletha isinengiso sencithakalo, futhi ukhula oluningi luyohlanjululwa ezweni, futhi ngiyofihla insali yami empini yalolo suku olukhulu, ngisho nosuku lokubuya kwami kwesibili, usuku uYahweh aluphawule emaphakadeni futhi olubekwe phakade ohlelweni lwakhe lwesikhathi sensindiso, futhi lolo suku luyasondela ngempela ngoba kulotshiwe.

Ngabona izulu livulekile, bheka, ihhashi elimhlophe; ogibele kulo ubizwa ngokuthi othembekileyo noqinisileyo, ngokulunga uyahlulela, uyalwa. Amehlo akhe ayenjengelangabi lomlilo, nasekhanda lakhe kukhona imiqhele eminingi; unegama elilotshiweyo, elingaziwa muntu ngaphandle kwakhe. Wayembethe ingubo ecwiliswe egazini; igama lakhe kuthiwa nguLizwi likaNkulunkulu. Amabutho asezulwini amlandela egibele amahhashi amhlophe, embethe ilineni elicolekileyo, elimhlophe, nelihlanzekileyo. Emlonyeni wakhe kuphuma inkemba ebukhali, ukuze ashaye ngayo izizwe; uyakuzibusa ngentonga yensimbi; unyathela isikhamo sewayini sentukuthelo nolaka lukaNkulunkulu uMninimandla onke. Engutsheni yakhe, nasethangeni lakhe unegama elilotshiweyo elithi, INKOSI YAMAKHOSI, NENKOSI YAMAKHOSI.

Kuyokwenzeka-ke ukuba ngize ngithathe ifa lami ngamandla ezandleni zabaphangi nabahlali abangenamvume, abanesikhathi sabo esesiphelile phakade, ngoba umbuso kaSathane ngeke uphinde uvuke ngendlela efanayo. Futhi ngiyohlanza ngihlanze umhlaba kukho konke ukungalungi, futhi uSathane uyoboshwa iminyaka eyinkulungwane ekuhlushweni nasek wesabeni okukhulu ifa lakhe laphakade elisondelayo kuleso sibabule esivuthayo esibi esivutha amalangabi ashisayo esijeziso.

Ngisho nalawo malangabi adalelwe ukuvuza zonke izenzo ezimbi abake bazenza kusukela ngesikhathi bedalwa bakhetha ukungalungi. Futhi zonke izono zabawileyo ziyobuyiselwa ngokugcwele komunye nomunye wabo, ngisho nazo zonke izono abazenza ngenzondo enkulu kuYahweh uqobo nasedalweni lwakhe abalonakalisile. Kanjalo-ke njengoba beboshiwe baphonswa bephila emalangabini ashisayo nahlanzekile kakhulu esijeziso lapho imililo iyovutha ishisa kakhulu ngombala oluhlaza okwesibhakabhaka onombala omhlophe qwa wokuhlanzeka. Kulawo malangabi esijeziso bayovuzwa ngokugcwele ngazo zonke izenzo zabo ezingelusizo nezinengekayo eziyonikezwa ngokugcwele ezifubeni zabo izikhathi eziningi kuze kube phakade.

Futhi bayovuzwa ngokugcwele ekwahluleleni okuphelele nokuphakade kukaYahweh okungalibali.

Futhi kanjalo ukubusa kwami kweminyaka eyinkulungwane kuzofika phezu komhlaba ngokuphelele ekupheleni kosizi lukaJakobe, futhi kuyoba ukubusa kokulunga. Phakathi nalesi sikhathi bonke abalungileyo bazovuswa, futhi yilowo nalowo uzovuswa ngesikhathi sakhe nangezinhlelo zokuvuna kwabo. Abathembekileyo kuKristu Jesu abaphilayo nalabo abalele kuKristu bayohlwithwa bayiswe emazulwini ngokuphazima kweso, futhi bayoklonyeliswa kakhulu futhi abanye bayolahlekelwa, kodwa bonke bayokwenziwa baphile ngokugcwele naphakade.

Khona-ke ekupheleni kosizi lukaJakobe kuyokwenzeka ukuvuka kwabalungileyo lapho ngiyovusa khona bonke abangcwele bosizi abafela igama lami futhi ngiyovusela ekuphileni okuphakade bonke abafa ngokholo ngaphansi kwezivumelwano eziningi zikaYahweh, ngisho nabo bonke abaphefumula okokugcina emhlabeni kusukela ku-Adamu kuze kube sekuzalweni kweBandla lami ngosuku lwePentekoste.

Nokho, kuyokwenzeka kanjalo zithandwa zami, ngakho-ke qondani lokho engisazokufeza: Ngiyokhipha abantwana bakwa-Israyeli phakathi kwabezizwe, lapho beye khona, ngibaqoqe nxazonke, ngibangenise ezweni labo. Yizwa, yizwa o Israyeli, yizwa izwi likaYahweh, uNkulunkulu Omkhulu noPhakade Wokulunga oBaba benu ababemazi futhi abamala lapho belahla indodana yami ethandekayo eyathunyelwa insindiso yabo ngisho nensindiso ka-Adamu wonke, indodana yami yokulahleka, abande njengesihlabathi solwandle. Yizwa izwi lami futhi uqonde ukuthi MINA nginguye ongunaphakade futhi ongaguquki, futhi MINA nginguye okuzo zonke izithembiso zami zihlale zinguYebo no-Amen ngoba akekho omkhulu kunoNkulunkulu wakho waphakade wokulunga okumele ubhekane naye.

Ngakho-ke qonda, ukuthi nakuba oBaba benu bamala uMesiya wabo weqiniso engamthumela kubo lapho iJudiya libuswa nguPontiyu Pilatu bekhetha ukuthi abakhululele uBaraba. Nokho, ngisanibiza ukuba namukele indodana yami ethandekayo ngoba ngiyithumela kini futhi ukuze nithole insindiso yenu ephelele futhi manje yizikhathi zokuhlupheka kukaJakobe okuyokhipha nakanjani uJakobe ekukhululweni eSinagogeni likaSathane okwahlulelwa kwalo akulibali.

Futhi qonda ukuthi ngiyayibona yonke inhluku eyenziwa yilolu khula olubi, futhi bayokhokha, futhi ngiyobuyisela ngokuphelele konke abakuthathile nakho konke abasazokuthatha kubantwana bami baphakade kaningi. Nokho nginike inkululeko yokuzikhethela ukuze bonke bakhethe, futhi ngokwenqaba kwenu uYeshua HaMashiach, ngisho uJesu Kristu, nizithathele onkulunkulu bamanga futhi abaningi benu bazihlanganise neSinagoge likaSathane. Ngakho-ke, ngiza kini ngalesi sikhathi futhi nginibiza ngala maZwi ngoba ngathatha oBaba benu njengesitshalo esithambile ngabondla ngomfundisi wami owayehlose ukubaletha kuKristu, ngisho uMthetho kaMose.

Futhi ngenze izivumelwano eziningi nabantu bami abakhethiweyo; izivumelwano abahluleka ukuzigcina ngenxa yesono nenkohlakalo esegazini labo. Nokho, ngithembekile emazwini ami futhi MINA nginguye othumela la mazwi kini, ngisho nangemisebenzi yesithunywa sami esinamandla engisidalile nganika iNdodana yami, uJesu Kristu ukuba isimvuse ngenxa yezinjongo zami zaphakade. Futhi sinjalo isimo somhlaba ukuthi abaningi bakhethe inkohlakalo nokuyekelela nezidalwa eziwileyo, isono eninecala laso nina O'Israyeli engimngcwelisile ngamikhethela ukuba isizwe esikhethekile kimi uma nje nigobela uYeshua HaMashiach ngoba ngeke ngiphinde ngithume omunye uMesiya.

Futhi MINA nginguye obonayo, aqophe, futhi akhumbule zonke izenzo ezenziwa yilabo engibadalile. Ikakhulukazi bonke labo abangihlubukele nezindlela zami; engiyobaphindisela ngokugcwele ngezindlela zabo ezimbi ngayo yonke imicabango emibi, nangawo wonke amacebo amabi ezinhliziyo zabo ezinengekayo, nangaso sonke isenzo esibi nesinengekayo kungakhathaliseki ukuthi sincane kangakanani ngoba ukulunga kwami nezahlulelo zami kuphelele ekufezekeni nasekusetshenzisweni kwazo. Ngoba labo abakhetha ukuphila ngokungalungi ngenxa yokukhetha kwabo bayodla ifa lokubhujiswa okuphakade, kanti bonke labo abakhetha ukuphila ngokulunga bayophila enjabulweni yaphakade, ekuphumuleni, nasekuthuleni futhi bayokhula njengamathole esibayeni.

Ngakho-ke, ngiyakuyala Israyeli, engikuthanda ngothando lwaphakade, ukuba usuke olwembeni lwesicabucabu futhi ngiyokukhululela ngokugcwele ekuphileni okuphakade nasenjabulweni. Suka kwiTalmud yakho ehlambalazayo ngoba mina, Yahweh, uNkulunkulu Omkhulu Waphakade, angiyithumelanga leyo ncwadi ngokuphefumulelwa noma ngesambulo futhi angigunyazanga nelilodwa igama kuleyo ncwadi, futhi iyisinengiso esidalelwe ukudukisa abantu bami abathandekayo, ngisho esadalwa yizidalwa eziwileyo ezinengekayo.

Futhi ngalokho ngiza kini ngamazwi afanayo engawanika uMose koBaba benu lapho ngibakhulula eGibhithe ngibayisa ezweni lesithembiso elantshontshwa ngumKhanani omubi. Futhi kanjalo uMose wamemeza koBaba benu: Ngibiza izulu nomhlaba kube ngofakazi namuhla ngani, ukuthi ngibeke phambi kwenu ukuphila nokufa, isibusiso nesiqalekiso. Ngakho-ke, khethani ukuphila, ukuze niphile nina nenzalo yenu, ngokuthanda iNkosi uNkulunkulu wenu, ngokulalela izwi layo, nangokunamathela kuyo: ngoba iyikho ukuphila kwenu, nobude bezinsuku zenu, ukuze nihlale ezweni iNkosi eyafungela okhokho benu, u-Abrahama, u-Isaka, noJakobe, ukubanika lona.

Futhi, ngenza uMose amemezele koBaba benu amaqiniso aphakade alandelayo awanikezwa yimi mayelana noBaba benu, isimemezelo engisithumela ngesithunywa sami futhi ukuze kulalelwe u-Israyeli, futhi la maZwi athunyelwe yimi uNkulunkulu waphakade ka-Israyeli okungekho omunye. Futhi uMose wamemezela ukuthi ngiyobavusela uMprofethi phakathi kwabafowabo onjengawe, futhi ngiyobeka amazwi ami emlonyeni wakhe, futhi uyokhuluma kubo konke engiyomyala ngakho. Futhi noma ubani ongawalaleli amazwi ami, ayowakhuluma egameni lami, ngiyokubiza kuye.
