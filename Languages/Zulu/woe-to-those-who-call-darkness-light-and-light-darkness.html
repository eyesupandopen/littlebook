Maye Kulabo Abathi Ubumnyama Bungukukhanya Nokukhanya Kungubumnyama!

Zwanini amazwi eNkosi yenu nomsindisi wenu, Bandla lami, yebo, Makoti wami, ngokuba yimina onika abahlakaniphileyo ukuhlakanipha, ukuze abahlakaniphileyo phakathi kwenu bande ngokuhlakanipha. Futhi ngingumkhandi ovuthela imililo yokuhlanjululwa, ngakho yizwani amazwi ami nonke, niguqe ngedolo esimemweni sami sokuphila okuphakade, nifune ukuhlakanipha, ngokuba ukuhlakanipha kuyinto eyinhloko, ngakho-ke zuza ukuhlakanipha. Ngakho-ke yizani kimi ngenhliziyo evulekileyo nezingalo ezivulekileyo, niguqe ngedolo, niqhubeke nokungenza mina, uJesu waseNazaretha, iNkosi yenu yaphakade, ngokuba ngisabiza bonke abayizindlalifa zokuphila okuphakade, nakuba abanye benezindlebe ezilukhuni.

Kodwa nikulahle ukuhlakanipha kwaleli zwe okuyize, ngokuba ukuhlakanipha kwezwe kuvela ezikhulwini zezwe ezizoboshwa maduze onke iminyaka eyinkulungwane kwalasha zisebuhlungwini nasesijezisweni ngenxa yezindlela zazo. Ngokuba nasezinketangweni ezisindayo abazoboshwa ngazo maduze, bayoliqonda ikusasa labo, futhi bayothuthumela baqhaqhazele iminyaka eyinkulungwane njengoba isiphetho sabo esimisiweyo kulelo chibi elivutha isibabule esibolileyo sigingqika futhi sivutha emalangabini ashisa kakhulu aluhlaza okwesibhakabhaka, ngokuba uYahweh ulungisile kanjalo, futhi ifa labo libalindele.

Ngokuba sinjalo isifiso senhliziyo yabo ngabantwana bakaNkulunkulu ophilayo, kanjalo uYahweh uyobuyisela isifiso senhliziyo yabo ezifubeni zabo, futhi bayosha kuze kube phakade naphakade. Ngokukhethekileyo, abawiweyo bayokwamukela okwezindlela zabo, ngokuba bangoyise besono, beseqo, bobubi, nokungalungi, nezenzo zabo ezinengekayo ziyobuyiselwa kubo phakade. Futhi bayokwazi bakhokhele ngokugcwele sonke isono abake basenza kaninginingi, isijeziso sabo siyoba kaningi, ngokuba kutholakele kanjalo ezinhliziyweni zabo nezenzo zabo ziyenyanyeka kuze kube phakade. Ngokuba zinjalo izindlela zikaYahweh, ngendlela umuntu ahlulela ngayo omunye, naye uyokwahlulelwa kanjalo, futhi sinjalo isiphetho nesijeziso sabo bonke abakhetha ukubhujiswa kwabo kunokuphila okuphakade kimi, uJesu waseNazaretha, ngokuba ngiyiWundlu likaNkulunkulu.

Ngokuba noma ngubani oyisiwula ngokwanele ukuba ahlubuke emthethweni wendalo, yebo, emithethweni yemvelo kaYahweh owadala ukuphila njengendwangu exhumene, uyokuhlanganyela isijeziso sabo. Ukuhlubuka emithethweni yokuphila kungukuletha ukufa nokubhujiswa kwabanye, nobubi obukhulu nomonakalo omkhulu kwenziwa yibo bonke abahlubuka ngaleyo ndlela nabalandela izinkanuko zabo kube yingozi kwabanye. Ukujabulela ukuhlubuka kwalowo owileyo kungukuhlanganyela ezindleleni zabo, futhi bonke abenza kanjalo bayohlanganyela ifa labo emalangabini aphakade, akukho okunye abangakukhetha.

Ngokuba kusukela endulo nasekuqaleni isintu sanikwa inkululeko yokuzikhethela, futhi u-Adamu wahendwa ngu-Eva owathatha enyangeni eyamkhohlisa ngobuqili bayo. Kanjalo u-Adamu no-Eva bakhetha ukufa njengemiphumela yesono, ngokuba abantu badalwa njengezingelosi, ngenhloso yokuba baqhubeke behlanzekile belungile, nokufa, okubhubhisa konke, kwakungeke kubabambe.

Kanjalo ulwazi lokuhle nokubi selukhohlakele bonke abake badla kusukela lapho. Futhi impi yenzalo isibhodlile yavutha kusukela kuGenesise nenzalo ethenjisiweyo yowesifazane, ekubhujisweni okukhulu, futhi kule mpi akukho okusindile emiphumeleni yayo. Kuze kube namuhla, yonke indalo iyabubula ikhala kabuhlungu ngisho namanje, ngokuba nendalo uqobo lwayo iyakukhululwa ekugqilazweni kokubola ingene enkululekweni ekhazimulayo yabantwana bakaNkulunkulu, futhi ukukhululwa akuphuzi! Kodwa qondani, bantabami, ukuthi kuze kube namuhla impi yenzalo inqotshwe ngokugcwele kimi, uKristu Jesu, ngokuba nizelwe kabusha ngenzalo engenakonakala, futhi ewundlwini likaNkulunkulu kwenziwa inhlawulo yezono zesintu kanye kuze kube phakade. Nangamandla angenakulinganiswa kaYahweh, ukufa kwanqotshwa ingunaphakade ekuvukeni kwami kwabafileyo, ngokuba akunakwenzeka ukuba ukufa kungibambe, ngokuba anginaso isono, futhi inkokhelo yesono ingukufa.

Nokho, ngakhokha inani elingenakucatshangwa ngenkululeko yenu, futhi abantwana bakaNkulunkulu ophilayo noma nini bazozithola bemi egumbini lesihlalo sobukhosi sikaNkulunkulu bembethe ukuphila okuphakade. Kunjalo futhi kuyoba njalo, ukuthi noma ubani obiza igama leNkosi uyosindiswa kuze kube phakade, uma ehlala ethembekile ekubizeni igama lami kuze kube sekupheleni. Ngokuba abawiweyo bayizitha ezinqotshiwe, futhi manje kufika izikhathi zokubhujiswa okuphelele kombuso wabo nesijeziso esiphelele njengoba ulaka lweWundlu nolaka lukaNkulunkulu luthululwa ezinsukwini ezizayo. Ngokuba usuku lweNkosi selufikile, ngakho lalelani izwi lami ngaphambi kokuba umnyango woMkhumbi uvalwe.

Yizwani amazwi ami niqonde inkulumo yami. Ngokuba ngikhuluma ngezinto eziqhubekayo njengamanje, ngisho nenkohliso enkulu engakhohlisa nabakhethiweyo uma bekhetha ukuvala izindlebe zabo ezwini lami. Manje sengizokhuluma ngezingulube zasendle ezifuna ukubonakala sengathi azikho ezibayeni ezivalelwe kuzo ngumvimbeli wami. Yebo, udwendwe nezinqubo zezingulube eziqhubekisela phambili ukubhujiswa usuku nosuku, ziletha umhlaba eduze kwempi ephelele, nenhlekelele ephelele, ngokuba kunikiwe kuzo ukufeza lokho, futhi uNkulunkulu ukufakile ezinhliziyweni zazo ukufeza intando yakhe, ngokuba nezitha zikaYahweh ziyalilalela izwi lakhe.

Izingulube ziyazigingqa ekungcoleni kwazo, zifuna odakeni, futhi azaneli neze, kodwa zizodla noma yini ezingayithola njengoba zifuna ngezingwanga zazo emhlabathini, ngokuba zingezomhlaba, futhi aziyiboni enye into ngaphandle kwesifiso sezisu zazo nezinkanuko zikayise ziyakuzenza. Ngokuba banjalo abaholi bamanje bezwe, futhi bakhala kabuhlungu njengoba beshaya izisulu zabo. Ngokuba akukho okunganelisa ngokugcwele lezi zingulube, futhi zinoshevu ngokuphelele ngaphakathi ngenxa yokudla kwazo, ngokuba zonke zingcolile futhi zingukhula lwangempela, futhi injalo imvelo yazo.

Lezi zilwane ezimbi ziyimithi eyonakele engenazithelo, efile kabili, esiphulwe ngezimpande. Azisoze zaba nokuphila okuphakade, futhi okwazo yizindlela zokufa nokubhujiswa okuphakade, ngisho nokubhujiswa okuqhubekayo efeni lawoyise kulelo chibi elikhulu lomlilo oluhlaza okwesibhakabhaka. Ziyizilwane ezihlaselayo, futhi ziyazithandisa ekuqaleni zifuna indawo yokukhosela njengoba zikhala zizenza izisulu, zize zisole abanye ngenzondo eziyenzayo ezizweni. Zikhala kabuhlungu njengoba zishaya labo ezifuna ukubabhubhisa ukuze zibonakale zilungile ezindleleni zazo ezimbi.

Ngokuba lezi zilwane eziyiziwula azinakuhawukelwa, ngokuba ezazo yizindlela zokubhujiswa, futhi lezi ziyokwenza izinkanuko zikaYise udeveli, ngokuba izinkanuko zakhe ziyintokozo yazo, futhi ziyaneliswa kanjalo. Maye kulabo abagubha ububi, nababiza ukukhanya ngokuthi ubumnyama, nobumnyama ngokuthi ukukhanya. Ngokuba ezabo yizindlela zokubhujiswa, futhi emililweni yaphakade abasoze baphunyuka. Qondani, bantabami, ukuthi noma nini manje izingulube zizokhululwa ezibayeni ezivalelwe kuzo ngumvimbeli. Ngokuba lapho umvimbeli esusiwe, lezi zingulube ezibonakala zifuyekile zingenangozi, ezenza sengathi zingabakhethiweyo bakaNkulunkulu, ziyophenduka izilwane zasendle ngokushesha nje lapho zikhululwa ekubanjweni okungenakunqamulwa komvimbeli, ngokuba injalo imvelo yazo yangempela.

Zizohlasela amazwe amaningi zidle konke ezikutholayo. Ziyozondla ngokufa nokubhujiswa kwabanye, ngokuba injalo imvelo yazo. Zizulazulile zisuka endaweni ngendawo, futhi zinemithetho yazo nezinhlaka zomphakathi, ngokuba noYahweh unika izinga lokuhlakanipha nokuhleleka ngisho nasezingulubeni, ngokuba izindlela zikaYahweh zilungile futhi ziqotho. Lezi zingulube azinako ukuzisola, azinayo imizwa, futhi azinankinga yokudla abafileyo ukuze zanelise izifiso zazo, futhi ziyodla nabaphilayo uma kufanele izinhloso zazo. Bhekani, ihora selifikile lapho izingulube zizobe zingavinjelwe, futhi lapho kunjalo, ziyolidla izwe, ziyodla okunonileyo kwezwe, ziyosusa ukuthula ngokuphelele emhlabeni, zidle futhi zigingqike ekuchithekeni kwegazi lempi ephelele. Ngoba nabaculi benu ababi bayahlabelela ngezindlela zazo, futhi izindlela zazo aziyona imfihlo.

Lezi zingulube zasendle zenzelwe ukubhujiswa nokudla ukungcola kwezwe ukuze zihlanze umhlaba ngenjongo kaYahweh, bese ziyoboshwa ziphonswe emililweni yaphakade naphakade. Ngokuba izono zazo zinkulu, futhi izono zazo ziningi. Kodwa qondani ukuthi lezi zingulube bezigcinwe esibayeni sazo izikhathi ezimisiwe, futhi eziningi zenza sengathi zimiswe njengezitsha ezingcwele zombuso wazo okhulayo. Manje yisikhathi sokukhululwa kwazo, futhi manje zizobonisa imvelo yazo yangempela ebeziyifihle ezingutsheni ezibonakala zingcwele, futhi ikhiyubhu elimnyama likaSaturn lisemaphakathi nenkanyezi yazo nangaphezu kwamehlo. Zifuna ukuzihlukanisa ngokuba zizikhethele ukuba ngezaNkulunkulu. Kodwa akekho umuntu oza kuYahweh ngaphandle kwami, uJesu waseNazaretha, owalahlwa oYise, futhi labo abangenze iNkosi bangabaka-Israyeli kaNkulunkulu weqiniso, futhi bayodalwa ngokuphelele kimi njengomuntu omusha oyedwa, noma umJuda noma owezizwe, noma isigqila noma okhululekileyo. Lolu khula lwama-Edomi luyodalulwa ngokugcwele njengabakhohlisi abayibo, futhi bayosuswa ngokuphelele ezweni nasemhlabeni wonke ngokwahlulela okukhulu nesijeziso esikhulu.

Kodwa ngaphambi kokuba basuswe, bayovunyelwa ukukhanuka kwabo igazi njengoba imvelo yabo yangempela idalulwa ukuze wonke umuntu ayibone, ngokuba okwamanje bazifihla ezintweni ezibonakala zingokwenkolo. Zidla udoti, zidle abafileyo, zihlasela zidle abaphilayo ngoba ukukhanuka kwazo igazi kukhulu futhi akunakwaneliswa! Futhi ngaso sonke isikhathi zenza sengathi zingcwele, zenza sengathi ngezezwe futhi ziyizindlalifa ezifanele, zenza sengathi azibona abahlaseli ngoba indawo yazo yemvelo yihlane hhayi izwe lesithembiso. Futhi injalo imvelo yengulube, ngoba ngokushesha nje lapho ibuyiselwa endle, imvelo yayo yangempela yasendle idalulwa ngokugcwele. Nokho, zenza sengathi ziphila impilo yalabo ezifuna ukubanqoba, futhi ziyosishulwa ngokuphelele ezweni, futhi azinampande nagatsha, ngokuba uYahweh uthembise kanjalo. Ngakho-ke qondani ukuthi lezi zingulube zibonakala zingabaholi abanomusa bezwe lesithembiso, kanti empeleni ziyizilwane ezihahayo ezichuma ekubulaleni uhlanga futhi zizofuna ukubhubhisa noma yini yami, uKristu Jesu, futhi zizokhuluma ngisho nalabo bezwe abalandela imiyalo yazo yobuTalmud ebabhubhisayo, kodwa insali yezwe iyokuza kimi, uYeshua HaMashiach, ngokuba ngisazomsindisa uJakobe!

Maye kulabo abathi ubumnyama bungukukhanya nokukhanya kungubumnyama! Ngisho nalabo abahlasela uYahweh, noYeshua HaMashiach ngokuhlasela abantwana bakaNkulunkulu Ophilayo. Ngokuba lokho abakwenzile koyedwa omncane kubafowethu, bakwenzile kimi, futhi impindiselo ngeyami, isho iNkosi yaphakade, futhi ngiyakuphindisela. Ngakho-ke vulani amehlo enu, bantabami, futhi nibalahle labo abaphakamisa inkanyezi kaRemfan esibhengezweni sabo! Ngoba ukuhlanganyela nabo kungukuhlala etabernakele likaMoloki nokukhothamela uBaphomet wabo! Anginitshelanga yini ukuba nibale inani lesilo, futhi lelo nani liboniswa ngokuziqhenya kulowo mfanekiso abazenzele wona ukuze bakhonze unkulunkulu wabo wenkanyezi, ngokuba phakathi nenkanyezi yabo kunekhiyubhu labo! Qondani lo mzekeliso, ngokuba amaphuzu ayisithupha aholela konxantathu abayisithupha abenza unxantandathu, futhi ngaphakathi kungenela ikhiyubhu labo, ngakho balani inani lesilo niyeke ukukholwa ngamanga ama-Edomi ngoba bakhonza abawiweyo futhi unkulunkulu wabo ungudrako.

Futhi kusukela ezinsukwini zikaNowa, ngisho nangemva kukazamcolo, izingxenye eziyisishiyagalolunye kweziyishumi zemimoya engenamzimba yamaNefili ibivinjelwe kwalasha, ngokuba uNowa wathandazela abazukulu bakhe ababehlaselwa yiyo futhi behlulwa yiyo. Kanjalo uYahweh wasihlonipha isicelo sikaNowa, wabopha ababi kakhulu nabanyanyekayo, futhi izingxenye eziyisishiyagalolunye enanini labo zigcinwe kwalasha kusukela lapho. Ngizweni kahle, Bantabami, futhi niliqonde izwi leNkosi yenu. Ekuqoqweni kwabathembekile eBandleni lami, ngisho noMakoti wami, kimi emafwini, umvimbeli uyosuswa, futhi kwalasha sekuzovulwa ngokuphelele.

Ububi ngeke busavinjelwa, ngokuba uYahweh uvimbele ngokwengeziwe ukugcwala kombuso kaSathane ukuze labo abayizindlalifa zensindiso yaphakade basindiswe, ngisho nalabo abangenze iNkosi yabo ngezikhathi zami zomusa nesihe. Njengoba isintu manje sesingalile, ububi ngeke busavinjelwa esintwini. Qondani ukuthi yonke, ngisho nesixuku esikhulu sale mimoya, yonke iyokhululwa, ngisho nenani elikhulu kakhulu elizogcwalisa umhlaba ngobubi obungavinjelwe obungakhulumeki. Ngokuba u-Enoke umbhali wabhala ngalokhu: Futhi imimoya yeziqhwaga iyahlukumeza, icindezele, ibhubhise, ihlasele, ilwe, isebenze ukubhujiswa emhlabeni, ibangele izinkinga: ayidli ukudla, kodwa nokho iyalamba yome, ibangele amacala. Futhi le mimoya iyovukela abantwana babantu nabesifazane, ngoba iphume kubo.

Anifundanga yini ezwini lami elingcwele lapho ngahamba khona ezweni lamaGadarene? Ngokuba ngakhipha imimoya kwababili ababenayo, futhi ngavumela le mimoya emibi ukuba ingene emhlanjini wezingulube owawuseduze. Qondani ukuthi ukugcwala kombuso kaDrako sekuzokhululwa isikhashana, ukuze kuqhutshwe labo abamiselwe ukuphila okuphakade ezingalweni zami ezisindisayo! Ngokuba le mimoya eboshiwe iyokhululwa ukuze ihlanganyele nombuso kaSathane ngokugcwele ebusweni bomhlaba. Futhi iyongena ezingulubeni futhi. Ngokuba zilwa nabantu futhi zingabahlula abantu ngoba izinkanuko zazo azisoze zaneliswa, futhi yile mimoya enika amandla izingulube futhi esebenza ngokubambisana nezingulube, futhi lezi zingulube zasendle ziyizithuthi zazo.

Akubona bodwa kuphela kodwa bonke abathatha uphawu lwabo lwesilo nabo bayoba izithuthi ezizimisele zaleyo mimoya eyolwa nesintu futhi. Ngokuba bayophinde badle umuntu wemvelo kulezi zikhathi zokugcina, ngokuba kumiswe ukuba kwenzeke kanjalo kulezi zikhathi zokuphela. Futhi ukuphela sekuqalile futhi kuza ngokugcwele maduze kakhulu njengoba ukuhlupheka kukaJakobe kwephula izwe. Imihelo isiseduze kakhulu manje ayinqamuki, futhi uYahweh wenze izibonakaliso eziningi emhlabeni, nezibonakaliso eziningi nezimangaliso emkhathini nasemazulwini nasemhlabeni, kodwa abaningi basazithethelela lezi zixwayiso ngokucabanga okungenakholo.

Bhekani! Esikhundleni sokuza ekuphendukeni, izixuku zize zijatshuliswe yizindimbane zezinambuzane ezithathe abaningi nasemazweni amaningi emhlabeni jikelele. Zihleka zihleka izixwayiso ezinikezwe nguYahweh emhlabeni, ngokuba uNkulunkulu waphakade uyosukuma endaweni yakhe yokuhlala, azibonakalise, futhi abawiweyo bayathuthumela ngoba izikhathi zabo zifikile, futhi babanjwe ukwesaba okuphakade. Ngokuba nina eniwileyo, benicabanga ukuthi ningamthatha uNkulunkulu waphakade ngobuqili, kanti into enjalo ayinakwenzeka. Ngokuba ngasiphi isikhathi izitsha zokudelelwa zasukuma zalwa futhi zathatha umbumbi ngobuqili? Kodwa kunjalo ukuhlanya nokuzidla kwabawiweyo, futhi ngeke basibalekele isithando sabo somlilo esivutha njalo.

Ngakho-ke ziqondeni izikhathi eniphila kuzo, nihlale kimi, Bandla lami, ngisho noMakoti wami! Ngokuba ukukhululwa kwenu okuphelele nefa lenu eligcwele akuphuzi, futhi anisoze nashiywa phakathi kwezingulube ngoba nikholwe egameni lami kulezi zinsuku zomusa nesihe, kanjalo umusa nesihe saphakade niyoba nakho phakade. Nabafowenu bayohlanganyela nani kuphakade nasembusweni wami ozayo wokulunga, ngisho nabo bonke abangakulungele ukubonakala kwami.

Ngokuba ngingumcwebisi ovuthela imililo yokuhlanjululwa ukuze ngihlanze amagugu kaYahweh, ngisuse ukungcola, futhi yimina ohlezi njengomkhandi ephethe isivuthelo esandleni, evuthela amalahle, futhi ogoqa akhande izinsimbi eziyigugu zikaYahweh ukuze akhiqize amandla angunaphakade nangenakuphela. Isixuku esikhulu, ngisho nesixuku sezixuku, siyokuza kimi ngokuhlupheka kukaJakobe, futhi siyovuna umvuzo omkhulu, futhi bayizindlalifa zensindiso yaphakade, futhi bayongena enjabulweni yaphakade, futhi u-Israyeli uyosindiswa! Ngakho-ke ningesabi, bantabami, futhi ningakhathazeki ngabathandekayo benu, nibe nokholo kimi, uKristu Jesu, ngokuba ngiyakwazi ukusindisa ngokuphelele bonke ababiza igama lami, futhi akekho noyedwa omiselwe ukuphila okuphakade ongahlwithwa esandleni sami, futhi ngiyosindisa abaningi emililweni yaphakade, ngibakhiphe ngisho nasemalangabini.

Mina, uYeshua HaMashiach, ngithumele lawa mazwi ngenceku yami, futhi ngenceku yami lawa mazwi athunyelwe!

Yimina onika abahlakaniphileyo ukuhlakanipha, ukuze umuntu ohlakaniphileyo ande ngokuhlakanipha.

NgiyiWundlu likaNkulunkulu elizayo njengeNgonyama yesizwe sakwaJuda.

Anginaso isono, kodwa ngakhokha inani elingenakucatshangwa ngenkululeko yenu.

Yimina ohlezi njengomkhandi ogoqa akhande izinsimbi eziyigugu zamagugu kaYahweh.

Ngiyakwazi ukusindisa ngokuphelele bonke ababiza igama lami, futhi akekho noyedwa omiselwe ukuphila okuphakade ongahlwithwa esandleni sami.

Qaphelani ukubonakala kwami, ngokuba izikhathi sezifikile. Isikhathi esisele sinikelwe kuphela ukufeza izinhloso zikaYahweh, bantabami, ngakho-ke ningazivumeli ukuba niduduzelwe ukuba nilale futhi, nize ningawanaki amazwi ami okuhlakanipha nezixwayiso eziningi.

Umuntu ohlakaniphileyo uyasibona isifo azicashe, kepha iziwula ziyaqhubeka, zilimale. Anginibizelanga yini ekuhlakanipheni okuhlala njalo kukaYahweh?

Futhi yimina ozonicashisa lapho beningeke nizicashe khona, ngokuba ngizonifihla egumbini lesihlalo sobukhosi sikaYahweh, ngisho nasezulwini lamazulu, lapho niyongena khona enjabulweni yeNkosi yenu, futhi amahholo edili asemazulwini ayonihalalisela nonke, ngokuba ngokushesha kunokuba nicabanga, niyobona ngamehlo enu ubukhazikhazi obukhulu, baphakade obunilindele efeni lenu. Qhubekani nihlezi othandweni lwami, ngokuba nithandwa kakhulu nguBaba, futhi niyinhlamvu yeso lami, engingenawo amahloni okuthi ningabafowethu.

Mina, uYeshua HaMashiach, ngikhulumile ngathumela lawa mazwi, kanjalo alotshiwe, futhi iVangeli Laphakade lambuliwe ngesikhathi salo.
