Kungani Niphelelwa Ukubekezela?

Wozani, nithobise izinhliziyo nezindlebe zenu nizwe amaZwi eNkosi yenu, Bandla lami ngisho noMlobokazi wami. Kungani nisuswa ngokusheshe kangaka ethembeni lenu ekubonakaleni kwami, sengathi angizi? Sengathi izimemezelo zikaYahweh zingaguqulwa? UNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade, uYahweh, usezikhohliwe yini izithembiso zakhe? Ngikhohliwe yini amazwi nezithembiso zami? Ngiqinisile, ngiqinisile, ngithi cha! Futhi bonke abathi uYahweh, uNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade, kanye nami, uYeshua HaMashiach, iNkosi enkulu yamaKhosi noMbusi wabaBusi, wenza ngobuwula, kufanele baphenduke ngokushesha. Celani intethelelo bantwana bami niqhubeke nokuzilungiselela ngoba ngizovela ngokungazelelwe, futhi niyokhangwa ukuthi isigameko esikhulu esilandelayo ohlelweni lukaYahweh lokuhlenga isintu senzeka ngokushesha okungakanani, ngoba siyokwenzeka ngokuzumayo nangokungalindelekile ngoba kumemezeleke kanjalo. Ngoba kuyokwenzeka ukuthi ngivuse kuqala abafileyo bekuKristu, nina eniphilayo nenisalayo niyothathwa niye kungihlangabeza emafwini, futhi kuyokwenzeka ngokuphazima kweso.

Kungani niphelelwa ukubekezela? Kungani abanye benu benza sengathi izinto sezibuyele endleleni ezaziyiyo? Ngokusobala akukho ukubuyela ezindleleni zakudala, ngoba umhlaba okhona manje uyohlangabezana nenhlekelele enkulu futhi uyoguqulwa kuze kube phakade, futhi uyoguquka nangokwengeziwe ngesikhathi sokuhlupheka kukaJakobe futhi umhlaba uyoba njengasezinsukwini zikaNowa. Lapho umthibazi esusiwe, akukho lutho oluyovimba amabutho amabi, ngisho namabutho abawileyo ayohlasela konke okungokwami, ngoba uYahweh unike udrako amandla okulwa nabangcwele nokubanqoba. Abaningi bayokuza kimi ngokubhujiswa kwenyama yabo, yona leyo nyama abayithandayo nesabophele. Ngoba bonke abakhalaza egameni leNkosi bayosindiswa, futhi inceku ayinkulu kunenkosi yayo.

Nakuba nginikeze ithuba lokubalekela ukubhujiswa okuzayo kwabathembekile, abaningi abakalungi kodwa bayosindiswa futhi basebenze inhloso enkulu eMbusweni wami ozayo, ngoba ngiyobusa esihlalweni sobukhosi sikaDavide futhi ngihlanganyela esihlalweni sobukhosi sikaBaba, nani niyohlanganyela esihlalweni sami sobukhosi nina bathembekile bami. Kulabo abangalungile bayohlupheka kakhulu ngenxa yegama lami, futhi bayohlupheka njengoba mina, uJesu waseNazaretha, ngahlupheka ukuze ngitholele isintu insindiso futhi ngokubethelwa kwami ngishiye isibonelo sokulalela kuze kube sekufeni ukuze bonke balandele. Futhi ngosizi olukhulu izixuku eziningi ziyosindiswa zithole umvuzo omkhulu kimi Kristu Jesu, zikhonze noYahweh ethempelini lakhe. Umvuzo omkhulu ulindele bonke abakhuleka egameni lami, nalabo abakhuleka egameni lami kuze kube sekupheleni bayosindiswa, ngisho nalabo abangakuthandi ukuphila kwabo kuze kube sekupheleni. Ngoba lo mhlaba uyabhubha futhi uzobhujiswa futhi ujeziswe ngokuphelele ngaphambi kokuba uMbuso wami wokulunga umiswe emhlabeni.

Ngakho-ke ningadangali Bantwana bami, ngoba izithembiso zami zingu-yebo no-amen futhi ngiyohlwitha umlobokazi wami ngimuse emazulwini njengoba kuthenjisiwe, futhi akekho noyedwa umuntu omiselwe insindiso yaphakade nguYahweh ngaphambi kokuba isisekelo somhlaba sibekwe, oyohlwithwa ezandleni zami zaphakade futhi insindiso yenu ivikelekile kimi, Kristu Jesu, kuphela hlalani nithembekile futhi nihlale kuKristu kuze kube sekupheleni. Lokhu bekulokhu kuyimfuneko evela kuYahweh, ukuthi umuntu atholakale Ethembekile ezindleleni zakhe. Futhi mina, uYeshua HaMashiach, ngiletha izindlela zikaYahweh futhi labo abafunda kimi bafunda izindlela zikaYahweh, ngoba ngimunye ngenhloso nobumbano noBaba. Maduze nizoba munye nathi, akubhalwanga yini ukuthi niyingxenye yoBuNkulunkulu baphakade, ngoba linjalo ifa lenu futhi unjalo umvuzo wami kumadodana kaNkulunkulu athembekile atholakale ethembekile kimi. Ngoba kimi, Kristu Jesu, kuhlala konke ukugcwala koBuNkulunkulu ngokomzimba, futhi niyohlanganyela eifeni lami ngenxa yokuthembeka kwenu, futhi niyohlanganyela esihlalweni sami sobukhosi njengamaKhosi nabaPristi futhi uMbuso wami awunasekupheleni.

Ngakho-ke qinani kimi kule mizuzwana yokugcina yesikhathi, futhi niqonde ukuthi isikhathi sesiphelile. Yebo, isikhathi sesiphelile sokuba iBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlakalo liphenduke. Izikhathi zami zomusa nesihe seziphelile, nakuba umusa nesihe kungokwami ingunaphakade. Manje kuza ukwahlulela okukhulu emhlabeni, nasezizweni ngisho nasezweni. Kodwa ukwahlulela ngeke kwehle ukuze kuqede bonke abaphilayo, kodwa kuyokwehla futhi kunqwabelane. Ukwahlulela phezu kokwahlulela, uphawu phezu kophawu, icilongo phezu kwecilongo, isitsha phezu kwesitsha, usizi phezu kosizi, nesifo phezu kwesifo. Futhi ngenxa yalokho abaningi, abaningi, abaningi abantu bayothola insindiso kimi, uJesu waseNazaretha, ngoba ngiyiWundlu lePhasika eliphelele lazo zonke izikhathi, futhi asisekho isidingo somhlatshelo. Kodwa kini, Mlobokazi wami othembekile, izikhathi zokulinda seziphelile.

Ngeke kusaba khona ukulibala, futhi ngeke kusaba khona ukulinda, futhi niyofinyelela emvuzweni wenu ogcwele naseifeni lenu kimi. Futhi nakuba abanye beyolahlekelwa, iso alikakuboni futhi indlebe ayikakuzwa, futhi akukakangeni enhliziyweni yomuntu, izinto uYahweh azilungiselele labo abamthandayo. Futhi kunjalo zithandwa zami ezithembekile, nibonise uthando lwenu ngami, uKristu Jesu, nothando lwenu ngoYahweh ngoba nilalele imiyalo yami ngokukholwa kulolu suku olubi. Yize ningangibonanga ngamehlo enu enyama, futhi ningayibonanga inkazimulo kaYahweh yaphakade ngamehlo enu enyama nihlale nithembekile kimi kuze kube sekupheleni, ngakho-ke ninomvuzo omkhulu eniwubekelwe emazulwini, ngakho ningadangali!

Qhubekani ngokubekezela, Bantwana bami, ngoba ngiyasondela. Ningabalaleli abaklolodayo abanihleka usulu ngoba ukukloloda kwabo kuzophenduka usizi olukhulu ngokushesha okukhulu, futhi abaningi abaklolodayo bayothula lapho ukuphila kwabo sekuphelile. Ngoba zinjalo izahlulelo ezizayo. Kulabo abenqabile izandla zami ezeluliwe futhi babala igazi lami lokuhlawulela njengento engcolile futhi bazibala njengabangafanele ukuphila okuphakade eMbusweni wami ozayo, bona labo bayodla ifa lobumnyama nokufa abakuthanda kakhulu nabakufisayo futhi abaningi bayowela ngokuzithandela ekufeni kwesibili.

Ngoba othandweni olukhulu lukaBaba, uYahweh uNkulunkulu waphakade ngoba akekho onjengaye, ngeke aphoqe noyedwa wezidalwa zakhe ukuba amlandele futhi amthande. Bonke banikezwe inkululeko yokuzikhethela futhi bonke bayothola umvuzo wokukhetha kwabo. Ngoba injalo imithetho kaYahweh. Labo abathanda ububi, abathanda izinjabulo zesono futhi bathanda ububi izono zabo ezibudalela abanye abahlupheka ngokungafanele ngenxa yezinkanuko zabo nezindlela zabo ezonakele bayodla ifa lezindlela zabo ezimbi ezibuyela ezifubeni zabo futhi sinjalo isijeziso sabo futhi sonke isono siyobuyiselwa ingunaphakade. Kodwa kulabo abamukela isivumelwano sokuthula sikaYahweh, mina Jesu waseNazaretha, bayodla ifa lokuphila okuphakade nokuthula nomvuzo nothando nenjabulo yaphakade ebukhoneni bami nasebukhoneni bukaYahweh uqobo, ngoba maduze indawo yokuhlala kaYahweh iyoba phakathi kwabantu ngoba kubhaliwe kanjalo. Futhi ngiyasondela ukuzonihlwitha nginiyise esigodlweni sobukhosi sikaYahweh uqobo, lapho usuvele uhleli khona, nemiqhele yenu inilindile.

Ningakholwa amanga abo bantwana bami, ngiyeza kini, hlalani niqinile omunye umzuzwana noma emibili, ngoba ukubonakala kwami sekusondele futhi kuzokwenzeka noma nini manje, futhi ngiyodlula okulindele. Zilungiseleleni futhi nizihlanze egazini lami lokuhlawulela. Qhubekani nokuphila kwenu kokuphenduka futhi niqhubeke nokubethela izindlela zenu zenyama, ngoba aziveli kimi futhi ayikho into embi eyongena eMbusweni wami futhi ayikho into embi eyoma ebukhoneni bukaYahweh. Ngingumhlatshelo wenu waphakade futhi nginenze ningenabala noma umbimbi futhi akukho sibi kwabathembekile bami ngoba igazi lami liyanihlanza njalo njengoba nivuma izono zenu ngokuphenduka, yize inyama yenu yonakele ngokuphelele.

Wozani kimi ngenhliziyo evulekile futhi ngizokukhombisa ngothando amaphutha enu ukuze nivume izono zenu kimi, futhi ngokwenza kanjalo ngizonihlanza futhi ngizoqhubeka nokunenza niphelele njengoba niyisidalwa esisha kimi, Kristu Jesu. Futhi ngiqinisile, ngiqinisile bonke abaphawulwe ngomoya wami ongcwele wesithembiso, noma umJuda noma oweZizwe, (noma isigqila noma okhululekile) bayodalwa kabusha ngokugcwele njengomuntu oyedwa omusha kimi Kristu Jesu futhi bayoma enkazimulweni yenkululeko kuze kube phakade! Ngoba ngingumoya onika ukuphila, ngoba uBaba ungenze kanjalo. Futhi ngiyilokho uMINA OMKHULU engingenze khona kuze kube phakade. Futhi manje nani niyokwenziwa nifane nami ngomzimba okhazinyulisiwe, umzimba owakhiwe njengomvuzo oyohlanganisa impumelelo yenu ekumeleni kwenu mina kulolu suku olubi. Akukho nomzamo owodwa wenu oyodlula ungavuzwanga: Ngoba uNkulunkulu akanakungalungi, ukuba akhohlwe umsebenzi wenu, nokukhandleka kothando, enikubonise eGameni lakhe, ngokuthi nikhonze abaNgcwele, futhi nisakhonza.

Ubunjalo benu beqiniso bufihliwe kimi, uMsindisi wenu noMesiya. Niyoba nami kuze kube phakade, futhi niyoba nomqondo owodwa nenhliziyo eyodwa nami Yeshua HaMashiach kanye noYahweh, ngoba u-El Shaddai Omkhulu, uNkulunkulu uSomandla uyoba yikho konke kukho konke ngoba izinhloso zakhe ziyogcwaliseka kini, nina ababiziweyo, abakhethiweyo nabathembekile bami. Ngiyeza kini, Mlobokazi wami, nilungile? Ninawo yini amafutha ezibhanini zenu? Sezigeze ngokuphelele yini egazini lami lokuhlawulela? Seniyekile yini ukugezana izinyawo?

Qaphelani! Bhekani! Lalelani! Qaphelani ukuvela kwami futhi nikhuthazane ngoba uSathane ubengafihla ukuvela kwami ukuba into enjalo ibingenakwenzeka. Bhekani kimi, umbhali nomphelelisi wokholo lwenu, ngoba ngokuphazima kweso niyoguqulwa nifaniswe nami kuze kube phakade. Lalelani amaZwi ami futhi nilungele ukuvela kwami futhi ngaphezu kwakho konke lalelani ngenxa yoThando ngoYahweh nangami ukugcina iZwi lami elingcwele ngobumsulwa benhliziyo, nazi ukuthi ukuhlengwa kwenu akusondele kodwa sekukhona.

Ngimunye ngenhloso nobumbano noBaba, futhi maduze nizoba munye nathi.
