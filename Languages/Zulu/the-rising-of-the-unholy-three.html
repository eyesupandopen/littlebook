Ukuvuka Kwabathathu Abangcwele!

Ngalokho-ke isikhathi sesiphelile futhi ukubonakala kwami kuyasondela! Manje sengizonitshela ukulandelana kwezenzakalo esezizokwenzeka maduzane, ngakho-ke lalelisisani ukuze nizwe izwi likaMesiya wenu, uYashuwa uMashiya, amazwi eNkosi yamakhosi, iNkosi yamaKhosi, uMesiya, nomphristi omkhulu kuze kube phakade ngokohlelo lukaMelkhizedeki. Mhlawumbe ukuzidla kwenu kobuzwe kuzophuphuthekisa izinhliziyo zenu kimi, O Bhabhiloni Oyimfihlakalo: ngisho nawe Melika. Ngoba nicabanga ukuthi nihlezi njengendlovukazi futhi alikho ishwa eliyonehlela, futhi nizitshela ukuthi, angisiye umfelokazi! Kodwa ukuchithwa kwenu kuyofika ngehora elilodwa futhi ngeke niphinde nivuke futhi nobuholi benu ngeke buphinde buhlupha umhlaba kaYawe, ngoba isahlulelo esiphindwe kabili simenyezelwe nguYawe, futhi lokho uYawe akumenyezelayo akekho ongakuguqula.

Ake nicabange, uma nivuma, umhlaba ongenabo ubuKristu nezizwe zobuKristu. Ngeke kusaba namasonto futhi ubuKristu buyoba bungekho emthethweni emhlabeni wonke futhi inkathi yeBandla isifinyelele esiphethweni, ngisho nenkathi yami yomusa eyamenyezelwa abaphostoli nabaphrofethi bami ngekhulu lokuqala, futhi bona batshala imbewu lapho nikwazi khona ukuthola isiphephelo kulezi zikhathi zezinkinga, ngisho iZwi lami elingcwele nezimfihlakalo zikaNkulunkulu! Kubalulekile ukuhumusha lezi zehlakalo ngolimi oluqondile olwasetshenziswa nguJohane umAmbululi, ngoba wathi: Ngase ngima phezu kwesihlabathi solwandle, ngabona isilo siphuma olwandle, sinezinhloko eziyisikhombisa nezimpondo eziyishumi, naphezu kwezimpondo zaso imiqhele eyishumi, naphezu kwezinhloko zaso amagama okuhlambalaza. Waphinda wathi: Ngase ngibona esinye isilo siphuma emhlabathini; sasinemphondo ezimbili zinjengezemvu, sakhuluma njengodrako. Waphinda wathi: udrako wasinika amandla aso, nesihlalo saso sobukhosi, negunya elikhulu. Kanjalo-ke kuyoba ukuvuka kwabathathu abangcwele, isilo sasolwandle, isilo sasemhlabathini esingumphrofethi wamanga noMashiya wamanga, nodrako obanika igunya elikhulu.

Akekho umphikukristu oyedwa omaphakathi, nakuba imimoya yomphikukristu isiphumile yaya ezweni futhi ikhohlisa abaningi, kodwa kunezilo ezimbili nodrako. Esinye sokubusa ulwandle, noma izizwe zabeZizwe, nesinye sokubusa amazwe angcwele, esivela esizweni sakwaDani futhi singumakhi wethempeli oyisilo. Isilo sasolwandle sizobusa izizwe, noma ama-goyim ngemithetho kaNowa, futhi leyo mithetho isivele isebenza, futhi yomibili imelene noKristu ngokwemvelo yayo. Umphikukristu uphikisana nami, uKristu Jesu, futhi uthatha nendawo kaKristu Jesu. Ngoba bhekani! Imfihlakalo yobubi isithuthuke kakhulu futhi uSathane ulindele ukuhamba kwenu, Bandla lami, ngisho noMlobokazi wami. Uyobusa ngenkolo ehlangene, exubile futhi uyozibiza ngoMahdi wabo. Ukuhlangana kwezinkolo kuyobahlanganisa, ngaphansi kwamanga okuthi zonke izindlela ziholela kunkulunkulu ofanayo. Ngaphandle kokuthi, mina Jesu Kristu uMesiya weqiniso ngeke ngamukelwe, futhi anginayo ingxenye kubo futhi ukubhujiswa kwabo akulibali. Ngakho ukuhlangana kwezinkolo zabo kuyoholela emphakathini wabo oyisilo lapho uphawu luyophoqelelwa ezizweni ukuze zibambe iqhaza emphakathini wabo. Ningaluthathi uphawu, ngoba noma ngubani oluthathayo uyokwabelana ngesiziba somlilo nesilo nomphrofethi wamanga. Futhi bonke abathatha uphawu bayoguqulwa ngokofuzo lwabo kuze kube phakade. Anifundanga yini? Lapho wabona khona insimbi ihlangene nodaka olubumbekayo, bayozihlanganisa nenzalo yabantu, kodwa ngeke banamathelane, njengalokhu insimbi ingahlangani nodaka. Labo abathatha uphawu bayoba yizigqila zohlelo oluvukayo futhi bayoba yizigqila zomqondo owodwa oyokwenzeka ngokuhlanganiswa kwenyama nobuchwepheshe.

Ulwandle lumele izizwe zabeZizwe. Imithetho yamanga kaNowa isivele isho ukuthi ubuKristu kuwukukhonza izithombe futhi inquma ukunqunywa kwekhanda njengesijeziso. Le mithetho isivele ikhona futhi iyosetshenziswa ngonya. Futhi labo ababiza igama leNkosi kuze kube sekupheleni bayosindiswa, ngoba kulotshiwe ukuthi: UNkulunkulu uyokwesula zonke izinyembezi emehlweni abo; ngeke kusaba khona ukufa, nosizi, nokukhala, nobuhlungu, ngokuba okokuqala kudlulile. Lowo owayehlezi esihlalweni sobukhosi wathi, Bheka, ngenza konke kube kusha. Wathi kimi, Loba, ngokuba lawa mazwi athembekile, aqinisekile. Kanjalo-ke kuyoba ikusasa lenu, nina bangcwele bami bosizi, ngoba ngisazomsindisa uJakobe nabo bonke ababiza igama lami elinamandla nganoma yiluphi ulimi ababiza ngalo.

Isilo sasemhlabathini, esingumphrofethi wamanga, sizobusa kwa-Israyeli, futhi sizobusa umhlaba wonke kusukela ezweni lesithembiso, kodwa hhayi abantu bakaNkulunkulu, hhayi u-Israyeli kaNkulunkulu ngoba nina ningu-Israyeli kaNkulunkulu, ngisho nabantwana boPhezukonke ababekwe uphawu lweNkosi uNkulunkulu oPhezukonke, futhi uphawu lukaYawe alunakwephulwa. Nesixuku esikhulu siyophuma osizini lukaJakobe futhi siyothatha izindawo zaso ezifanele phakathi kuka-Israyeli kaNkulunkulu, ngoba bobabili abangamaJuda nabeZizwe bayosindiswa futhi babe ngumuntu munye omusha ngoba ngiyobadala kabusha kimi ekuvukeni kwabalungileyo.

Bantwana bami, iSinagoge likaSathane selihlelile ukubusa kwalo futhi alinazwelo, nina niyizilwane ezinemilenze emibili kubo, ngoba balandela iTalmud yaseBhabhiloni ehlambalazayo, incwadi evela kuSathane hhayi kuYawe, ngoba u-Israyeli wamfulathela uYawe kudala lapho engamukeli mina, uYashuwa waseNazaretha, kodwa ngisazobasindisa abantu bami. Lolu wuhlobo olufanayo lokhula lapho ngatshela khona okhokho babo ngokusobala: Nina ningabakayihlo usathane, nifisa ukwenza izinkanuko zikayihlo; yena wayengumbulali kwasekuqaleni, akemanga eqinisweni, ngokuba alikho iqiniso kuye. Lapho ekhuluma amanga, ukhuluma okwakhe, ngokuba ungumqambimanga noyise wawo. Qondisisani Bantwana bami ukuthi uDani uliwayini lebhongo futhi uDani akamelwe kubantu abayizi-144,000. Lolu khula lulindele ngabomvu umakhi walo wethempeli, futhi sebevele bemazi njengenkosi yabo. Isilo sasemhlabathini yisona esiletha uphawu lwesilo. Ngisho nomkhankaso wembewu yempi, osuvivinyiwe wapheleliswa ukuze usatshalaliswe futhi uphoqelelwe ngokubulawa kwalabo abenqabayo. Umhlaba umele izwe lesithembiso.

Bantwana bami, yekani ukukholwa ngamanga alabo abangakhulumi egameni lami, ngoba angibathumanga. Abaphrofethi bami beqiniso bakhuluma ngazwi linye, futhi baningi, futhi abaphikisani, futhi abaphikisani nemibhalo, ngoba mina ngiyiZwi elaba yinyama. Lezi zilo ezimbili ezimbi azitholi amandla azo komunye ubaba wesonto okhohlakele, kodwa kuDrako uqobo lwakhe, ngoba uDrako ungusathane. Qondisisani ukuthi ubuKristu ngeke bube nandima nhlobo ngoba wonke amasonto amanga ayahlulelwa futhi abhubhiswe ngoba ukwahlulela kuqala endlini kaNkulunkulu, futhi iBandla lami leqiniso, ngisho nababiziweyo bami Abathembekile nabakhethiweyo bayothathwa bayiswe emazulwini ukuze bamukele imivuzo yabo futhi baqale ingunaphakade labo. Futhi kunikiwe kudrako ukuba abuse, futhi abafokazi bayovela, kodwa ababona abafokazi kodwa yizingelosi eziwileyo uDrako ayinhloko yazo. Iningi lalezi zigcinwe ngamaketanga kusukela ezinsukwini zikaNowa kodwa manje sezikhululiwe ukuze zehlulelwe okokugcina okungalali.

Isilo sasolwandle, sona kanye esigama laso lihunyushelwa esiNgisini lisuka esiHebherwini lithi: Umbani uwe ezulwini: fundani ukuze nazi. Ngoba akabafisi abesifazane, futhi akahloniphi nkulunkulu: ngokuba uyoziphakamisa ngaphezu kwakho konke. Isilo esingudrako sasolwandle, nesilo sasemhlabathini siyovuka emlotheni njengamaqhawe ngokubhubhisa iBhabhiloni Eyimfihlo, isifebe esikhulu, futhi bonke basebenzisana ngokufihla. Bayayizonda iMelika nanoma yini ehlangene negama likaJesu Kristu, kodwa bayokhothama ngedolo ngokufanayo ekwahlulelweni kwabo, lapho bengeke baphinde balulame khona. Ngakho-ke, imibhalo ayinakuzwisiseka esimweni samanje sezizwe nomhlaba uqobo. Ngoba uYawe ulungiselela ukuletha ukuzamazama okukhulu emhlabeni ngokwahlulela futhi imingcele iyodwetshwa kabusha. Lena yimpi yabo yokugcina yokubhubhisa uhlelo oludala, nokuletha uhlelo lukaSathane oluzobusa isintu ngesilo sasolwandle nesilo sasemhlabathini esingumphrofethi wamanga. Izimpondo eziyishumi, ezingamakhosi ayishumi ayothola amandla okubusa ihora elilodwa, ngoba kunikiwe kanjalo, zizonikeza amandla azo nemvume yazo kule nhlangano yezilo ezintathu.

Bhekani! Ngizoninika ukulandelana okuyisisekelo kwezenzakalo ukuze nazi izikhathi eniphila kuzo. Ezinye zalezi zehlakalo zenzeka zilandelana, kanti ezinye zenzeka ngasikhathi sinye, futhi zimi kanje!

  • IBhabhiloni Eyimfihlakalo iyabhujiswa njengoba impi yomhlaba isabalala emhlabeni, futhi manje sebevele behlela amabutho abo futhi bahlela ukuhlasela okuhlelwe kahle kakhulu iBhabhiloni Eyimfihlakalo. Ningakholwa abaholi benu, noma abezindaba, ngoba bayipulpiti labaqambimanga abayingxenye yokuhlasela.
  • Isilo sasolwandle, nesilo sasemhlabathini, nodrako bayovuka emlotheni wempi.
  • Lezi zilo ezimbili ezimbi ziyomelana nofakazi ababili abayophrofetha izinyanga ezingama-42, lapho beyobulawa khona bese benyukela emazulwini ngezinsuku ezintathu nengxenye futhi kanjalo kuyoba ukuvuka kwabo.
  • Njengoba ithempeli lakhiwa, impi emelene nabangcwele iyaqhubeka izinyanga ezingama-42 futhi uphawu lwesilo luyasungulwa, lusetshenziswe futhi luphoqelelwe ngaphansi kwesijeziso sokufa. Bese kuba yinguquko, lapho isinengiso sencithakalo senziwa futhi isilo sasemhlabathini sizofuna ukubulala bonke abangeza kimi, ngisho uYashuwa uMashiya ngalesi sikhathi.
  • Isilo sasemhlabathini sizimemezela singuNkulunkulu bese siphendukela kubantu ukuba sibabulale, ngoba sikhashela abantu ngendlela efanayo no-Antiyokusi Epifanesi.
  • Ngabheka, bheka, iWundlu limi entabeni yaseSiyoni, kumi nalo abayizinkulungwane eziyikhulu namashumi amane nane, bephethe igama likaYise libhalwe emabunzini abo. (Babusisiwe abafileyo abafela eNkosini kusukela manje, yebo, usho uMoya, ukuba baphumule emisebenzini yabo, nemisebenzi yabo iyabalandela.)
  • Abayizi-144,000 bami bayovikela abantu bami futhi babayise ezindaweni zokuphepha ukuze badlule esiphephweni njengoba izwe lihlanjululwa kukho konke ukungalungi, ngisho nokhula olubi. Ngoba ukube angizifinyezanga lezo zinsuku, bekungeke kusinde nyama, ngakho-ke ngizovikela abaningi ehlane. Lokhu kuyokwenzeka ngokwengxenye ukuze insali yesintu isindiswe.
  • Abayizi-144,000 beza emhlabeni bembethe izingubo engizobathungela zona ngokukhethekile, futhi ngeke zaphulwe futhi bayoba namandla amakhulu futhi akukho sitha esiyobanqoba, ngaleyo ndlela izinhloso zikaYawe ziyogcwaliseka.

NginguYashuwa uMashiya futhi ngithumele lawa mazwi ngenceku yami, futhi ngenceku yami amazwi ami athunyelwe.

Bhekani emazulwini nina Bandla lami elithembekile ngoba ngiyasondela futhi ngizoniyisa endlini kaBaba lapho nginilungiselele khona indawo futhi nibuse nami njengamakhosi nabapristi, umbuso wami ongenasiphelo, ngakho-ke ziduduzeni ngalawa Mazwi.
