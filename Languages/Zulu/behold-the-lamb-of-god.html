Ubufakazi Obukabili: Kwa-Israyeli
Okubabayo Nokumnandi
Novemba 16, 2024

Bhekani! IWundlu likaNkulunkulu!

Zwanini amazwi kaYeshua HaMashiach, nibeke izindlebe zenu emizindweni yamazwi ami, nisoke izinhliziyo zenu ukuze namukele uMesiya wenu weqiniso, O’ Israyeli, niqonde inkulumo yami ukuze nihlakaniphe ezindleleni zenu, nokuthi ningivumele ngiqondise kabusha izinyawo zenu ngiguqule indlela yenu isuke kuleyo ndlela ebanzi yokubhujiswa eniyilandelayo, iye endleleni encane yensindiso eyizindlela zami naleyo engiyiyo. Ngoba nginguye lowo uYahweh amthumile ngenxa yensindiso yenu, futhi ngiyimbewu ethenjisiwe yowesifazane eyochoboza ikhanda lenyoka, futhi nginibizela kimi bafowethu, yebo izihlobo zami engingumhlengi wazo. Ngakho nginiyala ukuba nisoke izinhliziyo zenu niyeke izimpikiswano zenu ezingatheli ukuze nithele izithelo kuMesiya wenu weqiniso; ngakho yizwani imiyalo yami eninikwe yona ukuze nilalele, ukuze niphile phambi kukaYahweh Omkhulu ENGINGUYE, ngokungamukela mina njengoMesiya wenu waphakade, enginguye ngempela, ngempela.

Futhi njengoba uYahweh wathumela umthetho kaMose koKhokho benu (lowo mthetho ongumfundisi wenu owayehloselwe ukuniletha kuKristu), kanjalo noYahweh ungithumile mina njengoba kwathenjiswa endulo lapho ethembisa insindiso koBaba, engikhuluma manje nezingane zabo, zona izingane zakwa-Israyeli engizithanda ngothando oluvuthayo njalo. Nokho, ngokungazi nangokungakholwa okungenalukholo, ngaliwa oKhokho benu, nganikelwa ezandleni zabantu abayizoni ukuze benze ngonya olukhulu nenzondo lezo zinto umthetho owawubavimbela ukuba bazenze; futhi ukuze babonakale belungile kubantu bezwe lapho bebulala iwundlu likaNkulunkulu ngemvume yoKhokho benu, icala elisavunyelwa abaningi benu ngobuwula.

Nokho, qondani Israyeli ukuthi angithathwanga ngendluzula yizitha zami nezitha zenu. Qondani, ukuthi angithathwanga ngendluzula, nangobuqili, futhi asikho isikhali esakhelwe ukulwa nami esingaphumelela, ngoba ushilo kanjalo uYahweh, nalokho okushiwo uYahweh akekho ongakuguqula! Impela, Israyeli, nginguye owathi kuJuda Iskariyothe lapho ngikhashelwa isandla sakhe ngezinhlamvu zesiliva ezingamashumi amathathu, lokhu okwenzayo, kwenze masinyane! Ngoba impilo yami yathengiselwa ezandleni zalabo abangigwazayo ngenani elifanele umninisigqila esasihlatshwe yinkunzi, linikezwa nangezandla zabapristi abakhulu neSanihedrini engabhekana nabo ngenxa yobubi babo nezindlela zokuzenzisa. Kanjalo-ke uJuda wangikhashela ezandleni zika-Esawu, yena lowo osephinde wabanga izwe lesithembiso ngendluzula, ibango u-Esawu alithengisa ngesinkwanyana sesinkwa. Nokho, ngazivumela ngokuzithandela ukuba ngithathwe yizitha zami, nakuba ngangibaleke kalula, futhi ngiqinisile, ngiqinisile, ukube ngangithandazile kuYahweh, wayeyonginika amabutho ezingelosi angaphezu kweshumi nambili, kodwa yayiyogcwaliseka kanjani-ke imibhalo, kanjalo kwaqedwa.

Nokho, kunjalo ukuthi izinjongo zami nezindlela zami azizange ziqondwe yizihlobo zami kanye nalabo besizwe sami engibathanda phakade ngothando olujulile noluvuthayo olungenakucinywa noma lunqotshwe, ngoba ngiyiDwala lenu leSisekelo okungekho makhi ongakha ngaphandle kwalo, nalelo abangakholwa abakhubeka kulo. Ngoba kwakungothandweni lwami ngani, nango-Adamu wonke onqotshwe abawileyo, lapho ngazinikela khona ngokuzithandela ezandleni zezitha zami ukuze nginihlenge esiqalekisweni somthetho.

Kanjalo-ke lona ngumthetho wami waphakade, wokuba nithandane, njengoba nami nginithandile. Alukho uthando olukhulu kunalolu, lapho umuntu enikela ngokuphila kwakhe kube ngumhlatshelo ngenxa yabangane bakhe, engakwenza ngokuzithandela ngazi ukuthi ngaphandle kokulalela kwami intando kaBaba, isintu sasingalahlwa phakade. Futhi ngingumsindisi ka-Adamu, yena lowo ohlengile isintu esimweni saso sokuwa, uma ekhona umuntu oza kimi ngamadolo aguqile evuma ukuthi ngiyiNkosi yakhe, futhi impela nginguMesiya wenu weqiniso, O’ Israyeli.

Qondani bafowethu, ukuthi angizange ngisho lutho ngami olwalungabhalwanga ngomunwe kaYahweh ezibhebheni zamatshe, futhi lwashiwo ngomlomo wezinceku zakhe abaprofethi ukuze kubhalelwe ukholo nokulalela kwezindlalifa zesithembiso, eniyizo nina O’ Israyeli, uma ningeke nizenze lukhuni izinhliziyo zenu njengoba oKhokho benu benza ehlane ekuvukeleni kwabo uYahweh, kwathi ehlane izimpilo zabo ezingenakholo zaphela ngeze elibi, futhi bona abazange balizuze ifa lezithembiso, kodwa izingane zokholo!

Nokho, ebumpumputheni benu obuyingxenye nikholwa amanga ka-Esawu okuthi ngingumkhohlisi, futhi impela ngaliwa ngu-Esawu ngoba u-Esawu ufuna ukubanga ifa alithengisela omncane, futhi ayikho indlela yokuphenduka ka-Esawu, nakuba wayifuna ngezinyembezi. Futhi oKhokho benu abazange baziqonde izinjongo zami zangempela ngoba babefuna umholi wezwe ozoketula labo ababezinikele ekubathobeleni, ukuze bagcine izikhundla zabo phakathi kwabantu. Nokho, ngathunyelwa ukuyokhulula isintu esandleni sabawileyo, u-Adamu abajoyina ngobuwula ekungalalelini kwakhe umyalo kaYahweh ngenxa yamaqhinga enyoka eyamkhohlisa yena nonina wabo bonke abaphilayo lapho bona e-Edeni ekuqaleni. Futhi nakuba izizwe eziningi ziye zavuka zawa, nemibuso eminingi yavuka yawa kusukela ezikhathini zomhlatshelo wami, u-Israyeli usagqilazekile ezitheni zakhe kuze kube namuhla, esebenzela izinhloso zika-Esawu ofuna ukumbhubhisa ngokuphelele.

Yebo, u-Israyeli uhlale ebugqilini ngoba isizwe senu sathathwa kini njengesijeziso sokubulala igazi elingenacala, nokubulala ngenhloso nangonya umuntu ongenasono owathunyelwa nguYahweh ngenxa yokuhlengwa kwenu. Mina kanye, uYeshua HaMashiach, lapho ukugcwala kwesikhathi kufika, uNkulunkulu wathumela iNdodana yakhe, yazalwa ngowesifazane, yaba ngaphansi komthetho, ukuze ahlenge labo ababephansi komthetho. Futhi empilweni yami engenasono nasekulaleleni kwami, ngazinikela njengewundlu likaNkulunkulu emhlatshelweni obuhlungu nonya lapho ngazinikela njengompristi omkhulu phakade ngokohlelo lukaMelkisedeki.

Futhi lowo msebenzi usuqediwe, futhi sengihlezi ngakwesokunene sikaBaba, yena uYahweh, Omkhulu ENGINGUYE. Futhi ngizuze konke ifa, futhi konke ezulwini nasemhlabeni kunikelwe ezandleni zami ezinamandla kuze kube yilapho izitha zami zenziwa isenabelo sezinyawo zami, kodwa uMbuso wami awusoze waba nasiphelo! Lowo Mbuso lapho iNkosi yenu iyobusa khona usondela ngokuqinisekile ekupheleni kosizi lukaJakobe, lapho ngizobhubhisa khona umbuso wezitha ngilethe ukwahlulela okukhulu nomphumela omubi kwabawileyo, bayongena esijezisweni ngenxa yezono zabo eziningi, nesijeziso sabo saphakade lapho kungeke kube nomzuzwana wokuphumula. Futhi abanye babo bayongena kulelo chibi elikhulu lomlilo nesibabule esivuthayo ekufikeni kwami kwesibili ukuzobusa umhlaba, lapho isilo nomprofethi wamanga bephonswa khona bephila. Futhi oSathane bayoboshwa ngalolo ketanga olukhulu abayoboshwa ngalo ekuhlushweni nasekwesabeni okukhulu, njengoba beboshwe ngokwesaba okulindele lokho okubalindele efeni labo laphakade lesijeziso ingunaphakade. Yebo, isigwebo esesabekayo uSathane angenakuze asibalekele, lapho eboshelwa khona aphonswe emlilweni oshisa kakhulu phakade ekupheleni kwesizini yakhe encane.

Ngoba nginguye owanikelwa ngenxa yensindiso kaJakobe, imbewu ethenjisiwe yowesifazane u-Adamu akholwa kuyo ngethemba, futhi uyovuselwa ekuphileni okusha kuze kube phakade. Ngoba nginguye othunyelwe ukuhlenga u-Adamu, futhi nganikela ngeconsi lami lokugcina legazi elachitheka lamunywa umhlabathi obomvu eKalvari, lapho ngasho khona lawo mazwi aphakade athi, Sekuphelile. Kunjalo-ke, ukuthi nginguye owathi othandweni lwami ngoYahweh nothando lwami ngomunye umuntu wazinikela emhlatshelweni ngobuhlungu obukhulu, ihlazo, nokuhlushwa, futhi nginqobile isono nokufa, futhi usuku luyasondela lapho zonke izinto ezimbi ziyobe zingasekho.

Futhi ngakwenza lokho ngokuzithandela, ngenxa yenkazimulo eyayibekwe phambi kwami, ngakwenza ngalidelela lonke ihlazo elalingesilona elami. Nokho, othandweni lwami ngani, ngathatha ihlazo nesijeziso senu emhlane wami, njengoba babedabula inyama yami emathanjeni ami ngeziqhwebu zabo. Yebo, ngathatha ihlazo nesijeziso senu ekuhlukunyezweni nasenzondweni yabantu abayizoni ababeqhutshwa abawileyo bona, njengoba babengibulala ngokungazi esikhundleni senu bengaqondi ezindleleni zabo zobulwane ukuthi ngokubulala iwundlu likaNkulunkulu babezibophela bona emlilweni kuze kube phakade, ngoba akunakwenzeka ukuthi ukufa kungibambe, ngoba angikaze ngone nakanye, futhi ngiqinisile ngeke ngize ngone!

Yebo, Israyeli, ngathatha indawo yenu esijezisweni nendawo yoKhokho benu abangala ngokungazi ngomyalo ka-Esawu! Kodwa wawungahlengwa kanjani ngaphandle kokukhashelwa kwabo okungenalukholo, ngoba ukuze kuhlengwe u-Adamu, igazi elingenasono kuphela elalingagcwalisa izimfuneko zesijeziso ezifunwa yimithetho yaphakade nelungile kaYahweh, ngoba ngaphandle kokuchithwa kwegazi akukho ukuthethelelwa kwezono. Futhi njengoba u-Adamu wadalwa engenasono ephelele ngosuku adalwa ngalo, kanjalo nami, uYeshua HaMashiach (oyiwundlu likaNkulunkulu) ngadalwa ngiphelele ngingenasono ukuze kube nokuhlengwa okuphelele kwesintu. Ngoba uma igazi lezinkunzi nelezimbuzi, nomlotha wesithokazi, kufafazwa phezu kwabangcolileyo, kungcwelisa mayelana nokuhlanzwa kwenyama, kakhulu kangakanani igazi likaKristu, owathi ngoMoya waphakade wazinikela kuNkulunkulu engenaphutha, liyakuhlanza unembeza wenu emisebenzini efileyo, ukuze nikhonze uNkulunkulu ophilayo?

Ngakho ngininika ukuqonda ukuthi ngathatha indawo yenu ekulahlweni nasesijezisweni, futhi ngakwenza ngokuzithandela nangaphandle kokuphoqwa; nakuba ngathandaza ensimini yaseGetsemane ukuthi uma ikhona enye indlela, kodwa makungenzeki intando yami kodwa ekaYahweh. Kanjalo-ke ehlazweni lami, ngakhishelwa ngaphandle kwamasango alowo muzi omkhulu, (iJerusalema elibulala abaprofethi) lapho ngathunyelwa khona kwabakithi, kwabelwana khona ngezingubo zami njengoba benza inkatho! Yebo, laphaya eKalvari, emva kokuba sengikhuthazelele amahora okuhlushwa okungapheliyo engangingenacala kukho, ngabethelwa lapho ngezandla zabantu abayizoni, engangingenaso isabelo ezonweni zabo nakancane. Nokho ngokuzithandela ngaboshelwa ngabethelwa ezandleni nasezinyaweni zami ukuze ngilenga kuleso sigxobo esilukhuni esikhundleni senu, isijeziso esasingesami ukusithwala. Ngoba kunini lapho kwanikezwa khona ongenacala ukuba ngokuzithandela ahlotshiswe ngehlazo umuthi wokudelelwa ngobuhlungu obukhulu ngenxa yensindiso yabafowabo abangalungile ukuze ahlupheke esikhundleni sabo, nokho othandweni lwami ngani ngakwenza lokho kanye. Qondani ukuthi noma nini ngangibavimbile ngokucela kuBaba, futhi wayeyonginika amabutho ezingelosi angaphezu kuka-12 ngokomyalo wami, nokho angizange ngicele kodwa ngakhetha ukukhuthazela ngokugcwele nangokuzithandela isijeziso senu esasingesami. Kanjalo-ke othandweni lwami ngazinikela ngenxa yenu, futhi ngangizophinda ngikwenze, nakuba into enjalo ingadingeki, futhi uYahweh uwamukele umhlatshelo wami kanye kuphela, ngokuphelele, naphakade; ngakho-ke akusekho mhlatshelo wezono.

Ngakho-ke, Israyeli, qondani uthando, ukubekezela, umusa nesihe enginikhombisa kona kulesi simo senu samanje sokwenqaba kokuvukela uMesiya wenu weqiniso, futhi ngiza kini kulesi sikhathi sokugcina othandweni lwami ngani olusengapheli nolungenakunqotshwa, uma kuphela ningamukela uMesiya wenu weqiniso. Ngoba nginguye owathatha ngokuzithandela indawo yenu phakathi kokulahlwa, isijeziso, nokubulawa kwezoni ngoba inkokhelo yesono ingukufa, nokho mina ngingenasono phakade, futhi ngihlala ngenza intando kaBaba, isibonelo enisilahlayo ekungalalelini kwenu uYahweh ngenkani, nasekwenqabeni kwenu uMesiya wenu weqiniso.

Kanti ngani ngenqatshwa? Ngisho noPontius Pilatus, owayenguMbusi waseJudiya ngaleso sikhathi, wayezimisele ukungidedela futhi wakhuluma iqiniso ngabamangaleli bami, yebo, nalabo bakwa-Esawu abaqoqela oKhokho benu ukuba bamemeze kuye bathi, "Sikhululele uBharaba!" Njengoba bememeza cishe ngaso leso sikhathi bethi, "Mbethele!", mayelana noPilatus ekhululela kubo uMesiya wabo weqiniso negazi elingenacala abalenqaba. Ngoba wayazi kahle ukuthi bangilethe ngomhawu. Futhi ngenkathi ehleli esihlalweni sokwahlulela, umkakhe wathumela kuye, wathi, "Ungabi nandaba nalowo muntu olungileyo, ngokuba ngihlukumezeke kakhulu namuhla ephusheni ngenxa yakhe," kwaba njalo-ke futhi kulotshiwe emibhalweni engcwele, uma nisazothola ukholo lokukholwa ubufakazi bami.

Futhi konke kungenxa yokuthi oKhokho benu babefisa izinto zaleli zwe futhi bethanda izikhundla zabo phakathi kwabantu lapho babengaphila khona ngokweshumi neminikelo yabantu, futhi beme njengabahlaziyi bamanga abazidlayo, lapho babeqeqesha khona ukuhlakanipha kuka-Esawu ezindlebeni zabantu: yebo, ukuhlakanipha okuyinkohliso okwaziswa kakhulu ngabadala babo kunelizwi nokuhlakanipha kukaYahweh, uMINA ENGINGUYE omkhulu, abamala ngenkathi bephika futhi bezibethelela uJesu waseNazaretha.

Kanjalo-ke, ekunginqatshweni kwenu, mina Jesu waseNazaretha, ngaphendukela kwabezizwe, futhi balizwa izwi lami isikhashana, futhi abaningi, abaningi ababengenalo ithemba, manje sebefinyelele ensindisweni eNkosini yabo, isixuku esikhulu engiyosivusela kimi ukuze ngisethule kuYahweh embuthanweni omkhulu wabangcwele osuzokwenzeka. Futhi abaningi baseJudiya nabaningi ababehlakazekile phesheya baphawulelwe insindiso ephelele. Futhi kubafowenu, ngisho nalabo abavela embusweni wasenyakatho abaphawulelwe insindiso yaphakade, abaningi babo manje sebaphawulwe ingunaphakade futhi bayovuselwa ebuntshani bokuphila. Ngiqinisile, ngiqinisile, bonke laba banishiyele isibonelo sokuphenduka okufanele nisilandele, uma nilalela izwi loMesiya weqiniso okuwukuphela kwakhe uYahweh ayoke amthumele, uJesu waseNazaretha, uMesiya wenu weqiniso!

Ngenxa yezono zazo, izizwe zasenyakatho zahlakazwa phakathi kwezizwe, lapho zigcinwe futhi zalondolozelwa insindiso, ngesandla esinamandla sikaYahweh. Nokho kulotshiwe ngesandla soMphostoli wami uPawulu ngenkathi ekhuluma nabaseRoma eminyakeni eminingi edlule: Siyazi ukuthi zonke izinto zisebenzela ukuhle kulabo abathanda uNkulunkulu, kulabo ababiziweyo ngokwenjongo yakhe. Ngoba labo abazi ngaphambili, wabamisa futhi ukuba bafane nesimo seNdodana yakhe, ukuze ibe yizibulo phakathi kwabazalwane abaningi. Ngaphezu kwalokho, labo abamisayo, wababiza futhi, futhi labo ababizayo, wabalungisa futhi, futhi labo abalungisayo, wabakhazimulisa futhi. Kanjalo-ke kwa-Israyeli kaNkulunkulu kuzofezeka ukubuthwa kwezizwe ezilahlekile, futhi ama-Israyeli amaningi afika ensindisweni ngokungivuma njengeNkosi yawo, uJesu waseNazaretha, phakathi nenkathi yami yomusa nensindiso abayizuzile.

Nakuba okwamanje nidelela imisebenzi yami, imisebenzi yami yensindiso engiyifezile ezizweni ngomyalo kaYahweh phakathi nenkathi yami yomusa manje esifike esiphelweni sayo; ngakho-ke qondani ukuthi lokho uYahweh akuhlanze ngegazi likaYeshua HaMashiach, ningakubizi ngokungcolile noma okungahlanzekile! Futhi mina, Yeshua HaMashiach, ngiyanincenga ukuba nilandele izindlela zabafowenu noma ngabe baseduze noma bekude abeze kimi ngokuphenduka okuphelele ukuze nginamukele futhi nginibuyisele kuYahweh, ngoba lowo onayo iNdodana unaye noYise, kodwa lowo ongenayo iNdodana akanaye noYise, futhi wamemezela kanjalo uYahweh!

Kanjalo-ke ngiphendukela kubantu bakithi futhi, njengoba ngifulathela izizwe kulesi sikhathi sokugcina sokuphetha, futhi ukuhlukaniswa kokugcina manje kuzokwenzeka phakathi nesonto lamashumi ayisikhombisa likaDaniyeli, elifikile ukuzohlambulula umhlaba ngomlilo wami wokucwenga, futhi u-Esawu uyohlukaniswa noJakobe unomphela. Ngoba u-Esawu wathengisa ubuzibulo bakhe, futhi uYahweh wamukela isinqumo sakhe unomphela ukuze sibe yisinqumo saphakade, ngoba abantwana besithembiso bayizindlalifa zezithembiso hhayi abantwana benyama; uma ningabakaKristu, ningabembewu ka-Abrahama, nezindlalifa ngesithembiso. Kanjalo-ke izithembiso zefa ezenziwa nguYahweh uqobo zenziwa ngokholo kulabo ezenziwa kubo, ngoba olungileyo uyophila ngokholo! Futhi ngemva kokuba kunikezwe izithembiso zokwethembeka, kwenziwa nezivumelwano, kwanikezwa nezibonakaliso zesithembiso, futhi izivumelwano zaba yizincwadi zefa ngokuchithwa kwegazi, nazo zonke izivumelwano zakudala nazo zonke izithembiso zikaYahweh kusukela kokuncane kakhulu kuye kokukhulu kakhulu zifakiwe eTestamenteni lami Elisha lesithembiso, lapho indalo iyakwenziwa ilungile ingunaphakade ekubuyiselweni okuphakade kuYise wezibani, kuye okungekho ukuguquguquka nasithunzi sokuphenduka.

Ngakho-ke ngiza kubantu bakithi ngothando nokuzithoba, futhi ngiza kini ngokuzithandela othandweni lwami ngani ngingafisi ukuba namunye wenu abhujiswe ekungakholweni okungenalukholo. Futhi ngiqinisile, ngiqinisile, insali iyosindiswa iphila kwaJakobe ngisho naphakathi kosizi lukaJakobe okuyisonto lamashumi ayisikhombisa likaDaniyeli. Ngakho-ke, ngiyanincenga bafowethu, lalelani izwi lami ngoba lesi yisikhathi sokugcina futhi lokhu kungukuhlukaniswa kokugcina ngaphambi kokuba intukuthelo ithululwe ngokugcwele, nangaphambi kokuba ngize ngizobhubhisa umbuso wesitha ngokuphelele, ngoba ngokulunga ngiza njengeNgonyama yesizwe sakwaJuda futhi ngiza ukuzokwahlulela nokulwa!

Futhi bonke abayophendukela kimi ngalesi sikhathi, O Israyeli, ngisho bonke engibayala abazalwa ngoKhokho basendulo ukuba balalele izwi lami, kanye nabo bonke abayobiza ngegama lami elinamandla kuze kube sekupheleni abavela ezizweni bayosindiswa futhi, bayosindiswa ngamaqoqo abo. Izizwe ziyothola udumo olufanayo nolwami lapho ngazinikela ngenxa yezono zesintu, futhi zizofela ukholo njengoba zibiza igama lami elisindisayo kuze kube sekupheleni. Ziyoya enkazimulweni enkulu ingunaphakade nemivuzo yazo iyohlala njalo ngenxa yokuma kwazo, futhi ngokulalela kwazo ziyobe zinqobile izindlela zazo zokungakholwa nezokungashisi nokungabandi, kunjalo-ke, kodwa ngomlilo.

Futhi ku-Israyeli, bafowethu, ngisazofika ngizonisindisa futhi ngizofika ngizonisiza ngezinkulungwane zami eziyi-144, labo abazihlanze ngokugcwele njengabanqobi bami egazini lami lokuhlawulela nelokuhlenga ngosuku lwabo lwensindiso, futhi bayobuthwa kimi kulowo mhlangano omkhulu osuzokwenzeka ngokuzumayo emhlabeni. Ngoba ngizohola izinkulungwane zami eziyi-144 zehlele eNtabeni iZiyoni, futhi zizongilandela isinyathelo ngesinyathelo njengoba zihola insali yami kaJakobe iye endaweni ephephile ehlane lapho uYahweh elungise khona indawo yokuphepha kwayo, futhi iyondliwa inakekelwe, kanti udrako, isilo, nomprofethi wamanga ngeke bayithinte, futhi ngeke bayithole, yona leyo nsali eningayo nina, O Israyeli. Kodwa uBharaba uyoletha isinengiso sencithakalo, futhi ukhula oluningi luyohlanzwa ezweni, futhi ngizofihla insali yami empini yalolo suku olukhulu, usuku lokubuya kwami lwesibili, usuku uYahweh aluphawule emaphakadeni futhi olumisiwe unomphela ohlelweni lwakhe lwensindiso, futhi lolo suku luyasondela ngempela, ngoba kulotshiwe kanjalo futhi kuzokwenziwa kanjalo.

Ngakho-ke, bantu bakithi enginithanda ingunaphakade, nokho ngisalihlonipha ilungelo lenu elinikezwe nguNkulunkulu lokuzikhethela ukuthi iyiphi indlela enizoyihamba. Ngakho-ke ngiyanibuza, ingabe nizohlala kuleyo ndlela ebanzi eya ekubhujisweni eholela emalangabini angenakucimeka ahloselwe abawileyo? Noma, ingabe nizolalela izwi loMesiya wenu weqiniso, yena owanikela ngalo lonke iconsi lokugcina legazi lakhe elingenasono ekuhlushweni nasezinhlungwini ezinkulu ukuze niphile phambi kukaNkulunkulu wenu, uYahweh uMINA ENGINGUYE Omkhulu? Yebo, uYahweh ochithe izinyembezi eziningi enhliziyweni yakhe ebuhlungu ngenxa yezimvu ezilahlekile zendlu ka-Israyeli, yebo, bonke labo abenqaba izwi lakhe nezwi lami. Ingabe nizohlanganyela nami ngokulalela uMesiya wenu weqiniso? Noma niyozihlanganisa ngokuzithandela noBharaba u-Esawu amthumelele ukubhujiswa kwenu?

Ngakho yizwani amazwi ami, O Israyeli, niqonde ukukhetha okufanele nikwenze futhi niqonde ukuthi ngiyanidonsela kimi, futhi ngizothumela ofakazi bami ababili abanamandla futhi nginiyala ukuba nibazwe ukuze namukele ubufakazi babo, ngoba bayofakaza ngezinto eziningi ezifihliwe ezifihlelwe izinhliziyo zabantu kodwa abazozembula, futhi ubufakazi babo buyoma ingunaphakade. Ngakho-ke, lalelani izwi leWundlu leqiniso likaNkulunkulu, O Israyeli, ngoba akukho nsindiso nokuhlengwa kwelinye igama elake lanikezwa, futhi ngiqinisile, ngiqinisile, alikho elinye igama eliyoke linikezwe ngale njongo, ngaphandle kukaYeshua HaMashiach onguJesu waseNazaretha, nakuba ngingasaziwa ngokwenyama futhi nginguKristu Jesu, yena oyiNkosi yamakhosi noMbusi wababusi.

NgiyiNgonyama yesizwe sakwaJuda futhi ngokulunga ngiza ukuzokwahlulela nokulwa.

Nginguye othumele lawa mazwi ngenceku yami futhi ngenceku yami lawa mazwi athunyelwe, futhi ngiyafakaza ngiqinisekise ukuthi amazwi eTestamente laphakade embulwe ngesandla sami, futhi impela nguYahweh uqobo, ayiqiniso futhi aphakade, futhi inceku yami iwakhulume ngokwethembeka amazwi embulwe kuyo ngesikhathi sawo futhi ayolondolozwa phakathi kwezinhlekelele ezizayo futhi ayothola amehlo, izindlebe, nezinhliziyo zalabo athunyelwe kubo.

NginguMesiya wenu waphakade olangazelela insindiso nokuhlengwa kwenu Israyeli, futhi ngiqinisile, ngiqinisile, ngizimisele ukwamukela kimi noma ubani ophendula izwi lami futhi niyongena eMbusweni weminyaka eyinkulungwane futhi niyoba yisizwe esikhethekile, niyophila impilo yaphakade futhi niyothola amadlelo aphakade phakathi kuka-Israyeli kaNkulunkulu, futhi ngeke niphinde nihlukaniswe nokubusa okulungileyo kweNkosi yenu, uMpristi omkhulu, noMesiya, futhi niyoholwa ingunaphakade ngokuthula nokulunga futhi niyokhula njengamathole esibayeni.

NginguYeshua HaMashiach futhi ngiyilokho uMINA ENGINGUYE omkhulu angenze ngakho, futhi ngiyiwundlu likaNkulunkulu elinikezwe njengephasika lenu eliphelele nelingenasono ingunaphakade.

Yiliphi iwundlu enizolikhetha, O Israyeli?
