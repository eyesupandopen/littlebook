Umhlaba UngoweNkosi!

Umhlaba ungowenkosi nokugcwala kwawo: Nakuba umphangi nesela bemisile izimangalo zabo, bangenza kuphela lokho uBaba wami akunikele esandleni sabo ukuba bakufeze ohlelweni lwakhe olungcwele lokuhlenga isintu. Ningamesabi! Kumelwe aqhubeke isikhashana, njengoba ihora lakhe selifikile; kodwa ningamesabi, nina balungileyo bami, abathembekileyo; ningamesabi! Angithi ngininikile ukunqoba phezu kwakhe? Inhlupheko kaJakobe ayiyona eyabathembekileyo bami, futhi labo abeza kimi ngezikhathi zokuhlunyeleliswa bayophakanyiswa kakhulu esihlalweni sobukhosi sikaNkulunkulu uqobo. Yebo, bantu bami abathembekileyo, umvuzo omkhulu nilondolozelwe wona ngenxa yokwethembeka kwenu enikukhombise ngokwethembeka kimi.

Nginenze amakhosi nabapristi kimi eMbusweni wami ozayo naphambi kukaBaba wethu, ngakho-ke, manini niqine, bantwana bami, kule mizuzu yokugcina yehora lokugcina. Ukubonakala kwami sekusondele, nokuhlengwa kwenu kusondele njengokuphefumula kwenu okulandelayo, ngakho manini niqine ngokwethembeka phambi kwami, nazi ukuthi ngingamandla enu nensindiso yenu futhi nivalelwe uphawu kimi, njengoba nganithenga ngegazi lami lapho nginikela ngokuphila kwami ngenxa yenu ukuze nibuyisane othandweni noBaba wethu osezulwini. Ngilangazelela lolo suku, noBaba wethu uyaulangazelela lolo suku, lapho niyokuma phambi kwakhe ngeimizimba enginiphathele yona ekuhlengweni kwenu okuphelele, futhi ngeke kusaba khona isiqalekiso phezu kwabakhethiweyo bami.

Oh, muntu, ungubani wena ukuba ubeke isimangalo efeni lami? Awazi yini ukuthi ngathenga umhlaba nakho konke okukuwo ngegazi lami elingenasono? Umhlaba ungowenkosi nokugcwala kwawo, futhi ukulunga kuyeza emhlabeni! Nakuba ubungawubhubhisa umhlaba futhi uwugwinciyele uwenze indawo yokuhlala yezinengiso zakho ezinengekayo, nakuba ubungafunga ukwethembeka esitheni sami saphakade futhi uzifunele ukuhlushwa kwakho ukuze uzuze okwesikhashana okuyisisekelo, awuqondi ukuthi ulwa nemiphefumulo yakho njengoba uziqoqela ulaka kuze kube usuku lolaka, lapho ngiyovuvuza khona umuntu ngamunye ngokwemisebenzi yakhe.

Oh, muntu oyisiwula, uthi enhliziyweni yakho onkulunkulu bakho bayokusindisa, kanti abanawo umvuzo wakho, futhi ukhohlisiwe futhi ukhohlisa abanye ezinkohlisweni zakho. Onkulunkulu bakho bamatshe abakwazi ukukhuluma; izithombe zakho ezinhliziyweni zenu azizwa; azinakukusindisa, wena okhonza izithombe! Bekuyoba ngcono kuwe ukuba wawungazalwanga, ngoba ulaka lukaNkulunkulu luhleli phezu kwakho ngenxa yezindlela zakho ezinenhliziyo elukhuni. Nakuba umlomo wakho usuphenduke ithuna elivulekileyo, ukhipha izinto ezinkulu ngokumelene neNkosi yamakhosi, amazwi akho ayobuyela kuwe, nezifiso zakho ezimbi ziyophendukela kuwe esijezisweni sakho saphakade esizayo. Oh, ukube wawuphendukele kimi wamukela insindiso esimweni sakho samanje sokuwa. Oh, ukube wawuvele walivuma igama lami ngezindebe zakho eziqamba amanga. Kodwa cha, uthande amanga akho, futhi uthande izinkohliso zakho, futhi uthengise imiphefumulo yakho ngokulahlwa kwaphakade ngohlavana lwesinkwa olungenakukusimamisa; ngokuba mina ngiyisinkwa sokuphila! Inhlekelele yakho iyokwehlela ngokuzumayo futhi kungabikho khambi. Ngiyosihlakaza isizwe sakho; ngiyobhubhisa ukuhweba nomnotho wakho; ngomzuzwana ngiyokwamuka ingcebo yakho; impela, ngiyokwemuka yonke injabulo kuwe ngomzuzwana, futhi ubumpofu nosizi lwakho kuyokuqeda. Ulaka lweWundlu luyasondela, lapho impindiselo yami uBaba anginike yona iyokwenziwa, ngokuba ngifanele ukuvula izincwadi nokuqaqa izimpawu.

Ngubani phakathi kwenu okhuluma amanga egameni lami? Nithi niyangazi, ningimele; abanye benu bacabanga nokuthi ningangiyala ukuba ngenze intando yenu futhi nizizuzele inzuzo yezinto ezibonakalayo ngemisebenzi yenu emihle, engeyona into engaphezu kwezidwedwe ezingcolileyo. Ningangikhulumela kanjani ningangazi? Ningangikhulumela kanjani nginganinikanga amazwi? Ningakhulumela kanjani abantu bami nginganithumanga? Cha, nizithumele nina, nidayisa izimpahla zenu ezonakele ngenxa yenzuzo yobugovu eyisisekelo nangenxa yokudunyiswa ngabantu enikufisa kakhulu. Siwula! Sukani kimi, njengoba angikaze nginazi!

Nina baprofethi bamanga, abathi ngiyiNkosi ngegama kuphela; nina baprofethi bamanga, enikhuluma amanga amakhulu ngokumelene nemiphefumulo yenu; niyobhubha ngokuphelele, futhi ngiyonikhafula emlonyeni wami. Ngakho-ke, phendukani! Buyelani kimi nami ngizobuyela kini. Phumani phakathi kwezithombe zenu nisuke emaqenjini enu aqalekisiweyo nize kimi ningenze iNkosi yenu! Oh, ukube beningamukela insindiso yami; nokho niyaphikelela ezonweni zenu futhi nizithethelele icala lenu njengoba nicaphuna iZwi lami elingcwele ngenhliziyo engenalutho, inhliziyo egcwele ukuphinga nobufebe njengoba nigijimela ezithombeni zenu. Ubani oyonisindisa olakeni lwami lapho nilahlekise abantu bami ngenxa yenzuzo yenu eyisisekelo? Ukube beningenze iNkosi njengoba imilomo yenu eqamba amanga isho, bengiyonisindisa ezonweni zenu nginenze niphelele kimi. Bengiyokwelusa ubuhlungu umubi anibangela bona. Bengiyoninika indlela nenjongo futhi nginithume emhlabeni ninemilomo egcwele izibusiso neqiniso njengoba beniyohola abantu bami kimi.

Ningacabangi ukuthi ngidinga usizo lwenu, njengoba ngiyiNkosi yeqiniso yezulu nomhlaba, futhi ngiyiNkosi yamakhosi neNkosi yababusi. Labo engibamisile enkonzweni banikezwe udumo olukhulu lokungikhulumela nokunakekela umhlambi wami, udumo engiyoluvuza ngengcebo yaphakade; nokho, nina nishintshe mina ngenguqulo yamanga kaKristu. Omunye uJesu nizakhele yena; nithande umoya womphikukristu, futhi nonile ingunaphakade emiphefumulweni yenu ngokumemezela ukuthi angikho eBandleni lami engalithenga ngegazi lami, isono engeke nilulame kuso. Impela, zonke lezi zinto nokunye kuyofika phezu kwenu olakeni lwami ngaphandle kokuba nizithobe phambi kwami futhi ningenze iNkosi ngeqiniso ngokuphenduka ngendwangu yesaka nomlotha. Ningazichazi izizathu zokuchitha amazwi ami njengoba nidlala ngamazwi enkulumweni yami! Phendukani! Hlalani phansi ngendwangu yesaka nomlotha nikhale ngezono zenu, futhi ngizonamukela kimi nginibuyisele emvuzweni wenu ebengingawunika omunye.
