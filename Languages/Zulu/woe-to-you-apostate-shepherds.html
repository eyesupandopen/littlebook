Ubufakazi Obubili: Ibandla
Emisebenzini kaPawulu Umphostoli
May 04, 2024

Maye Kini Belusi Abahlubukayo!

Maye kini, belusi abahlubukayo, ngokuba nitshale amabandla enu amanga kuwo wonke amazwe, futhi anikhulumi egameni lami, futhi angikunikezanga umsebenzi kuleyo misebenzi enizithathele yona. Futhi nenze imali uNkulunkulu wenu, ngokuba nethembele enzuzweni engcolile, futhi nifundise abantu bami ukuthi ukuzuza izinto ezibonakalayo kungukukhonza uNkulunkulu. Maye kini, belusi abahlubukayo, ngokuba amazwi kaJeremiya anichaza ngokunembile: Imbongolo yasendle ejwayele ihlane, ehogela umoya ngokuthanda kwayo; ngesikhathi sayo ngubani ongayibuyisa? Ngokuba ukuphinga kwenu nokufeba kwenu kukanye nobubi, ngokuba nikhethe oSathane babengonkulunkulu benu, futhi lezo zikhulu ezingelusizo lwalutho azibona onkulunkulu. Nizikhonzi zamanga ezizenza izikhonzi zokulunga, futhi akumangalisi, ngokuba uSathane uziguqula abe yingelosi yokukhanya.

Maye kini, belusi abahlubukayo, ngokuba nitshale amabandla enze amanzi ami amnandi okuphila ababa njengomhlonyane, eniwaphakela labo engibabizile, amanzi afudumele angcolile ababa esiswini. Ngisho namanzi enu ababayo aphakelwa emithonjeni ephukile, futhi aninamandla, nakuba niziqhayisa ngamandla amakhulu. Ningcolise amazwe ngezimfundiso zamanga futhi nakhe amabandla enu amanga abe ngamajele okomoya akhohlakaliswe uhulumeni wenu, ngokuba nidayise umhlambi wami esandleni sesitha; ngakho-ke, phumani kulo manje, bantu bami! Amabandla enu angenamsebenzi ahlubukayo asetshenziswa njengezikhungo zokulawula lapho nizoletha khona ukufa nokubhujiswa kulabo abasale ngemuva, futhi niyokhokha.

Nenze uRemfani uNkulunkulu wenu. Nikhonza uBali. Ninikela ngabantwana kuMoloki. Nikhohlakele ngokuphelele. Niyimithombo engenamanzi, izitsha ezingenalutho ezifanele ukubhujiswa, ngokuba ifa lenu liyisibabule esivuthayo, futhi niyokuba kulelo chibi elikhulu noyihlo, uDeveli. Ningabaphostoli bamanga, abaprofethi bamanga, abavangeli bamanga, abefundisi bamanga, nabafundisi bamanga; futhi labo enibaphendulela ezindleleni zenu ezilimazayo benziwa kabili babe ngabantwana besihogo kunani, ngokuba ningabokukhohlakala okukhohlakalisa ngokuphelele. Ngokuba uStefanu wabikezela ngezindlela zenu lapho ekhuluma ngokhokho benu, ngisho ukhula olwahlushwa u-Israyeli, ngokuba wathi: Yebo, nathatha itabernakele likaMoloki, nenkanyezi kankulunkulu wenu uRemfani, izifanekiso enazenza, ukuze nizikhonze. Nina belusi abahlubukayo nize niphakamise ngokungabi nahloni inkanyezi kaRemfani eniyihlobanisa ngokungazi noDavide, ukhokho wami; ngokuba mina, Jesu Kristu, ngizalwa kanjalo, futhi ngiyobusa esihlalweni sobukhosi sikaDavide ngokulunga. Ngokuba yimina onikezwe ukubusa okunjalo, ngokuba ngiyobusa umhlaba ngokulunga!

Maye kini, belusi abahlubukayo, ngiyoniphindisela ngokugcwele ngazo zonke izinkulumo zenu ezimbi, futhi zonke izindlela zenu ezimbi ziyobuyiselwa phezu kwamakhanda enu kuze kube phakade. Ngokuba niyohlanganyela noDeveli nezingelosi zakhe efeni labo elivela kuNkulunkulu oPhezukonke; ngisho uYahweh unilungiselele ichibi elikhulu lomlilo ongacimeki. Ninqabele insindiso kulabo ababeyifuna, kodwa yazini ukuthi ngiyobasindisa futhi nina niyokhokha. Ninqabele umoya wami kulabo ababeyovuma igama lami ngokubanika amazwi amanga okuwakhuluma nemithandazo eyisiphukuphuku, eyize nephindaphindwayo engasindisi. Ngizweni, bantu bami: insindiso iza ngokumemezela uJesu njengeNkosi yenu nokukholwa ukuthi uNkulunkulu wangivusa kwabafileyo, futhi kuphela ebudlelwaneni obusindisayo nami ningasindiswa; ngokuba ngigodla uphawu lukaNkulunkulu kulabo abavuma igama lami ngenhliziyo engenakholo, ngokuba ngihlola izinhliziyo ngivivinye nezinso.

Nicabanga ngokuzidla ukuthi ngenxa yokuthi ningibiza ngeNkosi phambi kwebandla lenu elithunjiwe, ngiyanazi? Ngiyothi kini ngalolo suku, sukani kimi, nina benzi bokubi, ngokuba angikaze nginazi! Nifundisa amanga niwenza iqiniso, futhi nidukisa labo abafuna mina, iNkosi uJesu Kristu, nibayise evangelini elikhohlakele, neliyisiphukuphuku, ngokuba alikho elinye ivangeli: Ngokuba izindaba ezinhle zikimi, Kristu Jesu! Ukubhedela kwenu okuyize sekuzophela, futhi intukuthelo yami ishisa phezu kwenu, futhi aniyikuphunyuka. Ngiyabona sonke isenzo sokuhlambalaza enisenzayo; ngizwa lonke izwi lokuhlambalaza enilikhuluma egameni lami, ngokuba ningumzenzisi. Nizenza sengathi nikhuluma amazwi ami, njengoba nizenza sengathi nginithumile ngezindaba zenu zokuzenzisa nezimangaliso zenu zokuzenzisa nokuphulukiswa kwenu okuhleliwe, ngokuba ningumzenzisi!

Maye kini, belusi abahlubukayo, ngokuba neba kulabo abangafika kimi, nibatshela amanga okuthi kudingeka ngoNkulunkulu baninike imali. Niphambanisa ngokungazi imiBhalo yami eNgcwele, nitshela labo enibadukisile ukuthi kufanele baninike okweshumi. Ngokuba ngamtshelani uPawulu lapho ebhalela labo baseKorinte: Kodwa ngithi, Ohlwanyela kancane, uyovuna kancane: nohlwanyela kakhulu, uyovuna kakhulu. Yilowo nalowo njengalokho enquma enhliziyweni yakhe, makanike kanjalo; kungabi ngokudabuka, noma ngokuphoqelelwa: ngokuba uNkulunkulu uthanda umuphi onamileyo. Kodwa cha, nifundisa amanga niwenza iqiniso futhi neba kubantu bami, nize nithumele nemali eniyiqoqayo ngezinjongo zikaSathane, futhi niyokhokha, ngokuba intukuthelo yami ibomvu ngokushisa phezu kwenu ngoba nidukisa abantu abafuna mina, Jesu Kristu, nibasuse ensindisweni nibayise ekubhujisweni. Kodwa yazini lokhu, belusi abahlubukayo, anisoze nahlwitha namunye walaba ezandleni zami, ngokuba kubo bonke uBaba anginike bona, ngalahlekelwa munye kuphela, indodana yokulahlwa, ngisho uJuda, ngoba wakhetha ukubhujiswa kwakhe kunokuphila kimi! Imisebenzi yenu nemizamo yenu iyize, o belusi abahlubukayo; nizibekelele ukwahlulelwa okukhulu nentukuthelo. Ngokuba ngakhuluma ngezindlela zokhokho benu, futhi niyibo abadla izindlu zabafelokazi futhi ngokuzenzisa benza imithandazo emide: NIYOKWAMUKELA ukulahlwa okukhulu!

Maye kini, belusi abahlubukayo, ngokuba niholele abantu bami ekusekeleni ohulumeni bezwe abangesibo abami, nakuba umhlaba ungowami. O, nina belusi abahlubukayo, nikhombisa abantu bami abaholi bezwe abangabakaDeveli, nibatshela ukuthi bathunyiwe nguNkulunkulu. Nidukisa abantu ekucabangeni ukuthi ngeke ngibabusise ngaphandle kokuba babusise labo abenza ukuqothulwa kohlanga kwabangenacala. Kodwa ngithini emibhalweni mayelana nani, ngokuba lokhu akukusha, futhi abaningi beza ngaphambi kwenu ngenkohliso, nakuba niyiziphukuphuku ngokwanele ukucabanga ukuthi ningabokuqala. Ngizweni, bantwana bami, ngokuba ngidalula lobu bubi ngenxa yenu. Ngokuba imibhalo yami ithi: Nina ningabakaNkulunkulu, bantwanyana, futhi nibanqobile: ngoba mkhulu lowo okini, kunalowo osezweni. Bona bangabezwe: ngakho-ke bakhuluma okwezwe, nezwe liyabezwa. Izintshumayelo zenu zezwe, imikhuba yenu yezwe, uzungu lwenu lwezwe noSathane, konke kuzofika esiphelweni esisheshayo, futhi anisoze navuka futhi. Kodwa wena, Makoti wami, ungowami, futhi la mazwi awasiwo awakho, ngokuba uyongena enjabulweni yeNkosi yakho, ngisho mina, Kristu Jesu.

Ngikhuluma manje namabandla amasonto ahlubukayo aholwa abelusi abahlubukayo, ngisho nawe, bandla lami laseLawodikeya. Ngiyayazi imisebenzi yakho, ukuthi awubandi futhi awushisi; ngangifisa sengathi ubuyabanda noma ushisa. Ngokuba uPawulu wabhalela uThimothewu mayelana nawe nabanye abafana nawe: Ngokuba isikhathi siyofika lapho bengeke babekezelele imfundiso eqinisileyo, kodwa ngokwezinkanuko zabo bayozibuthela abafundisi, benezindlebe ezilumayo: Futhi bayofulathelisa izindlebe zabo eqinisweni, baphendukele ezinganekwaneni. Baningi kakhulu kini abahambela la masonto ahlubukayo ngaphansi kwengcindezi yomphakathi nokwenza sengathi nizama ukungifuna, mina Kristu Jesu, okuyinto ngokuqinisekile eningayenzi.

Nifisa ukujatshuliswa, ngokuba ukuzijabulisa kuyisifiso senu, futhi ukuzijabulisa sekuyisithixo senu! Futhi anizongizwa mina, Jesu Kristu, futhi ngeke nilalele noma yiziphi izithunywa zami ngaphandle kokuba izindlebe zenu zikitazwe, kodwa amazwi ami awazikitazi izindlebe! Nina mabandla ahlubukayo: Ngizweni futhi ngizweni kahle, ngokuba ngathi encwadini enkulu yeSambulo: Futhi ngezwa elinye izwi livela ezulwini, lithi, Phumani kulo, bantu bami, ukuze ningabi nabahlanganyeli ezonweni zalo, futhi ukuze ningamukeli izinhlupho zalo: Ngokuba izono zalo zifinyelele ezulwini, futhi uNkulunkulu ukhumbule ukungalungi kwalo. Phumani kulo, noma niyohlanganyela ezinhluphweni zalo nasekwahlulelweni kwalo, ngokuba isikhathi sesifikile. KwabaseLawodikeya bami: Shiyani amasonto enu ahlubukayo manje, ngokuba isikhathi sesisondele, futhi ngiyanixwayisa, nakuba abaningi benu bevilapha kakhulu ukwenza ngokwamazwi ami.

Ningangabazi esexwayisweni sami, ngokuba nganikani uJohane ukuba akubhale encwadini yakhe yesibili? Noma ngubani oweqayo angahlali emfundisweni kaKristu, akanaye uNkulunkulu: lowo ohlala emfundisweni kaKristu, unabo bobabili uYise neNdodana. Uma kufika noma ngubani kini, futhi engalethi le mfundiso, ningamukeli endlini yenu, futhi ningambingeleli. Ngokuba lowo ombingelelayo, ungumhlanganyeli wezenzo zakhe ezimbi. Kodwa nina baseLawodikeya anilaleli kangangokuthi aniyinaki le mizwi futhi esikhundleni sokubafisela inhlanhla, okubi ngokwanele, niyaqhubeka ebubini futhi nibangenise ezindlini zenu futhi nibanikeze ukufinyelela kubantwana benu, futhi ninecala ngokuphelele phambi kwami, ngakho-ke Phendukani!

Ngokuba ngeqiniso, navulela iminyango yenu izwe nezindlela zalo; navumela izimpisi zangena, ngisho nabaphansi kunabo bonke, ukuba zidle abantwana bami, futhi ngaphandle kokuba niphenduke, niyokhokha. Phendukani noma ngizomelana nani, ngokuba ningabahlanganyeli bezenzo zabo ezimbi, futhi anilaleli kimi, Kristu Jesu, ngisho yena oyokwemuka umvuzo wenu awunike omunye uma niqhubeka nokunganginaki. Ngokuba umusa nesihe sami sekuzophela, futhi intukuthelo iyohlala esikhundleni sako, nakuba umusa nesihe kuhlala nami, futhi noma ngubani obiza egameni leNkosi kuze kube sekupheleni uyosindiswa. Ngokuba aninakuhlanganyela nezindlela zobumnyama ezingatheli, kodwa kufanele nizisole.

Kodwa aninalwazi kangangokuthi nicabanga ukuthi ngenxa yokuthi umuntu uqamba amanga futhi usebenzisa ngokuhlambalaza igama lami elingcwele emisebenzini yakhe engcwele, uthunywe yimi futhi ukhuluma amazwi ami. Zwanini amazwi ami, ngokuba niyobazi ngezithelo zabo! Buyelani eZwini lami elihlanzekile likaNkulunkulu nifunde ngami, ngokuba aningazi futhi izwi lami alaziwa kini, futhi aninaye uBaba ngoba aninayo iNdodana. Phendukelani kimi ngokuphenduka, futhi ngizonamukela. Hlalani ngesaka nomlotha ezinhliziyweni zenu futhi nizehlise ukuze nginiphakamise! Nganibizela ukuba ninqobe, futhi manje yithuba lenu lokugcina lokunqoba laba belusi abahlubukayo nezindlela zabo ezimbi; ngokuba ngiyeza, futhi ngiletha intukuthelo enkulu eBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo.

Isikhathi siwubukhazikhazi eningasenabo. Anisakwazi ukuvilapha, noma niyozithola osizini lukaJakobe, lapho ngangizonibekela khona indawo etafuleni lami futhi nginishade njengomakoti wami, kodwa anithembekile nisezingalweni zomunye, ngisho uSathane! Ngakho-ke yizani kimi ukuze nginivuselele emvuzweni wenu. Akwanele ukuphuma ngokunengeka; kufanele niphenduke kulobu bubi futhi nishintshe izindlela zenu futhi nivume lezi zono kimi, futhi ngizonihlambulula ngegazi lami elihlawulayo futhi ngisule amabala kini ngobumsulwa bami obuphelele, ngokuba ngihlanzekile nezingubo zami zimhlophe njengeqhwa elisha. Buyelani kimi futhi niyokuba nami phakade; phumani nize ezingalweni zami ezeluliwe, ngokuba ngilapha ukunitshela ukuthi aninaso isikhathi sokuchitha.

KuMakoti wami othembekile, olalele izwi lami walandela izindlela zami: Kuwe ngithi, qhubeka nokuthembeka kwakho kimi, futhi uma noma yimuphi kini enonyawo emnyango webandla laseLawodikeya, ngiyakuyala ukuba ulukhiphe emnyango futhi uvumele umnyango uvaleke! Ngokuba awukwazi ukwenza lokho iNkosi yakho engeke ikwenze! Aniyifundanga yini incwadi yami eya ebandleni laseLawodikeya eSambulo? Bheka, ngimi emnyango, ngingqongqoza: uma kukhona umuntu ozwa izwi lami, avule umnyango, ngiyongena kuye, ngidle naye, naye adle nami. Qaphelani, angishongo ukuthi ngizongena eBandleni laseLawodikeya, kodwa uma noma yimuphi umuntu ebandleni laseLawodikeya ezwa izwi lami futhi avule umnyango, ngiyongena ngidle naye, hhayi ibandla lakhe langaphambili elihlubukayo, nakuba ngibabiza nabo uma bezolalela kuphela. Makoti wami, inceku ayinkulu kunenkosi yayo, ngakho landela izindlela zami.

Makoti wami, sengicishe ngifike ukunihlwitha nginiyise emazulwini, ngakho qhubekani nokuzilungiselela futhi niqhubeke nokumemezela ukufika kwami. Ngokuba ngithume abaningi benu emsebenzini kaJohane uMbhapathizi ukumemezela ukufika kwami nokubiza ukuphenduka. Ngithume abaningi benu emsebenzini kaPetru ukwalusa nokondla umhlambi. Ngithume abanye benu njengabaphathi bemfihlakalo enkulu kaNkulunkulu, emsebenzini kaPawulu uMphostoli (nabanye abaphathi bemfihlakalo). Ngithume abaningi benu emsebenzini kaFiliphu uMvangeli, ngakho shumayelani izwi; phikelelani ngesikhathi nangaphandle kwesikhathi; solani, khuzani, yalanani ngokubekezela konke nemfundiso. Ngithume abanye benu emsebenzini womphostoli uJohane, owayengumfundisi futhi futhi enakekela umhlambi ngothando, ubumnene, nozwelo, eze elapha namanxeba abangelwe ezimvini zami ngomubi. Ningakungabazi, ngokuba nginithumile emisebenzini yabo; ngokuba isikhathi sesisondele, ngokuba sengizoshada noMakoti wami, ngokuba ngiyeza ngani, zithandwa zami! Qedani izifundo zenu, ngokuba ngiseduze. Isikhathi sesiphelile.

NginguJesu waseNazaretha.

Ngingulowo oyobusa esihlalweni sobukhosi sikaDavide, ngokuba ngizalwe kanjalo.

Ngiyadalula lobu bubi, nakuba uSathane efuna buhlale buyimfihlo.

Ngiyanixwayisa, nakuba abaningi benu bevilapha kakhulu ukwenza ngokwamazwi ami.

Ngiyeza, futhi ngiletha intukuthelo enkulu eBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo.

Ngihlanzekile nezingubo zami zimhlophe njengeqhwa elisha.

Ngilapha ukunitshela ukuthi aninaso isikhathi sokuchitha.

Ngiyabiza, ikakhulukazi wena, Bandla lami laseLawodikeya.

Sengicishe ngifike ukunihlwitha nginiyise emazulwini.

Sengizoshada noMakoti wami.

Ngiyeza ngani, zithandwa zami!

Ngiseduze futhi nginithanda kakhulu; nisesikhungweni senhliziyo yami.

Ngiyithemba nensindiso yenu, ngisho iNkosi yamakhosi neNkosi yamakhosi.
