Ubufakazi Obukabili: ISonto
Emisebenzini kaJohane uMbhapathizi
Mashi 25, 2024

Kholwani Kimi

Zwanini izwi lami, bantabami, nikholwe kimi, iNkosi uJesu Kristu. Nginibizele enkazimulweni nasebuhleni nokuba nime phambi kwami nihlanzekile ezindleleni zenu ngegazi lami lokuhlawulela, ngokuba igazi lewundlu lihlanza konke ukungcola. Nihlanjululiwe kimi. Yazini, bantabami, ukuthi akukho kulahlwa kulabo abakimi, okungukuthi, abakuKristu. Ukuhlangana nami nezinhloso zami kungukungenela enhlanganweni noBaba, uYahweh, nami, iNdodana yakhe uYeshua. NginguJehova onibizile ngamagama enu futhi onginike isimemo sokuphila okuphakade lapho kungeke kusaba khona ukuhlupheka, noma usizi, noma ukudabuka; ngokuba ngiyakwesula zonke izinyembezi emehlweni enu osindisweni lwami ngani. Ngingawuqala yini umsebenzi ngingawuqedi? UNkulunkulu wezulu nomhlaba angashiya yini okuthile kungenziwanga noma ehluleke ezithembisweni nasezwini lakhe? Anifundanga yini ukuthi akunakwenzeka ukuba uNkulunkulu aqambe amanga? Ngiqinisile, zonke izithembiso ziyogcwaliseka futhi zifezeke, ngakho-ke yazini ukuthi ukukhululwa kwenu kuyasondela â€” hhayi ukukhululwa okuyingxenye, kodwa ukukhululwa okuphelele kungashiywanga lutho lungenziwanga futhi kungekho lutho olungaphelele.

Zonke lezi zinto zigcwalisekile, futhi okungenakwenzeka kwenzekile kimi ngoba nganqoba ukufa ukuze ngingaphinde ngife. Yazini ukuthi wonke umsebenzi omubi uSathane azamile ukuwakha ezimpilweni zenu ngiyakuwuchitha. Yebo, ngiyakuchitha yonke imisebenzi kaDeveli; ngabonakaliswa ngale njongo. Ngininikile ukuphelela, futhi ngisazokuniphilisa ngokuphelele emizimbeni yenu eyonakele, futhi niyofana nami. Hlolani iZwi lami, zwanini amaZwi ami, niwaqhathanise nemiBhalo, futhi niyazi ukuthi ukukhululwa kwenu okuphelele kusondele njengokuphefumula kwenu okulandelayo futhi kuzokwenzeka ngokuphazima kweso. Ngimeleni niqine; ningikhulumele. Ningabi nakwenza nabakhonza izithombe nabaphingi abangene ngekhotha eSontweni lami nabazama ukudukisa abancane bami. Ulaka lwami luhleli phezu kwabo naphezu kwanoma ubani ohlasela abantu bami ukuze abadukise.

Ngoba akunakwenzeka ukuba nihlwitshwe esandleni sami, futhi labo engibabonise umusa yilabo abangenze iNkosi yabo ngokuvuma igama lami elinamandla, uJesu Kristu, njengeNkosi. Ngeke ngidanise, futhi akukangenanga enhliziyweni yomuntu izinto enizilindeleyo, njengoba ngizonihlwitha masinyane. Amagama amabi makangazikhathazi izinhliziyo zenu, bantabami, njengoba nisindisiwe olakeni lukaNkulunkulu, futhi abathembekileyo bami ngeke babekezelele isikhathi seminyaka eyisikhombisa solaka oseluzokhululwa. Izinhliziyo zenu mazibe nokuthula futhi ziphumule kimi, nasezinhluphekweni nasekushushisweni kwenu phumulani kimi, futhi ngizoninika ukuthula okungachazeki. Umusa wami nomusa wami kumi phakade, futhi ngikhokhe inani lezono zenu, ngakho-ke qhubekani niphuma kuzo. Ngiyabona isiyaluyalu enibhekana naso. Ngiyawuzwa onke amazwi anokhahlo akhulunywa kini nanganini. Ngaphezu kwalokho, ngiyaniqinisa ebuthakathakeni benu, futhi ngininikile umoya wami, futhi ninamandla futhi ninamandla kimi. Ngamandla enu uqobo niyokwehluleka, ngakho-ke phumulani emandleni ami, ngokuba uBaba ungiphakamisile ngaphezu kwakho konke.

Ngiyakuziphindiselela kwababi maduzane kakhulu, bantabami, ngakho-ke ningesabi. Lokhu kuphindisela akusona esenu, futhi akuhloselwe abantwana bakaNkulunkulu kodwa izitha zakhe ezone abantu kanye nabantu ababi ababulalayo nabaphanga umakhelwane nomfowabo.

Yebo, abantu abaningi ababi bahlangene noSathane ngenxa yenzuzo yobugovu, futhi banesabelo sabo nomvuzo echibini lomlilo elilungiselelwe uDeveli nezingelosi zakhe. Anisiwona yini nonke uhlanga olulodwa, isintu? Nokho, yazini ukuthi ulaka olukhulu seluzokhululwa futhi izulu selizokwambulwa emhlabeni. Zonke izenzo zilotshiwe. Lonke izwi eliyize elakhulunywa libhaliwe. Ayikho imfihlo engafihlwa kimi, futhi ayikho indawo yokucasha ekubukeni kwami. Nakuba ababi bemba imihubhe emhlabeni ukuze babalekele ulaka lwami olusondelayo, bayohlangabezana nokuphela kwabo bezama ukucasha kimi.

Ngiyakubuyisela, futhi ngiyakuziphindiselela ngimise ubulungiswa kwabonileyo. Nginenkemba ebukhali esika nhlangothi zombili ephuma emlonyeni wami, futhi akukho lutho futhi akekho ongangivimba, njengoba ngiyiNkosi yamakhosi neNkosi yababusi. Ubani onganihlukanisa nothando lukaNkulunkulu olukimi? Angikhokhelanga izono zenu futhi nganinika ukuphila okuphakade ukuze ngikhulule ulaka nokulahlwa phezu kwenu, abathembekileyo bami. Akukho lutho futhi akekho ongasihlukanisa, futhi maduze nizobheka uBaba wami nami ngamehlo enu. Nakuba nakhe ukwethemba nokukholwa okukhulu kimi, ningakaze ningibone ngamehlo enu, maduze nizobona, futhi nizokwazi njengoba naziwa. Ngubani lowo onikhathazayo? Ngeke yini aphelele ezeni nemisebenzi yakhe emibi ibuyiselwe kuye ngokuphindwe kabili? Abantu ababi bazibekelele ulaka olukhulu, yebo, ulaka olukhulu ngokuhlukumeza indalo kaBaba, futhi ngiyakubuyisela, isho iNkosi.

Yimani phambi kwami niqhubeke nizihlukanise naleli zwe elibi, elifayo futhi nivumele umsebenzi wami wokuhlawulela unigeze, unihlanze ngokuphelele. Bhekani! Ngenza konke kube kusha, nezono zenu nobubi benu ngeke ngisabukhumbula. Ngakho-ke phumani emisebenzini yenu nithathe imisebenzi yami, ngokuba ngokwenza kanjalo niyoletha ukuphelela emiphefumulweni yenu. Ningabesabi ababi! Ngokuba maduze imisebenzi yabo iyosakazeka njengothuli phambi komoya omkhulu, futhi ngiyokwenza konke kuhlanzeke kube kusha. Kukhona umvuzo omkhulu kwabathembekileyo bami nabakhethiweyo; futhi maduze, yebo, maduze kakhulu niyovuna umvuzo wemisebenzi yenu uma ningadangali.

Ngimeleni kuze kube sekupheleni, njengoba lowo olahlekelwa ukuphila kwakhe kimi uyothola ukuphila futhi uyovuna intokozo enkulu nensindiso nokuthokoza kuze kube phakade phambi kukaBaba. Nginenze amakhosi nabapristi phambi kukaBaba, njengoba ngingumpristi omkhulu waphakade ngokohlelo lukaMelkisedeki. Yimani niqine emuseni wami futhi niyazi ukuthi maduzane kakhulu umnyango uzovalwa, njengoba uNkulunkulu avalela uNowa nengcebo yakhe ngaphakathi koMkhumbi. Kanjalo nami ngiyonibuthela kimi emazulwini. Kukhona umnyango ovulekile okungekho muntu ongawuvula noma awuvale, futhi ngizonamukela kimi, Makoti wami. Ngakho-ke ngilindeleni njengoba isikhathi sesicishe siphelile.

Ngingulowo onibizele enkazimulweni, ebuhleni naseqinisweni.

NginguNkosi uJesu Kristu, iNdodana kaNkulunkulu eyabonakaliswa enyameni eyafelwa izono zenu.

Ngingulowo owavuswa ngosuku lwesithathu ukuze angafiyo futhi.

Ngingulowo uNkulunkulu amamukela emazulwini njengoba abaphostoli bami nabafundi bami bengibuka nginyukela emafwini.
