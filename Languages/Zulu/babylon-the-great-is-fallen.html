Ufakazi Ombaxa: Israyeli
Okubabayo Nokumnandi
Agasti 18, 2024

IBhabhiloni Elikhulu Liwile, Liwile!

Thobekisani izinhliziyo zenu emazwini ami nizwe inkulumo yami, nivumele amazwi ami angene ezinhliziyweni zenu: ngoba ngisho nabakholwayo phakathi kweBandla lami kanye noMlobokazi wami babhekana nezilingo zokuzithethelela nokungakholwa. Ngizweni kahle kakhulu, niboleke izindlebe zenu ukuze niqonde izikhathi eniphila kuzo, ngoba akekho kini owake waphila kulezi zigameko zamanje ezihlelele umhlaba ukwahlulelwa. Ngoba ukwahlulela nosizi sekukhona futhi sekuqalile, nakuba lokhu kuyimihelo nje yokuqala, engami manje, futhi ehlose ukuxwayisa labo abaphila benganakile. Kodwa, ngisho naphakathi kwabakhethiweyo nabakholwayo bami kukhona labo abangakhetha ngamabomu ubumpumputhe esikhundleni seqiniso, futhi bakhethe ngamabomu ukuzwa umsindo ongacacile esikhundleni semiyalo ecacile nenduduzo elethwa yiZwi leNkosi.

Ngoba mina, Yeshua HaMashiach, yena uJesu Kristu ngolimi lwesiNgisi, yimi engithumela lawa mazwi ngenceku yami engiyivusile ngayithuma, ngayifihla emgodleni wami isikhathi esinjengalesi. Futhi akumangalisi ngoba zinjalo izindlela zami, ngokuba mina Kristu Jesu, nginguye onamandla okuvusa abaphostoli, abaphrofethi, abavangeli, abefundisi nabafundisi ezixukwini ezingenazo izincwadi zaleli zwe eliwileyo, futhi abasibo abazihlanganise esivumelwaneni sobuhlanganyeli nezinceku zikaSathane uqobo ezibonakala ziyizinceku zokulunga. Futhi akumangalisi ngoba uSathane uqobo uziguqula abe yingelosi yokukhanya, ngakho-ke akuyona into enkulu ukuthi izinceku zakhe ziziguqule zibe sengathi ziyizinceku zokulunga, ngoba ngempela zifuna ukuhlanganisa bonke ngaphansi kwesibhengezo sazo sobuhlanganyeli somhlaba owodwa.

Futhi njengoba umngane wami uJohane uMbhapathizi asho eminyakeni eminingi edlule njengoba wayeqondisa izindlela engangizohamba kuzo: Ningacabangi ukuthi ningasho phakathi kwenu ukuthi, Sinobaba u-Abrahama; ngoba ngithi kini, uNkulunkulu unamandla okuvusa abantwana ku-Abrahama kulawa matshe. Futhi manje imbala izembe selibekiwe empandeni yemithi: ngakho-ke yonke imithi engatheli izithelo ezinhle iyanqunywa, iphonswe emlilweni.

Fundani niqonde, nicele ukuhlakanipha, bantwanyana bami, niwasebenzise lawa mazwi ezikhathini zenu. Ngoba ngisho ngokusobala kini ukuthi angithumanga abanamandla baleli zwe njengezithunywa nabaphrofethi bami, futhi angidingi izincwadi zezwe, oDkt lo naloya Mfundisi, amabizo abantu abazinika wona ukuze babonakale bengcwele. Ngoba umuntu owileyo uhlale efuna ukukhazimulisa inyama yakhe ezimpumelelweni zezwe, efuna ngisho nokuma elungile phambi kukaNkulunkulu uqobo enyameni eyonakele okungekho muntu ongayihlanza, ngaphandle kwangami Jesu waseNazaretha. Ngakho-ke ngiyabuza, yiziphi izincwadi zezwe engangiziphethe ngesikhathi senkonzo yami eJudiya, lapho ngangithunyelwe kuphela ezimvini ezilahlekileyo zendlu ka-Israyeli? Kwakungesilo yini isikhubekiso kwabangakholwayo phakathi kwabantu bakithi lapho bebuza ukuthi, kuvelaphi lokhu kuhlakanipha nemisebenzi emikhulu kulo muntu? Lo akasiyo indodana yombazi na?

Futhi njengoba kulotshiwe, kunjalo kugcwalisekile: Kepha uNkulunkulu wakhetha izinto zobuwula zomhlaba ukuba ajabhise abahlakaniphileyo; noNkulunkulu wakhetha izinto ezibuthakathaka zomhlaba ukuba ajabhise izinto ezinamandla; Nezinto eziphansi zomhlaba, nezinto ezidelelekileyo, uNkulunkulu uzikhethile, yebo, nezinto ezingekho, ukuba achithe izinto ezikhona: Ukuze kungabikho nyama ezozigqaja phambi kwakhe. Ngakho-ke yizwani amazwi ami okholo akhulunywa ezindebeni zabakholwayo bami futhi ngishilo ngaphambili, futhi manje ngiyaphinda ngithi abaphrofethi bami, abaningi, abaphikisani nemibhalo engcwele, futhi abaphikisani nami, uKristu Jesu iNkosi Eyenyukayo!

Qondani, nina bokholo oluncane, ukuthi abaphrofethi bami bakhuluma ngazwi linye, futhi bakhuluma amazwi engibanika wona. Anilizwa yini izwi leNkosi yenu? Amazwi ami angamanye yini kini, bantwanyana bami? Niyalesaba yini iqiniso? Ngoba mina ngiyiZwi likaNkulunkulu elenziwa inyama elihlala phakathi kwenu, futhi yimi enginiphile uMoya wami onamandla engiwuthululele phezu kwayo yonke inyama kulezi zikhathi zokugcina njengokwethembisa. Ngoba kulotshiwe: amadodana enu namadodakazi enu ayophendula, nezinsizwa zenu ziyobona imibono, namadoda enu amadala ayophupha amaphupho: Futhi phezu kwezinceku zami nezincekukazi zami ngiyothulula ngalezo zinsuku uMoya wami; futhi ziyophendula: Ngiyakubonisa izimangaliso ezulwini phezulu, nezibonakaliso emhlabeni phansi; igazi, nomlilo, nentuthu yomusi: Ilanga liyophenduka libe mnyama, nenyanga ibe yigazi, ngaphambi kokuba lufike usuku olukhulu noludumileyo lweNkosi.

Ngakho yizwani amazwi ami, namukele amazwi ami ngoba ayiqiniso futhi konke kuzogcwaliseka, ngisho nayo yonke incwadi encane kanye nalo lonke uphawu oluncane lwencwadi luzokwenzeka ngoba lonke iZwi likaNkulunkulu linikezelwe ukulalelwa futhi lonke iZwi likaNkulunkulu lizogcwaliseka ngokupheleleyo ngaphandle kokusilela, ngoba izindlela zikaYahweh ziphelele futhi noma yini abeka isandla sakhe ukuyifeza iveza ukuphelela okumangalisayo. Anizazi yini izindlela zikaYihlo wenu, bantwanyana bami?

Ngakho yizwani izwi lami, nina bami ababiziweyo abakholwayo nabakhethiweyo, ngisho iBandla lami kanye noMlobokazi wami! Nizoqhubeka nokuwashaya indiva amazwi ami futhi nifulathele izwi lami nivune amanga ezwe? Niyolandela yini izwi labawileyo njengoba nichitha izimpilo zenu nigqolozele isikrini ngokungenangqondo? Ngiyanincenga, yizwani izwi lami futhi ngithi kini, kungani izinhliziyo zenu zinihlukumeza? Aniqondi yini ukuthi yimi engihlezi ngiphethe isivunguzo ngesandla ngishoshozela amalangabi okuhlanza ukuze ngisuse insila ezinsimbini eziyigugu phakathi kwengcebo eminingi eyigugu kaYahweh?

Futhi kanjalo imisebenzi yezandla zami iveza ezinhliziyweni nasezimpilweni zenu zithandwa zami: ngoba yimi engisusa ukungakholwa, yimi engisusa izindlela zenu zokungathembeki, yimi onihlanza ngokupheleleyo kuyo yonke inhlobo yokonakala nokuphambuka kwenyama lapho niza kimi nivuma izono namaphutha enu ukuze ngisanihlanza ngokupheleleyo ngegazi lami lokuhlawulela ukuze nime ningenasici phambi kwesihlalo sobukhosi sikaYahweh lapho ngizonethula ngokupheleleyo kuBaba mathupha esikhathini senu esiseduze kakhulu.

Kodwa, abaningi benu bafuna ukubuyela ezimpilweni zenu zakudala lapho ngingakayenzi intando kaYahweh ngokwesikhathi nokulindela kwenu. Ngoba kunjalo, ngisho nabafundi nabaphostoli bami abaseduze babuyela ekudobeni ngemva kokubethelwa nokuvuka kwami, kodwa ngabenza abadobi babantu. Futhi kanjalo ngiza ukunibuthela futhi emsindweni wezwi lami, futhi aniziqondi ngempela izingozi ezinikakile kuleli zwe elibi lamanje. Ngakho-ke nginibizela kimi kulezi zikhathi zokugcina ukuze nize ngokupheleleyo kimi niphume ngokupheleleyo esonweni nasekungakholweni, ngoba yimi onibizele ebuhleni naseqinisweni, nakuba abaningi benu besathanda ukubuyela eGibhithe.

Ngakho-ke ngineluleka ukuba nithathe izifundo ezilotshwe kudala enhliziyweni ezinexwayiso nokuhlakanipha kwabahlakaniphileyo. Ngoba bathi kuMose: Ngenxa yokuthi kwakungekho mathuna eGibhithe, usikhiphele ukuba sif_ele ehlane na? Kungani usiphathe kanje, usikhiphe eGibhithe? Kungani nisalangazelela izinsuku zakudala? Nisalokothwe yini ukuphila ezintokozweni zesono? Kungani nifuna ukuhlala phakathi kwezintombi ezingahlakaniphile lapho ngimi phezu kwenu nginilulela isandla sokunibuyisela ezinyaweni zenu, ngoba niwile ninganakile futhi nisesothulini lomhlaba, ngoba uthuli luyoba ukudla kwenyoka. Ngakho vukani nina abalele nizithintithe uthuli lomhlaba, nivuke ngenkazimulo yasezulwini, nibe nokholo emazwini ami okholo abakholwayo abawakhulume ngezindebe zokholo!

Ngakho-ke qondani lokho okuqhubekayo manje, niqonde nezikhathi eniphila kuzo, ngoba abaningi bazivumel_ele ukuba balaliswe futhi bakholwa amazwi kaSathane angenakholo njengoba ehleb_ezela amanga amnandi anganengqondo angakhohlisa ngisho nabakhethiweyo uma bekhetha ukukholwa amanga akhe. Qondani ukuthi uSathane, yena udeveli okhohlisa umhlaba wonke, manje uphonswe phansi ezulwini futhi uboshelwe emhlabeni ukuze ahlulelwe ngokushesha okungabambeki.

Ngakho-ke kukhona ukuma kancane lapho abawileyo bavunyelwe ukuhambisa izingcezu zabo endaweni ebhodini lomhlaba le-chess, nokuma kancane lapho imiphefumulo yokugcina manje igibela uMkhumbi wokuphila okuphakade kimi uKristu Jesu iNkosi noMsindisi wabo ovukileyo. Nokho, abaningi benu ekucabangeni kwenu okungenakholo baphetha ngokuthi amazwi ami angamanga futhi ngobuwula baphetha ngokuthi ninesikhathi sokubuyela ezimpilweni zenu ezindala zesono nenjabulo. Ngaphandle kwalokho, ngokuzumayo, abakholwayo bayothathwa bayiswe ekuphepheni esihlalweni sobukhosi sikaYahweh uqobo ngomvuzo omkhulu nefa laphakade elikhazimulayo, futhi abakholwayo bayoguqulwa bonke kuze kube phakade.

Nizohlala phakathi kwabakholwayo yini? Ninakho yini ukuthobeka kokubona ukungathembeki kwenu siqu niphenduke ukuze nijoyine izinhlu zabakholwayo? Uma nikhetha ukubona, mina, Kristu Jesu, ngizonibonisa. Ngakho kungani nidangala ekwenzeni okuhle? Sinqumile yini ukuthi labo enginiletha kubo nalabo engibaletha kini abafanele ukuphila okuphakade? Ngakho-ke ngineluleka ukuba nibonise isihawu nomusa ofanayo kumuntu kini enikanye naye engiwubonisile kini kuleli khefu lokugcina ngaphambi kokuba kufike imbubhiso. Futhi ngokwenza kanjalo niyosindisa abaningi, ngoba nisengamanxusa ami. Ngakho-ke vulani imilomo yenu ngokholo, ningangabazi lutho, futhi ngizoniphefumulela amazwi okukhuluma.

Ngakho-ke abantwanyana bami ngisho nabakholwayo bami baya ngokungahlaliseki nokungabekezeli, ngisho nakuleli khefu lokugcina ngaphambi kokuba iBandla noMlobokazi wami bathathwe bayiswe emazulwini. Kodwa, njengoba kwakunjalo ehlane lapho uMose ehlangana noYahweh eSinayi, abantwana bavuka badla, baphuza, badlala, bazenzela ithole legolide ukuze balikhonze esikhundleni sikaNkulunkulu ophilayo, ubukhona bakhe ababengavunyelwe kodwa bema eduze babona inkazimulo yobukhona bukaNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade eSinayi! Futhi ngendlela efanayo njengoba uMose wancengela abantwana, kanjalo nami ngiyancengela bonke abayizindlalifa zensindiso yaphakade, ukuze bonke ababiziweyo bafike ngokupheleleyo ensindisweni nasekuphendukeni ezindleleni zabo ezimbi nezingenakholo. Buyelani ezwini lami nihlale ezikhungweni engininike zona, futhi ngizonivikela ekwahlulelweni okungesikho okwenu. Angishongo yini kini ukuthi phumani phakathi kwabo nihlukane nabo? Lalelani izwi lami!

Ngakho-ke ukwahlulela manje kwehlela emhlabeni futhi ukwahlulela kulungiselwe kusukela kudala abangakholwayo nabanenkani, ngisho nalabo ababambelela ngokuzithandela noma ngokungazi ekuhlubukeni kukaSathane. Ngoba abantu abaningi, abaningi, abaningi bayokhetha ngokuzithandela amalangabi ahloselwe udeveli nezingelosi zakhe esikhundleni sokusuka esonweni beze ekuphileni okuphakade ngokubiza uJesu waseNazaretha ukuze basindiswe. Futhi kuyoba njalo, ngoba u-El Shaddai, uNkulunkulu uSomandla uqobo, unikeze umuntu inkululeko yokuzikhethela ukuze umuntu akhethe ukuphila ngokulunga nangokuthula phambi kukaYahweh, noma akhethe ukubhujiswa kwaphakade kulelo chibi elikhulu lomlilo oluhlaza okwesibhakabhaka elenzelwe abawileyo lapho isibungu sabo singafi khona. Futhi izikhathi zokwahlulela yimanje.

Ngakho-ke ngitshela bonke abayozwa ngezigameko ezizokwenzeka ngaphambi kokuba zenzeke. Lezi zigameko azisithekile kodwa zimenyezelwe ngabaphrofethi nezinceku zami iminyaka eminingi manje, ngisho nabo bonke engibathumele eBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo. Ngoba izikhathi zokwahlulelwa kwalowo nkosikazi omkhulu sezifikile, futhi isilo esimthweleyo manje sizombhubhisa ngokupheleleyo. Ngoba uNkulunkulu ukufakile ezinhliziyweni zezimpondo eziyishumi ukugcwalisa intando yakhe, futhi bayamzonda unkosikazi, futhi bazoqhubeka nokumenza incithakalo futhi bamhlubule, badle inyama yakhe, baqhubeke nokumshisa ngomlilo. Futhi kanjalo iBhabhiloni eliyimfihlo liyokwenziwa incithakalo ngokupheleleyo futhi lidalulwe ngamahloni ukuze kubone wonke umuntu.

Amadolobha akhe ayekade emakhulu ayeyizibonelo zokusebenza kanzima nokukhiqiza nenala ka-El Shaddai manje ahlala emanxiweni nenzalo yakhe igxile ezidakamizweni iphila ekungcoleni emigwaqweni yakhe enengekayo. Futhi uhluts_uliwe njengoba izivikelo zakhe zisuswa kakhulu futhi ingcebo namandla akhe kunikezwa ezandleni zezitha zakhe zakwa-Edomi ezenza sengathi zingabangane. Unkosikazi omkhulu owalwa nazo zonke izizwe ayethunyelwe ukuba azinqobe manje useboshiwe, akasavikelekile futhi ulungele ukunqotshwa ngokupheleleyo. Futhi yonke inyama iyodliwa usizi futhi bonke abathunjiweyo bayobulawa futhi labo abakwazi ukusebenzela izinjongo zesilo bayothunjelwa ebugqilini kanti abanye bayobulawa, nakuba insali iyosinda. Futhi uyavutha futhi kunemililo eminingi vele emingceleni yakhe nasemazweni omakhelwane bakhe enyakatho abahlanganyela ezinhluphekweni zakhe. Kodwa lokhu kuyimihelo nje yokuqala futhi akusilo ukwahlulela kokugcina kodwa kuyisixwayiso kubo bonke ngokubhujiswa kwabo okuzayo. Ngoba kuyokwenzeka ngokuzumayo, futhi kuyokwenzeka ngokuphazima kweso. Kuyokwenzeka kuphela futhi ngisho nalapho ngihlw_itha abakholwayo bami emhlabeni ngibayise ekuphepheni futhi ngiyobutha ngokuzumayo ukolweni kaYahweh esibayeni sakhe saphakade, ngoba izikhathi zokuvuna sezifikile. Nokho akusilo isivuno sokugcina, kodwa esokuqala kweziningana, futhi kanjalo ukwahlulela kuqala endlini kaNkulunkulu.

Bhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo, izikhathi zakho sezifikile, nezinsuku zakho seziphelile. Mina, Jesu waseNazaretha, ngikhal_e izinyembezi ezingenakubalwa ngokubhujiswa kwakho okuzayo futhi ngithumele izibonakaliso phezulu, nezimangaliso esibhakabhakeni, nezibonakaliso emhlabeni kanye nezinhlekelele eziningi ezenzeka ngesikhathi sangempela naphakathi kwakhe kodwa iBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo lisavala izindlebe zalo ngokuhlubuka ngenkani, futhi manje selizodla ngokugcwele izindlela zalo zokuhlubuka. Ngakho-ke qondani, ukuthi njengoba ngibutha ngokuzumayo abakholwayo ngibayise ekuphepheni nasemvuzweni, kanjalo nokubhujiswa okuzumayo kuzokwehlela iBhabhiloni eyimfihlakalo njengoba umvimbeli esuswa ngokupheleleyo. Yizwani amazwi ami niqonde izwi lami ngoba umvimbeli ukuphela kwento egcina izinkanuko ezibhubhisayo zabawileyo ziboshiwe kuze kube yilapho lowo ovimbayo engasavimbi futhi esuswa phakathi.

Khona-ke ukubhujiswa okuzumayo kuzofika phezu komhlaba wonke. Imigqa yempi isidwetshiwe, futhi sebezilungiselele impi yabo yezwe yesithathu, ngisho nempi yabo yokuketula ngokupheleleyo izindlela zakudala nezinhlelo zakudala. Futhi kanjalo umlilo uzowela ngokuzumayo phezu kweBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo futhi amadolobha alo ayobhujiswa. Umlilo uzowela ngokuzumayo njengoba imicibisholo iwa emazweni alo, uwushiye ushile futhi uhluts_uliwe. Nokho amadolobha alo amaningi ogwini lwalo olunesono ayowela emanzini azoza ngokuzumayo futhi ashaye ogwini lwalo akhukhule angene phakathi nezwe amamayela amaningi, futhi lokhu kuzokwenzeka ngasempumalanga nangasentshonalanga. Umhlaba uzozamazama ngokuzumayo ngokungalawuleki, futhi ukubhujiswa okukhulu kuzofika emazweni eBhabhiloni eliyimfihlo, futhi amazwe ayokonakaliswa futhi aguqulwe. Amabhuloho alo azowa, nemigwaqo yalo ngeke isahambeka, futhi kuzosala ihlane nogwadule. Iphunga lokufa lizomboza amazwe alo futhi isifo nokugula kuzogcwala emazweni alo futhi liyobe lingenakuzisiza neze.

Futhi njengoba lokhu kuhlasela kokugcina kwenzeka, izixuku zeButho Elibomvu ziyokhululwa ngokupheleleyo phezu kwabakhileyo abasele bezwe. Ziyobulala bonke abamelanayo futhi zibulale abaningi abangamelani. Ngoba abantu baseBhabhiloni eliyimfihlo bathengiselwe ebugqilini ngezikweletu ezinkulu elinazo, ngoba ngenzalo abawileyo basebenzela ukubhubhisa izixuku. Futhi njengoba abantu balo bethembise futhi basayina izimpilo zabo ngenxa yenzuzo yezinto ezibonakalayo nezintokozo zesono, kanjalo nalabo abakhetha ukubagqilaza bayosebenzela labo abanezikweletu kubo emsebenzini ophoqelelwe futhi uphawu luzophoqelelwa kubo bonke abasindayo, kodwa ningaluthathi uphawu.

Ngalokho izwe elikhona manje, liyosheshe lithathelwe indawo umlotha wamanxiwa nezinsalela ezishunqayo zomphakathi owake wachuma. Bese indlala yehlela emazweni, ngokuba iBhabhiloni eliyimfihlo lilethe indlala emazweni amaningi, manje-ke indlala iyoba isimenywa salo sasemini, ngokuba linikile kanjalo, kanjalo liyokwamukela ngokuphindwe kabili. Amanzi ayoba yimpahla efunwayo, futhi amanzi amaningi ayokwenziwa ababa ngenxa yokubhujiswa nemililo yempi nangenxa yezifo zabangangcwatshiwe abayongcolisa izwe. Abaningi bayohlupheka ngokungenasidingo bafe ekwahlulelweni ngenxa yezono zabo, lapho into enjalo ibingadingeki kubo kodwa labo abasala phakathi kwabahlulelwayo bayokwahlulelwa nabo, nokho noma yibaphi nabobonke abakhuleka egameni leNkosi yabo kuze kube sekupheleni bayosindiswa, kodwa kuyodingeka bakhuthazele kuze kube sekuphefumuleni kwabo kokugcina. Ngokuba ukwethembeka kuze kube sekupheleni bekulokhu kuyimfuneko kwabakholwayo, ngisho nabantwana baka-Abrahama abangabakaYahweh uqobo abangabantwana besithembiso, ngokuba olungileyo uyophila ngokukholwa.

Ngalokho iBhabhiloni eliyimfihlo liyodabuka phakathi nendawo, amanzi agijimele lapho kwake kwaba khona umhlaba owomile ovundile, ngisho nezwe elingumongo liyokhukhuleka enkabeni. Kanjalo-ke umusa nomusa kaNkulunkulu kuyosuswa ngokuphelele emhlabeni lapho umvimbeli esuswa ngokuzuma, futhi incithakalo iyoshada nencithakalo, ngokuba lezi yizikhathi zencithakalo lapho umbuso kaSathane uyokwahlulelwa ngokugcwele futhi ubhujiswe ngokugcwele, kodwa kuqala bayosebenzela izinhloso zikaYahweh ekudleni ngokuphelele iBhabhiloni eliyimfihlakalo, kanjalo-ke liyoshiswa ngokuphelele futhi liqedwe emililweni eminingi evuthayo eyoligwinya iqale ukuhlanza izwe. Ngalokho incithakalo yeBhabhiloni eliyimfihlo iyokwenzeka ngehora elilodwa, ngokuba njengoba kulotshiwe kanjalo kuyokwenziwa, futhi liyokwamukela isabelo esiphindwe kabili sokwahlulelwa. Futhi njengoba kulotshiwe, kanjalo kuyokwenziwa.

Ngokuba kulotshiwe kusukela endulo nguJohane uMambuli: Ngabona enye iNgelosi yehla ivela ezulwini, inamandla amakhulu, kangangokuthi umhlaba wakhanyiswa ngenkazimulo yayo, Futhi yamemeza kakhulu ngezwi elikhulu, ithi, Liwile, liwile, iBhabhiloni lowo muzi omkhulu, futhi seliyindawo yokuhlala amademoni, nenqaba yayo yonke imimoya engcolileyo, nekheji layo yonke inyoni engcolileyo nenzondekayo. Ngokuba zonke izizwe ziphuze iwayini lolaka lokufeba kwalo, futhi amakhosi omhlaba afebile nalo, nabathengisi bomhlaba bacebile ngenxa yobuningi benjabulo yalo.

Futhi ngezwa elinye izwi livela ezulwini lithi, phumani kulo, bantu bami, ukuze ningabi nabahlanganyeli ezonweni zalo, futhi ningamukeli ezifweni zalo. Ngokuba izono zalo sezifinyelele ezulwini, futhi uNkulunkulu ukhumbule ukungalungi kwalo. Liphindiseleni, njengoba nalo liniphindisele, futhi linike ngokuphindwe kabili ngokwemisebenzi yalo: nasenkezweni elinigcwalisele yona, gcwalisani eyalo ngokuphindwe kabili. Njengoba nje lizidumisa, laphila ngenjabulo, kanjalo-ke linike ukuhlukunyezwa nosizi: ngokuba lithi enhliziyweni yalo, Ngihlezi ngiyindlovukazi, angisiye umfelokazi, futhi angisayikubona ukulila. Ngakho-ke, izifo zalo ziyofika ngosuku olulodwa, ukufa, nosizi, nendlala, futhi liyoshiswa ngomlilo: ngokuba uNkulunkulu olilahlayo, uyiNkosi enamandla!

Ngalokho lesi sizwe esake saba sikhulu sezifundazwe nemizi eminingi siyokuwa ngokuzuma, futhi ingelosi yencithakalo okwamanje indiza phezu komuzi waso omkhulu kunayo yonke emanzini asempumalanga nokuziqhenya nenjabulo yaso nomona wezwe kuyobhujiswa ngokuphelele kungaphinde kuvuke. Kanjalo-ke incithakalo yaso iyokwabelwana yibo bonke abahlanganyele naso, ngokuba siyileso sifebe esikhulu esihlezi phezu kwamanzi amaningi angabantu nezixuku nezizwe nezilimi.

Ngokuba ukwahlulelwa kwabo akulibali, futhi onke amakhosi omhlaba ajoyine izimpondo eziyishumi ekuhlaseleni kwawo okungalindelekile. Ngokuba abaholi bawo bakhonza ngokuzithandela labo abawileyo, ngokuba banjalo bonke ababusi bezwe. Akekho olungileyo, cha, ngisho noyedwa. Laba baholi basebenze iminyaka eminingi ukuletha lokhu kuhlasela futhi bakhashele izizwe zabo abathunyelwe ukuzikhohlisa. Futhi abantu ababalandela ngokuzithandela enjabulweni bayowela emlilweni wempi, isifo, ukufa nencithakalo. Futhi lezi zinto sezilungele ukukhululwa ngokuzuma ngokugcwele emhlabeni. Kanjalo zonke izizwe ezibizwa ngokuthi ezobuKristu ziyokuwa ngenkemba njengoba ukwahlulelwa kuqala endlini kaNkulunkulu. Futhi njengoba kwakunjalo ezinsukwini zasendulo lapho u-Israyeli eshiya uYise wezinkanyiso wakhonza abanye onkulunkulu, abangeyena onkulunkulu ngemvelo, kanjalo nalaba bayonikelwa ezandleni zezitha zabo, njengoba nje izizwe eziyishumi nambili zahlukaniswa futhi zanqotshwa endulo ngenxa yokukhaphela kwazo izivumelwano futhi kwalethwa isiqalekiso somthetho kwabangalaleli nabanenhliziyo elukhuni.

Futhi emlotheni wempi ephelele nencithakalo kuyovela uhlelo lokugcina lwesilo, ngokuba udrako useyalungiselela uhlelo lwesilo ukuba luvele, ngisho nalabo abajoyina amakhosi ayishumi ayonikeza amandla awo aphelele esilweni esivela olwandle nesilo esivela emhlabeni esingumprofethi wabo wamanga noMoshiach wamanga. Isilo esivela olwandle siyovela njengoMahdi wabo, futhi kuso, kunikezwe amandla okulwa nabangcwele nokubanqoba. Kanjalo-ke isilo esivela emhlabeni sabelana ngamandla afanayo nesilo esivela olwandle, futhi yiso oluyiMpondo Encane eyaprofethwa endulo nguDaniyeli.

Ngalokho lezi zilo ezimbili ziyonikezwa amandla ngokugcwele yilowo drako omkhulu obomvu futhi ziyovela ngokugcwele futhi zinqobe ziqhubekele phambili ekunqobeni, futhi ziyothi: Ubani onjengesilo? Futhi ubani okwazi ukulwa nesilo? Kanjalo-ke bonke bayodingeka ukuba bathathe uphawu lwaso abangahlanganyela naso emphakathini waso kasathane. Futhi ubuhlanya buyogcwala umhlaba wonke, njengoba umvimbeli engeke esaba khona ukuze ahlulele isintu ngesono futhi avimbele izinkanuko zaso nezifiso ezimbi. Kanjalo-ke ububi obugcwele, obungavinjelwe buzokhululwa emhlabeni futhi kwalasha kuyovulwa ngokugcwele, ngokuba abakhileyo emhlabeni bamlahle uYahweh, ngokuba bangilahlile mina, uJesu waseNazaretha othunyelwe ukusindiswa kwabo. Kodwa bazibale njengabangafanele ukuphila okuphakade futhi bamlahla uYahweh lapho uYahweh eza kubo ngothando, ubumnene, umusa, nesihawu futhi wanikeza isivumelwano sokuthula saphakade esintwini. Isivumelwano sokuthula asebesilahle manje. Nokho, noma ubani okhuleka egameni leNkosi kuze kube sekupheleni uyosindiswa.

Futhi nakuba izikhathi zokwahlulelwa, incithakalo enkulu, impi, indlala, isifo nokunye okunjalo kuyokhululwa ngokugcwele emhlabeni, leli sonto lokugcina ngisho nesonto lamashumi ayisikhombisa likaDaniyeli linikezelwe ukusindiswa kukaJakobe futhi mina, uYeshua HaMashiach, ngisazosindisa insali kwa-Israyeli eyongamukela njengoMesiya wabo weqiniso nofanele, ngokuba ngadalwa kanjalo, ngenziwa, futhi ngathunyelwa nguNkulunkulu uSomandla ngokuhlonipha izithembiso ezenziwa esivumelwaneni nasetestamenteni koyise.

Ngokuba uYahweh unguNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade ohlezi phezulu futhi manje uzosuka aziveze endalweni yakhe nami, uKristu Jesu, ngiyiNkosi yamakhosi neNkosi yamakhosi yombuso wayo ongenakuphela. Futhi ngizothumela abaprofethi bami ababili, ngisho nezinti zami ezimbili zezibani eziyoprofetha, futhi ngizothumela abayizi-144,000 bami ukusindisa labo abathunyelwe kubo ehlane endaweni ekhethwe nguYahweh futhi bayondliwa futhi banakekelwe nguYahweh. Futhi uNkulunkulu waphakade wamemezela kanjalo endulo. Ngokuba nguYahweh okhuluma ngezinto ezisazokwenzeka sengathi sezenzekile kakade, ngokuba zinjalo izindlela zikaNkulunkulu waphakade onginike iVangeli laphakade ukuba limenyezelwe phezu komhlaba ukuze insindiso yaphakade ifike kubo bonke abayobiza igama lami elinamandla, laphakade.

Mina Yeshua HaMashiach, ngisho noKristu Jesu, ngithumele la mazwi ngenceku yami futhi ngenceku yami la mazwi athunyelwe.
