Novemba 7, 2025

Ukwahlulela Okunjengomlilo KukaHeylel Ben Shachar

Yizwani, yizwani amazwi ka-Adonai-YHVH futhi nisondele ekuzweni kwezwi lami, ukuze nihlale ezinyaweni zami, nina bantwana bami, nizwe amazwi ami engiwakhulula ngomoya wesithunywa sami esinamandla nenceku yami. Amazwi anikezwe ngegunya elikhulu eNdodaneni yami ezelwe yodwa, eyavala la mazwi anamandla esithunyweni nasencekwini yethu, engayinikeza uKristu Jesu njengesitsha esikhethiweyo, ukuba ihlale ngakwesobunxele sakhe odumweni oluphindwe kabili, njengomalusi onothando novikelayo, ogcwele uzwelo ngoba ukhuliswe nguMalusi oMkhulu, onguYeshua HaMashiach, futhi wagcwala uthando lwami ngomusa ohlala njalo nomusa onikiweyo ngeWundlu lika-Adonai-YHVH othandweni lwakhe, lapho egoqe futhi wakhanda khona uBiblaridon:144 uqobo, njengoba nje ekufezile ngokugcwele kubo bonke abayizi-144,000 abanamandla, abangabantwana bakaYeshua HaMashiach uqobo abakhulise ngesandla sakhe, futhi akukho ukukhetha ubuso babantu ezindleleni zika-Adonai-YHVH, futhi uYeshua HaMashiach uyiZwi lika-Adonai-YHVH elenziwe inyama. Ngakho-ke, qondani nonke nina eniyizi-144,000: Nina ningabantwana bakaBaba uYeshua, futhi mina ngingu-Adonai-YHVH wenu onguBaba kini nonke kuKristu Jesu. Selah.

Ngakho-ke, qondani ukuphatha kwami okuphakade nobukhosi bami ukuthi mina nginguye okwazi ukuvusa amaqhawe anamandla ukuze uYeshua HaMashiach awayale, omi manje ngegunya eligcwele futhi onikwe amandla ngokugcwele futhi wathunyelwa ukuba ahlenge abathembekileyo kuKristu Jesu njengeNgonyama yesizwe sakwaJuda ephuma phakathi kwabo, ebangela ukubhujiswa okungazelelwe lapho ekhulula ihora elilodwa nesilo ekuhambeni kwenu (kungazelelwe kanjalo) ngaleso sikhathi esinikezwe ngokugcwele ezandleni zakhe ukuba akugcwalise ngokulalela okulungileyo nokujabulisayo, futhi nguYeshua HaMashiach ohlale enza intando ka-Adonai-YHVH wakhe.

Ngokunjalo, uBiblaridion:144 naye uyisikhali esinamandla nesinezinhlangothi eziningi esandleni sikaYeshua HaMashiach, ohambisana ngokugcwele nezinjongo zakhe njengoba zembuliwe kuze kube manje naphakade, futhi ongumenzeli wokwahlulela okumenyezelwayo nokwenziwayo, ozosetshenziswa ngokuyalelwa ukuba ashaye, futhi ogunyaziwe ukuba amemezele futhi enze ngqo njengoba kumenyezelwe ngaphansi kwegunya eligcwele leNgonyama yesizwe sakwaJuda. Ngeqiniso, uBiblaridon uyinkemba efakwe emgodleni ephethwe esandleni sikaYeshua HaMashiach, ezohosulwa ngokugcwele phakathi nesonto lamashumi ayisikhombisa likaDaniyeli, kanye nangezinjongo zami zaphakade ezisazokwembulwa lapho nonke senibuthelwe kimi ngokuphazima kweso, lapho ngizokwembula kini ubuntu benu beqiniso nezinjongo zenu ezifihliwe manje kuKristu Jesu, kodwa manje ezembulwe kancane.

Qondani bantwana bami ukuthi bonke abathembekileyo kuKristu Jesu, abayibandla lakhe, umzimba wakhe, kanye nomlobokazi wakhe, bakhethwe ngokukhethekile kuKristu Jesu ngaphambi kokusekelwa komhlaba. Nakuba abaningi behumusha la mazwi ngemishwana engenangqondo efana nokuthi "bathandwa kakhulu futhi babusisekile ngempela" ukuze baduduze inyama lapho kushiwo ngaphandle kokholo nokuqonda amaqiniso ami aphakade, akuyona leyo inhloso ephelele, kodwa kuyiqiniso eliqinisekile kwabathembekileyo kuKristu ngenxa yokulalela nokuphenduka kwabo.

Qondani, umusho othi "bakhethwa kuye ngaphambi kokusekelwa komhlaba" ukhombisa ukuthi u-Adonai-YHVH wenu unikhethe mathupha futhi wahola ngamandla agcwele entando nangomklamo onenjongo ephelele, ngaphandle kokwephula intando ekhululekile yanoma yimuphi umuntu nakancane, ngokukhetha ukubumba, ukwenza, nokudala kabusha kuKristu Jesu ngamunye wenu ngokuqonda okugcwele nobuchule obugcwele bobuciko nomklamo ngokunaka okumangalisayo imininingwane kumuntu ngamunye wenu, okuthathe sonke isikhathi kusukela ku-Adamu no-Eva kuze kube manje ukufeza ngokunemba kwami.

Nina niyimiklamo yami yobuciko edalwe kuKristu!

Lobo buciko, ukunemba, nomklamo kuhlwanyelwe ezakhini zenu zofuzo lapho ngikhethe khona ngeqiniso ngekhono elikhulu nobuciko yonke imininingwane yenu nonke ngezinjongo zami zaphakade ezizokwembulwa ngokugcwele esihlalweni sami sobukhosi, ngokushesha nangamandla ngomyalo wami. Ngakho-ke, qondani ukuthi nginibumbile, nganenza, futhi nganidala ngamunye wenu ngokukhethekile nangemininingwane emincane kangangokuthi aninakukucabanga lokho enizoguqulwa nibe yikho ngokuya ngezinjongo zami ezihlukile kakhulu nezikhethiweyo kumuntu ngamunye wenu.

Ngakho-ke, ningesabi, u-Adonai-YHVH wenu akakhethi ukwenza isidalwa esisodwa sihluke kwesinye ukuze inzuzo ecatshangwayo igujwe esonweni sokuzigqaja, noma ukuba isidalwa esisodwa sibuse phezu kwesinye ngodlame lwesono nenzondo, kodwa ukuze izinjongo zami zaphakade zigcwaliseke kini nonke. Ngeqiniso laphakade: Akukakangeni ngokugcwele enhliziyweni yomuntu ngesikhathi sokulotshwa kwalokhu lokho enizodalwa kabusha nibe yikho nangezinjongo ezithile. Ngakho-ke, ngiyanicela othandweni lwami ngani (nina enidalwe futhi nakhethwa ngenxa yokholo lwenu, bantwana bami abaphenduka ngokwethembeka) ukuba nibe nokholo olungangohlamvu lwesinaphi futhi niqonde ukuthi kusukela komncane kuya komkhulu phakathi kuka-Israyeli ka-Adonai-YHVH, nonke nenziwe ngokuphelele nangokumangalisayo futhi naklanywa futhi nakhandwa ngamandla amakhulu kangangokuthi ngu-Israyeli ka-Adonai-YHVH ozonconywa yibo bonke.

Ukuthula makube kini ngokugcwele.

Futhi lokhu ngaphandle kokukhetha ubuso babantu, kodwa kunalokho ngolwazi lwami olulungileyo noluphakade nolunamandla onke nokulawula okunembile kwazo zonke izinto lapho ngikhethe khona ngamunye wenu ngale ndlela ngezinjongo zami zaphakade kuKristu Jesu. Ngoba ngangazi ngokugcwele emaphakadeni adlule ukuthi ngamunye wenu, uzoza ngokugcwele kuKristu Jesu ngezikhathi zenu, ngakho ngalungiselela. Ngakho-ke, qondani ukuthi nakuba izimfihlo ezifihliweyo zembulwa ngobufakazi nesibonelo senkonzo kuBiblaridon:144, lezi zinto zenziwa ukuze nonke niqale ukuqonda ubukhulu befa lenu nezinjongo zaphakade lapho nganidala, nganikhetha, futhi nganenza khona nonke ngomklamo ohlakaniphile kuze kube semininingwaneni emincane kakhulu. Thokozani!

Ngakho-ke, yizani ngokugcwele kuYeshua HaMashiach, nani nonke enithembekile kuKristu Jesu, ukuze namukele umvuzo wenu ophindwe kabili ogcwele odumweni oluphelele, anawo esandleni awunikwe yimi kubo bonke abanqobayo ngendlela efanayo uKristu Jesu anqoba ngayo. Yizwani amazwi ami esikhuthazo nani nonke eniyizi-144,000, eningabantwana abatholwe nguYihlo uYeshua, futhi uzokwabelana nani zonke izinto bantwana bakhe, abangabanqobi bakhe abathembekile, abanqobe zonke izinto njengoba noYeshua HaMashiach anqoba. Nokho, laba akubona bodwa abanqobi abayonqoba zonke izinto ezimelene nabo, ngoba baningi, baningi phakathi kwabathembekileyo kuKristu Jesu abangeyona ingxenye yezi-144,000 abanqobe zonke izinto ngendlela efanayo noKristu Jesu, futhi mkhulu umvuzo wabo!

Yebo! Baningi abanqobi phakathi kuka-Israyeli ka-Adonai-YHVH futhi akukho ukukhetha ubuso babantu ku-Adonai-YHVH, futhi ikakhulukazi kuYeshua HaMashiach ohlale enza intando ka-Adonai-YHVH wakhe ngokuphelele. Ngakho-ke, ningakhathazeki futhi ningakhathazeki njengoba nizama ukuqonda okufihliwe eMazulwini okungakangeni ngokugcwele enhliziyweni yomuntu ngalesi sikhathi, futhi ikakhulukazi gwemani ukuziqhathanisa nomunye nomunye njengoba kuvamile esimweni senu samanje esiwile, kodwa esingcweliswe ngokugcwele kuKristu Jesu.

Ngakho qondani, eMbusweni waseZulwini, noma ubani ophakanyiswa ngaphezu komunye, kwenziwa ngegunya, kanye/noma ngomvuzo ngezinjongo zami kuphela, kodwa hhayi ukubakhazimulisa ukuze omunye ekudalweni kwami azizwe ephansi, noma ukuze labo abangase baphakanyiswe bazigqaje esonweni phezu komunye. Kodwa kunalokho, udumo nothando kunikezwa kini nonke ngokugcwele. Ngoba nganidala nonke, futhi ikakhulukazi abathembekile kuKristu Jesu ngenxa yokulalela kwenu kuKristu Jesu ukuze nginidale kabusha ngodumo olugcwele. Nina, ikakhulukazi, ningumsebenzi wami wobuciko odalwe kuKristu Jesu ukuze nenze imisebenzi emihle okufanele nihambe kuyo ngamandla amakhulu ngezinjongo zaphakade zika-Adonai-YHVH wenu, ezinikezwe kuKristu Jesu. Thokozani!

Futhi kunjalo-ke ukuthi Mina EnginguMkhulu, uBaba womkhwenyana, onguYeshua HaMashiach, unikeze uKukhala Kwaphakathi Kobusuku kumlobokazi nezihambeli zakhe, futhi bazilungiselele ukuze izibani zabo zigcwale amafutha omoya ongcwele nezintambo zenu zinqunywe konke ukungcola futhi izibani zenu zikhanyiswe ngalawo malangabi ahlanzekile enziwe ngalawo mafutha ahlanzekile omoya wami engiwanikeze ngoYeshua HaMashiach, futhi awakudalile ngokuhlukile ngaphakathi komunye nomunye wenu, nina enithembekile kuKristu.

Ngakho-ke, ngikhuthaza bonke abathembekile kuKristu Jesu ukuba baqhubeke nokulalela okuphelele nokunemininingwane ezintweni zonke kuMalusi wenu oMkhulu onakekela yonke imininingwane yazo zonke izimpilo zenu ngemininingwane emincane kakhulu nephelele. Yizani ngokugcwele eNkosini yenu yaphakade yokulunga enganilungiselela yona nonke lapho uYeshua waseNazaretha ezinikela njengePhasika eliphelele lesikhathi sonke ezandleni zaleyo nkosana encane ehluliwe ngokulalela okuphelele intando ka-Adonai-YHVH. Yizani ngokugcwele manje, ngaphandle kokungabaza futhi niqonde ukuthi Mina EnginguMkhulu ngiyanidonsa ngala mazwi ahlelwe ukuthola amehlo, izindlebe, nezinhliziyo zabo bonke labo athunyelwe kubo.

Qondani izikhathi eniphila kuzo, ngoba inkathi yami yomusa isifike esiphelweni, izonqanyulwa kungazelelwe nangokungalindelekile futhi ngokugcwele ngomyalo kaYeshua HaMashiach lapho umyalo unikwa nokumemeza okukhulu!

Khona-ke, kuzwakale icilongo elinamandla nelihle, lapho kungazelelwe ningasekho emhlabeni!

Lapho ngokungazelelwe ezulwini lamazulu niyokwamukelwa enkazimulweni enkulu, ekunqobeni, emandleni, nasodumweni efeni lenu lezikhathi ezidlule elilungele ukwabiwa kwabathembekileyo kuKristu Jesu. Ngeqiniso inkathi elindelwe yokuhlengwa kuka-Adamu kwabathembekileyo kuKristu Jesu isifikile eMazulwini kunanoma iyiphi enye inkathi eyake yaba khona, ngoba aBantwana bami baphakade abakhethwe ngokukhethekile ngala mazwi anikezwe kweyoku-1 Johane: "Bonani uthando olungaka uBaba asinike lona, ukuba kuthiwe singabantwana bakaNkulunkulu! Ngenxa yalokhu izwe alikunazi, ngokuba alimazi yena. Bathandekayo, manje singabantwana bakaNkulunkulu, kepha akukabonakali esiyakuba-yikho; kepha siyazi ukuthi, nxa ebonakaliswa, siyakufana naye, ngokuba siyakumbona njengalokhu enjalo. Futhi wonke umuntu onaleli themba kuye uyazihlanza, njengalokhu naye ehlanzekile", manje sekuzofanele bazalwe kabusha bephila futhi badalwe ngokusha ngokugcwele ebukhazikhazini, enkazimulweni, nasodumweni ngokushesha ngomyalo kaYeshua HaMashiach engimnike wona ngokukhethekile futhi manje nizoma kungazelelwe nangokungalindelekile esihlalweni sami sobukhosi nabo bonke abathembekileyo kuKristu Jesu.

IHarpazo yenu isifikile!

Futhi-ke, ngiyala bonke abathembekileyo kuKristu Jesu ukuba babambelele kula mazwi ngokulalela okuqinile ukuze uKristu Jesu anihlambulule nonke ngokugcwele ngaphansi kwegazi lakhe lokuhlawulela ngaphambi nje kokuba nithathwe futhi niguqulwe kuze kube phakade naphakade.

Hlalani nilungile!

Ngoba nizozalelwa esihlalweni sami sobukhosi, lapho ngilinde khona ngenjabulo njengoBaba olindelekileyo enginguye, ukulethwa okuphelele kuka-Israyeli ka-Adonai-YHVH ozobuthelwa MANJE.

Ngalo mbono engqondweni, ngiza ukusiza ngamunye wenu aqonde umzamo nemininingwane esetshenziswe ngokuhlakanipha okukhulu nekhono ngu-Adonai-YHVH wenu ukunifihla nokunivikela kuHeylel Ben Shachar obengabulala emhlabeni noma yimuphi wabantwana bami uma ethola ithuba, kodwa nokho anembule ngokwanele ukuze nibe senikhandwa ngokwezinjongo zami zaphakade kuKristu Jesu.

Ngakho-ke, naphawulwa futhi navikelwa, kodwa nafihlwa futhi nacashiswa obala, kodwa nembulwa ngokwanele: Futhi konke ngezandla zikaKristu Jesu! Ngoba nguyena owanithandayo futhi wazinikela, futhi wangcwelisa, futhi wahlambulula futhi walungisisa iBandla lakhe elinamandla elakhiwe ngabathembekileyo kuKristu Jesu. Ngoba ngeqiniso, amasango esihogo awasoze anqoba iBandla leqiniso kuyo yonke inkazimulo namandla alo amakhulu. Lelo Bandla elizoguqulwa futhi lishintshwe manje ukuze izinjongo zabo zangempela nezifihliwe manje zembulwe ngokugcwele, enizozazi ngokugcwele njengoba nani naziwa.

Bekezelani bantwana bami, izikhathi zenu ziyiMANJE.

Nokho, ngihlukanise iqembu elilodwa elihlukile eBandleni, ngibalungiselela kusengaphambili izinjongo zami (nakuba nonke ninokulungiselelwa) futhi ngabanika uKristu Jesu. Ngakho-ke, qondani lo mehluko nonke nina eniyizi-144,000 kanye nabo bonke abafunda la mazwi.

UKristu Jesu othandweni lwakhe ngani nonke, nothando lwakhe nokulalela kwakhe u-Adonai-YHVH wakhe, wakhulisela u-Adonai-YHVH abantwana abathembekileyo kuKristu Jesu futhi bavalwa ngalowo moya ongcwele wesithembiso futhi bahlukaniswa futhi banikezelwa ingunaphakade ukugcwalisa izikhundla ezasala eMazulwini ngodumo oluphindwe kabili, futhi badle ifa lomhlaba ngodumo oluphindwe kabili ngoba nakhetha ukuba nokholo ebufakazini bukaJesu waseNazaretha ngalolu suku olubi, lelo Wundlu likaNkulunkulu lapho lembulwa kini ngu-Adonai-YHVH wenu futhi lapho ninikwa ithuba lokuba nokholo olungangohlamvu lwesinaphi, nakholwa! Futhi leyo mbewu enamandla nephakade isikhule ngamandla amakhulu manje kini nonke! Kodwa yembulwa ngokugcwele esihlalweni sami sobukhosi kungazelelwe nangokungalindelekile manje.

HLALANI NILUNGILE!

Qondisisani ngokugcwele, ukuthi abakholwayo kuKristu Jesu bangabantwana baka-Adonai-YHVH, abayokhonza uKristu Jesu, onguYeshua HaMashiach, emisebenzini yenu nasezinhlelweni zenu engizilungiselele ngokuphelele futhi ngazenzela ngamunye wenu. Umvuzo wenu wenzelwe nina ngokuphelele futhi uyoba mkhulu kunomunye, kuphela ngokokwethembeka kwenu. Nokho, lokhu akusona isithiyo ekukhazinyulisweni kwenu ngefa, kodwa udumo olunikezwa ofanele udumo ngezinto eziningi ezenziwe enkonzweni kaKristu Jesu yinina nonke. Futhi nonke nihlukile omunye komunye, kodwa niyafana ezintweni enizibekezelele nazinqoba ngesandla esinamandla sikaKristu Jesu onisizile nonke.

Futhi, phakathi kwabakholwayo kuKristu Jesu kuthathwe abayi-144,000 ababekelwe eceleni ngokukhethekile njengabantwana bokutholwa bakaYeshua HaMashiach, abakhethwe phakathi kuka-Israyeli ka-Adonai-YHVH ngesandla sami esikhethiwe, nangentando yaphakade nengenazithiyo. Lawa maqhawe omlilo abunjwa osizini olukhulu futhi adlula ezilingweni nasezinhluphekweni eziningi ezandleni zikaHeylel Ben Shachar, futhi manje ahlanganisiwe futhi abunjwe ngamandla amakhulu ukuze abunjwe ngokugcwele ezinhliziyweni zawo ezingasoze zakhaphela izinjongo zika-Adonai-YHVH ezivezwa kuYeshua HaMashiach, ngisho nokulondolozwa othandweni olungenakunyakaziswa nolunamandla onke lwe-Agape. Selah.

Ngakho-ke, qondani nina 144,000 bami, ukuthi nonke nidalwe ngobukhulu nangokuphelele ngomklamo onamandla ogcwele nohlakaniphile, futhi ngomyalo kaKristu Jesu, onguYeshua HaMashiach, niyofeza izinjongo zethu zaphakade ngokumelene nale nyoka yesihogo, ukuze bonke abawiwe bahlulelwe ngokugcwele ngenhlawulo ephelele nesijeziso esinikezwe ekwahluleleni okunokulunga okugcwele komlilo kule nkosana encane ehlulekile neyisiphukuphuku. Noma kunjalo, nina nonke eniyi-144,000 ningabenzi balezi zenzo ezinikezwa ngokubuka okugcwele umbhalo obhalwe kanje: Impindiselo ngeyami: ngiyakuphindisela, isho iNkosi. Ngakho-ke, nina ningabenzi abaqokiwe balokhu kubuyisela okunokulunga kwenhlawulo kuwo onke amakhanda abawiwe, kodwa ikakhulukazi kuleyo nkosana encane eyisiphukuphuku, ehlulekile futhi ezigabisayo njalo, uHeylel Ben Shachar.

Ngisho naleyo nkosana encane ehlulekile eyayihlonishwe ngaphezu kwabafowabo ngenjongo yaphakade ka-Elohim, futhi enkanukweni nasekuziqhenyeni kwayo njengoba yayizidumisa, icabanga ukuthi konke engangimdalele khona kwakungokwenkazimulo nezinjongo zayo ukuze kusetshenziswe ngomyalo nangentando yayo, kanjalo yawa ekuhlubukeni okukhulu nokungalaleli ngendlela ehlazisa kakhulu ngehlazo elikhulu nokuziqhenya okuchotshoziwe ngokuphelele futhi ilondolozwe esimweni esidabukisa kakhulu nesikhubazekile engeke iphinde isilulame kuso futhi sizokuba sibi kakhulu kuze kube phakade.

Ngokunjalo, lapho uHeylel Ben Shachar (nabo bonke abalandela leli serafi) baphonswa ngempoqo, ngobudlova, nangobuhlungu obukhulu kalula ngamandla ami bevela eZulwini lamazulu, lapho behlutshulwa ngempoqo lonke igunya ngenxa yezono zabo ezimbi nezinenhloso nezihleliwe ezenziwa ngalo lonke ulunya nenzondo yokwakha itulo. Ngisho nezinto ezakhula ezinhliziyweni zabo ngoba uHeylel Ben Shachar wenqaba ukubusa imicabango yakhe elalela u-Elohim wakhe, esikhundleni sokugxila enkazimulweni nasemandleni ka-Elohim wakhe, ukuze ngimphakamisele esikhundleni sakhe saphakade senkonzo, samandla, nobukhosi, nenkazimulo, esikhundleni salokho wagxila enkazimulweni yakhe ebonakalisiwe azama ukuyithatha ngempoqo nangobudlova njengeyakhe.

Nasekukhohlisweni kwakhe okunesono okwenzeka lapho ezidumisa esonweni ngaphezu kuka-Elohim wakhe owamdala ngamandla nangenhloso enamandla wamthwesa umqhele wenkazimulo enkulu nodumo ngosuku adalwa ngalo ngokwezinhlelo nezinjongo zami, nekhono namandla, uHeylel Ben Shachar nabo bonke abamlandelayo baphonswa ngobudlova bevela emazulwini ngaphambi kokuba baphakame bagcwalise izikhundla zabo zaphakade zenkonzo, zamandla, nobukhosi, nenkazimulo ngoba bazinikela ngezifungo zokungalaleli okunenzondo ekuhlubukeni okungenangqondo esikhundleni salokho.

UHeylel Ben Shachar wabe esehlwanyela ngamabomu nangenhloso egcwele yokuhlela ukuhlubuka kwakhe ngobuqili phakathi kwabafowabo, sengathi u-El Shaddai wayengaziboni izenzo nemicabango nenhloso yenhliziyo yakhe.
