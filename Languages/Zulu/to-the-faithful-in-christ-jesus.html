Ufakazi Ombili: Israyeli
Okubabayo Okumnandi
Okthoba 25, 2024

Kwabakholwayo KuKristu Jesu!

Lalelani izwi leNkosi yenu yaphakade, uYeshua HaMashiach, ngoba yimi onibizele enkazimulweni nasebuhleni ngendlela yokuzalwa okusha enabekwa ngalo uphawu ngoMoya wami ongcwele wesithembiso. Yebo, nginenze abantwana bakaNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade, uYahweh uqobo, futhi akekho onjengaye! Ngakho-ke, qondani, zithandwa zami, ukuthi niphakathi kwalabo engibabekele uphawu ngalolo suku lokuhlengwa eniyakuma ngalo ngokugcwele ekulungeni engiyakunembathisa kona futhi abaningi bayothwala umvuzo omkhulu ngokukholwa kwabo, nakuba abanye beyolahlekelwa.

Qhubekani nokuphishekela ngamandla umgomo, umklomelo wobizo oluphakeme lukaNkulunkulu kuKristu Jesu, ukuze nizuze umvuzo wenu ophelele ezindleleni engizibeke kuzo izinyawo zenu, izindlela zenkonzo lapho ngihlele khona izinyathelo zenu ekungikhonzeni emhlabeni ngezikhathi zami zomusa. Futhi ningumsebenzi wezandla zami, enadalelwa kuKristu Jesu imisebenzi emihle, uNkulunkulu ayimisela ngaphambili, ukuba nihambe kuyo.

Futhi kunjalo ngempela, zithandwa zami, ukuthi yimi oninike injongo yaphakade kimi, iNkosi yenu yaphakade. Yimi enginobunye nenjongo ephelele noYahweh, uMINA ENGINGUYE omkhulu, ngakho-ke uma uNkulunkulu engakini, zithandwa zami, ubani ongamelana nani? Kungani ninikela nigobe ukugxila kwenu ezintweni ezibuthakathaka nezingelutho ezifuna ukunidukisa esandleni sami esinamandla saphakade, okungeke kwenzeke ukuba nisuswe kuso uma nigcina iso lenu linye ligxile kimi, uKristu Jesu iNkosi yenu eyenyuka!

Futhi ngisafundisa nanamuhla ukuze bonke abanezindlebe zokuzwa balalele, ukuba ningazibekeleli ingcebo emhlabeni, lapho inundu nokugqwala konakalisa khona, nalapho amasela efohla khona ebe. Kepha zibekeleleni ingcebo ezulwini, lapho inundu nokugqwala kungonakalisi khona, nalapho amasela engafohli khona engebi. Ngokuba lapho kukhona ingcebo yakho, kuyakuba khona nenhliziyo yakho futhi. Isibani somzimba yiso: Ngakho-ke uma iso lakho linye, umzimba wakho wonke uyakukhanya.

Zithandwa zami, aniwuqondi yini umzekeliso wami? Ingcebo yenu yimicabango yenu, ngakho-ke nginiyala ukuba nigcine imicabango yenu ezintweni eziphezulu ukuze ekuhambeni kwenu niveze futhi nihambe emisebenzini emihle engiyimisele ukuba izinyawo zenu ziyilandele ngenhliziyo eyodwa. Ngakho-ke, ngenza uPawulu abhalele abaseKorinte: Kepha ngiyesaba hleze, njengalokhu inyoka yakhohlisa u-Eva ngobuqili bayo, kanjalo izingqondo zenu zonakaliswe ekululameni okukuKristu.

Ngakho-ke, bhekani izinto eziphezulu, ningabheki izinto zasemhlabeni. Ngokuba nafa, ukuphila kwenu kufihliwe kanye noKristu kuNkulunkulu. Lapho mina, Kristu Jesu, engukuphila kwenu, ngibonakaliswa, khona-ke nani niyakubonakaliswa kanye nami enkazimulweni. Ngakho-ke, zithandwa zami, qondani ukuthi niyizibani zezwe, yebo, nonke eningabantwana bukaBaba wezibani! Ngakho-ke, gxilisani ngokuphelele ukunaka kwenu kimi, iNkosi yenu yaphakade yamaNkosi neNkosi yamakhosi, futhi ngiyosebenza ngamandla emoyeni wenu ukuze ukukhanya kwami kwaphakade kukhanye emzimbeni wami eniyingxenye yawo, ukuze izitsha zami zobumba zikhanye zibonise inkazimulo kaNkulunkulu waphakade ebusweni bukaJesu Kristu!

Ngakho ngiyanibuza makoti wami, ngiyanibuza mzimba wami, ngibuza abakholwayo bami kuKristu, kungani nivumela izitha zenu ukuba ziphazamise futhi zihlukanise ukunaka kwenu enkazimulweni nobukhazikhazi bami? Qondani ukuthi abawileyo bayazi kahle ukuthi ukuhlengwa kwenu okuphelele kuseduze njengokuphefumula kwenu okulandelayo, ngakho-ke bafuna ukuntshontsha injabulo yenu ngoba bazi ukuthi akunakwenzeka ukuba banihlwithe ezandleni zami zaphakade, nakuba bezama.

Ngakho-ke, ningesabi lutho ezitheni zenu, okuyisibonakaliso esisobala sokubhujiswa kwazo, kodwa kini kuyinsindiso, futhi lokho kuvela kuNkulunkulu. Ungaphambukisi ukunaka kwenu emsindweni wabawileyo, zithandwa zami, ungaphambukeli ngakwesokunene noma ngakwesokhohlo. Kodwa hlalani kulowo mgwaqo oqondile nomncane, indlela yensindiso lapho nifunda khona ngami uJesu waseNazaretha ngoba yimi onifundisa izindlela zensindiso lapho nisindiswa khona ngokuphelele njengoba nihlanjululwa egazini lami lokuhlawulela kuso sonke isono ukuze niphile ukulunga kukaNkulunkulu okuKristu okukini ithemba lenkazimulo!

Qondani ukuthi ngenkathi nihamba ngenxa yami, uSathane uyanihlasela njengoba efuna ukumisa imizamo yenu futhi aniphambukisele emisebenzini yakhe mbumbulu yobuze. Ngisho nevangeli mbumbulu lezenhlalo elingakunaki ukuphenduka nokuthethelelwa kwezono ngenxa yemisebenzi emihle. Nokho kunjalo, ukuthi ukondla abalambile, ukwembathisa abanqunu, ukuhlinzeka abangenamakhaya nokunye okuningi kuyimisebenzi emihle efanele ukuhlangabezana nezidingo ezikhona.

Kodwa mina, iWundlu likaNkulunkulu, angizange ngizinikele kanye, konke, futhi kuze kube phakade njengomhlatshelo wokugcina wesono kuleso sigxobo esinzima eKalvari ukuze ngikhokhele imisebenzi emihle, futhi noYahweh akangivusanga kwabafileyo ukuze ngibe umhlinzeki wezimpahla zalo mhlaba kulabo abaswele, kodwa ngalengiswa esihlahleni futhi ngachitha iconsi lami lokugcina legazi ebuhlungwini obukhulu esikhundleni nasenkokhelweni yesono kanye nemiphumela yezono zezoni.

Mina, Kristu Jesu, nginihlengile esiqalekisweni soMthetho, ngenziwa isiqalekiso ngenxa yenu, (ngokuba kulotshiwe, ukuthi: Uqalekisiwe wonke umuntu olenga esihlahleni) nokho leyo kwakuyinani elalingafanele ukuba ngilikhokhe, ngoba mina, Jesu waseNazaretha, ngingulowo ubumnsulwa bakhe busamile nanamuhla! Futhi ngingenasono kuze kube phakade ngoba ngihlala ngenza intando kaBaba. Ngakho-ke, phumulani emisebenzini engininike yona ukuba niyenze futhi nithathe ijoka lami ngokugcwele, ngokuba ijoka lami lilula, nomthwalo wami ulula.

Izikhathi zokwahlulela okukhulu zifike emhlabeni wonke, izikhathi zokuhlupheka kukaJakobe. Nokho ngaphambi kokuba ukuhlupheka kukaJakobe kufike ngokugcwele emhlabeni, kufanele ukuba abakholwayo bami bahlwithwe olakeni oluzayo, futhi mina, Kristu Jesu, nginguye ozayo emafwini ngezwi lengelosi enkulu nangecilongo likaNkulunkulu, futhi abafileyo kuKristu bayovuka kuqala, nina eniphilayo nenisalayo niyothathwa nabo emafwini futhi ningene egumbini lesihlalo sobukhosi sikaYahweh uBaba wenu waphakade.

Futhi usuku selufikile kini, bantwana bami, nonke enikholwayo kuKristu Jesu. Ngakho-ke, shiyani izinkathazo zalo mhlaba owileyo ngemuva bese nigxilisa ukunaka kwenu okuphelele eNkosini yenu yaphakade, uYeshua HaMashiach. Yebo, ukunaka kwenu okuphelele okungahlukanisiwe ngoba udeveli ufuna labo angabadla futhi njengengonyama ebhongayo uyahamba nokubhonga kwakhe okungenamsebenzi kodwa qondani ukuthi uyisitha esehluliwe zithandwa zami ngoba ngimehlule ngokuphelele futhi ukunqoba okuphelele kungokwami ngokugcwele kusukela emazwini ami okugcina engawakhuluma enyameni kuleso sigxobo esinzima: Kuphelile!

Futhi kunjalo ukuthi manje sengihamba ngokugcwele ukunihlengela ngokugcwele futhi ngiyowufeza lowo msebenzi ophelele ngaphakathi komunye nomunye wenu lapho niyodalwa khona ngokushesha nangokuphelele njengesidalwa esisha nesiphelele nesingenasono. Isidalwa esiphelele ukubola okungeke kwasibolisa, inkazimulo yaso engeke yaphela, nokukhanya kwaso okungeke kwafiphala. Ngokuba niyokwenziwa nifane nami, uKristu Jesu, ngoba ngingu-Adamu wokugcina, futhi ngingumoya onika ukuphila futhi ngingukaputeni wensindiso yenu.

Ngakho-ke, nakani izinto zomoya! Ningavumeli imicibisholo evuthayo yababi ukuba ingene emcebweni wenu. Qondani ukuthi uSathane angathanda ukubhubhisa ukugxila kwenu okukodwa eNkosini yenu yaphakade ngemicebo yakhe engenamsebenzi nebolile evame ukuba yinkohliso ekholisayo yeqiniso. Ngakho-ke yalani imicabango nemibono yakhe futhi nilahle zonke izindlela zakhe zokuhlubuka ngoba ezakhe ziyizindlela zokufa futhi ngokuqinisekile ufuna ukwabelana ngefa lakhe kulawo malangabi ashisayo esibabule esibolile nanoma ngubani okhetha lelo chibi lomlilo laphakade, kodwa leso siphetho asinakwenzeka kini zithandwa zami futhi nginibekele uphawu lwaphakade ngophawu olungunaphakade lukaNkulunkulu ophilayo.

Ngakho hlalani kimi ukuze ngenze izindlela zenu zicace, futhi nginikhombise ngokusobala izicupho uSathane angathanda ukubamba ngazo abakholwayo bami. Hlalani kimi, zithandwa zami, ngoba kuKristu akukho sicupho esingabekwa futhi ugibe lomthiyi alunakubekwa ngobuqili noma ngamandla. Ngakho-ke, qaphani imicabango yenu futhi nigcine amakhanda nezinhliziyo zenu zigcwele amazwi ami angcwele! (Ngokuba izikhali zokulwa kwethu akuzona ezenyama, kodwa zinamandla ngoNkulunkulu zokudiliza izinqaba.) Sidiliza imicabango, nakho konke okuphakemeyo okuziphakamisayo ngokumelene nolwazi lukaNkulunkulu, futhi sithumbela yonke imicabango ekulaleleni uKristu.

Ngokuba asilwi nenyama negazi, kodwa silwa nemibuso, namandla, nababusi bezwe, izikhulu zobumnyama baleli zwe, nobubi bomoya obusezindaweni eziphakemeyo. Ngenxa yalokho thathani izikhali zonke zikaNkulunkulu, ukuze nikwazi ukumelana ngosuku olubi, futhi senifeze konke, nime niqine.

Yebo, yimani niqine eNkosini yenu yaphakade, bantwana bami abancane, ngoba izikhathi phezu kwenu. Nakuba abaningi benu befuna ukubuyela esimweni sokulala, njengoba amaza alo mhlaba athobisayo ngamanga efuna ukuniyengela enkohlisweni enkulu. Ningaweli ekukhangweni kwabo, futhi fulathelani ubuthakathi nemilingo yabo. Fulathelani lokho abakubiza ngokuzijabulisa, ngoba akufanele ukuba abantwana abangcwele bakaNkulunkulu oPhezukonke abangabakaKristu Jesu ngokugcwele bajatshuliswe yisono.

Sengikuqinisekisile kwabakholwayo bami ukusondela neqiniso eliphelele lokubonakala kwami okuzayo ukuqoqela abakholwayo bami ekuphepheni kwamazulu. Ngisho nginike amaphupho, imibono, namazwi amaningi ebantwaneni abancane abathandwa kakhulu. Zithandwa zami, lesi senzakalo siziphonsa ngokugcwele kini futhi siphezu kwenu futhi sisondele kunalokho enizivumela ukuba nikucabange. Ngakho-ke, zilungiseleleni mina. Qhubekani nokuphuma kuso sonke isono futhi nivume izono zenu kimi ngokugcwele futhi nivumelane amaphutha enu omunye komunye.

Ngibabekele uphawu abakholwayo bami ngalowo moya ongcwele wesithembiso, ngakho-ke nginihlinzekele amafutha ezibani zenu, nonke enamukele ngokukhululeka kimi isipho sami saphakade sensindiso nokuphila okuphakade nefa laphakade kuKristu Jesu iNkosi yaphakade yamaNkosi neNkosi yamakhosi! Bhekani emazulwini zithandwa zami futhi labo abalindele ngokulalela nangokukholwa ukubonakala kwami bazobona ngamehlo abo inkazimulo yokubonakala kwami, ngoba le nto ngeke yenziwe ngasese futhi akunakwenzeka ukuba uSathane afihle inkazimulo yokubonakala kwami, nakuba ezokhohlisa futhi afune ukumboza ukubonakala kwami ngezihambi zasemkhathini mbumbulu nempi yezwe.

Ngakho-ke, lindelani mina ngokubekezela futhi niqede ngokugcwele imisebenzi engininike yona ukuba niyiqede ukuze niqede ngokugcwele izifundo zenu zenkonzo ohlelweni lwami lwaphakade lwensindiso lwempilo yenu ngayinye. Qondani zithandwa zami ukuthi ubulungiswa nomvuzo nefa nemivuzo kaYahweh akubalwa njengoba kubala umuntu wemvelo ngoba umuntu wemvelo akalungile futhi akanabulungiswa. Ngamunye wenu unikezwe imisebenzi yokuyikhiqiza ukuze niqhubekisele phambili izinjongo zami zensindiso futhi mina, Kristu Jesu, ngiyokwahlulela imisebenzi yenu eniyisebenzele ngomhlatshelo nasenkonzweni yeNkosi yenu yaphakade.

Qondani ukuthi nonke niyovuzwa kakhulu ngemisebenzi yenu yokholo eniyisebenze ngokwethembeka egameni lami elingcwele. Futhi, mina, Kristu Jesu, nginguye obona yonke imisebenzi eyenziwa ngabakholwayo kuKristu nenhloso yenhliziyo futhi ngizonivuza ngokugcwele. Njengoba ngangibheka abacebile befaka izipho zabo emnothweni, ngabona abacebile befaka izipho zabo emnothweni. Ngabona nomfelokazi othile ompofu efaka khona izinhlamvu ezimbili ezincane: Ngathi, Ngiqinisile ngithi kini, lo mfelokazi ompofu ufake okuningi kunabo bonke.

Ngakho ngiza kini zithandwa zami, khona lapha kule mizuzu yokugcina edlulayo ngaphambi kokuba nginiqoqele kimi futhi nginikhuthaza nonke ngothando lwaphakade lukaYahweh uqobo ukuba nime niqine futhi nibheke emazulwini ngoba ukuhlengwa kwenu kuphezu kwenu. Futhi igagasi ngalinye lenhlekelele liyokhula ngamandla futhi ukubhujiswa kuyokhula njengoba igagasi ngalinye lishaya emhlabeni wonke. Imihelo ayipheli manje futhi umhlaba usuzobeletha umntwana wawo ngoba emhlabeni kukhona amadodana ka-Adamu manje asephenduke amadodana kaNkulunkulu ngendlela ka-Adamu wokugcina. Futhi ngokuzumayo bonke bayoshintshwa kuze kube phakade futhi phakade babe senkululekweni ekhazimulayo yokuphelela futhi bayogqokiswa kuze kube phakade inkazimulo yaphakade nengunaphakade futhi banekusasa laphakade nenjongo yaphakade ezinhlelweni zaphakade zikaYahweh.

Qondani ukuthi lowo drago omkhulu obomvu uvunyelwe ukubeka isiteji futhi ahambise izingcezu zakhe ebhodini lomhlaba. Qondani ukuthi izenzakalo sezivele ziqhubeka nokuthi inkambo yamanje yomhlaba ayinakusimama futhi nakanjani izoholela enhlekeleleni ephelele nesheshayo futhi eziningi zalezi zenzakalo ezihlelanayo zizohlangana ngokuhlangana okuphelele.

Ngokuba kunjalo ukuhlela kwezinhlelo zikaYahweh, futhi yonke i-Yod neva le-Yod kuzohlangana ngokusebenza okuphelele nangokuvumelana, ngoba noma yini uYahweh abeka isandla sakhe kuyo iphumela ekupheleleni kwaphakade nasekulungeni kwaphakade ngoba nalabo abakhetha ukonakala namalangabi aphakade esijeziso bahlulelwa futhi bagwetshwe ngokulunga nangokuphelele.

Ngakho ngithi zithandwa zami, gcwalisani izintambo zenu futhi nizigcine ebuhleni obuphakeme kakhulu egazini lami lokuhlawulela elilungile nelihlanzayo. Gcwalisani izintambo zenu ngokuzigcina ebuhleni bomcabango obulethwa umbhalo wami waphakade ngoba uNkulunkulu waphakade, uYahweh uqobo, nguyena owathumela iZwi lakhe wabaphilisa.

Yimi onibizele enkazimulweni nasebuhleni.

Yimi oninike injongo engunaphakade kimi, iNkosi yenu yaphakade.

Yimi enginobunye nenjongo ephelele noYahweh, uMINA ENGINGUYE omkhulu.

Yimi onifundisa izindlela zensindiso lapho nisindiswa khona ngokuphelele.

Anginasono kuzo zonke izikhathi naphakade ngoba ngihlala ngenza intando kaBaba.

Ngingu-Adamu wokugcina.

Ngingumoya onika ukuphila futhi ngingukaputeni wensindiso yenu.

Futhi yimi othumela amazwi evangeli lami laphakade ngesithunywa sami engisivusile futhi ngasithuma ngenjongo le kanye nangesithunywa sami kuthunyelwe amazwi evangeli lami laphakade.

Ngiqinisile, ngiqinisile, ngingulowo uYahweh ahlanganyele naye isihlalo sakhe sobukhosi kuze kube yilapho izitha zami zenziwa isenabelo sezinyawo zami, kuze kube yilapho nginikela indalo ehlengwe ngokuphelele ezandleni zikaNkulunkulu waphakade wokulunga, ukuze ngempela uYahweh abe yikho konke kukho konke nokuthi konke kube ngu-yebo no-amen.

Futhi yimina, uYeshua HaMashiach, othumele amazwi evangeli laphakade ukuze kulalelwe bonke abayizindlalifa zokuphila okuphakade futhi la mazwi azogcinwa phakathi kwezinhlekelele nokwahlulela okuzayo futhi la mazwi azothola amehlo, izindlebe, nezinhliziyo zalabo athunyelwe kubo. Impela, ngiyeza masinyane. Amen. Umusa weNkosi yaphakade uJesu Kristu uthululwe ngokuchichimayo phezu kwabo bonke abakholwayo kuKristu, AMEN no-Amen.
