Kungani Nimlahla uMdali Wenu?

INkosi inginike lawa mazwi. Aveli kimi, futhi ngiwanikeza njengoba enjalo, angingezanga lutho futhi angisusanga lutho, ngokusemandleni ami onke.

Kungani nisamlahla uMdali wenu nezindlela zakhe na? Akazange yini aninikeze ukuphila okuphakade? Akazange yini aninikeze isinkwa sokuphila, mahhala kubo bonke abacelayo? Akazange yini aninikeze inkululeko kule ndalo ewileyo kimi, iNkosi uJesu Kristu omkhulu nothembekileyo? Mina ngiyindlela, neqiniso, nokuphila. NgiyiNkosi yamakhosi noMbusi wababusi, futhi nganikela ngokuphila kwami esiphambanweni ngenxa yenu, ukuze nibe nokuhamba embusweni wami uma nje ningamukela isipho sokuphila okuphakade. Anginibizanga yini ngegama? Kungani nisazibala njengabangakufanele ukuphila okuphakade, ekubeni igazi lami lokuhlawulela linganenza nifaneleke uma nje ningabiza egameni lami ningenze iNkosi? NginguNdodana kaNkulunkulu, iNdodana yoMuntu, uKristu: uJesu waseNazaretha, iNkosi ethembekileyo, yebo, iNkosi enkulu! Akekho omkhulu ngaphandle kukaNkulunkulu, onginike zonke izinto.

Bhekani! Usuku lwenhlekelele luphezu kwenu, usuku lokwahlulelwa nosizi. Izono zaseMelika zinjengezono zaseSodoma neGomora, futhi ukube wayengeke ahlulele isifebe esikhulu, wayeyobe engelona iqiniso ezizukulwaneni azisusa ngokwahlulela. Leso sifebe esikhulu, esikhanuka inyama engaziwa, ebesiyingxenye ebalulekile ekwakheni uhlelo lwesilo lwaleyo ndodana yokulahlwa, impela, ehamba phakathi kwenu ikhohlisa abakhethiweyo bami ngisho nangaphambi kwesikhathi sayo. Ukwahlulelwa okukhulu nenhlekelele kuyofika ngokuzuma. Melika, wahlulelwe ngaphandle wahlulelwa nangaphakathi.

Isandla sezitha zenu sesizoba sentanyeni yenu, futhi niyoketulwa ngodlame, nezizwe ngeke zisaphuza ezifebeni zenu zokukhanuka enithakethe ngazo bonke. Izitha zenu zinikwe ukunqoba phezu kwenu, nakuba nithembele ngokungaboni emandleni enu ezempi engiwasusile kini. Niyisizwe esisezinketangweni, esilindele ukukhunjulwa kwenu kokugcina phambi kweNkosi! Akukho lutho olukhulu ngani, kodwa izono zenu nobubi benu kuyiphunga elinukayo emakhaleni kaBaba.

Niyokwahlulelwa ngaphakathi, njengoba ukuzamazama okunamandla, okunodlame kuyonibhubhisa ngokuzuma nangaphandle kwekhambi. Isifebe esikhulu siyophonswa phansi njengesixwayiso kubo bonke futhi kuyoba isiqalo sehora elimnyama kunawo onke. Ngeke saphinde siketule izizwe ngokungemthetho.

Ngeke saphinde sihlupha umhlaba ngobubi baso. Ngeke saphinde izinengiso zaso ziyenge abantu bami. Isifebe esikhulu singcolise isivumelwano sikaBaba, lowo thingo lwenkosazana olukhazimulayo alnika uNowa njengesiqinisekiso sokungaphinde akhukhule umhlaba. Nakuba umhlaba ungeke uphinde ukhukhulwe, amagagasi okubhujiswa ayowela phezu kwabaningi, nomlilo oqothulayo uyasondela.

Bantu bami, ningabi ngabahlanganyeli ezonweni zaso, ezinhluphweni, ekwahlulelweni, nasenhlekeleleni. Ngisazonibekela indawo etafuleni lami uma ningaphendukela kimi ngenhliziyo yenu yonke.

Phendukani! Vumani izono zenu kimi, uKristu Jesu! Anifundanga yini ukuthi abangalungile abayikulidla ifa loMbuso kaNkulunkulu? Nifisa yini ukuthi umvuzo wenu wamukwe ninikwe omunye? Ningacabangi ukuthi niyongena ebukhoneni bami nisesimweni sokungalaleli ngamabomu; akashongo yini uBaba wami, "Ngingcwele, ngakho-ke yibani ngcwele nani"? Gijimelani kimi nizinikele ensindisweni yami, ngoba ngiyiNkosi yamakhosi neNkosi yezinkosi, nezinsuku zokuncenga isizukulwane esinenkani ziyasondela ekupheleni.

Sekukade ngelula isandla sami esisindisayo kini, kodwa nimlahla uNkulunkulu woyihlo nomusa wami osindisayo okwazi ukunisindisa ngokuphelele? Wozani kimi nicashe esifubeni sami; impela, ngizonifihla osukwini olukhulu lwenhlekelele oluphezu kwenu. Ningahlanganyeli nalabo abalilahlile igama lami; uKristu akahlangene noBheliyali, kodwa abantu bami basathakathiwe ukukhanya kwakhe kwamanga. Khalani nilile niphenduke ezindleleni zenu zokuba mlaza. Buyelani othandweni lwenu lokuqala nifune ubuso bami kusesekhona isikhathi.

Igazi lami lokuhlawulela lizonigeza uma nje ningaphuma ezonweni zenu. NginguNdodana yoMuntu, umpristi ophakeme kuze kube phakade ngokohlelo lukaMelkisedeki. Nginguye owazigcina emsulwa ebusweni bazo zonke izilingo, ngisho elingwa nguSathane uqobo kodwa ngahlala nginqoba. NginguNdodana kaNkulunkulu ezelwe yodwa ethele uMoya oNgcwele phezu kwabo bonke abaphendukele kimi nabo bonke abasazophendukela kimi. Mina, uJesu Kristu, ngiyizo zonke lezi zinto nokunye okuningi, futhi ngiyalaya labo engibathandayo ukuze ubizo lwabo lube luqinisekile futhi luphelele.

O muntu, izono zakho ziningi, kodwa uzimemezela wena ngokwakho ukuthi ungcwele! Izono zokuphinga nokufeba. Izono ze-pharmakeia, ukuthakatha, nemilingo eminingi. Abahahayo, abangenasihe, abazithandayo, abathanda injabulo, nabathanda imali. Kuyokwenzekani ngokukholwa kwenu engcebweni lapho ingcebo yenu iba yize ngokuphazima kweso nobumpofu nencithakalo enkulu kufika phezu kwenu ngokuzuma? Niyohlambalaza yini ebusweni bomusa? Niyosakhetha izono zenu? Labo abakhetha izono zabo ngenkani bayobhubha kuzo. Ningacabangi ukuthi uNkulunkulu uyahlekwa, njengoba esezohleka usulu abahleki bosulu; ningabi ngabahlanganyeli nabo.

Ihora selihambile, nemizuzu yokugcina iyasondela nesikhathi sesicishe siphele enkathini yami yomusa, nolaka luyokwehlela umhlaba njengoba iBandla leqiniso lisuswa phambi kwamehlo enu. Angithokozi ngokubona indalo yami ibhujiswa ngenxa yeziphambeko nokungalungi; kodwa anginikanga yini ithuba lokukhetha ukuphila embusweni wami, kodwa abaningi benqaba ngenkani? Phumani kuyo! Solani nishiye izindlela zenu; nivume mina, uJesu Kristu, njengeNkosi, niyeke ukona.

Angibanikanga yini ukuthula okukhulu amadodana namadodakazi ami, abanqobile izono zabo ngegazi lami uqobo? Kodwa abaningi basabunqaba ubufakazi nobungqina babo futhi basenqaba ukukholwa, bekhetha ukuzidla kwabo kunokuthobeka kwezindlela zami. Izindlela zenu zingugibe futhi ziyoniyisa ekubhujisweni okuphelele, lapho ningeke naphulukiswa khona. Phendukelani kimi kusesekhona imizuzwana embalwa yokwenza kanjalo. Ihora selihambile, futhi bonke abakhuleka egameni leNkosi bayosindiswa!

Ngiyanicela ukuba nihlanganyele enkazimulweni yesihlalo sami sobukhosi namamukele ukuphila nami. Phendukani ezindleleni zenu nibuyele kimi, ngoba ngiyiNkosi yaphakade futhi akukho nsindiso komunye ngaphandle kwami! Mina, uJesu Kristu, ngiyiNdodana kaNkulunkulu futhi ngeza ngenyama, ngabethelwa, ngavuswa ngosuku lwesithathu, ngenziwa umoya ophilayo futhi ngamukelwa emazulwini kuze kube sekupheleni kwezikhathi. Ngempela, ubusuku sebuyaphela nehora selihambile, nezikhathi zokubuyisela ziyasondela.

Bantu bami,imani niqine emandleni ami nibe nokuthula. Phumulani kimi, kanti kusasele isikhashana nje, ukukhazinyuliswa kwenu kuyofika ngokuphazima kweso, nomvuzo omkhulu nilungiselelwe wona endlini kaBaba. Izinto enginilungiselele zona azikaze zingene enhliziyweni yenu, impela, hhayi enhliziyweni yamuntu. Ukuthula nokulondeka niyokuthola, futhi nina enibe nethembekile ezintweni ezimbalwa ngiyonivuza kakhulu. Phumulani kimi nigxilise amehlo enu kimi. Zimemezeleni ukuzila ukudla phakathi kwenu nivuselele amandla enu kuleli hora lokugcina, nimemezele izindlela zami zokulunga nokwahlulela kwami okusondelayo, ngoba ngisho namanje abaningi bayosindiswa.

Nginibonise umusa omkhulu, ukubekezela okukhulu, nomusa omningi, kodwa anisezandiseli lezi zinto kwabanye. Niyodela umvuzo wenu ningiphoqe ukuba ngiwunike omunye? Noma niyothanda uNkulunkulu ngenhliziyo yenu yonke, umphefumulo, ingqondo, namandla enu, nomakhelwane wenu njengoba nizithanda nina? Kungani nimenza ize umakhelwane wenu futhi nimahlulele ngephutha njengongakufanele ukuphila okuphakade nibe nime lapho nithule? Angikuniki yini izimfihlakalo zami ukuba nizimemezele kubo bonke? Imani niqine kimi, futhi ngizoninika amandla okufinyelela labo omubi angazama ukubafihla esandleni sami esisindisayo. Vula umlomo wakho umemezele onke amazwi alokhu kuphila namazwi okukhuluma ngizokunika wona. Yabelanani ngothando lwenu kuze kube umzuzwana wokugcina, ngoba lowo mzuzwana wokugcina usondela njengoba icilongo selizobiza abantwana bami ekhaya.

NginguNkosi ethembekileyo engaphuthi.

Ngingukukhanya kwezwe, futhi noma ngubani obiza egameni lami elinamandla ngeke abhubhe emalangabini angenakucimeka; phezu kwabo, ukufa kwesibili akunamandla.

Ngingonamandla kunabo bonke abanamandla, futhi zonke izinto ezulwini nasemhlabeni zinikezwe ezandleni zami kuze kuphele ukubuyiselwa, lapho izitha zami ziyisenabelo sezinyawo zami. Ngiyindlela, neqiniso, nokuphila.

NgiyiNkosi yamakhosi noMbusi wababusi.

NginguNdodana kaNkulunkulu, iNdodana yoMuntu, uKristu: uJesu waseNazaretha. Ngingcwele, ngakho-ke yibani ngcwele nani.

Ngingumpristi ophakeme kuze kube phakade ngokohlelo lukaMelkisedeki. Nginguye owazigcina emsulwa ebusweni bazo zonke izilingo.

NginguNkosi yaphakade, futhi akukho nsindiso komunye ngaphandle kwami!
