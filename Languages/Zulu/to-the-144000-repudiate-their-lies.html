Kwabayi-144,000: Enqabeni Amanga Abo

Thobelani izwi lami, nizwe ukuhlakanipha kwami, ngokuba ukuhlakanipha kuyamemeza ezitaladini: niyolizwa yini izwi lami? NginguYeshua HaMashiach, iNdodana kaYahweh, owafika enyameni futhi ngiyiwundlu likaNkulunkulu elisusa isono sezwe. Qondani ukuthi umhlaba eniphila kuwo akuwona lowo mfanekiso wamanga enawudwetishelwa abafundisi benu bamaJesuit ukuze banidukise. Abaningi benu bagxunyekwe amanga amaJesuit, baze bafunda okuphambene nokufunda. Le mpi yeqiniso yakhelwa ukubumba izingqondo zabantu ukuba zithobele uSathane futhi izama ukususa nokuphuca abantu inkululeko yokuzikhethela ukuze abantu balandele ngokungenangqondo lokho abakufundisiwe.

Umlando weqiniso awunakuzwiwa emangeni ezazi zefilosofi zamaJesuit, ngokuba zingabaqambimanga abasakaza amanga ngenkohliso enkulu. Imibhalo icacile ngale ndaba, futhi eyabaseRoma imemezela iqiniso elishiwo ngokusobala: Ngokuba intukuthelo kaNkulunkulu yambulwa ivela ezulwini phezu kwakho konke ukungamesabi uNkulunkulu nokungalungi kwabantu abathiya iqiniso ngokungalungi.

Ngabe niwavumele lawo mazwi ukuba afinyelele ezinhliziyweni zenu? Abantu ngamabomu bathiya iqiniso ngokungamesabi uNkulunkulu nokungalungi, futhi intukuthelo kaYahweh imelene nalabo abenza kanjalo. Ngakho-ke imfundo yenu yenziwe yaba ngeyamanga ngenxa yamanga abo.

Bakwenze lokhu ukuze balungiselele indlela yokukhohlisa okukhulu okuzayo, futhi ukuze umbuso kaSathane ukhululwe ukuze ubuse ngenkohliso enkulu nangamanga. Amanga abanitshele wona kwezemfundo asebenza ukuphuphuthekisa izixuku eqinisweni: Azikho izidalwa zasemkhathini, kukhona kuphela izingelosi eziwile ezilibhekise echibini lomlilo ukuze ziboshwe futhi zishiswe kuze kube phakade. Ningakholwa amanga azo nigijimele kimi, uKristu Jesu, ngokuba kimi kuhlala lonke iqiniso eliyonikhulula. Ngiyanitshela lezi zinto ukuze ningakhohliswa kanye nalabo abayohlanganyela noSathane futhi bamukele uphawu lwesilo oluzayo.

Umlando uqondwa ngokulandela amaqiniso agxilile emibhalweni nasezincwadini ezingaphandle kweBhayibheli njengo-1 Enoke, uJasheri, amaJubili, nokunye, kusukela ekuqaleni. Ngokuba laba bantu abangamhloniphi uNkulunkulu namanga abawasebenzisayo ukufihla iqiniso bayingxenye nje yempi enkulu, impi yenzalo phakathi kukaYahweh noSathane eqhubekayo kuze kube yilo mzuzu, kodwa esenqotshiwe kuze kube phakade ngemisebenzi yami yokuhlawulela. Ngokuba impi yamiswa uYahweh kuleyo nyoka endala ngokuhlubuka kwayo, kusukela ekuqaleni kokudalwa kwesintu lapho uSathane azama ukubhubhisa u-Adamu no-Eva. UYahweh wathi enyokeni esimemezelweni sakhe: Ngizakubeka ubutha phakathi kwakho nowesifazane, naphakathi kwenzalo yakho nenzalo yakhe; yona iyakuchoboza ikhanda lakho, wena uyakuchoboza isithende sayo. Kanjalo-ke izimpi zenzalo zisaqhubeka kusukela lapho.

Ensimini yase-Edene, uSathane wakwazi ukukhohlakalisa inzalo ka-Adamu, elahla u-Adamu ngaphansi kwamandla nenkohlakalo yesono. (Kulokhu iNdodana kaNkulunkulu yabonakala, ukuba ichithe imisebenzi kaSathane.) Ngokuba uSathane wasebenzisa ulwazi lwakhe olukhohlakele lokuhle nokubi ukuze akhohlakalise ngokuphelele isintu, futhi kulabo abalalela izwi lami, leyo nkohlakalo iyachithwa kimi, uKristu Jesu, enikuyisidalwa esisha kuye esingasoze sakhohlakaliswa. Inzalo yami ayinakukhohlakaliswa, ngokuba uYahweh uyenze yangonakali, futhi ngendlela yokuzalwa okusha ngibeke uphawu kwabakhethiweyo nabathembekileyo bami ngisho nangalowo moya ongcwele wesithembiso!

UJohane encwadini yakhe yokuqala umemezela iqiniso laphakade: Lowo ozelwe nguNkulunkulu akenzi isono, ngokuba imbewu yakhe ihlala kuye; akanakona, ngokuba uzelwe nguNkulunkulu.

Esikhathini esifushane ngemva kokuwa kwe-Edene, lapho u-Adamu ona futhi wawa ekupheleleni waya enkolakalweni, abalindi behlela eNtabeni iHermoni ezinsukwini zikaJarede. Futhi bonke bazibopha ngezifungo ezifanayo, ngisho nesifungo sabo esingcwele lapho bazibopha khona phakade esonweni nasesijezisweni saphakade. Bakwenza lokho ukuze bonke bazithathele abafazi kubo bonke ababakhethayo, bashintshe kanjalo ukuhleleka kwemvelo yabo.

Bafika esintwini beletha ulwazi lwabo lwezingelosi eziwile olubolile, ngokuba bajoyina ukuhlubuka kukaSathane.

Bafundisa abantu ukwenza izinkemba, nemimese, nezihlangu, nezivikelo zesifuba, futhi babazisa ngezinsimbi zomhlaba nobuciko bokuzisebenza, namasongo, nezinto zokuhloba, nokusetshenziswa kwe-antimony, nokuhlobisa amehlo, nazo zonke izinhlobo zamatshe ayigugu, nazo zonke izinhlobo zokudaya. Bafundisa ukuthakatha, nokusika izimpande, ukuxazulula ukuthakatha, ukufundwa kwezinkanyezi, izinkanyezi, ulwazi ngamafu, izibonakaliso zomhlaba, izibonakaliso zelanga, nenkambo yenyanga.

Ngakho abalindi abawileyo bathatha abafazi babo futhi bafeba nabo, base bekhulelwa futhi babazalela izidlakela ezinkulu. Futhi ngokuhamba kwesikhathi le nzalo engcwele yadla konke okwakusemhlabeni, nakho konke okwakunikezwe isintu, futhi ekugcineni yadla ingxenye enkulu yesintu ngangokunokwenzeka. Ngokuba babephuza igazi futhi bedla abantu, futhi benza okubi okukhulu emehlweni kaYahweh, ngokuba ukuphila kwenyama kusegazini futhi isintu senziwa ngomfanekiso kaYahweh. Bakhula banda futhi ekugcineni bamisa imibuso yabo kuwo wonke umhlaba. Bhekani! Amanxiwa ezakhiwo zabo zamatshe amakhulu asagcwele umhlaba njengobufakazi obumelene nabo. Babusa umhlaba ngalezo zikhathi, futhi benzelwa ukubhujiswa kwesintu. Kanjalo-ke zonke izinhlobo zezinengiso zafezeka, ngokuba imibhalo engcwele imbala imemezela ukuthi kwenziwa kanjani.

Sengathi kwakunganele ukuba isintu sikhanuke inyama engaziwa, sifebe nezingelosi eziwile, futhi sizale inzalo yezidlakela, ngenxa yabalindi bonke bona ezinyonini, nasezilwaneni, nasezilwaneni ezihuquzelayo, nasezinhlanzini. Kusukela ekuqaleni kokudala, uYahweh wamisa uhlobo ngalunye lokuphila ngohlobo lwalo. Kodwa abalindi ababi baphazamisa indalo, baze bahlanganisa izidalwa zohlobo olulodwa nezidalwa zohlobo olufanayo ukuze balethe ukungazinzi kofuzo futhi bashintshe indalo yokuqala kaYahweh yokuphelela okuvumelanayo. Le nqubo yaphindwa, kwahlanganiswa izidalwa ezifanayo zohlobo olulodwa nezidalwa ezifanayo zolunye uhlobo kwaze kwafinyelelwa umphumela oyifunayo. Kanjalo-ke umhlaba owawukhona ngaleso sikhathi, wakhohlakaliswa yizo zonke izinhlobo zezinengiso ezinonya ezona kakhulu kuYahweh, esintwini, nakuyo yonke indalo. Qondani, abawileyo bafuna umhlaba wabo siqu, umhlaba wobumnyama ohluke ngokuphelele kuYahweh, futhi bayokudla ifa lalokho kanye echibini lomlilo abalilungiselelwe nguYahweh uqobo.

Kanjalo zafika izinsuku zikaNowa nomhlaba owawukhona ngaleso sikhathi, umi ngaphandle kwamanzi nasemanzini, wagcwala amanzi wabhubha. Ngokuba imithombo yotwa olukhulu yadabuka, yabangela udlame olukhulu lapho umhlaba udabuka ngamandla amakhulu amanzi anolaka, futhi amafasitela ezulu avulwa, avumela amanzi avela ngaphezu komkhathi (nangaphansi komhlaba) ukuba amboze futhi abhubhise umhlaba wonke.

Kanjalo-ke u-Enoke umbhali uchaza isiphetho salezi zidlakela kanje, ngokuba uYahweh wamemezela kanjalo: Futhi manje, izidlakela, ezizalwe emimoyeni nasenyameni, ziyobizwa ngokuthi imimoya emibi emhlabeni, nasemhlabeni kuyoba yindawo yazo yokuhlala. Imimoya emibi iphumile emizimbeni yazo; ngoba izelwe kubantu, kubalindi abangcwele isiqalo sazo nemsuka yazo; iyoba yimimoya emibi emhlabeni, futhi iyobizwa ngokuthi imimoya emibi. Ngokuqondene nemimoya yasezulwini, ezulwini kuyoba yindawo yayo yokuhlala, kodwa ngokuqondene nemimoya yomhlaba eyazalwa emhlabeni, emhlabeni kuyoba yindawo yayo yokuhlala. Futhi imimoya yezidlakela iyahlukumeza, icindezele, ibhubhise, ihlasele, ilwe, futhi yenze incithakalo emhlabeni, futhi idale inkinga: ayidli, kodwa nokho iyalamba futhi yome, futhi idale amacala. Futhi le mimoya iyovukela abantwana babantu nabesifazane, ngoba iphumile kubo. Qondani ukuthi ingxenye yesishiyagalolunye yale mimoya emibi isekwalasha ngenxa yesicelo nomthandazo kaNowa, futhi isintu sibhekane nengxenye eyodwa kuphela yeshumi yayo kusukela lapho. Lokho sekuzoshintsha lapho kwalasha kuzovulwa futhi konke kuzokhululwa.

Ngakho umhlaba, nazo zonke izinengiso zabalindi, kwabhujiswa. Abalindi ngokwabo baboshwa ezahlulelweni zabo ezahlukene izizukulwane ezingamashumi ayisikhombisa, ukuze bakhululwe ukuze bahlulelwe esikhathini sokwahlulela okukhulu. (Ngokuba ukwahlulela okukhulu sekuzoqala.) Nokho, uNowa wathola umusa emehlweni kaYahweh ngoba wayeyindoda elungileyo, nomndeni wakhe uYahweh wawusindisa bephela imiphefumulo eyisishiyagalombili isiyonke, kanye nezinye izidalwa eziningi emkhunjini. UNowa wazigcina yena nomndeni wakhe ekuguqulweni kofuzo ngaphambi kukazamcolo. Kodwa indodana yakhe uHamu nenzalo yakhe embi ekugcineni bayomphika uNowa ngisho noYahweh uqobo (owayebadalile futhi ebasindisile), bezihlanganisa nokuhlubuka kukaSathane.

Amazwi kaGenesise 6 agcwaliseka ngezono zenzalo kaNowa, ngisho nohlu lozalo lukaHamu. Kwakukhona izidlakela emhlabeni ngalezo zinsuku, nangemva kwalokho futhi. "Lezo zinsuku" kwakuyizinsuku kusukela kuJarede kuya kuNowa, futhi "izidlakela emhlabeni" kwakuyinzalo yabagcini nabantwana besifazane ngaphambi kukazamcolo. "Ngemva kwalokho" kungemva kwezinsuku zikaNowa, lapho ufuzo lwesintu lwaphinde lwangcoliswa ngolwazi lwezingelosi eziwile. Ngiyanicela ukuba ningabe nisakholelwa enkohlisweni yombono kaSethe onamaphutha kodwa nikholwe amazwi ombhalo ongcwele, ngokuba la mazwi ayiqiniso.

Lapho umhlaba wahlukaniswa ngenkatho phakathi kwamadodana kaNowa (uShemi, uHamu, noJafete), inkatho kaHamu kwakungeyakhe ukuba azuze ifa lamazwe aseningizimu, futhi kanjalo aba ngawakhe. Nokho, uKhanani wafisa ingxenye yefa likaShemi futhi wathuthela emazweni okwakungewona awakhe ukuba ahlale kuwo. Ngoba kwakunguKhanani uNowa amqalekisayo. Ngoba uKhanani washiya ifa lakhe futhi wasungula umuzi waseTire kulokho okwaba ugu lwaseFenike futhi kwaholela eSidoni, lapho uJezebeli avela khona. Wakwenza lokhu ukuze abe seduze neNtaba iHermoni, lapho abagcini behlela khona nalapho ngaguqulwa khona isimo.

UKhanani wathola lokho abagcini abakushiya ngemuva ngaphambi kukazamcolo kaNowa, ngoba babefuna ukulondoloza ukuhlubuka kwabo ngisho nangemva kukazamcolo, ngoba babeqophe okuningi emadwaleni nasematsheni. UKhanani wasebenzisa ulwazi lwabo lwezingelosi eziwile emilingweni emikhulu, futhi imikhuba eminingi emibi yenziwa ngamaKhanani, ngoba baze bahlabela ngabantwana babo. Babenyanyeka kangaka futhi besabalele kangangokuthi wakwazi ukuthelela izwe laseKhanani ngezinengiso zakhe ezisanda kwenziwa, lapho uSathane asebenza khona ngenxa yeziphambeko zakhe.

Futhi kanjalo izizwe zezidlakela zangemva kukazamcolo, izizwe ezinengekayo zamaKhanani, zavela eziphambekweni nasebubini nasemilingweni kaKhanani. Ezweni uKhanani alintshontsha kuShemi kwafika amaHeti, namaHivi, namaPerizi, namaGirigashi, nama-Amori, namaJebusi. Babengabanamandla, ngisho namaRefa. Kwakukhona izizwe ezingaphezu kwalezi ezibalulwe, kodwa laba abanamandla babelindile ezweni lesithembiso emzamweni wokubulala labo ababevikela uhlu lozalo lukaKristu, okhokho benu bama-Israyeli nobaba ababengamaHeberu.

UJoshuwa wangena ezweni lesithembiso ukuze afune lokho okwakunikezwe uShemi ngenkatho ngokufanelekile kanye nezwe elalingelika-Israyeli ngokufanelekile ngesithembiso sikaYahweh uqobo. Kodwa abantwana bakwa-Israyeli abayigcwalisanga ngokugcwele imiyalo kaYahweh yokungashiyi neyodwa yezinhlanyelo ziphila, ngoba zazinonya futhi zazingeyena umuntu, futhi imikhuba yazo yayimibi, futhi zenza okubi okuningi emehlweni kaYahweh, ngoba kwakuyimvelo yazo. Futhi kanjalo uYahweh watshela u-Israyeli njengoba kulotshiwe emibhalweni: Kodwa uma ningabaxosha abakhileyo bezwe phambi kwenu, kuyokwenzeka ukuthi labo enibashiya kubo, bayoba ngameva emehlweni enu, nameva ezinhlangothini zenu, futhi bayonihlupha ezweni enihlala kulo. Futhi kwaba njalo.

Umlando ingxenye yawo ubonisa ukwanda nokuhamba kwalezi zinengiso, izinhlanyelo ezatshalwa nguSathane uqobo, njengoba zihlala phakathi kukakolweni kusukela ezinsukwini zasendulo. Bakhe impucuko eminingi besebenzisa ubuchwepheshe babo kanye nolwazi lwezingelosi eziwile; bangabakhi abangochwepheshe, bangabathakathi, abaningi babo banamakhono angavamile, futhi baxhumene noyise uSathane, babelana ngezici zakhe nezinkanuko zakhe. Lezi zinhlanyelo zingene kuzo zonke izici zomphakathi, futhi akuzona zonke eziyizidlakela. Eziningi zibukeka zingabantu, ngaphandle kokuthi ziyizidalwa ezixubile ezishintshwe unomphela emiklamweni kaYahweh ekudaleni futhi ngeke zihlengwe. Ningazesabi, ngokuba mkhulu lowo okini kunalowo osezweni.

Lezi zinhlanyelo zifuna ukuthatha lokho okungokwefa nesithembiso kubantwana bakaNkulunkulu. Akunakuba khona isivumelwano nazo, ngoba zingozoyise uSathane, futhi izinkanuko zikayise ziyokwenza. Wayengumbulali kusukela ekuqaleni, futhi lapho ekhuluma amanga ukhuluma ngokwakhe, ngoba ungumqambimanga noyise wawo. Ziyocekela phansi, zingcolise, zibhubhise, zibulale, ziphange, ziphuce, futhi zenze konke okubi kakhulu kunoma yini engcwele ezibeka izinkanuko zazo kuyo. Azinakho ukuthula, futhi imvelo yazo yonakele ngokuphelele. Ziwugumbuqile umhlaba, futhi ziyomisa umbuso wazo ihora elilodwa njengoba udrago enokubusa kwakhe emhlabeni. Nokho bafuna ukubuyisela inkathi yabo yegolide, kodwa ngeke babe nayo inkathi yegolide ngaphandle kwesahlulelo esinzima, futhi ngeke baphunyuke.

Lezi zinhlanyelo ezimbi zikhohlakalise ulwazi, ukufunda, umlando, inkolo, isayensi, futhi ziqhubekile nokukhohlakalisa. Zisakaze ubuchwepheshe bezingelosi eziwile futhi akukho lutho olusha ngaphansi kwelanga. Njengoba izikhathi sezigcwalisekile, ziwumboze ngokuphelele umhlaba ngamanga, futhi umhlaba wonke ulele ebubini. Ngokukhanyiselwa kwazo, izinhlanyelo zamaJesuit ziwukhohlakalise ngokuphelele umphakathi futhi zaholela isintu olwandle lwamanga, lapho okuwukuphela kwendlela yokuphuma kungokuphenduka nokulahla zonke izono namanga. (Ngokuba iRoma yenza namaSulumane; fundisisani ukuze nifunde.) Ulwazi lwazo lwezingelosi eziwile namanga kumelene nomlando wemibhalo futhi kunalokho kunikeza izinganekwane ezingasho lutho eziphuphuthekisa izingqondo nezinhliziyo zabantu. Ngakho-ke, phumani phakathi kwazo nihlukane nazo.

Umhlaba namuhla ungowokuthi abantu abaningi kakhulu banokukhohlwa okuhlangene ngenxa yokufundiswa ngamaJesuit. Abaningi benu abagcinanga ngokukhohlwa izivumelwano nezindlela zikaYahweh kuphela, kodwa bakhohlwe ngokuphelele ukuthi ningobani nokuthi okhokho benu bangobani ngempela. Lokhu kwenziwa ngamabomu ngempi, amanga, kanye nemizamo yokuhlalisa kabusha abantu eminyakeni edlule okusabalalise abaningi emagumbini amane omhlaba. Ngalokhu ngokuzithandela abazi ukuthi akunakwenzeka ukufihla noma yini emehlweni kaYahweh; futhi mina, uYeshua HaMashiach, ngiyabazi abami nokuthi ngibizelani ensindisweni.

Abanye balabo abahlakazekile babeyizintandane futhi bathathwa emindenini yabo ngenxa yempi noma ukuthunjwa ngokusobala. Le miphefumulo yakhulela emlandweni wamanga, yaze yaqanjelwa amanga ngemibono yothando yesikhathi esedlule samanga, futhi ngaleyo ndlela inkohliso igxile kakhulu ezinhliziyweni zabantu, futhi abaningi baphuphekiswe unomphela ngamanga amukelwa njengeqiniso. Kodwa kulabo abaphendukela kimi, uYeshua HaMashiach, niyolazi iqiniso futhi iqiniso liyonikhulula. Umphakathi wenu wamanje wakhelwe phezu kwamanxiwa nomlotha wempucuko yabawileyo yasendulo, futhi kwenziwe umzamo omkhulu wokufihla iqiniso. Lokho obekukhona kuyoba khona futhi, futhi akukho lutho olusha ngaphansi kwelanga.

Ngaphandle komhlatshelo wami wokuhlawulela nomnikelo wami wensindiso, nonke beniyolahleka. Ngakho wozani kimi, uYeshua HaMashiach, futhi ngizonihola kulo lonke iqiniso futhi ngizonifundisa umlando wenu weqiniso. Ngizonixhumanisa kabusha nokhokho benu beqiniso, ngoba abaningi benu bavela ezweni, nakuba insindiso ingeyazo zonke izizwe. Ngizonifundisa ezindleleni zobungcwele, futhi niyophila ngokulunga ngokukholwa kimi, uJesu Kristu, futhi niyophila phakade. Akukho lutho oluyimfihlo olungeke luvele ekukhanyeni, futhi konke kuyodalulwa ngokugcwele, ukuze iqiniso laziwe yibo bonke.

Ngakho-ke yekani ukuzitika ngamanga njengoba nizigqiba ekunganakeni, ngoba izikhathi zokunganaki seziphelile. Izikhathi zokwembula seziseduze. Ngokuba bhekani! Njengasezinsukwini zikaNowa, kuyoba njalo. USathane usebenzise indwangu yakhe yamanga ukuze alungiselele izinkohliso zakhe ezinkulu zesikhathi sokugcina, futhi iningi lomhlaba liyolandela amanga akhe. Ngokuba wonke umbuso kasathane uyokhululwa kwalasha, futhi izidlakela ziyophinde zihlale phakathi kwabantu, ngoba ziza ukugcwalisa intukuthelo kaYahweh.

USathane ukhuluma amanga amakhulu, futhi laba abawileyo bayothi bavela kweminye imihlaba nokuthi bathuthuke ngaphezu kwesintu ngezinkulungwane eziningi zeminyaka. Bayofika beletha ulwazi olubonakala lunomusa, futhi bayozembathisa ngomusa wamanga. Kodwa laba bangabombuso kaSathane, ngakho-ke ningaweli ezinkohlisweni. Ningalandeli izindlela zezwe, ngoba izwe liyomangala ngombuso kaSathane, lize lithi, ubani onjengesilo?

Ngizonifihla, ngizonivikela, ngizoniqinisa, ngizonihola isinyathelo ngesinyathelo, futhi ngizolidabula ngokuphelele iveyili lamanga elimboze umhlaba ngenkohliso. Mina, uYeshua HaMashiach, ngizoninika amandla futhi ngenze nenze izimangaliso eziningi ezinamandla kulesi sikhathi sokuhlupheka kukaJakobe. Futhi ngakho ngiyanitshela lezi zinto, ukuze iqiniso linikhulule, nokuba nikhululeke ngempela futhi ningaweli enkohlisweni yazo enkulu. Ngoba izisekelo zayo ngamanga, inkohliso, nobuxoki obusakazwa yinzalo yenyoka.
