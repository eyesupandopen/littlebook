I-AmeruKa: Izwe Lenyoka Enophaphe / Zilungiselele, Ngiyeza!

Zwanini amazwi kaYeshua HaMashiach, onguKristu Jesu, futhi niqonde inkulumo yami ukuze niqonde imvelaphi yeqiniso yeBhabhiloni Elifihlakele, isimo salo, nenhloso yalo, ngoba ngempela liyizwe lenyoka enophaphe, i-AmeruKa uqobo, izwe elinyenya ngokunyenya futhi lidle abakhileyo kulo, lihlinze abangenacala futhi lenze umnikelo ngoyise nonina ngobuningi obukhulu kusukela ezikhathini zasendulo kuze kube manje. Izwe eliqalekisiwe elingcolisa abakhileyo kulo ngokunyenya nangobuningi bezinsalela zemikhosi engcolisayo neyokungcolisa yeminikelo enegazi nenonya egqitshwe kulo lonke leli zwe elingcolile ezinqwabeni eziningi zemifanekiso, imikhuba, ukushiswa kweThofethi, negazi eligeleza ngokukhululeka, igazi elifanayo elimanze umhlabathi walo lapho umhlaba ukhala khona ufuna ubulungiswa ngegazi elachithwa, nelisachithwa ngobuningi. Wona lawo mazwe angcolile akhala kakhulu futhi ngokuzwakalayo emazulwini efuna ubulungiswa khona manje, ngesikhathi lapho umhlaba uzamazama kakhulu njengendoda edakiwe esezokuwa, ufuna ukwahlulelwa okulungileyo okungaphuzi ukufika.

Futhi wena Bhabhiloni Elifihlakele, wena uqobo: Uyizwekazi elingcwele futhi elingcolile, izwe elakhiwa ekugcineni kusukela ezinsukwini zikaNowa, izwe elanikezelwa kuHeylel Ben Shachar nasezindleleni zenyoka, izwe eligcwele igazi labangenacala ngemikhuba nezenzo zalolu nzalo olungcolile lwezinyoka nabangcolisi abaphuma bayongcolisa ezikhathini zasendulo. Izwe elibekelwe ukuba libe yimfihlo ngezandla ezifihliwe zabaWileyo, elabekwa futhi lahlelelwa ukuba libe yi-Atlantis yabo Entsha, isizwe esakhelwe futhi sanikezelwa ukuphakama kwesilo sasolwandle nesilo sasemhlabeni. Isizwe esizishaya umKristu, kodwa eqinisweni siyisizwe sabakhonzi bakasathane abafihlekile abazisungula futhi bahlala ngokunethezeka ezikhungweni zaso ezingcolile nezingcolisayo, kusukela kwabancane kuya kwabakhulu, futhi yilezi zinhlangano eziyimfihlo ezinikezelwe kuHeylel Ben Shachar (abambiza ngokungafanele nangokungazi ngegama likaLusifa), bezibheka ngokungazi njengabakhanyayo nabakhanyiselwe, ekudukeni kwabo nasezinkohlisweni zabo lapho bona ngokwabo beboshelwe khona phakade ezifungweni zabo ezingcwele, bekhohlisa, kodwa ngaso sonke isikhathi bekhohliswa yilabo ababoshelwe kubo phakade njengamadodana esono.

Makwaziwe ukuthi iBhabhiloni Elifihlakele yi-United States of America, endulo eyayibizwa nge-Ameru-Ca, izwe lenyoka enophaphe elanyenya laziguqula laba yileso sifebe esikhulu esihleli phezu kwamanzi amaningi futhi liyisizwe esenziwa ukuze isilo sasolwandle nesilo sasemhlabeni sikwazi ukuvuka emlotheni waso ngezikhathi zaso zokwembulwa. Isizwe esiphingayo, isizwe sokufeba, isizwe esiyisifebe esikhulu esizishaya umKristu olungileyo olandela uJesu waseNazaretha. Kodwa eqinisweni siyisizwe abakhi baso abakhululekile EDwaleni Leqiniso Lesisekelo, lelo litshe legumbi elikhulu abakhubeka kulo abangakholwa, onguJesu waseNazaretha onguYeshua HaMashiach. Ngeqiniso, i-AmeruKa yisizwe esamiswa yileso sandla esingcwele nesifihliwe, izwe lezinhlangano eziyimfihlo ezinikezelwe enyokeni ngezindlela eziyimfihlo, futhi njengoba amandla eSiphambano SeRozi ahlasela i-AmeruKa, kwabekwa phezu kwalo uhlelo lokugcina lwe-Atlantis Entsha, uhlelo olwalufihlwe isikhathi eside olwaluzohlanganisa izinkolo zenyoka emhlabeni wonke, lubhubhise futhi lwenze umnikelo ngabo bonke abavela esigabeni sabapristi balezi zimpucuko eziwileyo, kanye nazo lezi zimpucuko ngokwazo ezazizozama ngokuzidla nangobuwula ukumisa yona leyo nyoka ezazinikezele kuyo ngezifungo eziningi ezinegazi ukuba ziyikhonze ngokuphelele, zingaqondi isimo salowo mbunjwa abazibopha kuwo.

Ngakho-ke uyi-AmeruKa, ngoba ngikhuluma nezwekazi elisuka enyakatho liye eningizimu, lisuka empumalanga liye entshonalanga, liyizwe elinikezelwe kuleyo nyoka enophaphe yasendulo engcolise futhi yasilaphaza la mazwe, njengoba imibuso iye yavuka yawa emingceleni yalo, imibuso eminingi engabhalwanga yenyoka, isizwe senyoka enezimpaphe nendizayo esinikezelwe emiphakathini yobufazi nentando yeningi lapho izixuku ezimbi zazingabusa khona ngaphansi kwethonya likaHeylel Ben Shachar, nenzalo yakhe yezinyoka engcolise amazwe eBhabhiloni elifihlakele phakade, okuyizwe elakhiwa njengoba umuntu ezulazula emhlabeni esuka lapho umkhumbi wafikela khona eNtabeni i-Ararati kudala. Futhi njengoba abantu bayalwa nguYahweh, uMINA ENGINGUYE OMKHULU, beboshelwe ngaphansi kwesivumelwano sikaNowa ukuba bazandise, bahlakazeke, futhi baphinde bagcwalise umhlaba ngokulunga kwesivumelwano; inzalo kaHamu esikhundleni salokho yazibuthanela ekungalalelini okukhulu ukuze izenzele igama emazweni aseShinari, izitshela yona yothi: “wozani, asizakhele umbhoshongo oqinileyo ukuze sizilondoloze, futhi ukuze silwe namazulu ngoba uNimrode wazenza umzingeli onamandla phambi kweNkosi,” umzingeli onamandla wabantu ayebazingela ukuze ajabule.

Ngakho-ke abantu beza ezweni lenyoka enophaphe bevela empumalanga bewela ibhuloho lomhlaba laseBering njengoba laziwa namuhla, futhi beza ngolwandle bevela entshonalanga ngemizila ehlukahlukene yokuhweba nokuhamba, bafika kula mazwe ayebekelwe uHeylel Ben Shachar ngezinjongo zakhe ezimbi, izwe elalithathwe ngokugcwele yizidalwa eziwileyo ezweni elalikhona ngaleso sikhathi, izwe lapho zangcwaba khona izimfihlo eziningi ezizovunyelwa ukuba zizikhiphe ngokuzumayo nangokungalindelekile, lapho nje umvimbeli wami esuswa futhi nokubusa kwazo okufushane kuvunyelwa ukuze kusindiswe uJakobe, futhi ukuze nazo ziwe ekwahlulelweni ngomlilo njengoba le ndaba ivunyelwe ukuqhubeka ukuze zibhuqwe ngokuphelele, ngoba isikhathi sezidalwa eziwileyo sesifikile ngempela, futhi yimina Kristu Jesu, iNgonyama Yesizwe SakwaJuda, ozocoboshisa ikhanda lale nyoka yasendulo ekwahlulelweni kokugcina.

Kanjalo uKhanani wakuthola lokho abalindi abakungcwaba, futhi waholwa ngokwenkanuko yenhliziyo yakhe njengoba ahlasela futhi weba izwe elalingelona elakhe ukuba alihlale, ephula isivumelwano sikaNowa. Nokho wafisa izinsuku zasendulo ezazibhubhiswe kulowo zamcolo omkhulu ezinsukwini zasendulo lapho izwe elalikhona ngaleso sikhathi labhubha ngenxa yokuyenga kukaSathane abalindi abawileyo ukuba bazihlanganise nabesifazane futhi bazale inzalo yabo eqalekisiweyo, inzalo engeyona into ngaphandle kwezifebe nabalahliweyo, ukulingiswa kwempilo lapho izinengiso zabo ziboshelwe khona phakade ukwenza izinkanuko ezinengekayo nezonakele zoyise babo, futhi izinkanuko zoyise babo bayozenza. Ngakho-ke, qondani ukuthi lolu khula olubi, oluyizinengiso ezinengekayo ngempela, alunampande nagatsha ezulwini, emhlabeni, ngisho nangaphansi komhlaba, futhi luzosishulwa ngokuphelele phakade, ngaphansi kwelanga ngokwesithembiso sesivumelwano sika-Adonai-YHVH uMINA ENGINGUYE OMKHULU, futhi luzoboshwa lushiswe kuleyo mililo emikhulu yembuyiselo lapho isibungu sabo singafi khona futhi bonke abaphonswa emalangabini bayokwamukela izono zabo izikhathi eziningi emabeleni abo siqu ekwahlulelweni komlilo okuphakade nokulungileyo abangeke baphunyuke kukho.

Kanjalo uHeylel Ben Shachar, leso sikhosana esincane esahluliwe, lowo muntu oziqhayisayo nomngcolisi wakho konke okumsulwa, wahamba wayotshala ukhula lwakhe ensimini kaYahweh, uMINA ENGINGUYE OMKHULU, yona leyo nsimu eyatshalwa lapho u-Elohim ethi makube khona ukukhanya kwaba khona ukukhanya. Ngoba ekuqaleni, u-Elohim wenza amazulu nomhlaba, futhi umhlaba waba yize engenalutho njengoba ubumnyama beza phezu kobuso botwa. Kanjalo uHeylel Ben Shachar nengxenye yesithathu yezingelosi zakhe ezalandela ukuhlubuka kwakhe baphonswa emhlabeni ngehlazo, nokwehlulwa, futhi nguHeylel Ben Shachar, leso sikhosana esincane esahluliwe esakhala ngehlazo nangokwehlulwa lapho sikhishwa ezulwini lamazulu ngolaka olukhulu, sibekelwe ukwahlulelwa kokugcina ngomlilo emlilweni walolo suku olukhulu, wona lowo mlilo obaselwe ngamalangabi aluhlaza ashisayo namsulwa, futhi ulungele ukwamukela bonke abajoyine ukuhlubuka kukaSathane kusukela kwabancane kuya kwabakhulu embusweni wezidalwa eziwileyo.

Ngakho-ke u-Elohim wathi makube khona ukukhanya kwaba khona ukukhanya, futhi kanjalo u-Elohim wamisa umhlaba owawusungulwe kabusha ngomyalo wakhe yedwa nangamazwi akhe yedwa nangeseluleko sakhe yedwa indalo yabunjwa, yenziwa, futhi yadalwa ngamazwi akhe anamandla naphakade namandla neseluleko sakhe yedwa, ngoba u-YHVH akadingi meluleki. Asikho isidalwa esifana noNkulunkulu Ophakade Wokulunga, nakuba beqamba amanga futhi bezenzisa ngendlela edabukisayo. Kanjalo umhlaba wenziwa nakho konke okwakuqukethwe kuwo futhi wona lowo mhlaba wanikwa u-Adamu, ngoba umuntu wenzelwa umhlaba, nomhlaba wenzelwa umuntu, owayedalwe ngomfanekiso womdali wakhe, u-YHVH – uMINA ENGINGUYE OMKHULU!

Nokho leyo nyoka enobuqili yahlasela ifa lika-Adamu ngobuqili bayo, ukuhlasela nokuphulwa okwalungiswa ngokuphelele eKhalvari, lapho mina, uJesu waseNazaretha, ngikhuluma amazwi aphakade athi “sekuphelile” futhi kanjalo ngithengile isintu, umhlaba, nakho konke okukuwo ngegazi lami elihlawulayo futhi umhlaba ungowenkosi nokugcwala kwawo, futhi ngiza njengeNgonyama Yesizwe SakwaJuda ukwahlulela nokulwa, ngakho-ke zwanini ukubhonga kwami, nina bakhileyo emhlabeni, ngoba isikhathi senu sesifikile!

Ngakho-ke izidalwa eziwileyo zaphuma zayongcolisa nokuguqula indalo isuke ekupheleleni kwayo yasekuqaleni eyadalwa ngakho. Zona ngokwazo azikwazi ukudala ngoba u-YHVH akazange azidale zinaleyo mandla noma amakhono. Ngakho-ke, qondani ukuthi lezi zidalwa eziwileyo ziyaziqhayisa ngokuthi zingonkulunkulu kanti zingabazenzisi abazenzisayo.

Ngakho-ke, yizwani amazwi kaYeshua HaMashiach, onguKristu Jesu, njengoba ngiza ukuzotshela ibandla lami, umzimba wami, ngisho nomakoti wami ngamazwe onakele futhi angcolile lapho abaningi benu bazalelwa futhi bahlala khona, njengoba okhokho benu bezulazula emhlabeni ngaphansi kokujeziswa ngu-Adonai-YHVH, uMINA ENGINGUYE OMKHULU, yena owaxosha izizwe zasenyakatho phakathi kwezizwe ephethe incwadi yesehlukaniso, futhi lapho, zihlakazekile phakathi kwezizwe, ziye zagcinwa zalondolozwa kusukela lapho ngumfundisi wazo, ngoba zabambelela emthethweni kaMose njengoba zihamba ziya emazweni asenyakatho lapho zanqotshwa khona ekungalalelini ngezandla zabase-Asiriya, kodwa zahlakazwa ngabaseBhabhiloni, bona abanqoba futhi bajezisa abangalaleli phakathi kokhokho bami abavela ezizweni zakwaJuda noBenjamini, umbuso waseningizimu wakwaJuda ezinsukwini zasendulo owaba yiJudiya lapho i-Idumea noJuda kwahlanganiswa ngosiko lwenkemba.

Ngakho-ke izimvu ezilahlekile zendlu ka-Israyeli ziye zahlakazwa phakathi kwezizwe ekungalalelini kwazo. Umbuso wasenyakatho wahlakazekela ezizweni zasenyakatho emazweni aseGogi naseMagogi, kulo lonke elaseYurophu naseMelika baya khona. Kanjalo abafowabo nabantu besizwe sabo bombuso waseningizimu bahlakazwa phesheya ezinsukwini zikaThithu noVespasian, naphinde ngesikhathi sokuvukela okwakuholwa nguSimon Bar Kokhba, futhi abaningi babantu bami bahlakazwa ngokuqhubekayo emazweni aseningizimu nasempumalanga yeJerusalema baya enyakatho ye-Afrika nase-Alexandria yaseGibhithe nakwezinye izindawo eziningi baye bazulazula ekujuleni kwabo, kodwa baye balondolozwa nguMose, nakuba abaningi bethunjwe ngu-Esawu, no-Ashkenazi, futhi abaningi babo bakhohlisiwe balindele uMahdi wabo, esikhundleni sikaYeshua HaMashiach onguMesiya weqiniso, nomkhululi ka-Israyeli owathenjiswa endulo ngu-Adonai-YHVH, Selah.

Nokho, njengoba kulotshiwe kunjalo ngempela ukuthi abantu bami abazi lutho futhi ababoni kahle ukuthi bangobani ngempela. Basishulwe ezimpandeni kuYahweh, uMINA ENGINGUYE OMKHULU, lapho umnqumo wemvelo uhlanganyela khona ekunoneni kwempande, yona leyo mpande enguYehovah-YHVH, uMINA ENGINGUYE OMKHULU.

Nokho abantu bami baye baphendulelwa ngokungazi omunye komunye ngamanga nenzondo nokungazazi ukuthi bangobani ngempela, njengoba baye bafundiswa ukukholelwa emangeni alolu nzalo lwabapristi benyoka kunoma yisiphi isizwe abazimisele kuso, lona lelo qembu labapristi benyoka abakhonza uHeylel Ben Shachar, lelo serafimi elivuthayo neliwileyo elibekelwe umlilo wesijeziso saphakade nokwahlulelwa, wona lowo mlilo obekelwe ukwahlulelwa okunzima kakhulu kule nyoka embi ezingcolisile ngokuhlubuka okungenangqondo, ibanjwe esimweni sokunqotshwa nokuwa engeke iphinde isuke kuso.

Kanjalo-ke abantu bakithi nabafowethu bafundiswe ukuzondana ngezindlela zamanga amaningi ahlanganiselwe ukufundisa izixuku, kodwa kulotshiwe kanje: Lezi zinto eziyisithupha uJehova uyazenyanya; yebo, eziyisikhombisa ziyisinengiso emphefumulweni wakhe: amehlo azidlayo, ulimi olunamanga, nezandla ezichitha igazi elingenacala, inhliziyo eyakha amacebo amabi, izinyawo ezishesha ukugijimela ebubini, ufakazi wamanga okhuluma amanga, kanye nalowo ovusa ukungezwani phakathi kwabazalwane. Ngalokho-ke abantwana boMbuso waseZulwini bafundiswe ngobuqili, ngenkohliso, nangenzondo enkulu ukuthi yibona kanye abayizigqilazi zabafowabo ngaphesheya kwe-Atlantic, isenzo esenziwa ngu-Esawu no-Ashkenazi besebenzela uHeylel Ben Shachar, lapho besola abantwana boMbuso waseZulwini ngezenzo zokhula olwantshontsha abantwana emazweni nakubomama nabobaba abathengiswa ngenzondo enkulu ngomakhelwane babo nezizwe zabo ezandleni zabantu ababi ezikhathini zasendulo. Ngakho-ke qondani ukuthi lezi zenzo ezimbi zilotshwe ngokuphelele emazulwini futhi ubulungiswa nempindiselo kuyonetha ngokuzumayo phezu kwabakhileyo emhlabeni abasafuna ukubhujiswa kukaJakobe, nakuba kuyikubhujiswa kwabo kanye okuyobehlela ngokuzumayo nangokungalindelekile manje, Selah.

Nokho, abaningi benu bahlala bengenalwazi ngoba ibala lesikhumba senu lehlukile kunelabafowenu ngobuciko nomklamo omuhle kaYahweh, kanti asikho esinye isidalwa emhlabeni esizizondayo ngale ndlela ngenxa yamanga enyoka kukho konke okudaliweyo kuka-Elohim, kepha abantu bami bayazivumela ukuba baboshwe ngobuwula obunjalo namanga angenangqondo anikezwe yibo kanye abawayo ukuze kubhujiswe abantu bami. Phendukani ebuwuleni obunjalo manje, ngoba anisenaso isikhathi!

Nokho, abaningi benu basangenalwazi ukuthi kuKristu Jesu akukho mJuda noma weZizwe, akukho sigqila noma okhululekileyo kodwa kuyisidalwa esisha kuKristu, yebo isidalwa esisha lapho izinhlu zozalo luka-Adamu zaphakade zichithwa ngomoya wobuNdodana, ngoba izithembiso ezenziwa ku-Abrahama othembekileyo ziyozuzwa njengefa ngabalungileyo abaphila ngokukholwa okufanayo kuka-Abrahama othembekileyo, manje okunikezwe ngokugcwele ebusweni bukaJesu Kristu ekuzalweni okusha, kepha abaningi benu bakhetha ukungazi ezindleleni zenu ezingatheni ezingesizo ezami, okumele niphenduke kuzo, ngoba nginiyalele ukuba nithande uNkulunkulu ngenhliziyo yenu yonke, nangomphefumulo wenu, nangengqondo yenu, nangamandla enu onke; futhi nithande umakhelwane wenu njengoba nizithanda nina. Umyalo abaningi benu abangawuvumeli enzwondweni yenu yobuhlanga yohlanga olufanayo luka-Adamu olunombala ohlukile ebuhleni bomklamo kaYahweh. Phendukani!

Futhi kwaba njalo ezinsukwini zasendulo, uhlu lozalo lukaKhanani lwaqhubeka luzingcolisa ngokuzihlanganisa nabawayo, ngisho nasezinsukwini zasendulo, futhi njengoba bezigcolisa esonweni zazibophezela ngokugcwele kwabawayo lapho emazweni aseShinari ngisho nasethunzini lalowo mbhoshongo omkhulu waseBhabheli, wona kanye umbhoshongo u-YHVH awudiliza ngokwehlulwa. Ngoba kulotshiwe kanje: Ngemva kokuba umhlaba ugcwele amanzi ezinsukwini zikaNowa, umhlaba wahlukaniswa ngenkuba yefa phakathi kwamadodana kaNowa ezinsukwini zikaPhelege, kanjalo-ke uJafete wangena efeni lakhe emazweni asenyakatho. Futhi abantu babenolimi olulodwa, namadodana kaHamu abuthanela ezweni elaba yiBhabhiloni. Futhi izikhulu zikaNimrode zakha icebo kanye noPhuthi, noMitsirayimi, noKushe, noKhanani nemindeni yabo ukuba bakhe umuzi wabo kuwo nombhoshongo oqinileyo, yebo umbhoshongo omude ukuze bakwazi ukulwa namazulu nokuthi bazibuthele ndawonye ukuze bazenzele igama ngokuhlubuka emyalweni kaYahweh wokuba bathele futhi bande ukuze baphinde bagcwalise umhlaba. Kodwa zinjalo-ke izindlela zomuntu wemvelo ongenakukholwa, okuthi ezinkanukweni nasekuziqhenyeni kwakhe akhonze isono ngalokho akhonze uBaba wesono; yena kanye usathane, ngendlela yokungalaleli kwesintu kuYahweh ngenxa yesono enyameni yomuntu wemvelo.

Futhi enzalweni kaNimrode eyakhula ebubini kuthiwa kwababi kuphuma ububi obuningi. Kanjalo-ke uNimrode wangcolisa umhlaba ngezindlela zabawayo futhi kubathakathi nabangoma bakhe kwavela izikole zezimfihlakalo zasendulo lapho abathakathi babawayo bafundisa khona izimfihlakalo zabo ezingenamsebenzi kulabo abayophonswa emalangabini naphakade ezonweni zaboSathane, futhi bayohlanganyela nabo efeni labo laphakade. Futhi uNimrode wavuka embusweni wokuqala ngemva kukazamcolo kaNowa, lapho uNimrode wabusa umhlaba ngokungalungi; umbuso kanye abawayo abazifunela wona ngokugcwele phezu komhlaba, kodwa angeke babe nayo inkathi yobukhazikhazi kodwa ukwahlulelwa okunzima kunalokho.

Kanjalo uYahweh ngokwahlulela kwakhe wenza ukuba umhlaba uvuleke ugwinye ingxenye yesithathu yombhoshongo wabo, nomlilo wehla uvela ezulwini wadla enye ingxenye yesithathu yombhoshongo, kwathi ingxenye yesithathu yavunyelwa ukuba isale njengesikhumbuzo ngabo. Ngalokho, igama lawo labizwa ngokuthi yiBhabheli, ngoba uYahweh wasanganisa khona ulimi lomhlaba wonke; futhi kusukela lapho uYahweh wabahlakaza phezu kobuso bomhlaba wonke. Futhi izizwe eziningi nabantu benqaba bakhohlwa uYahweh owangena esivumelwaneni noNowa, kanjalo bakhohlwa isivumelwano uNowa asenza noYahweh ngokwefa labo nangokwezilimi ababezikhuluma bakhohlwa isivumelwano sikaYahweh noNowa, baphendukela ezindleleni zabawayo ngoba akukho okunye ababengakukhetha. (Nokho u-Abrama waba ngu-Abrahama futhi olungileyo uyophila ngokukholwa!)

EBhabheli uNimrode wenza okubi okuningi njengoba enza izithombe eziningi futhi wazibiza ngonkulunkulu futhi wenza uziqu-zintathu wakhe ohlambalazayo, yebo abathathu abangcwele, ngenhloso yokuhlekisa ngobunkulunkulu beqiniso nangembewu ethenjisiwe yowesifazane nokudukisa amadodana abantu ekungalungini.
