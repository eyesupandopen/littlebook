Ngizweni Futhi Ningizwe Kahle!

Ngizweni futhi ningizwe kahle, nina baphrofethi bamanga enihlanekezela iZwi lami elingcwele, ngokuba aniveli kimi, futhi aninawo umoya wami ongcwele, futhi nihlasele uMlobokazi wami, lona iBandla lami engilibizile! Yizwani isixwayiso sami nilalele, O, sizwe esingakholwa, esiyisigwegwe nesonakele, ngokuba niyophendula ngazo zonke izenzo zenu ezimbi! Yizwani amazwi ami esixwayiso, nina enidukisa iBandla lami, ngokuba ulaka lwami luyavutha ngani, ngokuba ngiyiWundlu likaNkulunkulu! Ngokuba abaningi benu bayozifihla emihumeni nasemadwaleni ezintaba. Futhi niyothi ezintabeni nasemadwaleni, Welani phezu kwethu, nisifihle ebusweni balowo ohlezi esihlalweni sobukhosi, nasolakeni lweWundlu: Ngokuba usuku olukhulu lolaka lwakhe selufikile, futhi ngubani oyokwazi ukuma?

Ngokuba umhlaba wonke uyobona ukubonakala kwami, nokuzamazama okukhulu kuyofika kubo bonke, nokubhujiswa okungazelelwe kuyofica umhlaba lapho uMlobokazi wami ehlwithwa eyiswa endaweni ephephile. Ngokuba ngizomsusa umvimbeli, futhi bonke ububi buyokhululwa ngokuphelele, nokwahlulela okukhulu kuyophonswa phansi kuvela emazulwini, njengoba nje kulotshiwe kuleyo ncwadi enkulu yokwembulwa kwami, yona incwadi yeSambulo! Ngakho ngimemezela ukwahlulelwa phezu kwenu, nonke eningcolisa iBandla lami: izinsuku zenu zibaliwe, futhi maduzane izinhliziyo zenu ziyodangala lapho ulaka lwami lwembulwa. Nina baphrofethi bamanga abangenamsebenzi eningene eBandleni lami, nifundise ubuthakathi obuxutshanisiwe nemilingo neZwi lami elingcwele nezindlela zami ezingcwele, futhi niyokhokha.

Nibangele ukuba abancane bami bone futhi bathathwe yizinkinga ezinkulu, engiyozibuyisela kini ngekhulu ekwahluleleni kwami phezu kwenu. Nichaze kabusha amagama emibhalweni yami engcwele, nenze umkokotelo wemibhalo yami engcwele ngokuyibhala kabusha niguqule amazwi kaNkulunkulu waphakade, wona amazwi kaYahweh, futhi niyokhokha kuze kube phakade. Ningenise imibhalo eyonakele eBandleni lami ngendlela efanayo nokuthi okhokho benu abangenamsebenzi bafaka ushevu emithonjeni futhi bafaka ushevu ekudleni okunikezwe isintu ukuze siphile, futhi niyokhokha kuze kube phakade. Lonke izwi eliyize enilikhulumile, yonke imali engcolile eniyiqongelelayo ekubhujisweni kwenu, yonke imicabango emibi enake naba nayo, zonke izisusa ezimbi enake nazilandela niyokwethulelwa zona, futhi niyokhokha, kuze kube yimali encane yokugcina; futhi anisoze nakwazi ukuma phambi kwami, futhi niyothatha indawo yenu emalangabini uBaba awasilungisela uSathane nezingelosi zakhe.

Kodwa qondani ukuthi aniyona isimangaliso futhi akukho okumangazayo kini, ngokuba ningabaphostoli bamanga, izisebenzi ezenkohliso, eziziguqula zibe abaphostoli bakaKristu. Futhi akusona isimangaliso, ngokuba uSathane uqobo lwakhe uziguqula abe yingelosi yokukhanya. Ngakho-ke, akuyona into enkulu uma nezinceku zakhe ziguqulwa zibe njengezinceku zokulunga, eziphetho lazo liyoba ngokwemisebenzi yazo. Ngokuba ngaloba kanjalo eKorinte!

Niyoqhubeka nemisebenzi yenu nigcwalisa izono zenu ngokugcwele futhi nigcwalise iphakade ngokugcwele ngesijeziso senu nokuhlushwa kwenu, ngokuba intukuthelo yami inkulu ngani, futhi niyohlanganyela noyihlo emlilweni. Ngokuba ningukhula phakathi kukakolweni, futhi ngizonisiphula ngokuphelele; ngokuba uBaba, yena uYahweh, umemezele ukuthi anisoze naba nampande noma ifa, ngokuba imbewu yenu ngeyomubi, futhi nonke ningababulali nabaqambi manga. Ngokuba ngathini koyihlo eJudiya eminyakeni eminingi yeJubili edlule? Nina ningabakayihlo udeveli, nezinkanuko zikayihlo niyathanda ukuzenza: wayengumbulali kwasekuqaleni, futhi akemanga eqinisweni, ngokuba alikho iqiniso kuye. Lapho ekhuluma amanga, ukhuluma okwakhe: ngokuba ungumqambimanga, noyise wawo.

Yazini lokhu, nonke enazalelwa ekuhlambalazeni, khona lokho ukuhlambalaza uYahweh njengoba oyihlo abangenamsebenzi bazama ukuguqula isithombe sikaNkulunkulu, u-Adamu adalwa ngaso, sibe yizinengiso ezenzelwe ukubhujiswa kwesintu, izinsuku zenu sezicishe zaphela. Ngokuba yimuphi kubaphrofethi oyihlo abangamshushisanga? Futhi bababulala labo ababonisa ngaphambili ngokuza koLungileyo; enibe ngabathengisi nababulali bakhe manje. Anisoze naphunyuka, noma ngabe niya kude kangakanani emhlabeni ukucasha olakeni lwami; ulaka lwami luyonithola, ngokuba akukho okungafihlwa emehlweni ami, futhi umhlaba ungowami nokugcwala kwawo, ngokuba ngiwuthenge ngegazi lami uqobo okhokho benu abalichitha ngokuhlambalaza, ngaleyo ndlela baqinisa isiphetho senu emlilweni. Ngisho nami, uKristu Jesu, lowo owenyuswa wakhazinyuliswa, ngiyeza kini, futhi anisoze naphunyuka.

Ngizweni, O, nina eniwileyo, ngisho nonke enangihlaba, ngokuba nathi kini, simnqobile uYahweh futhi sambulala indodana yakhe ayithumele kithi ukuba inqobe umhlaba, futhi sinqobile kuze kube phakade, ngokuba uYeshua ufile, futhi nathokoza ngobuwula! Nina ziwula ezizidlayo ezingenakuqonda! Aniqondanga ukuthi uNkulunkulu wayeyongivusa, exegise izinhlungu zokufa: ngokuba kwakungenakwenzeka ukuba ngibanjwe yikho. Nilimaze isithende sami kuphela, futhi aninamandla phezu kwami, futhi aniqondanga ukuthi oPhezukonke, yena umdali wenu, uYahweh, wanibonisa izimfihlakalo ezingenamsebenzi kuphela!

Ngokuba wayazi ukuthi niyomvukela yena nendalo yakhe futhi niyozama ukuthatha lokho ayekulungisele mina, iNkosi uJesu Kristu. Abathobekileyo bami bayodla ifa lomhlaba, ngokuba umhlaba ungowami nokugcwala kwawo. Ngokuba nifundise isintu ulwazi olwenqatshelwe; nafundisa impi; nadukisa abesifazane ekuhlobiseni amajwabu amehlo ngobuso obudwetshiwe ukuze nifeze izinkanuko zenu kubo; nayonakalisa iNsimu kaNkulunkulu ngokutshala ukhula phakathi kukakolweni, futhi lonke ukhula enalutshala kuso sonke isikhathi luyosishulwa, luboshwe, luphonswe emlilweni kanye nani! Ngokuba nanqotshwa lapho ngazinikela ngokukhululekile ukuze ngisindise isintu, futhi uNkulunkulu wangivusa kwabafileyo ukuze ngingaphinde ngife!

Kodwa naningahlakaniphile kangangokuba anizange niqonde ukuthi ngangizonikela ngokuphila kwami ngenxa yothando lukaNkulunkulu aludale kimi, ngokuba ngingumsindisi wezwe! Uthando lukaYahweh alunakunqotshwa, ngokuba uYahweh akanakunqotshwa, futhi ayikho ndawo endalweni lapho kukhona isidalwa esifana naye, ngokuba unguPhezukonke, futhi ukukhanya nobukhosi bakhe kunamandla onke kusukela ephakadeni kuze kube ephakadeni! Ngokuba nganitshela, "lokhu okwenzayo kwenze masinyane!" Ngobuwula nobuqholo anizange niqonde ukuthi ngazivumela ukuba ngithathwe yizisebenzi zenu eziqashiwe ezingenamsebenzi ezenza umsebenzi ongcolile wokubulala igazi elingenacala! Ngokungazi kwenu okuhambisana nokuqhosha, nehluleka ukubona ukuthi nganginamabutho ezingelosi ayishumi nambili engangingawayala, ngokuba nganoma yisiphi isikhathi nganginganimisa. Kodwa yayingagcwaliseka kanjani intando kaYahweh, ngaphandle kothando lwami nokulalela uBaba wami? Ngokuba uthando lukaYahweh alukho ongalunqoba! Cha, hhayi ezulwini futhi hhayi emhlabeni, hhayi nganoma iyiphi indlela futhi hhayi nanini!

Nina eniwileyo, ngisho nani zikhulu zaleli zwe eliwileyo, nina boSathane, nonke niyosha emlilweni ongacimekiyo ngenxa yenzondo yenu engengcwele nezindlela zenu ezimbi zokungalaleli nokubhubhisa. Njengoba nikwenze komunye wabancane balaba, bafowethu, nikwenze kimi. O, nina balindi abawileyo, ngisho nani zingelosi ezingalalelanga ezashiya isimo senu sokuqala, aninakho ukuthula! Niyophonswa emlilweni eningasoze nasuka kuwo, futhi niyosha ngokuphelele. Lonke ukhula lwenu oluyisinengiso olungengcwele enalutshala ngokweqa imingcele kaYahweh ekuguquleni kwenu izakhi zofuzo phakathi komuntu nengelosi, phakathi komuntu nesilo, phakathi komuntu nezidalwa zasolwandle, phakathi kwezilo zezinhlobo ezahlukene, ngisho nayo yonke into enyanyekayo eniyenzile iyophonswa emlilweni kanye nani, ngokuba aninakho ukuthula noma intethelelo!

Nilandele oSathane abazama ukubhubhisa indalo kaYahweh, baze bafika ezingeni lokungibulala mina, uJesu Kristu, lapho ngazinikela ngokuzithandela ezandleni zakhe njengePhasika eliphelele lezwe, ngaleyo ndlela ngiqinisa ukuhlengwa kwesintu futhi ngiqinisa isiphetho senu saphakade emlilweni omkhulu ongacimekiyo! Niyokhokha ngokugcwele ngazo zonke izono zenu nezinengiso, futhi yonke imisebenzi emibi enake nayenza iyobuyiselwa phezu kwenu ngekhulu, ngokuba bonke bayovuzwa ngokwemisebenzi yabo. Futhi zonke izindlela zenu ezimbi ziyobuyiselwa phezu kwamakhanda enu izikhathi eziningi, ngokuba nacabanga ukuthi ninganginqoba, futhi ngokuzidla nisacabanga ukuthi ninganqoba kanti senahlulwa ngokuphelele.

Yizwani amazwi ami, O, nyoka yasendulo, ngokuba ukwahlulelwa kwakho kuphezu kwakho futhi ngiyabona ukwesaba kwakho, ngokuba wesaba ikusasa lakho ngaphezu kwanoma yini, O, wena bhubesi elingenamsebenzi elingenamazinyo! Impindiselo ingeyami, futhi ngiyobuyisela, futhi izikhathi zenu zifikile. Ulaka lukaNkulunkulu nolaka lwewundlu sekuzokhululwa njengoba ngizovula izimpawu eziyisikhombisa, njengoba kuthunyelwa izingelosi eziyisikhombisa ukuba zishaye amacilongo okwahlulela, futhi kuthunyelwa izingelosi eziyisikhombisa ezinezinhlupho eziyisikhombisa zokugcina, futhi zizokhululwa, ngokuba kuzo kugcwaliswe ulaka lukaNkulunkulu. Amashwa amaningi ayonifica nonke, njengoba ngijezisa ngokuphelele futhi ngihlakaza umbuso wenu. Inkosi yenu enkulu ngizoyinqoba nomprofethi wenu wamanga ngizomnqoba, futhi bazophonswa bephila echibini lomlilo ovutha isibabule; futhi lapho bazolinda nina ukuba nihlanganyele nabo, futhi lapho niyogwinywa umlilo nesibabule ingunaphakade, futhi ukwahlulelwa kwenu sekuzoqala!

Mlobokazi wami, ungesabi! Amazwi ami okwahlulela awekho kuwe kodwa angawokukududuza, ngokuba ngikusindisile olakeni oseluzokhululwa, futhi ngizokufihla endaweni ephephile nomvuzo omkhulu, futhi uyoba nami kuze kube phakade. Ngokuba ngikukhethile, ngokuba ngikubizile, futhi uyigugu lami elikhulu, futhi uyigugu likaYahweh elikhulu; ngisho uyiparele lenani elikhulu, ngokuba nginikele ngakho konke ngenxa yakho. Ngeluleka uMlobokazi wami ukuba afune ukuhlakanipha futhi eze kimi ukuze athole ukuhlakanipha, ngokuba ngizokufundisa. Ngokuba abanye benu balandele ezinye zezindlela zalaba baphrofethi bamanga; vele uphenduke ngokuphenduka eNkosini yakho, futhi ngizokwamukela.

Ngokuba bakuyengile ngobuthakathi bobuKristu, futhi ukholwe amanga amaningi othengiswe wona, ngisho owatshelwa njengeqiniso, kodwa angamanga ayisinengiso. Le mithombo engenamanzi izamile ukukudonsa kimi, futhi lokho akunakwenzeka, ngakho ungesabi, ngokuba angisoze nganqatshwa! Ngizoqhubeka ngikukhulula ukuze ukhululeke ngempela, futhi angisoze ngathuliswa! Ngikulwela ngezindlela eziningi kakhulu ongazi ngazo. Ngokuba nonke naphawulwa ngaphambi kokuba nizalwe, ngokuba uBaba wayenazi ngisho nasesiswini sikanina, futhi walinda omunye nomunye wenu isikhathi eside eningeke nisiqonde, futhi benilokhu nisenhliziyweni yakhe. Naphawulwa ekuzalweni, futhi izingelosi zami zanigcina ngomyalo wami futhi zanilonda zanivikela, ngokuba zithunyelwe kanjalo. Ngabe lokhu kuyakumangaza? Ngabe uzolalela lokho abamaHeberu abakushoyo? Kodwa yiyiphi kulezi zingelosi ake athi kuyo, hlala ngakwesokunene sami, ngize ngenze izitha zakho zibe yisenabelo sezinyawo zakho? Akuzona zonke yini imimoya ekhonzayo, ethunyelwe ukukhonza labo, abayoba yizindlalifa zensindiso?

Mlobokazi wami, niyizindlalifa zensindiso kimi, uJesu Kristu. Nibiziwe kimi; nivikelekile kimi futhi ngami; ninikezwe ithuba lokuzwa ukushunyayelwa kokholo ngami ukuze ningenze iNkosi yenu, ngakho-ke nginibek√© uphawu ngalowo moya ongcwele wesithembiso. Yazini ukuthi uphawu alunakwephulwa, futhi senizokhululwa ngokuphelele ningene ephakadeni, ngokuba anazi ngobugcwele bomsebenzi wokudala engisengizowufeza kini, Mlobokazi wami! Yazini ukuthi abawileyo nokhula lwabo banomhawu ngani, futhi bayakuzonda konke uYahweh akuthandayo, futhi ngizoniphindiselela ngokugcwele phezu kwamakhanda abo, ngokuba yimina oyochoboza ikhanda laleyo nyoka yasendulo, ngokuba kumenyezelwe, futhi lokho uYahweh akumenyezelayo akunakuguqulwa.

Akekho onganihlukanisa nami; akukho ububi obungakanani obungephula lolo phawu; ningabami futhi ningabakaBaba, ngokuba konke uBaba anginika khona ngiyokubuyisela kuNkulunkulu ukuze sibe yikho konke kukho konke! Futhi unginike zonke izinto ezulwini nasemhlabeni, ngaphandle kokuthi esihlalweni sakhe sobukhosi mkhulu kunami!

Futhi mina, uKristu Jesu, ngimemezela la mazwi kini, futhi yimina, uJesu Kristu, omemezela ukwahlulelwa ezitheni zenu, futhi ngizokwenza kwenzeke kuze kube yimali encane yokugcina. Mlobokazi wami, uthando lwami ngani alucimeki, futhi nginilungisele indawo, ngokuba endlini kaBaba kunezindlu eziningi! Ngakho maduzane kakhulu niyoshada nami kuze kube phakade, futhi niyoba nami ingunaphakade, futhi ekugcineni niyokuqonda ubukhulu benjongo yenu kimi enadalelwa ukuyifeza!

Ngokuba kimi, uYahweh unginike ikusasa nenjongo, ngisho nenjongo yezikhathi isizokwembulwa kini, ngokuba ngiyeza! Futhi angisoze nganqatshwa. Bhekani ukubonakala kwami. Bhekani ukukhanya kokufika kwami, ngokuba sekuzosa phezu komhlaba njengoba ngivela emafwini ngokumemeza okukhulu, ngezwi lengelosi enkulu, nangecilongo likaNkulunkulu, ngokuba nginitshela ngeqiniso ukuthi ngisho nezingelosi zilindele lelo cilongo elisizoshaywa, futhi zimi ngomumo! Nilungele mina futhi nibheke, nigade, niqaphe njengoba niyaliwe; ngokuba maduze nizongibona kanye nenkazimulo yami uBaba angembathise yona, ngokuba nami ngizonembathisa kanjalo, ngokuba niyofana nami, futhi niyongibona njengoba nginjalo.
