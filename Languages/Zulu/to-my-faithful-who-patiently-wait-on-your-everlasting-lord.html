Kuni Abathembekileyo Bami Abalindele Ngesineke INkosi Yenu Engunaphakade!

Zwanini amazwi eNkosi yenu engunaphakade, uYeshua HaMashiach uqobo, futhi nize kimi ngezinhliziyo ezilungele ukwamukela nokulindela umvuzo wenu esandleni sami, ngokuba isikhathi sesifikile ngempela. Lowo mzuzu osekukade ulindelwe lapho ngehla khona ezulwini ngokumemeza okukhulu, ngezwi lengelosi enkulu nangecilongo likaNkulunkulu (ngokuba lelo cilongo liyokhala) nabafileyo kuKristu bayovuswa bengenakonakala, nani enisekhona nisaphila niyokwenyuselwa emafwini lapho niyogqoka ukungafi, futhi kanjalo niyohlala ninjengeNkosi yenu yaphakade, kuze kube phakade.

NginguNkosi yamakhosi noMbusi wababusi, futhi yimi engathenga umhlaba ngegazi lami lokuhlawulela. Ngiqinisile, ngiqinisile, ngayikhokha inani lezono zesintu ukuze isintu sibuyisane noYahweh, ngoba mina, Jesu waseNazaretha, ngiyisivumelwano senu sokuthula esenza abantu babuyisane noNkulunkulu, kungakho ngenza ukuba uPawulu abhalele abaseKorinte athi: Ngakho-ke singamanxusa kaKristu, sengathi uNkulunkulu uyanincenga ngathi; siyanincenga esikhundleni sikaKristu, ukuba nibuyisane noNkulunkulu. Ngokuba lowo ongazange asazi isono wamenza isono ngenxa yethu, ukuze thina senziwe ukulunga kukaNkulunkulu kuye.

Ngakho-ke, bantwana bami, nginenze naba ngukulunga kukaNkulunkulu ngoKristu kini, ithemba lenkazimulo, wona lowo moya wobudodana baphakade enikhala ngawo nithi: ABHA, Baba; kanjalo nizalwa ngokusha ningamadodana kaNkulunkulu aphakade futhi nifakwe uphawu ngalowo moya ongcwele wesithembiso. Qondisisani ukuthi uphawu lukaYahweh alunakwephulwa, futhi alunakuguqulwa, nabo bonke abafakwe uphawu ngalo bavikelekile phakade; ngakho-ke qondisisani ukuthi lokho uYahweh akubhalileyo nakumemezeleyo akekho onakukuguqula. Pho, ngubani onganihlwitha ezandleni zami? Ngubani onganqoba uNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade, uYahweh uqobo, onguYise wezinkanyiso? Pho kungani nikhathazeka futhi nikhathazeke ngokuthi ngizonilahla? Nokho, yimuphi kini ongalahla abantwana bakhe, sengathi into enjalo iyindaba elula emehlweni eNkosi noMsindisi wenu, uJesu waseNazaretha?

Ngiqinisile, ngiqinisile, izipho nobizo lukaNkulunkulu azinakuhlehliswa, nalabo abaphiwe isipho sokuphila okuphakade ngokuvuma ukuthi uJesu uyiNkosi, bethemba ngaso sonke isikhathi ukuthi uNkulunkulu wangivusa kwabafileyo, baphiwa ukuphila okuphakade futhi benziwa ukulunga kukaNkulunkulu kuKristu Jesu iNkosi yabo. Manje uma umuntu enza lokho kuvuma ngenhliziyo engenakholo, ngiyagodla uphawu lukaNkulunkulu ophilayo ukuze ngibadonsele ngokugcwele kimi baze bafike ekuphendukeni okuphelele ezonweni, funa mhlawumbe imbewu yami iwele endaweni enamadwala. Kepha uma umuntu engenamoya kaKristu, akasiye owakhe. Yimi engihlola futhi ngivivinye izinhliziyo zabantu ukuze bahlanzwe emlilweni yami yokuhlambulula, ngoba yimi engihlezi nesivunguzo esandleni ngishisa amalangabi okuhlambulula, ngokuba ngingumkhandi ovunguzela amalahle ukuze nifike efeni lenu eligcwele kimi, kuKristu Jesu. Ngakho-ke, hlalani nithobekile emizamweni yami, ningamelani nokudonsa kwalowo moya wobudodana ozelwe ngaphakathi kini, oletha ukulunga nobungcwele beqiniso. Ngakho ngiyanincenga, bantwana bami, ukuba nihambe ngokoMoya ukuze ningafezi izinkanuko zenyama.

Nokho, abaningi benu bavumela izinhliziyo zenu ukuba zizikhathaze ngokungadingekile ngokuthi uphawu lukaNkulunkulu lungaphulwa ubuwula bomuntu. Ngiyanibuza, zithandwa zami? Ingabe u-El Shaddai ekwazini kwakhe kwangaphambili akazange abone ukuthi wayethumela mina, uJesu waseNazaretha, ukuba ngihlenge izoni ezaziyophinda zone ngemva kokusindiswa? Ingabe uYahweh, uNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade ongadingi meluleki nakuba izinkulungwane eziyishumi eziphindwe ngeziyishumi zizungeze isihlalo sakhe sobukhosi, nosebenza zonke izinto ngokwesiluleko sentando yakhe, akazange akuqonde ukungapheleli kuka-Adamu owonakele, yebo, bonke abantu abazalwe emhlabathini obomvu abahlanganyela egazini lika-Adamu?

Qha, bantwana bami, akunakwenzeka ukuthi uYahweh angaziqondi lezi zinto; futhi unilungiselele ngazo zonke izindlela futhi wanikeza zonke izindlela ukuze ukuhlengwa kwenu okuphelele kufike ngokugcwele kini, zithandwa zami. Ngakho hambani emthethweni wokholo nangamandla oMoya wami ozelwe ngaphakathi kini, nihambe ngokomoya wokuphila okuKristu Jesu. Ngokuba kuKristu Jesu ukusoka akusizi lutho, nokungasoki akusizi lutho, kodwa isidalwa esisha. Nabo bonke abahamba ngalo mthetho, ukuthula makube phezu kwabo, nomusa, naphezu kuka-Israyeli kaNkulunkulu. Aniqondi yini ukuthi olungileyo uyophila ngokholo? Ngakho qondisisani ukuthi akekho kwabathembekileyo bami ophelele ngalesi sikhathi, cha, kuze kube sekuhlengweni kwabo okuphelele. Futhi bonke abahlengiweyo abafakwe uphawu ngalowo moya ongcwele wesithembiso bayokona bonke, kungaba ngokwazi noma ngokungazi, usuku lonke. Ngakho-ke, zihlambululeni egazini lami lokuhlawulela nsuku zonke. Zani kimi, nivuselele ubuhlobo benu nami, nibugcine buqinile, zithandwa zami. Vumani izono zenu kimi nsuku zonke, nihlale nithobekile ezwini lami elincane elimnene lapho ngiza kini ukunilahla ngecala ngokulunga. Sengivele ngisondela kini futhi ngilapha phambi kwenu nginikhuthaza futhi ngigcina izindlela zenu zikhululekile ukuze nihambe leyo ndlela emcinyi nencane enibizelwe ukuyihamba. Futhi qhubekani nilangazelela lolo suku lokuhlengwa kwenu okuphelele lapho niyokuma niphelele phambi kukaYahweh uqobo, nembethe ukulunga engizonembathisa khona ngokugcwele, ngokuba yimi, Kristu Jesu, engizonembathisa ilineni elicolekileyo, elimhlophe, elingenabala, elingenasici, elingenamibimbi, nanoma yini enjalo.

Ngakho-ke, qondisisani, bantwanyana bami, ukuthi ukwahlulelwa sekufikile emhlabeni, khona lokho kwahlulelwa okukhulu okwaprofethwa kudala ngabaprofethi basendulo, esesifikile manje isikhathi sokugcwaliseka kwaso. Imihelo ayipheli emhlabeni manje njengoba ukwahlulela sekufikile futhi abahlengiweyo bami bayofika ekuhlengweni kwabo okuphelele. Futhi kuyokwenzeka kanjalo ukuthi izehlakalo ziyofika, igagasi negagasi. Igagasi ngalinye likhulu futhi linamandla ekubhujisweni kwalo kunelokugcina, kuze kuthi ngokungazelelwe nithathwe niyiswe endaweni ephephile, futhi ngokungazelelwe iBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo ibe ingasekho. Igagasi ngalinye liletha ukwahlulela, futhi igagasi ngalinye lishaya kanzima futhi kakhulu kunelokugcina. Zinjalo-ke izahlulelo zami nezixwayiso zami emhlabeni, ngokuba ngiza njengengonyama yesizwe sakwaJuda, futhi ngokulunga ngiza ukwahlulela nokulwa.

Futhi kuyokwenzeka ukuthi ngaphambi kokuba kufike inhlekelele yokugcina, kuyokwenzeka ngomyalo wami ukuthi abathembekileyo bami abalele kuKristu bayovuswa emathuneni abo, nabathembekileyo bami abasaphila nabaseleyo bayobuthelwa kanye nabo, niyongena ezulwini lamazulu, lapho niyokuma khona nonke phambi kwesihlalo esikhulu sokwahlulela sikaKristu, ukuze nonke namukele ngokwezenzo ezenziwe emzimbeni. Bonke abayiswa phambi kweBema yami abezi lapho ukuzokwahlulelwa ngenxa yezono zabo, ngoba namukela ukuthethelelwa kwezono ngosuku lomuntu onesono, yebo, ngalolu suku lapho umuntu onesono ebusa umhlaba, ukubusa kwakhe osekusondele ekupheleni. Ngoba umhlaba ungowami, Kristu Jesu, nokugcwala kwawo, futhi ngiyasondela, futhi ngiyothatha ngenkani ifa lami kwabahlala ngokungemthetho, nokwahlulelwa kwabo okuphelele akulibali. Zithandwa zami, qondisisani ukuthi nisukile ekufeni naya ngqo ekuphileni okuphakade kimi, uKristu Jesu iNkosi yenu. Futhi yimi enganihlambulula ngokuphelele esonweni ngegazi lami lokuhlawulela, ngoba ngiyiPhasika lenu eliphelele, engizinikele kuYahweh uNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade, kanye, konke, naphakade ngenxa yezono zesintu: ngakho-ke, akusekho mhlatshelo wezono.

Ngakho-ke, amadodana kaNkulunkulu azosondela esihlalweni sami sokwahlulela ukuze ngahlulele imisebenzi yawo, khona ukuze bonke bamukele ngokwalokho abakufezile, kungaba kuhle noma kubi. Futhi kanjalo nonke niyoletha iminikelo yokuphila kwenu okuhlengiweyo emhlabeni, enayinikela enkonzweni yeNkosi yenu yaphakade. Njengoba kulotshiwe, kuyokwenziwa kanjalo, njengoba nje kulotshiwe: Ngokuba akekho ongabe sibeka esinye isisekelo ngaphandle kwaleso esibekiweyo, esinguJesu Kristu. Kepha uma umuntu akha phezu kwalesi sisekelo igolide, isiliva, amatshe ayigugu, izingodo, utshani, noma izinhlanga, umsebenzi womuntu ngamunye uyobonakaliswa, ngokuba usuku luyokuveza, ngokuba luyokwambulwa ngomlilo, nomlilo uyovivinya umsebenzi womuntu ngamunye ukuthi unjani. Uma umsebenzi womuntu akhe phezu kwawo uma, uyokwamukela umvuzo. Uma umsebenzi womuntu usha, uyolahlekelwa, kodwa yena uqobo uyosindiswa, nokho kungathi kuphuma emlilweni.

Kanjalo kuyoba, zithandwa zami, ukuthi ngizonivuza ngokugcwele ngenkonzo yenu kimi, futhi lezo zinto ezishiswa zibe ngamakhoba yimililo yami yokwahlulela niyolahlekelwa, kodwa ngalezo zinto ezimelana nemililo yami yokwahlulela, yebo, imisebenzi engininike yona ukuba niyifeze, umvuzo omkhulu uyoba ngowenu. Ngokuba kulotshiwe: Iso alikabonanga, nendlebe ayikezwanga, futhi akukangenanga enhliziyweni yomuntu izinto uNkulunkulu azilungiselele labo abamthandayo. Sinjalo-ke isimo sefa lenu nomvuzo wenu, ukuthi akekho namunye umuntu emhlabeni oqonda ngokugcwele ubukhazikhazi nenkazimulo yefa lamadodana kaNkulunkulu aphakade azalwa ngokusha ngokomoya kaKristu, enizalwa ngawo ngokusha ningamadodana kaNkulunkulu kuze kube phakade.

Ngakho qondisisani zithandwa zami ukuthi usuku lokuhlengwa kwenu okuphelele lusondele kunalokho enikucabangayo, futhi lushesha luza kini khona manje, futhi lolo suku seluzogcwaliseka maduze, ngokushesha kunalokho enikulindele. Qondisisani ukuthi asisekho isikhathi esisele sokuxwayisa, asisekho isikhathi esisele sokunxusa, asisekho isikhathi esisele sokuzindla neBhabhiloni eliyimfihlo ngoba ukwahlulelwa kwayo kungujuqu futhi akunakuguqulwa. Cha, izikhathi zayo zokuphenduka zachithwa, futhi yawaziba ngokuphelele amazwi abaprofethi bami nabaphostoli, isho enhliziyweni yayo njengoba ihleka usulu amazwi ami ithi, "Ngihlezi njengendlovukazi futhi angisiye umfelokazi; kusasa kuyoba njenganamhlanje, nanamhlanje kunjengayizolo."

Nokho ngehora elilodwa inhlekelele yayo iyokwehlela phezu kwayo ngokugcwele, futhi ayisoze yaphinde ivuke. Kodwa esikhundleni sesizwe esake saba sikhulu esasihlonipha izindlela zikaYahweh, nalapho abantu babelandela uJesu waseNazaretha futhi bengenamahloni okuthanda omakhelwane babo, kuyosala incithakalo lapho iBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo liyochithwa ngehora elilodwa, ngoba inamandla iNkosi uNkulunkulu elahlulelayo. Futhi njengoba leyo nhlekelele isondela kakhulu zithandwa zami, kusondela kanjalo nokuhlengwa kwenu okuphelele, insindiso, umvuzo, nenjabulo yaphakade; ngakho-ke ningakhathazeki ngokwahlulelwa okusondelayo kwezoni, okungekhona okwenu uma niphuma ngokugcwele ezonweni zenu.

Kanjalo nokucwazimula kweso kuyasondela, lapho ngomzuzwana nje nizozithola nimi egumbini lesihlalo sobukhosi likaYahweh uqobo, lapho izihlalo zenu zobukhosi zihlotshisiwe futhi lapho nihlezi njengamakhosi nabapristi baphakade ngefa leNkosi yenu yaphakade, eniyobusa ngalo izizwe ngentonga yensimbi, ngokuba kunikezwe mina kanjalo, futhi ngiyokunika kanjalo labo abanqobayo. Obani abanqobi bami? Yibo labo abaphuma ngokugcwele esonweni ngokuphenduka okuphelele nokulahla izono zabo. Futhi yimi, Kristu Jesu, enganqoba ngokugcwele amaqhinga kasathane kuze kube phakade, ngamshiya eyisitha esichithiwe nesihluliwe ngokuphelele, esilungiselwe futhi esilindele amalangabi esijeziso saphakade, ngokuba linjalo ifa labo futhi bakufanele.

Ngakho-ke, ngilungisele indlela enamandla kubo bonke abeza kimi ngokugcwele ukuze banqobe ngokugcwele amaqhinga kasathane ngendlela efanayo nami. Ngakho-ke, yizani kimi ngokugcwele ukuze nginihole kuleyo ndlela emcinyi nencane eya ensindisweni yenu ephelele. Yizwani izwi lami nina enishelele futhi nawa endleleni, ngokuba umuntu olungileyo uwa kasikhombisa, aphinde avuke; ngakho vukani, madoda kaNkulunkulu, nize kimi ngokugcwele ukuze nginixhumanise kabusha nomvuzo wenu ngokunihlanza ngegazi lami lokuhlawulela, ukuze nime nembethe ngokugcwele ukulunga kwami. Qondisisani ukuthi abanqobi bami bayodla ifa lazo zonke izinto futhi abayikunqatshelwa lutho oluhle. Futhi kanjalo abanqobi bami bayokhanya phakade, njengokukhanya komkhathi; nani eniphendulele abaningi ekulungeni, niyokhanya njengezinkanyezi, kuze kube phakade naphakade. Ngakho manje njengoba nikwazi lokhu, bantwana bami, sekuyisikhathi sokushiya izinkathazo zalo mhlaba niphumule kimi, nihlale nimsulwa njengentombi egazini lami lokuhlawulela.

Ngakho-ke, zithandwa zami, manini niqine lapho nibhekene nenhlekelele esondelayo, ngoba niyalubona lolo suku olwesabekayo lusondela, futhi yimi engivule amehlo abathobekileyo enhliziyweni ukuze nibone iqiniso lomhlaba wenu futhi ninganqotshwa ukukhohliswa okunamandla okungekhona okwenu. Qondisisani ukuthi mina, uYeshua HaMashiach, nginguye owenza izimpumputhe zibone; ngakho-ke, yizani kimi ukuze ngigcobe amehlo enu ngesihlambe samehlo, futhi nibone ngokwengxenye lo mhlaba omubi njengoba unjalo ngempela eniphila kuwo. Ngakho ningesabi ukwahlulelwa kwezoni, ngokuba lokho kwahlulelwa akusibo obenu, zithandwa zami; cha, ukwahlulelwa akusibo obabahlengiweyo, ngoba nisindisiwe olakeni lweWundlu nasolakeni lukaNkulunkulu. Ngakho-ke, manini niqine futhi niphephe ensindisweni yenu, zithandwa zami, ngoba usuku luseduze futhi lushesha luza kini njalo ngomzuzwana.

Yebo, lowo mzuzu wokuhlengwa kwenu okuphelele usufikile phezu kwenu, zithandwa zami, futhi lowo mzuzu waphakade ususeduze kakhulu futhi usondela kini ngomzuzwana ngamunye. Ngikuqinisekisile emimoyeni yenu, ngibonise izibonakaliso phezulu nezibonakaliso emhlabeni, ngenze izimangaliso emazulwini, ngaze ngadedela nezinhlekelele njengesahlulelo ezonini nasekuvuseni abaleleyo ukuze baqonde ukuthi manje yisikhathi sokuhlengwa okuphelele kwabantwana bakaNkulunkulu uSomandla, yebo, umakoti wami nomzimba wami.

Ningahlanganyeli nabo ezindleleni zabo zokungakholwa, ngoba bafuna ukuzithathela udumo futhi bachaze izibonakaliso nezimangaliso eziningi eziboniswa emazulwini, eziningi zazo ezidwetshwe ngomunwe kaYahweh uqobo. Niyikhohliwe yini iGenesis, zithandwa zami? Ngokuba kulotshiwe: Wathi uNkulunkulu: Makube khona izinkanyiso emkhathini wezulu ukwahlukanisa imini nobusuku, zibe yizibonakaliso, nezinkathi, nezinsuku, neminyaka. Mazibe yizinkanyiso emkhathini wezulu ukukhanyisa emhlabeni; kwaba njalo. Ngakho-ke, qondisisani ukuthi uYahweh ubusa phezu kwakho konke futhi akukho lutho oluvunyelwayo endalweni yakhe angaluvumanga, nalokho angakwazi. Ngakho-ke, ningahlanganyeli nabo ekucabangeni kwabo okungenakholo kokuthi manje isintu sesilawula futhi siguqule amazulu, nakuba isintu sizama ngempela ukunqoba uNkulunkulu, kodwa imizamo yaso ihlale iphelela ezeni.

Nokho, abawiweyo bayazama ukufihla izibonakaliso zami kanye nezimemezelo eziningi uYahweh azibeke emazulwini zomshado womkhwenyana nomakoti wakhe. Ngakho-ke duduzelelani enkazimulweni kaNkulunkulu, nazi ukuthi noma yini abawiweyo abavunyelwe ukuyenza isebenzela kuphela izinhloso zaphakade zikaNkulunkulu ongunaphakade, ngokuba zinjalo izindlela zakhe ukuthi ngisho nezitha zakhe zilalela izwi lakhe. Kodwa ningalahleki, uYahweh ubusa ngokuphelele futhi akukho okwenzekayo angakuvumeli ngomusa wakhe, nesihe, nangesahlulelo sakhe saphakade esizofikela abangaphenduki nabawiweyo. Ngisho naleso sahlulelo esikhulu esamenyezelwa kusukela kudala, manje sesizogubha emhlabeni wonke ngendlela enodlame.

Futhi lolo suku selufikile phezu kwenu zithandwa zami, futhi NGIYEZA NGIZONILANDA! Nakuba abahlala ngokungemthetho befuna ukufihla inkazimulo yami, angisoze ngabelana ngenkazimulo yami nomunye, ngakho-ke imizamo yezinto ezibuthakathaka neyokunxiba iyize ukuze kufihleke ukubonakala kukaKristu Jesu kumakoti wami nomzimba wami. Cha, anginakuvinjwa futhi anginakufihlwa nakancane, futhi umhlaba wonke uyokwazi ngokubonakala kwami, nakuba abawiweyo beyofuna ukufihla ukubonakala kwami ngempi nangokuhlasela kwabo okungamanga kwezidalwa zasemkhathini. Qondisisani ukuthi azikho izidalwa zasemkhathini ezivela kwamanye amazwe ajikajikayo ngokungelona iqiniso, kodwa kukhona abawiweyo abavinjelwe emazulwini kuze kube phakade abazenzisayo ngoba bonke bangabazenzisi abeza ukuzokhohlisa nokudukisa.

Qondisisani ukuthi ukubonakala kwami akufani nokuza kwami kwesibili futhi kuyimfihlakalo efihlwe ngaphakathi kwemfihlakalo enkulu kaNkulunkulu, mina, uYeshua HaMashiach, engayembulela umphostoli wami uPawulu. Ukuza kwami kwesibili sekwaziwa futhi sekulindelwe iminyaka eminingi manje, futhi kuhambisana nokuvuka kwabalungileyo. Ekubonakaleni kwami ngiza ukuzobuthela kimi abangcwele bami ukuze ngibavuze futhi ngishade nomakoti wami futhi ngidle nezimenywa zami ezihloniphekile. Kusekubonakaleni kwami lapho ngizohlwitha khona ngibasondeze kimi abathembekile abeza kimi ngokuphenduka okuphelele, ngibayise ekuphepheni kwasezulwini kuze kudlule isahlulelo emhlabeni, ngoba izikhathi zokwahlulela zifike emhlabeni wonke ikakhulukazi phezu kwabawiweyo.

Qondisisani ukuthi ukuza kwami kwesibili yilapho ngiza nabahlengiweyo bami kanye nabayizi-144,000 bami, kanye nayo yonke impi yasezulwini ukuzobhubhisa ngokuphelele umbuso kaSathane, umbuso wakhe ongeke uphinde ululame kulokho kubhujiswa. Futhi ekuzeni kwami kwesibili bonke bazobona futhi bonke bazokwazi futhi akukho nkohliso engakhohlisa noma ubani kulesi senzakalo. USathane akayona inkosi futhi abawiweyo ababona onkulunkulu kodwa bangabazenzisi abazenzisayo. Futhi uSathane uzoboshwa aphonswe kwalasha iminyaka eyinkulungwane ukuze angabe esakhohlisa izizwe, kuze kube yilapho ekhululwa isikhashana sakhe, futhi uyohlala lapho eboshwe ngeketanga elikhulu ekuhlushweni nasekwesabeni okukhulu ifa lakhe kulelo chibi elivuthayo lomlilo nesibabule angeke aphunyuke kulo. Futhi emva kwesikhashana sakhe uyophonswa ngokuphelele kulelo chibi elivutha njalo lesibabule esinukayo esivutha amalangabi aluhlaza ashisa kakhulu. Khona-ke kuyokwenzeka isahlulelo sesihlalo esikhulu esimhlophe sobukhosi, ngokuqinisekile ngaphambi kokuba uYahweh adale izulu elisha nomhlaba omusha, enze zonke izinto zibe zintsha ekuphelelisweni okuphakade nokupheleleyo.

Ngakho kulotshiwe kanjalo, futhi kuzogcwaliseka maduze ukuthi ngizokwehla ezulwini ngokumemeza okukhulu ngezwi lengelosi enkulu nangecilongo likaNkulunkulu, futhi lokho kushaywa kwecilongo elinamandla akulibali futhi kuyicilongo lokugcina lenkathi yami yomusa njengoba umhlaba uphendukela ekubhekaneni nesahlulelo sawo esiseduze nesinzima. Futhi ngizobutha abakhethiweyo bami emagumbini omane omhlaba, ngisho nabo bonke abalele kuKristu, yebo bonke abalele kuKristu ababekwe uphawu ngalowo moya ongcwele wesithembiso. Futhi imihelo yokubeletha, engami, iyokwenza umhlaba ubelele labo abalele, kodwa okungenakwenzeka ukuba ukufa kubabambe nomzuzwana owodwa. Khona-ke nina eniphilayo nenisasele niyokwenyuselwa emoyeni kanye nabo ukuze nihlangane nami emafwini, futhi ngokucwayiza kweso nizobona.

Yebo, nizobona ngamehlo enu inkazimulo yaphakade kaNkulunkulu omkhulu ongunaphakade, uBaba wezinkanyiso olangazelela ubukhona benu futhi olungiselele lo mzuzu kusukela phakade ngoba benilokhu nisenhliziyweni kaYahweh. Futhi nizongibona, uMsindisi wenu waphakade, owazinikela ngokuphelele ebuhlungwini lapho ngizidela ukuze niphile phambi kukaBaba wezinkanyiso owazala izinkanyiso zomhlaba. Yebo, nizongibona ngamehlo enu, nina nonke eninokholo eNkosini yenu ekhazinyulisiwe eningakayiboni ngamehlo enu.

Nizobona ngamehlo enu inkazimulo yami ephelele uYahweh anginike yona efeni lami, engizoyabelana ngokugcwele nabanqobi bami futhi ngizoklomelisa ngayo bonke abeza kimi ngemisebenzi emelana novivinyo lomlilo. Ngiqinisile, ngiqinisile, nizobona ngamehlo enu impi yasezulwini, abaningi bayo abathunyelwe ukukhonza labo abayizindlalifa zensindiso yaphakade. Futhi niyokuma niphelele ngokuphelele, nidalwe kabusha ngokuphelela kwasezulwini, futhi niyokuma nimangale ngalokho ukuphela kwenu kwaphakade okuyokulethela kona nokuthi ingcebo yenkazimulo yefa lami kwabangcwele isho ukuthini ngempela kini.

Qondisisani zithandwa zami, (ngisho nani nonke eningathandi izimpilo zenu kuze kube sekupheleni) ngisho nani eniphumile ezonweni zenu ngokuphenduka okuphelele: Ukuphela kwenu kwaphakade kuyisiqalo senu saphakade lapho niyokuma khona emzuzwaneni wenu wokuqala wokuphila okuphakade futhi akukho lutho oluyophinde lunonakalise nakancane. Ngoba niyodalwa kabusha niphelele ebungcweleni obuphelele futhi anisoze naphinde nonakaliswe ngoba niyodalwa kabusha ningenakonakala.

Futhi niyokhonza uYahweh kuze kube phakade, futhi niyokuba nami kuze kube phakade nomaphi lapho ngiya khona. Futhi ngizonibuyisela konke okudliwe yisikhonyane nakho konke uSathane anebela kona. Niyokhanjiswa ngokugcwele ngemisebenzi yenu nomshikashika wothando eniwubonise egameni lami ngokuba nikhonze abangcwele futhi nisakhonza. Siyodla okunonileyo kwezwe ngesikhathi sokubusa kwami kweminyaka eyinkulungwane lapho niyobusa nami njengamakhosi ami nabapristi, futhi kuyoba ukubusa okulungileyo kuze kube phakade.
