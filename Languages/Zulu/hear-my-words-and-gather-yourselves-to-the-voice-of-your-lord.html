Zwanini Amazwi Ami Futhi Nizibuthisele Ezwi LikaNkosi Yenu!

Zwanini amazwi ami futhi nizibuthisele ezwini leNkosi yenu, iNkosi, uMesiya, noMpristi Ophakeme, uYeshua HaMashiach. Kungani ningabaza amazwi ami nokuhleleka kwawo kwezenzakalo, ngisho nemininingwane eyirhema yamazwi ami? Ngoba ngishilo ngokusobala mayelana neBabiloni eliyimfihlakalo nokuphela kwayo okusondelayo:

IsAmbulo 17

[17:13] Laba banomqondo munye, futhi bazonika amandla abo namandla abo esilwaneni.

[17:16] Futhi izimpondo eziyishumi owazibona phezu kwesilo, lezi zizozonda isifebe, zimshiye dengwane, zimhlubule, zidle inyama yaso, futhi zimshise ngomlilo.

[17:17] Ngoba uNkulunkulu ufake ezinhliziyweni zabo ukugcwalisa intando yakhe, nokuvumelana, nokunika umbuso wabo esilwaneni, kuze kugcwaliseke amazwi kaNkulunkulu.

Kodwa abaningi benu abanakho ukuqonda okufanele kwemibhalo futhi abaningi benu basenethemba kumuntu wemvelo. Ninethemba emakhonweni omuntu wemvelo, emandleni omuntu wemvelo, ezixazululweni zomuntu wemvelo, nasemangeni omuntu wemvelo, anifundanga yini ukuthi bonke abantu bangabaqambimanga bantabami? Ngizweni niqonde: Asikho isisombululo esinikezwa umuntu wemvelo esingaholeli kudragoni, futhi udrago uzobusa umhlaba isikhashana lapho umvimbeli esesusiwe, futhi ngisho namanje ungunkulunkulu waleli zwe, lokhu akulotshiwe yini ngokucacile emibhalweni?

Kungani nisangabaza amazwi ami, bathandwa bami nalabo engibalangazelelayo? Kholwani amazwi ami futhi niqonde ukuthi umuntu wemvelo akanamandla, umuntu wemvelo akanamakhono ngokwakhe okulwa noSathane, izixazululo zomuntu wemvelo zigcina ezeni futhi zisebenzela izinjongo zikaSathane, futhi alikho iqiniso kumuntu wemvelo nabaholi benu bezwe. Ngoba umuntu wemvelo wonakalisiwe kusukela ekuqaleni eNsimini yase-Edeni yinyoka futhi uyaphambuka esizalweni azalwe efile eziphambekweni nasezonweni, futhi ngenxa yaleyo njongo ngabonakaliswa mina, uJesu waseNazaretha, ukuze ngichithe imisebenzi kasathane kulabo abamukela umnikelo wami wokuthula nosindiso.

Bhekani! Bonke abaholi bezwe basebenzisana ngasese, futhi bonke bakhonza uSathane. Aninawo abaholi abakhethiwe, kodwa ninikezwa ukukhetha okungamanga ngoba anikaze nibakhethe labo enicabanga ukuthi niyabakhetha ngokuvota, kodwa bethulwa kini ukuze nivote ukuze bathole imvume yenu futhi imvume yenu iyisono ngoba aninakukhetha ngamabomu ububi obuncane nimele mina ngesikhathi esisodwa, lokho kungukukhohliseka. Phendukani! Phumani phakathi kwakhe futhi nahlukane! Mina, iNkosi uJesu Kristu ngolimi lwesiNgisi noYeshua HaMashiach ngesiHebheru, angizange nginikeze isisombululo sezombusazwe futhi akukho mholi wezwe oyovela ngoba anginikezanga muntu, futhi labo abakholelwa ngenye indlela bakhohliswe kabuhlungu. Abaholi bezwe bonke basebenzisana, futhi ngeke bahlukane ngoba abaholi benu bonke bakhonza uSathane kungaba ngasese noma obala. Nisafisa amazwi amahle nezinkulumo ezinhle yini? Aniqanjelwanga amanga ngokwanele yini, seniphile izimpilo zokukhohliswa kukaSathane kusukela ekuzalweni? Kungani nisafuna abaholi bezwe esikhundleni sami, uKristu Jesu? Ngoba mina ngiyiNkosi yenu, iSilo, uMesiya, noMpristi oPhakeme uYahweh amnikezileyo, futhi ngithenge umhlaba ngisho nezimpilo zabahlengiweyo ngegazi lami elihlawulayo elingenasono. Ngoba njengoMpristi wenu oPhakeme, ngazinikela njengewundlu lenu lePhasika elingenasono neliphelele. Futhi ngiyiNkosi yamakhosi neSilo samasilo, futhi ngiza njengeNgonyama yesiZwe sakwaJuda futhi ngizobusa esihlalweni sobukhosi sikaDavide emhlabeni futhi izinjongo zaphakade zikaYahweh zosindiso nokuhlengwa zizofezeka kimi lapho iNgonyama ilala newundlu eMbusweni wami ozayo nokubusa okungenakuphela.

Bhekani! Ngiyanitshela into ingakenzeki. I-Babiloni eliyimfihlo, yona le-United States eyake yasebenza njengesibani sokukhanya emhlabeni, isezinketangweni igcinelwe ukwahlulelwa ngomlilo. Ukwahlulelwa kwayo nokuchithwa kwayo kuzofika ngokuzuma futhi ngehora elilodwa izoshiswa ngomlilo, ngehora elilodwa izodwengulwa, ngehora elilodwa iziketi zayo zizogcwala amanzi kusukela empumalanga ngisho nasentshonalanga. Amabutho abomvu esilo asevele angenile emingceleni yayo futhi asevele alungiselela futhi alinde imiyalo, futhi azobulala wonke umuntu wesilisa, owesifazane, nomntwana ongakhonzi izinjongo zawo. Ngoba isilo nomprofethi wamanga abasebezokwambulwa sebevele basebenza ngokubambisana ekubhujisweni kweBabiloni eliyimfihlo, futhi sebevele bengaphakathi kwemingcele yayo.

Bobabili bangabaholi bangaphambili beBabiloni eliyimfihlo. Omunye uvela sengathi akasiye futhi uqhuba isidwedwe esidala esikhulile esikhona, kanti omunye uvela sengathi uhlushwa yisidwedwe esidala, kodwa abantu bezwe bamemezele ukuthi unguSirusi wabo futhi bamfuna njengenkosi yabo, uthi ungokhethiweyo ngoba udrago umkhethile ukuze akhohlise abantu bezwe lesithembiso, futhi uzokwakha ithempeli labo, ngisho nalapho kungekho sidingo sento enjalo ngoba mina Yeshua HaMashiach ngiyiPhasika eliphelele futhi nina niyithempeli likaNkulunkulu ophilayo, ithempeli elenziwe ngaphandle kwezandla zabantu kodwa ngamandla kaYahweh ukuze adunyiswe futhi uzonidumisa kimi uKristu Jesu iNkosi yenu!

Bhekani! Lezi zilo ezimbi nomfelandawonye wazo wezimpondo eziyishumi osumiwe sebevele bahlela ukubhujiswa kweBabiloni eliyimfihlo. Ngoba balungile emhlabeni wonke, futhi balungiselele futhi bazilungiselela emhlabeni wonke ukwehliswa kokugcina komhlaba njengoba niwazi futhi emlotheni kuzovuka uhlelo lwabo olusha, esiphithiphithini kuzovuka uhlelo lwabo lukaSathane futhi uphawu luzophoqelelwa ukuze kungene emphakathini wabo futhi abantu bakaNkulunkulu bazohlala ehlane njengasezinsukwini zikaNowa futhi bambalwa abayosinda. Ngoba isilo nomprofethi wamanga bazokulwa nabangcwele futhi babanqobe. Laba yilabo abazophuma osizini olukhulu, futhi bahlanze izingubo zabo, futhi bazenza zaba mhlophe egazini leWundlu. Ngakho-ke, baphambi kwesihlalo sobukhosi sikaNkulunkulu, futhi bamkhonza imini nobusuku eThempelini lakhe: futhi ohlezi esihlalweni sobukhosi uyohlala phakathi kwabo. Abasayikulamba, abasayikoma, nelanga aliyikubashisa, nakushisa okunye. Ngoba iWundlu eliphakathi kwesihlalo sobukhosi liyobondla, futhi liyobaholela emithonjeni yamanzi aphilayo: futhi uNkulunkulu uyosula zonke izinyembezi emehlweni abo.

Bhekani! Isitha siyeza futhi ukuhleleka kwezenzakalo kulandela: Umvimbeli uzovimba kuze kube yilapho ngibutha abathembekile eBandleni lami ukuze bangihlangabeze emafwini ekubonakaleni kwami ngeBandla lami, uMlobokazi wami. Nakuba abaningi benu bephika umbhalo ocacile ngalesi senzakalo, sizokwenzeka, futhi sizokwaziwa emhlabeni wonke. Akukho ukuhlwithwa okuyimfihlo, ngoba lawo ngamazwi abaprofethi bamanga bamaZiyoni, futhi ukubonakala kwami ngeke kuphikwe emhlabeni wonke. Bazofuna ukumboza ukuhamba kwenu ngempi nokuhlasela okungamanga kwezidalwa zasemkhathini, kodwa azikho izidalwa zasemkhathini kuphela abawileyo abazenzisayo. Lapho uMlobokazi wami ethathwa ezulwini futhi umvimbeli esusiwe ukubhujiswa okuzumayo kuzokwehla ngokuzuma emhlabeni wonke futhi zonke izizwe ezake zazenza sengathi zingamaKristu ziyophela ukuba khona ngendlela ezikhona ngayo njengamanje, kodwa kuyoba nabantu abaseleyo abazonqotshwa futhi bancishiswe. IBabiloni eliyimfihlakalo lizowa kuqala futhi ngokuzuma ngesikhathi esifanayo lapho kuqala ukuhlasela kwabo okuzumayo, kodwa ngeke liphinde livuke, ngoba uYahweh umemezele isabelo esiphindwe kabili sokwahlulelwa phezu kwalo.

Ngoba iBabiloni eliyimfihlakalo liholele izizwe esonweni esikhulu nasenkohlisweni ngobuthakathi balo kanye nemilingo yalo ngisho nokuzijabulisa kwalo okuthiwa okungokwesiko kanye nemilingo yalo enyanyekayo edluliselwe njengemithi eyiqiniso ingubuthi obubulalayo. Kanjalo-ke ukuphela kwalo kuyofika ngokuzuma futhi ngaphandle kwekhambi. Futhi kuyosala incithakalo, nakuba insali iyosindiswa, ngoba noma ubani obiza igama leNkosi futhi angathandi impilo yakhe kuze kube sekupheleni uyosindiswa.

Ngakho-ke qondani izikhathi eniphila kuzo futhi niqonde ukuhleleka kwezenzakalo okuzayo futhi niqonde ukuthi akukho lutho olungashintsha lezi zenzakalo, ngoba lokho uYahweh akumemezele akekho ongakushintsha!

Bhekani! NginguYeshua HaMashiach futhi ngithumele la Mazwi ngenceku yami, futhi ngenceku yami la Mazwi athunyelwe.

Ngizovela ngokuzuma, usulungile Mlobokazi wami?
