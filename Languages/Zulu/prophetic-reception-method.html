Indlela Yokwamukela Isiprofetho: Ngiyemukela Kanjani Imilayezo Yesiprofetho

Ngomhla zimbili kuNdasa, 2024, iNkosi uJesu Kristu yaqala ukunginika imilayezo yesiprofetho ukuze ngabelane nabantu mayelana nokuza kwayo osekuseduze kweBandla layo nezinye izihloko. Lezi ziprofetho zafika kimi ngendlela iBhayibheli eliyichaza ngayo lapho "isandla seNkosi" sehlela phezu komuntu. Kimina, kuba umuzwa ojulile wokusindwa emoyeni wami, umuzwa ongashabalali ngize ngiqede umbiko. UKristu ufaka amagama noma imishwana embalwa engqondweni yami engingakwazi ukuyixosha futhi engingayikhohlwa, futhi izikhathi eziningi ngiyaye ngizithumelele umlayezo wombhalo ukuze ngikhumbule. Lapho sengihlala phansi ngiqala ukubhala, iNkosi ingibonisa ngokoqobo amaZwi emoyeni wami njengoba ngibhala: Yebo, ngezinye izikhathi ngiba nemibono ebonakalayo yamagama, futhi izikhathi eziningi ngiyezwa amagama njengoba ngibhala, hhayi ngezindlebe kodwa emoyeni wami, futhi lokhu kuza ngendlela engenakuphikwa.

Uma ngenza iphutha ngibhala imicabango yami, amagama ayama ngize ngisuse lokho engikufakile. Uma ngizingabaza ngisusa umusho uKristu abehlose ukuthi uhlale, amagama ayama ngize ngibuyisele umusho njengoba wawunjalo, futhi lowo musho awusuki engqondweni yami ngize ngenze kanjalo. Muva nje, iNkosi inginikeze imilayezo ejulile yezimfundiso, futhi ingiyise emibhalweni ethile. Ifaka umusho emoyeni wami bese ngiyawubhala; bese ngiwusesha eBhayibhelini lami, ngithole ukuthi lowo musho usengxenyeni ethile yombhalo. Kuleso sikhathi-ke iyangikhombisa ukuthi yimiphi imibhalo okufanele ngiyicaphune, bese ngiyinamathisela endabeni, khona-ke amagama aqhubeka ngokushesha. Ngisamangala ukuthi amaZwi iNkosi enginika wona ahambisana ngokuphelele nezingxenye zemibhalo enginika zona ukuba ngizicaphune, futhi imibhalo engiyicaphunayo ihambisana nendaba ezikhathini eziningi ngendlela mina ngokwami ngingeke ngikwazi ukuyifinyelela. Angikaze ngenze into enjengale empilweni yami, kuze kube manje. Ngokuvamile angiwazi umbiko ogcwele ngize ngibuyele emuva ukuze ngihlole isipelingi nezimpawu zokuloba. Kuleso sikhathi ngiyawufunda futhi, bese iNkosi ingenza ngenze izinguquko ezincane, mhlawumbe ngifake umusho wokucacisa, mhlawumbe ngisuse igama ngifake elinye, njalo njalo. Khona-ke ukusindwa kuyasuka ngazi ukuthi sengiqedile.

Sicela uqaphele ukuthi kuyo yonke le milayezo yesiprofetho, angikaze ngiguqule lutho oluyisihloko, nakanye; futhi akukaze kube khona ushintsho olukhulu kokuqukethwe, nakanye. Iziprofetho ezinde ngokuvamile zithatha cishe ihora noma ngaphansi ukuzibhala, futhi inqubo yokucwenga inganezela elinye ihora noma amabili. Ngiyakuhlola konke ngemiBhalo ukuqinisekisa ukuthi angonanga lutho ekuqondeni kwami njengomuntu. Kuze kube manje angikakwenzi lokho, kodwa ukuze ngicacise: Amaningi alawa maZwi angaphezu kokuqonda kwami lapho ngiwabhala, futhi kudingeka ngiye emiBhalweni ukuze ngifunde kabanzi ngokusekelwe kulokho engikwamukele. Imilayezo ayikaze iphikisane nemibhalo, nakanye, nakuba iphonsela inselelo ukuqonda kwami imibhalo, nami ngingumfundi futhi. Manje, ngiyinceku kaNkulunkulu futhi ngineminyaka ecishe ibe ngama-30 ngisemiBhalweni futhi ngenze izintshumayelo eziningi, izimfundiso, nokunye. Mangisho lokhu ngolwazi lwami lweminyaka: Akunakwenzeka ukuba ngibhale noma yini yaleli zinga nobunyoninco ngesikhathi esifushane kangako ngemizamo yami nokuqonda kwami. Kungithatha izinsuku ezimbalwa, ngezinye izikhathi isonto noma ngaphezulu, ukulungiselela intshumayelo ende, enemininingwane, futhi lokhu akufani nalokho, nakancane. Lokhu akuveli kimi futhi kungaphezu kokuqonda kwami. Umoya wesiprofetho wehlele phezu kwami, ngoba ubufakazi bukaJesu bungumoya wesiprofetho.
