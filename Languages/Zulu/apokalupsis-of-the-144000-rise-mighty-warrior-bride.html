Ngase ngibona enye iNgelosi (isithunywa) indiza emkhathini, inevangeli laphakade ilishumayela kwabahlezi emhlabeni, nakuzo zonke izizwe nezilimi, nabantu, Ikhuluma ngezwi elikhulu, isithi, Mesabeni uNkulunkulu, nimnike inkazimulo, ngokuba ihora lokwahlulela kwakhe selifikile, nikhuleke kuye owadala izulu nomhlaba, nolwandle, nemithombo yamanzi. Kwalandela enye iNgelosi (isithunywa), ithi, Liwile, liwile iBabiloni lelo muzi omkhulu, ngokuba lenze zonke izizwe ziphuze iwayini lentukuthelo yokuphinga kwalo. NeNgelosi yesithathu (isithunywa) yabalandela, yathi ngezwi elikhulu, Uma ekhona okhuleka esilwaneni nomfanekiso waso, amukele uphawu lwaso ebunzini lakhe, noma esandleni sakhe, Yena lowo uyophuza iwayini lolaka lukaNkulunkulu, yebo, lewayini elihlanzekileyo, elithelwe endebeni yentukuthelo yakhe, ahlushwe ngomlilo nesibabule phambi kweziNgelosi ezingcwele, naphambi kweWundlu.
