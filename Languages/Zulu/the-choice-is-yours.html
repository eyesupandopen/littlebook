Ufakazi Ongumbhangqwana: kwa-Israyeli
Okubabayo Nokumnandi
Julayi 15, 2024

Ukukhetha Kungokwenu!

Lalelani amazwi oMsindisi wenu, iNkosi neMbusi yenu Bantwana bami, nithobele Amazwi eNkosi yenu, uJesu Kristu olungileyo, ngokuba ngiyiNdodana yokulunga futhi ngiphakama ngenkazimulo yami, nina-ke nikhule njengamathole esibayeni ngokuthula nokulondeka okukhulu, futhi nginikhulisele injongo yasezulwini esezokwambulwa ngokugcwele kini, nina bethembekile nabakhethiweyo bami. Bhekani! Nginguye ovivinya izinhliziyo zabantu ukuze nazi ngokuqinisekile ukuthi nisekukholweni ngempela yini, noma nikhohlisiwe futhi nithunjwe yizono zenu kanye nenkohliso enkulu yamanje ethiqise Izwe.

Ngokuba nginguye uYahweh ahlukanisele naye isihlalo sakhe sobukhosi, uKristu Jesu iNkosi evusiweyo nenikwe amandla, futhi ngiyiNkosi yamakhosi neMbusi yezimbusi, bhekani iNgonyama yesiZwe sakwaJuda iyeza isemafwini izokwamukela abethembekileyo bami ukuze bayiswe emazulwini emshadweni weWundlu, ngokuba uMlobokazi wami usezilungisile. Izibani zakhe zigcwele, nezintambo zazo uzilungisile, ulindele ukubonakala kwami njengentombi ehlakaniphile, labo-ke engibahlambulule ngokupheleleyo nabangenacala phambi kwesihlalo sobukhosi sikaNkulunkulu. Yebo, labo abangathunjwanga ngobuqili bukaBaraba, ngokuba uBaraba uletha udweshu olunobudlova, impi nosizi, ngokuba njengamanje uyinhloko eyodwa phakathi kwezinhloko eziningana ezisebenza ngokubambisana, kodwa uyovuka njengoMoshiach wamanga, futhi ukhethwe ngabantu bezwe ukuba abuse isinagoge likaSathane. Ngokuba uyindlalifa yalabo oyise babo abakhetha, futhi bazilungiselela ukumemukela njengeMbusi yabo.

Kodwa nina, o Bandla laseLawodikeya, manje sekufika izikhathi zenu zokuchithwa nokwahlulelwa. Ngokuba nizikhethele omunye umsindisi njengeMbusi yenu, lowo kanye okuza kwakhe kumenyezelwa ngezibonakaliso nezimangaliso zamanga nalowo oniholayo njengamanje ngemilingo nangobuthakathi. NginguYeshua HaMashiach, futhi nganithenga ngegazi lami elihlawulayo, elingenasono, futhi nginguye obeyoniphilisa, anidale kabusha ngomoya wami, ngokuba ngenziwe umoya ophilisayo njengoba uYahweh amisela iNdodana yakhe bonke ubukhulu, yona kanye engukupheleliswa kwewundlu likaNkulunkulu neNgonyama yesiZwe sakwaJuda! Ngokulunga ngiyokwahlulela ngilwe impi ngokuba iwundlu likaNkulunkulu neNgonyama yesiZwe sakwaJuda kupheleliswe kimi, uKristu Jesu, uYeshua HaMashiach, ngokuba nginguye!

Ngisazoyiqedela insindiso yenu, o Bandla laseLawodikeya, kodwa anikakulungeli ukungena eMbusweni wami ngalesi sikhathi ngoba niphambukile enjongweni yenu eyodwa kuKristu! Ngizonikhombisa iphutha lenu, futhi ninalo nakanjani ithuba lokuphenduka kuzo zonke izono, kodwa anisenaso isikhathi sokuxoxa nezwi leNkosi yenu, ngokuba lesi sono esibi singesinye seziningi uSathane azisebenzisile ukunibopha ukuze ningezi ngokuphelele kimi ngokuphenduka khona ngizokwazi ukunihlanza ngokuphelele egazini lami elihlawulayo ezonweni zenu.

Bhekani! Izikhathi zabeZizwe seziphelile futhi umnqumo wasendle owaxhunyekwa phakathi kwabo, kanye nabo uhlanganyela empandeni nasekunoneni koMthombo womnqumo, manje usuzonqunywa. Ngokuba anilalelanga aMazwi kaKristu Jesu, njengoba nganitshela ngokusobala ukuba ningazibongi ngamagatsha awela ngokungakholwa: kodwa uma nizibonga, anithwele impande, kodwa impande ithwele nina. NgiyiMpande kaJese, futhi nginguye omi njengesibani sethemba laso sonke isikhathi, lowo oletha insindiso nezindlela zikaYahweh emhlabeni futhi ngiyobusa ifa lami esihlalweni sobukhosi sikaDavide ngokulunga, ngiwuthengile umhlaba ngegazi lami elingenasono futhi ngizothatha lokho okungokwami ngokufanele ngamandla ezandleni zabahlali abangekho emthethweni, labo abawileyo.

Nginguye owanika umusa nesihe emhlabeni, ngokuba ngaphiwa nguYahweh inkathi yonke yomusa yensindiso yabeZizwe. Ngokuba izinhloso zikaYahweh kwa-Israyeli zazihlale ziwukuthi abeZizwe babusiseke bafinyelele ensindisweni ngokulalela kuka-Israyeli. Kodwa ngokuhamba kwesikhathi baba nokuzidla, nokuzigabisa, base benqunywa emthini womnqumo wemvelo ngokungakholwa. Kodwa nina, Bandla lami laseLawodikeya, niyisibonelo salolu hlobo olufanayo lokuzidla, ukungakholwa, nobumpumputhe obaboniswa ngamagatsha emvelo anqunywa ngokungakholwa, futhi manje nani nizokunqunywa emthini womnqumo wemvelo. Manje sengizoqhubeka ngiqedele umsebenzi wami emthini womnqumo wemvelo, u-Israyeli isihlobo sami nabantu bami, bonke labo engibakhethile ukuba bangamukele njengeNkosi yabo ukuze ngibadale kabusha babe muntu munye omusha nokuthi imfihlakalo kaNkulunkulu ipheleliswe, okuyisiphetho semisebenzi yami ehlawulayo yensindiso.

Nina-ke, Bandla lami laseLawodikeya, nilushiyile uthando lwenu lokuqala futhi nisezandleni zomkhohlisi enimfungile ukumkhonza. Nizikhethele uBaraba omusha njengeMbusi yenu, namala uJesu waseNazaretha njengeNkosi yenu, futhi nisezandleni zomunye umsindisi wamanga. Lowo ozenza umsindisi weBhabhiloni eliyimfihlakalo, okhuluma futhi enze njengomunye wenu, kodwa ekhuluma njengodrako. Ngokuba iBhabhiloni eliyimfihlakalo liphila ngokunethezeka nangenala enkulu yezinto ezibonakalayo, injabulo nengcebo.

Futhi iBhabhiloni eliyimfihlakalo lidakise umhlaba wonke ngokungcola kobufebe obusendebeni yalo yegolide, kodwa zinjalo izindlela zalabo abawileyo, abanikela ngokungcola okukhulu kunakho konke, nina-ke nikuphuza ngokuzithandela nangenjabulo enkulu ngoba niphuphuthekiswe yindebe yegolide ekungcoleni okukhulu enikudla ngokungcola. Ngokuba ngisho neBandla lami laseLawodikeya alisabuthaneli emibhalweni yami engcwele kodwa emibuthanweni kaBaraba, enithembele kuye. Ngakho-ke nizikhethela umsindisi omusha ngoba nithanda izwe nezinto zezwe futhi anikwazi ukucabanga ukuthi ningalahlekelwa yizibiliboco zenu eziyigugu, kepha ngizothatha yonke injabulo kini olakeni lwami olusondelayo, futhi manje ukulahlekelwa kwenu kuyanda!

Ngokuba niyizimpumputhe futhi nikhohlisiwe ngokuzidla kwenu ukuthi uYahweh anganikeza omunye umsindisi ngaphandle kwami, uJesu waseNazaretha, ngokuba ngiyinzalo ethenjisiwe yowesifazane kanti uDani uyibhubesi elincane okungeke kuvele kulo uMesiya. Ngizweni, ningizwe kahle, o bandla laseLawodikeya, ngokuba manje yisikhathi sokuphenduka kwenu esonweni sezinhliziyo zenu noma yisikhathi engizonishiya ngaso emlilweni wokucwenga, futhi bonke abakhuleka egameni leNkosi kuze kube sekupheleni bayosindiswa, nabaningi abavuma igama lami ngokungathembeki bayowela ekulibalekeni, bechithe ithuba labo ohlotsheni lwenkolo olwaphika amandla ayo: Nina baseLawodikeya, ngingamandla ayo, futhi kimi uYahweh ubeke bonke ubukhulu, nina-ke ningiphikile, iNkosi noMsindisi wenu, futhi ngizonikhafulela emlonyeni wami ngaphandle kokuba niphenduke manje!

Ngesikhathi senkathi yami yomusa, insindiso yasakazeka ezizweni, futhi izizwe ngezizwe zabelana futhi zahlanganyela empandeni yomuthi womnqumo wemvelo. Manje-ke, izizwe ngeke zisaba yisibani sensindiso futhi isikhathi sazo eNdodaneni safika ngokwenqatshwa kwezihlobo zami nabantu bami ngoba bamemeza ngokungakholwa bethi khululani uBaraba! Kodwa njengoba izimfihlakalo zikaYahweh zambulwa ngabaphostoli bami abangcwele nabaprofethi, ngimvimbezile uBaraba angamkhulula ngokugcwele, kodwa ngavumela kuphela imfihlakalo yobubi ukuba iqhubeke. Manje sekufikile izikhathi lapho ngizokhulula khona uBaraba ngokuphelele njengoba umvimbeli wami esusiwe futhi njengoba abethembekileyo bami, ababiziweyo nabakhethiweyo beyiswa emazulwini lapho ngibalungiselele khona izindawo zabo zokuhlala unomphela, futhi bayogqokiswa ilineni elicolekileyo, elingenabala, elingeke lanukubezwa noma langcoliswa.

Ingabe seniyizimpumputhe kangaka ezweni lenu, futhi niyizithulu kangaka ekuzweni kuze kube yilapho ningasalizwa izwi lami o Bandla laseLawodikeya? Nikholwe ngamanga alethwe yimfihlakalo yobubi, navumela imvubelo enobuthi ukuba ingene emaBhayibhelini enu onakele okuletha ukonakala okuphelele emaBandleni enu okungcolise izinhliziyo zenu ngezinganekwane zokungakholwa okuyiphutha. Navumela amanga ezangoma zesikhathi sokwahlukanisa ukuba zinihaye ngemibhalo yamanga nangamazwi amanga. Anisayiqondi imibhalo, futhi nizibizele izinkathazo ezinkulu ezizonehlela ngokuzumayo. Lonke iZwi likaNkulunkulu lihlanzekile futhi lenzelwe ukulalelwa kokukholwa nokwakha abemukelayo. Qondani aMazwi aMazwi ami futhi niqonde izikhathi eniphila kuzo.

Ngokuba ngenza uJohane uMambuli ukuba abhale: Ngase ngibona isilo siphuma olwandle, sinezinhloko eziyisikhombisa nezimpondo eziyishumi, naphezu kwezimpondo zaso kunemiqhele eyishumi, naphezu kwezinhloko zaso kunegama lenhlamba.

Futhi uJohane uMambuli waphinda wabhala: Ngabona owesifazane ehlezi phezu kwesilo esibomvu klebhu, sigcwele amagama enhlamba, sinezinhloko eziyisikhombisa nezimpondo eziyishumi. Owesifazane wayembethe okunsomi nokubomvu, ehlotshiswe ngegolide, nangamatshe ayigugu namapharele, ephethe esandleni sakhe indebe yegolide, igcwele amanyala nokungcola kobufebe bakhe.

Futhi futhi: Nezimpondo eziyishumi ozibonile phezu kwesilo, lezi ziyosizonda isifebe, zisichithe, zisihambaze, zidle inyama yaso, zisishise ngomlilo. Ngokuba uNkulunkulu ufake ezinhliziyweni zabo ukuba bafeze intando yakhe, bavumelane, banike isilo umbuso wabo, kuze kugcwaliseke amazwi kaNkulunkulu. Nowesifazane, ombonileyo, unguloya Muzikazi omkhulu obusa amakhosi omhlaba.

Bantwana bami, qondani ukuthi isilo singasho abantu ngabanye kanye nemibuso, fundani ukuze nazi. Isilo esithwele isifebe siyosibhubhisa isifebe futhi ngeke sisaba ngaphansi kwesifebe, ngoba sibuse amakhosi omhlaba ngobufebe baso. Ngakho isilo esizophuma olwandle siyokhipha uMahdi wabo olindelwe, kodwa futhi siyobhubhisa ngokuphelele iBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlakalo, ngoba uNkulunkulu unikele isifebe esikhulu ezandleni zezitha zaso esezivele zisizonda kakhulu.

Ngaphambi kokuba isilo sasolwandle nesilo sasemhlabeni zivuke ngokugcwele eMbusweni kadrako, kunababusi abaningi abahlangene ngenjongo yokunika amandla abo nokubusa kwabo kudrako, ngoba uNkulunkulu ukufakile ezinhliziyweni zabo ukuba bakwenze lokho. Futhi bayobhubhisa iBhabhiloni eliyimfihlakalo. Nicabanga ukuthi seniyiqonde kahle imibhalo yami engcwele ngoba nikholwe yizazi zenu zezimfundiso zamanga? Qondani nizwe Amazwi eNkosi yenu!

Futhi, ngenza uJohane uMambuli ukuba abhale: Nesilo engasibona, sasifana nengwe, nezinyawo zaso zinjengezebhere, nomlomo waso unjengomlomo wengonyama: nodrako wasinika amandla aso nesihlalo saso sobukhosi, negunya elikhulu. Ngase ngibona enye yezinhloko zaso sengathi ilinyazwe yafa, kodwa inxeba layo elibulalayo laphola, nomhlaba wonke wamangala, walandela isilo.

Bantwana bami, amazwi kaYahweh anokujula okunezinhlangothi eziningi, okungekho muntu ongakuqonda kahle. Isilo esichazwayo siwumbuso oyovuka ekubhujisweni kwesifebe, futhi ohlelweni oludala kuzovuka uhlelo olusha! Simele isilo sasolwandle, ngoba siyovuka njengoMahdi wabo, futhi simele uhlobo lobukhosi lapho siyobusa khona ulwandle. Kungani ningawahloniphi amazwi eZwi lami? Akushiwo ukuthi isilo sasolwandle sasilinyaziwe, ngoba yimuphi umuntu onezinhloko eziyisikhombisa ngokoqobo? Kodwa enye yezinhloko zaso sengathi ilinyazwe yafa. O, ziwula, nani eniphuzayo ngenhliziyo ukukholwa konke okwakhulunywa ngabaPhrofethi! SENGAthi ilinyazwe yafa. Amazwi athi "sengathi" bekufanele anivimbe, kodwa anisakholwa ngisho namazwi ami amancane, futhi mina ngiyiZwi elaba yinyama. Akucaci yini kini, ukuthi SENGAthi, kuyisisho sakudala? Akucaci yini kini ukuthi "sathi" kungokwesimo sokucabanga? Akucaci yini kini ukuthi kuwukufushanisa kokuthi, kube sengathi kunjalo?

Kodwa cha, nikholwa ngamanga kadrako, ngoba uBaraba ukhuluma njengodrako, kodwa akayena uBaraba, kodwa uyisilo sasemhlabeni. Manje-ke umhlaba wonke uzomangala ngesilo ngenxa yezibonakaliso nezimangaliso zaso zamanga, futhi sisenokwenza ezinye izibonakaliso nezimangaliso zamanga, futhi asikavuki ngokugcwele ngoba isifebe asikashiswa ngomlilo, kodwa manje sesembuliwe esikhathini saso. Ukhuluma njengodrako, ethi "uNkulunkulu kuphela owamsindisa enxebeni lekhanda lakhe" futhi lawa ngamanga ahlambalazayo. UYahweh, uNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade, ungithumile mina Jesu waseNazaretha njengomsindisi wezwe. Mina noBaba wami simunye ebunye nasenjongweni futhi uYahweh akamsindisanga uBaraba futhi nami angimenzanga kanjalo, uKristu Jesu. Nikhohlisiwe nathathwa futhi nathiyeka enkohlisweni enkulu, futhi ngithi kini phendukani nibuyele othandweni lwenu lokuqala futhi nikwenze lokho manje, ngoba asisekho isikhathi!

Ngokuba nginguye ovivinya izinhliziyo, futhi nginguye ovivinya bonke engibabizela ensindisweni, ngoba ngicwenga bonke emlilweni wokucwenga! Bhekani! Nginguye ohlezi njengomcwebi nomhlanzi wesiliva: ngicwenga amadodana kaYahweh, ngiwahlunga njengegolide nesiliva, ukuze anikele eNkosini umnikelo ngokulunga. Aniqondi yini ukuthi ngizonenza abapristi namakhosi phambi kukaBaba? Nakuba isipho sami somoya ongcwele, enizalwa ngaso kabusha ngembewu engonakali siphelele futhi asidingi kucwengwa, nivela ku-Adamu ngegazi futhi kufanele nivumele ngokugcwele umsebenzi wami ukuba wenziwe ezimpilweni zenu, ngaphandle kokuba imbewu yami iwele endaweni enamatshe.

Manje kufika izikhathi zokwahlulelwa futhi izikhathi zomusa seziphelile. Nakuba abaningi beyohleka amazwi ami futhi bafulathele ngokungakholwa, sekwaziwa kakade nguYahweh umphumela. Ngokuba uYahweh uhlale efakazela esintwini izindlela zaso ezonakele futhi asole isintu ngesono, azi ethandweni lwakhe nasekuhlakanipheni kwakhe kwaphakade ukuthi uyosindisa abanye. Futhi njengoba uYahweh enza lukhuni inhliziyo kaFaro, kanjalo uzokwenza lukhuni izinhliziyo zabaningi abezwayo kodwa abangalaleli. UYahweh uzovivinya umuntu kaninginingi. Futhi abanye bayoba njengoFaro benze lukhuni izinhliziyo zabo ngokwengeziwe ekungakholweni kodwa abanye bayoshiya izindlela zikaFaro bavukele ekuphileni okuphakade kuKristu Jesu, kepha ngomlilo wokucwenga.

Qondani ukuthi labo abakhetha uBaraba bazozihlanganisa namacala kaBaraba, nalabo abenqaba ukubiza igama lami kuze kube sekupheleni bonke bayolahlwa ngoba babenamajabulo ekungalungini. Ngokuba umsindisi wenu omusha akasiye umsindisi kodwa uyisilo esihahayo. Futhi uhlelo lwakhe nombuso wakhe wakhiwe yilabo abalimaza abantwana. Aniziqondi yini izindlela zikaDrako? Ngokuba labo abalandela izindlela zikadrako bahlukumeza abantwana. Banikela ngabantwana kuMoloki, bafuna umthombo wabo wobusha egazini labantwana abahlukunyeziwe, bahlukumeza abantwana ukuze bazanelise ngokugula kwabo, futhi benza umkhuba wokushaya umbungu esiswini ukuze babulale abancane bengakazalwa, futhi baningenele ngasese ezindleleni zabo, ngoba zinjalo izindlela zabaholi beBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo kanye nombuso kadrako. Futhi manje kufika ukwahlulelwa kukaNkulunkulu ngalezi zinto nezinye eziningi. Ngokuba ukuba beningazibonanga lezi zinto, beningeke nibe nesono, kodwa manje seniyabona nesono senu sihlezi.

Anifundile yini? Usho kanje uJehova, Kwazwakala izwi phezulu, ukulila nokukhala okubuhlungu. URakheli, ekhalela abantwana bakhe, wala ukududuzwa ngabantwana bakhe, ngoba babengekho. Ngokuba nehlulekile emsebenzini enanikwa wona, futhi naphathiswa izimfihlakalo zami futhi nema njengokukhanya kwensindiso nesibani sethemba. Kodwa manje senihlangene nalabo enganithuma ukuba nime ngokumelene nabo. Ngakho-ke manje kufika izikhathi engizonishiya kuzo nekusasa lenu.

Ngizweni ningizwe kahle nonke eningala izwi likaYeshua HaMashiach, izwi likaKristu Jesu: anisenaso isikhathi! Ngiyaneluleka ukuba ningizwe othandweni lwami ngani bantwana bami futhi niphenduke manje! Ngokuba umlilo wokucwenga usuzonehlela ngokugcwele, futhi uzohlanza izwe kulo lonke umbuso kadrako futhi umhlaba ngeke usaba ngaphansi kwezindlela zikaSathane futhi umbuso wakhe ngeke uphinde uvuke, ngoba ngiyiNkosi yamakhosi neMbusi yezimbusi, futhi ngizobabhubhisa ngokuphelele abawileyo. Bonke abenqaba ukuphenduka bayoba nengxenye yabo echibini lomlilo futhi bahlanganyele esijezisweni sikasathane nezingelosi zakhe kulelo chibi elikhulu lesibabule esivuthayo. Ngokunjalo, bonke abaphendukayo ngokuphuma esonweni nangokubiza igama lami kuze kube sekupheleni bayosindiswa futhi banikwe umvuzo omkhulu waphakade kimi.

Bhekani! Manje yisikhathi sokubuthwa kwabathembekileyo, futhi manje ngizoshiya labo abenqabayo kuBaraba, ngoba sebekwenzile ukukhetha kwabo.
