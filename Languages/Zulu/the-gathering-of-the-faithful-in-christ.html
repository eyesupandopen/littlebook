Ubufakazi Obumbaxambili: Israyeli
Okubabayo Okumnandi
Novemba 10, 2024

Ukubuthana Kwabathembekileyo KuKristu!

Bhekani, Bhekani! Qondisisani lokho osekuzodedelwa emhlabeni.

Bhekani, Bhekani! Impela ulaka lweWundlu luphezu kwakho Bhabhiloni eyimfihlakalo. Njengoba mina, uYeshua HaMashiach, ngiye ngaxwayisa, kanjalo kuyokwenziwa!

Bhekani! Bhekani! Usuku selufikile phezu kwakho Bhabhiloni eyimfihlo, nosuku lwakho lokwahlulelwa selufikile, usuku impela oluyokwembula lolo suku olukhulu olwathenjiswa kwasemandulo, usuku lweNkosi! Maye kini enakhileyo emhlabeni ngoba ulaka olukhulu seluzodedelwa ngokugcwele phezu kwenu, aniyikubaleka futhi impela, impela, isonto lamashumi ayisikhombisa likaDaniyeli selifikile.

Ngalokho ngeluleka ibandla lami, umzimba wami kanye nomakoti wami ukuba nizibhince ngokulunga kwami futhi nizilungiselele ukuma niqotho phambi kweNkosi yenu, khona kanye esihlalweni sobukhosi sikaYahweh, lapho ngiyokwethula khona ulibo, bonke labo engiyokubutha abalele kuKristu futhi bephumule belindele ukubizwa kwami, kanye nabo bonke abaphilayo emhlabeni abakanye noKristu ngokwethembeka ngesikhathi sokubonakala kwami. Ngendlela efanayo naleyo engazethula ngayo kuYahweh njengolibofuzo kwabafileyo, ngayo futhi nani nifaniswa neNkosi yenu yaphakade Yokulunga. Ngoba ngendlela efanayo enganyuselwa ngayo emazulwini, kanjalo nani niyonyuselwa emazulwini, lapho ngiyokwethula khona ulibo lwami lokuvuna olungenabala noma umbhidli nanoma iyiphi into enjalo.

Niyokuma nonke phambi kwesihlalo sami sokwahlulela, sona leso sihlalo esikhulu sokwahlulela lapho imisebenzi yenu iyokwahlulelwa khona, abanye bathole umvuzo, abanye balahlekelwe, kanti abanye bayothola kokubili ukulahlekelwa nomvuzo lapho ukhuni, utshani, nezinhlanga kushiswa ngoba akekho kini owahamba ngokuphelele phambi kwami, kodwa othandweni lukaYahweh niyomangaliswa kakhulu ubukhulu befay lenu. Kanti kwabanqobi bami, umvuzo wenu mkhulu futhi niyokudla ifa lazo zonke izinto njengoba nami ngenza lapho nginqoba abawileyo. Nokho, bonke ababuthiselwe eNkosini yabo yaphakade, uYeshua HaMashiach, bayofika ebuntshani bempilo njengoba bedalwa kabusha kuze kube phakade, anisoze nakonakaliswa noma niguqulwe ekupheleleni okubalindele, ngoba uYahweh ushilo kanjalo!

Futhi niyothathwa ngokuzumayo, nezwe liyokwazi ukuthi ngibonakele, nomvimbeli uyosuswa! Futhi ekungabini khona komvimbeli wami, wonke amandla nomthelela wobubi uyodedelwa ngokugcwele emhlabeni. Yimina, uYeshua HaMashiach, uKristu Jesu, osho la Mazwi, ngakho-ke la Mazwi ayokwaziwa emhlabeni, futhi ayothola amehlo, izindlebe, nezinhliziyo zalabo athunyelwe kubo.

Kunjalo-ke, futhi kuyoba njalo, kodwa ngesikhashana esifushane kakhulu umbuso kaDrako uyovuka ngokugcwele, ngoba lokho banikwe ukuba bakufeze. Ngoba lapho ngaliwa yizihlobo zami, wona uIsrayeli engingumhlengi wakhe oyisihlobo, ngaphendukela kwabezizwe ukuze izizwe zibusiswe, njengoba uYahweh athembisa kwasemandulo. Futhi Amazwi ami amukelwa ngamandla, naphezu kokushushiswa okukhulu nezilingo eziningi, namasango esihogo awazange alihlule iBandla lami. Nokho, noma kunjalo, njengoba iBandla lami lisuswa, nomvimbeli wami esuswa, ububi buyogcwala umhlaba wonke, abayikubaleka.

Bonke abathokoza ngokungalungi nalabo abazimisele ukulahla uJesu waseNazaretha bekhetha uBharabha, nalapho bethi ukuthula nokulondeka, khona-ke kuyofika ukubhujiswa okuzumayo, futhi abayikubaleka. Kodwa uDrako uyoziveza, futhi uboshelwe emhlabeni, nasemazulwini uxoshiwe unomphela, ngeke aphinde abuye, futhi unolaka olukhulu ngoba uyazi ukuthi isikhathi sakhe sifushane, kodwa ngobuwula ucabanga ukuthi usenganginqoba mina Yeshua HaMashiach, yize ngamnqoba ngokuphelele eKhalvari emisebenzini yami yokuhlawulela yensindiso eyagcina ngokuthi, Kuphelile!

Futhi abawileyo ngandlela thize bacabanga ukuthi bangabalekela ukwahlulelwa kwabo okwathenjiswa kudala ngoba besaba ikusasa labo ngaphezu kwanoma yini; ngoba bayaqonda ukuthi onkulunkulwini babo ababonisanga futhi ababonisi sihe. Ngoba bayazi ukuthi ababonisanga sihe, ngakho-ke, abayikuboniswa sihe. Bazi kahle ukuthi zonke izenzo zabo ezingalungile nezinengekayo ziyobuyiselwa kubo ingunaphakade ngokuphindaphindiwe kuleso chibi esikhulu somlilo wesibabule lapho amalangabi esijeziso engeke acinywe khona. Amalangabi adalelwe ukubuyisela yonke imisebenzi emibi abayenzile ezifubeni zabo, futhi bayokhokha ngokuphelele, babe lusizini olukhulu kuze kube phakade.

Ngoba bahlasela uYahweh futhi bahlasela insimu kaNkulunkulu, njengoba bezenza onkulunkulu, futhi ukwahlulelwa kwabo sekufikile. Nokho kunjalo, abawileyo balalise izixuku ngobuchwepheshe babo, namagagasi, nangenjabulo yesono, nangenkokhelo yokungalungi. Nokho, kunjalo ukuthi abantu bathanda ubumnyama kunokukhanya, ngakho manje, ngiyobanika ubumnyama ababufisa kangaka.

Ngoba yonke injabulo izosuswa emhlabeni, nezimpilo zokuchuma kwesono nokweqisa ngeke ziphinde zibonwe emhlabeni ngendlela efanayo lapho abantu bezibekelela ingcebo futhi belahla umakhelwane wabo ebugovwini nasekungilungini kwabo, beklelisa abaswele njengoba bebadlula endleleni yabo yokuqongelela ingcebo eyengeziwe. Umuntu wemvelo ucwile kakhulu enjabulweni yesono kangangokuthi ngeke aze aphakamise nomunwe ukusiza abaswele, ngakho nami ngeke ngiphakamise munwe ukumsiza abalekele ukwahlulelwa kwakhe ngoba umuntu wemvelo manje usenikelwe ezandleni zezitha zakhe. Kanjalo-ke lezi zinto ngeke zisinde esontweni lamashumi ayisikhombisa likaDaniyeli futhi ngeke ziphinde zenzeke ngaleyo ndlela embusweni wami wokulunga!

Nokho kuyoba njalo ukuthi lapho umvimbeli esuswa, ububi obufihlekile esintwini ngenxa yamaqhinga enyoka manje buyokhululeka, nobuhlanya buyogcwala umhlaba njengoba umzalwane ezobulala umzalwane, nabantu bezwe balwe nabantu bezwe labo, nesiphithiphithi esikhulu nempi kuyogcwala umhlaba. Kodwa lesi sigameko siwukubhujiswa nokwahlulelwa kweBhabhiloni eyimfihlakalo, izimpawu zizovulwa, nabagibeli bamahhashi bayogibela!

Ngakho-ke ukuhlubuka okukhulu kuyenzeka njengoba kwabikezelwa. Ngoba ngesikhathi sokubonakala kwami, lapho abaningi bekufanele ngabe belindile futhi bezihlanza Ezwini lami nasemoyeni wami, futhi esikhundleni sokuqedela insindiso yabo ezingalweni zami zothando, esikhundleni salokho balahla iNkosi yabo, uKristu Jesu, bekhetha uBharabha abamnike izinhliziyo zabo. Njengoba nje noPontiyu Pilatu, owayezimisele ukungikhulula emangeni abo angewona awobuFarisi nasekusolweni kwabo, wanikeza abantu bezwe ithuba lokukhetha. Wase ethi kubo uPilatu lapho sebebuthene, Nithanda ukuba nginikhululele bani, uBharabha, noma uJesu othiwa uKristu? Bathi, uBharabha. Wathi kubo uPilatu, Khona-ke ngiyokwenzani ngoJesu othiwa uKristu? Bathi kuye bonke, makabethelwe.

Lokhu kwenzeka ngoba u-Esawu wasebenzisa umthelela wakhe omubi kubantu ukuze bazithathele umbulali esikhundleni salowo owayethunyelwe insindiso yabo, uJesu waseNazaretha! Kodwa isijeziso somthetho kaMose sasiyogcwaliseka kanjani ngaphandle komhlatshelo wami engazidela kuwo ngobuhlungu obukhulu njengewundlu likaNkulunkulu, lapho ngazinikela ngenxa yezono zomhlaba njengompristi omkhulu kuze kube phakade ngokohlelo lukaMelkisedeki. Noma kunjalo, ekwazini kwangaphambili kukaYahweh wayazi ngokugcwele ukuthi uIsrayeli uyomlahla uMesiya wakhe weqiniso, ngakho wasebenzisa inzondo nomona wabawileyo ukuba imelane nabo, ngoba ukube babeyazi imfihlakalo enkulu kaNkulunkulu babengeke neze bayibethele iNkosi yenu yaphakade yeNkazimulo!

Nokho kunjalo, ukuthi uMelkisedeki iNkosi yaseSalema waletha isinkwa newayini: futhi wayenguMpristi kaNkulunkulu Osenyangweni Ephakeme. Kanjalo u-Abrama wenza iminikelo yakhe njengoba kwakudingeka ukuhlonipha uYahweh, kanjalo uMelkisedeki uwuhlelo lokholo olwaluyisithunzi sezinto ezizayo. Ngoba njengoba kulotshiwe ngo-Abrahama othembekileyo owaphila ngokholo kuNkulunkulu Osenyangweni Ephakeme, nangesibonelo sakhe olungileyo uyophila ngokholo kuze kube phakade! Kanjalo-ke abezizwe babusisiwe futhi bahlanganiswa ezizweni phakathi kukaIsrayeli kaNkulunkulu, futhi bahlanganyela ngokugcwele ezithembisweni ezenziwa koBaba ngoba bamukelwe nguYahweh uqobo ngesandla sami esinamandla, ngakho-ke lokho uNkulunkulu akuhlanzileyo ungakubizi ngokuthi kungcolile, O Israyeli!

Nokho kunjalo ukuthi usuku lwami selufikile. Futhi ngeke ngabelane ngenkazimulo yami nomunye, futhi uNkulunkulu uthumele inkohliso enamandla ukuze bakholwe amanga. Ngoba labo abalandela uBharabha benza kanjalo ngenxa yezinkanuko zabo ngoba bacabanga ukuthi bayingxenye yesikhathi sakhe segolide esithenjisiwe okuyinto empeleni engeyona eyabo, nakuba eqamba amanga. Ngoba inkathi yegolide ayithembisayo isuselwe enkathini yegolide yabawileyo ezinsukwini zikaNowa lapho abawileyo babebusa umhlaba ngokungalungi! Ngakho-ke, lapho uBharabha ekhuluma ngenkathi yegolide ukhuluma iqiniso ngenhloso yezinhliziyo zabo, ngaphandle kokuthi bafuna ngokugcwele ukubhujiswa okuphelele kuka-Adamu ukuze amanyala abo nokhula kunqobe insimu kaNkulunkulu kuze kube phakade. Kunjalo-ke, manje usuyaqonda ukuthi kungani kungekho nyama eyayiyosindiswa iphila ukube uYahweh akazange azifinyeze lezo zinsuku.

Nokho, ngeke babe nayo inkathi yegolide ngoba ngiyinzalo ethenjisiwe yowesifazane eyochoboza ikhanda lenyoka unomphela, futhi ngiyochoboza ngokuphelele umbuso wayo wonke, ngeke ume. Ngakho esikhundleni senkathi yabo yegolide abayifisayo, bayothola ukwahlulelwa okunzima futhi umbuso wabo uyobhujiswa phambi kwabo ungasaphinde uvuke, izimpawu ziyovulwa, amacilongo ayokhala, insali yami iyofihlwa bese kuthululwa izitsha ezinolaka olugcwele lukaNkulunkulu, babe sekuhlushweni okukhulu nasosizini olukhulu njengoba bebona ukunqotshwa kwabo kusondela ngokugcwele.

Ngakho-ke, labo abalandela uBharabha ngobuwula njengoba yena ngobuwula enza sengathi uthunywe nguYahweh, azi kahle ukuthi ungowedrako esikhundleni salokho, futhi nguye akhethelwe ukuhola izwe ngisho nomhlaba nesilo sasolwandle. Nokho kunjalo, abaningi abahlubukele ezingalweni zalo mzenzisi futhi balahla uKristu Jesu manje bazonikelwa ezandleni zakhe njengoba abathembekileyo bethathwa. Futhi kukhona abangasola mina ngokulahla iBandla lami, kanti empeleni yilabo abangekho kuKristu, abangakaze bafinyelele ensindisweni ephelele, abangilahlayo ngokuhlangana noBharabha, futhi ngabaxwayisa ukuthi kufanele bahlale emvinini ngoba ngaphandle kwami, aninakwenza lutho.

Yize abaningi abamagama abo ayengakaze alotshwe eNcwadini Yokuphila YeWundlu beyobhubha emhlabeni lapho ukwahlulelwa kwabo kufika, futhi ngokuzumayo amathemba abo ayoshayeka phansi ngokuphazima kweso.

Ngoba babengenalo ithemba lokulunga!

Ngoba babengenalo ithemba lokulungisiswa!

Ngoba babengenalo ithemba lokungcweliswa!

Ngoba balahlekelwa yithemba lonke lokubuya kweNkosi yabo kubo njengoba ngangithembisile eTestamenteni lami Elisha, futhi zonke izithembiso zikaYahweh noYeshua HaMashiach ziyogcwaliseka kuze kube phakade!

Ngoba inkathi yami yomusa nesihe isiphelile, nakuba umusa nesihe kunami kuze kube phakade, futhi njengoba kulotshiwe kuyokwenziwa ukuthi bobabili uYahweh noYeshua HaMashiach bayoba nesihe kulowo esiyoba nesihe kuye, ngoba ubani ongamela isihlalo sobukhosi sikaYahweh ngoba unguNkulunkulu Osenyangweni Ephakeme futhi akekho onjengaye! Ngakho-ke qondani ukuthi ulaka selulungisiwe futhi seluzodedelwa emhlabeni njengoba imihelo yami yokubeletha imemezele futhi yabikezela, futhi lolo suku selufikile!

Ngakho-ke ngimemezela ukubonakala kwami, futhi kuyokwenzeka njengoba kulotshiwe emibhalweni, futhi umakoti wami uyohlanganiswa unomphela esivumelwaneni nomkhwenyana wakhe, nabo bonke abathathwe baya emazulwini ngesikhathi sokubonakala kwami, noma bephila noma befile, bayodalwa kabusha kuze kube phakade ebuntshani obusha bempilo yaphakade. Nabo bonke abazinikele ngempela emazwini amahle nasezinkulumweni ezinhle zikaBharabha bayowa ekuhlanganeni kwami njengamaqabunga abunile esonweni. Nokho, abaningi abawayo bayoza kimi ngezilingo ezinkulu nokuhlupheka, futhi iningi labo liyobiza igama lami kuze kube sekupheleni, nesixuku esikhulu kakhulu siyophuma esontweni lamashumi ayisikhombisa likaDaniyeli singene ekuphileni okuphakade, nakuba abaningi beyothatha uphawu lwesilo futhi bawele emalangabini unomphela.

Ngakho-ke, mina, uKristu Jesu, iNkosi yenu evukile neyanyuswa, ngiyala abathembekileyo bami ukuba baphume ngokuphelele phakathi kwabo futhi bahlukane nabo ngokuphelele. Zilungiseleleni, zithandwa zami! Ningahlanganyeli emibhikishweni yabo, gwemani izixuku zabo, futhi ngazo zonke izindlela gwemani ububi obuzibuthela ngaphansi komoya wamanga wokuthula ngoba ukuhlasela kwabo sekulungile. Qondani, zithandwa zami, ukuthi ningabe ningena ogibeni lwabo uma ningahlangana nabo ngaleyo ndlela. Zithandwa zami, sekuyisikhathi sokulahla izinkathazo zalo mhlaba, ngoba izimpilo zenu zifihliwe kimi, uKristu Jesu, futhi sezizokwambulwa ngokugcwele ukuze nazi njengoba nani naziwa.

Ngakho-ke qondani inkohliso ephezu kwenu, zithandwa zami, nizicashe nilindele ukubonakala kwami, ngoba kuseduze! Qondani inkohliso yabo, ukuthi iwundlu elinezimpondo ezimbili liyokhohlisa ngenxeba lalo lasekhanda, futhi liyobonakala livuka kwabafileyo futhi selulekile enxebeni lalo lasekhanda, kodwa konke elikunikezayo njengesiqinisekiso sezimangalo zalo zamanga zokuthi linguMoshiach wabo kuyizibonakaliso nezimangaliso zamanga, futhi akusiye uYahweh uMINA OMKHULU, noma mina, uYeshua HaMashiach, esimthumile lo mkhohlisi, nakuba evunyelwe ukuba eze ngesikhathi sakhe esimisiwe ukuze abuse nesilo sasolwandle nedrako njengenhloko yabo, ngoba lokho banikwe ukuba bakufeze ohlelweni lwaphakade lukaYahweh lokuhlenga u-Adamu!

Ngakho-ke nginitshela zithandwa zami ukuba ningilinde ngokubekezela futhi ningawakholwa amanga abo ngoba akekho noma akukho okungavimba ukubonakala kwami kwabathembekileyo eBandleni lami, nakuba beyofuna ukusitha lesi sigameko ngokuhlasela kwamanga kwabafokazi nempi yomhlaba eyodlula zonke izimpi ezake zaba khona emhlabeni. Nokho, impi yabo iyoba buthaka uma iqhathaniswa nempi yalolo suku olukhulu lapho beyobhujiswa khona ngokuphelele lapho ngifika emhlabeni namabutho ezulu, ngoba ngokulunga ngiyeza ukwahlulela nokwenza impi!

Mina, uYeshua HaMashiach, ngithumele la Mazwi ngenceku yami eyisithunywa sami esithembekileyo, futhi yimina, uYeshua HaMashiach, oqinisekisa lobu bufakazi obunikezwe eVangelini Laphakade elambuliwe ngesikhathi salo.

Nginguye manje ozophendukela ku-Israyeli ukuba ngibahlenge emhlabeni njengomhlengi wabo oyisihlobo, futhi ngiyanibiza nina Israyeli ngoba sekuyisikhathi sokwamukela uMesiya wenu weqiniso, ngoba ngendlela efanayo lo mkhohlisi azokhashela futhi abhubhise iBhabhiloni eyimfihlakalo, ngendlela efanayo uzokhashela abantu bezwe esinyanyisweni sakhe sencithakalo esondelayo.

Ngakho-ke qondani ukuthi bayokhahlela baya emihumeni yabo ukuze bazicashe emadwaleni omhlaba futhi abaningi bayobhubhela khona. Ngakho ngiyabuza, umuntu angacasha kuphi ukuze abalekele ukubuka kwami kwaphakade, noma ubani ongabalekela ukubona kukaYahweh ngoba konke kuyabonakala futhi kuyaziwa, nanoma yini eyenzeka emhlabeni ivunyelwe ukwenzeka noma isekwahlulelweni noma isensindisweni yalabo abayobiza igama lami kuze kube sekupheleni noma yalabo abangamukelayo njengoMesiya wabo, nabo bonke abenza okubi bayoklonyeliswa ngokugcwele.

Futhi nginguye oyovela ngokuzumayo esibhakabhakeni ngenkazimulo enkulu futhi bayokwazi futhi abaningi bayoqonda ukuthi baphuthelwe yilesi sigameko esisodwa ababeqiniseke ngaso ukuthi bayingxenye yaso, kodwa banqatshiwe ngenxa yezimpilo zabo ezigcwele isono ngoba isono asisoze sangena emazulwini noma same phambi kobuso bami.

Bhekani! Izikhathi zifikile ngakho manje ngizothuthela ekubutheni abathembekileyo.

Bhekani! Bukani! Lalelani! Nilungile yini zithandwa zami? Usuku selufikile phezu kwenu.
