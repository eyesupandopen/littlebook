Kungani Nizilahlile Izimfihlakalo Zami?

Kungani nizilahlile izimfihlakalo zami, O bandla laseLawodikeya elisivivi? Kungani niyilahlile imfihlakalo enkulu kaNkulunkulu? Ninanisele izimfihlo ezingcwele kakhulu zikaBaba, zona kanye ezithi uKristu kini uyithemba lenkazimulo, ngenzuzo engcolile, futhi uNkulunkulu wenu usephenduke isisu senu. Nokho, niyothi kimi, "Alikho iphutha kimi. Angijahe inzuzo engcolile, ngoba ngilungile phambi kwakho." Mina ngiphendule ngithi: Angithi nijahe ivangeli lenkohliso lokuchuma elifundiswa emabandleni enu aseLawodikeya? Nidayise iqiniso lezimfihlakalo ezingcwele kakhulu zikaNkulunkulu ngenzuzo ephansi, futhi anisenalo ithemba lenkazimulo engifisa ngokukhulu ukulangazela ukuba niliqonde, kodwa senilishintshe ithemba lenkazimulo laba yithemba lenzuzo yezinto enifuna ukuzibuthela yona ngemicabango yenu engcolile. Anginibizelanga emalini nasekuthandeni izinto, kodwa nginibizele ekulungeni nasebungcweleni beqiniso obutholakala kimi.

Phendukani emaphutheni enu nithenge kimi umuthi wamehlo, ukuze nigcobe amehlo enu nibone amaqiniso emiBhalweni engininike wona ukuze nisindiswe. Nikhohlisiwe ukuba neseke ohulumeni bomhlaba, nicabanga ukuthi ngokubeseka neseka abakhethiweyo bakaNkulunkulu. Kufanele nibe namahloni phambi kwami ngoba ekungazini kwenu anazi ukuthi ungubani u-Israyeli weqiniso kaNkulunkulu, futhi anisaqondi imfihlakalo enkulu kaNkulunkulu yokuthi abezizwe kufanele babe yizindlalifa kanye nabo, babe ngabesiqu sinye, nabahlanganyeli besithembiso sakhe ngoKristu, ngeVangeli. Ngoba mina ngingukuthula kwenu, owenze kokubili kwaba munye, nowabhidliza udonga oluphakathi olwahlukanisayo.

Nivumile ukuthi ubuyena benu buntshontshwe yisinagoge likaSathane, futhi nizilahlekele emazwini abaprofethi bamanga. Aniqondi yini ukuthi ningu-Israyeli kaNkulunkulu? Bonke abahamba ngalo mthetho, ukuthula makube phezu kwabo, nomusa, naphezu kuka-Israyeli kaNkulunkulu. O, bandla elisivivi, ngubani oyokusindisa ekuphingeni kwakho okukonakalisile kukususa ekunamatheleni kokunye okukimi, uKristu Jesu iNkosi yenu? Anikwazi ukubambelela kulo mhlaba ofayo, nicabanga ukuthi ngokwenza njalo ningikhonza; futhi kufanele niphenduke ekwesekeni kwenu ohulumeni bomhlaba ababi, ngoba yikuphi ukuhlangana uKristu anako noBheliyali, futhi abangikhonzi mina kodwa bakhonza uSathane. Hambani niye emiBhalweni yami nifune ubuntu benu beqiniso engibufihlile ezincwadini zami; ngoba nifile, futhi ukuphila kwenu kufihliwe noKristu kuNkulunkulu. Nalowo ofileyo ukhululiwe esonweni, nomthetho woMoya onika ukuphila unikhiphile emthethweni wesono nokufa.

Ngakho-ke, uma umuntu ekuKristu, uyisidalwa esisha: okudala kudlulile, bhekani, konke sekube kusha. Nginidalile kimi njengosanda kuzalwa, niphefumula okokuqala ephakadeni; ngoba iphakade laqala kini ngomzuzwana nazalwa kabusha ngomoya wami, ngoba ngenze kwababili umuntu oyedwa omusha, ngaleyo ndlela ngenza ukuthula. Yebo, umuntu oyedwa omusha kimi owakhiwe ngumJuda nomhedeni ngokufanayo ebandleni lami likaNkulunkulu. Labo engibabizele ekuphileni okusha kimi bangabantu abakhethiweyo bakaNkulunkulu, futhi labo abazelwe ngomoya wami bangamadodana eqiniso kaNkulunkulu, futhi uma umuntu engenaye umoya kaKristu, akasiye owakhe.

Kungani ningazivumeli izingxenye zemiBhalo, nizibala njengezingcolile ngenxa yalokho enikubiza ngokucabanga okuhle? Kungani nihlukanisa imiBhalo, nicabanga ukuthi lonke iZwi likaNkulunkulu akulona elemfundiso yenu, ukwenza kwenu, nokufunda kwenu? Ukucabanga okuhle okungamanga okudela iqiniso kunivala amehlo ezindleleni zami nasezinjongweni zami engizinqumele nina kusengaphambili. Yebo, kufanele nimise imicabango yenu ezintweni eziphezulu, kodwa ningabizi izingxenye zemiBhalo yami ngokuthi ziwubhici nokudangala ngoba anikuthathi njengokucabanga okuhle. Nibhajwe ekuthakatheni kobuKristu, nenza lona kanye ulwazi lwezingelosi eziwileyo ezazifundisa isintu ukuthakatha nobuthakathi nokusika izimpande kwasekuqaleni.

Ulwazi lwenu lwezingelosi eziwileyo lungamanga aklanywe ngobuqili ukuze athiye umphefumulo wenu ngamazwi amahle nezinkulumo ezibushelelezi. Yazini ukuthi abantu abaningi ababi eminyakeni edlule balethe ubuthakathi ebuKristwini, benitshela ukuthi uma nicabanga okuthile isikhathi eside, kufanele kwenzeke njengoba nicabanga. Zithutha! UNkulunkulu akanidalelanga ukuba nizakhele inkohliso yenu ezungeze ubuKristu benu bokusizakala nemicabango eyenamayo. Nilandeia omunye uJesu futhi nilushiyile ukholo olukimi, futhi niwalahlile amazwi ami nemiyalo yami nivuna amazwi amahle nezinkulumo ezibushelelezi, futhi senihlukaniswe yizimpisi ezihahayo eziqamba amanga kini.

Ziqamba amanga kini futhi zisebenzise kabi umthetho kaMose, zitshela amaKristu ukuthi kufanele akhokhe okweshumi ukuze asekele izinhlangano zawo ezingakhiwe phezu kwami, itshe legumbi eliyinhloko. Hlolani imiBhalo, futhi niqonde ukuthi njengoba umuntu enquma enhliziyweni yakhe, makanike futhi anike ngentokozo, ngoba uNkulunkulu uyamthanda umuphi onika ngentokozo. Futhi lokhu kuyisimiso esiyisihluthulelo eTestamenteni Elisha esaqiniswa ngegazi lami. Ukweshumi kwakungokweseka abapristi abangamaLevi, futhi bona babeseka umpristi omkhulu ngomnikelo wokuphakanyiswa. Lokhu kuqedwe kimi, ngoba ngingumpristi omkhulu kuze kube phakade ngokohlelo lukaMelkisedeki futhi nginenze naba abapristi namakhosi phambi kwami. Kodwa nina, O, bandla elisivivi, niphunyiswe amehlo yimibono yamandla namakhono amanga, amakhono angatholakali kuKristu.

Anilawuli lutho ngemicabango yenu yodwa, futhi aningiboni mina nezinhloso zami enginilungisele zona. Nivale izindlebe zenu ekuzweni imfundiso eqondile nivuna izimfundiso zamanga ezikitaza izindlebe zenu. Akukhona lokho kuphela, kodwa nihlasela abazalwane abaphenduke isitha senu ngoba banitshela iqiniso kulezi zindaba, futhi nilahlile umqondo waseBhereya nivuna ukuthatheka kwemizwa okubizwa ngephutha ngokuthi ubuKristu. Qondani lokhu, belusi abadukayo: niyothola umvuzo ekwahlulelweni ngokuduhisa izimvu zami, futhi impindiselo ngeyami futhi ngiyophindisela.

Qondani lokhu, bantabami: anilawuli lutho ngokucabanga kwenu okuhle, futhi anisiye uzibuthe wokuchuma ngenxa yemicabango yenu eyenamayo. Nikhohlisiwe futhi nidukiswe abakhohlisi abaningi. Ngaphezu kwalokho, anikwazi ukwenza lutho ngaphandle kwami njengoba nje nami ngingeke ngenze lutho ngaphandle kukaBaba; ngoba uNkulunkulu onika amandla abalungileyo futhi uBaba odale konke, ngamunye ukuze adumise igama likaYahweh elilungile ngaphezu kwakho konke!

Phumani ebuhahandini benu nilahle uthando lwemali, ngoba uthando lwemali luyimpande yako konke okubi.

Lapho nivala amehlo enu kulo lonke ivangeli lami futhi nikhetha ngokukhetha lokho enikubheka "njengokuhle," nisemgodini nabaholi abayizimpumputhe abaniholele bobabili kuleso siphetho. Anginibizelanga ukuwela emgodini bantabami, kodwa nganibizela ukuba niphile phambi kwami ekulungeni nasebungcweleni beqiniso, ngakho-ke vukani odakeni. Nganibizela ukuba nilahle izono zenu niphenduke ezindleleni zenu zokungalungi. Kungenxa yegazi lami lokuhlawulela kuphela eningahlanzwa ezonweni zenu; imicabango yenu emihle ayenzi ukulunga, yize niyenze izithixo zezinhliziyo zenu.

Kungani nivumela abazalwane bamanga namaJuda phakathi kwenu ukuba bahlole inkululeko eninayo kimi, iNkosi elungileyo? Ukuba beniyiqondile imfihlakalo enkulu kaNkulunkulu, beniyokuqonda ukuthi uKristu uyisiphetho soMthetho sokulunga kuwo wonke umuntu okholwayo, Ngoba uMose uchaza ukulunga okungokomthetho, ukuthi umuntu owenza lezo zinto uyophila ngazo. Ngoba ngifakaza futhi kuye wonke umuntu osokiweyo, ukuthi unecala lokwenza wonke umthetho. Pho, kuthiwani ngomthetho kaMose?

Ngizigcwalisile izimfuneko zomthetho futhi ngalungisa umuntu ngokukholwa kimi, ngoba nginqobile, futhi ngiwugcwalisile umthetho, ngizinikela njengePhasika eliphelele kanye kuphela; ngakho-ke, olungileyo uyophila ngokukholwa. Anifundanga yini ukuthi angizanga ukuchitha umthetho noma abaprofethi kodwa ukugcwalisa umthetho nokunika ukulunga kubantu ngokuzihlanganisa kwabo nami, iNkosi yabo uJesu Kristu.

Ngisho lokhu-ke, hambani ngomoya, futhi anisoze nagcwalisa inkanuko yenyama, futhi awukho umthetho omelene nesithelo somoya. Ngakho-ke, nembelwe nami ngokubhabhadiswa ekufeni kwami, ukuze njengoba uKristu wavuswa kwabafileyo ngenkazimulo kaBaba: kanjalo nani nihambe ebuntshani bokuphila. Kusemisebenzini yami yokuhlawulela kuphela, njengewundlu likaNkulunkulu elingenasono, lapho umuntu enganqoba khona isono futhi akhululwe esiqalekisweni somthetho; ngoba ngenziwe isiqalekiso ngenxa yenu, ngoba uqalekisiwe noma yimuphi umuntu olenga esihlahleni.

UMose wachaza ukulunga okuvela emthethweni, Ukuthi umuntu owenza lezo zinto uyophila ngazo. Ngoba kuKristu Jesu ukusoka akusizi lutho nokungasoki, kodwa isidalwa esisha. UKristu uyisiphetho somthetho kuwo wonke umuntu okholwayo, nokho abaningi benu bazowalahla la mazwi ngoba nihlonipha amazwi abefundisi benu, abashumayeli, kanye norabi ngaphezu kwamazwi eNkosi noMsindisi wenu, uKristu Jesu. Aniqondi yini ukuthi nakhiwe phezu kwesisekelo sabaphostoli nabaprofethi, uJesu Kristu uqobo eyitshe legumbi eliyinhloko? Ngoba asikho esinye isisekelo umuntu angasibeka, ngaphandle kwaleso esibekiwe, onguJesu Kristu; ngakho-ke, anikho ngaphansi komthetho kodwa ngaphansi komusa.

Ngoba labo abakholwayo bangangena ekuphumuleni kwami, ngoba lowo ongenile ekuphumuleni kwami naye uphumuzwe emisebenzini yakhe, njengoba uNkulunkulu enza kweyakhe ngosuku lwesikhombisa. Anazi yini ukuthi umthetho wawungumfundisi wenu ukuniyisa kimi, uKristu Jesu? Nokho abaningi bangilahlile mina nomusa wami nesihe sami ngenxa yemisebenzi yabo engenanjongo yokulunga. Ubumpumputhe ngokwengxenye bufike ku-Israyeli kuze kufike ukuphelela kwabezizwe ekufezekeni. O, gatsha lomnqumo wasendle, ungazigabisi ngesihlahla somnqumo oxhunyekwe kuso, futhi ungalandeli ubumpumputhe babo; ngoba ngiyakwazi ukusindisa uJakobe futhi ngiyokwenza lokho njengoba insali iyosindiswa.

Ngakho-ke, makungabikho muntu onahlulelayo ngokudla, noma ngokuphuza, noma ngokunakekela noma yiluphi usuku olungcwele, noma ukwethwasa kwenyanga, noma izinsuku zeSabatha. Kungani nibeka umthwalo kubantabami, nibatshela ukuthi kufanele baphimisele igama lami ngolimi oluthile olungcwele? Ngiyaziqonda zonke izilimi futhi ngibheka inhliziyo, ngakho ngiyazi lapho abantabami bebiza igama lami nganoma yiluphi ulimi ababiza ngalo, futhi ngibabeka uphawu lukababa ngokufanele, futhi ngibalethe eBandleni likaNkulunkulu. Abobaba nani anikwazi ukugcina umthetho, kodwa umthetho ugcwalisekile kini ekulungeni kwami; ngoba umthetho wehlela emiyalweni emibili emikhulu: Thanda iNkosi uNkulunkulu wakho ngenhliziyo yakho yonke, umphefumulo, ingqondo, namandla, futhi uthande umakhelwane wakho njengoba uzithanda wena.

Ukulunga akuveli ngomthetho kodwa ngokukholwa kimi, iNkosi uJesu Kristu. Ngoba ngingumpristi omkhulu kuze kube phakade ngokohlelo lukaMelkisedeki, futhi ngegazi lami lokuhlawulela nilungisisiwe, ngoba ngiyiPhasika eliphelele, futhi ngiyiwundlu likaNkulunkulu. Makungabikho muntu oniduhisayo kulezi zindaba. Umthetho awenzelwanga umuntu olungileyo, kodwaenzelwe abangenamthetho nabangalaleli. Bhekani! Ngiyisulile incwadi ebhalwe ngezandla yemiyalo, eyayimelene nani, eyayiphambene nani, futhi ngayisusa endleleni, ngayibethela esiphambanweni sami. Nokho, nisafisa ukuba sebugqilini bezinto ezibuthakathaka nezingento yalutho. Ithini imiBhalo: Xosha isigqilakazi nendodana yaso, ngoba asibona abantwana besigqilakazi kodwa abakhululekileyo.

Angishongo ngokusobala yini: Bhekani! Nginikhombisa imfihlakalo, asizukulala sonke, kodwa sonke sizoguqulwa, ngoba ngizokwehla ezulwini ngokumemeza, ngezwi lengelosi enkulu nangecilongo likaNkulunkulu futhi abafileyo kuKristu bayovuka kuqala, bese nina eniphilayo nenisalayo niyothathwa emafwini nihlangabezane nami, uKristu Jesu emoyeni, futhi kanjalo niyohlala nami njalo. O, bandla laseLawodikeya, nilahla ngisho namazwi ami acace kakhulu kini. Ngakho-ke, yazini ukuthi ngizonilahla etafuleni lami, lapho ngifisa kakhulu ukunibekela indawo emazulwini.

Nicabanga ukuthi kuyinto elula ukulahla amazwi ami nokudukisa abanye ephutheni lenu. Ngakho-ke, njengoba nifuna usuku lweNkosi, ngizonishiya ukuba nibekezelele usuku lweNkosi, njengoba nicabanga ngokuzidla ukuthi ningama. Niyenqaba ukubonakala kwami futhi nikhulisa amazwi abaprofethi bamanga ngaphezu kwemfundiso eqondile engiyinikele kimi. Aniwufundanga yini umfanekiso wezintombi eziyishumi? Ngineluleka ukuba nibe nokholo emazwini ami futhi ningangiphiki, ukuze nami ngingadingi ukuniphika. Niyoba ngabaphi, bantabami? Niyoba phakathi kwezintombi ezihlakaniphileyo ezazilungiselele ngezintambo ezilungisiwe namafutha ezibhanini zazo? Noma niyothatha indawo yenu nezintombi ezingahlakaniphile, nithi, Nkosi, Nkosi sivulele; futhi ngiyothi kuzo, Anginazi!

Anifundanga yini? Ngenxa yalesi sizathu umuntu uyoshiya uyise nonina, futhi ahlanganiswe nomkakhe, futhi labo ababili bayoba nyamanye. Lena yimfihlakalo enkulu: kodwa ngikhuluma ngoKristu neBandla. Aniwazwa yini amazwi omyeni, makoti wami? Kufanele nibambelele kimi njengenhloko yenu ngendlela efanayo umfazi abambelela ngayo kumyeni wakhe njengenhloko yakhe, ngoba uMlobokazi uyiBandla lami nomzimba wami, futhi yimuphi umuntu owake wazonda inyama yakhe? Nokho, nivumela abelusi abadukayo baniduhise kule ndaba, futhi abaningi benu bathatha amazwi asontekile avela kubaprofethi bamanga abasontekile ngaphezu kweZwi lami Elingcwele.

Nibeke ukholo lwenu kubantu ababi, abantu engingabathumanga; nokho banikhohlisa ngamazwi amahle nezinkulumo ezibushelelezi engingabanikanga zona. Niyabalandela sengathi nilandela mina. Phendukani! Abaningi benu balandela izikhonzi zamanga ezibonakala njengezikhonzi zokulunga. Akumangalisi, nokho, ngoba uSathane uziguqula abe yingelosi yokukhanya, ngakho-ke akumelwe kumangaze ukuthi nezikhonzi zakhe zenza okufanayo. Abaningi benu baphunyiswe amehlo ukukhanya kwamanga, okuvela ezikhonzini zamanga engingazithumanga, futhi zikhuluma amazwi amakhulu azikhukhumezayo aphambene nemfundiso eqondile ekimi, iNkosi uJesu Kristu.

Zimeleleni, O maKristu angenamgogodla, niphume kulo futhi nisuke eBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlakalo. Ngifisa sengathi ngabe uyashisa noma uyabanda futhi awusivivi, futhi anginazo izifiso zokukuphalaza emlonyeni wami. Ngakho-ke, buyelani kimi, bantabami, futhi nibuyele emazwini ami. Ngibambeni njengenhloko yenu, ngoba ngiyiNkosi eyanifelayo, futhi navuka lapho nami ngenza njalo. Kungani nithembele kubaprofethi bamanga? Anidingi ukuba umuntu anifundise, kodwa ukugcotshwa enikwamukele kimi kuhlala kini futhi kunifundisa zonke izinto, O nina bokholo oluncane! Phendukelani kimi nibuyele endleleni emincane. Khalani, nilile, futhi niphenduke, bantabami! Bonke engibathandayo, ngiyabasola, ukuze ubizo lwenu nokukhethwa kwenu kuqiniseke.

Yazini ukuthi nginithanda ngokujulile futhi ngifisa inhliziyo yenu ephelele ngaphezu kwemisebenzi yenu. Wozani kimi ngenhliziyo evulekile ninginike konke eninakho, njengoba nami nginikele konke kwami ngenxa yenu. Ungesabi futhi ungazilahli, ngoba akukho kulahlwa kulabo abakuKristu Jesu. Vele ubuyele kimi uvume izono zakho, futhi ngizokuthethelela ngikubuyisele ezinjongweni naseziphiweni engikunike zona. Yimani niqine ninganyakazi, ngoba maduze kakhulu ngizovela ngokuzumayo, futhi umhlaba uyokwazi ukuthi nginithathile, njengoba le nto ngeke yenziwe ngasese. Isikhathi sifushane, bantabami, futhi maduze isikhathi ngeke sisaba khona.

Ngiyakwazi ukusindisa uJakobe futhi ngiyokwenza lokho njengoba insali iyosindiswa.

Ngingumpristi omkhulu kuze kube phakade ngokohlelo lukaMelkisedeki.

NgiyiPhasika eliphelele.

Ngiyiwundlu likaNkulunkulu.

NgiyiNkosi eyanifelayo, yebo uYeshua HaMashiach.
