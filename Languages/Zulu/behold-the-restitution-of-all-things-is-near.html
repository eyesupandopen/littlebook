Ubufakazi Obubili: Israyeli
Okubabayo Nokumnandi
Julayi 05, 2024

Bhekani! Ukubuyiselwa Kwezinto Zonke Kuseduze!

Kusuka KuYeshua HaMashiach:

Izikhathi zokubuyiselwa sezisondele emhlabeni, kodwa kuqala kufanele kugcwaliseke izikhathi nokuphela kokwahlulela okukhulu. Futhi mina, uKristu Jesu, ngiyokwahlulela umhlaba ngoba umhlaba ungowami kanye nokugcwala kwawo, futhi ngiwuthengile umhlaba ngegazi lami elihlawulayo eKalvari. Ngeqiniso elingaguqukiyo, nganikela ngalo lonke iconsi legazi lami elingenacala elachithelwa izono zesintu. Kulabo abamukela isipho sami sokuthula nokuphila okuphakade kuyoba khona ukuthula, nokuphila okuphakade, futhi izindlela zokulunga ziyobusa. Kulabo abenqaba isipho sami bakhethe izindlela zokufa, ukwahlulelwa, nesijeziso saphakade, ngoba umhlaba uyadlula futhi akukho okunye ukukhetha. Ngakho-ke bayothwala ukwahlulelwa kwabo naso sonke isono, izwi elibi, umcabango omubi, nesenzo esibi kuyodalulwa ukuze baphendule ngakho. Kodwa labo abenqaba insindiso yami abasoze bakwazi ukuma, futhi ngokuzenzakalelayo bakhetha ichibi lomlilo elalungiselelwe udeveli nezingelosi zakhe futhi into enjalo ayidingekile, kodwa ngamunye ukhululekile ukuzikhethela. Nginguye ozayo ukuzokwahlulela nokulwa, futhi izindlela zikaYahweh ziyobusa umhlaba ngokulunga embusweni wami ozayo. Nina Bandla lami, kanye noMlobokazi wami oyigugu niyothathwa niye emazulwini, futhi niyokwamukela ifa lenu, futhi niyothatha izindawo zenu phakathi kwezinhlelo enginilungisele zona, ngisho nendawo yenu phakathi kukaJakobe ngoba ninguIsrayeli kaNkulunkulu.

Ngoba kimi, uKristu Jesu, akukho mJuda noma owabezizwe kodwa yisidalwa esisha, futhi leso sidalwa siyohamba njengomuntu oyedwa omusha. Bhekani! Niyoba ngumuntu omusha, futhi niyoba yisidalwa esisha, angithi ngininikile isibambiso sikaMoya oNgcwele? Ngisho nophawu lukaNkulunkulu ophilayo, olungeke lwephulwe? Ngubani kini ongahlehlisa ukuzalwa kwabantwana benu bemvelo? Aninakukwenza lokho ngoba izakhi zenu zofuzo zidluliselwa ezizukulwaneni ngezizukulwane futhi abantwana bahlala bekhona. Kunjalo-ke, imbewu kaYahweh edalwe phakathi kwabantwana bakaNkulunkulu ayinakonakaliswa, ngoba akunakwenzeka ukuba nihlwitshwe ezandleni zami. Anifundanga yini? Ngoba nizelwe ngokusha, hhayi ngembewu efayo, kodwa ngengafiyo, ngezwi likaNkulunkulu eliphilayo, nelihlala kuze kube phakade. Bantwana bami, amazwi ami ayamangalisa kakhulu yini? Nivumele amazwi amanga anidukise ekubeni nomqondo owodwa kuKristu? Akukho lutho olungasusa imbewu kaYahweh uma isidaliwe ngaphakathi, ngoba izipho nobizo lukaNkulunkulu azinakuhoxiswa. Pho kungani ningabaza insindiso yenu? Nakuba inyama yenu iyonenza none, futhi ngiyonithethelela nginihlambulule njengoba nivuma izono zenu ngokuphenduka, akukho lutho olungasusa abantwana bakaNkulunkulu emndenini kaNkulunkulu. Kulabo abaphila ngokungalungi imisebenzi yabo iyosha, futhi bayokuma benqunu benehlazo, futhi kulabo abamisebenzi yabo imi njengelungileyo bayokwamukela umvuzo omkhulu, kodwa abantwana bakaNkulunkulu bathola insindiso yaphakade. Uma umuntu engenaye uMoya kaKristu, akasiye owakhe.

Kulabo abaphila impilo yokuhlubuka nakulabo abazenza abaphilela uKristu ngegama kuphela bayokuwa lapho iBandla leqiniso lihamba. Ngoba lowo ohlwanyelela inyama uyovuna ukonakala enyameni. Futhi kulabo abazama ukungibeka iNkosi ngenhliziyo engenakholo baqhubeka esonweni bayosala osizini lukaJakobe ukuze kubhujiswe inyama futhi kuyodingeka babize egameni leNkosi kuze kube sekupheleni, futhi kuyoba njalo. Labo abazikhohlisa kwaze kwaba sekuphefumuleni kwabo kokugcina futhi abangafelanga kuKristu futhi bengakaze babe nobudlelwano obusindisayo nami bayobhekana nokwahlulelwa kwabo. Nicabanga ukuthi uYahweh ophenya izinhliziyo uyisiphukuphuku? Nicabanga ukuthi mina, uKristu Jesu, angizivivinyi izinhliziyo zabantu ngokholo nokwethemba kimi? Kodwa kuyoba njalo, futhi ngiyothi kwabaningi abangibiza ngeNkosi: Sukani kimi, nina benzi bokubi, angizange nginazi.

Kodwa kini Bandla lami, ababiziweyo nabakhethiweyo bami abathembekileyo: Wonke umphefumulo ozuze insindiso wenziwa futhi indlalifa yezithembiso ezenziwa nguYahweh koBaba. Ngoba kulotshiwe ukuthi abezizwe babe yizindlalifa kanye nabo, babe ngumzimba munye, nabahlanganyeli besithembiso sakhe kuKristu ngevangeli, futhi ngibaxhumele eMthini Womnqumo wemvelo. Futhi bonke abadalwe kabusha kimi bayobuyiselwa ngokuphelele ekupheleleni nasekulungeni nasebungcweleni beqiniso. Anginithembisanga yini izingubo ezingenasici, nina bancane bami? Futhi nginimisele ifa lenu phakathi kwezizwe, ngoba ninguIsrayeli kaNkulunkulu engimhlengile emhlabeni.

Yekani ukucabanga ngohlu lozalo luka-Adamu, ngoba igazi lika-Adamu lonakele esonweni! Njengoba kulotshiwe: umuntu wokuqala u-Adamu wenziwa umphefumulo ophilayo, no-Adamu wokugcina wenziwa umoya ophilisayo. Anifundanga yini? Akubona bonke abangama-Israyeli, abavela kwa-Israyeli. Ngoba abantwana besithembiso bayinzalo ka-Abrahama ngokholo, futhi njengoba kulotshiwe Oqotho uyophila ngokholo. Futhi kimi, uKristu Jesu, zonke izinto ziphelelisiwe ngoba iNgonyama iyoba munye ngokuphelele neWundlu futhi nina nifaniswe ngokuphelele nami, uKristu Jesu iNkosi yenu. Bhekani! Kodwa kini enesaba iGama lami, kuyophuma ilanga lokulunga! Ngiyindodana Yokulunga, ngisho indodana ezelwe yodwa kaNkulunkulu ophakeme kunabo bonke, uYahweh, ngoba akekho onjengaye, futhi nginguye olapha ukuzohlwitha ibandla lami enhlekeleleni, ngisho nomlobokazi wami ngimuse ekhaya lakhe emazulwini, futhi uyohlala nami kuze kube phakade. Ngoba kuyoba njalo, ngiyonixhumanisa nomndeni wenu weqiniso ngoba u-Israyeli wahlakazeka phesheya futhi kwa-Israyeli kaNkulunkulu bayobuthaniswa, bangaphinde bahlakazeke futhi, ngoba ningu-Israyeli kaNkulunkulu!

Ngakho ngizohlwitha iBandla lami ngilise emazulwini, abathembekileyo bami, futhi niyoba nami kuze kube phakade. Ngoba niganiwe kumyeni oyedwa: uYeshua HaMashiach, nangomoya wami nginenze intombi emsulwa futhi ngahlambulula abathembekileyo ukuze bame belungile emhlatshelweni wami wokuhlawulela. Lapho nithathwa niyoklonyeliswa ngokuthembeka kwenu futhi niyothwala imiqhele eminingi, nakuba abanye beyolahlekelwa. Niyoklonyeliswa ngaphezu kwalokho eningakucabanga, ngoba iso alikabonanga nendlebe ayikezwa futhi akukaze kungene enhliziyweni yomuntu izinto uYahweh azilungiselele labo abamthandayo. Futhi abathembekileyo, ngisho noMlobokazi wami, bayoba nami kuze kube phakade, futhi bayobusa njengamakhosi nabapristi kuze kube phakade, ngoba awukho umkhawulo eMbusweni wami.

Futhi ingelosi yami iyokwenyukela lapho niyobe ninami emazulwini futhi ibeke uphawu kwabayizi-12,000 esizweni ngasinye. Aniwafundanga yini amaZwi ami anikezwe uJohane uMambuli? Ngabona enye ingelosi yenyuka ivela empumalanga, inophawu lukaNkulunkulu ophilayo: yamemeza ngezwi elikhulu ezingelosini ezine, ezanikwa amandla okulimaza umhlaba nolwandle, yathi, ningawulimazi umhlaba, nolwandle nemithi, size sibeke uphawu ezincekwini zikaNkulunkulu wethu emabunzini azo. Anilibonanga yini igama elithi enyuka? Izingelosi ezingcwele zezulu zehlela emhlabeni, ngoba ikhaya lazo lisemazulwini. Ukuze ingelosi engcwele yenyuke, yenyukela emazulwini. Izingelosi eziwileyo ezenyukela emhlabeni zisuka kwalasha, aniwahloniphi yini amaZwi ezwi lami elingcwele?

Futhi ziyobeka uphawu kwabayizi-12,000 esizweni ngasinye esibaluliwe: Juda, Rubeni, Gadi, Asheri, Nafitali, Manase, Simeyoni, Levi, Isakare, Zebuloni, Josefa, Benjamini. Ngakho-ke abayizi-144,000 bami bayoba nophawu lukaNkulunkulu ophilayo emabunzini abo. Futhi bayokuma nami eNtabeni iSiyoni, futhi bayohlabelela okungathi yiculo elisha phambi kwesihlalo sobukhosi, naphambi kwezilwane ezine, nabadala: futhi akekho umuntu owayengalifunda lelo culo ngaphandle kwezinkulungwane eziyikhulu namashumi amane nane, ababehlengiwe emhlabeni. Laba yibo abangazange bangcoliswe ngabesifazane; ngoba bayizintombi. Laba yibo abalandela iWundlu nomaphi lapho liya khona. Ngoba laba bahlengwa phakathi kwabantu, bengulibo kuNkulunkulu naseWundlwini. Futhi emlonyeni wabo akutholakalanga buqili: ngoba abanasici phambi kwesihlalo sobukhosi sikaNkulunkulu.

Abayizi-144,000 bakhethwe nguYahweh ngaphambi kokusekelwa komhlaba ngenxa yesikhathi esinjalo. Bakhandwa osizini, futhi bayoba namakhono ahlukile namandla engiyobagqokisa izingubo zabo ezintsha engiyobanika zona, ngoba izingubo zenu zenzelwe nina ngqo futhi ziyoba nempumelelo nezimfanelo zenu. Bayothathwa ekubuthweni kwabathembekileyo kodwa babekelwe eceleni izinhloso zami, ngisho nezinhloso zikaYahweh noYeshua HaMashiach. Bafika ngesikhathi sosizi olukhulu ukuhola u-Israyeli ekuphepheni. Kodwa kuqala bayathathwa ukuze bagane uKristu ngoba banguMlobokazi wakhe, ababiziweyo, abakhethiweyo, abathembekileyo, nabeqiniso. Ngisho nalabo uKristu abenze bamsulwa njengentombi egazini lakhe elihlawulayo. Futhi yinina mlobokazi wami eniyosebenzela izinhloso zikaYahweh ngomyalo wami ngezikhathi zezinkathazo ezinkulu kunazo zonke.

Ngoba niyizithelo zokuqala ezibuthelwe emasimini esivuno, ngoba ngiyiNkosi yesivuno, futhi manje sekuyisikhathi sokunibuthela esiphahleni sami ukuze namukele umvuzo wemisebenzi yenu. Ngoba labo ababuthe ukolweni bayoba nokuningi futhi labo ababuthe utshani bayolahlekelwa njengoba amakhoba esha emlilweni, kodwa nokho bayosindiswa. Kanjalo izisebenzi nabantu bami bayofika efeni labo kimi, uYeshua HaMashiach, ngoba ngiyiNkosi yamakhosi neNkosi yamakhosi futhi ngeke ngithuliswe futhi ngeke ngimiswe.

Akulotshiwe yini? Isho kanje iNkosi uNkulunkulu; Bheka, ngizokhipha abantwana bakwa-Israyeli phakathi kwabezizwe, nomaphi lapho beye khona, ngibabuthe nxazonke, ngibayise ezweni labo: Ngibenze babe yisizwe sinye ezweni ezintabeni zakwa-Israyeli; inkosi eyodwa iyoba yinkosi yabo bonke: abayikuba yizizwe ezimbili futhi, abayikwahlukaniswa babe yimibuso emibili futhi: Abayikuzingcolisa futhi ngezithombe zabo, nangezinto zabo ezinengekayo, nangesinye seziphambeko zabo: kodwa ngiyobasindisa kuzo zonke izindawo zabo zokuhlala, lapho bone khona, ngibahlambulule: kanjalo bayokuba ngabantu bami, nami ngibe nguNkulunkulu wabo.

Mina, uYeshua HaMashiach, ngithumele lawa Mazwi ngenceku yami, futhi ngenceku yami lawa Mazwi athunyelwe! Bhekani! Nginguye ozayo emafwini, ngakho-ke zilungiseni, nina zintombi zami ezihlakaniphile.

Kusuka KuYahweh UNkulunkulu Omkhulu Ongunaphakade:

Ngiyakubona ukwesaba kwenu, nina abawileyo, ngoba manje sekufika ukwahlulelwa kwenu ngisho nokwahlulelwa okukhulu okwathenjiswa endulo, nezikhathi zokwahlulelwa okukhulu okwamenyezelwa ezikhathini zasendulo yimina, uYahweh uNkulunkulu omkhulu ongunaphakade owamemezela ukwahlulelwa kwenu kwase kuzwakala imisindo yokuduma okuyisikhombisa ngakho-ke izwi likaNkulunkulu uSomandla liyozwakala! Futhi izinhloso zami ziyohlangana, ngisho nezinhlelo zami zeminyaka ziyopheleliswa endodaneni yami uJesu Kristu. Ngoba ezithelweni zokuqala kufika imivuzo yesivuno yezisebenzi ezithunyelwe nguYeshua HaMashiach nokolweni wami manje usuzobuthelwa esiphahleni sami futhi ukuhlukaniswa kokugcina emhlabeni kuzoqala futhi usizi lukaJakobe selufikile.

Bhekani! Ngiyokwehlukanisa amazulu phezulu nginyakazise umhlaba ngaphansi, futhi ngiyobutha izindlalifa zensindiso ezisele emhlabeni, zize ensindisweni, zibize egameni leNkosi kuze kube sekupheleni, zikhonze ethempelini lami futhi isixuku esikhulu nesinamandla siyosindiswa esontweni lamashumi ayisikhombisa likaDaniyeli. Mina, ngisho noYahweh, ngiyodabula umhlaba ngisuse izintaba ezindaweni zazo futhi wonke umhlaba uyokwazi ukuthi u-El Shaddai uvukile phezulu nokwahlulela okukhulu manje sekuzogqashuka phezu komhlaba lapho Abathembekileyo bami bebuthene ngoba iziphambeko ziyophela, ngisho neziphambeko zikaJakobe nezono zendlu ka-Israyeli.

Endaweni yabo kuyofika ukubusa kokulunga, ngisho nokubusa okulungileyo kwendodana yami ezelwe yodwa, uYeshua HaMashiach, engithokozayo kuye njalo. Nguye oyoshaya izizwe ngentonga yensimbi futhi kube ngukubusa kokulunga, lapho iNgonyama iyolala khona newundlu. Kanjalo-ke kuyoba umpristi omkhulu kuze kube phakade ngokohlelo lukaMelkisedeki futhi iNkosi yamakhosi neNkosi yamakhosi iyohlangana ngokuphelele ekuphelelisweni okuphakade futhi ukubusa kwayo kuyoba okuphakade, ngisho noYeshua HaMashiach ngoba yena futhi unguJesu Kristu!

Zilungiseleleni nina enihlala emhlabeni ngokungalungi ngoba ngithuma indodana yami ukuba yenze ukwahlulela kwami futhi iphahlaze konke ukubusa kwabawileyo, ngoba izikhathi zabo sezifikile futhi ukwahlulela kuyowela kanzima emhlabeni. Ngoba nizibekele ulaka olukhulu ngosuku lolaka futhi usuku lolaka phezu kwenu, nina abangenamsebenzi, ngoba nizigcolisile ekonakaleni okuphelele, futhi aninakuthula ngenxa yezindlela zenu ezingcolile nezinengekayo. Kodwa ngaphambi kokuba niye efeni lenu laphakade lapho niyogwinywa khona emalangabini ashisayo kakhulu aluhlaza okwesibhakabhaka, niyoqale nifeze izinhloso zami, ngoba nezitha zami ziyalilalela izwi lami.

Kanjalo ukwahlulelwa kuzoqala endlini kaNkulunkulu futhi ngiyothuma indodana yami ngisho uKristu Jesu uMesiya ovusiwe wakwa-Israyeli nomsindisi womhlaba, futhi uyokwenza konke engikumisile futhi ngakuthembisa oBaba nakubantwana futhi konke kuyoba ngu-yebo no-amen njengoba ukugcwaliseka kwezithembiso kwenzeka.

Ngoba amazulu enzelwe abantwana bami, ngisho bonke engibamisele izinhloso zami ngaphambi kokuqala kokudala. Ngoba ngakwazi ngamunye wenu ezikhathini ezedlule ezingunaphakade, futhi nganibeka njengemfihlakalo efihlwe enhliziyweni yami engayembula kuphela ngesikhathi esifanele ohlelweni lwami lwaphakade lwensindiso. Akukho lutho olungenzeka engingakwazi ngaphambi kokuba kwenzeke, futhi yimina, uNkulunkulu uSomandla, obiza izinto ezingekho njengokungathi sezikhona. O, nina abawileyo, niyosha ngokuphelele ekwahlulelweni kwenu okusondelayo, futhi naninobuwula obanele bokucabanga ukuthi ningathatha uYahweh ngobuqili, kodwa Nginguye othatha abaziqhenyayo ekwahlulelweni, ngoba bahlakaniphile emicabangweni yabo siqu. Ngoba nganibonisa kuphela izimfihlakalo ezingelusizo lwalutho, ngoba ngangazi ukuthi niyokukhaphela konke okungokwami. Anobuwula obungaka nina abawileyo futhi anihlakaniphile. Konke enikufezile ukuhlukunyezwa kwenu siqu nokubhujiswa okuqhubekayo emalangabini ashisayo kakhulu, futhi niyobhukuda esibabule esivuthayo, ngoba liyasha kanjalo ichibi elikhulu kuze kube phakade, futhi ngiyobeka uphawu, futhi niyokhohlwa ngokuphelele, futhi aniyikukhunjulwa.

Kodwa lesi siphetho akusona esenu abakhethiweyo bami, abathembekileyo bami, ngisho bonke ababizwe ngegama lami. Okwenu ikusasa lenjabulo yaphakade, injabulo yaphakade, intokozo yaphakade futhi ngeke kube khona ukuphela kwezinsuku futhi ngeke kuphinde kube nosuku olubi kulabo abangena epharadesi. Bhekani! Ngiyobuyisela zonke izinto ngisho ngizenze zibe ngcono kunokuphelela kwami kwasekuqaleni ngaphambi kokuwa kokonakala, ngoba ngiyodala izulu elisha nomhlaba omusha lapho kuhlala khona ukulunga futhi akukho lutho oluyongena olubi noma olungcolile. Ngoba ngaphandle kukhona izinja, nabathakathi, nezifebe, nababulali, nabakhonza izithombe, nabo bonke abathanda futhi benze amanga, futhi bonke bayoba nesabelo sabo kulelo chibi elivuthayo lapho impethu yabo ingafi.

Ngakho-ke akukho lutho okufanele nilwesabe ngekusasa lenu nina ababiziweyo, abakhethiweyo, nabathembekileyo bami, futhi niyobusa kuze kube phakade noYeshua HaMashiach futhi awukho umkhawulo embusweni noma ekubuseni kwakhe. Futhi mina uNkulunkulu omkhulu ongunaphakade, ngisho noYahweh ngokwami ngiyokwakha indawo yami yokuhlala phakathi kwabantu futhi kuyoba nokuthula nokuphelela okunobunye phezu kwayo yonke indalo yami futhi ngiyosula zonke izinyembezi emehlweni abo futhi akusoze kuphinde kuvuke omunye oyonikhathaza nakancane, ngoba mina, uYahweh, ngisho uNkulunkulu omkhulu ongunaphakade, ngifunga lezi zinto ngegama lami siqu ngoba angeke ngifunge ngomkhulu kunami. Kodwa kini enesaba igama lami kuyophuma iLanga lokulunga linokuphulukisa emaphikweni alo; niyophuma, nikhule njengamathole esibayeni.

Mina, Yahweh, uNkulunkulu Omkhulu Ongunaphakade, ngithumele amazwi ami ngenceku yami, futhi ngenceku yami amazwi ami athunyelwe.

NGINGU-MINA ENGINGUYE Omkhulu futhi akekho omunye!

Yangani iNdodana funa ithukuthele, nife endleleni, lapho ulaka lwayo luvutha kancane nje.
