Okubabayo Okumnandi
Julayi 17, 2024

Futhi Kanjalo Ziyashintsha Izikhathi Zonyaka!

Buthanani, Bandla lami, futhi niboleke izindlebe zenu ekuhlakanipheni. Thobekani ezwini leNkosi yenu eyiqhawe, yona kanye emi ukunilwela, mina kanye iNgonyama yesiZwe sakwaJuda oyiNkosi yamakhosi noMbusi wababusi. Nginguye uYahweh amnike zonke izinto ezulwini nasemhlabeni, ezandleni zami ezinamandla, ngokuba ngiphila phakade futhi ngiphethe izihluthulelo zokufa nezesihogo ezandleni zami, nomhlaba ungowami ngokuba ngiwuthengile ngegazi lami lokuhlawulela. Futhi nginegama eliphezu kwawo onke amagama, ukuba ngeGama likaJesu onke amadolo aguqe, ezinto ezisezulwini, nezinto ezisemhlabeni, nezinto ezingaphansi komhlaba. Nokuthi zonke izilimi zivume ukuthi uJesu Kristu uyiNkosi, kube yinkazimulo kuYise weziKhanyiso, kuye okungekho ukuguquguquka nasithunzi sokuphenduka. Futhi ngubani ongamelana nenkemba esika nhlangothi zombili ephuma emlonyeni wami? Ngokuba ngayo, ngizoshaya izizwe: futhi nginguye oyozibusa ngentonga yensimbi: futhi nginguye onyathela ngolaka isikhamo sewayini sentukuthelo enkulu ka-El Shaddai, uNkulunkulu uSomandla. Futhi nginguye onesambatho sakhe, nasethangeni lakhe kubhalwe igama elithi, INKOSI YAMAKHOSI, NOMBUSI WABABUSI, futhi isikhathi sami SIFIKILE!

Yini ekhona engatholakala kuyo yonke indalo kaYahweh, bantabami, eningayesaba? Yazini ngeqiniso, bancane bami, ukuthi abawileyo bayanesaba. Ngokuba bayazi ukuthi manje sebefana nani, futhi nina niyokwenziwa nikhazimule kakhulu kunabo, bonke labo abashiya isimo sabo sokuqala. Ukuphambuka emiyalweni uYahweh ayibekela indalo yakhe kuyisono esikhulu, ngokuba indalo kaYahweh ihloselwe ukusebenza ngokuzwana okuphelele. Futhi uYahweh unika inkululeko yokuzikhethela, ngakho-ke ukukhetha ukuhlubuka ezinkanukweni zobugovu kubanga ukufa nokuhlupheka phakathi kobuhle bempilo. Kanjalo isono sabawileyo siyananela kuze kube namuhla, nezikhathi zokwahlulelwa kwabo sezifikile! Anazi yini, bantabami, ukuthi niyokwahlulela izingelosi?

Ngubani oyokwehlukanisa nothando lwami? Noma ukuhlupheka, noma usizi, noma ukushushiswa, noma indlala, noma ubunqunu, noma ingozi, noma inkemba? Qha! Kuzo zonke lezi zinto ningabanqobi ngokwedlulele kimi uKristu Jesu iNkosi yenu enyukile nephiwe amandla, ngokuba uYahweh wabelane nami ngesihlalo sakhe sobukhosi futhi nami ngizokwabelana ngesihlalo sami sobukhosi nabathembekileyo, labo abangithandayo nalabo engibathandayo. Qondani ukuthi uYahweh uqobo unalele, futhi uYahweh uthume mina, iNkosi yenu, uMbusi nomvikeli wenu. Ngokuba ngiyinqaba yenu eqinile nedwala lokuvikela, ngokuba ngiyiDwala laPhakade, iNkosi noMsindisi wenu. Futhi ngingumcwebisi ohlezi nesivuthelo nesando esandleni ukubhebhezela imililo yokuhlambulula, ngisusa amanyela! Futhi nginguye oshaya futhi asonge izinsimbi eziyigugu zendlu yengcebo kaYahweh, ezingcebo zakhe eziningi eziyigugu okungamadodana kaNkulunkulu, aze abe sekupheleleni okukhazimulayo, futhi imisebenzi yami isizofezeka kini Bantabami, nina zithelo zami zokuqala, ababiziweyo nabakhethiweyo nabathembekileyo.

Bhekani! Sifikile isikhathi sokuqedelwa nokuhlengwa kwenu ngokuphelele, zithandwa zami, futhi ngizokuqedela ukukhululwa kwenu futhi nizongena manje ephakadeni lamaphakade! Futhi ngokuphazima kweso nizozithola nimi egumbini lesihlalo sobukhosi sikaYahweh ngokumangala kwasezulwini nenjabulo engunaphakade, ngokuba manje nizongena enjabulweni yeNkosi yenu. Futhi nizozithola nigubuzelwe ukukhanya okukhazimulayo kwaphakade kukaYahweh, uNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade, nokukhanya okungapheliyo kweNdodana Yokulunga, futhi niyodalwa kabusha ngokuphelele, futhi niyomangala ngobukhazikhazi eniyogqokiswa bona. Bantabami, yonke imisebenzi enake nayenza egameni lami nikhonza uKristu, njengoba nikhonze abangcwele futhi nisakhonza, ilotshiwe emazulwini! Iso alikabonanga, nendlebe ayikezwa, futhi akukaze kungene enhliziyweni yomuntu izinto uYahweh azilungiselele labo abamthandayo, nomvuzo wenu usesandleni sami, ngokuba ningabanqobi bami, futhi nginidale kabusha kimi kuze kube phakade, futhi anisoze nabhubha, futhi ifa lenu lingelaphakade elingapheliyo, nenkazimulo yenu ingeyaphakade. Ngokuba njengoba bethembekile entweni encane kakhulu, ngizobenza babe ngababusi phezu kokuningi.

Ngesikhashana, ngomzuzwana, ngokuphazima kweso ukuhlengwa kwenu ngokuphelele kuyokwenzeka futhi zonke izinkinga zenu ziyoshabalala masinyane. Ngokuphazima kweso ngeke kusaba khona ukugula, ngokuphazima kweso ngeke kusaba khona ubuhlungu, ngokuphazima kweso ngeke kusaba khona ukwesaba, ngokuphazima kweso niyoba senhlanganweni ephelele noYahweh, ngokuphazima kweso niyoba senhlanganweni ephelele noYeshua HaMashiach, ngokuphazima kweso niyozithola nigcwele injabulo, ukuthula, nokukhanya kwaphakade kuyokhanya kini ingunaphakade. Ngokuba njengoba nimkhazimulisile iWundlu likaNkulunkulu, iNgonyama yesiZwe sakwaJuda manje iyonikhazimulisa nina, ngokuba nginguye enikholwe kuye, futhi nginguye othembekileyo noqinisileyo, ogcinile lokho enikuphathise kimi kuze kube lolu suku. Futhi nina enginenze nahlakanipha, niyokhanya kuze kube phakade, njengokukhanya komkhathi; futhi nina eniphendulele abaningi ekulungeni, niyokhanya njengezinkanyezi, kuze kube phakade naphakade. Ngakho-ke manje yazini lokhu, bantabami, ukuthi manje sekuyisikhathi sokushiya izinkathazo zalo mhlaba niphumule kimi futhi nihlale nimsulwa njengentombi egazini lami lokuhlawulela.

Bhekani! Ummangaleli wabazalwane uphonswa phansi, uphonswa phansi, leyo nyoka endala, lowo drakoni omkhulu obomvu omsila wakhe wadonsa ingxenye yesithathu yezinkanyezi waziphonsa emhlabeni. Futhi kwaba khona impi ezulwini, uMikayeli nezingelosi zakhe balwa nodrakoni, nodrakoni walwa nezingelosi zakhe. Kodwa abanqobanga, nendawo yabo ayabe isatholakala ezulwini. Futhi udrakoni omkhulu, leyo nyoka endala, ebizwa ngokuthi udeveli noSathane, waphonswa phansi, yena okhohlisa umhlaba wonke: uphonswa emhlabeni, nezingelosi zakhe zaphonswa phansi kanye naye. Ngokuba kwaba khona ukubenyezela nokuduma futhi kwaba khona ukuzamazama, nesibonakaliso sobukhona bakhe sezwiwa, namanga omkhohlisi onolimi oluhlukene asevele esabalele ngoba bahlale beqaphele ukufika kwesibonakaliso sakhe. Isibonakaliso sakhe sasingaphezu kwesithombe sakhe esihlambalazayo.

Maye kulabo abakhileyo emhlabeni ngokuba udeveli nezingelosi zakhe baphonswe phansi phakathi kwenu futhi baboshelwe emhlabeni ukuze bahlulelwe futhi ayikho indawo ezulwini kubo kuze kube phakade futhi abasoze baphinde bangene. Thokozani nina mazulu, ngokuba ummangaleli wabazalwane uphonswe phansi obamangalelayo imini nobusuku phambi kukaNkulunkulu wethu, nendawo yakhe ayisekho emazulwini kuze kube phakade. Manje kuza insindiso, manje kuza inkazimulo yabangcwele, futhi bayokhazinyuliswa ngokuba bayokhazimulisa uYahweh kimi uKristu Jesu futhi ngiyobagqokisa ukulunga kwaphakade, ilineni elimhlophe qwa elingenabala elingasoze langcoliswa, futhi ngiyobanika umvuzo waphakade, ngibenze babe ngamaKhosi nabaPristi phambi kukaYahweh futhi bayobusa esihlalweni sami sobukhosi ngokothando nokwethembeka kwabo. Kwabathembekileyo kunomvuzo omkhulu, nakwabangathembekile kukhona ukulahlekelwa, kodwa bayosindiswa, kungathi ngomlilo.

Yizwani, nonke enihlala emhlabeni, nakuba niyizithulu futhi nalenqaba izibonakaliso zami! Udeveli manje usephonswe phansi phakathi kwenu, futhi uyazi ukuthi unesikhathi esifushane. Unolaka olukhulu nempindiselo, ngokuba uyisitha esinqotshiwe esiboshelwe izinjongo zikaYahweh eziyofezeka kulelo chibi elivuthayo le-cobalt blue lokubhujiswa okuqhubekayo. Futhi ngenxa yokuthi nina, o bantu bemvelo, nithanda ubumnyama kunokukhanya, ngenxa yokuthi nina, o bantu bemvelo, nithanda imisebenzi yenu emibi futhi nidelela isandla sami esisindisayo somusa, (Ngokuba wonke umuntu owenza okubi, uyakuzonda ukukhanya, futhi akezi ekukhanyeni, funa izenzo zakhe zivezwe) ngakho-ke manje ngizoninika ububi enibufisa kangaka. Ngenxa yokuthi manje nihlala etabernakele likaMoloki niphakamisa inkanyezi kankulunkulu wenu uRemfan esibhengezweni senu, izithombe enazenzela zona ukuze nizikhonze.

Njengoba ninikela ngezingane kuMoloki, njengoba nikhonza uBaphomet wenu oyisinengiso, bhekani! Udrakoni wenu manje usephakathi kwenu. Futhi niyofunda mathupha ukuthi isela alizi ngaphandle kokweba, ukubulala, nokubhubhisa. Ngaphandle kokuthi manje ngeke kusaba khona isifihlo, ngeke kusaba khona umvimbi, ngeke kusaba khona umusa nomusa ovela emazulwini ngoba nikhethe udrakoni njengonkulunkulu wenu, futhi nikhethe uBaraba njengomsindisi nenkosi yenu. Ngakho-ke manje ngibanika ukuba babuse phezu kwenu njengoba izinhliziyo zenu ezimbi zifisa kanjalo. Bhekani! Nakhu ukuhlakanipha, onokuqonda makabale inani lesilo ngokuba lisenkanyezini kaRemfan abayiphethe esibhengezweni sabo, ngokuba bayisinagoge likaSathane abafuna ukweba izwe babulale abakhileyo kulo abafanele futhi ngiyomsindisa uJakobe!

Bhekani! USathane ungena kuBaraba, futhi uthatha izintambo, futhi izibonakaliso zakhe ezingamanga nezimangaliso kuzoholela ekubhujisweni kwenu ngokuphelele. Ngokuba uyosebenzisa izimangalo zakhe zokuthi uNkulunkulu yedwa owamsindisa ukuze athi uthunywe nguNkulunkulu nokuthi unguNkulunkulu, ngokuba bakholwa kanjalo ukuthi banguNkulunkulu enyameni futhi konke okunye kuyizilwane. Uyosebenzisa izibonakaliso zakhe ezingamanga nezimangaliso futhi kanye nesilo sasolwandle uyolethela incithakalo enkulu emhlabeni, ngokuba nikhethe udrakoni, futhi manje niyodla ngokugcwele ukungcola okuphelele okulethwa ngudrakoni.

Nina, o bakhileyo emhlabeni, niwukhafulele umusa nesihawu sikaYahweh awuthumela kimi, ebusweni bukaJesu Kristu, futhi njengoba nalenqaba iWundlu likaNkulunkulu, manje seniyohlangana neNgonyama yesiZwe sakwaJuda. Ngokuba ngeza ngokuthula nothando nokuzwana okuhle nomusa nesihawu, futhi ngokobuwula nenqabile insindiso esikhundleni sokufa kwenu nesijeziso saphakade, ngokuba ayikho enye inketho. Ukukhafulela uYahweh lapho elula isandla sakhe ngokuthula nothando, eze enikela ngokuphila kweNdodana yakhe ezelwe yodwa, kuyisono saphakade futhi akukho ukuphunyuka olakeni ngaphandle kokubiza igama elinamandla likaJesu kuze kube sekupheleni, nalowo ongathandi ukuphila kwakhe kuze kube sekupheleni uyosindiswa, nesixuku sezixuku siyosindiswa.

Kodwa nina, o muntu wemvelo, nikhethe injabulo yesono lapho ninikwa umusa nanikwa isikhathi sokuphenduka. Ngokuba ngelulele isandla kini, ngelulele isandla koyihlo, ngelulele isandla koyihlo baboyihlo, emuva eminyakeni eminingi yama-jubilee manje kusukela ngathuma abaphostoli bami nabaprofethi futhi imfihlakalo enkulu kaNkulunkulu imenyezelwe kuze kube semikhawulweni yomhlaba! Futhi manje ukwenqaba kwenu kuphelele, futhi ngiyamukela isinqumo senu sokugcina, futhi nginguKristu Jesu, futhi nginguye manje othi izikhathi zabeZizwe seziphelile futhi manje kuza ukwahlulela kuqala endlini kaNkulunkulu. Njengoba izindebe zenu zimphikile uYahweh, iNkosi yemiMoya, ifa lenu elimiselweyo manje likulelo chibi elikhulu lomlilo, isibabule esigingqikayo, futhi Bekungaba kuhle kini ukuba anizange nizalwe. Nicabanga ngobuwula ukuthi ninaso sonke isikhathi emhlabeni nokuthi uYahweh uzoqhubeka nokulinda mhlawumbe ngolunye usuku ningaphendukela ekulungeni ezonweni zenu ezinengekayo. Futhi nginitshela ngokusobala manje, isikhathi sesiphelile futhi kwabaningi balahlekelwe unomphela yithuba labo lokuphila okuphakade ngisho nethuba labo lokuba phakathi koMlobokazi wami oyozobukwa yibo bonke ngalolo suku.

Ngokuba kunjalo isiphetho sabo bonke abathatha uphawu. Futhi ngakho ngimemezela ezizweni futhi:

UMA UMUNTU EKHONZA ISILO NOMFANEKISO WASO, AMUKELE UPHAWU LWASO ESIHLATHINI SAKHE, NOMA ESANDLENI SAKHE, YENA UYOPHENDULA IWAYINI LOLAKA LUNKULUNKULU, ELITHULULWE LINGAXUTSHIWE ENDEBENI YOKUTHUKUTHELA KWAKHE, FUTHI UYOHLUSHWA NGOMLILO NESIBABULE, EBUKHONENI BEZINGELOSI EZINGCWELE, NASEBUKHONENI BEWUNDLU: KANYE INTUTHU YOKUHLUSHWA KWABO IKHUPHUKA KUZE KUBE PHAKADE NAPAKADE. FUTHI ABANAKUPHUMULA EMINI NOMA EBUSUKU, ABONA ABAKHONZA ISILO NOMFANEKISO WASO, NANOMA NGUBANI OWAMUKELA UPHAWU LWEGAMA LASO.

Ngokuba uphawu luyasondela ngempela. Kuyoba yindlela yokungena emphakathini wabo, futhi bayokutshela ukuthi luyikhambi. Bayokutshela ukuthi luzoletha ukuphila okuphakade, bayokutshela ukuthi ngeke uphinde ugule, bayokutshela amanga amaningi ukuze uthathe umjovo wabo oyisilo, futhi bayokholisa abaningi ukuthi kungokwabo okuhle. Bhekani! Abawileyo beza bezenza abafokazi ngobuchwepheshe babo ezandleni zabo. Qondani ukuthi uphawu luyokwenza nibe yizigqila zobuchwepheshe babo, ngokuba bayozihlanganisa nenzalo yabantu: kodwa abayikunamathelana, njengoba insimbi ingaxutshwa nobumba. Uphawu lwabo oluyisilo, ukulunywa yinyoka, luzobhubhisa ngokuphelele bonke abathatha ubuthi babo futhi akukho ukuphunyuka.

Bhekani! Futhi ngezwa izwi elikhulu livela eThempelini, lithi ezingelosini eziyisikhombisa, Hambani nina, nithulule izitsha eziyisikhombisa zolaka lukaNkulunkulu emhlabeni. Futhi owokuqala wahamba wathululela isitsha sakhe emhlabeni: kwase kuvela isilonda esibi nesibuhlungu kubantu, ababenophawu lwesilo, nakubo ababekhonza umfanekiso waso. Futhi ngakho leli kusasa lilindele bonke abazamukela ngokuzithandela ukuhlushwa kwabo kwaphakade nalabo abakhethe ukufa kwesibili. Konke ngenxa yokuthi nimlahle uJesu waseNazaretha, obezonisindisa ngokuphelele. Futhi bonke abathatha uphawu bayoba nezilonda zabo ezimbi nezibuhlungu futhi bonke abathatha uphawu bayoba phakade efeni labo kulawo malangabi omlilo aluhlaza okwesibhakabhaka ebumnyameni obukhulu phakade, kodwa lesi siphetho sikhethwa ngokuzithandela yilabo esihloselwe bona, kanjalo kuyoba njalo!

Futhi ngakho ukuhlukaniswa kokugcina kuyaqala manje futhi njengoba kwakunempi emazulwini manje kuzoba nempi emhlabeni ngokuba ngizothatha ukuthula ngokushesha futhi ngokushesha umvimbi uyosuswa futhi uhlanya olusheshayo luyogqashuka emhlabeni wonke ngokuba okubi ngeke kusavinjelwa, futhi ngiyonika umuntu ngamunye ngokwemisebenzi yakhe. Futhi ngakho izikhathi zabeZizwe sezivaliwe futhi impi isizogqashuka emhlabeni wonke, futhi bayolungisa kabusha imingcele yabo ibe umbuso wesilo wokugcina lapho udrakoni, isilo sasolwandle nesilo sasemhlabeni naye onguMoshiach wabo wamanga bayovuka khona futhi inkinga kaJakobe iyogqashuka emhlabeni wonke ngokuphazima kweso, futhi ngeke baphunyuke!

Bhekani! Ngithumela ivangeli lami laphakade, ngokuba ukuthunyelwa kwalo kwamenyezelwa kusukela kudala nguJohane uMambuli lapho ngimenza abhale: Ngabona enye ingelosi indiza emkhathini, inevangeli laphakade, ukulishumayeza labo abakhileyo emhlabeni, nakuzo zonke izizwe, nezihlobo, nezilimi, nabantu. Bhekani! Ubufakazi bukaJesu bungumoya wesiprofetho futhi yazini ukuthi amazwi ami ayothola amehlo, izindlebe, nezinhliziyo zabo bonke abathunyelwe kubo futhi aMazwi evangeli laphakade embuliwe ngesikhathi sawo futhi ayolondolozelwa labo abathunyelwe kubo, ngakho-ke lalelani izwi likaMoshiach wenu weQiniso! Ngokuba nginguYeshua HaMashiach!

Futhi ngakho aMazwi kaJohane uMambuli ema njengenduduzo nesibani sethemba kulabo abasazothola insindiso:

IsAmbulo 21:1-7

Ngase ngibona izulu elisha nomhlaba omusha: ngokuba izulu lokuqala nomhlaba wokuqala kwase kudlulile, nolwandle lwalungasekho. Mina Johane ngabona umuzi ongcwele, iJerusalema elisha, wehla uvela kuNkulunkulu ezulwini, ulungiswe njengomlobokazi ohlotshiselwe umyeni wakhe. Ngase ngizwa izwi elikhulu livela ezulwini, lithi, Bheka, itabernakele likaNkulunkulu likubantu, futhi uyohlala nabo: bayoba ngabantu bakhe, noNkulunkulu uqobo uyoba nguNkulunkulu wabo kanye nabo. Futhi uNkulunkulu uyokwesula zonke izinyembezi emehlweni abo: ngeke kusaba khona ukufa, nosizi, nokukhala, futhi ngeke kusaba khona ubuhlungu: ngokuba izinto zokuqala sezidlulile. Nalowo owayehlezi esihlalweni sobukhosi, uthi, Bheka, ngenza zonke izinto zibe zintsha: wathi kimi, Bhala: ngokuba la mazwi athembekile futhi ayiqiniso. Futhi wathi kimi, Sekwenzekile, ngingu-Alfa no-Omega, isiqalo nesiphetho. Ngiyomnika owomileyo, emthonjeni wamanzi okuphila ngesihle. Onqobayo, uyodla ifa lazo zonke izinto, futhi ngiyoba nguNkulunkulu wakhe, futhi yena uyoba yindodana yami.

Nginguye uYahweh amnike zonke izinto ezulwini nasemhlabeni.
