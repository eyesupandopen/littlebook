Ngilindeni Isikhashana: Ngeke Ngilibale

Ngilindeni isikhashana, bantwana bami, futhi ngeke ngilibale. Angeke nginishiye noma nginilahle, ngoba niyinhliziyo kaBaba wami futhi niyinhliziyo yami uqobo. NinguMlobokazi wami, futhi yimuphi umkhwenyana oshiya umlobokazi wakhe ekwahlulelweni kwezoni?

Yazini ukuthi mina, iNkosi uJesu Kristu, ngizobahlenga abantu bami. Anifundanga yini emiBhalweni Engcwele? UBaba wami akamhlwathanga yini u-Enoke wamyisa emazulwini? UBaba wami akamhlwathanga yini u-Eliya wamyisa emazulwini? UBaba wami akabakhiphanga yini abantwana bakwa-Israyeli eGibhithe? Akamkhululanga yini uLoti eSodoma ngaphambi kokuba umlilo wehlele kuleyo mizi, wayibhubhisa yonke? Kakhulu kangakanani-ke ngizokhulula umlobokazi wami engamthenga ngegazi lami esandleni sokwahlulelwa osekuzokwehlela lo mhlaba? Nizothi kuze kube nini nginobuwula, mina Nkosi yenu, noBaba wami, uNkulunkulu wenu?

Ngininikile ukuphila okuphakade, futhi kunomvuzo omkhulu olondoloziwe kwabathembekileyo ezulwini, ngakho-ke ningesabi, njengoba ifa lenu selizogcwaliseka. NginguMelusi olungileyo ohlangula umhlambi ezimpisini ezihahela igazi. Zona lezi zimpisi maduze zizozwa kuthiwa, "Sukani kimi, nina benzi bokubi; angikaze nginazi, nakuba nithi, Nkosi, Nkosi!" NginguMelusi othembekileyo ongalahlekelwanga noyedwa uBaba anginike yena, ngaphandle kwendodana yokulahlwa eyangikhaphela ngezinhlamvu zesiliva ezingamashumi amathathu futhi umbhalo wagcwaliseka ngesiphambeko sayo nokwenqaba kwayo. Zifundo zini ezikhona kulokho kini, bantwana bami, ngaphandle kokuthi aninakulahleka esandleni sami futhi akakho onganihlwithel awaya kimi?

Ningakwesabi ukwahlulelwa kwabenzi bokubi, nina bathembekile bami, futhi niyazi ukuthi ulaka lukaBaba wenu alusoze lwanithinta. Yimuphi ubaba phakathi kwenu onganikela ngisho oyedwa kubantwana bakhe ezandleni zesitha? Nakuba lokhu kuyisono esivamile phakathi kwabantu basemhlabeni, akukho sithunzi sokuphenduka kuYise wezinkanyiso! Ngakho-ke ningesabi nibe nokuthula. Yekani imisebenzi yenu nphumule kimi. Phishekelani imisebenzi yami nijabulele ukuphumula kimi, iNkosi uJesu Kristu, owazinikela waba umhlatshelo wezono zabo bonke kanye kuphela.

O, nina bantwana bomhlaba, izono zenu ziphambi kwami, futhi nizibekelele ulaka olukhulu nokwahlulelwa! Esikhundleni sokuqongelela ifa lezibusiso, niqongelele ulaka phezu kolaka lwalolu suku olukhulu oseluzonifica. O, amazwi enu ayize eniwakhulumile, nithi, "Iphi iNkosi; iyinsumansumane nje engakaze ibekhona futhi ikusasa liyoba njenganamuhla nezolo." Ukwahlulelwa kwenu kuzonifica kungazelelwe, nezigameko zizoshesha kangangokuthi nizolahleka ekuhluphekeni. Amazwi enu amabi nezindlela zenu ezimbi kuzoletha phezu kwenu imbubhiso enkulu ngokuphazima kweso. Konke enikuthandayo kuyosuswa kini, nayo yonke ingcebo yenu iyonyamalala ezandleni zenu njengoba ngiyinikele ezandleni zalabo abanizondayo, ngoba ningizonde ngaphandle kwesizathu.

Ulaka lweWundlu seluzokwehliselwa phezu kwenu, futhi imihelo isondelene kakhulu manje, isondelene kangangokuthi niyiphambanisa nolaka lweWundlu. Yazi, wena muntu oyisiwula, ukuthi ekubekezeleni kwami ngiyakuxwayisa ngokubhujiswa kwakho, kodwa usahleka usulu ungenakukholwa. Maduze kakhulu ngizosusaphakathi kwenu umvimbeli, futhi ngizosusaphakathi kwenu uMlobokazi wami, bese imbubhiso ephelele ifika phezu kwenu kungazelelwe. USathane useluhlelile ubutho lwakhe nezikhali zakhe, elungele ukukhipha lonke ulaka nentukuthelo yakhe phezu kwenu, futhi ngininikele ezandleni zakhe. Isizwe senu esikhulu sesisuliwe futhi siphawulelwe imbubhiso ephelele; o, sengathi ningawaqonda amazwi athi, iBhabhiloni EyiMfihlakalo. Nokho kusesekhona isikhathi sokuphenduka, kodwa iningi lenu ngeke likwenze ezindleleni zenu ezinenkani zobuhlubuki.

Ukwahlulelwa kwenu kuzofika phezu kwenu ngezindlela ezimbili ezinkulu: ngaphakathi nangaphandle. Isifebe esikhulu sizodwengulwa sibe yizicucu; siyodliwa ngamanzi sishiswe ngomlilo, sisitshekelwe ngumashayabhuqe nendlala. Yebo, wena muntu wasemhlabeni, ngubani oyokukhulula esandleni sami? Akekho ozayo ukuzonisindisa. Ukubhujiswa kwenu sekuseduze. Amabutho abomvu angena esizweni senu phambi kwamehlo enu, futhi anizange ninake inqobo nje uma ninokuzijabulisa futhi nikhuluphele ngengcebo yesikhashana eyayingeyona eyenu kwasekuqaleni, ngoba umhlaba ungowenkosi nokugcwala kwawo. Anigcinanga ngokuzibizela imbubhiso, kodwa nithengise ngisho nabantwana benu nabazukulu benu embubhisweni ephelele, kodwa nisahleka usulu. Inzalo yenu ilahlekile. Ngeke nisahleka usulu isikhathi eside njengoba zonke izindlela zenu ezimbi neziphambukile zizonicwilisa osizini, ekuhlushweni, nasekubhujisweni, nakuba isixuku esikhulu siyocwengwa ngokuhlupheka.

Bonke abaphendukela kimi, babize igama lami, futhi bangayithandi impilo yabo kuze kube sekupheleni, ngiyobavuza ingunaphakade! Abasoze bahlupheka futhi, kodwa ukuhlupheka kwenu akusoze kwaphela, nina ababi. Kodwa wena, wena muntu wemvelo olahla uNkulunkulu, owamemezela impi kuMdali wakho wafisa ukuhlubuka kimi nezindlela zami. Ukubhujiswa kwakho kuzofika ngokuzumayo nangaphandle kosizo. Senibe yisizwe sezifebe, futhi kufaneleke kangakanani ukuthi izizwe zenu zibizwe ngesifebe emiBhalweni Engcwele. Amabutho ayoqeda okusele kwesizwe senu esake saba sikhulu nesinamandla, futhi ukuhlanjululwa okukhulu kuzofika ezweni, nesijeziso esikhulu siyonidla ngenxa yezindlela zenu ezimbi.

Ngisuse konke kini ngenxa yezindlela zenu zokuhlubuka nenhliziyo elukhuni; futhi nakuba ngazikhokhela izono zenu, nisalile isipho sami, nakhetha ukuzikhokhela nina, futhi niyozisola ngakho kuze kube phakade. Lapho umvimbeli esuswa, imbubhiso esheshayo iyoba umngane wenu; futhi ninikele nabantwana benu esiphethweni esifana nesenu, nakuba abaningi beyosindiswa. Ngisho nabanye babantwana benu bayophendukela kimi, futhi ngiyobamukela enkazimulweni, nakuba nibavimbile kimi, futhi nina niyongena ekulahlweni kulelo chibi elikhulu elilindele uSathane nezingelosi zakhe.

Bantwana bami abancane, ningesabi. Qhubekani nokuma niqine kimi kulo mzuzu wokugcina, yebo, umzuzu wenu wokugcina kulo mhlaba ofayo. Nginibizile, nginikhethile, nginikhethe ngesandla ngenxa yothando lwenu kimi nokuthembeka kwenu, futhi nemivuzo emikhulu inilindile endlini kaBaba. Nizoyiswa endaweni ephephile nomvuzo omkhulu; nakuba abanye beyolahlekelwa, bonke abakhethiweyo bami bayosindiswa ngokuphelele nangokugcwele.

Gxilani kimi, hlanganyelani nami; ngiseduze ukudlula umoya wenu olandelayo. Yazini, nina bakhethiweyo bami, ukuthi nginilwela; futhi nakuba ukushushiswa nokuhlupheka kwanda, niyosindiswa olakeni. Ngakho-ke, zihlanzeni ngokuphuma kulo mhlaba futhi nilahle izindlela zawo ezimbi. Gezani izandla zenu ezonweni zawo futhi nisuke ebubini bawo, ngoba noma ngubani osembhedeni wesifebe uyohlupheka kakhulu. Zihlanzeni ngegazi lami elihlawulelayo futhi nizigeze ngokuphelele. Bhekani, nginibeke uphawu lukaBaba, futhi ngizonihlenga ngokugcwele nginidale kabusha ngomfanekiso wami. Yebo, niyoba ngabantu abasha futhi niyisidalwa esisha kimi, futhi maduze kuzoba khona izulu elisha nomhlaba omusha, ngoba uBaba wenza zonke izinto zibe zisha. Ngakho-ke manini niqine futhi niyazi ukuthi uthando lwami lungumlilo ovuthayo, futhi niyigugu elikhulu nilangazelelwa. Maduze nizoba nami.
