i-AmeruKa: Yembuliwe futhi Yahlulelwa! UMkhwenyana Uyala UMakoti Wakhe, Zilungiselele: Ngiyeza Ngizokulanda!

Zwanini aMazwi kaYeshua HaMashiach, onguKristu Jesu, futhi qondani inkulumo yaMi ukuze nazi ukuqala kweqiniso kweBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo, isimo salo, nenjongo yalo. Ngoba, ngempela, liyizwe leNyoka Enophaphe, khona kanye i-AmeruKa, izwe elinyenya ngokunyenya, futhi lidle izakhamuzi zalo, futhi lihlinze abangenacala, futhi lenze umnikelo ngoyise nonina ngobuningi obukhulu kusukela ezikhathini zasendulo, kuze kube manje. Impela izwe eliqalekisiwe elingcolisa izakhamuzi zalo ngokunyenya, nangobuningi bezinsalela zemikhosi engcolisayo nezonakalisayo yemihlatshelo enegazi nenesihluku yasemandulo, embiwe kuso sonke lesi sizwe esingcolile ezinqwabeni eziningi zezithombe, imikhuba, ukushiswa kweThofeti, negazi lomhlatshelo eligeleza ngokukhululeka. Lelo gazi selicwilise umhlabathi walo lapho umhlaba ukhala kakhulu ufuna ubulungiswa ngegazi elichithekile, nelisachithwa ngobuningi obukhulu. Yona leyo mihlaba engcolile ekhala ngamandla ngezwi elikhulu emazulwini ifuna ubulungiswa khona manje, lapho umhlaba uzamazama kakhulu njengendoda edakiwe isizowa, uze ufune ukwahlulela okulungileyo okungaphuzi ukufika.

Futhi wena Bhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo, yebo wena: Wena uyizwekazi elingcwele nelingcolile, izwe elakhiwa ekugcineni kusukela ezinsukwini zikaNowa, izwe elinikezelwe kuHeylel Ben Shachar nezindlela zenyoka, izwe elicwile egazini labangenacala ngenxa yezimiso nezenzo zenzalo engcolile yezinyoka nabangcolisi abaphuma bayongcolisa ezikhathini zasendulo. Ngempela, izwe eligodliwe ngokunyenya yizandla ezifihliwe zezithunywa eziwileyo, ligodlelwe futhi lihloselwe ukuba yi-Atlantis yawo Entsha, isizwe esakhelwe futhi sanikezelwa ukuvuka kwesilo sasolwandle nesilo sasemhlabeni. Yebo, isizwe esizishaya umKristu, kodwa eqinisweni siyisizwe samasathane asekusithekeni azisungula futhi ahlala ngokunethezeka ezikhungweni zaso ezingcolile nezingcolisayo (kusukela kwabancane kuya kwabakhulu) futhi yilezi zikhungo ezibuswa yizinhlangano eziyimfihlo ezizinikele kuHeylel Ben Shachar (abambiza ngokungazi nangokuyiphutha ngokuthi uLusifa), bezicabangela ngokungazi njengabakhanyayo nabakhanyiselwe, ngisho nasenkohlisweni yabo lapho bona ngokwabo beboshelwe ingunaphakade ezifungweni zabo ezingcwele, bekhohlisa, kodwa ngaso sonke isikhathi bekhohliswa yilabo ababoshelwe kubo njengamadodana esono ezithunyweni eziwileyo kuze kube phakade.

Makwazeke ukuthi iBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo yi-United States Of America, endulo eyayibizwa ngokuthi i-Ameru-Ca, iZwe leNyoka Enophaphe, eliye lanyakaza lazenza leso sifebe esikhulu esihleli phezu kwamanzi amaningi futhi siyisizwe esenziwa, ukuze isilo sasolwandle nesilo sasemhlabeni sivuke emlotheni waso ezikhathini zokwembulwa kwaso. Isizwe esiphingayo, isizwe sobufebe, isizwe esiyisifebe esikhulu esizibiza ngokuthi singumlandeli olungileyo kaJesu waseNazaretha; kodwa eqinisweni siyisizwe abakhi baso bakhululekile eDweni leSisekelo leqiniso, lelo litshe eliyinhloko lokukhonjwa elikhubekisa abangakholwayo, khona kanye uJesu waseNazaretha onguYeshua HaMashiach.

Ngeqiniso, i-AmeruKa isiphenduke la ma-United States anamuhla, futhi iyisizwe esamiswa ngezandla zawo ezingcwele nezifihliwe, izwe lezinhlangano eziyimfihlo ezinikezelwe enyokeni ngezimvunulo eziyimfihlo; futhi njengoba amandla eRosy Cross angena e-AmeruKa, kanjalo kwamiswa isendlalelo sawo sokugcina se-Atlantis Entsha. Icebo elalifihliwe isikhathi eside elalizohlanganisa futhi lididise la mahlelo enyoka emhlabeni wonke ngokubhubhisa nangokwenza umhlatshelo ngabaningi abavela esigabeni sabapristi balezi zimpucuko eziwileyo, kanye nabaningi abavela kulezi zimpucuko bona ngokwabo ababeyofuna ngokuzidla nangobuwula ukumisa yona leyo nyoka abazibophezele kuyo ngezifungo eziningi ezinegazi ukuyikhonza ngokuphelele, bengaqondi isimo sento ababeboshelwe kuyo, nakuba izinsalela zalaba bantu zikulezi zizwe nanamuhla.

Ngalokho wena uyi-AmeruKa: Ngoba ngikhuluma nezwekazi ukuthi kusukela enyakatho kuya eningizimu futhi kusukela empumalanga kuya entshonalanga liyizwe elinikezelwe kuleyo nyoka enophaphe yasendulo eye yangcolisa futhi yonakalisa la mazwe ngenjongo yayo, njengoba imibuso iye yavuka yawa emingceleni yalo, imibuso eminingi engalotshiwe yenyoka, isizwe senyoka enezimpaphe nendizayo esinikezelwe emiphakathini yobufazi nentando yeningi lapho izixuku ezimbi zazingabusa ngaphansi kwethonya likaHeylel Ben Shachar. Ngaphezu kwalokho, inzalo yakhe yezinyoka iye yangcolisa ngokuphelele amazwe eBhabhiloni eliyimfihlo, okuyizwe elakhiwa njengoba umuntu ezulazula emhlabeni kusukela lapho uMkhumbi wama eNtabeni i-Ararati esikhathini eside esedlule. Kusukela lapho, abantu bayalwa nguYahweh, uMINA ENGINGUYE OMKHULU, beboshwe ngaphansi kwesivumelwano sikaNowa ukuba bazandise futhi bahlakazeke futhi baphinde bagcwalise umhlaba ngokulunga kwesivumelwano; nakuba inzalo kaHamu esikhundleni salokho yazibuthela ndawonye ngokungalaleli okukhulu ukuze izenzele igama eZweni laseShinari, izitshela yona ithi: Wozani, masizakhele umbhoshongo oqinile ukuze sizilonde, futhi ukuze sikwazi ukulwa namazulu ngoba uNimrodi wazenza umzingeli onamandla phambi kweNkosi, yebo umzingeli onamandla wabantu ayebazingela ukuze ajabule.

Kanjalo abantu bafika ezweni lenyoka enophaphe bevela entshonalanga, behamba ngasempumalanga bewela iBering land bridge njengoba yaziwa namuhla, futhi bafika bevela olwandle ngasempumalanga baya entshonalanga bewela i-Atlantic, ngezinzila ezahlukahlukene zohwebo nokuhamba bafika kula mazwe agodlwe nguHeylel Ben Shachar ngezinjongo zakhe ezimbi. Izwe izithunywa eziwileyo ezalithatha ngokuphelele emhlabeni owawukhona ngaleso sikhathi, izwe lapho zangcwaba khona izimfihlo eziningi eziyovunyelwa ukuzithola ngokungazelelwe, njengoba umvimbeli wami esusiwe futhi nokubusa kwazo okufushane kuvunyelwe ukuze kusindiswe uJakobe, futhi ukuze nazo ziwe ekwahlulelweni ngomlilo njengoba lolu daba luqhubeka kusukela ezinsukwini zikaNowa, ukuze zibhuqwe ngokuphelele, ngoba isikhathi sezithunywa eziwileyo sesiphelile ngempela, futhi yimi uKristu Jesu, iNgonyama yeSizwe sakwaJuda, oyochoboza ikhanda lale nyoka yasendulo ekwahlulelweni kokugcina.

Kunjalo-ke ukuthi uKhanani wathola lokho abalindi abakungcwaba, futhi waholwa ngokwezinkanuko zenhliziyo yakhe njengoba angena futhi weba izwe okwakungelona elakhe ukuba ahlale kulo ephula isivumelwano sikaNowa. Nokho wafisa izinsuku zasendulo ezazibhujiswe kulowo zamcolo omkhulu lapho izwe langaleso sikhathi labhubha ngenxa yokuyenga kukaSathane abalindi abawileyo ukuba bazihlanganise nabesifazane ukuze bazale inzalo yabo eqalekisiwe. Leyo nzalo ayilutho ngaphandle kwabantwana benkanuko nabalahliwe, ukulingisa impilo lapho izinengiso zabo ziboshelwe ingunaphakade ukuba zenze izinkanuko ezimbi nezibolile zoyise, futhi ziboshelwe ingunaphakade ukuba zenze izinkanuko zoyise. Ngakho-ke, qondani ukuthi lolu khula olubi, oluyizinengiso, izinengiso ngokuphelele, aluna mpande nagatsha ezulwini, emhlabeni, ngisho nangaphansi komhlaba futhi luzosishulwa ngokuphelele, kuze kube phakade, ngaphansi kwelanga ngokwesithembiso sesivumelwano sika-Adonai-YHVH uMINA ENGINGUYE OMKHULU! Futhi luyoboshwa lushiswe kuleyo mililo emikhulu yempindiselo lapho impethu yalo ingafi khona futhi bonke abaphonswa emlilweni bayokwamukela izono zabo izikhathi eziningi ezifubeni zabo ekwahlulelweni komlilo okuphakade nokulungileyo abangeke baphunyuke kukho.

Ngalokho uHeylel Ben Shachar, leyo nkosana encane ehluliwe, lowo mzigabisi omkhulu nomngcolisi wakho konke okumsulwa, waphuma watshala ukhula lwakhe ensimini kaYahweh, uMINA ENGINGUYE OMKHULU, yona leyo nsimu eyatshalwa lapho u-Elohim ethi makube khona ukukhanya kwaba khona ukukhanya. Ngoba ekuqaleni, u-Elohim wenza amazulu nomhlaba, futhi umhlaba waba yihlane elingenalutho lapho ubumnyama bufika phezu kobuso botwa. Lobo bumnyama kwakunguHeylel Ben Shachar nengxenye yesithathu yezingelosi zakhe ezalandela ukuhlubuka kwakhe futhi zaphonswa emhlabeni ngehlazo elikhulu, nangokwehlulwa. Futhi nguHeylel Ben Shachar, leyo nkosana encane ehluliwe eyakhala ngehlazo nangokwehlulwa njengoba yaphonswa emhlabeni isuka ezulwini lamazulu ngentukuthelo enkulu, igcinelwe ukwahlulelwa kokugcina ngomlilo osukwini olukhulu, yona leyo mililo evutha ngamalangabi aluhlaza ashisayo namsulwa kakhulu alungele ukwamukela bonke abajoyine ukuhlubuka kukaSathane kusukela kwabancane kuya kwabakhulu embusweni wezithunywa eziwileyo.

U-Elohim wathi "Makube khona ukukhanya kwaba khona ukukhanya!" Khona-ke u-Elohim wamisa umhlaba owawusumisiwe kabusha ngomyalo wakhe yedwa, nangaMazwi akhe yedwa, nangeseluleko sakhe yedwa indalo yabunjwa, yenziwa, futhi yadalwa. NgaMazwi akhe anamandla naphakade namandla neseluleko sakhe yedwa. Ngoba uYHVH, uMINA ENGINGUYE OMKHULU, akadingi meluleki nakuba isihlalo sakhe sobukhosi sizungezwe yizinkulungwane eziyishumi eziphindwe ngezinkulungwane eziyishumi, futhi akukho sidalwa esinjengoNkulunkulu oPhakade wokulunga, nakuba beqamba amanga futhi bazenza abangeyibo! Kanjalo umhlaba wenziwa nakho konke okuqukethwe kuwo futhi wona lowo mhlaba wanikwa u-Adamu, ngoba umuntu wenzelwa umhlaba, nomhlaba wenzelwa umuntu, owadalwa ngomfanekiso womdali wakhe, uYHVH - uMINA ENGINGUYE OMKHULU!

Kodwa leyo nyoka enobuqili yahlasela ifa lika-Adamu ngobuqili bayo, ukuhlasela nokwephula okwahlehliswa ngokuphelele eKalvari, lapho mina, Jesu waseNazaretha, ngakhuluma amazwi aphakade athi, "Sekuphelile!"; kanjalo-ke, sengithengile isintu, umhlaba, nakho konke okukuwo ngegazi lami lokuhlawulela. Ngakho-ke, umhlaba ungowenkosi nokugcwala kwawo, futhi ngiza njengeNgonyama yeSizwe sakwaJuda ukuzokwahlulela nokulwa, ngakho zwanini ukubhonga kwami, nina abakhileyo emhlabeni ngoba izikhathi zenu sezifikile!

Ngalokho izithunywa eziwileyo ziphumile zayongcolisa futhi ziguqule indalo isuke ekupheleleni kwayo kwasekuqaleni eyadalwa ngakho. Kodwa, zona azikwazi ukudala ngoba uYHVH, uMINA ENGINGUYE OMKHULU, akazange azidale zinamandla noma amakhono anjalo. Ngakho-ke, qondani ukuthi lezi zithunywa eziwileyo ziyazigabisa ngokuthi zingonkulunkulu kanti bonke bangabazenzisi abazenzayo. Selah.

Ngakho-ke, zwanini amazwi kaYeshua HaMashiach, khona kanye uKristu Jesu, njengoba ngiza ukuzokwembulela ibandla lami, umzimba wami, ngisho nomakoti wami iqiniso mayelana nalawo mazwe akhohlakele nangcolile lapho abaningi benu bazalelwa khona, futhi bahlala khona. Ngoba okhokho benu bazulazula emhlabeni ngaphansi kwesijeziso sika-Adonai-YHVH, uMINA ENGINGUYE OMKHULU, futhi kwakungaphansi kwesijeziso esikhulu lapho uYahweh axosha khona izizwe zasenyakatho zakwa-Israyeli phakathi kwezizwe enencwadi yesehlukaniso esandleni. Lapho, zihlakazekile phakathi kwezizwe ziye zazulazula futhi zagcinwa, futhi zalondolozwa kusukela lapho, futhi eziningi zazo manje ziyingxenye ka-Israyeli kaNkulunkulu, kuze kube phakade naphakade! Zigcinwe ngumfundisi wazo, ngoba zabambelela emthethweni kaMose njengoba zazihambela emazweni asenyakatho ngemva kokunqotshwa ngokungalaleli ngezandla zama-Asiriya. Ngakho izizwe zasenyakatho zahlukaniswa futhi zaphonswa ezizweni, kodwa zase zihlakazekile ekudingisweni lapho abaseBhabhiloni benqoba futhi bajezisa uMbuso waseNingizimu, labo abangalaleli phakathi kokhokho bami bezizwe zakwaJuda noBenjamini nabanye, abakha uMbuso wakwaJuda ezinsukwini zasendulo. Wonawona owaba yiJudiya lapho i-Idumea neJuda kwahlanganiswa ngobukhali benkemba.

Kanjalo izimvu ezilahlekileyo zendlu ka-Israyeli ziye zahlakazeka phakathi kwezizwe ngenxa yokungalaleli kwazo, nakuba insali iye yahlala ezweni lesithembiso kuze kube namuhla. UMbuso waseNyakatho wahlakazeka phakathi kwezizwe zasenyakatho, emazweni kaGogi noMagogi, eYurophu yonkana, naseMelika baya khona. Kanjalo-ke abafowabo nabanye abantu besizwe sabo baseMbusweni waseNingizimu nabo banqotshwa ngabaseBhabhiloni. Nokho, babuyela ukuzokwakha ithempeli lesibili futhi ngokuhamba kwesikhathi bahlakazeka phesheya ezinsukwini zikaVespasian noTitus, naphinda ngesikhathi sokuvukela okwakuholwa nguSimon Bar Kokhba, lapho abaningi babantu bami baqhubeka behlakazekela emazweni aseningizimu nasempumalanga yeJerusalema baya enyakatho ye-Afrika nase-Aleksandriya yaseGibhithe nakwezinye izindawo eziningi baye bazulazula ekuhlushweni kwabo. Nokho nabo baye balondolozwa nguMose, nakuba abaningi bengaphansi kobugqila buka-Esawu, no-Ashkenazi, futhi abaningi babo bakhohlisiwe futhi balindele uMahdi wabo, esikhundleni sikaYeshua HaMashiach onguMesiya wabo weqiniso, nomkhululi ka-Israyeli owathenjiswa endulo ngu-Adonai-YHVH, Selah.

Nokho, njengoba kulotshiwe, kunjalo ngempela, ukuthi abantu bami abaningi abazi lutho futhi ababoni kahle ukuthi bangobani ngempela. Basuswe empandeni kuYahweh, uMINA ENGINGUYE OMKHULU, lowo umnqumo wemvelo ohlanganyela ekuvundeni kwempande, yona leyo mpande enguYehovah-YHVH, uMINA ENGINGUYE OMKHULU.

Nokho, abaningi kakhulu babantu bami baye bazivumela ukuba baphendulwe ngokungazi omunye komunye ngamanga nenzondo futhi babangele ukungazi okujulile ngemvelaphi yabo yangempela, njengoba beye bafundiswa ukuba bakholwe amanga alesi sizukulwane sabapristi benyoka kunoma yisiphi isizwe abaye bazinza kuso, leso sizukulwane sabapristi benyoka abakhonza uHeylel Ben Shachar, leyo serafimi evuthayo newileyo ebekelwe imililo yesijeziso saphakade nokwahlulelwa, leyo mililo ethwele isijeziso esinzima kunazo zonke kule nyoka embi eye yazingcolisa ngokuvukela okungenangqondo, igcinwe esimweni esehluliwe nesiwileyo engeke isalulama kuso.

Ngalokho abantu bakithi nabafowethu baye bafundiswa ukuzondana ngezindlela eziningi zamanga ahlanganiselwe ukufundisa izixuku. Kanjalo kulotshiwe: Lezi zinto eziyisithupha iNkosi iyazonda: yebo, eziyisikhombisa ziyisinengiso emphefumulweni wayo: amehlo azidlayo, ulimi olunamanga, nezandla ezichitha igazi elingenacala, inhliziyo eceba amacebo amabi, izinyawo ezishesha ukugijimela ebubini, ufakazi wamanga okhuluma amanga, nalowo ovusa ukungezwani phakathi kwabazalwane.

Kunjalo-ke, ukuthi abantwana boMbuso waseZulwini baye bafundiswa ngobuqili, ngenkohliso, nangenzondo enkulu ukuthi bona bangabagqilazi babafowabo ngesikhathi sokuhweba ngezigqila ngaphesheya kwe-Atlantic. Kodwa lo msebenzi wenziwa ngu-Esawu no-Ashkenazi bekhonza uHeylel Ben Shachar. Lapho besola khona abantwana bombuso waseZulwini ngezenzo zokhula olweba abantwana konina noyise, nolweba oyise nonina nabantwana emazweni angaphandle kwawabo; ababe sebeyothengiswa ebugqilini e-AmeruKa, (nakwezinye izindawo) ngenzondo enkulu kulolu khula ngomakhelwane babo ababeyizizwe eziyimbangi ezazilwa ezabathengisa ezandleni zalaba bantu ababi ezikhathini zasendulo. Ngakho qondani ukuthi lezi zenzo ezimbi zilotshwe ngokuphelele emazulwini futhi ubulungiswa nempindiselo kuyokuna ngokungazelelwe phezu kwabakhileyo emhlabeni abasafuna ukubhujiswa kukaJakobe; esikhundleni salokho ukubhujiswa kwabo kuyobehlela ngokungazelelwe manje, Selah.

Nokho, abaningi benu basahlala bengazi lutho ngoba umbala wesikhumba senu uhlukile kowabafowenu ngenxa yobuciko nobuhle bukaYahweh. Nokho, ayikho enye indalo emhlabeni ezizonda ngale ndlela ngenxa yamanga enyoka kuyo yonke indalo ka-Elohim, kodwa abantu bami bayazivumela ukuba baboshwe ubuwula obunjalo namanga angasho lutho obuwula obungenasisekelo obunikezwe yizithunywa eziwileyo zona zokubhujiswa kwabantu bami.

Anisenaso isikhathi!

Nokho, abaningi benu basahlala bengenalwazi ukuthi kuKristu Jesu akukho mJuda, namuntu wezizwe, nasigqila, nakhululekile, kodwa kunesidalwa esisha kuKristu, yebo, isidalwa esisha lapho izizukulwane ezindala zika-Adamu zichithwa khona ingunaphakade ngomoya wobuNdodana. Ngoba izithembiso ezenziwa ku-Abrahama othembekile zizozuzwa njengefa ngabalungileyo abaphila ngokholo, ngendlela efanayo nanjengoba u-Abrahama othembekile wayephila, lolo kholo oluholela ngokugcwele ebusweni bukaJesu Kristu ekuzalweni okusha.

Kodwa, abaningi benu bakhetha ukungazi ezindleleni zenu ezingajulile ezingesizo ezami futhi okumelwe niziphendukele ngokushesha! Noma kunjalo, ngoba nginiyalile ukuba nithande iNkosi uNkulunkulu wenu ngenhliziyo yenu yonke, nangomoya wenu wonke, nangengqondo yenu yonke, nangamandla enu onke, futhi nithande nomakhelwane wenu njengoba nizithanda nina. Umyalo abaningi benu abangawuvumeli enzalweni yenu yobuhlanga emelene nohlanga olufanayo luka-Adamu olunezikhumba zemibala ehlukile kuneyenu ebuhleni bomklamo kaYahweh. Phendukani!

Ngakho-ke, ezinsukwini zasendulo, uhlu lozalo lukaKhanani lwazingcolisa ngokuzihlanganisa nezingelosi eziwileyo, yebo, ezinsukwini zasendulo, futhi njengoba zazizingcolisa esonweni zazizinikela ngokuphelele kulezo zingelosi eziwileyo ezweni laseShinari, ngisho nasethunzini lalowo mbhoshongo omkhulu waseBhabheli, wona lowo mbhoshongo u-YHVH, uMkhulu Nginguye, awubhidliza ekunqotshweni kwazo.

Ngoba kulotshiwe kanje: Ngemva kokuba umhlaba usukhukhulwe ngezikhukhula ezinsukwini zikaNowa, umhlaba wahlukaniswa ngenkatho yefa phakathi kwamadodana kaNowa ezinsukwini zikaPhelegi. Kanjalo uJafete wangena efeni lakhe emazweni asenyakatho, futhi abantu babenolimi olulodwa, futhi amadodana kaHamu ahlangana ezweni elaba yiBhabhiloni. Futhi izikhulu zikaNimrode zacebisana noPhuti, noMisrayimi, noKushe, noKhanani nemindeni yabo ukuba bakhe umuzi wabo futhi kuwo kube nombhoshongo oqinileyo, yebo umbhoshongo ophakemeyo ukuze balwe namazulu nokuba bazibuthele ndawonye ukuze bazenzele igama ngokuhlubuka umyalo kaYahweh wokuthi bazale bande ukuze baphinde bagcwalise umhlaba. Kodwa zinjalo izindlela zomuntu wemvelo ongenalo ukholo, yena, ezinkanukweni nasekuziqhenyeni kwakhe okhonza isono ngakho-ke okhonza uyise wesono onguSathane, ngokungalaleli komuntu kuYahweh, uMkhulu Nginguye, ngenxa yesono enyameni yomuntu wemvelo.

Futhi ohlwini lozalo lukaNimrode olwakhula ebubini: Kuthiwa ebubini kuphuma ububi obuningi.
