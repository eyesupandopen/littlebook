Emisebenzini kaJohane uMambuli
May 20, 2024

Kuni abayi-144,000: Yizwani Ubizo lwami!

Yizwani, O yizwani, zizwe zami eziyishumi nambili, izwi likaMesiya wenu, iNkosi, uKhosi noMpristi Ophakeme, yebo mina, uYeshua HaMashiach, iNdodana yoMuntu uYahweh ayivusa kwabafileyo. Ngoba:

NginguMesiya wenu weqiniso, owehla esihlalweni sobukhosi sikaDavide, futhi kungokuleso sihlalo sobukhosi engizobusa kuso iZwe leSithembiso ngenxa kaYahweh, uNkulunkulu waphakade, ngoba wathembisa kanjalo koYihlo. Ngakho-ke qondani ukuthi ngiyiNgonyama yesiZwe sakwaJuda, futhi akukho khosi namphrofethi owake wakhishwa kwaDani, lapho kuvela khona umphrofethi wamanga.

NgiyiNkosi yenu, ngokuba uYahweh ungenzile ngaba yiNkosi yamakhosi, futhi nganithenga ngegazi lami siqu njengePhasika lenu elipheleleyo, elanikelwa ngenxa yezono zabeZizwe namaJuda ngokufanayo, ukuze nobabili nibe muntu munye omusha kimi, yebo kuKristu Jesu. Ngokuba uYahweh unginikile igama eliphezu kwawo onke amagama futhi wangenza ngahlanganyela esihlalweni Sakhe sobukhosi ngenxa yokulalela kwami. Ngalokho-ke ubuNkosi bami bunikelwe njengophawu lwensindiso, ngokuba ngenziwe umoya onika ukuphila, futhi nguYahweh kuphela omkhulu kunami, ngokuba UnguNkulunkulu waphakade.

NgiyiNkosi yenu, ngokuba uYahweh ungenzile ngaba yiNkosi yamakhosi, futhi ngiyobusa umhlaba ngokulunga. Bhekani! Ngiletha uMlobokazi wami, oyobusa nami, ngokuba ngibenzile baba ngamakhosi nabapristi phambi kukaYahweh waphakade, ongenamunye onjengaYe. Ngokuba ekubuseni kwami kweminyaka eyinkulungwane okuthenjisiwe okuzayo, ngiyoba yisiphathimandla esiphakeme kunazo zonke, futhi ngiyothuma amakhosi ami nabapristi bami ukuba babuse efeni labo elikimi; ngokuba ngibadalile kabusha kimi, ngibathathile kumaJuda nakwabeZizwe ngokufanayo ngabenza umuntu munye omusha, yebo, badalwe kabusha ekupheleleni. Ngimunye ebumbeni nasenjongweni noBaba, uYahweh, futhi esihlalweni Sakhe sobukhosi Yena mkhulu kunami.

Ngingumpristi wenu ophakeme kuze kube phakade ngokohlelo lukaMelkisedeki, futhi nganikela ngegazi lami lokuhlawulela emhlatshelweni lapho ngazinikela njengePhasika lenu elipheleleyo neliphelele. Ngokuba amaHebheru anikeza ubufakazi bawo lapho ngithuma umphostoli uPawulu ukuba akhulume nawo esikhundleni sami (wayengowakwaBenjamini): Pho-ke, igazi likaKristu, owazinikela kuNkulunkulu engenabala ngoMoya waphakade, ngeke yini lihlambulule unembeza wenu emisebenzini efileyo ukuze nikhonze uNkulunkulu ophilayo? Ngalokho-ke umhlatshelo wami wami uqobo wenzelwa kanye kuphela futhi kuze kube phakade, futhi akukho omunye uMesiya.

Nakuba isinagoge likaSathane liyokwamukela umesiya walo wamanga nomphrofethi walo wamanga ongumakhi wethempeli osesimweni sesilo (ngokuba uyisilo esivela emhlabeni). Ngokuba nina niyithempeli likaNkulunkulu ophilayo, futhi asikho isidingo somhlatshelo wezilwane nanini futhi, ngokuba ngingumhlatshelo wokugcina, yebo, iPhasika elipheleleyo, elingenasono, futhi imisebenzi yami iphelile futhi iphelele. Ngakho-ke, akusekho sidingo sento enjalo, kodwa kuphela ukuthi nize kimi nezono zenu ngokuphenduka, futhi ngiyonihlanza emisebenzini yenu efileyo yenyama. Ngokuba kwabaseRoma kulotshiwe: Ngokuba umthetho woMoya wokuphila okuKristu Jesu ungikhululile emthethweni wesono nowokufa. Ngoba kanjalo nganikhulula kimi.

Qondani, zizwe zami nabantu bami, ukuthi izikhathi zabeZizwe sezisondele ekupheleni. Izikhathi zabeZizwe zafika ngenxa yobulukhuni bezinhliziyo zoyihlo. Ngokuba wathini uPawulu ezEnzweni ngale ndaba: Kwakudingeka ukuba izwi likaNkulunkulu likhulunywe kini kuqala: kodwa njengoba nililahla kude nani, futhi nizahlulela ngokwenu njengabangafanele ukuphila okuphakade, bhekani, siphendukela kwabeZizwe.

Ngiyaneluleka, bantu bami, ningenzi amaphutha afanayo futhi ningaphili ezonweni zoyihlo, kodwa phendukani ezonweni zenu futhi niphenduke nasezonweni zoyihlo, bese niza kimi futhi ngizokwamukela, ngokuba ningabantwana besithembiso. Futhi yonke i-yod noma isici se-yod iyogcwaliseka; ngokuba lokho okuthenjiswe uYahweh akunakwenzeka ukuguqulwa, ngokuba akunakwenzeka ukuthi uYahweh aqambe amanga. Ngakho-ke, ngingukugcwaliseka kwezithembiso eziningi kakhulu, ngokuba nginguMesiya wenu weqiniso, yebo, umfanekiso kaNkulunkulu waphakade. Ngokuba ngiyindodana yakhe ezelwe yodwa, yebo, iWundlu likaNkulunkulu elahlonishwa nguJohane uMbhapathizi kuze kube phakade. Ngokuba wathi: Bhekani iWundlu likaNkulunkulu elisusa isono seZwe!

Ngakho-ke ngaphendukela ezizweni ngoba izwe lami langilahla, ngisho nangemva kokuba ngifele izono zabo futhi ngavuswa. Ngokuba ngathuma abaphostoli bami nabaprofethi kubo, futhi abaningi basindiswa. Ngokuba ekuqaleni kwenkathi yomusa, ngabathumela eThempelini nasemasinagogeni, futhi abaningi baphendukela kimi futhi bangamukela njengeNkosi yabo. Ngakho-ke ngiyanibiza ukuba nipheleliswe ebuNkosini bami, ngokuba nginihlengile kuBaba wami ukuze nani nibe ngabantwana baKhe ngomoya wami engiyonibeka uphawu ngawo. Futhi-ke izikhathi zabeZizwe seziphelile, ngokuba manje nabo sebengilahle ngenxa yomunye uJesu abamkhonzayo njengonkulunkulu, ngaleyo ndlela bezenza abakhonzi bezithombe; ngokuba uSathane wabalinga kanjalo. Ngokuba kunoNkulunkulu oyedwa kuphela, uYahweh waphakade, futhi kunoMesiya oyedwa weqiniso, yebo mina, uYeshua HaMashiach.

Ngokuba balahlekelwa yizimfihlakalo zami, bazishaya indiva imibono yami nezinhloso zombhalo wami ongcwele. Balahlekelwa umbono wohlelo lukaBaba lokuhlenga, futhi ngokuzidla bayenqaba amazwi ami engawabhalisela amaRoma. Ngokuba uPawulu wathi: Ngithi-ke, UNkulunkulu usilahle yini isizwe sakhe? UNkulunkulu makube kude nalokho. Ngokuba nami ngingumIsrayeli, wenzalo ka-Abrahama, wesizwe sakwaBenjamini. UNkulunkulu akasilahlile isizwe sakhe, ayesazi ngaphambili. Futhi futhi: Khona-ke yini? U-Israyeli akakutholanga lokho akufunayo; kodwa abakhethiweyo bakutholile, futhi abanye baphunyiswe amehlo. Ngakho-ke ukukhethwa kwaqinisekiswa kulabo engibakhethile phakathi kwezizwe; ngokuba abaphostoli bami babengabomhlaba bonke nabesithembiso, ngakho-ke ngabathuma ezizweni, lapho abaholi benu benkolo behlambalaza ngamanga, bebabiza ngabamanga. Labo baholi bamanga kanye nabelusi abangenalusizo bayakuphika nokuvuka kwami kwabafileyo, ngokuba ngivusiwe kwabafileyo, futhi angisafi kuze kube phakade.

Ngokuba yini enye etshiwo ngamaRoma ngale ndaba: UDavide uthi, Itafula labo malibe ugibe, nethiyetha, nesikhubekiso, nempindiselo kubo: Amehlo abo mawenziwe mnyama ukuze bangaboni, futhi imihlane yabo mayihlale igobile. Ngithi-ke, Bakhubekile yini ukuze bawe? UNkulunkulu makube kude nalokho: kodwa kunalokho ngokuwa kwabo insindiso ifikile kwabeZizwe, ukuze ibabangele umona.

Ngokuba angithandi, bazalwane, ukuthi ningayazi le mfihlakalo, funa nihlakaniphe emicabangweni yenu; ukuthi ubuphofu ngokwengxenye bumehlele u-Israyeli, kuze kufike ukugcwala kwabeZizwe. Ngakho-ke ngimemezela ukuthi ukugcwala kwabeZizwe sekufikile, futhi izimfihlakalo zikaNkulunkulu zizobuyiselwa ku-Israyeli ukuze kulalelwe ukholo. Ngokuba ngisazomsindisa uJakobe, futhi insali ethembekile iyophendukela kimi ngokuthembeka, ngokholo olukhulu!

Ngakho-ke ngizile ukuzobanga umona ezinhliziyweni zenu ngami, uYeshua HaMashiach, noYahweh uNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade owangithuma, eningasamkhonzi ngokwazi, ngokuba isisitho sobuphofu siyosibekela noma ubani olandela umthetho kaMose. Ngokuba ngathini kwabaseKorinte ngale ndaba: Hhayi njengoMose, owabeka isisitho ebusweni bakhe, ukuze abantwana bakwa-Israyeli bangabheki ngokuqinile ekupheleni kwalokho okuchithiweyo: Kodwa izingqondo zabo zaphunyiswa amehlo: ngokuba kuze kube namuhla kusala isisitho esifanayo singasuswanga ekufundweni kweTestamente Elidala; leso sisitho esisuswa kuKristu. Kodwa kuze kube namuhla, lapho kufundwa uMose, isisitho sisemathunjini abo.

Nokho, lapho isiphendukela eNkosini, isisitho siyosuswa.

Ngokuba ngiwugcwalisile umthetho kaMose, ngokuba nina noyihlo anikwazi ukufeza ukulalela okuphelele ngenxa yesono esisegazini lenu. Ngokuba mina, uYeshua HaMashiach, ngiwugcwalisile umthetho ngokugcwele, futhi umthetho ugcinwa ngokuphelele kuphela ngokukholwa kimi, uJesu Kristu. Nibalwa njengabalungileyo ngokuhlonzwa kwenu okuphelele nami, futhi ngakho abalungileyo bayophila ngokukholwa. Ngalokho-ke ngiyokususa isisitho ezinhliziyweni zenu ukuze ningibone mina, uYeshua, uMesiya wenu weqiniso, ngokuba anginitshiyile! Futhi ngiza kini etestamenteni elisha, hhayi ebudaleni bencwadi. Ngokuba ngasiqinisa isivumelwano sami esisha ngegazi lami siqu, ngokwentando nezithembiso zikaYahweh, ukuze nihlengwe ngokugcwele. Futhi ngiyonihola efeni lenu, ngokuba izithembiso zikaNkulunkulu zingu-yebo no-amen. Akukho neyodwa i-yod noma isici se-yod esiyohluleka, ngokuba konke sekuzogcwaliseka; futhi ngesikhathi, ngempela, ngiyogcwalisa konke, ngokuba ngithunyiwe kanjalo nguYahweh ukuba ngifeze.

Ngakho-ke abeZizwe baphendukile kimi njengenja ebuyela ebuhlanzweni bayo, futhi sekufikile isikhathi sokubayeka kulokho. Ngokuba enkathini yami yomusa, akukho muntu noyedwa uBaba amkhethele insindiso ohlulekile, futhi akekho ongabahlwitha esandleni sami. Ngokuba labo abavuma mina, uJesu, njengeNkosi yabo, benokholo ezinhliziyweni zabo lokuthi uNkulunkulu wangivusa kwabafileyo, babekwa uphawu ngomoya ongcwele wesithembiso, futhi izinhloso zikaBaba zigcwalisekile kimi ngenxa yabo. Futhi ngakho-ke bayohlala nami njalo.

Ngokuba umuthi womnqumo wembula iqiniso. Futhi ngathini ngomuthi womnqumo encwadini kaPawulu eya kwabaseRoma: Uma amanye amagatsha ephuliwe, nawe, ungumuthi womnqumo wasendle, waxhunyelelwa phakathi kwawo, futhi uhlanganyela nawo empandeni nasekunoneni komuthi womnqumo; Ungazigabisi ngamagatsha. Kodwa uma uzigabisa, awuthwali impande, kodwa impande yona ikuthethe.

Ngakho-ke benze amaphutha futhi bathatha izono zoyihlo futhi baphendukela kude nami ezithombeni zabo, futhi abasazolizwa izwi lami. Nakuba uma bebiza egameni leNkosi, bayosindiswa labo abangakuthandi ukuphila kwabo kuze kube sekupheleni.

Ngokuba yini enye etshiwo ubufakazi bamaRoma emibhalweni njengesixwayiso ezizweni: Uzothi-ke, Amagatsha aphulwa, ukuze ngixhunyelelwe. Kuhle; ngenxa yokungakholwa aphulwa, futhi wena umi ngokukholwa. Ungaziphakamisi, kodwa yesaba: Ngokuba uma uNkulunkulu engawaphephisanga amagatsha emvelo, qaphela funa angakuyeki nawe. Bheka-ke ubuhle nobukhali bukaNkulunkulu: kulabo abawayo, ubukhali; kodwa kuwe, ubuhle, uma uqhubeka ebuhleni bakhe: kungenjalo nawe uzonqunywa. Futhi nabo, uma bengahlali ekungakholweni, bayoxhunyelelwa: ngokuba uNkulunkulu unamandla okubaxhumelela futhi. Ngokuba uma unqunywe emthini womnqumo osendle ngokwemvelo, futhi waxhunyelelwa ngokuphambene nemvelo emthini womnqumo omuhle: kangakanani-ke laba, okungamagatsha emvelo, bayoxhunyelelwa emthini wabo womnqumo?

Ngokuba angithandi, bazalwane, ukuthi ningayazi le mfihlakalo, funa nihlakaniphe emicabangweni yenu; ukuthi ubuphofu ngokwengxenye bumehlele u-Israyeli, kuze kufike ukugcwala kwabeZizwe. Futhi ngakho wonke u-Israyeli uyosindiswa: njengoba kulotshiwe, Kuyoza uMkhululi eSiyoni, futhi asuse ukungamesabi uNkulunkulu kuJakobe.

Ngakho-ke, zihlobo zami, izikhathi zabeZizwe sezisondele ekupheleni, futhi kukhona uguquko olukhulu oluqhubekayo, lapho umusa wami uzophenduka ngokushesha ube ukwahlulela. Futhi ngisho namanje izingcezu zivunyelwe ukuba zibekwe endaweni ngesandla sikaSathane, njengoba ezokwenza isenzo sakhe, nakuba ezocindezelwa ngokuphelele, futhi ngizowuqeda ngokuphelele umbuso wakhe; ngokuba akasiyona inkosi.

Futhi-ke manje sekufika isikhathi sokuxhumelela amagatsha emvelo emuva emthini womnqumo, ngokuba izinhloso zikaYahweh zigcwalisekile emseni ongafanelwe nesihawu esakhonjiswa kwabeZizwe (ngokuba umusa nesihawu sikaNkulunkulu akufanele muntu), ngokuba ngisho nalowo musa nesihawu kwasetshenziselwa ukuthola izimvu ezilahlekileyo zendlu ka-Israyeli. Ngokuba ningabi ngabangazi emlandweni wenu siqu, uma nibeka phansi amaTalmud enu aseBhabhiloni ahlambalazayo futhi nifunde ngami; ngokuba angiyithumanga leyo ncwadi, futhi kufanele niphenduke kulezo zimfundiso futhi nilahle amanga kaSathane. Buyelani ezwini lami elihlanzekileyo likaNkulunkulu futhi nisuke ezonweni zoyihlo, ngokuba ngizokunihlenga uma nizolilalela izwi lami.

Ngokuba angithunywanga ngaphandle kwezimvu ezilahlekileyo zendlu ka-Israyeli. Ngakho-ke izizwe eziyishumi zahlakazeka phakathi kwezizwe kusukela ekuthunjweni kwazo ngama-Asiriya sonke lesi sikhathi esedlule. Zazulazula futhi zazula emhlabeni njengezihambi nabafokazi. Kusukela e-Asiriya zaya emazweni asenyakatho, eScythia, naseYurophu, futhi ekugcineni zaya eMelika, lapho ngazenzela khona indawo yokuhlala enokuthula isikhathi esithile. Futhi ngabiza noma ubani ongavela ezizweni ensindisweni yami. Ngisho nama-Israyeli amaningi asindisiwe futhi ahlengiwe kanye nabaningi abavela ezizweni, ababengeke babe nethemba ngenye indlela. Futhi isixuku sisabelile, ngakho-ke izikhathi zabeZizwe seziphelile, ngokuba asisekho isikhathi. Kodwa qondani ukuthi bonke ababiza egameni leNkosi bayosindiswa uma bethembekile kuze kube sekupheleni, ngokuba ngizobamukela kungakhathalekile ukuthi bavelaphi isizwe.

Futhi kunjalo-ke, ukuthi usizi lukaJakobe lufika ezweni nasezizweni, ngokuba sekuyisikhathi sokuba ukwahlulela okukhulu kwehlele emhlabeni. Futhi ngakho-ke izilo ngamandla kadrako ziyovuka; kodwa ungathathi uphawu lwakhe futhi ungaweli ezinkohlisweni zakhe. Ngokuba usezithandekisile kubabusi besizwe senu, ngisho nesinagoge likaSathane ngezindlela zabo zama-Edomi. Futhi usekhuluma ukuhlambalaza okukhulu, ngokuba ulungiselelwa injongo yakhe yangempela.

Ngokuba isinengiso sencithakalo siyeza. Ngokuba ngathuma umuntu wami othembekile uLuka ukuba alobe ngani kuze kube phakade: Lapho nibona iJerusalema lihaqwe yizimpi, yazini ukuthi ukuchithwa kwalo sekusondele. Khona-ke labo abaseJudiya mababalekele ezintabeni; nalabo abaphakathi kwalo mabaphume; nalabo abasemazweni mabangangeni khona. Ngokuba lezi yizinsuku zempindiselo, ukuze kugcwaliseke zonke izinto ezilotshiweyo.

Futhi kuyoba kanjalo ngesikhathi esifanele, futhi ngizovikela abami, ngokuba ngiyanithuma njengabaprofethi bami, abelusi, abelaphi, nezinceku. Ngokuba ngiyanibizela enkonzweni, yebo, ukuze nigcwalise ubizo lwenu lwangempela olunikezwe ngesithembiso koyise nguYahweh uqobo, futhi ngesandla sami ziyogcwaliseka. Ngokuba uYahweh ubeke isihlalo sami sobukhosi esihlalweni Sakhe sobukhosi, futhi ngihlanganyele esihlalweni sami sobukhosi noMlobokazi wami, abanami njalo. Futhi ngakho-ke akekho emhlabeni omkhulu kunami; akekho kubaprofethi benu bamanga abangaqhathaniswa, ngokuba bonke babuthaka futhi bayizisweli futhi bathunyelwe ukudukisa nokulahlekisa, ngokuba bangabelusi abangenalusizo.

Yazini, bantu bami, ukuthi isitha sinibekele izingibe eziningi, ngakho-ke landelani ukuhola kwami futhi nizozigwema zonke. Ngokuba maduzane kakhulu yonke injabulo iyosuswa emhlabeni. Abaningi ngeke bathole ngisho ucezu, futhi ngeke babe netafula lokudlela kodwa bayoba ngababaleki emhlabeni ngenxa yokukholwa kwabo kimi.

Ningazivumeli ukuba nilingwe yimana yabo yamanga eyodinga uphawu lwesilo ukuze nithole ukungena emphakathini wabo ophambukile. Ngokuba uphawu ngeke luthethelelwe, futhi bonke abawela kulo mgibe ngeke bakhululwe; ngokuba lungolaphakade kubo bonke abenza leso sinqumo sokugcina sokugcina, ngokuba bayobe bezibophezele isiphetho sabo futhi leso siphetho singesokugcina. Ngakho ningalingwa yisiyengo, noma nizogcina nisesemgodini futhi nibhajwe unomphela emhlathini wenu.

Ngiyanithuma ukuba nibuthe umhlambi wami, futhi ngizonihola kuwo futhi ngiholele wona kini, futhi niyowelusa ngenxa yami, ngokuba ngingumelusi omkhulu. Aniyikwenqaba olunye uhlanga noma izwe noma yisiphi esinye isizwe, ngokuba isithembiso sikaYahweh simi, futhi bonke ababiza egameni leNkosi bayosindiswa. Ningabi yizintamo ezilukhuni njengoyihlo, futhi nithobeke ezwini lami. Ngokuba oyihlo bangilahla, ngakho vumani izono zoyihlo nezakho siqu ngokuphenduka kimi, futhi ngizonithethelela futhi nginithele uthando lwami nomusa, ngokuba ngiyokwenza imifula yamanzi aphilayo igeleze esiswini senu. Ngininike amandla ezimangaliso ezinkulu. Futhi ngiyanithuma kubazalwane benu ukuba nibaphendulele kimi, ngokuba insali isazosindiswa; futhi ngiyosindisa uJakobe esandleni sezitha zabo, ngokuba ngikushilo futhi kuyogcwaliseka.

Mina, yebo uYeshua HaMashiach uYahweh amthumile, nginguMesiya weqiniso ka-Israyeli nomhlaba. Ngisho mina ngiyothuma abaprofethi bami ababili, yebo, labo uYahweh abahlwitha emhlabeni ngezikhathi zabo: babethathelwe injongo enjalo, futhi bayothunyelwa kanjalo. Nginezinto eziningi engizokusho kini futhi lezo zinto ziyoshiwo, futhi ngizokulungiselela imisebenzi yenu, futhi niyongenzela imisebenzi emikhulu nenamandla, yebo, imisebenzi eningakaze niyicabange ukuthi kungenzeka. Ngokuba izikhathi ezimbi kunazo zonke zihlale ziveza amadoda amakhulu kaYahweh, yebo, amadoda angeke aphulwe, ngokuba niyophumula emandleni ami, futhi mina anginakuphulwa. Ngokuba kanjalo uYahweh ungenzile, futhi kanjalo ngizokwenza nani. Ngakho yizwani izwi lami, ngokuba ngiyabiza futhi ngiyothuma izingelosi zami ukuba zibeke uphawu kwabayi-144,000 ngophawu lukaYahweh.

Ngizokugcina uphephile esilwaneni nasenengisweni yaso; ngizokufihla, futhi uzondliwa ezulwini ngisho noma udinga, ngakho ungesabi. Ningesabi ngokuphila kwenu, ngokuba umvuzo omkhulu ungowenu ngenkonzo nokwethembeka, futhi ngizonivuza ngokuma kwenu.

NgiyiNgonyama yesiZwe sakwaJuda, futhi niyozwa ukubhonga kwami, ngokuba ngiyanibiza.

Ngingulowo oyobeka uphawu kubantu abayi-12,000 besizwe ngasinye ngaphandle kwakwaDan nokwa-Efrayimi.

Ngingulowo oyochoboza umdlwane wengonyama oyovela kwaDan.

Ngingulowo oyonqoba isilo nomprofethi wamanga, futhi ngiyobaphonsa bephila echibini elikhulu lomlilo.

Ngingulowo oyohola abantu bami emadlelweni aluhlaza ngabelusi bami, ngokuba ngingumelusi omkhulu.

Ngingulowo oyogcwalisa izithembiso ezenziwa koyise, ngokuba ngithunyelwe kanjalo.

Ngingulowo oyochoboza ikhanda likaSathane, ngokuba izikhulu zalo mhlaba ziyokhokha.

Ngingulowo oyobusa esihlalweni sobukhosi sikaDavide, ngokuba umbuso wami awunaphelo.

Ngingulowo oza emafwini, futhi ngizoza esizweni sami futhi ngithathe ukubusa kwami, futhi ngeke ngivinjwe.

Mina, yebo, uYeshua HaMashiach, ngithumele la mazwi kulabo engibabizayo. Ngakho yizwani amazwi ami ngokulalela, futhi yazini ukuthi ngizokhulula izwe nomhlaba wonke futhi ngibuse ngokulunga. Ngakho yizwani ubizo lwami, ngokuba ngiza kini futhi ngizokufundisa izindlela zami futhi nginiholele kulo lonke iqiniso. Ngakho ningayenqabi imibhalo yami, ngokuba izwi likaNkulunkulu liyobuyiselwa ku-Israyeli, futhi umhlaba uyokwazi ukuthi kukhona uNkulunkulu kwa-Israyeli, yebo uYahweh, uNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade, ngokuba akekho omunye, futhi akekho onjengaYe. Shalom! Shalom! Ngakho yibani nokuthula futhi nibe kahle, ngokuba ngizoniphilisa nonke ngokuphelele ababeka ukholo lwabo kuYeshua HaMashiach.
