Bhekani! Ukuvalwa Kwesikhashana Kobufakazi, Ukuhlangana, Nokwahlukaniswa

Bhekani! Mina, Yeshua HaMashiach, ngiyeza ukutshela iBandla lami, umzimba wami, ngisho nomakoti wami ngezinto esezizokwehlela umhlaba. Ukuze nize niqonde incazelo yezinto esezizokwenzeka, futhi ningaphuphuthekiswa ukubaluleka kwazo. Ngoba, abawileyo bafuna ukudukisa bonke ngamanga abo enkohliso, futhi umhlaba eniwaziyo usuguquliwe kwasuka emhlabeni enake nawazi futhi awusafani, futhi uzoqhubeka uguqulwa ohlelweni lwawo lwangaphambili olwabekwa kusukela kuzamcolo kaNowa.

Bhekani! Njengasezinsukwini zikaNowa, kanjalo umhlaba uyophinde ube njalo ukuze ukwahlulela okuphelele kwehlele ngokuphelele emakhanda abawileyo kanye nasemhlabeni uqobo, futhi ubizo lokugcina lokulunga manje luzozwakala kuwo wonke umhlaba ngokwahlulela okukhulu nentukuthelo elungele ukwehlela ngokuzumayo kangaka manje. Ngakho-ke, qondani izikhathi eniphila kuzo nokubaluleka kwezenzakalo ezizayo. Ngisho nezenzakalo eziyonqunywa zibe zifushane ngokulunga, ngoba ukuba izinsuku zokuhlupheka kukaJakobe bezingafinyezwanga, ngiqinisile, ngiqinisile, bekungeke kusale nyama iphila emhlabeni futhi konke bekuyokwenziwa incithakalo. Ngoba zinjalo izinkanuko zabawileyo, ukuthi ebugovwini babo bokweba umhlaba esintwini bangakujabulela ukubangela ukuba konke ukuphila kwenyama okusegazini kuphele ingunaphakade, ngoba umhlaba wenzelwa umuntu nomuntu ukuba abuse umhlaba ngokulunga. Ngisho kanjalo, njengoba kulotshiwe: Uyini umuntu ukuba umkhumbule? Nokho uyamthwesa umqhele wenkazimulo nodumo futhi umbeke phezu kwemisebenzi yezandla zakho.

Nokho, amacebo nezinhloso zaphakade zikaYahweh, uMINA ENGINGUYE Omkhulu, ayogcwaliseka ngokuphelele ngesandla sami esinamandla!

Ngakho-ke, qondani izikhathi nokubaluleka kwezenzakalo eziseduze, ukuze ningabanjwa yizo noma nithathwe ekwahlulelweni okungahloselwe abathembekileyo kuKristu. Qondani, ukuthi ekukhaleni kwecilongo lokugcina elisizophuma, lona lelo cilongo lokugcina lesikhathi sami somusa esinikezwe ezandleni zami ngomyalo ophelele wokuba likhale ngesikhathi esimisiwe esicishe sibe phezu kwenu ngokugcwele zithandwa zami, ukuthi umhlaba uyonikezelwa ngokuphelele ezandleni zikadrako ekubuseni kwakhe okufushane ngomusa.

Futhi lokhu kungenxa yokuthi umuntu wemvelo ukwamukele ngokuzithandela ukubusa okunikezwe amakhosi ayishumi, avuka ngenkohliso enkulu emindenini eyishumi yasendulo, eye yabusa umhlaba ngenkohliso enkulu nangokunyenya ngenxa yabawileyo selokhu yaqala ukuvuka ekuweni kweBhabheli nombhoshongo walo ezinsukwini zikaNimrode. Le mindeni eyishumi ilindile yabheka ukubuya koyise nonkulunkulu babo abangesibo obaba nabangesibo onkulunkulu, kodwa abazenzisi abenza sengathi. Abawileyo banikeze izilo zabo ukuba zibuse ngomyalo kadrako, ezithi ngokunyenya nangezithembiso eziyize nangokwahlukana okukhulu, zagumbuqela izizwe ukuze bonke abaholi bomhlaba manje bakhonze udrako ngokuphelele, futhi labo abenqabayo, babulewe noma bazobulawa ngezandla zabo ezifihliwe noma bazowa ngokuphelele ngempi yabo ezayo yokunqoba umhlaba.

Nokho unjalo umhlaba eniphila kuwo nezikhathi esezihlasela umhlaba ngobubi obukhulu nangenkohliso ephelele. Ngoba njengoba kunjalo, umhlaba manje usuwenqabile uMthetho wami oMusha Wokulunga owathi, ngiqinisile, ngiqinisile, waqiniswa futhi wagunyazwa ngegazi lami elingenasono engalinikela ngenkokhelo ephelele yesijeziso sikaMose. Ngisho kanjalo, ukuze umuntu owile abe nokukhetha ukuthi amukele ukuthethelelwa kwezono nosindiso olungcwele nokubuyiselwa kuYahweh, uMINA ENGINGUYE Omkhulu, ngendlela kaJesu waseNazaretha, noma umuntu owile angakhetha ukuhlala ewile futhi ekhonza izinkanuko zabawileyo, amukele ukwahlulelwa okuphelele kwazo zonke izono, nazo zonke izenzo ezingalungile azenzile ngokungalungi, kungakhathaliseki ukuthi zincane kangakanani. Qondani ukuthi bonke abakhetha ukuhlala bekhonza abawileyo bayohlanganyela nabo ngokugcwele esijezisweni sabo kulelo chibi lomlilo elikhulu, ngisho isijeziso esinokhahlo nesinzima esasingahloselwe umuntu, nokho bonke abakhetha ukuhlala nabawileyo bayohlanganyela ngokufanayo nabawileyo ngoba akukho okunye ukukhetha!

Nokho, ukwenqatshwa koMthetho wami oMusha akuchithi amandla aphelele nomphumela woMthetho wami oMusha ngoba uMthetho wami oMusha uyisivumelwano saphakade noMthetho waphakade oyohlala usebenza ingunaphakade naphakade ngisho nangamandla aphelele oMpristi Ophakeme ingunaphakade ngokohlelo lukaMelkisedeki, futhi uyosinda uhlale ingunaphakade naphakade njengeSivumelwano saphakade Sokulunga sokubusa kwaphakade nokuphelele nokulungileyo kwendalo kaYahweh. Ngisho nendalo yezinto ezulwini, nasemhlabeni, ngisho nangaphansi komhlaba futhi akukho ndawo yokucasha lapho ukubusa kwami okulungileyo kungeke kube nomphumela ophelele ekupheleni kweviki lamashumi ayisikhombisa likaDaniyeli, okuyikho futhi ukuhlupheka kukaJakobe, manje osekuhlangene ngokugcwele emiheleni yokuqala kokubeletha futhi ingane izozalwa!

Ngisho nengane engcwele yezithelo zokuqala zokulunga ekuvuneni okugcwele kwebhali okuzokwenzeka ngokugcwele ngokuzumayo nangokungalindelekile manje, ngisho nesikhathi saphakade sokugcwaliseka lapho abafileyo kuKristu beyozalwa besuka enhliziyweni yomhlaba lapho bephumule khona ngokuthula, nabathembekileyo kuKristu abaphilayo nabasalayo bayosuka ebusweni bomhlaba kanye nabo ukungihlangabeza emoyeni futhi kanjalo nonke niyothathelwa ezulwini lamazulu lapho niyokuma khona phambi kweNkosi yenu yaphakade Yokulunga ukwamukela ifa lenu eligcwele! Futhi ngizonethula ningenabala noma umbobo noma isisihla noma umbobo nanoma yini enjalo kuYahweh, uMINA ENGINGUYE Omkhulu, owanizala ngempela nonke ngomoya wami wobuNdodana enabekwa uphawu ngawo lolu suku lokuhlengwa!

Futhi nonke niyothatha izindawo zenu kwa-Israyeli kaNkulunkulu, ngoba noma ngabe nazalwa ningamaJuda, noma nivela ezizweni ezilahlekile zakwa-Israyeli, noma kwabezizwe: kuKristu Jesu ngeke nisaba yilutho lwalezi zinhlanga zasemhlabeni kodwa isidalwa esisha, umuntu omusha, ngisho nidalwe kabusha ngokuphelele ekupheleleni okuphelele kuKristu Jesu futhi niyomangala ubukhazikhazi baphakade eniyogqokiswa bona. Ngoba labo abakuKristu bayobunjwa bakheke ngendlela efanayo noKristu Jesu futhi abanqobi bami bayodla ifa lazo zonke izinto ngendlela efanayo nami nganqoba ngadla ifa lazo zonke izinto esandleni sikaYahweh, uMINA ENGINGUYE Omkhulu, futhi linjalo ifa labathembekileyo! Ngakho-ke, qondani ukuthi ngokucwayiza kweso okuzumayo nonke niyokhululwa ingunaphakade egazini elonakele lika-Adamu, ngisho ngo-Adamu wokugcina: lowo uYahweh amenze umoya ophilayo oyokudala kabusha nonke ngendlela efanayo neNkosi yenu yaphakade Yokulunga, Sela!

Ngakho-ke, ngiyokwenza umsebenzi ophelele nogcwele kini zithandwa zami, ngisho nangokulalela kwenu okuthembekileyo kokulunga ezikhathini zami zomusa nesihe. Ngoba umusa nesihe kushona njengelanga kulokhu kuhwalala kokuhlangana, lapha ekuqaleni kokuhlupheka kukaJakobe. Ngoba ukuhlangana engikhuluma ngakho kungukuhlangana kokulunga, okufinyelela umvuthwandaba ngokuthi abathembekileyo kuKristu bahlukaniswe ngokuphelele kulabo abakhileyo emhlabeni ngokungalungi, njengoba bethathelwa ekuphepheni kwamazulu ngokucwayiza kweso ngokuzumayo nangokungalindelekile manje.

Nokho, njengoba lo msebenzi wokulunga ufinyelela ekugcwalisekeni okugcwele, kanjalo kuyoba khona ukuhlangana kobubi emhlabeni njengoba umvimbeli wami esusiwe. Ngoba kunezindawo eziningi zokuvimbela ezivimbela abawileyo ukuba babe nokuhlangana kwabo abakufisayo, nokho njengoba abathembekileyo kuKristu bethathwa zonke izindawo zokuvimbela ziyosuswa kanye nomvimbeli wami futhi uMikayeli uyosukuma azisuse futhi iqhwa liyowa, nomhlaba uyoguqulwa ngodlame ukuze ube ngokugcwele njengasezinsukwini zikaNowa, uphelele ngamaNefili awo anengekayo enziwe futhi futhi azokhululwa. Bhekani! Ngiletha imidondoshiya ukugcwalisa intukuthelo yami!

Kanjalo, umhlaba uyonikezelwa ezandleni zikadrako isikhashana esifushane, ngisho isikhathi sokwahlulela okunokhahlo lapho abakhileyo emhlabeni beyoba nakho ngokugcwele ububi nobumnyama abakukhanukayo nabakufisayo, ngaphandle kokuthi ngeke babe nenkathi yegolide kodwa ukwahlulelwa okunokhahlo nokwaphakade esikhundleni salokho. Futhi abawileyo bayoba nempi yabo abayoyisebenzisa ukunqoba umhlaba ngokugcwele, ngobubi obungavinjelwe, nangokungalungi okungavinjelwe, futhi leso sihenqo esikhulu esibomvu siwela emalangabini okwahlulela njengoba iBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo lahlulelwa ngokuphelele futhi labhujiswa ekuhambeni kwabathembekileyo kuKristu, lingaphinde livuke! Qondani ukuthi bayozifihla emadolobheni abo angaphansi komhlaba nasezinqabeni, nasezikhungweni, nasezindaweni zokukhosela, kodwa abaningi babo ngeke baphunyuke kodwa bayongcwatshwa emhlabeni ekwahlulelweni okuphelele, ngisho nokulondolozwa ekuhlushweni ekufeni kuze kube yileso sihlalo esikhulu esimhlophe sokwahlulela futhi ngeke baphunyuke.

Bese isambulo nokuqaphela kuyokwehlela ngokushesha phezu komhlaba ukuthi ukwahlulela okuphelele nentukuthelo kufikile phezu komhlaba, futhi ngubani oyokwazi ukuma? Nokho, bonke ababiza igama likaJesu kuze kube sekupheleni kwabo bayofinyelela osindisweni olugcwele nolukhazimulayo futhi insali enamandla yakwa-Israyeli iyokwamukela uYeshua HaMashiach njengoMesiya wayo weqiniso futhi iyosindiselwa iphile embusweni wami weminyaka eyinkulungwane. Ngakho-ke qondani ukwahlukaniswa kokugcina okuzayo.

Ngoba insali elungileyo kaJakobe iyokwahlukaniswa no-Esawu no-Ashkenazi, ngisho isindiswe iphile ngesambulo esiyofezwa ofakazi bami ababili, ngisho u-Enoke uMbhali no-Eliya umTishibi.

Ngoba abasivivi nabaningi balabo ababenqabile ngaphambili uJesu waseNazaretha bayoshiyelwa emlilweni wokuhlanjululwa futhi bayokwahlukaniswa kulabo abamangala ngesilo futhi bayonqotshwa ekuqaleni yisilo sasolwandle empini yaso nabangcwele abasele; abayonqoba isilo sasolwandle ngokubiza uJesu njengeNkosi yabo kuze kube sekupheleni kwabo futhi enkazimulweni enkulu bayoya ingunaphakade!

Ngoba abanengekayo nabangalungile bayokwahlukaniswa ngokuphelele kwabalungileyo futhi babekwe uphawu ingunaphakade ngokulunywa yinyoka okusesandleni sesilo sasemhlabeni esiyosetshenziselwa ukubamba ngokugcwele bonke labo abathanda ububi futhi bajabulela ukungalungi, futhi ngeke baphunyuke.

Futhi kunjalo-ke, ukuthi izikhathi zokwambula sezimboze umhlaba ngokuphelele manje, futhi konke kuyokwambulwa ngokugcwele ngezikhathi zakhona ezifanele. Ekubuthaneni kwabathembekileyo kuKristu okunikezwe ngokumemeza okukhulu ngezwi lengelosi enkulu nalelo cilongo lokugcina eliyokhala ngokuqinisekile, labo abathathelwe emazulwini ngeke besabona ngengilazi ngokufiphele kodwa bayoqonda ngokugcwele zonke izinto njengoba baziwa ngokugcwele. Futhi bayobona ngamehlo abo, futhi bayozwa ngezindlebe zabo, futhi bayobona ngokugcwele baqonde inkazimulo kaYahweh uMINA ENGINGUYE Omkhulu, kanye noKristu Jesu iNkosi yabo yaphakade Yokulunga futhi yeka isikhathi esimnandi esiyoba yiso njengoba umkhwenyana ehlangana ngokugcwele nomakoti wakhe esivumelwaneni saphakade! Futhi amatafula aselungiwe namahholo edili aselungile!

Futhi kunjalo-ke, ukuthi izikhathi zokwambula ziyofika ngokugcwele phezu komhlaba manje, futhi zonke izici zombuso kaSathane nemiyalelo yokungalungi ziyokwambulwa ngokugcwele futhi zivezwe obala njengoba ukwahlulela kwehlela, ngoba iveli lokwahlukanisa liyosuswa ngokuzumayo nangamandla njengoba iveli ethempelini elalehlukanisa indawo engcwele kakhulu nendawo engcwele encane ladatshulwa kusuka phezulu kuya phansi ngesandla esinamandla sikaYahweh uqobo, lapho ngisho lawo mazwi okunqoba kwaphakade, SEKUPHELILE!

Ngakho-ke, qondani izikhathi eniphila kuzo futhi zilungiseleleni zithandwa zami. Qhubekani niphenduke futhi ninxuse igazi lami elihlawulelayo phezu kwezimpilo zenu nezimpilo zalabo enibathandayo ngokunxusela, ukuze nize nibe nokuthethelelwa okuphelele kwazo zonke izinto nokuthethelelwa okuphelele kwezono futhi abaningi bayosindiswa ngokunxusela kwenu okulungileyo. Qondani ukuthi manje yisikhathi sokuzihlukanisa ngokuphelele ezindleleni zalo mhlaba kanye nezinkathazo zalo mhlaba omubi wamanje ngoba ngokuzumayo nangokungalindelekile izikhathi zenu zokuphila kulo mhlaba onesono ziyoba sekupheleni kwaphakade, futhi ngeke niphinde nibe ngaphansi kobubi nokungalungi. Ngakho-ke qhubekani niphuma phakathi kwabo futhi nihlukane kuzo zonke izinto, nokho, ngiyanicela ukuba niqhubeke emisebenzini yenu, nisebenze ngenhliziyo njengokwenzela iNkosi ngezinhliziyo ezigcwele zokholo enisebenza ukulunga kuze kube yilapho ngokuzumayo emhlabeni ningasekho!

Futhi kanjalo ngiyeza ukutshela iBandla lami, umzimba wami, ngisho nomakoti wami ukuthi izikhathi zokuhlengwa kwabo sezifikile nezikhathi zomkhosi ophelele nefa nomvuzo zilungiselelwe ukwamukela abathembekileyo kuKristu emazulwini ngomyalo wami manje, ngoba uYahweh unikela zonke izinto ezandleni zami ezinamandla ukuze ngizifeze, futhi ngihlale ngenza intando kaBaba ukuze nani nihlengelwe ekulungeni okuphelele ukuze nani nihlale nenza intando kaYihlo ekupheleleni okulungileyo!

Ngakho-ke, ngendlela efanayo lokhu kudluliselwa kobukhosi okuyokwenzeka ngayo ngokubhujiswa okukhulu nangempi njengoba abawileyo benza lokho okunikezwe ezandleni zabo ukuze bakwenze ekwahlulelweni okuphelele kweBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo elihlezi phezu kwamanzi amaningi angabantu, nezixuku, nezizwe, nezilimi; ngisho njengoba benqoba izizwe ngenxa yokubusa kwabo okungcwele engiyokwenza kube ukuphela kwaphakade ekupheleni kokuhlupheka kukaJakobe.

Ukuthi ngendlela efanayo, ekufikeni kwami kwesibili, lapho ngibuyela emhlabeni nebutho lasezulwini nabathembekileyo kuKristu ngisho noMakoti wami, ukuthi ngokulunga ngiyokwahlulela ngenze impi ngibeke phansi ingunaphakade naphakade ngodlame olukhulu nangamandla konke ukubusa negunya nobukhosi bukadrako! Ngoba akayona inkosi, futhi akanambuso futhi ifa lakhe likulawo malangabi angunaphakade esijeziso lapho isibungu sabo singafi khona, lapho eboshwe khona ekwahlulelweni ukuze aye khona ingunaphakade.

Futhi kanjalo uMbuso wami weminyaka eyinkulungwane uyomiswa emhlabeni, ngisho ubuswa uMthetho wami oMusha wokulunga ingunaphakade naphakade. Sela!

Futhi kanjalo, mina Kristu Jesu, onguye futhi uJesu Kristu, onguye futhi uYeshua HaMashiach ngiyamemezela ukuthi izikhathi sezifikile nabathembekileyo balungele ukubuthwa futhi ukwahlukaniswa kokuqala kwezithelo zokuqala zokuvunwa kwebhali kuyovunwa kuthathelwe ezinqolobaneni nasekuphepheni kwamazulu lapho nonke niyokwamukela khona umvuzo wenu waphakade ezandleni zami ezinamandla ngokokuthembeka kwenu.

Futhi nginguNkosi yaphakade Yokulunga evala ubufakazi besithunywa sami esinamandla esikhamuluke ngokwethembeka aMazwi Okulunga esawambulelwa yimina, noYahweh uMINA ENGINGUYE Omkhulu. Ngakho-ke, qondani ukuthi isithunywa sami esinamandla siyobuthanwa nabathembekileyo futhi siyogcwalisa izinhloso zaso zaphakade efeni laso eliyokwenziwa yisandla sami kubo bonke abanqobayo ngendlela efanayo.
