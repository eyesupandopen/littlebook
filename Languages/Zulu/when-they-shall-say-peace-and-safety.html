Novemba 6, 2024

Lapho Beyothi Ukuthula Nokulondeka!

Okuvela kuYeshua HaMashiach onguKhristu Jesu futhi:

Zwanini Amazwi kaYeshua HaMashiach, lowo uYahweh amthumileyo njengoMesiya wenu, iNkosi, noMphristi Ophakeme phakade ngokohlelo lukaMelkhizedeki, nithobise izinhliziyo zenu ekulaleleni Amazwi ami, ngoba ngiza ukuzoxwayisa abathembekileyo ebandleni lami, ngisho noMlobokazi wami, (nabo bonke abanezindlebe zokuzwa noma beseduze noma bekude) futhi ngokuphathelene nenkohliso enamandla yamanje esebenza ngokugcwele emhlabeni wonke, eyoba yimbi kakhulu lapho umvimbeli esuswa masinyane, nangokuzumayo! Angishongo yini ukuthi yimuphi umcuphi obeka ugibe emehlweni enyoni? Oh, nina bokukholwa okuncane!

Ngakho-ke ngininika ukuqonda futhi lokho engakufundisa izihlobo zami lapho ngifika kwa-Israyeli njengeWundlu likaNkulunkulu, ngakho yizwani Amazwi ami! Ngoba kuyovela oKhristu bamanga, nabaprofethi bamanga, abayokwenza izibonakaliso ezinkulu nezimangaliso, ukuze kuthi, uma kungenzeka, badukise nabakhethiweyo. Bhekani, nginitshelile ngaphambili. Ngalokho, uma bethi kini: Bhekani, usehlane, ningaphumi; Bhekani, usemakamelweni angasese, ningakukholwa. Ngokuba njengokuba unyazi luphuma empumalanga, lubonakale nasentshonalanga, kuyokuba njalo nokuza kweNdodana yoMuntu! Ngakho-ke ngininika ukuqonda ukuthi ukubonakala kwami sekusondele, kuza ngokuzumayo nangamandla, futhi ngeke ngiphikwe impahla yami engayikhokhela ngegazi lami, ngisho umlobokazi wami othembekile nomzimba wami, ngisho ibandla lami elithembekileyo engilithanda ngothando lwaphakade! Ningabami!

Futhi kunjalo, zithandwa zami, ukuthi oKhristu bamanga sebeyavuka, futhi bebaningi. Kodwa manje izikhathi zikadrako sezifikile, nokuhlupheka kukaJakobe kufikile, nabo bonke abaseleyo abango-Adamu emhlabeni kumele benze isinqumo saphakade. Ngoba kunikiwe kudrako ukuba abuse umhlaba kanye nabelusi bakhe ababili abangenalutho, isilo sasolwandle nesilo somhlaba, ongumprofethi wabo wamanga. Niyozigcina yini ekuweleni enkohlisweni enamandla ngokulalela izwi lami? Niyozama ukulandela izwe ezinkohlisweni zalo nasemangeni alo kube ngukubhujiswa ngokuphelele ngokungalaleli izwi lami, nalapho ngiza ukuzonixwayisa ngenkohliso enkulu yamanje eseyithathe umhlaba wonke? Nivumele izinhliziyo zenu nokukholwa kwenu ukuba kunyakaziswe yinkohliso enamandla eninikwa yona ngethemba lamanga?

Futhi kunjalo-ke ukuthi akukho okungashintsha izimemezelo zokwahlulela ezanikezwa nguYahweh uqobo ngeBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo, ngisho nokwahlulela okunikezwe ezandleni zami ezinamandla ukuze ngikudedele lapho abathembekileyo sebebuthene nomvimbeli esusiwe, futhi kuyokwenzeka ngokuzumayo njengokuthenjisiwe! Yizona lezo zahlulelo ezamenyezelwa njengesixwayiso kubakhileyo baseBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo, isixwayiso abasishaya indiva ngokuphelele njengoba babeklolodela bengakholwa. Ngisho nezixwayiso zami eziningi kakhulu, ezinikezwe ngemilomo yabaprofethi bami abaningi abaye bamemezela la Mazwi esixwayiso ngokwethembeka iminyaka eminingi manje. Lezo zixwayiso ngaziyeka, esikhundleni sazo manje sekumi izimemezelo, izimemezelo zikaYahweh, nezikaYeshua HaMashiach, lezo zimemezelo okungenakwenzeka ukuba noma yisiphi isidalwa sizivimbe noma siziguqule. Ngoba lokho okumenyezelwe emandleni nasesihlalweni sobukhosi sikaYahweh akekho ongakuguqula, ngokuba zinjalo izindlela zikaNkulunkulu waphakade Wokulunga, owenza zonke izinto ngokwesiluleko sentando yakhe.

Ngakho-ke ngiyanitshela nina bathembekile bami, njengoba ngatshela uSimoni Petru ngehora lokuzidela kwami: Simoni, Simoni, bheka, uSathane ukufunile, ukuba akuhlungule njengokolweni. Kodwa ngikuthandazelele wena, ukuba ukukholwa kwakho kungapheli; ngakho-ke lapho usuphendukile, qinisa abazalwane bakho. Ngalokho ngiza kini sekuhlwile kuleli hora lokugcina (ngoba ihora seliyophela) ukuzonixwayisa ngendlela efanayo ukuze nizigcine endleleni, futhi ngiyaniyala nina enikhanya njengezibani Zezwe ukuba niqinise abazalwane benu, niqinisane ngala Mazwi ngokukholwa kothando nokulalela ukuze nonke nigcineke endleleni, ngakho lalelani izwi lami nihambe kulo.

Bhekani! Kulotshiwe ukuthi: Ngenxa yalokhu uNkulunkulu uyobathumela inkohliso enamandla, ukuze bakholwe amanga, ukuze bahlulelwe bonke abangakholwanga iqiniso, kodwa bajabula ngokungalungi. Niyozinikela yini kule nkohliso engeyona eyenu, zithandwa zami? Ngiqinisile, ngiqinisile, ngithi ebandleni lami, niyogijimela yini ekwamukelweni yilaba bakhohlisi? Ngisho nalabo abathunyelwe ukunibhubhisa ngudrako uqobo? Ngakho-ke, zithandwa zami, qhubekani niphuma phakathi kwabo, nihlukane! Ningahlanganyeli ezenzweni zabo ezimbi nasekulindeleni kwabo ngoba abaningi bathakathiwe ngemilingo yabo, nalowo abamkhethileyo manje ukhuluma njengodrako, enza izithembiso ezinkulu nezinkulumo ezinkulu ngaye, futhi abaningi bayolandela izindlela zakhe ezilimazayo, abaningi bayokhohliswa, babanjwe, bathathwe, ngakho phendukelani ekuzweni kwezwi lami, nizwe amazwi ami, nigijimele kimi zithandwa zami, ningaphambukeli ngakwesokunxele, noma ngakwesokudla, futhi ngiyonigcina niphephile entendeni yezandla zami zaphakade okungekho muntu ongakwazi ukunihlutha esandleni sami esinamandla.

Ngakho kungani nivumela izinhliziyo zenu ukuba zinihluphe, nina bathembekileyo nabeqiniso, ngisho nalabo engibathanda ngothando lwaphakade, ngisho nabo bonke labo udrako afuna ukubabamba ngenkohliso nangesahlulelo okungekhona okwenu. Ngakho-ke, ningahlanganyeli ezinkohlisweni zabo, phumani phakathi kwabo, nihlukane! Futhi kulabo kini abaye baphenduka ngokufanelekile enkohlisweni enkulu, ngiyaniyala ukuba ningabuyeli emuva nakancane. Bhekani! UYahweh, uNkulunkulu Omkhulu Waphakade, noYeshua HaMashiach, ngeke bathumele noma abathumelanga zixazululo zezombusazwe noma ababusi bezwe ukuze balungise izinkinga zezwe ngalesi sikhathi. Ngisho nezinkinga ezizayo njengesahlulelo kulabo abasemhlabeni ezivela kuYahweh uqobo, leso sahlulelo okungekho muntu ongaphunyuka kuso, ngaphandle kwabathembekileyo kuKhristu Jesu. Qondisisani kahle lokho engenza uPawulu akulobele abase-Efesu encwadini engamyalela ukuba ayibhalele ibandla lami elithandekayo:

Okokugcina, bazalwane bami, qinani eNkosini, nasemandleni ayo amakhulu. Hlomani zonke izikhali zikaNkulunkulu, ukuze nikwazi ukumelana namaqhinga kaSathane. Ngokuba asibambene nenyama negazi, kodwa sibambene nemibuso, namandla, nababusi bezwe bobumnyama baleli zwe, nemimoya emibi esezindaweni eziphakemeyo. Ngakho qondani zithandwa zami ukuthi amazwi ami awaguquki, ngakho-ke kungani mina, uKhristu Jesu iNkosi yenu eyenyukela ezulwini, ngingaze nginithumelele umbusi wasemhlabeni, noma umbusi noma inkosi eyomisa izahlulelo ezizayo? Wayengaziphikisa kanjani uYahweh, intando yakhe engiyifeza ngokuphelele, okungenakwenzeka ukuba aqambe amanga, ezinhlelweni zakhe zaphakade ezinikezwe kimi ngokugcwele ukuze ngiziqedele?

Ngoba bonke ababusi bomhlaba banikeze imvume yabo namandla abo kudrako, ngoba kunikezelwe kubo ukuba bafeze lokho, ngoba nezitha zikaYahweh ziyalalela izwi lakhe futhi ziyofeza intando yakhe abayinikiweyo kulesi sikhathi esifushane kakhulu esizotheleka emhlabeni ngolaka olukhulu! Ngoba izikhathi zolaka lweWundlu nolaka lukaNkulunkulu uSomandla sezifikile, futhi akukho okungavimba, noma kuphazamise nakancane lokho okuzokhululwa masinyane ngemva kokuba ngibuthele abathembekile bami emazulwini. Ngakho ngiyamemeza! Ubani ongamelana nentando yaphakade kaYahweh, uMINA ENGINGUYE omkhulu?!

Nokho, qondani ukuthi ngesikhathi sami somusa ngayalela iBandla lami ukuba lithandazele ababusi balo bezwe, ngazi ukuthi ababusi balo bezwe babebabi ngokwemvelo, ukuze ngokunxusa kwabangcwele ububi buvinjelwe ngokuqhubekayo (futhi umvimbeli wami usavimba), futhi laba babusi babengakhetha okungasebenzela okuhle nenhlalakahle yalabo abathanda uNkulunkulu, futhi kwakunjalo isikhathi sami somusa, yize izinhlupho zaziningi. Futhi nakuba umthandazo oshisekayo womuntu olungileyo unamandla amakhulu, qondani ukuthi abekho abaholi bezwe abanikezelwe insindiso yomhlaba ngize ngifike nebutho elikhulu lamaKhosi naBaphristi baphakade abayobusa umhlaba ngokulunga ngomyalo wami ngoba ngiyiNkosi yamakhosi noMbusi wababusi futhi nginguMpristi Ophakeme phakade ngokohlelo lukaMelkhizedeki.

Qondisisani ngokugcwele nangaphandle kokuyekethisa, abekho abaholi bezwe abangaletha ukuthula emhlabeni. Abekho abaholi bezwe abangaletha ukuphepha emhlabeni. Abekho abaholi bezwe abangabuyisela ukuchuma emhlabeni. Yize beyothembisa ukuthula nokuphepha nokuchuma okubuyisiwe, qondani ukuthi abanamandla okunikeza izinto abazithembisayo, nakuba beyonika izimpahla zezwe ezingelusizo kulabo abathatha uphawu lwesilo, ngisho nomvuzo wokungalungi oyosuswa kubo ngokuphelele esahlulelweni! Ngakho ningabalandeli, ngoba noma ngubani obalandelayo ekugcineni kuyodingeka athathe uphawu lwesilo, futhi labo abakwenzayo banikezelwa phakade emalangabini angenakucimeka, aphakade esijeziso, futhi izwe nezimpahla zalo ezingelusizo kuyadlula. Futhi udrako uyovuka ngokugcwele nezilo zakhe, nesilo sasemhlabeni sesilungise ubuthi baso obunikezwa ngokulunywa yinyoka ukuze ijove ubuthi bayo, nensimbi iyohlanganiswa nobumba, kodwa ngeke zibambane.

Kanjalo kulotshiwe kwaqoshwa nse ebufakazini bokwambulwa kwami, iSambulo sikaJesu Khristu, ukuze bonke abanamehlo okubona nezindlebe zokuzwa nenhliziyo ethambileyo yokuqonda, baqonde, kanjalo uJohane waloba: Ingelosi yathi kimi: Umangale ngani na? Ngizokutshela imfihlakalo yowesifazane, neyesilo esimthweleyo, esinezinhloko eziyisikhombisa nezimpondo eziyishumi. Isilo owasibonayo sasikhona, asisekho; sizakwenyuka siphume kwalasha, siye ekubhujisweni; nabakhileyo emhlabeni, amagama abo angalotshwanga encwadini yokuphila kusukela ekusekelweni kwezwe, bayomangala lapho bebona isilo esasikhona, singasekho, nokho sikhona.

Ngakho-ke, bantwanyana bami, yamukelani imfundo, futhi ngininika ukuqonda, yize umbuso kadrako ungeke uvuke ngokugcwele (ngoba umvimbeli uzovimba kuze kube yilapho umvimbeli engasavimbi, lapho abathembekileyo kuKhristu bethathwa baya emazulwini) abawiweyo bavunyelwe ukulungiselela ukuhlasela kwabo okuzumayo, noNkulunkulu uthumela labo abahlala emhlabeni ngokungalaleli inkohliso enamandla. Kodwa lezi zinto azihloselwe nina bathembekileyo bami nabakhethiweyo, futhi mina, uYeshua HaMashiach, ngiyamemezela futhi ngiyafakaza ngaleli qiniso ngala mazwi engiwathumele ngesithunywa sami nenceku yami ukuze afundelwe induduzo nokulalela yibo bonke labo la mazwi ahloselwe bona. Ngalokho ngibuza labo kini abangakhetha le nkohliso kunoBukhosi bami baphakade, kungani nimangala? Nisazokhetha amanga abelusi abangenalutho kunoBukhosi nenduduzo elethwa umoya wesiprofetho na?

Ngalokho, ngininika ukuqonda ukuthi laba belusi ababili abangenalutho, abakhona futhi abekho kodwa abayoba NGOKUGCWELE, abakavuki ngokugcwele, futhi bayovuka ngokugcwele emlotheni nasemanxiweni ohlelo oludala, nohlelo lwabo olusha luyovuka ngokugcwele! Manje sebezi khethele uMoshiach wabo wamanga, uBharaba, futhi uyokwakha ithempeli labo, nesilo sasolwandle sisaqhubeka nokulawula isigqila saso esidala esingasekho engqondweni, futhi bayophumelela, futhi bayoletha ukubhujiswa okuphelele kweBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo, ngokuba balwa ngenkohliso, futhi injalo imvelo yabo.

Futhi bathunyelwe yisicabucabu esihlasela amazwe, futhi yisicabucabu esikhethile ukuba sihole amazwe lapho sebevuke ngokugcwele. Ngoba iSinagoge likaSathane elenze isinqumo salo futhi lakhetha uMoshiach walo omusha wamanga, neBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo liphinde lanika imvume yalo kudrako, ngakho-ke bayonikezelwa ngokugcwele ezandleni zikadrako obanika amandla okuphatha ulaka nesahlulelo, isahlulelo esithunyelwe njengesabelo esiphindwe kabili esivela kuYahweh uqobo, leso sahlulelo esingalibali futhi esiseduze, ngakho bazikhethele ukubhujiswa kwabo.

Kanjalo-ke iBhabhiloni Elifihlakele manje limemezela ukuthi ikusasa lalo likhululekile ekwahlulelweni kukaYahweh njengoba befuna ukubaleka, bekhombisa ukungakholwa ezindleleni zabo. Ngoba iBhabhiloni Elifihlakele liboshwe ngokuphelele obishini lwesicabucabu, futhi alikwazi ukubaleka, futhi iBhabhiloni Elifihlakele lisezinketangweni lilindele ukwahlulelwa kwalo kokugcina. Futhi bazoqhubeka nokonakalisa, ukuphanga, ukubulala, ukuthumba nokuthatha ngenkani ukuchuma kwalo nenzalo yalo ngoba kunikezwe bona ukuba bakufeze lokho, futhi kuyosala incithakalo lapho beyoqhubeka nokuphanga umhlaba ngenxa yezinsiza abazikhanukayo.

Bese kufika ukubhujiswa okuzumayo, futhi ngehora elilodwa liyobhujiswa. Ngiphinda ngithi, impi iyogqashuka ngokuzumayo emhlabeni wonke, nomlilo uyokwehla, futhi bayothumela imicibisholo eminingi ukuze kulibhubhise. Idolobha lalo elikhulu kunawo wonke liyosha, namagagasi amakhulu okubhujiswa ayokhukhula ugu lwalo olusempumalanga nasentshonalanga. Umhlaba uyozamazama ngamandla, futhi liyodabuka phakathi nendawo, nalapho kwake kwaba khona izwe elichumayo, kuyosala amanzi ayolidabula kabili, futhi liyowa ngokuphelele esahlulelweni salo esiphindwe kabili. Yebo, isahlulelo abazikhethela sona lapho benganaki izwi lami engalithumela ngabaphostoli bami nabaprofethi nezinceku ezinkulu nezincane ngokufanayo: ngeqiniso balishaye indiva lonke, okwaholela ekubhujisweni kwabo, futhi ngeke baphinde bavuke. Kanjalo-ke umhlaba unikwa ezandleni zikadrako, futhi uyokunika ukubusa kwakhe kwesihogo kubelusi bakhe abanonya abaphakamisele izinhloso zakhe, futhi bonakele kuze kube phakade.

Ngakho-ke, ningaweli enkohlisweni yesilo esivela emhlabeni ngoba sikhuluma njengodrako. Ningabalandeli abaseBhabhiloni Elifihlakele abalindele ukwahlulelwa kwabo, ngoba ngeqiniso ukwahlulelwa kwabo kuyofika ngehora elilodwa bese kuthi shwaka! Ngoba labo ababengalandela uJesu waseNazaretha njengeNkosi yabo manje sebeyengwe bangena ezingalweni zomkhohlisi ngoba bathanda ukuphila ezinkohlisweni zabo, becabanga ukuthi njengoba kunjalo namuhla, kwakunjalo nayizolo, futhi ngeqiniso le nkosi enkulu izongenza ngibe mkhulu futhi! Kodwa, wena Bhabhiloni Elifihlakele, ngeke uphinde ube mkhulu, nezono zakho zisekhona, futhi ngeke ubalekele isahlulelo sakho esiphezu kwakho.

Futhi yimina, uYeshua HaMashiach, iNkosi yamakhosi noMbusi wababusi, ongazange nginikeze nkosi emhlabeni ngalesi sikhathi ngoba awukho omunye umbuso uYahweh awumisileyo, ngaphandle koMbuso waseZulwini, lapho mina, uYeshua HaMashiach, ngiyiNkosi yamakhosi noMbusi wababusi! Ngakho-ke yiqondeni ukuthi bonke abazinikele kulo mkhohlisi futhi balahla iNkosi noMsindisi wabo uJesu Kristu bayoshiyelwa emlilweni wokucwenga ngoba basezingalweni zikaKristu wamanga, ophinde abe nguMoshiach wabo wamanga. Kanjalo-ke ukwahlukaniswa kokugcina sekufikile esikhathini sami somusa futhi abaningi bakhohlisiwe futhi bathunjwa njengempango yisilo esivela emhlabeni. Futhi nakuba abaningi beyosuka enkohlisweni yakhe baye ekuphileni okuphakade, kuyodinga usizi lukaJakobe ukuze babakhulule ekukhohlisweni okunamandla uma bengayinaki imvumo yami yokuba baphenduke ngokushesha kule mizuzwana yokugcina.

Ngakho-ke ngininika ukuqonda ukuthi uYahweh unikeze icilongo lokugcina ukuba likhale, yebo, icilongo lami lokugcina esikhathini sami somusa, futhi icilongo liyokhala. Ngoba isikhathi sesifikile futhi siphezu kwenu zithandwa zami, lapho ngiyokwehla ezulwini ngezwi elikhulu, ngezwi lengelosi enkulu nangecilongo likaNkulunkulu, futhi abafileyo bekuKristu bayovuka kuqala, nalabo kini abakuKristu bayohlangana nabo emoyeni, kanjalo niyoba nami kuze kube phakade zithandwa zami, futhi ngeke ngishiye abathembekile bami emlilweni wokucwenga ngoba ukucwengwa kwabo ngikuqedile othandweni lwami ngabo ngoba beza kimi ngokuzithandela nalapho ngembulwa kubo.

Ngakho-ke yiqondeni zithandwa zami ukuthi ihora selifikile lokuba udrako avuke ngokuphelele, nelokuba impi nokwahlulela kufike ngokuphelele, nokuba isilo sasolwandle nesilo sasemhlabeni sivuke ngokuphelele nodrako wabo. Ukuba angizange ngifushanise izinsuku, bekungeke kusinde muntu, ngakho-ke ukuhlasela kwabo okuzumayo kuyofika emhlabeni wonke futhi bayolibhubhisa ngokuphelele iBhabhiloni Elifihlakele. Ngakho ningaweli ezinkohlisweni nasemangeni abo njengoba bezishayela ihlombe bethi obala ebusweni bezahlulelo zikaYahweh nolaka lweWundlu "ukuthula nokulondeka" "ukuthula nokulondeka"! Ngoba nginguYeshua HaMashiach oyosusa ngokuphelele ukuthula nokulondeka emhlabeni kuze kube sekupheleni kwesonto lamashumi ayisikhombisa likaDaniyeli lapho ngimisa umbuso wami ngokuphelele emhlabeni. Ngakho-ke, ningaweli enkohlisweni yabo.

Futhi yimina othumela la Mazwi ngesithunywa sami nenceku yami, futhi ngesithunywa sami nenceku yami la Mazwi athunyelwe.

Bhekani! Isikhathi sesifikile sokuba iNkosi yenu ikhanyise inkazimulo nobukhosi bayo esibhakabhakeni ukuze bonke babone, futhi ngiyoqoqa bonke abathembekile bami kuKristu kusukela emikhawulweni yomhlaba njengoba ngivela emoyeni ukuqoqa abathembekile kuKristu, nokubonakala kwami kuyobonakala esibhakabhakeni ngendlela efanayo nombani ovela empumalanga, ubonakale entshonalanga, futhi ngeke ngiphikwe!

NginguYeshua HaMashiach oyokuza ukuzokwahlulela nokulwa emhlabeni, nombuso wami awuyikuba nasiphelo.

Ngingulokho uMkhulu ENGINGUYE angenze ngakho, nezikhathi sezifikile.

NgiyiNgonyama yesizwe sakwaJuda futhi izikhathi zami zifikile.

Konke kuyoba kuze kube phakade, Yebo no-Amen!

Kusuka kuYahweh uNkulunkulu omkhulu Waphakade:

Yizwani, yizwani amazwi kaNkulunkulu Omi Phakade Nongunaphakade! Izikhathi zokuhlupheka kukaJakobe sezifikile emhlabeni nezikhathi zokuvuna sezifike. Bese ngokuzumayo nangokungalindelekile iNdodana yami, uKristu Jesu, ophinde abe nguYeshua HaMashiach, iyovela emafwini ukuze bobabili abafileyo nabaphilayo kuKristu bavuke emhlabeni ngokungafi nangokungaboli ukuze beme besanda kudalelwa ingunaphakade, ngisho nalapho beyokuma khona kanye egumbini lami lesihlalo sobukhosi, isikhathi engisilangazelele nengisifisile emaphakadeni adlule.

Ngakho-ke, lalelani uYeshua HaMashiach ngoba nginikeze umyalo, futhi leso sikhathi ngeqiniso siyasondela, nosuku nesikhathi esiqondile manje sekwaziwa emazulwini, nakuba kungakembulwa ngokuphelele emhlabeni. Kanjalo-ke izikhathi zalelo cilongo lokugcina sezifikile, nolibo lokuqala luyobuthelwa, nomkhwenyana uyozihlanganisa ngesivumelwano saphakade nomakoti wakhe othembekile, futhi bayogcwalisa izinhloso zami zaphakade, neTestamente Elisha likaYeshua HaMashiach liyomiswa ngokuphelele kuze kube phakade, ngoba zonke izithembiso zami zinguYebo no-Amen!

Abathembekile kuKristu bayokuma esihlalweni sokwahlulela seNkosi yabo bamukele ngemisebenzi yabo, noma mihle noma mibi, kodwa bonke banokuphila okuphakade. Futhi abaningi bayothwala umvuzo omkhulu kangangokuthi akukaze kungene enhliziyweni yomuntu ngokuphelele izinto engibalungisele zona.
