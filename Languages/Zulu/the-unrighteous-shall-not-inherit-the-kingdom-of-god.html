Ephreli 19, 2024
Ukuduma 5 & 6

Abangalungile Abayikulidlalifa Umbuso KaNkulunkulu!

Okuvela KuYeshua HaMashiach:

Ndodana yami, uma uyokwamukela amazwi ami, ufihle imiyalo yami kuwe; ukuze uthambekisele indlebe yakho ekuhlakanipheni, unikele inhliziyo yakho ekuqondeni. Ningazenzi lukhuni izinhliziyo zenu, njengasekucunuleni, ngosuku lokulingwa ehlane: futhi nazazi ukuthi mina, iNkosi uJesu Kristu, nginani njalo futhi ngikhona njalo ezimpilweni zenu njengoba nibambelele kimi nasensindisweni yami, ngokuba mina ngiyinhloko yoMzimba, futhi ngiyiNkosi yenu ethembekile, Makoti wami othandekayo. Ngokuba nginilungisele indawo nekusasa, futhi kukhona umvuzo omkhulu kwabizwa bami, nabathembekile, nabakhethiweyo.

Anginibizelanga ekulahlekelweni nasekujezisweni, futhi anginibizelanga ehlazweni nasekuzilahleni; anifundanga yini ukuthi akukho ukulahlwa kulabo abakuKristu Jesu, abangahambi ngokwenyama kodwa ngokomoya? Ngakho-ke kholwani amazwi ami! Anginibizelanga ukuba niphile ethunzini lezono zenu zakudala, noma ukuba niphile ethunzini lezenzo zenu zobusha, kodwa nginibizele ukuba nife ezindleleni zenu ezindala zesono futhi nithathe impilo entsha kimi njengoba nilahla inyama yenu, njengoba nilahla izono zenu nihambe ekuphileni okusha kimi. Bhekani! Ngenza konke kube kusha! Alilona yini ithemba lenu leli?

Bantwana bami, nginikhiphile ekonakaleni kwaleli zwe lamanje elifayo, futhi nginikhululile emandleni obumnyama, nganihumusha nganingenisa embusweni wami, umbuso kaNkulunkulu, oholwa yimi, iNkosi yamakhosi noMbusi wababusi, lapho uYahweh enguNkulunkulu, ngokuba akekho omunye. Nginibizele enkazimulweni nasebuhleni kimi, futhi nginikhululile embusweni kaSathane, umbuso wobumnyama, nganingenisa eMbusweni wami, lapho kukhona ukukhanya! Ngakho-ke, vuka wena oleleyo, uvuke kwabafileyo, futhi mina, uJesu Kristu, ngizokunika ukukhanya.

Anifundanga yini ezincwadini engazithumela kuMakoti wami, engazithumela kini, lona kanye iBandla lami? Anazi yini ukuthi abangalungile abayikulidla ifa lombuso kaNkulunkulu? Ningakhohliswa: nazifebe, nabakhonza izithombe, naziphingi, nabesilisa abathotshisiwe, nabazisebenzisa kabi nabesilisa, Namasela, nabahahayo, nazidakwa, nazithuki, nabaphangi, abayikulidla ifa lombuso kaNkulunkulu. Futhi babenjalo abanye benu: kodwa senihlanzekile, kodwa seningcwelisiwe, kodwa senilungisisiwe eGameni leNkosi uJesu, nangoMoya kaNkulunkulu wethu.

Aniziqondi yini izimiso zami, o, bandla elisivivi laseLawodikeya? Anginibizelanga ukuba nibuyele enyameni yenu, futhi anginibizelanga ukuba niqhubeke ezindleleni zenu ezingalungile. Cabangani, niqondisise; lowo ocabanga ukuthi umi, makaqaphele angawi! Ziqapheleni nina, nemfundiso: qhubekani kuzo: ngokuba ngokwenza lokhu, uyozisindisa wena, nalabo abakuzwayo. Qondani, bantwanyana bami abasivivi, ukuthi anikwazi ukuphila impilo yokuyekelela phakathi kokuhle nokubi, nicabanga ukuthi nilungile phambi kwami, lapho nikhetha okubi okuncane emicikilishweni yenu yokhetho engingayivumeli noma engiyisekeliyo.

Phumani kuyo, bantu bami, njengoba NGINGANIKEZANGA isixazululo sezepolitiki, futhi angininikanga neze umholi wezwe ozokwenza noma yini ibe nkulu futhi. Nikhohlisiwe, futhi nilungiselelwa ukulandela nokuvumela indodana yokulahlwa ehamba obala phakathi kwenu, nakuba ingakavezwa okwamanje. Kodwa nina enicela ukuqonda nizokwazi ukuthi ingubani, ukuze nigweme inkohliso yayo. Cha, ukuyekelela akusikhona okwenu, njengoba ningabantwana bami nabafowethu, futhi angiyizuzanga insindiso yenu ngokunikela ngokuphila kwami njengesihlengo sabaningi ngokuyekelela noSathane; ngokuba ukuyekelela kuwukuhlanganyela nebutho labo. Ngakho-ke ungenzi iphutha: Noma ungimele mina ngokulunga nangobungcwele beqiniso, noma usuhlangene nebutho likaSathane embusweni wakhe ofayo, ngakho-ke PENDUKANI!

Nizuzani ngokuyekelela nobubi? Aniqondi yini ukuthi kufanele ninqobe okubi ngokuhle? Ngakho-ke yimani niqinile kimi niyeke ukuntengantenga njengenhlanzi ephume emanzini. Abaningi benu basapheshulwa yiyo yonke imimoya yemfundiso nobuqili bezandla, lapho beqamele khona ukuze bakhohlise. Mina, Kristu Jesu, ngikhulumile, futhi ngeke ngithuliswe. Iningi lenu liziyekelele nina nebandla lami, futhi ngiyanibuza: koze kube nini nintenga phakathi kwemibono emibili? Uma iNkosi ingoNkulunkulu, mlandeleni: kodwa uma kunguBali, mlandeleni yena. Kalula nje, anikwazi ukuyekelela nikhethe noma yiluphi uhlobo lobubi nicabange ukuthi ningimele mina; lapho nikhetha okubi, nikhetha imithombo yalobo bubi njengenkosi nomphathi wenu nibe yisigqila sesono. Anazi yini ukuthi lowo enizinikela kuye njengezinceku ukuba nimlalele, niyizinceku zakhe enimlalelayo: noma ezesono kuze kube sekufeni, noma ezokulalela kuze kube sekulungeni?

Ngizweni, futhi ningizwe kahle, Bandla lami; yazini ukuthi ngimi ngokunengwa yimikhuba eyandile esizweni senu nasemhlabeni wonke, eniyivumile nayivumela ukuba ingene eBandleni lami ukuze ihlupha abantwanyana bami. Njengasezinsukwini zikaNowa, kuyokuba njalo. Izinsuku zikaNowa kwakuyisikhathi lapho umhlaba wawubuswa yizinengiso ezimbi ezenziwe yizingelosi eziwileyo, inzalo yazo kanye, inzalo yobubi eyenzelwe ukubhujiswa kwenu okuphelele: futhi zizobuya, ngokuba bhekani, ngiletha imidondoshiya ukuze igcwalise ulaka lwami. Lokhu zakwenza ngokususa nangokwephula imingcele yemvelo uBaba wami ayibekayo ukuze ukuthula nokulunga kubuse, kodwa ngokususa nangokuwela ngokungafanele imingcele ecacile emvelweni uqobo, uBaba wami ayimisayo, abaningi benu bazingcolisile ngokuvuma kwenu ububi obandile ezikhathini zenu. Into ebike yaba khona, iyona eyoba khona: nalokho okwenziwe, yikho okuyokwenziwa; futhi akukho lutho olusha ngaphansi kwelanga. Anifundanga yini, o, nina abokholo oluncane? Ukususa imingcele kaNkulunkulu uqobo kubanga uhlanya, nokugula, nokudideka kwazo zonke izinto. Abaningi benu bajabulela ukugula kwengqondo, nicabanga ukuthi owesilisa angaba ngowesifazane nowesifazane angaba ngowesilisa, futhi nihola abantwana benu enkeni yenu. Abaningi benu baya emasontweni aholwa ngabefundisi bamanga abayizitabane abavulekile nabazidlayo ekuguleni kwabo, futhi nicabanga ukuthi bakhulumela mina? Yazini lokhu: uma nikuleso simo sesono, ngizoninikela ezinkanukweni enizifisa ngobudlova, futhi niyovuna isithelo sezindlela zenu ezingcolile.

Bhekani! Ngenxa yalesi sizathu, uNkulunkulu wabanikela ezinkanukweni ezilihlazo: ngokuba nabesifazane babo bashintsha ukusetshenziswa kwemvelo bakushintshela kulokho okuphambene nemvelo: Kanjalo nabesilisa, beshiya ukusetshenziswa kwemvelo kowesifazane, bavutha enkanukweni yabo omunye komunye, abesilisa nabesilisa besebenza lokho okungafanele, bezamukelela bona umvuzo wephutha labo obuwafanele. Kodwa cha, akwanele ukuholwa ngabathotshisiwe namanga abo; nivumela abantwana benu ukuba bayekelelwe baze bacwiywe phambi kwami.

Kunomphumela omkhulu olindelwe yilezi zenzo, futhi ngeke zihambe zingajezisiwe. Kodwa nina niyaqhubeka ehlanyeni lenu, futhi ngaphandle kokuba niphenduke, ngizonikhafula emlonyeni wami. Wozani kimi ngokuphenduka, nihlale endwangwini yesaka nasemlotheni, ukuze ngazi ubuqotho bokuphenduka kwenu, nikhale nililele izono zenu ukuze ningakhokhi inani ekwahlulelweni. Iningi lenu liyazidla kakhulu ngoba nizama ukungenza iNkosi nisesenhliziyweni engenakholo, ngokuba ngenhliziyo umuntu ukholwa kuze kube sekulungeni. Ngakho-ke yibani nokholo ukuze nisindiswe; ukholo lwenu luyobonakaliswa kubo bonke ngokulahla kwenu isono nokuphuma ezindleleni zenu ezingcolile. Ababi bahamba nxazonke, lapho kukhushulwa khona abantu abanyanyekayo. Kodwa nina nisaqhubeka nokuvumelana nezindlela zesodomeli, futhi nisiza ukuletha ububi obukhulu ezweni, kangangokuthi empeleni nivotela abahlukumezi bezingane ukuba bahole amazwe enu. Aninalo ngisho nezinga eliyisisekelo lokuqonda, futhi anilandeli indlela yami; futhi uma niqhubeka, ngizoninikela ngokuphelele ezinkanukweni zenu ezinengekayo ukuze kubhujiswe inyama ukuze umoya usindiswe.

Ake ngivule amehlo enu, nina eningazi ngamabomu futhi niyizimpumputhe, nina enihleli ekungcoleni nasebumnyameni, nizigingqa odakeni. Anikwazi ukunikeza imvume yenu ebubini nicabange ukuthi nikhonza mina, ngokuba ngamelana nobubi nesilingo kwaze kwaba yilapho iconsi lokugcina legazi ligeleza livela kimi liya emhlabathini obomvu, ngokuba ngingu-Adamu wokugcina futhi ngenziwe umoya ophilayo. Iningi lenu liphuphuthekiswe ngamazwi amahle nangezinkulumo ezimnandi futhi ninikeza imvume yenu egcwele ezigebengwini nakubantu ababi abahlukumeza izingane. Bahlukumeza izingane, bahlukumeza izingane ngenxa yezinkanuko zabo ezimbi: Ngokuba kanjalo luza ulaka lukaNkulunkulu phezu kweBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlakalo, kodwa iningi lenu liyaqhubeka nokunikeza imvume yenu kulezi zilwane ezinonya ezibekelwe imililo yesihogo saphakade engeke icishwe. Phumani kuyo, bantu bami, noma nizohlanganyela ezinhluphekweni zayo nasekwahlulelweni okwesabekayo okuzayo phezu kwayo; futhi nginixwayisile. Ngokuba kunakuhlangana kuni uKristu noBeliyali? Uma nihlanganyela emangeni ayo, nizohlanganyela ezinhluphekweni zayo; ngakho-ke zahlukaniseni ngokuphelele ngezwi nangesenzo, futhi ningavumelani nayo nakancane. Ningaphili izimpilo ezinganaki, ezithambile; ngokuba ngizobajezisa bonke abenza kanjalo. Nicabanga ukuthi ngenxa yokuthi ninokuphila okuphakade anikwazi ukubhekana nokulahlekelwa okukhulu nemiphumela? Ngizokwaphuca imivuzo yenu ngiyinike abanye, futhi niyoba abancane kunabo bonke embusweni wezulu uma niqhubeka, ngokuba usho kanje uYeshua HaMashiach, uMyeni, kuMakoti wami!

Okuvela KuYahweh, uNkulunkulu Omkhulu Ophakade:

O, nina eningenamsebenzi enilimaza indalo yami, nina eningenamsebenzi enizidayisele kuSathane, nina eningenamsebenzi enenze uSathane uNkulunkulu wenu, nina eningenamsebenzi enenqaba indodana yami uJesu Kristu nensindiso engiyinikeze ngegama lakhe, nina eningenamsebenzi enivumelile abantwana benu ukuba bashe emlilweni kaMoloki. Ngizoninika izwe enilifisayo, futhi nginilungisele izwe elingenami, izwe elingenandodana yami uKristu Jesu, izwe elinechibi elikhulu lomlilo nesibabule elenzelwe nina nje.

Ngizoninika ubumnyama enibufisa kangaka, ngokuba ukukhanya kwami ngeke kube khona lapho, futhi niyochitha iphakade nihlukaniswe nami, Yahweh, uNkulunkulu omkhulu okhazimulayo, onkazimulo nokukhanya kwakhe kungekho nyama engakubona futhi ongowaphakade naphakade. Nina enihlubukayo, enikhohlakele, enabhubhisa indalo yami kusukela ekuqaleni ezinkanukweni zenu nenzondo nomona, yazini ukuthi nizibangele isijeziso esikhulu nokuhlushwa okukhulu engeke niphinde nelapheke kukho nabo bonke abadukiswa ngamanga enu benqaba uJesu Kristu, benqaba ukusuka emangeni enu niye eqinisweni lami.

Nicabanga ukuthi ukuphambanisa izindlela zami nokususa imingcele yami kuyinto elula, kodwa niyoluqonda ulaka lwami ngokushesha nje lapho ilangabi lokuqala linithinta, futhi niyogwinywa ngumlilo nesibabule engeke niphinde niphunyuke kukho. Ngaphezu kwalokho, nibulele, nadlwengula, naphanga, nahlukumeza, nalimaza, nabulala, nabhubhisa, futhi nahlanganyela emacaleni amabi kakhulu, naze nafisa nenyama engaziwa; futhi nizihlanganise nezingelosi eziwileyo, ngakho-ke ngizonihlanganisa nechibi lomlilo engililungisele uDeveli nezingelosi zakhe, futhi ngeke niphunyuke. Aninakho ukuthula.

Ngizokhipha ukwahlulela kini, ngokuba nginguNkulunkulu wokwahlulela, futhi nginguNkulunkulu wempindiselo. Nokho, niyoma ngokuzidla ngokumelana nami, nimemeze icala lenu, nicabanga ukuthi ngizokwesaba izindlela zenu zobusathane zokuthi: yenzani enikuthandayo, kuyoba wonke umthetho. Aninakho ukuthula. Ngiyophindisela, futhi nithi nginguNkulunkulu onothando (engiyilo), kodwa-ke nilindele ukuba ngingakhiphi ukwahlulela kulabo abangcolise indalo yami, kulabo abahlukumeza babulala indodana yami ezelwe yodwa njengoba yazinikela yaba yisihlengo sabo bonke; yebo, nilindele ukuba ngingakhiphi ukwahlulela kini ngenxa yezindlela zenu ezingenamsebenzi lapho nibulele, nahlukumeza, futhi nalimaza abangenacala futhi nilambise abantwanyana bami ngenxa yezinhlelo zenu.

Ngempela, uSathane uqobo wayezobhubhisa konke okungokwami ngenxa yobugovu bakhe, futhi nangaleso sikhathi nizama ukungisola ngobuwula ezahlulelweni zami ezilungileyo lapho ngiza ukuvikela abami. Izinsuku zenu zokudukisa abantwanyana bami sezizophela. Bhekani! Isilo senu nomprofethi wamanga bayobhujiswa ngokuphelele, futhi ngizobaphonsa bephila kulelo chibi elikhulu elivutha umlilo nesibabule; bayolinda ukuba nihlanganyele nabo efeni lenu.

Ukwahlulela okukhulu kuphezu kwakho, o, nkosana yobumnyama; umbuso wakho nabo bonke abakulandelayo sebezocekelwa phansi ngokuphelele. Ngokuba anibona onkulunkulu, futhi anikwazi ukuzikhulula nina eningenamsebenzi esandleni sami, futhi ngizonibopha ebumnyameni obujulile enibufisa kangaka, ngisho naseluthaleni olujule kakhulu ngizonibopha ngeketanga elikhulu engeke nilikhiphe. Yebo, eluthaleni niyoba khona usuku olulodwa, ngokuba usuku olulodwa eNkosini lunjengeminyaka eyinkulungwane, neminyaka eyinkulungwane injengosuku olulodwa. Futhi nangaleso sikhathi ngizonikhulula isikhashana ukuze nilinge niqoqe abokugcina balabo abanyanyekayo abayolandela nina nezindlela zenu. Futhi ngiyobe senginiqeda nonke ngomlilo, nezihlalo zobukhosi zokwahlulela ziyobekwa, futhi ngizokwahlulela nina nonke benzi bokubi, futhi niyoya esijezisweni saphakade kude nobuso beNkosi. Niyosha phambi kwezingelosi naphambi kwewundlu, futhi ziyonibuka nihlushiwe futhi nijezisiwe, ngokuba nonile kubo bonke, futhi niyokhokhela ngokugcwele izono zenu kuze kube phakade.

Khona-ke niyovalelwa ejele lenu, futhi niyokhohlwa, futhi ngeke niphinde nikhunjulwe, ngokuba izinto zangaphambili ziyodlula zikhohlwe. Ngokuba bhekani, ngizokwenza izulu elisha nomhlaba omusha okuhlala kuwo ukulunga; futhi mina, Yahweh, uNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade wabo bonke onkulunkulu, ngokuba akekho onjengami, bhekani, ngizokwenza zonke izinto zibe zintsha, ngokuba mina, Yahweh ngikukhulumile! UYahweh, uNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade, uyokwenza isihlalo sakhe sobukhosi emhlabeni, nendawo yami yokuhlala iyoba phakathi kwabantu, futhi ngeke niphinde nibe khona, o, nina eningenamsebenzi, futhi niyosha phakade ejele lenu elikhohliwe lokuhlushwa engeke niphinde niphunyuke kulo, ngokuba ngizobeka uphawu futhi akekho ongalephula.

Nokho, iningi lenu elizwelana noSathane lingisola ngobuwula ngokuthatha ukwahlulela nempindiselo ezandleni zami ukuze ngikhululele emalangabini labo abazenze bafanelwe yisijeziso saphakade. Yizwani amazwi ami, o, nina enakhile emhlabeni: iNdodana yami iyeza, futhi iza ngempindiselo enkulu, intukuthelo, nolaka ngezenzo ezimbi ezenziwe yizandla zenu. Izikhathi zokwahlulela, ngisho nokwahlulelwa kosuku olukhulu lukaKristu sekuzoqhuma emhlabeni wonke, futhi ngeke kubekhona ikhambi kwababi, akukho khambi kuSathane, futhi akukho khambi kubo bonke abahlangene naye, njengoba bonke beyoya ekulahlweni kwaphakade, futhi ngizobuyisela kubo konke abakufake kwabangenacala.

Ngakho-ke, ngizobuyisela izifiso zezinhliziyo zabo ezimbi nayo yonke imisebenzi yabo emibi kubo kuze kube phakade. Bonani intukuthelo yami ibila futhi nazazi ukuthi izikhathi zokubuyiselwa kwazo zonke izinto seziseduze, futhi azisekho izinombolo emawashini emazulwini, ngokuba zonke izinombolo ziwile ebusweni bewashi, ngokuba isikhathi sesiphelile! Ngeluleka bonke abafunda lawa mazwi ukuba bagijimele endodaneni yami uJesu Kristu ngokuphenduka benze yena abe yiNkosi; ngokuba akekho oyokuza kimi ngaphandle kukaJesu Kristu, ngokuba alikho elinye igama engilinikezileyo lokusindiswa, futhi zonke izithombe zenu ziyosha. Kusesekhona isikhathi sensindiso, futhi noma ubani obiza igama leNkosi kuze kube sekupheleni uyosindiswa. Ngakho gijimelani kuJesu Kristu nisesenethuba, ngokuba usuku lweNkosi selufikile, futhi ngizoyidedela iNdodana yami, nolaka lweWundlu luyokwaneliswa. Yangeni iNdodana funa ithukuthele, nife endleleni, lapho ulaka lwayo luvutha kancane: Babusisiwe bonke abathembela kuyo.


Ukuze uthole ireferensi: U-Isaya 13:1 ovela kwiSeptuagint: Ngiyanikeza umyalo, futhi ngiyabaletha: imidondoshiya iyeza ukuzogcwalisa ulaka lwami, ijabula ngasikhathi sinye futhi ithuka.
