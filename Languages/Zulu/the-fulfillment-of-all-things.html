Ukugcwaliseka Kwezinto Zonke!

Zwanini Amazwi eNkosi yenu ephilayo noMsindisi, uMesiya wenu weqiniso, uYeshua HaMashiashi. Qondisisani nilalele Amazwi ami bantwana bami ngoba manje ngizokhuluma mayelana nokugcwaliseka kwezinto zonke, ngokuba ukugcwaliseka kwazo sekusondele. Ngakho-ke qondisisani, ngenxa yokuthi isintu manje sesisilahle isivumelwano sikaYahweh sokuthula, samala noJesu waseNazaretha (ngokuba yimi isivumelwano senu sokuthula), izinkathi zomhlaba manje zizoguquka zisuke emseni ziye ekwahlulelweni, zisuke esiHhawini ziye enhlekeleleni, zisuke ekuthuleni ziye empini, zisuke ebumneneni ziye olakeni, manje zizoguqukela empindiselweni ephelele nembubhiso ephelele kulabo abakhileyo emhlabeni. Ngisho nabo bonke labo abenqabile ukungamukela njengeNkosi nokwamukela uthando lwami nentethelelo yami ngabo, obekuyothi ngayo ngibabuyisele kuMdali wabo ngibanike ukuphila okuphakade, ukuze bangaphinde bafe.

Kodwa esikhundleni sokuba umuntu wemvelo eze kimi ngokuphelele, abaningi benqabile ngokuzidla kobuhlubuki bekhetha esikhundleni salokho ukuhlala noSathane nokuhlubuka kwabo, futhi ngokuzenzakalelayo bahlanganyela nabo kungaba ngokungazi, noma bekwazi kahle ukukhetha kwabo. Ngalokho-ke labo abakhethe ngobuwula obungaka manje bazoba ngaphansi kolaka lukaNkulunkulu nolaka lweWundlu oseluzothululwa ngokugcwele maduzane. Futhi nakuba abaningi ngalesi sikhathi benqabile, isixuku esikhulu kakhulu sizokuza kimi sivume uJesu njengeNkosi, futhi siyobiza egameni likaMesiya wabo kuze kube sekupheleni. Ngalokho-ke ngenxa yegunya likaYeshua HaMashiashi bayosindiswa. Kodwa abaningi kakhulu bayobhubha, ngisho nabo bonke labo amagama abo angalotshwanga eNcwadini Yokuphila kusukela ekusekelweni komhlaba, kanjalo kuyoba ukukhetha kwabo.

Ngakho-ke, ngendlela efanayo nalezi zinguquko ezenzeka emhlabeni ekwahlulelweni, ukuthula sekuzinzile emazulwini futhi manje impi isizogubuzela umhlaba wonke. Kwaba khona impi ezulwini futhi ummangaleli wabazalwane waphonswa phansi, waphonswa phansi, futhi lowo dlangamandla omkhulu obomvu manje useboshwe emhlabeni ulindele ukwahlulelwa kwakhe, futhi ukwahlulelwa kwakhe akuphuzi. Ngokwehlulwa okuphelele uyofeza izinjongo zakhe ezinhlelweni zaphakade zikaYahweh, ngokuba ngisho nezitha zakhe ziyalilalela izwi lakhe, ngakho-ke unikwe amandla alinganiselwe kakhulu futhi umbuso wakhe uyobhujiswa ngokuphelele. Bhekani! Leyo nyoka endala, yebo lowo dlangamandla omkhulu obomvu, manje isiphonswe phansi emhlabeni, futhi inolaka olukhulu ngoba yazi ukuthi isikhathi sayo sifushane! Ngakho-ke, mina, uYeshua HaMashiashi, ngeluleka bonke ngabanye ukuba bagijimele ekuzwakaleni kwezwi lami babize egameni lami elinamandla kuze kube sekupheleni, ngokuba labo abangakuthandi ukuphila kwabo kuze kube sekugcineni bayovukela enkazimulweni yaphakade, enjabulweni, nasemvuzweni, futhi mkhulu umvuzo wabo! Ngoba bonke ababiza egameni lami kuze kube sekupheleni bayosindiswa, kungakhathaliseki ukuthi babiza ngaluphi ulimi, futhi mina Jesu Kristu, enginguYeshua HaMashiashi, ngimemezela lawa mazwi!

Ngalokho izivumelwano nezithembiso zasendulo ziyogcwaliseka, yebo, zonke izivumelwano zazo zonke izikhathi ngokuba linjalo ivangeli laphakade. Futhi njengoba impi igubuzela umhlaba, ukuthula manje sekumisiwe emazulwini lapho amazulu manje eselungiselelwe ukwamukela abathembekileyo kuKristu, ngisho nalabo abafe bethembekile kuKristu, nalabo abaphilayo nabasasele bephila izimpilo zabo kuKristu. Ngoba bonke abahamba ngomthetho womoya wokuphila kuKristu Jesu enkathini yami yomusa bayobuthelwa ndawonye bamukeliswe umvuzo wabo waphakade futhi bayoqonda bamukele izikhundla zabo ukuze bafeze injongo yaphakade kaYahweh abadalelwe yona ngokusha kuKristu. Kanjalo-ke umshado woMkhwenyana noMakoti manje usuzokwenzeka, futhi kuyoba nomgubho omkhulu emazulwini enkazimulweni nasebukhazikhazini baphakade!

Ngoba kusukela ekuqaleni, uNkulunkulu wadala u-Adamu, kwathi ku-Adamu wadala u-Eva ukuze abe ngumngane wakhe waphakade futhi abe ngunina wabo bonke abaphilayo, nokuba bathele bazale bande. Bayalwa ukuthi kuyo yonke imithi esensimini yase-Edeni, bangadla ngokukhululeka kunoma yimuphi umuthi ngaphandle komuthi wolwazi lokuhle nokubi. Kanjalo-ke ngokuyenga okunobuqili kwenyoka u-Eva wakhohliswa, wathatha isithelo esenqatshelwe wadla, wanika umyeni wakhe u-Adamu, yena wadla azi, kanjalo isono sangcolisa isintu, ekufeni kwabo sebeye bafa selokhu. Ngakho-ke u-Adamu no-Eva abazange basifeze isikhundla sabo esiphakeme sokubusa nokunqoba umhlaba ngokulunga, nokuthela bazale bande bagcwalise umhlaba ngokulunga, kodwa esikhundleni salokho bona benza isono baphila ngenkokhelo yesono kwaze kwathi leyo nkokhelo yagcina ngokufa kwabo, nasekufeni isintu sesilokhu sifa. Ekupheleni kokuvuka kwabalungileyo bayokwamukela umvuzo wabo nembuyiselo yabo ngokuba babethembekile kuze kube sekupheleni.

Kanjalo-ke uYahweh, ngokwazi kwakhe kwangaphambili okuphakade, wayazi ngaphambi kokuba kwenzeke ukuthi isintu sizozikhethela isono nokufa kusukela ekuqaleni. No-El Shaddai, ngisho nasothandweni lwakhe olukhulu athanda ngalo isintu, (nayo yonke indalo yakhe), walungiselela umusa nesihe esingenakulinganiswa lapho ememezela ukuthi wayezobeka ubutha phakathi kwenzalo kaSathane nenzalo yowesifazane. Inzalo kaSathane yalimala isithende sami eKhalivari ekugcwalisekeni kwamaZwi kaYahweh, futhi manje sengizothatha isinyathelo sokuchoboza ikhanda lenyoka ngokuphelele, ngoba linjalo ikusasa layo nabo bonke abayilandelayo, futhi njengoba kulotshiwe, kuzoqhubeka kwenziwe kuze kube sekuphelelweni.

Nokho, uNkulunkulu ukhethile ngokuhlakanipha kwakhe okungenamkhawulo ukwembula izinhlelo zakhe zomusa, isihe, nokuthula ukuze kuhlengwe isintu ngokugcwele ngezigaba, kodwa ubelokhu enika ithemba elikhulu kwabalungileyo lokuthi ngolunye usuku oluseduze bayovuselwa ebuntshani bokuphila kwabafileyo ukuze bangaphinde bafe, futhi babeyophila enaleni nasekuthuleni zonke izinsuku zokuphila kwabo, okuyizinsuku ezingeke zibe nasiphelo. Kuyo yonke le minyaka abathembekileyo baye bafa benokholo oluphelele kuYahweh ukuthi uzofeza izithembiso zakhe, futhi baye bafa bethembele ezivumelwaneni zikaYahweh njengethemba labo lensindiso, futhi zonke izivumelwano zikaYahweh ziyogcwaliseka ngokugcwele ebusweni bukaKristu Jesu, yebo, mina, iNkosi yenu evusiwe noMsindisi. Kanjalo bakholwa uNkulunkulu, futhi kubo lokho kwabalelwa njengokulunga, ngisho nakubo bonke abakholwa futhi baba nethemba enzalweni ethenjisiwe yowesifazane.

Kanjalo-ke uhlelo lwezikhathi lukaYahweh lokuhlengwa kwesintu ngokugcwele belulokhu lugxile kimi, uKristu Jesu, ngisho noJesu waseNazaretha onguYeshua HaMashiashi, ngokuba ngiyiNkosi yamaNkosi enyukele ezulwini, eyiWundlu likaNkulunkulu, futhi engilethe insindiso yaphakade kumJuda noweZizwe ngokufanayo, futhi ngisazomsindisa uJakobe kulabo abahlasela izwe lesithembiso, futhi ukubhujiswa kwabo akuphuzi. Nginguye okuyothi kuye ibhubesi lilale newundlu, ngokuba kimi ibhubesi lesizwe sakwaJuda newundlu likaNkulunkulu kuzofinyelela ekugcwalisekeni okuphelele. Ngoba ngiyibhubesi lesizwe sakwaJuda futhi ngiyiWundlu likaNkulunkulu, ngoba ngiyiNkosi yamaKhosi neNkosi yamaNkosi, futhi nginguMpristi Ophakeme phakade ngokohlelo lukaMelkisedeki.

Kanjalo u-Adamu no-Eva baphila ngokwethembeka beqhubeka phambi kweNkosi uNkulunkulu wabo, bathela bazala banda, nokho uSathane waphinda wahlela okunye ukuhlasela ngokuhlubuka kwezingelosi eziwile. Zahlanganyela ekuhlubukeni kukaSathane, futhi ukuze zanelise izinkanuko zazo zashiya inkazimulo yaphakade yesimo sazo sokuqala, ezadalelwa sona, ukuze zizingcolise nabesifazane, ngisho nenyama negazi, ngoba zazizohlala enkazimulweni yaphakade kodwa esikhundleni salokho zakhetha isijeziso sazo saphakade ukuze zifeze izinkanuko zazo ngokulimaza nokubulala indalo eyayikhona ngaleso sikhathi.

Ngakho-ke lezi zingelosi eziwile zafundisa umuntu owile ukuthi angazibhubhisa kanjani ngohlu lozalo lukaKhayini nangolwazi olwenqatshelwe namacebo abawafundisa isintu. Zazenza onkulunkulu zafuna ukukhonzwa FUTHI zazithathela abafazi ngendluzula kubo bonke ezabakhetha, futhi abesifazane abaningi bazinikela ngokuyalelwa nguSathane ukuze balinge futhi bahehe lezi zingelosi ukuba ziwelwe ezonweni zazo zenkanuko abazibophela kuzo phakade esiqalekisweni sazo, ngokuba bafungelana ngaphakathi kwabo. Lokhu kwenzeka ezinsukwini zikaJerede, futhi amanyala azo, ngisho namaNefili azo, asakazeka emhlabeni wonke embusweni wazo womhlaba wonke phakathi nalokho okubizwa ngokuthi inkathi yazo yegolide.

Izingelosi nenzalo yazo zakonakalisa umhlaba nakho konke okukuwo, futhi kwenziwa izinhlobo ezixakile zofuzo futhi zanda emhlabeni, ngokuba zona kuzo zonke izinyama futhi izinkanuko zazo zazingena mkhawulo, futhi zazenza onkulunkulu, ngisho nonkulunkulu abadala kodwa empeleni abayilutho ngaphandle kwabazenzisi ababamba iqhaza ekuxubeni izinhlobo zezilwane okuyisinengiso. Kanjalo umhlaba wonakaliswa ngokuphelele wabuswa amaNefili, ayedla abantu nempahla yabantu. Futhi bonke ababesasele bengangcolisiwe ngesikhathi sikaNowa (ngokuba uNowa wayelungile eqotho futhi wahamba noYahweh wathola umusa emehlweni kaYahweh) kwakuyimiphefumulo eyisishiyagalombili eyasinda kanye nezilwane uYahweh aziletha eMkhunjini ukuze kusinde izinhlobo zazo. Kanjalo-ke uNkulunkulu wakhukhula umhlaba ngamanzi futhi yonke inkohlakalo yezwe elalikhona ngaleso sikhathi yabhubha, kanti lezo zingelosi azinakho ukuthula, noma intethelelo futhi ingunaphakade naphakade isijeziso sazo esinzima siyoba ngesazo ngokuba zifanele. Futhi akuzona lezi zingelosi zodwa, kodwa bonke abazihlanganisa nokuhlubuka kukaSathane bayohlupheka ngaleyo ndlela futhi banxeshezelwe phakade kulelo chibi elikhulu lomlilo wesibabule esiluhlaza okwesibhakabhaka esivutha amalangabi ashisayo nacwebileyo esijeziso.

Ngemuva kukazamcolo, uNkulunkulu wamisa umhlaba okhona manje, ngisho wamisa ekuqaleni ngokulunga. Futhi uYahweh wangena esivumelwaneni noNowa, lapho uYahweh amema khona uNowa ukuba avuse ukulunga emhlabeni, athele azale ande ukuze kufike inzalo ethenjisiwe yowesifazane. UYahweh wathembisa ukuthi ngeke aphinde akhukhule umhlaba futhi naphezu kwemvelo embi yesintu, uNkulunkulu wasebenza nesintu ukuze avikele futhi abusise izizukulwane zokulunga emhlabeni ezavela kuzo mina, uYeshua HaMashiashi.

UNkulunkulu wababusisa ukuba bathele bazale bande, bagcwalise umhlaba, futhi uNkulunkulu wathembisa bobabili umuntu nesilwane ukuthi ngeke aphinde abhubhise yonke inyama emhlabeni ngamanzi futhi ngeke aphinde u-El Shaddai akhukhule umhlaba wonke kodwa uyolondoloza umhlaba, alondoloze isintu nenzalo yaso, futhi lona lona leli zwe lilondoloziwe kusukela ngalolo suku lwesivumelwano lapho uBaba wezibani abeka khona uthingo lwakhe emazulwini njengophawu nesikhumbuzo sesivumelwano sakhe noNowa. Ngoba lokhu bakushaya indiva ngamabomu, ukuthi ngezwi likaNkulunkulu amazulu ayekhona endulo, nomhlaba umi ngaphandle kwamanzi nasemanzini: Ngalokho izwe elalikhona ngaleso sikhathi, ligcwele amanzi, labhubha: Kodwa amazulu nomhlaba, akhona manje, ngalo lona lelo zwi agciniwe, abekelwe umlilo ngosuku lokwahlulelwa nokubhujiswa kwabantu abangamesabi uNkulunkulu.

Kanjalo uYahweh wamisa u-Abrama ukuba abe ngu-Abrahama, wathembisa u-Abrama ukuthi igama lakhe ngeke lisaba ngu-Abrama kodwa ngu-Abrahama nokuthi uzomenza uYise wezizwe eziningi nokuthi uzonika u-Abrahama nenzalo yakhe izwe laseKhanani libe yifa laphakade nokuthi uYahweh uzoba nguNkulunkulu wabo! Kanjalo u-Abrahama waba nguYise wezizwe eziningi, uYahweh amthembisa zona esivumelwaneni. Manje, ku-Abrahama nasenzalweni yakhe kwenziwa izithembiso. Akathi, Nasezinzalweni, njengokungathi ziningi; kodwa njengokungathi yinye, NaseNzalweni yakho, enguKristu. Kanjalo abalungileyo bayophila ngokukholwa!

Kanjalo abantwana bawela ekugqilazweni eGibhithe ngemuva kokuba abantwana besithembiso beye lapho ngaphansi kwesivikelo nokondliwa kukaJosefa ngokuba uJosefa wabusa ngokulunga ngaphansi kukaFaro owayemkhulu kunoJosefa esihlalweni sobukhosi, kodwa uJosefa wakhonza uMINA ENGINGUYE OMKHULU! Kwavela uFaro owayengamazi uJosefa, futhi lolu khula lwacindezela futhi lwazama ukubulala uhlu lozalo lukaKristu futhi, ngisho nabantwana besithembiso. Futhi uYahweh wavusa umkhululi ongumHebheru endlini kaFaro, yena lowo owakhishwa emanzini wasindiswa ngomusa kaYahweh. KwakunguMose uqobo lwakhe owayezohola abantwana besithembiso baphume ekugqilazweni baye enkululekweni ekhazimulayo yezwe lesithembiso, ngoba bangabantwana besithembiso futhi ngisazobavusa ebuthongweni babo futhi ngivuse abathembekile nabalungileyo kakhulu, ngisho nalabo abahlala bethembekile kuze kube sekupheleni. Kanjalo uMose wakhulula abantwana bakwa-Israyeli esandleni sabacindezeli babo futhi bonke ababaxosha baminza olwandle, ngoba ngubani ongamela u-El Shaddai?

Kanjalo uYahweh wahola uMose nabantwana besithembiso baya eNtabeni iSinayi lapho uYahweh anika khona izimiso, imiyalelo, nemithetho equkethwe emiyalweni kuMose ukuze ilalelwe yibo bonke abantwana. Futhi izibusiso neziqalekiso zamiswa ngenxa yokulalela nokungalaleli. Kwathi lapho uYahweh esenike uMose esivumelwaneni imiyalelo equkethwe emithethweni yokulalela, uMose wabe esesiqinisa isivumelwano saba yiTestamente ngokuchithwa kwegazi. UMose wathatha uhhafu wegazi, walifaka ezitsheni, nohhafu wegazi wawufafaza e-Altare. Wathatha incwadi yesivumelwano, wafunda ezindlebeni zabantu; base bethi, Konke iNkosi ekushilo, sizokwenza, futhi silalele. UMose wathatha igazi walifafaza phezu kwabantu, wathi, Bhekani igazi leSivumelwano iNkosi elenze nani, mayelana nawo wonke la mazwi. Ngaphezu kwalokho, wafafaza ngegazi iThabernakele, nazo zonke izitsha zeNkonzo. Futhi cishe zonke izinto ngoMthetho zihlanjululwa ngegazi; futhi ngaphandle kokuchithwa kwegazi akukho ukuthethelelwa kwezono.

Kanjalo umlando wenzeka njengoba kulotshiwe, futhi ngokuhamba kwesikhathi isizwe sakwa-Israyeli samiswa ngokugcwele, nokho kwakungakabi bikho indawo yokuhlala kaYahweh phakathi kwabantu. Futhi uYahweh wavusa iNkosi elungileyo, yebo, umuntu ongenhliziyo kaNkulunkulu! Ekuhambeni kwesikhathi uYahweh wathumela uNathani umProfethi kuDavide wamtshela ukuthi lapho izinsuku zakho sezigcwalisekile, futhi uyolala nokhokho bakho, ngiyomisa inzalo yakho emva kwakho, eyophuma ezibilinini zakho, ngimise umbuso wayo. Iyokwakhela igama lami indlu, ngimise isihlalo sobukhosi sombuso wayo kuze kube phakade. Ngiyoba nguyise, yona ibe yindodana yami. Indlu yakho nombuso wakho kuyomiswa phambi kwakho kuze kube phakade; isihlalo sakho sobukhosi siyomiswa kuze kube phakade. Ngokwawo wonke la mazwi, nangokwawo wonke lo mbono, wakhuluma kanjalo uNathani kuDavide.

Kanjalo isintu silandele uYahweh kusukela esivumelwaneni kuya esivumelwaneni, kwanezelwa esivumelwaneni esaqiniswa ngomhlatshelo wezilwane futhi isivumelwano sanezelwa kwiTestamente. Nokho, ngayo yonke imizamo emihle yomuntu akakaze akwazi ngamandla akhe ukufinyelela ekulungeni kukaNkulunkulu, ngisho nalowo mthetho olungileyo nolungileyo kaMose engiwuthandayo nengawugcwalisa. Uma kukhona obiza umthetho kaMose nezivumelwano eziningi zikaYahweh njengeziqalekisiwe, makaqalekiswe. Ngoba umthetho muhle ulungile futhi ungcwele futhi zonke izithembiso zikaYahweh zinguyebo no-amen. Nokho, umthetho wanikezwa njengomfundisi ukuze ubaholele kuKristu. Ngoba mina, uJesu waseNazaretha, ngingulowo uYahweh amnikele ukuba agcwalise lokho isintu esingenakukwazi ngenxa yesono esedluliselwa kusuka esizukulwaneni kuya esizukulwaneni kusukela egazini lika-Adamu, elonakele phakade. Ngoba lokho umthetho owawungenakukwenza njengoba wawubuthakathaka ngenxa yenyama: lokho uNkulunkulu wakwenza, wathumela indodana yakhe ifana nenyama enesono.

Ngakho-ke ekugcwalisekeni kwezikhathi mina, uJesu waseNazaretha, ngazalwa ngowesifazane ngokwezidingo zomthetho kaMose nasekugcwalisekeni kwezivumelwano, futhi ngiyinzalo ethenjisiwe yowesifazane eyabikezelwa nguYahweh uqobo eminyakeni eminingi edlule. Ngoba uNkulunkulu walithanda izwe kangaka waze wanikela ngeNdodana yakhe ezelwe yodwa, ukuze noma ngubani okholwa yiyo angabhubhi, kodwa abe nokuphila okuphakade. Ngakho-ke ngiyindodana ezelwe yodwa kaNkulunkulu, yebo, leyo nzalo yesithembiso abaprofethi abaprofetha ngayo kusukela endulo kuze kube manje. Futhi ngingulowo omiselwe ukugcwaliseka kwezinto zonke futhi kimi, uKristu Jesu, zonke izinto nazo zonke izithembiso ziyogcwaliseka, futhi ngeke kusale nesithembiso esisodwa esingagcwalisekanga, kodwa konke kuyogcwaliseka ingunaphakade.

Ngoba kulotshiwe kumaHebheru lapho ngenza khona uPawulu abhale: Ngoba uma igazi leZinkunzi, nelezimbuzi, nomlotha wesithokazi esifafazwa phezu kwabangcolileyo, lingcwelisa ekuhlanzweni kwenyama: Kakhulu kangakanani igazi likaKristu, owathi ngoMoya waphakade wazinikela engenabala kuNkulunkulu, liyohlanza unembeza wenu emisebenzini efile, ukuze nikhonze uNkulunkulu ophilayo? Ngenxa yalesi sizathu unguMlamuleli weTestamente Elisha, ukuze ngokufa, ngenxa yokuhlengwa kweziphambeko ezenziwa ngaphansi kweTestamente lokuqala, labo ababiziwe, bamukele isithembiso sefa laphakade.

Ngakho-ke qondani ukuthi kimi, uKristu Jesu, izithembiso zasendulo kanye nezivumelwano zasendulo kugciniwe futhi isivumelwano sakudala sigciniwe futhi sagcwaliseka, ngoba ngiyisigcwaliseko sazo esiphelele. Nokho zisekhona izithembiso ezisazogcwaliseka, futhi manje yizikhathi zokugcwaliseka. Ngoba zinjalo izikhathi, lapho ngizovusa khona ngokuzumayo ekulaleni labo abalele kuKristu, futhi ngizobutha ngokuzumayo labo abaphilayo nabasalayo ukuze bangihlangabeze emafwini, futhi ngokuzumayo siyohlanganyela egumbini lesihlalo sobukhosi likaYahweh uqobo lwakhe. Ngoba nina Bandla lami nomlobokazi wami niyisigcwaliseko sesithembiso sesivumelwano, futhi nizalwe kabusha ngembewu engenakonakala futhi nahlengwa ngezithembiso eziningi eziseTestamenteni lami Elisha, eliyisivumelwano esisha esihlanganisa zonke izithembiso zezivumelwano zasendulo, futhi esenezezela izithembiso ezinkulu kakhulu neziyigugu enihlengelwe ngazo ekuphileni okuphakade nasemvuzweni waphakade, futhi siyohlala sindawonye kuze kube phakade.

Qondani ukuthi izimpilo eniziphilile enyameni, ngisho nani enikhethiweyo bami abathembekile, lezo zimpilo sezifike esiphelweni ekuhlengweni kwenu okuphelele. Qondani ukuthi nihlonzwa ngokuphelele nami, uKristu Jesu, kuwo wonke amazinga. Ngenkathi ngibethelwa ngenxa yezono zenu, nani nabethelwa kanye nami. Ngenkathi ngifa kuleso sigxobo esinzima, nani nafa kanye nami. Ngenkathi ngivuswa ekufeni ngemva kwezinsuku ezintathu nobusuku obuthathu enhliziyweni yomhlaba, nani navuselwa ekuphileni okusha, futhi ngenkathi ngenyuselwa ezulwini lamazulu, nani nenyuselwa kanye nami, ngakho-ke nihlezi ezulwini. Ngoba ngiyiPhasika lenu eliphelele, futhi ngingumkhulumeli wenu owathatha indawo yenu ekwahlulelweni nasesijezisweni esanifanele. Futhi mina, Jesu waseNazaretha, nganihlengela ngokuzithandela esiqalekisweni somthetho ngoba ngenxa yenyama yenu enesono anisoze nakwazi ukuwufeza umthetho ngemizamo yenu siqu.

Qondani ukuthi angizanga ukuzochitha umthetho, noma izivumelwano, kodwa ukuzogcwalisa konke ezikhathini zokugcwaliseka. Uma kukhona obiza umthetho kaMose nezivumelwano eziningi zikaYahweh njengeziqalekisiwe, makabe oqalekisiweyo. Ngoba umthetho muhle, ulungile futhi ungcwele, futhi zonke izithembiso zikaYahweh zingu-yebo no-amen. Nokho, umthetho wanikezwa njengomfundisi wokubayisa kuKristu. Ngoba mina, Jesu waseNazaretha, ngingulowo uYahweh amnikele ukuba agcwalise lokho isintu esingakwazi ukukwenza ngenxa yesono esadluliselwa kusuka esizukulwaneni kuya kwesinye sisuka egazini lika-Adamu, elonakele kuze kube phakade. Ngoba lokho umthetho owawungenakukwenza ngenxa yokuba buthakathaka kwawo ngenxa yenyama: lokho kwenziwa uNkulunkulu, wathumela iNdodana yakhe ifana nenyama enesono.

Zonke izithembiso sezizofezeka ngokugcwele futhi zigcwaliseke kimi uKristu Jesu futhi ngizongena esivumelwaneni somshado noMlobokazi wami ukuze ngokubambisana babuse nami efeni lami, futhi bona, ngokuhola kwami benami, bayophulukisa umhlaba, baqhubeke babuse njengabapristi namakhosi emhlabeni, futhi zonke izivumelwano kusukela ku-Adamu kuya kuMose ziyogcwaliseka ebusweni bukaJesu Kristu. Lezi yizithembiso zokuphila okuphakade eziyogcwaliseka kwabalungileyo, kubo bonke labo abadalwa kabusha njengomuntu munye omusha kuKristu Jesu. Ngoba bonke bayodalwa kabusha ngokuphelele futhi bavuzwe ingunaphakade ngesabelo sabo, futhi konke kuyoba ngu-yebo no-amen. Ngoba wonke ama-yod, nawo wonke ameva ama-yod ayogcwaliseka ngokuphelele naphakade.

Qondani ukuthi niyithempeli likaNkulunkulu ophilayo, Bandla lami elithembekile, futhi nginidalile njengendlu eyenziwe ngaphandle kwezandla ukuze nibe yindawo yokuhlala yaphakade kaNkulunkulu waphakade, uYahweh uYihlo. Futhi ngizoshada nomlobokazi wami futhi ngihlale nabo kuze kube phakade esivumelwaneni saphakade, ukuthi lapho ngikhona, nabo bayoba khona, futhi kuze kube phakade bayofana nami, uKristu Jesu iNkosi yabo yaphakade. Futhi qondani nina abathembekile bami ukuthi izikhathi zokwehlukana seziseduze nokuphela futhi akusayikubakho ukwehlukana phakathi kukaYahweh namadodana akhe athandekayo, futhi akusayikubakho ukwehlukana phakathi kukaKristu Jesu iNkosi yenu yaphakade nalabo engibathanda kakhulu. Qondani ukuthi siyohamba njengomuntu munye futhi ngeke kube khona isono noma isithiyo. Niyokwenza imisebenzi eminingi emikhulu engakaze ingene ezinhliziyweni zabantu. Niyoba amakhosi ami anamandla nabapristi, mina engiyiNkosi yamakhosi noMbusi wababusi, futhi siyobusa umhlaba ngokulunga.

Futhi abaningi bayovuswa ekufeni ekupheleni kweviki lamashumi ayisikhombisa likaDaniyeli futhi bonke bayovuzwa ngokuvumelana nokuthembeka kwabo. Futhi bonke abafa bethembekile benethemba lokuthi iNkosi yabo noMesiya ngeke bashiye imiphefumulo yabo ethuneni, bayovukela ekuphileni okusha nasekubuyiselweni kwaphakade. Ngoba ngizobahlanganisa nabathandekayo babo, futhi ngizobuyisela kubo lokho okwadliwa yisikhonyane, futhi ngizobuyisela kubo lokho uSathane akweba kubo ngezikhathi beyizihambi emhlabeni belindele ukuhlangulwa kwabo okuphelele kulo mhlaba owileyo nomubi.

Futhi linjalo ivangeli laphakade, futhi kuze kube phakade zonke izinto ziyogcwaliseka ngokulunga, ubungcwele, neqiniso. Ngoba akukho okungcolile noma okubi, noma okuthanda nokwenza amanga okuyongena emasangweni ombuso wami, kodwa ukubusa kwami kuyoba ngeqiniso nokulunga kwaphakade. Ngakho yizwani izwi likaYeshua HaMashiach, nina nonke enithunyelelwe lawa mazwi. INkosi yenu yaphakade ayizikhohliwe izithembiso zasendulo, futhi azikho izivumelwano eziye zaba yize. Kodwa konke kuyogcwaliseka, futhi impela, kuningi osekugcwalisekile, ngakho bhekani kulezo zinto ukuze nibe nethemba. Ngoba njengoba kulotshiwe, kuyokwenziwa kanjalo, futhi linjalo ivangeli laphakade kulezi zikhathi zokugcwaliseka.

Mina, Yeshua HaMashiach, ngithumele la Mazwi ngenceku yami, futhi ngenceku yami la mazwi athunyelwe.

Ngakho ngininika ukuqonda ukuthi ukugcwaliseka kwazo zonke izinto kulapha futhi sekuvele kuqhubeka. Labo bomlobokazi wami nomzimba wami bayosebenza futhi baphile ukuphila okusha kanye nalabo abayovuswa ngokulunga ovukweni lwabalungileyo ngemva kweviki lamashumi ayisikhombisa likaDaniyeli. Futhi ngokulunga nokuthula siyophila ngokuzwana nendalo kaNkulunkulu futhi kuyoba nokuthula okuhlala njalo nokwaphakade kubantwana besithembiso, futhi bayokhonza omunye nomunye, futhi bayongikhonza, futhi bayokhonza uYahweh, futhi ngokuthula nokuzwana indalo kaYahweh iyobuyiselwa.

Ngoba kusekhona ukuphumula kwabantu bakaNkulunkulu. Ngakho njengoba inkathi yami yomusa isondela esiphelweni, inkathi yokugcina isisondele bantabami, lolo suku lokugcina lapho sonke siyophumula khona emisebenzini yethu nasezinkingeni zethu, futhi siyojabulela ukunotha kwezwe njengoba siphumula osukwini lokuphumula, yebo kuyoba nosuku lweSabatha lokuzwana. Kube nezinsuku eziyisithupha zomsebenzi, ngoba usuku olulodwa eNkosini lunjengeminyaka eyinkulungwane, neminyaka eyinkulungwane injengosuku olulodwa. Ngakho-ke, ekubuseni kwami kweminyaka eyinkulungwane abathembekile bayochitha usuku lokuphumula noMdali wabo, uYise, noNkulunkulu waphakade ngaphambi kokuba uYahweh adale izulu elisha nomhlaba omusha lapho kuyohlala khona kuphela ukulunga kwaphakade. Bhekani, izikhathi sezingamanje, ngakho-ke lungani!
