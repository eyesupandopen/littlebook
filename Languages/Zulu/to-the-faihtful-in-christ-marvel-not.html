Kwabakholwayo KuKristu: Ningamangali!

Ningamangali, bazalwane bami, ningamangali, noma leli zwe linizonda. Yazini ukuthi izwe langizonda mina ngaphambi kokuba lizonde nina, zithandwa zami, futhi inceku ayinkulu kunenkosi yayo. Ngakho-ke, qondisisani izikhathi eniphila kuzo futhi nisondele nakakhulu ezinhliziyweni zenu kuYeshua HaMashiach, ngoba nginibizela eduze kwami bandla lami, umzimba wami, ngisho nomakoti wami, futhi nginibizela eduze kakhulu kulezi zikhathi ngaphambi nje kokuba niyiswe emazulwini ukuze ninqobe ngokugcwele zonke izinto kuKristu.

Futhi-ke ngokuzuma nangokungalindelekile manje niyothathwa niyiswe emazulwini, ngoba ngiza ngehora eningalilindele. Bhekani! Ngiza njengesela ebusuku! Kodwa njengoba kwakunjalo ezinsukwini zikaNowa, kuyokuba njalo nokufika kweNdodana yomuntu. Ngoba njengasezinsukwini zangaphambi kukazamcolo, babedla bephuza, beganwa, futhi bendisa, kwaze kwaba usuku uNowa angena ngalo emkhunjini, bengazi lutho, kwaze kwafika uzamcolo, wabakhukhula bonke, kuyokuba njalo nokufika kweNdodana yomuntu.

Ngakho-ke nginelulekile, zithandwa zami, ukuba nisuke ezinkathazweni zaleli zwe, nazi ukuthi ukusuka kwenu kuleli zwe elibi sekusondele. Nokho, kusekhona ukuphila okufanele nikuphile, futhi kufanele niqhubeke nize ningabikho, ngoba lapho nithathwa niyiswa emazulwini ngokungalindelekile ngesandla sami esinamandla sensindiso, izifundo zenu ziyobe ziphelile nempilo yenu emhlabeni iphelele ngaleso sikhathi. Ngakho-ke ngiyabeluleka, bantwanyana bami, philani izimpilo zenu ngokulalela okuphelele izwi leNkosi noMsindisi wenu, uKristu Jesu iNkosi yenu eyenyuka, nami ngiyonenza ninqobe ngokugcwele!

Ngakho-ke, njengoba niphila izimpilo zenu, nikeleleni konke kimi ngokuzinikela kwezinhliziyo zenu nogqozi ngoba ngingukwanela kwenu ezintweni zonke. Ningacabangi ukuthi ezinhliziyweni zenu niphumelele futhi nichumile ngamandla ezandla zenu, noma ngeziqu zasemhlabeni eziningi zenu ezizazisa ngokobuwula ngaphezu kokwazi uYahweh, amanga enathengiselwa wona njengeqiniso emfundweni yenu yobuxoki eyakhelwe ngokuphelele phezu kwamanga, nokho niziqhayisa ngokobuwula ngamakhono enu eniwazuze ngayo. Futhi ngakho-ke ngiphinda nginitshela, phumani phakathi kwabo futhi nihlukane nabo ezintweni zonke, noma niyohlanganyela ekwahlulelweni nasezinhluphekweni zabo lapho into enjalo ingadingeki, ngoba ngazo zonke izikhathi zamanje ngiyanibiza futhi ngiyanincenga ukuba nize ngokugcwele kimi othandweni lwami ngani bantwana bami. Ngakho-ke sukani ekuziqhenyeni okuyize okulethwa ulwazi!

Ngalokho, nginika ukuqonda ukuthi noma yini eniyizuzile kule mpilo ephathelene nempahla yaleli zwe inikezwe ngokuthokoza nangomusa nangesihe sikaYahweh, uMINA ENGINGUYE OMKHULU, ofisa ngaphezu kwakho konke ukuba nichume futhi nibe nempilo njengoba umphefumulo wenu uchuma. Nokho, ningabi yiziwula kangako ukucabanga ukuthi nizenzile noma ukuthi ekudunyisweni kwenu kwasemhlabeni nimukelekile phambi kukaYahweh, uMINA ENGINGUYE OMKHULU noma uJesu Kristu, ngoba ayikho inyama elungisiswayo emehlweni ethu. Ngakho-ke qondani ukuthi labo abaziphakamisayo ngaleyo ndlela bayohlupheka ukulahlekelwa okukhulu ngenxa yokuziqhenya kwabo, okwandulela ngempela incithakalo enkulu.

Ngenxa yenu, ngiyanincenga ukuba niphume kuleli zwe nakuzo zonke izimo zalo ezingamesabi uNkulunkulu zokuzilungisa, ukuzenzela, ukuzithethelela kwamanga okungamesabi uNkulunkulu nokuziqhayisa kwenyama. Ngoba, ngiqinisile, ngiqinisile, ukuba umusa kaYahweh, uMINA ENGINGUYE OMKHULU, wawungathululwanga othandweni lwakhe olungunaphakade ngani, lapho uthando lwakhe olungunaphakade nokukhanya kukhanye ezinhliziyweni nasezimpilweni zenu ebusweni bukaJesu Kristu: nonke beniyoba njengeSodoma neGomora futhi beningeke nibe nampande nagatsha, ngakho-ke ukuziqhayisa kwenu makube kuKristu. Nokho, leyo nhlanhla akuyona eyenu zithandwa zami, ngakho-ke, othandweni lwenu ngami lalelani izwi lami ukuze ninqobe zonke izinto ngoba uma nilalela izwi lami, ngizonenza ninqobe!

Kunjalo, futhi kuyoba njalo, ukuthi kwabanqobi bami kunomvuzo omkhulu, ngisho nakulabo abanqobile ngendlela efanayo nami, futhi umvuzo wabo uyoba mkhulu kunayo yonke. Nokho, abaningi babakholwayo bami kuKristu bayalingwa futhi badukiswe, njengokungathi babuyela kwabafileyo! Ngisho naphambi kwemiyalo yami enganikeza uPawulu eminyakeni eminingi yamaJubili edlule encwadini yakhe yabase-Efesu, wabhala kanjalo, futhi nami ngiyanikhumbuza zithandwa zami: Ngakho-ke uthi, vuka wena oleleyo, usukume kwabafileyo, uKristu uzokukhanyisela. Qaphelisisani-ke ukuthi nihamba ngokucophelela, kungabi njengeziwula, kodwa njengabahlakaniphileyo, nisebenzise kahle isikhathi, ngokuba izinsuku zimbi. Ngalokho, ningabi ngabangenangqondo, kodwa qondani ukuthi iyini intando yeNkosi.

Niyayiqonda yini intando yeNkosi yenu, bantwanyana bami? Ingabe yikona enikugxilile? Noma nizikhonza nina ezinhliziyweni zenu ngokuzinikela, nifuna ingcebo eyengeziwe enqolobaneni yenu usuku nosuku? Ingabe nizihlanganise izinhliziyo zenu nokukhonza izinhlangano, noma izinkampani zabantu abanesono ngenxa yenzuzo engcolile, ngisho nangesikhathi lapho imali uqobo amadoda ayiqongelelayo futhi ayazisa ngokona maduze izomenyezelwa njengengenamsebenzi phambi kwamehlo awo? Noma niqhubekile nokulalela izwi lami futhi nicabangisise ngeZwi ngalinye engininika lona ukuze nisindiswe? Uma niqhubeka nilalela izwi lami ngizonenza ninqobe zonke izinto, ngakho-ke, ningadangali!

Ngisanincenga, bonke abakholwayo kuKristu nabo bonke abasazokholwa kuKristu ukuba niqhubeke niphendukela kimi ngoba nginimemela eduze kakhulu nami ukuze ukusondelana okugcwele kothando lwami ngani kunimatise futhi kunisekele ezintweni zonke, ngakho-ke, ningesabi, futhi ningachithi isikhathi ngokuzisola. Ngakho-ke, qhubekani nishisekela othandweni nasekuzinikeleni kwezinhliziyo zenu eNkosini yenu uJesu Kristu, bantwanyana bami, ngoba asisekho isikhathi sokumosha! Futhi lapho kukhala icilongo niyoba phakathi kwezintombi eziyiziwula ezishiyelwa emlilweni wokucwenga, noma niyofunyanwa phakathi kwezintombi ezihlakaniphile eziyothathwa ziyiswe emazulwini nomkhwenyana wazo noma nini manje. Niyoba ngubani futhi niyoma kuphi lapho ngivela ngokuzuma noma nini manje?

Nisazongivumela yini ukuba nginikeze amafutha ezibani zenu ukuze nime esihlalweni sami sokwahlulela? Nisazozilungisa kahle yini izintambo zenu ngokulalela imiyalo yami? Noma niyokhetha ngobuwula ukuhlala ningukwanela kwenu, nimi ekulungeni kwenu enizizuzele kona, futhi nihlale nimi phakathi kwezintombi eziyiziwula? Ngisho nalapho nginihlelele okungcono, uma nilalela ngokugcwele izwi lami. Ngakho-ke, ngiyabeluleka abakholwayo bami, qhubekani niza kimi ngalo lonke uthando nokulalela ukuze nginiphakamise kakhulu niye emazulwini aphezulu kakhulu ukuze nikhanye njengezinkanyezi zomkhathi ngenxa yalezo zinto enizenzile ngokulalela nikhonza iNkosi yenu yaphakade Yokulunga!

Ngakho, ngibuza nonke enilaziyo izwi lami, nani enibizwe ngegama, nenibiza igama lami: Ingabe niphapheme ngokugcwele? Ingabe amehlo enu avuleke ngokugcwele ezweni njengoba linjalo namuhla? Qondani ukuthi izwe eniphila kulo manje alifani nelalikhona ngisho nezinyanga eziyisithupha ezedlule, futhi umhlaba uyaqhubeka nokushintsha kakhulu phambi kwamehlo enu, nakuba abaningi benu besazichitha izibonakaliso nezimangaliso ngokungakholwa okungenalukholo befuna ukubuyela ekulaleni. Ngakho-ke, zihloleni nina nezinhlelo zenu neNkosi yenu yaphakade ukuze ngiqondise izinyathelo zenu futhi nginivikele ebubini othandweni nasemuseni wami.

Kunjalo, amanga kadrako manje agcwele umhlaba ngaphezu kwalokho ayesevele enjalo ngaphambi kokuba udrako aphonswe phansi; ngoba udrako uphonswe phansi, uphonswe emhlabeni futhi uboshelwe ngaphansi komkhathi ukuze ahlulelwe, futhi uyazi ukuthi unesikhathi esifushane futhi intukuthelo yakhe inkulu! Futhi uyeza ukuzolwa nezimpi zakhe zizoba zinkulu, kodwa leyo nyoka endala ayisoze yaba nobukhulu ngaphandle kwesibabule esivuthayo nesigingqikayo esivutha amalangabi ashisayo esijeziso.

Futhi-ke ngiza kini zithandwa zami ukunikhuthaza ukuba niqhubeke nisondele enhliziyweni yami ngokulalela kwenu amaZwi ami, futhi ezindongeni zenhliziyo yami amagama enu aphelele aqoshwe othandweni lwami ngani lapho, olungunaphakade nolwaphakade. Yebo, othandweni lwami ngani, (ngazi ngamunye wenu kangcono kunokuba nizazi nina) nginibambe eduze kakhulu nenhliziyo yami ngaso sonke isikhathi ikakhulukazi ezikhathini zenu zokucindezeleka nezilingo enibhekana nazo. (Ngiqoqe yonke inyembezi yenu ebhodleleni lami lezinyembezi ukuze ngininxephezele ngayinye yazo) Nasezimpini ezishubile kule mpi yenzalo eqhubekayo, enqotshwa kuKristu futhi eshubile ngokumelene nabawileyo; qondani, ukuthi izimpi zenu zinqotshwe ngokugcwele eNkosini yenu yaphakade! Ngakho-ke, phumulani eNkosini yenu yaphakade, uKristu Jesu, futhi niyeke imisebenzi yenu eyize bese niphumula ngokugcwele lapho, futhi niqedele imisebenzi yokulunga engininike yona ukuze ninqobe izitha zenu nami ngizoninika iSabatha! Selah!

Ngakho-ke, qondani izinsuku eniphila kuzo ngoba akekho noyedwa kini owake waphila lezi zinsuku esezifike emhlabeni kulesi sikhathi sokugcina, nakuba zibukeka ngokukhohlisa zifana nalokho enikwaziyo. Ngoba umhlaba useshintshweni futhi ngempela umhlaba uyashintshwa, futhi ngiqinisile, ngiqinisile umhlaba uyoqhubeka nokushintsha ngamandla ngokwezinjongo nemiklamo kaYahweh.

Qondani ukuthi isikhathi soguquko olukhulu sesifikile emhlabeni, isikhathi lapho umhlaba uhlelwa kabusha futhi uhlelwa kabusha ngezehlakalo nezinhlekelele nezahlulelo ezizayo ukuze kube nokubuyiselwa komhlaba owawukhona ezinsukwini zikaNowa! Futhi izenzakalo nezahlulelo ziyokwenzeka njengoba kwabikezelwa abaprofethi basendulo ukuthi kuzokwenzeka ngokugcwele ngesikhathi sosizi lukaJakobe. Ngalokho qondani, ukuthi izahlulelo eziningi seziqalile ukuwela ngisho nakulesi sikhathi soguquko ngoba bekungeke kwenzeke ukuthi izinto zenzeke nganoma iyiphi enye indlela.

Futhi-ke kulotshiwe, futhi kuyokwenziwa kanjalo, lapho ngiqoqa abakholwayo kuKristu maduze nangokungalindelekile manje lapho umvimbeli ezosuswa, neqhwa liwe, nezimpawu zivulwe, namacilongo akhale! Ukuze kuvuke ofakazi bami ababili abayoyala umlilo ukuba wehle uvela emazulwini, abayoshaya umhlaba ngazo zonke izinhlobo zezinhlupho kaningi ngendlela abathanda ngayo, nabayobangela ukuba lingani phezu komhlaba ngezinsuku zokuprofetha kwabo ngomyalo wabo, kodwa abaningi bayokwenqaba ukuphenduka! Qondani ukuthi bayomelana nodrako, nesilo nomprofethi wamanga futhi uma intando yanoma yimuphi umuntu iwukubalimaza, lowo muntu kufanele alinyazwe ngendlela efanayo. Uma intando yanoma yimuphi umuntu iwukubabulala, lowo muntu kufanele abulawe ngendlela efanayo. Lezi yizinti zami ezimbili zezibani nemithi yami emibili yomnqumo futhi izinto eziningi, eziningi bayozembula ngezikhathi zokwembulwa kwabo futhi ubufakazi babo buyoqoshwa phakade naphakade.

Lezi yizikhathi eniphila kuzo, bantwanyana bami, futhi lezi yizikhathi okufanele niqhubeke nizibekezelela ngokusondela kakhulu entandweni yami ngezimpilo zenu ngoba kulapho ukuphepha nensindiso yenu kukhona. Qondani, ukuthi nakhula njengamathole esibayeni ngezikhathi zami zomusa nesihe ezanikezelwa ukusindisa izizwe ezilahlekile ezazihlakazekile phakathi kwezizwe, futhi ngaleyo ndlela, izizwe zabusiswa futhi zathola uthando, ububele nomusa futhi abaningi bathola insindiso ngakho. Kodwa manje izikhathi sezishintshe kakhulu, futhi ngiyaninika umyalo wokuba nihambe ngokuhlakanipha OKUKHULU, futhi nibe nobuqili njengezinyoka nibe mnene njengamajuba ngoba lezi zikhathi ziyingozi ngokukhohlisa.

Ngoba udrako manje uboshelwe emhlabeni nabawileyo bayaqina umzuzwana nomzuzwana, nokho abanawo amandla okulwa nezwi lami elinamandla namandla amakhulu! Ngakho-ke, ngiyabakhuza futhi ngibavimbele ngenxa yenu ngaphezu kwalokho eningakuqonda ngalesi sikhathi futhi ngibeke zonke izinto zombuso wesitha ngaphansi kwezinyawo zenu ukuze nikwazi ukuzinyathela uma kunesidingo. Ngakho-ke, ningabesabi ngoba nginqobile zonke izinto, ngisho nombuso wonke kaSathane; futhi nani niyokunqoba ngendlela efanayo, uma nihlala nithembekile emazwini ami emiyalo engiyinikezile nengiyinikezayo ukuze nilalele.

Ningamangali, zithandwa zami, ngisho nalapho abawileyo bezembula emhlabeni, ningamangali nakancane! Ngoba izinsuku zikaNowa sezifikile! Ngisho nezinsuku lapho oSathane nezingelosi zabo bephonswa emhlabeni, bengaphinde babuyele emazulwini lapho manje bexoshwe khona phakade ekunqotshweni okuphelele. Ngisho nezinsuku lapho abalindi manje bekhululwa emajele abo lapho babeboshiwe futhi behlukanisiwe bodwa ekuhlushweni okukhulu ngamacala abo anengekayo abawenza kuYahweh nendalo yakhe ezinsukwini zasendulo futhi ekunqotshweni okuphelele babeboshiwe ekuhlubukeni kwabo nasezifungweni abazifunga ukuba bazifeze. Nokho, izifungo zabo ziyogcwaliseka phakade lapho beboshiwe futhi bephonswa emlilweni wesijeziso kulelo chibi elikhulu lesibabule esivuthayo lapho impethu yabo ingafi khona.

Ngisho nezinsuku, futhi, ezibuyayo njengasezinsukwini zikaNowa lapho abalindi behlela eNtabeni iHermoni ezinsukwini zikaJarede. Ngisho nezinsuku esezibuyile lapho abawileyo manje besebenza ngokubambisana namakhosi omhlaba nababusi bawo basemhlabeni abacasha ngemuva kwabo abahlanganyela emiklamweni yabo ehlose ukuletha incithakalo enkulu nenhlekelele nempi ngokushesha nje lapho umvimbeli esusiwe, lapho ububi babo buyokhululwa ngokugcwele. Yebo, bakhululiwe emhlabeni, futhi lapho umvimbeli esusiwe umbuso kaSathane ogcwele uyokhululwa lapho kuwa iqhwa, kwalasha kuvulwa, namasango abo ayophila futhi. Bhekani! Ngiletha imidondoshiya ukuba igcwalise ulaka lwami!

Ningamangali, zithandwa zami, ngisho nalapho abawileyo bekhanyisa izulu ngaphansi komkhathi ngezibani zabo, nama-orb, njengoba manje bezibonakalisa ezimweni zabo abazithathayo futhi imishini yabo manje isikhululekile ukundiza futhi inkohliso enkulu nokudideka bakubangele ebusweni bomhlaba ngobuchwepheshe babo, ngisho nangesikhathi sokuphela kwemihelo, ezizophela ngokuzuma nangokungalindelekile enhlekeleleni maduzane manje. Futhi-ke umuntu wemvelo uyamangala futhi ucabange futhi achithe okungaphezu kwemvelo manje esekuyimvelo futhi.

Yebo, isintu ngokungenalukholo sichitha amandla kaYahweh aboniswa ngenhloso yokuthi isintu sivukele ekulungeni futhi sithole insindiso. Esikhundleni sokunika uYahweh udumo ngomsebenzi wezandla zakhe nezibonakaliso ezihloselwe ukubaxwayisa ngokwahlulela okuzayo; isintu esikhundleni salokho sincoma isintu nobuchwepheshe baso obungenamsebenzi ebasithola kwabawileyo: ngakho-ke kuza isahlulelo esinzima nesibi! Ningabi nandima nasabelo ekucabangeni kwabo okungenalukholo futhi niqhubeke nokudumisa uYahweh ngemisebenzi yakhe yensindiso enikezwe ezinhlelweni zakhe zaphakade zokusindisa u-Adamu ebusweni bukaJesu Kristu, ongu-Adamu wokugcina.

Ngakho-ke ngiyanazisa: noma ngabe yisiphi isidalwa esidala izinguquko ezulwini noma emhlabeni, noma izinguquko esimweni somkhathi womhlaba, noma ngubani nanoma ngubani oguqula indalo kaYahweh nganoma iyiphi indlela: Ngiyanilaya ukuba niqonde ngokugcwele ukuthi uYahweh unobukhosi obuphelele phezu kwendalo yakhe futhi noma yini eyenzekayo yenzeka kuphela ngemvume kaYahweh, nangolwazi lwakhe olugcwele, futhi nokugcwalisa amacebo akhe aphakade okusindisa indodana yakhe yolahleko u-Adamu. Qondani, ukuthi uYahweh uvumela abangalungile, abanenhliziyo elukhuni, nabangalaleli ukuba baphile izimpilo zabo zesono ukuze bagcwalise izono zabo ngokugcwele futhi ukwahlulelwa kwabo okuzayo ngeke bakubalekele, ngaphandle kokuba beze kuJesu uKristu ngokuphenduka okuphelele kusenethuba.

Noma kunjalo, uYahweh uvumela abangalungile ukuba bone ukuze ekwahlulelweni bathole umvuzo ogcwele ngokwamukela ngokugcwele izenzo zabo ezimbi zibuyele emakhanda abo kuze kube phakade naphakade emalangabini esijeziso, ngisho noma into enjalo ingadingeki futhi ingagwenywa ensindisweni ngokulalela ukulunga. Ngomusa kaYahweh, nesihe sakhe ecebweni lakhe laphakade lokusindisa u-Adamu; uYahweh ufisa ukuthi kungalahleki muntu emalangabini, nokho, bonke abantu banikwe inkululeko yokuzikhethela ukukhetha okubi, noma ukukhetha ukulunga ebusweni bukaJesu Kristu ozodala kabusha bonke abeza ensindisweni njengomuntu munye omusha, futhi bayophila phakade naphakade.

Futhi kanjalo abawiweyo bayokhululwa ngokugcwele ekwahlulelweni phezu kwabakhileyo emhlabeni abangalaleli ngokwenqaba igama likaYeshua HaMashiach onguJesu uKristu! Ngakho-ke, qondani ukuthi bonke abenqaba uJesu uKristu njengeNkosi, noma abaphila ngokungathembeki ngokungalaleli imiyalo yami bayosala ngaphansi kokucindezelwa nonya lokubusa kwabo lapho behlose khona ukusula u-Adamu ebusweni bomhlaba ngonya nangokuphelele; emacebweni abo asukela kudala lapho behlose khona ukuntshontsha umhlaba ezandleni zami ezinamandla, imbala amacebo angenakwenzeka ukuba bawafinyelele ebuhlanyini babo bokuhlubuka.

Nokho, isintu siqhubeka sihamba ngenhloso siqonde ngqo ogibeni lwabo, ngisho nalapho sichaza ngezibonakaliso esiziphiwe zokwahlulelwa kwakhe okuzayo asalokhu enqaba ukukholelwa ukuthi kuyeza ekucabangeni kwakhe kokungakholwa okunikezwa yimfundo yakhe yamanga lapho ingqondo yakhe iphuphuthekiswe khona ngokuphelele emhlabeni weqiniso ahlala kuwo, wona lowo mhlaba owadalwa nguYahweh uMina Omkhulu Enginguye, omkhona isintu esifuna ukuwuphika emfundweni yaso yobuJesuit yamanga yokufunda okuphikisana nokufunda. Uma nginitshela izinto zasemhlabeni ningakholwa, niyokholwa kanjani, uma ngiyonitshela ngezinto zasezulwini? Ngokuba akekho owenyukela ezulwini, ngaphandle kwalowo owehla evela ezulwini, yona leyo Ndodana yomuntu eseZulwini. Niyozithoba yini izinhliziyo zenu nikholwe amaZwi eNdodana yomuntu? Noma niyonamathela ezithombeni zezinhliziyo zenu nasemangeni ezihlakaniphi ezifundile ezikhuluma ngokuphikisana ngqo namaZwi anikezwe nguYahweh ebusweni bukaJesu Kristu?

Ekucabangeni kwesintu esingenakukholwa sifuna ngobuwula ukunikeza izimpendulo zamanga ezibonakalisweni nezimangaliso zamanga zabawiweyo. Ngisho nezimpendulo ezinikezwa ngabawiweyo uqobo ngezinye izikhathi kubo bonke abenqaba amaZwi ka-Adonai Yahweh, awo lawo maZwi anikezelwe ukulalelwa ngesandla sendlela yeqiniso nephilayo, yona leyo ndlela enguKristu Jesu ngokuqinisekile.
