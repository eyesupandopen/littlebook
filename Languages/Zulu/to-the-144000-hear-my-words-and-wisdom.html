Meyi 25, 2024

Kwabayizi-144,000: Yizwani Amazwi Ami Nokuhlakanipha!

Zwanini amazwi ami nibeke indlebe ekuhlakanipheni kwami, ngokuba mina, iNkosi uJesu Kristu, ngizokhuluma nani uma nizolalela amazwi ami. Ngokuba ngizobeka uphawu kwabayizi-144,000 bami; anifundanga yini na? Futhi ngabona enye ingelosi yenyuka ivela eMpumalanga, inophawu lukaNkulunkulu ophilayo: yamemeza ngezwi elikhulu ezingelosini ezine ezazinikwe amandla okulimaza umhlaba nolwandle, yathi, ningoni umhlaba, noma ulwandle, noma imithi, size sibeke uphawu ezincekwini zikaNkulunkulu wethu emabunzini azo. Ngakho-ke ngizothuma labo engizobeka kubo uphawu, niyongenzela imisebenzi emikhulu, futhi nizohamba ngamandla amakhulu. Kodwa lokhu ngeke kube njengenkathi yomusa, lapho abashumayeli emapulpiti babefundisa ngemibhalo engcwele. (Abanye bakhuluma amanga kanti abanye bangenzela imisebenzi emikhulu emabandleni abo.) Inkathi yebandla iyaphela, futhi inkathi yami yomusa isicishe yaphela, ngakho-ke izikhathi zokuvivinywa zizoqala, ngisho nosizi lukaJakobe.

Ngakho-ke ngizokuthuma ukuba weluse umhlambi wami oyohlakazwa kakhulu futhi uvivinywe yizinhlekelele zilandelana. Ngokuba izinsuku ziyoba zimbi, futhi niyokhanya njengezibani nime njengezinsika phakathi kwabahlakazekile. Ngalokho nginithuma ukuba nithande umakhelwane wenu nimnakekele. Ngokuba nina enithembekile obizweni lwami nizophila ngomthetho engiwunikile, hhayi umthetho kaMose. Ngokuba mina, uYeshua HaMashiach, ngiwufezile umthetho kaMose, futhi iTorah igcinwa ngokukholwa kimi, ngokuba olungileyo uyophila ngokukholwa.

Kuyokuba kanje: Thanda iNkosi uNkulunkulu wakho ngenhliziyo yakho yonke, nangomphefumulo wakho wonke, nangengqondo yakho yonke, nangamandla akho onke, futhi uthande umakhelwane wakho njengalokhu uzithanda wena. Ngokuba ngokwenza lezi zinto ngokuqinisekile uyosindisa umphefumulo wakho ekubhujisweni kanye nalabo engikuthume kubo. Lezi akuzona izikhathi zokuphila isikhathi eside nokuchuma, ngokuba lezo zinsuku zidlulile emhlabeni ngize ngibuye ngizobusa umhlaba ngokulunga. Lowo ongathandi ukuphila kwakhe kuze kube sekupheleni uyosindiswa aphile phakade.

Niphila ezikhathini zempi, nolaka olukhulu, nokwambulwa okukhulu kwazo zonke izinto. Ngokuba uNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade, uYahweh uqobo, ngeke esazifihla emhlabeni; ngokuba uyogqashuka endaweni yakhe yokuhlala, futhi uthuma mina, uYeshua HaMashiach uqobo, ukuba ngifeze ulaka lwakhe futhi ngifeze izithembiso azenza koBaba. Kodwa kuqala, ukwahlulela kuyokwehlela, nolaka olukhulu luyokwehlela umhlaba, futhi ngeke nisakwazi ukuhlanganyela emphakathini wabo. Ngokuba ukuhlanganyela emphakathini wabo oyovuka emlotheni kuyodinga ukuba wonke umuntu amukele uphawu lwabo. Uma nihlanganyela nabo, niyohlanganyela nabo ekulahlweni kwabo kwaphakade kuleso sibhakabhaka somlilo esikhulu, futhi leso sibhakabhaka salungiselelwa uNkulunkulu uDeveli nezingelosi zakhe, ngakho-ke ningahlanganyeli nabo.

Aniyikuthola ukuthula emhlabeni wabo ovelayo kodwa ububi obungenazithiyo bodwa, ngokuba ngiyobe ngisusile umvimbeli. Futhi uma ningeyona ingxenye yengqondo yabo eyisidleke, khona-ke ngeke nibe nanhlangano nabo, ngokuba bayobulala bonke abaphikisayo. Ngokuba uphawu luyobaguqula phakade labo abalamukelayo ngokushintsha i-DNA yabo uqobo, ngokuba isintu senziwa ngomfanekiso kaYahweh, nakuba uSathane efuna ukuguqula lowo mfanekiso ngokuhlambalaza okungenakuhlehliswa ngemithi yakhe ehutshwa ngamanga amakhulu. Bayonithembisa ukuphila isikhathi eside, ukuthula, nokuchuma, kodwa lawo manga ayisingibane nje. Ngokuba uma uphawu selwamukelwe, akukho lutho oluyolungisa umonakalo, ngokuba uYahweh ushilo kanjalo!

Ngokuba inceku ayinkulu kunenkosi yayo, futhi nginibizela emisebenzini lapho ngizonihola khona isinyathelo ngesinyathelo, futhi njengasezinsukwini zikaNowa, kuyoba njalo. Ngalokho mina, uYeshua HaMashiach, ngafeza injongo yami njengewundlu likaNkulunkulu, ngokuba nganikela ngegazi lami elingenasono kuYahweh ngokwentando yami ekhululekile nangenxa yothando oluphelele oluvutha enhliziyweni yami.

Ngenxa yentokozo eyayibekwe phambi kwami, ngakhuthazelela usizi olukhulu, ukuhlukunyezwa, nosizi, (ngisho ngokungafanele) ngoba ngathatha yonke imiphumela yazo zonke izono zesintu phezu kwami. Futhi ngakhokhela sonke isono, ngokugcwele, futhi ngakwenza ngokuzithandela. Ngakho bhekisisani kimi, uJesu, umqalisi nomphelelisi wokukholwa kwenu, okwathi ngenxa yentokozo eyayibekwe phambi kwakhe, wakhuthazelela isiphambano, walidelela ihlazo, futhi uhlezi ngakwesokunene sesihlalo sobukhosi sikaNkulunkulu. Ngakho landelani isibonelo sami ekuphumeleleni okukhulu, ngokuba ngisho nasehlane ngiyokwenza indlela yenu ibe sobala.

Ngakho-ke, qondani, akuyikubakho ukuthula, futhi nginithuma ukuba nikhonze esikhathini sempi enkulu nosizi. Ngokuba udrako uyokulwa nabangcwele abanqobe, ngokuba unikiwe amandla okwenza lokho. Futhi labo abakhuleka egameni leNkosi bayosindiswa, ngokuba labo abangathandi ukuphila kwabo kuze kube sekupheleni bayokuthola ukuphila kwabo phakade kimi, uKristu Jesu. Ngokuba ngihlawulele izono zesintu ngegazi lami elingenasono, futhi ngingumpristi ophakeme phakade ngokohlelo lukaMelkisedeki, owanikela ngegazi lami elingenasono kuYahweh kanye kuphela. Ngakho-ke, ningabuyeli ezindleleni ezindala, namasiko azo, neTalmud yazo, neTorah; ngokuba iTorah igcinwa ngokugcwele ngokukholwa kimi, uJesu Kristu.

Ngakho-ke ngezinhlupheko ezinkulu, ngobuhlungu obukhulu, nangemizabalazo emikhulu, abangcwele bami bayokhanya, futhi umvuzo wabo uyoba mkhulu, ngokuba umvuzo wami mkhulu. Ngokuba sithini isithembiso: Laba yibo abaphuma osizini olukhulu, bahlanza izingubo zabo, bazenza zaba mhlophe ngegazi leWundlu. Ngalokho, baphambi kwesihlalo sobukhosi sikaNkulunkulu, futhi bamkhonza imini nobusuku eThempelini lakhe: futhi lowo ohlezi esihlalweni sobukhosi uyohlala phakathi kwabo. Abasayikulamba, abasayikoma, nelanga alisayikushaya phezu kwabo, namakhaza. Ngokuba iWundlu, eliphakathi kwesihlalo sobukhosi, liyobondla, libaholele emithonjeni yamanzi aphilayo: futhi uNkulunkulu uyokwesula zonke izinyembezi emehlweni abo.

Ngalokho ngizothuma labo engibabizile. Ngiyanibiza ukuba nithande uNkulunkulu ngenhliziyo yenu yonke, nangomphefumulo, nangengqondo, nangamandla, futhi nginithuma ukuba nithande umakhelwane wenu njengalokhu nizithanda nina. Ngokuba ukusinda kuyovela emazwini anika ukuphila uBaba wami awanikele ukuze alalelwe yibo bonke. Ngalokho thandani umakhelwane wenu, nakekelanani, sizanani futhi nisekelane; ngokuba abantu bami bayohlakazeka. Bayohlangana emiphakathini ehlane, futhi nakuba ihlane lingeke liphephe, ngiyohlinzeka ngesiphephelo kubantu bami futhi ngibahlinzeke ngezidingo zabo.

Ngalokho ngizothuma labo engibabizile. Nginithuma ukuba niyale okungenzeki njengoba nihamba othandweni nasemandleni amakhulu, ngisho namandla engizonembathisa wona. Ngokuba niyohamba ngenxa yami isinyathelo ngesinyathelo izikhathi eziningi ngokulalela, njengoba nethembele kimi, uKristu Jesu, oyokunihola ngomoya wami; futhi ukugcotshwa eninakho kuyonenza nihambe ngokunqoba. Kodwa kuyokwenzeka njengoba kusho umbhalo: Babusisiwe abafileyo abafela eNkosini, kusukela manje, yebo, usho uMoya, ukuba baphumule emisebenzini yabo, nemisebenzi yabo iyabalandela.

Isinyathelo ngesinyathelo niyohamba, futhi ngiyonihola ngaleyo ndlela. Ngokuba ngiyisiphephelo senu nenqaba yenu, ngisho noMesiya wenu weqiniso enithembele kuye. Futhi nakuba kuyokuwa inkulungwane eceleni kwakho, nezinkulungwane eziyishumi ngakwesokunene sakho: kodwa ngeke kusondele kini. Ngokuba niphawulelwe ukufeza izinjongo zami, futhi ngininike umvuzo omkhulu nesithembiso ukuze nginisekele futhi nginiqinise, ngokuba nginithuma emisebenzini uBaba wami ayale yona ngami, ngakho nginimisa njengezinceku zami nabaprofethi.

Ngakho njengoba nihamba niyala okungenzeki, yazini ukuthi niyoyala okuningi ngomoya ozelwe phakathi kwenu. Ngalokho yalani izintaba ukuba zisuswe ziphonswe olwandle, njengoba ninokukholwa kuYahweh. Ngalokho yalani imimoya ukuba isuke njengoba nikhuluma amazwi anamandla ngokukholwa okukhulu kuYahweh. Ngalokho yalani zonke izifo nobhubhane ukuba zisuke njengoba nikhuluma amazwi okumelwe alalelwe (ngisho yizo zonke izinto) njengoba nikhuluma amazwi anamandla amakhulu ngokukholwa okukhulu kuYahweh. Ngokuba ngezinhlelo nezinhloso zikaYahweh uqobo ngithunyiwe, futhi kulezo zinhlelo nezinhloso nginithuma, futhi niyongenzela imisebenzi emikhulu. Ngokuba uYahweh uyikho konke kukho konke ngomoya wami, engizobeka ngawo uphawu kwabakhethiweyo.

Ngokuba kimi, uYeshua HaMashiach, kuhlala konke ukugcwala, ngokuba uBaba unginike zonke izinto ezulwini nasemhlabeni, ukuze ngifeze konke. Ngakho uYahweh uyikho konke kukho konke, futhi ninokufinyelela kuBaba naseNdodaneni yakhe, ngokuba uMose wafundisa kanje: INkosi uNkulunkulu wakho iyakukuvusela umProfethi phakathi kwakho, kubafowenu, onjengami, iyakulalela yena. Ngingulowo mprofethi uMose akhuluma ngaye (owavuswa esizweni sakwaJuda), futhi ngingaphezu kwalokho; ngokuba uBaba unqume ukuthi mina, uYeshua HaMashiach, kufanele ngibe nobukhulu phezu kwakho konke, futhi kimi kufanele kuhlale konke ukugcwala. Ngingulowo mprofethi uMose akhuluma ngaye, owavuswa kwabafileyo ngenjongo enjalo.

Ngalokho ngizoninakekela nina nalabo engizonithuma kubo. Ngokuba niyoba isibani sokukhanya, esifihlwe obala emhlabeni omnyama nongazinzile. Niyoshumayela zonke izinto eziphathelene nami, iNkosi yenu nomsindisi wenu, ngokuba ngiyiwundlu likaNkulunkulu, ngisho nePhasika eliphelele. Alukho usindiso kwelinye igama ngaphandle kwelami, uJesu Kristu oLungileyo.

Abanye benu ngiyobasusa endaweni ethile ngibayise kwenye, futhi niyoba abakhulumeli bami, abaprofethi, nezinceku. Kuzodingeka nihlole imimoya yalabo enihlangana nabo futhi nihambe ngokuhlakanipha okukhulu; ngokuba umhlaba eniwaziyo uyothathelwa indawo umhlaba ongenamthetho wezindawo eziyincithakalo eziningi, futhi abanye benu bayothunyelwa ukuba bayokhonza labo abasezincithakalweni, futhi niyoyiqonda imiyalelo yami, futhi niyokwenza izenzo ezinkulu.

Ngakho-ke qondani ukuthi ngemva kwempi nokubhujiswa okuzokwenzeka ngemva kokuba ngiqoqile abathembekile bami (futhi ngisuse umvimbeli), kuyoba khona isiphithiphithi esikhulu nokubhujiswa. Abantu bayogijima baya le nale, futhi abaningi bayobhubha ekungakholweni okungenalukholo, futhi bayozingela babambe babulale abaningi. Futhi kwabaningi, izwi lokuqala abayolizwa ngeke libe elami, nakuba lelo zwi lizonikeza insindiso yamanga. Cha, ngisho nesilo esiyovuka ngamandla amakhulu sivela kudrako siyobiza abaningi ngamanga ekupheleni kwabo kwaphakade okungenathemba.

Bayokwenza sengathi, ngokuba bangabakhohlisi abakhulu, futhi bayokwenza sengathi bangoMesiya weqiniso, futhi abalandeli babo bayosho ukuthi bamele uMesiya weqiniso noNkulunkulu weqiniso; kodwa angibathumanga, ngakho-ke ningalandeli izibani zabo zamathemba amanga. Ngokuba bayonikeza ukungena emphakathini wabo omusha ukuze bakhe umhlaba wabo omusha; kodwa ukuze ungene, umuntu kufanele amukele uphawu. Futhi kulabo abamukela uphawu, ukungena kuyonikezwa emalangabini aluhlaza okwesibhakabhaka aleso sibhakabhaka somlilo esivutha isibabule. Ngokuba bonke abamukela uphawu bayoba nesabelo sabo emlilweni ongenakucinywa futhi bayobe beshintshise ukuphila okuphakade ngesiqephu sesinkwa ngezindlela zika-Esawu.

Kuzodingeka ningawanaki wonke amazwi ngaphandle kwelami futhi nihlukane nabo bonke abangangamukeli. Kuzodinga ukuhlakanipha okukhulu, ikhono, nobuqili ukuze usinde, futhi ngizoninika ulimi lwabahlakaniphileyo ukuze nazi ukuthi nimphendule kanjani umuntu ngamunye. Ngokuba ngizokwenza izwi lami licace kini, nakuba liyizwi elincane, elithuleyo. Niyolazi izwi lami, ngokuba ngiyoliqinisa izwi lami kini izikhathi eziningi, ngakho-ke ningabi abangakholwayo kodwa nibe nokukholwa okukhulu emazwini ami esithembiso.

Ngizonifundisa zonke izinto, futhi niyokwazi ukwenza imisebenzi enginithuma ukuba niyifeze. Izwi lami liyizwi eliphelele, ngisho izwi lomemeza ehlane, futhi ngikhuluma ngezwi lomemeza ehlane; ngokuba bathunyiwe kanjalo, ngisho nabaprofethi bami nezinceku zami abahambe phambili ukwenza izindlela zenu ziqonde nezindlela zenu zibe sobala. Nginimisele imisebenzi eminingi emikhulu, futhi niyokwenza ngokukhulu.

Ngalokho, yazini zizwe zami, zihlobo zami, nabantwana besithembiso: isivuno sikhulu ngempela, kodwa izisebenzi ziyingcosana; ngakho-ke thandazani eNkosini yesivuno, ukuba ithumele izisebenzi esivunweni sayo. Hambani ngezindlela zenu: Bhekani, nginithuma njengamawundlu phakathi kwezimpisi. Ngokuba ngiyiNkosi Yesivuno, futhi nakuba izisebenzi bezilokhu ziyingcosana, kukhona umvuzo omkhulu nenzuzo yaphakade nenkazimulo ngomsebenzi eniyowenza. Ngokuba niyovuna umvuzo omkhulu nezithelo kimi; kuphela ningadangali osizini lwenu nasezinhluphekweni zenu. Lokhu kungukuhlukaniswa kokugcina, futhi bonke abamiselwe ukuphila okuphakade kusukela ekusekelweni komhlaba bayotholwa, futhi abasoze balahleka, ngokuba akekho okwaziyo ukubahlwitha esandleni sami.

Ngakho imisebenzi yenu iyolandela ingene ephakadeni, futhi ezenzweni zenu eziningi niyodumisa uYahweh, ongithumile, uYeshua HaMashiach, njengoMesiya wenu weqiniso. Nangezenzo zenu eziningi ezinamandla namazwi amaningi anosawoti eqiniso eniyowakhuluma, niyomemezela ukubuya kwami okuseduze futhi nihambe emisebenzini kaJohane uMbhapathizi ukulungiselela ukuza kweNkosi: ngakho memezelani ukubuya kwami, ngokuba ngiyowafeza ngokuqinisekile amazwi ami esithembiso. Ngokuba impela ekupheleni kosizi lukaJakobe, ngisho nasekupheleni kwesonto lokugcina leminyaka, ngisho isonto lamashumi ayisikhombisa likaDaniyeli, ngiyobuya namabutho asezulwini ukumisa uMbuso wami eMhlabeni, ngisho uMbuso kaNkulunkulu; futhi ukubusa kwami kweminyaka eyinkulungwane kuyoqala, ngisho ukubusa kokulunga, ubungcwele, nokuthula njengoba iNgonyama iyolala neWundlu!

Nginibizela emisebenzini lapho ngizonihola khona isinyathelo ngesinyathelo.

Nginibiza ukuba nithande uNkulunkulu ngenhliziyo yenu yonke, nangomphefumulo, nangengqondo, nangamandla, futhi nithande umakhelwane wenu njengalokhu nizithanda nina.

Nginithuma ukuba niyale okungenzeki njengoba nihamba othandweni olukhulu nasemandleni amakhulu.

Ngiyisiphephelo senu nenqaba yenu, ngisho noMesiya wenu weqiniso enithembele kuye.

Nginithuma emisebenzini uBaba wami ayale yona ngami, ngakho nginimisa njengezinceku zami nabaprofethi.

Nginithuma, futhi niyongenzela imisebenzi emikhulu.

Ngingulowo mprofethi uMose akhuluma ngaye, owavuswa kwaJuda ngenjongo enjalo.

Ngingaphezu kwalokho, ngokuba kimi, uYeshua HaMashiach, kuhlala konke ukugcwala.

Ngiyiwundlu likaNkulunkulu, ngisho nePhasika eliphelele.
