Manje Isikhathi Sesifikile

Sekuyisikhathi, bantwana bami, sekuyisikhathi. Sekuyisikhathi sokuza ekhaya, lapho kukhala icilongo, nebandla lami libuthelwa ndawonye, lapho uYahweh elindele ngenjabulo enhliziyweni yakhe ukufika kwenu kungazelelwe. Sekuyisikhathi sokuza ekhaya, lapho ukukhenceza kwecilongo kubiza abantwana boPhezukonke ukuba baye lapho kuhlala khona izingelosi zizulazula. Sekuyisikhathi bantwana bami sokuba nginibuthe ndawonye emagumbini omane omhlaba, ngisho nabo bonke abalele kuKristu bayovuka kuqala, nani eniphilayo nisasele niyokwenyuselwa phezulu kanye nabo emafwini. Ngoba mina Kristu Jesu ngiyiNkosi noMsindisi wenu ophilayo, futhi nginibizela ekhaya lenu emazulwini lapho imivuzo yenu ilindele khona, lapho nginilungiselele indawo endlini kaBaba, ngoba endlini kaBaba kukhona izindlu eziningi. Nginibizela ekhaya ukuba nime phambi kweNkosi yenu, ukuze bonke bamukeliswe izinto abazenzile besesemzimbeni, noma kukuhle noma kubi, ukuze bonke bamukeliswe umvuzo wabo ovela eNkosini, nokuba bonke bathathe izindawo zabo ezinhlwini zasezulwini ezilungiselelwe umuntu ngamunye wenu.

Bhekani! NginguYeshua HaMashiach, futhi ngingumkhwenyana osondele ukuzolanda umakoti wami ngoba izikhathi zomshado wethu sezifikile, lapho ngiyongena esivumelwaneni saphakade nabakhethiweyo bami, labo abazilungisile, nalabo engibahlanzile egazini lami lokuhlawulela ngoba beza kimi ngokuphelele futhi banikela izinhliziyo zabo zonke kimi ngothando nangokuzinikela. Nakuba bengazange bahambe ngokuphelele, futhi abaningi banqoba isono esibi, bazihlanza ngokuphelele egazini leWundlu, futhi manje bemi phambi kwami behlanzekile ngokuphelele ezonweni zabo futhi bamsulwa njengezintombi ezingazange zifebe futhi zazigcina zikude nomubi. Bhekani! Abanqobi bami engibathethelele kuzo zonke izono futhi ngathela amafutha ezibhanini zabo ngoba beza kimi ngezikhathi zabo zokudinga futhi ngabakhombisa indlela yokulungisa izintambo zabo futhi ngabaphilisa ngabahlukanisela mina, ngisho bonke labo uYahweh abakhethile ngaphambi kokusekelwa komhlaba ukuba babe ngumakoti wami futhi babe nami kuze kube phakade, futhi bayobusa nami njengamaKhosi nabaPristi baphakade ngoba babizwa kanjalo, bangcweliswa futhi bakhethwa kimi, uKristu Jesu iNkosi yabo evusiweyo.

Futhi abayizi-144,000 bami bayoguqulwa, bananyatheliswe uphawu, futhi badalwe kabusha ngokuphelele ngenjongo ababizelwe futhi balungiselelwa yona. Ngiqinisile, ngiqinisile, yonke imiphefumulo ekholiwe egameni lami elinamandla futhi yangenza iNkosi yayo yaphakade iyoguqulwa ngokuphelele futhi idalwe kabusha njengesidalwa esisha, futhi iyobunjwa njengoba nginjalo. Futhi abasoze baphinde bone, noma ngokuzidla noma ngokungamesabi uNkulunkulu, futhi abasoze baphinde baqalekiswe, futhi akusoze kwaphinde kube khona ukugula noma ubuhlungu, noma ukufa noma usizi okuyophinde kubathinte ngoba izinto zakuqala sezidlulile futhi ukukhanya kweqiniso manje sekuyakhanya. Futhi yimina, uKristu Jesu, ozohamba manje ukuze ngiziveze kuJakobe nanoma ngubani obiza egameni lami elinamandla kuze kube sekupheleni uyosindiswa, ngisho bonke abakhileyo emhlabeni abamiselwe ukuphila okuphakade, futhi kuyoba nesixuku esikhulu esiyongamukela njengoMesiya wabo futhi ngiyobe ngibasindise ngokuphelele.

Ngiyeza ngani, zithandwa zami, futhi izikhathi sezikhona manje. Ngilinde isikhathi sonke somusa futhi ngisindise abaningi, futhi abaningi bayovuselwa ekuphileni okusha kimi, uKristu Jesu iNkosi yabo yaphakade, futhi abafileyo kuKristu bayovuka kuqala, futhi icilongo lokugcina selimi ngomumo manje. Izibonakaliso eziningi, izimangaliso, nezimangaliso ziningi emhlabeni wonke ngobuhle bazo, futhi izibonakaliso azipheli emhlabeni wonke manje ezimemezela ukubonakala kwami, ezimemezela umshado weWundlu nomakoti walo othembekileyo, futhi amahholo edili emazulwini alungisiwe futhi alindile.

Futhi kunjalo-ke, umkhosi wasezulwini ulindele abathembekileyo bami, ababiziweyo, nabakhethiweyo, futhi manje yisikhathi sokuba baphumule kuze kube phakade emisebenzini yabo abayisebenzile emhlabeni ezindlini zabo zobumba, futhi basebenzile enyameni njengezisebenzi zami futhi babutha baletha abaningi kimi, iNkosi uJesu Kristu, ukuze bamukele insindiso yaphakade kuze kube phakade. Ngoba nakuba inyama yabo yayonakaliswe esonweni nguSathane, ngabanika injongo futhi ngabamema ukuba bashiye unomphela ubuze ababelahlwe kubo yisono.

Futhi-ke banale ngcebo ezitsheni zobumba ukuze amandla kaNkulunkulu aphile futhi ahlale kubo, futhi benza imisebenzi eminingi emikhulu egameni lami, futhi yimina engabanika amandla okuba babe yizindlalifa zensindiso nezindlalifa zokuphila okuphakade, futhi ngibahlengile futhi ngabanika leso sibambiso saphakade ekuzalweni okusha, futhi ngibabekile bonke uphawu ngalowo moya ongcwele wesithembiso, lolo phawu lwesithembiso olungenakwephulwa. Futhi manje sekuyisikhathi sokuhlenga ngokuphelele impahla ethengiweyo, ngisho bonke abangenze iNkosi ngenhliziyo egcwele ukholo, bazi ukuthi uYahweh wangivusa kwabafileyo ukuze ngingaphinde ngife. Manje yizikhathi zokuhlengwa kwabo ngokugcwele lapho bezokwamukela ngokuphelele isibambiso sabo abasithola kimi ekuzalweni okusha kukaKristu kini, ithemba lenkazimulo!

Bhekani! Yizwani izwi lami futhi niqonde amazwi ami futhi nazi ukuthi ikusasa lenu elikhazimulayo alikude ngeminyaka, futhi alimiselwe ukufika ngamaJubili azayo, kodwa phezu kwenu zithandwa zami, futhi kungazelelwe ngomzuzwana waphakade nonke nizoguqulwa, futhi izikhathi zoshintsho sezikhona manje. Nizozithola kungazelelwe seniguquliwe naba senkazimulweni yaphakade, futhi ngizonembathisa izingubo ezingenasici ezingasoze zangcoliswa. Kungazelelwe nizozithola nimi egumbini lesihlalo sobukhosi sikaYahweh, ngisho ebukhazikhazini nasenkazimulweni yaphakade kaBaba weziKhanyiso, futhi niyohlanganyela kuze kube phakade emvelweni wakhe waphakade ngoba ningamadodana oNkulunkulu ophilayo, ngisho bonke abathathwa ku-Adamu futhi bahlengwa emhlabeni obomvu.

Futhi kwakuyigazi lami lokuhlawulela elachithelwa emhlabeni eGolgotha elaqinisekisa insindiso yenu kanye, naphakade njengoba nganikela ngokuphila kwami ngamazwi athi "Sekuphelile!" Futhi mina, Kristu Jesu, ngiyiNkosi yamaNkosi noMbusi wabaBusi ovusiweyo nowenyukela ezulwini kuze kube phakade ngoba uYahweh wangivusa kwabafileyo ukuze ngingaphinde ngife! Ngingu-Adamu wokugcina, futhi ngiyindodana yomuntu ehlengile amadodana abantu ababiza egameni lami futhi yabenza amadodana kaNkulunkulu aphakade ayohlala kuze kube phakade enkazimulweni yaphakade nasebukhazikhazini baphakade, futhi kuze kube phakade uYahweh uyodunyiswa kubo, nabo kuYahweh, ngoba kimi, uYeshua HaMashiach, kuhlala konke ukugcwala kuze kube phakade, futhi uYahweh uyoba yikho konke kukho konke futhi kuze kube phakade siyohamba njengesidalwa esisodwa esisha nomuntu oyedwa omusha. Futhi nina, ababiziweyo bami abakhethiweyo nabathembekileyo, nihlengiwe kuze kube phakade, futhi senizokuqonda ukuphila kwenu kweqiniso ngoba ukuphila kwenu kungokwaphakade. Ngakho-ke qondani ukuthi nifile, futhi ukuphila kwenu kufihliwe kanye nami, uKristu Jesu, kuYahweh. Lapho mina, Kristu Jesu, ongukuphila kwenu, ngibonakala, khona-ke nani niyobonakala kanye nami enkazimulweni. Anazi yini ukuthi ubuntu benu beqiniso bufihliwe kimi, uKristu Jesu?

Pho kungani nidangele, bakhethiweyo bami, abathembekileyo bami, iBandla lami ngisho noMakoti wami? Ngiyeza ngani, futhi bhekani, ngiyasondela kini. Futhi ngizonibutha kungazelelwe kimi. Futhi njengoba umhlaba ungena ekwahlulelweni nasempini, amazulu ayojabula ngokuba khona kwenu futhi kuyoba nomkhosi omkhulu lapho amadodana kaNkulunkulu ehlanganiswa kuze kube phakade neNkosi yawo yamaNkosi noMbusi wabaBusi enkazimulweni yaphakade nasekuphileni okusha okuyoba khona kubo bonke. Futhi akusoze kwaphinde kube khona ukuhlukana phakathi kwalabo abahlengiwe ngegazi leWundlu noYise noNkulunkulu wabo.

Ngoba manje yisikhathi uYahweh asikhethile ukuqala ukuziveza ngokuphelele endalweni yakhe, futhi ukuziveza kwakhe ngokuphelele kuqala endlini kaNkulunkulu, futhi sekuzoziveza ngokuphelele kubakhethiweyo nabathembekileyo bakhe, ngisho izithelo zokuqala, ngoba labo engibabuthela kimi lapho kukhala icilongo lokugcina bayizithelo zokuqala kwabafileyo. Futhi manje yisikhathi sokuba bazi ngokugcwele, njengoba nonke naziwa ngokugcwele yimina noBaba. Ngakho-ke ngimemezela ukubonakala kwami futhi ngineluleka nina bathembekileyo bami ukuba niqhubeke nizilungiselela ukubonakala kwami, ngoba manje yizikhathi njengoba ukwahlulelwa kuqala endlini kaNkulunkulu. Futhi kungazelelwe, kuyokwenzeka.

Bhekani! Ningazesabi iziphepho ezizayo enizibonayo abaningi zihlangana: iziphepho zempi, iziphepho zenhlekelele esondelayo, iziphepho zezinhlekelele zemvelo ezizokwenzeka ngokuqinisekile. Qondani ukuthi akukho lutho oluyonilimaza nganoma iyiphi indlela, nakuba abaningi bephila ezilingweni nasempini yokomoya enqotshwa ngokuphelele kimi, uKristu Jesu. Bhekani! Isikhathi sokubonakala kwami kubathembekileyo bami sesifikile, futhi njengoba imihelo yokubeletha ingapheli, kungazelelwe bayobuthelwa ndawonye bahlwitshwe ekwahlulelweni okungesikho okwabo.
