UMalusi Othembekile Uthi

UMalusi othembekile uthi kwabathembekileyo: Ngiqinisile, ngiqinisile, ngithi kini, sukani ebubini ningoni. Ngokuba umvuzo wami unami, wokumnika umuntu yilowo nalowo njengokwemisebenzi yakhe. Nginibizile ngothando lukaBaba wami ukuze nibe nokuphila, futhi nibe nakho okuphakade. Nginibizele ukuba nihambe eMbusweni wami, ningikhonze futhi nihlale emhlabeni ngokulunga. Ngininikile umusa wami ukuze nikhulele kimi, iNkosi yenu uJesu Kristu. Ningadangali ngenxa yokusola kwami, ngokuba bonke engibathandayo ngiyabakhuza, ukuze umusa wami osindisayo ugxile ekujuleni kobuntu benu ukuze nenze imisebenzi emikhulu, imisebenzi yezibusiso, imisebenzi yokuphilisa, nokuba nithele izithelo eziningi kimi, bantu bami.

Ngokuba uBaba wami unguNkulunkulu onothando, futhi uthando lukaBaba alunakunqotshwa. Kimi, iNkosi elungileyo, kuhlala konke ukugcwala uBaba anginike khona. Ubeke izinto zonke ezandleni zami, futhi njengoba uFaro waphakamisa uJosefa wanikela kuye iGibhithe lonke, kanjalo noBaba ungiphakamisile futhi wanikela izinto zonke ezulwini nasemhlabeni ezandleni zami. Unginikile igama eliphezu kwawo onke amagama, ukuze ngegama likaJesu amadolo onke aguqe, ezulwini nasemhlabeni, futhi zonke izilimi zivume ubuNkosi bami, ezulwini nasemhlabeni.

Ngininikile ukuphila okuphakade, futhi nginivulele indlela yokuphunyuka ehlane nokuphunyuka kulolo suku olukhulu lwentukuthelo olusondelayo, ngakho-ke ningapheli amandla! Ningakhathali ekwenzeni okuhle, kodwa manini phambi kwami, futhi ngizoqhubeka nginisondeza ebukhoneni bami. Nihlezi ezindaweni zasezulwini futhi nizohlanganyela esihlalweni sami sobukhosi njengoba nami ngihlanganyela esihlalweni sikaBaba. Nginemiqhele eminingi nezibusiso eziningi enizibekelweyo nina, bantu bami abathembekileyo. Yimani phambi kwami futhi nibe ngabapheleleyo kimi. Dedani ezonweni zenu nivumele izikhathi zokuqabuleka ziphephethe amakhoba empilweni yenu. Kimi kukhona ukuphila okuphakade, futhi kimi kukhona indalo entsha, umuntu omusha, yena kanye uKristu phakathi kwenu, ithemba lenkazimulo! Dedani ebubini nidede esonweni. Qhubekani ningimemezela phakathi kwabezizwe, njengoba kusekhona abantu okusamele basindiswe kulelihora lokugcina. Qhubekani nimemezela igama lami, ngokuba alikho elinye igama ngaphansi kwezulu abantu abangasindiswa ngalo. Tholani induduzo ekuphumuleni engininike khona, futhi nibeke imithwalo yenu ezinyaweni zami; ngokuba umthwalo wami ulula, nejoka lami libucayi.

Kwabathembekileyo (labo abanqobe isono nobunzima ngegazi lami elihlawulayo) kwabathembekileyo, ngithi: Nginomvuzo omkhulu eniwugcinelweyo endlini kaBaba. Iso alikakuboni, indlebe ayikakuzwa, futhi akukangenanga ezinhliziyweni zabantu izinto abazilungiselweyo. Ngizokuvuza kakhulu ngemiqhele eminingi, ngakho-ke ngimeleni futhi nithembeke ezwini lami nasemazwini ami; ngokuba kulawo mazwi nizothola ukuphila. Angisiyena yini uLizwi owenziwa inyama wahlala phakathi kwenu?

Ngingumahluleli olungileyo.

Ngingumalusi omuhle owelusa umhlambi.

Ngingukuphulukiswa kwenu, futhi ngizoniphulukisa ngokupheleleyo.

Ngazalwa ngowesifazane ngenziwa inyama, nakuba kungekho sono kimi.

Ngabethelwa ngenxa yezono zenu, futhi ngeqiniso, akekho owathatha ukuphila kwami, kodwa ngakunikela ngokuzithandela ngenxa yothando enginalo ngamadodana namadodakazi kaBaba.

Ngavuswa ekufeni ngosuku lwesithathu, ngokuba kwakungenakwenzeka ukuba ukufa kungibambe. UBaba waphula izibopho zokufa ngamandla okuvuka kwami.

Ngingulibo lwabafi.

Maduze nizongihlangabeza emoyeni, futhi imihelo yokufa uqobo izokhulula labo abaleleyo, bese nina eniphilayo nenisasele niyohlwithwa kanye nabo, futhi niyoba nami kuze kube phakade. NginguNdodana kaNkulunkulu, futhi esandleni sami kukhona umvuzo wami kwabathembekileyo bami.imani niqine, bantwana bami, ningadangali. Qinisani eNkosini nasemandleni obukhosi bami, ngokuba nguBaba wami kuphela omkhulu kunami. Ngizizwile izicelo zenu, futhi nginamukele njengabami.

Bekezelani niqhubeke ningisebenzela. Ngoba maduze nizobe nisekhaya nami, futhi kuze kube phakade siyobe senza umsebenzi kaBaba wethu. Qinani nithokoze. Ngiyazi ukuthi abaningi benu bayabona usizi nenhlekelele isondela, futhi abaningi benu bayibonile imikhosi yomubi iwela imingcele yenu. Ningabesabi! Ningabesabi, nakuba bebengakubhubhisa ukuba bebengakwazi, kodwa abezeli abathembekileyo bami, njengoba ukubaleka okukhulu nokuhlangana okukhulu kuyosindisa abathembekileyo bami ehoreni lokulingwa nesilingo esizofikela umhlaba wonke. Phumulani othandweni lwami, nazi ukuthi angisoze nganishiya noma nganilahla, njengoba noBaba engazange angishiye noma angilahle ehoreni lami lomhlatshelo. Ngoba insindiso yenu iphephile kimi; ngakho-ke, phokophelani kimi, njengoba ngisanoniqinisa nakakhulu kunakuqala. Inkulu iMfihlakalo yokumesaba uNkulunkulu!
