Busuku Buyasondela

Izwi leNkosi lehla phezu kwami.

Busuku buyasondela lapho kungasekho muntu ongasasebenza. Ukuprofetha kuyophela, izilimi ziyothula, izipho zonke zebandla ziyophela lapho umakoti wami esehlwithiwe. Bese kuba usuku lobumnyama lapho kuyoba khona indlala ezweni, indlala yezwi lami iyoba khona. Nokho ngisho nangaleso sikhathi izingelosi zami nabaprofethi babakhethiweyo bayoqhubeka bekhuluma egameni lami. Isikhathi siyasondela lapho izilo zasendle ziyohlasela futhi abaningi bayobhubha. Usuku lweNkosi selumemezeliwe Ezwini lami Elingcwele, futhi maduzane luzokwenzeka. Ukwahlulela kuyogqashuka ngokuzuma; ngakho-ke, phendukani ezonweni zenu nize kimi, iNkosi uJesu Kristu, njengoba ngizikhokhele izono zenu, futhi ningaba mhlophe njengeqhwa phambi kwami nilengubo yelineni elicolekileyo uma kuphela ningalahla ezanu izingubo. Phendukelani kimi ngokuphenduka. Zithobeni phambi kweNkosi kusesemini, ngoba maduzane ubusuku buyongena futhi akukho muntu ongasasebenza. Nokho ngisho nangalezo zikhathi, noma ubani obiza igama leNkosi uyosindiswa, kodwa kunjengokungathi uphunyuke emlilweni.
