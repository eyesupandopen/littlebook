Ephreli 01, 2024

Angikwenzanga yini ukuba ulunge?

Thula futhi ube nokuthula, wazi ukuthi ngiyiNkosi ekubizile ukuba ume phambi kwami uqotho ezindleleni zakho zonke. Angikwenzanga yini ukuba ulunge? Angikwenzanga yini ukuba ulunge kimi, iNkosi uJesu Kristu? Awuhlangene yini nami ekuhluphekeni kwami nasenkazimulweni yami? NgiyiNkosi yamakhosi neKosi yamakosi, futhi angiqambi manga. Akunjalo yini okumenyezelwe yimiBhalo Engcwele? Yima uqotho futhi ulungile, futhi ube nokuthula ezinhliziyweni zenu, futhi nazi ukuthi mina, iNkosi uJesu Kristu, ngininikile intethelelo ephelele nokuthula phambi kwami uma nizokuzwa amazwi ami namukele imfundiso yami.

Ukuhlakanipha kuyinto eyinhloko; ngakho-ke, tholani ukuhlakanipha — hhayi ukuhlakanipha kwezwe okuyize, kodwa ukuhlakanipha kweNkosi. Futhi phumulani ngaphansi kwesandla sami esinamandla. Ngokuba ukwesaba iNkosi kungukusuka ebubini nokuhamba ngokulunga phambi kwami, isho iNkosi enamandla, yebo iNkosi uJesu Kristu. Yizwani izwi lami! Yizwani izwi lami ningalaleli elinye! Amazwi amaningi aphikisana neZwi lami futhi aniqambela amanga, njengoba efuna ukubusa phezu kwemiphefumulo yenu ukuze anenze impango uma niwalandela. Ningalithandi izwe, bantwana bami, nezinto ezisezweni. Leli zwe elibi elikhona liyaphela, futhi mina, iNkosi, ngishilo, futhi ngeke ngithuliswe ukungakholwa kwenu, futhi ngeke ngithuliswe ukuntula kwenu ukukholwa. Cha, ngiyomemeza kakhulu ukuze nazi iqiniso lamazwi engiwakhulumayo. Qondanisa wona neMibhalo Engcwele. Angisilo yini izwi elenziwa inyama elihlala phakathi kwenu? Angininikanga yini ugcobo noMduduzi ngize ngifike?

Yazini, bantwana bami, ukuthi nginithengile ngegazi lami, futhi umhlaba ungowami, futhi masinyane ukulunga kuyobusa emhlabeni. Ngikukhulumile. Ngininikile ukuphila kimi; ningazilahli izindlela zami. Ngininikile inhloso; ningakulahli ukuphila enginibizele kukho. Ngininikile ubizo, yebo, ubizo oluphakeme olungunaphakade nolungenasiphelo sezinsuku uma nizophendukela kimi. Phendukelani kimi ngobuso obuvulekile nenhliziyo, ningivumele nginihlanze ezonweni zenu nasebubini benu egazini lami lokuhlawulela. Bonke engibathandayo, ngiyabasola, ukuze nime phambi kwami niqotho nilengubo emhlophe ecwebezelayo engizoninika yona maduzane.

Zonke izingubo zenu zingamadlavu angcolile enizikhethele wona ukuze nicashe kimi. Kodwa ayikho indawo lapho umuntu angacasha khona ekubukeni kwami, futhi nginibona njengoba ninjalo ngempela, nanjengoba nginidalile kabusha kimi. Kukhona umvuzo omkhulu obabekelelwe abantwana bami abathembekileyo; kodwa labo abangalahlayo, ngiyobalahla. Kungani nisabambelele kuleli zwe elifayo nezinkinga zalo nobuhlungu balo? Ninokukhetha kimi, ukukhetha izizukulwane ezedlule ezingenazo ngaphambi komhlatshelo wami wokuhlawulela. Ngakho-ke, noma ngubani ongalahlayo ngezikhathi zokuvuselela unasono esikhulu. Uthando lwami ngani lungunaphakade futhi alunakwephulwa. Mina noBaba simunye, nezinjongo nemiklamo yethu akuhlukaniseki. UBaba unginike zonke izinto ukuze ngisindise abanye.

Ningazibeki endaweni yabangakholwayo, njengoba ngininikile izizathu eziningi zokukholwa okukhulu. Ukukholwa okungangohlamvu lwesinaphi yikho konke enikudingayo, ukwethemba okuncane kakhulu. Gqokani izingubo engininike zona. Angithululanga yini umoya wami phezu kwenu, o nina bangabazayo? Ningabi ngabangakholwayo kodwa kholwani kuphela, njengoba izikhathi nezinkathi phezu kwenu futhi akukho ukubuyela emuva. Izwe enake nalazi ngeke libuye, nezwe enilazi namuhla ligcwele inkohliso enkulu. Nakuba izinto zisebonakala zijwayelekile kini, konke enake nakuthanda kuthathwe ngomubi; ngokuba unesikhathi esibekiwe, isikhathi okungathi ukuba angizange ngisifinyeze, bekungeke kusale nyama iphila. Kungani nifisa usuku lweNkosi oluzayo ezweni ukuhlola izinhliziyo zabantu? Kuwusuku lobumnyama lapho kungekho muntu ongema khona. Kuwusuku olwesabekayo lokwahlulela nemiphumela, futhi nasezinsukwini ezimnyama kakhulu, noma ngubani obiza igama leNkosi kuze kube sekupheleni uyosindiswa.

Izikhathi nezinkathi ziyashintsha phambi kwamehlo enu, kodwa namanje anikholwa ngamehlo enu. Nakuba ngenza izibonakaliso nezimangaliso kini naphakathi kwenu, nisavala amehlo enu nivala izindlebe zenu ngokucabanga kokungakholwa. Umusa wami nesihe sami kungunaphakade ezizukulwaneni ngezizukulwane, kodwa izikhathi ziyashintsha, nokwahlulela nenhlekelele kusondela ezweni enilithanda kakhulu, nezinto enizithandayo ziyochitheka phakathi kweminwe yenu njengesihlabathi. Maduze izwe ngeke lisaba nesimemo somusa nesihe, kodwa labo abayosindiswa kuyodingeka bakhuthazele kuze kube sekuphefumuleni kwabo kokugcina ukuze basindiswe kimi. Phendukelani kimi namukele isimemo sami, ngokuba ukuba kuKristu kungukuba nobudlelwano obusindisayo nami, iNkosi yamakhosi neKosi yamakosi. Nguwena wedwa ongamisa ukusindiswa kwakho ngokungifulathela ngokungakholwa futhi wenqabe ubizo lwami. Cabanga ngo-Esawu, owathengisa ubuzibulo bakhe ngesinkwa esilahlekileyo, owanikela ngefa lakhe kumfowabo ngokungakholwa. Ukungawanaki amazwi ami nokuhamba ebumnyameni kuyisono, ngakho-ke ningoni nime phambi kwami.

Kungani nizibala njengabangafanele umusa wami, ekubeni okumele nikwenze nje kungukwamukela? Yamukelani imisebenzi yami yokuhlawulela ezimpilweni zenu ningivumele ngingene nginisize esikhathini senu sokudinga, ukube izinhliziyo zenu ezingakholwayo zingangivumela. Khethani ukuphila, ngokuba namuhla yisikhathi sokukhetha; yizwani izwi lami ningazenzi lukhuni izinhliziyo zenu ehlane njengoba okhokho benza bawa endleleni. Ningawi kude nami; ningalandeli ukubhujiswa nezindlela ezilimazayo zokungakholwa, kodwa bhekani kimi nethembe izwi lami. Yebo, izimvu zami ziyalizwa izwi lami, ngakho ngilandeleni niye endaweni ephephile. Ningacabangi ukuthi inhliziyo yokungakholwa ingema ingcwelisiwe phambi kwami, ngokuba ukungakholwa kungukulahlwa kokukholwa engininike khona kimi.

Ngingcweliseni, iNkosi uJesu Kristu, ezinhliziyweni zenu ningesabi, ngokuba ukubonakala kwami kuseduze kakhulu. Themba izwi lami uhambe ezinyathelweni zami, ngokuba indlela enginibekele yona iholela ekuphileni okusha phambi kukaNkulunkulu. Ngokuba ngiyindlela yodwa eya kuNkulunkulu; ngiyindlela, neqiniso, nokuphila. Nakuba omubi ethembisa izinto eziningi, akanalutho angalunikeza, futhi akakwazi ukuzikhulula esandleni sami. Cha, intukuthelo enkulu ibekelwe yena, noSathane unezuziso ezizayo azikhethele zona kulelo chibi elivuthayo. Bonke abamkhethayo, bonke abathatha uphawu lwakhe, bonke abakhonza isilo nomfanekiso waso bayomlandela efeni lakhe lentukuthelo engapheliyo kaNkulunkulu.

Akulungile yini kuBaba ukuvikela indalo yakhe? Akulungile yini ekwahluleleni kwakhe nasezindleleni zakhe azibeke phambi kwenu? Ngakho-ke, khethani ukuphila nikhethe ifa engininike lona, ngokuba ngithembisa izulu elisha nomhlaba omusha lapho kuhlala khona ukulunga. Nginithembisa ukuphila okuphakade, ukuphila kwenala enkulu, ukuthula nenjabulo. Nokho, ekuntuleni kwenu ukukholwa, nisamelana. Ngenzeni kini ukuze nginone kangangokuba nenqabe ubudlelwano nami, indodana kaNkulunkulu, noBaba wenkazimulo? Ukukhetha leli zwe nezindlela zalo kungukukhetha ukufa, futhi ekugcineni ukukhetha komuntu wonke kuyoba kokugcina, njengoba izikhathi zokwahlukanisa sezifikile; futhi labo abangakhethi, isinqumo siyokwenziwa ngabo.

Ayikho injabulo ekubhujisweni okuzayo okuzofika ezweni lenu maduze. Izinhlekelele zemvelo ziyokwanda, nomhlaba uyozamazama, namagagasi amakhulu ayoshaya iBhabhiloni eliyimfihlakalo; ngokuba umhlaba uyodabuka ezindaweni eziningi. Umlilo omkhulu uyodla izwe, nabathengisi bayokuma kude ngokwesaba ukuhlukunyezwa kwalo. Incithakalo enkulu isiphezu kwesifebe, iBhabhiloni elikhulu, elenze izwe ladakwa ngobufebe nokufeba kwalo, ngokuba linabathandi abaningi. Labo abangeke bazihlukanise nalo bayohlanganyela ekwahlulelweni kwalo, ngakho kungani nisakhetha ukufa kunokuphila engininike kona, okwakhokhelwa ngegazi lami elingenasono? Impi isizofika ngokugcwele ezweni lenu nasesizweni senu, futhi nakuba isiqalile — yebo, impi engamenyezelwanga abayilwa nani — maduze izosabalala emhlabeni wonke njengoba izimpawu zivulwa.

Impi enkulu nokushushiswa okukhulu kuzokwehlela umhlaba njengoba bezama ukubhubhisa noma yini kaNkulunkulu noKristu wakhe njengoba ekugcineni bezomenyezela obala impi engcwele kini. Bayothumba abaningi, futhi abaningi bayothathwa, nalabo abayosindiswa bayoba nokukhetha okukodwa: ukukhuthazela kuze kube sekupheleni bangakuthandi ukuphila kwabo kuze kube sekupheleni bese bebiza igama lami kuze kube sekupheleni. Abaholi benu banikhaphele futhi bayonikela ngabaningi komubi ezimpini ezizovutha maduzane. Isizwe senu siyohlaselwa ngaphakathi nangaphandle. Amabutho abomvu asevele ekhona, futhi alinde imiyalo yawo; ngokuba ahlela ukuhlasela ngokuzumayo, futhi abaningi bayothathwa. Amabutho omubi ayothatha konke ezweni kube okwawo nakulabo abangalahlayo. Bayontshontsha izwe lenu, amakhaya enu, inzalo yenu; futhi izimpilo eziningi ziyolahleka ekungakholweni. Impi iyeza ivela ngaphandle njengoba izizwe eziningi zihlangene ngokumelene nani; futhi zinemvume yabaphathi benu, futhi abaphathi benu basebenzela ukufa kwenu. Bafuna izwe labo, izwe elinikezelwe kuSathane lapho bengalandela khona ngokugcwele ububi obuhlala kubo, futhi akukho okuyovimbela izifiso zabo ezimbi, njengoba umvimbeli eyobe engekho.

Bayolishisa ngokuphelele iBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo ngomlilo, futhi noma ngubani ongashiswa amalangabi uyobhujiswa yindlala; noma ngubani ongabhujiswa yindlala uyobhujiswa yisifo; noma ngubani ongabhujiswa wusiko lwenkemba uyolethwa ezigxotsheni ngenxa yegama lami, futhi abaningi bayonqunywa amakhanda njengoba iZwi lami eliNgcwele lisho. Futhi labo abafela igama lami bayoba nomvuzo omkhulu, futhi ngiyosula zonke izinyembezi emehlweni abo.

Yazini, bantwana bami, ukuthi konke okumnandi nenjabulo yesono abantu ababi abazibekelela kona kuyonyamalala, nosizi nokuhlupheka kuyothatha izindawo zabo. Ubumnyama buyokwemboza izwe, kube nokusweleka kweZwi lami, nezwi lomkhwenyana nezwi lomakoti ngeke lisazwakala kulo.

Nangaleso sikhathi, ngiyothumela abaprofethi bami phakathi kwenu, njengoba ngiyobeka uphawu esibalweni sezizwe ezisele ukuze sikhonze labo, futhi bayokwenza izimangaliso ezinkulu. Izibonakaliso eziningi nezimangaliso ziyonikezwa kulezi zinsuku zobumnyama, futhi labo ababiza igama leNkosi kuze kube sekupheleni bayosindiswa. Imibhoshongo yenu iyowa. Amabhuloho enu ayodilika. Indlala iyofika ezweni, kube khona ukungabi namthetho ngokuphelele, futhi bayozingela labo abangimelelayo, futhi abaningi bayofela ukholo; nokho umvuzo omkhulu ulungiselelwe labo abema kuze kube sekupheleni. Kungani nifisa usuku lweNkosi? Ngokuba lwaluyobhubhisa yonke inyama emhlabeni ukube angizange ngifinyeze izinsuku. Khona-ke izwe liyongibona ngivela emazulwini namabutho asezulwini, ngokuba umhlaba ungowenkosi nokugcwala kwawo.

Yebo, nginitshela lezi zinto ngaphambi kokuba zenzeke, hhayi ukuthi nime lapho ningakholwa, kodwa ukuze nibe nokukholwa okungangohlamvu lwesinaphi, ngokuba luyokhula lube lukhulu kunezinye izihlahla futhi ngeke lunyakaziswe kimi uma ningivumela ukuba ngihlanze izono zenu futhi uma niza kimi ukuze nelashwe nivuselelwe. Ukukhala kwecilongo sekusondele kakhulu, futhi ngiyohlwitha umakoti wami phakathi. Ngisazobeka indawo etafuleni lami kunoma ngubani oyongenza iNkosi ngokuvula iminyango yakhe; ngokuba bhekani, ngimi ngiyangqongqoza!
