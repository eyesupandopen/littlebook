Ubufakazi Obumbaxambili: Ibandla
Emisebenzini KaPawulu Umphostoli
May 13, 2024

Ebandleni Lami, UMzimba Wami, Ngisho NoMlobokazi Wami: Yizwani Amazwi ENkosi Yenu!

Ebandleni lami, uMzimba wami, ngisho noMlobokazi wami: Ngiyanicela ukuba niboleke izindlebe zenu ekuhlakanipheni kwami nizwe izwi lami, ngoba manje ngizokhuluma nani. Niyalalele yini? Bekani eceleni izimfundiso zenu ezahlukahlukene nezenziwe ngabantu kanye nalokho okuthiwa yizimfundiso zenkolo, futhi nithobele izinhliziyo zenu emiBhalweni Engcwele, izwi lami elicwengekileyo. Bekani eceleni izimfundiso zenu ezinemvubelo, ngoba noma yikuphi ukuxutshwa kwamaZwi kaNkulunkulu, ngisho neZwi likaNkulunkulu elicwengekile, kubilisa yonke inhlama, anibe ningasenawo umbhalo. Ngoba mina ngiyiZwi likaNkulunkulu elenziwa inyama, futhi ngihlala phakathi kwenu, futhi nginibizela ekhaya lenu emazulwini maduzane kakhulu.

Bekani kude izincazelo zenu, futhi niyeke ukulalela amazwi amaningi angamaqili, ngisho namazwi angesiwo awami; kodwa esikhundleni salokho, bambani amaZwi ami ezinhliziyweni zenu niwaqhathanise nombhalo ukuze nibe nokholo emaZwini ami nokholo kimi, uKristu Jesu. Ngoba uma kufika kini umuntu ongakhulumi imfundiso yeNkosi, ngisho namaZwi kaNkulunkulu acwengekileyo, ningamfiseli nenhlanhla. Ngoba ngizonenza nihlakaniphe emiBhalweni yami eNgcwele, ngisho naseZwini lokuphila, futhi ukuhlakanipha kwami akonakalisi. Ngakho-ke, buthelani amagugu enu ezulwini, lapho kungonakalisi ukugqwala noma inundu, nalapho amasela engagqekezi khona noma ebe. Ngoba noma ngabe ikuphi ingcebo yenu, kuyoba lapho nezinhliziyo zenu. Bantabami, mina ngiyingcebo yenu, ngakho gcwalisani imicabango nezinhliziyo zenu kimi ngedwa ngenhliziyo eyodwa.

Aniwafundile yini amaZwi embula le mfihlakalo enkulu kaNkulunkulu? Ngoba sinalo leli gugu ezitsheni zebumba ukuze amandla alo amakhulu abonakale engakaNkulunkulu, hhayi awethu. Lowo uNkulunkulu abefuna ukumazisa ukuthi iyini ingcebo yenkazimulo yale mfihlakalo phakathi kwabeZizwe, onguKristu phakathi kwenu, ithemba lenkazimulo. Ngakho ningesabi, ngoba nizelwe kabusha ngomoya wami ongcwele; ngakho-ke, qondisisani amaZwi ezwi lami. Ngoba ngizonitshela ukuhlakanipha okuzoniduduza kunivikele ekudukisweni okunamandla okuthumbe umhlaba; ngoba umhlaba uboshelwe ukwahlulelwa futhi usesabeni olukhulu nosizi.

Ngalokho, ngize ukuzokhuluma nani ukuze ngifakazele abahlakaniphileyo phakathi kwenu ngabaholi ababili bomhlaba ababonakala manje bengabelusi abalungile, ngisho nezimvu ezikhethwe emhlambini ukuba zikhonze emisebenzini yomphakathi, ababonakala sengathi beze ukwelusa izimvu ezilahlekile zeBhabhiloni Eyinganekwane baziyise ekuphepheni. Kodwa laba belusi ababili abanomhlwenga abasiyona imvu, kodwa bayizilo ezimbi ezizifihle njengezimvu futhi bavela kwesinye isibaya, ngisho esikadrako ongumelusi. Udrako ongumelusi akayena umelusi weqiniso wezimvu, ngoba umhlambi wakhe awusiwona umhlambi kodwa yibandla labawiwe abawile enkazimulweni futhi sebeyizinto zonke ezingcolile ezifanele ukubhujiswa. Ngikhuluma ngenkohliso yamanje esetshenziselwa ukulungiselela ukuvela kwabathathu abangcwele. Ngoba kukhona abathathu abafakazayo ezulwini, uBaba, iZwi, noMoya ongcwele: futhi laba abathathu bamunye. Futhi kukhona abathathu abangcwele abasukumayo (ngisho nokulingisa okukhulu kwalabo abathathu abafakazayo ezulwini): udrako, isilo, nomprofethi wamanga (naye oyisilo). Futhi lezi zilo ezinonya zenzelwe ukubhujiswa, ngoba ikusasa lazo liwumlilo ovuthayo nesibabule kulelo chibi elikhulu lomlilo elivutha amalangabi amhlophe aluhlaza ebumnyameni obukhulu kuze kube phakade.

Ngikhuluma ngesimo sabo samanje nokubusa kwabo kwamanje, ngoba abakambulelwa ngokugcwele, ngoba umvimbeli usekhona; kodwa ngizobabonisa kwabakholekileyo bami, ngisho nalabo abanamehlo okubona nezindlebe zokuzwa. Qondani ukuthi laba bakhohlisi benza inkohliso enkulu ngisho nangaphambi kokwembulwa kwabo nokunikwa amandla kweqiniso, ngoba bazonikezwa igunya elivela kudrakon; ngoba lokho unikwe isikhathi esifushane kakhulu nangendlela elinganiselwe ukuhlola nokuvivinya umhlaba, nokulwa nabangcwele nokubanqoba. Futhi nakuba imfihlakalo yobubi isebenza ngamandla kulezi zinhloso, ukwembulwa kwabo akukakafiki kodwa sekusondele.

Ngoba ngisho namanje babonakala bengamadoda amakhulu, amadoda anomusa, amadoda esibindi nesihle, aphila izimpilo zokuzidela. Kodwa ningakhohliswa ngabelusi abanomhlwenga, ngoba babonakala behlanzekile futhi becwebile esixukwini esizovele simangale ngabo futhi maduze sizothi: Ubani ofana nesilo? (Ngoba ngisho namanje badukisa abalahlekileyo, abathakwe ngubuthakathi babo.) Kodwa kulabo abanamehlo okubona nezindlebe zokuzwa, babonakala beyilokho abayikho ngempela: ngoba bangabelusi abanomhlwenga abathola amandla abo kudrakon omkhulu, uSathane uqobo lwakhe. Ngoba omunye uyovela olwandle futhi njengamanje usebenza ngasese (ngemuva kwesithunzi sesidwedwe esidala esisekelwayo) ukuze kubhujiswe iBhabhiloni Eyinganekwane; kanti omunye uyovela emhlabeni futhi uthunywe ngokungemthetho kulabo abaseZweni Lesithembiso futhi uyoba ngumprofethi wamanga wesinagoge likaSathane, ngisho noMoshiach wabo wamanga.

Ngoba umprofethi wamanga oyisilo usebenzisana nesilo, esibusa ngasese, futhi bobabili basebenza ngokubambisana ukuze baqhubekisele phambili ngokugcwele imfihlakalo yobubi, ukuze maduze bambulwe; ngoba izikhathi zabo zifikile, nakuba bengenambuso okwamanje. Ningavumeli ukukhohliswa, ngoba umelusi oyedwa onomhlwenga obusa ngasese udlalisa isidwedwe esidala, kanti omunye umelusi onomhlwenga obonakala eshushiswa yisidwedwe esidala usebenzisana nomelusi onomhlwenga obusa ngasese. Ngoba bazenza izitha okwamanje, kodwa bonke bavela emhlambini ofanayo onomhlwenga, befeza izinhloso zikadrakon.

Ngosizi, abaningi abacabanga ukuthi basindisiwe ngokuvuma uJesu njengeNkosi ngenhliziyo engakholwayo ngezindebe zokungakholwa (ngoba ngenhliziyo umuntu ukholwa kuze kube ukulunga) bathakwe yilawo madimoni, futhi bazokhohlisa nabakhethiweyo bami uma kungenzeka, ngisho nalabo ababalandela ngokungazi. Ngoba laba belusi abacwebile, futhi izambatho zabo zenziwe ngoboya obungcolile nobugulayo, ngoba bangabelusi abanomhlwenga abelusa umhlambi onomhlwenga, futhi niyobazi ngezithelo zabo.

Abacwebile futhi abamhlophe njengeqhwa, njengezimvu zami; ngoba mina ngiyiWundlu elingenabala nasici, elakhethwa emhlambini ukuze libe yiPhasika eliphelele lesikhathi sonke. Ngihlanza izimvu zami egazini lami lokuhlawulela, futhi akukho mhlwenga otholakala kuzo. Nakuba bethi bathunywe nguNkulunkulu uSomandla, uNkulunkulu uSomandla akabathumanga. Nakuba abaprofethi abaningi bamanga befakaza ngamanga ngaye, angimthumanga, kodwa udrakon. Ngoba mina ngingumpristi ophakemeyo phakade ngokohlelo lukaMelkisedeki, futhi awekho amanye amawundlu ngaphandle kwami, uKristu Jesu.

Insindiso isegameni lami ngedwa, ngoba alikho elinye igama elinikezwe phakathi kwabantu abantu abangasindiswa ngalo ngaphandle kwelami, iNkosi uJesu. Ngoba akekho noyedwa umuntu engihlose ukuba amukele amaZwi ami ongeke awazwe. Ukuthi bayawamukela yini amazwi ami ngokholo noma bayawenqaba ngokungakholwa yisinqumo uBaba anikeze ngamunye amandla okusenza, ngoba uNkulunkulu akaziphoqi yena nothando lwakhe kumuntu namunye. Futhi ngiyeza ukuzokhipha isahlulelo phezu kwabo bonke futhi ngilahle bonke abangamesabi uNkulunkulu phakathi kwabo, ngazo zonke izenzo zabo zokungamesabi uNkulunkulu abazenze ngokungamesabi, nangawo wonke amazwi abo alukhuni, izoni ezingamesabi uNkulunkulu ezikhulume ngaye.

Ngikhulume nani ngezindlela eziningi, nasemisebenzini yami eminingi ngithumele abaprofethi bami nezinceku zami, ngoba isikhathi siphelile. Futhi ngakho-ke ngizokhuluma ngezinto ezingaqinisekisiwe ezizukulwaneni ezedlule kodwa manje sezembuliwe, ngoba izikhathi zokwembulwa kwami sezifikile. Ngoba sengizokwembulwa ngivela emazulwini, ngisho nami, ngoba nginguMesiya weqiniso, uYeshua HaMashiach. Futhi ngizokhuluma ngemifanekiso kulabo abangaphandle, kodwa labo abanamehlo okubona nezindlebe zokuzwa bayoqonda, ngoba ngizokuqinisa emoyeni wami odaliwe kubo.

Ngize ukuzokhuluma ngamazwi uJohane uMambuli awanikezwa, futhi ngizonenza indlela icace kini uma nizokwamukela amaZwi ami futhi nithobise izinhliziyo zenu ezwini lami.

Bhekani! Ngikhuluma ngababusi ababili abazayo abasebenza ngokubambisana, futhi bazokhohlisa umhlaba futhi bathathe abaningi nabo baye emlilweni wakwalasha. Ngisho nalabo amagama abo angabhalwanga eNcwadini Yokuphila bayobalandela futhi bayoshaywa ngokulunywa yinyoka, okwaxwayiswa ngakho umhlaba, ngisho nophawu okungekho muntu oyophulukiswa kulo; ngoba izexwayiso zingezokugcina, futhi lokho uNkulunkulu akushilo akunakuguqulwa.

Laba baholi, ngisho nabelusi abanomhlwenga, njengamanje bazenza izisebenzi zikahulumeni zaseBhabhiloni Eyinganekwane. Ngaphansi kwezambatho zabelusi abanomhlwenga abazigqokile (ngoba ngamunye uzifihle ngendlela yakhe), bayizilo ezihahayo ezingasoze zaneliseka ukukhanuka kwazo, futhi bazofuna ukuba bonke babakhonze nomfanekiso wesilo nodrakon kube sengathi banguNkulunkulu; ngoba sebezochithwa lapho umvimbeli esusiwe.

Ngoba maduzane kakhulu futhi ngokuzumayo, lapho ngibutha ibandla lami kimi emafwini, ukubhujiswa okuzumayo kuzofika emhlabeni; ngoba bazobhubhisa iBhabhiloni Eyimfihlo, futhi impi enkulu izosabalala ezweni lonke futhi ukubhujiswa okuzumayo kuzofika emhlabeni. Laba abathathu abangcwele bayovuka emlotheni wamanxiwa nesiphithiphithi esishiywe ngabamahhashi, abazokhululwa maduzane ngemva kokuba uMlobokazi wami ethathwe aye kimi emazulwini. (Ngoba yikhona kuphela ngizovula izimpawu zesivumelwano sami ngoba ibandla lami lisindisiwe olakeni oluzayo, futhi ngizobahlenga.) Bazovuka emlotheni wempi, isifo, indlala, nokungabi nabulungiswa okukhulu, nasekufeni kwezixuku eziningi, eziningi.

Bazothembisa ukuthula nokuphepha, futhi bazokhuluma amanga amaningi abazowadlulisela njengokuhlakanipha kukaNkulunkulu, futhi bazokhuluma izinto eziningi ezihlambalazayo nezilahlayo; futhi omunye uzokhomba omunye, futhi esandleni sabo kuyoba yisixazululo sabo, ngisho nobuthi babo obungenakuhlehliswa noma buthethelelwe. Ngoba wonke umuntu ozinikela ekulunyweni yinyoka, uyohlala ephawulwe kuze kube phakade. Kodwa leyo kuyoba yintengo yokungena emphakathini wabo omusha, ngisho nomthetho wabo omusha owenziwe nguSathane nezingelosi zakhe abawenzela ngokuyisiwula inzalo yabo enengekayo.

Bazothembisa umphakathi omusha ongenakufa nokubhujiswa okuzayo emhlabeni wonke, lapho ukubhujiswa okuzumayo kufika ekubuthaneni kwebandla lami. (Bafuna ukuvuselela inkathi yabo yegolide uNkulunkulu ayibhubhisa kuzamcolo kaNowa.) Bazothembisa ukuthula, impilo ende, inkululeko ezifweni, impilo nokuphepha, futhi bazothembisa usuku olusha. Kodwa laba belusi ababili abanomhlwenga (ababukeka sengathi bavela emhlambini) bazothi uNkulunkulu uSomandla ubathumile, futhi umhlaba wonke uyomangala ngabo, kodwa ababona abelusi. Ngiyanibuza, udrako angalusa umhlambi? Nini lapho udrako ake anakekela izimvu, ngaphandle kokubeka uphawu lokubhujiswa kwazo okuphelele nokuhlukaniswa noBaba Wezibani, uYahweh? Ngoba isilo nomprofethi wamanga bazothola amandla abo kudrakon, futhi bayizigqila zezinkanuko nentando kadrakon.

Ngesikhathi sokwembulwa kwabo, bazokhipha izifihla-buso zabo zokuba yizisebenzi zikahulumeni eziphansi; futhi konke ukuzazisa, ngisho nezambatho zabo ezinomhlwenga zezikhumba zezimvu ezinomhlwenga kanye nemvelo yabo yangempela, amandla, nezinhloso zizokwembulwa. Izwe liyomangala ngabo nangezindlela zabo, ngisho nokubakhonza kuze kube sekubhujisweni kwalo. Bayongena esiteji somhlaba ngamandla amakhulu, futhi umfelandawonye wamakhosi ayishumi uyobusa. Ngisho namanje, umfelandawonye usuvele uhlele amabutho awo ngasese ukuze uhlasele ngasese iBhabhiloni Eyimfihlo. Bazilungiselele ngasese, futhi ngokuqinisekile bahlele ukuhlasela, futhi bahlasele obala iBhabhiloni Eyimfihlo ngokwesahlulelo esimenyezelwe nguNkulunkulu esizomehlela, angeke aphinde avuke kuso.

Ngoba ububi bakhe bufikile phambi koPhezukonke, nezono zakhe phambi kwesihlalo sobukhosi esikhulu sasezulwini, ngisho naphambi kukaNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade, ngoba akekho omkhulu; futhi isahlulelo simenyezelwe kuye. Ngoba u-El Shaddai usebenzisa nezandla zezitha zakhe ukufeza amacebo akhe ngisho nezitha zakhe zilalela izwi lakhe; ngoba ngubani ongamelana noNkulunkulu uSomandla? Ungenzi iphutha, abelusi ababili abanomhlwenga njengamanje basebenzisana ngemuva kwesithombe sesidwedwe. Abakembulwa okwamanje emhlabeni, futhi lapho bembulwa, umhlaba uyomangala ngabo futhi ubalandele ekufeni kwabo. Nokho abasiyona imvu nhlobo, kodwa bayizilo ezithukuthele ngaphakathi ezizothi maduze zinikwe amandla amakhulu ngudrako, ngoba lokho kunikezwe yena isikhashana esifushane. Ngoba bazoziphakamisa ngokumelene, nasesikhundleni, somelusi omuhle; ngaphandle kwalabo abanokuqonda okungokomoya abazobona umhlwenga, ngisho nobubi nokonakala okuphelele okungaphakathi kubo, ngoba banemvelo engahlanzekile futhi bamabi ekujuleni kobuntu babo. Usuwazi ukuthi bazenza bani, kodwa anazi ukuthi bangobani ngempela, futhi bazinikeze amagama angamanga, ngakho-ke inombolo ayinakubalwa.

Ngoba isilo esiyovela olwandle ukubusa izizwe sase sikhona, futhi sibonakala sengathi manje asikho kodwa empeleni sisekhona, siqondisa izandla zikanopopi ongenangqondo. Lo ngulowo oqanjwe ngegama lesiHebheru kusetshenziswa amagama engawakhuluma lapho ngibona uSathane njengombani uwa ezulwini. Umbani uwa ezulwini: fundisisa ukuze ufunde. Ngoba akafisi abesifazane, futhi akahloniphi nkulunkulu: ngoba uyozikhulisa ngaphezu kwakho konke. Ngiyanibuza manje nibone ukuthi indoda iyehelelwa yini? Nokho umkayo ubonakala engowesifazane onezingane, nakuba zingezona ezakhe.

Isilo esiyovela emhlabeni usuyasazi, futhi iningi lenu liyamlandela ngokungazi, selikhohlisiwe lapho kufanele ngabe nishumayela ngokumelene nenkohliso. Ngoba ungowezwe, kodwa esizweni sakhe akekho namunye obekwe uphawu lwabayi-144,000. Futhi ngoDani wathi, uDani uyibhubesi elincane: uyogxuma eBashani, futhi ungowesizwe sakwaDani, ngisho nokuziqhenya ngohlu lozalo lwakhe, ngoba igazi eliluhlaza ligeleza emithanjeni yakhe. Uhlonzwa njengoKoresi wosuku lwanamuhla futhi uyoba ngumakhi wethempeli ukuze alethe isinengiso sencithakalo ngesikhathi sakhe, futhi njengamanje useyakha eZweni Lesithembiso isinagoge likaSathane.

Bantwanyana bami, nginishiya nokuhlakanipha eningakuzindla ngakho, futhi lezi akuseyona izimfihlakalo kodwa sezembuliwe ngesikhathi sazo. Yizikhathi zabo kuphela ezingeke zembulwe emhlabeni kuze kususwe umvimbeli, futhi kuzokwenzeka ngokushesha okukhulu kunalokho enikucabangayo. Ngoba lezi zinto sezizokwenzeka, futhi asisekho isikhathi, ngoba izinombolo ziwile ebusweni bewashi. Hlalani nithembekile kimi, futhi lapho sengikuqinisekisile emoyeni wenu ukuthi bangobani, phendulani abafowenu. Bhekani, ukubonakala kwami sekuzophela.

NginguLizwi kaNkulunkulu owenziwa inyama, futhi ngihlala phakathi kwenu.

Nginibizela ekhaya lenu emazulwini maduzane kakhulu.

NgiyiWundlu elingenabala nasici.

Ngingumpristi omkhulu kuze kube phakade ngokohlelo lukaMelkisedeki. NginguMesiya weqiniso, uYeshua HaMashiach.

Ngize ukuzokhipha isahlulelo kubo bonke.

Ngize ukuzokhuluma ngamazwi uJohane uMambuli awanikezwa.

Bhekani, mina, uKristu Jesu, ngithumele la mazwi kwabakholekileyo bami; manje yizwani amazwi ami!
