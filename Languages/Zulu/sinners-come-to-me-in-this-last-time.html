Ufakazi Omabili: IBandla
Emisebenzini KaPawulu Umphostoli
May 10, 2024

Zoni! Wozani Kimi Kulesi Sikhathi Sokugcina!

Zwanini Amazwi ami, wona Amazwi kaJesu waseNazaretha, ngoba ngiza kini ngomoya wothando, ububele, ukuthobeka, umusa, kakhulukazi isihawu. Ngoba masinyane umusa wami nesihawu kuzothathelwa indawo usizi olukhulu, lona kanye usizi lukaJakobe, futhi yonke le nkathi yeminyaka eyisikhombisa ngeke ifane nanoma yini umhlaba owake wayibona, kusukela ekuqaleni kokudalwa. Kuzoqala ngenhlekelele engazelele nokushushiswa okuyokwanda kuphela. Ngakho-ke, ngiza okokugcina ezonini ezingakaphenduki emuseni nasesihawini sami. Ngoba kusekhona umzuzwana nje osele wokuphenduka kwenu uma niza kimi, uJesu Kristu, nivume ukuthi ngiyiNkosi yenu futhi nikholwe ezinhliziyweni zenu ukuthi uNkulunkulu ungivusile kwabafileyo. Ngelulele izingalo zami ngomusa nangesihawu ezweni elifayo isikhathi sonke somusa manje, futhi abaningi bangenze iNkosi yabo, abaningi njengalabo iNkosi uNkulunkulu wethu ababizile nabakhethile ngaphambi kokusekelwa komhlaba ukuze bambulwe kimi, uJesu Kristu iNkosi enamandla: Ngoba ngiyiNkosi yamakhosi neNkosi yamakhosi.

Futhi ngibizile abakhethiweyo, nabo bonke abathembekile ensindisweni yami. Baningi abasindisiwe! Usindiswe kuphi, uyabuza? Usindiswe ekwahlulelweni nasekubhujisweni osekuzokhululwa phezu kwalo mhlaba, onkulunkulu bawo, ababusi bawo, imibuso yawo, nabo bonke abalandela uSathane, ngoba ungunkulunkulu walo mhlaba futhi ukwahlulelwa kwakhe sekufikile. Ngisho nokusindiswa ekwahlulelweni kwesihlalo esikhulu esimhlophe nasechibini lomlilo elilungiselelwe uDeveli nezingelosi zakhe. Ukwahlulelwa sekudluliselwe kubabusi balobu bumnyama bamanje, futhi sekuzofinyelela esiphelweni nasekuqedweni, ngoba umhlaba uzobuswa yisandla sami ngokulunga, ngoba isikhathi sami sesifikile sokuba ngembulwe, futhi umhlaba ungowami nokugcwala kwawo, mina kanye, uYeshua HaMashiach!

Ukwahlulela kuyeza futhi kukhona kakade kodwa kungakagcwaliseki. Futhi abaningi benu basakholelwa ukuthi banesikhathi sokuzitika ezonweni zenu futhi nifune ukwaneliseka kwezinkanuko zenu eziyisono ezivela enyameni yenu eyonakele. Qondani ukuthi anginaso isifiso sokwahlulela nokulahla noma yimuphi wenu, ngoba nginikele ngokuphila kwami othandweni lwami ngani nangenkokhelo ephelele yezono zenu ngobuhlungu obukhulu nokuhlushwa esiphambanweni! Ngoba alukho sono olulodwa olungakhokhelwanga ngokugcwele ngomhlatshelo wami wokubuyisana njengePhasika eliphelele. NgiyiWundlu likaNkulunkulu elisusa izono zomhlaba. Futhi angizanga ukuzonilahla, kodwa ngilapha ukuzonisindisa. Umhlaba njengoba niwazi usuzoshintsha kakhulu kangangokuthi ngeke nikwazi ukubona ukuthi yini ezovuka emlotheni, ngoba ingxenye enkulu yomhlaba isizoshiswa ngamalangabi futhi ibhubhe ngezindlela eziningi ekwahluleleni kukaYahweh okulungile. Futhi usizi olukhulu luphuma kulo mlotha.

Ngoba umbusi walo mhlaba wahlulelwe, futhi ukwahlulelwa kwakhe kuzogcwaliseka futhi kupheleliswe ngokugcwele. Ngoba uSathane wenze ububi obuningi futhi izindlela zakhe ziyanengeka, futhi uza kuphela ukuzokweba, ukubulala, nokubhubhisa. Wonakalisile isintu ngokugcwele nangezindlela ezihlukahlukene, futhi ufuna ukubhubhisa konke uNkulunkulu akukhathalelelayo. Ngoba uNkulunkulu walithanda izwe kangaka, waze wanikela ngeNdodana yakhe ezelwe yodwa, ukuba yilowo nalowo okholwa yimi angabhubhi, kodwa abe nokuphila okuphakade. Ngakho qondisisani ngokugcwele ukuthi ngiyiwundlu lePhasika lokugcina lazo zonke izikhathi (ngeke kube khona elinye), futhi ngikhokhele zonke izono. USathane wonakalise izakhi zofuzo zesintu kusukela ensimini yase-Edeni, lapho alinga u-Adamu no-Eva ukuba bamukele isithelo sakhe esibolile futhi bakholwe ulwazi lwakhe olungenalusizo, ngoba ulwazi lwakhe luletha ukufa kubo bonke abadlayo. Ngenxa yalesi sizathu uNkulunkulu uSomandla unikeze isivumelwano sokuthula endalweni yakhe, yebo, mina, uJesu Kristu.

Ngoba lithini izwi labase-Efesu, lapho uPawulu ebabhalela mayelana nemisebenzi yami eqediwe (ngoba ngasho ekuphefumuleni kwami kokugcina esiphambanweni, "Sekuphelile")! Kodwa manje, kuKristu Jesu, nina enake nikude enziwe naseduze ngegazi likaKristu.

Ngoba nguye ukuthula kwethu, owenze kokubili kwaba kunye, futhi odilize udonga olwaluyisivimbelo phakathi kwethu. Zwanini izwi lami. Ngiyikuthula kwenu, ngoba umthetho walahla isono enyameni; yona kanye inyama negazi lenu lonakele esonweni, ngisho nokungcola kusukela e-Edeni. Mina, uJesu Kristu, emhlatshelweni wami wokubuyisana eKalvari ngidilize udonga oluphakathi oluhlukanisayo phakathi komJuda nowezizwe, ngenza bobabili baba muntu munye omusha, ngisho nokubadala kabusha ekulungeni nasebungcweleni beqiniso kubo bonke abamukela ukuthula ngokumemezela mina njengeNkosi yabo evukile!

Kodwa kukhona okunye okuvela kwabase-Efesu (kube sengathi lokhu akukuhle ngokwanele), ngakho-ke abase-Efesu bayaqhubeka: futhi ngesikhathi esifanayo wasusa ngenyama yakhe imbangela yenzondo (okungukuthi, umthetho wemiyalo oqukethwe emthethweni obhaliwe) ukuze enze kwababili kube muntu munye omusha kuye, ngokwenza kanjalo ukuthula: futhi abuyisanise bobabili noNkulunkulu emzimbeni munye ngesiphambano sakhe, wabulala kanjalo inzondo. Ngizwe, o muntu owenqabile ubizo lwami: Ngikubiza manje kule mizuzwana yokugcina, futhi isikhathi sesicishe siphelile! Yamukela mina njengomnikelo wakho wokuthula, ngoba ngikunika mahhala insindiso nokuhlengwa emizimbeni yenu eyonakele, yebo, ukuphila okuphakade kimi, uKristu Jesu. Ungaphazami: Zonke izipho ezinhle, nazo zonke izipho eziphelele zivela phezulu, zehla zivela kuYise wezibani, kuye okungekho ukuguquguquka, ngisho nesithunzi sokuphenduka.

Zwanini izwi lami niqonde ukuthi noma yini enhle ezimpilweni zenu, noma yini eyigugu nobuhle, ingekaBaba futhi inikwe nguBaba wezibani ukuze nibe nokugcwala kokuphila futhi nize kimi, iNkosi uJesu, njengomsindisi wenu siqu. Futhi ukuze nazi ukuthi kukhona ukuphila okuphakade okwenzelwe nina uma nje namukela. Ingabe nizizonda kangaka nize nizimisele ukudela konke enikukhathalelelayo empilweni enikunikiwe nguBaba ukuze nanelise izinkanuko zenu zobugovu? Ingabe izono zenu zibaluleke kangaka kini nize nizokhetha ukulahlwa okuphakade noSathane kunobuhle bukaNkulunkulu nobusha bokuphila kimi, uKristu Jesu? Ingabe izono zenu zibaluleke kangako kini?

Ngiyanibiza, o muntu wemvelo, futhi nginimemela ekuthuleni nasekuphileni okuphakade, impilo engenakho ukugula nesono, yebo impilo engenazo izifo nobuhlungu benhliziyo, impilo engenakho ukuhlupheka lapho ningeke nisabuka labo enibakhathalelelayo behlupheka; ngoba izinto zangaphambili zizodlula masinyane. Asisekho isikhathi sokwanelisa izono zenu. Ngoba abaningi benu bawuthathele phansi umusa kaNkulunkulu futhi bakholwa amanga esela, elifuna ukuntshontsha izimpilo zenu kanye ukuze nihlanganyele nalo esijezisweni salo saphakade. Ningakhohliseki. Ukwenqaba mina, uJesu waseNazaretha (yebo lowo owazinikela ngenxa yezono zenu nensindiso), kuwukwenqaba ukuphila nokukhetha ingunaphakade ukufa kwesibili uSathane nezingelosi zakhe abalahlelwe kukho: Ngoba abanayo intethelelo, futhi abayikuthola ukuphumula, futhi abanakho ukuthula. Futhi ichibi lomlilo lilungiselelwe uDeveli nezingelosi zakhe.

Ukwenqaba umhlatshelo wami kuwukwenqaba ukuphila okuphakade nokwenqaba uNkulunkulu ngokugcwele. Ngakho-ke, ngeke kube khona isihawu, umusa, uthando, ukukhanya, noma ukuthula kodwa ukuzisola kuphela, ubumnyama, nokuhlushwa lapho niyovuna khona isithelo esibolile senyama yenu kuze kube phakade. Aniboni yini ukuthi lokhu kuwubuwula obungakanani? Ngikhokhele izono zenu ngegazi lami lokubuyisana! Kodwa niyanqaba izindlela zami, nakuba nginibiza njengamanje futhi kwabaningi okokugcina. Niyanqaba uthando lwami, nakuba inhliziyo yami isadabukela nina nemindeni yenu, futhi nginganisindisa ngokuphelele uma nje niphenduka futhi niyeka izono zenu. Aniqondi yini ukuthi ngahlupheka njengesoni esiphansi kunazo zonke emhlatshelweni wami wokubuyisana ukuze nginiphakamisele kuNkulunkulu ensindisweni? Ngoba ngaphakanyiswa esiphambanweni ukuze ngisindise imiphefumulo yenu futhi nginibuyisele kuBaba, futhi bengilokhu ngingenasono, ngakho yamukelani igazi lami elingenasono!

Ngoba ithini imiBhalo? Ngoba ngokuqinisekile nizwile ukuthi uNkulunkulu walithanda izwe kangaka waze wanikela ngeNdodana yakhe ezelwe yodwa: ukuba yilowo nalowo okholwa kuye angabhubhi, kodwa abe nokuphila okuphakade. Ngiyazi ukuthi lawa mazwi anijwayele, ngoba ngisho nabangcebelezi benu abanigcina ekugqilazekeni kuSathane bawasebenzise kabi amazwi ami ngokuhlambalaza. Kodwa bekungaba kuhle ukuba niqhubeke nokufunda, ngakho ngicela nizwe izwi lami! Ngoba uNkulunkulu akayithumelanga iNdodana yakhe ezweni ukuzolahla izwe: kodwa ukuba izwe ngayo lisindiswe. Lowo okholwa kuye, akalahlwa: kodwa lowo ongakholwa, usevele elahliwe, ngoba akakholwanga eGameni leNdodana ezelwe yodwa kaNkulunkulu.

Ingabe inkulumo yami ayicacile ngokwanele? Ingabe amazwi ami awazwakali kalula? Uma ningenqaba mina, iNkosi uJesu, iWundlu likaNkulunkulu, niyohlala unomphela nesitha sami, labo abanecala lokuchitha igazi elingenacala: Ngoba ukungenqaba mina kuwukuhlanganyela ekuhlubukeni kukaSathane, ngoba uDeveli wonile kusukela ekuqaleni. Futhi lokhu kuwukwahlulelwa, ukuthi ukukhanya kufikile ezweni, futhi abantu bathanda ubumnyama kunokukhanya, ngoba izenzo zabo zazimbi. Kunjalo neqiniso ngani, o muntu wemvelo, ngisho nalabo kini abangawenqaba umoya wami wokuphila, futhi ngaphandle kwakho konke lokhu ngisanibiza, nakuba lolu kuwubizo lokugcina ngaphambi kokuba kuqale ukwahlulelwa. Ngoba asisekho isikhathi. Ingabe izono zenu zibaluleke kangaka kini nize nizochitha ingunaphakade ebumnyameni bangaphandle nasesijezisweni kulelo chibi elikhulu lomlilo elilungiselelwe uDeveli nezingelosi zakhe? Ngoba uNkulunkulu akasifisi lesi sijeziso kini futhi ungithumele njengomnikelo wakhe wokuthula ukuba nginibuyisele kuye ngegazi lami lokubuyisana.

Zwanini amazwi ami namukele ukuthula kwami. Kubo bonke abezwa lawa mazwi, yazini ukuthi mina, uJesu waseNazaretha, ngiyanibiza; futhi anginandaba nokuthi yiziphi izono ezinibophayo ngoba ngizikhokhele zonke. Qondani ukuthi ukwamukela uphawu lwesilo ngeke kuthethelelwe, futhi ngizama ukunisindisa kulesi silingo (nakuba uphawu lungekho okwamanje); ngoba abaningi bazobulawa ngenxa yokwenqaba. Kodwa qondani lokhu okulandelayo: Anikwazi ukwehlela phansi kakhulu kangangokuthi ngingakwazi ukunifinyelela; kuphela ningalwamukeli uphawu. Ngisho nabazidlayo abakwazi ukuziphakamisa esonweni kakhulu kangangokuthi ngingakwazi ukubehlisela ensindisweni ngobumnene futhi ngibavale ngomoya wami ukuze ngibaphakamise! Ngoba ngikhokhele zonke izono zesintu zazo zonke izikhathi, futhi angishiyanga lutho lungenziwanga. Ziningi kakhulu izono, kodwa ngizobiza abanye benu ngokuqondile ngezici zenu ezimbi. Yazini ukuthi ngizothethelela zonke izono zenu hhayi lezi kuphela, futhi ngizonihlambulula ngokugcwele ngegazi lami kuzo zonke izono zenu.

Kumlutha wotshwala nezidakamizwa, ngithi: Phendukelani kimi manje esikhathini saphakade sokucaca futhi nizwe amazwi ami, ngoba uma nizolalela ngokukholwa, amazwi ami azoletha ukukhanya okukhazimulayo kwensindiso kini. Amazwi ami azobopha amademoni anenza niphuze futhi nisebenzise izidakamizwa, nakuba ningawenza abangane benu. Ngininika ukucaca okuyisimangaliso ukuze nikwazi ukunquma ukuthi yikuphi enizoba nakho kuze kube phakade: Ukuphila okuphakade nami, uJesu Kristu, ebunye obuphelele noNkulunkulu uqobo? Noma ukulahlwa kwaphakade emlilweni ongacimi? Ingabe isono enisibambe ngobugovu esandleni senu, ngisho nokuluthwa kwenu utshwala nezidakamizwa, sibaluleke kangaka nize nizokhetha ukubhujiswa, nize nidlulisele isiphetho senu kulabo enibakhathalelelayo abanilandela esikhundleni sami?

Anikho phansi kakhulu kangangokuthi ngingeke ngakwazi ukuniphakamisa, futhi ngifisa kakhulu ukunithethelela, kodwa kufanele niphendule izwi lami futhi niphume ezindleleni zenu ngokuphenduka. Chithani utshwala benu niyeke ukuzifaka ubuthi, futhi nize kimi ngokuphenduka, futhi ngizonikhulula. Asikho isikhathi sokumosha; ningangizibi futhi ningene ekudakweni nikhohlwe amazwi ami, ngoba amazwi enu enqaba azoqoshwa emazulwini njengobufakazi obumelene nani ngosuku lokwahlulelwa, futhi ngeke niphunyuke!

Kumbulali, ngithi: Yekani ngokushesha ubudlova benu bokubulala labo abenziwe ngomfanekiso kaNkulunkulu, bona kanye abantu bakini, ngoba lokhu kuyisono esibi. Ngiqinisile, ngeqiniso elingenakuguqulwa, nizibulele nina ngayo yonke impilo eniyithathile, futhi ngaphandle kwami alikho ikhambi esijezisweni senu esizayo. Kodwa ngisho nokubulala kwenu kwakhokhelwa ngokugcwele emhlatshelweni wami, ngoba angishiyanga lutho lungenziwanga. Ngibonakalise umusa wami empilweni yoMphostoli omkhulu uPawulu njengofakazi wesikhuthazo, o nina babulali.

Ngoba ngisho noSawulu wayekhohlisiwe futhi ebheke ekwahlulelweni okukhulu endleleni eya eDamaseku, lapho ngamsindisa khona kuye uqobo. UPawulu washushisa, walimaza, futhi wavuma futhi wabulala iBandla lami elingcwele! Ngoba uyavuma lokho kuZenzo: Futhi ngashushisa le ndlela kwaze kwaba sekufeni, ngibopha futhi ngifaka ejele kokubili amadoda nabesifazane. Kodwa wasamukela isipho sami sokuphila okuphakade futhi wenza mina, uJesu Kristu, iNkosi yakhe futhi wangikhonza ngokwethembeka njengesibonelo sokuphenduka kubo bonke, futhi ngamphakamisa kakhulu ngenxa yokulalela kwakhe. Ngikhokhele izindlela zenu zokubulala uma nizophenduka kuzo futhi nize kimi ngokuphenduka kweqiniso, ngoba uma nenza kanjalo manje, ngizonamukela ngomusa nangesihawu. Ningakhohliswa; asikho isikhathi sokumosha.

Kwabaphansi kakhulu, ngithi: Ngisho nanenzele indlela yokuphuma ekuhlanyeni nasekudidekeni kwenu, ngoba nginikele kini ezinkanukweni zenu ezinyanyekayo njengoba kwabaseRoma kumenyezela: Ngenxa yalokhu uNkulunkulu wabanikela ezinkanukweni ezinyanyekayo: ngoba ngisho nabesifazane babo bashintsha ukusetshenziswa kwemvelo bakushintshela kulokho okuphambene nemvelo: Kanjalo namadoda, eshiya ukusetshenziswa kwemvelo kowesifazane, ashisana ekunxanelaneni kwawo, amadoda namadoda enza okungafanele, ezamukelela wona umvuzo ofanele wephutha lawo.

Phumani ekungcoleni kwenu, futhi niphume ekuzigqajeni kwenu okungenamsebenzi, futhi niyeke ukuhlambalaza uphawu olunikezwe uNowa nazo zonke izidalwa eziphilayo, lona kanye uthingo oluzibonakalisa emafwini emvula. Uthingo lwenzelwe ukuba izwe lazi ukuthi uNkulunkulu ngeke aphinde abhubhise izwe ngamanzi, nakuba ukwahlulela ngomlilo sekuseduze. Wozani kimi niphenduke, niguqule izindlela zenu futhi nibize igama lami, ningivume njengeNkosi ngenhliziyo egcwele ukholo ekuvukeni kwami, futhi ngizonamukela. Sheshani! Ngoba aninaso isikhathi sokucabanga, ngoba ukwahlulela sekuzokhululwa. Asikho isikhathi sokumosha.

Zwanini amazwi ami nize kimi, ngoba angithokozi ekwahlulelweni nasekubhujisweni okuzayo, noYahweh akanjalo, cha, ngisho noNkulunkulu uSomandla! Ngoba u-Adamu wadalwa ngothando lukaBaba nangekupheleleni kokulunga nobungcwele beqiniso. UNkulunkulu wamnika impilo ephelele engantuli lutho. Futhi kanjalo uSathane wahlasela indalo wonakalisa inyama yenu kanye nemvelo yenu ukuze niphambuke esizalweni, ngoba isono sisegazini lenu: Ukuphila kwenyama kusegazini. Nokho uNkulunkulu, ngesihe sakhe esikhulu, ngoba umusa wakhe umi phakade, ngisho noNkulunkulu uSomandla wanginika mina, iNdodana yakhe ezelwe yodwa, njengomhlatshelo ophelele ozonisindisa ngokupheleleyo uma nje ningeza kimi ngokuphenduka ezonweni zenu. Ngizochitha imisebenzi kaSathane ezimpilweni zenu uma niza kimi. Ngiyanincenga, wozani, ngoba izingalo zami ngeke zisahlala zeluliwe isikhathi eside!

Kusele isikhathi esincane kakhulu, futhi usizi lukaJakobe seluzoqhuma emhlabeni wonke, nezinhliziyo zabantu zizobaphela amandla ngenxa yobukhulu balokho okuzayo. USathane ulungiselele izinengiso eziningi azozidedela, ngoba ukwenqaba ubizo lwami kuwukukhetha umbuso kaSathane wokufa nokubhujiswa; futhi ngizonishiyela ekukhetheni kwenu, futhi ngizoninika ubumnyama nosizi eniluthanda kangaka, futhi niyodla izithelo zezindlela zenu ngaphandle kokuba niphenduke. Ngoba yilokhu-ke, lolu wubizo lokugcina ngaphambi kokuba ukwahlulelwa kufike ngokushesha. Ngisho nezinceku zami nabakhonzi bami bayakhala kuze kube semikhawulweni yomhlaba ngalo kanye lo mzuzu, ngaphandle kokuthi bayokhulekela ukusindiswa kwenu baze bahlwithelwe kimi emafwini lapho ngiza kumakoti wami emoyeni maduzane, futhi lokhu kuzokwenzeka ngokungalindelekile, nakuba nginitshela ngokusobala ngaphambi kokuba kwenzeke. Bese kuphela ubizo lwami lokugcina lomusa nesihe. Futhi lapho ngihlwithela umakoti wami ekuphepheni, ukubhujiswa okuzumayo kuzogqashuka emhlabeni wonke, futhi abaningi benu abezwa la mazwi ngeke basinde. Abanye benu bacabanga ngobuwula ukuthi ningaqhubeka nokuhlukumeza umusa wami futhi uzoqhubeka phakade, kodwa iqiniso elidabukisayo liwukuthi umusa uzophenduka ukwahlulelwa, futhi kuzokwenzeka ngokuzumayo, futhi abaningi ngeke basinde.

Kulabo abangabulawa yilokhu kubhujiswa okuzumayo okuzokwethulwa maduze eBhabhiloni Eyinganekwane bayozithola besemhlabeni abangakaze bawuphuphe, umhlaba lapho kunokufa nokubhujiswa okuqhubekayo, ngoba uSathane uzosusa ngokuhlelekile noma yini ephathelene nami, uJesu Kristu, futhi uzokwenza lokho ngaphandle kwesihe. Kuyoba nokuhlushwa ezweni lonke nonya olungenangqondo, futhi ukuza kimi ngalesi sikhathi sokwahlulelwa nokuvivinywa kuyoba nzima kakhulu; kodwa noma ngubani obiza igama leNkosi uyosindiswa, futhi kuyomelwe bakhuthazele kuze kube sekupheleni, futhi abaningi bayonqunywa amakhanda, futhi abaningi bayokufa kabuhlungu kakhulu.

Kodwa ngisho nangaleso sikhathi, uNkulunkulu uyosula zonke izinyembezi emehlweni abo, ngoba uJohane uMambuli wamemezela ngabo wathi: Laba yibo abaphuma osizini olukhulu, futhi bahlanza izivatho zabo, bazenza zaba mhlophe ngegazi leWundlu. Ngakho-ke, baphambi kwesihlalo sobukhosi sikaNkulunkulu, futhi bamkhonza imini nobusuku ethempelini lakhe: futhi lowo ohlezi esihlalweni sobukhosi uyohlala phakathi kwabo. Abasayikulamba futhi, abasayikoma futhi, futhi iLanga alisayikukhanya phezu kwabo, nanoma yikuphi ukushisa. Ngoba iWundlu, eliphakathi nesihlalo sobukhosi, liyobondla, futhi liyobaholela emthonjeni wamanzi ophilayo: futhi uNkulunkulu uyosula zonke izinyembezi emehlweni abo.

Mina, ngisho nami, uKristu Jesu, iNkosi evusiwe futhi yenyuka, ngisho noMesiya weqiniso: Ngiyanibiza, futhi ithemba lami nokulindela kwami wukuthi nizokwamukela umnikelo wami wokuthula nize ensindisweni yami ukuze niphile phakade nami esifubeni sikaBaba! Kuphela ningalenqabi ubizo lwami, ngoba ngeke ngisabiza isikhathi eside, futhi ngeke ngiphinde ngibize ngomusa nesihe ngale ndlela, ngoba isikhathi siphelile (nakuba umusa nesihe kunami phakade). Ngiyanicela, wozani kimi ukuze nginisindise kule mizuzwana yokugcina yomusa. Ngoba anihlukile kulabo abakholwa ekuqaleni kwenkathi yami yomusa, futhi insindiso nomvuzo wami kuyoba okwenu uma nilalela izwi lami khona manje. Manje yisikhathi sensindiso! Phendukani nize kimi ngokuthobeka. Ngenzeni iNkosi yenu futhi nazi ukuthi ngiphila kuze kube phakade, ngoba uNkulunkulu wangivusa kwabafileyo ukuze ngingaphinde ngife. Uma namukela umnikelo wami, niyobe ninqobile, futhi niyokwamukela imivuzo yomnqobi:

Bheka, ngimi emnyango, ngiyangqongqoza: uma umuntu ezwa izwi lami, avule umnyango, ngiyongena kuye, ngidle naye, naye adle nami. Onqobayo, ngiyomnika ukuba ahlale nami esihlalweni sami sobukhosi, njengoba nami nganqoba, futhi ngahlala noBaba esihlalweni sakhe sobukhosi. Funda lokhu ngokuphindaphindiwe, ngoba ngikunikeza ithuba lokugcina lokusindiswa ngomusa. Kuphela ungalwenqabi ubizo lwami, ngoba asisekho isikhathi sezinto ezinjalo. Kwabaningi benu, ukungamukeli mina kuyoba okokugcina nokwaphakade, futhi ngeke nilulame futhi ngeke nisinde ekwahlulelweni okuzayo, ngoba ukubhujiswa okuzumayo kuphezu kwenu. Futhi uMoya noMlobokazi bathi, Woza. Nalowo ozwayo makathi, Woza. Nalowo owomileyo makeze. Futhi noma ngubani othandayo, makathathe amanzi okuphila ngesihle.

Futhi ekugcineni: Lowo ongalungile, makabe ngongalungile namanje: nalowo ongcolile, makabe ngongcolile namanje: nalowo olungileyo, makabe ngolungileyo namanje: nalowo ongcwele, makabe ngongcwele namanje. Futhi bheka, ngiyeza masinyane, nomvuzo wami unami, ukuze nginike umuntu ngamunye njengokomsebenzi wakhe. Kanjalo makwenziwe.
