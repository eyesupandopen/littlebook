Ubufakazi Obumbaxambili: IBandla
Emisebenzini KaJohane Umambuli
Juni 04, 2024

Bhekani! Ukwahlulela Kuyeza Futhi Izikhathi Yimanje!

Izikhathi sezifikele ukugcwaliseka, nina bantwanyana bami, impela ukugcwaliseka kwazo zonke izinto eziphathelene neviki lamashumi ayisikhombisa likaDaniyeli. Ngoba iviki lamashumi ayisikhombisa likaDaniyeli liyiviki leminyaka lapho usuku ngalunye luwunyaka. Iviki lamashumi ayisikhombisa likaDaniyeli selizogcwaliseka futhi liyoqala lapho abathembekileyo eBandleni lami behlwithelwa phezulu ukuze bangihlangabeze emafwini. Icilongo lokugcina liyokhala futhi ngezwi elinamandla lengelosi enkulu abafileyo kuKristu bayovuka kuqala, bese nina eniphilayo nenisasele niyokwenyuselwa emafwini kanye nabo, ukuze nibe nami njalo, mina Kristu Jesu, ngoba nginguMnazaretha ovusiweyo owafela izono kanye futhi ongabe esafa. Ngoba umbuso wami ungowaphakade naphakade, futhi umbuso wami awuyikuba nasiphelo, ngoba uYahweh ukushilo kanjalo.

Lapho abathembekileyo bami bethathwa bayiswa emazulwini, kanjalo nomvimbeli uyosuswa, futhi kanjalo isiqalo seviki lamashumi ayisikhombisa likaDaniyeli siyoqala. Abaningi benu banenkinga nomqondo wokuthi mina, Yeshua HaMashiach, ngihlwithe abathembekileyo bami ekwahluleleni okungabafanele, kodwa ngizonenza niqonde, uma niza kimi ngokuthobeka komntwana omncane, ngisho nobumsulwa nokulangazelela ukwazi komncane. Ngoba nina ningabantwanyana bami, impela abantwanyana bami ababona ngokungacacile futhi abaprofetha ingxenye. Ngalokho ngithi kini, zithobeni futhi niphenduke ekuziqhenyeni kwenu okuyize, ngoba kuyisono, nize kimi ukuze nifunde futhi namukele imibhalo engcwele. Ukwenqaba noma yisiphi isigaba sombhalo kuyisono, ngoba lonke iZwi likaNkulunkulu lihlanzekile futhi kufanele lamukelwe ngenhlonipho yokulalela, futhi mina, Jesu waseNazaretha, ngiyizwi elenziwa inyama.

Ukubuthelwa kwabangcwele bami kimi emafwini kwakuyimfihlakalo efihlwe ngaphakathi kwemfihlakalo enkulu kaNkulunkulu. Ngalokho kwakuyimfihlakalo ngaphakathi kwemfihlakalo futhi ngenxa yalokho ayizange yambulwe kwaze kwaba yembulwa kuMphostoli uPawulu ezincwadini zakhe. Kuye kwambulwa imfihlakalo enkulu kaNkulunkulu, ngoba uPawulu wayengumphathi wezimfihlakalo zikaNkulunkulu, ngoba ngamthuma kanjalo. Ngalokho qondani ukuthi ukwenqaba imibhalo ngalesi senzakalo kuwukungiphika mina nokuba phakathi kwezintombi eziyiziwula.

Futhi-ke ngizonichazela izinto eziningi esezizokwenzeka, ngisho nokuqhubeka nemisebenzi engayinika uJohane uMambuli, ngoba ubufakazi bokwambula kwami bubhalelwe ukufihla njengoba bwambula. Futhi-ke manje ngizonitshela izinto ngaphambi kokuba zenzeke ukuze nilungele izinsuku ezizayo. Ngoba mina, Kristu Jesu, ngizokwenza abantu bami bame emisebenzini lapho ngibathumela khona ukuze niqedele ngokupheleleyo imisebenzi eminingi emikhulu enginayo ukuba niyifeze.

Njengoba ukubuthana kwenzeka, umvimbeli uyasuswa, futhi ukubhujiswa okukhulu kuyofika emhlabeni, ngokuzumayo nangokuphazima kweso. Ngoba njengoba umvimbeli esusiwe, akukho okuyosala okuvimbela ububi emhlabeni kanye nezinkanuko nezifiso ezimbi zesintu. Impi enkulu iyogqashuka kuwo wonke umhlaba, futhi umfowabo uyophinda avuke abulale umfowabo, futhi kukhulu kuyoba ukubhujiswa kwabo. Izizwe eziningi ziyowa njengoba iBhabhiloni Eyimfihlakalo, leliya Sifebe esikhulu esihleli phezu kwamanzi amaningi, liyokwenziwa incithakalo ngehora elilodwa.

Lowo muzi omkhulu, leso sizwe seZifundazwe Eziningi Ezihlangene, siyobhujiswa ngokuphelele; ngoba sizuzele isabelo esiphindwe kabili sokwahlulelwa, futhi siyakufanelekela. Ngoba saphila ngokunethezeka esonweni esikhulu; sihlezi ngokuzidla okukhulu sicabanga ukuthi asisoze sawa futhi izinto ziyoqhubeka, izolo njenganamuhla, futhi kusasa kube njalo kungaguquki. Futhi isifebe esikhulu sonakalise umhlaba ngenkomishi yegolide esandleni saso, egcwele amanyala nokungcola kobufebe baso. Ngalokho ukwahlulelwa kwaso kuyofika ngokuzumayo. Umlilo omkhulu uyofika emizini yaso njengoba imicibisholo iwa; kuyoba nokuzamazama okukhulu, futhi leso sizwe siyodabuka phakathi nendawo. Izingxenye zaso ezisogwini ziyokhukhulwa kakhulu, futhi asisoze saphinda savuka, ngoba zinkulu izono zaso futhi kukhulu nokwahlulelwa kwaso.

Njengoba impi igqashuka ezweni futhi abagibeli bamahhashi bekhipha izahlulelo zabo emhlabeni, usizi olukhulu nencithakalo kuyofika emhlabeni. Umhlaba okhona manje awusoze waba njalo futhi; lapho umvimbeli esusiwe, konke kuyoba kuhluke. Kuyoba khona ukufa okukhulu, isifo esiwumshayabhuqe, indlala enkulu, nokungabi nabulungiswa okukhulu; kodwa abayizi-144,000 bami bayovikelwa olakeni nasekwahlulelweni, ngoba bayobe bephethe uphawu lukaNkulunkulu oPhilayo, impela uYahweh, emabunzini abo; ngoba bayophawulwa kanjalo, futhi ngithuma izingelosi zami ukuba zibaphawule. Ngalokho isiphithiphithi esikhulu nokungabi namthetho kuyobusa usuku. Izinyanga zokuqala ezingama-42 ziyoba yizikhathi zokushushiswa okukhulu nobunzima obukhulu, kodwa usizi olukhulu luyokwenzeka ezinyangeni zesibili ezingama-42: ngoba kunezinyanga ezingama-84 ezinsukwini eziyisikhombisa zeminyaka yosizi lukaJakobe. Ngalokho ingxenye yesibili iwusizi olukhulu, kodwa usizi nokushushiswa ezweni kuyoba kubi kubakhileyo bomhlaba isikhathi sonke.

Ngoba akumina, Kristu Jesu iNkosi Eyenyuka, noma uYahweh, uNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade (ngoba akekho onjengaYe), onanoma iyiphi injabulo ekwahlulelweni nasezinkingeni ezizayo. UBaba wami akadalisanga isintu ngothando ukuze akwazi ukusahlulela nokusibhubhisa. Kodwa, uSathane wonakalise insimu yomusa kaNkulunkulu, futhi manje ukonakala kuzohlanzwa ukuze ukulunga kubuse. Ngalokho ayikho enye indlela yokusindiswa kulabo abenqaba ubizo lwami lokusindiswa ngenkathi yomusa. Abantu kumele bavuke ebuthongweni babo nasenjabulweni yesono, ngoba asisekho isikhathi futhi manje yisikhathi sokukhetha ingunaphakade, noma ukukhetha kuyokwenziwa ekwahlulelweni kulabo abangiphikayo ngokwenqaba ukukhetha. Izinkinga eziningi nezahlulelo ezizayo ziyovusa abaningi ensindisweni yaphakade, ngisho nokubahlwitha emlilweni waphakade; ngoba abaningi badakiwe yizinjabulo zesono, ngalokho izinjabulo ziyosuswa. Ngeke besaba nenjabulo kulezi zinsuku, futhi ukusinda kuyoba yindaba yomzuzu nomzuzu ngezinye izikhathi, futhi ingozi enkulu iyokwanda.

Kuyokuba njalo-ke ukuthi abathembekileyo bami nabakhethiweyo phakathi kweBandla lami bayofihlwa ezikhathini zosizi lukaJakobe emazulwini. Futhi imisebenzi yabo iyokwahlulelwa ngomlilo, futhi noma ngubani omisebenzi yakhe isalayo uyokwamukela umvuzo futhi noma ngubani omisebenzi yakhe ishile uyolahlekelwa, kodwa bona uqobo bayosindiswa, njengokungathi ngomlilo. Bayothatha izindawo zabo esimisweni abalungiselelwe sona emazulwini. Ngoba mina, Yeshua HaMashiach, ngikufanele ukuthatha incwadi nokuvula izimpawu, futhi ngokuvulwa kwezimpawu abagibeli bamahhashi bayakhululwa. Futhi bayohamba emhlabeni beyonqoba, ukuze balwe, ukuze babangele isifo esiwumshayabhuqe nokufa okukhulu. Amacilongo ayisikhombisa ayokhala, kuyeza omaye, nezitsha eziyisikhombisa zentukuthelo yolaka lukaNkulunkulu ziyothululelwa emhlabeni ngokugcwele.

Impi yomhlaba ezayo ngeke ithathe iminyaka, ngoba uSathane uyazi ukuthi unesikhathi esifushane, kodwa impi iyohlala ikhona ngalezi zikhathi zosizi. Ezweni eliyincithakalo nasezesabekeni zempi kuyovuka isilo sasolwandle, nesilo somhlaba, esingumprofethi wamanga. Laba ababili bayosebenza ngokubambisana, njengoba sebevele besebenza ngokubambisana ngasese ekubhujisweni kweBhabhiloni Eyimfihlo. Futhi izimpondo eziyishumi, lezi ziyosizonda isifebe, futhi ziyosenza incithakalo, sibe nqunu, futhi ziyoyidla inyama yaso, futhi zisishise ngomlilo. Ngoba uNkulunkulu ufake ezinhliziyweni zazo ukugcwalisa intando yakhe, nokuvumelana, nokunika umbuso wazo esilweni, kuze kugcwaliseke amazwi kaNkulunkulu. (Futhi kanjalo abagibeli bamahhashi bayogibela.)

Bayovuka benikela ukuthula, ukuphepha, nokuzinikela okungamanga. Bayokwenqabela noma yini ephathelene nami, uJesu Kristu, emhlabeni wonke, futhi bayolwa nabangcwele babanqobe, ngoba kunikezwe ezandleni zabo ukuba bakwenze lokho. Abafokazi babo ababizwa kanjalo bayoziveza futhi banikele usizo lwabo esintwini, kodwa lezi yizingelosi eziwile eziletha inkohliso enkulu. Bhekani, ngiletha imidondoshiya ukuze igcwalise ulaka lwami, futhi uYahweh ushilo kanjalo. Njengasezinsukwini zikaNowa, kuyokuba njalo ezingelosini eziwileyo zasendulo; ngisho nalabo ababizwa ngonkulunkulu basendulo (abasiwona onkulunkulu) bayofuna ukwakha kabusha inkathi yabo yegolide emhlabeni, futhi bakhululelwa ukwahlulelwa, kodwa ngeke babe nayo inkathi yegolide. Amanyala abo ayokhululwa, ngoba umvimbeli uyobe engekho futhi esuswe ngokuphelele, futhi akukho okuyobabamba. Labo abathanda ukuphila kwabo bayolahlekelwa yikho ingunaphakade, ngoba bayodinga uphawu ukuze bahlanganyele emphakathini wabo omusha, ngisho nasesimisweni sabo esisha. Futhi bonke abamukela uphawu lwesilo bayoba nesabelo sabo echibini lomlilo.

Abayizi-144,000 bami abathunyelwanga ukuba bahlale emizini yabo, lapho kuyodingeka khona ukuba bamukele uphawu ukuze baphile. Kodwa bayothunyelwa emuva naphambili futhi bayobuthela abaningi kubo futhi banakekele abaningi futhi bayosiza ukuletha izixuku ezinkulu ensindisweni kimi, uYeshua HaMashiach (nganoma yiluphi ulimi): bonke abayobiza egameni lami kuze kube sekupheleni bayosindiswa. UNkulunkulu uyosula zonke izinyembezi emehlweni abo; futhi ngeke kusaba khona ukufa, nosizi, nokukhala, futhi ngeke kusaba khona ubuhlungu: ngoba izinto zangaphambili zidlulile. Njengasezinsukwini zikaNowa, labo abakholwayo bayohlala ngaphandle komphakathi wabo omubi wezingelosi eziwile, ngoba owabo uyoba ngumphakathi wesono oholela ekulahlweni kwaphakade emlilweni ongacimeki; ngakho ningahlanganyeli nabo ngoba ukukhetha kungunaphakade. Futhi-ke abayizi-144,000 bami bayoletha insindiso nosizo kwabaningi.

Bayokwenza izibonakaliso ezinkulu, izimangaliso, nezimangaliso, futhi ngiyobahola isinyathelo ngesinyathelo, futhi ngiyoqondisa izindlela zabo kulabo engibathumela kubo. Futhi ngiyobuthela izimvu zami kubo ukuze baholelwe ekuphepheni. Futhi noma ngubani ongakuthandi ukuphila kwakhe kuze kube sekupheleni uyosindiswa. Futhi labo abahlakaniphileyo bayokhanya njengokukhanya komkhathi; futhi labo abaphendulela abaningi ekulungeni njengezinkanyezi kuze kube phakade naphakade.

Isilo sasolwandle siyovuka futhi siyosebenzela izinhloso ezimbi zalabo ababusa izwe lesithembiso esonweni esikhulu, ngisho nesinagoge likaSathane. Siyobusa izizwe futhi siyokhuluma izinhlamba eziningi, futhi senze izibonakaliso nezimangaliso zamanga ezinkulu, futhi bakhuleka kudrako owanika amandla isilo, futhi bakhuleka isilo, bethi, Ngubani onjengesilo? Ngubani ongalwa naso? Isinagoge likaSathane liyokwenza konke elingakwenza ukubhubhisa noma ngubani obiza egameni lami, futhi isilo nomprofethi wamanga bayophoqelela ngonya iNoahide ezizweni futhi bayovela sengathi bahlanza ububi ezweni. Kodwa esikhundleni salokho bayolwa nabangcwele babanqobe, futhi kunikezwe isilo sasolwandle ukuba siqhubeke izinyanga ezingama-42.

Isilo somhlaba naso siyovuka emlotheni. Siyoba nawo onke amandla esilo sokuqala, futhi siyokwenza izibonakaliso nezimangaliso eziningi zamanga phambi kwesilo sokuqala, futhi siyokwenza umfanekiso wesilo, futhi umoya uyovunyelwa ukuba wenze umfanekiso wesilo ukhulume, uthi noma ngubani ongakhothami akhuleke kumfanekiso wesilo sokuqala kufanele abulawe.

Ngoba isilo somhlaba bayosithwesa ubukhosi bamazwe esithembiso, futhi siyoba nguMoshiach wabo osekunesikhathi elindelwe, futhi siyokwakha ithempeli labo (nakuba uYahweh engasithumanga), futhi kuyoba njalo. Bayosamukela njengombusi omkhulu, onomusa, umprofethi, nenkosi. Futhi isilo somhlaba (ngisho nomprofethi wabo wamanga) siyokwenza bonke, abancane nabakhulu, abacebile nabampofu, abakhululekile nabagqilaziwe, ukuba bamukele uphawu esandleni sabo sokunene noma emabunzini abo, Nokuthi kungabikho muntu ongathenga noma athengise, ngaphandle kwalowo onophawu, noma igama lesilo, noma inombolo yegama laso. Sebeyilungiselele umhlaba ukuba wamukele uphawu, futhi uhlelo selucolisisiwe ngesikhathi salokho abakubiza ngokuthi wubhubhane.

(Kulotshiwe eSambulo: Ingelosi yesithathu yabalandela, isho ngezwi elikhulu, Uma ekhona okhuleka esilweni nasemfanekisweni waso, amukele uphawu lwaso ebunzini lakhe, noma esandleni sakhe, Yena lowo uyophuza iwayini lolaka lukaNkulunkulu, elithululwa lingaxutshiwe enkomishini yentukuthelo yakhe; futhi uyohlushwa ngomlilo nesibabule phambi kwezingelosi ezingcwele, naphambi kweWundlu: Futhi intuthu yokuhlushwa kwabo iyenyuka kuze kube phakade naphakade: futhi abanakho ukuphumula imini nobusuku, labo abakhuleka esilweni nasemfanekisweni waso, futhi noma ngubani owamukela uphawu lwegama laso.)

Ezinyangeni zokuqala ezingama-42, ngiyonika amandla ofakazi bami ababili, futhi bayoprofetha izinsuku eziyinkulungwane namakhulu amabili namashumi ayisithupha, bembethwe indwangu yesaka. Lezi yizihlahla ezimbili zomnqumo, nezinti zezibani ezimbili ezimi phambi kukaNkulunkulu womhlaba. Futhi uma kukhona ofuna ukubalimaza, kuphuma umlilo emilonyeni yabo, uqothule izitha zabo: futhi uma kukhona ofuna ukubalimaza, kufanele abulawe ngale ndlela. Laba banamandla okuvala izulu, ukuze lingani ngezinsuku zokuprofetha kwabo: futhi banamandla phezu kwamanzi ukuwaphendula igazi, nokushaya umhlaba ngazo zonke izinhlupho, kaningi ngangokunokwenzeka. Futhi lapho sebeqedile ubufakazi babo, isilo esenyuka emgodini ongenamkhawulo siyolwa nabo, sibanqobe, sibabulale.

Isilo somhlaba, ngisho nomprofethi wabo wamanga, siyoqiniseka ukuletha isinengiso sencithakalo. Siyokwenza umhlatshelo wansuku zonke unqamuke futhi sibeke isinengiso sencithakalo endaweni yawo. Futhi ngasho kanjalo noMathewu wabhala: Ngalokho, lapho nibona isinengiso sencithakalo, esakhulunywa ngumprofethi uDaniyeli, simi endaweni engcwele (ofundayo makaqonde), Khona-ke abaseJudiya mababalekele ezintabeni: Osephezu kwendlu makehle angene athathe utho endlini yakhe: Noma osemasimini angabuyeli emuva ukuyothatha izingubo zakhe. Maye kwabakhulelweyo, nakwabancelisayo ngalezo zinsuku! Kodwa khulekani ukuthi ukubaleka kwenu kungabi sebusika, noma ngosuku lwesabatha: Ngoba ngaleso sikhathi kuyoba khona usizi olukhulu, olungakaze lube khona kusukela ekuqaleni komhlaba kuze kube manje, cha, futhi alusoze lwaba khona.

(Babusisiwe abafileyo abafela eNkosini kusukela manje: Yebo, kusho uMoya, ukuze baphumule emisebenzini yabo; futhi imisebenzi yabo iyabalandela.)

Ngalokho ngiyovikela abaningi ngibafihle ehlane osizini olukhulu, ngoba ukube izinsuku bezingafinyezwanga, bekungeke kusinde nyama. Khona-ke isilo nomprofethi wamanga bayobutha amakhosi omhlaba nabo bonke abami nawo embusweni kadrako ngalolo suku olukhulu lwempi. Futhi ngalokho ngabhalisa uJohane uMambuli: Ngabona izulu livulekile, bheka, ihhashi elimhlophe; nohlala phezu kwalo wayebizwa ngokuthi oThembekileyo noQinisileyo, futhi ngokulunga uyahlulela futhi ulwa. Amehlo akhe ayenjengelangabi lomlilo, futhi ekhanda lakhe kwakukhona imiqhele eminingi; futhi wayenegama elibhalwe, elingaziwa muntu, ngaphandle kwakhe uqobo. Futhi wayembethe isambatho esicwiliswe egazini: futhi igama lakhe libizwa ngokuthi yiZwi likaNkulunkulu. Futhi amabutho ayesemazulwini amlandela egibele amahhashi amhlophe, embethe ilineni elicolekileyo, elimhlophe nelihlanzekileyo. Futhi emlonyeni wakhe kuphuma inkemba ebukhali, ukuze ngayo, ashaye izizwe: futhi uyozibusa ngentonga yensimbi: futhi unyathela isikhamo sewayini sentukuthelo nolaka lukaNkulunkulu uSomandla.

Futhi isilo nomprofethi wamanga bobabili bayothathwa bephila futhi baphonswe echibini lomlilo elivutha isibabule. Futhi abaseleyo babulawa ngenkemba yalowo owayehlezi ehhashini, leyo nkemba eyaphuma emlonyeni wakhe: futhi zonke izinyoni zazisuthiswa ngenyama yabo. Futhi ngabona izihlalo zobukhosi, bahlala phezu kwazo, futhi ukwahlulelwa kwanikezwa kubo: futhi ngabona imiphefumulo yalabo abanqunywa amakhanda ngenxa yobufakazi bukaJesu, nangenxa yezwi likaNkulunkulu, futhi ababengakhulekanga esilweni, noma emfanekisweni waso, noma bengamukelanga uphawu lwaso emabunzini abo, noma ezandleni zabo; futhi baphila babusa noKristu iminyaka eyinkulungwane.

Ngalokho ingelosi yami iyokwehla ivela emazulwini, inesihluthulelo somgodi ongenamkhawulo kanye neketango elikhulu eliyobopha ngalo uSathane emgodini ongenamkhawulo iminyaka eyinkulungwane futhi ibeke uphawu ukuze angabe esakhohlisa izizwe iminyaka eyinkulungwane. Ekupheleni kokubusa kwami kweminyaka eyinkulungwane, uSathane uyokhululwa kwalasha isikhashana esifushane.

Uyophuma ayokhohlisa izizwe emagumbini amane omhlaba, uGogi noMagogi ukuze ababuthe ndawonye njengesihlabathi solwandle, futhi uyohlasela ikamu labangcwele azungeze umuzi ongcwele, futhi kuyokwehla umlilo ovela kuYahweh futhi ubaqothule ngokuphelele. Futhi udeveli owabakhohlisayo uyoboshwa futhi aphonswe ephila kulelo chibi elikhulu lomlilo, elivutha isibabule emalangabini ashisayo kakhulu aluhlaza. Futhi kuyoba khona impindiselo enkulu yobubi futhi ubulungiswa obukhulu buyokwenziwa ekhanda likaSathane uqobo, ezingelosini zakhe eziwile, nabo bonke abahlanganyela ekuhlubukeni kwabo; futhi ngeke baphinde bavuke, futhi bayokhohlwa ngokuphelele; futhi ngeke baphinde bakhunjulwe.

Khona-ke uYahweh uyokwenza izulu elisha nomhlaba omusha, futhi uyokwenza indawo yakhe yokuhlala phakathi kwabantu, futhi kuyoba ngokuthula nokuzwana kuze kube phakade, futhi ngeke kuphinde kube nesikhathi sosizi, futhi ngeke baphinde bone, kungaba ngokuzidla noma ngokungakholwa, futhi zonke izinsuku zabo ziyobusiswa.

NginguMnazaretha ovusiweyo owafela izono kanye futhi ongabe esafa.

NginguYeshua, impela uMesiya wenu weqiniso, uYahweh amthumileyo ukugcwalisa izithembiso ezenziwa koBaba.

Ngingulowo onamehlo anjengelangabi lomlilo, nasekhanda lami kunemiqhele eminingi.

NgiyiNkosi yamakhosi neNkosi yamakhosi, futhi ngokulunga ngiyeza ukuzokwahlulela nokulwa.

Ngingulowo oyohola futhi ahole abayizi-144,000 bami, futhi bayohlabelela ingoma entsha engengafundwe muntu.

Ngingulowo oyohlula umbuso kaSathane, futhi ngiyochoboza ikhanda lakhe.

Ngingulowo uYahweh amthumelele insindiso yabo bonke abakholwayo, futhi isikhathi sami sesifikile. Bhekani! Ukwahlulela kuyeza futhi izikhathi yimanje.
