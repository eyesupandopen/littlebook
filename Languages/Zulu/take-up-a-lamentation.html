Khuphulani Isililo

Amazwi iNkosi enginike wona, ngiwamemezela obala: Yizwani Izwi leNkosi!

Khuphulani isililo; impela, khuphulani isililo ngenxa yalabo abasanamathele kuleli zwe eliwileyo. Ngokuba bathi ezinhliziyweni zabo, "Yini engangehlela na? Njengoba kwakunjalo endulo, kuzoqhubeka unomphela; mina nabami sizoqhubeka nokuzitika, futhi sizoziqoqela okuhle kwaleli zwe nengcebo yemali, eyothi isisindise ngesikhathi sosizi." Nithi ezinhliziyweni zenu, "Sihlakaniphile, futhi siyachuma; inhlekelele ngeke isehlele, njengoba ingasoze yasehlela, ngoba uNkulunkulu wethu ngeke avumele isizwe sethu siwe; ngokuba sihlakaniphile futhi sinamandla." Kanti anihlakaniphile, kodwa nizishaya abahlakaniphileyo; futhi nginikele izitha zenu ukuba zinqobe phezu kwenu.

O, muntu, ubani oyokusindisa ehoreni lakho lokwahlulelwa elisondela ezweni lonke? Abaholi bakho bayokusindisa na? Ngiqinisile, ngithi cha, njengoba indodana yokulahlwa isendleleni yakho futhi isihamba obala phakathi kwenu, ikhohlisa abaningi ngamazwi amahle nangenkulumo emnandi. Imali yakho iyokusindisa na? Hhayi, yonke ingcebo yakho iyomenyezelwa njengengento yalutho ngokuphazima kweso, futhi ngeke kubekhona ukunxeshezelwa ngemisebenzi yempilo yakho eniyiqoqe ngobugovu. O, sengathi ngabe anizishiyanga izindlela zeNkosi futhi ngabe nithande umakhelwane wenu nasiza abaswele ngenala yenu.

Kodwa cha, niyayigodla yona kanye inala uNkulunkulu aninike yona ukuze yanelise umphefumulo wenu. Ngiqinisile, abaningi benu ngibanike okungaphezu kokwanele nina futhi nokubusisa umakhelwane wenu; kodwa cha, nithi, ngizozigcinela le nala enkulu ngoba ngokuqinisekile uNkulunkulu ungibusisile ngenxa yobuhle bami namandla ami. Bhekani, nginitshela into ingakenzeki, ukuze ehoreni lenu lenhlekelele niyokhumbula izwi lami namazwi omlomo wami engiwaphefumulele kubaphrofethi bami. Ngokuba angibathumi ukuba bakhulume okwabo, kodwa ngibathuma njengesixwayiso kini, ukuze nisindise imiphefumulo yenu ngokuphendukela kimi, iNkosi uJesu Kristu, onamandla okunisindisa ngokupheleleyo. Kodwa nithi enhliziyweni yenu, Usindiso? Angidingi kusindiswa, njengoba ngimuhle ngendlela engazalwa ngayo. Kodwa izono zenu ziyiphunga elibi emakhaleni kaBaba, futhi izwe selibolile ngenxa yobubi benu. Anazi yini ukuthi nazalwa nifile eziphambekweni nasezonweni, okonakalisa yona kanye imvelo yenu?

Yize nginganinika imvelo entsha, ehlanzekile, umhlatshelo wami niwubheka njengongento yalutho ninamathele enyameni yenu ebolile.

Khuphulani isililo ngenxa yabantu besizwe senu esake saba sikhulu abenqaba izwi lami. Ngokuba usizi olukhulu nomhelo kuyonehlela ngokuzuma. Kuyoba khona ukuzamazama okukhulu, futhi ibutho lami elinamandla lasezulwini liyokhithiza imbubhiso enkulu phezu kwesifebe esikhulu, futhi ngeke siphinde sivuke, nakuba insali iyosindiswa. Ngisho nasenhlekeleleni yenu nasekubhujisweni okusondelayo ngokuqinisekile ngiyothumela abaphrofethi bami kini. Niyobazi ngezithelo zabo, amazwi, nokuphulukisa; ngiyokwenza iziphulukiso eziningi nezimangaliso ngezandla zabo. Nokho, ngisho kunjalo abaningi bayobhubha bafe ezonweni zabo, kodwa abamnene bayodla ifa lomhlaba; ngokuba ifa lami likimi, lilungele ukwabiwa kwabathembekileyo. Ngingalulela isandla sami isikhathi esingakanani kubantu abaphikayo abamelana nami nezindlela zami futhi ekukhohlisweni kwabo bazimemezela njengabalungileyo phambi kukaNkulunkulu ngezingubo zabo ezingcolile?

Phendukani! Khalani nilile ngenxa yezono zenu, futhi nikhale kimi ukuze nginisindise. Amasonto enu amaningi angenwe yizimpisi ezihahayo ezinishumayeza amazwi ayoniholela ekupheleni kwenu, futhi nokho aniyi kuphenduka kuzo niphendukele kimi ngobuso obuvulekile nenhliziyo, njengoba nginganisindisa osizini lwenu futhi ngigcine abantwana benu bephephile emabuthweni omubi, ahamba obala emigwaqweni yenu futhi asevele enebela kakhulu. Izinkanuko zawo ngeke zaneliswe aze athathe konke kini futhi aqumqude abaningi. Kodwa labo ababiza igama lami bayosindiswa uma bekhuthazela kuze kube sekupheleni.

Kungani nilukhuni izinhliziyo zenu ngokumelene neNkosi Yamabandla? Kungani nisaqamba amanga ezinhliziyweni zenu, nithi, "Ayikho inhlekelele; njengoba kwakunjalo izolo, kuyoba njalo nanamuhla, nakusasa kuletha okufanayo?" Nina bahleki bosulu niyovuna umvuzo wezindlela zenu zokungakholwa, futhi labo abangangamukeli bayobhujiswa ngokuphelele babe sosizini olukhulu. Yizwani amazwi ami, o, sizukulwane esiphingayo nesibi. Yizwani amazwi ami futhi nithobise izindlebe zenu kuwo ukuze nizwe uhlamvu ngalunye engizolukhuluma. Ukuzamazama okukhulu kuzodabula isizwe senu enisazisa kangaka, futhi ekuzamazameni okukhulu kuyovela imbubhiso enkulu. Ngizochitha amadolobha enu ake aba makhulu futhi ngiyolethela namanzi ukuba agubuzele ugu lwenu olunesono; ngokuba animthandi uNkulunkulu kodwa ningabathandi benjabulo kunokuthanda uNkulunkulu.

Angijabuli ngokwahlulelwa kwenu, njengoba umusa nomhawu wami uvame kini isikhathi eside manje. Abaningi balabo abangcolise isivumelwano sikaNowa bona ngokwabo bayogutshuzelwa emanzini ngenxa yokungcolisa uthingo olungcwele lwemibala eminingi uBaba alunikeza njengesibambiso. Umlilo uyokhithika phezu kwenu ngesandla sezitha zenu, futhi abaningi bayobhubha emalangabini nasobuthi obulandelayo. Izwe lenu liyodatshulwa, futhi inhlekelele enkulu iyoba yisidlo senu sakusihlwa, ngokuba nimlahle iNkosi yamakhosi neNkosi yamakhosi.

Omubi usevele engenisile iButho lakhe eliBomvu emadolobheni enu, emadolobhaneni, nasemizaneni. Bahamba phakathi kwenu, behlela ukubhujiswa kwenu; futhi balinde imiyalo yabo nokusuka komvimbeli wami, engizomkhulula maduze. Bayoshanela babhubhise futhi banqobe okusele. Akukhona lokho kuphela, kodwa bayokubiza njengokwabo, futhi bayothumba abaningi benu babayise emakamu abo asebewalungisile. Abaningi bayonqunywa amakhanda ngenxa yegama lami, futhi ngiyobavuza kakhulu lapho behlala bethembekile kuze kube sekupheleni. Ngeke kubekho ukukhanya; kuyoba namabhuloho awileyo nemigwaqo egumbuqelwe. Ubani oyoniphakela ukudla namanzi lapho isizwe senu silele singasenakuvuka? Kodwa ngisazoyisindisa insali, futhi insali ngiyoyisindisa, ngokuba ngikumemezelile!

Ningesabi, bantu bami nabathembekileyo bami. Ngizonihlwitha phakathi naphakathi ngokuphazima kweso nginiyise endlini kaBaba, lapho nginilungiselele indawo netafula ukuze sidle ndawonye, bantwana bami. Ngilangazelela ubukhona benu, futhi maduze nizobona ubuso bami ngamehlo enu, futhi niyoba nomvuzo wokholo lwenu kimi ngoba ninokholo enhliziyweni yenu lapho ningakaboni ngisho nangamehlo enu inkazimulo yami, noma inkazimulo kaBaba, omkhulu kunami. Futhi nginibizile futhi nginikhethile njengabami, futhi anisoze nasuka kimi, njengoba niyohlala nineNkosi nomhlengi wenu. Yazini ukuthi ngizonivuza kakhulu futhi nginithele ngezinbusiso eningeke nizazi ngalesi sikhathi. Hlalani nithembekile kuze kube sekupheleni, ngokuba isikhashana nje lezi zinto ziyokwenzeka; impela, ihora selihambile, futhi ubusuku sebuvele buwelile emhlabeni.

NgiyiNkosi yamakhosi neNkosi yamakhosi, futhi nginikwe igama elingaphezu kwawo onke amagama, ukuze ngegama likaJesu Kristu onke amadolo ezulwini nasemhlabeni aguqe.

NgiyiNkosana Yokuthula, futhi ngaphandle kwami akukho ukuthula.

Nginguye oninike ukuphumula, ukuze niphumule kimi futhi nithole isiphephelo emseni wami.

Nginguye ohamba phakathi kwezinti zezibani eziyisikhombisa, oyala izingelosi zikaBaba, futhi zonke izinto ezulwini nasemhlabeni zisezandleni zami, njengoba uBaba wanginika zona ekunqobeni kwami omubi kuze kube yilapho izitha zami zenziwa isenabelo sezinyawo zami.

NgiyiNkosi yenu nomhlengi wenu, futhi ngingumalusi omuhle owathweswa umqhele wenkazimulo yonke, futhi ngiphethe ukuphila okuphakade ezandleni zami kulabo abayozithoba bacele.

Memezelani ukuthi ngiyiNkosi! Ningesabi, bantwana bami abancane, njengoba kuyizikhathi zokugcina futhi isixuku esikhulu siyosindiswa osizini oluzayo. Noma ubani obiza igama likaJesu Kristu uyosindiswa, futhi ngiyobaphulukisa futhi ngibavuze ngokuthembeka kwabo. Impela, ngihamba phakathi kwenu, futhi yini enkulu iNkosi eyihlelile engazange iyitshele abaphrofethi bayo ingakenzeki? Ngiyanitshela ngokusobala, lalelani izwi lami, njengoba kusele isikhathi esincane kakhulu ngaphambi kokukhala kwecilongo lokugcina. Ngisho nezingelosi zimi zilindile emazulwini phezulu, zilindele ukushaywa kwecilongo! Ukuhlengwa kwenu okuphelele kusemnyango, ngakho-ke ningesabi ninamathele kimi, ngokuba impilo yenu ifihliwe kimi futhi isizokwambulwa.
