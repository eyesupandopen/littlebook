Ubuso Bewashi Buwugwadule Ngokuba Izinombolo Ziwile

Usulungele yini mina, Makoti wami? (Ngokuba ngiyeza) Usulungile? Uyalalela? Uyabuka? Ngokuba ngiyeza kuwe, ngakho-ke ungapheli amandla futhi ube nethemba elikhulu nokulangazelela, ngokuba amagugu uqobo lwaseZulwini alindele abathembekileyo bami futhi umvuzo wakho usesandleni sami! Kodwa maye kubo bonke abangangishayanga indiva ekungakholweni okungenalukholo, yebo mina, uJesu waseNazaretha! Ngalokho usuku lokwahlulelwa kwakho luphezu kwakho, o siyifebe esikhulu esihleli phezu kwamanzi amaningi, nesikhathi sakho esimisiweyo sesifikile.

Imbubhiso yesiqubu isiphezu kwakho nezitha zakho sezizilungisile, nokho usadla izibiliboco nakuba uyisizwe esisezibophweni phambi kwami, uKristu Jesu. Isikhathi sakho sokuchuma safika ngokuba isikhashana oYihlo balandela iNdodana kaNkulunkulu futhi bagcina amazwi ami. Kodwa inkathi yeBandla lami isiphelile ngokuba nathengisa ngamagugu ezimfihlakalo zami, ngisho nenhlalakahle nothando lwabantu bami nikwenze impahla yokuthengisa ngenxa yenzuzo engcolileyo nokudunyiswa phambi kwabantu.

Nazithengisa ngobugovu obuyisisekelo nokungathembeki, futhi ningishiyile, yebo uJesu Kristu, owafela izono zezwe. Kodwa nilinqabile izwi lami, nikunqabile ukusola kwami, nilinqabile izeluleko zami, ninqabile amanxusa ami, ninqabile abaprofethi bami, ninqabile abaphostoli bami, futhi ningishayile indiva, mina Jesu waseNazaretha, ngalokho nimshayile indiva uYahweh, futhi manje ngizonishaya indiva nani, o Bhabhiloni eliyimfihlo. Kanjalo ukwahlulela kuqala eNdlini kaYahweh!

Ngokuba ngenkathi naningaba nokuthula nokulondeka, esikhundleni salokho nakhetha ukulandela ngokungaboni labo abonakalisa isizwe senu. Ngisho nokhula lwabakwa-Edomi oluhlupha amazwe, nolukhathaze umhlaba kusukela omubi asakaza amanyala akhe phezu kwendalo kaYahweh.

Ngakho-ke yazini, ukuthi mina, yebo mina Kristu Jesu, ngizoliqeda nya iBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlakalo ngomlilo futhi ngehora elilodwa ukubhujiswa kwenu kuzofika, ngokuba kulotshiwe futhi lokho uYahweh akushilo akekho ongakuguqula. O, nina bakhonzi bezithixo abayiziwula bezwe laseBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo, ukube beniqonda isimo senu. Ukube benithobele izindlebe zenu emazwini ami ngokuba ngibizile, ngancenga, ngakhuleka, ngayala, ngamemeza, ngabonisa izibonakaliso phezulu nezibonakaliso emhlabeni kepha esikhundleni sokwembathisa inhliziyo yenu ngendwangu yesaka, esikhundleni sokuhlala emlotheni wokuphenduka, esikhundleni salokho nijatshuliswa sengathi nibuka imilingo.

Maye kulabo abajatshuliswa yizibonakaliso zikaYahweh emkhathini ezimemezela ukwahlulelwa kwabo, futhi maye kabili kuwe Bhabhiloni Eliyimfihlakalo, ngokuba unesabelo esiphindwe kabili sokwahlulelwa esizokhululwa, futhi ngeke uphunyuke noma ululame. Bhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo uyizwe lokwaneliseka okusheshayo, futhi awuhlakaniphile neze. Uhehe izizwe ezindleleni zakho zobubi nokuyekethisa, futhi uzidakise ngokungcola kobufebe bakho. Aniziqondi izindlela zami, ngokuba yilowo nalowo phakathi kwenu uhamba ngezindlela zakhe njengoba ngobusaSathane benza noma yini abayithandayo, "yenza intando yakho" uwonke umthetho wabo wobuSathane, kanjalo abangenamthetho bayize leze.

Aniziqondi izindlela zami, ngokuba izindlela zami ziyizindlela zikaYahweh, futhi mina ngiyindlela, neqiniso, nokuphila, ngokuba akekho oza kuBaba ngaphandle kwami, futhi uma ningibonile mina, nimbonile noBaba. Kodwa nivale izingqondo zenu, nivale izindlebe zenu, nenze lukhuni izinhliziyo zenu, futhi nivule imilomo yenu ekungakholweni okuhlambalazayo, nihlambalaza ngisho nesivumelwano sokuthula uYahweh aninike sona. Ngokuba mina Jesu Kristu, ngiwukuthula kwenu!

Kodwa cha, esikhundleni sokungenza iNkosi, esikhundleni salokho nisebenzisa igama lami elingcwele, yebo elikaJesu Kristu, ngeze njengelinye igama lokuveza ukungcola ezinhliziyweni zenu. Kodwa anikwazi ukungcolisa igama lami, ngokuba uYahweh unginikile igama eliphezu kwawo onke amagama, ukuze ngegama likaJesu Kristu onke amadolo aguqe, aseZulwini nasemhlabeni. Futhi nizokhokhela wonke amazwi ayize, nezikhathi zenu sezifikile. Kanjalo-ke manje, njengoba nizivalele ezindlini zenu nicabanga ukuthi ningacasha ekwahlulelweni kukaYahweh, manje ngizovala umnyango wokungena emazulwini kwabangathembekile njengoba ngihlwitha abathembekileyo bami nami ezulwini ngokuba umkhwenyana uyeza.

Ngobuwula nicabanga ukuthi konke sekubuyele esimeni, nempi ezayo ingabukwa kumabonakude njengezinye izimpi abaholi benu abazilwa ngokungemthetho. Njengoba benijatshuliswe ukufa nokubhujiswa kwezinye izizwe, kanjalo nesizwe senu sizobhujiswa ngokuphelele, nokufa nokubhujiswa kwenu kuzonikeza isinxephezelo kulabo ababhujiswe ngemvume yenu.

Ngobuwula nicabanga ukuthi ningalungiselela intukuthelo ezayo ngokuphila ngomhlaba, endaweni ethile engekho ohlelweni, kodwa nizoqothulwa ngokuphelele, nithunjwe, nibulawe, nizingelwe, nibulawe yindlala, niguliswe, futhi izinhlupho eziningi zizoehlela bonke labo abangishaya indiva mina Yeshua HaMashiach, ngokuba ukungishaya indiva kusho ukushaya indiva uNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade, yebo uYahweh uqobo, futhi ngeke kube khona ikhambi. Ngakho-ke gijimelani kimi manje!

Ngobuwula aniqondi ukuthi izimpondo eziyishumi zibambisene nesilo sasemhlabeni nesilo sasolwandle ezingakakhuli ngokugcwele emandleni, nokuthi isizwe senu esasikade sikhulu sinikelwe ngokuphelele ezandleni zazo ukuze sibhujiswe ngokuphelele. (Futhi ngokubhujiswa okuphelele kwesizwe senu izilo zizovuka!) Ngokuba uNkulunkulu ukufakile ezinhliziyweni zazo ukufeza izinjongo zakhe.

Nizungezwe ngokuphelele yimikhumbi yezitha zenu, nivumele izigidi nezigidi zamaButho aBomvu, yebo ibutho lesilo ukuba lingene emazweni enu. Nizivumelile ukuba zenze izenzo zokucekela phansi, zicekele phansi imigudu yenu yokuhlinzeka, zicekele phansi ukudla kwenu, zicekele phansi umnotho wenu, zakhe amakamu okukhipha inkani, okuhlukumeza nokubulala noma ngubani ongahambisani nezinjongo zazo. Qondani ukuthi ngisho nangaleso sikhathi noma ngubani obiza egameni lami, yebo elikaJesu Kristu, kuze kube sekupheleni uyosindiswa. Labo abenza isinqumo sabo sokwenqaba sibe esokugcina bayohlala phakade nemiphumela yokungakholwa emalangabini amakhulu alungiselelwe udeveli nezingelosi zakhe, ngokuba akukho okunye ukukhetha.

Ngeluleka bonke abafunda lawa mazwi ukuba basondele kimi. Yekani izinkulumo-mpikiswano zenu ezingenangqondo niyeke nokuqophisana kwenu okungenangqondo phakathi kwenu nishumayele ivangeli lensindiso njengoba lilotshiwe emibhalweni futhi nishumayele izwi lami, ngisho namazwi amazwi ami kule mizuzwana yokugcina. Shumayelani phakathi kwenu, futhi nihlale nikulungele ukusiza abaswele. Yekani ukwahlulela abafowenu nodadewenu esikhundleni salokho sizanani ngothando ukuze nime emseni nasesihawini engininike sona, ngokuba kwanele.

Vumani niphenduke ezonweni zenu futhi nikwenze lokho manje, aninaso isikhathi! Ngenzeni, mina Jesu waseNazaretha, iNkosi yenu, ngokuba isikhathi sesiphelile. Zani kimi manje, ngokuba awekho amanye amagama anikiweyo ensindiso, ngaphandle kwelami, elikaJesu Kristu. Zani kimi manje, nimuke ebubini, nami ngizonamukela kimi uma nje nimemezela ukuthi ngiyiNkosi ngenhliziyo egcwele ukholo nazi ukuthi ngivusiwe kwabafileyo ukuze ngingabe ngisafa. Ngelule izandla zami ezivulekile ezweni elifayo amaJubili amaningi manje, nezikhathi zomusa nesihe seziseduze nokuphela, nakuba umusa nesihe kuhlala kunami ngokuba ngiyiNkosi enomusa.

Futhi-ke manje, nilindile kule mizuzwana yokugcina, o Bhabhiloni eliyimfihlakalo. Ngokuba kuzokwenzeka ngokuzumayo. Ngokuzumayo umlilo uzowa, ngokuzumayo amanzi azogcwalisa iziketi zakho eziphakanyiswe ngaphezu kwezinqe zakho, o siyifebe esikhulu, ngokuzumayo ukuzamazama okunodlame kuzofika phezu kwakho futhi kukudabule, ngokuzumayo umyalo uzonikezwa futhi izigidi zizogcwala izwe lonke zibulale noma imuphi umuntu ophilayo ongahambisani nezinjongo zabo. Ngeke babe nesihe, abanako ukuzisola, akunakuncengwa kubo, abanalo uzwelo, kodwa bakhonza uSathane futhi bazofeza izinjongo abanikezwe zona ukuba bazifeze, ngokuba bazofeza intukuthelo kaNkulunkulu eBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlakalo futhi ngeke liphinde livuke.

Bhekani! Izikhathi sezifikile. Phendukani nisakwazi ukudonsa umoya, ngokuba kuzokwenzeka ngokuzumayo. Yizwani amazwi ami niqonde: umvimbeli ungukuphela kwamandla agcine lobu bubi bungenzeki, futhi isikhathi enicabanga ukuthi ninaso simane nje siyisikhathi esivunyelwe ukuhlela nokuhambisa izingcezu zibe sezindaweni zazo ebhodini lomhlaba futhi lezi zikhathi zisebenzela izinjongo zikaYahweh.

Khona-ke ngokuzumayo ngizobutha abathembekileyo eBandleni lami ukuze bangihlangabeze emafwini futhi ngizohlwitha uMakoti wami ngimuse emakamelweni akhe, ngokuba endlini kaBaba kukhona izindlu eziningi futhi ngaya ukuyonilungisela indawo Makoti wami, namazulu anilungiselwe!

Futhi ngokuzumayo, lapho ingcebo eyigugu kaBaba isigcinwe ngokuphepha esiphongweni sasezulwini (ngokuba kuzokwenzeka ngokuphazima kweso) yonke into encane nayo yonke imininingwane emincane izofezeka, yebo lonke izwi engilikhulume ngabaprofethi bami beqiniso nabaphostoli, ngokuba baningi futhi abaningi abangabami bakhuluma ngazwi linye futhi bakhuluma ngokuvumelana nemibhalo yami. Qondani lokhu, labo abenqaba amazwi acacile emibhalweni mayelana nokubuthwa kweBandla lami ukuze lingihlangabeze emafwini, bona labo abaphikayo nabo bazophikwa futhi bathathe indawo yabo ngokuzithandela entukuthelweni lapho into enjalo ibingadingekile, kodwa bangaki kini abayenqaba ngenkani izwi lami ngenxa yalabo abakitaza izindlebe zenu? Nokho, kuyoba njalo.

Uma kukhona ongakhulumi ngokwamazwi ami, ngokuba mina Kristu Jesu, ngiyiZwi elenziwa inyama, ningabafiseli ngisho inhlanhla ngokuba abawaqondi kahle amazwi ami ngokuba abaprofethi bami abasoze baphikisana nombhalo ngokuba angikwazi ukuziphikisa. Ngingamazwi kaYahweh enziwe inyama, futhi nginguYeshua HaMashiach, iWundlu likaNkulunkulu eliza ukuhlanza izono zesintu.

Ngiza njengeNgonyama yesizwe sakwaJuda futhi intukuthelo yami ishisa emhlabeni, ngokuba ngokulunga ngiyahlulela futhi ngilwe. Phendukani nina enikhohlakele bomhlaba ngokuba ukwenqaba kwenu kuyoba okuphakade. Ekupheleni kweminyaka eyisikhombisa yosizi lukaJakobe, umhlaba uyophumula ngokuba kuyoba khona usuku lokuphumula, ngokuba iminyaka eyinkulungwane ifana nosuku olulodwa emehlweni kaYahweh. Futhi iNgonyama iyolala neWundlu ngokuba izinjongo zami zeWundlu likaNkulunkulu ziyopheleliswa yiNgonyama yesizwe sakwaJuda. Isikhathi sesiphelile.

Mina Yeshua HaMashiach ngithumele lawa mazwi ngenceku yami, futhi ngenceku yami lawa mazwi athunyelwe!
