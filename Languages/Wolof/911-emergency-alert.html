Kon, buleen bokk ci gumbaam gi ngir ngeen bañ a sonn ci ay jàngoroom, te beruleen ci seen biir ngir ngeen waaj bu wér ngir feeñug seen Boroom bu sax dàkk bi. Seetleen, sama Aroos bi! Jéeg bi mu ngi ñëw, kon yorleen seeni làmp yu fees, fitila yu ñu dagg te taal, ci xaar gu dëggu seen Boroom biy ñëw, ndaxte léegi jamonoy dajale la, te jamonoy dajale léegi la.
