Adansefoɔ Mmienu: Asɔre no
Wɔ Peter Nnwuma Mu
Oforisuo 20, 2024

Na Onyankopɔn nyɛ basabasa Nyame!

Na Onyankopɔn nyɛ basabasa Nyame, na mmom ɔyɛ asomdwoeɛ deɛ, sɛdeɛ ɛteɛ wɔ ahoteefoɔ asɔre nyinaa mu no! Kenkan nsɛm yinom na tie me nne. Brɛ w’aso ase, na tie anyansafoɔ nsɛm, na fa wo koma to me nimdeɛ so. Ebia ɛsɛ sɛ wosuasua me nhwɛsoɔ a ɛfiri turo no mu berɛ a mede yaw bɔɔ mpaeɛ, berɛ a mehwee fam wɔ m’Agya anim wɔ mpaebɔ ne ahobrɛaseɛ mu, na ɔhyɛɛ me den sɛ mennye mo ankasa bɔne abufuo kuruwa a mede faa mo soɔ no, ɛfiri sɛ metumi asoa. Mekasa kyerɛ ahomasoɔfoɔ a wɔwɔ m’Asɔre no mu, na mekasa kyerɛ mpaapaemu bebree a ɛwɔ mo mu no. Nanso adɛn na wobu wo nua atɛn? Anaasɛ adɛn na wubu wo nua animtia? Na yɛn nyinaa bɛgyina Kristo atɛnnwa no anim. Wɔankyerɛw no wɔ krataa a mede kɔmaa wɔn a wɔdi Akwan no akyi wɔ Roma no mu anaa? Nanso ɛdeɛn na Sakaria ka fa asɛm no ho: Nanso wɔampɛ sɛ wɔtie, na wɔtwee wɔn mmatire, na wɔsii wɔn aso, sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a wɔrente. Me mma nketewa, monkenkanee Yesaia anaa? Na ɛdeɛn na tete adiyifoɔ no ka? Na Yesaia ka bio sɛ, Teteɛ mu mobɛte, na morente aseɛ; na hwɛ mu mobɛhwɛ, na morenhunu.

Me mma, makyerɛ mo ɔkwan a ɛkyɛn so, nanso mo mu bebree afiri adom mu ahwe, na mo ahomasoɔ a ɛyɛ ahantan no ama moama mo ho so sene sɛdeɛ ɛsɛ. Nanso, me nsɛm a mede maa ɔsomafoɔ Paulo maa Galatia no ka no pefee sɛ: Na sɛ onipa bi dwene sɛ ɔyɛ biribi, berɛ a ɔnyɛ hwee no, ɔdaadaa ne ho. Me mma nketewa, menim sɛ mo koma pere sɛ mobɛsom me, na menim sɛ modɔ me, nanso ɛtɔ da a ɛnyɛ nimdeɛ so. Monnte aseɛ sɛ Onyankopɔn si ahantanfoɔ kwan na ɔdom ahobrɛasefoɔ anaa? Enti, mommrɛ mo ho ase wɔ Onyankopɔn nsa a ɛwɔ tumi no ase, na ama wama mo so wɔ berɛ a ɛsɛ mu. Saa ara nso na mo mmeranteɛ ne mmaayewa, mommrɛ mo ho ase mma mo mpanimfoɔ. Aane, mo nyinaa mommrɛ mo ho ase mma mo ho mo ho, na momfa ahobrɛaseɛ nhyɛ mo ho. Onyankopɔn si ahantanfoɔ kwan na ɔdom ahobrɛasefoɔ. Monnsusu sɛ m’asɛm no yɛ ntĩmu, na adansefoɔ baanu anaa baasa anomu na asɛm biara si pi. Monte ase, me mma, sɛ montumi mfa mo honam nsom me: Na deɛ ɛwɔ wiase nyinaa, honam akɔnnɔ, ne aniwa akɔnnɔ, ne asetena mu ahomasoɔ, ɛmfiri Agya no hɔ, na mmom ɛfiri wiase. M’Asɔre, mommrɛ mo ho ase mma mo ho mo ho wɔ adwene a ahobrɛaseɛ ahyɛ mu ma mu, ɛfiri sɛ ebia me na mede nsɛm a wɔbɛka ne nkanyan a wɔde bɛkyerɛkyerɛ mo no rema, ɛwɔ mu sɛ mo mu bebree bɛto ahobrɛasefoɔ nne no akyene de agye obi din ne ne nipasu atom. Monni Onyankopɔn akyi, me mma, na nokorɛm Onyankopɔn nhwɛ nnipa anim.

Hwɛ! Ɛyɛ Onyankopɔn animuonyam sɛ ɔde biribi sie: nanso ɛyɛ ahemfoɔ ni sɛ wɔhwehwɛ asɛm mu. Monte aseɛ sɛ mɛyɛ mo ahemfo ne asɔfoɔ wɔ yɛn Nyankopɔn ne yɛn Agya anim, enti monte anyansafoɔ a wɔwɔ mo mu no nsɛm ase: Onyankopɔn mmaa mo aso mmienu ne ano baako anaa? Monkenkanee anaa? Mo mu biara wɔ nnwom, ɔwɔ nkyerɛkyerɛ, ɔwɔ tɛkrɛma, ɔwɔ adiyisɛm, ɔwɔ nkyerɛaseɛ: Momma wɔnyɛ nnoɔma nyinaa mfa nsi dan! Nanso daabi, mode mo ahomasoɔ ka na mopete mo ho mo ho; mode mo ahantan taa wɔn a wɔbɔ mmɔden sɛ wɔbɛkyerɛkyerɛ mo no so, na moayɛ wɔn a wontumi nkyerɛkyerɛ wɔn. Mogyigye akyinnye, mokasakasa, mogyigye akyinnye, na moko tia mo ho mo ho mmom sene sɛ mobɛbrɛ mo ho ase asusu deɛ ɔfoforɔ no ka ho. Wɔ afa foforɔ no, mo ho so araa ma mosusu sɛ ɛfiri sɛ nsɛm no firi mo anomu nti ɛyɛ nokorɛ ɛfiri sɛ MO na mokaeɛ? Mommrɛ mo ho ase mma mo ho mo ho. Monka mo tɛkrɛma ntɔ mu na momfa mo nuanom nsɛm nkɔ kyerɛwsɛm mu nkɔsɔ nnoɔma nyinaa nhwɛ na monkura deɛ ɛyɛ no mu. Montu mo aso na montie koraa na monsusu, na sɛ moyɛ saa a, mɛma moayɛ anyansafoɔ wɔ mo ahobrɛaseɛ mu, enti monnyɛ anyansafoɔ wɔ mo ankasa adwene mu. Metu mo nyinaa fo, montie me nsɛm. Na ɛdeɛn na saa nsɛm no ka? Na meka sɛ, ɛnam adom a wɔde ama me no so, kyerɛ onipa biara a ɔwɔ mo mu sɛ, ɔnnsusu ne ho nkyɛn sɛdeɛ ɛsɛ sɛ ɔsusu, na mmom ɔnsusu no anifere so, sɛdeɛ Onyankopɔn akyɛ gyidie susudeɛ ama onipa biara no.

Daabi, gyina, susuw nsɛm a mede ama wo seesei ara no ho, bue wo Twerɔnsɛm no na kenkan nsɛm yinom bio. Tie na gye di, ɛfiri sɛ ɛsɛ sɛ wosusu wo ho yie, na mama wo ne me atra ase na mama wo ne me atra ɔsoro tenabea wɔ me, Kristo Yesu mu. Nanso ɛsɛ sɛ wosusu no anifere so, ɛfiri sɛ Onyankopɔn de gyidie susudeɛ korɔ no ara ama onipa biara a ɔwɔ mo mu, enti honhom a wɔde ama no nkyɛn anaa ɛnsua wɔ wo nuanom mu, na mmom wɔde adom ama mo mu biara, sɛdeɛ Kristo akyɛdeɛ susudeɛ teɛ. Ɛno nti na ɔka sɛ: Berɛ a ɔforo kɔɔ soro no, ɔfaa nnommumfa, na ɔde akyɛdeɛ maa nnipa. Mede ebinom ama sɛ asomafoɔ: na ebinom sɛ adiyifoɔ: na ebinom sɛ asɛmpakafoɔ: na ebinom sɛ asɔfoɔ, ne akyerɛkyerɛfoɔ: Sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a ahoteefoɔ bɛyɛ pɛ, ama ɔsom adwuma no, ama Kristo nipadua no anya nkɔsoɔ. Mommrɛ mo ho ase mma mo ho mo ho na monnyã mo ahantan a mfasoɔ nni so no, ɛfiri sɛ ɛma mpaapaemu ba mo mu. Adɛn nti na monnte aseɛ sɛ ɛnsɛ sɛ mpaapaemu biara ba mo mu, me mma. Kristo nipadua no mu apaapae anaa? Ɛdeɛn na meka kyerɛɛ Korintofoɔ no?

Afei me srɛ mo, anuanom, wɔ yɛn Awurade Yesu Kristo din mu, sɛ mo nyinaa nka asɛm korɔ, na mmpaapaemu biara nna mo mu: na mmom momma wɔnka mo abɔ mu koraa wɔ adwene korɔ, ne atemmuo korɔ mu. Monnyɛ baako wɔ me, Awurade Yesu Kristo mu anaa? Nanso mpaapaemu pii wɔ mo mu, na mode nkitinkiti nketenkete ho ka na mopete mo ho mo ho. Monhu sɛ wɔ mo mpaapaemu ne mo akyidi kasa mu no, moma Satan ani gye, na ɔhoahoa ne ho wɔ mo mmɔdenbɔ a mfasoɔ nni so a mode ko tia mo ho mo ho no ho, ɛfiri sɛ mo mu bebree nni ahobrɛaseɛ. Na wɔasi mo aso sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a morentumi nte mo ho mo ho nne, na moamua mo ani, na wɔde ahantan ne gyidie a wonni ahyɛ mu ma, ɛfiri sɛ mo mu pii susuw sɛ wɔwɔ nokorɛ nyinaa. Na mobu mo nuanom nsɛm animtiaa, na mobu nsɛm a mede ama wɔn ama mo no animtiaa, na wɔ mo ahantan mu no, monni ahobrɛaseɛ a ɛho hia sɛ mobɛtie na moasusu. Na sɛ onipa nte asɛm a, ɛyɛ nkwaseasɛm ne aniwuo ma no. Na bio, adɛn nti na wubu wo nuanom animtiaa, na ɛsɛ sɛ yɛn nyinaa gyina Kristo atɛnnwa no anim? Metu wo fo, m’Asɔre, sɛ monpo mo ahantan ne mo ahomasoɔ akwan no na mommra me nkyɛn sɛ nkwa Asɛm no suani, na mene Onyankopɔn Asɛm a ɛyɛɛ honam no.

Enti fa wo kra nyinaa tie na fa w’aso to nnyigyei a ɛwɔ w’aso mu no so na hu me nsɛm. Enti te nnoɔma a ɛsisi nnidisoɔ nnidisoɔ a wɔde ama wɔ m’Asɛm no mu no ase, na wɔadaadaa mo mu pii ama wɔayɛ mpataku a wɔyɛ kɔm a mode onnimdeɛ di wɔn akyi, ɛfiri sɛ moatie nguanhwɛfoɔ a wɔayera no. Hwan na woyɛ a woka asɛmpa sɛ m’Asɔre no rennhyiam mma me nkyɛn, sɛ merenhyia m’Asɔre wɔ wiem berɛ a mede nteɛmu kɛseɛ ne ɔbɔfo panyin nne ne Onyankopɔn totorobɛnto bɛba no? Na totorobɛnto no bɛhyɛn na awufoɔ a wɔwɔ Kristo mu no bɛsɔre kane na mo a mote ase na moaka no renni wɔn anim, na wɔbɛfa mo nyinaa akɔ mununkum no mu akɔhyia me na daa daa mobɛtena me nkyɛn. Nsɛm yinom yɛ papa dodo sɛ mobɛgye adi anaa? Anaasɛ mobɛgye Yudafoɔ nguanhwɛfoɔ a wɔayera no nsɛm adi asene mo Awurade ne mo agyenkwa, Kristo Yesu nne? Adɛn nti na monni gyidie wɔ m’Asɛm no mu anaa nsɛm a ɛka bɔ m’Asɛm no mu? Monnte deɛ Twerɔnsɛm mu ahintasɛm yɛ anaa ne atirimpɔ anaa?

Mɛka akyerɛ wo: Ahintasɛm yɛ biribi a wɔde sie nanso wɔka ho asɛm sie a wɔda no adi wɔ berɛ a ɛsɛ mu. Na adɛn nti na Agya no bɛyɛ biribi a ɛte saa, wobisa? Wonkenkanee m’amaneɛbɔ no anaa? Nanso yɛka Onyankopɔn nyansa wɔ ahintasɛm mu, nyansa a ahintaw a Onyankopɔn hyɛɛ ansa na wiase reba, de kɔ yɛn animuonyam mu. Eyi a wiase yi mu ahemfo no mu biara nnim: na sɛ wɔnim a, anka wɔremmɔ animuonyam Awurade asɛnnua mu. Hwan na woyɛ a wubu m’Agya nhyehyɛeɛ ne n’akwan animtiaa? Na ɔnhia ɔfotufoɔ, ɛwɔ mu sɛ mpempem du mpempem du atwa ne tenabea ahengua no ho ahyia deɛ.

Enti momfa me nsɛm nkyekye mo werɛ, na wɔagye mo afiri abufuo mu wɔ me mu. Afei, wɔ mo gyidie a enni mu akwan so no, mogyigye akyinnye fa adeyɛ yi berɛ ho. Ɛwɔ mu sɛ mo mu binom ka sɛ nhyiamu biara nni hɔ deɛ, nanso mo mu pii gyigye akyinnye fa berɛ a ɛbɛsi wɔ Yakob amanehunu ho no ho. Mɛma no ada hɔ fann ama wo sɛ wobɛtie a, ɛwɔ mu sɛ mo mu pii bɛpo adeyɛ yi denneennen, na wɔbɛpo me mpo na wɔde wɔn nkrabea ahyɛ mu koraa sɛ wɔbɛkɔ akɔfa wɔn gyinabea wɔ mmaabunu a wonnim nyansa no mu. Na ɛdeɛn na m’Asɛm no ka?

Afei yɛsrɛ mo, anuanom, wɔ yɛn Awurade Yesu Kristo ba a ɔreba no, ne yɛn a yɛboaboa yɛn ano kɔ ne nkyɛn no nti, Sɛ ɛnsɛ sɛ wɔkanyan mo ntɛm fi mo adwene mu, na ɛnsɛ sɛ honhom anaa asɛm anaa krataa biara haw mo, sɛnea ɛteɛ sɛ efi yɛn hɔ, te sɛ nea Kristo da no abɛn. Mma obiara nnaadaa mo wɔ ɔkwan biara so: na saa da no remma, gye sɛ ɔtwe a ɔtwe ne ho no aba kane, na wɔada bɔne nipa no adi, owuo ba no mpo. (Monhwɛ Byzantine, me mma, na Twerɔnsɛm aporɔ pii afiri gnostic nkyerɛwee mu, nkyerɛwee a ɛnyɛ nokorɛ a nnipa bɔnefoɔ de maeɛ. Monnte nsonsonoeɛ a ɛda Twerɔnsɛm mu nkyerɛwee ne Twerɔnsɛm nkyerɛaseɛ ntam anaa?) Wɔbɛhwe ase afiri gyidie mu wɔ ɔwaeɛ mu, na wɔn a wɔpo sɛ wɔbɛfiri gyidie no mu no bɛfiri asase so akɔhyia me wɔ wiem.

Na yei mu na mmaabunu du no abɛbuo no wɔ. Mmaabunu nnum a wɔnim nyansa tɔɔ ngo na wɔtetee wɔn kaneadua na wɔsiesiee wɔn ho berɛ a ayeforɔkunu no duruiɛ, na nnum ansiesie wɔn ho na wɔammɔ mmɔden antɔ ngo, na mmom wɔbɔɔ mmɔden sɛ wɔbɛgye afiri afoforɔ ahiadeɛ mu. Mmaabunu nnum a wɔasiesie wɔn ho no toaa so ne ayeforɔkunu no, na nnum a wonnim nyansa no srɛɛ sɛ wɔmma wɔn kwan akyire yi na wɔka kyerɛɛ wɔn sɛ, monfiri me so, mennim mo da. Monnnum me honhom a ɛwɔ mo mu a ɛbɛdi nsɛm yinom ho adanseɛ no. Te aseɛ sɛ owuo ba no ntumi nnya tumi na Satan ahennie a ɛdi mu no ntumi nsɔre mfiri bun no mu kɔsi sɛ wɔbɛyi deɛ ɔsi ano kwan no afiri hɔ, na ɛsiane sɛ wɔagye wo afiri abufuo mu nti, wɔbɛyi deɛ ɔsi ano kwan no afiri hɔ berɛ a maboaboa m’ayeforɔ ano akɔ me nkyɛn wɔ mununkum no mu berɛ a ɔhyia me wɔ wiem no.

Afei Yakob amanehunu mfeɛ nson no awieeɛ no, ateneneefoɔ wusɔreɛ no ba. Hwɛ! Na mehunuu ahengua, na wɔtenaa so, na wɔde atemmuo maa wɔn: na mehunuu wɔn a wɔtwaa wɔn ti maa Yesu adanseɛ, ne Onyankopɔn asɛm nti, ne wɔn a wɔansɔre aboa no, ne ne suban, ne wɔn a wɔannya n’agyiraehyɛdeɛ no wɔn moma so, anaa wɔn nsam no kra; na wɔtenaa ase na wɔne Kristo dii hene mfeɛ apem. Nanso awufoɔ a aka no antenase bio kɔsii sɛ mfeɛ apem no twaam. Yei ne owusɔreɛ a ɛdi kan. Nhyira ne kronkron ne deɛ ɔwɔ kyɛfa wɔ owusɔreɛ a ɛdi kan no mu: saafoɔ so na owuo a ɛtɔ so mmienu no nni tumi, na mmom wɔbɛyɛ Onyankopɔn, ne Kristo Asɔfoɔ, na wɔne no bɛdi hene mfeɛ apem.

Wogye me nsɛm nni ara anaa? Nokorɛm, mo mu pii bɛkura mo mfomsoɔ ne nguanhwɛfoɔ a wɔayera no nsɛm mu denneennen, na mo mu pii a anka mobɛtumi aguan afiri Onyankopɔn abufuo a ɛba sɛ ɛbɛsɔ asase ahwɛ wɔ Yakob amanehunu berɛ mu no, na mmom mopɛ sɛ mopɛ abufuo kɛseɛ berɛ a madaso mo adi na masɔ mo ahwɛ, gye sɛ mobɛpo me pue a mɛpue no denneennen, na mobɛkura abufuo a wɔanhyɛ da sɛ ɛbɛba mo so no mu. Manka ankyerɛ wo sɛ firi ne mu na wo ne no annya kyɛfa wɔ ne ɔhaw no mu anaa?

Nanso, owusɔreɛ baako bio bɛba. Na mehunuu ahengua fitaa kɛseɛ bi, ne deɛ ɔte soɔ, a asase ne ɔsoro guanee firii n’anim, na wɔanhunu baabiara amma wɔn. Na mehunuu awufoɔ, nketewa ne akɛseɛ, sɛ wɔgyina Onyankopɔn anim: na wɔbuebuee nwoma no: na wɔbuee nwoma foforɔ, a ɛne Nkwa Nwoma no: na wɔbuu awufoɔ no atɛn firii nnoɔma a wɔakyerɛw wɔ nwoma no mu no mu, sɛdeɛ wɔn nnwuma teɛ. Na ɛpo no yii awufoɔ a wɔwɔ mu no maeɛ: na owuo ne asamando yii awufoɔ a wɔwɔ mu no maeɛ: na wɔbuu onipa biara atɛn sɛdeɛ n’adwuma teɛ, na yei ne atreneefoɔ wusɔreɛ me nkurɔfoɔ.

Adɛn nti na monnte aseɛ sɛ m’Asɔre no a wɔboaboa wɔn ano bɛhyia me wɔ mununkum no mu berɛ a mɛpue no yɛ soronko firi me a me ne m’Ayeforɔ ne ɔsoro dɔm bɛsan aba no ho? Adɛn nti na monnte aseɛ sɛ m’Asɔre no a wɔboaboa wɔn ano no yɛ ahintasɛm a ɔsomafoɔ Paulo daa no adi wɔ Onyankopɔn Ahintasɛm Kɛseɛ no mu. Enti ɛyɛ ahintasɛm a ɛwɔ ahintasɛm mu, na wɔan-da no adi koraa amma nnipa mma kɔsii sɛ wɔdaa no adi kyerɛɛ Paulo a ɔkyerɛwee, na makora so ama wo.

Nanso, nhwɛsoɔ ahodoɔ pii wɔ hɔ a Onyankopɔn fa nnipa kɔ ɔsoro de yɛ atirimpɔ ahodoɔ. Henok firii hɔ. Elia firii hɔ. Mefirii ɔsoro berɛ a m’asomafoɔ hwɛɛ no nwanwa so. Wɔfirii Filipo firii baabi kɔɔ baabi foforɔ berɛ a Agya no faa no maa me atirimpɔ nti. Na saa ara na wɔbɛboaboa m’ayeforɔ ano akɔ me nkyɛn wɔ mununkum no mu na wɔafa wɔn akɔ ɔsoro akɔware me na wɔasom me gyeɛ atirimpɔ ahodoɔ.

Monnte aseɛ sɛ m’Ayeforɔ bɛyɛ ahemfo ne asɔfoɔ wɔ m’Onyankopɔn ne m’Agya anim na ɔne me bɛdi hene wɔ m’ahennie a ɛreba no mu na ɔne me bɛtena daa anaa? Adɛn nti na mo bo afu sɛ mobɛgye me nsɛm adi? Wɔahyɛ mo anaa wɔde mo ayera afiri nguanhwɛfoɔ a wɔayera no nkyɛn? Nanso, mo mu pii bɛyɛ nkɔmhyɛ a ɛma ɛba mu na mode mo ho bɛhyɛ aseɛ sɛ mobɛhwe ase afiri me nkyerɛkyerɛ mu, ne titire no, nkyerɛkyerɛ a ɛfa m’Asɔre a wɔboaboa wɔn ano kɔ me nkyɛn no ho. Aniwu nka mo a mode nkyerɛkyerɛ yi ka adiyifoɔ atorofoɔ a wɔwɔ abakɔsɛm a atwam nnansa yi mu ho, na mode ka sɛ wɔbɛfa wɔn akɔ ahintasɛm mu a ɛne nkyerɛkyerɛ foforɔ a ɛfa nkyekyɛmu ahodoɔ ho no wɔ abusuabɔ.

Te aseɛ sɛ mmerɛ ahodoɔ wɔ Onyankopɔn berɛ nhyehyɛeɛ mu na ɛnyɛ nkyekyɛmu ahodoɔ, na ɛnyɛ adwene korɔ no ara. Sua adeɛ na kyerɛ sɛ wɔagye wo atom, na gyae adwene mu aniyɛdeɛ a ɛte saa no. Gyae sɛ wobɛhwɛ na woakenkan obiara nkyerɛaseɛ a ɔkyerɛ deɛ wɔakyerɛw no, na tena ase sɛdeɛ wɔakyerɛw no: Na ɛnhia sɛ onipa biara bɛkyerɛkyerɛ wo. Nanso, mo mu pii bɛdi me fɛw na wɔapo m’afotuo. Gye sɛ wɔrenyɛ biribi a ɛte sei wɔ ahintasɛm mu na Satan de nnaadaa kɛseɛ bɛba abɛkata adeyɛ yi so ɛfiri sɛ wɔbɛhu no wiase nyinaa. Wɔbɛwosow kɛseɛ, na wiase nyinaa bɛsesa afiri wiase a woanim no mu, na wɔbɛda onipa bɔnefoɔ no adi na wasɔre afiri nsõ a apɔnkɔsotefoɔ nnan no agya hɔ no mu. Ɛberɛ aso sɛ mobɛsiesie mo ho, m’Ayeforɔ, na monyɛ no ntɛm, na hwɛ me pue a mɛpue no abɛn!

Mene Onipa Ba a Henok a Onyankopɔn faa no kɔɔ ɔsoro no hyɛɛ ne ho nkɔm no.

Mene deɛ wɔsakraa no na ɔne Mose ne Elia kasaeɛ faa me firi a mɛfiri hɔ no ho, na Onyankopɔn faa Elia kɔɔ ɔsoro wɔ ne berɛ so.

Mene deɛ wɔfaa me kɔɔ mununkum mu berɛ a wɔgyee me kɔɔ ɔsoro kɔsii sɛ nnoɔma bɛsan aba tebea pa mu no.

Mene deɛ ɔsomaa Filipo kɔɔ Etiopiani piamni no nkyɛn ansa na Onyankopɔn refa no akɔ baabi foforɔ wɔ asase so.

Mene deɛ ɔfaa Paulo kɔɔ ɔsoro a ɛtɔ so mmiɛnsa na ɔkyerɛɛ no nnoɔma a wontumi nka a na ɔnni hokwan sɛ ɔbɛkyɛ no.

Mene deɛ ɔbɛsiane afiri ɔsoro de nteɛmu kɛseɛ abɛboaboa m’Ayeforɔ ano abɛhyia me wɔ wiem.

Mene deɛ ɔbɛma m’adansefoɔ mmienu no anya nkwa bio berɛ a wɔawu nnafua mmiɛnsa ne fa akyi, na mɛfa wɔn akɔ ɔsoro.

Mene Alfa ne Omega, na mene mfitiaseɛ ne awieeɛ, na hwɛ! Meba ntɛm sene sɛdeɛ wosusu, enti siesie wo ho na hyia me anaa hwe ase.

Obiara a ɔbɛbɔ Awurade din akɔsi awieeɛ no, wɔbɛgye no nkwa.
