Adansefoɔ Mmienu: Israel
Deɛ Ɛyɛ Nwono na Ɛyɛ Dɛ
Ɔsanaa 09, 2024

Prɛko pɛ na W’anidasoɔ bɛda Adi!

Mommoa mo ho ano na montie mo Awurade nne, Yesu Nasareni no nne, ɛfiri sɛ mene deɛ ɔde ne ho maeɛ prɛko, ne nyinaa, na daa daa sɛ Twam Aduan Gwan a ɔyɛ pɛ de maa Yahweh ɛnam mo bɔne nti, Onyankopɔn kɛseɛ a ɔwɔ hɔ daa no, sɛ mo ɔsɔfoɔ panin a ɔdi Melkisedek nhyehyɛeɛ akyi daa sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a mobɛnya bɔnefakyɛ. Mommra me nkyɛn, na momfa mo aso ne mo akoma mmra na momfa gyidie a ɛyɛ ma ntie me gyidie nsɛm no! Aane, mesrɛ mo sɛ montie, na ɛnyɛ sɛ montie me nsɛm no nnyegyeɛ nko, na mmom momfa mo akoma ntie sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a mobɛhunu m’adwene na moanya awerɛkyekyerɛ wɔ ɔdɔ a mewɔ ma mo no mu na momfa ahobrɛaseɛ nnye asɛm a wɔadua wɔ mo mu, a ɛtumi gye mo kra nkwa no.

Na mekasa kyerɛ anokwafoɔ a wɔwɔ m’Asɔre mu, me nipadua no, m’ayeforɔ no, wɔn a merebɛboa wɔn ano aba me nkyɛn wɔ ɔsoro ahobammɔ mu wɔ Yahweh ahennwa dan mu no nyinaa. Na mene deɛ ɔhwɛ mo kra so na ɔhwɛ mo nkɔsoɔ so sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a mobɛnyini sɛ afifideɛ tenenee wɔ m’anim, na mene deɛ Yahweh de me ama sɛ otufoɔ ne ɔhwɛfoɔ ma ne turo kronkron a ɛfa agyefoɔ kra ho no. Na mo ne aboɔden aboɔ a ɛsom bo na Yahweh agyapadeɛ pii mu no, mowɔ deɛ ɛsom bo a wɔannya ɔboɔ biara anhwɛ ase wɔ mo nkwagyeɛ mu na wɔammɔ mmɔden biara sɛ wɔbɛpra mo na wɔaboa mo na wɔde mo akyerɛ Yahweh, mo Agya a ɔwɔ tumi nyinaa no sɛ ababaa kronkron a nkekaeɛ, anaa nsensɛn, anaa biribiara a ɛte saa nni ne ho. Enti monnsuro, na mma adwenem naayɛ biara ntra mo mu fa me Nsɛm no mu deɛ ɛsua koraa ho!

Na enti mesrɛ mo, me dɔfoɔ, adɛn nti na mo akoma haw mo? Adɛn nti na mowoso mo ti te sɛ deɛ mokura animguaseɛ? Moama Asatane no awiawia ne ho akɔ mo akoma mu na ɔbobɔ awerɛkyekyerɛ nsɛm a ɛnyɛ nokware a ɛde gyidie a enni mu ba berɛ a wɔpɛ sɛ wɔtete na wɔsɛe deɛ ɛyɛ kronkron nyinaa mu? Mobɛfa ɔwɔ dada no wɔ n’anifereɛ a ɛdaadaa daa ne ne nnaadaa a ɛkɔ so daa a ɔbɛsesa ne ho akɔ mo aso mu de nsɛm a ɛdaadaa a ɛbɛbutu mo gyidie agu, sɛdeɛ ɛsi yɛɛ pii a wɔama wɔn adwene asɛe afiri adwene baako a wɔde si me, Kristo Yesu mo Awurade so no?

Na obi foforɔ biara nni hɔ a ɔbɛhwɛ mo, na afoforɔ nyinaa yɛ nnaadaafoɔ a wɔyɛ wɔn ho sɛ wɔyɛ saa na Yesu foforɔ biara nni hɔ ka deɛ ɛyɛ nnaadaa ne deɛ ɛnsom bo a wɔayɛ sɛ wɔde bɛdaadaa. Enti mesrɛ mo sɛ monnbu ani nngu nhunumu a mede bɛma mo yi so sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a mobɛkɔ so agyina sɛ kanea hann wɔ anibereɛ a ɛtwa toɔ yi mu ansa na wiase a moanim no atwam na wɔasakra no afiri ne mprempren abosonsom mu akɔ daa atemmuo a ɛbɛba seesei sɛ daa asotwe ama abɔnefoɔ a wɔpɛ sɛ wɔfa m’agyapadeɛ no. Na asase yɛ me dea ne deɛ ɛwɔ mu nyinaa, na mene Awurade mu Awurade ne Ahemfo mu Hene kɛseɛ a ɔwɔ hɔ daa no, mo Awurade tenenee no, na mene deɛ ɔbɛgye m’agyapadeɛ afiri nkurɔfoɔ a wɔnni ho kwan na wɔnyɛ ahemfo na wɔnyɛ anyame na wɔnyɛ ahɔhoɔ a wɔfiri wiase foforɔ mu na mmom wɔn a wɔahwe ase a wɔyɛ wɔn ho sɛ wɔyɛ saa ɛfiri sɛ wɔn nyinaa yɛ nnaadaafoɔ a wɔyɛ wɔn ho sɛ wɔyɛ saa. Na wɔn nna bɛba awieeɛ a ɛwɔ hɔ daa wɔ wɔn daa agyapadeɛ mu wɔ ogya framfram a ɛyɛ bruu mu.

Na awoɔ yaw no nnyae seesei, na nsɛnkyerɛnneɛ pii wɔ soro ne nsɛnkyerɛnneɛ pii wɔ asase so a ɛda adi pefee na ennyae. Na wɔde yei ama sɛ dawubɔ na moanya anidasoɔ, enti mummmu no adewa na monnkyerɛkyerɛ mu sɛdeɛ spotɔfoɔ no yɛ no! Yahweh nsa ano adwuma a ɔnam n’abɔfoɔ akɛseɛ no nsa so de maeɛ, na nokorɛm no, Onyankopɔn ankasa nsateaa na ɛtwerɛɛ no wɔ berɛ bi mu, wɔ n’ahyɛdeɛ a ɛwɔ hɔ daa no so no bɔ ɔsoro nsɛm a ɛreba yi ho dawuro, na ɛda no adu na ɛwɔ mo so, me dɔfoɔ, enti monnsuro! Mmerɛ no adu sɛ wɔbɛboaboa m’anokwafoɔ ano akɔhyia me wɔ mununkum mu berɛ a mede ɔbɔfoɔ panin nne ne Onyankopɔn totorobɛnto kɛseɛ no bɛsiane afiri soro, enti monnye me Nsɛm no nni ɛfiri sɛ ɛyɛ aane ne amen!

Hwan ne deɛn na ɛwɔ hɔ a ɛsɛ sɛ ɛhaw mo, me mma? Mosuro sɛ ayeforɔkunu no remma na ɔrenso n’ayeforɔ nsa wɔ daa apam mu sɛdeɛ wɔhyɛɛ ho bɔ tete no? Onyankopɔn Asɛm no ayɛ deɛ mfasoɔ nni so? Onyankopɔn mma! Sɛdeɛ wɔatwerɛ na wɔabɔ ho dawuro no, saa ara na ɛbɛyɛ nokorɛ sɛ prɛko pɛ na mɛtwe mo afiri abufuo mu akɔ ɔsoro ahobammɔ mu baabi a makɔsiesie baabi ama mo, na me Agya fie no, adan pii wɔ hɔ. Na saa ara na mobɛtena me nkyɛn daa na mobɛyɛ ahemfo ne asɔfoɔ wɔ Yahweh, Onyankopɔn kɛseɛ a ɔwɔ hɔ daa no anim, na saa na wahyehyɛ na wasiesie wɔ mo asetena mu, na hwan na ɔbɛtumi asi Onyankopɔn a ɔwɔ hɔ daa no atirimpɔ ne ne nhyehyɛeɛ no kwan?

Meka nokorɛ kyerɛ mo sɛ, biribiara ne obiara nni hɔ a ɔbɛtumi asi El Shaddai atirimpɔ no kwan koraa na obiara ntumi nnyina (wɔ ɔsɔretia ketewaa bi mpo mu) a wɔremma no nnyɛ komm wɔ nwonwa mu wɔ tumi, animuonyam ne ahopopo a ɛba berɛ a Yahweh keka ne ho kakra sɛ ɔbɛsɔre afiri n’ahennwa a ɛwɔ hɔ daa no so na ɔsoro ne asase nyinaa woso wɔ ne mmaeɛ kɛseɛ no anim. Na ɛwom sɛ Yahweh de ne ho ahunta ayamyɛ, ɔdɔ, ne mmɔborɔhunu mu nna pii seesei (na da koro te sɛ mfeɛ apem wɔ Onyankopɔn a ɔwɔ hɔ daa no anim) nanso seesei ne berɛ a Yahweh apaw a ɔbɛyi ne ho adi akyerɛ n’abɔdeɛ, berɛ a wɔn nyinaa bɛhunu sɛ Onyankopɔn wɔ soro, na wɔbɛte aseɛ yie sɛ sɛdeɛ ɛteɛ wɔ soro no, saa ara na ɛbɛyɛ wɔ fam ha wɔ asase so ɛfiri sɛ Yahweh yɛ ɔsoro ne asase nyinaa Nyankopɔn na ɔdi hene kɛseɛ wɔ n’abɔdeɛ so na obi foforɔ biara nni hɔ a ɔka ne ho wɔ ahoɔden, animuonyam ne tumi mu. (Na wɔn a wɔahwe ase no deɛ, daa suro akye wɔn) Enti monnsuro, Ɔsorosoroni no Mma, na Onyankopɔn kɛseɛ a ɔwɔ hɔ daa no, Yahweh ankasa ne deɛ ɔko ma mo na ɔno na ɔbɛgye, atɔ, na wasakra mo asetena ankasa de ayɛ no daa animuonyam wɔ anibuankaeɛ mu.

Enti adɛn nti na mo bo ntumi ntɔ mo yɛm? Mote nka sɛ mo Awurade a ɔwɔ hɔ daa ne ayeforɔkunu no apo mo, m’ayeforɔ? Mo nkwagyeɛ a ɛwɔ hɔ daa no agya ne nipadua no, na wama no ayɛ ti nni? Onyankopɔn mma sɛ wɔbɛdwene biribi a ɛte saa ho mpo. Deɛn na Yahweh abɔ ho dawuro akyerɛ n’abɔdeɛ sɛ ɛrebɛba ntɛm a amma akyire yi? Noa Nsɛm no tɔɔ asase kesee so sɛ anka bɔhyɛ hunu berɛ a na ɔyɛ ɔtenenee sɛnkafoɔ mfeɛ ɔha aduonu wɔ wiase a na ɛwɔ hɔ saa berɛ no mu? Dabi! Na mmom prɛko pɛ na wɔde ahoɔden kɛseɛ gyaa nsuo no mu wɔ anibuankaeɛ mu na ɛforo kɔɔ mmepɔ a ɛware paa wɔ asase so no so wɔ nna a ɛdi akyire no mu.

Na enti metu mo fo sɛ monnkɔka wɔn a wɔpɛ sɛ wɔyɛ deɛ wɔnnim no ho bio. Wɔpɛ sɛ wɔyɛ deɛ wɔnnim sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a wɔbɛbu wɔn ani agu so sɛ Onyankopɔn asɛm so na ɔsoro wɔ hɔ firi tete, na asase gyina nsuo so ne nsuo mu: Ɛno nti wiase a na ɛwɔ hɔ saa berɛ no, a nsuo yiri faa so no, sɛee: Na ɔsoro ne asase a ɛwɔ hɔ seesei no, wɔnam asɛm korɔ no ara so akora so, na wɔde asie ama ogya de akɔsi atemmuo ne nnipa abɔnefoɔ ɔsɛeɛ da no. Na enti mepae mu ka bio sɛ ogya ne atemmuo ne nnipa abɔnefoɔ ɔsɛeɛ da no adu, sɛdeɛ wɔhyɛɛ ho bɔ tete no, na saa ara na ɛbɛba prɛko pɛ.

Na enti mesrɛ mo bio, me mma nketewa, Onyankopɔn yɛ onipa na ɔbɛdi atorɔ? Me, Yesu Kristo tenenee no, wontumi mfa me ho nto so sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a me werɛ bɛfiri mo na mɛma mo anidasoɔ a ɛnyɛ nokware? Me werɛ afiri mo na mmom makɔpɛ nam? Mesrɛ mo, me dɔfoɔ, mede me mogya a ɛtwa toɔ no maeɛ wɔ amanehunu mu wɔ asɛnnua no so sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a mɛgya mo berɛ a mahyɛ mo bɔ a ɛso na ɛsom bo, bɔhyɛ a Yahweh ankasa de ama wɔ ne daa ahintasɛm mu, Onyankopɔn ahintasɛm kɛseɛ no?

Onyankopɔn mma sɛ wɔbɛdwene nneɛma a ɛte saa ho mpo na enti monhunu Satan adwenem bɔne a ɔto twene mo no, na mompo na monka n’anim na momfa kronkron twerɛnsɛm, Yahweh bɔhyɛ a ɛyɛ aane ne amen no nsi ananmu. Na mmom mmerɛ nyinaa ne titire mmerɛ a ɛte sɛ yei mu no, wɔatwerɛ na enti Paulo twerɛɛ Korintofoɔ no wɔ m’ananmu sɛ: Na mesuro sɛ sɛdeɛ ɔwɔ no nam n’anifereɛ so daadaa Hawa no, saa ara nso na mo adwene bɛsɛe afiri adwene baako a ɛwɔ Kristo mu no ho. Na enti metu mo fo, me mma, me dɔfoɔ a mepɛ mo asɛm na mehwɛ mo kwan no sɛ monkɔ so nnya adwene baako a mode si me so, ne me nsɛm so, na mene Onyankopɔn Asɛm a ɛyɛɛ ɔhonam na Onyankopɔn Asɛm biara bɛba mu nokorɛm, na me Nsɛm no mu deɛ ɛsua koraa no mu baako mpo rempa abɔ a Honhom Kronkron kɛseɛ a ɔwɔ hɔ daa, a ɔyɛ Yahweh, Onyankopɔn kɛseɛ a ɔwɔ hɔ daa no de kanyan no!

Na enti metu mo fo, me mma, me dɔfoɔ a mepɛ mo asɛm na mehwɛ mo kwan no, mo m’anigyeɛ ne m’abotire no: monhome wɔ me mu na monhome wɔ me nnwuma mu na monnyae mo ankasa deɛ no. Na wɔatwerɛ sɛ: Enti ahomegyeɛ da hɔ ma Onyankopɔn nkurɔfoɔ. Na deɛ wakɔ ne homegyeɛ mu no, ɔno nso agyae n’ankasa nnwuma, sɛdeɛ Onyankopɔn yɛɛ ne deɛ no. Enti mommrɛ mo ho sɛ mobɛkɔ me homegyeɛ no mu, na obiara anhwe ase wɔ gyidie a enni mu ho nhwɛsoɔ korɔ no ara akyi. Me mma, mommrɛ mo ho sɛ mobɛgyae mo ankasa nnwuma na monyɛ nnwuma a mede ma mo sɛ monwie na mummfa mo ho nnto mo ankasa nteaseɛ so na mmom mo akwan nyinaa mu no, monnye me ntom, Kristo Yesu mo Awurade a ɔkɔɔ soro no, na mobɛnya ahomegyeɛ wɔ adidibea a ɛyɛ frɔmfrɔm ne ahobammɔ mu, titire wɔ me nsa a ɛwɔ tumi no hwɛ ne ne banbɔ ase, na mene Yeshua HaMashiach a ɔsan yɛ mo guanhwɛfoɔ panin a ɔrebɛyi ne ho adi no!

Na enti mesrɛ mo, me dɔfoɔ, mobɛsan akɔtena spotɔfoɔ nkwaseafoɔ a wɔdi fɛw na wɔpɛ sɛ wɔsɔ mo hwɛ kɔ wɔn gyidie a enni mu akwan no mu? Mobɛbu mo ani agu me nsɛm ne nsɛnkyerɛnneɛ, anwanwadeɛ ne nwonwadeɛ pii a atwa mo ho ahyia no so? Mo adwene yɛ mo sɛ mobɛtumi akɔ so atena sɛ wɔn a wɔnnim amanehunu ne atemmuo a ɛreba no? Mobɛsan akɔtena wɔn a wɔabu wɔn atɛn dedaw, a wɔpo adwensakra na mmom wɔpaw atemmuo ne asotwe a emu yɛ den no mu? Meka kyerɛ mo pefee sɛ sɛ mopɛ a, mobɛhunu mo ho sɛ nkwaseafoɔ a wɔkɔ so bu me kɔkɔbɔ so na wɔtwe wɔn aso a ɛho nhia na enti wɔabɔ mo kɔkɔ.

Monte aseɛ sɛ wiase a mote mu seesei no nte sɛdeɛ na ɛteɛ mpo nnawɔtwe kakra a atwam ni, na ɛkɔ so sakra ntɛmntɛm. Monte aseɛ sɛ mo mu biara ntenaa wiase a ɛwɔ hɔ seesei no mu da, na wiase a mote mu seesei no de nkɔnsɔnkɔnsɔn agu atemmuo mu nanso wɔma no kwan ma ɛde ayeforohyia a ɛreba wɔ soro a me Kristo Yesu bɛware m’ayeforɔ anokwafoɔ no daa na saa ara na wɔbɛtena me nkyɛn daa no ho dawubɔ a ɛhyerɛn no ma. Na sɛdeɛ wɔtwe m’ayeforɔ ne m’Asɔre no prɛko pɛ no, saa ara na ɔsɛeɛ a ɛba prɛko pɛ bɛba spotɔfoɔ ne nnipa bɔne a wɔserew mo ka sɛ: Ɔkyena bɛyɛ sɛ nnɛ, na nnɛ yɛ sɛ nnera, na merenhu amanehunu biara na menyɛ okunafoɔ na mmom meyɛ ɔhemmaa a medi nneɛma nyinaa so hene! Gye sɛ prɛko pɛ na wɔn sɛeɛ bɛba wɔn so sɛ ɔbaa a ɔrewo, na wɔrennya kwan nguan.

Enti momfiri wɔn mu na monyɛ soronko wɔ nneɛma nyinaa mu ne mo akwan nyinaa mu. Montumi nnya kyɛfa biara wɔ wɔn mu anaa mobɛnya kyɛfa wɔ wɔn ɔhaw no mu. Monte aseɛ sɛ nsɛm asi dedaw, nsɛm a ɛbɛka abɔnefoɔ no, nanso mede ahobammɔ bɛma m’anokwafoɔ sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a saa atemmuo yi remmpira mo, me mma. Na saa awoɔ yaw a ennyae yi yɛ deɛ ɛbɛnyane wɔn a wɔada no mu deɛ ɔtwa toɔ na ɛbɛpaapae wɔn a wɔbɛkɔ Adaka no mu ansa na amanehunu aba prɛko pɛ na berɛ asa no mu deɛ ɔtwa toɔ. Montumi ntena asetena a anidahɔ nni mu bio na ɛsɛ sɛ monantew nyansa kɛseɛ mu enti montie me kɔkɔbɔ na monte aseɛ sɛ mede nyansa ne nteaseɛ kɛseɛ ama mo sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a mobɛtumi ahunu mmerɛ a mote mu no, mpo wɔ wɔn a wɔada nko pa ara wɔ amanehunu a ɛreba no ho na wɔbɛhu amane esiane so no mu.

Enti monte aseɛ sɛ berɛ a aka no nka hwee na ɛsom Yahweh atirimpɔ nko ansa na atemmuo aba prɛko pɛ. Aane, wɔma ɔtamfo no kwan ma ɔsiesie ne ho sɛ ɔbɛto ahyɛ Ahuntaeɛ Babilon so na saa na Yahweh de ama no sɛ ɔnyɛ. Na mmom monte aseɛ yie sɛ Satan yɛ ɔtamfo a wɔadi ne so nkonim a wɔde no abɔ asase so ama atemmuo na wɔma no kwan sɛ ɔnkɔ so nyɛ Yahweh atirimpɔ nko na ɔbɛtena komm atwɛn sɛ wɔbɛyi deɛ ɔsi kwan no afiri hɔ na ɔntumi nyɛ biribiara nsen deɛ wɔma no kwan, enti monnsuro gyata a se nni n’anom no bobɔmo ɛfiri sɛ wɔde ɔheneba ketewa no abɔ nkonimdi mu! Na nokorɛm no, wɔn a wɔahwe ase no suro mo na wɔte aseɛ na wɔnim sɛ wɔn a wɔyɛɛ wɔn basabasa pɛn no ne wɔn a wɔde atemmuo ne asotwe tenenee bɛba wɔn ankasa bosoom mu daa daa. Dɛn? Monnim sɛ mobɛbu abɔfoɔ atɛn?

Na enti mesrɛ mo, me dɔfoɔ, mobɛpo m’adom ne me mmɔborɔhunu a wɔde ama wɔn a wɔayera no, berɛ a wɔahyehyɛ atemmuo ne amanehunu sɛ ɛbɛba prɛko pɛ no? Mo werɛ afiri mfeɛ pii a mede adom ne mmɔborɔhunu a ɛnsɛ mo maeɛ mo sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a mobɛdi mo bɔne ho dwuma ansa na moasakra aba tenenee mu? Mobɛpo wɔn a wɔpɛ sɛ wɔkɔ Adaka no mu sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a yɛbɛyɛ baako anibuankaeɛ bi ansa? Mewɔ mo mu gyidie pa, me mma, na enti mesrɛ mo aboterɛ ne ntoboaseɛ, ansa na amanehunu aba prɛko pɛ.

Sɛ mo mu bi wɔ hɔ a ɔdwene sɛ ɔwɔ biribi a ɔde bɛba atemmuo so wɔ Satan ne n’ahennie so a, mewɔ pii sene deɛ ɛbɛba mo akoma mu da. Na mo mu hwan na wako atia Satan, de akɔsi mo mogya a ɛtwa toɔ so berɛ a mopɛɛ sɛ mosɛn dua a ɛyɛ basabasa no so wɔ amanehunu kɛseɛ mu. Na mepɛɛ sɛ mesoa m’asɛnnua na mede me ho maeɛ wɔ nkonimdie a ɛwɔ hɔ daa mu berɛ a abɔnefoɔ yɛɛ me bɔne, berɛ a megyinaa mo ananmu wɔ atemmuo mu wɔ amanehunu kɛseɛ mu wɔ apono no akyi wɔ abɔnefoɔ a wɔyɛ abofono paa no mu. Me mogya yɛ deɛ bɔne nni ho na wɔantumi amfa bɔne biara ammɔ me so. Nanso wɔ ɔdɔ a mewɔ ma me Agya ne ɔdɔ a mewɔ ma mo nti, mepɛɛ sɛ mede me nkwa to hɔ na mɛsan ayɛ bio wɔ anibuankaeɛ mu, ɛwom sɛ biribi a ɛte saa ho nhia deɛ. Enti mpo wɔ mo mprempren ahokyere mu no, monni me ntoboaseɛ ho nhwɛsoɔ so na monim sɛ prɛko pɛ na mobɛkɔ daa nkwagyeɛ mu!

Na enti mesrɛ mo sɛ monkɔ so nnyina pintinn de akɔsi awieeɛ no, awieeɛ a wɔtwe mo prɛko pɛ wɔ anibuankaeɛ mu kɔ ɔsoro kɔ daa akatua ne daa atirimpɔ mu, nanso awufoɔ a wɔwɔ Kristo mu no bɛsɔre kane na mobɛka wɔn ho prɛko pɛ wɔ daa anigyeɛ mu. Na daa atirimpɔ a wɔafrɛ mo, apaw mo, asɔ mo ano, na wɔakora mo so no bɛhyɛ Onyankopɔn a ɔkorɔn sen biara no daa atirimpɔ no ma na biribiara rebɛda adi wɔ mo mu, me mma, enti monhyerɛn mma me sɛ me nkanea seesei ara wɔ ahomegyeɛ a ɛtwa toɔ yi mu ansa na amanehunu a ɛba prɛko pɛ no abɔ asase. Na mede wɔn bɛbrɛ mo sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a mode wɔn bɛbrɛ me, na modaa so yɛ m’ananmusifoɔ de kɔsi sɛ wɔbɛtwe mo akɔ ɔsoro prɛko pɛ. Na prɛko pɛ na amanehunu kɛseɛ bɛba.

Monte aseɛ sɛ berɛ asa na biribiara ntumi nsan nneɛma a ɛreba na ɛresiesie ne ho atia asase so tefoɔ no akyi, na abɔnefoɔ atemmuo a ɛreba no so. Na enti mesrɛ na mehyɛ mo sɛ monyɛ saa mmɔborɔhunu, adom ne mmɔborɔhunu korɔ no ara a Yahweh de akyerɛ mo wɔ Yesu Kristo anim ama mo nkwagyeɛ no. Mobɛtumi ayɛ anokwafoɔ de akɔsi awieeɛ? Mobɛma mo ho kwan ama moakɔ me daa ahomegyeɛ no mu a morennyae wɔ simma a ɛtwa toɔ no mu? Mobɛde mo akoma ne mo adwene nyinaa asi me nsɛm so na moate aseɛ sɛ prɛko pɛ na ɛbɛsakra daa na prɛko pɛ ne seesei.

Berɛ biara nni hɔ a aka sɛ Ahuntaeɛ Babilon bɛsakra n’adwene na berɛ biara nni hɔ bio wɔ m’adom ne mmɔborɔhunu mmerɛ yi mu ɛfiri sɛ berɛ asa. Na mmerɛ biara wɔ ne berɛ ne n’atirimpɔ na mmerɛ awieeɛ no, ɔgyeɛ a ɛba prɛko pɛ bɛba wɔn a wɔagye wɔn no so enti ma asase so agyefoɔ no nka sɛ saa na ɔno na watɔ wɔn afiri ɔtamfo no nsam. Na mma ɔkorɔmfoɔ no nnwia mo anigyeɛ na ayeforɔ bɛn na ɔdi awerɛhoɔ wɔ n’ayeforohyia da, ayeforohyia a ɛyɛ animuonyam, kronkron a obiara ntumi nsɛe no na ɔnha no.

Enti monnya awerɛkyekyerɛ wɔ me awerɛkyekyerɛ Nsɛm no mu na monnyae asetena sɛ wɔn a wɔagu wɔn anim ase wɔ aniwu ne afɔbuo mu. Na mo, m’ayeforɔ, ne wɔn a mayɛ mo deɛ nkekaeɛ nni ho na mfomsoɔ nni mo ho wɔ Yahweh ahennwa no anim, na moyɛ nnipa a wɔsɔre firii awufoɔ mu no mu nnipa a wɔdi kan. Ɛwom sɛ mo a mote ase na moaka no mu deɛ morenhu owuo da deɛ, nanso wɔwoo mo wɔ owuo mu wɔ mmarato ne bɔne mu. Na moatwam afiri owuo mu tẽẽ (a sɛ mowuiɛ a anka mobɛwu), akɔ daa nkwa mu a moanya ne nsaano ka dedaw, Yahweh nsɔano kɛseɛ a wɔde asɔ mo ano ama saa ɔgyeɛ da a ɛba prɛko pɛ no. Na wɔawo mo bio firi aba a ɛnsɛe mu na moyɛ Ɔsorosoroni Nyankopɔn no mma, Onyankopɔn a ɔwɔ tumi nyinaa no mma!

Enti, monnya awerɛkyekyerɛ na monhome na mo ne me nyɛ adwuma wɔ mmerɛ yi nkonimdie mmirikatuo yi mu. Na berɛ biara nni hɔ a ɛte sɛ yei wɔ asase so da na berɛ biara a ɛte sɛ yei remma bio. Mpo berɛ a Onyankopɔn mma a wɔwɔ hɔ daa no de adwene hwɛ wɔn daa nkwa ahome a ɛdi kan no kwan sɛ mmofra a wɔwɔ daa nkwa mu kɔ Onyankopɔn a ɔwɔ hɔ daa no anim baabi a wɔbɛware wɔn Awurade a ɔwɔ hɔ daa no wɔ daa apam mu, na...prɛko pɛ, na ɛbɛyɛ!

Mene deɛ ɔde ne ho maeɛ prɛko, ne nyinaa, na daa daa sɛ Twam Aduan Gwan a ɔyɛ pɛ no.

Merebɛboaboa anokwafoɔ ano aba me nkyɛn wɔ ɔsoro ahobammɔ mu wɔ Yahweh ahennwa dan mu.

Mene deɛ ɔhwɛ mo kra so na ɔhwɛ mo nkɔsoɔ so sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a mobɛnyini sɛ afifideɛ tenenee.

Mene deɛ Yahweh de me ama sɛ otufoɔ ne ɔhwɛfoɔ ma ne turo kronkron a ɛfa agyefoɔ kra ho no.

Mene Awurade mu Awurade ne Ahemfo mu Hene kɛseɛ a ɔwɔ hɔ daa no, mo Awurade tenenee no.

Mene deɛ ɔbɛgye m’agyapadeɛ afiri nkurɔfoɔ a wɔnni ho kwan no nsam.

Mene Onyankopɔn Asɛm a ɛyɛɛ ɔhonam na Onyankopɔn Asɛm biara bɛba mu nokorɛm.

Mene Yeshua HaMashiach a ɔsan yɛ mo guanhwɛfoɔ panin a ɔrebɛyi ne ho adi no!

Me, Yeshua HaMashiach, nam m’akoa so na mede saa Nsɛm yi amena, na me somfoɔ no so na wɔnam de saa Nsɛm yi amena!

Na enti mehyɛ mo nkuran, me dɔfoɔ, sɛ mma mo koma nntu na monnyina pintinn de akɔsi awieeɛ a ɛba prɛko pɛ a ɛbɛma mo berɛ a ɛwɔ asase so wɔ mo mprempren tebea mu no aba awieeɛ daa no. Prɛko pɛ, na wɔ anibuankaeɛ mu no, mobɛhunu mo ho sɛ moggyina Yahweh ahennwa dan mu wɔ daa anigyeɛ mu na mobɛte aseɛ yie na moahunu sɛdeɛ wɔte mo aseɛ yie na wɔnim mo no. Na saa ara na bɔhyɛ bɛhyia ne mmamu wɔ daa anigyeɛ ne daa animuonyam a ɛtwɛn mo no mu na biribiara bɛba mu wɔ mo mu wɔ anibuankaeɛ mu, na prɛko pɛ na ɛbɛyɛ. Montena me mu, me mma, na medɔ mo yie na m’ani nwu sɛ mɛfrɛ mo me nuanom.
