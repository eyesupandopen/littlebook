Adanseɛ Mmienu: Asɔre no
Wɔ Ɔsomafoɔ Paulo Nnwoma Mu
Kɔtɔnimaa 03, 2024

Mede Anidasoɔ Kɛseɛ Ama Mo!

Mo mra me nkyɛn na montie me nsɛm, m'ayeforɔ, ɛfiri sɛ mewɔ ha sɛ mede awerɛkyekyerɛ bɛbrɛ mo kra, akyekyere mo werɛ, na mayɛ mo mu deɛ wɔ me, Kristo Yesu mu. Monnpa aba wɔ me nkaanim nsɛm no ho, ɛfiri sɛ deɛ medɔ no, na mete no so, sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a mo frɛ ne mo yiyɛ bɛtim wɔ me, Ayeforɔkunu no, a ɔne Kristo Yesu mu. Me mma, momfa mo aso ntie me nne na montie me nsɛm, ɛfiri sɛ mammfrɛ mo amma atemmuo ne afobuo mu, na mmom mapae mu aka sɛ moyɛ ateneneefoɔ wɔ me, mo Awurade Yesu Kristo mu. Monntee deɛ Paulo ka kyerɛɛ Korintofoɔ no wɔ ne krataa a ɛdi kan no mu anaa? Na afei gyidie, anidasoɔ ne ɔdɔ, yeinom mmiɛnsa yi ara na ɛwɔ hɔ: nanso yeinom mu kɛseɛ ne ɔdɔ. Enti ɔdɔ ne yeinom mmiɛnsa mu kɛseɛ, ɛfiri sɛ wɔntumi nni Yahweh dɔ so. Yahweh dɔ ne deɛ ɛka bom. Ɛno ne kanyanfoɔ kɛseɛ, na sɛ wode ma kwa sɛdeɛ wo nso wonyaeɛ kwa no yɛ Onyankopɔn suasua!

Mo mu pii anidasoɔ asa, mpo mo anidasoɔ a mowɔ wɔ me, Yesu Kristo mu, ɛfiri sɛ mede anidasoɔ kɛseɛ ama mo, me mma. Monhunu sɛ ɔkorɔmfoɔ no bɛbɔ mo adwene nu na ɔde mo akɔ ɔkwammɔne so, nanso Onyankopɔn nyɛ basabasa yɛfoɔ, na mmom asomdwoeɛ sɛdeɛ ɛteɛ wɔ ahoteefoɔ asɔre nyinaa mu no. Satan abɔ mmɔden sɛ ɔbɛkata me mmara nsɛm a mede maeɛ wɔ m'adaeɛ a ɛfa Onyankopɔn Ahintasɛm Kɛseɛ no ho kyerɛɛ Ɔsomafoɔ Paulo mfeɛ pii a atwam no so. Me mma, mo anidasoɔ ne adwene a ɛma motumi gyina sɔhwɛ ne amanehunu pii a mohyia wɔ asetena yi mu ano. Sɛ mo anidasoɔ nni hɔ a, anka mobɛyɛ sɛ deɛ Paulo ka kyerɛɛ Korintofoɔ no: Sɛ yɛwɔ anidasoɔ wɔ Kristo mu wɔ asetena yi nkutoo mu a, yɛyɛ nnipa nyinaa mu mmɔborɔfoɔ. Nanso afei Kristo asɔre afiri awufoɔ mu, na wayɛ wɔn a wɔadeda no mu adikan. Me mma, mede anidasoɔ kɛseɛ ama mo anidasoɔ, ɛfiri sɛ maka sɛ: mo a mote ase na moaka no, wɔbɛpagya mo akɔ mununkum mu, sɛdeɛ awufoɔ a wɔwɔ Kristo mu no bɛsɔre kane, na wɔbɛsakra mo nyinaa, ɛfiri sɛ saa ara na mobɛyɛ sɛ me daa, na saa ara na m'ayeforɔ bɛtena me nkyɛn daa.

Na ɛdeɛn ne mo anidasoɔ wɔ Kristo mu, me mma? Ɛnyɛ nkwa ahyɛdeɛ a ɛfa ɔgyeɛ ne nsanoyɛ a ɛdi mu, a mobɛhunu ɔsoro ne Yahweh animuonyam ne n'anuonyam wɔ mo ara mo ani so anaa? Mo anidasoɔ nyɛ daakye a mobɛsom me wɔ me tenenee ahennie mu, ɛfiri sɛ sɛdeɛ moayɛ nokorɛfoɔ wɔ nneɛma nketenkete mu no, saa ara na mɛma mo adi nneɛma pii so anaa? Mo anidasoɔ nyɛ nkwa a ɛnni awieeɛ ahyɛdeɛ a mobɛdidi afiri nkwa dua no so na moanante wɔ Yerusalem Foforɔ mmɔnten so, baabi a Yahweh hann ne Yeshua HaMashiach hann bɛhyerɛn daa wɔ animuonyam kɛseɛ mu anaa? Mo anidasoɔ nyɛ animuonyam a ɛwɔ abotire ne akatua pii a ɛwɔ hɔ ma mo nokorɛdie ne ɔdɔ a mowɔ ma me, Kristo Yesu anaa? Mo anidasoɔ nyɛ apɔmuden a ɛdi mu, yareɛ anaa ɔyareɛ biara nni hɔ bio anaa? Mo anidasoɔ nyɛ sɛ mɛbrɛ mo atamfoɔ ase ahyɛ mo nan ase na mpo mayɛ Satan hyiadan no ma wɔabrɛ wɔn ho ase wɔ mo nan ase anaa? (Sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a wɔbɛhunu sɛ madɔ mo!) Mo anidasoɔ nyɛ anigyeɛ a ɛnni awieeɛ wɔ Yahweh ankasa anim anaa? Sɛ ɔbɛtena nnipa mu, na ahennie a apae mu nni hɔ bio, na mmom Onyankopɔn Ahennie nko ara, ɛfiri sɛ obiara nni hɔ a ɔsene Yahweh; aane, obiara ntumi ntotɔ ne ho nhwɛ Onyankopɔn kɛseɛ a ɔwɔ hɔ daa no animuonyam, a ɔne yɛn Agya!

Monni ahurisie wɔ mo daakye ho, me mma, ɛfiri sɛ mede daakye ne atirimpɔ kronkron ama mo wɔ me, mo Awurade a ɔhyerɛn no mu! Mo anidasoɔ nyɛ anidasoɔ kɛseɛ a wɔagye mo afiri abufuo mu wɔ me mu anaa? Na mo deɛ ne anidasoɔ kɛseɛ pa ara, anidasoɔ a me nnwinnadeɛ a mayi no, Ɔsomafoɔ Paulo, Onyankopɔn Ahintasɛm Kɛseɛ no sɔfoɔ no kaa ho asɛm no? Sɛ medaa deɛ ɔtwerɛɛ no kyerɛɛ Kolosefoɔ no adi kyerɛɛ Paulo: Ahintasɛm a wɔde ahunta mfeɛ ne awoɔ ntoatoasoɔ mu, nanso afei wɔada no adi akyerɛ n'ahoteefoɔ, A wɔn a Onyankopɔn pɛ sɛ ɔma wɔhunu ahintasɛm yi animuonyam ahonya a ɛwɔ Amanamanmufoɔ mu no, a ɛne Kristo a ɔwɔ mo mu, animuonyam anidasoɔ no. Mo deɛ yɛ animuonyam anidasoɔ a ɛfata ɔhene, ɛfiri sɛ mɛyɛ mo ahemfo ne asɔfoɔ wɔ yɛn Nyankopɔn anim; ɛfiri sɛ meyɛ ɔsɔfopanin daa sɛdeɛ Melkisedek nnidisoɔ teɛ, na bɛbiara nni hɔ bio.

Me mma nketewa, monnsuro ɔtamfo no nnaadaa, ɛfiri sɛ sɛ ɔtumi a, anka ɔbɛma moabrɛ wɔ ahuhudeɛ mu; nanso wahyia ne pɛ wɔ me mu na madi ne so nkonim koraa, na mereba abɛsɛe n'ahennie, ɛfiri sɛ wɔsomaa me sɛ memmɛsɛe ɔbonsam nnwuma, na saa ara na masoma mo! Ɔwɔ deda no ne n'ahennie nyinaa nni anidasoɔ. Wɔnim sɛ wɔabu wɔn fɔ sɛ wɔbɛsɛe na wɔanya asotweɛ a ɛnni awieeɛ, baabi a wɔbɛhyew wɔ ogya a ɛyɛ sin a ɛhyeɛ paa no mu berɛ a wɔredware sufre a ɛredɛre mu daa no. Wɔn ani abere mo na wɔde biribiara bɛma sɛ anka wɔyɛ mo, enti monntie wɔn nsɛm. Tie me, m'ayeforɔ; Satan bɛyɛ biribiara de ayi mo afiri mo akatua a ɛwɔ me, Kristo Yesu mu no mu. Ɔbɛyɛ biribiara de akata mo anidasoɔ a mede ama mo no so, na enti n'akwan a ɔfa so yɛ yei no taa yɛ deɛ ɔde brɛ nokorɛ no aseɛ denam atosodie ne ne tɛkrɛma a ɛdi atoro no so.

Monnsuro no, na mmom monfiri ne nnaadaa no ho na monkae mo anidasoɔ ankasa, na monkenkanee anaa? Honhom no ankasa di adanseɛ, ne yɛn honhom, sɛ yɛyɛ Onyankopɔn mma. Na sɛ yɛyɛ mma a, yɛyɛ adedifoɔ, Onyankopɔn adedifoɔ, ne Kristo adedifoɔ apamfoɔ: sɛ ɛba sɛ yɛne no hunu amane a, sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a yɛne no bɛnya animuonyam nso. Sɛ mebu no a, ɛberɛ yi mu amanehunu, ɛnsɛ sɛ wɔde toto animuonyam a wɔbɛda no adi wɔ yɛn mu no ho. Me mma, sɛ mohunu deɛ ɛkyerɛ sɛ moyɛ Kristo adedifoɔ apamfoɔ no kɛseyɛ a, nanso ɛrenkyɛ na mobɛhunu sɛdeɛ wɔnim mo no. Monhunu sɛ Onyankopɔn de nneɛma nyinaa ahyɛ me nsa wɔ soro ne asase so, na m'agyapadeɛ ne agyapadeɛ kɛseɛ pa ara anaa? Me mma, monnim mo ho ankasa, ɛfiri sɛ mo ho ankasa ahunta wɔ me mu, na sɛ meyi me ho adi a, saa ara na mo nso mobɛyi mo ho adi ne me wɔ animuonyam mu!

Monnsuro, na monnyina pintinn wɔ mo dɔ ne mo gyidie mu, ɛfiri sɛ mo anidasoɔ bɛma moanante wɔ ntoboaseɛ kɛseɛ mu; na ɛdeɛn na m'anidasoɔ ne anigyeɛ a Agya no de sii m'anim no maa metumi yɛeɛ? Sɛ Hebrifoɔ nwoma no ka sɛ: Yɛhwɛ Yesu a ɔyɛ yɛn gyidie Titenani ne ne Wiefoɔ no, ɔno na anigyeɛ a wɔde sii n'anim no nti, ɔgyinaa asɛnnua no ano, buu animguaseɛ animtiaa, na ɔte Onyankopɔn ahengua no nifa so. Mamma mo nhwɛsoɔ anaa, na manyɛ deɛ ɛsɛ sɛ modie n'akyi anaa? Monhunu anaa? Sɛ deɛ ɔgye me die no, ɛnyɛ nnwuma a meyɛɛ wɔ me ba a ɛdi kan no mu nko na ɔbɛyɛ, na mmom nnwuma a ɛsene deɛ meyɛeɛ no? Ɛfiri sɛ makɔ Agya no nkyɛn na mahyɛ mo den na megyina ma mo ne me tumi nyinaa, ɛfiri sɛ meyɛ otumfoɔ, ɛfiri sɛ saa na Onyankopɔn ayɛ me wɔ me setie mu.

Me nkurɔfoɔ, nsɛm yi ɛsɛ sɛ ɛhyɛ mo nkuran na ɛma mo ahoɔden sɛ mobɛgyina, ɛfiri sɛ mede anigyeɛ kɛseɛ asi mo anim! Monnnye ɔtamfo no nnaadaa a ɛfa mo anidasoɔ ho no nni, ɛfiri sɛ ɔbɛbɔ mmɔden sɛ ɔbɛgya mo a anidasoɔ nni hɔ na wɔatiatia mo so, na mpo wɔ mo mmerɛyɛ mu no, wɔahyɛ mo den wɔ me, mo Awurade Yesu Kristo mu! Afei mɛbɔ mo daakye no mua, m'ayeforɔ, enti momfa mo aso ntie m'anomu nsɛm, ɛfiri sɛ ɛyɛ gyidie nsɛm, ɛyɛ ɔdɔ nsɛm, na me nsɛm bɛsan ahyɛ mo anidasoɔ den wɔ nokorɛ no mu, ɛfiri sɛ anidasoɔ kɛseɛ wɔ nokorɛ a mede ama mo no mu!

Nea ɛdi kan, monhunu sɛ mafrɛ mo; magye mo wɔ me tumi ne ɔba su honhom mu; mayɛ mo abɔdeɛ foforɔ wɔ me, Kristo Yesu mu! Esiane m'afɔrebɔ sɛ Twam a ɛdi mu no nti, wɔn a wɔgye me die wɔ nkwagyeɛ gyidie a ɛfiri wɔn akoma mu nyinaa nso bɛnya nkwagyeɛ afiri abufuo mu wɔ me mu. Me mma, monhunu sɛ nsɛm yi yɛ nwanwa dodo ma nnipa dodoɔ no ara, ɛfiri sɛ wɔn bɔne bɔ wɔn gu afodi, afobuo, ne animguaseɛ mu, na yei si ɛfiri sɛ mpɛn pii no wɔmpɛ sɛ wɔde me mpata mogya no hohoro wɔn ho, na wɔbu wɔn ani gu wɔn bɔne ka so, na wɔbu wɔn ani gu wɔn ne me, asomdwoeɛ hene, ayɔnkofa so, ɛfiri sɛ nkwagyeɛ firi ayɔnkofa gyidie a ɛwɔ me, Awurade kɛseɛ no mu!

Mo mu pii nhunu sɛ mabu mo bem, na esiane saa bembuo a ɛwɔ me mu no nti, wɔagye mo afiri abufuo mu, ɛfiri sɛ meka kyerɛɛ Romafoɔ no sɛ: Afei a wɔnam ne mogya so abu yɛn bem no, ɛkame ayɛ sɛ wɔbɛgye yɛn afiri abufuo mu denam ne so. Bua me yei, m'ayeforɔ a mereba abɛfa woɔ, adɛn nti na wopɛ Awurade da no? Adɛn nti na woatie Satan asomfoɔ a wɔhyɛ wɔn ho sɛ m'asomfoɔ berɛ a wɔka sɛ ɛsɛ sɛ wogyina Yakob amanehunu ne amanehunu kɛseɛ a ɛsi wɔ Daniel nnawɔtwe aduɔson fa a ɛtwa toɔ no mu no ano? Wɔdi atoro kyerɛ mo na mogye wɔn die. Wɔfrɛ mo bɔne su, ka kyerɛ mo sɛ monsɛ hwee berɛ a moyɛ agudeɛ a ɛsom bo kɛseɛ no; wɔka kyerɛ mo sɛ mosɛ abufuo kɛseɛ wɔ me mu, na wɔdi atoro! Wɔka kyerɛ mo sɛ monnhwɛ kwan sɛ wɔbɛgye mo afiri amanehunu mu, na wɔfa me Asɛm kronkron no ɔkwammɔne so. Sɛ nokorɛ mu na meka kyerɛɛ Paulo deɛ ɛdidi soɔ yi sɛ ɔnkyerɛkyerɛ Timoteo: Ɔtaa, amanehunu a ɛbaa me so wɔ Antiokia, wɔ Ikoniom, wɔ Listra, ɔtaa bɛn na megyinaa ano: nanso emu nyinaa mu no Awurade gyee me. Aane, na wɔn a wɔbɛtena ase Onyankopɔn som mu wɔ Kristo Yesu mu nyinaa, wɔbɛhunu ɔtaa. Na nokorɛm, Satan bɛtaa wɔn a wɔtena ase Onyankopɔn som mu nyinaa, na ne ɔtaa nyinaa mu no mɛgye mo berɛ a mobɛdane mo ho abɛhwɛ me wɔ mo haw mu na moagyina pintinn wɔ mo anidasoɔ mu. Monhunu nsonsonoeɛ no, ɛfiri sɛ Satan abɔ asɛm no adwene nu: Satan bɛtaa wɔn a wɔtena ase Onyankopɔn som mu nyinaa, na ɛno ne ɔtaa; na mpɛn pii no ɛsɛ sɛ mogyina n'abufuo ano, ɛfiri sɛ ne nkoguo mu no ɔwɔ abufuo kɛseɛ. Nanso monnsuro ne bobɔ mu, ɛfiri sɛ mekyerɛkyerɛɛ Petro sɛ: Monnyɛ anifere, na monwɛn: na mo tamfo ɔbonsam sɛ gyata a ɔbobɔ mu nantini, rehwehwɛ deɛ ɔbɛmene no: Ɔno na monnyina ne so pintinn wɔ gyidie mu, na monim sɛ amanehunu korɔ no ara na ɛba mo nuanom a wɔwɔ wiase no so.

Monhunu, me mma, sɛ abufuo a ɛreba no nyɛ Satan abufuo na mmom Onyankopɔn abufuo ne Oguammaa no abufuo a wɔahyɛ ama Satan. Sɛ Satan yɛ ohufoɔ a ɔwosow ne ho wɔ abɔdeɛ nyinaa mu sɛ ɔbɛbɔ mmɔden ahunta ne ho afiri abufuo a waboa ano no ho, susuo sɛ ɔbɛtumi adi Yahweh so nkonim wɔ nsɛm a ɛte saa mu; nanso Agya no atirimpɔw ne sɛ: Owura, wonnuaa aba pa wɔ w'afuo mu anaa? Ɛhe na nwura firi baeɛ? Ɔka kyerɛɛ wɔn sɛ, Ɔtamfo na wayɛ yei. Asomfoɔ no ka kyerɛɛ no sɛ, Wopɛ sɛ yɛkɔ na yɛkɔboaboa ano anaa? Nanso ɔkaa sɛ, Daabi: anyɛ saa a, berɛ a moreboaboa nwura no ano no, mobɛtu ayuo no nso afiri hɔ. Ma ne mmienu nyinaa nyini bom kɔsi twaberɛ: na twaberɛ no, mɛka akyerɛ atwafoɔ no sɛ, Monboaboa nwura no ano kane, na monkyekyere no nkasɛɛ nkɔto gya mu: nanso monboaboa ayuo no ano nkɔ me adekoradan mu. Saa ara na wɔbɛboaboa ayuo no ano wɔ adekoradan no mu, nanso nwura ne wɔn a wɔduaeɛ na wɔguu so nsuo nyinaa no, wɔbɛto wɔn a wɔte ase no agu ogya ne sufre tadeɛ kɛseɛ a m'Agya asiesie ama ɔbonsam ne n'abɔfoɔ no mu, nanso Onyankopɔn abufuo no fa biara nni hɔ mma mo, ɛwom sɛ Satan bɛbɔ mmɔden sɛ ɔde mo bɛyɛ ne kyɛm na ɔbɛhwehwɛ sɛ ɔde mo bɛkɔ nnommum sɛ asadeɛ. Monnsuro, ɛfiri sɛ abufuo nni hɔ mma mo. Mehunu sɛ akra a ɛntim pii dane bɛ yi, ka kyerɛ mo sɛ mobɛhunu abufuo ne wɔn, nanso wɔyɛ saa denam kyerɛwsɛm no nnidisoɔ a ɛda adi pefee a wɔbu wɔn ani gu so no so, na wɔbɔ mmɔden sɛ wɔde me bɛ no a wɔfa no ɔkwammɔne so no bɛma mo ani afira.

Monhunu sɛ mɛpagya mo akɔ soro, ɛfiri sɛ masiesie ahobanbɔ ne akatua beaeɛ ama m'ayeforɔ; ɛfiri sɛ m'Agya fie na mɛhunta mo na adan pii wɔ hɔ. Enti aane, mɛboaboa mo ano aba me nkyɛn wɔ m'yi a merebɛyi me ho adi no mu, a ɛrebɛsi. Sɛ meka kyerɛɛ Tesalonikafoɔ no sɛ: Sɛ Awurade ankasa de nteɛmu, de ɔbɔfoɔ panyin nne, ne Onyankopɔn totorobɛnto bɛsiane afiri soro: na awufoɔ a wɔwɔ Kristo mu no bɛsɔre kane. Afei yɛn a yɛte ase, na yɛaka no, wɔbɛpagya yɛn ne wɔn abɔ mu akɔ mununkum mu, akɔhyia Awurade wɔ wiem: na saa ara na yɛne Awurade bɛtena daa. Enti, momfa nsɛm yi nkyekyere mo werɛ. Mebisa mo, m'ayeforɔ a wɔadaadaa mo no, afei mobɛpo Satan nnaadaa na moasɔ me nsɛm mu na moanya awerɛkyekyerɛ wɔ mu na moakyekyere mo nuanom werɛ anaa? Sɛ ɛrenkyɛ na ɛbɛba saa, na mepɛ mo nkyɛn tenabea, ɛfiri sɛ medɔ mo yie, ɛfiri sɛ meyɛ mo Ayeforɔkunu.

Sɛ wɔyi mo firi asase so pɛ a, Yakob amanehunu bɛpae wɔ asase so na ɔsɛeɛ kɛseɛ bɛba mpofirim, nanso ɛnyɛ mo dea, ɛfiri sɛ wɔde mo bɛkɔ soro. Na afei mobɛgyina m'atemmuo adwa no anim na mede akatua ama mo sɛdeɛ mo nnwuma teɛ. Na wɔn a wɔyɛ nokorɛfoɔ no wɔ akatua kɛseɛ ne abotire pii, na wɔde ofie a mayɛ ama mo bɛfira mo, ofie a wɔbɛyɛ no pɛpɛɛpɛ ama mo na ɛbɛkura mo nnwuma pii a matumi ayɛ no mu, a mede tumi pii bɛhyɛ mu, ɛfiri sɛ saa na nipadua foforɔ a merehwɛ mo kwan no teɛ. Yareɛ biara nni hɔ, ɛyaw biara nni hɔ, awerɛhoɔ biara nni hɔ, na nnome biara nni hɔ bio, me mma, ɛfiri sɛ nnome no bɛdane agu wɔn a wɔadome wɔn no ti so. Na saa ara na mobɛwɔ soro saa mfeɛ nson no, a ɛne Daniel nnawɔtwe aduɔson no. Na afei, mobɛtena me so ne ɔsoro dɔm nyinaa, ɛfiri sɛ wɔahyɛ nkɔm na saa na ɛbɛyɛ: Na mehunuu ɔsoro a abue, na hwɛ ɔpɔnkɔ fitaa, na deɛ ɔte ne so no wɔfrɛ no nokorɛfoɔ ne nokwafoɔ, na ɔnam tenenee so bu atɛn na ɔko. N'ani te sɛ ogya frama, na abotire pii hyɛ ne ti so, na ɔwɔ din a wɔatwerɛ, a obiara nnim gye ɔno ara. Na ɔfira atadeɛ a wɔde abɔ mogya mu, na ne din de, Onyankopɔn asɛm. Na asraafoɔ dɔm a wɔwɔ soro no dii n'akyi wɔ apɔnkɔ fitaa so, wɔfira nwera pa, fitaa ne kronkron.

Me mma, mo daakye yɛ animuonyam a wontumi nsusu, na biribiara renhia mo bio, ɛfiri sɛ moada mo ho adi akyerɛ me, na sɔhwɛ foforɔ biara nhia mo; enti adɛn nti na ɛsɛ sɛ mohunu Yakob amanehunu? Sɛ n'atirimpɔw ne sɛ ɔbɛgye Yakob, ɛfiri sɛ nkaeɛ bi bɛnya nkwagyeɛ. Nanso monnya ahotɔ, wɔn a wɔpa m'yi a merebɛyi me ho adi no wɔ wɔn mmɔdenbɔ a ɛyɛ huhu sɛ wɔbɛsɛe mo anidasoɔ ankasa no, mpo ahwɛfoɔ a wɔayera no, wɔbɛhunu Yakob amanehunu, na wɔbɛnya nkwagyeɛ sɛ wɔbɔ me din na wɔyɛ nokorɛfoɔ kɔsi awieeɛ a. Nanso wɔrennya beaeɛ wɔ me pon so, ɛfiri sɛ sɛ wopa m'yi a merebɛyi me ho adi no a, ɛkyerɛ sɛ wopa me, Ayeforɔkunu no, berɛ a mereba abɛfa m'ayeforɔ no, na ɛno ara na merebɛyɛ.

Monhwɛ m'yi a merebɛyi me ho adi no animuonyam! Montie, na mobɛte me nne ne totorobɛnto a ɛtwa toɔ no nne dennen; ɛfiri sɛ ɛwom sɛ meyɛ Onyankopɔn Guammaa deɛ, nanso mereba sɛ Yuda Gyata, na Satan ahennie nyinaa asuro me, Kristo Yesu, ɛfiri sɛ wɔn berɛ adu, na wɔasɔ ogya no mu hye ama wɔn. Nanso ɛremmɛn mo, ɛfiri sɛ masiesie akatua kɛseɛ ne agyapadeɛ kɛseɛ ama mo, m'ayeforɔ. Monkenkan me nsɛm wɔ Adiyisɛm nwoma kɛseɛ no mu, na mɛkyerɛ mo mo daakye ho anisoadehunu: Na Ahengua no ho nyinaa na nhengua aduonu anan wɔ hɔ, na nhengua no so no mehunuu Mpanyimfoɔ aduonu anan a wɔtete hɔ, wɔfira atadeɛ fitaa, na wɔhyɛ sika kɔkɔɔ abotire. (na bio) Na sɛ mmoa no de animuonyam, ne anuonyam, ne aseda ma deɛ ɔte Ahengua no so, a ɔte ase daa daa no a, Mpanyimfoɔ aduonu anan no hwe ase wɔ deɛ ɔte Ahengua no so no anim, na wɔsom deɛ ɔte ase daa daa no, na wɔto wɔn abotire gu Ahengua no anim, ka sɛ, Wosɛ, O Awurade, sɛ wogye animuonyam, ne anuonyam, ne tumi: na wo na wobɔɔ nneɛma nyinaa, na wo pɛ nti na ɛwɔ hɔ, na wɔbɔeɛ.

Me mma, monnsuro mo daakye, na mmom monhwɛ me wɔ anidasoɔ kɛseɛ mu, ɛfiri sɛ ɛrenkyɛ na mobɛwɔ soro reto mo abotire agu Onyankopɔn a ɔkorɔn sen biara no ahengua no anim!

Meyɛ ɔtemmufoɔ tenenee na ɛrenkyɛ na mobɛnya akatua kɛseɛ wɔ me Bema no so.

Meyɛ Ayeforɔkunu ma m'ayeforɔ.

Mereba abɛpagya m'ayeforɔ!

Meyɛ Yuda Abusuakuo no mu Gyata na wiase rebɛte ase.

Mene deɛ wɔama no dibea a ɛkorɔn wɔ soro ne asase so, ɛfiri sɛ me din sene edin nyinaa gye Yahweh.

Meyɛ Onyankopɔn Ba a ɔbaa nam mu maa adesamma bɔne, na wɔnyanee me da a ɛtɔ so mmiɛnsa na wɔfaa me kɔɔ soro.

Mene deɛ ɔbɛdi hene wɔ tenenee mu wɔ asase so, na m'ayeforɔ bɛdi hene ne me.

Mene deɛ wafrɛ mo akɔ animuonyam ne suban pa mu, enti monnpa aba!

Mene no.

Monhwɛ m'yi a merebɛyi me ho adi no kwan! כך אחר אותך אתפוס (etfos otha ahar kah - Mɛfa wo akyire yi)
