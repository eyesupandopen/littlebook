Adɛn Nti na Mo Abam Abu?

Mommra, mommrɛ mo koma ne mo aso ase na montie mo Awurade, m’Asafo ne me yerenom mpo Nsɛm. Adɛn nti na moatwe mo ho afiri anidasoɔ a mowɔ wɔ me ba a mɛba no mu mpofirim saa, te sɛ deɛ meremma? Sɛnea ɛteɛ a yɛbɛtumi asesa Yahweh nkaeɛ? Onyankopɔn kɛseɛ a ɔwɔ hɔ daa, Yahweh, werɛ afi ne bɔhyɛ ah? Me werɛ afi me nsɛm ne me bɔhyɛ ah? Nokorɛ, nokorɛ, mese dabi! Na wɔn a wɔde nkwaseasɛm bata Yahweh, Onyankopɔn kɛseɛ a ɔwɔ hɔ daa no ho, ne me, Yeshua HaMashiach, Awurade mu Awurade ne Ahemfo mu Hene no, ɛsɛ sɛ wɔsakra ntɛm ara. Monsrɛ bɔnefafiri me mma na monkɔ so nsiesie mo ho, ɛfiri sɛ mɛba mpofirim, na mo ho bɛdwiri mo wɔ sɛnea Yahweh nnipa nkwagyeɛ ho nhyehyɛeɛ mu adeyɛ kɛseɛ a ɛdi hɔ no bɛba mu ntɛmntɛm no ho, ɛfiri sɛ ɛbɛsi mpofirim na wɔanhwɛ kwan sɛnea wɔaka ato hɔ no. Na ɛbɛba sɛ mɛnyane owufoɔ a wɔwɔ Kristo mu no kane, na mo a mote ase na moaka no, wɔbɛfa mo akɔhyia me wɔ mununkum no mu, na ɛbɛsi wɔ anibuankankyerɛ mu.

Adɛn nti na mo abam abu? Adɛn nti na mo mu bi yɛ wɔn ho sɛ nneɛma asan akɔ sɛnea na ɛteɛ kane no? Akyinnyeɛ biara nni ho sɛ yɛrentumi nsan nkɔ tete nneɛma so bio, ɛfiri sɛ wiase a ɛwɔ hɔ seesei yi bɛhyia ɔhaw kɛseɛ na asesa afebɔɔ, na ɛbɛsesa bio wɔ Yakob haw berɛ mu na asase bɛyɛ sɛ Noa nna no mu deɛ no. Sɛ wɔyi deɛ ɔsi ano kwan no firi hɔ a, biribiara nni hɔ a ɛbɛsi ahɔhoɔ bɔne no kwan, mpo ɔbonsam asraafoɔ a wɔbɛto ahyɛ deɛ ɛyɛ me dea nyinaa so no, ɛfiri sɛ Yahweh de tumi ama ɔtweaseɛ no sɛ ɔne ahoteefoɔ no nni ako na ɔnni wɔn so. Nnipa pii bɛba me nkyɛn ɛnam wɔn honam sɛeɛ so, mpo honam a wɔdɔ na ɛkyekyere wɔn no. Na obiara a ɔbɔ Awurade din no, wɔbɛgye no nkwa, na ɔsomfoɔ nso nkyɛn ne wura.

Ɛwom sɛ mede ɔsɛeɛ a ɛreba no mu anigyeɛ ama anokwafoɔ deɛ, nanso pii nsiesiee wɔn ho nanso wɔbɛgye wɔn nkwa na wɔasom atirimpɔ kɛseɛ wɔ m’Ahennie a ɛreba no mu, ɛfiri sɛ mɛdi hene afiri Dawid ahennwa so na me ne m’Agya kyɛ n’ahennwa, na mo nso, m’anokwafoɔ, mobɛkyɛ m’ahennwa no bi. Wɔn a wɔnsiesiee wɔn ho no bɛhu amane pii ɛnam me din nti, na wɔbɛhu amane sɛnea me, Yesu Nasareni no, huu amane de gyee nnipa nkwa na ɛnam m’asɛnnua so wuo no so no, mede osetie a ɛkɔsi owuo mu mfatohoɔ mato hɔ ama obiara sɛ ɔnni akyi. Na ɛnam amanehunu kɛseɛ so na nnipadɔm pii bɛnya nkwagyeɛ na wɔanya akatua kɛseɛ wɔ me Kristo Yesu mu, na wɔasom Yahweh mpo wɔ n’asɔredan mu. Akatua kɛseɛ da hɔ ma wɔn a wɔbɔ me din nyinaa, na wɔn a wɔbɔ me din kɔsi awieeɛ no, wɔbɛgye wɔn nkwa, mpo wɔn a wɔnnɔ wɔn nkwa kɔsi awieeɛ no. Na wiase yi resɛe na wɔbɛsɛe no na wɔatwe n’aso koraa ansa na m’ahennie a ɛyɛ tenenee no asi asase so.

Enti mma mo abam mmu, me Mma, ɛfiri sɛ me bɔhyɛ yɛ yoo ne amen na mɛfa m’ayeforɔ akɔ soro sɛnea wɔahyɛ bɔ no, na onipa baako mpo a Yahweh ahyɛ no sɛ ɔnnya daa nkwa ansa na wɔreto asase fapem no, wɔrenntu no mfiri me nsa a ɛwɔ hɔ daa no mu na mo nkwagyeɛ wɔ me, Kristo Yesu mu, monkɔ so nni nokorɛ na montena Kristo mu kɔsi awieeɛ. Yei yɛ biribi a Yahweh hwehwɛ firi tete, sɛ wɔbɛhu onipa sɛ ɔyɛ Nokwafoɔ wɔ n’akwan so. Na me, Yeshua HaMashiach, mede Yahweh akwan no ba na wɔn a wɔsua me no sua Yahweh akwan, ɛfiri sɛ me ne m’Agya yɛ baako wɔ adwenkoro ne atirimpɔ mu. Ɛrenkyɛ na mo ne yɛn ayɛ baako, wɔankyerɛw sɛ wɔayɛ mo Onyankopɔn a ɔwɔ hɔ daa no fa, ɛfiri sɛ ɛno ne mo agyapadeɛ na ɛno nso ne m’akatua ma Onyankopɔn mma anokwafoɔ a wɔhunuu wɔn sɛ wɔyɛ anokwafoɔ wɔ me mu no. Na me, Kristo Yesu mu na Onyankopɔn yɛ mu ma nyinaa teɛ wɔ honam mu, na mobɛnya kyɛfa wɔ m’agyapadeɛ mu ɛnam mo nokwaredie nti, na mobɛnya kyɛfa wɔ m’ahennwa so sɛ Ahemfo ne Asɔfoɔ na m’Ahennie nni awieeɛ.

Enti monnyina pintinn mma me wɔ mmerɛ yi awieeɛ simma yi mu, na monhunu sɛ berɛ aso. Aane, berɛ aso sɛ Ahintasɛm Babilon bɛsakra. M’adom ne me mmɔborɔhunu berɛ no aso, ɛwom sɛ mmɔborɔhunu ne adom wɔ me nkyɛn daa deɛ. Afei atemmuo kɛseɛ reba asase so, ne amanaman ne asase no mpo so. Nanso atemmuo remma a ɛbɛyi ateasefoɔ nyinaa afiri hɔ, na mmom ɛbɛtɔ na aboaboa ano. Atemmuo so atemmuo, nsɔano so nsɔano, totorobɛnto so totorobɛnto, kuruwa so kuruwa, nnome so nnome, ne ɔyaredɔm so ɔyaredɔm. Na ɛnam so no, nnipa pii, pii, pii bɛnya nkwagyeɛ wɔ me, Yesu Nasareni mu, ɛfiri sɛ meyɛ Twam Aduan a ɛyɛ pɛ ma mmerɛ nyinaa, na afɔrebɔ ho nhia bio. Nanso mo, m’Ayeforɔ anokwafoɔ, ɔtwɛn berɛ no aba awieeɛ.

Twɛn nso nni hɔ bio, na mobɛnya mo akatua ne agyapadeɛ a ɛyɛ ma wɔ me mu. Na ɛwom sɛ ebinom bɛhwere nneɛma deɛ, nanso ani nhunuiɛ, na aso ntee, na ɛnkɔɔ nnipa komam, nneɛma a Yahweh asiesie ama wɔn a wɔdɔ no. Na ɛte saa me dɔfoɔ anokwafoɔ, moada mo dɔ adi akyerɛ me, Kristo Yesu, ne mo dɔ a modɔ Yahweh ɛfiri sɛ modii me mmara nsɛm so ɛnam gyidie so wɔ saa da bɔne yi mu. Ɛwom sɛ moamfa mo ani anhunu me, na moanhunu Yahweh anounyam a ɛwɔ hɔ daa no deɛ, nanso moakɔ so adi nokorɛ wɔ me mu akɔsi awieeɛ, enti akatua kɛseɛ wɔ hɔ ma mo wɔ soro, enti mma mo abam mmu!

Monkɔ so nnya aboterɛ, me Mma, ɛfiri sɛ merebɛn. Monntie fɛwdifoɔ a wɔdi mo ho fɛw no ɛfiri sɛ wɔn fɛwdie no bɛdan awerɛhoɔ kɛseɛ ntɛm ara, na fɛwdifoɔ pii bɛyɛ komm berɛ a wɔn nkwa aba awieeɛ. Na saa na atemmuo a ɛreba no teɛ. Wɔn a wɔapo me nsa a matene na wɔabu me mogya a ɛsɔ pata no sɛ adeɛ a ɛho nte na wɔabu wɔn ho sɛ wɔmfata daa nkwa wɔ m’Ahennie a ɛreba no mu no, wɔn ara nso bɛnya sum ne owuo a wɔdɔɔ na wɔpɛɛ no no agyapadeɛ na pii de wɔn pɛ bɛhwe ase akɔ owuo a ɛtɔ so mmienu no mu.

Na m’Agya dɔ kɛseɛ no mu, mpo Yahweh Onyankopɔn a ɔwɔ hɔ daa a obiara nni hɔ a ɔte sɛ ɔno no, ɔrenhyɛ n’abɔdeɛ no mu baako mpo sɛ ɔnni n’akyi na ɔnnɔ no. Wɔma obiara ahofadie na obiara bɛnya ne suahunu no so akatua. Na saa na Yahweh mmara teɛ. Wɔn a wɔdɔ bɔne, wɔn a wɔdɔ bɔne mu anigyeɛ na wɔdɔ bɔne a wɔn bɔne de ba afoforɔ a wɔhu amane kwa ɛnam wɔn akɔnnɔ bɔne ne wɔn akwan a ɛnkɔ turodoo so no bɛnya wɔn ara wɔn akwan bɔne no agyapadeɛ asan aba wɔn koko so na saa na wɔn asotwe bɛyɛ na wɔbɛtua bɔne biara so ka daa. Nanso wɔn a wɔgye Yahweh asomdwoeɛ apam no tom, ɛne me Yesu Nasareni no, bɛnya daa nkwa ne asomdwoeɛ ne akatua ne ɔdɔ ne anigyeɛ a ɛwɔ hɔ daa wɔ m’anim ne Yahweh ankasa anim, ɛfiri sɛ ɛrenkyɛ na Yahweh tenabea bɛba nnipa mu sɛnea wɔakyerɛw no. Na merebɛn sɛ mɛfa mo akɔ Yahweh ankasa ahennwa dan mu, faako a mote hɔ deda no, na mo ahenkyɛw retwɛn mo.

Monnnye wɔn nnaadaa no nni me mma, mereba abɛfa mo, monkɔ so nnyina pintinn simma baako anaa mmienu bio, ɛfiri sɛ me ba a mɛba no abɛn na ɛbɛsi simma biara seesei, na mɛboro mo anisoadehunu so. Monsiesie mo ho na monhunu mo ho wɔ me mogya a ɛsɔ pata no mu. Monkɔ so nnu mo ho na monkɔ so mmɔ mo honam akwan asɛnnua mu, ɛfiri sɛ ɛnyɛ me dea na biribiara a ɛyɛ bɔne renkɔ m’Ahennie mu na biribiara a ɛyɛ bɔne rennyina Yahweh anim. Meyɛ mo afɔrebɔ a ɛwɔ hɔ daa na mayɛ mo a nsensanee anaa nwunu nni mo ho na dɛm biara nni m’anokwafoɔ so ɛfiri sɛ me mogya no te mo ho berɛ biara a moka mo bɔne wɔ adwensakra mu, ɛwom mpo sɛ mo honam asɛe koraa deɛ.

Mommfa koma a ɛbue mmra me nkyɛn na mede ɔdɔ bɛkyerɛ mo mo mmerɛyɛ na moatumi aka mo bɔne akyerɛ me, na ɛno mu na mɛte mo ho na makɔ so ayɛ mo yie sɛnea moyɛ abɔdeɛ foforɔ wɔ me, Kristo Yesu mu no. Na nokorɛ, nokorɛ, wɔn a wɔde me honhom kronkron a ɛyɛ bɔhyɛ no asɔ wɔn ano nyinaa, sɛ Yudafoɔ anaa Amanamanmufoɔ, (sɛ nkoa anaa adehyeɛ) no, wɔbɛbɔ wɔn foforɔ koraa sɛ onipa foforɔ baako wɔ me Kristo Yesu mu na wɔagyina anounyam ahofadie mu daa! Na meyɛ honhom a ɛma nkwa, ɛfiri sɛ saa na m’Agya ayɛ me. Na meyɛ deɛ ME a ɔyɛ ME KƐSEƐ no ayɛ me daa. Na afei nso, wɔbɛyɛ mo sɛ me wɔ nipadua a ɛwɔ anounyam mu, nipadua a wɔde ayɛ sɛ akatua a ɛbɛda mo nneyɛɛ adi wɔ gyina a mogyina maa me wɔ saa da bɔne yi mu no. Mo mmɔdenbɔ no mu baako mpo remmɔ kwa: Na Onyankopɔn nyɛ ɔteneneeni, sɛ ne werɛ bɛfiri mo adwuma, ne ɔdɔ brɛ a modii, a moada no adi akyerɛ ne Din, sɛdeɛ moasom Ahoteefoɔ no, na modaso som no.

Mo ankasa nipasu ahunta wɔ me mu, mpo mo agyenkwa ne mo Mesia. Daa daa mobɛtena me nkyɛn, na mobɛyɛ adwene ne koma baako ne me Yeshua HaMashiach ne Yahweh mpo, ɛfiri sɛ El Shaddai Kɛseɛ no, mpo Otumfoɔ Nyankopɔn no bɛyɛ biribiara wɔ biribiara mu ɛfiri sɛ n’atirimpɔ bɛhyɛ ma wɔ mo, me nkurɔfoɔ a wɔafrɛ mo, wɔapaw mo na moyɛ anokwafoɔ no mu. Mereba abɛfa mo, m’Ayeforɔ, moasiesie mo ho? Ngo wɔ mo nkanea mu? Moahohoro mo ho koraa wɔ me mogya a ɛsɔ pata no mu? Moagyae mo nan ho hohoro?

Monhwɛ! Monhwɛ! Montie! Monhwɛ me ba a mɛba no kwan na monhyɛ mo ho mo ho nkuran ɛfiri sɛ sɛ ɛbɛyɛ yie a, Satan bɛhunta me ba a mɛba no. Monhwɛ me, mo gyidie hyɛsefoɔ ne ne wieyɛfoɔ, na anibuankankyerɛ mu na wɔbɛsesa mo na wɔayɛ mo sɛ me daa. Montie me Nsɛm na monsiesie mo ho mma me ba a mɛba no na nea ɛsen ne nyinaa no, monyɛ osetie mfiri ɔdɔ a modɔ Yahweh ne me mu na mode koma a ɛho teɛ nni me Asɛm kronkron no so, na monhunu sɛ mo nkwagyeɛ mmɛn bio na mmom adu.

Me ne m’Agya yɛ baako wɔ adwenkoro ne atirimpɔ mu, na ɛrenkyɛ na mo ne yɛn ayɛ baako.

Meyɛ Twam Aduan a ɛyɛ pɛ ma mmerɛ nyinaa.

Merebɛn. Monntie fɛwdifoɔ a wɔdi mo ho fɛw no.

Mereba abɛfa mo, monkɔ so nnyina pintinn simma baako anaa mmienu bio.

Meyɛ mo afɔrebɔ a ɛwɔ hɔ daa.

Meyɛ honhom a ɛma nkwa, ɛfiri sɛ saa na m’Agya ayɛ me.

Meyɛ deɛ ME a ɔyɛ ME KƐSEƐ no ayɛ me daa.

Mereba abɛfa mo, m’Ayeforɔ, moasiesie mo ho?
