Wɔ Yohane Adiyifoɔ no Nnwuma Mu

Ma 144,000 no: Sɛdeɛ ɛteɛ no, Wɔafrɛ Mo na Wɔasɔ Mo Ano Ama Mmerɛ a Ɛte Sɛɛ

Munnyina mma me na monni me nhwɛsoɔ akyi, na ɛdeɛn na Hebrifoɔ ka fa me gyinabea a megyina ma Yahweh ho? Nea ɔnam anigyeɛ a wɔde sii n’ani so no, gyinaa asɛnnua no ano, na ɔbuu animguaseɛ animtiaa, na ɔte Onyankopɔn ahengua no nifa so. Enti, me dɔ a medɔ Agya no, a ɛntumi ntetew mu no mu, ne me setie a ɛfiri ɔdɔ mu, ne me dɔ a medɔ nnipa ne me pɛ a mepɛ sɛ mɛbɔ me ho afɔdeɛ de agye mo nkwa no mu no, megyinaa asotwe, amanehunu, ne ɔbrɛ a ɛyɛ hu ano na meyɛɛ no me pɛ mu, a anigyeɛ hyɛ me koma ma. Menim sɛ ɛnam m’afɔrebɔ ne me wuo so no, nnipa bebree bɛyɛ ateneneefoɔ na wɔanya nkwa denam me wusɔreɛ tumi so, ɛfiri sɛ Yahweh ankasa nyanee me firii awufoɔ mu, na ɛntumi nyɛ yie sɛ owuo bɛkura me mu. Na ɛnam saa tumi korɔ no ara so no, saa ara nso na wɔbɛnyane mo akɔ nkwa foforɔ mu, ɛfiri sɛ matua ka no denam me mogya a ɛho nni dɛm a mede maeɛ sɛ agyedeɛ ama wɔn a wɔbɛgye me din adi nyinaa no so, na obiara a ɔbɛbɔ Awurade din no, wɔbɛgye no nkwa, wɔn a wɔnnɔ wɔn nkwa kɔsi awieeɛ!

Na afei merebisa mo, m’anokwafoɔ ne wɔn a wɔayi mo, mpo me nuanom, sɛ monnyina mma me na monntwe mo ho. Na mekaa no pefee wɔ me nwoma a mekɔmaa Hebrifoɔ no mu sɛ: Afei deɛ ɔteneneeni nam gyidie so bɛtena ase: nanso sɛ obi twe ne ho a, me kra rennya ne mu anigyeɛ.

Momma ɛno nsi mo mu, na monkenkan bio, na monntwe mo ho nkɔ Torah so, ɛfiri sɛ wɔnam gyidie a wɔwɔ wɔ me mu so na ɛdi Torah so; enti, ɔteneneeni nam gyidie so bɛtena ase. Wɔde mo abata me ho, na mene nea ɔnam m’asopatɔ nnwuma ne me mogya a ɛho nni dɛm so abɔ mo bem.

Mo mu pii di aperepereɛ wɔ adwene a ɛne sɛ megyee mo ananmu wɔ afɔbuo mu wɔ dua no so wɔ bɔne a menyɛɛ ho, na meyɛɛ no me pɛ mu ne anigyeɛ so de de nkwagyeɛ brɛɛ onipa a ɔwɔ ɔhonam mu a wawuo, sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a mɛtumi agye wɔn ama Yahweh, mpo mo Nyankopɔn ne mo Agya, mpo Hann Agya a nsakraeɛ anaa nsakraeɛ sunsuma biara nni ne mu no. Na Yahweh nsakra; ɔte saa ara nnora, ɛnnɛ, ne daa!

Ɔno ne Onyankopɔn korɔ no ara a mo agyanom penee so wɔ apam mu, mpo wɔ Bepɔ Sinai so berɛ a Mose nyaa mmara no firii Yahweh ankasa hɔ no. Na ɛwom sɛ mmara no yɛ kronkron, na ahyɛdeɛ no yɛ kronkron, na ɛteɛ, na ɛyɛ papa deɛ, nanso nnipa antumi annyina ne gyinapɛn ahorow ano da, ɛfiri sɛ wɔde mmara no maeɛ sɛ ɛmma wɔnhu bɔne wɔ ɔhonam mu, na wɔawo mo nyinaa wɔ afɔdie ne bɔne mu a mowuwuiɛ, ɛfiri sɛ bɔne wɔ mo honam mu. Wɔamfa Torah amma ateneneefoɔ na mmom wɔde maa amumɔyɛfoɔ, sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a bɔne bɛda adi sɛ bɔne wɔ ɔhonam mu. Wɔde Torah maeɛ sɛ mo sukuu kyerɛkyerɛfoɔ a ɔde mo bɛba me, Kristo Yesu, Awuranom Awurade ne Ahemfo Hene a ɔkɔɔ soro no nkyɛn!

Na enti meyɛɛ m’adwene sɛ mɛfa bɔne so nsunsuansoɔ no agu me ho so, ɛfiri sɛ saa na wɔwoo me sɛ mɛgye nnipa afiri mmara no nnome mu. Na wɔannome mmara no; aane, momma mmara no nnyina hɔ na wɔnnome onipa biara a ɔdome Mose mmara no, na mamma sɛ merebɛsɛe mmara no, na mmom sɛ merebɛhyɛ mmara no mu den na mpo matra Mose mmara no so. Wose abususɛm?

Hebrifoɔ adanseɛ no gyina yɛn ntam sɛ ogyinafoɔ: Na sɛ anantwinini ne apɔnkye mogya, ne nantwi ba nso a wɔhyee no nso a wɔde petepete wɔn a wɔn ho nteɛ so no, te wɔn ho ma ɔhonam no mu tew a: Dɛn nti na Kristo mogya a ɔnam Honhom a ɛwɔ hɔ daa no so de ne ho bɔɔ afɔdeɛ a dɛm nni ne ho maa Onyankopɔn no, rente mo ahonim ho mfiri nnwuma a awu mu na moasom Onyankopɔn teasefoɔ no?

Esiane sɛ modɔ Mose (sɛdeɛ me nso medɔ no no), me tu mo fo sɛ monni Mose ahyɛdeɛ so ɔdɔ mu; na ɔkaa sɛ: Yahweh mo Elohim bɛma Odiyifoɔ bi asɔre ama mo afiri mo mu, afiri mo nuanom mu, a ɔte sɛ me; ɔno na mobɛtie no. Enti montie Yeshua HaMashiach nne! Wɔatew mo ho afiri bɔne ne bɔne biara a moyɛ no ho denam me mogya a ɛho nni dɛm a mede maeɛ sɛ agyedeɛ ama mo nkwa no so, na mpo magye mo afiri ɔtamfo no nsam. Monnyɛ bɔne! Na sɛ onipa bi yɛ bɔne a, monka mo bɔne nkyerɛ me, na mene nea ɔte mo ho wɔ me mogya a ɛsɔ pata no mu.

Mempata mo bɔne ne ne nsunsuansoɔ a ɛwɔ mu no ne apɔnkye a wɔde no kɔ sare so, nso memmɔɔ odwan ba a wadi afe afɔdeɛ; na mene Onyankopɔn Odwan Ba a wadi afe, na mene nea ɔde ne ho bɔɔ afɔdeɛ a dɛm ne nkekaeɛ biara nni ne ho, mpo ɔyɛ kronkron, ɔho tew, na wɔahyira no so ama Yahweh ankasa (mpo m’Agya) wɔ mo bɔne ho prɛko pɛ, mpo daa nyinaa. Mepataa mo bɔne wɔ me mogya a ɛho nni dɛm no mu; enti, afɔrebɔ biara nni hɔ bio mma bɔne. Na sɛ wopo me, Yeshua HaMashiach, mpo Yahweh asomdwoeɛ apam a ɛyɛ pɛ a ɔde brɛɛ nnipa no a, ɛne sɛ wopo Yahweh na wokɔka Satan atuateɛ no ho, ɛfiri sɛ Satan te atua tia Onyankopɔn nhyehyɛeɛ firi mfitiaseɛ.

Na meka kyerɛɛ Hebrifoɔ no sɛ: Na sɛ yɛyɛ bɔne wɔ yɛn pɛ mu berɛ a yɛanya nokorɛ no ho nimdeɛ akyi no, afɔrebɔ biara nni hɔ bio mma bɔne. Enti sɛ wɔnam me mogya a ɛsɔ pata no a wɔbɛpo so ayɛ bɔne wɔ wɔn pɛ mu, na ɛno nti wɔapo asomdwoeɛ apam a Yahweh de somaeɛ no a, ɛyɛ yaw pa ara, na afɔrebɔ biara nni hɔ bio. Na wɔatwerɛ wɔ Hebrifoɔ nwoma no mu sɛ: Nea ɔbuu Mose mmara animtiaa no wuiɛ a ahummɔborɔ nni mu wɔ adansefoɔ mmienu anaa mmiɛnsa ano: Asotwe a ɛyɛ den bɛn na mosusu sɛ ɔfata, nea watiatia Onyankopɔn Ba no so, na wabu apam no mogya a wɔde tee ne ho no sɛ adeɛ a ɛho nte, na wayɛ animtiaabu atia adom Honhom no?

Enti mehyɛ mo nyinaa nkuran sɛ monnye m’afɔrebɔ no ntom na monhu sɛ afɔrebɔ biara nni hɔ a ɛho hia bio na Mesia nokwafoɔ baako pɛ na ɔwɔ hɔ, ɛno ne me, Yeshua HaMashiach. Na wɔatwerɛ afa me ho sɛ: Nanso ɔbarima yi, berɛ a ɔbɔɔ afɔdeɛ baako maa bɔne daa no, ɔtenaa ase wɔ Onyankopɔn nifa so; Efi saa berɛ no rekɔ no, ɔretwɛn kɔsi sɛ wɔde n’atamfo bɛyɛ n’anan ntiasoɔ. Na ɔnam afɔrebɔ baako so ayɛ wɔn a wɔate wɔn ho no pɛ daa.

Enti monhu sɛ matew mo ho wɔ biribiara mu ne afa biara a ɛfa bɔne ne ne nsunsuansoɔ ho. Wɔatew mo ho wɔ me mu; wɔabu mo bem wɔ me mu; wɔayɛ mo Onyankopɔn tenenee wɔ me mu; na magye mo koraa ama me ho, mpo me, Yeshua HaMashiach, a Yahweh asoma mo sɛ mo Mesia no. Na enti monhu sɛ Lewifoɔ Asɔfoɔ Panyin biara nni hɔ bio, na Aaron Asɔfodie no aba na atwam, na na ɛyɛ nneɛma a ɛbɛba no sunsuma. Na mene mo sɔfoɔ panyin daa sɛdeɛ Melkisedek nhyehyɛeɛ teɛ.

Efirisɛ mmara no anyɛ biribiara pɛ: na mmom na ɛyɛ anidasoɔ pa bi a yɛde bɛn Yahweh no nkyerɛkyerɛmu. Na bio: Sɔfoɔ Panyin a ɔte saa na ɛsɛ sɛ yɛnya a ɔyɛ kronkron, ɔho nni asɛm, ɔho nni dɛm, ɔtetew ne ho firi nnebɔneyɛfoɔ ho, na wɔama no so sene ɔsoro. Na bio: Na mmara no ma mmarima yɛ asɔfoɔ, a wɔwɔ mmerɛyɛ: nanso ntam a ɛbaa mmara no akyi no asɛm no ma ɔba no yɛ sɔfoɔ, a ɔyɛ pɛ daa. Monkenkanee deɛ kyerɛwsɛm ka fa me ho? Yahweh kaa ntam na ɔrennu ne ho: Woyɛ Sɔfoɔ daa sɛdeɛ Melkisedek nhyehyɛeɛ teɛ.

Monhu sɛ biribiara nni hɔ a ɛhɔ aka wɔ m’afɔrebɔ no mu, na mo ho yɛ den wɔ me, Kristo Yesu mu. Wɔnam gyidie a wɔwɔ wɔ me, Yeshua HaMashiach mu so na ɛdi Torah so, na ɛntumi nyɛ yie sɛ mobɛbu Onyankopɔn mmara no mu biara so denam me, Kristo Yesu mu a mobɛnantew so. Na monantew wɔ nkwa Honhom no mmara mu, na mayɛ mo ahofadie afiri bɔne ne owuo mmara mu. Enti monnya gyidie wɔ me mu, na ɔteneneeni nam gyidie so bɛtena ase. Mewɔ nnwuma akɛseɛ pii a mafrɛ mo sɛ monyɛ, na mobɛboa de nkwagyeɛ abrɛ nnipa pii. Anidasoɔ kɛseɛ ne akatua wɔ hɔ a wɔde asie ama mo wɔ ɔsoro esiane mo gyinabea ne mo nokwaredie nti.

Na mesoma mo, a wɔasɔ mo ano wɔ mo moma so de Yahweh ankasa nsɔano, kɔ wiase a ɛbɛtan mo koraa mu, na ɛnyɛ nwanwa, na wiase tan me koraa kane, na ɔsomfoɔ nso sene ne wura. Moyɛ m’adansefoɔ ne m’adiyifoɔ ne me nnurayɛfoɔ ne wɔn a mode nneɛma ma. Mesoma mo kɔ esum a emu yɛ duru pa ara a ɛbɛkata Yahweh abɔdeɛ so da mu. Mmerɛ a ɛte sɛɛ nti na wɔafrɛ mo, wɔayi mo, na wɔasɔ mo ano, na saa mmerɛ yi bɛyɛ nea ɛyɛ aniwusɛm na ɛyɛ bɔne pa ara a wiase ahu pɛn, mpo ɛsen Noa nna no. Wɔn mfinimfini na mesoma mo kɔ, na meka kyerɛ mo sɛ monnsuro! Monnsuro wɔn ahunahuna; monnsuro deɛ onipa bɛtumi ayɛ mo; monnsuro wɔn anim; monnsuro ahonhom a ɛma wɔn tumi no; monnsuro aboa no ne odiyifoɔ atorofoɔ no; na monnsuro Satan ankasa: Mpo ɔtweaseɛ kɛseɛ no. Na mobɛtiatia Satan ahennie no afa biara so, na biribiara rentumi nsi mo kwan koraa sɛ motena me nne no mu nokware na momma menkyerɛ mo kwan anammɔn biara. Na nokorɛ mu, moyɛ adwumayɛfoɔ a me ne wɔn yɛ adwuma wɔ Yakob ahokyere berɛ yi mu, na mama mo tumi.

Moyɛ den na mowɔ tumi, na mesoma mo wɔ ahobrɛaseɛ ne brɛbrɛ mu. Mesoma mo sɛ monkɔdi mmara no so denam mo gyidie a mowɔ wɔ me mu so, na enti monfa mo koma, mo kra, mo adwene, ne mo ahoɔden nyinaa nnɔ Yahweh, na monnɔ mo yɔnko sɛ mo ho. Hwan ne wo yɔnko? Wɔn a wɔhia mo mmoa a meresoma mo akɔ wɔn nkyɛn nyinaa, na meremfa ɔman hyeɛ nsi mo kwan. Monkɔ na monkɔhwɛ me nnwan so, na mene Odwanhwɛfoɔ Panyin a mewɔ mo akatua wɔ me nsam ma nokwaredie. Monkɔhwɛ me nnwan, monkɔsa wɔn apirakuro, momma wɔn aduane, monsa wɔn yareɛ, monni wɔn anim, na nea ɛsen ne nyinaa no, momfa wɔn mmra me nkyɛn; na mo ara deɛ moyɛ hwee, nanso wɔ me mu no, moyɛ me mmarima a mowɔ tumi, Onyankopɔn mmarima a wɔyɛ den. Monkyerɛkyerɛ wɔn ma wɔmmɔ me din kɔsi awieeɛ, na obiara a ɔbɛyɛ saa no, wɔbɛgye no nkwa.

Momma mo ani nna abasobɔdeɛ ne anigyeɛ a wɔde asi mo anim no so, na mommia mo anim nkɔ anim nkɔ botaeɛ no so mma abasobɔdeɛ a ɛne Onyankopɔn frɛ a ɛkorɔn wɔ Kristo Yesu mu no. Na mo akatua bɛyɛ kɛseɛ, na mo ho bɛdwiri mo wɔ deɛ wɔde asie ama mo wɔ ɔsoro no ho. Na m’akatua nyɛ berɛ tiaa mu deɛ na mmom ɛyɛ daa deɛ, na mo akatua rennsɛe na mmom ɛbɛyɛ agyapadeɛ a ɛwɔ hɔ daa de ahyɛ bɔhyɛ ahorow a Yahweh hyɛɛ agyanom no ma; yod biara anaa yod nsɔeɛ biara bɛba mu. Morenhu mo nnwuma no kɛseyɛ kɔsi saa akatua da no, nanso Kyerɛwsɛm no da adi pefee wɔ mo nnwuma no aba ho.

Na Yohane Adiyifoɔ no twerɛɛ deɛ mekyerɛɛ no no nokorɛ mu sɛ: Nneɛma yi akyi no, mehunuu, na hwɛ, nnipadɔm kɛseɛ bi a obiara ntumi nkan wɔn, a wofiri aman ne mmusuakuo, ne nkurɔfoɔ, ne kasa nyinaa mu, gyinagyina ahengua no anim, ne Odwan Ba no anim, a wɔhyɛ atadeɛ fitaa atenten, na wɔkura mmeduaa wɔ wɔn nsam. Na wɔde nne kɛseɛ teateaam sɛ, Nkwagyeɛ firi yɛn Nyankopɔn a ɔte ahengua no so, ne Odwan Ba no hɔ. Na nnipadɔm kɛseɛ bi na mobɛboa agye wɔn nkwa na moasom wɔn, na mo nokwaredie akatua bɛyɛ kɛseɛ.

Na mobɛdi m’akyi baabiara a mɛkɔ, na moyɛ nnipa mu nnipa a wɔtɔɔ wɔn kane, na mobɛto dwom foforɔ wɔ ahengua no anim a mo nko ara na mobɛtumi asua. Mobɛdi me hene nso wɔ m’Ahennie mu mfeɛ apem na mo ne me abɛtena daa wɔ daa nkwa mu. Na mo deɛ yɛ anigyeɛ a ɛwɔ hɔ daa, na mo ne me bɛtena m’Ahennie mu wɔ asase so berɛ a meredi asase so hene wɔ tenenee mu afiri Dawid ahengua no so, na m’Ahennie nni awieeɛ. Na bɔne biara remma mu, na ɔhaw biara remma bio, na me deɛ yɛ tenenee ahennie a odwan ne gyata bɛdeda faako wɔ asomdwoeɛ mu. Na me mfeɛ apem ahennie no awieeɛ no, wɔbɛyi Satan ama no berɛ tiaa bi na ɔboaboa n’ ntua a ɛtwa toɔ no ano, na ogya bɛfiri Yahweh hɔ abɛsɛe wɔn pasaa. Wɔbɛto Satan akyene ogya tadeɛ no mu, na ɔrensɔre bio da, na Yahweh bɛbɔ ɔsoro foforɔ ne asase foforɔ, na ɔbɛtena nnipa mu, na enti mobɛtena Yerusalem Foforɔ mu, mpo kuro kronkron a Yahweh ankasa bɔeɛ no mu.
