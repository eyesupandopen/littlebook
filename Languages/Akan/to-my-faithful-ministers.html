Adanse Mmienu: Asafo no
Wɔ Asomafoɔ Paulo Nnwuma Mu
Kɔtɔnimaa 06, 2024

Mekɔma Mo Tumi: Mo a Moyɛ Me Somfoɔ Nokwafoɔ!

Mekasa kyerɛ me somfoɔ nokwafoɔ ne wɔn a wɔafiri Laodikea!

Me Yere, tie me nne na bra me nkyɛn, na matumi akyekye wo werɛ na mahyɛ wo den wɔ me tumi mu. Nsuro! M’abufuo nni wo so, na mɛka akyerɛ wo sɛ: Ayikoo, akoa pa ne ɔnokwafoɔ; woayɛ nokwafoɔ wɔ nneɛma nketenkete mu, mɛyɛ wo sodifoɔ wɔ nneɛma pii so: kɔ w’Awurade anigyeɛ mu. Na mewɔ nsɛm a ɛyɛ den, ntɛnsoɔ nsɛm, ma wɔn a wɔyɛ asoɔden; nanso saa ntɛnsoɔ no nkyerɛ wo, ɛfiri sɛ woyɛ ɔnokwafoɔ, ɛwom sɛ ɛsɛ sɛ wotie m’asɛm deɛ, na woanhwe ase.

Me Yere nokwafoɔ, woyɛ m’ani so biribi a ɛsom bo, na afobu biara nni hɔ ma wo, ɛfiri sɛ wowɔ Kristo mu, na wode wo nkwa asi me, Yesu Kristo, so, ɔboɔ a ɛdi anim a adansifoɔ atorofoɔ no apo no no. Kɔ so yɛ nokwafoɔ wɔ me mu, na merema tumi kɛseɛ aba wo so, ɛfiri sɛ mmerɛ yi awieeɛ abɛn. Meresoma wo wɔ tumi kɛseɛ ne nnwuma a woansusu da wɔ wo ho mu, na woannim sɛ wɔwoo wo wɔ Onyankopɔn ahintasɛm mu firi wo home a ɛdi kan no, nanso woanyini yie wɔ m’adom mu, na afei deɛ wohunu. Na m’adom yɛ adom a tumi wom a wontumi nni so, na me mmɔborɔhunu firi tete de kɔsi daa.

Hwɛ! Yeshua HaMashiach na ɔse. Tie me na kyerɛw m’asɛm gu wo koma tɛkrɛma a ɛyɛ honam no so. Na meresoma wo; aane, merema wo tumi wɔ nsɛm yi mu, na wɔn a mede tumi ma wɔn no bɛnya me pɛ a ɛyɛ anigyeɛ no ho adanseɛ a wontumi nnye ho akyinnyeɛ. Meresoma wo wɔ me nnipa akɛseɛ no nnwuma mu, nnwuma a wɔhyɛɛ aseɛ mfeɛ pii a atwam ni, na saa nnwuma no kɔ so de bɛsi nnɛ. Na wɔnnya nkyerɛw Asomafoɔ Nnwuma nwoma no nwieeɛ kɔsi sɛ me Yere bɛba me nkyɛn. Afei wobɛte ne nyinaa ase firi nwoma a ɛwɔ soro no mu, na wɔakyerɛw mo ankorankoro nnwuma akɛseɛ no wɔ hɔ, na yɛn nyinaa bɛdi ahurisie wɔ wo nokwaredie ho wɔ Yahweh anim, na ɔne yɛn di ahurisie na ɔwɔ mo nyinaa mu.

Hwɛ! Meresoma mo mu bi wɔ Paulo som adwuma mu sɛ me ahintasɛm so ahwɛfoɔ ne asomafoɔ ma amanaman no. Hwɛ! Meresoma mo mu bi wɔ Elia honhom mu sɛ montia Yesebel, odiyifoɔ ɔbaa torofoɔ no, na monhyɛ nkɔm mma me, Kristo Yesu! Hwɛ! Meresoma mo mu bi wɔ ɔsɛmpakafoɔ Filipo adwuma mu, faako a mɛfa wo afiri baabi akɔ baabi a m’asɛmpa nsɛm wɔ w’anom, a wɔayɛ no pɛpɛɛpɛ ama wɔn a wɔnnya nnye nkwagyeɛ no ahiadeɛ, na wode ɔsɛn no ho asɛmpaka bɛgye wɔn nkwa! Hwɛ! Meresoma mo mu bi wɔ Petro adwuma mu, na ɔhwɛɛ me nguan so na ɔmaa wɔn Onyankopɔn asɛm kronkron no diiɛ. Na Laodikea asomfoɔ nkwaseafoɔ no ma me nguan di aduane bɔne, nsɛm a gyidie nni mu a tumi biara nni mu. Hwɛ! Mayɛ mo akyerɛkyerɛfoɔ sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a mobɛkyerɛkyerɛ na moatu me nkurɔfoɔ fo wɔ tenenee akwan so, na mobɛkyerɛkyerɛ wɔn sɛdeɛ mekyerɛ! Na mobɛhwɛ wɔn so sɛdeɛ mekyerɛ! Na mobɛka asɛmpa no akyerɛ wɔn sɛdeɛ mekyerɛ! Na me nkɔmhyɛfoɔ bɛka nsɛm a mede bɛma wɔn no, a nteaseɛ ne koma mu nsakraeɛ biara nni mu! Na m’asomafoɔ bɛma me nsɛmpa a ɛyɛ anigyeɛ a ɛfa Onyankopɔn ahintasɛm kɛseɛ no ho no ahyerɛn, sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a wobetumi ayi Mose nkatasoɔ no afiri mo koma so. Na Korintofoɔ adanseɛ no teatea mu! Nanso de bɛsi nnɛ, sɛ wɔkenkan Mose a, nkatasoɔ no da wɔn koma so. Nanso, sɛ ɛdan kɔ Awurade nkyɛn a, wɔbɛyi nkatasoɔ no. Afei Awurade ne saa honhom no, na baabi a Awurade Honhom no wɔ no, ɛhɔ na ahofadie wɔ. Nanso yɛn nyinaa, yɛde anim a ɛda hɔ hwɛ Awurade animuonyam sɛdeɛ ɛteɛ wɔ ahwehwɛ mu, na wɔsakra yɛn kɔ su korɔ no ara mu, firi animuonyam mu kɔ animuonyam mu, sɛdeɛ ɛteɛ firi Awurade honhom no mu. Me, Kristo Yesu, meyi mo nkatasoɔ no!

Hwɛ, meresoma wo wɔ sima a ɛtwa toɔ yi mu ansa na wo ne me ahyia wɔ m’ahoyie mu. Mehia sɛ wotie me nne na wodi m’akwankyerɛ akyi, sɛdeɛ woyɛ nokwafoɔ no, me Yere nokwafoɔ, na woyɛ nokwafoɔ! Nkɔ adwuma a meresoma wo no mu wɔ wo ara wo nteaseɛ mu. Nkɔ adwuma a meresoma wo no mu wɔ wo ara wo tumi mu. Nkɔ adwuma a meresoma wo no mu wɔ wo ara wo nimdeɛ mu. Nkɔ adwuma a meresoma wo no mu wɔ wo ara wo ahoɔden mu. Tie m’asɛm no ne wo koma nyinaa! Na me, me ara, Kristo Yesu Awurade a wɔahyɛ no animuonyam no, mereka akyerɛ wo sɛ: Mene wo nteaseɛ, na mene wo tumi, na mene wo ahoɔden, na mene wo nimdeɛ: nnye akyinnyeɛ koraa!

Yɛ nnwuma a mɛkyerɛ wo sɛ yɛ, na hwɛ nnipa a meresoma wɔn wo nkyɛn no so. Wobɛbrɛ wo ho ase ama nnipa a wɔwɔ dibea a ɛba fam? Wobɛtena ase, aka asɛm akyerɛ, agye nkwa, na wo ne wɔn a wonni fie no abɔ fekuo? Na meresoma mo mu bi wɔ saa atirimpɔw no nti. Wobɛsrɛ Laodikea asomfoɔ no wɔ ahobrɛaseɛ mu sɛdeɛ obi bɛyɛ ama Agya? Na saa na m’akwan teɛ. Wobɛka nsɛm a mede bɛma wo no, sɛ mpo wonte aseɛ yie anaasɛ wompene so a? (Fa kyerɛwsɛm hwehwɛ m’asɛm mu.) Na meresoma wo sɛ yɛ me pɛ, na ɛnyɛ wo ara wo deɛ. Wobɛhwɛ nguan no so wɔ ogya a ɛwɔ wo honhom mu a mɛsɔ ama wo no ayaresa tumi mu? (Na sɛ oguanhwɛfoɔ panin no pue a, wobɛnya animuonyam abotire a ɛmpa da.) Yɛ me nnwuma, me Yere, na gyae wo ara wo nnwuma, na ɛnni hɔ bio, na ɛrenkyɛ na kanea no adum.

Te aseɛ, meresoma wo wɔ me nnwuma mu, na sɛ woyɛ nokwafoɔ a, wubenya akatua kɛseɛ, na wowɔ agyapadeɛ pii a wɔasie wɔ soro, na adan pii a ɛwɔ m’Agya fie no asiesie ama wo. Na Yakob amanehunu yɛ nna nson a ɛyɛ mfeɛ nson, faako a wɔbu afe biara sɛ da koro. Saa ara nso na Ayeforokunu ne ne Yere ayeforohyia no teɛ. Na nna nson a ɛyɛ mfeɛ nson bɛba wɔ soro, faako a wɔbu afe biara sɛ da koro, na wo ne me bɛtena daa, me Yere. Afei yɛbɛtena apɔnkɔ so!

Fa w’akwan so na fa ɔkwan a merekyerɛ wo no so. Momfa ɔdɔ ne ahobrɛaseɛ mmrɛ mo ho ase mma mo ho mo ho. Hwɛ me turo no so, na madua nkwa a ɛnni awieeɛ turo a ɔbɔnefoɔ no ntumi ntutu anaasɛ ɔnsɛe no. Wubedi nkwa dua no ankasa a wadi nkoguo wɔ ne bammɔ a ɔbɔɔ wo ho no mu, na nkwa a ɛdɔɔso nni Satan ogya tadeɛ a ɔrekɔ mu no mu, na asotwe, nuhoɔ, aniwuo, ne ɔhaw nko ara na ɛwɔ hɔ. Nneɛma yi nyɛ wo dea, me Yere, na owuo a ɛtɔ so mmienu no nni tumi wɔ wo so, na woafiri owuo mu aba nkwa mu wɔ me mu, Yesu Kristo, wo gyefoɔ ne w’Awurade.

Enti kɔ faako a meresoma wo, na yɛ adwuma ne nnipa a meresoma wɔn wo nkyɛn, ne wɔn a meresoma wɔn wo nkyɛn no. Na wɔmmra wo nkyɛn kwa, na ɛfiri sɛ mafiri aseɛ dedaw rekyerɛ wɔn kwan aba wo nkyɛn. Tena me ahintasɛm no mu na tena sɛdeɛ wɔtenaa aseɛ wɔ Asomafoɔ Nnwuma mu no, na wone ti a ɛtwa toɔ a wɔrekyerɛw berɛ a wote ase ma me no, na ɛrenkyɛ na woahunu berɛ a wowɔ soro akyire yi a matwe wo aba me nkyɛn no, na woyɛ me dea! Woyɛ me yere, m’Asafo a animuonyam wom, a mɛyɛ wo ama me ho, a dɔteɛ anaa ntwitwirii biara nni wo ho, anaasɛ biribi a ɛte saa biara: na mmom sɛ ɛsɛ sɛ woyɛ kronkron na mfomsoɔ biara nni wo ho, na mede me mogya a ɛsɔ gya no ahodwo wo ho!

Mefre wo, me Yere, na mefre wo kɔ som adwuma a woansusu da sɛ wubetumi ayɛ mu, na Laodikea asomfoɔ no bɔɔ mɔden sɛ wɔbɛma woatena sum mu. Nguan mfiri tumi a meresɔ wɔ wo mu no ho, na Awurade nsa, me nsa a ɛyɛ nhyira no, wɔ wo so. Nguan mfiri me som adwuma a meresɔ wɔ wo honhom mu no ho. Na mɛsɔ ogya kɛseɛ bi wɔ wo honhom mu, me Yere, ogya a ɛbɛnane nneɛma a ɛsi wo kwan nyinaa. Nsuro! Nante wɔ me tumi mu, na Laodikea asomfoɔ a wonni gyidie no abɔ mɔden sɛ wɔbɛma woagye adi sɛ wonni tumi, na anomuyɛ kɛseɛ wɔ hɔ ma wɔn sɛ wɔannu wɔn ho a.

Hwɛ! Meresoma wo kɔ nnwuma a afoforɔ adi nkoguo wɔ mu no mu, na wobɛkɔ wɔn ananmu, na woyɛ nokwafoɔ: Sɛe w’ahomasoɔ nko ara, na woanyɛ bɔne so adeɛ sɛdeɛ wɔayɛ no. Nka w’ano, na masane no. Ka nsɛm a mede ama wo no, na di m’anim wɔ tumi kɛseɛ mu. Wosusu sɛ meyɛ nnipa mu animhwɛfoɔ? Na seesei yɛ anwanwadeɛ titire berɛ, sɛdeɛ mekyerɛw wɔ Asomafoɔ Nnwuma mu no: Na Onyankopɔn nam Paulo nsa so yɛɛ anwanwadeɛ titire: Sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a wɔde ntama anaa aprɔn firi ne nipadua so brɛɛ ayarefoɔ, na nyarewa no firii wɔn so, na ahonhommɔne no firii wɔn mu. Me Yere a wowɔ gyidie kɛseɛ, wie nnwuma a mefrɛ wo sɛ yɛ no ma me, na yɛrehyɛ me atirimpɔw a ɛwɔ wo mu no mu ma, na owia retɔ wɔ m’adom mmerɛ no so, na ɛrenkyɛ na anadwo aduru wiase no so koraa, na worefiri hɔ. Hwɛ! Me, Yesu Kristo, mɛyɛ anwanwadeɛ akɛseɛ pii, anwanwadeɛ titire mpo, denam wo nsa so, na yei ne tumideɛ kɛseɛ a ɛtwa toɔ ma m’Asafo ansa na wɔatwe wo akɔ animuonyam mu.

Kɔ, asomdwoeɛ mu, na mɛkyerɛ wo kwan. Hunu sɛ wo nnamfo, w’abusua, ne w’atamfo nyinaa bɛsɔre atia wo kɛseɛ. Yei ne mpaapaemu mmerɛ a meretwa wɔn a wɔnnyɛ nokwafoɔ no na meretwitwa m’olibo dua no ho afiri wɔn gyidie a enni mu akwan no ho, na wɔansiesie wɔn ho amma ayeforokunu no, na wɔne mmaabunu nkwaseafoɔ no bɛtena. Na meretwitwa me nokwafoɔ no ho afiri bɔne mu sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a wɔbɛsow nnua foforɔ a ɛsow aba pii. Nsuro, nsusu sɛ womfata anwanwadeɛ. Na mepene so, mo ara mo mu deɛ, mo mu biara ntumi nyɛ anwanwadeɛ; nanso mɛma nsɛnkyerɛnneɛ, anwanwadeɛ, ne anwanwadeɛ pii wɔ me nokwafoɔ no nsa ano. Kɔ na ka kyerɛ mmepɔ no sɛ, montu na monkɔgu po mu, na nnye akyinnyeɛ na mmom nya gyidie wɔ Yahweh, ne Me, Yeshua HaMashiach mu.

Honhom mu hama biara nni hɔ a wɔde bɛbɔ, enti gyae wo ara wo mmɔdenbɔ. Na wubetumi anante nsuo so sɛ wode w’ani si me so na wonnye akyinnyeɛ na wonhwɛ benkum anaa nifa na mmom wode w’ani si me so a, na worenkɔ ase. Hyɛ ahonhom no, na wɔbrɛ wɔn ho ase ma me na enti wɔbrɛ wɔn ho ase ma wo. Hyɛ ahonhom no sɛ wɔmfiri hɔ; mmisa, na mmom hyɛ! Mma wɔnnkasa, na mmom yɛ wɔn komm; nnka asɛm nkyerɛ wɔn, na wɔn nsɛm yɛ awuduro; na mmom pam wɔn kɔ serɛ so faako a wonni fie, na ɛrenkyɛ na wɔatow wɔn agu ogya no mu. Sa ayarefoɔ yareɛ! Kasa tia yareɛ no wɔ me din mu, na ka kyerɛ no sɛ ɔmfiri hɔ; nsuro, na mɛma wo nsɛm no. Nsuro! Enhia sɛ woka nsɛm a ɛyɛ fɛ; ka gyidie nsɛm a mɛkanyan wo koma na nnye akyinnyeɛ.

Hwɛ! Kɔ ma me, me Yere, a wonnye biribiara ho akyinnyeɛ. Mereto nsa afrɛ wo aba nnwuma a mereyɛ awie no mu, a mo mu pii yɛ me mfɛfoɔ adwumayɛfoɔ dedaw. Akra da so wɔ hɔ a ɛsɛ sɛ wɔgye wɔn nkwa, mpo wɔn a Satan rebɔ mɔden sɛ ɔde wɔn bɛsie no. Mɛbubu wɔn afiri ne nkɔnsɔnkɔnsɔn mu denam wo nnwuma akɛseɛ ne nnwuma akɛseɛ a yɛbɛbom ayɛ no so. Kɔ so, me Yere, a wonnye biribiara ho akyinnyeɛ, na masiesie wo wo nkwa nna nyinaa ama tumi ne osetie sima kakra yi, na me ne wɔn a masoma wɔn sɛ wɔnhwɛ w’akra so no atete wo yie.

Me Yere nokwafoɔ, medɔ wo, na woyɛ m’anigyeɛ ne m’ahurisidie. Meda so serew wo so daa, nanso wonhu me wɔ wo ara w’ani so, na woda so wɔ gyidie. Mesu wɔ wo haw ho, ɛwom sɛ mesu komm mpɛn pii deɛ. Mete wo mmoa sufrɛ, na meyɛ mmoa a ɛwɔ hɔ daa wɔ berɛ a ehia mu. Medɔ wo na medwene wo ho, na woyɛ m’ani so adeɛ a ɛsom bo, na ɛrenkyɛ na yɛabom. Menim sɛ woabrɛ, na woayɛ nokwafoɔ ama m’Asɛm ne me din, enti mɛhyɛ wo den wɔ Onyankopɔn a ɔnni anohyetoɔ ne daa no ahoɔden mu, Yahweh, na obiara nni hɔ a ɔte sɛ ɔno! Mpa abaw, na ɛwom sɛ ebia wo honam abrɛ deɛ, nanso mɛhyɛ wo den wɔ me tumi mu, na me, Yeshua HaMashiach, maka!

Mede tumi kɛseɛ reba wo nkyɛn.

Mede tumi kɛseɛ resoma wo.

Mefre wo, me Yere, kɔ som adwuma a woannim sɛ wowɔ kɔsi seesei mu.

Meresoma mo mu bi sɛ monkɔwie wɔn a wɔnnyɛ nokwafoɔ no nnwuma, na mobɛnya wɔn akatua.

Meretwa wɔn a wɔnnyɛ nokwafoɔ no.

Meretwitwa me nokwafoɔ no ho afiri mo bɔne mu sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a mobɛsow aba pii.

Meyɛ mmoa a ɛwɔ hɔ daa wɔ berɛ a ehia mu.

Fa wo kwan so, na gyina Asɔredan no mu, na ka nkwa yi ho nsɛm nyinaa kyerɛ nkurɔfoɔ no. Na woyɛ Onyankopɔn teasefoɔ no asɔredan!
