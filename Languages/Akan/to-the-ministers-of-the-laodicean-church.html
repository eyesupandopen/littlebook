Adansefoɔ Mmienu: Asɔre no
Wɔ Ɔsomafoɔ Paulo Nnwoma mu
May 06, 2024

Mo Laodikea Asɔre mu Asomfoɔ: Muntie me Nsɛm!

M’ayefam ne m’Asɔre, muntie me wɔ ɔdɔ a mewɔ ma mo, me mma no mu, na momfa mo aso nto me kasa so. Muntie me Nsɛm, o me Laodikea asomfoɔ, mo a mama mo akyɛdeɛ no mpo. Na mama nnipa akyɛdeɛ, na mama ebinom asomafoɔ, na ebinom adiyifoɔ, na ebinom asɛmpakafoɔ, na ebinom asɔfoɔ, ne akyerɛkyerɛfoɔ. Mama mo akyɛdeɛ yi de ahyɛ ahoteefoɔ no mã, de ama ɔsom adwuma no, de akyekyere Kristo nipadua no. Na ɛdeɛn na meka kyerɛɛ m’asomafoɔ ne m’asuafoɔ no wɔ Yohane asɛmpa nkyerɛwee no mu: Na hwɛ, mɛsoma m’agya bɔhyɛ no aba mo so. Nanso montena Yerusalem kuro no mu, kɔsi sɛ wɔde tumi a ɛfiri soro bɛhyɛ mo mã.

Na wɔ m’Asomafoɔ Nnwuma no mu no, wɔakyerɛw bɔhyɛ yi mmamu no ho asɛm sɛ: Na Pentekoste da no duruiɛ no, wɔn nyinaa hyiaa baabiako wɔ adwenkoro mu. Na mpofirim no, nnyigyei bi te sɛ ahum kɛseɛ a ɛrebɔ firii soro bae, na ɛhyɛɛ efie a wɔte mu no nyinaa mã. Na tɛkrɛma a wɔapae mu te sɛ ogya yii ne ho adi kyerɛɛ wɔn, na ɛtenatenaa wɔn mu biara so. Na Honhom Kronkron hyɛɛ wɔn nyinaa mã, na wɔfirii aseɛ kasaa kasa foforɔ, sɛdeɛ Honhom no maa wɔn tumi sɛ wɔnka no. Na wɔakyerɛw m’Asɔre no fapem ho asɛm wɔ kyerɛwsɛm no mu sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a mobɛhunu asɛmpa bɔhyɛ kɛseɛ yi mmamu, sɛ mahyɛ mo mã tumi a ɛfiri soro, awoɔ foforɔ no mu mpo, me, Kristo Yesu mo Awurade no mu mpo. Na mene nipadua no, Asɔre no, ti: Mene mfiaseɛ, owufoɔ mu abakan, sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a nnoɔma nyinaa mu no, mɛdi kan: Na ɛsɔɔ Agya no ani sɛ ME mu na mmamu nyinaa bɛtena.

Me Laodikea asomfoɔ, me mmamu no ntena mo asɔre a moahyɛ da ayɛ no sɛdeɛ mo ankasa tesɛ no mu, na megyina apono no ano merobɔ mu! Me Laodikea asomfoɔ, mankora Agya no mmamu a ɛwɔ me mu no nyinaa pɛsɛmenkominya mu, na mmom mehwiee me honhom guu so na memaa mo ahoɔden, na mede bɔhyɛ honhom kronkron no asɔ mo ano. Biribiara ntumi mmu nsɔano no, ɛwom sɛ sɛ mopɛ a, mobɛtumi anante akɔ ɔpae mu. Nanso Agya no, Yahweh onnyina daa Nyankopɔn no nsɔano no, wontumi mmu; na ɛnyɛ yie sɛ wɔbɛbu Onyankopɔn nsɔano, na mowɔ me mu dennen sɛ moyɛ nokorɛdifoɔ wɔ me mu a, na me bɔhyɛ yɛ yoo ne amen!

Me Laodikea asomfoɔ, akyɛdeɛ a mede ama mo no, mo akwan a ɛyɛ nwunu no nti moabu ani agu so, na moadum honhom a mede ama mo no, na mopɛ sɛ mobue akyɛdeɛ a mede ama mo no, na moama m’akyɛdeɛ no atena hɔ a wɔmmueeɛ wɔ adwempaeɛ ne animtiaabu mu. Yei nyɛ asɛm ketewa a wɔde agorɔ, na ɛyɛ bɔne kɛseɛ a ɛda mo so, na moantie me nne. Mommu me nsɛm, na mommu m’akwan anaa monkyerɛkyerɛ. Na ɛdeɛn na meyɛɛ ma Paulo kyerɛw kɔmaa m’Asɔre a na wɔate foforɔ saa berɛ no kannifoɔ abɔfra no, Timoteo mpo: Mmu akyɛdeɛ a ɛwɔ wo mu no animtia, a wɔnam Nkɔmhyɛ so, ne ɔpanin bi nsa a ɔde guu so so de maa wo no. Yɛ saa nnoɔma yi ho adwuma, na fa wo ho hyɛ mu, na ama wɔahunu sɛdeɛ wunya mfasoɔ wɔ nnoɔma nyinaa mu.

O, anka moakenkan me nsɛm no na moate aseɛ! O, anka mobɛkae me nsɛm no wɔ gyidie mu, nanso mo nte aseɛ na monni gyidie! O, anka mobɛtie me nsɛm, na ɔsetie a wɔyɛ ma me, Awurade Yesu Kristo, yɛ ɔsetie a wɔyɛ ma nkwa nsutire, na nsuo a ɛte aseɛ asubɔnten bɛsene afiri mo yafunu mu, na mama mo me honhom. Nanso sɛ anka mobɛkenkan me nsɛm no, mohwɛ mo mfoni ntokuro so wɔ ɔsetie a adwene nnim mu; mufiri mfoni ntokuro so kɔ foforɔ so, bɔne na ɛgyigye mo ani! Na moyɛ nkwaseafoɔ araa ma modwene sɛ ɔsetie a adwene nnim yɛ gyidie a ɛyɛ adwuma wɔ me mu, na wɔadaadaa mo. Moabu akyɛdeɛ a mede ama mo no animtia, onipa foforɔ no mpo, na mmom mode berɛ ayɛ mo onipa dada no su ho adwuma wɔ ɔkwa mu berɛ a mohwehwɛ sɛ mobɛma mo honam ani agye.

Sɛ anka mobɛyɛ mo ho adwuma wɔ honhom mu nnoɔma mu no, mode ahuhudeɛ yɛ mo nipadua a asɛe na ɛresɛe no ho adwuma, na apɔmuteneteneɛ ma mfasoɔ berɛ tiawa bi, na mo nipadua resɛe. Na me nne gyegye berɛ nyinaa mu firi Paulo krataa a ɔde kɔmaa Efeso no mu: Ɔnam ne honam mu ayi ɔtan no afiri hɔ, Mmara no Mmara a ɛwɔ Ahyɛdeɛ mu no mpo, sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a ɔbɛyɛ ne ho mu, afiri mmienu mu, onipa foforɔ baako, na ɔnam so de asomdwoeɛ aba. Muntie me, me mma, na medɔ mo na medwene mo kra ho, me Laodikeafoɔ a moyɛ nwunu no mpo. Na mopɛ sɛ monante mo onipa dada no ne n’akwan so, mo honam a asɛe no mpo, na mode animtew nyinaa ma mo honam. Sɛ anka ɛno na ɛte saa a, anka ɛsɛ sɛ mode mo adwene ne mo akoma si me ne me nsɛm biara so: Na ɛnyɛ brodo nko ara na onipa de bɛtena ase, na mmom asɛm biara a ɛfiri Onyankopɔn anomu. Na ɛnam ɔsetie a ɛda adi pefee a moyɛ ma me nsɛm (Onyankopɔn Asɛm no mpo, na mene Asɛm a wɔyɛɛ no honam na metena mo mu ne mo mu) so no, monante honhom mu na ɛnyɛ honam mu.

Na maka akyerɛ Korintofoɔ Asɔre no sei: Nanso yɛn nyinaa, yɛde anim a ɛda adi pefee hwɛ Awurade animuonyam no sɛ ahwehwɛ mu, na wɔresakra yɛn ayɛ sɛdeɛ ɔno ara te, firi animuonyam mu kɔ animuonyam mu, sɛdeɛ ɛnam Awurade honhom so no. Me mma, me nkrataa a mede kɔmaa m’Asɔre (ɔdɔ nkrataa a mede kɔmaa m’Ayefam no mpo) ne mo ankasa ho ahwehwɛ, na mo ankasa ho ahunta Onyankopɔn ahintasɛm kɛseɛ no mu. Ɛnam ɔsetie a moyɛ ma me nsɛm ne m’ahyɛdeɛ so no, mobɛnante honhom mu, na moremma mo honam a asɛe no ani nnye. Ɛsɛ sɛ wɔbu kyerɛwsɛm nyinaa na wɔsua, nanso moyɛ ahintasɛm a na ahunta Onyankopɔn mu na afei wɔayi no adi, na ɛsɛ sɛ mosua deɛ meyii kyerɛɛ Paulo no na moate abɔdeɛ foforɔ a mereyɛ mo wɔ me, Kristo Yesu mu no ase.

Me Laodikea asomfoɔ, mfomsoɔ kɛseɛ bi wɔ mo mu. Moabu me nsɛm animtia koraa, na moabu akyɛdeɛ a mede ama mo no animtia araa ma moansa mpo, na mmom mode m’asafo no hyɛ nnuruyɛ ne dade twerɛ nsa; na sɛ anka moyɛ osetie ma me a, anka mɛma wɔnam mo nsa so ayɛ anwanwadeɛ. Nanso mobu ɔpaefoɔ nguanhwɛfoɔ no nsɛm sene Yeshua HaMashiach nsɛm, na m’asafo a ɛwɔ Laodikea no tua ka wɔ mo asoɔden no ho; na monhwɛ wɔn bio, na mmom moama wɔabrɛ wɔ tumi a enni mu bɔne mu. Wɔnam nsɛm a mede maa Yohane wɔ ne krataa a ɛdi kan no mu so na ɛhunu ɔpaefoɔ nguanhwɛfoɔ: Mma nketewa, ɛyɛ berɛ a ɛtwa toɔ: na sɛdeɛ moate sɛ Antikristo bɛba no, seesei mpo Antikristo pii wɔ hɔ, na ɛno na yɛnam so hunu sɛ ɛyɛ berɛ a ɛtwa toɔ. Wɔfirii yɛn mu kɔe, nanso na wɔnyɛ yɛn dea: na sɛ na wɔyɛ yɛn dea a, anka akyinnyeɛ biara nni ho sɛ wɔbɛkɔ so atena yɛn nkyɛn: nanso wɔfirii mu kɔe sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a wɔbɛda adi, sɛ na wɔnyɛ yɛn nyinaa dea.

Mo nte aseɛ sɛ wɔn a antikristo honhom di wɔn so no de Yesu foforɔ si oguanhwɛfoɔ nokorɛ no, me, Kristo Yesu ananmu? Na yeinom ne Kristo asɛnnua no atamfoɔ, na wɔtia me, na wɔde wɔn ho si m’ananmu, rehwehwɛ sɛ wɔbɛfa me tumi, a ɛnyɛ yie. Yei nom abɛfra mo asɔre, mo nyamekyerɛ sukuu, mo ahyehyɛdeɛ ahodoɔ mu, na wɔdi mo asɔre so ɛnam sɛ mopene so sɛ mobɛyɛ adwuma wɔ 501(c)(3) ase, na wɔn a wɔwɔ saa apam no ase nyinaa, wiase aban abɔ wɔn ano. Mo 501(c)(3) no kyerɛ anibereɛ a mowɔ ma sika fĩ, na moayɛ mo ho aguamammɔ wɔ Balaam akwan so, na mode nsɛm a ɛnyɛ nokorɛ ma sika sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a mobɛtumi akora sika a me nketewa no de maa mo wɔ mo toɔ a wɔayi afiri mu awaresɛeɛ no mu nyinaa ama mo ho! Moayɛ wiase adamfoɔ, na wiase reba biribiara a ɛbata me din ho so berɛ a wɔayi nea ɔsi ano kwan no afiri hɔ akyi. Monni honhom mu nhunumu na mo ani afira! Monni adɔeɛ a mode bɛbɔ kuo no ho ban afiri mpataku a wɔyɛ kɔm yi ho, na moani gye abodin a ɛbata mo din ho, abodin a memmaa mo; nanso mo ani bere. Na maka akyerɛ mo sɛ mompere akyɛdeɛ a ɛkyɛn so no ho denneennen: Na afei nso mekyerɛ mo ɔkwan a ɛkyɛn so: ɔkwan a ɛkyɛn so a mopɛ wɔ mo anihaw, nwunu akwan so.

Monka bɔne ne adwensakra a ɛfiri bɔne mu ho asɛm bio, na mmom moka anigyeɛ anansesɛm a ɛnyɛ me dea, na mo anansesɛm no yɛ atoro nsɛm na ɛde kɔ akwan a ɛnyɛ nokorɛ so; na monyɛ biribiara sɛ anansesɛm kyerɛfoɔ a ɔpete wɔn a wɔyɛ anihaw no aso. Mereka asɛm akyerɛ mo, m’asomafoɔ: na mo ani ayɛ sum, na monhunu me hann bio, na moka atoro! Mereka asɛm akyerɛ mo, m’adiyifoɔ: na mokasa ma Yesu foforɔ, na moabu me nsɛm animtia, na monnim me, na mohyɛ daakye a ɛnyɛ nokorɛ ho nkɔm! Mereka asɛm akyerɛ mo, m’asɛmpakafoɔ: Na mopaka asɛmpa no kyerɛ aman no ma wɔkɔ abonsam nkyerɛkyerɛ a ɛnyɛ nokorɛ mu, na moma wɔnom nsuo fĩ, na mode ahonhom a ɛho nteɛ hyɛ wɔn mã! Mereka asɛm akyerɛ mo, m’asɔfoɔ: Na modwene mo ankasa akɔnnɔ bɔne ho, na moayɛ mo ankasa yafunu mo nyame de mo ho mmoa nyansapɛ nwura; wo ɔkwasea, wontumi mfa tɛkrɛma a ɛdi atoro nsa m’nguan yareɛ! Mereka asɛm akyerɛ mo, m’akyerɛkyerɛfoɔ: Na monkyerɛkyerɛ m’asɛmpa no nsɛm kronkron no bio, na mmom mokyerɛkyerɛ asɔrekuo biakoyɛ nsɛm, na mfomsoɔ kɛseɛ bi wɔ mo nyinaa mu, me Laodikea asomfoɔ. Mowɔ yareɛ korɔ no ara bi: Monni obu mma ahyɛdeɛ a ɛwɔ m’Asɛm no mu bio, na monni obu mma nsɛmfua ankorankoro a ɛwɔ me kyerɛwsɛm no mu bio.

Mode ahomasoɔ dwene sɛ moyɛ nkyerɛasefoɔ ɛfiri sɛ mobɛtumi apaw nteaseɛ afiri wiase nsɛm asekyerɛ nwoma ne wiase nsɛm asekyerɛ nwoma mu de agye mo awieeɛ: Nnwoma a ɛnyɛ me na mekyerɛwee, na mekyerɛw kyerɛwsɛm nam m’adiyifoɔ so, na nkɔmhyɛ honhom ne Yesu adanseɛ! Nkyerɛasefoɔ a wɔka kasa baako pɛ, na moayɛ sereɛ! Mmom no, mobu ɔpaefoɔ nguanhwɛfoɔ no nsɛm, awia a mfasoɔ nni so a wɔyɛ sɛ wɔyɛ no nsɛm. Wɔbɛfraa mo asafo no mu sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a wɔbɛyɛ sɛ wɔyɛ mo (na moyɛ awia a anka mɛboaboa ano akɔ m’aduanekorabea sɛ mosan ba me nkyɛn a). Nanso mmom wɔyɛ awia, anuanom atorofoɔ a wɔbɛyɛɛ asomfoɔ atorofoɔ a wɔdaadaa mo. Wɔyɛ afifideɛ a ɛyɛ atorɔ na ɛwɔ awuduro!

Saa nnipa bɔne yi mfi yɛn Asɔre no mu nkɔ bio sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a wɔbɛda adi, na mmom mofrɛ wɔn ba na mohyɛ wɔn sɛ wɔne mo nni aduane na monni wɔn nsɛm a ɛnyɛ nokorɛ ne wɔn tɛkrɛma a ɛdi atoro no ni sene nsɛm a anka mede bɛma mo, Onyankopɔn Teasefoɔ no nsɛm, Yahweh nsɛm mpo! Mode me nsɛm di dwa sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a mobɛnya animuonyam ne nkamfoɔ a ɛsɛ me, mo Awurade Yesu Kristo no nyinaa. Nokorɛm, moanya mo akatua, na mɛyi mo akatua no na mede ama obi a na ɔyɛ nokorɛ wɔ mo ananmu. Na morenkasa mma me mpo bio, na ɛsɛ sɛ meba m’Asɔre no so sɛ nne a ɛteam wɔ serɛ so, na moato apono no mu wɔ m’anim, ɛwom sɛ megyina hɔ rebɔ mu deɛ! Aniwuo ne animtiaabu kɛseɛ da mo so gye sɛ mosakra!

Sɛ moanni me nsɛm so na moantie me nne, sɛ mokɔ so wɔ mo akwan a ɛyɛ nwunu no so a, mɛgya mo ama Yakob amanehunu; na meka kyerɛɛ mo kane, me Laodikeafoɔ, mɛfe mo afiri m’anomu gye sɛ mosakra! Na mesan kaa bio faa odiyifoɔ kunafoɔ Isebel ho berɛ a mekyerɛw kɔmaa Tiatira no, na ɛdeɛn na meka kyerɛɛ Yohane Adiyifoɔ no?

Nanso, mewɔ nnoɔma kakra bi tia wo, ɛfiri sɛ woama ɔbaa Isebel, a ɔfrɛ ne ho Odiyifoɔ no kwan, sɛ ɔnkyerɛkyerɛ na ɔnnaadaa m’asomfoɔ ma wɔnni aguamammɔ, na wɔnni nnoɔma a wɔde abɔ afɔdeɛ ama abosom. Na memaa no berɛ sɛ ɔnsakra mfiri n’aguamammɔ no mu, na wansakra. Hwɛ, mɛto no ahyɛ mpa mu, ne wɔn a wɔne no di awaresɛeɛ no, akɔ amanehunu kɛseɛ mu, gye sɛ wɔsakra wɔn nneyɛɛ.

Mo nkenkanee saa nsɛm yi? Moyɛ ahomasoɔ araa ma modwene sɛ Yahweh anaa Yeshua HaMashiach bu nnipa animhwɛ? Modwene sɛ mebu abodin a mode ma mo ho de di me nkurɔfoɔ so, berɛ a mode mo ho di Onyankopɔn agyapadeɛ so no?

Ɔsɔfoɔ kɛseɛ, Ɔsɔfoɔ panin; sɛ moanni m’akyɛdeɛ no annyɛ adwuma a, anka mo akyɛdeɛ no bɛma mo anya kwan wɔ nnipa akɛseɛ anim na anka morenhia mo abodin huhuo no, ɛfiri sɛ anka mɛhyɛ mo animuonyam na mo nso moahyɛ me animuonyam, nanso moagu m’anim ase! Asɛm bi wɔ mo mu ampa, na me bo afu mo so, ɛfiri sɛ moadi nkoguo wɔ nnwuma a mede ahyɛ mo nsa no mu na mmom moayɛ asɔretiafoɔ, na moato m’adesuafoɔ nketewa no mpo agu amena a moasi mo ntomadan no mu. Mopɛ nsuo a ɛho nte a ɛwɔ mo amena mu no kyɛn me honhom kronkron nsuo kronkron a obiara a ɔbɛnom no, sukɔm renne no da bio no!

Mode nsuo a ɛho nte, a ɛyɛ fi ma m’adesuafoɔ nketewa no wɔ nsuo aduhwa a abubɔ mu, na me bo afu mo so! Mɛtu mo akatua afiri mo nsam na mede ama ɔfoforɔ, ɛfiri sɛ sɛdeɛ maka afa mo ho no: Nokorɛm, moanya mo akatua. Moanya mo akatua a ɛyɛ mo ankasa animuonyam a mopɛ no, na moanya mo abodin a mfasoɔ nni so no akatua, na moanya sika fi akatua, na mobɛnya me boɔfuɔ akatua gye sɛ mosakra na mode ahobrɛaseɛ san ba me nkyɛn. Na me boɔfuɔ akatua ne abufuo, na sɛ mode mo nnua, wira, ne aburoo nnyina brɛ me a, ɛbɛhyew ayɛ nsõ wɔ nnipa nyinaa anim, na wɔbɛhu mo sɛ asomfoɔ atorofoɔ!

Asɛm bi wɔ mo nyinaa mu ampa, mo mpo, m’asomafoɔ, adiyifoɔ, asɛmpakafoɔ, asɔfoɔ, ne akyerɛkyerɛfoɔ! Monnkura mo ho mu bio wɔ nkwagyeɛ abusuabɔ mu ne me, Yesu Kristo, na mmom moagyae me mu; ɛfiri sɛ mene nipadua no ti, na ɛsɛ sɛ mokura me mu kyɛn biribiara! MORENTUMI nsom mma me sɛ monnim me nne mpo a. Moayɛ asotifoɔ, na mo aso ayɛ dɛm afebɔɔ gye sɛ mosakra, ɛfiri sɛ mɛsa mo aso yareɛ afiri nna honhom no mu sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a mobɛte me nne, ɛfiri sɛ merekasa akyerɛ mo! Gye sɛ mokura me mu sɛ mo ti na modi me Nsɛm ni, mɛgya mo anifirafoɔ ne wɔ amena no mu ne wɔn a wɔdii mo anim no. Moayɛ anifirafoɔ, na mo ani ayɛ dɛm afebɔɔ gye sɛ mosakra, ɛfiri sɛ anka mɛsa mo ani yareɛ afiri nna honhom no mu sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a mobɛtumi akenkan me nsɛm na moahunu m’anim: nanso nna kakra bio, nsa a wɔabɔ mu kakra bio.

Moayɛ ahoseresɛm ne animguaseɛ, ɛfiri sɛ mo su a ɛyɛ nwini no gu me din kronkron no ho fi. Ɛnyɛ sɛ monnkura me, Kristo Yesu, mu sɛ mo ti nko ara (ɛfiri sɛ sɛ moyɛɛ saa a, anka mobɛtumi akasa ama me), na mmom mokɔ akyiri wɔ asɔretia mu denam me nsɛm kronkron no a mogu ho fi no so, ɛfiri sɛ mene Asɛm a ɛyɛɛ ɔhonam no! Mebisa mo sɛ, monkenkanee me kɔkɔbɔ no anaa? Na medi adanseɛ kyerɛ onipa biara a ɔte nwoma yi nkɔmhyɛ nsɛm no, Sɛ obi de biribi ka yeinom ho a, Onyankopɔn de ɔhaw a wɔatwerɛ wɔ nwoma yi mu no bɛka ne deɛ ho. Na sɛ obi yiyi biribi firi nwoma yi nkɔmhyɛ nsɛm no mu a, Onyankopɔn bɛyi ne kyɛfa afiri Nkwa Nwoma no mu, ne kuropɔn kronkron no mu, ne nneɛma a wɔatwerɛ wɔ nwoma yi mu no mu. Moayɛ nkwaseafoɔ, asotifoɔ, ne anifirafoɔ araa ma monnte aseɛ sɛ memaa saa kɔkɔbɔ no ɛnyɛ me daa kyerɛ nwoma kɛseɛ, Adiyisɛm no nko ara nti! Na mmom saa kɔkɔbɔ no fa atwerɛsɛm kronkron no nyinaa ho! Na me Mmebusɛm no teatea mu wɔ mmɔnten so ma wɔn a wɔwɔ aso a wɔde te nyinaa: Onyankopɔn nsɛm nyinaa yɛ kronkron na ɛho te; na ɔyɛ kyɛm ma wɔn a wɔde wɔn ho to ne so nyinaa. Enti mfa hwee nka ne nsɛm ho, na wammɔ wo soboɔ na woanyɛ ɔtorofoɔ.

Me Laodikea asomfoɔ, a mo ne wiase ayɛ adɔfoɔ. Me Laodikea asomfoɔ, a modi Onyankopɔn agyapadeɛ so hene, na mokyerɛ sɛ moyɛ nnipa akɛseɛ bi. Me Laodikea asomfoɔ, a moabu mo ani agu me honhom a mabɔ wɔ mo mu no so. Me Laodikea asomfoɔ, a mode abodin huhuo ne nsaano ka mo din ho. Me Laodikea asomfoɔ: Montie me nsɛm na mommua me, na tɛkrɛma biara bɛka!

Hwan na ɔmaa mo kwan sɛ monsesa me nsɛm, Awurade Asɛm a mede ama me nkurɔfoɔ no mpo? Mo akwan, o mo Laodikea asomfoɔ akwadworɔfoɔ, nyɛ foforɔ, ɛfiri sɛ ɛhɔ ara a wɔkyekyɛɛ me nkrataa no maa m’Asafo ahyeaseɛ a wɔatwerɛ wɔ Asomafoɔ Nnwuma ne mmeaeɛ foforɔ no, Gnostic akuo a Gnostic adiyifoɔ atorofoɔ di wɔn anim no sesaa me nsɛm na wɔsan twerɛɛ kyerɛwsɛm, ɛfiri sɛ wɔfirii m’Asafo no mu na obiara ahunu sɛ wɔyɛ nguanhwɛfoɔ asɔretiafoɔ!

Mobɛtwa wɔn akwan so aba, ɛfiri sɛ mo ne wɔn abɔ mu, na mote wɔn akwan ase, na moda honhom fi a ɛfiri Yesebel, odiyifoɔ kunafoɔ a mofrɛ no kronkron no adi! Mehu mo nnwuma bɔne no, na wɔatwerɛ agu soro wɔ nsɛm pefee mu atia mo! Ɛsɛ sɛ moka nsɛm a mede ma mo no, na ɛsɛ sɛ moka no nokorɛdie mu, pɛpɛɛpɛ, a monsesa nkɔmhyɛ biara mu asɛm baako mpo! Menkasa ntia me ho, ɛfiri sɛ maka akyerɛ mo sɛ: Sua adeɛ na da wo ho adi sɛ obi a Onyankopɔn apene no so, ɔdwumayɛni a ɛnsɛ sɛ n’ani wu, a ɔkyekyɛ nokorɛ asɛm no mu pɛpɛɛpɛ. Wɔasesa nsɛm pii wɔ me ntwereɛ no mu mfeɛ pii ni, na sɛ mobɛsua na moadi me nsɛm a mede ama no ni a, mɛdi mo anim akɔ nokorɛ nyinaa mu, ɛfiri sɛ saa na mede ɔwerɛkyekyefoɔ no ama.

Mɛkyerɛ mo, sɛ mobɛtie nne pii a magya ama mo no a. Monni ntwereɛ a mede maeɛ wɔ Antiokia no akyi, ɛfiri sɛ ɛyɛ adansefoɔ pii nne, na m’adansefoɔ nni atorɔ na wɔne wɔn ho nni abirabɔ. Monhwɛ Textus Receptus no na monntwe mo ho mfiri Twerɛ Kronkron a ɛyɛ asɔretiafoɔ deɛ no ho: sua adeɛ na woate aseɛ! Nanso mo, me Laodikea asomfoɔ, monni me nsɛm ni, na mosesa me nsɛm de di mo atirimpɔ bɔne ho dwuma, mokyerɛkyerɛ me nkurɔfoɔ sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a wɔbɛkɔ so ayɛ bɔne, na mpo modi wɔn anim kɔ mo akwan fi no mu kɔ akyiri! Asɛm bi wɔ mo mu ampa, na monni m’akwan so, ɛfiri sɛ moyɛ ahomasoɔfoɔ ne akoma denfoɔ, na mɛgya mo ama ɔhonam sɛeɛ gye sɛ mosakra na mosesa mo akwan. Na me, Yesu Kristo, na masoma saa nsɛm yi! Monnbu mo ani nngu so, na monnu mo ho wɔ ayitoma ne nsõ mu. Monnu mo ho baguam mma mo asafo no na monka mo bɔne nkyerɛ wɔn a modii wɔn anim hweteɛ no, na mɛyi mo kaneadua no afiri mo nkyɛn. Monnu mo ho na monnanteɛ, na mede ogya foforɔ a wɔasɔ bɛsan de mo kaneadua no ama mo, ɛfiri sɛ me na mɛsɔ me honhom gya no wɔ mo kra mu.

Monsan nkɔ mo ɔdɔ a ɛdi kan no so, ɛfiri sɛ modɔ me, nanso ɛnyɛ nimdeɛ so. Monnko ntia me nsɛm, na mereba ntɛm kyɛn sɛdeɛ mosusu, na meba sɛ owifoɔ anadwo. Mo mfomsoɔ mu mpo, mosusu sɛ owifoɔ anadwo yɛ obi a ɔforo mfɛnsere mu te sɛ ɔdebɔneyɛfoɔ. O nkwasea! O, sɛ anka mote Hebrifoɔ no amanneɛ ase a. Owifoɔ anadwo no ne ɔsɔfoɔ panin a ɔbaa a wammɔ amanneɛ sɛ ɔrebɛhwɛ sɛ n’asɔfoɔ no di nokorɛ wɔ wɔn asɔredan mu nnwuma mu anaa, ɛfiri sɛ nnwuma bi kɔ so anadwo kɔsi sɛ wɔbɛwie saa nnwuma no sɛdeɛ mmara kyerɛ. Sɛ ɔsɔfoɔ panin no hu ɔsɔfoɔ bi a ɔwɔ n’ase sɛ wada berɛ a ɔreyɛ adwuma a, ɔbɛto gya hyehyeɛ bi agu n’atadeɛ mu, na ɔsɔfoɔ no awoso n’atadeɛ a ɛredɛre no agu na watu mmirika afiri asɔredan no mu adagya ne aniwu so. Monnna berɛ a mereba no, anyɛ saa a mobɛhwe ase wɔ m’anim adagya ne aniwu so wɔ aniwu a ɛyɛ hu paa ara mu, na m’ani bɛwu mo ho na mayi akatua nyinaa afiri mo so. Me nteɛsoɔ wɔ mo so na me nsa yɛ duru tia mo, na gye sɛ mosakra, mobɛnya amanehunu kɛseɛ paa a anka mobɛtumi aguan afiri ho. Monnu mo ho, na matumi asiesie baabi ama mo wɔ me pono so, na mo ne m’Ayeforɔ no abɛka me, Kristo Yesu, Ayeforɔkunu no, ho wɔ awareɛ mu.

Mene Asɛm a ɛyɛɛ ɔhonam a mete mo mu no.

Mene oguanhwɛfoɔ pa no na me nguan te me nne.

Mene Onyankopɔn Aduanetenenee a wɔsomaa no maa wiase bɔne no.

Mene ɔsɔfoɔ panin daa daa sɛdeɛ Melkisedek nhyehyɛeɛ teɛ.

Mereba na m’akatua ka me ho.

Mene nea ɔnam kaneadua nson ne abɔfoɔ nson no mu no.

Mene odiyifoɔ a wɔmaa no so firii anuanom no mu a Mose kaa ne ho asɛm no: Montie me nne wɔ osetie mu.

Mene nea ɔde akyɛdeɛ ama nnipa no, enti monnbu akyɛdeɛ a mede ama no animtia.

Mereba ntɛm kyɛn sɛdeɛ mosusu; monsiesie mo ho, m’Ayeforɔ!

Me, Awurade Yesu Kristo, na masoma saa nsɛm yi na mayɛ ɔteateamfoɔ nne wɔ serɛ so; na me Laodikea Asafo no yɛ nwini na ɛrenkyɛ na wɔbɛyɛ adagya ne aniwu gye sɛ wɔnu wɔn ho. Megyina ɔpono no ano rebɔ mu kakra bio! Nnɔnma biara nni dɔn no so bio, na nnɔnma no ahwehwe ase; na nwea biara nni hɔ a wɔbɛhunu bio berɛ a nwea a ɛtwa toɔ no bɛsene afa dɔn-nwea no mu, na ɛrebɛtɔ.
