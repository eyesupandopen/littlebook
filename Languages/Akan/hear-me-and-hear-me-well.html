Adansefoɔ Mmienu: Asɔre no
Wɔ Ɔsomafoɔ Paulo Nnwoma mu
May 01, 2024
Aprannaa 7

Montie Me na Montie Me Yie!

Montie me na montie me yie, mo adiyifoɔ atorofoɔ a mopɛ sɛ mosɛe me Asɛm kronkron no, ɛfiri sɛ momfiri me, na monni me honhom kronkron no, na moato me Yere no so ahyɛ, me Asɔre a mafrɛ no no! Tie na fa me kɔkɔbɔ no yɛ adwuma, oo, ɔman a wonni gyidie na woakyea na woasɛe, ɛfiri sɛ mobɛbu akonta wɔ mo amumuyɛsɛm biara ho! Montie me kɔkɔbɔ nsɛm no, mo a mokyekyere me Asɔre no kwan no, ɛfiri sɛ m’abufuo ano ayɛ hye tia mo, ɛfiri sɛ mene Onyankopɔn Odwammaa no! Na mo mu bebree bɛhunta wɔ abodan mu, ne mmepɔ so abotan mu. Na mobɛka akyerɛ mmepɔ ne abotan no sɛ, montɔ nguu yɛn so, na monhunta yɛn mfiri nea ɔte ahennwa no so no anim, ne Odwammaa no abufuo no anim: ɛfiri sɛ n’abufuo da kɛseɛ no adu, na hwan na ɔbɛtumi agyina?

Na wiase bɛhunu me pue a mebɛpue no, na owosoɔ kɛseɛ bɛba nnipa nyinaa so, na ɔsɛeɛ a ɛyɛ mpofirim bɛba asase so berɛ a wɔbɛfa me Yere no akɔ ahobammɔ mu. Na mɛyi nea ɔsi kwan no afiri hɔ, na bɔne nyinaa bɛnya ahofadie koraa, na atemmuo kɛseɛ bɛfiri soro aba fam, sɛdeɛ wɔatwerɛ no wɔ me nnwoma kɛseɛ a ɛyi nneɛma adi no mu, anaa Adiyisɛm nwoma no mu! Enti mede atemmuo reto mo so, mo a mobɛgu me Asɔre no ho fi nyinaa: mo nna ahyia, na ɛnkyɛ na mo akoma bɛtu berɛ a wɔbɛyi m’abufuo no adi. Mo adiyifoɔ atorofoɔ a monni mfasoɔ a moawura me Asɔre no mu, moakyerɛkyerɛ abayisɛm ne ahintasɛm a wɔde afrafra me Asɛm kronkron ne m’akwan kronkron no, na mobɛtua so ka.

Moama me mma nketewa no ayɛ bɔne na wɔde wɔn ahyɛ amanehunu kɛseɛ mu, a mɛsan de aba mo so ɔha wɔ m’atemmuo a mede bɛba mo so no mu. Moasesa nsɛmfua nkyerɛaseɛ wɔ me twerɛsɛm kronkron no mu, moayɛ me twerɛsɛm kronkron no atoro denam nsɛm no a moasan atwerɛ na moasesa Daapem Nyankopɔn, anaa Yahweh nsɛm no, na mobɛtua so ka daa. Moade abɛda nsɛm a asɛe aba me Asɔre no mu sɛdeɛ mo agyanom a wɔnni mfasoɔ no boroo abura na wɔboroo aduane a wɔde maa adesamma no, na mobɛtua so ka daa. Asɛm hunu biara a moaka, sika fi biara a moaboaboa ano de resɛe mo ho, adwemmɔne biara a mo adwene mu apue pɛn, ɔpɛ bɔne biara a moadi akyi pɛn no, wɔde bɛba mo anim, na mobɛtua so ka, kɔpem sika ketewaa a ɛtwa toɔ no; na morentumi nnyina m’anim koraa, na mobɛkɔ mo fa wɔ ogya a m’Agya asiesie ama ɔbonsam ne n’abɔfoɔ no mu.

Nanso monhunu sɛ monyɛ anwanwadeɛ na anwanwasɛm biara nni mo mu, ɛfiri sɛ moyɛ asomafoɔ atorofoɔ, adwumayɛfoɔ nnaadaafoɔ, a mosakra mo ho yɛ mo ho Kristo asomafoɔ. Na ɛnyɛ nwanwa, ɛfiri sɛ Satan ankasa sakra ne ho yɛ hann bɔfoɔ. Enti, ɛnyɛ ade kɛseɛ sɛ n’asomfoɔ nso sakra wɔn ho sɛ tenenee asomfoɔ a wɔn awieeɛ bɛyɛ sɛdeɛ wɔn nnwuma teɛ. Na saa na metwerɛ kɔmaa Korintofoɔ!

Mobɛkɔ so ayɛ mo nnwuma de ahyɛ mo bɔne ma ayɛ ma na moahyɛ daapem ma ayɛ ma wɔ mo ankasa asotwe ne ɔhaw mu, ɛfiri sɛ m’abufuo ano yɛ den tia mo, na mobɛkɔka mo agyanom ho wɔ ogya no mu. Na moyɛ nwura frafra ayuo mu, na mɛtu mo ase koraa; ɛfiri sɛ m’Agya, anaa Yahweh, aka sɛ morennya ntini anaa agyapadeɛ da, ɛfiri sɛ mo ase firi ɔbɔnefoɔ no, na mo nyinaa moyɛ awudifoɔ ne atorofoɔ. Na ɛdɛn na meka kyerɛɛ mo agyanom a wɔwɔ Yudea mfeɛ pii a atwam ni? Mo firi mo agya ɔbonsam, na mo agya no akɔnnɔ na mopɛ sɛ moyɛ: ɔyɛɛ owudifoɔ firi mfitiaseɛ, na wannnyina nokorɛ no mu, ɛfiri sɛ nokorɛ biara nni ne mu. Sɛ ɔka atorɔsɛm a, ɔfiri ne ankasa mu na ɔka: ɛfiri sɛ ɔyɛ ɔtorofoɔ, ne ne papa.

Monhunu yei, mo a wɔawo mo nyinaa wɔ abususɛm mu, anaa abususɛm a wɔsɛee no tiaa Yahweh sɛdeɛ mo agyanom a wɔnni mfasoɔ no bɔɔ mmɔden sɛ wɔbɛsakra Onyankopɔn suban, a wɔbɔɔ Adam wɔ mu no, ayɛ no akyiwadeɛ a wɔyɛ maa adesamma sɛeɛ, mo nna reyɛ asa. Na adiyifoɔ no mu hwan na mo agyanom antaa no? Na wɔakunkum wɔn a wɔkaa Onipa Tenenee no ba a ɔbɛba no ho asɛm too hɔ; nea seesei moayɛ no amammɔfoɔ ne awudifoɔ. Morennya kwan nwan, ɛmfa ho baabi a mobɛkɔ asase mu akɔhunta afiri m’abufuo no anim; m’abufuo bɛhunu mo, ɛfiri sɛ biribiara rentumi nhunta mfiri m’anim, na asase yɛ me dea ne ne mayɛ, ɛfiri sɛ mede me mogya a mo agyanom hwie guuɛ abususɛm so no na ɛtɔɔ, de sii mo ankasa nkrabea pi wɔ ogya no mu. Me, Kristo Yesu, nea wɔama no so na wɔahyɛ no animuonyam no, me ara na mereba mo so, na morennya kwan nwan.

Montie me, oo, mo a moahwe ase no, anaa mo nyinaa a mowɔɔ me no, ɛfiri sɛ mokae wɔ mo tirim sɛ, yɛadi Yahweh so nkonim na yɛakum ne ba a ɔsomaa no baa yɛn so sɛ ɔmmɛbrɛ asase ase no, na yɛadi nkonim daa, ɛfiri sɛ Yeshua awu, na mo ani gyee wɔ nkwaseasɛm mu! Mo ahomasoɔfoɔ a monni nteaseɛ! Moante aseɛ sɛ Onyankopɔn bɛnyane me, asan owuo mu mpokyere no: ɛfiri sɛ na ɛntumi nyɛ yie sɛ ɛbɛkura me. Moapira me nantin nkutoo, na monni tumi biara wɔ me so, na moante aseɛ sɛ ɔsorosoroni no, anaa mo bɔfoɔ, Yahweh, kyerɛɛ mo ahintasɛm a ɛnni mfasoɔ no nkutoo!

Na ɔnim sɛ mobɛdane atia no ne n’abɔdeɛ na mobɛbɔ mmɔden sɛ mobɛfa deɛ wasiesie ama me, Awurade Yesu Kristo no. Me brɛfoɔ na wɔbɛnya asase no adi, ɛfiri sɛ asase yɛ me dea ne ne mayɛ. Na moakyerɛkyerɛ adesamma nimdeɛ a wɔabara; moakyerɛkyerɛ ɔko; moadaadaa mmaa wɔ wɔn aniwa a wɔde sika kɔkɔɔ siesie de di wɔn akɔnnɔ wɔ wɔn so; moasɛe Onyankopɔn Turo no denam nwura a moduaa wɔ ayuo no mu, na nwura biara a moduaeɛ no, wɔbɛtu ase, akyekyere, na wɔde mo ahyɛ ogya no mu! Na wɔdii mo so nkonim berɛ a mede me ho maeɛ kwa maa adesamma nkwagyeɛ, na Onyankopɔn nyanee me firii awufoɔ mu sɛ menwu bio!

Nanso na monni nyansa sɛ mobɛhunu sɛ mɛde me nkwa ato hɔ ɛnam Onyankopɔn dɔ a ɔbɔɔ wɔ me mu no nti, ɛfiri sɛ mene wiase Agyenkwa no! Wɔrentumi nni Yahweh dɔ so nkonim, ɛfiri sɛ wɔrentumi nni Yahweh so nkonim, na abɔdeɛ biara nni hɔ a ɔte sɛ no, ɛfiri sɛ ɔne ɔsorosoroni no, na n’animuonyam ne ne kɛseyɛ yɛ tumi nyinaa firi daapem kɔsi daapem! Na meka kyerɛɛ mo sɛ, "yei a woyɛ yi yɛ no ntɛm!" Moantumi ante aseɛ wɔ mo nkwaseasɛm ne ahomasoɔ mu sɛ mepenee so sɛ mo apaafoɔ a wɔnni mfasoɔ a wɔyɛɛ adwumaden a ɛne mogya a ɛnni fɔ hwieguo no bɛfa me! Wɔ mo ahomasoɔ nkwaseasɛm mu no, moanhunu sɛ mewɔ abɔfoɔ dɔm dumienu a wɔhyɛ m’ase, na berɛ biara na metumi asi mo kwan. Nanso ɛbɛyɛ dɛn na Yahweh pɛ ayɛ hɔ, gye sɛ ɛnam me dɔ ne osetie a meyɛ ma m’Agya no so? Na obiara rentumi nni Yahweh dɔ so nkonim! Dabi, ɛnyɛ ɔsoro na ɛnyɛ asase so, ɛnyɛ ɔkwan biara so na ɛnyɛ da biara da!

Mo a moahwe ase no, anaa mo wiase a ahwe ase yi mu ahemfo, mo Satanfoɔ, mo nyinaa bɛhyew wɔ ogya a ɛnnume da mu ɛnam mo ɔtan a ɛnyɛ kronkron ne mo osetie a monni ne ɔsɛeɛ akwan a ɛyɛ bɔne kɛseɛ no nti. Sɛdeɛ moyɛɛ no maa me nuanom yi mu akumaa no mu baako no, moyɛɛ no maa me. Oo, mo awɛmfoɔ a moahwe ase, anaa mo abɔfoɔ a moanyɛ osetie a mofirii mo tenabea a ɛdi kan no, monni asomdwoeɛ! Wɔbɛto mo ahyɛ ogya a morenfiri mu da no mu, na mobɛhyew koraa. Mo akyiwadeɛ nwura a ɛnyɛ kronkron a moduaeɛ denam Yahweh hyeɛ a motwaeɛ wɔ mo awosuo mu nsakraeɛ a ɛda onipa ne ɔbɔfoɔ ntam, onipa ne aboa ntam, onipa ne ɛpo mu abɔdeɛ ntam, mmoa ahodoɔ ntam, anaa akyiwadeɛ biara a moayɛ no, wɔde mo bɛto ogya no mu, ɛfiri sɛ monni asomdwoeɛ anaa bɔnefafirie!

Moadi Satanfoɔ a wɔbɔɔ mmɔden sɛ wɔbɛsɛe Yahweh abɔdeɛ no akyi, kɔpem sɛ wɔkumm me, Yesu Kristo, berɛ a mede me ho hyɛɛ ne nsa mu sɛ Twam a ɛyɛ pɛ ma wiase, na ɛnam so de adesamma nkwagyeɛ sii pi na ɛde mo nkrabea sii pi wɔ ogya kɛseɛ a ɛnnume da no mu! Mobɛtua mo bɔne ne akyiwadeɛ nyinaa so ka koraa, na amumuyɛsɛm biara a moayɛ no bɛsan aba mo so ɔha, ɛfiri sɛ wɔbɛtua obiara ka sɛdeɛ ne nnwuma teɛ. Na mo amumuyɛsɛm nyinaa bɛsan aba mo ankasa ti so mpɛn pii, ɛfiri sɛ modwenee sɛ mobɛtumi adi me so nkonim, na mo da so ara dwene wɔ ahomasoɔ mu sɛ mobɛtumi adi nkonim berɛ a wɔadi mo so nkonim koraa.

Montie me nsɛm, oo, ɔwɔ dadaa, na w’atemmuo aba wo so na mehunu wo suro, ɛfiri sɛ wosuro wo daakye kyɛn biribiara, oo, wo gyata a wonni se a wonni mfasoɔ! Aweredie yɛ me dea, na mɛtua ka, na mo berɛ adu. Onyankopɔn abufuo ne odwammaa no abufuo reyɛ aba berɛ a mɛbu nsɔano nson no, berɛ a wɔbɛsoma abɔfoɔ nson sɛ wɔnhyɛn totorobɛnto atemmuo no, na wɔasoma abɔfoɔ nson a wɔkura ɔhaw nson a ɛtwa toɔ no, na wɔbɛyi wɔn adi, ɛfiri sɛ wɔn mu na Onyankopɔn abufuo ahyɛ ma. Nnoboa pii bɛba mo nyinaa so, berɛ a mɛtwe mo aso koraa na masɛe mo ahennie no. Mɛdi mo hene kɛseɛ no so nkonim na mɛdi mo odiyifoɔ atorofoɔ no so nkonim, na wɔbɛto wɔn a wɔte aseɛ agu ogya tadeɛ a ɛne sufre dɛre no mu; na ɛhɔ na wɔbɛtwɛn ama mo abɛka wɔn ho, na ɛhɔ na mobɛhyɛ ogya ne sufre mu daa, na mo atemmuo reyɛ afi ase!

Me Yere, nsuro! M’atemmuo nsɛm no nnyɛ ma wo na mmom ɛyɛ ma wo awerɛkyekyerɛ, ɛfiri sɛ magye wo afiri abufuo a ɛreyɛ aba no mu, na mɛhunta wo wɔ ahobammɔ ne akatua kɛseɛ mu, na wo ne me bɛtena daa. Na mapaw wo, na mafrɛ wo, na woyɛ m’agyapadeɛ a ɛsom bo paa, na woyɛ Yahweh agyapadeɛ a ɛsom bo paa; woyɛ aboɔden abo a ɛsom bo paa no, ɛfiri sɛ mede me biribiara ama wo. Metu me Yere fo sɛ ɔnhwehwɛ nhunumu na ɔmmra me nkyɛn mmɛgye nyansa, ɛfiri sɛ mɛkyerɛkyerɛ wo. Na mo mu binom adi adiyifoɔ atorofoɔ yi akwan no bi akyi; monsan nko ara nnu mo ho nkyerɛ mo Awurade, na mɛgye mo.

Na wɔde Kristofoɔ abayisɛm adaadaa mo, na moagye atorɔsɛm pii a wɔatɔn ama mo adi, a wɔaka akyerɛ mo sɛ ɛyɛ nokorɛ, nanso ɛyɛ atorɔsɛm a ɛyɛ akyiwadeɛ. Abura a nsuo nni mu yi abɔ mmɔden sɛ wɔbɛtwe mo afiri me ho, na ɛno rentumi nyɛ yie, enti nsuro, ɛfiri sɛ wɔrempow me! Mɛkɔ so agye mo ama moanya ahofadie ampa, na wɔremma me komm! Mekɔ ma wo wɔ akwan pii so a wonnim. Na wɔhyɛɛ mo nyinaa agyiraeɛ ansa na wɔrewo mo, ɛfiri sɛ Agya no nim mo wɔ mo maame yafunu mu mpo, na ɔtwɛn mo mu biara kyɛɛ a morentumi nte aseɛ, na moayɛ n’akoma so adeɛ daa. Wɔhyɛɛ mo agyiraeɛ berɛ a wɔwoo mo, na m’abɔfoɔ hwɛɛ mo so wɔ m’ahyɛdeɛ so na wɔabɔ mo ho ban na wɔahwɛ mo so, na saa na wɔsoma wɔn. Yei yɛ wo nwanwa anaa? Wobɛtie deɛ Hebrifoɔ no bɛka? Nanso abɔfoɔ no mu hwan na ɔka kyerɛɛ no pɛn sɛ, tena me nifa so, kɔsi sɛ mede w’atamfo bɛyɛ wo nan ntiasoɔ? Wɔn nyinaa nyɛ ahonhom a wɔsom, a wɔsoma wɔn sɛ wɔnkɔsom wɔn a wɔbɛnya nkwagyeɛ no anaa?

Me Yere, moyɛ nkwagyeɛ adedifoɔ wɔ me, Yesu Kristo mu. Wɔafrɛ mo wɔ me mu; wɔabɔ mo ho ban wɔ me mu na ɛnam me so; wɔama mo hokwan sɛ mobɛtie gyidie ho asɛnka a ɛfa me ho sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a mobɛtumi ayɛ me mo Awurade, na ɛno nti mede honhom kronkron a ɛyɛ bɔhyɛ no asɔ mo ano. Monhunu sɛ wɔrentumi mmu nsɔano no, na wɔreyɛ agye mo koraa akɔ daapem, ɛfiri sɛ monnim adebɔ adwuma a ɛyɛ ma a mereyɛ awie wɔ mo mu no, me Yere! Monhunu sɛ wɔn a wɔahwe ase ne wɔn nwura no ani abere mo, na wɔkyiri deɛ Yahweh dɔ biara, na mɛtɔ mo so awerɛ koraa wɔ wɔn ti so, ɛfiri sɛ mene nea ɔbɛpira ɔwɔ dadaa no ti, na wɔaka, na deɛ Yahweh ka no, wɔrentumi nsakra no.

Obiara rentumi ntetew mo ne me ntam; bɔne biara rentumi mmu saa nsɔano no; moyɛ me dea na moyɛ Agya no dea, na deɛ Agya no de ma me nyinaa mɛsan de ama Onyankopɔn sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a yɛbɛyɛ adeɛ nyinaa wɔ adeɛ nyinaa mu! Na ɔde nneɛma nyinaa a ɛwɔ soro ne asase so ahyɛ me nsa, gye sɛ ɔwɔ n’ahennwa so no ɔso sene me!

Na me, Kristo Yesu, meka saa nsɛm yi kyerɛ mo, na ɛyɛ me, Yesu Kristo, na mede atemmuo to mo atamfo so, na mɛma ayɛ hɔ kɔpem sika ketewaa a ɛtwa toɔ no. Me Yere, me dɔ a medɔ wo no nnume da, na masiesie baabi ama wo, na m’Agya fie adan pii wɔ hɔ! Enti ɛnkyɛ koraa na wo ne me bɛware daa, na saa na wo ne me bɛtena daa, na awieeɛ koraa no wobɛte wo botaeɛ kɛseyɛ a ɛwɔ me mu a wɔabɔ wo sɛ di ho dwuma no ase!

Na wɔ me mu no, Yahweh ama wo daakye ne botaeɛ, anaa mmerɛ no botaeɛ no reyɛ ayi adi akyerɛ wo, ɛfiri sɛ mereba! Na wɔrempow me. Monhwɛ me pue a mɛpue no kwan. Monhwɛ me pue a mɛpue no hann kwan, ɛfiri sɛ ɛreyɛ adane wɔ asase so berɛ a mɛpue wɔ mununkum mu a meteɛm dennen, a ɔbɔfo panyin no nne ne Onyankopɔn totorobɛnto ka ho, na mereka nokorɛ akyerɛ mo sɛ abɔfoɔ no mpo twɛn saa totorobɛnto a ɛreyɛ ahyɛn no, na wɔasiesie wɔn ho! Monsiesie mo ho mma me na monhwɛ, monwɛn, monhwɛ sɛdeɛ wɔahyɛ no; na ɛnkyɛ na mobɛhunu me ne m’animuonyam a Agya no de ahyɛ me no, na saa na mɛhyɛ mo, na mobɛyɛ sɛ me, na mobɛhunu me sɛdeɛ meteɛ.

Mene Onyankopɔn Odwammaa no!

Mereba abɛtwe Satan ahennie nyinaa aso, na wɔrennya kwan nwan.

Mene Wiase Agyenkwa no!

Mene Kristo Yesu na aweredie yɛ me dea na mɛtua ka!

Mene nea ɔbɛpira ɔwɔ no ti, na Onyankopɔn Tumfoɔ no aka! Mereyɛ apue wɔ mununkum mu ama me Yere, a mɛfa no akɔ.

Mene nea ɔte ɔpɔnkɔ fitaa so no.

Mene nea wɔfrɛ no Nokwafoɔ ne Nokorɛ, na tenenee mu na mɛbu atɛn na mako.

Mereba!
