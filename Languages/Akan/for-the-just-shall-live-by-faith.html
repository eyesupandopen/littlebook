Adanseɛ Mmienu: Asɔre no
Wɔ Ɔsomafoɔ Paulo Nnwoma mu
Oforisuo 27, 2024

Na ɔteneneeni nam gyidie so bɛtena ase!

Na ɔteneneeni nam gyidie so bɛtena ase! Nyan, na nyan wo nuanom, m’asɔre a ɛyɛ nwini a ɛwɔ Laodikea, a wo werɛ afi me ahintasɛm no; na dɔn no abɔ akyiri na ɛberɛ no reyɛ aso. Me mma, momfa nsa a abue ne akoma a abue mmra me nkyɛn (na me nsa ne m’akoma abue ama mo); momfa akoma a ɛyɛ mmerɛ na ɛsɔ me nsɛm no so mmra me nkyɛn. Na ɛdeɛn na me nua Yakobo kaa? Enti monto efi ne bɔne a ɛboro soɔ nyinaa ngu, na momfa ahobrɛaseɛ nnye asɛm a wɔadua no, a ɛtumi gye mo kra no. Me mma, monni m’akyi, na meyɛ odwofoɔ na mebrɛ me ho ase akoma mu, na momfa ahobrɛaseɛ nnye Asɛm a wɔadua no, a mede ato mo mfinimfini sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a mobɛnya aniwa ahunu na moanya aso ate. Mondane mo akoma ma me, na mene ɔkwan ne nokorɛ ne nkwa no, na obiara mma Agya no nkyɛn gye sɛ ɔnam me so, na saa na m’Agya pɛ da adi pefee, na wayɛ no saa.

Adɛn nti na mohwanyan mo akoma, me mma? Adɛn nti na modane mo ho firi me nsɛm ho na mode mo mfomsoɔ di afoforɔ anim kɔ ɔman nkyɛn? Na mo, m’asɔre a ɛwɔ Laodikea, mo werɛ afi onipa ko a moyɛ wɔ me, Kristo Yesu mu, na mo ahwere me ahintasɛm no, ɛwom sɛ mede hyɛɛ mo nsa deɛ. Na mene Awurade a mede me mogya tɔɔ mo no; mene Awurade mu Awurade ne Ahemfo mu Hene a m’Agya de asase ama me no, na mede m’agyapadeɛ bɛkyɛ wɔn a wɔdi nokorɛ ne wɔn a wɔayi wɔn no; mede m’agyapadeɛ bɛkyɛ mo, m’ayeforɔ! Enti monnsuro, na ɛwom sɛ moyɛ ahiafoɔ deɛ, nanso moyɛ adefoɔ wɔ me mu, na Agya no de nnoɔma nyinaa ahyɛ me nsa. Ao, Laodikea asɔre, woayɛ ɔbaa a ɔnni gyidie a ɔpene biribiara so.

Daabi, mo ani nnye me nsɛm a emu dɔ no ho, na momfa mo akoma nyinaa nni m’akyi nso; monkenkanee Abɛbuo no anaa? Nteaseɛ pa nya animuonyam, nanso wɔn a wɔnni gyidie no kwan yɛ den. Na akwan a mopene so a moafa so no yɛ den; mo akwan a enni gyidie no mfiri me, na mobɔ mmɔden sɛ mobɛfa ɔkwan a mede ama mo no so a nan baako wɔ ɔkwan no so na baako nso wɔ amena mu, nanso mowɔ akokoɔduru sɛ mobɛbɔ me, Yeshua HaMashiach, soboɔ, berɛ a moatɔ amena no mu sɛdeɛ wɔadi mo anim no. Na mo akwantuo no wɔ ahweaseɛ bebree, na anidasoɔ a mewɔ ma mo no ne sɛ mo ahweaseɛ no te sɛ ɔteneneeni ahweaseɛ: Na ɔteneneeni hwe ase mpɛn nson na ɔsɔre bio: nanso amumuyɔfoɔ bɛhwe amanehunu mu.

Memfrɛɛ mo sɛ monhwe amanehunu mu, me mma, mo mpo, m’asɔre a ɛwɔ Laodikea, na mennya ngyaa mo mmaa mmere a wɔde sɔ mo hwɛ no mu. Na hwɛ! Meda so gyina ɔpono no ano rebɔ mu, ɛfiri sɛ meremfa me ho nhyɛ mo akwan a ɛyɛ nwini na ɛpene biribiara so no mu koraa, na mmom meresrɛ mo sɛ monyɛ tenenee. Meresrɛ mo sɛ monnyina tenenee mu, na ahwɛfoɔ a wɔyɛ nnaadaafoɔ no adaadaa mo, de mo akɔ Bileam akwan so; na mo mu bebree hyɛ nkɔm hunu denam Yesebel ahonhom so, a wɔpɛ sɛ wɔsɛe me adiyifoɔ nokorɛfoɔ no nsɛm na wɔpɛ sɛ wɔkum adiyifoɔ a mate wɔn ho wɔ me mu no, na mene deɛ mede akyɛdeɛ ama nnipa. Na mekyerɛw kɔmaa Galatia berɛ a wɔhintiwiiɛ sɛdeɛ mohintiw no: Me ho dwiri me sɛ, moatwe mo ho afiri deɛ ɔnam Kristo adom so frɛɛ mo no ho ntɛm saa, akɔ Asɛmpa foforɔ bi so: A ɛnyɛ foforɔ. Aane, monkenkan no bio, na Asɛmpa foforɔ biara nni hɔ na din foforɔ biara nni hɔ a wɔde ama a ɛsɛ sɛ nnipa nam so nya nkwagyeɛ gye me deɛ, Yesu Kristo. Adɛn nti na wɔtwe mo firi me ho, me mma nketewa? Menhwɛ mo na menhwɛ mo yie anaa, na mene oguanhwɛfoɔ pa a ɔde ne nkwa too hɔ maa nnwan no. Nanso nsɛm a menkaeɛ, a mo ani gye ho na modi akyire no, ɛrepiri mo aso; na saa atenka no abɛyɛ mo ɔdɔ a ɛdi kan wɔ mo ahomasoɔ akwan so; na mode atosɛm sie ma mo ho te sɛ nea ɛyɛ nokorɛ, na mo ahwere nhunumu nyinaa.

Mo werɛ afi asɔre abakɔsɛm titire no anaa? Moayɛ akwadworɔ dodo sɛ mobɛsua? Asɛm yi mpiri mo aso sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a mobɛte me nsɛm na moagye adi anaa? Na onipa nte ase ɔpaanoo nko ara so na mmom asɛm biara a ɛfiri Onyankopɔn anomu! Wɔwoo m’asɔre wɔ Pentekoste da na wɔakyerɛw wɔ Asomafoɔ Nnwuma mu sɛ monhwɛ. Na m’asɔre hyɛɛ aseɛ wɔ Israel, Yudea, ɛwom sɛ wɔante nsɛm a mekaa no nam Petro so da a ɛyɛ animuonyam no a mehwiee honhom kronkron guu wɔn a mayi wɔn no so, berɛ a wɔkaa kasa foforɔ sɛdeɛ honhom no maa wɔn tumi no! Na ɛdeɛn na Petro ka kyerɛɛ dɔm a wɔwɔ Yerusalem no saa da no berɛ a ɔhyɛɛ nkɔm maa me no? Na bɔhyɛ no wɔ mo ne mo mma, ne wɔn a wɔwɔ akyirikyiri nyinaa, sɛdeɛ Awurade yɛn Nyankopɔn bɛfrɛ wɔn nyinaa. Me mma, moyɛ wɔn a wɔwɔ akyirikyiri no, na mo mu bebree nyɛ Israelni wɔ honam mu, nanso mede mo afiri amanaman mu wɔ m’adom mu sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a mobɛgyina m’ahummɔborɔ mu, na wɔafrɛ mo.

Monkenkanee anaa? Nanso seesei wɔ Kristo Yesu mu no, mo a na kane no mowɔ akyirikyiri no, wɔnam Kristo mogya so de mo abɛn. Nanso wɔ mo som afie a mopene biribiara so a moguan ho de hyira so wɔ me din mu no (ɛwom sɛ merenwura mu na mmom mɛgyina ɔpono no ano abɔ mu), mosusu sɛ moagye Mose mmara no afiri me hɔ, na wɔ mo ahomasoɔ mu no mo nte aseɛ sɛ na Mose firi Israel, na wɔde Torah maa Israel sɛ wɔnni so, adwuma a wɔantumi anyɛ; na onipa biara ntumi nni Mose mmara no so ɛnam ɔsɛeɛ a ɔwɔ no de baa mo honam mu nti, na ne nimdeɛ sɛe adeɛ daa, na bɔne akatua ne owuo. Na Korintofoɔ di me nsɛm ho adanseɛ: Ɔno nso na wayɛ yɛn asomfoɔ a yɛfata ama Apam Foforɔ no, ɛnyɛ nkyerɛwdeɛ no, na mmom honhom no: na nkyerɛwdeɛ no kum, na honhom no ma nkwa. Wɔafrɛ mo sɛ monantew honhom mu, ɛwom sɛ mo mu bebree pɛ mmara no nkyerɛwdeɛ a ɛmfata, a ɛnyɛ mo dea, na mmom ɛhyɛɛ mã wɔ me mu.

Mommue me sei: Honhom no nam mo ankasa Torah a modii so no so na ɛbaa mo nkyɛn? Anaasɛ ɛnam me tenenee a mede dii Torah so sɛ mo Twam a ɛyɛ pɛ no so? Na ɛdeɛn na Galatiafoɔ adanseɛ no su frɛ? Yei nko ara na anka mepɛ sɛ mesua firi mo hɔ, monyaa honhom no, ɛnam Mmara no nnwuma so, anaa ɛnam gyidie a moteeɛ no so? Na bio Paulo ka kyerɛɛ Galatiafoɔ no sɛ: Mempo Onyankopɔn adom no: na sɛ tenenee nam Mmara no so ba a, ɛnneɛ Kristo wuiɛ kwa. Mo nte aseɛ sɛ mmerɛ no mfiaseɛ no na Yudea asɔre ne Amanaman mu asɔre wɔ hɔ? Ɛwom sɛ wɔwoo m’asɔre wɔ Yudea deɛ, nanso Paulo ka kyerɛɛ Galatiafoɔ no na mekoraa so wɔ hɔ: Na deɛ ɔyɛɛ adwuma yie wɔ Petro mu maa twetiatwafoɔ Asomafoɔ adwuma no, ɔno ara na ɔyɛɛ den wɔ me mu kyerɛɛ Amanaman mufoɔ. Na nea ɛdi kan no mesomaa Petro kɔɔ Amanaman mufoɔ nkyɛn, na wɔakyerɛw wɔ Asomafoɔ Nnwuma mu berɛ a meka kyerɛɛ Petro sɛ: Deɛ Onyankopɔn ate ho no, mfrɛ no efie. Na berɛ a mesomaa Petro kɔɔ Kornelio nkyɛn no, wɔkyerɛw adanseɛ a ɛdidi soɔ yi: Na wɔn a wɔfiri twetiatwafoɔ mu a wogye diiɛ no ho dwirii wɔn, sɛdeɛ wɔn a wɔne Petro baeɛ nyinaa, ɛfiri sɛ wɔhwiee honhom kronkron akyɛdeɛ no guu Amanaman mufoɔ no so nso. Mebisa mo sei, me Laodikeafoɔ, Kornelio nyaa nkwagyeɛ ɛnam Torah a ɔdii akyire so (na ɔyɛ onipa a ɔsom Nyame)? Anaasɛ ɔnam gyidie so nyaa bɔhyɛ no? Korintofoɔ wɔ asɛm bi ka wɔ asɛm no ho berɛ a Paulo kyerɛw kɔmaa wɔn no: Ɛno nso so na wɔgye mo nkwa, sɛ mokura deɛ mekaa no kyerɛɛ mo no mu a, gye sɛ moagye adi kwa. Nkwagyeɛ nam gyidie a wɔkaa ho asɛm no so na ɛbaeɛ na ɛnyɛ mmara no nkyerɛwdeɛ so, na sɛ obi de ne ho bɛhyɛ Mose mmara ase a, ne gyidie mpo yɛ ɔkwa.

Na enti Romafoɔ de too dwa sɛ: Nanso seesei Onyankopɔn tenenee a Mmara nni mu no ada adi, a Mmara ne Adiyifoɔ no di ho adanseɛ. Onyankopɔn tenenee mpo, a ɛnam Yesu Kristo gyidie so ba ma nnipa nyinaa, ne wɔn a wogye di nyinaa so: na nsonsonoeɛ biara nni hɔ: Na nnipa nyinaa ayɛ bɔne, na wɔnni Onyankopɔn animuonyam. Na meka kyerɛɛ m’asɔre a ɛwɔ Galatia a Paulo de anigyeɛ kyerɛwee no sɛ: Na wɔ Yesu Kristo mu no, twetiatwa mfa mfasoɔ biara, na twetiatwa a wɔantwa nso mfa mfasoɔ biara, na mmom gyidie a ɛnam ɔdɔ so yɛ adwuma. Na bio Paulo kyerɛw kɔmaa Galatiafoɔ sɛ: Na wɔ Kristo Yesu mu no, twetiatwa mfa mfasoɔ biara na twetiatwa a wɔantwa nso mfa mfasoɔ biara, na mmom abɔdeɛ foforɔ. Enti wɔmfa mmara nnwuma so mma nkwagyeɛ, na wɔmfa mmara nnwuma so nso ntra ase na wɔnhunu mo nkwagyeɛ. Na wɔkyerɛw kɔmaa Romafoɔ sɛ: Hwɛ, mede hintibuɔ boɔ, ne ɔbotan a ɛsɛe adeɛ reto Sion: na obiara a ɔgye no di no, n’ani renwu. Me mma, mene hintibuɔ boɔ ne ɔbotan a ɛsɛe adeɛ no, na mene Yesu Nasareni no, Onyankopɔn guammaa no ankasa, na madi mmara no so.

Ao, mo a mofiri Laodikea, mosusu sɛ mo nko ara na moahintiw wɔ asɛm yi ho? Mese daabi. Na mobɛtumi akyekyɛ me nkrataa no mu akuo mmienu a ɛyɛ mmerɛ, gye sɛ wɔakyerɛw ne nyinaa ama onipa foforɔ baako wɔ Kristo Yesu mu. Na wɔakyerɛw me nkrataa no ama Amanaman mu asɔre ahodoɔ, na ebinom de kɔma Yudea asɔre ahodoɔ, na enti Petro kyerɛw faa Ahintasɛm no adiyie a meda no adi kyerɛɛ Paulo no ho: Sɛdeɛ ɛteɛ nso wɔ ne Nkrataa nyinaa mu, ɔkasa wɔ emu fa nnoɔma yinom ho, a emu bi yɛ den sɛ wɔbɛte aseɛ, a wɔn a wɔnsuaa adeɛ na wɔnnyina pintinn no kyea, sɛdeɛ wɔyɛ Kyerɛwsɛm nkaeɛ no nso, de kɔ wɔn ankasa ɔsɛeɛ mu.

Enti mo ahokyere no nyɛ foforɔ, na sɔhwɛ biara ntoo mo gye deɛ ɛtaa ba nnipa so: nanso Onyankopɔn di nokorɛ, ɔremma wɔnsɔ mo nhwɛ ntra deɛ mobɛtumi; na mmom ɔne sɔhwɛ no bɛma ɔkwan a mobɛfa so aguan, sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a mobɛtumi agyina ano. Mobɛfa ɔkwan a Onyankopɔn de ama mo wɔ m’adom ne m’ahummɔborɔ mu no so aguan afiri wɔn a wɔpɛ sɛ wɔfa mo akatua firi mo nsam denam ma a wɔma mohwe firi adom mu no so anaa? Mobɛpo me nsɛm de agye Yudafoɔ a wɔde nnaadaa ka sɛ ɛsɛ sɛ modi Torah so na moanya nkwagyeɛ no anaa? Moyɛ nkwaseafoɔ dodo sɛ mobɛsusu sɛ, berɛ a moahyɛ nkwa mmara no ase wɔ Yesu Kristo honhom mu no, seesei wɔde mo ahyɛ bɔne ne owuo mmara ase? Na mmara no ho deɛ ɛ? Me mma, mɛka akyerɛ mo. Monnsusu sɛ meyɛ nne bɔne a ɛde mo rekɔ mfomsoɔ mu, na mo mu bebree nnim me nne na monnim me nso, ɛwom sɛ meresrɛ mo deɛ! Monnsusu sɛ maba sɛ merebɛsɛe mmara no anaa adiyifoɔ no. Mamma sɛ merebɛsɛe, na mmom sɛ merebɛhyɛ mã. Na nokorɛ mese mo sɛ, Kɔsii sɛ ɔsoro ne asase bɛtwam no, yod baako anaa yod nsɔeɛ biara remfiri mmara no mu koraa, kɔsii sɛ biribiara bɛhyɛ mã.

Mene deɛ wadi Mose mmara no so, na wɔdi ne mmara ahodoɔ so denam me nsɛm a ɛfa adom ne mmɔborɔhunu ho a ɛwɔ me nkrataa mu ne Yesu Kristo gyidie so. Mode nnaadaa susuw sɛ ɛfiri sɛ mene mmara no ahyɛdeɛ no nti na mepene so sɛ obiara bɛbu Onyankopɔn mmara so, sɛ ɛyɛ Mose mmara anaa Onyankopɔn abrabɔ mmara. Na ɛdeɛn na meka kyerɛɛ wɔn a wɔte Korinto no? M’asɔre a ɛwɔ Laodikea, a medɔ mo, ɛnyɛ mo ne nnipa a ɛdi kan a wɔdii saa sɔhwɛ yi ho dwuma, na enti memaa Paulo kyerɛwee sɛ: Ɔno nso na wayɛ yɛn asomfoɔ a yɛfata ama Apam Foforɔ no, ɛnyɛ nkyerɛwdeɛ no, na mmom honhom no: na nkyerɛwdeɛ no kum, na honhom no ma nkwa. Nanso sɛ owuo som adwuma a wɔkyerɛwee, na wɔde sensan aboɔ so no, yɛɛ animuonyam, sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a Israel mma antumi anhwɛ Mose anim yie, ɛnam n’anim animuonyam nti, a na ɛsɛ sɛ wɔyi saa animuonyam no firi hɔ: Sɛn na honhom no som adwuma no rennyɛ animuonyam kɛseɛ? Monnyɛ mfomsoɔ, me mma, nkyerɛwdeɛ no kum ɛnam bɔne a ɛwɔ mo honam mu a nkyerɛwdeɛ korɔ no ara bu no fɔ nti, Mose mmara no mpo: Enti mmara no yɛ kronkron, na ahyɛdeɛ no yɛ kronkron na ɛteɛ na ɛyɛ. Na deɛ ɛyɛ no, ɛbɛyɛɛ owuo maa me anaa? Onyankopɔn mma. Na mmom bɔne, sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a ɛbɛda adi sɛ bɔne, ɛnam deɛ ɛyɛ no so yɛ owuo adwuma wɔ me mu: sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a bɔne nam Ahyɛdeɛ no so bɛyɛ bɔne kɛseɛ. Na enti memaa Paulo ka kyerɛɛ Galatiafoɔ no sɛ: Mempo Onyankopɔn adom no: na sɛ tenenee nam Mmara no so ba a, ɛnneɛ Kristo wuiɛ kwa. Me mma, wɔatwe mo afiri kwan so, na meka kyerɛɛ mo sɛ ɛsɛ sɛ wɔde nsa foforɔ gu nsa kotokuo foforɔ mu, enti monnyina pintinn wɔ adom a me, Kristo Yesu, de ama mo ahofadie no mu.

Me mma, montumi mmu Onyankopɔn mmara so berɛ a monantew honhom mu, na ɛntumi nyɛ yie. Wɔasi mo wɔ asomafoɔ ne adiyifoɔ fapem so, na Yesu Kristo ankasa ne twea tumpan no. Na wɔka kyerɛɛ Hebrifoɔ no sɛ: Na mɛhu wɔn amumuyɔsɛm mmɔbɔ, na wɔn bɔne ne wɔn amumuyɔsɛm merenkae bio. Sɛ ɔka sɛ, apam foforɔ, na wayɛ deɛ ɛdi kan no dada. Afei deɛ ɛporɔ na ɛyɛ dada no abɛn sɛ ɛbɛyera. Me mma, tenenee mfa mmara a wɔdi so so mma, na ɛyɛ akyɛdeɛ a wɔnam gyidie a wɔwɔ wɔ me, Kristo Yesu mu so nya. Na ɛfiri sɛ wɔayɛ mo ateneneefoɔ wɔ me mu nti, wɔagye mo afiri bɔne ne owuo mmara no mu.

Na ɛyɛ abususɛm sɛ wobɛka sɛ nkwa honhom mu a wɔbɛnantew no yɛ bɔne, na obiara a ɔgye di saa no bu m’afɔrebɔ a mede bɔɔ nnipa bɔne ho no sɛ adeɛ a ɛnho. Na meyɛ kɛseɛ sene Mose, na Mose ankasa ka sɛ: Awurade wo Nyankopɔn bɛma Odiyifoɔ bi asɔre ama wo afiri wo mfinimfini, afiri wo nuanom mu, te sɛ me, ɔno na mobɛtie no. Montie me nne, mma, na montie me kasa, na mammɔ mo mmara, na Satan mma na wɔnni mmara, ɛnyɛ Onyankopɔn mma! Na wɔ Romafoɔ mu no, Paulo kyerɛw pefee sɛ: Enti seesei afobu biara nni hɔ mma wɔn a wɔwɔ Kristo Yesu mu, a wɔnnantew honam akyi, na mmom honhom akyi. Na nkwa honhom mmara no, wɔ Kristo Yesu mu, agye me afiri bɔne ne owuo mmara no mu.

Na deɛ mmara no antumi anyɛ, ɛfiri sɛ na ɛyɛ mmerɛ ɛnam honam no so, Onyankopɔn somaa ne Ba no, wɔ bɔne honam sɛso mu, na ɔde bɔne buu bɔne fɔ wɔ honam mu: Sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a mmara no tenenee bɛhyɛ mã wɔ yɛn mu, yɛn a yɛnnantew honam akyi, na mmom honhom akyi. Na bio Romafoɔ kasa sɛ: Sɛ mede bɛda adi, mese, saa berɛ yi ne tenenee: sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a ɔbɛyɛ ɔteneneeni, ne deɛ ɔbu deɛ ɔgye Yesu di no bem. Afei, ɛhe na ahomasoɔ wɔ? Wɔayi afiri hɔ. Mmara bɛn so? Nnwuma so? Daabi: na mmom Gyidie Mmara no so. Enti, yɛde ba awieeɛ sɛ, wɔnam gyidie so bu onipa bem, a Mmara no nnwuma nka ho. Na ɛdeɛn na maka: Na emu na Onyankopɔn tenenee da adi firi gyidie mu kɔ gyidie mu; sɛdeɛ wɔakyerɛw no, ɔteneneeni nam gyidie so bɛtena ase.

Nanso mo mu bebree de adesoa a ɛmmfata to mo ho mo ho so ɛfiri sɛ moagye Satan adiyifoɔ a wɔsomaa wɔn sɛ wɔmmɛdaadaa mo ma mo nni Torah so sɛ nkwagyeɛ ho ahyɛdeɛ ne nso sɛ mobɛtra nkwagyeɛ mu no adi; na mo mu binom ka abususɛm sɛ madi mmara no so denam me ho a mede bɔɔ afɔdeɛ no so sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a seesei mobɛtumi adi Torah so. Faako no, woteɛ, ɛfiri sɛ sɛ wobɛnantew nkwa honhom mmara no mu a, ɛne sɛ wobɛdi Torah so pɛpɛɛpɛ denam wo ne me a wɔde wo abata ho no so; na m’asɔre no gyina pintinn wɔ tenenee ne kronkronyɛ nokorɛ mu wɔ me, Kristo Yesu, twea tumpan no so. Modi Torah so denam gyidie a ɛwɔ me mu no a modie so. Adɛn nti na mopaw Yudafoɔ nne sene me nne kronkron no? Adɛn nti na mode adesoa a ɛmmfata to mo nuanom so, na monkenkanee anaa? Na meka kyerɛɛ Kolosefoɔ no deda sɛ: Enti mma obiara mmmu mo atɛn wɔ aduane, anaa anonneɛ mu, anaa Afahyɛ da biara ho, anaa Ɔsrane Foforɔ, anaa Homeda nna ho A ɛyɛ nnoɔma a ɛbɛba no sunsuma, na nipadua no yɛ Kristo dea.

Me mma, memmu mmara no fɔ; mede kyerɛ deɛ ɛda adi pefee no ara sɛ ɛnam bɔne a ɛwɔ mo honam mu nti montumi nni Torah so da gye sɛ ɛnam mo gyidie a mowɔ wɔ me mu no so, na ɔteneneeni nam gyidie so bɛtena ase! Ebia wobɛbisa sɛ, ɛdeɛn ne gyidie a mowɔ wɔ me mu? Gyidie a mowɔ wɔ me mu no gyina me nsɛm a wogye di ne nteaseɛ a wote ase sɛ wɔde wo abata me ho wɔ afã biara so, na woyɛ m’asɔre a mede me mogya a bɔne nni mu no ahore wo ho; na bɔne fafiri ntumi nyɛ yie denam mmoa afɔrebɔ so. Monnye me nni, na ɛnam gyidie a mowɔ wɔ me mu so na wɔdi Torah so, ɛnam gyidie a mowɔ wɔ me mu so na mmara no hyɛ mã wɔ mo mu, na moyɛ ateneneefoɔ, wɔabu mo bem, na wɔate mo ho! Berɛ a wɔnyanee me kɔɔ nkwa foforɔ mu no, saa ara nso na wɔnyanee mo; na berɛ a wɔmaa me so kɔɔ Agya no nifa so no, saa ara nso na wɔde mo tenaa ɔsoro. Na Efesofoɔ di adanseɛ sɛ: Nanso Onyankopɔn a ɔyɛ ɔdefoɔ wɔ mmɔborɔhunu mu, ɛnam ne dɔ kɛseɛ a ɔde dɔɔ yɛn nti, Berɛ a yɛawu mpo wɔ bɔne mu no, ɔne Kristo anyan yɛn (adom nti na wɔagye mo nkwa), Na wanyane yɛn abom, na ɔne yɛn atena abom wɔ ɔsoro mmeaeɛ wɔ Kristo Yesu mu. Ɛsɛ sɛ mote ase sɛ Mose mmara no nyɛ mo apam, na mo deɛ ne apam foforɔ a wɔde me mogya asie so na wɔde me amanehunu atua ho ka.

Enti monyɛ osetie mma Gyidie no, me nkurɔfoɔ, na monnyɛ nwini bio. Monpaw nkwa wɔ me, Yesu Kristo mu, na saa na wɔfrɛɛ mo. Monyɛ nnwuma akɛseɛ bebree a mede ama mo no, mpo wɔ simma a ɛtwa toɔ yi mu. Mehia sɛ mote ase sɛ me nka ho a, montumi nyɛ hwee, na modi Torah afã horoɔ nyinaa so denam gyidie a mowɔ wɔ me mu so. Afei nso, Yudafoɔ a wɔde din kronkron frɛ wɔn no ahyɛ m’asɔre mã, de adesoa a ɛmmfata ato wɔn a wɔhwehwɛ nkwagyeɛ ne wɔn a wɔyɛ me dea no so. M’Agya maa me din a ɛboro din biara so, sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a Yesu Kristo din mu no, kotodwe biara bɛkoto. Mosusu sɛ mede adesoa a ɛmmfata bɛto aman a wɔka kasa ahodoɔ bebree no so sɛ wɔmfa me din, Yeshua HaMashiach, nni dwuma wɔ Hebri kasa mu nko ara? Montie me na montie me yie, na mereba, na mɛdi wɔn a wɔyɛ asoɔden ne wɔn a wɔpɛ sɛ wɔhaw mo no ho dwuma.
