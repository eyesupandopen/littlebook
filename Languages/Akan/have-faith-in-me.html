Adansefoɔ Mmienu: Asɔre no
Wɔ Yohane Osuboni Nnwuma Mu
Ɔbɛnem 25, 2024

Gye Me Di

Me mma, montie me nne, na monnye me, Awurade Yesu Kristo, nni. Mafrɛ mo aba animuonyam ne papayɛ mu na moagyina m’anim a wɔatew mo ho afi mo akwan ho wɔ me mpata mogya no mu, efisɛ Oguammaa no mogya hohoro efĩ nyinaa fi hɔ. Wɔatew mo ho wɔ me mu. Me mma, monhu sɛ, wɔn a wɔwɔ me mu, kyerɛ sɛ, wɔ Kristo mu no, wonni ɔbu. Sɛ wo ne me ne m’atirimpɔw yɛ biako a, ɛno ne sɛ wo ne Agya no, Yahweh, ne me, ne ba Yeshua, abɛyɛ biako. Mene Awurade a wafrɛ wo din na wama wo nkwa a enni awiei ho nsafrɛ, faako a ɔhaw, anaa ahoyeraw, anaa awerɛhow rennɔɔso bio; efisɛ mɛpepa w’anisu nyinaa wɔ me nkwagye a mede ma wo no mu. Mɛhyɛ adwuma ase na menwie anaa? Ɔsoro ne asase so Nyankopɔn bɛgyaa biribi a wɔanyɛ anaa ɔremmu n’ahyɛde ne n’asɛm so anaa? Monkenkanee sɛ, ɛrentumi nyɛ yiye sɛ Onyankopɔn bɛdi atoro? Nokwarem, bɔhyɛ biara bɛba mu na abɛyɛ hɔ, enti monhu sɛ mo gyefo rebɛn — ɛnyɛ ɔgye a ɛyɛ fa, na mmom ɔgye a ɛyɛ pɛ a hwee nka akyi na biribiara nni hɔ a enwiei.

Nneɛma yi nyinaa abam, na nea ɛntumi nyɛ yiye no aba mu wɔ me mu efisɛ midii owu so nkonim sɛ merenwu bio. Monhu sɛ ɔbonsam nnwuma bɔne biara a wabɔ mmɔden sɛ ɔbɛkyekyere wɔ mo asetena mu no, mɛsɛe. Aane, mɛsɛe ɔbonsam nnwuma nyinaa; atirimpɔw yi nti na wɔdaa me adi. Mama mo ayaresa a ɛyɛ pɛ, na mɛsa mo yare koraa afi mo nipadua a aporɔw no mu, na mobɛyɛ sɛ me. Monhwɛ m’Asɛm, montie m’Asem, momfa toto Kyerɛwsɛm no ho, na monhu sɛ mo gyefo a edi mu no bɛn sɛnea mo home a edi hɔ no te na ɛbɛba prɛko pɛ. Monnyina pintinn mma me; monkasa mma me. Monnyɛ hwee wɔ abosonsomfo ne aguamammɔfo a wɔawura me Asɔre no mu na wɔbɛbɔ mmɔden sɛ wɔbɛdaadaa me mma nketewa no ho. M’abufuw da wɔn ne obiara a ɔtow hyɛ me nkurɔfo so sɛ ɔbɛdaadaa wɔn no so.

Efisɛ ɛrentumi nyɛ yiye sɛ wɔbɛtwe mo afi me nsam, na wɔn a mahu wɔn mmɔbɔ no ne wɔn a wɔnam me din kɛse, Yesu Kristo, a wɔkae sɛ Awurade no so ayɛ me wɔn Awurade. Meremma mo anim ngu ase, na nneɛma a ɛretwɛn mo no nnya mmaa onipa komam, efisɛ ɛrenkyɛ na mɛfa mo akɔ. Me mma, mma nsɛm bɔne nnhaw mo koma, efisɛ wɔagye mo afi Onyankopɔn abufuw no mu, na m’anokwafo renfa mfe ason abufuw bere a ɛreba no mu. Momma mo koma nna hɔ dinn na monhome wɔ me mu, mpo wɔ mo ahohia ne ɔtaa mu no monhome wɔ me mu, na mɛma mo asomdwoe a wontumi nkyerɛkyerɛ mu. Me mmɔborɔhunu ne m’adom tim hɔ daa, na matua mo bɔne ho ka, enti monkɔ so mfi mu mma. Mihu ɔhaw a mofa mu. Mete nsɛm a ɛyɛ den a wɔka kyerɛ mo ne nea wɔka fa mo ho nyinaa. Afei nso, mehyɛ mo den wɔ mo mmerɛwyɛ mu, na mama mo me honhom, na moyɛ den na mowɔ tumi wɔ me mu. Wɔ mo ankasa ahoɔden mu no, mubedi nkogu, enti monhome wɔ m’ahoɔden mu, efisɛ m’Agya ama me so asen biribiara.

Me mma, ɛrenkyɛ na mɛtɔ abɔnefo so were, enti munnsuro. Saa aweretɔ yi nyɛ mo dea, na wɔanyɛ ama Onyankopɔn mma na mmom wɔayɛ ama n’atamfo a wɔayɛ adesamma bɔne ne nnipa bɔne a wokum na wɔfow wɔn yɔnko ne wɔn nua.

Aane, nnipa bɔne pii akɔka ɔbonsam ho de anya mfaso a ɛyɛ anibere, na wɔwɔ wɔn kyɛfa ne wɔn akatua wɔ ogya tade a wɔasiesie ama ɔbonsam ne n’abɔfo no mu. Monnyɛ abusuakuw biako, nnipa abusuakuw no nyinaa anaa? Nanso, monhu sɛ abufuw kɛse reba na ɔsoro rebeyi ne ho adi akyerɛ asase. Wɔakyerɛw adeyɛ biara agu hɔ. Wɔakyerɛw asɛm hunu biara a wɔkae no agu hɔ. Ahintasɛm biara nni hɔ a wobetumi de ahintaw me, na baabiara nni hɔ a wobetumi ahintaw afi m’aniwa anim. Ɛwom sɛ abɔnefo tu fam de guan m’abufuw a ɛreba no de, nanso wobehyia wɔn awiei bere a wɔrebɔ mmɔden sɛ wɔbɛhintaw me no.

Mɛtua ka, na mɛtɔ were na masi atɛntrenee ama wɔn a wɔadi wɔn amim. Mewɔ nkrante a ano yɛ nnam a ɛwɔ ano abien a efi m’anom, na biribiara ne obiara ntumi nsiw me kwan, efisɛ mene Ahemfo Hene ne Awuranom Awurade. Hwan na obetumi atete wo afi Onyankopɔn dɔ a ɛwɔ me mu no ho? Mantua mo bɔne ho ka na mamma mo daa nkwa sɛ mɛma abufuw ne ɔbu aba mo so, m’anokwafo. Biribiara ne obiara ntumi ntetew yɛn ntam, na ɛrenkyɛ na mode mo ankasa aniwa bɛhwɛ m’Agya ne me. Ɛwom sɛ moanya ahotoso ne gyidi kɛse wɔ me mu, a monnhuu me da de mo ankasa aniwa de, nanso ɛrenkyɛ na mubehu, na mubehu sɛnea wɔnim mo no. Hwan ne nea ɔhaw wo? Ɔrenyɛ hwee na wɔremfa ne nnwuma bɔne no nsan mma no mmɔho abien anaa? Nnipa bɔne asoɛ abufuw kɛse, aane, abufuw kɛse wɔ m’Agya abɔde a wɔsɛe no ho, na mɛtua ka, Awurade na ɔse.

Monnyina m’anim na monkɔ so ntetew mo ho mfi wiase bɔne a ɛreba awiei yi ho na momma me mpata adwuma no nhwie ngu mo so, na ɛnteɛw mo ho koraa. Hwɛ! Meyɛ nneɛma nyinaa foforo, na mo bɔne ne mo amumuyɛsɛm no, merenkae bio. Enti mumfi mo ankasa nnwuma mu na momfa me nnwuma, efisɛ sɛ moyɛ saa a, mode ayaresa a edi mu bɛba mo ankasa kra mu. Munnsuro abɔnefo! Efisɛ ɛrenkyɛ na wɔn nnwuma bɛbɔ ahwete sɛ mfutuma wɔ mframa kɛse anim, na mɛyɛ nneɛma nyinaa ho fĩ na ɛbɛyɛ foforo. Akatua kɛse wɔ hɔ ma m’anokwafo ne wɔn a wɔapaw wɔn; na ɛrenkyɛ, aane, ɛrenkyɛ koraa na mubetwa mo nnwuma ho akatua no sɛ mompa abaw a.

Monnyina mma me nkosi awiei, efisɛ nea ɔhwere ne nkwa wɔ me mu no benya nkwa na obetwa anigye ne nkwagye ne ahurusi kɛse daa daa wɔ m’Agya anim. Mayɛ mo ahemfo ne asɔfo wɔ m’Agya anim, sɛnea mene ɔsɔfopanyin a ɔwɔ hɔ daa wɔ Melkisedek nhyehyɛe no so no. Monnyina pintinn wɔ m’adom mu na monhu sɛ ɛrenkyɛ na wɔbɛto ɔpon no mu, sɛnea Onyankopɔn too Noa ne n’ahode guu Adaka no mu no. Saa ara na mɛboaboa mo ano akɔ me nkyɛn wɔ soro. Ɔpon a abue hɔ a onipa biara ntumi mmue anaa ɔnto mu wɔ hɔ, na megye mo aba me nkyɛn, m’Ayeforo. Enti monhwɛ me kwan efisɛ bere no asa.

Mene Awurade nokwafo no.

Mene ɔkwan, nokware, ne nkwa. Mene nipadua no ti.

Mene ayeforo no kunu, na mese bra!

Mene nea ɔrenwu bio, efisɛ ɛrentumi nyɛ yiye sɛ owu bɛkura me mu.

Mene nea wafrɛ mo aba animuonyam, papayɛ, ne nokware mu.

Mene Awurade Yesu Kristo, Onyankopɔn ba a ɔdaa ne ho adi wɔ ɔhonam mu a wawu ama mo bɔne.

Mene nea wɔanyan no da a ɛto so abiɛsa sɛ ɔrenwu bio.

Mene nea Onyankopɔn agye no akɔ soro sɛnea m’asomafo ne m’asuafo hwɛɛ me sɛ mekɔɔ mununkum no mu no.

Mene ɔsɔfopanyin a ɔwɔ hɔ daa wɔ Melkisedek nhyehyɛe no so.
