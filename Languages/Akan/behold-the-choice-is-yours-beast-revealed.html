Adansefoɔ Mmienu: Israel
Deɛ ɛyɛ Nwono na ɛyɛ Dɛ
Kitawonsa 15, 2025

Hwɛ! Wo na Wowɔ Hokwan Sɛ Woyi | Wɔada Mmoa Adi | Wɔde Nkɔmhyɛ mu Nkaebɔ ato dwa

Tie wo gyefoɔ, Awurade ne Ɔhene, me Mma, nsɛm na fa w’aso to wo Awurade, Yesu Kristo ɔteneneeni no nsɛm so, ɛfiri sɛ meyɛ tenenee ba na mesɔre wɔ m’anuonyam mu, na moanyini sɛ anantwi mma a wɔwɔ buo mu wɔ asomdwoeɛ ne ahobammɔ mu, na matete mo ama ɔsoro atirimpɔ a ɛrebɛda adi koraa wɔ mo mu, me nokwafoɔ ne wɔn a wɔayi mo. Hwɛ! Mene nea ɔsɔ nnipa akoma hwɛ sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a mobɛhunu nokorɛ mu sɛ mowɔ gyidie no mu anaa, anaa sɛ wɔadaadaa mo na mo bɔne ne nnaadaa kɛseɛ a ɛredi wiase so seesei no afa mo.

Na mene nea Yahweh ne no akyɛ n’ahennwa, a ɛne Kristo Yesu, Awurade a wasɔre na wɔama no tumi no, na meyɛ Awurade mu Awurade ne Ahemfo mu Hene na hwɛ Yuda Abusuakuo no mu Gyata no nam omununkum mu reba abɛfa me nokwafoɔ akɔ me nkyɛn sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a wɔbɛfa wɔn akɔ soro ama Odwammaa no ayeforohyia, ɛfiri sɛ m’ayeforɔ asiesie ne ho. Na ne nkanea ayɛ ma, na wasiesie ne ntama, na ɔretwɛn m’adieyi sɛ ɔbaabunu nyansafoɔ, ɛne wɔn a matwe wɔn ho koraa na mfomsoɔ biara nni wɔn ho wɔ Onyankopɔn ahennwa no anim. Mpo wɔn a Baraba amfa anifere annye wɔn, ɛfiri sɛ Baraba de atuateɛ a ɛyɛ hu, ɔko ne amanehunu ba, ɛfiri sɛ seesei ɔyɛ atitire pii a wɔbom yɛ adwuma no mu baako, nanso ɔbɛsɔre sɛ Moshiach atorofoɔ, na ɔman no mu nnipa ayi no sɛ ɔnni Satan asɔredan no so. Na ɔyɛ nea wɔn agyanom yii no no adedifoɔ, na wɔresiesie wɔn ho sɛ wɔbɛgye no atom sɛ wɔn Hene.

Nanso mo, oo Laodikea Asɔre, afei mo amanehunu ne atemmuo berɛ aduru. Na moayi ɔgyefoɔ foforɔ sɛ mo Hene, ɛne deɛ n’adɛnkyerɛne ne anwanwadeɛ a ɛyɛ nnaadaa di n’anim na seesei ɔde adutoɔ ne nkonyaayi adi mo anim. Meyɛ Yeshua HaMashiach na mede me ara me bɔnefakyɛ, mogya a ɛho nni dɛm atɔ mo, na mene nea ɔbɛma moayɛ yie, na mabɔ mo foforɔ wɔ me honhom mu, ɛfiri sɛ wɔayɛ me honhom a ɛma nkwa ɛfiri sɛ Yahweh ahyɛ sɛ ne Ba no na ɔwɔ tumi nyinaa, ɛne deɛ ɔyɛ Onyankopɔn odwammaa ne Yuda Abusuakuo no mu Gyata no nkabom! Na tenenee mu na mɛbu atɛn na mako ɛfiri sɛ Onyankopɔn odwammaa ne Yuda Abusuakuo no mu Gyata no abom wɔ me, Kristo Yesu mu, ɛne Yeshua HaMashiach ɛfiri sɛ ɛno ne me!

Mɛwie mo nkwagyeɛ, oo Laodikea Asɔre, nanso monsiesiee mo ho sɛ mobɛwura m’Ahennie mu saa berɛ yi ɛfiri sɛ moafi mo adwene baako a mowɔ wɔ Kristo mu no ho! Mɛkyerɛ mo mo mfomsoɔ, na mowɔ hokwan koraa sɛ mobɛsakra firi bɔne nyinaa ho, nanso monni berɛ a mode bɛgyegye mo Awurade nne no ho akyinnyeɛ, ɛfiri sɛ bɔne a ɛyɛ hu yi yɛ pii a Satan de abɔ mo sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a morentumi mma me nkyɛn koraa wɔ adwensakra mu na matumi atwe mo ho koraa afiri mo bɔne ho wɔ me bɔnefakyɛ mogya no mu.

Hwɛ! Amanamanmufoɔ berɛ no aba awieeɛ na ngodua a ɛyɛ wuram a wɔde fraa wɔn mu no, na wɔne wɔn nyaa Ngodua no ntini ne ne sradeɛ no mu kyɛfa no, afei wɔbɛtwa no ato hɔ. Na moabu Kristo Yesu Nsɛm no animtiaa, ɛfiri sɛ meka kyerɛɛ mo pefee sɛ monnhoahoa mo ho ntia nnubaa a ɛhwee ase wɔ gyidie a wonni mu no: nanso sɛ mohoahoa mo ho a, ɛnyɛ mo na mosoa ntini no, na mmom ntini no na ɛsoa mo. Meyɛ Yese Ntini, na mene nea ɔgyina hɔ sɛ anidasoɔ nsoromma ma mmerɛ nyinaa, ɛne deɛ ɔde nkwagyeɛ ne Yahweh akwan ba asase so na mɛdi m’agyapadeɛ so firi Dawid ahennwa so wɔ tenenee mu na mede me mogya a ɛho nni dɛm atɔ asase na mɛgye deɛ ɛyɛ me dea no dennen firi wɔn a wɔte hɔ kwa no nsam, ɛne wɔn a wɔahwe ase no.

Mene nea ɔde adom ne mmɔborɔhunu ama asase, ɛfiri sɛ wɔde adom mmerɛ tenten maa me firi Yahweh hɔ de maa Amanamanmufoɔ nkwagyeɛ. Na Yahweh atirimpɔ wɔ Israel mu ne sɛ wɔbɛhyira Amanamanmufoɔ na wɔnam Israel osetie so anya nkwagyeɛ. Nanso mmerɛ kakra akyi no, wɔyɛɛ ahomasoɔ, na wɔhoahoaa wɔn ho, na wɔtwaa wɔn firii ngodua ankasa no ho wɔ gyidie a wonni mu. Nanso mo, me Laodikea Asɔre, mpo mo ne ahomasoɔ, gyidie a wonni, ne anifira a ɛte sɛ deɛ nnubaa ankasa a wɔtwaa wɔn firii ho wɔ gyidie a wonni mu no daa no adi no su, na afei wɔbɛtwa mo afiri ngodua ankasa no ho. Na afei mɛkɔ akɔwie m’adwuma wɔ ngodua ankasa no so, ɛne Israel me nuanom ne me man, mpo wɔn a mayi wɔn sɛ wɔbɛgye me atom sɛ wɔn Awurade sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a mɛbɔ wɔn sɛ onipa foforɔ baako na Onyankopɔn ahintasɛm no bɛwie, ɛne me bɔnefakyɛ nkwagyeɛ nnwuma no awieeɛ.

Na mo, me Laodikea Asɔre, moagya mo ɔdɔ a ɛdi kan no na mowɔ ɔdaadaafoɔ a moaka ntam sɛ mobɛdi no nokorɛ no nsam. Moayi Baraba foforɔ sɛ mo Hene na moapo Yesu Nasareni no sɛ mo Awurade na mowɔ ɔgyefoɔ atorofoɔ foforɔ nsam. Mpo deɛ ɔyɛ ne ho sɛ ahintasɛm Babilon gyefoɔ, a ɔkasa na ɔyɛ n’ade sɛ mo mu baako, nanso ɔkasa sɛ ɔwɔ. Na ahintasɛm Babilon te ase dɛdɛɛdɛ na ɔwɔ ahonyadeɛ, anigyeɛ ne ahodeɛ pii.

Na ahintasɛm Babilon ama wiase nyinaa abow wɔ aguamammɔ fi a ɛwɔ ne sika kuruwa mu no ho, nanso saa na wɔn a wɔahwe ase no akwan te, wɔn a wɔde fi a ɛyɛ bone paa ma, na monom no pɛsɛmenkominya ne anigyeɛ kɛseɛ mu ɛfiri sɛ sika kuruwa no ama mo ani afira afiri fi a ɛyɛ hu a monom no fi so no ho. Na mpo me Laodikea Asɔre no mpo nhyia bio wɔ me twerɛsɛm kronkron no ho na mmom wɔ Baraba nhyiamu ase, deɛ mowɔ ne mu ahotosoɔ. Na enti moayi ɔgyefoɔ foforɔ ɛfiri sɛ modɔ wiase ne wiase nnoɔma na morentumi nsusu sɛ mobɛhwere mo nnuane a ɛsom bo no, gye sɛ mede m’abufuo a ɛreba no bɛyi anigyeɛ nyinaa afiri mo ho, na mpo seesei mo hwereɛ redɔɔso!

Na mo ani afira na mo adwene ayɛ basaa wɔ mo ahomasoɔ mu sɛ Yahweh bɛma ɔgyefoɔ foforɔ ka me, Yesu Nasareni no ho, ɛfiri sɛ meyɛ ɔbaa no asefoɔ a wɔhyɛɛ ho bɔ no na Dan yɛ gyata ba a Mesia biara remfiri ne mu nsɔre. Tie me na tie me yie, oo Laodikea asɔre, ɛfiri sɛ afei ɛyɛ mo adwensakra firi mo akoma mu mfomsoɔ ho anaa afei ne berɛ a mɛgya mo ama ogya a ɛtɔ ho no na obiara a ɔbɔ Awurade din kɔsi awieeɛ no bɛnya nkwagyeɛ na pii a wɔkaa me din wɔ gyidie a wonni mu no bɛhwe ase akɔ ɔsɛeɛ mu, na wɔasɛe wɔn hokwan wɔ onyamesom su a ɛpaa tumi a ɛwɔ mu no ho: Me Laodikeafoɔ, mene tumi a ɛwɔ mu no na me mu na Yahweh de tumi nyinaa ahyɛ, na moapa me, mo Awurade ne mo gyefoɔ, na mɛfe mo afiri m’anomu gye sɛ mosakra afei!

Wɔ m’adom mmerɛ no mu no, nkwagyeɛ trɛɛ amanaman no mu, na ɔman biara kyɛɛ na wɔnyaa ngodua ankasa no ntini no mu kyɛfa. Na afei, amanaman no rennyɛ nkwagyeɛ nsoromma bio na wɔn berɛ a wɔwɔɔ Ɔba no mu no nam me nuanom ne me man a wɔpoo me no so na ɛbaaɛ ɛfiri sɛ wɔteateaam wɔ gyidie a wonni mu sɛ gyaa Baraba ma yɛn! Nanso sɛdeɛ Yahweh ahintasɛm no daa adi denam m’asomafoɔ kronkron ne nkɔmhyɛfoɔ so no, masi Baraba kwan na mamma wammue ne ho koraa, na mmom mama bɔne ahintasɛm no nkɔ so. Na afei mmerɛ aduru a mɛgyaa Baraba koraa sɛdeɛ me si kwan no bɛfiri hɔ na sɛdeɛ wɔfa me nokwafoɔ, wɔn a wɔafrɛ wɔn, ne wɔn a wɔayi wɔn no kɔ soro baabi a masiesie wɔn tenabea a ɛwɔ hɔ daa no, na wɔbɛhyɛ nwera pa a ɛho tew a ɛnsɛe da na ɛnni dɛm da.

So mo ani afira ama mo wiase, na mo aso ayɛ den ama morente me nne bio oo Laodikea Asɔre? Moagye nnaadaa a bɔne ahintasɛm no de aba no adi, na moama mmɔkaw a ɛyɛ awuduro aba mo Twerɛsɛm a asɛe no mu a ɛde ɔsɛeɛ koraa aba mo Asɔre ahorow mu a ɛde anansesɛm agu mo ara mo akoma mu wɔ gyidie a wonni a ɛmfata mu. Moama nnaadaa ahyɛnsoɔ adwumayɛfoɔ nnaadaa adaadaa mo de twerɛsɛm atorofoɔ ne nsɛm a ɛyɛ nnaadaa. Monte twerɛsɛm ase bio, na moaboaboa ɔhaw akɛseɛ ano ama mo ho a ɛbɛba mo so mpofirim. Onyankopɔn Asɛm biara yɛ kronkron na wɔahyɛ sɛ ɛbɛma gyidie mu osetie ne wɔn a wɔgye no no nyini. Te me Nsɛm no ase na te mmerɛ a mote mu no ase. Na memaa Yohane a ɔdaa nnoɔma adi no twerɛɛ sɛ: Na mehunuu aboa bi sɛ ɔfiri ɛpo mu reforo, na ɔwɔ atifi nson, ne mmɛn du, na ne mmɛn so na ahenkyɛw du wɔ, na ne ti so na abususɛm din wɔ.

Na bio Yohane a ɔdaa nnoɔma adi no twerɛɛ sɛ: Mehunuu ɔbaa bi sɛ ɔte aboa kɔkɔɔ bi so, a abususɛm din ayɛ no ma, na ɔwɔ atifi nson, ne mmɛn du. Na ɔbaa no hyɛɛ ntadeɛ a ɛyɛ kɔkɔɔ ne bibire, na ɔde sika, ne aboɔden abo ne nhene a ɛsom bo asiesie ne ho, na ɔkura sika kuruwa bi wɔ ne nsam, a akyideɛ ne n’aguamammɔ fi ayɛ no ma.

Na bio: Na mmɛn du a wohunuu no wɔ aboa no so no, yeinom bɛtane ɔbaa no, na wɔbɛma wadan amamfo, na wada adagya, na wɔadi ne nam, na wɔde ogya ahyɛ no. Na Onyankopɔn de ahyɛ wɔn akoma mu sɛ wɔnyɛ ne pɛ, na wɔnyɛ adwene, na wɔmfa wɔn ahennie mma aboa no, kɔsi sɛ Onyankopɔn nsɛm bɛbam. Na ɔbaa no, a wohunuu no no, ne Kuro kɛseɛ no a ɛdi ahemfo a wɔwɔ asase so so no.

Me Mma, monte ase sɛ aboa betumi akyerɛ ankorankoro ne ahennie nso, monsua na monhunu. Aboa a ɔsoa ɔharlot no bɛsɛe ɔharlot no na ɔrenhyɛ ɔharlot no ase bio, ɛfiri sɛ wadi ahemfo a wɔwɔ asase so so wɔ n’aguamammɔ mu. Enti aboa a ɔbɛfiri ɛpo mu asɔre no bɛma wɔn Mahdi a wɔhwɛ kwan no aba, nanso ɔbɛsɛe Ahintasɛm Babilon koraa, ɛfiri sɛ Onyankopɔn de ɔharlot kɛseɛ no ahyɛ n’atamfo a wɔtane no dedaw no nsa.

Ansa na aboa a ɔfiri ɛpo mu ne aboa a ɔfiri asase so no bɛsɔre koraa wɔ ɔwɔ no Ahennie mu no, ahemfo pii wɔ hɔ a wɔayɛ adwene baako sɛ wɔde wɔn tumi ne wɔn nniso bɛma ɔwɔ no, ɛfiri sɛ Onyankopɔn de ahyɛ wɔn akoma mu sɛ wɔnyɛ. Na wɔbɛsɛe ahintasɛm Babilon. Modwene sɛ moadi me twerɛsɛm kronkron no so nkonim ɛfiri sɛ moagye mo nyamekyerɛfoɔ atorofoɔ no adi? Monte ase na montie mo Awurade Nsɛm!

Bio, memaa Yohane a ɔdaa nnoɔma adi no twerɛɛ sɛ: Na aboa a mehunuu no no, ɔte sɛ ɔsebɔ, na ne nan te sɛ sisire, na n’ano te sɛ gyata ano: na ɔwɔ no maa no ne tumi ne n’ahennwa, ne tumi kɛseɛ. Na mehunuu ne ti baako sɛ wɔapira no akum no, nanso ne pira a ɛde owuo ba no ho yɛɛ den, na wiase nyinaa ho dwirii wɔn na wɔdii aboa no akyi.

Me Mma, Yahweh nsɛm no wɔ emu dɔ a ɛdɔɔso, a onipa biara ntumi nni so nkonim. Aboa a wɔka ne ho asɛm no yɛ ahemman a ɛbɛsɔre afiri ɔharlot no sɛeɛ mu, na nhyehyɛeɛ foforɔ no bɛfiri nhyehyɛeɛ dada no mu asɔre! Ɛgyina hɔ ma ɛpo mu aboa no, ɛfiri sɛ ɔbɛsɔre sɛ wɔn Mahdi, na ɛgyina hɔ ma ahennie su a ɔde bɛdi ɛpo no so. Adɛn nti na mommu me Asɛm no nsɛm? Ɛnka sɛ ɛpo mu aboa no ankasa na wɔpiraa no, na onipa bɛn na ɔwɔ atifi nson ankasa? Na mmom ne ti baako sɛ wɔapira no akum no. Oo, nkwaseafoɔ, ne akoma a ɛyɛ brɛoo sɛ mobɛgye deɛ Nkɔmhyɛfoɔ no nyinaa aka no adi! SƐ WƆAPIRA no akum no. Asɛmfua "sɛ wɔapira" no ɛsɛ sɛ ɛma mogyina, nanso monnye me nsɛm nketenkete no mpo nni bio, na mene Asɛm a ɛyɛɛ ɔhonam no. Ɛnna adi pefee mma mo, SƐ WƆAPIRA, yɛ kasakoa a akyɛ? Ɛnna adi pefee mma mo sɛ "wɔapira" yɛ nsusuiɛ? Ɛnna adi pefee mma mo sɛ ɛyɛ tiawa a ɛkyerɛ sɛ, sɛ ɛte saa a?

Nanso daabi, mogye ɔwɔ no nnaadaa di, ɛfiri sɛ Baraba kasa sɛ ɔwɔ, nanso ɔnyɛ Baraba, na mmom ɔyɛ asase so aboa no. Na afei wiase nyinaa ho bɛdwiri wɔn wɔ aboa no ho ɛnam ne nnaadaa nkyerɛkyerɛmu ne anwanwadeɛ nti, na ɔbɛyɛ nnaadaa nkyerɛkyerɛmu ne anwanwadeɛ pii, na wansɔre koraa ɛfiri sɛ wɔnhyee ɔharlot no ogya mu, nanso afei wɔada no adi wɔ ne berɛ mu. Ɔkasa sɛ ɔwɔ, ɔka sɛ "Onyankopɔn nko ara na ɔgyee no firii ne ti pira mu" na yei yɛ abususɛm a ɛyɛ nnaadaa. Yahweh, Onyankopɔn kɛseɛ a ɔwɔ hɔ daa no, asoma me Yesu Nasareni no sɛ wiase gyefoɔ. Me ne m’Agya yɛ baako wɔ adwene ne atirimpɔ mu na Yahweh annye Baraba na me, Kristo Yesu nso annye no. Wɔadaadaa mo na wɔakyere mo na wɔde mo ahyɛ nnaadaa kɛseɛ mu, na meka kyerɛ mo sɛ monsakra na monsan nkɔ mo ɔdɔ a ɛdi kan no ho na monyɛ no seesei, ɛfiri sɛ berɛ nni hɔ!

Na mene nea ɔsɔ akoma hwɛ, na mene nea ɔsɔ obiara a mefrɛ no ma nkwagyeɛ no hwɛ, ɛfiri sɛ metwe obiara ho wɔ ogya a ɛtɔ ho no mu! Hwɛ! Mene nea ɔte sɛ dwetɛ ho tɔfoɔ ne ne ho tewfoɔ: na metwe Yahweh mma no ho, na metwe wɔn ho sɛ sika ne dwetɛ, sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a wɔbɛbɔ afɔdeɛ ama Awurade wɔ tenenee mu. So monte ase sɛ mɛma moayɛ asɔfoɔ ne ahemfo wɔ m’Agya anim? Ɛwom sɛ me honhom kronkron akyɛdeɛ no, a ɛnam so ma wɔwo mo foforɔ firi aba a ɛnsɛe da mu no yɛ pɛ na ɛnhia sɛ wɔtɔ ho, nso mofiri Adam mu nam mogya so na ɛsɛ sɛ moma m’adwuma no wie koraa wɔ mo asetena mu, gye sɛ m’aba no ahwe abotan so.

Afei atemmuo mmerɛ no aduru na adom mmerɛ no awie. Ɛwom sɛ pii bɛdi me nsɛm ho fɛw na wɔadan wɔn ho wɔ gyidie a wonni mu deɛ, nanso Yahweh nim deɛ ɛbɛfiri mu aba dedaw. Na Yahweh daa kyerɛ nnipa ne ara ne akwan a asɛe no na ɔkasa tia nnipa wɔ bɔne ho, na ɔnim wɔ ne dɔ ne ne nyansa a ɛwɔ hɔ daa mu sɛ ɔbɛgye ebinom. Na sɛdeɛ Yahweh pirimoo Farao akoma no, saa ara na ɔbɛpirim pii a wɔte nanso wɔntie no akoma. Yahweh bɛsɔ onipa hwɛ bio ne bio. Na ebinom bɛyɛ sɛ Farao na wɔbɛpirim wɔn akoma kɔ akyiri wɔ gyidie a wonni mu nanso ebinom bɛfiri Farao akwan no ho na wɔasɔre akɔ nkwa a ɛnni awieeɛ mu wɔ Kristo Yesu mu, nanso ɛnam ogya a ɛtɔ ho no so.

Monte ase sɛ wɔn a wɔyi Baraba no bɛma wɔn ho adi kyɛfa wɔ Baraba nsɛmmɔne mu, na wɔn a wɔpo sɛ wɔbɛbɔ me din kɔsi awieeɛ no nyinaa bɛsɛe ɛfiri sɛ wɔn ani gyee tenenee a ɛnni mu ho. Na mo gyefoɔ foforɔ no nyɛ gyefoɔ na mmom ɔyɛ aboa a ɔyɛ kɔm. Na ne nhyehyɛeɛ ne n’ahennie no yɛ wɔn a wɔhaw mmofra no dea. So monte Ɔwɔ no akwan ase? Na wɔn a wɔdi ɔwɔ no akwan akyi no di mmofra so. Wɔbɔ mmofra afɔdeɛ ma Molok, wɔhwehwɛ wɔn mmeranteberɛ mu nsuo firi mmofra a wɔayɛ wɔn ayayadeɛ no mogya mu, wɔto mmofra mmonnaa ma wɔn ara wɔn anigyeɛ a ɛyɛ yaw, na wɔyɛ awotwaa mu abofra a wɔbɔ no de kum nketewa no ansa na wɔawo wɔn, na wɔde mo akɔ wɔn akwan mu kokoam, ɛfiri sɛ saa na Ahintasɛm Babilon ne ɔwɔ no ahennie no mu akannifoɔ akwan te. Na afei Onyankopɔn atemmuo reba saa nnoɔma yi ne pii ho. Na sɛ moanhu saa nnoɔma yi a, anka morenni bɔne, nanso afei mohunu na mo bɔne da so wɔ hɔ.

So monkenkanee? Sɛ Awurade se, Wɔtee nne bi wɔ soro, awerɛhoɔ ne osu a ɛyɛ nwono. Rahel, a ɔresu n’amma, wampene sɛ wɔbɛkyekye ne werɛ ama n’amma, ɛfiri sɛ na wɔnni hɔ.
