Dɔn no Anim Ada Mpan a Nɔma no Ahwehwe Agu

W’asiesie wo ho ama me, m’Ayeforɔ? (Na meraba) W’asiesie wo ho? Woretie? Worehwɛ? Na meraba abɛfa wo, enti mma wo koma nntu na nya anidasoɔ ne ahwɛhwɛdeɛ kɛseɛ, na ɔsoro ahonyadeɛ ankasa retwɛn me nokwafoɔ na w’akatua wɔ me nsam! Na nnome nka wɔn a wɔnam gyidie a wonni so apo me no nyinaa, me Yesu Nasareni no! Enti w’atemmuo da no adu wo so, O ɔbaa buulufoɔ kɛseɛ a wote nsuo bebree soɔ, na wo berɛ a wɔahyɛ no adu.

Ɔsɛeɛ a ɛyɛ mpofirim aba wo so na w’atamfoɔ asiesie wɔn ho, nanso woda so te ase yie ɛwom sɛ woyɛ ɔman a wɔde nkɔnsɔnkɔnsɔn agu wo m’anim, Kristo Yesu. Wo berɛ a wohyɛneeɛ no baeɛ ɛfiri sɛ ɛberɛ bi mu no, w’Agyanom dii Onyankopɔn Ba no akyi na wɔdii me Nsɛm so. Nanso m’Asɔre berɛ no aba awieeɛ ɛfiri sɛ wotɔn m’ahintasɛm ahonyadeɛ no, na woadwadie mpo me nkurɔfoɔ yiedie ne ɔdɔ de agye sika fi ne animuonyam wɔ nnipa anim.

Motɔn mo ho wɔ anibereɛ ne deɛ ɛnni nokorɛdie mu, na moapa me, Yesu Kristo a ɔwuuɛ maa wiase bɔne no. Nanso moapo me nsɛm, moapo me nkaanim, moapo m’afotuo, moapo m’ananmusifoɔ, moapo me nkɔmhyɛfoɔ, moapo m’asomafoɔ, na moapo me, Yesu Nasareni, enti moapo Yahweh, na afei me nso mɛpo wo, O Babilon ahuntaeɛ. Na afei atemmuo firi aseɛ wɔ Yahweh Fie!

Na berɛ a anka mobɛtumi anya asomdwoeɛ ne ahobammɔ no, moantumi anyɛ saa na mmom mopɛɛ sɛ modidi wɔn a wɔasɛe mo man no akyi w’anifuraeɛ mu. Edomfoɔ nwura a ɛhyɛ nsase no so ma, na wɔahaw asase firi berɛ a ɔbɔnefoɔ no trɛɛ n’akyiwadeɛ mu wɔ Yahweh abɔdeɛ so no.

Enti monhu sɛ, me, Kristo Yesu, mɛsɛe Ahintasɛm Babilon pasaa wɔ ogya mu na dɔnhwere baako mu na wo sɛeɛ bɛba, na wɔatwerɛ na deɛ Yahweh aka no, obiara ntumi nsesa. O, mo abosonsomfoɔ nkwaseafoɔ a ɛwɔ Ahintasɛm Babilon asase so, sɛ anka mote mo tebea no ase a. Sɛ anka mobrɛɛ mo aso ase maa me nsɛm ɛfiri sɛ mafrɛ, masrɛ, makɔto, mahyɛ nkuran, mateam, makyerɛ nsɛnkyerɛnne wɔ soro ne nsɛnkyerɛnne wɔ asase so na sɛ anka mode atweaatam bɛfura mo koma, sɛ anka mobɛtena nso mu anuonyam mu no, na mmom mo ani agye te sɛ deɛ morehwɛ anwanwadeɛ.

Nnome nka wɔn a wɔn ani gye Yahweh nsɛnkyerɛnne a ɛwɔ wiem a ɛda wɔn atemmuo adi no ho, na nnome mmɔho mmienu nka wo Ahintasɛm Babilon, na woanya atemmuo abɔ ho mmienu a wɔrebɛgu agu, na worentumi nkwati na worensan nnya wo ho. Ahintasɛm Babilon woyɛ asase a wɔpɛ nneɛma ntɛm ara, na monni nyansa koraa. Moadaadaa aman no ama wɔabɛdi mo akwammɔne ne apam akwan so, na moama wɔabobɔ wɔn aguamammɔ fi no nsa. Monnte m’akwan ase, na mo mu biara nam n’ankasa akwan so sɛdeɛ wɔyɛ deɛ wɔpɛ biara wɔ satan kwan so, yɛ deɛ wopɛ ne wɔn satan mmara nyinaa enti wɔn a wonni mmara no bɛyɛ hwee.

Monnte m’akwan ase, na m’akwan yɛ Yahweh akwan, na mene ɔkwan ne nokorɛ ne nkwa, na obiara nni hɔ a ɔnam so ba Agya no nkyɛn gye sɛ ɔnam me so na sɛ moahunu me a, moahunu Agya no. Nanso moato mo adwene mu, moasi mo aso, moapirim mo koma, na moabue mo ano wɔ abususɛm a ɛnni gyidie mu, na moaka abususɛm atia asomdwoeɛ apam a Yahweh de ama mo no mpo. Na me Yesu Kristo, mene mo asomdwoeɛ!

Nanso daabi, sɛ anka mobɛyɛ me Awurade no, na mmom mode me din kronkron, Yesu Kristo, bɔ kwa sɛ asɛmfua foforɔ a mode bɛda deɛ ɛyɛ fi a ɛwɔ mo ara mo koma mu no adi. Nanso morentumi ngu me din ho fi, na Yahweh ama me din a ɛkorɔn sene din nyinaa, sɛ Yesu Kristo din mu na nkotodwe nyinaa nkotodwe wɔ soro ne asase so. Na mobɛtua asɛm hunu biara a mobɛka no ho ka, na mo berɛ adu. Na afei, ɛsiane sɛ moato mo ho ahyɛ mo ankasa abodan mu na modwene sɛ mobɛtumi ahunta afiri Yahweh atemmuo ho nti, afei mɛto ɔsoro kwan no mu ama wɔn a wɔnni nokorɛ no berɛ a mɛtwe me nokwafoɔ akɔ me nkyɛn wɔ soro ɛfiri sɛ ayeforɔkunu no rebɛn.

Mo nkwaseasɛm mu no, modwene sɛ biribiara asan ayɛ sɛdeɛ ɛteɛ, na ɔko a ɛreba no, wɔbɛtumi ahwɛ no wɔ TV so te sɛ akoko foforɔ a mo mpanimfoɔ dii no ɔkwammɔne so no. Sɛdeɛ mo ani agye aman foforɔ owuo ne ɔsɛeɛ ho no, saa ara na wɔbɛsɛe mo man no pasaa, na mo owuo ne ɔsɛeɛ no bɛma wɔn a wɔsɛee wɔn no anya akatua wɔ mo pene so.

Mo nkwaseasɛm mu no, modwene sɛ mobɛtumi asiesie mo ho ama abufuo a ɛreba no denam asase so asetena so, baabi a ɛnni intanɛt so, gye sɛ wɔbɛsɛe mo pasaa, wɔakyere mo, wɔakum mo, wɔabɔ mo, wɔama kɔm ade mo, wɔayare mo, na ɔhaw bebree bɛto wɔn a wɔpo me Yeshua HaMashiach no nyinaa, na sɛ wopo me a, ɛkyerɛ sɛ wopo Onyankopɔn kɛseɛ a ɔte hɔ daa no, Yahweh ankasa na nnuru biara nni hɔ. Enti mommrɛ mmra me nkyɛn seesei!

Mo nkwaseasɛm mu no, monnte aseɛ sɛ mmɛn du no ne aboa a ɔfiri asase mu ne aboa a ɔfiri po mu a wɔnnya nyiniiɛ koraa no ayɛ adwuma, na mo man a ɛyɛ kɛseɛ pɛn no, wɔde ahyɛ wɔn nsa koraa ama wɔasɛe no pasaa. (Na sɛ wo man no sɛe pasaa a, mmoa no bɛsɔre!) Na Onyankopɔn de ahyɛ wɔn koma mu sɛ wɔnyɛ n’atirimpɔ.

W’atamfoɔ nneɛma atwa wo ho ahyia koraa, woama ɔpepem pii a wɔyɛ Asraafoɔ Kɔkɔɔ no, aboa no asraafoɔ no mpo, aba wo nsase so. Woama wɔayɛ nsɛmmɔnedie, wɔasɛe wo nneɛma a wɔde ma wo no, wɔasɛe w’aduane, wɔasɛe wo sikasɛm, wɔayɛ nsra a wɔde bɛyi, ayɛ ayayadeɛ na wɔakum obiara a ɔmfata wɔn atirimpɔ ho. Monhu sɛ saa berɛ no mpo no, obiara a ɔbɔ me din, Yesu Kristo, frɛ no kɔsi awieeɛ no, wɔbɛgye no nkwa. Wɔn a wɔyɛ wɔn apo a etwa toɔ no bɛtena daa wɔ gyidie a wonni no so konsequensiɛse mu wɔ ogya kɛseɛ a wɔasiesie ama ɔbonsam ne n’abɔfoɔ no mu, na adwenkyerɛ foforɔ biara nni hɔ.

Metu wɔn a wɔkenkan nsɛm yi nyinaa fo sɛ wɔmmɛn me. Monnyae mo akyinnyegye a nteaseɛ nni mu no na monnyae mo ntam akasakasa no na monka nkwagyeɛ asɛmpa no sɛdeɛ wɔatwerɛ no wɔ kyerɛwsɛm mu no na monka me nsɛm, me nsɛm no mpo wɔ sekan a ɛtwa toɔ yi mu. Monka no mo ntam, na monsiesie mo ho daa sɛ mobɛboa wɔn a wɔhia mmoa. Monnyae mo nuanom mmarima ne mmaa atemmuo na mmom mommoa mo ho mo ho wɔ ɔdɔ mu na monnyina adom ne mmɔborɔhunu a mede ama no mu, na ɛdɔɔso.

Monka mo bɔne na monnu mo ho na monyɛ no seesei, monni berɛ! Monfa me, Yesu Nasareni, nyɛ mo Awurade, na berɛ asa. Mommrɛ mmra me nkyɛn seesei, na din foforɔ biara nni hɔ a wɔde ma nkwagyeɛ, gye me deɛ, Yesu Kristo. Mommrɛ mmra me nkyɛn seesei na momfiri amumuyɛ mu na mɛgye mo ato me ho so sɛ mode gyidie a ɛhyɛ koma ma mu bɛpae mu aka sɛ mene Awurade na monim sɛ wɔanyane me afiri awufoɔ mu sɛ merenwu bio. Matrɛ me nsa a abue mu akyerɛ wiase a ɛrewu mfeɛ pii a atwam ni na adom ne mmɔborɔhunu berɛ reyɛ aba awieeɛ, ɛwom sɛ adom ne mmɔborɔhunu wɔ me hɔ daa deɛ, na meyɛ Awurade a ɔyɛ ɔdomfoɔ.

Na afei, moretwɛn wɔ sekan a ɛtwa toɔ yi mu, O ahintasɛm Babilon. Na mpofirim na ɛbɛsi. Mpofirim na ogya bɛtɔ, mpofirim na nsuo bɛkata wo ntadeɛ a ɛwɔ w’asen so no so, O ɔbaa buulufoɔ kɛseɛ, mpofirim na owosoɔ kɛseɛ bɛba wo so na ɛbɛtetɛ wo mu, mpofirim na wɔbɛma ahyɛdeɛ no na ɔpepem pii bɛbubu asase no so abɛkum onipa biara a ɔte ase a ɔmfata wɔn atirimpɔ ho. Wɔrenhu mmɔbɔ, wɔnnu wɔn ho, wɔrentumi nsrɛ wɔn, wonni ayamhyehyeɛ, na mmom wɔsom Satan na wɔbɛdi atirimpɔ a wɔde ama wɔn sɛ wɔnni so no so, na wɔbɛdi Onyankopɔn abufuo so wɔ Ahintasɛm Babilon so na ɔrensan nsɔre bio.

Hwɛ! Mmerɛ no adu. Monnu mo ho berɛ a motumi home, na mpofirim na ɛbɛsi. Montie me nsɛm na monte ase: Deɛ ɔsi ano kwan no nko ara ne tumi a ɛsi saa bɔne yi ano kwan, na berɛ a modwene sɛ mowɔ no yɛ berɛ a wɔama kwan sɛ wɔde bɛyɛ nhyehyɛeɛ na wɔde nneɛma no akɔ baabi a ɛsɛ sɛ ɛkɔ wɔ wiase pon so na saa mmerɛ yi som Yahweh atirimpɔ.

Afei mpofirim mɛboaboa nokwafoɔ a wɔwɔ m’Asɔre mu no ano sɛ wɔne me nhyia wɔ mununkum no mu na mɛtwe m’Ayeforɔ akɔ ne dan mu, na m’Agya fie adan pii wɔ hɔ na mekɔsiesiee baabi maa wo m’Ayeforɔ na ɔsoro asiesie ne ho ama wo!

Na mpofirim, sɛ wɔde m’Agya agyapadeɛ a ɛsom bo no sie ɔsoro adekoradan mu yie a (na ɛbɛsi wɔ anibu kakra mu) yod biara ne yod biara nsɔeɛ bɛba mu, asɛm biara a mekaeɛ no mpo denam me nokware nkɔmhyɛfoɔ ne asomafoɔ so, na wɔdɔɔso na wɔn a wɔyɛ me deɛ no pii ka adwene baako na wɔka sɛdeɛ me kyerɛwsɛm teɛ. Monhu yei, wɔn a wɔpo Nsɛm a ɛda adi pefee wɔ kyerɛwsɛm mu a ɛfa m’Asɔre a wɔboaboa ano sɛ wɔne me bɛhyia wɔ mununkum mu no ho, wɔn a wɔpa no ara nso bɛpa wɔn na wɔde wɔn ho bɛhyɛ abufuo mu berɛ a na ɛho nhia, nanso mo mu ahe na mobɛyɛ asoɔden apo me nne de agye wɔn a wɔkiti mo aso no atom? Nanso, saa ara na ɛbɛyɛ.

Sɛ obi anka sɛdeɛ me nsɛm teɛ, na me Kristo Yesu, mene Asɛm a ɛyɛɛ ɔhonam no, monnsrɛ no mpo sɛ Onyankopɔn nka ne ho na wɔnte me Nsɛm ase yie na me nkɔmhyɛfoɔ rentumi ne kyerɛwsɛm nni abirabɔ na merentumi ne me ho nni abirabɔ. Mene Yahweh nsɛm a ɛyɛɛ ɔhonam no, na mene Yeshua HaMashiach, Onyankopɔn Odwan a ɔbaa sɛ ɔbɛhoro adesamma bɔne ho no.

Mereba sɛ Yuda Abusuakuo no Gyata na m’abufuo ayɛ hye wɔ asase so, na tenenee mu na medi atɛn na medi ako. Monnu mo ho O asase so amumuyɔfoɔ na mo apo no bɛyɛ daa. Mfeɛ nson a Yakob haw no ba awieeɛ no, Wiase bɛhome na ahomegyeɛ da bɛba mfeɛ apem nso ɛyɛ sɛ da koro wɔ Yahweh ani so. Na Gyata no ne Odwan no bɛda ɛfiri sɛ Yuda Abusuakuo no Gyata no bɛwie m’atirimpɔ a ɛfa Onyankopɔn Odwan no ho no. Berɛ asa.

Me Yeshua HaMashiach de nsɛm yi amena me ɔsomfoɔ so, na me ɔsomfoɔ so na wɔde nsɛm yi amena!
