Hlala phantsi uthule wena serafi evukelayo!

Nonke nina bawileyo niya kwenza kuphela njengoko nivunyelwe ukwenza ehlazweni nasekoyisweni kwenu, kodwa iyure yenu ifikile nkosi encinci, kwaye ubona ngokucacileyo, nangona uxoka kakhulu, wena sirhubuluzi. Kodwa iindlela zakho ziphelile kwaye iNkathazo kaYakobi eyiveki yamashumi asixhenxe kaDaniyeli iya kucoca umhlaba kwiindlela zakho ngonaphakade: Ngaphandle kwexesha lakho lokugqibela elincinci lokuvukela uya kuqokelela umququ oseleyo oya kuhluma ngokutsha ekuvukeleni phantsi koLawulo loBulungisa lweVangeli engunaPhakade ngoku etyhiliweyo ngenxalenye kwiNcwadi eNcinci yeSityhilelo Isahluko se-10, kwanencwadi encinci evuliweyo endiyivule esandleni somkhonzi wam.

Kodwa kunjalo! Intsalela yeTestamente eNtsha iya kunikwa uYakobi oya kuhlala ezinyaweni zamangqina am amabini aya kufika ngequbuliso emazweni ngendlela ongenakuyiqikelela, wena nkosi encinci eyoyisiweyo. Kwaye iMithi yam emiBini yeMinquma, eziziPhatho zam zeZibane ezibini ziya kulungiselela kwaye zahlule kwanentsalela enamandla yengqolowa eya kuqubuda kuYeshua HaMashiach ngaloo ndlela isuse ubumfama obuyinxalenye kubantu bam bokwenyani abaya kwahlulwa kumdiza wakho! Kodwa intendelezo yabeentlanga iya kunyathelwa phantsi emfazweni echasene nabangcwele eqhutywa lirhamncwa eliphuma elwandle, elizibiza ngokuba ngumbane owela ezulwini ngesiHebhere, kwanekhowudiweyo njengoBarack Hussein Obama osebenza kunye nerhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni elinobuhlungu balo nokulunywa yinyoka esandleni eliya kudibanisa isinyithi nodongwe kodwa aziyi kunamathelana.

Kwaye uEnoki uMbhali noEliya umTishbhi baya kulungiselela ingqolowa yam ukuba isuswe, kwanengqolowa yam egcinwe ngonaphakade ngokhuseleko lomnqophiso nesithembiso koonyana abangalunganga bokungeva ngonaphakade ekuzalisekisweni kweTestamente yam eNtsha yoBulungisa apho iBhayibhile yam eNgcwele yayiyintlawulo yokuqala kuphela kwimpahla ethengiweyo eya kuhlala imi enyanisweni engunaphakade kuba ndiliLizwi lika-Adonai-YHVH elenziwe inyama, okwalisiseko senu esingunaphakade sosindiso, kwanelitye lenu lembombo eliyintloko, eliya kuthi kungekudala likhuphe umyalelo: Nesikhalo esikhulu! Kwaye olo luvuthelo lwexilongo luya kucula kwaye luvakalise ngesiquphe, ngamandla, nangokungalindelekanga ngoku ekubhiyozeleni abathembekileyo kuKristu, ukuze liqondwe kuphela ngabo balungiselelwe ubuninzi balo ekuguqukeni kobulungisa, abaneentambo zezibane ezithenwe ekuthobeleni iindlela zam!

Abo bevayo: Mabaqonde izandi zikhala elukholweni naseluxolweni, kuba nguwuphi uBawo owayenokunika inyoka kubantwana bakhe, ngaphandle kokuphosa phantsi kweenyawo zabo, ibotshwe ekoyisweni okuthulileyo ukuze ikhululwe ilawule abangathembekanga, kwananjengoko kuphela abathembekileyo kuKristu bemka ngesiquphe ngoku!

Kwaye xa ubuqaqawuli bokukhanya kwam buqhekeza esibhakabhakeni kwaye elo xilongo linamandla likhala kamnandi kangaka kwisimemo esilindelweyo soMtshakazi wam neeNdwendwe zethu esiya kukhala kwabathembekileyo kuKristu, kwanani nonke endinilungiselele ngoku kubuninzi belo xilongo linamandla likaThixo elilixilongo lokugqibela lexesha lam lobabalo eliza kuphela ngesiquphe nangokungalindelekanga: Yivani aMazwi bantu bam kuba umgcini nomkhululi wenu ngoku umi ekulungele ukunikhulula ngokupheleleyo Bandla lam, Mzimba wam kwanaye uMtshakazi wam, Vuyani!

Zilungiseleleni mtshakazi wam kwaye nihlale ecaleni kwendlela nezibane zenu zizele ngokupheleleyo yioyile yam ecocekileyo, elitywina loBulungisa nomoya woBunyana, ongumoya ongcwele wesithembiso ozelwe ngaphakathi komntwana ngamnye wokukhanya, abanentlawulo epheleleyo yaloo mpahla ithengiweyo, ekufuphi nokukhululwa ngokupheleleyo nguKristu Yesu, iNkosi yabo engunaPhakade ngamazwi acocekileyo obungqina bam nomoya wam kuba ingqumbo yam ayiyeyenu.

Ngoko ke, yekani imisebenzi yenu nize niphumle eluthandweni lwam bantu bam, kodwa yimani niqinile kwaye nivumele ikrele lomoya wam eliya kwahlula umququ wokugqibela ebomini benu zithandwa zam.

Zilungiseleleni zithandwa zam kuba inkululeko yenu ifikile kwaye kungekudala niya kuva isikhalo esikhulu kwaye niya kutsalelwa phezulu ngesiquphe ngoku nangoko kwigumbi letrone likaYHVH ngokwakhe, ngoko ke zilungiseleleni bantu bam kuba ndifika njengesela ebusuku hayi njengesiganeko esicwangcisiweyo, nangona kunjalo makume ukwahlula kwam kwaye nibale amanani ngokwamaZwi am aNgcwele neendlela zam; hayi abathakathi abakhonkothayo nabakrokrelayo, ningabotshelelwanga kwimihla engahambelaniyo kwikhalenda yenu engcolileyo kaGregory.

Yivani aMazwi am bemi bomhlaba kuba ndiyiNgonyama yeSizwe sakwaYuda kwaye ndiza kugweba ndenze imfazwe!

Yivani ilizwi lobulumko niguqe ngamadolo kwaye nive ilizwi eliphilayo likaYeshua HaMashiach, kuba ndiyiNgonyama yeSizwe sakwaYuda, kwaye ndinguKumkani wooKumkani neNkosi yeeNkosi! Kwanaye lowo uEl Shaddai, kwanaloo Thixo uSomandla, amphakamise kakhulu wamnika igama elingaphezu kwawo onke amagama. Ukuze ngeGama likaYesu onke amadolo aguqe, ezinto ezisezulwini, nezinto ezisemhlabeni, nezinto eziphantsi komhlaba: Kwaye zonke iilwimi zivume, ukuba uYesu Kristu uyiNkosi, kube luzuko kuYise weziKhanyiso, kwanaye uYahweh, uThixo ongunaphakade nowaphakade, kuba akukho namnye onjengoBawo wethu!

Yivani ilizwi leNkosi yenu kwaye niqonde izinto esele ziqhubeka nezinto eziza kwenzeka kungekudala, kuba yabonani ndinguye othetha ngezinto ezizayo ngokungathi sele zenzekile kuba ngokuqinisekileyo iNkosi yenu enkulu, kwanam Kristu Yesu oyena ubalaseleyo andiyi kwenza nto, ngaphandle kokuba ndityhile iimfihlo zam kubakhonzi bam abaProfeti. Kutheni na niphulaphula amazwi amaninzi kangaka aphikisanayo, kwaye kutheni nithandabuza phakathi kwezimvo ezimbini? Anditshongo na ukuba abaprofeti bam bathetha ngamxhelo mnye kwaye abasokuze baphikise amazwi am angcwele? Kuba ndiliLizwi elenziwe inyama elihlala phakathi kwenu ngomoya wam ongcwele ozelwe ngaphakathi kwenu ngonaphakade, kuba umphefumlo ngamnye ozelwe ngokutsha utywinwe ngaloo moya ungcwele wesithembiso oyimbewu yam engenakonakala kwaye itywina likaThixo ophilayo alinakwaphulwa. Kwaye umhla wokukhululwa kwempahla ethengiweyo usondele kwaye inkululeko yenu epheleleyo ilapha.

Itsho iNkosi yemikhosi, musani ukuva amazwi abaprofeti abaprofeta kuni, abanifundisa amampunge: bathetha umbono wentliziyo yabo, hayi ophuma emlonyeni weNkosi. Yizani kum bantwana bam kuba intambiso enayifumana kum, ihleli kuni: kwaye anidingi mntu ukuba anifundise: Kodwa, njengoko yona loo ntambiso inifundisa ngezinto zonke, kwaye iyinyaniso, kwaye ayibobuxoki: kwananjengoko inifundisile, niya kuhlala kum. Bantwana bam hlalani kum kwaye nithelekise amazwi akhe nabani na othi uthetha egameni lam nesibhalo kwaye nindicele ukuba ndiqinisekise emoyeni wenu, ukuba mhlawumbi niya kuliphulaphula ilizwi lam kuba uninzi lwenu lukhuni ukuva, kwaye uninzi lwenu luthanda amazwi obuxoki achukumisa iindlebe zenu ukuze nibonakale nizizilumko kodwa endaweni yoko nilandele amazwi obuxoki ezidenge ezingenantloni.

Yabonani! Ndithetha ngamaxesha enkathazo kaYakobi ekulungele ukuqhambuka kuwo wonke umhlaba. Kuba umgwebo sele uqhubeka kwaye awunakunqandwa, kuba oko uYahweh akubhengeza akukho mntu unokukutshintsha! Iinyembezi ezininzi, ezininzi ndizikhalile ngenxa yomgwebo weBhabheli eyiMfihlo kwaye ndithumele abaprofeti bam iminyaka emininzi, emininzi ngoku kwaye ndithumele nabasemzini abavela kumazwe angaziwayo ukuba bangqine kwaye baprofete ngezigwebo ezazisondela kodwa ngoku zilapha.

Kuba kuya kuba njalo kuba kubhaliwe kwathiwa: Iimpondo ezilishumi owazibona phezu kwerhamncwa, ezi ziya kumthiya umfazi olihenyukazi, zimshiye elubala, zihambe ze, zidle inyama yakhe, zimtshise ngomlilo. Kuba uThixo ufake ezintliziyweni zabo ukuba benze ukuthanda kwakhe, bavumelane, banikele ubukumkani babo kwirhamncwa, ade azaliseke amazwi kaThixo. Waza umfazi, owambonayo, nguloo mzi mkhulu ulawula ookumkani bomhlaba.

Yivani amazwi am, umgwebo weBhabheli eyiMfihlo sele uqhubeka. Iimpondo ezilishumi zilungiselele uhlaselo lwazo olothusayo kwaye uhlaselo lwazo luyasondela kwaye lusemnyango wenu. Kuba kwanamajelo enu afuna ukunithuthuzela nilale kwaye abaninzi bahleli ebumnyameni bengayiboni inyani besithi: Ndihleli njengoKumkanikazi kwaye andingomhlolokazi kwaye andiyi kubona zila. Ngomso kuya kuba njenganamhlanje, kuba namhlanje kunjengayizolo. Kangangoko ezukisa yena, ahlale eyonwabele, mnikeni intuthumbo nosizi olungako: Ngoko ke izibetho zakhe ziya kufika ngamhla mnye, ukufa, nosizi, nendlala, kwaye uya kutshiswa ngomlilo: kuba uYahweh omgxekayo, nguThixo uSomandla kwaye akukho mkhulu kwaye akukho nto inokumisa isandla esinamandla sikaEl Shaddai!

Kwaye ngoko ilanga liya kuphuma kungekudala ngomhla wentlekele eningayilindelanga, kwaye loo mhla usondele kunokuba nicinga. Kwaye loo mhla uya kuzisa intshabalalo epheleleyo kwiBhabheli eyiMfihlo, kuba inesabelo esiphindwe kabini somgwebo obhengezwa kuyo. Ulwaphulo-mthetho lwakhe nobuhenyu bakhe buninzi kwaye isohlwayo sakhe asilibazisi kwaye isohlwayo esikhulu siya kuwa phezu kwabangaguqukiyo kwananjengoko iBandla lam elithembekileyo kwanaye uMtshakazi wam othembekileyo baya kuqokelelwa kum ngesiquphe emafini kwaye basindiswe kwingqumbo, kwanenkathazo enkulu yeveki yamashumi asixhenxe kaDaniyeli. IBhabheli eNkulu iwile, iwile! Yivani amazwi am kuba ndithetha ngokungathi sele yenzekile kuba akukho nto iya kutshintsha, icothise, ithintele, okanye imise izigwebo zikaYahweh ekuweni emhlabeni.

Ngeyure enye iya kutshiswa ngomlilo, ezinye zezixeko zayo ziya kutsha, ezinye zezixeko zayo ziya kukhukuliswa ngogonyamelo, iinqaba zayo ziya kuwa ziqhekeke zibe ziingceba ezininzi, iibhulorho zayo ziya kuwa, kwaye kuya kubakho ukushukuma okuninzi. Umhlaba uya kukrazulwa ngogonyamelo kwaye umhlaba omninzi uya kugutyungelwa ngamanzi anogonyamelo. Iziketi zakhe ziya kugutyungelwa ukusuka empuma nokusuka entshona ngamaza amakhulu kwaye phezulu phakathi amaza agijimayo nokushukuma okunogonyamelo. Oko kuseleyo kuya kuba yinkqantosi kwaye akasayi kuphinda avuke, kuba esi sizwe samazwe amaninzi nezixeko ezininzi asisayi kuphinda sivuke kwaye kuya kuba kukhulu ukuwa kwaso! Kuba umhlaba yoweNkosi nokuzala kwawo, kwaye zonke iinkalo zempembelelo yawo embi ziya kucinywa ngonaphakade ezintlangeni ngexesha leveki yamashumi asixhenxe kaDaniyeli, kuba ndiya kuwucoca umhlaba ngengqumbo yam. Mna, Kristu Yesu, iNgonyama yeSizwe sakwaYuda, ndiwabhengeze la mazwi, kwaye ukulungiselela ukuzalisekiswa kwawo sele kuqhubeka kwaye kuya kuzalisekiswa ngequbuliso ngeyure enye.

Kwaye ngoko imfazwe iya kuqhambuka kuwo wonke umhlaba njengenxalenye yohlaselo olothusayo kwiBhabheli eyiMfihlo, kuba oku kucwangcisiwe kwaye kulungelelaniswe iminyaka emininzi ngoku, kuba imfihlelo yobugwenxa ibisebenza ukusukela kwiintsuku zakudala kwaye iya kufikelela ekugqityweni okupheleleyo xa umthinteli esusiwe njengoko abathembekileyo bam bethathwa basiwa emazulwini. Imfazwe iya kuwa kuwo wonke umhlaba kwaye iya kufika kubo bonke abantu nazo zonke iintlanga, kuba le yimfazwe yesithathu enjalo, njengoko abawileyo befuna ukutshabalalisa iiodolo ezindala zehlabathi kwaye batshintshe zonke izinto ngeodolo yabo entsha, kwaneyodolo yeNamba. Irhamncwa eliphuma elwandle nerhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni ziya kuvuka ngomsindo omkhulu, kwaye ziya kulwa nabangcwele, kuba kunikiwe kuzo ukuba zoyise abangcwele. Kuya kuba njalo ukuba bonke abangabathandi ubomi babo kwaye babize egameni leNkosi kude kube sekupheleni baya kusindiselwa kuvuyo olungunaphakade kude kube ngonaphakade kanaphakade. Kwaye mna Yesu waseNazarete ndishiye umzekelo ongunaphakade wokuthobela kude kube sekufeni, kuba ukuthobela kwam uYahweh kwakukukufa komnqamlezo njengoko ndeva ubunzima ndahlawulela izono zoluntu njengemvana kaThixo kwaye yabonani ndiza njengeNgonyama yeSizwe sakwaYuda kwaye andiyi kunqandwa okanye ndithintelwe!

Kwaye ngoko abaprofeti bam ababini abayimithi emibini yeminquma neziphatho zezibane ezibini ezimi phambi koThixo womhlaba baya kuprofeta iinyanga ezingama-42 bembathiswe amalaphu okuzila, kuba bathathwa basiwa emazulwini ngamaxesha akudala. Kwaye ukuba kukho umntu oya kubenzakalisa, kuphuma umlilo emlonyeni wabo, utshabalalise iintshaba zabo: kwaye ukuba kukho umntu oya kubenzakalisa, kufuneka abulawe ngale ndlela. Aba banamandla okuvala izulu, banqande imvula ngeentsuku zokuprofeta kwabo: kwaye banamandla phezu kwamanzi ukuwajika abe ligazi, kwaye babulale umhlaba ngazo zonke izibetho, rhoqo njengoko bethanda.

Obabo bubungqina obungunaphakade, yaye baya kuvakalisa iindaba ezilungileyo ezingunaphakade, yaye baya kuthetha ngezicwangciso zikaYahweh kuwo onke amaxesha, yaye baya kundithethela mna Krestu Yesu, yaye iimfihlelo ezininzi baya kuzityhila yaye baya kuguqula iintliziyo zabaninzi zibuyele kuYise weziKhanyiso ongenaguquguquko nasithunzi sakujika. Baya kungqina ngam mna Yeshua HaMashiach kuba ndim lo uYahweh amthumele kwaSirayeli ukuze azalisekise izithembiso ezenziwa kooyise, yaye ndinguye uMesiya wenene ngoku nangonaphakade yaye ubukumkani bam abunaziphelo! Kuba ngawaphi amaxesha apho uYahweh azishiye engenabungqina? AKUZANGE kubekho xesha apho uYahweh azishiye engenabungqina, yaye bonke abamiselwe usindiso olungunaphakade baya kuza basindiselwe kubunaphakade obungunaphakade novuyo olungunaphakade, kuba ukuzaliseka kwezinto zonke sele kuqhubeka.

Ngoko ke irhamncwa eliphuma elwandle liya kuphakama kunye nerhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni, elikwangumprofeti wobuxoki. Yaye liya kuvula umlomo walo lithethe izinyeliso ezikhulu ngoYahweh ukuze linyelise igama lakhe nomnquba wakhe nabo bahlala ezulwini. Yaye liya kunikwa ukuba lilwe nabangcwele liboyise yaye liya kunikwa amandla okulawula yonke imizalwane, neelwimi, neentlanga kuba liya kukhokela ihlabathi. Irhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni liya kulawula nomhlaba njengoMoshiach wabo wobuxoki yaye liya kuba ngumakhi wetempile yabo. Irhamncwa eliphuma elwandle liya kulawula ngokwemiyalelo yabo yobunyelisi kaNowahide njengoko befuna ukutshayela yonke into enento yokwenza noThixo wenene nonyana wakhe okuphela kwamzeleyo uYesu Krestu ebusweni bomhlaba.

Irhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni liya kubangela ukuba umhlaba unqule irhamncwa, elalinexeba elibulalayo elaphiliswayo. Yaye liya kwenza imimangaliso emikhulu ukuze likhohlise abo bahlala emhlabeni, kuba liya kuvunyelwa ukuba lenze imiqondiso emininzi yobuxoki nemimangaliso, yaye liya kubangela ukuba umlilo uhle emhlabeni emehlweni abantu yaye liya kukhokelela bonke abantu ukuba bamangaliswe ngabo. Kodwa lingumchasi, yaye liyaziphakamisa ngaphezu kwayo yonke into ekuthiwa nguThixo, okanye enqulwayo: ukuze lihlale njengoThixo eTempileni kaThixo, lizibonakalisa ukuba linguThixo.

Yaye liya kwenza umfanekiso werhamncwa yaye liya kubangela ukuba bonke banqule umfanekiso eliya kuwenza. Yaye liya kuvunyelwa ukuba linike umoya kumfanekiso werhamncwa ukuze umfanekiso werhamncwa uthethe yaye uya kubangela ukuba bonke abangayi kunqula umfanekiso werhamncwa babulawe. Yaye liya kwenza bonke, abazizityebi nabangamahlwempu, abakhululekileyo namakhoboka, ukuba bamkele uphawu lwalo esandleni sabo sokunene okanye emabunzini abo.

UKUBA NABANI NA UYANQULA IRHAMNCWA NOMFANEKISO WALO, AZE AMKELE UPHAWU LWALO EBUNZINI LAKHE, OKANYE ESANDLENI SAKHE, YENA UYA KUSELA KWIWAYINI YENGQUMBO KATHIXO, EGALELWE INGENAMXUBE ENDEBENI YOMSINDWANO WAKHE, YAYE UYA KUTHUTHUNJISWA NGOMLILO NESULFARE, PHAMBI KWEZITHUNYWA EZINGCWELE, NAPHAMBI KWEMVANA: YAYE UMSI WENTUTHUMBO YABO UNYUKA USE ENAPHAKADENI KANAPHAKADE. YAYE ABANAKUPHUMULA EMINI NASEBUSUKU, ABO BANQULA IRHAMNCWA NOMFANEKISO WALO, NAYE NABANI NA OWAMKELA UPHAWU LWEGAMA LALO.

Ihlabathi elikhoyo ngoku alisoze liphinde libekho, yaye ubukumkani obunyukayo beNamba buya kukhahlelwa phantsi ngokupheleleyo, njengoko ubukumkani bukaSathana businga esiphelweni sabo sokugqibela, kuba bangabahlali-mkhukwini yaye abangookumkani. Yaye eli lihlabathi abaninzi abaya kuzifumana bekulo, nkqu nabo basindayo kwiintlekele ezizayo nemfazwe. Musani ukuthatha uphawu bantu bam, kuba baya kuza benikela ngoxolo, baya kuza benikela ngokhuseleko, baya kunixelela ukuba konke kungenxa yokulunga kwenu. Baya kuphehlelela oko bakubiza ngokuba ngoobhubhane kuba sele becebile ukweyisela ihlabathi ukuba liyagula. Yaye zonke iindidi zezifo nezibetho ziya kwanda kuba zinjalo iziphumo zabakhweli-mahashe abazayo yaye baya kukhwela!

Baya kunikela ngempilo nokuphiliswa kwizigwebo zikaThixo, endaweni yokuba umntu aguquke ebubini bakhe. Baya kunikela ngokungena kuluntu lwabo kwizigwebo ezoyikekayo eziza kuqhambuka kuwo wonke umhlaba. Kuba mna, Krestu Yesu, ndiya kunixelela ngaphambi kokuba kwenzeke ukuze niyazi inyaniso yamazwi am nokuze nikwazi ukuhamba kumanzi anobungozi apho abaninzi baya kuzifumana bekubo, nkqu nabo banesibindi sokubiza egameni lam kude kube sekupheleni, kuba baziindlalifa zosindiso olungunaphakade. Kuba bonke ababiza kuYesu waseNazarete njengeNkosi yabo, nkqu nabo bonke ababiza egameni lam elinamandla yaye bengabathandi ubomi babo kude kube sekupheleni, baya kusindiselwa kubomi obungunaphakade.

Anifundanga na? Aba ngabo baya kuphuma kwimbandezelo enkulu, yaye bahlambe iingubo zabo, bazenza mhlophe egazini leMvana. Ngoko ke, baphambi kwetrone kaThixo, yaye bamkhonza imini nobusuku eTempileni yakhe: yaye lowo uhleli eTroneni uya kuhlala phakathi kwabo. Abayi kuphinda balambe, abayi kuphinda banxanwe, nelanga aliyi kubatshisa, nabuphi na ubushushu. Kuba iMvana, ephakathi kwetrone, iya kubondla, yaye iya kubakhokelela kwimithombo ephilayo yamanzi: yaye uThixo uya kosula zonke iinyembezi emehlweni abo.

Nangona kunjalo, nangona isihlwele sezihlwele siya kusindiswa kwiveki yamashumi asixhenxe kaDaniyeli yaye usindiso luya kuza kwaSirayeli kuba intsalela isaya kuguqa kum mna Yeshua HaMashiach, (Kuba ndinguye uMesiya wenene apho imvana kaThixo iya kunika indlela kwiNgonyama yeSizwe sakwaYuda, yaye zombini ziya kuzalisekiswa ngemvisiswano engunaphakade kum, kuba kum Krestu Yesu iNgonyama iya kulala phantsi ngoxolo nemvana.) okulusizi, kwaye ngokungeyomfuneko, uninzi lwehlabathi luya kumangaliswa yaye lulandele aba balawuli barhamncwayo, kuba baya kuthakatha abaninzi. Ngobugqwirha babo nokuthakatha kwabo, baya kuhenda ihlabathi ukuba lamkele ityhefu yabo, yaye baya kubatyhefa ngonaphakade abo bathanda ihlabathi ngobugqi babo, kuba uphawu lwerhamncwa luya kuzifihla njengeyeza. Nkqu nokulunywa yinyoka apho inkqubo yabo sele isunguliwe yaze yavavanywa, yaye izihlwele sele ziqheliselwe ukwamkela uphawu lwabo yaye umsebenzi wazo wembewu yemfazwe waba yimpumelelo.

Ngoko ke inkqubo yabo ilungele ixesha eliphambili, yaye balungiselele amanqaku abo oluntu yaye bonke abahlala ezixekweni zabo nakuluntu lwabo baya kulawulwa. Kuba kuya kufuneka bathathe uphawu lwerhamncwa ukuze bathathe inxaxheba kuluntu lwabo lobusathana, nkqu nobukumkani beNamba. Abo bathatha uphawu baya kutshintshwa ngonaphakade. Imfuzo yabo kanye iya kutshintshwa, kuba baya kuzixuba nembewu yabantu: kodwa abayi kunamathelana, njengoko intsimbi ingaxutywa nodongwe. Uphawu lwabo loburhamncwa, nkqu nokulunywa yinyoka, luya kubatshabalalisa ngokupheleleyo bonke abathatha ityhefu yabo yaye akukho kusinda.

Nkqu nabo bonke abathatha uphawu baya kutshintshwa ngonaphakade, yaye baya kuzifumana beyinxalenye yenkqubo yabo, yaye baya kudityaniswa bubuchwepheshe yaye iingqondo zabo azisayi kuba zezabo. Baya kulawulwa yaye baya kuba njengabafileyo abahambayo, baya kuba ngamakhoboka ngonaphakade kwingqondo edibeneyo yenkqubo yabo, nkqu nobuxhakaxhaka babo kuba baya kudityaniswa ne-intanethi yezinto ezizayo. Baya kuphulukana nokuzikhethela kwabo, yaye abayi kuba nazimvakalelo yaye nantoni na kaThixo iya kucinywa ngonaphakade kubo kuba baya kuzitshintsha babe kwimeko engenakuhlangulwa, yaye baya kuzidibanisa ngokupheleleyo nangonaphakade nabawileyo yaye ngonaphakade baya kuba ngabenamba yaye baya kuya kwichibi elikhulu lomlilo ngonaphakade kanaphakade. Yaye kuya kuba njalo isiphelo abazikhethele sona.

Kuba isigqibo sabo sokugqibela asinakurhoxiswa. Yaye izibetho zabo nezilonda nezifo zabo ziya kuba ninzi, yaye nangona baya kuthenjiswa impilo noxolo, impilo noxolo abasayi kuze babe nazo. Kuba uSathana uzama ukusebenzisa ubugqi bakhe ukulwa nengqumbo nezigwebo zikaThixo nokutyhefa nokubulala izihlwele, kodwa abayi kuba nasiqabu yaye abo bathatha inxaxheba kubugqi bakhe abasayi kuze basinde. Abo bathatha uphawu baya kukhanya phantsi kwezibane zabo ezimnyama, yaye izibane zabo ziya kulayina izitrato zezixeko zabo abazilungiselele amakhoboka abo. Qonda ukuba aba baya kuba ngabalahliweyo abahambayo abangenaxolelo nabangenakuze bachache, kuba aba ngabo amagama abo angabhalwanga eNcwadini yoBomi yeMvana, eyaxhelwayo kwasekusekweni kwehlabathi. Yaye akukho lusindiso luya kuze lufumaneke phakathi kwabo okanye kuluntu lwabo, kuba ukuhamba phakathi kwabo nokujoyina uluntu lwabo kuya kufuna ngokupheleleyo uphawu lwerhamncwa.

Amangqina am amabini aya kungqina de amaxesha obungqina bawo agqitywe kuba kunikwe iinyanga ezingama-42. Yaye kuya kunikwa irhamncwa elinyuka liphuma enzonzobileni ukuba lilwe nabo, yaye liya kuboyisa, libabulale, nkqu nemithi yam yeminquma emibini, nezibane ezibini xa begqibile ubungqina babo. Yaye izidumbu zabo ziya kulala esitratweni sesixeko esikhulu, esithi ngokomoya yiSodom neYiputa, apho neNkosi yenu yabethelelwa khona. Yaye abangendawo baya kwenza umbhiyozo omkhulu baze banikane izipho ngenxa yokufa kwabo. Yaye emva kweentsuku ezintathu enesiqingatha uMoya wobomi ovela kuThixo uya kungena kubo: yaye baya kuma ngeenyawo zabo, yaye uloyiko olukhulu luya kuwela phezu kwabo bonke abababonayo. Yaye beva ilizwi elikhulu livela ezulwini, lisithi kubo, nyukelani apha. Yaye banyukela ezulwini ngefu, yaye iintshaba zabo ziya kubabona benyukela emazulwini kubomi obungunaphakade yaye ngonaphakade baya kutshintshwa. Yaye iNgelosi yesixhenxe iya kuvakala, yaye kuya kubakho amazwi amakhulu ezulwini, esithi, Ubukumkani beli hlabathi bube bubukumkani beNkosi yethu, noKrestu wayo, yaye iya kulawula ngonaphakade kanaphakade.

(Banoyolo abafileyo abafela eNkosini, ukususela ngoku, ewe, utsho uMoya, ukuze baphumle emisebenzini yabo, yaye imisebenzi yabo iyabalandela.) Yaye ngoko ke imbandezelo enkulu kunayo yonke iya kuqala, nangona imbandezelo ihleli ikhona ngexesha leveki yamashumi asixhenxe kaDaniyeli. Yaye uya kuqinisekisa umnqophiso nabaninzi iveki enye: yaye phakathi evekini uya kubangela ukuba umbingelelo nomnikelo uphele, yaye ngenxa yokusasazeka kwamasikizi, uya kuyenza ibe yintlango, kude kube sekuzalisekeni okumiselweyo kuya kugalelwa phezu kwentlango.

Kuba aba ngabo bathathwayo ekuhlanganisweni kwabangcwele kum, Krestu Yesu, emoyeni ekukhaleni kwexilongo lokugqibela yaye ixilongo sele liza kukhala.

Aba ngabam abathembekileyo, nkqu nabo kwakungafunyanwanga buqhetseba kubo, kuba bazihlambulule njengentombi enyulu egazini leMvana endalinikela ngonaphakade njengombingeleli wabo omkhulu, kuba ndingumbingeleli omkhulu ngonaphakade ngokohlobo lukaMelkitsedeke. Aba ngumtshakazi wam, yaye baya kundilandela naphi na apho ndiya khona. Yabonani! Ndiya kubenza babe namandla, yaye akukho nto yakha yabonwa emhlabeni enokuthelekiswa nezakhono endiya kubambathisa ngazo. Baya kwambathiswa ngelinen ecikizekileyo, emhlophe, engenabala yaye izakhono zabo ziya kubonakalisa impumelelo yabo yaye baya kuba nezakhono ezikhulu kuba bathembekile babizwa yaye bakhethwa nkqu nangaphambi kokusekwa kwehlabathi nguYahweh ngezi njongo.

Baya kukhokelelwa kukhuseleko kwizibetho ezisixhenxe kuba kwezi zigwebo kukho ubukhali bengqumbo kaThixo eza kugalelwa esihlalweni serhamncwa nokuthuthumbisa ubukumkani benamba kuba sikhulu isohlwayo sabo. Yaye abali-144,000 baya kubakhokelela kukhuseleko, baya kubaphilisa kumanxeba abo, baya kubasiza yaye babanike ithemba elikhulu lokubiza egameni leNkosi kude kube sekupheleni ukuba kukho imfuneko.
