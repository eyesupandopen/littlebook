Iindudumo ezi-7

Ndive Kwaye Undive Kakuhle!

Ndive kwaye undive kakuhle, nina baprofeti bobuxoki eningaligqwetha iLizwi lam elingcwele, kuba aniveli kum, kwaye aninawo umoya wam ongcwele, kwaye nihlasele uMtshakazi wam, kwaelo Bandla lam endilibizileyo! Yivani kwaye nithobele isilumkiso sam, Owu, nina sizwe esingakholwayo nesigwenxa nesiphambukileyo, kuba niya kuphendula ngaso sonke isenzo esikhohlakeleyo! Yivani amazwi am esilumkiso, nina nilahlekisa iBandla lam, kuba ingqumbo yam iyatshisa ngani, kuba ndiyiMvana kaThixo! Kuba abaninzi benu baya kuzimela emiqolombeni, nasemaweni eentaba. Kwaye niya kuthi kwiintaba nasemaweni, wani phezu kwethu, nisifihle ebusweni balowo uhleli etroneni, nakwingqumbo yeMvana: Kuba umhla omkhulu wengqumbo yakhe ufikile, kwaye ngubani na oya kuba nako ukuma?

Kuba ihlabathi liya kukubona ukubonakala kwam, kwaye ukuzamazama okukhulu kuya kufika kubo bonke, kwaye intshabalalo yesiquphe iya kulifumana ihlabathi njengoko uMtshakazi wam exhwilwa esusiwa esiyiswa kukhuseleko. Kuba ndiya kumsusa umthinteli, kwaye bonke ububi buya kukhululwa ngokupheleleyo, kwaye umgwebo omkhulu uya kuwiswa uvela emazulwini, kanye njengoko kubhaliwe kuloo ncwadi inkulu yokutyhilwa kwam, kwa yona incwadi yeSiTyhilelo! Ngoko ke ndivakalisa umgwebo phezu kwenu, nonke eningcolisa iBandla lam: imihla yenu ibaliwe, kwaye kungekudala iintliziyo zenu ziya kunityela xa ingqumbo yam ityhilwa. Nina baprofeti bobuxoki abangenaxabiso abangenelele kwiBandla lam, nifundise ubugqwirha obuxutyiweyo nobuhedeni neLizwi lam elingcwele neendlela zam ezingcwele, kwaye niya kuhlawula.

Nibangele abancinane bam ukuba bone kwaye bathathwe ziinkathazo ezinkulu, endiya kuzibuyisela kuni ngekhulu ekunigwebeni kwam. Niwachaze ngokutsha amagama kwizibhalo zam ezingcwele, nizenze ezingeyonyani izibhalo zam ezingcwele ngokuzibhala ngokutsha kwaye nitshintshe amazwi kaThixo ongunaPhakade, kwa amazwi kaYahweh, kwaye niya kuhlawula ngonaphakade. Ningenise imibhalo eyonakeleyo kwiBandla lam ngendlela efanayo neyokuba ookhokho benu abangenaxabiso batyhefele amaqula kwaye batyhefele kwa ukutya okunikelwe ukuxhasa uluntu, kwaye niya kuhlawula ngonaphakade. Lonke ilizwi elingenamsebenzi enilithethileyo, yonke imali yokugqibela emdaka eniyiqwebela intshabalalo yenu, yonke ingcinga embi en penakhe nayicinga, yonke inkuthazo embi en penakhe nayilandela iya kuboniswa kuni, kwaye niya kuhlawula, kude kube yeyona peni yokugqibela; kwaye aniyi kuba nako nangayiphi na indlela ukuma phambi kwam, kwaye niya kuthatha indawo yenu emalangatyeni uBawo awulungiselele uMtyholi neengelosi zakhe.

Kodwa qondani ukuba aningommangaliso kwaye akukho zimanga kuni, kuba ningabapostile bobuxoki, abasebenzi abakhohlisayo, niziguqula nibe ngabapostile bakaKristu. Kwaye akukho mmangaliso, kuba uSathana ngokwakhe uguqulwa abe yingelosi yokukhanya. Ngoko ke, asiyonto inkulu ukuba abakhonzi bakhe nabo baguqulwe njengabakhonzi bobulungisa, abasiphelo sabo siya kuba ngokomsebenzi wabo. Kuba ndabhala njalo kwabaseKorinte!

Niya kuqhubeka nemisebenzi yenu nizalisa izono zenu ngokupheleleyo kwaye nizalise unaphakade ngokupheleleyo ngesohlwayo nentuthumbo yenu, kuba umsindo wam unamandla ngani, kwaye niya kudibana nooyihlo emlilweni. Kuba ningumhloin phakathi kwengqolowa, kwaye ndiya kunincothula ngokupheleleyo; kuba uBawo, kwa uYahweh, uvakalisile ukuba anisoze nibe neengcambu okanye ilifa, kuba imbewu yenu yeyomkhohlakali, kwaye nonke ningababulali namaxoki. Kuba ndathini na kooyihlo baseJudiya kule minyaka emininzi yeeJubili edlulileyo? Nina ningaboyihlo uMtyholi, kwaye niya kwenza iinkanuko zoyihlo: wayengumbulali kwasekuqaleni, kwaye akazange ahlale enyanisweni, kuba akukho nyaniso kuye. Xa ethetha ubuxoki, uthetha okwakhe: kuba ulixoki, noyise wabo.

Yazini oku, nonke nina nizelwe kunyeliso, kwa unyeliso oluchasene noYahweh njengoko ooyihlo abangenaxabiso bazama ukuguqula umfanekiso kaThixo, awadalwa ngawo uAdam, ube ngamasikizi enzelwe intshabalalo yoluntu, imihla yenu iphelile. Kuba nguwuphi na kubaprofeti abangazange batshutshiswe ngooyihlo? Kwaye bababulala abo babexela ngenxa engaphambili ngokuza koLungileyo; enithe ngoku naba ngabangcatshi nababulali bakhe. Aniyi kusinda, nokuba ningaya kude kangakanani na emhlabeni ukuze nizimele kwingqumbo yam; ingqumbo yam iya kunifumana, kuba akukho nto inokufihlwa emehlweni am, kwaye umhlaba ngowam nokuzala kwawo, kuba ndiwuthenge ngegazi lam elo ookhokho benu balichitha ngokunyelisa, bade batyikitya isiphelo senu emililweni. Kwa mna, Kristu Yesu, lowo unyusiweyo wazukiswa, ndiyeza kuni, kwaye aniyi kusinda.

Ndive, Owu, nina bawileyo, kwa nonke nina enandihlabayo, kuba natsho ngaphakathi kwenu, simoyisile uYahweh kwaye simbulale unyana wakhe awamthuma ngokuchasene nathi ukuba oyise umhlaba, kwaye soyisile ngonaphakade, kuba uYeshua ufile, kwaye ngobudenge navuya! Nina zihula ezikratshayo ezingenakuqonda! Anizange niqonde ukuba uThixo uya kundivusa, ekhulule iintlungu zokufa: kuba kwakungenakwenzeka ukuba ndibanjwe kuko. Nalityumza nje isithende sam, kwaye aninamandla ngokuchasene nam, kwaye anizange niqonde ukuba Osenyangweni, kwa umdali wenu, uYahweh, wanibonisa nje iimfihlelo ezingenaxabiso!

Kuba wayesazi ukuba niya kumjikela yena nendalo yakhe kwaye niya kuzama ukuthatha oko wayekulungiselele mna, iNkosi uYesu Kristu. Abalulamileyo bam baya kuwudla ilifa umhlaba, kuba umhlaba ngowam nokuzala kwawo. Kuba nifundise uluntu ulwazi olwalelweyo; nifundise imfazwe; nalahlekisa abafazi ekuhombiseni amehlo ngobuso obuqatyiweyo ukuze nizalisekise iinkanuko zenu kubo; nawonakalisa uMyezo kaThixo ngokutyala umhloin phakathi kwengqolowa, kwaye wonke umhloin enawutyalayo kulo lonke ixesha uya kuncothulwa, ubotshwe, kwaye uphoswe emlilweni kunye nani! Kuba noyiswa xa ndazinikela ngokukhululekileyo ngenxa yosindiso loluntu, kwaye uThixo wandivusa kwabafileyo ukuze ndingaphindi ndife!

Kodwa naningalumkanga kangako ukuba ningaqondi ukuba ndiya kubuncama ubomi bam ngenxa yothando lukaThixo awaludalayo kum, kuba ndingumsindisi wehlabathi! Uthando lukaYahweh alunakuoyiswa, kuba uYahweh akanakoyiswa, kwaye akukho ndawo endalweni apho kukho isidalwa esifana naye, kuba nguye Osenyangweni, kwaye ukukhanya nobukhulu bakhe kunamandla onke ukusuka kunaphakade kuse kunaphakade! Kuba ndanixelela, "oku ukwenzayo kwenze ngokukhawuleza!" Ngokungazi nangekratshi anizange niqonde ukuba ndazivumela ukuba ndithathwe ngabaqeshwa benu abangenaxabiso abenza isenzo esibi sokubulala igazi elimsulwa! Ekungazini kwenu okukratshayo, anizange niqonde ukuba ndandinemikhosi yeengelosi elishumi elinambini endandiyilawula, kuba nangaliphi na ixesha ndandinokunimisa. Kodwa yayiya kwenzeka njani enye intando kaYahweh, ngaphandle kothando lwam nokuthobela kwam uBawo? Kuba uthando lukaYahweh akukho mntu unokuloyisa! Hayi, kungekho ezulwini kwaye kungekho emhlabeni, kungekho nangayiphi na indlela kwaye kungekho naphakade!

Nina bawileyo, kwa nina zikhulu zeli hlabathi liwileyo, nina Sathana, nonke niya kutsha emlilweni ongacimekiyo ngenxa yentiyo yenu engengcwele neendlela zenu ezikhohlakeleyo zokungathobeli nentshabalaliso. Njengoko nakwenzayo komnye wabancinane kwaba, bazalwana bam, nakwenza kum. Owu, nina balindi abawileyo, kwa nina zingelosi ezingathobeliyo ezashiya isikhundla senu sokuqala, aninalo uxolo! Niya kuphoswa emlilweni eningasoze nimke kuwo, kwaye niya kutsha ngokupheleleyo. Wonke umhloin wenu ongengcwele onyelisayo enawutyalayo ngokuwela imida kaYahweh kwiinguqulelo zenu zemfuzo phakathi komntu nengelosi, phakathi komntu nerhamncwa, phakathi komntu nezidalwa zaselwandle, phakathi kwezilwanyana zeentlobo ngeentlobo, kwa yonke into enyelisayo eniyenzileyo iya kuphoswa emlilweni kunye nani, kuba aninalo uxolo okanye uxolelo!

Nilandele ooSathana abazama ukutshabalalisa indalo kaYahweh, kude kube kwinqanaba lokundibulala mna, Yesu Kristu, xa ndazinikela ngokuzithandela ezandleni zakhe njengePasika egqibeleleyo yehlabathi, ngaloo ndlela ndityikitya inkululeko yoluntu kwaye ndityikitya isiphelo senu kunaphakade emililweni emikhulu engenakuze icinywe! Niya kuhlawula ngokupheleleyo zonke izono zenu namasikizi enu, kwaye sonke isenzo esibi en penakhe nasenza siya kujikwa phezu kwenu ngekhulu, kuba bonke baya kuvuzwa ngokwemisebenzi yabo. Kwaye zonke iindlela zenu ezimbi ziya kubuyiselwa ezintloko zenu amaxesha amaninzi, kuba nacinga ukuba ningandoyisa, kwaye ngekratshi nisacinga ukuba ningaphumelela kanti noyiswe ngokupheleleyo.

Yivani amazwi am, Owu, nyoka yamandulo, kuba umgwebo wakho uphezu kwakho kwaye ndiyabona uloyiko lwakho, kuba woyika ikamva lakho ngaphezu kwayo nayiphi na into, Owu, wena ngonyama engenamazinyo engenaxabiso! Impindezelo yeyam, kwaye ndiya kubuyisela, kwaye amaxesha enu afikile. Ingqumbo kaThixo nengqumbo yemvana sele iza kukhululwa njengoko ndiya kuqhekeza amatywina asixhenxe, njengoko iingelosi ezisixhenxe zithunyelwa ukuba zivakalise izigwebo zexilongo, kwaye iingelosi ezisixhenxe zithunyelwa ezinezibetho ezisixhenxe zokugqibela, kwaye ziya kukhululwa, kuba kuzo kuzaliswe ingqumbo kaThixo. Iintlungu ezininzi ziya kunifumana nonke, njengoko ndisohlwaya ngokupheleleyo kwaye ndichitha ubukumkani benu. Ukumkani wenu omkhulu ndiya kumoyisa nomprofeti wenu wobuxoki ndiya kumoyisa, kwaye baya kuphoswa bephila echibini lomlilo elivutha isulfure; kwaye apho baya kulinda ukuba nidibane nabo, kwaye apho niya kuhlala nigutyungelwe ngumlilo nesulfure, kwaye umgwebo wenu sele uza kuqala!

Mtshakazi wam, musa ukoyika! Amazwi am omgwebo ayingawenu kodwa ngawokukuthuthuzela, kuba ndikusindisile kwingqumbo esele iza kukhululwa, kwaye ndiya kukufihla kukhuseleko nomvuzo omkhulu, kwaye ngonaphakade uya kuba nam. Kuba ndikukhethile, kuba ndikubizile, kwaye uyeyona ntywenka yam inkulu, kwaye uyeyona ntywenka inkulu kaYahweh; ukwayiperile yexabiso elikhulu, kuba ndinikele konke kwam ngenxa yakho. Ndicebisa uMtshakazi wam ukuba afune ukuqonda aze eze kum ukuze afumane ubulumko, kuba ndiya kumfundisa. Kuba abanye benu balandele ezinye zeendlela zaba baprofeti bobuxoki; guqukani nje niguqukele eNkosini yenu, kwaye ndiya kunamkela.

Kuba banilukuhle ngobugqwirha bobuKristu, kwaye nikholelwe kubuxoki obuninzi enathengiselwa bona, naxa naxelelwa ukuba yinyaniso, kodwa bubuxoki obuyinqambi. La maqula angenamanzi azamile ukunitsala kum, kwaye oko akunakwenzeka, ngoko musani ukoyika, kuba andiyi kwaliwa! Ndiya kuqhubeka ndinikhulula ukuze nikhululeke ngenene, kwaye andiyi kuthuliswa! Ndinilwela ngeendlela ezininzi, ezininzi eningazaziyo. Kuba nonke naphawulwa ngaphambi kokuba nizalwe, kuba uBawo wayenazi nakwisibeleko sikanina, kwaye walinda ngamnye kuni ixesha elide eningenakuliqonda, kwaye nanisoloko nisesazulwini sentliziyo yakhe. Naphawulwa ekuzalweni, kwaye iingelosi zam zanigcina ngomyalelo wam kwaye zanilinda zanikhusela, kuba zithunyelwe njalo. Ngaba oku kuyakumangalisa? Ngaba uya kuphulaphula oko amaHebhere anokukuthetha? Kodwa kowuphi na kwiiNgelosi wakha wathi, hlala ngasekunene kwam, ndide ndenze iintshaba zakho isihlalo seenyawo zakho? Asizizo zonke na imimoya ekhonzayo, ethunyelwe ukuba ikhonze abo baya kuba ziindlalifa zosindiso?

Mtshakazi wam, niziindlalifa zosindiso kum, Yesu Kristu. Nibiziwe kum; nikhuselwe kum kwaye ndim; ninikwe ithuba lokuva intshumayelo yokholo ngam ukuze nindenze iNkosi yenu, kwaye ngoko ndinitywinile ngaloo moya ongcwele wesithembiso. Yazini ukuba itywina alinakuqhekeka, kwaye sele niza kukhululwa ngokupheleleyo kunaphakade, kuba aninalwazi ngobuninzi bomsebenzi wokudala endiza kuwugqibezela kuni, Mtshakazi wam! Yazini ukuba abawileyo nomhloin wabo banomona ngani, kwaye bayakuthiya nantoni na ayithandayo uYahweh, kwaye ndiya kuniphindezela ngokupheleleyo ezintloko zabo, kuba ndim lowo uya kutyumza intloko yenyoka yamandulo, kuba kuvakalisiwe, kwaye oko akuvakalisayo uYahweh akunakuguqulwa.

Akukho mntu unokunahlula kum; akukho bungakanani bobubi obunokuqhekeza elo tywina; ningabam kwaye ningabakaBawo, kuba konke uBawo andinika kona ndiya kukubuyisela kuThixo ukuze sibe yinto yonke kuyo yonke into! Kwaye undinike zonke izinto ezulwini nasemhlabeni, ngaphandle kwakwitrone yakhe mkhulu kunam!

Kwaye mna, Kristu Yesu, ndivakalisa la mazwi kuni, kwaye ndim, Yesu Kristu, ovakalisa umgwebo kwiintshaba zenu, kwaye ndiya kuwuzalisekisa kude kube yeyona peni yokugqibela. Mtshakazi wam, uthando lwam ngani alunacimi, kwaye ndinilungiselele indawo, kuba endlwini kaBawo kukho amagumbi amaninzi! Kungekudala kakhulu niya kutshata nam ngonaphakade, kwaye niya kuhlala ninam ngonaphakade, kwaye ekugqibeleni niya kuqonda ubukhulu benjongo yenu kum enidalelwe ukuyizalisekisa!

Kuba kum, uYahweh unikunike ikamva nenjongo, kwa injongo yeminyaka sele iza kutyhilwa kuni, kuba ndiyeza! Kwaye andiyi kwaliwa. Khangelani ukubonakala kwam. Khangelani ukukhanya kokuza kwam, kuba kusemnyango wokuphuma phezu komhlaba njengoko ndibonakala emafini ngesidumo esikhulu, ngelizwi lesiphatha-zingelosi, nangexilongo likaThixo, kuba ndinixelela inyaniso ukuba neengelosi zilindele elo xilongo sele liza kuvakala, kwaye zikulungele! Nilungele mna kwaye nikhangele, nilinde, niqwalasele njengoko kuyalelwe; kuba kungekudala niya kundibona nozuko lwam uBawo andambathise lona, kuba ndiya kunambathisa njalo, kuba niya kufana nam, kwaye niya kundibona njengoko ndinjalo.

NdiyiMvana kaThixo!

Ndiyeza kuzisa ingqumbo kubo bonke ubukumkani bukaSathana, kwaye abayi kusinda.

Ndingumsindisi weHlabathi!

NdinguKristu Yesu kwaye impindezelo yeyam kwaye ndiya kubuyisela!

Ndim lowo uya kutyumza intloko yenyoka, kuba uThixo uSomandla ukuvakalise oko! Sele ndiza kutyhilwa emafini ngenxa yoMtshakazi wam, endiya kumxhwila.

Ndim lowo uzayo ngehashe elimhlophe.

Ndim lowo ubizwa ngokuba nguThembekile noNyaniso, kwaye ngobulungisa ndiya kugweba ndenze imfazwe.
