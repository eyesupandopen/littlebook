Livani Ilizwi Lam!

ILizwi leNkosi lehla phezu kwam. Yivani ilizwi lam, bantwanana bam, nize ezingalweni zam ezoluliweyo. Yazini ukuba uthando lwam ngani aluqhawuki yaye alunakoyiswa, kuba luthando lukaBawo ekubeni enikile oonyana bakhe ubume bakhe, yaye kum kuhlala yonke inzaliseko. Ndinibizile ngamagama yaye ndinimemile ukuba nime phambi kwam. Izimvu zam ziyaliva ilizwi lam, njengoko zilazi ilizwi lomalusi wazo. Anikalivi na ilizwi lam? Nizivalile iindlebe zenu kwilizwi lam? Ndikhala kakhulu ezitratweni nasezitratweni ezimxinwa; ndiye ndatyelela neentolongo zenu ndazisa usindiso kwabo baxhatshazwayo nabalityelweyo.

Owu, mntu, yini na ukuba uyilahle iNkosi yakho? Yini na ukuba ujike ezindleleni zam? Kanti, uya kuthi, andijikanga ezindleleni zakho; ndingulowo iNkosi imbizileyo. Inene, ndibizile, waza wasabela okwexeshana, ixesha lobutsha elinovuyo olukhulu. Abanye benu basiwa apho kuhlala khona iingcuka, yaye uninzi lwenu lulahlekisiwe ngamazwi amnandi, iintetho ezintle, neelwimi ezixokayo eziza kuthi cwaka kungekudala.

Khumbula uthando lwakho lokuqala, uze uguquke; zivume izono zakho kum, njengoko ndisoloko ndilungele ukuzihlamba izono zakho egazini lam lentlawulelo.

Livani ilizwi lam, bantwana bam, njengoko amazwi amaninzi evakala ngokufanayo yaye nooKrestu abaninzi bobuxoki bezama ukuxelisa ilizwi lam. Inene, ndiyalikhathalela yaye ndiyalalusa ibandla lam ngesandla sam, ukuze ndiniveze kuThixo ninyulu njengentombi enyulu yaye nambethe ilinen emhlophe ecolekileyo, amabala ezono zenu exolelwe, esusiwe, yaye elityelwe, njengoko ndiwaphosele kwezona nzonzobila zinzulu, angasayi kuphinda avuke. Kanti, uninzi lwenu luphazamisekile yaye luthakathiwe leli hlabathi likhoyo elingendawo. Bahlasela ngobugqwirha babo ngokulunywa yinyoka, kodwa qondani ukuba nangona inyoka iniluma, akukho ngozi iya kunihlela, kuba itywina likaBawo alinakwaphulwa. Gxininisani ingqalelo yenu kum, uNyana wakhe okuphela kwamzeleyo, awamnikela ngokuzithandela ukuba ahlawulele izono zenu ukuze siphinde simanyane, singasayi kwahlukana kwakhona. Mandiniphilise; mandiniphilise ngokupheleleyo ndisuse namanxeba enu ukuze nibe sempilweni niphelele phambi kwam. Landelani iindlela zam nilandele neLizwi lam, kuba:

Ndingulowo ubukumkani bakhe ingebobeli hlabathi, nomvuzo wam unam owabanyuliweyo bam abathembekileyo.

Ndingulowo uhamba phakathi kweziphatho zezibane ezisixhenxe yaye ndiyalela izithunywa zezulu ezisixhenxe zeBandla.

Bantwana bam, ndiyanixelela inene ukuba ndisolule isandla sam kwihlabathi elifayo kangangeeYubhili ezininzi ngoku, yaye isandla sam asisayi kuhlala soluliwe ngenceba nobabalo, nangona inceba nobabalo zihleli zinam. Ndibize abaninzi, yaye abaninzi bale. Noko ke, abo ndibabizileyo, ndabakhetha, ndabangcwalisa, ndabahlamba, ndabacoca, ndabaphilisa, bona kanye baya kuma phambi kwesihlalo sam sokugweba baze bathwale umvuzo omkhulu ngenxa yokuthembeka kwabo.

Nangona ndinemisebenzi emininzi enisaza kuyenza, yaye nangona nenze lukhulu ngenxa yam negama lam, ndinqwenela intliziyo yenu iphela ngaphezu kwemisebenzi yenu. Ndinqwenela ukuba niguqukele kum nimke ezonweni zenu. Yizani nicocekile kum, njengoko iNkosi yenu ilungile inesandla sayo sokuphilisa esoluliweyo ukuze ndinenze nicoceke njengekhephu elitsha. Nangona izandla zam zahlaba, igazi lam lentlawulelo laphuma kuzo nasemzimbeni wam kwada kwayithontsi lokugqibela. Ndancama konke endinako ukuze niphile phambi koBawo ngonaphakade kanaphakade.

Inene, andihlawulelanga sono sinye; inene, andihlawulelanga izono ezimbalwa; inene, andihlawulelanga izono ezininzi, kodwa ndahlawulela zonke izono zalo lonke ixesha ukuba nje ningasabela kum xa ndeyisela iintliziyo zenu ngoMoya wam onamandla. Aniqondi na ukuba andinqweneli kunigweba? Kukho lowo unaloo mnqweno yaye unqwenela ukufa kwenu aze animangalele imini nobusuku phambi koBawo, nangona ndingumthetheleli wenu. Ulahliwe waza woyiswa; kanti, yini na ukuba nimphakamisele isela kwindawo ephakamileyo ezintliziyweni zenu ekubeni ndinoloyiso olupheleleyo kuye nakubukukumkani bakhe obuza kutshabalala kungekudala? Yithini tu nibe seluxolweni nize kum ngentliziyo ethobekileyo. Anazi na ukuba ukuba benizigweba, beningayi kugwetywa? Celani kum ukuba ndinibonise ubomi benu, njengoko ndiya kutyhila izinto ezininzi kuni ukuze nivume iziphoso zenu nize niphiliswe.

Musani ukoyika, bantwanana bam, kuba asindim onigwebayo. Phulaphulani isazela senu ningalichithi ilizwi lam ngokuzithethelela. Eli hlabathi liza kulahlwa, yaye njengoko eli hlabathi liguqulwa ngonaphakade, niya kunyuselwa emazulwini phezulu, ukuze ndiniveze kuBawo, ukuze niqonde ilifa lenu nize nifumane imivuzo yenu yokuthembeka. Kudala ndilangazelela imini esiya kubonana ngayo emazulwini ukuze ekugqibeleni nityhilelwe ubomi benu bokwenene njengoko nisindile kobu bomi bexeshana bobubi, isono, nenkohlakalo. Ndingulowo uvavanya iintliziyo zabantu, kungekhona ukuba ndinigwebe, kodwa ukuze ngomlilo womsuli nihlanjululwe kwiindlela zenu ezindala nize niqonde iindlela zam zothando, ukukhanya, uvuyo, uxolo, ukuzeka kade umsindo, nobumnene.

Yivani isohlwayo sam nize namkele ukuthethisa kwam kuba uYihlo uniphatha njengabantwana, yaye nguwuphi umntwana othi uYise amthande angasohlwaywa aze athethiswe naye. Ukuthethisa kwam kuvelisa iziqhamo zoxolo zobulungisa, njengoko ukuthethisa kwam kukukuhlanjulula ngokupheleleyo ezonweni zenu ukuba nje ningeva amazwi am nilandele ilizwi lam. Hambani, nime, nithethe onke amazwi obu bomi. Abanye basaza kufumana usindiso kweli lixa lokugqibela, yaye kuphela ngegama likaYesu Krestu, igama lam elinamandla apho kukho usindiso, kuba akukho gama limbi elinikwe usindiso. Igama lam elinamandla limi liqinile, lilindele ukuba nibazise kum, ukuze ndiveze abaguqukayo kuBawo njengosana olusandul' ukuzalelwa kunaphakade. Ndinothando olukhulu ngani, yaye njengoko uBawo endinikile, ndiya kunika abathembekileyo nabanyuliweyo bam.

NdinguNyana kaThixo oze enyameni, ongenasono yaye onyulu ngonaphakade kanaphakade. Ngokunjalo, ndingulowo wanikela ngegazi lam elingenasono ngenxa yaboni ukuze badle ilifa lobomi obungunaphakade. Anikho na nina, bathenjwa nabanyuliweyo bam, phakathi kwabo badla ilifa lozuko olungunaphakade? Yini na ukuba nisahleli nithe cwaka ngokungathi nilindele amazwi afanelekileyo. Thethani! Vulani imilomo yenu nithethe, njengoko ndiya kukhokela intetho yenu ukuze nikhonze kanye kuloo ntlungu ababiziweyo bam abasayivayo, yaye abaninzi basaza kusabela. Zimanyeni nam emsebenzini wam kweli lixa lokugqibela phambi kokuba ndishukumise iziseko zehlabathi ndiliqhekeze kwiindawo ezininzi nomgwebo omkhulu ugubungele umhlaba. Ndiya kuwubuyisela kuni lo mhlaba kuBukumkani bam obuzayo, kodwa kuqala ubukumkani babangendawo buya kutshatyalaliswa buthatyathwe ngomgwebo bukhonkxwe enzonzobileni, yaye bayazi ukuba ichibi elitshisayo elimhlophe apho baya kuchitha unaphakade lilindile.

Ndiya kuzitshabalalisa ngokupheleleyo izihlalo zabo zamandla; ndiya kuzitshabalalisa ngokupheleleyo zonke iinkululeko zabo; ndiya kuzitshisa ngokupheleleyo zibe luthuthu ngomlilo ongacimekiyo, njengoko nam, iNkosi ethembekileyo, ndinixelele phambi kokuba kwenzeke. Endlwini kaBawo kuneendawo zokuhlala ezininzi, yaye ubukhulu nobuhle obukhulu bunilindile. Ngoko ke, makungabikho mntu unikhohlisayo ngomvuzo wenu yaye nihlale nithembekile kum. Gxininisani ingqondo yenu enye kum, iNkosi engunaphakade, kwanakwiNkosi yozuko. Ndingumalusi olungileyo; ndinguye kanye uKrestu okhethwe nguBawo kwasekusekweni kwehlabathi. Ndingulowo weza kwabakhe waza walaqathekiswa ngabakhe, nangona ngovavanyo olukhulu uYakobi uya kusindiswa sisandla sam esinamandla. Ukugqitywa kwezinto zonke kusondele, nesiphelo senkosana yeli hlabathi sikufuphi. Nangona uya kukhululwa okwexeshana elingephi, umgwebo wakhe ongunaphakade uyalandela, apho angasayi kuze ahlangulwe yaye akusayi kubakho nyango. Buyelani kum, thethani nam, nikhonze ngenxa yam, njengoko uBawo endinike zonke izinto, nomvuzo wam kuni ugcinwe emazulwini owabanyuliweyo bam abathembekileyo yaye uqinisekile njengetywina likaBawo endinitywine ngalo. Kusele imizuzu embalwa yexesha lobabalo, yaye umgwebo wam uya kufika ngequbuliso njengoko ingqumbo yeMvana isihla emhlabeni. Zilungiseleleni iintliziyo zenu nizilungiselele, kuba ixesha lisondele.
