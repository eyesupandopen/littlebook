Yeha Kwabo Babiza Ubumnyama Ngokuba Kukukhanya Nokukhanya Ngokuba Bubumnyama!

Yivani amazwi eNkosi yenu nomsindisi wenu, Bandla lam, ewe, Mtshakazi wam, kuba ndingulowo unika izilumko ubulumko, ukuze izilumko eziphakathi kwenu zibe nobulumko ngakumbi. Kwaye ndingumkhandi ovuselela imililo yokuhlanjululwa, ngoko ke yivani amazwi am nonke niphela, niguqe ngedolo kwisimemo sam sobomi obungunaphakade nifune ubulumko kuba ubulumko yeyona nto iphambili, ngoko ke zuza ubulumko. Ngoko yizani kum ngentliziyo evulekileyo nangeengalo ezivulekileyo niguqe ngedolo niqhubeke nindenza mna, uYesu waseNazarete, iNkosi yenu engunaphakade kuba ndisababiza bonke abaziindlalifa zobomi obungunaphakade, nangona abanye beneendlebe ezilukhuni.

Kodwa bulahle ubulumko beli hlabathi obungento yanto, kuba ubulumko behlabathi buvela kwiinkosana zehlabathi eziza kuthi kungekudala zibotshwe iminyaka eliwaka enzonzobileni zithuthunjiswe zohlwaywe ngenxa yeendlela zazo. Kuba nakumatyathanga anzima eziya kubotshwa ngawo kungekudala ziya kuliqonda ikamva lazo, kwaye ziya kungcangcazela zithuthumele iminyaka eliwaka njengoko unaphakade wazo omiselweyo kwelo chibi livutha ngonaphakade lesulfure enukayo liqengqeleka kwaye litsha ngawona malangatye ashushu e-cobalt blue kuba uYahweh ulungiselele njalo kwaye ilifa lazo lilindile.

Kuba unjalo umnqweno weentliziyo zabo ngabantwana boThixo ophilayo, ngoko uYahweh uya kubuyisela umnqweno weentliziyo zabo ezifubeni zabo, kwaye baya kutsha ngonaphakade ngokuphindaphindiweyo kude kube ngunaphakade kanaphakade. Kuba ngokukodwa abo bawayo baya kwamkela ezabo iindlela kuba bangooyise besono, isigqitho, ubugwenxa, nokuchasa umthetho kwaye izenzo zabo ezinezothe ziya kubuyiselwa kubo ngonaphakade. Kwaye baya kwazi kwaye bahlawule ngokupheleleyo ngaso sonke isono abakhe basenza amaxesha amaninzi ngokuphindaphindiweyo siya kuba sisohlwayo sabo, kuba kufunyenwe njalo ezintliziyweni zabo kwaye izenzo zabo zinezothe ngonaphakade kanaphakade. Kuba zinjalo iindlela zikaYahweh, ngendlela umntu agweba ngayo omnye, uya kugwetywa ngayo naye ngokwenene kwaye sinjalo isiphelo nesohlwayo sabo bonke abakhetha intshabalalo yabo endaweni yobomi obungunaphakade kum mna Yesu waseNazarete kuba ndiyiMvana kaThixo.

Kuba nabani na osisidenge ngokwaneleyo ukuba avukele ucwangco lwendalo, nkqu nemiyalelo yendalo kaYahweh owayila ubomi njengomluko odibeneyo, uya kwabelana nabo esohlwayweni sabo. Ukuvukela imiyalelo yobomi kukuzisa ukufa nentshabalalo kwabanye, kwaye ububi obukhulu nomonakalo omkhulu wenziwa ngabo bonke abavukela ngolo hlobo nabalandela iinkanuko zabo kube kubi kwabanye. Ukuzonwabisa ngemvukelo yalowo uwileyo kukuzibandakanya neendlela zabo kwaye bonke abakwenzayo baya kwabelana nabo ngonaphakade kwilifa labo kumalangatye angunaphakade, kwaye akukho olunye ukhetho.

Kuba kwakudala kwaye ukususela ekuqaleni uluntu lwanikwa inkululeko yokuzikhethela kwaye uAdam wahendwa nguEva owathatha kwinyoka eyamkhohlisayo ngobuqhetseba bayo. Ngoko uAdam noEva bakhetha ukufa njengeziphumo zesono, kuba abantu badalwa kanye njengeengelosi, ngenjongo yokuba bahlale benyulu kwaye belungile, kwaye ukufa, okutshabalalisa yonke into, ngekungabanga namandla phezu kwabo.

Kwaye ngoko ulwazi lokulungileyo nokubi lwonakalise bonke abakhe batya ukususela ngoko. Kwaye imfazwe yembewu iye yagquma kwaye yaqhushumba ukususela kwiGenesis kunye nembewu ethenjisiweyo yomfazi, kwintshabalalo enkulu, kwaye kule mfazwe akukho nto iye yasinda kwiziphumo zayo. Kude kube namhlanje yonke indalo iyathuthumela kwaye iyancwina kabuhlungu nanamhlanje kuba nendalo ngokwayo iya kukhululwa ebukhobokeni bokonakala ingene enkululekweni ezukileyo yoonyana bakaThixo, kwaye ukuhlangulwa akulibazisi! Kodwa qondani bantwana bam ukuba kude kube namhlanje imfazwe yembewu iphunyelelwe ngokupheleleyo kum, uKristu Yesu, Kuba nizelwe ngokutsha ngembewu engenakonakala kwaye kwiMvana kaThixo kwenziwa intlawulelo yezono zoluntu kanye ngonaphakade. Kwaye ngamandla angenakulinganiswa kaYahweh, ukufa kwoyiswa ngonaphakade ekuvukeni kwam kwabafileyo, kuba akunakwenzeka ukuba ukufa kundibambe kuba andinasono kwaye umvuzo wesono kukufa.

Noko ke, ndahlawula ixabiso elingenakucingeleka ngenxa yokuhlangulwa nenkululeko yenu, kwaye oonyana boThixo ophilayo baya kuthi nangawuphi na umzuzu bazifumane bemi kwigumbi letroni likaThixo bembathiswe ngonaphakade ubomi obungunaphakade. Kunjalo kwaye kuya kuba njalo, ukuba nabani na onqula igama leNkosi uya kusindiswa ngonaphakade kanaphakade, ukuba uhlala ethembekile ekunquleni igama lam kude kube sekupheleni. Kuba abo bawayo ziintshaba ezoyisiweyo, kwaye ngoku kufika amaxesha entshabalalo epheleleyo kubukumkani babo nesohlwayo esipheleleyo njengoko ingqumbo yeMvana nengqumbo kaThixo ithululwa ngeentsuku ezizayo. Kuba usuku lweNkosi lufikile, ngoko ke phulaphulani ilizwi lam phambi kokuba umnyango woMkhombe uvalwe.

Yivani amazwi am kwaye niyiqonde intetho yam. Kuba ndithetha ngezinto eziqhubekayo ngoku kwaye nkqu nenkohliso enkulu enokukhohlisa nabanyulwa kanye ukuba bakhetha ukuvala iindlebe zabo kwilizwi lam. Ngoku ndiza kuthetha ngeehagu zasendle ezifuna ukubonakala ngathi azikho ezibayeni ezigcinwe kuzo ngumthinteli wam. Nkqu nomngcelele nemiyalelo yeehagu eqhubela phambili intshabalalo imihla ngemihla isondeza umhlaba kwimfazwe epheleleyo, nakwisibetho esipheleleyo, kuba kunikwe zona ukuba zifezekise oko kwaye uThixo ukufakile ezintliziyweni zazo ukuba zizalisekise ukuthanda kwakhe kuba nkqu neentshaba zikaYahweh ziyalithobela ilizwi lakhe.

Iihagu ziqengqeleka kubumdaka bazo, zifuna ukutya eludakeni, kwaye azaneli, kodwa ziya kudla nantoni na ezinokuyifumana njengoko zifuna ukutya ngeempumlo zazo emhlabeni kuba zezomhlaba, kwaye aziboni nto ngaphandle kwempembelelo yesisu sazo kwaye ziya kwenza iinkanuko zikayise wazo. Kuba banjalo abakhokeli bangoku belizwe, kwaye bayakhala kabuhlungu njengoko bebetha amaxhoba abo. Kuba akukho nto inokuzanelisa ngokupheleleyo ezi hagu, kwaye zinetyhefu ngokupheleleyo ngaphakathi ngenxa yezinto ezizidlayo, kuba zonke azicocekanga kwaye zingumhloinza wokwenene kwaye injalo indalo yazo.

La marhamncwa mabi yimithi eyonakeleyo kwaye engenasiqhamo, efile kabini, kwaye incothulwe ngeengcambu. Awasoze abe nobomi obungunaphakade, kwaye iindlela zawo zezokufa nentshabalalo engunaphakade, nkqu nentshabalalo engapheliyo kwilifa looyise babo kwelo chibi likhulu lomlilo oluhlaza. Ziyindalo ehlaselayo, kwaye zizenza zithandeke ekuqaleni zifuna ikhusi njengoko zikhala zizenza amaxhoba, zityhola abanye ngentiyo eziyenzayo ezizweni. Ziyakhala kabuhlungu njengoko zibetha abo zifuna ukubatshabalalisa ukuze zibonakale zilungile kwiindlela zazo ezimbi.

Kuba la marhamncwa akrwada akanakusizelwa kuba iindlela zawo zezentshabalalo kwaye aya kwenza iinkanuko zikaYise wawo umtyholi kuba iinkanuko zakhe ziyolonwabo lwawo kwaye aneliswa ngaloo ndlela. Yeha kwabo babhiyozela ububi nababiza ukukhanya ngokuba bubumnyama nobumnyama ngokuba kukukhanya. Kuba iindlela zabo zezentshabalalo, kwaye kwimililo engunaphakade abasoze basinde. Qondani bantwana bam ukuba nangawuphi na umzuzu ngoku iihagu ziya kukhululwa ezibayeni ebezigcinwe kuzo ngumthinteli. Kuba njengoko umthinteli esusiwe, ezi hagu zibonakala zifuyekile kwaye zingenabungozi ezenza ngathi zinyuliweyo zikaThixo ziya kuba zasendle nje ukuba zikhululwe ekubanjweni okungenakuqhawulwa komthinteli, kuba injalo indalo yazo yokwenene.

Ziya kuhlasela amazwe amaninzi kwaye zidle konke ezikufumanayo. Ziya kutya ukufa nentshabalalo yabanye, kuba injalo indalo yazo. Ziye zabhadula ukusuka kwenye indawo ukuya kwenye, kwaye zinemiyalelo yazo nezakhiwo zentlalo, kuba uYahweh unika nenqanaba lobukrelekrele nocwangco nkqu nakwiihagu, kuba iindlela zikaYahweh zilungile kwaye zibubulungisa. Ezi hagu azinazintloni, azinaziva, kwaye azinangxaki yokutya abafileyo ukuze zanelise iminqweno yazo, kwaye ziya kutya nabaphilayo ukuba oko kuyahambelana neenjongo zazo. Khangelani, ilixa ngoku apho iihagu ziya kukhululwa kwaye xa zikhululwe, ziya kulidla ilizwe, ziya kudla ukutyeba kwelizwe, ziya kulutshabalalisa uxolo ngokupheleleyo emhlabeni kwaye ziya kutya ziqengqeleke kuphalazo-gazi lwemfazwe epheleleyo. Kuba nkqu nabazivavanyisi benu ababi bayacula ngeendlela zazo, kwaye iindlela zazo aziyomfihlakalo.

Ezi hagu zasendle zenzelwe intshabalalo nokutya ubumdaka belizwe ukuhlanjululwa komhlaba ngeenjongo zikaYahweh, emva koko ziya kubotshwa ziphoswe kwimililo engunaphakade ngonaphakade. Kuba izono zazo zikhulu, kwaye izono zazo zininzi. Kodwa qondani ukuba ezi hagu zigcinwe esibayeni sazo ngamaxesha amisiweyo kwaye ezininzi zenza ngathi zimiselwe njengezitya ezingcwele zobukumkani bazo obunyukayo. Kwaye ngoku lixesha lokukhululwa kwazo, kwaye ngoku ziya kubonisa indalo yazo yokwenene ebezifihle kwizambatho ezibonakala zihloniphekile, kwaye ityhubhu emnyama yeSaturn iseziko lenkwenkwezi yazo kwaye nkqu nangaphezulu kwamehlo. Zifuna ukuzahlula kuba zizikhethele ukuba zezikaThixo. Kodwa akukho mntu uza kuYahweh ngaphandle kwam, uYesu waseNazarete, owaliwa ngooyise bazo kwaye abo bandenze iNkosi bangamaSirayeli okwenene kaThixo kwaye baya kudalwa ngokutsha ngokupheleleyo kum njengomntu omnye omtsha, nokuba ngumYuda okanye weNtlanga, nokuba likhoboka okanye ukhululekile. Lo mhloinza wakwaEdom uya kutyhilwa ngokupheleleyo njengamaqhetseba kwaye uya kususwa ngokupheleleyo elizweni nakumhlaba wonke ngomgwebo omkhulu nesohlwayo esikhulu.

Kodwa phambi kokuba zisuswe, ziya kuvunyelwa ukunxanela kwazo igazi njengoko indalo yazo yokwenene ityhilwa ukuze bonke babone, kuba okwangoku zizifihla kwizinto ezibonakala zingezonqulo. Zitya inkunkuma, zitya abafileyo, zihlasela kwaye zidle abaphilayo kuba ukunxanela kwazo igazi kukhulu kwaye akunakwaneliswa! Kwaye ngalo lonke elo xesha zenza ngathi zingcwele, zenza ngathi zezelizwe kwaye ziziindlalifa ezifanelekileyo, kwaye zenza ngathi azingabahlaseli kuba indawo yazo yokuhlala yendalo yintlango hayi ilizwe lesithembiso. Kwaye injalo indalo yehagu kuba nje ukuba ibuyiselwe endle indalo yayo yokwenene yasendle ityhilwa ngokupheleleyo. Noko ke, zenza ngathi ziphila ubuni babo zifuna ukuboyisa kwaye ziya kuncothulwa ngokupheleleyo elizweni kwaye azinangcambu nasebe kuba uYahweh uthembise njalo. Ngoko qondani ukuba ezi hagu zibonakala zingabakhokeli abanobubele belizwe lesithembiso kanti eneneni zingamarhamncwa ahlaselayo achuma ngokubulala abantu kwaye aya kufuna ukutshabalalisa nantoni na yam, uKristu Yesu, kwaye aya kungcatsha nkqu nabo baseLizweni abalandela imiyalelo yabo yeTalmud ukuya entshabalalweni kwaye noko intsalela yelizwe iya kuza kum, uYeshua HaMashiach, kuba ndiya kumsindisa uYakobi!

Yeha kwabo babiza ubumnyama ngokuba kukukhanya nokukhanya ngokuba bubumnyama! Nkqu nabo bahlasela uYahweh, noYeshua HaMashiach ngokuhlasela abantwana boThixo ophilayo. Kuba ngokuba bakwenzile komncinci kubazalwana bam bakwenzile kum, kwaye impindezelo yeyam, itsho iNkosi engunaphakade, kwaye ndiya kubuyisela. Ngoko vulani amehlo enu, bantwana bam, kwaye nibalahle abo baphakamisa inkwenkwezi kaRemphan kwibhanile yabo! Kuba ukuzibandakanya nabo kukuhlala kumnquba kaMoloki nokuguqa phambi kweBaphomet yabo! Andinixelelanga na ukuba nibale inani lerhamncwa kwaye elo nani liboniswe ngokuzingca kuloo mfanekiso bazenzele wona ukuze banqule uthixo wabo wenkwenkwezi, kuba esizikithini senkwenkwezi yabo kukho ityhubhu yabo! Wubambe lo mzekeliso, kuba iingongoma ezintandathu zikhokelela koonxantathu abathandathu abenza unxantandathu kwaye ngaphakathi ingena ityhubhu yabo, ngoko balani inani lerhamncwa kwaye niyeke ukukholelwa kubuxoki bamaEdom kuba banqula abo bawayo kwaye uthixo wabo yinamba.

Kwaye ukususela kwimihla kaNowa, nasemva konogumbe, isithoba kwishumi semimoya engenamzimba yamaNefili siye sathintelwa enzonzobileni, kuba uNowa wathandazela abazukulwana bakhe ababehlaselwe yiyo kwaye boyiswa yiyo. Kwaye ngoko uYahweh wasihlonipha isicelo sikaNowa waza wababopha abona babi nabona bakhohlakeleyo, kwaye isithoba kwishumi senani labo siye sagcinwa enzonzobileni ukususela ngoko. Ndiveni kwaye nindive kakuhle, Bantwana bam, kwaye niliqonde ilizwi leNkosi yenu. Ekuhlanganisweni kwabathembekileyo eBandleni lam kwaye nkqu noMtshakazi wam kum emafini umthinteli uya kususwa, kwaye inzonzobila imiselwe ukuba ivulwe ngokupheleleyo.

Ububi abusayi kuphinda buthintelwe, kuba uYahweh uye wabuthintela ngakumbi ubukumkani bukaSathana obupheleleyo ukuze abo baziindlalifa zosindiso olungunaphakade basindiswe, nkqu nabo bandenze iNkosi yabo ngamaxesha am obabalo nenceba. Ekubeni uluntu ngoku lundilahlile, ububi abusayi kuphinda buthintelwe eluntwini. Qondani ukuba bonke, nkqu nesihlwele esikhulu sale mimoya bonke baya kukhululwa, nkqu nenani elikhulu kakhulu eliya kuwugubungela ngokupheleleyo umhlaba ngobubi obungathintelekiyo nobungathethekiyo. Kuba uEnoki umbhali wabhala ngokuphathelele kwezi: Kwaye imimoya yeengxilimbela iyaxhwalekisa, icinezele, itshabalalise, ihlasele, ilwe, kwaye isebenze intshabalalo emhlabeni, kwaye ibangele ingxaki: ayithathi kutya, kodwa noko kunjalo iyalamba kwaye iyanxanwa, kwaye ibangele izikhubekiso. Kwaye le mimoya iya kuvukela abantwana babantu nabafazi, kuba iphume kubo.

Anifundanga na kwilizwi lam elingcwele apho ndahamba khona kwilizwe lamaGadara? Kuba ndakhupha imimoya kwababini ababenayo, kwaye ngoko ndayivumela le mimoya mibi ukuba ingene kumhlambi weehagu owawukufuphi. Qondani ukuba ubukumkani obupheleleyo beNamba sele buza kukhululwa okwexeshana elifutshane, ukuze kuqhutyelwe abo bamiselwe ubomi obungunaphakade ezingalweni zam ezisindisayo! Kuba le mimoya ivalelweyo iya kukhululwa ukuze ijoyine ubukumkani bukaSathana ngokupheleleyo ebusweni bomhlaba. Kwaye iya kungena kwiihagu kwakhona. Kuba zilwa nabantu kwaye ziya kuboyisa abantu kuba iinkanuko zazo azinakwaneliswa kwaye yile mimoya enika amandla iihagu kwaye esebenza kunye neehagu kwaye ezi hagu zasendle sisithuthi sazo.

Hayi zona kuphela kodwa bonke abathatha uphawu lwazo lwerhamncwa nabo baya kuba zizithuthi ezivumayo zale mimoya eya kulwa noluntu kwakhona. Kuba ziya kuphinda zidle umntu wendalo kula maxesha okugqibela, kuba kumiselwe ukuba kwenzeke njalo kula maxesha okuphela. Kwaye isiphelo sele siqalile kwaye siza ngokupheleleyo kungekudala njengoko imbandezelo kaYakobi iqhambuka kulo lonke ilizwe. Iintlungu zokubeleka zisondele kakhulu ngoku azipheli kwaye uYahweh wenze imiqondiso emininzi emhlabeni nemiqondiso emininzi nemimangaliso esibhakabhakeni nasemazulwini nasemhlabeni, noko ke abaninzi basazichitha ezi zilumkiso ngengqiqo engenakholo.

Khangelani! Endaweni yokuba ziguquke, izihlwele zide zonwatyiswe yimikhosi yezinambuzane eye yagubungela abaninzi nakumazwe amaninzi kuwo wonke umhlaba. Zihleka kwaye zigigitheka ngezilumkiso ezinikwe nguYahweh emhlabeni kuba uThixo ongunaphakade uya kuvuka endaweni yakhe yokuhlala aze azibonakalise kwaye abo bawayo bayangcangcazela kuba amaxesha abo afikile, kwaye babanjwe luloyiko olungunaphakade. Kuba nina bawayo benicinga ukuba ningamthatha uThixo ongunaphakade ngobuqhetseba, kanti into enjalo ayinakwenzeka. Kuba ngeliphi ixesha izitya zokungahlonipheki ziye zavuka ukuze zilwe zithathe umbumbi ngobuqhetseba? Kodwa bunjalo ubuphambane nekratshi labo bawayo, kwaye abayi kusinda kwiziko labo elivutha ngonaphakade.

Ngoko ke qondani amaxesha eniphila kuwo kwaye nihlale kum, Bandla lam, nkqu noMtshakazi wam! Kuba inkululeko yenu epheleleyo nelifa lenu elipheleleyo alilibazisi, kwaye aniyi kushiywa phakathi kweehagu kuba nikholwe kwigama lam kule mihla yobabalo nenceba, kwaye ngoko ubabalo nenceba engunaphakade niya kuba nazo ngonaphakade. Kwaye abazalwana benu baya kunijoyina kunaphakade nakwiBukumkani bam obuzayo bobulungisa, nkqu nabo bonke abangakulungelanga ukubonakala kwam.

Kuba ndingumcoci ovuselela imililo yokuhlanjululwa ukuze ndihlambulule ubutyebi bukaYahweh, ndisuse inkunkuma, kwaye ndingulowo uhleli njengomkhandi ephethe imfutho esandleni evuselela amalahle kwaye osonga aze akhande iintsimbi ezixabisekileyo zikaYahweh ukuze avelise amandla angunaphakade nangenakuphela. Isihlwele esikhulu, nkqu nesihlwele sezihlwele, siya kuza kum ngembandezelo kaYakobi, kwaye siya kuvuna umvuzo omkhulu, kwaye siziindlalifa zosindiso olungunaphakade kwaye siya kungena kuvuyo olungunaphakade kwaye uSirayeli uya kusindiswa! Ngoko musani ukoyika bantwana bam, kwaye ningakhathazeki ngabathandekayo benu kwaye nibe nokholo kum uKristu Yesu, kuba ndinako ukusindisa ngokugqibeleleyo bonke abanqula igama lam, kwaye akukho namnye omiselwe ubomi obungunaphakade onokuxhwilwa esandleni sam, kwaye ndiya kusindisa abaninzi kwimililo engunaphakade, nkqu nokubarhola emalangatyeni.

Mna Yeshua HaMashiach ndithumele la mazwi ngomkhonzi wam, kwaye ngomkhonzi wam la mazwi athunyelwe!

Lindelani ukubonakala kwam, kuba amaxesha afikile. Ixesha eliseleyo linikwe kuphela ukukhonza iinjongo zikaYahweh bantwana bam, ngoko musani ukuzivumela ukuba nilaliswe kwakhona, nkqu nokungahoyi amazwi am obulumko nezilumkiso ezininzi.

Kwaye ndingulowo uya kunifihla apho ningasoze nizifihle khona, kuba ndiya kunifihla kwigumbi letroni likaYahweh nkqu nasezulwini lamazulu apho niya kungena kulonwabo lweNkosi yenu kwaye iiholo zesidlo sasezulwini ziya kunibhiyozela nonke, kuba kungekudala kunokuba nicinga niya kubona ngamehlo enu ubuhle obukhulu, obungunaphakade obunilindeleyo kwilifa lenu. Qhubekani nisahleli eluthandweni lwam, kuba nithandwa kakhulu nguBawo kwaye niliqula leso lam, endingenazintloni ukunibiza ngokuba ningabazalwana bam.
