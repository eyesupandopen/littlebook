Imfihlelo yeNcwadi eNcinci

Kusuka kuYesu HaMashiach:

Yabonani, imfihlelo yencwadi encinci! Kuba iingelosi ezisixhenxe zithumela iindudumo zazo, kwaye iindudumo zikhuphile amazwi azo. Ziyimfihlelo yencwadi encinci, kuba incwadi encinci yayitywiniwe. Kuba kwafika ilizwi livela ezulwini, lixelela uYohane umTyhili: Tywina ezo zinto zithethwe ziindudumo ezisixhenxe, ungazibhali. Yabona, watya incwadi encinci, kuba ityhiliwe, yaye yayimnandi njengobusi ezindlebeni zakho ulele, kodwa yakrakra esiswini sakho nto leyo yakuvusa embonweni; kwaye yabona, uvukile ebuthongweni bakho! Yabona, imfihlelo yencwadi encinci ifikile ukuze ityhilwe ngeli xesha, kuba iyimfihlelo yokugqibela eza kutyhilwa kwixesha lam lobabalo, kwanencwadi encinci yobumnandi obukrakra. Kuba amazwi eendudumo aza kutyhilwa, kwaye ngoku lixesha lokutyhila, kuba imfihlelo yencwadi encinci inkulu, nangona iyincwadi encinci. Kuba loo ncwadi incinci yatyhilwa kuYohane umTyhili, kwaye ngoku ndiza kuyityhila kuwe, kuba ukutyhilwa kwayo kusondele. Liva ilizwi lam, mtshakazi wam, uthobe indlebe yakho kum, uKrestu Yesu, kuba ixesha lifikile. Kuba alisekho ixesha.

Ndingunyana kaThixo oze enyameni, kwaye mna, uYesu HaMashiach, ndithumele la mazwi.

Kusuka kuYehova, uThixo ongunaphakade:

Ilizwi leendudumo ezisixhenxe LIYATHETHA! Zilungiseleleni, owu, zintlanga, kuba umgwebo omkhulu uphezu kwenu, kwaye amazwi eendudumo ezisixhenxe ayatyhilwa. Jikani iindlebe zenu nive iintetho zemfihlelo yeNcwadi eNcinci. Kuba imiqulu iya kukhululwa, kwaye ukukhululwa kwamatywina kuyeza phezu kwehlabathi lonke, kwanomgwebo omkhulu nentlekele. Kuba izono zomhlaba livumba elibi ezimpumlweni zam, kwaye mna, kwanam uYehova, ndiza kuwuhlambulula umhlaba, kwaye ndiza kuyidiliza yonke into embi; kwaye unyana wam, endimthumayo, uza kulawula ngobulungisa emhlabeni, kwaye uxolo olukhulu luya kuba kwabathembekileyo endibakhuseleyo kusuku olubi. Kwaye uxolo olukhulu ndiza kulunika bonke abanqula igama likaYesu Krestu, kwanaye uYesu HaMashiach, kuba bonke abanqula igama leNkosi baya kusindiswa. Kwaye ngoxolo nokhuseleko ndiza kubakhokela, kwaye ngobulungisa ndiza kubambathisa, kwaye ngobungcwele bokwenene baya kundizukisa; kuba ndibazukisile, kwaye ubomi obutsha baya kuba nabo.

Ngoko ke ndizisa ubulungisa bam nengqumbo yam ukuze ndibuyekeze abo benza ububi ngeendlela zabo ezimdaka, ngoko ke nabani na ondalayo aze akhethe ububi, makabe mubi nangoku, kwaye ingqumbo kaThixo ihleli! Kwaye nabani na oya kuba lilungisa nabo bonke abasukayo ebubini njengoko benqula igama leNkosi baya kuba nomvuzo omkhulu; ngoko ke mababe neziqhamo zoxolo zobulungisa, kwaye baya kuba nelifa elikhulu kunyana wam, uKrestu Yesu, ophethe umvuzo wabo esandleni sakhe. Ngoko ke nabani na oya kuba mubi, makabe mubi nangoku. Kwaye nabani na ojikela ebulungiseni aze anqule igama leNkosi, makabe lilungisa nangoku, kuba usuku luya kuyibhengeza. Yabonani, into endiza kunixelela yona ingekenzeki, kuba iziprofeto ziza kuzaliseka, kwaye akusayi kubakho kulibaziseka. Kuba amaxesha obabalo nenceba ayaphela, kwaye ixesha lengqumbo enkulu liza kukhululwa emhlabeni, kwaye akuzange kubekho xesha linje emhlabeni, kwanangemihla kaNowa, kwaye akusayi kuphinda kubekho xesha elinje kwakhona. Kuba mna, kwanam, ndifunge ngegama lam kwaye ndizibhengeze ezi zinto. Nantoni na endiyibhengezayo mna, kwanam uYehova uThixo ongunaphakade, ayinakuguqulwa kwaye iya kwenzeka. Kuba ngemihla yengelosi yesixhenxe, xa iya kuqala ukuvuthela, kwanemfihlelo kaThixo iya kugqitywa.

Ndinguye endinguYE, ngoko baxelele ukuba uMkhulu onguYE uthumele la mazwi, kwanaye uYehova, uThixo omkhulu nongunaphakade!

Ubungqina namaVava, nguWilliam Brooks

Kwaye ngoku ukuze siqonde ngakumbi le nxalenye yesi sigidimi apho uYesu HaMashiach wathi, "Yabona, watya incwadi encinci, kuba ityhiliwe, yaye yayimnandi njengobusi ezindlebeni zakho ulele, kodwa yakrakra esiswini sakho nto leyo yakuvusa embonweni; kwaye yabona, uvukile ebuthongweni bakho." Kwithuba elithile eladlulayo kwinyanga ephelileyo okanye ngaphezulu, andiwazi umhla ochanekileyo, kodwa ndandilele ezinzulwini zobusuku, ndaza ndavukela embonweni ndiphuma ebuthongweni (okanye yayiliphupha elicace gca ndaza ndavuka ekuqaleni). Ilizwi lalifunda amazwi engqondweni nasemoyeni wam, kwaye ndandisazi ukuba lilizwi leNkosi, kwaye lalifunda ezindlebeni zam amazwi endingakwazanga ukuwaqonda kuba amazwi ayequkuqela engapheli. Kodwa kunye namazwi kwafika uxolo olukhulu olwahlala phezu kwam ngokupheleleyo. Amazwi ayemnandi kakhulu ezindlebeni zam, kwaye ndandiphumle. Emva koko ngequbuliso, ndaphuma kuloo meko ndinesitshisa esibi kakhulu, isisu sam sasijijekile, ndaza ndatsiba ndaphuma ebhedini, ndikrwitshwa ndikhohlela. Ndehla ndaya ezantsi ndasela amanzi amaninzi, nto leyo yanceda kancinci kodwa ayizange isizole isisu sam. Ndahlala ndiphaphile okwethutyana emva koko, kodwa andizange ndiyazi ukuba kuthetha ntoni konke oku.

Kwaye malunga nobungqina, apho uYehova athi, "Ilizwi leendudumo ezisixhenxe LIYATHETHA!" Ngomhla we-7 kuMeyi, 2024, ndibe nosuku olunzima kakhulu, ndaza ndafika ekhaya ndalala kwangoko. Ndandilele kade kakhulu ngobusuku obandulelayo kuba ndandingakwazi ukulala, nto leyo ehlala indenzekela. Kwakukho isiphango esibi esinemvula enkulu xa ndandilala, kodwa yonke imibane neendudumo zazikude lee. Ndalala ukuze ndilale malunga nentsimbi yesi-7 ngobo busuku, nto leyo ekwangoko kum, ndaza ndazunywa yinto endacinga ukuba bubuthongo. Andazi nokuba ndakha ndalala na, kodwa ndangena embonweni okanye kwimeko yephupha elicace gca, kwaye ndandisiqonda kakuhle isiphango esasenzeka. Amazwi namazwi ayeza kum evela kwiindawo ezibonakala zahlukile, kwaye ayekuyo yonke indawo. Andikhumbuli umxholo wamazwi ahlukeneyo, ngaphandle kwelinye. Yayililizwi elicace gca, kwaye onke amanye amazwi athi cwaka. Ilizwi lathi, "Ilizwi leendudumo ezisixhenxe LIYATHETHA!" Xa lawo mazwi ayethethwa, ndavuswa kwangoko sesona sandi sikhulu sendudumo endakha ndasiva. Ndahlala apho ebhedini yam, ndimangalisiwe ngokupheleleyo.

Xa ndabelana ngale nto nomfazi wam emva kosuku okanye ezimbini, sasikwivulandi yethu yangaphambili. Wandibamba ngesandla wandikhokelela ngasemva kwendlu ukuya kwiyadi engasemva. Wandixelela ukuba yena nonyana wethu babekwindawo yokuhlala xa eso sandi sendudumo satshoyo. Ukudanyaza kombane kwakhanyisa kwaza kwashukumisa igumbi lokuhlala. Ngokucacileyo, wawutshone kufutshane kakhulu neyadi yethu engasemva, encinci kakhulu, njengoko sihlala edolophini. Kodwa asizange sibone monakalo naphi na. Ndikhe ndabona isiphumo xa umbane utshona ngogonyamelo emhlabeni okanye entweni esemhlabeni, kwaye ushiya intshabalalo kwinqanaba elithile. Wabona ukudanyaza kombane, kwaye sobabini seva isandi esikhulu kakhulu sendudumo, kodwa akukho monakalo wawufumaneka naphi na.


Juni 12, 2024

INkosi indityhilele kanye ukuba uyintoni lo msebenzi, kwaye unesihloko esinje. Ndifumene umxholo weNcwadi eNcinci esezandleni zeNgelosi enamandla kwiSityhilelo 10, eyambathiswe ngelifu kwaye umnyama wawusentloko yayo kwaye ubuso bayo babunjengelanga neenyawo zayo zinjengeentsika zomlilo. Ezi zimela amaxesha neminqophiso ehambelanayo, zonke eziphelela ebusweni bukaYesu Krestu, okuphela koZelweyo kaYehova, nomfanekiso kaThixo ongunaphakade.

Umnyama luphawu olwanikwa kumnqophiso kaNowa awawenza noNowa emva konogumbe. Ilifu neentsika zomlilo zimela umnqophiso kaThixo noMoses. Ubuso bukaYesu HaMashiach bukhanya njengelanga kwaye bungumnqophiso omtsha. Ezi zigidimi zisixhenxe zinomgwebo kwabawileyo nakwabanye abaphambi konogumbe kaNowa ukuya kuthi ga esiphelweni, kwinto esekwilixa elizayo ukusukela kumhla wanamhlanje.
