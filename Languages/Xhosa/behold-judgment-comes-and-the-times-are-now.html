Ubungqina Obubini: Ibandla
Emisebenzini kaYohane uMtyhileli
Juni 04, 2024

Yabonani! Umgwebo Uyeza kwaye Amaxesha Ngala Ngoku!

Amaxesha afikile okuzaliseka, bantwanana bam, ewe ukuzaliseka kwezinto zonke eziphathelele kwiveki yamashumi asixhenxe kaDaniyeli. Kuba iveki yamashumi asixhenxe kaDaniyeli yiveki yeminyaka apho usuku ngalunye lungunyaka. Iveki yamashumi asixhenxe kaDaniyeli sele iza kuzalisekiswa kwaye iya kuqala xa abathembekileyo eBandleni lam bexwilwa besiya kundihlangabeza emafini. Ixilongo lokugqibela liya kuvakala kwaye ngelizwi elinamandla lesiphatha-zithunywa, abafileyo kuKrestu baya kuvuka kuqala, nani nina baphilayo niseleyo niya kunyuswa emafini kunye nabo, kwaye niya kuhlala ninam ngonaphakade, mna Krestu Yesu, kuba ndingumNazarete ovusiweyo owafela isono kwaba kanye nongasayi kuphinda afe. Kuba ubukumkani bam buvela kunaphakade ukuya kunaphakade, kwaye ubukumkani bam abuyi kuba nasiphelo, kuba uYahweh utshilo.

Xa abathembekileyo bam bethathwa besisiwa emazulwini, nomthinteli uya kususwa, kwaye kuya kuqala iveki yamashumi asixhenxe kaDaniyeli. Uninzi lwenu luyasokola ngombono wam, uYeshua HaMashiach, ndixhwila abathembekileyo bam kumgwebo ongabafanelanga, kodwa ndiya kunenza niqonde, ukuba nithe neza kum ngokuthobeka komntwana omncinci, ewe ubumsulwa nomdla wokwazi womncinci. Kuba nina ningabantwanana bam, ewe abantwanana bam ababona ngokungacacanga kwisibuko nabaprofeta ngokuyinxenye. Ngoko ke ndithi kuni, zithobeni niguquke kwikratshi lenu elingenaxabiso, kuba sisono, kwaye yizani kum nifunde kwaye namkele isibhalo esingcwele. Ukwala naliphi na icandelo lesibhalo sisono, kuba lonke iLizwi likaThixo linyulu kwaye kufuneka lamkelwe ngentlonipho yokuthobela, kwaye mna, uYesu umNazarete, ndililizwi elenziwe inyama.

Ukuhlanganiselwa kwabangcwele bam kum emafini kwakuyimfihlelo efihliweyo ngaphakathi kwimfihlelo enkulu kaThixo. Ngoko yayiyimfihlelo ngaphakathi kwimfihlelo kwaye ke ngoko ayizange ityhilwe de yatyhilwa kuMpostile uPawulos kwiileta zakhe. Kuye kwatyhilwa imfihlelo enkulu kaThixo, kuba noPawulos wayeligosa leemfihlelo zikaThixo, kuba ndamthuma ngolo hlobo. Ngoko ke qondani ukuba ukukhanyela isibhalo kwesi siganeko kukundikhanyela mna nokuba phakathi kweentombi eziziyatha.

Ngoko ke ndiza kunicacisela izinto ezininzi eziza kwenzeka, ndiqhubeka nemisebenzi endayinika uYohane uMtyhileli, kuba ubungqina bokutyhilwa kwam bubhalelwe ukufihla njengoko butyhila. Ngoko ke ngoku ndiza kunixelela izinto phambi kokuba zenzeke ukuze nilungele iintsuku ezizayo. Kuba mna, Krestu Yesu, ndiya kubangela ukuba abantu bam beme emisebenzini apho ndibathumela khona ukuze nigqibe ngokupheleleyo imisebenzi emininzi enamandla endinayo ukuba niyifeze.

Njengoko ukuhlanganisana kusenzeka, umthinteli uyasuswa, kwaye intshabalalo enkulu iya kuwuthimba umhlaba, ngequbuliso nangokuqhwanyaza kweliso. Kuba njengoko umthinteli esusiwe, akukho nto iya kuhlala ithintela ububi ehlabathini neenkanuko neminqweno emibi yoluntu. Imfazwe enkulu iya kuqhambuka emhlabeni wonke, kwaye umzalwana uya kuphinda avukele ukubulala umzalwana wakhe, kwaye iya kuba nkulu intshabalalo yabo. Izizwe ezininzi ziya kuwa njengoko iBhabheli eyiMfihlelo, ewe elohenyukazi likhulu elihleli phezu kwamanzi amaninzi, liya kwenziwa isizwe esingenamntu ngeyure enye.

Eso sixeko sikhulu, ewe eso sizwe seZizwe eziManyeneyo ezininzi, siya kutshatyalaliswa ngokupheleleyo; kuba sifanele isahlulo esiphindwe kabini somgwebo, kwaye siyakufanela. Kuba saphila kamnandi esonweni esikhulu; sihleli ngekratshi elikhulu sicinga ukuba asisokuze siwe kwaye izinto ziya kuqhubeka, izolo njenganamhlanje, nangomso kuya kuhlala kunjalo kungaguquki. Kwaye ihenyukazi elikhulu lilonakalisile ihlabathi ngendebe yegolide esesandleni salo, izele ngamasikizi nokungcola kohenyo lwalo. Ngoko ke umgwebo walo uya kuwa ngequbuliso. Umlilo omkhulu uya kuthimba izixeko zalo njengoko imijukujelwa isiwa; kuya kubakho ukuzamazama okukhulu, kwaye eso sizwe siya kwahlulwa phakathi. Iinkalo zalo zonxweme ziya kukhukuliswa kakhulu, kwaye asisayi kuphinda sivuke, kuba zikhulu izono zalo kwaye mkhulu nomgwebo walo.

Njengoko imfazwe iqhambuka elizweni kwaye abakhweli-mahashe besenza imigwebo yabo emhlabeni, usizi olukhulu nentshabalalo ziya kufika ehlabathini. Ihlabathi elikhoyo ngoku alisayi kuphinda libekho; xa umthinteli esusiwe, yonke into iya kwahluka. Kuya kubakho ukufa okukhulu, indyikitya yokufa, indlala enkulu, nentswela-bulungisa enkulu; kanti ke abayi-144,000 bam baya kukhuselwa kwingqumbo nomgwebo, kuba baya kuthwala itywina likaThixo ophilayo, ewe uYahweh, emabunzini abo; kuba baya kutywinwa ngolo hlobo, kwaye ndithumela iingelosi zam ukuba zibatywine. Ngoko ke isiphithiphithi esikhulu nokuchas' umthetho kuya kulawula usuku. Iinyanga ezingama-42 zokuqala ziya kuba ngamaxesha entshutshiso enkulu nenkathazo enkulu, kodwa imbandezelo enkulu iya kwenzeka kwiinyanga ezingama-42 zesibini: kuba zikho iinyanga ezingama-84 kwiintsuku ezisixhenxe zeminyaka yenkathazo kaYakobi. Ngoko ke isiqingatha sesibini yimbandezelo enkulu, kodwa imbandezelo nentshutshiso elizweni iya kuba yoyikeka kubemi bomhlaba lonke elo xesha.

Kuba mna, Krestu Yesu iNkosi eNgunyusiweyo, noYahweh, uThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade (kuba akukho namnye onjengaye), asinayo nayiphi na intyikityha kwimigwebo neenkathazo ezizayo. UBawo akazange adale uluntu ngothando ukuze alugwebe alutshabalalise. Noko ke, uSathana wonakalisile isitiya sikaThixo sobabalo, kwaye ngoku ukonakala kuya kucocwa ukuze ubulungisa bulawule. Ngoko ke akukho lukhuni lukhetho losindiso kwabo balukhanyele ubizo lwam losindiso ngexesha lobabalo. Abantu kufuneka bavuke ebuthongweni babo nakwintyikityha yesono, kuba akusekho xesha kwaye ngoku lixesha lokukhetha ngonaphakade, okanye ukhetho luya kwenziwa emgwebeni kwabo bandikhanyelayo ngokwala ukukhetha. Iinkathazo ezininzi nemigwebo ezayo ziya kuvusa abaninzi kusindiso olungunaphakade, zibaxhwila emlilweni ongunaphakade; kuba abaninzi banxilile yintyikityha yesono, ngoko ke intyikityha iya kususwa. Abayi kuba nayo intyikityha kwezi ntsuku, kwaye ukusinda kuya kuba ngumcimbi womzuzu nomzuzu ngamanye amaxesha, kwaye ingozi eninzi iya kwanda.

Kuya kuba njalo ukuba abathembekileyo nabanyuliweyo bam phakathi kweBandla lam baya kufihlwa kude namaxesha enkathazo kaYakobi emazulwini. Kwaye imisebenzi yabo iya kugwetywa ngomlilo, kwaye nabani na omisebenzi yakhe ishiyekileyo uya kufumana umvuzo kwaye nabani na omisebenzi yakhe itshiweyo uya kulahlekelwa, kodwa bona ngokwabo baya kusindiswa, ngomlilo. Baya kuthatha iindawo zabo kulungelelwaniso abalulungiselelweyo emazulwini. Kuba mna, uYeshua HaMashiach, ndikufanele ukuthatha incwadi nokuvula amatywina, kwaye ngokuvulwa kwamatywina abakhweli-mahashe bayakhululwa. Kwaye baya kuphuma emhlabeni ukuze boyise, benze imfazwe, babangele indyikitya yokufa nokufa okukhulu. Amaxilongo asixhenxe aya kuvakala, ooyeha bayeza, kwaye izitya ezisixhenxe zomsindo wengqumbo kaThixo ziya kugalelwa emhlabeni ngokupheleleyo.

Imfazwe yehlabathi ezayo ayiyi kuthatha iminyaka, kuba uSathana uyazi ukuba unexesha elifutshane, kodwa imfazwe iya kuhlala ikhona ngala maxesha enkathazo. Emabhodlweni nakwinkxwaleko yemfazwe kuya kuphuma irhamncwa elivela elwandle, nerhamncwa elivela emhlabeni, elikwangumprofeti wobuxoki. Aba babini baya kusebenza kunye, njengoko besebenza kunye emfihlekweni ukuze kutshatyalaliswe iBhabheli eyiMfihlelo. Kwaye iimpondo ezilishumi, ezi ziya kulithiya ihenyukazi, zilenze kube senkangala, libe ze, zitye inyama yalo, zilitshise ngomlilo. Kuba uThixo ufake ezintliziyweni zabo ukuba bafeze intando yakhe, bavumelane, banike ubukumkani babo kwirhamncwa, de amazwi kaThixo azaliseke. (Ngoko ke abakhweli-mahashe baya kukhwela.)

Baya kuphakama benikela ngoxolo, ukhuseleko, nobuhloneli bobuxoki. Baya kwenza kungabikho mthethweni nantoni na enento yokwenza nam, uYesu Krestu, ehlabathini lonke, kwaye baya kulwa nabangcwele baboyise, kuba kunikwe ezandleni zabo ukuba bakwenze oko. Abantu babo ekuthiwa ngabasemzini baya kuzibonakalisa kwaye baya kunikela ngoncedo lwabo eluntwini, kodwa ezi ziingelosi eziwileyo ezizisa inkohliso enkulu. Yabonani, ndizisa iingxilimbela ukuze ndizalisekise ingqumbo yam, kwaye utshilo uYahweh. Njengoko kwakunjalo ngemihla kaNowa, kuya kuba njalo kwiingelosi eziwileyo zamandulo; kwanabo kuthiwa ngoothixo bamandulo (abangoothixo) baya kufuna ukwakha kwakhona ixesha labo legolide emhlabeni, kwaye bakhululelwe umgwebo, kodwa abayi kuba nalo ixesha legolide. Amasikizi abo aya kukhululwa, kuba umthinteli uya kuba engekho kwaye esusiwe ngokupheleleyo, kwaye akukho nto iya kubabamba. Abo bathanda ubomi babo baya kulahlekelwa bubo ngonaphakade, kuba baya kufuna uphawu ukuze bathathe inxaxheba kuluntu lwabo olutsha, ewe ucwangciso lwabo olutsha. Kwaye bonke abathatha uphawu lwerhamncwa baya kuba nesabelo sabo kwichibi lomlilo.

Abayi-144,000 bam abathunyelwanga ukuba bahlale ezixekweni zabo, apho kuya kufuneka bathathe uphawu ukuze baphile. Kodwa baya kuthunyelwa ngapha nangapha kwaye baya kuhlanganisa abaninzi kubo kwaye bakhathalele abaninzi kwaye baya kunceda ukuzisa izihlwele ezikhulu elusindisweni kum, uYeshua HaMashiach (ngalo naluphi na ulwimi): bonke abaya kunqula igama lam kude kube sekupheleni baya kusindiswa. UThixo uya kuzosula zonke iinyembezi emehlweni abo; yaye akusayi kubakho kufa, nasijwili, nakukhala, kwaye akusayi kubakho ntlungu: kuba izinto zangaphambili zidlule. Njengoko kwakunjalo ngemihla kaNowa, abo bahlonela uThixo baya kuhlala ngaphandle koluntu lwabo olubi lweengelosi eziwileyo, kuba olwabo luya kuba luluntu lwesono olukhokelela ekugwetyweni okungunaphakade emlilweni ongacimekiyo; ngoko ke musani ukuzimanya nabo kuba ukhetho lusisigxina. Ngoko ke abayi-144,000 bam baya kuzisa usindiso noncedo kwabaninzi.

Baya kwenza imiqondiso emikhulu, imimangaliso, nezimanga, kwaye ndiya kubakhokela inyathelo ngenyathelo, kwaye ndiya kulathisa iindlela zabo kwabo ndibathumela kubo. Kwaye ndiya kuhlanganisa iigusha zam kubo ukuze zikhokelelwe kukhuseleko. Kwaye nabani na ongathandi ubomi bakhe kude kube sekupheleni uya kusindiswa. Kwaye abo balumkileyo baya kukhanya njengokuqaqamba kwesibhakabhaka; kwaye abo bajikela abaninzi ebulungiseni njengeenkwenkwezi ngonaphakade kanaphakade.

Irhamncwa elivela elwandle liya kuphakama kwaye liya kukhonza iinjongo ezimbi zabo balawula ilizwe lesithembiso esonweni esikhulu, ewe indlu yesikhungu kaSathana. Liya kulawula iintlanga kwaye liya kuthetha izinyeliso ezininzi, kwaye liya kwenza imiqondiso nezimanga zobuxoki ezikhulu, kwaye banqula inamba eyanika amandla kwirhamncwa, kwaye banqula irhamncwa, besithi, Ngubani onjengerhamncwa? Ngubani onokulwa nalo? Indlu yesikhungu kaSathana iya kwenza konke okusemandleni ayo ukutshabalalisa nabani na onqula igama lam, kwaye irhamncwa nomprofeti wobuxoki baya kunyanzelisa ngoburhalarhume umthetho kaNowa kwiintlanga kwaye baya kubonakala ngathi bahlambulula ububi elizweni. Kodwa endaweni yoko baya kulwa nabangcwele baboyise, kwaye kunikwe irhamncwa elivela elwandle ukuba liqhubeke iinyanga ezingama-42.

Irhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni liyakuphuma nalo eluthuthwini. Liya kuba nawo onke amagunya erhamncwa lokuqala, yaye liya kwenza imiqondiso emininzi yobuxoki nemimangaliso phambi kwerhamncwa lokuqala, yaye liya kwenza umfanekiso werhamncwa, kwaye kuya kuvunyelwa umoya ukuba ubangele umfanekiso werhamncwa uthethe, usithi nabani na ongayi kuqubuda anqule umfanekiso werhamncwa lokuqala makabulawe.

Kuba irhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni baya kulithwesa isithsaba njengokumkani wamazwe esithembiso, yaye liya kuba nguMoshiach wabo ekukudala bemlindile, yaye liya kwakha itempile yabo (nangona uYahweh engalithumanga), yaye kuya kuba njalo. Baya kulamkela njengomlawuli omkhulu, onobubele, umprofeti, nokumkani. Kwaye irhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni (kwanomprofeti wabo wobuxoki) liya kubangela bonke, abancinane nabakhulu, izityebi namahlwempu, abakhululekileyo namakhoboka, ukuba bamkele uphawu esandleni sabo sokunene okanye emabunzini abo, Nokuba kungabikho namnye unokuthenga nokuba unokuthengisa, ngaphandle kwalowo unophawu, negama lerhamncwa, nenani legama lalo. Bade bawuqhelanisa umhlaba nokwamkela uphawu, yaye le nkqubo iye yaphuculwa ngexesha lento yabo ekuthiwa ngubhubhane.

(Kubhaliwe kwisiTyhilelo: Saza esesithathu isithunywa sezulu sazilandela, sisithi ngezwi elikhulu, Ukuba kukho bani na olinqulayo irhamncwa nomfanekiso walo, elwamkela uphawu lwalo ebunzini lakhe, nokuba kusesandleni sakhe, Yena lowo uya kusela kuyo iwayini yomsindo kaThixo, egalelwe ingaxutywanga endebeni yengqumbo yakhe; yaye uya kuthuthunjiswa ngomlilo nesulfure phambi kwezithunywa ezingcwele, naphambi kweMvana: Nomsi wentuthumbo yabo unyuka use ephakadeni lasephakadeni: yaye abanakuphumla imini nobusuku, abo banqula irhamncwa nomfanekiso walo, naye nabani na owamkela uphawu lwegama lalo.)

Kwiinyanga ezingama-42 zokuqala, ndiya kuwanika amandla amangqina am amabini, yaye aya kuprofeta iwaka elinamakhulu amabini anamashumi amathandathu eentsuku, ambethe ezirhwexayo. Le yiminquma emibini, nezibane ezibini ezimi phambi koThixo womhlaba. Ukuba kukho bani na ofuna ukuwenzakalisa, kuphuma umlilo emlonyeni wawo, uzitshabalalise iintshaba zawo: yaye ukuba kukho bani na ofuna ukuwenzakalisa, umele abulawe ngolu hlobo. La anamandla okuvala izulu, ukuze lingani ngeentsuku zokuprofeta kwawo: yaye anamandla phezu kwamanzi ukuwajika abe ligazi, nokubetha umhlaba ngazo zonke izibetho, nanini na athandayo. Xa ke aya kube ebugqibile ubungqina bawo, irhamncwa elinyuka liphuma enzonzobileni liya kulwa nawo, liwoyise, liwabulale.

Irhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni, kwanomprofeti walo wobuxoki, ngokuqinisekileyo liya kuzisa isikizi lesiphanziso. Liya kubangela ukuba idini lemihla ngemihla liyeke lize libeke isikizi lesiphanziso endaweni yalo. Kwaye ndatsho njalo noMateyu wabhala wathi: Xa ngoko nithe nalibona isikizi lesiphanziso, ekwakuthethwa ngalo nguDaniyeli umprofeti, limi endaweni engcwele (lowo ulesayo makaqiqe), Mabathi abakwelakwaYuda basabele ezintabeni: Lowo uphezu kwendlu makangehli aye kuthatha nto endlwini yakhe: Nalowo usentsimini makangabuyeli umva aye kuthatha iingubo zakhe. Yeha ke kwabamithiyo, nakwanyisayo ngaloo mihla! Kodwa thandazani ukuba ukusaba kwenu kungabi sebusika, kungabi ngomhla wesabatha: Kuba ngoko kuya kubakho imbandezelo enkulu, ekungazanga kubekho injalo kususela ekuqalekeni kwehlabathi kude kube ngoku, hayi, kwaye akusayi kuze kubekho.

(Banoyolo abafileyo, abo bafela eNkosini kususela ngoku: Ewe, utsho uMoya, ukuze baphumle emisebenzini yabo; nemisebenzi yabo iyabalandela.)

Kwaye ngoko ndiya kukhusela abaninzi ndibafihle abaninzi entlango kwimbandezelo enkulu, kuba ukuba imihla ibingashunqulwanga, bekungayi kusinda nyama. Emva koko irhamncwa nomprofeti wobuxoki baya kuhlanganisa ookumkani bomhlaba nabo bonke abemi nabo abavela kubukumkani benamba ngaloo mini inkulu yedabi. Kwaye ngoko ndenza uYohane uMtyhileli wabhala wathi: Ndabona izulu livulekile, nanko ihashe elimhlophe; nalowo wayehleli phezu kwalo wayebizwa ngokuba nguThembekile noNyaniso, yaye ngebulungisa uyagweba alwe imfazwe. Amehlo akhe ayenjengelangatye lomlilo, nasentloko yakhe kwakukho izithsaba ezininzi; yaye wayenegama elibhaliweyo, ekungekho mntu wayelazi, ngaphandle kwakhe. Waye ambethe isambatho esithiwe nkxu egazini: negama lakhe kuthiwa nguLizwi likaThixo. Nemikhosi eyayisezulwini yamlandela ikhwele amahashe amhlophe, yambethe ilinen ecikizekileyo, emhlophe necocekileyo. Nasemlonyeni wakhe kuphuma ikrele elibukhali, ukuze ngalo, abethe iintlanga: yaye uya kuzilawula ngentonga yentsimbi: yaye unyathela isixovulelo sewayini yoburhalarhume nomsindo kaThixo uSomandla.

Kwaye irhamncwa nomprofeti wobuxoki baya kubanjwa bobabini bephila baze baphoswe echibini lomlilo elivutha isulfure. Nentsalela yabulawa ngekrele lalowo wayehleli phezu kwehashe, elo krele laliphuma emlonyeni wakhe: nazo zonke iintaka zahlutha yinyama yazo. Ndabona iitrone, bahlala phezu kwazo, banikwa umgwebo: ndabona imiphefumlo yabo babenqunyulwe iintloko ngenxa yobungqina bukaYesu, nangenxa yelizwi likaThixo, nababengalinqulanga irhamncwa, nomfanekiso walo, bengalamkelanga uphawu lwalo emabunzini abo, nasezandleni zabo; yaye baphila balawula noKristu iminyaka eliwaka.

Kwaye ngoko isithunywa sam siya kuhla sivela emazulwini, sinesitshixo senzonzobila nomxokelelwane omkhulu esiya kumbopha ngawo uSathana enzonzobileni iminyaka eliwaka size sibeke itywina ukuze angaphindi azikhohlise iintlanga iminyaka eliwaka. Ekupheleni kolawulo lwam lweminyaka eliwaka, uSathana uya kukhululwa enzonzobileni okwexeshana elincinane.

Uya kuphuma aye kuzikhohlisa iintlanga ezimbombeni zone zomhlaba, uGogi noMagogi ukuze abahlanganisele ndawonye njengentlabathi yolwandle, yaye uya kuhlasela inkampu yabangcwele aze angqinge isixeko esingcwele, kwaye kuya kuhla umlilo uvela kuYahweh uze ubatshise ngokupheleleyo. Nomtyholi owabakhohlisayo uya kubotshwa aze aphoswe ephila kwelo chibi likhulu lomlilo, elivutha isulfure kumalangatye ashushu kakhulu aluhlaza. Kwaye kuya kubakho umvuzo omkhulu wobubi kwaye kuya kwenziwa ubulungisa obukhulu entloko kaSathana, kwiingelosi zakhe eziwileyo, nakubo bonke abazibandakanye nemvukelo yabo; yaye abasayi kuphinda bavuke, yaye baya kulityalwa ngokupheleleyo; yaye abasayi kuphinda bafike engqondweni.

Emva koko uYahweh uya kwenza izulu elitsha nomhlaba omtsha, yaye uya kwenza ikhaya lakhe phakathi kwabantu, yaye kuya kuba seluxolweni nasekubeni mvisiswano ngonaphakade, yaye akusayi kuphinda kubekho ixesha lenkathazo, yaye abasayi kuphinda bone, kungaba ngekratshi okanye ngokungahloneli Thixo, yaye yonke imihla yabo iya kusikelelwa.

NdingumNazarete ovusiweyo owafa esonweni kwaba kanye nongasafiyo.

NdinguYeshua, kwanaye uMesiya wenu wenene, lowo uYahweh amthumileyo ukuba azalisekise izithembiso ezenziwa kooBawo.

Ndingulowo amehlo akhe anjengelangatye lomlilo, nasentloko yam kune zithsaba ezininzi.

NdinguNkosi yeeNkosi noKumkani wooKumkani, yaye ngebulungisa ndiza kugweba ndenze imfazwe.

Ndingulowo uya kulawula akhokele abali-144,000 bam, yaye baya kucula ingoma entsha ekungekho mntu unokuyifunda.

Ndingulowo uya koyisa ubukumkani bukaSathana, yaye ndiya kutyumza intloko yakhe.

Ndingulowo uYahweh amthumele usindiso lwabo bonke abakholwayo, kwaye ixesha lam lifikile. Khangelani! Umgwebo uyeza kwaye amaxesha ngoku.
