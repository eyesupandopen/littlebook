Kude kube Nini?

ILizwi leNkosi elinikwe nguYesu Krestu.

Kude kube nini, kude kube nini, abantu bam bengayihoyi intswahla yam? Baya kunamathela ezonweni zabo kude kube nini? Baya kuphila kulonwabo lwesono kude kube nini? Guqukani. Guqukani. Guqukani! Buyelani kum ngentliziyo yenu iphela. Buyelani eNkosini uThixo wenu nasezingalweni zam ezoluliweyo!

KumTshakazi wam: Ndikunike ubomi, kodwa ubomi bakho bufihliwe kum; yaye xa ndiya kubonakala, nawe uya kubonakala kubo obo bomi ndikunike bona. Wamkele ngesisa, kodwa kwandixabisa kakhulu ukuhlawulela wena. Ingaba uya kulusebenzisa ubabalo lwam njengethuba lokozela? Okanye uya kulusebenzisa ubabalo lwam njengethuba lokuzihlambulula ngamandla egazi lam endakophulela lona eKalvari? Uya kusamkela na isimemo sam sokushumayeza ihlabathi elifayo uze usindise abaninzi kubazalwana bakho? Okanye uya kusigatya isimemo sam uze ukhethe iintywenka zesono zexesha eliphelayo?

Kuni nonke, ndithi: Buyelani kum ngentliziyo yenu iphela; guqukani ngomzimba nangomphefumlo wenu wonke! Funani ubuso bam nize namkele uxolelo lwam, kuba usuku lweNkosi lusemnyango, njengoko ndiye ndabalumkisa abantu bam. Andiyi kuthi cwaka, yaye ndiyanibongoza ukuba nivule imilomo yenu ukuze nisindise abo nibathandayo nabo ndiya kubazisa kuni. Khumbulani uthando lwenu lokuqala, nize nize kum phambi kokuba kuvakale ixilongo.
