Selah!

Nceda uqaphele, oku kufunda kusebenza njengesikhokelo seziganeko eziza kwenzeka kungekudala, kwaye oku kwenziwa ngenjongo yokuba abantu beNkosi, abathembekileyo kuKrestu, noMtshakazi kaKrestu oza kuhlwithwa ngokukhawuleza ngoku. Kunikwe ukuze sazi kwaye sikwazi ukuvakalisa isiphelo kwasekuqaleni, nangaphambi kokuba senzeke. Ezi ziganeko ziya kwenzeka kanye njengoko zinikiwe nguKrestu Yesu nangesandla sikaYHVH, uMkhulu onguYE NDINGUYE, apho zonke izinto zivela khona ezinikwe ngegama elinamandla negunya likaKrestu Yesu, okwanguYeshua HaMashiach.

Oku kuthathe ixesha nomzamo ukuqulunqwa, kwaye kusebenza njengokufundwa kwesigwebo esishunqulweyo kwiBhabheli Eyimfihlo, kunye noluhlu lweziganeko ukuze uMtshakazi wokwenene kaKrestu, Abathembekileyo kuKrestu, kunye ne-144,000 bangalahlekiswa yimilinganiso yobuxoki. YiNcwadi Encincinci eya kulawula amaxesha okuphela ngeeNdudumo ezisiXhenxe esele zityhiniwe, hayi abaprofeti bobuxoki abazithumayo bethetha ububhanxa obuvela ezintliziyweni zabo. Selah!

Nceda uqaphele: Oku kujongwe njengesikhokelo sokufunda kwabathembekileyo ukuze nazi ukuba kuza ntoni kwaye sonke siphumle kuKrestu kwaye sixhomekeke kumazwi AKHE hayi kumazwi abaprofeti bobuxoki abangayaziyo iNkosi yethu, nabangathethi egameni layo nakancinci.

1Abangemalungisa Abayi Kulidla Ilifa Ubukumkani BukaThixo!Epreli 19, 2024
Kivela kuYeshua HaMashiach:

Nyana wam, ukuba uthe wamkela amazwi am, wayifihla imiyalelo yam kuwe; ukuze uthobe indlebe yakho kubulumko, uyise intliziyo yakho ekuqondeni. Musani ukuzenza lukhuni iintliziyo zenu, njengasekuxhokonxeni, ngomhla wokulingwa entlango: nazi ukuba mna, iNkosi uYesu Krestu, ndihlala ndinani kwaye ndihlala ndikhona ebomini benu njengoko nibambelele kum nosindiso lwam, kuba mna ndiyintloko yoMzimba, kwaye ndiyiNkosi yenu ethembekileyo, Mtshakazi wam othandekayo. Kuba ndinilungiselele indawo nekamva, kwaye kukho umvuzo omkhulu kwababiziweyo bam, nabathembekileyo, nabanyuliweyo.

Andinibizelanga elahlekweni nasesohlwayweni, kwaye andinibizelanga ehlazweni nasekuzigwebeni; anifundanga na ukuba akukho kugwetywa kwabo bakuKrestu Yesu, abangahambi ngokwenyama kodwa behamba ngokomoya? Ngoko ke kholwani ngamazwi am! Andinibizelanga ukuba niphile emthunzini wezono zenu zangaphambili, okanye niphile emthunzini wezenzo zenu zobutsha, kodwa ndinibizele ukuba nifele kwiindlela zenu ezindala zesono nithabathe ubomi obutsha kum njengoko nilahla inyama yenu, njengoko nilahla izono zenu nihamba ebomini obutsha kum. Yabonani! Ndenza zonke izinto zibe ntsha! Alikho na eli ithemba lenu?

Bantwana bam, ndinikhuphe kububi beli hlabathi lifayo langoku, kwaye ndinihlangule emandleni obumnyama ndaniguqulela ebukumkanini bam, ubukumkani bukaThixo, obukhokelwa ndim, uKumkani wooKumkani neNkosi yeeNkosi, apho uYahweh enguThixo, kuba akukho wumbi. Ndinibizele eluzukweni nasebunyulu kum, kwaye ndinihlangule ebukumkanini bukaSathana, ubukumkani bobumnyama, ndanisa eBukumkanini bam, apho kukho ukukhanya! Ngoko ke, vuka wena uleleyo, uvuke kwabafileyo, kwaye mna, uYesu Krestu, ndiya kukunika ukukhanya.

Anifundanga na kwiincwadi endazithumela kuMtshakazi wam, endazithumela kuni, lona kanye iBandla lam? Anazi na ukuba abangemalungisa abayi kubudla ilifa ubukumkani bukaThixo? Musani ukukhohliswa: kuba abahenyuzi, nabakhonzi bezithixo, nabakrexezi, nabafanasini, nabazisebenzisa kakubi nabantu besini esinye, Namasela, nababawayo, namanxila, nabatshabhisi, nabaphangi, abayi kubudla ilifa ubukumkani bukaThixo. Kwaye babenjalo abanye benu: kodwa nihlanjiwe, kodwa ningcwalisiwe, kodwa nigwetyelwe egameni leNkosi uYesu, nangoMoya kaThixo wethu.

Aniziqondi na iimfundiso zam, o, bandla elidiki-diki laseLawodike? Andinibizelanga ukuba nibuyele enyameni yenu, kwaye andinibizelanga ukuba niqhubeke kwiindlela zenu ezingalunganga. Cingani, niqwalasele; lowo ucinga ukuba umi, makalumke angawi! Zilumkeleni, nemfundiso: hlalani kuzo: kuba ngokwenza oku, niya kuzisindisa nina, nabo banivayo. Qondani, bantwana bam abancinci abadikidiki, ukuba aninakuhlala ubomi bokulalanisa phakathi kokulungileyo nokubi, nicinga ukuba nithe tye phambi kwam, xa nikhetha okuncinci kububi obubini kwizithethe zenu zonyulo endingazivumeliyo okanye ndingazixhasiyo.

Phumani kuyo, bantu bam, njengoko NDINGANIKEZANGA sisombululo sezopolitiko, kwaye andinikanga nkokeli yehlabathi ukuba yenze nantoni na ibe nkulu kwakhona. Nikhohlisiwe, kwaye nilungiselelwa ukulandela nokunika imvume kunyana wentshabalalo ohamba phakathi kwenu, nangona engekatyhilwa ngokupheleleyo. Kodwa nina enicela ukuqonda niya kumazi ukuba ungubani, ukuze nikuphephe ukukhohliswa kwakhe. Hayi, ukulalanisa akukho kuni, njengoko ningabantwana bam nabazalwana bam, kwaye andiluzuzanga usindiso lwenu ngokunikela ubomi bam njengentlawulelo yabaninzi ngokulalanisa noSathana; kuba ukulalanisa kukuzibandakanya nemikhosi yabo. Ngoko ke musani ukwenza impazamo: Ninokuma nam ebulungiseni nasebungcweleni bokwenene, okanye nizibandakanye nemikhosi kaSathana kubukumkani bakhe obufayo, ngoko ke GUQUKANI!

Nizuza ntoni ngokulalanisa nobubi? Aniqondi na ukuba nimele ukoyisa ububi ngokulungileyo? Ngoko ke yimani niqine kum niyeke ukuguqu-guquka njengentlanzi ephume emanzini. Uninzi lwenu lusaphetshethwa yiyo yonke imimoya yeemfundiso nobuqhetseba, apho balalela khona ukuze bakhohlise. Mna, Krestu Yesu, ndithethile, kwaye andiyi kuthuliswa. Uninzi lwenu luzilalanisile nina nebandla lam, kwaye ndiyanibuza: niya kudinga-dinga phakathi kwezimvo ezimbini kude kube nini? Ukuba iNkosi inguThixo, mlandeleni: kodwa ukuba nguBhahali, mlandeleni yena. Ngokulula, aninakulalanisa nikhethe naluphi na uhlobo lobubi nicinge ukuba nimele mna; xa nikhetha ububi, nikhetha imithombo yobo bubi njengenkosi nomphathi wenu nibe ngabakhonzi besono. Anazi na, ukuba enizinikela kuye njengabakhonzi bokuthobela, ningabakhonzi bakhe enibathobelayo: nokuba kukwesono kuse ekufeni, nokuba kukokuthobela kuse ebulungiseni?

Ndiveni kwaye nindive kakuhle, Bandla lam; yazini ukuba ndimi ndicaphukile zizenzo ezixhaphakileyo eluhlangeni lwenu nakwihlabathi liphela, eniye nazilalanisa nazivumela eBandleni lam ukuze nicaphukise abancinci bam. Njengakwimihla kaNowa, kuya kuba njalo. Imihla kaNowa yayilixesha apho umhlaba wawulawulwa ngamasikizi amabi enziwe ziingelosi eziwileyo, imbewu yazo kanye, imbewu yobubi eyenzelwe intshabalalo yenu epheleleyo: kwaye ziya kubuya, kuba yabonani, ndizisa iingxilimbela ukuze ndizalisekise ingqumbo yam. Zakufezekisa oku ngokususa nokwaphula imida yendalo awayibekayo uBawo ukuze uxolo nobulungisa lulawule, kodwa ngokususa nokuwela ngokuziphatha okubi imida kanye ebonakalayo kwindalo ngokwayo, awayimisayo uBawo, uninzi lwenu luzingcolisile ngemvume yenu yobubi obuxhaphakileyo kumaxesha enu. Into ebe ikho, yiyo leyo iya kubakho: naleyo yenziweyo, yiyo leyo iya kwenziwa; kwaye akukho nto intsha phantsi kwelanga. Anifundanga na, o, nina bokholo oluncinci? Ukususa imida kaThixo kubangela ukuphambana, nokugula, nokudideka kwezinto zonke. Uninzi lwenu luyaziyolisa kwisigulo sengqondo, nicinga ukuba indoda inokuba ngumfazi nomfazi abe yindoda, kwaye nikhokelela abantwana benu kwiinkohliso zenu. Uninzi lwenu luya kwiicawa ezikhokelwa ngabafundisi bobuxoki abangamafanasini abavulelekileyo nabazidlayo kwisigulo sabo, kwaye nicinga ukuba bathetha egameni lam? Yazini oku: ukuba nikuloo meko yesono, ndiya kuninikela kwiinkanuko enizilangazelela ngokungakhathali, kwaye niya kuvuna isiqhamo seendlela zenu ezingcolileyo.

Yabonani! Ngenxa yesi sizathu, uThixo wabanikela kwiinkanuko ezilihlazo: kuba nabafazi babo batshintsha ukusetyenziswa kwendalo bakwenza okuchasene nendalo: Kanjalo namadoda, eshiya ukusetyenziswa kwendalo komfazi, atsha kwiinkanuko zawo omnye komnye, amadoda namadoda esenza okungafanelekanga, kwaye efumana kuwo umvuzo wempazamo yawo owawufanelekile. Kodwa hayi, akwanelanga ukukhokelwa ngabafanasini nobuxoki babo; nivumela abantwana benu ukuba balalaniswe kwaye banqunyulwe phambi kwam. Kukho isohlwayo esikhulu esilindele ezi zenzo, kwaye aziyi kushiywa zingohlwaywanga. Kanti nisaqhubeka nokuphambana kwenu, kwaye ngaphandle kokuba niguquke, ndiya kunikhafula emlonyeni wam.

Yizani kum niguqukile, nihlale engxoweni nasethuthwini, ukuze ndiyazi inyaniso yokuguquka kwenu kwaye nilile nikhale ngezono zenu ukuze ningahlawuli ixabiso esigwebeni. Uninzi lwenu lunekratshi kakhulu kuba nizama ukundenza iNkosi ngelixa nisenentliziyo engenakholo, kuba ngentliziyo umntu ukholelwa ebulungiseni. Ngoko ke yibani nokholo ukuze nisindiswe; ukholo lwenu luya kubonakaliswa kubo bonke ngokulahla kwenu isono nokuphuma kwiindlela zenu ezimdaka. Abangendawo bahamba macala onke, xa abona bantu babi bephakanyiswa. Kodwa nisaqhubeka nivumelana neendlela zabantu baseSodom, kwaye nincedisa ukuzisa ububi obukhulu elizweni, kangangokuba nivotela abaxhaphazi babantwana ukuba bakhokele amazwe enu. Niswele nelona nqanaba lisisiseko sokuqonda, kwaye anilandeli ndlela yam; kwaye ukuba niyaqhubeka, ndiya kuninikela ngokupheleleyo kwiinkanuko zenu ezilihlazo zokutshatyalaliswa kwenyama ukuze umoya usindiswe.

Mandivule amehlo enu, nina eningaziyo ngokuzithandela kwaye niyimfama, nina nihleli ebumdakeni nasebumnyameni, niqengqeleka eludakeni. Aninakunika imvume yenu ebubini nicinge ukuba nikhonza mna, kuba ndabuchasa ububi nesihendo kwada kwaphuma ithontsi lokugqibela legazi kum laya emhlabeni obomvu, kuba ndingumAdam wokugqibela kwaye ndenziwe umoya ophilileyo. Uninzi lwenu lumfanyekiswe ngamazwi amnandi neentetho ezintle kwaye ninika imvume yenu epheleleyo kwizaphuli-mthetho nabantu aboyikekayo abaxhaphaza abantwana. Baxhaphaza abantwana, bathuthumbisa abantwana ngenxa yeenkanuko zabo ezimbi: Kuba sinjalo isohlwayo sikaThixo esiza kwiBhabheli Eyimfihlakalo, kodwa uninzi lwenu luyaqhubeka lunika imvume yenu kwezi zilo zingenangqondo ezibekelwe bucala imililo yesihogo engunaphakade engayi kucinywa.

Phumani kuyo, bantu bam, kungenjalo niya kwabelana ngezibetho zayo nesigwebo esoyikekayo esiza kuyo; kwaye ndinilumkisile. Kuba bunantoni na ubudlelwane bukaKrestu noBheliyali? Ukuba nabelana ngobuxoki bayo, niya kwabelana ngezibetho zayo; ngoko ke zahlukaniseni ngokupheleleyo ngelizwi nangesenzo, kwaye ningavumelani nayo nakancinci. Musani ukuphila ubomi obungakhathaliyo, obulalanisayo; kuba ndiya kubohlwaya bonke abenjenjalo. Nicinga ukuba ngenxa yokuba ninobomi obungunaphakade aninakufumana ilahleko enkulu nesohlwayo? Ndiya kunihlutha imivuzo yenu ndiyinike abanye, kwaye niya kuba ngabona bancinci ebukumkanini bezulu ukuba niyaqhubeka, kuba utsho uYeshua HaMashiach, uMyeni, kuMtshakazi wam!


Kivela kuYahweh, uThixo Omkhulu Ongunaphakade:

O, nina bangento abenzakalisa indalo yam, nina bangento abazithengise kuSathana, nina bangento abenze uSathana uThixo wenu, nina bangento abamgatya unyana wam uYesu Krestu nosindiso endilunikeze egameni lakhe, nina bangento abavumele abantwana benu ukuba batshiswe emlilweni kaMoloki. Ndiya kuninika ihlabathi enilinqwenelayo, kwaye ndinilungiselele ihlabathi elingenam, ihlabathi elingenanyana wam uKrestu Yesu, ihlabathi elinechibi elikhulu lomlilo nesulfure elenzelwe nina kuphela.

Ndiya kuninika ubumnyama enibunqwenela kangaka, kuba ukukhanya kwam akuyi kubakho apho, kwaye niya kuchitha unaphakade nihlukene nam, uYahweh, uThixo omkhulu oqaqambileyo, obuzuko nokukhanya kwakhe kungekho nyama inokukubona kwaye ovela ngonaphakade kuse ephakadeni. Nina bavukeli, abakhohlakeleyo abaye batshabalalisa indalo yam kwasekuqaleni ngeenkanuko zenu nentiyo nomona, yazini ukuba nizifumbalele isohlwayo esikhulu nentuthumbo eningasoze niphiliswe kuyo nabo bonke abalahlekiswe bubuxoki benu nokwala uYesu Krestu, nokwala ukuguquka kubuxoki benu niye enyanisweni yam.

Nicinga ukuba ukugqwetha iindlela zam nokususa imida yam yinto elula, kodwa niya kuyiqonda ingqumbo yam kwakamsinya nje lakunichukumisa ilangabi lokuqala, kwaye niya kugutyungelwa ngumlilo nesulfure eningasoze nisinde kuko. Ngaphezu koko, nibulele, nadlwengula, naphanga, naxhaphaza, nenzakalisa, nabulala, natshabalalisa, kwaye nathatha inxaxheba kwezona zono zibi kakhulu, nkqu nokunqwenela inyama yasemzini; kwaye nizihlanganise neengelosi eziwileyo, ngoko ke ndiya kunihlanganisa nechibi lomlilo endililungiselele uMtyholi neengelosi zakhe, kwaye aniyi kusinda. Aninalo uxolo.

Ndiya kukohlwaya ngokobulungisa, kuba ndinguThixo wobulungisa, yaye ndinguThixo wempindezelo. Kodwa wena, uya kuma phambi kwam ngenkani, uzithethelela, ucinga ukuba ndiya kusuka ndoyike iindlela zakho zikasathana zokuthi: yenza oko ukuthandayo, mayibe ngumthetho opheleleyo. Akunalo uxolo. Ndiya kubuyisela, yaye uthi ndinguThixo onothando (nto leyo endiyiyo), kodwa ke ulindele ukuba ndingabohlwayi ngokobulungisa abo bangcolise indalo yam, abo bathuthumbisa baza babulala uNyana wam okuphela kwamzelweyo xa wayezinikela njengentlawulelo yabo bonke; ewe, ulindele ukuba ndingakohlwayi ngokobulungisa ngenxa yeendlela zakho ezingenaxabiso xa ubulele, wathuthumbisa, waza wenzakalisa abamsulwa yaye ulambise abantwana bam abancinci ngenxa yeenjongo zakho.

Inene, uSathana ngokwakhe ubuya kutshabalalisa yonke into eyam ngenxa yenzuzo yakhe yokubawa, kwaye nangoku uzama ukundityhola ngobuyatha kwizigwebo zam ezilungileyo xa ndiza kukhusela abam. Iintsuku zakho zokulahlekisa abantwana bam abancinci sele ziza kuphela. Khangela! Irhamncwa lakho nomprofeti wakho wobuxoki baya kutshatyalaliswa ngokupheleleyo, yaye ndiya kubaphosela bephila kwelo chibi likhulu livutha umlilo nesulfure; baya kukulinda ukuba uze kubo kwilifa lakho.

Umgwebo omkhulu uphezu kwakho, o, nkosana yobumnyama; ubukumkani bakho nabo bonke abakulandelayo sele buza kutshatyalaliswa ngokupheleleyo. Kuba aningoothixo, yaye aninakuzihlangula nina ningenaxabiso esandleni sam, yaye ndiya kunibopha kwesona sigxina sobumnyama enisinqwenelayo, ewe, ndiya kunibopha kwenzonzobila enzulu ngetyathanga elikhulu eningenakuze niliqhawule. Ewe, niya kuba senzonzobileni usuku olunye, kuba usuku olunye kuYehova lunjengeminyaka eliwaka, neminyaka eliwaka injengosuku olunye. Kwaye nasemva koko ndiya kunikhulula okwexeshana elincinci ukuze nihende kwaye niqokelele abokugqibela kwabo banyanyekayo abaya kulandela nina neendlela zenu. Kwaye ndiya kunidiliza nonke ngomlilo, zize iitrone zomgwebo zibekwe, ndize ndinigwebe nonke nina benzi bobubi, kwaye niya kuya kwisohlwayo esingunaphakade nisuke ebusweni beNkosi. Niya kutsha phambi kweengelosi naphambi kweMvana, yaye ziya kunibona nisesintuthwini nasesohlwayweni, kuba nizonele zonke, yaye niya kuzihlawulela ngokupheleleyo izono zenu ngonaphakade kanaphakade.
