Phulaphulani amazwi oMsindisi wenu, iNkosi noKumkani Bantwana bam, nithobe iindlebe zenu Emazwini eNkosi yenu, uYesu Krestu olilungisa, kuba ndingunyana wobulungisa kwaye ndiphakama ebuqaqawulini bam, kwaye nikhule njengamathole asesitalini eluxolweni nasekhuselekweni, kwaye ndinikhulisele injongo yasezulwini ekufuphi ukuba ityhilwe ngokupheleleyo kuni, banyulwa bam abathembekileyo. Yabonani! Ndingulowo uvavanya iintliziyo zabantu ukuze nazi ngokuqinisekileyo ukuba nikholo ngokwenene na, okanye nilahlekisiwe kwaye nithatyathwe zizono zenu nenkohliso enamandla yangoku ethiyelele iHlabathi.

Kuba ndingulowo uYehova abelane naye ngetrone yakhe, uKrestu Yesu iNkosi evusiweyo nenikwe amandla, kwaye ndiyiNkosi yeeNkosi noKumkani wooKumkani kwaye yabona iNgonyama yeSizwe sakwaYuda isiza emafini ukwamkela abathembekileyo bam kum ukuze bathatyathelwe emazulwini kumtshato weMvana, kuba uMtshakazi wam uzilungisile. Nezibane zakhe zizele, kwaye uzicandile iintambo zezibane zakhe, kwaye ulindele ukubonakala kwam njengentombi enobulumko, kwabo ndibahlambulule ngokupheleleyo kwaye abanasiphoso phambi kwetrone kaThixo. Kwanabo bangathinjwanga ngobuqhetseba nguBharabhas, kuba uBharabhas uzisa imvukelo enogonyamelo, imfazwe nentlekele, kuba ngoku uyintloko enye phakathi kweentloko ezininzi ezisebenza ngokubambisana, kodwa uya kuvela enguMoshiach wobuxoki, kwaye unyulwe ngabantu belizwe ukuba alawule indlu yesikhungu kaSathana. Kuba uyindlalifa yalowo ookhokho babo bamkhethayo, kwaye bazilungiselela ukumamkela njengoKumkani wabo.

Kodwa nina, O Bandla laseLawodike, ngoku kufika amaxesha enu enkangala nomgwebo. Kuba nizikhethele omnye umsindisi njengoKumkani wenu, kwanalowo ukufika kwakhe kuvakaliswa yimiqondiso nemimangaliso yobuxoki kwaye ngoku unikhokele ngookhubalo nobugqwirha. NdinguYeshuwa HaMashiach kwaye ndinithengile ngegazi lam elihlawulelayo, elingenasono, kwaye ndingulowo uya kuniphilisa, anidale ngokutsha kumoya wam, kuba ndenziwe umoya onika ubomi kuba uYehova umisele bonke ubukhulu kuNyana wakhe, kwanalowo ungumzila wokugqibela wemvana kaThixo neNgonyama yeSizwe sakwaYuda! Kwaye ngobulungisa ndiya kugweba ndenze imfazwe kuba imvana kaThixo neNgonyama yeSizwe sakwaYuda zizila wokugqibela kum, uKrestu Yesu, kwanaye uYeshuwa HaMashiach kuba ndinguye!

Ndisaza kulugqibezela usindiso lwenu, O Bandla laseLawodike, kodwa anikalungeli kungena eBukumkanini bam ngeli xesha kuba niphambukile kwinjongo yenu enye kuKrestu! Ndiya kunibonisa impazamo yenu, kwaye ninalo ithuba lokuguquka kuzo zonke izono, kodwa anisenalo ixesha eliseleyo lokuphikisana nelizwi leNkosi yenu, kuba esi sono sibi sesinye sezininzi uSathana azisebenzisileyo ukunibopha ekuzeni ngokupheleleyo kum enguqukweni ukuze ndinihlambulule ngokupheleleyo ngegazi lam elihlawulelayo ezonweni zenu.

Yabonani! Amaxesha eeNtlanga aphelile kwaye umnquma wasendle owaxhunyelelwa phakathi kwazo, kwaye kunye nazo wabelana ngengcambu nokutyeba koMnquma, ngoku uza kunqunyulwa. Kuba niwatyeshele Amazwi kaKrestu Yesu, kuba ndanixelela ngokucacileyo ukuba ningaziqhayisi ngamasebe awawa ekungakholweni: kodwa ukuba niyaziqhayisa, aniyithwali ingcambu, kodwa ingcambu inithwele nina. NdinguNgcambu kaYese, kwaye ndingulowo umiyo njengophawu lwethemba lonke ixesha, kwanalowo uzisa usindiso neendlela zikaYehova emhlabeni kwaye ndiya kulawula ilifa lam kwitrone kaDavide ngobulungisa kwaye ndiwuthengile umhlaba ngegazi lam elingenasono kwaye ndiya kuthatha oko kungokwam ngokufanelekileyo ngogonyamelo ezandleni zabahlali abangekho mthethweni, kwanabo bawileyo.

Ndingulowo unike ubabalo nenceba emhlabeni, kuba ixesha lonke lobabalo ndalinikwa nguYehova losindiso lweeNtlanga. Kuba iinjongo zikaYehova kuSirayeli zazisoloko zikukuba iiNtlanga zisikelelwe kwaye zifike elusindisweni ngokuthobela kukaSirayeli. Kodwa ekuhambeni kwexesha baba nekratshi, baba namagingxigingxi, baza banqunyulwa kumnquma wemvelo ekungakholweni. Kodwa nina, Bandla lam laseLawodike, kwananina niyimbonakaliso yalo hlobo lunye lwekratshi, ukungakholwa, nobumfama obaboniswa ngamasebe emvelo anqunyulwa ekungakholweni, kwaye ngoku niza kunqunyulwa kumnquma wemvelo. Kwaye ngoku ndiza kuqhubela phambili ndigqibezele umsebenzi wam kumnquma wemvelo, uSirayeli izalamane nabantu bam, kwanabo bonke endibakhethileyo ukuba bandamkele njengeNkosi yabo ukuze ndibadale ngokutsha njengomntu omnye omtsha kwaye imfihlelo kaThixo igqitywe, kwanokugqitywa kwemisebenzi yam yokuhlawulela yosindiso.

Kwaye nina, Bandla lam laseLawodike, nilushiyile uthando lwenu lokuqala kwaye nisezandleni zomkhohlisi enifunge kuye ukuthembeka. Nizikhethele uBharabhas omtsha njengoKumkani wenu kwaye nimkhabile uYesu waseNazarete njengeNkosi yenu kwaye nisezandleni zomnye umsindisi wobuxoki. Kwanalowo uzenza umsindisi weBhabhiloni eyimfihlelo, othetha azenze omnye wenu, kodwa uthetha njengenamba. Kuba iBhabhiloni eyimfihlelo iphila kamnandi nakwintabalala yezinto eziphathekayo, ulonwabo nobutyebi.

Kwaye iBhabhiloni eyimfihlelo ilinxilisile lonke ihlabathi ngamanyala obuhenyu obukwindebhe yayo yegolide, kodwa zinjalo iindlela zabo bawileyo, abanikezela ngawona manyala mabi, kwaye niyawasela ngokuzithandela nangolonwabo olukhulu kuba nimfanyekiswe yindebhe yegolide kumanyala agqithisileyo eniwasebenzisa ngamanyala. Kuba neBandla lam laseLawodike alisahlanganisani ngeenxa zonke kwizibhalo zam ezingcwele kodwa kwiindibano zikaBharabhas, enithembele kuye. Kwaye ke nikhetha umsindisi omtsha kuba nithanda ihlabathi nezinto zehlabathi kwaye aninakuyicinga into yokuba ningalahlekelwa zizinto zenu ezixabisekileyo, ngaphandle kokuba ndiya kuzisusa zonke iintywenka kuni kwingqumbo yam esondelayo, kwaye nangoku ilahleko zenu ziyanda!

Kuba nimfama kwaye ninenkohliso kwikratshi lenu lokuba uYehova anganikezela ngomnye umsindisi ngaphandle kwam, uYesu waseNazarete, kuba ndiyimbewu ethenjisiweyo yomfazi kwaye uDan yingonyama entsha ekungekho Mesiya uya kuvela kuyo. Ndiveni kwaye nindive kakuhle, O bandla laseLawodike, kuba ngoku lixesha lokuguquka kwenu kwisono seentliziyo zenu okanye lixesha endiya kunishiya ngalo emlilweni wokucikida kwaye bonke ababiza egameni leNkosi kude kube sekupheleni baya kusindiswa kwaye abaninzi abavuma igama lam ngokungathembeki baya kuwa ekulibalekeni, bechithe ithuba labo kuhlobo lokuhlonela uThixo olukhanyela amandla alo: Nina baseLawodike, ndingamandla alo kwaye kum uYehova ubeke bonke ubukhulu, kwaye nindikhanyele mna, iNkosi noMsindisi wenu, kwaye ndiya kunikhafulela ngaphandle komlomo wam ngaphandle kokuba niguquke ngoku!

Ngexesha lam lobabalo, usindiso lwasasazeka phakathi kweentlanga, kwaye isizwe emva kwesizwe sabelana saza sathatha inxaxheba kwingcambu yomnquma wemvelo. Kwaye ngoku, iintlanga azisayi kuba luphawu losindiso kwaye ixesha lazo kuNyana lafika ngokwaliwa kwezalamane nabantu bam kuba bakhwaza ekungakholweni besithi khulula kuthi uBharabhas! Kodwa njengoko iimfihlelo zikaYehova zatyhilwa ngabapostile bam abangcwele nabaprofeti, ndimthintelile uBharabhas andamkhulula ngokupheleleyo, kodwa ndavumela kuphela imfihlelo yobugwenxa ukuba iqhubeke. Kwaye ngoku kufikile amaxesha apho ndiya kumkhulula ngokupheleleyo uBharabhas njengoko umthinteli wam esusiwe kwaye njengoko abathembekileyo bam, ababiziweyo, nabanyuliweyo bethatyathelwa emazulwini apho ndibalungiselele khona amakhaya abo asisigxina, kwaye baya kwambathiswa ngelinen ecikizekileyo engenabala engenakuze idyojwe okanye ingcoliswe.

Nimfama kangaka na kwihlabathi lenu, kwaye nizizithulu kangaka ekuveni ukuba anisaliva ilizwi lam O Bandla laseLawodike? Nikholelwe ubuxoki obuziswe yimfihlelo yobugwenxa, kwaye nivumele igwele elinetyhefu kwiBhayibhile zenu ezonakeleyo elizise ukonakala okupheleleyo kwiCawa zenu ezingcolise iintliziyo zenu ngeentsomi ekungakholweni okuneempazamo. Nivumele ubuxoki babathakathi be-dispensational ukuba banikhubaze ngezibhalo zobuxoki namazwi axokayo. Anisaziqondi izibhalo, kwaye nizifumbalele iinkathazo ezinkulu eziya kunihlela ngequbuliso. Lonke iLizwi likaThixo linyulu kwaye lenzelwe ukuthotyelwa kokholo nokwakhiwa kwabamkeli. Qondani Amazwi amaLizwi am kwaye niqonde amaxesha eniphila kuwo. Kuba ndenza uYohane uMtyhili wabhala wathi: Ndabona irhamncwa liphuma elwandle, lineentloko ezisixhenxe, neempondo ezilishumi, naphezu kweempondo zalo izithsaba ezilishumi, naphezu kweentloko zalo igama lokunyelisa.

Waphinda uYohane uMtyhili wabhala wathi: Ndabona umfazi ehleli phezu kwerhamncwa elibomvu, lizele ngamagama onyeliso, lineentloko ezisixhenxe, neempondo ezilishumi. Kwaye umfazi wayambethe ezimfusa nezibomvu, ehonjiswe ngegolide, namatye anqabileyo neeperile, ephethe indebe yegolide esandleni sakhe, izele ngamasikizi namanyala obuhenyu bakhe.

Kwakhona: Kwaye iimpondo ezilishumi owazibonayo phezu kwerhamncwa, ezi ziya kumthiya umfazi olihenyukazi, zimshiye elubala, aze, zitye inyama yakhe, zimtshise ngomlilo. Kuba uThixo ufake ezintliziyweni zabo ukuba bazalisekise intando yakhe, bavumelane, banike ubukumkani babo kwirhamncwa, de amazwi kaThixo azaliseke. Kwaye umfazi, owambonayo, nguloo Mzi mkhulu olawula ookumkani bomhlaba.

Bantwana bam, qondani ukuba irhamncwa linokubhekisa kubantu ngabanye nakwizikumkani, fundani ukuze nifunde. Irhamncwa elithwele ihenyukazi liya kulitshabalalisa ihenyukazi kwaye alisayi kuphinda libe phantsi kolawulo lwehenyukazi, kuba lilawule ookumkani bomhlaba ngobuhenyu balo. Ngoko ke irhamncwa eliza kuphuma elwandle liya kuvelisa uMahdi wabo olindelekileyo, kodwa liya kuyitshabalalisa ngokupheleleyo iBhabhiloni eyimfihlelo, kuba uThixo ulinikele ihenyukazi elikhulu ezandleni zeentshaba zalo esele zilithiye ngokupheleleyo.

Phambi kokuba irhamncwa elivela elwandle nerhamncwa elivela emhlabeni liphakame ngokupheleleyo eBukumkanini benamba, baninzi abalawuli abamanyeneyo ngenjongo yokunika amandla nolawulo lwabo kwinamba, kuba uThixo ukufakile ezintliziyweni zabo ukuba bakwenze oko. Kwaye baya kuyitshabalalisa iBhabhiloni eyimfihlelo. Nicinga ukuba nizazi kakuhle izibhalo zam ezingcwele kuba nikholelwe kwiimfundiso zenu zobuxoki? Qondani kwaye nive Amazwi eNkosi yenu!

Kwakhona, ndenza uYohane uMtyhili wabhala wathi: Kwaye irhamncwa endalibonayo, lalifana neNgwe, neenyawo zalo zinjengezebhere, nomlomo walo unjengomlomo wengonyama: kwaye inamba yalinika amandla ayo netrone yayo, negunya elikhulu. Ndabona enye yeentloko zalo ngokungathi yenzakele yafa, kodwa inxeba layo elibulalayo laphola, kwaye lonke ihlabathi lamangaliswa laza lalandela irhamncwa.

Bantwana bam, amazwi kaYehova anobunzulu obuneenkalo ezininzi, ekungekho mntu unokuwazi kakuhle. Irhamncwa elichaziweyo bubukumkani obuya kuvela ekutshatyalalisweni kwehenyukazi, kwaye kulawulo oludala kuya kuvela ulawulo olutsha! Limele irhamncwa lolwandle, kuba liya kuvela njengoMahdi wabo, kwaye limele uhlobo lobukumkani aya kulawula ngalo ulwandle. Kutheni ningawahloniphi amazwi eLizwi lam? Alitsho ukuba irhamncwa elivela elwandle ngokwalo lenzakala, kuba nguwuphi umntu oneentloko ezisixhenxe ngokoqobo? Kodwa enye yeentloko zalo ngokungathi yenzakele yafa. O, zizidenge, neentliziyo ezicothayo ukukholelwa kuko konke okuthethwe ngabaProfeti! NGOKUNGATHI yenzakele yafa. Amazwi "ngokungathi" kufanele ukuba aninqumamise, kodwa anisakholelwa nakwelona lincinane lamazwi am, kwaye ndiliLizwi elenziwe inyama. Akucaci na kuni, NGOKUNGATHI, sisaci esidala? Akucaci na kuni ukuba "were" yi-subjunctive? Akucaci na kuni ukuba sisishunqulelo sika, ngokungathi kunjalo?

Kodwa hayi, nikholelwa kubuxoki benamba, kuba uBharabhas uthetha njengenamba, kodwa ayinguye uBharabhas, kodwa lirhamncwa elivela emhlabeni. Kwaye ngoku lonke ihlabathi liya kumangaliswa lirhamncwa ngenxa yemiqondiso nemimangaliso yalo yobuxoki, kwaye liya kwenza eminye imiqondiso nemimangaliso yobuxoki, kwaye alikaphakami ngokupheleleyo kuba ihenyukazi alikatshiswa ngomlilo, kodwa ngoku lityhiliwe ngexesha lalo. Lithetha njengenamba, lisithi "NguThixo kuphela owamsindisayo kwinxeba lakhe lentloko" kwaye obu bubuxoki obunyelisayo. UYehova, uThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade, undithumele mna Yesu waseNazarete njengomsindisi wehlabathi. Mna noBawo simunye kubunye nasenjongweni kwaye uYehova akamsindisanga uBharabhas kwaye nam andimenzanga, uKrestu Yesu. Nikhohlisiwe nathinjwa kwaye narhintyelwa kwinkohliso enkulu, kwaye ndithi kuni guqukani nibuyele eluthandweni lwenu lokuqala kwaye nikwenze oko ngoku, kuba akusekho xesha!

Kuba ndingulowo uvavanya iintliziyo, kwaye ndingulowo uvavanya bonke endibabizela elusindisweni, kuba ndihlambalula bonke emlilweni wokucikida! Yabonani! Ndingulowo uhleliyo njengomcikidi nomhlanjululi wesilivere: kwaye ndihlanjulula oonyana bakaYehova, ndibacikide njengegolide nesilivere, ukuze banikele eNkosini umnikelo ngobulungisa. Aniqondi na ukuba ndiya kunenza ababingeleli nookumkani phambi koBawo? Nangona isipho sam somoya oyingcwele, enizalwa ngaso ngokutsha ngembewu engenakonakala sigqibelele kwaye asidingi kucikidwa, niphuma ku-Adam ngegazi kwaye kufuneka nivumele ngokupheleleyo umsebenzi wam wenziwe ebomini benu, ngaphandle kokuba imbewu yam iwile emhlabeni onamatye.

Ngoku kufika amaxesha omgwebo kwaye amaxesha obabalo agqityiwe. Nangona abaninzi beya kuhleka amazwi am kwaye bajike ekungakholweni, sele kusaziwa nguYehova isiphumo. Kuba uYehova usoloko engqina eluntwini iindlela zalo ezonakeleyo aze alukhalimele uluntu ngesono, esazi eluthandweni lwakhe nobulumko obungunaphakade ukuba uya kusindisa abanye. Kwaye njengoko uYehova wayenza lukhuni intliziyo kaFaro, uya kwenza lukhuni neentliziyo zabaninzi abevayo kodwa bangaphulaphuli. UYehova uya kumvavanya umntu ngokuphindaphindiweyo. Kwaye abanye baya kuba njengoFaro kwaye benze iintliziyo zabo zibe lukhuni ngakumbi ekungakholweni kodwa abanye baya kusuka kwiindlela zikaFaro baze bavukele kubomi obungunaphakade kuKrestu Yesu, kodwa ngomlilo wokucikida.

Qondani ukuba abo bakhetha uBharabhas baya kuzenza abathathi-nxaxheba kulwaphulo-mthetho lukaBharabhas, kwaye abo bala ukubiza egameni lam kude kube sekupheleni baya kugwetywa bonke kuba babeyoliswa kukungabikho kokusesikweni. Kuba umsindisi wenu omtsha ayingomsindisi kodwa lirhamncwa elirhalayo. Kwaye inkqubo yakhe nobukumkani bakhe benziwe ngabo benzakalisa abantwana. Aniziqondi na iindlela zeNamba? Kuba abo balandela iindlela zenamba baxhamla ngabantwana. Babingelela abantwana kuMoleki, bafuna umthombo wabo wobutsha egazini labantwana abathuthunjisiweyo, baxhaphaza abantwana ukuze bazanelise ngokugula kwabo, kwaye baqhelisela ukubetha usana esibelekweni ukuze babulale abancinane phambi kokuzalwa, kwaye banikhokelele ngokufihlakeleyo kwiindlela zabo, kuba zinjalo iindlela zeenkokeli zeBhabhiloni eyimfihlo kunye nobukumkani benamba. Kwaye ngoku kuza umgwebo kaThixo ngezi zinto nezinye ezininzi. Kuba ukuba beningazibonanga ezi zinto nge ningabanga naso isono, kodwa ngoku niyabona kwaye isono senu sihleli.

Anifundanga na? Utsho uYehova, Kwavakala ilizwi phezulu, isijwili nesililo esikrakra. URakeli, elilela abantwana bakhe, wala ukuthuthuzelwa ngabantwana bakhe, kuba babengekho. Kuba nisilele kumsebenzi enawunikwayo, kwaye nathenjwa ngeemfihlelo zam kwaye nema njengokukhanya kosindiso nophawu lwethemba. Kodwa ngoku nizimanye nabo ndanithuma ukuba nime ngokuchasene nabo. Kwaye ngoku kufika amaxesha endiya kunishiya kwikamva lenu.

Ndiveni kwaye nindive kakuhle nonke eniya kulala ilizwi likaYeshuwa HaMashiach, kwanelizwi likaKrestu Yesu: aninalo ixesha! Ndinicebisa ukuba nindive eluthandweni lwam ngani bantwana bam kwaye niguquke ngoku! Kuba umlilo wokucikida sele uza kuwa phezu kwenu ngokupheleleyo, kwaye uya kulihlambulula ilizwe kulo lonke ubukumkani benamba kwaye umhlaba awusayi kuba phantsi kweendlela zikaSathana kwakhona kwaye ubukumkani bakhe abuyi kuphinda buvuke, kuba ndiyiNkosi yeeNkosi noKumkani wooKumkani, kwaye ndiya kubatshabalalisa ngokupheleleyo abo bawileyo. Bonke abala ukuguquka baya kuba nesabelo sabo echibini lomlilo kwaye baya kwabelana ngesohlwayo somtyholi neengelosi zakhe kwelo chibi likhulu lesulfure evuthayo. Ngokunjalo, bonke abaguqukayo ngokuphuma esonweni nangokubiza egameni lam kude kube sekupheleni baya kusindiswa banikwe umvuzo omkhulu ongunaphakade kum.

Yabonani! Ngoku lixesha lokuhlanganiswa kwabathembekileyo, kwaye ngoku ndiya kushiya abo bala uBharabhas, kuba benze ukhetho lwabo.

Kwaye abaninzi eBhabheli eyiMfihlo, nkqu nabo bakholelwa kubuxoki bakhe balandele amazwi akhe alungileyo neentetho ezimnandi bayathinjwa baze babanjiswe luxolo lobuxoki apho bacinga ukuba ubomi babo obumnandi nobonayo buya kuqhubeka apho namhlanje kunjengayizolo kwaye ingomso liya kufana nanamhlanje.
