Kuba olilungisa uya kuphila ngokholo!

Kuba olilungisa uya kuphila ngokholo! Vuka, uvuse nabazalwana bakho, bandla lam lidikidiki laseLawodike, elibalityelweyo ziimfihlelo zam; kuba ilixa lihambile kwaye ixesha seliphantse laphela. Yizani kum, bantwana bam, ngezandla ezivulekileyo nentliziyo evulekileyo (kuba iingalo zam nentliziyo yam zivulelekile kuni); yizani kum ngentliziyo ethambileyo nelungele ukwamkela amazwi am. Kuba wathetha wathini na umzalwana wam uYakobi? Ngoko ke, lahlayo yonke inkunkuma, nokudlekeka kobubi, namkele ninobulali ilizwi elimilisiweyo, elinako ukuyisindisa imiphefumlo yenu. Bantwana bam, yibani ngabalandeli bam, kuba ndinobulali, ndithobekile ngentliziyo, namkele ninobulali iLizwi elimilisiweyo, endilibeke phakathi kwenu ukuze nibe namehlo okubona neendlebe zokuva. Guqulelani iintliziyo zenu kum, kuba ndim indlela, nenyaniso, nobomi, kwaye akukho namnye uzayo kuBawo engezi ngam, kuba injongo kaBawo ibonakaliswe ngokucacileyo, kwaye uyenze yanjalo.

Kutheni nivumela iintliziyo zenu zinihlupha, bantwana bam? Kutheni nisuka emazwini am nize nilahlekise abanye ngeempazamo zenu? Kuba nina, bandla lam laseLawodike, nilibele ukuba ningoobani na kum, uKrestu Yesu, kwaye nilahlekelwe ziimfihlelo zam, nangona ndaniphathisa zona. Kuba ndiyiNkosi eyanithenga ngegazi lam; ndiyiNkosi yeeNkosi noKumkani wooKumkani olizuze ilifa umhlaba kuBawo, kwaye ndiza kwabelana ngelifa lam nabathembekileyo nabanyuliweyo; ndiza kwabelana ngelifa lam nawe, Mtshakazi wam! Ngoko musani ukoyika, kuba nangona nihlwempuzekile, nityebile kum, kuba uBawo unikele ezandleni zam zonke izinto. Owu, bandla laseLawodike, usuke waba ngumfazi ongakholwayo wokulalanisa.

Hayi, anikhathali ngenyama yelizwi lam, kwaye anindilandeli ngentliziyo yenu yonke; anifundanga na uMzekeliso? Ukuqonda okulungileyo kuzuzisa ubabalo, kodwa indlela yabangakholwayo inzima. Kwaye iindlela zokulalanisa enizikhethileyo zinzima; iindlela zenu zokungakholwa aziveli kum, kwaye nizama ukuhamba indlela endininike yona ngonyawo olunye endleleni nolunye emjelweni, kanti ninobuganga bokundibeka ityala mna, uYeshua HaMashiach, xa niwela emjelweni njengoko nikhokelwe. Kuba uhambo lwenu lunokuwa okuninzi, kwaye ithemba lam ngani lisahleli kukuba ukuwa kwenu kufane nokuwa kwabalungisayo: Kuba olilungisa uwa kasixhenxe aze avuke kwakhona: kodwa abangendawo baya kuwela ebubini.

Andinibizelanga ekuweleni ebubini, bantwana bam, kwananina, bandla lam laseLawodike, kuba andikanishiyi kumaxesha ezilingo okwangoku. Kuba yabonani! Ndisemi emnyango ndinkqonkqoza kuba andisayi kuthatha nxaxheba kwiindlela zenu zokudikidika, zokulalanisa, kodwa ndinibizela ebulungiseni. Ndinibizela ekumeni ngokuqinileyo ebulungiseni, kuba abelusi abalahlekisayo banikhohlisile, benikhokelela kwiindlela zikaBhileham; kwaye abaninzi benu baprofeta ngobuxoki ngoomoya bakaIzebhele, abangcolisa amazwi abaprofeti bam bokwenyani kwaye banqwenela ukubulala abaprofeti endibangcwalisileyo kum, kuba ndim owanika izipho ebantwini. Kuba ndabhalela kwabaseGalati xa bakhubekayo njengoko nikhubeka: Ndimangalisiwe, kukuba nisuke kamsinya kangaka kulowo wanibizelayo elubabalweni lukaKrestu, naya kwenye iVangeli: Engeyiyo enye. Ewe, fundani oko kwakhona, kuba akukho Vangeli yimbi kwaye akukho gama limbi linikiweyo eliya kusindiswa ngalo abantu ngaphandle kwelam, uYesu Krestu. Kutheni nilahlekiswa kum, bantwana bam abancinci? Andinondli kwaye andinikhathaleli na, kuba ndingumalusi olungileyo owanikela ngobomi bakhe ngenxa yeegusha. Kodwa amazwi endingawathethanga, eniwanqwenela kakhulu kwaye niwalandela, ayanincokolisa ezindlebeni zenu; kuba loo mvakalelo iye yaba luthando lwenu lokuqala kwiindlela zenu zokuzidla; kuba nizixabisile iinyaniso ezingeyonyani ngokungathi ziyinyaniso, kwaye nilahlekelwe yiyo yonke ingqiqo.

Niyilibele na imbali yebandla esisiseko? Nibe nonqena kakhulu ukuba ningafunda? Ngaba esi sihloko asizonwabisi ngokwaneleyo iindlebe zenu ukuba nive amazwi am kwaye nibe nokholo kuwo? Kuba umntu akayi kuphila ngesonka sodwa kodwa ngalo LONKE ilizwi eliphuma emlonyeni kaThixo! Ibandla lam lazalwa ngomhla wePentekoste kwaye libhalwe kwiZenzo ukuze nifunde. Kuba ibandla lam laqala kwaSirayeli, eYuda, nangona bengawaqondanga amazwi endawathethayo ngoPetros ngaloo mini izukileyo xa ndathulula umoya oyingcwele kubanyulwa bam, njengoko babethetha ngezinye iilwimi njengoko umoya wabanikayo ukuba bathethe! Kuba wathini uPetros kwisihlwele saseYerusalem ngaloo mini xa wayeprofeta endaweni yam? Kuba isithembiso sesenu, nesabantwana benu, nesabo bonke abakude, kwanabo bonke iNkosi uThixo wethu eya kubabiza. Bantwana bam, nina ningabo bakude, kuba abaninzi benu abangabo bakwaSirayeli ngokwenyama, kodwa ndinithathile ezintlangeni ngobabalo lwam ukuze nime ncenceba yam, kuba nibiziwe.

Anifundanga na? Kodwa ngoku kuKrestu Yesu, nina enanikade nikude, nisondeziwe ngegazi likaKrestu. Kodwa kwiindlu zenu zokunqula ezilalanisayo enizinyathela ngokunyelisa egameni lam (nangona andiyi kungena kodwa ndime emnyango ndinkqonkqoze), nicinga ukuba nifumene umthetho kaMoses kum, kwaye ngekratshi lenu aniyiqondi into yokuba uMoses wayengowakwaSirayeli, kwaye yayikwaSirayeli apho iTorah yanikwa ukuba igcinwe, umyalelo abasilela kuwo; kuba akukho mntu unako ukuwugcina umthetho kaMoses ngenxa yobubi obabangelwa yinyoka enyameni yenu, kuba ulwazi lwayo luhlala lonakalisa, kwaye umvuzo wesono kukufa. Kuba abaseKorinte bayangqina ngamazwi am: Onguye owasenza saba ngabalungiseleli abafanelekileyo boMnqophiso oMtsha, kungekhona owombhalo, kodwa owomoya: kuba umbhalo uyabulala, kodwa umoya uyanika ubomi. Nibizelwe ukuhamba emoyeni, nangona abaninzi benu benqwenela ngokungafanelekanga umbhalo womthetho, ongeyenu, kodwa uzalisekiswe kum.

Ndiphenduleni koku: Ngaba umoya weza kuni ngokuzalisekisa kwenu iTorah? Okanye weza ngokuzalisekisa kwam okulungileyo kweTorah njengePasika yenu egqibeleleyo? Kuba sithini isingqino sabaseGalati? Ndingathanda ukufunda oku kuphela kuni, namfumana na umoya, ngemisebenzi yoMthetho, okanye ngokuva kokholo? Kwaye kwakhona uPawulos uthi kwabaseGalati: Andiluchithi ubabalo lukaThixo: kuba ukuba ubulungisa buza ngoMthetho, ngoko uKrestu wafela ilize. Aniqondi na ukuba ekuqaleni kwexesha kwakukho ibandla lamaYuda nebandla leeNtlanga? Nangona ibandla lam lazalelwa eYuda, uPawulos wathi kwabaseGalati kwaye ndakugcina apho: Kuba lowo wasebenza ngokufanelekileyo kuPetros kubuPostile bolwaluko, yena lowo wayenamandla kum kwiiNtlanga. Kuba kuqala ndathumela uPetros kwiiNtlanga, kwaye kubhaliwe kwiZenzo xa ndathi kuPetros: Oko uThixo akuhlambululeyo, musa ukukubiza ngokuba yinto engcolileyo. Kuba xa ndathumela uPetros kuKorneli, kwabhalwa obu bungqina bulandelayo: Kwaye abo bolwaluko ababekholwa, bamangaliswa, bonke ababeze noPetros, kuba nakwiiNtlanga kwathululwa isipho somoya oyingcwele. Ndiyanibuza oku, nina baseLawodike, ngaba uKorneli walufumana usindiso ngokulandela iTorah (kuba wayeyindoda ezinikeleyo)? Okanye wasifumana isithembiso ngokholo? AbaseKorinte banelizwi kulo mba xa uPawulos wababhalela: Enisindiswa ngayo, ukuba niyayigcina inkumbulo yoko ndanivumelisayo, ngaphandle kokuba nikholwe lilize. Usindiso lweza ngokushunyayelwa kokholo kungekhona ngombhalo womthetho, kuba ukuzibeka phantsi komthetho kaMoses, kwanokholo lwenu lulilize.

Kwaye ngoko abaseRoma bathi: Kodwa ngoku ubulungisa bukaThixo ngaphandle koMthetho bubonakalisiwe, bungqinwa nguMthetho nabaProfeti. Kwanobulungisa bukaThixo, obungokholo lukaYesu Krestu kubo bonke, naphezu kwabo bonke abakholwayo: kuba akukho mahluko: Kuba bonke bonile, basilela eluzukweni lukaThixo. Kuba ndalixelela ibandla lam kwabaseGalati apho uPawulos wabhala ngokuthobela: Kuba kuYesu Krestu, akukho nto ixabisekileyo, nokuba kukwaluka, nokuba kukungaluki, kodwa kukholo olusebenza ngothando. Kwaye kwakhona kwabaseGalati uPawulos wabhala: Kuba kuKrestu Yesu akukho nto ixabisekileyo nokuba kukwaluka nokuba kukungaluki, kodwa kukuba sisidalwa esitsha. Ngoko usindiso alunikezelwa ngemisebenzi yomthetho, kwaye usindiso lwenu aluphilwa kwaye aluzalisekiswa ngemisebenzi yomthetho. Kuba kwabaseRoma kwabhalwa: Yabonani, ndibeka eZiyon ilitye lokukhubekisa, nelitye lesikhubekiso: kwaye nabani na okholwayo kuye, akayi kudaniswa. Bantwana bam, ndilelo litye lokukhubekisa nelitye lesikhubekiso, kuba ndinguYesu waseNazarete, imvana kaThixo, kwaye ndiwuzalisekisile umthetho.

Owu, nina baseLawodike, nicinga ukuba nini nodwa enikhubekayo kulo mba? Ndithi hayi. Kuba ningaziqokelela iincwadi zam ngokubanzi kwiindidi ezimbini ezisisiseko, ngaphandle kokuba zombini zibhalelwe umntu omnye omtsha kuKrestu Yesu. Kuba iincwadi zam zibhalelwe amabandla eeNtlanga, kwaye ezinye zibhekiswe kumabandla amaYuda, kwaye ngoko uPetros wabhala ngesityhilelo seMfihlelo endayityhilela uPawulos: Njengakuzo zonke iiNcwadi zakhe, ethetha kuzo ngezi zinto, apho kukho izinto ezinzima ukuqondwa, ezo abo bangafundanga nabangazinzanga bazigqwethayo, njengoko benjenjalo nakwezinye iZibhalo, ukuze bazitshabalalise.

Ngoko ke ingxaki yenu ayintsha, kuba akukho sihendo sinibambileyo ngaphandle kwesiqhelekileyo ebantwini: kodwa uThixo uthembekile, ongayi kunivumela ukuba nihendwe ngaphezu koko ninako; kodwa uya kwenza indlela yokuphuma kunye nesihendo, ukuze nibe nako ukusinyamezela. Ngaba niya kuthatha indlela yokuphuma uThixo aninike yona ngobabalo lwam nenceba yam ukuze nibaleke abo banganiqhwaga umvuzo wenu ngokunibangela ukuba niwe elubabalweni? Ngaba niya kuwala amazwi am ngenxa yamaYudaizer anifundisa ngokungazi ukuba kufuneka nigcine iTorah ukuze nisindiswe? Ngaba nisizidenge kangangokuba nicinge ukuba, emva kokuqala umthetho wobomi emoyeni kaYesu Krestu, ngoku ninikelwe kumthetho wesono nokufa? Kuba kuthekani ngomthetho? Bantwana bam, ndiza kunixelela. Musani ukugqiba ngobudenge ukuba ndilizwi lobuxoki elinikhokelela empazameni, kuba abaninzi benu abalazi ilizwi lam kwaye anindazi, nangona ndinibiza! Musani ukucinga ukuba ndize kutshabalalisa umthetho okanye abaprofeti. Andizanga kutshabalalisa, kodwa ndize kuzalisekisa. Kuba inene ndithi kuni, de kudlule izulu nomhlaba, akuyi kudlula nakanye iyod enye okanye isuntswana leyod emthethweni, de konke kuzalisekiswe.

Ndim lowo uwuzalisekisileyo umthetho kaMoses, kwaye imiyalelo yawo igcinwa ngokuthobela amazwi am obabalo nenceba kwiincwadi zam nangekholo likaYesu Krestu. Ngokungazi nicinga ukuba ngenxa yokuba ndikukuzalisekiswa komthetho ndikhuthaza nabani na ukuba aphule imithetho kaThixo, nokuba ngumthetho kaMoses okanye imithetho kaThixo yokuziphatha. Kuba ndathini kwabo bahlala eKorinte? Bandla lam laseLawodike, endilithandayo, aningoowokuqala ukujongana nesi sihendo, kwaye ngoko ndenza uPawulos wabhala: Onguye owasenza saba ngabalungiseleli abafanelekileyo boMnqophiso oMtsha, kungekhona owombhalo, kodwa owomoya: kuba umbhalo uyabulala, kodwa umoya uyanika ubomi. Kodwa ukuba ulungiselelo lokufa olubhalwe, lwakrolwa ematyeni, lwaluzukile, kangangokuba abantwana bakwaSirayeli babengenako ukujonga ngokuqinileyo ubuso bukaMoses, ngenxa yozuko lobuso bakhe, olo zuko lwaluzakupheliswa: Akuyi kuba njani na ulungiselelo lomoya, lube nozuko olungakumbi? Musani ukwenza impazamo, bantwana bam, umbhalo uyabulala ngenxa yesono esisenyameni yenu esigwetywa nguloo mbhalo, kwanomthetho kaMoses: Ngoko umthetho ungcwele, nomyalelo ungcwele, ulungile, ulungile. Ngaba ke oko kulungileyo, kwenziwa ukufa kum? Makube lee. Kodwa isono, ukuze sibonakale sisisono, sisebenza ukufa kum ngoko kulungileyo: ukuze isono ngoMyalelo sibe nesono esigqithisileyo. Kwaye ngoko ndenza uPawulos waxelela abaseGalati: Andiluchithi ubabalo lukaThixo: kuba ukuba ubulungisa buza ngoMthetho, ngoko uKrestu wafela ilize. Bantwana bam, nilahlekisiwe, kwaye ndanixelela ukuba iwayini entsha kufuneka ifakwe kwiintsuba ezintsha, ngoko yimani niqinile elubabalweni endithe mna, Krestu Yesu, ndanikhulula ngalo.

Bantwana bam, aninakuyaphula imithetho kaThixo xa nihamba emoyeni, kuba akunakwenzeka. Nakhiwe phezu kwesiseko sabapostile nabaprofeti, uYesu Krestu ngokwakhe elilitye lembombo eliyintloko. Kwaye kwabaseHebhere kwathiwa: Kuba ndiya kuba nenceba kubo abangemalungisa, nezono zabo nezikreqo zabo andisayi kuphinda ndizikhumbule. Ekuthini kwakhe, umnqophiso omtsha, uwenzile owokuqala waba mdala. Ngoku oko konakalayo nokugugayo kulungele ukunyamalala. Bantwana bam, ubulungisa abuzi ngokugcina umthetho, kuba sisipho esifunyanwa ngokholo kum, uKrestu Yesu. Kwaye ngenxa yokuba nenziwe malungisa kum, nikhululwe kumthetho wesono nokufa.

Kuba kukunyelisa ukugqiba ukuba ukuhamba emoyeni wobomi kuyisono ngandlela ithile, kuba nabani na okholwa oko uthatha umbingelelo wam ngenxa yezono zabantu njengento engcwele. Kuba ndimkhulu kunoMoses, kuba uMoses ngokwakhe uthi: INkosi uThixo wenu iya kunivelisela uMprofeti phakathi kwenu, kubazalwana benu, onjengam, kuye niya kumphulaphula. Yivani ilizwi lam, bantwana, niphulaphule intetho yam, kuba andinenzi nibe ngabangenamthetho, kuba oonyana bakaSathana abanamthetho, kungekhona oonyana bakaThixo! Kuba kwabaseRoma uPawulos wabhala ngokucacileyo: Akukho kugwetywa ngoku kwabo bakuKrestu Yesu, abangahambi ngokwenyama, kodwa ngokomoya. Kuba umthetho womoya wobomi, kuKrestu Yesu, undikhulule kumthetho wesono nokufa.

Kuba oko umthetho wawungenakukwenza, ekubeni wawubuthathaka ngenxa yenyama, uThixo ethumela uNyana wakhe, efana nenyama enesono, kwaye ngenxa yesono wagweba isono enyameni: Ukuze ubulungisa bomthetho buzalisekiswe kuthi, abangahambi ngokwenyama, kodwa ngokomoya. Kwaye kwakhona abaseRoma bathetha: Ukubonakalisa, ndithi, ngeli xesha ubulungisa bakhe: ukuze abe lilungisa, nomthetheleli walowo ukholwayo kuYesu. Kuphi ke ukuqhayisa? Kukhutshiwe. Ngawuphi uMthetho? Owemisebenzi? Hayi: kodwa ngoMthetho wokholo. Ngoko ke, sigqiba kwelokuba, umntu ugwetyelwa ngokholo, ngaphandle kwemisebenzi yoMthetho. Kuba ndithe: Kuba kuyo ubulungisa bukaThixo butyhilwa ukusuka elukholweni kuye elukholweni; njengoko kubhaliwe, Olilungisa uya kuphila ngokholo.

Kanti abaninzi benu babeka imithwalo engenasidingo omnye komnye kuba nikholwe ngabaprofeti bakaSathana abathunyelwe ukunibambisa ekugcineni iTorah ngobuxoki njengemfuneko yosindiso kwanokuphila usindiso; kwaye abanye benu banyelisa besithi ndawuzalisekisa umthetho ngombingelelo wam ukuze ngoku nikwazi ukugcina iTorah. Kwelinye icala, unyanisile, kuba ukuhamba emthethweni womoya wobomi kukugcina iTorah ngokugqibeleleyo ngokuzifanisa nam; kuba ibandla lam lakhiwe ngokuqinileyo ebulungiseni nasebungcweleni bokwenene phezu kwam, uKrestu Yesu, ilitye lembombo eliyintloko. Niyayigcina iTorah ngokuthobela ukholo olukum. Kutheni nikhetha ilizwi lamaYudaizer ngaphezu kwelizwi lam elingcwele? Kutheni nibeka imithwalo engenasidingo kubazalwana benu, kuba anifundanga na? Kuba ndasele ndixelele abaseKolose: Makungabikho mntu ngoko unigwebayo ekutyeni, okanye ekuseleni, okanye ngokubhekiselele kuyo nayiphi na imini eNgcwele, okanye kwiNyanga eNtsha, okanye kwimihla yeSabatha Ezizithunzi zezinto ezizayo, kodwa umzimba ngokaKrestu.

Bantwana bam, andiwugwebi umthetho; ndikhomba kuphela into ecacileyo yokuba ngenxa yesono esisenyameni yenu aninakuze niyigcine iTorah ngaphandle kokholo lwenu kum, kuba olilungisa uya kuphila ngokholo! Luyintoni ukholo kum, ningabuza? Ukholo kum lwakhiwe phezu kokuthemba amazwi am nokuqonda ukuba nifaniswe nam kuwo onke amanqanaba, kuba nina nilibandla lam endilihlambileyo ngegazi lam elingenasono; kuba ukuxolelwa kwezono akunakwenzeka ukuba kufunyanwe ngombingelelo wezilwanyana. Yibani nokholo kum, kuba ngokholo kum iTorah iyagcinwa, ngokholo kum umthetho uzalisekiswa kuni, kwaye nina nilungile, nigwetyelwe, kwaye ningcwalisiwe! Xa ndavuswa kubomi obutsha, nani navuswa; kwaye xa ndanyuselwa esandleni sokunene sikaBawo, nani nahlaliswa ezulwini. Kuba abase-Efese bayangqina: Kodwa uThixo osisityebi ngenceba, ngenxa yothando lwakhe olukhulu awasithanda ngalo, Kwanaxa sasifile ezonweni, wasiphilisa kunye noKrestu (ngobabalo nisindisiwe), Wasivusa kunye, wasihlalisa kunye kwiindawo zasezulwini kuKrestu Yesu. Kufuneka niqonde ukuba umthetho kaMoses awungomnqophiso wenu, kuba owenu ngumnqophiso omtsha oqinisekiswe ngegazi lam kwaye uhlawulelwe kukubandezeleka kwam.

Ngoko ke thobelani uKholo, bantu bam, ningabi sadikidiki. Khethani ubomi kum, uYesu Krestu, kuba nabizelwa oko. Yenzani imisebenzi emininzi enamandla endininike yona, nakulo mzuzu wokugqibela. Ndifuna niqonde ukuba ngaphandle kwam, aninakwenza nto, kwaye nigcina zonke iinkalo zeTorah ngokholo kum. Ngaphezu koko, amaYudaizer amagama angcwele aligcwalisile ibandla lam, ebeka imithwalo engenasidingo kwabo bafuna usindiso nakwabo bangabam. UBawo wandinika igama elingaphezu kwawo onke amagama, ukuze ngegama likaYesu Krestu onke amadolo aguqe. Nicinga ukuba ndingabeka umthwalo ongenasidingo kwiintlanga ezithetha iilwimi ezininzi ukuba zisebenzise igama lam, uYeshua HaMashiach, ngesiHebhere kuphela? Ndiveni kwaye nindive kakuhle, kuba ndiyeza, kwaye ndiza kujongana nabangathobeliyo nabo banihluphayo.
