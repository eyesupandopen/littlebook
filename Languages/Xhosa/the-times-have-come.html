Ubungqina Obubini: Ibandla
KwiMisebenzi kaYohane uMtyhileli
Meyi 16, 2024
Iindudumo 3 neye-4

Amaxesha Afikile!

Kivela kuYeshua HaMashiach:

Yabonani! Amaxesha olwahlulo ayasondela esiphelweni, kwaye ukubonakala kwam sele kuphantse kwafika ngoku. Kusondele kakhulu kwaye nezithunywa zezulu nomkhosi wasezulwini balindele isandi sexilongo. Ndolulele iingalo zam kwihlabathi elifayo kangangeminyaka emininzi yeejubhili ngoku, kwaye abaninzi baye basamkela isivumelwano soxolo uBawo, uYahweh, awasithumela eluntwini, mna lo, uKrestu Yesu, kuba ndiluxolo lwenu. Ukanti abaninzi baye basala isipho sam nobabalo nenceba kaBawo ethe yathululwa emhlabeni.

Abo batywinwe bakhululwa ngabo bakhethwa kwasekusekweni kwehlabathi. Kuba uBawo wayenazi ukusuka kunaphakade ukuya kunaphakade, kwaye nanisoloko nisesazulwini sentliziyo yakhe, njengokuba nam bendisesazulwini sentliziyo yakhe, kuba mna, Yesu waseNazarete, ndingunyana okuphela kwamzeleyo kaYahweh. Ukanti ngemisebenzi yam egqityiweyo njengePasika egqibeleleyo nangokuthobela kwalowo ukuphela kwamzeleyo, oonyana abaninzi, abaninzi bakaThixo bazelwe ngokutsha kunaphakade kanaphakade, bethathwe koonyana babantu, kuba ndinguye uNyana woMntu!

La maxesha obabalo ayeyimfihlelo efihliweyo entliziyweni kaYahweh awayengayixeleli mntu de wayityhila kum, kwaye nam ndiyityhile kubapostile bam abangcwele nabaprofeti kwaye ndiligcinile iLizwi lam elingcwele ezindlebeni nasezintliziyweni zabakhethiweyo nabathembekileyo. Kuba nina, Mtshakazi wam, niyinxalenye yemfihlelo enkulu kaThixo, kwaye ubomi benu (obufihliweyo kum) sele buza kutyhilwa. Kwaye ndiya kubavuza abathembekileyo ngayo yonke imisebenzi yabo yokuthembeka abayenzileyo ngokholo olukhulu kum, uYesu Krestu.

Kwaye ngoko ndisindise izihlwele ezikhulu ngegazi lam lentlawulelo, kuba igazi lam lalingenasono kwaye unanamhla andinasono, kuba andisokuze ndone. Abo baphile ubomi babo ngokuthembeka kuKrestu baya kufumana umvuzo omkhulu, kwaye baya kukhanya njengeenkwenkwezi ezulwini, kwaye uzuko olukhulu lolwabo, kuba abazange bazizukise ngokungathi bangabantu abakhulu. Kodwa bandizukisile mna, Yeshua HaMashiach, kwaye ndiya kubazisa kuBawo bengenabala okanye imibimbi, kwaye iingubo zabo ziya kuba mhlophe qhwa kwaye azisayi kuze zidyojwe. Kwaye bonke baya kuphila kwaye bangaphindi bone, nokuba kungokungahloneli Thixo okanye ngekratshi. Kwaye baya kuzukiswa kum.

Kuba ndingumalusi oyintloko, kwaye ndikhokelele umhlambi wam kumadlelo aluhlaza de kwafika amaxesha okupheliswa, asondela ngokukhawuleza emhlabeni, kuba akusekho santini iseleyo ukuba idlule kwiglasi. Kwaye ndiya kubavuza bonke, ukusuka koyena mncinci ukuya koyena mkhulu, ngendlela eningenakuyiqonda ngeli xesha; kodwa oko kuqonda kusemnyango wenu, kuba niya kunyukuselwa emazulwini kungekudala. Kwaye uya kunyuselwa ke uMtshakazi wam eluzukweni, apho ndiya kugweba imisebenzi yakhe ndize ndikhulule ngokupheleleyo yonke indoda, umfazi, nomntwana wokugqibela. Ndiya kubavuza kakhulu nangezona zinto zincinci ezenziweyo ekundikhonzeni. Kuba uThixo akanantswela-bulungisa, ukuba angawulibala umsebenzi wenu nomzamo wenu wothando, enalubonisayo egameni lakhe, ekubeni nakhonzayo kwabangcwele, kwaye nisakhonza.

Musani ukoyika, Mtshakazi wam, kuba uThixo akagwebi njengokuba umntu egweba, kuba umntu akanabulungisa kwaye ukhetha ubuso. Umntu uzukisa abo umntu ababona bebalulekile, kwaye umntu wemvelo phantse uhlala engakuhoyi ukuthobeka nesidima ethanda abanekratshi nabazifezileyo. Kuba umntu wemvelo ukhala ecela ukubalelwa njengolilungisa phambi koThixo, kodwa umntu wemvelo akanakuyoyisa imvelo yakhe yesono kwaye uyala ukukholelwa ukuba wonakele, ade ayikhanyele iNkosi yeeNkosi, ebeya kumsindisa.

Kwaye ngoko ikratshi landulela intshabalalo, nomoya wekratshi wandulela ukuwa. Endaweni yokuba bathobele iintliziyo zabo kumazwi am nobulumko nothando endilukhukulise emhlabeni, umntu wemvelo ukhetha ukulandela iinkanuko neminqweno yakhe, kuba iindlela zehlabathi ziyilwe nguSathana ukuhenda nokuvusa ezo nkanuko, kwanemivuzo yokungalungisi. Kwaye ngoko amaxesha eeNtlanga ayasondela esiphelweni, kwaye lixesha lembandezelo kaYakobi; kuba ndiya kumkhulula uSirayeli, kwanabanyulwa bam abathembekileyo, abaya kuza kum ngenguquko ngenxa yezono zabo nezono zooyise ababendalayo. Kwaye njengoko kubhaliwe, makwenzeke.

Owu, mntu wemvelo, kutheni usakhetha izono zakho kwaye ungaluhoyi ubabalo lwam nosindiso ngokungathi yinto elula? NoYahweh nam, Krestu Yesu, asinaneliseki yimigwebo ezayo noloyiko olukhulu oluza kukhululwa kungekudala. Kodwa uThixo akayikhethanga le nxalenye yesicwangciso sakhe ukuze abe nenkohlakalo kwindalo yakhe: kuba uThixo luthando, kwaye uThixo ukukhanya, kwaye akukho bumnyama kuye konke konke. Imbandezelo enkulu ezayo yenzelwe ukunika uluntu ithuba lokugqibela phambi kokuba kube semva kakhulu kulo. Isihlwele esikhulu siya kusindiswa kwimbandezelo, kwanabo babambelela egameni lam de baphefumle okokugqibela kwaye ababathandi ubomi babo de kwasekupheleni.

Mntu wemvelo, unenkani kakhulu, kwaye awuphulaphuli, kwaye ngobuyatha uyala ubabalo lwam, kwaye ndisathandaza kwangolu hlobo: Bawo, baxolele, kuba abakwazi oko bakwenzayo. Kwabo bashiyelwe kwimbandezelo enkulu, kwanabo baye basala isipho sam soxolo, abaninzi baya kulahlekelwa yintshabalalo, kuba baye bayala iNkosi uYesu Krestu. Ukanti, abaninzi baya kusindiswa, kwanabo bonke abanqula igama leNkosi baya kusindiswa. Kwaye ngoko izilingo zomlilo sele ziza kuqala, kuba umgwebo uyasondela.

Umgwebo omkhulu uyeza ebukumkanini bukaSathana! Kwaye wena, mntu wemvelo, nakwinkani yakho yokungakholwa, uyala nesona sicelo silula. Kuba ngamaxesha am obabalo, usindiso belusimahla kwaye lulula, kwaye konke umntu ekwakufuneka ekwenzile kukuvuma uYesu njengeNkosi nokukholelwa ukuba uThixo wandivusa kwabafileyo. Kwaye ngoku kuya kubakho indlala yazo zonke izinto ezilungileyo elizweni, kwanalo iLizwi lam eliNgcwele. Ixesha elikhulu, elikhulu lembandezelo liyasondela, kwaye uSathana ngobuyatha ulungiselela ukuma kwakhe, ade acinge ngekratshi ukuba angaphumelela ukuze yena nezithunywa zakhe babe nehlabathi labo, ihlabathi elingenaye uThixo, kuba iindlela zabo zigwenxa. Inene, oko kuzenze kwagwenxa akunakulungiswa, kwaye akukho kuxolelwa okanye luxolo kuSathana nezithunywa zakhe, okanye inzala yabo.

Ndingunyana kaThixo oweza enyameni.

NdiyiNgonyama yesizwe sakwaYuda.

Ndingulowo uya kulawula iintlanga ngentonga yentsimbi.

Ndingulowo uya kukhulula amatywina kungekudala.

Ndingulowo uzela uMtshakazi wam.

Ndingulowo uya kunihlangabeza esibhakabhakeni.

Ndinguye endinguye, kwaye ixilongo sele liza kukhala.

Mna, Yeshua HaMashiach, ndithumele la Mazwi.

Kuvela kuYahweh, uThixo Omkhulu Ongunaphakade:

Ngoku kuza umgwebo kaThixo ongunaphakade, uYahweh ngokwakhe, kuba ndithumela uNyana wam, kwaye akusekho xesha liseleyo. Owu, nina balilize enacinga ukuba ningamoyisa uYahweh, uThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade! Owu, nina balilize enacinga ngobuyatha ukuba ningamoyisa uNyana wam okuphela kwamzeleyo, uYeshua HaMashiach. Nizifumbalele ingqumbo engunaphakade, kwaye umsindo wam uvutha bhe ngakunina, kuba ndinguThixo onekhwele. Kuba kwasekuqaleni niye nazichasa izicwangciso zam, kwaye niye nayikhathaza, nayicinezela, nayitshutshisa, nayingcungcuthekisa, nayitshabalalisa indalo yam. Aninalo uxolo.

Ndiphulaphuleni kwaye nindive kakuhle, kuba ndiyavakalisa oko kuza kwenzeka kuni, kwaye oko akutshoyo uYahweh akukho mntu unokukuguqula, nakanye, naphakade. Kwaye ke umgwebo wenu uyeza. Ndiza kuthumela uNyana wam ukuba ahlanganise abanyulwa bam nabathembekileyo, kwanoonyana baka-Adam ngoku abangoonyana bakaThixo. Nisilele, owu, wena nyoka ililize, etyibilikayo ekunganyanisekini nasebuxokini. Kuba aningoothixo, kwaye aninakuze nizihlangule esandleni sam. Kuba ndiza kuphindezela!

Ndiza kubuyisela kuni yonke imisebenzi emibi nayo yonke intlungu nokubandezeleka enikubangele abamsulwa, kwaye niya kutsha ngonaphakade kumalangatye ashushu kakhulu aluhlaza, kwaye umsi onetyhefu ekrakra uya kuba ngumoya wenu, kwaye niya kuba kwintuthumbo enkulu ngonaphakade. Kuba nindibangele ukuba nditshabalalise indalo yam, kwaumhlaba njengoko wawunjalo phambi konogumbe kaNowa, ngenxa yembewu yenu elilize enayenzela intshabalalo yoluntu; kwaye ke ngenxa yezi zinto, ndiza kunibhubhisa kwilifa lenu lomlilo.

Kwaye nazama ukuwutshabalalisa umhlaba emva konogumbe kaNowa, niphinda nenza amasikizi enu kwakhona, kodwa awayi kuba nangcambu nasebe; kuba wonke umdiza uya kuncothulwa, ubotshwe, uphoswe emlilweni. Kwaye ke ndiya kuwutshabalalisa umhlaba ngomgwebo ovuthayo, kuba ndathembisa ukuba andisayi kuphinda ndiwukhukulise wonke umhlaba kwakhona. Kodwa umgwebo wam uza ngomlilo, kuba ndingumlilo odlayo, kwaye umsindo wam uyaphuphuma ngakwabangendawo. Kwaye ke ngenxa yezi zinto, ndiza kunibhubhisa kowona mlilo ushushu nesulfure evuthayo.

Ndiphulaphuleni kwaye nindive kakuhle, kuba ndiyavakalisa oko kuza kwenzeka kuni, nakunina balindi balilize abashiya isikhundla senu sokuqala. Aninalo uxolo, kwaye nangona ndinikhulule emakhonkcweni enu okwexeshana elifutshane, ndikwenze oko kuphela ukuze nikhonze iinjongo zam. Emva koko umgwebo omkhulu uphezu kwenu, kwaye aninalo uxolo. Kuba nindibangele ukuba ndiwukhukulise umhlaba ngenxa yamasikizi enu. Nina ningoobani ukuba nizidlalele umdali ngendalo kaYahweh? Nina ningoobani ukuba ningcolise isitiya sikaThixo ngeendlela zenu ezingahlambulukanga? Nenza abantwana benu basemzini nabalahliweyo ngokuzingcolisa nabafazi, kwaye baya kwabelana nani kwilifa lenu lomlilo. Kuba ndamxelela ntoni uMalaki: Kuba, yabonani, iyeza imini, itsha njengeziko; bonke abanekratshi, ewe, nabo bonke abenza okungendawo, baya kuba ziindiza; kwaye imini ezayo, iya kubatshisa, utsho uYehova wemikhosi, ingabashiyeli ngcambu nasebe. Yabonani loo mini isondela ngokuqinisekileyo, kwaye nonke niya kuhlawula ngokweendlela zenu ezimbi.

Kuba ndibanikile abantu abafazi ukuze bangasweli nto, abafazi bobudlelane nokubazalela abantwana. Kodwa nina nehla eNtabeni iHermon, nishiya isikhundla senu sokuqala sozuko, kwaye nizidyobhile nabafazi, kwaye aninalo uxolo okanye ukuphumla. Njengoko ndenza uEnoki umbhali wanixelela kwixesha lesono senu: Aninalo uxolo: isigwebo esiqatha siphumele phezu kwakho sokukubeka emakhonkcweni: Kwaye akuyi kuba nanyamezelo okanye sicelo osinikiweyo, ngenxa yokungalungisi okufundisileyo, nangenxa yayo yonke imisebenzi yokungahloneli Thixo nokungalungisi nesono osibonise abantu. Kwaye emakhonkcweni niya kuya njengoko niphoswa echibini lomlilo.

Niyenzakalisile indalo yam ngenxa yeenkanuko zenu, nithatha ngogonyamelo abaninzi enabakhetha njengabafazi benu, kwaye niya kuhlawula. Nizimanye nooSathana, kwaneenkosana ezazixhobe ngokuchasene nam, kwaye nakhetha ubuxoki beeserafi. Ngoko ke, niya kuzimanya nazo kwichibi elikhulu lomlilo ongunaphakade, kwaye niya kutsha ngonaphakade, kwaye anisoze nifumane kuphumla. Niyenzakalisile indalo yam kwaye nabulala abaninzi, kwaye aninankathalo ngobomi endibudalileyo. Ngoko ke, andiyi kuba nankathalo ngani, kwaye ndinidalile, kwaye ngoku kuza umgwebo wenu nentshabalaliso yenu ekugqibeleni.

Ndithi kooSathana, kubalindi, kwiikherubhi eziwileyo, kwiiserafi eziwileyo, kuye wonke umntu ozimanye nemvukelo kaSathana: niya kutsha ngokupheleleyo, ngokuphindaphindiweyo ngonaphakade; kuba ndiya kunibopha ndiniphose emlilweni apho impethu yenu ingafiyo, kwaamalangatye abekelwe nina balilize aya kutshisa ngawona malangatye ashushu ngenxa yenu, kuba nifanelekile. Kuba ndiya kwenza kuni kanye oko nikwenzileyo kwabanye. Yonke intlungu, usizi, ukuzisola, ukubandezeleka, kwasenzo ngasinye esenyanyekayo siya kubuyekezwa kuni ngonaphakade, kwaye aniyi kubaleka. Ndiya kunaneka phambi kweentlanga ndiniveze njengamaqhetseba eningawona, kwaye ziya kuthi koothixo abancinci (abangoothixo): Ingaba nawe usuke waba buthathaka njengathi? Ingaba usuke wanjengathi?

Ndiya kuzibuyisela ngokupheleleyo iindlela zenu ezimbi ezifubeni zenu, kwaye niya kuhlawula ngonaphakade. Niya kuphoswa niphila emalangatyeni, kwaye niya kutsha phambi kweMvana, neengelosi nawo onke amalungisa eniwaonileyo aya kufumana kum ngokuphindaphindiweyo kunoko nakuthathayo kuwo. Kuba xa nibuyekezelwa ububi benu, ndiya kube ndibabuyekezele abantwana bam abanyuliweyo nabathembekileyo ngomvuzo omkhulu oya kugqitha oko nikuthethileyo ngokuphindaphindiweyo nangaphezulu kwezenzo zenu ezililize.

Kwaye kuya kuba njalo. Ndiya kudala izulu elitsha nomhlaba omtsha apho kuhlala ubulungisa, kwaye abantwana bam baya kuhlala nam, kwaye ndiya kwenza ikhaya lam phakathi kwabantu, kwaye kuya kuba njalo. Kodwa nina niya kutsha kwiindlela zenu ezimbi, kwaye yonke into embi eniyenzileyo iya kubuyiselwa kuni ngonaphakade. Kwaye ke ndiya kubeka itywina, kwaye niya kulityalwa, kwaye aniyi kuza engqondweni. Kwaye bonke baya kuhlala eluxolweni, kwaye nina anisoze nibe noxolo.

Yabonani, ixesha liphelile, kwaye ndithumela uNyana wam kuMtshakazi wakhe, uYesu Krestu olilungisa! Kuba kuye ndikholisekile! Kwaye bonke abo abasindisileyo nabaphilisileyo kwiindlela zenu ezililize, kwabo nibamangalela ngokungekho sikweni, baya kuphumla kum kwaye babe noxolo olugqibeleleyo, uvuyo, nothando. Kuba ndiya kunenza njengabo kwaye ndibenze babe nozuko ngaphezu kokuba naninjalo, kwaye aninabo ubukumkani, kuba aningokumkani.

Yeha, abemi bomhlaba, kuba ingqumbo enkulu iphezu kwenu. Kuba nimkhanyele umdali wenu kwaye nithanda ukufa. Kuba ukundikhanyela kukuthanda ukufa, kwaye ekufeni niya kufa. Ingqumbo yam iyatshisa ngani, kwaye ndiya kususa zonke iintywenka, lonke uvuyo, kwanezidingo zenu ezisisiseko aziyi kuhlangatyezwa. Kuba xa ndandinigalela ubabalo nenceba, nakhetha endaweni yoko ukusebenzisa ubabalo njengethuba lokuzimanya nabalilize, ubukumkani bukaSathana, ngoxa naninokubaleka. Kwaye ke ngoku abaninzi baya kutshabalala, amagama abo angazange abhalwe eNcwadini yoBomi kwasekuqalekeni. UNyana wam, uYesu Krestu, uyasondela, kwaye uya kumxhwila umtshakazi wakhe ezulwini, kwaye ndiya kumsusa umthinteli. Yabonani! Ndithumela iingxilimbela ukuzalisekisa ingqumbo yam! Naluthatha ubabalo lwam nenceba yam ngeze kwaye naphila ubomi besono obumnandi, kwaye ndiya kuphindezela.

Kuba ngoxa naninokuba noxolo ngonaphakade, ubomi obungunaphakade obuzele luvuyo nolonwabo, nakhetha ukufa kwenu, kwaye ndiya kuninika oko nikunqwenelayo. Nifuna ilizwe lobubi nobumnyama; ndiya kuninika kanye oko nikunqwenelayo, kwaye ndiya kunibuyekezela ngayo yonke intlungu eniyibangele abanye njengoko naniphila esonweni esikhulu. Ndiya kunihlutha yonke intywenka, kwaye niya kudla ubumdaka beendlela zenu. Umgwebo omkhulu uphezu komhlaba, kwaye akusayi kubakho kulibazisa.

Yabonani! Bonke abanqula igama leNkosi baya kusindiswa. Kuba isihlwele esikhulu siya kusindiswa kwimbandezelo ezayo, kwaye ndiya kubamkela kum. Kuba kubhaliwe kwathiwa: Aba ngabo baphuma embandezelweni enkulu, bahlamba iingubo zabo, bazenza mhlophe egazini leMvana. Ngenxa yoko baphambi kwetrone kaThixo, bamkhonza imini nobusuku etempileni yakhe; nalowo uhleli etroneni uya kuhlala phakathi kwabo. Abasayi kulamba, abasayi kunxanwa, nelanga alisayi kubatshisa, nabuphi na ubushushu. Kuba iMvana ephakathi kwetrone, iya kubalusa, ibakhokelele emithonjeni ephilayo yamanzi; kwaye uThixo uya kuzosula zonke iinyembezi emehlweni abo.

Ndiyanibongoza nonke ukuba ninqule igama likaYesu Krestu, uMesiya wokwenene, uNyana okuphela kwamzeleyo kaThixo, kuba uyeza, kwaye ingqumbo yeMvana nengqumbo kaYahweh sele iza kukhululwa.

NdinguYahweh, uThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade.

Ndingu-El Shaddai, uThixo uSomandla.

NdinguYehova Yire.

NdinguElohim omkhulu, uThixo kaSirayeli.

Ndingulowo wathumela uNyana wakhe okuphela kwamzeleyo ukuhlangula abo baya kuhlangulwa.

Ndingulowo wamvusayo uKrestu Yesu kwabafileyo wamnika igama elingaphezu kwawo onke amagama, ukuze onke amadolo agobe kwaye zonke iilwimi zivume.

NDINGUYE OMKHULU ENDINGUYE, kwaye ingqumbo yam sele iza kuzalisekiswa, kwaye akukho mkhulu kunam, kwaye ndithethile.

Mna, Yahweh, uThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade, ndithumele amazwi endiwathumeleyo, kwaye ndithumele la mazwi. Ndithumele unyana wam, uYeshua HaMashiach, kwaye yena uthumele amazwi akhe kwabathembekileyo. Ixesha liphelile.
