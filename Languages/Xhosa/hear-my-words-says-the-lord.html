Yivani Amazwi Am, Itsho INkosi!

Yivani amazwi am, itsho iNkosi! Usuku lweNkosi sele luza kunithi gqabhu okomlilo odlayo yonke into, kwaye ngomlilo ziya kuhlanjululwa izono zabaninzi abaya kubiza egameni lam bengayithandi imiphefumlo yabo kude kube sekupheleni. Izimvu zam ziyaliva ilizwi lam, kwaye ndiya kuzenzela indlela, nangona ziya kudibana neenkathazo ezininzi. Abo babambelele kwigama lam elinamandla baya kusindiswa, kwananjengokungathi kuphunywe emlilweni. Utshaba luphezu kwenu, nina bakhukhumeleyo abalukhabilayo uxolo endanibonelela ngalo. Izono zenu zifike phambi kwam kwaye umgwebo omkhulu uphezu kwenu. Nangona umlilo wam ungazukutshabalalisa abantwana bam abancinane, kwaye amaxesha aya kuba ngawona anzima kakhulu kunanini na, abaninzi abangcwele baya kubaleka ukufa kwesibini ngokuguqukela egameni lam ngeyure yabo yenkathazo nangokuma beqinile kude kube sekupheleni. Mna, iNkosi uYesu Krestu, ndikuthethile kwaye ndithumele isilumkiso kwabo baneendlebe zokuva namehlo okubona.

Owu, nina zizwe, nina zizwe ezikhonza izithixo ezirhweba ngezono ezikhulu. Ewe, nibe ngabarhwebi bobubi abazukisa okhohlakeleyo ngawo onke amazwi enu amabi neendlela zenu ezimbi enizifundele ukuzitshabalalisa. Nibe ngabarhwebi benyama nabantu, kuba kwanabantwana baye baba yimpahla yenu yokurhweba. Ingqumbo yeMvana, ewe, ingqumbo yam iya kuwutshabalalisa umhlaba wenu, kwaye umlilo wam uya kuwuhlambulula umhlaba kwimisebenzi yabenzi bobubi. Phambi kokuba bufike ubukumkani bam, intlambululo enkulu iya kuwuthimba umhlaba, kwaye umhlaba uya kungcoliswa ligazi labenzi bobubi. Ukanti, ndiya kubakha ubukumkani bam emhlabeni, ubukumkani bobulungisa noxolo apho kungasayi kungena nto imbi, kwaye ndiya kuzilawula iintlanga ngentonga yentsimbi.

Le nto ikhoyo ngoku iya kutshatyalaliswa kwaye ayisayi kuphinda yakhiwe, kwaye ndiya kuziwisa phantsi ngokupheleleyo izithixo zenu, neendawo eziphakamileyo zeengqondo zenu ziya kuthotywa. Kuba nithe entliziyweni yenu, "Andingomhlolokazi!" Ukanti intshabalalo yenu iya kunifikela ngeyure enye, kwaye iya kuwuginya ngokupheleleyo ize iwubumbe ngokutsha umhlaba njengoko beniwazi. Kutheni ninqula ukufa kwenu, nina bakhonzi bezithixo bomhlaba? Kutheni nibeka bucala kwaye ningcwalise isohlwayo senu enizifumbele sona kwimiphefumlo yenu? Anibonanga na? Anivanga na? Anifundanga na? Hayi, niziimfama, kwaye nizizithulu, kwaye aninakuyiqonda imizwi yam kuba nimkhabile umoya wam oyingcwele ebendiya kuwuthumela phezu kwenu ndize ndinihlambulule ngokupheleleyo phambi kwam ukuba beningayamkela nje into endinipha yona.

Ndiya kubuyisela, itsho iNkosi. Ndiya kubuyisela umntu ngamnye ngokwemisebenzi yakhe, kwaye ndiya kunika isahlulo esiphindwe kabini somgwebo njengoko ndibuyisela imisebenzi yenu ibe yingozi kwimiphefumlo yenu, kwaye aniyi kuphinda nichache. Umgwebo sele uza kuqhambuka emhlabeni, kwaye uninzi lwenu lusahoye ubizo lwam kwaye aluwathobeli amazwi am ebeya kunisindisa ngokuqinisekileyo kwintlekele yenu. Iyure yomgwebo wenu iyasondela, kwaye abaninzi baya kulwamkela uphawu, becinga ukuba bangazisindisa iintamo zabo baze baphile kwizono abazithandayo ixesha elide elingakumbi. Unyana wentshabalalo uya kuthembisa izinto ezininzi kwaye uya kulukuhla umhlaba wonke njengoko bemangaliswa lirhamncwa, besithi, "Ngubani onjengerhamncwa?"

Ukanti, lowo ukhohlakeleyo uzayo, nemiqondiso yakhe yobuxoki nemimangaliso, uya kuningcatsha, kwaye niya kutshabalala ngokupheleleyo. Niya kufela ezonweni zenu nize nivukele kumgwebo wetrone emhlophe enkulu apho niya kuphendula ngesono ngasinye, ngengcinga nganye embi, ewe, ngelizwi ngalinye elingenamsebenzi niya kuphendula ngalo. Kwaye kwiingubo zenu ezimdaka niya kufunyanwa nintswele, kwaye aniyi kuma nithe tye, kuba akukho nyama ithe tye emehlweni akhe, kodwa bonke baye balahleka baza banqula ukonakala kwabo. Owu, mntu, nabani na ondilahlayo nolahlileyo uMdali wakhe uya kutshabalala ngokupheleleyo ngaphandle konyango. Ngoku lixesha losindiso. Niya kubalekela kubani xa nithinjwa nize niphoswe niphila kwichibi lomlilo? Umqamelo wenu uya kuba sisulfure evuthayo, kwaye iingubo zenu ziya kuba ngamadangatye anaphakade awawalungiselela uMtyholi neengelosi zakhe uBawo. Ukanti, ngokundilahla, nizikhethele isiphelo senu naza nazimanya nokhohlakeleyo, kwaye niya kwabelana naye ngomgwebo wakhe.

Mna, iNkosi uYesu Krestu, owenziwa waba yinyama waza wahlala phakathi kwenu, inene, ndiya kubutshabalalisa ngokupheleleyo ubukumkani botshaba, kwaye umhlaba uya kuba lidabi lokugqibela. Balekani ngoku kusenokwenzeka kuba ixesha seliphantse laphela! Kusele iinkozo ezimbalwa zentlabathi ukuba ziwe kwiglasi yexesha, kwaye ixilongo liya kukhala. Njengoko iBandla lam lixhwilwa kwaye umtshakazi wam esiwa kumagumbi akhe, uSathana nomkhosi wakhe baya kuphoswa emhlabeni, kwaye uya kulwa imfazwe ngokupheleleyo nabemi bomhlaba. Umwonyo uya kuvulwa, kwaye izidalwa ezininzi ezimdaka ziya kunyuka zifuna nantoni na ezinokuyidla. Owu, mntu osisidenge, awuqondi na ukuba ngobabalo lwam nenceba yam ndibuthintelile ubukumkani botshaba obupheleleyo ukuba bungezi phezu kwenu?

Ukanti, ndiya kuninika oko nikunqwenelayo kwiinkanuko zenu ezinesono, kwaye ndiya kubukhulula bonke ubukumkani bakhe phezu kwenu, kwaye niya kunikelwa ngokupheleleyo ezandleni zeentshaba zenu. Injongo yakhe kukutshabalalisa lonke uluntu kude kube ngumphefumlo wokugqibela, kuba ufuna umhlaba ongenathixo wakhe, nto leyo angasokuze ayifumane. Ukuba bendingayinqumlanga imihla, ebengaphumelela, kwaye bekungasayi kusala nyama iphila! Ukanti, nisakhetha izono zenu kunentsindiso, uthando, nokukhanya endinalo nina ukuba beningakhetha nje ubomi nize nivume igama lam njengeNkosi! Ewe, ndenzeni mna, uYesu Krestu, iNkosi yenu, njengoko ndiphila ngonaphakade njengesikhanyiso kwabo sele beza kutshabalala. Abo babiza egameni lam baya kusindiswa.

Bantwana bam abancinane, ixesha seliphantse laphela. Ndiyazi ukuba uninzi lwenu ludiniwe kwaye lukhathazekile kude kube semiphefumlweni yenu bububi obuvele ngeenxa zonke kuni. Le yimizuzu yokugqibela yemihelo yokuzala, kwaye abanyulwa bam baya kusindiselwa emazulwini, apho ndibonelele ngendawo yokhuseleko apho banokuzimela khona. Umvuzo wam usesandleni sam wabathembekileyo kwanabangathembekanga, abaya kufumana ilahleko kodwa basindiswe baphile ngonaphakade kuba bendenze iNkosi yabo. Ngoko ke, hlukani kwizono zenu nize nikhangele kum, ukuze ndikhanyise ukukhanya kwam entliziyweni yenu nize nizihlambulule ngegazi lam lentlawulelo. Musani ukuwoyika amaxesha kwaye musani ukuwoyika umsebenzi oseleyo kuni, kuba ixesha lisondele.

Njengoko iziganeko zityhileka phambi kwamehlo enu, yazini ukuba ezi ziganeko ziya kukhawuleza kwaye izinto ziya kwenzeka ngokukhawuleza okukhulu, kuba umgwebo awulali. Amaxesha obabalo namaxesha okuhlaziya iBandla lam ayaphela. Inene, andilibazisanga ngokuphathelele kwizithembiso kodwa ndibonise ukuzeka kade umsindo okukhulu nomonde ukuze wonke umphefumlo wokugqibela endiwukhethileyo uze kum. Umgwebo uphezu kwenu, nina mhlaba, kwaye ngabathembekileyo kuphela eBandleni lam abaya kusinda. Kutheni nisavumela abakhohlisi nabaprofeti bobuxoki ukuba banikhathaze ngamazwi abo akhathazayo abawathethayo ngokuchasene nokulula okufunyanwa kum? Kutheni ningabavumela ukuba banirhwebeshe kude nomtshato wam nomtshakazi wam xa bendiya kunilungiselela isihlalo etafileni ukuba beningaphuma nje ezonweni zenu nize nikholwe ngamazwi okwenene aLizwini lam eliNgcwele?
