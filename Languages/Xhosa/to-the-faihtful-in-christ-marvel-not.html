Kwabanyanisekileyo KuKristu: Musani Ukumangaliswa!

Musani ukumangaliswa, bazalwana bam, musani ukumangaliswa, nangona eli hlabathi linithiyile. Yazini ukuba ihlabathi landithiya mna ngaphambi kokuba lithiye nina, zithandwa zam, kwaye umkhonzi akamkhulu kunenkosi yakhe. Ngoko ke, ziqondeni iixesha eniphila kuzo kwaye nisondele ngakumbi ezintliziyweni zenu kuYeshua HaMashiach, kuba ndinibizela kufutshane kum bandla lam, mzimba wam, nkqu nomtshakazi wam, kwaye ndinibizela kufutshane ngakumbi kula maxesha kanye phambi kokuba nithathwe nisiwe emazulwini ukuze noyise ngokupheleleyo zonke izinto ngoKristu.

Kwaye ngoku ngequbuliso nangokungalindelekanga niya kuthathwa nisiwe emazulwini, kuba ndiza ngeyure eningayilindelanga. Yabonani! Ndiza njengesela ebusuku! Kodwa njengoko yayinjalo imihla kaNowa, kuya kuba njalo nokufika koNyana woMntu. Kuba njengoko kwakunjalo ngemihla yangaphambi konogumbe, babesitya besela, bezeka bethwala, kwada kwayimini awangena ngayo uNowa emkhombeni, bengazi nto, wada wafika unogumbe, wabatshayela bonke, kuya kuba njalo nokufika koNyana woMntu.

Ngoko ke ndinicebisile zithandwa zam, ukuba nimke kwiinkathazo zeli hlabathi, nisazi ukuba ukumka kwenu kweli hlabathi lingendawo kusondele. Noko ke, kunjalo ukuba nisenobomi ekufuneka nibuphilile, kwaye kufuneka niqhubeke de ningabikho, kuba xa nithathwa ngequbuliso nisiwe emazulwini ngesandla sam esinamandla sosindiso, iindlela zenu ziya kube zigqityiwe kwaye nobomi benu emhlabeni buya kube bugqityiwe ngelo xesha. Ngoko ke ndiyanicebisa bantwana bam, philani ubomi benu ngokuthobela ngokupheleleyo ilizwi leNkosi noMsindisi wenu, uKristu Yesu iNkosi yenu enyukileyo, kwaye ndiya kwenza ukuba noyise ngokupheleleyo!

Ngoko ke, njengoko niphila ubomi benu, nikela konke kum ekuzinikeleni nasekukhuthazekeni kweentliziyo zenu kuba ndikukwanela kwenu ezintweni zonke. Musani ukucinga ezintliziyweni zenu ukuba niphumelele kwaye nichumile ngamandla ezandla zenu, okanye ngesidanga seziko lemfundo abaninzi benu abasixabise ngobuyatha ngaphezu kolwazi lukaYahweh, nkqu nobuxoki enathengiselwa bona njengenyaniso kwimfundo yenu engeyonyani eyakhelwe ngokupheleleyo phezu kobuxoki, kodwa ngobuyatha niyaqhayisa ngezakhono zenu enicinga ukuba nizifumene ngaloo ndlela. Kwaye ngoko ke ndiyanixelela kwakhona, phumani phakathi kwabo nahlukane nabo ezintweni zonke, kungenjalo niya kwabelana nabo kwizigwebo nezibetho zabo xa into enjalo ingeyomfuneko, kuba ngalo lonke eli xesha langoku ndiyanibiza kwaye ndiyanicenga ukuba nize ngokupheleleyo kum eluthandweni lwam ngani bantwana bam. Ngoko ke, lahlanani nekratshi elingenaxabiso eliziswa lulwazi!

Kungoko, ndinika ukuqonda ukuba nantoni na eniyizuzileyo kobu bomi ngokuphathelele kwimpahla yeli hlabathi inikwe ngokukholekileyo nangobubele nangofefe lukaYahweh, uMkhulu onguMNA NDINGUYE, onqwenela ngaphezu kwako konke ukuba nichume nibe sempilweni njengoko umphefumlo wenu uchuma. Noko ke, musani ukuba ziziyatha kangako nicinge ukuba nizenzile okanye ukuba kwiimbeko zenu zehlabathi nimelwe phambi koYahweh, uMkhulu onguMNA NDINGUYE, okanye uYesu Krestu, kuba akukho nyama igwetyelwayo emehlweni ethu. Ngoko ke qondani ukuba abo baziphakamisayo ngaloo ndlela baya kulahlekelwa kakhulu ngenxa yekratshi labo, elithi ngokuqinisekileyo landulele intshabalalo enkulu.

Ngenxa yenu, ndiyanicenga ukuba niphume kweli hlabathi nazo zonke iimo zalo zokuzigwagwisa ezingalunganga, zokuzenzela, zokuzithethelela okungeyonyani okungamkelekanga kuThixo, nokuzingca kwenyama. Kuba, inene, inene, ukuba ubabalo lukaYahweh, uMkhulu onguMNA NDINGUYE, belungaphalazwanga eluthandweni lwakhe olungunaphakade ngani, apho uthando lwakhe olungunaphakade nokukhanya kwakhe kukhanye ezintliziyweni nasebomini benu ebusweni bukaYesu Krestu: nonke beniya kuba njengeSodom neGomora kwaye beningayi kuba nangcambu nasebe, ngoko ke makuqhayiswe ngoKristu. Noko ke, eso siphelo asisesenu zithandwa zam, ngoko ke, eluthandweni lwenu ngam, thobelani ilizwi lam ukuze noyise zonke izinto kuba ukuba niyalithobela ilizwi lam, ndiya kwenza ukuba noyise!

Kunjalo, kwaye kuya kuba njalo, ukuba kuboyisi bam kukho umvuzo omkhulu, nkqu nakwabo boyisileyo ngendlela efanayo nam, kwaye umvuzo wabo uya kuba ngowona mkhulu. Noko ke, abaninzi kwabanyanisekileyo bam kuKristu bayahendwa kwaye balaliswe, ngokungathi kunjalo, babuyiselwe kwabafileyo! Nkqu naphambi kwemiyalelo yam endayinika uPawulos kwiminyaka emininzi eyadlulayo kwileta yakhe eya kwabase-Efese, kwaye ke wabhala, kwaye ngoko ke ndiyanikhumbuza zithandwa zam: Kungoko athi, vuka wena uleleyo, uphakame kwabafileyo, kwaye uKristu uya kukunika ukukhanya. Lumkani ngoko ukuba nihambe ngokulumkileyo, ningabi njengeziyatha, kodwa nibe njengezilumko, nilisebenzise kakuhle ixesha: kuba imihla mibi. Kungoko, musani ukuba ziziyatha, kodwa qondani ukuba yintoni intando yeNkosi.

Ngaba niyayiqonda intando yeNkosi yenu, bantwana bam? Ngaba yiyo yonke into enijolise kuyo? Okanye ngaba nizikhonza nina ekuzinikeleni kweentliziyo zenu, nifuna ubutyebi obungakumbi ukuze nongeze kwizikhwama zenu yonke imihla? Ngaba nizilungelelanise iintliziyo zenu ekukhonzeni iinkampani, okanye amaqela abantu abanesono ngenxa yenzuzo emdaka, nkqu nangexesha apho imali kanye amadoda ayiqwebayo kwaye ayixabise ngesono iya kuthi kungekudala ibhengezwe njengengenaxabiso phambi kwamehlo awo? Okanye ngaba niqhubekile ekuthobeleni ilizwi lam kwaye niqwalasele ngononophelo ilizwi ngalinye endininika lona ngenxa yosindiso lwenu? Ukuba niyaqhubeka ekuthobeleni ilizwi lam ndiya kwenza ukuba noyise zonke izinto, ngoko ke, musani ukutyhafa!

Ndisanicenga, nonke nina banyanisekileyo kuKristu nabo bonke abaya kuthi babe nokholo kuKristu ukuba niqhubeke niguqukela kum kuba ndinimemela kufutshane nangakumbi kum ukuze ubuhlobo obupheleleyo bothando lwam ngani bunomeleze kwaye bunixhase ezintweni zonke, ngoko ke, musani ukoyika, ningachithi xesha ekuzigwebeni. Ngoko ke, qhubekani nizondelela eluthandweni lwenu nasekuzinikeleni kweentliziyo zenu kwiNkosi yenu uYesu Krestu, bantwana bam, kuba akusekho xesha lokuchitha! Kwaye xa kukhala ixilongo niya kuba phakathi kweentombi eziziyatha ezishiyelwe emlilweni wokucokisa, okanye niya kufunyanwa phakathi kweentombi ezilumkileyo eziya kuthathwa ziye emazulwini nomyeni wazo nangawuphi na umzuzwana ngoku. Uya kuba nguwuphi kwaye uya kuma phi xa ndivela ngequbuliso nangawuphi na umzuzwana ngoku?

Ngaba nisaya kundivumela ukuba ndinike ioli yezibane zenu ukuze nime esihlalweni sam sokugweba? Ngaba nisaya kuzicanda kakuhle iintambo zezibane zenu ngokuthobela imiyalelo yam? Okanye ngaba niya kukhetha ngobuyatha ukuhlala nizanele nina nime kubulungisa benu enizifezekisele bona kwaye nihlale nimi phakathi kweentombi eziziyatha? Nkqu naxa ndilungiselele okungcono kuni, ukuba beniya kulithobela ngokupheleleyo ilizwi lam. Ngoko ke, ndiyanicebisa nina banyanisekileyo bam, qhubekani niza kum ngalo lonke uthando nentobelo ukuze ndiniphakamise kakhulu kwezona ndawo ziphakamileyo zamazulu ukuze nikhanye njengeenkwenkwezi zesibhakabhaka ngenxa yezo zinto nizenzileyo ngokuthobela ekukhonzeni iNkosi yenu engunaphakade yobulungisa!

Ngoko ke, ndiyabuza nonke nina enilaziyo ilizwi lam, nkqu nani nibizwe ngegama, nabiza egameni lam: Ngaba nivuke ngokupheleleyo? Ngaba amehlo enu avuleke ngokupheleleyo kwihlabathi njengoko linjalo namhlanje? Qondani ukuba ihlabathi eniphila kulo ngoku alifani nanjengoko lalinjalo kwanakwiinyanga ezintandathu ezidlulileyo, kwaye umhlaba uyaqhubeka utshintsha kakhulu kanye phambi kwamehlo enu, nangona abaninzi benu besazithethelela iimpawu nemimangaliso ngokungakholwa okungenalukholo befuna endaweni yoko ukubuyela ekulaleni. Ngoko ke, zihloleni nina nezicwangciso zenu neNkosi yenu engunaphakade ukuze ndikhokele amanyathelo enu kwaye ndinigcine engozini eluthandweni nakubabalweni lwam.

Kunjalo, ubuxoki benamba ngoku buzele emhlabeni nangaphezu kokuba kwakunjalo ngaphambi kokuba inamba iphoswe phantsi; kuba inamba iphoswe phantsi, iphoselwe emhlabeni kwaye ibotshelelwe phantsi kwesibhakabhaka ukuze igwetywe, kwaye iyazi ukuba inexesha elifutshane kwaye ingqumbo yayo inkulu! Kwaye iza kulwa kwaye iimfazwe zayo ziya kuba nkulu, kodwa akukho bukhulu buya kuba nabo loo nyoka indala ngaphandle kwesulfure evuthayo neqengqelekayo evutha awona malangatye ashushu esohlwayo.

Kwaye ngoko ke ndiza kuni zithandwa zam ukuba ndinikhuthaze ukuba niqhubeke nisondele ngakumbi entliziyweni yam ngokuthobela kwenu amazwi am, kwaye ezindongeni zentliziyo yam igama ngalinye lenu elipheleleyo likrolwe eluthandweni lwam ngani apho, olungunaphakade nolungapheliyo. Ewe, eluthandweni lwam ngani, (ndiyazi ngamnye wenu ngcono kunokuba nizazi nina) ndinibambe kufutshane kakhulu entliziyweni yam ngamaxesha onke kwaye ngakumbi kumaxesha enu eenkxwaleko neemvavanyo enijongene nazo ngokuqinisekileyo. (Ndiqokelele yonke inyembezi yenu kwibhotile yam yeenyembezi ukuze ndinibuyekeze ngenye nenye) Kwaye kumadabi aqhubekayo kule mfazwe yembewu eqhubekayo, ephunyelelweyo ngoKristu kwaye eqhubeka ngokuchasene nabawileyo; qondani, ukuba amadabi enu aphunyelelwe ngokupheleleyo kwiNkosi yenu engunaphakade! Ngoko ke, phumulani kwiNkosi yenu engunaphakade, uKristu Yesu, kwaye niyeke imisebenzi yenu yobuyatha kwaye niphumle ngokupheleleyo apho, kwaye nigqibezele imisebenzi yobulungisa endininike yona ukuba niyenze ukuze noyise iintshaba zenu kwaye ndiya kuninika iSabatha! Selah!

Ngoko ke, yiqondeni imihla eniphila kuyo kuba akukho namnye kuni owakhe waphila le mihla ifikileyo emhlabeni kweli xesha lokugqibela, nangona ibonakala ngokukhohlisayo ifana noko benikwazi. Kuba umhlaba useguqukweni kwaye ngenene umhlaba uyatshintshwa, kwaye ngenene, ngenene umhlaba uya kuqhubeka utshintsha ngamandla ngokweenjongo nemiyalezo kaYahweh.

Qondani ukuba ixesha lotshintsho olukhulu lifikile emhlabeni, nkqu nexesha apho umhlaba ulungelelaniswa ngokutsha kwaye uhlelwa ngokutsha ziziganeko neentlekele nezigwebo ezizayo ukuze kubuyiselwe umhlaba owawukho ngemihla kaNowa! Kwaye iziganeko nezigwebo ziya kwenzeka kanye njengoko kwaxelwa kwangaphambili ngabaprofeti bakudala ukuba ziya kwenzeka ngokupheleleyo ngexesha lenkathazo kaYakobi. Kungoko qondani, ukuba izigwebo ezininzi sele ziqalisile ukuwa nkqu nakweli xesha lotshintsho kuba bekuya kuba yinto engenakwenzeka ngokwesiqhelo ukuba izinto zenzeke ngayo nayiphi na enye indlela.

Kwaye ngoko kubhaliwe, kwaye kuya kwenziwa njalo, xa ndiqokelela abanyanisekileyo kuKristu kungekudala nangokungalindelekanga ngoku xa umthinteli eya kususwa, kwaye umkhenkce uya kuwa, kwaye amatywina aya kuvulwa, kwaye amaxilongo aya kukhala! Ukuze amangqina am amabini avuke aya kuyalela umlilo ukuba uhle uvela emazulwini, aya kubetha umhlaba ngazo zonke iintlobo zezibetho rhoqo njengoko ethanda, kwaye aya kubangela ukuba kungani emhlabeni ngeentsuku zokuprofeta kwawo ngomyalelo wawo, kwaye abaninzi baya kwala ukuguquka! Qondani ukuba aya kuma ngokuchasene nenamba, nerhamncwa nomprofeti wobuxoki kwaye ukuba kukho intando yomntu yokubenzakalisa loo mntu kufuneka enzakaliswe ngendlela efanayo. Ukuba intando yomntu kukubabulala loo mntu kufuneka abulawe ngendlela efanayo. Ezi ziziphatho zam zezibane ezibini nemithi yam emibini yeminquma kwaye zininzi, zininzi izinto abaya kuzityhila ngamaxesha okutyhilwa kwabo kwaye ubungqina babo buya kubhalwa ngonaphakade.

La ngamaxesha eniphila kuwo, bantwana bam, kwaye la ngamaxesha ekufuneka niqhubeke niwanyamezele ngokusondela ngakumbi kwintando yam ebomini benu kuba apho kukhona ukhuseleko nosindiso lwenu. Qondani, ukuba nakhula njengamathole esitalini kumaxesha am obabalo nenceba awayenikelwe usindiso lwezizwe ezilahlekileyo ezazisasazwe phakathi kweentlanga kwaye ngaloo ndlela, zasikelelwa iintlanga kwaye zagalelwa uthando, imfesane, nobabalo kwaye abaninzi bazuza usindiso ngaloo ndlela. Kodwa ngoku amaxesha atshintshe kakhulu, kwaye ndiyanilayela ukuba nihambe ngobulumko OBUNINZI, kwaye nibe nobuqili njengeenyoka nibe msulwa njengamahobe kuba la maxesha anobungozi obukhohlisayo.

Kuba inamba ngoku ibotshelelwe emhlabeni kwaye abawileyo baya besomelela ngomzuzwana, kodwa abanamandla kwaphela okumelana nelizwi lam elinamandla namandla am apheleleyo! Ngoko ke, ndiyabakhalimela kwaye ndibathintele ngenxa yenu ngaphezu kokuba ninokuqonda ngeli xesha kwaye ndibeke zonke izinto zobukumkani beentshaba phantsi kweenyawo zenu ukuze nizinyathele ngenene ukuba kukho imfuneko. Ngoko ke, musani ukuboyika kuba ndoyisile zonke izinto, nkqu nobukumkani bukaSathana obupheleleyo; kwaye nani niya koyisa ngendlela efanayo, ukuba nihlala nithembekile kumazwi am omyalelo endiwanikileyo nendiwanikayo ukuze nithobele.

Musani ukumangaliswa, zithandwa zam, nkqu nanjengoko abawileyo bezityhila emhlabeni, musani ukumangaliswa nakancinci! Kuba imihla kaNowa ifikile! Nkqu nemihla apho ooSathana neengelosi zabo baphoselwa emhlabeni, bangaphindi babuyele emazulwini apho ngoku bagxothwe khona ngonaphakade ekoyisweni okukhulu. Nkqu nemihla apho abagcini ngoku bakhululwa kwiintolongo zabo apho babebotshelelwe kwaye bagcinwe bodwa omnye komnye kwintuthumbo enkulu ngenxa yolwaphulo-mthetho lwabo olucekisekayo abalwenza ngokuchasene noYahweh nendalo yakhe ngemihla yakudala kwaye ekoyisweni okukhulu babebotshelelwe kwimvukelo yabo nezifungo abazifungayo ukuba bazifezekise. Noko ke, izifungo zabo ziya kuzaliseka ngonaphakade xa bebotshelelwe kwaye bephoselwe emlilweni wesohlwayo kwelo chibi likhulu lesulfure evuthayo apho impethu yabo ingafi.

Nkqu nemihla, kwakhona, ebuyayo njengakwimihla kaNowa xa abagcini behla eNtabeni iHermon ngemihla kaYerede. Nkqu nemihla ebuyileyo apho abawileyo ngoku basebenza ngokuthe ngqo kunye nookumkani bomhlaba kunye nabaphathi babo behlabathi abazimela ngabo ababandakanyekayo kwizicwangciso zabo ezijolise ekuziseni intshabalalo enkulu nentlekele nemfazwe kungekudala nje ukuba umthinteli asuswe, xa ububi babo buya kukhululwa ngokupheleleyo. Ewe, bakhululwe emhlabeni, kwaye xa umthinteli esusiwe ubukumkani bukaSathana obupheleleyo buya kukhululwa xa umkhenkce usiwa, kwaye kwalasha kuvulwe, kwaye iingcango zabo ziya kuphila kwakhona. Yabonani! Ndizisa iingxilimbela ukuze ndizalisekise ingqumbo yam!

Musani ukumangaliswa, zithandwa zam, nkqu nanjengoko abawileyo bekhanyisa izibhakabhaka phantsi kwesibhakabhaka ngezibane zabo, nee-orbs, njengoko ngoku bezibonakalisa kwiimo abazithathayo kwaye oomatshini babo ngoku bakhululekile ukubhabha kwaye inkohliso eninzi nokudideka bakubangele ebusweni bomhlaba ngetekhnoloji yabo, nkqu nangesiphelo sentlungu yokubeleka, eza kugqitywa ngequbuliso nangokungalindelekanga kwintlekele kungekudala ngoku. Kwaye ngoko ke umntu wemvelo uyamangaliswa kwaye uqikelela kwaye uzithethelela izinto ezingaphezu kwendalo eziye zaba yindalo kwakhona.

Ewe, uluntu ngokungakholwa luyawathethelela amandla kaYahweh aboniswe ngenjongo yokuba uluntu luvukele ebulungiseni kwaye lufumane usindiso. Endaweni yokunika uYahweh imbeko ngomsebenzi wezandla zakhe neempawu ezijolise ekubalumkiseni ngesigwebo esizayo; uluntu endaweni yoko lunika imbeko kuluntu kunye netekhnoloji yalo engenaxabiso eluyifumene kwabawileyo: ngoko ke isigwebo esirhabaxa nesinzima siyeza! Musani ukuba nenxaxheba okanye ilifa kwiingqiqo zabo ezingenankolo kwaye niqhubeke nizukisa uYahweh ngemisebenzi yakhe yosindiso enikwe kwizicwangciso zakhe ezingunaphakade zosindiso luka-Adam ebusweni bukaYesu Krestu, ongu-Adam wokugqibela.

Ngoko ke ndinika ukuqonda: nokuba leliphi na iqumrhu elibangela utshintsho ezulwini okanye emhlabeni, okanye utshintsho kumoya womhlaba, okanye nabani na otshintsha indalo kaYahweh nangayiphi na indlela: ndiyanilayela ukuba niqonde ngokupheleleyo ukuba uYahweh ulawula ngokupheleleyo indalo yakhe kwaye nantoni na eyenzekayo yenzeka kuphela ngemvume kaYahweh, nangolwazi lwakhe olupheleleyo, kwaye nokuzalisekisa izicwangciso zakhe ezingunaphakade zosindiso lonyana wakhe wolahleko u-Adam. Qondani, ukuba uYahweh uvumela abangalunganga, abaneentliziyo ezilukhuni, nabangathobeliyo ukuba baphile ubomi babo obunesono ukuze bazalise izono zabo ngokupheleleyo kwaye isigwebo sabo esizayo abayi kusibaleka, ngaphandle kokuba beze kuYesu Krestu ngenguquko epheleleyo ngelixa kusekho ixesha.

Nangona kunjalo, uYahweh uyabavumela abangemalungisa ukuba bone ukuze ekugwetyweni bahlawulelwe ngokupheleleyo, befumana imbuyekezo epheleleyo yezenzo zabo ezikhohlakeleyo phezu kweentloko zabo ngonaphakade kanaphakade emalangatyeni esohlwayo, nokuba loo nto ayifuneki kwaye inokuphetshwa elusindisweni ngokuthobela ubulungisa. Kubabalo lukaYahweh, nenceba yakhe kwicebo lakhe elingunaphakade losindiso luka-Adam; uYahweh unqwenela ukuba kungabikho mntu ulahlekelwa ngamadangatye, nangona kunjalo, bonke abantu banikwe inkululeko yokuzikhethela ukukhetha ububi, okanye ukukhetha ubulungisa ebusweni bukaYesu Krestu oya kudala ngokutsha bonke abo beza elusindisweni njengomntu omnye omtsha, kwaye baya kuphila ngonaphakade.

Kwaye ngoko ke abawileyo baya kukhululwa ngokupheleleyo ekugwetyweni kwabemi bomhlaba abangathobeliyo ngokwala igama likaYeshua HaMashiach onguYesu uKrestu! Ngoko ke, qondani ukuba bonke abo balayo uYesu Krestu njengeNkosi, okanye abaphila ngokungathembeki bengayithobeli imiyalelo yam baya kushiywa phantsi kolawulo lwabo lwenkohlakalo nolawulo lwabo abazimisele ngalo ukumcinezela ngenkohlakalo baze bamcime ngokupheleleyo uAdam ebusweni bomhlaba; kumacebo abo ekudala bewaceba abazimisele ngawo ukuba umhlaba ezandleni zam ezinamandla, kwanamacebo angenakwenzeka ukuba bawafumane kubugeza babo bokuvukela.

Noko ke, uluntu luhlala luhamba ngabom lusiya ngqo emgibeni wabo, kwananjengoko echaza imiqondiso ayinikiweyo yesigwebo sakhe esizayo asafuniyo ukukholelwa ukuba siyeza kwiingqiqo zakhe zokungakholwa ezibonelelwe yimfundo yakhe yobuxoki apho ingqondo yakhe imfanyekiswe ngokupheleleyo kumhlaba wokwenene ahlala kuwo, kwanawuphi umhlaba owadalwa nguYahweh Omkhulu Ndinguye, obukho bakhe uluntu lufuna ukubuphika kwimfundo yakhe yobuxoki eyilwe ngamaJesuit yokufunda ngokuchasene nokufunda. Ukuba xa ndinixelela izinto zasemhlabeni nize ningakholwa, ningathini na ukukholwa, ukuba ndithe ndanixelela ngezinto zasezulwini? Kuba akukho mntu unyukela ezulwini, ngaphandle kwalowo wehlayo evela ezulwini, kwanaye uNyana woMntu osezulwini. Niya kuzithoba na iintliziyo zenu nize nikholwe ngamazwi oNyana woMntu? Okanye niya kubambelela kwizithixo zentliziyo yenu nakubuxoki bezidenge ezifundileyo ezithetha ngokuchasene ngqo namazwi anikwe nguYahweh ebusweni bukaYesu Krestu?

Kwiingqiqo zoluntu zokungakholwa, ngobudenge lufuna ukunika iimpendulo zobuxoki kwimiqondiso nemimangaliso yobuxoki yabawileyo.
