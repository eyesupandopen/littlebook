Umhlaba NgoweNkosi!

Umhlaba yoweNkosi nenzaliseko yawo: Nangona umxhagi nesela libeke ibango lalo, linokwenza kuphela oko uBawo akunikezele esandleni salo ukuba likufezekise kwicebo lakhe elingcwele lokuhlangula uluntu. Musani ukumoyika! Kumele aqhubeke okwethutyana, njengoko ilixa lakhe lifikile; kodwa musani ukumoyika, nina malungisa am, abathembekileyo bam; musani ukumoyika! Andikuniki na uloyiso phezu kwakhe? Imbandezelo kaYakobi ayiyeyabam abathembekileyo, kwaye abo beze kum ngamaxesha okuhlaziya baya kuphakanyiswa kakhulu kwigumbi kanye letroni kaThixo. Ewe, bantu bam abathembekileyo, umvuzo omkhulu niwuqwebelene ngenxa yokuthembeka kwenu enikubonise ngokuthembekileyo kum.

Ninenze ookumkani nababingeleli kum eBukumkanini bam obuzayo naphambi koBawo wethu, ngoko ke yimani niqine, bantwana bam, kule mizuzu yokugqibela yeyure yokugqibela. Ukubonakala kwam kusondele, kwaye ukuhlangulwa kwenu kusondele njengokuphefumla kwenu okulandelayo, ngoko ke yimani nithembekile phambi kwam, nisazi ukuba ndingamandla enu nosindiso lwenu kwaye nitywiniwe kum, njengoko ndanithenga ngegazi lam njengoko ndanikela ngobomi bam ngenxa yenu ukuze nixolelaniswe eluthandweni noBawo wethu osezulwini. Ndiyawulangazelela loo mhla, noBawo wethu uyawulangazelela loo mhla, xa niya kuma phambi kwakhe kwimizimba endinayo ngenxa yenu ekuhlangulweni kwenu okupheleleyo, kwaye akusayi kubakho siqalekiso kubanyulwa bam.

Owu, mntu, ungubani na wena ukuba ubeke ibango kwilifa lam? Akwazi na ukuba ndawuthenga umhlaba nako konke okukuwo ngegazi lam elingenasono? Umhlaba yoweNkosi nenzaliseko yawo, kwaye ubulungisa buyeza emhlabeni! Nangona unokutshabalalisa umhlaba uwenze indawo eyintlango yamanyundululu akho anyanyekayo, nangona unokufungela utshaba lwam lwanaphakade kwaye ufune intuthumbo yakho ngenxa yenzuzo yexeshana esisiseko, awuqondi ukuba ulwa nemiphefumlo yakho njengoko uziqwebela ingqumbo ngomhla wengqumbo, xa ndiya kuvuza umntu ngamnye ngokwemisebenzi yakhe.

Owu, mntu osisidenge, uthi kuwe oothixo bakho baya kukusindisa, xa bengenamvuzo wakho, kwaye ukhohlisiwe kwaye ukhohlisa abanye kwiinkohliso zakho. Oothixo bakho belitye abanakuthetha; izithixo zakho ezintliziyweni zakho azinakukuva; azinakukusindisa, wena mnquli-zithixo! Bekuya kuba ngcono kuwe ukuba ubungazalwanga, kuba ingqumbo kaThixo ihleli phezu kwakho ngenxa yeendlela zakho ezilukhuni. Nangona umlomo wakho usuke waba lingcwaba elivulekileyo, uthetha izinto ezinkulu ngokuchasene neNkosi yeeNkosi, amazwi akho aya kubuyela kuwe, kwaye iminqweno yakho emibi iya kujikwa phezu kwakho kwisohlwayo sakho esizayo sanaphakade. Owu, ukuba ubungajikela kum wamkele usindiso kwimeko yakho yangoku yokuwa. Owu, ukuba ubungavuma nje igama lam ngemilebe yakho exokayo. Kodwa hayi, uthande ubuxoki bakho, kwaye uthande iinkohliso zakho, kwaye uthengise imiphefumlo yakho kwisigwebo sanaphakade ngeqhekeza lesonka elingenakukuxhasa; kuba ndingumsonka wobomi! Intlekele yakho iya kuza phezu kwakho ngequbuliso kwaye ngaphandle konyango. Ndiza kulukrazula uhlanga lwakho; ndiza kulutshabalalisa urhwebo noqoqosho lwakho; ngomzuzwana ndiza kubuhlwitha ubutyebi bakho kuwe; ngenene, ndiza kukuhluba lonke ulonwabo kuwe ngomzuzwana, kwaye ubuhlwempu nosizi lwakho luya kukudla. Ingqumbo yeMvana iyasondela, xa impindezelo yam uBawo andinike yona iya kwenziwa, kuba ndifanelekile ukuvula iincwadi nokukhulula amatywina.

Ucinge ngempazamo ukuba ukuzeka kade umsindo kwam nomonde wam bubuthathaka nokungakhathali. Uthe ezintliziyweni zenu: Iphi na iNkosi? Yintsomi nje yamandulo. Sifunde okuninzi ngaphezu kweendlela zosindiso zeNkosi; kulwazi lwam, ndingcono, kuba iitekhnoloji zam zinamandla ngaphezu kwencwadi endala yokuphikisana neentsomi. Ngenene, uninzi lwenu alukholelwa nokuba ndakha ndafika kwasekuqaleni ndaza ndaphalaza igazi lam elihlangulayo ngenxa yezono nosindiso loluntu. Kungcono ukuba ubungazalwanga, kuba ingqumbo oyiqwebileyo ngokuchasene nomphefumlo wakho iya kuza phezu kwakho ngaphandle konyango, kwaye uya kuzazi kakuhle izono zakho, kwaye usizi lwakho olupheleleyo nentlango iya kuba liqabane lakho. Uya kudla ubumdaka bakho, kwaye iindlela zakho ezimdaka ziya kuhlala nawe, njengoko ungcwatywe kwiziqhamo zengqumbo kwizenzo zakho ezibi ozenzileyo ngobubi obukhulu ngokuchasene negama lam. Mkani kum; andinazi!

Ngubani na phakathi kwenu othetha ngobuxoki egameni lam? Uthi uyandazi, undimele; abanye benu bacinga nokuba ningandiyalela ukuba ndenze umyalelo wenu kanye kwaye nizizisele inzuzo yezinto eziphathekayo ngemisebenzi yenu elungileyo, engeyonto ngaphandle kwamadlavu amdaka. Ungathetha njani egameni lam xa ungandazi? Ungathetha njani egameni lam xa ndingakunikanga amazwi? Ungathetha njani egameni lam ebantwini bam xa ndingakuthumanga? Hayi, nizithumele nina, nithengisa iimpahla zenu ezonakeleyo ngenxa yenzuzo yokubawa esisiseko nangenxa yokunconywa ngabantu enikunqwenela kakhulu. Sidenge! Mkani kum, njengoko ndingazanga ndinazi!

Nina baprofeti bobuxoki, enithi ndiyiNkosi ngegama kuphela; nina baprofeti bobuxoki, enithetha ubuxoki obukhulu ngokuchasene nemiphefumlo yenu; niya kutshabalala ngokupheleleyo, kwaye ndiya kunihlanza emlonyeni wam. Ngoko ke, guqukani! Buyelani kum kwaye nam ndiya kubuyela kuni. Phumani phakathi kwezithixo zenu kwaye nimke kumahlelo enu aqalekisiweyo nize kum nindenze iNkosi yenu! Owu, ukuba ubungalamkela usindiso lwam; kodwa uyaqhubeka ezonweni zakho kwaye uzithethelele ityala lakho njengoko ucaphula iLizwi lam elingcwele ngentliziyo engenanto, intliziyo ezele kukrexeza nombulo njengoko ubalekela kwizithixo zakho. Ngubani na oya kukusindisa kwingqumbo yam xa ulahlekise abantu bam ngenxa yenzuzo yakho esisiseko? Ukuba ubundenzile iNkosi njengoko imilomo yakho exokayo ibhengeze, ngendikusindisile ezonweni zakho ndakuphilisa kum. Ngendizaluse iintlungu umtyholi azibangela kuwe. Ngendikunike indlela nenjongo ndakuthuma emhlabeni nemilomo ezele ziintsikelelo nenyaniso njengoko ubuya kukhokelela abantu bam kum.

Musani ukucinga ukuba ndifuna uncedo lwenu, njengoko ndiyiNkosi yokwenene yezulu nomhlaba, kwaye ndinguKumkani wooKumkani neNkosi yeeNkosi. Abo ndibamisele ebulungiselelweni banikwe iwonga elikhulu lokuthetha egameni lam nokunyamekela umhlambi wam, iwonga endiya kulivuza ngobuncwane obungunaphakade; kodwa, nindananise ngoguqulelo lobuxoki lukaKristu. Omnye uYesu nizenzele nina; niwuthandile umoya womchasi-Kristu, kwaye nonile ngonaphakade ngokuchasene nemiphefumlo yenu ngokubhengeza ukuba andikho kwiBandla lam endalithenga ngegazi lam, isono eningayi kuphila kuso. Ngenene, zonke ezi zinto nangaphezulu ziya kuza phezu kwenu kwingqumbo yam ngaphandle kokuba nizithobe phambi kwam kwaye nindenze iNkosi ngokwenene ngokuguquka ngengubo erhwexayo nothuthu. Musani ukuwathethelela amazwi am njengoko nidlala ngentsingiselo yentetho yam! Guqukani! Hlalani engutyeni erhwexayo nothuthu nililele ubugwenxa benu, kwaye ndiya kunamkela kum ndinibuyisele kumvuzo wenu endiya kuthi ngenye indlela ndiwunike omnye.

NdinguYehova olilungisa.

Ndingumsindisi wehlabathi, ndikwa nguKumkani wooKumkani neNkosi yeeNkosi.

Ndim ohamba phakathi kweziphatho zezibane ezisixhenxe noyalela izithunywa zezulu ezisixhenxe zamabandla asixhenxe.

Ndim imvana eyaxhelwayo kwasekusekweni kwehlabathi, kwaye kum kukho ubomi obungunaphakade.

Ndiphethe izitshixo zokufa nezesihogo, kwaye uBawo wabelane ngesihlalo sakhe sobukhosi nam, ukuze nam ndabelane ngesihlalo sam sobukhosi nabam abathembekileyo, ababiziweyo, nabanyuliweyo. Ndihamba nabantu bam kwaye ndithetha nabantu bam, kwaye ndingumalusi wokwenene owalusa loo mhlambi uBawo andinike wona. Nina niluphawu lwamehlo am, nina bathembekileyo bam, kwaye niluphawu lwamehlo kaBawo wam, kwaye ngeli xesha nangele lixa nibuyiselwe ebulungiseni nasebungcweleni bokwenene.

Iyure iyasondela, kwaye kufuneka niyeke imisebenzi yenu kwaye nilindele ukubonakala kwam, kuba sele kuza kufika ehlabathini njengoko ndinibutha ezimbombeni zone zomhlaba. Inene, inene, ndiya kuzibuthela iigusha zam zokwenene esitalini sam phambi kokuba kufike izaqhwithi eziya kutshabalalisa eli hlabathi likhohlakeleyo langoku, kuba ubukumkani bukaSathana buya kukrazulwa butshatyalaliswe ngokupheleleyo ukuze kuvuleke indlela yeNkosi elungileyo eya kulawula umhlaba kwisihlalo sobukhosi sikaDavide ngobulungisa nobungcwele bokwenene, kwaye ndiya kumxhasa kakhulu umtshakazi wam kubukumkani obuzayo, kuba bangookumkani nababingeleli bam, kwaye baya kulawula ngobulungisa bokwenene nobungcwele, kuba umvuzo wam usesandleni sam ngenxa yabantu abathembekileyo.

Ndingamandla enu nosindiso lwenu, kwaye nitywiniwe kum.
