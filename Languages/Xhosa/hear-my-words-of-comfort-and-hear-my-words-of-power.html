Meyi 08, 2024

Yivani Amazwi Am Entuthuzelo Kwaye Nive Amazwi Am Anamandla!

Yivani amazwi eNkosi, kwanoamazwi kaYeshua HaMashiach. Yizani kum, Mtshakazi wam; yizani kum nikulungele, kuba ndiyeza kunilandela. Phumulani kum, bantwanana bam, nihlale ezinyaweni zam. Yivani amazwi am entuthuzelo, kwaye nive amazwi am anamandla, kuba ndininikile umthuthuzeli kwaye kuloo mthuthuzeli kukho amandla amakhulu. Ndiphulaphuleni nikholwe ngamazwi am, thobani ikratshi lenu ukuze nive amazwi ezithunywa zam, kuba zithobekile entliziyweni. Andithumelanga kuni abanamandla beli hlabathi; andithumelanga kuni abanobutyebi obukhulu nodumo (nangona bezenzisa). Hayi, ndinithumele abo ihlabathi libalahlele kude ngendelelo kuba bathethela mna kwaye umoya wam ukubo kwaye awunakususwa.

Ningamangaliswa kukuba ihlabathi linithiyile, kuba ihlabathi landithiya kuqala, kwaye umkhonzi akamkhulu kunenkosi yakhe. Ukuba bandithiyile mna, nani ke banithiyile. Kodwa ningoobani na nina ukuba noyike oko umntu anokukwenza kuni? Ningoobani na nina ukuba nihlale ebumnyameni nasekoyikeni ngoxa ndinikhokelele kuBawo wam noYihlo wenu, kwakuye uThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade, kuba akukho wumbi! Mna, ewe mna, Kristu Yesu, ndinihlalise ezulwini, kwaye bonke abasezulwini balindele ngenkulu imincili ukufika kwenu okukufuphi, Mtshakazi wam.

Kuba nkqu noThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade, kwanoBawo wethu, kwanoYahweh ngokwakhe (ongunaphakade kanaphakade) ulindele ngenkulu imincili! Kuba kungekudala niza kuvela phambi kwetrone yakhe ninozuko olukhulu, kwaye kwigumbi lakhe letrone niya kuhlaliswa, kuba niya kuthatha inxaxheba kwitrone yam. Izithunywa zezulu zikulangazelela ngenkulu imincili nani, bantwana bam, kuba kukho uvuyo phambi kwazo naxa kusenzeka ukuba umoni omnye aguquke. Kuba kunjalo: Kodwa nina nize entabeni iZiyon, nakumzi kaThixo ophilayo, iYerusalem yasezulwini, nakwishumi elinengenakubalwa lezithunywa zezulu. Nkqu nezinye zezi zithunywa zezulu zingabalungiseleli kwabo baya kudla ilifa losindiso, kwaye oko kuquka nani, Mtshakazi wam. Kwaye mna, ewe mna Kristu Yesu, ndilangazelela ubukho benu phambi kwam. Kuba ndiyanithanda kwaye ndiyanilangazelela, kwaye kungekudala siza kumanyana ngonaphakade ngomtshato weMvana. NdinguMyeni olangazelela uMtshakazi wakhe, kwaye ndiyeza kunilandela! Ndinilungiselele izinto ezininzi, kwaye ubutyebi obuninzi nemivuzo yokuthembeka inilindile. Kungekudala niza kufumana ilifa lenu elipheleleyo kum, kwaye niya kuba nonaphakade wonke ukuze niqonde ubukhulu nobuhle bento endinihlanganisela yona kum ukuze niyifeze, nento endiya kunenza yona, kuba niyindalo entsha, umntu omtsha, odalwe kum! Kwaye niya kundenzela izinto ezininzi, ezininzi, kungekuphela kuBukumkani bam boBulungisa apho ndiya kulawula umhlaba ngobulungisa usuku olunye! Kodwa ngakumbi kumazulu amatsha nomhlaba omtsha apho ubukhulu benu buya kukhanya, kwaye niya kuba nam ngonaphakade. Kuba usuku olunye eNkosini lunjengeminyaka eliwaka, neminyaka eliwaka injengosuku olunye. Ndinombuzo kuni, zithandwa zam, ewe mna, Yesu Kristu, iNkosi yeeNkosi noKumkani wooKumkani! Ndiphulaphuleni niqwalasele amazwi am, kuba amazwi eNkosi ngamazwi acocekileyo: anjengesilivere evivinywe ezikweni lomhlaba, yahlanjululwa kasixhenxe. Kutheni nizijongela phantsi? Ndiyaqonda ukuba ninobulali kwaye nithobekile entliziyweni, kwaye oko kuvela kwindalo entsha endiyidalileyo ngaphakathi kwenu, kuba umntu wemvelo uhlala enekratshi. Kodwa kutheni nizibona ningenaxabiso, nizibuza ukuba kutheni uThixo angade afune ukujongana nani, kwaye kutheni ndinganinika usindiso ngoxa kukho abangcono? Kutheni nihleli apho nixinzelekile, nizigweba xa nonile? Kutheni ningazivuthululi? (Uthuli luya kuba kukutya kwenyoka.) Vukani! Sukumani! Vukani! Ewe, nonke niya kona ukusukela kusasa kude kutshone ilanga. Ngaba le yinto uYahweh awayeyijongele phantsi kulwazi lwakhe lwangaphambili? Yintoni ke ngoku? Ngaba uThixo walibala ukuba niya kona emva kosindiso? Makube lee: ewe, uThixo makabe nenyaniso, kodwa wonke umntu makabe lixoki! Kuba kwicebo lakhe lokuhlangula, nkqu namacebo akhe andiphathelele mna, nkqu nemfihlelo enkulu kaThixo, zonke ezi zinto zadityaniswa kunye ngobabalo nenceba; yamkelani nje ubabalo lwam nenceba yam, kuba uninzi lwenu luyazibetha endaweni yoko.

Bantwana bam, ndalibhatala ixabiso lezono zenu. Xa nihamba ngokuthe tye nangokunyaniseka kwaye nona ngenxa yokuba inyama yenu yonakele, vumani nje isono kum kwaye nizihlambulule egazini lam elihlangulayo, kuba mna ndiyinkululeko yenu kwaye ndinihlangule. Mabathi abakhululweyo beNkosi: abo ubakhululeyo esandleni sotshaba. Akukho nto ninokuyenza ukongeza kwimisebenzi yam egqityiweyo, kuba yintoni enye eyifunayo imisebenzi egqitywe ngokugqibeleleyo? Xa ndathi emnqamlezweni ngomphefumlo wam wokugqibela kuloo ndlu yomhlaba obomvu, ndandambethe ngelo xesha: Kugqityiwe, andishiyanga nto ingenziwanga. Kwaye xa uThixo amkela idini eligqityiweyo, eligqibeleleyo, makungabikho mntu uzama ukongeza nto; kuba leyo yimisebenzi yobuyatha, nkqu nekratshi lenu elifihlakeleyo nobuyatha benu, kuba emhlabeni kukho ubuyatha obukhulu. Amampunge apheleleyo, utsho uMtshumayeli: amampunge apheleleyo, konke kungamampunge. Kuba ubuyatha buthetha, kwaye bunixokisa, buthi anilungile ngokwaneleyo kwaye idini lam aligqibelelanga.

Ngoko ke, kufuneka nenze enye into engaphezulu ukuze ningqine ukufaneleka kwenu xa niguquka phambi kwam. Inene ndithi, iziqhamo ezifanele inguquko zamkelekile, kodwa hayi ukuzigweba, hayi ukuzithiya, hayi iingcinga zokuzeyisa zoxinzelelo. Kuba xa nizibandakanya kwezi zenzo zingenaxabiso zenyama, ngokungazi nibiza inyaniso bubuxoki kwaye nizama ukuxolelanisa inyama yenu nomoya. Bantwana bam, oko kuzelwe yinyama kuyinyama kwaye oko kuzelwe nguMoya kungumoya. Kuba inyama ikanukana noMoya, noMoya ukanukana nenyama; kwaye ezi zinto ziyachasana: ukuze ningenzi izinto eninqwenela ukuzenza. Kuba ngedini lam nixolelanisiwe nam, iNkosi yenu uYesu Kristu, kwaye ndinixolelanisile noThixo, kuba injalo imfihlelo enkulu kaThixo eniyinxalenye yayo. Kutheni niwathandabuza amazwi am? Xelelani inyama yenu ukuba ithule, kwaye nithulise amazwi achasene nemfundiso yam ephilileyo! Kuba ndinilungiselele ubukhulu. Ndinixhobisile ngomoya wam wobunyana enikhala ngawo nithi, ABHA, Bawo. Ndinixhobile ndanenza nakwazi ukundenzela izinto ezinkulu; kwaye ngaphezu kwako konke, ndinithumile kum, nkqu nakwiileta zam zothando eziya kuMtshakazi wam. Nkqu neencwadi endazibhalisa uPawulos eziya kwiBandla lam ziyayichaza le nto ngentetho ecacileyo ukuze niqonde kwaye nikholwe. Kuba ndathini kwiBandla lam laseKorinte ngesandla sikaPawulos? Ngoko ke, ukuba kukho umntu okuKristu, usisidalwa esitsha; izinto ezindala zidlule, yabona, zonke izinto zibe ntsha. Kwaye zonke izinto zivela kuThixo, osixolelanise naye ngoYesu Kristu, wasinika ubulungiseleli boxolelaniso. Bantwana bam, xa nihamba emoyeni wam kwaye nihlala nithembekile kwimiyalelo yam, ndinidala ngokutsha kum.

Kuba umntu omdala womhlaba obomvu uyatshabalala, kwaye umYuda neNtlanga ngokufanayo baya kwenziwa umntu omnye omtsha kum, nkqu nangomoya wam wobunyana, kuba ndiya kusindisa abanye kubo bonke. Ngoku, ukuba kukho nawuphi na umntu ongenamoya kaKristu, lowo ayingowakhe. Enikwathi emva kokuba nikholwe, natywinwa nguloo moya ungcwele wesithembiso, Ongumqala welifa lenu, kude kube yinkululeko yelifa elithengiweyo, ukuze kudunyiswe uzuko lwakhe. Ngoko hambani kubutsha bobomi kwaye niqonde ukuba kungekudala ilifa lam elithengiweyo liya kwamkela ngokupheleleyo (ninentlawulo yokuqala yomoya wam), kwaye niya kwambathiswa imizimba emitsha, imizimba engonakaliswanga, imizimba enamandla ngobubanzi nangokukwazi, nkqu nezindlu ezifanele ukumkani! Kuba niya kuba ngookumkani nababingeleli phambi koThixo wethu. Ngoko ndinixolelanisile noThixo, kwaye akusekho butshaba, kwaye ndininikile ubulungiseleli boxolelaniso. Owu, kodwa kutheni ndingenza into enjalo, ungabuza? Mamela ndikuxelele.

Kwaye ngoko amaKorinte ayaqhubeka nobungqina bawo: Oko kukuthi, ukuba uThixo wayekuKristu, exolelanisa ihlabathi naye, engababaleli izono zabo, kwaye usinike ilizwi loxolelaniso. Ngoku ke singoonozakuzaku bakaKristu, ngokungathi uThixo uyanicenga ngathi; siyanithandaza endaweni kaKristu, ukuba nixolelaniswe noThixo. Bantwana bam, ninoonozakuzaku bam, kwaye nibambe isikhundla esihloniphekileyo kakhulu, kuba nimele mna. Kuba ukuba ngunozakuzaku wam kukumela uKumkani wooKumkani neNkosi yeeNkosi kwihlabathi elifayo, kwaye mna ndingumbingeleli omkhulu ngonaphakade ngokohlobo lukaMelkitsedeke. Ngoko, ndicela nindive: ndinenze noonozakuzaku bam. Ndinixolelanisile noThixo uSomandla, kwanoEl Shaddai, kuba iinjongo zakhe ziya kwenziwa! Ndininikile ilizwi loxolelaniso, nkqu namazwi obuciko afanele oonozakuzaku bam, kuba iLizwi likaThixo livela ngonaphakade kanaphakade. Kwaye ndinike wonke umntu kuni ubulungiseleli boxolelaniso. Oku kuthetha ukuba ndigunyazise wonke umphefumlo ozelwe ngokutsha ngomoya wam onamandla njengabalungiseleli bam abanamandla, kwaye utshaba lunoyika kakhulu kwaye luya kubaleka kuni xa nihamba emandleni am, nangona luya kuzama ukunisebezela amazwi obuxoki kwaye lugqume njengengonyama. Kodwa umlomo walo obolileyo awunamazinyo, kuba aphulwe ngamandla ovuko lwam, kwaye aluyi kuphinda luchache. Yityesheleni loo ngonyama ingenamazinyo, kwaye nigxile kwilizwi lam, nkqu naMazwi eLizwi lam.

Kwesi sikhundla sikhulu segunya namandla (kuba nisebenza egameni lam kwaye igama lam likhulu kunawo onke amagama, kwaye nguYahweh kuphela omkhulu!) ndinibeke njengabalungiseleli noonozakuzaku bam, kwaye ndinilungisile ndanixhobisa ndanambathisa ngamandla amakhulu! Bantwana bam, aniyiqondi imiyalelo yamaZwi am, kuba abefundisi abaqeshiweyo abawexukileyo baqeshe abalungiseleli baseLawodike ukuba banixokise malunga namandla nesipho esidalwe kuni ekuzalweni okutsha. Kuba uPawulos wachaza ubukhulu bamandla anikwe emoyeni wobunyana odalwe kuni. Kuba waxelela amaKorinte: Kwezinto zokomoya, bazalwana, andithandi ukuba ningazi. Kwaye kwakhona: Kukho izipho ezahlukeneyo, inene, kodwa mnye umoya. Kwaye kwakhona: Kodwa ukubonakaliswa koMoya kunikwa wonke umntu, ukuze kungenelwe. Ningabi ngabangenalwazi kwimicimbi yokomoya; qondani umahluko phakathi kwesipho nokubonakaliswa, kuba ayingamagama afanayo kwisicatshulwa.

Ndinike isipho somoya oyingcwele, kwaye kweso sipho kukho ukubonakaliswa. Kuba lithini iLizwi lam: Kodwa ukubonakaliswa komoya, kunikwa wonke umntu ukuze kungenelwe.

Abalungiseleli benu baseLawodike abadididizi baphindaphinde ubuxoki uSathana abuxelele kuyo yonke iminyaka, ubuxoki bokunenza nikholwe ukuba aninamandla, nobuxoki bokuzama ukufihla amandla enu okwenene kum, kuba umoya wenu nguKristu kuni, ithemba lozuko! Ngoko ke ukubonakaliswa kwam komoya kudweliswe ngolu hlobo: ilizwi lobulumko, ilizwi lolwazi, ukholo, izipho zokuphilisa, ukusebenza kwemimangaliso, isiprofeto, ukwahlula oomoya, iintlobo ngeentlobo zeelwimi, ukutolikwa kweelwimi. Phambi kokuba siqhubeke, bekani phantsi iincwadi zenu zehlabathi zokuchaza, nezichazi-magama zenu zehlabathi, neekhonkodensi zenu zehlabathi, kwaye niyeke ukuba zikhohlise ukuze zichaze ngokutsha isibhalo ngeenjongo zikaSathana.

Kuyo yonke imimoya kuye wonke umntu kukho la mandla okubonakalisa amandla avela phezulu! Kodwa zonke ezi zinto zisebenza nguloo moya mnye, wabela umntu ngamnye ngokwahlukeneyo njengoko ethanda. Bantwana bam, kutheni nicinga ukuba ezi zizipho ngoxa isibhalo sisithi zikukubonakaliswa? Banixokisile; fundani amazwi am nibe nokholo kumazwi abaguquleli bam abawakhethileyo, kuba ndandinabo. Ninazo zonke ezi zikhono, kwaye ukuba niyafuna ukuzibonakalisa, ndiya kuzisebenza kuni ukuze ndikhulule abathinjwa ekubanjweni kukaSathana ndibase ekukhanyeni okuzukileyo konaphakade. Ningamhoyi nabani na othetha ngokuchaseneyo nala mazwi, kwaye nizifundise eLizwini lam. Yekani ukukhathazeka malunga nokuba ezi zinto zisebenza njani, kuba azisebenzi ngamazwi obuciko neengcinga ezonwabileyo. Zisebenza ngokwemfuno, ngomnqweno womntu omele abe nentando yokwenza intando yam, kwaye ngakumbi ngokholo kum. Kuba ndiya kwenza imisebenzi emikhulu ngezandla zenu, kwaye ndinithumile, mtshakazi wam, kuba esi sisigunyaziso sokugqibela.

Ndinike isipho somoya oyingcwele, kwaye kweso sipho kukho nezinye izipho ezininzi, kuba bathini abase-Efese: Kodwa kumntu ngamnye kuthi kunikwe ubabalo, ngokomlinganiselo wesipho sikaKristu. Kungoko athi: Xa wenyukela phezulu, wathimba ukuthinjwa, wanika izipho ebantwini. Kwaye kwakhona kwabaseRoma: Kuba ndithi, ngobabalo endilunikiweyo, kumntu ngamnye ophakathi kwenu, ukuba angazicingeli ngaphezu koko afanele ukucinga ngako, kodwa acinge ngokuphangaleleyo, njengoko uThixo abele umntu ngamnye umlinganiselo wokholo. Ngoko qondani ukuba nifanele nizicingele phezulu kuba nidalwe ngokutsha ngobulungisa nobungcwele bokwenene nkqu nokubonisa uzuko lukaThixo ngokwakhe, nkqu noYise wezikhanyiso, kuba nina nizizikhanyiso zehlabathi. Kodwa cinganani ngokuphangaleleyo, kuba anibakhulu kunabazalwana noodade wenu abazelwe ngomoya ofanayo nani: Kuba nonke ninazo ezi zikhono, kwaye nonke ninezi zipho.

Kuba ndinike izipho ebantwini: Kwaye ndanika abanye abapostile; nabanye abaprofeti; nabanye abavangeli; nabanye abefundisi, nabafundisi: Ukulungiselela abangcwele, umsebenzi wobulungiseleli, ukwakha umzimba kaKristu. Zininzi iinkonzo endizinike uMtshakazi wam, kwaye zezakho ukuba uyanqwenela. Celani kum kwaye ndiya kuzisebenza ezi nkonzo kuni, kodwa kufuneka nibe nomnqweno. Ngoko ke zilangazeleleni ngenkuthalo ezona zipho zibalaseleyo: Kwaye ndiya kunibonisa indlela egqwesileyo. Loo ndlela igqwesileyo, bantwana bam, luthando lukaThixo olungenakoyiswa. Nifanele nilangazelele ngenkuthalo ezona zipho zibalaseleyo, kuba ndinixhobisile. Kodwa ukukhonza ngenxa yothando lukaThixo, ukunikela imibingelelo yemilebe yenu nendumiso yentliziyo yenu ethembekileyo eluthandweni, kungaphezulu. Yiphani ngesisa njengoko namkele ngesisa. Hambani kula mandla kuba niyamthanda uThixo kwaye niyamthanda mna, uYesu Kristu, kwaye niphane ngako konke enikunikiweyo, kwaye nihambe, ningathandabuzi nto.

Mtshakazi wam, ngoko ndinithumile ukuba nigqibe le misebenzi, kwaye kungekudala iikhosi zenu ziya kugqitywa, kwaye xa ndinihlanganisela kum emafini, niya kwazi, kanye njengoko ndandisazi emnqamlezweni, kwaye niya kukwazi ukuthi (njengoko ndakha ndatsho): Kugqityiwe. Kungekudala niya kungena kulonwabo lweNkosi yenu!

Ndiyeza kunilandela kungekudala.

Ndilangazelela ubukho benu phambi kwam.

NdinguKristu Yesu kwaye ndinidale ngokutsha kum.

Ndim lowo uninike ubulungiseleli namazwi oxolelaniso.

Ndim lowo unithumele njengoonozakuzaku bam abathembekileyo.

Ndinguye endinguye, ngoko baxelele ukuba uNdinguye unithumile.
