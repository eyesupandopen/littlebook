Ukuhlangana Kwabathembekileyo KuKristu!

Yabonani, Yabonani! Qondani into eza kukhululwa emhlabeni.

Yabonani, Yabonani! Ngokuqinisekileyo ingqumbo yemvana iphezu kwakho Bhabhiloni eyimfihlelo. Kwaye njengoko mna, Yeshua HaMashiach, ndiye ndalumkisa, kuya kwenziwa ngokunjalo!

Yabonani! Yabonani! Usuku luphezu kwakho Bhabhiloni eyimfihlelo kwaye usuku lwakho lomgwebo lufikile, usuku oluya kukhulula olo suku lukhulu lwathenjiswa mandulo, usuku lweNkosi! Yeha kuni bemi bomhlaba kuba ingqumbo enkulu iza kukhululwa ngokupheleleyo phezu kwenu, kwaye aniyi kusinda kwaye ngenene, ngenene iveki yamashumi asixhenxe kaDaniyeli ifikile.

Ngoko ke ndiyaluleka ibandla lam, umzimba wam kwanomtshakazi wam ukuba nizibhinqise ngobulungisa bam kwaye nizilungiselele ukuma nithe nkqo phambi kweNkosi yenu, kanye kwigumbi letrone likaYahweh, apho ndiya kunikela khona iziqhamo zokuqala, bonke abo ndiya kubahlanganisa abalele kuKristu kwaye baphumle belindele ubizo lwam, kwanabo bonke abaphilayo emhlabeni abathembekileyo kuKristu ngexesha lokubonakala kwam. Kwaye ngendlela efanayo endazinikela ngayo kuYahweh njengeziqhamo zokuqala kwabafileyo, ngendlela enjalo nani nichongiwe neNkosi yenu engunaPhakade yoBulungisa. Kuba ngendlela efanayo endanyuswa ngayo ndaya emazulwini, nani niya kunyuswa niye emazulwini, apho ndiya kunikela khona iziqhamo zam zokuqala zesivuno ezingenabala okanye umbimbi okanye nayiphi na into enjalo.

Kwaye nonke niya kuma phambi kwesihlalo sam sokugweba, eso sihlalo sikhulu sokugweba apho imisebenzi yenu iya kugwetywa kwaye abanye baya kuvuzwa, kwaye abanye baya kulahlekelwa kwaye abanye baya kufumana ilahleko nomvuzo njengoko iinkuni, ifula, neendiza zitshiswa kuba akukho namnye kuni oye wahamba ngokugqibeleleyo phambi kwam, kodwa eluthandweni lukaYahweh niya kumangaliswa kakhulu bubukhulu belifa lenu. Kwaye kuboyisi bam umvuzo wenu mkhulu kwaye niya kudla ilifa lezinto zonke njengam xa ndoyisa abawileyo. Nangona kunjalo, bonke abahlanganiselwe kwiNkosi yabo engunaPhakade, uYeshua HaMashiach, baya kufikelela kubutsha bobomi njengoko bedalwa ngokutsha ngonaphakade kwaye anisoze nonakaliswe okanye nitshintshe kugqibelelo olunilindeleyo, kuba uYahweh utshilo!

Kwaye ngequbuliso niya kuthathwa, kwaye ihlabathi liya kwazi ukuba ndibonakele, kwaye umthinteli uya kususwa! Kwaye ekungabikho komthinteli wam wonke umkhosi nempembelelo yobubi iya kukhululwa ngokupheleleyo emhlabeni. Kwaye ndim, uYeshua HaMashiach, uKristu Yesu, ovakalisa la Mazwi kwaye ngoko la Mazwi aya kwaziwa emhlabeni, kwaye aya kufumana amehlo, iindlebe, neentliziyo zabo athunyelwe kubo.

Kwaye kunjalo, kwaye kuya kuba njalo, kodwa kuphela ixesha elifutshane kakhulu ubukumkani beNamba buya kuvuka ngokupheleleyo, kuba kunikwe bona ukuba bakufezekise oko. Kuba xa ndaliwa zizihlobo zam, uSirayeli endiligazi lakhe, ndajikela kwiintlanga ukuze iintlanga zisikelelwe, njengoko uYahweh wathembisa mandulo. Kwaye aMazwi am amkelwa ngamandla, naphakathi kwentshutshiso enkulu novavanyo oluninzi, kwaye amasango esihogo akazange aloyise iBandla lam. Kodwa, nangona kunjalo njengoko iBandla lam lisuswa, nomthinteli wam esuswa ububi buya kulawula ngokupheleleyo umhlaba, kwaye abayi kusinda.

Kwanabo bonke abanoyolo kokungalunganga nabazimisele ukumala uYesu waseNazarete endaweni kaBharabhas kwaye xa besithi uxolo nokhuseleko, kuya kufika intshabalalo ngequbuliso, kwaye abayi kusinda. Kwaye iNamba iya kuzityhila, kwaye ibotshelelwe emhlabeni kwaye emazulwini igxothiwe ngonaphakade ayisayi kuphinda ibuye, kwaye inengqumbo enkulu kuba iyazi ukuba ixesha layo lifutshane, kodwa ngobudenge icinga ukuba isenokundoyisa mna Yeshua HaMashiach, nangona ndayoyisa ngokupheleleyo eKalvari kwimisebenzi yam yokucamagushela usindiso eyaphela ngoKugqityiwe!

Kwaye abawileyo ngandlel' ithile bacinga ukuba banokusinda kumgwebo wabo othenjiswe kudala kuba boyika ikamva labo ngaphezu kwayo nayiphi na into; kuba bayaqonda ukuba kwinkohlakalo yabo ababonakalisanga kwaye ababonakalisi nceba. Kuba bayazi ukuba ababonakalisanga nceba, ngoko ke, akukho nceba iya kuboniswa kubo. Bayazi kakuhle ukuba zonke izenzo zabo ezingalunganga nezicekisekayo ziya kunikwa bona ngonaphakade izihlandlo ezininzi kwelo chibi likhulu lomlilo wesulfure apho amadangatye esohlwayo angenakuze acinywe. Kwanamadangatye adalelwe ukubuyisela wonke umsebenzi ombi abawenzileyo ezifubeni zabo, kwaye baya kuhlawula ngokupheleleyo kwaye baya kuba sentlungwini engunaphakade.

Kuba bahlasela uYahweh kwaye bahlasela umyezo kaThixo, kanye njengoko babezenza oothixo kwaye umgwebo wabo ufikile. Kodwa kunjalo, abawileyo balalise ubuthongo izihlwele ngeteknoloji yabo, namaza abo, nangeziyolo zesono, nomvuzo wokungalunganga. Kodwa, kunjalo ukuba abantu bathanda ubumnyama kunokukhanya, ngoko ngoku, ndiya kubanika ubumnyama ababunqwenela kangaka.

Kuba lonke uyolo luza kususwa emhlabeni kwaye ubomi besono nobutyebi obugqithisileyo abuyi kuphinda bubonwe emhlabeni ngendlela efanayo apho abantu bazifumbela ubutyebi baze balahle ummelwane wabo ekubaweni kwabo nasekungabini nabulungisa, bade bajongele phantsi abo basweleyo njengoko benyathela phezu kwabo endleleni yabo yokuqokelela ubutyebi obuninzi. Umntu wemvelo uzinze kakhulu kwiziyolo zesono kangangokuba akayi kuphakamisa nomnwe ukunceda abo basweleyo, ngoko ke nam andiyi kuphakamisa mnwe ukumnceda asinde kumgwebo wakhe kuba umntu wemvelo ngoku unikelwe ezandleni zeentshaba zakhe. Kwaye kuya kuba njalo ukuba ezi zinto aziyi kusinda kwiveki yamashumi asixhenxe kaDaniyeli kwaye aziyi kuphinda zenzeke ngendlela enjalo ebukumkanini bam bobulungisa!

Kodwa kuya kuba njalo ukuba xa umthinteli esusiwe ububi obufihlakeleyo eluntwini ngenxa yamaqhinga enyoka ngoku buya kuhamba bungathintelwanga kwaye ubuphambano buya kulawula umhlaba njengoko umzalwana eya kubulala umzalwana kwaye abantu belizwe baya kulwa nabantu belizwe labo kwaye isiphithiphithi esigqithisileyo nemfazwe ziya kulawula umhlaba. Kodwa esi siganeko siyintshabalalo nomgwebo weBhabhiloni eyimfihlelo, kwaye amatywina alungele ukukhululwa, kwaye abakhweli-mahashe baya kukhwela!

Kwaye ngoko ke ukuwa okukhulu kuyenzeka njengoko kwaxelwa kwangaphambili. Kuba ngexesha lokubonakala kwam, xa abaninzi bekufanele ukuba babelindile kwaye bezihlambulula eLizwini lam nasemoyeni wam, kwaye endaweni yokugqibezela usindiso lwabo ezingalweni zam zothando, endaweni yoko bayilahla iNkosi yabo, uKristu Yesu, bekhetha uBharabhas abanikele kuye iintliziyo zabo. Kwaye kanye njengoPontiyo Pilato, owayezimisele ukundikhulula kubuxoki babo bobuFarisi obungenasiseko nezityholo, wanika abantu belizwe ukhetho. Waza wathi uPilato kubo xa babehlanganisene, nokuba nithanda ukuba ndinikhululele bani na, uBharabhas, okanye uYesu ekuthiwa nguKristu? Bathi, uBharabhas. Wathi uPilato kubo, Ndenze ntoni ke ngoYesu, ekuthiwa nguKristu? Bathi bonke kuye, makabethelelwe emnqamlezweni.

Kwaye oku kwakunjalo kuba uEsawu wasebenzisa impembelelo yakhe embi phezu kwabantu ukuze bazithathele umbulali endaweni yalowo wayethunyelwe usindiso lwabo, uYesu waseNazarete! Kodwa sasiya kuzalisekiswa njani isohlwayo somthetho kaMoses ngaphandle kokuba ndizincame kwintlungu ekrakra njengemvana kaThixo, apho ndazinikela ngenxa yezono zehlabathi njengombingeleli omkhulu ngonaphakade ngokohlobo lukaMelkitsedeke. Nangona kunjalo, kulwazi lwangaphambili lukaYahweh wayesazi ngokupheleleyo ukuba uSirayeli uya kumala uMesiya wakhe wokwenene kwaye ngoko wasebenzisa intiyo nomona wabawileyo ngokuchasene nabo, kuba ukuba babeyazi imfihlelo enkulu kaThixo ngebengazange bayibethelele emnqamlezweni iNkosi yenu engunaPhakade yoZuko!

Kodwa kunjalo, ukuba uMelkitsedeke uKumkani waseSalem wakhupha isonka newayini: kwaye wayenguMbingeleli kaThixo Osenyangweni. Kwaye ngoko uAbram wenza iminikelo yakhe njengoko kufuneka ukuhlonipha uYahweh kwaye ngoko uMelkitsedeke luhlobo lokholo olwalusisithunzi sezinto ezizayo. Kuba njengoko kubhaliwe ngoAbraham othembekileyo owayephila ngokholo kuThixo Osenyangweni kwaye ngomzekelo wakhe olilungisa uya kuphila ngokholo ngonaphakade! Kwaye ngoko ke iintlanga zisikelelwe kwaye zibandakanyiwe kwizizwe phakathi koSirayeli kaThixo, kwaye ziyathatha inxaxheba kwizithembiso ezenziwa kooBawo ngokupheleleyo kuba zamkelwe nguYahweh ngokwakhe ngesandla sam esinamandla, ngoko ke oko uThixo akuhlambululeyo musa ukukubiza ngokuba yinto eqhelekileyo okanye engcolileyo, Sirayeli!

Kodwa kunjalo ukuba usuku lwam lufikile. Kwaye andiyi kwabelana ngozuko lwam nomnye, kwaye uThixo uthumele inkohliso enamandla ukuze bakholelwe kubuxoki. Kuba abo balandela uBharabhas benza njalo ngenxa yeenkanuko zabo kuba bazicinga beyinxalenye yexesha lakhe legolide elithenjisiweyo elingenzelwanga bona, nangona exoka. Kuba ixesha legolide alithembisayo lisekelwe kwixesha legolide labawileyo ngemihla kaNowa apho abawileyo babelawula umhlaba ngokungalunganga! Ngoko ke, xa uBharabhas ethetha ngexesha legolide uthetha ngenene ngenjongo yeentliziyo zabo, ngaphandle kokuba bafuna ngokupheleleyo intshabalalo epheleleyo ka-Adam ukuze amanyala abo nomdiza wabo uthathe umyezo kaThixo ngonaphakade. Kwaye kunjalo, ngoku niyaqonda ukuba kutheni kungekho nyama ibiya kusindiswa iphila ukuba uYahweh ebengayinqumlanga imihla.

Kodwa abayi kuba nalo ixesha legolide kuba ndiyimbewu ethenjisiweyo yomfazi eya kuyityumza ngokupheleleyo intloko yenyoka ngonaphakade kwaye ndiya kubutyumza ngokupheleleyo bonke ubukumkani bayo kwaye abayi kuma. Ngoko endaweni yexesha labo legolide elinqwenelwayo, baya kuba nomgwebo ongqongqo kwaye ubukumkani babo buya kutshatyalaliswa ngokupheleleyo phambi kwabo bungaphinde buvuke, kwaye amatywina aya kukhululwa, kwaye amaxilongo aya kuvuthelwa, kwaye intsalela yam iya kufihlwa kwaye ke izitya ezinengqumbo epheleleyo kaThixo ziya kuthululwa, kwaye baya kuba sentlungwini enkulu nasentlungwini njengoko bebona ukoyiswa kwabo kusondela ngokupheleleyo.

Ngoko ke, abo balandela uBharabhas ngobudenge njengoko esenza ngobudenge ukuba uthunywe nguYahweh, esazi kakuhle ukuba ungowenamba endaweni yoko, kwaye ukhethelwe ukukhokela ilizwe kwanelizwe lonke nerhamncwa eliphuma elwandle. Kodwa kunjalo, abaninzi abawileyo bangena ezingalweni zalo mkhohlisi baza bamlahla uKristu Yesu ngoku baya kunikelwa ezandleni zakhe njengoko abathembekileyo bethathwa. Kwaye kukho abo bangandityhola ngokulahla iBandla lam kanti ngabo abangekho kuKristu, abangazange bafikelele kusindiso olupheleleyo abandilahlileyo ngokuzimanya noBharabhas, kwaye ndabaxwayisa ukuba mabahlale emdiliyeni kuba ngaphandle kwam, aninakwenza nto.

Nangona abaninzi baya kutshabalala amagama abo angazange abhalwe eNcwadini yoBomi yeMvana kwaye baya kutshabalala emhlabeni xa umgwebo wabo ufika, kwaye ngequbuliso amathemba abo aya kuphela ngokuphanyaza kweliso.

Kuba babengenamathemba obulungisa!

Kuba babengenamathemba okugwetyelwa!

Kuba babengenamathemba okungcwaliswa!

Kuba balahlekelwa lulo lonke ithemba lokubuya kweNkosi yabo kubo njengoko ndathembisa kwiTestamente yam eNtsha kwaye sonke isithembiso sikaYahweh noYeshua HaMashiach siya kuzalisekiswa ngonaphakade kanaphakade!

Kuba ixesha lam lobabalo nenceba lifikelele esiphelweni, nangona ubabalo nenceba zikunye nam ngonaphakade, kwaye njengoko kubhaliwe kuya kwenziwa ukuba bobabini uYahweh noYeshua HaMashiach baya kuba nenceba kwabo siya kuba nenceba kubo kuba ngubani onokuma ngokuchasene netrone kaYahweh kuba unguThixo Osenyangweni kwaye akukho bani unjengaye! Ngoko qondani ukuba ingqumbo ilungisiwe kwaye iza kukhululwa emhlabeni kanye njengoko iinimba zam zokuzala zibhengeze kwaye zaxela kwangaphambili, kwaye olo suku lufikile!

Kwaye ngoko ndibhengeza ukubonakala kwam, kwaye kuya kwenzeka kanye njengoko kubhaliwe esibhalweni kwaye umtshakazi wam uya kumanywa ngonaphakade emnqophisweni nomyeni wakhe kwaye bonke abathathwe baya emazulwini ngexesha lokubonakala kwam, nokuba baphilile okanye bafile, baya kudalwa ngokutsha ngonaphakade kubutsha obungunaphakade bobomi. Kwaye bonke abo bazinikele ngokwenene kumazwi alungileyo neentetho ezintle zikaBharabhas baya kuwa ekuhlanganyeleni kwam kunye njengamagqabi abunile esonweni. Kanti ke, abaninzi abawayo baya kuza kum ngeemvavanyo ezinkulu nembandezelo kwaye uninzi lwabo luya kunqula igama lam kude kube sekupheleni kwaye isihlwele sezihlwele siya kuphuma kwiveki yamashumi asixhenxe kaDaniyeli singene kubomi obungunaphakade, nangona uninzi luya kuthatha uphawu lwerhamncwa luze luwele emalangatyeni ngonaphakade.

Ngoko ke, mna, Kristu Yesu, iNkosi yenu evukileyo yanyuka, ndiyayalela abathembekileyo bam ukuba baphume ngokupheleleyo phakathi kwabo kwaye bahlukane nabo ngokupheleleyo. Zilungiseleleni zithandwa zam! Ningathathi nxaxheba kuqhanqalazo lwabo, niphephe izihlwele zabo, kwaye ngazo zonke iindleko niphephe ububi obuzihlanganisayo phantsi komoya wobuxoki woxolo kuba uhlaselo lwabo ngokuqinisekileyo lulungile. Qondani zithandwa zam ukuba niya kube ningena emgibeni wabo ukuba beningazimanya nabo ngolo hlobo. Zithandwa zam, lixesha lokulahla iinkathazo zeli hlabathi, kuba ubomi benu bufihliwe kum, uKristu Yesu, kwaye buza kutyhilwa ngokupheleleyo ukuze nazi njengoko nani naziwa.

Ngoko qondani inkohliso ephezu kwenu zithandwa zam kwaye nizifihle nilinde ukubonakala kwam, kuba kusondele! Qondani inkohliso yabo, ukuba imvana eneempondo ezimbini iya kwenza ngathi inenxeba entloko, kwaye iya kubonakala ivuka kwabafileyo kwaye iphilisiwe kwinxeba layo lentloko kodwa konke ekunikezelayo njengesiqinisekiso samabango ayo obuxoki okuba ingumMoshiach wabo yimiqondiso yobuxoki nemimangaliso kwaye akukho noYahweh Omkhulu Ndinguye, nam, uYeshua HaMashiach, esimthumileyo lo mkhohlisi, nangona evunyelwe ukuba eze ngexesha lakhe elimisiweyo ukuze alawule nerhamncwa eliphuma elwandle nenamba njengentloko yabo kuba kunikwe bona ukuba bakufezekise oko kwicebo likaYahweh elingunaphakade lokuhlangula uAdam!

Kwaye ngoko ndithi kuni zithandwa zam lindani ngomonde kwaye ningakholelwa kubuxoki babo kuba akukho mntu okanye nto inokumisa ukubonakala kwam kwabathembekileyo eBandleni lam, nangona baya kufuna ukusigquma esi siganeko ngohlaselo lobuxoki lwasemzini nemfazwe yehlabathi eya kugubungela zonke iimfazwe ezakha zeza ngaphambili emhlabeni. Kodwa, imfazwe yabo iya kuba buthathaka xa ithelekiswa nedabi lolo suku lukhulu apho baya kutshatyalaliswa ngokupheleleyo xa ndifika emhlabeni nemikhosi yasezulwini, kuba ngobulungisa ndiza kugweba ndenze imfazwe!

Mna, Yeshua HaMashiach, ndithumele la Mazwi ngomkhonzi wam ongumthunywa wam othembekileyo, kwaye ndim, uYeshua HaMashiach, oqinisekisa obu bungqina bunikwe kwiVangeli engunaPhakade etyhiliweyo ngexesha layo.

Ndingulowo uza kujikela kuSirayeli ngoku ukuze ndibahlangule emhlabeni njengomhlanguli wabo wegazi kwaye ndiyanibiza Sirayeli kuba lixesha lokwamkela uMesiya wenu wokwenene, kuba ngendlela efanayo lo mkhohlisi uya kungcatsha aze atshabalalise iBhabhiloni eyimfihlelo, ngendlela efanayo uya kungcatsha abantu belizwe kwisikizi lakhe lesiphanziso elisondelayo ngokuqinisekileyo.

Ngoko qondani ukuba baya kurhubuluza baye kwiindawo zabo zokuzimela ukuze bazifihle ematyeni omhlaba kwaye abaninzi baya kutshabalala apho. Kwaye ngoko ndiyabuza, kuphi na apho nabani na anokuzimela khona ukuze asinde ekujongeni kwam okungunaphakade, okanye ngubani onokusinda emehlweni kaYahweh kuba konke kubonwa kwaye kuyaziwa kwaye nantoni na eyenzekayo emhlabeni ivunyelwe ukuba yenzeke nokuba kusemgwebeni okanye kusindiso lwabo baya kunqula igama lam kude kube sekupheleni okanye kwabo bandamkelayo njengoMesiya wabo, kwaye bonke abenza ububi baya kuvuzwa ngokupheleleyo.

Kwaye ndingulowo uya kubonakala ngequbuliso esibhakabhakeni ngozuko olukhulu kwaye baya kwazi kwaye abaninzi baya kuqonda ukuba baphoswe sesi siganeko sinye babeqiniseke ngaso ukuba bayinxalenye yaso, kodwa bayalelwa ngenxa yobomi babo obunesono kuba isono asisayi kuze singene emazulwini okanye sime phambi kobuso bam.

Yabonani! Amaxesha afikile kwaye ngoko ngoku ndiya kuhamba ndiye kuhlanganisa abathembekileyo.

Khangelani! Bukelani! Mamelani! Nikulungele na zithandwa zam? Usuku luphezu kwenu.
