Ixesha Lakho LikaNGOKU

NGOKU kuza uYeshua HaMashiach ukuba anixelele izizathu zezinto endizithethayo, ukuze niweve ngokupheleleyo amazwi am ukuze niqonde izizathu ezikhokelele kweli xesha likaNGOKU! Ukuze niqonde iziphumo zokuwa ngeyure enye nerhamncwa ngequbuliso nangokungalindelekanga koku kugqibela kukaNGOKU, nokuze nilungiswe ngokupheleleyo kudibaniso olupheleleyo oluya kuwa kulo mhlaba ngomgwebo womlilo opheleleyo njengoko abathembekileyo kuKrestu Yesu bemka kwangoko. Selah.

Gxilisani NGOKUPHELELEYO iintliziyo zenu, imiphefumlo yenu, iingqondo zenu, namandla enu konke ekuthobeleni uKrestu Yesu NGOKU njengoko lemihelo yokuzala iphela ngequbuliso NGOKU. Apho niya kuxhwilwa ngequbuliso nangokungalindelekanga ngoku, nize nisiwe kwigumbi letroni lika-Adonai-YHVH.

Kunjalo ke, mna Krestu Yesu, ndiza kunixelela ngani nina bathembekileyo bam kuKrestu Yesu ukuze niziqonde iinjongo zika-Adonai-YHVH wenu endanahlula ngazo kweli hlabathi ligoso neligwenxa phambi kokuba kufike umgwebo.

Nilibandla lam, umzimba wam, kwanomtshakazi wam: Ngoko, qondani ukuba ningoobani kuKrestu Yesu. Kuba ndiyanilawula nonke NGOKU, ukuba iingqondo zenu nizibeke ngokupheleleyo kwizinto eziphezulu, hayi kwizinto ezisemhlabeni, eziza kutshatyalaliswa ngomgwebo. Kuba nifile, nobomi benu bufihliwe noKrestu Yesu ku-Adonai-YHVH. Xa athe wabonakala uKrestu Yesu, ongubomi benu, niya kubonakala nam nina ebuqaqawulini.

Qondani nonke nina bathembekileyo kuKrestu Yesu ukuba ndiniyalele NONKE ukuba niphume kwimisebenzi yenyama kwaye nimke kwiindlela zeBhabheli eyiMfihlo eziye zakhokelela kumgwebo wayo ophindwe kabini womlilo, oya kwanabela nakwezinye iintlanga ezingamahenyukazi ngokokurhweba nokuthengisa kwazo.

Ukanti abaninzi benu bayayala ngenkani imiyalelo yam, echazwe ngamagama ale mihla ngoku hayi ngeelwimi eziguquliweyo, kodwa niyaqhubeka nihleli ebumnyameni ningathobeli ekuhleni ngokungathi amazwi am anokukhethwa kwiindlela zenu zokudikidiki. Aninasizathu.

Endaweni yokukhonza iNkosi uYesu Krestu eyathenga ubomi benu esandleni seentshaba zenu zanaphakade, nina endaweni yoko nasebenzisa ukuhlangulwa kwenu njengethuba lenyama kwaye ngokuzenzekelayo nahlala nikhonza abawileyo kwimisebenzi yenyama kubuwexuki obuhanahanisayo ngokufanayo.

Qondani, nonke nina bazondla ngala maza angangcwele kwizono zenu zenyama kumakhoboka e-dopamine anxulumene nemidlalo, ukuba ngenxa yokungathobeli kwenu nentliziyo yenu elukhuni enenkani kuKrestu Yesu, aniyi kungena eBukumkanini bamaZulu ngeli xesha! Kwaye oku akungenxa yesenzo sokudlala umdlalo, kodwa yimidlalo eniyidlalayo. Yiphepheni imisebenzi yenyama nangayiphi na indleko, fundisisani ukuze nifunde.

Endaweni yoko niya kuzifumana ningalifumananga ilifa kwaye ninikelwe ngokupheleleyo kwimisebenzi yenyama ngexesha lembandezelo kaYakobi. Apho kufuneka niguquke kuyo imisebenzi yenyama kwaye nihlale nikwimeko yokuguquka nokulungela njengoko kuyalelwe ukuze ndinikhulule ngokupheleleyo kumgwebo ongqwabalala ongenzelwanga abathobelayo abathembekileyo kuKrestu Yesu. Kodwa ngowabangathobeliyo nabaneentliziyo ezilukhuni abathi NGOKU basale isicelo sam sokugqibela besiya kulo mgwebo ngokufanayo. Selah.

Qondani, ningazisebenzisa ezi zixhobo ziyinxalenye ebalulekileyo yoluntu lwenu oluwileyo ngeenjongo zobulungisa nokuphilisa nokugqibelela nokufunda ubungqina bam ngamazwi am aqulethwe apha kule ncwadana ivuliweyo endiyivule esandleni somthunywa wam nesicaka sam. Ukuze nihambe ngokuthe tye phambi kwam ekuthobeleni konke okunothando. Zonke izinto ngokumodareyitha! Mukani kwizono zenyama! Hayi ukuba ningazimodareyitha izono zenu zenyama, nangona, abaninzi benu besenjenjalo. Zikhwebuleni kuko konke okubonakala kubi kwaye nicikide zonke izinto, nibambelele kokulungileyo. Abangemalungisa abayi kubudla ilifa ubukumkani buka-Adonai-YHVH, nilunyukisiwe ngokupheleleyo NGOKU.

Ukudlala imidlalo ngokwako akusosono; ingekuko nombono wako. Nangona kunjalo, ukuvuma ngokuzithandela kwimisebenzi yenyama njengoko abanye besenza izono zabo ngelixa bonwatyiswa zizenzo zabo kuyafana nokwenza ezi zono ngokwenu. Nokuba yeyiphi na indlela yobunyhukunyhuku: Nokuba ngumabonakude, iincwadi, iitablethi, iifowuni, nezinto ezinjalo: Ndiyanixelela ngokucacileyo: Bonke abo bazondla ngobunyhukunyhuku baya kushiywa phakathi kwabanyhukunyhuku, kwaye oko kuthenjisiwe NGOKU.

Qondani, nonke nina nimkileyo kwiBandla laseLawodike nifuna ukubalwa phakathi kwabathembekileyo kuKrestu Yesu, njengoko kufanelekile. Kufuneka nimke kwiindlela zenu zokudikidiki zokungathobeli ngokuyinxenye amazwi am kwaye ninikele ngentliziyo yenu iphela, umphefumlo, ingqondo namandla ngeenkcukacha ezinyanisekileyo ukuze nomelezwe phakathi kwabathembekileyo kuKrestu Yesu. Kuba akwanelanga ukushiya amabandla enu aseLawodike kwaye nitshintshe indawo yenu ukuze niqale ubomi obutsha kunye/okanye isazisi esitsha ngaphandle kwenguquko epheleleyo kuzo zonke izono zenu, aninakumane niqale ubomi obutsha kuba ezi ziindlela zabawileyo. Ukuba nabani na kuni ucinga ngaphakathi kwakhe ebugwenxeni bakhe ukuba ndingalincama eli vuzo ngenxa yesi sono, ke ngenene anikalungeli ukungena eBukumkanini bamaZulu ngeli xesha.

Qondani: Nonke nina bangathembekanga kuKrestu Yesu abafunda la mazwi: Ninokuza kum NGOKU, kwaye nihlale enguqukweni epheleleyo, apho nivuma izono zenu kum kwaye nicela uxolo kwaye niyeke kwangoko okanye niya kushiyelwa kumlilo womsuli. Ndiniyalela ukuba nihlale ngokuthembekileyo phantsi kwegazi lam lentlawulelo eliya kunihlambulula ngokupheleleyo, kungenjalo, ningawahoyi amazwi am kwaye nihlale phantsi kolawulo lwala maza anesono kwaye nijongane nomgwebo waboni ongenzelwanga nina.

Abaninzi benu bazithanda kakhulu izixhobo zenu kangangokuba anisamqondi noKrestu Yesu kungasathethwa ke ngokujikela ingqalelo yenu kum ngenxa yosindiso lwenu. Abanye benu bavumela ezi zixhobo zixokayo ukuba zikuxelele ukuba amazwi am angcwele anikwe kule Ncwadana incinci yemigwebo yobulungisa ayinyaniso ngokuyinxenye, okanye bubuxoki obucacileyo obunibophayo kumakhubalo nobuqhetseba bazo apho esinye isifundiswa singavumelani nesinye, apho uninzi lwenu ningezizo izifundiswa ngoko ningazi njani? Okanye nokuba ungasosifundiswa seziphathamandla, zininzi ezinye izifundiswa ezingavumelaniyo, ngoko ke, ngubani onokuyazi inyaniso. Kwaye ke, amazwi athi "abahlala befunda kodwa bengakwazi ukufikelela elwazini lwenyaniso" ami njengogwebo lwanaphakade lwabo bonke abathanda ukwenza nokuphila ubuxoki babo, okanye ubuxoki babanye ngokufanayo.

Yivani amazwi am, akukho mivuzo iyinxenye kwaye akukho mihla ehlisiweyo kwaye akukho zii-akhawunti zokonga ebukumkanini bamaZulu kuba ezi zinto ziphelisiwe ngonaphakade kuni NGOKU kwinkululeko yenu epheleleyo. Ngaphezu koko, qondani, ekupheleni kweveki yamashumi asixhenxe kaDaniyeli ezi zinto ziya kube ziphelile kumgwebo womlilo onikwe ngu-Adonai-YHVH ngomyalelo kaKrestu Yesu ngonaphakade.

Into eya kwenzeka kwabangathembekanga ilula: Abayi kusiwa kwilifa lanaphakade NGOKU kodwa baya kushiywa nabagwetyiweyo ukuze bazingqine bethembekile ekufeleni ukholo egameni lam. Ukuba bangazifumana befanele eli wonga. Nangona kunjalo, kubo bonke abanikwa eli wonga niya kufumana usindiso ukuba niyala ukuyeka. Ngoko ndiyanibongoza nonke eluthandweni lwam olusoloko luvutha ngani: Phumani ngokupheleleyo kuko konke ukungathobeli NGOKU kwaye niyeke kwangoko ukungayihoyi imiyalelo yam ye-rhema kule ncwadana ivuliweyo ukuze nimke ngokupheleleyo kwiindlela zeBhabheli eyiMfihlo, fundani kwaye niwaqonde amazwi am kwaye nindicele ukuba ndinibonise ubomi benu, ndinithanda ngonaphakade naphakade!

NiliBandla LAM: I-Ekklesia yam ebizelwe ngaphandle eqokelelweyo yatywinelwa iinjongo zam kula maxesha obabalo aphelileyo NGOKU. Abathembekileyo kuKrestu Yesu bathathwa kwaSirayeli beqala eJudiya ngemihla kaPontiyo Pilato, baze ke baye kuzo zonke iintlanga ngokufanayo ukuze la masebe omnquma wasendle NGOKU axhunyelwe kumnquma wemvelo ngonaphakade kanaphakade. Apho nixhunyelwe kwiSirayeli ka-Adonai-YHVH ngomyalelo ka-Adonai-YHVH ngokwakhe, owenziwe zizandla zam ezinamandla kuba aboyisi bam abathembekileyo NGOKU bangqiniwe bethembekile kuKrestu Yesu, VUYANI!

Hlalani KUM, zithandwa zam, kwaye ningazivumeli izandla zenu ukuba zihlale zimdaka ukuba zithe zangcola. Vumani izono zenu NGOKU, ndilapha nani. Ndiya kuzisusa kuni ndinihlambulule ngokupheleleyo.

Kunjalo ke, mna Yeshua HaMashiach, ndiza kunixelela izizathu zeziphumo zexesha lam lezizathu zezinto endizithethayo, ukuze niyitye le Ncwadana incinci ivuliweyo endiyivule esandleni somthunywa wam nesicaka sam, kwanaye uBiblaridion:144, nangona imnandi emlonyeni ikrakra esiswini. Ukuze niqonde ukuthanda kwam ngobomi benu kuzo zonke iindlela zenu, nokuze niliqonde eli xesha lokugqibela likaNGOKU elimisiweyo elinikwe ukuze nonke nimke ngokupheleleyo kwizono zenyama, okanye niya kuhlala kumlilo wam wokusulungekisa ukuze nibize egameni lam kude kube sekupheleni, okanye niya kuwa kumgwebo wanaphakade kwichibi lomlilo ukuba nithe nawexuka ngokupheleleyo. Kuba akukho luhlu lwezinye iinketho, yile qha.

UBiblaridion:144 yindoda elilungisa ngobulungisa bam ndedwa obunikwe kumoya wam ohlambululayo nakwisohlwayo esikhulu nasekuhlanjululweni kubulungisa beendlela ZAM zika-Adonai-YHVH wakhe, ezinikwe ngezandla zikaYise uYesu. Kwaye ngezi ndlela, ufunyanwa ethembekile kwaye ekwangumoyisi onamandla owoyise kungekuphela uHeylel Ben Shachar ngokwakhe, (owamhlasela ngokuthe ngqo), kodwa naba balindi bawileyo abangangcwele ababenikwe inkululeko kwaye bamhlasela nabo. Ukanti elubabalweni lwam nasencebeni yam nasemlilweni wam wokusulungekisa uBiblaridion:144 uwe kasixhenxe, kunye nezinye izihlandlo ezisixhenxe, kodwa, usabuya ngokuthembekileyo eme ngeenyawo zakhe omelele ngakumbi ngomyalelo wam, kwaye ezinikele ngakumbi entliziyweni kuko konke ukuthobela okulungileyo engalindelanga nto njengembuyekezo, kodwa ehamba elukholweni olupheleleyo njengoko mna ndedwa ndimnika ngezandla zabanye.

Mna, Kristu Yesu, okwanguye uYeshua HaMashiach, ndamahlula uBiblaridion:144 kwinkonzo yakhe endayimisele yona eBandleni lam apho ndambeka khona ukuze ndimfundise iindlela zam nendlela yokukhonza ngokwenene ummelwane wakhe ngothando, ndibaxolela endaweni yokuhlasela xa kuxhokonxwa enye ingqondo, kwaye ngokwenene akusekho zingqondo zahlukileyo ziseleyo kuBiblaridion:144 kodwa uliNgqina elithembekileyo ezintweni zonke. Ndamfundisa nokuhlala kwiitafile zesidlo sangokuhlwa esisesikweni kananjalo nokufikelela kwabona baphantsi kakhulu kwiindlela ezingemva. Ndamthatha ke ndamfihla kuni nonke ukuze ndimhlambulule emlilweni wam wokucikida kuko konke okwenziwa kuye nokuze ndimhlambulule ezonweni zakhe ezazininzi kwaye zizibi.

Qondani ukuba izono awawela kuzo zabangelwa koko kwenziwa ngokungekho mthethweni kuye nakudade wabo ngeekhemikhali zaba balindi bawiweyo besengabantwana phambi kweminyaka yokuphendula. Ezi zinto zenziwa kuye ngokungekho mthethweni kwiinkqubo eziyimfihlo nezikhethiweyo zokusebenza kwengqondo ezifakwe ngaba balindi bangcwele ngaphakathi kwezi zikolo zeemfihlakalo ekuthiwa yimfundo kawonke-wonke, la maziko amdaka asasazeke kuyo yonke iBhabhiloni eyiMfihlo. Wanikwa iziyobisi kakhulu ebutsheni bakhe wanyanzelwa, waza ke wavuma wathatha inxaxheba kuqeqesho lwabo ngemvume yakhe yobuyatha yobutsha. Apho wafumana inceba ngenxa yobutsha bakhe nokutshatyalaliswa kosapho lwakhe ngenxa yeempembelelo ezimbi zangaphandle.

Enyanisweni, wanikelwa kwaba balindi bangcwele nguyise owayekholelwa kubuxoki babo bokuba uya kwenziwa indoda engcono nephumeleleyo phantsi kononophelo lwabo kwaye baya kusebenza njengomkhuluwa ukuze bambuyisele eluntwini aze abone amanqaku amahle ngenxa yoko. Bamahlula ngeendlela ezininzi ezazivumelekile kolu luhlu lweziganeko kwaye bamqeqesha kubugcisa obuninzi bemimoya nakubugcisa obuninzi bokulwa obabuntuntumbiswayo baza banqandwa ngobulungisa ngenxa yeenjongo zam.

Noko ke, uAdonai-YHVH wayelwazi kakuhle olu luhlu lweziganeko kwangaphambi kokusekwa kwehlabathi kwaye endaweni yokutsala uBiblaridion:144 ezandleni zabo, wavumela ukuba anyamezele kanye oko kwakufanele iinjongo ezichanekileyo zika-Adonai-YHVH wam kuYeshua HaMashiach ukuze someleze kwaye sikhande esi sitya sinamandla somgwebo wesigqeba nemfazwe. Ukanti, mna, Kristu Yesu, ndazifihla kubo bonke ababandakanyekayo kodwa ndahlala naye lonke ixesha. Noko ke, wenziwa waba yingozi kwaye wakwazi ukwenza inkonzo yakhe awayeyenzelwe yona kuHeylel Ben Shachar, eziswa kuye ngaba balindi bawiweyo badweliswe kobu bungqina. Eluthandweni lwam ngaye ndimzalisile ngothando novelwano nobukhuseli ndamphilisa, ndada ndadala ngokutsha ingqondo yakhe eluthandweni lwam ngaye. Inene, ungumalusi othembekileyo olingqinisileyo iLizwi lakhe kum kuba ungqine ukuba ndingathembela kuye kwakhona.

Ngoko ke oku kwavunyelwa ukuze kuboniswe kubo bonke ukuba akunakwenzeka nakubani na kubo ukuba axwitshe NABANI NA ezandleni ZAM EZINAMANDLA kananjalo ukuze uBiblaridion:144 abelane ngeentlungu zam kwaye uye wabandezeleka kakhulu kwaye uye wabandezeleka ixesha elide, kwaye ukanti usami ngokuthobela ecaleni kwam engalindelanga nto imbuyekezo.

Ngoko ke, kwavunyelwa ukuba aqeqeshwe ngokuzolileyo nangasese kwiindlela zabo zobuchule nezokulwa, kodwa egadwe sisandla sam kuba ndamtywinwa uBiblaridion:144 ebutsheni bakhe phambi kokuba angene kwimfundo kawonke-wonke, apho yena nodade wabo bachongwa kwaye bajoliswa kwezi zikolo zeemfihlakalo ngenxa yoko kuthiwa kukuphakama kwabo, nto leyo eneneni yayilutywino lomoya oyingcwele olwabekwa phezu kobomi babo sisandla sam kananjalo nezakhono zendalo eziyilwe nguAdonai-YHVH kwezi zitya zombini, ezikhethiweyo nezigcinelwe iinjongo zam. Kwaye ke, aba balindi bawiweyo bamamkela ngovuyo kwaye abazange bonge nayiphi na indleko kwiinzame zabo zokwaphula olo tywino lungcwele lobulungisa endimtywine ngalo. Kuzo zonke iinzame zabo bonke basilela kabuhlungu njengoko kungqinwa yile ncwadana ivuliweyo noko kuza kuphunyezwa ngezandla zikaBiblaridion:144 kumgwebo waba balindi bawiweyo onguNGOKU.

Qondani ukuba wayevela kusapho olwalujolisiweyo nto leyo eqhelekileyo kumazwe aseAmeruKa, kwaye uninzi lwenu luvela kwezi meko zinye.

Udade wabo, owaxhatshazwa ebutsheni bakhe phambi kweminyaka yokuphendula, uya kuma endlwini yam ngenxa yezinto awazinyamezelayo ebutsheni bakhe ezandleni zaba balindi bawiweyo nangenxa yoko wanyanzelwa kuye ngokungazi ngobungqwabalala ebutsheni bakhe okwamenzakalisa kakhulu kangangokuba akazange aphinde achache, kangangokuba akazange asakhathalele ukuphila kuba wazibeka ityala ngokulahlekelwa ngumntwana wakhe owahluthwa kuye ngokungekho sikweni esonweni.

Ngoko ke, ndibaxolele bonke abo beze kum kulo mbandela befuna uxolelo phantsi kwegazi lam lentlawulelo, kuba eKalvari ndahlawulela izono ezaziya kubangelwa ngaba balindi bawiweyo kananjalo nazo zonke izinto abathanda ukuzenza ngeekonsathi zabo, nangeziyobisi zengqondo.

Qondani ukuba ubabalo nenceba kuboniswa ngale ndlela ukuze kungabikho mntu uwela emgwebeni ngenxa yeenjongo zika-Adonai-YHVH ngaphandle kokuba umgwebo wenzelwe bona ngenxa yobulukhuni bentliziyo yabo. Kwaye ngobulungisa obupheleleyo nangokusesikweni okupheleleyo akukho kukhetha buso kwezi zigqibo, kodwa umgwebo ogqibeleleyo onikwe nguAdonai-YHVH osekelwe kwimvana ka-Adonai-YHVH eya kuzalisekiswa ngokupheleleyo yiNgonyama yeSizwe sakwaYuda. Ngoko ke, thuthuzelekani kakhulu ngenxa yabathandwa benu enicinga ukuba balahlekile kodwa eneneni batywinwe, balele emhlabeni ukuze bavuswe kanye phambi kwenu nina niphilayo niseleyo. Selah.

Ngoko ke, uya kuma endlwini yam ngokunjalo kuba ezinkathazweni nasezohlwayweni zakhe waguquka ngokupheleleyo kwimizuzu yakhe yokugqibela kwaye kwezo zenzo ngeengcinga neenjongo zentliziyo yobomi uxolelwe kwaye uya kuzalisekisa iinjongo zakhe ezingunaphakade ngokunjalo. Wayesazi kakuhle ukuba ndandinaye kuba ndatyhila imvelo yam eqaqambileyo kuye ukuze ndimthuthuzele kwaye ndimazise ukuba aphumle lula kuba wayesoyisiwe kwaye etyhefiswe ngabantu abakhohlakeleyo abathunyelwe ngaba balindi bawiweyo abaza kuwela kumgwebo ongunaphakade ekupheleni kweveki yamashumi asixhenxe kaDaniyeli kwaye amaxesha abo afikile ngokwenene.

Ngoko ke, qondani kwaye nikhuthazeke kakhulu kuba ndiya kwenzela inceba lowo ndiya kumenzela inceba kuba iindlela zam ziindlela zika-Adonai-YHVH wam osoloko etyebile ngenceba nobabalo kwaye usoloko emi ukuze aphakamise abo boyisiweyo. Kwanabo ezintliziyweni nasezingqondweni zabo bafuna kuphela ukoyisa zonke izinto, kodwa ubomi babo bunqunyulwa ngokukhawuleza. Noko ke, akunakwenzeka ukunixwitsha ezandleni zam nokuba izinto zingabonakala zinjani na.

Qondani ukuba ndingubani: Ndim imvana ka-Adonai-YHVH kwaye bonke ngaphandle koonyana bokungathobeli banokuguquka kuba umkhonzi akahlali endlwini yesono ngonaphakade kodwa unyana uhlala. Ngenxa yale njongo, uNyana ka-Adonai-YHVH ubonakalisiwe ukuze ndikhulule ngokupheleleyo kwaye nditshabalalise yonke imikhondo ebusweni bomhlaba yayo yonke imisebenzi kaHeylel Ben Shachar. Kwaye kunjalo: Ngamandla omoya wam ongcwele wesithembiso, endinikhanda ngawo nonke, ndiyayichitha imisebenzi kaHeylel Ben Shachar ebomini benu nokuba nihlaselwe kwaye nibotshwe ngayiphi na indlela.

Ngoko ke, qondani, ukuba mna Yesu waseNazarete okwanguye imvana ka-Adonai-YHVH ndize kusindisa aboni eninzi lenu elingabona bakhulu kubo. Kwaye kunjalo, ukuba uAdonai-YHVH ukhethe ukwenza imizekelo ngani nonke ngenceba nobabalo lwakhe aluthululele ngokukhululekileyo emhlabeni ngoKristu Yesu, okwanguye uYeshua HaMashiach. Kwaye ke, ndiyahlula kwaye ndiphakamise imizekelo ngamanye amaxesha phakathi kwabathembekileyo bam ukuze nonke nazi ukulunga okungunaphakade kuka-Adonai-YHVH, kwaYena uEl Shaddai Omkhulu!

Kwaye kunjalo, bantwana bam bolahleko nibuyele kuAdonai-YHVH wenu ngokuthobela okunothando kwaye nahlala nithembekile kuwo nawuphi na umsebenzi okanye imisebenzi enayinikwa zizandla zam. Ngoko ke, NGOKU ndiya kuniphakamisa nonke niye ngqo kwigumbi letrone lika-Adonai-YHVH!

Niyabuqonda ubukhulu bolu phakamiso?

NINGABUQONDA NJANI?
