Ingqina eliMbaxa: uSirayeli
Okukrakra Okumnandi
Septemba 02, 2024

Ngenkohliso Balwa Imfazwe!

Yabonani! Nguwuphi umthiyeli obeka umgibe phambi kwentaka? Kodwa inene, inene, abantu belizwe lesithembiso benzelwa umgibe, kwaye imfazwe isemhlabeni njengoko ingqumbo nomgwebo sele uza kuwa. Zihlanganiseni kwilizwi likaYeshua HaMashiach nive amazwi am. Yabonani! Ndinixelela ngezinto ezizayo zingekenzeki, ukuze amazwi am eme njengesilumkiso kubo bonke abathunyelwe kubo. Kuba injalo imvelo yamazwi obomi endithetha ngawo, kuba mna, Yeshua HaMashiach, ndiliLizwi likaThixo elenziwe inyama, kwaye ndihlala ngaphakathi kwenu, kuba nina niyitempile kaThixo ophilayo, kwaye mna ndingumbingeleli omkhulu ngonaphakade ngokohlobo lukaMelkitsedeke.

Inene, inene, amazwi am aya kufumana amehlo, iindlebe, neentliziyo zabo athunyelwe kubo, kwaye aya kugcinwa kwiimfazwe neenkathazo ezizayo, ngaphandle kokuba kuya kubakho ukunqaba kwezibhalo kwihlabathi lonke kuba xa umhlaba wawugalelwe ngobuninzi ngamazwi kaYahweh, uThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade, ihlabathi lawamkela loo mazwi okwexeshana. Kodwa ngoku ihlabathi liye lawala amazwi kaYahweh ngenxa yobuxoki beNamba, ngoko ke ngoku iLizwi likaThixo liya kunqaba, kwaye kuya kubakho indlala yeLizwi likaThixo, kuba zinjalo izigwebo ezizayo. Noko ke, wonke umphefumlo ophilayo obiza egameni leNkosi kude kube sekupheleni uya kusindiswa, kwanabo bangabathandiyo ubomi babo kude kube sekupheleni.

Kodwa yivani niqonde amazwi am, nazi niqonde ukuba amazwi am aya kugcinwa, kwaye aya kutyhilwa ehlabathini, kwaye amaxesha okutyhilwa afikile. Kwaye ke mna, Yeshua HaMashiach, ndithumela ivangeli engunaphakade kumaxesha enkathazo enkulu, kwanenkathazo kaYakobi, ukuze isihlwele sezihlwele sisindiswe ngokungathi kungomlilo. Kwaye ke ivangeli engunaphakade ifikile ukuze ityhilwe ngexesha layo, kwaye ubungqina buya kubhalwa, kwaye amazwi kaYahweh aya kumpompoza njengemilambo yamanzi aphilayo, kwaye abhalelwe ukuthotyelwa ngabo bonke abaneendlebe zokuva namehlo okubona nentliziyo yokufunda. Kwaye amazwi kaYahweh ngamazwi anaphakade angenakuphulukana nawo mntu, awonakalise, okanye awathulise, kuba umntu owenza njalo uya kuzilahlekisa, kodwa amazwi kaThixo ongunaphakade ahlala ngonaphakade.

Yabonani! Amaxesha enkathazo kaYakobi afikile, kwaye amaxesha afikile okutyhilwa kwezinto zonke, kuba uYahweh ukhethe eli xesha lokuzityhila kwindalo yakhe, naphambi konyana wakhe okuphela kwamzeleyo, uYesu Krestu. Kuba mna ndinguYesu Krestu, iNkosi yeeNkosi noKumkani wooKumkani enyukileyo, kwaye ezandleni zam kunikwe itayitile yomhlaba kuba ndawuthenga ngegazi lam elingenasono, kwaye umvuzo wam unam. Kwaye ndiya kwabela ilifa lam phakathi kwabathembekileyo, kwaye lowo woyisayo uya kudla ilifa lezinto zonke esandleni sam njengomvuzo wokuthembeka kwabo, ukuze ngonaphakade babonise uzuko olungunaphakade lukaYahweh. Kwaye uYahweh uya kubazukisa, kwaye abo bazithobileyo ngokuthobeka, ukuthobela, nenguquko baya kuphakanyiswa eluzukweni olungunaphakade kwaye baya kukhanya njengeenkwenkwezi esibhakabhakeni, kwaye ke uzuko lwabo luya kukhanya ngonaphakade. Ngoko ke ndiyanikhuthaza, zithandwa zam, ukuba nilive ilizwi lam nithobe iintliziyo zenu ekuthobeleni.

Ndiveni, kwaye nindive kakuhle; imfazwe ilapha, kwaye iBhabheli eyiMfihlo ibotshelelwe intshabalalo; kwaye isigwebo nesohlwayo sayo asilibazisi kodwa sisondele. Kwaye njengoko ndixhwila iBandla lam, uMzimba wam, kwanomtshakazi wam ndimsusa kwintlekele ndimsa kukhuseleko lwamazulu, umthinteli uya kususwa, kwaye baya kuba nohlaselo lwabo olothusayo, kwaye ixesha lokuhlasela kwabo lingoku. Ookumkani abalishumi baye bacebisana, kwaye ngokuqinisekileyo bazibeke izandla zabo ekulungeni; baye balungiselela, baye balungisa, kwaye baye bagubungela umhlaba ngobuxoki babo, ukuze babonakale bethetheleleka ekubulaweni kwezigidi ngezigidi eziya kuwa kwintlekele neentlungu zemfazwe yabo.

Kwaye yindlu yesikhungu kaSathana elawula ihlabathi ngoku, kwaye bazifihla elizweni lesithembiso njengesigcawu esizifihlayo kwixhoba laso de ixhoba liwele kumnatha waso. Inene, inene, baye baluka umnatha wabo kwihlabathi lonke, kwaye baye babamba ookumkani bomhlaba kumnatha wabo wenkohliso, kuba ngenkohliso balwa imfazwe, kunye nokubulala, ukunyoba, ukugrogrisa, ukuphanga, nezinye izinto ezininzi ezimbi. Kuba iindlela zabo zisonakele ngokupheleleyo, kuba uSathana ubeka abaphantsi kwabaphantsi nabo bonakele ngokupheleleyo njengeenkokheli zehlabathi ukuze balawuleke lula kwaye basetyenziswe ngokupheleleyo lukhula uSathana alutyale phakathi kwamasimi engqolowa kaYahweh. Kwaye amaxesha okuvuna okokugqibela ayasondela apho ingqolowa iya kuqokelelwa kwindlu yovimba engunaphakade kaYahweh ngokuvuna okuzayo, kwaye ukhula lukaSathana luya kuncothulwa ngokupheleleyo, lubotshwe, luze luphoswe luphila kwelo chibi lomlilo elivutha umlilo wesulfure elivutha awona malangatye ashushu aluhlaza kwilifa looyise; kwaye linjalo ikamva labo, ngoko musani ukuboyika.

Kodwa kunjalo, isigcawu silawula umnatha waso njengoko sifuna ukulawula umhlaba; kuba umhlaba unikwe ezandleni zenamba ngendlela elinganiselweyo okwexeshana elifutshane, kwanemveki yamashumi asixhenxe kaDaniyeli, amaxesha ayo angoku. Kwaye ke abathembekileyo eBandleni lam baya kusiwa emazulwini kumvuzo omkhulu, kwaye baya kufumana ilifa labo, kwaye umyeni uya kuhlangana nomtshakazi wakhe kumnqophiso ongunaphakade, kwaye ngonaphakade baya kuba nam naphi na apho ndiya khona. Kwaye ke itayitile yomhlaba endiyiphetheyo ezandleni zam etywinwe ngoku iza kutywinulwa, kwaye amatywina aya kukhululwa, kwaye baya kuvuthela amaxilongo abo, kwaye izitya ziya kugalelwa ngengqumbo. Kwaye isigwebo esikhulu nentshabalaliso ziya kugubungela umhlaba, kwaye intlekele iya kuwa kwihlabathi lonke njengoko imfazwe yokugqibela yokudibanisa iintlanga phantsi kolawulo lukaSathana iqhubeka, ukuze irhamncwa nomprofeti wobuxoki baphakame balawule umhlaba ngenkohlakalo enkulu, kwaye kuya kuba njalo. Baya kumemezela imfazwe kuzo zonke izinto zikaThixo wenene, kwaye ubuKrestu buya kuba ngokungekho mthethweni, kwaye kunikwe amarhamncwa ukuba alwe nabangcwele aze aboyise, kwaye abaninzi baya kulahlekelwa ziintloko zabo ngenxa yegama lam, kwaye bonke abenza njalo baya kufumana ubomi babo ngonaphakade kumvuzo omkhulu nobukhazikhazi obungunaphakade, kwaye baya kuphila ubomi obungunaphakade boxolo, kwaye baya kunandipha ukutyeba kwelizwe.

Noko ke, njengoko ihlabathi liguqiswa ngamadolo alo angenakholo ekuthobeleni ububi obunyulu ngemfazwe enkulu, inkohlakalo, nentshabalaliso, irhamncwa elivela elwandle nerhamncwa elivela emhlabeni ziya kuphakama ngokupheleleyo, nangona ngoku zizenza iintshaba kumaqhinga azo angoku nenkohliso yokutshabalalisa iBhabheli eyiMfihlo. Qondani ukuba uyise wazo mnye, kuba zezikaSathana, kwanaloo mbini inyanyekayo iNamba eyikhethileyo ukufezekisa iinjongo zayo. Kwaye ke irhamncwa elivela elwandle nerhamncwa elivela emhlabeni ziya kuphakama, kwaye ziya kuthethathethana ngoxolo phakathi kweentlanga, ngaphandle kokuba luya kuba luxolo lobuxoki. Qondani ukuba akukho luxolo ngaphandle kwesivumelwano soxolo esinikwe nguYahweh ngenxa yezono zehlabathi, kuba mna, uYesu waseNazarete, ndiyiNkosana yoxolo kwaye ndizisa uxolo olungunaphakade, kwaye ngobulungisa ndiya kulawula umhlaba ndisuka kwilizwe lesithembiso kwitrone kaDavide, kuba mna ndiyindlalifa yakhe esemthethweni.

Kwaye ke baya kuphakama. Imfazwe yehlabathi yesithathu iya kuxhokonxwa phakathi kwabo baqubuda kwityhubhu yabo emnyama kwaye baya kulwa imfazwe emhlabeni njengoko befuna ukuphelisa nantoni na enento yokwenza negama likaYesu Krestu. Ngaphandle kokuba abo baqubuda kwityhubhu yabo emnyama baya kuziswa phantsi kolawulo olupheleleyo lwerhamncwa elivela elwandle, eliya kubakhokela njengoMahdi wabo. Kwaye abo bakumazwe esithembiso baya kuba nokumkani wabo wobuxoki, umprofeti wobuxoki, uMoshiach wobuxoki, kwaye uya kuba ngumakhi wetempile werhamncwa, kwaye irhamncwa elivela emhlabeni liya kubakhokela ngenkohliso enkulu. Kwaye abo banxiba ityhubhu emnyama emabunzini abo baya kuthethathethana ngoxolo nabo baqubuda kwityhubhu yabo emnyama, kwaye ihlabathi liya kumanyana njengoko belandela la marhamncwa kwaye besithi phakathi kwabo, ngubani onjengerhamncwa? Kwaye ngubani onokulwa nerhamncwa? Noko ke mna, Krestu Yesu, onguKumkani wooKumkani neNkosi yeeNkosi, ndiya kuza kugweba ndilwe imfazwe, kwaye ndiya kulitshabalalisa ngokupheleleyo irhamncwa nomprofeti wobuxoki, kwaye baya kuphoswa bephila kwelo chibi likhulu livutha umlilo nesulfure apho baya kulindela iNamba ukuba ihlangane nabo ngexesha elimisiweyo.

Kwaye ke baya kwenza izithembiso ezinkulu zoxolo nokhuseleko, kwaye baya kuqokelela abo balahlekileyo ngonaphakade kwintshabalalo yabo. Baya kuthembisa uluntu olutsha nomyalelo omtsha oya kufuna uphawu lwabo lwerhamncwa ukuze bathathe inxaxheba, ngoko musani ukuthatha uphawu. Ngophawu lwabo baya kuthembisa unyango lwesibetho kwaye baya kuthembisa impilo nobomi obude, kwaye baya kuzicingela ikamva elikhulu, kwaye baya kwenza izithembiso ezininzi zobuxoki kwabo baya kutshabalala ngokuthatha uphawu lwabo lwerhamncwa.

Kwaye ke ndithi kwakhona kwabo bahlala emhlabeni, ukuze bonke beve:

UKUBA NABANI NA UYALINQULA IRHAMNCWA NOMFANEKISO WALO, AZE AMKELE UPHAWU LWALO EBUNZINI LAKHE, OKANYE ESANDLENI SAKHE, YENA UYA KUSELA KWIWAYINI YENGQUMBO KATHIXO, EGALELWE INGENAMXUBE KWINDEBE YOMSINDWANO WAKHE, KWAYE UYA KUTHUTHUNJISWA NGOMLILO NESULIFARE, PHAMBI KWEENGELOSI EZINGCWELE, NAPHAMBI KWEMVANA: KWAYE UMSI WENTUTHUMBO YABO UNYUKA NGONAPHAKADE KANAPHAKADE. KWAYE ABANAKUPHUMULA EMINI OKANYE EBUSUKU, ABO BANQULA IRHAMNCWA NOMFANEKISO WALO, NAYE NABANI NA OWAMKELA UPHAWU LWEGAMA LALO.

Kodwa qondani, bantwana bam, ukuba uluntu lwabo aluyi kuba naxolo, kwaye izithembiso zabo sisirhintyelo sokurhintyela abangakrokreliyo kumgibe wabo; kuba nguwuphi umthiyeli obeka umgibe phambi kwentaka? Noko ke iintaka ezininzi ziya kubanjwa kumgibe wabo, kwanabo bonke amagama abo angazange abhalwe eNcwadini yoBomi. Qondani ukuba bonke abathatha uphawu baya kutshintshwa ngonaphakade, kwaye baya kuba benze unyeliso kuYahweh, ngokutshintsha i-DNA yabo kwaye ngaloo ndlela bonakalise umfanekiso kaThixo owenziwe ngawo umntu, kwaye bonke abenza njalo abanaxolelo, ngoko musani ukuthatha uphawu lwabo. Kungcono nilahlekelwe bubomi benu basemhlabeni ukuze ndinisindise ngonaphakade kwaye nifumane ubomi obungunaphakade kum, Yeshua HaMashiach, ngokubiza egameni lam kude kube sekupheleni kunokuba nizame ngobuyatha ukusindisa ubomi benu basemhlabeni kwaye nilahlekelwe naliphi na ithuba lobomi obungunaphakade ekufeni kwesibini kwamadangatye anaphakade apho impethu yabo ingafiyo. Ngoko khethani ngobulumko!

Ihlabathi eliya kuphuma eluthuthwini lwemfazwe liya kuba lihlabathi lesibetho, ukufa, indlala, ungcoliseko, intswela-bulungisa, inkohlakalo, ubugeza, nosizi olukhulu. Lonke ulonwabo luya kususwa emhlabeni, kwaye noko ke irhamncwa elivela emhlabeni liya kunyanzela uphawu lwalo ehlabathini, kwanokulunywa yinyoka, kwaye nabani na ochasayo uya kulahlekelwa bubomi bakhe. Kwaye olo lukhetho abaninzi abaya kufuneka balwenze ukuze bafumane ubomi obungunaphakade elusindisweni, kwaye bonke ababiza egameni leNkosi kude kube sekupheleni baya kusindiswa. Noko ke, uluntu lwabo olutsha aluyi kuzisa xolo, kwaye bonke abathatha uphawu baya kuba yinxalenye yengqondo edibeneyo, kwaye baya kuxutywa neeteknoloji, kwaye baya kuba ngamakhoboka entando yenamba, kwaye yiyo abaya kuyikhonza, kwaye baya kuya kwelo chibi likhulu lomlilo, kwaye kunjalo ukufa kwesibini.

INamba, irhamncwa elivela elwandle, nerhamncwa elivela emhlabeni ziya kukhokela umanyano lookumkani, kwaye ziya kulwa imfazwe nabo bonke abafuna ukuphila iindlela zikaYahweh, kwaye ziya kundibeka ityala, mna, Yeshua HaMashiach, ngeentlekele eziwela umhlaba. Ngokugweba nangemvukelo enkulu baya kunyelisa igama likaThixo wasezulwini nonyana wakhe okuphela kwamzeleyo, kwaye baya kwenza amabango amakhulu onyeliso. Irhamncwa elivela elwandle nerhamncwa elivela emhlabeni ziya kuzimanyanisa ngokuchasene namangqina am amabini nezibane zam ezibini kwaye ziya kuma zichasene nazo ngemiqondiso emininzi yobuxoki nemimangaliso, kwaye ziya kuthetha amazwi amaninzi onyeliso. Kwaye xa amangqina am amabini nezibane zam ezibini zigqibile ubungqina bazo, irhamncwa elinyuka enzonzobileni liya kulwa nazo, lize lizoyise, lizibulale. Noko ke ngeentsuku ezintathu ezinesiqingatha ziya kuvuswa, kwaye ilizwi elikhulu elivela ezulwini liya kuthi kuzo, Nyukelani apha. Kwaye ziya kunyukela ezulwini efini, kwaye iintshaba zazo ziya kuzibona.

Kwaye ke irhamncwa elivela emhlabeni, kwanalowo uza nophawu lwakhe lwerhamncwa esandleni, kwanalowo uya kuba ngumprofeti wabo wobuxoki noMoshiach wobuxoki, uya kuza kulawula ilizwe lesithembiso. Kwaye sele ezithandekisile kubalawuli belizwe, kwanakwiinkokheli zendlu yesikhungu kaSathana, kuba wabelana ngemigca yegazi labo eyonakeleyo, kwaye uyazingca ngomnombo wakhe, kwaye uDan yintshontsho lengonyama elingasoze livelise Mesiya. Kwaye ke uya kwakha itempile yabo njengoko befuna ngobuyatha ukuphinda bamise umthetho kaMoses, izinto ezifezekiswe ngokugqibeleleyo kwidini lam lokuzincama njengeMvana kaThixo, kuba mna ndingulowo wazinikela njengePasika egqibeleleyo ngenxa yezono zoluntu. Kodwa nguwuphi umthiyeli obeka umgibe phambi kwentaka?

Inene, inene, baya kubeka umgibe eZiyon, kwaye baya kufuna ukuthatha abo belizwe, kuba uSathana ufuna ukutshabalalisa wonke umphefumlo wokugqibela wohlanga luka-Adam, lona kanye uluntu; kwaye ukuba imihla yembandezelo kaYakobi ibinganqunyulwanga, bekungayi kusinda nanye inyama. Kodwa ke iqonga liya kumiswa ngetempile yabo yesithathu, apho isikizi lesiphanziso liya kwenzeka khona. Kwaye ke ndiye ndalumkisa: Xa nithe ngoko nalibona isikizi lesiphanziso, ekwakuthethwa ngalo nguDaniyeli uMprofeti, limi endaweni engcwele (lowo ulesayo, makaqonde). Ngelo xesha abo baseYuda, mababalekele ezintabeni. Lowo uphezu kwendlu, makangehli, aye kuthatha nantoni na endlwini yakhe: Kwaye lowo usentsimini makangabuyeli umva aye kuthatha iimpahla zakhe. Yeha ke kwabakhulelweyo, nakwabanyisayo ngaloo mihla. Kodwa thandazani ukuba ukubaleka kwenu kungabi sebusika, kungabi ngomhla weSabatha: Kuba ngelo xesha kuya kubakho imbandezelo enkulu, ekungazanga kubekho injalo ukususela ekuqalekeni kwehlabathi kude kube ngoku, hayi, kwaye akusayi kuze kubekho.

Qonda ukuba eli xesha likufuphi kwaye liya kwenzeka emva kweenyanga zokuqala ezingama-42 zembandezelo kaYakobi. Ebudeni beenyanga zokuqala ezingama-42, amangqina amabini aya kuprofeta, kwaye aya kwakha itempile yawo ngexesha into enjalo ingasafuneki; kuba mna, uYesu waseNazarete, ndiliPasika eligqibeleleyo lezono zoluntu ngonaphakade. Kodwa, la marhamncwa akrwada ayandichasa, kwaye irhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni liya kuzama ukuzibeka njengoMoshiach wabo wobuxoki, kwaye lo ngumgibe wabo.

Baya kubhenela kwabo belizwe abasakholwayo kum, uYeshua HaMashiach, uMesiya wabo wokwenene, kwaye abaninzi baya kubaleka baye kuhlanganisana kuMoshiach wabo wobuxoki, kodwa ngokungazi bamlandela ukuya ekutshabalaleni kwabo. Kuba irhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni ngulo ubeka umgibe elizweni, kwaye uya kurhwebesha abaninzi kubuqhinga bakhe; emva koko isigcawu siya kutsibela ixhoba laso, kwaye siya kungcatsha abantu belizwe. Anifundanga na? Kuba, kubhaliwe kwathiwa: Makungabikho namnye umntu unikhohlisayo nangayiphi na indlela: kuba loo mini ayiyi kufika, ngaphandle kokuba kufike kuqala ukumka, kwaye loo mntu wesono atyhilwe, unyana wentshabalalo, Ongumchasi, kwaye uya kuziphakamisa ngaphezu kwako konke okubizwa ngokuba nguThixo, okanye okunqulwayo: ukuze ahlale njengoThixo eTempileni kaThixo, ezibonakalisa ukuba unguThixo. Ngoko qonda ukuba ukumka kuqala kulapho ndiya kuqokelela khona abathembekileyo beBandla lam kum ukuze ndidibane nabo esibhakabhakeni kwaye ngelo xesha umthinteli uya kususwa kwaye loo mntu wesono uya kutyhilwa ngexesha lakhe.

Kodwa uYahweh wakha wayishiya nini indlalifa yosindiso kwintshabalalo engunaphakade, kwaye ndiyivakalisa esidlangalaleni phambi kokuba yenzeke: Ukuba mna, uYeshua HaMashiach, ndisaya kusindisa intsalela kaYakobi, kwaye abaninzi belizwe baya kundamkela njengoMesiya wabo. Kwaye ndiya kubahlangula. Yabona, kubhaliwe kwathiwa: Ndakhangela, nalo iMvana imi phezu kwentaba yeZiyon, kwaye inaye abali-144,000 benegama likaYise libhalwe emabunzini abo. Kwaye kuya kuba njalo ukuba ndiya kuza kuhlangula intsalela entshabalalweni, kwaye ndiza nomkhosi ongenakunqandwa oya kuhamba inyathelo ngenyathelo njengoko ndibakhokelela ekukhokeleleni abancinane bam ekhuselekweni. Mhlawumbi ungazibuza ukuba bangoobani aba-144,000 kwaye yeyiphi injongo abalandela ngayo kum eNtabeni yeZiyon?

Aba-144,000 bakhethwe nguYahweh phambi kokusekwa kwehlabathi ngexesha elinje. Bakhandwe embandezelweni, kwaye baya kuba nezakhono ezizodwa namandla endiya kubambathisa ngawo kwiingubo zabo ezintsha endiya kubanika, kuba iingubo zabo zithungelwe bona ngokukodwa kwaye ziya kuba neempumelelo neempawu zabo. Baya kuthathwa ekuqokelelweni kwabathembekileyo kodwa babekelwe bucala iinjongo zam, zona iinjongo zikaYahweh noYeshua HaMashiach. Baza ngexesha lembandezelo enkulu ukuze bakhokelele uSirayeli ekhuselekweni kodwa kuqala bathathwa ukuze batshate noKristu, kuba mna ndinguMyeni, kwaye bona banguMtshakazi wam, ababiziweyo, abakhethiweyo, abathembekileyo, nabayinyaniso, bona kanye endibenze banyulu njengentombi enyulu egazini lam lentlawulelo. Kwaye nini, Mtshakazi wam, eniya kukhonza iinjongo zikaYahweh ngomyalelo wam ngamaxesha eembandezelo ezinkulu.

Yabona, kubhaliwe! Kwaye umfazi wabalekela entlango, apho anendawo alungiselelwe yona nguThixo, ukuze bamondle apho iintsuku eziwaka, namakhulu amabini, anamashumi amathandathu. Kwaye kuya kuba njalo ukuba ndiya kubonelela ngendawo yokusabela entlango kubantu bam, kuba ndisaya kumsindisa uYakobi kwiimbandezelo zabo, kwaye abaninzi belizwe lesithembiso baya kuphendukela kum, uYeshua HaMashiach, onguMesiya wabo wokwenene. Kwaye ndiya kubasindisa kumgibe abaya kuwubekelwa, kwaye ke ndilumkisa bonke phambi kokuba kwenzeke ukuba bahlale kude nomgibe kwaye bangabalandeli. Ningakholelwa kwimiqondiso yabo yobuxoki nemimangaliso, ningakholelwa nasemazwini abo onyeliso, kodwa bizani egameni leNkosi, uYeshua HaMashiach, onguYesu Kristu ngolwimi lwesiNgesi, kuba nangaluphi na ulwimi umntu abiza ngalo igama lam, uya kusindiswa xa ebiza egameni lam kude kube sekupheleni.

Kwaye ke ndiza kwazisa abantu bam ngomgibe abaya kuwubekelwa ngumprofeti wobuxoki oza nophawu lwakhe lwerhamncwa esandleni. Kwaye ke ndixelela abantu bam ngokucacileyo phambi kokuba kwenzeke oko kuza kufika emhlabeni phambi kokuba kwenzeke. Ngokuqinisekileyo, iNkosi uThixo ayiyi kwenza nto, kodwa ityhila imfihlelo yayo kubakhonzi bayo abaprofeti. Kwaye ndiya kuza kuhlangula abaninzi kwaSirayeli ababeya kutshabalala kwaye ndibakhokelele entlango kwindawo abayilungiselelweyo nguYahweh, kwaye apho baya kondliwa, badliswe, kwaye bakhathalelwe de idlule imbandezelo. Kuba kuya kwenzeka ekupheleni kwembandezelo kaYakobi, kwaye kubhaliwe kunjalo kwaye kuya kwenziwa njalo:

Ndabona izulu livulekile, nalo ihashe elimhlophe, nalowo wayehleli phezu kwalo wayebizwa ngokuba nguThembekile noNyaniso, kwaye ngobulungisa uyagweba aze alwe imfazwe. Amehlo akhe ayenjengelangatye lomlilo, kwaye entloko yakhe kwakukho izithsaba ezininzi, kwaye wayenegama elibhaliweyo, elingaziwa mntu ngaphandle kwakhe. Kwaye wayambethe isambatho esifakwe egazini, kwaye igama lakhe libizwa ngokuba, Lilizwi likaThixo. Kwaye imikhosi eyayisezulwini yamlandela ikhwele amahashe amhlophe, yambethe ilinen ecikizekileyo, emhlophe necocekileyo. Kwaye emlonyeni wakhe kuphuma ikrele elibukhali, ukuze ngalo abethe iintlanga: kwaye uya kuzilawula ngentonga yentsimbi: kwaye unyathela isixovulelo sewayini sobushushu nengqumbo kaThixo uSomandla. Kwaye unesambatho sakhe, nasethangeni lakhe igama elibhaliweyo, UKumkani Wookumkani, NeNkosi Yeenkosi.

NdinguYeshua HaMashiach, kwaye ndithumele la Mazwi ngomkhonzi wam, kwaye ngomkhonzi wam la mazwi athunyelwe. Kwaye mna, uYeshua HaMashiach, ndingulowo utyhile ivangeli engunaphakade ngexesha layo, kwaye amaxesha okutyhila angoku.

Yabona! Amaxesha okutyhila afikile emhlabeni, kwaye ndiyavakalisa ukuba imbandezelo kaYakobi, yona iveki yamashumi asixhenxe kaDaniyeli, ifikile. Ngesiquphe ndiya kuxhwila abathembekileyo emhlabeni ndibase ekhuselekweni lamazulu, kwaye ngesiquphe umthinteli uya kususwa, kwaye ngesiquphe intlekele enkulu, umgwebo, nentlungu ziya kuwela phezu kwabemi bomhlaba. Kwaye bonke abo banyulelwe ubomi obungunaphakade baya kufumana usindiso kum, uKristu Yesu, iNkosi evusiweyo engunaphakade. Kwaye ubukumkani bukaSathana buya kohlwaywa ngokupheleleyo butshatyalaliswe, bungaphinde buvuke.

Ndingulowo uza kugweba nokulwa imfazwe, kwaye ndiza njengeNgonyama yesizwe sakwaYuda, kwaye intsalela kaYakobi ndiya kuyisindisa, kwaye ndiya kulawula umhlaba ngobulungisa kubukumkani bam beminyaka eliwaka, kwaye ndizisa umkhosi omkhulu wookumkani nababingeleli ukuze balawule umhlaba ngobulungisa ngomyalelo wam.

NdinguYeshua HaMashiach, uKumkani omkhulu wookumkani neNkosi yeenkosi, ngoko yivani aMazwi am, kuba amaxesha okuzaliseka kwawo afikile.
