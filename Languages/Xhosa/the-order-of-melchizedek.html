UMyalelo kaMelkitsedeke: Ingubo Edlulisiweyo, Ukumiswa, kunye NokuBuyiselwa

Kuvela kuYahweh, MNA OMkhulu Ndinguye:

Yivani aMazwi kaYahweh uThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade nindivumele ndidlulisele ubulumko kwindoda elumkileyo phakathi kwenu. Kwaye ke ndininika ukuba niqonde uMyalelo kaMelkitsedeke, kwalowo myalelo uKrestu Yesu amiselwe kuwo njengoMbingeleli oMkhulu, kwanangokomyalelo kaMelkitsedeke ngonaphakade kanaphakade! Kodwa kunjalo ukuba ngenxa yezono zoluntu, iindlela zam zilityelwe, neminqophiso emininzi ilahliwe, nobumnyama obuninzi neendlela ezingafundiswanga ziwugubungele umhlaba ngenxa yobuxoki babo bawaiyo. Kwaye oku kungenxa yobuxoki obenziwe ngabom kunye nabo bafuna ukucinezela inyaniso ngeendlela zabo ezingalunganga nangobuxoki babo obungeyonyani apho ihlabathi lithinjwe yinkohliso yabo enkulu efikele ihlabathi lonke. Kwanenkohliso ngokuyinxenye ethi bonke ubomi buvela kuqhushumbo olukhulu olucingelwayo olwanikwa ukufundisa uluntu kubuxoki babo bawaiyo abazama ukufihla uYahweh, MNA OMkhulu Ndinguye, kwihlabathi endilidalileyo ngesandla sam.

Kwaye MNA NdinguYehova wemimoya, kwaye MNA NdinguNyangeleko weMihla ngokwenene, ngokwenene ngulowo wayekho ngaphambi kokuba iintsuku zenziwe kwaye MNA ndingulowo wadala iingelosi ezingcwele ekubhalwe ngazo: Wenza imimoya ibe zizithunywa zakhe, nabalungiseleli bakhe babe lilangatye lomlilo, naxa indalo yadalwayo yaza yaphuma kubutyebi bentliziyo yam engunaphakade esebenza zonke izinto ngokwecebo lentando yam. Kuba ngenyaniso ndandisazi ukuba uAdam uya kona ngokuzithandela kwakhe nangaphambi kokuba uAdam one kwaye urhwaphilizo luya kuwa luvela ezulwini kubo abawaiyo. Ngenxa yoko, ndayila isicwangciso sam esingunaphakade sokuhlangulwa okupheleleyo kuka-Adam esibekwe ngokupheleleyo ebusweni bukaYesu Krestu okwanguYeshuwa HaMashiyakhi kwiTestamente yakhe eNtsha yobulungisa nenyaniso!

Kwaye ngokufana nomfanekiso ka-Adam wokugqibela uluntu luya kuhlangulwa lube luvuyo noxolo lwanaphakade, apho luya kuzalisekisa khona ngothando iinjongo ezingunaphakade zikaYahweh, MNA OMkhulu Ndinguye. Ngenyaniso, indalo yam yadalwa yaza yenziwa ngothando ukuze izidalwa zam zize kum ngothando ngako konke endizinike kona eluthandweni lwam ngazo. Kwaye ukuze indalo yam ihambe kunye nam ezinhlosweni zam ezingunaphakade ekuzalisekiseni intando yam engunaphakade, kwaye zona ziya kuba novuyo olugqibeleleyo nolupheleleyo, uxolo, nobomi obungunaphakade njengoko zikhokelwa phambili eluxolweni sisandla soMalusi wam oyiNtloko, uYesu waseNazarete, ekungekho mntu unokusondela kuYahweh, MNA OMkhulu Ndinguye, ngaphandle kwakhe, kuba nditshilo.

Kwaye abo bawaiyo basasaze ubuxoki babo phezu kobuso bendalo yam nesiphumo sokuba umhlaba ngoku ugcwele iinkohliso zabo, apho balalela khona ukuze bakhohlise. Kwanenkohliso efike ngokupheleleyo ngenxa yemfihlakalo yobugwenxa eyasinda kunogumbe kaNowa ngendlela yolwazi olungeyonyani lwabo bawaiyo abalunika umntu wemvelo ukuze atshatyalaliswe ngokupheleleyo. Akukhathaliseki, kuba ndithumela bonke abo banoyolo ebugwenxeni inkohliso enamandla ukuze bonke bagwetywe abala ulwazi lwenyaniso bekhetha ubuxoki obungeyonyani babo bawaiyo. Kodwa kunjalo, ukuba umntu wemvelo ngokungazi ufuna ukuzithethelela ityala lakhe lobulungisa obenziwe nguye phambi kokujonga kwam okungunaphakade nokulungileyo, ke ngoko, qonda ukuba akukho nyama igwetyelwayo emehlweni am ngenxa yorhwaphilizo egazini lakho ngenxa yesono enyameni. Kuba ubomi benyama busegazini!

Ngesi sizathu unyana kaYahweh wabonakaliswa kubemi bomhlaba, ukuze ngedini elihlangulayo lemvana kaThixo, uAdam ahlangulwe ngosindiso olupheleleyo kumgwebo wakhe ozayo kubo bonke abo bathobela isivumelwano sam soxolo phakathi kwam noluntu olunguYesu waseNazarete owenziwe waba ngumoya onika ubomi ngamandla esandla sam. Kwaye kananjalo, ngeNgonyama yesizwe sakwaYuda, endiyithumela emhlabeni ngobulungisa ukuze igwebe yenze imfazwe kwaye ke indalo iya kubuyiswa ngenkani ezandleni zabahlali abahlala benenkani kwaye bafuna ukunqanda intando yam engunaphakade, ukunqanda kwayo akunakwenzeka ngonaphakade, kuba ngubani okhoyo onokumelana nesandla sam esinamandla? Kwaye ngamanani abo, nasebubini babo, nasemayelenqe abo, nasemaqhinga abo, kwanakwiitekhnoloji zabo ezingenaxabiso (abaziqhayisa ngazo) abo bawaiyo boyiswa ngokupheleleyo lidini lemvana kaThixo, eyanikela ngegazi layo elingenasono njengoMbingeleli oMkhulu ngonaphakade ngokomyalelo kaMelkitsedeke ngokuthobela ngokupheleleyo lowo wamthumayo, kwanaye uYahweh, MNA OMkhulu Ndinguye Ongunaphakade!

Kwaye ubukumkani bakhe abuyi kuba nasiphelo, kuba ngabo bonke ubulungisa nobungcwele bokwenene uwuthengile umhlaba ngegazi lakhe elihlangulayo ukuze ulawulo olulungileyo luze phezu komhlaba ngexesha lawo kwaye sibe nokonwabela usuku lokuphumla (kuba iwaka leminyaka kuYahweh linjengosuku nosuku lunjengewaka leminyaka emehlweni am), kwanokuphumla kosuku lwesabatha ngaphambi kokuba loo nyoka indala iphoswe ngonaphakade emalangatyeni ngentlungu epheleleyo nengunaphakade ingaphinde ikhunjulwe kwaye ngaphambi kokuba loo mgwebo wetrone emhlophe umiselwe ukuba wenzeke. Kwaye kungomsebenzi wombingeleli omkhulu woMyalelo kaMelkitsedeke apho uAdam ahlangulwe khona kurhwaphilizo ngendlela kaYesu waseNazarete owanikela idini lakhe elingunaphakade kwintlungu ebuhlungu ukuze ahlawule isohlwayo sikaMoses ukuze imithetho yam engunaphakade yaneliswe apho indalo yam engunaphakade ilawulwa ngokugqibeleleyo kwinkululeko elungileyo.

Kwaye ngenene kuya kwenzeka ukuba ndidale izulu elitsha nomhlaba omtsha kuba izulu lokuqala, nomhlaba wokuqala uya kudlula, kwaye akusayi kubakho lwandle; kwaye akusayi kubakho mntu uya kunikhathaza ngonaphakade kanaphakade. Kwaye kuya kwenzeka ngokukhawuleza ngoku ukuba aboyisi bam baya kuzidla ilifa zonke izinto, kwaye ndiya kuba nguThixo wabo, kwaye baya kuba ngoonyana bam. Kodwa aboyikayo nabangakholwayo, nabacekisekayo nababulali, nabahenyuzi, nabathakathi, nabakhonzi bezithixo, nawo onke amaxoki baya kuba nesabelo sabo echibini, elitsha ngomlilo nesulfure, okukukufa kwesibini.

Ngoko ke, ngubani enifanele nimoyike bantwana bam, kuba nenziwe abantwana bakaThixo ophilayo ngemisebenzi kaYeshuwa HaMashiyakhi okwanguYesu Krestu, kwanaye endimnike amandla ngaphezu kwako konke kwindalo yam, ngaphandle kwakwitrone yam ndigcina igunya lam, ngoko ke ngubani onokumelana nesandla esinamandla sikaKrestu Yesu, iNkosi evusiweyo yanyuswa? Ngenene, ngenene, njengoko bekunjalo, kwaye kunjalo, kwaye kuya kuba njalo ngonaphakade kanaphakade ukuba uYahweh noYeshuwa HaMashiyakhi banye kubunye nenjongo kwaye ndimnike amandla ngokupheleleyo kubo bonke ubulungisa kwaye nguye endimnike umyalelo phezu komkhosi wonke wezulu, kwaye ulawula ngokuthobela ngokugqibeleleyo intando yam engunaphakade. Kwaye ke ndininika ukuba niqonde ubuqhetseba obufikileyo emhlabeni, kwanobuxoki babo bawaiyo abaya kufuna ukufihla inyaniso yosindiso lwenu nenyaniso yeNkosi yenu engunaphakade, uKrestu Yesu!

Ngoko ke, qondani ukuba abafundisi benu abangamaJesuit babangele ubumfama ukuba buwe ezintliziyweni zenu ngokufihla inyaniso yendalo yam nangokufihla imbali yenu yokwenene kuni, ukuze nibhadule ebumnyameni nizalisekisa iinkanuko zabo ezimbi, kwanokuba amahobe am ahlangane nabo kwilifa labo elingunaphakade lokutsha nokugingqika kwesulfure kuba injalo injongo yabo. Kwaye kunjalo ukuba uYahweh, MNA OMkhulu Ndinguye, andizange ndizishiye ngaphandle kwengqina. Kwaye ngomnqophiso ngamnye endiwunikileyo, nangeTestamente nganye endiyinikileyo ndayamkela, andizange ndizishiye ngaphandle kwengqina elucacileyo lwezibhalo kunye nolwalathiso olunikezwe ngamazwi abaprofeti bam bakudala, endabamisayo. Kwaye kananjalo kunikwe ngamazwi abaprofeti beTestamente eNtsha abamiselwe nguYeshuwa HaMashiyakhi abaya kumkhonza njengababingeleli bakhe abanamandla nookumkani!

Kuba unjalo uMyalelo kaMelkitsedeke, kwanomyalelo ongunaphakade onikwe uluntu, apho ulawulo awayeza kulusebenzisa uAdam emhlabeni lwalulolwalowo umiselwe njengombingeleli omkhulu, ukumkani, nomprofeti! Kuba wawunjalo (kwaye unjalo) uMyalelo kaMelkitsedeke owanikwa uluntu, kwanokuba uluntu lube nolawulo olulungileyo nangona kukho loo nyoka indala eyamkhohlisayo uEva ngobuqhetseba bayo. Kwaye kananjalo waqhubeka uMyalelo kaMelkitsedeke, kwanokuhlala kuAdam kwasekuqaleni owawunikwayo, kodwa ekufeni kwabo ekugqibeleni bafa kwaye umyalelo kaMelkitsedeke wadluliselwa phambili okwethutyana, ngeentsuku ukusuka kuAdam ukuya kuYakobi.

Kwaye ke kwiHlabathi elalikhona ngaphambi kukazamcolo omkhulu uMyalelo kaMelkitsedeke wadlula kuAdam, waya kuKainan, waya kuEnos, waya kuEnoki, waya kuMetusela, waya kuNowa kwaye wanjalo wadlula uMyalelo kaMelkitsedeke ngeentsuku zangaphambi kukazamcolo omkhulu. Kodwa, abafundisi benu abangamaJesuit bazibhalile ngokutsha izibhalo, kwaye benza ubuqhetseba obuninzi ukuze bafihle inyaniso yembali yokwenene ka-Adam kwananjengoko bezama ukwenza uluntu lube luthuli olungenamsebenzi kwindalo yabo yobuxoki ekucingelwa ukuba yavela kuqhushumbo olukhulu kwixesha labo elidlulileyo abalicingelayo.

Kuba amaJesuit ashumayela omnye uYesu, kwanaye uYesu owenziwe ngomfanekiso kaNimrodi waseBhabheli, owayengumlawuli wokuqala wohlobo lwakhe emva kukazamcolo. Kwaye nangona abalawuli bam abalungileyo abamiselweyo kudala phantsi koMyalelo kaMelkitsedeke banika uluntu ubulungisa endaweni yesono, ukubandezeleka, ukufa, nesohlwayo esingunaphakade. Umntu wemvelo nangona kunjalo uhlala ekhetha isono, ukubandezeleka, ukufa, nesohlwayo esingunaphakade. Nangona kunjalo, bonke abakwindalo yam abanikezwe inkululeko yokuzikhethela bakhululekile ukuzikhethela ukuba baya kuphilela uYahweh ngendlela kaKrestu Yesu, okanye bakhethe isohlwayo sabo esingunaphakade ngenxa yezonwabiso zesono okwexeshana elifutshane. Kwaye kubhaliwe ukuba bonke abasuka kwihlabathi elalikhona ngaphandle kwemiphefumlo esibhozo basindiswa bephila ngenxa kaNowa, owayeyindoda elungileyo eyayilawula umhlaba ngokomyalelo kaMelkitsedeke okwethutyana kuba ekufeni kwakhe wafa.

Ngoko ke, ziqondeni iindlela zabo bawaiyo, ukuba nazo zonke iintliziyo zabo bafuna ukugqwetha, ukuxoka, nokufihla inyaniso kumahobe am, kwanabo endibamisele kubomi obungunaphakade. Kwaye kunjalo ukuba njengoko isicwangciso sam sosindiso luka-Adam satyhilwa ngokuthe ngcembe, ndathumela imibhalo engcwele ngomoya wesiprofeto owahlala phezu kwabo, kwanomoya wesiprofeto owathi ekugqibeleni wazalwa koonyana bakaThixo ngokuzalwa okutsha okunikezwe kwiTestamente eNtsha kaYeshuwa HaMashiyakhi, kwanakuye endihlala ndikholiswa nguye. Kwaye njengoko ndatyhila isicwangciso sam ngokuthe ngcembe, ndanika nemibhalo engcwele ngokuthe ngcembe, kwaye uKrestu Yesu unike imibhalo engcwele eyamkelwe kwisandla sam, kwanaye osebenza zonke izinto ngokwecebo lentando yam.

Kwaye benjenjalo abo bawaiyo besebenza ukurhwaphiliza inyaniso, kwanangendlela efanayo njengoko bazirhwaphilizile ngonaphakade kanaphakade. Kuba bazibhalile ngokutsha izibhalo, banike iiBhayibhile zokufunda zobuxoki, banike iileksikhoni neekhonkhodensi zobuxoki apho abaninzi bakhohliswa ngeenkcazelo ezingcolileyo ngaloo ndlela, ngokungathi uYahweh akanakukwazi ukubonelela abaguquleli abanobuchule bokuguqulela iLizwi lam kuba iLizwi lam alinakwaphulwa okanye lifihlwe, nangona bezama. Ngenene, luzuko lukaYahweh ukufihla umcimbi kwaye liwonga looKumkani ukuwuphengulula! Ngoko ke, ndiyaniyalela ukuba niphengulule iinyaniso zikaYahweh ebusweni bukaYeshuwa HaMashiyakhi!

Ngoko qondani ukuba abo bakhonza elohenyukazi likhulu, kwanabafundisi benu abangamaJesuit, bakhethe umxube okhethwe ngononophelo wobuxoki obunxulumene kancinci apho balalela khona ukuze bakhohlise. Bafihle ubungqina obuninzi obungcwele kwaye babhale ngokutsha iiBhayibhile ezininzi ezisekwe kwimibhalo yobuxoki kwaye benza ubuxoki obuninzi obungeyonyani malunga nenyaniso yendalo yam. Kwaye kulwazi lwam lwangaphambili nezigwebo zam ndamvumela lowo uwileyo ukuba enjenjalo, kuba ukuba imibhalo yam engcwele yayigcinwe kwaye yagcinwa ngokufanelekileyo njengoko kuyalelwe kwaye kuphathisiwe, ubudenge obunjalo babungenakuze bufezekiswe. Kodwa zinjalo iindlela zomntu onesono kwaye zinjalo iindlela zabo bawaiyo abalahlekisa uluntu kwiinkanuko zakhe ezimbi ezihlala zivutha. Nangona kunjalo, iLizwi lam alinakwaphulwa kwaye liya kuyifeza loo nto lithunyelwe ukuyifeza.

Kwaye kubhaliwe kuJasher ukuba emva kukazamcolo omkhulu ingubo kaMelkitsedeke yahlala kuNowa kwaye ekugqibeleni yadluliselwa kuShem owayengowesithandathu kulandelelwano lokunxiba ingubo kwaye ekuhambeni kwexesha umyalelo kaMelkitsedeke wadluliselwa kuAbram owathi ekuhambeni kwexesha waba nguYise weentlanga ezininzi, kwaye kwiindlalifa zakhe zesithembiso ziya izithembiso zelifa ukuze zizalisekiswe kuba unyana womkhonzazana akayi kuba yindlalifa yezithembiso zoBukumkani bukaThixo kodwa olilungisa eliphila ngokholo, kwananjengoko uAbraham othembekileyo wayephila ngeentsuku zakudala.

Ngoko ke, ndininika ukuba niqonde intsingiselo kaMelkitsedeke ethetha uKumkani woBulungisa. Kwaye ke i-etymology iguqulelwe isiHebhere yaba sisiNgesi apho uMelek athetha uKumkani noZedek athetha ubulungisa ngentsingiselo enikiweyo neyenzelweyo enguKumkani woBulungisa. Kwaye benjenjalo aba Kumkani balungileyo belawula indalo yam, kwanamadoda alungileyo awayelindele uMesiya wawo, kwanaye owayeza kuzalwa kwisizwe sakwaYuda.

Kwaye njengoko kubhaliwe kumaHebhere ngesandla sikaPawulos (owayeligosa leemfihlakalo zikaKrestu Yesu) kwaye ke wabhala: Kuba lo Melkitsedeke wayenguKumkani waseSalem, uMbingeleli kaThixo Osenyangweni, owahlangana noAbraham, xa wayebuya ekubulaweni kooKumkani, wamsikelela: Kuye kananjalo uAbraham wanika isishumi seento zonke: othe kuqala ngokutolikwa wanguKumkani woBulungisa: emva koko, ukwanguKumkani waseSalem, oko kukuthi, uKumkani woxolo, ongenayise, ongenanina, ongenamlibo, kwaye akanasiqalo seentsuku zakhe, nasiphelo sobomi: kodwa ufaniswa noNyana kaThixo, kwaye uqhubeka enguMbingeleli ngonaphakade. Kwaye yanjalo ingubo kaMelkitsedeke yadluliselwa kuAbraham othembekileyo ekulondolozweni kwayo kwanomyalelo olondoloziweyo nowenzelwe uYesu Krestu okwanguYeshuwa HaMashiyakhi.

Kodwa, uninzi lwenu lukholelwe kubuxoki babafundisi benu abangamaJesuit abenze uninzi lwenu lwaba ziimfama ngokufunda ngokuchasene nokufunda ukuba ezi vesi zibhekisa kuYesu Krestu ngokwakhe hayi kuMyalelo kaMelkitsedeke ngokwawo. Kuba ngokucacileyo, uYesu waseNazarete unoYise, kwanaye uYahweh MNA OMkhulu Ndinguye, kwaye ngokucacileyo uYesu waseNazarete unonina, kwanaye uMariya owayengowaseNazarete yaseGalili owathi makube kum ngokwentando yakho, kwanomzekelo ongunaphakade wokholo onikwe ukuze abathembekileyo bazazi iindlela zokholo abahamba ngazo. Kuba ngokucacileyo uYesu waseNazarete unezihlobo zakhe angumhlanguli wazo, kutheni ningawahloniphi aMazwi am acacileyo anikwe kwisibhalo enzelwe ukunikhokelela ngokukhuselekileyo kude nobuxoki babo obungeyonyani? Kwaye kunjalo uninzi lwenu lukholelwa kobu buxoki ngaphezu kokucaca nokuchaneka kwamaZwi am.

Kodwa, kubhaliwe ukuba uShem wahlala ethembekile kuNowa, kuba uHam wawulahla ngokupheleleyo umnqophiso kaNowa waza wazilibala iindlela zikaYahweh kwaye uHam nabazukulwana abaninzi bakaNowa bazama ukwakha isixeko sabo esikhulu nenqaba njengoko ubukumkani bukaNimrodi obungalunganga bavuka ukuze bulawule umhlaba ngezo ntsuku ngokungalunganga, ngokweendlela zabo bawaiyo awayebakhonza, nangona ndasebenza amaxesha amaninzi ukunika uNimrodi ukhetho lokubuyela kum ngenguquko, ukhetho awalwalayo ngonaphakade kanaphakade.

Kanti kunjalo kubhaliwe ngokucacileyo kuYashere ngokuphathelele kuAbram noShem owanikezela ingubo kuAbram ekubulaleni kwakhe uKedorlawomere nemikhosi yakhe: Waza akubuya ekubulaleni abo kumkani, yena namadoda akhe badlula entlanjeni yaseSidim apho ookumkani babesilwa khona. Waphuma uBhera ukumkani waseSodom, namanye amadoda akhe awayenaye, emingxunyeni yodaka ababewele kuyo, baya kuhlangabeza uAbram namadoda akhe. Waphuma noAdonitsedeke (okanye uMelkitsedeke) ukumkani waseYerusalem, owayenguShem, namadoda akhe, waya kuhlangabeza uAbram nabantu bakhe, ephethe isonka newayini, baza bahlala kunye entlanjeni yaseMeleki. Wamsikelela uAdonitsedeke (okanye uMelkitsedeke) uAbram, waza uAbram wamnika isishumi kuko konke awayekuthathile emaxhobeni eentshaba zakhe, kuba uAdonitsedeke (okanye uMelkitsedeke) wayengumbingeleli phambi koThixo.

Kanti kunjalo kubhaliwe kuYashere: Yaye imihla kaSara yaba yiminyaka elikhulu elinamashumi amabini anesixhenxe waza wafa, waza uAbraham wenza isijwili esikhulu nesinzima, waza wenza izithethe zokuzila iintsuku ezisixhenxe. Bonke abemi belizwe bamthuthuzela uAbraham noIsake unyana wakhe ngenxa kaSara. Yakuba idlule imihla yokuzila kwabo, uAbraham wamkhupha unyana wakhe uIsake, waza waya endlwini kaShem noEbhere, ukuze afunde iindlela zeNkosi nemiyalelo yayo, waza uAbraham wahlala apho iminyaka emithathu. Ngaloo ndlela ke ingubo kaMelkitsedeke yadlulela kuIsake owathi walinikezela ilungelo lobuzibulo komncinane, kuba uEsawu wayezicaphukela izinto zikaThixo waza wathengisa ngobuzibulo bakhe ngesitya sesuphu: Kwanasesigqibo uYahweh asamekeleyo ngonaphakade.

Kanti kunjalo kubhaliwe kuYashere ngokuphathelele kuMyalelo kaMelkitsedeke: Emva koku uShela unyana ka-Arpakishadi wafa ngaloo nyaka, onguweshumi elinesibhozo wobomi bukaYakobi noEsawu; yaye yonke imihla yokuphila kukaShela yayiyiminyaka engamakhulu amane anamashumi amathathu anesithathu waza wafa. Ngelo xesha uIsake wathumela unyana wakhe omncinane uYakobi endlwini kaShem noEbhere, waza wafunda imiyalelo yeNkosi, waza uYakobi wahlala endlwini kaShem noEbhere iminyaka engamashumi amathathu anesibini, kodwa uEsawu umkhuluwa wakhe akazange aye, kuba wayengafuni ukuya, waza wahlala endlwini kayise kwilizwe laseKanan.

Kanti kunjalo kubhaliwe kuYashere ngokuphathelele kuMyalelo kaMelkitsedeke, ukuba ekuhambeni kwexesha, mna, Yahweh Omkhulu Ndinguye, ndamtyhilela uYakobi ukuba umyalelo kaMelkitsedeke wawuza kwahlulwa ube zizikhundla ezithathu ngokwamazwi abhalwe kuYashere ukuze niyazi inyaniso yamazwi am: Waba nomona uRubhen ngenxa kanina uLeya ngale nto, wazala ngumsindo, waza wasukuma ngomsindo wakhe waya kungena ententeni kaBhila waza wasusa apho ukhuko lukayise. Ngelo xesha isabelo sobuzibulo, kunye nezikhundla zobukumkani nezobubingeleli, zasuswa koonyana bakaRubhen, kuba wayengcolise ukhuko lukayise, baza ubuzibulo banikwa uYosefu, isikhundla sobukumkani sanikwa uYuda, saza isikhundla sobubingeleli sanikwa uLevi, kuba uRubhen wayengcolise ukhuko lukayise.

Kwaba njalo ke kwaye kubhaliwe ukuba iimbopheleleko eziqulethwe kuMyalelo kaMelkitsedeke zabekelwa bucala ukuze kulandelwe umkhomba-ndlela woMyolelo kaMoses, phantsi koMyolelo wakhe oMdala owabonelela ngezikhundla ezithathu ezahlukeneyo nezingafaniyo kule misebenzi ingcwele, ngaloo ndlela uSirayeli waba nomnombo wookumkani, waba neSikhundla sobubingeleli bobuLevi, waba nesikolo sabaprofeti, isikolo esenziwe ngokomfuziselo wesikolo sabaprofeti bamandulo, ukusukela kwiintsuku zangaphambi konogumbe.

Ngoko ke iimbopheleleko nemiyalelo yoMbingeleli, uKumkani, noMprofeti zonke zadlula kuSirayeli ngokwemilibo yabo yeminombo ebhalwe kwizibhalo ezingcwele kwaye ekuhambeni kwexesha, kwafika indoda egama linguYohane eyazalwa nguZekariya owayengowomnombo wobubingeleli kaTsadoki. Kwaye ngaloo ndlela kwakufanelekile ukuba uYohane uMbhaptizi azalisekise bonke ubulungisa xa wayebhaptiza imvana kaThixo kumlambo iYordan apho uYesu wabhaptizelwa khona, waza waphuma ngqo emanzini. Yabona, amazulu avuleka kuye, waza uYohane wabona umoya kaThixo usehla njengehobe, usiza phezu kwakhe. Yabona, kwaphuma ilizwi ezulwini, lisithi, Lo nguNyana wam endimthandayo, endikholisiweyo nguye.

Waza ngaloo ndlela uYesu waseNazarete wamiselwa njengombingeleli ngonaphakade ngokwoMyalelo kaMelkitsedeke kwaye zonke izikhundla zibuyiselwe phantsi kwengubo yobu bubingeleli bungunaphakade, ngoku nangonaphakade.

Kuba uMyalelo kaMelkitsedeke ngumyalelo wobubingeleli, ingubo yawo ayisayi kusuka kuYeshua HaMashiach, kuba uvukile ukuze angabi safa!

Kuba uMyalelo kaMelkitsedeke ngumyalelo wookumkani wolawulo olulungileyo lwendalo yam.

Kuba uMyalelo kaMelkitsedeke ngumyalelo wabaProfeti abathetha baze bafundise imbali, iindlela, nekamva lezicwangciso zokugqibelela zikaYahweh ezinikwe ebusweni bukaYesu Krestu.

NDINGU-YHVH, kwaNdinguye Omkhulu kwaye ndithumele la Mazwi ngomthunywa wam nomkhonzi wam kwaye ngomthunywa wam nomkhonzi wam athunyelwe la Mazwi. Inene, kumthunywa wam nomkhonzi wam okhonza phantsi kukaKrestu Yesu iNkosi yakhe engunaphakade kwaye ngomoya wesiprofeto uYahweh noKrestu Yesu batyhilele umthunywa wethu nomkhonzi wethu iVangeli engunaPhakade ukuze kuthotyelwe kusindiswe bonke abathunyelwe la Mazwi. Kwaye aMazwi eVangeli engunaPhakade aya kufumana amehlo, iindlebe, neentliziyo zabo bonke abathunyelweyo.

NDINGU-YHVH, kwaNdinguye odale, wenza waza wathumela uNyana wam okuphela kwamzeleyo, kwaYeshua HaMashiach onguYesu uMesiya wosindiso lwabo bonke abazelwe nguAdam kwaye bamkela inkululeko epheleleyo ukuba abo babiziweyo bangamphendula umsindisi wabo xa ebiza. Ngoko ke, qonda ukuba ndim, YHVH, Omkhulu Ndinguye otyhila uYesu waseNazarete kwabalahlekileyo, ukuze uMalusi wabo oyiNtloko afumane aze abuyise zonke iigusha zam ezilahlekileyo azise koovimba bam bokhuseleko nendawo yokusabela ngonaphakade kanaphakade.

NDINGU-YHVH, kwaNdinguye owahlula ububingeleli obungunaphakade bukaMelkitsedeke baba zizikhundla ezithathu ezahlukeneyo nezingafaniyo zoKumkani, uMbingeleli, noMprofeti phantsi kukaYakobi ngamaxesha akhe.

NDINGU-YHVH, kwaNdinguye obubuyisileyo ubuBingeleli obungunaphakade bukaMelkitsedeke kuKrestu Yesu, ekubhalwe ngaye kwathiwa: Kuba kuyabonakala, ukuba iNkosi yenu yavela kwaYuda, isizwe uMoses angathethanga nto ngaso, ngokuphathelele kuBuBingeleli. Kwaye yinto ebonakala ngakumbi, kuba ngokufana noMelkitsedeke, kuvuke omnye uMbingeleli, ongamenziwanga mbingeleli ngokomthetho womyalelo wenyama wamandulo, kodwa ngokwamandla obomi obungapheliyo. Kuba uyangqina ngolu hlobo, UnguMbingeleli ngonaphakade, ngokohlelo lukaMelkitsedeke. Kuba umyalelo owawukho ngaphambili, utshitshisiwe, ngenxa yobuthathaka bawo, nokungabi nanzuzo kwawo.

Kuba uMthetho awenzanga nto yagqibelela, kodwa ukungeniswa kwethemba elingcono kwenza kwagqibelela, esithi ngalo sisondele kuYahweh. Kwaye kangangoko kungekho ngaphandle kwesifungo (kuba aba benziwa abaBingeleli ngaphandle kwesifungo: Kodwa lo wenziwa ngesifungo ngulowo wathi kuye, UYahweh ufungile, kwaye akayi kuzisola, UnguMbingeleli ngonaphakade, ngokohlelo lukaMelkitsedeke) Ngokunjalo uYesu wenziwe isiqinisekiso soMyolelo ongcono. Kwaye phakathi kwabo abaninzi benziwa abaBingeleli, kuba babengenakuqhubeka, ngenxa yokufa kwabo. Kodwa le ndoda, ngenxa yokuba ihleli ngonaphakade, inoBuBingeleli, obungenakusuka komnye buye komnye. Ngoko ke, unako nokubasindisa ngokugqibeleleyo abo beza kuThixo ngaye, ekubeni ehlala ehleli, ukuze abathethelele.

Ngoko ke, ndiyaninika ukuba niwuqonde uMyalelo ongunaPhakade kaMelkitsedeke nenyaniso kaYeshua HaMashiach, okwanguYesu uKrestu kuba phantsi kwengubo yakhe engunaphakade yombingeleli omkhulu ngokwoMyalelo kaMelkitsedeke uKrestu Yesu uya kuthwesisa izithsaba aze angcwalise umkhosi omkhulu wookumkani nababingeleli phantsi koMyalelo kaMelkitsedeke kwaye ngaloo ndlela uBukumkani baseZulwini buya kulawulwa sisandla esingunaphakade seNkosi yabo engunaPhakade yoBulungisa, abaya kulawula phantsi kwayo ngokugqibeleleyo ngobulungisa nobungcwele benene ngonaphakade, kuba ngokuphathelele kuBukumkani bukaKrestu Yesu, akukho siphelo.
