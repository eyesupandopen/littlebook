Ngesiquphe Ithemba Lenu Liyatyhilwa!

Zihlanganiseni nize nive ilizwi leNkosi yenu, lona ilizwi likaYesu waseNazarete kuba ndingulowo wazinikela kanye, ngokupheleleyo, nanaphakade njengeMvana yePasika egqibeleleyo kuYahweh ngenxa yezono zenu, yena uThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade, njengombingeleli wenu omkhulu ngonaphakade ngokohlobo lukaMelkitsedeke ukuze nifumane ukuxolelwa kwezono. Yizani kum, nize neendlebe neentliziyo zenu ukuze nimamele ngokholo olupheleleyo kumazwi am okholo! Ewe ndiyanicela ukuba nimamele, ningavi nje izandi zamazwi am kodwa nimamele ngeentliziyo zenu ukuze niqonde injongo yam nifumane intuthuzelo eluthandweni lwam ngani, nokuba nilamkele ngobulali ilizwi elimiliselweyo kuni, elinako ukuzisindisa imiphefumlo yenu.

Kuba ndithetha nabathembekileyo eBandleni lam, wona umzimba wam, yena umtshakazi wam, bona bonke abo ndiza kubahlanganisela kum kukhuseleko lwamazulu kwigumbi letrone likaYahweh. Kuba ndingulowo ukhathalele imiphefumlo yenu nalowo unyamekela ukukhula kwenu okuqhubekayo ukuze nikhule njengesityalo esinobulungisa phambi kwam, kuba ndingulowo uYahweh amnike njengomlimi nomgcini wesitiya sakhe esingcwele kakhulu semiphefumlo yabakhululweyo. Kuba niyiperile yexabiso elikhulu yaye phakathi kobutyebi obuninzi bukaYahweh niphakathi kwabo bangenaxabiso, ekungekho litye lishiywe lingajikwanga elusindisweni lwenu kwaye akukho mzamo wongiweyo wokunithena nokunixhasa nokunibonisa kuYahweh, uYihlo uSomandla, njengentombi enyulu engenabala, nasisihwinjwiri, nayiphi na into enjalo. Ngoko musani ukoyika, ningavumeli nakuphi na ukuthandabuza malunga nelona lincinane lamaZwi am!

Ngoko ke ndiyanibuza, zintanda zam, kutheni iintliziyo zenu zinikhathaza nje? Kutheni nihlunguzela iintloko zenu ngokungathi nithwele ingcikivo? Ingaba nibavumele ooSathana ukuba bangene eentliziyweni zenu baze basebeze amazwi obuxoki entuthuzelo akhokelela kuphela ekungakholweni njengoko befuna ukukrazula nokudabula konke okungcwele? Ngaba niya kuzithabathela loo nyoka yamandulo kubuqhetseba bayo obusoloko buxoka nobuqhophololo bayo obusoloko buqhubela phambili ebuya kungena ezindlebeni zenu kwala mazwi axokayo anokubhukuqa ukholo lwenu, njengoko ngokwenene kwenzekile kwabaninzi abavumele iingqondo zabo ukuba zonakaliswe ekugxileni kwengqondo enye kum, uKrestu Yesu iNkosi yenu?

Kuba akukho wumbi uya kunikhathalela, kwaye bonke abanye bangabazenzisi abenza ngathi, kwaye akukho wumbi uYesu kodwa kukho kuphela iikopi ezingexabiso eliphantsi kunye neemveliso zomgunyathi ezingaxabisekanga ezenzelwe ukukhohlisa. Ngoko ke ndiyanicela ukuba ningayihoyi le ngqiqo ndingayinika nina ukuze niqhubeke nimi njengesibane sokukhanya nakule mizuzu yokugqibela phambi kokuba ihlabathi ebenilazi liyeke ukubakho lize liguqulwe ngonaphakade ukusuka kubungendawo balo bangoku ukuya kumgwebo ongunaphakade oya kuthi ngoku wehle njengesohlwayo esingunaphakade phezu kwabangendawo abafuna ukubanga ilifa lam. Kuba umhlaba lolwam nokuzala kwawo, kwaye ndiyiNkosi enkulu engunaphakade yeeNkosi noKumkani wooKumkani, yona iNkosi yenu elungileyo kwaye ndingulowo uya kuthatha ilifa lam ezandleni zabahlali abangenabango kwaye abangengabo ookumkani kwaye abangengabo oothixo kwaye abangengabo abaphambukeli abavela kwamanye amazwe kodwa ngabawileyo abazenzisayo kuba bonke bangabazenzisi abenza ngathi. Kwaye imihla yabo iya kwenziwa isiphelo esingunaphakade kwilifa labo lanaphakade kumadangatye ashushu kakhulu aluhlaza.

Kuba iinimba zokubeleka azipheli ngoku, kwaye kukho intlaninge yemiqondiso phezulu nentlaninge yemiqondiso emhlabeni esoloko ibonakala kwaye ingapheli. Kwaye ezi zinikwa njengesibhengezo kwaye ukuze nibe nethemba, ngoko musani ukuzijongela phantsi nize nizichaze ngendlela enjengabanyelisi! Umsebenzi wezandla zikaYahweh owenziwe ngezandla zeengelosi zakhe ezinamandla, kwaye ngokwenene uzotywe ngomnwe kaThixo ngokwakhe maxa wambi, nangomyalelo wakhe ongunaphakade, uyazibhengeza ezi ziganeko zasezulwini ezisondelayo, kuba imini ilapha kwaye iphezu kwenu zintanda zam, ngoko musani ukoyika! Amaxesha alapha okuhlanganiswa kwabathembekileyo bam ukuze badibane nam emafini njengoko ndisihla ezulwini ngelizwi lesiphatha-zithunywa nangeXilongo elinamandla likaThixo, ngoko kholwani ngamaZwi am kuba ango-ewe no-amen!

Ngubani kwaye yintoni ekhoyo enokunikhathaza bantwana bam? Ngaba noyika ukuba umyeni akayi kufika aze athathe isandla somtshakazi wakhe kumnqophiso ongunaphakade njengoko kwathenjiswayo mandulo? Ingaba iLizwi likaThixo alisebenzi? Makube lee! Njengoko kubhaliwe kwaye kwabhengezwa, kuya kuba njalo ngokuqinisekileyo ukuba ngesiquphe ndiya kunihlutha engqumbini ndinise kukhuseleko lwamazulu apho ndiye ndaya kunilungiselela indawo, kuba endlwini kaBawo kukho iindawo zokuhlala ezininzi. Kwaye niya kuhlala ninam ngonaphakade kwaye niya kuba ngookumkani nababingeleli phambi koYahweh uThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade, kuba wenze injongo walungiselela oko ebomini benu, kwaye ngubani onako ukumisa iinjongo nokulungiselela kukaThixo ongunaphakade?

Ndiyanixelela inyaniso, akukho nto kwaye akukho mntu unako ukuthintela nakancinane iinjongo zika-El Shaddai kwaye akukho namnye unokuma (nakweyona nkcaso incinane) ngaphandle kokuthuliswa ngokoyika kumandla apheleleyo, ubungangamsha nokungcangcazela okubangelwa xa uYahweh eshukuma kancinane ukuze asuke kwitrone yakhe engunaphakade kwaye lonke izulu nomhlaba ziyashukuma kubukho bakhe obunamandla. Kuba nangona uYahweh ezifihlile ngobulali, uthando, novelwano iintsuku ezininzi ngoku (kuba usuku lunye lunjengeminyaka eliwaka emehlweni kaThixo ongunaphakade) ngoku lixesha uYahweh alikhethileyo apho aya kuzityhila kwindalo yakhe, xa bonke baya kwazi ukuba kukho uThixo ezulwini, kwaye ngokuqinisekileyo baya kuqonda ukuba njengoko kunjalo phezulu, kuya kuba njalo nasezantsi nasemhlabeni kuba uYahweh unguThixo wamazulu nomhlaba kwaye ulawula ngokugqithisileyo phezu kwendalo yakhe kwaye akukho wumbi ngaphandle kwakhe ngamandla, ubungangamsha namandla. (Kwaye abawileyo babanjwe luloyiko olungunaphakade) Ngoko musani ukoyika Bantwana BakaPhezukonke kuba uThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade, uYahweh ngokwakhe ngulowo unilwelayo nalowo uya kunihlangula, anikhulule, aze aguqule ubomi benu bube bubungangamsha obungunaphakade ngokuqhwanyaza kweliso.

Ngoko kutheni nisiba nomonde ongako? Ngaba nivakalelwa ngokungathi iNkosi yenu engunaphakade nomyeni unishiyile, mtshakazi wam? Ngaba umsindisi wenu ongunaphakade uwushiyile umzimba wakhe, wawushiya ungenantloko? Makube lee ukuba into enjalo icingwe. Yeyiphi into uYahweh ayibhengeze kwindalo yakhe njengesondeleyo engakhange yenzeke emva koko? Ngaba amaZwi kaNowa awela kumhlaba owomileyo njengezithembiso ezingenalutho xa wayengumshumayeli wobulungisa iminyaka elikhulu elinamashumi amabini kwihlabathi elalikhona ngoko? Hayi! Kodwa ngesiquphe nangobundlobongela obukhulu amanzi akhululwa ngomzuzwana aza anyuka ngaphezu kwezona ntaba zinde emhlabeni ngeentsuku ezalandelayo.

Kwaye ke ndiyanicebisa ukuba ningaphindi nizibandakanye nabangazi ngokuzithandela. Bakhetha ukungazi ngokuzithandela ukuze bangahoyi ukuba ngelizwi likaThixo amazulu ayekho mandulo, nomhlaba umi uphuma emanzini usemanzini: Ngaloo nto ihlabathi elalikhona ngoko, ligutyungelwe ngamanzi, latshabalala: Kodwa amazulu nomhlaba, akhoyo ngoku, ngalo elo lizwi agciniwe, abekelwe umlilo nxamnye nomhla womgwebo nentshabalalo yabantu abangendawo. Kwaye ke ndibhengeza kwakhona ukuba umhla womlilo nomgwebo nentshabalalo yabantu abangendawo ulapha, kanye njengoko kwathenjiswayo mandulo, kwaye kuya kuba njalo ngesiquphe.

Kwaye ke ndiyanibuza kwakhona, bantwanana bam, Ingaba uThixo ungumntu ukuba angaxoka? Mna, Yesu Krestu olungileyo, andithembekanga kangangokuba ndinganilibala ndize ndininike ithemba lobuxoki? Ngaba ndinilibele ndaza ndaya kuloba endaweni yoko? Ndiyanibuza zintanda zam, ngaba ndalinikela ithontsi lam lokugqibela legazi ndisentlungwini emnqamlezweni ukuze ndinishiye xa ndinithembise izithembiso ezinkulu kakhulu nezixabisekileyo, zona izithembiso ezinikwe nguYahweh ngokwakhe kwimfihlelo yakhe engunaphakade, yona imfihlelo enkulu kaThixo?

Makube lee ukuba izinto ezinjalo zicingwe kwaye ke chongani iingcinga ezimbi zikaSathana aziphosa kuni, nize nizale nize nizikhalimele nize nithathe indawo yeengcinga ezinjalo ezingento yanto nezimbi ngesibhalo esingcwele, zona izithembiso zikaYahweh ezingo-ewe no-amen. Kodwa kuwo onke amaxesha kwaye ngakumbi amaxesha anje kubhaliwe kwaye ke uPawulos wabhalela abaseKorinte egameni lam: Kodwa ndiyoyika hleze njengoko inyoka yamkhohlisayo uEva ngobuqhetseba bayo, ngokunjalo iingqondo zenu zonakaliswe ekugxileni okukodwa kuKrestu. Kwaye ke ndiyanicebisa bantwana bam, nani endinithanda kakhulu nendinilangazelelayo ukuba nigcine ukugxila kwenu kwengqondo enye kum, nakumazwi am, kuba ndiliLizwi likaThixo elenziwe inyama kwaye lonke iLizwi likaThixo liya kwenzeka ngokwenene, kwaye akukho nelinye lawona mancinane amaZwi am liya kusilela athe athiwa kwaye aphefumlelwa nguMoya oyiNgcwele omkhulu ongunaphakade, onguYahweh, uThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade!

Kwaye ke ndiyanicebisa bantwana bam, nani endinithanda kakhulu nendinilangazelelayo, nani luvuyo lwam nesithsaba sam: phumulani kum nize niphumle kwimisebenzi yam nize niyeke eyenu. Kuba kubhaliwe: Kusekho ke ukuphumla kubantu bakaThixo. Kuba lowo ungenileyo ekuphumleni kwakhe, naye uphumle kwimisebenzi yakhe, njengoko uThixo wenzayo kweyakhe. Zikhuthazeni ke ukuba ningene ekuphumleni kwam, hleze kubekho umntu owayo emva komzekelo ofanayo wokungakholwa. Bantwana bam zikhuthazeni ukuba niyeke imisebenzi yenu nize nenze imisebenzi endininika yona ukuba niyifeze kwaye ningayami kwingqiqo yenu kodwa kuzo zonke iindlela zenu ndivumeni mna, uKrestu Yesu iNkosi yenu enyukileyo, kwaye niya kufumana ukuphumla phakathi kweenduli eziluhlaza zamadlelo nokhuseleko, ngakumbi phantsi kokhathalelo nokhuseleko lwengalo yam enamandla, kuba ndinguYeshua HaMashiach okwangumalusi wenu oyintloko oza kubonakala!

Kwaye ke ndiyanibuza, zintanda zam, ngaba niya kubuyela nihlale phakathi kwabanyelisi abazizidenge abahlekayo baze bafune ukunihendela kwiindlela zabo zokungakholwa? Ngaba niya kuwahoya amazwi am kwanemiqondiso emininzi, imimangaliso nezimanga eziningqongileyo? Ngaba nicinga ngobudenge ukuba ningaqhubeka niphila njengabo bangaziyo ngentlekele nomgwebo ozayo? Ngaba niya kubuyela ekuhlaleni phakathi kwabagwetyiweyo abahambahambayo, abalayo ukuguquka baze bakhethe endaweni yoko umgwebo ongqongqo nesohlwayo? Ndiyanixelela ngokucacileyo ukuba ningazama niya kuzifumana ninjengabangenabuqili abaqhubeka bedlula kwizilumkiso zam baze bohlwaywe ngokungeyomfuneko kwaye ke nilunyukisiwe.

Qondani ukuba ihlabathi enihlala kulo ngoku alifani nanjengoko lalinjalo kwiiveki ezimbalwa ezidlulileyo, kwaye liyaqhubeka litshintsha ngesantya esikhulayo. Qondani ukuba akukho namnye kuni owakhe waphila kwihlabathi elikhoyo ngoku, kuba ihlabathi enihlala kulo ngoku libotshelelwe emgwebeni kwaye kodwa livunyelwe ukuba likhuphe izibhengezo ezibengezelayo zomtshato ozayo emazulwini apho mna Krestu Yesu ndiya kutshata ngonaphakade umtshakazi wam othembekileyo kwaye baya kuhlala benam ngonaphakade. Kwaye njengoko umtshakazi wam neBandla lam behluthiwe ngesiquphe, ke intshabalalo yesiquphe iya kuwela phezu kwabo bonke abanyelisi nabantu ababi abanihleka usulu besithi: Ngomso kuya kufana nanamhlanje, kwaye namhlanje kuyafana nayizolo, kwaye andiyi kubona ntlekele kwaye andinguye umhlolokazi kodwa ndingukumkanikazi olawula phezu kwazo zonke izinto! Ngaphandle kokuba ngesiquphe intshabalalo yabo iya kubafikela njengomfazi onenimba, kwaye abayi kusinda.

Ngoko phumani phakathi kwabo nize nahlukane kuzo ZONKE izinto nakuzo zonke iindlela zenu. Aninakuba nasabelo okanye iqashiso nabo okanye niya kwabelana ngezibetho zabo. Qondani ukuba iziganeko sele ziqhubeka, neziganeko eziya kuchaphazela aboni, kodwa ndiya kubonelela ngokhuseleko kubathembekileyo bam ukuze le migwebo inganenzakalisi bantwana bam. Kuba ezi nimba zokubeleka ezingapheliyo zenzelwe ukuvusa abokugqibela abaleleyo nokwahlula abokugqibela abaya kungena eMkhombeni phambi kokuba intlekele iwe ngesiquphe kwaye ixesha liphele. Anisakwazi ukuphila ubomi obungenankathalo kwaye kufuneka nihambe ngobulumko obukhulu ngoko thobelani izilumkiso zam nize niqonde ukuba ndininike ubulumko obukhulu nokuqonda ukuze nikwazi ukuqonda amaxesha eniphila kuwo, naphakathi kwabo balele ubuthongo obunzulu kwintlekele esondelayo nabaya kubandezeleka ngenxa yoko.

Ngoko qondani ukuba ixesha eliseleyo alibalulekanga kwaye lisebenzela kuphela iinjongo zikaYahweh phambi kokuba umgwebo uwe ngesiquphe. Ewe, utshaba lunikwa ithuba lokulungiselela uhlaselo lwalo kwiBhabhiloni eyiMfihlo kuba lunikwe lona nguYahweh ukuba lufeze oko. Kodwa qondani ngokupheleleyo ukuba uSathana lutshaba oloyisiweyo olubotshelelwe emhlabeni ukuze lugwetywe kwaye luvunyelwe kuphela ukuqhubela phambili iinjongo zikaYahweh kwaye luya kuhlala cwaka lulindele ukuba umthinteli asuswe kwaye alunako ukwenza nto ngaphandle koko luvunyelweyo, ngoko musani ukoyika ukugquma kwaloo ngonyama ingenamazinyo kuba inkosana encinane ibotshelelwe ekoyisweni! Kuba ngenyaniso, abawileyo bayanoyika kwaye bayaqonda kwaye bayazi ukuba abo babekhe babaxhaphaza baya kuba ngabo bazisa umgwebo nesohlwayo esinobulungisa ezifubeni zabo ngonaphakade nanaphakade. Yintoni? Anazi na ukuba niya kugweba iingelosi?

Kwaye ke ndiyanibuza zintanda zam, ngaba niya kulwala ngoku ubabalo lwam nenceba yam olwandiselwe abalahlekileyo, kanye njengoko umgwebo nentlekele zilungiselelwe ukuwa ngesiquphe? Ngaba niyilibele iminyaka emininzi yobabalo nenceba endanipha yona ngokungafanelekanga ukuze nizalisekise izono zenu phambi kokuba niguqukele ebulungiseni? Ngaba niya kubakhanyela abo bafuna ukungena eMkhombeni ukuze simanyane umzuzwana ngaphambili? Ndiqinisekile ngcono ngani, bantwana bam, kwaye ke ndicela umonde wenu nokuzeka kade umsindo, naphambi kokuba intlekele iwe ngesiquphe.

Ukuba kukho nabani na phakathi kwenu ocinga ukuba unesizathu sokuzisa ubulungisa kuSathana nobukumkani bakhe, ndinesizathu esingaphezulu kunokuba sakha sangena ezintliziyweni zenu. Kuba nguwuphi kuni oye walwa noSathana, kude kube lithontsi lenu lokugqibela legazi njengoko nanixhonywe ngokuzithandela kweso sibonda esirhabaxa kwintlungu epheleleyo. Kuba ndawuthwala ngokuzithandela umnqamlezo wam ndaza ndazinikela kuloyiso olungunaphakade njengoko ndaphathwa gadalala ezandleni zabenzi bobubi, njengoko ndathatha indawo yenu emgwebeni kwintlungu enkulu ngaphandle kwamasango phakathi kwabona boni babi kakhulu. Igazi lam lalingenatyala kwaye akukho bubi babunokubekwa ngokufanelekileyo kwi-akhawunti yam. Kodwa eluthandweni lwam ngoBawo naseluthandweni lwam ngani, ndabuncama ngokuzithandela ubomi bam kwaye ndandiya kuphinda ndikwenze oko ngomzuzwana, nangona into enjalo ingafuneki. Ngoko ke nakwintlungu yenu yangoku landelani umzekelo wam wonyamezelo nisazi ukuba ngesiquphe niya kungena kuhlangulo olungunaphakade!

Kwaye ke ndiyanicela ukuba niqhubeke niqinile kude kube sekupheleni, sona isiphelo apho nithathwa ngesiquphe ngokuqhwanyaza kweliso nisiwe emazulwini kumvuzo ongunaphakade nenjongo engunaphakade, kwaye noko abafileyo kuKrestu baya kuvuka kuqala kwaye niya kuzibandakanya nabo ngesiquphe kumbhiyozo ongunaphakade. Kuba iinjongo ezingunaphakade enibizelwe kuzo, enikhethiweyo, enitywinwe, nenigcinelwe zona ziya kuzalisekisa iinjongo ezingunaphakade zikaThixo ophezukonke kwaye konke kuza kutyhilwa kuni bantwana bam, ngoko ndikhanyiseleni njengezibane zam ngoku kule nqumamo yokugqibela phambi kokuba intlekele yesiquphe ibethe umhlaba. Kwaye ndiya kubazisa kuni ukuze nibazise kum, kuba nisengabameli bam kude kube ngesiquphe nithathwa nisiwe emazulwini. Kwaye ngesiquphe kuya kuwela intlekele enkulu.

Qondani ukuba ixesha liphelile kwaye akukho nto inokubuyisela umva iziganeko ezizayo nezizilungelelanisayo nxamnye nabemi bomhlaba, kuba mkhulu umgwebo ozayo waboni. Kwaye ke ndiyacela kwaye ndiyaninyanzela ngayo loo nceba, ubabalo novelwano uYahweh anibonise lona ebusweni bukaYesu Krestu ngenxa yosindiso lwenu. Ngaba ningahlala nithembekile kude kube sekupheleni? Ngaba niya kuzivumela ukuba ningene ekuphumleni kwam okungunaphakade ngaphandle kokuyeka ngomzuzu wokugqibela? Ngaba niya kugxila ngentliziyo yenu epheleleyo nangengqondo yenu epheleleyo kumazwi am nize niqonde ukuba ngesiquphe kuya kutshintsha ngonaphakade kwaye ngesiquphe kungoku.

Akusekho xesha liseleyo lokuba iBhabhiloni eyiMfihlo iguquke kwaye akusekho xesha kwixesha lam lobabalo nenceba kuba ixesha liphelile. Kwaye kwixesha ngalinye kukho ixesha nenjongo kwaye ekuzalisekeni kwamaxesha kuya kuwela intlawulelo yesiquphe phezu kwabakhululweyo ngoko mabathi abakhululweyo bomhlaba abo abakhululeyo esandleni sotshaba. Kwaye ningalivumeli isela ukuba libe uvuyo lwenu kuba nguwuphi umtshakazi olilela usuku lomtshato wakhe, nomhla womtshato ongcwele, ongcwele ongenakukonakalisa okanye akhathaze mntu.

Ngoko thuthuzelani kumaZwi am entuthuzelo nize niyeke ukuphila njengabo baphoswe phantsi ngehlazo netyala. Kuba nina mtshakazi wam, ningabo ndibenze bangabi nabala kwaye bangabi nasiphoso phambi kwetrone kaYahweh, kwaye niyintlahlela yabafi. Nangona abo kuni abaphilayo nabasalayo bengayi kuze bangcamle ukufa, nazalwa nifile ezonweni nasezikonweni. Kwaye niya kuba nisuke ngqo ekufeni (apho ngokufa ngenife), naya ngqo kubomi obungunaphakade enisele niphila isibambiso sabo, sona isitywina esikhulu sikaYahweh enitywinelwe ngaso loo mhla wesiquphe wentlawulelo. Kuba nizelwe ngokutsha ngembewu engenakonakala kwaye ningoonyana bakaThixo ophezukonke, noonyana bakaThixo uSomandla!

Ngoko ke, thuthuzelani nize niphumle nisebenze nam kulo mjikelo woloyiso weminyaka. Kuba akuzange kubekho xesha emhlabeni elinje ngeli kwaye akusayi kuze kubekho xesha elinje ngeli kwakhona. Kwanexesha apho oonyana bakaThixo abangonaphakade balindele ngokwazi umphefumlo wabo wokuqala wobomi obungunaphakade njengeentsana zikanaphakade kubukho obungunaphakade bukaThixo ongunaphakade apho baya kutshata iNkosi yabo engunaphakade kumnqophiso ongunaphakade, kwaye... ngesiquphe, kuya kuba njalo!

Ndingulowo wazinikela kanye, ngokupheleleyo, nanaphakade njengeMvana yePasika egqibeleleyo.

Ngesiquphe, nangokuqhwanyaza kweliso niya kuzifumana nimi kwigumbi letrone likaYahweh kuvuyo olungunaphakade kwaye niya kuqonda ngokupheleleyo nize nazi kanye njengoko niqondwa ngokupheleleyo naziwa. Kwaye ke isithembiso siya kudibana nokuzaliseka kuvuyo olungunaphakade nozuko olungunaphakade olunilindeleyo kwaye konke kuya kuzalisekiswa kuni ngokuqhwanyaza kweliso, kwaye ngesiquphe kuya kuba njalo.
