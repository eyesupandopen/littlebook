Ngaba niya kuzigcina ekuweni kwinkohliso enamandla ngokuthobela ilizwi lam? Okokugqibela, bazalwana bam, yomelelani eNkosini, nasemandleni obungangamsha bayo. Nxibani sonke isikrweqe sikaThixo, ukuze nibe nako ukumelana namaqhinga omtyholi. Ngoko ke qondani zithandwa zam ukuba amazwi am akaguquki, ngoko kutheni mna, uKrestu Yesu iNkosi yenu enyukileyo, ndingaze ndinithumele umlawuli wasemhlabeni, okanye umphathi okanye ukumkani oya kumisa imigwebo ezayo? Angathini uYahweh, ontando yakhe ndiyigqibezela ngokugqibeleleyo kwaye ekungenakwenzeka ukuba axoke, ukuba aziphikise kwizicwangciso zakhe ezingunaphakade ezinikwe kum ngokupheleleyo ukuze ndizigqibezele?

Kuba bonke abalawuli behlabathi bayinike imvume namandla abo inamba, kuba kunikwe bona ukuba bakwenze oko kuba neentshaba zikaYahweh ziyalithobela ilizwi lakhe kwaye ziya kuyizalisekisa intando yakhe abayinikiweyo ngeli xesha lifutshane kakhulu eliza kugaleleka phezu komhlaba ngomsindo omkhulu! Kuba amaxesha omsindo weMvana nomsindo kaThixo uSomandla afikile kwaye akukho nto inokumisa, okanye ithintele nakancinane oko kuza kukhululwa kungekudala emva kokuba ndihlanganise abathembekileyo bam emazulwini. Kwaye ngoko ndiyakhwaza! Ngubani onokumelana nentando engunaphakade kaYahweh, uMkhulu onguMNA NDINGUYE?!

Noko ke, qondani ukuba ngexesha lam lobabalo ndayalela iBandla lam ukuba lithandazele abalawuli balo behlabathi, ndisazi ukuba abalawuli balo behlabathi babekhohlakele ngokwemvelo kwaye ukuze ngokuthethelela kweengcwele ububi buthintelwe ngakumbi (kwaye umthinteli wam usathintela), kwaye aba balawuli babenokukhetha oko kuya kusebenzela okulungileyo nentlalo-ntle yabo bamthandayo uThixo, kwaye lalinjalo ixesha lam lobabalo, nangona iintshutshiso zazininzi. Kwaye nangona, umthandazo oshushu osebenzayo womntu olilungisa unamandla kakhulu, qondani ukuba akukho nkokeli zehlabathi ezinikwe usindiso lomhlaba de ndize nomkhosi omkhulu wooKumkani nababingeleli abangunaPhakade abaya kulawula umhlaba ngobulungisa ngomyalelo wam kuba ndiyiNkosi yeeNkosi noKumkani wooKumkani kwaye ndinguMbingeleli oMkhulu ngonaphakade ngokohlobo lukaMelkitsedeke.

Qondani ngokupheleleyo nangaphandle kokuyekelela, akukho nkokeli zehlabathi ezinokuzisa uxolo emhlabeni. Akukho nkokeli zehlabathi ezinokuzisa ukhuseleko emhlabeni. Akukho nkokeli zehlabathi ezinokubuyisela impumelelo emhlabeni. Nangona, baya kuthembisa uxolo nokhuseleko nempumelelo ebuyiselweyo, qondani ukuba abanamandla okunika izinto abazithembisayo, nangona baya kunika iimpahla ezingenaxabiso zehlabathi kwabo bathatha uphawu lwerhamncwa, nkqu nomvuzo wokungalungisi oya kuthathwa kubo ngokupheleleyo emgwebeni! Ngoko ke musani ukubalandela kuba nabani na olandelayo uya kufuneka ekugqibeleni athathe uphawu lwerhamncwa kwaye abo benjenjalo banikelwe ngonaphakade kumadangatye angacimekiyo, anaphakade esohlwayo kwaye ihlabathi neempahla zalo ezingenaxabiso ziyadlula. Kwaye inamba iya kuvuka ngokupheleleyo namarhamncwa ayo, kwaye irhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni lilungisile ityhefu yalo elawulwa kukulunywa yinyoka ukutofa ityhefu yalo kwaye isinyithi siya kuxutywa nodongwe, kodwa aziyi kunamathelana.

Kwaye kubhaliwe kwaye kwashicilelwa kanye kubungqina bokutyhilwa kwam, nkqu iSiTyhilelo sikaYesu Krestu ukuze bonke abanamehlo okubona neendlebe zokuva, nentliziyo ethambileyo yokuqonda, baqonde, kwaye uYohane wabhala wathi: Yaza ingelosi yathi kum, Yini na ukuba umangale? Ndiya kukuxelela imfihlelo yomfazi, neyerhamncwa elimthweleyo, elineentloko ezisixhenxe neempondo ezilishumi. Irhamncwa owalibonayo, lalikhona, alikho; kwaye liza kunyuka liphuma enzonzobileni, liye entshabalalweni; kwaye abo bahlala emhlabeni baya kumangaliswa amagama abo ayengabhalwanga encwadini yobomi ukusuka ekusekweni kwehlabathi, xa bebona irhamncwa elalikhona, alikho, kwaye likhona.

Ngoko ke, bantwana bam abancinane yamkelani imfundiso kwaye ndiyaninika ukuba niqonde nangona ubukumkani benamba abunakuvuka ngokupheleleyo (kuba umthinteli uya kuthintela de umthinteli angabi sathintela, nkqu naxa abathembekileyo kuKrestu bethathwa basiwa emazulwini) abawileyo bavunyelwe ukuba balungiselele uhlaselo lwabo olothusayo kwaye uThixo uthumela abo bahlala emhlabeni ngokungathobeli inkohliso enamandla. Kodwa ezi zinto azenzelwanga nina bathembekileyo nabanyuliweyo bam, kwaye mna, uYesu HaMashiach ndiyabhengeza kwaye ndiyayingqina le nyaniso ngala mazwi endiwathumele ngomthunywa wam nomkhonzi wam ukuba afundelwe intuthuzelo nentobelo ngabo bonke la mazwi enzelwe bona. Kwaye ndiyabuza kwabo kuni abangakhetha le nkohliso kunenyaniso yam engunaphakade, kutheni nimangalisiwe? Ngaba nisaza kukhetha ubuxoki babalusi abanxebunxebu kunenyaniso nentuthuzelo eziswa ngumoya wesiprofeto?

Ngoko ke, ndiyaninika ukuba niqonde ukuba aba balusi babini banxebunxebu abakhoyo nabangekhoyo kwaye abaza kuba NGOKUPHELELEYO, abakavuki ngokupheleleyo, kwaye baya kuvuka ngokupheleleyo eluthuthwini nasemanxoweni olawulo oludala kwaye ulawulo lwabo olutsha luya kuvuka ngokupheleleyo! Ngoku bazikhethele uMoshiach wabo wobuxoki, nkqu noBharabhas, kwaye uya kwakha itempile yabo, kwaye irhamncwa eliphuma elwandle lisadlala ngesidenge salo esidala kwaye baya kuphumelela, kwaye baya kuzisa intshabalalo epheleleyo yeBhabhiloni eyiMfihlo, kuba ngobuqhetseba balwa imfazwe kwaye injalo imvelo yabo.

Kwaye bathunyelwe sisigcawu esihlasela imihlaba, kwaye sisigcawu esikhethe yena ukuba akhokele imihlaba xa bevuke ngokupheleleyo. Kuba yiNdlu yesikhungu kaSathana eyenze ukhetho lwayo yakhetha uMoshiach wayo omtsha wobuxoki kwaye iBhabhiloni eyiMfihlo iphinde yanika imvume yayo kwinamba, kwaye ngoko baya kunikelwa ngokupheleleyo ezandleni zenamba ebaxhobisayo ukuba benze ingqumbo nomgwebo, umgwebo othunyelwe njengesabelo esiphindwe kabini esivela kuYahweh ngokwakhe, mgwebo lowo ungalibazisiyo kwaye usondele kwaye ngoko bazikhethele intshabalalo yabo.

Kwaye kunjalo ukuba iBhabhiloni eyiMfihlo ngoku ibhengeza ikamva layo ukuba likhululekile kumgwebo kaYahweh njengoko befuna ukubaleka behlekisa ngeendlela zabo zokungakholwa. Kuba iBhabhiloni eyiMfihlo iphothiwe ngokupheleleyo kumnatha wesigcawu, kwaye ayinakubaleka, kwaye iBhabhiloni eyiMfihlo isemaketangeni ilindele umgwebo wayo wokugqibela. Kwaye baya kuqhubeka bephanga, beba, bebulala, bexhwila kwaye bethatha ngamandla impumelelo yayo nenzala yayo kuba kunikwe bona ukuba bakwenze oko kwaye kuya kusala inkqantosi apho baya kuphanga umhlaba ngakumbi ngezixhobo abazinqwenela kangaka.

Kwaye ke intshabalalo yesiquphe iya kuwa ngesiquphe, kwaye ngeyure enye iya kutshatyalaliswa. Ndiphinda ndithi, imfazwe iya kuqhambuka ngesiquphe kuwo wonke umhlaba, kwaye umlilo uya kuwa, kwaye baya kuthumela imijukujelwa emininzi ukuze itshatyalaliswe. Esona sixeko sayo sikhulu siya kutsha kwaye amaza amakhulu entshabalalo aya koyisa unxweme lwayo lwasempuma nentshona. Umhlaba uya kunyikima ngogonyamelo, kwaye uya kwahlulwa phakathi kwaye apho kwakumi intliziyo yelizwe eliphumeleleyo, kuya kusala amanzi aya kuwahlula kubini, kwaye uya kuwa ngokupheleleyo kwisabelo sawo esiphindwe kabini somgwebo. Nkqu nomgwebo abazikhethela wona xa babesityeshela ilizwi lam endalithumela ngabapostile bam nabaprofeti nabakhonzi abakhulu nabancinane: ngenene uwutyeshele omnye nabo bonke ekuzitshabalaliseni kwawo kwaye awusayi kuphinda uvuke. Kwaye ngoko umhlaba unikelwe ezandleni zenamba kwaye iya kunika ulawulo lwayo lwesihogo kubalusi bayo abanxebunxebu ebabavusele iinjongo zayo kwaye bonakele ngonaphakade.
