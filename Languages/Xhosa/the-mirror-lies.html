Ingqina eliMbaxa: uSirayeli
Okukrakra Okumnandi
Disemba 5, 2024

Isipili Siyaxoka!

Sipili, sipili eludongeni, ndixelele ngehlabathi apho izikrini zininzi zithimba bonke! Ngokuphinda-phinda ubuxoki obusoloko buxoxwa, ngemifanekiso exokayo engapheliyo ukuze iingqondo zabo zibunjwe ngenkohliso enkulu ngokuzonwabisa okungamasiko ukuze iigusha zilahlekiswe yimibono ezigcinwe ziingcinga zabo! Nangona kunjalo, bathinjiwe yinkanuko yenyama, nenkanuko yamehlo nekratshi lobomi elivuselelwayo nelidangazeliswayo kulwazi lobuxoki olunikelwa kwinto ekuthiwa yimfundo naphi na apho ubuxoki babo buthengiswa ngobuqhetseba. Ngaloo ndlela, iindlebe ziyanyumbanyumbaziswa zize zithuthuzelwe ngamaza aphindaphindwayo ukuze abanekratshi nabazizidenge balawulwe baze baqhathwe. Kwaye ekuhambeni kwexesha baye bafumanisa ukuba ngemibongo yabantwana elawula ingqondo, indlela yokuziphatha yoluntu iyatshintshwa ngokunjalo kwaye kwikhubalo labo uyabanjwa!

Noko ke lihlabathi elo uhlala kulo, Sirayeli, ihlabathi elilele ngokupheleleyo ebubini, ihlabathi elithinjiweyo nelithabathekileyo ngokupheleleyo ngamalaphu obuxoki anikwayo ukucwangcisa izihlwele kwiimbali zabo zokubulala ezinikwa ngokungapheliyo ngoku zokutshatyalaliswa kuka-Adam. Izibuko, zibonakaliswe kuyo yonke indawo, kodwa ekujongeni kwesipili inyaniso ayikho, kuba inyaniso ayibonwa ngamehlo ayijongileyo kuba izibuko zabo ziyakhohlisa ukuze ziluke inyani yobuxoki eyenziwe bubuchwepheshe babawiweyo obuyilelwe ukukhobokisa uluntu kubuxoki babo ukuze u-Adam afe ngonaphakade.

Izibuko ezimnyama, zibonakaliswe kuyo yonke indawo kwaye zibekwe ukuba ziboniswe naphi na apho amehlo anokuzulazula khona, ukuze izibuko zixele ubuxoki obufanayo obulungelelaniswe yinjongo enobubi, ukuze umntu wemvelo onwatyiswe sisono nokufa kwaye athabatheke zizinto ezininzi nezinkulu ezikhangayo ezipakishwe njengobulumko nempumelelo kodwa endaweni yoko zibangele ukufa nentshabalalo kuba akukho nyaniso kwimifanekiso exokayo abayinikezelayo. Izibuko ezimnyama, zibonakaliswe kwaye ziphathwe ngesandla apho umfanekiso emva komfanekiso wakha ukuphambana nenkohliso enamandla elizweni, apho inyaniso ingahlulwanga kubuxoki, apho bonke abanoyolo ekungabini nalungisa abayi kusinda kodwa baya kufa ngonaphakade emalangatyeni entshabalalo engunaphakade, isohlwayo ababotshelelwe kuso ngonaphakade kwaye akukho ndawo banokubalekela kuyo okanye bazimele kuyo apho ukuphepha umgwebo kunokufunyanwa khona.

Owu, yivani becula! Mamelani nize nive amazwi esihlwele seentaka ezihlekisayo ecula ngamxhelo mnye, ehambelana nemifanekiso exokayo ehambelanayo ebonelelwa zizibuko zazo ezimnyama rhoqo. Zicula kufutshane kakhulu neengoma zeentaka zokwenyani kodwa eneneni eyazo yingoma yomgunyathi exela ubuxoki ukuze amazwi azo ahambelane nokunyaniseka kobuxoki kwiinkohliso zabo ngaloo ndlela zifuna ukuzifihla. Kanti ezi ntaka zihlekisayo zixokayo azinazingoma zazo kodwa zikopa kwaye ziphinda iingoma zeentaka zokwenyani ngaphandle kokubhala okanye ukuqonda amagama akwiingoma eziziculayo, kwaye ngoko ke imifanekiso esasazwa kwizibuko ezimnyama eziyizikrini iyahambelana ukuze ubuxoki buphindaphindwe kwaye izihlwele zikhubazeke ngaloo ndlela. Kwanemibono yobuxoki kunye nenyani exokayo apho umhlaba wonke uthathwe kubuxoki babawiweyo, inyani yobuxoki eye yafika emhlabeni ukuze yande kuphela njengoko ndisusa umthinteli wam xa ndihlanganisela abathembekileyo kuKrestu kukhuseleko lwamazulu ngequbuliso nangokungalindelekanga ngoku.

Ngoko ke, yivani amazwi kaYeshua HaMashiach niliqonde ihlabathi eniphila kulo nenkohliso evunyelweyo ukuba ibambe umhlaba ngengalo yawo exokayo. Inkohliso enikwe emgwebeni apho abawiweyo bavunyelweyo ukuba babe nolawulo lwabo, kwaye kungenxa yokuba umntu wemvelo unqwenela ukujoyina ooSathana kwimvukelo yabo ngenxa yomvuzo wobugwenxa, ukuze abe noyolo ekungabini nalungisa kude kube sekufeni kwakhe, kwaye ngoko ke umntu wemvelo ngoku uya kunikelwa ezandleni zeentshaba zakhe, kodwa nangona kunjalo isihlwele siya kusindiswa nangomlilo wocikido. Kwaye ukwahlukana okungakumbi nokupheleleyo kuya kwenzeka ngexesha lembandezelo kaYakobi, ukwahlukana apho intsalela yakwaSirayeli ikhululwa ebukhobokeni buka-Esawu kwaye ikhululelwe kubukumkani bam bewaka leminyaka obuza kumiselwa kungekudala emhlabeni!

Noko ke, umntu wemvelo ukhetha inyani yobuxoki yabawiweyo apho bafuna ukufihla uYahweh (nto leyo ukuyifihla kungenakwenzeka), kodwa yinkohliso leyo enamandla abayisasaze emhlabeni apho bonke abamkhanyelayo uYesu waseNazarete, uYeshua HaMashiach, bakhobokiswe ngayo. Ngoko ke, yivani amazwi kaYeshua HaMashiach, ukuze niliqonde ihlabathi eniphila kulo nenkohliso enamandla ebambe umhlaba ngokuwubamba kwayo. Qonda, Sirayeli, ukuba mna, Yeshua HaMashiach okwanguKrestu Yesu ndingulowo uya kususa ubumfama bakho obuyinxalenye xa uguqula iintliziyo zakho ngokuthobela ngokupheleleyo isandi selizwi lam, elithethwa kwimilebe ethembekileyo yabantu abathembekileyo abaphila ngokholo kuNyana kaYahweh, kuba olilungisa uya kuphila ngokholo!

Yintoni le ibikho? Yiyo leyo iya kubakho: kwaye yintoni le yenziweyo? Yiyo leyo iya kwenziwa kwakhona: kwaye akukho nto intsha phantsi kwelanga. Ngaba ikhona into, umntu anokuthi ngayo, Yabona, intsha le? Sele ikho kumaxesha amandulo awayengaphambi kwethu. Akukho nkumbulo yeli xesha langaphambili, kwaye akuyi kubakho nkumbulo yexesha lasemva eliya kubakho, kunye nabo baya kuza emva koko. Inene, inene, kuya kufika ixesha apho abawiweyo abayi kukhunjulwa, bengayi kungena engqondweni kwaye iimfihlelo zabo zokuxoka ziya kuphela ngonaphakade xa bephoswa emalangatyeni.

Ngoko ke, buqondeni ubuxoki bamandulo obunikezelwe ngabawiweyo eluntwini kwihlabathi elalikhona ngaphambi kukazamcolo omkhulu kaNowa, kwala buxoki bunye buphinda bupakishwe kwaye busetyenziselwe ukutshabalalisa uluntu kwakhona. Ngoko ke qondani, abawiweyo bazisa ubuchwepheshe babo nobuqhetseba babo nemiqondiso yobuxoki nemimangaliso ababeyisebenzisa ukuthakatha nokutshabalalisa uluntu ngemihla kaYerede. Kwaye umhlaba uya kubuyela njengoko wawunjalo ngemihla kaNowa kwaye iya kuba njalo imbandezelo kaYakobi, ixesha emhlabeni elilawulwa ziintshaba zikaYahweh. Kwanaleyo namba inkulu yamandulo ngoku ebotshelelwe emhlabeni ukuze igwetywe, kwaye amasikizi amaninzi aya kukhululwa kwihlabathi labantu; kanye njengoko uYahweh evakalisile ukuba uya kuzisa iingxilimbela ukuze azalisekise ingqumbo yakhe.

Kuba oku bakutyeshela ngabom, ukuba ngelizwi likaThixo amazulu ayekho kwakudala, nomhlaba umi uphuma emanzini, nasemanzini, apho ihlabathi elalikhona ngoko, lagutyungelwa ngamanzi ngogonyamelo, latshabalala. Kodwa amazulu nomhlaba okhoyo ngoku, ngaLowo Lizwi linye agciniwe, abekelwe umlilo nxamnye nomhla womgwebo, nentshabalaliso yabantu abangamhloneliyo uThixo.

Ngelishwa, izihlwele ezininzi, ezininzi emhlabeni azazi ngabom ukuba ihlabathi elalikhona ngoko uYahweh walikhukulisa ngogonyamelo laza latshabalala ngenxa yamaNefili namasikizi awo amaninzi; kwaye ngenxa yabawiweyo abazenza oothixo xa bonakalisa iindlela zoluntu emhlabeni ngeemfihlelo zabo ezingenaxabiso ezifundiswa uluntu ukuze litshabalale. Ngoko yivani inyaniso, niwaqonde amazwi am, nilive ilizwi likaYeshua HaMashiach ukuze niyiqonde inyaniso kwaye ningabanjiswa bubuchwepheshe babo kwaye ningaguqulwa luphawu lwerhamncwa nibe lisikizi elibekelwe amadangatye esohlwayo ngonaphakade.

Kuba ubuchwepheshe babo yile nto yenziwe ngaphambili, kwaye ikhona ngoku, kwaye iya kuza ngokupheleleyo kwakhona okokugqibela. Ngokuqinisekileyo, ubuchwepheshe babo buya kutyhilwa ngokupheleleyo njengoko beqhubeka nokukhupha inkqubo yabo yokugqibela yerhamncwa, kunye nayo yonke imiqondiso yobuxoki nemimangaliso enikelwe kwabo batshabalalayo. Kwaye ngoko inamba iya kuvuka ilawule umhlaba ngenkohliso enkulu ngendlela yemvana eneempondo ezimbini.

Kwaye abawiweyo baya kuzityhila njengezidalwa ezinobubele ezivela kumazwe ajikelezayo ngobuxoki kwindalo yabo exokayo yamazwe asoloko ejikeleza ngobuxoki asetyenziswa kumaxesha amandulo. Qonda ukuba ngaphezulu komhlaba kukho amazulu nesibhakabhaka kwanamazulu amazulu apho uYahweh ahlala khona etroneni yakhe, kwaye inene, inene uYahweh ulawula ngokupheleleyo phezu kwayo yonke indalo yakhe awayidala yedwa ekuqaleni ngokwecebo lentando yakhe nangenxa yeenjongo zakhe ezingunaphakade.

Ngoko ke, fundisisa ukuze uliqonde ilizwe lakho njengoko linjalo ngokwenene kwaye uqonde ukuba iiplanethi ziinkwenkwezi ezibhadulayo ezinikelwe imiqondiso namaxesha onyaka kwaye azikho amanye amazwe anezinye iintlobo zobomi, ngoko ke, bukhanyele ubuxoki babawiweyo. Aba kuthiwa ngabaphambukeli ngabo bavukela uYahweh kwasekuqaleni kwaye bangabaphambukeli ngengqiqo yokuba imvelaphi yabo isemazulwini ngomhla abadalwa ngawo begqibelele ngobulungisa nguYahweh ngokwakhe. Noko ke, kwimvukelo yabo bawa kwimfezeko yabo yantlandlolo, kwaye baziguqule ngonaphakade, kwaye abayi kusinda. Noko ke umntu wemvelo ukhetha obu buxoki ukuze azalisekise iinkanuko zenyama yakhe ebolileyo kubo bonke ubugwenxa nokungalungisi, kwaye bonke abahamba kule ndlela ibanzi yentshabalaliso baya kuwela emalangatyeni angunaphakade ngaloo ndlela.

Kwaye ngoko baya kuvela, bezenza ngathi bayayikhathalela imicimbi yoluntu kwaye baya kuzityhila ngokupheleleyo xa umthinteli esusiwe njengoko iBandla lam lihlanganiselwa kum emafini. Baya kuvela bebanga ukuba beze kukhusela uluntu kuhlaselo nokusindisa uluntu emfazweni kwaye baya kuza nobuxoki obuninzi obupakishwe kakuhle nobumnandi. Noko ke, iimpondo ezilishumi zinentliziyo enye kwaye ziya kunika amandla negunya lazo kwirhamncwa! Kwaye iimpondo ezilishumi ngabo baya kulithiya ihenyukazi, balenze kube senkangala kwaye balihlube, badle inyama yalo, balitshise ngomlilo kwimfazwe yabo yesithathu yehlabathi esezakugubungela umhlaba. Kanti baya kuvela nobuxwepheshe babo esandleni, kodwa bazisa kuphela izisombululo zobuxoki neendlela zobuxoki nenkohliso ezikhokelela kumgwebo ongunaphakade nokugwetywa kwabo bonke abajoyina imvukelo yabo.

Noko ke, baya kuvuka, irhamncwa eliphuma elwandle nerhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni, kwaye baya kuvuka njengemvana eneempondo ezimbini ethetha njengenamba, iMvana yeNamba u-Esawu azikhethele yona ukuba ilawule umhlaba ngokungalungisi. Ulawulo apho iintlanga zilawulwa ngqwabalala yi-Ashkenaz nge-Noahide ngerhamncwa eliphuma elwandle kwaye irhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni lilawula umhlaba nelizwe ngeTalmud ka-Esawu ngenkohliso enkulu njengoko ebeka umgibe wakhe kuYakobi.

Qonda ukuba irhamncwa eliphuma elwandle lizikhwebule kwiBhabheli eyiMfihlo ukuze lizikhwebule kulwaphulo-mthetho lwalo oluninzi, kwaye baya kutyhola igama lalo kwaye babonakale besenza izityholo ngalo. Kodwa qonda ukuba irhamncwa eliphuma elwandle nerhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni livela kwiminombo efanayo enesikizi kwaye lisebenza kunye, isibini esirhamncwayo, ukukhonza nokwanelisa iinjongo zenkanuko yenamba. Irhamncwa eliphuma elwandle liya kuvuka ngexesha lalo kwaye liya kuvuka libe ligorha labo elithenjisiweyo, iMahdi yabo abayilindeleyo ngoku. Kwaye liya kufihla imvelo yalo yokwenyani nexesha lalo elidlulileyo nolwaphulo-mthetho lwalo, nangona iinzame ezinjalo zililize kuba sonke isono esakha salenza sibhalwe ngokugqibeleleyo emazulwini. Nangona kunjalo, liya kukhohlisa abo balilandelayo ngezithembiso zalo ezingenanto.

Kwaye njengoko bephuma eluthuthwini lwemfazwe, baya kuzimanyanisa ngokukhululekileyo nenamba nabawiweyo abasele bengabo, kwaye baya kukhawuleza ukuzimisa kubukhosi babo bokugqibela, abasele besibeke isakhelo, kwangaphambi kokuba iBhabheli eyiMfihlo itshatyalaliswe ngendlela yemfihlelo yobugwenxa. Noko ke, baya kufuna ukwakha oko sele kukho njengoko abawiweyo befuna ukumisa ixesha labo elitsha legolide, ixesha legolide abangasoze balifumane kodwa umgwebo ongqongqo endaweni yoko. Nangona kunjalo, baya kuvuka ngamandla amakhulu kwaye irhamncwa elinye liya kukhomba kwelinye kwaye baya kungqinana elinye ngelinye nelinye ngelinye kwaye zininzi izinto ezinkulu nezinyelisayo abaya kuzithetha kwaye mininzi imiqondiso yobuxoki nemimangaliso abaya kuyizisa.

Nangona kunjalo, baya kuvuka ngokokubhaliweyo. Kwaye iMvana eneempondo ezimbini iya kusebenza kunye nenamba kwaye ziya kudibana zibe yinto enye kuzo zonke izinto kuluntu lwazo olutsha. Ziya kudibanisa iinkqubo zazo zemali, ziya kudibanisa oorhulumente bazo, ziya kubopha zonke izinto zoluntu lwazo olutsha kunye kwaye zidityaniswe njengento enye kulungelelwaniso lwazo olutsha. Ziya kudibanisa iinkolo zazo eziyimfihlakalo ezinyelisayo zamandulo zibe yinkolo entsha eya kubonakala ngathi yinkolo yooYise babo abanqula iityhubhu zabo ezimnyama, kodwa ziya kudibanisa iinkolo zazo kwenye inkolo yobuxoki ooYise babo ababengayazi kodwa iityhubhu zabo ziya kuhlala. (Noko ke, akayi kumhlonela uThixo wooyise, nokunqwenelwa ngabafazi, akayi kukhathalela nawuphi na uThixo: kuba uya kuzikhulisa ngaphezu kwabo bonke).

Qondani into abaza kuyenza, O' Sirayeli, kuba baza kulwa nabo bangcwele baze baboyise, kwaye bonke abanqula igama likaYesu waseNazarete onguYeshua HaMashiach kude kube sekupheleni baya kusindiswa, kuba kuthenjisiwe kwaza kwabhalwa. Baza kutyhola abo banqula igama likaYesu ngolwaphulo-mthetho lwabo kwaye uBharabhas uza kungcatsha abangcwele beBhabhiloni eyiMfihlo kuba uninzi lwabo lunikele iiCawa zabo ngokungazi kwirhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni, elingekafiki ngexesha lokubhalwa koku. Kwaye lizenze iNkosi yabo laza lathengisa iiBhayibhile egameni lalo, kodwa liyavuma ukuba alingomKristu, nangona abaninzi abaziziyatha bekholelwa ukuba linjalo.

Kwaye njengoko umhlaba ujikeleza uphuma kulawulo uze ungene emfazweni enkulu, nakwindyikitya yokufa, nakwindlala, nakwintswela-bulungisa nasekufeni, baza kutyhola zonke izinto zikaKristu ngeengxaki zabo, abazibangele bona, kwaye baza kuthi abaphambukeli bathathe abathembekileyo abahlanganiseneyo kuKristu, kanti inene ndiya kuba ndibahlanganisele kwigumbi letroni likaYahweh ngenxa yomtshato womyeni nomtshakazi wakhe, ukuze bafumane ilifa labo baze bazukiswe ngonaphakade. Kodwa emhlabeni baza kuxoka kakhulu kwaye baza kuyila izinto ezininzi, ezininzi kwizibuko zabo ezimnyama besebenzisa ubuqhetseba nobuchwepheshe babo bawiweyo kwaye baza kusasaza imiqondiso emininzi yobuxoki nemimangaliso kwizikrini zabo eziya kukholelwa lula zizihlwele.

Qondani ukuba abo bahlala ezixekweni zabo nabo bamkela uphawu lwerhamncwa baza kutshintshwa ngonaphakade kwaye baza kudibanisa isinyithi nodongwe kodwa abayi kunamathelana. Abo bamkela uphawu lwabo baza kutshintshwa baze babe ngamakhoboka engqondo yabo edibeneyo nakwi-intanethi yezinto ezizayo kwaye baza kuba sebukhobokeni bokwenza ukuthanda kwenamba, abaza kuzibophelela kuyo ngonaphakade kuba baza kuba bezitshintshile kumfanekiso kaYahweh abadalwa ngawo. Kwaye bonke abenza izinto ezinjalo abasoze baxolelwe, kuba oko kugwenxa akunakulungiswa.

Ngoko ke qondani ukuba uluntu lwabo olutsha luza kusekelwa ngokupheleleyo kwinkohliso nakubuxoki kwaye abemi bomhlaba baza kwahlulwa ngokupheleleyo kwinkohliso kumhlaba wokwenene nakwinyani abaza kuphila kuyo. Umhlaba wonke uza kubotshwa ngenkohliso enkulu kwaye izihlwele ziya kulandela irhamncwa zimangalisiwe zisithi, ngubani onjengerhamncwa kwaye ngubani onokulwa nalo? Kwaye abantu abaninzi, abaninzi baza kuwa emalangatyeni ngokuwela kubuxoki babo kwaye kwanaba bantu amagama abo angazange abhalwe encwadini yobomi kwasekusekweni kwehlabathi baza kuwa ngonaphakade kuba baza kuba bemele uYahweh ngokupheleleyo ngokwala uYesu uKristu onguYeshua HaMashiach.

Oku kwala kuza kuba kokokugqibela kwaye abayi kuxolelwa kuba baye bajonga amazwi osindiso awayeza kubasindisa ngokupheleleyo njengento engangcwele baza bagqiba kwelokuba inyaniso kaThixo bubuxoki, kwanasono abangayi kuphinda bachache kuso. Kodwa kuya kuba njalo ukwahlukana kokugqibela kwamaxesha ukuze kungabikho nto imbi ingena kuBukumkani bam bewaka leminyaka kodwa kuphela abo beza kuYeshua HaMashiach onguYesu uKristu ngenguquko ngamaxesha osindiso kuba akukho mntu unokuza kuYahweh ngalo naliphi na elinye igama kwaye akukho enye indlela, kuba mna ndiyindlela eyinyani nephilayo.

La ngamaxesha eniphila kuwo, O' Sirayeli, kwaye aninakuhlala ezixekweni zabo kwaye aninakuzimanya noluntu lwabo kuba ukuzimanya nabo kufuna uphawu kwaye bonke abamkela uphawu lwerhamncwa abasoze baxolelwe. Nangona kunjalo, uluntu lufundiswe ngemfundo yalo exatyisiweyo yobuxoki, kwanemfundiso ethengiswa njengemfundo kwaye izihlwele ziqheliswe ngayo, kwaye ziya kwamkela ubuxoki babo kwaye uninzi lwabantu bendalo luya kuzimanya nabo bawiweyo ngonaphakade.

Kodwa kuya kuba njalo, ukuba bonke abakholelwa ngabom kubuxoki kwaye bayakonwabela ukungalungisi banokubotshwa kwisohlwayo esingunaphakade ngonaphakade ngokuzimanya nabo bawiweyo, kwaye bonke abazimanya nabo bawiweyo baya kwabelana ngokupheleleyo kwilifa labo elinikwa ngaphandle kwenceba kuba ababonisanga nceba kodwa kuphela intiyo ekhohlakeleyo kuzo zonke izinto zikaYahweh.

Ndim oza kutshabalalisa inkohliso enkulu kunye nenyani yobuxoki yabo bawiweyo, kwaye indalo iya kukhululwa ingene kwinkululeko ezukileyo yenyaniso nobulungisa, ingene kwinkululeko ezukileyo yoonyana bakaYahweh.

Ndim obiza abo bakwaSirayeli, kwaye ngokwenene bonke abakwa-Adam elusindisweni ukuze baxolelaniswe ngokupheleleyo noYahweh ngegazi lemvana, kuba ndinjalo.

NdinguKristu Yesu iNkosi yenu enyukileyo nevusiweyo kwaye ndenziwe umoya onika ubomi ukuze bonke abeza kum ngentliziyo evulekileyo nobuso obuvulekileyo beze ngokupheleleyo elusindisweni kwiNkosi yabo engunaphakade!

Ngoko ke, qondani amaxesha okungcatsha eniphila kuwo apho umhlaba wonke uya kuthwalwa ngobugeza nenkohliso kwaye ububi buya kukhululwa ngokupheleleyo kwaye kodwa isihlwele esikhulu, esikhulu siya kufumana usindiso olungunaphakade ngokunqula igama leNkosi yabo uYesu kude kube sekupheleni. Kwaye ngokunjalo mna, Yeshua HaMashiach onguMesiya wokwenene kaYakobi, ndiya kusindisa insali enamandla yakwaSirayeli eya kuthatyathwa inikwe ukungena kuBukumkani bam bewaka leminyaka apho zonke izithembiso ziya kuba ngu-ewe no-amen!

Ngoko ke, O' Sirayeli, bukhabeni ubugcisa babo kwaye ningaqhubeki nokuthakathwa zizibuko zabo ezimnyama, kwanazikrini zabo apho abantu ngoku bangamakhoboka njengoko bechitha ubomi babo kwinto engekhoyo besuka kwisikrini besiya kwisikrini njengoko bethinjiwe kwaye bonwatyiswa sisono. Kwanenyani yobuxoki ebonakala iyinyani kodwa eneneni, iyimbali yobuxoki kwaye ayinto yimbi ngaphandle kweentsomi ezilukwe ngobuchule ezijoliswe ekutshatyalalisweni koluntu.

Ngoko ke, phumani phakathi kwabo nahlukane nabo ezintweni zonke. Zihlambululeni egazini lam lentlawulelo ngokuvuma zonke izono zenu kum kwaye nizihlambulule ngelizwi lam elingcwele, kwanamazwi kaYahweh, kuba ndiliLizwi elenziwa inyama elihlala phakathi kwenu kwaye ngokwenene, ngaphakathi kwenu ngomoya wam wesithembiso ukuba nithe nathobela amazwi am.

Ndiyanithanda bantu bakuthi, kwaye ngokwenene ndiyanilwela kwaye ndiya kunihlangula ngokupheleleyo ukuba anityhafi ezinkathazweni zenu kodwa niphumle emandleni am nakumandla am, kuba ndiza ngegunya elipheleleyo likaYahweh Omkhulu Ndinguye owabelane ngetroni yakhe de iintshaba zam zenziwe isihlalo seenyawo zam kwaye ulawulo lwam alunasiphelo kwaye ndiya kubasindisa abathobelayo ngokupheleleyo eluthandweni olungunaphakade naselusindisweni kwaye niya kuba ngabantu abakhethekileyo kwakhona, O' Sirayeli, Selah no-Amen!
