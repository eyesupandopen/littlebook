Yizani Ngokupheleleyo KwiNkosi Yenu Engunaphakade!

Yivani, yivani! Yivani ilizwi leNkosi yenu nize nive amazwi kaYeshua HaMashiach nithobe iintliziyo zenu ekuthobeleni intando yeNkosi yenu, nivumele amazwi am ukuba athuthuzele imiphefumlo yenu. Ngoko ke ndinibuza umbuzo? Ingaba niyaphulaphula na? Xa athe weza uNyana woMntu, ndolufumana na ukholo emhlabeni? Inene, inene, ndiya kulufumana ukholo phakathi kwabathembekileyo bam endiya kubathabathela kukhuseleko lwamazulu ngenxa yokuthembeka kwabo, kodwa uninzi lwabo basemhlabeni abanakholo. Ngoko ke ndiyaqhubeka ndinilumkisa zithandwa zam ukuba nimke ezonweni zaboni, niphume phakathi kweendlela zabo nahlukane nabo, hleze nibe nenxaxheba kwizigwebo zabo.

Noko ke, kunjalo, ukuba baninzi abantu abasondela kum ngokuhanahanisa ngemilebe yabo logama iintliziyo zabo zikude kum. Ngaloo mini baninzi abaya kuthi kum Nkosi Nkosi, ndize ndithi kubo, mkani kum andizanga ndinazi! Qondani ukuba akwanelanga ukundibiza ngokuba ndiyiNkosi ngentliziyo engenakholo, kodwa abo bandenza iNkosi ngokwenene ngabo baguqukayo bashiye izono zabo baze bafune ukuphila ngobulungisa phambi kwam. Anazi na ukuba mna, Yesu waseNazarete, ndingakhetha intliziyo yenu iphela eluthandweni nasekunikezeleni kunemibingelelo yemilebe yenu? Ewe, ndingathanda inceba kunombingelelo, kuba inxalenye yenceba yam, ukubanjwa komsindo ofanelekileyo, lubonakalisa uthando lwam, uthando lukaYahweh uThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade! Noko ke amazwi am esilumkiso athotyelwa ngabo bandithandayo nabasabelele ngenguquko eluthandweni lwam ngabo njengoko ndizityhilile kubo ukuze basindiswe. Kwaye kunjalo ke, uthando lwam lwamkelwa phakathi kwabo iintliziyo zabo zithambileyo kwilizwi lam, kwaye, ngokwenene, kubo bonke abandithandayo nabo ndibayalileyo ukuba bafunde ngam, kwanabo ndibakhokelela kwiindlela zam zosindiso.

Kutheni na nivumela iintliziyo zenu ukuba zinihlupha, bantwanana bam? Abapostile bam nabaprofeti abazanga balumkise ngale mini kusukela ekuqaleni kwexesha? Abaprofeti bamandulo abaxela kwangaphambili ngeveki yamashumi asixhenxe kaDaniyeli ezayo ngomgwebo omkhulu emhlabeni? Ngoko ke, qondani, niphila kwiintsuku ezingaphambi kokuba inkathazo kaYakobi iqhambuke ngokupheleleyo kuwo wonke umhlaba kwaye ngezi ntsuku kuye kwaxelwa kwangaphambili ngeentlekele ezininzi, imiqondiso, imimangaliso, izimanga, kwanemigwebo, kwaye ngoku zikhululwe, ngoko kutheni nimangalisiwe? Ezi zizilumkiso zokugqibela kumlinganiselo omncinci wento ezayo kuwo wonke umhlaba xa abathembekileyo bam bemkile kum besiya emazulwini besuka emhlabeni njengoko ndibahlanganisa njengamathole esitali, kwaye baya kufumana ukhuseleko olupheleleyo nokwaneliseka njengoko bemi kwimizuzu yabo yokuqala bezalwe ngokutsha kunaphakade, apho bangasayi kuze batshabalale, bangabandezeleki, okanye banyamezele ukugula, kodwa baya kuba novuyo olugqibeleleyo nemvisiswano egqibeleleyo noYahweh, kwaye ngokwenene nam Yeshua HaMashiach kwaye ndiya kubanika umvuzo omkhulu nelifa, kuba ndiphethe umvuzo wabo esandleni sam esinamandla.

Baninzi kakhulu abathembekileyo bam abaye bafakwa igwele leemfundiso zobuxoki zevangeli yempumelelo eshunyayelwa ngempazamo ngabaprofeti nabafundisi bobuxoki abaxokisela abantu bam, bebaxelela ukuba intlekele ayisayi kuze ibachukumise lo gama becinga iingcinga ezonwabisayo. La maqula angenamanzi ashumayela omnye uYesu kwaye abamazi mna, Yeshua HaMashiach, kwaye nam andibathumanga. Soloko nithelekisa amazwi abo bathi bathetha egameni lam nesibhalo kwaye apho baphikisana nesibhalo kulapho bathetha khona ngeentliziyo zabo ukuze bazuze ngokubawa kwabo.

Inene, inene baninzi abalungiseleli bobuxoki abahlwayele ubuxoki phakathi kwamabandla am kwaye ivangeli yobuxoki yempumelelo ikhokelela kwindlela ebanzi yentshabalaliso apho amasango esihogo aya kuvuleka kakhulu kwabo balandelayo ukuya kwelo chibi lomlilo livutha ngonaphakade elivutha awona malangatye ashushu kakhulu aluhlaza okwesibhakabhaka alungiselelwe abawileyo nabo babalandelayo, ngoko ke musani ukukhohliswa bubuxoki babo! Andithethi ngabaprofeti bam abathembekileyo abanokuthi baphazame bathethe ilizwi egameni lam, okanye abo benza impazamo enyanisekileyo baze bazilungise. Qondani ukuba akukho namnye kubaprofeti bam oprofeta ngokupheleleyo, kodwa bonke abaprofeti bam baprofeta ngenxalenye kwaye abasayi kuze baphikisane nesibhalo xa bethetha ngokwenene egameni lam, mna Krestu Yesu, kuba ndililizwi likaThixo elenziwe inyama elihleli phakathi kwenu, nangona ngokwenyama andisaziwa.

Bantwanana bam musani ukukhohliswa kuba kweli hlabathi kuhlala kukho intshutshiso nosizi, ngakumbi ngoku ekupheleni kobukumkani bukaSathana njengoko abawileyo besiza emgwebeni kwaye njengoko belungiselela ukwenza oko bavunyelweyo ukuba bakwenze emgwebeni wabo kuba nezitha zikaYahweh ziyalithobela ilizwi lakhe. Anifundanga na kwabaseRoma apho ndayalela uPawulos kwiindlela zam? Ngubani na oya kusahlula eluthandweni lukaKrestu? Yimbandezelo na, luxinzelelo na, yintshutshiso na, yindlala na, bubuze na, yingozi na, likrele na? (Njengoko kubhaliwe kwathiwa, Ngenxa yakho sibulawa imini yonke, Sibaliwe njengezimvu zokuxhelwa.) Hayi, kuzo zonke ezi zinto singaphezu kwaboyisi, ngaye owasithandayo. Kuba ndeyisekile kukuba, nakufa, nabomi, nazithunywa zezulu, nazilawuli, naziphathamandla, nanto zikhoyo, nanto zizayo, nabuphakamo, nabunzulu, naso nasiphi na esinye isidalwa, asiyi kuba nako ukusahlula eluthandweni lukaThixo, olukuKrestu Yesu iNkosi yethu.

Kwaye ngoko abanyulwa bam baye bahlupheka ngezinto ezininzi ngenxa yegama lam kwaye kuye kwakho isihlwele esikhulu sabafel' ukholo kwixesha lam lobabalo abaye banikela ngobomi babo kwinkonzo yokuqhubela phambili ivangeli yosindiso, kwanayo kanye ivangeli eye yasindisa imiphefumlo yenu ngokholo kuYesu waseNazarete kuba inxalenye yayo yayikukuba ngenxa yomsebenzi wabafel' ukholo bam ivangeli yosindiso yagubungela umhlaba. Noko ke, uninzi lwenu luye lwafakwa igwele legwele leemfundiso zobuxoki ezahlukeneyo zokuyibiza nokuyifumana eziye zagubungela iBandla lam laseLawodike.

Ezi mfundiso zimbi aziveli kum kwaye iingcinga zenu azenzi nto ngaphandle kwenu ngaphandle kokuba nithetha amazwi am ngemilebe yokholo xa ndinika imiqondiso, imimangaliso, nezimanga ukuba zenziwe ngezandla zenu. Ngoko ke aninakuzicingela niphuma kwiingxaki nakwiintshutshiso, kwaye nababiziweyo bam, abanyulwa nabathembekileyo abakhululekanga kwintlekele. Kuphi na eLizwini likaThixo apho kuthiwa nabani na okholwayo kum akasayi kuze abandezeleke yimbandezelo, okanye luxinzelelo, okanye yintshutshiso, okanye yindlala, okanye bubuze, okanye yingozi, okanye likrele? Ngokuchaseneyo ezinye okanye zonke ezi ziqinisekisiwe kubo bonke abaphila ngokuthembeka kum, ngoko ke ndinicebisa ukuba niwalahle amazwi abo kuba amazwi abo ngamabali ayilwe ngobuqhetseba athunyelwe ngabawileyo ukuze baguqule kanye ukholo lwabanyulwa ukuba into enjalo ibinokwenzeka. Noko ke, qondani ukuba bonke abathembekileyo bam abandilandela ngentliziyo yabo iphela bayasindiswa kumsindo weMvana nakumsindo kaThixo kwaye baya kusiwa kukhuseleko kwigumbi letроне likaYahweh phambi kokuba umgwebo wehle ngokupheleleyo emhlabeni.

Kwaye kunjalo ke bantwana bam ukuba uninzi lwenu luya kubandezeleka ngeentlekele ezahlukeneyo neentshutshiso eziziswa ngabawileyo kwaye abaninzi baya kulahlekelwa yinzuzo yeli hlabathi kule meko yokugqibela phambi kokuba ndihlanganise abathembekileyo bam. Ngomzuzwana ngamnye odlulayo, ukubonakala kwam kusondele ngakumbi. Ngoko ke ndiyanikhuthaza, zithandwa zam, ukuba nimele mna nibambelele eluthandweni lwam njengamandla enu akhuthazayo okunyamezela oko kufunekayo ukuze nizise usindiso kwabalahlekileyo njengoko nibakhokelela eMkhombeni phambi kokuba intlekele ifike.

Qondani oko ndamnika uPawulos ukuba akubhalele abaseKolose: Ngoku ndiyavuya ngeentsizi zam endizivayo ngenxa yenu, ndizalisekise oko kuseleyo kwiinkanuko zikaKrestu enyameni yam ngenxa yomzimba wakhe, olibandla, endenziwe umphathiswa walo ngokommiselo kaThixo, lowo mmiselo ndawunikelwayo kuni, ukuze ndizalisekise ilizwi likaThixo. Kunjalo ke, zithandwa zam, ukuba xa niva ubunzima ngenxa yegama lam, niyaqhubeka kwaye nibe nenxaxheba kwiintsizi zam endazinyamezelayo eKalvari. Nangona, andishiyanga nto ingenziwanga elusindisweni lwenu, kwaye ndiyiPasika egqibeleleyo nephilileyo yalo lonke ixesha, ndibize ngamnye wenu ukuba anyamezele izinto ezininzi ngenxa yegama lam, ngoko ke kubaleleni ekubeni luvuyo olukhulu! Yaye yazini bantwana bam ukuba iliso alikabonanga, nendlebe ayikavanga, kananjalo akukhange kungene ezintliziyweni zabantu izinto uThixo azilungiselele abo bamthandayo. Ngoko ke balekani, balekani ukuze nizuze! Kuba uThixo akalilo itshinga, ukuba angawulibala umsebenzi wenu, nombulaleko wothando, enalubonisayo kulo iGama lakhe, ekubeni nakhonzayo kwabaNgcwele, nisakhonza.

Uninzi lwenu oluye lwalahlekiswa kulindelo lwenu kangangokuba anisaqondi ukuba amaxesha amaninzi ababiziweyo bam, abanyulwa, nabathembekileyo banyamezela ngokuzithandela ubunzima, ukuthotywa, ilahleko, nokunye okuninzi, kwaye isiphumo sokunyamezela kwabo kwiintsizi zikaKrestu lusindiso kwabalahlekileyo nomvuzo omkhulu nembuyekezo kwabo bazincamayo ngaloo ndlela. Anifundanga na kwiZenzo apho uPawulos noSilas banyamezela ubunzima obukhulu eMakedoni, bebethwa kabuhlungu baze baphoswa entolongweni ngokungekho sikweni bavalelwa esitokisini eFilipi?

Noko ke ndathumela inyikima ukuba ibakhulule kwiimbophelelo zabo, ngoko ke umgcini-ntolongo waseFilipi nendlu yakhe bazuza usindiso ngokunyamezela kukaPawulos noSilas. Kwaye kuninzi lwabo bandilandelayo, baya kunyamezela ubunzima nentshutshiso nembandezelo. Kodwa kuzo zonke ezi zinto ningaphezu kwaboyisi kwaye ndiyanilwela bantwana bam, ukuba nje nihlala nithobela ilizwi lam kwaye nilandela intando yam ngentliziyo yenu iphela kwaye ndiya kunenza noyise zonke izinto ukuba nje nihlala nithembekile kwilizwi lam kwaye nindivumele ndikhokele amanyathelo enu, kuba usindiso lwenu lusekuthobeleni umsindisi wenu ongunaphakade.

Ngoko ke ndinicebisa, zithandwa zam, ukuba nimele mna kwaye ninike uthando lwam kwihlabathi elifayo neligwetyiweyo elimi ngamatyathanga njengoko umgwebo sele uza kuwa ngokupheleleyo. Ndiya kunivumela na ukuba ndincede noyise ukudimazeka ukuze nibambe ithemba lenu engqondweni? Niwalibele na amazwi am, zithandwa zam? Ke ngoko, kumi ukholo, ithemba, uthando, kodwa olona lukhulu kuzo luthando. Ngoko ke ndinithuma ukuba nithande abalahlekileyo, kuba kukulunga kukaThixo okukhokelela abantu enguqukweni amaxesha amaninzi.

Ngoko ke, ndikhonzeni kwiindawo zenu, thandazelani abo balahlekelwe yiyo yonke into kuba ngelixa besaphefumla kusenokwenzeka ukuba ilahleko yeli hlabathi iya kubangela ukuba bazuze unaphakade. Shumayelani kuboni nize nizise usindiso kwabalahlekileyo kwaye nikhumbule ukuba ningabathunywa bam zithandwa zam kwaye nizalisekise ubizo lwenu kum kwaye nihlale niqinile kule meko yokugqibela yamaxesha. Ngoko ke ndinithuma ukuba nithande abamelwane benu kwaye nibonelele ngesondlo noncedo kwabo basweleyo. Kwanamanzi okusela okanye isitya sokutya, nikani nantoni na eninokuyinika ukuze nincede abo babandezelekayo ningathandabuzi nto. Kwaye ukuba kwenzeka ukuba nibandezeleke kunye nabalahlekileyo ke bathandeni ngokupheleleyo kwaye nibonise ubungqina bam namandla am kwaye nithethe egameni lam ngemilebe yokukholwa evela entliziyweni ezaliswe lukholo, ithemba, nothando!

Ngoko ke ndiyanibuza, ingaba niyoyika na ukunceda aboni? Aniqondi na ukuba xa ndandingathunyelwanga ngaphandle kwezimvu ezilahlekileyo zendlu kaSirayeli ndachitha ixesha lam nabaqokeleli berhafu naboni? Nililibele na ukuba nanikhe naphila ubomi besono esiqhelekileyo? Nililibele na ukuba ndim ndedwa umntu ongenasono kwanemvana kaThixo? Ngoko ke mabathi abakhululwa beNkosi abo ndibahlawuleleyo esandleni sotshaba. Thandani, thandani, thandani kude kube sekuphefumleni kwenu kokugqibela kuba kungcono ukufa ukhonza eluthandweni lukaKrestu kunokushwabana ngenxa yoloyiko kwimizamo elusizi yezinto ezibuthathaka nezilambathayo, endizoyisile ngokupheleleyo ndaza ndanika uloyiso kubo bonke abandenza iNkosi yabo engunaphakade. Ngoko ke musani ukubuthanda ubomi benu kude kube sekupheleni kwaye nilandele umzekelo wam wenkonzo engenaloyiko phambi kobubi nokufa okuye kwoyiswa ngokupheleleyo luthando olungunaphakade lukaThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade, kwanaye uEl Shaddai!

Musa ukoyika, kuba ndinawe: musa ukunkwantya, kuba ndinguThixo wakho: ndiya kukomeleza, ndikuncede, ndikuxhase ngesandla sasekunene sobulungisa bam. Ngoko ke yizani kum bantwana bam nize niphumle eluthandweni lwam ngani kwaye ndiya kuthuthuzela imiphefumlo yenu ndiphilise amanxeba enu kwaye ndiya kunomeleza ekwaphukeni kwenu ukuze nibe nento eninokuyinika abo banifunayo kuba nithunyelwe njalo. Kuba nini bathunywa bam abanganyulwanga kuphela kwaye babizelwa ebuqaqawulini boBukumkani bam, kodwa nokuba nandezeleke ngenxa yegama lam.

Inene, inene, ningabahlanganyeli beentsizi zam, landelani umzekelo wam kuphela kwaye ningatyhafi kwiyure yenu yokuphelelwa lithemba. Kuba ndinani onke amanyathelo endlela, kwaye ndim, iNkosi uYesu Krestu ohlanza imiphefumlo yenu emlilweni wam wokucikida, nangona nizizitya zodongwe ezandleni zombumbi, imililo yokucikida iya kwenza ubuso benu bube njengeqhwitha ukuze ningaphulwa emandleni am kwaye noko kusebuthathakeni benyama yenu. Noko ke, kunjalo ukuba ebuthathakeni benyama enesono uthando lwam luyenziwa lugqibelele kuni njengoko ndinitsalela kufutshane nam kwaye ndinilungiselela ngakumbi iinjongo zenu ezingunaphakade. Ngoko ke, kuyinto elungileyo ukuba abakhululwa babandezeleke ngenxa yegama lam kuba ndanikezela ngobukho bam bonke kwidini elibuhlungu ukuze niphile phambi koYahweh, kwanaye uThixo omkhulu onamandla onke. Qondani ukuba kwiimvavanyo nakwiintsizi zenu anizisi nje kuphela ubabalo lwam olusindisayo kwabo basweleyo kodwa nisebenzela usindiso lwenu ngoloyiko nangokungcangcazela ukuze ndinise kumvuzo wenu opheleleyo kwaye ngoko ithemba lenu sele liza kuzaliseka!

Mhlawumbi uye weva ngokunyamezela kukaYobhi? Indlela uYobhi awayeyindoda ethembekileyo eyahlangabezana namaxesha anzima kwaye yahlaselwa ngokungekho sikweni nguSathana. Mhlawumbi uye weva ngesiphelo sikaYobhi kuba uYobhi wayengenguye umntu ongalunganga owoyiswa bububi, kodwa wayeyindoda elungileyo eyafumanisa inceba eyoyisa ububi ngokulungileyo ngokulandela amazwi kaYahweh nokuhlala ebulungiseni naphambi kwentshutshiso embi. Kwaye ngoko uYahweh wambuyisela kuYobhi okuphindwe kabini kuko konke okwathathwa kuye ngokungekho sikweni, kwaye uYahweh wambuyekeza uYobhi ngeenkathazo nentshutshiso yakhe. Yabonani, sibabiza ngokuba basikelelekile abo banyamezelayo. Niwuvile umonde kaYobhi, kwaye niyazi ukuba iNkosi yenza siphi isiphelo. Kuba iNkosi inemfesane kakhulu kwaye inenceba.

Kwaye ngoko kuhlala ukholo, ithemba, nothando, kwaye olona lukhulu kuzo luthando. Ngoko ke qondani zithandwa zam ukuba kwabathembekileyo ithemba sele liza kuzaliseka ngokupheleleyo njengoko nixhwilwa nisiwa kukhuseleko lwamazulu kwizigwebo ezizayo. Yivani ilizwi lam kwaye niluqonde uthando lwam ngani nina babiziweyo bam, abathembekileyo, nabanyulwa, kwanomzimba wam kwanomtshakazi wam! Ngoko ke, phumlani eluthandweni lwam kuba nakweyona nkathazo inkulu uthando lwam luhlala lomelele kwaye alunakoyiswa kuba ndimnye kubunye nakwinjongo noBawo, kwaye luthando lukaYahweh olungunaphakade endilunika bonke abaziindlalifa zobomi obungunaphakade nabo bonke abeza kuYesu waseNazarete ukuze basindiswe.

Qondani akukho mntu unokuza kuYahweh ngaphandle kwam, mna Krestu Yesu. Kwaye kunjalo ke ukuba uthando lukaYahweh alunakoyiswa kuba uYahweh akanakoyiswa, kwaye ngoko ke nam andinakoyiswa, kuba ndoyisile. Kwaye ndiyiNkosi engunaphakade yosindiso, kwanaye lowo uYahweh amenzileyo iNkosi yeeNkosi noKumkani wooKumkani, kwaye undinike igama elikhulu kunalo naliphi na igama ukuze ngegama likaYesu Krestu onke amadolo aguqe ezulwini nasemhlabeni naphantsi komhlaba. Qondani ukuba abawileyo bayoyika nina zithandwa zam kwaye uloyiko olukhulu lubamba iintliziyo zabo ngenxa yomgwebo wabo ozayo ongalibazisiyo.

Ndiyaninika umyalelo kwaye ndiyanikhuthaza, bantwana bam, ukuba nise uthando lwam kweli hlabathi ligulayo nelifayo neligwetyiweyo kweli xesha lokugqibela phambi kokuba intlekele iwe ngokupheleleyo. Ndithi kuni philisani abagulayo, vusani abafileyo, hlambululani abaneqhenqa, khupani iidemon kuba lowo ukholwayo kum imisebenzi endayenzayo emhlabeni naye uya kuyenza kwaye nemisebenzi emikhulu ngokwenene kuba ndiye kuBawo wethu. Hamba ngothando olukhulu nangokuthobeka, kwaye uhlangabezane nabo basweleyo ngamandla kaKrestu endiya kuwasebenzisa ngamandla kuni. Kuba kukuwe, zitya zam zodongwe, apho amandla obungangamsha bukaThixo okuKrestu kuwe ahlala khona. Thetha kwiingxaki ojongene nazo ngokholo. Yiba nokholo kuYahweh, nokholo kuYeshua HaMashiach kwaye uthi kuloo ntaba SUKA UPHOSWE ELWANDLE! Kwaye njengoko usenjenjalo ngokuthembeka, ndiya kunika imiqondiso emininzi imimangaliso nezimanga ukuba zenziwe ngezandla zabathembekileyo.

Yiba nesibindi nobugorha, kuba ndiyiNgonyama yesizwe sakwaYuda ekulwelayo. Ndiyanibongoza kwaye ndiyaninyanzela ngothando lukaThixo olukuKrestu Yesu iNkosi yenu ukuba ninyamezele ngokuzithandela ngenxa yegama lam kwaye nizise abalahlekileyo ekhaya kuBawo wethu nakweli xesha lokugqibela. Ndiyanibongoza nina banyulwa bam miselani umzekelo kuba nina ningabathembekileyo nabanyulwa bam endibakhande embandezelweni ukuze ebuthathakeni nasekwaphukeni kwenyama yenu nibonakalise amandla kaThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade, kwanaye uYahweh ngokwakhe njengoko nisenza inkonzo negunya elikhulu kwabo ndinithuma ukuba nibakhonze.

Mna, Yeshua HaMashiach, ndithumele la mazwi ngesicaka sam, kwaye ngesicaka sam amazwi am athunyelwe ukuze kuthotyelwe kukhuthazwe bonke abo la mazwi abafumanayo. Kwaye amazwi am aya kufumana amehlo, iindlebe, neentliziyo zabo athunyelwe kubo. Yimani niqine zithandwa zam kuba umvuzo wenu usemnyango wenu, kuphela ningatyhafi. Phumlani eluthandweni lwam kwaye niphumle emisebenzini yam, niyekile eyenu kwaye ngoku lixesha lokuhlangulwa kwenu kwaye ngoku lixesha losindiso lwenu olupheleleyo, kwaye ndiyeza kuni zithandwa zam. Yivani mna kwaye nive amazwi am, andiyi kunishiya okanye ndinilahle nina bathembekileyo bam kuKrestu, kwanabo bonke abathobela ilizwi lam. Khangelani emazulwini, kuba ukubonakala kwam sele kuza kuqhambuka esibhakabhakeni ngexesha nangeyure eningayilindelanga. Ndiyeza kuni bathembekileyo bam, nikulungele na?
