Ingqina eliMbaxa: uSirayeli
Okukrakra Okumnandi
Septemba 5, 2024

Imfihlelo Yesithathu!

Yivani ilizwi likaYeshua HaMashiach, zithandwa zam, nize kum ukuze nifumane intuthuzelo nifumane nokuphumla eluthandweni lwam ngani, bantwanana bam. Kuba nithembekile ezintweni ezimbalwa, yaye ngoku ndiza kunenza abaphathi phezu kwezinto ezininzi njengoko ningena kuvuyo lweNkosi yenu. Umgwebo asiyowenu, bathembi bam; nkqu nabo bonke abakuKrestu nabo bonke abalele kuKrestu baya kuvuselwa kubutsha bobomi ngequbuliso nangokuqhwanyaza kweliso. Kuba ngenene, ndiya kuhla ezulwini ndiduma, ngelizwi lesiphatha-zithunywa, nangexilongo likaThixo: baze abafileyo kuKrestu bavuselwe kubomi obungenakonakala kwaye nina baphilayo nabasalayo niya kuxhwilwa kunye nabo emafini kwaye niya kuhlala ninjalo ninayo kwaye nifana neNkosi yenu engunaphakade. Niya kuguqulwa ngoko nangoko ukusuka kwimeko yenu yangoku ewileyo niye kwindalo ekhululwe ngokupheleleyo yokungonakali, nkqu nidalwe ngokutsha njengomntu omnye omtsha, kwaye njengoko ndinjalo, nani niya kuba njalo; kwaye njengoko kubhaliwe, kuya kwenziwa ngokukhawuleza okunjalo.

Kutheni iintliziyo zenu zizikhathaza, bantwana bam? Kutheni nivumela amazwi ommangali anikhathaze nicinge ukuba akukho kuxhwilwa, okanye anikakulungelanga ukufumana ubabalo lwam olusindisayo? Ndiyanicebisa, bantwana bam, ukuba niqhubeke nihamba ngokholo kungekhona ngokubona; kwaye ndingulowo unenze nafaneleka, nkqu nabo bonke abakuKrestu kwaye benobudlelwane obusindisayo neNkosi yabo elilungisa, ngoko ke musani ukuthandabuza! Nangona, anindibonanga ngamehlo enu enyama, niyazi kakuhle ukuba ndiyiNkosi yenu ephilayo nomsindisi, kwaye kum kukho ukuzala kobomi nenkululeko. Inene, inene, lowo ukholwayo kum akayi kuphinda alambe okanye anxanwe kwakhona.

Kuba ndingumqathazana wobomi, kwaye bonke abatyayo abasayi kuphinda balambe; kwaye ndingulowo kuphuma kuye imilambo yamanzi aphilayo ngobuninzi obukhulu nobutsha bobomi, kwaye lowo uselayo kuwo akayi kuphinda anxanwe. Kodwa nonke niya kwenziwa niphile kwimpilo egqibeleleyo, imvisiswano egqibeleleyo, nomanyano olugqibeleleyo noYahweh, neNkosi yenu ephilayo nomsindisi, kuba ndinguKrestu Yesu, owanithenga ngegazi lam lentlawulelo, kwaye ngenene anixabiseki. Kwaye yaba yintlawulo engenaxabiso endayinikelayo ngonaphakade ndim ngokwam njengemvana kaThixo, kwaye ndalinikela idini lam egameni lenu njengombingeleli omkhulu ngonaphakade ngokohlobo lukaMelkitsedeke ndaza ndahlawula intlawulelo yenu ngegazi lam elingenasono.

Kwaye kunjalo ngonaphakade ukuba akusekho mfuneko yedini lesono, kuba ukuba igazi leenkunzi zeenkomo neleebhokhwe, nothuthu lwethokazi, lifefa abo bangahlambulukanga, liyangcwalisa ngokuphathelele ekuhlanjululweni kwenyama, Kobeka phi na ke ukugqithisela kwegazi likaKrestu, owathi ngoMoya ongunaphakade wazinikela engenabala kuThixo, ukusihlambulula isazela senu kwimisebenzi efileyo, ukuze nikhonze uThixo ophilayo? Kwaye oko idini lezilwanyana lalingenakukwenza, igazi lam lentlawulelo lakwazi ukukwenza kube kanye ngonaphakade, njengoko ndenze intlawulelo yezono zoluntu kube kanye ngonaphakade, nkqu nakubo bonke abenza ukhetho lokwamkela usindiso egameni lam, kuba alikho elinye igama elinikiweyo phantsi kwezulu elinokuthi abantu basindiswe ngalo. Ngoko ke musani ukukholelwa kubuxoki babo, kuba inamba, irhamncwa, nomprofeti wobuxoki baya kuvuka, kwaye baya kufuna ukumanya zonke iinkolo zehlabathi ezingeyonyani phantsi kwebhanile entsha, ngaphandle kokuba uYesu Krestu akayi kuvunyelwa phakathi kwabo, nokuba kusegameni okanye esenzweni, kwaye banikiwe ukuba balwe nabangcwele baze baboyise. Inene, inene, andinayo inxalenye okanye isabelo nabo, kwaye ndingulowo uya kubatshabalalisa ngokupheleleyo, kuba ngobulungisa ndiza kugweba ndilwe.

Kwaye ngoku ndithetha nabo baya kuza kum ngexesha lembandezelo kaYakobi, nkqu nabangcwele bexesha lembandezelo abaya kufumana umvuzo omkhulu nelifa elingunaphakade kwiNkosi yabo engunaphakade nengapheliyo noMesiya, nkqu uYeshua HaMashiach. Kwaye kubhaliwe njengenyaniso engunaphakade, kwaye nditsho ngonaphakade nangonaphakade ukuba nimele ningene ngesango elimxinwa: kuba lisango elibanzi, nendlela ebanzi esa entshabalalweni: kwaye baninzi abangena ngalo, Kuba isango limxinwa, nendlela imxinwa esa ebomini, kwaye bambalwa abayifumanayo. Ngoko ke ndiyanilawula, bantwanana bam, ukuba nihlale endleleni ethe tye nemxinwa kwaye niphephe intshabalalo yezidenge, nkqu nabo amagama abo angazange abhalwe eNcwadini yoBomi ukusuka ekusekweni kwehlabathi, kuba bayazithanda iindlela zentshabalalo, kwaye bayakuthanda ukungalungisi, kwaye bakhetha inkohliso enamandla kunobulungisa nenyaniso.

Kuba baya kubaleka ngapha nangapha, kwaye kuza imfazwe epheleleyo emhlabeni, kwaye intshabalalo epheleleyo isondela kwaye ikufuphi; ingozi, isilumkiso esingxamisekileyo sika-911, kwaye imini isondela kwaye iphezu kwenu. Kwaye baya kuphuma eluthuthwini lwemfazwe, kwaye eluthuthwini lwemfazwe baya kuzityhila eluntwini kwakhona, kwaye uluntu luya kumangaliswa ngabawileyo ngokumangaliswa okukhulu ngamaqhinga abo abaye balumkisa ngayo abalahlekileyo kwasekuqaleni. Baya kudibanisa iinkolo zabo ezingeyonyani, kwaye baya kunixelela ubuxoki obuninzi obuthethwa yimilomo yoomoya bobuxoki bomchasi-Krestu, kuba baninzi abachasi-Krestu abaphumele ehlabathini.

Yintoni umchasi-Krestu, bantwana bam? Umchasi-Krestu uchasene noKrestu, umsindisi wokwenene noMesiya, kwaye ukwathatha indawo kaKrestu; kuba ndinguYeshua HaMashiach, kwaye akukho wumbi. Kwaye ubuxoki babo sele buwugcwalisile umhlaba njengoko belungiselele izihlwele ukuba zithobele abathathu abangcwele, nkqu inamba, irhamncwa eliphuma elwandle, nerhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni, elikwangumprofeti wabo wobuxoki. Kodwa wathini uYohane ngalo mbandela kwileta yakhe yokuqala? Andinibhalelanga, kuba aniyazi inyaniso: kodwa kuba niyayazi, kwaye akukho buxoki buyinyaniso. Ngubani ixoki, ngaphandle kwalowo ukhanyelayo ukuba uYesu unguKrestu? UnguMchasi-Krestu, lowo ukhanyelayo uYise, noNyana. Nabani na okhanyelayo uNyana, akanaye noYise: kodwa lowo uvumayo uNyana, unaye noYise.

Kwaye kunjalo, ukuba mna, Yesu waseNazarete, ndiyindlela, inyaniso, nobomi; kwaye akukho namnye uza kuYahweh ngaphandle kwam. Ndingumnyango okuphela kwawo, kwaye ndiyindlela ekuphela kwayo eya kuYahweh, uThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade; kuba uYahweh utshilo kwaye uYahweh ulungiselele waza wagqiba kwangaphambi kokusekwa komhlaba kulwazi lwakhe lwangaphambili olungunaphakade nobulumko. Ngubani kuni onokumcebisa uYahweh, uThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade? Nangona amashumi amawaka aphindwe ngamashumi amawaka ejikeleze itrone yakhe, akadingi mcebisi; kwaye nguYahweh, uThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade, osebenza zonke izinto ngokwecebo lentando yakhe ukuba nibe ludumo lozuko lwakhe nina enaqala ukuthemba kuKrestu.

Ngoko ke yiqondeni inkohliso ezayo esele ifikile emhlabeni. Kuba abakhonzi bakaSathana bathetha bechasene nenyaniso, kwaye bayakhwaza phesheya kwaye bakhwaza kakhulu ukuba zonke iinkolo zehlabathi zikhokelela kuthixo omnye kungakhathaliseki ukuba zivela kuliphi na ukholo, kungakhathaliseki iinkolelo zabo, ukuba onke amanye amagama anikwe ngabawileyo ayinyaniso kwaye akhokelela kuthixo wokwenene, kodwa akhokelela kwinamba yodwa. Inene, inene ndithi kuni, musani ukukholelwa kubuxoki babo, kwaye musani nokubanqwenelela impumelelo abo bakholelwa kububhanxa obunjalo. Kuba libanzi isango elisa entshabalalweni, elikhokelela kwelo chibi elikhulu lomlilo, kwaye uSathana ufuna ukutyhala abaninzi kangangoko anako endleleni yakhe eya kwintshabalalo engunaphakade, kuba lilo ilifa lakhe, kwaye yiyo intiyo yakhe kuni nonke. Musani ukukholelwa kubuxoki babo bokumanya iinkolo, kwaye nibaleke kumanyano lwabo olungcwele, kuba isiphelo salo ngawona malangatye ashushu aluhlaza njengoko isulfure evuthayo iqengqeleka kwaye itsha ngonaphakade, kwaye akukho namnye uya kusinda kwabo babotshiweyo baphoswe emlilweni.

Qondani, bantwana bam, ukuba onke amagama abo akhokelela kuthixo omnye, nkqu uSathana, kuba abangothixo kodwa bangabazenzisi abazenzisayo. Kwaye baya kuphephezelisa iiflegi zabo zokwahlukahlukana, kwaye baya kuthi amagama amaninzi akhokelela kuthixo omnye. Kwaye kwelinye icala bachanekile kuba iindlela ezininzi zikhokelela kuthixo weli hlabathi, ogwebo lwakhe lufikile kwaye isohlwayo sakhe nentshabalalo ayilibazisi. Kwaye kwelinye icala, baxokisa ihlabathi kwaye bafuna ukurhwebesha bonke abaya kulandela iindlela zabo zokutshabalalisa kuluntu lwabo olutsha oluya kuphuma eluthuthwini lwemfazwe oluya kufuna uphawu ukuze lungene.

Musani ukuthatha uphawu lwabo, kuba bonke abaluthathayo bagwetyelwe ngonaphakade kwaye baguqulwe ngonaphakade. Kwaye baxoka kabuhlungu ukuba onke amagama akhokelela kuThixo wokwenene, nkqu uYahweh, onguThixo ongunaphakade kaSirayeli, kuba uYahweh wakhetha uYakobi kuye ukuze nibe ngabantu abakhethekileyo kuye. Kwaye uSathana uhlala efuna ukwenza umgunyathi weendlela zikaThixo omnye wokwenene, nkqu uEl Shaddai, onguThixo uSomandla! Kwaye wenze iinkolo ezininzi zomgunyathi ezenzelwe ukwenza umgunyathi weendlela zikaYahweh, uThixo ongunaphakade waphakade, kwaye zonke ezi nkolo zomgunyathi ziphela kwintshabalalo engunaphakade kwabo babanjiswe ngolo hlobo. Kuba nguwuphi umthiyeli obeka umgibe emehlweni entaka?

Kwaye sele benika iintsikelelo kubo bonke, nkqu nakuzo zonke iinkolo, kwaye sele befuna ukumanya zonke iinkolo phantsi kweebhanile zabo zokulalanisa ngokweenkolo, kuba zenziwa ngolo hlobo iindlela zabo zokulawula ingqondo. Kwelinye icala babonisa ithisisi; kwelinye icala babonisa i-antithisisi yabo; kwaye kungquzulwano olulawulwayo bavelisa isinthesisi yabo, kuba bafuna ukudibanisa zonke iinkcubeko, iintlanga, nezizwe neelwimi kuluntu lwabo lobusathana ukuze batshabalalise bonke abalandela iindlela zabo zokutshabalalisa. Kwaye ngoko qondani, oko kugwenxa akunakulungiswa, kwaye amazwi abo amahle neentetho zabo ezintle zenzelwe ukubhenela kwiimvakalelo zomntu nenkanuko kunye novelwano lobuxoki abaya kulubiza ngokuba bubungcwele. Qondani ukuba uluntu ngokwalo lonakele ngokupheleleyo kwaye alunakulungisa indlela yalo, kwaye akukho kulunga eluntwini, kodwa uluntu lonakaliswe kukungathobeli kuka-Adam kwaye ngakumbi bubuxoki namaqhinga enyoka, entloko yayo ndiya kuyityumza ngokuqinisekileyo, kuba uYahweh utshilo kwasekuqaleni isiphelo saloo nyoka ixokayo etya uthuli lomhlaba.

Kube njalo, kunjalo, kwaye kuya kuba njalo, ukuba akukho nto intsha phantsi kwelanga. Kwaye njengoko ihlabathi lisuka kubabalo lisiya emgwebeni, intlekele yequbuliso isondele ukuwa, kwaye iya kuba ngequbuliso. Ngoko ke ndicebisa bonke abafunda la mazwi ukuba baqonde ukuba la mazwi awanifumananga ngengozi, kodwa athunyelwe nguYahweh ngokwakhe ngendlela yonyana wakhe okuphela kwamzeleyo, nkqu uYeshuah HaMashiach, ethethwa ngamxhelo mnye ngabakhonzi bam abaprofeti. Kwaye ndim, Yesu waseNazarete, nkqu uYeshua HaMashiach, owahlawula ixabiso elingunaphakade losindiso lwenu nosindiso lwabo bonke abaya kuza kubomi obungunaphakade eluthandweni lwam olungapheliyo nolungunaphakade uYahweh awandidalela ukuba ndiluzalisekise. Ndithi kuni okufanayo noko ndikutshilo kwasekuqaleni: ukuba mna, Yesu uKrestu, nkqu uYeshua HaMashiach, ndiyindlela, inyaniso, nobomi, kwaye akukho mntu uza kuYise ngaphandle kwam; kwaye alikho elinye igama elinikiweyo emhlabeni, naphakade, ekufuneka abantu basindiswe ngalo, kwaye onke amanye amagama kungakhathaliseki ukuba umgunyathi uqinisekisa kangakanani na awakhokeleli kuThixo wokwenene, nkqu uYise weziKhanyiso ongenaguquguquko nasithunzi sokujika. Ngoko qondani, bantwanana bam, ukuba niziinkanyiso zehlabathi uYahweh azizeleyo, nkqu ezidalwe ngokutsha kum, uKrestu Yesu iNkosi elungileyo engunaphakade, kwaye ndenziwe umoya onika ubomi.

Yabonani, ndinibonisa imfihlelo eya kunika ubulumko kwizilumko kwaye iya kuthintela izidenge nabangafundanga ukuba bangazibandakanyi nabo babophelelwe kwintshabalalo ngeendlela zabo. Isithathu, isibini, sinye: kwaye kwiinkolo ezininzi zehlabathi kukho ezintathu ezilawulayo eziya kukhokelela komnye wobuxoki ongengcwele, kwaye ezi zintathu zibonakala njengeentshaba kwaye zilwe iimfazwe ezininzi. Kodwa, kwezi zintathu, ezimbini ziyimfihlo zizihlobo, kwaye enye iya kusebenzisa enye, kwaye zombini zikhokelela entshabalalweni. Kuba kwezi zintathu, ezimbini ziya kujikela ngokukrakra kwenye, nkqu kuleyo ingcwele abayithiyileyo kodwa aboyiswe yiyo ngokupheleleyo. Kwaye ngoko ke leyo ingcwele iya kuzisa umgwebo endlwini kaThixo kuqala, kuba umgwebo uqala endlwini kaThixo kwaye imini yomgwebo ifikile.

Nangona leyo ingcwele yoyisile, ezimbini kwezintathu ziya kujika zitshabalalise leyo inye, kodwa loo ntshabalalo ayinakwenzeka, kwaye leyo inye sele ifumene uloyiso olupheleleyo. Kodwa inkohliso iya kuba yile yokuba ezimbini kwezintathu ziya kujikela leyo inye kwaye zenze izityholo zobuxoki emilonyeni yamangqina amaninzi obuxoki, kwaye ziya kulwa nabangcwele ziboyise. Kwaye ngobuxoki obuninzi obunyelisayo bayawugubungela umhlaba, kodwa leyo inye, nkqu leyo ingcwele kaThixo, sele iboyisile kwaye iya kuyityumza intloko yabo ngokupheleleyo. Kodwa ezi zimbini ziya kubonakala zoyisile leyo inye zifuna ukuyitshabalalisa, kwaye kwezi zimbini ziya kulwa naleyo inye, nangona intshabalalo yazo yokugqibela ingalibazisi. Kwaye ngoko ke umgibe uya kubekelwa ihlabathi, ukuba kugonyamelo olukhulu nemfazwe ziya kuzidibanisa ukuze isithathu, isibini, sinye sibe sisibini, sinye, isithathu kuba ziya kuzidibanisa zibe yinkolo enye yobuxoki ekhokelwa ngabathathu abangengcwele, nkqu inamba, irhamncwa eliphuma elwandle, nerhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni, elikwangumprofeti wabo wobuxoki.

Kwaye ngoko ndinibonisa impendulo yemfihlelo yam yesithathu. Ezi zintathu ziinkolo ezintathu ezilawulayo ezilawula ihlabathi elikhoyo ngoku; kwaye kwezi zintathu, inye kuphela eyinyaniso, nkqu leyo ingcwele kaThixo; kuba mna, Yeshua HaMashiach, ndingulowo ungcwele kaThixo owoyisileyo omubi kwaye oyiNkosi yeeNkosi noKumkani wooKumkani. Andinakoyiswa, kwaye ndinikwe igama elingaphezu kwawo onke amagama, ukuba egameni likaYesu uKrestu, nkqu uYeshua HaMashiach (kuba ndingumsindisi weNtlanga nomYuda ngokufanayo) onke amadolo aguqe, kokubini ezulwini nasemhlabeni nkqu naphantsi komhlaba. Kwaye iinkolo ezimbini zobuxoki zibonakala zichasene kodwa zifune ukuzithandisa kuleyo inye, kwaye ngenene uSathana wenze umgunyathi webandla lam lokwenene ngehenyukazi lobuxoki elizukisa kwaye linqule ukumkanikazi wezulu. Kwaye bafuna umanyano lobuxoki kwaye baxube ubuxoki obuninzi enyanisweni, ngaphandle kokuba ubuxoki nenyaniso azixubani: unokuba nobuxoki okanye ube nenyaniso: akukho lunye ukhetho.

Kwaye njengoko ibandla lam lokwenene lithathwa lisiwa emazulwini kwaye umthinteli uyasuswa, ngoko ke ibandla lomgunyathi liya kutshatyalaliswa, kwaye ngoko ke leyo inye iyasuswa ngokupheleleyo. Iinkolo ezimbini eziseleyo ziya kuvuka kuloyiso lobuxoki, kwaye ziya kuba noxolo lobuxoki, kwaye ziya kufuna umanyano lobuxoki ukuze zimanye abalahlekileyo, nkqu nokunika ubomi obungunaphakade, impilo egqibeleleyo, kwaye inamba iya kunika usuku olutsha. Kwaye iya kumanya ezi zimbini zibe yinkolo ooyise babo ababengayazi kodwa iya kubonakala ifana kuba uluntu aluyazi imbali yalo yokwenene. Kwaye ngale sinthesisi yentshabalalo baya kuya emlilweni ongunaphakade, kuba bonke abazibandakanya nabo ngabo bayonwabela ukungalungisi, kwaye kungekhona oko kuphela, kodwa abo bathanda kwaye bakholelwa kwaye benza kwaye banqula ubuxoki kuba iindlela zabo ziyinkohliso kwaye zigwenxa kwaye iindlela zabo zikhokelela ekufeni kwesibini.

Kwaye ngoko baya kulilahlekisa ihlabathi, abo baya kumangaliswa emva kwabo, besithi, ngubani ofana nerhamncwa? Kwaye elinye irhamncwa liya kunikwa amandla elinye irhamncwa, kwaye irhamncwa eliphuma elwandle liya kubalawula ngokokulawula kwalo, kwaye irhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni liya kulawula umhlaba ngokokulawula kwalo, kwaye baya kulawula umhlaba ngokungalungisi. Kwaye kwababini abaseleyo, baya kudibana babe banye. Ngaphandle kokuba, iindlela zabo kukufa, kwaye apho umgibe ubekiwe, kuba nguwuphi na umthiyeli obeka umgibe emehlweni entaka? Bonke abalandela iindlela zabo zobumbano lweenkolo baya kumanywa, kuphela kwilifa labo bawayo; kwaye bonke abazimanya ngokolunywa yinyoka yabo baya kwabelana ngelifa lowo uwileyo kwelo chibi likhulu lomlilo wesulfure apho batsha ngonaphakade kwaye impethu yabo ayifi. Kwaye ngoko ubumbano lwabo lweenkolo luzaliswe yimigibe efihlwe ngobuchule, kuba nguwuphi na umthiyeli obeka umgibe emehlweni entaka?

Ngoko ndiyabacebisa bonke abafunda la mazwi nabo bonke abanamehlo okubona neendlebe zokuva nentliziyo yokuqonda, ukuba usindiso nobomi obungunaphakade noThixo oyinyaniso, uYahweh ngokwakhe, alufumaneki kwelinye igama ngaphandle kwegama lonyana okuphela kwamzeleyo kaThixo, uYesu Krestu, okwanguye uYeshua HaMashiach; kwaye ndingumsindisi walo lonke uluntu, kwanalo uhlanga luka-Adam oluthi lonke uhlanga lwabantu lwabelana ngegazi lalo ngokupheleleyo. Kwaye ngaluphi na ulwimi, nakuso nasiphi na isizwe, bonke bangeza ngokukhululekileyo bamkele isipho sam sosindiso olungunaphakade ngokubiza egameni lam elingcwele kude kube sekupheleni. Kuba ndiyiNkosi nomsindisi wenu, kuphela musani ukuliyeka igama lam liphume emilebeni yenu, kwaye ndiya kunisindisa ngokugqibeleleyo.

Musani ukuzimanya nobumbano lwabo lweenkolo, kuba abakwazi oko bakunqulayo, kwaye bathakathwe bubuxoki obuya kubasa kwintshabalalo yabo engunaphakade. Kwaye kunjalo ke ukuba usindiso alufumaneki kwelinye igama, kwaye akukho gama linye linikiweyo apho uluntu lunokusindiswa khona nalapho uluntu lunokusondela kuYahweh, kuba lowo ungenaye uNyana akanaye noYise. Kwaye ngoko ndiya kubaxhasa ndibomeleze bonke ababiza egameni lam elingcwele kude kube sekupheleni. Kwaye niya kundenzela imisebenzi emikhulu, kwaye ndinithuma ukuba niyithande iNkosi uThixo wenu ngentliziyo yenu iphela, nangomphefumlo wenu uphela, nangengqondo yenu iphela, nangamandla enu ephela kwaye nithande ummelwane wenu njengoko nizithanda nina kwaye nincedane nithandane ngamaxesha amabi kakhulu aza kuqhambuka emhlabeni wonke.

Ngoko ke bathathu, babini, mnye, apho ababini baya kujikela omnye, kwaye ababini abaseleyo baya kudibana babe banye kwaye baya kulawulwa ngabathathu abangcwele, abaya kulawula abo amagama abo angazange abhalwe eNcwadini yoBomi ngaphambi kokusekwa kwehlabathi. Kuphela musani ukuzimanya nabo, bangcwele bam; kuba kungcono nilahlekelwe bubomi benu ukuze nibufumane kunokuba nithande ubomi benu nize nilahlekelwe bubomi benu ngonaphakade kwintshabalalo engunaphakade. Yabonani! Ukwahlukana kokugqibela kuza kuqala kwaye amaxesha ngoku. Musani ukuboyika, bantwanana bam, kodwa thembelani kwiNkosi nomsindisi wenu ongunaphakade. Ndiluncedo olungunaphakade ngamaxesha entswelo, kwaye ndisaza kusindisa isihlwele sezihlwele ngexesha lembandezelo kaYakobi, kwaye ndisaza kusindisa intsalela yakwaSirayeli, kwaye akukho namnye unako ukunixwitsha ezandleni zam.

Qondani ukuba ithemba lenu lithemba elingunaphakade, kwaye andinishiyanga ningenathemba, kwaye ndinishiyele umzekelo ongunaphakade. Kuba kubhaliwe ngam kwabaseHebhere: Sijonge kuYesu umqalisi nomgqibelelisi wokholo lwethu, owathi ngenxa yovuyo olwalubekwe phambi kwakhe, wawunyamezela umnqamlezo, walicukida ihlazo, waza wahlala ngasekunene kwetrone kaThixo. Kwaye ngoko qondani ukuba umzekelo wam wokuthobela uhlokoma njengomzekelo wokomeleza ukholo lwenu ukuze ninyamezele kude kube sekupheleni, kanye njengokuba ndanyamezelayo kude kube sekupheleni xa ndathetha la mazwi okugqibela emnqamlezweni: Kugqityiwe. Kwaye ngoko uThixo wandivusa kwabafileyo kuba kwakungenakwenzeka ukuba ukufa kundibambe, kuba lelam ligazi elimsulwa.

Kwaye ngoko ndininike ithemba elikhulu, bantwanana bam, kangangokuba ningazinzisa iingqondo zenu kulovuyo lubekwe phambi kwenu kuba iintlungu zembandezelo kaYakobi azithelekiseki kwaye azifanelekanga ukukhankanywa xa zithelekiswa novuyo olungunaphakade olubekwe phambi kwenu. Yabonani! Kubhaliwe nguYohane uMtyhileli: Aba ngabo baphuma kwimbandezelo enkulu, kwaye bazihlambile iingubo zabo, bazenza mhlophe egazini leMvana. Kungoko bephambi kwetrone kaThixo, bekhonza yena imini nobusuku eTempileni yakhe: kwaye lowo uhleli eTroneni uya kuhlala phakathi kwabo. Abasayi kuphinda balambe, abasayi kuphinda banxanwe, nelanga alisayi kubatshisa, nabuphi na ubushushu. Kuba iMvana, ephakathi kwetrone, iya kubondla, kwaye iya kubakhokelela kwimithombo ephilayo yamanzi: kwaye uThixo uya kuzosula zonke iinyembezi emehlweni abo. Baya kuba ngabaBingeleli bakaThixo, nabakaKrestu, kwaye baya kulawula naye iwaka leminyaka.

Kwaye linjalo ke ithemba lenu, zithandwa zam, ukuze ninyamezele kude kube sekupheleni. Kwaye elenu lithemba elingunaphakade kuba u-El Shaddai, uThixo uSomandla, uya kudala izulu elitsha nomhlaba omtsha apho kumi ubulungisa, kwaye niya kunikwa ukungena kuloo mzi ungcwele ulungiswe wahonjiswa nguYahweh ngokwakhe njengomtshakazi elungiselelwe umyeni, kwaye niya kutya kumthi wobomi. Kwaye njengoko kubhaliwe: Lowo woyisayo, uya kuzidla ilifa izinto zonke, kwaye ndiya kuba nguThixo wakhe, kwaye yena uya kuba ngunyana wam.

Mna, Yeshua HaMashiach, ndingulowo uthumele amazwi am ngomkhonzi wam, kwaye ngomkhonzi wam amazwi am athunyelwe ukuba athotyelwe ngabo bonke abafundayo. Kwaye ndiyityhilile ivangeli engunaphakade, kuba amaxesha okutyhilwa kwayo ngoku.

Ndingulowo uzayo ngenxa yabo bakuKrestu kwaye ndiya kuhlangula abathembekileyo ebandleni lam kumlilo womsuli nakwingqumbo ezayo.

Ndingulowo wahlula ingqolowa kumakhula, kwaye akukho namnye kuni onokuxhwilwa esandleni sam.

Yimani nam, bangcwele bam bembandezelo, kwaye yizani kum, Owu, Sirayeli, kuba ndinguYeshua HaMashiach, uMesiya wenu oyinyaniso, kwaye ndininike ithemba elikhulu namandla amakhulu. Kwaye abaninzi benu baya kundenzela imisebenzi enamandla, kwaye akukho namnye kuni oya kushiywa engavuzwanga, kuba nezinto ezincinane enizenzayo ngamaxesha embandezelo enkulu zizisa umvuzo ongunaphakade, ozukiso lwazo iintlungu zenu zingafanelekanga ukukhankanywa; ngoko bizani egameni lam elinamandla, bantu bam! Usindiso nomvuzo omkhulu kusesandleni sam kwabo boyisayo, kuba uthembisa njalo uYeshua HaMashiach kwabathembekileyo.

Yabonani, amaxesha ngoku!
