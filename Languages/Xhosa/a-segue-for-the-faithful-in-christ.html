Yabonani! Utshintshelo lwamandla olusuka kuBukumkani baseZulwini lusiya kwinkqubo yokugqibela yerhamncwa, ekungekhe kuphinde kubekho enye. Yabonani! NdinguYeshua HaMashiach yaye ndiza kuzisa iindaba ezimnandi zoxolo novuyo kwabathembekileyo kuKrestu, ukuze uvuyo lwenu luzale, ningatyhafi kulindelo lwenu kwaye ningalahli ugxininiso lwethemba lenu, eliza kutyhilwa ngesiquphe ngoku xa ndibonakala emafini ngokungalindelekanga, kwaye niya kuthathwa. Kuba, ndiza kuhlwitha abathembekileyo kuKrestu ndibayise emazulwini, kwaye ngubani na onokunqanda isandla sam esinamandla? Ndithi ngubani?!

Kuba ndiza namandla apheleleyo negunya elivela kwitrone kaYehova ngokwakhe kwaye ndiza kuthatha ilifa lam endilithenge ngegazi lam elihlawulelayo, lona igazi lam elingenasono endalinikela ngokuzithandela ngamandla amakhulu nangothando olungenakuqhawulwa ngenxa yezono zehlabathi. Ukuze izono zika-Adam zihlale phezu koYesu waseNazarete, yena kanye lowo uYohane umBhaptizi wamchaza ngamazwi athi "Yabonani! IMvana kaThixo, esusa izono zehlabathi!"

Kunjalo ke, ukuba inene, inene, ndisihlawule isohlwayo sikaMoses kubo bonke abamkeleyo uYesu waseNazarete njengesivumelwano sabo soxolo phakathi kwabo noYehova, kuba u-Adam ngunyana wolahleko owamphikayo uYehova waza waba phantsi komhlambi weehagu kaSathana njengabakhonzi besono. Noko ke, amazwi am ahlala eqinisekile kuba lowo ungumkhonzi wesono akahlali phantsi kolawulo loBukumkani bukaSathana ukuba bayamkela baze bamvume uYesu njengeNkosi. Inene, inene, umkhonzi akahlali endlwini yesono ngonaphakade ukuba bakhetha ukwamkela usindiso olungunaphakade kuYesu Krestu kuba alikho elinye igama eliyakuze linikwe usindiso luka-Adam, kwaye abawiweyo abasoze baxolelwe.

Noko ke, abo basikhahlelayo isimemo sam nabo bonke abaziguquleyo ngeendlela zaseKanan nabo bonke abaya kuthi balamkele uphawu lwerhamncwa baya kuhlala endlwini yesono ngonaphakade kuba abo bangabantwana besono baya kuhlala phantsi kwamandla okonakala kofuzo lwenyoka ngonaphakade kanaphakade, nabo bonke abanyelisa igama likaYehova, awadalwa ngalo u-Adam, abasoze baxolelwe, ngokwenene oko kuzenze kugoso akunakulungiswa. Ngoko ke, kubulungisa obugqibeleleyo bukaYehova bonke abo bazenzayo ngokupheleleyo izono zabo bawayo baze baguqule imizila yabo yofuzo benze isono esisa ekufeni, kanye njengoko ndabhalisa uYohane umTiyili kwiileta zakhe ezazisiya kwabathembekileyo kuKrestu. Kwaye bonke abo balilahlileyo igazi elingcwele nelingenasono likaYesu waseNazarete njengento engcolileyo baya kuhlangana nabo bawayo kwilifa labo lesulfure evuthayo, kuba inene, inene, intlawulelo yenu yandixabisa kakhulu, into ebendingavuma ukuyihlawula kwakhona, nangona into enjalo ingasoze ifuneke ngonaphakade.

Kutheni nityhafile, zithandwa zam? Kutheni umdla wenu uphela? Ndingabuza ukuba nguwuphi umtshakazi ohleli ebuhlungu, kanye njengokuba umyeni esondela? Kanye njengokuba umyeni wenu ebhengeze ukusondela kokufika kwakhe, kodwa uninzi lwenu lunentliziyo ebuhlungu ngokungathi ndinilahlile? Kanti ke, kunjalo ukuba abo basondeleyo ngexesha kwindibano enkulu yabathembekileyo kuKrestu baye bazivumela ukuba balahlekelwe luvuyo lwabo, ngokuthi bamamele iingqiqo ezingenakholo zabaprofeti nabalungiseleli bobuxoki, nangokuvumela iintolo ezivuthayo ukuba zihlale ezingqondweni zabo ezinokubhukuqa ukholo lwabo ukuba azicinywanga ngamazwi am nangomoya wam! Kuba amazwi am, angumoya, kwaye abubomi kwabo bahamba ngokholo! Ngoko ke, musani ukuzivumela ukuba nilibale ukuba kukho isithsaba sobulungisa esibabekelwe bonke abo bakuthandayo ukubonakala kwam, ningazincami izithsaba zenu zithandwa zam, kuba uSathana ufuna ukuniphanga ilifa lenu.

Kutheni umtshakazi wam enobuso obude obubuhlungu nobunodano, ngokungathi ulahliwe ngumyeni ongathembekanga? Mna, Krestu Yesu, ndakha ndanithethela amazwi angathembekanga, okanye ndakha ndanixokisa, zithandwa zam? Hayi, ngokwenene ndiyiNkosi eNgunaphakade eThembekileyo neNgunaphakade yoBulungisa ekuhlala kukho kuyo u-ewe no-amen kuba ndingulowo kuye zonke izithembiso zikaYehova noYeshua HaMashiach zifumana inzaliseko ngonaphakade kanaphakade. Ngoko ke, qondani uhlobo lotshintsho oluza kwenzeka kuni, nina kanye endithetha nani, nina bathembekileyo kuKrestu! Kwanelibandla lam, umzimba wam, kwanaye uMtshakazi wam, endithumela kuye la mazwi entuthuzelo! Ngoko vuyani!

Uninzi lwenu luthatha ukuzalwa ngokutsha njengamandla phezu kwezono zenu, kwaye ngokwenene ndiyanixhobisa phezu kwezono zenu ukuze nibe ngaboyisi kwaye noyise ezintweni zonke, kodwa ukuzalwa ngokutsha kungaphezu kokoyisa izono. Nangona ndibatywinile abathembekileyo kuKrestu, lona olo tywino lungenakuqhawulwa lukaYehova ngokwakhe, kwanangalo moya wobunyana ongenguye ngokwawo ukuzala ngokutsha okupheleleyo, kodwa yintlawulo yokuqala yelifa elithengiweyo lenkululeko yabo epheleleyo ezayo nethenjisiweyo. Kanye njengoko kubhaliwe, enathe nina nakholosa kuye, emva kokuba nilivile ilizwi lenyaniso, iVangeli yosindiso lwenu, enathi emva kokuba nikholiwe, natywinwa ngomoya oyingcwele wesithembiso, Osisibambiso selifa lethu, sokuhlawulelwa kwenkululeko ethengiweyo ukuze kudunyiswe uzuko lwakhe.

Ngoko ke, uphawu lukaThixo ophilayo luhlala luqinisekile kwaye luzinzile kubo bonke endibatywinileyo, kwaye ninokuzibiza ngokuba nizelwe ngokutsha, kwaye ngokwenene ndinixhobisile ngemvelo yam entsha ukuze nihambe ngobulungisa nangokuthe tye kuko konke ukuthobela kokholo njengoko nihamba ngomoya, kuba olilungisa liya kuphila ngokholo! Ngoko ke, qondani ukuba nangona ninokholo lonke, nazo zonke iimbonakaliso zamandla, kwanazo zonke izipho nezixhobo ezinikwe kuloo moya wobunyana enawufumanayo xa nanitywinwayo, amandla apheleleyo nobukhazikhazi bokuzalwa ngokutsha aziyi kuqondwa de ngequbuliso emhlabeni ningabikho!

Kuba, ndiza kunihlwitha ndinise emazulwini, apho niya kumangaliswa bubukhazikhazi eniya kudalwa ngabo nize nambathiswe. Kwaye ingelosi iya kutywina abayi-144,000 bam emabunzini abo kwaye zininzi izixhobo, izakhono, namandla eniya kuba nawo nonke ngokokuthembeka kwenu.
