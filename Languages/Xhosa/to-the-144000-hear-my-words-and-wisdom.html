Kuma-144,000: Yivani Amazwi Am Nobulumko Bam!

Yivani amazwi am nithobe iindlebe zenu kubulumko bam, kuba mna, iNkosi uYesu Krestu, ndiza kuthetha nani ukuba niyawava amazwi am. Kuba ndiza kubatywina abam abali-144,000; anifundanga na? Ndabona esinye isithunywa sezulu sinyuka sivela empumalanga, sinetywina likaThixo ophilayo; sadanduluka ngezwi elikhulu kwizithunywa ezine, ezanikwa igunya lokuwona umhlaba nolwandle, sisithi, musani ukuwona umhlaba, nolwandle, nemithi, side sibatywine abakhonzi boThixo wethu emabunzini abo. Ngaloo ndlela ndiza kuthumela abo ndiza kubatywina, kwaye niza kundenzela imisebenzi emikhulu, kwaye niza kuhamba ngamandla amakhulu. Kodwa oku akuyi kuba njengakwixesha lobabalo, apho abashumayeli ezindlwini zokushumayela babefundisa kwizibhalo ezingcwele. (Abanye bathetha ubuxoki kwaye abanye bandenzela imisebenzi emikhulu ezicaweni zabo.) Ixesha lecawa liyasondela esiphelweni, kwaye ixesha lam lobabalo sele liza kugqitywa, kwaye ngoko amaxesha ovavanyo aza kuqala, kwanengxaki kaYakobi.

Ngaloo ndlela ndiza kunithuma ukuba niye kunyamekela umhlambi wam oza kuchithakala ngakumbi kwaye uvavanywe yintlekele emva kwentlekele. Kuba imihla iya kuba mibi, kwaye nina niya kukhanya njengezibane kwaye nime njengeentsika phakathi kwabachithakeleyo. Ngaloo ndlela ndinithuma ukuba nithande kwaye nikhathalele ummelwane wenu. Kuba nina enithembekileyo kubizo lwam niya kuphila umthetho endininike wona, hayi umthetho kaMoses. Kuba mna, uYeshuwa HaMashiyakhi, ndiwuzalisekisile umthetho kaMoses, kwaye iTorah igcinwa ngokholo kum, kuba olilungisa uya kuphila ngokholo.

Kwaye kuya kuba nje: Thanda iNkosi uThixo wakho ngentliziyo yakho yonke, ngomphefumlo wakho wonke, ngengqondo yakho yonke, nangamandla akho onke, kwaye uthande ummelwane wakho njengoko uzithanda wena. Kuba ngokwenza ezi zinto ngokuqinisekileyo niya kusindisa imiphefumlo yenu entshabalalweni kunye neyaleyo ndinithuma kuyo. La ayingamaxesha obomi obude nempumelelo, kuba loo mihla idlulile emhlabeni ndide ndize kulawula umhlaba ngobulungisa. Lowo ungabuthandiyo ubomi bakhe kude kube sekupheleni uya kusindiswa aze aphile ngonaphakade.

Niphila kumaxesha emfazwe, nengqumbo enkulu, nentyilelo enkulu yazo zonke izinto. Kuba uThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade, kwanaye uYahweh, akasayi kuzifihla emhlabeni; kuba uya kugqobhoza aphume kwindawo yakhe yokuhlala, kwaye uthumela mna, kwanaye uYeshuwa HaMashiyakhi, ukuba ndizalisekise ingqumbo yakhe kwaye ndizalisekise izithembiso zakhe awazenzayo kooBawo. Kodwa kuqala, umgwebo uya kuwa, nengqumbo enkulu iya kuwa phezu kwehlabathi, kwaye anisayi kuba nakho ukuthatha inxaxheba kuluntu lwabo. Kuba ukuthatha inxaxheba kuluntu lwabo oluza kuvuka eluthuthwini kuya kufuna ukuba wonke umntu amkele uphawu lwabo. Ukuba nithe nangenela kubo, niya kwabelana nabo ekugwetyweni kwabo okungunaphakade kwelo chibi lomlilo likhulu, kwaye elo chibi lalilungiselelwe nguThixo uMtyholi nezithunywa zakhe, ngoko musani ukungenela kubo.

Aniyi kufumana luxolo kwihlabathi labo elivukayo kodwa kuphela ububi obungenazithintelo, kuba ndiya kuba ndimsusile umthinteli. Kwaye ukuba aniyonxalenye yengqondo yabo edibeneyo, ngoko aniyi kuba nanto yakwenza nabo, kuba baya kubulala bonke abaphikisayo. Kuba uphawu luya kubaguqula ngonaphakade abo balwamkelayo ngokuguqula i-DNA yabo kanye, kuba uluntu lwenziwa ngokomfanekiso kaYahweh, nangona uSathana efuna ukunyelisa loo mfanekiso ngokungenakuguqulwa ngamachiza akhe athengiswa ngobuxoki obukhulu. Baya kunithembisa ubomi obude, uxolo, nempumelelo, kodwa obo buxoki busisithiyelo nje. Kuba xa sele luthathiwe uphawu, akukho nto iya kulungisa umonakalo, kuba uYahweh utshilo!

Kuba umkhonzi akamkhulu kunenkosi yakhe, kwaye ndinibizela emisebenzini apho ndiya kunikhokela inyathelo ngenyathelo, kwaye njengakwimihla kaNowa, kuya kuba njalo. Ngaloo ndlela mna, uYeshuwa HaMashiyakhi, ndayizalisekisa injongo yam njengemvana kaThixo, kuba ndanikela ngegazi lam elingenasono kuYahweh ngokuzithandela kwam kwaye ngenxa yothando olugqibeleleyo oluvutha entliziyweni yam.

Ngaloo ndlela ngenxa yovuyo olwalubekwe phambi kwam, ndanyamezela ingxaki enkulu, intuthumbo, nosizi, (kwanangokungekho sikweni) kuba ndathatha phezu kwam zonke iziphumo zazo zonke izono zoluntu. Kwaye ndahlawulela sonke isono, ngokupheleleyo, kwaye ndakwenza oko ngokuzithandela. Ngoko khangelani kum, uYesu, umbhali nomgqibelelisi wokholo lwenu, owathi ngenxa yovuyo olwalubekwe phambi kwakhe, wawunyamezela umnqamlezo, walidela ihlazo, waza wahlala ngasekunene kwetrone kaThixo. Ngoko landelani umzekelo wam ukuze nifumane uloyiso olukhulu, kuba nakwentlango ndiya kuyenza icace indlela yenu.

Ngoko qondani, akuyi kubakho luxolo, kwaye ndinithuma ukuba nikhonze ngexesha lemfazwe enkulu nengxaki. Kuba inamba iya kulwa nabangcwele kwaye iboyise, kuba inikwe amandla okwenza oko. Kwaye abo babiza egameni leNkosi baya kusindiswa, kuba abo bangabathandiyo ubomi babo kude kube sekupheleni baya kufumana ubomi babo ngonaphakade kum, uKrestu Yesu. Kuba ndizicamagushele izono zoluntu ngegazi lam elingenasono, kwaye ndingumbingeleli omkhulu ngonaphakade ngokohlobo lukaMelkitsedeke, onikele ngegazi lam elingenasono kuYahweh kwaba kanye. Ngoko ke, musani ukubuyela kwiindlela ezindala, nezithethe zabo, neTalmud yabo, neTorah; kuba iTorah igcinwa ngokupheleleyo ngokholo kum, uYesu Krestu.

Ngaloo ndlela ngeengxaki ezinkulu, ngeentlungu ezinkulu, ngemizabalazo emikhulu, abangcwele bam baya kukhanya, kwaye uya kuba mkhulu umvuzo wabo, kuba mkhulu umvuzo wam. Kuba sithini isithembiso: Aba ngabo baphuma kwimbandezelo enkulu, bazihlambe iingubo zabo, bazenza mhlophe egazini leMvana. Ngenxa yoko, baphambi kwetrone kaThixo, bamkhonza imini nobusuku eTempileni yakhe; kwaye lowo uhleli eTroneni uya kuhlala phakathi kwabo. Abasayi kuphinda balambe, abasayi kuphinda banxanwe, nelanga alisayi kubatshisa, nobushushu bungabikho. Kuba iMvana, ephakathi kwetrone, iya kubondla, kwaye iya kubakhokelela kwimithombo ephilayo yamanzi: kwaye uThixo uya kuzisula zonke iinyembezi emehlweni abo.

Ngaloo ndlela ndiza kuthumela abo ndibabizileyo. Ndinibizela ekuthandeni uThixo ngentliziyo yenu yonke, ngomphefumlo wenu wonke, ngengqondo yenu yonke, nangamandla enu onke, kwaye ndinithuma ukuba nithande ummelwane wenu njengoko nizithanda nina. Kuba usindiso luya kuvela kumazwi anika ubomi uBawo awanikeleyo ukuze athotyelwe ngabo bonke. Ngaloo ndlela thandani ummelwane wenu, khathalelanani, ncedanani kwaye nixhasane; kuba abantu bam baya kuchithakala. Baya kuhlanganisana kwiindawo zokuhlala entlango, kwaye nangona intlango ingayi kukhuseleka, ndiya kubonelela ngendawo yokusabela abantu bam kwaye ndiya kubonelela ngeentswelo zabo.

Ngaloo ndlela ndiza kuthumela abo ndibabizileyo. Ndinithuma ukuba niyalele okungenakwenzeka njengoko nihamba eluthandweni nasemandleni amakhulu, kwanamandla endiya kunambathisa wona. Kuba niya kundihambela inyathelo ngenyathelo amaxesha amaninzi ngokuthobela, njengoko nixhomekeke kum, uKrestu Yesu, oya kunikhokela ngomoya wam; kwaye intambiso eninayo iya kunenza nihambe ngoloyiso. Kodwa kuya kwenzeka njengoko sitsho isibhalo: Banoyolo abafileyo abafela eNkosini, ukususela ngoku, ewe, utsho uMoya, ukuze baphumle emisebenzini yabo, kwaye imisebenzi yabo iyabalandela.

Inyathelo ngenyathelo niya kuhamba, kwaye ndiya kunikhokela ngaloo ndlela. Kuba ndiyindawo yenu yokusabela nenqaba yenu, kwanaye uMesiya wenu wokwenene enithembele kuye. Kwaye nangona iwaka liya kuwa ecaleni kwakho, neshumi lamawaka ngasekunene kwakho: kodwa akuyi kusondela kuwe. Kuba nitywinelwe ukuzalisekisa iinjongo zam, kwaye umvuzo omkhulu nesithembiso ndininikile ukuba sinixhase kwaye sinomeleze, kuba ndinithuma emisebenzini uBawo andimisele yona, ngoko ndinimisela njengabaphathiswa bam nabaprofeti bam.

Ngoko njengoko nisiyalela okungenakwenzeka, yazini ukuba niya kuyalela kakhulu ngomoya ozelwe ngaphakathi kwenu. Ngoko yalelani iintaba ukuba zisuswe kwaye ziphoswe elwandle, njengoko ninokholo kuYahweh. Ngoko yalelani oomoya ukuba bemke njengoko nithetha amazwi anamandla ngokholo olukhulu kuYahweh. Ngoko yalelani zonke izifo nezifo eziwubhubhisayo ukuba zimke njengoko nithetha amazwi ekufuneka athotyelwe (kwanazo zonke izinto) njengoko nithetha amazwi anamandla amakhulu ngokholo olukhulu kuYahweh. Kuba ngezicwangciso neenjongo zikaYahweh ngokwakhe ndithunyiwe, kwaye kwezo zicwangciso neenjongo ndinithuma nina, kwaye niya kundenzela imisebenzi emikhulu. Kuba uYahweh ukuko konke ngomoya wam, endiya kubatywina ngawo abanyuliweyo.

Kuba kum, uYeshuwa HaMashiyakhi, kuhlala konke ukuzala, kuba uBawo undinike zonke izinto ezulwini nasemhlabeni, ukuze ndizalisekise konke. Ngaloo ndlela uYahweh ukuko konke, kwaye ninokufikelela kuBawo nakuNyana wakhe, kuba watsho uMoses: INkosi uThixo wakho iya kukuvelisela uMprofeti phakathi kwakho, kubazalwana bakho, onjengam, uya kumphulaphula yena. Ndinguloo mprofeti athethe ngaye uMoses (kwanaye ovela kwisizwe sakwaYuda), kwaye ndingaphezulu kakhulu; kuba uBawo umisele ukuba mna, uYeshuwa HaMashiyakhi, ndibe nokongama konke, kwaye kum kuhlale konke ukuzala. Ndinguloo mprofeti athethe ngaye uMoses, kwanaye owavuswa kwabafileyo ngenxa yenjongo enjalo.

Ngaloo ndlela ndiya kunibonelela nani nalabo ndiya kunithuma kubo. Kuba niya kuba yibhikhoni yokukhanya, kwanaye ofihliweyo phambi kwamehlo kwihlabathi elimnyama nelingazinzanga. Niya kushumayela zonke izinto ezingam, iNkosi yenu nomsindisi wenu, kuba ndiyimvana kaThixo, kwanaye iPasika egqibeleleyo. Akukho lusindiso kwelinye igama ngaphandle kwelam, kwanaye uYesu Krestu Olilungisa.

Abanye benu ndiya kubathatha ndibasuse kwenye indawo ndibayise kwenye, kwaye niya kuba ngabathetheli bam, abaprofeti, nabaphathiswa. Kuya kufuneka nivavanye oomoya babo nidibana nabo kwaye nihambe ngobulumko obukhulu; kuba ihlabathi enilaziyo liya kuthatyathelwa indawo lihlabathi elingenamthetho leenkqantosi ezininzi, kwaye abanye benu baya kuthunyelwa ukuba bakhonze abo basezinkqantosini, kwaye niya kuyiqonda imiyalelo yam, kwaye niya kwenza izinto ezinkulu.

Ngaloo ndlela qondani ukuba emva kwemfazwe nentshabalaliso eza kwenzeka emva kokuba ndihlanganise abathembekileyo bam (kwaye ndisuse umthinteli), kuya kubakho isiphithiphithi esikhulu nentshabalaliso. Abantu baya kubaleka besiya ngapha nangapha, kwaye abaninzi baya kutshabalala ekungakholweni okungenalukholo, kwaye baya kuzingela kwaye babambe kwaye babulale abaninzi. Kwaye kwabaninzi, ilizwi lokuqala abaya kuliva aliyi kuba lelam, nangona elo lizwi liya kunika usindiso lobuxoki. Hayi, kwanerhamncwa eliya kuvuka ngamandla amakhulu kwinamba liya kubiza abaninzi ngobuxoki kwisiphelo sabo esingunaphakade esingenathemba.

Baya kwenza ngathi, kuba bangabakhohlisi abakhulu, kwaye baya kwenza ngathi banguye uMesiya wokwenene, kwaye abalandeli babo baya kuthi bamele uMesiya wokwenene noThixo wokwenene; kodwa andibathumanga, ngoko musani ukulandela iibhikhoni zabo zobuxoki zethemba. Kuba baya kunika ithuba lokungena kuluntu lwabo olutsha ukuze bakhe ihlabathi labo elitsha; kodwa ukuze ungene, umntu kufuneka athathe uphawu. Kwaye kwabo bathatha uphawu, ukungena kuya kunikwa kumadangatye omlilo aluhlaza kwelo chibi lomlilo likhulu livutha isulfure. Kuba bonke abathatha uphawu baya kuba nesabelo sabo emlilweni ongacimekiyo kwaye baya kuba batshintshe ubomi obungunaphakade ngeqhekeza lesonka ngeendlela zikaEsawu.

Kuya kufuneka ningawahoyi onke amazwi ngaphandle kwelam kwaye nimke kubo bonke abandilahlileyo. Kuya kufuneka ubulumko obukhulu, ubuchule, nobuqili ukuze usinde, kwaye ndiya kukunika ulwimi lwabulumko ukuze wazi ukuba umphendule njani umntu ngamnye. Kuba ndiya kulenza licace ilizwi lam kuwe, nangona liyilizwi elizolileyo, elincinci. Uya kulazi ilizwi lam, kuba ndiya kuliqinisekisa ilizwi lam kuwe amaxesha amaninzi, ngoko musa ukungakholwa kodwa ube nokholo olukhulu kumazwi am esithembiso.

Ndiya kunifundisa zonke izinto, kwaye niya kwazi ukuba niyenze njani imisebenzi endinithuma ukuba niyenze. Ilizwi lam lilizwi eligqibeleleyo, kwanelizwi lomntu okhalayo entlango, kwaye ndithetha ngelizwi lomntu okhalayo entlango; kuba bathunyiwe njalo, kwanabaprofeti bam nabaphathiswa bam abahambe phambili ukwenza iindlela zenu zithe tye neendlela zenu zicace. Ndinimisele imisebenzi emininzi emikhulu, kwaye niya kwenza ngokukhulu.

Ngoko ke, yazini zizwe zam, zihlobo zam, nabantwana besithembiso: isivuno ngokwenene sikhulu, kodwa abasebenzi bambalwa; thandazani ngoko eNkosini yesivuno, ukuba ithumele abasebenzi esivunweni sayo. Hambani ngeendlela zenu: Yabonani, ndinithuma njengeemvana phakathi kweengcuka. Kuba ndiyiNkosi yeSivuno, kwaye nangona abasebenzi bebesoloko bembalwa, kukho umvuzo omkhulu nenzuzo engunaphakade nozuko ngomsebenzi eniya kuwenza. Kuba niya kuvuna umvuzo omkhulu neziqhamo kum; kuphela musani ukutyhafa kwiimbandezelo neengxaki zenu. Olu lwahlulo lokugqibela, kwaye bonke abamiselwe ubomi obungunaphakade kwangaphambi kokusekwa kwehlabathi baya kufunyanwa, kwaye abasayi kuze balahleke, kuba akukho mntu unako ukubaxutha esandleni sam.

Ngaloo ndlela imisebenzi yenu iya kulandela kude kube ngunaphakade, kwaye kwizenzo zenu ezininzi niya kumzukisa uYahweh, ondithumileyo mna, uYeshuwa HaMashiyakhi, njengoMesiya wenu wokwenene. Kwaye ngempumelelo yenu emininzi emikhulu namazwi amaninzi anetyuwa enyaniso eniya kuwathetha, niya kubhengeza ukubuya kwam okukufuphi kwaye nihambe emisebenzini kaYohane uMbhaptizi ukulungiselela ukuza kweNkosi: ngoko bhengeza ukubuya kwam, kuba ngokuqinisekileyo ndiya kuwazalisekisa amazwi am esithembiso. Kuba ngenene ekupheleni kwengxaki kaYakobi, kwanasekupheleni kweveki yokugqibela yeminyaka, kwaneyeveki yamashumi asixhenxe kaDaniyeli, ndiya kubuya nemikhosi yasezulwini ukumisa uBukumkani bam eMhlabeni, kwanaye uBukumkani bukaThixo; kwaye ulawulo lwam lweminyaka eliwaka luya kuqala, kwanolawulo lobulungisa, ubungcwele, noxolo njengoko iNgonyama iya kulala neMvana!

Ndinibizela emisebenzini apho ndiya kunikhokela inyathelo ngenyathelo.

Ndinibizela ekuthandeni uThixo ngentliziyo yenu yonke, ngomphefumlo wenu wonke, ngengqondo yenu yonke, nangamandla enu onke, nasekuthandeni ummelwane wenu njengoko nizithanda nina.

Ndinithuma ukuba niyalele okungenakwenzeka njengoko nihamba eluthandweni olukhulu nasemandleni amakhulu.

Ndiyindawo yenu yokusabela nenqaba yenu, kwanaye uMesiya wenu wokwenene enithembele kuye.

Ndinithuma emisebenzini uBawo andimisele yona, ngoko ndinimisela njengabaphathiswa bam nabaprofeti bam.

Ndinithuma, kwaye niya kundenzela imisebenzi emikhulu.

Ndinguloo mprofeti athethe ngaye uMoses, kwanaye owavuswa kwaYuda ngenxa yenjongo enjalo.

Ndingaphezulu kakhulu, kuba kum, uYeshuwa HaMashiyakhi, kuhlala konke ukuzala.

Ndiyimvana kaThixo, kwanaye iPasika egqibeleleyo.
