Niya Kuguqukela Kubani Na?

Lafika ilizwi leNkosi kum.

INkosi uYesu Krestu ithi: Niya kuguqukela kubani na ukuze nisabe ingqumbo yam? Niya kukholosa ngabantu abakhohlakeleyo abathunyelwe ukulahlekisa nabanyulwa kanye, ukuba kunokwenzeka? Niya kukholosa ngenkqubo yerhamncwa laseBhabheli, elohenyukazi likhulu? Nikhohlisiwe na nacinga ukuba ningamkhetha ukumkani wenu ngonyulo? Aniqondi na ukuba uSathana unguthixo weli hlabathi kwaye ulahlekisa ihlabathi lonke? Niya kukholosa ngemibutho eyenziwe ngabantu nangezindlu zabaprofeti bobuxoki abathunyelwe ukulahlekisa abanyulwa bam kanye? Niya kuguqukela kubani na? Niya kuya phi na xa kungekho nto inokufihlakala emehlweni am?

Niyababona abakhohlakeleyo belungiselela ukuzifihla emiqolombeni yabo emhlabeni abazixabelele yona ukuze bandisabe, kodwa baxabelele ilize. Ingqumbo yam iyasondela kwaye akukho mntu unokuma. Bize igama lam kusekho ixesha. UBawo uzibeke ezandleni zam zonke izinto, ezulwini nasemhlabeni, kwaye ndiya kuzalisekisa izicwangciso zakhe. Akukho nto kwaye akukho mntu unokundinqanda kuba uBawo undixhobisile, kwaye andinakunqandwa. Ingqumbo yeMvana isemnyango wakho, nangona ndisamile emnyango ndinkqonkqoza. Ndilangazelela ukungena ndidle nawe ukuba ungawuvula umnyango wakho.

Bantu bam vukani!! Vukani!! VUKANI!! Vukelani ebulungiseni nize ningoni. Ndiyivile imithandazo yenu, kwaye ndikubonile ukukhathazeka komoya wenu nosizi lwenu. Yizani kum ngentliziyo yenu iphela nimke esonweni. Mkani ezonweni zeBhabheli neenkokeli zayo; ndiyanixelela ngokucacileyo mkani kuyo ngokupheleleyo, nakumabandla ayo awexukileyo. Kuba umgwebo weBhabheli usemnyango, kwaye uya kufika ngequbuliso, uyishiye iyintlango ngeyure enye. Kuba zikhulu izono zayo; ngoko ke, mkhulu nomgwebo wayo.

Ukusinda kwenu kuphela kukhuseleko lweengalo zam kwaye akukho mntu unokunixwila ezandleni zam. Musani ukudikidiki, njengoko amaxesha olwahlulo esele eqalile. Ebantwini: niya kuguqukela phi na xa ndibutha umtshakazi wam phakathi kwenu? Anifundanga na? Anivanga na?

Ndilangazelela ukungena ndidle nawe ukuba ungawuvula umnyango wakho.


Indima endiphefumlelwe ukuba ndabelane ngayo:

IsiTyhilelo 18

Nokukhanya kwesibane akusayi kuba sakhanya kuwe; nezwi lomyeni nelomtshakazi alisayi kuba savakala kuwe; ngokuba abarhwebi bakho babengamanene omhlaba; ngokuba ngeentsinga zakho zahlanhlathekiswa zonke iintlanga.

Ilizwi lomyeni nelomtshakazi alisayi kuphinda livakale kuba ndiya kumbutha umtshakazi wam ndimfihle emagumbini endiwalungiselele ixesha elizayo, kuba endlwini kaBawo zininzi iindawo zokuhlala.
