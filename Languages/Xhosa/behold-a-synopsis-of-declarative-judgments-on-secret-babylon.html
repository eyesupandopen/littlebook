Juni 16, 2025

Khangela! Isishwankathelo Sezigwebo Ezivakalisayo NgeBhabhiloni Efihlakeleyo Phambi Kokuba Isigwebo Siwe Ngokupheleleyo

Ezi zigwebo zilandelayo zivela KwiNcwadi Encincane YesiTyhilelo Isahluko se-10, ekwayiVangeli Engunaphakade. Isigwebo ngeBhabhiloni Efihlakeleyo sivakaliswe ngoku kangangeeminyaka emininzi kunye neminyaka. Okulandelayo zizigwebo ezivakalisayo ezithathwe kwiNcwadi Encincane eziza kwenzeka ngokuqinisekileyo ngokukhawuleza nangokungalindelekanga ngoku. Kulo msebenzi, ndisebenza njengomthunywa wenkundla othunyelwe ukuba avakalise izigwebo ezivela etroneni kaYahweh, ezinikwe ebusweni bukaYesu Krestu, endimkhonzayo.

Makwazeke, ukuba namhlanje kwanangoJuni 16, 2025 kwiKhalenda kaGregory (kwayona khalenda isetyenziswa yiBhabhiloni Efihlakeleyo) ukuba iNkosi yam Enyukileyo uKrestu Yesu, kwanaye uYeshua HaMashiach undiyalela ukuba ndifunde ezi zigwebo ngeBhabhiloni Efihlakeleyo, kwanazo ezi United States of America, ukuze nabani na aguquke ngelixa kusekho umzuzwana wexesha elidlulayo, nokuze isigwebo sivakaliswe phambi kokuba kuwe umlilo. Selah!

Esi sibhengezo asiphelelanga njengoko ezi zingezizo izigwebo ezitsha kodwa zikhululwe ngokupheleleyo ukusukela ngoMatshi 2, 2024, ukuya kuMatshi 20, 2025. Uninzi lwezi zivela kuYeshua HaMashiach, kunye necandelo elinye elivela kuYahweh, uMkhulu Ndinguye. Ngoku ndiza kuqhubeka nokuvakalisa.

1Khangela! Isigwebo Siyeza kwaye Amaxesha Angoku!Juni 4, 2024 (Ilizwi: Yeshua HaMashiach)

Njengoko ukuhlangana kusenzeka, umthinteli uyasuswa, kwaye intshabalalo enkulu iya kuligubungela ihlabathi, ngequbuliso nangokuphanyaza kweliso. Kuba njengoko umthinteli esusiwe, akukho nto iya kuhlala ithintela ububi ehlabathini kunye neenkanuko neminqweno emibi yoluntu. Imfazwe enkulu iya kuqhambuka emhlabeni wonke, kwaye umzalwana uya kuphinda avuke abulale umzalwana wakhe, kwaye iya kuba nkulu intshabalalo yabo. Izizwe ezininzi ziya kuwa njengeBhabhiloni Eyimfihlakalo, kwanalo ihenyukazi elikhulu elihleli phezu kwamanzi amaninzi, liya kwenziwa isizwe esingenamntu ngeyure enye. Eso sixeko sikhulu, kwaneso sizwe seUnited States ezininzi, siya kutshatyalaliswa ngokupheleleyo; kuba sizuzele isahlulo esiphindwe kabini sesigwebo, kwaye siyifanele.

Kuba saphila kamnandi esonweni esikhulu; sihleli ngekratshi elikhulu sicinga ukuba asisoze siwe kwaye izinto ziya kuqhubeka, izolo njenganamhlanje, nangomso kuya kuhlala kunjalo kungatshintshi. Kwaye ihenyukazi elikhulu lilonakalisile ihlabathi ngendebe yegolide esesandleni salo, izele ngamasikizi nokungcola kohenyo lwalo. Kwaye ke isigwebo salo siya kuwa ngequbuliso. Umlilo omkhulu uya kuzigubungela izixeko zalo njengoko imijukujelwa isiwa; kuya kubakho ukuzamazama okukhulu, kwaye eso sizwe siya kwahlukana phakathi. Iinkalo zalo zolwandle ziya kukhukuliswa kakhulu, kwaye asisayi kuphinda sivuke, kuba zikhulu izono zalo kwaye sikhulu isigwebo salo.

2Yivani Amazwi Am kwaye Niwaqonde Amaxesha Eniphila Kuwo!Meyi 23, 2024 (Ilizwi: Yeshua HaMashiach)

Yivani Amazwi am, bantu bam, kwanani nonke endinibizayo, kwaye niwaqonde amaxesha eniphila kuwo. Kuba ihlabathi enilaziyo ngoku liya kuphela, kwaye endaweni yalo kuya kusala iinkqantosi ezininzi, kwanothuthu namabhodlo eBhabhiloni Eyimfihlakalo, kuba imfazwe ifikile. Kuba njengoko ndixhwila uMtshakazi wam ndimsa emazulwini, intlekele enkulu iya kuyigubungela iBhabhiloni Eyimfihlakalo, kwaye iya kutshatyalaliswa ngeyure enye, kwaye ngeyure enye iya kutshatyalaliswa, kwaye akukho nto inokutshintsha isiphelo sayo, kuba uYahweh uvakalisile njalo. Kuba wena, Bhabhiloni Efihlakeleyo, ulilahlile ilizwi lam waza wandibeka mna, Yesu Krestu Olilungisa, ecaleni ngenxa yezonwabiso zesono. Kuba nezilumkiso ezikhulu ezinikwe phezu kwesizwe sakho emazulwini zakhonza kuphela ukubonelela ukonwabisa iintliziyo zenu ezingakholwayo namehlo enu anenkanuko, kwaye kuni yayingomnye nje umhla njengoko naniphila kamnandi ezonweni zenu eziniqhubelela entshabalalweni yenu. Nganiguqukile! Kuba nibe sisizwe sabakhonzi bezithixo, isizwe sabaxhokonxi bemfazwe, isizwe esitya ubuxoki nesithanda iinkohliso zaso, isizwe esilizalise ihlabathi ngamasikizi enu; kwaye izizwe zisela kwindebe yenu yegolide ezele ngamasikizi nokungcola kohenyo lwenu.

Kwaye ke eso siphelo siya kufika ngokupheleleyo xa ndihlanganisela abathembekileyo beBandla lam kum emafini naxa ukumka kwabo kushukumisa ihlabathi lonke. Kuya kulishukumisa ngokwenene ihlabathi, kwaye ukuzamazama okukhulu kuya kuyigubungela iBhabhiloni Efihlakeleyo, kwaye iya kukrazulwa phakathi njengoko abangcwele bam bemka. Iya kutshiswa ngokupheleleyo ngomlilo ovela esibhakabhakeni njengoko imijukujelwa isiwa. Iziketi zayo ziya kugutyungelwa ngamanzi avela empuma nasentshona! Kuba ingelosi enamandla iya kuthatha ilitye elinjengelitye elikhulu lokusila, iliphose elwandle, isithi, Ngaloo ndlela ngogonyamelo eso sixeko sikhulu iBhabhiloni siya kuwiswa phantsi, kwaye asisayi kuphinda sifunyanwe konke konke. Kuba kubhaliwe njalo kwaye kuya kwenziwa njalo! Kuba sikhulu isigwebo sayo, kwaye isigwebo siqalile endlwini kaThixo. Iicawa zayo eziwexukileyo ziya kutshatyalaliswa, kuba ndizigwebile zonke ezingezizo ezam, kwanazo zonke iicawa zomgunyathi ezithetha ubuxoki obukhulu ngokuhanahanisa. Bhabhiloni Eyimfihlakalo, owu, ukuba wawuguqukele kum, Yesu Krestu, kwiindlela zakho ezimbi, kuba ngendikusindisile, kwaye mna noYahweh asinayo nayiphi na intyikityha kwizigwebo eziza kukhululwa.

Owu, Bhabhiloni Efihlakeleyo, iibhulorho zakho ziya kuwa; iindlela zakho ziya kubhukuqwa, zaphulwe, kwaye zingenakuhanjwa; amakhonkco akho okubonelela sele edilika kwaye kungekudala aya kuba yinkqantosi. Akukho mibonelelo iya kuziswa kuwe, kuba akukho mntu uya kuza kukunceda, kwaye uya kuba wedwa kwiyure yakho yentshabalalo, owu, henyukazi elikhulu. Awuyi kuba nalo ulonwabo, kwaye abo basindayo baya kuzifumana besilwa nje ukuze bafumane amanzi okusela. Indlala iya kuligubungela ilizwe. Kwaye isibetho esikhulu siya kukugubungela, kwanazo nezilwanyana ziya kuhlasela, kuba amaxesha alapha.

Imikhosi ebomvu urhulumente wakho ongcatshayo awayivumelayo ukuba ingene emideni yakho iya kubulala yonke indoda, umfazi, nomntwana ongahambelani neenjongo zayo. Kuba iya kubulala kwaye ikhobokise bonke ababafumanayo. Yimikhosi yerhamncwa eye yahlanganiswa kwiimbombo zone zomhlaba ngenxa yale njongo, kwaye irhamncwa elifihliweyo nomprofeti wobuxoki ofihliweyo bayihlanganisile.

Abanalo uvelwano, akukho nceba, akukho lubabalo, akukho kuzisola, kwaye bayabathiya ngokupheleleyo abemi beBhabhiloni Eyimfihlakalo ngenxa yezono ezoyikekayo iinkokeli zenu eziye zazenza kumazwe abo. Kwaye ngalo lonke elo xesha uninzi lwenu lwalonwabele iimfazwe zenu ezingekho sikweni njengoko nanibukele ninokonwaba koomabonakude benu, njengoko nanithatha uyolo olukhulu ezonweni zeenkokeli zenu. Ninike imvume yenu kwiinkokeli zenu eniye nazixhasa izono zazo ngevoti, kwaye ke niya kwabelana nazo kwisigwebo sazo, kuba nizenze nifanele isahlulo esiphindwe kabini sesigwebo, kwaye siya kunigubungela ngequbuliso. Kwaye ke uMtshakazi wam uya kuvela esihlalweni sam sokugweba, kwaye abanye baya kufumana ilahleko, abanye baya kuthwala umvuzo omkhulu, kwaye bonke baya kwamkela ngokupheleleyo intlawulo yokuqala abasele beyifumene: Kuba ndiya kubanika imizimba emitsha, engenasono engasayi kuze yonakale!

3KwiBandla Lam, Umzimba Wam, Kwanaye uMtshakazi Wam: Yivani Amazwi eNkosi Yenu!Meyi 13, 2024 (Ilizwi: Yeshua HaMashiach)

Baya kungena eqongeni lehlabathi ngamandla amakhulu, kwaye umfelandawonye wookumkani abalishumi uya kulawula. Kwanangoku, umfelandawonye sele uwuhlanganisile umkhosi wawo emfihlekweni ukuze uhlasele ngokufihlakeleyo iBhabhiloni Efihlakeleyo. Bazilungiselele ngokufihlakeleyo, kwaye ngokuqinisekileyo balungelelanise uhlaselo, kwaye bayihlasela ngokucacileyo iBhabhiloni Efihlakeleyo ngokwesigwebo esivakaliswe nguThixo esiza kuyehlela, apho ingasayi kuphinda ivuke. Kuba ubugwenxa bayo bufikile phambi koPhezukonke, nezono zayo phambi kwetrone enkulu yasezulwini, kwanaphambi koThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade, kuba akukho mkhulu kunaye; kwaye isigwebo sivakaliswe ngayo.

Ndizisa ingqumbo enkulu kwiBhabhiloni Efihlakeleyo. Ixesha bubunewunewu eningasenabo. Aninakonqena kwakhona, kungenjalo niya kuzifumana kwingxaki kaYakobi, xa ngendinilungiselele indawo etafileni yam ndaza ndanitshata njengomtshakazi wam, kodwa anithembekanga nisezandleni zomnye, kwanaye uSathana! Ngoko yizani kum ukuze ndinibuyisele kumvuzo wenu. Akwanelanga ukubaleka ninesothe; kufuneka niguquke kobu bubi kwaye nitshintshe iindlela zenu kwaye nizivume ezi zono kum, kwaye ndiya kunihlambulula ngegazi lam lokucamagushela kwaye ndisule amabala kuni ngobunyulu bam obugqibeleleyo, kuba ndicocekile kwaye iingubo zam zimhlophe njengekhephu elitsha. Buyelani kum kwaye niya kuba nam ngonaphakade; phumani nize ezingalweni zam ezoluliweyo, kuba ndilapha ukuze ndinixelele ukuba aninalo naliphi na ixesha lokuchitha.

5Abangengomalungisa Abayi Kubudla Ilifa Ubukumkani BukaThixo! (Iindudumo 5 no-6)Epreli 19, 2024 (Ilizwi: Yeshua HaMashiach, Isihloko esiphindwe kabini kuMyalezo)

Mandivule amehlo enu, nina ningaziyo kwaye niyimfama ngokuzithandela, nina nihleli ebulongweni nasebumnyameni, niqengqeleka eludakeni. Aninakunika imvume yenu ebubini nicinge ukuba nindikhonza mna, kuba ndabuchasa ububi nesihendo kwada kwaphuma ithontsi lokugqibela legazi lam emhlabeni obomvu, kuba ndingumAdam wokugqibela kwaye ndenziwe umoya ophilisa. Uninzi lwenu lufunjathiswe ngamazwi alungileyo neentetho ezintle kwaye ninika imvume yenu epheleleyo kwizaphuli-mthetho nabantu aboyikekayo abaxhaphaza abantwana. Baxhaphaza abantwana, bathuthumbisa abantwana ngenxa yeenkanuko zabo ezimbi: Kuba iza ngolo hlobo ingqumbo kaThixo kwiBhabhiloni Eyimfihlakalo, kodwa uninzi lwenu luyaqhubeka nokunika imvume yenu kwezi zilwanyana zikrwada ezibekelwe bucala imililo yesihogo engunaphakade engasayi kucinywa. Phumani kuyo, bantu bam, kungenjalo niya kwabelana ngezibetho zayo nesigwebo esoyikekayo esiza kuyo; kwaye ndinilumkisile. Kuba yintoni imvisiswano anayo uKrestu noBheliyali? Ukuba nabelana ngobuxoki bayo, niya kwabelana ngezibetho zayo; ngoko zahlukaniseni ngokupheleleyo ngelizwi nangesenzo, kwaye ningavumelani nayo nakancinane. Musani ukuphila ubomi bokungakhathali, obunokuyekelela; kuba ndiya kubohlwaya bonke abenjenjalo. Nicinga ukuba ngenxa yokuba ninobomi obungunaphakade aninakufumana ilahleko enkulu nesiphumo? Ndiya kuyihlutha imivuzo yenu ndiyinike abanye, kwaye niya kuba ngabona bancinane ebukumkanini bezulu ukuba niyaqhubeka, kuba utsho njalo uYeshua HaMashiach, uMyeni, kuMtshakazi wam!

6Mlindi, Vuka!Epreli 5, 2024 (Ilizwi: Yeshua HaMashiach)

Ndiyeza, yaye andiyi kuthula, nomsindo wam uyatshisa nxamnye neBhabheli eyiMfihlo, ewe, iBhabheli eyiMfihlo ethengisa ngabantwana ezandleni zabantu abakhohlakeleyo, abaza kunqandwa. Bekuya kuba ngcono ukuba bebengazalwanga kunokuba bazalwe baze benze izono ezingenamthetho nezibuhlungu nxamnye nabantwana. Bekuya kuba ngcono ukuba ilitye lokusila belixhonywe entanyeni yabo, baze baphoswe elwandle; kodwa bagcinelwe umlilo ongunaphakade, yaye ubulungisa buza kwenziwa kungekudala, njengoko ingqumbo yeMvana isiza kugubungela umhlaba wonke nenkathazo kaYakobi iqala xa umnqandi esusiwe. Kodwa wena, Owu, Bhabheli eyiMfihlo enkulu, uwungcolisile umnyama kaBawo onemibala emininzi awawunika uNowa nalo lonke uluntu njengesithembiso sokuba akayi kuphinda awutshabalalise umhlaba wonke ngamanzi, yaye uya kuligcina ilizwi lakhe. Kodwa wena, Owu, Bhabheli eyiMfihlakalo, uya kwenziwa isizwe esiyinkangala ngeyure enye.

Umlilo omkhulu uya kuza phezu kwakho; ukushukuma okukhulu kuya kukutyumza; iinqaba zakho ziya kuwa; iziseko zakho ziya kunyamalala zibe ngamanxuwa, namaza amakhulu aya kugubungela unxweme lwakho, yaye ndiya kukuvuza ngokwemisebenzi yakho, nomgwebo omkhulu unxamnye nawe. Kuba wakha waba yisibane sethemba nokhanyiselo ehlabathini ixesha elifutshane, kodwa ukhethe ukubawa, ukuzicingela, norhwaphilizo njengamaqabane akho, Owu, henge elikhulu! Ngubani oya kukusindisa kum nomsindo wam nengqumbo yam esondelayo etshisayo phezu kwakho? Umi ngamatyathanga, ulindele umgwebo wakho wokugqibela, oya kubonakala ehlabathini lonke, noloyiko olukhulu luya kuza kwabaninzi.

Yabona! Zininzi iintlanga uBawo azibhukuqileyo emgwebeni, kuba umisa enye aze ahlise enye, kuba uYahweh ulongamile yaye akukho mkhulu kunoThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade omelayo aze asindise abakhe emlilweni ogcinelwe abakhohlakeleyo. Yabona! Iintlanga ezenza ubukhosi obukhulu zisekhona, kodwa ngendlela encitshisiweyo, kodwa ziqhubekile. Kodwa wena, Owu, henge elikhulu, uzihengezisile nabathandi abaninzi ngenxa yokubawa okubi nezono ezimnandi. Ubalahlekisile abantu bam kum ehlabathini lonke ngobunyhukunyhuku obunike iintlanga ukuba zitye zisele, yaye uya kuwiswa phantsi ngokupheleleyo, ungaphindi ubekho. Yaye eluthuthwini lwakho akuyi kuphakama isizwe esikhulu, kodwa intsalela iya kusindiswa. Isandla sotshaba sisezintanyeni zenu, yaye ndizise iintlenge emideni yenu. Ziya kuphanga, zihluthe, zithimbe okuseleyo.

Ngubani oya kukunceda? Kuba umkhanyele ummelwane wakho, yaye akukho koyika iNkosi ezintliziyweni zenu. Uthe, Andingomhlolokazi, kodwa ungumhlolokazi kwaye uyimfama nesisithulu phambi kwam. Akukho lusindiso kuwe ngenxa yokuhenyuza kwakho okukrexezayo okwenzileyo nehlabathi; ngoko ke, ndiya kukuzisela intshabalalo epheleleyo. Iziganeko ezizayo ziya kwenzeka ngokukhawuleza nangakumbi; emva koko unogumbe omkhulu uya kugubungela izikhaka zakho. Umlilo uya kukutshisa ngokupheleleyo uze uhlambulule abenzi bobubi elizweni, nabathinjwa bam baya kukhululwa, yaye akukho nto inokunqanda okuza kwenzeka. NdinguYehova uYesu Krestu, yaye ndikuxelela kungekenzeki, yaye ndivakalisa okuza kwenzeka ngokungathi sekwenzekile, kuba ziqinisekile ezi ntetho, yaye akukho nto inokuziguqula, njengoko iziprofeto ziya kuzaliseka. Yimani niqinile, bantu bam, njengoko ndiza kumthabatha umtshakazi wam; kuba ndinezicwangciso neenjongo ngaye kubukumkani bam obuzayo.

7Andikugwebelanga Na?Epreli 1, 2024 (Ilizwi: Yeshua HaMashiach)

Abo bangazahluliyo kuye baya kwabelana naye emgwebeni wakhe, ngoko ke kutheni usakhetha ukufa kunobomi endikunike bona, obuhlawulelwe ngegazi lam elingenasono? Imfazwe isiza kuza ngokupheleleyo elizweni nesizwe sakho, yaye nangona sele iqhubeka — ewe, imfazwe engavunywanga abayilwayo nxamnye nawe — kungekudala iya kuqhambuka ehlabathini lonke njengoko amatywina ekhululwa. Imfazwe enkulu nentshutshiso enkulu iya kuza phezu komhlaba njengoko beya kuzama ukutshabalalisa nantoni na kaThixo noKrestu wakhe njengoko beya kuthi ekugqibeleni bavakalise imfazwe engcwele nxamnye nawe. Baya kubamba abaninzi, yaye abaninzi baya kuthathwa, yaye abo baya kusindiswa baya kuba nokhetho olunye kuphela: ukunyamezela kude kube sekupheleni bangabathandi ubomi babo kude kube sekupheleni baze babize egameni lam kude kube sekupheleni.

Iinkokeli zenu zinijikele yaye ziya kubingelela abaninzi komubi emadabini aza kuqhambuka kungekudala. Isizwe sakho siya kuhlaselwa ngaphakathi nangaphandle. Imikhosi ebomvu sele ilapha, yaye ilindele imiyalelo yayo; kuba iceba uhlaselo olothusayo, yaye abaninzi baya kuthathwa. Imikhosi yomubi iya kuthatha yonke into elizweni ukuze ibe yeyayo nakwabo bandikhabayo. Baya kuba umhlaba wenu, amakhaya enu, inzala yenu; yaye ubomi obuninzi buya kulahleka ekungakholweni. Imfazwe iza ngaphandle njengoko iintlanga ezininzi zimanyene nxamnye nani; yaye zinemvume yeenkokeli zenu, yaye iinkokeli zenu zisebenzela ukufa kwenu. Bafuna ilizwe labo, ilizwe elinikezelwe kuSathana apho banokulandela ngokupheleleyo ububi obuhleli kubo, yaye akukho nto iya kuthintela iminqweno yabo emibi, njengoko umnqandi eya kuba engekho.

Baya kuyitshisa ngokupheleleyo iBhabheli eyiMfihlo ngomlilo, yaye nabani na ongasindiyo emalangatyeni uya kubulawa yindlala; nabani na ongasindiyo endlaleni uya kubulawa sisibetho; nabani na ongasindiyo elunxwemeni lwekrele uya kunikelwa esibondeni ngenxa yegama lam, yaye abaninzi baya kunqunyulwa iintloko njengoko ilizwi lam elingcwele livakalisa. Yaye abo bafela igama lam baya kuba nomvuzo omkhulu, yaye ndiya kuzosula zonke iinyembezi emehlweni abo. Yazini, bantwana bam, ukuba zonke izinto ezimnandi nezono ezixatyiswe ngabantu abakhohlakeleyo ziya kunyamalala nje, yaye imbandezelo nosizi ziya kuma endaweni yazo. Ubumnyama buya kugubungela ilizwe, yaye kuya kubakho ukunqaba kwelizwi lam, nelizwi lomyeni nelizwi lomtshakazi aziyi kuphinda ziviwe kuye.
