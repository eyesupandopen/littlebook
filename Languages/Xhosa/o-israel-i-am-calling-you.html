Ingqina eliMbaxa: Sirayeli
Okukrakra Okumnandi
Novemba 3, 2024

Owu Sirayeli, NDIYAKUBIZA!

KuYahweh, UThixo Omkhulu Ongunaphakade:

Yivani aMazwi kaYahweh, uMkhulu Ndinguye kwaye nicinge ngombuzo wam. Kutheni iimpuku zibaleka indlu ewayo, bantwana bam? Impendulo ecacileyo yeyokuba zisindisa ubomi bazo, ngaphandle kweempuku ezibaleka iBhabhiloni Efihlakeleyo zikwenza oko ukuze zifihle ulwaphulo-mthetho lwazo, ukuze zikwazi ukuyiphanga ngokupheleleyo ukuze zifumane ulawulo olufutshane endizinika lona. Ekubeni umntu wemvelo ethanda ubumnyama, ngoku ndiza kumnika ubumnyama abuthanda kangaka. Kodwa, ndibhale (kwaye ndisaza kubhala) zonke izenzo zazo zonke izidalwa kwindalo yam ezinenkululeko yokuzikhethela kuba lowo unikwe okuninzi kufuneka okuninzi kuye.

Kwaye akukho nto ifihlakeleyo ekujongeni kwam, kwaye akukho nto ifihlakeleyo emehlweni am, kwaye Ndinguye obonayo, obhalayo, nokhumbulayo SONKE isenzo esibi esakha senziwa kwindalo yam. Kwaye akukho nto inokwenzeka endingayaziyo kwaye ndingayivumeli! Emehlweni am, zonke iingcinga zityhilwe. Emehlweni am zonke iinjongo zentliziyo zityhilwe kwaye akukho nto okanye mntu owadaliwayo okanye owenziwayo ongekho phantsi kwesandla sam esinamandla, kuba ndinguThixo Ongunaphakade wobulungisa ekufuneka niphathane naye, Owu Sirayeli kwaye ndiyanibona ngokupheleleyo, nangona nikhetha ukungandithobeli kubumfama obuyinxalenye ngenxa yobulukhuni beentliziyo zenu.

Kuba, xa ndafika kuni ndingumntu ekuphela koMesiya endakha ndamthuma kwaye ekuphela koMesiya endiya kuze ndimthume, ndaliwa ngokupheleleyo ngooyihlo. Kuba, Ndinguye owadala uYeshua HaMashiach enyameni ukuze kusindiswe bonke ababiza egameni lakhe elinamandla, enithi ngokungazi nimale nkqu ngokukrakra nangokunyelisa nimbize isithixo se-goyim. Owu Sirayeli, uninzi lwenu nizidibanise neNdlu yesiNtu kaSathana kwaye ngoku nilindele ngethemba uMoshiach wenu wobuxoki u-Esawu amlungiselele ukuxhelwa kukaYakobi.

Ngoko ke yivani ilizwi loMKHULU NDINGUYE kuba ilizwi lam elidudumayo, elingunaphakade liyanibiza kweli xesha lokugqibela kwaye ndithumela uNyana wam kuni kwakhona ukuze ndikhulule uYakobi nentsalela eyamkela uNyana wam Ongunaphakade, uYeshua HaMashiach, iya kusindiswa nangesandla sakhe esinamandla kwaye akukho olunye ukhetho losindiso. Kwaye akusayi kuze kubekho naluphi na olunye ukhetho okanye igama elinikiweyo losindiso kuba ndimnike igama elingaphezu kwawo onke amagama, ukuze egameni likaYesu waseNazarete onke amadolo agobe. Kwaye abo bangavumiyo, baya kunyanzeliswa ukuba baqubude kwiNgonyama yeSizwe sakwaYuda endiyithumela emhlabeni ukuze agwebe kwaye alwe nkqu ngobulungisa obungunaphakade, kwaye nguye oya kuzisa yonke indalo yam phantsi kolawulo lobulungisa kwaye uya kulawula ngentonga yentsimbi kwaye umgwebo, ubabalo, nenceba zikunye naye ngonaphakade. Qondani ukuba ibanzi indlela eya entshabalalweni kwaye amagama amaninzi noothixo bobuxoki bafuna ukunisa kwilifa labo lechibi lomlilo elivutha ngonaphakade, elitshisa ngesulfure amadangatye ashushu esohlwayo.

Owu Sirayeli, hayi indlela eniyaphule ngayo intliziyo kaYahweh ekungathobelini kwenu kwaye intliziyo yam isaphuka ngenxa yenu ukusukela koonaphakade abadlulileyo apho ndandisazi ekwazini kwam kwangaphambili ukuba kwicebo lam lokuhlangula ooyihlo benu baya kumala uYesu waseNazarete bekhetha uBharabhas, owayengumbulali nenkokeli yeemvukelo zokuvukela umbuso kumazwe ngelo xesha. Kwaye ngoko ndamenza ukuba ajikele kwabeeNtlanga, kwaye bamva okwethutyana, kodwa ubumfama ngokuyinxenye benzeka kuSirayeli ukuze intsalela kaSirayeli isenokufikelela elusindisweni ngexesha elifanelekileyo. Qondani ukuba abaninzi bakwaYuda bamenza iNkosi yabo kwaye bafumana usindiso olungunaphakade kuSirayeli kaThixo kwaye baya kuba nonyana wam ngonaphakade. Kwaye Ndinguye othumele uYesu waseNazarete phakathi kweentlanga ukuze ndikhulule abazalwana benu ababesasazeke phesheya ngenxa yokuvukela kwabo, nkqu nabo babengenathemba kwaye abaninzi abavela ezintlangeni bafumene usindiso olungunaphakade kwaye ngoku baxhunyelelwe ngonaphakade ezizweni. Ngoko qonda Owu Sirayeli ukuba oko uNaphakade Ndinguye akuhlanjululeyo ungakubizi ngokuba yinto eqhelekileyo okanye engcolileyo!

Kodwa nina Owu Sirayeli, namala naza nambulala unyana wam uYesu Krestu kwaye namnikela ezandleni zabantu abanesono ababezimisele ukumkhulula ekubeni engenatyala. Kodwa belingaphumelela njani elinye icebo lam losindiso olungunaphakade ngaphandle kokuba iimfuno zemithetho engunaphakade nengenakuguqulwa kaYahweh zifezekiswe, yona mithetho endayimisela ukuze ucwangco lobulungisa lwendalo yam yonke lulawulwe ngobulungisa obugqibeleleyo. Kwaye ngoko uYesu waseNazarete wazinikela njengeMvana kaThixo njengoMbingeleli wenu oMkhulu ongunaphakade ngokohlobo lukaMelkitsedeke ukuze ahlawule isohlwayo esabekwa nguMoses ngenxa yezono nezikreqo zoluntu kwaye ahlangule uluntu ngokupheleleyo kwisiqalekiso somthetho. Kuba uMoses weza njengomfundisi-ntsapho kaSirayeli ukuze athathe aze alondoloze uSirayeli ngenxa kaYeshua HaMashiach kwaye uhlawule ngokupheleleyo ngenkululeko yenu, kwaye nangona ningabafundi abavukelayo intsalela iya kusabela kohlwayo lwam nkqu nengxaki eza kuni ngeveki yamashumi asixhenxe kaDaniyeli esemnyango wenu kwaye kungekudala iza kukhaba ivule iingcango zenu kukhetho olungunaphakade.

Ngoko qondani ukuba ngubani enimalayo, Owu Sirayeli, endimthanda ngothando olungunaphakade, kuba nina ningoabo ndathembisa ooyihlo benu kwiminqophiso emininzi kwiminyaka edlulileyo ukuba kula maxesha omgwebo ndiya kunigcina ngenxa yabo, kwaye ndinguye uThixo Ongunaphakade Wobulungisa ongaguqukiyo! Kwaye NDINGUYE okhumbula izithethelelo ezininzi ezenziwa ngooyihlo benu ngenxa yenu kwaye zonke izithethelelo ndizikhumbula ilizwi nelizwi kwaye ndiya kusihlonipha isicelo sabo kwabo kuni bazimisele nabathobelayo. Kwaye nangoku uNyana wam, enithi ngokungazi nimthiye, uyanithethelela eluthandweni lwakhe olungunaphakade ngenxa yezalamane zakhe kuba uYeshua HaMashiach ngumkhululi osisalamane sikaSirayeli, nkqu naye othenge umhlaba ngegazi lakhe lokucamagushela kwaye unikulule ezandleni zotshaba ukuba nikhetha ukwamkela. Nangona, abaninzi phakathi kwenu baludle ilifa uthando lwabo lweYiputa kooyihlo benu, musani ukuzivumela ukuba niwe ngonaphakade entlango yabangakholwayo.

Kuba uYeshua HaMashiach nguye oya kusindisa intsalela kuYakobi eya kuza kuye ngokupheleleyo, nkqu nangezilingo zeveki yamashumi asixhenxe kaDaniyeli. Ngoko ke qondani amaxesha eniphila kuwo kwaye nivuke kubumfama benu obuyinxalenye ngomyalelo wam xa nijikela kuYeshua HaMashiach, endimthumele usindiso lwenu njengoMesiya wenu (xa ningasayi kuba mfiliba ngenxa yobulukhuni beentliziyo zenu). Kuba niya kujikela kunyana wobulungisa kwaye uya kunamkela kuye, kwaye uya kunisa ekhuselekweni aze anifihle kwintshabalalo yenu ezandleni zikaMoshiach wobuxoki ka-Esawu.

Ngoko qondani ukuba niphathana nabani kuba NDINGUThixo weminqophiso emininzi, kwaye NDINGUThixo wezithembiso ezininzi, kwaye NDINGUThixo Ongunaphakade Wobulungisa oza kwakhona ukubiza abantu bam ukuba babuyele kumalusi wabo oyintloko endimthumele usindiso luka-Adam. Kwaye zininzi iinkalo zokuba ndingubani, kwaye igama ngalinye linentsingiselo yalo ngokunxulumene noThixo Ongunaphakade. Ngoko ndiveni kwaye nindive kakuhle kwaye niqonde uYahweh, onibizela kum ukuze nifumane usindiso. Kwaye ngoko ndiyanikhumbuza kwaye ndinifundise njengeentsana, xa eneneni nifanele ukuba ngabafundisi, Owu Sirayeli! Ngoko fundani ngoThixo wenu Ongunaphakade Wobulungisa!

Kwaye maninzi amanye amagama neempawu endinazo, ngoko yivani ilizwi lam eligquma njengeendudumo emazulwini, Owu Sirayeli, kuba NDINGUThixo wooyihlo enithi nimnqule ngobuxoki ngenkani.

NDINGUThixo ka-Abraham enimlahlileyo!

NDINGUThixo kaYakobi nkqu nalowo nimshiyileyo ukuze niye ezingalweni zomnye, kwaye nibalekele koothixo benu bobuxoki nkqu niziqhenya ngokuvukela kwenu. Qonda Sirayeli ukuba usezintanjeni zeNdlu yesiNtu kaSathana nkqu nabo bathi bangabakaSirayeli abaxokayo kwaye abangabo.

Liveni ilizwi lam, O Sirayeli, nize kuYeshua HaMashiach kweli xesha lokugqibela kuba ndiyanibiza eluthandweni lwam ngani kwaye ndiyanolulela ubabalo lwam nenceba yam kweli xesha. Kodwa niluhlanga olunenkani, yaye ubulukhuni nentliziyo elukhuni yooyihlo nizenzile ezakuni, yaye nimkhabile uThixo wooyihlo enimnqulayo ngendlela engafundiswanga neyobuxoki, kuba abantu bam bayatshabalala ngenxa yokuswela ulwazi, ngoko yizani kuYeshua HaMashiach nifunde ngaye ukuze nisafumana usindiso ngesandla sakhe esinamandla.

O Sirayeli, intliziyo yam yaphuka kanjani ngenxa yenu, kuba ndanithabatha njengesityalo esithambileyo kwintlango eyinkqantosi ekulungele ukutshabalala nokubuna ngonaphakade, ndaninika ubomi ndanenza abantu abakhethekileyo kum. Ndenza iminqophiso emininzi nooyihlo, kwaneminqophiso ababengenakuyigcina ngenxa yesono norhwaphilizo olukwigazi lika-Adam. Kodwa MNA NdinguYehova uThixo wenu okhumbulayo nozigcinileyo izithembiso neminqophiso yam, kwaye ndisaza kuzalisekisa sonke isithembiso nomnqophiso owakhe wenziwa eluntwini ebusweni bukaYeshua HaMashiach, onguNyana wam oyintanda uYesu Krestu.

O Sirayeli, niya kuhlala ningakholwa yaye ningathobeli na? Niya kuhlala nimanyana neentshaba zenu kwisohlwayo sazo sanaphakade na? Kwaye kunjalo ke ukuba anithobelanga kwaye nihambe ekungabonini okuyinxalenye, kwaye ndilususile uhlanga lwenu kuni kwaye anisalawulwa nguYehova uThixo Omkhulu Ongunaphakade. Kodwa ekuvukeleni kwenu nilawulwa sisandla seentshaba zenu ezenza ngathi zingooYakobi kanti eneneni zingoo-Esawu. Qondani ukuba bonke abamkhabayo uYeshua HaMashiach bajikile kuYehova baya koothixo bobuxoki nokungakholwa, kwaye abaninzi bathakathiwe babanjwa bubukhoboka kwiityhubhu zabo ezimnyama abazinqula ngokungazi ekungakholweni. Ngoko qondani, bonke abo balahlekiswayo nguMoshiach wabo wobuxoki okanye uMahdi wabo wobuxoki kwiinkolo zabo zobuxoki ezingakhokeleli kuYehova okanye kubomi obungunaphakade, baya kufumana intshabalalo engunaphakade bonke abo bakhetha ukuhlala kuzo.

O Sirayeli, yizani kum ngoNyana wam uYeshua HaMashiach okwanguYesu waseNazarete, enimkhabayo njengoko benjenjalo ooyihlo ngaphambi kwenu. Musani ukutshabalala ezonweni zooyihlo, namkele uthando lwam nedini endalinikela ngenxa yenu, idini elo lonke uluntu luka-Adam lwasikelelwa ngalo, kwaye abaninzi abavela kumazwe neentlanga beza eNkosini yabo ngeguquko kwaye basikelelwe bahlangulwa ngonaphakade. Kwaye qondani ukuba MNA ndingulowo ubeke zonke izinto ezandleni zikaKrestu Yesu ezivusiweyo, ezinamandla neziguquliweyo, kuba MNA ndingulowo umenze umoya ophilisa kwaye ulawulo nobukumkani bakhe abuyi kuba nasiphelo!

Ngoko ke, yonke iminqophiso yam ukusuka ku-Adam ukuya kuMoses, nkqu noMyolelo kaMoses ngoku uza kuzalisekiswa ebusweni bukaYesu Krestu ngoMnqophiso wam Omtsha onguMyolelo wam Omtsha onikelwe ukuzalisekisa zonke izinto ukusuka ku-Adam ukuya kuKrestu. Kuba abo bafumana ubomi obungunaphakade kufuneka bazithobe baguqe idolo kuNyana wam okuphela kwamzeleyo, endisoloko ndikholiswa nguye. Ngoko yizani kum ebusweni bukaYeshua HaMashiach, owathi ngegazi lakhe elingenasono wawuqinisa uMnqophiso wakhe Omtsha, onguMyolelo Omtsha, endiwamkele ngokupheleleyo ndawucwangcisa ngaphambi kokuba kusekwe iziseko zehlabathi. Kwaye ndiya kuthumela ama-144,000 am, ekhokelwa nguYeshua HaMashiach, ukuba anisindise, kwaye baya kunifihla endaweni endiyilungiselele nina, kwaye apho niya kunyanyekelwa nisindiswe niphila ku-Esawu olungiselela intshabalalo yenu.

Noko ke, u-Esawu, ocinezela abantu bam, uyayishiya iBhabhiloni eyimfihlakalo kuba beneenjongo zokugquma izono zabo. Isono iimpuku ezishiya indlu ewayo zingenatyala laso. Ngoko ke, baya kuzama ukungcwaba ulwaphulo-mthetho lwabo nkqu nemihlakulo yabo yesono abayisebenzisileyo ukugquma ulwaphulo-mthetho lwabo, kwaye ndisaza kubabhenca, itsho iNkosi yemikhosi. Kuba bayiphanzisile ngokupheleleyo iBhabhiloni eyimfihlo kanye njengoko ndikuvumeleyo ukuba kwenzeke kwisabelo sam esiphindwe kabini somgwebo phezu kwaloohenyukazi mkhulu! Kwaye ngoko baziqokelelele bonke ubukhosi bemfazwe beBhabhiloni eyimfihlo kwaye baya kuqhubeka bephanga de iBhabhiloni eyimfihlo itshatyalaliswe kungekudala ngeyure enye kwaye ayisayi kuphinda ivuke. Qondani ukuba akukho sibhamu senziweyo okanye esakhiweyo nxamnye nani esiya kuphumelela ukuba nisabiza uMesiya wenu wokwenene, uYeshua HaMashiach!

Ngoko qondani ukuba iNdlu yesikhungu kaSathana inemo yokuhlonela uThixo kwinkangeleko yayo yangaphandle, kodwa bangaboyise umtyholi kwaye iinkanuko zoyise ziya kuzenza kuba u-Esawu wayonakalisa inzala yakhe ngonaphakade. Lo mungu ungendawo, nkqu neenkokeli zenu ezibonakala zilungile zingumungu ongendawo kwaye abaninzi bangabaxhaphazi babantwana abazingela abancinci nabalahlekileyo ezonweni zeSodom neGomora, kwaye bazingela abantwana, kwaye abangobalusi abalungileyo kodwa banomhlwa. Kwaye inamba enguyise wabo ngulowo ubakhokelayo, kwanayona yanika abalusi ababini abanomhlwa ukuba balawule kwaye baluse umhlaba ngokungalunganga. Kwanerhamncwa elivela elwandle eliya kuphakama njengoMahdi wabo kunye nerhamncwa elivela emhlabeni, elikwanguMoshiach wabo wobuxoki kwaye liya kuza kulawula ilizwe kwaye ngenene liya kulawula ihlabathi ngokungalunganga.

Kwaye ibala lomhlwa wabo liyangcolisa umzimba wabo wonke, kwaye usindiso abanakuze balufumane. Kuba bonke abo baziguqula ngokwemfuza ngolo hlobo okanye abazalwa ngabawileyo abanasindiso kuba benziwe ngokomfanekiso wenamba kwaye ngaloo mfanekiso banjalo kuko konke. Oko kugwenxa akunakwenziwa kuthi tye, kwaye onke amasikizi abawileyo anokwenza ububi kuphela kuba enzelwe ukuzisa intshabalalo yoluntu, kwaye ngebaphumelele ukuba andibamisanga ngemihla kaNowa.

Kwaye noko ke ziya kuphakama kwakhona, kuba ndizisa iingxilimbela ukuzalisekisa ingqumbo yam! Kwaye iNdlu yesikhungu kaSathana isebenzela ukugquma yonke imikhondo yokuba bangoobani na ngokwenene phambi kokuba intlekele ifike kwaye inkohliso yabo ifikelele kwintlekele kuba abawileyo bavunyelwe ukutshabalalisa iBhabhiloni eyimfihlo. Noko ke sonke isono siya kunikelwa ngokupheleleyo ezifubeni zabo kwaye ngoko ke, akunakwenzeka ukuzifihla kuYehova noNyana wam uYeshua HaMashiach kwaye ingqumbo yethu iya kubafumana kanye njengoko bezama ukuzifihla entliziyweni yomhlaba. Akukho namnye ophantsi komgwebo omkhulu onokubaleka ichibi lomlilo, ngaphandle kwabo babhaliweyo encwadini yobomi yeMvana kwaye akukho namnye onokubaleka izono zakhe ngaphandle kwabo usindiso lujoliswe kubo nkqu nabo bamkela isivumelwano sam soxolo. Nokuba zingaphi na iintlanga abazitshabalalisayo kwaye nokuba bazifihla kude kangakanani na emhlabeni, abayi kubaleka impindezelo nengqumbo abaziqokelelele yona kwaye ndiya kubabuyisela ngokupheleleyo itsho iNkosi engunaphakade.

Kuba ndiya kuwuguqula umhlaba ukusuka kulungiselelo lwawo lwangoku uye kubuqaqawuli bawo bokuqala, kwaye abahlangulweyo bomhlaba baya kuhamba apho bekhuselekile. Kwaye ngoko oko kukhoyo, kuya kuguqulwa kube koko kuya kubakho, kwaye abayi kubaleka, nkqu nabo bonke abashiyelwe umgwebo apho kuya kwenzeka iintlekele ezininzi, kwaye umkhenkce uya kunyibilika, kwaye iintlanga ziya kutshintsha, kwaye umhlaba uya kubuyiselwa kubuqaqawuli bawo bangaphambili. Ubuqaqawuli abawileyo ababuguqulela ekonakaleni ukuze bufanelane neenkanuko zabo.

Kwaye mna ndinguYehova, uThixo Omkhulu Ongunaphakade, kwanalowo wadala amazulu nomhlaba, kwanalowo ungunaphakade kanaphakade, kwaye ngokwentando yam necebo lam ndathetha izinto ezikhoyo nkqu nakoko kwakungekho. Ngoko liveni ilizwi lam, O Sirayeli, nkqu nani nonke enifuna ukuwatyeshela amazwi am ngobulukhuni bentliziyo. Umgwebo sele uza kufika ngokupheleleyo emhlabeni kwaye ndiya kumbhenca wonke u-Esawu ukuba ungubani na ngokwenene, kwaye ndiya kubabhenca ngokupheleleyo ooyise babo ababotshelelwe ngonaphakade kwiinkanuko zabo.

Yabonani! Abo baka-Esawu babanga ubulungisa babo, becinga ngokungazi ukuba bangabantu bam abanyuliweyo, kwaye uMoshiach wabo wobuxoki, kwanolo phondo luncinane, uya kubanga ukuba yena, ngokwakhe, unguThixo! (Akukho namnye onjengoYehova, uThixo Omkhulu Ongunaphakade noSomandla!) Kwaye abawileyo abanakuzihlangula ezandleni zam ezinamandla onke, kwaye uYeshua HaMashiach uya kuyityumza ngokupheleleyo intloko kaSathana njengoko ndathembisa u-Adam. Akukho nzulu emhlabeni apho banokuzifihla khona, nangona bezama, kwaye ngoku babotshelelwe emhlabeni nkqu naphantsi kwesibhakabhaka abangenakubaleka kuso. Akukho ezinye izazisi abanokuzithatha apho ndingayi kubabona ukuba bangoobani na ngokwenene. Akukho zimo banokuzithatha apho ndingabazi kwaye ndibabone ngokupheleleyo, kude kube seentliziyweni zabo ezingcolileyo. Kwaye wonke umntu wokugqibela wabawileyo ukusuka komncinci ukuya komkhulu nabo bonke abalandela iindlela zabo ezimbi baya kuba nelifa labo lanaphakade kuloo malangatye esohlwayo kwelo chibi lomlilo elivuthayo ngonaphakade apho impethu yabo ingafi.

Ngoko ke yenzani isililo, O Sirayeli, ngenxa yenu enala ngenkani isimemo sam sobomi obungunaphakade, nkqu nabo bonke abaya kugqiba ukulandela abawileyo nabo bonke abaya kuzimanya nenamba nabo bonke abaka-Adam ababenokhetho losindiso kodwa endaweni yoko bakhetha izono zabo eziya kubakhokelela ekufeni kwesibini ukuba ababizanga egameni likaYeshua HaMashiach ukuze basindiswe.

Ngoko ke ndiza kucacisa iindlela enisele nazo. Ninokwamkela uMesiya wenu wokwenene, uYeshua HaMashiach endimthumileyo, kwaye niya kusindiselwa kumvuzo nozuko lwanaphakade, apho izilingo nentshutshiso yenu zingafanelekanga ukuthelekiswa nazo. Okanye ukuba nabani na uyaqhubeka okanye azimanye nenamba kulungelelwaniso lwayo olutsha, uya kube wala ubomi obungunaphakade kwaye endaweni yoko wamkela ngokupheleleyo ukugwetywa kwakho ngenxa yezono zenyama, kwaye uya kuwuthwala ngokufanelekileyo umgwebo wakho xa wawucengwa ukuba ubaleke ngokuthobela, nto leyo uya kube uyalile.

Nabani na othatha uphawu lwerhamncwa, uya kube ewuguqule ngonaphakade umfanekiso kaYehova awadalwa ngawo, emshiya ekwimeko engenakuhlangulwa. Ngoko ke, ndiyanicenga ukuba nikhethe ubomi ukuze niphile ngonaphakade eluthandweni neentsikelelo zomdali wenu ezibonakaliswe ngokupheleleyo ebusweni bukaYesu Krestu, uYeshua HaMashiach! Kungenjalo, niya kuzimanya nabawileyo kwilifa labo lesulfure evuthayo kwelo chibi lomlilo lanaphakade, apho isohlwayo nembuyekezo yezono kuphela konaphakade eniya kukwazi.

Qondani, ukuba injongo yam asikokuba ndibe nenkohlakalo okanye ndingabi nabulungisa kwindalo yam, kodwa imithetho yam yobulungisa ayiguquki kwaye ayinakuguqulwa. Kwaye ngoko indalo iyancwina ikhala kum ngamandla phantsi kobunzima bezono zabawileyo nezono zoluntu, kwaye amaxesha okuhlanjululwa nokucocwa komhlaba ngomgwebo ebubini afikile; nangona imbewu yovukelo iya kuhlala kodwa ubukumkani beentshaba abuyi kuphinda buvuke. Nangona uSathana eya kuzama uhlaselo lwakhe lokugqibela kwixesha lakhe elifutshane phambi kokuba aphoswe ngonaphakade emlilweni kuba ayinguye ukumkani, kodwa itrone yakhe iya kuba ngamadangatye angenakucinywa obunyulu obushushu kakhulu, kwaye abayi kuba nanceba.

Kuba ithini na imithetho yam engunaphakade kwabangathobeliyo, ngoko khumbulani ukuba umvuzo wesono kukufa, kwaye ngoko u-Adam wona waza wabuphelisa ubomi bakhe kumvuzo wesono, okukufa. Noko ke amadini anyukayo namadini amandulo ayenganelanga ukumhlangula ngokupheleleyo unyana wam wolahleko, kwaye ngoko umntu ofezekileyo ongenasono kuphela kwedini elamkelekileyo kuba u-Adam wayefezekile kwaye engenasono xa wonayo.

Ngoko mna, uYehova Omkhulu Ndinguye, ndithumele uYeshua HaMashiach njengoko kwathenjiswayo njengemvana kaThixo eyaphalaza igazi layo elingenasono ngokuzithandela ukuhlawula isohlwayo sikaMoses ngokuphalaza igazi layo elingenasono kwintlungu engathethekiyo ukuze uluntu luhlangulwe kwisiqalekiso somthetho, nkqu nabo bonke abakhetha ukuba sisidalwa esitsha kuKrestu Yesu ngonaphakade kuba lo Krestu Yesu mnye unguYeshua HaMashiach!

MNA NdinguYehova, uThixo Omkhulu Ongunaphakade, kwaye MNA Ndinguye Endinguye.

MNA ndingulowo uthumele uYesu Krestu, uYeshua HaMashiach, ukusindisa indalo yam ngoko ke mphulaphuleni nithobele amazwi akhe njengoko uMoses wayalela.

MNA ndingulowo uthumele la mazwi ngesithunywa sam nomkhonzi wam uNyana wam uKrestu Yesu ammisileyo wamvusa ngenjongo enjalo ngomyalelo wam kuba uYeshua HaMashiach usoloko esenza ukuthanda kukaYise kwaye simanyene ngenjongo.

MNA NdinguThixo Ophilayo onibizayo, O Sirayeli, ukuze bonke abamiselwe ubomi obungunaphakade beze kukhuseleko lomalusi wabo oyintloko.

Ngoko ke liveni ilizwi lam O Sirayeli nize kuNyana wam ngenguquko epheleleyo namkele idini lakhe, kuba ngenene ukuba niyalukhaba ubizo lwakhe akusekho dini lesono kodwa umgwebo ongunaphakade kuba niya kube nikhethe ububi ngaphezu kobulungisa ngokuzithandela nangonaphakade, kwaye ndiya kubanika bonke abenza olo khetho iziphumo ezipheleleyo zesigqibo sabo, kodwa qondani ukuba bonke bakhululekile ukukhetha.

MNA ndingulowo uya kwenza zonke izinto zibe ntsha!

MNA NdinguYise wezikhanyiso oya kwenza ikhaya lakhe nabantu kumazulu amatsha nomhlaba omtsha endiya kuwudala ngokupheleleyo ngobulungisa kwaye akukho nto imbi iya kuze ingene kuwo. Kwaye ndiya kunika eso sixeko singcwele iYerusalem eNtsha aboyisi bam nabo bonke abeza kuYeshua HaMashiach, okwanguYesu Krestu kwaye baya kudla kumthi wobomi kwaye baya kuba nokungena kweso sixeko singcwele nesigqibeleleyo kwaye ndiya kuba nguThixo wabo, kwaye baya kuba ngabantu bam ngonaphakade, Ewe no-Amen.

Mangeni uNyana, hleze aqumbe nize nitshabalale endleleni.

Amaxesha ngoku.
