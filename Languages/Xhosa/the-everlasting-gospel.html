Ingqina eliMbaxa: uSirayeli
Okukrakra Okumnandi
Agasti 04, 2024

IVangeli Engunaphakade!

Yivani amazwi kaYeshua HaMashiach nithobe iindlebe zenu ngokuthobela. Ngoko ke amaxesha onyaka ayatshintsha kwaye usuku lufikile apho intlekele iya kuwela ngequbuliso emhlabeni. Ngesiquphe ndiya kuwahlanganisela kum kwamafu amathembekileyo am kwaye ngesiquphe intshabalalo iya kuwela kuwo wonke umhlaba kwaye ngesiquphe umhlaba eniwaziyo uya kutshintshwa ngonaphakade, ungaphinde ubuyele kule meko yonakeleyo neyesono. Nangona isiphelo solawulo lukaSathana lwesono sibonakala, olo suku aluyi kufika ngokupheleleyo de onke amagunya, negunya leNamba libhukuqwe ekufikeni kwam kwesibini xa ndiya kuza kulawula umhlaba ngobulungisa namajoni asezulwini, kwanemikhosi emikhulu yasezulwini apho kuya kubakho iziqhamo zokuqala ezivela kwabafileyo kwaye ndiya kuzihlanganisela kum.

Kuba abo baya kuba nam babiziwe, kwaye bakhethiwe, kwaye bathembekile kwaye bayinyaniso. Kwaye bazibonakalise bethembekile kwizinto ezimbalwa, ngoko ke ndiya kubenza babe ngabaphathi phezu kwezinto ezininzi kwaye bangena eluvuyweni lweNkosi yabo. Baya kuba ngumkhosi ongenakutshatyalaliswa nongunaphakade ongenakoyiswa nangayiphi na indlela okanye umiswe, kwaye baya kunyathelisa ngokoqobo phantsi kweenyawo zonke iinkalo zoBukumkani beentshaba ngentshabalaliso epheleleyo nokuthotywa kwazo zonke izinto ezimbi. Kuba impindezelo yeyam, itsho iNkosi, kwaye ndiya kubuyisela. Kwaye mna, Yeshua HaMashiach, ndiyiNkosi yeeNkosi noKumkani wooKumkani, kwaye ngobulungisa ndiza kugweba ndenze imfazwe.

Ngoko yivani amazwi am kwaye niliqonde ithemba lendalo yonke, kuba kwanendalo ilindele ngenzondelelo ukubonakaliswa koonyana bakaThixo! Kuba indalo yenziwa yaphantsi kwamampunge, kungekhona ngokuzithandela, kodwa ngenxa yalowo uyibeke phantsi kwethemba: Kuba indalo ngokwayo nayo iya kukhululwa ebukhobokeni bokonakala, iye kwinkululeko ezukileyo yabantwana bakaThixo. Ngoko ke mna, Yesu Krestu, ndiya kuyihlangula indalo ngobulungisa kwaye ndiya kuwuphilisa umhlaba kwaye ngobulungisa ndiya kulawula. Kwaye ndiya kulibhukuqa lonke igunya nolawulo lotshaba, kwaye ubukumkani bukaSathana abuyi kuphinda buvuke.

Njengoko kubhaliwe, kuya kuba njalo, kwaye ngoko ndenza uYohane uMtyhileli wabhala: Ndabona izulu livulekile, nanko ihashe elimhlophe, nalowo uhleli phezu kwalo ubizwa ngokuba nguThembekile noNyaniso, kwaye ngobulungisa uyagweba enze imfazwe. Amehlo akhe ayenjengedangatye lomlilo, kwaye entloko yakhe kwakukho izithsaba ezininzi, kwaye wayenegama elibhaliweyo, elingaziwa mntu ngaphandle kwakhe. Kwaye wayambethe isambatho esifakwe egazini, kwaye igama lakhe libizwa ngokuba, Lilizwi likaThixo. Kwaye imikhosi eyayisezulwini yamlandela kumahashe amhlophe, yambethe ilinen ecikizekileyo, emhlophe necocekileyo. Kwaye emlonyeni wakhe kuphuma ikrele elibukhali, ukuze ngalo, abethe iintlanga: kwaye uya kuzilawula ngentonga yentsimbi: kwaye unyathela isixovulelo sewayini sobushushu nengqumbo kaThixo uSomandla. Kwaye unesambatho sakhe, nasethangeni lakhe igama elibhaliweyo, UKUMKANI WOOKUMKANI, NENKOSI YEENKOSI. Ngoko ke lilo ithemba lokubuya kwam kwesibini, kwaye olo suku lusondela ngakumbi ngomzuzwana ngamnye odlulayo.

Yabonani! Ngaloo mini ndiya kuzisa amajoni am kuba lilo ilifa lawo, kwanomkhosi ongenakufaniswa nawuphi na, kuba umkhosi onjengowam awuzange ubekho, umkhosi ongenakoyiswa owenziwe ngabahlangulweyo, kwanabo bazalwa befile kwizigqitho nezono kodwa ngoku baya kuphila ubomi obungunaphakade kwaye abanakubulawa okanye boyiswe. Kuba ndinikwe amandla nguYahweh ngenjongo enjalo, kwaye ndiya kuba ndibanike amandla ndabavuza ngokuthembeka kwabo nothando lwabo kum negama lam, kwanuYesu uKrestu! Kuba nabani na othanda aze akhonze uYeshuah HaMashiach uyathanda aze akhonze noYahweh, kuba akukho mntu mkhulu kunoThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade kaSirayeli! Ngaloo mini isondelayo niya kubona amazulu evulekile, kwaye niya kundibona mna, Yesu Krestu, ndikhokela umkhosi omkhulu nonamandla wasezulwini uphuma emazulwini, kwaye uya kuba ngumkhosi ongenakufaniswa nawuphi na, naphakade! Kuba uMtshakazi wam uya kuba nam.

Kwaye ndiya kulityumza ngokupheleleyo irhamncwa nomprofeti wobuxoki nayo yonke imikhosi yabo nawo onke amasikizi abo abawenze ngokukakade kwaye ngoko baya kutshatyalaliswa kwanangokukhanya kokufika kwam nangelo krele libukhali macala omabini liphuma emlonyeni wam, kwaye abayi kuma kodwa baya kutshatyalaliswa ngokupheleleyo. Kwaye irhamncwa nomprofeti wobuxoki baya kuphoswa bephila kwelo chibi livuthayo lomlilo nesulfure kwaye abasayi kuze bakhululwe kwintuthumbo yabo, kuba bazenze bafaneleka. Kodwa kuqala umhlaba kufuneka uhlanjululwe ngenkathazo kaYakobi kwaye abaninzi baya kuza kum, kwaninzi lezihlwele kwaye wonke umphefumlo obiza egameni leNkosi kude kube sekupheleni uya kusindiswa ngokupheleleyo, kwaye uSirayeli kufuneka ahlanganiswe kuba ndisaza kusindisa intsalela kuYakobi eya kuthi iseze kuMesiya wayo wokwenene nongunaphakade, kuba ndinguYeshua HaMashiach kwaye ndiza ngokwezithembiso ezenziwa kooYise ezabhengezwa mandulo nguYahweh ngokwakhe, kwaye ndiyinzaliseko yazo kwaye ixesha lam lifikile.

Ngoko ke kwabo balilahlileyo igama lam elisindisayo, kwanuYesu waseNazarete, ndiyanicebisa ukuba nive amazwi am kwaye niwaqonde amaxesha eniphila kuwo kwaye nibalekele kwisandi selizwi lam kwaye nibize egameni lam ngoku kanye kwaye nindivume njengeNkosi yenu, kuba aninalo ixesha lokuchitha. Kanye njengokuba kwakukho abo bangamazibulo ekuzalweni okutsha ekuzalweni kwebandla lam ngomhla wePentekoste njengoko kubhaliwe kwisibhalo, kuya kubakho nabokugqibela abaza kuza ekuzalweni okutsha kweli xesha lobabalo, kwanabo bonke abaza kusindiswa njengoko ukuthululwa kwam komoya oyingcwele kwixesha lam lobabalo nenceba kufikelela esiphelweni.

Kwaye ngoko ukunqaba kwazo zonke izinto zobuthixo kuya kuwela emhlabeni kwaye isono ngoku asiyi kuthintelwa njengoko ndisusa umthinteli wam xa ndihlanganisa iBandla lam. Umntu wemvelo ulilahlile ilizwi lam, umntu wemvelo ululahle ubabalo lwam, umntu wemvelo uyilahlile inceba yam, umntu wemvelo usilahlile isimemo sam sobulungisa, kwaye umntu wemvelo undilahlile mna Yesu waseNazarete kwaye ke ngoko umntu wemvelo umlahlile uYahweh uThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade owandithumayo, kwaye ngoku ndiya kunika umhlaba ubumnyama ababunqwenela kangaka.

Kodwa qondani ukuba abaninzi baya kundenzela imisebenzi emikhulu, kwaye amangqina am amabini, kwaniziphatho zam zezibane ezibini ziya kuprofeta, kwaye ndiya kuthumela ama-144,000 am ukuba ahlangule abaninzi, ngoko musani ukoyika! Usindiso luya kufezekiswa, kwanangentshabalalo yenyama kwaye izihlwele ezikhulu ziya kuza elusindisweni ngeli xesha. Kuba ngenene, uYahweh akazange kwaye akasayi kuze azishiye engenangqina!

Amaxesha eeNtlanga azale iziqhamo ezininzi phakathi kwabo ngebeya kuhlala bengenathemba ngaphandle kokuba babandakanywe kwizithembiso ezenziwa kooYise, kwanabo baphila ngokholo loNyana kaThixo owabathandayo wazinikela ngenxa yosindiso lwabo. Kuba njengoko kubhaliwe: Olungileyo uya kuphila ngokholo! Kwaye ngoko ngokholo kum, Krestu Yesu, baye baba ngabantwana bokholo, kwanabantwana bokholo luka-Abraham. Kuba u-Abraham wakholwa kuThixo phambi kokuba kunikwe itywina lokwaluka, kwaye kwabalelwa kuye njengobulungisa, kwananjengoko uMoses engqina! Kwaye ngoko u-Abraham unguyise wabo bonke abakholwayo nabo baye bagwetyelwa ngokholo kuYesu Krestu Olungileyo, kuba ndingolungileyo nomgwebeli wabo bonke abakholwayo egameni lam elinamandla!

Noko ke abaninzi basebenzise ubabalo lwam nenceba yam njengemvume yokona, bade bonwabele baziqhayise ngeendlela zabo zesono nezitshabalalisayo, bade bafundise abantwana babo ukuba baphile iindlela zabo zesono kwaye ngoko izizukulwana zikhohlakele ngeendlela zazo kwaye iindlela zazo ziyacekiseka kwaye ngoko abantu abakhohlakeleyo nabakhohlisi baye baba babi ngakumbi nangakumbi. Musani ukukhohliswa, ukuba umntu uhlwayelela inyama loo mntu uya kuvuna ukonakala enyameni. Kwaye bonke abalukhanyelayo unikelo lwam lobomi obungunaphakade baze bakhethe ukonakala kuka-Adam baya kuvuna isiqhamo saloo konakala kunye nomgwebo ongunaphakade, kuba umgwebo ulapha kwaye amaxesha okutyhila afikile.

Inene, inene, amaxesha omgwebo omkhulu awaprofetwa mandulo nguEnoki umbhali (kuba ubungqina bakhe banikwa esi sizukulwana), afikile emhlabeni kwananjengoko kubhaliwe kwaye bonke baya kuphendula. Abo balwamkeleyo unikelo lwam losindiso nabafunyanwa bethembekile kuKrestu ekubonakaleni kwam baya kutshintshwa ngonaphakade, nokuba balele kuKrestu okanye baphilile kwaye bahleli kuKrestu, abo bakuKrestu ekubonakaleni kwam baya kusiwa ezulwini lamazulu. Ukuba kuKrestu kukuhamba ngomoya wam onamandla, kwanomthetho womoya wobomi! Kwaye abo babephila ngobulungisa phambi kokuzalwa kweBandla lam nakwiingcwele zembandezelo luvuko lwabalungileyo, apho abalungileyo baya kuvukela kubutsha bobomi ngomvuzo omkhulu, ongunaphakade nongapheliyo. Ekupheleni kolawulo lwam lweminyaka eyi-1000 kuya kubakho umgwebo wetrone emhlophe enkulu kwaye bonke abo babephila bonwabela ukungalungisi nesono baya kuvuzwa ngokwemisebenzi yabo kwaye abaninzi baya kuwela ekufeni kwesibini kuba bakhethe njalo.

Kwaye ngoko amaxesha okutyhila afikile, kwaye ivangeli engunaphakade ityhiliwe ngexesha layo kwanencwadana esesandleni sengelosi yam enamandla ngoku ityhiliwe kwaye ukuthetha kweendudumo ezisixhenxe kubhengeziwe! Kuba lo msebenzi ngumsebenzi ongunaphakade, kwaye sisiphelo seminqophiso nezithembiso zakudala kwiminqophiso nezithembiso zomtsha, kwanuMthetho wam oMtsha endiwuqinisekisileyo ngegazi lam. Kuba mna Yeshua HaMashiach, kwanuYesu Krestu Olungileyo onguKumkani wooKumkani neNkosi yeeNkosi, ndingulowo unikwe ukuzalisekiswa kwazo zonke izithembiso nayo yonke iminqophiso.

Kuba uYohane uMtyhileli wabhala oko wakubonayo, waza wabhala wathi: Ndabona enye ingelosi enamandla isihla ivela ezulwini, yambethe ilifu, nomnyama wawuphezu kwentloko yayo, nobuso bayo babunjengelanga, neenyawo zayo zinjengeentsika zomlilo. Yaye yayiphethe incwadana evuliweyo esandleni sayo: yaza yabeka unyawo lwayo lwasekunene elwandle, nolwasekhohlo emhlabeni. Waphinda uYohane uMtyhileli wabhengeza wathi: Ndabona enye ingelosi ibhabha esazulwini sezulu, inevangeli engunaphakade, ukuba iyishumayele kwabo bahleli emhlabeni, nakuzo zonke iintlanga, nezizwe, neelwimi, nabantu, isithi ngezwi elikhulu, moyikeni uThixo, nimzukise, kuba lifikile ixa lokugweba kwakhe: nimnqule lowo wenza izulu nomhlaba, nolwandle, nemithombo yamanzi.

Kwaye ngoko kubhaliwe kwaye ngoko kwenziwe. Ivangeli engunaphakade ifikile ukuba ityhilwe ngexesha layo kwaye incwadana ayisekho yimfihlakalo. Ingelosi enamandla eyambethe ilifu nomnyama wawuphezu kwentloko yayo nobuso bayo babunjengelanga neenyawo zayo zinjengeentsika zomlilo. Umnyama luphawu olwanikwa kumnqophiso kaNowa awawenza uYahweh noNowa emva konogumbe wokuba akayi kuphinda awukhukulise umhlaba wonke. Ilifu neentsika zomlilo zimele umnqophiso kaThixo noMoses, owakhelwe phezu kweminqophiso yangaphambili. Kuba xa uMoses wayethethe yonke imiyalelo kubo bonke abantu ngokomthetho, wathatha igazi lamathole neleebhokhwe, namanzi, noboya obubomvu, nehisope, wafefa incwadi, nabo bonke abantu kwaye ngoko uMthetho oMdala waqinisekiswa!

Kwaye njengoko kubhaliwe kumaHebhere: Kuba ukuba igazi leenkunzi zeenkomo neleebhokhwe, nothuthu lwethokazi olufefa abangahlambulukanga, luyangcwalisa ekuhlanjululweni kwenyama: Kobeka phi na ke ukugqithisela kwegazi likaKrestu, owathi ngoMoya ongunaphakade wazinikela engenabala kuThixo, ukusihlambulula isazela senu kwimisebenzi efileyo ukuze nikhonze uThixo ophilayo? Kwaye ngenxa yesi sizathu ungumlamleli womthetho omtsha, ukuze ngokufa, ngenxa yokuhlangulwa kwezigqitho eziphantsi komthetho wokuqala, abo babiziweyo bafumane isithembiso selifa elingunaphakade.

Kwaye ngoko njengoko kubhaliwe kunjalo kufezekisiwe nina bathembekileyo bam, kwaye unjalo uMthetho oMtsha. Kwaye kwananjengokuba inguEl Shaddai owalawula ukukhanya ukuba kukhanye ebumnyameni ekuqaleni, ngendlela efanayo uThixo uSomandla ukhanyisile ezintliziyweni zethu ukunika ukukhanya kolwazi lozuko lukaThixo, ebusweni bukaYesu Krestu. Kunjalo, kumnqophiso wam omtsha onamandla ngakumbi, kuba ndawuqinisekisa ngegazi lam elingenasono, kuzalisekiswa yonke iminqophiso yalo lonke ixesha, kuba ndathi eKalvari ekufeni kwam: Kugqityiwe. Kwaye ngoko umnqophiso omtsha waqinisekiswa ngegazi lam waba nguMthetho oMtsha!

Kwaye ngoko kum, Krestu Yesu, iminqophiso yakudala izalisekiswa ngozuko olutyhilekileyo, kwanasebusweni bomsindisi wenu ongunaphakade noMesiya, kwanam Yeshuah HaMashiach. Kwaye le ngelosi uYohane uMtyhileli ayichazayo yayinonyawo olunye elwandle nolunye emhlabeni kuba usindiso lwam luvulelekile kubo bobabini abavela elwandle lweentlanga, (iiNtlanga) nabo bavela kwilizwe lesithembiso kwanabo bonke abandenze iNkosi yabo engunaphakade, kwanabaninzi njengoko iNkosi uThixo wethu iya kubabiza! Kwaye ngoko ivangeli yam engunaphakade iya kufumana amehlo, iindlebe, neentliziyo zabo bonke ethunyelwe kubo kwaye abaninzi baya kuza elusindisweni kuba lonke ilizwi likaThixo liphilile kwaye lingunaphakade kwaye alikho nelinye ilizwi likaYahweh eliya kusilela nangayiphi na indlela okanye lidlule de konke kuzalisekiswe. Kuba kuYahweh zonke izithembiso ngu-ewe no-amen kwaye zonke ziya kuzalisekiswa kwaye mna, Krestu Yesu, ndazinikela kwintuthumbo ebuhlungu ukuze nditywine umnqophiso wam omtsha ngegazi lam elingenasono, kube kanye ngonaphakade kwaye umthetho omtsha uya kuphila ngonaphakade.

Ngoko yivani amazwi am kwaye niyiqonde intetho yam, kwanelizwi leNkosi yenu noMesiya. Amaxesha afikile bantwana bam kwaye amazwi am awugcwalisile umhlaba kwaye abaprofeti bam nabaphathiswa bam bazigubungele iziphelo zomhlaba, kwaye kuzo zonke iilwimi, neentetho, neentlanga ivangeli yam yosindiso iviwe, kwanaseziphelweni zomhlaba. Kwaye kunjalo, kwaye ndiyabhengeza: Iindaba zam ezilungileyo zihlala zakhiwe phezu kwesiseko somthetho nabaprofeti kwaye ndililitye lembombo eliphambili apho yonke itempile kaThixo ophilayo iphumle khona! Bantwana bam, nina niyitempile kaThixo ophilayo!

Kwaye ngoko ndinika ukuba niqonde ukuba zininzi iinxalenye zevangeli kaKrestu! Ngaba niyaziyatha ngokwaneleyo ukuba nikholelwe ukuba zine kuphela iivangeli? Kwaye zikho nezinye izinto ezininzi endazenzayo, ezo, ukuba bezinokubhalwa zonke, ndicinga ukuba kwanomhlaba ngokwawo awunakuqulatha iincwadi ebezifanele ukubhalwa. Kwaye kunjalo kukho iindaba ezilungileyo zokufika kwam kokuqala, kukho iindaba ezilungileyo zemfihlakalo kaThixo endayityhilela uPawulos uMpostile, kukho ivangeli engunaphakade esikhokelela kwisiphelo sazo zonke izinto. KuYahweh akukho siphelo seendaba ezilungileyo!

Kwaye ngoko ndiyanibuza umbuzo? Ngaba ningumcebisi kaYahweh? Ngaba abefundisi benu abaxokayo nabaphathiswa benu abadikidiki bayakwazi ukumcebisa uYahweh? Ungubani wena, mntundini, ukuba umisele ukuba uYahweh uthetha nini kwaye njani nomxholo nendalo yamazwi akhe, okanye ukuba ukhetha ukuthumela amazwi akhe kubani? Kwaye kunjalo. Amazwi kaThixo aya kuqhubeka ehamba kwaye bonke ubungqina buya kugqitywa kwaye iincwadi ziya kubhalwa ngonaphakade kuba iLizwi likaThixo liyaphila kwaye lihlala ngonaphakade!

Kwaye ngoko ndiyanibuza, ngaba uMthetho oMtsha kaYesu Krestu wawucima uMthetho oMdala neminqophiso emidala? Makube lee! Lonke iLizwi likaThixo linyulu kwaye konke kuya kuzalisekiswa kwanakanye, konke, kwaye ngonaphakade! Kwaye injalo indalo kaThixo ongatshintshiyo! Ecaleni kwetrone yakhe kukho amashumi amawaka aphindwe ngamashumi amawaka kodwa akadingi mcebisi. UYahweh, uThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade wenza zonke izinto ngokwecebo lentando yakhe kwaye unguThixo ongunaphakade! Noko ke uzityhilile izicwangciso zakhe, kwanentando yakhe engunaphakade nengapheliyo ngokwezigaba. Ungabuza ukuba kutheni uYahweh enokwenza into enjalo? Anifundanga na oko uPawulos uMpostile wakubhala xa ndandimenza abhale? Kodwa sithetha ubulumko bukaThixo ngemfihlakalo, kwanobulumko obufihliweyo, awayebumisele uThixo phambi kwehlabathi, ukuze sizukiswe. Obungaziwa nayinye kwiinkosana zeli hlabathi: kuba ukuba bezibazi, ngebengayibetheleli emnqamlezweni iNkosi yozuko.

Kwaye ngoko uThixo ongunaphakade, kwanuYahweh ongadingi mcebisi kwaye kubulumko bakhe obungunaphakade utyhila iindlela zakhe namazwi akhe ngendlela akhetha ngayo ekuzalisekisweni kwezicwangciso zakhe ezingunaphakade zokuhlangula uluntu. Qondani zithandwa zam, kwanuMtshakazi wam, ukuba benisezintliziyweni zikaYahweh kwaphambi kokudalwa kwaye nifihlwe ngobuchule entliziyweni kaBawo kwaye nifihlelwe oko niya kuba yikho ekubonakaleni kwam, kuba nifile kwaye ubomi benu bufihliwe kum Krestu Yesu. Kwaye xa ndibonakala, nani niya kubonakala nam eluzukweni! Bantwana bam abancinane sele niza kudalwa ngokutsha ngokupheleleyo kum kwaye niya kumangaliswa koko uYahweh akulungiselele abo bamthandayo. Kuba njengoko kungekho mntu emhlabeni oqonde ngokupheleleyo kanye oko oonyana bakaThixo baya kuba yikho ekuhlangulweni kwabo okupheleleyo, kwaye sele niza kufumanisa kuba usuku lufikile kwaye ukubonakala kwam kusondele.

Ndicebisa omnye kwaye ndicebisa bonke, kwanuKrestu Yesu iNkosi yenu engunaphakade! Yamkelani amazwi am nizuze ubulumko nokuqonda, kuba ubuntu benu bokwenene buyimfihlakalo engunaphakade efihliweyo kuThixo ngokwakhe esele iza kutyhilwa ngokupheleleyo. Kungekudala niya kuzifumana nimi kwigumbi letrone likaYahweh, kwanuBawo wethu, kwaye ulindele ngenzondelelo kwaye ulangazelela ubukho benu ngoku, kwanakwaphakade elidlulileyo. Kwaye iiholo ezinkulu ezulwini zilungiselelwe ukufika kwenu kwaye iitafile zilungisiwe kwaye umtshato woMyeni noMtshakazi wakhe usondele kwaye umtshakazi wam uzilungisile kwaye ndiya kubamkela kum kwaye ngonaphakade baya kuhlala benam kumnqophiso ongunaphakade kwaye baya kwenza imisebenzi emininzi emikhulu engazange ingene ezintliziyweni zabantu ngeli xesha.

Kwaye ngoko uYahweh uThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade nonaphakade ulungisile kwaye wanilungiselela izinto ezininzi, kwaye mna, Krestu Yesu iNkosi ephakanyisiweyo nenyukileyo ndinomvuzo wenu esandleni sam kwanakwabathembekileyo bam. Kwaye umkhosi wasezulwini ulindele ngenzondelelo ubukho benu kwaye ngoku niya kungena kumvuzo wasezulwini nongunaphakade. Ngoko musani ukoyika kuba ubomi benu ngoku buza kutyhilwa kwaye nangawuphi na umzuzwana ngoku ixilongo lokugqibela liya kukhala, nikulungele na ukubonakala kwam bantwana bam abancinane?
