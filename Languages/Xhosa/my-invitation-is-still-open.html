Isimemo Sam Sisavuliwe

Ilizwi leNkosi lehla phezu kwam, yaye ndilinikela ndingongezi nto, ndingathabathi nto.

Yabonani! Isimemo sam sisavuliwe! Yizani nonke, kwaye makaze nabani na onako, kuba mna Nkosi uYesu Krestu ndimiselwe ukuba ndibe liliwa lenu lokuzikhusela, ndibe yinqaba eyomeleleyo kuni. Akukho gama limbi linikiweyo phantsi kwezulu abanokusindiswa ngalo abantu, ngaphandle kwegama lam, uYesu Krestu. Ndililitye lembombo eliphambili abalilahlileyo, becinga ukuba bakhululekile. Inkululeko ayifumaneki ndawo ngaphandle kwakum, uKrestu Yesu. Yini na ukuba nivumele abaqeshwa banilahlekise? Yini na ukuba nivumele abangenaxabiso banonakalise ekunyanisekeni okufumaneka kum? Zininzi iindlela ezisa entshabalalweni. Kodwa mna ndiyindlela, ndiyinyaniso, ndibubomi, indlela yokwenene eya kuBawo ezisa usindiso nenkululeko: usindiso kuba ndinikela ngoxolelo lwezono ngegazi lam elihlawulelayo, nenkululeko kuba uNyana uya kunikhulula nibe nikhululekile ngokwenene.

Kutheni nidiniwe nje, bantu bam? Yini na ukuba nityhafe? Andiniphumzanga na? Andizoyisanga na zonke izinto, ndanenzela indlela yokuba nilandele uloyiso lwam nibe nesabelo kwiintlungu zam? Kuphela kungokwamkela idini lam endazenza lona eninokuhlangulwa ngalo kwintshabalalo eqinisekileyo. Kuphela kungalo igazi lam enifumana ngalo uxolelo lwezono, kwaye kungomzimba wam owaphukileyo kunikwa ukuphiliswa, kwaye nomzimba omtsha ndiya kuninika wona ngaloo mini, ewe, kwabanyanisekileyo nabanyuliweyo bam. Kuba yenze njalo iNkosi yenu ukunilungiselela. Ndenze indlela apho bekungekho ndlela: indlela entlango ephelela ekuzukisweni kwenu, ukuba nje ningayeka ukona.

Eli hlabathi lonakele, ulwazi lwalo lonakele, iinzululwazi zalo azinyanisanga, nomnatha wenkohliso ubambisa abantu bam, ubakhokelela ngaphandle kwendlela endibakhethele yona. Aniqondi na ukuba ndinibizile? Ukuba ndininyulile? Aniqondi na ukuba kufuneka nife kwisiqu senu ukuze nifumane ubomi kum, njengoko ubomi benu bokwenene bufihliwe kum, iNkosi ethembekileyo? Xa ndiya kubonakala, nani niya kubonakala nisebuqaqawulini; ngoko ke, ngentliziyo enye, ndilandeleni. Ngengqondo enye, yizani kum ngokholo oluthobekileyo. Kufuneka kuphela ukholo olungangokhozo lwemostade, kwaye umthi uya kukhula ube mkhulu kunayo yonke.

Amaxesha eeNtlanga ayasondela esiphelweni, kodwa uYakobi uya kusindiswa, kwananjengokungathi kungomlilo. Eli hlabathi liya kugwetywa ngomlilo otshis' iindlela. Inene, inkosana yeli hlabathi igwetyiwe kwaye ilutshaba oloyisiweyo. Isigwebo simisiwe kuyo, kubukumkani bayo, kubalandeli bayo, nakwihlabathi ebizama ukulithatha kuBawo. Inene, inqwenela ukuniphanga ilifa lenu, njengoko ndinilungiselele indlela yokuba nibe nesabelo ebuqaqawulini betrone yam njengoko nam ndinesabelo ebuqaqawulini betrone kaBawo. Ndaya kunilungisela indawo. Aniqondi na ukuba zininzi iindawo zokuhlala endlwini kaBawo? Ngaba ikho into enzima kum? Izithembiso zam zikhe zaniphoxa na? Okanye nilahlekisiwe kwinjongo enye endininike yona? Kutheni nisakhetha iindlela zenu?

Guqukani, owu, bantwana bam! Zivumeni izono zenu kum kwanazo nezono zooyihlo, kuba ndiya kuniva ngexesha elimisiweyo. Ndiya kunizukisa; ndiya kuninika ubomi obutsha kum. Yizani kum nifumane iingubo zelinen ecikizekileyo! Yizani kum nifumane igama elitsha endiya kuninika lona elityeni endinenzele lona. Makungabikho mntu unikhohlisayo ngomvuzo wenu. Intshabalalo enkulu nosizi lusemnyango wenu. Ihlabathi sele liza kungena ebubini obungenazithintelo. Inzonzobila iya kuvulwa, nobukumkani bukaSathana obupheleleyo buya kukhululwa. Ndiya kuzishukumisa iimazulu, ndiya kulushukumisa ulwandle, ukushukuma okukhulu kuya kufika emhlabeni. Unyana wentshabalalo uhamba phakathi kwenu njengengcuka enxibe isikhumba segusha, elindele ukuba ubukumkani bakhe bobusathana bubonakale ngokupheleleyo ukuze atyhilwe ngexesha lakhe. Musani ukumlandela!

Balekelani kum, bantwana bam, kuba ingqumbo enkulu nentshabalalo enkulu iya kufikela isizwe senu, kwaye izizwe ezininzi ziya kushukunyiswa zibe ziingqushu kungekudala. Owu, akwaba beningajikela kum niwaqonde amaxesha enu. Kule ntshabalalo izayo kuya kuvela lowo wobuxoki, njengoko eyenga abaninzi ngolwimi oluxokayo kwaye uya kubonisa imimangaliso emininzi yobuxoki neemiqondiso exokayo. Kwanangoku imikhosi yakhe ingene ezixekweni zenu yaze yazalisa izitrato zenu. Musani ukuboyika. Abanye babo baya kufumana usindiso. Umququ uya kunikelwa emlilweni. Inkathazo enkulu iya kufikela eli hlabathi ngequbuliso. Ngequbuliso intlekele yabo iya kubawela, kwaye abayi kusinda. Lilixa lokugqibela. Musani ukuba nesabelo kwizibetho zakhe; phumani kuye, bantu bam, kuba ihenyukazi elikhulu ligwetyiwe, kwaye intlekele yalo iya kulifumana ngeyure enye. Abathengisi abakhulu ababerhweba ngobugqwirha bakhe baya kuwela kusizi olukhulu, nomsi wokutsha kwakhe uya kubonwa ngumntu wonke. Abathengisi behlabathi baya kulilela usizi lwakhe bekude.

Kodwa ndinezicwangciso ngani, bantu bam. Ubomi benu bufihliwe kum. Nangona ndinivavanyile okwexeshana, ndinisulungekisile njengegolide emlilweni womsuli. Phumani kuye! Vukelani ebulungiseni ningoni. Zihlambululeni egazini lam lentlawulelo. Lilani nenze isijwili ngenxa yezono zenu; jikani kwizono zenu ngentliziyo yenu iphela. Ndim ovavanya iintliziyo zabantu; aninako ukundifihlela nto. Yizani kum ningenankohliso. Yizani kum ngokunyaniseka nangokukhululekileyo, kwaye ndiya kudla nani, ndiya kunibekela nendawo etafileni yam. Kungekudala uMtshakazi uya kuqokelelwa, kungekudala kunokuba nicinga.

Xa uMtshakazi wam ethathwa emoyeni, umthinteli uya kukhululwa, kwaye akukho nto imbi iya kuthintelwa kumntu wemvelo. Kwaye baya kulandela ngokukhululekileyo iinkanuko nobubi ezintliziyweni zabo; ngoko ke, umgwebo omkhulu uya kubafikela, kwaye awuyi kulungiswa. Jikelani kum, uYesu Krestu! Nqulani igama lam ndisafumaneka! Kwanangomhla omkhulu wentshabalalo, igama lam liya kuzisa usindiso kwabo babizayo. Yizani kum ngoku niphume kuye, kuba ixesha seliphantse laphela. Imikhosi yasezulwini ilungile, ilindele ixilongo.

Ndililitye lembombo eliphambili abalilahlileyo, becinga ukuba bakhululekile.

Ndiyindlela, ndiyinyaniso, ndibubomi: indlela yokwenene eya kuBawo ezisa usindiso nenkululeko.

Ndim ovavanya iintliziyo zabantu; aninako ukundifihlela nto.

IZenzo 2:16-21

Le yinto leya kuthethwa nguYoweli umprofeti;

Kuya kuthi ke ngemihla yokugqibela (utsho uThixo), Ndiya kuthulula kuMoya wam phezu kwayo yonke inyama; Oonyana benu neentombi zenu ziya kuprofeta, Abafana benu babone imibono, Namadoda enu amakhulu aphuphe amaphupha:

Ewe, naphezu kwabakhonzi bam, naphezu kwabakhonzazana bam, Ndiya kuthulula ngaloo mihla kuMoya wam, baprofete. Ndiya kwenza imimangaliso ezulwini phezulu, Neemiqondiso emhlabeni phantsi: Igazi, nomlilo, nesiqhumo somsi. Ilanga liya kujika libe mnyama, Nenyanga ibe ligazi, Phambi kokuba ifike imini enkulu, ebonakalayo, yeNkosi. Kuya kuthi ke, bonke abasukuba belinqula igama leNkosi, basindiswe.
