Intlekele Enkulu Iyasondela

ILizwi leNkosi lafika kum; ndicela nive.

Yivani ilizwi lam, itsho iNkosi yemikhosi! Bantu bam, buyelani kum, kuba intlekele enkulu isezakufikela isizwe senu. Intlekele enkulu isemnyango wenu, kwaye intlekele enkulu iyakufika kunxweme lwenu. Buyelani kum nindoyike, itsho iNkosi yemikhosi. UThixo ulugwebile ulongamo lweMelika kumacala amabini. Kuyakubakho umgwebo ovela ngaphakathi kwaye kuyakubakho umgwebo ovela ngaphandle. Ngaphakathi kuyakubakho ukushukuma okukhulu, ukushukuma okukhulu, kwaye iMelika iyakuthi bhukuqu ngamandla ngokushukuma okukhulu. Ngaphandle, imikhosi yakhe ifikile ukuze ibe, ibulale, kwaye itshabalalise yonke into esendleleni yayo. Akukho nceba ezintliziyweni zayo. Inangqondo-nye. Kwaye iyakuthatha ubutyebi benu kuni; iyakuthatha iimpahla zenu kuni; iyakuthimba inzala yenu; kwaye indlala enkulu iyakufika elizweni lenu. Lowo ungabulawanga yimfazwe uyakubulawa yintlekele; lowo ungabulawanga yintlekele uyakubulawa ngamarhamncwa asendle; lowo ungabulawanga ngamarhamncwa uyakufa yindlala. Kanti naphakathi kwentlekele yenu, nabani na onqula igama leNkosi uyakusindiswa.

Kwaye ndiyakubasindisa abantu bam, nangona kuya kuba ngenxa yembandezelo enkulu nangenxa yomlilo womsuli. Yizani kum, bantu bam, lisasekhona ixesha. Yizani kum, iNkosi uYesu Krestu, kuba ndiyiNkosi elilungisa. NdinguKumkani wooKumkani. NdinguMalusi olungileyo, kwaye akukho namnye unokuzixwila iigusha zam esandleni sam. Yizani kum! Yizani kum lisasekhona ixesha, nivume izono zenu; livumeni igama lam elinamandla njengeNkosi, kuba akukho lusindiso kwelinye igama. Kuba mna, Yesu Krestu, ndimiselwe usindiso lwenu endaluthenga ngegazi lam. Yizani kum, ewe, yizani kum eluthandweni lwam endinalo ngani. Ningandichasi, kodwa yizani kum, ukuze ndikwazi ukunisindisa ndininike ubomi obungunaphakade ukuze ndisikelele inzala yenu. Yizani kum, bantu bam, ningazenzi lukhuni iintliziyo zenu, njengoko ooyihlo benza entlango. Kuba iNkosi uThixo wenu iwuvakalisile umgwebo omkhulu kwisizwe senu, kuba izono zaso zininzi kwaye zoyikeka.

Ewe, ndazihlawulela ezo zono nazo zonke izono zoluntu, kodwa isipho sam neengalo zam aziyi kuhlala zoluliwe ngonaphakade. Ngomhla wentlekele, usindiso luyakufunyanwa ngoxinzelelo olukhulu nangembandezelo enkulu endiyakuyizisa ehlabathini ukuze ndivavanye iintliziyo zabantu, kwaye lowo unyamezelayo kude kube sekupheleni uyakusindiswa. Ningamlandeli umntu wesono. Ningaluthathi uphawu lwakhe kwaye ningalithathi iyeza lakhe, kuba kukho ukulunywa yinyoka kulo, kwaye aniyi kuphiliswa kwityhefu yalo. Nabani na oyakuthatha uphawu lwerhamncwa uyakuba nesabelo sakhe kwichibi lomlilo kunye nerhamncwa nomprofeti wobuxoki. Akubhalwanga na kwiLizwi lam eliNgcwele? Anifundanga na? Anivanga na? Buyelani eluthandweni lwam nize kum, bantwana bam, kuba ndiyanibiza kweli lixa lokugqibela, kuba intlekele enkulu isemnyango wenu.

Ndiveni, itsho iNkosi yemikhosi:

Kuba ndinguye, uNyana woMntu owabethelelwa emnqamlezweni phantsi koPontiyo Pilato, owafela izono zenu waza wavuswa ngosuku lwesithathu. Ndavuswa ndaza ndazukiswa.

NdinguNkosi yeeNkosi noKumkani wooKumkani.

NdinguNyana woMntu, iNkosi elilungisa, iNkosi yokwenene ethembekileyo nenyanisekileyo, kwaye akukho namnye kuni onokuxwilwa ezandleni zam.

Ndingumalusi olungileyo, kwaye akukho namnye unokuzixwila iigusha zam esandleni sam.

Konke uBawo andinike kona, ndiyakukubonisa kuye, kwaye ndinqwenela ukunibonisa kuBawo ningenasiphoso, nasibatha, nasisole, nambethe ilinen emhlophe etshiswe egazini lam. Indlela ekuphela kwayo nawuphi na umntu anokuma ngayo phambi kwam kukwamkela idini lam lezono zenu. Ningalibali naboni! Ningalibali; ningalibali! Phumani phakathi kwabo nizahlule ezonweni zabo. Vumani izono zenu kum kwaye nivume izono zooyihlo kum, kuba zidlulela kuni. Philani ubomi endininike bona, nihambe ngendlela endinibekele yona, njengoko ndinibize ngegama. Ningajikeli ngasekhohlo ningajikeli ngasekunene, kodwa nikani ingqwalasela nengqondo enye kumazwi am. Kanti, abaninzi bayakusala isimemo sam, kwaye abaninzi bayakutshabalala. Kholwani kum, njengoko uthando lukaThixo luyakusindisa bonke abanqula igama leNkosi. Kholwani kum, kwaye imilambo yamanzi aphilayo iyakumpompoza esiswini senu njengoko izono zenu zihlanjwa zicoceka nomoya wenu wenziwa mtsha. Yizani kum lisasekhona ixesha; livumeni igama lam; ndenzeni iNkosi, kuba akukho lusindiso kwelinye igama elinikiweyo phantsi kwezulu elinokuthi abantu basindiswe ngalo.
