Epreli 05, 2024

Mlindi, Vuka!

Mlindi, vuka, ubhinqe inyaniso esinqeni sakho, unxibe isigcina-sifuba sobulungisa!

Mlindi, vuka, uthabathe isigcina-ntloko sosindiso nekrele loMoya, eliliLizwi likaThixo!

Mlindi, vuka, iinyawo zakho zixhotyiselwe ukulungela iVangeli yoxolo!

Lumkisa abantu bam, ungawuvaleli umlomo wakho, njengoko ndiwukhululileyo! Lwela abantu bam, ulumkise abanyulwa bam, kuba uya kufumana umvuzo womprofeti ukuba uthetha amazwi endiya kukunika wona. VUKA! Andibaxelelanga na abantu bam ukuba balindele ukuza kwam? Amaxesha namathuba awabonakali na, akho phezu kwenu? Ngoko ke, khangelani ukubonakala kwam, kuba kusondele. Khangelani imiqondiso nezimanga esibhakabhakeni, kuba uBawo uzicwangcisile iindlela zazo kwasekuqaleni, kwaye usuku luphawuliwe. Khangelani ukukhanya kwam esibhakabhakeni njengoko ixhilongo seliza kukhala; yabonani, ndikutshilo.

Bantu bam, hlolani iLizwi lam nikhangele kwisiBhalo sam esingcwele ukuze niqonde ukuba ndiya kumxhwila umtshakazi wam kumgwebo wabenzi bobubi. Ndiya kumkhusela umtshakazi wam nomzimba wam; kuba yiyiphi na indoda eyakha yayithiya eyayo inyama, endaweni yokuba iyondle iyixabise? Khangelani, qaphelani, gcinani amaxesha, nazi ukuba amaxesha afikile kwaye usuku luya kuthi gqi ngequbuliso njengelanga elikhulu eliphuma ngequbuliso elundini. Andiyi kwaliwa, kwaye andiyi kunqandwa. Nangona isela liya kuzama, liya kugquma njengengonyama; kodwa yingonyama engenamazinyo kuphela. Aliyi kundinqanda, kwaye andiyi kwaliwa ilifa lam, kuba ndiwuthengile umhlaba kwaye ngokukodwa abantu bam ngegazi lam elingenasono. Negama lam limi ngaphezu kwawonke onke ngaphandle kwelikaBawo, omkhulu kunam; kwaye andiyi kwaliwa, andiyi thintelwa, andiyi kunqandwa.

Ndiyeza, kwaye andiyi kuthula, nomsindo wam uyatshisa nxamnye neBhabheli eyimfihlo, ewe, iBhabheli eyimfihlo ethengisa ngabantwana abancinci ezandleni zabantu abakhohlakeleyo, abaya kunqandwa. Bekuya kuba ngcono ukuba babengazalwanga kunokuba bazalwe benze izono ezingekho mthethweni nezibuhlungu nxamnye nabantwana. Bekuya kuba ngcono ukuba bekuxhonywe ilitye lokusila entanyeni yabo, baphoswe elwandle; kodwa bagcinelwe umlilo ongunaphakade, kwaye ubulungisa buza kwenziwa kungekudala, njengoko ingqumbo yeMvana isiza kugubungela umhlaba wonke kwaye inkathazo kaYakobi iqala xa umthinteli esusiwe.

Zizinziseni, bantu bam, nime niqine kum ngentliziyo enye nenjongo enye. Zilahleni izinto zehlabathi ebenizinqwenela, nenze mna, iNkosi uYesu Krestu, umnqweno wenu; kuba ndiyinqaba yenu eyomeleleyo, iliwa lokuzikhusela, nomkhosi wenu wangasemva. Ngubani na oya kunichasa onokumelana nesandla sam esinamandla? Abanyulwa bam nabathembekileyo basezandleni zam ezoluliweyo, kwaye akukho namnye onokunixhwila kum. Ningabam, kwaye nikhuselekile kum; ngoko phumulani kum kwimisebenzi yenu, kuba amaxesha embuyekezo aya kuqhambuka ngequbuliso. Umbuyekezo womgwebo phezu kwabenzi bobubi ufikile, nombuyekezo wabathembekileyo bam uphezu kwabanyulwa bam, njengoko ndiya kubavuza ngelifa labo, kuba ndibenze bafaneleka.

Owu, wena namba ingenaxabiso, engenamazinyo; wena nyoka yakudala: ixesha lakho lifikile nomgwebo uphezu kwakho. Nangona ngekratshi ucinga ukuba uya koyisa, ndiya kuthatha yonke into oyibuthileyo ezandleni zakho zobusela, nabo bakulandelayo ekubaweni kwabo okunenkanuko baya kwabelana nawe kwilifa lakho kwichibi lomlilo, apho wonke umntu aya kuvuzwa ngokwemisebenzi yakhe izihlandlo ezininzi. Ingqumbo kaThixo iya kuhlala phezu kwakho ngonaphakade, kwaye akusayi kuze uphumle.

Isohlwayo esikhulu siya kuba ngumthuthuzeli wakho, nomsi orhabaxa wesulfure etshayo uya kuhlala usemathatheni akho ngonaphakade, kwaye uya kuhlawula ngonaphakade. Nomsi wentuthumbo yakho uya kunyuka ngonaphakade, nabo bonke abakulandelayo ukusuka koyena mncinci ukuya koyena mkhulu baya kuba nelifa labo nomvuzo wabo kunye nawe kumalangatye amhlophe ashushu aluhlaza aya kukugubungela ngonaphakade njengoko uya kubotshwa uphoswe kwisiphelo sakho. Kuya kubakho ukulila okukhulu nokutshixiza kwamazinyo. Kwaye nanicinga ukuba ninako ukumelana noYahweh noKrestu wakhe, kodwa niya kwenziwa into engekhoyo ngaphandle kwendelelo engunaphakade kwintuthumbo yenu engapheliyo, kwaye ngonaphakade niya kuvalelwa kude. Kwaye anisoze niyenzakalise indalo yam kwakhona, kwaye niya kulityalwa ngokupheleleyo, kwaye aniyi kuza engqondweni.

Ngoko bantu bam, mtshakazi wam othembekileyo endimthanda kakhulu, yintoni ekhoyo enimele niyoyike? Ingqumbo yam nengqumbo kaBawo ayiyeyenu kwaye ayiyi kuza phezu kwenu, kwaye anisoze nibe sengozini yokufa kwesibini. Ngoko musani ukoyika njengoko ndiya kuphumeza impindezelo egqibeleleyo kwiintshaba zenu, kuba impindezelo yeyam, itsho iNkosi! Anazi na ukuba niya kugweba iingelosi? Anazi na ukuba niya kuligweba ihlabathi? Ngoko zihlanjululeni kum, kuba ligazi lam lokucamagushela kuphela eliya kunenza nifaneleke ukuba nime phambi kwam eluzukweni. Bophelelani ilizwi lam ezintliziyweni zenu, nigcine imibhalo yam ebunzini lenu, niphulule kumazwi am amandulo; kuba bubomi kuni, nobomi benu bufihliwe kum, kwaye ndiya kubutyhila!

Owu, Bhabheli eyimfihlo, ukuba nje wawuwuqondile umbingelelo woxolo uBawo akuthumele wona, kwaye ngoku undalile mna, uYesu waseNazarete, kwaye kwenzekile ngokwemibhalo engcwele ukuba abantu abakhohlakeleyo nabakhohlisi baya kuya besiba babi ngakumbi. Kodwa wena, owu, Bhabheli enkulu eyimfihlo, uwungcolisile umnyama kaBawo wemibala emininzi awawunika uNowa nalo lonke uluntu njengesithembiso sokuba akayi kuphinda awutshabalalise wonke umhlaba ngamanzi kwakhona, kwaye uya kuligcina ilizwi lakhe. Kodwa wena, owu, Bhabheli eyimfihlakalo, uya kwenziwa isizwe esingenamntu ngeyure enye.

Umlilo omkhulu uya kuza phezu kwakho; ukushukuma okukhulu kuya kukutyumza; iinqaba zakho ziya kuwa; iziseko zakho ziya kunyamalala zibe ngamanxuwa, namaza amakhulu aya kugubungela unxweme lwakho, kwaye ndiya kukuvuza ngokwemisebenzi yakho, nomgwebo omkhulu unxamnye nawe. Kuba wakha waba sisikhanyiso sethemba nokhanyiselo ehlabathini ixesha elifutshane, kodwa ukhethe ukubawa, ukuzicingela, norhwaphilizo njengamaqabane akho okulala, owu,henyukazi elikhulu! Ngubani oya kukusindisa kum nomsindo wam nengqumbo yam esondelayo eshushu phezu kwakho? Umi ngamatyathanga, ulindele umgwebo wakho wokugqibela, oya kubonakala kwihlabathi liphela, noloyiko olukhulu luya kuza kwabaninzi.

Yabonani! Zininzi iintlanga uBawo azibhukuqileyo emgwebeni, kuba umisa enye aze ahlise enye, kuba uYahweh ulongamile kwaye akukho mkhulu kunoThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade omelayo aze asindise abakhe emililweni ebekelwe abakhohlakeleyo. Yabonani! Iintlanga ezenza ubukhosi obukhulu zisekhona, kodwa ngendlela encitshisiweyo, kodwa ziqhubekile. Kodwa wena, owu, henyukazi elikhulu, uzenzile ihenyukazi nabathandi abaninzi ngenxa yokubawa okubi neziyolo zesono. Ubalahlekisile abantu bam kum kwihlabathi liphela ngamanyala obunike iintlanga ukuba ziwatye ziwasele, kwaye uya kulahlwa phantsi ngokupheleleyo, kwaye akusayi kuphinda ubekho. Kwaye eluthuthwini lwakho akuyi kuphakama isizwe esikhulu, kodwa intsalela iya kusindiswa. Isandla sotshaba sisezintanyeni zenu, kwaye ndizise iintlenge emideni yenu. Ziya kuphanga, zihluthe, zithimbe okuseleyo. Ngubani oya kukunceda?

Kuba umkhanyele ummelwane wakho, kwaye akukho koyika iNkosi ezintliziyweni zenu. Uthe, Andingomhlolokazi, kodwa ungumhlolokazi kwaye uyimfama nesisithulu phambi kwam. Alukho usindiso kuwe ngenxa yokuhenyuza kwakho kokukrexeza okwenzileyo nehlabathi; ngoko ke, ndiya kukuzisela intshabalalo epheleleyo. Iziganeko ezizayo ziya kwenzeka ngokukhawuleza nangakumbi; emva koko unogumbe omkhulu uya kugubungela izikhaka zakho. Umlilo uya kukutshisa ngokupheleleyo kwaye uya kucoca abenzi bobubi elizweni, nabathinjwa bam baya kukhululwa, kwaye akukho nto inokunqanda oko kuza kwenzeka. Ndim iNkosi uYesu Krestu, kwaye ndiyanixelela ngaphambi kokuba kwenzeke, kwaye ndivakalisa oko kuza kwenzeka ngokungathi sekwenzekile, kuba ziqinisekile ezi ntetho, kwaye akukho nto inokuziguqula, njengoko iziprofeto ziya kuzaliseka.

Yimani niqine, bantu bam, njengoko ndiya kumxhwila umtshakazi wam; kuba ndinezicwangciso neenjongo ngaye kubukumkani bam obuzayo. Uya kuhlala enam, kwaye akasayi kuze emke kum. Ndiyamthanda kwaye ndiyamxabisa ngothando olugqibeleleyo novelwano. Ndiya kunenza nibe namandla eluzukweni lwam, kwaye niya kwenza imisebenzi emikhulu kwilifa lenu elizayo, ngoko musani ukoyika. Yazini ukuba intliziyo yam iyaphuka, kwaye ndilusizi ngabantu bam abangazilungiselelanga. Abanye baya kushiywa ngasemva kuba uthixo weli hlabathi uvale iingqondo zabo, kwaye abaninzi abakulungelanga. Musani ukoyika, nangona niya kunyamezela lukhulu ngenxa yam, nonke niya kusindiswa nina eninophawu lukaThixo ophilayo. Ndiya kunibonelela ukuba anilikhanyeli igama lam, ukuba, nisebenzela mna emsebenzini endininike wona.

Niya kwenza imisebenzi emikhulu ukuba niyandicela ukuba ndinithume. Ndinibizile kwaye ndinithanda kakhulu, nabo babiza egameni lam banyamezele kude kube sekupheleni baya kusindiswa baze bavuswe babe seluzukweni olukhulu baze bavuzwe ngonaphakade ngenxa yokuma kwenu, njengoko ndinithanda kwaye ndikuthethile ezindlebeni zenu. Kukho izivuno ezininzi kwaye ngasinye ngexesha laso, ngoko musani ukoyika; kuba amaxesha anzima ahlala enikwa amadoda amakhulu kaThixo ukuba aluse umhlambi, kwaye ndinibizile. Iingelosi zam ziya kubatywina abayi-144,000, kwaye abanye benu baya kubanceda emisebenzini yabo nakuthumo endibanike lona. Musani ukoyika, kuba ndiya kwenza imiqondiso emininzi, imimangaliso, nezimanga ngezandla zenu, ngoko phumulani eluthandweni lwam niphulule emandleni am; kuba ndinani kwaye andisayi kuze ndinishiye okanye ndinilahle.

Niya kwazi ukuba ndimxhwilile umtshakazi wam, kwaye ndiya kuba ndibuthele abathembekileyo kum ukuze ndivelise inguquko kwabaninzi kwihlabathi elifayo. Ngoko ke mayidlule ihlabathi kwaye ningabelani nentlekele nesiphelo salo kuba asiyeyenu ukuba nisaya kuyingcwalisa iNkosi ezintliziyweni zenu niphilele mna ngokwahlukileyo. Kuba ububi obukhulu buyasondela, kwaye abuzange buphakame ngale ndlela, kwaye abuyi kuphinda buphakame, bantwana bam abancinci, kuba ndikutshilo. Thuthuzelekani kwindlela endinibekele yona, kwaye gcina iinyawo zenu zizinzile endleleni. Ndiya kuba nani kwaye ndiya kuninika uxolo olungaphaya kokuqonda, kwaye niya kuba noxolo phakathi kwehlabathi lesiphithiphithi, uxolo oluya kunigcina ukuba anijikeli ngasekhohlo okanye ngasekunene.

Musani ukuthatha uphawu, kwaye ningawakholelwa amanga. Baya kunika uxolo nempilo nokhuseleko kwabo bathatha uphawu, kwaye baya kunixelela ukuba ningena kwihlabathi elitsha loxolo nempumelelo nokhuseleko. Ningawakholelwa amanga enyoka, kuba ukumkani wabo ngokuqinisekileyo akayi kuligcina ilizwi lakhe. Loo mntu wesono unomsebenzi omnye kuphela, kwaye ngulo: ukutshabalalisa.

Akukho nyaniso kuye, kwaye uSathana ngokwakhe uya kulawula iintambo zakhe. Akuyi kubakho luxolo luvela kuye, kodwa lonke ilizwi liya kuba bubuxoki obuthobayo, obuyilelwe ukukhokelela ihlabathi kwintshabalalo epheleleyo. Kwaye ukuba bendingayinqumlanga imihla, bekungayi kusindiswa nyama kwisibetho aya kusizisa elizweni, kuba uya kunikwa amandla okugqibezela iinjongo zam ixesha elifutshane. Emva koko uya kubotshwa ngamatyathanga anzima obumnyama enzonzobileni ukuze akhululwe ixesha elincinane ukuze akhohlise ihlabathi okokugqibela. Xa ebabuthele kuye, baya kuhlasela eso sixeko sikhulu, nomlilo ovela kuThixo uya kubatshabalalisa bonke. Emva koko umgwebo wokugqibela, ewe, umgwebo wetrone enkulu emhlophe uya kuqhambuka phezu kwabo bonke, kwaye abayi kuba nanyango. Ndiyanixelela ezi zinto ukuze ndinike ithemba kwaye ndilungiselele abantu bam, kuba iMfihlelo eNkulu kaThixo iya kuzalisekiswa.

Ndim indlela, inyaniso, nobomi.

Ndim ofanele ukuwavula amatywina, kwaye amaxesha afikile.

Ndim ovavanya iintambo zabanyulwa bam ukuze ndinihlanjulule ndinicoce ngegazi lam lokucamagushela.

NdinguNyana kaThixo oze enyameni, wabethelelwa emnqamlezweni, waza wavuka ngosuku lwesithathu, wabonwa ngabaninzi, waza wamkelwa emazulwini phambi kwamehlo abo.

Ndingunyana kaDavide, kwaye ndiya kubutha intsalela kaYakobi phantsi kweengalo zam zentuthuzelo namandla.

Ndim oya kuzalisekisa konke ukuthanda kukaBawo.

Ndisele ndiza kwenza iintshaba zam isihlalo seenyawo zam, kwaye yeha kwabo bandichasayo, kuba inkulu ingqumbo yeMvana.

Ndim oya koyisa indalo kaBawo aze anikele zonke izinto esandleni sakhe, ukuze uThixo abe yinto yonke kuyo yonke into.

Ndim obukumkani bakhe bungunaphakade kanaphakade, kwaye ndiya kunika abanyulwa bam ukuba batye emthini wobomi baze basele emilanjeni yamanzi aphilayo, kwaye anisoze nilambe okanye ninxanwe kwakhona, kwaye ndiya kuzosula zonke iinyembezi emehlweni enu.

Mna, iNkosi, ndikuthethile, kwaye ndiya kwenza kube njalo. Ixesha lifikile, ngoko lindelani ukubonakala kwam, njengoko kungekho nto inokunqanda isandla sam esinamandla.
