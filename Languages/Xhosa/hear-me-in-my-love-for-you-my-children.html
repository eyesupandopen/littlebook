Ndive Kuthando Lwam Ngani Bantwana Bam!

(Ngokomxholo wesigidimi esiprofetayo, “umEdom” ubhekisa kwabo bamkela iindlela zamaKanan zokuguqula imfuza, ubugqi obumnyama, ukubingelela abantwana, ukudlana, njalo njalo. UEsawu watshata abafazi ababini abangamaHiti, abangamaKanan, waza wathatha umfazi wesithathu endlwini kaIshmayeli. Ukutshata kukaEsawu abafazi abangamaKanan kubonisa ukungathobeli nokuyekelela kunye nokungcoliswa kwemfuza, kuba uKanan wayephazamise imfuza ngandlel’ ithile. Igama elithi umEdom lisetyenziswa kulo mxholo kwabo basenza ezi zithethe zikaSathana, kwaye esi sihloko sinokufanela naliphi na iqela lohlanga okanye isizwe esihambelana nenkcazo. UKumkani uHerode wayengumEdom, kwaye nabaninzi babaFarisi babenjalo, kwaye uYesu wababiza ngokuba ngoonyana boMtyholi, nto leyo ebonisa imbewu. Qaphela, nangona kunjalo, igama elithi umEdom njengoko lisetyenziswe kwesi sigidimi alibhekiseli nakubani na ngokusekelwe kuhlanga okanye ubuzwe babo. Sisazisi esisetyenziselwa ukubiza abo benza ubugqi bamaKanan ngasese okanye abo baphuma kuloo minombo yegazi kwaye basasazeke kuzo zonke iintlanga.)

— William Brooks


Waveni amazwi am, Cawa yam, nithobe iindlebe zenu kubulumko nenyaniso, kuba yabonani: Amazwi am bubulumko nenyaniso. Ubulumko bukhwaza ezitratweni, nokuqonda kufuna intetho nawe; ngaba ngoku uya kundiboleka indlebe yakho ngokuthobela ngokuzithandela? Kuba mna, Yeshua HaMashiach, ndikhala kakhulu ukuze ndiviwe nguMtshakazi wam, kwanabo bonke abasemzimbeni kaKristu. Waveni amazwi am, utsho uYehova omkhulu nothembekileyo, kwanalowo ubukumkani bakhe ingebubo obeli hlabathi, kuba ubukumkani bam bubukumkani bamazulu. Ngoko ke ebukumkanini bam, njengoko kunjalo ezulwini, kuya kuba njalo nasemhlabeni; kuba ndiya kuzisa ubulungisa emhlabeni, kwaye akukho nto imbi iya kungena emasangweni am, kuba ubukumkani bam bubukumkani bobulungisa apho ingonyama iya kubuthuma nemvana ngoxolo nokhuseleko. Uninzi lwenu, Mtshakazi wam, niye naba nekratshi ngezi ntsuku zolwazi olwandileyo, kwaye oku akulindelekanga, kuba lithini iLizwi lam: Kuba ulwazi luyakhukhumeza! Kodwa uthando luyakha. Ngoku ndiveni kuthando lwam ngani, bantwana bam!

Waveni amazwi am, kuba ndiza kuthetha ngamaEdom aphakathi kwenu, kuba uninzi lwenu anazi ukuba ndibhekisa kubani. Ndiza kuthetha ngabo banxiba iimpahla zeegusha kodwa beziingcuka eziphangayo, kuba bayazifihla kwaye bahamba phakathi kwenu. Ndiza kuthetha ngendlu yesikhungu kaSathana nabo bonke abangenelele kwindalo kaBawo, bethunyelwe nguSathana ngokwakhe, kuba bayimbewu kanye yenyoka. Owu, yima, musani ukuvala iindlebe zenu nigqume amehlo enu kuba anivumelani namazwi am, owu, bantwana bam abancinci abaziimfama nabazizithulu ngokuzithandela, kodwa guqani ngamadolo ngokuthobeka nithobe indlebe yenu kumazwi am, kuba ikratshi lihamba phambi kwentshabalalo nomoya okhukhumeleyo phambi kokuwa.

Yazini, bantwana bam abancinci, ukuba amazwi akwiLizwi lam likaThixo ayinyaniso, achanekile, kwaye athembekile: nokuba aniwava la mazwi kwaye nizama ukususa inyaniso yokoqobo, nisithi kuni: Owu, oko akukho ngokoqobo kodwa kungumfuziselo. Owu, oko akubhalelwanga mna kodwa komnye umntu. Owu, ezo zintsomi nje zakudala nezixhobo zoncwadi. Owu, la ngamabali nje amaxesha adlulileyo kwaye akufuneki athathwe ngokoqobo. Sidenge! Nivumela abelusi abalahlekileyo ukuba bathi iLizwi likaThixo kanye, la mazwi afanayo eza ngawo usindiso lwenu, amazwi kaYahweh ngokwakhe ayingonyaniso kodwa anokuchazwa ngokungakholwa? Owu, sukani kwiindlela zenu zokungakholwa, bantu bam, nize kum ngentliziyo evulekileyo nengqondo enxibe ukuthobeka.

Anifundanga na? Ithini iGenesis ngalo mbandela xa uYahweh wayejongene nenyoka ngenxa yobubi bayo bobuqhetseba eyabenzayo eMyezweni kule minyaka emininzi yeYubhili edlulileyo? Ndiya kumisa ubutshaba phakathi kwakho nomfazi, naphakathi kwembewu yakho nembewu yakhe: yona iya kutyumza intloko yakho, wena uya kutyumza isithende sakhe. Nicinga ukuba la mazwi yintelekelelo nomfuziselo, bantwana bam? Nikholelwa kumazwi okungakholwa okhanyiselo olwenze iingqondo zenu zangaboni kumazwi am ngalo lonke eli xesha ngoku? Ubuxoki obamkelweyo kwinzululwazi yenu, kuba benziwe bungebubo ngobuxoki babo. Ubuxoki obamkelweyo kwindlela enibona ngayo indalo, kuba benziwe bungebubo ngobuxoki babo. Ubuxoki obamkelweyo kwimbali yenu, kuba nilibele ukuba ningoobani, bantwana bam abancinci, kwaye niye nahlukaniswa nembali yenu yokwenyani kwaye niphila ubuxoki botshaba ekungazini ngokupheleleyo ihlabathi lokwenyani eniphila kulo.

Ngokuqinisekileyo njengokuba ndiyimBewu ethenjisiweyo yomfazi, ewe, loo mfazi umangalisayo esibhalweni (abanye abamnqula ngesono) owathi, Makube kum ngokwentando yakho, endafika ngaye kweli hlabathi. Ndazalwa nguMariya eBhetelehem yaseYuda ndaza ndakhuliswa njengonyana womchweli, ndakhuliswa nguYosefu owayengowomnombo kaDavide, xa wayengafuni ukumenza umboniso woluntu (kuba wayemthanda kakhulu), kuba wafunyanwa ekhulelwe nguMoya oyiNgcwele. Kanye njengokuba ndiyimBewu ethenjisiweyo ethunyelwe nguThixo, naye uSathana unembewu. Ingekuko ukuba unokudala nantoni na ngokwakhe, kuba ungumnyeli nelesela, kodwa wenze amasikizi okonakalisa kwenu.

Kutheni kunzima kangaka ukuba nikukholelwe oku? Nimamela ubuxoki obudluliswa njengenyaniso, niye nazigatya izinto ezisisiseko, kuba ndinixelele izinto zasemhlabeni, anikholwa: niya kukholwa njani ukuba ndinixelela ngezinto zasezulwini? Aniwafundanga na amazwi kaYahweh kumkhonzi wakhe othembekileyo uYobhi? Wawuphi na wena, xenikweni ndandiseka umhlaba? Xela, ukuba unokuqonda. Ngubani na owamisa imilinganiselo yawo, ukuba uyazi? Okanye ngubani na owolula umgca wokulinganisa phezu kwawo? Zibethelelwe phezu kwantoni na iziseko zawo? Okanye ngubani na owabeka ilitye lawo lembombo? Xa iinkwenkwezi zokusa zazicula kunye, nabo bonke oonyana bakaThixo beduma yimivuyo. Fundani loo nxalenye yokugqibela kwakhona, bantwana bam,

"oonyana bakaThixo beduma yimivuyo" — ewe, iingelosi zazikho ekudalweni, kwaye ziyazazi iimfihlelo ezininzi zezulu ezingenzelwanga uluntu ukuba luzazi, kuba uluntu luye lwafuna iminyaka emininzi kwiinkanuko zalo zamandla nozuko.

Olu lulwazi lweengelosi eziwileyo, bantwana bam, anifundanga na? Okanye nisakholelwa kubuxoki bamaEdom abahlwayele phakathi kwenu? Kodwa ithini iGenesis ngalo mbandela? Kwakukho izigebenga emhlabeni ngaloo mihla: kwanasemva koko, xa oonyana bakaThixo bangena kwiintombi zabantu, zaza zabazalela abantwana; bona baba ngamadoda anamandla, awayekho mandulo, amadoda adumileyo. Uninzi lwenu luyawudinisa umonde wam nikholelwa kwintsomi enempazamo yokuba oonyana bakaThixo babephuma kuSete, kwaye batshata iintombi ezikhohlakeleyo zabantu baza bavelisa izigebenga ebubini. Uluntu belukhohlakele ukusukela ekuweni, kwaye nokuba bangakanani na abantu abakhohlakeleyo abazalanayo, inzala yabo ayiyi kuba zizigebenga ezikhulu kakhulu. Hayi. Leyo yintsomi eyenzelwe ukukhohlisa, kuba inxalenye yempumelelo kaSathana kungenxa yemfihlo yakhe yokunganyaniseki, kwaye woyisiwe, ngoko impumelelo yakhe yexeshana iphelele ekusileleni okupheleleyo kwaye akayi kulibaleka ichibi lakhe. Musani ukumoyika, kodwa waqondeni amazwi eLizwi lam kumxholo wawo webhayibhile, kwaye ningabi ziziwula nabangayaziyo inyaniso.

Oonyana bakaThixo ngabalindi kulo mxholo, iingelosi ezaziyalelwe ukunceda uluntu kodwa endaweni yoko zashiya isikhundla sazo sokuqala, kuba zazikhanukela iintombi zabantu. Kuba ndamxelela ntoni uYuda ukuba avakalise egameni lam? Kwaye iiNgelosi ezingagcinanga isikhundla sazo sokuqala, kodwa zashiya ikhaya lazo, uzigcinele ematyathangeni angunaphakade phantsi kobumnyama, kude kube ngumgwebo wosuku olukhulu. Yifundeni kwakhona kwaye ningabi ngabangakholwayo, kodwa kholwani kuphela. Oonyana bakaThixo ziingelosi eziwileyo ezazingcolisa nabafazi; uninzi lwaba bafazi lwathathwa lwaza lwaxhatshazwa, nangona abanye babo benza oko ngokuzithandela kwaye baya kuthwala umgwebo wabo.

Inzala abayivelisayo ekungcoleni kwabo, ewe, lo manyano lungcwele lwavelisa izigebenga, iindalo ezikhohlakeleyo ezenzelwe ukonakalisa kwenu. Nizenzela umonakalo omkhulu ngokuxhomekeka ngokumxinwa kwiincwadi zenu ezingama-66 kuphela. Zininzi iincwadi ezamkelweyo kwaye iincwadi zenu ezingama-66 zamaProtestanti azinazo zonke iziprofeto ezakha zanikwa kodwa zinezo zinto zonke eziphathelele kubomi nokuhlonela uThixo kum uKristu Yesu. Fundani iincwadi zika-1 Enoki noYashere neeYubhili. Azizo zibhalo kuni kodwa ngokuqinisekileyo zicetyiswa ukuba zifundwe kwaye zicatshulwe kwizibhalo kanye enizithandayo. Musani ukulawulwa kukucinga ukuba ziincwadi ezivunyelweyo kuphela kwiqela elithile leencwadi ezamkelweyo ezizizo zonke iziprofeto ezakha zanikwa. Fundani ngam.

Noko ke, uThixo walitshabalalisa ihlabathi elalikhona ngoko, kuba uthini umhlobo wam uPetros: Kuba oku bakutyeshela ngabom, ukuba ngezwi likaThixo amazulu ayekho kwakudala, nomhlaba umi uphuma emanzini, nasemanzini, Ngaloo nto ihlabathi elalikhona ngoko, ligutyungelwe ngamanzi, latshabalala. Kwaye ngemihla kaNowa uBawo wathumela unogumbe omkhulu owatshayela onke amasikizi abo amabi, kuba benza amasikizi amaninzi azisa intshabalalo enkulu emhlabeni. Baphula umthetho wendalo wobomi baza bangenisa ukungazinzi kwi-DNA kanye yoluntu, kwaye umntu waxutywa nerhamncwa nerhamncwa laxutywa nentlanzi kwaye uhlobo olunye lwerhamncwa lwazaliswa nolunye uhlobo lwerhamncwa de kwafikelelwa kumasikizi awayefunwa. Emva koko amasikizi atshabalalisa uluntu. kwaye ngoko uBawo wawatshabalalisa, wasindisa imiphefumlo esibhozo iphila. UNowa wafumana umusa emehlweni kaThixo, kwaye uNowa nosapho lwakhe babengabo bonke ababengonakaliswanga emhlabeni kukuphazamisa kwabo imfuza, kuba uNowa wayeyindoda elilungisa.

Fundani amazwi am kwaye niliqonde ihlabathi lenu, kuba uninzi lwenu lukholelwa kubuxoki bokhanyiselo lobuxoki olwalela nantoni na enobume bokomoya kwaye lwanceda ukwenza izihlwele zibe zizidenge kubuxoki bazo bokungakholwa bokuba izinto zasemhlabeni kuphela kwezinto ezifanelekileyo. Aniqondi na, bamalela uThixo kwiintetho zabo zokungakholwa? Banikhohlisile ngabom ngokufunda ngokuchasene nokufunda: fundisisani ukuze niyiqonde le mfundiso yamaJesuit.

Kodwa ithini iGenesis ngalo mbandela? Kwakukho iziGebenga emhlabeni ngaloo mihla: kwanasemva koko.

Emva kwayiphi imihla, uyabuza? Emva kwemihla kaNowa. Kuba emva konogumbe umhlaba wahlulwa ngeqashiso lelifa, kwaye ngemihla kaPelege umhlaba wahlulwa ngelifa phakathi koonyana bakaNowa, uShem, uHam noYafete. Ngeenjongo zethu siya kulandela umnombo kaHam, kuba kuHam kwaphuma iminombo yegazi yabaqalekisiweyo, kwanuNimrodi waseKushe naseYiputa okanye eMizrayim, namaKanan amabi ehla kuKanan okhohlakeleyo. Kuba yayinguKanan lowo uNowa wamqalekisayo. Kuba uKanan walishiya ilifa lakhe waza waseka isixeko saseTire kunxweme olwaba lolwamaFenike waza wakhokelela eTsidon apho uYezebhele wehla khona. Wenza oku ukuze abe kufutshane neNtaba iHermon, apho abalindi behla khona nalapho ndaguqulwa khona isimo.

UKanan wafumana oko abalindi bakushiyayo, kwaye wasebenzisa ulwazi lwabo lweengelosi eziwileyo kubugqi obukhulu, kwaye izithethe ezininzi ezikhohlakeleyo zenziwa ngumKanan, kuba bade babingelela ngabantwana babo. Babekhohlakele kwaye bexhaphake kangangokuba wakwazi ukosulela ilizwe laseKanan ngamasikizi akhe asandul’ ukwenziwa, apho uSathana wasebenza ngenxa yezono zakhe. Ngoko ke njengoko uBawo wakhupha amaHebhere ekuthinjweni eYiputa, ngoko wabayalela ukuba bathathe ilifa labo kwezi zizwe zamasikizi ezigebenga. (Anisakholelwa na kumazwi am?) Anifundanga na ingxelo embi eyanikwa ngabahloli abalishumi kwabalishumi elinesibini abathunyelwa kwilizwe laseKanan nguMoses ukuba balihlole? Kuba nguYoshuwa noKalebhe kuphela abathetha amazwi okholo, kodwa ingxelo embi eyanikwayo yayiyinyaniso ngokoqobo, ngaphandle kokuba yayiyingxelo enesono, yokungakholwa eyayikhanyela amazwi kaBawo endaweni yoko bakubonayo ngamehlo abo, kuba uThixo mkhulu kunamehlo kabani na, bantwana bam abancinci. Fundani ingxelo kwiDuteronomi: Kodwa amadoda awayenyuke naye, athi, Asinakunyuka siye kulwa nabo bantu, kuba banamandla kunathi. Baza bazisa ingxelo embi yelizwe ababelihlolile, kubantwana bakwaSirayeli, besithi, Ilizwe esihambe kulo, ukulihlola, lilizwe elibadlayo abemi balo, nabo bonke abantu esibabonileyo kulo, ngamadoda amade kakhulu. Kwaye apho sabona izigebenga, oonyana baka-Anaki, abaphuma kwizigebenga: kwaye sasifana neenkumbi emehlweni ethu, kwaye sasinjalo emehlweni abo.

Ngoko uYoshuwa walicoca ilizwe kula masikizi mabi, kwaye ayengekho oko ningakubiza ngokuba ngabantu; ayengamasikizi axutyiweyo enzelwe ukutshatyalaliswa komnombo kaKristu, njengoko uSathana wayenqwenela ukunqanda ukuza kwam kokuqala. Noko ke, abantwana bakwaSirayeli abazange bawenze ngokuthembeka umsebenzi uBawo awabanika wona wokubanga ilifa labo kubahlaseli abanobundlobongela. Uninzi lwezi zidalwa lwaludlana; anifundanga na apho ilizwe ladla abemi balo? Kuba bathini abaGwebi ngalo mbandela: Kwaye uYehova wayenoYuda, waza wabagxotha abemi bentaba, kodwa akakwazanga ukubagxotha abemi bentlambo, kuba babeneenqwelo zentsimbi.

Kwaye abaGwebi bathi kwakhona: NoManase akazange abagxothe abemi baseBheteshehan, nemizi yayo, okanye iTaanaki nemizi yayo, okanye abemi baseDore, nemizi yayo, okanye abemi baseIbleam, nemizi yayo, okanye abemi baseMegido, nemizi yayo: kodwa amaKanan ayeza kuhlala kwelo lizwe. Kwaza kwathi xa uSirayeli womelela, wabeka amaKanan erhafini, kwaye akazange awagxothe ngokupheleleyo. Kuba ithini iNumeri ngale ngxelo: Kodwa ukuba aniyi kubagxotha abemi belizwe phambi kwenu, kuya kwenzeka ukuba abo nibayekayo kubo, babe ngameva emehlweni enu, nameva emacaleni enu, kwaye baya kunikhathaza elizweni enihlala kulo.

Kwaye ngoko isibhalo sazaliseka kwaye izizwe zamaKanan zisabakhathaza abantwana bakaThixo unanamhla. Bazixube phakathi kwendalo kaThixo kwaye bazifihle phambi kwamehlo, bade bathatha inkcubeko neendlela zokuziphatha zezizwe abahlala kuzo. Kodwa isiphelo siyafana ngalo lonke ixesha: Bazitshabalalisa ngabom izizwe abahlala kuzo (uThixo ubenze iimbaleki nabahambi-hambi emhlabeni). Bathethe ubuxoki obukhulu baza bathengisa kwangobo buxoki bunye njengenyaniso. Bawathathile amazwe enu, kwaye anibaboni nokubabona, kuba niziimfama kwiindlela zabo kuba uninzi lwenu luye lwayekelela kubo, kwanendlu yesikhungu kaSathana.

Buyelani kwiLizwi lam elinyulu kwaye ningawahoyi amazwi neendlela zamaEdom. Yekani ukuphakamisa amazwi anesono ngaphezu kweLizwi likaBawo eliNgcwele kakhulu. Kuba uninzi lwaba baKanan baba ngamaIdumeya ekuhambeni kwexesha baza bahlala kufutshane noYuda. Xa uJohn Hyrcanus woyisa iIdumeya, waguqulela amaIdumeya kubuYuda, kwaye iYudeya yazalwa kukudityaniswa koYuda neIdumeya. Kwaye ngoko ndema ndichasene nabo kwaSirayeli kwiminyaka engama-2000 eyadlulayo, kwaye ukuma kwam kubhaliwe kwiincwadi zevangeli ukuze nihambe njengoko ndahambayo. Amazwi abo ayijikela iTorah, kwaye izithethe zabo zomlomo nezithethe zabo zobugqi zaseBhabhiloni zazifihla elizweni lam, kuba ilizwe lam lithathwe ngabakhohlisi, iNdlu yesikhungu kanye kaSathana.

Ukususela kwiGenesis 3:15 kuye kwakho imfazwe yembewu phakathi koThixo noSathana kwaye imbewu kaSathana ngokwakhe ilwela ubukhulu, angayi kuba nabo. Ndizuze uloyiso, bantwana bam, kwaye ndinixhobisile, bantwana bam, ngokuchasene nazo zonke iintlobo zobubi, kwaye akukho nto iya kuninqanda nangayiphi na indlela; kuba mna, Yesu Kristu, ndiboyisile ubukumkani botshaba. Yimani nichasene nabo kwaye niyeke ukukhuthaza iindlela zabo ezimbi. Phumani phakathi kwabo kuba bazenze bafana nani. Anikholelwa na kumzekeliso wam wengqolowa nomdiza? Wenza omnye umzekeliso kubo, esithi; Ubukumkani bamazulu bufaniswa nomntu owahlwayela imbewu elungileyo entsimini yakhe: Kodwa ngoxa abantu babelele, utshaba lwakhe lweza lwahlwayela umdiza phakathi kwengqolowa, lwemka. Phumani phakathi kwabo kwaye nizahlule kwindlu yesikhungu kaSathana abathi bangamaYudeya kodwa baxoka kwaye abanjalo. Kuba uSirayeli usasazekile phakathi kweeNtlanga, kwaye andithunyelwanga ngaphandle kwiigusha ezilahlekileyo zendlu kaSirayeli; ngoko amaxesha eeNtlanga aphantse agqitywa, kwaye ndenze kwababini, umntu omnye omtsha kum, ngoko ndenza uxolo.

Bantwana bam, ndininika imbali yenu ngamafutshane, kuba imbali yoluntu isusela kuNowa isiya ku-Adam, yaye uThixo wazenza ngegazi elinye zonke iintlanga. Bantwana bam, ixesha seliphantse laphela, kodwa oko akuthethi kuthi umsebenzi wenu uphelile. Ndisababiza abantwana bam ukuba bandimele baze bandikhonze. Kuba ndinenze abakhonzi abanobuchule boMnqophiso oMtsha osegazini lam. Anifundanga na apho ndaxelela abalandeli bam ukuba ukuba bathe kule ntaba 'suka uphoswe elwandle', baze bangathandabuzi, baya kuzifumana izinto abaziyalelayo? Ndinicebisa ukuba nibuyele umva ivesi enye, kuba elo baliso liqala ngokuthi 'kholwani kuThixo' (kuba kuThixo akukho nto ingenakwenzeka) kwaye aninakwenza nto ngaphandle kwam, bancinane bam.

Uninzi lwenu lufundiswe ngamaEdom ubuxoki obunikhokelela kwiindlela zawo. Kodwa ukuba ninokholo kuThixo, niya kuyalela izinto ezingenakwenzeka, kwaye kuya kufuneka nenjenjalo kanye kumsebenzi endinithuma kuwo. Ndiyanithuma, bantwana bam, ukuba niqokelele iigusha eziseleyo, kuba baninzi abaya kuza kum kule mizuzu yokugqibela kwaye andiyi kubakhaba, kodwa ndisabafuna ngolangazelelo. Vulani imilomo yenu ngokholo nize nindimele nikhonze abantwana bam.

Ndinixelele indlela yokuwasebenzisa amandla am, kodwa niyayala imiyalelo yam, ngoko ke ndiza kuphinda ndixelele abalibalayo phakathi kwenu. Kutheni nicela umoya ukuba uphume kumntu ohlala kuye ngokuthintitha nangokusebenzisa igama lam, uYesu Krestu, ngendlela engenalukholo? Musani ukulisebenzisa kakubi igama lam! Yalalani imimoya ngegama lam ngokholo olukhulu nibe nokholo kumazwi endiya kuninika wona, yaye imimoya emdaka iya kumka ngamandla am asebenza ngani!

Kutheni nicela ukugula ukuba kumke ngokoyika, ekubeni nifanele nithethe nesifo eso ngokuzithemba egameni lam, niyalele okungenakwenzeka egameni lam, niphilise abagulayo? Yithi kuloo ntaba, 'suka uphoswe elwandle', philisa abagulayo, khupha imimoya, vusa abafileyo, uyalele okungenakwenzeka, kuba ndinenze nakwazi ukwenjenjalo.

Anifundanga na ukuba abokuqala baya kuba ngabokugqibela, nabokugqibela babe ngabokuqala? Yazini, bantwana bam, ukuba ndisebenza ngamandla amakhulu kunalawo endandisebenza ngawo kwinkulungwane yokuqala, kwaye ndiwuthulule uMoya wam ngokupheleleyo kuwo wonke umhlaba. Ngubani kuni onobuganga bokundimela? Wokuthetha endaweni yam amazwi endininika wona? Ngubani kuni onokholo lokungoyiki iindaba ezimbi kodwa ajongane nobubi ngqo? Kuba mkhulu lowo ukuni kunalowo usehlabathini. Musani ukuthobela umKanan, kuba uqalekisiwe ukuba athobele nina. Ngapha koko, musani ukuzibophelela kwiminombo yezizwe, kuba ndithunyelwe kuzo zonke iintlanga ukuze ndisindise abanye, kwaye ngokwenjenjalo, ndisindisa uSirayeli wokwenene, kuba uphoselwe phakathi kweentlanga, kwaye nina nonke ninguSirayeli wokwenene kaThixo!

Ndimeleleni nithethe endaweni yam, kuba ixesha seliphantse laphela. Ingaba niya kuba ngomnye wabo baya kusondela esihlalweni sam sokugweba beqokelele iinkuni, ifula neendiza ngaloo mini inkulu isondelayo? Okanye niya kundinika igolide, isilivere namatye anqabileyo, kuba abantu bam bubutyebi bam. Khululani abathinjwa nisebenze nam kule mizuzu yokugqibela. Kwaye ngokungathandabuzekiyo, inkulu imfihlelo yobuthixo: UThixo wabonakaliswa esenyameni, wagwetyelwa ebuMoyeni, wabonwa ziiNgelosi, washunyayelwa kwiiNtlanga, kwakholwa kuye ehlabathini, wamkelwa wenyuselwa ezukisweni.

Bantwana bam, ndiliLizwi elenziwa inyama, kwaye mna noBawo simnye. Ngokunjalo, yibani mnye nathi ngokundithobela kwam, kuba kungekudala niya kufana nam nize namkelwe ezukisweni, kuba ndiziintlahlela zokuqala kwabafileyo, kwaye ndiya kuninikela niliyibandla elizukileyo, elingenabala, nasishwabulo, nayiphi na into enjalo; kodwa ukuze libe ngcwele lingenasiphako. Ixesha seliphantse laphela, ngoko musani ukuthandabuza. Sebenzani nam kule mizuzu yokugqibela, kwaye ningathandabuzi ukuvula imilomo yenu. Ndiza kubazisa kuni, ngoko yenzani imisebenzi yam niyeke eyenu. Ndiya kwenza imisebenzi emininzi enamandla ngezandla zenu, kuphela nje musani ukuthandabuza!
