Kuni Nina Bawayo: Ngoku Kufika uMgwebo Wenu!

Kusuka kuYeshua HaMashiach:

Banyulwa bam, abakhethiweyo, nabathembekileyo, yivani amazwi eNkosi yenu ephilayo nomsindisi, uYeshua uMesiya! Iintlanga enihlala kuzo kunye nembali eniyifundiswe ngabafundisi-buchopho benu abangamaJesuit ziyimvelaphi yenkohliso enkulu, etyhilwa ngokupheleleyo sele isondele kwaye sele iqalile, apho abawayo baya kubonakala njengabaphambukeli abanobubele kodwa eneneni zizithunywa zezulu eziwileyo ezinobubi. Yintoni le ibikho? Yileyo iya kubakho: kwaye yintoni le yenziweyo? Yileyo iya kwenziwa: kwaye akukho nto intsha phantsi kwelanga.

USathana unikezele ngolwazi lwakhe ekuthiwa luphucukileyo kwasekuqaleni, nkqu nolwazi lwakhe lokulungileyo nokubi. Ungumkhohlisi nomxoki oyincutshe ofundise uluntu izinto ezininzi uYahweh ebengazimiselanga, kuba ezi ziindlela zokufa. Indalo kaYahweh ayenzelwanga ukuba isetyenziswe ngobuqhetseba ukuze kufunyanwe amandla nenzuzo yobuqu okanye njengendlela yokubaleka umgwebo kaThixo, okanye ukuseka iindlela nomyalelo womntu. Kuba uSathana wonakalisile uluntu kwasekuqaleni, kwaye wonakalisile abalindi abawayo abazibandakanye nemvukelo yabo.

Owu, wena lutshaba olukhulu lwabo bonke ubulungisa, ewe nina booSathana: izono zenu zokuqala zabhalwa nguEnoki umbhali kulo lonke eli xesha lidlulileyo, kwaye zimi unanamhla, kuba izono zenu zabhalwa ngolo hlobo. Ngoko ke ndiza kushwankathela, kuba nizenze ezi zinto, kwaye nenze okuninzi ngakumbi: Nabalahlekisa bonke oonyana bakaThixo, nabahlisela emhlabeni, nabalahlekisa ngeentombi zabantu. Nanika oonyana bakaThixo abangcwele icebo elibi, nabalahlekisa ukuze bayingcolise imizimba yabo ngeentombi zabantu. Nabonisa abantwana babantu zonke izibetho zokufa, nalahlekisa uEva, nabonisa izixhobo zokufa koonyana babantu: ikhaka nengubo yentsimbi, nekrele lokulwa, nazo zonke izixhobo zokufa kubantwana babantu. Kwaye kwisandla senu ziye zaqhubela phambili kwabo bahlala emhlabeni ukusukela ngaloo mini kuse kube ngunaphakade.

Nina booSathana nafundisa abantwana babantu okukrakra nokumnandi, kwaye nabafundisa zonke iimfihlo zobulumko babo. Nafundisa uluntu ukubhala nge-inki nephepha, kwaye ngenxa yoko abaninzi bona ukususela kunaphakade kuse kube ngunaphakade kude kube namhlanje. Kuba abantu abadalelwanga injongo enjalo, yokungqina ukholo lwabo olulungileyo ngepeni ne-inki. Kuba abantu badalwa kanye njengezithunywa zezulu, ngenjongo yokuba bahlale benyulu kwaye belungile; kwaye ukufa, okutshabalalisa yonke into, ngekungeke kubabambe, kodwa ngolu lwazi bayatshabalala, kwaye ngala mandla kuyabadla abantu.

Nina booSathana nabonisa abantwana babantu zonke izibetho ezikhohlakeleyo zemimoya needemon, nezibetho zombungu esibelekweni, ukuze udlule, nezibetho zomphefumlo, ukulunywa yinyoka, nezibetho ezenzeka ngobushushu basemini, unyana wenyoka ogama linguTabââ'ĕt. Kuba nade nacela uMikayeli ukuba anibonise igama elifihliweyo, ukuze nilibize kwisifungo, ukuze abo bangcangcazele phambi kwelo gama nesifungo abatyhila konke okwakusemfihlekweni kubantwana babantu.

Kuba nabangela ukuba abalindi bazibandakanye nemvukelo yenu njengoko naqhubeka nokonakalisa uluntu. Kwaye ke izono zabalindi zabhalwa nguEnoki umbhali, kwaye ndiza kushwankathela ngolu hlobo: Bafundisa abantu ukwenza amakrele, neemela, namakhaka, nezihlangu zesifuba, kwaye babazisa iintsimbi zomhlaba nobugcisa bokuzisebenza, namasongo, nezihombiso, nokusetyenziswa kwe-antimoni, nokuhombisa iinkophe, nazo zonke iintlobo zamatye axabisekileyo, nazo zonke iidayi ezinemibala. Bafundisa ubugqwirha, nokusika iingcambu, ukusombulula ubugqwirha, ukuvumisa ngeenkwenkwezi, amaqoqo eenkwenkwezi, ulwazi ngamafu, imiqondiso yomhlaba, imiqondiso yelanga, kunye nendlela yenyanga.

Kwaye ke ngenxa yokonakala nobuxoki benu, niyingcolisile indalo kaYahweh ngeendlela zenu ezimbi namasikizi enu anyelisayo. Nilulahlekisile uluntu nona kuYahweh, kwaye nalonile uluntu, nkqu nona kwiintaka, nakwizilo, nakwizirhubuluzi, nakwiintlanzi, kwaye izono zenu zizisa umgwebo omkhulu eningasayi kuphinda niwufumane. Niyingcolisile indalo kaYahweh ngetekhnoloji nobuqhetseba benu, kwaye nabangela ukuba uluntu lumangaliswe ngamaqhinga nobuxoki benu. Kuba uYahweh wanibonisa kuphela iimfihlelo ezingenaxabiso zendalo yakhe waza wagcina iimfihlelo zakhe ezixabisekileyo zifihliwe entliziyweni yakhe kude nokungcatsha kwenu.

Nina bakhohlakeleyo nafundisa iindlela zenu ezingenaxabiso kuluntu njengoko babesanda ebusweni bomhlaba, kwaye nazisa usizi nentshabalaliso enkulu ngamacebo nobuchwepheshe benu obubi. Kuba anizi ngaphandle kokuba nibe, nibulale, nitshabalalise. Nafundisa ukuphatha umoya, kuba niyinkosana yamandla omoya, kuba niyawuphatha umoya ophezulu. Kuba xa uKayin wonayo, wakha isixeko; kwaye nina nifundise uluntu iimfihlo nobuqhetseba benu obungenaxabiso. Ezi zixeko zazisetyenziselwa ulawulo nobuqhetseba nokuzisa intshabalaliso yoluntu, njengoko nazisebenzisela ukwenza amasikizi enu entshabalaliso.

Nibangele ukuba isizwe sivukele isizwe emfazweni nakwingxabano engapheliyo, kwaye nabangela abantu bam ukuba bone, kwaye uYahweh uzisasazile iZizwe zakwaSirayeli kwimimoya emine. Akukhathaliseki, injongo yenu embi iqhubekisele phambili iinjongo zikaYahweh, kuba ngokusasazwa kwazo kuya kufezekiswa ukuqokelelwa kweegusha eziseleyo. Kwaye ndiya kuthumela amangqina am amabini ukuba aprofete iintsuku ezili-1260 enxibe amaxhama. Kuyo yonke imizamo yenu yokutshabalalisa abantwana bakaYahweh nisilele kabuhlungu, kwaye anikwazanga ukubona ukuba imizamo yenu iqhubekisele phambili iinjongo zikaYahweh, kuba nezitha zakhe ziyalithobela ilizwi lakhe.

Kuba nizitshutshisile nazikhathaza izizwe ezili-12 kuwo wonke umhlaba njengoko nanifuna intshabalaliso yazo. Nibe abantwana koomama, nathatha abantwana kwiintsapho nabafudusela kuwo wonke umhlaba, emva koko nabaxokisa nababangela ukuba bakholelwe kwiintsomi zamaJesuit malunga nembali yabo yokwenyani, iinjongo, nemvelaphi. Akukhathaliseki, nisilele, kuba mna, Yeshua HaMashiach, ndiya kubadibanisa ngokupheleleyo nexesha labo elidlulileyo, isizwe kunye nelifa labo lokwenyani, kwaye ndiya kwenza bonke abaziindlalifa zosindiso babe mntu mnye omtsha, ewe, kum, uKrestu Yesu.

Ngobuqhetseba obukhulu nentshabalaliso enkulu, nizifihlile ngobuchule iitekhnoloji zenu, nkqu nokuxoka ukuba abantu bexesha elidlulileyo babengenalwazi kwaye bengenatekhnoloji. Kuba nabonisa abantu bamandulo iimfihlelo zenu ezingenaxabiso, kwaye akukho nto intsha phantsi kwelanga. Kwaye ke niyayifihla inyaniso malunga nobuqhetseba benu bendalo, nkqu nokuyifihla kwabo beniya kubakhohlisa ngenkohliso yenu enkulu. Niphinde nazisa amaqhinga enu omlingo kwakhona, njengoko nilungise iqonga ukuze nizifihle njengabaphambukeli. Kwaye ngohlaselo lwenu lobuxoki lwabaphambukeli niya kubangela ukuba bonke bamangaliswe yimiqondiso nemimangaliso yenu yobuxoki, nkqu nabo amagama abo angabhalwanga eNcwadini yoBomi kwasekusekweni kwehlabathi. Kodwa mna, Yeshua HaMashiach, ndiya kunibhenca ngokupheleleyo, kwaye yonke imfihlo efihliweyo iya kuvela ekukhanyeni.

Yabonani! Ndiya kuxhwila abathembekileyo bam ndibase emazulwini, kuba ndiza kumtshakazi wam kwaye andiyi kunqandwa. Kwaye mna, Yeshua HaMashiach, ndiza kugweba ndenze imfazwe ndizise umgwebo omkhulu emhlabeni, kwaye aniyi kubaleka.

Mna, Yeshua HaMashiach, Nkosi yeeNkosi noKumkani wooKumkani, ndithumele la mazwi ngomkhonzi wam, kwaye ngomkhonzi wam amazwi am athunyelwe, kwaye konke kuya kuzaliseka.

Kusuka kuYahweh, uThixo Omkhulu Ongunaphakade:

Owu, nina booSathana, niyikhohlisile indalo yam ukuba ilandele imiqondiso nemimangaliso yenu yobuxoki, kwaye ingqumbo yam ishushu kuni. Abantu bam baya kwahlulwa ngokupheleleyo kumdiza wenu eniwuhlwayele phakathi kwengqolowa yam kwintsimi yam yobabalo; kwaye uNyana wam, uKrestu Yesu, uya kuqokelela ingqolowa yam esiphaleni sam, kwaye ke iNkosi yesivuno iya kuqokelela, ibophe kwaye itshise umdiza wenu. Umgwebo omkhulu uphezu kwenu, kwaye aniyi kusinda esandleni sam. Kwasekuqaleni, narhubuluza nazifihla phakathi kwabantwana bam, nkqu nokonakalisa abancinci bam kwasekuqaleni ngobuxoki nenkohliso yenu.

Aningoothixo, kwaye nabo balandela inkohliso yenu neenkohliso zoloyiso abangoothixo nabo. Aninakudala, kuba aninawo amandla, kwaye andizange ndinidale nezo zakhono kuba bendisazi ukuba niya kuvukela ngaphambi kokuba nidalwe; kwaye kubulungisa nothando lwam ndanidala ebulungiseni, kwaye nanigqibelele ngemini enadalwa ngayo. Ngeqhayiya lenu elingenaxabiso niyaqhayisa ngobukhulu benu, kodwa aninabo ubukhulu, kwaye ndiya kunibhenca ngokupheleleyo ngenxa yobuqhetseba enibubo. Ndiya kunibeka phambi kooKumkani, kwaye baya kunibona kwaye babone ukuba aninawo amandla kangakanani. Unyana wam, uYeshua HaMashiach, wanoyisa ngokupheleleyo eKalvari, kwaye aninalo ithemba.

Nivumelekile ukwenza kuphela oko ndikuvumelayo, njengoko nilutshaba oloyisiweyo. Neba itayitile yomhlaba ngobuqhetseba nobuxoki benu kuAdam, kwaye ngoku unyana wam okuphela kwamzeleyo, uYeshua HaMashiach, unoyisile eluthandweni lwakhe kum nakwindalo yam; kwaye umhlaba ngowakhe, ewe, iNkosi yeeNkosi noKumkani wooKumkani, kuba nditshilo. Kuba wawuthenga umhlaba noluntu ngegazi lakhe elingenasono, kwaye abo bakhetha ubomi baya kuhlangulwa. Yonke imisebenzi yenu engenaxabiso iya kutshitshiswa, kwaye bonke abakhetha ubomi baya kuphuma kubuxoki nenkohliso yenu baye kubutsha bobomi, kwaye ndiya kwenza abathembekileyo babe nozuko ngakumbi kunokuba naninjalo nina. Kuba nisilele, owu zinkosana ezibuthathaka nezihlwempuzekileyo zomhlaba, ewe yonke inkosana kumyalelo wenu isilele kunye nani, owu nyoka erhubuluzayo, exokayo.

Ngoku kufika ixesha loMgwebo wenu nentshabalaliso epheleleyo yobukumkani benu, nkqu nobukumkani enibakhileyo kwiinzame zenu zokuzama ukundifihla, ewe uYahweh, kwindalo yam. Aniyi kubaleka. Anisayi kuphinda ningene emazulwini, kuba niphoselwe emhlabeni ningasayi kubuya, kwaye indawo yenu ayisekho emazulwini. Nibotshelelwe emhlabeni enawunqwenelayo, owawungengowenu ukuba niwuthathe. Wonke umsebenzi wenu uya kutshitshiswa ebomini babo enazama ukubatshabalalisa (nasemhlabeni ngokwawo); baya kuhlala ngonaphakade eluxolweni nasekhuselekeni, kwaye baya kuba nolonwabo olugqibeleleyo, kwaye ndiya kuhlala phakathi koluntu kwaye ndiya kuba nguThixo wabo.

Kodwa wena, owu rhubuluzayo, awunawo amandla ekujongeni kwam, kwaye awunakwenza nto ukundinqanda. Kwiintsuku ezintandathu ukususela ekuweni komntu, kuba usuku olunye luyiminyaka eliwaka emehlweni am, anifezekisanga nto ngaphandle komgwebo wenu, ukugwetywa, intshabalaliso, nentuthumbo kuni nakubo bonke abalandelayo. Kuba niya kubotshwa niphoswe kwelo chibi likhulu livuthayo apho ndenzele nina awona malangatye ashushu, nkqu namalangatye aluhlaza okwesibhakabhaka, kwaye niya kutsha ngonaphakade, kwaye ndiya kunibuyisela wonke umsebenzi ombi eniwenzileyo, amaxesha amaninzi. Nibulele izihlwele phezu kwezihlwele, kwaye niya kuhlawulela wonke umphefumlo olahlekileyo, kwaye anisoze nisinde. Niya kuzazi zonke izono enizenzileyo, kwaye anisoze nisinde emgwebeni, kwaye niya kuthuthunjiswa ngonaphakade ngenxa yezono enizenzileyo. Niya kuzazi kakuhle izono nezikreqo zenu ekuthuthunjisweni kwenu, kwaye abo nibathiyileyo baya kuba noloyiso lokugqibela phezu kwenu. Kwaye bonke abangemalungisa enabakhohlisela ukuba bone baze bazinikele ekuzibandakanyeni nemvukelo yenu, nkqu nabo amagama abo angazange abhalwe eNcwadini yoBomi, baya kwabelana nentuthumbo yenu. Niya kuhlawulela sonke isono enisenzileyo, kuba nonile kuYahweh nakwindalo yam, kwaye ndiya kubuyisela. Kuba kubulungisa bam ndiya kuzibuyisela ngokupheleleyo izono zenu, izenzo, neendlela ezimbi ezifubeni zenu amaxesha amaninzi kwaye ngonaphakade. Kwaye mna, Yahweh, uThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade, ndifunga ezi zinto ngegama lam, kuba andinakufunga ngomkhulu kunam.

Niya kukhonza iinjongo zam, kwaye kwintiyo nentshabalaliso yenu niya kuqhuba abanyulwa bam kum; kuba ndiya kubakhokela ngesandla sikaYeshua HaMashiach nomkhosi wakhe wamadoda anamandla, kuba baya kuqokelela iigusha zam eziseleyo. (Yabonani! I-144,000 ethunyelwe nguYeshua HaMashiach iya kwenza imisebenzi emikhulu ebusweni bobubi benu obuyinyakanyaka.) Kwaye akukho namnye endimmisele ubomi obungunaphakade oya kuwela kumacebo enu, kuba abanakuxhwilwa esandleni sam, kwaye akukho mntu unokubaxhwila ezandleni zoNyana wam: Kuba ndimnike igama elingaphezu kwawo onke amagama, kwaye uza ngamandla kaYahweh ngokwakhe, kuba ndabelane naye ngetrone yam. Kwaye uya kuyityumza ngokupheleleyo intloko yakho. Usilele, nkosana encinci, kwaye ixesha lakho lifikile.

Mna, Yahweh, uThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade, ndithumele la mazwi ngomkhonzi wam, kwaye ngomkhonzi wam amazwi am athunyelwe, kwaye aya kwenzeka. Kuba uYahweh, uThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade, utshilo, kwaye akukho nto inokwaphula izibhengezo zam. Ndingu-El Shaddai!
