Ingqina eliMbaxa: uSirayeli
Okumnandi Okukrakra
Disemba 3, 2024

Igazi leMvana kaYahweh

Yizani kwiNkosi yenu engunaphakade nize nive amaZwi am, zihlobo zam, nithobe iintliziyo zenu ngokuthobela amaZwi kaYeshua HaMashiach. Kuba mna, Yeshua HaMashiach, ndiya kwenza isilumko phakathi kwenu, Sirayeli, sibe nobulumko obungakumbi xa nithobela imiyalelo yam ukuze ke nifumane ubulumko nolwazi olongezelelweyo, kuba ubulumko nolwazi buya kuba luzinzo lwamaxesha enu namandla osindiso, ukoyika iNkosi bubutyebi benu.

Ngoko ke, qondani amaxesha eniphila kuwo ukuze nihambe ngokuchanekileyo kwaye iinjongo zam zosindiso zifezeke ngokupheleleyo kuni nina babizwe ngegama lam, ewe nani Sirayeli, nangona okwangoku ningandazi yaye ningaphili ngeendlela zam kuba uSirayeli ulawulwa nguEsawu noAshkenazi, ekubeni kutshiwo ngokucacileyo ukuba omdala uya kukhonza omncinci kwaye akukho namnye kuEsawu nakuAshkenazi onelungelo kwilifa lokuzalwa likaYakobi. Kanti ke, uYakobi wamala uYeshua HaMashiach ndaza ndajikela kwiintlanga okwexeshana ukuze ndizalisekise oko uSirayeli angazange akuthobele ngokupheleleyo, ukuba iintlanga ziya kusikelelwa ngoSirayeli nokuba intsalela yezizwe ezilahlekileyo iya kusindiswa.

Noko ke, xa nijikisa iintliziyo zenu ngokuthobela isandi samaZwi omyalelo kaMesiya wenu wenene, niya kubuyiselwa njengabantu abakhethekileyo kuYahweh, nangona ooyihlo bamshiyayo uYahweh kudala. Ewe, bamshiya uYahweh xa bandalayo mna, uYesu waseNazarete, okwanguYeshua HaMashiach, kwanakwiintsuku zokulawula kukaPontiyo Pilato eYuda xa ndanikelwayo ngababingeleli babo abakhulu neSanhedrin ezandleni zabantu abanesono ukuze benze ezo zinto umthetho wawubalela ngokucacileyo ukuba bazenze, kodwa ngenxa yentiyo yabo kum nakuzo zonke izinto zikaYahweh benza oko ngokukhawuleza ngenkohlakalo enkulu. Kwaye ke usindiso luka-Adam lufezekiswa ngezandla zeentshaba zakhe, ukuze nabani na okholwayo nobiza egameni likaYesu waseNazarete okwanguYeshua HaMashiach asindiswe ngokupheleleyo.

Kwaye kunjalo kuba ndiliMvana likaYahweh, ingafezekiswa njani enye indlela intlawulelo yezono zoluntu ngaphandle kokuba igazi lam elingenasono liphalazwe njengedini lokuhlawula ixabiso elingenakulinganiswa nanto. Qondani ukuba nangona umntu wemvelo eqhayisa ngekratshi elikhulu esithi uzenzile kwaye uyazanelisa kuzo zonke izinto, umntu wemvelo uwele kwimeko yesono nentlontlo elusizi efanelwe ngokufanelekileyo.

Kodwa, ngobabalo lwam nenceba yam kunye nokuzincama kwam eGolgotha xa ndazinikela kuYahweh njengemvana kaThixo, xa ndazinikela ngentlungu enkulu njengoko ithontsi lam lokugqibela legazi laliqukuqela eGolgotha, kude kube sekuphefumleni kwam kokugqibela xa ndathetha amazwi athi kugqityiwe. Ndaza ndangcwatywa, ndavuka kwakhona ngosuku lwesithathu ngokwezibhalo; kanye njengoko uYahweh wathembisayo ukuba uya kwenza, kuba akunakwenzeka ukuba ukufa kubambe igazi elingenasono. Kwaye uYahweh wandivusa, apho, kamsinya emva koko ndangena kweyona ngcwele yeengcwele emazulwini ngokwawo, apho ndazinikela njengombingeleli omkhulu ngonaphakade kuYahweh, uMkhulu onguMNA, idini lakhe awalemkelayo ngonaphakade. Ekubeni ke ninombingeleli omkhulu ongako, ongenileyo ezulwini, uYesu unyana kaYahweh, masibambelele siqinise ekuvumeni kwethu. Kuba aninaye, Sirayeli, umbingeleli omkhulu ongenakuvelana nobuthathaka benu, kodwa owahendwayo ngeendlela zonke, kodwa engenasono.

Ngoko ndiyabuza, Sirayeli, yintoni egqityiweyo? Ngoko ndiphendula ngokucacileyo: Umnqophiso wam omtsha, ongunaphakade, iTestamente yam eNtsha! Kuba itestamente iqinisekiswa xa abantu befile: kuba ayinamandla okoko nje lowo wayenzayo esaphila; kwaye phantse zonke izinto zihlanjululwa ngegazi ngokomthetho, kwaye ngaphandle kokuphalazwa kwegazi akukho kuxolelwa kwesono. Ngendlela efanayo neTestamente eNdala xa uMoses wayethethe yonke imiyalelo ebantwini, ngokomthetho, wathatha igazi lamathole neleenkunzi zeebhokhwe, kunye namanzi noboya obumsobo nehisope, wafefa incwadi kunye nabo bonke abantu, esithi, Eli ligazi letestamente, uThixo animisele yona.

Kodwa, itestamente eNdala, itestamente kaMoses yangaphambili, yayingazange yenzelwe isiphelo secebo likaYahweh lokuhlangula uluntu, yaye noSirayeli wayengafanele ahlangane ngekratshi, enza amaqela aqhekekileyo ngokwezahlulo ezininzi ezingeyomfuneko nezibulalayo. Kodwa endaweni yoko uSirayeli wayefanele eme njengesikhanyiso sosindiso kwiintlanga ukuze iintlanga zibone ukuba kukho uThixo kwaSirayeli kwaye ukuze iintlanga zithontelane ukuya kwaSirayeli zifunde ngoYahweh njengoko kwakubhaliwe kumaxesha amandulo.

Ngoko ke, qondani ubume bexeshana bukaMoses njengoko babuqhutywa nguSirayeli kumaxesha angaphambi kweTestamente yam eNtsha kwaye niqonde ukuba uMoses uphila ngokupheleleyo kuMesiya wenu wenene, oyingcwele kaThixo owaphila ngokugqibeleleyo ngokukaMoses nowanikela ngobomi bakhe kanye, konke, ngonaphakade ukuze ahlawule isohlwayo sikaMoses ngenxa yenu. Njengoko kubhaliwe, ngoko ke, qondani iTestamente yam eNtsha, Sirayeli: Kuba uMthetho onesithunzi sezinto ezilungileyo ezizayo, kungekhona umfanekiso wenene wezinto, awunakuze ngaloo madini, abawanikela unyaka nonyaka ngokuqhubekayo, abangcwalise abo beza kuwo. Kuba ngenxa yoko, ngebengayekanga na ukunikelwa, kuba abo beza, bakuba behlanjululwe kanye, ngebengasenaso isazela sezono? Kodwa kuloo madini, kukho ukukhunjuzwa kwakhona kwezono nyaka ngamnye.

Kuba akunakwenzeka ukuba igazi leenkunzi zeenkomo neleleenkunzi zeebhokhwe lisuse izono. Kwaye kunjalo, zihlobo zam, ukuba uMoses wayenokuhlanjululwa kwezono okwexeshana kuphela, kanti izono ezininzi phantsi kukaMoses zahlala apho abo benza izinto ezinjalo banqunyulwayo ebantwini ngonaphakade ngenxa yezono zabo. Ngoko ke, uMoses wayesisithunzi sezinto ezizayo, izinto ezizalisekiswe ngokupheleleyo kwidini lam, kuba igazi lam elingenasono liyacamagushela zonke izono, kanye, konke, ngonaphakade, ukuze bonke abaka-Adam bazuze usindiso ngalo. Kodwa kwabo bamkela uphawu lwerhamncwa, abasayi kuze baxolelwe.

Kwaye kunjalo kubhaliwe ngoYeshua HaMashiach, ukuba ngobomi bam nentobelo yam u-Adam wayenokubuyiselwa kuElohim, umdali wakhe ongunaphakade nonobulungisa. Ngoko ke, xa eza ehlabathini, uthi, idini nomnikelo awuzifunanga: kodwa umzimba undilungiselele. Kwiminikelo etshiswayo, nakwiminikelo yesono, akukholiswanga. Ndaza ndathi, Yabona, ndiyeza (Ekuqaleni kwencwadi kubhaliwe ngam) ukuba ndenze intando yakho, Thixo. Ngasentla, xa wathi, Idini nomnikelo, neminikelo etshiswayo, neminikelo yesono awuzifunanga, yaye akukholiswanga kuzo (ezinikelwa ngoMthetho) Waza wathi, Yabona, ndiyeza ukwenza intando yakho, Thixo, uyasusa okokuqala, ukuze amise okwesibini. Ngaloo ntando siyangcwaliswa, nangomnikelo womzimba kaYesu Kristu owenziwe kanye. Kwaye wonke umbingeleli kaLevi umi imihla ngemihla elungiselela, kwaye amaxesha amaninzi unikela uhlobo olunye lomnikelo, ongenakuze ususe izono: Kodwa le ndoda emva kokuba inikele idini elinye lezono, ihleli ngonaphakade ngasekunene kukaThixo, Kwaye ukususela ngoku ilindile, de iintshaba zayo zenziwe isihlalo seenyawo zayo. Kuba ngomnikelo omnye ubagcwalisile ngonaphakade abo bangcwaliswe ngalo.

Kuba noYahweh uyasingqinela: kuba emva kokuba ethe ngaphambili, Le yiTestamente endiya kuyenza nabo emva kwaloo mihla, utsho uElohim, ndiya kuyibeka imithetho yam entliziyweni yabo, nasezingqondweni zabo ndiya kuyibhala. Kwaye izono zabo nobugwenxa babo andisayi kuphinda ndibukhumbule. Ngoku apho kukho ukuxolelwa kwezi zinto, akusekho mnikelo wesono. Kwaye nimi ngexesha lokungazi kwesizwe apho ooyihlo kudala bamalayo lowo uyiMvana yePasika egqibeleleyo kaYahweh ngokwakhe, apho uYahweh walithanda kangaka ihlabathi wada wanikela ngonyana wakhe okuphela kwamzeleyo ukuze nabani na okholwayo egameni likaYeshua HaMashiach, uYesu uKristu, loo mntu asindiswe ngokupheleleyo. Kwaye ngoko ndiyanibiza ukuba nijike ekungazini kwenu nasebumfamekeni benu obuyinxalenye, Sirayeli, ngokwala nokuchasa ngokupheleleyo ubulumko obunyelisayo bukaEsawu obukwiTalmud yakhe emdaka nize nibuyele kumaZwi acocekileyo kaYahweh, lawo maZwi agcinwe kumgca weMibhalo yaseByzantine, iTextus Receptus njengoko igcinwe kumbhalo kaStephens.

Ngoko ke, qondani ukuba ukwala uYeshua HaMashiach kuthetha ntoni ngonaphakade kuni Sirayeli ukuze niqonde ubunzulu bolo kwala. Kuba ukuba nona ngabom emva kokuba nifumene kwaye nayivuma loo nyaniso, akusekho dini lezono, kodwa kukho ukulindela okoyikekayo komgwebo, nomlilo ovuthayo, oya kudla abachasi. Lowo wawudelayo uMthetho kaMoses, wafa ngaphandle kwenceba phantsi kwamangqina amabini, okanye amathathu: Nicinga ukuba uya kufanelwa sisohlwayo esingakanani na, onyathela phantsi uNyana kaThixo, kwaye ebala igazi leTestamente eNtsha njengento engcwele. Ngoko ke, lamkelani idini lam nize nivume izono zenu kum ukuze ndinixolele kuba ndithembekile kwaye ndinobulungisa ukunixolela izono zenu ukuze nihlanjululwe egazini lam elicamagushayo nize nivumane iziphoso zenu omnye komnye.

Ngoko ke, ndiza kuni ngenceba yam nangobabalo lwam ukuze ndinisuse kubumfama benu obuyinxalenye, Sirayeli, ukuze nizuze usindiso ngokuthobela ilizwi lam kwaye ukuze nimke ekwaleni kooyihlo okugcinwe ngonaphakade kwiTalmud yenu emdaka nakwinkolo yenu emdaka uEsawu ayenzele intshabalalo yenu, kodwa enibambelele kuyo ngokungathi ubulumko bakhe bunikwe nguYahweh ngokwakhe, kungekhona nguSathana. Sirayeli, liveni ilizwi lam elisindisayo nize nilamkele uthando lwam ngani nize nize kum ngeentliziyo zenu ezipheleleyo zivulekile kwaye zilungele ukwamkela ukuze nizaliswe luzuko lonke nesidima.

Ndinika ukuba niqonde uxolelo olupheleleyo nokubuyiselwa okuziswa ligazi lam elicamagushayo kubo bonke abandamkelayo njengeNkosi yabo esindisayo, kwananjengoMesiya wabo wenene, kuba ndinguye. Kwaye njengoko u-Adam wadalwa, wabunjwa, waza wenziwa engenasono kwaye egqibelele ngokupheleleyo de kwaba lusuku awona ngalo ngabom, ngokunjalo intlawulo yesono sika-Adam ifuna igazi elingenasono, imfuneko engenakwenzeka kubo bonke abahleli emhlabeni ngenkohlakalo ukuba bayinikeze. Kwaye ngothando lukaYahweh wanikezela ukugqibelela okunjalo negazi elingenasono emzimbeni wonyana wakhe okuphela kwamzeleyo onikelwe njengedini lezono zika-Adam, ngoko ke akusekho dini lesono.

Ndingulowo wazinikela ngokupheleleyo ngenxa yezono zika-Adam owileyo.

Ndingulowo wazinikela ngokupheleleyo kwintlawulelo engunaphakade ukuze bonke abazinikela kusindiso olungunaphakade bahlanjululwe ngalo.

Ndingulowo unguMbingeleli wenu oMkhulu ngonaphakade ngokomyalelo kaMelkitsedeke, lowo wazinikela njengePasika egqibeleleyo kanye, konke, ngonaphakade.

NdinguYeshua HaMashiach, lowo unguMesiya wenu wenene kwaye ulawulo lwam alunasiphelo.

NdinguKristu Yesu, kwaye ndithumele amaZwi am ngesithunywa sam nomkhonzi wam kwaye ngesithunywa sam nomkhonzi wam amaZwi am athunyelwe.

Ngoko ke, qondani ukuba ukuzaliseka kwezinto zonke kufikile, Sirayeli, kwaye ndiyanibizela kum ngeVangeli engunaPhakade enikwe ngamaxesha afanelekileyo okutyhila kwaye amaxesha okutyhila angoku. Kuba kwasekuqaleni uYahweh wathi kwinyoka emyezweni ngamaxesha apho isono sangena khona ehlabathini, Ndiya kubeka ubutshaba phakathi kwakho nomfazi naphakathi kwembewu yakho nembewu yakhe, wena uya kutyumza isithende sakhe, kwaye yena uya kutyumza intloko yakho.

Kwaye ke ekuhambeni kwexesha uDavide, xa wayeyinkwenkwana engumalusi, owaba nguKumkani kaSirayeli phambi kokuba ubukumkani bohlulwe; owaba yindoda ngokwentliziyo kaYahweh, wakhusela uSirayeli waza wabulala eso sigebenga somFilisti, uGoliyati waseGati owayengumhlanga owayephuma kumaRefayim! Ngoko uDavide inkwenkwana engumalusi waphuma waya emfazweni noGoliyati waseGati njengomalusi, enomsimelelo, ingxowa nesilingi, esiya edabini nesigebenga esiligorha, kodwa uDavide waya emfazweni egameni likaYahweh, ababemdelela amaFilisti, kwaye uDavide wema walwa neentshaba zikaSirayeli ukuze umkhosi kaElohim woyise.

Njengoko ilitye elivela kwisilingi sikaDavide labangela esi sigebenga singcwele siwele emhlabeni siqubuda, uDavide warhola ikrele leso sigebenga waza wanqumla intloko yaso. Kwaye njengoko kubhaliweyo mandulo: Kwaye uDavide wathatha intloko yomFilisti, wayizisa eYerusalem, kodwa isikrweqe sakhe wasibeka ententeni yakhe. Ngoko uDavide wangcwaba ukhakhayi lukaGoliyati waseGati ngaphandle kweendonga zesixeko saseYerusalem. Kodwa, kwidini lam njengemvana kaThixo, ndathathwa ndasiwa ngaphandle kweendonga zesixeko kwaye apho eGolgotha ndabethelelwa emnqamlezweni, kwindawo yokhakhayi. Kwaye njengoko ndandibethelwa emnqamlezweni, bandibethelela kuloo mthi urhabaxa apho batyumza isithende sam ngezikhonkwane zabo xa bandibethelela kuloo mthi urhabaxa. Kwaye ngoko uloyiso lwazuzwa apho ku-Adam xa isithende sam satyunyuzwa apho eKalvari apho intloko kaSathana nayo yatyunyuzwa njengoko igazi lam laliqukuqela lizisa uloyiso phezu kwembewu yenyoka ngonaphakade. Ngoko ke, ngendlela efanayo njengoko inkwenkwana engumalusi yoyisa imbewu kaSathana, ngokunjalo uMalusi oyintloko woyisa uSathana ngonaphakade kwidini lam lothando endalenzela izono zika-Adam.

Kodwa, njengoko ndandisifa ngentlungu enkulu njengesaphuli-mthetho esiqhelekileyo (ubugebenga endimsulwa kuso) ngaphandle kwamasango eso sixeko singcwele, igazi lam elingenasono laphuma emzimbeni wam apho njengombonakaliso opheleleyo womntu onesono womhlaba obomvu efikelela kwinkululeko epheleleyo. Kwaye njengoko igazi lam lalimanisa umhlaba obomvu kwindawo yokhakhayi apho eKalvari intloko yenyoka yatyunyuzwa ngonaphakade, kwaye ekugqityweni kwezinto zonke loo namba indala ngokwayo iya kubotshwa kwaye iphoswe ngonaphakade emlilweni wesohlwayo apho impethu yabo ingafi, kuba linjalo ilifa layo.

Kwaye ndingulowo uzayo ukutshabalalisa ngokupheleleyo ubukumkani beentshaba ukuze inamba ibotshwe kwaye ivalelwe enzonzobileni iminyaka eliwaka ukuze ulawulo lwam olunobulungisa emhlabeni lumiselwe. Ekupheleni kwayo iya kukhululwa okwexeshana layo elifutshane apho iya kuqokelela imvukelo yayo yokugqibela. Kwaye njengoko behlasela inkampu yabangcwele umlilo uya kuhla uvela kuYahweh ngokwakhe uze ubadle ngokupheleleyo ngelo xesha uSathana uya kuphoswa emlilweni ongunaphakade otshayo ngonaphakade nesulfure enukayo.

Ngoko ke, ndinika ukuba niqonde, Sirayeli, isohlwayo sikaMoses sihlawulwe ngokupheleleyo lidini likaYeshua HaMashiach onguYesu uKristu ngolwimi lwesiNgesi ukuze niqonde ukugqibelela kwedini lam nokugqibelela okufezekiswe ligazi lam elicamagushayo ngonaphakade elaphalazwa laza lanikelwa ngenxa yezono zika-Adam. Qondani ngokupheleleyo, Sirayeli, ukuba abawileyo babeyiqondile imfihlelo kaYahweh, ngebengazange bayibethelele emnqamlezweni iNkosi yenu yozuko! Kuba ngokubethelelwa kwam emnqamlezweni apho inyoka yatyumza isithende sam okwexeshana, intloko yenyoka ngoku ityunyuziwe ngonaphakade, ukutyunyuzwa angasayi kuze aphile kuko.

Ngoko ke, Sirayeli, veni amaZwi am nize niluqonde uthando lwam ngani nokuba ndiyanibiza kweli xesha lokugqibela. Ngoko yiqondeni intetho yam kuba kubhaliwe: UThixo woxolo owabuyisa kwabafileyo uYeshua HaMashiach, umalusi omkhulu wezimvu, ngegazi lomnqophiso ongunaphakade, anenze nigqibelele kuyo yonke imisebenzi elungileyo, ukwenza intando yakhe, esebenza kuni oko kukholekileyo emehlweni akhe ngoYesu Kristu, makubekho kuye udumo ngonaphakade kanaphakade, Amen.
