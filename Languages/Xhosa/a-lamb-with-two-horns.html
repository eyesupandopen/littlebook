IMvana Eneempondo Ezimbini

Kusuka ku-YHVH: Mna Ndinguye Omkhulu!

Yabonani! Inkohliso enkulu iphume kwiBhabhiloni Efihlakeleyo yaza yathimba umhlaba ngengalo yayo ebulalayo! Sondelani ukuze niyiqonde inkohliso enkulu, nina bahlali bomhlaba, nize niwaqonde amaxesha eniphila kuwo, kuba inkohliso enkulu ilapha, leyo umhlaba wonwatyiswa yiyo kuba ilizwe labo liqonga kwaye iinkokeli zabo ngabadlali, abazenzisi abakhohlisayo. Ngoko ke ngoku ndiza kutyhila inyaniso malunga nesekisi yabo abayisebenzisela ukuthakatha izihlwele.

Lumkani! Inkohliso enkulu inifikele inemiqondiso emininzi yobuxoki nemimangaliso kwaye le nkohliso inkulu ijoliswe kabukhali ekutshatyalalisweni kukaYakobi, ewe, iinjongo uEsawu azigcine ziyimfihlo kuyo yonke iminyaka, kodwa ngoku ziza kutyhilwa zize zibhencwe ukuze wonke umntu abone. Njengoko unyana wam, uYesu waseNazarete, waxelela ooyise bakwaYuda kule minyaka yeYubhili idlulileyo: Nina ningaboyihlo umtyholi, kwaye niya kwenza iinkanuko zoyihlo. Ngoko ke ndiza kuni, Sirayeli, ukuze ningathinjwa yile nkohliso inkulu, kuba nina niyeyona nto kujoliswe kuyo (nangona umhlaba uya kugungqa) kuba nguwuphi umthiyeli obeka umgibe emehlweni entaka?

Ndim, uYahweh, Mna Ndinguye Omkhulu, ozayo kulumkisa abantu bam ngala Mazwi endiwatyhilileyo ndawathumela ngomthunywa wam nomkhonzi wam omiselwe ezi njongo. Ngoko ke ndiza kwabo bezizwe, nokuba bakufuphi okanye bakude, nokuba baselizweni okanye basasazekile, kwaye ndiya kuqokelela yonke intsalela yam kuMesiya wabo noMalusi Oyintloko, uYeshua HaMashiach.

Kuba nguye oya kukhokelela uSirayeli kukhuseleko ngezandla zabam abali-144,000 abanamandla, kuba uYesu waseNazarete ndimenze uKumkani wooKumkani neNkosi yeeNkosi! Kwaye nguye oya kulawula umhlaba ngobulungisa ngexesha lolawulo lwakhe lweminyaka eliwaka esetroneni kaDavide kwaSirayeli ayizuze njengelifa. Kuba ndatsho ukuthembisa ooyihlo kwaye Ndim lowo uzalisekisa sonke isithembiso endisenzayo amaxesha amaninzi kakhulu kubulungisa bam obungunaphakade ukuze konke kube ngu-ewe no-amen kwaye Ndim uThixo Ongunaphakade Wobulungisa ongatyhafisiyo!

Noko ke, kuya kwenzeka ukuba inamba iphakame ngokupheleleyo kwaye irhamncwa eliphuma elwandle lizifihlile ukuze liphakame ngokupheleleyo phakathi kwabo liya kubakhokela, kwaye liya kuphakama eluthuthwini ngexesha lalo elimisiweyo. Liphange laza lalungiselela intshabalalo epheleleyo yeBhabhiloni Efihlakeleyo, lisebenzisana nerhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni ukulungiselela nokwenza intshabalalo yabo, kwaye ngoko ke ndibanikele ukuba bakwenze oko. Kwaye umhlaba uthakathiwe lihenyukazi elikhulu kwaye awusakwazi ukucinga ngelizwe elingenangcinezelo yehenyukazi elikhulu elicinezele ngayo abantu bomhlaba laza lonakalisa indalo yam ngobunyhukunyhuku bombulo walo obunikezelwe kwihlabathi liphela ngaloo ndebe yegolide isesandleni salo.

Ngoko ke, ndiza kukulimkisa, Sirayeli, ngenkohliso enkulu esezakuthi qwenge umhlaba kwimfazwe epheleleyo nesiphithiphithi, inkohliso abayiqhelisele besebenzisa iteknoloji yabo kuba bonke bangabazenzisi abakhohlisayo, kwaye ngenkohliso yabo umprofeti wabo wobuxoki uya kungena elizweni njengoMoshiach wobuxoki kaEsawu.

Kwaye uEsawu uyazifihla azigqume phakathi koSirayeli, azenze uSirayeli njengoko efuna ukuzixuba noSirayeli, efihla nobume bakhe bokwenyani phakathi kwabo azenza bona. (Bangumdlwane phakathi kwengqolowa kwaye iNkosi Yesivuno iya kubancothula ngokupheleleyo.) Ngoko ke inamba iyaphakama kuba lilo ixesha layo, kwaye uKristu Yesu uya kubasusa ngequbuliso abathembekileyo kuKristu emhlabeni, (bobabini abaphilayo nabafileyo) kwaye ke umgwebo uya kufika ngokupheleleyo kwiBhabhiloni Efihlakeleyo, elo henyukazi likhulu libomvu elaxeliswa kwangaphambili nguYohane uMtyhili kule minyaka emininzi yeeyubhili idlulileyo.

Kwaye Ndim uNyangelemihla, iNkosi Yemimoya, kwaye ndiyangqina, ukuze bonke bayiqonde intetho yam: Into eyayikho iya kubuya yenzeke: kwaye into eyenziweyo, iyenziwa kwakhona, kwaye akukho nto intsha phantsi kwelanga. Ngoko ke abaWileyo basebenzele ukutshabalalisa uAdam emhlabeni kwasekuqaleni ukuze bazithabathele wona, umyezo wam wobomi apho ndinyamekelayo ndikhulise imiphefumlo yabantu ukuze ndibakhulule ekonakaleni ngoKristu Yesu endimdalileyo njengoAdam wokugqibela. Kwaye kukuMesiya wenu, Sirayeli, apho uSirayeli kaThixo uya kuqokelelwa ngokupheleleyo aze ahlangulwe njengomntu omnye omtsha owenziwe ngendlela efanayo neNkosi yenu nomkhululi wenu, owathenga inkululeko yenu ngegazi lakhe lentlawulelo.

Ngoko qonda Sirayeli ukuba uYesu waseNazarete nguYeshua HaMashiach okwanguKristu Yesu (qonda ukugxininiswa kwamagama akhe kuba usindiso lwenu lugqibelele) ngoko yivani amazwi akhe ngokuthobela ngenxa yemiphefumlo yenu, kuba anisenaxesha lokuphikisana nomdali wenu kwaye anisenaxesha lokwala uMesiya wenu wokwenyani.

Kwaye kubhaliwe njengoko ndathethayo, kwaye ngoko ndenza uYeshua HaMashiach atyhilele umpostile wakhe uPawulos ngaye: Kungoko ke, uElohim naye wamphakamisa kakhulu, wamnika igama elingaphezu kwawo onke amagama, Ukuze ngegama likaYesu onke amadolo agobe, ezinto ezisezulwini, nezinto ezisemhlabeni, nezinto eziphantsi komhlaba, Kwaye zonke iilwimi zivume ukuba uYesu Kristu uyiNkosi, kube luzuko kuThixo uYise. Ngoko ke, mna YHVH, Mna Ndinguye Omkhulu, ndiyabuqinisekisa kwakhona ubunyaniso bala Mazwi ukuze ukholo lwenu lungabi nakushukunyiswa.

Inkohliso enkulu ilapha kwaye abaninzi baya kubanjiswa kuyo kwaye abaninzi baya kuwela emalangatyeni, bonke abo amagama abo angazange abhalwe kwiNcwadi YoBomi YeMvana kwasekuqaleni. Kuba, mna El Shaddai, ndim owazi isiqalo nesiphelo kwaye nangona, ndinika wonke umphefumlo ithuba lokuphila ubomi bobulungisa phambi kwam, ndiyazi ukuba ngubani oya kukhetha ukuzalisa izono zakhe ngokupheleleyo kwabo baya kuza kuKristu ukuze badalwe ngokutsha ebulungiseni kwaye izigwebo zam zigqibelele ebulungiseni bam.

Kodwa nina Sirayeli, aninabo ubulungisa kuba nimkhabile uThixo Ongunaphakade Wobulungisa kuba niyamala uNyana wam okuphela kwamzeleyo endamthumela ukuba axolele izono njengemvana kaThixo, ngoko ke, izono zenu zisekhona. Ngoko ke ndiyanicebisa, Sirayeli, yivani amazwi kaMna Ndinguye Omkhulu kweli xesha lokugqibela nize namkele idini lam!

Ewe, Sirayeli, ndanibonelela ngedini eligqibeleleyo kuba uAdam wonakaliswa ekugqibeleleni kwaye ubomi benyama busegazini. Ngoko ke isono sidluliselwa ngegazi elingubomi benyama ukusuka kuYise kuye kwinzala yakhe nokuba yindoda okanye ngumfazi bazuza ukonakala kuba uAdam wonakele ngonaphakade. Kwinceba yam nothando lwam olungunaphakade ndabonelela ngento engenakwenzeka ukuba yenziwe nasiphi na esinye isidalwa: Ndadala ndaza ndanikela ngoNyana wam okuphela kwamzeleyo, lowo uMoses ayalela ukuba nimthobele, ngenxa yezono zoluntu lonke, nokuba ngamaYuda okanye ziintlanga, nokuba ngamakhoboka okanye ngabakhululekileyo.

Noko ke kunjalo Sirayeli ukuba iintshaba zenu zinqwenela intshabalalo yenu kuba zinqwenela ukuba nexesha lazo legolide kwakhona, ixesha legolide abangasoze balifumane kwakhona kuba endaweni yexesha labo legolide langaphambi konogumbe, elalizele sisono ngokuzithandela apho kwakuzulazula amasikizi azo zonke iintlobo, baya kuba nomgwebo ongqongqo nesohlwayo njengoko ubukumkani babo butshatyalaliswa ngokupheleleyo. Kwaye kuza kwenzeka kungekudala ukuba irhamncwa nomprofeti wobuxoki baphoswe bephila kwelo chibi livuthayo lomlilo nesulfure kwaye uSathana abotshwe iminyaka eliwaka ekuthuthunjisweni nasekoyikeni okukhulu kwelifa lakhe elizayo kumalangatye angunaphakade esohlwayo apho impethu yabo ingafiyo.

Kwaye inamba ibotshelelwe emhlabeni ukuze ingaphindi ingene emazulwini ngonaphakade kodwa igcinwe phantsi kwesibhakabhaka ibotshelelwe emhlabeni ukuze igwetywe njengoko yayinqwenela, kwaye inamba inomsindo omkhulu ekoyisweni kwayo, kuba yoyiswe ngokupheleleyo ngezandla zikaYeshua HaMashiach oyimbewu ethenjisiweyo kanina wabo bonke abaphilayo, kuba ndatsho ukuthembisa uAdam noEva eluthandweni lwam ngabo. Ngoko ke uvunyelwe kuphela ukuzalisekisa iinjongo zam ezingunaphakade phambi kokuba athunyelwe ngonaphakade emalangatyeni, kwaye ndiya kubeka itywina, kwaye abayi kuza engqondweni okanye bakhankanywe kuba linjalo ikamva lenu lesithembiso apho nikhululweyo kubacinezeli benu ngonaphakade kwaye niya kuphila ngoxolo olungunaphakade nolugqibeleleyo ukuba nje nithe nagoba idolo kuMesiya wenu wokwenyani, uYeshua HaMashiach, kuba akukho ndlela yimbi!

Ngoko ke ngoku ndiza kuninika ukuqonda izinto eziza kwenzeka, kwaye ngokuqinisekileyo umgibe obekwe ngumthiyeli uza kutyhilwa, umgibe obekwe yinyoka efuna ukubambisa amahobe am axabisekileyo, abantu bam abakhethekileyo endizigcinele bona. Kwaye Ndim owabangela ukuba ihobe likaNowa libuye linegqabi lomnquma emlonyeni walo, kwaye ngendlela efanayo, Ndim othumele isivumelwano sam soxolo esingunaphakade ebusweni bukaYesu Kristu, uMesiya wenu ovusiweyo, Sirayeli!

Ngoko ke, qondani amaxesha eniphila kuwo. ISinagoge kaSathana ihamba nemiqondiso emininzi yobuxoki nemimangaliso, kwaye ngobuqhetseba bayalwa. Benza izithethe zabo ekuhleni, zigqunywe kancinci njengokonwabisa neendaba neentlanganiso zezopolitiko. Bayigqibile inkqubo yabo yokukhetha, apho uEsawu ngoku akhethe imvana yakhe phakathi kweSinagoge kaSathana ngenkohliso yabo enyelisayo abafuna ukubambisa ngayo. Kodwa irhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni asiyiyo imvana kaThixo abayigxekayo ngobuyatha nangokunyelisa, kodwa ngumprofeti wabo wobuxoki abazikhethele yena ngoku oneempondo ezimbini njengemvana, kodwa othetha njengenamba.

Kwaye ngoko ndenza uNyana wam, uYesu Kristu Olilungisa, atyhile kwi-Ukhetho Lusezandleni Zakho (funda ukuze ufunde) xa uBharabhas wafumana inxeba entloko ngokungathi laliya ekufeni kwaye eso siganeko sasiineentsingiselo ezininzi angazange azityhile ngelo xesha, kodwa kwakukule ntlanganiso, isithethe sakhe sokukhetha apho bathi, uYahweh ngokwakhe wamgqobhoza umngxuma endlebeni yakhe emenza umkhonzi wam wobuthixo. Ngoko ke, ndifunga ngegama lam, mna YHVH Ndinguye Omkhulu, ukuba andimenzanga umkhonzi wam, kwaye andinikanga namnye uMesiya ngaphandle koNyana wam okuphela kwamzeleyo, uYeshua HaMashiach, oyena mvana kaThixo ndiya kuze ndimthumele. Ngoko ke umzamo wakhe wokubulawa wasetyenziswa njengomnye wemiqondiso emininzi yobuxoki nemimangaliso apho lo mzenzisi enza ngathi. Kwaye lo mzenzisi akayiyo indalo yam okanye umhlambi wam kwaye ulutshaba lwam.

Noko ke, abaninzi kwiBhabhiloni Efihlakeleyo ababenza ngathi uYesu Kristu uyiNkosi yabo ngoku bayishiyile iNkosi yabo enokubakho ngenxa kaBharabhas, othetha njengenamba kodwa eyimvana eneempondo ezimbini. Kanti abaninzi kwabo benze uBharabhas iNkosi yabo baya kushiyelwa kumlilo wocikido kuba usindiso lwabo aluzange lugqitywe ngokupheleleyo kuba abazanga kuKristu ngokupheleleyo ngeentliziyo ezizaliswe lukholo neziguqukileyo kodwa ngeenkanuko eziveliswa yivangeli yempumelelo. Kuba iSinagoge kaSathana yonakalise izibhalo zabo ezingcwele ngemvume yabo kwaye ngoku bangamaZiyonisti. Kanti abaninzi kwaba miphefumlo, baya kunikwa iwonga lokufela ukholo ukuze basindiswe ukuba bakhetha ukwamkela usindiso ngokubiza igama leNkosi yabo uYesu Kristu kude kube sekupheleni, kwaye ndiya kubamkela ngonaphakade kwaye iintlungu zabo aziyi kuthelekiswa nozuko oluza kutyhilwa kubo.

Ngoko, lo moshiach wobuxoki uyahamba ashumayele ethetha njengenamba kwaye uyazenzisa athi izinto ezininzi ezinkulu nezinyelisayo nangaphambi kokuba aphakame ngokupheleleyo, kwaye uya kuthi uyaphakama. Qondani bantu bam ukuba lo mprofeti wobuxoki okwalirhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni elikhonza iSinagoge kaSathana ngoku ukhethwe njengemvana yabo. Kwaye kuya kwenzeka ukuba intloko yakhe ibonakale ngathi yenzakele yafa, kodwa inxeba layo elibulalayo laphola, kwaye umhlaba wonke uya kumangaliswa uze ulandele irhamncwa. Kwaye ngoko lo mkhohlisi uya kuphakama.

Ekuvukeni kwakhe uya kuthi uzincamile njengemvana kaThixo ezisa uxolo kwaSirayeli njengehobe labo elibaphathela igqabi lomnquma! Kwaye ayingoSirayeli kuphela, kodwa nomhlaba ngokwawo kwaye kuya kubonakala ngathi ngoku ubuyile ukuzisa uxolo, kodwa iindlela zoxolo akazazi kwaye ekugwetyweni kweBhabheli eyiMfihlo kuya kulandela imfazwe epheleleyo. Kuba umoya wobuxoki wentuthuzelo ovela kwinamba uhleli emhlabeni, kwaye ndithumele nenkohliso enamandla ukuze bonke abo bayoliswa kukungabikho kobulungisa bakholwe bubuxoki ukuze bonke abo bayoliswa kukungabikho kobulungisa bagwetywe. Kuba xa besithi uxolo nokhuseleko, yosuka ifike intshabalalo ngequbuliso kwaye abayi kusinda.

Kwaye abaninzi eBhabheli eyiMfihlo, nkqu nabo bakholwa bubuxoki bakhe baze balandele amazwi akhe alungileyo neentetho zakhe ezimnandi, bayathinjwa baze babanjiswe luxolo lobuxoki apho bacinga ukuba ubomi babo obumnandi nobonayo buya kuqhubeka apho namhlanje kunjengayizolo kwaye ingomso liya kufana nanamhlanje kwaye abaninzi baya kukhala, babhomboloze, balile besithi ithemba labo ngoku lithathiwe kubo, kwaye baya kulwa nabantu bakowabo kude kube sekufeni kwaye ngalo lonke elo xesha uhlaselo lwabo olupheleleyo lokuqhusheka luya kukhululwa ngokupheleleyo, kanye njengokuba umthinteli wam esusiwe emhlabeni kwaye iBhabheli eyiMfihlo iya kuwela kwisabelo esiphindwe kabini somgwebo ngeyure enye njengoko kusiwa iintlekele ezininzi.

Kwaye iNdlu yesikhungu kaSathana iya kuba nemfazwe yayo abaye babulala ngayo abamsulwa ngenkohliso yabo ukuze baphembelele kwaye abo banqula iityhubhu zabo ezimnyama baya kuzidinisa ekubulaleni, nkqu nokubulala okubangelwa ngabom ngenkohliso enkulu kwaye baya kuvuka eluthuthwini. Kwaye abo bawayo baya kuzityhila ngokupheleleyo benika uluntu uncedo olubonakala lunobubele kodwa iindlela zabo nobuchwepheshe babo bujolise ekubambiseni bonke abanako kwilifa labo emalangatyeni esohlwayo, abangasoze basinde kulo kwaye apho baya kwamkela ngokuphindwe kaninzi ngaso SONKE isono abasenzileyo kwaye ubulungisa buya kwenziwa. Kodwa nguwuphi umthiyeli obeka umgibe emehlweni entaka?

Ngoko ke ndikunika ukuba uqonde, ukuba umgwebo weBhabheli eyiMfihlo ulungisiwe kwaye bakulungele. Kodwa kuqala le mvana yobuxoki ineemphondo ezimbini iya kuzenza ilungile kanti ingumalusi onexhwele, enye yeemphondo ezimbini zokulawula, nkqu nophondo oluncinane lukaDaniyeli. Kwaye uya kubonakala ngathi uzamile ukusindisa iBhabheli eyiMfihlo ngokuhlanjululwa kobuqhetseba bayo kodwa eneneni ungumkhohlisi omkhulu owabambisa abaninzi aza kubahlanjululwa ngoku aze abatyhole ngobubi bakhe awanyanzela ngabo abaninzi ukuba bathobele. Kwaye ngoku konke okungahambelani neenjongo zabo, okanye onokungqina ngokwakhe ngobubi babo kuya kususwa ngezandla zalo malusi unexhwele kwaye batyholwe ngezono abasathatha inxaxheba kuzo nabakhokelela abanye kuzo, kwaye abayi kusinda.

Kwaye, qonda kwakhona ukuba abaninzi abayinxalenye yolwaphulo-mthetho lwabo nabangumqukumbelo njengaba balusi banexhwele baya kungena kwiindawo zabo zokuzimela baze bazifihle entliziyweni yomhlaba kwingqumbo ezayo kwaye baya kutshabalalisa bonke ubungqina bobomi ababebuphila eBhabheli eyiMfihlo ngenkohliso enkulu, kwaye abayi kuyisinda ingqumbo ezayo! Ukanti, kunjalo ukuba izono zabo ziya kutyhilwa ngohanahaniso ngulo mprofeti wobuxoki kwaye uya kubonakala elungile kwaye ethe tye, nkqu nendoda ethunywe nguThixo ukusindisa uSirayeli kwaye ke umgibe ukulungele ukugqabhuka. Kanti ke, zonke izenzo zabo ndizibhalile ezincwadini emazulwini, kwaye mna ngokwam ndikhumbula yonke inkcukacha kwaye ndiyazi lonke ulwaphulo-mthetho lwabo kwaye ndiya kubatyhila kwaye baya kuhlawula ngonaphakade ngenxa yezono zabo ezikhohlakeleyo.

Kwaye ke irhamncwa eliphuma elwandle liyazilungiselela ukuvuka njengoMahdi wabo kwaye irhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni liyazilungiselela ukuvuka njengemvana yobuxoki kuba yeyiphi imvana eneempondo ezimbini kwaye ithetha njengenamba. Ukanti uEsawu ulungiselele ukumamkela kuye kwilizwe afuna ukuliba kwaye baya kuncothulwa ngokupheleleyo ngesandla esinamandla sikaYeshuwa HaMashiach endimxhobisileyo, ndide ndabelane ngetrone yam noNyana wam oyintanda kwaye uya kuyityumza ngokupheleleyo intloko yenyoka kwaye zonke iintshaba zakhe ziya kwenziwa isihlalo seenyawo zakhe kuba ngabo bawayo abala ukuguqa ngamadolo kumyalelo wam ekuvukeleni kwabo kwaye ngabo abahlasela indalo yam ngobuqhetseba nenkohlakalo kwaye baya kuhlawula ngonaphakade.

Kwaye ke ndifunga ngegama lam kwakhona ukuba, mna YHVH uMkhulu Ndinguye, ndiyafunga ukuba okuphela koMesiya endakha ndamthumela kwaSirayeli kwaye ngokwenene endiya kumthumela kuphela koNyana wam ozelweyo, uYeshuwa HaMashiach owaliwa ngooyihlo nani nimkhaba ngenxa yobuxoki babo enabudla ilifa kwiintliziyo zabo ezilukhuni ezadluliselwa kwiTalmud yenu enyelisayo. Kwaye ke ndiza kuni eluthandweni lwam ngani O' Sirayeli ukuze ndinityhilele ngokupheleleyo umgibe eniwubekelwe nguEsawu osafuna intshabalalo kaYakobi.

Irhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni linithakathile njengomprofeti wenu wobuxoki kwaye liya kuvuka lize kuni njengemvana yenu yedini eyazincamela uSirayeli kwaye iya kuzenza uMoshiach wenu wokwenene. Kanti eneneni ungcoliswe nguEsawu kwaye akukho Moshiach uya kuze aphume kwaDan. Ewe, uzele intshabalalo kaYakobi kodwa umgibe awubekayo uya kubambisa abo bamlandela ngokuzithandela kwaye ke baya kuthathwa ngonaphakade bubuxoki bakhe kwaye baya kwamkela uphawu lwakhe aya kufuna ukulunyanzela ehlabathini ukuze libe likhoboka kwaye litshabalale.

Kwaye ke ndithi kuni, O' Sirayeli, guqani ngamadolo nithobele uMesiya wenu wokwenene owafika kuni njengemvana kaThixo, owaliwa ngooyihlo ngenxa kaEsawu. Guqani ngamadolo kwiNkosi yenu yeeNkosi noKumkani wooKumkani kwaye namkele usindiso lwam ukuze ndikwazi ukunihlangula kwisiqalekiso somthetho kuba akukho mntu ulilungisa oza kum ngoMoses, ngaphandle komntu uKristu Yesu endimguqule ngonaphakade, kwaye ngokwenyama akasaziwa. Kuba, ndimzukisile ngendlela efanayo noFaro amzukisa ngayo uYosefu waza wabelana ngetrone yakhe, ngaphandle kokuba etroneni wayemkhulu kunoYosefu waza waligcina igunya lakhe.

Kuba:

NDINGUYE okhoyo kususela kwaphakade kude kube phakade kwaye akukho mkhulu kunoMkhulu Ndinguye!

NDINGUYE othumele la Mazwi kubantu bam abathandekayo ukuze ndize kuni ndisuse ubumfama benu obuyinxalenye xa iintliziyo zenu zijikela ngokupheleleyo kuMesiya wenu! Nkqu noMesiya wenu wokwenene, uKristu Yesu iNkosi evusiweyo nezukisiweyo!

Ngoko ke ndiyanibongoza, O' Sirayeli, ukuba nithobele ilizwi likaYHVH nkqu noElohim wenu owayengumalusi kooyihlo ngesandla somkhonzi wam uMoses endamhlangulayo xa wayerholwa emanzini omnayile esiya kukhuseleko nakulungiselelo lobukumkani endlwini kaFaro awayoyisayo ngokuthobela uYHVH!

NDINGUYE, nkqu noYHVH uMkhulu Ndinguye, ofunga ngegama lam kwaye ndiqinisekisa ngokupheleleyo kwinyaniso engunaphakade ukuba uYeshuwa HaMashiach wawuzalisekisa umthetho kaMoses njengePasika egqibeleleyo yexesha lonke endayilungiselela ngokugqibeleleyo ngesandla sam, ngoko akusekho mfuneko yedini.

NDINGUYHVH, uMkhulu Ndinguye, kwaye ndifunga ngegama lam ukuba ndim owavusa uYesu waseNazarete kwabafileyo emva kokuba ezinikele njengemvana kaThixo, nkqu nedini endalamkelayo ngonaphakade nangonaphakade ngenxa yokuhlangulwa kuka-Adam njengomntu omtsha kuKristu Yesu onguYeshuwa HaMashiach uMesiya wenu wokwenene!

Kwaye Ndinguye oqinisekisa ubunyaniso bala Mazwi endiwathumele kwiVangeli engunaPhakade etyhilwe ndim, noYeshuwa HaMashiach kumthunywa wethu onamandla olungiswe nguYeshuwa HaMashiach ngoluleko olukhulu nokuhlanjululwa ngeenjongo zam ezingunaphakade kwaye ngokuthembeka ubuyele kuYeshuwa HaMashiach kwaye ke ubuyiselwe!

Kwaye ke ndiyamema nina bantu bam, nkqu noSirayeli endimthanda ngothando olungunaphakade, ukuba njoyine uSirayeli kaThixo nize kuYahweh, nkqu noThixo ka-Abraham, noIsake, noYakobi ngendlela kaYeshuwa HaMashiach ukuze nihlanganiswe ngonaphakade kwaSirayeli kaThixo kwaye nibuyele kum ngendlela yoMesiya wenu wokwenene, nkqu uYeshuwa HaMashiach. Kwaye ndiya kunamkela kum ndinibuyisele kwilifa lenu nomvuzo wenu okwisandla esinamandla sikaKristu Yesu.
