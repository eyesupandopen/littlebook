  24. Bhabhiloni Efihlakeleyo! Khangela! Ungqongwe ngokupheleleyo ziinqanawa zeentshaba zakho, uvumele izigidi ngezigidi zeMikhosi eBomvu, nkqu nomkhosi werhamncwa ukuba ungene kumazwe akho.   26. Ngequbuliso amanzi aya kugubungela iziketi zakho eziphakanyiswe ngaphezu kwezinqe zakho, owu, henge elikhulu.

  28. Ngequbuliso umyalelo uya kunikwa kwaye izigidi ziya kuqhambuka kulo lonke ilizwe ukubulala wonke umntu ophilayo ongazifanelanga iinjongo zabo. Abayi kuba nanceba, abanako ukuzisola, abanakucengwa, abanalo uvelwano, kodwa bakhonza uSathana kwaye baya kuzalisekisa iinjongo abazinikiweyo ukuba bazizalisekise, kuba baya kuzalisekisa ingqumbo kaThixo kwiBhabhiloni Eyimfihlakalo kwaye asisayi kuphinda sivuke.
  29. Khangela! Ndikuxelela into phambi kokuba yenzeke. IBhabhiloni Efihlakeleyo, nkqu la Mazwe aManyeneyo awayekhe asebenza njengesibane sokukhanya ehlabathini, akwimixokelelwane nkqu agcinelwe umgwebo ovuthayo.
  30. Umgwebo nentshabalalo yayo iya kuza ngequbuliso kwaye ngeyure enye iya kutshiswa ngomlilo, ngeyure enye iya kukrazulwa, ngeyure enye iziketi zayo ziya kugutyungelwa ngamanzi nkqu avela empuma nkqu nasentshona.
  31. Imikhosi ebomvu yerhamncwa sele ingene emideni yayo kwaye sele ilungiselela ilindele imiyalelo, kwaye iya kubulala yonke indoda, umfazi, nomntwana ongakhonzi iinjongo zayo. Kuba irhamncwa nomprofeti wobuxoki abaza kutyhilwa sele besebenza kunye ekutshatyalalisweni kwebhabhiloni efihlakeleyo, kwaye sele bekwimida yayo.
  32. Xa uMtshakazi wam ethathwa asiwe emazulwini kwaye umthinteli esusiwe intshabalalo yesiquphe iya kuwa ngequbuliso kuwo wonke umhlaba kwaye zonke iintlanga ezazikhe zazenza ngathi zingamaKristu ziya kuyeka ukubakho ngendlela ezikhoyo ngoku, kodwa kuya kubakho abantu abaseleyo aboyisiweyo nabacuthiweyo.
  33. IBhabhiloni Eyimfihlakalo iya kuwa kuqala kwaye ngequbuliso ngaxeshanye njengoko uhlaselo lwabo olumangalisayo luqala, kodwa ayisayi kuphinda ivuke kwakhona, kuba uYahweh ubhengeze isabelo esiphindwe kabini somgwebo kuyo.
  34. Kwaye kuya kusala inkqantosi, nangona intsalela iya kusindiswa, kuba nabani na obiza egameni leNkosi kwaye engabathandi ubomi babo kude kube sekupheleni uya kusindiswa. Ngoko qondani amaxesha eniphila kuwo kwaye niqonde ucwangciso oluzayo lweziganeko kwaye niqonde ukuba akukho nto inokutshintsha ezi ziganeko, kuba oko uYahweh akubhengeze akukho mntu unokukutshintsha!
  35. Iimpondo ezilishumi zilungiselele uhlaselo lwazo olumangalisayo kwaye uhlaselo lwazo luyasondela kwaye lusemnyango wakho. Kuba nkqu namajelo akho eendaba afuna ukukuthomalalisa ulale kwaye abaninzi bahleli ebumnyameni bengayiboni inyani besithi: Ndihleli njengoKumkanikazi kwaye andinguye umhlolokazi kwaye andiyi kubona zila.
  36. Ngenxa yoko izibetho zayo ziya kuza ngamini-nye, ukufa, nosizi, nendlala, kwaye iya kutshiswa ngomlilo: kuba uYahweh oyigwebayo, nguThixo uSomandla kwaye akukho mkhulu kwaye akukho nto inokumisa isandla esinamandla sika-El Shaddai!
  37. Kwaye ngoko ilanga liya kuphuma kungekudala ngosuku lwentlekele ongayilindelanga, kwaye olo suku lusondele kunokuba ucinga. Kwaye olo suku luya kuzisa intshabalalo epheleleyo kwiBhabhiloni Eyimfihlakalo, kuba inesabelo esiphindwe kabini somgwebo esibhengeziswe kuyo.
  38. Yivani amazwi am kuba ndithetha ngokungathi sele yenzekile kuba akukho nto iya kutshintsha, icothise, ithintele, okanye imise imigwebo kaYahweh ekuweni emhlabeni.
  39. Ngeyure enye iya kutshiswa ngomlilo, ezinye zezixeko zayo ziya kutsha, ezinye zezixeko zayo ziya kukhukuliswa ngogonyamelo, iinqaba zayo ziya kuwa zaphuke zibe ziziqwenga ezininzi, iibhulorho zayo ziya kuwa, kwaye kuya kubakho ukushukuma okuninzi.
  40. Umhlaba uya kukrazulwa ngogonyamelo kwaye umhlaba omninzi uya kugutyungelwa ngamanzi anogonyamelo.
  41. Iziketi zayo ziya kugutyungelwa ukusuka empuma nasentshona ngamaza amakhulu kwaye phakathi kuqhube amaza nokushukuma okunogonyamelo.
  42. Koko kuseleyo kuya kuba yinkqantosi kwaye ayisayi kuphinda ivuke, kuba esi sizwe sezizwe ezininzi nezixeko ezininzi asisayi kuphinda sivuke kwaye kuya kuba kukhulu ukuwa kwaso!

Mna, Kristu Yesu, iNgonyama yeSizwe sakwaYuda, ndiwabhengeze la mazwi, kwaye ukulungiselela ukuzaliseka kwawo sele kuqhubeka kwaye kuya kuzaliseka ngequbuliso ngeyure enye.

Nincamele igazi labamsulwa kwaye amanyala abawenzileyo aya kunizingela anilandele kude kube seziphelweni zomhlaba kuba bebesoloko besitya abemi belizwe kwaye ukhula olubi olwenziwe ngezandla zabawileyo alunakuxolelwa ngenxa yezono zabo nolwaphulo-mthetho lwabo kuYahweh, kuba uluntu lwenziwe ngomfanekiso kaYahweh kwaye ukungcolisa ubomi benyama esegazini sisono esibi kwaye asiyi kujongelwa phantsi kwabo baye bala usindiso lwam ngamaxesha am obabalo nenceba. Khangelani! Ndithumela izigebenga ukuba zizalisekise ingqumbo yam!
  46. Imikhosi yenamba enkulu ebomvu ikungqongile wena Bhabheli eyimfihlo kwaye ingaphakathi kwemida yakho ngokupheleleyo. Kuba kwanabakhokeli bakho abangamaZayoni abangcatshiyo bakuthengisile ngesuntswana lesonka kwiintshaba zakho kananjalo ukuze baqhubele phambili amayelenqe abo kasathana.
  47. Kwaye u-El Shaddai uwavumele la maphaku-phaku ukuba aqhubeke, ekufaka ezintliziyweni zawo ukuba azalisekise iinjongo zakhe.
  48. Kwakhona, qonda ukuba iintshaba zeBhabheli eyimfihlo zinikwe ubutyebi bayo ngokupheleleyo, kwaye iBhabheli eyimfihlo ngoku liqokobhe eliphangiweyo elalingeyiyo, elingenalo ithuba lokuphila, kwaye soze siphinde sivuke esi sizwe sonayo nesenyanyekayo, isizwe esenziwa ngasese ngabawileyo esakhonza injongo engacetywanga yokubonelela ngekhusi kwabatshutshiswayo ababekhonza uKrestu Yesu (kuba kwanazo iintshaba zikaYehova ziyalithobela ilizwi lakhe) nangenjongo yokuba irhamncwa eliphuma elwandle nerhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni liphume lize likhethelwe iinjongo zabo ngabawileyo ngamaxesha abo.
  49. Unamandla u-El Shaddai okugwebayo, wenahenyukazi elibomvu elihleli ngokuzidla liyoliswa nangona uhlutyiwe lonke uzuko, uhlutyiwe zonke izinto ezimnandi, uhlutyiwe kwanamakhaya akho kuba ndinikele amakhaya akho nemihlaba yakho ezandleni zeentshaba zakho. Kwanasezandleni zerhamncwa nomprofeti wobuxoki, kuba basebenzisana kunye ukuze bakutshabalalise ngokupheleleyo.
  50. Yiva amazwi am uqonde okwenzekayo ngoku, kuba uhlaselo lwabo olothusayo luyaqhubeka.
  51. Ndithetha ngoku neBhabheli eyimfihlo nangona isisithulu, isimumu, kwaye iyimfama kwaye ibotshelelwe ngokupheleleyo ngamatyathanga okugwetywa kwayo. Njengoko belulungiselela uhlaselo lwabo olothusayo iminyaka emininzi ngoku, kwaye bayiqinisekisile intshabalalo yeBhabheli eyimfihlo ngobuxhaphazi, nangezinye iindlela zenkohliso.
  52. Ekubeni izinikele ekukhonzeni iintshaba zayo zanaphakade, ngoku ziya kuzisa intshabalalo yayo yanaphakade kuba soze iphinde ivuke iBhabheli eyimfihlo.
  53. Ngoku ndiya kukunikela ngokupheleleyo ezandleni zabo kwaye uYehova ude wavakalisa isahlulo esiphindwe kabini sokugwetywa kuwe Bhabheli eyimfihlo. Kwaye abaninzi babantwana bakho baya kuthunyelwa kwiinkampu zabo zokusebenza nezokufundiswa ngokutsha kwaye abaninzi baya kusiwa kumazwe angaphandle kuba inamba ivunyelwe ukuba ibange imihlaba nabantu abandigatshileyo mna, Yeshua HaMashiach, bekhetha uBharabhas umesiya wabo wobuxoki.
  54. Akusenamandla okanye izixhobo zokulwa imfazwe, kuba mna, kwanaye uKrestu Yesu, ndiwuhluthile umkhosi wakho ndawunikela ezandleni zeentshaba zakho. Izixeko zakho ezazikade zizikhulu ngoku zihleli kumanxuwa olwaphulo-mthetho nobuhlwempu, enizidla ngako kakhulu nina nabazisi-zolonwabo benu. Ama-Edom kudala ayisusa imizi-mveliso yawo emihlabeni yakho, kwaye awunalo igunya lokuvelisa owawukade unalo kwanazo nezinto ezisisiseko zokuvelisa oko ukudingayo ukuze ulwe iimfazwe zakho ezingapheliyo.
  55. Yabona! Ihenyukazi elikhulu, kwanayo iBhabheli eyimfihlo isizwe samazwe amaninzi, nguloo mfazi waboniswa kuYohane uMtyhileli, kwanalowo mfazi unxilileyo ligazi labaNgcwele, neligazi labafeli-nkolo bam kwaye ukugwetywa kwakho kuphezu kwakho.
  56. Bhabheli eyimfihlo, unikele ngesizukulwana sonke sabantwana abangamadoda akwiminyaka yobudala yomkhosi ababenokukukhusela kodwa ngoku bahlala ezitalatweni zezixeko zakho ezenyanyekayo kwaye bangamakhoboka angenathemba ala makhubalo kwaye baye baba zizombi zabahambi abafileyo njengoko ezi ziyobisi zibola inyama yabo besaphila.
  57. Ngoku kuza ukugwetywa kwakho ngokupheleleyo Bhabheli eyimfihlo kwaye ungumboniso wokuba wonke umntu abone, nangona ubuqhwabela izandla njengoko iinkokeli zakho zizisa intlekele kunxweme lwezizwe ezingenakubalwa kwiimfazwe zazo ezingenalungelo owawuzonwabisa ngazo njengoko ubukele kumabonakude.
  58. Wahlulahlulwe phakathi kweentshaba zakho kwaye sele ziwonwabela amaxhoba emfazwe kwanangaphambi kokuba imijukujelwa iwe. Kuba ukoyiswa kweBhabheli eyimfihlo kuqinisekisiwe kwaye kunikwe nguYehova ngokwakhe kwaye akukho nto iya kuyinqanda intshabalalo yayo epheleleyo kwaye soze iphinde ivuke.
  59. Izixeko zayo ezazikade zizikhulu ezingumzekelo wokusebenza nzima nokuvelisa kunye nentabalala ka-El Shaddai ngoku zihleli kumanxuwa nenzala yayo ingamakhoboka eziyobisi kwaye ihlala kubumdaka ezitalatweni zayo ezenyanyekayo.
  60. Yenziwe yaze njengoko ukhuselo lwayo lususelwa kude ngakumbi kwaye ubutyebi namandla ayo anikelwa ezandleni zeentshaba zayo ezingama-Edom ezenza ngathi zingabahlobo.
  61. Ihenyukazi elikhulu elalwa imfazwe nazo zonke izizwe elathunyelwa ukuba lizoyise ngoku libotshelelwe, kwaye alinakuzikhusela kwaye lilungele ukoyiswa ngokupheleleyo.
  62. Kwaye yonke inyama iya kudliwa lusizi kwaye bonke abathinjwayo baya kubulawa kwaye abo bakwaziyo ukukhonza iinjongo zerhamncwa baya kusiwa ebukhobokeni kwaye abanye baya kubulawa, nangona intsalela iya kusinda.
  63. Iyavutha kwaye sele kukho imililo emininzi kwimida yayo nasemihlabeni yabamelwane bayo abasemantla ababelana ngezibetho zayo. Kanti ke ezi zizintlungu zokuzala kuphela kwaye azikokugwetywa kokugqibela kodwa sisilumkiso kubo bonke ngentshabalalo yabo ezayo.
  64. Yiva amazwi am uqonde ilizwi lam kuba umthinteli kuphela kwento egcina iinkanuko ezitshabalalisayo zabawileyo zithintelwe de lowo uthintelayo angabe esathintela aze asuswe phakathi.
  65. Kwaye ngoko indlala iya kuhla phezu kwemihlaba, kuba iBhabheli eyimfihlo ibangele indlala kwimihlaba emininzi kwaye ngoku indlala iya kuba sisidlo sayo sangokuhlwa, kuba inike ngolo hlobo kwaye ngoku iya kwamkela ngokuphindwe kabini.
  66. Amanzi aya kuba yinto efunwayo, kwaye amanzi amaninzi aya kwenziwa abe krakra yintshabalalo nemililo yemfazwe nangezibetho zabangangcwatywanga abaya kungcolisa umhlaba.
  67. Kwaye ngoko iBhabheli eyimfihlo iya kwahlulwa ngokupheleleyo phakathi kwaye amanzi aya kukhukulisa apho kwakukade kukho umhlaba owomileyo ochumileyo, kwanemihlaba esembindini iya kukhukuliswa ukuya kutsho embindini.
  68. Kwaye ngoko inceba nobabalo lukaThixo ziya kususwa ngokupheleleyo emhlabeni xa umthinteli esuswa ngequbuliso kwaye intshabalalo iya kutshata nentshabalalo, kuba la ngamaxesha entshabalalo apho ubukumkani bukaSathana buya kugwetywa ngokupheleleyo butshatyalaliswe ngokupheleleyo, kodwa kuqala baya kukhonza iinjongo zikaYehova ekudleni ngokupheleleyo iBhabheli eyimfihlo kwaye ngoko iya kutshiswa ngokupheleleyo idliwe yimililo emininzi evuthayo eya kuyigubungela iqale ukuhlanjululwa komhlaba.
  69. Kwaye ngoko esi sizwe sasikade sizikhulu samazwe nezixeko ezininzi siya kuwa ngequbuliso, kwaye ingelosi yentshabalalo okwangoku ijikeleza phezu kwesona sixeko sayo sikhulu nesona sikhulu kumanzi asempuma kwaye ikratshi novuyo lwayo nomona wehlabathi ziya kutshatyalaliswa ngokupheleleyo zingaphinde zivuke.
  70. Kwaye ngoko intshabalalo yayo iya kwabelwana ngayo nabo bonke abazimanye nayo, kuba lilo elo henyukazi likhulu elihleli phezu kwamanzi amaninzi angabantu, nezihlwele, nezizwe, neelwimi.
  71. Ngelo xesha umthinteli asuswayo, baya kwenza uhlaselo lwabo oluyimfihlo abalucwangcisileyo balulungelelanisa iminyaka emininzi ngoku, kuba kunikwe ezandleni zabo ukuba bakufeze, kwaye iBhabheli eyimfihlo iya kutshatyalaliswa ngokupheleleyo ngeyure enye.
  72. Kwaye iimpondo ezilishumi ziya kulitshabalalisa ihenyukazi elikhulu kuba ziyalithiya ihenyukazi, kwaye ziya kulenza kube senkangala zilihambise ze, zidle inyama yalo, zilitshise ngomlilo.
  73. Kwaye ukuzamazama okukhulu kuya kufika emhlabeni, kwaye liya kwahlulwa, kwaye amanzi aya kugubungela unxweme lwalo, kwaye amanzi aya kugubungela intliziyo yalo, kwaye liya kwahlulwa kubini.
  74. Yaza ingelosi enamandla yaphakamisa ilitye elingangetye elikhulu lokusila, yaliphosa elwandle, isithi, Ngalo olo gonyamelo siya kuphoswa eso sixeko sikhulu iBhabheli, singabi safunyanwa.
  75. Kwaye kuya kuba njalo ukuba esi sizwe sasikade sizikhulu, endihlangule abaninzi kuso, ngoku siya kuthathwa sigwetywe, kwananjengoko iBandla lam noMtshakazi wam bethathelwa emazulwini, apho ndibalungiselele indawo yokhuseleko nekhusi apho baya kwamkela ilifa labo.   76. Inene, inene, zonke izixeko zayo ezonayo nezenyanyekayo ziya kuwa, neenqaba zayo ziya kuwa, kwaye imililo emikhulu nentshabalalo ziya kwenza iindlela zayo zibe nzima ukuhamba.
  77. Ke kaloku imikhosi ebomvu, kwanemikhosi yerhamncwa eliyingenisileyo ukuze itshatyalaliswe, iya kukhululwa phezu koko kuseleyo, kwaye iya kudla inyama yayo.
  78. Abo baseleyo bathengiswa ebukhobokeni ngobuxhaphazi kuba iinkokeli zeBhabheli eyimfihlo zingcatshile abemi bayo kwaye zibanikele njengesibambiso, kwaye intlawulo ngoku ifunekayo.
  79. Kwaye ngoko abelusi abanxilayo bayinikele iBhabheli eyimfihlo ezandleni zeentshaba zayo, kuba bathunyelwe ngolo hlobo. Balenze laze ihenyukazi, kuba banikwe ukuba bakwenze oko, kuba kwanazo iintshaba zikaYehova ziyalithobela ilizwi lakhe. Qonda ukuba ngabom bashiye amandla ayo omkhosi kumazwe angaphandle kwaye banike iimfihlo zayo kwiimpondo ezilishumi ukuze zilawule ngamandla.
