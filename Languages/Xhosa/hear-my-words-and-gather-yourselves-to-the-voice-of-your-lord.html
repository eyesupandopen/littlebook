Yivani Amazwi Am Kwaye Nizihlanganisele Kwilizwi LeNkosi Yenu!

Yivani amazwi am kwaye nizihlanganisele kwilizwi leNkosi yenu, uKumkani, uMesiya, noMbingeleli Omkhulu, uYeshua HaMashiach. Kutheni nithandabuza amazwi am nocwangciso lweziganeko zawo, kwanazo iinkcukacha zerhema zamazwi am? Kuba ndithethe ngokucacileyo malunga nemfihlelo yeBhabhiloni nesiphelo sayo esisondelayo:

IsiTyhilelo 17

[17:13] Aba banangqondo-nye, yaye baya kunikela amandla negunya labo kwirhamncwa.

[17:16] Kwaye iimpondo ezilishumi owazibonayo phezu kwerhamncwa, ezi ziya kulithiya ihenyukazi, zilenze kube senkangala, zilihambise ze, zidle inyama yalo, zilitshise ngomlilo.

[17:17] Kuba uThixo ubeke ezintliziyweni zabo ukuba benze ukuthanda kwakhe, bavumelane, banikele ubukumkani babo kwirhamncwa, ade azaliseke amazwi kaThixo.

Ukanti uninzi lwenu alunaqiqo lwezibhalo olufanelekileyo kwaye uninzi lwenu lusathembele kumntu wemvelo. Ninokholo kwizakhono zomntu wemvelo, emandleni omntu wemvelo, kwizicombululo zomntu wemvelo, nakubuxoki bomntu wemvelo, anifundanga na ukuba bonke abantu ngamaxoki bantwana bam? Ndiphulaphuleni niqonde: Akukho sicombululo sinikwa ngumntu wemvelo esingakhokeleliyo kwinamba, kwaye inamba iya kulawula umhlaba okwexeshana elingephi xa umthinteli esusiwe, kwaye nangoku inguthixo walo mhlaba, ayibhalwanga ngokucacileyo na le nto esibhalweni?

Kutheni nisawathandabuza amazwi am, zintanda zam nabo ndibanqwenelayo? Kholwani ngamazwi am kwaye niqonde ukuba umntu wemvelo akanamandla, umntu wemvelo akanazakhono ngokwakhe zokulwa uSathana, izicombululo zomntu wemvelo ziphelela ebuzeni kwaye zikhonza iinjongo zikaSathana, kwaye akukho nyaniso kumntu wemvelo nakwiinkokeli zenu zehlabathi. Kuba umntu wemvelo wonakaliswa kwasekuqaleni eMyezweni wase-Eden yinyoka kwaye uyalahleka kwasesizalweni ezelwe efile kwizigqitho nasezonweni, kwaye ngenxa yenjongo enjalo mna, uYesu waseNazarete, ndabonakaliswa ukuze ndichithe imisebenzi kamtyholi kwabo bamkela isithembiso sam soxolo nosindiso.

Yabonani! Zonke iinkokeli zehlabathi zisebenzisana ngasese, kwaye zonke zikhonza uSathana. Aninazo iinkokeli ezinyuliweyo, kodwa ninikwa ukhetho lobuxoki kuba anizange nibakhethe abo nicinga ukuba niyabanyula ngokuvota, kodwa banikezelwa kuni ukuze nivote ukuze bafumane imvume yenu kwaye imvume yenu sisono kuba aninakukhetha ngokuzithandela ububi obuncinci nime nam ngaxeshanye, oko kukulukuhla. Guqukani! Phumani phakathi kwabo nahlukane! Mna, iNkosi uYesu Krestu ngolwimi lwesiNgesi noYeshua HaMashiach ngesiHebhere, andinikezelanga sicombululo sezopolitiko kwaye akukho nkokeli yehlabathi iya kuvela kuba andinikezelanga namnye, kwaye abo bakholelwa ngenye indlela bakhohliswe kabuhlungu. Iinkokeli zehlabathi zonke zisebenzisana ngasese, kwaye aziyi kwaphula umthetho kuba iinkokeli zenu zonke zikhonza uSathana mhlawumbi ngasese, okanye ekuhleni. Nisawanqwenela na amazwi amnandi neentetho ezimnandi? Anixokiselwanga ngokwaneleyo na, niphila ubomi benkohliso kasathana kwasekuzalweni? Kutheni nisafuna iinkokeli zehlabathi endaweni yam, uKrestu Yesu? Kuba ndiyiNkosi yenu, uKumkani, uMesiya, noMbingeleli Omkhulu uYahweh amnikezeleyo, kwaye ndiwuthengile umhlaba kwanobomi babakhululweyo ngegazi lam elingenasono elihlawulelayo. Kuba njengoMbingeleli wenu Omkhulu, ndazinikela njengemvana yenu yePasika engenasono negqibeleleyo. Kwaye ndiyiNkosi yeeNkosi noKumkani wooKumkani, kwaye ndiza njengeNgonyama yesizwe sakwaYuda kwaye ndiya kulawula kwitrone kaDavide emhlabeni kwaye iinjongo zikaYahweh ezingunaphakade zosindiso nentlawulelo ziya kuzalisekiswa kum xa iNgonyama ilele nemvana kuBukumkani bam obuzayo nolawulo olungenasiphelo.

Yabonani! Ndinixelela into ingekenzeki. IBhabhiloni eyimfihlo, ezi United States ezakha zakhonza njengesibane sokukhanya ehlabathini, ikhonkxiwe kwaye igcinelwe umgwebo womlilo. Umgwebo nentshabalalo yayo iya kufika ngequbuliso kwaye ngeyure enye iya kutshiswa ngomlilo, ngeyure enye iya kukrazulwa, ngeyure enye iziketi zayo ziya kugutyungelwa ngamanzi ukusuka empuma kwanasentshona. Imikhosi ebomvu yerhamncwa sele ingenele imida yayo kwaye sele imi ilindele imiyalelo, kwaye iya kubulala yonke indoda, umfazi, nomntwana ongakhonzi iinjongo zayo. Kuba irhamncwa nomprofeti wobuxoki abaza kutyhilwa sele besebenza kunye ekutshatyalalisweni kwebhabhiloni eyimfihlo, kwaye sele bephakathi kwemida yayo.

Bobabini babezinkokeli zangaphambili zeBhabhiloni eyimfihlo. Omnye ubonakala ngathi akayiyo kwaye ulawula isidenge esidala esiyiyo, kwaye omnye ubonakala etshutshiswa sisidenge esidala, kodwa abo belizwe bambhengeze njengoKoreshi wabo kwaye bamfuna njengokumkani wabo, uthi ungokhethiweyo kuba inamba imkhethile ukuba akhohlise abo belizwe lesithembiso, kwaye uya kwakha itempile yabo, naxa kungekho mfuneko yento enjalo kuba mna Yeshua HaMashiach ndiyiPasika egqibeleleyo kwaye nina niyitempile kaThixo ophilayo, itempile eyenziwe ngaphandle kwezandla zabantu kodwa ngamandla kaYahweh uzuko lwakhe kwaye uya kunizukisa kum Krestu Yesu iNkosi yenu!

Yabonani! La marhamncwa mabi nomfelandawonye wawo owakhekayo weempondo ezilishumi sele elungelelanise intshabalalo yeBhabhiloni eyimfihlo. Kuba alungele kwihlabathi lonke, kwaye alungiselele kwaye amile kwihlabathi lonke ukuze kugqitywe ngokupheleleyo ihlabathi njengoko nilazi kwaye eluthuthwini kuya kuvela ucwangco lwawo olutsha, kwisiphithiphithi kuya kuvela ucwangco lwawo lobusathana kwaye uphawu luya kunyanzeliswa ukungena kuluntu lwawo kwaye abantu bakaThixo baya kuhlala entlango njengemihla kaNowa kwaye bambalwa abaya kusinda. Kuba irhamncwa nomprofeti wobuxoki baya kulwa neengcwele bazoyise. Aba ngabo baya kuphuma kwimbandezelo enkulu, kwaye bahlambe iingubo zabo, bazenza mhlophe egazini leMvana. Ngoko ke, baphambi kwetrone kaThixo, kwaye bamkhonza imini nobusuku eTempileni yakhe: kwaye lowo uhleli eTroneni uya kuhlala phakathi kwabo. Abayi kuphinda balambe, abayi kuphinda banxanwe, nelanga aliyi kubatshisa, nabuphi na ubushushu. Kuba iMvana, ephakathi kwetrone, iya kubondla, kwaye iya kubakhokelela kwimithombo ephilayo yamanzi: kwaye uThixo uya kosula zonke iinyembezi emehlweni abo.

Yabonani! Utshaba luyeza kwaye ucwangciso lweziganeko lulandelayo: Umthinteli uya kuthintela ndide ndihlanganise abathembekileyo eBandleni lam ukuze ndidibane nabo emafini ekubonakaleni kwam kwiBandla lam, uMtshakazi wam. Nangona uninzi lwenu lusiphika isibhalo esicacileyo ngesi siganeko, siya kwenzeka, kwaye siya kwaziwa kwihlabathi lonke. Akukho kuxwilwa kuyimfihlo, kuba lawo ngamazwi abaprofeti bobuxoki bamaZiyon, kwaye ukubonakala kwam kuya kuba kungenakuphikiswa kwihlabathi lonke. Baya kufuna ukugubungela ukumka kwenu ngemfazwe nohlaselo lobuxoki lwezidalwa zasemzini, kodwa akukho zidalwa zasemzini kuphela ngabawileyo abazenzisayo. Xa uMtshakazi wam ethathwa ezulwini kwaye umthinteli esusiwe intshabalalo ngequbuliso iya kuwa ngequbuliso kwihlabathi lonke kwaye zonke iintlanga ezakha zazenza ngathi zingamaKrestu ziya kuyeka ukubakho ngendlela ezikhoyo ngoku, kodwa kuya kubakho abantu abaseleyo aboyisiweyo nabacuthiweyo. Imfihlelo yeBhabhiloni iya kuwa kuqala kwaye ngequbuliso ngaxeshanye nokuqala kohlaselo lwabo olothusayo, kodwa ayisayi kuphinda ivuke kwakhona, kuba uYahweh umisele isabelo esiphindwe kabini somgwebo kuyo.

Kuba iMfihlelo yeBhabhiloni ikhokelele iintlanga kwisono esikhulu nenkohliso ngobugqwirha nobuthakathi bayo kwanokonwabisa kwayo okubizwa ngokuba yimisitho nangenxa yamasikizi ayo obugqwirha adluliselwe njengamayeza ekuyinyaniso ayityhefu ebulalayo. Kwaye ke isiphelo sayo siya kufika ngequbuliso kwaye ngaphandle konyango. Kwaye kuya kusala inkangala, nangona insali iya kusindiswa, kuba nabani na obiza egameni leNkosi kwaye engabathandi ubomi babo kude kube sekupheleni uya kusindiswa.

Ngoko qondani amaxesha eniphila kuwo kwaye niqonde ucwangciso oluzayo lweziganeko kwaye niqonde ukuba akukho nto inokutshintsha ezi ziganeko, kuba oko uYahweh akutshilo akukho mntu unokukutshintsha!

Yabonani! NdinguYeshua HaMashiach kwaye ndithumele la Mazwi ngomkhonzi wam, kwaye ngomkhonzi wam la Mazwi athunyelwe.

Ndiya kubonakala ngequbuliso, ukulungele na Mtshakazi wam?
