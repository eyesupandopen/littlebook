Ubungqina Obubini: Ibandla
Emisebenzini kaYohane uMbhaptizi
Matshi 28, 2024

Khangelani kum, iNkosi uYesu Krestu!

Bantwana bam, vulani amehlo enu nikhangele kum, iNkosi uYesu Krestu. Vukani ebuthongweni nibuyele kum; buyelani kum, kuba iingalo zam zolulelwe kuni, banyulwa bam. Kungekudala niza kuba nam, njengoko ilixa lifikelele kumzuzu wokugqibela namaxesha obabalo sele eza kuphela, yaye ndiza kuwazalisekisa ngokupheleleyo. Vukani ebuthongweni, yaye ndiza kwaphula amakhubalo ezipelingi zeli hlabathi emehlweni enu kanye. Lilani nenze isijwili ngenxa yezono zenu nokwala kwenu ukuza kum, nazi ukuba ndiyanibiza ngalo mzuzu wokugqibela. Ndiyanibizela emva kum, uthando lwenu lokuqala, nangona ningenise abanye ezintliziyweni zenu.

Babalekeni bonke, kuba bangabakhohlisi; ewe, baziingcuka ezambethe izikhumba zeegusha, yaye nilele ebuthongweni kwingalo yabo engendawo. Musani ukutyhafa, bantwana bam, kodwa vukani nilindele mna, njengoko imiqondiso yam sele isezulwini yaye ndiza njengesela ebusuku. Ndiza kufumana ntoni xa ndibuya neBandla lam? Ndiza kulufumana na ukholo? Owu, bandla laseLawodike, udikidiki, kwaye ngaba ubunokubanda okanye ushushu. Guqukelani kum kwakhona, yaye ndiza kunihlambulula ebuthongweni benu, yaye ndiza kuthomalalisa amanxeba eniwenzwe ngabangendawo. Andinqweneli kunikhafulela ngaphandle komlomo wam, ngoko yizani kwakhona kum, yaye ndiza kunamkela. Yazini ukuba uthando lwam ngani alufi yaye luhlala lomelele kwaye aluguquki.

Akukho namnye ngaphandle kwam onokunisindisa, akukho namnye ngaphandle kwam onokuniphilisa aze aninyange, akukho namnye ngaphandle kwam onokunikhusela aze aninike ubomi obungunaphakade, akukho namnye ngaphandle kwam onokunisindisa ezonweni zenu, kuba akukho gama limbi linikiweyo abantu abanokusindiswa ngalo. Ndilithemba lenu elingunaphakade elinidale ngokutsha ekuzalweni kwam okutsha endikuthululele phezu kwenu. Yazini ukuba ningabantwana bakaBawo, yaye ninelifa elikhulu ngenxa yokuthembeka kwenu. Ukanti, nangona ndiza kuyigweba imisebenzi yenu ukuze nifumane umvuzo okanye ilahleko, nisukile ekungabini nalungisa nangena kubomi obungunaphakade ngokholo lwenu kum nasebukhosini bam obungunaphakade. Yabonani! Ndinenze ookumkani nababingeleli phambi koBawo, kuba ndingumbingeleli omkhulu ngonaphakade ngokohlobo lukaMelkitsedeke.

Ngubani na lowo ungazixhobisa ngokuchasene nani? Kuba ukuchasana nabazalwana bam kukuchasana nam, yaye ndiza kubuyisela, itsho iNkosi enkulu engunaphakade yozuko evela phezulu! Zonke izinto ezulwini nasemhlabeni zinikelwe ezandleni zam, ngoko yazini ukuba ndinegunya eliphezulu, kwaye akukho mntu mkhulu kunam ngaphandle koBawo, ondiphakamisileyo ngaphezu kwako konke. Ngoko ngubani na lo nimele nimoyike? Ayithi na intshutshiso nembandezelo inenze nomelele ngakumbi kum? Akunjalo na ukuba xa amandla enu ephela, ndilapho kanye ukuze ndinomeleze ebuthathaka benu?

Ngoko yimani niqine nibe namandla kum! Kutheni niwathandabuza amazwi am? Kodwa endaweni yoko, yibani nokholo kum nithethe amazwi endininika wona, kuba la mazwi makhulu kwaye anamandla ukuba nithe nawathetha ngokholo. Thethani ngokuzithemba nangegunya, niyalele izinto ezibuthathaka egameni likaYesu Krestu, igama lam elinamandla ngaphezu kwako konke. Kungekudala niza kundibona ngamehlo enu, yaye ubukho benu ezulwini bunqwenelwa kakhulu ndim noBawo, ewe, nkqu nanguYeshuwa noYahweh. Niluncedo olukhulu lukaBawo, yaye anixabiseki. Kuba liphi na elinye igazi elingenasono elanikelwa ngenxa yezono zenu ngaphandle kwelam?

Owu, nina bangendawo abahleliyo bevuya, becinga ukuba ninoloyiso olukhulu. Ndiza kuziphazamisa izicwangciso zenu zobubi, yaye niza kukwazi ukufezekisa kuphela oko uBawo amisele ukuba nikwenze ngokuhambelana nokuqhubela phambili izicwangciso zakhe. Nibuthathaka, anantsingiselo kwaye noyisiwe ngokupheleleyo. Owu, mntu wemvelo, kutheni usakhetha iindlela ezingendawo zabangendawo abanilahlekisa ngobubi? Ngaba iziyolo zesono zifanele ukwahlukaniswa ngonaphakade nozuko lukaThixo ongunaphakade? Niza kubufumana phi ubomi ngaphandle kwam? Ngubani oza kunihlangula ngaphandle kwalowo nisamkhabayo? Aluthi na ubabalo lwam nenceba yam zibambe nkqu nesandla sotshaba?

Nantoni na elungileyo eninayo yehle ivela kuYise wezikhanyiso, kuye akukho kuguquka nasithunzi sakujika. Kodwa hayi, nilukhanyele ubabalo lwam nasebenzisa inceba yam ukuze nizidlakazele kwaye nizibhaskise ebugwenxeni benu. Yabonani! Intshabalalo enkulu iza kunihlela. Ningcatshwe ziinkokeli zenu, eziceba intshabalalo yenu kwaye zivumele imikhosi ebomvu ukuba ingene. Intshabalalo nobubi obukhulu buza kunihlela ngequbuliso, yaye buza kufika kanye xa ndixhwila umtshakazi wam phakathi kweendlela zenu ezimbi. Emva koko intshabalalo yequbuliso iza kunibambisa, kuba nguwuphi umthiyeli obeka umgibe emehlweni entaka? Hayi, umgibe ogutyungelwe kancinci ulungele ukugqabhuka, yaye kuya kwenzeka njengoko umthinteli wam esusiwe, yaye aniyi kusinda.

Emva koko iintliziyo zenu ziya kunyibilika ngaphakathi kwenu namandla enu aya kubaleka njengoko nibona ngamehlo enu izidalwa eziza kukhululwa kwiinzulu zomhlaba phezu kwenu. Ndiza kuvula enzonzobileni, kuba ndinegunya naphezu kwayo; kuba ndinezitshixo zokufa nezesihogo, kwaye akukho mntu unokuzixhwila kum. Ukuba beniqondile kuphela ukuba aninanto emhlabeni, kwaye konke kusisipho nje sobabalo nenceba, kuba uthando lukaThixo lukhokelela abantu enguqukweni. Kodwa hayi, ngokubawa niyabugcina ukulunga kukaThixo nibe nodwa kwaye nibulale ummelwane wenu endaweni yokumthanda njengoko nizithanda, njengoko ndiyalile. UThixo walithanda kangaka ihlabathi, wada wanikela ngoNyana wakhe okuphela kwamzeleyo, uYesu waseNazarete, ukuze anixolelanise naye eluthandweni ngam.

Kodwa ngekratshi lenu neendlela zenu zehlabathi, nizilukhuni iintliziyo zenu ngokuchasene nam nosindiso lwam. Ngoko ke, intshabalalo epheleleyo iza kufikela isizwe senu ngeyure enye, yaye iBhabhiloni eyimfihlakalo ayisayi kubakho. Ewe, Bhabhiloni enkulu, iimfihlo zakho ziya kutyhilwa ukuze wonke umntu abone, kwaye abo banamathele kuwe baya kufumana isigwebo esifanayo.

Ngoko ke, owu, mntu owalayo uThixo, zithobe uze kum ngoku! Ixesha lakho lesidingo lingoku. Guqukela kum; ndiyakubongoza, yaye ndikubiza ngegama! Phuma kweli hlabathi lifayo, kuba kukho usindiso kum ukuba uya kwamkela.

Andinayolo ekugwetyweni kwenu, yaye uBawo unqwenela ukuba niphile phambi kwakhe, yaye eluthandweni lwakhe unike izidalwa zakhe ukhetho: Ndibeka phambi kwenu namhlanje ubomi nokufa, ngoko ke, khethani ubomi ukuze niphile nina nembewu yenu! Ngoko yizani kum kungekabi semva kwexesha nazi ukuba eli hlabathi libi ninamathele kulo liza kutshatyalaliswa ngokupheleleyo, yaye kuya kwenzeka ngomsindo weMvana. Akayi kubagweba na uThixo abenzi bobubi ababulele abantwana bakhe, abaqhwalelise nabaxhaphaza abamsulwa, ababulele unyana okuphela kwamzeleyo kaThixo? Nangona ndabuncama ngobomi bam ngenxa yenu kwaye kungekho mntu wabuthathayo ubomi bam, kukho umgwebo omkhulu nobulungisa obuza kwenziwa ndim. Kuba ndawuthenga umhlaba ngegazi lam nangedini lam lentlawulelo, yaye ungowam. Ndiza kumisa ubukumkani bobulungisa nobungcwele bokwenene, apho ingonyama nemvana ziya kulala kunye, apho kungekho nto imbi iya kungena. Ngoko ke, phumani ezonweni zenu, njengoko ndinithanda, yaye ndibiza ngomoya wam wokugqibela phambi kokuba iinkozo zokugqibela zentlabathi ziwe. Kwaye sele kuza kufika.
