Ubungqina Obubini: Ibandla
Emisebenzini KaPawulos Umpostile
Epreli 30, 2024

UThixo Luthando!

Bantwana bam, qondani imvelo yenu yokwenyani, kuba eyenu yimvelo kanye enanikwa nguThixo kum, uKrestu Yesu, kuba uThixo luthando! Kanti, uthando lukaThixo alufani nothando lomntu ngendlela efanayo neendlela zikaThixo ezingezizo iindlela zomntu; kodwa nifunda iindlela zikaThixo kum, kuba ndingumfundisi nomalusi wenu. Kuba wenjenje uThixo ukulithanda kwakhe ihlabathi, ude wancama uNyana wakhe okuphela kwamzeleyo, ukuze bonke abakholwayo kuye bangatshabalali, koko babe nobomi obungunaphakade. Kwaye kwesi senzo sikaThixo sokundinikela mna, Yesu Krestu, uNyana wakhe okuphela kwamzeleyo ngenxa yezono zoluntu, uthando lwakhe lubonakaliswa ngemfezeko epheleleyo kum. Kuba ndingowoBawo kwaye ndikwayimvelo enye nomoya omnye, kuba uThixo unguMoya, kwaye abo bamnqulayo kufuneka bamnqule ngoMoya nangenyaniso, kuba mna noBawo simnye emoyeni, ebumbini, nasenjongweni.

Qondani ukuba uthando lukaThixo alufani nothando lomntu, kuba uthando lomntu luhlala lunemiqathango, kwaye umntu owileyo uthanda ukubonakalisa uthando lwakhe ekunikezeni nasekwamkeleni izinto, kwaye uthando lomntu lujikela ngaphakathi ukuze kungenelwe umntu othandayo, kuba lunjalo uthando lomntu. Uthando lukaThixo amaxesha amaninzi lubonakaliswa ekunikezeni ngesisa ukuze kungenelwe omnye, nasekukhonzeni ummelwane wakho ngaphandle kwembuyekezo ngamanye amaxesha, nasekutyeleleni abahlolokazi, ekuphiliseni abagulayo, nasekwakheni uMzimba kaKrestu. Ngoko ke ndiyanibuza, bantwana bam, ukuba nifunde ngam uKrestu Yesu, kuba lowo undibonileyo mna, umbonile uBawo, kwaye nangona ningekandiboni ngamehlo enu, kungekudala kakhulu niya kundibona.

Kuba unoyolo lowo ungabonanga kodwa ekholwayo, kwaye nindibonile emoyeni nasenyanisweni, kwaye nindibonile ezindleleni zam nangothando endiluthumelayo nangomsebenzi abalungiseleli bam abawenzela mna ngenxa yenu. Ngoko ke niyandazi kwaye nindibonile, kwaye xa nihamba emoyeni kaKrestu ozelwe ngaphakathi, ngokwenene nihamba eluthandweni, kuba injalo imvelo yam endininike yona, kwaye unjalo noBawo ondinike imvelo yakhe: Bonani, lunjani na uthando uBawo asithande ngalo, ukuze kuthiwe singabantwana bakaThixo; ngenxa yoko ihlabathi alisazi, kuba lingamazanga yena. Kwaye kwakhona kwincwadi yokuqala kaYohane: Lulo ke uthando; ingekukuba thina samthandayo uThixo, ikukuba yena wasithandayo thina, wamthuma uNyana wakhe ukuba abe licamagushelo lezono zethu. Zintanda, ukuba uThixo wasithanda ngokunje, nathi sifanele ukuthandana.

Bantwanana bam, ningabantwana boThixo ophilayo, uYahweh, kuba ngendlela efanayo uYahweh watyala umyezo ngasempumalanga e-Eden, utyale imbewu yomoya wobunyana kanye kuni, kuba ndingumlimi wakhe onyamekela imiphefumlo yenu. Kuba nidalwe ngokutsha kum, kwaye nidalwe eluthandweni, kwaye uthando lukaThixo lukwimvelo yenu kanye. Qondani ukuba inkululeko yenu epheleleyo isemnyango xa niya kwazi njengoko nani naziwa. Mtshakazi wam, kuninzi kakhulu ekufuneka ndikucacisele kona kwaye ndikufundise kona kwaye ndikutyhilele kona ngani nangeenjongo eningcwaliselwe ukuzifeza, kuba ibiyinto efihlakeleyo entliziyweni kaBawo kwangaphambi kokudalwa, kwaye kungekudala niya kuqonda.

Ngoko ke ngubani na oya kunahlula eluthandweni lukaThixo? Yimbandezelo na, luxinzelelo na, yintshutshiso na, yindlala na, bubuze na, yingozi na, likrele na? Hayi, ningaphezu kwaboyisi kum, uKrestu Yesu, kwaye uthando lwam luziswa ekuzalisekeni okupheleleyo kuni, Mtshakazi wam, kuba amaxesha asondele kwaye kungekudala niya kwazi. Uthando lukaBawo alunakoyiswa, kuba yena akanakoyiswa, kuba uThixo luthando, kwaye uthando yimvelo kanye kaYahweh kuba uThixo luthando. Enye yeenkohliso ezininzi zikaSathana azisebenzisayo ukunilahlekisa kukubonisa uthando njengobuthathaka, olungenazibophelelo, oluzicingelayo nolungenamsebenzi: Kuba ezi ziindlela zomntu wemvelo.

Ngenene, uSathana uyaphika, kuba nguye owenziwe waba lilize, kwaye uthando lukaBawo alunakoyiswa, kuba akukho namnye onjengaye! Kum uthando lwakhe lwenziwa lwagqibelela enyameni, kuba kwakungothando lwam ngoBawo nani, Mtshakazi wam, endazinikela njengentlawulelo engenasono; kuba nithandwa kakhulu. Kwaye yazini ukuba niliqula lehlo likaYahweh, kwaye nina, Mtshakazi wam, niliqula lehlo lam, kwaye eluthandweni ndanihlawulela esandleni sotshaba ngokugqibeleleyo, kungekho nto isilelayo, ngegazi lam elicamagushayo. Mabathi abakhululweyo beNkosi, abo ibakhululeyo esandleni sotshaba!

Bantwana bam, ekubeni uThixo enithanda kangaka, ngoko ke kufanelekile ukuba namkele uthando lwakhe kwaye namkele amazwi akhe anganani nangenkululo enkulu uBawo awayisebenzayo edabini lam njengePasika yenu egqibeleleyo. Uninzi lwenu aluyiqondi kakuhle imfezeko yendalo endinenze yona kum, uKrestu Yesu, nasemoyeni endiwudalileyo ngaphakathi kwenu, kuba loo moya unamandla, kwaye uluthando; kuba yimvelo kanye kaBawo, kuba uYahweh luthando. Yabonani, akukho namnye unothando olukhulu kunolu, lokuba umntu abuncamele abahlobo bakhe ubomi bakhe. Kwaye ndabuncama ubomi bam ngenxa yabahlobo bam, kwaye ngokwenjenjalo ndinenze abazalwana bam kwaye ndinenze oonyana bakaThixo: Yabonani, lunjani na uthando uBawo asinike lona, ukuze kuthiwe singoonyana bakaThixo; ngenxa yesi sizathu eli hlabathi alikwazi, kuba alimazi yena.

Yivani amazwi am endawanika uYohane kwincwadi yakhe yokuqala, kwaye niwave kakuhle. UYahweh unikezele olona ncedo luphakamileyo kuni ngokuchasene nommangaleli nangokuchasene nabo banigwebayo, kwaye uzenze izityholo zabo zibe zingenasiseko ngokuchasene nani, kuba nithengiwe ngegazi lam elingenasono, kwaye nikhululwe kuzo zonke izityholo emsebenzini wam wokucamagushela. Anifundanga na into endayixelela amaRoma kwiminyaka emininzi eyadlulayo? Ngubani na oya kumangalela abanyulwa bakaThixo? NguThixo ogwebelayo: Ngubani na ogwebayo? NguKrestu owafa, ewe, kunoko wavuka kwakhona, lowo ukwasekunene kukaThixo, lowo usithethelelayo. Anibuvanga na ubungqina bamaHebhere, apho ilizwi lawo lingqina ngobubingeleli bam obungaguqukiyo? Kungoko anako nokubasindisa ngokugqibeleleyo abo beza kuThixo ngaye, ekubeni ehlala ephila ukuze abathethelele.

Yivani la mazwi, kwaye avumele angene nzulu kuni, kwaye nive ilizwi endalinika ibandla laseRoma: Kuba wamenza isono ngenxa yethu, yena wayengasazi isono, ukuze thina senziwe ubulungisa bukaThixo kuye. Ngaba la mazwi ayamangalisa kakhulu kuni? Ngaba niya kuzivumela ukuba niqonde, okanye niya kuvala iindlebe zenu ngezithethe zenu ezingenaxabiso kunye nobungqina bobuxoki obungenaxabiso enibambelele kubo, obunikezwe ngabaprofeti bobuxoki abangenaxabiso abenzelwe ukunilahlekisa? Ngaba yinto enzima kakhulu ukwamkela ukuba nenziwe ubulungisa bukaThixo kum, uKrestu Yesu?

Ndifuna nizole kakhulu ezingqondweni nasezintliziyweni zenu kwaye nivumele ilizwi lam lizolise umoya wenu kwaye nive amazwi am. Thambisani iintliziyo zenu kwaye nidilize ikratshi lenu; kuba ndinento endifuna ukunixelela yona eya kunikhulula ebukhobokeni ukuba ningakholwa ngamazwi athile endiza kuninika wona kwakhona. Kuba ilizwi lamaRoma lithetha nani njengoko nifunda, ngoko fundani kwaye niqonde; kwaye ekuqondeni, yamkelani inyaniso yokuma kwenu kum, uKrestu Yesu. Kwaye amazwi am ngala: Ngoko ke, akusekho kugwetywa kwabo bakuKrestu Yesu, abangahambi ngokwenyama, kodwa ngokomoya. Kuba umthetho womoya wobomi, kuKrestu Yesu, undikhulule emthethweni wesono nokufa. Musa ukuvumela amehlo akho ajonge nje amazwi, kodwa funda ngalinye lawo kancinci nangokholo, ukholo kum, kuba ndithethe la mazwi phezu kwenu, bantwana bam; kwaye mna, Krestu Yesu, ndinikhulule kuko konke ukugwetywa kum. Aniqondi na ukuba nenziwe ubulungisa bukaThixo? Ndiqondeni kwaye nive ilizwi lam kwaye niphulaphule amazwi am: Ngubani na onigwebayo? NguKrestu owafa, ewe, kunoko wavuka kwakhona, lowo ukwasekunene kukaThixo, lowo usithethelelayo.

Bantwana bam, andinibizelanga kum ebulungiseni nasebungcweleni bokwenyani ukuze ndinigwebe, kwaye kufuneka niyiqonde inyaniso: Aninalungelo lokuzigweba, kwaye uninzi lwenu luyakwenza oko, kwaye ubomi benu buyathuthunjiswa ngenxa yolu kuzithiya utshaba elubeke phezu kwenu. Vumelani ndiqhawule la makhamandela obuxoki otshaba, kwaye nive amazwi am kwaye niwakholwe, kuba ayinyaniso, kwaye abhalelwe nina ukuze nibe nikhululekile ngokwenene kum. Kuba ndinikhulule kuko konke ukugwetywa, kwaye utshaba nommangaleli wabazalwana uphoselwe phantsi: Ngoku kufikile usindiso, namandla, nobukumkani bukaThixo wethu, namandla kaKrestu wakhe: kuba ummangaleli wabazalwana bethu uphoselwe phantsi, lowo wayebamangalela phambi koThixo wethu imini nobusuku.

Qondani, bantwana bam, ukuba ningamalungisa, kwaye ukuzigweba kukutyeshela amazwi am uBawo aninike wona; ngoko ke musani ukungakholwa, kodwa yibani nokholo olukhulu emazwini am, kuba ayinyaniso, kwaye akukho bungqina buyinyaniso obukhe banikwa. Qondani umahluko phakathi kokuzigweba nomoya wam osebenza kuni ukunohlwaya ngesono; kuba ndathini kuYohane kwincwadi yakhe yokuqala: Kuba ukuba intliziyo yethu iyasigweba, uThixo mkhulu kunentliziyo yethu, kwaye wazi zonke izinto. Zintanda, ukuba intliziyo yethu ayisigwebi, ngoko sinokholo kuThixo. Aniqondi na ukuba umoya wam endiwudalileyo ngaphakathi komnye nomnye wenu, Mtshakazi wam, eneneni unikhohlwaya ngezono zenu nasemva kokuba nizelwe ngokutsha kum, uKrestu Yesu, iNkosi yenu?

Anifundanga na ubungqina bam obubhalwe kwiindaba ezilungileyo zikaYohane malunga nomthuthuzeli? Kwaye xa efikile, uya kohlwaya ihlabathi ngesono, nangobulungisa, nangomgwebo. Ngesono, kuba abakholwa kum. Ngobulungisa, kuba ndiya kuBawo, kwaye anisayi kundibona. Ngomgwebo, kuba inkosana yeli hlabathi igwetyiwe. Bantwana bam, umthuthuzeli wanohlwaya ngesono, kwaye niguqukele kum, kwaye ekuzalweni ngokutsha nasemandleni omoya endiwudalileyo ngaphakathi kwenu, kwanaloo moya wobunyana, kwanaye uKrestu kuni ithemba lobuqaqawuli, ndinenze ubulungisa bukaThixo kum. Ngoko ke, anisohlwaywa ngesono, kodwa nohlwaywa ngobulungisa xa nona, naxa nibuyela kwiindlela zenu ezindala, kuba inyoka iyathanda ukulahlekisa abantu bam ize izise isityholo phambi koBawo naphambi kwam ngemisebenzi yayo engendawo, kwaye ndingumthetheleli wenu.

Kuba kwakhona, ubungqina bukaYohane kwincwadi yakhe yokuqala buthetha ngalo mbandela: Bantwanana bam, ndinibhalele ezi zinto, ukuze ningoni. Kwaye ukuba umntu wona, sinommeli kuBawo, uYesu Krestu olilungisa: Kwaye yena ulicamagushelo lezono zethu: kwaye kungekhona ezethu kuphela, kodwa nezono zehlabathi lonke. Bantwana bam, ndingummeli wenu, kwaye ningamalungisa kum, kwaye ningamalungisa ngonaphakade, kuba ubulungisa buzelwe kanye kwimvelo yenu. Ngoko ke yibani nentliziyo ethambileyo kum, kwaye ndiya kunibonisa izono neempazamo zenu, kwanemikhwa yenu emibi neendlela zenu zangaphambili ezimbi enilingeka ngamanye amaxesha ukuba nibuyele kuzo. Kuba xa intliziyo yenu inohlwaya, guqukelani kum kwaye nivume izono zenu, kuba sithini isibhalo: Kodwa ukuba sihamba ekukhanyeni, njengoko yena esekukhanyeni, sinobudlelwane omnye nomnye, kwaye igazi likaYesu Krestu uNyana wakhe liyasihlambulula kuso sonke isono. Ukuba sithi asinasono, siyazikhohlisa, kwaye inyaniso ayikho kuthi. Ukuba siyazivuma izono zethu, uthembekile, kwaye ulilungisa ukusixolela izono zethu, nokusihlambulula kuko konke ukungalungisi.

Bantwana bam, ndinigwebe njengamalungisa kum, kwaye uYahweh unikhulule kuzo zonke izityholo kum; kutheni ningazivumeli ukuba nikholwe ngamazwi am anobabalo nothando? Kuba uthando lukaThixo lubonakaliswa kuwo kunye nothando lwam ngoMtshakazi wam kwanalo lonke ihlabathi, kuba nabani na othandayo makaze, yizani nje phambi kokuba umnyango uvalwe kuba ixesha liphelile. Buyelani kum mihla le kwaye nizihlambulule ngegazi lam elicamagushayo, kuba idini lam ligqibelele kwaye lenziwa kanye kuphela: Kuba amaHebhere athi ngokucacileyo ngam: Lowo engukukhanya kobuqaqawuli, nomfanekiso oqingqiweyo wobuntu bakhe, kwaye ethwele zonke izinto ngelizwi lakhe elinamandla, uzihlambulule ngokwakhe izono zethu, kwaye uhleli ngasekunene koBukhulu kwiindawo eziphakamileyo. Bantwana bam, qondani ukuba umthuthuzeli ukwalohlwayile ihlabathi ngomgwebo, kuba inkosana yeli hlabathi igwetyiwe, kwaye isigwebo sayo siyifanele. Kodwa ukuza kuthi ga ngoku ifuna ukunitsalela phantsi ekugwetyweni kwayo okuyifaneleyo, ngoko iza kunigweba; ngoko ke musani ukuphulaphula amazwi ayo kwaye nilahle ubuxoki bayo.

Bantwanana bam, hambani eluthandweni lukaThixo kum, uKrestu Yesu iNkosi yenu. Thandani uBawo kwaye nindithande ngokugcina imiyalelo yam, kuba ithini iNcwadi yokuQala kaYohane? Lowo uthi, ndiyamazi, kwaye akayigcini imiyalelo yakhe, ulixoki, kwaye inyaniso ayikho kuye. Kodwa lowo ugcina ilizwi lakhe, kuye ngokwenene uthando lukaThixo lugqibelele: ngale nto siyazi ukuba sikuye. Lowo uthi uhlala kuye, naye ufanele ukuhamba, njengoko wahambayo. Ndiyaniyala, Mtshakazi wam, ukuba niqhubeke nihamba njengoko ndahambayo kwaye nigcine imiyalelo yam, kuba ndandisoloko ndisenza ukuthanda kukaBawo, kwaye nani nifanele nizame ukusoloko nisenza ukuthanda kwam, kuba okwam kukuthanda kukaYahweh. Ekugcineni imiyalelo yam kuya kufuneka niguqukele kubazalwana, kuba ithini iNcwadi yokuQala kaYohane? Ngale nto siyazi ukuba siyabathanda abantwana bakaThixo, xa sithanda uThixo kwaye sigcina imiyalelo yakhe. Kuba lulo olu uthando lukaThixo, ukuba sigcine imiyalelo yakhe, kwaye imiyalelo yakhe ayinzima. Yivani ilizwi lam kwaye nilandele amazwi am kude kube sekuthobeleni, kwaye niqhubeke nithanda abazalwana benu kwaye nikhonze eluthandweni, kungekhona ngenxa yenzuzo ephantsi; kuba umvuzo wenu usezandleni zam, kungekhona ezandleni zabo nibakhonzayo.

Makungabikho nto yenziwa ngokuxabana okanye ngekratshi; kodwa ngokuthobeka kwengqondo ngamnye makabone omnye engcono kunaye.

Ndingumfundisi nomalusi wenu.

NdingowoBawo kwaye ndikwayimvelo enye nomoya omnye.

Ndingubulungisa bukaThixo, kwaye ndinenze ubulungisa bukaThixo.

Ndingulowo ugwetyelweyo ezindleleni zakhe.

Andinasono, kwaye ndinigwebile kum, uKrestu Yesu.

Ndingulowo uhlala engcwalisiwe ngonaphakade, kuba obam buBukumkani bukaThixo, kwaye ndiningcwalisile ngomoya wobunyana.

Ndingulowo wanikela ithontsi lokugqibela legazi lakhe emnqamlezweni ukuze ndinihlambulule kuso sonke isono, kuba akukho sono emoyeni wobunyana endininike wona.

Ndingumthetheleli wenu.

Ndingummeli wenu.

Yimani nilungile, Mtshakazi wam, kuba amaxesha okubuyiselwa asondele, kwaye ukubonakala kwam kusondele kunokuba nicinga.
