Kutheni Nizilahlile Iimfihlelo Zam?

Kutheni nizilahlile iimfihlelo zam, Owu, bandla laseLawodike elidiki-diki? Kutheni niyilahlile imfihlelo enkulu kaThixo? Niyitshintshise iimfihlo ezingcwele kakhulu zikaBawo, kwa uKristu kuni ithemba lobuqaqawuli, ngemali emdaka, kwaye isisu senu senziwe uThixo wenu. Kanti, niya kuthi kum, "Akukho siphoso kum. Andileqeki mali imdaka, kuba ndithe tye phambi kwakho." Ndiya kuphendula ndithi: Anileqeki na ivangeli yobutyebi bobuxoki efundiswa emabandleni enu aseLawodike? Niyithengisile inyaniso yeemfihlelo ezingcwele kakhulu zikaThixo ngenzuzo engento, kwaye anisenalo ithemba lobuqaqawuli endinqwenela ngamandla ukuba niliqonde, kodwa nilitshintshile ithemba lobuqaqawuli laba lithemba lenzuzo yezinto eziphathekayo enifuna ukuziqokelelela ngeengcinga zenu ezingahlambulukanga. Andinibizelanga emalini nasekuthandeni izinto eziphathekayo, kodwa ndinibizele ebulungiseni nasebungcweleni bokwenene obufumaneka kum.

Buyani kwiimpazamo zenu nithenge kum iyeza lamehlo, ukuze nithambise amehlo enu ukuze nizibone iinyaniso eziseZibhalweni endininike zona ukuze nisindiswe. Niqhathiwe ekuxhaseni oorhulumente behlabathi, nicinga ukuba ngokubaxhasa nixhasa abanyulwa bakaThixo. Nifanele ukuba neentloni phambi kwam kuba ekungazini kwenu aniyiqondi ukuba ngoobani na amaSirayeli okwenene kaThixo, kwaye anisenakuyiqonda imfihlelo enkulu kaThixo yokuba iiNtlanga zifanele ukuba ziindlalifa kunye nani, zibe zomzimba omnye, nababelani besithembiso sakhe kuKristu, ngeVangeli. Kuba ndiluxolo lwenu, mna ndenze zombini zaba yinto enye, ndaludiliza udonga olwahlulayo phakathi kwenu.

Nivumele ukuba isazisi senu sibiwe yindlu yesikhungu kaSathana, kwaye nizilahlekisile emazwini abaprofeti bobuxoki. Aniqondi na ukuba nina ningamaSirayeli kaThixo? Bonke abahamba ngalo mgaqo, makube luxolo kubo, nenceba, naphezu kwamaSirayeli kaThixo. Owu, bandla elidiki-diki, ngubani na oya kunisindisa kuhenyu lwenu lokukrexeza olunionakalisileyo ekucingeni okumanyeneyo okukum, mna Kristu Yesu iNkosi yenu? Aninakubambelela kweli hlabathi lifayo, nicinga ukuba ngokwenjenjalo niyandikhonza; kwaye nifanele niguquke ekuxhaseni kwenu oorhulumente abakhohlakeleyo behlabathi, kuba yintoni na intsebenziswano anayo uKristu noBheliyali, kwaye abakhonzi mna kodwa bakhonza uSathana. Yiyani kwiZibhalo zam nifune ubuntu benu bokwenene endibufihle kwiincwadi zam; kuba nifile, kwaye ubomi benu bufihliwe noKristu kuThixo. Kwaye lowo ufileyo ukhululwe esonweni, nomthetho woMoya onika ubomi unikulule kumthetho wesono nokufa.

Ngoko ke, ukuba umntu ukho kuKristu, usisidalwa esitsha: izinto ezindala zidlule, yabona, zonke izinto zibe ntsha. Ndinidalile kum njengosana olusandul' ukuzalwa, oluphefumla okokuqala kunaphakade; kuba unaphakade waqala kuni ngomzuzwana nazalwa ngokutsha ngomoya wam, kuba ndenze kwababini umntu omnye omtsha, ndenza uxolo. Ewe, umntu omnye omtsha kum oqulunqwe ngumYuda neNtlanga ngokufanayo ebandleni lam likaThixo. Abo ndibabizele kubutsha bobomi kum ngabantu abanyuliweyo bakaThixo, kwaye abo bazelwe ngomoya wam ngabantwana bokwenene bakaThixo, kwaye ukuba umntu akanaye umoya kaKristu, akangowakhe.

Kutheni ningawavumeli amacandelo eZibhalo, niwabala njengangangcwele ngenxa yoko nikubiza ngokuba kukucinga okulungileyo? Kutheni nilahlula iZibhalo, nicinga ukuba iLizwi likaThixo liphela asilolakho ukuba libe yimfundiso, isenzo, nokufunda kwenu? Ukucinga okulungileyo kobuxoki okonakalisa inyaniso kunenza nibe ziimfama kwiindlela zam neenjongo zam endizimisele kwangaphambili kuni. Ewe, nifanele ukuba iingcinga zenu zibe sezintweni eziphezulu, kodwa ningawabizi amacandelo eZibhalo zam ngokuba yintshabalalo nosizi kuba anicingi ukuba kukucinga okulungileyo. Nibanjiswe kubugqwirha bobuKristu, nisebenzisa ulwazi kanye lweengelosi eziwileyo ezifundisa uluntu ubugqi nobugqwirha nokusika iingcambu kwasekuqaleni.

Ulwazi lwenu lweengelosi eziwileyo bubuxoki obuyilwe ngobuchule obenzelwe ukurhintyela umphefumlo wenu ngamazwi amnandi neentetho ezintle. Yazini ukuba amadoda amaninzi akhohlakeleyo kule minyaka ingenise ubugqi kubuKristu, enixelela ukuba ukuba nicinga into ixesha elide, kufuneka yenzeke njengoko nicinga. Zidenge nina! UThixo akanidalelanga ukuba nizakhele inkohliso yenu ejikeleze ubuKristu benu bokuzinceda neengcinga ezonwabileyo. Nilandela omnye uYesu kwaye nilushiyile ukholo olukum, kwaye niwalahlile amazwi am nemiyalelo yam nithanda amazwi amnandi neentetho ezintle, kwaye niqokelelwe ziingcuka ezibawayo ezinixokisayo.

Ziyanixokisa kwaye ziwusebenzisa kakubi umthetho kaMoses, zixelela amaKristu ukuba afanele ukunikela isishumi ukuze axhase imibutho yazo engakhelwanga phezu kwam, ilitye lembombo eliyintloko. Jonga iZibhalo, kwaye uqonde ukuba njengoko umntu egqiba entliziyweni yakhe, makaphe kwaye aphe ngovuyo, kuba uThixo uyamthanda umphi onovuyo. Kwaye lo ngumyalelo ongundoqo kwiTestamente eNtsha eqinisekiswe egazini lam. Isishumi sasikukuxhasa ababingeleli abangabaLevi, kwaye bona babexhasa umbingeleli omkhulu ngomnikelo wokuphakanyiswa. Oku kuphelisiwe kum, kuba ndingumbingeleli omkhulu ngonaphakade ngokohlobo lukaMelkitsedeke kwaye ndinenze ababingeleli nookumkani phambi kwam. Kodwa nina, Owu, bandla elidiki-diki, nimfanyekiswe yimibono yamandla obuxoki nobuchule, ubuchule obungafumanekiyo kuKristu.

Anilawuli nto ngeengcinga zenu kuphela, kwaye nimfamekile kum nakwiinjongo zam endizilungiselele nina. Nizivale iindlebe zenu ekubeni ningayiva imfundiso ephilileyo nithanda iimfundiso zobuxoki ezikiti-kitiza iindlebe zenu. Ayiphelelanga apho, kodwa nihlasela abazalwana abaye baba ziintshaba zenu kuba benixelela inyaniso kule micimbi, kwaye niyilahlile ingqondo yaseBhereya nithanda ukubanjwa ziimvakalelo okubizwa ngokuba bubuKristu ngempazamo. Yiqondeni le nto, balusi abalahlekileyo: niya kubuyekezwa ngomgwebo ngokulahlekisa iigusha zam, kwaye impindezelo yeyam kwaye ndiya kubuyisela.

Yiqondeni le nto, bantwana bam: anilawuli nto ngokucinga kwenu okulungileyo, kwaye aningomazibuthe wobutyebi ngenxa yeengcinga zenu ezonwabileyo. Nilahlekisiwe kwaye nikhokelelwe ekulahlekeni ngabakhohlisi abaninzi. Ngaphezu koko, aninakwenza nto ngaphandle kwam kanye njengokuba nam ndingenakwenza nto ngaphandle kukaBawo; kuba nguThixo onika amandla amalungisa kwaye nguBawo odale konke, kwaye ngamnye ukuba azukise igama likaYahweh elilungileyo ngaphezu kwako konke!

Phumani ekubaweni kwenu nilahle ukuthanda imali, kuba ukuthanda imali yingcambu yabo bonke ububi.

Xa nivala amehlo enu kwivangeli yam iphela kwaye nikhetha ngokukhetha oko nicinga ukuba "kulungile," nise mgodini neenkokeli eziyimfama ezanikhokelela nobabini kweso siphelo. Andinibizelanga ukuba niwele emgodini bantwana bam, kodwa ndinibizele ukuba niphile phambi kwam ngobulungisa nobungcwele bokwenene, ngoko phakamani eludakeni. Ndinibizele ukuba nilahle izono zenu niguquke kwiindlela zenu ezingalunganga. Kuphela ngegazi lam lentlawulelo ninokuhlanjululwa ezonweni zenu; iingcinga zenu ezilungileyo azisebenzi bulungisa, nangona nizenze izithixo zentliziyo yenu.

Kutheni nivumela abazalwana bobuxoki nabaYudayiza phakathi kwenu ukuba bahlole inkululeko eninayo kum, iNkosi elungileyo? Ukuba beniyiqondile imfihlelo enkulu kaThixo, beniya kuqonda ukuba uKristu sisiphelo soMthetho sobulungisa kuye wonke umntu okholwayo, Kuba uMoses ubuchaza ubulungisa obungoMthetho, ukuba umntu owenza ezo zinto uya kuphila ngazo. Kuba ndiyangqina kwakhona kuye wonke umntu owalukileyo, ukuba unetyala lokwenza umthetho uphela. Uthini ke ngomthetho kaMoses?

Ndizifezekisile iimfuno zomthetho kwaye ndamgwebela umntu ngokholo kum, kuba ndoyisile, kwaye ndiwufezekisile umthetho, ndizinikela njengePasika egqibeleleyo kanye kuphela; ngoko ke, olungileyo uya kuphila ngokholo. Anifundanga na ukuba andizanga kuzokutshabalalisa umthetho okanye abaprofeti kodwa ukuba ndiwufezekise umthetho kwaye ndinike ubulungisa ebantwini ngokuzifanisa kwabo nam, iNkosi yabo uYesu Kristu.

Ndithi ke, hambani ngomoya, kwaye aniyi kuzalisekisa inkanuko yenyama, kwaye akukho mthetho uchasene nesiqhamo somoya. Ngoko ke, ningcwatywe nam ngobhaptizo ekufeni kwam, ukuze njengoko uKristu wavuswa kwabafileyo ngobuqaqawuli bukaYise: ngokunjalo nani nifanele ukuhamba kubutsha bobomi. Kuphela kwimisebenzi yam yentlawulelo, njengemvana kaThixo engenasono, nabani na anokoyisa isono kwaye akhululwe kwisiqalekiso somthetho; kuba ndenziwe isiqalekiso ngenxa yenu, kuba uqalekisiwe nawuphi na umntu oxhonywe emthini.

UMoses ubuchaza ubulungisa obungoMthetho, Ukuba umntu owenza ezo zinto uya kuphila ngazo. Kuba kuKristu Yesu akukho nto incedayo ukwaluka okanye ukungaluki, kodwa isidalwa esitsha. UKristu sisiphelo somthetho kuye wonke umntu okholwayo, kodwa uninzi lwenu luya kuwala la mazwi kanye kuba nihlonipha amazwi abefundisi benu, abashumayeli, noorabhi ngaphezu kwamazwi eNkosi noMsindisi wenu, uKristu Yesu. Aniqondi na ukuba nakhelwe phezu kwesiseko sabapostile nabaprofeti, uYesu Kristu ngokwakhe elilitye lembombo eliyintloko? Kuba akukho sinye isiseko anokusibeka umntu, ngaphandle kweso sibekiweyo, esinguYesu Kristu; ngoko ke, anikho phantsi komthetho kodwa niphantsi kobabalo.

Kuba abo bakholwayo banokungena ekuphumleni kwam, kuba lowo ungenileyo ekuphumleni kwam naye uphumle kwimisebenzi yakhe, njengoko uThixo wenza kweyakhe ngomhla wesixhenxe. Anazi na ukuba umthetho wawungumfundisi wenu wokunizisa kum, uKristu Yesu? Kanti abaninzi bandilahlile mna nobabalo lwam nenceba yam ngenxa yemisebenzi yabo engenamsebenzi yobulungisa. Ubumfama ngokuyinxenye bufikile kwaSirayeli de ukuzala kweeNtlanga kufike esiphelweni. Owu, gatya lomnquma wasendle, musa ukuziqhayisa ngomthi womnquma ofakwe kuwo, kwaye ungalandeli ubumfama babo; kuba ndinako ukumsindisa uYakobi kwaye ndiya kwenza kanye oko njengoko intsalela iya kusindiswa.

Makungabikho mntu ke onigwebayo ekutyeni, okanye ekuseleni, okanye ngokubhekiselele kuwo nawuphi na umhla ongcwele, okanye ekuthwaseni kwenyanga, okanye kwimihla yeSabatha. Kutheni nibeka umthwalo ebantwaneni bam, nibaxelela ukuba bafanele ukubiza igama lam ngolwimi oluthile olungcwele? Ndiziqonda zonke iilwimi kwaye ndijonga intliziyo, ngoko ndiyazi xa abantwana bam bebiza egameni lam ngalo naluphi na ulwimi ababiza ngalo, kwaye ndibatywina ngetywina likaBawo ngokufanelekileyo, kwaye ndibazisa eBandleni likaThixo. Nooyihlo nani aninakho ukuwugcina umthetho, kodwa umthetho uzalisekiswa kuni ebulungiseni bam; kuba umthetho wehlela kwimiyalelo emibini emikhulu: Thanda iNkosi uThixo wakho ngentliziyo yakho yonke, ngomphefumlo wakho wonke, ngengqondo yakho yonke, nangamandla akho onke, kwaye uthande ummelwane wakho njengoko uzithanda.

Ubulungisa abuzi ngomthetho kodwa buza ngokholo kum, iNkosi uYesu Kristu. Kuba ndingumbingeleli omkhulu ngonaphakade ngokohlobo lukaMelkitsedeke, kwaye ngegazi lam lentlawulelo niyagwetyelwa, kuba ndiyiPasika egqibeleleyo, kwaye ndiyimvana kaThixo. Makungabikho mntu unikhohlisayo kule micimbi. Umthetho awenzelwanga umntu olungileyo, kodwa wenzelwe abangenamthetho nabangathobeliyo. Yabonani! Ndilusulile ushicilelo lwemiyalelo, olwaluchasene nani, olwaluchasene nani, ndaluthatha endleleni, ndalubethelela emnqamlezweni wam. Kanti, nisafuna ukuba sebukhobokeni kwizinto ezibuthathaka nezingento. Zithini iZibhalo: Mkhuphe umkhonzazana nonyana wakhe, kuba asingobantwana bomkhonzazana kodwa singabakhululekileyo.

Anditshongo na ngokucacileyo: Yabonani! Ndinixelela imfihlelo, asiyi kulala sonke, kodwa sonke siya kuguqulwa, kuba ndiya kuhla ezulwini ngesimemelelo, ngelizwi lesiphatha-zithunywa nangekhalipha likaThixo kwaye abafileyo kuKristu baya kuvuka kuqala, emva koko nina niphilayo niseleyo niya kuthathwa emafini ukuze nihlangane nam, uKristu Yesu emoyeni, kwaye niya kuhlala nam ngonaphakade. Owu, bandla laseLawodike, niwala namazwi am acace gca kuni. Ngoko ke, yazini ukuba ndiya kunala etafileni yam, xa ndinqwenela kakhulu ukunibekela indawo emazulwini.

Nicinga ukuba yinto elula ukwala amazwi am kwaye nikhokelele abanye ekulahlekeni kwimpazamo yenu. Ngoko ke, ekubeni nifuna umhla weNkosi, ndiya kunishiya ukuba ninyamezele umhla weNkosi, ndibona ukuba ngokuzikhukhumeza nicinga ukuba ningema. Niyakwala ukubonakala kwam kwaye nikhulisa amazwi abaprofeti bobuxoki ngaphezu kwemfundiso ephilileyo endiyinike kum. Anifundanga na umzekeliso weentombi ezilishumi? Ndinicebisa ukuba nibe nokholo emazwini am kwaye ningandikhanyeli, ukuze nam ndingakhanyeli nina. Niya kuba ngabaphi, bantwana bam? Niya kuba phakathi kweentombi ezilumkileyo ezazilungiselele ngeentambo ezichetyiweyo neoli ezibane zazo? Okanye niya kuthatha indawo yenu neentombi ezingalumkanga, nisithi, Nkosi, Nkosi sivulele; kwaye ndiya kuthi kuzo, Andinazi!

Anifundanga na? Ngenxa yesi sizathu indoda iya kushiya uyise nonina, kwaye iya kumanyana nomfazi wayo, kwaye bobabini baya kuba nyama-nye. Le yimfihlelo enkulu: kodwa ndithetha ngoKristu neBandla. Aniyiqondi na amazwi omyeni, mtshakazi wam? Nifanele ukubambelela kum njengentloko yenu ngendlela efanayo umfazi abambelela ngayo kumyeni wakhe njengentloko yakhe, kuba uMtshakazi liBandla lam nomzimba wam, kwaye yeyiphi na indoda eyayithiya inyama yayo? Kanti, nivumela abalusi abalahlekileyo ukuba banikhokelele ekulahlekeni kulo mcimbi, kwaye uninzi lwenu luthatha amazwi ajijekileyo avela kubaprofeti bobuxoki abajijekileyo ngaphezu kweLizwi lam eliNgcwele.

Nibeke ukholo lwenu kumadoda akhohlakeleyo, amadoda endingawathumanga; kanti anikhohlisa ngamazwi amnandi neentetho ezintle endingabanikanga. Niyabalandela ngokungathi nilandela mna. Guqukani! Uninzi lwenu lulandela abefundisi bobuxoki ababonakala njengabefundisi bobulungisa. Akumangalisi, nangona kunjalo, kuba uSathana uziguqula abe yingelosi yokukhanya, ngoko akufanele kumangalise ukuba abefundisi bakhe benze okufanayo. Uninzi lwenu lumfanyekiswe kukukhanya kobuxoki, okuvela kubefundisi bobuxoki endingabathumanga, kwaye bathetha amazwi amakhulu adumbileyo achasene nemfundiso ephilileyo ekum, iNkosi uYesu Kristu.

Zimeleleni, Owu, maKristu angenamqolo, kwaye niphume kuye nimke eBhabheli eyiMfihlelo. Ndingathanda ukuba benishushu okanye nibanda kwaye ningabidiki-diki, kwaye andinqweneli ukunikhafulela emlonyeni wam. Ngoko ke, buyelani kum, bantwana bam, kwaye nibuyele emazwini am. Ndibambeni njengentloko yenu, kuba ndiyiNkosi eyanifelayo, kwaye navuka xa nam ndavukayo. Kutheni nixhomekeke kubaprofeti bobuxoki? Anidingi ukuba nawuphi na umntu anifundise, kodwa intambiso eniyamkeleyo kum ihleli kuni kwaye inifundisa zonke izinto, Owu, nina bokholo oluncinane! Jikani nize kum kwaye nibuyele endleleni emxinwa. Lilani, bhombolozani, niguquke, bantwana bam! Bonke endibathandayo, ndiyabakhalimela, ukuze ubizo lwenu nonyulo lwenu luqiniseke.

Yazini ukuba ndinithanda kakhulu kwaye ndinqwenela intliziyo yenu epheleleyo ngaphezu kwemisebenzi yenu. Yizani kum ngentliziyo evulekileyo kwaye nindinike konke okwenu, njengoko nam ndinike konke okwam ngenxa yenu. Musani ukoyika kwaye ningazigwebi, kuba akukho kugwetywa kwabo bakuKristu Yesu. Buyelani nje kum nivume izono zenu, kwaye ndiya kunixolela ndinibuyisele kwiinjongo nezipho endininike zona. Yimani niqine ningagungqi, kuba kungekudala ndiya kubonakala ngequbuliso, kwaye ihlabathi liya kwazi ukuba ndinixwile, njengoko le nto ayiyi kwenziwa emfihlekweni. Ixesha lifutshane, bantwana bam, kwaye kungekudala ixesha aliyi kubakho.

Ndinako ukumsindisa uYakobi kwaye ndiya kwenza kanye oko njengoko intsalela iya kusindiswa.

Ndingumbingeleli omkhulu ngonaphakade ngokohlobo lukaMelkitsedeke.

NdiyiPasika egqibeleleyo.

Ndiyimvana kaThixo.

NdiyiNkosi eyanifelayo, kwa uYeshua HaMashiach.
