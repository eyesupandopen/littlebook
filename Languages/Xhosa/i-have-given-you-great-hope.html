Meyi 03, 2024

Ndiniphe Ithemba Elikhulu!

Yizani kum nive amazwi am, Mtshakazi wam, kuba ndilapha ukuze ndithuthuzele imiphefumlo yenu, ndithobe iintliziyo zenu, ndinifeze kum, uKrestu Yesu. Musani ukutyhafa ngamazwi am okululeka, kuba endimthandayo, ndiyamohlwaya, ukuze ubizo nonyulo lwenu luqiniseke kum, uMyeni, uKrestu Yesu. Bantwana bam, thobelani ilizwi lam nive amazwi am, kuba andinibizelanga mgwebo nakugwetywa, kodwa ndinivakalise njengamalungisa kum, kwiNkosi yenu uYesu Krestu. Anivanga na into awayixelela abaseKorinte uPawulos kwincwadi yakhe yokuqala? Ngoku ke kuhleli ukholo, ithemba, nothando, ezo zinto zontathu; kodwa eyona inkulu kuzo luthando. Ngoko ke uthando lolona lukhulu kwezi zintathu, kuba uthando lukaYahweh alunakoyiswa. Uthando lukaYahweh yiglue edibanisayo. Yeyona nto ikhuthazayo, kwaye ukupha ngesisa njengoko namkele ngesisa kukulinganisa uThixo!

Uninzi lwenu lulahlekelwe lithemba, nkqu nethemba lenu kum, uYesu Krestu, kuba ndininike ithemba elikhulu, bantwana bam. Qondani ukuba isela linganibhida linilahlekise, kodwa uThixo ayinguye umbhali wesiphithiphithi, kodwa ngowoxolo njengakuma bandla onke abangcwele. USathana uzamile ukufihla imiyalelo yam endayinika kwisityhilelo sam seMfihlelo Enkulu kaThixo kuMpostile uPawulos kwiminyaka emininzi yeeJubili eyadlulayo ngoku. Bantwana bam, ithemba lenu yingcamango kanye enenza nikhuthazelele ukunyamezela iimvavanyo ezininzi neembandezelo eninazo kobu bomi. Kuba ngaphandle kwethemba lenu, ningaba njengoko uPawulos watshoyo kwabaseKorinte: Ukuba kobu bomi kuphela sinethemba kuKrestu, singabona lusizi kunabo bonke abantu. Kodwa ngoku uKrestu uvukile kwabafileyo, waba yintlahlela yabo baleleyo. Bantwana bam, ndininike ithemba elikhulu ngethemba lenu, kuba ndithe: nina baphilayo nabasalayo niya kuthathwa niye emafini, njengoko abafileyo kuKrestu baya kuvuka kuqala, kwaye niya kutshintshwa nonke, kuba niya kufana nam ngonaphakade kwaye noMtshakazi wam uya kuba nam ngonaphakade.

Kuba liyintoni ithemba lenu kuKrestu, bantwana bam? Asisosithembiso sokuhlangulwa okupheleleyo nokubuyiselwa, apho niya kubona izulu nobukhulu nozuko lukaYahweh ngamehlo enu? Ithemba lenu asilokamva apho niya kundikhonza ebukumkanini bam bobulungisa, kuba njengoko benithembekile kwizinto ezimbalwa, ndiya kunenza abaphathi bezinto ezininzi. Ithemba lenu asisosithembiso sobomi obungunaphakade apho niya kudla kumthi wobomi nihambe ezitratweni zeYerusalem eNtsha, apho ukukhanya kukaYahweh nokukhanya kukaYeshua HaMashiach kuya kukhanya ngonaphakade kubukhazikhazi obuzukileyo? Ithemba lenu asilozuko lwezithsaba ezininzi nemivuzo ngenxa yokuthembeka nothando lwenu kum, uKrestu Yesu? Ithemba lenu asilo themba lempilo egqibeleleyo, kungabikho kugula nasifo? Ithemba lenu asikokuba ndiya kuzithoba iintshaba zenu phantsi kweenyawo zenu kwaye ndenze nesinagoge kaSathana iqubude ezinyaweni zenu? (Kuba baya kwazi ukuba ndinithandile!) Ithemba lenu asilolo vuyo lwanaphakade phambi kobuso bukaYahweh ngokwakhe? Kuba uya kwenza ikhaya lakhe phakathi kwabantu, kwaye akusayi kubakho bukumkani bohlukene, kodwa uBukumkani bukaThixo kuphela, kuba akukho mkhulu kunoYahweh; hayi, akukho nto inokuthelekiswa nobukhazikhazi bukaThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade, uBawo wethu!

Vuyani ngekamva lenu, bantwana bam, kuba ndininike ikamva nenjongo engcwele kum, iNkosi yenu ekhanyayo! Ithemba lenu asilo themba elikhulu apho nisindiswe kwingqumbo kum? Kuba elenu lelolona themba likhulu, ithemba ekwakuthethwa ngalo sisitya sam esinyuliweyo uMpostile uPawulos, umgcini kanye weMfihlelo Enkulu kaThixo? Kuba ndatyhilela uPawulos into awayibhalela abaseKolose: Kwanembali ebifihliwe kumaphakade, nakwizizukulwana, kodwa ngoku yenziwe yabonakala kubangcwele bakhe, Abo uThixo wayefuna ukubazisa ukuba buyintoni ubutyebi bozuko lwale mfihlelo phakathi kweeNtlanga, enguKrestu kuni, ithemba lozuko. Elenu lithemba elizukileyo elifanele ukumkani, kuba ndiya kunenza ookumkani nababingeleli phambi koThixo wethu; kuba ndingumbingeleli omkhulu ngonaphakade ngokohlobo lukaMelkitsedeke, kwaye akusayi kubakho wumbi.

Bantwana bam abancinci, musani ukoyika ubuxoki botshaba, kuba lunganidinisa ngelize ukuba nje lungakwazi; kodwa ludibene nomlingani walo kum kwaye ndiloyisile ngokupheleleyo, kwaye ndiyeza kutshabalalisa ubukumkani balo, kuba ndathunyelwa ukuba ndichithe imisebenzi kaMtyholi, kwaye ngoko ndinithumile nani! Inyoka yamandulo nabo bonke ubukumkani bayo abanathemba. Bayazi ukuba bagwetyelwe intshabalalo nesohlwayo sanaphakade, apho baya kutsha kweyona mililo ishushu yeblue njengoko bequbha kwisulfure evuthayo ngonaphakade. Banomona ngani kwaye banganikela ngayo nantoni na ukuze babe nina, ngoko musani ukuphulaphula amazwi abo. Ndiveni, Mtshakazi wam; uSathana uya kwenza nantoni na ukunilahlekisa kumvuzo wenu kum, uKrestu Yesu. Uya kwenza nantoni na ukufihla ithemba lenu endininike lona, kwaye ngoko iindlela zakhe kolu luhlu amaxesha amaninzi kukwenza ingcamango ibe cheap ngokwenza umgunyathi wenyaniso nangolwimi lwakhe oluxokayo.

Musani ukumoyika, kodwa yemkani kubuxoki bakhe nigcine ithemba lenu lokwenyani engqondweni, kuba anifundanga na? Umoya ngokwawo uyangqina, kunye nomoya wethu, ukuba singabantwana bakaThixo. Kwaye ukuba singabantwana, ke siziindlalifa, iindlalifa zikaThixo, neendlalifa kunye noKrestu: ukuba kunjalo siva ubunzima kunye naye, ukuze sibe nozuko kunye naye. Kuba ndicinga, ukuba iintlungu zeli xesha langoku, azifanelekanga ukuthelekiswa nozuko oluya kutyhilwa kuthi. Bantwana bam, ukuba nje beninokuqonda ubukhulu bento ekuthetha yona ukuba yindlalifa kunye noKrestu, kodwa niya kwazi kungekudala njengoko naziwa. Aniqondi na ukuba uThixo undinike zonke izinto ezulwini nasemhlabeni, kwaye ilifa lam lelolona lifa likhulu? Bantwana bam, anizazi ngokwenene, kuba ubuni benu bokwenene bufihliwe kum, kwaye xa ndiya kubonakala, nani niya kubonakala kunye nam eluzukweni!

Musani ukoyika, kwaye nihlale niqinile eluthandweni nasekholweni lwenu, kuba ithemba lenu liya kunenza nihambe ngokunyamezela okukhulu; kuba ithemba novuyo lwam uBawo awalubeka phambi kwam lwabangela ukuba ndifeze ntoni? Kuba amaHebhere athi: Sijonge kuYesu uMbhali nomfezekisi wokholo lwethu, owathi ngenxa yovuyo olwalubekwe phambi kwakhe, wanyamezela umnqamlezo, elidela ihlazo, kwaye uhleli ngasekunene kwetrone kaThixo. Andinishiyelanga mzekelo na, kwaye andibanga luhlobo enifanele ukululandela? Aniqondi na? Ukuba lowo ukholwayo kum akayi kwenza kuphela imisebenzi endayifezayo ngexesha lokuza kwam kokuqala, kodwa imisebenzi emikhulu kunaleyo ndayenzayo? Kuba ndiye kuBawo kwaye ndinixhobisile kwaye ndinimele ngamandla am onke, kuba ndinamandla, kuba uThixo undenze njalo ekuthobeleni kwam.

Bantu bam, la mazwi afanele anikhuthaze aninike amandla okuma, kuba ndibeke uvuyo olukhulu phambi kwenu! Musani ukukholelwa kubuxoki botshaba malunga nethemba lenu, kuba lungazama ukunishiya ningenathemba kwaye ninyathelwe phantsi kweenyawo, kwaye nakwiintsilelo zenu nenziwe nanamandla kum, iNkosi yenu uYesu Krestu! Ngoku ndiza kushwankathela ikamva lenu, Mtshakazi wam, ngoko thobelani amazwi omlomo wam, kuba ngamazwi okholo, ngamazwi othando, kwaye amazwi am aya kulivuselela ithemba lenu enyanisweni, kuba ithemba elikhulu lisenyanisweni endininike yona!

Okokuqala, qondani ukuba ndinibizile; ndinisindisile ngomoya wam wamandla nobuntwana; ndinenze isidalwa esitsha kum, uKrestu Yesu! Ngenxa yombingelelo wam njengePasika egqibeleleyo, bonke abakholwayo kum ngokholo olusindisayo oluvela ezintliziyweni zabo baya kusindiswa kwingqumbo kum. Bantwana bam, qondani ukuba la mazwi amangalisa kakhulu kubantu abaninzi, kuba izono zabo zibabetha zibenze bazive benetyala, begwetywa, benehlazo, kwaye oku kwenzeka kuba amaxesha amaninzi bayala ukuzihlambulula egazini lam elihlawulelayo, kwaye bayakutyeshela ukuvuma izono zabo, kwaye bayakutyeshela ubudlelwane babo nam, inkosana yoxolo, kuba usindiso luvela kukholo lobudlelwane kum, iNkosi enkulu!

Uninzi lwenu aluqondi ukuba ndinilungisile, kwaye ngenxa yoko kulungiswa kum, nisindisiwe kwingqumbo, kuba ndaxelela abaseRoma: Ngakumbi ke ngoku, njengoko silungisiwe ngegazi lakhe, siya kusindiswa kwingqumbo ngaye. Ndiphenduleni koku, Mtshakazi wam endiyeza ngenxa yakhe, kutheni ninqwenela usuku lweNkosi? Kutheni niphulaphule abakhonzi bakaSathana abazenza abakhonzi bam xa besithi kufuneka ninyamezele ingxaki kaYakobi nkqu nembandezelo enkulu eyenzeka ngesiqingatha sokugqibela seveki yamashumi asixhenxe kaDaniyeli? Bayanixokisa kwaye niyabakholelwa. Babhenela kwindalo yenu yesono, benixelela ukuba anixabisekanga ngoxa niyiperile yexabiso elikhulu; banixelela ukuba nifanelwe yingqumbo enkulu kum, kwaye bayaxoka! Banixelela ukuba akufuneki nilindele ukuhlangulwa kwimbandezelo, kwaye bacaphula iLizwi lam elingcwele ngokungachanekanga. Kuba ngenyaniso ndaxelela uPawulos oku kulandelayo ukuyalela uTimoti: Iintshutshiso, iimbandezelo ezehla phezu kwam eAntiyokwe, eIkoniyo, eListra, zintshutshiso ezindazinyamezelayo: kodwa kuzo zonke iNkosi yandihlangula. Ewe, kwaye bonke abaya kuphila ngokuhlonela uThixo kuKrestu Yesu, baya kutshutshiswa. Kwaye ngenene, uSathana uya kutshutshisa bonke abaphila ubomi obuhlonela uThixo, kwaye kuzo zonke iintshutshiso zakhe ndiya kunihlangula xa nithe najikela kum kwiingxaki zenu nahlala nithembekile ethembeni lenu. Qondani umahluko, kuba uSathana uwudidekile lo mcimbi: uSathana uya kutshutshisa bonke abaphila ngokuhlonela uThixo, kwaye oko kukutshutshiswa; kwaye amaxesha amaninzi kufuneka ninyamezele ingqumbo yakhe, kuba ekoyisweni kwakhe unomsindo omkhulu. Kodwa musani ukoyika ukugquma kwakhe, kuba ndayalela uPetros, ndisithi: Yibani nobungcathu, nilinde: kuba umchasi wenu umtyholi njengengonyama egqumayo ujikeleza, efuna umntu anokumqwenga: Mchaseni niqinile elukholweni, nisazi ukuba ezo ntlungu ziyafana nezenzeka kubazalwana benu abasehlabathini.

Qondani, bantwana bam, ukuba ingqumbo ezayo ayingqumbo kaSathana kodwa yingqumbo kaThixo nengqumbo yeMvana eyenzelwe uSathana. Kuba uSathana ligwala elirhubuluze kuyo yonke indalo lizama ukuzifihla kwingqumbo eliyiqwebileyo, licinga ukuba lingamoyisa uYahweh kwimicimbi enjalo; kodwa iinjongo zikaBawo zichazwe ngolu hlobo: Mhlekazi, awuhlwayelanga mbewu ilungileyo entsimini yakho? Luvela phi ke ukhula? Wathi kubo, Utshaba lwenze oku. Bathi abakhonzi kuye, Ufuna ke siye kuluhlanganisa? Kodwa wathi, Hayi: hleze ngelixa nihlanganisa ukhula, nincothule nengqolowa kunye nalo. Ziyekeni zombini zikhule kunye kude kube lixesha lokuvuna: kwaye ngexesha lokuvuna, ndiya kuthi kubavuni, Hlanganisani kuqala ukhula, nilubophe ngezithungu ukuze lutshiswe: kodwa hlanganisani ingqolowa kwisisele sam. Ngoko ke ingqolowa iya kuhlanganiswa esiseleni, kodwa ukhula nabo bonke abalutyalayo nabalunkcenkceshelayo baya kuphoswa bephila kwelo chibi likhulu lomlilo nesulfure uBawo wam alilungiselele uMtyholi neengelosi zakhe, kodwa akukho nxalenye yengqumbo kaThixo eyeyenu, nangona uSathana eya kuzama ukuzikhusela ngani kwaye eya kufuna ukunithimba njengexhoba. Musani ukoyika, kuba ingqumbo ayiyeyenu. Ndiyaqonda ukuba imiphefumlo emininzi engazinzanga iyawujija lo mzekeliso, inixelela ukuba niya kunyamezela ingqumbo kunye nabo, kodwa bakwenza oko ngokungahoyi ucwangciso olucacileyo lwesibhalo kulo mcimbi, kwaye bazama ukunimfamekisa ngokucaphula umzekeliso wam ngokungachanekanga.

Qondani ukuba ndiya kunixhwila ndinise emazulwini, kuba ndilungiselele indawo yokhuseleko nomvuzo kuMtshakazi wam; kuba endlwini kaBawo wam ndiya kunifihla kwaye zininzi iindlu. Ngoko ewe, ndiya kunihlanganisela kum ekubonakaleni kwam, okukufuphi ukwenzeka. Kuba ndatsho kwabaseTesalonika: Kuba iNkosi ngokwayo iya kuhla ezulwini ngesimeme, ngelizwi leNgelosi eyiNtloko, nangexilongo likaThixo: kwaye abafileyo kuKrestu baya kuvuka kuqala. Emva koko thina baphilayo, nabasalayo, siya kuthathwa kunye nabo emafini, sihlangabeze iNkosi emoyeni: kwaye ngoko siya kuhlala sineNkosi ngonaphakade. Ngoko ke, thuthuzelanani ngala mazwi. Ndiyanibuza, Mtshakazi wam oxokiselweyo, ngaba ngoku niya kubugatya ubuxoki bukaSathana nibambelele kumazwi am nithuthuzeleke kuwo nithuthuzele abazalwana benu? Kuba kuya kuba njalo kungekudala, kwaye ndikunqwenela ubukho benu, kuba ndinithanda kakhulu, kuba ndinguMyeni wenu.

Nje ukuba nithathwe emhlabeni, ingxaki kaYakobi iya kuqhambuka emhlabeni wonke kwaye intshabalalo enkulu iya kufika ngequbuliso, kodwa ayiyeyenu, njengoko niya kusiwa emazulwini. Kwaye ke niya kuma phambi kwesihlalo sam sokugweba ukuze ndinivuze ngokwemisebenzi yenu. Kwaye kwabathembekileyo kukho umvuzo omkhulu nezithsaba ezininzi, kwaye niya kwambathiswa indlu endinenzele yona, indlu eya kulungiselelwa nina kanye kwaye iqulethe impumelelo yenu emininzi, endiya kuyinika izakhono ezininzi, kuba unjalo umzimba omtsha endinilindele wona. Akukho kugula, akukho ntlungu, akukho lusizi, kwaye akusekho siqalekiso, bantwana bam, kuba isiqalekiso siya kujikela ezintlokweni zabaqalekisiweyo. Kwaye ngoko niya kuba semazulwini kuloo minyaka isixhenxe, kanye iveki yamashumi asixhenxe kaDaniyeli. Kwaye ke, niya kukhwela nam njengoko uya kwenza wonke umkhosi wezulu, kuba kuprofetiwe kwaye kuya kuba njalo: Kwaye ndabona izulu livulekile, kwaye yabona ihashe elimhlophe, kwaye lowo wayehleli phezu kwalo wayebizwa ngokuba nguThembekile noNyaniso, kwaye ngobulungisa uyagweba enze imfazwe. Amehlo akhe ayenjengelangatye lomlilo, kwaye entloko yakhe kwakukho izithsaba ezininzi, kwaye wayenegama elibhaliweyo, elalingaziwa mntu ngaphandle kwakhe. Kwaye wayembethe isambatho esifakwe egazini, kwaye igama lakhe libizwa ngokuba, ILizwi likaThixo. Kwaye imikhosi eyayisezulwini yamlandela kumahashe amhlophe, yambethe ilinen ecikizekileyo, emhlophe necocekileyo.

Bantwana bam, ikamva lenu lelozuko olungenakulinganiswa, kwaye anisoze niswele kwakhona, kuba nizibonakalisile kum, kwaye anifuni kuvavanywa kwakhona; ngoko kutheni kufuneka ninyamezele ingxaki kaYakobi? Kuba injongo yayo kukusindisa uYakobi, kuba intsalela iya kusindiswa. Kodwa qinisekani, abo bakhanyela ukubonakala kwam kwiinzame zabo ezingento zokutshabalalisa ithemba lenu lokwenene, kwanabo balusi balahlekileyo, baya kunyamezela ingxaki kaYakobi, kwaye baya kusindiswa ukuba babiza egameni lam kwaye bathembeke kude kube sekupheleni. Kodwa abayi kuba nandawo etafileni yam, kuba ukukhanyela ukubonakala kwam kukundikhanyela mna, uMyeni, xa ndiza kuMtshakazi wam, kwaye ndikufuphi ukwenza kanye loo nto.

Jongani ukukhanya kokubonakala kwam! Phulaphulani, kuba niya kuliva ilizwi lam nesandi esinamandla sexilongo lokugqibela; kuba nangona ndiyiMvana kaThixo, ndiyeza njengeNgonyama kaYuda, kwaye bonke ubukumkani bukaSathana bubanjwe luloyiko lwam, uKrestu Yesu, kuba amaxesha abo afikile, kwaye amadangatye abaselwe ashushu ngenxa yabo. Kodwa akuyi kusondela kuni, kuba ndinilungiselele umvuzo omkhulu nelifa elikhulu kuni, Mtshakazi wam. Fundani amazwi am kwincwadi enkulu yeSityhilelo, kuba ndiya kunibonisa umboniso wekamva lenu: Kwaye ngeenxa zonke kwiTrone kwakukho izihlalo ezingamashumi amabini anesine, kwaye phezu kwezihlalo ndabona amadoda amakhulu angamashumi amabini anesine ehleli, embethe iingubo ezimhlophe, kwaye babenezithsaba zegolide ezintlokweni zabo. (kwaye kwakhona) Kwaye xa ezo zilo zinika uzuko, nembeko, nombulelo kulowo uhleli eTroneni, ophila ngonaphakade kanaphakade, Amadoda amakhulu angamashumi amabini anesine awa phambi kwalowo uhleli eTroneni, aze anqule lowo uphila ngonaphakade kanaphakade, aze aphose izithsaba zawo phambi kweTrone, esithi, Ufanelekile, Nkosi, ukwamkela uzuko, nembeko, namandla: kuba wena wadala zonke izinto, kwaye ngenxa yentando yakho zikhona, kwaye zadalwa.

Bantwana bam, musani ukoyika ikamva lenu, kodwa jongani kum ngolindelo olukhulu, kuba kungekudala niya kuba semazulwini niphosa izithsaba zenu phambi kwetrone kaThixo ophezukonke!

Ndingumgwebi olilungisa kwaye kungekudala niya kufumana umvuzo omkhulu kwiBema yam.
