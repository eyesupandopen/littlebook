INdibaniso Enamandla Yabathembekileyo KuKristu

Yivani amazwi eNkosi yenu engunaPhakade, bandla lam, mzimba wam, nkqu nomtshakazi wam, nithobe iindlebe zenu ngokuthobela amazwi kaYeshua HaMashiach. Yivani ilizwi lam nize eNkosini yenu ngeentliziyo ezilungele ukwamkela amazwi am, ukuze intuthuzelo ifike phezu kwenu ngokupheleleyo, kuba amaxesha entuthuzelo nenkululeko epheleleyo aphezu kwenu zintanda zam, ngoko ke lungani!

Njengoko kunjalo, mna Yesu waseNazarete, kwanalowo ukwanguYeshua HaMashiach ngolwimi lwesiHebhere, ndiza kunilungisa zintanda zam kuba alisekho ixesha kwaye indibaniso yabathembekileyo kuKristu imiselwe ukuqala ngesiquphe nangokungalindelekanga ngoku. Ngoko musani ukoyika zintanda zam, niqonde ukuba izigwebo zabo bayalileyo izilumkiso zam azingozenu, kwaye niya kusindiswa ngokupheleleyo kwingqumbo ezayo! Inene, inene, ngenxa yokuba nikholiwe namkela usindiso noxolelo lwezono ekuzalweni okutsha ngokweTestamente yam eNtsha ngamaxesha obabalo nenceba, nisindiswe ngokupheleleyo kwingqumbo ezayo!

Noko ke, kunjalo, ukuba imiphefumlo emininzi engazinzanga nenganyanisekanga ethetha ubuxoki ngohanahaniso kwaye igqwetha amazwi am ukuze izigwebe ize itshatshaliswe, ethi ngobuqhetseba nangokuzidla kwenyama ibange ukuba ingamandla amakhulu kaYahweh, ize ilahlekise bonke abeva nabathobela ubuxoki bayo obuthethwa ngeentliziyo ezinobuqhophololo. Ngoko ke, ziqondeni izithembiso zam endazityhilela uPawulos xa ndandimfundisa iMfihlelo eNkulu kaThixo etyhilwe ngokupheleleyo kwiincwadi zakhe, imfihlelo leyo uninzi lwenu eningekayiqondi. Nangona kunjalo, wangqina waza wanikela ubungqina ngengqumbo kaYahweh: Kodwa uThixo ubonakalisa uthando lwakhe kuthi, ekubeni sisengaboni, uKristu wasifela. Ngakumbi ke ngoko, njengoko ngoku sigwetyelwe ngokuphuma kwegazi lakhe, siya kusindiswa kwingqumbo ngaye. Kuba ukuba xa sasiyintshaba, saxolelaniswa noThixo ngokufa koNyana wakhe, kokukhona ke, njengoko sixolelanisiwe, siya kusindiswa ngobomi bakhe!

Owu, nina bokholo oluncinane, ndiya kuninyamezela kude kube nini na, zintanda zam? Ngoko ke ndiza kuninika intuthuzelo kuba andisokuze ndinishiye okanye ndinilahle, nonke nina nikuKristu, kwaye ngenene ndiya kuninyamezela ngonaphakade kuba niya kuba nam ngonaphakade kwaye niya kufana nam ngonaphakade. Selah.

Ngoko ke, qondani ukuba bonke abaphila ngokuhlonela uThixo baya kutshutshiswa ngandlela ithile, kwaye loo ngonyama ingenamazinyo isazulazula ifuna umntu enokumqwenga, ngoko ke yichaseni ngokupheleleyo kwaye iya kubaleka. Nangona kunjalo, amasango esihogo akayi kuloyisa iBandla lam okanye abathembekileyo eBandleni lam abathobela ilizwi lam nabathi mna ndibanike amandla ngamandla ngomoya wam woBunyana owadalwa ngaphakathi kwabo bonke abandenze iNkosi ngentliziyo ezele lukholo, kwaye nitywinwe ngetywina likaThixo ongunaPhakade woBulungisa, uYahweh Omkhulu onguMNA NDINGUYE, kwaye elo tywina alinakwaphulwa kwaye aninakuxhwilwa ezandleni zam, nangona bexoka!

Ngoko ke, qondani ukuba nisindisiwe kwingqumbo, kwaye nisindisiwe kuyo yonke ingqumbo. Ngoko qondani ukuba ingqumbo yeMvana ayenzelwanga abathobelayo, kodwa yeyokuqalisa umgwebo wokugqibela kwabawileyo nabo bonke abazimanya ngokupheleleyo okanye abahlala nabo, nokuzisa usindiso lokugqibela kwabangathobeliyo nabanenkani abaya kuthobela iVangeli engunaPhakade ngamaxesha abo enkxwaleko nosizi kuba bazityeshela izilumkiso zam ngamaxesha esilumkiso asele ephelile ngoku, kwaye endaweni yezilumkiso, ngoku kumi izibhengezo!! Noko ke, bonke abathembekileyo kuKristu, nkqu nabo bonke abamkelwe ngokupheleleyo njengooNyana bakaYahweh abangunaPhakade kwaye ngoku bamiliselwe kumthi womnquma wemvelo kaSirayeli kaThixo apho kuhlala khona konke ukuzala abangaphantsi kwengqumbo yeMvana, kwanengqumbo kaYahweh, Selah!

Kuba ingqumbo yeMvana iza kuqala kwaye iya kuwa kanzima kakhulu kwabangathobeliyo nabangathembekanga bendlu kaThixo, nokuba bangabakaSirayeli okanye beentlanga. Kuba le yinceba enikwe ngesithembiso kwiminqophiso yakudala esathenjisiweyo kwaye imiselwe ukuzalisekiswa ngonaphakade nangonaphakade kwiTestamente yam eNtsha yokuzalisekiswa okupheleleyo! Ingqumbo yeMvana iza xa ndikhulula amatywina kumsongo wam welifa, isenzo somhlaba nkqu nelifa lam elinamandla endalinikwe ezandleni zam ezinamandla nguYahweh ngokwakhe ukuze ndabelane naboyisi bam kuba kuzo zonke izinto ndoyisile! Amangqina amabini aya kuvuka ngexesha lawo kwaye aya kwenza imimangaliso emikhulu nenamandla, kwaye aya kutyhila izityhilelo ezininzi, kwaye aya kungqina ubungqina obuninzi bakudala ngamaxesha awo amisiweyo okutyhila, kwaye aya kuyalela izibetho ezininzi, imimangaliso, imiqondiso, nemisebenzi engummangaliso ngamazwi nemiyalelo yeentliziyo zawo ezizaliswe lukholo nemilomo yawo enamandla! Selah.

Kananjalo, ngeli xesha kunikwe inamba, irhamncwa eliphuma elwandle, nomprofeti wobuxoki ukuba alwe nabangcwele aze aboyise. Noko ke, abo bangabathandiyo ubomi babo kude kube sekupheleni nabo bonke abanqula igama likaYesu kude kube sekupheleni kwabo baya kufumana usindiso olungunaphakade nelifa elikhulu elingunaphakade elinobunzima obungafanelekanga ukuthelekiswa neentshukumo zabo abaya kuzinyamezela ngenxa yegama lam. Kwaye mna, Yeshua HaMashiach, ndiya kukhokela ama-144,000 am anamandla ukuhlangula uYakobi, loo ntsalela inamandla eya kundamkela njengoMesiya wabo, bonke abavela kwaSirayeli abayinzala yokwenene yesithembiso kwaye baya kwahlulwa bathathwe bephila bayiswe entlango kwindawo abayilungiselelweyo nguYahweh apho baya kuhlala befihliwe ngokukhuselekileyo kwimbandezelo enkulu eyakha yehlela umhlaba, kwaye ngenene amaxesha enkathazo akasayi kuphinda awufikele umhlaba, kuba utshilo uYahweh, Omkhulu onguMNA NDINGUYE!

Ngoko ke, waqondeni amazwi am nize niphulaphule ilizwi lam zintanda zam, niqonde ukuba nisindisiwe kwingqumbo ezayo, nangona nisemhlabeni nonke kwaye nisalwa iimfazwe ezininzi eziqhubekayo kula maxesha okugqibela emfazwe yembewu yeGenesis 3:15 esaqhubekayo unanamhla phakathi koYahweh, namazulu, noosathana abathimbe abantu abakhohlakeleyo emhlabeni ukuze benze umsebenzi wabo ngokuthanda imali nangezithembiso ezininzi zobuxoki ezikhokelela ekugqibeleni kwichibi lomlilo apho impethu yabo ingafiyo. Nangona kunjalo, ndiboyisile kwaye zonke iimfazwe ziya kuphela ngamandla amakhulu nesandla esingqongqo seNgonyama yesizwe sakwaYuda, xa ndisiza ngobulungisa ukuze ndigwebe ndenze imfazwe! Selah.

Ewe, nisindisiwe kwingqumbo kwaye amasango esihogo akayi kunoyisa! Ngoko ke, zibonakaliseni kuKristu Yesu iNkosi yenu engunaphakade, kwanalowo unihlanguleyo esandleni lotshaba, ewe zibonakaliseni eNkosini yenu kuba amaxesha okuboniswa kwenu ezulwini asondele. Lixesha lesithembiso esingunaphakade esifihlwe entliziyweni kaYahweh esele sityhiliwe ngoku ngokupheleleyo, lixesha apho isithembiso esingunaphakade kunye nokulindela kudibana nokuzalisekiswa nokuboniswa kuba, mna, Kristu Yesu, nkqu neNkosi yenu engunaPhakade yoBulungisa ndiya kubonisa abathembekileyo kuKristu kuYahweh, Omkhulu onguMNA NDINGUYE, kwigumbi lakhe letroni ezulwini lamazulu.

Kwaye NDIYA kubonisa abathobelayo, bengenabala okanye isiphako, okanye ibala, okanye nayiphi na into enjalo kodwa ndiya kunambathisa nonke ngelinen emhlophe ecocekileyo, engenabala kwaye ziya kuhlala zinjalo izindlu zenu ngonaphakade, kuba izambatho, iimpahla, nezindlu zonke zibhekisa kwimizimba yenu evusiweyo negqibeleleyo eya kuthi ngesiquphe ngoku nizifumane nimi kuyo ngonaphakade xa ndidala ngokutsha abathembekileyo kuKristu ngokuqhwanyaza kweliso kuloo mkhwazo mkhulu weNgelosi eyiNtloko naloo mculo mnandi wexilongo lokugqibela, kupheliswe ngokusesikweni amaxesha am oBabalo nenceba, oko kuya kuphawula ukuphela okupheleleyo kwamaxesha eentlanga, nangona, bonke abanqula igama likaYesu waseNazarete kude kube sekupheleni baya kusindiswa ngokupheleleyo.

Kwaye ke ndiza kubhenca abo banganikhathazayo, nkqu nabo bonke enisabeka iindlebe kumazwi abo anomhlaza, amazwi obuxoki anikwe ngenkohliso kwiintliziyo ezimbi ezilawulwa ziinkanuko zenyama yabo. Nkqu nabo bagqwetha amazwi acocekileyo evangeli yam ngeentsomi namabali angangcwele alubuxoki, enzelwe ngokupheleleyo ukutshabalalisa bonke abanokukholelwa kula mazwi angenalukholo. Zintanda zam, gxininisani ingqwalasela yenu epheleleyo kum nakwiLizwi lam kuba ndim uzinzo lwamaxesha enu namandla osindiso, kwaye ngubani na onokumelana neNgonyama yesizwe sakwaYuda!?

Yabonani! Ndiwakhusele amazwi am acocekileyo kumgca weMibhalo yaseByzantine kwaye mna Yeshua HaMashiach, kunye noAdonai Yahweh, siwatyhilile amazwi ethu kwiVangeli engunaPhakade, nkqu nakuloo Ncwadi incinci yoBulungisa eye yamisa kwakhona iinyaniso ezininzi phakathi kwabathembekileyo kuKristu, ngoko ke, thelekisani ngenkuthalo ilizwi ngalinye elivela kulo naliphi na iqumrhu elithi lithethela uYeshua HaMashiach, okanye uYahweh, Omkhulu onguMNA NDINGUYE kumazwi am acocekileyo aqulethwe esibhalweni nize nifunde ukuze nizibonakalise nivunyiwe!

Kuba nasiphi na isiprofeto esakha sanikwa nguYeshua HaMashiach okanye uAdonai Yahweh, asisayi kuphikisana neenyaniso ezimisiweyo ezatyhilwa kumaxesha amandulo okanye kumaxesha angoku. Zichaseni iinyaniso zabo nize niqonde ukuba abathembekileyo kuKristu abayi kunyamezela ubumnyama obukhulu njengezibetho zaseYiputa, kwaye abayi kubakho emhlabeni xa kuvulwa inzonzobila, kwaye neBandla lam aliyi koyiswa ngamasango esihogo, ukuze bazimele emakhayeni abo njengamagwala angenamandla phezu kobubi! Qondani, unyeliso lwabo bonke abathetha ezi zinto aluyi kushiywa lungavuzwanga kubo bonke abalayo inguquko kubo ububhanxa obunjalo.

Qondani ngakumbi, ama-144,000 anyulwe nguYahweh phambi kokusekwa kwehlabathi ngexesha elinje. Bakhandwe embandezelweni kwaye bonke banezakhono namandla awodwa. Baya kuthathwa ekuxwilweni kwabathembekileyo kodwa babekelwe bucala iinjongo zikaYeshua HaMashiach kwaye baya kutywinwa ngokufanelekileyo emabunzini abo emazulwini. Baza kuza kamva emhlabeni ngexesha lembandezelo enkulu ukuze bakhokelele uSirayeli kukhuseleko. Kodwa kuqala bathathwa ukuze batshate noMyeni wabo kuba banguMtshakazi wam, ababiziweyo, abanyuliweyo, abathembekileyo, nabanyanisekileyo. Nkqu nabo mna, Kristu Yesu, ndibenze banyulu njengentombi enyulu egazini lam lentlawulelo. Ngoko ke, nabani na othetha ngokuchasene nala mazwi akandazi, kwaye akathetheli mna! Selah!

Ngoko ke qondani, ukuba amasango esihogo akayi kuboyisa abathembekileyo kuKristu abaliBandla lam lokwenene kuba kubhaliwe kwathenjiswa njalo mandulo, kwaye kusabhaliwe kwathenjiswa kumaxesha angoku, kuba amazwi kaYahweh noYeshua HaMashiach ngamazwi acocekileyo anjengesilivere, evavanywe ezikweni lomhlaba, esulungekiswe kasixhenxe. Ewe, abawileyo abanamandla ngokuchasene nabathembekileyo kuKristu kwaye ndiya kubangela ukuba abathembekileyo bam banyathele phantsi kweenyawo zabo nayiphi na inkalo yobukumkani beentshaba, nkqu nokunyathela abo bafuna ukunqanda okunganqandekiyo.

Ngoko ke, ndiyaniyalela ukuba nibe nokholo kwiNkosi yenu engunaPhakade nize nivumele uThixo ongunaPhakade woBulungisa anifake amandla angaphakathi ebusweni beNkosi yenu, uKristu Yesu! Hlalani ninokholo olukhulu kuYeshua HaMashiach, onguYesu waseNazarete kuba ndininikile amandla ukuze ninyathele phantsi kweenyawo zonke iinkalo zobukumkani beentshaba. Ngoko ke, qondani, ukuba neengelosi eziwileyo ziphantsi kweenyawo zam zoyisiwe ngokupheleleyo, kwaye ukuba nithe nadibana noSathana ngokwakhe kuya kufuneka niphendule njengoko waphendulayo uMikayeli xa wayephikisana ngomzimba kaMoses, esithi ngokholo olunamandla, INkosi mayikukhalimele Sathana! Kwaye ndiya kukhalimela, ndibophe nabani na kwabawileyo onokuzama ukunenzakalisa okanye anithintele ekwenzeni ukuthanda kwalowo unithumileyo, ukuba niyaqhubeka nihamba ngokuthembeka elukholweni njengoko nithetha amazwi endiya kuninika wona elukholweni.

Kwaye ke ndiyaniyalela zintanda zam ukuba nizilungiselele, kuba ukumka kwenu kusondele! Kwaye ndiza kunilumkisa ukuba nihlale nahlukile kwiBhabheli eyiMfihlo kuzo zonke izinto nangeendlela ezininzi endiya kunikhokelela kuzo ukuze ningcwaliswe nahlulwe. Thobelani amazwi eVangeli engunaPhakade nize nivumele yonke inyaniso yam etyhiliweyo kula maxesha okutyhila ibe sisibane ezinyaweni zenu ukuze indlela yenu ikhanyiselwe, kuba inye kuphela indlela, loo ndlela imxinwa necukileyo ekhokelela kubomi obungunaphakade kuKristu Yesu iNkosi yenu engunaphakade!

Zilungiseleleni, mtshakazi wam, nithenge ioli kum ukuze izibane zenu zizale! Ioli yam ifumaneka emazwini am sithandwa sam, kwakula mazwi niwafundayo ngoku kwaye ndininike intlaninge yamazwi okuthuthuzela, okugcina, nokunikhokelela kubo bonke ubulungisa apho lowo ungendawo anganichukumisiyo!

Zilungiseleleni, zintanda zam, kuba lihlala lixesha lokuthena iintambo zezibane zenu, kwaye niya kuthena iintambo zenu ngokuguquka ezonweni nokuvelisa isiqhamo somoya njengoko noyisa imisebenzi yenyama ngomoya wam oyingcwele ozelwe ngaphakathi kwenu ngokupheleleyo, kwaye ongenakuze ususwe xa sele uzinzile ezintliziyweni zabantu ngobungcwele nobulungisa bonke. Gcinani iintliziyo zenu zithambile eluthandweni lwam nakwimiyalelo yam, ukuze nigcinwe nikhuselekile ngokupheleleyo kuko konke okubi entendeni yezandla zam ezingunaphakade.

Zilungiseleleni, O' Bathembekileyo kuKristu, nize niqonde ukusondela kweyure yokugqibela xa kusenzeka indibaniso yabathembekileyo kuKristu, enixhwila nonke nikuse kukhuseleko olupheleleyo nomvuzo wamazulu osele uza kugubungela umhlaba ekubonakaleni kwam okuzukileyo. Njengoko ubuqaqawuli bam bukhanya njengombane ovela empuma uye entshona, uninzi lwenu luya kububona ubuqaqawuli bokufika kwam.

Ke, inene, inene, xa umkhwazo omkhulu weNgelosi eyiNtloko uqhekeka ehlabathini lonke, njengoko loo mculo mkhulu wexilongo uvakaliswa emhlabeni wonke, abafileyo kuKristu baya kuzalwa bephuma emhlabeni ngamandla amakhulu; ke nina baphilayo nabasalayo niya kuxwilelwa phezulu kunye nabo ukuze nihlangabeze iNkosi yenu engunaPhakade yoBulungisa esibhakabhakeni, kwaye niya kuzifumana kwangoko nambethe uzuko olupheleleyo nendalo eqaqambileyo kakhulu, nimi ezulwini lamazulu. Niya kubona ngokupheleleyo, niya kuva ngokupheleleyo, niya kwazi njengoko naziwa, kwaye uninzi lwenu luya kuphinda luhlangane nabathandekayo benu kwaye bonke baya kuvuya!

Nina baphilayo nabasalayo niya kusiqonda isiganeko enizifumana nisesiquphe kuso kwaye niya kuva amazwi athi "Nyukelani apha!" kwaye niya kuziva nitsalwa ngamandla nangokukhawuleza phezulu ekuqaqambeni kobuqaqawuli bam kwaye iintlungu zokubeleka ziya kuphela njengoko abantwana bakaYahweh abangunaPhakade bezalelwa emazulwini apho baya kuma kubuqaqawuli obukhulu nomvuzo ongunaphakade ngokokuthembeka kwabo.

Ndim, Kristu Yesu, iNkosi engunaPhakade yoBulungisa, othumele la mazwi ngesithunywa sam esinamandla nomkhonzi wam, kwaye ngesithunywa sam esinamandla nomkhonzi wam athunyelwe amazwi am, ukuze abathembekileyo kuKristu balunge ngokupheleleyo balindele ukusondela koMyeni wabo, osondelayo ngoku kumtshakazi wakhe! Selah.

Zilungiseleleni, O' Bathembekileyo kuKristu, kuba iyure yenkululeko yenu epheleleyo sele iza kunithatha ngesiquphe nangokungalindelekanga kuzo zonke iinkathazo eziya kuwela emhlabeni ngomgwebo omkhulu nowoyikekayo kuba amaxesha omgwebo afikile kwaye kufuneka azalisekiswe, zahluleni ngokupheleleyo kuzo zonke izinto ngelizwi nangesenzo kwabangakholwayo ukuze ningabelani ngezigwebo zabo, okanye nabelane ngezibetho zabo.

Zilungiseleleni, Mtshakazi wam, nize nize kum ngokupheleleyo ngenguquko epheleleyo nize nimke kuko konke okubi ukuze izibane zenu zizale neentambo zenu zithenwe kwaye nilungiselelwe uMyeni wenu osondelayo ngokuqinisekileyo kwaye iyure yohambo lwam isondele ekugqityweni! Selah!

Kwibandla lam, umzimba wam, nkqu nomtshakazi wam, ndithi amaxesha enu enkohlakalo epheleleyo afikile, ngoko ke, ndilindeni nibe nilungile kwaye nibambelele kubungqina endibunikileyo kuba obu bungqina buya kunixhasa bunigcine kulowo ungendawo oza ngokuchasene nani ngentiyo enkulu nengqumbo engeyiyo eyenu. Qondani, ukuba ndiyabakhalimela ndibathintele abawileyo ngaphezu kokuba nabani na kuni eqonda kwaye bathintelwe de umthinteli wam asuswe. Ngoko ke, zigcineni nahlukile kwizihlwele ezigwetyiweyo kwaye ningathathi nxaxheba kwiindibano zabo kuba sele bezisa isiphithiphithi nentshabalaliso kwabo baya kunikwa ngokupheleleyo umgwebo ngesiquphe njengoko umthinteli wam esuswa. Ngoko ke, musani ukoyika izinto abazithethayo, ningoyiki nezinto abazenzayo kwaye ningoyiki nezinto abazicebayo nabaziqhayisayo ukuba baya kuzenza ngokuqinisekileyo, kuba ezi zinto azingozozabathembekileyo kuKristu, ngoko ke, musani ukuboyika.

Ngubani onokumelana neNgonyama yesizwe sakwaYuda nengqumbo epheleleyo namandla am!? Yivani amazwi am nindive kakuhle, nonke nina bawileyo ukusuka komncinane kuye komkhulu. Mna, Yesu uKristu, nkqu noKristu Yesu, ndingulowo ubhengeza etroneni nasemandleni kaYahweh, Omkhulu onguMNA NDINGUYE, ukuba ndingulowo uzayo, kwaye ngokuqinisekileyo ndiya kuthatha ngokupheleleyo bonke abahlangulwe ngokupheleleyo ngegazi lam lentlawulelo kwaye batywinwe ngetywina likaThixo ophilayo, uYahweh, Omkhulu onguMNA NDINGUYE, kwaye amaxesha abo afikile. Ngoko ke, andiyi kwaliwa kwaye iinzame zenu zokundichasa ziya kuwa ngokupheleleyo phezu kweentloko zabo nibathumayo ukuba bazame. Ngubani na phakathi kwenu onokumelana namandla am!? Ngubani?!

Ukuthanda kukaYahweh, Omkhulu onguMNA NDINGUYE makwenzeke ngonaphakade, kanye, konke nangonaphakade! Yabonani, ndiyasondela, kwaye bonke abafuna ukuthintela baya koyiswa ngokupheleleyo ngomgwebo abangayi kubaleka kuwo, ngonaphakade nangonaphakade. Selah!
