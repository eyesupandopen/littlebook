Kuma-144,000: Lahlanini Ubuxoki Babo

Thobelani indlebe zenu kwilizwi lam nive ubulumko bam, kuba ubulumko bukhala kakhulu ezitratweni: niya kuliva na ilizwi lam? NdinguYeshua HaMashiach, unyana kaYahweh, oweza esenyameni, ndikwayimvana kaThixo esusa isono sehlabathi. Qondani ukuba ihlabathi eniphila kulo asikokubonakaliswa kobuxoki abafundisi-buchule benu abangamaJesuit abakupeyintela lona ukuze banilahlekise. Uninzi lwenu lufundiswe ubuxoki bamaJesuit, kwanokufunda ngokuchasene nokufunda. Le mfazwe nxamnye nenyaniso yayiyilelwe ukubumba iingqondo zabantu ukuba zithobele uSathana kwaye izama ukususa nokuthimba inkululeko yokuzikhethela yomntu ukuze umntu alandele ngokungenangqondo imfundiso yakhe.

Imbali yokwenyani ayinakuqondwa kubuxoki beefilosofa ezingamaJesuit, kuba zingamaxoki athengisa ubuxoki ngenkohliso enkulu. Isibhalo sicacile ngalo mbandela, kwaye eyabaseRoma iyayibhengeza inyaniso ngokucacileyo: Kuba ingqumbo kaThixo iyatyhilwa ivela ezulwini nxamnye nako konke ukungahloneli Thixo nokungalungisi kwabantu, abayibambezelayo inyaniso ngokungalungisi.

Ngaba uwavumele la mazwi afikelele entliziyweni yakho? Abantu bayayibambezela ngabom inyaniso ngokungahloneli Thixo nangokungalungisi, kwaye ingqumbo kaYahweh inxamnye nabo benjenjalo. Ngoko ke iimfundo zenu zenziwe ezingeyonyani bubuxoki babo.

Oku bakwenzele ukulungiselela indlela yenkohliso enkulu ezayo, ukuze ubukumkani bukaSathana bukhululwe ukuze bulawule ngenkohliso enkulu nangobuxoki. Ubuxoki abanixelele bona kwezemfundo busebenzela ukumfamekisa isininzi kwinyaniso: Azikho izidalwa zasemzini, zizithunywa zezulu eziwileyo kuphela ezisingise kwichibi lomlilo ukuze zibotshwe zitshiswe ngonaphakade. Ningakholwa bubuxoki babo nize nibalekele kum, uKristu Yesu, kuba kum ihleli yonke inyaniso eya kunikhulula. Ndinixelela ezi zinto ukuze ningakhohliswa kunye nabo baya kuzimanya noSathana bamkele uphawu lwerhamncwa oluzayo.

Imbali iqondwa ngokulandela iinyaniso ezimiselweyo esiBhalweni nakwiincwadi ezingaphandle kweBhayibhile, u-1 Enoki, uYashere, iiJubili, njl.njl., ukusukela ekuqaleni. Kuba aba bantu bangahloneli Thixo kunye nobuxoki ababusebenzisayo ukufihla inyaniso bayinxalenye nje yemfazwe enkulu, imfazwe yembewu phakathi koYahweh noSathana eqhubekayo kude kube ngulo mzuzu, kodwa ephunyelelwe ngonaphakade yimisebenzi yam yokucamagushela. Kuba imfazwe yabhengezwa nguYahweh kuloo nyoka indala ngenxa yemvukelo yayo, ukususela ekuqaleni kokudalwa koluntu xa uSathana wazama ukutshabalalisa uAdam noEva. UYahweh wathi kwinyoka kwisibhengezo sakhe: Ndiya kubeka ubutshaba phakathi kwakho nomfazi, naphakathi kwembewu yakho nembewu yakhe: yona iya kukutyumza intloko, wena uya kuyityumza isithende. Kwaye ke iimfazwe zembewu ziye zaqhuba ukususela ngoko.

EGadini yase-Eden, uSathana wakwazi ukonakalisa imbewu ka-Adam, wamgwebela uAdam phantsi kwamandla nokonakala kwesono. (Kule nto uNyana kaThixo wabonakala, ukuze ayichithe imisebenzi kaMtyholi.) Kuba uSathana wasebenzisa ulwazi lwakhe olonakeleyo lokulungileyo nokubi ukuze alonakalise ngokupheleleyo uluntu, kwaye kwabo bathobela ilizwi lam, olo konakala luyachithwa kum, uKristu Yesu, eninguye kuye isidalwa esitsha esingenakuze sonakaliswe. Imbewu yam ayinakonakaliswa, kuba uYahweh uyenze yangonakali, kwaye ngendlela yokuzalwa ngokutsha ndibatywinile abanyulwa bam nabathembekileyo kwanangalo moya ungcwele wesithembiso!

UYohane kwileta yakhe yokuqala ubhengeza inyaniso engunaphakade: Lowo uzelwe nguThixo akenzi sono; ngokuba imbewu yakhe ihleli kuye, yaye akanakona, ngenxa yokuba ezelwe nguThixo.

Kwixesha elifutshane emva kokuwa kwe-Eden, apho uAdam wona wawa ukusuka ekugqibeleleni waya ekonakaleni, abalindi behla kwiNtaba yeHermon ngemihla kaYerede. Kwaye bonke bazibophelela ngeziqalekiso ezifanayo, kwanangesifungo sabo esingcwele abazibophelela ngaso ngonaphakade esonweni nakwisohlwayo esingunaphakade. Benjenjalo ukuze bonke bazithabathele abafazi kubo bonke ababakhethayo, ngaloo ndlela batshintshe ucwangco lwendalo yabo.

Beza eluntwini benikela ngolwazi lwabo lwezithunywa zezulu eziwileyo olubolileyo, kuba bazimanya nemvukelo kaSathana.

Bafundisa abantu ukwenza amakrele, neemela, namakhaka, nezixhobo zokuzikhusela esifubeni, babazisa iintsimbi zomhlaba nobugcisa bokuzisebenza, nezacholo, nezihombiso, nokusetyenziswa kwe-antimoni, nokuhonjiswa kweenkophe, nazo zonke iintlobo zamatye anqabileyo, nazo zonke iidayi ezinemibala. Bafundisa ukukhafula, nokusika iingcambu, ukusombulula ukukhafula, ukuvumisa ngeenkwenkwezi, amakroza eenkwenkwezi, ulwazi lwamafu, imiqondiso yomhlaba, imiqondiso yelanga, kunye nokuhamba kwenyanga.

Ngoko abalindi abawileyo bazithabathela abafazi babo baza bahenyuza nabo, baza bakhawula bazala kubo iingxilimbela ezinkulu. Ekuhambeni kwexesha le mbewu ingcwele yadla konke okwakusemhlabeni, nako konke okwakunikiwe uluntu, yaza ekugqibeleni yadla uluntu oluninzi kangangoko yayinako. Kuba babengabaseli begazi nabatyi babantu, yaye benza ububi obukhulu emehlweni kaYahweh, kuba ubomi benyama busegazini kwaye uluntu lwenziwa ngokomfanekiso kaYahweh. Bakhula banda baza ekugqibeleni bamisa ubukumkani babo kuwo wonke umhlaba. Khangela! Amanxuwa ezakhiwo zabo ezinkulu kakhulu asawugcwele umhlaba njengobungqina obuchasene nabo. Babewulawula umhlaba ngaloo maxesha, yaye babenzelwe intshabalalo yoluntu. Kwaye ke zonke iintlobo zamasikizi zafezwa, kuba izibhalo ezingcwele zide zibhengeze indlela okwenziwa ngayo.

Ngokungathi kwakunganelanga ukuba uluntu lunkanuke inyama yasemzini, luhenyuze nezithunywa zezulu eziwileyo, luze luvelise inzala yeengxilimbela, ngenxa yabalindi bonke bona nxamnye neentaka, namarhamncwa, nezirhubuluzi, neentlanzi. Ukususela ekuqaleni kwendalo, uYahweh wamisa uhlobo ngalunye lobomi ngokohlobo lwalo. Kodwa abalindi abakhohlakeleyo baphazamisa indalo, bade badibanisa izidalwa zohlobo olunye nezidalwa zohlobo olufanayo ukuze bazise ukungazinzi kofuzo baze batshintshe indalo yokuqala kaYahweh yokugqibelela okuvisisanayo. Le nkqubo yaphindwa, kudityaniswa izidalwa ezifanayo zohlobo olunye nezidalwa ezifanayo zolunye uhlobo de kwafikelelwa kwisiphumo esinqwenelekayo. Kwaye ke ihlabathi elalikhona ngoko, lonakaliswa ngazo zonke iintlobo zamasikizi anobubi awona kakhulu nxamnye noYahweh, uluntu, nayo yonke indalo. Qonda, abawileyo bafuna ihlabathi labo, ihlabathi lobumnyama elahlulwe ngokupheleleyo kuYahweh, kwaye baya kulidla ilifa kanye elo kwichibi lomlilo abalilungiselelwe nguYahweh ngokwakhe.

Yaza ke yafika imihla kaNowa kwaye ihlabathi elalikhona ngoko, limi ngaphandle kwamanzi nasemanzini, lakhukuliswa ngamanzi laza latshabalala. Kuba imithombo yenzonzobila enkulu yagqabhuka, yabangela ugonyamelo olukhulu njengoko umhlaba wawuqhekeka ngamandla anobundlobongela amanzi anogonyamelo, kwaye iifestile zezulu zavulwa, zavumela amanzi avela ngaphezulu kwesibhakabhaka (naphantsi komhlaba) ukuba agubungele aze atshabalalise ihlabathi lonke.

Kwaye ke uEnoki umbhali uchaza isiphelo sezi ngxilimbela ngolu hlobo, kuba uYahweh watsho njalo: Kwaye ngoku, iingxilimbela, eziveliswe yimimoya nenyama, ziya kubizwa ngokuba yimimoya emibi emhlabeni, kwaye emhlabeni kuya kuba yindawo yazo yokuhlala. Imimoya emibi iphume emizimbeni yazo; ngenxa yokuba zizalwe ngabantu, kubalindi abangcwele yimvelaphi yazo yokuqala; ziya kuba yimimoya emibi emhlabeni, kwaye ziya kubizwa ngokuba yimimoya emibi. Ngokubhekiselele kwimimoya yezulu, ezulwini kuya kuba yindawo yayo yokuhlala, kodwa ngokubhekiselele kwimimoya yomhlaba eyazalelwa emhlabeni, emhlabeni kuya kuba yindawo yayo yokuhlala. Kwaye imimoya yeengxilimbela iyaxhwalekisa, icinezele, itshabalalise, ihlasele, ilwe, kwaye isebenze intshabalalo emhlabeni, kwaye ibangele inkathazo: ayithathi kutya, kodwa nangona kunjalo iyalamba inxanelwe, kwaye ibangele izikhubekiso. Kwaye le mimoya iya kuvukela abantwana babantu nabafazi, ngenxa yokuba iphume kubo. Qonda ukuba isithoba kwishumi sale mimoya mibi sisenzonzobileni ngenxa yesibongozo nomthandazo kaNowa, kwaye uluntu luye lwaqhubana nesinye kwishumi kuphela ukususela ngoko. Oko sele kuza kutshintsha xa inzonzobila iza kuvulwa kwaye yonke into iya kukhululwa.

Ngoko umhlaba, nawo onke amasikizi abalindi, watshatyalaliswa. Abalindi ngokwabo bakhonkxwa kwizigwebo zabo ezahlukeneyo kangangezizukulwana ezingamashumi asixhenxe, ukuze bakhululwe ukuze bagwetywe ngexesha lomgwebo omkhulu. (Kuba umgwebo omkhulu sele uza kuqala.) Nangona kunjalo, uNowa wafumana ubabalo emehlweni kaYahweh ngenxa yokuba wayeyindoda elilungisa, kwaye usapho lwakhe uYahweh walusindisa luphila, imiphefumlo esibhozo iyonke, kunye nezinye izidalwa ezininzi eMkhombeni. UNowa wazigcina yena nosapho lwakhe ekuguqulweni kofuzo ngaphambi konogumbe. Kodwa unyana wakhe uHam nenzala yakhe ekhohlakeleyo ekugqibeleni babeya kumkhaba uNowa kwanaye uYahweh (owayebadalile waza wabasindisa), bezimanya nemvukelo kaSathana.

Amazwi eGenesis 6 azalisekiswa zizono zenzala kaNowa, kwanomnombo kaHam. Kwakukho iingxilimbela emhlabeni ngaloo mihla: kwanasemva koko. "Loo mihla" yayiyimihla ukususela kuYerede ukuya kuNowa, kwaye "iingxilimbela emhlabeni" yayiyinzala yabalindi neentombi zabantu ngaphambi konogumbe. "Emva koko" kusemva kwemihla kaNowa, xa ufuzo loluntu lwaphinda lonakaliswa lulwazi lwezithunywa zezulu eziwileyo. Ndiyakucela ukuba ungaphindi ukholwe yinkohliso yethiyori kaSete enempazamo kwaye ukholwe ngamazwi esibhalo esingcwele, kuba la mazwi ayinyaniso.

Xa umhlaba wahlulwa ngeqashiso phakathi koonyana bakaNowa (uShem, uHam, noYafete), iqashiso likaHam lalikukuba adle ilifa lamazwe asemzantsi, yaye ke aba ngawakhe. Noko ke, uKanan walinqwenela inxalenye yelifa likaShem waza wafudukela kumazwe awayengafanele ahlale kuwo. Kuba yayinguKanan awamqalekisayo uNowa. Kuba uKanan walishiya ilifa lakhe waza waseka isixeko saseTire kunxweme olwaba lolwamaFenike lwaza lwakhokelela eTsidon, apho uIzebhelele wayephuma khona. Oku wakwenza ukuze abe kufutshane neNtaba yeHermon, apho abalindi behla khona nalapho ndenziwa kumila kumbi.

UKanan wafumana oko abalindi bakushiyayo ngaphambi konogumbe kaNowa, ngenxa yokuba bafuna ukugcina imvukelo yabo nasemva konogumbe, kuba bakrola kakhulu ematyeni nakwizinto ezenziwe ngamatye. UKanan wasebenzisa ulwazi lwabo lwezithunywa zezulu eziwileyo kubugqwirha obukhulu, kwaye izithethe ezininzi ezikhohlakeleyo zenziwa ngamaKanan, kuba bade babingelela ngabantwana babo. Babekhohlakele kakhulu kwaye bexhaphake kangangokuba wakwazi ukosulela ilizwe lakwaKanan ngamasikizi akhe asandul' ukwenziwa, apho uSathana wasebenza ngenxa yezigqitho zakhe.

Kwaye ke izizwe zeengxilimbela zasemva konogumbe, kwanazo izizwe ezilisikizi zamaKanan, zavela kwizigqitho zikaKanan nakubugwenxa bakhe nakubugqwirha bakhe. Kwilizwe uKanan awalibayo kuShem kweza amaHiti, namaHivi, namaPerizi, namaGirgashi, nama-Amori, namaYebhusi. Babengabo boyikekayo, kwanamaRafa. Kwakukho izizwe ezingaphezu kwezidwelisiweyo, kodwa aba boyikekayo babelindile kwilizwe lesithembiso benomgudu wokubulala abo babekhusela umnombo kaKristu, kwanookhokho benu abangamaSirayeli nooyihlo ababengamaHebhere.

UYoshuwa wangena kwilizwe lesithembiso ukuya kubanga oko kwakunikwe uShem ngokufanelekileyo ngeqashiso, nomhlaba owawungokaSirayeli ngokufanelekileyo ngesithembiso sikaYehova ngokwakhe. Kodwa abantwana bakwaSirayeli abazange bayiphumeze ngokupheleleyo imiyalelo kaYehova yokuba kungashiywa namnye kuwo umdiza ephila, kuba babenobubi bengengobantu, nezenzo zabo zazikrwada, yaye benza ububi obukhulu emehlweni kaYehova, kuba yayinjalo imvelo yabo. Waza ke uYehova waxelela uSirayeli njengoko kubhaliwe esibhalweni: Kodwa ukuba aniyi kubagxotha abemi belizwe phambi kwenu, kuya kuthi ke abo nithe nabayeka kubo, babe ngameva emehlweni enu, namakhakakhaka ezimbanjeni zenu, banixine ezweni enihlala kulo. Kwaba njalo ke.

Imbali ngenxalenye ibonisa ukwanda nokuhamba kwezi zinto zenyanyekayo, kwanomdiza otyalwe nguSathana ngokwakhe, njengoko bebesahlala phakathi kwengqolowa kususela kwiintsuku zamandulo. Baye bamisa iimpucuko ezininzi besebenzisa ubugcisa babo nolwazi lwezithunywa eziwileyo; bangabakhi abaziincutshe, bangamagqwirha, abaninzi kubo banezakhono ezingaqhelekanga, yaye banxibelelene noyise uMtyholi, bade babelane ngeempawu neenkanuko zakhe. Lo mdiza ungene kuzo zonke iinkalo zoluntu, kwaye ayingawo onke angam гиганты. Abaninzi bakhangeleka njengabantu, ngaphandle kokuba bazizidalwa ezixutyiweyo eziguqulwe ngonaphakade kwiinjongo zikaYehova endalweni kwaye abayi kuhlangulwa. Musani ukuboyika, kuba mkhulu lowo ukuni kunalowo usehlabathini.

Lo mdiza ufuna ukuthatha oko kungokwelifa nesithembiso kubantwana bakaThixo. Akunakubakho sivumelwano nawo, kuba ungowoyise uMtyholi, kwaye iinkanuko zoyise uya kuzenza. Wayengumbulali kwasekuqaleni, yaye xa ethetha ubuxoki uthetha okwakhe, kuba ulixoki noyise wako. Uya kugqwetha, angcolise, atshabalalise, abulale, aphange, ahluthe, aze enze okona kubi kuko konke okungokobuthixo azibeka kuko iinkanuko zawo. Awunalo uxolo, kwaye imvelo yawo yonakele ngokupheleleyo. Uwugqwethile umhlaba, kwaye uya kumisa ubukumkani bawo iyure enye njengoko inamba inolawulo lwayo emhlabeni. Ukanti ufuna ukuphinda umise ixesha lawo legolide, kodwa akukho xesha legolide uya kuba nalo ngaphandle komgwebo onzima, kwaye awuyi kusinda.

Lo mdiza ungendawo wonakalise ulwazi, imfundo, imbali, inkolo, isayensi, kwaye uqhubekile wonakalisa. Usasaze ubugcisa bezithunywa eziwileyo kwaye akukho nto intsha phantsi kwelanga. Njengoko amaxesha ezalisekile, uwugqume ngokupheleleyo umhlaba ngobuxoki, kwaye ihlabathi lonke lisengendawo. Ngokukhanyiselwa kwawo, umdiza wamaJesuit wonakalise ngokupheleleyo uluntu kwaye wakhokelela uluntu elwandle lobuxoki, apho ukuphela kwendlela yokuphuma kukuguquka nokuzikhaba zonke izono nobuxoki. (Kuba iRoma yenza namaSilamsi; funda ukuze ufunde.) Ulwazi lwawo lwezithunywa eziwileyo nobuxoki luyachasana nembali yezibhalo kwaye endaweni yoko lunikeza iintsomi ezingento ezimfamekisa iingqondo neentliziyo zabantu. Ngoko ke, phumani phakathi kwabo nahlukane nabo.

Ihlabathi lanamhlanje lelinye apho abantu abaninzi, abaninzi kakhulu banesifo sokulibala esihlangeneyo ngenxa yendyebo yamaJesuit. Uninzi lwenu alulibalanga nje kuphela iminqophiso neendlela zikaYehova, kodwa nilibele ngokupheleleyo ukuba ningoobani nokuba ookhokho benu bangoobani ngokwenene. Oku kwenziwa ngabom ngemfazwe, ubuxoki, kunye neenzame zokuhlaliswa kwabantu ngokutsha kule minyaka idlulileyo ezithe zasasaza abaninzi kangaka kumacala omane omhlaba. Koku bayakutyeshela ngabom ukuba akunakwenzeka ukufihla nantoni na emehlweni kaYehova; kwaye mna, Yeshua HaMashiach, ndiyabazi abam nabantu endibabizela elusindisweni.

Abanye babo basasazwayo babeziinkedama kwaye bathathwa kwiintsapho zabo nokuba kungemfazwe okanye ngokuqweqwediswa ngqo. Le miphefumlo yakhulela kwimbali yobuxoki, ide yaxokiselwa ngeengcinga ezihonjisiweyo zexesha elidlulileyo lobuxoki, kwaye ngoko inkohliso imiliselwe nzulu ezintliziyweni zabantu, kwaye abaninzi bamfanyekiswe ngonaphakade bubuxoki obamkelwa njengenyaniso. Kodwa kwabo baguqukela kum, Yeshua HaMashiach, niya kuyazi inyaniso kwaye inyaniso iya kunikhulula. Uluntu lwenu lwangoku lwakhiwe phezu kwamabhodlo nothuthu lweempucuko zakudala zabo bawileyo, kwaye kwenziwe umgudu omkhulu wokufihla inyaniso. Oko kwakha kwabakho kuya kubuya kubekho, kwaye akukho nto intsha phantsi kwelanga.

Ngaphandle kwedini lam lentlawulelo kunye nesipho sam sosindiso, beniya kulahleka nonke. Ngoko yizani kum, Yeshua HaMashiach, kwaye ndiya kunikhokelela kuyo yonke inyaniso kwaye ndiya kunifundisa imbali yenu yokwenyani. Ndiya kunidibanisa kwakhona nookhokho benu bokwenyani, kuba uninzi lwenu luvela kweli lizwe, nangona usindiso lolweentlanga zonke. Ndiya kunifundisa ngeendlela zobungcwele, kwaye niya kuphila ngobulungisa ngokholo kum, uYesu Krestu, kwaye niya kuphila ngonaphakade. Akukho nto ifihlakeleyo engayi kuvela ekukhanyeni, kwaye konke kuya kutyhilwa ngokupheleleyo, ukuze inyaniso yaziwe ngabo bonke.

Ngoko yekani ukuzingxala ngobuxoki njengoko nizingcwaba ekuzanelisekeni, kuba amaxesha okuzaneliseka aphelile. Amaxesha okutyhila asondele. Kuba yabonani! Njengoko kwakunjalo ngemihla kaNowa, kuya kuba njalo. USathana usebenzise ilaphu lakhe lobuxoki ukulungiselela inkohliso yakhe enkulu yexesha lokuphela, kwaye uninzi lwehlabathi luya kulandela ubuxoki bakhe. Kuba bonke ubukumkani bomtyholi buya kukhululwa enzonzobileni, kwaye izigebenga ziya kuphinda zihlale phakathi kwabantu, kuba ziza kuzalisekisa ingqumbo kaYehova.

USathana uthetha ubuxoki obukhulu, kwaye aba bawileyo baya kuthi bavela kwamanye amazwe kwaye baphucuke ngaphezu koluntu ngamawaka amaninzi eminyaka. Baya kuza benikezela ngolwazi olubonakala lunobubele, kwaye baya kuzigquma ngobubele bobuxoki. Kodwa aba bangobobukumkani bukaSathana, ngoko musani ukuwela kwiinkohliso. Musani ukulandela iindlela zehlabathi, kuba ihlabathi liya kumangaliswa bubukumkani bukaSathana, lide lithi, ngubani na ofana nerhamncwa?

Ndiya kunifihla, ndiya kunikhusela, ndiya kunomeleza, ndiya kunikhokela inyathelo ngenyathelo, kwaye ndiya kuwudiliza ngokupheleleyo umkhusane wobuxoki ogqume ihlabathi ngenkohliso. Mna, Yeshua HaMashiach, ndiya kunixhobisa ndinenze nenze imimangaliso emininzi enamandla ngeli xesha lembandezelo kaYakobi. Kwaye ke ndinixelela ezi zinto, ukuze inyaniso inikhulule, kwaye ukuze nikhululeke ngokwenene ningaweli kwinkohliso yabo enkulu. Kuba iziseko zayo bubuxoki, inkohliso, nobufelefele obusasazwe yimbewu yenyoka.
