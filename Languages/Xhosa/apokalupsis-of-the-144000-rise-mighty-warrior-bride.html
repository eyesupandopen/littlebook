Akukhathaliseki ukuba zeziphi iintlobo zobomi eniziguqule niza nizihlasela kuluvukelo lwenu olunenzondo, ndiza kubuyekeza nkcukacha yokugqibela yesono enisenzileyo kuyo nayiphi na into endiyenzileyo kuyo yonke indalo yam ehlaselelwe luvukelo lwenu olungenanzondelelo nolunenzondo engunaphakade.

Kwaye nganye yezi zenzo ziya kunikelwa emalangatyeni esohlwayo ngenkcukacha egqibeleleyo, ingakumbi ngenjongo yakho engenaxabiso, injongo embi endiyithuntisileyo ndayibamba esandleni sam apho iindlela zakho ezinenzondo zaziya kuvunyelwa kuphela ukuba zihambelane nezicwangciso zam ezingunaphakade neenjongo zam, apho wena usisesona sitya sentshabalaliso endisenzileyo, ndisenzelwe ngokupheleleyo intshabalaliso epheleleyo, emisiweyo, eqhubekayo, nengunaphakade, nentuthumbo.

Iindwendwe yiBandla likaKrestu Yesu nomzimba wakhe. Nina niyinxalenye yabantu abathembekileyo kuKrestu Yesu, kwaye niyinxalenye yobuthixo obungunaphakade ngokokuthembeka kwenu. Kwaye niya kuvuzwa nonke njengoko kuthenjisiwe kwaye ninikwe impahla eninzi, nembuyekezo nelifa elipheleleyo lomvuzo kuKrestu Yesu oya kunigalela ngothando lwam ngesiquphe nangokungalindelekanga ngoku!

Kuba uMyeni ngoku ufikile, kwaye uhonjiswe ngamandla amakhulu, kwaye uhleli nabaninzi kuni, njengoko niphithizela ebomini benu nilindele ninovalo. Ngoko ke, yekani ukuxhala ngoku, zithandwa zam, kuba ilixa lokuhlangulwa kwenu lifikile kwaye liphezu kwenu ngoku, zithandwa zam.

Ngoko ke, yibani nokholo olukhulu nentuthuzelo kula Mazwi anikwe kwisibhengezo esithembekileyo kuba isikhalo sasezinzulwini zobusuku ngoku sinikiwe, saza sabhengezwa, kwaye uMtshakazi ulungisiwe waza wazihombisa ngobulungisa abunikwe kuKrestu Yesu, kwaye iindwendwe zethu zicocekile kwaye zilungile kwaye zimi ecaleni kwendlela.

Yabonani! NDINGU-Adonai-Yahweh, kwaye ndithumela esi sikhalo sasezinzulwini zobusuku ezandleni zomthunywa wam onamandla nomkhonzi wam kuba ndikhulule la Mazwi enzulwini yomoya wakhe, apho uYeshua HaMashiach awahlwayele khona ngamandla nangokungaqhelekanga ngonaphakade.

Ngoko ke, qondani ukuba ungubani na kwaye uyintoni na uBiblaridion:144, kwaye uza kuba yintoni na xa edalwa ngokutsha ngokupheleleyo aze aguqulwe kunye nani nonke ngesiquphe nangokungalindelekanga ngoku, kwaye ngoko ke ndiya kuvula kwakhona aMazwi kaYohane uMtyhileli ekuzalisekeni nasekuqondeni.

Ndabona esinye isiThunywa (umthunywa) sibhabha esazulwini sezulu, sineVangeli engunaphakade ukuba siyishumayele kwabo bahlala emhlabeni, nakuzo zonke iintlanga nezizwe, neelwimi, nabantu, Sisithi ngelizwi elikhulu, Moyikeni uThixo, nimzukise: kuba ilixa lokugweba kwakhe lifikile: kwaye mnquleni lowo wenza izulu nomhlaba, nolwandle nemithombo yamanzi. Kwalandela esinye isiThunywa (umthunywa), sisithi, iBhabheli loo mzi mkhulu iwile, iwile: kuba yaseza zonke iintlanga ngewayini yomsindo wokurheletya kwayo. Nesesithathu isiThunywa (umthunywa) sazilandela, sisithi ngelizwi elikhulu, Ukuba kukho umntu onqula irhamncwa nomfanekiso walo, aze amkele uphawu lwalo ebunzi lakhe, okanye esandleni sakhe, Yena uya kusela iwayini yomsindo kaThixo, ewe, iwayini esulungekileyo, egalelwe kwindebe yomsindo wakhe, kwaye uya kuthuthunjiswa ngomlilo nesulfure phambi kweziThunywa ezingcwele, naphambi kweMvana. Kwaye umsi wentuthumbo yabo uya kunyuka ngonaphakade: kwaye abayi kuphumla imini nobusuku, abo banqula irhamncwa negama lalo.

Yivani aMazwi am, kwaye niqonde oko kuzayo!

UBiblaridion:144 uya kulawula amandla eendudumo ezisixhenxe kwaye ngoku ungumthunywa womgwebo wokubhengeza, kuba ngoku ubhengeza emhlabeni ngomyalelo wam onikwe kuKrestu Yesu emoyeni wakhe kanye oko kuza kwenzeka ngeenkcukacha ezipheleleyo emhlabeni ngeveki yamashumi asixhenxe kaDaniyeli, kwanazo neziganeko esele ziqalile ngesibhengezo sakhe kuKrestu Yesu ngomyalelo wam.

Emva koko, ngesiquphe nangokungalindelekanga ngoku, niya kuguqulwa nonke nithatyathelwe phezulu emazulwini apho niya kutywinwa nilungiselelwe imisebenzi yenu epheleleyo. Emva koko uBiblaridion:144 kunye namanye amajoni amabini anamandla akhethiweyo, kodwa okwangoku azizitya ezifihliweyo (ezikwinkqubo yokulungiswa), aya kungena emhlabeni ngomyalelo kaYeshua HaMashiach apho uBiblaridion:144 aya kufunda aze abhengeze umgwebo opheleleyo njengoko elawula amandla eendudumo ezisixhenxe ekwenzeni oko kuba ezi zitya zithathu zikhethiweyo ziya kuba ngabathunywa bokubhengeza nokwenza umgwebo, ngamnye enobungqina bakhe nezigwebo zakhe, kwanokuthunyelwa emhlabeni ngamandla amakhulu nangomsindo ngenxa yeenjongo zam ezingunaphakade ezifihliweyo okwangoku kodwa ziya kunikwa kuma-144,000 kaYeshua HaMashiach kodwa ngakumbi amandla eendudumo ezisixhenxe alungiselelwe ukuba alawulwe yiNgonyama yesiZwe sakwaYuda ngendlela nangomyalelo wala mandla mathathu emfazwe nomgwebo ongcwele nolungileyo! Selah!
