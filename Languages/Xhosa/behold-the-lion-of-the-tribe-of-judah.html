Khangela! INgonyama yesizwe sakwaYuda!

Vula iindlebe zakho, undivumele ndivule amehlo akho, O' Sirayeli, uvuke kubumfama bakho obuyinxalenye uze ulive ilizwi loMesiya wakho wokwenene, kwanaye iNgonyama yesizwe sakwaYuda, kuba ndinguye. Ngoko ke waqonde amazwi kaYeshua HaMashiach, kwanaye lo unento yokwenza naye, O' Sirayeli, kuba ndinguye.

Inene, inene, yivani amazwi am bazalwana bam ukuze niwaqonde amaxesha eniphila kuwo nokuze niliqonde ithemba lobizo lwenu elikukubuyela kwilizwe lesithembiso, kuba ndilithemba lobizo lwenu, kwaye ndiyanibiza, O' Sirayeli, ukuze niqonde oko kuzayo kananjalo nengqumbo eza kukhululwa kula maxesha niphila kuwo. Kwaye ndiyiNgonyama yesizwe sakwaYuda kwaye ngebulungisa ndiza kugweba ndenze imfazwe, ngoko ke buqondeni ubunzulu bamaZwi am, kwanamaZwi alowo nani ninento yokwenza naye kuba uYahweh undenze ndaba njalo.

Kuba ndililo elo litye likhubekisayo, kwanelo litye likhulu lesikhubekiso apho bonke abangakholwayo baya kukhubeka, kuba amalungisa akasaphili ngoMoses kodwa amalungisa aya kuphila ngokholo! Kuba uMoses uhleli kuMesiya wenu wokwenene kwanaye owawugcina ngokugqibeleleyo wona kanye umthetho ongenakwenzeka ukuba uSirayeli awugcine, kwanabo bonke abaka-Adam ukuba bawugcine ngenxa yesono enyameni yenu eyonakeleyo. Kwaye ndingulowo wasihlawulayo isohlwayo sikaMoses xa ndazinikela njengemvana kaThixo kuYahweh kwidini lokucamagushela elingunaphakade kuba isohlwayo saboni sasingekho kum ukuba ndisihlawule, kodwa eluthandweni lwam ngoYahweh, naseluthandweni lwam ngoluntu ndazihlawula izohlwayo zenu ngokupheleleyo. Kwanizohlwayo zentuthumbo enkulu, iintlungu, nokufa ndazihlawula ngokupheleleyo kwaye uYahweh ulamekele idini lam ngonaphakade.

Ngoko ke qondani, ingqumbo yeMvana sele iza kutyhilwa kwaye amaxilongo sele eza kuvuthelwa, kwaye, khangelani, izitya zomsindo wengqumbo kaThixo ngokuqinisekileyo ziyasondela kwaye zisemnyango wenu, ngoko ke, yivani ilizwi lam ukuze nina nabantwana benu niphile kwaye nisinde kumgibe uBharabhas asebenzela ukuwubekela abantu bam, kwanemvana yegongqongqo ekhethwe ngu-Esawu njengoMoshiach wabo, okwalilo irhamncwa elingumakhi wetempile. Kwaye uya kuphakama njengemvana eneempondo ezimbini, kwaye uza kwahlula imihlaba ngakumbi kubemi abafanelekileyo abagxothiweyo kukubulawa kwabo, ukuze ahlule phakathi kwabahlali abangekho mthethweni abavela ku-Esawu abenze ibango lobuxoki, nabahlaseli abathunyelwe yigongqongqo abayinzala ka-Ashkenazi hayi kaYakobi kodwa abazimanye no-Esawu ngeendlela zabo zeTalmud.

Kwaye ndingulowo uYahweh wamthumayo njengemvana kaThixo, kwananjengesivumelwano soxolo esigqibeleleyo, kwananjengePasika yenu egqibeleleyo kwanedini lokugqibela lezono zoluntu kube kanye, konke, ngonaphakade kwaye akusekho dini lezono. Kwaye ndafika kwabam ngothando nangoxolo, kwanabo balalayo idini lam, kodwa ndithunyiwe njengePasika egqibeleleyo ngokuthobela intando kaYahweh, kwaye ndiyimvana kaThixo, leyo uYahweh wayilungiselela ngokugqibeleleyo izono zenu, ngoko ke yivani ilizwi lam O' Sirayeli kwaye niqonde ukuba andisayi kuza emhlabeni njengemvana kaThixo, kwaye uxolo luza kususwa emhlabeni ngomgwebo ofikileyo. Kwanomgwebo oza kukhululwa ngequbuliso kuqala endlwini kaThixo kuba umgwebo uqala endlwini kaThixo.

Kuba abathembekileyo kuKristu baya kunyuselwa emazulwini, kwaye abadidididi baya kushiyelwa kumlilo womsuli wokutshatyalaliswa kwenyama, ukuze umoya usindiswe kodwa kube kungathi kuphuma emlilweni. Kwaye izihlwele ezininzi, ezininzi ziya kuza kuYesu waseNazarete zivela kuzo zonke iintlanga nezizwe neelwimi kwaye ndiya kuzihlambulula ngobulungisa kwaye ndiya kuzamkela kum kwaye ndiya kuzidala ngokutsha ngobulungisa nobungcwele bokwenene kwaye umvuzo wazo awunakuthelekiswa neentsizi eziya kuzinyamezela, kuba ubunzima bomvuzo wazo buya kugqitha kakhulu kulindelo lwabantu kwaye ngonaphakade baya kuhlala eluxolweni olugqibeleleyo nasebungcweleni nasebulungiseni bokwenene.

Kuba kubhaliwe ngabo kwaye kuya kuba njalo: Aba ngabo baphuma embandezelweni enkulu, bazihlamba iingubo zabo ezinde, bazenza mhlophe iingubo zabo ezinde egazini leMvana. Ngoko ke, baphambi kwetrone kaThixo, kwaye bamkhonza imini nobusuku eTempileni yakhe, kwaye lowo uhleli etroneni uya kuhlala phakathi kwabo. Abasayi kulamba, abasayi kunxanwa, nelanga alisayi kubatshisa, nabuphi na ubushushu. Kuba iMvana, ephakathi kwetrone, iya kubalawula, kwaye iya kubakhokelela kwimithombo ephilayo yamanzi, kwaye uThixo uya kuzosula zonke iinyembezi emehlweni abo.

Ngoko ke qondani ukuba ndingulowo uyigqibileyo imisebenzi yam phakathi kweentlanga kwaye amaxesha am obabalo nenceba afikelele esiphelweni, nangona ubabalo nenceba zihleli nam ngonaphakade, kwaye uYahweh ngokwakhe uvakalisile ukuba uya kuba nenceba kulowo aya kuba nenceba kuye, ngoko ke ubabalo nenceba ngokwenene zihleli ngonaphakade. Inene, inene, ndaya kwiintlanga kwananjengomgxothwa owaliweyo ngabam abathinjwe ngu-Esawu ebukhobokeni bezithethe zakhe ezinyelisa uThixo abafuna ukonakalisa ngazo amaZwi kaYahweh angunaphakade nangonaphakade, nto leyo engenakwenzeka. Kanti ke bazenzela iinkolelo zabo zesihogo ngezandla zababhali babo abaqulunqa izithethe zabantu abakhohlakeleyo zaba yincwadi ekuthiwa ingcwele, kodwa ingaba njani incwadi yonyeliso ibe ngcwele? Inene, inene, yeha kwabo bathi ubumnyama bukhanya nokukhanya kube bubumnyama, kuba umgwebo wabo awulibazisi!

Kwaye amaxesha am phakathi kweentlanga afikelele esiphelweni kuba bawele kwizono ezifanayo ooYihlo abawela kuzo xa babeyala imvana kaThixo awayithumelayo uYahweh kubo bengayifanelanga njengesivumelwano soxolo esingunaphakade kwiintlanga, apho isihlwele esikhulu sifumene usindiso. Kwaye ngoku abo babenokuba ngabantwana besithembiso phakathi koSirayeli kaThixo baziqhayisile ngamasebe endalo kwanomthi womNquma wendalo abaxhunyelelwa kuwo endaweni yawo xa amasebe endalo anqunyulwayo kuko oko kungakholwa.

Kanti ke bathathwa kumthi womnquma wasendle, kwanomthi ongenathemba nasithembiso kwaye kwabo beza kum bandenza iNkosi yabo yobulungisa engunaphakade ngamaxesha oxolo, ubabalo, nenceba ngoku bamkelwe kuSirayeli kaThixo kwaye bathatha inxaxheba ngokupheleleyo kwizithembiso ezenziwa kooYihlo kwaye abaninzi baba baya kundikhonza ngonaphakade njengomzimba wam kwaye ndiya kubathatha kum njengomtshakazi wam kwaye ndiya kubatywina abam abali-144,000 abazihlambululeyo egazini lam lokucamagushela bacoceka njengeentombi ezinyulu ezingadyojwanga lihenyukazi elikhulu. Kwaye ngosindiso lweentlanga zasikelelwayo iintlanga kwaye ngokwenene abo bezizwe ezilahlekileyo abathobela ilizwi lam bafumene usindiso eNkosini yabo kwaye baya kuhlala ngonaphakade phakathi kwezizwe kuSirayeli kaThixo! Ngoko ke qondani, ndiya kusindisa abanye kubo bonke abavela ku-Adam naphi na apho wabhadula khona emhlabeni ngeentsuku zakhe zohlwayo.

Kuba uYahweh undenze umoya ophilisa, kwaye emva kwenyama andisaziwa. Inene, inene, ndingulowo ngoku uwunqumlileyo umthi womnquma wasendle ngenxa yokungakholwa kwabo kwaye ngenxa yokuba bawaphule umnqophiso wam, kwanaye uMyolelo wam oMtsha. Kwaye kungenxa yokuba kufuneka babe neNkosi enye, kwanam, uKristu Yesu iNkosi yeeNkosi noKumkani wooKumkani kwaye ebukhosini bam kukho ithemba labo kuphela. Kanti ke bazithabathele ithemba lobuxoki kuBharabhas kwaye ngobuyatha babeka amathemba abo kwinkosi yobuxoki eyahlasela iiCawa zabo ngamathemba obuxoki obutyebi bezinto eziphathekayo, kwanaye uMsindisi wobuxoki othembisa ukusindisa iBhabhiloni eyimfihlo kodwa osebenzela ngokufihlakeleyo intshabalalo yeBhabhiloni eyimfihlo. Kwanaye lo mzenzisi uliRhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni eliya kuphakama njengoMoshiach wobuxoki ka-Esawu, kwanemvana yegongqongqo eya kuphakama ineempondo ezimbini. Kwaye ngoko iCawa yam yaseLawodike efunqulelweyo yaphazama kwaye ngoku iyavumelana nokufa kwayo, kuba uBharabhas uzisa ukufa kuphela, kwaye uya kulonakalisa ngokupheleleyo aze alitshabalalise elo henyukazi likhulu, kwanayo iBhabhiloni eyimfihlo, umgwebo wayo ofikileyo ngenxa yobubi bayo obugqithisileyo nokonakala ekonakalise ngako umhlaba ngokuhenyuza kwayo.

Ngoko ke ngokuvukela bazikhethele kwanaye lo uzisa intshabalalo yabo njengomsindisi wabo wokuzenzisa, kuba iNdlu yesikhungu kaSathana ngabazenzisi abazenzisayo bonke. Ngoko ke fundani, O' Sirayeli, ukuba uBharabhas uza kufeza ntoni na kuba ubathengisele ezandleni zeentshaba zabo, kwaye uya kwenza ngathi wenzakele entloko aze azifihle emhlabeni kwiintlekele eziza kufika. Kwaye uya kuphakama ngenkohliso enkulu njengoMoshiach wobuxoki kuSirayeli kuba baya kuba nemfazwe yabo, kwanemfazwe yabo yesithathu enjalo, kwaye iya kuba yeyona imbi kwezintathu. Kwananjalo, kuba kubhaliwe njalo kwaye kuya kwenziwa njalo: Ndabona irhamncwa liphuma elwandle, lineentloko ezisixhenxe, neempondo ezilishumi, naphezu kweempondo zalo izithsaba ezilishumi, naphezu kwentloko yalo igama lonyeliso. Nerhamncwa endalibonayo lalinjengengwe, neenyawo zalo zinjengezebhere, nomlomo walo unjengomlomo weNgonyama: negongqongqo yalinika amandla ayo netrone yayo, negunya elikhulu. Ndabona enye yeentloko zalo ngathi yenzakele yafa, kodwa inxeba layo elibulalayo lapholiswa, kwaye lonke ilizwe lamangaliswa laza lalandela irhamncwa.

Kwaye kunjalo ukuba amazwi esibhalweni ayalityhila ubuqhophololo bakhe, kwanamaZwi endawanika uYohane uMtyhili kule minyaka emininzi yeeyubhile edlulileyo. Kwaye kuya kubonakala ngathi uBharabhas uya kufumana inxeba entloko, kodwa uya kuxoka aze akhohlise ilizwe eliya kuyikholelwa ngokupheleleyo imiqondiso yakhe yobuxoki neemimangaliso kuba unikwe oko ukuze atsalele kuye bonke abanoyolo ekungabini nabulungisa ngenkohliso enamandla, kuba ungumdyobhisi nomtshabalalisi wabo. Kwaye ezandleni zakhe uzisa ukulunywa yinyoka, kwanelo hlaba lirhamncwa, kwaye baya kudibanisa isinyithi nodongwe nangona bengayi kunamathelana, kodwa imvana yegongqongqo iya kuphakama. Kwaye uya kuphakama phezu kwenkqubo yokugqibela yerhamncwa lowileyo, eya kuba bubukhosi babo bokugqibela bokulawula umhlaba bevela kwilizwe lesithembiso. Kodwa akayi kuphakama yedwa, kodwa uya kuphakama kunye nerhamncwa elivela elwandle, kwaye baya kulawula umanyano lwabo lookumkani abanike amandla abo apheleleyo negunya kwigongqongqo kuba bonke bayayinqula igongqongqo kwaye bazinikele ngokupheleleyo kuyo kuba inguYise wabo, kwanaye otyale ukhula lwakhe olubi phakathi kwengqolowa kaYahweh, kwaye ndiya kubancothula ngokupheleleyo ndibaphose emlilweni.

Kwaye ngoko imvana yegongqongqo iya kuphakama ngenkohliso epheleleyo kwaye imiqondiso emininzi yobuxoki neemimangaliso iya kuyibonisa ukuze ingqine iziprofeto zayo zobuxoki, kwaye iya kwenza amabango amaninzi oqhayiso nonyeliso ngayo ngokwayo kwanokubanga ukuba yona ngokwayo inguThixo. Kwaye irhamncwa elinye liya kukhomba kwelinye kwaye baya kuba nolawulo oluhlangeneyo, kwanangokwezicwangciso zegongqongqo eya kulawula ngazo ubukumkani bayo obubi okwexeshana elifutshane. Kwaye baya kwenza zonke izinto zobuKristu zibe ngokungekho mthethweni, kwaye baya kuyivala imibhalo engcwele, kwaye kunikwe ezandleni zabo ukuba balwe nabangcwele baze baboyise, kwaye baya kufeza oko. Kodwa baya kudibanisa iinkonzo zehlabathi ukuze bonke banqule iityhubhu zabo ezimnyama kuba ezinyaweni zikaSathana bazimanye ekuvukeleni kokugqibela kwaye abayi kusinda esandleni sam esinamandla, utsho uMesiya wenu ongunaphakade, kwanaye uYeshua HaMashiach!

Kanti ke kwintiyo yabo yazo zonke izinto eziphathelele kuKristu Yesu baya kubangela ukuba usindiso lufike kwisihlwele esikhulu abaya kubulala njengabafeli-nkolo, kwanabo baya kunqula igama likaYesu waseNazarete kude kube sekuphefumleni kwabo kokugqibela kwaye ndiya kubomeleza ndibanike ithemba elikhulu, kwaye baya kuza kum ngokupheleleyo, kwanabo bonke abaziindlalifa zosindiso olungunaphakade, kwaye akukho namnye unako ukubaxutha ezandleni zam kwaye bonke abo amagama abo abhalwe encwadini yobomi yemvana baya kusindiswa. Ngoko ke musani ukukhathazeka ngezithandwa zenu ezibonakala zilahlekile, kuba uninzi lwazo lusezandleni zam ezinamandla kwaye ndisaza kuzizisa elusindisweni kuKristu Yesu kwaye nangaluphi na ulwimi ababiza ngalo igama lam elinamandla ndiya kubeva!

Kodwa, kuya kuba njalo ukuba imvana yobuxoki eneempondo ezimbini iya kuphakama ukuze ilawule umhlaba isuka kwilizwe lesithembiso u-Esawu afuna ukulibanga njengelakhe, kodwa amabango akhe angamanga kwaye ilungelo lokuzalwa likaYakobi liqinisekile ngonaphakade, kwanelo lungelo lokuzalwa u-Esawu walithengisa ngeqhekeza lesonka. Kuba u-Esawu uzimanye nabawileyo kwaye uzimanye no-Ashkenazi abazithabathele amasiko nezenzo zamaKanan akhohlakeleyo kanti ke bayaxoka bathi bangabakwaSirayeli kanti abango. Kuba yayinguKanan owanqwenela iindlela zabawileyo emva konogumbe omkhulu, ngaloo ndlela etshintsha ngonaphakade inzala yakhe yaya kugwetywa ngonaphakade kuba bonke abazitshintshayo ngokwemfuza kumfanekiso kaThixo abadalwe ngawo abanaxolelo kuba ezi ziindlela zikaSathana u-Esawu aziqulunqe ngokuyinxenye kwiTalmud yakhe enyelisayo apho inkohliso yakhe ivela khona.

Kwaye ngoko u-Ashkenazi, ongokaYafeta, uzimanye no-Esawu kumabango akhe obuxoki elifa, apho bafuna ukuba ilifa likaYakobi kubantwana besithembiso. Nangona kunjalo, iinzame ezinjalo azinamsebenzi kwaye aziyi kuma kuba ndingulowo uYahweh anike zonke izinto ezandleni zam ezinamandla nezinobuchule. Ngoko ke qondani ukuba ndingulowo uzuze ilifa lazo zonke izinto ezathenjiswa uYakobi kwaye ndinguMesiya wenu wokwenene, kwaye ezandleni zam kukho imivuzo yenu O' Sirayeli kwaye ezandleni zikaBharabhas intshabalalo yenu engunaphakade ilindile, ngoko ke musani ukuzimanya naye!

Ngoko ke qondani ukuba imivuzo yesithembiso eyenziwa kooYihlo bakudala ihleli ngokuqinileyo ezandleni zam kwaye akukho namnye unako ukuba oko kungokwam ngokufanelekileyo kuba anjalo amandla endiwanikiweyo nguMkhulu onguMNA kwaye ndiya kunika eli lifa kubantwana besithembiso, kwanabo balungisayo abaphila ngokholo kuNyana kaYahweh, owabathandayo wazinikela ngenxa yosindiso olungunaphakade lwabantwana besithembiso, ngoko ke O' Sirayeli ndamkeleni njengoMesiya wenu ukuze nina nabantwana benu niphile kwaye niphumelele endleleni. Kwaye ndiya kunigcina ndinilondoloze ukuze nifike kwisabelo senu esipheleleyo selifa kubukumkani bam beminyaka eliwaka obusemnyango wenu. Ngoko ke yivani ubizo lwam, O' Sirayeli! Kuba ndiyanibiza ukuba nizithobe phantsi kwesandla sam esinamandla ukuze nani nibe ngabantwana besithembiso kuba uMoses ugcinwe ngokupheleleyo ngokholo kum, uYeshua HaMashiach, ngoko ke, phumulani kwimisebenzi yam niyeke eyenu.

Kwaye ngoko u-Ashkenazi uzimanye no-Esawu kumabango akhe obuxoki, apho bafuna ukuba ilifa likaYakobi kubantwana besithembiso. Nangona kunjalo, iinzame ezinjalo azinamsebenzi kwaye aziyi kuma kuba ndingulowo uYahweh anike zonke izinto ezandleni zam ezinamandla nezinobuchule. Ngoko ke qondani ukuba ndingulowo uzuze ilifa lazo zonke izinto ezathenjiswa uYakobi, kwaye ndinguMesiya wenu wokwenene, kwaye ezandleni zam kukho umvuzo wenu O' Sirayeli kwaye ezandleni zikaBharabhas intshabalalo yenu ilindile. Ngoko ke, qondani ukhetho ekufuneka nilwenzile, nalowo nani ninento yokwenza naye, kuba ndingulowo uthunywe nguYahweh ngenxa yosindiso lwenu, kwanaye lowo uMoses wayalela ukuthobela kwenu, O' bazalwana bam abangathobeliyo.

Kwaye ngoko njengoko kubhaliwe ngabo kwaye kuya kuzalisekiswa sisandla sam esinamandla, kuba uYahweh wayityhila inyaniso ka-Esawu kubaprofeti bakhe ngeentsuku zezono zabo ezibe ninzi. Kuba kubhaliwe kwakudala: Ngenxa yokukhohlakala kwakho kumntakwenu uYakobi, ihlazo liya kukugubungela, kwaye uya kunqunyulwa ngonaphakade. Xa wawumi kwelinye icala, ngemini abasemzini abathimba impahla yakhe, nabasemzini bangena emasangweni akhe, benza amaqashiso ngeYerusalem, nawe wawunjengomnye wabo.

Ngoko ke qondani O' Sirayeli ukuba ukwahlukana kokugqibela kukaYakobi no-Esawu kufikile phezu kwenu, kwaye ndiza kuhlanza ilizwe lesithembiso ezandleni zabawileyo kwaye u-Esawu uya kuncothulwa ngokupheleleyo, kwaye ngo-Esawu kubhaliwe: Kwaye indlu kaYakobi iya kuba ngumlilo, nendlu kaYosefu ibe lilangatye, nendlu ka-Esawu ibe njengeendiza, kwaye baya kubasa kubo babatshise: kwaye akuyi kubakho ntsalela yendlu ka-Esawu: kuba iNkosi ithethile. Ngoko ke qondani nina bakwa-Esawu abafunda amaZwi am, oko uYahweh akuvakalisileyo akukho mntu unokukutshintsha kwaye isiphelo senu siya kuba sesokugqibela kwaye niya kuncothulwa ngokupheleleyo kwaye anisayi kuphinda nibe kho.

Kuba ndiyiNgonyama yesizwe sakwaYuda kwaye ngebulungisa ndiza kugweba ndenze imfazwe. Ngoko ke yivani amaZwi am O' Sirayeli nizifihle kwimini ephezu kwenu. Zifihleni kumntu wesono oza ngendlela yenu, kuba uBharabhas uza nokufa okungunaphakade ezandleni zakhe okunikelwa njengonyango olungummangaliso oluthenjiswe ngobuxoki ukunika ubomi obungunaphakade. Kodwa abayi kuba nobomi bokuphila ekufeni kwesibini kuphela intshabalalo eqhubekayo kumalangatye ashushu esohlwayo apho impethu yabo ingafiyo.

Ndingulowo uya kuqhekeza amatywina kumsongo wam welifa, kwanayo kanye itayitile yomhlaba endiyithenge ngegazi lam lokucamagushela esandleni sikaYahweh ngokwakhe. Kwaye ndiza kuncothula abahlali abangekho mthethweni nabo bonke abathunyelwe ngabawileyo, kwanabo bawileyo ngokwabo kwaye amaxesha abo aphelile. Ngoko ke qondani oko kuzayo, Sirayeli, kuba baya kumisa ubukumkani babo kwaye baya kubonakala boyisile kwaye imiqondiso emininzi yobuxoki neemimangaliso baya kuyibonisa ukuze bakhohlise bonke abanoyolo ekungabini nabulungisa. Kwaye baya kucinga ukuba bamiselwe isiqalo sexesha labo legolide abanqwenela ukulimisela, kuba bangabakaYise wabo umtyholi, kwaye umtyholi yileyo gongqongqo inkulu, ngoko ke, iinkanuko zegongqongqo baya kuzenza.

Noko ke, abayi kuba nalo ixesha legolide kodwa baya kugwetywa ngokungqongqo nangobukhali, isigwebo eso siya kuqalisa ngokukhawuleza xa abathembekileyo bam kuKrestu behlanganiselwa kum emafini xa ndibonakala, nto leyo iya kubonwa lihlabathi.
