Okukrakra Okumnandi
Novemba 13, 2024

Owu Sirayeli! Yeyiphi iMvana oya kuyikhetha?

Yivani amazwi kaYehova Omkhulu Ndinguye nize nibalekele kwisandi samazwi am ukuze nifumane ubulumko, nokuba indoda elumkileyo phakathi kwenu yongeze ubulumko.

Yabonani! Ndiza kunixelela ngezinto ezizayo ukuze niyazi inyaniso yamazwi am, nize nifikelele kusindiso lwenu olupheleleyo ngoYeshua HaMashiach, oyena Mesiya wenu wokwenene, uKumkani, iNkosi, noMbingeleli Omkhulu ngonaphakade ngokohlobo lukaMelkitsedeke.

Ngoko ke, ndiyan命令a ukuba nimphulaphule ngomoya wenu wonke, nize naluse iintliziyo zenu ngokuthobela! Kuba, nguYeshua HaMashiach, uYesu Krestu ngolwimi lwesiNgesi, onikwe kungekuphela nje usindiso lukaSirayeli, kodwa nosindiso lalo lonke uhlanga luka-Adam, kuba bonke abanqula igama leNkosi kude kube sekupheleni baya kusindiswa, nokuba bangamaSirayeli okanye beentlanga, kwaye uYeshua HaMashiach uya kusindisa insali kaSirayeli ephilayo kwanakuhlanga luka-Adam oluya kuqhubeka ukuya kubukumkani beminyaka eliwaka.

Kwaye kunjalo, ndalungiselela oko kwakungenakwenzeka ukuba uhlanga luka-Adam luzilungiselele lona usindiso lwalo, kuba uhlanga luka-Adam lwalunokufumana intlawulelo epheleleyo kuphela egazini lemvana. Kuba ndim, Yehova, Omkhulu Ndinguye, owanibonelela ngePasika yenu egqibeleleyo kanye, ngoku, nanaphakade, kwaye umbingelelo wakhe wamkelekile ngonaphakade, ngoko ke, akusekho mfuneko yombingelelo, Owu Sirayeli.

Njengoko kubhaliwe kwabaseHebhere, ndiyaphinda ndithi kuni ngoku: Kuba ukuba igazi leenkunzi zeenkomo, neleebhokhwe, nothuthu lwethokazi olufefa abangahlambulukanga, luyangcwalisa ekuhlanjululweni kwenyama: Kobeka phi na ke ukugqithisela kwegazi likaKrestu, owathi ngoMoya ongunaphakade wazinikela kuThixo engenabala, ukusihlambulula isazela senu kwimisebenzi efileyo, ukuze nikhonze uThixo ophilayo? Ngoko ke ndiyanicebisa, Owu Sirayeli, wamkeleni umbingelelo wam kuba akusekho mbingelelo useleyo wesono!

Ngoko qonda Sirayeli, ukuba akunakwenzeka ukuba nabani na asondele kuThixo ongunaphakade woBulungisa ngoMoses kwakhona, kuba, ndimenze uYeshua HaMashiach indlela, inyaniso, nobomi, ekungekho mntu unokuza kuYehova ngaphandle kukaYeshua HaMashiach!

Kwaye ndiyalile ilizwi lakhe, kwanangeTorah apho ilizwi likaMoses likhala khona unanamhla njengengqina nxamnye nani nakwiindlela zenu zokungafundi nokungakholwa endaweni yokuthobela, endaweni yoko ngokungazi nifuna ukuphinda nimise itempile eyenziwe ngumntu, njengoko nilahlekiswa ngu-Esawu! Kuba niyalile itempile eyakhiwe ngumdali wenu, itempile eyenziwe ngaphandle kwezandla, yona kanye itempile eyenziwe nguYehova ngokwakhe kuSirayeli kaThixo, apho uYesu Krestu alilitye lembombo eliyintloko. Ngoko ke, yekani imisebenzi yenu nize niphumle kwimisebenzi yoMesiya wenu wokwenene, kwaye ndiya kuninika iSabatha.

Ngoko ndiyakuyalela Owu Sirayeli kwaye ndim owanika amazwi kanye awathethwa nguMoses kooyihlo eSinayi, amazwi endiyalela ukuba niwathobele rhoqo nanaphakade kwanakukungathobeli kwenu ngenkani nangentliziyo elukhuni. Kwaye uMoses wathi ukuze bonke bathobele: INkosi uThixo wenu iya kunivelisela umProfeti onjengam, phakathi kwenu, kwanakubazalwana benu: uya kumphulaphula!

Ngelixa uMoses wayengumlamleli woMnqophiso wokuqala (owakrolwa ngomnwe wam eSinayi) owanikwa ukugwebela isono enyameni, umnqophiso lowo ngoku ukhanya ebusweni bukaYesu Krestu, uYeshua HaMashiach oyena Nkosi noMesiya wenu wokwenene! Kanti uMthetho kaMoses usisithunzi sezinto ezizayo, izinto ezo zizalisekiswe kuKrestu Yesu, kwaye ndimenze umoya ophilisa, kwaye ngokwenyama uYesu waseNazarete akaziwa ngakumbi!

Kwaye njengoko kwaphunyezwa ngesandla sam esinamandla, kwanangomnwe kanye kaYHVH omkhulu Ndinguye, ndakrola imimiselo yam kumacwecwe amatye eSinayi apho ndanika uMoses imimiselo yam ukuze uSirayeli aphile phambi kwam. Noko ke, oko kwanikwa uSirayeli eSinayi kwaba kukugwetywa kwesono enyameni ukuze uSirayeli abe nomfundisi wokumzisa kuMesiya wakhe wokwenene, uYeshua HaMashiach endimthumileyo ukuba anisindise ngokupheleleyo.

Kwaye kunjalo, umthetho kaMoses ulungile, unobulungisa, ugqibelele, kwaye ungcwele, kwaye ukuba nawuphi na umntu uthuka umthetho kaMoses makagwetywe ngawo ngobungqongqo obupheleleyo obumiselwe phantsi kwesiqalekiso nesohlwayo somthetho kaMoses. Kanti oko kulungileyo nokunobulungisa nokugqibeleleyo nokungcwele kwasebenza ukufa kubo bonke abagwetyiweyo kuwo ngesono enyameni, kuba apho kungekho mthetho isono asibalwa, ngoko umthetho kaMoses unikwe ukutyhila nokugwebela isono enyameni.

Kanti kungomoya womthetho, kungekhona unobumba, apho uluntu lufumana inceba eluthandweni lwam ngalo, kuba unobumba womthetho uzisa ubungqongqo (Anifundanga na ukuba ndingathanda inceba kunombingelelo?) kwaye ke eluthandweni lwam ngabantwana bakwaSirayeli endijonge ukubasindisa ukuba nithe nathobela uYeshua HaMashiach, ndamisela umbingelelo kuba ngaphandle kokuphalazwa kwegazi akukho kuxolelwa kwezono. Kwaye ngesandla esilumkileyo sikaMoses abantu bam bagcinwe kwada kwafika uKrestu, nangona uninzi lwenu lujikele koothixo bobuxoki kwaye nithanda iityhubhu zenu ezimnyama, izinto ezizizothe ngokupheleleyo emehlweni am, Owu Sirayeli, ngoko ke mkani ngoko nangoko!

Ngoko qondani umsindo wam kuni Owu Sirayeli, kuba ndinguYHVH, omkhulu Ndinguye kwanalowo unguThixo ongunaphakade woBulungisa ongapheliyo amandla kwaye akukho nto inzima kakhulu ukuba isandla sam esinamandla singayiphumeza. Kwaye ndim, El Shaddai, uThixo uSomandla owabumba indalo ngobulumko bam obungunaphakade kwaye ngamandla amakhulu omoya wam zonke izinto zibambene ngokuthobela ilizwi lam.

NdinguElohim wenu, apho amashumi amawaka aphindwe ngamashumi amawaka ajikeleze itrone yam, kwanomkhosi omkhulu weengelosi zemimoya elungiselelayo, abalungiseleli bayo balilangatye lomlilo abagqwesileyo ngobulumko kwaye badalwe ngokugqibeleleyo nangokugqibeleleyo njengoko ndibadalile eluthandweni lwam ngabo! Kwaye eluthandweni lwabo ngam, bathobela ngokugqibeleleyo intando yam engunaphakade kwaye ndinike uYeshua HaMashiach ulawulo phezu kwabo bonke.

Noko ke, mna YHVH Omkhulu Ndinguye, kwanalowo ujikelezwe ngumkhosi weengelosi ongenakubalwa AKUDINGI MCEBISI kubulumko bam obungunaphakade kwaye kwindalo yam ndilawula kwaye ndisebenza ZONKE izinto ngokwecebo lentando yam, kwaye kwezona zilumko zikhulu zaphakade akukho namnye olumkileyo kunomkhulu Ndinguye! Ngoko yivani ilizwi lam nize nithulise iilwimi zenu ukuba nikhetha ubomi obungunaphakade kunamadangatye esohlwayo angenakucinywa Owu Sirayeli kuba olo lukhetho ekufuneka umntu wonke alwenze.

Kwaye ngobulumko bam obungunaphakade ndathumela uYesu waseNazarete kwaSirayeli, kwanaxa iJudiya yayilawulwa nguPontiyo Pilato, kwanaxa ababingeleli abakhulu neSanhedrin bamtyhola ngokungekho sikweni kwaye ngezandla ezikhohlakeleyo imvana kaThixo yabulawa yaza yabethelelwa emnqamlezweni ukuze umzimba wesono obopha uAdam utshatyalaliswe kubo bonke abaphila ngokholo ngokuthobela, kuba olilungisa uya kuphila ngokholo! Kwaye ndim, kwanuEl Shaddai (uThixo uSomandla) obeke zonke izinto ezulwini nasemhlabeni ezandleni ezinamandla zikaKrestu Yesu, uYeshua HaMashiach kwaye uyiNgonyama yeSizwe sakwaJuda endiyithumayo ukuba igwebe kwaye ilwe kwaye isindise abathembekileyo nabathobelayo. Ngoko ke, yivani amazwi am ngokuthobela uMesiya wenu wokwenene, kuba akukho wumbi!

Ngoko qondani umsindo wam kuni Owu Sirayeli ukuba kubumfama benu obuyinxalenye enabuzuza kooyihlo abaneentliziyo ezilukhuni, abalizwi labo leTalmud nilihlonipha ngaphezu koElohim wenu. Ewe, nibambe izithethe zezo zilumko zenu nabantu abalumkileyo ngentlonipho enkulu kunamazwi Omkhulu Ndinguye, ongumdali ongunaphakade nowaphakade wako konke okudaliweyo! Kuba ngoYHVH zadalwa zonke izinto, ezisezulwini, nezisemhlabeni, izinto ezibonakalayo nezingabonakaliyo: nokuba ziitrone okanye ulawulo, okanye iziphathamandla, okanye amagunya, zonke izinto zadalwa ndim, kwaye zenzelwe mna, Kwaye ndingaphambi kwazo zonke izinto, kwaye kuYHVH zonke izinto zimi.

Noko ke, nicinga ukuba ningabacebisi bam kwaye ngokobudenge nizibiza ngokuba ningabantu bam abanyuliweyo ngeli lixa nisala amazwi am ngokungathobeli ngenxa yokungakholwa? Owu Sirayeli niphambukele kwizithixo ezizidenge zentliziyo nakumazwi obuxoki ka-Esawu enicebo lakhe nilikhetha ngaphezu koThixo ongunaphakade woBulungisa, uElohim wenu! Kwaye nikhubeka kubumfama obuyinxalenye kuba kuMoses iintliziyo zenu zigutyungelwe. Noko ke, xa intliziyo yenu iya kujikela kuMesiya wenu wokwenene, isigqubuthelo siya kususwa kwaye endaweni yobumfama obuyinxalenye niya kubona!

Ungubani wena, Owu Sirayeli, ukuba ungazimisela ukuba ngumcebisi kaYehova? Noko ke endaweni yamazwi kaYehova anikwe ebusweni bukaYeshua HaMashiach, nikholwe bubuxoki obudluliselwe kwizithethe zabadala benu ababekuthinjweni ku-Esawu, enithi ngemibhalo yakhe nizibophelele ku-Esawu ngendlela efanayo kwaye nikhonkxwe ku-Esawu! Kwaye ndabhala ngokucacileyo ukuba uMoses anike ooyihlo eSinayi: Nantoni na endiniyalela yona, yigcineni niyenze: aniyi kongeza kuyo, ninganciphisi kuyo. Kwaye kwakhona ndakubhalisa kubulumko bukaSolomon, obulumko buma ngonaphakade: Lonke ilizwi likaElohim linyulu: ulikhaka kwabo bamthembayo. Ungongezi nto emazwini akhe, hleze akukhalimele, ufunyanwe ulixoki.

Owu Sirayeli, ukuba ningathobela iintliziyo zenu ngokuthobela amazwi anikwe usindiso lwenu novuyo olungunaphakade, ukuba ningaphinda nithobele amazwi omdali wenu! Kwaye ndim owayeza kunibuyisela aze anizise phakathi kwesibaya esingunaphakade esiphantsi kwesandla esinamandla soMalusi wam oyiNtloko endimnike zonke izinto! KwanuYeshua HaMashiach, owalusa uSirayeli wokwenene kaThixo ngokugqibeleleyo eluthandweni lwakhe lokuthobela ilizwi lam, kwanomzekelo eniwudelelayo. Kuba uYeshua HaMashiach ukuphela komntu owake wathobela ngokugqibeleleyo intando kaYHVH, omkhulu Ndinguye endihlala ndikholiswa nguye, ngoko ke ndimenze umoya ophilisa ngoko mphulaphuleni lowo ndimthumele ngenxa yenu kuba akukho wumbi.

Kwaye niziqaqadekisile iintliziyo zenu njengeLitye leSiseko elithinjwe yiNdlu yesikhungu kaSathana kuba niwalile amazwi ecebo lam anikwe ebusweni bukaKrestu Yesu, enithi ngokungazi nimale, ngaloo ndlela nilahla ithemba lenu elikuphela kwalo lokubuyiselwa nosindiso ngonaphakade kanaphakade. Kwaye ngoko nimi nimfama, nize, kwaye nineentloni esonweni senu, nicinga ngokungazi ukuba nibe ngumcebisi kuYHVH, Omkhulu Ndinguye!

Ngoko ndiyaninika ukuqonda kubumfama benu obuyinxalenye ukuba nikhubekile kwilitye lam lembombo eliyintloko abakhi benu abalilahlileyo, kwanelitye lam lokukhubekisa. Kuba uYeshua HaMashiach lilitye lembombo eliyintloko kwanelitye lesiseko lokwenene, uYesu waseNazarete enimalayo ngokungathobeli ngenkani nangona ndanikhethela mna njengabantu abakhethekileyo kwaye intliziyo yam iyaphuka.

Yivani, yivani, Owu Sirayeli kwaye niqonde ukuba ndim okrola umthetho wam ongunaphakade, kwanembonakaliso yentando yam engunaphakade kwicebo lam elingunaphakade lokuhlangula endalenza ngokwecebo lentando yam engunaphakade negqibeleleyo kumacwecwe amatye ukuze uSirayeli athobele eSinayi! Ngoko ke, ndim oya kubhala umthetho wam kumacwecwe enyama eentliziyo zenu kuba eluthandweni lwam ngani, ubuncinane nje bamandla am buyakwazi ukukrola umthetho wam kumacwecwe enu amatye kwaye ngobabalo lwam nenceba yam ebonakaliswe ebusweni bukaYeshua HaMashiach ndiya kuwacola amacwecwe enu amatye abe ludongwe lombumbi ukuze nidalwe ngokutsha ngonaphakade ebulungiseni nasebungcweleni bokwenene njengoko ndafunga kwiminqophiso yam kooyihlo ukuba ndiya kwenza.

Ngoko ke, Owu Sirayeli, ndiyaniyalela ukuba nithobele ilizwi likaYeshua HaMashiach kuba akukho sindiso kwelinye igama elinikiweyo ngaphandle kukaYesu Krestu olilungisa, enithi ngokungazi nimbize isithixo sama-goyim kuba nisebukhobokeni ku-Esawu!

Noko ke kubhaliwe kwaye kuye kwenzeka: Ukusuka ku-Abraham ukuya ku-Isake, xa uYakobi no-Esawu bazalwa nguye, isandla sikaYakobi saqala sabamba isithende sika-Esawu. Kuba u-Esawu sisiphelo sehlabathi, kwaye uYakobi sisiqalo salo esilandelayo. Isandla somntu siphakathi kwesithende nesandla. Noko ke kunjalo ukuba ngoku ndiya kuhamba ndohlule u-Esawu kuYakobi kuba u-Esawu yiNdlu yesikhungu kaSathana engumhloinza ombi oye wahlasela ilizwe lesithembiso kwakhona, kwaye uSirayeli uphinde wahlaselwa ngamaKanan.

Kwaye njengoko kubhaliwe kuya kwenziwa utsho Omkhulu Ndinguye: Kuba, yabonani, iyeza imini, evutha njengeziko, nabo bonke abanekratshi, ewe, nabo bonke abenza okubi baya kuba ziindiza: kwaye imini ezayo, iya kubatshisa, itsho iNkosi yemikhosi, ukuba ayiyi kubashiya nengcambu nasebe. Kodwa kuni enoyika iGama lam, uNyana woBulungisa uya kuphuma enokuphiliswa emaphikweni akhe, kwaye uya kuphuma akhule njengamathole esitalini.

Kwaye njengoko uYeshua HaMashiach ehlanganisa abathembekileyo ebandleni, kwanasemzimbeni wakhe, nomtshakazi wakhe ngoku ndiya kuhambisa uYeshua HaMashiach ukuba ahlule okokugqibela phakathi kuka-Esawu noYakobi, kwaye uYakobi uya kusindiswa kwanangezilingo zeveki yamashumi asixhenxe kaDaniyeli. Ngoko ke, makungabikho mntu uhenyuzayo, okanye umntu ongahloneli Thixo njengo-Esawu, owathi ngenxa yesabelo esinye senyama wathengisa ubuzibulo bakhe. Kuba niyazi ukuba nasemva koko xa wayefuna ukuyidla ilifa intsikelelo, waliwa: kuba akafumananga ndawo yokuguquka, nangona wayeyifuna loo ntsikelelo ngeenyembezi.

Kwaye ngoko uluntu luphakathi kwesandla sikaYakobi nesithende sika-Esawu, ngaphandle kokuba u-Esawu ufuna ukunika uYakobi inxeba entloko lokugqibela, endingenakukulivumela. Kwaye kunjalo, ukuba bonke abemi bomhlaba abashiyelwe engxakini kaYakobi ukuba banyamezele umlilo womsuli baya kuba ngabakaYakobi ngendlela kaYesu waseNazarete, abangabantwana belifa, okanye baya kulahlwa phakathi ko-Esawu owathengisa ubuzibulo bakhe ngeqhekeza lesonka. Ngoko ke, umntu wemvelo uya kunqula igama likaYesu waseNazarete kude kube sekupheleni kwakhe ukuze avuswe abe kuSirayeli kaThixo okanye uya kuthengisa ngokufanayo ilifa lakhe elinokubakho nelingunaphakade ngeqhekeza lesonka elinikwe ngesandla sikaBharabhas, kuba uBharabhas uya kukhokela iNdlu yesikhungu kaSathana kwaye uyimvana yenamba.

Noko ke umgibe uya kubekwa elizweni ukuze ubambise uYakobi ngokusisigxina kwaye uBharabhas uzisa inkohliso enkulu ngolawulo lwakhe kwaye uya kuphumeza ububi obuninzi elizweni naselwandle. Noko ke, kuya kuba njalo ukuba njengoko imililo yemfazwe ivutha kwaye itshisa uBharabhas uya kuvuka ade abange ukuba uvusiwe ukuze azalisekise iinjongo zikaThixo kwaye uya kubanga izinto ezininzi ezinyelisayo nezimbi. Kwaye uya kuzisa isebe lakhe lomnquma kwaye uya kuzibanga enguNowa, ephilile ukusindisa ihlabathi kumgwebo owenzelwe abantu abakhohlakeleyo.

Kuba irhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni elikwangumprofeti wabo wobuxoki liza kuzisa isicombululo salo kuba liyixhwane elineempondo ezimbini elithetha njengenamba. Kwaye ulawulo lwalo luya kuba lwezinto ezimbini njengoko luya kufuna ukumanya lonke uluntu phantsi kolawulo lwenamba, enguloo mtyholi wamandulo ngokwakhe. Kuba uBharabhas akezi kulawula yedwa nangona eya kubanga ukuba yena ngokwakhe unguThixo kodwa uzisa umnqophiso wokwahlukana apho iimpondo ezimbini ziya kulawula zombini ulwandle nomhlaba, kwaye kwenye yezo zimbini kukho uphondo oluncinane lukaDaniyeli. Kanti ndithembisile ukuba uSirayeli akasayi kuphinda ahlulwe kwaye kwizizwe zakhe ezimbini akusayi kubakho sicombululo kodwa uYeshua HaMashiach uya kumanya uSirayeli ngokupheleleyo nangonaphakade.

(Noko ke kuya kwenzeka zintanda zam ngoko qondani into endisaza kuyiphumeza ngoYeshua HaMashiach: Ndiya kuthabatha abantwana bakwaSirayeli phakathi kwabahedeni, apho baye khona, ndibabuthe macala onke, ndibazise elizweni labo. Ndiya kubenza babe sisizwe esinye elizweni, ezintabeni zakwaSirayeli, aze ukumkani abe mnye kubo bonke: yaye abasayi kuba zizizwe ezibini, bengasayi kwahlulwa babe zizikumkani ezibini ukususela ngoku. Abasayi kuphinda bazidyobhe ngezithixo zabo, okanye ngamasikizi abo, okanye ngazo naziphi na izigqitho zabo: kodwa ndiya kubasindisa kuzo zonke iindawo zabo zokuhlala, apho bonileyo khona, ndibahlambulule: ukuze babe ngabantu bam, nam ndibe nguThixo wabo.)

Elinye lirhamncwa eliphuma elwandle ekubhalwe ngalo kwathiwa: Kuba akanqweneli bafazi, akayihoyi nayiphi na ithixo: kuba uya kuzikhulisa ngaphezu kwako konke. Ndiyanibuza ngoku nikhangele ukuba ikhona na indoda ezalayo? Kanti umfazi wayo ubonakala engumfazi onabantwana, nangona bengengabo abantwana bayo. Kwaye irhamncwa eliphuma elwandle liya kuphakama limanyene ngokupheleleyo ngenjongo nerhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni kwaye benza ngathi ziintshaba ezibonakala zahlukene ngeembono nangenkolo.

Kodwa bayaxoka kwaye bayazenzisa kwaye uyise wabo yile namba inye eyenze la marhamncwa mabini akhohlakeleyo ngeenjongo zayo ezimbi, nkqu nokutshabalalisa uAdam ukuze ibe umhlaba ngonaphakade ibe ngowayo kwaye akunakwenzeka ukuba iphumeze amacebo ayo amabi kuba umhlaba ngoweNkosi nokuzala kwawo, utsho uYHVH, uMkhulu onguMNA NDIKHONA!

Kuba uBharabhas uzisa amazwe amabini aya kumanywa phantsi kwenamba kwaye baya kuba noxolo lobuxoki njengoko bethembisa ubomi obungunaphakade nempilo noxolo ukuba nje ningathatha uphawu lwabo nize nizibandakanye nabo njengoko bexuba isinyithi nodongwe kodwa abayi kunamathelana. Qondani, nonke nina enicinga ukuzihlanganisa ngonaphakade nabawileyo ngophawu lwabo ukuba baya kuxela ubuxoki obuninzi besithi liyeza elimangalisayo eliya kunika ubomi nempilo kwaye ligqibelele ngokumangalisayo kuyilo lwalo!

Kodwa bonke abathatha ityhefu yabo baya kuziguqula ngonaphakade nkqu noguqulo ekungekho kubuyela kulo. Kuba ukunyelisa umfanekiso kaYHVH awadalwa ngawo uAdam kukuzibandakanya nezono zabawileyo abazenza oothixo baze bangabi ngabo, kwaye bonke abakwenzayo abanaxolelo kwaye abanalo uxolo kwaye kwelo chibi lomlilo elingacimekiyo baya kuya ngonaphakade kuba baya kuba bekhethe njalo.

Noko ke, njengoko abathathu abangcwele bephakama nawo amangqina am amabini aya kuphakama, aze aprofete iintsuku ezili-1260 enxibe ezirhwexayo kwaye aya kunika ubungqina obuninzi. Aya kuza kwaSirayeli anike ubungqina ngoThixo ongunaPhakade woBulungisa kwaye aya kungqina ngamandla ngoYeshua HaMashiach nkqu noYesu waseNazarete ongenaye akukho mntu unokuza ku-El Shaddai! Noko ke, abathathu abangcwele baya kumxela uYesu Krestu njengogewu kwaye baya kuzenza ezingekho mthethweni izibhalo ezingcwele apho uYeshua aliLizwi elenziwa inyama.
