Ingqina eliMbaxa: Ibandla
Emisebenzini kaYohane uMbhaptizi
Matshi 25, 2024

Kholwani Ndim

Yivani ilizwi lam, bantwana bam, nikholwe ndim, iNkosi uYesu Krestu. Ndinibizele eluzukweni nasesulungwini, nokuba nime phambi kwam nihlanjululwe ezindleleni zenu ngegazi lam lentlawulelo, kuba igazi lemvana lihlamba konke ukungcola. Nihlanjululwe kum. Yazini, bantwana bam, akukho kugwetywa kwabo bakum, oko kukuthi, abakuKrestu. Ukumanyana nam neenjongo zam kukungenela ubudlelane noBawo, uYahweh, kunye nam, unyana wakhe uYeshua. NdinguYehova onibize ngegama, waninika isimemo sobomi obungunaphakade apho kungekho nkwathazo, naxinzelelo, nalusizi; kuba ndiya kuzisula zonke iinyembezi emehlweni enu ngosindiso lwam kuni. Ndingawuqala na umsebenzi ndingawugqibi? UThixo wezulu nomhlaba angashiya na into ingagqitywanga okanye angazifezekisi na izithembiso nelizwi lakhe? Anifundanga na, ukuba akunakwenzeka ukuba uThixo axoke? Inene, zonke izithembiso ziya kuzaliseka zifezeke, ngoko ke yazini ukuba inkululeko yenu isondele — ingeyiyo inkululeko enesiqingatha, kodwa yinkululeko epheleleyo kungekho nto ishiyiweyo ingagqitywanga kwaye kungekho nto ingaphelelanga.

Zonke ezi zinto zizalisekisiwe, kwaye okungenakwenzeka kwenzekile kum kuba ndoyisile ukufa ukuze ndingaphindi ndife. Yazini ukuba wonke umsebenzi ombi uSathana azame ukuwakha ebomini benu ndiya kuwuchitha. Ewe, ndiya kuyitshabalalisa yonke imisebenzi kaMtyholi; kuba ngenxa yale njongo ndabonakaliswa. Ndininikile ukuphelela, kwaye ndisaya kuniphilisa ngokupheleleyo kwimizimba yenu eyonakeleyo, kwaye niya kufana nam. Jonga iLizwi lam, yiva amazwi am, uwathelekise nesiBhalo, kwaye wazi ukuba inkululeko yenu epheleleyo isondele njengokuphefumla kwenu okulandelayo kwaye iya kwenzeka kwangoko. Yimani niqine ngenxa yam; thethani ngenxa yam. Ningabi nanto yakwenza nabakhonzi bezithixo nabakrexezi abangene ngokunyenya eBandleni lam nabaya kuzama ukulahlekisa abantwana bam abancinane. Ingqumbo yam ihleli phezu kwabo naphezu kwakhe nabani na ohlasela abantu bam ukuze abalahlekise.

Kuba akunakwenzeka ukuba nixwilwe esandleni sam, kwaye abo ndibenzele inceba ngabo bandenze iNkosi yabo ngokuvuma igama lam elinamandla, uYesu Krestu, njengeNkosi. Andiyi kunidanisa, kwaye akukangeni entliziyweni yomntu izinto ezilindele nina, njengoko ndiza kunixhwila kungekudala. Musani ukuvumela amazwi amabi ukuba akhathaze iintliziyo zenu, bantwana bam, njengoko nisindisiwe kwingqumbo kaThixo, kwaye abathembekileyo bam abayi kunyamezela ixesha leminyaka esixhenxe yengqumbo esezakuphalazwa. Iintliziyo zenu mazibe seluxolweni ziphumle kum, nasezinkathazweni nasezintshutshisweni zenu phumlani kum, kwaye ndiya kuninika uxolo olungenakuchazwa. Inceba yam nobabalo lwam luhlala luhleli ngonaphakade, kwaye ndilihlawule ixabiso lezono zenu, ngoko qhubekani niphuma kuzo. Ndiyayibona isiphithiphithi enisinyamezelayo. Ndiweva onke amazwi arhabaxa athethwa kuni nakuni. Ngaphezu koko, ndiyanomeleza ebuthathakeni benu, kwaye ndininikile umoya wam, kwaye ninamandla kwaye ninamandla kum. Ngamandla enu niya kusilela, ngoko phumlani emandleni am, kuba uBawo undiphakamisile ngaphezu kwayo yonke enye into.

Ndiya kuphindezela kwabangendawo kungekudala, bantwana bam, ngoko musani ukoyika. Le mpindezelo ayiyeyenu, kwaye ayenzelwanga abantwana bakaThixo kodwa iintshaba zakhe ezone abantu kunye nabantu abakhohlakeleyo ababulalayo nabaphanga ummelwane nomzalwana wabo.

Ewe, abantu abaninzi abakhohlakeleyo bazimanye noSathana ngenxa yenzuzo yokubawa, kwaye banesabelo sabo nomvuzo wabo kwichibi lomlilo elilungiselelwe uMtyholi neengelosi zakhe. Anilona lonke uhlanga olunye, uhlanga lwabantu? Ukanti, yazini ukuba ingqumbo enkulu isezakuphalazwa kwaye izulu lisezakutyhilwa emhlabeni. Sonke isenzo sibhaliwe. Lonke ilizwi elingenamsebenzi elithethiweyo libhaliwe. Akukho mfihlo inokufihlwa kum, kwaye akukho ndawo yokuzimela ekujongeni kwam. Nangona abangendawo bemba umjelo emhlabeni ukuze babaleke ingqumbo yam esondelayo, baya kudibana nesiphelo sabo bezama ukuzimela kum.

Ndiya kubuyisela, kwaye ndiya kuphindezela ndimise ubulungisa kwabonelweyo. Ndinekrele elibukhali elintlangothi-mbini eliphuma emlonyeni wam, kwaye akukho nto kwaye akukho mntu unokundinqanda, njengoko ndinguKumkani wooKumkani neNkosi yeeNkosi. Ngubani onganahlula eluthandweni lukaThixo olukum? Andizihlawulelanga izono zenu ndaninika ubomi obungunaphakade ukuze ndiphalaze ingqumbo nokugwetywa phezu kwenu, nina bathembekileyo bam. Akukho nto kwaye akukho mntu unokusahlula, kwaye kungekudala niya kukhangela kuBawo nakum ngamehlo enu. Nangona nakhe ukuthemba nokholo olukhulu kum, ningazange nindibone ngamehlo enu, kungekudala niya kubona, kwaye niya kwazi njengoko naziwa. Ngubani na lo unikhuphayo? Akayi kuba yinto engento na nemisebenzi yakhe emibi ibuyiselwe kuye kabini? Abantu abakhohlakeleyo baziqwebele ingqumbo enkulu, ewe, ingqumbo enkulu ngokuxhaphaza indalo kaBawo, kwaye ndiya kubuyisela, itsho iNkosi.

Yimani phambi kwam kwaye niqhubeke nizahlula kweli hlabathi likhohlakeleyo, elifayo kwaye nivumele umsebenzi wam wentlawulelo ukuba unihlambe, unihlambulule ngokupheleleyo. Yabonani! Ndenza zonke izinto zibe ntsha, kwaye izono zenu nobugwenxa benu andisayi kuphinda ndibukhumbule. Ngoko phumani emisebenzini yenu nithabathe imisebenzi yam, kuba ngokwenjenjalo niya kuzisela ukuphelela kwimiphefumlo yenu. Musani ukoyika abangendawo! Kuba kungekudala imisebenzi yabo iya kuchithakala njengothuli phambi komoya omkhulu, kwaye ndiya kwenza zonke izinto zicoceke zibe ntsha. Kukho umvuzo omkhulu kwabathembekileyo nabanyuliweyo bam; kwaye kungekudala, ewe, kungekudala kakhulu niya kuvuna umvuzo wemisebenzi yenu ukuba anityhafi.

Yimani ngenxa yam kude kube sekupheleni, njengoko lowo ulahlekelwa bubomi bakhe kum uya kufumana ubomi kwaye uya kuvuna uvuyo olukhulu nosindiso kunye nokuvuya ngonaphakade phambi koBawo. Ndinenze ookumkani nababingeleli phambi koBawo, njengoko ndingumbingeleli omkhulu ongunaphakade ngokohlobo lukaMelkitsedeke. Yimani niqine elubabalweni lwam kwaye nazi ukuba kungekudala kakhulu umnyango uya kuvalwa, kanye njengokuba uThixo wamvalela uNowa nobutyebi bakhe ngaphakathi eMkhombeni. Ndiya kunihlanganisela kum ngokunjalo emazulwini. Kukho umnyango ovulekileyo ongenakuvalwa okanye avulwe mntu, kwaye ndiya kunamkela kum, Mtshakazi wam. Ngoko ndilindeleni njengoko ixesha seliphelile.

NdinguYehova othembekileyo.

Ndim indlela, inyaniso, nobomi. Ndim intloko yomzimba.

Ndingumyeni womtshakazi, kwaye ndithi yiza!

Ndim lowo ungasafiyo, kuba akunakwenzeka ukuba ukufa kundibambe.

Ndim lowo unibizele eluzukweni, esulungwini, nasenyanisweni.

NdinguYehova uYesu Krestu, unyana kaThixo owabonakaliswa enyameni owafela izono zenu.

Ndim lowo uvusiweyo ngomhla wesithathu ukuze angaphindi afe.

Ndim lowo uThixo amamkeleyo emazulwini njengoko abapostile nabafundi bam babendibukele ndisiya emafini.

Ndingumbingeleli omkhulu ongunaphakade ngokohlobo lukaMelkitsedeke.
