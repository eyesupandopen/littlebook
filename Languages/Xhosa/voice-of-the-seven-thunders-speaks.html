1Ukusuka kuYeshua HaMashiach: Indudumo yokuQala!

Banyuliweyo bam, abakhethiweyo, nabathembekileyo, yivani amazwi eNkosi yenu ephilayo nomsindisi, uYeshua uMesiya! Iintlanga enihlala kuzo kunye neembali enizifundiswe ngabafundisi-buchopho benu abangamaJesuit ziyimvelaphi yenkohliso enkulu, ukutyhilwa kwayo okupheleleyo kusondele kwaye sele kuqhuba, apho abawileyo baya kubonakala njengabaphambukeli abanobubele kodwa eneneni zizithunywa zezulu eziwileyo ezinobubi. Yintoni le ibikho? Yileyo iya kubakho: kwaye yintoni le yenziweyo? Yileyo iya kwenziwa: kwaye akukho nto intsha phantsi kwelanga.

USathana unikezele ngolwazi lwakhe olubizwa ngokuba luphucukileyo kwasekuqaleni, nkqu nolwazi lwakhe lokulungileyo nokubi. Uyingcaphephe yokukhohlisa nexoki elifundise izinto ezininzi eluntwini uYahweh ebengazimiselanga, kuba ezi ziindlela zokufa. Indalo kaYahweh ayenzelwanga ukuba isetyenziswe ngendlela engeyiyo ukuze kufunyanwe amandla obuntu kunye nenzuzo okanye njengendlela yokubaleka umgwebo kaThixo, okanye ukuseka iindlela nomyalelo womntu. Kuba uSathana wonakalisile uluntu kwasekuqaleni, kwaye wonakalisile abalindi abawileyo abazibandakanye nemvukelo yabo.

Owu, wena lutshaba olukhulu lwabo bonke ubulungisa, ewe nina booSathana: izono zenu zokuqala zabhalwa nguEnoki umbhali kwakudala, kwaye zimi nanamhlanje, kuba izono zenu zabhalwa ngolo hlobo. Kwaye ke ndiza kushwankathela, kuba nizenze ezi zinto, kwaye nenze okuninzi ngakumbi: Nalulahlekisa bonke oonyana bakaThixo, nabazisa emhlabeni, nabalahlekisa ngeentombi zabantu. Nanika oonyana bakaThixo abangcwele icebo elibi, nabalahlekisa ukuze bangcolise imizimba yabo ngeentombi zabantu. Nabonisa abantwana babantu zonke izibetho zokufa, nalahlekisa uEva, nabonisa izixhobo zokufa koonyana babantu: ikhaka nengubo yentsimbi, nekrele lokulwa, nazo zonke izixhobo zokufa kubantwana babantu. Kwaye kwisandla senu ziye zaqhubela phambili kwabo bahlala emhlabeni ukusukela ngaloo mini kwaye ngonaphakade.

Nina booSathana nabafundisa abantwana babantu okukrakra nokumnandi, kwaye nabafundisa zonke iimfihlo zobulumko babo. Nalufundisa uluntu ukubhala nge-inki nephepha, kwaye ngenxa yoko abaninzi bona ukusukela kunaphakade kuse kunaphakade kude kube namhlanje. Kuba abantu abadalelwanga injongo enjalo, yokunika isiqinisekiso kukholo lwabo olulungileyo ngepeni ne-inki. Kuba abantu badalwa kanye njengezithunywa zezulu, ngenjongo yokuba bahlale banyulu kwaye belungile; kwaye ukufa, okutshabalalisa yonke into, ngekungeke kubabambe, kodwa ngolu lwazi bayatshabalala, kwaye ngala mandla kuyabadla abantu.

Nina booSathana nabonisa abantwana babantu zonke izibetho ezikhohlakeleyo zemimoya needemon, nezibetho zombungu esibelekweni, ukuze udlule, nezibetho zomphefumlo, ukulunywa yinyoka, nezibetho ezenzeka ngobushushu basemini, unyana wenyoka ogama linguTabââ'ĕt. Kuba nade nacela uMikayeli ukuba anibonise igama elifihliweyo, ukuze nilibize kwisifungo, ukuze abo bangcangcazele phambi kwelo gama nesifungo abatyhila konke okwakusemfihlekweni kubantwana babantu.

Kuba nabangela ukuba abalindi bazibandakanye nemvukelo yenu njengoko naqhubeka nokonakalisa uluntu. Kwaye ke izono zabalindi zabhalwa nguEnoki umbhali, kwaye ndiza kushwankathela ngolu hlobo: Bafundisa abantu ukwenza amakrele, neemela, namakhaka, nezivatho zesifuba, kwaye babazisa iintsimbi zomhlaba nobugcisa bokuzisebenza, nezacholo, nezihombiso, nokusetyenziswa kwe-antimony, nokuhombisa iinkophe, nazo zonke iintlobo zamatye axabisekileyo, nazo zonke iidayi ezinemibala. Bafundisa ubugqwirha, nokusika iingcambu, ukusombulula ubugqwirha, ukuvumisa ngeenkwenkwezi, amaqoqo eenkwenkwezi, ulwazi ngamafu, imiqondiso yomhlaba, imiqondiso yelanga, kunye nendlela yenyanga.

Kwaye ke ngenxa yokonakala nobuxoki benu, niyingcolisile indalo kaYahweh ngeendlela zenu ezimbi namasikizi anyelisayo. Nilulahlekisile uluntu kwaye nonile kuYahweh, kwaye nilonile uluntu, kwaye nade nona kwiintaka, nakwizilo, nakwizirhubuluzi, nakwiintlanzi, kwaye izono zenu zizisa umgwebo omkhulu eningayi kuphinda niwufumane. Niyingcolisile indalo kaYahweh ngetekhnoloji yenu nokukhohlisa, kwaye nabangela ukuba uluntu lumangaliswe ngamaqhinga nobuxoki benu. Kuba uYahweh wanibonisa kuphela iimfihlelo ezingenaxabiso zendalo yakhe waza wagcina iimfihlelo zakhe ezixabisekileyo zifihliwe entliziyweni yakhe kude nokungcatsha kwenu.

Nina bakhohlakeleyo nafundisa iindlela zenu ezingenaxabiso eluntwini njengoko babesanda ebusweni bomhlaba, kwaye nazisa usizi olukhulu nentshabalaliso ngamacebo nobuchwepheshe benu obubi. Kuba anizi ngaphandle kokuba nibe, nibulale, nitshabalalise. Nafundisa ukukhohlisa umoya, kuba niyinkosana yamandla omoya, kuba nikhohlisa i-aithēr. Kuba xa uKayin wonayo, wakha isixeko; kwaye nifundise uluntu iimfihlelo zenu ezingenaxabiso nokukhohlisa. Ezi zixeko zazisetyenziselwa ulawulo nokukhohlisa nokuzisa intshabalaliso yoluntu, njengoko nazisebenzisela ukwenza amasikizi enu entshabalaliso.

Nibangele ukuba uhlanga luvukele uhlanga emfazweni nakwingxabano engapheliyo, kwaye nabangela ukuba abantu bam bone, kwaye uYahweh uzisasazile iZizwe zakwaSirayeli kwimimoya emine. Akukhathaliseki, injongo yenu embi iqhubekisele phambili iinjongo zikaYahweh, kuba ngokusasazwa kwazo kuya kufezekiswa ukuhlanganiswa kweegusha eziseleyo. Kwaye ndiza kuthumela amangqina am amabini ukuba aprofete iintsuku ezili-1260 enxibe ezirhwexayo. Kuyo yonke imizamo yenu yokutshabalalisa abantwana bakaYahweh nisilele kabuhlungu, kwaye anikwazanga ukubona ukuba imizamo yenu iqhubekisele phambili iinjongo zikaYahweh, kuba nezitha zakhe ziyalithobela ilizwi lakhe.

Kuba nizitshutshisile kwaye nazikhathaza izizwe ezili-12 kuwo wonke umhlaba njengoko nanifuna intshabalaliso yazo. Nibe abantwana koomama, kwaye nathatha abantwana kwiintsapho nabafudusela kuwo wonke umhlaba, emva koko nabaxokisa nababangela ukuba bakholelwe kwiintsomi zamaJesuit malunga nembali yabo yokwenyani, iinjongo, nemvelaphi. Akukhathaliseki, nisilele, kuba mna, Yeshua HaMashiach, ndiza kubadibanisa ngokupheleleyo nexesha labo elidlulileyo, uhlanga kunye nelifa lokwenyani, kwaye ndiza kwenza bonke abaziindlalifa zosindiso babe mntu mnye omtsha, nakum, uKristu Yesu.

Ngobuqhetseba besandla nentshabalaliso enkulu, nizifihlile ngobuchule iitekhnoloji zenu, nide naxoka ukuba abantu bexesha elidlulileyo babengenalwazi kwaye bengenatekhnoloji. Kuba nabonisa abantu bamandulo iimfihlelo zenu ezingenaxabiso, kwaye akukho nto intsha phantsi kwelanga. Kwaye ke niyayifihla inyaniso malunga nokukhohlisa kwenu indalo, nide niyifihle kwabo beniya kubakhohlisa ngenkohliso yenu enkulu. Niphinde nazisa amaqhinga enu omlingo kwakhona, njengoko nisete iqonga ukuze nizifihle njengabaphambukeli. Kwaye ngohlaselo lwenu lobuxoki lwabaphambukeli niya kubangela ukuba bonke bamangaliswe yimiqondiso nemimangaliso yenu yobuxoki, nkqu nabo amagama abo angabhalwanga eNcwadini yoBomi kwasekusekweni kwehlabathi. Kodwa mna, Yeshua HaMashiach, ndiza kunibhenca ngokupheleleyo, kwaye yonke imfihlelo efihliweyo iya kuvela ekukhanyeni.

Yabonani! Ndiza kuxhwila abathembekileyo bam ndibase emazulwini, kuba ndiza kumtshakazi wam kwaye andiyi kunqandwa. Kwaye mna, Yeshua HaMashiach, ndiza kugweba ndenze imfazwe kwaye ndizise umgwebo omkhulu emhlabeni, kwaye aniyi kubaleka.

Mna, Yeshua HaMashiach, Nkosi yeeNkosi noKumkani wooKumkani, ndithumele la mazwi ngomkhonzi wam, kwaye ngomkhonzi wam amazwi am athunyelwe, kwaye konke kuya kuzaliseka.

2Ukusuka kuYahweh, uThixo Omkhulu Ongunaphakade: Indudumo yesiBini!

Owu, nina booSathana, niyikhohlisile indalo yam ukuba ilandele imiqondiso nemimangaliso yenu yobuxoki, kwaye ingqumbo yam ishushu kuni. Abantu bam baya kwahlulwa ngokupheleleyo kumdiza wenu eniwuhlwayele phakathi kwengqolowa yam kwigadi yam yobabalo; kwaye uNyana wam, uKristu Yesu, uya kuhlanganisa ingqolowa yam esiphelweni sam, kwaye ke iNkosi yesivuno iya kuhlanganisa, ibophe kwaye itshise umdiza wenu. Umgwebo omkhulu uphezu kwenu, kwaye aniyi kusinda esandleni sam. Kwasekuqaleni, narhubuluza nazifihla phakathi kwabantwana bam, nide nonakalise nabancinane bam kwasekuqaleni ngobuxoki nenkohliso yenu.

Aningoothixo, kwaye nabo balandela inkohliso yenu neenkohliso zoloyiso abangoothixo nabo. Aninakudala, kuba niswele amandla, kwaye andizange ndinidale ngezo zakhono kuba bendisazi ukuba niya kuvukela ngaphambi kokuba nidalwe; kwaye kubulungisa bam nothando ndanidala ebulungiseni, kwaye nanigqibelele ngemini enadalwa ngayo. Kwikratshi lenu elingenaxabiso niyaqhayisa ngobukhulu benu, kodwa aninabukhulu, kwaye ndiza kunibhenca ngokupheleleyo ngenxa yobuqhetseba enibubo. Ndiza kunibeka phambi kooKumkani, kwaye baya kunibona kwaye babone ukuba aninamandla kangakanani. Unyana wam, uYeshua HaMashiach, wanoyisa ngokupheleleyo eKalvari, kwaye aninathemba.

Nivumelekile ukwenza kuphela oko ndikuvumelayo, njengoko nilutshaba oloyisiweyo. Neba itayitile yomhlaba ngobuqhetseba nobuxoki benu kuAdam, kwaye ngoku unyana wam okuphela kwamzeleyo, uYeshua HaMashiach, unoyisile eluthandweni lwakhe kum nakwindalo yam; kwaye umhlaba ngowakhe, nkqu neNkosi yeeNkosi noKumkani wooKumkani, kuba nditshilo. Kuba wawuthenga umhlaba noluntu ngegazi lakhe elingenasono, kwaye abo bakhetha ubomi baya kuhlangulwa. Yonke imisebenzi yenu engenaxabiso iya kutshitshiswa, kwaye bonke abakhetha ubomi baya kuphuma kubuxoki nenkohliso yenu baye kubutsha bobomi, kwaye ndiza kwenza abathembekileyo babe nozuko ngakumbi kunokuba naninjalo. Kuba nisilele, owu zinkosana ezibuthathaka nezihlwempuzekileyo zomhlaba, nkqu nayo yonke inkosana kumyalelo wenu isilele kunye nani, owu nyoka erhubuluzayo, exokayo.

Ngoku liza ixesha loMgwebo wenu nentshabalaliso epheleleyo yobukumkani benu, nkqu nobukumkani enibakhileyo kwiinzame zenu zokuzama ukundifihla, mna Yahweh, kwindalo yam. Aniyi kubaleka. Anisayi kuphinda ningene emazulwini, kuba niphoselwe emhlabeni ningasayi kubuya, kwaye indawo yenu ayisekho emazulwini. Nibotshelelwe emhlabeni enawunqwenelayo, owawungengowenu ukuba niwuthathe. Wonke umsebenzi wenu uya kutshitshiswa ebomini babo enazama ukubatshabalalisa (nasemhlabeni ngokwawo); baya kuhlala ngonaphakade eluxolweni nasekhuselekeni, kwaye baya kuba nolonwabo olugqibeleleyo, kwaye ndiza kuhlala phakathi koluntu kwaye ndibe nguThixo wabo.

Kodwa wena, owu rhubuluzayo, awunamandla ekujongeni kwam, kwaye awunakwenza nto ukundinqanda. Kwiintsuku ezintandathu ukusukela ekuweni komntu, kuba usuku lunye luyiminyaka eliwaka emehlweni am, anifezekisanga nto ngaphandle komgwebo wenu, ukugwetywa, intshabalaliso, nentuthumbo kuni nakubo bonke abalandelayo. Kuba niya kubotshwa niphoswe kwelo chibi likhulu livuthayo apho ndenzele nina awona malangatye ashushu, nkqu namalangatye aluhlaza okwesibhakabhaka, kwaye niya kutsha ngonaphakade, kwaye ndiza kunibuyisela wonke umsebenzi ombi eniwenzileyo, amaxesha amaninzi. Nibulele izihlwele phezu kwezihlwele, kwaye niya kuhlawulela wonke umphefumlo olahlekileyo, kwaye anisoze nisinde. Niya kuzazi zonke izono enizenzileyo, kwaye anisoze nisinde emgwebeni, kwaye niya kuthuthunjiswa ngonaphakade ngenxa yezono enizenzileyo. Niya kuzazi kakuhle izono nezikreqo zenu ekuthuthunjisweni kwenu, kwaye abo nibathiyileyo baya kuba noloyiso lokugqibela phezu kwenu. Kwaye bonke abangemalungisa enabakhohlisela ukuba bone kwaye bazibophelele ukuba bajoyine imvukelo yenu, nkqu nabo amagama abo angazange abhalwe eNcwadini yoBomi, baya kwabelana ngentuthumbo yenu. Nonke niya kuhlawulela sonke isono enisenzileyo, kuba nonile kuYahweh nakwindalo yam, kwaye ndiza kubuyisela. Kuba kubulungisa bam ndiza kubuyisela izono zenu, izenzo, neendlela ezimbi ngokupheleleyo ezifubeni zenu amaxesha amaninzi kwaye ngonaphakade. Kwaye mna, Yahweh, nkqu noThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade, ndifunga ezi zinto ngegama lam, kuba andinakufunga ngomkhulu.

Niya kukhonza iinjongo zam, kwaye kwintiyo nentshabalaliso yenu niya kuqhuba abanyuliweyo bam kum; kuba ndiza kubakhokelela ngesandla sikaYeshua HaMashiach nomkhosi wakhe wamadoda anamandla, kuba baya kuhlanganisa iigusha zam eziseleyo. (Yabonani! Abayi-144,000 abathunyelwe nguYeshua HaMashiach baya kwenza imisebenzi emikhulu ebusweni bobubi benu obuyenyanyekayo.) Kwaye akukho namnye endimmisele kubomi obungunaphakade oya kuwa kumacebo enu, kuba abanakuxhwilwa esandleni sam, kwaye akukho mntu unokubaxhwila ezandleni zoNyana wam: Kuba ndimnike igama elingaphezu kwawo onke amagama, kwaye uza ngamandla kaYahweh ngokwakhe, kuba ndabelane naye ngetrone yam. Kwaye uya kuyityumza ngokupheleleyo intloko yakho. Usilele, nkosana encinane, kwaye ixesha lakho lifikile.

Mna, Yahweh, uThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade, ndithumele la mazwi ngomkhonzi wam, kwaye ngomkhonzi wam amazwi am athunyelwe, kwaye aya kwenzeka. Kuba uYahweh, uThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade, utshilo, kwaye akukho nto inokwaphula izibhengezo zam. Ndingu-El Shaddai!

3KuYeshua HaMashiach: Indudumo Yesithathu

Yabonani! Amaxesha ohlulo ayasondela esiphelweni, kwaye ukubonakala kwam kuphantse kwafika ngoku. Kusondele kakhulu kwaye nezithunywa zezulu nomkhosi wasezulwini bakulindele ukukhaliswa kwexilongo. Ndolulele iingalo zam kwihlabathi elifayo kangangeminyaka emininzi yeejubhili ngoku, kwaye abaninzi basisamkele isivumelwano soxolo uBawo, uYahweh, awasithumela eluntwini, ndim ke, uKrestu Yesu, kuba ndiluxolo lwenu. Ukanti abaninzi baye basala isicelo sam nobabalo nenceba kaBawo eye yathululwa emhlabeni.

Abo batywinweyo nabahlangulweyo ngabo banyulwa kwasekusekweni kwehlabathi. Kuba uBawo wanazi ukusuka kunaphakade ukuya kunaphakade, kwaye nanisoloko nisesizikithini sentliziyo yakhe, njengokuba nam bendisesizikithini sentliziyo yakhe, kuba mna, Yesu waseNazarete, ndingunyana okuphela kwamzeleyo kaYahweh. Ukanti ngemisebenzi yam egqityiweyo njengePasika egqibeleleyo nangokuthobela kwalowo ukuphela kwamzeleyo, oonyana abaninzi, abaninzi bakaThixo bazelwe ngokutsha kude kube ngunaphakade, bethathwe koonyana babantu, kuba ndinguNyana woMntu!

La maxesha obabalo ayeyimfihlelo efihliweyo entliziyweni kaYahweh awayengaxeleli mntu ngayo de wayityhila kum, kwaye nam ndiyityhile kubapostile bam abangcwele nabaprofeti kwaye ndiligcinile iLizwi lam elingcwele ezindlebeni nasezintliziyweni zabanyuliweyo nabathembekileyo. Kuba nina, Mtshakazi wam, niyinxalenye yemfihlelo enkulu kaThixo, kwaye ubomi benu (obufihliweyo kum) buza kutyhilwa. Kwaye ndiya kubavuza abathembekileyo ngayo yonke imisebenzi yabo yokuthembeka abayenzileyo ngokholo olukhulu kum, uYesu Krestu.

Kwaye ngoko ndisindise izihlwele ezikhulu ngegazi lam lentlawulelo, kuba igazi lam lalingenasono kwaye unanamhla andinasono, kuba andisokuze ndone. Abo baphile ubomi babo ngokuthembeka kuKrestu baya kufumana umvuzo omkhulu, kwaye baya kukhanya njengeenkwenkwezi ezulwini, kwaye ubuqaqawuli obukhulu bobabo, kuba abazange bazizukise ngokungathi bangabantu abakhulu. Kodwa bandizukisile mna, Yeshua HaMashiach, kwaye ndiya kubabonisa kuBawo bengenabala okanye imibimbi, kwaye iingubo zabo ziya kuba mhlophe qhwa kwaye azisayi kuze zidyojwe. Kwaye bonke baya kuphila kwaye bangaphindi bone, nokuba kungokungahloneli Thixo okanye ngekratshi. Kwaye baya kuzukiswa kum.

Kuba ndingumalusi oyintloko, kwaye ndikhokelele umhlambi wam kumadlelo aluhlaza de kwafika amaxesha okuphela, asondela ngokukhawuleza emhlabeni, kuba akusekho santini iseleyo ukuba idlule kwiglasi. Kwaye ndiya kubavuza bonke, ukusuka koyena mncinci ukuya koyena mkhulu, ngendlela eningenakuyiqonda ngeli xesha; kodwa oko kuqonda kusemnyango wenu, kuba niya kunyuselwa emazulwini kungekudala. Kwaye uya kunyuselwa ke uMtshakazi wam eluzukweni, apho ndiya kugweba imisebenzi yakhe kwaye ndihlawulele ngokupheleleyo yonke indoda, umfazi, nomntwana wokugqibela. Ndiya kubavuza kakhulu nangezona zinto zincinci ezenziweyo ekundikhonzeni. Kuba uThixo akanantswela-bulungisa, ukuba angawulibala umsebenzi wenu nomgudu wothando, enalubonisayo kwiGama lakhe, ngokuthi nikhonze aBangcwele, kwaye nisakhonza.

Musani ukoyika, Mtshakazi wam, kuba uThixo akagwebi njengokuba umntu egweba, kuba umntu akanabulungisa kwaye ukhetha ubuso. Umntu uzukisa abo umntu ababona bebalulekile, kwaye umntu wemvelo uhlala engakuhoyi ukuthobeka nesidima ekhetha abanekratshi nabazifezileyo. Kuba umntu wemvelo ukhala ecela ukubalelwa ebulungiseni phambi koThixo, kodwa umntu wemvelo akanakuyoyisa imvelo yakhe yesono kwaye uyala ukukholelwa ukuba wonakele, ade ayikhanyele iNkosi yeeNkosi, ebeya kumsindisa.

Kwaye ngoko ikratshi landulela intshabalalo, nomoya wekratshi uwandulela ukuwa. Endaweni yokuthobela iintliziyo zabo kumazwi am nobulumko nothando endiluthululele emhlabeni, umntu wemvelo ukhetha ukulandela iinkanuko neminqweno yakhe, kuba iindlela zehlabathi ziyilwe nguSathana ukuhenda nokuvusa loo minqweno, kwanemivuzo yokungalungisi. Kwaye ngoko amaxesha eeNtlanga ayasondela esiphelweni, kwaye lixesha lenkathazo kaYakobi; kuba ndiya kuhlangula uSirayeli, kwanabanyulwa bam abathembekileyo, abaya kuza kum benguqukele ezonweni zabo nasezonweni zooyise abandilahlileyo. Kwaye njengoko kubhaliwe, makwenzeke.

Owu, mntu wemvelo, kutheni usakhetha izono zakho kwaye ungaluhoyi ubabalo lwam nosindiso ngokungathi yinto elula? NoYahweh nam, Krestu Yesu, asinakuyoliswa yimigwebo ezayo noloyiko olukhulu oluza kukhululwa kungekudala. Kodwa uThixo akayikhethanga le nxalenye yesicwangciso sakhe ukuze abe nenkohlakalo kwindalo yakhe: kuba uThixo luthando, kwaye uThixo kukukhanya, kwaye akukho bumnyama kuye konke konke. Inkathazo enkulu ezayo yenzelwe ukunika uluntu ithuba lokugqibela phambi kokuba kube kade kakhulu kulo. Isihlwele esikhulu siya kusindiswa kwimbandezelo, kwanabo babambelele egameni lam de baphefumle okokugqibela kwaye abangabathandi ubomi babo de kwasekupheleni.

Ndiza kuninika ubumnyama enibunqwenela kangaka, kuba ukukhanya kwam akuyi kubakho apho, kwaye niya kuchitha unaphakade nohlukene nam, uYahweh, uThixo omkhulu oqaqambileyo, obungangamsha nokukhanya kwakhe kungekho nyama inokukubona nokhoyo ukusuka kunaphakade kuse kunaphakade. Nina bavukeli, nina bakhohlakeleyo enonakalise indalo yam kwasekuqaleni ngeenkanuko zenu nentiyo nomona, yazini ukuba nizifumbalele isohlwayo esikhulu nentuthumbo eningasoze niphiliswe kuyo nabo bonke abalahlekiswe bubuxoki benu nokukhaba uYesu Krestu, nokwala ukuguquka kubuxoki benu nize kwinyaniso yam.

Nicinga ukuba ukugqwetha iindlela zam nokususa imida yam yinto elula, kodwa niya kuyiqonda ingqumbo yam kwakamsinya nje lakunichukumisa ilangabi lokuqala, kwaye niya kugutyungelwa ngumlilo nesulfure eningasoze nisinde kuko. Ngaphezu koko, nibulele, nadlwengula, naphanga, naxhaphaza, nenzakalisa, nabulala, natshabalalisa, kwaye nathatha inxaxheba kwezona zono zibi kakhulu, nkqu nokunqwenela inyama yasemzini; kwaye nizimanye neengelosi eziwileyo, ngoko ke ndiya kunimanya nechibi lomlilo endililungiselele uMtyholi neengelosi zakhe, kwaye aniyi kusinda. Aninalo uxolo.

Ndiza kwenza ubulungisa kuni, kuba ndinguThixo wobulungisa, kwaye ndinguThixo wempindezelo. Ukanti, niya kuma ngokukrakra nichasene nam, nizithethelela, nicinga ukuba ngandlel’ ithile ndiya koyika iindlela zenu zobusathana zokuthi: yenza oko ukuthandayo, kuya kuba ngumthetho opheleleyo. Aninalo uxolo. Ndiya kubuyisela, kwaye nithi ndinguThixo onothando (nto leyo endiyiyo), kodwa ke nilindele ukuba ndingabohlwayi abo bangcolise indalo yam, abo bathuthumbisa baza babulala okuphela koNyana wam ozelweyo njengoko wazinikela waba yintlawulelo yabo bonke; ewe, nilindele ukuba ndingabohlwayi ngenxa yeendlela zenu ezingento yanto ngoxa nibulele, nathuthumbisa, nenzakalisa abamsulwa kwaye nilambise abantwana bam abancinci ngenxa yeenjongo zenu.

Inene, uSathana ngokwakhe uya kutshabalalisa yonke into eyam ngenxa yenzuzo yakhe yokubawa, kwaye nangoko nizama ukundityhola ngobuyatha kwizigwebo zam ezilungileyo xa ndiza kukhusela abam. Iintsuku zenu zokulahlekisa abantwana bam abancinci sele ziza kuphela. Yabonani! Irhamncwa lenu nomprofeti wobuxoki baya kutshatyalaliswa ngokupheleleyo, kwaye ndiya kubaphosa bephila kwelo chibi likhulu livutha umlilo nesulfure; baya kunilinda ukuba nihlanganyele nabo kwilifa lenu.

Umgwebo omkhulu uphezu kwakho, owu, nkosana yobumnyama; ubukumkani bakho nabo bonke abakulandelayo sele buza kutshatyalaliswa ngokupheleleyo. Kuba aningoothixo, kwaye aninakuzihlangula nina zinto zingento yanto esandleni sam, kwaye ndiya kunibopha ebumnyameni obunzulu enibunqwenela kangaka, nditsho nakwenzonzobila enzulu ndiya kunibopha ngetyathanga elikhulu eningenakuze niliqhawule. Ewe, enzonzweni niya kuba lusuku olunye, kuba usuku olunye kuYehova lunjengeminyaka eliwaka, neminyaka eliwaka injengosuku olunye. Kwaye nangoko ndiya kunikhulula okwexeshana elincinane ukuze nihende kwaye niqokelele abokugqibela kwabo bamasikizi abaya kulandela nina neendlela zenu. Kwaye ke ndiya kunidla nonke ngomlilo, kwaye iitrone zomgwebo ziya kumiswa, kwaye ndiya kunigweba nonke nina benzi bobubi, kwaye niya kuya kwisohlwayo esingunaphakade kude nobuso beNkosi. Niya kutsha phambi kweengelosi naphambi kweMvana, kwaye ziya kunibona nisesichengeni nasesohlwayweni, kuba nizonile zonke, kwaye niya kuhlawula ngokupheleleyo izono zenu ngonaphakade.

Niya kuvalelwa ke entolongweni yenu, kwaye niya kulityalwa, kwaye anisoze nikhunjulwe kwakhona, kuba izinto zangaphambili ziya kudlula zilityalwe. Kuba, yabonani, ndiya kwenza izulu elitsha nomhlaba omtsha okuhlala ubulungisa; kwaye mna, Yahweh, uThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade woothixo bonke, kuba akukho namnye onjengam, yabonani, ndiya kwenza zonke izinto zibe ntsha, kuba mna, Yahweh, ndikuthethile! UYahweh, uThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade, uya kwenza itrone yakhe emhlabeni, kwaye indawo yam yokuhlala iya kuba phakathi kwabantu, kwaye anisoze niphinde nibe kho, owu, nina zinto zingento yanto, kwaye niya kutsha ngonaphakade kwintolongo yenu elityelweyo yentuthumbo eningenakuze nisinde kuyo, kuba ndiya kubeka itywina kwaye akukho namnye unokuliqhawula.

Ukanti, uninzi lwenu oluvelana noSathana lundityhola ngobuyatha ngokuthatha ubulungisa nempindezelo ezandleni zam ukuze ndihlangule abo bazenze bafanelwe sisohlwayo esingunaphakade emalangabini. Yivani amazwi am, owu, bemi bomhlaba: uNyana wam uyeza, kwaye uza nempindezelo enkulu, umsindo, nengqumbo ngenxa yobubi obenziwe zizandla zenu. Amaxesha omgwebo, nkqu nomgwebo wosuku olukhulu lukaKrestu sele uza kuqhambuka emhlabeni wonke, kwaye akuyi kubakho nyango kwabakhohlakeleyo, akukho nyango kuSathana, kwaye akukho nyango kubo bonke abazimanye naye, njengoko bonke baya kuya kwintshabalalo engunaphakade, kwaye ndiya kubuyisela kubo konke abakwenzileyo kwabamsulwa.

Ngoko ke, ndiya kubuyisela iminqweno yeentliziyo zabo ezikhohlakeleyo nayo yonke imisebenzi yabo emibi kubo ngonaphakade. Bonani umsindo wam ubila kwaye nazi ukuba amaxesha okubuyiselwa kwezinto zonke asekufuphi, kwaye akusekho manani aseleyo kwiiwotshi ezulwini, kuba onke amanani awile ebusweni bewotshi, kuba ixesha liphelile! Ndicebisa bonke abafunda la mazwi ukuba babalekele kunyana wam uYesu Krestu benguquko kwaye bamenze iNkosi; kuba akukho namnye uya kuza kum ngaphandle kukaYesu Krestu, kuba alikho elinye igama endilinikele usindiso, kwaye zonke izithixo zenu ziya kutsha. Lisekho ixesha losindiso, kwaye nabani na obiza egameni leNkosi kude kube sekupheleni uya kusindiswa. Ngoko balekelani kuYesu Krestu ngoku nisengakwazi, kuba usuku lweNkosi lufikile, kwaye ndiya kukhulula uNyana wam, kwaye ingqumbo yeMvana iya kwaneliswa. Mange uNyana hleze aqumbe, nize nitshabalale endleleni, xa ingqumbo yakhe ivutha kancinane: Banoyolo bonke abakholose ngaye.

7Kusuka kuYeshua HaMashiach: Indudumo yesixhenxe!

Ndiveni kwaye nindive kakuhle, nina baprofeti bobuxoki eningaligqwetha iLizwi lam elingcwele, kuba aningabam, kwaye aninawo umoya wam oyingcwele, kwaye nihlasele uMtshakazi wam, nkqu neBandla lam endilibizileyo! Yivani kwaye nithobele isilumkiso sam, owu, nina sizwe esingakholwayo nesigwenxa nesiphambukileyo, kuba niya kuphendula ngesenzo ngasinye esibi! Yivani amazwi am esilumkiso, nina nilahlekisa iBandla lam, kuba ingqumbo yam iyatshisa ngani, kuba ndiyiMvana kaThixo! Kuba uninzi lwenu luya kuzimela emiqolombeni, nasemaweni eentaba. Kwaye niya kuthi kwiintaba nasemaweni, wani phezu kwethu, nize nisifihle ebusweni balowo uhleli etroneni, nakwingqumbo yeMvana: Kuba usuku olukhulu lwengqumbo yakhe lufikile, kwaye ngubani na oya kuba nako ukuma?

Kuba ihlabathi liya kukubona ukubonakala kwam, kwaye ukuzamazama okukhulu kuya kufika kubo bonke, kwaye intshabalalo yesiquphe iya kulithimba ihlabathi njengoko uMtshakazi wam exhwilwa esusiwa ekhuselwe. Kuba ndiya kumsusa umthinteli, kwaye bonke ububi buya kukhululwa ngokupheleleyo, kwaye umgwebo omkhulu uya kuwiswa uvela emazulwini, kanye njengoko kubhaliwe kuloo ncwadi inkulu yokutyhilwa kwam, nkqu nencwadi yeSityhilelo! Ngoko ke ndivakalisa umgwebo ngani, nonke eningcolisa iBandla lam: iintsuku zenu zibaliwe, kwaye kungekudala iintliziyo zenu ziya kunityhafisa xa ingqumbo yam ityhilwa. Nina baprofeti bobuxoki abangento yanto abangene eBandleni lam, nifundise ubugqwirha obuxutyiweyo nobuhedeni neLizwi lam elingcwele neendlela zam ezingcwele, kwaye niya kuhlawula.

Nibangele abantwana bam abancinci ukuba bone kwaye bathathwe kwiinkathazo ezinkulu, endiya kuzibuyisela kuni ngekhulu ekugwebeni kwam kuni. Nigqwethe amagama kwizibhalo zam ezingcwele, nizenze iikopi ezingeyonyani zezibhalo zam ezingcwele ngokuzibhala ngokutsha kwaye nitshintshe amazwi kaThixo Ongunaphakade, nkqu namazwi kaYahweh, kwaye niya kuhlawula ngonaphakade. Ningenise imibhalo eyonakeleyo eBandleni lam ngendlela efanayo neyokuba ookhokho benu abangento yanto batyhefa amaqula kwaye batyhefa ukutya kanye okunikelwe ukuxhasa uluntu, kwaye niya kuhlawula ngonaphakade. Lonke ilizwi elingenamsebenzi enilithethileyo, yonke imali yokugqibela emdaka eniyigcinela intshabalalo yenu, yonke ingcinga embi enikhe nanayo, yonke inkuthazo embi enikhe nayilandela iya kunikwa nina, kwaye niya kuhlawula, kude kube yeyona peni yokugqibela; kwaye aniyi kuba nako ukuma phambi kwam, kwaye niya kuthatha indawo yenu emalangabini uBawo awulungiselele uMtyholi neengelosi zakhe.

Kodwa qondani ukuba aningommangaliso kwaye akukho zimanga kuni, kuba nina ningabapostile bobuxoki, abasebenzi abakhohlisayo, niziguqula nibe ngabapostile bakaKrestu. Niya kuqhubeka nemisebenzi yenu nizalisa izono zenu ngokupheleleyo kwaye nizalise unaphakade ngokupheleleyo ngesohlwayo nentuthumbo yenu, kuba umsindo wam unamandla ngani, kwaye niya kuhlanganyela nooyihlo emlilweni. Kuba nina ningumgubo phakathi kwengqolowa, kwaye ndiya kunincothula ngokupheleleyo; kuba uBawo, nkqu noYahweh, uvakalisile ukuba anisoze nibe neengcambu okanye ilifa, kuba imbewu yenu yeyomkhohlakeleyo, kwaye nonke ningababulali namaxoki. Kuba ndathini kooyihlo baseJudiya kule minyaka emininzi yeJubili edlulileyo? Yazini oku, nonke nina nizelwe kunyeliso, nkqu nonyeliso oluchasene noYahweh njengoko ooyihlo abangento yanto bazama ukuguqula umfanekiso kaThixo, owadalwa ngawo uAdam, waba ngamasikizi enzelwe intshabalalo yoluntu, iintsuku zenu sele ziphelile. Kuba nguwuphi kubaprofeti abangazange batshutshiswe ngooyihlo? Kwaye bababulele abo babonisa ngaphambili ngokuza koLungileyo; enibe ngabangcatshi nababulali bakhe ngoku. Aniyi kusinda, nokuba ningaya kude kangakanani emhlabeni ukuze nizimele kwingqumbo yam; ingqumbo yam iya kunifumana, kuba akukho nto inokufihlwa emehlweni am, kwaye umhlaba ngowam nokuzala kwawo, kuba ndiwuthenge ngegazi lam elanyeliswa ngookhokho benu, nkqu nokutywina isiphelo senu emlilweni. Ndiveni, owu, nina bawileyo, nkqu nani nonke enandihlabayo, kuba natsho ezintliziyweni zenu, simoyisile uYahweh saza sabulala unyana wakhe awamthuma kuthi ukuba oyise umhlaba, kwaye soyisile ngonaphakade, kuba uYeshua ufile, kwaye navuya ngobuyatha! Nina bawileyo, nkqu nina zinkosana zeli hlabathi liwileyo, nina boSathana, niya kutsha nonke emlilweni ongacimekiyo ngenxa yentiyo yenu engcwele neendlela zenu ezikhohlakeleyo zokungathobeli nentshabalaliso. Njengoko nakwenzayo komnye wabancinane kwaba, bazalwana bam, nakwenza kum. Owu, nina balindi abawileyo, nkqu nina zithunywa zezulu ezingathobeliyo ezashiya isikhundla senu sokuqala, aninalo uxolo! Nilandelé ooSathana abazama ukutshabalalisa indalo kaYahweh, kude kuse ekundibulaleni mna, Yesu Krestu, xa ndazinikela ngokwam ezandleni zakhe njengePasika egqibeleleyo yehlabathi, ngaloo ndlela nditywina inkululeko yoluntu kwaye nditywina isiphelo senu kunaphakade kwimililo emikhulu engenakuze icinywe! Ukumkani wenu omkhulu ndiza kumoyisa nomprofeti wenu wobuxoki ndiza kumoyisa, kwaye baya kuphoswa bephila echibini lomlilo elivutha isulfure; kwaye apho baya kulinda ukuba nize kubajoyina, kwaye apho niya kuhlala nigutyungelwe ngumlilo nesulfure, kwaye umgwebo wenu sele uza kuqala!

Mtshakazi wam, musa ukoyika! Amazwi am omgwebo ayingawenu kodwa ngawokukuthuthuzela, kuba ndikusindisile kwingqumbo esele iza kukhululwa, kwaye ndiza kukufihla endaweni ekhuselekileyo nenomvuzo omkhulu, kwaye uya kuba nam ngonaphakade. Kuba ndikukhethile, kuba ndikubizile, kwaye uyeyona ndyebo yam inkulu, kwaye uyeyona ndyebo kaYahweh inkulu; ukwayiperile yexabiso elikhulu, kuba ndinikele ngako konke kwam ngenxa yakho. Ndiyamcebisa uMtshakazi wam ukuba afune ukuqonda aze eze kum efuna ubulumko, kuba ndiza kumfundisa. Kuba abanye benu balandele ezinye zeendlela zaba baprofeti bobuxoki; kuphela buyelani ngenguquko eNkosini yenu, kwaye ndiza kunamkela.

Kuba banilukuhlile ngobugqwirha bobuKrestu, kwaye nikholelwe kubuxoki obuninzi enathengiselwa bona, naxa naxelelwa ukuba yinyaniso, kodwa bubuxoki obuyenyanyekayo. La maqula angenamanzi azamile ukunitsala kum, kwaye oko akunakwenzeka, ngoko musani ukoyika, kuba andisayi kwaliwa! Ndiza kuqhubeka ndinikhulula ukuze nikhululeke ngokwenene, kwaye andisayi kuthuliswa! Ndinilwela ngeendlela ezininzi, ezininzi eningazaziyo. Kuba nonke naphawulwa ningekazalwa, kuba uBawo wayenazi nasesizalweni sikanina, kwaye walinda ngamnye kuni ixesha elide eningenakuliqonda, kwaye nanisoloko nisembindini wentliziyo yakhe. Naphawulwa ekuzalweni, kwaye izithunywa zam zanigcina ngomyalelo wam kwaye zanilinda zanikhusela, kuba zithunyelwe loo nto. Ngaba oku kuyakumangalisa? Uya kuphulaphula na oko amaHebhere akuthileyo? Kodwa kwesiphi na isithunywa sezulu wakha wathi, hlala ngasekunene kwam, ndide ndizenze iintshaba zakho isihlalo seenyawo zakho? Azingabo bonke na oomoya abalungiselelayo, abathunyelwe ukuba balungiselele abo baya kuba ziindlalifa zosindiso?

Mtshakazi wam, niziindlalifa zosindiso kum, Yesu Krestu. Nibiziwe kum; nikhuselwe kum nakum; ninikwe ithuba lokuva intshumayelo yokholo ngam ukuze nindenze iNkosi yenu, kwaye ke ngoko ndinitywinile ngaloo moya ungcwele wesithembiso. Yazini ukuba elo tywina alinakwaphulwa, kwaye sele niza kukhululwa ngokupheleleyo kuse ephakadeni, kuba aninalwazi ngobuninzi bomsebenzi wendalo endiza kuwugqibezela kuni, Mtshakazi wam! Yazini ukuba abawileyo nokhula lwabo banomona ngani, kwaye bayakuthiya nantoni na ayithandayo uYahweh, kwaye ndiza kuniphindezela ngokupheleleyo ezintlokweni zabo, kuba ndim oza kutyumza intloko yale nyoka yamandulo, kuba kubhengeziwe, kwaye oko kubhengeza uYahweh akunakuguqulwa.

Akukho mntu unokunahlula kum; akukho bungakanani bobubi obunokwaphula elo tywina; ningabam kwaye ningaboBawo, kuba konke andinika kona uBawo ndiza kukubuyisela kuThixo ukuze sibe yinto yonke kuyo yonke into! Kwaye undinike zonke izinto ezulwini nasemhlabeni, ngaphandle kokuba etroneni yakhe mkhulu kunam!

Kwaye mna, Krestu Yesu, ndibhengeza la mazwi kuni, kwaye ndim, Yesu Krestu, obhengeza umgwebo kwiintshaba zenu, kwaye ndiza kuwuzalisekisa kude kuse kwisenti yokugqibela. Mtshakazi wam, uthando lwam ngani alunqandeki, kwaye ndinilungiselele indawo, kuba endlwini kaBawo zininzi iindawo zokuhlala! Kungekudala niza kutshata nam ngonaphakade, kwaye niya kuhlala ninam, kwaye ekugqibeleni niya kuqonda ubukhulu benjongo yenu kum enadalelwa ukuyizalisekisa!

Kuba kum, uYahweh unikunike ikamva nenjongo, kwanenjongo yeminyaka sele iza kutyhilwa kuni, kuba ndiyeza! Kwaye andisayi kwaliwa. Khangelani ukubonakala kwam. Khangelani ukukhanya kokufika kwam, kuba kusemnyango wokuqaqamba emhlabeni njengoko ndibonakala emafini ngesidumo esikhulu, ngelizwi lesithunywa esikhulu, nangexilongo likaThixo, kuba ndiyanixelela inyaniso ukuba nezithunywa zezulu zilindele elo xilongo sele liza kukhala, kwaye zikulungele! Nilungele mna kwaye nijonge, nilinde, niqwalasele njengoko kuyalelwe; kuba kungekudala niza kundibona nozuko lwam andambathise lona uBawo, kuba ndiza kunambathisa nani, kuba niya kufana nam, kwaye niya kundibona njengoko ndinjalo.
