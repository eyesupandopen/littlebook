Kwicawa Yam, Umzimba Wam, Kwanomtshakazi Wam: Yivani Amazwi E Nkosi Yenu!

Kwicawa yam, uMzimba wam, kwanomtshakazi wam: Ndiyanicela ukuba niboleke iindlebe zenu kubulumko bam nize nive ilizwi lam, kuba ngoku ndiza kuthetha nani. Niyamamela na? Zibekeleni ecaleni iimfundiso zenu ezahlukeneyo nezenziwe ngabantu kunye nezo kuthiwa ziimfundiso ngezobuthixo, nize nithobele iintliziyo zenu kwisiBhalo esiNgcwele, kwanelizwi lam elinyulu. Zibekeleni ecaleni iimfundiso zenu ezinegwele, kuba nawuphi na umxube wamaZwi kaThixo, kwanelizwi elinyulu likaThixo, ubilisa yonke intlama kwaye anisenaso isibhalo. Kuba ndiliLizwi likaThixo elenziwe inyama, kwaye ndihlala ngaphakathi kwenu, kwaye ndiyanibizela ekhayeni lenu emazulwini kungekudala.

Zibekeni kude izichazi-magama zenu, niyeke ukumamela amazwi amaninzi angenanyaniso, kwanamazwi angengawam; kodwa endaweni yoko, bambani amaZwi am ezintliziyweni zenu niwathelekise nesibhalo ukuze nibe nokholo kumaZwi am nakum, uKristu Yesu. Kuba ukuba umntu uza kuni engayithethi imfundiso yeNkosi, kwanamaZwi kaThixo anyulu, musani nokumnqwenelela impumelelo. Kuba ndiya kunenza nilumke kwisiBhalo sam esiNgcwele, kwanelizwi lobomi, kwaye ubulumko bam abonakalisi. Ngoko ke, qokelelani ubutyebi benu ezulwini, apho kungonakalisi mhlwa namabhu, nalapho amasela angagqobhoziyo okanye ebe. Kuba apho bukhona ubutyebi benu, ziya kuba lapho neentliziyo zenu. Bantwana bam, ndingubutyebi benu, ngoko gcwalisani iingcinga neentliziyo zenu kum ndedwa ngengqondo enye.

Aniwafundanga na amaZwi atyhila le mfihlelo inkulu kaThixo? Kuba sinobu butyebi ezityeni zodongwe ukuze amandla abalaseleyo avela kuThixo, hayi kuthi. Lowo uThixo angathanda ukumazisa ukuba buyintoni na ubutyebi bobuqaqawuli bale mfihlelo phakathi kweeNtlanga, obunguKristu ngaphakathi kwenu, ithemba lozuko. Ngoko musani ukoyika, kuba nizelwe ngokutsha ngomoya wam ongcwele; ngoko ke, waqondeni amaZwi elizwi lam. Kuba ndiya kunixelela ubulumko obuya kunithuthuzela bunigcine kude kwinkohliso enamandla ethe yagubungela ihlabathi; kuba ihlabathi libotshelelwe umgwebo kwaye likweyikweni olukhulu nosizi.

Ngoko ke, ndize kuthetha nani ukuze ndingqine kwabalumkileyo phakathi kwenu ngeenkokeli ezimbini zehlabathi ezikhoyo ngoku ezibonakala zingabelusi abalungileyo, kwananjengeegusha ezikhethwe phakathi komhlambi ukuba zikhonze emsebenzini woluntu, ezibonakala zize kukhokelela iigusha ezilahlekileyo zeBhabhiloni eyimfihlelo kukhuseleko. Kodwa aba belusi babini banomhlaza abazozigusha, kodwa ngamarhamncwa amabi azifihle njengeegusha kwaye avela kwesinye isibaya, esikamalusi oyinamba ngokwakhe. Umalusi oyinamba ayingomalusi wokwenene weegusha, kuba umhlambi wakhe asinguwo umhlambi kodwa ngumkhosi wabawiweyo abawileyo eluzukweni baza baba zizinto zonke ezingahlambulukanga ezifanele intshabalalo. Ndithetha ngenkohliso yangoku esetyenziswayo ukulungiselela ukuvela kwabathathu abangcwele. Kuba kukho abathathu abangqina ezulwini, uYise, uLizwi, noMoya oyiNgcwele: kwaye aba bathathu banye. Kwaye kukho abathathu abangcwele abavelayo (kwanokuxeliswa okukhulu kwabathathu abangqina ezulwini): inamba, irhamncwa, nomprofeti wobuxoki (okwalirhamncwa). Kwaye la marhamncwa angenangqondo enzelwe intshabalalo, kuba ikamva lawo ngumlilo ovuthayo nesulfure kwelo chibi lomlilo elivutha amadangatye amhlophe aluhlaza kubumnyama obukhulu ngonaphakade.

Ndithetha ngemeko yabo yangoku nolawulo lwabo lwangoku, kuba abakatyhilwa ngokupheleleyo, kuba umthinteli usekho; kodwa ndiya kubachaza kwabathembekileyo bam, kwanabo banamehlo okubona neendlebe zokuva. Qondani ukuba aba bakhohlisi basebenza inkohliso enkulu nangaphambi kokutyhilwa kwabo nokuxhotyiswa kwabo kokwenene, kuba baya kunikwa igunya kwinamba; kuba oko kunikwe yona ixesha elifutshane kakhulu nangendlela elinganiselweyo ukuvavanya nokulinga umhlaba, nokulwa nabangcwele nokuboyisa. Kwaye nangona imfihlelo yobugwenxa isebenza ngamandla ngezi njongo, ukutyhilwa kwabo akukafiki kodwa kusondele.

Kuba nangoku babonakala bengamadoda amakhulu, amadoda anobubele, amadoda anesibindi nobothando, aphila ubomi bokuzincama. Kodwa musani ukukhohliswa ngabelusi abanomhlaza, kuba babonakala becocekile kwaye benyulu kwizihlwele esele zibamangalele kwaye kungekudala ziya kuthi: Ngubani ofana nerhamncwa? (Kuba nangoku balahlekisa abalahlekileyo, abathakathiweyo bubugqwirha babo.) Kodwa kwabo banamehlo okubona neendlebe zokuva, babonakala benjengoko benjalo ngokwenene: kuba bangabelusi abanomhlaza abafumana amandla abo kwinamba enkulu, kwanakuSathana ngokwakhe. Kuba omnye uya kuphuma elwandle kwaye ngoku usebenza emfihlekweni (ngasemva kwesigqubuthelo sesidenge esidala esixhasiweyo) ukuze kutshatyalaliswe iBhabhiloni eyimfihlelo; kwaye omnye uya kuphuma emhlabeni kwaye uthunyelwe ngobuxoki kwabo baseZweni leSithembiso kwaye uya kuba ngumprofeti wobuxoki wendlu yesikhungu kaSathana, kwanomMoshiach wabo wobuxoki.

Kuba umprofeti wobuxoki olirhamncwa usebenzisana nerhamncwa, elilawula emfihlekweni, kwaye bobabini basebenza kunye ukuqhubela phambili ngokupheleleyo imfihlelo yobugwenxa, ukuze batyhilwe kungekudala; kuba amaxesha abo afikile, nangona bengenabukumkani okwangoku. Musani ukunikezela kwinkohliso, kuba umalusi omnye onomhlaza olawula emfihlekweni ulawula isidenge esidala, ngelixa omnye umalusi onomhlaza obonakala etshutshiswa sisidenge esidala esebenza kunye nomalusi onomhlaza olawula emfihlekweni. Kuba benza ngathi baziintshaba ngeli xesha, kodwa bonke bavela kumhlambi omnye onomhlaza, bezalisekisa iinjongo zenamba.

Ngelishwa, abaninzi abacinga ukuba basindisiwe ngokuvuma uYesu njengeNkosi ngentliziyo engakholwayo kwimilebe yokungakholwa (kuba ngentliziyo umntu ukholwa kuse ebulungiseni) bathakathiwe zezi demoni, kwaye baya kukhohlisa abanyulwa bam kanye ukuba kunokwenzeka, kwanabo babalandela ngokungazi. Kuba aba belusi abanyulu, kwaye iingubo zabo zenziwe ngoboya obumdaka nobugulayo, kuba bangabelusi abanomhlaza abalusa umhlambi onomhlaza, kwaye ngeziqhamo zabo niya kubazi.

Abanyulu kwaye abamhlophe njengekhephu, njengeegusha zam; kuba ndiyiMvana engenabala nasisiphako, eyakhethwa emhlambini ukuba ibe yiPasika egqibeleleyo yexesha lonke. Ndihlambulula iigusha zam egazini lam lentlawulelo, kwaye akukho mhlaza ufunyanwa kuzo. Nangona besithi bathunyiwe nguThixo uSomandla, uThixo uSomandla akabathumanga. Nangona abaprofeti abaninzi bobuxoki bengqina ngobuxoki ngaye, andimthumanga, kodwa yinamba. Kuba ndingumbingeleli omkhulu ngonaphakade ngokohlobo lukaMelkitsedeke, kwaye akukho ezinye iimvana ngaphandle kwam, uKristu Yesu.

Usindiso lukwigama lam ndedwa, kuba akukho gama linye elinikiweyo phakathi kwabantu elinokusindisa abantu ngaphandle kwelam, iNkosi uYesu. Kuba akukho namnye umntu endijolise ukuba afumane amaZwi am ongayi kuweva. Ukuba bayawamkela amazwi am ngokholo okanye ukuba bayawala ngokungakholwa lukhetho uYise alunike umntu ngamnye ukuba alwenze, kuba uThixo akazinyanzeli yena nothando lwakhe kumntu namnye. Kwaye ndiyeza kuzisa umgwebo phezu kwabo bonke nokohlwaya bonke abangendawo phakathi kwabo, ngezenzo zabo zonke zokungendawo abazenzileyo ngokungendawo, nangawo onke amazwi abo aqatha, athethwe ngaboni abangendawo nxamnye naye.

Ndithethe nani ngeendlela ezininzi, kwaye kwimisebenzi yam emininzi ndithumele abaprofeti bam nabalungiseleli bam, kuba ixesha liphelile. Kwaye ke ndiya kuthetha ngezinto ezingaqinisekiswanga kwizizukulwana zangaphambili kodwa ngoku zityhiliwe, kuba amaxesha okutyhilwa kwam afikile. Kuba ndiza kutyhilwa ndivela emazulwini, mna kanye, kuba ndingumMesiya wokwenene, kwanaye uYeshua HaMashiach. Kwaye ndiya kuthetha ngemizekeliso kwabo bangaphandle, kodwa abo banamehlo okubona neendlebe zokuva baya kuqonda, kuba ndiya kukuqinisekisa emoyeni wam odalwe ngaphakathi kwabo.

Ndize kuthetha ngamazwi awanikiweyo uYohane uMtyhili, kwaye ndiya kuyenza icace indlela kuni ukuba niya kuwamkela amaZwi am nize nithobe iintliziyo zenu kwilizwi lam.

Yabonani! Ndithetha ngabalawuli ababini abazayo abasebenza kunye, kwaye baya kulikhohlisa iHlabathi baze bathathe abaninzi kunye nabo baye emlilweni wenzonzobila. Kwanabo amagama abo angabhalwanga eNcwadini yoBomi baya kubalandela kwaye baya kuthwaxwa kukulunywa yinyoka, elumkiswe ngako ihlabathi, kwanophawu ekungekho mntu uya kuphiliswa kulo; kuba izilumkiso zezokugqibela, kwaye oko uThixo akutshilo akunakuguqulwa.

Ezi nkokeli, kwanabelusi abanomhlaza, ngoku zizifihla njengabasebenzi bakarhulumente beBhabhiloni eyimfihlelo. Kuba phantsi kweengubo zabelusi abanomhlaza abazinxibileyo (kuba ngamnye uzifihle ngendlela yakhe), ngamarhamncwa alambileyo ankanuko yawo ingenakuze yaneliswe, kwaye baya kufuna ukuba bonke babanqule nomfanekiso werhamncwa nenamba ngokungathi banguThixo; kuba sele beza kukhululwa xa umthinteli esusiwe.

Kuba kungekudala kakhulu nangesiquphe, xa ndihlanganisela iCawa yam kum emafini, intshabalalo yesiquphe iya kuligubungela ihlabathi; kuba baya kuyitshabalalisa iBhabhiloni eyimfihlelo, kwaye imfazwe enkulu iya kuqhambuka elizweni kwaye intshabalalo yesiquphe iya kuligubungela ihlabathi. Abathathu abangcwele baya kuphuma eluthuthwini lwamabhodlo nesiphithiphithi esishiywe ngabakhweli bamahashe, abaza kukhululwa kungekudala emva kokuba uMtshakazi wam exhwilwe waya kum emazulwini. (Kuba kungoko kuphela ndiya kuvula amatywina omyolelo wam kuba iCawa yam isindisiwe kwingqumbo ezayo, kwaye ndiya kubahlangula.) Baya kuphuma eluthuthwini lwemfazwe, isibetho, indlala, nentswela-bulungisa enkulu, nasekufeni kwezihlwele ezininzi, ezininzi.

Baya kunika uxolo nokhuseleko, kwaye baya kuthetha ubuxoki obuninzi abaya kubudlulisa njengobulumko bukaThixo, kwaye baya kuthetha izinto ezininzi ezinyelisayo nezilahlekayo; kwaye omnye uya kukhomba komnye, kwaye esandleni sabo kuya kuba sisisombululo sabo, kwanetyhefu yabo engenakuguqulwa okanye ixolelwe. Kuba wonke umntu ozinikela kukulunywa yinyoka, uya kuhlala ephawulwe ngonaphakade. Kodwa elo liya kuba lixabiso lokungena kuluntu lwabo olutsha, kwanolawulo lwabo olutsha olwenziwe nguSathana neengelosi zakhe abalwenzela ngokobudenge imbewu yabo enyanyekayo.

Baya kuthembisa uluntu olutsha olukhululekileyo ekufeni nasekutshatyalalisweni okuzayo kwihlabathi lonke, xa intshabalalo yesiquphe ifika ekuhlanganisweni kweCawa yam. (Bafuna ukuvuselela ixesha labo legolide elatshatyalaliswa nguThixo kunogumbe kaNowa.) Baya kunika uxolo, ubomi obude, inkululeko kwizifo, impilo nokhuseleko, kwaye baya kuthembisa usuku olutsha. Kodwa aba belusi babini banomhlaza (abonakala ngathi bavela emhlambini) baya kuthi uThixo uSomandla ubathumile, kwaye ihlabathi lonke liya kubamangalela, kodwa abangobelusi. Ndiyanibuza, inamba ingalusa umhlambi? Inamba yakha yazalusa nini iigusha, ngaphandle kokutywina intshabalalo yazo epheleleyo nokwahlukana noYise weziKhanyiso, kwanaye uYahweh? Kuba irhamncwa nomprofeti wobuxoki baya kufumana amandla abo kwinamba, kwaye bangamakhoboka eenkanuko nentando yenamba.

Ngexesha lokutyhilwa kwabo, baya kulahla izifihlo zabo zabasebenzi bakarhulumente abathobekileyo; kwaye bonke ubungangamsha, kwanazo iingubo zabo ezinomhlaza zeentlonze zeegusha ezinomhlaza nendalo yabo yokwenene, amandla, neenjongo zabo ziya kutyhilwa. Ihlabathi liya kubamangalela neendlela zabo, kwanokubanqulela ekutshabalaleni kwabo. Baya kungena eqongeni lehlabathi ngamandla amakhulu, kwaye umfelandawonye wookumkani abalishumi uya kulawula. Kwanangoku, umfelandawonye sele uwuhlele umkhosi wawo emfihlekweni kuhlaselo oluyimfihlo kwiBhabhiloni eyimfihlelo. Bazilungiselele ngokufihlakeleyo, kwaye ngokuqinisekileyo balungelelanise uhlaselo, kwaye bayihlasela ngokuvakalayo iBhabhiloni eyimfihlelo ngokomgwebo owabhengezwa nguThixo oza kuwela phezu kwayo, apho ingasayi kuphinda ivuke.

Kuba ubugwenxa bayo bufikile phambi koPhezukonke, nezono zayo phambi kwetrone enkulu yezulu, kwanaphambi koThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade, kuba akukho mkhulu kunaye; kwaye umgwebo ubhengeziwe phezu kwayo. Kuba uEl Shaddai usebenzisa nezandla zeentshaba zakhe ukuzalisekisa izicwangciso zakhe kwaye nezitha zakhe ziyalithobela ilizwi lakhe; kuba ngubani onokumelana noThixo uSomandla? Musani ukwenza impazamo, abelusi ababini banomhlaza ngoku basebenzisana ngasemva komfanekiso wesidenge. Abakatyhilwa eHlabathini, kwaye xa betyhilwa, ihlabathi liya kubamangalela lize libalandele ekufeni kwabo. Kodwa abazozigusha konke konke, kodwa ngamarhamncwa anomsindo ngaphakathi aza kunikwa amandla amakhulu kwinamba kungekudala, kuba oko kunikwe yona ixesha elifutshane. Kuba baya kuzibeka nxamnye, nasendaweni, yomalusi olungileyo; ngaphandle kwabo banokuqonda kokomoya abaya kubona umhlaza, kwanobubi nokonakala okupheleleyo okungaphakathi kubo, kuba banendalo engahlambulukanga kwaye bakhohlakele kude kube sekuzikeni kweenzulu zabo. Sele nisazi ukuba benza ngathi bangoobani, kodwa anazi ukuba bangoobani ngokwenene, kwaye bazinike namagama obuxoki, ngoko inani alinakubalwa.

Kuba irhamncwa eliya kuphuma elwandle lilawule iintlanga sele likho, kwaye libonakala ngathi alikho ngoku kodwa eneneni lisekho, likhokela izandla zopopi ongenangqondo. Lo ngulowo ubizwa ngesiHebhere esebenzisa amazwi endawathethayo xa ndabona uSathana njengombane esiwa ezulwini. Umbani usiwa ezulwini: fundisisa ukuze ufunde. Kuba akanqweneli bafazi, akayihloneli nayiphi na ithixo: kuba uya kuziphakamisa ngaphezu kwako konke. Ndiyanibuza ngoku nize nibone ukuba indoda iyazala na umntwana? Kodwa umfazi wayo ubonakala engumfazi onabantwana, nangona bengengobantwana bayo.

Irhamncwa eliza kuphuma emhlabeni sele nilazi, kwaye abaninzi benu balandela ngokungazi, sele nikhohlisiwe ngoku bekufanele nishumayele nxamnye nenkohliso. Kuba ungowelizwe, kodwa kwisizwe sakhe akukho namnye umntu otywinweyo kuma-144,000. Kwaye ngoDan wathi, uDan yintshontsho leNgonyama: uya kutsiba eBhashan, kwaye ungowesizwe sakwaDan, kwanokuzingca ngomnombo wakhe, kuba igazi eliluhlaza lihamba emithanjeni yakhe. Uchazwa njengoKoresi wale mihla kwaye uya kuba ngumakhi wetempile ukuze azise isikizi lesiphanziso ngexesha lakhe, kwaye ngoku sele esakha kwiLizwe leSithembiso kwindlu yesikhungu kaSathana.

Bantwana bam abancinane, ndinishiya nobulumko bokucinga, kwaye ezi aziseyomfihlelo kodwa zityhiliwe ngexesha lazo. Kuphela amaxesha azo awayi kutyhilwa ehlabathini de umthinteli asuswe, kwaye kuya kwenzeka ngokukhawuleza kunokuba niqonda. Kuba ezi zinto ziza kwenzeka, kwaye akusekho xesha, kuba amanani awile ebusweni bewotshi. Hlalani nithembekile kum, kwaye xa ndiqinisekisile emoyeni wenu ukuba bangoobani, guqulani abazalwana benu. Yabonani, ukubonakala kwam kuphantse kwagqitywa.

NdinguLizwi likaThixo elenziwe inyama, kwaye ndihlala ngaphakathi kwenu.

Ndiyanibizela ekhayeni lenu emazulwini kungekudala kakhulu.

NdiyiMvana engenabala nasisiphako.

Ndingumbingeleli omkhulu ngonaphakade ngokohlobo lukaMelkitsedeke. NdingumMesiya wokwenene, kwanaye uYeshua HaMashiach.

Ndiyeza kuzisa umgwebo phezu kwabo bonke.

Ndize kuthetha ngamazwi awanikiweyo uYohane uMtyhili.

Yabonani, mna, kwanaye uKristu Yesu, ndithumele la mazwi kwabathembekileyo bam; ngoku yivani amaZwi am!
