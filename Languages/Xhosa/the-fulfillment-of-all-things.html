Ingqina eliMbaxa: uSirayeli
Okukrakra Okumnandi
Agasti 11, 2024

Inzaliseko Yezinto Zonke!

Yivani amazwi eNkosi yenu ephilayo noMsindisi, uMesiya wenu wokwenene, uYeshua HaMashiach. Qondani niphulaphule amazwi am bantwana bam kuba ngoku ndiza kuthetha malunga nokuzaliseka kwezinto zonke, kuba ukuzaliseka kwazo kufuphi. Ngoko ke qondani, ngenxa yokuba uluntu ngoku luwukhanyele umnqophiso woxolo kaYahweh kwaye lwamkhanyela uYesu waseNazarete (kuba ndingumnqophiso wenu woxolo) amaxesha emhlabeni ngoku aza kutshintsha ukusuka elubabalweni aye emgwebeni, aza kutshintsha ukusuka encebeni aye kwintlekele, aza kutshintsha ukusuka eluxolweni aye emfazweni, aza kutshintsha ukusuka ebulalini aye engqumbini, aza kutshintsha ngoku aye kwimpindezelo epheleleyo nentshabalaliso epheleleyo kwabo bahleli emhlabeni. Kwanabo bonke abaye benqaba ukundenza iNkosi kwaye bamkele uthando lwam noxolelo lwam kubo, apho ngendingabuyisele kubo kumdali wabo kwaye ndibanike ubomi obungunaphakade, bangaphinde bafe.

Kodwa endaweni yokuba umntu wemvelo eze kum ngokupheleleyo, abaninzi baye bala benekratshi elinenkani bakhetha endaweni yoko ukuhlala nooSathana nemvukelo yabo kwaye ngokungagqibekanga baye bazimanya nabo mhlawumbi bengazi, okanye besazi kakuhle ukhetho lwabo. Kwaye ke abo bakhethe ngobuyatha obunjalo ngoku baya kuba phantsi kwengqumbo kaThixo nengqumbo yemvana ekufuphi kakhulu ukuba ithululwe ngokupheleleyo. Kwaye nangona abaninzi ngeli xesha beye bala, isihlwele sezihlwele siya kuza kum kwaye sivume uYesu njengeNkosi kwaye sibize egameni likaMesiya wabo kude kube sekupheleni. Kwaye ke ngenxa kaYeshua HaMashiach baya kusindiswa. Kodwa abaninzi ngakumbi baya kutshabalala, kwanabo bonke amagama abo angazange abhalwe eNcwadini yoBomi abaxhelwa kususela ekusekweni kwehlabathi kwaye kuya kuba njalo ukhetho lwabo.

Kwaye ke ngendlela efanayo njengoko ezi nguqu zenzeka emhlabeni emgwebeni, uxolo luzinzile emazulwini kwaye imfazwe ngoku iya kuwudla ngokupheleleyo umhlaba. Kwakukho imfazwe ezulwini kwaye ummangaleli wabazalwana uphoselwe phantsi, uphoselwe phantsi, kwaye loo namba inkulu ibomvu ngoku ibotshelelwe emhlabeni kwaye ilindele umgwebo wayo kwaye umgwebo wayo awulibazisi. Ekoyisweni okupheleleyo uya kukhonza iinjongo zakhe kwizicwangciso zikanaphakade zikaYahweh, kuba neentshaba zakhe ziyalithobela ilizwi lakhe, kwaye ke unikwe kuphela ngendlela elinganiselweyo kakhulu kwaye ubukumkani bakhe buya kutshatyalaliswa ngokupheleleyo. Khangelani! Loo nyoka indala, kwanaloo namba inkulu ibomvu, ngoku iphoselwe phantsi emhlabeni, kwaye inengqumbo enkulu kuba iyazi ukuba inexesha elifutshane kuphela! Kwaye ke mna, Yeshua HaMashiach, ndiyacebisa wonke umntu ukuba abaleke asinge kwisandi selizwi lam kwaye abize egameni lam elinamandla kude kube sekupheleni kuba abo bangabathandiyo ubomi babo kude kube sekupheleni baya kuvuka baye kubukhazikhazi obungunaphakade, uvuyo, nomvuzo kwaye mkhulu umvuzo wabo! Kuba bonke ababiza egameni lam kude kube sekupheleni baya kusindiswa, nangaluphi na ulwimi ababiza ngalo kwaye mna Yesu Krestu, okwanguYeshua HaMashiach, ndiyawavakalisa la mazwi!

Kwaye ke iminqophiso nezithembiso zakudala ziya kuzaliseka, kwanayo yonke iminqophiso yalo lonke ixesha kuba injalo ivangeli engunaphakade. Kwaye njengoko imfazwe izinza phezu komhlaba, uxolo ngoku lumiselwe emazulwini apho amazulu ngoku alungiselelwe ukwamkela abathembekileyo kuKrestu, kwanabo bafe bethembekile kuKrestu, nabo baphilayo nabasalayo kwaye baphila ubomi babo kuKrestu. Kuba bonke abahamba ngomthetho womoya wobomi kuKrestu Yesu kwixesha lam lobabalo baya kuqokelelwa kwaye bafumane umvuzo wabo ongunaphakade kwaye baya kuqonda kwaye baya kumiswa kwiindidi zabo ukuze bazalisekise injongo kanaphakade kaYahweh abadalelwe yona ngokutsha kuKrestu. Kwaye ke umtshato woMyeni noMtshakazi uza kwenzeka ngoku, kwaye kuya kubakho umbhiyozo omkhulu emazulwini kubukhazikhazi nozuko olungunaphakade!

Kuba kwasekuqaleni, uThixo wadala uAdam, waza kuAdam wadala uEva ukuze abe liqabane lakhe lanaphakade kwaye abe ngunina wabo bonke abaphilayo kwaye babe nesiqhamo kwaye bande. Bayalelwa ukuba kuyo yonke imithi esemnqubeni wase-Eden, banokutya ngokukhululekileyo kuwo nawuphi na umthi ngaphandle komthi wokwazi okulungileyo nokubi. Kwaye ke ngesilingo sobuqhetseba senyoka uEva wakhohliswa, waza wathatha kwisiqhamo esalelweyo waza watya, waza wanika umyeni wakhe uAdam, waza watya esazi kwaye ngaloo ndlela isono sonakalisa uluntu kwaye ekufeni baye bafa ukusukela ngoko. Kwaye ke uAdam noEva abazange balizalisekise ubizo lwabo oluphezulu lokulawula nokoyisa umhlaba ngobulungisa nokuba nesiqhamo nokwanda nokugubungela umhlaba ngobulungisa kodwa endaweni yoko bona baza baphila umvuzo wesono de loo mvuzo wafikelela kuvuthondaba ekufeni kwabo kwaye ekufeni uluntu luye lwafa ukusukela ngoko. Kwaye ekuvukeni kwabalungileyo baya kufumana umvuzo wabo nembuyekezo kuba babenyanisekile kude kube sekupheleni.

Kwaye ke kunjalo ukuba uYahweh, kulwazi lwakhe lwangaphambili lwanaphakade, wayesazi ngaphambi kokuba kwenzeke ukuba uluntu luya kuzikhethela isono nokufa kwasekuqaleni. Kwaye u-El Shaddai, nakuthando lwakhe olukhulu aluthanda ngalo uluntu, (nayo yonke indalo yakhe), wabonelela ngobabalo nenceba engenakulinganiswa xa wavakalisa ukuba uya kubeka ubutshaba phakathi kwembewu kaSathana nembewu yomfazi. Imbewu kaSathana yasityumza isithende sam eKalvari ekuzalisekeni kwamazwi kaYahweh kwaye ngoku ndiza kuhambela phambili ukutyumza ngokupheleleyo intloko yenyoka kuba linjalo ikamva layo nabo bonke abayilandelayo, kwaye njengoko kubhaliwe, kuya kuqhubeka kwenziwa kude kugqitywe.

Noko ke, uThixo ukhethe kubulumko bakhe obungenasiphelo ukutyhila izicwangciso zakhe zobabalo, inceba, noxolo ukuze uluntu luhlangulwe ngokupheleleyo ngezigaba, kwaye noko usoloko enika ithemba elikhulu kwabalungileyo lokuba ngenye imini kungekudala baya kuvuswa babe butsha bobomi kwabafileyo bangaphinde bafe, kwaye baya kuhlala kwintabalala noxolo yonke imihla yobomi babo kwaye apho imihla ingayi kuba nasiphelo. Ukutyhubela iminyaka abathembekileyo baye bafa benokholo olupheleleyo kuYahweh ukuba azalisekise izithembiso zakhe kwaye baye bafa bethembele kwiminqophiso kaYahweh njengethemba labo losindiso kwaye yonke iminqophiso kaYahweh iya kuzaliseka ngokupheleleyo ebusweni bukaKrestu Yesu, mna kanye, iNkosi yenu evusiweyo noMsindisi. Kwaye ke bakholwa kuThixo kwaye kubo kwabalelwa kubo njengobulungisa kwanakubo bonke abakholwayo kwaye babenethemba kwimbewu ethenjisiweyo yomfazi.

Kwaye ke isicwangciso sikaYahweh seminyaka sokuhlangulwa okupheleleyo koluntu sihlale sigxile kum, uKrestu Yesu kwanaye uYesu waseNazarete okwanguYeshua HaMashiach kuba ndiyiNkosi yeeNkosi enyukileyo eyimvana kaThixo kwaye ezise usindiso lwanaphakade kumYuda nakweyeNtlanga ngokufanayo, kwaye ndisaza kusindisa uYakobi kwabo baye bahlasela ilizwe lesithembiso kwaye intshabalaliso yabo ayilibazisi. Ndim lowo ingonyama iya kulala phantsi nemvana kuba kum ingonyama yesizwe sakwaYuda nemvana kaThixo iya kufikelela ekugqityweni okupheleleyo. Kuba ndiyingonyama yesizwe sakwaYuda kwaye ndiyimvana kaThixo kuba ndinguKumkani wooKumkani neNkosi yeeNkosi kwaye ndinguMbingeleli oMkhulu ngonaphakade ngokohlobo lukaMelkitsedeke.

Kwaye ke uAdam noEva baphila ngokuthembeka beqhubela phambili phambi kweNkosi uThixo wabo, kwaye baba nesiqhamo baza banda, kodwa uSathana wayeza kuphinda ahlele olunye uhlaselo ngemvukelo yabagcini abawileyo. Bazimanya nemvukelo kaSathana kwaye ukuze banelise iinkanuko zabo balushiya uzuko lwanaphakade lwesimo sabo sokuqala, abadalelwa sona, ukuze bazingcolise nabafazi, kwanenyama negazi, kuba ngebahlala kubukhazikhazi obungunaphakade kodwa endaweni yoko bakhetha isohlwayo sabo sanaphakade ukuze bazalisekise iinkanuko zabo ngeendleko nokufa kwendalo eyayikho ngoko.

Kwaye ke ezi zithunywa zezulu ziwileyo zafundisa umntu owileyo indlela yokuzitshabalalisa ngomnombo kaKayin nangolwazi olwalelweyo nobuqhetseba ababufundisa uluntu. Bazenza oothixo baza bafuna unqulo kwaye BAZITHATHELA abafazi ngamandla kubo bonke ababakhethayo, kwaye abafazi abaninzi bazinikela ngokuzithandela ngokomyalelo kaSathana ukuze balinge kwaye bahendele abagcini kwizono zabo zenkanuko abazibophelela kuzo ngonaphakade kwisiqalekiso sabo kuba benza phakathi kwabo iziqalekiso ezifanayo. Oku kwenzeka ngemihla kaYerede kwaye amanyala abo, kwanamaNefili abo asasazeka phezu komhlaba kubukhosi babo behlabathi lonke ngexesha elibizwa ngokuba lixesha labo legolide.

Kwaye abagcini nembewu yabo bawonakalisa umhlaba nako konke okukuwo kwaye iinguqu zemfuza ezingaqhelekanga zazo zonke iintlobo zenziwa kwaye zanda emhlabeni kuba bona nxamnye nayo yonke inyama kwaye iinkanuko zabo zazingazi mida, kwaye bazenza oothixo, kwanaboothixo abadala kodwa eneneni abanto ngaphandle kwabazenzisi abathatha inxaxheba ekuxubeni okubi phakathi kweentlobo. Kwaye ke umhlaba wonakala ngokupheleleyo kwaye wawulawulwa ngamaNefili, awayedla abantu nezinto zabantu. Kwaye bonke ababesele bengenabala ngexesha likaNowa (kuba uNowa wayelungile kwaye ethe tye kwaye wahamba noYahweh waza wafumana umusa emehlweni kaYahweh) yayiyimiphefumlo esibhozo eyasindiswa iphila kunye nezilwanyana uYahweh awazizisa eMkhombeni ukuze kusindiswe iintlobo zazo. Kwaye ke uThixo wawukhukulisa umhlaba kwaye lonke ukonakala kwehlabathi elalikho ngoko kwatshabalala, kwaye abagcini abanalo uxolo, okanye uxolelo kwaye ngonaphakade naphakade umgwebo wabo oqatha uya kuba njalo kuba bafanelekile. Kwaye kungekhona abagcini kuphela, kodwa bonke abazimanya nemvukelo kaSathana baya kubandezeleka ngaloo ndlela kwaye babuyekezwe ngonaphakade kwelo chibi likhulu lomlilo wesulfure eluhlaza elivutha awona malangatye ashushu nanyulu esohlwayo.

Emva kukazamcolo, uThixo wamisa ihlabathi elikhoyo ngoku, kwanelo lamiswa ekuqaleni ngobulungisa. Kwaye uYahweh wangena kumnqophiso noNowa, apho uYahweh wamema uNowa ukuba avuse ubulungisa emhlabeni kwaye abe nesiqhamo kwaye ande ukuze imbewu ethenjisiweyo yomfazi ifike. UYahweh wathembisa ukuba akayi kuphinda awukhukulise umhlaba kwaye nangona ububi bendalo yomntu, uThixo wasebenza noluntu ukukhusela nokusikelela iminombo yegazi elungileyo emhlabeni apho mna Yeshua HaMashiach ndavela khona.

UThixo wabasikelela ukuba babe nesiqhamo kwaye bande kwaye bawuzalise umhlaba, kwaye uThixo wathembisa umntu nesilo ukuba akayi kuphinda ayitshabalalise yonke inyama emhlabeni ngamanzi kwaye akayi kuphinda u-El Shaddai awukhukulise umhlaba wonke kodwa uya kuwugcina umhlaba, kwaye agcine uluntu nembewu yalo, kwaye eli hlabathi linye ligciniwe ukusukela kuloo mini yomnqophiso apho uYise weziKhanyiso wabeka umnyama wakhe emazulwini njengophawu nesikhumbuzo somnqophiso wakhe noNowa. Kuba oku bakutyeshela ngabom, ukuba ngelizwi likaThixo amazulu ayekho kwakudala, nomhlaba umi uphuma emanzini nakumanzi: Apho ihlabathi elalikho ngoko, likhukuliswe ngamanzi, latshabalala: Kodwa amazulu nomhlaba, akhoyo ngoku, ngelo lizwi linye agcinelwe, abekelwe umlilo nxamnye nomhla womgwebo nentshabalaliso yabantu abangamhloneliyo uThixo.

Kwaye ke uYahweh wamvusa uAbram ukuba abe nguAbraham, waza wathembisa uAbram ukuba igama lakhe aliyi kuba nguAbram kodwa nguAbraham kwaye uya kumenza uYise weentlanga ezininzi kwaye uya kunika uAbraham nembewu yakhe ilizwe laseKanan libe lilifa lanaphakade kwaye uYahweh uya kuba nguThixo wabo! Kwaye ke uAbraham waba nguYise weentlanga ezininzi, awazithembisa uYahweh ngomnqophiso. Ngoku, kuAbraham nembewu yakhe zenziwa izithembiso. Akatsho ukuthi, Nakwiimbewu, ngokungathi zininzi; kodwa ngokungathi yinye, Nakwimbewu yakho, enguKrestu. Kwaye ke olilungisa uya kuphila ngokholo!

Kwaye ke abantwana bawela ebukhobokeni eYiputa emva kokuba abantwana besithembiso beye khona phantsi kokhuseleko nokuxhaswa kukaYosefu kuba uYosefu walawula ngobulungisa phantsi kukaFaro owayemkhulu kunoYosefu etroneni, kodwa uYosefu wakhonza uMNA OMKHULU! Kwavela ufaro owayengamazi uYosefu, kwaye lo mnga wawucinezela waza wazama ukubulala umnombo kaKrestu kwakhona, kwanabantwana besithembiso. Kwaye uYahweh wamvusa umhlanguli ongumHebhere endlwini kaFaro, kwanaye lowo wakhutshwa emanzini waza wasindiswa ephila ngobabalo lukaYahweh. Yayinguye uMoses ngokwakhe owayeza kukhokela abantwana besithembiso baphume ebukhobokeni baye kwinkululeko ezukileyo yelizwe lesithembiso, kuba bangabantwana besithembiso kwaye ndisaza kubavusa ebuthongweni babo kwaye ndibavuze abathembekileyo nabalungileyo ngokutyebileyo, kwanabo bahlala bethembekile kude kube sekupheleni. Kwaye ke uMoses wabahlangula abantwana bakwaSirayeli esandleni sabacinezeli babo kwaye bonke ababasukelayo barhaxwa elwandle, kuba ngubani onokuma nxamnye no-El Shaddai?

Kwaye ke uYahweh wakhokelela uMoses nabantwana besithembiso kwiNtaba yeSinayi apho uYahweh wanika imiyalelo, imithetho nemimiselo equlethwe kwimithetho kuMoses ukuze ithotyelwe ngabo bonke abantwana. Kwaye iintsikelelo neziqalekiso zamiselwa ngenxa yokuthobela nangenxa yokungathobeli. Kwaye xa uYahweh wayenike ngomnqophiso kuMoses imithetho equlethwe kwimimiselo yokuthobela, uMoses ke wawuqinisa umnqophiso waba yiTestamente ngokuphalazwa kwegazi. Waza uMoses wathatha isiqingatha segazi, wasifaka ezityeni, waza isiqingatha segazi wasitshiza eSibhacweni. Waza wathatha incwadi yomnqophiso, wayifunda kubaphulaphuli babantu: baza bathi, Konke akuthethileyo uYehova, siya kukwenza, sithobele. Waza uMoses wathatha igazi walitshiza ebantwini, wathi, Nalo igazi loMnqophiso awenze nani uYehova, ngokuphathelele onke la mazwi. Ngaphezu koko, watshiza ngegazi zombini iTabernakele, nazo zonke izitya zoBulungiseleli. Kwaye phantse zonke izinto ngokoMthetho zihlanjululwa ngegazi: kwaye ngaphandle kokuphalazwa kwegazi akukho kuxolelwa kwezono.

Kwaye ke imbali yenzeka njengoko kubhaliwe, kwaye ekuhambeni kwexesha isizwe sakwaSirayeli samiselwa ngokupheleleyo, kodwa kwakungekho ndawo yokuhlala kaYahweh phakathi kwabantu. Kwaye uYahweh wamvusa uKumkani olilungisa, kwanendoda enentliziyo efana nekaThixo! Ekuhambeni kwexesha uYahweh wathumela uNatan uMprofeti kuDavide waza wamxelela ukuba xa imihla yakho izalisekile, kwaye uya kulala nooyihlo, ndiya kumisa imbewu yakho emva kwakho, eya kuphuma emathunjini akho, kwaye ndiya kumisa ubukumkani bayo. Uya kwakha indlu yegama lam, kwaye ndiya kumisa itrone yobukumkani bakhe ngonaphakade. Ndiya kuba nguyise, kwaye uya kuba ngunyana wam. Kwaye indlu yakho nobukumkani bakho buya kumiswa ngonaphakade phambi kwakho: itrone yakho iya kumiswa ngonaphakade. Ngokwawo onke la mazwi, nangokwawo wonke lo mbono, watsho uNatan kuDavide.

Kwaye ke uluntu luye lwalandela uYahweh ukusuka kumnqophiso ukuya kumnqophiso longeza kumnqophiso owawuqiniswa ngedini lezilwanyana kwaye umnqophiso wongezwa kwiTestamente. Noko ke, ngeyona migudu mikhulu yoluntu, yena ngamandla akhe akazange akwazi ukuphila ngokobulungisa bukaThixo, kwanalowo mthetho ulungileyo nolungileyo kaMoses endiwuthandayo nendiwuzalisekisileyo. Ukuba nabani na ubiza umthetho kaMoses neminqophiso emininzi kaYahweh njengento eqalekisiweyo, makabe ngumqalekiswa. Kuba umthetho ulungile kwaye ulungile kwaye ungcwele kwaye zonke izithembiso zikaYahweh zingu-ewe no-amen. Noko ke, umthetho wanikwa njengomfundisi-ntsapho ukuba ubazise kuKrestu. Kuba mna, Yesu waseNazarete, ndingulowo uYahweh amnike ukuba azalisekise oko uluntu lungenakukwazi ngenxa yesono esidluliselwa kwisizukulwana ngesizukulwana kwigazi lika-Adam, elonakele ngonaphakade. Kuba oko umthetho wawungenakukwenza kangangokuba wawubuthathaka ngenxa yenyama: oko wakwenza uThixo, waza wathumela unyana wakhe efana nenyama enesono.

Ngoko ekuzalisekeni kwamaxesha mna, Yesu waseNazarete, ndazalwa ngumfazi ngokweemfuno zomthetho kaMoses nasekuzalisekeni kweminqophiso, kwaye ndiyimbewu ethenjisiweyo yomfazi eyaxelwa kwangaphambili nguYahweh ngokwakhe kule minyaka mininzi idlulileyo. Kuba uThixo walithanda kangaka iHlabathi wada wanikela ngoNyana wakhe okuphela kwamzeleyo, ukuze nabani na okholwayo kuye angatshabalali, kodwa abe nobomi obungunaphakade. Kwaye ke ndingunyana okuphela kwamzeleyo kaThixo, kwanaleyo mbewu yesithembiso abaprofeti abaprofeta ngayo kwakudala kude kube ngoku. Kwaye ndingulowo umiselwe ukuzaliseka kwezinto zonke kwaye kum, uKrestu Yesu, zonke izinto nazo zonke izithembiso ziya kufikelela ekuzalisekeni kwaye akukho sithembiso sinye esiya kushiywa singenziwanga, kodwa zonke ziya kuzaliseka ngonaphakade.

Kuba kubhaliwe kumaHebhere apho ndenza uPawulos wabhala: Kuba ukuba igazi leeNkunzi, neleebhokhwe, nothuthu lwethokazi elitshiza abangcolileyo, liyangcwalisa ekuhlanjululweni kwenyama: Kobeka phi na ke ukugqithisela kwegazi likaKrestu, owathi ngoMoya ongunaphakade wazinikela engenabala kuThixo, ukuba lisihlambulule isazela senu kwimisebenzi efileyo, ukuze nikhonze uThixo ophilayo? Kwaye ngenxa yesi sizathu ungumLamleli weTestamente eNtsha, ukuze ngokufa, ngenxa yokuhlangulwa kwezono ezaziphantsi kweTestamente yokuqala, abo babiziweyo, bafumane isithembiso selifa elingunaphakade.

Ngoko ke qondani ukuba kum, mna Kristu Yesu, izithembiso zamandulo zigciniwe neminqophiso yamandulo igciniwe netestamente endala igciniwe yaze yazalisekiswa kuba ndiyinzaliseko yazo egqibeleleyo. Kanti ke zisekho izithembiso ekusafuneka zizalisekisiwe kwaye ngoku ngamaxesha enzalaiseko. Kuba anjalo amaxesha, apho ndiya kuthi ngequbuliso ndibavuse ekulaleni abo baleleyo bekuKristu, kwaye ndiya kuthi ngequbuliso ndibahlanganise abo baphilayo nabasalayo ukuze bandikhawulele emafini kwaye ngequbuliso siya kumanywa kwigumbi letroni likaYahweh ngokwakhe. Kuba nina Bandla lam kwanomtshakazi wam nikwayinzaliseko yesithembiso somnqophiso kwaye nizelwe ngokutsha ngembewu engenakonakala kwaye nikhululwe ngezithembiso ezininzi kwiTestamente yam eNtsha, engumnqophiso omtsha oquka zonke izithembiso zeminqophiso yamandulo kwaye wongeza nezithembiso ezinkulu kakhulu nezinqabileyo enikhululwe ngazo kubomi obungunaphakade nomvuzo ongunaphakade kwaye siya kuhlala kunye ngonaphakade.

Qondani ukuba ubomi enibuphilileyo nisenyameni, kwananina bathembekileyo bam nabanyuliweyo bam, obo bomi bufikelele esiphelweni ekuhlangulweni kwenu okupheleleyo. Qondani ukuba nimanyene nam ngokupheleleyo, mna Kristu Yesu, kuwo onke amanqanaba. Xa ndabethelelwa ngenxa yezono zenu, nani nabethelelwa kunye nam. Xa ndafela kweso sibonda sirhabaxa, nani nafa kunye nam. Xa ndavuswa ebomini emva kweentsuku ezintathu nobusuku obuthathu entliziyweni yomhlaba, nani navuselwa kubomi obutsha, kwaye xa ndenyukela ezulwini lamazulu, nani nenyuka kunye nam kwaye ngoko nihleli ezulwini. Kuba ndiyiPasika yenu egqibeleleyo, kwaye ndingummeli wenu owathatha indawo yenu emgwebeni nakwisohlwayo enanifanele. Kwaye mna, Yesu waseNazarete, ndanikhulula ngokuZithandela esiqalekisweni somthetho kuba ngenxa yenyama yenu enesono aninakuze nikwazi ukuwuzalisekisa umthetho ngeenzame zenu.

Qondani ukuba andizelanga kuchitha umthetho, neminqophiso, kodwa ukuzalisekisa konke ngamaxesha enzalaiseko. Ukuba nabani na uthi umthetho kaMoses neminqophiso emininzi kaYahweh uqalekisiwe, makabe uqalekisiwe. Kuba umthetho ulungile kwaye unobulungisa kwaye ungcwele, nazo zonke izithembiso zikaYahweh zingu-ewe no-amen. Noko ke, umthetho wanikwa njengomhlohli wokubasa kuKristu. Kuba mna, Yesu waseNazarete, ndingulowo uYahweh amnikele ukuba azalisekise oko uluntu lungakwaziyo ngenxa yesono esadluliselwa kwisizukulwana ngesizukulwana ngegazi lika-Adam, elonakele ngonaphakade. Kuba oko umthetho wawungenakukwenza, njengoko wawubuthathaka ngenxa yenyama: oko wakwenza uThixo, wathumela unyana wakhe efana nenyama enesono.

Sonke isithembiso sele siza kuqondwa ngokupheleleyo kwaye sizalisekiswe kum mna Kristu Yesu kwaye ndiza kungena kumnqophiso womtshato noMtshakazi wam ukuze kunye balawule nam kwilifa lam kwaye baya kuthi, phantsi kolwalathiso lwam kunye nam, baphilise umhlaba baze bahambe balawule njengababingeleli nookumkani emhlabeni kwaye wonke umnqophiso uya kuzalisekiswa ukusuka ku-Adam ukuya kuMoses ebusweni bukaYesu Kristu. Ezi zizithembiso zobomi obungunaphakade eziya kuzalisekiswa kumalungisa, kwanabo bonke abenziwa ngokutsha babe mntu mnye omtsha kuKristu Yesu. Kuba bonke baya kwenziwa ngokutsha ngokupheleleyo baze bavuzwe ngonaphakade ngesabelo sabo kwaye konke kuya kuba ngu-ewe no-amen. Kuba yonke iyod, nawo onke ameva eyod aya kuzalisekiswa ngokupheleleyo nangonaphakade.

Qondani ukuba niyitempile kaThixo ophilayo, nina Bandla lam elithembekileyo kwaye ndinidale njengendlu eyenziwe ngaphandle kwezandla ukuze nibe yindawo yokuhlala engunaphakade kaThixo ongunaphakade, kwanokaYahweh uYihlo. Kwaye ndiza kutshata nomtshakazi wam kwaye ndiya kuba nabo ngonaphakade kumnqophiso ongunaphakade, ukuze apho ndikhoyo, nabo babe khona, kwaye ngonaphakade baya kufana nam, mna Kristu Yesu iNkosi yabo engunaphakade. Kwaye qondani nina bathembekileyo bam ukuba amaxesha okwahlukana asondele esiphelweni kwaye akusayi kubakho kwahlukana phakathi koYahweh noonyana bakhe abathandekayo kwaye akusayi kubakho kwahlukana phakathi koKristu Yesu iNkosi yenu engunaphakade nabo ndibathanda kakhulu. Qondani ukuba siya kuhamba njengamntu mnye kwaye akusayi kubakho sono nasithintelo. Niya kwenza imisebenzi emininzi enamandla engazanga yangena ezintliziyweni zabantu. Niya kuba ngookumkani bam abanamandla nababingeleli bam endinguKumkani wooKumkani neNkosi yeeNkosi kwaye siya kulawula umhlaba ngobulungisa.

Kwaye abaninzi abangakumbi baya kuvuselwa ebomini ekupheleni kweveki yamashumi asixhenxe kaDaniyeli kwaye bonke baya kuvuzwa ngokokuthembeka kwabo. Kwaye bonke abathe bafa bethembekile belindele ukuba iNkosi yabo noMesiya abayi kuyishiya imiphefumlo yabo engcwabeni baya kuvukela kubomi obutsha nokubuyiselwa okungunaphakade. Kuba ndiya kubadibanisa nabathandwa babo kwaye ndiya kubabuyisela oko iinkumbi zakutyayo kwaye ndiya kubabuyisela oko uSathana wakubayo kubo ngamaxesha ababengabaphambukeli emhlabeni belindele ukuhlangulwa kwabo ngokupheleleyo kweli hlabathi liwileyo nelingendawo.

Kwaye zinjalo iindaba ezilungileyo ezingunaphakade kwaye ngonaphakade zonke izinto ziya kuzalisekiswa ngobulungisa, ubungcwele, nenyaniso. Kuba akukho nto iya kungena emasangweni obukumkani bam eyenziwe inqambi okanye embi okanye ethanda kwaye yenza ubuxoki kodwa ngenyaniso nobulungisa obungunaphakade luya kuba lulo ulawulo lwam. Ngoko ke liveni ilizwi likaYeshua HaMashiach kwanabo bonke abathunyelwe la mazwi. INkosi yenu engunaphakade ayizilibelanga izithembiso zaMandulo kwaye akukho testamente iye yalilize. Kodwa zonke ziya kuzalisekiswa kwaye, ngokwenene, kuninzi osekuzalisekisiwe ngoko khangelani kwezo zinto ukuze nibe nethemba. Kuba njengoko kubhaliwe kuya kwenziwa njalo kwaye zinjalo iindaba ezilungileyo ezingunaphakade kula maxesha enzalaiseko.

Kubekho iintsuku ezintandathu zomsebenzi, kuba usuku olunye kuYehova lunjengeminyaka eliwaka, neminyaka eliwaka injengosuku olunye.
