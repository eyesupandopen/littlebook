Septemba 03, 2024

Ndizisa Iingxilimbela Ukuze Ndizalisekise Ingqumbo Yam!

Yabonani, ziyeza! Ndizisa iingxilimbela ukuze ndizalisekise ingqumbo yam! Yivani amazwi am, nina bahlali bomhlaba, kwangawo amazwi kaYehova, uThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade. Kuba njengokuba kwakunjalo ngemihla kaNowa, kuya kuba njalo kwakhona: Kwaye kwakukho iingxilimbela emhlabeni ngaloo mihla, kususela kwimihla kaYerede ukuya kwimihla kaNowa zenziwa zaza zazulazula emhlabeni zikhohlakele kakhulu. Kwaye njengokuba bekunjalo, kuya kuba njalo kwakhona, kwaye iingxilimbela ziya kubuya. Ngemihla kaYerede abagcini behla phezu kwentaba yeHermon, bengamakhulu amabini bebonke ngemihla kaYerede behla besuka emazulwini phezulu. Baza bazibophelela kwiziqalekiso ezifanayo, kwanesifungo sabo abazibophelela ngaso esiye saba sisiqalekiso esingunaphakade kubo bonke ababotshelelwe kuso nabangasoze basibaleke, baza ngaloo ndlela bahenda balahlekisa uluntu lwawela esonweni esikhulu.

Abalindi bahendwa ngooSathana, ngokweenkanuko zeentliziyo zabo, baza bawela esilingweni besuka kwimeko yabo yokuqala ezukileyo, yaye babenobomi obungunaphakade emazulwini, kuba badalwa ngolo hlobo. Kodwa bawa, njengeenkwenkwezi eziwayo kwenzeka ukuwa kwabo, baza bazimanya nooSathana ngonaphakade. Ngoko ke ooSathana bahenda abafazi abathandayo baza babafundisa ukuba bahende abalindi ngokweenkanuko zeentliziyo zabo, yaye abanye abafazi bazinikela ngokuzithandela kubalindi, yaye umgwebo wabo baya kuwuthwala. Abanye bathathwa ngenkani ngenxa yokuba abalindi baguqula iinkokeli zabo zehlabathi zamandulo, baza baya kuthatha abafazi ngenkani kumadoda abo neentsapho zabo, ngoko ke abalindi bathatha bonke ababakhethayo baba ngabafazi babo, baza baxhaphaza abaninzi, yaye kulo manyano lungcwele kwazalwa iingxilimbela, iingxilimbela ezinkulu zamandulo. Ngenxa yokuba abalindi bonakalisa ngakumbi imizila yemfuza yoluntu. Baza ngoko bona kwizilo, nakwiintaka, nakwiintlanzi nakuzo zonke iintlobo zenyama bazinxulumanisa enye nenye, ngokungathobeli ngokuzithandela nangentiyo imida eyamiselwa ndim, mna Yehova, uThixo Omkhulu Ongunaphakade! Ukusukela ekuqaleni ndadala umhlaba nembewu nganye ngokohlobo lwayo, ngoko ke kwawelwa imida ekwakungafanelekanga ukuba yaphulwe, kwaye ukuwela imida yendalo emiselwe nguYehova kukuzisa ubuphambene, intlungu, nokufa kwindalo kaYehova, kwaye bonke abakwenzayo baya kuthwala umgwebo wabo. Ubomi buyilwe njengomluko onxibeleleneyo, apho bonke ubomi bunxibelelene ngoxolo nemvisiswano, apho uthando lulawula usuku kwaye bonke bafuna impilontle yabanye kungekhona eyabo kuphela. Kwanesidalwa apho kungekho nkanuko nomnqweno ombi wokuzibonakalisa ekufeni nasekutshabalaleni, kwaye kuya kuba njalo ngonaphakade nangonaphakade, kwaye amaxesha okuzaliseka alapha.

Ngoko ke zine kwiingelosi eziphambili zamazulu, uMikayeli, uUriyeli, uRafayeli, noGabriyeli, zamangalela oko kwakwenziwe ngabahlobo bazo bangaphambili emhlabeni, ngoko ke umgwebo wavakaliswa waza wenziwa ngokukhawuleza kubo bonke ababandakanyekayo nasemhlabeni ngokwawo, ngenxa yokuba ihlabathi elalikho ngoko lonakaliswa ngokupheleleyo. AmaNefili, abantwana babalindi, enziwa ukuba alwe omnye nomnye kude kube sekufeni kumadabi amaninzi amakhulu phakathi kwezizwe ezichaseneyo, yaye oko kungenxa yokuba ndasusa uxolo phakathi kwabo, baza balwa kude kube sekufeni baza babulalana njengoko ooyise (abalindi) babenyanzelwa ukuba babukele ukutshatyalaliswa kwezidalwa zabo ezinezothe ababezidalele ukutshatyalaliswa koluntu. Ukusuka apho, abalindi babotshelelwa esohlwayweni nasemgwebeni baza bagcinwa bodwa omnye komnye kangangezizukulwana ezingamashumi asixhenxe. Kwaye ngendlela efanayo ekwakufuneka ndibukele ngayo indalo yam endandiyithanda kakhulu itshatyalaliswa ngamasikizi amabi, ngoko ndabangela ukuba izidalwa ezibi zitshabalalisane njengesohlwayo kooyise, kwaye ngoko ndabuyekeza, kuba ndililungisa kwimigwebo yam rhoqo nangonaphakade, kwaye ndinguThixo wemigwebo yobulungisa omigwebo yakhe ingasasileliyo, ingalibazisekiyo ekuzalisekeni.

Ndaza ndazisa unogumbe omkhulu emhlabeni, kwaye unogumbe waba sisiganeko esinobundlobongela ngemihla kaNowa. Imithombo yenzonzobila enkulu yagqabhuka yaza imvula yagaleleka ngobundlobongela isuka emhlabeni ngokwawo, kwaye iifestile zezulu zavulwa, zavumela amanzi angaphezu kwesibhakabhaka ukuba akhukulise umhlaba, nangaphezu kweentaba ezinde kakhulu, yaza yarhaxisa bonke ubomi emhlabeni, bonke ngaphandle kwemiphefumlo esibhozo eyasindiswa iphila emkhombeni, ngenxa yokuba uNowa wafunyanwa elilungisa phambi kokujonga kwam. Kwaye ngenxa yesi sizathu ndathatha uNowa ndaza ndamyalela ukuba enze umkhombe ngokweenkcukacha ezinikwe sisandla sam. Ndaza ndazisa kuye nezilwanyana ezaziya kusindiswa ziphila, ngoko ke uNowa wathembeka ekufezeni konke awayekuyalelwe, waza unogumbe weza njengoko kwakuxeliwe kwangaphambili kwaza kwavunyelwa, kuba oko ndikuvakalisayo, mna Yehova uThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade, akukho mntu unokukuguqula okanye akuthintele nakancinane!

Ngoko ke uEnoki, umbhali wamandulo, uchaza isiphelo sezi ngxilimbela ngolu hlobo, kuba mna, Yehova, ndatsho njalo: Kwaye ngoku, iingxilimbela, eziveliswe yimimoya nenyama, ziya kubizwa ngokuba yimimoya emibi phezu komhlaba, kwaye emhlabeni kuya kuba yindawo yazo yokuhlala. Imimoya emibi iphume emizimbeni yazo; ngenxa yokuba zizalwe ngabantu, kubalindi abangcwele yimvelaphi yazo yokuqala; ziya kuba yimimoya emibi emhlabeni, kwaye ziya kubizwa ngokuba yimimoya emibi. Ngokubhekiselele kwimimoya yezulu, ezulwini kuya kuba yindawo yayo yokuhlala, kodwa ngokubhekiselele kwimimoya yomhlaba eyazalelwa phezu komhlaba, emhlabeni kuya kuba yindawo yayo yokuhlala. Kwaye imimoya yeengxilimbela iyaxhaphaza, icinezele, itshabalalise, ihlasele, ilwe, kwaye isebenze intshabalalo emhlabeni, kwaye ibangele inkathazo: ayityi, kodwa noko iyalamba inxanelwe, kwaye ibangele izono. Kwaye le mimoya iya kuvukela abantwana babantu nabafazi, ngenxa yokuba iphume kubo.

Ngoko ke ngexesha elifanelekileyo, uNowa wafika eNtabeni iArarati, apho umkhombe wema khona njengoko amanzi ayebohla. Ndaza ndangena emnqophisweni noNowa, kwaye ekuhambeni kwexesha abazukulwana bakaNowa baqala ukusuka kumnqophiso endawenza noNowa, kwaye ngenxa yoko imimoya emibi yaqala ukubahlasela. Ngoko ke le mimoya, eyayininzi, yoyisa abantwana boonyana bakaNowa, ngoko ke uNowa wabathethelela, ndaza ndabophelela isithoba kwishumi sale mimoya mibi enzonzobileni, apho igcinwe khona ukusukela ngelo xesha kude kube namhlanje. Ngoko ke ndayalela, waza uNowa wafundiswa onke amayeza abo, nezihendo wafundiswa, ukuze uNowa abaphilise ngemifuno yomhlaba. Ngoko ke isinye kwishumi sale mimoya savunyelwa ukuba sihlale sikhululekile, ukuze silahlekise ebubini oonyana babantu ababenqwenela ubuxoki babo. Ngoko ke imimoya eseleyo eyayibotshelelwe umgwebo enzonzobileni iya kukhululwa ngamaxesha embandezelo kaYakobi, xa inzonzobila ivulwa ngokupheleleyo kwaye isithoba kwishumi sayo esibotshiweyo siya kukhululwa ukuze sisebenze iindlela zaso ezimbi nezobubi njengenxalenye yomgwebo womntu wemvelo olapha, kwaye uya kuqhambuka kuwo wonke umhlaba ngequbuliso nangentshabalalo enkulu!

Ngoko ke uKanan, umzukulwana kaNowa awamqalekisayo ngenxa yobubi bakhe, wenza isono esikhulu (kwakhona) esebenzisa ulwazi lweengelosi eziwileyo, kwaye ngeendlela zikaSathana wahamba ngokuzithandela, apho wakhokelwa ngokuzithandela, waza wenza amanye amasikizi emva konogumbe kaNowa. Kwaye la masikizi angenisa amazwe kaShem awayenikwe ngelifa, apho uKanan oqalekisiweyo wawathimba waza wazebela wona, baza balibiza ilizwe lakhe elibiweyo ngokuba lilizwe lakwaKanan. Uninzi lwenzala yakhe enezothe yayizizidalwa eziguqulwe ngokwemfuza ezazingcolise ngokupheleleyo amazwe esithembiso, apho ndathumela uMoses ukuba ahlanganise abantwana bakwaSirayeli kumazwe aseYiputa ukuze bathathe ilifa labo kwabanezothe. Ngoko ke uYoshuwa walwa imfazwe enkulu nxamnye nala masikizi njengoko kubhaliweyo kwizibhalo ezingcwele, baza bathatha kula masikizi ilifa labo elifanelekileyo, kwaye ngoko kubhaliwe njengesithunzi sezinto ezizayo. Kuba ngendlela efanayo uya kuza unyana wam, uYeshua HaMashiach, endimenze iNkosi yeeNkosi noKumkani wooKumkani, eze kuthatha ilifa lakhe kwabahlali abangekho mthethweni nakubathimbi, kuba ukuhlala kwabo akukho mthethweni. Abango kumkani kwaye abanabukumkani, kodwa ichibi elikhulu elivutha umlilo nesulfure lilifa labo lanaphakade.

Noko ke, uSirayeli akazange athembeke ngokupheleleyo kubizo lwakhe, yaye intsalela yeengxilimbela yavunyelwa ukuba ihlale. Kodwa nakwinkcaso yabo ndiye ndawathintela la masikizi ndaza ndatshabalalisa amaninzi awo emhlabeni ukusukela ngaloo maxesha. Kwaye ndiye ndaqhubela amaninzi awo ngaphantsi komhlaba, yaye amaninzi awo aye kumazwe angaziwayo ngaphaya komkhenkce apho uluntu lungazulazuli ngokukhululekileyo. Qonda ukuba uluntu aluwuqondi umhlaba elenziwe kuwo neliphila kuwo, kuba uluntu lutyiswa bubuxoki obungeyonyani obuthengiswa njengenyaniso, buphinde bupakishwe njengemfundo, ngoko ke uluntu luhlala lungazi ngokuzithandela ukuba ngelizwi likaThixo amazulu ayekho kwakudala, nomhlaba umi ngaphandle kwamanzi nasemanzini: Ngaloo ndlela ihlabathi elalikho ngoko, likhukuliswe ngamanzi, latshabalala: Kodwa amazulu nomhlaba, akhoyo ngoku, ngelizwi elinye agcinelwe umlilo nxamnye nomhla womgwebo nentshabalaliso yabantu abangamhloneliyo uThixo.

Ngoko ke ekuhambeni kwexesha ndazisusa iingxilimbela emhlabeni ngenceba yam nobabalo lwam nomnqweno wam wokusindisa uluntu entshabalalisweni, yaye iintsalela zazo zisasazeke kuwo wonke umhlaba zaza zangcolisa indalo yam njengobungqina bobukho bazo. Kwanentsalela yempucuko yazo yamandulo isemi njengobungqina bokuba zakha zalawula ubukhosi bezizwe emhlabeni ngexesha elo kuthiwa lixesha lazo legolide, kwanangemihla kaNowa phambi konogumbe. Amanxuwa azo amakhulu asasazeke njengobungqina obuchasene nazo, kwaye ubukho bale manxuwa bumi njengobungqina obuchasene nobuxoki bukaSathana, ozama ngenkuthalo ukufihla inyaniso ukuze abe nohlaselo lwakhe olumangalisayo ngokupheleleyo. Ngoko ke ndixelela abo baneendlebe zokuva namehlo okubona nentliziyo yokufunda oko kuza kwenzeka ngaphambi kokuba kwenzeke, ukuze iintliziyo zabo zingaphelelwa ngamandla kukothuka nokungakholwa, kodwa ukuze bayazi inyaniso baze balungiselele oko kuzayo ukuze basindiswe, bonke abanqula igama likaYesu Kristu kude kube sekupheleni kwabo.

Ngoko ke kwavunyelwa kwaye kwenzeka ekuzalisekeni kwamaxesha, ukuba ndikhulule abalindi kwiintolongo zabo apho babebotshwe khona entuthumbeni nasesohlwayweni, bodwa omnye komnye babebotshiwe. Baza bakhululwa emva kokuba amaxesha okubotshwa kwabo egqityiwe: izizukulwana ezingamashumi asixhenxe. Imbali ayinakukufihla ngokupheleleyo ukukhululwa kwabo, kwaye iimfazwe ezininzi neentlekele nezinye iziganeko zenzeka kwihlabathi lonke ngokukhululwa kwabo, baza baziphosa empuma, baza bazimanya nemvukelo kaSathana, ngokupheleleyo, kwakhona, kuba bazibophelela ngonaphakade kwisifungo sabo esiqalekisiweyo, kwaye echibini lomlilo izifungo zabo ziya kuzalisekiswa ngonaphakade, kuba babotshelelwe kwisohlwayo sikaSathana nakwilifa lakhe ngonaphakade.

Ngoko ke bazilungiselele ngexesha elinikiweyo nangendlela ethintelweyo kangangeeyubhile ezininzi ngoku. Ngokuqinisekileyo bavelise amasikizi abo kwakhona, kwaye emfihlekweni bayazilungiselela kwaye balindele ukususwa komthinteli xa beya kuzama kwakhona ukwaphula imida endibabekele yona, kuba bahlala benemvukelo. Kwaye nangona bebotshelelwe ukuzalisekisa iinjongo zika-El Shaddai, kuba ndinguThixo uSomandla, baya kufuna ukuphinda baseke ixesha labo legolide baze bathathe umhlaba ube ngowabo, nto leyo engenakwenzeka; kuba umhlaba yinkosi kunye nokuzala kwawo, kwaye itayitile yomhlaba ithengwe ngunyana wam, uYeshua HaMashiach, kwaye inikwe ezandleni zakhe ezinamandla njengelifa elingunaphakade, kwaye uya kwabelana ngalo ngokupheleleyo nabathembekileyo, ababiziweyo, abanyuliweyo, nabakhululweyo.

Ngoko ke ooSathana nabalindi bazilungisile, kwaye balindele ngenzondelelo ukususwa komthinteli, kuba ekususweni kwakhe ubukumkani babo bukhululwa ngokupheleleyo, kwaye umhlaba uya kuphinda ube njengemihla kaNowa. Yabonani! Ndiyanika umyalelo, ndize ndibazise: iingxilimbela ziyeza ukuzalisekisa ingqumbo yam, zivuya ngaxeshanye zithuka, kwaye ndiya kuzivumela ukuba zikhululwe emhlabeni kwakhona ukuze ndizalisekise iinjongo zam. Ziya kuphuma ngequbuliso apho zizimele khona, kwaye zizimele kwizixeko zazo eziphantsi komhlaba, kwaye zizimele ngaphandle komkhenkce oza kutshintshwa kungekudala, kwaye kwihlabathi labantu ziya kuvunyelwa ukuba zihlasele, ziphange, kwaye zoyise, kwaye iNamba iya kuzikhokela kuba ziyinxalenye yobukumkani bayo. Kodwa kuphela ixesha elifutshane, iveki yamashumi asixhenxe kaDaniyeli, iveki yeminyaka esixhenxe ziya kukhululwa apho usuku ngalunye lungunyaka, ngoko ke kuya kuba yiminyaka esixhenxe. Kwaye ziya kudla kakhulu, kwaye iintliziyo zabantu ziya kubaphela ngenxa yobuso bazo obukrakra kunye nobukhulu bazo obukhulu xa zithelekiswa nomntu wemvelo.

Yabonani, NDINGUYE omkhulu endinguYe, othumele amazwi am ngomkhonzi wam, kwaye ngomkhonzi wam amazwi am athunyelwe. Ngoko ke ndinixelela izinto ngaphambi kokuba zenzeke ukuze abantu bam balunge kwaye baqonde oko kuzayo, kwaye babe nokuqonda njengoko ezi zinto ziza kwenzeka kungekudala. Kwaye bonke abanqula igama likaYeshua HaMashiach, uYesu Kristu ngolwimi lwesiNgesi, baya kusindiswa.

Ngoko ke ingqumbo enkulu nomgwebo omkhulu njengoko kwathenjiswayo mandulo ngoku kumiselwe ukuba kufike ngokupheleleyo phezu komhlaba, kwaye amaxesha ngoku, utsho uThixo Omkhulu Ongunaphakade! Kangangexesha lonke lobabalo ivangeli yosindiso nemfihlelo enkulu kaThixo (eyatyhilwa kuMpostile uPawulos) izalise umhlaba kwaye iye kwiindawo ezikude kakhulu zomhlaba. Kwaye nangona abaninzi, abaninzi, abaninzi basindiswa, bonke abo beza kuYesu waseNazarete ngenguquko epheleleyo, bemenza iNkosi yabo engunaphakade, amaxesha afikile okuzaliseka ngenxa yokuba uluntu luphinde lwamgatya umdali walo. Kwaye mna, El Shaddai, uThixo uSomandla, ndinguye oye wathintela ububi obuninzi ukuze iindlalifa zosindiso zifike kubomi obungunaphakade kuKristu Yesu, iNkosi yazo enyukileyo. Kwaye ngoku ndivakalisa ezi zinto ngaphambi kokuba zenzeke, kuba ndinguYehova, uMkhulu NDINGUYE! Ngoko baxelele uMKHULU NDINGUYE uvakalisa ngokunyanisekileyo ukuba ekuhlanganiseni abantwana bam abathembekileyo kunyana wam, xa bephakanyiselwa ukumhlangabeza emoyeni kwaye njengoko umthinteli esusiwe, ukuba bonke ubukumkani bukaSathana buya kukhululwa phezu komhlaba kwaye umhlaba uya kutshintshwa.

Ndivakalisa emgwebeni phezu kwabemi bomhlaba ukuba ndiya kukhulula ubukumkani bukaSathana ngokupheleleyo, kwaye onke amasikizi abo aya kuqhambuka kuwo wonke umhlaba, kwaye yonke loo mimoya endayibophelela enzonzobileni ngemihla kaNowa iya kukhululwa emhlabeni, kuba amaxesha okwahlukana kokugqibela nomgwebo wokugqibela afikile emhlabeni, kwaye umhla womgwebo omkhulu umisiwe. Ngoko ke kuya kuba ukuba ubukumkani bukaSathana buya kunikwa ulawulo phezu komhlaba, ngenxa yokuba ebulungiseni bam, uluntu lunqwenele ukulawulwa ngooSathana, kwanokukholelwa kubuxoki bakhe bokuba unguThixo onobubele nomkhanyisi okhanyisayo, ukuba ngoku ndiya kunika uluntu oko lukunqwenela ngamandla.

Kwaye abawileyo bakhululwe ngokupheleleyo ngenxa yomgwebo wabo, kwaye benze amasikizi abo kwakhona. Kwaye babotshelelwe emhlabeni nenamba, kuba lixesha labo, kwaye baya kuzenza abaphambukeli, izidalwa ezinobubele ezivela kumazwe angaphandle. Noko ke, mna, Yehova uThixo Omkhulu Ongunaphakade, ndifunga ngegama lam, kuba andinakufunga nganto iphezulu, ukuba akukho mazwe ajikelezayo ngobuxoki kububanzi obungapheliyo bento engekhoyo, kodwa ngaphezu komhlaba kukho amazulu, izulu lamazulu elingelona liphezulu kumazulu, apho uThixo Osenyangweni ahleli khona kulawulo olungunaphakade etroneni yakhe kwaye akukho nto inokuzifihla ekujongeni kwam.

Yabonani! Sele bezazisa, kwaye izinto zabo neengqukuva zabo zikuyo yonke indawo, kwaye umhlaba uyatshintsha ngokukhawuleza ngoku. Kwaye njengoko umntu wemvelo ngoku ewugatyile umnqophiso wam woxolo, unyana wam okuphela kwamzeleyo, uYesu waseNazarete, ngoko ke ngoku ndiyamgatya umntu wemvelo kwaye ndiya kumshiya ukuba alawulwe ngokupheleleyo ziintshaba zakhe, kwaye zikunqwenela ukutshatyalaliswa kwakhe. Noko ke, ukugatya kwam akuphelelanga kwaye akukokugqibela, ngenxa yokuba isihlwele esikhulu siya kusindiswa ngexesha lembandezelo kaYakobi, bonke abo banqula igama leNkosi, uYesu Kristu, kude kube sekupheleni. Kuba usindiso ngexesha lembandezelo kaYakobi alunakufezekiswa ngenye indlela. Ngoko ke ukwahlukana kokugqibela kuya kuqala, kwaye bonke endibamisele kubomi obungunaphakade ngaphambi kokuba kusekwe iziseko zomhlaba baya kufika kubomi obungunaphakade, kwaye ndiya kubavusela kubutsha bobomi, kwaye umvuzo omkhulu baya kuba nawo.

Ngoko yivani amazwi am kwaye nigxininise iintliziyo zenu kumazwi am esilumkiso nesibhengezo, ngenxa yokuba amaxesha eeNtlanga ngoku aphelile, kwaye ingqumbo nomgwebo nentyilelo enkulu ngoku ziphezu komhlaba, ngenxa yokuba mna, El Shaddai, uThixo uSomandla, ndinguye ohleli phezulu, kwaye akukho nto ifihliweyo emehlweni am, kwaye ngoku ndiya kuhamba ndisindise ngokupheleleyo bonke abaziindlalifa zosindiso njengoko ndibahlula kwabo bamiselwe intshabalalo engunaphakade. Ngoko ke ingqolowa iya kwahlulwa kumdiza ngonaphakade, kwaye bonke abo bayinxalenye yemvukelo kaSathana baya kunikwa umgwebo, kwaye amaxesha omgwebo wabo amiselwe ukuba aqhambuke kuwo wonke umhlaba.

Yivani amazwi am kwaye niqonde ubunzima boko kuzayo, ngenxa yokuba umhla weNkosi ngumhla wobumnyama, kwaye ngubani onokuma?

Mna, Yehova, uThixo Omkhulu Ongunaphakade, ndivakalisa ezi zinto ngaphambi kokuba zenzeke, kwaye akusekho xesha. Nabani na oza kuza, makaze asele ngokukhululekileyo kumanzi obomi obungunaphakade, kwaye isimemo sam sobomi obungunaphakade simi, kwaye bonke abo banqula igama leNkosi kude kube sekupheleni baya kusindiswa.

Mna, Yehova, uThixo Omkhulu Ongunaphakade, ndithumele la mazwi ngomkhonzi wam, kwaye ngomkhonzi wam la mazwi athunyelwe!

Amaxesha ngoku!
