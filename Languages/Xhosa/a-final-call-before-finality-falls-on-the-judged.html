Ubizo Lokugqibela Phambi Kokuba Isigqibo Sokugqibela Siwele Kwabagwetyiweyo

Yabonani! NDINGUYehova, uMkhulu NDINGUYE, kwaye ndiza kuthumela amazwi am othando kunye nobizo lokugqibela kwabo bajongene nesigqibo sokugqibela somgwebo ukuba bayalukhaba ubizo lwam lokugqibela. Nangona bonke abanqula igama likaYesu kude kube sekupheleni baya kufumana usindiso olukhulu nolwanaphakade kunye nomvuzo omkhulu, abantu abaninzi kakhulu baya kuwela emgwebeni ngenxa yezono zabo nobulukhuni beentliziyo zabo. Kuba, NDINGUYE ogwebayo abangemalungisa ukuba baguquke, kwaye abo bayalayo inguquko ndiyaqhubeka nokubagweba de iintliziyo zabo zibe lukhuni ngokupheleleyo baze bathathwe emgwebeni.

Ngoko ke, ndiza kunika lo myalezo eluthandweni lwam ngani, kwaye ndithumele ubabalo lwam olunganifanelanga kwihlabathi eligwetyiweyo kwakhona kwiinzame zam zokuthambisa iintliziyo zenu ezingaguqukiyo ukuba zamkele usindiso; okanye ukuba kwintiyo yenu nasekuvukeleni kwenu nishiywe kwimeko yokwala kokugqibela, nivuthiwe kwaye nilungele umgwebo wokugqibela. Qondani, alisekho ixesha kumaxesha am obabalo nenceba obomi bonke benguquko ngenxa yezicwangciso zam zosindiso lwalo lonke uluntu oluya kufikelela kusindiso olupheleleyo ngamaxesha alo nangolungelelwano lwalo kwabo baya kwamkela uthando lwam ngabo. Ngoko ke, amaxesha enu ngoku kwaye ubizo lokugqibela luyathunyelwa ngoku. Selah.

Yivani, O' Yivani ilizwi loThixo oMkhulu ongunaphakade woBulungisa, uYehova, uMkhulu NDINGUYE, okwanguye uThixo ongunaphakade woBulungisa kwaye niyiqonde intsingiselo yentetho yam, kwaye niyazi ukuba ilizwi lam linjengesandi samanzi amaninzi aphilayo, kwaye niqonde ukuba ilizwi lam liyaduduma njengesandi seendudumo ezininzi, (kwanazo iindudumo zeendudumo) kwingqumbo yam yobulungisa kubemi bomhlaba abahleli kuwo ngokungeva okukhulu nangokungalungisi okukhulu nesono.

Yabonani! NDINGULO NDINGUYE, kwanaye uMkhulu NDINGUYE, kwaye ndidalile amazulu nomhlaba nako konke okukuwo, kwaye kuyilo lwam lobulungisa ndenza amazulu nomhlaba wagqibelela ngeendlela zam zokugqibelela, ndawadala ekugqibeleleni okuvisisanayo nokobulungisa apho zonke iindalo zam zaziya kuphila ngokugqibeleleyo okuvisisanayo nangothando nenkonzo eluthandweni omnye komnye. Kwaye eluthandweni lwabo ngoYise nomdali wabo, babeya kulithobela ilizwi lam nemiyalelo yam ebomini babo ukuze ngokwenene baziqonde njengoko babedaliwe kwaye baziqonde neendlela zam. Noko ke, ubugwenxa bafunyanwa kuHeyileli, kwanaye owa wayo ekugqibeleleni okuzukileyo, kwanaye owadalwa ngobukhazikhazi obukhulu noboyikekayo, waza wawa emhlabeni, ethotywe esonweni nasebumnyameni ukuze ahlale kwisimo senyoka eqalekisiweyo ngenxa yokungathobeli kweeSerafi. Selah.

Ngenxa yenyaniso engunaphakade, iindlela zam ziphakamile kuneendlela zomntu, kwaye NDINGUYE ojikeleze itrone yam ngamawaka alishumi aphindwe ngamawaka alishumi, kwanenani elingenakubalwa leengelosi ezigqwesileyo ngobulumko, ngamandla, nangokomelela, nezithanda uYehova nezithanda ukufunda iindlela zikaYehova, uMkhulu NDINGUYE ngentobeko yonke evuthayo nengagungqiyo: Selah.

Inene, ngenyaniso enkulu nengunaphakade, ndizidalile zinamandla kwaye zizukile kwaye ndazambathisa ngobukhazikhazi obuqaqambileyo, nangobulungisa obungunaphakade, nangembeko engunaphakade. Inene, ngenyaniso enkulu nengunaphakade, azizange ziswele nto okanye zifune nto nakwezona zinto zincinane kakhulu; kwaye zindikhonza mna nendalo yam ngokupheleleyo ezintliziyweni zazo zothando ngoYise nomdali wazo, uElohim, ekuthobeleni kwazo uKristu Yesu.

(Kwanaye, uYehova, uMkhulu nonaphakade NDINGUYE, obeke zonke izinto ezulwini nasemhlabeni ezandleni zakhe ezinamandla nezingunaphakade nezithobelayo.)

Ngoko ke yazini ngenyaniso engunaphakade ukuba uKristu Yesu yiNkosi yeeNkosi noKumkani wooKumkani kwaye nguye oya kuzisa uxolo emazulwini nakumhlaba kwaye nguye oya kubuyela esandleni sam esizukileyo (eluthandweni lwakhe ngoYise noMdali wakhe, uElohim, kwanaye uMkhulu NDINGUYE) uBukumkani obuthotyiweyo ekupheleni kwezinto zonke xa izandla zakhe ezinamandla ziya kube zifezekise zonke izinto ezibhaliweyo nezaxeliweyo kwangaphambili ngeNkosi engunaphakade yoBulungisa! Kwanaxa loo Nyana woMntu eya kube egqibe zonke izinto ngamandla amakhulu, nangothando olukhulu. Kwanangexesha elo, ndiya kube ndifezekise ngamandla am ngezandla zakhe ezinamandla nangombingelelo wakhe zonke izinto ezinikwe kwizicwangciso zam ezingunaphakade neemfihlelo zam ukuba azifezekise.

Ngoko ke, ndinika nina ukuba niqonde, ukuba NDINGUYE onokuthi ngamandla onke nangolawulo olupheleleyo aqonde, afezekise, azi ngokupheleleyo, aze alawule yonke inkcukacha ephathelele yonke inkalo yendalo yam nokuba inkulu kangakanani na okanye ibonakala inamandla kangakanani na kude kuse kwinkcukacha encinane, kwaye akukho nto inokufihlakala emehlweni am! NDINGUYE onokuthi ngokwenene afezekise zonke izinto kwangoko ngamandla am nangamandla am angunaphakade. Noko ke, ndikwanguye osebenza zonke izinto ngokwecebo lentando yam kwaye ongadingi mcebisi, ngoko ke, izicwangciso zam neemfihlelo zam ziya kuzalisekiswa ngokupheleleyo kula maxesha okuzalisekiswa kwazo, kwanamaxesha ANGOKU.

Noko ke, eluthandweni lwam ngendalo yam nakumnqweno wam wabantwana nosapho, ndidala izidalwa nabantu abathile (abane-free will) ngeenjongo zam ezingunaphakade ezinikwe abathobelayo ngenkonzo enkulu nembeko, kwaye ndibanika amandla ngokufanelekileyo. Ngoko ke, ndiyanicebisa ukuba nifunde iindlela zam elusindisweni okanye nikhethe amadangatye entshabalaliso ngonaphakade. Kodwa yazini, O' mntu owileyo, unokhetho oluya kutshintsha ngokukhawuleza ngoku lube wawunokhetho, kwaye bonke abo baya kube bekhethe ububi ekugqibeleni xa kufika isigqibo sokugqibela, abasayi kuze bazisabe izigwebo zabo ngonaphakade kanaphakade. Selah.

Ungubani na wena, O' mntu owileyo, kwanawena uzimanye nabo bavukeli abangenangqondo nabazizidenge nabaphambeneyo babawileyo kuko konke ukungalungisi, ukubawa, inkanuko, nokugqwetha ukuze niziphakamise ngekratshi nxamnye noThixo oMkhulu noNgunaphakade onidalelwe iinjongo zam nolonwabo lwam?

Ungubani na wena, O' mntu owileyo, ukuba ekungazini kwakho nasenkanukweni yakho ungade ube nesibindi sokuphakamisa isithende sakho nxamnye noThixo omkhulu nongunaphakade weZulu lamaZulu (kwanaye owakunika ubomi) ngeendlela zooSathana? Ngoko ke, ziqondeni iinjongo zam nenjongo yam kuba ndithumela la mazwi KUNI njengobizo lwam lokugqibela kuninzi lwenu nina nicinga ngobudenge ukuba ninexesha eningenalo, kwaye ndiyaninika namazwi esilumkiso eluthandweni lwam olungapheliyo ngani ngokuthi ndinikhumbuze kwakhona ngezibhengezo zomgwebo nentlekele esele zinikiwe enizityeshele ngokupheleleyo ezenziwe nguKristu Yesu etroneni nasemandleni kaYehova, kwaye zabhengezwa nanguYehova ngokwakhe, kwanaye uMkhulu NDINGUYE!

(Aniqondi na ukuba izibhengezo zam azinakuze zitshintshwe?)

Noko ke, nide nilityeshele nelizwi leendudumo ezisixhenxe endazityhilayo, ndaza ndenza uYohane uMtyhili wazitywina ngexesha lakhe de kwafika amaxesha afanelekileyo okutyhilwa kwawo lawo maxesha angoku. (Kwaye ziya kuqhubeka de ubungqina bamangqina am amabini bugqitywe emgwebeni wokugqibela, kanye phambi kokuba imbandezelo enkulu kunazo zonke iwele ngokupheleleyo kwabangemalungisa, Selah.) Kwanazo iindudumo ezabhengezwa nguYeshuwa HaMashiach noYehova, uMkhulu NDINGUYE, zisuka etroneni yam enamandla yesibhengezo nomgwebo, kwaye ngoku zinikwa ngesandla somthunywa wam onamandla endithumela ngaye la mazwi othando nezilumkiso eziqinileyo kakhulu nezinzima, ukuze bonke abanokuthi beze beze, phambi kokuba umgwebo uwe ngokupheleleyo kungabikho xesha!

Ngoko ke, ukuba ninqwenela ukusaba umgwebo wenu nentshabalaliso engunaphakade, ndiyan命令 ukuba nimenze uYesu abe yiNkosi yenu ngokupheleleyo nangaphandle kokuthandabuza. Kuba izigwebo zam ziyawa, kwaye ziya kuwela nakubani na ezithunyelwe kuye, kwanazo ezo zigwebo zokugwetywa ezingunaphakade ekugqibeleni kwazo kwanaphakade kanaphakade. Ngoko ke, ndicebisa bonke abangamvumanga uYesu uKristu njengeNkosi ngentliziyo ezele lukholo ukuba benze njalo ngoku, besazi ukuba NDINGUYE owamvusayo kwabafileyo ngamandla amakhulu kakhulu ukuba angaphindi afe kwaye aqhubeke kwilifa lakhe ngonaphakade kanaphakade. Noko ke, uninzi luya kuwela kumgwebo omkhulu nongunaphakade kanye njengoko kubhaliwe kwizibhalo ezingcwele zakudala nakwizibhalo ezingcwele zangoku.

Kodwa ndiza kunika izilumkiso zam nangona kunjalo, nasemva kokuba izibhengezo zomgwebo wenu wokugqibela zibhengeziwe kwaye sele ziqalile. Kuba iindlela zam ziindlela zobabalo nenceba nothando kwaye ngenyaniso engunaphakade, ndinqwenela ukuba kungabikho namnye otshabalalayo kodwa bonke abantu bafikelele kusindiso olungunaphakade nasekubuyiselweni, nangona uninzi luya kusala isipho sam kwaye lukhethe intuthumbo yalo engunaphakade nomgwebo endaweni yoko. Nangona, ndiza kulumkisa abantu, akukho zilumkiso zinikiweyo eziya kutshintsha izigwebo zam, okanye ixesha lazo, okanye iziphumo zazo, okanye izicwangciso zam ezingunaphakade zokukhulula iziqhamo zam zokuqala, kwanokuzixhwila ukuze zizalisekise ilifa lazo elingunaphakade phambi kokuba intlekele iwe. Selah.

Kwaye ndiwuthumela lo myalezo wothando nesilumkiso eluthandweni lwam kumntu wemvelo ombi, kwanakwalo uthando ndithumela imiyalezo yothando nesilumkiso kubantwana bam abamkele uYesu uKristu ngokuthobela; kuba ndenza ukuba line kwabalungileyo nabangalunganga ngokufanayo. Ngenxa yothando lwam ngani, ndiza kunika ubizo lwam lokugqibela phambi kokuba intlekele ibethe isuka kwitrone yam yamandla angunaphakade, yomgwebo, neyesibhengezo apho ilizwi lam elidudumayo lisaduduma ngomsindo omkhulu nolungileyo. Kwananjalo, kuba NIYALA ukundiva nangona ndilumkisile ndalumkisa iminyaka emininzi, emininzi yeejubhili ngoku! Ndiveni kwaye nindive kakuhle kuba amaxesha enu entshabalaliso engunaphakade aya kuthunyelwa ukuba abophe emgwebeni bonke abathanda benze ubuxoki, nabathakazelela ukungalungisi, kwaye ukuba niyandala kula maxesha obabalo, niya kuwela emgwebeni ongunaphakade kuba akukho lunye ukhetho.

Noko ke, ngekratshi lenu eliyimpumputhe elineentliziyo ezilukhuni, niyamala umntu omnye endamthumelayo ukuba nisindiswe ngokupheleleyo, kwanaye uYesu waseNazarete, kwanaye loo Nyana uthobekileyo nomkhonzi kaYehova endimenze umoya ophilisa, ndamenza waba ngaphantsi kancinane kunoYehova ngokwakhe ekuvukeni kwam kwabafileyo nasekuzukisweni kukaKristu Yesu! Kuba NDINGUYE ophakamileyo nolawula yonke indalo yam kwaye ngenyaniso engunaphakade nengatshintshiyo, NDINGUYE othumela imvula kwabalungileyo nabangalunganga ngokufanayo eluthandweni lwam ngendalo yam, kwaye kwiindlela zam akukho kuze kubekho kukhetha buso kwizigwebo zam zobulungisa kwindalo yam.

Ngoko ke, qondani ukuba ukuba ngumkhethi buso yenye yeendlela ezininzi ezingcolileyo zabawileyo, ngoko ke ndiza kulumkisa kwanoyena moni umbi phakathi kwabemi bomhlaba phambi kokuba umgwebo wabo ubabethe ngokupheleleyo, ukuze basinde kwimizuzwana embalwa eseleyo nephaphazelayo yobabalo phambi kokuba intlekele ibethe. Ngoko ke ndicebisa bonke abeva okanye abafunda la mazwi ukuba bajikele kuYesu waseNazarete kwangoko, bengathandabuzi nto kuba kwisihlwele esikhulu akusayi kubakho elinye ithuba, kuba kwizono zabo ezimbi nakwindevelo yabo epheleleyo yazo zonke izinto ezingcwele, baya kuwela emgwebeni ongunaphakade, kwanokugcinwa kwintuthumbo apho baya kulindela khona loo mgwebo wetrone emhlophe enkulu, apho abayi kuma kodwa endaweni yoko babotshwe baze banikelwe emadangatheni esohlwayo apho izono zabo ziya kunikelwa emadangatheni ukuze kubuyekezwe ngokupheleleyo ubulungisa ngonaphakade.

Noko ke, wena, O' mntu owileyo, ubuya kuhlala kwiindlela zakho zokungalungisi, kwanokoyiswa ngokupheleleyo ngabawileyo ekungazini kwakho nasebumfama bakho obubangelwe bubuxoki babo, kwanobuxoki obuthandayo kuba uyazithanda iindlela zobuxoki bakho. Ngoko ke, ndithumele emhlabeni inkohliso enkulu neyomeleleyo ukuze nonke nigwetywe nina nithakazelela ukungalungisi kwaye niyamala uYesu waseNazarete, kwanaye uYeshuwa HaMashiach. Kwaye njengoko niphuthaphutha ekungazini kwiindlela zenu eziyimfama nezineentliziyo ezilukhuni, njengoko nihleli ebumnyameni ekungakhathalini nasesonweni esikhulu, njengoko nihamba ngeze nizibekela ingqumbo enkulu nesiphumo esingunaphakade ekungeveni kwenu, ndisaya kuliphakamisa ilizwi lam, ndithumela la mazwi njengobungqina nxamnye nabo baya kwala, kwanethemba lokuba ningajikela kuKristu Yesu, ukuze ndinisindise emgwebeni wabawileyo: Selah.

O' Mntu wemvelo, kwanani nonke bantwana baka-Adam abangakhululwanga nabangaguqukiyo, kwanani nonke endinohlwayayo emhlabeni eluthandweni lwam ngani! Noko ke nikholelwe obona buxoki busisidenge enibunikwe njengemfundo ekufundisweni kwenu ngabawileyo. Kwanemfundo yokufa enikwe kumaziko enu abolileyo nabolisa okufa apho iindlela zesodomite ngoku zilawula njengoko ziphawula kwaye zilungisa abantwana benu ukuze bazonwabise ngabamsulwa kwiinkanuko zabo ezibolileyo. Noko ke, nithabatheke kakhulu ziziyolo zesono, kwaye nithwele kanzima kukubawa kwenu ukuqokelela ubutyebi obuninzi, kangangokuba ngokuzithandela ninikela ngabantwana benu ngeendlela zikaBhahali ngenxa yeqhekeza lesonka esingcolileyo. Ngoko ke, ndibavumele abawileyo ukuba batyhefe ukutya kwenu, kwaye ndibanike intabalala yenu epheleleyo ukuze inamba, irhamncwa eliphuma elwandle, nerhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni zizalisekise iinjongo zam, kuba neentshaba zam ziyaliva ilizwi lam.

Kuba ezi zinto nizibiza ngokuba yimfundo ziyinkohliso kwaye zonakele ngoyilo kwaye oku kungenxa yokuba niyala ulwazi lokwenyani neendlela zikaElohim umdali wenu, nikhetha endaweni yoko ukulotywa zizipili zenu ezimnyama eniziphethe ngokuthembeka okukhulu esandleni njengoko nisuka kwiscreen nesiya kwiscreen kobona bumnyama bunzulu. Noko ke noyiswe ngokupheleleyo kwaye nimfanyekiswe lulonwabo oluphantsi noludlulayo njengoko iingqondo zenu zitshintshwa ngeekhemikhali ngoyilo ngeendlela zezixhobo enizibophelela ngazo esonweni. Kwaye ke ulwazi lwenkohliso lubangela ikratshi kwaye ikratshi lihamba phambi kwentshabalaliso enkulu. Qonda, O' Mntu, ukuba ulwazi lwakho alunako ukukusindisa kwizigwebo zam, kwaye umgwebo wabawileyo ngoku umiselwe ukuwa. Noko ke, wena, O' Mntu wemvelo usisidenge kakhulu ukuba ungaqondi ukuba abawileyo bakufundisile ngobuxoki babo, ukuze babangele abantu abaninzi kangangoko kunokwenzeka ukuba bazimanye nabo kwilifa labo lokutsha, elibolileyo, nelisongwayo lesulfure. Kwanasohlwayo esirhabaxa esenzelwe umtyholi neengelosi zakhe, hayi uAdam. Selah.

Noko ke, ngemvume yenu yokuzithandela niyazingca kakhulu ngeempumelelo zenu zehlabathi ezingenaxabiso apho niqokelela ngokubawa amaphepha enu okuzenzisa angenaxabiso kunye needijithi zenu zokuzenzisa ezingenaxabiso kwiiakhawunti zokuzenzisa ezingenaxabiso eziya kuthathwa kuni ngokukhawuleza emgwebeni ngoku, kwanangenxa yokuba ezi ziindlela zikaEsawu owathengisa ubuzibulo bakhe ngeqhekeza lesonka. Noko ke, ngemvume yenu yokuzithandela niyabavumela abawileyo ukuba bathathe imali kuni ngesoyikiso samandla ukuze nihlawulele ukufundiswa kwabantwana benu ngeendlela zokufa. Ukuze bonke abafundiswe ekungazini nasebumfama obunjalo bakhonze ngokuzithandela abawileyo becinga ukuba benza uThixo ngokwakhe umusa omkhulu ezonweni zabo, ngokungathi uYehova kufuneka asuke ecaleni kwindalo yakhe nakulawulo lwakhe ngenxa yekratshi labo elingenaxabiso, elinesono. Kwanento engenakwenzeka engasoze yenzeke, kuba NDINGUYE.

Kwaye kwiindlela zenu ezineentliziyo ezilukhuni zokungalungisi nide nalahlekelwa ziindlebe zenu kwaye nimi nizizithulu, nizizimumu, kwaye nizimfama kwiindlela zenu zokungalungisi ezininzise ebukhobokeni njengekhoboka lesono ukuze nithomalalise iinkanuko zabawileyo, enibakhonzayo kwiindlela zenu ezingenakholo. Noko ke, umkhonzi akahlali endlwini ngonaphakade ukuba ngokuthobela ukhetha ukungena ngomnyango okuphela kwawo oya emazulwini endiya kuze ndiwuvulele uluntu: uYesu uKristu okwanguye, uYesu uMesiya, ogama lakhe likwanguYeshuwa HaMashiach, onguloo mnyango mkhulu novulekileyo kuphela wosindiso owakhe wavulwa sisandla sam kwabathembekileyo ngokobulungisa kuKristu Yesu. Selah!

NDINGUYE, kwaye ndithumele la mazwi othando kwabagwetyiweyo ngomthunywa wam nomkhonzi wam, kwaye ngomthunywa wam nomkhonzi wam ndithumele la mazwi kwabo baya kugwetywa kungekudala.

Qondani nonke nina niva la mazwi, ukuba esi asisosibhengezo sokugqibela somgwebo, esizisa isigqibo sokugqibela kumntu wemvelo. Ngokuchaseneyo, lo myalezo usebenza ukulumkisa abangevayo nabangemalungisa beBhabhiloni eyimfihlo kwanabo bonke abathunyelwa la mazwi, (kwaye abanye baya kufika elusindisweni kule mizuzwana yokugqibela) kodwa kananjalo njengobungqina bokugqibela besilumkiso esinikwe phambi kokuba umgwebo uwele ngokupheleleyo kwiBhabhiloni eyimfihlo.

Qondani, ukuba amazwi am aya kufumana amehlo, iindlebe, neentliziyo zabo athunyelwe kubo, kwaye akukho mzamo ovela kwabawileyo, amasikizi abo, okanye iitekhnoloji zabo ezingenaxabiso nezingaziyo ezinokumisa oko uMkhulu NDINGUYE anenjongo yokukufezekisa.

Kwaye ke ndityikitya ileta yam yothando yobuqu kwabagwetyiweyo ngethemba lokuba ngokuthobela amazwi am athunyelwe eluthandweni lwam ngabo baya kuzisaba izigwebo zabo phambi kokuba eso sandla sokugqibela sibethe loo mzuzwana wokugqibela.

Kwaye ke ndityikitya ileta yam yothando ngoThando olukhulu neGunja elingunaphakade!
