Khangelani! IMvana kaThixo!

Yivani aMazwi kaYesu Krestu nithobe iindlebe zenu kwizandi zamaZwi am, noluse iintliziyo zenu ukuze namkele uMesiya wenu wokwenene, O' Sirayeli, niliqonde ilizwi lam ukuze nibe nobulumko ezindleleni zenu, nokuze nindivumele ndiphinde ndiziqondise iinyawo zenu nditshintshe nendlela yenu kuloo ndlela ibanzi yentshabalalo eniyilandelayo, niye kwindlela emxinwa yosindiso eziziindlela zam nendinguye. Kuba ndingulowo uYehova wamthumayo ukuba anisinde, kwaye ndiyimbewu ethenjisiweyo yomfazi eya kutyumza intloko yenyoka, kwaye ndiyanibizela kum bazalwana bam, nkqu nezalamane zam endingumkhululi wazo. Ngoko ke ndiniyalela ukuba noluse iintliziyo zenu niyeke iingxoxo zenu ezingenasiqhamo ukuze nithwale isiqhamo kuMesiya wenu wokwenene, ngoko ke yivani imiyalelo yam eninikwe yona ukuze nithobele, ukuze niphile phambi koYehova uMkhulu onguYE NDINGUYE, nkqu ngokundamkela njengoMesiya wenu ongunaphakade, onyanisileyo, onyanisileyo: ndinguye.

Kwaye kanye njengoko uYehova wathumela umthetho kaMoses kooyihlo (lowo mthetho ungumfundisi-ntsapho wenu ojoliswe ekuniziseni kuKrestu), ngokunjalo uYehova undithumile njengoko kwathenjiswayo mandulo apho wathembisa usindiso kooyihlo endithetha nabantwana babo ngoku, nkqu nabantwana bakwaSirayeli endibathanda ngothando oluvutha ngonaphakade. Kodwa ngenxa yokungazi nokungakholwa okungenalukholo ndaliwa ngooyihlo ndaza ndanikelwa ezandleni zabantu abanesono ukuba benze ngenkohlakalo enkulu nenkohliso ezo zinto umthetho wawungabavumeli ukuba bazenze; kwaye kananjalo ukuze babonakale bengamalungisa ebantwini belizwe xa babebulala imvana kaThixo ngemvume yooyihlo, ulwaphulo-mthetho oluninzi lwenu nanamhlanje nisavumelana nalo ngobuyatha.

Noko ke, qonda Sirayeli ukuba andithathwanga ngenkani ziintshaba zam neentshaba zenu. Qonda, ukuba andithathwanga ngenkani, kwaye kungekho buqhetseba kwaye akukho sikhali senziwe ngokuchasene nam sinokuphumelela, kuba utshilo uYehova kwaye oko akutshoyo uYehova akukho mntu unokukuguqula! Ewe, Sirayeli, ndingulowo wathi kuYuda Sikariyoti xa ndangcatshwa sisandla sakhe ngamaqhosha esilivere angamashumi amathathu, oku ukwenzayo, kwenze ngokukhawuleza! Kuba ixabiso elalifanele umnini-khoboka owayenekhoboka elahlatywa yiNkabi ubomi bam bathengiswa ezandleni zabo bandihlabayo, banikelwa nangezandla zababingeleli abakhulu neSanhedrin endandijongene nabo ngenxa yeendlela zabo ezimbi nezihanahanisayo. Kwaye ngoko uYuda wandingcatsha ezandleni zika-Esawu, nkqu nalowo uye wabanga ilizwe lesithembiso kwakhona ngenkani, ibango elo u-Esawu walithengisa ngesiqwengana sesonka. Kwaye ke, ndazivumela ngokuzithandela ukuba ndithathwe ziintshaba zam ngoxa ndandisenako ukubaleka lula, kwaye inene, inene, ukuba ndandithandazile kuYehova, ngeyendinike umyalelo wemikhosi yeeNgelosi engaphezu kweshumi elinesibini, kodwa zazingazaliseka njani ke iZibhalo, kwaye kwagqitywa.

Noko ke, kunjalo, ukuba iinjongo neendlela zam aziqondwanga zizalamane zam nabo besizwe sam endibathanda ngonaphakade ngothando olunzulu noluvuthayo olungenakucinywa okanye loyiswe, kuba ndiliLitye lenu leSiseko ekungekho makhi unokwakha ngaphandle kwalo, nalapho abangakholwayo bakhubeka khona. Kuba kwakungothando lwam ngani, nangoAdam wonke owoyisiweyo ngabawileyo, apho ndazinikela ngokuzithandela ezandleni zeentshaba zam ukuze ndinihlawulele kwisiqalekiso somthetho.

Kwaye ngoko ke umyalelo wam ongunaphakade ngulo, wokuba nithandane, njengoko nam ndanithandayo. Akukho mntu unothando olukhulu kunolu, xa nawuphi na umntu enikela ngobomi bakhe njengedini ngenxa yabahlobo bakhe, into endayenza ngokuzithandela ndisazi ukuba ngaphandle kokuthobela kwam intando kaBawo uluntu lunokulahlwa ngonaphakade. Kwaye ndingumsindisi ka-Adam, nkqu nalowo uhlawulele uluntu kwimeko yalo yokuwa ukuba nawuphi na umntu angaza kum ngamadolo aguqileyo aze andivume njengeNkosi yakhe kwaye ngokwenene ndinguMesiya wenu wokwenene O' Sirayeli.

Qondani bazalwana bam, ukuba andenzanga mabango ngam angabhalwanga ngomnwe kaYehova emacwecweni amatye kwaye athethwa ngomlomo wabakhonzi bakhe abaprofeti ukuba abhalelwe ukholo nokuthobela kweendlalifa zesithembiso, eningabo nina O' Sirayeli, ukuba aniyi kuzenza lukhuni iintliziyo zenu njengoko ooyihlo benza entlango ekuvukeleni kwabo uYehova, kwaye entlango ubomi babo bokungakholwa baphela bungenamsebenzi, kwaye bona ngokwabo abazuzanga zithembiso kodwa abantwana bokholo!

Kanti ke nasebumfameni benu obuyinxalenye nikholelwa kubuxoki buka-Esawu bokuba ndingumkhohlisi kwaye ke ndaliwa ngu-Esawu kuba u-Esawu ufuna ukubanga ilifa awalithengisela omncinci kwaye akukho ndlela yenguquko ku-Esawu, nangona wayifuna ngeenyembezi. Kwaye ooyihlo abazange baziqonde iinjongo zam zokwenene kuba babefuna inkokeli yehlabathi ukuba ibhukuqe abo babezibophelele kubo ekuthobeleni ukuze bagcine izikhundla zabo phakathi kwabantu. Noko ke, ndathunyelwa ukuba ndihlangule uluntu esandleni sabawileyo awathi uAdam wabajoyina ngobuyatha ekungathobelini kwakhe umyalelo kaYehova ngenxa yamaqhinga enyoka eyamkhohlisayo kunye nonina wabo bonke abaphilayo xa bona e-Eden ekuqaleni. Kwaye nangona iintlanga ezininzi ziye zavela zawa, nobukumkani obuninzi buye bavela bawa ukusukela kumaxesha edini lam, uSirayeli usebukhobokeni kwiintshaba zakhe unanamhla kwaye ukhonza iinjongo zika-Esawu, ofuna intshabalalo yakhe epheleleyo.

Ewe, uSirayeli uhleli ebukhobokeni kuba isizwe senu sathathwa kuni njengesohlwayo sokubulala igazi elimsulwa, nkqu ngokuzithandela nangobubi ukubulala kanye loo mntu ungenasono uthunywe nguYehova ukuze anihlawulele. Nkqu nam, uYesu Krestu, ekuzalisekeni kwexesha, uThixo wathumela uNyana wakhe ezelwe ngumfazi, waza waba phantsi komthetho, ukuze abakhulule abo babephantsi komthetho. Kwaye ebomini bam obungenasono nasekuthobeleni kwam ndazibonakalisa njengemvana kaThixo kwidini elibuhlungu nelikhohlakeleyo xa ndazinikela njengombingeleli omkhulu ngonaphakade ngokohlobo lukaMelkitsedeke.

Kwaye olu thumo lugqityiwe kwaye ndihleli ngasekunene kukaBawo, nkqu uYehova, uMkhulu onguYE NDINGUYE. Kwaye ndizuzile zonke izinto, kwaye ezandleni zam ezinamandla kunikelwe zonke izinto ezulwini nasemhlabeni de iintshaba zam zenziwe isihlalo seenyawo zam kodwa uBukumkani bam abuyi kuba nasiphelo! Obo Bukumkani apho uKumkani wenu uya kulawula ngokuqinisekileyo busondela ekupheleni kwenkathazo kaYakobi apho ndiya kutshabalalisa ubukumkani beentshaba ndize ndizise umgwebo omkhulu nesohlwayo kwabawileyo kwaye baya kungena esohlwayweni ngenxa yezono zabo ezininzi nomgwebo wabo ongunaphakade apho kungasayi kubakho mzuzu wokuphumla. Kwaye abanye babo baya kungena kwelo chibi likhulu lomlilo nesulfure evuthayo ekufikeni kwam kwesibini ukuza kulawula umhlaba xa irhamncwa nomprofeti wobuxoki bephoswa bephila apho. Kwaye ooSathana baya kubotshwa ngelo tyathanga likhulu abaya kubotshwa ngalo ekuthuthunjisweni nasekoyikeni okukhulu njengoko bebotshiwe kuloyiko olulindelekileyo lwento ebalindeleyo kwilifa labo elingunaphakade lesohlwayo ngonaphakade. Nkqu nesigwebo esoyikekayo angenakuze asibaleke uSathana apho abotshiweyo aze aphoswe kweyona mililo ishushu ngonaphakade ekupheleni kwexesha lakhe elifutshane.

Kuba, ndingulowo wanikelwa usindiso lukaYakobi, nkqu nembewu ethenjisiweyo yomfazi awayekholelwa kuyo uAdam ngethemba, kwaye uya kuvuselwa kubutsha bobomi kude kube ngunaphakade. Kuba ndingulowo uthunyelwe ukuhlawulela uAdam, kwaye ndanikela ngethontsi lam lokugqibela legazi elaphalazwayo laza lafunxwa ngumhlaba obomvu eKalvari, nkqu nalapho ndathetha laa mazwi angunaphakade athi Kugqityiwe. Kwaye kunjalo, ukuba ndingulowo othandweni lwam ngoYehova nothando lwam ngommelwane wam wazinikela njengedini kubuhlungu obukhulu, ehlazweni, nasekuthuthunjisweni kwaye ndoyisile isono nokufa kwaye umhla uyasondela apho yonke into embi ingasayi kubakho.

Kwaye ndakwenza oko ngokuzithandela, nkqu ngokuzinikela ngenxa yozuko olwalubekwe phambi kwam kwaye ndakwenza oko ndilidela lonke ihlazo elalingelolam ukuba ndilithwale. Noko ke, eluthandweni lwam ngani, ndathatha ihlazo nesohlwayo senu emqolo wam, kanye njengoko babekrazula inyama yam emathanjeni am ngezabhokhwe zabo. Ewe, ndathatha ihlazo nesohlwayo senu ekuphathweni gadalala nakwintiyo yabantu abanesono ababeshukunyiswa ngabawileyo ngokwabo njengoko babendingxwelerha ngokungazi endaweni yenu bengaqondi ngeendlela zabo zoburhamncwa ukuba ngokubulala imvana kaThixo babetywina iikamva zabo emililweni ngonaphakade kuba akunakwenzeka ukuba ukufa kundibambe, kuba andizange ndone nakanye, nangaliphi na ixesha, kwaye ngokwenene andisokuze!

Ewe, Sirayeli, ndathatha indawo yenu esohlwayweni nendawo yooyihlo abandala ngokungazi ngokuyalelwa ngu-Esawu! Kodwa naninokuhlawulelwa njani ngaphandle kokungcatsha kwabo okungenalukholo kuba ukuhlawulela uAdam kwakunokuzalisekisa kuphela igazi elingenasono iimfuno zesohlwayo ezifunwa yimithetho engunaphakade nenobulungisa kaYehova kuba ngaphandle kokuphalazwa kwegazi akukho kuxolelwa kwesono. Kwaye ekubeni uAdam wadalwa engenasono kwaye egqibelele ngomhla wadalwa ngawo, ngokunjalo nam, uYesu Krestu (ongumvana kaThixo) ndadalwa ndigqibelele kwaye ndingenasono ukuze kuhlawulelwe ngokupheleleyo uluntu. Kuba ukuba igazi leenkunzi zeenkomo neleebhokhwe, nothuthu lwethokazi, lutshizwa phezu kwabangahlambulukanga, luyangcwalisa ngokuphathelele ekuhlanjululweni kwenyama, kobeka phi na ke ukungakumbi igazi likaKrestu, owathi ngoMoya ongunaphakade wazinikela engenabala kuThixo, ukusihlambulula isazela senu kwimisebenzi efileyo, ukuze nikhonze uThixo ophilayo?

Ngoko ke ndiyaninika ukuba niqonde ukuba ndathatha indawo yenu ekugwetyweni nasesohlwayweni, kwaye ndakwenza oko ngokuzithandela nangaphandle kokunyanzelwa; nangona ndathandaza kumyezo waseGetsemane ukuba ikhona na enye indlela, kodwa makungabi yintando yam kodwa ekaYehova eyenziweyo. Kwaye ke ehlazweni lam, ndasiwa ngaphandle kwamasango aloo mzi mkhulu, (nkqu iYerusalem ebulala abaprofeti) apho ndathunyelwa kwabam, kwaye apho zahlulwa iingubo zam njengoko babesenza amaqashiso! Ewe, apho eKalvari, emva kokuba ndandisele ndinyamezele iiyure zokohlwaywa okungapheliyo endingenatyala kulo, ndabethelelwa emnqamlezweni apho ngezandla zabantu abanesono, ababengenaso isabelo okanye iqashiso ezonweni zabo nangona kuncinane. Noko ke, ngokuzithandela nangokuzinikela ndabotshwa ndaza ndabethelelwa ngezandla neenyawo zam ukuba ndixhomeke kuloo mthi urhabaxa endaweni yenu, nkqu nesohlwayo esasingafanelanga ndisithwale. Kuba k peni na kwakhe kwanikwa abamsulwa ukuba bazihombise ngokuzithandela ngehlazo umthi wendelelo ehlazweni elikhulu nakubuhlungu obukhulu ukuze kusindiswe abazalwana babo abangemalungisa ukuze babandezeleke endaweni yabo, kanti ke eluthandweni lwam ngani ndenza kanye loo nto. Qonda ukuba nangaliphi na ixesha ndandisenokubamisa ngokucela uBawo kwaye ngeyendinike imikhosi yeengelosi engaphezu kwe-12 ngokomyalelo wam, kanti ke, andizange ndicele kodwa ndakhetha ukunyamezela ngokupheleleyo nangokuzithandela isohlwayo senu esasingafanelanga ndisithwale. Kwaye ke eluthandweni lwam ndazinikela ngenxa yenu, kwaye ndingaphinda ndikwenze, nangona into enjalo ingeyomfuneko kwaye uYehova ulwamkele idini lam kanye, konke, kwaye ngonaphakade, ngoko ke akusekho dini lisesele ngenxa yesono.

Ngoko ke, Sirayeli, luqonde uthando, ukuzeka kade umsindo, ubabalo nenceba endikubonisa yona kule meko yenu yangoku yokwala ngokuvukela uMesiya wenu wokwenene, kwaye ndiza kuni kweli xesha lokugqibela eluthandweni lwam ngani olusengapheliyo nolungenakoyiswa, ukuba ningamkela kuphela uMesiya wenu wokwenene. Kuba ndingulowo wathatha ngokuzithandela indawo yenu phakathi kokugwetywa, ukohlwaywa, nokubulawa kwaboni kuba umvuzo wesono kukufa, kanti ke mna andinasono ngonaphakade, kwaye ndisoloko ndisenza intando kaBawo, nkqu nomzekelo eniwulayo ekungathobelini kwenu uYehova ngenkani, nasekulaleni kwenu uMesiya wenu wokwenene.

Kwaye ndaliwa ngasiphi isizathu? Nkqu noPontiyo Pilato owayeyiPrefekti yaseYuda ngelo xesha wayezimisele ukundiyeka kwaye wathetha inyaniso ngabamangaleli bam, nkqu nabo bakwa-Esawu abaqokelela ooyihlo ukuba bamemeze kuye sikhululele uBharabhas! Kanye njengoko babekhwaza phantse ngomphefumlo omnye besithi "Mbethelele emnqamlezweni!", ngokuphathelele uPilato ekhululela kubo uMesiya wabo wokwenene negazi elimsulwa abalilayo. Kuba wayesazi kakuhle ukuba bandinikele ngomona. Kwakhona xa wayehleli esihlalweni sokugweba, umfazi wakhe wathumela kuye, esithi, musa ukuba nanto yakwenza naloo mntu ulilungisa: kuba ndibandezeleke izinto ezininzi namhlanje ephupheni ngenxa yakhe, kwaye kwaba njalo kwaye kubhaliwe kwizibhalo ezingcwele ukuba niya kufumana ukholo lokukholelwa kubungqina bam.

Kwaye konke kungenxa yokuba ooyihlo babenqwenela izinto zalo mhlaba kwaye bezithanda izikhundla zabo phakathi kwabantu apho babenokuphila ngezishumi neminikelo yabantu kwaye beme njengabaprofeti bobuxoki abazicingela ukuba bangamalungisa apho babephindaphinda ubulumko buka-Esawu ezindlebeni zabantu: nkqu nobulumko bobuxoki ababexabise kakhulu abakhulu babo kunelizwi nobulumko bukaYehova, uMkhulu onguYE NDINGUYE, abamala xa bamala baza bazibethelela emnqamlezweni uYesu waseNazarete.

Kwaye kunjalo, ukuba ekundaleni kwenu, nkqu uYesu waseNazarete, ndajikela kwiintlanga, kwaye baliva ilizwi lam okwexeshana kwaye abaninzi, abaninzi ababengenathemba ngenye indlela, ngoku bafikelele elusindisweni eNkosini yabo nkqu nesihlwele esikhulu endiya kusivusa siphila kum ukuze ndisibonise kuYehova kwindibano enkulu yabangcwele esezakwenzeka. Kwaye abaninzi abavela eYuda nabaninzi ababesabalele phesheya batywinelwe usindiso olupheleleyo. Kwaye kubazalwana benu, nkqu nabo basebukumkanini basentla abaphawulelwe usindiso olungunaphakade, nkqu nabaninzi babo ngoku batywinwe ngonaphakade kwaye baya kuvuselwa kubutsha bobomi. Inene, inene, bonke aba baye bashiya umzekelo wenguquko ukuze niwulandele, ukuba ningalithobela ilizwi loMesiya okuphela kwakhe wokwenene uYehova aya kuze amthume, nkqu uYesu waseNazarete uMesiya wenu wokwenene!

Kungenxa yezono zazo ezabangela ukuba izizwe zasentla zisasazeke phakathi kweentlanga, apho ziye zalondolozwa zagcinelwa usindiso, ngesandla esinamandla sikaYahweh. Kanti ke kubhaliwe ngesandla soMpostile wam uPawulos xa wayethetha namaRoma kwiminyaka emininzi eyadlulayo esithi: Siyazi ukuba zonke izinto zisebenzela ukulunga kwabo bamthandayo uThixo, abo babizwe ngenjongo yakhe. Kuba abo wayebazi kwangaphambili, wabamisela kwangaphambili ukuba bafane nomfanekiso woNyana wakhe, ukuze abe ngowamazibulo phakathi kwabazalwana abaninzi. Ngaphezu koko, abo wabamisela kwangaphambili, wababiza, waza abo wababizayo, wabagwebela, waza abo wabagwebelayo, wabazukisa. Ngaloo ndlela kwaSirayeli kaThixo kuya kuzalisekiswa ukuhlanganiswa kwezizwe ezilahlekileyo, yaye amaSirayeli amaninzi afumene usindiso ngokundivuma njengeNkosi yawo, mna Yesu waseNazarete, ngexesha lam lobabalo nosindiso abalufumeneyo.

Nangona okwangoku niyeyisa imisebenzi yam, kwanemisebenzi yam yosindiso endiyifezileyo ezintlangeni ngomyalelo kaYahweh ngexesha lam lobabalo ngoku elifikelele esiphelweni; qondani ke ukuba oko uYahweh akuhlanjululeyo ngegazi likaYeshua HaMashiach, ningakubizi ngokuba yinto eqhelekileyo okanye engcolileyo! Kwaye mna, Yeshua HaMashiach, ndiyanibongoza ukuba nilandele iindlela zabazalwana benu, nokuba bakufuphi okanye bakude, abeza kum ngenguquko epheleleyo ukuze ndinamkelele kum ndinibuyisele kuYahweh, kuba lowo unoNyana unoYise naye, kodwa lowo ungenaye uNyana akanaye noYise, utshilo uYahweh!

Kungoko ndibuyela kwakhona kwizihlobo zam njengoko ndijikela kude neentlanga kweli xesha lokugqibela lesiphelo, yaye ukwahlukana kokugqibela kuya kwenzeka ngoku kwiveki yamashumi asixhenxe kaDaniyeli, efikileyo ukuhlanjulula ihlabathi ngomlilo wam wokucikida, aze uEsawu ahlulwe kuYakobi ngonaphakade kanaphakade. Kuba uEsawu wathengisa ngobuzibulo bakhe, waza uYahweh wasamkela ngonaphakade isigqibo sakhe ukuze sibe sisigqibo sanaphakade, kuba abantwana besithembiso baziindlalifa zezithembiso, hayi abantwana benyama; ukuba ningabakaKristu, ngoko niyimbewu ka-Abraham, neendlalifa ngesithembiso. Ngaloo ndlela izithembiso zelifa ezenziwa nguYahweh ngokwakhe zenziwa ngokholo kwabo zenziwa kubo, kuba olilungisa uya kuphila ngokholo! Yaye emva kokuba kunikwe izithembiso zokholo, kwenziwa iminqophiso kwanikwa imiqondiso yesithembiso, yaye iminqophiso yaba yimiyolelo ngokuphalazwa kwegazi, yaye yonke iminqophiso yakudala nazo zonke izithembiso zikaYahweh ukusuka kweyona incinane kuye kweyona inkulu zibandakanyiwe kuMyolelo wam oMtsha wesithembiso, apho indalo iya kwenziwa ibe lilungisa ngonaphakade ekubuyiselweni okungunaphakade kuYise wezikhanyiso, ongenakuguquka nasithunzi sakujika.

Ngoko ke ndiza kwizihlobo zam ngothando nangokuthobeka okukhulu, yaye ndiza kuni ngokuzithandela eluthandweni lwam ngani, ndinganqweneli ukuba nabani na kuni atshabalale ekungakholweni okungenalukholo. Inene, inene, intsalela iya kusindiswa iphile kuYakobi naphakathi kwexesha lembandezelo kaYakobi, eliyiveki yamashumi asixhenxe kaDaniyeli. Ngoko ke, ndiyanibongoza bazalwana bam, phulaphulani isandi selizwi lam kuba eli lixesha lokugqibela kwaye oku kukwahlukana kokugqibela phambi kokuba ingqumbo ithululwe ngokupheleleyo naphambi kokuba ndize kutshabalalisa ubukumkani beentshaba ngokupheleleyo, kuba ngobulungisa ndiza njengeNgonyama yesizwe sakwaYuda kwaye ndiza kugweba ndenze imfazwe!

Nabo bonke abaya kubuyela kum ngeli xesha, Owu Sirayeli, kwanabo bonke endibayalelayo abazalwa ngooYise bakudala ukuba baphulaphule ilizwi lam, kwanabo bonke abaya kunqula igama lam elinamandla kude kube sekupheleni bevela ezintlangeni baya kusindiswa, kwaye baya kusindiswa ngokweendidi zabo. Iintlanga ziya kufumana imbeko efanayo neyam xa ndazinikela ngenxa yezono zoluntu, kwaye ziya kufela ukholo njengoko zibiza igama lam elisindisayo kude kube sekupheleni. Ziya kungena ebuqaqawulini obukhulu ngonaphakade kwaye imivuzo yazo yeyanaphakade ngenxa yokuma kwazo, kwaye ngokuthobela kwazo ziya kuba zoyisile iindlela zazo zokungakholwa nezokudikidiki, nangona kunjalo kuya kuba ngokomlilo.

Ke kuSirayeli, bazalwana bam, ndisaza kunisindisa kwaye ndiza kuninceda kunye nabam abali-144,000, abo bazihlambulule ngokupheleleyo njengaboyisi bam egazini lam elihlawulelayo nelihlangulayo ngomhla wosindiso lwabo, kwaye baya kuhlanganiselwa kum kuloo ndibano inkulu sele iza kwenzeka ngequbuliso emhlabeni. Kuba ndiya kukhokelela abam abali-144,000 ukuba behlele phezu kweNtaba yeZiyon, kwaye baya kundilandela inyathelo ngenyathelo njengoko bekhokelela intsalela yam kaYakobi ekhuselekweni entlango apho indawo ilungiswe nguYahweh ukuze bakhuseleke, kwaye baya kondliwa bakhathalelwe, kwaye inamba, irhamncwa, nomprofeti wobuxoki abayi kubachukumisa, bengayi kubafumana, nina eniyintsalela, Owu Sirayeli.
