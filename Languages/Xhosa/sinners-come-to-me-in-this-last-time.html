Ubungqina Obumbaxa: Icawa
KwiMisebenzi kaPawulos uMpostile
Meyi 10, 2024

Boni! Yizani Kum Ngeli Thuba Lokugqibela!

Yivani amazwi am, kwangawo amazwi kaYesu waseNazarete, kuba ndiza kuni ndinomoya wothando, uvelwano, ukuthobeka, ubabalo, ingakumbi inceba. Kuba kungekudala ubabalo lwam nenceba yam ziya kuthatyathelw' indawo yinkathazo enkulu, kwaneenkathazo zikaYakobi, yaye esi sixa seminyaka esixhenxe siza kwahluka kuyo nantoni na eyakhe yabonwa ngumhlaba, kususela ekuqaleni kwendalo. Kuya kuqala ngentshabalalo engekalindeleki nentshutshiso eya kuya isiba mandundu. Ngoko ke, ndiza okokugqibela kuboni abangekaguquki kubabalo lwam nakunceba yam. Kuba kusekho umzuzwana wokuba niguquke ukuba nithe neza kum, mna Yesu Krestu, nindivume njengeNkosi yenu kwaye nikholwe entliziyweni yenu ukuba uThixo undivusile kwabafileyo. Ndolule iingalo zam ngobabalo nenceba kwihlabathi elifayo isizukulwana sonke sobabalo ngoku, yaye abaninzi bandenze iNkosi yabo, kwanabaninzi kangangoko iNkosi uThixo wethu ibabizileyo yabakhetha kwangaphambi kokusekwa kwehlabathi ukuze batyhilwe kum, mna Yesu Krestu iNkosi enamandla: Kuba ndiyiNkosi yeeNkosi noKumkani wooKumkani.

Yaye ndibabizile abanyuliweyo, nabo bonke abathembekileyo elusindisweni lwam. Abaninzi basindisiwe! Usindiswe entweni, uyabuza? Usindiswe kumgwebo nakwintshabalalo esele iza kukhululwa phezu kweli hlabathi, oothixo balo, abalawuli balo, iinkulumbuso zalo, nabo bonke abalandela uSathana, kuba unguthixo weli hlabathi kwaye umgwebo wakhe ufikile. Kwanokusindiswa kumgwebo wetrone enkulu emhlophe nakwidike lomlilo elilungiselelwe uMtyholi neengelosi zakhe. Umgwebo ugqithisiwe kubalawuli bobu bumnyama bangoku, kwaye kungekudala uza kuzalisekiswa ugqitywe, kuba umhlaba uya kulawulwa sisandla sam ngobulungisa, kuba ixesha lam lokutyhilwa lifikile, kwaye umhlaba ngowam nokuzala kwawo, ndim, uYeshua HaMashiach!

Umgwebo uyeza kwaye sele ulapha kodwa awukagqitywa. Kwaye abaninzi benu basacinga ukuba banexesha lokuzifica ezonweni zenu baze bafune ukwanelisa iinkanuko zenu zesono ezivela enyameni yenu eyonakeleyo. Qondani ukuba andinqweneli kugweba nokunigwebela nonke, kuba ndinikele ngobomi bam eluthandweni lwam ngani nanjengentlawulo epheleleyo yezono zenu kwintlungu enkulu nakwintuthumbo emnqamlezweni! Kuba akukho nasinye isono esingahlawulelwanga ngokupheleleyo lidini lam lentlawulelo njengePasika egqibeleleyo. NdiyiMvana kaThixo esusa izono zehlabathi. Kwaye andizelanga ukunigweba, kodwa ndilapha ukuze ndinisindise. Ihlabathi njengoko nilazi sele liza kutshintsha kakhulu kangangokuba aniyi kuyazi into eza kuphuma eluthuthwini, kuba inxalenye enkulu yehlabathi sele iza kutshiswa ngamadangatye ize itshabalale ngeendlela ezininzi kumgwebo kaYehova olungileyo. Kwaye uloyiko olukhulu luphuma kolu thuthu.

Kuba inkosana yeli hlabathi igwetyiwe, kwaye umgwebo wayo uya kuzalisekiswa ugqitywe ngokupheleleyo. Kuba uSathana wenze ububi obuninzi kwaye iindlela zakhe ziyamanyala, kwaye uza kuphela ukuba, ukubulala, nokutshabalalisa. Ulonakalisile uluntu ngokupheleleyo nangeendlela ezahlukeneyo, kwaye ufuna ukutshabalalisa yonke into ethandwa nguThixo. Kuba wenjenje uThixo ukulithanda kwakhe ihlabathi, ude wancama uNyana wakhe okuphela kwamzeleyo, ukuze bonke abakholwayo kum bangatshabalali, koko babe nobomi obungunaphakade. Ngoko qondani ngokupheleleyo ukuba ndiyimvana yokugqibela yePasika yalo lonke ixesha (akusayi kubakho enye), kwaye ndizihlawulele zonke izono. USathana wonakalise imfuzo yoluntu ukusukela kumyezo wase-Eden, xa wahenda uAdam noEva ukuba bamkele isiqhamo sakhe esibolileyo baze bakholwe kulwazi lwakhe olungenaxabiso, kuba ulwazi lwakhe luzisa ukufa kubo bonke abatyayo. Kwaye ngenxa yesi sizathu uThixo uSomandla unikezele ngesivumelwano soxolo kwindalo yakhe, mna, Yesu Krestu.

Kuba lithini ilizwi lama-Efese, xa uPawulos wababhalela malunga nemisebenzi yam egqityiweyo (kuba ndathi ekuphefumleni kwam kokugqibela emnqamlezweni, "Kugqityiwe")! Kodwa ngoku, kuKrestu Yesu, nina enibe nikude kade, nisondeziwe ngegazi likaKrestu.

Kuba nguye uxolo lwethu, owenze zombini zaba nye, waza waluchitha udonga olwalusisithintelo phakathi kwethu. Yiva ilizwi lam. Ndiluxolo lwenu, kuba umthetho wagweba isono enyameni; kwaneyakho inyama negazi lonakele esonweni, kwanokungcola ukusukela e-Eden. Mna, Yesu Krestu, kwidini lam lentlawulelo eKalvari ndiluchithile udonga oluphakathi lokwahlula phakathi komYuda neNtlanga, ndabenza bobabini baba mntu mnye omtsha, ndabaphinda ndabadala ngobulungisa nobungcwele bokwenene kubo bonke abamkela uxolo ngokundivuma njengeNkosi yabo evukileyo!

Kodwa kukho okungakumbi okuvela kwabase-Efese (ngokungathi oku akumnandi ngokwaneleyo), ngoko ke ama-Efese ayaqhubeka: kwaye wasusa nangenxa yenyama yakhe unobangela wentiyo (oko kukuthi, umthetho womyalelo oqulethwe emthethweni obhaliweyo) ukuze enze kwababini umntu omnye omtsha kuye, enjenjalo esenza uxolo: nokuxolelanisa bobabini kuThixo emzimbeni omnye ngomnqamlezo wakhe, wabulala intiyo ngaloo ndlela. Ndive, o wena mntu owalileyo ubizo lwam: Ndikubiza ngoku kule mizuzwana yokugqibela, kwaye ixesha seliphelile! Wamkele njengomnikelo wam woxolo, kuba ndikunika ngokukhululekileyo usindiso nentlawulelo kwimizimba yenu eyonakeleyo, kwanobomi obungunaphakade kum, Krestu Yesu. Ungaphazami: Sonke isipho esilungileyo, naso sonke isipho esigqibeleleyo sivela phezulu, size sehle sivela kuYise wezikhanyiso, ongenaguquguquko, nasithunzi sakujika.

Yiva ilizwi lam uqonde ukuba nantoni na elungileyo ebomini bakho, nantoni na exabisekileyo nenesidima, ivela kuBawo kwaye inikwe nguYise wezikhanyiso ukuze ube nokuzala kobomi kwaye uze kum, iNkosi uYesu, njengomsindisi wakho. Kwaye ukuze wazi ukuba kukho ubomi obungunaphakade kuwe ukuba uyayamkela. Ngaba unithiye kangangokuba nizimisele ukuncama yonke into eniyithandayo ebomini eniyinikwe nguBawo ukuze nanelise iinkanuko zenu zokuzicingela? Ngaba izono zenu zibaluleke kangangokuba niya kukhetha ukugwetywa ngonaphakade noSathana kunokulunga kukaThixo nobomi obutsha kum, Krestu Yesu? Ngaba zibaluleke kangako izono zenu?

Ndikubiza, o wena mntu wemvelo, kwaye ndikumemela eluxolweni nakubomi obungunaphakade, ubomi obukhululekileyo ekuguleni nasesonweni, kwanobomi obukhululekileyo kwizifo nakwintlungu yentliziyo, ubomi obukhululekileyo ekubandezelekeni apho ungeke usababukela abo ubathandayo bebandezeleka; kuba izinto zangaphambili ziya kudlula kungekudala. Akusekho xesha lokwanelisa izono zenu. Kuba abaninzi benu baluthathe lula ubabalo lukaThixo bakholwa bubuxoki besela, elifuna ukunibela ubomi benu ukuze nihlanganyele nalo kwisohlwayo salo esingunaphakade. Ungaphazami. Ukundala mna, Yesu waseNazarete (kwanalowo wazinikela ngenxa yezono zenu nosindiso lwenu), kukwala ubomi nokukhetha ngonaphakade ukufa kwesibini apho uSathana neengelosi zakhe bagwetyelwe khona: Kuba abanaxolelo, kwaye abayi kuphumla, kwaye abanalo uxolo. Kwaye idike lomlilo lilungiselelwe uMtyholi neengelosi zakhe.

Ukwala idini lam kukwala ubomi obungunaphakade nokwala uThixo ngokupheleleyo. Ngoko ke, akuyi kubakho nceba, babalo, thando, kukhanya, naxolo kodwa kuphela ukuzisola, ubumnyama, nentuthumbo apho uya kuvuna isiqhamo esibolileyo senyama yakho ngonaphakade. Awuboni na ukuba bubudenge obungakanani obu? Ndizihlawulele izono zenu ngegazi lam lentlawulelo! Kodwa niyazala iindlela zam, nangona ndinibiza ngoku kwaye kwabaninzi okokugqibela. Niyalwala uthando lwam, nangona intliziyo yam isaphukile ngenxa yenu neentsapho zenu, kwaye ndinganisindisa ngokupheleleyo ukuba nithe naguquka naziyeka izono zenu. Aniqondi na ukuba ndabandezeleka njengomoni ophantsi kwidini lam lentlawulelo ukuze ndiniphakamisele kuThixo elusindisweni? Kuba ndaphakanyiswa emnqamlezweni ukuze ndisindise imiphefumlo yenu ndinixolelanise noBawo, kwaye bendisoloko ndingenasono, ngoko lamkeleni igazi lam elingenasono!

Kuba sithini isiBhalo? Ngokuqinisekileyo nivile ukuba wenjenje uThixo ukulithanda kwakhe ihlabathi, ude wancama uNyana wakhe okuphela kwamzeleyo: ukuze bonke abakholwayo kuye, bangatshabalali, koko babe nobomi obungunaphakade. Ndiyazi ukuba la mazwi aqhelekile kuni, kuba kwanabantu benu bokuzonwabisa abanigcina ebukhobokeni bukaSathana baye bawasebenzisa kakubi amazwi am ngokunyelisa. Kodwa kuya kuba kuhle ukuba niqhubeke nifunda, ngoko ke ndicela nive ilizwi lam! Kuba uThixo akamthumanga uNyana wakhe ehlabathini ukuze aligwebe ihlabathi: kodwa ukuze ihlabathi ngaye lisindiswe. Lowo ukholwayo kuye, akagwetywa: kodwa lowo ungakholwayo, usele egwetyiwe, kuba akakholwanga eGameni loNyana okuphela kwamzeleyo kaThixo.

Ayicacanga ngokwaneleyo na intetho yam? Akulula na ukuqondwa kwamazwi am? Ukuba niyandala mna, iNkosi uYesu, iMvana kaThixo, niya kuhlangana ngokusisigxina notshaba lwam, kwanabo banoxanduva lokuphalaza igazi elimsulwa: Kuba ukundala mna kukuhlangana nemvukelo kaSathana, kuba uMtyholi wonile kwasekuqaleni. Kwaye oku kukugwetywa, ukuba ukukhanya kufikile ehlabathini, kwaye abantu bathanda ubumnyama kunokukhanya, kuba izenzo zabo zazimbi. Kwaye injalo inyaniso ngani, o wena mntu wemvelo, kwanani nina eningawala umoya wam wobomi, kwaye kuko konke oku ndisanibiza, nangona olu lubizo lokugqibela ngaphambi kokuba umgwebo uqale. Kuba akusekho xesha. Ngaba izono zenu zibaluleke kangangokuba ningachitha unaphakade ebumnyameni obungaphandle nakwisohlwayo kwelo dike likhulu lomlilo elilungiselelwe uMtyholi neengelosi zakhe? Kuba uThixo akasinqweneleli esi sohlwayo kwaye undithumele njengomnikelo wakhe woxolo ukuze anixolelanise naye ngegazi lam lentlawulelo.

Yivani amazwi am namkele uxolo lwam. Kubo bonke abawavayo la mazwi, yazini ukuba mna, Yesu waseNazarete, ndiyanibiza; kwaye andikhathali nokuba ziziphi izono ezinibophayo kuba ndizihlawulele zonke. Qondani ukuba ukuthatha uphawu lwerhamncwa akuyi kuxolelwa, kwaye ndizama ukunisindisa kwesi sihendo (nangona uphawu alukabikho okwangoku); kuba abaninzi baya kulahlekelwa bubomi babo kwintuthumbo ngenxa yokwala. Kodwa qondani oku kulandelayo: Aninakuhla kakhulu kangangokuba ndingakwazi ukunifikelela; kuphela ningaluthathi uphawu. Kwanabakhukhumeleyo abanakuziphakamisa esonweni kangangokuba ndingakwazi ukubehlisela elusindisweni ngobunono ndibatywine ngomoya wam ukuze ndibaphakamise! Kuba ndizihlawulele zonke izono zoluntu lonke ixesha, kwaye andishiyanga nto ingenziwanga. Zininzi izono, kodwa ndiza kubiza abanye benu ngokukodwa ngezono zenu. Yazini ukuba ndiya kuzixolela zonke izono zenu hayi ezi kuphela, kwaye ndiya kunihlambulula ngokupheleleyo egazini lam kuzo zonke izono zenu.

Kumnxila nakwilikhoboka leziyobisi, ndithi: Jikani nize kum ngoku kumzuzu ongunaphakade wokuqonda kwaye nive amazwi am, kuba ukuba nithe namamela ngokholo, amazwi am aya kunizisela ukukhanya okuzukileyo kosindiso. Amazwi am aya kubopha iidemon ezenza nisele kwaye nisebenzise iziyobisi, nangona ningazenza abahlobo benu. Ndikunika ukuqonda okumangalisayo ukuze ukwazi ukugqiba ukuba yeyiphi oya kuba nayo ngonaphakade: Ubomi obungunaphakade nam, Yesu Krestu, kumanyano olugqibeleleyo noThixo ngokwakhe? Okanye ukugwetywa ngonaphakade emlilweni ongacimiyo? Ngaba isono enisibambe ngobugovu esandleni senu, kwanobunxila benu nokukhotyokiswa ziziyobisi, sibaluleke kangangokuba ningakhetha ukutshabalala, kwanokwenza isiphelo senu kwabo nibathandayo abanilandela endaweni yam?

Aniphantsi kangangokuba ndingakwazi ukuniphakamisa, kwaye ndinqwenela kakhulu ukunixolela, kodwa kufuneka nisabele kwilizwi lam kwaye niphume ezindleleni zenu niguquke. Phalazani utywala benu niyeke ukuzityhefa, nize kum niguquke, kwaye ndiya kunihlangula. Akukho xesha lokuchitha; ningandihoyi kwaye ningenelele ekunxileni niwalibale amazwi am, kuba amazwi enu okwala aya kubhalwa emazulwini njengobungqina obuchasene nani ngomhla womgwebo, kwaye aniyi kusinda!

Kumbulali, ndithi: Yekani ngoko nangoko ubundlobongela benu bokubulala abo benziwe ngokomfanekiso kaThixo, kwanabantu bakuni, kuba esi sisono esibi. Inene, kwaye ngokwenene ngenyaniso engenakuguqulwa, nizibulele nina ngabo bonke ubomi enibuthathileyo, kwaye ngaphandle kwam akukho nyango kwisohlwayo senu esizayo. Kodwa kwanokubulala kwenu kwahlawulwa ngokupheleleyo kwidini lam, kuba andishiyanga nto ingenziwanga. Ndibonakalise ubabalo lwam ebomini boMpostile omkhulu uPawulos njengobungqina bokukhuthaza, o nina babulali.

Kuba noSawule wakhohliswa kwaye wayesinga kumgwebo omkhulu endleleni eya eDamasko, apho ndamsindisa kuye. UPawulos watshutshisa, wenzakalisa, kwaye wavuma waza wabulala iCawa yam engcwele! Kuba uyavuma kwiZenzo: Kwaye ndayitshutshisa le ndlela kwada kwasekufeni, ndibopha ndaza ndanikezela entolongweni amadoda nabafazi. Kodwa wasamkela isipho sam sobomi obungunaphakade waza wandenza, mna Yesu Krestu, iNkosi yakhe waza wandikhonza ngokuthembekileyo njengomzekelo wenguquko kubo bonke, kwaye ndamphakamisa kakhulu ngenxa yokuthobela kwakhe. Ndizihlawulele iindlela zenu zokubulala ukuba nithe naguquka kuzo nize kum ngenguquko yokwenene, kuba ukuba nithe nenjenjalo ngoku, ndiya kunamkela ngobabalo nenceba. Ningakhohliswa; akukho xesha lokuchitha.

Kowona uphantsi kakhulu, ndithi: Ndikwenzele indlela yokuphuma ekuphambaneni nasekubhidekeni kwakho, kuba ndikunikele kwiinkanuko zakho ezimbi njengoko kwabaseRoma kutsho: Ngenxa yesi sizathu uThixo wabanikela kwiinkanuko ezimbi: kuba kwanabafazi babo batshintsha ukusetyenziswa kwemvelo koko kungokuchasene nemvelo: Kwaye ngokunjalo namadoda, eshiya ukusetyenziswa kwemvelo komfazi, atsha kwiinkanuko zawo omnye komnye, amadoda namadoda esebenza oko kungafanelekanga, kwaye amkela kuwo umvuzo wesiphoso sawo obufanelekileyo.

Phumani kubumdaka benu, niphume kwikratshi lenu elingenaxabiso, niyeke ukunyelisa umqondiso owanikwa uNowa naso sonke isidalwa esiphilayo, umnyama obonakala emafini emvula. Umnyama wenzelwe ukuba ihlabathi lazi ukuba uThixo soze aphinde alitshabalalise ihlabathi ngamanzi, nangona umgwebo ngomlilo usondele. Yizani kum niguquke, nitshintshe iindlela zenu nibize egameni lam, nivume ukuba ndiyiNkosi ngentliziyo ezaliswe lukholo ekuvukeni kwam, ndaye ndiya kunamkela. Khawulezani! Kuba aninalo ixesha lokucamanga, kuba umgwebo sele uza kukhululwa. Akukho xesha lokuchitha.

Yivani amazwi am nize kum, kuba andiyoliswa ngumgwebo nentshabalaliso ezayo, kwaye noYahweh akayoliswa, hayi, noThixo uSomandla! Kuba uAdam wadalwa eluthandweni lukaBawo nasekugqibeleleni kobulungisa nobungcwele bokwenene. UThixo wamnika ubomi obugqibeleleyo obungenanto isilelayo. Waza ke uSathana wahlasela indalo wayonakalisa kanye inyama yenu nendalo yenu ukuze nilahleke kwasesizalweni, kuba isono sisegazini lenu: Ubomi benyama busegazini. Kanti uThixo, ngenceba yakhe enkulu, kuba inceba yakhe ihleli ngonaphakade, noThixo uSomandla wanikela mna, unyana wakhe okuphela kwamzeleyo, njengedini eligqibeleleyo eliya kunisindisa ngokupheleleyo ukuba nithe neza kum niguquka ezonweni zenu. Ndiya kuyitshitshisa imisebenzi kaSathana ebomini benu ukuba nithe neza kum. Ncedani nize, kuba iingalo zam azisayi kolulwa ixesha elide!

Kusele ixesha elincinane kakhulu, yaye imbandezelo kaYakobi sele iza kuqhambuka emhlabeni wonke, neentliziyo zabantu ziya kubatyhafa ngenxa yobunzima bezinto ezizayo. USathana ulungiselele amasikizi amaninzi aza kuwakhulula, kuba ukwala ubizo lwam kukukhetha ubukumkani bukaSathana bokufa nentshabalaliso; yaye ndiya kuniyekela kukhetho lwenu, ndininike ubumnyama nosizi eniluthanda kangaka, yaye niya kudla isiqhamo seendlela zenu ngaphandle kokuba niguquke. Kuba yiyo le, lolu lubizo lokugqibela phambi kokuba umgwebo ufike ngokukhawuleza. Kwanabakhonzi bam nabaphathiswa bam bayakhala kwiincam zehlabathi kanye ngalo mzuzwana, ngaphandle kokuba baya kukhalelela usindiso lwenu de baxwilelwe kum emafini xa ndiza kulanda umtshakazi wam emoyeni kungekudala, yaye oku kuya kwenzeka ngokungalindelekanga, nangona ndinixelela ngokucacileyo phambi kokuba kwenzeke. Luze ke luphele ubizo lwam lokugqibela lobabalo nenceba. Yaye xa ndixwila umtshakazi wam ndimsa endaweni ekhuselekileyo, intshabalaliso yesiquphe iya kuqhambuka emhlabeni wonke, yaye abaninzi benu abawavayo la mazwi abayi kusinda. Abanye benu ngobuyatha bacinga ukuba bangaqhubeka nokuxhaphaza ubabalo lwam luze luqhubeke ngonaphakade, kodwa inyaniso ebuhlungu yeyokuba ubabalo luya kujika lube ngumgwebo, yaye kuya kwenzeka ngequbuliso, yaye abaninzi abayi kusinda.

Kuba abo bangabulawanga yile ntshabalaliso yesiquphe eza kukhululwa kungekudala kwiBhabhiloni eyimfihlakalo baya kuzifumana kwihlabathi abangazange baliphupha, ihlabathi apho kukho ukufa nentshabalaliso rhoqo, kuba uSathana uya kuyicoca ngokucwangcisiweyo nantoni na enento yokwenza nam, uYesu Krestu, yaye uya kukwenza oko ngaphandle kwenceba. Kuya kubakho intuthumbo kulo lonke ilizwe nenkohlakalo engenangqondo, yaye ukuza kum ngeli xesha lomgwebo novavanyo kuya kuba nzima kakhulu; kodwa nabani na obiza egameni leNkosi uya kusindiswa, yaye kuya kufuneka anyamezele kude kube sekupheleni, yaye abaninzi baya kunqunyulwa iintloko, yaye abaninzi baya kufa kwintuthumbo enkulu.

Kodwa naxa kunjalo, uThixo uya kuzosula zonke iinyembezi emehlweni abo, kuba uYohane uMtyhileli waxela ngabo esithi: Aba ngabo baphuma embandezelweni enkulu, bazihlamba iingubo zabo, bazenza mhlophe egazini leMvana. Ngenxa yoko, baphambi kwetrone kaThixo, bamkhonza imini nobusuku eTempileni yakhe: nalowo uhleli eTroneni uya kuhlala phakathi kwabo. Abasayi kuphinda balambe, abasayi kuphinda banxanwe, neLanga alisayi kubatshisa, nabuphi na ubushushu. Kuba iMvana, ephakathi kwetrone, iya kubondla, ibakhokelele emthonjeni ophilayo wamanzi: yaye uThixo uya kuzosula zonke iinyembezi emehlweni abo.

Mna, ewe mna, uKrestu Yesu, iNkosi evusiweyo yanyuswa, uMesiya wokwenene: Ndiyanibiza, yaye ithemba lam nolindelo lwam kukuba niya kwamkela umnikelo wam woxolo nize kusindiso lwam ukuze niphile ngonaphakade nam esifubeni sikaBawo! Kodwa ningalwali ubizo lwam, kuba andisayi kubiza ixesha elide, yaye andisayi kubiza ngobabalo nenceba ngale ndlela kwakhona, kuba ixesha liphelile (nangona ubabalo nenceba zihleli nam ngonaphakade). Ncedani nize kum ukuze ndinisindise kule mizuzwana yokugqibela yobabalo. Kuba anahlukanga kwabo bakholwayo ekuqaleni kwexesha lam lobabalo, yaye usindiso lwam nomvuzo wam uya kuba ngowenu ukuba niyalithobela ilizwi lam ngoku. Ngoku lixesha losindiso! Guqukani nize kum ngokuthobeka. Ndenzeni iNkosi yenu nazi ukuba ndiphila ngonaphakade kanaphakade, kuba uThixo wandivusa kwabafileyo ukuze ndingaphindi ndife. Ukuba nithe nasamkela isicelo sam, niya kuba noyisile, yaye niya kufumana imivuzo yomeyisi:

Yabona, ndimi emnyango, ndiyankqonkqoza: ukuba kukho umntu oliphulaphulayo ilizwi lam, aze avule umnyango, ndiya kungena kuye, ndidle naye, naye adle nam. Lowo woyisayo, ndiya kumnika ukuba ahlale nam etroneni yam, njengokuba nam ndoyisayo, ndahlala noBawo etroneni yakhe. Funda oku ngokuphindaphindiweyo, kuba ndikunika ithuba lokugqibela losindiso ngobabalo. Kodwa ungalwali ubizo lwam, kuba akusekho xesha lezinto ezinjalo. Kwabaninzi benu, ukundala kuya kuba kokokugqibela nokusisigxina, yaye aniyi kuchacha okanye nisinde kumgwebo ozayo, kuba intshabalaliso yesiquphe iphezu kwenu. NoMoya noMtshakazi bathi, Yiza. Nalowo uvayo makathi, Yiza. Nalowo unxaniweyo makeze. Naye nabani na othandayo, makawathabathe amanzi obomi ngesisa.

Kwaye okokugqibela: Lowo ungenabulungisa, makahlale engenabulungisa: nalowo ungcolileyo, makahlale engcolile: nalowo ulilungisa, makahlale elilungisa: nalowo ungcwele, makahlale engcwele. Yabona, ndiyeza kamsinya, nomvuzo wam undiphethe, ukuze ndinike umntu ngamnye ngokomsebenzi wakhe. Ngoko ke makwenzeke.
