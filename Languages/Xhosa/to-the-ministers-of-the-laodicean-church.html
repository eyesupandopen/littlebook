Meyi 06, 2024

Kubalungiseleli beBandla laseLawodike: Yivani Amazwi Am!

Mtshakazi wam nebandla lam, ndivéni eluthandweni lwam ngani, bantwana bam, nithobe iindlebe zenu kwintetho yam. Yivani Amazwi am, owu balungiseleli bam baseLawodike, kwanani endininike iziphiwo. Kuba ndinike abantu iziphiwo, ndanika abanye baba ngabapostile, nabanye baba ngabaprofeti, nabanye baba ngabavangeli, nabanye baba ngabefundisi, nabanye baba ngabafundisi-ntsapho. Ndininike ezi ziphiwo ukuze kugqibeleliswe abangcwele, ukuze kwenziwe umsebenzi wobulungiseleli, ukuze kwakhiwe umzimba kaKristu. Kuba ndathini kubapostile nabafundi bam kwimibhalo yeendaba ezilungileyo zikaYohane: Yabonani, ndiya kuthumela isithembiso sikaBawo phezu kwenu. Kodwa hlalani kwisixeko saseYerusalem, nide nambathiswe amandla avela phezulu.

NakwiZenzo zabaPostile bam kubhaliwe ukuzaliseka kwesi sithembiso: Kwathi xa wawuzalisekile umhla wePentekoste, babendawonye bonke ngamxhelo mnye. Kwaza ngequbuliso kwavela isandi esivela ezulwini, ngathi sesomoya ovuthuzayo onamandla, sayizalisa yonke indlu ababehleli kuyo. Kwabonakala kubo iilwimi ezahlukeneyo, ezinjengomlilo, zahlala phezu komntu ngamnye kubo. Bazaliswa bonke nguMoya oyiNgcwele, baqala ukuthetha ngezinye iilwimi, njengoko uMoya wayebanika ukuba bathethe. Kwaye ukusekwa kweBandla lam kubhalwe ngolu hlobo esibhalweni ukuze nazi ukuzaliseka kwesi sithembiso sikhulu seendaba ezilungileyo, sokuba ndinizele amandla avela phezulu, nakuzalweni olutsha, nakum, uKristu Yesu iNkosi yenu. Kwaye mna ndiyintloko yomzimba, iBandla: ndingumqali, owamazibulo kwabafileyo, ukuze ezintweni zonke ndibe ngowokuqala: Kuba kwamkholisa uBawo ukuba kum kuhlale konke ukuzala.

Balungiseleli bam baseLawodike, ukuzala kwam akuhlali emabandleni enu enziwe ngemifanekiso yenu, kuba ndimi emnyango ndiyankqonkqoza! Balungiseleli bam baseLawodike, andizange ndibawomelele ngokuzicingela konke ukuzala kukaBawo okukum, kodwa ndawuphalaza umoya wam ndanenza nanamandla, kwaye ndanitywina ngaloo moya ungcwele wesithembiso. Akukho nto inokuliqhawula elo tywina, nangona ninokuhamba niye eluwexukweni ukuba nikhetha njalo. Kodwa itywina likaBawo, uYahweh uThixo ongunaphakade, alinakuqhawulwa; kuba akunakwenzeka ukuliqhawula itywina likaThixo, kwaye niqinisekile kum ukuba nithembekile kum, kuba izithembiso zam zingu-ewe no-amen!

Balungiseleli bam baseLawodike, iziphiwo zam kuni azihoywa ngenxa yeendlela zenu zokudikidiki, kwaye niwucimile umoya endininike wona, kwaye niyala ukuvula iziphiwo endininike zona, kwaye niyeka iziphiwo zam zihlale zingavulwanga ngokungakhathali nangokungahoyi. Lo asingomcimbi mncinci wokuba uthathwe lula, kuba sisono esinzulu kwingxelo yenu, kwaye anithobeli ilizwi lam. Aniwahloniphi amazwi am, kwaye anizihloniphi okanye anizifundisi iindlela zam. Kuba ndenza uPawulos wabhalela ntoni inkokeli eselula yeBandla lam elalisandul' ukutyalwa, uTimoti: Musa ukusidelela isiphiwo esikuwe, owasinikwayo ngesiprofeto, nangokubekwa kwezandla zomdala. Ziqhelise ezi zinto, uzinikele kuzo, ukuze kubonakale indlela oxhamla ngayo ezintweni zonke.

Owu, ukuba beniwafundile amazwi am ngokuqonda! Owu, ukuba beniwakhumbula amazwi am ngokholo, kodwa aniqondi kwaye aninalukholo! Owu, ukuba beniwathobela amazwi am, kuba ukuthobela mna, iNkosi uYesu Kristu, kukuthobela umthombo wobomi, kwaye esiswini senu kuya kuphuma imilambo yamanzi aphilayo, kuba ndininikile umoya wam. Kodwa endaweni yokufunda amazwi am, nijonga izikrini zenu ngokungathobeli okungenangqondo; nisuka kwisikrini niye kwisikrini, nizonwabisa ngesono! Kwaye ninobuyatha obungako bokucinga ukuba ukungathobeli okungenangqondo kukholo olusebenzayo kum, kwaye nikhohlisiwe. Nizityeshele iziphiwo endininike zona, kwanomntu omtsha, kwaye endaweni yoko nichithe ixesha niqhelisa imvelo yenu yomntu omdala ngelize njengoko nifuna ukwanelisa inyama yenu.

Endaweni yokuziqhelisa kwimicimbi yokomoya, nina ngamampunge niqhelisa imizimba yenu eyonakeleyo netshabalalayo, kuba ukuzilolonga komzimba kunceda ixesha elifutshane kuphela, kwaye imizimba yenu iyatshabalala. Kuba ilizwi lam liyavakala kuyo yonke imbali ukusukela kwileta kaPawulos eya kwabase-Efese: Elutshitshisile elunyameni lwakhe ubutshaba, wona umthetho wemithetho equlethwe kwimimiselo, ukuze enze kuye, kwababini, umntu omtsha omnye, enjenjalo esenza uxolo. Ndivéni, bantwana bam, kuba ndiyanithanda kwaye ndiyikhathalele imiphefumlo yenu, kwanani maLawodike am adikidiki. Kuba nikhetha ukuhamba kumntu wenu omdala neendlela zakhe, kwanenyama yenu eyonakeleyo, kuba niyibonisa yonke into eniyithandayo inyama yenu. Endaweni yoko, kufuneka nigxininise iingqondo neentliziyo zenu kum nakwilizwi lam ngalinye: Kuba umntu akayi kuphila ngasonka sodwa, kodwa ngamazwi onke avela emlonyeni kaThixo. Kuba ngokuthobela amazwi am ngobuso obutyhiliweyo (kwanelizwi likaThixo, kuba ndiliLizwi elenziwe inyama kwaye ndihlala phakathi kwenu nakuni), nihamba emoyeni hayi enyameni.

Kuba ndalixelela iBandla laseKorinte oku kulandelayo: Kodwa thina sonke, ngobuso obutyhiliweyo sibuka njengasesipilini ubuqaqawuli beNkosi, siguqulwa sibe mfanekiso mnye, sisuka ebuqaqawulini siye ebuqaqawulini, kwananjengokomoya weNkosi. Bantwana bam, iincwadi zam eziya kwiBandla lam (kwanazo iileta zothando endazithumela kuMtshakazi wam) sisipili sobuqu benu bokwenene, kuba ubuqu benu bokwenene bufihliwe kwimfihlelo enkulu kaThixo. Ngokuthobela amazwi nemiyalelo yam niya kuhamba emoyeni, kwaye aniyi kwanelisa inyama yenu eyonakeleyo. Sonke isibhalo kufuneka sihlonitshwe kwaye sifundwe, kodwa nina niyimfihlelo eyayifihliwe kuThixo kwaye ngoku ityhiliwe, kwaye kufuneka nifunde oko ndakutyhilayo kuPawulos ukuze niqonde indalo entsha endinenza yona kum, uKristu Yesu.

Balungiseleli bam baseLawodike, kukho isiphoso esipheleleyo phakathi kwenu. Niyilahle yonke intlonipho ngamazwi am, kwaye nizityeshela iziphiwo endininike zona kangangokuba anisenakuphilisa kodwa ninikela ibandla lam kwi-pharmakeia nakwisitshetshe; kuba ukuba benindithobela, bendingavumela imimangaliso yenziwe ngezandla zenu. Kodwa nihlonipha amazwi abelusi abawexukileyo ngaphezu kwamazwi kaYeshua HaMashiach, kwaye ibandla lam laseLawodike lihlawula ixabiso lokungathobeli kwenu; kuba anisenabakhathalelanga, kodwa endaweni yoko nibayeka batyhafe esonweni esingenamandla. Abelusi abawexukileyo bachongwa ngamazwi endawanika uYohane kwincwadi yakhe yokuqala: Bantwana abancinane, lixesha lokugqibela: kwaye njengoko nivile ukuba uMchasi-Kristu uza kuza, kwanangoku baninzi aBachasi-Kristu, esazi ngako ukuba lixesha lokugqibela. Baphuma kuthi, kodwa babengengobethu: kuba ukuba babengobethu, ngokuqinisekileyo ngebehlale nathi: kodwa baphuma ukuze babonakaliswe, ukuba babengengobethu sonke.

Aniqondi na ukuba abo balawulwa yimimoya yomchasi-kristu babonisa omnye uYesu endaweni yomalusi wokwenene, mna, uKristu Yesu? Kuba ezi ziintshaba zomnqamlezo kaKristu, kwaye zichasene nam, kwaye zizibeka endaweni yam, zifuna ukuthatha igunya lam, nto leyo engenakwenzeka. Aba bangene emabandleni enu, kwiisemina zenu, kumaziko enu ahlukeneyo, kwaye balawula amabandla enu ngokuvuma kwenu ukusebenza phantsi kwe-501(c)(3), kuba bonke abaphantsi kwesivumelwano esinjalo bavalwe imilomo ngoorhulumente behlabathi. Ii-501(c)(3) zenu zibonisa ukubawa kwenu inzuzo emdaka, kuba nizithengisile phantsi kweendlela zikaBhileham, kwaye ninika amazwi obuxoki ngemali ukuze nizigcinele yonke imali abantwana bam abancinane abaninike yona ekukrexeni kwenu okungahlawulelwa rhafu! Nibe ngumhlobo wehlabathi, kwaye ihlabathi liza kuthatha nantoni na enxulunyaniswe negama lam xa umthinteli esusiwe. Aninangqiqo yokomoya kwaye niyimfama! Aninankathalo yokukhusela umhlambi kwezi ngcuka zibawayo, kuba nixabisa izibizo ezinxulunyaniswe namagama enu, izibizo endinganinakanga; kodwa niyazinqwenela. Kuba ndinixelele ukuba nizinqwenele ngokunyanisekileyo ezona ziphiwo zibalaseleyo: Kwaye ndisanibonisa indlela egqwesileyo: indlela egqwesileyo eniyilayo kwiindlela zenu zokonqena, zokudikidiki.

Anisashumayeli isono nenguquko esonweni, kodwa nishumayela iintsomi ezivakala zizonwabile ezingekho kum, kwaye iintsomi zenu zenziwe ngamazwi obuxoki kwaye zikhokelela kwiindlela zobuxoki; kuba aninto ingaphaya kombalisi webali okhathaza iindlebe zabavilaphayo. Ndithetha nani, bapostile bam: kuba amehlo enu amnyama, kwaye anisaluboni ukhanyiso lwam, kwaye nivakalisa ubuxoki! Ndithetha nani, baprofeti bam: kuba nithethela omnye uYesu, kwaye niyawatyeshela amazwi am, kwaye anindazi, kwaye niprofeta ngekamva lobuxoki! Ndithetha nani, bavangeli bam: Kuba nivangela iintlanga iimfundiso zobuxoki zeedemon, kwaye nibenza basele amanzi amdaka, kuba nibazalisa ngemimoya engcolileyo! Ndithetha nani, bafundisi bam: Kuba nikhathalele iinkanuko zenu, kwaye nenze isisu senu sibe nguthixo wenu ngenkunkuma yenu yefilosofi yokuzinceda; siyathandini, aninakuphilisa iigusha zam ngolwimi oluxokayo! Ndithetha nani, bafundisi-ntsapho bam: Kuba anisenawafundisi amazwi acocekileyo evangeli yam, kodwa nifundisa amazwi okuyekelela kobu-ecumenical, kwaye kukho isiphoso esipheleleyo phakathi kwenu nonke, balungiseleli bam baseLawodike. Nonke ninesifo esinye esifanayo: Anisenayihloniphi imiyalelo eseLizwini lam, kwaye anisenawahloniphi amagama ngamanye akwisibhalo sam.

Nicinga ngokuzidla ukuba ningabaguquleli kuba ninokukhetha intsingiselo kwiileksikhoni zehlabathi nakwiikhonkhodensi zehlabathi ukuze zihambelane neenjongo zenu: Iincwadi endingazibhalanga, kuba ndibhala isibhalo ngabaprofeti bam, kuba umoya wesiprofeto bubungqina bukaYesu! Abaguquleli abathetha ulwimi olunye kuphela, kwaye nibe yintlekisa! Endaweni yoko, nihlonipha amazwi abelusi abawexukileyo, kwanabakhohlisi bomdiza abangaxabisekanga. Bangena emabandleni enu ukuze benze ngathi ningabantu (kuba nina niyingqolowa endiya kuyibuthela esiphelweni sam ukuba nisabuyela kum). Kodwa endaweni yoko bangumdiza, abazalwana bobuxoki ababa ngabalungiseleli bobuxoki abanilahlekisayo. Sisityalo somkhohlisi nesinetyhefu bona!

Aba bakhohlakeleyo abasaphumi phakathi kweBandla lam ukuze babonakaliswe, kodwa niyabamema ningene kwaye nibanyanzele ukuba batye nani kwaye nihloniphe amazwi abo obuxoki neelwimi ezixokayo ngaphezu kwamazwi endiya kuninika wona, kwanamazwi oThixo ophilayo, kwanamazwi kaYahweh! Niyathengisa ngamazwi am ukuze nibe nalo lonke uzuko nodumo olulolwam, iNkosi yenu uYesu Kristu. Inene niwufumene umvuzo wenu, kwaye ndiya kuwuhluba umvuzo wenu ndiwunike umntu owayethembekile endaweni yenu. Kuba anisenakundithethela, kwaye kufuneka ndize kwiBandla lam njengelizwi lomntu okhalayo entlango, kuba nivalile umnyango ebusweni bam, nangona ndisemi ndinkqonkqoza! Kukhona ihlazo elipheleleyo nengcikivo phezu kwenu ngaphandle kokuba niguquke!

Ukuba ningawatyeshela amazwi am kwaye ningaliva ilizwi lam, ukuba niyaqhubeka kwiindlela zenu zokudikidiki, ndiya kunishiya kwinkathazo kaYakobi; kuba ndatsho ngaphambili kuni, maLawodike am, ndiya kunikhafula emlonyeni wam ngaphandle kokuba niguquke! Kwaye kwakhona ndatsho malunga naloo mprofetikazi wobuxoki uIzebhele xa ndandibhalela iTiyatira, kuba ndathini kuYohane uMtyhili?

Noko ke, ndinezinto ezimbalwa ezichasene nawe, kuba uyamnyamezela loo mfazi uIzebhele, ozibiza ngokuba ngumprofetikazi, ukuba afundise kwaye alukuhle izicaka zam ukuba zikrexeze, kwaye zitye izinto ezibingelelwe kwizithixo. Kwaye ndamnika ithuba lokuguquka ekukrexeni kwakhe, kwaye akazange aguquke. Yabona, ndiya kumphosa ebhedini, nabo bakrexeza naye, kwinkathazo enkulu, ngaphandle kokuba baguquke kwizenzo zabo.

Aniwafundanga na la mazwi? Ninobukhulu obungako bokucinga ukuba uYahweh okanye uYeshua HaMashiach bangabakhethi buso babantu? Nicinga ukuba ndiyazihlonipha izibizo enizinika zona ukuze nizixhome phezu kweentloko zabantu bam, njengoko nizenza iinkosi phezu kwelifa likaThixo?

Mfundisi lo, Bhishophu laa; ukuba beningazityeshelanga iziphiwo zam, isiphiwo senu nge sinenzele indawo phambi kwabantu abakhulu kwaye beningazidingi izibizo zenu ezingaxabisekanga, kuba bendinganihlonipha kwaye beningandihlonipha, kodwa anindihloniphi! Kukho isiphoso esipheleleyo phakathi kwenu, kwaye umsindo wam uvutha phezu kwenu, kuba niyisilele imiyalelo endininike yona kwaye endaweni yoko nibe ngabawexuki, kwanokukhokelela abantwana bam abancinane emseleni apho nimise iintente zenu. Nikhetha amanzi angcolileyo emseleni wenu ngaphezu kwamanzi am acocekileyo omoya oyingcwele apho bonke abaselayo abasayi kuphinda banxanwe!

Nindinika amanzi angcolileyo, amdaka, kubantwana bam abancinane ngeemeko eziqhekekileyo, kwaye ingqumbo yam iphezu kwenu! Ndiza kunihlutha umvuzo wenu ndiwunike omnye, kuba njengoko nditshilo ngani: Inene, niwufumene umvuzo wenu. Niwufumene umvuzo wenu wezihlalo zenu enizikhethele zona zokuzibeka phezulu, kwaye niwufumene umvuzo wenu wezikhundla ezingenamsebenzi, kwaye niwufumene umvuzo wenu wenzuzo emdaka, kwaye niya kufumana umvuzo wengqumbo yam ngaphandle kokuba niguquke nibuyele kum ngokuthobeka. Kuba umvuzo wengqumbo yam yingqumbo, kwaye xa nindizisela iinkuni zenu, ifula, neendiza, ziya kutsha zibe luthuthu phambi kwabo bonke, kwaye niya kufunyanwa ningabalungiseleli bobuxoki!

Kukho isiphoso esikhulu phakathi kwenu nonke, kwanani, baphostile bam, baprofeti, bavangeli, bafundisi, neetitshala! Anisazibambi kubudlelwane obusindisayo nam, uYesu Krestu, kodwa nindiyekile; kuba mna ndiyintloko yomzimba, kwaye nifanele nindibambe ngaphezu kwayo yonke enye into! ANINAKUNDILUNGISELELA ukuba anilazi nelizwi lam. Nizizithulu, kwaye ninomonakalo osisigxina ezindlebeni ngaphandle kokuba niguquke, kuba ndiza kuphilisa iindlebe zenu koomoya bobuthongo ukuze nilive ilizwi lam, kuba ndiyathetha nani! Ngaphandle kokuba nindibambe njengentloko yenu kwaye niwahlonele amazwi am, ndiza kunishiya niziimfama nasemhadini kunye nabo banikhokeleyo. Niziimfama, kwaye ninomonakalo osisigxina emehlweni ngaphandle kokuba niguquke, kuba bendiya kuphilisa amehlo enu koomoya bobuthongo ukuze nifunde amazwi am nibubone ubuso bam: kodwa kusekho ukulala okuncinane, kusekho ukusonga izandla okuncinane.

Nibe yintlekisa nehlazo, kuba iindlela zenu zokudikidiki zilihlambela igama lam elingcwele. Anigcini nje ngokungandibambi mna, Krestu Yesu, njengentloko yenu (kuba ukuba benisenza njalo, beniya kukwazi ukuthetha endaweni yam), kodwa niqhubela phambili eluwexukweni ngokuhlambela amazwi am angcwele, kuba mna ndiliLizwi elenziwa inyama! Ndiyanibuza, anizifundanga na izilumkiso zam? Kuba ndiyangqina kuye wonke umntu ovayo amazwi esiprofeto sale ncwadi, Ukuba nabani na uthe wongeza kwezi zinto, uThixo uya kongeza kuye izibetho ezibhaliweyo kule ncwadi. Kwaye ukuba nabani na uthe wasusa kumazwi encwadi yesi siprofeto, uThixo uya kusisusa isabelo sakhe kwiNcwadi yoBomi, nakumzi ongcwele, nakwizinto ezibhaliweyo kule ncwadi. Niziintsilini, nizizithulu, kwaye niziimfama kangangokuba anikuqondi ukuba eso silumkiso andisinikanga nje ngenxa yencwadi enkulu yokutyhilwa kwam, iSiTyhilelo! Kodwa eso silumkiso sisebenza kuzo zonke izibhalo ezingcwele! Kuba iMizekeliso yam ikhala kakhulu ezitratweni kubo bonke abaneendlebe zokuva: Onke amazwi kaThixo acocekile kwaye ahlambulukile; kuba ulikhaka kubo bonke abakholosa ngaye. Ngoko ke musa ukongeza nto kumazwi akhe, hleze akukhalimele ufunyanwe ulixoki.

Balungiseleli bam baseLawodike, enenze ubuhlobo nehlabathi. Balungiseleli bam baseLawodike, enizenza iinkosi phezu kwelifa likaThixo, nisithi ningoosaziwayo. Balungiseleli bam baseLawodike, eningawuhoyanga umoya wam endiwudalileyo kuni. Balungiseleli bam baseLawodike, enizinika izikhundla ezingenaxabiso nezibizo eniziqamathisela kumagama enu. Balungiseleli bam baseLawodike: Yivani amazwi am nindiphendule, kuba zonke iilwimi ziya kuvuma!

Ngubani oninike igunya lokutshintsha amazwi am, kwanelizwi leNkosi endilinike ngabathunywa bam? Iindlela zenu, owu nina balungiseleli baseLawodike abanqenayo, azinto zintsha, kuba kwakamsinya nje zakuba iileta zam zisasazwe kumaBandla am asandul' ukuzalwa abhalwe kwiZenzo nakwezinye iindawo, amahlelo obuGnostiki akhokelwa ngabaprofeti bobuxoki bobuGnostiki atshintsha amazwi am aza abhala kwakhona isibhalo, kuba baphuma phakathi kweBandla lam ukuze bonke bazi ukuba babengabelusi abawexukileyo!

Niya kuvuna imivuzo yeendlela zabo, kuba nizimanye nabo, kwaye niphila iindlela zabo, kwaye nibonakalisa oomoya abangcolileyo bakaIzebhele, umprofetikazi wobuxoki enimbiza ngokuba ungcwele! Ndiyayibona imisebenzi yenu emibi, kwaye ibhaliwe emazulwini ngeenkcukacha ezigqibeleleyo ngokuchasene nani! Kufuneka nithethe amazwi endininika wona, kwaye kufuneka niwathethe ngokunyaniseka, ngengqibelelo, ngaphandle kokutshintsha nelimdaka legama laso nasiphi na isiprofeto! Andiziphikisi, kuba ndinixelele: Zondeleleni ukuzimisa phambi koThixo ningumntu ovunyiweyo, umsebenzi ongenazintloni, olahlula ilizwi lenyaniso ngokufanelekileyo. Amazwi amaninzi atshintshiwe kwizibhalo zam kule minyaka idlulileyo, kwaye ukuba niya kufunda kwaye niwahlonele amazwi am endiwanikileyo, ndiya kunikhokelela kuyo yonke inyaniso, kuba ndimnike ngolo hlobo uMthuthuzeli.

Ndiza kunibonisa, ukuba niya kumamela amazwi amaninzi endinishiyele wona. Landelani izibhalo endazinika eAntiyokwe, kuba ngamazwi amangqina amaninzi, kwaye amangqina am akaxoki kwaye akaphikisani. Khangelani kwiTextus Receptus kwaye nihlale kude neeBhayibhile zabawexuki: fundelani ukuqonda! Kodwa nina, balungiseleli bam baseLawodike, aniwahloneli amazwi am, kwaye niyatshintsha amazwi am ukuze alungele iinjongo zenu ezimdaka, nifundisa abantu bam ukuze baqhubeke esonweni, kwanokubakhokelela nzulu kwiindlela zenu ezimdaka! Kukho isiphoso esikhulu phakathi kwenu, kwaye anilandeli iindlela zam, kuba ninekratshi kwaye nineentliziyo ezilukhuni, kwaye ndiza kunishiya ekutshatyalalisweni kwenyama ngaphandle kokuba niguquke nitshintshe iindlela zenu. Kuba mna, uYesu Krestu, ndithumele la mazwi! Musani ukuwatyeshela, kwaye niguquke ngengubo yokuzila nothuthu. Guqukani esidlangalaleni emabandleni enu kwaye nivume izono zenu kwabo nibalahlekisileyo, kuba ndiza kusisusa isiphatho senu sezibane. Guqukani nihambe ngokuthe tye, kwaye ndiza kusibuyisela isibane senu ngedangatye elitsha, kuba ndim oza kuphemba umlilo womoya wam emphefumlweni wenu.

Buyelani eluthandweni lwenu lokuqala, kuba niyandithanda, kodwa hayi ngokokolwazi. Musani ukuwathandabuza amazwi am, kuba ndiyeza kungekudala kunokuba nicinga, kuba ndiza njengesela ebusuku. Nakwimpazamo yenu, nicinga ukuba isela ebusuku ngumntu ongena efestileni njengesaphuli-mthetho. Zidenge! Owu, ukuba beniziqonda iindlela zamaHebhere. Isela ebusuku lalingumbingeleli omkhulu owayefika engalindelwanga ukuze abone ukuba ababingeleli bakhe bathembekile kwimisebenzi yabo yasetempileni, kuba eminye imisebenzi yayiqhubeka ubusuku bonke de loo misebenzi igqitywe ngokomthetho. Ukuba umbingeleli omkhulu wayenokufumana umbingeleli ophantsi kweliso lakhe elele esemsebenzini, wayephosa ilahle elishushu kwiingubo zakhe, ukuze umbingeleli avuthulule iingubo zakhe ezivuthayo abaleke aphume etempileni ehamba ze kwaye eneentloni. Ningalele xa ndifika, kungenjalo niya kuwa phambi kwam nihamba ze kwaye nidanile ngehlazo elibi kakhulu onokulicinga, kuba ndiya kuba neentloni ngani kwaye ndinihluthe wonke umvuzo. Isohlwayo sam siphezu kwenu kwaye isandla sam sinzima phezu kwenu, kwaye ngaphandle kokuba niguquke, niya kuba nengxaki enkulu, enkulu ebeninokuyiphepha. Guqukani, ukuze ndisanibekela indawo etafileni yam, kwaye niya kuzimanya noMtshakazi wam emtshatweni nam, uKrestu Yesu, uMyeni.

Ndim iLizwi elenziwa inyama elihleli phakathi kwenu.

Ndingumalusi olungileyo kwaye izimvu zam ziyaliva ilizwi lam.

Ndim iMvana kaThixo ethunyelwe ngenxa yezono zehlabathi.

Ndingumbingeleli omkhulu ngonaphakade ngokohlobo lukaMelkitsedeke.

Ndiyeza kwaye umvuzo wam unam.

Ndim ohamba phakathi kweziphatho zezibane ezisixhenxe neengelosi ezisixhenxe.

Ndim umprofeti oveliswe phakathi kwabazalwana awathetha ngaye uMoses: Yivani ilizwi lam ngokuthobela.

Ndim onike izipho ebantwini, ngoko ke musani ukuzityeshela izipho endizinikileyo.

Ndiyeza kungekudala kunokuba nicinga; zilungiseleleni, Mtshakazi wam!

Mna, iNkosi uYesu Krestu, ndithumele la mazwi kwaye ndibe lilizwi lomnye odandulukayo entlango; kuba iBandla lam laseLawodike lidikidiki kwaye kungekudala liza kuba ze kwaye lidane ngaphandle kokuba liguquke. Ndimi emnyango ndiyankqonkqoza umzuzwana nje omfutshane! Akusekho manani ewotshini, kuba amanani awile; kwaye akusekho nkozo zentlabathi zifumanekayo xa inkumbula yokugqibela iqukuqela kwiglasi yexesha, kwaye sele iza kuwa.
