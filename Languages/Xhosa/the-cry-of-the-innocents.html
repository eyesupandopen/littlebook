Isikhalo Sabangenatyala!

Thabathani isililo, nilile nenze isijwili ngenxa yokuxhelwa kwabangenatyala. Kuba abenzanga lutyala, abaphilanga ubomi, yaye abenzanga sono, kuba ukumka kwabo ngaphambi kwexesha kwakukukhuseleko lwesibeleko sikanina, yaye phambi kokuba bafumane ithuba lokuphefumla okokuqala, abasekho!

Thabathani isililo, nilile nenze isijwili ngenxa yobomi obulahlekileyo babangenatyala negazi labo elichithekileyo liyakhala emazulwini lifuna ubulungisa. Yabonani! Umhlaba uyajikeleza uze ugungqe njengoko uxinzelelo lusanda, kuba ubunzima bokuxhelwa kwabangenatyala bukhulu!

Thabathani isililo nangenxa yabantwana bakaRakeli, kuba itsho iNkosi; Kweviwa izwi eRama, isililo nokulila okukrakra: uRakeli elilela abantwana bakhe, wala ukuthuthuzelwa ngenxa yabantwana bakhe, kuba babengekho. Nangona nicinga ukuba le yinto elula, qondani ukuba ezi zono azinakuhoywa yaye ubunzima besohlwayo sazo bukhulu kakhulu yaye umgwebo ngokuqinisekileyo uphezu kwabo bonke abenze ezi zono nabalile isithembiso sam soxolo!

Yivani amazwi am, bantu bam, nithobele iintliziyo zenu kumazwi am nize nive ubulumko bam kuba umgwebo asingowenu. Qondani usizi olukhulu neenyembezi ezikrakra zeNkosi yenu nomsindisi wenu, uYesu waseNazarete! Kuba iinyembezi ezininzi, ezininzi, ndiye ndazikhupha rhoqo ngenxa yokuxhelwa kwabangenatyala. Phumani phakathi kwakhe bantu bam nindivumele ndichithe ngokupheleleyo imisebenzi kamtyholi ebomini benu kule, nakuyo yonke imicimbi bantwana bam. Kuba umoya wam awusezi kunigwebela isono, kuba nenziwe ubulungisa bukaThixo kum kuba namkele isipho sam sobomi obungunaphakade nesipho sam sobulungisa, enenziwe ngaso amalungisa, nobulungisa bukaThixo kum ngegazi lam lentlawulelo nangomoya kaThixo ophilayo, umoya wobunyana enitywinwe ngawo umhla wenkululeko, yaye niyindalo entsha kum. Ngoko musani ukoyika!

Nidalwe ngobulungisa nobungcwele bokwenene yaye xa umoya wam ongaphakathi unigwebela, ndim Krestu Yesu onigwebela ubulungisa. Ewe, ndinigwebela ubulungisa ukuze niphile ngobulungisa kuba ningubulungisa bukaThixo yaye nimele niqhubeke nokubulala inyama, nihambe ngomoya wam kuba ngoko akusekho kugwetywa kwabo bakuKrestu Yesu, abangahambi ngokwenyama, kodwa ngokomoya! Ngoko qondani ukuba umgwebo asingowenu, kuba umphefumlo ngamnye uYahweh amxoleleyo kum Krestu Yesu awuyi kuza emgwebeni ngenxa yezono kodwa uya kusuka ekufeni uye kubomi obungunaphakade kuba namkele uYesu waseNazarete, isivumelwano soxolo sikaYahweh kulo lonke uluntu naphakade kubo bonke abamkela umbingelelo wam!

Yizani kum nivume nezono enizibangele ukuba nizilibale nize nivukele ebulungiseni ningoni. Yizani kum nivume iziphoso zenu, ukuze ndinihlambulule kuwo onke amabala ngegazi lam lentlawulelo, ukuze imisebenzi kamtyholi icinywe ngokupheleleyo nangonaphakade ebomini benu. Kutheni nibambelele ngenkani ezonweni zenu? Kutheni nifuna ubuthongo ukuze nikhululeke? Ningazifihla na kuThixo ophilayo xa nivala amehlo enu? Ngaba ninamathele kwintsomi yeNciliba nifuna ukulinganisa le ntsomi? Andinibizelanga ukuba niphile intsomi yeNciliba okanye ukuba ningcwabe iintloko zenu esantini okanye ukuba nigqume izono zenu ngolonwabo nokulibala. Kuba iNciliba ibeka intloko yayo emhlabeni ukuze ikhathalele amaqanda eentsana zayo ezingekazalwa, hayi ukuzifihla kwinyani. Kodwa ndinibizele ukuba nijongane nezono zexesha lenu elidlulileyo nize nizivume kum ukuze ndiniphilise ndize ndichithe imisebenzi kaSathana ebomini benu kuba ufuna ukunibophelela ezonweni zakhe aze anityhole ngonaphakade ngobuyatha!

Ngoko ke, qhubekani nivuma izono zenu ekuhleni neziphoso zenu omnye komnye nize niqonde ukuba njengoko nisenza iintshaba zenu ziyangcangcazela kuba zibona ubungqina obungenakuphikiswa bosindiso lwenu oluqhubekayo nobungqina obungenakuphikiswa bentshabalalo yazo ezayo nomgwebo ongunaphakade kwelo chibi likhulu apho awona malangatye acocekileyo aluhlaza aya kutsha ngonaphakade yaye impethu yazo ayifi! Ngoko phumani phakathi kwabo nahlukane nize niqonde ukuba ninoxolelo lwezono. Njengoko niqhubeka kubudlelwane benu obusindisayo nam, Krestu Yesu, ndiya kunihlambulula ngokugqithisileyo kuzo zonke izono zenu ukuze ndizenzele iBandla elizukileyo elingenabala okanye umbimbi okanye nayiphi na into enjalo, kodwa ukuze nibe ngcwele ningenasiphoso phambi kwam eluthandweni. Nokuze nambathiswe ngelinen ecikizekileyo kuba linjalo ikamva lenu elikufuphi. Ngoko qondani ukuba kum, Krestu Yesu, akukho kugwetywa okanye umgwebo kuni bantwana bam yaye amazwi am arhabaxa asingawenu kodwa ndinicebisa ukuba nifunde ngam nize niqonde ubunzulu bento endinihlangule kuyo kuba nikhululwe emgwebeni.

Yaye yazini ukuba sonke isenzo sibhaliwe emazulwini kuso sonke isidalwa esiphantsi kwezulu esiphantsi komgwebo omkhulu. Yaye ndibonile ndaza ndabhala amazwi nemisebenzi yabaprofeti bobuxoki, nabo bazenza ngathi bathetha egameni lam yaye baye bakuxhasa ukuxhelwa kwabangenatyala ngenxa yeendlela zabo ezonakalisayo. Kuba aba ngabo bangamaqula angenamanzi, nabo ukukrexeza kwabo kwakhokelela ekuxhelweni kwabangenatyala. Abathi, endaweni yokuguquka ekukrexezeni nasekuqhekezini kwabo, babulala iintsana zabo ezingekazalwa ukuze baqhubeke besondla umhlambi wam endaweni yokondla umhlambi wam, yaye baya kujongana nomsindo omkhulu ngenxa yolwaphulo-mthetho lwabo kuba akunakwenzeka ukuba iingcuka ezirhwaphilizayo zizifihle epulpitini ekujongeni kwam okunaphakade, yaye baya kuthwala umgwebo wabo bonke abangayi kuguquka kwezi zenzo!

Ngoko ke ndithetha nawe mntu wemvelo, yaye ndithetha nawe Bhabhiloni eyiMfihlo. Kuba unetyala legazi labangenatyala, nabo ubathathileyo kukhuseleko lwesibeleko sikanina. Usongele ukubethwa kombungu kwingqiqo yobuxoki nakwisizathu esenza isilingo sakho sokuzingca sokubulala abangenatyala sibe yinto eqhelekileyo njengotyelelo lukagqirha, yaye iikliniki zakho zokubulala zilinuka ezimpondweni zikaBawo, nelinuka ezimpondweni zam. Kuba igazi labangenatyala liyakhala emhlabeni lisiya kutsho kwitrone kaYahweh ezulwini lamazulu obona konke, nani nonke enifuna ukuzifihla ekujongeni kwakhe okunaphakade. Yaye mna Yeshua HaMashiach ndithunyiwe nguBawo emgwebeni, yaye ndiyiNgonyama yeSizwe sakwaYuda, kuba niyalile iMvana kaThixo yaye ngoku kuza umgwebo wenu. Yaye ndiya kuwuhlambulula ngokupheleleyo umhlaba kwiindlela zenu zokubulala yaye niya kudla ubumdaka beendlela zenu ezimbi, kuba uYahweh umisele isahlulo esiphindwe kabini somgwebo phezu kwenu, oh Bhabhiloni eyiMfihlo.

Kuba nibulele abangenatyala, nicinga ngokungazi ukuba esi senzo sibi yinto elula. Nithengise abantwana benu abangekazalwa ngemali emdaka ukuze nikhulise imisebenzi yenu, nemisebenzi yenu yobuyatha obupheleleyo kuba yonke iya kuphela yaye imizamo yenu yobuyatha exabiseke kakhulu yehlabathi iya kunyamalala emgwebeni ingaze ikhunjulwe. Kuba nibulele ngokuzithandela abantwana benu abangekazalwa kumbingelelo ongaziwayo kuMoleki, yaye nibulala abantwana benu abangekazalwa ngekratshi elikhulu. Yaye kuya kuba njalo, kuba ikratshi landulela ukuwa nomoya onekratshi wandulela intshabalalo yaye umgwebo nentshabalalo yenu ayilibazisi kodwa iphezu kwenu yaye sele iqalile yaye iintlungu zokubeleka azipheli ngoku.

Kodwa endaweni yenguquko, endaweni yokuza kum, uYesu waseNazarete ngokuthobeka xa ndinike ubomi obungunaphakade noxolelo lwezono neziphumo ezivela kumgwebo ohambelanayo nikhethe endaweni yoko izono zenu nesohlwayo senu esingunaphakade. Nizibophelele kwaye nikhethe endaweni yam uYesu waseNazarete, uBharabhas, uMoshiach wenu wobuxoki. Endaweni kaYahweh uThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade, nikhethe inamba njengothixo wenu yaye ngoku ndiya kuninikela kubo kuba nizimanye neendlela zabo ngekratshi nangeqhayiya lenu yaye ngoku niya kudla ubumdaka beendlela zenu.

Nenze umbingelelo ngegazi labangenatyala ezibingelelweni zokuzanelisa, yaye nichitha imibungu yenu ebethiweyo ngokungathi ngamalaphu angcolileyo alahliweyo kwimigqomo yenkunkuma yezonyango, yaye niwubale umoya wobomi osegazini njengento engcwele yaye umgwebo wenu uphezu kwenu. Kodwa abenu kanye, nabantwana benu abangekazalwa, babengasafani nefashoni yenu ehlala iphela yaye ubomi babo babungumqobo kuni ukuba nizigrumbe ngokupheleleyo kulonwabo lwezono.

Niyathethelela nize nifune igazi labangenatyala kwiingcamango zenu zopolitiko zokubulala, yaye ngokwazi nizimanye nabawiweyo kwimvukelo yabo, kuba yayingooSathana abafundisa ukubethwa kombungu esibelekweni, neenkqubo zenu zokubulala ezixabisekileyo eninekratshi lokuzikhusela nokuziqhayisa ngazo. Ngoko ke igazi labangenatyala liyakhala emhlabeni yaye ubunzima bezono zenu bubangele ukuba umhlaba ujingajinge njengendoda enxilileyo, yaye kuya kuba kukhulu ukushukuma kwaye kuya kuba kukhulu ukuwa! IBhabhiloni eNkulu iwile, iwile!

Oh mntu wemvelo, Oh Bhabhiloni eyiMfihlo, hayi indlela owile ngayo kubabalo nenceba endakubeka phezu kwakho ngokungafanelekanga. Yaye nangona ubabalo lwam nenceba yam ingelolize, kuba iinjongo zikaYahweh, neenjongo zakhe ezifihliweyo kwimfihlelo enkulu kaThixo zizalisekisiwe kwiintlanga zeeNtlanga yaye amaxesha azo agqityiwe. Yaye ndim oya kujika ngoku ndisindise uYakobi entshabalalweni, kuba baya kujikela kum njengoMesiya wabo, nabo bonke uYahweh ngokwakhe ababizileyo kususela kunaphakade odlulileyo ukuba bahambe phambi kwakhe njengomntu omnye omtsha kum Krestu Yesu, nesizwe esinye esitsha yaye akusayi kuphinda uSirayeli kaThixo ahlulwe yaye akusayi kubakho lwandle. Kodwa kuqala imihlaba kufuneka ihlanjululwe yaye ubukumkani bukaSathana buya kutshatyalaliswa ngokupheleleyo, bungaphinde buvuke. Oh buyatha, bubi yaye bonakele mntu wemvelo, nawe ongenawo umoya wam nalowo ubulele ubomi obungunaphakade kum Krestu Yesu.

Qonda ukuba abantwana lilifa leNkosi, yaye kungakuhle ukuba umntu angaze azalwe kunokuba enzakalise abangenatyala phambi kokuphefumla kwabo kokuqala; ukwenzakalisa nomnye wabancinane bam kubuhlungu yaye kuthwele isohlwayo esingunaphakade. Kodwa noko kunjalo niyaqhubeka. Nangona ndiphefumlele abaninzi ukuba beme ngaphandle kweekliniki zenu bezama ukunithintela ekutywineni iziphelo zenu. Kodwa anifunanga nto yazo, yaye nizilile izilumkiso zam yaye ngoku niya kuthwala umgwebo wenu.

Niphambukele kwiindlela zikaBhahali yaye niye nanjengoko uSathana enikhokelele, kuba anisenamvakalelo kwiindlela zenu zokungakholelwa. Yenzani enikuthandayo ngumthetho wabo wonke eniwenzileyo owenu. Nikhokelelwe kwiindlela zamaKanan, kuba nawo abingelela abantwana bawo, ngekratshi elikhulu novuyo, kanye njengoko niqhubeka nisenza! Kodwa ngekratshi lenu nicinga ukuba anifani nalawo mandulo ayiziyatha nangafundanga yaye iindlela zenu zingaphezulu kwezi ndlavini zamandulo, kodwa nenza okufanayo. Kodwa ngokobuyatha nicinga ukuba nahlukile, yaye nifuna ukusebenzisa ukungazi ngokuzithandela njengesigqubuthelo, yaye nifuna ukwenza izizathu nize nifihle izono zenu kwimfundo yenu yobuxoki enikhokelele emgwebeni. Kuba ubunzima begazi elingenatyala liyakhala emazulwini nanamhlanje yaye lifuna ubulungisa, yaye uYahweh uzivile izikhalo zabo!

Ngokungazi ninikele abangekazalwa benu ukuqhubela phambili inkqubo yerhamncwa, yaye abawiweyo bayamkele imibingelelo yenu. Basebenzise igazi labangenatyala njengoko bebesoloko besenza ekwenzeni amanyala abo, yaye njengoko kwakunjalo ngemihla kaNowa kuya kuba njalo. Kuba xa umthinteli esusiwe, niya kuqonda ubukhulu obukhulu nenani lamanyala abo, kuba baya kukhululwa ngokupheleleyo bengenazithintelo.

Nenze umbingelelo ngegazi labangenatyala yaye amanyala abawenzileyo aya kunizingela aze anisukele kude kube seziphelweni zomhlaba kuba bebesoloko besitya abemi bomhlaba yaye ukhula olubi olwenziwe ngezandla zabawiweyo alunakuxolelwa ngenxa yezono zabo nolwaphulo-mthetho lwabo nxamnye noYahweh, kuba uluntu lwenziwe ngomfanekiso kaYahweh yaye ukungcolisa ubomi benyama esegazini sisono esikhulu yaye asiyi kuhoywa kwabo baye bala usindiso lwam ngamaxesha am obabalo nenceba. Yabonani! Ndithumela izigebenga ukuba zizalisekise ingqumbo yam!

Ngoku ndiya kuthetha nabawiweyo, yaye bayeva yaye bayangcangcazela! Iindlela zenu zibangele intshabalalo yenu epheleleyo engunaphakade kuba eyenu yintshabalalo engunaphakade apho niya kubuyiselwa isono ngasinye amaxesha amaninzi. Ubomi ngabunye enibuthethileyo kwiindlela zenu ezimbi ezizele kukubawa nenkanuko niya kubuyekezwa ngamakhulu amaxesha. Kuba nisebenzise igazi labangenatyala kwizithethe zenu, nakwizinto enizenzayo, nakwizithethe zenu yaye ninze amanyala amaninzi njengoko nanidlala uthixo! Kodwa aningoothixo yaye aninakuzihlangula esandleni sam, kuba mna Krestu Yesu ndiyimbewu ethenjisiweyo yomfazi eya kuyityumza ngokupheleleyo intloko yenu!

Ngoku kuza umgwebo wenu nentshabalalo esondeleyo. Kuba aniphalazanga nje igazi elingenatyala, mna Yesu waseNazarete, kodwa ninetyala legazi labangenatyala ukusukela kowokuqala kude kube kowokugqibela kuba nini abafundisa ukubethwa kombungu ukuze udlule. Iindlela nezenzo zenu ezinyanyekayo ziya kubuyekezwa ngokupheleleyo kuni ngonaphakade yaye aniyi kuze nisabe okanye nibe nomzuzwana wokuphumla kwisohlwayo senu esingunaphakade. Kuba nini abanoxanduva lokufa nentshabalalo yabangenatyala yaye bonke abalandele iindlela zenu ezitshabalalisayo ngentiyo engaguqukiyo baya kwabelana ngelifa lenu lomlilo ongunaphakade njengoko nihlawulela isenzo ngasinye esenyanyekayo enisenze nxamnye nendalo kaYahweh. Kuba zona ezo zinto nizenzileyo kwabanye ziya kunikwa ezifubeni zenu amaxesha amaninzi kude kube kunaphakade kanaphakade yaye aniyi kusinda.

Kuba nazama ukuba ilifa lam kum, ngaphandle kokuba nohluleka kuba nazama into engenakwenzeka: Nazama ukubulala igazi elingenatyala, mna Yesu waseNazarete, kuba akunakwenzeka ukuba ukufa kundibambe. Kuba ndanikezela ngobomi bam ngokuzithandela yaye akukho mntu wabuthatha kum, kodwa ndabeka phantsi ubomi bam ngokuzithandela njengePasika egqibeleleyo yalo lonke ixesha. Yaye mna, noKrestu Yesu, ndiya kuyichitha yonke imisebenzi yenu emibi ebomini bamaxhoba enu, nabo beze kum ngenguquko yeendlela zenu ezimbi nabo baya kubiza egameni lam kude kube sekupheleni, kuba isihlwele ESİKHULU siya kusindiswa. Kwanomphefumlo ngamnye uYahweh awumiselele ubomi obungunaphakade kususela kwiiseko zomhlaba yaye aniyi kuxhwila noyena mncinane kwaba ezandleni zam ezingunaphakade, oh namba yohlulekileyo. Kuba abangenatyala enibabuleleyo baya kuvuka babe butsha kubomi obutsha obungunaphakade novuyo olungunaphakade phambi koYahweh yaye ngonaphakade baya kuphila kuvuyo olungunaphakade yaye baya kunandipha ukutyeba komhlaba! Kuba uthembise ntoni uYahweh?

Yabonani! Itsho iNkosi, Kweviwa izwi phezulu, isijwili nokulila okukrakra. URakeli elilela abantwana bakhe, wala ukuthuthuzelwa ngenxa yabantwana bakhe, kuba babengekho. Itsho iNkosi, Thintela ilizwi lakho ekulileni, namehlo akho kwiinyembezi: kuba umsebenzi wakho uya kuvuzwa, itsho iNkosi, yaye baya kubuya kwilizwe lotshaba: Yaye likho ithemba ekupheleni kwakho, itsho iNkosi, ukuba abantwana bakho baya kubuyela kwimida yabo.

Yabonani! Mna, noYeshua HaMashiach, ndiya kubavusa bonke abangenatyala abalahlekileyo yaye ndiya kuliphindezela ngokupheleleyo igazi labo kwabanetyala! Ndiya kubavusela kubomi obungunaphakade yaye ubomi babangekazalwa buya kuvela bube bubomi obungunaphakade novuyo olungunaphakade yaye ndiya kubadibanisa kwakhona nabakhululweyo, nabo ndibenzele inceba abeza kum ngenguquko yaye ndiya kubabuyisela oko nikuthethileyo, oh nina bawiweyo. Yaye eyabo iya kuba luvuyo olungunaphakade yaye ndiya kubabuyisela abangenatyala ababuleweyo kwiintsapho zabo yaye uYahweh ngokwakhe uya kuba nguYise wabo yaye baya kuthatha iindawo zabo phakathi kwezizwe ezingunaphakade yaye baya kuphindezelwa kwiintloko zenu ezingenaxabiso!

Yabonani! Baya kwenziwa babe namandla, yaye bazukiswe, yaye abangenatyala banendawo ekhethekileyo entliziyweni kaYahweh yaye iinjongo zakhe ziya kuzalisekiswa kubomi babo obungunaphakade yaye ngonaphakade baya kukhanya phambi koYise wabo noThixo wabo yaye baya kuzukiswa ngonaphakade kum Krestu Yesu oya kubavusa aze abubuyisele ngokupheleleyo ubomi babo obabubiweyo phambi kokuba babe nethuba lokuphefumla.

Ngoko thuthuzeleka Rakeli, kuba abantwana bakho baya kubuyiselwa baze baphumle phantsi kwesandla sam esinamandla sokuthuthuzela usazi ukuba abancinane bakho baya kuhlala bephila ngonaphakade yaye abayi kuze batshabalale, kuba mna Yeshua HaMashiach nditshilo yaye akukho nto inokutshintsha isibhengezo sam. Yaye uYahweh uThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade uyavuka ngomsindo nomgwebo yaye umgwebo ngoku uya kuwa!

Kutheni nifuna ukubuyela ebuthongweni bam ababiziweyo nabanyuliweyo? Oko ndikuthembisileyo ndiya kukuhlangula kuba ndiza ngamandla nozuko lukaYahweh uThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade yaye ndimnye kubunye nenjongo noBawo, noEl Shaddai uThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade kuba akukho namnye onjengaye! Qondani ukuba ukubonakala kwam kuyasondela yaye ngoku kusemnyango wenu! Ixesha liphelile lokuba iBhabhiloni eyiMfihlo iguquke yaye umgwebo wayo sele uqalile. Ngesiquphe xa umthinteli esusiwe yaye abantu bam bevusiwe yaye betshintshiwe ukuze bandihlangabeze emoyeni, intshabalalo yesiquphe iya kuwela phezu kwabangendawo yaye abayi kusinda yaye igazi labangenatyala balo lonke ixesha liya kuphindezelwa ngokupheleleyo ngonaphakade ngomhla womgwebo omkhulu, yaye ngoku umgwebo omkhulu uya kuwa. Thuthuzelanani ngala Mazwi, kuba nisindisiwe kwingqumbo Bandla lam, noMtshakazi wam!

Mna Yeshua HaMashiach ndithumele la mazwi ngomkhonzi wam, yaye ngomkhonzi wam la mazwi athunyelwe!

Ndicebisa bonke abangaguqukiyo ukuba bamkele usindiso ngobabalo phambi kokuba umnyango woMkhombe uvalwe. Aninalo ixesha, ngoko ndenze iNkosi ngoku nisazi ukuba uYahweh wandivusa kwabafileyo!

Yaye bonke abashiyelwe kumlilo wokucoca ndibacebisa ukuba babize egameni lam kude kube sekupheleni yaye bangamkeli uphawu lwabo, yaye niya kusindiselwa kuvuyo olungunaphakade!
