Indlela Yokwamkela Isiprofeto: Indlela Endifumana Ngayo Imiyalezo Yesiprofeto

NgoMatshi 2, 2024, iNkosi uYesu Krestu yaqala ukundinika imiyalezo yesiprofeto ukuze ndabelane nabantu malunga nokubonakala kwayo okukufuphi kwiBandla layo kunye nezinye izihloko. Ezi ziprofeto zeza kum ngendlela iBhayibhile eyichaza ngayo xa "isandla seNkosi" siphezu komntu. Kum, yimvakalelo enzulu yobunzima emoyeni wam, engeyi kushiya de ndiwugqibe umyalezo. UKrestu uya kucinezela amagama ambalwa okanye amabinzana engqondweni yam endingenakukwazi ukuwasusa kwaye ndingasoze ndawalibala, kwaye amaxesha amaninzi ndizithumelela elo binzana ngomyalezo ukuze ndikhumbule. Xa ndihlala phantsi ndiqalisa ukuchwetheza, iNkosi iya kundibonisa kanye la Mazwi emoyeni wam njengoko ndichwetheza: Ewe, ngamanye amaxesha ndiba nemibono yokwenene yamagama, kwaye amaxesha amaninzi ndiyawava la mazwi njengoko ndichwetheza, kungekhona ngokuviwa ngeendlebe kodwa emoyeni wam, kwaye avakala ngendlela engenakuphikiswa.

Xa ndenza impazamo ndichwetheza iingcinga zam, amazwi ayema de ndicime oko ndikongezileyo. Xa ndizithandabuza ndicima ibinzana uKrestu awayefuna lihlale, amazwi ayema de ndibuyisele isivakalisi ngendlela esasime ngayo, kwaye eso sivakalisi asisuki engqondweni yam de ndenjenjalo. Kutshanje, iNkosi indinike imiyalezo enzulu kakhulu yezemfundiso, kwaye indise kwizibhalo ezithile. Iya kufaka ibinzana emoyeni wam ndize ndilichwetheze; emva koko ndiya kukhangela elo binzana eBhayibhileni yam, ndize ndifumane ukuba elo binzana likwicandelo lesibhalo. Ngaloo mzuzu iya kundibonisa ukuba zeziphi izibhalo ekufuneka ndizicaphule, ndize ndizincamathisele kwibali, aze amazwi aqhubeke kwangoko. Ndisamangalisiwe kukuba aMazwi iNkosi endinika wona ahambelana ngokugqibeleleyo nezahlulo zezibhalo endinika zona ukuba ndizicaphule, kwaye isibhalo endisicaphulayo sihamba nebali amaxesha amaninzi ngendlela endingenakukwazi ukuyiphumeza ndedwa. Andizange ndenze into enje ebomini bam, kude kube ngoku. Ngokuqhelekileyo andiyazi intsingiselo epheleleyo yomyalezo de ndibuyele umva ndijonge upelo neziphumlisi. Ngaloo mzuzu ndiya kuwuphinda ndiwufunde, kwaye iNkosi iya kundenza utshintsho oluncinci, mhlawumbi ndongeze isivakalisi sokucacisa, mhlawumbi ndicime igama ndilifakele elinye, njalo njalo. Emva koko ubunzima buyemka ndize ndazi ukuba ndigqibile.

Nceda uqaphele ukuba kuyo yonke le miyalezo yesiprofeto, andizange nditshintshe nantoni na ephathelele umxholo, nakanye, soze; kwaye akuzange kubekho tshintsho lukhulu kumxholo, nakanye, soze. Iziprofeto ezinde zithatha malunga neyure okanye ngaphantsi ukuzichwetheza, kwaye inkqubo yokulungisa ingongeza enye iyure okanye ezimbini. Ndijonga yonke into ngesibhalo ukuqinisekisa ukuba andonakalisi nto ekuqondeni kwam kobuntu. Ukuza kuthi ga ngoku andikenzi njalo, kodwa mandicacise: Amaninzi kula Mazwi angaphaya kokuqonda kwam xa ndiwachwetheza, kwaye kufuneka ndiye esibhalweni ukuze ndifunde ngakumbi ngokusekelwe koko ndikufumeneyo. Akuzange kwenzeke nakanye ukuba imiyalezo iphikisane nesibhalo, nangona icela umngeni ekuqondeni kwam isibhalo, kwaye nam ndingumfundi. Ngoku, ndingumfundisi kwaye ndineminyaka ekufutshane kuma-30 ndisesibhalweni kwaye ndenze iintshumayelo ezininzi, iimfundiso, njalo njalo. Manditsho oku kumava am eminyaka: Akunakwenzeka ukuba mna ndichwetheze nantoni na yeli nqanaba neenkcukacha ngelo xesha lifutshane ngemizamo yam nokuqonda kwam. Kundithatha iintsuku ezininzi, ngamanye amaxesha iveki okanye ngaphezulu, ukulungiselela intshumayelo ende, eneenkcukacha, kwaye oku akufani, akufani kwaphela. Oku akuveli kum kwaye kungaphaya kokuqonda kwam. Umoya wesiprofeto uhle phezu kwam, kuba uBungqina bukaYesu bungumoya wesiprofeto.
