Kuma-144,000 Nakwiingcwele zeMbandezelo yeNkathazo kaYakobi

Kuma-144,000 nakwiingcwele zembandezelo yenkathazo kaYakobi: Yabonani! Ndinixelela izinto ezininzi zingekenzeki ukuze isilumko phakathi kwenu esilivayo ilizwi lam sibe nokulumka ngakumbi, nokuze niyazi inyaniso yamazwi am. Kuba uSathana uya kuzama ukukugquma ukumka kwabathembekileyo ebandleni lam (kuba ndiya kubaxhwila ekumkeni kwabo emhlabeni ndibasa emazulwini) ngemfazwe nangokuvela kobuxoki kwabazibiza ngokuba ziindwendwe ezivela kwezinye iiplanethi, ekungekho nto yimbi ngaphandle kwabawileyo abazityhilayo emhlabeni kubo ubugwenxa phezu kobugwenxa obusekelwe ngokupheleleyo kubuxoki babo. Kuba bona bayinkohliso.

Baxokile malunga nento ekuthiwa yindalo iphela; kuba akukho ndalo iphela eyandayo engenanto, kodwa ngaphezulu komhlaba likho izulu lamazulu, lona kanye (izulu eliphezulu) apho ahlala khona uYahweh onguThixo ophezukonke etroneni yakhe, kwaye uya kwenza ikhaya lakhe nabantu. Ukanti ukho kuyo yonke indawo kwaye ukubo bonke ngaphakathi kuMzimba kaKristu. Emhlabeni kuhlala iperile yakhe yexabiso elikhulu, bona abo bakhethwa kwaphakade kwintliziyo kaYahweh ngokwakhe. Ngaloo ndlela umhlaba ngoweNkosi nenzaliseko yawo; kuba mna, Yesu waseNazarete, ndiwuthengile umhlaba ngegazi lam elihlawulelayo.

Fundani inyaniso yenu ngendalo (fundisisani ukuze nifunde) kwaye nikholwe yinkcazo namazwi akwiZibhalo Ezingcwele malunga nendalo, kuba kunjengoko uYahweh eyichazile eZibhalweni, kwaye akunakwenzeka ukuba uYahweh axoke. Ukuba, xa ndinixelela izinto zasemhlabeni, anikholwa, ningathini na ukukholwa ukuba ndithe ndanixelela ngezinto zasezulwini? Hayi, akukho ndwendwe zivela kwezinye iiplanethi ngendlela ubuxoki babo obuchaza ngayo, kodwa ziingelosi eziwileyo, abavukeli, yena kanye uSathana nobukumkani bakhe abaxokileyo bakhohlisa umhlaba ukuze bonke bagwetywe abo bayoliswa kukungabikho kokusesikweni.

Baluke iintsomi ezininzi eziyilwe ngobuchule zaba ngumnatha wobuxoki, kwaye baye balawula omabini amacala eengxelo zabo kwasekuqaleni njengoko beqhuba iinjongo zabo. Kuba balawula ingcamango engundoqo kwaye balawula nengcamango echaseneyo, kwaye bayidibanisa imisebenzi yabo emibi inyathelo ngenyathelo, kwaye inkohliso yabo inkulu! Kodwa ukukhanya kweZibhalo Ezingcwele kuyabutyhila ubuqhophololo, kodwa umntu uyaluncedo lukaThixo de umgwebo umiswe ize intlekele ifike. Emva koko uluntu lufuna ukuguquka xa sele kusemva kwexesha emva kokuba umgwebo ugqithisiwe, kwaye abo baphila ngaloo ndlela abayi kuwubaleka umgwebo wabo. Kuba nantoni na evakaliswe nguYahweh akukho mntu unokuyitshintsha!

Abaninzi baya koyiswa ziinkohliso ezizayo eziya kuvela kwimfazwe yehlabathi nakwintlekele esezakufikela umhlaba. Kuba baya kuzibonakalisa eluntwini njengezidalwa ezinobubele ezivela kwihlabathi eliphucukileyo ukuze zabelane ngolwazi lwazo olunobubele. Kodwa lulwazi nje lokulungileyo nokubi oluphinde lwapakishwa ukuze luhambelane neenjongo zabo. Baya kuba neeteknoloji eziphezulu kwaye baya kubonakala bephucuke kakhulu, ngaphaya komntu wemvelo. Kodwa inkqubela yabo ikukwenza okubi kuphela, kwaye abangabo abalo mhlaba kodwa badalwa nguYahweh ekuqaleni kwendalo emazulwini. Babekhona xa uYahweh wayedala umhlaba. UBawo kulwazi lwakhe lwangaphambili wayesazi ukuba baya kungcatsha waza ke wagcina imfihlelo yakhe exabisekileyo yentliziyo ifihliwe kwabo wayesazi ukuba baya kuzisa intshabalalo embi kwindalo yakhe.

Ndisabizela umntu wemvelo ebulungiseni, kodwa umntu wemvelo ukhetha ukugula, isono, nobumnyama; ngoko ke ndiya kulunika uluntu oko lukunqwenela ngamandla. Ekubeni umntu wemvelo esenza okubi kwaye ethanda ubumnyama, ndiya kumnika ubumnyama obungenazithintelo.

Kuba okwangoku, umntu wemvelo ngokukratshi umi elubabalweni lwam esebenzisa inceba njengesizathu sokubeka isono phezu kwesono, endaweni yokwamkela isivumelwano soxolo sikaYahweh, mna kanye, uYeshua HaMashiach, owanika igazi lam elingenasono ngokuthobela uYahweh njengentlawulo yazo zonke izono zoluntu lonke ixesha. Ungaphazami: NdinguMesiya wokwenene, kwaye bonke abanye ngabaxelisa abangaxabisekanga.

USathana wavukela kamsinya emva kokuqala kwendalo waza wayingcolisa indalo kaBawo ngendlela engenazintloni, embi, enobutshaba, netshabalalisayo; kodwa kwenziwa ngobuqhetseba obukhulu nangemiqondiso noommangaliso bobuxoki, kwanangeeteknoloji eziphucukileyo ezimangalisa iimvakalelo. Baze ke bona bafune unqulo nentobelo engqongqo kwiindlela zabo, kodwa iindlela zabo zihlala zizisa ukufa nentshabalalo eluntwini, kuba ziyilelwe loo nto.

Akukho nto intsha phantsi kwelanga, bantwana bam. Oothixo ababizwa ngokuba ngoothixo bembali yamandulo nenzala yabo enyanyekayo ekubhekiselwa kuyo njengoothixo abangabantu baya kubuya, kwaye njengokuba kwakunjalo ngemihla kaNowa, kuya kuba njalo. Kuba bayakhululwa emakhonkcweni abo nasentuthumbisweni ukuze bafumane umgwebo wabo opheleleyo, kwaye ndiya kubuyisela, itsho iNkosi uYesu Kristu, kwaye uYahweh uya kubabuyisela ngokupheleleyo.

USathana nabawileyo bakhe bala ukuguqa ngedolo kumyalelo kaYahweh baza endaweni yoko bazama ukubela uluntu umhlaba ngenkohliso enkulu. USathana ude wazama nokuba ilifa lam kuYise weziKhanyiso! Kodwa akukho mntu unako ukuxhwila noyena mncinane kuni ezandleni zam, kwaye akukho mntu unokundihlutha ilifa lam; kuba linikwe nguYahweh ngokwakhe, kwaye nabani na owalayo ukuguqa ngedolo kum, Yesu Kristu, uyawuvukela umyalelo kaYahweh, ezimanya nabawileyo ngonaphakade.

Ngoko ke ndithi kuni, musani ukukholelwa kubuxoki bukaCopernicus, owayengumJesuit kunye neFreemason owayekhonza uSathana. Waphinda nje wafundisa amazwi eentanda-bulumko zamandulo zamaGrike angamahedeni. Ngaloo ndlela ke imbono yenu eyamkelekileyo ngendalo yacingwa ngabanquli belanga namahedeni ngokusekelwe kubuxoki bukaSathana, kwanamaJesuit anyelisayo aye azonakalisa iinkqubo zenu zemfundo ngokufunda okuchasene nokufunda, ngenjongo epheleleyo yokuba indalo yabo yobuxoki ibhalwe ezingqondweni zabantu ngobuxoki obupakishwe njengemfundo. Endaweni yoko, kuye kwenzeka ukufundiswa okungenangqondo, kwaye ihlabathi njengoko linjalo ngokwenene aliqondwa ngabo bahlala emhlabeni.

Ngoku iqonga lilungiselelwe inkohliso enamandla kakhulu, kwaye ubuxoki sele bakhiwe, kuba iziseko zabo bubugwenxa nenkohliso enkulu. Ezi nkohliso zisekelwe kwimfundo yenu yamaJesuit. Kuba ihlabathi lonke likhohliswe bubuxoki babo; kwanabanyulwa kanye bebeya kukhohliswa ukuba bekunokwenzeka. Ngaloo ndlela umgibe ulungele ukugqabhuka, kwaye ekuphela kwento ewubambileyo ngumthinteli, oza kususwa ngequbuliso kwaye kungekudala. Emva koko imfazwe enkulu iya kuqhambuka kuwo wonke umhlaba njengoko abathembekileyo bam bebuthelwa kum emazulwini. Emva koko eluthuthwini kuya kuphakama irhamncwa nomprofeti wobuxoki, abaya kuba ngundoqo kwinkohliso ezayo.

Ngoko ke musani ukukholelwa kubuxoki babo, zigorha zam, kuba nisisizwe esikhethekileyo kwaye ndinibizile ndanitywina ngetywina likaThixo ophilayo, ukuze ingqumbo kaThixo inganichukumisi. Ndiya kunithuma njengezigorha zam. Niya kuba ngamazwi am amaninzi akhala entlango naphi na apho nithunyelwe khona, kwaye ndinithumela ukuba niqokelele iigusha zam ezilahlekileyo eziseleyo.

Kuba isihlwele esikhulu siya kuphuma kwinkathazo kaYakobi, kwaye niya kwenza imisebenzi emikhulu niyalele okungenakwenzeka, kuba ndiya kunika imiqondiso emininzi enamandla, imimangaliso, noommangaliso ukuba yenziwe ngezandla zenu. Niya kwenza imimangaliso emikhulu yamandulo ebhalwe kwiZibhalo Ezingcwele, kwanangamandla ka-Eliya, ngaphandle kokuba niya kwenza imisebenzi emikhulu nenamandla ngakumbi kunaleyo ibhaliweyo ngaye wamandulo.

Kuba iZibhalo zithi lowo ukholwayo kum, uYeshua HaMashiach, akayi kwenza kuphela imisebenzi endayenzayo emhlabeni kubulungiseleli bam kwaYuda njengemvana kaThixo, kodwa niya kwenza imisebenzi emikhulu kunaleyo ndayenzayo mna, kuba ndiya kunika imiqondiso emikhulu, imimangaliso, noommangaliso ngezandla zenu njengoko niyalela okungenakwenzeka ngokholo kum. Ngoko ke khangelani kum rhoqo. Kwaye gcwalisani amehlo enu kum, kuba ndiya kunikhokela inyathelo ngenyathelo. Uhambo lwenu luya kuba lolobulumko, kwaye niya kuba nolwimi lwezilumko, kwaye ndiya kuba ngamandla enu nosindiso lwenu, kuba kum, uKristu Yesu, kuhlala yonke inzaliseko.

Ngoko ke ndiyanicebisa nonke ukuba kungekudala imali iya kuba yinto engenaxabiso, kwaye ekuphela kwemali eyamkelekileyo iya kufuna uphawu ukuze ufikelele kwiimpahla zehlabathi. Musani ukuthatha uphawu, kuba kungakuhle ukufa kobu bomi (ningabathandi ubomi benu kude kube sekupheleni) kunokuba nithathe uphawu nize niphoswe kwichibi lomlilo elingunaphakade elilungiselelwe uMtyholi neengelosi zakhe, kuba amaxesha azo afikile; kuphela musani ukuzimanya nazo. Kuba ziya kuthembisa izithembiso ezibonakala zizikhulu kwaye zixabisekile, kodwa amazwi azo bubuxoki, kwaye kukho ityhefu emazwini azo, kwaye izithembiso zazo bubuxoki kwaye zizele zizo zonke iintlobo zezinto ezingcolileyo. Azisokuze zikwazi ukuzizalisekisa izithembiso zazo, kuba umgwebo wazo uyeza; azinalo uxolo, kwaye uxolo luya kususwa ngokupheleleyo emhlabeni.

Ububi boluntu buya kuba bungenazithintelo ngokupheleleyo; kwaye kwabaninzi, xa umthinteli esusiwe, baya kuzinikela ngonaphakade ekuphanzisweni nasebubini. Ngaphandle kokuba lowo uphila ngekrele umele abulawe ngekrele, kwaye ngokuqinisekileyo baya kuwa ngasekhohlo nangasekunene ngenani elikhulu, kodwa akuyi kuza kuni njengoko nihamba ngenxa yam; kuphela musani ukuthanda ubomi benu kude kube sekupheleni kwaye nilandele umzekelo wam wenkonzo yokuzincama nombingelelo. Kuba umvuzo omkhulu uya kuba ngowenu ngenxa yentobelo nokuthembeka.

Ngoko ke iinkanuko nobubi boluntu ziya kuba zingenazithintelo ngokupheleleyo, kwaye uMtyholi neengelosi zakhe baya kuba bengenazithintelo ngokupheleleyo, kwaye onke amanyala abo amabi aya kuba engenazithintelo ngokupheleleyo, kwaye baya kuza njengabancedi bakudala. Inkohliso yabo inzulu kwaye ineengcambu ezinzulu eziza kuncothulwa ngokupheleleyo kungekudala. Abangoothixo, kwaye abangabo abaphucukileyo abanobubele, kodwa bangabakhohlisi, kwaye bazingcolisile ngeendlela zonke. Ngaloo ndlela baya kuba bengenazithintelo ngokupheleleyo, kwaye inzaliseko yobukumkani bukaSathana iya kukhululwa enzonzobileni, kuba iya kuvulwa.

Oku kuya kwenziwa ukubonisa umntu wemvelo oko akukhethileyo nokunika ithuba lokugqibela lokuguquka; kuba ububi abuyi kuphinda bufihlwe kwaye buthintelwe, kwaye yonke imfihlelo iya kuvela ekukhanyeni. Kwanobuxoki benkohliso yabo enkulu neenkohliso ezinamandla ziya kutyhilwa ukuze bonke babone, kuba uYahweh ukuvakalisile oko kwiZibhalo Ezingcwele, kwaye oko akuvakalisayo uYahweh akukho mntu unokukuguqula.

Ngaloo ndlela ke kuya kwenzeka ukwahlukana kokugqibela, kwaye abaninzi kwabazithembileyo endibathandayo baya kushiyelwa kwiimvavanyo novavanyo oluzayo, ukuze basindiswe. Kuba alisekho ixesha, kwaye ubabalo lwam nenceba yam azisayi kuphinda zinikwe ngendlela efanayo, kwaye alisekho ixesha leendlela zokudikidiki. Wonke umntu kuya kufuneka enze ukhetho; kwaye aboyikayo nabangakholwayo baya kuba bekhethe uSathana nobukumkani bakhe ngoxa babenokukhetha mna, yena kanye uYesu Kristu, unyana kaThixo oze enyameni kwaye ndizinikele njengePasika egqibeleleyo yazo zonke izono zoluntu kube kanye. Abaninzi baya kusala benenkani besala isandla sam soncedo ngexesha lentswelo enkulu, kuba uncedo lwam luncedo olungunaphakade noluhlala luhleli lomvuzo omkhulu, inceba, ubabalo, nothando.

Noko ke, uninzi lwehlabathi luya kukholelwa kubuxoki kwaye luya kulandela irhamncwa ngokumangala, lusithi, ngubani onjengerhamncwa? Baya kuzivumela ngokulula ukuba balunywe lulunya lwenyoka olunobuhlungu obungenakuze bunyangwe, kuba buhlala buhleli. Ukuba umntu akhethe ububi ngamaxesha okukhetha kuya kuba sisigqibo esingunaphakade, kwaye abo benza olo khetho baya kuma phambi komgwebo omkhulu.

Abo bakhetha ububi ngokuzithandela baya kuphendula ngayo yonke ingcinga embi, ngalo lonke ilizwi elibi, nangeso sonke isenzo esibi abasenzileyo ngokukhohlakeleyo emhlabeni, kuba iincwadi ziya kuvulwa kwaye akuyi kubakho nceba. Kwaye akukho mntu uya kukwazi ukuma ngobulungisa bakhe, kuba umntu akanabo ubulungisa bakhe; kuba inyoka yabonakalisa kwasekuqaleni, kwaye imihla yayo yokonakalisa iyeza esiphelweni. Ngenxa yoko uYahweh wandithuma, mna Nyana wakhe okuphela kwamzelweyo, njengesivumelwano soxolo kumntu wemvelo, kwaye umntu wemvelo ukhabile uxolo lwanaphakade kuba umntu wemvelo uthanda ububi.

Umgwebo omkhulu sele uza kukhululwa phezu kobukumkani bukaSathana. Lo mgwebo uya usiba mbi ngakumbi, kwaye iziganeko ezenziwa emgwebeni ziya zisiba mbi ngakumbi. Usizi luya kongezelelwa phezu kosizi kwaye intuthumbo iya kongezelelwa phezu kwentuthumbo kubo bonke abalayo ukuguquka kwizenzo zabo ezibi. Kuba abaninzi abayi kuguquka kwimisebenzi yezandla zabo, ukuze banganquli iidemon, nezithixo zegolide, nezesilivere, nobhedu, nelitye, nomthi: ezingenakubona, zingenakuva, zingenakuhamba: Kananjalo abayi kuguquka ekubulaleni kwabo, nasekuqhatheni kwabo, nasekuhenyuzeni kwabo, nasebuseleni babo.

Kuba abo bawayo abangothixo, kodwa bangabaxoki abakhohlisayo, abazenzisi, abadlali, nabakhohlisi. Bazisa kuphela ukufa nentshabalaliso kwabo balandelayo, kwaye bonke abakhohlisiweyo baya kudana ngokupheleleyo, kuba ulindelo lwabo luya kunyamalala kwelo chibi lomlilo likhulu apho kungekho themba. Kwaye mna, Krestu Yesu, ndiya kubuchitha ngokupheleleyo ubukumkani bukaSathana ngomgwebo omkhulu, kuba umgwebo omkhulu uya kukhululwa phezu kwabo bonke, kwaye mna ndiyiNgonyama yesizwe sakwaYuda.

Kwaye ke itywina ngalinye liya kongeza umgwebo phezu komgwebo, kwaye ixilongo ngalinye liya kongeza umgwebo phezu komgwebo, usizi ngalunye luya kongeza usizi phezu kosizi, kwaye ngesitya ngasinye kuza ingqumbo epheleleyo kaThixo, ingqumbo phezu kwengqumbo. Ubukumkani bukaSathana buya kubandezeleka kakhulu, kwaye ubukumkani bakhe buya kubhangiswa ngokupheleleyo kwaye butshatyalaliswe.

Kuba baya kuzihlanganisa baze bazilungiselele ukuchasana nokuza kweNkosi, kuba ngokuqinisekileyo ndiya kuza. Ukusukela ekuqaleni kwenkathazo kaYakobi, kuya kubakho iintsuku ezisixhenxe zeminyaka, kuba iveki yamashumi asixhenxe kaDaniyeli iya kuzalisekiswa ukuze kutshatyalaliswe ukungalungisi nokusindiswa kukaSirayeli. Kuba ndiya kumhlambulula uSirayeli kububi obubambe ilizwe.

Kuba akukho noYahweh nam ngokwam, Yeshua HaMashiach, oye wathuma okanye wagunyazisa abalawuli babo, kuba balawula ngokungalungisi kwaye ukubulawa kwabo kwabantu ngobuninzi kuyacekiseka, kwaye abantu bam bahlawula ixabiso leemfazwe zabo. Kanti ke uninzi lweBandla lam lukholelwa kubuxoki babo bobuZiyonisti, ngoko ke qondani ukuba andinguye umZiyonisti, kuba bona bangabaphakamisi bobuYuda, kwaye bangumgobo, imbewu kaSathana kanye; kwaye ndiya kubancothula elizweni, kuba baye babanga ilifa lam, kwaye ndiyeza kuthatha oko kokwam. Kuba mna, Yeshua HaMashiach, ewe mna, ndiphethe itayitile yomhlaba; kuba ndawuthenga ngegazi lam elingenasono, kwaye ndinezitshixo zokufa nesihogo. Kwaye ndizihlawulele izono zoluntu, kwaye nabani na obiza egameni leNkosi uya kusindiswa.

Ngoko ke umhlaba ngowam nokuzala kwawo, kwaye ndiya kulihlambulula ilizwe ebubini babo, kwaye indlu yesikhungu kaSathana iya kutshatyalaliswa ngokupheleleyo, kwaye ngoko ke ndiya kuzisa umnqophiso omtsha emhlabeni kwaye ndiya kulawula ngobulungisa. Kuba njengoko umhlaba usiba mnyama kwaye usiba mubi ngomzuzwana, ukuza kwam kwesibini kusondele ngomzuzwana.

Yibani nethemba elikhulu ngokuza kwam; kuba ndiya kuza kwaye ndiya kuvela ezulwini nemikhosi yasezulwini, umkhosi ongenakufaniswa nawuphi na, kuba umkhosi onjengowam awuzange ubekho, umkhosi ongenakoyiswa owenziwe ngabo baphila ubomi obungunaphakade kwaye abanakubulawa okanye boyiswe. Kuba ndinikwe amandla nguYahweh ngenjongo enjalo, kwaye ndiya kuba ndibanike amandla kwaye ndibavuzile ngokuthembeka kwabo nothando lwabo kum negama lam, ewe uYesu Krestu! Kuba nabani na othanda aze akhonze uYeshua HaMashiach uyathanda aze akhonze noYahweh, kuba akukho mntu uphakamileyo kunoThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade kaSirayeli! Nabani na okhanyelayo uNyana, akanaYe uYise; kwaye mna ndinguYesu Krestu okuphela kwamzelweyo kaYise owazinikela ngenxa yezono zabo bonke, kwaye ndingumbingeleli omkhulu ngonaphakade ngokohlobo lukaMelkitsedeke.

Kwaye ndiya koyisa ngokupheleleyo inamba, irhamncwa, nomprofeti wobuxoki. Kuba irhamncwa nomprofeti wobuxoki baya kuphoswa bephila kwelo chibi livuthayo elivutha awona malangatye ashushu aluhlaza angenakuze acinywe kwaye apho kungayi kubakho nomzuzwana wokuphumla. Kodwa zonke izenzo zabo ezibi ziya kubuyiselwa kubo ngonaphakade. Yonke intlungu abayibangeleyo iya kuhlawulwa ngonaphakade, kuba uYahweh uya kuhlawula sonke isenzo esibi kude kube yeyona peni yokugqibela ezifubeni zabo ezimbi, kwaye baya kuhlawula ngonaphakade ngenxa yolwaphulo-mthetho lwabo.

Kwaye ke uSathana uya kubotshwa enzonzobileni iminyaka eliwaka, kuba kutshiwo njalo nguYahweh. Mna, Yeshua HaMashiach, ndiya kulawula umhlaba ngobulungisa usuku olunye. Kuba kuYahweh usuku lunjengeminyaka eliwaka kwaye iminyaka eliwaka injengosuku. Kwaye ndiya kulawula ndisetroneni kaDavide, kwaye kwaSirayeli ulawulo lwam luya kuhlala ekuzalisekisweni kwezithembiso ezenziwa nguYahweh kooYise, kwaye usuku lwam sele lukufuphi.

Kodwa kuqala umgwebo kufuneka uze emhlabeni kwaye umhlaba uya kuhlanjululwa kuyo yonke into embi. Kuba akukho nto imbi iya kuvunyelwa ebukumkanini bam, apho kuya kubakho uxolo nokhuseleko njengoko kuthenjisiwe nguYahweh ngokwakhe. Kwaye ingonyama iya kubuthuma nemvana. Mna, ewe mna, Yeshua HaMashiach, ndivakalisa amazwi phambi kokuba enzeke, ukuze nazi ukuba uThixo kaSirayeli uze kuhlanganisa iigusha zakhe ezilahlekileyo zendlu kaSirayeli kum, uMalusi oyiNtloko, kwaye ndiya kuzibetha iintlanga ngentonga yentsimbi, kwaye ndiya kuyoyisa ngokupheleleyo indalo kaBawo ngobulungisa nobungcwele bokwenene.
