Yabonani! Ukuvalwa koBungqina okwexeshana, iNdibano, noKwahlulwa

Yabonani! Mna, Yeshua HaMashiach, ndiza kuxelela iBandla lam, umzimba wam, kwanomtshakazi wam izinto eziza kwenzeka emhlabeni. Ukuze niziqonde iintsingiselo zezinto eziza kwenzeka, ningabi ziimfama kubaluleko lwazo. Kuba, abawileyo bafuna ukulahlekisa bonke ngamanga abo enkohliso kwaye umhlaba ebeniwazi uyaguqulwa kulo mhlaba beniwazi ngaphambili kwaye awusafani kwaye uza kuqhubeka uguqulwa kulungiselelo lwawo lwangaphambili olwabekwa ukusukela kunogumbe kaNowa.

Yabonani! Njengokuba kwakunjalo ngemihla kaNowa, uya kuba njalo kwakhona umhlaba ukuze umgwebo opheleleyo wehlele ngokupheleleyo phezu kweentloko zabawileyo naphezu komhlaba ngokwawo kwaye ubizo lokugqibela lobulungisa ngoku luza kuvakala kuwo wonke umhlaba ngomgwebo omkhulu nengqumbo eza kufika ngequbuliso ngoku. Ngoko ke, waqondeni amaxesha eniphila kuwo nokubaluleka kweziganeko ezizayo. Kwanazo iziganeko eziya kunqunyulwa zibe mfutshane ebulungiseni, kuba ukuba imihla yembandezelo kaYakobi ibinganqunyulwanga, inene, inene, bekungayi kusala nanye inyama iphila emhlabeni kwaye konke bekuya kwenziwa kube senkangala. Kuba zinjalo iinkanuko zabawileyo, ukuba ekubaweni kwabo ukuba umhlaba eluntwini bangathanda ukubangela ukuba bonke ubomi benyama esegazini buphele ngonaphakade, kuba umhlaba wenzelwa umntu nomntu ukuba alawule umhlaba ngobulungisa. Kwananjengokuba kubhaliwe: Uyintoni na umntu le nto umkhumbulayo? Kanti umthwesa isithsaba sozuko nembeko, wammisa phezu kwemisebenzi yezandla zakho.

Noko ke, izicwangciso neenjongo ezingunaphakade zikaYahweh, uMNA NDIKHONA Omkhulu, ziya kuzalisekiswa ngokupheleleyo sisandla sam esinamandla!

Ngoko ke, waqondeni amaxesha nokubaluleka kweziganeko ezikufuphi, ukuze ningabanjiswa ngazo okanye nithathwe ngumgwebo ongenzelwanga abathembekileyo kuKristu. Qondani, ukuba ekukhaleni kwexilongo lokugqibela eliza kukhala, kwanelo xilongo lokugqibela lexesha lam lobabalo elinikwe ezandleni zam ngomyalelo opheleleyo wokuba likhale ngexesha elimisiweyo eliphantse libe phezu kwenu ngokupheleleyo zintanda zam, ukuba umhlaba uya kunikelwa ngokupheleleyo ezandleni zenamba kulawulo lwayo olufutshane ngenceba.

Kwaye oku kungenxa yokuba umntu wemvelo ulwamkele ngokuzithandela ulawulo olunikezwe ngookumkani abalishumi, abavuka ngenkohliso enkulu kwiintsapho ezilishumi zakudala, ezilawule umhlaba ngenkohliso enkulu nangobunono ngenxa yabawileyo ukusukela oko baqala ukuvuka ekuweni kweBhabheli nenqaba yayo ngemihla kaNimrodi. Ezi ntsapho zilishumi ziye zalinda zajonga ukubuya kooyise noothixo bazo abangengooyise kwaye abangengoothixo, kodwa abazenzisi abazenzisayo. Abawileyo banikezele amarhamncwa abo ukuba alawule ngomyalelo wenamba, abathi ngobuqhetseba nangezithembiso ezingento yanto nangokwahlukana okukhulu, bazibhukuqa iintlanga ukuze zonke iinkokeli zehlabathi ngoku zikhonze inamba ngokupheleleyo, kwaye abo bangavumiyo, baye babulawa, okanye baya kubulawa, zizandla zabo ezifihlakeleyo okanye baya kuwa ngokupheleleyo yimfazwe yabo ezayo yokoyisa ihlabathi.

Noko ke, linjalo ihlabathi eniphila kulo namaxesha aligubungeleyo ihlabathi ngobubi obukhulu nenkohliso epheleleyo. Kuba njengoko kunjalo, ihlabathi ngoku liwuchasile uMyolelo wam oMtsha woBulungisa owathi, inene, inene, watywinwa waqiniswa ngegazi lam elingenasono endalinikela intlawulo epheleleyo yesohlwayo sikaMoses. Kwananjalo, ukuze umntu owileyo abe nokhetho lokwamkela ukuxolelwa kwezono nosindiso olungcwele nokubuyiselwa kuYahweh, uMNA NDIKHONA Omkhulu, ngendlela kaYesu waseNazarete okanye umntu owileyo angakhetha ukuhlala ewile kwaye ekhonza iinkanuko zabawileyo, amkele umgwebo opheleleyo ngaso sonke isono, naso sonke isenzo sokungalungisi abasenzileyo ngokungalungisi, nokuba sincinci kangakanani na. Qondani ukuba bonke abakhetha ukuhlala bekhonza abawileyo baya kuhlangana nabo ngokupheleleyo esohlwayweni sabo kwelo chibi lomlilo likhulu, kwanomgwebo oqatha nongqongqo ongazange wenzelwe umntu, noko ke bonke abakhetha ukuhlala nabawileyo baya kuhlangana nabawileyo ngokunjalo kuba akukho lunye ukhetho!

Noko ke, ukuchaswa koMyolelo wam oMtsha akulicimi igunya elipheleleyo nomphumo woMyolelo wam oMtsha kuba uMyolelo wam oMtsha ngumnqophiso ongunaphakade noMyolelo ongunaphakade oya kuhlala usebenza ngonaphakade nangonaphakade kwanangegunya elipheleleyo loMbingeleli oMkhulu ngonaphakade ngokohlobo lukaMelkitsedeke, kwaye uya kusinda uhlale ngonaphakade nangonaphakade njengoMnqophiso ongunaphakade woBulungisa kulawulo olungunaphakade nolugqibeleleyo nolulungileyo lwendalo kaYahweh. Kwanendalo yezinto ezisezulwini, nasemhlabeni, naphantsi komhlaba kwaye akukho ndawo yokuzimela apho ulawulo lwam olulungileyo aluyi kusebenza ngokupheleleyo ekupheleni kweveki yamashumi asixhenxe kaDaniyeli, ekwayimbandezelo kaYakobi, ngoku edibene ngokupheleleyo kwindibano yeentlungu zokubeleka kwaye umntwana uya kuzalwa!

Kwanomntwana ongcwele weziqhamo zokuqala ezilungileyo kwisivuno serhasi esipheleleyo esiza kwenzeka ngokupheleleyo ngequbuliso nangokungalindelekanga ngoku, kwanexesha elingunaphakade lokuzalisekiswa apho abafileyo kuKristu baya kuzalwa bevela entliziyweni yomhlaba apho baphumle khona ngoxolo, kwaye abathembekileyo kuKristu abaphilayo nabasalayo baya kumka ebusweni bomhlaba kunye nabo ukuze bandikhawulele emoyeni kwaye niya kuthathwa nonke niye ezulwini lamazulu apho niya kuma phambi kweNkosi yenu engunaPhakade yoBulungisa ukuze nifumane ilifa lenu elipheleleyo! Kwaye ndiya kuninikela ningenabala, nasisihla, nasibatha, nambimbi, nayiphi na into enjalo kuYahweh, uMNA NDIKHONA Omkhulu, oninike ngokwenene ooyihlo nonke ngomoya wam woBunyana enitywinelwe ngawo le mini yenkululeko!

Kwaye niya kuthatha nonke iindawo zenu kwaSirayeli kaThixo, kuba nokuba nizalwe ningamaYuda, okanye kwizizwe ezilahlekileyo zakwaSirayeli, okanye kwiintlanga: kuKristu Yesu anisayi kuba yiyo nayiphi na yale minombo yegazi yasemhlabeni kodwa nibe yindalo entsha, umntu omtsha, kwanadalwa ngokutsha ngokupheleleyo kugqibelelo olupheleleyo kuKristu Yesu kwaye niya kumangaliswa bubuhle obungunaphakade eniya kwambathiswa ngabo. Kuba abo bakuKristu baya kubunjwa benziwe ngendlela efanayo noKristu Yesu kwaye aboyisi bam baya kuzidla ilifa zonke izinto ngendlela efanayo endoyisa ngayo ndadla ilifa zonke izinto esandleni sikaYahweh, uMNA NDIKHONA Omkhulu, kwaye linjalo ilifa labathembekileyo! Ngoko ke, qondani ukuba ngephanyazo lelizwi niya kukhululwa nonke ngonaphakade kwigazi elonakeleyo lika-Adam, kwanango-Adam wokugqibela: lowo uYahweh umenze umoya ophilisa oya kunidala ngokutsha nonke ngendlela efanayo neNkosi yenu engunaPhakade yoBulungisa, Selah!

Ngoko ke, ndiya kwenza umsebenzi ogqibeleleyo nopheleleyo kuni zintanda zam, kwananjengoko ukuthobela kwenu okuthembekileyo kubulungisa bekunjalo kumaxesha am obabalo nenceba. Kuba ubabalo nenceba kuyatshona njengelanga ngolu rhatyelo lwendibano, apha ekuqaleni kwembandezelo kaYakobi. Kuba indibano endithetha ngayo yindibano yobulungisa, efikelela kuvuthondaba ngokuthi abathembekileyo kuKristu bahlulwe ngokupheleleyo kwabo bahleli emhlabeni ngokungalungisi, njengoko bethathwa basiwa kukhuseleko lwamazulu ngephanyazo lelizwi ngequbuliso nangokungalindelekanga ngoku.

Noko ke, njengoko lo msebenzi ulungileyo usenziwa ugqitywe ngokupheleleyo, kuya kubakho kwakhona indibano yobubi emhlabeni njengoko umthinteli wam esusiwe. Kuba kukho iindawo ezininzi zokuthintela ezithintela abawileyo ekubeni babe nendibano yabo abayinqwenelayo, noko ke njengoko abathembekileyo kuKristu bethathwa zonke iindawo zokuthintela ziya kususwa kunye nomthinteli wam kwaye uMikayeli uya kusukuma azisuse kwaye umkhenkce uya kuwa, kwaye umhlaba uya kuguqulwa ngogonyamelo ukuze ube ngokupheleleyo njengakwimihla kaNowa, uphelele namaNefili awo amasikizi enziwe kwakhona kwaye aya kukhululwa. Yabonani! Ndizisa izigebenga ukuze zizalisekise ingqumbo yam!

Ngaloo ndlela, umhlaba uya kunikelwa ezandleni zenamba ixesha elifutshane, kwanexesha lomgwebo oqatha apho abemi bomhlaba baya kuba nobubi nobumnyama ababunqwenela nababunqwenelayo ngokupheleleyo, ngaphandle kokuba abayi kuba nexesha legolide kodwa umgwebo oqatha nongunaphakade endaweni yoko. Kwaye abawileyo baya kuba nemfazwe yabo abaya koyisa ngayo umhlaba ngokupheleleyo, ububi obungathintelwanga, nokungalungisi okungathintelwanga ngokupheleleyo, kwaye elo henge likhulu libomvu liya kuwa emalangabini omgwebo njengoko iBhabheli eyimfihlo igwetywa ngokupheleleyo itshatyalaliswe ekumkeni kwabathembekileyo kuKristu, ingaphindi ivuke! Qondani ukuba baya kuzifihla kwizixeko zabo ezingaphantsi komhlaba nakwiindawo zokuhlala, nakwiziseko, nakwiindawo zokukhusela, kodwa abaninzi babo abayi kusinda kodwa baya kungcwatywa emhlabeni ngomgwebo opheleleyo, kwanagcinwa ekuthuthunjisweni ekufeni kude kube nguloo mgwebo wetrone emhlophe kwaye abayi kusinda.

Emva koko isityhilelo nokuqonda kuya kuhla ngokukhawuleza phezu komhlaba ukuba umgwebo opheleleyo nengqumbo zifikelele phezu komhlaba, kwaye ngubani na oya kuba nako ukuma? Noko ke, bonke abanqula igama likaYesu kude kube sekupheleni kwabo baya kufikelela kusindiso olupheleleyo noluzukileyo kwaye intsalela enamandla yakwaSirayeli iya kwamkela uYeshua HaMashiach njengoMesiya wabo wokwenene kwaye iya kusindiswa iphile kubukumkani bam beminyaka eliwaka. Ngoko ke kuqondeni ukwahlulwa kokugqibela okuzayo.

Kuba intsalela elungileyo kaYakobi iya kwahlulwa ku-Esawu no-Ashkenazi, kwanasindiswe iphile sisityhilelo esiza kufezwa ngamangqina am amabini, kwanu-Enoki uMbhali no-Eliya umTishbhi.

Kuba abadidididi nabaninzi kwabo babekhe bamala uYesu waseNazarete baya kushiyelwa emililweni yokuhlanjululwa kwaye baya kwahlulwa kwabo bamangaliswa lirhamncwa kwaye baya koyiswa ekuqaleni lirhamncwa eliphuma elwandle emfazweni yalo kwiingcwele eziseleyo; abaya koyisa irhamncwa eliphuma elwandle ngokunqula uYesu njengeNkosi yabo kude kube sekupheleni kwabo kwaye baya kungena eluzukweni olukhulu ngonaphakade!

Kuba abacekisekayo nabangalunganga baya kwahlulwa ngokupheleleyo kwabalungileyo batywinwe ngonaphakade lulumo lwenyoka olusesandleni serhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni eliya kusetyenziselwa ukubambisa ngokupheleleyo bonke abo bathanda ububi kwaye bayonwabela ukungalungisi, kwaye abayi kusinda.

Kwaye kunjalo, ukuba amaxesha okutyhila agubungele ngokupheleleyo umhlaba ngoku, kwaye konke kuya kutyhilwa ngokupheleleyo ngamaxesha afanelekileyo. Ekuhlanganisweni kwabathembekileyo kuKristu okunikezwe ngokudanduluka okukhulu ngelizwi lesiphatha-zithunywa nangelo xilongo lokugqibela eliya kukhala ngokuqinisekileyo, abo bathathwe basiwa emazulwini abasayi kubona ngesipili ngokungacacanga kodwa baya kuziqonda ngokupheleleyo zonke izinto kwananjengoko zaziwa ngokupheleleyo. Kwaye baya kubona ngamehlo abo, kwaye baya kuva ngeendlebe zabo, kwaye baya kubona ngokupheleleyo baqonde uzuko lukaYahweh uMNA NDIKHONA Omkhulu, kwanaye uKristu Yesu iNkosi yabo engunaPhakade yoBulungisa kwaye liya kuba lixesha elimnandi kangakanani na njengoko umyeni emanyana ngokupheleleyo nomtshakazi wakhe kumnqophiso ongunaphakade! Kwaye iitafile zilungisiwe namagumbi esidlo akulungele!

Kwaye kunjalo, ukuba amaxesha okutyhila aya kufika ngokupheleleyo phezu komhlaba ngoku, kwaye yonke inkalo yobukumkani bukaSathana nemiyalelo yokungalungisi iya kutyhilwa ngokupheleleyo kwaye ikhutshelwe ekubonweni ngokupheleleyo njengoko umgwebo usiwa, kuba isigqubuthelo sokwahlula siya kuphakanyiswa ngequbuliso nangamandla njengesigqubuthelo esasetempileni esahlula indawo engcwele kakhulu kwindawo engcwele encinci sakrazulwa ukusuka phezulu kuye ezantsi sisandla esinamandla sikaYahweh ngokwakhe, xa ndathetha elo binzana loloyiso olungunaphakade, KUGQITYIWE!

Ngoko ke, waqondeni amaxesha eniphila kuwo kwaye nizilungiselele zintanda zam. Qhubekani niguquka kwaye nibongoze igazi lam elicamagushelayo phezu kobomi benu nobomi babo nibathandayo ekuthetheleleni, ukuze nibe nokuxolelwa okupheleleyo kwazo zonke izinto nokuxolelwa okupheleleyo kwezono kwaye abaninzi baya kusindiswa kukuthethelela kwenu okulungileyo. Qondani ukuba ngoku lixesha lokuzahlula ngokupheleleyo kwiindlela zeli hlabathi kwanakwiinkathalo zeli hlabathi likhoyo elibi kuba ngequbuliso nangokungalindelekanga amaxesha enu okuhlala kweli hlabathi linesono aya kuba sekupheleni okungunaphakade, kwaye anisayi kuphinda nibe phantsi kobubi nokungalungisi. Ngoko ke qhubekani niphuma phakathi kwabo kwaye nahlukane kuzo zonke izinto, nangona, ndiyanicela ukuba niqhubeke kwiikhosi zenu, nisebenza ngenkuthalo njengokuba nisenzela iNkosi ngeentliziyo ezipheleleyo zokholo olusebenza ubulungisa kude kube ngequbuliso emhlabeni anisekho!

Kwaye ngoko ndiza kuxelela iBandla lam, umzimba wam, kwanomtshakazi wam ukuba amaxesha enkululeko yabo afikile kwaye amaxesha okubhiyozela okupheleleyo nelifa nomvuzo amiselwe ukwamkela abathembekileyo kuKristu emazulwini ngomyalelo wam ngoku, kuba uYahweh unikele zonke izinto ezandleni zam ezinamandla ukuba ndizifeze, kwaye ndihlala ndisenza intando kaBawo ukuze nani nikhululwe ningene ebulungiseni obupheleleyo ukuze nani nihlale niyenza intando kaYihlo ngogqibelelo olulungileyo!

Ngoko ke, ngendlela efanayo oluya kwenzeka ngayo olu tshintsho lolawulo ngentshabalalo enkulu nemfazwe njengoko abawileyo besenza oko kunikelwe ezandleni zabo ukuba bakwenze kumgwebo opheleleyo weBhabheli eyimfihlo ehleli phezu kwamanzi amaninzi angabantu, nezihlwele, neentlanga, neelwimi; kwananjengoko boyisa iintlanga kulawulo lwabo olungcwele endiya kuluphelisa ngonaphakade ekupheleni kwembandezelo kaYakobi.

Ukuba ngendlela efanayo, ekubuyeni kwam kwesibini, xa ndibuyela emhlabeni nomkhosi wasezulwini nabathembekileyo kuKristu kwanomtshakazi wam, ukuba ebulungiseni ndiya kugweba ndenze imfazwe ndibeke phantsi ngonaphakade nangonaphakade ngogonyamelo olukhulu namandla lonke ulawulo negunya nolongamo lwenamba! Kuba ayingokumkani, kwaye akanabukumkani kwaye ilifa lakhe likulawo malangatye angunaphakade esohlwayo apho impethu yabo ingafi, apho abotshelelwe khona emgwebeni ukuba aye ngonaphakade.

Kwaye ngoko ke uBukumkani bam beMinyaka eliwaka buya kumiselwa emhlabeni, kwanalawulwa nguMyolelo wam oMtsha wobulungisa ngonaphakade nangonaphakade. Selah!

Kwaye ngoko, mna Kristu Yesu, okwanguYesu uKristu, okwanguYeshua HaMashiach ndiyavakalisa ukuba amaxesha afikile kwaye abathembekileyo bamiselwe ukuba bahlanganiswe kwaye ukwahlulwa kokuqala kweziqhamo zokuqala zesivuno serhasi kuya kuvunwa kuthathwe kusiwe koovimba nokhuseleko lwamazulu apho niya kufumana nonke umvuzo wenu ongunaphakade kwizandla zam ezinamandla njengoko ukuthembeka kwenu bekunjalo.

NdinguMbingeleli oMkhulu ngokohlobo lukaMelkitsedeke ngonaphakade, kwaye emagxeni am ingubo kaMelkitsedeke ayisokuze imke! Selah!

NdinguNkosi noKumkani ongunaPhakade woBulungisa ngokwam: oya kubonakala ngequbuliso njengoko uzuko lwam nokukhanya kwam kubonwa kuwo wonke umhlaba, kwananjengoko umbane uphuma empuma ubonwa entshona kwaye abathembekileyo kuKristu baya kuhlanganiselwa ngequbuliso kwiNkosi yabo engunaPhakade yoBulungisa, kwaye elo xilongo lokugqibela liza kukhala ngokupheleleyo ezindlebeni zabathembekileyo, njengoko besiva: Nyukelani apha!

Kwaye ndinguNkosi engunaPhakade yoBulungisa ovala ubungqina bomthunywa wam onamandla othe wavakalisa ngokuthembekileyo aMazwi oBulungisa atyhilwe kuye ndim, noYahweh uMNA NDIKHONA Omkhulu. Ngoko ke, qondani ukuba umthunywa wam onamandla uya kuhlanganiswa nabathembekileyo kwaye uya kuzalisekisa iinjongo zakhe ezingunaphakade kwilifa lakhe eliya kunikwa sisandla sam kubo bonke aboyisayo ngendlela efanayo.

Kwaye njengoko ubungqina beVangeli engunaPhakade, kwanaleyo Ncwadi encinane yeSityhilelo Isahluko se-10 isondezwa esiphelweni, ndiyaninika ukuba niqonde ukuba ukusukela ekuqaleni kobungqina ngoMatshi 2 ka-2024, ukuya kuJanuwari 13, 2025 kuye kwabakho iinyanga ezilishumi neentsuku ezilishumi elinanye ngokwekhalenda kaGregory, kwaye ngoko ndiyaninika ukuba niqonde ukuba ngendlela efanayo iSityhilelo isahluko seshumi sivala ngendinyana yeshumi elinanye, la Mazwi afanayo anikwe ngokuqonda nobulumko: Wathi kum, Umele uphinde uprofete phakathi kwabantu neentlanga, neelwimi, nakooKumkani abaninzi.

Kwaye kuya kwenzeka kwananjengoko kubhaliwe: Ndabona esinye isiThunywa siphaphazela esazulwini sezulu, sineVangeli engunaphakade, ukuba siyishumayele kwabo bahleli emhlabeni, nakuzo zonke iintlanga, nemizalwane, neelwimi, nabantu: Sisithi ngelizwi elikhulu, Moyikeni uThixo, nimzukise: kuba ilixa lomgwebo wakhe lifikile: kwaye nimnqule lowo wenza izulu nomhlaba, nolwandle, nemithombo yamanzi. Selah!
