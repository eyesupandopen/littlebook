Yimani Ngokomelela Nizihlambulule

INkosi ethembekileyo ithi kwabanyuliweyo nabathembekileyo bayo: Yimani ngokomelela nizihlambulule ngokubeka phantsi nokuvuma izono zenu. Phumani kuyo, bantu bam, nahlukane nezono zayo ukuze ningabelani ngomgwebo nezibetho zayo. Ubani yona, uyabuza? AsiyoBhabhiloni Enkulu na, leyo umgwebo wayo ufikileyo? Asilohenyukazi elikhulu na elinxile ligazi labangcwele? Ukhuseleko noxolo ndiya kuninika lona, kwaye ndiya kunikhokelela ekhusini endlwini kaBawo de ixesha lidlule.

Ndim iNkosi uYesu Krestu, onibizele eluzukweni nasesulungwini, ngoko ke musani ukutyhafa ekwenzeni okulungileyo. Ndininikile ilizwi lokuthetha endaweni yam nokuvakalisa ukufika kwam okukufuphi. Kodwa abaninzi eBandleni lam balilahle ilizwi labo baza baba ngabangakholwayo kulindelo uBawo awalubekela nina eLizwini lakhe. Musani ukungakholwa kodwa kholwani kuphela. Khangelani imiqondiso yokuza kwam, njengoko uBawo eyivakalisile emazulwini kwaye sele isaziwa kakuhle. Kutheni nisacotha nje ukukholwa? Thembani kum, bantwana bam, kuba ndiyaninceda; ewe, ndithena lonke isebe elingathwali siqhamo kuni. Thembani kubuNkosi bam kuni kwaye nityhalele elusindisweni lwam njengoko nisebenzela usindiso lwenu ngokoyika nangokungcangcazela. Guqukelani kum ngomthandazo nindicele ukuba ndinibonise izono zenu ukuze nizivume kum kwaye nizoyise ngegazi lam. Ngoko musani ukoyika, kuba usindiso lwenu lukufuphi; ewe, usiba lwesibini sele luphantse lwagqiba ukujikeleza kwalo kokugqibela kwixesha lam lobabalo.

Guqukelani kum nize kum njengoko ndilungise indawo etafileni yam kuni kwaye ndilungise indawo yokuhlala endlwini kaBawo ukuze nisabe itsunami yeentlekele ezizayo. Ziyasondela kwabo bahlala emhlabeni, kwaye umgwebo omkhulu sele uza kuwa kuwo wonke umhlaba. Musani ukulilahla igama lam! Ndiyazi ukuba nidinwe kweli lixa lokugqibela, kodwa phumulani kum nifumane amandla kum kwaye nivakalise ukufika kwam okukufuphi. Andinikanga mini okanye lixa lokuba nililindele, kuba beniya kwakha ubomi benu bonke sijikeleze eso siganeko. Kodwa musani ukuzikhohlisa: umhla nelixa ziyaziwa emazulwini ngoku, kwaye iingelosi zam zikulungele, zilindele ukuvuthelwa kwexilongo xa ndiya kuqokelela abo baleleyo emhlabeni kuqala, kwaye abo baphilayo nabasalayo baya kuhlangana nabo ukuze bandikhawulelele esibhakabhakeni, kwaye ndiya kunisa kwilifa lenu uBawo alifihle kum. Kuphela musani ukutyhafa.

Kuya kwenzeka ngokuqhwanyaza kweliso, xa abaninzi benu beya kube beqhuba imihla yenu, kwaye kwangoko niya kuma phambi kwam njengoko ndiniveza kuBawo; kwaye siyalulangazelela olo suku njengoko sobabini silulangazelela ubukho benu. Ndingabonakala ngokwenene ndishumayele kwabaninzi, kwaye ngubani onokuthi andizange ndenjenjalo ngamanye amaxesha? Ngaba niya kuvakalisa oko iNkosi inokukwenza? Okanye niya kunciphisa amandla am nobuNkosi bam ngokungakholwa kwenu? Akunakwenzeka! Nangona kunjalo, ndinenze abasebenzi kunye nam kwaye ndinifakile emsebenzini uBawo awandinika wona ukuba ndiwufeze, kwaye yazini ukuba liwonga novuyo endiniphe lona ngenxa yosindiso lwenu, umvuzo wenu, nokugqibelela kwenu.

Njengoko nilungiselele abanye egameni lam, andinilungiselelanga na nani? Yiphani ngesisa kwaye musani ukuyigodla inkcazelo yokuza kwam, nangona abaninzi beya kuchasa kwaye niya kubalwa njengabaphambeneyo. Ubuxoki botshaba buya kunyibilika phambi kwamazwi enu azaliswe ngamandla njengoko nithetha endaweni yam kwaye nivakalisa ukubonakala kwam kwaye nimemeze umgwebo ozayo, njengoko nakule mizuzwana yokugqibela abanye beya kufumana usindiso. Abaninzi abakakulungeli kwaye baya kuwala amazwi enu; bathandeni kunjalo, njengoko isihlwele esikhulu siya kusindiswa kwiimvavanyo ezinkulu nembandezelo, kwaye yazini ukuba amazwi enu anokusebenza njengesibane sokukhanya nethemba kwiimvavanyo abanokujongana nazo.

Owu, bemi bomhlaba abalwalileyo ubizo lwam kwaye nizibale njengabangafanelekanga kwisipho sam sosindiso nako konke okuzisayo: Umgwebo omkhulu uvakaliswe emhlabeni kwaye sele uphantse wafika kuni, nangona nizixokisa, nisithi, "Ndinezicwangciso ezikhulu ngam kwaye ingomso liya kufana nanamhlanje!" Sidenge! Anikuqondi nokuguquka kwamaxesha onyaka, njengoko izulu limemeza umtshato womtshakazi wam kum emazulwini, kwaye izulu liguquka lisuka kubabalo lisiya emgwebeni phambi kwamehlo enu. Intlekele enkulu iya kuza kuni kungekudala, kwaye abaninzi baya kufa ezonweni zabo. Asikokuthanda kwam ukuba nitshabalale, kodwa niyalichitha idini lam elingenasono ngenxa yenu elalinokunisindisa ngokugqibeleleyo.

Ihlabathi enilaziyo namhlanje liya kunyamalala phambi kwamehlo enu, kwaye zonke iintywenka zesono kobu bomi aziyi kuba ngabahlobo benu; kodwa niya kusukelwa nizingelwe ngumkhosi weentshaba zenu ezinqwenela ukufa kwenu kwaye zilangazelela impahla yenu. Kuya kubakho ukuzamazama okukhulu phantsi kweenyawo zenu, kwaye umhlaba uya kucandeka, kwaye abaninzi baya kuwela emhlabeni. Iinkokeli zenu sele ziningcatshile, nangona ngobudenge nisajonge kuzo, nizama ukucinga ngevoti yenu. Iinqaba zenu ziya kuwa, neebhulorho zenu ziya kudilika, kwaye iziseko zophuhliso zenu ziya kunyamalala phambi kwamehlo enu, kwaye akukho nto iya kuphinda ifane.

Niya kubalekela phi xa nilahlile iNkosi engcwele, mna, iNkosi uYesu Krestu? Ngubani oya kunisindisa ngoku kwixesha lenu lomgwebo? Owu, kodwa uthi, andifanelanga mgwebo, uthetha ukuthini? Animgatyanga na uMdali wenu xa endithumele kuni ngenxa yobabalo lwakhe olusindisayo, ubabalo olusindisayo enizibala njengabangafanelekanga ukulufumana? Kutheni nindigatyile? Ndenze tyala lini kuni? Ndinikele ngethontsi lam lokugqibela legazi kwintuthumbo enkulu nakubuhlungu ukuze niphile phambi kwam, kodwa anikakholwa ngamangqina am endiwathume egameni lam.

Naxa ndinilumkisa ngentlekele yenu esondelayo, mna noBawo asinayo nayiphi na intywenka koko sele kuza kukhululwa. Kodwa njengoko nonwatyiswe sisono, ngoku niya kuginjwa niphila ziziphumo eniziqwebela zona. Njengoko ninqwenela imali, impahla, nodumo lwezemali, anenzanga nto ngaphandle kokufumba ingqumbo phezu kwetafile yenu yesidlo sangokuhlwa, kwaye niya kudla imbandezelo nosizi. Ndiya kuninika ubumnyama enibuthanda kakhulu. Ubumnyama buya kuwa phezu kwelizwe lenu kwaye buya kugubungela umhlaba, ubumnyama obukhulu kangangokuba ilanga lasemini liya kubonakala ngathi licinyiwe. Nithi kum, sono sini endinaso sokuzisa ingqumbo yakho; asenzanga misebenzi mininzi inamandla egameni lakho?

Inene, nenze imisebenzi emininzi, kodwa ayisiyo misebenzi endininike yona; hayi, nibe ngabaprofeti bobuxoki, niprofeta amazwi asuka enyameni yenu egwenxa. Hayi, nenza imisebenzi yenu ngenxa yozuko lwenu, njengoko nenze isisu senu uthixo wenu. Nibambe amazwi alungileyo neentetho ezimnandi zabantu abakhohlakeleyo ngaphezu kweLizwi lam elingcwele. Andililo na iLizwi elenziwe inyama? Ngewadla mna isonka sobomi wasinda entshabalalweni, ungaphindi ulambe. Ngewasela emilanjeni yamanzi aphilayo aqukuqela kum; ewe, ngewasela umoya wam ungaphindi unxanwe. Kodwa ekundaleni kwenu, niya kungena kwindlala enkulu nasekubandezelekeni, nangona kulo mzuzu kanye nisacinga ukuba ayiyi kuza kuni ngephanyazo. Uqoqosho lwenu lutshatyalalisiwe, kwaye ubutyebi enibunqwenelayo buya kuba yinto engenaxabiso kwangoko. Nibeke ukholo lwenu kubaphathi bobu bumnyama bangoku kwaye nixabise ubuxoki bukaSathana ngaphezu kokukhanya kwenyaniso yam ehlala ikhona.

Ngoko ke, niya kufuna indawo etafileni yabo, nize nifumanise ukuba akukho ndawo ibekelwe nina. Baya kuba nentabalala abaziqwebela yona, kwaye nina niya kufikelela kuphela ngokuthatha uphawu lwerhamncwa. Nangona irhamncwa lingekatyhilwa ngokupheleleyo ngexesha lalo, ndilumkisile ngalo kwaye ndalichaza kwabathembekileyo bam ukuze bangakhohliswa ngamazwi alo alungileyo neentetho ezimnandi, nangona ngeli xesha lingenabukumkani, okwangoku. Musani ukuthatha uphawu lwalo, njengoko bonke abakwenzayo baya kuba nesabelo sabo ecaleni kwerhamncwa nomprofeti wobuxoki echibini lomlilo, kwaye umsi wentuthumbo yabo uya kunyuka ngonaphakade.

Nilunyukisiwe, kwaye nangona ndisafuna ukunihlangula entshabalalweni yenu, nisalwala ngenkani ubizo lwam. Ngoko ke, ndiya kuninika isiqhamo seendlela zenu.

Niya kufumana indlala enkulu, imfazwe enkulu, intshabalalo enkulu. Imikhosi ebomvu iya kunisukela, inithuthumbise, kwaye itshabalalise abaninzi benu; kwaye kuya kwenzeka ukuba ningqingwe ziingozi nentshabalalo. Lowo ungathinjwa aye entuthumbisweni nasekufeni ekugqibeleni uya kuwa likrele; lowo ungawi likrele uya kuhlangana namarhamncwa asendle, aya kubulala abaninzi; lowo ungafi ngamarhamncwa asendle uya kubulawa yindlala, kwaye iindlela zokufa ziya kwanda elizweni.

Imbandezelo enkulu, intlekele, intshabalalo iya kuza kuni ngephanyazo, kwaye amaza amakhulu olwandle aya kuza phezu kwesizwe senu. Anisayi kuphinda nibe sisizwe esinamandla, kodwa insali isaya kusindiswa. Nabani na obiza egameni leNkosi uya kusindiswa, kodwa lowo uthanda ubomi bakhe kude kube sekupheleni uya kuba nesabelo sakhe echibini lomlilo, kwaye niya kwabelana ngentuthumbo nesohlwayo sikaSathana kuba nibambelele kwihlabathi lakhe elifayo kude kube sekuphefumleni kwenu kokugqibela kwenkani, kwaye ngokuqinisekileyo niya kutshabalala neli hlabathi lifayo. Guqukelani kum ngoku kusekho ixesha ukuze ndinisindise nani neentsapho zenu kule ntlekele izayo. Kanye njengoko isihlwele safumana usindiso ngomhla wePentekoste kwiminyaka emininzi eyadlulayo kwimizuzu yokuqala yeli xesha, ngokunjalo isihlwele siya kusindiswa kwimizuzu yokugqibela yeli xesha, kwaye isimemo sam sisemi ixesha elifutshane kakhulu.

Ububi obuzayo abuyi kuhlonipha abafazi okanye abantwana okanye abalupheleyo okanye abagulayo. Hayi, obo bubi buya kudla konke okunokukwenza buse kwintshabalalo enkulu nakubuhlungu. Balekelani kum ngoku kusekho ixesha, njengoko iwotshi sele iza kuma, kwaye akusayi kubakho xesha. USathana uya kuphoswa emhlabeni kungekudala, kwaye ubukumkani bakhe buya kukhululwa ngokupheleleyo kwihlabathi lenu, kwaye unomsindo omkhulu kuba uyazi ukuba unexesha elifutshane. Guqukelani kum ngoku nisengakwazi ukubusaba ububi obuzayo.

Ndim lowo uyalela imikhosi yasezulwini, njengoko uBawo ebeke umyalelo wayo ezandleni zam.

Ndim iLizwi elenziwe inyama elahlala phakathi kwenu.

Ndim ingcambu kaYese, kwaye ubukumkani bam abunaphelo.

Ndim unyana kaThixo oze enyameni ukucamagushela izono zabo bonke, ukuba nje bonke bebengamkela.

Abo balwamkeleyo ubabalo lwam kwaye bathembekile baya kulawula nam ebukumkanini bam kwaye babe nesabelo etroneni yam, njengoko ndinenze ookumkani nababingeleli phambi koThixo wethu. Kukho umvuzo omkhulu kuni ogcinwe emazulwini; kuphela yimani niqinile kwaye nithembekile imizuzu embalwa nje ngaphezulu.
