Kutheni Nimlahla Nje UMdali Wenu?

INkosi indinike la mazwi. Asingawo awam, kwaye ndiyaninika wona ndingongezanga nto kwaye ndingasusanga nto ngokwam amandla onke.

Kutheni nisemlahla nje uMdali wenu neendlela zakhe? Akakunikelanga na ngobomi obungunaphakade? Akakuniki na isonka sobomi, simahla kubo bonke abacelayo? Akakunikelanga na ngenkululeko kule ndalo iwileyo kum, mna Nkosi enkulu nethembekileyo uYesu Krestu? Ndim indlela, inyaniso, nobomi. NdinguKumkani wooKumkani neNkosi yeeNkosi, yaye ndanikela ngobomi bam emnqamlezweni ngenxa yenu, ukuze ninyanise ukuba ningahamba ebukumkanini bam ukuba nithe nasamkela isipho sobomi obungunaphakade. Andikubizanga na ngegama? Kutheni nisazibala njengabangafanelwe bubomi obungunaphakade, lo gama igazi lam lentlawulelo liza kunenza nifaneleke ukuba nithe nalibiza igama lam naza nandenza iNkosi? NdinguNyana kaThixo, uNyana woMntu, uKrestu: uYesu waseNazarete, iNkosi ethembekileyo, ewe, iNkosi enkulu! Akukho mkhulu ngaphandle koThixo, ondinike zonke izinto.

Yabonani! Umhla wentlekele uphezu kwenu, umhla womgwebo nosizi. Izono zaseMelika zifana nezono zeSodom neGomora, kwaye ukuba ebengayi kuligweba ihenyukazi elikhulu, ebeya kuba akalunganga kwizizukulwana awazithabathayo emgwebeni. Elo henyukazi likhulu, elinqwenela inyama yasemzini, elibe yinxalenye ebalulekileyo ekwakheni inkqubo yerhamncwa yaloo nyana wentshabalalo, inene, ohamba phakathi kwenu ekhohlisa abanyulwa bam nangaphambi kwexesha lakhe. Umgwebo omkhulu nentlekele ziya kuwa ngesiquphe. Melika, ugwetyiwe ngaphandle kwaye ugwetyiwe ngaphakathi.

Isandla seentshaba zakho sele siza kuba sentanyeni yakho, kwaye uya kubhukuqwa ngogonyamelo, kwaye iintlanga aziyi kuphinda zisele kwimibulo yakho yokukrexeza othe wathakatha ngayo bonke. Iintshaba zakho zinikwe uloyiso phezu kwakho, nangona uthembele ngobumfama kumkhosi wakho endiwushenxisileyo kuwe. Uluhlanga olusemakhonkcweni, olulindele inkumbulo yakho yokugqibela phambi kweNkosi! Akukho nto inkulu ngawe, kodwa izono zakho nobugwenxa bakho livumba elibi ezimpumlweni zikaBawo.

Uya kugwetywa ngaphakathi, njengoko ukushukuma okunamandla, okunogonyamelo kuya kukutshabalalisa ngesiquphe kwaye kungabikho nyango. Ihenyukazi elikhulu liya kuwiswa phantsi njengesilumkiso kubo bonke kwaye liya kuba sisiqalo seyure emnyama kakhulu eyakha yakho. Alisayi kuphinda libhukuqe iintlanga ngokungekho sikweni.

Alisayi kuphinda lihambise isibetho kwihlabathi ngobugwenxa balo. Alisayi kuphinda amanyundululu alo alukuhle abantu bam. Ihenyukazi elikhulu liwungcolisile umnqophiso kaBawo, loo mnyama uzukileyo awawunika uNowa njengesiqinisekiso sokuba akasayi kuphinda alikhukulise ihlabathi. Nangona ihlabathi lingasayi kuphinda likhukuliswe, amaza entshabalalo aya kuwa phezu kwabaninzi, kwaye umlilo odla konke uyasondela.

Bantu bam, maningabi ngabathabathi-nxaxheba ezonweni zalo, kwizibetho, emgwebeni, nasentlekele. Ndisaza kunibekela indawo etafileni yam ukuba nithe nabuyela kum ngentliziyo yenu iphela.

Guqukani! Zivumeni izono zenu kum, Krestu Yesu! Anifundanga na ukuba abangemalungisa abayi kubudla ilifa uBukumkani bukaThixo? Ninqwenela na ukuba umvuzo wenu uhlutshwe kwaye unikwe omnye? Musani ukucinga ukuba niya kungena ebukhosini bam nikwimeko yokungathobeli ngenjongo; uBawo akatshongo na, "Ndingcwele, ngoko ke yibani ngcwele?" Balekelani kum nize nizithobe elusindisweni lwam, kuba ndiyiNkosi yeeNkosi noKumkani wooKumkani, kwaye imihla yokubongoza isizukulwana esineentamo ezilukhuni iyeza esiphelweni.

Kukade kangakanani ndolula isandla sam esisindisayo kuni, kanti nimlahla uThixo wooyihlo nobabalo lwam olusindisayo olunako ukunisindisa ngokugqibeleleyo? Yizani kum nize nizifihle esifubeni sam; inene, ndiya kunifihla kumhla omkhulu wentlekele ophezu kwenu. Musani ukuzimanya nabo balikhanyele igama lam; uKrestu akanamvisiswano noBheliyali, kanti abantu bam basathakathwe kukukhanya kwakhe kobuxoki. Lilani nenze isijwili niguquke kwiindlela zenu zokudikidiki. Buyelani eluthandweni lwenu lokuqala nize nifune ubuso bam kusekho ixesha.

Igazi lam lentlawulelo liya kunihlamba ukuba nithe naphuma ezonweni zenu. NdinguNyana woMntu, umbingeleli omkhulu ngonaphakade ngokohlobo lukaMelkitsedeke. Ndim lowo wazigcina enyulu ebusweni bazo zonke izilingo, ndada ndalingwa nguSathana ngokwakhe ndaza ndasoloko ndisoyisa. Ndim okuphela koNyana kaThixo ozelweyo othulule umoya oyingcwele phezu kwabo bonke abathe baguqukela kum nabo bonke abasaza kuguqukela kum. Mna, Yesu Krestu, ndizizo zonke ezi zinto nangaphezulu, kwaye ndiyabohlwaya abo ndibathandayo ukuze ubizo lwabo lube luqinisekile kwaye lugqibelele.

Owu mntu, izono zakho zininzi, kanti uvakalisa ubungcwele bakho! Izono zokukrexeza nombulo. Izono ze-pharmakeia, ubugqwirha, noosiyazi abaninzi. Ababawayo, abangenanceba, abazithandayo, abathandi beziyolo, nabathandi bemali. Kuya kuthini na ngokholo lwenu ebutyebini xa ubutyebi benu buba yinto engenaxabiso ngoko nangoko kwaye intlupheko nenkangantso enkulu ifika phezu kwenu ngesiquphe? Niya kunyelisa na ebusweni benceba? Niya kusazikhethela na izono zenu? Abo bakhetha izono zabo ngenkani baya kutshabalala kuzo. Musani ukucinga ukuba uThixo uyahlekwa, njengoko esezakwenza intlekisa ngabahleki; maningabi ngabathabathi-nxaxheba kunye nabo.

Ixesha lihambile, kwaye imizuzu yokugqibela iyasondela kwaye ixesha sele liza kuphela kwixesha lam lobabalo, kwaye ingqumbo iya kulithatha ihlabathi njengoko iBandla lokwenyani lithathwa phambi kwamehlo enu. Andinayolo ekuboneni indalo yam itshatyalaliswa ngenxa yezigqitho nokungalungisi; kodwa andinikanga na ukhetho lokuphila ebukumkanini bam, kanti abaninzi bayala ngenkani? Phumani kulo! Zikhalimeleni nize nizilahle iindlela zenu; nivume mna, Yesu Krestu, njengeNkosi, nize niyeke ukona.

Andibanikanga na uxolo olukhulu oonyana bam neentombi zam, abazoyisileyo izono zabo ngegazi lam kanye? Kanti abaninzi basabukhanyela ubungqina nofakazo lwabo kwaye basala ukukholwa, bekhetha endaweni yoko ikratshi labo kuneendlela zam ezithobekileyo. Iindlela zenu zingumgibe kwaye ziya kunisa kwintshabalalo epheleleyo, eningasokuze niphiliswe kuyo. Guqukelani kum kusekho imizuzwana embalwa eseleyo yokwenza oko. Ixesha lihambile, kwaye bonke abanqula igama leNkosi baya kusindiswa!

Ndiyanimema ukuba nibe nenxaxheba ebuqaqawulini betrone yam nize namkele ubomi kunye nam. Jikani kwiindlela zenu nize nibuyele kum, njengoko ndiyiNkosi engunaphakade kwaye akukho lusindiso mntwini ngaphandle kwam! Mna, Yesu Krestu, ndinguNyana kaThixo kwaye ndize ngenyama, ndabethelelwa emnqamlezweni, ndavuswa ngomhla wesithathu, ndenziwa umoya opha ubomi ndaza ndamkelwa emazulwini de amaxesha azalisekiswe. Inene, ubusuku buhambile kakhulu kwaye ilixa lihambile, kwaye amaxesha okubuyiselwa ayasondela.

Bantu bam, yimani niqine emandleni am nibe seluxolweni. Phumulani kum kwaye kwithutyana elingephi uzuko lwenu luya kufika ngoko nangoko, kwaye umvuzo omkhulu nilungiselelwe wona endlwini kaBawo. Izinto endinilungiselele zona azikangeni nasentliziyweni yenu, inene, hayi entliziyweni yamntu. Uxolo nokhuseleko niya kulufumana, kwaye nina ebenithembekile ezintweni ezimbalwa ndiya kunivuza kakhulu. Phumulani kum nize nigcine ingqwalasela yenu kum. Miselani uzilo lokutya phakathi kwenu nize nihlaziye amandla enu kweli lixa lokugqibela nize nivakalise iindlela zam ezilungileyo nize nivakalise umgwebo wam osondelayo, njengoko kwananje abaninzi baya kusindiswa.

Ndinibonise inceba enkulu, ukuzeka kade umsindo okukhulu, ubabalo olukhulu oluphuphumayo, kanti anisezoluleli ezi zinto kwabanye. Niya kuwuncama na umvuzo wenu nize nindinyanzele ukuba ndiwunike omnye? Okanye niya kumthanda uThixo ngentliziyo yenu iphela, ngomphefumlo wenu wonke, ngengqondo yenu iphela, nangamandla enu onke nommelwane wenu njengoko nizithanda? Kutheni nimenza into engekhoyo ummelwane wenu nize nimgwebe ngephutha njengongafanelwe bubomi obungunaphakade lo gama nime apho nithe cwaka? Andininikanga na iimfihlelo zam ukuba nizivakalise kubo bonke? Yimani niqine kum, kwaye ndiya kunixhobisa ukuba nifikelele kwabo lowo ungendawo ebesaza kuzama ukubafihla esandleni sam esisindisayo. Vulani imilomo yenu nize nithethe onke amazwi obu bomi kwaye amazwi kanye eniya kuwathetha ndiya kuninika. Yabelanani ngothando lwenu kude kube ngumzuzwana wokugqibela, kuba loo mzuzwana wokugqibela usondela ngokuzinzileyo njengoko ixilongo seliza kubiza abantwana bam ekhaya.

Ndim iNkosi ethembekileyo engasileliyo.

Ndim ukukhanya kwehlabathi, kwaye nabani na onqula igama lam elinamandla akayi kutshabalala emalangatyeni angenakucinywa; kwabanjalo, ukufa kwesibini akunamandla.

Ndim onamandla kunabo bonke abanamandla, kwaye zonke izinto ezulwini nasemhlabeni zinikelwe ezandleni zam de ukubuyiselwa kugqitywe, xa iintshaba zam zisisihlalo seenyawo zam. Ndim indlela, inyaniso, nobomi.

NdinguKumkani wooKumkani neNkosi yeeNkosi.

NdinguNyana kaThixo, uNyana woMntu, uKrestu: uYesu waseNazarete. Ndingcwele, ngoko ke yibani ngcwele.

Ndim umbingeleli omkhulu ngonaphakade ngokohlobo lukaMelkitsedeke. Ndim lowo wazigcina enyulu ebusweni bazo zonke izilingo.

Ndim iNkosi engunaphakade, kwaye akukho lusindiso mntwini ngaphandle kwam!
