Yeha Nini Balusi Abawexukileyo!

Yeha nini, balusi abawexukileyo, kuba nityale amabandla enu obuxoki kuwo onke amazwe, kwaye anithethi egameni lam, kananjalo andinithumanga kule misebenzi nizithathele yona. Kwaye niyenze imali uThixo wenu, kuba nithembele kwindyorho emdaka, kwaye nibafundisile abantu bam ukuba inzuzo yezinto eziphathekayo bubuthixo. Yeha nini, balusi abawexukileyo, kuba amazwi kaYeremiya anichaza ngokuchanekileyo: I-esile lasendle eliqhele intlango, elisebeza umoya ngokuzithandela kwalo; ngexesha lalo ngubani onokulijika? Kuba ukukrexeza kwenu nokurheletya kwenu kukunye nobubi, kuba nikhethe ooSathana njengothixo wenu, kwaye ezo nkosana zingenaxabiso asingoothixo. Ningabalungiseleli bobuxoki abazenza abalungiseleli bobulungisa, kwaye akumangalisi, kuba uSathana uziguqula abe yingelosi yokukhanya.

Yeha nini, balusi abawexukileyo, kuba nityale amabandla enze amanzi am amnandi obomi abe ngumhlonyane okrakra, eniwasevisayo kwabo ndibabizileyo, amanzi adikidiki amdaka akrakra esiswini. Kwanamanzi enu akrakra anikwa ngemithombo eyaphukileyo, kwaye aninamandla, nangona niqhayisa ngamandla amakhulu. Niwangcolisile amazwe ngeemfundiso zobuxoki kwaye nakha amabandla enu obuxoki njengeentolongo zokomoya ezonakaliswe ngurhulumente wenu, kuba niwuthengisile umhlambi wam esandleni sotshaba; ngoko ke, phumani kuye ngoku, bantu bam! Amabandla enu angenaxabiso awexukileyo asetyenziswa njengamaziko olawulo apho nizakuzisa ukufa nentshabalaliso kwabo bashiyekileyo, kwaye nizakuhlawula.

Nimenze uRemfan uThixo wenu. Nikhonza uBhahali. Nibingelela abantwana kuMoloki. Nonakele ngokupheleleyo. Ningamaqula angenamanzi, izitya ezingenanto ezilungele intshabalaliso, kuba ilifa lenu sisulfure evuthayo, kwaye nizakuba kwelo chibi likhulu kunye noyihlo, uMtyholi. Ningabapostile bobuxoki, abaprofeti bobuxoki, abavangeli bobuxoki, abefundisi bobuxoki, nabafundisi bobuxoki; kwaye abo nibaguqulela kwiindlela zenu ezitshabalalisayo benziwa babe ngabantwana besihogo ngokuphindwe kabini kunani, kuba ningabobubi obonakalisa ngokupheleleyo. Kuba uStefano waxela kwangaphambili ngeendlela zenu xa wayethetha ngezinyanya zenu, kwanomdiza owakhathaza uSirayeli, kuba wathi: Ewe, nathabatha uMnquba kaMoloki, nenkwenkwezi kathixo wenu uRemfan, imifanekiso enayenzayo, ukuze niyinqule. Nina balusi abawexukileyo nide niyiphakamise ngenkankulu inkwenkwezi kaRemfan eniyayamanisa ngokungazi noDavide, isinyanya sam; kuba mna, Yesu Krestu, ndizalwa kuloo mnombo, kwaye ndizakulawula kwitrone kaDavide ngobulungisa. Kuba ndingulowo unikwe ulawulo olunjalo, kuba ndizakulawula umhlaba ngobulungisa!

Yeha nini, balusi abawexukileyo, ndiza kunibuyisela ngokupheleleyo ngazo zonke iintetho zenu ezimbi, kwaye zonke iindlela zenu ezimbi ziya kubuyela phezu kweentloko zenu ngonaphakade. Kuba nizakuhlangana noMtyholi neengelosi zakhe kwilifa lazo elivela kuThixo Osenyangweni; kwanaye uYehova unilungiselele ichibi elikhulu lomlilo ongacimekiyo. Nibalela usindiso kwabo babefuna, kodwa yazini ukuba ndiza kubasindisa kwaye nina nizakuhlawula. Niwukhanyele umoya wam kwabo bebeya kulivuma igama lam ngokubanika amazwi obuxoki okuba bawathethe nemithandazo yobuyatha, engenantsingiselo nephindaphindwayo engasindisiyo. Ndive, bantu bam: usindiso luza ngokuvuma uYesu njengeNkosi yenu nokukholelwa ukuba uThixo wandivusa kwabafileyo, kwaye kuphela kubudlelwane obusindisayo nam apho ninokusindiswa khona; kuba ndiyalubamba uphawu lukaThixo kwabo bavuma igama lam ngentliziyo engenakholo, kuba ndiphengulula iintliziyo ndilinge nezintso.

Nicinga ngenkukhumezo ukuba ngenxa yokuba nindibiza ngeNkosi phambi kwebandla lenu elihluthiweyo ndiyakwazi? Ndiza kuthi kuni ngaloo mini, mkani kum, nina benzi bobubi, kuba andizange ndinazi! Nifundisa ubuxoki endaweni yenyaniso, kwaye nilahlekisa abo bandifunayo, mna Nkosi Yesu Krestu, nibasa kwivangeli eyonakeleyo, yobuyatha, kuba akukho yimbi ivangeli: Kuba iindaba ezilungileyo zikum, Krestu Yesu! Ukubhedengu kwenu okungenantsingiselo kuza kuphela, kwaye ingqumbo yam ishushu phezu kwenu, kwaye aniyi kusinda. Ndiyayibona yonke intshukumo yonyeliso eniyenzayo; ndiyaliva lonke ilizwi lonyeliso enilithethayo egameni lam, kuba ningumzenzisi. Niyazenzisa ukuba nithetha amazwi am, njengoko nizenzisa ukuba ndinithumile ngamabali enu okuzenzisa nemimangaliso yenu yokuzenzisa nokuphiliswa kwenu okucwangcisiweyo, kuba ningumzenzisi!

Yeha nini, balusi abawexukileyo, kuba niba kwabo bebezakuza kum, nibaxelela ubuxoki bokuba bafuneka nguThixo ukuba baninike imali. Nizijija ngokungazi iZibhalo zam eziNgcwele, nixelela abo nibalahlekisileyo ukuba kufuneka banikele isishumi kuni. Kuba ndamxelela ntoni uPawulos xa wayebhalela abo baseKorinte: Kodwa ndithi, Lowo uhlwayela kancinane, uya kuvuna kancinane: nalowo uhlwayela kakhulu, uya kuvuna kakhulu. Umntu ngamnye njengoko egqibe entliziyweni yakhe, makanike; kungabi ngokukrokra, okanye ngokunyanzelwa: kuba uThixo uthanda umniki ochwayitileyo. Kodwa hayi, nifundisa ubuxoki endaweni yenyaniso kwaye niba abantu bam, nide nithumele imali eniyiqokelelayo ngeenjongo zikaSathana, kwaye nizakuhlawula, kuba umsindo wam ubomvu krwe phezu kwenu kuba nikhokelela abantu abandifunayo, mna Yesu Krestu, kude nosindiso nibase entshabalalisweni. Kodwa yazini oku, balusi abawexukileyo, anisoze nixwitheni namnye kwaba ezandleni zam, kuba kubo bonke uBawo andinike bona, ndalahlekelwa ngomnye kuphela, unyana wentshabalaliso, kwanaye uYuda, kuba wakhetha intshabalaliso yakhe kunobomi kum! Imisebenzi yenu nemigudu yenu ililize, owu balusi abawexukileyo; niziqwebele umgwebo omkhulu nengqumbo. Kuba ndathetha ngeendlela zezinyanya zenu, kwaye ningoabo badla izindlu zabahlolokazi baze ngenxa yokuzenzisa benze imithandazo emide: NINA niya kwamkela ukugwetywa okukhulu!

Yeha nini, balusi abawexukileyo, kuba nikhokelele abantu bam ukuba baxhase oorhulumente behlabathi abangengabo abam, nangona umhlaba ungowam. Owu, nina balusi abawexukileyo, nibhekisa abantu bam kwiinkokeli zehlabathi ezingoMtyholi, nibaxelela ukuba zithunyelwe nguThixo. Nilahlekisa abantu bacinge ukuba andiyi kubasikelela ngaphandle kokuba basikelele abo benza ukubulawa kwabantu abamsulwa. Kodwa ndithini esibhalweni ngani, kuba oku akukutsha, kwaye baninzi abeza phambi kwenu ngenkohliso, nangona nina niyiziyatha ngokwaneleyo ukuba nicinge ukuba ningabokuqala. Ndive, bantwana bam, kuba ndibutyhila obu bubi ngenxa yenu. Kuba isibhalo sam sithi: Nina ningabakaThixo, bantwana abancinane, kwaye niboyisile: kuba mkhulu lowo ukuni, kunalowo usehlabathini. Bona bangabehlabathi: ngoko bathetha okwehlabathi, kwaye ihlabathi liyabeva. Iintshumayelo zenu zehlabathi, izenzo zenu zehlabathi, iyelenqe lenu lehlabathi noSathana, konke kuya kufika esiphelweni esiqubulayo, kwaye aniyi kuphinda nivuke. Kodwa wena, Mtshakazi wam, ungowam, kwaye la mazwi awangawenu, kuba uya kungena kulonwabo lweNkosi yakho, kwanam, Krestu Yesu.

Ndithetha ngoku kumabandla amabandla awexukileyo akhokelwa ngabalusi abawexukileyo, kwanani, bandla lam laseLawodike. Ndiyayazi imisebenzi yakho, ukuba akubandi kwaye akushushu; ngamana ubungabanda okanye ushushu. Kuba uPawulos wabhalela uTimoti ngani nabanye abafana nani: Kuba ixesha liya kufika apho bengayi kuyinyamezela imfundiso ephilileyo, kodwa ngokweenkanuko zabo baya kuzifumbela abafundisi, beneendlebe ezirhawuzelayo: Kwaye baya kuzisonga iindlebe zabo kwinyaniso, baze bajikiselwe kwiintsomi. Uninzi lwenu luya kula mabandla awexukileyo phantsi koxinzelelo loluntu nokuzenzisa ukuba niyandifuna, mna Krestu Yesu, nto leyo ngokuqinisekileyo ningayenziyo.

Ninqwenela ukonwatyiswa, kuba ukonwatyiswa kukunqwenela kwenu, kwaye ukonwatyiswa kuye kwaba sisithixo senu! Kwaye aniyi kundiva, mna Yesu Krestu, kananjalo aniyi kumamela namnye kubathunywa bam ngaphandle kokuba iindlebe zenu ziyakutikitwa, kodwa amazwi am akatikitisi iindlebe! Nina mabandla awexukileyo: Ndive kwaye nindive kakuhle, kuba ndathi kwincwadi enkulu yeSiTyhilelo: Ndeva elinye ilizwi livela ezulwini, lisithi, Phumani kuye, bantu bam, ukuze ningabi ngabathathi-nxaxheba ezonweni zakhe, nokuze ningamkeli kwizibetho zakhe: Kuba izono zakhe zifikelele ezulwini, kwaye uThixo uzikhumbule izenzo zakhe zobubi. Phumani kuye, okanye niya kwabelana ngezibetho zakhe nomgwebo, kuba ixesha lifikile. KumaLawodike am: Shiyani amabandla enu awexukileyo ngoku, kuba ixesha lisondele, kwaye ndiyanilumkisa, nangona uninzi lwenu luvilapha kakhulu ukuba lwenze ngokwamazwi am.

Musa ukuthandabuza kwisilumkiso sam, kuba ndamnika ntoni uYohane ukuba ayibhale kwileta yakhe yesibini? Nabani na owaphulayo kwaye angahlali kwimfundiso kaKrestu, akanaye uThixo: lowo uhlala kwimfundiso kaKrestu, unaye uYise noNyana. Ukuba kukho nabani na oza kuni, engayizisi le mfundiso, musani ukumamkela endlwini yenu, kananjalo ningambulisi. Kuba lowo umbulisayo, uthatha inxaxheba kwizenzo zakhe ezimbi. Kodwa nina maLawodike anithobeli kangangokuba niyawatyeshela la mazwi kwaye endaweni yokubanqwenelela impumelelo, nto leyo imbi ngokwaneleyo, niqhubela phambili ebubini kwaye nibamemela ezindlwini zenu kwaye nibanika ukufikelela kubantwana benu, kwaye ninetyala ngokupheleleyo phambi kwam, ngoko GUQUKANI!

Kuba ngenyaniso, nivulele ihlabathi neendlela zalo iingcango zenu; ningenise iingcuka, kwanabona baphantsi kwabaphantsi, ukuba badle abantwana bam, kwaye ngaphandle kokuba niguquke, nizakuhlawula. Guqukani okanye ndiza kulwa nani, kuba ningabathathi-nxaxheba kwizenzo zabo ezimbi, kwaye anindithobeli, mna Krestu Yesu, kwanalowo uza kunihlutha umvuzo wenu aze awunike omnye ukuba niyaqhubeka nokungandihoyi. Kuba ubabalo lwam nenceba yam ziza kuphela, kwaye ingqumbo iya kuhlala endaweni yazo, nangona ubabalo nenceba zihleli zinam, kwaye nabani na obiza egameni leNkosi kude kube sekupheleni uya kusindiswa. Kuba aninakuba nabudlelwane neendlela ezingavelisi siqhamo zobumnyama, kodwa kufuneka nizikhalimele.

Kodwa anazi nto kangangokuba nicinga ukuba ngenxa yokuba umntu exoka kwaye esebenzisa ngokunyelisayo igama lam elingcwele kwimisebenzi yakhe engcwele ukuba uthunywe ndim kwaye uthetha amazwi am. Yivani amazwi am, kuba ngeziqhamo zabo niya kubazi! Buyelani kwiLizwi lam elinyulu likaThixo nifunde ngam, kuba anindazi kwaye ilizwi lam lingaqhelekanga kuni, kwaye aninaye uYise kuba aninaye uNyana. Jikani nize kum ngenguquko, kwaye ndiya kunamkela. Hlalani kwilaphu elirhwexayo nothuthu ezintliziyweni zenu kwaye nizithobe ukuze ndiniphakamise! Ndinibizele ukuba noyise, kwaye ngoku lithuba lenu lokugqibela lokoyisa aba balusi bawexukileyo neendlela zabo ezimbi; kuba ndiyeza, kwaye ndizisa ingqumbo enkulu kwiBhabhiloni eyimfihlo.

Ixesha yinto eninqabileyo eningasenayo. Aninakuba savilapha kwakhona, okanye niya kuzifumana kwingxaki kaYakobi, xa bendiya kunibekela indawo etafileni yam ndize ndinitshate njengomtshakazi wam, kodwa anithembekanga nisezandleni zomnye, kwanaye uSathana! Ngoko yizani kum ukuze ndinibuyisele kumvuzo wenu. Akwanelanga ukubaleka ngekratshi; kufuneka niguquke kobu bubi kwaye nitshintshe iindlela zenu kwaye nivume ezi zono kum, kwaye ndiya kunihlambulula ngegazi lam lentlawulelo ndize ndisule amabala kuni ngobunyulu bam obugqibeleleyo, kuba ndicocekile kwaye iingubo zam zimhlophe njengekhephu elitsha. Buyelani kum kwaye ngonaphakade niya kuba nam; phumani nize ezingalweni zam ezoluliweyo, kuba ndilapha ukunixelela ukuba aninalo ixesha lokuchitha.

KuMtshakazi wam othembekileyo, ophulaphule ilizwi lam walandela iindlela zam: Kuwe ndithi, qhubeka nokuthembeka kwakho kum, kwaye ukuba kukho namnye kuni ononyawo emnyango webandla laseLawodike, ndiyakuyalela ukuba ulukhuphe emnyango kwaye uvumele ucango luvalwe! Kuba aninakwenza oko iNkosi yenu ingayi kukwenza! Aniyifundanga na ileta yam eya kwibandla laseLawodike kwiSiTyhilelo? Yabona, ndimi emnyango, ndinkqonkqoza: ukuba kukho umntu ova ilizwi lam, aze avule ucango, ndiya kungena kuye, ndidle naye, naye adle nam. Qaphela, anditshongo ukuba ndiya kungena kwiBandla laseLawodike, kodwa ukuba kukho nawuphi na umntu kwibandla laseLawodike ova ilizwi lam aze avule ucango, ndiya kungena ndidle naye, hayi ibandla lakhe langaphambili eliwexukileyo, nangona ndibabiza nabo ukuba banokuphulaphula kuphela. Mtshakazi wam, umkhonzi akamkhulu kunenkosi yakhe, ngoko landela iindlela zam.

Mtshakazi wam, ndiphantse ndifike apho ukuze ndinixhwilele emazulwini, ngoko qhubekani nizilungiselela kwaye niqhubeke nokuvakalisa ukufika kwam. Kuba ndithumele uninzi lwenu emsebenzini kaYohane uMbhaptizi ukuvakalisa ukufika kwam nokubiza inguquko. Ndithumele uninzi lwenu emsebenzini kaPetros ukwalusa nokondla umhlambi. Ndithumele abanye benu njengamagosa emfihlelo enkulu kaThixo, emsebenzini kaPawulos uMpostile (nabanye abagcini bemfihlelo). Ndithumele uninzi lwenu emsebenzini kaFilipu uMvangeli, ngoko shumayelani ilizwi; zingisani ngexesha elifanelekileyo nangexesha elingafanelekanga; khalimelani, kohlwayani, khuthazani ngako konke ukuzeka kade umsindo nemfundiso. Ndithumele abanye benu emsebenzini woMpostile uYohane, owayekwangumfundisi kwaye ekhathalela umhlambi ngothando, ububele, novelwano, kwanokuphilisa amanxeba abangelwe ezimvini zam ngulowo ungendawo. Ningakuthandabuzi, kuba ndinithumele emisebenzini yabo; kuba ixesha lisondele, kuba ndiza kutshata uMtshakazi wam, kuba ndiyeza ngenxa yenu, zithandwa zam! Gqibezelani iikhosi zenu, kuba ndikufuphi. Ixesha liphelile.

NdinguYesu waseNazarete.

Ndingulowo uza kulawula kwitrone kaDavide, kuba ndizalwa kuloo mnombo.

Ndibutyhila obu bubi, nangona uSathana efuna buhlale buyimfihlo.

Ndiyanilumkisa, nangona uninzi lwenu luvilapha kakhulu ukuba lwenze ngokwamazwi am.

Ndiyeza, kwaye ndizisa ingqumbo enkulu kwiBhabhiloni eyimfihlo.

Ndicocekile kwaye iingubo zam zimhlophe njengekhephu elitsha.

Ndilapha ukunixelela ukuba aninalo ixesha lokuchitha.

Ndiyabiza, ngakumbi nina, Bandla lam laseLawodike.

Ndiphantse ndifike apho ukuze ndinixhwilele emazulwini.

Ndiza kutshata uMtshakazi wam.

Ndiyeza ngenxa yenu, zithandwa zam!

Ndikufuphi kwaye ndinithanda kakhulu; nisesazulwini sentliziyo yam.
