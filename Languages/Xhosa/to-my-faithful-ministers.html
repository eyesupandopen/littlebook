Kubalungiseleli Bam Abathembekileyo: Ndiyanithuma!

Kubalungiseleli bam abathembekileyo nakwabo bayishiyileyo iLaodike!

Mtshakazi wam, liva ilizwi lam uze kum, ukuze ndisathomalalisa amanxeba akho ndikomeleze ngamandla am. Musa ukoyika! Umsindo wam awukho phezu kwakho, kuba ndiya kuthi kuwe: Wenze kakuhle, mkhonzi ulungileyo nothembekileyo; uthembekile ezintweni ezimbalwa, ndiya kukumisa phezu kwezinto ezininzi: ngena eluvuyweni lwenkosi yakho. Kuba ndinamazwi arhabaxa, amazwi okohlwaya, kwabangathobeliyo; kodwa olo hlobo lokohlwaya alujoliswanga kuwe kuba uthembekile, nangona kunjalo kufuneka usawathathele ingqalelo amazwi am, hleze uwe.

Mtshakazi wam othembekileyo, ulihlo lam, kwaye akukho kugwetywa kuwe, kuba ukuKristu, kwaye ubomi bakho ubakhile phezu kwam, uYesu Kristu, lona kanye ilitye lembombo eliyintloko abalilahlileyo abakhi bobuxoki. Qhubeka uhlale uthembekile kum, kuba ndiyeza kuwe ngamandla amakhulu, kuba ukuphela kwexesha kusemnyango. Ndiyanithuma ngamandla amakhulu nangemisebenzi eningazange niyicingele, kuba beningazi ukuba nizalelwe kwimfihlelo kaThixo ngomphefumlo wenu wokuqala, kodwa nikhule ngokumangalisayo elubabalweni lwam, kwaye ngoku niyabona. Kuba ubabalo lwam lubabalo olunamandla olungenakoyiswa, neenceba zam zihlala ngonaphakade kanaphakade.

Yabonani! utsho uYeshua HaMashiach. Ndiphulaphuleni nize niwakrole amazwi am emacaleni enyama eentliziyo zenu. Kuba ndiyanithuma; ewe, ndiyanithuma ngala mazwi, kwaye abo ndibathumayo baya kufumana isiqinisekiso esingenakuphikiswa sentando yam evuthayo. Ndiyanithuma emisebenzini yabakhulu bam, imisebenzi abayiqala kwiminyaka emininzi eyadlulayo, kwaye loo misebenzi isaqhubeka nanamhlanje. Kuba incwadi yeZenzo ayikagqitywa ukubhalwa de uMtshakazi wam abe nam. Niya kuqonda ngokupheleleyo kwiincwadi ezisezulwini, kuba izenzo zenu ngabanye zikrolwe apho, kwaye sonke siya kuvuya ngokuthembeka kwenu phambi koYahweh ngokwakhe, kuba uyavuya nathi kwaye ukuni nonke.

Yabonani! Ndithuma abanye benu kubulungiseleli bukaPawulos njengamagosa eemfihlelo zam nanjengabapostile kwiintlanga. Yabonani! Ndithuma abanye benu ngomoya ka-Eliya ukuze nime nichase uIzebhele, umprofetikazi wobuxoki, nize niprofete ngenxa yam, uKristu Yesu! Yabonani! Ndithuma abanye benu emsebenzini kaFilipu umvangeli, apho ndiya kunisusa ndinise kwiindawo ngeendawo namazwi evangeli yam emilebeni yenu, alungiselelwe iimfuno zabangasindiswanga, kuba niya kubasindisa ngokushumayela umnqamlezo! Yabonani! Ndithuma abanye benu emsebenzini kaPetros, kuba wawondla umhlambi wam waza wawupha ilizwi likaThixo elinyulu. Kuba abalungiseleli baseLaodike abanqenayo bondla umhlambi wam ukutya okungenamsoco, namazwi angenakholo angenamandla. Yabonani! Ndinenze ootitshala ukuze nifundise kwaye niyalele abantu bam ngeendlela zobulungisa, kuba niya kubafundisa njengoko ndikhokelayo! Kuba niya kubalusa njengoko ndikhokelayo! Kuba niya kubavangela njengoko ndikhokelayo! Kuba abaprofeti bam baya kuthetha amazwi endibanika wona, engatshintshwanga ntsingiselo nasentliziyweni! Kuba abapostile bam baya kukhanyisa ukukhanya kweendaba zam ezilungileyo ezizukileyo zemfihlelo enkulu kaThixo, ukuze isigqubuthelo sikaMoses sisuswe ezintliziyweni zenu. Kuba ubungqina baseKorinte buyakhala kakhulu! Kodwa kude kube namhlanje, xa kufundwa uMoses, isigqubuthelo sisephezu kweentliziyo zabo. Noko ke, xa ithe yaguqukela eNkosini, isigqubuthelo siya kususwa. Ke iNkosi inguloo moya, kwaye apho ukhoyo uMoya weNkosi, kukho inkululeko. Kodwa thina sonke, ngobuso obutyhiliweyo sibuka njengasesipilini uzuko lweNkosi, siguqulwa sibe mfanekiso mnye, sisuka ebuqaqawulini siye ebuqaqawulini, kwananjengoko kusenziwa nguMoya weNkosi. Mna, Kristu Yesu, ndiyasusa isigqubuthelo senu!

Yabonani, ndiyanithuma kule mizuzu yokugqibela phambi kokuba niqokelelwe kum ekubonakaleni kwam. Kufuneka nilive ilizwi lam kwaye nilandele imiyalelo yam, njengoko nisenza ngokuthembekileyo, Mtshakazi wam othembekileyo, kuba nithembekile! Musani ukuhamba emsebenzini endinithuma kuwo ngokuqonda kwenu. Musani ukuhamba emsebenzini endinithuma kuwo ngamandla enu. Musani ukuhamba emsebenzini endinithuma kuwo ngobuchule benu. Musani ukuhamba emsebenzini endinithuma kuwo ngokomelela kwenu. Wevani amazwi am ngentliziyo yenu yonke! Kuba mna, ewe mna, Kristu Yesu iNkosi ezukisiweyo, ndithi kuni: Ndim ukuqonda kwenu, kwaye ndim amandla enu, kwaye ndim ukomelela kwenu, kwaye ndim ubuchule benu: kuphela musani ukuthandabuza!

Yenzani imisebenzi endiya kunikhokelela ukuba niyifeze, kwaye nibakhathalele abantu endibathumayo kuni. Ngaba niya kuzithoba kubantu abaphantsi? Ngaba niya kuhlala, nishumayele, nisindise, kwaye nibe nobudlelane nabangenamakhaya? Kuba ndithuma abanye benu ngaloo njongo. Ngaba niya kubongoza abalungiseleli baseLaodike ngokuthobeka njengoko umntu ebenokwenza kuBawo? Kuba zinjalo iindlela zam. Ngaba niya kuwathetha amazwi endininika wona, nokuba aniwacacelwanga ngokupheleleyo okanye anivumelani nawo? (Qinisekisani amazwi am ngesibhalo.) Kuba ndiyanithuma ukuba nenze intando yam, hayi eyenu. Ngaba niya kuwalusa umhlambi ngamandla okuphilisa omlilo osemoyeni wenu endiya kuwuphemba kuni? (Kwaye xa athe wabonakala umalusi oyintloko, niya kwamkela isithsaba sozuko esingabuniyo.) Yenzani imisebenzi yam, Mtshakazi wam, nize niyeke eyenu imisebenzi, kuba alisekho ixesha, kwaye izibane ziya kucima kungekudala.

Qondani, ndiyanithuma emsebenzini wam, kwaye niya kuvuna umvuzo omkhulu ukuba nithembekile, kuba sele ninobuncwane obuninzi obubekelwe nina emazulwini, kwaye izindlu ezininzi endlwini kaBawo zilungiselelwe nina. Kuba inkathazo kaYakobi ziintsuku ezisixhenxe zeminyaka esixhenxe, apho unyaka ngamnye ubalwa njengosuku. Unjalo nomtshato woMyeni noMtshakazi wakhe. Kuba kuya kubakho iintsuku ezisixhenxe zeminyaka esixhenxe emazulwini, apho unyaka ngamnye ubalwa njengosuku, kwaye niya kuhlala ninam ngonaphakade, Mtshakazi wam. Emva koko sikhwele!

Hambani ngeendlela zenu kwaye nihambe ngendlela endinikhokelela kuyo. Zithobeni eluthandweni nasekuthobekeni omnye komnye. Khathalelani isitiya sam, kuba ndityale isitiya sobomi obungunaphakade angenakusincothula okanye asitshabalalise ongendawo. Niya kudla kuwo kanye umthi wobomi angaphumelelanga ukuwugcina kude nani, kwaye abukho ubomi obuninzi echibini lomlilo likaSathana, apho ayakhona, kodwa kukho isohlwayo, ukuzisola, ihlazo, nentuthumbo kuphela. Ezi zinto asizozakho, Mtshakazi wam, kwaye ukufa kwesibini akunamandla phezu kwakho, kuba usukile ekufeni waya ebomini kum, ewe kuYesu Kristu, uMsindisi neNkosi yakho.

Ngoko hambani apho ndinithuma khona, kwaye nisebenze nabantu endinithuma kubo, nendibathumayo kuni. Kuba abezi kuni ngengozi, kodwa kungenxa yokuba sele ndiqalisile ukubalathisa kuni. Philani imfihlelo yam kwaye niphile njengoko babesenza kwiZenzo, kuba nina nisahluko sokugqibela esibhalwayo njengoko niphilela mna, kwaye kungekudala niya kubona xa nise mazulwini xa ndithe ndanixhwila ndanisa kum, kuba ningabam! Ningumtshakazi wam, iBandla lam elizukileyo, endiya kuzenzela lona, lingenabala nasisihwinqa, nasinye isinto esinjalo: kodwa ukuze nibe ngcwele ningenakusoleka, kuba ndinihlambile egazini lam lentlawulelo!

Ndiyanibiza, Mtshakazi wam, kwaye ndinibizela kubulungiseleli eningazange niphupha ukuba ningabufeza, kuba abalungiseleli baseLaodike bazama ukunenza nihlale ebumnyameni. Musani ukubaleka kumandla endiwaphembayo kuni, kuba isandla seNkosi, ewe isandla sam sentsikelelo, siphezu kwenu. Musani ukubaleka kubulungiseleli bam endibuphembayo emoyeni wenu. Kuba ndiya kuphemba umlilo omkhulu emoyeni wenu, Mtshakazi wam, umlilo oya kunyibilikisa zonke izinto ezinibambezelayo. Musani ukoyika! Hambani ngamandla am, kuba abalungiseleli baseLaodike abangathembekanga bazamile ukuneyisela ukuba aninamandla, kwaye kukho isiphumo esibi kubo ukuba abaguquki.

Yabonani! Ndiyanithuma emisebenzini apho abanye baye basilela khona, kwaye niya kuhamba endaweni yabo, kuba nithembekile: Kuphela litshabalaliseni ikratshi lenu, hleze nibe lixhoba lobubi njengabo. Musani ukuzibamba iilwimi zenu, kuba ndizikhulule. Thethani amazwi endininike wona, kwaye nihambe phambi kwam ngamandla amakhulu. Nicinga ukuba ndingumkhethi buso? Kuba ngoku lixesha lemiqondiso ekhethekileyo, njengoko ndabhala kwiZenzo: Kwaye uThixo wenza imiqondiso ekhethekileyo ngezandla zikaPawulos: Kangangokuba kwathathwa emzimbeni wakhe kwasiwa kwabagulayo iitshefu okanye iifaskoti, zaza izifo zemka kubo, nemimoya emdaka yaphuma kubo. Mtshakazi wam wokholo olukhulu, ndigqibezeleni imisebenzi endinibizela kuyo, kuba sigqibezela iinjongo zam kuni, kwaye ilanga liyatshona kwixesha lam lobabalo, kwaye kungekudala ubusuku buya kuhlela ngokupheleleyo ehlabathini, kuba niyahamba. Yabonani! Mna, Yesu Kristu, ndiya kwenza imimangaliso emininzi enamandla, kwanemiqondiso ekhethekileyo ngesandla senu, kuba olu luthumo lokugqibela olukhulu lweBandla lam phambi kokuba nixhwilwe niye eluzukweni.

Hambani, ngoxolo, kwaye ndiya kukhokela. Yazini ukuba niya kuchaswa kakhulu ngabahlobo, usapho, neentshaba ngokufanayo. La ngamaxesha okwahlukana xa ndithena abangathembekanga kwaye ndihlanza umthi wam womnquma kwiindlela zabo zokungathembeki, kuba abazilungiselelanga umyeni, kwaye baya kuthatha iindawo zabo kunye neentombi eziziyatha. Kwaye ndihlambulula abathembekileyo bam esonweni ukuze bavelise amasebe amatsha athwala isiqhamo esininzi. Musani ukoyika, nicinga ukuba anifanelekanga yimiqondiso. Kuba ndiyavuma, ngokwenu akukho namnye kuni onokwenza imiqondiso; kodwa ndiya kunika imiqondiso emininzi, izimanga, nemimangaliso ngezandla zabathembekileyo bam. Hambani nithi kwiintaba, suswa uphoswe elwandle, kwaye ningathandabuzi kodwa nibe nokholo kuYahweh, nakum, uYeshua HaMashiach.

Akukho hamile yokomoya yokujula, ngoko yekani iinzame zenu. Kuba ningahamba phezu kwamanzi ukuba nigcina amehlo enu kum kwaye anithandabuzi kwaye anijongi ekhohlo okanye ekunene kodwa nigcina amehlo enu kum, kwaye aniyi kutshona. Yalelani imimoya, kuba iphantsi kolawulo lwam kwaye ke ngoko iphantsi kolawulo lwenu. Yalelani imimoya ukuba imke; musani ukucela, kodwa yalelani! Musani ukuyivumela ithethe, kodwa yithuliseni; musani ukuthetha nayo, kuba amazwi ayo ayityhefu; kodwa yikhupheleni entlango apho ingenakhaya, kwaye kungekudala iya kuphoswa emlilweni. Philisani abagulayo! Thethani nesifo egameni lam, kwaye nisixelele ukuba simke; musani ukoyika, kuba ndiya kuninika amazwi. Musani ukoyika! Akunyanzelekanga ukuba nithethe amazwi acikizekileyo; thethani nje amazwi okholo endiya kuwaphefumlela ezintliziyweni zenu kwaye ningathandabuzi.

Yabonani! Hambani ngenxa yam, Mtshakazi wam, ningathandabuzi nto. Ndinimemela emisebenzini endiyigqibayo, apho uninzi lwenu sele ningabasebenzi-kunye nam. Kusekho imiphefumlo ekufuneka isindiswe, kwanabo uSathana azama ukubafihla. Ndiya kubakhulula kwiibhondi zakhe ngemisebenzi yenu emininzi enamandla nezenzo esiya kuzenza kunye. Hambani, Mtshakazi wam, ningathandabuzi nto, kuba ndinilungiselele ubomi benu bonke kule mizuzwana imbalwa yamandla nentobeko, kwaye niqeqeshwe kakuhle ndim, nabo ndibathumileyo ukuba bakhathalele imiphefumlo yenu.

Mtshakazi wam othembekileyo, ndiyakuthanda, kwaye uluyolo lwam novuyo lwam. Ndincuma phezu kwakho yonke imihla, kodwa awundiboni ngamehlo akho, kwaye usenalo ukholo. Ndiyalila ngenxa yeentlungu zakho, nangona ndilila ndithe cwaka amaxesha amaninzi. Ndiyakuziva izikhalo zakho zokucela uncedo, kwaye ndiluncedo olusoloko lukho ngexesha lemfuneko. Ndiyakuthanda kwaye ndiyakukhathalela, kwaye ulihlo lam, kwaye kungekudala siya kuba kunye. Ndiyazi ukuba udiniwe, kuba uthembekile kwiLizwi lam nakwigama lam, ngoko ke ndiya kukomeleza ngamandla kaThixo ongenamda nongunaphakade, ewe uYahweh, kuba akukho namnye onjengaye! Musa ukutyhafa, kuba nangona inyama yakho inokuba idiniwe, ndiya kukomeleza ngamandla am, kuba mna, Yeshua HaMashiach, nditshilo!

Ndiyeza kuwe ngamandla amakhulu.

Ndiyanithuma ngamandla amakhulu.

Ndiyanibiza, Mtshakazi wam, ndinibizela kubulungiseleli eningazange nazi ukuba ninabo kude kube ngoku.

Ndithuma abanye benu ukuba bagqibezele imisebenzi yabangathembekanga, kwaye niya kuba nomvuzo wabo.

Ndiyathena abangathembekanga.

Ndihlambulula abathembekileyo bam ezonweni zenu ukuze nithwale isiqhamo esininzi.

Ndiluncedo olusoloko lukho ngexesha lemfuneko.

Hamba indlela yakho, ume eTempileni, uthethe ebantwini onke amazwi obu bomi. Kuba nina niyitempile kaThixo ophilayo!
