Kuma-144,000: Nibizelwe Kwaye Natywinwa Kanye Ngexesha Elinje

Yimani ngenxa yam kwaye nilandele umzekelo wam, kuba athini na amaHebhere ngokuphathelele ukuma kwam ngenxa kaYehova? Lowo, ngenxa yovuyo olwalubekwe phambi kwakhe, wawunyamezela umnqamlezo, elidela ihlazo, waza wahlala ngasekunene kwetrone kaThixo. Ngoko ke eluthandweni lwam ngoBawo, olungenakuqhawulwa, nasekuthobeleni kwam ngenxa yothando, naseluthandweni lwam ngoluntu nomnqweno wam wokuzincama ukuze ndinisindise, ndanyamezela isohlwayo esoyikekayo, intlungu, nokubandezeleka kwaye ndakwenza oko ngokuzithandela, ndinovuyo entliziyweni yam. Ndandisazi ukuba ngedini lam nokufa kwam, abaninzi baya kwenziwa amalungisa baphile ngamandla ovuko lwam kuba uYehova ngokwakhe wandivusa kwabafileyo, kuba akunakwenzeka ukuba ukufa kundibambe. Kwaye ngala mandla manye, nani niya kuvuselwa kubomi obutsha, kuba ndiyihlawule intlawulo ngokunikela ngegazi lam elingenasono njengentlawulelo yabo bonke abaya kukholwa egameni lam, kwaye nabani na obiza egameni leNkosi uya kusindiswa, abo bangabathandiyo ubomi babo kude kube sekupheleni!

Ngoko ke ngoku ndiyanibuza, nina bathembekileyo bam nabanyuliweyo, kwanani zizalwana zam, ukuba nime ngenxa yam kwaye ningarხoxisi. Kuba ndatsho ngokucacileyo kwisincoko sam kumaHebhere: Ke olilungisa uya kuphila ngokholo; kodwa ukuba nabani na uthe warhoxa, umphefumlo wam awuyi kukholiswa nguye.

Mayizike loo nto, kwaye niyifunde kwakhona, kwaye ningarხoxeli kwiTorah, kuba iTorah igcinwa ngokholo kum; ngoko ke, olilungisa uya kuphila ngokholo. Nimanyene nam, kwaye ndingulowo unigwebulele kwimisebenzi yam yokucamagushela nange gazi lam elingenasono.

Uninzi lwenu lusokola ngengcamango yokuba ndathatha indawo yenu ekugwetyweni emthini ngenxa yezono endingazenzanga, kwaye ndakwenza oko ngokuzithandela nangovuyo ukuze ndizise usindiso kumntu wemvelo owonakeleyo, ukuze ndibakhululele kuYehova, uThixo wenu noYihlo wenu, kwanaye uYise wezikhanyiso ongenaguquguquko nasithunzi sakujika. Kuba uYehova akaguquki; uhleli enjalo izolo, namhlanje, nangonaphakade!

UnguThixo omnye lowo ooyihlo benu bavumelana naye emnqophisweni, kwanaseNtabeni yeSinayi xa uMoses wafumana umthetho kuYehova ngokwakhe. Kwaye nangona umthetho ungcwele, nomyalelo ungcwele, ulungile, kwaye ulungile, uluntu aluzange lukwazi ukuphila ngokwemigangatho yawo kuba umthetho wanikwa ukuze woyise isono enyameni, kwaye nonke nizalwa nifile ezonweni nasezonweni kuba isono sisenyameni yenu. ITorah ayizange inikwe abalungileyo kodwa yenzelwe abangalunganga, ukuze isono sibonakale siyisono enyameni. ITorah yanikwa njengomfundisi-ntsapho wenu ukuba inise kum, kwanakuKristu Yesu, iNkosi yeeNkosi noKumkani wooKumkani enyukileyo!

Ngoko ke ndazithwalela ngokuzithandela iziphumo zesono, kuba ndazalelwa ukukhulula uluntu kwisiqalekiso somthetho. Kuba umthetho wawungaqalekiswanga; ewe, umthetho mawume kwaye wonke umntu makaqalekiswe oqalekisa umthetho kaMoses, kuba andizanga kuzokuchitha umthetho, kodwa ukumisa umthetho kwanokugqitha kumthetho kaMoses. Uthi ndiyanyelisa?

Ubungqina bamaHebhere bumi njengomlamli phakathi kwethu: Kuba ukuba igazi leenkunzi zeenkomo neleebhokhwe, nothuthu lwethokazi olufefa abangahlambulukanga, luyangcwalisa ekuhlanjululweni kwenyama: Kobeka phi na ke ukugqithisela kwegazi likaKristu, owathi ngoMoya ongunaphakade wazinikela engenabala kuThixo, ukusihlambulula isazela senu kwimisebenzi efileyo ukuze nikhonze uThixo ophilayo?

Ekubeni nimthanda uMoses (njengam), ndinicebisa ukuba nithobele ngothando imiyalelo kaMoses; kuba wathi: UYehova uThixo wenu uya kunivelisela uMprofeti phakathi kwenu, kubazalwana benu, onjengam; kuye, niya kumphulaphula. Ngoko ke liveni ilizwi likaYeshua HaMashiach! Nihlanjululwe esonweni nakuzo zonke izono enizenzayo ngegazi lam lokucamagushela elingenasono endalinikela kuYehova njengentlawulelo yobomi benu, kwaye ndanikhulula esandleni sotshaba. Musani ukona! Kwaye ukuba nabani na uthe wona, zivume izono zakho kum, kuba ndingulowo unikhuphayo ngegazi lam lokucamagushela.

Andizange ndicamagushele izono zenu neziphumo zazo ngebhokhwe yokulahla, kwaye andizange ndinikele ngemvana yonyaka wokuqala; kuba ndiyiMvana kaThixo yonyaka wokuqala, kwaye ndingulowo wazinikela engenabala kwaye engenasisihla, kwanobungcwele, ubunyulu, nokungcwaliselwa kuYehova ngokwakhe (kwanakuBawo) ngenxa yezono zenu kube kanye, kwanakumaxesha onke. Ndacamagushela izono zenu ngegazi lam elingenasono; ngoko ke, akusekho dini lesono. Kwaye ukundala mna, uYeshua HaMashiach, kwanaye isivumelwano soxolo esigqibeleleyo sikaYehova kuluntu, kukwala uYehova nokuzibandakanya nemvukelo kaSathana, kuba uSathana uvukela ucwangco lukaThixo kwasekuqaleni.

Kuba ndaxelela amaHebhere: Kuba ukuba sona ngabom emva kokuba silufumene ulwazi lwenyaniso, akusekho dini lezono. Ngoko ke ukona ngabom ngokwala igazi lam lokucamagushela, kwaye ngaloo ndlela usala isivumelwano soxolo esithunyelwe nguYehova, kubi ngokukhethekileyo, kwaye akusekho dini. Kuba kubhaliwe kumaHebhere: Lowo wayedelela umthetho kaMoses wafa engenanceba phantsi kwamangqina amabini okanye amathathu: Nicinga ukuba, uya kubalelwa ukuba ufanele isohlwayo esingakanani na esibi ngakumbi, lowo unyathele phantsi koNyana kaThixo, walibala igazi lomnqophiso, awangcwaliswa ngalo, liyinto engcolileyo, waza wamngcikiva uMoya wobabalo?

Ngoko ke ndiyanikhuthaza nonke ukuba namkele idini lam kwaye niqonde ukuba akusekho madini afunekayo kwaye kukho uMesiya omnye wokwenene, kwanam, uYeshua HaMashiach. Kwaye kubhaliwe ngam: Kodwa le ndoda, emva kokuba inikele idini elinye lezono ngonaphakade, yahlala ngasekunene kukaThixo; Ukusukela ngoku ilindele de iintshaba zayo zenziwe isihlalo seenyawo zayo. Kuba ngomnikelo omnye ubagqibelelisile ngonaphakade abo bangcwaliswayo.

Ngoko ke qondani ukuba ndinihlambulule kuzo zonke iinkalo nemiba ephathelele isono neziphumo zaso. Ningcwalisiwe kum; nigwetyelwe kum; nenziwe ubulungisa bukaThixo kum; kwaye ndinikhulule ngokupheleleyo kum, kwanam, uYeshua HaMashiach, lowo uYehova amthumileyo njengoMesiya wenu. Kwaye ngoko ke qondani ukuba akusekho Babingeleli abakhulu bamaLevi, kwaye ubuBingeleli buka-Aron beza badlula, kuba babusisithunzi sezinto ezizayo. Kuba ndingumbingeleli wenu omkhulu ngonaphakade ngokohlobo lukaMelkitsedeke.

Kuba umthetho awuzange wenze nto igqibeleleyo: kodwa wawusisaziso sethemba elingcono, ithemba esisondela ngalo kuYehova. Kwaye kwakhona: UMbingeleli oMkhulu onjalo kwakufanelekile ukuba sibe naye ongcwele, ongenabungozi, ongadyojwanga, owahlulwe kuboni, kwaye owenziwe mkhulu kunezulu. Kwaye kwakhona: Kuba umthetho wenza abantu babe ngababingeleli, abanobuthathaka: kodwa ilizwi lesifungo elafika emva komthetho, lenza unyana abe ngumbingeleli, ogqibeleleyo ngonaphakade. Anifundanga na oko kuthethwa sisibhalo ngam? UYehova wafunga kwaye akayi kuzisola: UnguMbingeleli ngonaphakade ngokohlobo lukaMelkitsedeke.

Qondani ukuba akukho nto isilelayo kwidini lam, kwaye nikhuselekile kum, uKristu Yesu. ITorah igcinwa ngokholo kum, uYeshua HaMashiach, kwaye akunakwenzeka ukuba nophule nawuphi na umthetho kaThixo ngokuhamba kum, uKristu Yesu. Kuba nihamba emthethweni woMoya wobomi, kwaye ndinikhulule emthethweni wesono nokufa. Ngoko ke yibani nokholo kum, kuba olilungisa uya kuphila ngokholo. Ndinemisebenzi emininzi enamandla endinibizele kuyo, kwaye niya kunceda ukuzisa usindiso kwabaninzi. Kukho ithemba elikhulu nomvuzo eniwubekelweyo emazulwini ngenxa yokuma kwenu nokuthembeka kwenu.

Kuba ndiyanithuma, nitywinwe emabunzini enu ngophawu lukaYehova ngokwakhe, kwihlabathi eliya kunithiya ngokupheleleyo, kwaye akumangalisi, kuba ihlabathi landithiya ngokupheleleyo kuqala, kwaye umkhonzi akangaphezu kwenkosi yakhe. Ningamangqina am nabaprofeti bam nabaphilisi bam nababoneleli bam. Ndinithuma kobona bumnyama bumnyama obukhe bagubungela indalo kaYehova. Ngexesha elinje niye nabizwa, nanyulwa, natywinwa, kwaye la maxesha aya kuba ngawona agwenxa kakhulu kwaye amabi ihlabathi elikhe lawabona, kwanokubi ngaphezu kwemihla kaNowa. Phakathi kwabo ndiyanithuma, kwaye ndithi kuni musani ukoyika! Musani ukoyika izisongelo zabo; musani ukoyika oko umntu anokukwenza kuni; musani ukoyika ubuso babo; musani ukoyika imimoya ebanika amandla; musani ukoyika irhamncwa nomprofeti wobuxoki; kwaye musani ukoyika uSathana ngokwakhe: Kwanaleyo namba inkulu. Kuba niya kunyathela yonke inkalo yobukumkani bukaSathana, kwaye akukho nto iya kuninqanda nangayiphi na indlela ukuba nihlala nithembekile kwilizwi lam kwaye nindivumele ndinikhokele inyathelo ngenyathelo. Kuba ngenene, ningabasebenzi kunye nam ngeli xesha lenkathazo kaYakobi, kwaye ndinixhobisile.

Ninamandla kwaye ninamandla, kwaye ndiyanithuma ninobulali nokuthobeka. Ndiyanithuma ukuba nigcine umthetho ngokholo lwenu kum, kwaye ngoko ke thandani uYehova ngentliziyo yenu yonke, ngomphefumlo wenu wonke, ngengqondo yenu yonke, nangamandla enu onke, kwaye nithande ummelwane wenu njengoko nizithanda. Ngubani ummelwane wenu? Bonke abo badinga uncedo lwenu endinithuma kubo, kwaye andinithinteli ngemida yelizwe. Hambani niyokwalusa umhlambi wam, kuba ndingumAlusi oyiNtloko onomvuzo wenu esandleni sam ngenxa yokuthembeka. Hambani niyokunyamekela umhlambi wam, nyamekelani amanxeba awo, wondleni, philise, khokele, kwaye ngaphezu kwako konke nizise kum; kuba ngokwenu aniyonto, kodwa kum, ningamadoda am anamandla, anamandla kaThixo. Bafundiseni ukubiza egameni lam kude kube sekupheleni, kuba nabani na owenzayo uya kusindiswa.

Zigcineni amehlo enu kwibhaso nakuluvuyo olubekwe phambi kwenu, kwaye niqhubekele phambili elujolisweni lwebhaso lobizo oluphezulu lukaThixo kuKristu Yesu. Kuba umvuzo wenu uya kuba mkhulu, kwaye niya kumangaliswa koko nikubekelweyo emazulwini. Kwaye umvuzo wam awungowexeshana kodwa ungunaphakade, kwaye imivuzo yenu ayiyi kutshabalala kodwa iya kusebenza njengelifa elingunaphakade ekuzalisekeni kwezithembiso uYehova awazenza kooyihlo; yonke iyod okanye isuntswana leyod iya kuzaliseka. Aniyi kubuqonda ubukhulu bemisebenzi yenu kude kube yiloo mini yomvuzo, kodwa isiBhalo sicacile ngesiqhamo semisebenzi yenu.

Kuba uYohane uMtyhileli wabhala ngokuthembekileyo oko ndambonisa kona: Emva kwezi zinto ndabona, kwaye yabona isihlwele esikhulu, esingenakubalwa mntu, sazo zonke iintlanga nezizwe, nabantu, neelwimi, simi phambi kwetrone, naphambi kweMvana, sinxibe iingubo ezinde ezimhlophe, namasebe esundu ezandleni zaso. Kwaye badanduluka ngelizwi elikhulu, besithi, Usindiso luvela kuThixo wethu, ohleli etroneni, nakwiMvana. Kuba isihlwele esikhulu niya kunceda ukusisindisa nokusikhonza, kwaye iya kuba mikhulu imivuzo yenu yokuthembeka.

Kwaye niya kundilandela naphi na apho ndiya khona, kwaye niziintlahlela ezithengiweyo ebantwini, kwaye niya kucula ingoma entsha phambi kwetrone eninokuyifunda nina nodwa. Niya kulawula nam eBukumkanini bam iminyaka eliwaka kwaye niya kuhlala ninam ngonaphakade. Kuba olwenu luvuyo olungunaphakade, kuba niya kuba nam eBukumkanini bam emhlabeni njengoko ndilawula umhlaba ngobulungisa kwitrone kaDavide, kwaye uBukumkani bam abunaphelo. Kwaye akukho nto imbi iya kungena, kwaye akusayi kubakho nkathazo, kwaye obam bubukumkani bobulungisa apho imvana nengonyama ziya kulala phantsi kunye ngoxolo. Kwaye ekupheleni kolawulo lwam lweminyaka eliwaka, uSathana uya kukhululwa ixesha elincinane ukuze ahlanganise uhlaselo lwakhe lokugqibela, kwaye umlilo uya kuhla uvela kuYehova ubatshabalalise ngokupheleleyo. USathana uya kuphoswa echibini lomlilo, angaphinde avuke, kwaye uYehova uya kudala izulu elitsha nomhlaba omtsha, kwaye uya kwenza indawo yakhe yokuhlala phakathi kwabantu, kwaye ngoko niya kuhlala eYerusalem eNtsha, kwanakuloo mzi ungcwele owadalwa nguYehova ngokwakhe. Kwaye uYehova ngokwakhe uthe: Lowo woyisayo uya kuzidla ilifa zonke izinto; kwaye ndiya kuba nguThixo wakhe, kwaye yena uya kuba ngunyana wam.
