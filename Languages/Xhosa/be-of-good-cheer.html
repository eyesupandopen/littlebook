Ubungqina Obumbini: Ibandla
KwiMisebenzi kaPetros
Epreli 10, 2024

Yibani Nemihlali!

Bantwanana bam, yibani nemihlali; andimoyisanga na ongendawo ndanihlawulela ngegazi lam? Kutheni nityhafa nje? Kutheni iintliziyo zenu zinzima nje; nicinga ukuba ndinilibele? Hayi, endaweni yokukhangela ekuqondeni kwenu, funani ubulumko bam, obuvela phezulu; kuba niya kukhokelwa bubulumko bobuthixo, ngoko musani ukubulahla! Ubulumko abusadanduluki na ezitratweni? Ngoko musani ukoyika, yibani nemihlali, kuba ndoyisile kwaye nani niya koyisa, ngegazi lam. Vumelani uxolo lwam luthuthuzele iintliziyo zenu, nivumele namazwi am athomalalise imiphefumlo yenu, kuba ndinani.

Ngubani na oya kunahlula eluthandweni lwam? Yingozi na, likrele na, yindlala na eya kunahlula? Ndithi hayi, khangelani nazi ukuba nikhuselekile kum. Yazini ukuba ndinitywinile ngetywina likaBawo, kwaye elo tywina alinakwaphulwa okanye lihluthwe kuni, ngoko hlalani kum, iNkosi yenu, amandla, nomhlanguli wenu. Yabonani! Ndinithengile ngegazi lam, ndinibizile ngegama, ndinitywinile ngokokholo lwenu egameni lam, kwaye nabani na okholwayo kulo uya kusindiswa ngokholo, kuba olilungisa liya kuphila ngokholo! Gcinani ithemba lenu liphila kwaye malinomeleze, nongeze unyamezelo eluthandweni nasekholweni lwenu, kuba nisindiselwe ethembeni. Lelenu ithemba elikhulu nolindelo olukhulu, kuba nditshilo.

Kutheni nityhafile kwaye nibuhlungu nje? Yabonani! Ndoyisile, kwaye kungekudala niya kuba nam, niya kundibona ngamehlo enu, kwaye niya kwazi njengoko naziwa.

Khangelani! Itsho iNkosi enkulu, iNkosi enamandla eyiNkosi yeeNkosi. Khangelani kum ebunzimeni nasezinkathazweni zenu. Ndiyazazi iintliziyo zenu, kwaye ndazi kakuhle ukuba niyandithanda kwaye ninqwenela ukuba nam njengoko nam ndinqwenela ukuba nani; kuphela musani ukutyhafa. Ndiphenduleni koku, ixesha elifutshane eliseleyo ekulindeni kwenu — lingathelekiswa na nonaphakade nam, mtshakazi wam? Hayi, iintlungu neembandezelo zenu emhlabeni azifanele kuthelekiswa nozuko endiza kulutyhila kuni, kuba niya kuba njengam, kwaye niya kundibona njengoko ndinjalo, ngoko musani ukuvumela iintliziyo zenu zikhathazeke. Qondani amaxesha enu kwaye nihlale nikulungele, kuba ukubonakala kwam kuya kwenzeka ngequbuliso, kwaye kuya kugqitha kulindelo lwenu.

Yabonani, bantwana bam! Ihlabathi liya kwazi ukuba ndiyanithanda kwaye ningabam, kwaye uSathana uya kubangela inkohliso enkulu ukukhohlisa abo batshabalalayo, kodwa ayisayi kusondela kuni. Hayi, umgwebo asiyowenu, kwananjengoko ingqumbo yam ingeyoyenu. Qondani, bantwana bam, ukuba kum, nilungile, ningalunganga ngemisebenzi yenu, kodwa nilungile ngobulungisa endinambathise bona. Musani ukulahla izambatho endinambathise zona, kwaye musani ukuwucima umoya wam. Nangona ingamaxesha okuphela, ndiya kuwuthulula umoya wam, kwaye oonyana neentombi zenu baya kuprofeta, namadoda enu amadala aya kuphupha amaphupha. Kusekho umsebenzi omawenziwe, kwaye intshukumo yam yokugqibela kula maxesha obabalo iya kugqitha usuku lwePentekoste xa ndaqala ukuthululela phezu kwabanyulwa bam umoya endawamkelayo kuBawo. Imisebenzi emikhulu isezandleni zenu kwaye baninzi abasaza kusindiswa, ngoko yenzani umsebenzi wam nilungiselele abantwana bam nifune iigusha zam ezilahlekileyo. Celani kum kwaye ndiya kunithuma, njengoko ndinixhobisile ngesixhobiso esikhulu kwaye ndinike izipho ebantwini. Ngubani onokunichasa, nangona ninolu butyebi ezityeni zodongwe? Kutheni nisandithandabuza nje? Ebuthakathakeni benu amandla am enziwa agqibelele kuni. Ngoko ke, phuphumani emsebenzini weNkosi, bantwana bam.

Jongani iLizwi lam eliNgcwele, kuba njengoko ndavusa abafileyo, nani ningenza okufanayo. Thambisani amehlo enu ngesithambiso samehlo ukuze nibone iimphiliso ezininzi ezinamandla endazenzayo ukukhulula abathinjwa xa ndafika kwabam; nani ningenza okufanayo. Anditshongo na ukuba lowo ukholwayo kum, uya kwenza yona loo misebenzi, kwaye uya kwenza imisebenzi emikhulu kunayo kuba ndiye kuBawo wam noYihlo wenu, nakuThixo wam noThixo wenu: Ningookumkani nababingeleli phambi kwam kwaye nihleli ezulwini, ngoko kutheni nisathandabuza nje? Yibani nokholo olungangokhozo lwemostade, kuba yimbewu encinane kakhulu evelisa umthi omkhulu, niya kuba njalo nani ukuba niyaqhubeka niqinile kum.

Kutheni niwabhida amazwi am kwaye ningazihoyi iinkcukacha kwintetho yam? Andixoki: ningakwenza oko ndinithume kona ukuba nikwenze ukuba nihlala nithembekile. Thethani iLizwi lam ngegunya elikhulu nangokungafihlisiyo. Yithini kwezo ntaba, Phoselwani elwandle! Amazwi am ayinyaniso nobomi, ngoko musani ukukholelwa kubuxoki babalusi abalahlekileyo abathi aninakukwenza oko ndinixelela kona. Thathani isifundo. Nifuna ukuphilisa abagulayo? Yalelani isifo ukuba simke egameni lam, kwaye nithethe amazwi ngokholo kum nangokholo kumazwi endiya kuninika wona. Kuthetheni kube njalo, kuba ubomi nokufa kusemandleni olwimi; lunamandla kangakanani na ulwimi lwenu xa nithetha amandla kaThixo niyalela okungenakwenzeka.

Thethani ngokungafihlisiyo. Thethani ngokuzithemba. Thethani ngokholo, ningathandabuzi nto; kuba ndinithumile ndanixhobisa. Kutheni nicela umoya ukuba umke egameni lam ninoloyiko emilebeni yenu? Wuyalele ukuba umke egameni lam ngokungafihlisiyo nangokuzithemba konke, kuba mkhulu lowo ukuni kunalowo usehlabathini. Yabonani! Imimoya emdaka iyanoyika kwaye iyashwabana phambi kwamazwi enu, ngoko kutheni noyika izinto ezibuthathaka nezingento ezoyisiweyo nezilindele umlilo? Musani ukoyika, kodwa kholwani kuphela!

Hambani phambili egameni lam nenze imisebenzi yam emikhulu. Khumbulani uthando lwenu lokuqala nize niphumle kuphumlo endininike lona. Yekani umsebenzi wenu nemithwalo yenu, nithabathe idyokhwe yam phezu kwenu; kuba idyokhwe yam ilula, nomthwalo wam unzima. Yenzani imisebenzi endinilungiselele yona nihambe kuyo, kuba ngokwenjenjalo niya kusindisa abaninzi emalangatyeni, kwaye isiqhamo siya kwanda kwingxelo yenu. Ixesha lisondele, mtshakazi wam, nesiphelo sohambo lwenu sikufuphi, kufutshane kunokuba nicinga. Kodwa ndiveni, bantwana bam; ndiveni, mtshakazi wam! Thethelelani kusekho ixesha. Philisani abagulayo ngoku uNyana esakhanya ebomini benu, enixhobisa ngamandla am.

Funani iigusha zam ezilahlekileyo; zibizeni, kuba baninzi abasaza kusindiswa kule mizuzwana yokugqibela yokuhlaziya. Kuba kungekudala niya kufika kwilifa lenu kum, kwaye niya kubuqonda ubomi benu obupheleleyo kum; kuba ubomi benu bufihliwe kum, kwaye xa ndiya kubonakala, kuya kubonakala nobomi obupheleleyo uBawo aninike bona kum. Ndinithanda kakhulu, bantwanana bam, ngoko thandani abalahlekileyo ngendlela efanayo. Musani ukubagweba okanye niyidele imeko yabo, kuba boyisiwe nguSathana njengoko nani naninjalo; ngoko khumbulani inkululo endayenzela nina niyinike abo bayifunayo. Nangona abaninzi beya kunichasa, musani ukuyeka de iigusha zam zihlanganiswe, kuba kungekudala ingqolowa yam iya kuhlanganiselwa ngokukhuselekileyo kuvimba wam. Abo balukhanyelayo ukubonakala kwam bayandikhanyela mna kwaye bayalikhanyela ilizwi lam; ngoko zomelezeni elizwini lam niyeke ukukholelwa kumasela, kuba amasela nawo angamaxoki.

Ndingulowo wanyamezelayo kwada kwayimphefumlo yakhe yokugqibela, esilwa nesono ngegazi lam elingenasono.

Ndingulowo wavuswayo ngomhla wesithathu wanyukela kuBawo.

Ndingulowo wahamba phakathi kwabafundi bam nabapostile bam iintsuku ezingamashumi amane, ndabonwa nangaphezu kwamakhulu amahlanu abazalwana ngaxeshanye, ndabonwa ndaphathwa ngabaninzi, kuba umoya awunanyama negazi.

Ndingulowo wathathwayo, bekhangele, waya emafini.

Ndingulowo ukukhanya kwakhe kuqaqambe ngaphezu kwelanga, kuba ndibonakalisa uzuko lukaBawo.

Ndikufuphi nokutyhila uzuko olukhulu kuni.

Ndinani maxa onke, kude kube sekupheleni kwephakade.
