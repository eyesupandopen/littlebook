Ingqina eliMbini: uSirayeli
Okukrakra Okumnandi
Julayi 05, 2024

Yabonani! Ukubuyiselwa Kwezinto Zonke Kusondele!

Kusuka kuYeshua HaMashiach:

Amaxesha okubuyiselwa asondela emhlabeni, kodwa kuqala kufuneka kuzalisekiswe amaxesha nokugqitywa komgwebo omkhulu. Kwaye mna, Krestu Yesu, ndiya kuwugweba umhlaba kuba umhlaba ungowam nokuzala kwawo, kwaye ndiwuthengile umhlaba ngegazi lam lokucamagushela eKalvari. Inyaniso engenakuguqulwa yeyokuba, ndanikela lonke ithontsi legazi lam elingenatyala elaphalalela izono zoluntu. Kwabo bamkela isicelo sam soxolo nobomi obungunaphakade kuya kubakho uxolo, nobomi obungunaphakade, kwaye iindlela zobulungisa ziya koyisa. Kwabo basichithayo isicelo sam bakhethe iindlela zokufa, zomgwebo, nesohlwayo esingunaphakade, kuba ihlabathi liyadlula kwaye akukho olunye ukhetho. Kwaye ngoko baya kuthwala umgwebo wabo naso sonke isono, ilizwi elibi, ingcinga embi, nesenzo esibi ziya kutyhilwa ukuze baphendule. Kodwa abo balukhanyelayo usindiso lwam abasayi kuze bakwazi ukuma, kwaye ngokungagqibekanga bakhetha ichibi lomlilo elilungiselelwe umtyholi neengelosi zakhe kwaye into enjalo ayimfuneko, kodwa ngamnye ukhululekile ukukhetha. Ndingulowo uzayo kugweba nokulwa, kwaye iindlela zikaYahweh ziya kulawula umhlaba ngobulungisa kubukumkani bam obuzayo. Nina Bandla lam, nkqu noMtshakazi wam oxabisekileyo niya kunyuselwa emazulwini, kwaye niya kufumana ilifa lenu, kwaye niya kuthatha iindawo zenu phakathi kweendidi endinilungiselele zona, nkqu nendawo yenu phakathi koYakobi kuba ninguSirayeli kaThixo.

Kuba kum, Krestu Yesu, akukho mYuda namGentile kodwa kukho isidalwa esitsha, kwaye eso sidalwa siya kuhamba njengomntu omnye omtsha. Yabonani! Niya kuba ngumntu omtsha, kwaye niya kuba sisidalwa esitsha, andinikanga na intlawulo yokuqala yomoya oyingcwele? Nkqu netywina likaThixo ophilayo, elingenakwaphulwa? Ngubani kuni onokutshitshisa ukuzalwa kwabantwana benu bendalo? Aninakukwazi kuba imfuza yenu idluliselwa kwizizukulwana ngezizukulwana kwaye abantwana bahleli ngonaphakade. Kwaye kunjalo, imbewu kaYahweh eyadalwa ngaphakathi kwabantwana bakaThixo ayinakonakaliswa, kuba akunakwenzeka ukuba nixhwilwe ezandleni zam. Anifundanga na? Kuba nizelwe ngokutsha, kungekhona ngembewu enokufa, kodwa ngeyokungafi, ngelizwi likaThixo eliphilayo, nelihleli ngonaphakade. Bantwana bam, amazwi am ayamangalisa kakhulu na? Nivumele amazwi obuxoki anilahlekise ekunyanisekeni kuKrestu? Akukho nto inokususa imbewu kaYahweh yakuba idaliwe ngaphakathi, kuba izipho nobizo lukaThixo azinazijiko. Ngoko kutheni niluthandabuza usindiso lwenu? Nangona inyama yenu iya kunenza none, kwaye ndiya kunixolela ndinihlambulule njengoko nivuma izono zenu ngenguquko, akukho nto inokususa abantwana bakaThixo kusapho lukaThixo. Kwabo baphila ngokungalunganga imisebenzi yabo iya kutsha, kwaye baya kuma benqunu beneentloni, kwaye kwabo imisebenzi yabo imi njengelungileyo baya kufumana umvuzo omkhulu, kodwa abantwana bakaThixo bafumana usindiso olusisigxina. Ukuba nawuphi na umntu akanamoya kaKrestu, akangowakhe.

Kwabo baphila ubomi bokreqo nakwabo bazenza ngathi baphilela uKrestu ngegama kuphela baya kuwa xa iBandla lokwenyani lihamba. Kuba lowo uhlwayelela inyama uya kuvuna ukonakala enyameni. Kwaye kwabo bazama ukundenza iNkosi ngentliziyo engenakholo baza baqhubeka esonweni baya kushiyelwa kwingxaki kaYakobi ukuze kutshatyalaliswe inyama kwaye kuya kufuneka babize egameni leNkosi kude kube sekupheleni, kwaye kuya kuba njalo. Abo bazikhohlisayo de baphefumle okokugqibela baza abafa bekuKrestu kwaye abazange babe nobudlelwane obusindisayo nam baya kujongana nomgwebo wabo. Nicinga ukuba uYahweh ongumphengululi weentliziyo usisidenge? Nicinga ukuba mna, Krestu Yesu, andizivavanyi iintliziyo zabantu ngokholo nokuthembela kum? Kodwa kuya kuba njalo, kwaye ndiya kuthi kwabaninzi abandibiza ngeNkosi: Mkani kum, nina benzi bobubi, andizange ndinazi.

Kodwa kuni Bandla lam, ababiziweyo bam nabathembekileyo nabanyuliweyo: Wonke umphefumlo ofumene usindiso wenziwe waba yindlalifa yezithembiso ezenziwa nguYahweh kooYise. Kuba kubhaliwe ukuba iiNtlanga zimele zibe ziindlalifa kunye nabo, zibe zomzimba omnye, nabaxhamli besithembiso sakhe kuKrestu ngeendaba ezilungileyo, kwaye ndibaxhumele kwiMnonono yendalo. Kwaye bonke abadaliweyo ngokutsha kum baya kubuyiselwa ngokupheleleyo ekugqibeleleni nasebulungiseni nasebungcweleni bokwenyani. Andinithembisanga na iingubo ezingenabala, bantwanana bam? Kwaye ndimisele ilifa lenu phakathi kwezizwe, kuba ninguSirayeli kaThixo endimkhululeyo emhlabeni.

Yekani ukucinga ngokwemizila yozalo luka-Adam, kuba igazi lika-Adam lonakele esonweni! Njengoko kubhaliwe: umntu wokuqala uAdam wenziwa umphefumlo ophilayo, kwaye uAdam wokugqibela wenziwa umoya ophilisa. Anifundanga na? Asingabo bonke abangamaSirayeli, abavela kwaSirayeli. Kuba abantwana besithembiso yimbewu ka-Abraham ngokholo, kwaye njengoko kubhaliwe oLungileyo uya kuphila ngokholo. Kwaye kum, Krestu Yesu, zonke izinto zigqityiwe kuba iNgonyama iya kuba kumanyano olugqibeleleyo neMvana kwaye nina nichongwe ngokupheleleyo nam, Krestu Yesu iNkosi yenu. Yabonani! Kodwa kuni abaloyikayo iGama lam, kuya kuphuma unyana wobulungisa! Ndingunyana woBulungisa, nkqu nonyana okuphela kwamzelweyo kaThixo ophezukonke, uYahweh, kuba akukho namnye onjengaye, kwaye ndingulowo ulapha ukuze ndixhwile ibandla lam kwingozi, nkqu nomtshakazi wam ndimse ekhayeni lakhe emazulwini, kwaye uya kuhlala enam ngonaphakade. Kuba kuya kuba njalo, ndiya kunidibanisa nosapho lwenu lokwenyani kuba uSirayeli wasasazwa phesheya kwaye kuSirayeli kaThixo baya kuqokelelwa, bangaphinde basasazwe kwakhona, kuba ninguSirayeli kaThixo!

Ngoko ke ndiya kulixhwila iBandla lam ndilise emazulwini, abathembekileyo bam, kwaye niya kuhlala nam ngonaphakade. Kuba niqeshelwe indoda enye: uYeshua HaMashiach, kwaye ngomoya wam ndinenze intombi enyulu kwaye ndabahlambulula abathembekileyo ukuze bame belungile kwidini lam lokucamagushela. Xa nithathwa niya kuvuzwa ngokuthembeka kwenu kwaye zininzi izithsaba eniya kuzithatha, nangona abanye baya kulahlekelwa. Niya kuvuzwa ngaphaya kweengcinga zenu eziphambeneyo, kuba iliso alikabonanga nendlebe ayikavanga kwaye akukangeni entliziyweni yomntu izinto uYahweh azilungiselele abo bamthandayo. Kwaye abathembekileyo, nkqu noMtshakazi wam, baya kuhlala nam ngonaphakade, kwaye baya kulawula njengookumkani nababingeleli ngonaphakade, kuba akukho siphelo kuBukumkani bam.

Kwaye ngoko ingelosi yam iya kunyukela apho niya kuba nam emazulwini kwaye itywina i-12,000 kwisizwe ngasinye. Aniwafundanga na aMazwi am anikwe uYohane uMtyhili? Kwaye ndabona enye ingelosi inyuka empumalanga, inetywina likaThixo ophilayo: yaye yadanduluka ngelizwi elikhulu kwiingelosi ezine, ezanikwa ukuba ziwonakalise umhlaba nolwandle, isithi, musani ukuwonakalisa umhlaba, nolwandle nemithi, side sibatywine abakhonzi boThixo wethu emabunzini abo. Anilibonanga na igama elithi nyuka? Iingelosi ezingcwele zezulu zehlela emhlabeni, kuba ikhaya lazo lisemazulwini. Ukuze ingelosi engcwele inyuke, inyukela emazulwini. Ziingelosi eziwileyo ezinyukela emhlabeni ziphuma enzonzobileni, aniwahloniphi na aMazwi elizwi lam elingcwele?

Kwaye ziya kutywina i-12,000 kwisizwe ngasinye esidwelisiweyo: uYuda, uRubhen, uGadi, uAshere, uNafetali, uManase, uSimeyon, uLevi, uIsakare, uZebhulon, uYosefu, uBhenjamin. Kwaye ngoko i-144,000 yam iya kuba netywina likaThixo ophilayo emabunzini abo. Kwaye baya kuma nam eNtabeni yeZiyon, kwaye baya kucula ngokungathi yingoma entsha phambi kwetrone, naphambi kwezilo ezine, nabadala: kwaye akukho mntu unokuyifunda loo ngoma ngaphandle kwekhulu elinamashumi amane anesine lamawaka, abakhululweyo emhlabeni. Aba ngabo bangazidyobhisanga nabafazi; kuba baziintombi. Aba ngabo balandelayo iMvana naphi na apho iya khona. Kuba aba bakhululwa phakathi kwabantu, bengumlibo kuThixo nakwiMvana. Kwaye emlonyeni wabo akufunyanwanga buqhinga: kuba abanasiphako phambi kwetrone kaThixo.

I-144,000 ikhethwe nguYahweh phambi kokusekwa kwehlabathi ngexesha elinje. Bakhandwe embandezelweni, kwaye baya kuba nezakhono ezizodwa namandla endiya kubanxibisa ngeengubo zabo ezintsha endiya kubanika, kuba iingubo zenu zithungelwe nina ngokukodwa kwaye ziya kuba nempumelelo neempawu zenu. Baya kuthathwa ekuqokelelweni kwabathembekileyo kodwa babekelwe bucala iinjongo zam, nkqu neenjongo zikaYahweh noYeshua HaMashiach. Baza ngexesha lembandezelo enkulu ukukhokelela uSirayeli kukhuseleko. Kodwa kuqala bathathwa ukuze batshate noKrestu kuba banguMtshakazi wakhe, ababiziweyo, abanyuliweyo, abathembekileyo, nabanyanisekileyo. Nkqu nabo uKrestu abenze banyulu njengentombi ngegazi lakhe lokucamagushela. Kwaye nguwe mtshakazi wam oya kukhonza iinjongo zikaYahweh ngomyalelo wam ngamaxesha eengxaki ezinkulu.

Kuba niziintlahlela eziqokelelweyo kumasimi esivuno, kuba mna ndiyiNkosi yesivuno, kwaye ngoku lixesha lokuba ndiniqokelelele esiphandeni sam ukuze nifumane umvuzo wemisebenzi yenu. Kwabo baqokelele ingqolowa baya kuba nentabalala kwaye abo baqokelele iindiza baya kulahlekelwa njengoko umququ utsha emlilweni, kodwa noko baya kusindiswa. Kwaye ngoko abasebenzi bam nabantu bam baya kuza elifeni labo kum, uYeshua HaMashiach, kuba ndiyiNkosi yeeNkosi noKumkani wooKumkani kwaye andiyi kuthuliswa kwaye andiyi kunqandwa.

Akubhalwanga na? Itsho iNkosi uYehova; Yabonani, ndiya kubathabatha abantwana bakwaSirayeli phakathi kweentlanga, naphi na apho baye khona, ndibabuthe macala onke, ndibazise elizweni labo: Ndiya kubenza uhlanga olunye elizweni ezintabeni zakwaSirayeli; nokumkani omnye uya kuba ngukumkani kubo bonke: kwaye abasayi kuba zizizwe ezibini kwakhona, bengasayi kwahlulwa babe zizikumkani ezibini kwakhona konke konke: Bengasayi kuzidyobhisa kwakhona ngezithixo zabo, nangezinto zabo ezicekisekayo nangezono zabo zonke: kodwa ndiya kubasindisa kuzo zonke iindawo zabo zokuhlala, apho bonileyo khona, ndibahlambulule: ngoko baya kuba ngabantu bam, kwaye mna ndiya kuba nguThixo wabo.

Mna Yeshua HaMashiach ndithumele la Mazwi ngomkhonzi wam, kwaye ngomkhonzi wam la Mazwi athunyelwe! Yabonani! Ndingulowo uzayo emafini, ngoko zilungiseleleni, zintombi zam ezilumkileyo.

Kusuka kuYahweh uThixo Omkhulu Ongunaphakade:

Ndiyabona uloyiko lwenu, nina bawileyo, kuba ngoku kuza umgwebo wenu nkqu nomgwebo omkhulu owathenjiswa mandulo, namaxesha omgwebo omkhulu owabhengezwa kumaxesha adlulileyo ndim, uYahweh uThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade owabhengeza umgwebo wenu kwaye iindudumo ezisixhenxe zakhupha amazwi azo kwaye ngoko ilizwi likaThixo usomandla liya kuviwa! Kwaye iinjongo zam ziya kudityaniswa, nkqu nezicwangciso zam zeminyaka ziya kugqitywa kunyana wam uYesu Krestu. Kuba kwiintlahlela kuza imivuzo yesivuno sabasebenzi abathunyelwe nguYeshua HaMashiach kwaye ingqolowa yam ngoku iya kuqokelelwa esiphandeni sam kwaye ukwahlulwa kokugqibela emhlabeni kuya kuqala kwaye ingxaki kaYakobi ilapha.

Yabonani! Ndiya kuwahlula amazulu phezulu ndishukumise umhlaba ngaphantsi, kwaye ndiya kuqokelela iindlalifa eziseleyo zosindiso emhlabeni, kwaye ziya kuza elusindisweni, kwaye ziya kubiza egameni leNkosi kude kube sekupheleni, kwaye ziya kundikhonza etempileni yam kwaye isihlwele esinamandla nesikhulu siya kusindiswa kwiveki yamashumi asixhenxe kaDaniyeli. Mna, nkqu noYahweh, ndiya kuwukrazula umhlaba ndisuse iintaba ezindaweni zazo kwaye lonke ihlabathi liya kwazi ukuba uEl Shaddai uvukile phezulu kwaye umgwebo omkhulu ngoku uya kuqhambuka phezu komhlaba xa aBathembekileyo bam beqokelelwe kuba izono ziya kupheliswa, nkqu nesono sikaYakobi nezono zendlu kaSirayeli.

Endaweni yabo kuya kuza ulawulo lobulungisa, nkqu nolawulo olulungileyo lonyana wam okuphela kwamzelweyo, uYeshua HaMashiach, endihlala ndikholiswa nguye. Nguye oya kuzibetha iintlanga ngentonga yentsimbi kwaye kuya kuba lulawulo lobulungisa, apho iNgonyama iya kulala phantsi nemvana. Kwaye kuya kuba njalo umbingeleli omkhulu ngonaphakade ngokohlobo lukaMelkitsedeke neNkosi yeeNkosi noKumkani wooKumkani baya kumanyana ngokupheleleyo ekugqityweni okungunaphakade kwaye ulawulo lwakhe luya kuba ngonaphakade, nkqu noYeshua HaMashiach kuba okufanayo kukwanguYesu Krestu!

Zilungiseleleni nina bahlala emhlabeni ngokungalunganga kuba ndithumela unyana wam ukuba enze umgwebo wam kwaye aqhekeze zonke iilawulo zabo bawileyo, kuba amaxesha abo afikile kwaye umgwebo uya kuwa nzima emhlabeni. Kuba niziqwebele ingqumbo enkulu ngomhla wengqumbo kwaye umhla wengqumbo uphezu kwenu, nina bangenaxabiso, kuba nizidyobhile kububi obugqithisileyo, kwaye aninaluxolo ngeendlela zenu ezimdaka nezicekisekayo. Kodwa phambi kokuba niye kwilifa lenu elingunaphakade apho niya kugutyungelwa ngawona malangatye ashushu aluhlaza, niya kuqala nikhonze iinjongo zam, kuba nkqu neentshaba zam ziyalithobela ilizwi lam.

Kwaye ngoko umgwebo uya kuqala endlwini kaThixo kwaye ndiya kuthumela unyana wam nkqu noKrestu Yesu uMesiya ovusiweyo kaSirayeli nomsindisi wehlabathi, kwaye uya kwenza konke endikumisileyo ndakuthembisa kooYise nakubantwana kwaye konke kuya kuba nguhili no-amen njengoko ukuzaliseka kwezithembiso kusenzeka.

Kuba amazulu enzelwe abantwana bam, nkqu nabo bonke endibamisele iinjongo zam phambi kokuba indalo iqale. Kuba ndandimazi ngamnye wenu koonaphakade abadlulileyo, kwaye ndanibeka njengemfihlelo efihliweyo entliziyweni yam endayityhila kuphela ngexesha elifanelekileyo kwisicwangciso sam esingunaphakade sosindiso. Akukho nto inokwenzeka endingayaziyo phambi kokuba yenzeke, kwaye ndim, uThixo uSomandla, obiza izinto ezingekhoyo ngokungathi sele zikho. Owu, nina bawileyo, niya kutsha ngokupheleleyo kumgwebo wenu osondelayo, kwaye nanizizidenge ngokwaneleyo ukucinga ukuba ningamthatha uYahweh ngobuqhetseba, kodwa Ndingulowo uthatha abanekratshi emgwebeni, kuba balumkile kwiingcinga zabo. Kuba ndanibonisa kuphela iimfihlelo ezingenaxabiso, kuba ndandisazi ukuba niya kungcatsha konke okwam. Hayi indlela enizizidenge ngayo nina bawileyo kwaye anilumkanga. Konke enikufezileyo kukuzithuthumbisa kwenu nokutshatyalaliswa okuqhubekayo kumalangatye ashushu kakhulu, kwaye niya kuqubha kwisulfure evuthayo, kuba ngoko ichibi elikhulu litsha ngonaphakade, kwaye ndiya kubeka itywina, kwaye niya kulityalwa ngokupheleleyo, kwaye aniyi kuza engqondweni.

Kodwa esi siphelo asisesenu banyuliweyo bam, abathembekileyo bam, nkqu nabo bonke ababizwe ngegama lam. Elenu likamva lovuyo olungunaphakade, ulonwabo olungunaphakade, ulonwabo olugqithisileyo kwaye akuyi kubakho siphelo seentsuku kwaye akusayi kuze kubekho usuku olubi kwabo bangena eparadesi. Yabonani! Ndiya kuzibuyisela zonke izinto kwaye ndizenze zibe ngcono kunokugqibelela kwam koqobo phambi kokuwa kokonakala, kuba ndiya kudala izulu elitsha nomhlaba omtsha apho kuhlala ubulungisa kwaye akukho nto iya kungena embi okanye edyobhekileyo. Kuba ngaphandle kukho izinja, nabathakathi, nabahenyuzi, nababulali, nabakhonzi bezithixo, naye nabani na othanda enze ubuxoki, kwaye bonke baya kuba nesabelo sabo kwelo chibi livuthayo apho impethu yabo ingafi.

Ngoko akukho nto yokoyika kwikamva lenu babiziweyo bam, abanyuliweyo, nabathembekileyo, kwaye niya kuhlala nilawula noYeshua HaMashiach kwaye akukho siphelo kubukumkani bakhe okanye kulawulo lwakhe. Kwaye mna uThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade, nkqu noYahweh ngokwakhe ndiya kwenza ikhaya lam phakathi kwabantu kwaye kuya kubakho uxolo nokugqibelela okunobunye phezu kwayo yonke indalo yam kwaye ndiya kuzosula zonke iinyembezi emehlweni abo kwaye akusayi kuphinda kuvuke mntu uya kunikhathaza nakancinane, kuba mna, uYahweh, nkqu noThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade, ndizifunga ezi zinto ngegama lam kuba andinakufunga ngomkhulu. Kodwa kuni enoyika igama lam kuya kuphuma uNyana woBulungisa enokuphilisa emaphikweni akhe; kwaye niya kuphuma, nikhule njengamathole esitalini.

Mna Yahweh, uThixo Omkhulu ongunaphakade, ndithumele aMazwi am ngomkhonzi wam, kwaye ngomkhonzi wam aMazwi am athunyelwe.

NDINGULOWO NDINGUYE kwaye akukho wumbi!

Mange uNyana hleze aqumbe, nize nitshabalale endleleni, xa ingqumbo yakhe ivutha kancinane.
