Ubungqina Obubini: Icawa
KwiMisebenzi kaYohane uMbhaptizi
Matshi 10, 2024

Abalusi Abaphambukileyo

INkosi Enkulu, uYesu Krestu, ithi kubalusi abaphambukileyo:

Nithi nithetha egameni leNkosi, mna, Yesu Krestu; kanti nilazi nje igama lam kodwa aniphili ngeendlela zam yaye anithetheli mna. Nakhela abantu bam imithombo eyaphukileyo engenamanzi, baze banxanwe. Ngaphezu koko, nizondle ngenkohlakalo izisu zenu njengoko nivuyela iinkanuko zeentliziyo zenu, yaye ninika inyama yenu konke ukwaneliseka nentlonipho. Ezintliziyweni zenu nikhohlisiwe njengoko nicinga ukuba nindilandela ngokupheleleyo yaye nimele mna ebantwini bam, kanti nithetha ubuxoki obungenantsingiselo, ubuxoki obufanele buphoswe enzulwini enkulu, amazwi angenalukholo anganikwanga ndim, amazwi obumnyama nokuzixhobisa ngoxa ningenamandla kwaphela, ningenamandla okukhokela yaye ningenamandla okwalusa. Thengani iyeza lamehlo kum nize nithambise amehlo enu. Vulani iindlebe zenu nize niphulaphule ilizwi lam.

Intlekele enkulu nentshabalalo zikulo ihenyukazi elikhulu, yaye amaxesha alo amisiwe nemihla yalo ibaliwe. Umgwebo walo uya kufika ngequbuliso yaye ungenaluncedo. Kanti wena uthe entliziyweni yakho, Ndingummeli kaThixo. Ndingumalusi olilungisa. Ndiluzuko lukaThixo, yaye ngemisebenzi yezandla zam ndingumzekelo omkhulu wothando lukaThixo. Siyathandini! Ungabalusa njani abantu bam ngoxa ungandazi nokundazi mna, Yesu Krestu, uMalusi oyinyaniso nolilungisa? Mkani kum, kuba andinazi! Usuke waba ngumqeshwa, ulangazelela ukunqulwa ngabantu. Ululibele uthando lwakho lokuqala. Guqukani nibuyele kum. Yimelani mna. Lusani abantu bam ningxibe ezirhwexayo nothuthu lwenguquko. Nizondla izisu zenu yaye ningalithengisa iLizwi lam ngenxa yenzuzo yenu yobugwenxa njengoko nifundisa abantu bam esonweni.

Inene, umgwebo uphezu kohlanga lwenu, yaye nangona bendiya kunahlulela ukuba nibe ngabantu bam, niyabaleka kwilizwi lam nize nizale iindlela zam, kanti ekukhohlisweni kwenu nizibiza ngokuba ningcwele yaye ningumzekelo emhlambini. Gxilisani ingqalelo neendlebe zenu kwilizwi lam. Guqukani nize kum, ukuze ndisabasindise nina nabantu abanilandayo; bendiya kunisindisa kwintlekele nasekubandezelekeni! Abo bathatha usindiso njengemvume yokona baya kuthwala umgwebo wabo, yaye imisebenzi yenu iya kutshiswa. Abo baphila ngolo hlobo baya kuvuna ubumnyama obungaphandle. Nithi nizizityebi, kodwa nilihlwempu. Nithi ninamandla, niyalela wona amandla kaThixo ngamazwi enu, kodwa amazwi enu amahle neentetho zenu ezintle azinamandla kwaphela. Andinibizanga na? Andininikanga njongo na? Kanti nithi andikho kwiCawa yam, kanti nizenza ukuba ningabikho ebuNkosini bam. Into ayihlwayelayo umntu, uya kuyivuna kwakhona.

Umgwebo uya kufika ngequbuliso kuhlanga lwenu, yaye niya kuma nam ukuze ndinihlangule, okanye niya kuma nabagwetyiweyo nize nibe nenxaxheba kwizono zalo nezibetho zomgwebo ezizayo. Isono sinemiphumo nakwabo baphethe itywina likaThixo ophilayo. Inene, lifikile ixesha apho umgwebo uya kuqala endlwini kaThixo. Zihlambeni ezonweni zenu egazini lam lentlawulelo! Guqukani elubala ngeendlela zenu. Jikani nize kum kuba ixesha seliphantse laphela. Nawuphi na umntu ozibalela ukuba uyinto ngoxa engeyonto ukhohlisiwe yaye ubanjwe kumnatha wobugwenxa owenze amehlo akhe angaboni waza wavala iindlebe zakhe. Guqukani nize kum ngentliziyo yenu yonke kungekabi mva kakhulu.

NdinguYehova onenceba. Ndinikwe igama elingaphezu kwawo onke amagama, yaye ndingoyena ubalaseleyo, owaxhelwayo kwasekusekweni kwehlabathi. Ndingumgwebi olilungisa, iNkosi yenu nomkhululi wenu, uKrestu Yesu! Ndingunyana kaThixo owenziwa ngokwenyama kodwa wathatha inxaxheba kuphela njengoko ndahlala ndingenasono. Ndabethelelwa emnqamlezweni phantsi koPontiyo Pilato ndaza ndafa njengePasika yenu engenasono. Akukho mntu wathatha ubomi bam, kodwa ndabunikezela ngokukhululekileyo. Ndavuswa ngomhla wesithathu ndaza ndenyukela emazulwini. NdinguNyana kaThixo oweza esenyameni. NdinguMalusi olilungisa ongalahlekelwanga nanguye nomnye endimnikiweyo. Yivani amazwi am ningawathandabuzi. Ningawabekeli ecaleni amazwi am ngokungathi yinto elula leyo.

Bonke endibathandayo, ndiyabohlwaya. Bonke endibathandayo, ndiyabakhalimela. Ukuba uBawo uniphatha njengonyana, ke nisikelelekile, kuba utata uyabohlwaya abantwana bakhe ukuze beme baqine yaye bathe tye baze bafumane umvuzo omkhulu. Imivuzo yokuthembeka nezithsaba ezininzi ziya kubekwa ezintlokweni zabo ngenxa yokuthobela. Jikani nize kum ngentliziyo yenu yonke nize nive amazwi am. Ndiyayibona imisebenzi yenu, kodwa ndinqwenela intliziyo yenu epheleleyo ngaphezu kwemisebenzi yenu. Lumkisani abantu bam ngesaqhwithi esizayo, ukuze bazilungiselele baze bahlambuluke ngenxa yokuphuncuka kwam okukhulu okuzayo, xa ndiya kuwuhlanganisela kum uMzimba wam ndize ndibafihle endlwini kaBawo kude nomhla wembandezelo. Yahlukanani nesono nize nibuyele kum, Yesu Krestu, bantu bam, njengoko ndinibizile yaye ndinqwenela ukulwenza luqinisekile unyulo lwenu.

NdinguYehova onenceba, yaye ndinikwe igama elingaphezu kwawo onke amagama. Ndingoyena ubalaseleyo, owaxhelwayo kwasekusekweni kwehlabathi. Ndingumgwebi olilungisa, iNkosi yenu nomkhululi wenu, uKrestu Yesu!

NdinguNyana kaThixo owenziwa ngokwenyama kodwa wathatha inxaxheba kuphela njengoko ndahlala ndingenasono.

NdinguNyana kaThixo oweza esenyameni.

NdinguMalusi olilungisa ongalahlekelwanga nanguye nomnye endimnikiweyo. Ndizizo zonke ezi zinto nokunye okuninzi.
