Okukrakra Nokumnandi
Julayi 17, 2024

Aze Ngoko Atshintshe Amaxesha Onyaka!

Zihlanganiseni, Bandla lam, niboleke iindlebe zenu kubulumko. Thobani iindlebe zenu kwilizwi loKumkani wenu oligorha, yena kanye omiyo ukuze anilwele, mna kanye iNgonyama yesizwe sakwaYuda onguNkosi yeeNkosi noKumkani wooKumkani. Ndim lowo uYahweh amnike zonke izinto ezulwini nasemhlabeni, ezandleni zam ezinamandla kuba ndiphila ngonaphakade kwaye ndiphethe izitshixo zokufa nezesihogo ezandleni zam, kwaye umhlaba ngowam kuba ndiwuthengile ngegazi lam lentlawulelo. Kwaye ndinegama elingaphezu kwawo onke amagama ukuze ngeGama likaYesu onke amadolo aguqe, ezinto ezisezulwini, nezinto ezisemhlabeni, nezinto eziphantsi komhlaba. Kwaye ukuze zonke iilwimi zivume ukuba uYesu Krestu uyiNkosi, kube luzuko kuYise wezikhanyiso, kuye kungekho kuguquka nasithunzi sokujika. Kwaye ngubani onokumelana nekrele elintlangothi-mbini eliphuma emlonyeni wam? Kuba ngalo, ndiya kuzibetha iintlanga: kwaye ndim oya kuzilawula ngentonga yentsimbi: kwaye ndim onyathela isixovulelo sewayini yomsindo nengqumbo ka-El Shaddai, uThixo uSomandla. Kwaye ndim lowo onesambatho sakhe, nasethangeni lakhe kubhalwe igama elithi, UKUMKANI WOOKUMKANI, NENKOSI YEENKOSI, kwaye ixesha lam LIFIKILE!

Yintoni ekhoyo enokufunyanwa kuyo yonke indalo kaYahweh, bantwana bam, enifanele niyoyike? Yazini inyaniso, bancinane bam, ukuba abawileyo bayoyika nina. Kuba bayazi ukuba ngoku banjengani, kwaye nina niya kwenziwa nozuko olukhulu kunabo babekade benjalo, bonke abo balishiyayo ilungelo labo lokuqala. Ukusuka kwimithetho ayibekayo uYahweh kwindalo yakhe sisono esinzulu kuba indalo kaYahweh yenzelwe ukusebenza ngemvisiswano egqibeleleyo. Kwaye uYahweh unika inkululeko yokuzikhethela, ngoko ukukhetha ukuvukela kwiinkanuko zokuzicingela kubangela ukufa nokubandezeleka kubomi obuhle. Kwaye ke isono sabawileyo sisavakala nanamhlanje, kwaye amaxesha okugwetywa kwabo ngoku! Anazi na, bantwana bam, ukuba niya kugweba iingelosi?

Ngubani oya kunahlula eluthandweni lwam? Yimbandezelo, luxinzelelo, yintshutshiso, yindlala, bubuze, yingozi, likrele? Hayi! Kuzo zonke ezi zinto ningaphezu kwaboyisi kum Krestu Yesu iNkosi yenu enyukileyo nenikwe amandla, kuba uYahweh wabelane nam ngetrone yakhe kwaye nam ndiya kwabelana ngetrone yam nabathembekileyo, abo bandithandayo nabathandwa ndim. Qondani ukuba uYahweh ngokwakhe uniyalwela kwaye uYahweh uthume mna, iNkosi yenu, uKumkani nomkhuseli wenu. Kuba ndiyinqaba yenu eyomeleleyo neliwa lokuzikhusela, kuba ndiliLiwa laMaphakade, iNkosi noMsindisi wenu. Kwaye ndingumcoci ohleli nesivuthelo nesando esandleni ukuze aphembe imililo yokuhlanjululwa esusa inkunkuma! Kwaye ndim okhanda aze asonge iintsimbi ezixabisekileyo zendlu yobuncwane kaYahweh, apho ubuncwane bakhe obuninzi bububuncwane obungenaxabiso obungoonyana bakaThixo ukuze babe nokugqibeleleka okuzukileyo, kwaye imisebenzi yam isondele ekugqityweni kuni bantwana bam, nina zintlahlela zam nababiziweyo nabakhethiweyo nabathembekileyo.

Yabonani! Ixesha lifikile lokugqitywa nokukhululwa ngokupheleleyo kwenu, zithandwa zam, kwaye ndiya kuyigqibezela inkululeko yenu kwaye ngonaphakade kanaphakade niya kungena ngoku! Kwaye ngephanyazo leso niya kuzifumana nimi kwigumbi letrone kaYahweh nimangalisiwe ezulwini kwaye ninovuyo olungunaphakade, kuba ngoku niya kungena eluvuyweni lweNkosi yenu. Kwaye niya kuzifumana nigutyungelwe kukukhanya okuzukileyo okungunaphakade kukaYahweh, uThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade nokukhanya okungunaphakade koNyana woBulungisa kwaye niya kudalwa ngokutsha ngokupheleleyo, kwaye niya kumangaliswa bubuhle eniya kwambathiswa bona. Bantwana bam, wonke umsebenzi enikhe nawenza egameni lam ekukhonzeni uKrestu, ekubeni nikhonze abangcwele kwaye nisakhonza, ubhaliwe emazulwini! Iso alikabonanga, nendlebe ayivanga, yaye akukangeni entliziyweni yomntu izinto uYahweh azilungiselele abo bamthandayo kwaye umvuzo wenu usesandleni sam, kuba nina ningaboyisi bam, kwaye ndinidale ngokutsha kum ngonaphakade, kwaye anisoze nonakale kwaye ilifa lenu lelonaphakade elingasoze liphele kwaye uzuko lwenu lolunaphakade. Kuba ekubeni nithembekile kwezona zinto zincinane ndiya kunenza abalawuli bezinto ezininzi.

Ngesiquphe, ngomzuzwana, ngeqhezu lomzuzwana inkululeko yenu epheleleyo iya kwenzeka kwaye zonke iingxaki zenu ziya kunyamalala kwangoko. Ngesiquphe akusayi kubakho kugula, ngesiquphe akusayi kubakho ntlungu, ngesiquphe akusayi kubakho loyiko, ngesiquphe niya kuba kubunye obugqibeleleyo noYahweh, ngesiquphe niya kuba kubunye obugqibeleleyo noYeshua HaMashiach, ngesiquphe niya kuzifumana nizele luvuyo, luxolo, nokukhanya okungunaphakade kuya kukhanya kuni ngonaphakade. Kuba ekubeni nizukise iMvana kaThixo, iNgonyama yesizwe sakwaYuda iya kunizukisa ngoku, kuba ndim enikholiweyo kuye, kwaye ndim othembekileyo nonyanisekileyo, ogcine oko nikunikele kum ngalo mhla. Kwaye nina endinenze nabalumkileyo, niya kukhanya ngonaphakade, njengokukhanya kwesibhakabhaka: kwaye nina abaguqule abaninzi babuyela ebulungiseni, niya kukhanya njengeenkwenkwezi, ngonaphakade kanaphakade. Ngoko ke ngoku niyakwazi oku bantwana bam ukuba ngoku lixesha lokushiya amaxhala eli hlabathi nize niphumle kum kwaye nihlale ninyulu njengentombi enyulu egazini lam lentlawulelo.

Yabonani! Ummangaleli wabazalwana uphoselwe phantsi, ewe, loo nyoka indala, ewe, loo namba inkulu ibomvu enomsila warhola isahlulo sesithathu seenkwenkwezi waziphosa emhlabeni. Kwaye kwabakho imfazwe ezulwini, uMikayeli neeNgelosi zakhe balwa nenamba, yaza yalwa inamba neengelosi zayo. Kodwa azoyisanga, nendawo yazo ayifunyanwanga ezulwini. Yaza inamba enkulu, loo nyoka indala, ebizwa ngokuba ngumtyholi noSathana, yaphoswa ngaphandle, leyo ilahlekisa ihlabathi lonke: iphoselwe emhlabeni, neengelosi zayo zaphoswa ngaphandle kunye nayo. Kuba kwabakho ukudanyaza nesandi esikhulu kwabakho ukushukuma kwaye umqondiso wobukho bakhe wevakala, kwaye ubuxoki bomkhohlisi onolwimi olukrazukileyo sele busasazekile kuba bebesoloko belindele ukuza komqondiso wakhe. Umqondiso wakhe wawuphezu komfanekiso wakhe onyelisayo.

Yeha, abemi bomhlaba kuba umtyholi neengelosi zakhe baphoselwe phantsi phakathi kwenu kwaye babotshelelwe emhlabeni ukuze bagwetywe kwaye akukho ndawo emazulwini kubo ngonaphakade kwaye abasayi kuze baphinde bangene. Vuyani nina mazulu, kuba ummangaleli wabazalwana uphoselwe phantsi obemangalela imini nobusuku phambi koThixo wethu, kwaye indawo yakhe ayisekho emazulwini ngonaphakade. Ngoku kuza usindiso, ngoku kuza uzuko lwabangcwele, kwaye baya kuzukiswa kuba baya kuzukisa uYahweh kum Krestu Yesu kwaye ndiya kubambathisa ngobulungisa obungunaphakade, ilinen emhlophe qhwa engenabala engenakuze idyojwe, kwaye ndiya kubavuza ngomvuzo ongunaphakade kwaye ndibenze ooKumkani nababingeleli phambi koYahweh kwaye baya kulawula besesitroneni sam ngokokuba lunjani uthando nokuthembeka kwabo. Kwabathembekileyo kukho umvuzo omkhulu kwaye kwabangathembekanga kukho ilahleko, kodwa baya kusindiswa, kunjalo ke, ngokungathi kuphuma emlilweni.

Ndiveni, nonke nina abemi emhlabeni, nangona nizizithulu kwaye niyala imiqondiso yam! Umtyholi ngoku uphoselwe phantsi phakathi kwenu, kwaye uyazi ukuba unexesha elifutshane. Unomsindo omkhulu nempindezelo, kuba ulutshaba oloyisiweyo olubotshelwe iinjongo zikaYahweh eziya kugqitywa kwelo chibi lomlilo eliluhlaza okwesibhakabhaka lokutshatyalaliswa okuqhubekayo. Kwaye ngenxa yokuba nina, hleze mntu wemvelo nithanda ubumnyama kunokukhanya, ngenxa yokuba nina, hleze mntu wemvelo nithanda imisebenzi yenu emibi kwaye nidele isandla sam esisindisayo sobabalo, (Kuba wonke umntu owenza okubi, uyakuthiya ukukhanya, aze eze ekukhanyeni, hleze izenzo zakhe zohlwaywe) ngoko ke ngoku ndiya kuninika ububi enibunqwenela kangaka. Ngenxa yokuba ngoku nihlala kumnquba kaMoloki niphephezelisa inkwenkwezi kathixo wenu uRemfan kwibhanile yenu, imifanekiso enayenzayo ukuze niqubude kuyo.

Ekubeni nibingelela abantwana kuMoloki, ekubeni nithanda uBaphomet wenu osenyanyekayo, yabonani! Inamba yenu ngoku iphakathi kwenu. Kwaye niya kufunda ngokwenu ukuba isela alizi ngaphandle kokuba lize kuba, lixhele, litshabalalise. Ngaphandle kokuba ngoku akusayi kubakho sigqubuthelo, akusayi kubakho mthinteli, akusayi kubakho nceba nobabalo oluvela emazulwini kuba nikhethe inamba njengothixo wenu, kwaye nikhethe uBharabhas njengomsindisi nokumkani wenu. Ngoko ke ngoku ndibanika ukuba balawule phezu kwenu njengoko iintliziyo zenu ezimbi zinqwenela. Yabonani! Nabu ubulumko, lowo unokuqonda makabale inani lerhamncwa kuba likwinkwenkwezi kaRemfan abayiphetheyo kwibhanile yabo, kuba bayindlu yesikhungu kaSathana abafuna ukuba ilizwe baze babulale abemi balo abafanelekileyo kwaye ndiya kumsindisa uYakobi!

Yabonani! USathana ungena kuBharabhas, kwaye uthatha iintambo, kwaye imiqondiso yakhe yobuxoki nemimangaliso iya kukhokelela ekutshatyalalisweni kwenu ngokupheleleyo. Kuba uya kusebenzisa amabango akhe okuba uThixo yedwa wamsindisa ukuze abange ukuba uthunywe nguThixo kwaye unguThixo, kuba bakholelwa ukuba banguThixo enyameni kwaye bonke abanye bazizilwanyana. Uya kusebenzisa imiqondiso yakhe yobuxoki nemimangaliso kwaye kunye nerhamncwa eliphuma elwandle uya kuzisa intshabalalo enkulu emhlabeni, kuba nikhethe inamba, kwaye ngoku niya kudla ngokupheleleyo ubumdaka obugqithisileyo obuziswa yinamba.

Nina, hleze bemi bomhlaba, nitshicele ubabalo nenceba kaYahweh awayithumela kum, ebusweni bukaYesu Krestu, kwaye ekubeni niyalile iMvana kaThixo, ngoku niya kudibana neNgonyama yesizwe sakwaYuda. Kuba ndize ngoxolo nothando nentando elungileyo nobabalo nenceba, kwaye ngobuyatha nilalile usindiso endaweni yokufa kwenu nesohlwayo esingunaphakade, kuba akukho lunye ukhetho. Ukutshicela kuYahweh xa esolula isandla sakhe ngoxolo nothando, enikela ngobomi boNyana wakhe okuphela kwamzeleyo, sisono esingunaphakade kwaye akukho kubaleka kwingqumbo ngaphandle kokubiza igama elinamandla likaYesu kude kube sekupheleni kwaye lowo ungabuthandiyo ubomi bakhe kude kube sekupheleni uya kusindiswa, kwaye isihlwele sezihlwele siya kusindiswa.

Kodwa nina, hleze mntu wemvelo, nikhethe iiyolo zesono xa nanikwe ubabalo kwaye nanikwe ithuba lokuguquka. Kuba ndolule isandla sam kuni, ndolule isandla sam kooyihlo, ndolule isandla sam kooyihlo booyihlo, kude kube yiminyaka emininzi yeeyubhile ngoku ukususela oko ndaqala ukuthumela abapostile bam nabaprofeti kwaye imfihlelo enkulu kaThixo ivakalisiwe kude kube seziphelweni zomhlaba! Kwaye ngoku ukwala kwenu kugqityiwe, kwaye ndiyamkela isigqibo senu sokugqibela, kwaye ndinguKrestu Yesu, kwaye ndim ngoku ovakalisa ukuba amaxesha eeNtlanga aphelile kwaye ngoku kuza umgwebo oqala endlwini kaThixo. Ekubeni imilebe yenu imkhanyele uYahweh, iNkosi yooMoya, ilifa lenu elimiselweyo ngoku likwelo chibi likhulu lomlilo, isulfure eqengqelekayo, kwaye ngekukulungele ukuba ubungazalwanga. Ngobuyatha nicinga ukuba ninalo lonke ixesha ehlabathini kwaye uYahweh uya kuqhubeka elindile de mhlawumbi ngenye imini niguqukele ebulungiseni kwizono zenu ezinyanyekayo. Kwaye ndinixelela ngokucacileyo ngoku, ixesha liphelile kwaye kwabaninzi baliphose ngonaphakade ithuba labo lobomi obungunaphakade kwanethuba labo lokuba phakathi koMtshakazi wam oya kuza anconywe ngabo bonke ngaloo mini.

Kuba linjalo ikamva labo bonke abathatha uphawu. Kwaye ke ndiyazisa kwiintlanga kwakhona:

UKUBA NABANI NA UYANQULA IRHAMNCWA NOMFANEKISO WALO, AZE AMKELE UPHAWU EBINZINI LAKHE, OKANYE ESANDLENI SAKHE, YENA UYA KUSELA IWAYINI YENGQUMBO KATHIXO, ETHULULWE INGENAMXUBE KWIKOMITYI YOMSIDO WAKHE, KWAYE UYA KUTHUTHUNJISWA NGOMLILO NESULFERE, PHAMBI KWEENGELOSI EZINGCWELE, NAPHAMBI KWEMVANA: KWAYE UMSI WENTUTHUMBO YABO UNYUKA NGONAPHAKADE KANAPHAKADE. KWAYE ABANAKUPHUMULA IMINI NOBUSUKU, ABO BANQULA IRHAMNCWA NOMFANEKISO WALO, NAYE NABANI NA OWAMKELA UPHAWU LWEGAMA LALO.

Kuba uphawu ngokuqinisekileyo luyasondela. Luya kuba yindlela yokungena kuluntu lwabo, kwaye baya kunixelela ukuba lunyango. Baya kunixelela ukuba luya kuzisa ubomi obungunaphakade, baya kunixelela ukuba anisoze niphinde nigule, baya kunixelela ubuninzi bobuxoki ukuze nithathe isitofu sabo soburhamncwa, kwaye baya koyisela abaninzi ukuba kungenxa yokulunga kwabo. Yabonani! Abawileyo beza bezenza abaphambukeli ngeeteknoloji zabo ezandleni zabo. Qondani ukuba uphawu luya kunenza amakhoboka kwiiteknoloji zabo, kuba baya kuzixuba nembewu yabantu: kodwa abayi kunamathelana, njengoko intsimbi ingaxutywa nodongwe. Uphawu lwabo loburhamncwa, ukulunywa yinyoka, luya kubatshabalalisa ngokupheleleyo bonke abathatha ityhefu yabo kwaye akukho kubaleka.

Yabonani! Kwaye ndeva ilizwi elikhulu liphuma eTempileni, lisithi kwiiNgelosi ezisixhenxe, Hambani, nize nithulule izitya ezisixhenxe zengqumbo kaThixo emhlabeni. Kwaye eyokuqala yahamba yaza yathulula isitya sayo emhlabeni: kwaza kwawa isilonda esibi, nesibuhlungu phezu kwabantu, ababenophawu lwerhamncwa, naphezu kwabo babenqula umfanekiso walo. Kwaye ke eli kamva lilindele bonke abazamkela ngokuzithandela intuthumbo yabo engunaphakade nabo bakhetha ukufa kwesibini. Konke kungenxa yokuba nimale uYesu waseNazarete, obengasindisa nina ngokupheleleyo. Kwaye bonke abathatha uphawu baya kuba nezilonda zabo ezimbi nezibuhlungu kwaye bonke abathatha uphawu baya kuba ngonaphakade kwilifa labo kuloo malangatye omlilo aluhlaza kubumnyama bobumnyama ngonaphakade, kwaye oku ikamva likhethwa ngokuzithandela ngabo lenzelwe bona kwaye kuya kuba njalo!

Kwaye ke ukwahlukana kokugqibela kuyaqala ngoku kwaye njengoko kwakukho imfazwe emazulwini ngoku kuya kubakho imfazwe emhlabeni kuba ndiya kulususa uxolo kwangoko kwaye kwangoko umthinteli uya kususwa kwaye ubuphambana obukhawulezileyo buya kuqhambuka emhlabeni kuba ububi abusayi kuphinda buthintelwe, kwaye ndiya kunika umntu ngamnye ngokwemisebenzi yakhe. Kwaye ke amaxesha eeNtlanga avaliwe kwaye imfazwe isondele ukuqhambuka emhlabeni, kwaye baya kuphinda baqwalasele imida yabo ibe bubukumkani bokugqibela berhamncwa apho inamba, irhamncwa eliphuma elwandle nerhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni elikwangumMoshiach wabo wobuxoki liya kuphakama kwaye ingxaki kaYakobi iya kuqhambuka emhlabeni ngesiquphe, kwaye abayi kubaleka!

Yabonani! Ndithumela ivangeli yam engunaphakade, kuba ukuthunyelwa kwayo kwavunywa mandulo nguYohane uMtyhileli xa ndandimenza abhale: Ndabona enye iNgelosi ibhabha esazulwini sezulu, inevangeli engunaphakade, ukuba iyishumayele kwabo bahleli emhlabeni, nakuzo zonke iintlanga, nezizwe, neelwimi, nabantu. Yabonani! Ubungqina bukaYesu ngumoya wesiprofeto kwaye yazini ukuba amazwi am aya kufumana amehlo, iindlebe, neentliziyo zabo bonke abathunyelwe kubo kwaye Amazwi evangeli engunaphakade atyhiliwe ngexesha lawo kwaye aya kugcinelwa abo athunyelwe kubo, ngoko ke phulaphulani ilizwi loMoshiach wenu oyiNyaniso! Kuba ndinguYeshua HaMashiach!

Kwaye ke Amazwi kaYohane uMtyhileli ami njengentuthuzelo nesibane sethemba kwabo baya kuthi bafumane usindiso:

IsiTyhilelo 21:1-7

Ndabona izulu elitsha, nomhlaba omtsha: kuba elokuqala izulu, nowokuqala umhlaba udlule, nolwandle alusekho. Kwaye mna Yohane ndabona isixeko esingcwele iYerusalem Entsha sisihla sivela kuThixo ezulwini, silungiswe njengomtshakazi ehonjiselwe umyeni wakhe. Ndeva ilizwi elikhulu liphuma ezulwini, lisithi, Yabonani, uMnquba kaThixo unabantu, kwaye uya kuhlala nabo: kwaye baya kuba ngabantu bakhe, kwaye uThixo ngokwakhe uya kuba nguThixo wabo kunye nabo. Kwaye uThixo uya kuzosula zonke iinyembezi emehlweni abo: kwaye akusayi kubakho kufa, nasizi, nakukhala, kwaye akusayi kubakho ntlungu: kuba izinto zokuqala zidlule. Kwaye lowo uhleli etroneni, uthi, Yabonani, ndenza zonke izinto zibe ntsha: waza wathi kum, Bhala: kuba la mazwi athembekile kwaye ayinyaniso. Waza wathi kum, Kugqityiwe, ndinguAlfa no-Omega, isiqalo nesiphelo. Ndiya kumnika lowo unxaniweyo, emthonjeni wamanzi obomi ngesisa. Lowo woyisayo, uya kuzidla ilifa zonke izinto, kwaye ndiya kuba nguThixo wakhe, kwaye uya kuba ngunyana wam.

Ndim lowo uYahweh amnike zonke izinto ezulwini nasemhlabeni.

Ndim oya kuzilawula ngentonga yentsimbi.

Ndim onyathela ngomsindo isixovulelo sewayini yomsindo nengqumbo ka-El Shaddai.

Ndim lowo onesambatho sakhe, nasethangeni lakhe kubhalwe igama elithi, UKUMKANI WOOKUMKANI, NENKOSI YEENKOSI, kwaye ixesha lam LIFIKILE.

Ndiyinqaba yenu eyomeleleyo neliwa lokuzikhusela, kuba ndiliLiwa laMaphakade, iNkosi noMsindisi wenu.

Ndingumcoci ohleli nesivuthelo nesando esandleni ukuze aphembe imililo yokuhlanjululwa esusa inkunkuma.

Ndim okhanda aze asonge iintsimbi ezixabisekileyo zendlu yobuncwane kaYahweh, apho ubuncwane bakhe obuninzi bububuncwane obungenaxabiso obungoonyana bakaThixo ukuze babe nokugqibeleleka okuzukileyo.

Ndim enikholiweyo kuye.

Ndim othembekileyo nonyanisekileyo, ogcine oko nikunikele kum ngalo mhla.

Mna, Yeshua HaMashiach, ndithumele la Mazwi ngomkhonzi wam, kwaye ngomkhonzi wam la Mazwi athunyelwe. Phulaphulani Amazwi am kuba kuwo kukho ubomi obungunaphakade.
