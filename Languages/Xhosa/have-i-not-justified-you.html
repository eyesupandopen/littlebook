Ubungqina Obumbaxa: Ibandla
Emisebenzini kaYohane uMbhaptizi
Epreli 01, 2024

Andikugwebelanga na?

Yithi cwaka ube seluxolweni, wazi ukuba ndiyiNkosi ekubizele ukuba ume phambi kwam uthe tye kuzo zonke iindlela zakho. Andikugwebelanga na? Andikwenzanga ube lilungisa kum, iNkosi uYesu Krestu? Akumanyaniswanga nam na ezintlungwini zam nasebuqaqawulini bam? NdiyiNkosi yeeNkosi noKumkani wooKumkani, kwaye andixoki. Izibhalo Ezingcwele azitshongo na? Yima unyanisekile uthe tye, ube seluxolweni ezintliziyweni zenu, nazi ukuba mna, iNkosi uYesu Krestu, ndininike uxolelo olugqibeleleyo noxolo phambi kwam ukuba ningaphulaphula amazwi am namkele imfundiso yam.

Ubulumko yeyona nto iphambili; ngoko ke, fumanani ubulumko — kungekhona ubulumko behlabathi obuphelela entweni engekhoyo, kodwa ubulumko beNkosi. Phumla phantsi kwesandla sam esinamandla. Kuba ukoyika iNkosi kukusuka ebubini nokuhamba uthe tye phambi kwam, itsho iNkosi enamandla, ewe iNkosi uYesu Krestu. Yivani ilizwi lam! Yivani ilizwi lam ningaphulaphuli elinye! Amazwi amaninzi ayaphikisana neLizwi lam aze anixokisele, njengoko efuna ukulawula imiphefumlo yenu kanye ukuze anenze ixhoba ukuba niyawalandela. Ningalithandi ihlabathi, bantwana bam, kwanazo nezinto ezisehlabathini. Eli hlabathi likhoyo lingendawo liza kuphela, kwaye mna, iNkosi, nditshilo, kwaye andisayi kuthuliswa kukungakholwa kwenu, kwaye andisayi kuthuliswa kukungabi nalukholo kwenu. Hayi, ndiza kukhwaza ngakumbi ukuze niyazi inyaniso yamazwi endiwathethayo. Wathelekiseni neZibhalo Ezingcwele. Andinguye na ilizwi elenziwa inyama elihleli phakathi kwenu? Andininikanga na intambiso nomthuthuzeli ndide ndifike?

Yazini, bantwana bam, ukuba ndinithengile ngegazi lam, kwaye umhlaba ngowam, kwaye kungekudala ubulungisa buza kulawula emhlabeni. Ndikuthethile. Ndininike ubomi kum; kodwa ningazali iindlela zam. Ndininike injongo; kodwa ningabukhali ubomi endinibizele kubo. Ndininike ubizo, ewe, ubizo oluphezulu olungunaphakade nolungenasiphelo semihla ukuba ningabuyela kum. Buyelani kum ngobuso nentliziyo evulekileyo, nindivumele ndinihlambulule ezonweni zenu nasebugwenxeni benu egazini lam lentlawulelo. Bonke endibathandayo, ndiyabakhalimela, ukuze nime phambi kwam nithe tye nilambethe ilinen emhlophe ecolekileyo endiza kuninika yona kungekudala.

Zonke iimpahla zenu zingamajita amdaka enizikhethele wona ukuze nizimele kum. Kodwa akukho ndawo nabani na anokuzimela kuyo ekujongeni kwam, kwaye ndinibona njengoko ninjalo ngenene, nanjengoko ndinidale ngokutsha kum. Kukho umvuzo omkhulu owabekelwa abantwana bam abathembekileyo; kodwa kwabo bandalayo, nam ndiya kubala. Kutheni nisabambelele kweli hlabathi lifayo neenkathazo neentlungu zalo? Ninokhetho kum, ukhetho izizukulwana zangaphambili zazingenawo phambi komnikelo wam wentlawulelo. Ngoko ke, nabani na ondikhanyelayo ngamaxesha okuhlaziya unesityholo esikhulu. Uthando lwam ngani lungunaphakade kwaye alunakuqhawulwa. Mna noBawo simnye, kwaye iinjongo neenjongo zethu azinakwahlulwa. UBawo undinike zonke izinto ukuze ndisindise abanye.

Ningazibeki kwindawo yabangakholwayo, njengoko ndininike izizathu ezininzi zokholo olukhulu. Ukholo olungangokhozo lwemostade luko konke enikudingayo, intembelo encinane nje. Zinxibeni iimpahla endininike zona. Andiwuthululanga na umoya wam phezu kwenu, owu, nina bathandabuzayo bam? Musani ukungakholwa kodwa kholwani kuphela, njengoko amaxesha namaxesha aphezu kwenu kwaye akukho kubuya mva. Ihlabathi enanikade nilazi alisayi kuze libuye, kwaye ihlabathi enilaziyo namhlanje lelenkohliso enkulu. Nangona izinto zisabonakala ziqhelekile kuni, konke enanikade nikuxabisile kuthatyathwe ngongendawo; kuba uneyure emisiweyo, iyure ethe ukuba andiyinqumlanga, bekungayi kusala nanye inyama iphila. Kutheni ninqwenela usuku lweNkosi oluzayo ehlabathini ukuvavanya iintliziyo zabantu? Lusuku lobumnyama apho kungekho mntu unokuma. Lusuku oloyikekayo lomgwebo nesiphumo, kwaye nakwezona ntsuku zimnyama, nabani na obiza egameni leNkosi kude kube sekupheleni uya kusindiswa.

Amaxesha namaxesha ayatshintsha phambi kwamehlo enu, kwaye noko anikholwa ngamehlo enu. Nangona ndisenza imiqondiso nemimangaliso kuni naphakathi kwenu, nisawavala amehlo enu nize nithi nkqi iindlebe zenu ngengqiqo yokungakholwa. Ubabalo nenceba yam zihlala ngonaphakade kwizizukulwana ngezizukulwana, kodwa amaxesha ayatshintsha, kwaye umgwebo nentlekele kuyasondela kwihlabathi enilithanda kakhulu, kwaye izinto enizixabisileyo ziya kutyibilika eminweni yenu njengentlabathi. Kungekudala ihlabathi alisayi kuba naso isimemo sobabalo nenceba, kodwa abo baya kusindiswa kuya kufuneka banyamezele kude kube sekuphefumleni kwabo kokugqibela ukuze basindiswe kum. Buyelani kum namkele isimemo sam, kuba ukuba kuKrestu kukuba nobudlelwane obusindisayo nam, iNkosi yeeNkosi noKumkani wooKumkani. Nguwe wedwa onokulumisa usindiso lwakho ngokundifulathela ngokungakholwa nangokwala ubizo lwam. Khumbulani uEsawu, owathengisa ngobuzibulo bakhe ngeqhekeza lesonka esalahlwa kwimbalela, owanika ilifa lakhe kumntakwabo ngokungakholwa. Ukungahoyi amazwi am nokuhamba ebumnyameni sisono, ngoko musani ukona nime phambi kwam.

Kutheni nisazibala njengabangafanelekanga lubabalo lwam, ekubeni into ekufuneka niyenzile kukwamkela? Yamkelani imisebenzi yam yentlawulelo ebomini benu nindivumele ndingene ndinincede ngexesha lenu lokuswela, ukuba nje iintliziyo zenu ezingakholwayo zinganivumela. Khethani ubomi, kuba namhlanje lixesha lokukhetha; yivani ilizwi lam ningazenzi lukhuni iintliziyo zenu entlango njengoko ooyihlo benjenjalo baza bawa ecaleni kwendlela. Musani ukuwa kude nam; musani ukulandela intshabalalo neendlela ezonakalisayo zokungakholwa, kodwa khangelani kum nithembe ilizwi lam. Ewe, izimvu zam ziyaliva ilizwi lam, ngoko ndilandeleni niye kukhuseleko. Musani ukucinga ukuba intliziyo yokungakholwa inokuma ingcwalisiwe phambi kwam, kuba ukungakholwa kukukwala ukholo endininike lona kum.

Ndingcwaliseni, iNkosi uYesu Krestu, ezintliziyweni zenu ningoyiki, kuba ukubonakala kwam kusondele kwaye kusondele kakhulu. Thembani ilizwi lam nihambe emanyathelweni am, kuba indlela endinibekele yona ikhokelela kubutsha bobomi kanye phambi kobuso bukaThixo. Kuba ndim ndedwa indlela eya kuThixo; ndim indlela, inyaniso, nobomi. Nangona ongendawo ethembisa izinto ezininzi, akananto anokuyinikezela, kwaye akanakuzihlangula esandleni sam. Hayi, ingqumbo enkulu ibekelwe yena, kwaye uSathana unelifa elizayo alikhethileyo kwelo chibi livuthayo. Bonke abamkhethayo, bonke abathatha uphawu lwakhe, bonke abanqula irhamncwa nomfanekiso walo baya kumlandela kwilifa lakhe lengqumbo engapheliyo kaThixo.

Ubawo akalunganga na ekukhuseleni indalo yakhe? Akalunganga na kwimigwebo yakhe neendlela zakhe azibeke phambi kwenu? Ngoko ke, khethani ubomi nikhethe ilifa endininike lona, kuba ndithembisa izulu elitsha nomhlaba omtsha apho kumi ubulungisa. Ndinithembisa ubomi obungunaphakade, ubomi obunentabalala enkulu, uxolo, nolonwabo. Noko ke, ekusweleni kwenu ukholo, nisachasa. Ndenzeni kuni ndanikhubekisa kangangokuba nala ubudlelwane nam, unyana kaThixo, noBawo wozuko? Ukukhetha eli hlabathi neendlela zalo kukukhetha ukufa, kwaye ekugqibeleni ukhetho lomntu wonke luya kuba lolokugqibela, njengoko amaxesha okwahlukana elapha; kwaye abo bangathathi sigqibo, isigqibo siya kwenzelwa bona.

Akukho luyolo kwintshabalalo ezayo eza kuligubungela kungekudala ihlabathi lenu. Iintlekele zemvelo ziya kwanda, nomhlaba uya kunyikima, namaza amakhulu aya kuntlitheka kwiBhabhiloni eyimfihlakalo; kuba umhlaba uya kukrazulwa kwiindawo ezininzi. Umlilo omkhulu uya kuligubungela ilizwe, nabarhwebi baya kuma kude ngenxa yokoyika intuthumbo yayo. Intshabalalo enkulu iphezu kwehenyukazi, iBhabhiloni enkulu, eyenze ihlabathi lanxila ngokuhenyuza nokrexezo lwayo, kuba ibineentanda ezininzi. Abo bangazahluliyo kuyo baya kwabelana ngomgwebo wayo, ngoko kutheni nisaza kukhetha ukufa kunobomi endininike bona, obuhlawulelwe ngegazi lam elingenasono? Imfazwe sele iza kufika ngokupheleleyo elizweni nasesizweni senu, kwaye nangona sele iqhubeka — ewe, imfazwe engabhengezwayo abayilwayo nani — kungekudala iya kuqhambuka kwihlabathi lonke njengoko amatywina ekhululwa.

Imfazwe enkulu nentshutshiso enkulu iya kufika emhlabeni njengoko beya kuzama ukutshabalalisa nantoni na kaThixo noKrestu wakhe njengoko ekugqibeleni beya kubhengeza imfazwe engcwele kuni. Baya kubamba abaninzi, kwaye abaninzi baya kuthinjwa, kwaye abo baya kusindiswa baya kuba nokhetho olunye: ukunyamezela kude kube sekupheleni bangabathandi ubomi babo kude kube sekupheleni baze babize egameni lam kude kube sekupheleni. Iinkokeli zenu ziningcatshile kwaye ziya kubingelela abaninzi kongendawo kumadabi aza kuqhambuka kungekudala. Isizwe senu siya kuhlaselwa ngaphakathi nangaphandle. Imikhosi ebomvu sele ilapha, kwaye ilindele imiyalelo yayo; kuba iceba uhlaselo olothusayo, kwaye abaninzi baya kuthinjwa. Imikhosi yongendawo iya kuthatha yonke into elizweni ibe yeyayo nakwabo bandalayo. Baya kuba umhlaba wenu, amakhaya enu, inzala yenu; kwaye ubomi obuninzi buya kulahleka ekungakholweni. Imfazwe iza ngaphandle njengoko izizwe ezininzi zimanyene ngokuchasene nani; kwaye zinemvume yeenkokeli zenu, kwaye iinkokeli zenu zisebenzela ukufa kwenu. Bafuna ihlabathi labo, ihlabathi elinikezelwe kuSathana apho banokulandela ngokupheleleyo ububi obuhleli kubo, kwaye akukho nto iya kuthintela iminqweno yabo emibi, njengoko umthinteli uya kuba engekho.

Baya kuyitshisa ngokupheleleyo iBhabhiloni eyimfihlo ngomlilo, kwaye nabani na ongoyiswayo ngamadangatye uya koyiswa yindlala; nabani na ongoyiswayo yindlala uya koyiswa sisibetho; nabani na ongoyiswayo lucalo lwekrele uya kunikelwa emnqamlezweni ngenxa yegama lam, kwaye abaninzi baya kunqunyulwa iintloko njengoko iLizwi lam eliNgcwele libhengeza. Kwaye abo bafela igama lam baya kuba nomvuzo omkhulu, kwaye ndiya kosula zonke iinyembezi emehlweni abo.

Yazini, bantwana bam, ukuba zonke izinto ezimnandi nezolonwabo lwesono abantu abangendawo bazibekela zona ziya kuthi shwaka emoyeni, kwaye imbandezelo nosizi ziya kuma ezindaweni zazo. Ubumnyama buya kuligubungela ilizwe, kwaye kuya kubakho ukunqaba kweLizwi lam, nelizwi lomyeni nelizwi lomtshakazi alisayi kuphinda livakale kuyo.

Nangona kunjalo, ndiya kuthumela abaprofeti bam phakathi kwenu, njengoko ndiya kutywina inani elithile kwizizwe eziseleyo ukuze zikhonze abo, kwaye ziya kwenza imimangaliso emikhulu. Imiqondiso emininzi nemimangaliso iya kunikwa ngezi ntsuku zobumnyama, kwaye abo babiza egameni leNkosi kude kube sekupheleni baya kusindiswa. Iinqaba zenu ziya kuwa. Iibhulorho zenu ziya kudilika. Indlala iya kuqhambuka elizweni, kwaye kuya kubakho ukuchithwa komthetho ngokupheleleyo, kwaye baya kuzingela abo bandimelayo, kwaye abaninzi baya kufela ukholo; kodwa umvuzo omkhulu ulungiselelwe abo bama kude kube sekupheleni. Kutheni ninqwenela usuku lweNkosi? Kuba luya kutshabalalisa yonke inyama emhlabeni ukuba bendingayinqumlanga imihla. Emva koko ihlabathi liya kundibona ndigqabhuka emazulwini nemikhosi yasezulwini, kuba umhlaba yoweNkosi nenzaliseko yawo.

Ewe, ndinixelela ezi zinto zingekenzeki, kungekhona ukuze nime apho ningakholwa, kodwa ukuze nibe nokholo olungangokhozo lwemostade, kuba luya kukhula lube lukhulu kuneminye imithi kwaye luya kuba olungagungqiyo kum ukuba ningandivumela ndihlambulule izono zenu kwaye ukuba ningeza kum ukuze niphiliswe nihlaziywe. Isandi sexilongo sisondele kakhulu kwaye siphantse sibe phezu kwenu, kwaye ndiya kumxhwila umtshakazi wam phakathi. Ndisaza kubeka indawo etafileni yam kuye nabani na oya kundenza iNkosi ngokuphendula iingcango zabo; kuba, yabonani, ndimi ndankqonkqoza!

NdinguNyana kaDavide kwaye ndiya kulawula umhlaba kwitrone yakhe.

Ndim ucango lwezimvu, kwaye akukho zingcuka zihahayo zinokungena.

Ndim isonka sobomi; nabani na osidlayo akasayi kuze alambe.

Ndim imvana kaThixo efanelekileyo ukuvula amatywina abopha umsongo.
