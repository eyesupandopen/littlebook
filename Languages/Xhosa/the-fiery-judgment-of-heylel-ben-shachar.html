Umgwebo Ovuthayo kaHeylel Ben Shachar

Yivani, Yivani aMazwi ka-Adonai-YHVH nisondele kufutshane kwisandi selizwi lam, ukuze nihlale ezinyaweni zam, bantwana bam, nive aMazwi am endiwakhululayo kumoya womthunywa wam onamandla nomkhonzi wam. Amazwi anikwe ngegunya elikhulu kuNyana wam okuphela kwamzelweyo, owatywina la Mazwi anamandla kumthunywa nomkhonzi wethu endimnike uKristu Yesu njengesitya esikhethiweyo, ukuba ahlale ngasekhohlo lakhe ngembeko ephindwe kabini njengomalusi onothando nokhuselayo, ozaliswe yimfesane kuba ukhuliswe nguMalusi oyiNtloko onguYeshua HaMashiach, waza wazaliswa luthando lwam ngenceba engapheliyo nobabalo olunikezelweyo kwiMvana ka-Adonai-YHVH, eluthandweni lwakhe awambumba waza wamkhanda ngalo uBiblaridon:144 ngokwakhe, kanye njengoko ekugqibe ngokupheleleyo kubo bonke abali-144,000 bakhe abanamandla, abangabantwana bakaYeshua HaMashiach awabakhulisa ngesandla sakhe, kwaye akukho mkhethe mntu ezindleleni zika-Adonai-YHVH, kwaye uYeshua HaMashiach uliLizwi lika-Adonai-YHVH elenziwe inyama. Ngoko ke, qondani nonke nina bali-144,000: Ningabantwana bakaBawo uYeshua, kwaye MNA ndingu-Adonai-YHVH wenu onguBawo kuni nonke ngoKristu Yesu. Selah.

Ngoko ke, qondani ubungqonyela bam obungunaphakade nolawulo lwam lokuba NDINGUYE okwaziyo ukuvelisa amagorha anamandla ukuze uYeshua HaMashiach awayalele, yena ngoku omi ngegunya elipheleleyo elivakalayo, exhotyiswe ngokupheleleyo waza wathunyelwa ukuba ahlangule abathembekileyo kuKristu Yesu njengeNgonyama yesizwe sakwaYuda ephakathi kwabo, ebangela intshabalalo yesiquphe njengoko ekhulula iyure enye nerhamncwa ekumkeni kwenu (ngesiquphe) ngelo xesha linikezelwe ngokupheleleyo ezandleni zakhe ukuba alizalisekise ngentobelo yonke elungileyo nenemincili, kwaye nguYeshua HaMashiach osoloko esenza ukuthanda kuka-Adonai-YHVH wakhe.

Ngokunjalo, uBiblaridion:144 usesisixhobo esinamandla nesineenkalo ezininzi esandleni sikaYeshua HaMashiach, ohambelana ngokupheleleyo neenjongo zakhe njengoko zityhiliweyo kude kube ngoku nangonaphakade, okwangumthunywa womgwebo wokuvakalisa nowokwenza, oza kusetyenziswa ngomyalelo ukuba abethe, kwaye okwathunyiweyo ukuba avakalise aze enze kanye njengoko kuvakalisiwe phantsi kwegunya elipheleleyo leNgonyama yesizwe sakwaYuda. Inene, uBiblaridion ulikrele elisesingxobeni eliphethwe esandleni nguYeshua HaMashiach, eliza kurholwa ngokupheleleyo ebudeni beveki yamashumi asixhenxe kaDaniyeli, kwanangenjongo zam ezingunaphakade ezisaza kutyhilwa xa nonke nihlanganiselwe kum ngokuqhwanyaza kweliso, apho ndiya kunityhilela khona ubuntu benu bokwenene neenjongo zenu ezifihliweyo ngoku kuKristu Yesu, kodwa ngoku zityhilwe ngokuyinxenye.

Qondani bantwana bam ukuba bonke abathembekileyo kuKristu Yesu abalibandla lakhe, umzimba wakhe, kwanomtshakazi wakhe, bakhethwe ngokukodwa kuKristu Yesu phambi kokusekwa kwehlabathi. Nangona abaninzi bewatolika la Mazwi ngamabinzana angenangqondo anjengokuthi "bathandwa kakhulu kwaye basikelelwe ngokwenene" ukuze bathuthuzele inyama xa kuthethwa ngokungakholwa nangaphandle kokuqonda iinyaniso zam ezingunaphakade, ayisiyiyo leyo injongo epheleleyo kodwa ngokuqinisekileyo iyinyaniso kwabathembekileyo kuKristu ngenxa yentobelo yabo nenguquko yabo.

Qonda, ibinzana elithi "abakhethwe kuye phambi kokusekwa kwehlabathi" libonisa ukuba u-Adonai-YHVH wenu unikhethe ngokwakhe waza wakhokela ngamandla apheleleyo okuthanda nangoyilo olunenjongo egqibeleleyo, ngaphandle kokuze aphule inkululeko yokuzikhethela yakhe nawuphi na umntu nakancinane, ngokubumba, ngokwenza, nangokudala ngokutsha kuKristu Yesu ngamnye wenu ngokuqonda okupheleleyo nangegunya elipheleleyo lobugcisa noyilo, ngenkathalo emangalisayo kwiinkcukacha zomntu ngamnye kuni, nto leyo ethathe yonke iminyaka ukususela kuAdam noEva kude kube ngoku ukuyifeza ngokuchaneka kwam.

Ningumsebenzi wam wobugcisa obalaseleyo owadalwa ngoKristu!

Obo bugcisa, ukuchaneka noyilo zityalwe kwikhowudi yenu yofuzo apho ndikhethe khona ngobuchule obukhulu nobugcisa zonke iinkcukacha zenu nonke ngeenjongo zam ezingunaphakade eziza kutyhilwa ngokupheleleyo kwigumbi lam letroni, ngoko nangoko nangamandla ngomyalelo wam. Ngoko ke, qondani ukuba ndimbumbile, ndamenza, ndaza ndamdala ngamnye wenu ngokukodwa nangeyona nkcukacha incinane kangangokuba aninakuyiqonda into eniza kuguqulwa nibe yiyo nonke ngokweenjongo zam ezikhethekileyo nezahlukileyo zomntu ngamnye kuni.

Ngoko ke, musani ukoyika, u-Adonai-YHVH wenu akamenzi omnye umntu wahluke komnye ngokukhetha ukuze inzuzo ebonakalayo ibhiyozelwe ngesono sokuzidla, kwaye akenzi enye indalo ilawule enye ngogonyamelo olunesono nolunentiyo lobudenge, kodwa ukuze iinjongo zam ezingunaphakade zizaliseke kuni nonke. Inyaniso engunaphakade yile: Akukangeni ngokupheleleyo entliziyweni yomntu ngexesha lokubhalwa koku ukuba niya kudalwa ngokutsha nibe yintoni kwaye ngeziphi iinjongo. Ngoko ke, ndiyanicela eluthandweni lwam ngani (nina badalwa bam nabakhethiweyo ngenxa yokholo lwenu, bantwana bam abaguqukayo ngokuthembekileyo) ukuba nibe nokholo olungangokhozo lwemostade nje, niqonde ukuba ukususela koyena mncinane ukuya koyena mkhulu phakathi koSirayeli ka-Adonai-YHVH, nonke nenziwe ngokugqibeleleyo nangokumangalisayo, noyiliwe, naza nakhandwa ngamandla amakhulu kangangokuba nguSirayeli ka-Adonai-YHVH oza kunconywa ngabo bonke.

Uxolo malube kuni ngokupheleleyo.

Kwaye oku kungenamkhethe mntu, kodwa endaweni yoko ngolwazi lwam olulungileyo nolungunaphakade olunamandla onke nolawulo oluchanekileyo lwazo zonke izinto, apho ndakhetha khona ngamnye wenu ngolo hlobo ngeenjongo zam ezingunaphakade ngoKristu Yesu. Kuba ndandisazi ngokupheleleyo kwiminyaka engunaphakade eyadlulayo ukuba ngamnye wenu, ngabanye, uya kuza ngokupheleleyo kuKristu Yesu ngamaxesha enu, ngoko ke ndalungiselela. Ngoko ke, qondani ukuba ngoxa iimfihlelo ezifihliweyo zityhilwa ngobungqina nomzekelo wenkonzo kuBiblaridion:144, ezi zinto zenziwa ukuze nonke niqale ukuqonda ubukhulu belifa lenu neenjongo zenu ezingunaphakade endinidale, ndanikhetha, ndaza ndanenza ngazo nonke ngoyilo olukrelekrele kude kube kweyona nkcukacha incinane. Vuyani!

Ngoko ke, yizani ngokupheleleyo kuYeshua HaMashiach, kwanani nonke bathembekileyo kuKristu Yesu, ukuze nifumane umvuzo wenu opheleleyo ophindwe kabini ngembeko epheleleyo, anawo esandleni sakhe endiwunike bonke aboyisayo ngendlela efanayo awayoyisa ngayo uKristu Yesu. Yivani amazwi am okhuthazo, kwanani nonke bali-144,000, nina bantwana abamkelweyo boYihlo uYeshua, kwaye uya kwabelana nani ngeento zonke bantwana bakhe, abakwangaboyisi bakhe abathembekileyo, aboyise zonke izinto njengoko woyisayo uYeshua HaMashiach. Noko ke, asingabo bodwa aboyisi abaya kube bezoyisile zonke izinto ezichasene nabo, kuba abaninzi, abaninzi phakathi kwabathembekileyo kuKristu Yesu abangengabo abali-144,000 bazoyisile zonke izinto ngendlela efanayo noKristu Yesu, kwaye mkhulu umvuzo wabo!

Ewe! Baninzi aboyisi phakathi koSirayeli ka-Adonai-YHVH kwaye akukho mkhethe mntu ku-Adonai-YHVH, kwaye ingakumbi kuYeshua HaMashiach osoloko esenza ngokugqibeleleyo ukuthanda kuka-Adonai-YHVH waKhe. Ngoko ke, musani ukuxhalaba nikhathazeke njengoko nizama ukuqonda oko kufihliweyo emaZulwini okungekangeni ngokupheleleyo entliziyweni yomntu ngeli xesha, kwaye ingakumbi kuphephe ukuzithelekisa omnye nomnye njengoko kuqhelekile kwimeko yenu yangoku ewileyo, kodwa engcwaliswe ngokupheleleyo kuKristu Yesu.

Ngoko qondani, eBukumkanini bamaZulu, nabani na owakha waphakanyiswa ngaphezu komnye kwenziwa ngegunya, kunye/okanye ngomvuzo ngeenjongo zam kuphela, kodwa kungaze kube kukumzukisa ukuze omnye kwindalo yam azive engaphantsi, okanye ukuze abo banokuphakanyiswa baqhayise ngesono ngaphezu komnye. Kodwa endaweni yoko, imbeko nothando zinikwa nina nonke ngokupheleleyo. Kuba ndinidale nonke, kwaye ngokukodwa abathembekileyo kuKristu Yesu ngenxa yentobelo yenu kuKristu Yesu ukuze ndinidale ngokutsha ngembeko epheleleyo. Nina, ngokukodwa, ningumsebenzi wam wobugcisa obalaseleyo owadalwa ngoKristu Yesu ukuze nenze imisebenzi elungileyo enifanele nihambe kuyo ngamandla amakhulu ngeenjongo ezingunaphakade zika-Adonai-YHVH wenu, ezinikwe ngoKristu Yesu. Vuyani!

Kwaye kunjalo ukuba MNA NDINGUYE OMKHULU, kwanaye uYise womyeni, onguYeshua HaMashiach, unike iSikhalo saseZulwini zobusuku kumtshakazi neendwendwe zakhe, kwaye bazilungisile ukuze izibane zabo zizaliswe nge-oli yomoya oyingcwele kwaye imicu yenu yezibane isusiwe konke ukungcola kwaye izibane zenu zikhanyiswe ngaloo malangatye acocekileyo enziwe yiloo oli inyulu yomoya wam endiwunike ngoYeshua HaMashiach, kwaye ayidale ngokukhethekileyo ngaphakathi komntu ngamnye wenu, nina bathembekileyo kuKristu.

Kwaye ngoko, ndikhuthaza bonke abathembekileyo kuKristu Yesu ukuba baqhubeke ngentobelo epheleleyo nechanekileyo kuzo zonke izinto kuMalusi wenu oyiNtloko okhathalele yonke inkcukacha yobomi bomntu ngamnye wenu ngeyona nkcukacha incinane negqibeleleyo. Yizani ngokupheleleyo kwiNkosi yenu engunaPhakade yoBulungisa endinilungiselele yona nonke xa uYeshua waseNazarete wazinikela njengePasika egqibeleleyo yalo lonke ixesha ezandleni zaloo nkosana incinane yoyisiweyo ngentobelo yonke ekuthandeni kuka-Adonai-YHVH. Yizani ngokupheleleyo ngoku, ngaphandle kokuthandabuza kwaye niqonde ukuba MNA NDINGUYE OMKHULU ndiyanitsala ngala Mazwi alungiselelwe ukufumana amehlo, iindlebe, neentliziyo zabo bonke abo athunyelwe kubo.

Qondani amaxesha eniphila kuwo, kuba ixesha lam lobabalo lifikelele esiphelweni, liza kupheliswa ngesiquphe nangokungalindelekanga nangokupheleleyo ngomyalelo kaYeshua HaMashiach xa umyalelo unikwa nesikhalo esikhulu!

Emva koko, isandi esikhulu nesihle sexilongo, xa ngequbuliso emhlabeni ningasekho!

Xa ngequbuliso ezulwini lamazulu niya kwamkelwa kubuqaqawuli obukhulu, uloyiso, amandla, nembeko kwilifa lenu leminyaka elilungele ukwabiwa kwabathembekileyo kuKristu Yesu. Inene ixesha elilindelekileyo lokuhlangulwa kuka-Adam kwabathembekileyo kuKristu Yesu lifikile emaZulwini kungekho naliphi na elinye ixesha elakha lafana nalo, kuba aBantwana bam abangunaPhakade abachongwe ngokukodwa ngala Mazwi anikwe kuYohane wokuQala: "Bonani uthando olunjani asinike lona uBawo, lokuba sithiwe singabantwana bakaThixo: Ngenxa yesi sizathu eli hlabathi aliniyazi, kuba alimazi yena. Zintanda, ngoku singabantwana bakaThixo, kodwa, akukabonakali ukuba siya kuba yintoni na: kwaye siyazi ukuba xa eya kubonakaliswa: siya kufana naye: kuba siya kumbona njengoko anjalo. Kwaye wonke umntu onalo eli themba kuye uyazihlambulula, njengoko naye ehlambulukile", ngoku baza kuzalwa ngokutsha bephila kwaye badalwe ngokutsha ngokupheleleyo kubuqaqawuli bonke, uzuko, nembeko ngoko nangoko ngomyalelo kaYeshua HaMashiach endimnike wona ngokukodwa kwaye NGOKU niya kuma ngesiquphe nangokungalindelekanga kwigumbi lam letroni nabo bonke abathembekileyo kuKristu Yesu.

I-Harpazo yenu ifikile!

Kwaye ngoko, ndiyabayalela bonke abathembekileyo kuKristu Yesu ukuba babambelele kula Mazwi ngeyona ntobelo ingqongqo ukuze uKristu Yesu anihlambulule nonke ngokupheleleyo phantsi kwegazi lakhe lentlawulelo kanye phambi kokuba nithathwe niguqulwe ngonaphakade nangonaphakade.

Hlalani Nilungile!

Kuba niza kuzalelwa kwigumbi lam letroni, apho ndilinde ngomdla njengoBawo olindileyo endiNguye, ukuhlangulwa okupheleleyo koSirayeli ka-Adonai-YHVH oza kuhlanganiswa NGOKU.

Nale mbono engqondweni, ndiza kunceda ngamnye wenu aqonde umgudu neenkcukacha ezisetyenziswe ngobulumko obukhulu nobuchule ngu-Adonai-YHVH wenu ukunifihla nokunikhusela kuHeylel Ben Shachar obengabulala emhlabeni nawuphi na umntwana wam ukuba ebelifumene ithuba, kodwa ke anityhile ngokwaneleyo ukuze nikhandwe ngokweenjongo zam ezingunaphakade ngoKristu Yesu.

Ngoko ke, naphawulwa naza nakhuselwa, kodwa nafihlwa kwaye natyakatywa phambi kwamehlo abantu, kodwa natyhilwa ngokwaneleyo nje: Kwaye konke ngezandla zikaKristu Yesu! Kuba nguye owanithandayo waza wazinikela, waza wangcwalisa, waza wahlambulula waza waligwebela iBandla lakhe elinamandla elenziwe ngabathembekileyo kuKristu Yesu. Kuba Inene, amasango esihogo akasayi kuze aloyise iBandla lokwenene kubo bonke ubuqaqawuli balo obukhulu namandla alo. Kwanelo Bandla liza kuguqulwa litshintshwe ngoku ukuze iinjongo zabo zokwenene nezifihliweyo ngoku zityhilwe ngokupheleleyo eniya kuzazi nonke ngokupheleleyo njengoko nani naziwa.

Yibani nomonde bantwana bam, amaxesha enu NGOKU.

Noko ke, ndahlule iqela elikhethekileyo eBandleni, ndilibeke kwangaphambili ngeenjongo zam (nangona nonke ninobizo) kwaye ndalinika uKristu Yesu. Ngoko ke, yiqondeni le ntlukwano nonke nina zili-144,000 kwanabo bonke abafunda la Mazwi.

UKristu Yesu eluthandweni lwakhe kuni nonke, nothando nentobelo yakhe ku-Adonai-YHVH wakhe, wavelisela u-Adonai-YHVH abantwana abangabathembekileyo kuKristu Yesu, batywinwa ngaloo moya ungcwele wesithembiso, babekelwa bucala, banikezelwa ngonaphakade ukuze bazalise imiyalelo eyayingenamntu eZulwini ngembeko ephindwe kabini, baze balidle ilifa umhlaba ngembeko ephindwe kabini, kuba nakhetha ukuba nokholo kubungqina bukaYesu waseNazarete ngalo mhla ungendawo, kwanaleyo mvana kaThixo xa wayetyhilwa kuni ngu-Adonai-YHVH wenu, nathi xa nanikwa ithuba lokuba nokholo olungangokhozo lwemostade, nakholwa! Kwaye loo mbewu inamandla engunaphakade ikhule ngamandla ngoku kuni nonke! Kodwa ityhilwe ngokupheleleyo kwigumbi lam letroni ngesiquphe nangokungalindelekanga ngoku.

Hlalani NILUNGILE!

Qondani ngokupheleleyo, ukuba abathembekileyo kuKristu Yesu ngabantwana baka-Adonai-YHVH, abaza kukhonza uKristu Yesu, okwanguye uYeshua HaMashiach, kwizabelo zenu nakwimiyalelo yenu endiyilungiselele ngokugqibeleleyo ndayenza yafanela ngamnye kuni. Umvuzo wenu wenziwe wafanela nina ngokugqibeleleyo kwaye uya kuba mkhulu komnye kunomnye, kuphela ngokokuthembeka kwenu. Noko ke, oku akusiso isithintelo ekuzukisweni kwenu ngelifa, kodwa yimbeko enikwa lowo imfaneleyo ngenxa yezinto ezininzi enizifezileyo ekukhonzeni uKristu Yesu nina nonke. Kwaye nonke nahluke kakhulu omnye komnye, kodwa niyafana kwizinto eniye nazinyamezela nazoyisa ngesandla esinamandla sikaKristu Yesu onincedileyo nonke.

Kananjalo, phakathi kwabathembekileyo kuKristu Yesu kuthatyathwe abali-144,000 ababekelwe bucala ngokukodwa njengabantwana abamkelweyo bakaYeshua HaMashiach, abakhethwe phakathi koSirayeli ka-Adonai-YHVH ngesandla sam esikhethiweyo, nangentando engunaphakade nengenazithintelo. La magorha omlilo aye abunjwa kwimbandezelo enkulu, kwaye baye banyamezela iimvavanyo ezininzi nezilingo ezininzi ezandleni zikaHeylel Ben Shachar, kwaye ngoku basongekile babunjwa ngobukhulu bamandla namandla ukuze babunjwe ngokupheleleyo ezintliziyweni zabo ezingasokuze zingcatsha iinjongo zika-Adonai-YHVH ezibonakaliswe kuYeshua HaMashiach, kwanokulondolozwa kuthando lweAgape olungagobekiyo nolunamandla onke. Selah.

Ngoko ke, qondani nina bam bali-144,000, ukuba nonke nidalwe ngobukhulu nangokugqibeleleyo kuyilo olunamandla nolunobukrelekrele obupheleleyo, kwaye ngomyalelo kaKristu Yesu, okwanguye uYeshua HaMashiach, niya kuzalisekisa iinjongo zethu ezingunaphakade ngokuchasene nale nyoka yesihogo, ukuze bonke abawileyo bagwetywe ngokupheleleyo ngembuyekezo epheleleyo nesohlwayo esinikwe ngomgwebo opheleleyo nobulungisa bomlilo kule nkosana incinci eyasilelayo neyobudenge. Kwananjalo, nonke nina zili-144,000 niziintonga zezi zenzo ezinikwe ngokujonga ngokupheleleyo isibhalo esibhalwe kanye ngolu hlobo: Impindezelo yeyam: Ndiya kubuyisela, itsho iNkosi. Ngoko ke, niziintonga ezimiselweyo zale mbuyekezo yobulungisa yembuyekezo kuzo zonke iintloko zabawileyo, kodwa ngakumbi kuloo nkosana incinci isisidenge, eyasilelayo nesoloko iqhayisa, uHeylel Ben Shachar.

Kwanaloo nkosana incinci eyasilelayo eyayibekekile ngaphezu koogxa bayo ngeenjongo ezingunaphakade zikaElohim, kwaye ekunqweneleni kwayo nasekratshini layo njengoko yayizukisa yona, icinga ukuba konke endandiyidalele kona kwakukokwayo uzuko neenjongo zokuba isebenze ngomyalelo nentando yayo, yaza yawa kuvukelo olukhulu nokungathobeli ngeyona ndlela ihlazisayo kakhulu ngehlazo elipheleleyo nekratshi elityunyuziweyo ngokupheleleyo kwaye igcinwe kweyona meko ilusizi kakhulu nekhubazekileyo angasokuze aphinde ayifumane kwaye iya kuba mandundu ngakumbi ngonaphakade.

Ngokunjalo, xa uHeylel Ben Shachar (nabo bonke abalandela le serafi) baphoswa ngenkani, nangobundlobongela, nangentlungu enkulu ngamandla am ngaphandle komzamo eZulwini lamaZulu, xa bahluthwa ngenkani lonke igunya ngenxa yezono zabo ezoyikekayo nezenziwe ngenjongo nezacwangciswa kwangaphambili ezenziwa ngayo yonke inkohlakalo nentiyo yeyelenqe. Kwananezinto ezakhula ngaphakathi ezintliziyweni zabo kuba uHeylel Ben Shachar wala ukulawula iingcinga zakhe ngokuthobela uElohim wakhe, endaweni yokugxila kubuqaqawuli namandla kaElohim wakhe, ukuze ndimphakamisele kwisikhundla sakhe esingunaphakade senkonzo, amandla, ubukhulu, nozuko, endaweni yoko wagxila kubuqaqawuli bakhe obubonakalisiweyo awazama ukububamba ngenkani nangobundlobongela njengobakhe.

Nakwiinkohliso zakhe zesono ezenzeka xa wazukisa isono ngaphezu koElohim wakhe owamdalayo ngamandla nangenjongo enamandla waza wamthwesa ngobuqaqawuli obukhulu nembeko ngomhla wadalwa ngokwezicwangciso neenjongo zam, nobuchule namandla, uHeylel Ben Shachar nabo bonke abamlandelayo baphoswa ngobundlobongela eZulwini ngaphambi kokuba baphakame bazalise izikhundla zabo ezingunaphakade zenkonzo, amandla, ubukhulu, nozuko kuba bazinikela ngezifungo zokungathobeli okunentiyo kuvukelo olungenangqondo endaweni yoko.

UHeylel Ben Shachar ke ngoko ngenjongo nangokucwangcisa okupheleleyo wahlwayela uvukelo lwakhe ngocoselelo phakathi koogxa bakhe, ngokungathi uEl Shaddai akazibonanga izenzo neengcinga neenjongo zentliziyo yakhe.
