Nawe Bhabheli eyimfihlo, kwanani: Nili lizwekazi elingcwele nelingcolileyo, kwanelizwe elamiwa mva ukusukela kwiintsuku zikaNowa, ilizwe elanikezelwa kuHeylel Ben Shachar neendlela zenyoka, kwanelizwe elinyakanyiswe egazini labamsulwa ngezithethe nezenzo zale nzala ingcolileyo yamaphimpi nabangcolisi abahamba bayokungcolisa kumaxesha amandulo. Kwaye ke abantu beza kwilizwe lenyoka eneentsiba bevela empuma benqumla ibhulorho yomhlaba yaseBering njengoko isaziwa namhlanje, kwaye beza bevela elwandle bevela entshona ngeendlela ezahlukeneyo nezininzi zorhwebo nohambo beza kula mazwe agcinwe nguHeylel Ben Shachar ngeenjongo zakhe ezimbi, ilizwe elathi ngokwenene izithunywa eziwileyo zalibanga ngokupheleleyo kwihlabathi elalikhona ngoko, kwanelizwe apho zangcwaba iimfihlo ezininzi eziya kuthi ngokukhawuleza nangokungalindelekanga zivunyelwe ukuba zizifumane, kwananjengoko umthinteli wam esusiwe kwaye njengoko ulawulo lwazo olufutshane luvunyelwe usindiso lukaYakobi, kwaye kananjalo ukuze ziwe kumgwebo ovuthayo ngokwazo njengoko lo mcimbi uvunyelwe ukuqhubeka ukuze zitshatyalaliswe ngokupheleleyo, kuba amaxesha ezithunywa eziwileyo afikile ngokwenene kwaye ndim uKrestu Yesu, kwanayo iNgonyama yeSizwe sakwaYuda eya kutyumza intloko yale nyoka yamandulo ekugqibeleni komgwebo.

Kwaye ke kwenzeka ukuba uKanan wafumana oko abalindi babekungcwabile, kwaye wakhokelwa ngokwenkanuko yentliziyo yakhe njengoko wahlasela waza waba ilizwe elalingelolakhe ukuba alime ngokwaphula umnqophiso kaNowa. Ngoko ke, qondani ukuba lo mungu ukhohlakeleyo, ongamanyala amabi kakhulu, awunangcambu nasebe ezulwini, emhlabeni, kwanaphantsi komhlaba kwaye uya kuncothulwa ngokupheleleyo, ngonaphakade kanaphakade, phantsi kwelanga ngokwesithembiso somnqophiso sika-Adonai-YHVH uMkhulu onguMNA NDIKHONA, kwaye baya kubotshwa batshiswe kuloo mlilo mkhulu wembuyekezo apho impethu yabo ingafiyo kwaye bonke abaphoswa emalangatyeni baya kwamkela izono zabo izihlandlo ezininzi kwiimbeleko zabo kumgwebo ovuthayo ongunaphakade nolungileyo abangasoze basinde kuwo.

Kwaye ke kwenzeka ukuba uHeylel Ben Shachar, kwanayo inkosana encinci eyoyisiweyo, kwanalowo mqhayisi mkhulu nomngcolisi wako konke okunyulu, wahamba wayokutyala umungu wakhe kuwo wonke umyezo kaYahweh, uMkhulu onguMNA NDIKHONA, kwanalowo myezo mnye owatyalwa xa uElohim wathi makubekho ukukhanya kwabakho ukukhanya. Kuba ekuqaleni, uElohim wenza amazulu nomhlaba, kwaye umhlaba waba yinto engenamimo nalilize njengoko ubumnyama beza phezu kobunzulu. Kwaye ke kwenzeka ukuba uHeylel Ben Shachar nesahlulo sesithathu seengelosi zakhe ezalandela imvukelo yakhe baphoswa emhlabeni ngehlazo, nangokoyiswa, kwaye nguHeylel Ben Shachar, kwanayo inkosana encinci eyoyisiweyo eyakhala ngehlazo nangokoyiswa njengoko yaphoswa ezulwini lamazulu ngomsindo omkhulu, kwanokugcinelwa umgwebo wokugqibela novuthayo emlilweni waloo mini inkulu, kwanayo imililo evutha ngawona malangatye ashushu nanyulu aluhlaza, kwaye ilungele ukwamkela bonke abazibandakanye nemvukelo kaSathana ukusukela koyena mncinci kuye koyena mkhulu phakathi kobukumkani bezithunywa eziwileyo.

Ngoko ke, yivani aMazwi kaYeshua HaMashiach, kwanaye uKrestu Yesu njengoko ndisiza kuxelela iBandla lam, uMzimba wam, kwanaye umtshakazi wam ngamazwe onakeleyo nangcolileyo apho uninzi lwenu lwazalelwa khona laza lahlala khona, njengoko ooYihlo babezulazula emhlabeni phantsi kwesohlwayo sika-Adonai-YHVH, uMkhulu onguMNA NDIKHONA, kwanaye owagxotha izizwe zasemantla phakathi kweentlanga ephethe incwadi yoqhawulo-mtshato, kwaye apho, zisasazeke phakathi kweentlanga ziye zagcinwa zalondolozwa ukusukela ngoko kwanangumfundisi wazo, kuba zabambelela kumthetho kaMoses njengoko zazihambela kumazwe asemantla xa zoyiswa ngokungeva ngezandla zama-Asiriya, kanti zasasazwa ngamaBhabhiloni, wona athi oyisa aza ohlwaya abangevayo phakathi kooBawo abavela kwizizwe zakwaYuda noBhenjamin, kwanaboBukumkani baseMazantsi bakwaYuda ngeentsuku zakudala obaba yiJudiya xa i-Idumea noYuda badityaniswa ngencula yekrele.

Noko ke, njengoko kubhaliwe kunjalo ngokwenene ukuba abantu bam abazi nto kwaye ababoni ngokuyinxenye ngeziqu zabo zokwenyani nezazisi zabo. Kwanokunqunyulwa iingcambu kuYahweh, uMkhulu onguMNA NDIKHONA, othi umnquma wemvelo uthathe inxaxheba ekutyebeni kwengcambu, yona ingcambu inguYehovah-YHVH, uMkhulu onguMNA NDIKHONA.

Noko ke abantu bam baye bajikiswa ngokungazi omnye komnye ngobuxoki nentiyo nokungazi iziqu zabo zokwenyani, kwananjengoko baye bafundiswa ukuba bakholelwe kubuxoki bale nzala yabefundisi benyoka kulo naluphi na uhlanga abazinzise kulo, kwanayo inzala yabefundisi benyoka abakhonza uHeylel Ben Shachar, kwanayo iserafi evuthayo newileyo egcinelwe imililo yesohlwayo esingunaphakade nomgwebo apho imililo igcinelwe owona mgwebo ukhohlakeleyo kule nyoka imbi eye yazingcolisa ngemvukelo engenangqondo, kwanokubanjwa kwimeko eyoyisiweyo newileyo angasoze aphinde ayifumane.

Kwaye ke abantu belizwe lam nabazalwana bam baye bafundiswa ukuba bathiyane ngeendlela ezininzi zobuxoki obupakishwe ukufundisa izihlwele, noko ke kubhaliwe kwathiwa: Ezi zinto zintandathu iNkosi iyazithiya: ewe, umphefumlo wayo uyazicekisa ezisixhenxe: amehlo aqhankqalazayo, ulwimi oluxokayo, nezandla ezichitha igazi elimsulwa, intliziyo ecinga amayelenqe amabi, iinyawo ezikhawulezayo ukubalekela ebubini, ingqina elixokayo elithetha ubuxoki, nalowo uvusa iingxabano phakathi kwabazalwana. Kwaye kunjalo ke ngemihla yakudala, umnombo kaKanan waziqhubela phambili ekonakaleni ngokuzidibanisa nabawileyo, nakwimihla yakudala, kwaye njengoko babezingcolisa esonweni bazinikela ngokupheleleyo kwabawileyo apho kumazwe aseShinare naphantsi kwesithunzi saloo nqaba inkulu yaseBhabheli, kwanaloo nqaba uYHVH awayidilizayo ekoyisweni. Kodwa zinjalo iindlela zomntu wemvelo ongakholwayo, othi ekhanukweni nasekratshini lakhe akhonze isono aze ngoko akhonze uYise wesono; kwanomtyholi, ngendlela yokungathobeli kukaYahweh koluntu ngenxa yesono esisenyameni yomntu wemvelo.

Kwaye kwinzala kaNimrodi eyakhula ebubini kuthiwa ebubini kuphuma ububi obuninzi.
