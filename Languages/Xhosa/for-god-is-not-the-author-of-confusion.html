Kuba uThixo akayiMbangeli Yesiphithiphithi!

Kuba uThixo akayiMbangeli yesiphithiphithi, kodwa uloxolo, njengakuzo zonke iicawa zabangcwele! Fundani la mazwi niphulaphule ilizwi lam. Thobani indlebe yenu, nive amazwi ezilumko, niyibhekise intliziyo yenu elwazini lwam. Mhlawumbi nimele nilandele umzekelo wam wasegadini xa ndandithandaza ndisentlungwini, ndawa phantsi emhlabeni phambi koBawo wam ndithandaza ndithobekile, waza wandomeleza ukuba ndamkele indebe yengqumbo yezono zenu kanye endazithwalela nina, kuba ndinako ukuzithwala. Ndithetha nabakhukhumeleyo eCaweni yam, kwaye ndithetha nezahlukano ezininzi phakathi kwenu. Kodwa yini na ukuba umgwebe umzalwana wakho? Kwaye yini na ukuba umdelele umzalwana wakho? Kuba sonke siya kuma phambi kwesihlalo sokugweba sikaKristu. Akubhalwanga na kwileta endayithumela kubalandeli beNdlela eRoma? Kodwa uthini na uZekariya ngalo mbandela: Kodwa bala ukuphulaphula, balishenxisa igxalaba, bazivingca iindlebe zabo, ukuze bangeva. Anifundanga na Isaya, bantwanana bam? Kuba bathini na abaprofeti bamandulo? Kuba uIsaya uphinda athi, Ngokuva niya kuva, ningaqondi; nokubona niya kubona, ningaboni.

Bantwana bam, ndinibonisile indlela egqithisileyo, kodwa uninzi lwenu luwile elubabalweni kwaye ikratshi lenu elineqhayiya linibangele ukuba niziphakamise ngaphezu komlinganiselo. Kanti, amazwi am endawanika uMpostile uPawulos kwabaseGalati athi ngokucacileyo: Kuba ukuba umntu uba uyinto, engento, uyazikhohlisa. Bantwanana bam, ndiyazi ukuba nineentliziyo ezinyamekileyo ukundikhonza, kwaye ndiyazi ukuba niyandithanda, kodwa ngamanye amaxesha kungekhona ngokwazi. Aniqondi na ukuba uThixo uyabachasa abanekratshi kodwa abanike ubabalo abathobekileyo? Ngoko ke, zithobeni phantsi kwesandla esinamandla sikaThixo, ukuze aniphakamise ngexesha elifanelekileyo. Ngokunjalo, nina bantu abatsha, zithobeni kubadala benu. Ewe, nonke nithobelane, nambathe ukuthobeka. UThixo uyabachasa abanekratshi kodwa abanike ubabalo abathobekileyo. Ningacingi ukuba amazwi am ayaphindaphindwa, kuba emlonyeni wamangqina amabini okanye amathathu makumiselwe ilizwi ngalinye. Qondani, bantwana bam, aninakundikhonza ngenyama yenu: Kuba konke okusehlabathini, inkanuko yenyama, nenkanuko yamehlo, nekratshi lobomi, asiveli kuBawo, kodwa livela ehlabathini. Cawa yam, thobelanani ngengqondo eyambethe ukuthobeka, kuba kusenokwenzeka ukuba ndim onika amazwi okuthetha nenkuthazo yokunifundisa, nangona uninzi lwenu luya kulibeka ecaleni ilizwi lothobekileyo ngenxa yodumo nomntu othile. Yibani ngabalandeli bakaThixo, bantwana bam, kuba ngenene uThixo akakhethi buso bamntu.

Yabonani! Luzuko lukaThixo ukufihla into: kodwa luwonga looKumkani ukuphengulula umcimbi. Qondani ukuba ndiya kunenza ookumkani nababingeleli phambi koThixo wethu noBawo wethu, ngoko ke qondani intetho yezilumko phakathi kwenu: uThixo akaninikanga na iindlebe ezimbini nomlomo omnye? Anifundanga na? Ngamnye kuni uneNdumiso, unemfundiso, unolwimi, unesityhilelo, unokutolika: Zonke izinto mazenziwe ngokwakha! Kodwa hayi, niyaluma nidlane ngeqhayiya lenu; ngekratshi lenu nihlasela abo bazama ukunifundisa, kwaye niye naba ngabangenakufundiseka. Niyaphikisana, nixabane, niphikisane, nilwe omnye nomnye endaweni yokuqwalasela ngokuthobekileyo oko kuthethwa ngomnye. Kwelinye icala, ninekratshi kangangokuba nicinga ukuba ngenxa yokuba amazwi ephuma emilebeni yenu ayinyaniso kuba NIWATHETHE nina? Thobelanani. Bamba ulwimi lwakho uthathe amazwi abazalwana bakho uwase esibhalweni ukuze uvavanye zonke izinto ubambelele kokulungileyo. Vula iindlebe zakho uphulaphule ngokupheleleyo uqwalasele, kuba ngokwenjenjalo ndiya kunenza izilumko ekuthobekeni kwenu, ngoko ke musani ukuba zizilumko ngokwenu. Ndicebisa wonke umntu phakathi kwenu, yivani amazwi am. Kwaye athini lawo mazwi? Kuba ndithi, ngobabalo endalunikiweyo, kuye wonke umntu ophakathi kwenu, makangazicingeli ngaphezu koko afanele ukucinga ngako, kodwa makacinge ngobungcathu, njengoko uThixo abele umntu ngamnye umlinganiselo wokholo.

Hayi, yima, qwalasela amazwi endisandul' ukukunika wona, vula iiBhayibhile zakho uze uwafunde kwakhona la mazwi. Yivani niwakholelwe, kuba kufanelekile ukuba nizicingele kakuhle, kuba ndinivusile ndawonye ndanihlalisa ndawonye ezindaweni zasezulwini kum, uKristu Yesu. Kodwa kwangaxeshanye nimele nicinge ngobungcathu, kuba uThixo unike umntu ngamnye phakathi kwenu umlinganiselo ofanayo wokholo, ngoko ke umoya onikiweyo awumkhulu okanye umncinci kubazalwana benu, kodwa ngamnye kuni unikwe ubabalo, ngokomlinganiselo wesipho sikaKristu. Kungoko athi: Wathi akunyukela phezulu, wathimba abathinjwa, wanika abantu izipho. Ndinike abanye abapostile; nabanye abaprofeti; nabanye abavangeli; nabanye abefundisi, nabafundisi: Ukwenzela ukugqibelelisa abangcwele, umsebenzi wobulungiseleli, ukwakha umzimba kaKristu. Thobelanani kwaye nililahle ikratshi lenu elingenaxabiso njengoko libangela amaqela phakathi kwenu. Kutheni ningaqondi ukuba akufanele kubekho zahlukano phakathi kwenu, bantwana bam. Umzimba kaKristu wahlulwe? Ndithini na kwabaseKorinte?

Ngoku ndiyanibongoza bazalwana ngegama leNkosi yethu uYesu Kristu, ukuba nonke nithethe into enye, kungabikho zahlukano phakathi kwenu: kodwa ukuba nimanyane ngokugqibeleleyo ngengqondo enye, nangomgwebo omnye. Aniyiyo na into enye kum, iNkosi uYesu Kristu? Kanti ninamaqela amaninzi phakathi kwenu, kwaye niyaluma nidlane ngenxa yezona ntsukuntshu zincinci. Yazini ukuba kumaqela enu nasekuhlebeni kwenu niyonwabisa uSathana, kwaye uqhayisa ngobuyatha benu bomzamo owuchithwe ekulweni phakathi kwenu kuba uninzi lwenu luswele ukuthobeka. Kwaye iindlebe zenu zivaliwe ukuze ningakwazi ukuva amazwi omnye nomnye, kwaye nivalile amehlo enu, kwaye ancanyathiselwe ngekratshi nokungakholwa, kuba uninzi lwenu lucinga ukuba lunayo yonke inyaniso. Kwaye niyawadelela amazwi abazalwana benu, kwaye niyawadelela kwala mazwi endibanike wona ngenxa yenu, kwaye ngekratshi lenu elineqhayiya niswele ukuthobeka okusisiseko kokuva nokuqwalasela. Kuba ukuba umntu akayiva into, bubuyatha nehlazo kuye. Kwaye kwakhona, kutheni nidelela abazalwana benu, kuba sonke kufuneka sime phambi kwesihlalo sokugweba sikaKristu? Ndiyakucetyiswa, Cawa yam, ukuba ulikhabe ikratshi lakho neendlela zakho zokukhukhumala uze uze kum njengomfundi weLizwi lobomi, kuba ndiliLizwi likaThixo elenziwe inyama.

Ngoko ke phulaphula ngomphefumlo wakho wonke kwaye unikele ingqalelo kwizandi zeendlebe zakho kwaye uqonde amazwi am. Ngoko ke qonda ukulandelelana kweziganeko ezinikwe eLizwini lam, kuba uninzi lwenu lukhohliswe ziingcuka eziphangayo enizilandela ngokungazi, kuba niphulaphule abelusi abalahlekileyo. Ungubani wena ukuba ushumayele ukuba akukho kuhlangana kweCawa yam kum, ukuba andiyi kudibana neCawa yam emoyeni xa ndiza ngesimeme esikhulu nelizwi lesiphatha-zithunywa nangeXilongo likaThixo? Kuba ixilongo liya kuvakala kwaye abafileyo kuKristu baya kuvuka kuqala kwaye nina baphilayo nabasalayo aniyi kubandulela, kwaye nonke niya kuthathwa emafini ukuze nidibane nam kwaye ngonaphakade niya kuba nam. Ngaba la mazwi alunge kakhulu ukuba ningawakholelwa? Okanye niya kukholelwa kumazwi abelusi abalahlekileyo abangamaYuda ngaphezu kwelizwi leNkosi yenu nomsindisi, uKristu Yesu? Kutheni ningenalo ukholo eLizwini lam okanye kumazwi kanye enza iLizwi lam? Aniqondi na ukuba yintoni imfihlelo yebhayibhile okanye injongo yayo?

Ndiza kunixelela: Imfihlelo yinto egcinwe iyimfihlo kodwa yaprofethwa ngayo etyhilwa ngexesha elifanelekileyo. Kwaye kutheni uBawo enokwenza into enjalo, uyabuza? Akuyifundanga na ingxelo yam? Kodwa sithetha ubulumko bukaThixo ngemfihlelo, nobulumko obufihliweyo uThixo awabumisela phambi kwehlabathi, ukuze kube luzuko lwethu. Ezingazange zazaziwa naziphatha-mandla zeli hlabathi: kuba ukuba babeyazi, ngebengayibetheleli emnqamlezweni iNkosi yozuko. Ungubani wena ukuba udelelise izicwangciso neendlela zikaBawo? Kuba akadingi mcebisi, nangona amashumi amawaka ngamashumi amawaka engqonge itrone yakhe.

Ngoko ke thuthuzelanani ngamazwi am, kuba nisindisiwe engqumbweni kum. Emva koko, ngeendlela zenu zokungakholwa, niphikisana ngexesha lesi siganeko. Nangona abanye benu ngokungachanekanga besithi akukho kuhlangana, uninzi lwenu luphikisana ngexesha lokwenzeka kwaso ngokunxulumene nexesha lembandezelo kaYakobi. Ndiza kuyenza icace kuni ukuba niya kuphulaphula, nangona uninzi lwenu luya kusiphika ngenkani esi siganeko, nide nindikhanyele mna kwaye niyitywine ngokupheleleyo ikamva lenu lokuthatha indawo yenu phakathi kweentombi eziziziyatha. Kuba lithini iLizwi lam?

Ngoku siyanibongoza, bazalwana, ngokuza kweNkosi yethu uYesu Kristu, nangokuhlangana kwethu kuye, Ukuba ningashukunyiswa ngequbuliso engqondweni yenu, ningakhathazwa nangomoya nangalizwi nangaleta, ngokungathi ivela kuthi, ngokungathi umhla kaKristu usondele. Makungabikho mntu unikhohlisayo nangayiphi na indlela: kuba loo mini ayiyi kufika, ngaphandle kokuba kufike kuqala ukumka, kwaye loo mntu wesono atyhilwe, unyana wentshabalalo. (Jongani kwiByzantine, bantwana bam, kuba iiBhayibhile ezininzi ezonakeleyo zivela kwimibhalo ye-gnostic, imibhalo yobuxoki enikwe ngabantu abanesono. Aniwuqondi na umahluko phakathi kombhalo webhayibhile kunye nenguqulelo yeBhayibhile?) Kuya kubakho ukuwa elukholweni kubuwexuki, kwaye abo bala ukumka elukholweni baya kumka emhlabeni ukuze badibane nam emoyeni.

Kuba nanku umzekeliso weentombi ezilishumi. Iintombi ezintlanu ezizilumko zathenga ioli zalungisa imisonto yazo kwaye zazilungele xa umyeni efika, kwaye ezintlanu zazingekalungi kwaye zityeshele ukuthenga ioli, zizama endaweni yoko ukuthatha kwiimfuno zabanye. Iintombi ezintlanu ezilungiselelweyo zaqhubeka nomyeni, kwaye ezintlanu eziziziyatha zacela ukungeniswa emva koko zaxelelwa, mkani kum, andizange ndinazi. Musani ukuwucima umoya wam kuni ongqinela la mazwi. Qondani ukuba unyana wentshabalalo akanakungena emandleni kwaye ubukumkani obupheleleyo bukaSathana abunakunyuka enzonzobileni de kususwe umthinteli, kwaye njengoko nisindisiwe engqumbweni, umthinteli uya kususwa xa ndihlanganisela uMtshakazi wam kum emafini xa edibana nam emoyeni.

Emva koko ekupheleni kweminyaka esixhenxe yexesha lembandezelo kaYakobi kuza uvuko lwamalungisa. Yabonani! Ndabona iitrone, bahlala phezu kwazo, banikwa umgwebo: ndabona imiphefumlo yabo babenqunyulwe iintloko ngenxa yobungqina bukaYesu, nangenxa yelizwi likaThixo, nababengamnqulanga irhamncwa, nomfanekiso walo, bengaluwamkelanga uphawu lwalo emabunzini abo, nasezandleni zabo; baphila balawula noKristu iminyaka eliwaka. Kodwa abanye abafileyo abaphindanga baphila de yaphela iminyaka eliwaka. Olu luvuko lokuqala. Unoyolo kwaye ungcwele lowo unesabelo kuvuko lokuqala: kwabanjalo ukufa kwesibini akunamandla, kodwa baya kuba ngabaBingeleli bakaThixo, nabakaKristu, kwaye baya kulawula naye iminyaka eliwaka.

Anisakholelwa na kumazwi am? Inene, uninzi lwenu luya kubambelela ngenkani kwimpazamo yenu nakumazwi abelusi abalahlekileyo, kwaye uninzi lwenu ebeninokusinda kwingqumbo kaThixo eza kuvavanya umhlaba ngexesha lembandezelo kaYakobi, nikhetha endaweni yoko ukunyamezela ingqumbo enkulu xa sendinivavanyile ndanilinga, ngaphandle kokuba niya kusala ngenkani ukubonakala kwam, kwaye niya kubambelela kwingqumbo engeyiyo eyenu. Andikuxelelanga na ukuba uphume kuyo hleze wabelane ngezibetho zayo?

Kanti, kuya kubakho olunye uvuko. Ndabona itrone enkulu emhlophe, nalowo uhleli phezu kwayo, owathi ubuso bakhe babaleka umhlaba nezulu, akwafunyanwa ndawo yazo. Ndabona abafileyo, abancinci nabakhulu, bemi phambi koThixo: zavulwa iincwadi: yavulwa nenye incwadi, eyiNcwadi yoBomi: bagwetywa abafileyo ngezo zinto zibhaliweyo ezincwadini, ngokwemisebenzi yabo. Ulwandle lwabakhupha abafileyo ababekulo: ukufa nesihogo zakhupha abafileyo ababekuzo: bagwetywa umntu ngamnye ngokwemisebenzi yakhe, kuba olu luvuko lwabangemalungisa bantu bam.

Kutheni ningaqondi ukuba ukuhlangana kweCawa yam ukuze idibane nam emafini ekubonakaleni kwam kwahlukile ekubuyeni kwam noMtshakazi wam nemikhosi yasezulwini? Kutheni ningaqondi ukuba ukuhlangana kweCawa yam kuyimfihlelo etyhilwe nguMpostile uPawulos kumxholo weMfihlelo eNkulu kaThixo. Ngoko ke yimfihlelo ngaphakathi kwemfihlelo, kwaye ayizange ityhilwe ngokupheleleyo koonyana babantu de yatyhilwa kuPawulos owayibhala phantsi, kwaye ndiyigcinele nina.

Kanti, kukho imizekelo eliqela kaThixo ethatha abantu abayise emazulwini ngeenjongo ezithile. UEnoki wemka. UEliya wemka. Ndemka ndaya emazulwini ngoxa abapostile bam babebukele bemangalisiwe. UFilipu wasuswa kwenye indawo wayiswa kwenye njengoko uBawo wamthabathela iinjongo zam. Kwaye uya kuhlanganyelwa kum uMtshakazi wam emafini ukuze asiwe emazulwini ukuze atshate nam kwaye akhonze iinjongo zam zokuhlangula.

Aniqondi na ukuba uMtshakazi wam uya kuba ngookumkani nababingeleli phambi koThixo wam noBawo wam kwaye uya kulawula nam ebukumkanini bam obuzayo kwaye uya kuba nam ngonaphakade? Kutheni nicotha kangaka ukukholelwa kumazwi am? Ngaba nithakathiwe nalahlekiswa ngabelusi abalahlekileyo? Sekunjalo, uninzi lwenu luya kuba sisiprofeto esizizalisekisayo kwaye niya kuzitywinela ekuweni ngokuthi niwexuke kwimiyalelo yam, ngakumbi imfundiso yokuba iCawa yam ihlanganiselwe kum. Ihlazo kuni nina eniqhelanisa le mfundiso nabaprofeti bobuxoki kwimbali yakutshanje, nibhengeza ukuxhwilwa okuyimfihlo okubotshelelwe kwezinye iimfundiso zobuxoki zeedispensations.

Qondani ukuba kukho amaxesha kumgca wexesha likaThixo hayi iidispensations, kwaye azikho ingqiqo enye. Fundisisani ukuze nizibonakalise nivunyiwe, kwaye niyeke ukuba ngamavila engqondo. Yekani ukuxhomekeka nokufunda ukutolika komnye umntu koko kubhaliweyo, kwaye niphile ngokubhaliweyo: Kuba anidingi mntu ukuba anifundise. Kanti, uninzi lwenu luya kugculela lusale isiluleko sam. Ngaphandle kokuba le nto ayiyi kwenziwa emfihlethweni kwaye uSathana uya kuphehlelela inkohliso enkulu ukugubungela esi siganeko kuba siya kwaziwa kwihlabathi liphela. Kuya kubakho ukushukuma okukhulu, kwaye ihlabathi lonke liya kutshintsha kwihlabathi ebenilazi, kwaye loo mntu wesono uya kutyhilwa aze aphakame eluthuthwini olushiywe ngamahashe amane. Lixesha lokuba nizilungiselele, Mtshakazi wam, kwaye nenjenjalo ngokukhawuleza, kuba yabonani ukubonakala kwam kusondele!

Ndingunyana woMntu owaprofethwa nguEnoki, owathathwa nguThixo waya emazulwini.

Ndim lowo wenziwa kumila kumbi ndathetha noMoses noEliya malunga nokumka kwam, kwaye uThixo wamthatha uEliya wamsa emazulwini ngexesha lakhe.

Ndim lowo wathathwa waya efini njengoko ndamkelwa emazulwini de kwafika amaxesha okubuyiselwa.

Ndim lowo wathumela uFilipu kumthenwa wase-Etiyopiya phambi kokuba uThixo amse kwenye indawo emhlabeni.

Ndim lowo wamxhwila uPawulos wamsa kwizulu lesithathu wambonisa izinto ezingenakuthethwa awayengakhululekanga ukuba abelane ngazo.

Ndim lowo uya kuhla emazulwini ngesimeme esikhulu ukuze ndihlanganisele uMtshakazi wam ukuze adibane nam emoyeni.

Ndim lowo uya kuvusa amangqina am amabini ebomini emva kokuba efile iintsuku ezintathu enesiqingatha, kwaye ndiya kuwasa emazulwini.

Ndim lowo onguAlfa no-Omega, kuba ndiyingqalo nesiphelo, kwaye yabonani! Ndiza kungekudala kunokuba nicinga, ngoko ke lungelani ukudibana nam okanye niwe.

Nabani na obiza egameni leNkosi kude kube sekupheleni uya kusindiswa.
