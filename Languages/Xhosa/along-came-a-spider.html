Ingqina eliMbaxa: uSirayeli
Okukrakra Okumnandi
Novemba 25, 2024

Kwafika Isigcawu

Yivani, yivani, Mazwi am O' Sirayeli nithobe iintliziyo zenu ngokuthobela isandi selizwi lam kwaye namkele ngobulali amaZwi kaYeshua HaMashiach ukuze ngamaZwi eNdaba Ezilungileyo ezingunaPhakade nizuze usindiso nifike kubomi obungunaphakade kuMesiya wenu wokwenene onyeneleyo onilwelayo ngothando. Ngoko ke ndiza kunilumkisa, kwanabo bakwaYakobi nabo bonke abafunda bamkele amazwi eNdaba Ezilungileyo ezingunaPhakade ngokholo olusindisayo ukuze babe nokholo kuYeshua HaMashiach okwanguYesu Krestu, kwaye babe nokholo kuYahweh uMkhulu Ndinguye onentliziyo yakhe iNdaba Ezilungileyo ezingunaPhakade zahlala zifihliwe de kwangamaxesha okutyhilwa kwazo kwaye ngoko uYohane uMtyhili wawatywina amazwi eendudumo ezisixhenxe awawavayo amazwi azo. Kwaye ke ndiza kunilumkisa ngesigcawu esiluke ubulembu baso kwaye sibambe ihlabathi ekusingatheni kwaso; kanye njengokuba sisitya igazi labanye, ukuze nina niphephe ubulembu baso obuninzi ukuze ningabanjwa ekusingatheni kwaso okubulalayo.

Ngoko ke, thobani iintliziyo zenu ngokuthobela kwaye nive amazwi oMesiya wenu wokwenene, uYeshua HaMashiach! Kuba ngenene ndiza kwenza umntu olumkileyo phakathi kwenu abe nobulumko obukhulu ngakumbi ukuze niqonde amaxesha eniphila kuwo kwaye niqonde iintshaba zenu nendalo yazo neendlela zazo zobuxoki; kuba zihamba ngemfihlo enkulu nangokunyenya kwaye ngobuqhetseba ziyalwa. Kuba bonke bangabazenzisi abazenzisayo, kwaye isigcawu sihamba ngemfihlo enkulu nangokunyenya de ixhoba lesigcawu libanjwe ebulembini baso apho isigcawu sihlala sisisebenzisela iinjongo zaso ezikhohlakeleyo.

Ngoko ke, ndiza kunilumkisa ngesigcawu kwaye ndinibonise iindlela zaso ukuze niphephe ityhefu yaso kwaye nizigcine ekusingatheni kwaso okukhubazayo nokubulalayo. Kodwa sesiphi isigcawu esakha sazenzela ubukhosi nesizwe? Kwaye sesiphi isigcawu esakha sajoyina umanyano lwabawileyo, sisenza ngenkuthalo isabelo saso kububi obunemvukelo? Kwaye sesiphi isigcawu esakha sathatha izazisi zabo babanjwe ebulembini baso? Ukanti sinjalo isigcawu endiza kunilumkisa ngaso kwaye ngenene, ngenene, isigcawu sesinye sezinto ezininzi zobukumkani beentshaba kwaye sisisigcawu esinetyhefu nesinobuhlungu ngenene.

Kuba isigcawu siluka ubulembu baso ngokunyenya nangobuqhetseba. Kwanobulembu bobuqhetseba nobulembu betyala silukwa sisigcawu, kwaye ubulembu baso ngoku bugubungela umhlaba wonke ekusingatheni kwaso okunyenyezelayo. Cinga ngesigcawu kwindawo yaso yendalo, siluka ubulembu baso ngokunyenya kwiindawo apho sinokubamba khona ixhoba laso kanye njengokuba ixhoba laso lihlala lingazi ebulembini baso. Kwaye njengokuba ixhoba laso lisilwa ukuze lizikhulule ebulembini elabanjwa kulo lingazi lizibophelela ngakumbi, isigcawu siyatsiba sijove ityhefu yaso njengokuba sigubungela ixhoba laso ebulembini baso. Emva koko isigcawu sigcina ixhoba laso ngeenjongo zaso kwaye sitya kancinci kwixhoba laso de kungabikho nto iseleyo, sishiya ixhoba laso liyinkangala ngokupheleleyo de ngenene ixhoba laso lingabikho.

Ukanti zinjalo iindlela zesigcawu sendalo esadalelwa iinjongo zikaYahweh, ngaphandle kokuba nguwuphi kuni owakha wabona isigcawu sisebenza kunye nobukhosi nesizwe sezinye izigcawu; kodwa abawileyo bazimanyanisile izigcawu zabo kula maxesha okuphela. Kwaye ke ndiza kunilumkisa ngabantu abaphuma kuYafete, owazala uGomere, kwaye kuGomere kwaphuma uAshkenazi, kwaye kuAshkenazi isizwe kuba uAshkenazi ukwitafile yezizwe kwimiyalelo yakudala kwanakwimihla apho abantu babephila phantsi komnqophiso kaNowa apho uluntu lwawulahlayo loo mnqophiso; kanti kwakukho uAbram othembekileyo owayefuna iindlela zikaYahweh xa umhlaba waphinda wavukela umdali wawo. Ukanti nangona, umntu wemvelo wawulahla umnqophiso kaYahweh; uYahweh uthembekile kwizithembiso zakhe ngokufanayo. Kwaye akukho nasinye isithembiso somnqophiso uYahweh awawenza noNowa (owayeyindoda elilungisa emehlweni kaYahweh) esakha sawela emhlabeni singazalisekiswanga kwaye zonke ziya kuba ngu-ewe no-amen ngonaphakade kuba uYahweh uhlala ethembekile ngokugqibeleleyo!

Emva kokuba umhlaba wakhukuliswe ngemihla kaNowa, umhlaba wahlulwa ngamaqashiso elifa phakathi koonyana bakaNowa ngemihla kaPelegi, kwaye ke uYafete waya kwilifa lakhe kumazwe asentla. Kwaye abantu babelwimi lunye, kwaye oonyana bakaHam bahlangana kwilizwe elaba yiBhabheli. Kwaye iinkosana zikaNimrodi zacebisana kunye noFuti, uMitsrayim, uKushi, noKanan neentsapho zabo ukwakha isixeko sabo kwaye kuso inqaba eyomeleleyo kwanenqaba ephakamileyo ukuze bakwazi ukulwa namazulu kwaye bazihlanganise ukuze bazenzele igama ngokuvukela umyalelo kaYahweh wokuba baqhame bande ukuze baphinde bazalise umhlaba. Kodwa zinjalo iindlela zomntu wemvelo ongakholwayo, othi kwiinkanuko zakhe nekratshi akhonze isono kwaye ngoko akhonze uYise wesono; kwanomtyholi, ngendlela yokungathobeli kukaAdam kuYahweh ngenxa yesono esenyameni yomntu wemvelo.

Kwaye kwinzala kaNimrodi eyakhula ebubini kuthiwa kongendawo kuphuma ububi obuninzi. Kwaye ke uNimrodi wawungcolisa umhlaba ngeendlela zabawileyo kwaye kubagqirha bakhe nabathakathi kwaphuma izikolo zemfihlakalo zakudala apho abagqirha babawileyo bafundisa iimfihlakalo zabo ezingento yanto kwabo baya kuphoswa emalangatyeni ngonaphakade kwizono zikaSathana, kwaye baya kujoyina nabo kwilifa labo elingunaphakade. Kwaye uNimrodi waphakama kubukhosi bokuqala emva kukazamcolo kaNowa, kwanapho uNimrodi walawula umhlaba ngokungalunganga; kwanolawulo abawileyo abazifunela lona ngokupheleleyo phezu komhlaba, kodwa abayi kuba nalo ixesha legolide kodwa umgwebo onzima.

Ngoko uYahweh emgwebeni wakhe wenza ukuba umhlaba uvuleke kwaye uginye isithathu senqaba yabo, kwaye umlilo wawa uvela ezulwini watshisa esinye isithathu senqaba, kwaye isahlulo sesithathu savunyelwa ukuba sihlale njengesikhumbuzo nxamnye nabo. Ngoko ke, igama laso kuthiwa yiBhabheli, kuba uYahweh apho wadidanisa ulwimi lomhlaba wonke: kwaye ukusuka apho uYahweh wabasasaza phezu kobuso bomhlaba wonke. Kwaye izizwe ezininzi nabantu bamala kwaye bamlibala uYahweh owangena kumnqophiso noNowa, kwaye ke bawulibala umnqophiso uNowa awawenza noYahweh ngokwelifa labo nangokweelwimi ababezithetha bawulibala umnqophiso kaYahweh noNowa, kwaye bajikela kwiindlela zabawileyo kuba akukho lunye ukhetho. (Kanti uAbram waba nguAbraham kwaye olilungisa uya kuphila ngokholo!)

EBhabheli uNimrodi wenza ububi obuninzi kwananjengokuba wenza izithixo ezininzi wazibiza ngothixo waza wenza uziqu-zithathu wakhe onyelisayo, kwanabathathu abangcwele, ababejolise ekugxekeni ubuthixo bokwenene nembewu ethenjisiweyo yomfazi nokulahlekisa oonyana babantu ekungalungini. Kwaye ke uNimrodi wamisa izikolo zakhe ezibizwa ngokuba ziimfihlakalo njengoko ulwazi lwabawileyo lwaphinda lwaqala ukusasazeka lugubungele umhlaba kwaye uluntu lwaphinda lwayiguqula inyaniso kaYahweh yaba bubuxoki kwaye lwanqula lwakhonza indalo ngaphezu komdali obongwayo ngonaphakade, Amen!

Kwaye njengokuba abantu babesasazekile besuka eBhabheli ngokunjalo nolwazi olungeyonyani neendlela zabawileyo zasasazeka kunye nabo, kwanazo zonke izithethe zabo ezicekisayo nezinyelisayo zabo batya inyama negazi nabo basela igazi lika-Adam apho ubomi benyama buhlala khona; kwanemikhwa eyonakele ngokupheleleyo necekisekayo uYahweh awayivalayo kanye, konke, nangonaphakade kuba uAdam wadalwa ngomfanekiso kaYahweh kwaye ubomi benyama busegazini. Ukanti bayaqhubeka ngeendlela zabo ezicekisayo nezinyelisayo kude kube namhlanje, kwaye imikhwa yabo nezithethe zabo zigcinwe kwabo banqula iidemon baqubude kwizithixo ezizidenge kwaye ezi ziindlela zesigcawu, kanti isigcawu sizenza uYakobi olilungisa angenasabelo nasahlulo kuye.

Kwaye ke iindlela zobubi zazinzinziswe kwiindlela zabantu bezizwe kwakhona. Kwaye kuGomere kwaphuma uAshkenazi kwaye kuAshkenazi ekuhambeni kwexesha kwaphuma isizwe esikhulu sabantu ababa ngabafuduki ababehlala kwaye bezulazula kwithafa laseYurophu naseAsiya nakwiindawo ezikufutshane nalo kwanapho benza ubukhosi babo. Kwanabo, ubukhosi babo bakhula ekungalungini bawa kwiintshaba zabo ngokufanayo. Kwanabo iintshaba zabo zabahlasela ngenxa yokungalungi kwabo kwaye endaweni yokwamkela uYesu waseNazarete kwaye bemke ebubini endaweni yoko bazenza uYakobi, kwaye kuYakobi akuzange kubekho sizwe sika-Ashkenazi kaYafete. Kwananjalo bamala uYesu waseNazarete kuba bangaboyise umtyholi kwaye iinkanuko zakhe bahlala bebotshelelwe ukuzenza ngonaphakade.

Kwaye kubantu bakwa-Ashkenazi ababe neendlela ezimbi nezikhohlisayo kwaphuma abantu, ngaphandle kohlanga luka-Adam babengekho ngokupheleleyo, kodwa ukhula olubi olutyalwe phakathi kwengqolowa kaYahweh abazonakalisile ngonaphakade ekungalungini. Kwanabo bazalwa nguyise umtyholi kuba imbewu yomtyholi isekho, nangona ndiya kubancothula ngokupheleleyo ndibaphose ezikweni kanye njengokuba uYahweh evakalisile. Kwaye kuAshkenazi kwaphuma isigcawu kodwa sasingesiso isigcawu sendalo, kodwa isizwe sezigcawu ezicekisekayo kwanokuguqulwa kumfanekiso kaYahweh umdali wazo, ababotshelelwe ngonaphakade kwiinkanuko zikaYise umtyholi abaya kuzenza iinkanuko zakhe. Kwaye ke izigcawu zasasazeka, kwananjengokuba babebaleka ezandleni zeentshaba zokungalungi ezazichasene neendlela zesigcawu. Kwaye ke uAshkenazi wamala uYeshua HaMashiach, angenasabelo nasahlulo kuye.

Ngoko ke ngenyaniso uluntu lulahlekiswe ngabawileyo ukusukela oko uAdam wawa kwimeko yakhe engenasono awayedalwe kuyo kumyezo wase-Eden kwaye ukonakala kwasasazeka emhlabeni ngomnombo wegazi likaKayin. Oko konakala abalindi abawileyo bakonakalisa ngakumbi xa behla kwiNtaba yeHermon ngemihla kaYerede. Ngoko ke, uYahweh wakuhlamba ukonakala kwabo ebusweni bomhlaba kuloo nkhukula mkhulu ngemihla kaNowa kanti abawileyo baziphinda iindlela zabo emva kukazamcolo kuba uYahweh wavumela uluntu ukuba luzikhethele. Kuba ngenene, ngenene, uYahweh unike bonke abadale ngenkululeko yokuzikhethela kwindalo yakhe inkululeko yokukhetha ukuba baya kuphila iindlela zikaYahweh okanye bakhethe ukukhonza ooSathana kwimvukelo yabo apho imvukelo sele iza kuphela. Kuba mna, kwanuYeshua HaMashiach, ndingulowo uya kutyumza intloko yenyoka, kwanalowo uya kubutshabalalisa ngokupheleleyo ubukumkani bakhe bungaphinde buvuke.

Kunjalo kwaye yinyaniso leyo endayifundisayo malunga nobukumkani bamazulu xa ndafika kwaSirayeli njengeMvana kaThixo ngexesha lam ndisenyameni eYuda. Kuba, kubhaliwe kwathiwa: Ubukumkani bamazulu bufaniswa nomntu owahlwayela imbewu elungileyo entsimini yakhe. Kodwa ngexesha abantu baleleyo, kwafika utshaba lwakhe, lwahlwayela ukhula phakathi kwengqolowa, lwahamba. Kwaye xa ikhaba lihlume, laza lavelisa isiqhamo, lwabonakala nolukhula. Baza beza abakhonzi bomninindlu, bathi kuye, Nkosi, awuhlwanyelanga imbewu elungileyo entsimini yakho? Luvele phi ke ukhula? Wathi kubo, Umntu onomona wenze oku. Baza bathi abakhonzi kuye, Ufuna ke ukuba siye siluhlanganise? Kodwa wathi, Hayi, hleze ngelixa nisaya kuhlanganisa ukhula, nincothule nengqolowa kunye nalo. Makukhule zombini kude kube sekuvuneni, kwaye ngexesha lokuvuna ndiya kuthi kubavuni, Hlanganisani kuqala ukhula, nilubophe ngezithungu ukuze nilutshise: kodwa hlanganisani ingqolowa kwisisele sam.

Kwananjalo kuba ingqolowa iyathoba ngokuthobela ngelixa ukhula lumi nkqo ngenkani embi. Selah!

Ukanti kunjalo ukuba amaxesha okuhlanganisa afikile, kwaye ndiya kuhlanganisa ingqolowa yam kwisisele sikaYahweh kuba ixesha lokuvuna iziqhamo zokuqala lifikile. Kwaye kuya kwenzeka ukuba ndiya kuluncothula ngokupheleleyo ukhula ndilubophe, kwaye luya kuphoswa emlilweni emgwebeni walo. Ngoko ke wuqonde umhlaba ophila kuwo kwanemfazwe owazalelwa kuyo. Kwanemfazwe yembewu eqhubekileyo ukusukela kumyezo wase-Eden apho uYahweh wavakalisa imfazwe enyokeni nakwabawileyo ngenxa yobubi babo nangokona umyezo kaYahweh. Apho imfazwe iphela ngokoyiswa ngokupheleleyo kwabo bonke abavukele ngolo hlobo nabawileyo kwaye ilifa labo lesulfure evuthayo lilindile.

Kwaye ke uYahweh wavakalisa: Ndiya kubeka ubutshaba phakathi kwakho nomfazi naphakathi kwembewu yakho nembewu yakhe, kwaye uya kutyumza isithende sakhe kwaye yena uya kutyumza intloko yakho. Kwaye ke isithende sam satyunyuzwa ekubethelweni kwam emnqamlezweni xa ndazinikela njengePasika egqibeleleyo njengeMvana kaThixo ngokuzithandela ezandleni zeentshaba zam ezikhohlakeleyo. Kwaye ndiyimbewu ethenjisiweyo yomfazi oya kuyityumza ngokupheleleyo intloko kaSathana kwaye ndiya kwenza iintshaba zam isihlalo seenyawo zam, kuba ndiyiNgonyama yesizwe sakwaYuda kwaye ngobulungisa ndiza kugweba ndenze imfazwe!

Ngoko ke, qonda ukuba imfazwe yembewu iphunyelelwe ngokupheleleyo kuKrestu Yesu kwananjengokuba ndenza uPetros wabhala le minyaka emininzi eyadlulayo: Nizelwe ngokutsha, kungekhona ngembewu enokonakala, kodwa ngengenakonakala, ngelizwi likaThixo eliphilayo nelihleliyo ngonaphakade. Kwaye kwakhona ndenza uYohane uMtyhili wabhala kwileta yakhe: Nabani na ozelwe nguThixo, akenzi sono: kuba imbewu yakhe ihleli kuye, kwaye akanakona, kuba uzelwe nguThixo. Koku abantwana bakaThixo bayabonakala, nabantwana bomtyholi: nabani na ongenzi bulungisa, ayingowakaThixo, kwanalowo ungamthandiyo umzalwana wakhe. Ngoko ke, bonke abatywinwe kuKrestu abanakonakaliswa ngolo hlobo kwaye abanakona apho baguqulwe khona kwitywina likaThixo ophilayo, kwanuYahweh abangabakhe. Ngoko ke, musani ukuvumela ubudenge obunjalo bunikhathaze ukuba abo bazelwe ngokutsha ngumoya wam ongcwele wobunyana kwaye batywinwe ngetywina likaYahweh banakho ukuxhwilwa esandleni sam esinamandla nangayiphi na indlela. Ngoko ke, kwabo bazuze usindiso usindiso lwenu lukhuselekile.

Ngoko ke iindlela zesigcawu ndiza kuzityhila, kwaye ndiza kusityhila ngokupheleleyo isigcawu, nangona izigcawu zithanda ukuhamba ngobuqhetseba obukhulu, ndiza kuzibhenca iindlela zazo. Kuba ezi zigcawu ziguquliweyo zezo zithatha izazisi zabo bazicebela ukubabulala. Ezi zigcawu zichuma kwinkohlakalo enkulu kwaye zithanda ukuthuthumbisa amaxhoba azo kungekho nasinye isizathu ngaphandle kokwanelisa iinkanuko zikaYise wazo ezibophelelekileyo ukuba zimkhonze kwaye ziyinzala yakhe. Ezi zigcawu ziguquliweyo endithetha ngazo zikwangabaphangi, namasela, nababulali nabakhohlisi (kwaye banetyala lezinto ezininzi kakhulu) abangenakwanela kwaye abanqwenela ukufa kuka-Adam ukuze babe nexesha labo legolide ngonaphakade kunye nabo bawayo. Ngoko ke izigcawu ziye zasasazeka kwihlabathi lonke zaza zaluka iindlu zazo kunye ngobuqhetseba obukhulu nangemfihlo.

Kwanomnatha omkhulu wetyala kuba isigcawu siyachuma kwaye sizityebisile ngegolide esiyifumene ngenzala engekho sikweni. Isigcawu sizifihlile phakathi kwaye sizifihle njengoYakobi kwaye senze isifihlo esikholisayo. Kuba isigcawu asinakuzibonakalisa ngokwenene njengesigcawu esikhohlakeleyo, esiguquliweyo; kuba nguwuphi umthiyeli obeka umgibe emehlweni entaka? Kodwa isigcawu siyazifihla kwaye sitshintshe inkangeleko yaso kunye neendlela zokuziphatha ukuze sihambelane nabo sinqwenela ukubondla. Kwaye njengoko isigcawu siqala ukuluka umnatha waso phakathi kwabo baye bavumela isigcawu indawo yokuluka phakathi kwesizwe sabo bengazi; isigcawu ke sizisa izigcawu ezininzi ezinye eziza kudibana naso kwiintshukumo zaso ezimbi okanye ngendlela yomdiza omninzi oqanduselwa kwingxowa yamaqanda esigcawu ezikhawulezayo ukuya kuluka iindlu zazo kwaye ziqhubeke nomjikelo wokufa. Ngoko ke uAdam ubotshelelwe kwaye wambathiswe zezi arachnids zikhohlakeleyo. Kwaye kancinci nangovuyo olukhulu ziyabathiyela kwaye zondle uAdam kwaye ngenkohlakalo enkulu nenkohliso isigcawu sifuna umhlaba wonke sibe sesaso kunye nooYise baso ezibotshelelwe kwiinkanuko zabo.

Kuba ngenkohliso yaso izigcawu zaseAshkenazi ziye zazimanya noEsawu, ngendlela yeTalmud kuba kwiminyaka emininzi edlulileyo bayamkela iTalmud njengenkosi nomlawuli wabo, kodwa abangobaguqukeli bokwenene bakaMoses, kwaye abayilandeli indlela kaYeshua HaMashiach kodwa bayazenzisa. Kwaye ababulali bam babengoEsawu kwaye ngobulumko bobuxoki bukaEsawu, kwanamasiko abo anyelisayo abantu abawaphakamisayo ngaphezu kweendlela zikaYahweh, bamngcolisile uYakobi kwaye bamthiyela umntakwaboEsawu amaxesha amaninzi. Kwananjalo ndathi kubo ngexesha lam ndisenyameni njengeMvana kaThixo: Nina ningaboyihlo umtyholi, kwaye niya kwenza iinkanuko zoyihlo: ebesoloko engumbulali kwasekuqaleni, kwaye akemi enyanisweni, kuba akukho nyaniso kuye. Xa ethetha ubuxoki, uthetha okwakhe: kuba ulixoki, noyise wako.

Noko ke isigcawu asizange sisuke kwiindlela zabo bawayo kwaye isigcawu sisathatha inxaxheba kwaye siyakonwabela izenzo zaso ezimbi. Kuba isigcawu sixhaphaza abantwana esibambathisileyo njengexhoba laso kwaye sisela igazi labo kwabancinci esibathuthumbisayo ekusingatheni kwaso. Kuba isigcawu sizalisekisa iinkanuko zaso ezicekisayo kwabancinci ababathimbayo kwisizwe sabo sokubamkela ngobuqhetseba obukhulu njengoko abancinci bethathwa kumnatha waso. Kwaye sesiphi isigcawu esikhe sabakho esiluka umnatha kwaye singene emhlabeni. Kuba umnatha wesigcawu ude ungene phantsi komhlaba kwaye umnatha waso ugubungela indawo enkulu kwaye isigcawu sifuna ubukhosi nobukumkani behlabathi lonke. Ngoko ke isigcawu sihamba ngenkohliso enkulu kuba ngenkohliso silwa imfazwe.

Noko ke aba bakhohlisi abaye bazimanya nabo bawayo ngonaphakade kwaye bayinzala yabo ngokwenene; bazenza uYakobi kwaye babanga ilizwe lesithembiso ngobuxoki, abangenayo inxalenye okanye iqashiso kulo kodwa bangabakhohlisi abazenzisayo. Kwaye kwiindlela zabo kuza umgibe oza kubekwa nguYakobi lirhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni kuba ngeTalmud kaEsawu uAshkenazi umanyene noEsawu, kwaye baya kumanyaniswa ngakumbi yimvana yenamba, kwanemvana yabo yobuxoki enamaphondo amabini. Kwanemvana engeyoyamvelo apho olunye uphondo lulawula ulwandle ngokukaNoahide kwaye olunye uphondo lulawula ilizwe lesithembiso ngonyeliso olukhulu nenkohliso kuba lulophondo oluncinci lukaDaniyeli, kwaye ngoko umhlaba uya kulawulwa lulawulo lwabo okwexeshana elifutshane. Kuba baya kumanyanisa yonke imikhosi namaqela obubi phantsi kolawulo lwabo, kwaye baya kubahlanganisela ndawonye emfazweni ngaloo mini inkulu, apho bonke baya kuwiswa ngogonyamelo ngonaphakade kwaye baya kungena kwisohlwayo esingunaphakade nomgwebo ngonaphakade.

Noko ke iindlela zesigcawu ziindlela zokunyanzelisa, nokuphanga, nokubulala, norhwaphilizo neendlela ezininzi ezimbi esizifundisayo nesiziphilayo isigcawu kuba isigcawu sisoloko sifuna ukwenza izigcawu ezininzi ezingabantwana besihogo ngokuphindwe kabini njengaso. Ngoko ke izigcawu ziyadanisa kwaye zizibhiyozele ngokubulala kwazo abantu belizwe, zide zidanise zibambene ngeengalo ngoxa zidanisa ngovuyo ekufeni nasekutshatyalalisweni kwamaxhoba azo amaninzi angabafazi nabantwana neentsana; ngoko ke intshabalalo yazo ayilibazisi. Noko ke zithathile iinkokeli zehlabathi zaza zabathiyela ookumkani bomhlaba ngokuvuma nangokoyisa, kwaye sele ziphantse zoyisa umhlaba wonke kwiindlu zazo ezininzi kwaye kwiindlu zazo umhlaba ubotshelelwe kwaye unikwe ezandleni zenamba ukuze ugwetywe kwaye utshatyalaliswe okuphantse kwafika ngokupheleleyo. Noko ke isigcawu sikhala kakhulu ngeentlungu njengoko sisondla ixhoba laso kwananjalo ukuze sihlale sibonakala silihlwempu elixhoba elitshutshiswayo, kanti eneneni sisigcawu esitshutshisayo nesixhaphaza uAdam.

Ngoko ke, phulaphulani ilizwi lam bantu bakuthi, kwanani nonke enifunda la mazwi kwaye nizahlule kwisigcawu nakwiindlu zaso, kuba xa nabani na ejika kwintshabalalo yakhe ukuze abize egameni leNkosi, kwanuYesu waseNazarete okwanguYeshua HaMashiach onguMesiya wokwenene kaSirayeli; inene, inene loo mntu uya kusindiswa.

Kuba isigcawu siyiNdlu yesikhungu kaSathana, esizalwe sisizwe sika-Ashkenazi kaYafete abangenayo inxalenye okanye iqashiso kwilifa likaShem, kwaye ngakumbi elo likaYakobi olilungisa endiya kusindisa kuye insali enamandla.

Ndim lowo uza kulumkisa abantu bakuthi ngale nkohliso inkulu nentshabalaliso eneneni egubungele umhlaba kumnatha wayo kuba izigcawu ziye zaluka iindlu zazo kwihlabathi lonke ngoku.

Kwaye bonke abaza kuMesiya wabo oNgunaphakade kwaye bamkele mna neTestamente yam eNtsha baya kufumana usindiso ngokwemiyalelo yabo. Abo babiza egameni leNkosi uYesu Kristu kwimbeko yokufela ukholo baya kuvuswa ekuvukeni kwabalungisayo kwaye ubunzima bomvuzo wabo ongunaphakade buya kugqitha kakhulu imizabalazo neengxaki zabo endiya kubomeleza ukuba bazoyise kanye njengoko uYahweh wandomeleza ukuba ndoyise.
