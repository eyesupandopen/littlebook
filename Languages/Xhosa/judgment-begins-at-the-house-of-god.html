Umgwebo Uqala Endlwini KaThixo!

Yivani amazwi kaYeshua HaMashiach, bantu bakuthi, ukuze niziqonde iindlela zam nokuze nize kuMesiya wenu wokwenene, kuba ndiyanibiza, O' Sirayeli, nisaza kuliva na ilizwi lam? Ngoko ke, thobani iindlebe zenu ngokuthobela, noluse iintliziyo zenu, ukuze isilumko phakathi kwenu sibe nobulumko obungakumbi ukuba siyayithobela imfundiso yam size sithozamele imiyalelo yam. Kuba ndiza kunixelela ngabo baya kwamkela uphawu lwerhamncwa, ukuze niqonde isiqalo sabo kude kuse ekupheleni kwabo, nokuze ningabanjiswa nithatyathwe lulo.

Ngoko ke, qondani amaxesha eniphila kuwo kwaye niqonde ukwahlulwa kokugqibela okuza kuqalisa kwakamsinya nje xa abathembekileyo bam kuKrestu behlanganiselwe kum emafini ngequbuliso nangokungalindelekanga ngoku. Kunjalo kwaye kuya kuba njalo ukuba ndiya kuhla ngequbuliso nangokungalindelekanga ndisuka emazulwini ndiye emafini ukuze ndihlanganise abathembekileyo kuKrestu xa ixilongo likaThixo likhala ngesidumo esikhulu ngelizwi lesiphatha-zithunywa. Baya kuthi ke abafileyo kuKrestu bavuke kuqala, baze abo baphilayo nabasalayo bahlanganiselwe emafini ukuze bahlangabeze iNkosi yabo engunaphakade esibhakabhakeni, yaye ngaloo ndlela baya kusiwa kukhuseleko lwasezulwini, kwaye baya kuhlala benayo ngonaphakade, baguqulwe ukuze bafane neNkosi yabo engunaphakade yobulungisa!

Noko ke, ukubonakala kwam akuyi kwenziwa emfihlekweni njengoko zifundisa ngempazamo iiBhayibhile zokufunda ezingeyonyani, yaye akuyi kubakho luhlelo lobuxoki oluya kuhla emhlabeni lwahlule uSirayeli kwiBandla lam lokwenene, kuba abathembekileyo kuKrestu Yesu bangamaSirayeli kaThixo yaye baya kuthatha iindawo zabo phakathi kweendidi nezizwe emazulwini kwaye bangumntu omnye omtsha. Kubhaliwe ke ngoko kwileta kaPawulos eya kwabaseGalati: Kuba kuKrestu Yesu akukho kwaluswa kunceda nto, nakungoluswa, kodwa kukho isidalwa esitsha. Nabo bonke abahamba ngalo mthetho, uxolo malube phezu kwabo, nenceba, naphezu koSirayeli kaThixo. Ngoko ke, qondani ukuba ndingulowo uya kwenza ukuba uSirayeli angaphindi abe bubukumkani obahluleleneyo ngonaphakade kanaphakade. Ngoko ke, bukhabeni ubuxoki babahleli-zihlandlo, noosiyazi abangamaZiyon, namkele inyaniso kaYeshua HaMashiach!

Ngoko ke ndinika nina ukuqonda ukuba izihlandlo nokuxwilwa okuyimfihlo ziingcamango eziqanjwe ngooSathana abafuna ukurhintyela abangenabuqili baze bathathe abathobekileyo nabalulamileyo entliziyweni ngobuqhetseba ngeentsomi eziyilwe ngobuchule namabali obuxoki. Noko ke, ukubonakala kwam kuya kwenzeka kanye njengoko kubhaliwe yaye kuya kubonwa ngabo bonke, kwaye ihlabathi liya kwazi ukuba uKrestu Yesu ubonakele njengoko ebethembisile kwiingcwele zakhe ezithembekileyo, eshiya abadikidiki kwintshabalalo yenyama yabo yesono ebabethimbe esonweni, kwaye kuya kwenziwa njalo.

Noko ke, abathembekileyo kuKrestu baya kuhlanganiswa njengoko kubhaliwe esibhalweni kwiNkosi yabo engunaphakade! Yaye andisayi kuphikwa nangayiphi na indlela ilifa lam endilithengileyo, yaye ndiya kubaxwila ngamandla bonke abo bangabam ngokuthembekileyo, enoba balele entliziyweni yomhlaba okanye baphakathi kwabaphilayo ebusweni bomhlaba: Baya kuhlanganiselwa kum esibhakabhakeni kwangokunjalo. Noko ke, abawileyo baya kuxoka ngohlaselo lwabo lwasemzini lobuxoki apho baya kuthi abasemzini bathathe abo bahlanganiswe kuKrestu kwaye imfazwe enkulu iya kuqhambuka kuwo wonke umhlaba, kwaye abawileyo baya kuxoka amaxesha amaninzi, kodwa ningakholelwa nelimdaka ilizwi abalithethayo. Kuba ukuhlanganiswa kwabathembekileyo kuKrestu kunikwa njengethemba nesiqinisekiso sokuba ukubuya kwam kwesibini kusondele ekupheleni kweveki yamashumi asixhenxe kaDaniyeli, leyo iyiveki yeminyaka esixhenxe apho usuku ngalunye lweveki lubalwa njengonyaka.

Noko ke, abadikidiki abasayi kuphinda bahlale bedikidiki kuba amaxesha okwahlulwa kokugqibela aya kukhululwa ngokupheleleyo xa umthinteli wam esusiwe kwaye umkhenkce uya kuwa. Ngokunjalo nabantu abadikidiki baya kufikelela kwizigqibo zabo, apho abanye baya kuba shushu ngokuzinikela kwabo nangeenkolelo zabo kwiNkosi yabo uYesu Krestu, yaye abanye baya kubanda njengomkhenkce oza kuwa elwandle, bekhetha intshabalalo yabo engunaphakade endaweni yoko.

Noko ke, mna, Krestu Yesu, ndiya kuvuselela ukuzimisela okungenakoyiswa kubo bonke abo bakhethe ubomi obungunaphakade kuYesu waseNazarete yaye ndiya kuba ndiphefumlele ukuzimisela okungenakuqhawulwa kubo ukuba babize iNkosi yabo uYesu kude kube sekupheleni, yaye ngaloo ndlela baya kufikelela kumvuzo wabo ongunaphakade apho iintlungu zingafanelekanga ukuthelekiswa nobunzima obungunaphakade nozuko lwemivuzo emininzi engunaphakade enikwa abo boyisayo; yaye ndiya kubangela ukuba abo bangaboyisi bam boyise ngendlela efanayo neNkosi yabo, nakumaxa ndoyisa abawileyo ngendlela efanayo ngexesha lokubingelelwa kwam njengeMvana kaThixo.

Kuya kuba njalo ke, ukuba abaninzi ababenokuthi bazinikele iintliziyo zabo kwiNkosi yabo uYesu Krestu kude kube sekupheleni baya kuthi endaweni yoko babe ngumkhenkce kwiNkosi yabo engunaphakade baze ngokulusizi bajike ngonaphakade kubomi obungunaphakade baye kwimililo engunaphakade yesohlwayo kuba baya kuba bemalile ngokupheleleyo uYesu waseNazarete ngenxa yeziyolo zesono kwaye baya kukhetha intuthumbo yabo engunaphakade ngoxa babenokukhetha uvuyo olungunaphakade phambi koYahweh ngonaphakade. Kwaye abaninzi ababekhe banokhetho olungunaphakade lokuba ngooNyana bakaThixo ngonaphakade baya kuthi endaweni yoko bajike ukuze bazibandakanye nabahamba begwetyiwe abaya kuwa ngonaphakade ekufeni kwesibini.

Noko ke, bonke bakhululekile ukukhetha, ngoko ke abanye baya kukhetha ngobulumko yaye abaninzi baya kukhetha ngobuyatha. Ngoko ngenani ngesango elimxinwa: kuba lisango elibanzi, nendlela ebanzi esa entshabalalweni: kwaye baninzi abangena ngalo. Kuba isango limxinwa, nendlela imxinwa esa ebomini, kwaye bambalwa abayifumanayo indlela esa ngonaphakade kubomi bobulungisa obungapheliyo kwaye akukho olunye ukhetho olunokwenziwa. Ngoko ke, khetha ubomi ukuze wena nembewu yakho niphile!

Noko ke, ndiza kunilumkisa, O' Sirayeli, ukuze bonke bayive imiyalelo yam baze bazithobele izilumkiso zam, bonke abakufuphi nabo bonke abakude nabachithakeleyo, kuba ukhetho olufanayo lokwahlulwa kokugqibela luhlala lukhona kubo bonke abaka-Adam abahleli emhlabeni kungakhathaliseki isizwe, okanye uhlanga, okanye ulwimi abazalelwe kulo. Kuya kuba njalo ke ukuba njengoko abathembekileyo bam kuKrestu bethatyathwa emhlabeni basiwa emazulwini ukuze bafihlwe kwingqumbo ezayo baze bavuzwe ngokupheleleyo ngokugqiba iikhosi zabo ngokuthobela ngokuthembekileyo uKrestu Yesu iNkosi yabo evusiweyo yanyuswayo; ukuba ihlabathi liya kuguqiswa ngamadolo ngokungakholwa okungenalukholo ekubonakaleni kwam nakwintshabalalo yequbuliso elandelayo.

Kunjalo, kuba ndiya kubonisa ubuqaqawuli bam ngokungalindelekanga ekubonakaleni kwam ngequbuliso emafini kwaye ukukhanya kwam kuya kukhanyisa izulu kwaye bonke baya kubona ubuqaqawuli beNkosi buzala iindima ngokuqaqamba okuzukileyo kwaye abaninzi baya kuthathwa ngequbuliso, kwaye umhlaba uya kujikeleza kwaye ungcangcazele kwaye uthuthumele kwaye unyikime phambi kobukho bukaKrestu Yesu, ekukhanyeni kwakhe abaninzi baya kufuna ukufihla ubuso babo. Kuba njengokuba umbane uphuma empumalanga, ubonakale nasentshonalanga, kuya kuba njalo nokufika koNyana woMntu.

Kwaye isihlwele esikhulu, abathembekileyo kuKrestu, siya kunyamalala ngequbuliso nangokungalindelekanga ngaphandle komkhondo emhlabeni, kuba ubomi babo emhlabeni enyameni eyonakeleyo buphelile kwaye iikhosi zabo baya kuba bezigqibile. Ngoko ke, baya kusiwa kubuqaqawuli bamazulu, apho baya kuzukiswa ngonaphakade baze bavuzwe ngonaphakade. Yabonani! Baya kwenziwa bafane neNkosi yabo engunaphakade yoBulungisa, yaye baya kwambathiswa ilinen emhlophe ecocekileyo engenabala engenakuze idyojwe okanye yonakaliswe. Baya kunikwa umvuzo omkhulu ngokuthembeka kwabo nangemisebenzi yobulungisa abayenzileyo egameni lam, imisebenzi endabanika yona ukuba bayifeze ukuze basindiswe, bazuze, kwaye bazukiswe.

Kwaye abaninzi baya kuthwala imivuzo enjengaleyo ingakhange ingene ngokupheleleyo ezintliziyweni zabantu ngeli xesha. Nangona kunjalo, abanye baya kulahlekelwa xa imisebenzi yabo itshiswa ngumlilo womsuli ukuze wonke umququ ususwe ebomini babo ngobunyulu obungunaphakade nokuze ibala lemisebenzi emibi lihambe kubo ngonaphakade. Qondani, ukuba akukho namnye kuni oye wahamba ngokugqibeleleyo phambi kwam, ngoko ke, abanye baya kulahlekelwa, abanye baya kulahlekelwa baze bafumane umvuzo, yaye abanye baya kufumana umvuzo abangaboyisi bam abaya kuba boyisile ngendlela efanayo nam ndoyisa abawileyo kwaye ndihleli ngasekunene kukaYahweh ngokwakhe, uMkhulu noNgunaphakade onguMNA NDIYI. Noko ke, bonke abathathiweyo nabahlanganisiweyo baya kuba nobomi obungunaphakade ngonaphakade kwaye ubomi bobulungisa obungunaphakade baya kuphila ngonaphakade kuba bamiselwe njalo.

Ngokunjalo, njengoko abathembekileyo bethatyathwa, nabo bashiyekileyo baya kubuqonda ubunzima bemeko yabo kwaye isihlwele esikhulu siya kuyamkela inyaniso nezilingo ekufuneka bazinyamezele, kwaye abaninzi baya kulila bakhale ngenguquko kwaye abaninzi baya kuphila ubomi bokuzohlwaya bothando nombingelelo, kwaye abaninzi baya kulahlekelwa bubomi babo eluthandweni nasenkonzweni kubamelwane babo abaya kuba kwiimeko ezimbi nabo kwaye isihlwele esikhulu siya kuza kum ngokubiza igama likaYesu Krestu oyiNkosi yabo engunaphakade kude kube sekupheleni!

Kwaye umhlaba uya kujikeleza kwaye ungcangcazele, kwaye intshabalalo enkulu iya kuqhambuka kuwo wonke umhlaba, njengoko imfazwe yequbuliso nesibetho, nentswela-bulungisa, nendlala nokufa kuya kubangela intshabalalo enkulu emhlabeni kwaye umhlaba uya kutshintshwa kwimo yawo yangoku ngeenyikima ezininzi ezinkulu kwaye abaninzi baya kutshabalala ngequbuliso kwaye ngaphandle konyango. Kwaye abaninzi ngakumbi baya kuhlala bephila kwaye baya kusinda kwiintlekele ezizayo kwaye abaninzi baya kushiyelwa kwiimbandezelo zikaYakobi ezifikileyo emhlabeni ukuze kuvavanywe abo bahleli emhlabeni, ukuze kusindiswe bonke abo baya kuthi bafikelele elusindisweni kuYeshua HaMashiach.

Ngoko ke, qondani ukuba kukho izivuno eziliqela zika-Adam. Isivuno sokuqala serhasi sesabathembekileyo bam kuKrestu, bonke abo bahamba ngokuthembekileyo ngomoya wam ngamaxesha am obabalo nenceba, nabo baye bavelisa isiqhamo somoya ebomini babo ngokuthobela amazwi am njengoko babehamba kumoya wam wobunyana apho batywinelwe khona usuku olukhulu lokuhlangulwa. Aba ngabathembekileyo kuKrestu kwaye bangaboyisi bam nomtshakazi wam kunye ne-144,000 yam endiya kuyithumela ukusindisa uYakobi esandleni sika-Ashkenazi no-Esawu.

Kuya kuthi ke kuqalise isivuno sengqolowa apho ingqolowa ivuthwa emasimini ize iqubude ngokuthobela ngenxa yobunzima besiqhamo esivelisiweyo, kwaye ukhula, lungenasiqhamo, luya kuma nkqo ngokuchasa kwaye kuya kubakho ukubhula okukhulu, kwaye ingqolowa yam iya kuhlanganiselwa kubomi obungunaphakade kwaye ukhula luya kuhlanganiswa, lubotshwe, lutshiswe kwimililo engunaphakade yesohlwayo. Ngoko qondani ukuba bonke abalayo ukuvunwa ngokuthobela ekubhuleni kwam baya kuzibandakanya nolu khula lubi kwilifa labawileyo lemidangatye engunaphakade eyenzelwe ngokukodwa abangathobeliyo nabavukelayo, yaye abayi kusinda. Kwaye kuya kuba njalo ngabangakholwayo nabangathembekanga abaye bawala umnqophiso woxolo kaYahweh, uYesu waseNazarete obengabosindisile ngokupheleleyo.

Siya kuba njalo ke isiphelo sobukumkani bukaSathana, bantu bakuthi, lixesha lokwahlulwa nezivuno ezigcina amalungisa ngonaphakade zize zinikele abangemalungisa kwisohlwayo esingunaphakade kwilifa labawileyo, isiphelo esigcinelwe bonke abaqhubeka nokungathobeli nabaqhubeka nemvukelo yooSathana, nabaLindi, nabo bonke abalandela iindlela zabo ezimbi. Aba baya kulwa neMvana, yaye iMvana iya kuboyisa: kuba iyiNkosi yeeNkosi, noKumkani wooKumkani: kwaye abo bangakwicala layo, ababiziweyo, nabanyuliweyo, nabathembekileyo.

Ngoko ke, qondani ukuba baya kuphuma eluthuthwini lwemfazwe, irhamncwa eliphuma elwandle, irhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni, nenamba! Baya kuphuma eluthuthwini lwentlekele enkulu abayibangele ngokwabo. Noko ke, baya kutyhola intshabalalo yabo abayibangeleyo kwiintshaba zasemzini, nakumaKrestu, nakuzo zonke izinto eziphathelele kuYesu waseNazarete, yaye ngaloo ndlela uKrestu uya kwenziwa umphuli-mthetho kubukumkani babo bobuSathana. Inene, inene, mna, Krestu Yesu, andinayo inxalenye okanye isabelo nabo kwangokunjalo, kodwa bonke ababiza igama leNkosi yabo uYesu kude kube sekupheleni baya kusindiswa.

Baya kwenza ke imfazwe neengcwele baze bazoyise ngenkohlakalo enkulu eya kubuyiselwa kubo ngaphandle kwenceba ngonaphakade. Kunjalo, isihlwele esikhulu sezigidi siya kusindiswa kwintshabalalo engunaphakade ngokutshatyalaliswa kwenyama yabo yesono eyayibabambe ebukhobokeni besono. Kwaye ngaloo ndlela ndiya kususa zonke iziyolo emhlabeni kwaye ndiya kwaphula imilingo nobugqwirha abenzi bobubi ababenzileyo kubo ukuze bavuke ebuthongweni babo baphile ngobulungisa phambi kwam njengoko bephuma esonweni ngenguquko epheleleyo nokuchasa ubukumkani bukaSathana, obo babekade bebuxhasa ngemvume ekungathobelini.

Noko ke, baya kuzityhila ngolo hlobo abawileyo njengoko bezenza abasemzini abavela kwezinye iihlabathi ezijikelezayo zobuxoki ezingekhoyo ngendlela efanayo nabafundisi babo abangamaJesuit abafundise ngayo izihlwele ngokufunda ngokuchasene nokufunda. Ukuze ubuxoki babawileyo bukholelwe ngokuqhatha okukhulu ngabo banoyolo ekungabini nalungisa. Kwaye ngaloo ndlela iqonga limiselwe aba baqhathi ukuba bazityhile kuba bonke bangabazenzisi abazenzisayo.

Baya kuzenza abancedi abanobubele abeze kusindisa u-Adam kwintshabalalo yakhe, intshabalalo leyo, enyanisweni, baya kuba bezityhile ukuba bayayiququzelela ngobuqhetseba obukhulu nangenkohliso yonke nangemiqondiso emininzi yobuxoki nemimangaliso. Kuba baya kuzisa iitekhnoloji zabo nobuqhetseba namasikizi abo kwaye umwonyo uya kuvulwa ngokupheleleyo. Ububi abuyi kuphinda buthintelwe kwaye zonke iintlobo zobubi ziya kulawula umhlaba ngexesha lembandezelo kaYakobi. Ngenxa yokuba umntu wemvelo unqwenela ububi nobumnyama kakhulu, kangangokuba uYahweh ngoku uya kunikela umntu wemvelo ngokupheleleyo kubukumkani bukaSathana ukuze abaninzi basindiswe, njengokungathi kungomlilo. Kwaye oku kungenxa yokuba uluntu lubi kwaye izenzo zalo zimbi, uluntu olubi luyazithanda kwimvukelo yalo nooSathana. Ngoko ke, uluntu luya kunikelwa ngokupheleleyo kuSathana osele eceba isiphelo sika-Adam esingunaphakade, nangona izicwangciso zabo ziya kuba lilize kwaye baya kuba nomgwebo oqatha nonzima kuphela kuba bafanelekile.

Baya kuvela ke nezisombululo zabo neetekhnoloji zabo ezandleni zabo kwaye baya kuvela bezenza abasemzini abavela kwezinye iihlabathi zobuxoki ezingekhoyo. Kwaye uBharabhas uya kuvuka, irhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni kwaye livuka nokulunywa yinyoka esandleni. Kwaye uya kwenza bonke, abancinci nabakhulu, abazizityebi nabangamahlwempu, abakhululekileyo nabangamakhoboka, ukuba bamkele uphawu esandleni sabo sasekunene okanye emabunzini abo. Kwaye kungabikho mntu unokuthenga okanye athengise, ngaphandle kwalowo unophawu okanye igama lerhamncwa okanye inani legama lalo. Nabu ubulumko. Lowo unokuqonda, makabale inani lerhamncwa: kuba linani lomntu, kwaye inani lalo ngamakhulu amathandathu anamashumi amathandathu anesithandathu.

Ngoko ke, qondani oko kubhaliweyo noko uMoya kaYahweh ukuchaza ngokucacileyo kula maxesha okuphela, bantu bakuthi, ukuze niqonde ingozi yamaxesha eniphila kuwo: Ukuba nabani na unqula irhamncwa nomfanekiso walo, aze amkele uphawu lwalo ebunzini lakhe, okanye esandleni sakhe, Yena uya kusela iwayini yengqumbo kaThixo, ewe, iwayini esulungekileyo, egalelwe kwindebe yengqumbo yakhe, kwaye uya kuthuthunjiswa ngomlilo nesulfure phambi kweeNgelosi ezingcwele, naphambi kweMvana. Nomsi wentuthumbo yabo uya kunyuka ngonaphakade: kwaye abayi kuphumla imini nobusuku, abo banqula irhamncwa negama lalo.

Noko ke, ziya kuvuka kwangokunjalo, kwanerhamncwa eliphuma elwandle nerhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni, nenamba, kwaye ziya kuvuka kulawulo lwazo olungcwele kwaye ziya kulawula umhlaba ngokungalungisi nobubi kwaye akukho nto iya kuthintelwa kumnqweno weentliziyo zazo ezimbi. Kwaye ke ziya kuthembisa izinto ezininzi ezinkulu nezinamandla. Kwaye ziya kugubungela kwaye zihombise ukulunywa yinyoka ngenkohliso enkulu nangamanga amaninzi kwaye ziya kuxela izithembiso zobuxoki ezingqonge obu bungozi apho kungekho mntu unokusinda xa sele ethathe inxaxheba. Ngoko ke, bonke abathatha uphawu lwerhamncwa baya kuba beziguqule ngonaphakade, kwanoguquko abangenakuze balubaleke kuba oko kugwenxa akunakulungiswa.

Kwaye ke ziya kuthembisa ubomi obungunaphakade, kwaye ziya kuthembisa impilo egqibeleleyo nenkululeko kwizifo kwaye ziya kuthembisa imvisiswano egqibeleleyo ngetekhnoloji yazo eziya kuthi zenzelwe uxolo, nempilo nokuphilisa. Kwaye bonke abathatha inxaxheba ekulunyweni yinyoka baya kuguqulwa ngonaphakade kumfanekiso kaYahweh abadalwe ngawo, ngaloo ndlela bezenza abahambi abaqalekisiweyo abaya kuba ngamakhoboka ngonaphakade kwiinkanuko zenamba. Kuba ngenene uphawu lwerhamncwa lunikelwe intshabalalo ka-Adam ukuze abawileyo babe nexesha labo legolide emhlabeni bodwa kunye namasikizi abo kwaye bengenaye u-Adam abamthiyileyo. Kwanamalungiselelo angenakwenzeka kuba abayi kuba nalo ixesha legolide kodwa umgwebo oqatha nonzima endaweni yoko.

Inene, inene, ngokwamkela ukulunywa yinyoka baya kuba bencame inkululeko yabo yokuzikhethela ngokuzithoba kwinamba kuba baya kuzixuba nembewu yabantu: kodwa abayi kumanyana omnye nomnye, njengoko intsimbi ingenakuxutywa nodongwe. Ukanti baya kuziguqula kwangokunjalo ngokulunywa yinyoka nangetekhnoloji equlethwe kuyo. Kwaye baya kuzifumana beqhagamshelwe kwi-intanethi yezinto ezizayo, kwanaleyo baya kulawula kwaye baqhube amakhoboka abo ukuba enze ngokuthobela intando yenamba njengoko behamba ngengqondo enye yezombi abangenakuze basinde kuyo kwaye apho bonke ababanjiswe kuyo baya kulawulwa ngamaza nemifanekiso yabo ngokupheleleyo.

Ukanti, inamba iyazi ukuba inexesha elifutshane kuphela, kwaye ngenene, lixesha elifutshane nelifutshane kuphela elinalo inamba. Abo bamkela ukulunywa yinyoka, kwanophawu lwerhamncwa, baya kukhawuleza bazifumane bengcatshiwe kwaye baya kuqonda ngokukhawuleza ukuba bangcatshwe ngezithembiso ezininzi zobuxoki, ukanti baya kuba likhoboka lokuphumeza iinkanuko zenamba kwaye baya kwenza ububi obuninzi njengoko belawulwa kwaye beyalelwa yitekhnoloji yabo apho ukusinda kuya kuba yinto engenakwenzeka.
