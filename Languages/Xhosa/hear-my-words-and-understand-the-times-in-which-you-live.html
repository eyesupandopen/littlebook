Vani Amazwi Am Kwaye Niqonde Amaxesha Eniphila Kuwo!

Vani Amazwi am, bantu bam, nani nonke endinibizayo, kwaye niqonde amaxesha eniphila kuwo. Kuba ihlabathi enilaziyo ngoku liza kuphela, kwaye endaweni yalo kuya kusala iinkqantosi ezininzi, kwanothuthu namabhodlo eBhabheli eyiMfihlelo, kuba imfazwe ifikile. Kuba xa ndixhwila uMtshakazi wam ndimsa emazulwini, intlekele enkulu iya kuyigubungela iBhabheli eyiMfihlelo, kwaye iya kutshatyalaliswa ngeyure enye, kwaye ngeyure enye iya kutshatyalaliswa, kwaye akukho nto inokutshintsha isiphelo sayo, kuba uYahweh utshilo.

Kuba wena, Bhabheli eyiMfihlelo, ulilahlile ilizwi lam waza wandibeka ecaleni mna, Yesu Krestu Olilungisa, ngenxa yezono. Kuba nezilumkiso ezikhulu enazinikwayo esizweni senu emazulwini zaba yinto yokonwabisa iintliziyo zenu ezingakholwayo namehlo enu ankanukayo, kwaye kuni yaba yenye imini nje njengoko naniphila kamnandi ezonweni zenu eziniqhubelela entshabalalweni yenu. Nganinoguqukile!

Kuba nibe luhlanga lwabanquli-zithixo, uhlanga lwabaxhokonxi-mfazwe, uhlanga olutyebiswa bubuxoki noluthanda iinkohliso zalo, uhlanga oluzalise umhlaba ngamasikizi alo; kwaye iintlanga zisela kwindebe yenu yegolide ezele ngamasikizi nokungcola kohenyo lwenu.

Ngoko ke, ndichasene nani, kwaye intshabalalo yenu ilapha kwaye sele iqalile, kuba aniluhoyanga ubizo lukaYahweh olunikezwe ngam, uYeshua HaMashiach, kwaye ngendinisindisile. Kwaye ndilile ngenxa yeendlela zenu ezineentliziyo ezilukhuni, kodwa anikafuni ukubuya ebubini; ngoko ke ndinifulathele, kwaye amaxesha eeNtlanga sele eza kuzalisekiswa ngokupheleleyo xa ndihlanganisa abathembekileyo beBandla lam ngexilongo.

Kodwa niluhlanga olubotshwe ngamatyathanga, kwaye niya kutshatyalaliswa ngokupheleleyo, kwaye anisoze niphinde nivuke. Xa uMtshakazi wam ethathwa, ihlabathi liya kumangaliswa kakhulu koko likubonileyo, kuba esi siganeko asiyi kwenzeka emfihlekweni, kodwa esidlangalaleni, ukuze ihlabathi lonke lazi ukuba kukho uThixo ezulwini kwaye undithumile mna, Krestu Yesu, ngenxa yegolide, isilivere, namatye anqabileyo omhlaba. Kuba obona butyebi bukhulu bukaBawo, kwanazo iiperile zexabiso elikhulu, ziya kususwa emgwebeni nasentshabalalweni ezayo.

Kuba uMtshakazi wam usindisiwe kwingqumbo ngenxa yedini lam, kwaye ndiya kubahlangula abathembekileyo nabo bafunyanwa bethembekile kuKrestu ngexesha lokubonakala kwam nabo bonke abathe bafa bethembekile kuKrestu, batywinwe ndim, Krestu Yesu, kuba ndenziwe umoya onika ubomi. Kwaye ndiya kubavusa abafileyo kuKrestu, kwaye abo baphilayo nabasalayo baya kuhlanganiswa kunye nabo ukuze bandihlangabeze emafini. Ngoko ke qondani ukuba esi siganeko sesokuvuza abathembekileyo nokugqibezela umtshato woMyeni kuMtshakazi wam, kuba siya kudityaniswa ngonaphakade emnqophisweni kwaye abasayi kuze bemke kum. Kwaye ndim lowo uya kubenza ookumkani nababingeleli phambi koBawo.

Kwaye ngoko baya kuwushiya umhlaba, kwaye ngoko ihlabathi liya kubona, kwaye abaninzi baya kumangaliswa kakhulu. Kuba, ngenxalenye, isizathu sesi siganeko kukukhuthaza inguquko phakathi kwabo bashiyekileyo, kwanabo balwalayo ubizo lwam ngexesha lobabalo, kwanabaninzi abangevanga ngokwaneleyo ukuba bakholwe abaya kusindiswa ngexesha lembandezelo kaYakobi, ngakumbi abo belizwe lesithembiso.

Kuba ndiyanibiza, owu, Sirayeli: nikulungele na ukuva ilizwi likaMesiya wenu wokwenene, iNgonyama yeSizwe sakwaYuda? Kuba ilizwi likaMesiya wenu wokwenene liyabiza, kwaye ndim lowo uya kulawula kwitrone kaDavide kwisixeko esingcwele, mna, Yeshua HaMashiach; kuphela ningawatyesheli amazwi obizo lwam! Kwaye ngoko ixesha lobabalo, kwanamaxesha okuhlaziya, ngoku ayavalwa ngokupheleleyo. Kwaye ngoko oko kugqibela kuya kufika ngokupheleleyo xa ndihlanganisela abathembekileyo beBandla lam kum emafini naxa ukumka kwabo kushukumisa ihlabathi lonke.

Kuya kuwushukumisa umhlaba ngokoqobo, kwaye ukushukuma okukhulu kuya kuyigubungela iBhabheli eyiMfihlelo, kwaye iya kukrazulwa phakathi xa abangcwele bam bemka. Iya kutshiswa ngokupheleleyo ngomlilo ovela esibhakabhakeni xa imijukujelwa isiwa. Iziketi zayo ziya kugutyungelwa ngamanzi avela empuma nasentshona! Kuba ingelosi enamandla iya kuthatha ilitye elinjengelitye elikhulu lokusila, iliphose elwandle, isithi, Ngaloo ndlela ngogonyamelo eso sixeko sikhulu iBhabheli siya kuwiswa phantsi, kwaye asisayi kuphinda sifunyanwe konke konke. Kuba kubhaliwe njalo kwaye kuya kwenziwa njalo!

Kuba mkhulu umgwebo wayo, kwaye umgwebo uqalile endlwini kaThixo. Iicawe zayo eziwexukileyo ziya kutshatyalaliswa, kuba ndizigwebile zonke ezingezizo ezam, kwanazo zonke iicawe zobuxoki ezithetha ubuxoki obukhulu ngohanahaniso. Bhabheli eyiMfihlelo, owu, ukuba wawuguqukele kum, Yesu Krestu, kwiindlela zakho ezimbi, kuba ngendikusindisile, kwaye mna noYahweh asinayo nayiphi na intyikityha kwimigwebo esele iza kukhululwa.

Kodwa nangona kunjalo, imigwebo iya kuwa njengoko kutshiwo, kwaye loo mgwebo uya kuba nzima kwaye ube ngunaphakade. Kuba ndandiguqukele kwiiNtlanga okwethutyana, kwaye ngoku ndiya kuzifulathela iiNtlanga ndize ndiguqukele kumhlambi endavela kuwo, uSirayeli. Nangona nabani na obiza egameni leNkosi kude kube sekupheleni uya kusindiswa, nokuba ngumYuda okanye yiNtlanga, kuba usindiso alubotshelelwanga yimida yamazwe ngamazwe.

Owu, Bhabheli eyiMfihlelo, iibhulorho zakho ziya kuwa; iindlela zakho ziya kugungqiswa, zaphulwe, zibe ezingahambekiyo; amakhonkco akho okubonelela sele edilika kwaye kungekudala aya kuba yinkqantosi. Akukho zibonelelo ziya kuziswa kuwe, kuba akukho mntu uya kuza kukunceda, kwaye uya kuba wedwa kwiyure yakho yentshabalalo, owu, henge elikhulu. Akusayi kuba nalo ulonwabo, kwaye abo basindayo baya kuzifumana besilwela nje ukusela amanzi. Indlala iya kuloyisa ilizwe. Kwaye isibetho esikhulu siya kukufikela, kwaye nezilwanyana ziya kuhlasela, kuba amaxesha afikile.

Imikhosi ebomvu urhulumente wakho ongcatshayo awayivumelayo ukuba ingene emideni yakho iya kubulala yonke indoda, umfazi, nomntwana ongahambelaniyo neenjongo zayo. Kuba iya kubulala kwaye ikhobokise bonke eba fumana. Yimikhosi yerhamncwa eye yahlanganiswa kwiimbombo zone zomhlaba ngenxa yale njongo, kwaye irhamncwa elifihliweyo nomprofeti wobuxoki ofihliweyo bayihlanganisile.

Abanalo uvelwano, akukho nceba, akukho babalo, akukho kuzisola, kwaye bayabathiya ngokupheleleyo abemi beBhabheli eyiMfihlelo ngenxa yezono ezimbi iinkokeli zenu ezizenze kumazwe abo. Kwaye ngalo lonke elo xesha uninzi lwenu lwalonwabele iimfazwe zenu ezingekho sikweni njengoko nanibukele ngonwabo koomabonakude benu, njengoko nanonwaba kakhulu ezonweni zeenkokeli zenu.

Ninike imvume kwiinkokeli zenu enazixhasa izono zazo ngevoti, kwaye ngoko niya kwabelana nazo emgwebeni wazo, kuba nizenze nafaneleka isabelo esiphindwe kabini somgwebo, kwaye uya kunigubungela ngequbuliso.

Kwaye ngoko uMtshakazi wam uya kuvela esihlalweni sam sokugweba, kwaye abanye baya kulahlekelwa, abanye baya kuthwala umvuzo omkhulu, kwaye bonke baya kwamkela ngokupheleleyo intlawulo yokuqala abasele beyifumene: Kuba ndiya kubanika imizimba emitsha, engenasono engasayi kuze yonakale! Kwaye ngoko amatywina aya kukhululwa, kuba nangona imbandezelo nosizi zande emhlabeni iminyaka emininzi ngoku, ayiyongqumbo kaThixo okanye ingqumbo yeMvana.

Uninzi lwenu ngokobuyatha lucinga ukuba kufuneka lunyamezele ingqumbo kaThixo, nicinga ngekratshi ukuba niyakwazi ukuthwala oko kuzayo. Qondani ukuba ndilisindisile iBandla lam kwingqumbo, kuba ndafika kwabam nakwiiNtlanga ngexesha lobabalo njengesivumelwano soxolo, ndinikela uxolo noxolelwaniso kuBawo weziKhanyiso, uEl Shaddai! Kwaye abo ndibasindisileyo baye bamkela ngentando yabo ekhululekileyo ubabalo lwam olungabafanelanga, ngoko landelani umzekelo wabo!

Kwaye ndiya kutywina abali-144,000 bam kwizizwe ezilandelayo, abali-12,000 kwisizwe ngasinye kwezi zilandelayo: uYuda, uRubhen, uGadi, uAshere, uNafetali, uManase, uSimeyon, uLevi, uIsakare, uZebhulon, uYosefu, noBhenjamin. Ngoko ke livani ilizwi lam!

Kwaye niya kundenzela imisebenzi emininzi enamandla, kwaye niya kukhokelela abaninzi elusindisweni kwihlabathi elifayo nelinogonyamelo. Kuba uSathana sele elungiselele inkohliso enkulu kwaye uya kwenza konke okusemandleni akhe ukuxoka, ukugquma, nokufihla indibano enkulu emafini esele iza kwenzeka. Ubuxoki bakhe sele bumile ezingqondweni nasezintliziyweni zenu ngenxa yemfundo yenu yamaJesuit ephika uBawo wam kwaye eniyiziselwe ngabawileyo. Benze zonke izinto zibe mbumbulu, bade bakhokelela uninzi lwenu ekukholelweni ukuba ngaphezulu komhlaba kukho isithuba esingapheliyo seeplanethi nobunye ubomi, kwaye abaninzi baya kuba lula kangangokuba bazigwinye ezi nkcukacha. Kwanabo amagama abo angazange abhalwe eNcwadini yoBomi, kuba baya kwamkela uphawu lwabo.

Yazini, bantu bam, kwaye sive isilumkiso sam. Akukho bomi kwezinye iiplanethi! Fundani ukuze nifunde, kuba iiplanethi ziinkwenkwezi ezibhadulayo ezimiselwe ukuxela umsebenzi wezandla zikaYahweh esibhakabhakeni phantsi kwesibhakabhaka. Aba asiyi kuba ngabaphambukeli abavela kwamanye amazwe ajikelezayo ngobuxoki; nangona kunjalo, abangabo bomhlaba, kuba baziingelosi eziwileyo ezizezasemazulwini, kwaye benze amasikizi amaninzi. Ningabakholelwa!

Ngoko ke eluthuthwini lomkhweli-hashe nakwintshabalalo enkulu kuya kuvela irhamncwa nomprofeti wobuxoki, kuba imfihlelo yobugwenxa sele isebenza. Kuba iBhabheli eyiMfihlelo inikelwe ezandleni zabo nakumanyano olwakhekayo, kwanazo iimpondo ezilishumi ziya kuyitshabalalisa, kuba zizikumkani ezilishumi ezinikela konke kwazo kwinamba, kwirhamncwa, nakumprofeti wobuxoki. Kuba xa umthinteli esusiwe, baya kuvela ngokupheleleyo ngokweZibhalo; kuba kwisiphithiphithi kuvela ucwangco lwabo olutsha, kwanocwangco lweNamba, kuba ingamandla negunya labo, kuba unikwe lona ngendlela elinganiselweyo okwexeshana elifutshane kakhulu.

Inamba inengqumbo enkulu kwaye iya kuhamba ngomoya kaHaman ukuze yenze zonke izinto zikaKrestu zibe ezingekho mthethweni, kwaye nabani na onxulumene negama lam, uYesu Krestu, uya kuzingelwa abulawe. Kwaye abo bangabathandiyo ubomi babo kude kube sekupheleni baya kusindiswa babe nomvuzo omkhulu. Ngoko gcina ingqondo nentliziyo yakho kuvuyo olubekwe phambi kwakho, kuba ngaloo ndlela ndaluqinisekisa usindiso lwakho kwintuthumbo ebuhlungu eyayiyeyakho, ngaphandle kokuba ndathatha ngokuzithandela isohlwayo sakho ndaza ndazihlawulela ngokupheleleyo izono zoluntu kanye kuphela, kwanalo lonke ixesha.

Irhamncwa liya kuhamba ngokukhawuleza, kuba liya kuqhubeka iinyanga ezingama-42 lithetha izinto ezinkulu zokunyelisa. Liya kulwa nabangcwele, kwaye linikwe ukuboyisa. Liya kunqumla iintloko zabaninzi. Liya kwenza imiqondiso emininzi yobuxoki nemimangaliso, kwaye eluthuthwini kuya kuvela ucwangco lwalo olutsha. Kwaye umprofeti wobuxoki uya kwalatha kulo alidumise aze enze bonke banqule umfanekiso walo, kuba ezi zinto zibhalwe ngokucacileyo eZibhalweni. Kwaye ngoko irhamncwa liya kuwucoca umhlaba kuyo nayiphi na into elungileyo, engcwele, nexabisekileyo. Ukanti, ndiya kubafihla abathembekileyo bam, kwaye baya kwenza imimangaliso emininzi, kwaye abaninzi baya kusindiswa.

Kodwa umprofeti wobuxoki, irhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni, uya kubangela bonke, abancinci nabakhulu, abazizityebi nabangamahlwempu, abakhululekileyo nabangamakhoboka, ukuba bamkele uphawu esandleni sabo sasekunene okanye emabunzini abo: Kwaye kungabikho mntu unokuthenga okanye athengise, ngaphandle kwalowo unophawu, okanye igama lerhamncwa, okanye inani legama lalo. Ngoko ke qondani ukuba ukuthatha inxaxheba kuluntu lwabo olutsha lobuxoki kuya kufuna uphawu, kwaye bonke abathatha uphawu abasoze baxolelwe. Kwaye baya kuba nesabelo sabo kwelo chibi likhulu livutha umlilo nesulfure ngonaphakade kanaphakade, kwaye ngoko umsi wentuthumbo yabo uya kunyuka.

Amangqina am amabini aya kuvela, kwaye aya kuprofeta enxibe amalaphu okuzila! Le yimithi emibini yomnquma, nezibane ezibini ezimi phambi koThixo womhlaba. Kwaye akukho mntu uya kukwazi ukumelana nabo, kuba baya kuprofeta iinyanga ezingama-42. Kwaye ukuba kukho umntu oya kubenzakalisa, umlilo uphuma emlonyeni wabo, utshise iintshaba zabo: kwaye ukuba kukho umntu oya kubenzakalisa, kufuneka abulawe ngale ndlela. Aba banamandla okuvala izulu, ukuze kungani mvula ngeentsuku zokuprofeta kwabo: kwaye banamandla phezu kwamanzi ukuwajika abe ligazi, nokubetha umhlaba ngazo zonke izibetho, rhoqo njengoko bethanda.

Kwaye xa begqibile ubungqina babo, irhamncwa elinyuka emngxunyeni ongenasiphelo liya kulwa nabo, liboyise, libabulale. Kwaye baya kushiya imizimba yabo ezitratweni iintsuku ezintathu enesiqingatha, kwaye ihlabathi lonke liya kuvuya, kwaye emva koko baya kuvuswa bathatyathelwe emazulwini.

Umprofeti wobuxoki sele ezithandekisile kwabo baselizweni ukuze bacinge ukuba unguMesiya wabo nokumkani wabo. Ungumakhi wetempile wobuxoki kwaye uvela kwisizwe sakwaDan, kwaye akayena uKoreshi. Uya kunika lonke uncedo kwirhamncwa lokuqala eliphuma elwandle. Kuba irhamncwa lokuqala liya kunyanzelisa ngobundlobongela iNoahide kwiintlanga ngokungekho mthethweni okukhulu, kwaye umprofeti wobuxoki uya kulawula kwitrone engeyiyo eyakhe, kuba yeyam, mna Yeshua HaMashiach. Kwaye ngoko itempile iya kwakhiwa, kwaye umprofeti wobuxoki uya kwenza imiqondiso emininzi yobuxoki nemimangaliso, kuba unikwe amandla ngokufanayo nerhamncwa lokuqala, kwaye ngoko aba babini baya kusebenza ngengqondo enye nenjongo enye, kwanangengqondo neenjongo zaloo namba inkulu yobuxoki, kodwa ayinaxabiso kwaye ayinamazinyo, ngoko musani ukumoyika!

Kuba uyazi ukuba ndiyeza kutshabalalisa ubukumkani bakhe, kuba ayinguye ukumkani kodwa lutshaba oloyiswe ngokupheleleyo, olungenathemba kwaphela. Ngoko musani ukumoyika kwaye nihlale ninengqondo enye kum. Kuba, yabonani, ndinixelela ezi zinto phambi kokuba zenzeke ukuze nibe nokholo kum kwaye nibe nokholo kuBawo ondithumileyo, uYahweh uThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade, kwanam nyana wakhe, uYeshua HaMashiach, oya kuzalisekisa izithembiso ezenziwa koobawo.

Kwaye ngoko ekupheleni kweenyanga zokuqala ezingama-42 isikizi lentshabalaliso liya kwenzeka ngesandla somprofeti wobuxoki, irhamncwa eliza kuphuma emhlabeni. Ngexesha leenyanga zesibini ezingama-42, kwanangelo xesha kuya kubakho eyona mbandezelo inkulu eyakha yabakho nesikizi lentshabalaliso, kwaye uya kuzibiza ngoThixo! Abaninzi baya kutyunyuzwa kwaye abaninzi baya kutshabalala, kodwa abo bathobela ilizwi lam baya kusiwa endaweni entlango apho baya kufihlwa bagcinwe bekhuselekile okwethutyana, njengoko la aya kuba ngawona maxesha anobundlobongela, kwaye la maxesha ezilingo novavanyo aya kuba ngawona anzima kakhulu. Emva koko niya kubona amazulu evuliwe, kwaye niya kundibona mna, Yesu Krestu, ndikhokela umkhosi omkhulu nonamandla wasezulwini, kwaye uya kuba ngumkhosi ongenakufaniswa nawuphi na, naphakade! Kuba uMtshakazi wam uya kuba nam. Kwaye ndiya kulityumza irhamncwa nomprofeti wobuxoki nayo yonke imikhosi yabo nawo onke amasikizi abo abawenze ngokukhohlakeleyo, kwaye ngoko baya kutshatyalaliswa nangokukhanya kokufika kwam nangelo krele libukhali elintlangothi-mbini eliphuma emlonyeni wam, kwaye abayi kuma kodwa baya kutshatyalaliswa ngokupheleleyo. Kwaye irhamncwa nomprofeti wobuxoki baya kuphoswa bephila kwelo chibi livuthayo lomlilo nesulfure, kwaye abasayi kuze bakhululwe kwintuthumbo yabo, kuba bazenze bafaneleka.

Kwaye emva koko kuya kwenzeka uvuko lwamalungisa, xa abangcwele bakudala nabangcwele bexesha lembandezelo beya kuvuswa kwabafileyo kubomi obutsha, kwaye abasayi kuphinda bafe, kwaye ndiya kubanika umvuzo omkhulu, onjengalowo ungazange ungene ezintliziyweni zabantu ngeli xesha. Kuba ndiyiNkosi enenceba noKumkani onenceba, kwaye ndikukuzala kobuThixo ngokwasemzimbeni.

Kwaye ngoko ulawulo lwam lweminyaka eliwaka emhlabeni luya kuqala, kwaye ubulungisa buya kulawula emhlabeni, kwaye ndiya kulawula iintlanga ngentonga yentsimbi, kwaye umhlaba uya kuzala ngeendlela zikaYahweh. Kuba ndiyeza nesihlwele sookumkani nababingeleli, kwaye baya kulawula kwitrone yam, kwaye baya kulawula ngobulungisa. Kwaye ingonyama iya kulala nemvana.
