Ubungqina Obubini: IBandla
Kwiincwadi zikaPawulos uMpostile
Meyi 15, 2024

Yabona, Umvuzo Wam Unam!

KumTshakazi wam othandekayo, othembekileyo, ondithande ngokupheleleyo ngentliziyo yakhe yonke. Ulihlo lam, yaye kungekudala ndiyeza kuwe, kungekudala kunokuba ucinga. Ngenxa yokuthembeka kwakho, kukho umvuzo omkhulu emazulwini olungiselelwe wena. Yabona! Iliso alikabonanga, nendlebe ayikevanga, kungangenanga nasentliziyweni yomntu, izinto uThixo abalungiselele zona abamthandayo. Zikwanjalo neenyaniso ezimangalisayo ezingokuwe, sithandwa sam, kuba uya kuba nesabelo kwilifa lam ngokokuba ukuthembeka kwakho kungqine esihlalweni sam sokugweba, kwanakuloo Bema mkhulu, apho ndiya kugweba imisebenzi yalowo nalowo wabiziweyo wam, nonyuliweyo, nothembekileyo.

Kuba abo imisebenzi yabo itshayo, baya kulahlekelwa, kodwa bona ngokwabo baya kusindiswa, ngokungathi kuphuma emlilweni; kwaye abo imisebenzi yabo ihleliyo, baya kufumana ubutyebi obuninzi, kuba loo mini iza kukubonakalisa kungekudala. Kuba le asiyongcamango ingaqhelekanga kuwe, kodwa yileyo uyifunde kubungqina bamaKorinte kwincwadi yesibini kaPawulos: Kuba thina sonke simelwe kukuthi sivele phambi kwesihlalo sokugweba sikaKristu, ukuze ngamnye wethu amkele izinto azenzileyo esemzimbeni, ngokoko akwenzileyo, nokuba kulungile nokuba kubi. Siyazi ngoko ukoyikeka kweNkosi, siyabacenga abantu; kodwa senziwe sabonakala kuThixo, yaye, ndiyathemba kananjalo, senziwe sabonakala kwizazela zenu.

Kuba isazela sakho siya kungqina namazwi akho xa kungekudala uza kuziphendulela phambi koMgwebi olilungisa, mna ke, uYesu Kristu. Abanye baya kuma phambi kwam baze babonise imisebenzi yabo eya kutshiswa ngumlilo womcocisi oya kubangela ihlazo nelahleko. Abanye baya kuma phambi kwam bethe nkqo, bezihlambile egazini lam lentlawulelo, apho niya kundibonisa imisebenzi endaninika yona ukuba niyenze. Kuba ukungena ekuphumleni kwam kukuphumla kwimisebenzi yenu. Ngoko ke kwabo baphumle kwimisebenzi yam kukho umvuzo omkhulu kwabathembekileyo. Kodwa nonke niya kusindiswa, kwaye akukho namnye kuni ohambe ngokugqibeleleyo phambi kwam, kwaye ke loo mini iya kukubonakalisa. Musani ukoyika, ndiya kunivuza ngokugqithisileyo, kuba yonke imisebenzi emikhulu enindenzelwe yona emfihlekweni iya kubonakaliswa, ngoko ke loo mini iya kukubonakalisa.

Kutheni iintliziyo zenu zikhathazwa ngala mazwi? Anifundanga na kwincwadi yokuqala kaYohane, apho ndamenza wabhala? Kuba ukuba intliziyo yethu iyasigweba, uThixo mkhulu kunentliziyo yethu, kwaye wazi zonke izinto. Zintanda, ukuba intliziyo yethu ayisigwebi, ngoko sinokholo kuThixo. Kwaye kwakhona: Ukuba sithi asonanga, siyazikhohlisa, kwaye inyaniso ayikho kuthi. Ukuba siyazivuma izono zethu, uthembekile, kwaye ulilungisa ukuba asixolele izono zethu, kwaye asihlambulule kuko konke ukungalungisi. Ngoko, zivumeni izono zenu kum yonke imihla kwaye rhoqo kangangoko nithanda, kwaye nizihlambulule egazini lam lentlawulelo.

Kuba ndingumthetheleli wenu, kwaye nathengwa ngedini lam njengePasika egqibeleleyo ukwenza isiphelo sezono, nokwenza uxolelwaniso ngenxa yobugwenxa, nokuzisa ubulungisa obungunaphakade. Yabona, Mtshakazi wam! Amaxesha okubuyiselwa asekufuphi, kuba uPetros waprofeta ngeli xesha xa waphilisa indoda eSangweni leTempile elihle: Kwaye uya kuthumela uYesu Kristu, owayeshunyayelwe kuni ngaphambili. Lowo izulu limele ukumamkela, kude kube ngamaxesha okubuyiselwa kwezinto zonke. Kwaye ngoku afikile amaxesha okubuyiselwa, Mtshakazi wam omhle, kuba ninyulu phambi kwam kwaye nihlanjululwe kuko konke ukungalungisi egazini leMvana, kuba ndiyiMvana kaThixo! Ngoko ke, zivumeni izono zenu ngokuthembekileyo.

Kwaye ke umgwebo, ukubuyiselwa, ilahleko, nomvuzo omkhulu konke kuqala ngendlu kaThixo. Kuba kungekudala, niya kuqokelelwa ndawonye emafini ukuze nihlangane nam emoyeni, kuba ndiya kuhla ndivela emazulwini ndiduma, nangelizwi lesiphatha-zithunywa, kwaye ixilongo lokugqibela liya kukhala! Qhubekani nokujonga amazulu nize niqwalasele, kuba uThixo wenze imiqondiso emininzi enamandla emazulwini, wade wabhengeza umtshato womyeni nomtshakazi wakhe, kwanomnqophiso ongunaphakade phakathi kwam nomtshakazi wam, oza kwenzeka kungekudala. Ngoko zivumeni izono zenu nize nindicele ukuba ndinibonise izono enizilibeleyo ukuze nizivume nazo; kwaye ngaphezu kwako konke, yimani niqinile eluthandweni lukaThixo, kuba uthando lukaThixo lugubungela izono ezininzi. Ngoko musani ukoyika, kuba akunakwenzeka ukuba nizivume zonke.

Ngokomgwebo: kuba nisukile ekufeni naya ebomini kum, iNkosi yenu evusiweyo uYesu Kristu. Kuba ndabuncama ubomi bam; akukho mntu wabuthathayo. Ndabunikezela ngokuzithandela kwaye ndingaphinda ndikwenze ukuba bekufuneka. Kodwa akukho mfuneko yento enjalo, kuba ndabaxelela ntoni amaHebhere: Kuba akunakwenzeka ukuba igazi leenkunzi zeenkomo neleebhokhwe, lisuse izono. Kodwa le ndoda, emva kokuba inikele idini elinye ngenxa yezono ngonaphakade, yahlala phantsi ngasekunene kukaThixo. Mtshakazi wam, andiyi kunigweba ngokwezono zenu; kuba bonke abakuKristu bagwetyelwe, ewe bangcwalisiwe, bade benziwa amalungisa kum, uKristu Yesu, oya kuphila aze alawule ngonaphakade, kuba ubukumkani bam abunaphelo. Ngoko ke nikhululwe ezonweni zenu; ngoko ke, aniyi kuba semgwebeni waboni kodwa emgwebeni wamalungisa, kuba umgwebo uqala endlwini kaThixo.

Ngokubuyiselwa: kuba ndiya kubuyekeza, itsho iNkosi. Uninzi lwenu lushiye izindlu, amazwe, iintsapho kwaye lwenza amadini amaninzi, amaninzi kakhulu; kwaye abaninzi phakathi koMtshakazi wam bade bancama nobomi babo, bangamaNgqina am. Ngoko ke kuya kubakho ukubuyiselwa kwezinto ezenziwe idini kwanelahlekileyo. Kuba saba yintoni isiphelo sikaYobhi: Kwaye iNkosi yakuguqula ukuthinjwa kukaYobhi, xa wayethandazela abahlobo bakhe: kananjalo iNkosi yamnika uYobhi okuphindwe kabini kunoko wayenako ngaphambili. Nelizwi likaYakobi umntakwethu nalo lithi: Yabonani, sithi banoyolo abo banyamezelayo. Nivile ngomonde kaYobhi, kwaye nisibonile isiphelo seNkosi: ukuba iNkosi inenceba enkulu kwaye inemfesane. Unjalo ke noYihlo, kwaye ndinjalo nam, kuba ndingowoMkhulu onguNDINGUYE!

Ngelahleko: kuba abaninzi baya kulahlekelwa, kuba ndithe ngokuphathelele kwisihlalo sam sokugweba kumaKorinte: Kuba thina sonke simelwe kukuthi sivele phambi kwesihlalo sokugweba sikaKristu, ukuze ngamnye wethu amkele izinto azenzileyo esemzimbeni, ngokoko akwenzileyo, nokuba kulungile nokuba kubi. Kwaye kwakhona amaKorinte ayangqina: Umsebenzi womntu ngamnye uya kubonakaliswa. Kuba loo mini iya kuwubonakalisa, kuba uya kutyhilwa ngomlilo, kwaye umlilo uya kuvavanya umsebenzi womntu ngamnye ukuba unjani na. Ukuba umsebenzi wakhe nawuphi na umntu utshile, uya kulahlekelwa: kodwa yena ngokwakhe uya kusindiswa: kodwa kunjalo, ngokungathi kuphuma emlilweni.

Ngomvuzo omkhulu: kuba amaKorinte athetha kwakhona: Iliso alikabonanga, nendlebe ayikevanga, kungangenanga nasentliziyweni yomntu, izinto uThixo abalungiselele zona abamthandayo. Bathembekileyo bam abanyuliweyo, aninakuwuqonda umbhiyozo omkhulu nezidlo ezilungiselelwe nina nguBawo emazulwini; kuba niza ngombhiyozo, kwaye ubutyebi obukhulu bugcinelwe nina emazulwini. (Kuba nibuyela ekhaya kum, uKristu Yesu, nakuBawo wethu, uYahweh, uThixo ongunaphakade.) Kwaye kwakhona: ukuba umsebenzi wakhe nawuphi na umntu uhleli, awakhileyo phezu kwawo, uya kwamkela umvuzo. Ndinemivuzo emininzi kangaka yokunika, kwaye ilifa lam linam; ngoko musani ukoyika, kuba niya kuba nesabelo kwilifa lam; kwaye apho ndikhoyo, nani niya kuba lapho, kuba amaxesha okwahlukana aphelile.

Izithsaba zomvuzo negunya zikulindile. Ndidwelise ezinye esibhalweni: isithsaba esingenakonakala, isithsaba sovuyo, isithsaba sobulungisa, isithsaba sozuko, nesithsaba sobomi.

Kwabo balubalekileyo ugqatso, kwanemikhondo yenkonzo endibabekele yona, baya kwamkela isithsaba esingenakonakala: kuba niya kube nizilawula phantsi komoya wam, kwaye niya kube niziluleka ngokufanelekileyo njengaye nawuphi na umdlali. Kwabo baluphumeleleyo ugqatso ngokusemthethweni sesabo isithsaba esingenakonakala.

Kukho isithsaba sovuyo. Kuba liyintoni ithemba lethu, okanye uvuyo, okanye isithsaba sovuyo? Asingabo na nina ebukhosini beNkosi yethu uYesu Kristu ekufikeni kwayo? Kuba, nina niluzuko novuyo lwethu. Kwaye ke siya kuba novuyo olukhulu, kwaye nonke niya kuba sebuqaqawulini nize nizukiswe kum! Kwabo basebenze ngokuthembekileyo ukuguqula nokukhulisa abazalwana babo sesabo isithsaba sovuyo.

Kukho isithsaba sobulungisa kwabo bagqiba imikhondo yabo nakwabo bathanda ukubonakala kwam. Ngoko qhubekani nijonge phezulu, kuba ndikufuphi kwaye ndinolindelo olukhulu lokubonakala kwam. Kuba ulindelo olukhulu lokubonakala kwam lukwazisa umvuzo omkhulu. Kwabo banolindelo olukhulu lokubonakala kwam sesabo isithsaba sobulungisa.

Kukho isithsaba sozuko kwabo bawondlayo umhlambi kaThixo. Kuba uPetros wabhala: wondlani umhlambi kaThixo ophakathi kwenu, niwuvelela, kungekhona ngokunyanzelwa, kodwa ngokuzithandela: kungekhona ngenxa yenzuzo emdaka, kodwa ngengqondo ekulungeleyo. Kwabo bangazigqali njengeenkosi phezu kwelifa likaThixo kwaye bengaqhutywa yinzuzo emdaka nabo bandikhonze ngokuthembekileyo ngokuthobeka nangentliziyo efanelekileyo yothando sesabo isithsaba sozuko.

Kukho isithsaba sobomi endiya kusinika amaNgqina am, kwabo baphathwa gadalala baze batshutshiswe ngenxa yegama lam nakwabo banyamezele ngomonde amaqhinga namayelenqe kamtyholi. Kwabo bazoyisile ngokuthembekileyo ezi zinto sesabo isithsaba sobomi.

Niya kwenziwa nifane nam, uAdam wokugqibela kwaye niya kufana nam, kwaye niya kundibona njengoko ndinjalo, kwaye nani niya kuba njalo. Yabonani! Ndiya kunidalela imizimba emitsha engafaniyo nayo nayiphi na into eninokuyicinga. Jonga iingxelo esibhalweni apho ndabonakala khona kwabathembekileyo bam ngaphambi kokuba ndinyuselwe emazulwini. Ndiya kuwulungisa lo mzimba ngokukodwa kuni, kwaye uya kuthwala izakhono zenu, impumelelo yenu, izinto enizifezileyo; kwaye niya kumangaliswa ngokupheleleyo.

Aniqondi na into endayenza uPawulos wayibhalela amaKorinte? Kukho nemizimba yasezulwini, nemizimba yasemhlabeni: Kodwa ubuqaqawuli bobasezulwini bobunye, nobuqaqawuli bobasemhlabeni bobunye. Kukho ubuqaqawuli obunye belanga, obunye benyanga, nobunye beenkwenkwezi: kuba inkwenkwezi enye yahlukile kwenye inkwenkwezi ngobuqaqawuli. Kunjalo ke novuko lwabafileyo: ihlwayelwa ekonakaleni, ivuswa ekungenakonakaleni. Bantwana bam, niya kuba nezakhono ezininzi eningazange niziphuphange. Nangona ngamnye wenu eya kwahluka komnye, nonke niya kuhamba ngokugqibeleleyo kum, kuba ndiya kunenza ookumkani nababingeleli phambi koThixo wethu!

Kuya kubakho izinto ezininzi eningasayi kuphinda nithwaliswe zona. Akusayi kubakho kugula, akusayi kubakho sono kuni, akusayi kubakho kufa kuni, akusayi kubakho loyiko, akukho xhala; kuba nonke niya kudalwa ngokutsha ngokugqibeleleyo kum, uKristu Yesu iNkosi. Ngoko ndithi kuni, Mtshakazi wam, yibani ngaboyisi, kuba imivuzo yomoyisi ndiya kuyinika, kwaye ingaphaya kweengcinga namaphupha enu amakhulu. Kuba umthi wobomi, owawuthwala iintlobo ezilishumi elinambini zeziqhamo, waza wavelisa isiqhamo sawo nyanga nenyanga: kwaye amagqabi omthi ayengawokuphilisa iintlanga. Kwaye akusayi kubakho siqalekiso, kodwa itrone kaThixo, neyeMvana iya kuba kuyo, kwaye abakhonzi bakhe baya kumkhonza. Kwaye baya kubona ubuso bakhe, negama lakhe liya kuba semabunzini abo. Kwaye akusayi kubakho busuku apho, kwaye abadingi khandlela, kungekho kukhanya kwelanga, kuba iNkosi uThixo ibanika ukukhanya, kwaye baya kulawula ngonaphakade kanaphakade. Ngoko musani ukoyika, Mtshakazi wam othembekileyo, umvuzo omkhulu uza kwenzeka kungekudala kuni. Kuba uThixo akalilo ilungisa, ukuba alibale umsebenzi wenu nomgudu wothando, eniwubonakalisileyo kwiGama lakhe, ngokuthi nikhonze aBangcwele, kwaye nisakhonza.

Ndiyeza kuwe, kungekudala kunokuba ucinga.

Ndingumthetheleli wenu.

Ndinguye kwaye nani niya kuba njalo, kuba niya kundibona njengoko ndinjalo.

Ndiyeza, kwaye umvuzo wam unam kwabathembekileyo.

Ndingulowo uya kuninika ngokupheleleyo ilifa lenu, kuba linikelwe kum.

Ndingulowo uya kuninika imizimba emitsha endaweni yomzimba wesono.

Ndinguye, kuba ndingowoMkhulu onguNDINGUYE!

Jongani ukubonakala kwam emafini, njengoko imiqondiso iya kundibhengeza ngaphambi kokuba ukukhanya kwam kuqaqambe esibhakabhakeni. Mna, uYeshua HaMashiach, ndithumele la Mazwi.
