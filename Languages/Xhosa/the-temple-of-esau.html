Ingqina eliMbini: Sirayeli
Okukrakra Okumnandi
Novemba 23, 2024

Itempile ka-Esawu

Yivani, yivani! O' Sirayeli ilizwi likaYeshua HaMashiach, kwanaye okuphela koNyana ozelweyo kaYahweh, uMkhulu Ndinguye! Ukuze ndinilumkise ngezinto ezizayo, nokuze niqonde ngakumbi umgibe aza kuwubeka uBharabhas ukuze ningabanjiswa nabangakholwayo; izinto ebezibhalwe ngenxalenye ngaphambili kwiVangeli Engunaphakade. Kodwa ndize kunixelela ngomgibe isigcawu esiwubeka kwilizwe lesithembiso nokuwucacisa ukuze ningatshatyalaliswa ngamayelenqe abo. Kwaye ndinqwenela ukwenza iindlela zenu zicace kwaye ndinigcine kwiindlela zobulungisa, kwanazo iindlela zikaYahweh, ezindlela zakhe ziphila ngokugqibeleleyo kuYeshua HaMashiach uMesiya wenu wokwenene osoloko ethobela ngokugqibeleleyo intando kaYahweh. Kananjalo ukuze nigcine unyawo lwenu kuloo ndlela icutheneyo nemxinwa, endiya kugcina iinyawo zenu zimi nkqo kuyo ukuba nje niyaqhubeka nindilandela ngokholo oluthobelayo. Kuba olilungisa uya kuphila ngokholo!

Ngoko ke qondani ukuba ixesha lifikile, kanye njengoko kwabhengezwa kwaza kwaziswa apho ndiya kubahlanganisa ngequbuliso abathembekileyo kuKristu ndibase emazulwini, nokuba baphilile okanye bafile; kwaye njengoko abangcwele abathembekileyo bethathwa ngequbuliso, intshabalalo yesiquphe iya kufikela iBhabheli Eyimfihlo ehleli phezu kwamanzi amaninzi, abantu, nezihlwele, neentlanga, neelwimi. Kwaye njengoko uthuthu lwemfazwe luqhumisa kwaye ulungelelwaniso lwangoku lweentlanga lutshatyalaliswa kwaye luhlengahlengiswa ukuze lulungele iinjongo zabo, inkqubo yokugqibela yerhamncwa lowileyo iya kuvela ngokupheleleyo.

Noko ke, irhamncwa eliphuma elwandle nerhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni badlale indima yabalusi abalungileyo abeza kwalusa iigusha ezilahlekileyo zeBhabheli Eyimfihlo ngezithembiso ezininzi ezingenanto; kwanazo izithembiso zobuxoki ezithi ekugqibeleni zizise ukufa nentshabalalo. Kwaye benza ngathi baziintshaba emva kwesigqubuthelo sexhego elidala eliyisidenge abazifihla emva kwalo nelo sidenge sikhoyo ukuze sithwale ityala lolwaphulo-mthetho lwabo; kwaye balawula yonke intshukumo yalo kwaye babhala onke amazwi alo. Kuba le nkohliso inkulu inikwe njengomgwebo kwiBhabheli Eyimfihlo; ukuze inkohliso enamandla iyifikele ngokupheleleyo kuba iwugqume umhlaba ngobuxoki bayo inendebe yegolide esandleni sayo, izele ngamasikizi nokungcola kohenyo lwayo.

Kwaye noko la marhamncwa mabini akhohlakeleyo ayonakalisile iBhabheli Eyimfihlo, kwanangaphambi kokuba intshabalalo yayo ifike kwaye athathe amandla ayo omkhosi nobuninzi bayo ngeenjongo zenamba kwinkqubo yawo yokugqibela yerhamncwa. Kuba imfazwe ezayo iya kuyidla iBhabheli Eyimfihlo, kwaye ngenene iya kutshatyalaliswa ngeyure enye, kuba kutshiwo njalo nguYahweh, kwanaye uMkhulu Ndinguye. Noko ke, oku kuyilwe kwaye kukwacwangciswe kwicebo likaYahweh lokuhlangula (ukuze iintshaba zakhe zilithobele ilizwi lakhe) kwaye ke umgwebo weHeneli elikhulu ubhengeziwe, kwaye ubhaliwe, kwaye kuya kwenziwa njalo ngonaphakade, kwaye alisoze liphinde livuke. Nangona kunjalo, baya kuwucanda umhlaba kwaye kuya kubakho ukuzamazama okukhulu, kwaye bayilungiselele intshabalalo yalo ukuze baqokelele ubutyebi ababunqwenelayo obungcwatywe emhlabeni. Kwanabo ubutyebi ababufunayo ukuze banike amandla kwaye bandise iteknoloji yabo abawileyo; kwanaleyo ihlabathi ngoku litsalwe kwaye likhankathekiswe yiyo kwinkohliso yalo enamandla, ligcinwe likhankathekiswe ziifrikhwensi zabo kunye neenkohliso zabo abavuselelwa kwaye balawulwe ngazo.

Kwaye kubantu abaya kusala emva kwentshabalalo ezayo yeBhabheli Eyimfihlo; irhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni nerhamncwa eliphuma elwandle bangenise umkhosi wabahlaseli nababulali ngaphakathi kwemida yayo abamanani abo anjengesibetho esikhulu seenkumbi, kwaye baya kukhululwa ngomqondiso wabo! Kuba le yimikhosi yerhamncwa eye yahlanganiselwa intshabalalo yeBhabheli Eyimfihlo. Uninzi lwabo luvela kumazwe enamba nakumazwe ebhere, kwaye baya kwenza njengoko iBhabheli Eyimfihlo yenza kubo ngasese kwiminyaka edlulileyo: Baya kubulala yonke indoda, umfazi, nomntwana ngaphandle kokuzisola abangazifezekisiyo iinjongo zabo kwaye abaninzi abavunyelweyo ukuba bahlale bephila baya kunyanzelwa ukuba baye kwiinkampu zabo zamakhoboka ukuze basebenze emigodini yabo de bafe. Kwaye baya kubulaleka kwaye basebenze de baphefumle okokugqibela; kuba konke oko iBhabheli Eyimfihlo ikwenzileyo ngasese kwabanye ngoku kuya kwenziwa kuyo esidlangalaleni njengomgwebo nesohlwayo. Kuba iBhabheli Eyimfihlo inxilile ligazi labangcwele, nalo igazi labafeli-nkolo bakaYesu ebabulale ngokobuninzi obukhulu kumazwe angaphandle; ngalo lonke elo xesha isithi yona ikhonza uYesu waseNazarete. Kodwa eneneni ikhonza abawileyo kwaye iinjongo zayo ngoku ziphelile, kwaye bayanqwenela intshabalalo yayo ukuze bagqume ulwaphulo-mthetho lwabo nokuze abalusi babo abanxilileyo babonakale belungile xa bevuka ukuze bafihle umgibe wabo.

Noko ke, abasoze bakwazi ukuzifihla kuYahweh, nakuYeshua HaMashiach kwaye ulwaphulo-mthetho lwabo lubhalwe ngokugqibeleleyo emazulwini kwaye sonke isono sibhalwe ngeenkcukacha ezigqibeleleyo kunye neengcinga neenjongo zeentliziyo zabo ezingcolileyo. Kwaye irhamncwa eliphuma elwandle nerhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni ngamasikizi akhohlakeleyo adalelwe intshabalalo yoluntu. La marhamncwa akhohlakeleyo ngabaxhaphazi babantwana, kwaye baqokelela kwaye basebenzise imizimba yabancinane ababulele ukwenza amasikizi abo kwaye ngoko konke abakutshabalalisayo kunikelwa njengedini kuthixo wabo. Kuba bathabatha uMnquba kaMoloki, nenkwenkwezi kathixo wabo uRemfan, imifanekiso abayenzileyo, ukuze bayinqule, kwaye benze okuninzi kakhulu okuya kutyhilwa ngokupheleleyo ngamaxesha afanelekileyo okutyhila kuba yonke imfihlo iya kuvela ekukhanyeni.

Kuya kuba njalo ukuba irhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni liya kubonakala livuka engathi livela engcwabeni; kwanokuthi livukile kwabafileyo, kanye entliziyweni yomhlaba kuba ukufa akukwazanga ukulibamba! Kwaye liya kuba novuko lwalo lobuxoki kwaye lizise uxolo lobuxoki emhlabeni ukuze liphelise imfazwe yabo, kwaye liya kubonakala ngathi lize kusindisa uluntu. Kwaye abawileyo baya kuzityhila eluntwini njengabancedi abanobubele abagqwesileyo kakhulu ngobulumko kwaye bephucukile kwiindlela zeteknoloji, amayeza, noxolo.

Baya kuvelisa imiqondiso emininzi yobuxoki nemimangaliso, kwaye baya kuthi abo bahlanganiswe kuKristu kwindibano enkulu kwaye bathathelwa kukhuseleko lwamazulu ngaphambi kokuba kufike intlekele enkulu; ukuba kwanabo bathathwe ngamandla lutshaba oluzenza lwasemzini. Kwanombutho wasemzini onobutshaba oya kuphinda uze kutshabalalisa uluntu, kwaye baya kuhlanganisa umntu wemvelo ukuba amanyane nabo ukuze azisindise kobu bubi ababucingayo kwindalo yabo yobuxoki yehlabathi elijikelezayo elingeyonyani kwisithuba esihlala sisanda selize.

Kwanayo i-cosmology yobuxoki abawileyo ababeyicinga kwaye bayisebenzisa ngaphambili kwixesha labo legolide lakudala, elo xesha legolide lafikelela esiphelweni salo ngezandla zikaYahweh kunogumbe omkhulu ngemihla kaNowa, iingxelo zazo zikrolwe kwimibhalo engcwele yakudala. Kwaye obo buxoki buya kuphoselwa emhlabeni kuba inkohliso yabo enkulu iya kutyhilwa ngokupheleleyo ukuze bonke babone aba bazenzisi abazenza oko bangakuko ngenene kuba bazizidalwa eziwileyo ezivela kwindawo yasezulwini ezizone ngokwazo kudala kwimvukelo engenamsebenzi kubulungisa bukaYahweh ukuze zizuze intshabalalo yazo eziyenzele zona kwilifa lazo lesulfure evuthayo ezingasoze ziphuncuke kuyo.

Kwaye ke irhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni liya kuvuka libonakala ngathi livela kwabafileyo kwaye ngemiqondiso emininzi yobuxoki nemimangaliso liya kubonakala, kwanalo nesebe lalo lomnquma esandleni. Kwaye liya kuvuka njengetakane elineempondo ezimbini njengoko livuka ngamandla apheleleyo negunya lenamba kwanalo nerhamncwa eliphuma elwandle, kwanalo elikhethwe yinamba nerhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni ukuze lilawule iintlanga ukuze lilawule ulwandle lweentlanga ngokwemigaqo yabo enyelisayo neyokubulala abantu kaNowa. Noko ke, amazwe alo amabini akasoze abe sisisombululo kuba ndinguye oya kumanyanisa uSirayeli ngonaphakade angaze aphinde ahlulwe abe zizikumkani ezibini kwakhona.

Kwaye ke baya kusebenzisa imimiselo yabo emibi neziphumo eziqulethwe kuNowa ukuze balwe nabangcwele kwaye baboyise, ngaphandle kwakhe nabani na obiza egameni leNkosi kude kube sekupheleni uya kusindiswa, kwanabo babiza uYesu waseNazarete njengeNkosi yabo esindisayo eya kubasindisa ngokupheleleyo ukuba abatyhafi kwiinkxwaleko zabo. Kwaye ke irhamncwa eliphuma elwandle liya kukhululwa phezu kweentsalela zeentlanga abaya kube bezitshabalalisile, nothuthu lwazo lwaluziintlanga ababekade bezilawula ngenkohliso enkulu. Kwaye baya kubonakala bevuka njenge-phoenix enkulu eluthuthwini nasemalangatheni emfazwe esaqhumisa ngenxa yenkohliso yabo. Kwaye iya kuba yimfazwe enkulu, kwaneyona inkulu kwiimfazwe ezintathu ezinjalo abazivuseleleyo nabazilawulayo; ukuze inamba ivuke ngokupheleleyo ngexesha layo namarhamncwa ayo amabi, anesikizi aya kuba nolawulo lwayo olufutshane, ingaphinde ilawule kwakhona.

Kuba imfihlelo yobugwenxa ibisebenza kuyo yonke iminyaka; apho iheneli elikhulu elihleli phezu kweentaba ezisixhenxe, kwanelo heneli likhulu elibomvu lokunyelisa elinqule ukumkanikazi wezulu nelifundisa omnye uYesu njengenxalenye kathathu wabo wobuhedeni. Kwanalo iingcambu zalo zimiliselwe eBhabheli yamandulo apho uNimrodi waqala iintsomi zakhe ngokumkanikazi wezulu. Owalifa lakhe nababingeleli neminombo yegazi balandelela umnombo wabo kubawileyo ababelawula iBhabheli yamandulo ngobuthathu babo obungcwele ababusebenzisa ukugculela ubuthixo bokwenene, nembewu ethenjisiweyo yomfazi. Ezi zithethe nemisitho yobuhedeni zilawule umhlaba ngasese kakhulu apho ababingeleli abaqalisiweyo babawileyo banqula ilanga ngasese kodwa bathi baNguMbutho kaYesu; kwaye ke icawa yabo yobuxoki nenyelisayo esebenzise ingcinezelo nentshutshiso phezu komhlaba ngeentsuku imfihlelo yobugwenxa yasasazeka ngasese. Ngoko ke, intshabalalo yabo ayilibazisi kwaye amaxesha abo afikile!

Noko ke la marhamncwa mabini akhohlakeleyo, kwanalawo ngoku asebenza kunye ukuze kutshatyalaliswe ngokupheleleyo iBhabheli eyimfihlo, kwanerhamncwa eliphuma elwandle nerhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni; kwanaba babini ababekade bezintloko ezilawulayo zeBhabheli Eyimfihlo nabalawule ngezithembiso ezininzi ezikhulu nezingenanto abathi ngazo aba balusi babini banxilileyo baluse iigusha ezilahlekileyo zeBhabheli Eyimfihlo bethembisa impumelelo yezinto eziphathekayo yobuxoki nobuninzi kwabo babalusayo. Kwaye ezandleni zabo nangamayelenqe abo ngokuqinisekileyo balungiselele kwaye baya kwenza oko kunikwe ezandleni zenamba ukuba ikufezekise.

Kuba iBhabheli Eyimfihlo, kwanelo heneli likhulu elihleli phezu kwamanzi amaninzi luhlanga olwenziwa ukuze la marhamncwa mabini akhohlakeleyo avuke eluthuthwini lwayo ngamaxesha okutyhilwa kwawo. Kwanalo uhlanga olukrexezayo, kwanalo uhlanga lohenyo, uhlanga ngokwenene oluliheneli elikhulu elizibanga lingumlandeli olilungisa kaYesu waseNazarete. Kodwa eneneni luhlanga abakhi balo bakhululekile kwiLiwa leSiseko lokwenene, kwanelo litye lembombo elikhulu abakhubeka kulo abangakholwayo, kwanaye uYesu waseNazarete okwanguYeshua HaMashiach. Kwaye ke aba bakhi bakhohlakeleyo bazikhulule ngokwabo elusindisweni kwaye bazinikela kwelo chibi lomlilo apho impethu yabo ingafi ngokwala ilitye lembombo eliyintloko neliwa lokukhubekisa kwaye abayi kuphuncuka!

Noko ke abawileyo bebesoloko beneenjongo ezifanayo engqondweni kwaye amacebo neenjongo zabo zihlala zifana. Kuba banenjongo yokumtshabalalisa ngokupheleleyo uAdam emhlabeni, kwaye banenjongo epheleleyo yokumtshabalalisa uYakobi phantsi kwelanga ukuze baqhubeke behleli njengabahlali abangemthethweni nabathimbi abangalunganga bomhlaba ukuze babe nexesha labo legolide abalinqwenelayo. Kodwa qondani ukuba ixesha labo legolide lalilixesha apho uAdam wayegutyungelwe kwaye elawulwa bubukhosi behlabathi lonke bamasikizi akhohlakeleyo awayenzelwe intshabalalo epheleleyo yoluntu, kwaye amasikizi abo aya kubuya! Kodwa abayi kuba nalo ixesha legolide kodwa kuphela amathemba aqhekekileyo nomgwebo ongqongqo njengoko ubukumkani babawileyo butshatyalaliswa ngokupheleleyo phambi kwamehlo abo ngesandla sam esinamandla!

Kwaye ixesha labo ekuthiwa lelegolide lalilixesha apho umnombo olungileyo kaSete wazizimeza entlango kuluntu lwabo olukhohlakeleyo kwihlabathi elalikhona ngoko. Ukuze umnombo wegazi likaSete uhlale unyulu kungcoliseko lwemfuza kwaye imbewu ethenjisiweyo yomfazi (endinguye), ifike njengoko kwathenjiswa kudala nguYahweh ngokwakhe. Yabonani! UYahweh ubhengeze ukuba Uya kuzisa iingxilimbela ukuze azalisekise ingqumbo yakhe, kwaye kuya kuba njalo. Noko ke, ubukhosi babo obubi buya kuvuka kwaye bulawule umhlaba wonke kwaye buya kuzisa ububi obuninzi, kwaye amasikizi amaninzi aya kukhululwa ngaphaya komkhenkce nasentliziyweni yomhlaba kwaye umwonyo uya kuvulwa.

Kuya kubakho ububi obukhulu emhlabeni kwaye nangona beya kuvuka ukuze bazise uxolo lobuxoki; imfazwe iya kuhlala ilawula ulawulo lwabo kuba uxolo luza kususwa emhlabeni njengomgwebo ekususweni komthinteli wam. Kwaye abayi kuba nalo uxolo kuba uYahweh wabhengeza kudala ukuba abawileyo abanalo uxolo kuba bonakalise uluntu kwiinkanuko zabo ezimbi nentiyo yazo zonke izinto ezenziwe nguYahweh. Kwaye baya kumisa uxolo lobuxoki elizweni kwaye irhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni liya kungena ngokupheleleyo kumandla alo, kwaye liya kulawula njengetakane elineempondo ezimbini elithetha njengenamba.

Kuba imvana ka-Esawu, kwanerhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni elikwanguBharabhas, likhethiwe njengomakhi wetempile yabo yoburhamncwa kwaye linikwe njengemvana yenamba kuMoshiach wabo wobuxoki nakumkani wabo wokuzenzisa. Inene, inene, uya kumanya u-Esawu no-Ashkenazi nabo bonke abazimanya nabo phantsi kwebhanile yonqulo olungaziwayo ngooyise, kwaye iindlela zabo zeTalmud ziya kulawula umhlaba. Baya kuzithoba ngenkohlakalo iintlanga phantsi kweNoahide kwaye baya kulawula iintlanga ngenkohlakalo enkulu; kwaye irhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni liya kubangela bonke, abancinci nabakhulu, abazizityebi nabangamahlwempu, abakhululekileyo nabangamakhoboka ukuba bamkele uphawu lwalo ezandleni zabo zokunene okanye emabunzini abo ukuze kungabikho mntu unokuthenga okanye athengise, ngaphandle kwalowo unophawu, okanye igama lerhamncwa, okanye inani legama lalo. Kuba lizisa ukulunywa yinyoka apho kungekho mntu unokuphila kuko kwaye bonke abathatha inxaxheba baya kuziguqula ngonaphakade, apho kungayi kubakho kuxolelwa kuloo nguqulelo, kwaye baya kuxuba isinyithi nodongwe, kodwa abayi kunamathelana. Oko kugwenxa akunakulungiswa kwaye bonke abazenza bagwenxe ngaloo ndlela baya kuba nesabelo sabo kwelo chibi likhulu lomlilo wesulfure wamadangatye angenakucinywa.

Ngoko ke irhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni elikwangumprofeti wabo wobuxoki noMoshiach wobuxoki liya kwenza izibhengezo zalo kwaye liya kwakha itempile yalo kuba lithunyelwe njengomakhi wetempile yoburhamncwa ozakhele iinqaba ezininzi kubukumkani bakhe ezinikezelwe kwigama lakhe, kwaye liya kuthetha ngaye ngokuzithanda okukhulu lide libhengeze ukuba lona ngokwalo linguThixo. Kuba liza kwenza izithembiso ezininzi ezikhulu elingenamandla okuzizalisekisa, kwaye liza kuthetha izinto ezininzi ezinkulu nezinyelisayo, kwaye liya kwenjenjalo kuba linikwe ukuba likwenze oko. Kuba lingumakhi nomphuhlisi ozenzele iinqaba ezininzi neendawo zokuchithela iiholide, kwaye ngenene lihleli phezu kobukumkani balo elizinikezele elubekweni lwalo; kuba lihlala lizidumisa kuba zinjalo iindlela zenamba enguYise walo wokwenene.

Njengoko itempile yalo yakhiwayo kwaye igqitywa phakathi kwemibhiyozo emikhulu nezithembiso ezininzi ezinyelisayo nezobuxoki, ngoko liya kwenza izibhengezo ezininzi zobuxoki esikhundleni salo njengomprofeti wobuxoki kwaye ulawulo lwalo luya kuba lolungqwabalala nolunenkani. Noko ke, liya kubeka umgibe walo kodwa nguwuphi umthiyeli obeka umgibe emehlweni entaka? Ngobuqhetseba obukhulu libeka umgibe walo kwaye ngesithembiso esikhulu liya kuthembisa ukunikezela itempile yalo kubantu belizwe kwaye ngovuyo olukhulu u-Esawu no-Ashkenazi baya kuzibhiyozela ngeenxa zonke kwimvana ka-Esawu, kwaye baya kwenza ngathi baphinda bamisa iindlela zeTorah, kodwa bazisa iindlela zokufa ezibhalwe encwadini yabo yokufa enyelisayo, kwaneyabo iTalmud. Yabonani! Irhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni lingumbulali nenkokeli yemvukelo evukela uYahweh ngeendlela zikaBharabhas. Liya kungcatsha bonke abo lenza kubo izithembiso zalo ezinkulu kwaye ukungcatsha kwalo kuya kuba ngaphandle kokuzisola kwaye ngentiyo enkulu eliyizuze njengelifa kuYise walo, umtyholi, elibotshelelwe bubugwenxa ukuba lizalisekise iinkanuko zakhe.

Noko ke, yileyo indalo yesigcawu esiluke umhlaba ngokupheleleyo ngomnatha waso. Isigcawu sihleli silindele ixhoba laso ukuba liwele emnatheni waso njengoko sisithi xhuma ukuze sibambe, sikhubaze kwaye sisongelane ixhoba laso ukuze sitye kancinci kubomi bamathinjwa aso; njengoko betyiswa kancinci ngokupheleleyo bububi ababanjiswe ngabo. Kanti izihlwele ziya kuza zivela kufuphi nakude ngesandi sokubiza kwaso; kwaye isimemo sokunikezelwa nokuhlanjululwa kwetempile yaso siya kuvakala kuwo wonke umhlaba kwaye abaninzi abala uMesiya wabo wokwenene baya kubanjwa emgibeni waso baze batshabalale ngaloo ndlela. Noko ke, umgibe awuyi kubonakala njengomgibe kwabo sibathakathileyo, kodwa uya kubonakala njengovuselelo lukaMoses olukhokelwa yiSanhedrin yabo kwaye lulawulwa zizithethe nobulumko buka-Esawu obuqulathwe kwincwadi yabo engcwele enyelisayo. Kwaye iqela lababingeleli bobuxoki abangesibo abakaLevi kuba uLevi ufunyanwa ngokupheleleyo kuMbingeleli oMkhulu kaMelkitsedeke, kuba mna, Yeshua HaMashiach, ndingumBingeleli wenu oMkhulu ngonaphakade ngokohlobo lukaMelkitsedeke, kuba wenjenjalo uYahweh ukubhengeza.

Noko ke, njengoko ndandifundisa inyaniso ngexesha lam xa ndandisiza kwabam njengeMvana kaThixo; xa ndaliwayo ngabantu bakuthi, noko ke ndayifundisa inyaniso ngokunjalo, ngoko ke, O' Sirayeli awunasizathu. Nangona, eluthandweni lwam ngani nakwizithembiso neminqophiso yakudala, ndiza kunixelela kwakhona ukuze uYakobi angaweli ku-Esawu.

Kuba kubhaliwe kwizibhalo ezingcwele: Xa nithe ngoko nalibona isikizi lesiphanziso, ekwakuthethwa ngalo nguDaniyeli umprofeti, limi endaweni engcwele, (lowo ulesayo makaqonde). Ngelo xesha abo baseYuda mababalekele ezintabeni. Lowo uphezu kophahla lwendlu, makangehli, aye kuthatha nto endlwini yakhe: Nalowo usentsimini, makangabuyeli umva aye kuthatha iingubo zakhe. Yeha ke kwabakhulelweyo, nakwabanyisayo ngaloo mihla. Kodwa thandazani ukuba ukubaleka kwenu kungabi sebusika, kungabi ngomhla weSabatha: Kuba ngelo xesha kuya kubakho imbandezelo enkulu, ekungazanga kubekho injalo kususela ekuqalekeni kwehlabathi kude kube ngoku, hayi, kwaye akusayi kuze kubekho.
