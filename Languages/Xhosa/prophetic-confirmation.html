Okthobha 28, 2024

Isiqinisekiso Esiprofetayo nguJ. Leland Earls

Yivani amazwi am, bantu bam; phulaphulani oko ndinityhilela kona ngeli lixa. Kuba andizi na kuni ngoku njengomthunywa onamandla ukuba ndityhile kuni oko bekufihliwe kwiiminyaka nezizukulwana? Kuba nangoku ndingathanda ukutyhilela abo baneendlebe zokuva oko ndikulungiselele umzimba wam kula maxesha okuphela. Nicinga ukuba ndiza kunishiya ningenalathiso nolwazi ngeenjongo zam ngani kula maxesha okuphela? Nicinga ukuba andinakukwazi ukukhetha abathunywa abakhethekileyo endiza kutyhila ngabo isigidimi sam nenjongo yam ngani ekuzalisekeni kwezinto zonke? Yivani amazwi am: Ndiyaphinda ndithi, phulaphulani oko ndikuthethayo kuni ngeli lixa. Kuba ngoku ndiphethe incwadana ezandleni zam, kwaye ndiyazisa iziqulatho zale ncwadi kubakhonzi bam abakhethwe ngokukodwa.

Ithini yona? Ithi lento. Bantu bam, ndiza kuni ngesigidimi sam senjongo yam yexesha lokuphela, eziya kuba kukuzaliseka kwako konke endinilungiselele kona kwiizukulwana ezininzi neminyaka. Kwaye ndiya kunenza ningene ekuzalisekeni koko ndikunilungiseleleyo, ewe, ukuzaliseka koko ndakugqibezelayo emnqamlezweni xa ndaphalaza igazi lobomi bam ngenxa yenu, ukuze ndiqinisekise uMnqophiso ongunaPhakade kuni, ewe, umnqophiso wokufezeka kubantu bam; umnqophiso oqulethe konke ukuzala kobuqaqawuli bam namandla am kwabo balungiselelwe ngokukodwa ukuba bamkele.

Kuba ndithi kuni, bambalwa, ewe, intsalela, eya kulungiselelwa ukwamkela esi sityhilelo senjongo yexesha lokuphela, endiyazisayo ngoku. Kuba sinamandla kakhulu ukuba singamkelwa ngaphandle liqela elilungisiweyo, ewe, "umhlambi omncinane," oya kwamkela ukuzala komnqophiso. Kuba niya kuhamba ngezi ntsuku zokugqibela ekuzaleni koko ndinilungiselele kona. Kuba nangoku ndiyaninika isityhilelo soko kwatywinwayo, kwaqinisekiswa, kwenziwa kwafumaneka kwaye kwalunga xa igazi lobomi bam laliqukuqela eKalvari. Kuba akubhalwanga na ukuba ndathetha amazwi athi, "Kugqityiwe"? Kwagqitywa ntoni? Ukuzala komnqophiso wam kwagqitywa; kwenziwa ngokupheleleyo ngoko ukuhlangulwa okupheleleyo kuwo onke amandla otshaba, nokungena okupheleleyo kwako konke endikulungeleyo nendinako ukukunika, kuquka nokupheliswa kokufa. Ewe, amandla apheleleyo okoyisa yonke imisebenzi yotshaba, nobuqaqawuli obupheleleyo obulungiswe kwasekusekweni kwehlabathi kwabo bakulungeleyo ukwamkela nokungena.

Kodwa ngoku akubhalwanga na nokuba bamlinganisela oyiNgcwele kaSirayeli? Bantwana bam, NDINGUYE oyiNgcwele kaSirayeli! Kodwa abantu bandilinganisele. Bazilukhuni iintliziyo zabo bakholwa bubuxoki baza ngaloo ndlela basilela ukuxhamla ngokupheleleyo konke endikulungisileyo ndakutywina, ndakuqinisekisa, emnqamlezweni waseKalvari. Ewe, ndithi kuni zonke izinto zazilungile ngoko. Kodwa ndandingabafumana phi abantu ababelungile—ababekulungele ukukholwa ngokupheleleyo? Ewe, akufunyanwanga namnye, ngaphandle kwabanjengoYoshuwa noKalebhi. Lowo uneendlebe, makeve! Kuba nalapha ndiyabona ukuba iindlebe zenu zinzima, kwaye andinakukwazi ukunityhilela ngokupheleleyo ngeli xesha ukuba kuthetha ntoni oku, kodwa niya kuqonda kungekabi ntsuku zininzi ezidlulileyo.

Kodwa ngoku ndiza kutyhila kangangoko nikulungeleyo ukwamkela. Kuba le ncwadana iqulethe okuninzi ngale mini neli xesha. Kodwa kufuneka ndiyityhile kancinci kancinci, kuba nangoku abaninzi benu banjengeentsana ezisandul' ukulumliswa ebeleni kwaye anikalungeli inyama. Abanye baya kuqonda ngakumbi koko ndikuthethayo. Ngoko ke mandinilungiselele ndize ndizaluse iintliziyo zenu ngokupheleleyo ukuze namkele esi sigidimi sexesha lokuphela. Kuba lowo unikwe okuninzi, kufunwa okuninzi kuye. Ningathanda ukwazi iziqulatho zale ncwadana? Ke lungisani iintliziyo zenu ukuze namkele. Kuba iya kutyhilwa kuni kancinci kancinci njengoko nikulungele ukwamkela.

Kuba nangoku ndilungisa abantu abaya kulidla ilifa ngokupheleleyo ilizwe elilungisiweyo—abaya kuwela amaza eYordan—abaya kuhleka imikhosi yenamba—ewe, yona ekuza kwayo kunemiqondiso nezimanga zobuxoki nokusebenza kwayo kunjengamanzi agalelayo eYordan. Cingani ngale nto, bantu bam! Kuba akubhalwanga na ukuba inamba iya kuza njengomkhukula onamandla wokufa nentshabalalo? Kwaye ayiyi kuza na loo namba inye ngokuchasene nabantu bam inomkhukula wobuxoki neenkohliso ngale mini?

Kodwa aniyi koyiswa, kuba ndiya kwenza amanzi adame ngelixa niwela ngesandla sam esinamandla ukuze nilidle ilifa ilizwe enililungiselweyo. Kuba ngenene, lilizwe elihle eli. Kwaye abantu bam sele bebhadule ixesha elide ngokwaneleyo kwintlango yokuphelelwa ngumoya. Ndithi kuni 'iminyaka engamashumi amane yovavanyo' igqityiwe, kwaye okwesibini abantu bam beza kwiLizwe lesiThembiso. Nangoku, ndiyanilungiselela ukuwela ngamandla omoya. Phulaphulani nize nicinge ngoko ndikuthethayo kuni. Kuba le ncwadana ndiyiphethe ezandleni zam iqulethe zonke iimfihlelo neemfihlakalo endinqwenela ukuba nizazi ngezi ntsuku.

Owu bantu bam, andilindanga na ixesha elide ngolangazelelo ngeli lixa ukuze ndinityhilele oko bekufihliwe? Asilolixa eli yonke imbali ebililindele? Asikuko na oku kwathethwa ngabaprofeti, nangona bona ngokwabo babengakuqondi? Owu bantu bam, nikhe nacinga na ngembeko nozuko enilunikiweyo lokuphila ngeli lixa nokuthatha inxaxheba kolu lungiselelo lwexesha lokuphela? Owu, akwaba ningavuswa ekutyhafisweni kwenu ukuze nibone ubuqaqawuli obulungiselelwe eli lixa. Kutheni nisithi entliziyweni yenu ixesha alikafiki? Kutheni nilinda ningangeni ngentliziyo yenu yonke nomphefumlo wenu wonke? Ndithi kuni, NGOKU LIXESHA LOKUNDIFUNA NGENTLIZIYO YENU YONKE, ukuze ndithethe nani kwigumbi elingaphakathi lentliziyo yenu ndize ndinazise icebo lam ngeli lixa. Musani ukuphulaphula abo bangafuna ukunityhafisa baze banenze iimfama koko ndikuthethayo.

Baphi na abo banesibindi SOKUPHUMA baye phezu kwamanzi obunzima novavanyo BAZE BAHAMBE BEZA KUM NGOKHOLO, besazi ukuba ndinako ukunixhasa nokuniphakamisa ngeli lixa nangonaphakade, ukuze nihambe ngoloyiso phezu kwamanzi embandezelo nesiphithiphithi, eziya kuvavanya bonke abantu ngale mini? Hambani, ndithi, nigxininise amehlo enu kum, ningajongi ngasekunene nangasekhohlo; ningajongi kwabo baningqongileyo okanye kumanzi anomsindo anganqwenela ukuniginya.

Kuba ndithi kuni anisoze nifikelele kubizo novuko lwexesha lokuphela de nibe nikulungele ngokholo ukuphuma esikhepheni sokukhululeka nokhuseleko lwenu lwangoku ningene KUMSINGA WENJONGO YAM YOBUThixo, ningavumeli amaza okuthandabuza noloyiko anoyise, ningavumeli nabo bakhetha ukuhlala esikhepheni banithintele. Kodwa YIZANI BANTU BAM, kuba ndiya kuba nabo banesibindi sokuhamba phezu kwamanzi koko kungazange kwenziwe ngaphambili, baze badibane nam njengoko ndisiza kuni ngamandla nobuqaqawuli ngale mini.

Abazange babone na ukuba yayingumzimba ohamba phezu kwamanzi hayi umoya njengoko babecinga? Kwaye uPetros, emzimbeni wakhe, akazange ahambe na phezu kwamanzi? Kanjalo, bantu bam, ayingomoya kuphela kodwa NGUMZIMBA WENYAMA oya kuziswa kum ngale mini ukuze wenze izenzo ezimangalisayo phezu kwamanzi okudideka nosizi lwabantu. Aninguye na UMZIMBA kaKristu; aniyonyama yam namathambo am na? Ngaloo ndlela niya kuba mnye nam ngale mini; ngaloo ndlela siya kudityaniswa kunye PHEZU kwamanzi ukuze siqhubeke ngale mini, itsho iNkosi. Kwaye niya kuba njalo ngeentsuku ezizayo, itsho iNkosi, UMZIMBA WABOYISI—abo boyise konke ukungcola kweemvakalelo kwamanzi anzongonzongo otshaba nabahamba ngoloyiso ngamandla okoyisa.

Bantu bam, yivani amazwi am! Iiasidi esiswini azingumfuziselo na wemisebenzi yam phakathi kwenu? Ningafumana isondlo kwinto engeyosonka nakwinto enganelisiyo na? Ndithi kuni, ukusebenza koMoya wam kuyimfuneko ukuze namkele oko kunika ubomi. Kuba andinakukwazi na ukuthatha oko kubalulekileyo eLizwini lam ndikwenze kufakwe kubuntu benu ukuze nifumane eso sondlo nisifunayo? Kodwa kutheni nisitya oko kungelilo ilizwi lam? Kutheni nifuna oko niyaziyo ukuba akuyi kwanelisa ingaphakathi lenu?

Ndithi kuni, ukuba aniyi kundifuna ngokupheleleyo nize nivumele uMoya wam unike amandla oko nikutya emizimbeni yenu, niya kuzifumana nikwimeko yomoya efana nale ichazwe apha. Ukusebenza komoya wam kuyimfuneko. Kungenjalo, nithatha kuphela oko kufileyo kwaye okungenakwenza nto ukuzisa ubomi bomoya kwingaphakathi lenu. Ngoko ke, phulaphulani oko nikufundayo, kuba kuyinyani kumzekelo woko kwenzekayo kubantu bam namhlanje. Kuba akunakwenzeka, kwimeko yangoku yelizwe lecawe, ukuba ndisebenze ngoMoya wam ngendlela yokuba iLizwi lam linike ubomi nempilo nenkululeko kubo bonke abasweleyo. Ngoko ke, ndiyaphinda ndithi, ndifuneni nize ndininike oko kuyimfuneko ukuze niphile ubomi bomoya obunamandla nobuzalisekileyo, itsho iNkosi uYesu Kristu.

Amagqabantshintshi nguWilliam Brooks:

Kabini ngoku, ndiye ndazama ukunxibelelana nomgcini wale webhusayithi ogcina imisebenzi kaMnu. Earls kodwa andiphumelelanga, ngoko ke ngoku ndinokucinga kuphela ukuba akazukuphendula kwakhona. Ngaphandle koko, andinanto yakuthetha ngabantu abathathe uxanduva lokugcina imisebenzi yakhe, kwaye oku akubonakalisi nto ngemisebenzi yobuprofeti awayenzayo uMnu. Earls namazwi akhe asamileyo nanamhlanje. INkosi ingumgwebi wethu sonke, ngoko ke simi okanye siwe phambi koKristu, kwaye uKristu unako ukusimisa!

Nangona kunjalo, ukucacisa, iwebhusayithi endiyidibanisileyo ineendlela ezininzi zokufundisa nezikhundla endingabelani nazo okanye endingazixhasiyo, kwaye andivumelani neBhayibhile kwezinye zezinto ezibhaliweyo. Uninzi lwezi zinto ndizikhankanyayo azibhalwanga nguMnu. Earls, kodwa nokuba ngubani obhala ntoni, zifundisise ukuze uzinikele kuThixo ungumsebenzi ovunyiweyo, ongenazintloni, owahlula ilizwi lenyaniso ngokufanelekileyo.

Kwaye ndiza kuthatha eli thuba ndiphinde nditsho. Nantoni na ethethwa ngumntu xa esithi uthetha noThixo, ngobuprofeti okanye ngenye indlela, thatha loo mazwi uye nawo esibhalweni kwaye ngakumbi eNkosini ukuze ufumane isiqinisekiso nokuqonda. Akuncedi nto ukundibuza ukuba semthethweni komnye umprofeti okanye omnye. Andiyi kuphendula kuba andiyi kubanjiswa kumgibe wokuthetha kakubi ngomprofeti wokwenene kaThixo, kwaye andinalo nexesha lokujikisa ingqondo yam ukuze ndingqine imisebenzi yomnye umntu. Singobani thina ukuba sigwebe umkhonzi womnye umntu? Bemi okanye bewa phambi kwenkosi yabo, kwaye ke sikhonza iNkosi yethu uYesu Kristu. Ngoko ke, yiNkosi ekufuneka iqinisekise okanye ikhanyele ukuba semthethweni kwakhe nabani na othi uthetha egameni leNkosi. Ukuba iNkosi ikhetha ukuthetha ngomntu othile, ndiyakuthembisa ukuba banobudlelwane obuqinileyo noKristu, kwaye wazi ngakumbi ngabo kunokuba bezazi bona. Yise eNkosini!
