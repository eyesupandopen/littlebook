Ingqina eliMbaxa: uSirayeli
Okukrakra Okumnandi
Okthobha 29, 2024

Lifikile Ixesha Lokuzaliseka: Ewe noAmen

Kusuka kuYeshua HaMashiach:

Lowo uMoses awayalela uSirayeli ukuba amphulaphule aze amthobele.

Thobani iindlebe zenu nive amazwi kaYeshua HaMashiach kweli xesha lokugqibela, nithobe iintliziyo zenu ngokuthobela nize nivumele iintliziyo zenu zamkele imfundiso yam. Kuba amazwi am abubomi kwabo bawafumanayo, kwaye azisa impilo nesithembiso, nakumaxesha enkxwaleko enkulu. Ngoko ke qondani ukuba inkxwaleko enkulu ifikile kwaye intlekele iyasondela, kwaye amaxesha ngoku. Inene, inene, yinyaniso engunaphakade ukuba ndim, uYeshua HaMashiach, kwanjengeNgonyama yeSizwe sakwaYuda ezayo kugweba nokulwa, kwaye qondani ukuba akukho nto ifihliweyo emehlweni am, kwaye akukho nto ifihliweyo emehlweni kaYahweh kwaye ndiyayibona intswela-bulungisa nembubhiso eyenziwa kubantu belizwe ngabahlaseli abavela kwiNdlu yesiNtu kaSathana kwaye ndiya kubuyisela itsho iNkosi eLilungisa!

Kuba ngenene, kangangeminyaka emininzi yovuyo abawileyo baye baqhubela phambili imfihlelo yabo yobugwenxa, kude kube ngoku apho amayelenqe abo akulungele ukuzaliseka ngokupheleleyo. Kodwa qondani ukuba bavunyelwe kuphela ukuzalisekisa iinjongo zikaYahweh kwizicwangciso zakhe ezingunaphakade nezingapheliyo zosindiso ukuze uluntu lukhululwe ngokupheleleyo. Kuba umntu wemvelo uye walaliswa ngobuxoki obukhulu nokuzanelisa okukhulu.

Kwaye ke abawileyo basebenzisa uluntu ukuqhubela phambili intshabalalo yoluntu ngelixa bebiza ukukhanya ngokuba bubumnyama nobumnyama ngokuba kukukhanya. Kwaye kunjalo ke nakwiNdlu yesiNtu kaSathana abahlasela imihlaba yesithembiso kwaye ndiya kubancothula ngokupheleleyo. Kuba iNdlu yesiNtu kaSathana ifuna ukubanga izithembiso zikaYahweh ezibhaliweyo neziqinisekisiweyo kooBawo bakudala. INdlu yesiNtu kaSathana izele lukhula olubi olutyalwe ngabawileyo phakathi kwengqolowa yam kwaye amaxesha okuncothulwa kwalo afikile kwaye kubhaliwe kunjalo kwaye kuya kwenziwa njalo: Kuba, yabonani, iyeza imini etshisa njengeziko, nabo bonke abanekratshi, ewe, nabo bonke abenza okubi, baya kuba ziindiza, kwaye imini ezayo, iya kubatshisa, itsho iNkosi yemikhosi, kwaye ayiyi kubashiya nengcambu nasebe.

Noko ke, kuye kwafezekiswa ngabawileyo ukuba isigcawu siye saluka ubulembu baso kwihlabathi lonke ngoku, kwaye ngenkohliso bayalwa. Kwaye ngoku lixesha lokuvuka kwabo kwaye ulawulo lwabo olufutshane olungangcwele lusemnyango. Kuba uYahweh ebulungiseni bakhe uya kunika umntu wemvelo kanye oko akunqwenela ngobuyatha ekusukeleni kwakhe iziyolo zesono. Kwaye ke ubumnyama nobubi obunqwenelwa ngumntu wemvelo uya kubufumana ngoku. Noko ke, ukuba iVangeli yam ifihliwe, ifihlelwe abo balahlekileyo. Kubo uthixo weli hlabathi uye wamfamekisa iingqondo zabo bangakholwayo, ukuze ukukhanya kweVangeli ezukileyo kaKristu, ongumfanekiso kaThixo, kungakhanyisi kubo. Kwaye ke umntu wemvelo ekungazini kwakhe uye wazibandakanya nesigcawu kwinkohliso yaso, kuba ngenkohliso bayalwa.

Ngoko ke, u-El Shaddai, ubathumela inkohliso enamandla ukuze bakholwe bubuxoki obenziwe ngabawileyo, ukuze bonke bagwetywe abangayikholwanga inyaniso, kodwa bayoliswa kukungabikho kokusesikweni. Noko ke, kwisigwebo sikaYahweh nenceba yakhe engapheliyo, iziyolo ziya kususwa ngoku emhlabeni kwaye izinto ezimnandi umntu wemvelo azinqwenela ngamandla ukuziqwebela ziya kususwa emhlabeni. Kwaye ke neziyolo zesono ziya kususwa emhlabeni, kangangokuba abantu baya kuphambana njengoko befuna ukwanelisa iinkanuko zeentliziyo zabo. Noko ke, uYahweh akazikhethi ezi zenzo ukuze abe nenkohlakalo kwindalo yakhe kodwa kukohlwaya abenzi bobubi nokuvusa abo, ngenye indlela, bebeya kwala ukuvuka ebuthongweni babo kuba ngenxa yeziyolo zesono nomvuzo wokungabikho kokusesikweni baye batyetyiswa kwaye bamfanyekiswa. Endaweni yesigwebo esipheleleyo nesikhawulezileyo umntu wemvelo unikwa ubabalo kuba umntu wemvelo woyisiwe ngabawileyo.

Ewe, kubabalo nenceba kaYahweh nakwisicwangciso sakhe esingunaphakade uYahweh ufuna ukuvusa umntu wemvelo esonweni nokubiza umntu wemvelo ebulungiseni. Olubizo abaninzi baye basabela ngokuzithandela kusindiso lwam ngamaxesha am obabalo ngendlela yeVangeli yoSindiso. Ngawo la maxesha, usindiso lwalusisipho sasimahla apho umntu wayenokundenza iNkosi aze abe nexesha lokusebenzela usindiso lwakhe ngoloyiko nangokungcangcazela njengoko esiza kum ngokupheleleyo kwanokuphuma ngokupheleleyo esonweni. Kwaye ke kunjalo ukuba amaxesha am obabalo afikelele esiphelweni, nangona ubabalo nenceba zihlala zinam. Kwaye kuya kwenzeka kungekudala ukuba amaxesha okulinda angabikho kwaye ubukumkani beli hlabathi bube bubukumkani beNkosi yenu, noKristu wenu, kwaye ndiya kulawula ngonaphakade nanaphakade nabathembekileyo kwilifa lethu elingunaphakade endiya kwabelana ngalo ngokupheleleyo naboyisi bam.

Kwaye isihlwele esikhulu siya kuza kum ngexesha lenkxwaleko kaYakobi ukuze sibasindise ngonaphakade. Kwaye bonke abanqula igama leNkosi kude kube sekupheleni baya kusindiswa, kungakhathaliseki ulwimi okanye isizwe ababiza kuso. Kwaye ndiyashukuma ukusindisa uSirayeli, kwaye ndibiza izalamane zam zokwenyani ezilahlekiswe ziinkolo zobuxoki kwaye zacinezelwa yiNdlu yesiNtu kaSathana, kwaye baya kufumana usindiso olungunaphakade kwaye ndiya kumsindisa uYakobi olusindiso lwakhe luya kufezekiswa ngomlilo wocikido. Kodwa qondani ukuba amaxesha obomi obude nempumelelo yezinto eziphathekayo emhlabeni afikelele esiphelweni de uBukumkani bam bumiselwe kungekudala, kwaye umhlaba ubanjwe ngamatyathanga ulindele izigwebo zawo zokugqibela ukuba zithululwe ngomsindo omkhulu. Yabonani! Umsindo weMvana uza kuqala, kwaye iinkathazo ezinkulu ziya kugubungela umhlaba njengonogumbe wentshabalalo epheleleyo, kwaye abaninzi baya kutshabalala.

Noko ke, iinkathazo ezinkulu neenkxwaleko ziya kukhululwa xa amatywina evulwa nommsindo omkhulu, nezibetho ezininzi nesifo esingubhubhani, nendlala, nembalela, nemfazwe, nentswela-bulungisa, nokufa nokunye okuninzi kuya kuza ngokupheleleyo emhlabeni; noko ke isihlwele sezihlwele siya kuvuka kwiindlela zaso zokudikidiki. Kwaye baya kuba buhlungu kakhulu kwaye babe sentlungwini yomphefumlo xa beqonda inyaniso engenakuphikiswa yesibhalo nendlela abaphose ngayo ngenkani ithuba labo losindiso nokubaleka umsindo nesigwebo ekuhlanganisweni kweBandla lam elithembekileyo kum. Noko ke kwiinkxwaleko zabo ezibuhlungu nasentlungwini yabo ndiya kubaphefumlela isigqibo esingenakoyiswa ngaphakathi kwabo kwaye ndiya kubatsalela kum ngokupheleleyo, kwanabo bonke abeza kum besentlungwini nasenkxwalekweni nangezilingo zokufela ukholo. Kwaye baya kuba nomvuzo omkhulu, umvuzo omkhulu kangangokuba iinkathazo zabo ezizayo azifanelekanga ukuthelekiswa nozuko olungunaphakade oluza kutyhilwa ngokupheleleyo kubo.

Kunjalo ke kubhaliwe kwaye kuya kugqitywa ngokupheleleyo: Aba ngabo baphuma kwimbandezelo enkulu, kwaye bahlambe iingubo zabo, bazenza mhlophe egazini leMvana. Ngoko ke, baphambi kwetrone kaThixo, kwaye bamkhonza imini nobusuku eTempileni yakhe: kwaye lowo uhleli eTroneni uya kuhlala phakathi kwabo. Abayi kuphinda balambe, abayi kuphinda banxanwe, nelanga aliyi kukhanya phezu kwabo, nabuphi na ubushushu. Kuba iMvana, ephakathi kwetrone, iya kubondla, kwaye iya kubakhokelela kwimithombo ephilayo yamanzi: kwaye uThixo uya kosula zonke iinyembezi emehlweni abo.

Kwaye ke isigcawu siye saluka ubulembu baso kwihlabathi lonke ngenkohliso enkulu, ukunyanzelisa, ukuphanga kwaye ngeendlela ezininzi ityhefu yesigcawu isebenza ububi bayo ngeendlela zayo ezikhohlisayo nezinetyhefu. Kuba siluke ubulembu benkohliso kwaye siluke ubulembu betyala kangangokuba ihlabathi ngoku lilukwe ngokupheleleyo ekwangiwe yisigcawu kwaye likhubazekile yityhefu yaso. Isigcawu sihambe ngobuqhetseba obukhulu nenkohliso ngemfihlelo yobugwenxa, kwaye umhlaba ngoku usebulembwini baso ngokupheleleyo. Kwaye ngenkohliso enkulu aba bazenzisi (abenziwe ngabawileyo) baye bahlasela imihlaba njengeenkumbi. Kwanangendlela efanayo isigcawu esigquma ngayo amaqanda aso de isihlwele samantshontsho aso sigqobhoze siphume size sikhase siye kuluka ubulembu baso ecaleni kwaso. Kwaye ke indlu yesintu kaSathana iye yalithatha ihlabathi ngamandla nangobuqhetseba.

Kwaye ekuhambeni kwexesha nangeendlela ezininzi namayelenqe baye bazenza banamandla, kuba banikwe njalo nguYahweh kwizicwangciso zakhe ezingunaphakade zosindiso ukuze bazifezekise. Kuba umntu wemvelo umfanyekiswe bubuxoki babo kwaye ubalandela ngokungazi ukuya ekufeni kwakhe. Ezicwangciso ndithetha ngazo ziya kuphelela ekwahlukaneni kokugqibela phakathi kwengqolowa nokhula naphakathi kwabazinikele ngokupheleleyo nabo bathanda izono zabo kwaye bakhetha amadangatye ngokuzithandela kuthando lwabo olumdaka lokuzitshabalalisa, kwaye baya kuzisola ngonaphakade ngokukhetha kwabo ukuzilungelelanisa neenjongo zesigcawu. Kwaye ke izitya zengqumbo yomsindo kaThixo ziya kuthululwa ngokupheleleyo.

Qondani ukuba isigcawu yiNdlu yesiNtu kaSathana esele ilungele ukulawula umhlaba kwinkqubo yabo yokugqibela yerhamncwa endiya kuyiphelisa ngonaphakade kwaye intshabalalo nesigwebo sayo asilibazisi. Qondani, zithandwa zam, ukuba iNdlu yesiNtu kaSathana bonke bangabazenzisi abazenzisayo. Balukhula lwama-Edom olukhokho babo ndajongana nabo njengemvana kaThixo njengoko ndazinikela ngokuzithandela ukuhlawulela izono zoluntu. Kwaye ke ndabaveza, kwaye ndabaxelela ngqo ebusweni babo obubi: Nina ningaboyihlo umtyholi, kwaye iinkanuko zoyihlo niya kuzenza: ebesoloko engumbulali kwasekuqaleni, kwaye akahlalanga enyanisweni, kuba akukho nyaniso kuye. Xa ethetha ubuxoki, uthetha okwakhe: kuba ulixoki, noyise wako.

Kwaye ke ukusukela kuhlaselo lwabo lokuqala lomhlaba wesithembiso ongekho wabo ukuba bawubange, baye batshutshisa, bathuthumbisa, kwaye benza imbubhiso epheleleyo kubemi abafanelekileyo bemihlaba yesithembiso. Benza ngathi ziindlalifa ezifanelekileyo zosindiso, bade basebenzise kakubi iinxalenye zomthetho kaMoses kwinkolo yabo yobuxoki yeTalmud. Kwaye ngeendlela ezinjalo baye bamfamekisa abaninzi abacinga ukuba bayayithobela intando yam becinga ukuba ndiya kumsikelela nabani na osikelela olu khula kwaye ndiya kumqalekisa nabani na oqalekisa olu khula. Kwaye ke abaninzi babantu bam bazimanya ngokungazi neNdlu yesiNtu kaSathana ngokuvuma kwaye ngokuqinisekileyo ndiyaniyalela ukuba niguquke ngokupheleleyo kubuxoki babo enibukhethele inkohliso yenu.

Bantwana bam qondani ukuba mna, Kristu Yesu, andizange ndibhale naziphi na iiBhayibhile zokufunda kodwa ezi zisetyenziswa njengezixhobo zenkohliso ngobuxoki obunikezelwe emacaleni ukutolika inyaniso. Kwaye abawileyo baye baguqula amazwi am kwiiBhayibhile zobuxoki, ngoko ke, khangelani kumgca wemibhalo yaseByzantine, kwanakwiTextus Receptus apho ndagcina khona amazwi am. Ngoko ke, yizani kum zithandwa zam nifunde ngam kwaye ndiya kunisa ngokupheleleyo enyanisweni yeenkohliso zabo zangoku ukuba niya kuthobela intliziyo yenu kwilizwi lam elinikwe ngabo bathetha endaweni yam.

Qondani ukuba ndim, uYesu waseNazarete, owajongana nolu hlobo lunye lwabazenzisi ngenxa yezithethe zabo zomlomo ababeziphakamise ngaphezu kwamazwi acocekileyo kaYahweh awanika uMoses. Kuba uMthetho kaMoses ungcwele, kwaye ulungile, kwaye uthe tye ngonaphakade kwaye ukuba nawuphi na umntu uqalekisa uMthetho kaMoses ke loo mntu uya kuqalekiswa naye. Kuba mna, uYesu waseNazarete, ndawufezekisa uMthetho kaMoses njengoko ndanikezela ngegazi lam elingenasono eKalvari ngenxa yezono zika-Adam (nayo yonke inzala yakhe) kwaye uMthetho kaMoses ugcinwa ngokupheleleyo zizithandwa zam ezihamba ngokholo kum, Kristu Yesu, iNkosi yabo enyukileyo nengunaphakade.

Ngoko ke qondani ukuba akunakwenzeka ukwaphula uMthetho kaMoses xa umntu ehamba ngokholo njengoko ndimkhokela ngomoya wam endiwudalileyo ngaphakathi kwakhe ngonaphakade. Nangona kunjalo, nabani na ohamba enyameni uyawaphula kuba uMoses weza kugweba isono enyameni ngokunika loo mthetho ungunaphakade wovuyo obhalwe ngomnwe kaYahweh ngokwakhe. Kwaye akukho nyama ilungileyo emehlweni am kwaye nakwamehlo kaThixo ongunaphakade wobulungisa. Ngoko ke, vumani izono zenu neentsilelo zenu kum, imvana kaThixo engumbingeleli wenu omkhulu ongunaphakade ngokommiselo kaMelkitsedeke kwaye ndiya kunihlambulula ngokupheleleyo egazini lam lokucamagushela elaphalazelwa ukuhlanjululwa kwabo bonke abeza kum ukuze basindiswe.

Ngubani na owakha weva into enjalo? Ngubani na owakha wabona izinto ezinjalo? Ngaba umhlaba uya kuveliswa ngosuku olunye? Okanye ngaba isizwe siya kuzalwa ngaxeshanye? Noko ke kunjalo ukuba isizwe sakwaSirayeli sabuyela ebukhweni njengesizwe ngesibhengezo sokhula, kwaye yayinguThixo ngokwakhe owavumela iintshaba zakhe ukuba zenze ngathi azazi kwinkohliso yazo ukuba zinguYakobi hayi u-Esawu, kuba yayingu-Esawu owathengisa ilungelo lakhe lokuzalwa komncinci. Kwaye ke isigcawu ngokungazi nangekratshi sicinga ukuba siya kuzisa ixesha legolide elingunaphakade emhlabeni kuba zinjalo iinkanuko zooYise abaya kuzenza kwaye abawileyo ngooYise babo kwanaboSathana abanemvelo yabo.

Noko ke baye bahlasela imihlaba ngogonyamelo olukhulu kuba banikwe njalo ukuba bakwenze ukuvusa abo baleleyo, kwanabo bonke abakubumfama obuyinxalenye abaya kusabela kubizo lukaMesiya wabo, kwanakuYeshua uMesiya wokwenene. Kwaye qondani ukuba ndiyayibona yonke isenzo esibi abasenzayo, kwaye ndiyayibona yonke intswela-bulungisa abayenzayo kwabamsulwa, kwaye ndiya kubuyisela itsho iNkosi engunaphakade uYahweh ayenze uMesiya, uKumkani, uMbingeleli oMkhulu, neNkosi! Sonke isenzo esibi sibhalwe ngokugqibeleleyo emazulwini, kwaye baya kubuyekezwa ngonaphakade nangokupheleleyo kwilifa labo elingunaphakade kumadangatye ashushu kakhulu e-cobalt blue kubumnyama bobumnyama ngonaphakade.

Kwaye ndiyambona uRakeli elilela abantwana bakhe njengoko bebulawa ngokungenabulungisa kwaye becinezelwa yiNdlu yesiNtu kaSathana kwaye ndiya kubuyisela, itsho iNkosi yeeNkosi noKumkani wooKumkani kwaye ndiya kusindisa intsalela kaSirayeli ephilayo kwiveki yamashumi asixhenxe kaDaniyeli, kwanenkxwaleko kaYakobi. Kwisicwangciso sikaYahweh esingunaphakade, uyaqonda ukuba abaninzi abayi kuze bemke baye ebulungiseni kwiziyolo neenkohliso zesono. Ngoko ke endaweni yokuzisa isigwebo esikhawulezileyo, uYahweh endaweni yoko ukhethe ukuzisa isigwebo phezu kwesigwebo ukuze iziganeko ziqokelelane kwaye zibe mbi ngakumbi. Kwaye isigwebo siya kuqokelelana ngolo hlobo bonke abo ekugqibeleni baya kukhetha ukubiza kum kwinkxwaleko yabo nasentlekele yabo kwanabo ndiya kubasindisa ngokupheleleyo, kwaye baya kukhokelelwa ekhuselekile.

Kwaye kunjalo ukuba iinjongo zabo ngoku ziya kutyhilwa. Kuba ndiya kukhokelela abam abayi-144,000 ukuba behlele phezu kweNtaba yeZiyon, kwaye baya kundilandela inyathelo ngenyathelo njengoko bekhokelela intsalela yam kaYakobi kukhuseleko entlango apho indawo ilungiswe nguThixo ukuze bakhuseleke, kwaye baya kondliwa banyanyekelwe kwaye inamba, irhamncwa, nomprofeti wobuxoki abayi kubachukumisa, bengayi kubafumana. Kodwa umprofeti wobuxoki uya kuzisa isikizi lesiphanziso kwaye ukhula oluninzi luya kucocwa ezweni, kwaye ndiya kuyifihla intsalela yam kude nedabi laloo mini inkulu, ewe, imini yokuza kwam kwesibini apho uYahweh wayiphawulayo kumaphakade adlulileyo kwaye imiselwe ngonaphakade kwishedyuli yakhe yosindiso kwaye loo mini ngokuqinisekileyo iyasondela kuba kubhaliwe.

Ndabona izulu livulekile, nalo ihashe elimhlophe; lowo wayekhwele phezu kwalo kuthiwa nguThembekileyo noNyaniso, yaye egweba enze imfazwe ngobulungisa. Amehlo akhe ayenjengedangatye lomlilo, nasentlokweni yakhe kwakukho izithsaba ezininzi; wayenegama elibhaliweyo, elingaziwayo mntu ngaphandle kwakhe. Wayambethe ingubo etshizwe ngegazi, negama lakhe kuthiwa, NguLizwi likaThixo. Nemikhosi eyayisezulwini yamlandela ikhwele amahashe amhlophe, yambethe ilinen ecikizekileyo, emhlophe necocekileyo. Emlonyeni wakhe kuphuma ikrele elibukhali, ukuze ngalo abethe iintlanga; aze azilawule ngentonga yentsimbi; unyathela isixovulelo sewayini yomsindo nengqumbo kaThixo uSomandla. Unalo negama elibhaliweyo engutyeni yakhe, nasethangeni lakhe, elithi, Kumkani Wookumkani, NeNkosi Yeenkosi.

Kuya kwenzeka ke ukuba ndize kuthatha ilifa lam ngamandla ezandleni zabahluthi nabahleli ngokungekho mthethweni abexesha labo lifikelele esiphelweni esingunaphakade, kuba ubukumkani bukaSathana abusayi kuphinda buvuke ngokufanayo. Kwaye ndiya kuwucoca ndiwuhlanze umhlaba kuko konke ukungalungisi, kwaye uSathana uya kubotshwa iminyaka eliwaka ekuthuthunjisweni nasekoyikeni okukhulu kwelifa lakhe elisondelayo elingunaphakade kuloo mlilo wesulfure ovuthayo otshisa awona malangatye ashushu esohlwayo.

Kwanalawo malangatye adalelwe ukubuyekeza isenzo ngasinye esikhohlakeleyo abakha basenza ukusukela kumzuzu abadalwa ngawo bakhetha ukungalungisi. Kwaye isono ngasinye seziwileyo siya kubuyiselwa ngokupheleleyo kumntu ngamnye kubo, naso sonke isono abasonileyo ngenkohlakalo enkulu nxamnye noYahweh ngokwakhe nendalo yakhe abayonakalisileyo. Kwaye kunjalo njengoko bebotshiwe kwaye bephoswa bephila kweyona mililo ishushu neyona inyulu yesohlwayo apho imililo iya kutshisa eyona blue ishushu kunye nemibala emhlophe qhwa yobunyulu. Kwaye kuloo malangatye esohlwayo baya kubuyekezwa ngokupheleleyo kuzo zonke izenzo zabo ezingenaxabiso nezicekisekayo eziya kunikwa ngokupheleleyo ezifubeni zabo izihlandlo ezininzi ngonaphakade.

Kwaye baya kubuyekezwa ngokupheleleyo kumgwebo kaYahweh ogqibeleleyo nongunaphakade ongalibazisiyo.

Kwaye ke uya kufika ulawulo lwam lweminyaka eliwaka phezu komhlaba ngokupheleleyo ekupheleni kwenkathazo kaYakobi, kwaye luya kuba lulawulo lobulungisa. Ngeli xesha onke amalungisa aya kwenziwa aphile, kwaye ngamnye uya kube evusiwe ngexesha lakhe nangokulandelelana kwesivuno sakhe. Abathembekileyo kuKristu Yesu abaphilayo nabo balele ukufa bekuKristu baya kuxhwilelwa emazulwini ngephanyazo, kwaye baya kuvuzwa kakhulu kwaye abanye baya kulahlekelwa, kodwa bonke baya kwenziwa baphile ngokupheleleyo nangonaphakade.

Emva koko ekupheleni kwenkathazo kaYakobi kuya kwenzeka uvuko lwabalungileyo apho ndiya kuvusa bonke abangcwele bexesha lembandezelo abafela igama lam kwaye ndiya kuvusela kubomi obungunaphakade bonke abo bafa bekholwa phantsi kweminqophiso emininzi kaYahweh kwanabo bonke abafikelela ekuphefumleni kwabo kokugqibela emhlabeni ukusuka kuAdam ukuya ekuzalweni kweBandla lam ngomhla wePentekoste.
