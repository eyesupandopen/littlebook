Iindudumo 5 & 6

Abalungisanga Abayi Kulidla Ilifa Ubukumkani BukaThixo!

Kusuka kuYeshua HaMashiach:

Nyana wam, ukuba uthe wawamkela amazwi am, wayifihla imiyalelo yam kuwe; ukuze uthobe indlebe yakho kubulumko, uyise intliziyo yakho ekuqondeni. Musani ukuzenza lukhuni iintliziyo zenu, njengasekuxhokonxeni, ngomhla wesihendo entlango: yaye yazini ukuba mna, iNkosi uYesu Krestu, ndihlala ndinani kwaye ndihlala ndikhona ebomini benu njengoko nibambelele kum nosindiso lwam, kuba mna ndiyintloko yoMzimba, kwaye ndiyiNkosi yenu ethembekileyo, Mtshakazi wam othandekayo. Kuba ndinilungiselele indawo nekamva, kwaye kukho umvuzo omkhulu kwababiziweyo bam, nabathembekileyo, nabanyuliweyo.

Andinibizelanga elahlekweni nasesohlwayweni, kwaye andinibizelanga ehlazweni nasekuzigwebeni; anifundanga na ukuba akukho kugwetywa kwabo bakuKrestu Yesu, abangahambi ngokwenyama kodwa behamba ngokomoya? Ngoko ke kholwani ngamazwi am! Andinibizelanga ukuba niphile phantsi kwesithunzi sezono zenu zangaphambili, okanye niphile phantsi kwesithunzi sezenzo zenu zobutsha, kodwa ndinibizele ukuba nifele kwiindlela zenu ezindala zesono nithabathe ubomi obutsha kum njengoko nilahla inyama yenu, njengoko nilahla izono zenu nihamba ebomini obutsha kum. Yabonani! Ndenza zonke izinto zibe ntsha! Alilo elo na ithemba lenu?

Bantwana bam, ndinikhuphe kububi beli hlabathi lifayo langoku, kwaye ndinihlangule emandleni obumnyama ndaniguqulela ebukumkanini bam, ubukumkani bukaThixo, obukhokelwa ndim, uKumkani wooKumkani neNkosi yeeNkosi, apho uYahweh enguThixo, kuba akukho wumbi. Ndinibizele eluzukweni nasekulungeni kum, kwaye ndinihlangule ebukumkanini bukaSathana, ubukumkani bobumnyama, ndanisa eBukumkanini bam, apho kukho ukukhanya! Ngoko ke, vuka wena uleleyo, uvuke kwabafileyo, kwaye mna, uYesu Krestu, ndiya kukunika ukukhanya.

Anifundanga na kwiileta endazithumela kuMtshakazi wam, endazithumela kuni, lona kanye iBandla lam? Anazi na ukuba abalungisanga abayi kulidla ilifa ubukumkani bukaThixo? Musani ukukhohliswa: abenzi bombulo, nabakhonzi bezithixo, nabakrexezi, nabafanasini, nabazisebenzisa kakubi nabantu besilisa, Namasela, nababawayo, namankwali, nabatshabhisi, nabaphangi, abayi kulidla ilifa ubukumkani bukaThixo. Kwaye babenjalo abanye benu: kodwa nihlanjiwe, kodwa ningcwalisiwe, kodwa nigwetyelwe egameni leNkosi uYesu, nangoMoya kaThixo wethu.

Aniyiqondi na imiyalelo yam, O, bandla elidiki-diki laseLawodike? Andinibizelanga ukuba nibuyele enyameni yenu, kwaye andinibizelanga ukuba niqhubeke neendlela zenu ezingalunganga. Cingani, niqwalasele; lowo ucinga ukuba umi, makalumke hleze awe! Zilumkeleni, nemfundiso: hlalani kuzo: kuba ngokwenza oku, niya kuzisindisa nina, nabo banivayo. Qondani, bantwana bam abancinci abadikidiki, ukuba aninakukwazi ukuphila ubomi bokulalanisa phakathi kokulungileyo nokubi, nicinga ukuba nithe tye phambi kwam, xa nikhetha okuncinci kububi obubini kwizithethe zenu zonyulo endingazivumeliyo okanye endingazixhasiyo.

Phumani kuyo, bantu bam, njengoko NDINGANIKEZANGA sisombululo sezopolitiko, kwaye andinikanga nina ngenene inkokeli yehlabathi ukuba yenze nantoni na ibe nkulu kwakhona. Nikhohlisiwe, kwaye nilungiselelwa ukuba nilandele kwaye ninike imvume kunyana wentshabalalo ohamba phakathi kwenu ngokukhululekileyo, nangona engekatyhilwa ngokupheleleyo. Kodwa nina enicela ukuqonda niya kumazi ukuba ungubani, ukuze nikuphephe ukukhohlisa kwakhe. Hayi, ukulalanisa akukokwenu, njengoko ningabantwana bam nabazalwana bam, kwaye andiluzuzanga usindiso lwenu ngokunikela ngobomi bam njengentlawulelo yabaninzi ngokulalanisa noSathana; kuba ukulalanisa kukuzibandakanya nemikhosi yabo. Ngoko ke musani ukwenza impazamo: Ninokuma nam ebulungiseni nasebungcweleni bokwenene, okanye nizibandakanye nemikhosi kaSathana kubukumkani bakhe obufayo, ngoko ke GUQUKANI!

Nizuza ntoni ngokulalanisa nobubi? Aniqondi na ukuba nimele noyise ububi ngokulungileyo? Ngoko ke yimani niqine kum niyeke ukuguqu-guquka njengentlanzi ephume emanzini. Uninzi lwenu lusaphephethwa yiyo yonke imimoya yeemfundiso nobuqhetseba bezandla, apho balalela khona ukuze bakhohlise. Mna, Krestu Yesu, ndithethile, kwaye andiyi kuthuliswa. Uninzi lwenu luzilalanisile nebandla lam, kwaye ndiyanibuza: niya kuxhwaleka kude kube nini phakathi kwezimvo ezimbini? Ukuba iNkosi inguThixo, mlandeleni: kodwa ukuba nguBhahali, mlandeleni yena. Ngokulula, aninakulalanisa nikhethe naluphi na uhlobo lobubi nicinge ukuba nimele mna; xa nikhetha ububi, nikhetha imithombo yobo bubi njengenkosi nomphathi wenu nibe ngumkhonzi wesono. Anazi na, ukuba lowo nizenza abakhonzi bakhe ukuba nimthobele, ningabakhonzi bakhe eniyamthobelayo: nokuba kukwesono kuse ekufeni, nokuba kukokuthobela kuse ebulungiseni?

Ndiveni kwaye nindive kakuhle, Bandla lam; yazini ukuba ndimi ndicaphukile zizenzo ezixhaphakileyo esizweni senu nakwihlabathi liphela, eniye nazilalanisa nazivumela ukuba zingene eBandleni lam ukuze zikhathaze abantwana bam abancinci. Njengokuba kwakunjalo ngemihla kaNowa, kuya kuba njalo. Imihla kaNowa yayilixesha apho umhlaba wawulawulwa ngamasikizi amabi enziwe zizithunywa zezulu eziwileyo, imbewu yazo kanye, imbewu yobubi eyenzelwe intshabalalo yenu epheleleyo: kwaye ziya kubuya, kuba yabona, ndizisa iingxilimbela ukuze zizalisekise ingqumbo yam. Zakufezekisa oku ngokususa nokwaphula imida yendalo awayibekayo uBawo ukuze uxolo nobulungisa loyise, kodwa ngokususa nokuwela ngokuziphatha okubi yona kanye imida ecacileyo kwindalo ngokwayo, awayimisayo uBawo, uninzi lwenu luzingcolisile ngemvume yenu yobubi obuxhaphakileyo kumaxesha enu. Into ebesikho, yiyo leyo iya kubakho: naleyo yenziweyo, yiyo leyo iya kwenziwa; kwaye akukho nto intsha phantsi kwelanga. Anifundanga na, O, nina bokholo oluncinci? Ukususa imida kaThixo kubangela ukuphambana, nokugula, nokudideka kwezinto zonke. Uninzi lwenu luyaziyolisa kwizifo zengqondo, nicinga ukuba indoda inokuba ngumfazi nomfazi abe yindoda, kwaye nikhokelela abantwana benu kwiinkohliso zenu. Uninzi lwenu luya kwiicawa ezikhokelwa ngabafundisi bobuxoki abangamafanasini abavulelekileyo nabazidlayo kwisigulo sabo, kwaye nicinga ukuba bathetha egameni lam? Yazini oku: ukuba nikuloo meko yesono, ndiya kuninikela kwiinkanuko enizilangazelela ngokungenantloni, kwaye niya kuvuna isiqhamo seendlela zenu ezingcolileyo.

Yabonani! Ngenxa yesi sizathu, uThixo wabanikela kwiinkanuko ezilihlazo: kuba nabafazi babo bawuguqula umsebenzi wendalo bawusa koko kungaphandle kwendalo: Kanjalo namadoda, eshiya umsebenzi wendalo womfazi, atsha kwiinkanuko zawo omnye komnye, amadoda namadoda esenza oko kungafanelekanga, ezamkelela isohlwayo esifanelekileyo sesiphoso sawo. Kodwa hayi, akwanelanga ukukhokelwa ngabafanasini nobuxoki babo; nivumela abantwana benu ukuba balalaniswe baze banqunyulwe phambi kwam.

Kukho isiphumo esikhulu esilindele ezi zenzo, kwaye aziyi kuhamba zingohlwaywanga. Kanti ke niyaqhubeka nokuphambana kwenu, kwaye ngaphandle kokuba niguquke, ndiya kunikhafula emlonyeni wam. Yizani kum niguqukile, nihlale kwirhonya nothuthu, ukuze ndiyazi intembeko yokuguquka kwenu kwaye nilile nikhale ngenxa yezono zenu ukuze ningahlawuli ixabiso ekugwetyweni. Uninzi lwenu lunekratshi kakhulu kuba nizama ukundenza iNkosi ngelixa nisenentliziyo engenakholo, kuba ngentliziyo umntu ukholelwa ebulungiseni. Ngoko ke yibani nokholo ukuze nisindiswe; ukholo lwenu luya kubonakaliswa kubo bonke ngokulahla kwenu isono nokuphuma kwiindlela zenu ezimdaka. Abangendawo bahamba macala onke, xa abona bantu babi bephakanyiswa. Kodwa nisaqhubeka nivumelana neendlela zabantu baseSodom, kwaye ninceda ukuzisa ububi obukhulu elizweni, kangangokuba nivotela abaxhaphazi babantwana ukuba bakhokele amazwe enu. Niswele nelona nqanaba lisisiseko sokuqonda, kwaye anilandeli ndlela yam; kwaye ukuba niyaqhubeka, ndiya kuninikela ngokupheleleyo kwiinkanuko zenu ezilihlazo ukuze kutshatyalaliswe inyama ukuze umoya usindiswe.

Mandivule amehlo enu, nina eningaziyo kwaye niyimfama ngokuzithandela, nina nihleli kubumdaka nobumnyama, niqengqeleka eludakeni. Aninakunika imvume yenu kububi nicinge ukuba nikhonza mna, kuba ndabuchasa ububi nesihendo de kwaphuma ithontsi lokugqibela legazi kum laya emhlabeni obomvu, kuba ndingumAdam wokugqibela kwaye ndenziwe umoya ophilayo. Uninzi lwenu lufunjathiswe ngamazwi amnandi neentetho ezintle kwaye ninika imvume yenu epheleleyo kwizaphuli-mthetho nabantu aboyikekayo abaxhaphaza abantwana. Baxhaphaza abantwana, bathuthumbisa abantwana ngenxa yeenkanuko zabo ezimbi: Kuba yiyo leyo indlela efika ngayo ingqumbo kaThixo kwiBhabhiloni eyimfihlakalo, kodwa uninzi lwenu luyaqhubeka lunika imvume yenu kula marhamncwa akrwada abekelwe bucala imililo yesihogo engunaphakade engayi kuze icinywe. Phumani kuyo, bantu bam, kungenjalo niya kwabelana ngezibetho zayo nesigwebo esoyikekayo esiza phezu kwayo; kwaye ndinilumkisile. Kuba luluphi ulwalamano analo uKrestu noBheliyali? Ukuba nabelana ngobuxoki bayo, niya kwabelana ngezibetho zayo; ngoko ke zahlukaniseni ngokupheleleyo ngelizwi nangesenzo, kwaye ningavumelani nayo nakancinci. Musani ukuphila ubomi obungenankathalo, obulalanisayo; kuba ndiya kubohlwaya bonke abenjenjalo. Nicinga ukuba ngenxa yokuba ninobomi obungunaphakade aninakufumana ilahleko enkulu nesiphumo? Ndiya kuyihlutha imivuzo yenu ndiyinike abanye, kwaye niya kuba ngabona bancinci ebukumkanini bamazulu ukuba niyaqhubeka, kuba utsho uYeshua HaMashiach, uMyeni, kuMtshakazi wam!

Kusuka kuYahweh, uThixo Omkhulu Ongunaphakade:

O, nina bangenaxabiso abenzakalisa indalo yam, nina bangenaxabiso abazithengise kuSathana, nina bangenaxabiso abenze uSathana waba nguThixo wenu, nina bangenaxabiso abamgatya unyana wam uYesu Krestu nosindiso endilunikezele egameni lakhe, nina bangenaxabiso abavumele abantwana benu ukuba batshiswe emlilweni kaMoloki. Ndiya kuninika ihlabathi enilinqwenelayo, kwaye ndinilungiselele ihlabathi elingenam, ihlabathi elingenanyana wam uKrestu Yesu, ihlabathi elinechibi elikhulu lomlilo nesulfure elenzelwe nina kanye.

Ndiya kuninika ubumnyama enibunqwenela kangaka, kuba ukukhanya kwam akuyi kubakho apho, kwaye niya kuchitha unaphakade nihlukene nam, uYahweh, uThixo omkhulu oqaqambileyo, obuzuko nokukhanya kwakhe kungekho nyama inokukubona kwaye ongowaphakade naphakade. Nina bavukeli, nina bakhohlakeleyo abaye batshabalalisa indalo yam kwasekuqaleni ngeenkanuko zenu nentiyo nomona, yazini ukuba nizifumbalele isohlwayo esikhulu nentuthumbo eningasoze niphiliswe kuyo nakubo bonke abalahlekiswe bubuxoki benu nokwala uYesu Krestu, nokwala ukuguquka kubuxoki benu niye enyanini yam.

Nicinga ukuba ukugqwetha iindlela zam nokususa imida yam yinto elula, kodwa niya kuyiqonda ingqumbo yam kwakamsinya nje lakunichukumisa ilangabi lokuqala, kwaye niya kugutyungelwa ngumlilo nesulfure eningasoze nisinde kuko. Ngaphezu koko, nibulele, nadlwengula, naphanga, naxhaphaza, nenzakalisa, nabulala, natshabalalisa, kwaye nathatha inxaxheba kwezona zono zibi kakhulu, nide nanqwenela inyama yasemzini; kwaye nizihlanganise nezithunywa zezulu eziwileyo, ngoko ke ndiya kunihlanganisa nechibi lomlilo endililungiselele uMtyholi nezithunywa zakhe, kwaye aniyi kusinda. Aninalo uxolo.

Ndiya kubeka ubulungisa phezu kwenu, kuba ndinguThixo wobulungisa, kwaye ndinguThixo wempindezelo. Kanti, niya kuma ngokukrakra nichasene nam, nikhunga ityala lenu, nicinga ukuba ndiya kuthi ngandlel' ithile ndirhoxe kwiindlela zenu zobusathana zokuthi: yenza oko uthanda, kuya kuba ngumthetho wonke. Aninalo uxolo. Ndiya kubuyisela, kwaye nithi ndinguThixo onothando (nto leyo endiyiyo), kodwa ke nilindele ukuba ndingabeki bulungisa kwabo bayingcolisileyo indalo yam, kwabo bamthuthumbisayo bambulala unyana wam okuphela kwamzelweyo njengoko wazinikela njengentlawulelo yabo bonke; ewe, nilindele ukuba ndingabeki bulungisa phezu kwenu ngenxa yeendlela zenu ezingenaxabiso xa nibulele, nathuthumbisa, nenzakalisa abamsulwa kwaye nilambise abantwana bam abancinci ngenxa yeenjongo zenu.

Ngenene, uSathana ngokwakhe uya kutshabalalisa konke okwam ngenxa yobugovu bakhe, kwaye nangoko nizama ukundityhola ngobuyatha kwizigwebo zam ezilungileyo xa ndiza kukhusela abam. Iintsuku zenu zokulahlekisa abantwana bam abancinci ziza kuphela. Yabonani! Irhamncwa lenu nomprofeti wobuxoki baya kutshatyalaliswa ngokupheleleyo, kwaye ndiya kubaphosa bephila kwelo chibi likhulu livutha umlilo nesulfure; baya kunilinda ukuba nizibandakanye nabo kwilifa lenu.

Isigwebo esikhulu siphezu kwakho, O, nkosana yobumnyama; ubukumkani bakho nabo bonke abakulandelayo sele beza kutshatyalaliswa ngokupheleleyo. Kuba aningoothixo, kwaye aninakuzihlangula nina bangenaxabiso esandleni sam, kwaye ndiya kunibopha kobona bumnyama bunzulu enibunqwenelayo, nditsho nakwenzonzobila enzulu ndiya kunibopha ngetsheyini elikhulu eningenakuze niliqhawule. Ewe, enzonzobileni niya kuba lusuku olunye, kuba usuku olunye kuYehova lunjengeminyaka eliwaka, neminyaka eliwaka injengosuku olunye. Kwaye nangoko ndiya kunikhulula okwexeshana elincinane ukuze nihende kwaye niqokelele abokugqibela kwabo bamasikizi abaya kulandela nina neendlela zenu. Kwaye ndiya kunidla nonke ngomlilo, kwaye iitrone zokugweba ziya kumiswa, kwaye ndiya kunigweba nonke nina benzi bobubi, kwaye niya kuya kwisohlwayo esingunaphakade kude nobuso beNkosi. Niya kutsha phambi kwezithunywa zezulu naphambi kwemvana, kwaye ziya kunibona nithuthunjiswa kwaye nohlwaywa, kuba nibonile bonke, kwaye niya kuhlawulela ngokupheleleyo izono zenu ngonaphakade.

Ndiya ke nitywinwe entolongweni yenu, kwaye niya kulityalwa, kwaye anisoze nikhunjulwe kwakhona, kuba izinto zangaphambili ziya kudlula zilityalwe. Kuba yabona, ndiya kwenza izulu elitsha nomhlaba omtsha apho kuhlala ubulungisa; kwaye mna, uYahweh, uThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade wabo bonke oothixo, kuba akukho namnye onjengam, yabona, ndiya kwenza zonke izinto zibe ntsha, kuba mna, uYahweh, ndikuthethile! UYahweh, uThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade, uya kwenza itrone yakhe emhlabeni, kwaye indawo yam yokuhlala iya kuba phakathi kwabantu, kwaye anisoze niphinde nibe kho, O, nina bangenaxabiso, kwaye niya kutsha ngonaphakade kwintolongo yenu elityelweyo yentuthumbo eningenakuze nisinde kuyo, kuba ndiya kubeka itywina kwaye akukho namnye unokuliqhawula.

Kanti, uninzi lwenu olunovelwano noSathana lundityhola ngobuyatha ngokuthatha ubulungisa nempindezelo ezandleni zam ukuze ndihlangule abo bazenze bafanele isohlwayo esingunaphakade emalangabini. Yivani amazwi am, O, bemi bomhlaba: uNyana wam uyeza, kwaye uza nempindezelo enkulu, umsindo, nengqumbo ngenxa yezinto ezigwenxa ezenziwe zizandla zenu. Amaxesha okugweba, nkqu nesigwebo sosuku olukhulu lukaKrestu sele luza kuqhambuka emhlabeni wonke, kwaye akuyi kubakho nyango kwabakhohlakeleyo, akukho nyango kuSathana, kwaye akukho nyango kubo bonke abazimanye naye, njengoko bonke baya kuya kwintshabalalo engunaphakade, kwaye ndiya kubuyisela kubo konke abakwenzileyo kwabamsulwa.

Ngoko ke, ndiya kubuyisela iminqweno yeentliziyo zabo ezimbi nayo yonke imisebenzi yabo emibi phezu kwabo ngonaphakade. Bonani umsindo wam ubilayo kwaye yazini ukuba amaxesha okubuyiselwa kwezinto zonke asekufuphi, kwaye akusekho manani aseleyo kwiiwotshi emazulwini, kuba onke amanani awele ebusweni bewotshi, kuba ixesha liphelile! Ndicebisa bonke abafunda la mazwi ukuba babalekele kunyana wam uYesu Krestu beguqukile kwaye bamenza iNkosi; kuba akukho namnye uya kuza kum ngaphandle kukaYesu Krestu, kuba alikho elinye igama endilinikeleyo losindiso, kwaye zonke izithixo zenu ziya kutsha. Lisekho ixesha losindiso, kwaye nabani na obiza egameni leNkosi kude kube sekupheleni uya kusindiswa. Ngoko ke balekelani kuYesu Krestu ngoku nisesenako, kuba usuku lweNkosi lufikile, kwaye ndiya kumkhulula uNyana wam, kwaye ingqumbo yeMvana iya kwaneliswa. Mange uNyana hleze aqumbe, nize nitshabalale endleleni, xa ingqumbo yakhe ivutha kancinane: Banoyolo bonke abakholose ngaye.


Ukuze uqonde: Isaya 13:1 kwiSeptuagint: Ndinika umyalelo, kwaye ndiyabazisa: iingxilimbela ziyeza ukuze zizalisekise ingqumbo yam, zivuya kwangaxeshanye kwaye zithuka.
