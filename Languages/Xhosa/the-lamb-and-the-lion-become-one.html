Ubungqina Obubini: Sirayeli
Okumnandi Nokukrakra
Novemba 20, 2024

IMvana neNgonyama Ziba Mntu Mnye!

Yizani kuYeshua HaMashiach, zihlobo zam, niwuqonde umsebenzi womkhululi wenu, kuba ndingumkhululi wenu oyinzalelwane. Kuba ndingulowo uya kuthi akhulule intsalela kaYakobi ukuze iphile phambi koMkhulu onguMNA, uYHVH, uThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade nongapheliyo. Yaye ulawulo lwam nobukumkani bam abuyi kuba nasiphelo, ngoko ke, phulaphulani aMazwi eNkosi yenu engunaphakade yeSithembiso, uYeshua HaMashiach, okwanguYesu Krestu, okwanguKrestu Yesu, kuba wenjenjalo uMkhulu onguMNA ukundenza ngonaphakade.

Kwaye ngenene uYahweh undinike igama elingaphezu kwawo onke amagama ukuze ngegama likaYesu onke amadolo agobe, ezulwini nasemhlabeni naphantsi komhlaba. Kuba ndingulowo uYahweh anikele zonke izinto ezulwini nasemhlabeni ezandleni zam ezinamandla ukuze kuzaliseke zonke izinto. Ngoko ke, Sirayeli, liveni ilizwi lam ngokuthobela okunothando ngenxa yothando endinibonise lona ngokunifela kwiintlungu ezibuhlungu ezingezizo ezam. Noko ke, eluthandweni lwam ngani, ndathatha indawo yenu ukuze ndinikhulule kwisohlwayo sikaMoses, isohlwayo eningenakukwazi ukuzihlawulela sona ngenxa yesono esisenyameni yenu. Ewe, ngenene ndathunyelwa ehlabathini ngenxa yokukhululwa kuka-Adam.

Kuba uMoses ulilungisa, ungcwele kwaye unobulungisa, yaye uMoses ngenene uhleli kuMesiya wenu wokwenene neNkosi engunaphakade yoBulungisa, kuba ndinguye. Kwaye ndingulowo wawugcina ngokugqibeleleyo umthetho kaMoses ngothando lwam nangokuthobela kwam uYahweh owandikhokelayo eluthandweni lwakhe olungapheliyo ngohlanga luka-Adam olwoniweyo nolwoyisiweyo ngabawiweyo, ngoko ke inceba nobabalo zinikwe ngobuninzi obukhulu ukuze kubanjwe ubungqongqo obupheleleyo bomgwebo nengqumbo ukuze kwenzeke ukwahlukana okupheleleyo kokugqibela phakathi kwengqolowa kaYahweh nomdiza wabawiweyo, kwaye ngoko bonke abaziindlalifa zobomi obungunaphakade kuKrestu Yesu baya kukhululwa ngokuzikhethela kwabo ukuzithoba kuYeshua HaMashiach, onguKrestu Yesu, nonguYesu waseNazarete.

Kwaye uYahweh wandithuma ezintlangeni, ndaza ndaya kukhonza izizwe ezilahlekileyo zakwaSirayeli kuba ndaliwa zizihlobo zam ngokungakholwa okungenalukholo, nangababingeleli abakhulu neSanhedrin abakhonza iNamba ngasese kuba bangabaka-Esawu ozimanye nentiyo yakhe ngomntakwabo uYakobi noAshkenazi kaYafete, ukuze baziqokelelele izinto ezezame nokuba bamnike uYakobi inxeba lokugqibela entloko elingayi kuvunywa nguYahweh, isenzo angasayi kusivumela ngesandla sam esinamandla nesithobelayo. Ngoko ke, zihlobo zam, zithobeleni ilizwi lam ukuze niphile phambi kwam, nokuze niphile phambi koYahweh ekungekho mntu unokusondela kuye ngaphandle koYesu Krestu kuba wenjenjalo uYahweh ukukhetha kwicebo lakhe elingunaphakade lokukhulula nobulumko. Ngoko qondani ukuba ngaphandle koMesiya wenu wokwenene, uYeshua HaMashiach, nihleli nifile ezonweni nakwiziphoso endazoyisayo xa ndahlawula ngokupheleleyo isohlwayo sikaMoses ngonaphakade nakubo bonke abakhetha ukwamkela idini lam.

Kwaye kunjalo ukuba uMoses uhleli kuYeshua HaMashiach, kwaye ndingulowo uMoses wayalela ukuba nimthobele ngokupheleleyo, ngoko ke, yivani amazwi am nizaluse iintliziyo zenu neendlebe zenu ukuze niyive intetho yam. Kuba iintliziyo zenu zityebile kukungathobeli, kwaye nizalise iindlebe zenu ngobulumko buka-Esawu nazingcolisa iintliziyo zenu ngobuxoki babo obunyelisayo, beTalmud. Qondani ukuba uMoses ugqibelele, ulilungisa kwaye ungcwele, kwaye ukuba nawuphi na umntu uthi umthetho kaMoses uqalekisiwe, makabe lowo mntu uqalekisiwe ngenxa yonyeliso lwakhe, nangomthetho kaMoses awawuqalekisayo. Ngoko ke, ndiyanazisa ukuba umthetho wanikwa ukuze woyise isono enyameni, kuba apho kungekho mthetho isono asibalwa ngokomthetho, nangona isono sikhona enyameni ngokunjalo. Ngoko ke, ngemisebenzi yomthetho kaMoses akukho nyama iya kugwetyelwa phambi koYahweh: kuba ngomthetho kaMoses kuza ulwazi lwesono.

Noko ke, njengoko ndenza uPawulos wabhalela iBandla lam kwixesha lam loBabalo, ngoku bubulungisa bukaThixo obubonakaliswe ngaphandle komthetho kaMoses, bunobungqina bomthetho nabaProfeti. Owu, zithunywa zam zangaphambili eziwe kwisabelo senu sangaphambili esabelwa isizwe senu esakha saba sikhulu nguYahweh ngokwakhe kuba nanimele nibe ngabantu abakhethekileyo, abahlukileyo nabahlukileyo kwiintlanga! Noko ke, nindalile ngokunjalo nooYihlo benu kwimvukelo yenu enentliziyo elukhuni ngokuchasene neenjongo namacebo angunaphakade kaThixo uSomandla, u-El Shaddai. Kwaye kungenxa yokwala kwenu aMazwi am anikwe kwivangeli yosindiso xa ndatyhila imfihlelo enkulu kaThixo kubapostile bam abangcwele nakubaprofeti, niye naba njengabadinga ubisi, kwaye anikwazi ukwetyisa ukutya okuqinileyo kuba nisaziintsana ezingenakuqonda ngokupheleleyo, nangona ngokuzikhukhumeza nizibala njengezilumko ezihlakaniphileyo nezikhanyiso zokukhanya kodwa nikhanyiselwe ngobuxoki ngabawiweyo ukuze nilahlekise abantu ngeendlela zenu ezingafundanga.

Ngoko ke, qondani, zihlobo zam, ukuba olilungisa uya kuphila ngokholo kwaye ngemisebenzi yomthetho akukho nyama iya kugwetyelwa! Kubhaliwe kwizibhalo ezingcwele ezinikwe ngezandla zikaPawulos, owayengumBhenjamin othobelayo owayekade engathobeli kwaye enenkani ekumchukeni kwakhe uYesu waseNazarete, ngokufanayo nani: Ngoko ke abo bangabokholo, bayasikelelwa kunye no-Abraham othembekileyo. Kuba bonke abangabemisebenzi yomthetho, baphantsi kwesiqalekiso somthetho: kuba kubhaliwe kwathiwa, Uqalekisiwe wonke umntu ongahlaliyo ezintweni zonke, ezibhaliweyo encwadini yoMthetho, ukuba azenze.

Noko ke, ngenxa yenkohlakalo, ubuqhetseba, namaqhinga enyoka ngomhla u-Adam wawa phezulu; isono siye salawula enyameni ukusukela ngoko kwaye imeko eyonakeleyo ka-Adam asilokhaya lenu, kwaye anijoliswanga ukuba nichithe unaphakade ezindlwini zodongwe apho ezo zindlu zibuyela eluthulini lomhlaba apho isiqu senu sathathwa khona. Ngoko ke, uYahweh undenze umoya onika ubomi, kwaye ndingum-Adam wokugqibela. Kwaye ndingulowo wenziwe njengomzekelo wobomi obungunaphakade ukuze nonke nidalwe ngokutsha ngokugqibeleleyo nangobunye, njengomntu omnye omtsha. Kuba akusayi kubakho iintlobo ezimbini zabantu ngonaphakade kodwa ibe nye kuba imeko ka-Adam iya kutshabalala ngonaphakade kunye nokonakala okukhoyo enyameni eyonakeleyo.

Ngoko ke, qondani ukuba abawiweyo baya kunifihlela inyaniso kubumfama benu obuyinxalenye nangoku nekratshi, inyaniso eya kunikhulula ngokupheleleyo inkululeko endizama ukunikhulula ngayo. Ngoko ke ndiyanazisa, Sirayeli (endimthandayo), ukuba akukho mntu ugwetyelwayo ngemisebenzi yomthetho kaMoses phambi koYahweh, kuyabonakala: kuba olilungisa uya kuphila ngokholo. Kwaye umthetho asiwokholo: kodwa umntu oya kuzenza ezo zinto, uya kuphila kuzo. Kwaye mna, Yeshua HaMashiach, ndinikhulule kwisiqalekiso somthetho, ndenziwa isiqalekiso ngenxa yenu, kuba kubhaliwe kwathiwa, Uqalekisiwe wonke umntu oxhonywe emthini. Ngoko ke, Sirayeli, lamkeleni idini lam ukuze nina nenzala yenu niphile kwaye nibe nokufikelela kubukumkani bam bewaka leminyaka obunikwa njengokuphumla kosuku lweSabatha kumacebo angunaphakade kaYahweh okukhulula uluntu, kuba iwaka leminyaka linjengosuku kwaye usuku lunjengewaka leminyaka phambi koYahweh, enimele nenze naye, Sirayeli.

Ngoko ke, mkani kwabo banixokisayo nizahlule kuloo ncwadi yobuxoki enyelisayo ethunyelwe ngabawiweyo ukuze banikhohlise banibophelele kwimpazamo ukuze nitshatyalaliswe ngokupheleleyo. Mkani kubuxoki benyoka niphume phakathi kwabo, nahlukane, Sirayeli, ndaye ndiya kunamkela ndinilondoloze niphile kwintshabalalo yeveki yamashumi asixhenxe kaDaniyeli, imbandezelo kaYakobi ezayo ukucoca umhlaba ezandleni zabenzi bobubi ngomgwebo omkhulu nolungileyo.

Kuba amaxesha okwahlukana ayeza ngokupheleleyo emhlabeni apho uYakobi, olilifa lakhe lisisithembiso kubantwana besithembiso abaphila ngokholo, uya kwahlulwa ngokupheleleyo nangonaphakade kumntakwabo ongathobeliyo, u-Esawu ofuna ukuba oko wakuthengisayo ngokungakholwa okungenalukholo kuba wayengenantlonipho okanye ukholo kwizithembiso ezithembekileyo ezenziwa ngokholo nguYahweh ku-Abraham othembekileyo, kodwa endaweni yoko wayekhathalele ngakumbi izinto zomhlaba engumhenyuzi nomntu ongendawo owathengisa ilifa lakhe elingunaphakade nelungelo lobuzibulo ngeqhekeza lesonka.

Ngoko ke ndiyanazisa ngekamva endinibizela ukuba nithathe inxaxheba kulo kwaye endiya kunigcina kulo kukhuseleko lwamandla am ukuba niya kuzithoba kulawulo lwam negunya lam, kuba ngokwenjenjalo ndiya kunibuyisela ngokupheleleyo kuYahweh onguYise wezikhanyiso ongenaguquguquko nasithunzi sokujika. Kwaye kubhaliwe kwizithembiso zakudala kwaye kuya kuba njalo zihlobo zam endingumkhululi wazo!

Kwaye kuya kwenzeka ekupheleni kwembandezelo kaYakobi ukuba ndiya kumisa ubukumkani bam ngobulungisa emhlabeni, kwaye akukho nto imbi iya kungena khona. Qondani ukuba ndiya kuzisa isiphelo esingunaphakade kubukumkani bukaSathana kwaye inamba ayisayi kuphakama ilawule kwakhona kwaye irhamncwa elivela elwandle nerhamncwa elivela emhlabeni ziya kuphoswa ziphila kwelo chibi likhulu lomlilo wesulfure evuthayo, ejikelezayo etshisa awona malangatye ashushu aluhlaza kwaye kwelo chibi linukayo lesohlwayo ziya kuphoswa ngonaphakade, apho impethu yazo ingafi kuba zifanelekile.

USathana ngokwakhe uya kuthathwa abotshwe ngekhonkco elikhulu lentuthumbo nesohlwayo apho uya kulinda esoyika kwaye enxunguphele ngentshabalaliso yakhe ezayo, ilifa aya kwabelana ngalo nabo bonke abamlandelayo. Ngenene, ngenene, enzonzobileni uya kuhlala etywiniwe kwaye ebotshelelwe isithuba sewaka leminyaka, ekupheleni kwalo ixesha uya kukhululwa ukuze aqokelele abo baya kube bevukile baza bajoyina iindlela zakhe, imvukelo yakhe yokugqibela ukuze ububi butshatyalaliswe ngonaphakade ekugqityweni komgwebo. Kwaye ekupheleni kwexesha lakhe elifutshane xa eya kube ebaqokelele kunye njengentlabathi yolwandle ngokuchasene nenkampu yabangcwele kububanzi bomhlaba, umlilo uya kuwa uvela kuYahweh phezulu oya kubatshisa ngokupheleleyo ubadle bonke, kwaye uSathana uya kubotshwa ngonaphakade aphoswe kwilifa lakhe elingunaphakade kuloo malangatye avuthayo angunaphakade esohlwayo kwaye loo mgwebo wetrone emhlophe enkulu uya kuqala.

Kwaye kwelo chibi livuthayo lesohlwayo elitshisa awona malangatye ashushu entuthumbo, apho uSathana uya kuhlawula ngokupheleleyo sonke isono asenzileyo izihlandlo ezininzi, kwaye sonke isenzo esibi sibhalwe ngokugqibeleleyo emazulwini kwaye siya kunikwa kumalangatye esohlwayo njengelifa lakhe elingunaphakade. Ewe, sonke isidalwa esilandele sazibophelela kwinjongo yakhe siya kujongana nesohlwayo esifanayo kwaye asiyi kuba nanceba kuba asibonisanga nceba, ngoko ke akukho nceba iya kunikwa ngonaphakade. Kwaye njengoko benze kwabanye, kwaye njengoko beyenzakalisile indalo kaYahweh kuya kubuyekezwa ezintlokweni zabo, njengoko kwathenjiswa mandulo kuba akukho sithembiso sinye sikaYahweh siya kuze siwele emhlabeni singazalisekiswanga. Ngenene, ngenene bonke abo balalayo isipho sam sosindiso baya kuphoswa emlilweni kwaye akukho kukhetha buso.

Noko ke, isiphelo sabawiweyo abazikhetheleyo bazenzela sona kwimvukelo yabo embi ngokuchasene noYahweh nendalo yakhe asiyosenu zihlobo zam, ukuba niya kundamkela njengoMesiya wenu. Hayi, ngokuchaseneyo, mininzi imivuzo neentsikelelo enizibekelweyo eniya kuziqonda ngokupheleleyo ebukumkanini bam endiya kulawula ngabo ngobulungisa obungunaphakade. Kuya kubakho uKumkani wookumkani omnye neNkosi yeenkosi enye eya kulawula umhlaba ngobulungisa obungunaphakade, ukusuka eYerusalem, ukusuka kwitrone kaDavide endiyindlalifa yayo engunaphakade! Kwaye kumtshakazi wam othembekileyo endiya kumthabathela kumnqophiso ongunaphakade ndiya kuthwesisa isithsaba ndingcwalise umkhosi omkhulu wooKumkani nabaBingeleli ngokokuthembeka kwabo kwaye baya kulawula ngobulungisa ngemvisiswano egqibeleleyo noKumkani wabo wookumkani neNkosi yeenkosi kwaye siya kulawula njengabanye.

Kwaye abantu belizwe nabantu beentlanga baya kukhanda amakrele abo abe ngamakhuba kwaye iintlanga azisayi kuphinda zilwe, kwaye abantu bomhlaba abayi kufunda ukulwa nokulwa ebukumkanini bam obungcwele. Njengoko kubhaliwe ngabaprofeti bakudala: Ingcuka iya kuhlala nemvana, ingwe ilale netakane, ithole, nengonyama, nenkomo etyetyisiweyo kunye, nomntwana omncinci uya kuziqhuba. Inkomo nebhere ziya kudla: amankonyana azo aya kulala kunye: nengonyama iya kudla umququ njengenkunzi yenkomo. Umntwana olunyulwayo uya kudlala emngxunyeni wephimpi, nomntwana olunyulweyo uya kubeka isandla sakhe emngxunyeni werhamba. Akuyi kubakho mntu wenzakalisayo okanye otshabalalisayo kuyo yonke intaba yam engcwele: kuba umhlaba uya kuzala lulwazi lweNkosi, njengamanzi agubungele ulwandle.

Ngaloo mini kuya kuphuma amanzi obomi eYerusalem, isiqingatha sawo siye ngaselwandle lwaseMpuma, nesiqingatha sawo siye ngaselwandle olukude, kwaye kuya kuba njalo, ehlotyeni nasebusika. Kwaye iNkosi iya kuba nguKumkani phezu komhlaba wonke: ngaloo mini kuya kubakho iNkosi enye, neGama layo liya kuba linye. Lonke ilizwe liya kujikwa libe lithafa ukusuka eGeba ukuya eRimon, ngasezantsi kweYerusalem, kwaye liya kuphakanyiswa, lihlalwe endaweni yalo: ukusuka esangweni likaBhenjamin ukuya kwindawo yesango lokuqala, ukuya esangweni lekona, nokusuka kwinqaba kaHananeli, ukuya kwizixovulelo zewayini zooKumkani. Kwaye abantu baya kuhlala kulo, kwaye akusayi kubakho ntshabalalo, kodwa iYerusalem iya kuhlalwa ngokukhuselekileyo.

Ngoko qondani, Sirayeli, into endiya kuyenzela bonke abo bandithandayo ngokuthobela imiyalelo yam engunaphakade yobomi noxolo endiniyalela ngayo. Ngoko qondani ukuba ukuthanda uYahweh kukugcina imiyalelo yakhe kwaye imiyalelo kaYahweh iyalela ukuthotyelwa koNyana kaYahweh, endinguye: ngoko ke, mangeni uNyana hleze ndibe nomsindo nize nitshabalale endleleni.

Kuba, obam bubukumkani apho iinjongo zam ezingunaphakade ziya kudibana ngokugqibeleleyo nangokuzaliseka okupheleleyo njengoko imvana isiba nye nengonyama.

Kuba, ndiyiMvana kaThixo, enikelwe ngenxa yezono zoluntu eligazi layo lingumbingelelo ongunaphakade wokucamagushela apho abangalunganga benziwa mhlophe njengekhephu kubunyulu obungcwele.

Kuba, ndiyiNgonyama yesizwe sakwaYuda elwela nina Sirayeli kwaye eya kuzitshabalalisa ngokupheleleyo iintshaba zenu njengoko kubhaliwe ngam; impindezelo yeyam kwaye ndiya kubuyisela, itsho iNkosi yeenkosi noKumkani wookumkani!

Ngoko ke, qondani iinjongo zam nokuba uYahweh umthumele bani ukuba anikhulule kwaye zininzi iinkalo kwiinjongo zam zosindiso. Kwaye ndingoyena ubalaseleyo, lowo uphumelele usindiso lwenu ngonaphakade ukuba nikhetha ukwamkela isandla sam sothando kwaye namkele umnqophiso wam omtsha.

Kuba kungomnqophiso wam omtsha endawuqinisekisa ngegazi lam, oseli liTestamente yam eNtsha ekuya kuthi ngayo umhlaba ulawulwe ngobulungisa! Kwaye ulwazi ngoYahweh luya kuwugubungela umhlaba nozuko lwam luya kukhanya luphuma eYerusalem ukuze iintlanga zizibuthe zize zingene kumasango angcwele aloo mzi ungcwele ukuze zifunde ngoYahweh zize ziphile ngeendlela zakhe emhlabeni.

Akusayi kubakho ebukumkanini bam usana lweentsuku, nexhego elingazalisekanga imihla yalo: kuba umntwana uya kufa eneminyaka elikhulu ubudala: kodwa umoni, eneminyaka elikhulu ubudala, uya kuqalekiswa. Kwaye kuya kuthi lowo uphilela ukubona iminyaka elikhulu uya kuba njengosana nje.

Nangona kunjalo, umhlaba uya kubuyiselwa kubuqaqawuli bawo bangaphambili phambi kokuba abawileyo bangcolise umhlaba ngamasikizi abo xa behla eNtabeni yeHermon ukuze benze isifungo sabo esiqalekisiweyo ukuze baphilele iinkanuko zabo esonweni esikhulu nakwimvukelo kwimiyalelo yobomi ababedalelwe kuyo ngokugqibeleleyo. Abayi kubakho ebukumkanini bam; kananjalo umhlaba awuyi kulawulwa ngogonyamelo olunentiyo apho abona bomeleleyo baphilayo, kuba ububhanxa obunjalo kukuguqulwa kwemithetho kaYahweh apho uSathana ahlala efuna ukuguqula imiyalelo kaYahweh ukuze intshabalalo ilawule.

Kwaye ndim, uYeshua HaMashiach, oya kulawula iintlanga ngentonga yentsimbi ngezandla zooKumkani nababingeleli abaninzi endiya kubathwesa izithsaba ndibamisele kwinkonzo ephakamileyo, kwaye bona baziintlahlela zabafileyo, kwaye abafi abasayi kuphinda bafe kwaye umvuzo wabo awunaphelo, kuba beza kum ngokuzithandela ngemihla yobabalo ndaza ndabaqokelelela kum emzimbeni wam owadalwa ngokutsha njengomntu omnye omtsha nokuba babengamaYuda okanye beeNtlanga, nokuba babengamakhoboka okanye bekhululekile. Baya kuba neziphiwo ezininzi nezakhono neempawu abazinikiweyo ngenxa yokuthembeka kwabo kuba bangaboyisi bam aboyisa zonke izinto ngegazi leMvana.

Kuba yiMvana kaThixo eyoyisa abawileyo eKalvari ngonaphakade kwaye yiNgonyama yeSizwe sakwaYuda eya kuyithobela ngokupheleleyo indalo kuYahweh, ukuze uYahweh abe yinto yonke ezintweni zonke xa ndiya kunikela kuye indalo ethotyiweyo kuba kufuneka ndilawule de zonke iintshaba zam zenziwe isihlalo seenyawo zam, kwaye yiNgonyama yeSizwe sakwaYuda eya kunyathel' yonk' into yobukumkani bukaSathana phantsi kweenyawo zam ezinamandla nezingunaphakade.

Ngoko ke, ndim lowo iMvana iya kumanyana kuye ngoxolo olupheleleyo nomanyano neNgonyama kuba iinjongo zam ezingunaphakade ziya kwenziwa ngonaphakade kwaye akukho nto kwaye akukho mntu unokumelana noYeshua HaMashiach kwaye amaxesha am afikile apho ndiya kuzityhila kubemi bomhlaba. Yabonani, ndiza ngamafu, yaye onke amehlo aya kundibona: ewe, nabo bandihlabayo: kwaye zonke izizwe zomhlaba ziya kulila phambi kwam, kunjalo, Amen.

NdinguYeshua HaMashiach osekufuphi ukuzityhila emhlabeni kwaye ihlabathi lonke liya kwazi ukuba ukhona uThixo ezulwini kwaye iindlela zikaYahweh ziya kuzalisa umhlaba ngobulungisa obungcwele.

NdinguKristu Yesu, iNkosi yenu enyukileyo nevusiweyo, uKumkani, uMbingeleli oMkhulu, noMesiya!

Ndim lowo uthumele amaZwi am angcwele ngesithunywa sam nomkhonzi wam kwaye ngesithunywa sam nomkhonzi wam athunyelwe amaZwi am angcwele! Kuba isithunywa sam esinamandla sibuyele ngokuthembekileyo kubizo lwaso lwanaphakade emva kokuthethiswa okukhulu, inguquko, nokuhlanjululwa egazini lam lentlawulelo; kwaye ndisamkele kum eluthandweni lwam kwabaguqukayo nokuba bangoobani na abeza kum ngentliziyo eyaphukileyo. Kwaye kwisithunywa sam ndayityhila iNcwadi eNcinci awayiyalelwa uYohane uMtyhili ukuba ayitywine ukuze ityhilwe ngexesha layo, leyo ncwadi inelizwi leendudumo ezisixhenxe kwanayo yonke eyiVangeli engunaPhakade kwaye uwavakalise amaZwi am ngokuthembekileyo njengoko wayalelwayo, obo bungqina uYohane uMtyhili wabhala waza wabungqina.

Ndim lowo ungakhethi buso bamntu, ndinguYeshua HaMashiach, kwaye nabani na oza kum eluhlangeni luka-Adam ngolo hlobo ndiya kumamkela, ngaphandle kwabo bathatha uphawu lwerhamncwa ngobuyatha, okanye abaziguqula ngolo hlobo. Inene, inene bonke abeza kum ngolo hlobo abaguqukela ekuphileni ubomi bobulungisa baya kuvuzwa ngonaphakade kwaye ubunzima bomvuzo wenu bungunaphakade.

Kwaye ke zihlobo zam, usindiso lusisipho sobomi obungunaphakade, endathi mna Yeshua HaMashiach ndasizuza ngokunamathela kwam ngokuthembekileyo kumthetho kaMoses nangedini lam lanaphakade lam njengeMvana kaThixo engenasono negqibeleleyo. Ngoko ke, bonke abeza kum ngenguquko epheleleyo nentobeko nentobelo ndiya kubabuyisela kum nakuYahweh kwaye ndiya kubanika imisebenzi emininzi yobulungisa abeya kufuna uYahweh bayifeze kwiinjongo zakhe ezingunaphakade nezingapheliyo. Ngoko ke, isipho sobomi obungunaphakade sisezandleni zam kubo bonke abacela ngobulali nangenguquko nabaza kum ngamaxesha anikiweyo enguquko.
