Kwabanyanisekileyo KuKristu Yesu!

Phulaphulani ilizwi leNkosi yenu Engunaphakade, uYeshua HaMashiach, kuba ndingulowo unibizele eluzukweni nasesulungwini ngendlela yokuzalwa okutsha enitywinwe ngako ngoMoya wam oyiNgcwele wesithembiso. Ewe, ndinenze abantwana boThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade, uYahweh ngokwakhe, kwaye akukho namnye onjengaye! Ngoko ke, qondani, zithandwa zam, ukuba niphakathi kwabo ndibatywinileyo ndibalungiselela loo mini yenkululeko eniya kuthi ngayo nime ngokupheleleyo ebulungiseni endiya kunambathisa bona kwaye abaninzi baya kuthwala umvuzo omkhulu ngenxa yokunyaniseka kwabo, nangona abanye baya kulahlekelwa.

Qhubekani nisukela ngamandla emgomeni, ngenxa yebhaso lobizo oluphakamileyo lukaThixo kuKristu Yesu, ukuze nifumane umvuzo wenu opheleleyo ngeendlela endizibeke iinyawo zenu kuzo, ewe, iindlela zenkonzo apho ndiyalathise amanyathelo enu enkonzweni kum apha emhlabeni ngamaxesha am obabalo. Kwaye ningumsebenzi wezandla zam, nidalelwe kuKristu Yesu imisebenzi elungileyo, awayimisela kwangaphambili uThixo, ukuze nihambe kuyo.

Kwaye kunjalo ngokwenene, zithandwa zam, ukuba ndingulowo unininike injongo engunaphakade kum, iNkosi yenu engunaphakade. Ndingulowo ukumanyano nenjongo egqibeleleyo noYahweh, uMkhulu onguMNA NDINGUYE, ngoko ke ukuba uThixo unakum, zithandwa zam, ngubani na onokunichasa? Kutheni ninikezela kwaye nigxilise ingqondo yenu kwizinto ezibuthathaka nezingento ezifuna ukunilahlekisa kwisandla sam esinamandla esingunaphakade, nto leyo ukulahlekiswa kuyo kungenakwenzeka ukuba nigcina iliso lenu linye ligxile kum, uKristu Yesu iNkosi yenu enyukileyo!

Kwaye ke ndisafundisa nanamhlanje ukuze bonke abaneendlebe zokuva bathobele ukuba bangaziqwebeli ubutyebi emhlabeni, apho inundu nomhlwa zonakalisayo, nalapho amasela aqhekezayo, ebe. Kodwa ziqwebeleni ubutyebi ezulwini, apho kungonakalisi nundu namhlwa, nalapho amasela angaqhekeziyo, engebi. Kuba apho bukhona ubutyebi benu, yoba lapho nentliziyo yenu. Isibane somzimba liliso: Ukuba ngoko iliso lakho linye, umzimba wakho wonke uya kuzala kukukhanya.

Zithandwa zam, aniwuqondi na umzekeliso wam? Ubutyebi benu ziingcinga zenu, kwaye ke ndiniyalela ukuba nigcine iingcinga zenu kwizinto eziphezulu ukuze ekuhambeni kwenu nivelise kwaye nihambe kwimisebenzi elungileyo endiyimisele iinyawo zenu ukuba ziyilandele ngentliziyo enye. Ngoko ke, ndenza uPawulos wabhalela abaseKorinte esithi: Kodwa ndiyoyika hleze, njengoko inyoka yamlukuhlayo uEva ngobuqhetseba bayo, neengqondo zenu zonakaliswe ekululameni okukuKristu.

Ngoko ke, gxilisani iimvakalelo zenu kwizinto eziphezulu, hayi kwizinto ezisemhlabeni. Kuba nifile, kwaye ubomi benu bufihliwe nam kuThixo. Xa mna, Kristu Yesu, ongubomi benu, ndithe ndabonakala, niya kubonakala nani nam eluzukweni. Ngoko ke, zithandwa zam, qondani ukuba niziinkanyiso zehlabathi, ewe, bonke abo bangabantwana boYise weziKhanyiso! Ngoko ke, gxilisani ingqondo yenu yonke kum, iNkosi yenu engunaphakade yeeNkosi noKumkani wooKumkani kwaye ndiya kusebenza ngamandla emoyeni wenu ukuze ukukhanya kwam okungunaphakade kukhanye kumzimba wam eniyinxalenye yawo, kwaye ke izitya zam zodongwe ziyakhanya kwaye zibonise uzuko lukaThixo ongunaphakade ebusweni bukaYesu Kristu!

Kwaye ke ndiyabuza kuwe mtshakazi wam, kwaye ke ndiyabuza kuwe mzimba wam, kwaye ke ndiyabuza kwabanyanisekileyo bam kuKristu, kutheni nivumela iintshaba zenu ukuba ziphazamise kwaye zahlule ingqondo yenu eluzukweni nakubukhazikhazi bam? Qondani ukuba abawileyo baqonda ngokupheleleyo ukuba inkululeko yenu epheleleyo isondele njengokuphefumla kwenu okulandelayo, kwaye ke bafuna ukunibela uvuyo lwenu kuba bayazi ukuba akunakwenzeka ukunixwitsha ezandleni zam ezingunaphakade, nangona bezama.

Ngoko ke, ningoyiki nto kwiintshaba zenu, nto leyo engumqondiso ocacileyo wentshabalalo kuzo, kodwa kuni ilusindiso, kwaye olo luvela kuThixo. Musa ukuyiphambukisa ingqondo yakho kwinkcaso yabawileyo, zithandwa zam, ungajikeli ngasekunene nangasekhohlo. Kodwa hlalani kuloo ndlela icutheneyo nemxinwa, ewe, indlela yosindiso enifunda ngayo ngam mna Yesu waseNazarete kuba ndingulowo unifundisa iindlela zosindiso enisindiswa ngazo ngokugqibeleleyo njengoko nihlanjululwa egazini lam elihlawulelayo kuso sonke isono ukuze niphile ubulungisa bukaThixo kuKristu kuni ithemba lozuko!

Qondani ukuba njengoko nihamba ngenxa yam, uSathana uyanihlasela njengoko efuna ukumisa iinzame zenu aze aniphambukisele kwimisebenzi yakhe yomgunyathi yobuyatha. Kwanaleyo vesi yentlalo yomgunyathi engayihoyiyo inguquko nokuxolelwa kwezono ngenxa yemisebenzi elungileyo. Kodwa kunjalo, ukondla abalambileyo, ukwambathisa abahambileyo, ukunika amakhaya abangenamakhaya nokunye okuninzi yimisebenzi elungileyo efanelekileyo ukuhlangabezana neentswelo ezikhoyo.

Kodwa mna, iMvana kaThixo, andizange ndizinikele kube kanye, konke, ngonaphakade njengedini lokugqibela lesono kuloo mthi umbi eKalvari ukuze ndihlawulele imisebenzi elungileyo, kwaye noYahweh akazange andivuse kwabafileyo ukuze ndibe ngumboneleli weempahla zeli hlabathi kwabo basweleyo, kodwa ndaxhonywa emthini ndaza ndaphalaza ithontsi lam lokugqibela legazi kwintlungu ekrakra endaweni yethu nanjengentlawulo yesono neziphumo zezono zaboni.

Mna, Kristu Yesu, ndinikhulule esiqalekisweni soMthetho, ndenziwa isiqalekiso ngenxa yenu, (kuba kubhaliwe kwathiwa, Uqalekisiwe wonke umntu oxhonywe emthini) kanti elo lixabiso elalingafanelanga ndilihlawule, kuba mna, Yesu waseNazarete, ndingulowo ubumsulwa bakhe bukhona nanamhlanje! Kwaye andinaso isono onke amaxesha ngonaphakade kuba ndisoloko ndisenza ukuthanda kukaBawo. Ngoko ke, phumulani kwimisebenzi endininike yona ukuba niyenze kwaye nithabathe idyokhwe yam phezu kwenu ngokupheleleyo, kuba idyokhwe yam ilula, nomthwalo wam ulula.

Amaxesha omgwebo omkhulu afikile phezu kwehlabathi lonke, ewe, amaxesha embandezelo kaYakobi. Kodwa phambi kokuba imbandezelo kaYakobi ifike ngokupheleleyo ehlabathini kufuneka ukuba abanyanisekileyo bam baxhwilwe kwingqumbo ezayo kwaye mna, Kristu Yesu, ndingulowo uzayo emafini nelizwi lesiphatha-zithunywa nangeXilongo likaThixo kwaye abafileyo kuKristu baya kuvuka kuqala kwaye nina baphilayo niseleyo niya kuxhwilelwa emafini kunye nabo kwaye ningene kwigumbi letrone likaYahweh uYihlo wenu ongunaphakade.

Kwaye ke imini inifikele, bantwana bam, ewe, bonke abo banyanisekileyo kuKristu Yesu. Ngoko ke, shiyani ngasemva iinkathazo zeli hlabathi liwileyo kwaye nigxilise ingqondo yenu yonke ngokupheleleyo kwiNkosi yenu engunaphakade, uYeshua HaMashiach. Ewe, ingqondo yenu yonke engohlulwanga kuba umtyholi ufuna abo anokubaginya kwaye njengengonyama egqumayo ujikeleza ngengxolo yakhe engenaxabiso kodwa qondani ukuba ulutshaba oloyisiweyo zithandwa zam kuba ndimloyise ngokupheleleyo kwaye uloyiso olupheleleyo lolwam ngokupheleleyo ukusukela kumazwi am okugqibela endawathetha ndisenyameni kuloo mthi umbi: Kugqityiwe!

Kwaye ke kunjalo ukuba ngoku ndihamba ngokupheleleyo ngenxa yenkululeko yenu epheleleyo kwaye ndiya kuwugqibezela loo msebenzi ugqibeleleyo ngaphakathi komntu ngamnye wenu apho niya kudalwa ngokutsha kwangoko nangokupheleleyo njengesidalwa esitsha nesigqibeleleyo nesingenasono. Ewe, isidalwa esigqibeleleyo esingenakuze sonakaliswe kukonakala, esinozuko olungasokuze luphele, nesinokukhanya okungasokuze kufiphale. Kuba niya kwenziwa nifane nam, Kristu Yesu, kuba ndingumAdam wokugqibela, kwaye ndingumoya ophilisa okwangumphathi wosindiso lwenu.

Ngoko ke, khathalelani izinto zomoya! Ningavumeli iintolo ezivuthayo zomngendawo zitshise indlela yazo zingene kubutyebi benu. Qondani ukuba uSathana uya kufuna ukutshabalalisa ukugxila kwenu okunye kwiNkosi yenu engunaphakade ngobutyebi bakhe obungenaxabiso nobolileyo obusoloko bungumgunyathi oqinisekisayo wento yokwenene. Ngoko ke, yalani iingcinga nemibono yakhe kwaye nizilahle zonke iindlela zakhe zokuvukela kuba ezakhe ziindlela zokufa kwaye ngokuqinisekileyo ufuna ukwabelana ngelifa lakhe kuloo malangatye ashushu kakhulu esulfure ebolileyo nabani na okhetha elo chibi lomlilo lanaphakade, kodwa eso siphelo asinakwenzeka kuni zithandwa zam kwaye ndinitywine ngonaphakade ngetywina elingunaphakade likaThixo ophilayo.

Ngoko hlalani kum ukuze ndizenze zicace iindlela zenu, kwaye ndinibonise ngokucacileyo imigibe uSathana aya kufuna ukubambisa ngayo abanyanisekileyo bam. Hlalani kum, zithandwa zam, kuba kuKristu akukho mgibe unokubekwa kwaye umgibe womthiyeli awunakubekwa ngobuqhetseba okanye ngamandla. Ngoko ke, londolozani iingcinga zenu kwaye nigcine iintloko neentliziyo zenu zizele ngamazwi am angcwele! (Kuba izixhobo zokulwa kwethu asizezenyama, kodwa zinamandla ngoThixo ekudilizeni iinqaba.) Sidiliza iingcinga, nayo yonke into ephakamileyo eziphakamisayo ngokuchasene nolwazi lukaThixo, kwaye sithimbela yonke ingcinga ekuthobeleni uKristu.

Kuba asizamani nenyama negazi, kodwa sizamana neziphathamandla, namagunya, nabaphathi behlabathi, iinkosana zobumnyama beli hlabathi, nomoya wenkohlakalo, osezindaweni eziphezulu. Ngesi sizathu, thabathani sonke isikrweqe sikaThixo, ukuze nibe nako ukumelana ngomhla ombi, kwaye, nigqibile zonke izinto, nime niqinile.

Ewe, yimani niqinile kwiNkosi yenu engunaphakade bantwana bam abancinci kuba amaxesha anifikele. Nangona abaninzi benu befuna ukubuyela kwimeko yokozela, kanye njengoko amaza eli hlabathi athomalalisayo ngobuxoki efuna ukunilalisa kwinkohliso enkulu. Ningaweli kumakhubalo abo, kwaye nisuke ekuthakatheni nakumayelenqe abo. Sukani koko bakubiza ngokuba yimincili, kuba akufanelekanga ukuba abantwana abangcwele boThixo ophezukonke abangabakaKristu Yesu ngokupheleleyo bonwatyiswe sisono kwakhona.

Ndiqinisekisile kwabanyanisekileyo bam ukusondela nobunyaniso obupheleleyo bokubonakala kwam okusondelayo ukuze ndiqokelele abanyanisekileyo bam kubukhuseleko bamazulu. Ndinike namaphupha, imibono, namazwi amaninzi kubantwana abancinci abathandwa kakhulu. Zithandwa zam, esi siganeko siziphosa ngokupheleleyo kuni kwaye sinifikele kwaye sisondele kunokuba nizivumela ukuba nicinge. Ngoko ke, zilungiseleleni mna. Qhubekani niphuma kuso sonke isono kwaye nivume izono zenu kum ngokupheleleyo kwaye nivume iziphoso zenu omnye komnye.

Ndibatywinile abanyanisekileyo bam kuloo moya ungcwele wesithembiso kwaye ke ndibonelele nge-oyile yezibane zenu, ewe, bonke abo bamkele ngokukhululekileyo kum isipho sam esingunaphakade sosindiso nobomi obungunaphakade nelifa elingunaphakade kuKristu Yesu iNkosi engunaphakade yeeNkosi noKumkani wooKumkani! Khangelani emazulwini zithandwa zam kwaye kwabo balindele ngokuthobela nangokunyaniseka ukubonakala kwam baya kubona ngamehlo abo uzuko lokubonakala kwam, kuba le nto ayiyi kwenziwa emfihlekweni kwaye akunakwenzeka ukuba uSathana alufihle uzuko lokubonakala kwam, nangona eya kuxoka aze afune ukugubungela ukubonakala kwam ngabaphambukeli bomgunyathi nemfazwe yehlabathi.

Ngoko ke, lindelani mna ngomonde kwaye niyenze ngokupheleleyo imisebenzi endininike yona ukuba niyigqibe ukuze nigqibe ngokupheleleyo iindlela zenu zenkonzo kwicebo lam elingunaphakade losindiso kubomi benu ngabunye. Qondani zithandwa zam ukuba ubulungisa bukaYahweh nomvuzo nelifa nemivuzo ayibalwa njengoko umntu wemvelo ebala kuba umntu wemvelo akalunganga kwaye akanabulungisa. Ngamnye wenu unikwe imisebenzi yokuyivelisa ukuze niqhubekisele phambili iinjongo zam zosindiso kwaye mna, Kristu Yesu, ndiya kugweba imisebenzi yenu eniyisebenzileyo ngedini nangenkonzo kwiNkosi yenu engunaphakade.

Qondani ukuba nonke niya kuvuzwa kakhulu ngemisebenzi yenu enyanisekileyo yenkonzo eniyisebenze ngokunyaniseka egameni lam elingcwele. Kwaye, mna, Kristu Yesu, ndingulowo ubona wonke umsebenzi owenziwa ngabanyanisekileyo kuKristu kunye nenjongo yeentliziyo kwaye ndiya kunivuza ngokugqibeleleyo. Njengoko ndandibona izityebi ziphosa izipho zazo kwindawo yobuncwane ndabona izityebi, eziphosa izipho zazo kwindawo yobuncwane. Ndabona nomhlolokazi othile olihlwempu ephosa khona iimalana ezimbini: Ndathi, Inene ndithi kuni, lo mhlolokazi ulihlwempu uphose ngaphezu kwabo bonke.

Kwaye ke ndiza kuni zithandwa zam, kanye apha kule mizuzwana yokugqibela ebalekayo phambi kokuba ndiniqokelele kum kwaye ndinikhuthaze nonke ngothando olungunaphakade lukaYahweh ngokwakhe ukuba nime niqinile kwaye nikhangele ezulwini kuba inkululeko yenu inifikele. Kwaye iliza ngalinye lentlekele liya kwanda ngamandla kwaye intshabalalo iya kwanda njengoko iliza ngalinye liwela emhlabeni. Iinimba zokuzala azipheli ngoku kwaye umhlaba uza kuzala umntwana wawo kuba emhlabeni kukho oonyana baka-Adam abangoku bangoonyana bakaThixo kanye ngendlela ka-Adam wokugqibela. Kwaye ngequbuliso bonke baya kutshintshwa ngonaphakade nangonaphakade babe kwinkululeko ezukileyo yokugqibelela kwaye baya kwambathiswa ngonaphakade uzuko olungunaphakade nolungapheliyo kwaye banekamva elingunaphakade nenjongo engunaphakade kwizicwangciso zikaYahweh ezingunaphakade.

Qondani ukuba loo namba inkulu ibomvu ivunyelwe ukuba ilungise iqonga kwaye ihambise amaqhekeza ayo endaweni kwibhodi yehlabathi. Qondani ukuba iziganeko sele ziqhubeka kwaye ikhondo langoku lehlabathi alizinzanga kwaye ngokuqinisekileyo liya kukhokelela kwintlekele epheleleyo neququzelelweyo kwaye uninzi lwezi ziganeko zihambelanayo ziya kudibana ngokuhlangana okugqibeleleyo.

Kuba linjalo ixesha lezicwangciso zikaYahweh, kwaye yonke iYod neva leYod ziya kudibana ngokuphunyezwa okugqibeleleyo nangokuvumelana, kuba nantoni na uYahweh ayibeka isandla sakhe ikhokelela ekugqibeleleni okungunaphakade nakubulungisa obungunaphakade kuba nabo bakhetha ukonakala namalangatye esohlwayo angunaphakade bagwetywa ngokulungileyo nangokugqibeleleyo.

Kwaye ke ndithi zithandwa zam, gcwalisani imicu yenu kwaye nizigcine kweyona nyulu iphakamileyo egazini lam elihlawulelayo elilungisayo nelihlanjululwayo. Gcwalisani imicu yenu ngokuzigcina kubunyulu bengcinga eziswa sisibhalo sam esingunaphakade kuba uThixo ongunaphakade, uYahweh ngokwakhe, ngulowo uthumele ilizwi lakhe waza wabaphilisa.

Ndingulowo unibizele eluzukweni nasesulungwini.

Ndingulowo unininike injongo engunaphakade kum, iNkosi yenu engunaphakade.

Ndingulowo ukumanyano nenjongo egqibeleleyo noYahweh, uMkhulu onguMNA NDINGUYE.

Ndingulowo unifundisa iindlela zosindiso enisindiswa ngazo ngokugqibeleleyo.

Andinaso isono onke amaxesha ngonaphakade kuba ndisoloko ndisenza ukuthanda kukaBawo.

NdingumAdam wokugqibela.

Ndingumoya ophilisa okwangumphathi wosindiso lwenu.

Kwaye ndingulowo uthumela amazwi evangeli yam engunaphakade ngomthunywa wam endimvusele kwaye ndamthuma ngenxa yale njongo kwaye ngomthunywa wam athunyelwe amazwi evangeli yam engunaphakade.

Inene, inene, ndingulowo uYahweh abelane naye ngetrone yakhe de iintshaba zam zenziwe isihlalo seenyawo zam, ewe, de ndibuyisele indalo ekhululwe ngokupheleleyo ezandleni zikaThixo ongunaphakade wobulungisa, ukuze ngokwenene uYahweh abe yinto yonke kuyo yonke into kwaye yonke into ibe ngu-ewe no-amen.

Kwaye ndim, uYeshua HaMashiach, othumele amazwi evangeli engunaphakade ukuze kuthotyelwe bonke abaziindlalifa zobomi obungunaphakade kwaye la mazwi aya kugcinwa kwiintlekele nemigwebo ezayo kwaye la mazwi aya kufumana amehlo, iindlebe, neentliziyo zabo athunyelwe kubo. Ngokuqinisekileyo, ndiyeza ngokukhawuleza. Amen. Ubabalo lweNkosi engunaphakade uYesu Kristu luphalazwe ngokobuninzi phezu kwabo bonke abanyanisekileyo kuKristu, AMEN noAmen.
