Ingqina eliMbaxa: uSirayeli
Okukrakra Okumnandi
Agasti 18, 2024

IBhabhiloni Enkulu iwile, iwile!

Thobelani amazwi am ngeentliziyo zenu, niweve amazwi am, nivumele amazwi am angene ezintliziyweni zenu: kuba kwanabona bathembekileyo phakathi kweBandla lam kwanaye uMtshakazi wam bajongene nezilingo zokuzithethelela nokungakholwa. Ndive kakuhle, niboleke iindlebe zenu ukuze niqonde amaxesha eniphila kuwo, kuba akukho namnye kuni owakhe waphila kwiziganeko zangoku ezilungelelaniswe ngokuchasene nehlabathi ukuze ligwetywe. Kuba umgwebo nentlekele zilapha yaye sele ziqhubeka, nangona ezi ziziminya nje zokuzala, ezingapheliyo ngoku kwaye zenzelwe ukulumkisa abo baphila benganakile. Kanti, naphakathi kwabanyulwa bam nabathembekileyo kukho abo bangakhetha ngabom ubumfama kunenyani kwaye bakhethe ngabom ukuva ingxolo endaweni yomyalelo ocacileyo nentuthuzelo eziswa ngamazwi eNkosi.

Kuba mna, Yeshua HaMashiach, kwanaye uYesu Krestu ngolwimi lwesiNgesi, ndingulowo uthumela la mazwi ngomkhonzi wam endimvuseleleyo ndamthuma ndamzimela kumphongolo wam ngexesha elifana neli. Kwaye akumangalisi kuba zinjalo iindlela zam, kuba mna Krestu Yesu, ndingulowo ukwaziyo ukuvusa abapostile, abaprofeti, abavangeli, abefundisi, nabafundisi-ntsapho kwizihlwele ezingenazo iincwadi zokundlela zeli hlabathi liwileyo bengengabo nabo bazidibanise nesivumelwano seenkonzo zonqulo noSathana ngokwakhe ababonakala bengabaphathiswa bobulungisa. Kwaye akumangalisi kuba uSathana ngokwakhe uziguqula abe yingelosi yokukhanya, ngoko ke asiyonto inkulu ukuba abaphathiswa bakhe baziguqule ngokungathi bangabaphathiswa bobulungisa, kuba ngokuqinisekileyo bafuna ukumanya bonke phantsi kwebhanile yabo yehlabathi elinye leenkonzo zonqulo.

Kwaye njengoko umhlobo wam uYohane umBhaptizi watshoyo kwiminyaka emininzi eyadlulayo xa wayelungisa iindlela endandiza kuhamba kuzo: Musani ukuthi ngaphakathi kwenu, SinoAbraham njengobawo wethu: kuba ndithi kuni, uThixo unako ukuvusa abantwana baka-Abraham kula matye. Kwaye ngoku izembe libekiwe engcanjini yemithi: ngoko ke wonke umthi ongavelisi siqhamo silungileyo uyanqulwa, uphoswe emlilweni.

Fundani niqonde kwaye nicele ubulumko, bantwanana bam, niwasebenzise la mazwi kumaxesha enu. Kuba ndithetha ngokucacileyo kuni ukuba andithumanga abanamandla beli hlabathi njengabathunywa bam nabaprofeti, kwaye andifuni zincwadi zokundlela zehlabathi, uGqirha lo noMfundisi laa, izibizo abantu abazinika zona ukuze babonakale bengcwele. Kuba umntu owileyo usoloko efuna ukuzukisa inyama yakhe kwimpumelelo yehlabathi, efuna nokuma elilungisa phambi koThixo ngokwakhe enyameni eyonakeleyo ekungekho mntu unokuyihlambulula, ngaphandle kwam Yesu waseNazarete. Kwaye ke ndiyabuza ukuba zeziphi iincwadi zokundlela zehlabathi endandiziphethe ngexesha lobulungiseleli bam kwaYuda, xa ndandingathunyelwanga ngaphandle kwezimvu ezilahlekileyo zendlu kaSirayeli? Asibanga sisikhubekiso na kwabangakholwayo phakathi kwabantu bakowethu xa babebuza ukuba buvela phi obu bulumko nemisebenzi emikhulu kulo mntu? Asinguye na lo unyana womchweli?

Kwaye njengoko kubhaliwe kunjalo kuyazaliseka: Kodwa uThixo ukhethe izinto zobuyatha zehlabathi ukuze adanise izilumko; kwaye uThixo ukhethe izinto ezibuthathaka zehlabathi ukuze adanise izinto ezinamandla; Nezinto ezidelekileyo zehlabathi, nezinto ezicukucezweyo, uzikhethile uThixo, ewe, nezinto ezingekhoyo, ukuze enze into engekhoyo izinto ezikhoyo: Ukuze kungabikho nyama izukiswayo phambi kwakhe. Ngoko ke yivani amazwi am okholo athethwa ngemilebe yabam abathembekileyo kwaye ndithe ngaphambili, kwaye ngoku ndithi kwakhona ukuba abaprofeti bam, abaninzi, abaphikisani nesibhalo esingcwele, kwaye abaphikisani nam, Krestu Yesu iNkosi enyukileyo!

Qondani, nina bokholo oluncinane, ukuba abaprofeti bam bathetha ngazwi-nye, kwaye bathetha amazwi endibanika wona. Anilizazi na ilizwi leNkosi yenu? Angamazwi asemzini na kuni, bantwanana bam? Noyika inyaniso? Kuba ndiliLizwi likaThixo elenziwe inyama elihleli phakathi kwenu kwaye ndingulowo unikele ngomoya wam onamandla endiwuthululele phezu kwayo yonke inyama kula maxesha okugqibela njengoko kuthenjisiwe. Kuba kubhaliwe: oonyana benu neentombi zenu baya kuprofeta, nabafana benu baya kubona imibono, namadoda enu amadala aya kuphupha amaphupha: Nakubakhonzi bam nakubakhonzazana bam ndiya kuthulula ngaloo mihla uMoya wam; kwaye baya kuprofeta: Kwaye ndiya kubonisa imimangaliso ezulwini phezulu, nemiqondiso emhlabeni phantsi; igazi, nomlilo, nomphunga womsi: Ilanga liya kujika libe mnyama, nenyanga ibe ligazi, phambi kokuba ifike loo mini inkulu nedumileyo yeNkosi.

Ngoko ke yivani amazwi am kwaye namkele amazwi am kuba ayinyaniso kwaye yonke into iya kuzaliseka, kwanayo yonke iyod nayo yonke intlontlo yeyod iya kwenzeka kuba lonke ilizwi likaThixo linikelwe ukuba lithotyelwe kwaye lonke ilizwi likaThixo liya kuzaliseka ngokugqibeleleyo kungekho nto isilelayo, kuba iindlela zikaYahweh zigqibelele kwaye nantoni na abeka isandla sakhe ukuba ayifeze ivelisa ukugqibelela okumangalisayo. Anizazi na iindlela zikaYihlo, bantwanana bam?

Ngoko ke yivani ilizwi lam, nina bam ababiziweyo abathembekileyo nabanyuliweyo, kwanalo iBandla lam kwanaye uMtshakazi wam! Niza kuqhubeka niwatyeshela amazwi am kwaye nisuke kwilizwi lam nithande ubuxoki behlabathi? Niza kulandela ilizwi labawileyo njengoko nichitha ubomi benu nijonge ngokungenangqondo kwiscreen? Ndiyanicenga, yivani ilizwi lam kwaye ndithi kuni, kutheni iintliziyo zenu zinikhathaza? Aniqondi na ukuba ndingulowo uhleli ephethe imfutho esandleni evuselela amadangatyhe okuhlanjululwa ukuze ndisuse inkunkuma kwizinyithi ezixabisekileyo phakathi kobutyebi obuninzi obungenaxabiso bukaYahweh?

Kwaye yenza njalo imisebenzi yezandla zam ezintliziyweni nasempilweni yenu zithandwa zam: kuba ndingulowo ususa ukungakholwa, ndingulowo ususa iindlela zenu zokungathembeki, ndingulowo unihlanjulula ngokupheleleyo kulo lonke uhlobo lokonakala kwenyama nobubi njengoko nisiza kum nivume izono neziphoso zenu ukuze ndinokunihlanjulula ngokupheleleyo egazini lam lentlawulelo ukuze nime ningenabala phambi kwetrone kaYahweh xa ndiya kuninikela ngokupheleleyo kuBawo ngokobuqu kwikamva lenu elikufuphi kakhulu.

Kanti, uninzi lwenu lufuna ukubuyela kubomi benu bakudala xa ndingakhange ndenze intando kaYahweh ngokwexesha lenu nolindelo lwenu. Kuba kunjalo, kwanabona bafundi bam abasenyongweni nabapostile babuyela ekulobeni emva kokubethelelwa kwam nokuvuka kwam, kanti ndabenza abalobi babantu. Kwaye ke ndiza kunihlanganisa kwakhona kwisandi selizwi lam, kwaye aniziqondi ngokwenene iingozi eziningqongileyo kweli hlabathi likhohlakeleyo langoku. Kwaye ke ndinibizela kum kula maxesha okugqibela ukuze nize ngokupheleleyo kum kwaye niphume ngokupheleleyo esonweni nasekungakholweni, kuba ndingulowo unibizele esidimeni nasenyanisweni, nangona uninzi lwenu lusathanda ukubuyela eYiputa.

Kwaye ke ndinicebisa ukuba nithathele iingqalelo izifundo ezibhalwe kudala ezinika isilumkiso nobulumko kwizilumko. Kuba bathi kuMoses: Ngenxa yokuba kwakungekho mangcwaba eYiputa, usikhuphile na ukuze sife entlango? Kutheni usiphathe ngolu hlobo, usikhupha eYiputa? Kutheni nisalangazelela imihla yakudala? Nisathakathwe na kukuphila iziyolo zesono? Kutheni nifuna ukuhlala phakathi kweentombi eziziziyatha xa ndimi phezu kwenu ndinika isandla sokunibuyisela ezinyaweni zenu, kuba niwile ningazi kwaye niseluthulini lomhlaba, kuba uthuli luya kuba yinyama yenyoka. Ngoko ke vukani nina baleleyo nivuthulule uthuli lomhlaba kwaye nivuke kubukhazikhazi basezulwini kwaye nibe nokholo kumazwi am okholo athe athethwa ngabathembekileyo ngemilebe yokholo!

Kwaye ke qondani okwenzekayo ngoku kwaye niqonde amaxesha eniphila kuwo, kuba abaninzi bazivumelile ukuba balaliswe kwaye bakholelwe kumazwi angenakholo kaSathana njengoko esebeza ubuxoki obuvakala bumnandi obunokukhohlisa nabona banyulwa ukuba bakhetha ukukholelwa kubuxoki bakhe. Qondani ukuba uSathana, kwanaye umtyholi okhohlisa ihlabathi lonke, ngoku uphoselwe phantsi emazulwini kwaye ubotshelelwe emhlabeni ngenxa yomgwebo ozayo ongalibazisiyo.

Kwaye ke kukho ikhefu apho abawileyo bavunyelweyo ukuba bahambise iziqwenga zabo endaweni yazo kwibhodi yechess yehlabathi, nekhefu apho imiphefumlo yokugqibela eshiyekileyo ngoku ingena kwiTyeya yobomi obungunaphakade kum Krestu Yesu iNkosi yabo evukileyo noMsindisi. Kanti, uninzi lwenu kwingqiqo yenu engenakholo lugqiba kwelokuba amazwi am abubuxoki kwaye ngokobuyatha nigqibe kwelokuba ninexesha lokubuyela kubomi benu bakudala besono neziyolo. Ngaphandle kokuba, ngequbuliso, abathembekileyo baya kuthathwa baye kwindawo yokusabela kwigumbi letrone likaYahweh ngokwakhe ngomvuzo omkhulu nelifa elingunaphakade, kwaye abathembekileyo baya kutshintshwa ngonaphakade.

Niza kuhlala phakathi kwabathembekileyo? Ninako na ukuthobeka kokubona ukungathembeki kwenu kwaye niguquke ukuze nibe phakathi kwabathembekileyo? Ukuba nikhetha ukubona, mna Krestu Yesu, ndiya kunibonisa. Ngoko kutheni nityhafa ekwenzeni okulungileyo? Nigqibe kwelokuba abo ndinisa kubo nabo ndibazisa kuni abafanelanga ubomi obungunaphakade? Kwaye ke ndinicebisa ukuba nibonise inceba nobabalo olufanayo kumntu okufuphi nani endilubonisileyo kuni kweli khefu lokugqibela phambi kokuba intshabalalo yehle. Kwaye ngokwenza njalo niya kusindisa abaninzi, kuba nisengabathunywa bam. Ngoko ke vulani imilomo yenu ngokholo, ningathandabuzi nto, kwaye ndiya kuphefumlela ngaphakathi kwenu amazwi eniya kuwathetha.

Kwaye ke bantwanana bam kwanabathembekileyo bam bayaxhalaba kwaye abanamonde, kwanakweli khefu lokugqibela phambi kokuba iBandla lam noMtshakazi wam bathathwe baye emazulwini. Kanti, njengoko kwakunjalo entlango xa uMoses wadibana noYahweh eSinayi ukuba abantwana bavuka batya basela badlala, kwaye bazenzela ithole legolide ukuze balinqule endaweni yoThixo ophilayo, obukho bakhe babengavumelekanga kodwa bema kufutshane babona uzuko lobukho bukaThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade eSinayi! Kwaye ngendlela efanayo njengoko uMoses wathethelela abantwana, nam ndiyathethelela bonke abaziindlalifa zosindiso olungunaphakade, ukuze bonke ababiziweyo beze ngokupheleleyo elusindisweni nasekuguqukeni kwiindlela zabo ezimbi nezingenakholo. Buyelani kwilizwi lam kwaye nihlale kumakhusi endininike wona, kwaye ndiya kunikhusela kumgwebo ongeyenu. Anditshongo na kuni ukuba niphume phakathi kwabo nahlukane? Thobelani ilizwi lam!

Kwaye ke umgwebo ngoku uwela emhlabeni kwaye umgwebo ulungiselelwe kwakudala abangakholwayo nabanentamo elukhuni, kwanabo ngokuzithandela okanye ngokungazi babambelele kwimvukelo kaSathana. Kuba abaninzi, abaninzi, abaninzi abantu baya kukhetha ngokuzithandela amadangatye enzelwe umtyholi neengelosi zakhe endaweni yokusuka esonweni beze kubomi obungunaphakade ngokubiza uYesu waseNazarete ukuze basindiswe. Kwaye kuya kuba njalo, kuba u-El Shaddai, kwanaye uThixo uSomandla, unike inkululeko yokuzikhethela eluntwini ukuze uluntu lukhethe ukuphila ngobulungisa nangoxolo phambi koYahweh, okanye lukhethe intshabalalo engunaphakade kwelo chibi likhulu lomlilo oluhlaza okwesibhakabhaka elenzelwe abawileyo apho impethu yabo ingafiyo. Kwaye amaxesha omgwebo angoku.

Ngoko ke ndixelela bonke abaya kuva ngeziganeko eziya kwenzeka phambi kokuba zenzeke. Ezi ziganeko azimfihlo kodwa zivakaliswe ngabaprofeti bam nabakhonzi bam iminyaka emininzi ngoku, kwanabo bonke endibathumele kwiBhabhiloni eyimfihlo. Kuba amaxesha oMgwebo waloo nkazimazi inkulu alapha, kwaye irhamncwa elimthweleyo ngoku liya kumtshabalalisa ngokupheleleyo. Kuba uThixo ukufakile ezintliziyweni zeempondo ezilishumi ukuba zizalisekise intando yakhe, kwaye bayamthiya unkazimazi, kwaye baya kuqhubeka bemenza isizwe esingenamntu kwaye bemenza aze, kwaye baya kudla inyama yakhe, kwaye baqhubeke bemtshisa ngomlilo. Kwaye ke iBhabhiloni eyimfihlo iya kwenziwa isizwe esingenamntu ngokupheleleyo kwaye ihlaziswe ukuze bonke babone.

Izixeko zayo ezazikhe zaba zikhulu ezaziyimizekelo yokusebenza nzima nokuvelisa nentabalala ka-El Shaddai ngoku zihleli zingamabhodlo nenzala yayo ikhotyokiswe ziziyobisi kwaye ihlala kubumdaka kwizitrato zayo ezicekisekayo. Kwaye yenziwe yaze njengoko ukhuselo lwayo lususwa lusiwa kude kwaye ubutyebi namandla ayo anikwa ezandleni zeentshaba zayo zama-Edom ezizenza ngathi zingabahlobo. Unkazimazi omkhulu owalwa nazo zonke iintlanga awayethunyelwe ukuba azoyise ngoku ubotshelelwe, kwaye akanakhuselo kwaye ukulungele ukoyiswa ngokupheleleyo. Kwaye yonke inyama iya kudliwa lusizi kwaye bonke abathinjwayo baya kubulawa kwaye abo bakwaziyo ukukhonza iinjongo zerhamncwa baya kusiwa ebukhobokeni kwaye abanye baya kubulawa, nangona intsalela iya kusinda. Kwaye uyatsha kwaye sele kukho imililo emininzi emideni yakhe nakumazwe abamelwane bakhe abasemantla ababelana ngezibetho zakhe. Kanti ezi ziziminya nje zokuzala kwaye asiyomigwebo yokugqibela kodwa sisilumkiso kubo bonke ngentshabalalo yabo ezayo. Kuba ngequbuliso iya kwenzeka, kwaye ngequbuliso iya kwenzeka. Iya kwenzeka kuphela kwaye kanye xa ndihlutha abathembekileyo bam emhlabeni ndibasa endaweni ekhuselekileyo kwaye ngequbuliso ndiya kuhlanganisa ingqolowa kaYahweh kwisisele sakhe esingunaphakade, kuba amaxesha okuvuna alapha. Kanti asisovuno lokugqibela, kodwa sesokuqala kwezininzi, kwaye ke umgwebo uqala endlwini kaThixo.

Bhabhiloni eyimfihlo, amaxesha akho afikile, kwaye imihla yakho iphelile. Mna, Yesu waseNazarete, ndikhalile iinyembezi ezingenakubalwa ngentshabalalo yakho ezayo kwaye ndithumele imiqondiso phezulu, nemimangaliso esibhakabhakeni, nemiqondiso emhlabeni kwaye kwanentlaninge yeentlekele ezenzeka ngequbuliso ngexesha lokwenene naphakathi kwayo kwaye iBhabhiloni eyimfihlo isavala iindlebe zayo kwimvukelo enenkani, kwaye ngoku iya kuzitya ngokupheleleyo iindlela zayo zokuvukela. Ngoko ke qondani, ukuba njengoko ndihlanganisa ngequbuliso abathembekileyo ndibasa endaweni ekhuselekileyo nasemvuzweni, ngokunjalo intshabalalo yequbuliso iya kuwela phezu kweBhabhiloni eyimfihlakalo njengoko umthinteli esuswa ngokupheleleyo. Yivani amazwi am kwaye niqonde ilizwi lam kuba umthinteli kuphela kwento egcina iinkanuko ezitshabalalisayo zabawileyo zithintelwe de lowo uthintelayo angabe esathintela kwaye asuswe phakathi.

Ngebhaqo intshabalalo iya kufikela ihlabathi lonke. Imida yedabi itsaliwe, yaye balungiselele imfazwe yabo yesithathu yehlabathi, kwanemfazwe yabo yokubhukuqa ngokupheleleyo iindlela zakudala nemiyalelo yakudala. Ngaloo ndlela umlilo uya kuwa ngequbuliso phezu kweBhabheli eyiMfihlo yaye izixeko zayo ziya kutshatyalaliswa. Umlilo uya kuwa ngequbuliso njengoko imijukujelwa itsho phantsi kulo lonke ilizwe layo, uyishiye itshile, ihamba ze. Ukanti izixeko zayo ezininzi ezingaselunxwemeni lwayo olunesono ziya kuwa ngamanzi aya kuza ngequbuliso aze antlitheke elunxwemeni lwayo ekhukulisa umhlaba ongaphakathi iimayile ezininzi, yaye oku kuya kwenzeka ukusuka empuma nase ntshona. Umhlaba uya kunyikima ngequbuliso ngokungalawulekiyo, yaye intshabalalo enkulu iya kufika kumazwe eBhabheli eyimfihlo, yaye amazwe aya konakaliswa aze aguqulwe. Iibhulorho zayo ziya kuwa, neendlela zayo zibe zingahambeki, kuze kusale intlango nenkqantosi. Ivumba lokufa liya kuwugubungela umhlaba wayo, nesibetho nezifo ziya kuzalisa amazwe ayo, yaye iya kuba engenakuzinceda konke konke.

Kwaye njengoko olu hlaselo lokugqibela lusenzeka, imikhosi yoMkhosi oBomvu iya kukhululwa ngokupheleleyo kubemi abaseleyo belizwe. Baya kubulala bonke abachasayo kwaye baya kubulala abaninzi abangachasiyo. Kuba abantu baseBhabheli eyimfihlo bathengiselwa ebukhobokeni ngamatyala amakhulu awafumeneyo, kuba ngokwenza inzala ngokungekho mthethweni abawileyo basebenzela ukutshabalalisa abantu abaninzi. Kwaye kangangoko abantu bayo bathembise kwaye batyikitya ubomi babo ukuze bafumane inzuzo yezinto eziphathekayo kunye nolonwabo lwesono, nabo abo bakhetha ukubakhobokisa baya kukhonza abo banetyala kubo ngomsebenzi onyanzeliswayo kwaye uphawu luya kunyanzeliswa kubo bonke abasindayo, kodwa musani ukuthatha uphawu.

Ngaloo ndlela ihlabathi elikho ngoku, liya kuthatyathelw' indawo ngequbuliso luthuthu lwamabhodlo neentsalela ezitshayo zoluntu olwalukhe lwadlamka. Ngaloo ndlela indlala iya kuhlela phezu kwamazwe, kuba iBhabheli eyiMfihlo ibangele indlala kumazwe amaninzi kwaye ngoku indlala iya kuba sisidlo sayo sangokuhlwa, kuba inike ngolo hlobo kwaye ngoku iya kwamkela ngokuphindwe kabini. Amanzi aya kuba yinto efunwayo, yaye amanzi amaninzi aya kwenziwa krakra yintshabalalo nemililo yemfazwe nangezibetho zabangangcwatywanga abaya kungcolisa ilizwe. Kwaye abaninzi baya kubandezeleka ngokungeyomfuneko baze batshabalale ekugwetyweni ngenxa yezono zabo, xa into enjalo ibingeyomfuneko kubo kodwa abo baseleyo phakathi kwabagwetyiweyo nabo baya kugwetywa, kodwa nabani na obiza egameni leNkosi yakhe kude kube sekupheleni uya kusindiswa, kodwa kuya kufuneka banyamezele kude kube sekuphefumleni kwabo kokugqibela. Kuba ukuthembeka kude kube sekupheleni bekusoloko kuyimfuneko kwabathembekileyo, kwanabantwana bakaYahweh abangabaka-Abraham abangabantwana bedinga, kuba olilungisa uya kuphila ngokholo.

Ngaloo ndlela iBhabheli eyimfihlo iya kwahlulwa kubini kanye embindini yaye amanzi aya kukhukulisa apho kwakukhe kwakho umhlaba owomileyo ochumileyo, kwanamazwe angumbindi aya kukhukuliswa ukuya kutsho embindini. Ngaloo ndlela inceba nobabalo lukaThixo ziya kususwa ngokupheleleyo emhlabeni xa umnqandi esuswa ngequbuliso yaye intshabalalo iya kutshata nentshabalalo, kuba la ngamaxesha entshabalalo apho ubukumkani bukaSathana buya kugwetywa ngokupheleleyo buze butshatyalaliswe ngokupheleleyo, kodwa kuqala baya kukhonza iinjongo zikaYahweh ekuyidleni ngokupheleleyo iBhabheli eyimfihlakalo yaye ngaloo ndlela iya kutshiswa ngokupheleleyo ize idliwe yimililo emininzi evuthayo eya kuyigubungela ize iqalise ukuhlanjululwa komhlaba. Ngaloo ndlela intshabalalo yeBhabheli eyiMfihlo iya kwenzeka ngeyure enye, kuba njengoko kubhaliwe kuya kwenziwa njalo, yaye iya kufumana isahlulo esiphindwe kabini somgwebo. Kwaye njengoko kubhaliwe, kuya kwenziwa njalo.

Kuba kwabhalwa kwakudala nguYohane uMtyhili: Ndabona esinye isiThunywa sisihla ezulwini, sinamandla amakhulu, waza umhlaba wakhanyiselwa bubuqaqawuli baso, Sadanduluka ngamandla ngelizwi elikhulu, sisithi, Iwile, iwile, iBhabheli isixeko esikhulu eso, yaza yaba likhaya leedemon, nendawo yokuhlala yayo yonke imimoya engcolileyo, nekheji yazo zonke iintaka ezingcolileyo nezithiyekileyo. Kuba zonke iintlanga zisele iwayini yomsindo wokukrexeza kwayo, nookumkani bomhlaba bakrexeze nayo, nabarhwebi bomhlaba batyeba ngobuninzi bolonwabo lwayo.

Ndeva elinye ilizwi liphuma ezulwini lisithi, Phumani kuyo, bantu bam, ukuze ningabi naxanduva ezonweni zayo, nokuze ningamkeli kwizibetho zayo. Kuba izono zayo zifikelela ezulwini, yaye uThixo ubukhumbule ubugwenxa bayo. Yibuyiseleni, njengoko nani yanibuyiselayo, niyinike okuphindwe kabini ngokwemisebenzi yayo; nasendebeni eniyizalisele yona, yizaleleni eyayo kabini. Kangangoko yazizukisayo, yahlala kulonwabo, yipheni intuthumbo nosizi olungako; kuba ithi entliziyweni yayo, Ndihleli ndingukumkanikazi, andingomhlolokazi, andisayi kubona kubalisa. Ngako oko, izibetho zayo ziya kufika ngamini-nye, ukufa, nosizi, nendlala, yaye iya kutshiswa ngomlilo; kuba uThixo oyigwebayo, yiNkosi eyomeleleyo!

Ngaloo ndlela olu hlanga lwalukhe lwaba lukhulu lwemimandla nezixeko ezininzi luya kuwa ngequbuliso, yaye isithunywa sentshabalalo okwangoku sijikeleza phezu kwesona sixeko saso sikhulu nesona sikhulu kumanzi asempuma, nekratshi nolonwabo lwaso nomona wehlabathi ziya kutshatyalaliswa ngokupheleleyo zingaphindi ziphakame. Kanjalo ke intshabalalo yayo iya kwabelwana ngayo nabo bonke abazimanye nayo, kuba ingulo nkazimayikazi mkhulu uhleli phezu kwamanzi amaninzi angabantu, nezihlwele, neentlanga, neelwimi.

Kuba umgwebo wabo awulibazisi, yaye bonke ookumkani bomhlaba bazimanye neempondo ezilishumi kuhlaselo lwabo olothusayo. Kuba iinkokeli zabo zikhonza abawileyo ngokuzithandela, kuba banjalo bonke abalawuli behlabathi. Akukho namnye ulungileyo, hayi, akukho namnye. Ezi nkokeli zisebenze iminyaka emininzi ukuzisa olu hlaselo kwaye zingcatshile iintlanga zazo ezathunyelwa ukuba zizikhohlise. Kwaye abantu ababalandela ngokuzithandela kulonwabo baya kuwa kwimililo yemfazwe, isibetho, ukufa kunye nentshabalalo. Kwaye ezi zinto zikulungele ukukhululwa ngequbuliso ngokupheleleyo emhlabeni. Ngaloo ndlela zonke iintlanga ekuthiwa zezobuKristu ziya kuwa ngekrele njengoko umgwebo uqala endlwini kaThixo. Kwaye njengoko kwakunjalo kwiintsuku zakudala xa uSirayeli wamshiyayo uYise wezikhanyiso waza wakhonza abanye oothixo, abangengoothixo ngokwendalo, nabo baya kunikelwa ezandleni zeentshaba zabo, kanye njengokuba izizwe ezilishumi elinambini zahlulwa zaza zoyiswa mandulo ngenxa yokungcatsha kwazo iminqophiso kwaye kwaziswa sisiqalekiso somthetho kwabangathobeliyo nabanentliziyo elukhuni.

Kwaye kuthuthu lwemfazwe epheleleyo nentshabalalo kuya kuvela inkqubo yokugqibela yerhamncwa, kuba inamba sele ilungiselela inkqubo yerhamncwa ukuba ivuke, kwanabo bazimanya nookumkani abalishumi abaya kunika amandla abo apheleleyo kwirhamncwa eliphuma elwandle nerhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni elingumprofeti wabo wobuxoki noMoshiach wobuxoki. Irhamncwa eliphuma elwandle liya kuphakama njengoMahdi wabo, yaye linikwe amandla okulwa nabangcwele nokuboyisa. Ngaloo ndlela irhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni linamandla afanayo nerhamncwa eliphuma elwandle, yaye lilo olu luPhondo oluNcinci olwaprofetwa ngalo mandulo nguDaniyeli.

Ngaloo ndlela la marhamncwa mabini aya kuxhotyiswa ngokupheleleyo yileyo namba inkulu ibomvu aze avuke ngokupheleleyo aze oyise aze ahambe esoyisa, yaye aya kuthi: Ngubani na ofana nerhamncwa? Yaye ngubani na onako ukulwa nerhamncwa? Ngaloo ndlela bonke kuya kufuneka bathathe uphawu lwabo abaya kuzimanya nabo kuluntu lwabo lobusathana. Kwaye ubuphambene buya kuwugubungela ngokupheleleyo umhlaba, njengoko umnqandi engasayi kubakho ukweyisela uluntu ngesono nokuthintela iinkanuko zakhe ezimbi. Ngaloo ndlela ububi obupheleleyo, obungathintelwanga buza kukhululwa emhlabeni yaye enzonzobileni kuya kuvulwa ngokupheleleyo, kuba abemi bomhlaba bamgatya uYahweh, kuba bandigatya mna, uYesu waseNazarete othunyelwe usindiso lwabo. Kodwa bazibale njengabangafanelekanga kubomi obungunaphakade baza bamgatya uYahweh xa uYahweh weza kubo ngothando, ngobulali, ngobabalo, nangeceba waza wanika uluntu isivumelwano soxolo esingunaphakade. Isivumelwano soxolo abasigatya ngoku. Ukanti, nabani na obiza egameni leNkosi kude kube sekupheleni uya kusindiswa.

Kwaye nangona amaxesha omgwebo, intshabalalo enkulu, imfazwe, indlala, isibetho nezinto ezinjalo ziya kukhululwa ngokupheleleyo emhlabeni, le veki yokugqibela kwanale veki yamashumi asixhenxe kaDaniyeli inikelwe usindiso lukaYakobi kwaye mna, uYeshua HaMashiach, ndisaya kusindisa intsalela yakwaSirayeli eya kundamkela njengoMesiya wabo wokwenene nofanelekileyo, kuba ndadalwa, ndenziwa, ndathunyelwa nguThixo uSomandla ngokuhlonipha izithembiso ezenziwa kumnqophiso nakum завещание kooyise.

Kuba uYahweh nguThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade ohleli phezulu kwaye ngoku uya kushukuma ukuze azityhile kwindalo yakhe kwaye mna, uKristu Yesu, ndiyiNkosi yeeNkosi engunaphakade noKumkani wooKumkani obukumkani bakhe bungapheliyo. Kwaye ndiya kuthumela abaprofeti bam ababini, kwanazo iziphatho zam zezibane ezibini eziya kuprofeta, kwaye ndiya kuthumela abam abali-144,000 ukuba basindise abo bathunyelwe kubo entlango kwindawo ekhethwe nguYahweh kwaye baya kondliwa baze bakhathalelwe nguYahweh. Kwaye uThixo ongunaphakade watsho njalo mandulo. Kuba nguYahweh othetha ngezinto eziseza kuba ngokungathi sezenzekile kuba zinjalo iindlela zikaThixo ongunaphakade ondinike iVangeli engunaphakade ukuba ivakaliswe phezu komhlaba ukuze usindiso olungunaphakade lufike kubo bonke abaya kubiza egameni lam elinamandla, elingunaphakade.

Mna Yeshua HaMashiach, kwanaye uKristu Yesu, ndiwathumele la mazwi ngomkhonzi wam yaye ngomkhonzi wam la mazwi athunyelwe.

Yazini ukuba ndingulowo uThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade nonaphakade anikele zonke izinto ezandleni zam ezinamandla waza wandinika igama elingaphezu kwawo onke amagama, ukuze egameni likaYesu onke amadolo aguqe ezulwini, emhlabeni naphantsi komhlaba. Kwaye ndingulowo oyiNgonyama yesizwe sakwaYuda kwaye ngobulungisa ndiza kugweba ndenze imfazwe ndenze umgwebo kwabawileyo, kwaye ilixa lokugweba selifikile ngoku. Ngaloo ndlela ndiyabacebisa abo bahleli emhlabeni ukuba balandele amazwi kaYahweh baze bamange uNyana, hleze ndibe nomsindo, nize nitshabalale endleleni, xa umsindo wam uvutha kancinane.

Banoyolo bonke abo bakholosa ngam, kwanaye uYeshua HaMashiach nabo bonke ababiza egameni lam kude kube sekupheleni baya kusindiswa, kwanabo bonke abaziindlalifa zosindiso ngaphambi kokuba kusekwe iziseko zomhlaba.
