KwiMisebenzi kaMpostile uPawulos
Epreli 24, 2024

Yivani Amazwi eNkosi: Imfihlelo Enkulu Ityhiliwe!

UYeshua HaMashiach uthi kuMtshakazi wakhe: Phulaphulani nive amazwi am, banyulwa bam, niyiqonde intetho yam, kuba inkulu imfihlelo yobuThixo! Uninzi lwenu alunangqiqo ngemfihlelo enkulu kaThixo, kanti yona isentliziyweni nakwisiseko sabalandeli beNdlela, ngokwemfundiso nangokwenza: Yeyona nto ingundoqo yobuKrestu endinidalele yona kum, kwaye nisisidalwa esitsha kuKrestu Yesu, kuba mna, Yesu Krestu, ndinenze njalo.

Kuninzi lwenu, imfihlelo enkulu kaThixo iye yalahleka ngenxa yexesha nezithethe nobuqhetseba obunenkohliso abalalele ukulukuhla ngabo. Musani ukoyika, bantwana bam, yivani amazwi am nentetho yam, kuba ndiza kunichazela yona apha kule mizuzu yokugqibela yeli phakade. Qondani, bantwana bam, ukuba ukudideka okukhulu nobuxoki obuninzi buzingeniswe ebandleni lam ziingcuka eziphangayo neemfundiso zazo zobuxoki. Balahlukanise ilizwi lam (noMzimba wam) ngobuxoki ngokusebenzisa imfundiso yamaphakama, kwaye obu bubuxoki obukhulu obuziswe ngabo bakhe izindlu zabo phezu kwentlabathi, hayi kum, ilitye lembombo eliyintloko, kuba ngaphandle kwam akukho mntu unokwakha.

Intlabathi abakhetha ukuyenza ilitye lembombo lezakhiwo zabo ziiBhayibhile ezonakeleyo ezikhokelela kumanyano lweenkolo ngeenjongo anazo umntu wesono entliziyweni yakhe, iinjongo zolawulo nentshabalalo nokufa. Intlabathi abayisebenzisileyo ukwakha yenziwe yimibhalo eyonakeleyo, imibhalo yesiGrike eye yadluliselwa isuka e-Alesandire. Landelani imibhalo evela kwimigca yaseAntiyokwe, iByzantine, kwaye ezi zikhokelela kwiTextus Receptus, utshaba olukhulu lwaseRoma. Akukho maphakama, kwaye uThixo akahlukaniswanga, nangona ndiza kuyichaza imfundiso yamaphakama njengoko ivela kwiincwadi zam. UThixo uye wasebenza noluntu esebenzisa amaphakade; fundani ukuze nazi. Ngoko ke qondani, olu lwahlukano aluveli kum kodwa lwenziwe ngobunjineli ukuze lonakalise iBandla lam kwasekuqaleni.

Imfihlelo enkulu ifihliwe kuni, bantwana bam, kodwa ayifihlakalanga, kuba ndayityhilela uMpostile uPawulos kwiminyaka emininzi yeeJubili eyadlulayo. Ifihlwe ngobuxoki, inkohliso, iimfundiso eziphosakeleyo ezingezozam; kodwa ifihlwe phambi kwamehlo, kwaye isela lingathanda ukuba ningaze niyiqaphele, kodwa yindlovu esendlwini. Kuba eyona nto imoyikisa kakhulu uSathana ngeBandla lam kukuba uyazi ukuba akanamandla okunilawula xa nihamba ngamandla am apheleleyo nobudlelwane obusobala nam. Ngoko ke, ekubeni engenakuwaphula uphawu lukaThixo endilubeke phezu kwenu, kwaye kungekho mntu unokunixwitsha ezandleni zam okanye ezandleni zikaBawo, angakhetha ukunifuthanisela entluphekweni. Hayi intlupheko yezinto eziphathekayo; iBandla lam bekungafanelanga ukuba likhuthaze ivangeli yempumelelo ephosakeleyo kuba ayiveli kum, nangona uninzi lwenu luzikholelwa ezo ntsomi. USathana unqwenela intlupheko yokomoya kuni ukuze anifihlele ubuntu benu bokwenene aze anihendele ukuba nikholelwe ukuba nisisitsalo seenyawo esingenamandla, esihlala simiselwe ukunika esinye isidlele nokubaleka njengegwala, nto leyo kukusetyenziswa gwenxa kwemiyalelo yam ukuze kulawulwe oonyana bam bangahambi ngamandla abo okwenene. Hayi, andinithumanga ukuba ninike esinye isidlele ukuze nibe ngamaxhoba kwaye noyiswe bububi; ndinithume ukuba noyise ububi ngokulungileyo kwaye nichithe imisebenzi kaMtyholi, kuba ndithunyiwe njalo, kwaye nam ndinithume njalo. Kodwa qondani: impindezelo yeyam, ngoko musani ukuziphindezela, kodwa nikani esinye isidlele xa kufanelekile ukuze kusindiswe omnye kwaye ninike indawo ingqumbo, kuba imiphefumlo emininzi isindiswe ngale ndlela.

Qondani: banikhohlisile ngobuxoki babo, kwaye babeka obu buxoki buchasene nenyaniso yam kanye ecaleni kwemibhalo engcwele. Benza oko kwimida yeeBhayibhile zokufunda; benza oko besebenzisa izichazi-magama eziphosakeleyo; benza oku ngobuqhetseba ngokungahambelani neentsingiselo ezithile zesiGrike ezisekwe kwinkcubeko yamandulo yobuhedeni hayi inyaniso yeLizwi likaThixo eliNgcwele. Ningayisebenzisa le mithombo ngamanye amaxesha, kodwa lumkani kakhulu kwaye niyeke ukuvumela umntu wemvelo azame ukunichazela imicimbi yokomoya; kuba umntu wemvelo akanakuqonda imicimbi yokomoya, kuba bubudenge kuye. Ngoko ke, yazini ukuba uninzi lwala maqhophololo axhela isibhalo alunalo ukholo kwiLizwi likaThixo kwaye alufanelekanga kumsebenzi abazinike wona kuba abanawo umoya kaKrestu kwaye abaveli kum; kuba ukuba babengabam, ngendibakhokelele enyanisweni. Ndim lowo unikitye uphawu lokuhlangulwa okupheleleyo ngamandla omoya wam ongcwele.

INkosi uYesu Krestu ithi kuMtshakazi wayo othandekayo: Ndifuna uqonde injongo yakho yokwenene, kwaye ukuba awuyiqondi imfihlelo enkulu njengoko inxulumene nawe, awusayi kuhamba ngokupheleleyo kwamandla endikubizele ukuba uhambe kuwo, kodwa uya kuncitshiswa kwiinzame zakho njengoko uninzi lwenu lunjalo. Kuyandikhathaza kwaye kuyayidabukisa intliziyo yam ukubona ukuba abantwana bam abaziqondi iimpondo zabo zokwenene kwaye abanamnqweno wokukhangela. Kufuneka nihlale nikhangele amazwi am ngenkuthalo yonke; kodwa iimfundiso zobuxoki zenza abaninzi baneliseke, kuba akukho sondlo kuzo. Kuba zibubuxoki bukaSathana, kodwa abaninzi baphila ngokutya okukhawulezayo neziselo ezinoshukela, kwaye uninzi alunayo amandla okutya ukutya endikunikileyo nokusela isiselo endikunikileyo, kuba ngokwenza njalo anisoze nilambe okanye ninxanwe kwakhona.

Ngoko yivani amazwi am, Mtshakazi wam onelisekileyo; lixesha lokuba uvukele ubizo lwakho lokwenene kwaye wenze imisebenzi endikulungiselele yona, kwaye wazi ukuba ndiya kwenza imimangaliso emininzi ngezandla zakho njengoko uhamba ngenxa yam ngokholo nolwazi lokwenene. Kuba ndathini kubulungiseleli bam kwaSirayeli ngexesha ndandihamba esidlangalaleni kwaJuda? Kuba ithini ingxelo kaLuka ngalo mbandela: Wathi kubo, Nina nikunikiwe ukuyazi imfihlelo yobukumkani bukaThixo; kodwa kwabo bangaphandle, zonke ezi zinto zenziwa ngemizekeliso. Ngoko qondani, abapostile bam bakhethwa ukuba bamkele imfihlelo enkulu kaThixo endayityhilela kubo. Bayibhala phantsi, kwaye ndiyikhusele imibhalo ukuze niyazi inyaniso yalo mbandela.

Uninzi lwenu luphulaphula amaJudaizers anganibuyisela phantsi kweTorah. Kuyathakazelisa ukuqaphela ukuba ukuba bekungekho ngenxa yemfihlelo enkulu kaThixo, uninzi lwenu belungayi kuyazi iTorah; kuba ayizange inikwe iintlanga zeeNtlanga kodwa kwaSirayeli wamandulo ukuba ayigcine, ibe ngumfundisi wabo wokubazisa kum. Kuba mna ndikukuzaliseka komthetho, kwaye abo babambelela ngamandla kumthetho kaMoses bafuna ukuzukisa inyama yabo kwaye bahambe ngobulungisa babo, kwaye abanaqiqo lokuba iTorah igcinwa ngokupheleleyo nangokuchanekileyo ngokuzichonga kwenu nam. Kuba ndim ndedwa owakwaziyo ukuwugcina umthetho, kwaye mna, Yesu waseNazarete, ndiyizalisekisile iimfuno zobulungisa zomthetho. Ndiyiqinisekisile impumelelo yam ngegazi lam, ndaza ndanihlamba emanzini aqukuqelayo omoya wam njengoko nibhaptizelwe kum.

Kuba ndathi uPawulos makaxelele ntoni abaseKorinte? Kuba ngoMoya mnye sonke sabhaptizelwa mzimbeni mnye, nokuba singamaJuda okanye siiNtlanga, nokuba singamakhoboka okanye sikhululekile; kwaye sonke senziwe ukuba sisele kuMoya mnye. Ukuba niya kuwugcina umthetho, niya kusilela kwaye niya kulahlekelwa lubizo lwenu, njengoko ndiwushwankathele umthetho kwimiyalelo emibini emikhulu: Uze uyithande iNkosi uThixo wakho ngentliziyo yakho yonke, nangomphefumlo wakho wonke, nangengqondo yakho yonke, nangamandla akho onke: lo ngumyalelo wokuqala. Nowesibini uyafana nawo, ngulo, Uze umthande ummelwane wakho njengoko uzithanda. Yazini, bantwana bam, ukuba akukho miyalelo mikhulu kunale, kuba kule miyalelo iyagcinwa.

Qondani: Ndikuchaza konke oku kwiincwadi endazithumela kumabandla am ngamaxesha adlulileyo. Ngoko qondani ukuba ndiyityhilile imfihlelo enkulu kaThixo, kwaye abaprofeti bamandulo bakhangela ngenkuthalo kwaye babuza ngale mfihlelo ababeyazi, kodwa abazange banikwe ukuba bayazi. Ndingathanda ukuba niqonde ukuba nangona isiciko seeBhayibhile zenu sisithi "Ngcwele," asibungcwele ngokwaso, kuba sisisiciko sencwadi nje. Into eyenza iBhayibhile ibe ngcwele kukuba iincwadi abazibophelelayo zineSibhalo esiNgcwele esithunyelwe ngumoya kubantwana bOsenyangweni, esithunyelwe nguYahweh, uThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade, kuba akukho namnye onjengaye. Ngoko yivani amazwi am angcwele ngomphefumlo wenu wonke! Kuba wathini uPetros kuni, bantwana bam? Usindiso olo abaprofeti baye babuza ngalo, bakhangela ngenkuthalo, abaprofeta ngobabalo olwaluzakuza kuni, bekhangela ukuba lixesha elinjani, okanye uhlobo lwexesha olwaluboniswa nguMoya kaKrestu owawukubo, xa wayengqina kwangaphambili iintlungu zikaKrestu, nozuko olwaluzakulandela.

Bantwana bam, niphila ngokungazi isibambiso sozuko oluzakulandela, kwaye ilifa lenu elipheleleyo liza kutyhilwa kuni kum, uKrestu Yesu, kuba kungekudala niya kuxhwilwa kwaye nizukiswe ngokupheleleyo. Ngoko yintoni le mfihlelo? Kwaye niyifumana phi? Ndiza kunixelela, kuba ndiyiNkosi uYesu Krestu, owalusa umhlambi, kwaye ndinguMyeni kuMtshakazi wam, kwaye niphila imfihlelo enkulu kaThixo, nangona uninzi lwenu luhamba ebumnyameni kulo mbandela. Musani ukoyika, ndiza kuninika ukuqonda, kwaye kwizilumko nabathobekileyo ndiza kunika ubulumko. Kodwa kwabo phakathi kwenu baya kuwatyeshela amazwi am, nenza oko ngokuchasene nam; kwaye nokuba ningakwicala lam okanye nichasene nam, akukho phakathi. Kuba ndinicebisa nonke ukuba nifunde kum, yivani amazwi am kwaye ningalitshitshisi ilizwi lam, kuba ndininikile ikamva nenjongo kum. NdinguMalusi olungileyo: umalusi olungileyo ubuncamela ubomi bakhe iigusha, kwaye iigusha zam ziyaliva ilizwi lam, ngoko yekani ukulandela abaqeshwa abazenza mna!

Yabonani! Umoya uthi: Kukho iimfihlelo ezininzi ngaphakathi kweMfihlelo Enkulu kaThixo! Ndiza kuqala ngeMfihlelo Enkulu, emva koko ndithethe ngezinye iimfihlelo ezingaphakathi kuyo. Phambi kokuba siqhubele phambili, sulani inkungu yemfundiso yamaphakama kwizibuko zenu kwaye nivule amehlo enu ukuze nibone: IiVangeli njengoko nizazi, imisebenzi kaMateyu, uMarko, uLuka, noYohane, zibalulekile kubo bonke. Ezi ncwadi zibonisa ukuzaliseka kwam komthetho ngokuchanekileyo, kungekho nto ishiyiweyo. Zindityhila ekuzeni kwam kwaSirayeli. Ngokuthobela iivangeli ezine njengoko zaziwa, nifunda ukuhamba njengoko ndahambayo, kuba niyalelwe njalo. Nangona kunjalo, azikho zodwa iivangeli; hayi, azikho zodwa iindaba ezilungileyo ezingam.

Kuba ndanika iindaba ezilungileyo zeMfihlelo Enkulu kaThixo kuMpostile uPawulos kwiincwadi zakhe ezibhalelwe ibandla, ezithunyelwe ndim kuye ukuze azityhile kuni. Ngaba niya kuyigculela le ngcamango? Ngaba nicinga ukuba ndishumayela enye ivangeli kwaye ndilizwi lobuxoki? Ngaba niqhele kakhulu ukulandela amazwi abaqeshwa kangangokuba anisalazi ilizwi lam? Kwabo bangagculelayo, kufuneka niphulaphule ilizwi labaseGalati xa uPawulos wathi: Kodwa ndiyaniqinisekisa, bazalwana, ukuba iVangeli eyashunyayelwa ndim, ayiveli mntwini. Kuba andizange ndiyamkele emntwini, kwaye andizange ndiyifundiswe, kodwa ngentyilelo kaYesu Krestu. UPawulos wayithetha ngokuthembekileyo inyaniso endamnika yona, kanti uninzi lwenu luyawatyeshela amazwi endamnika wona, nide niwanciphise amazwi ngeentetho ezinje: Owu, yinto nje leyo eyabhalwa nguPawulos, okanye, zezo nje iincwadi zikaPawulos; ayingomazwi kaKrestu kuba awabhalwanga ngombala obomvu! Sidenge! Uphosisile; vula amehlo akho ekukhanyeni nakwinyaniso yam! Ndamnika uPawulos ivangeli yemfihlelo. Ngaba nisizidenge kangako ukuba nikholelwe ukuba kukho imisebenzi emine emifutshane kuphela eneVangeli yam?

Ncedani nive amazwi kaKrestu Yesu, bantwana bam, kuba ndiza kufundisa abazimisele. Kuba inkulu iMfihlelo yobuThixo nevangeli engaphakathi kuyo, kuba ndazivakalisa iindaba ezilungileyo zemfihlelo. Kodwa andiyi kunishiya nelizwi lengqina elinye; yonke into mayiqinisekiswe ngomlomo wamangqina amabini okanye amathathu. Kuba incwadi endayithumela kwabaseRoma ingathanda ukuthetha nani: Ngoku kuye onamandla okukuzinzisa ngokwevangeli yam, nokushunyayelwa kukaYesu Krestu, ngokwentyilelo yemfihlelo, eyayigcinwe iyimfihlo kwasekuqaleni kwehlabathi. Iindaba ezilungileyo zikaPawulos yintyilelo yemfihlelo eyayigcinwe iyimfihlo kwasekuqaleni kwehlabathi, kanye njengokuba uMateyu, uMarko, uLuka, noYohane beziindaba ezilungileyo zokuza kwam kokuqala kwaSirayeli.

Ndim iNkosi eThembekileyo kwaye amazwi am ayinyaniso, ngoko ndibolekeni indlebe yenu kwaye ngakumbi intliziyo yenu, kuba ngentliziyo umntu ukholelwa ebulungiseni. Ndiyanicela ukuba nibe nokholo olungangokhozo lwemostade, kuba loo mbewu iya kukhula ngamandla kuni ngononophelo olufanelekileyo. Kuba wathini uPawulos kwabaseKorinte: Kodwa sithetha ubulumko bukaThixo ngemfihlelo, nobulumko obufihliweyo uThixo awabumisela phambi kwehlabathi, ukuze sizukiswe. Obungekho namnye kwiinkosana zeli hlabathi owabaziyo: kuba ukuba babebazi, ngebengazange bayibethelele emnqamlezweni iNkosi yozuko. Kubhaliwe ngokucacileyo, bantwana bam, kwaye akufuneki ngcaciso, kodwa ndiza kuchaza kancinci.

Ngaba niyawaqonda la mazwi, bantwana bam? Ngaba niyaqonda ukuba nixabiseke kangakanani na ukuba uThixo ebeke iliso lakhe kuni kwaphambi kokudalwa kwehlabathi, kwaye njengoko engunaphakade, nani ninjalo, kuba beniphambili engqondweni yakhe kwaphambi kwexesha, kwaye uninikile ubomi obungunaphakade kum, uKrestu Yesu? Amaxesha enu nobomi benu bumiselwe nguYahweh, uThixo omkhulu nomangalisayo, oqaqambe kangangokuba akukho nyama inokumbona. Ukuba uSathana wayewazi amacebo kaBawo, ngebengazange andibethelele emnqamlezweni, kuba ekuthobeleni kwam kwada kwasekufeni nasekuvukeni kwam kwagqitywa amandla amoyisileyo kwaye wonakele ngokupheleleyo. Ngebengasoze alifune ibandla elinamandla elixhotyiswe ngumoya wam nelo ndenza imisebenzi emikhulu ngezandla zalo.

Ngaba oko kuyavakala kuni? Yekani ukuxhalaba kwengqondo kuba abathunywa bam banixelela into eningafuni kuyiva, kwaye noyise ikratshi lenu, kuba ndiza kukhokela abathobekileyo kuphela kwaye ndiza kubachasa abanekratshi. Yivani amazwi am kwaye avumele angene enzulwini yemiphefumlo yenu; kuba ndinidalile kum, kwaye ninamandla, kwaye ningummangaliso; kuba nihlanjululwe ngegazi lam, lona igazi lemvana. Kuba ndiyintloko yoMzimba wam owenza imisebenzi emininzi emikhulu kubantu bam endisababizayo, bambelelani kuphela kwiNkosi yenu, uYeshua HaMashiach, ngentliziyo enye nomoya omnye, kuba ndiyiNkosi yeeNkosi.

Ingqina elithembekileyo, nowokuqala ozelwe kwabafileyo uthi kwabathembekileyo nabanyulwa bakhe! Kwakukho ibandla lamandulo e-Efese, kwaye kubo kwatyhilwa ngokupheleleyo iMfihlelo Enkulu kaThixo kwincwadi ethunyelwe nguPawulos evela kum, kuba wayengazithetheli. Ndiza kuchaza kancinci, kodwa yazini ukuba ndilindele ukuthobela kwenu ngokholo kum, Yesu waseNazarete, kwaye kuye onikwe okuninzi kulindelwe okuninzi. Phambi kokuba ndiqhube, ndifuna niqwalasele oku kulandelayo okuvela eKolose, kuba ndathumela incwadi nakwabaseKolose: Kuba nifile, kwaye ubomi benu bufihliwe noKrestu kuThixo. Xa uKrestu, ongubomi bethu, eya kubonakala, niya kubonakala nani ninaye eluzukweni.

Bantwana bam, nafa xa ndafayo, kwaye niyafa kuni mihla le ngokubala imvelo yomntu omdala ifile; kuba nabethelelwa emnqamlezweni nam, kwaye akukho ndawo enye esibhalweni eniya kufumana kuyo isipili sobuqu benu bokwenene, kuba akukho candelo leevangeli eliyityhilayo ngaphandle kwevangeli yeMfihlelo Enkulu endayityhilela iBandla lam. Nangona nibona kuphela ngesipili esiluthuthu, oko kuza kutshintsha kungekudala, kwaye niya kwazi njengoko nani naziwa. Zintywilisele kwiVangeli yeMfihlelo Enkulu kaThixo, kuba ibandla lam laseLawodike liye lazilahla iimfihlelo zam, kuba uninzi lwenu alufane luzifunde iincwadi zam ezibhalelwe iBandla kuba abalusi abalahlekileyo banikhokelele kwenye indawo nikhangele amazwi abomvu. Bantwana bam, ndiyazi ukuba niyandithanda, ngoko ndithandeni ngokwazi, kwaye ningahambi ngenkohliso.

Ngoko ndiyanibuza, ithini incwadi eya kwabase-Efese? Kuba yabonani, imfihlelo enkulu ikhona! Esazisile imfihlelo yokuthanda kwakhe, ngokwenjongo yakhe elungileyo, awayeyimisile kuye, Ukuze ngexesha lokuzaliseka kwamaxesha, ahlanganisele ndawonye zonke izinto kuKrestu, ezisezulwini, nezisemhlabeni, kuye. Ndinazisile imfihlelo yokuthanda kukaYahweh, awayeyifihle kuye, kuba akadingi mcebisi. Musa ukucinga ngentsingiselo yobuxoki enayifunda kubaprofeti bobuxoki kumanqaku abo angenaxabiso angasemacaleni angokwamaphakama, kuba uSathana ngobuqhetseba uphinda achaze iingqiqo zesibhalo ukuze zihambelane nokuthanda kwakhe namacebo akhe angenamsebenzi.

Le mfihlelo yanikwa uPawulos, kwaye wayeligosa eliphathiswe ukuyisasaza kwiBandla lam, nakuwe ukuba uneendlebe zokuva namehlo okubona. Kuba ivangeli yeMfihlelo Enkulu yanikwa uPawulos, kwaye ndamthuma endleleni eya eDamasko. Ekuzalisekeni kwamaxesha, uBawo uya kuhlanganisa zonke izinto ezisezulwini nezisemhlabeni kum, uKrestu Yesu. Akusayi kubakho lwahlukano, akusayi kubakho kwahlukana phakathi koThixo nabantwana bakhe, akusayi kubakho butshaba phakathi komJuda neNtlanga, kuba nonke nizelwe ngokutsha kwaye nisisidalwa esitsha kum, uKrestu Yesu. Ngaba la mazwi ayamangalisa kakhulu kuni? Ngaba amazwi am anenza niwachase kuba nikholelwa kwinkcazelo yabefundisi abalahlekileyo ngawo?

Fundani kum ngokuthobeka ukuze niqonde. Kwakhona, kwabase-Efese uPawulos wabhala amazwi endamnika wona: Ngenxa yesi sizathu mna Pawulos, umbanjwa kaYesu Krestu ngenxa yenu ziiNtlanga, Ukuba nivile ngobugosa bobabalo lukaThixo, endabunikwayo kuni: Ukuba ngentyilelo wandazisa imfihlelo. Kwakhona, musani ukukhubeka ngamazwi abaprofeti bobuxoki, njengoko ubugosa bobabalo lukaThixo yayikukulungiselela kwakhe iBandla, kuba yena (nabanye) bakhethwa njengamagosa eemfihlelo zikaThixo.

Kanjalo: Yona le nto, kwezinye izizukulwana, ingazange yaziswa koonyana babantu, njengokuba ngoku ityhiliwe kubapostile bakhe abangcwele nakubaprofeti bakhe ngoMoya; yokuba iintlanga ziziindlalifa nathi, zinxalenye zomzimba omnye, zikwangamahlulelana nathi esithembisweni sakhe esikuKristu, ngeVangeli. Ewe, yifunde ngokuchanekileyo uphinde uyifunde: iintlanga ezikholwayo kwigama lam elingcwele, lona elinguYesu Kristu, ziziindlalifa kunye nathi kwaye ziyinxalenye yomzimba omnye njengabo bakwaSirayeli abandenze iNkosi, kuba akukho mahluko kum.

Kanjalo: Kwanale mfihlelo ibifihliwe kumaphakade nakwizizukulwana, kodwa ngoku ibonakalisiwe kubangcwele bakhe, abo uThixo athande ukubazisa ukuba buyintoni na ubutyebi bobuqaqawuli bale mfihlelo phakathi kweentlanga, obunguKristu phakathi kwenu, ithemba lobuqaqawuli. Ewe, nizelwe ngokutsha ngoMoya wam, kwaye uMoya wam uhlala ngonaphakade kubantwana bam, kuba unguMoya wobunyana, isibambiso selifa lenu, kwaye loo Moya uqulethe zonke izakhono endininike zona, kuba ndinike izipho ebantwini. Nenziwe ngomfanekiselo wam, kwaye nidalwe ngokutsha kum; kuba uBawo unizele kum, kuba ndingumoya ophilisa ngokukhawuleza.

UKristu Yesu onyusiweyo uthi: Bantwanana bam, thuthuzelekani kakhulu kum, kuba usindiso lwam, olufunyenweyo ngokundenza iNkosi yenu ngokholo nangenguquko, alunakuphulukana okanye lutshitshiswe; kuba nguwuphi na kuni onokutshitshisa indalo yendalo yeempawu zakho kubantwana bakho, nangona benganakuphulukana nelifa ukuba ubungabalahla. Usindiso sisipho esinikwa nguBawo wam ngam, uYeshua HaMashiach, kwaye izipho nobizo lukaThixo azinakuguqulwa. Ngoku, ndiza kuninika iimvuthuluka eniza kuzilandela kuhambo lwenu lokuya kwiimfihlelo eziqulethwe kwiMfihlelo enkulu kaThixo, ngoko ke khuthalelani ukuzibonakalisa nikholekile kuThixo, nibe ngabasebenzi beLizwi, nilahlula ngokufanelekileyo ilizwi lenyaniso, ukuze ningabi nazintloni phambi kwam ekufikeni kwam.

Kukho imfihlelo kwabaseRoma, eqwalasela ubumfama bukaSirayeli ngenxalenye. Kukho imfihlelo yokuhlanganiswa komtshakazi wam, abanye abakubiza ngokuthi kukuXhwilwa, kwaye oko kukwabaseKorinte (nakwezinye iindawo); kuba ngomzuzwana, ngephanyazo, kuya kuvakala ixilongo lokugqibela, kwaye ukukhala kwalo kuphezu kwenu. Emva koko kukho imfihlelo yendoda nomfazi besiba nyama-nye, kwaye le yimfihlelo enkulu: kodwa ndithetha ngoKristu nebandla, kwaye oko ungakufumana kwabase-Efese. Kuba ndiya kubususa ubumfama kuSirayeli xa beguqulela iintliziyo zabo kum. Kuba ndiya kumhlanganisela kum uMtshakazi wam, kuba ndinguMyeni. Ndim lo niya kutshata naye, ukuze sibe banye ngonaphakade kum nakuBawo wam, kuba uThixo uyinto yonke kubo bonke. Intliziyo yam iphumle phezu kwenu, bantwana bam, kwaye kungekudala niya kundibona ubuso ngobuso; kwaye ningaphazami, ndiyanibizela kum, zintombi zam ezilumkileyo, kwaye ndiyeza kungekudala kakhulu, kwaye ndinqwenela ukunibona nonke apho siya kuba ubuso ngobuso, kwaye niya kwazi, njengokuba nani naziwa.
