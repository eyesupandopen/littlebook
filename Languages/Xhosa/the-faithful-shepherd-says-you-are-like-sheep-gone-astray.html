Ndiyanilumkisa ngqongqo, bantu bam, yaye ndibiza izulu nomhlaba ukuba zibe ngamangqina amazwi am: MUSANI ukuwathatha amazwi esiprofeto endiwanikwe mna, iNkosi uYesu Krestu, njengento engengcwele; kodwa ke, civani lonke ilizwi endilinikayo. Ukuba niyala, sisono kuni oko.

Ndiyanixelela phambi kokuba ndiqhubele phambili, abaninzi benu baya kuvingca iindlebe zabo baze bavale amehlo abo kumazwi endiza kuwathetha, yaye ndiyanicebisa ukuba ningoyiki. Abaninzi benu baya kunikezela kuxinzelelo oluvela kubahlobo nosapho, uxinzelelo oluvela kwiicawa zobuxoki nakumaziko ehlabathi, nicinga ukuba izifundiswa zindimele ngoxa izifundiswa zehlabathi zilulwazi lukaSathana lobuxoki, yaye ulwazi lwakhe lokulungileyo nokubi luhlala luzisa ukufa, kuba wawenza amazwi akhe ukuba afezekise oko. Musani ukuba phakathi kwabo bawalahlayo amazwi am, kodwa phulaphulani ngomphefumlo wenu wonke nize nicinge ngoko ndiza kukuthetha, kuba amazwi am bubomi, yaye aya kulwenza luqiniseke ubizo nonyulo lwenu.

Andithethi ngokuchasene nabam, nangona kukho abo phakathi kwenu abalumkileyo ngokweengcinga zabo abaya kutsho kanye loo nto.

Jongani umnombo wam, njengoko ndazalwa ngoobawo, yaye umnombo wam wozalo uqinisekile; yaye ukuba kukho nabani na phakathi kwenu onenzondelelo ngoSirayeli, mna ndingaphezulu, kuba ndabuncama ubomi bam ngenxa yabo! Ndiyanahlulela: okulungileyo nokubi, kuba abaninzi benu abanako ukwahlula kokomoya yaye bamfanyekiswe bubuxoki babakhohlakeleyo. Anifundanga na ukuba uThixo uzalisekisa iindlela zakhe ngamanye amaxesha ngesandla seentshaba zakhe, kuba kwanazo iintshaba zakhe ziyalithobela ilizwi lakhe? Ewe, uSirayeli wamiselwa kwakhona ngo-1948, yaye ndithanda abaninzi kwelo lizwe yaye ndiya kubahlangula; kodwa uSirayeli wamiselwa kwakhona ngabantu abakhohlakeleyo, yaye iinkokeli zabo zineenjongo ezifihlakeleyo ezikhohlakeleyo. Asikokwenu, bantwana bam, ukuba nibe nesandla kule nxalenye yomsebenzi kaBawo ngeli xesha, nangona niya kunceda kwixesha elizayo eBukumkanini bam; kodwa kuxhomekeke kum ukusindisa uYakobi, hayi nina!

Nilahlekiswe ngamazwi amnandi neentetho ezintle yaye niwele kubuqhetseba obukhulu. Nithakathiwe yimimoya kaIzebhele, esebenza ngeendlela zikaBhileham. Yazini ukuba ndiya kuyaphula imixokelelwane yayo kuni ndize ndivule amehlo neendlebe zenu ukuba nje nithe najikela kum ngokuthobeka nangokuzimisela ukuza kwiLizwi lam nize nifunde ngam ngaphandle kweengcinga zenu enizifunde kule mimoya yobuxoki eprofeta ngabaprofeti bayo bobuxoki. Imimoya kaIzebhele ethetha ubuxoki obukhulu kubaprofeti bayo bobuxoki, kwanemiqondiso nemimangaliso yobuxoki, yaye iprofeta ngenxa yokubawa ngokomoya kaBhileham, le ingenelele kwiicawa zenu, yaye nitya ubumdaka obuvela kanye ezandleni zayo.

Nikhohliswe yindlu yesikhungu kaSathana ukuba ningene kumanyano lobuxoki nayo, indlu yesikhungu kaSathana. Abanye kwiBandla lam abathembekanga kumazwi am nakwimimiselo yam kuba bakholelwe kubuxoki obuthethwe egameni lam, besabelana nabo ngobumfama babo, nto leyo efanelekileyo. Musani ukuvumela ukuba nifunyanwe phakathi kwabo xa ndifika; anditshongo na, phumani kuyo nahlukane, kuba bunantoni na ubudlelwane bukaKrestu noBheliyali? Hlambululani udaka emehlweni enu ngegazi lam lentlawulelo nize niwavule ukuze nibone ukukhanya kokwenene endiya kuninika kona, njengoko amehlo enu egutyungelwe ludaka, yaye aninakukwazi ukuwavula ngaphandle kwam.

Njengoko kubhaliwe, uThixo ubanike umoya wobuthongo, amehlo ukuze bangaboni, neendlebe ukuze bangeva unanamhla, yaye ngamazwi amnandi neentetho ezintle niye nazimanya nabo ebumfameni babo, ningabi ziimfama kuphela kwiinjongo zam ngoSirayeli kodwa nikwamfanyekisiwe kubizo lwenu lokwenene kum, uKrestu Yesu, iNkosi yenu! Ukuba niziimfama, anikhululekanga ukuba nifunde nize niwaqonde amazwi am, yaye anihambi ngokomoya. Ngoko ke, landelani amazwi am, bantu bam; kuba nangona nindazile ngokwenyama, ngokwenyama anisenakundazi. Kuba ndiguqulwe ndaba ngumoya onika ubomi, onike umoya wobomi kuSirayeli kaThixo, kwanomoya wokuba ngabantwana enikhala ngawo nithi ABHA, Bawo!

Ke kaloku iNkosi inguloo Moya: yaye apho ukhoyo uMoya weNkosi, kukho inkululeko; ngoko ke, yimani niqine enkululekweni uKrestu anikhulule ngayo yaye ningabuyi nibhajiswe yidyokhwe yobukhoboka. Thobani iindlebe zenu nize niguqule iintliziyo zenu ukuze nive iLizwi lam, yaye ningalali! UMoya unika ubomi; inyama ayincedi lutho. Amazwi endiwathethayo kuni angumoya yaye abubomi. Ngoko ke, musani ukumilisa okukaSirayeli ngokwenyama, nize niphume kubuxoki bakhe bezopolitiko, kodwa milisani okomoya wam owenziwe ngokomfanekiso kaThixo!

Aniqondi na? Ubuxoki babo buvunyelwe ukuba bulungiselele unyana wentshabalalo, ukuze bonke bagwetywe abo bayoliswa kukungabikho kokusesikweni, abamagama abo angabhalwanga eNcwadini yoBomi kwasekusekweni kwehlabathi. Phulaphulani amazwi am okukhalima nize ningazenzi lukhuni iintliziyo zenu, kuba amazwi am aya kunisindisa kwintlekele ezayo yaye aya kunahlula kwabo balwa nam nabandilahlileyo kangangeminyaka ephantse ibe ngama-2000 ngoku. Yaye nangona ubumfama benzeke ngenxalenye kuSirayeli, intsalela kaYakobi iya kujikela kum yaye yamkele ubulungisa obungaveli emthethweni kodwa obuza ngokholo kum. Hlanganisani iingcinga zenu nize nivuthulule amazwi obuxoki eengcuka abawasebeze ngobuxoki ezindlebeni zenu, bethetha nobuxoki egameni lam, kuba andibathumanga.

Kukho ububi obukhulu phezu komhlaba wam, yaye ndiza kuhlangula. Kukho ububi obukhulu kuYakobi kuba baye bazivumela ukuba baguqulwe ngabaxoki abangamaEdom. Anikholwanga na yingxelo yam yokuba indlu yesikhungu kaSathana ikhona yaye iyinyani? Yazini ukuba ihamba phakathi kwenu! Aniqondi na ukuba iimfama azinakukhokela iimfama, yaye uSirayeli umfanyekiswe ngenxalenye de kungene ukuzala kweeNtlanga? Ndisaza kusindisa intsalela ethembekileyo kuYakobi, yaye nditywina abali-144,000. Yaye baya kundenzela imisebenzi emikhulu, yaye ndiya kubathuma besiya ngapha nangapha ngexesha lembandezelo kaYakobi, yaye baya kusindisa abaninzi, nemisebenzi emininzi emikhulu baya kuyenza, yaye ndiya kubakhokelela ekukhokeleleni abanye, kuba akukho ndlela yimbi eya kuBawo ngaphandle kwam, iNkosi uYesu Krestu, uMesiya abamlahlayo.

Kodwa nina, owu, maKrestu ale mihla, nina bantwana bam abadikidiki, nicinga ukuba nazi ngcono kuba nifunde imibhalo yabo eziswe ngokufihlakeleyo phakathi kwenu. Yaye nikholelwe kwingxelo yabo yobuxoki, ingxelo yobuxoki evela kwindlu yesikhungu yamaEdom kaSathana, ihlelo elakha itempile, bona kanye abatshabalalisi uSathana abathumele ukuba boyise ihlabathi. Yaye bakhokelela isizwe eninokusinqula endaweni kaBawo ukuba nilandela iintsomi zabo eziyilwe ngobuqhetseba, bezibiza ngokuba ngabantu abanyuliweyo bakaThixo yaye beniqinisekisa ukuba nibasikelele ngoxa beya kunitshabalalisa ngethuba lokuqala. Yazini ukuba andithumelanga sisombululo sezopolitiko, yaye oorhulumente behlabathi bakhonza uSathana, obathengileyo ngomvuzo wokungalungisi.

Anifundanga na yaye aniqondanga na ukuba ndim kuphela isisombululo, yaye ndinguKumkani oligorha othunywe nguYahweh ngokwakhe ukuba oyise ubukumkani ngobulungisa aze aluchithe lonke ulawulo negunya likaSathana? Ewe, ndiya kulwaphula ulawulo lwakhe lube ziingceba, lungaphindi luchache, yaye ndiya kuyilimaza intloko yakhe exokayo njengoko kwaprofetwayo, yaye uya kutsha ngonaphakade kumadangatye ashushu aluhlaza, yaye uya kuqubha kwisulfure evuthayo kwelo chibi likhulu ngaloo mini inkulu, yaye akayi kuze achache kodwa uya kutshatyalaliswa aze atshe ngonaphakade. Yazini ukuba akukho nkohlakalo ingekho sikweni kubulungisa bukaBawo, kodwa yimbuyekezo elula yezenzo zikaSathana ngokwakhe neminqweno yakhe ngabantwana bakaBawo nendalo, ngoko uBawo uya kumnika oko akunqwenelelayo nina kubulungisa bakhe.

Anifundanga na? UKumkani uya kuphendula athi kubo, Inene ndithi kuni, Njengoko nikwenzileyo komnye wabancinane kwaba bazalwana bam, nikwenzile kum. Kutheni nilandela iinkokeli zezopolitiko zobuxoki ngokungathi nilandela mna? Ndiza kunixelela isizathu ukuze niqonde ngokupheleleyo: Abaninzi benu balandela ubuxoki kwiibhayibhile zenu zokufunda endingazibhalanga, yaye andibathumanga abo baqulunqa oko kubizwa ngokuba yimibhalo yobuKrestu bobuZiyon. Akukho nto injengobuKrestu bobuZiyon, kuba andinguye umJuda ophakamileyo. Ukuba ndandingumZiyon, ngendingazange ndijikele kwiiNtlanga, yaye izizwe zandemkela okwexeshana. Hayi, basebenzisa ubuZiyon ukuzisa unyana wentshabalalo ngetempile yabo abaya kuyakha ngokungazi egameni likaBawo, yaye bayamhlazisa uBawo kuba bayandala mna, uYesu Krestu. Andizanga nje kuphela kwabam ndaza ndaliwa ngabam, kodwa abaninzi babo basandala unanamhla. Kodwa nina, owu, bandla laseLawodike, nicinga ukuba ningakukhawulezisa ukuzaliseka kwesiprofeto ngokulandela impambano yabo; kuba indlu yesikhungu kaSathana ifuna ihlabathi layo, ihlabathi elingenani, bantwana bam. Andinisindisanga na kubo?

Yazini ukuba uSathana wazama ukuba ilifa lam kum, kuba umhlaba yoweNkosi nokuzala kwawo. USathana ufuna ihlabathi elingenaye uThixo, ihlabathi elikhululekileyo kuThixo, ihlabathi elikhululekileyo kum, ilitye lembombo eliyintloko, nehlabathi apho abomeleleyo baphilayo njengoko betshabalalisa umhlaba kuyo yonke into ngaphandle kwembewu kaSathana, abayiyo. USirayeli ungenelwe kangangamawaka eminyaka ngoku yimibhalo yabo yaseBhabhiloni (ewe, baqulunqa izithethe zomlomo ezadluliselwayo) ezindigwebayo, mna Nkosi uYesu Krestu, nto leyo engenakwenzeka ukuyifumana njengoko ndingayi kwaliwa. Yaye ihlabathi liza kuyiqonda ingqumbo namandla am, kuba ndimkhulu kunabo bonke ngaphandle koBawo, yaye akunakwenzeka ukundinqanda.

Ndilinde ngomonde nangokuzeka kade umsindo, ndivumela igama lam ukuba linyeliswe nombingelelo wam wentlawulelo uthatyathwe njengento engengcwele ukuze uMtshakazi wam asindiswe, kwa loo Mtshakazi wayefihliwe kumaxesha adlulileyo kwimfihlelo enkulu kaThixo, imfihlelo endayityhila ngokupheleleyo kuMpostile uPawulos. Yaye ningayifunda imibhalo yakhe ukuze niqonde, kuba akazithethelanga, kodwa wavakalisa ivangeli yeMfihlelo enkulu kaThixo endamnika yona ukuba andityhilele yona. Akukho mntu uya kuthatha oko kokwam, kuba ndikuhlawulele ngegazi lam lentlawulelo, yaye ndiya kubabuyekeza abenzi bobubi.

USathana ufuna ihlabathi elingenani, bantwana bam, kodwa abaninzi benu ngobuyatha banikela imali yenu ukuxhasa indlu yesikhungu kaSathana neenjongo zayo ezingengcwele emhlabeni wam nakwiintsalela zesizwe sakwaSirayeli, isizwe apho ndiya kulawula umhlaba kwitrone kaDavide kubukumkani bam obuza kutyhilwa kungekudala. Nixhasa ukufa kwenu, nicinga ukuba niqhubezela phambili ivangeli. Kutheni ningakwazi ukuqonda ukuba ukufa nentshabalaliso nabalawuli ababulalayo ASIBO bam kodwa yimbewu kaSathana; ewe, uyise wabo nguMtyholi, uYise weNkxoki, uSathana ngokwakhe. Yazini ukuba lutshaba oloyisiweyo, yaye uyazi ngoloyiko ukuba imihla yakhe imfutshane, yaye woyika ikamva lakhe ngaphezu kwayo nayiphi na into, kuba uyazi ukuba akusayi kuze kubekho siqabu kwisohlwayo sakhe, kuba uThixo ngokwakhe uyakuvakalisa oko! Bantwana bam, aninakulandela uSathana nize nikhonze mna. Kuba akukho mntu unokukhonza iinkosi ezimbini: kuba uya kuyithiya le, aze ayithande leya; okanye uya kubambelela kule, aze ayidele leya. Ngoko yenzani ukhetho lwenu lokuba ngubani eniya kumkhonza, kuba andizange ndimisele iBandla lam ukuba libe nanto yakwenza noko kubizwa ngokuba bubuKrestu bobuZiyon, yaye loo ntshukumo asiyoyam, kodwa yekaSathana, yaye uyisebenzisela ukuzisa unyana wentshabalalo. Abaninzi benu baye balahlekelwa yintsingiselo yamazwi am, becinga ukuba "anti" kuthetha ukuchasa ngokuqinileyo kwaye akukho nto ingaphaya koko. Hayi, umntu wesono uthunyelwa ngobuqhetseba endaweni yam kubantu abandilahlileyo, kanti ndiya kumsindisa uYakobi ngembandezelo enkulu, yaye andiyi kuhlambulula kuphela umhlaba wam welifa, kodwa ndiya kuhlambulula ihlabathi lonke ebubini nasesonweni. Qondani ukuba ndiya kumsindisa uYakobi ngezithembiso uBawo awazenza kooyise, yaye akungenxa yokufaneleka okanye ubulungisa babo, kuba akukho nyama ilungileyo emehlweni am.

Ndiyanicebisa ukuba nizibeke phantsi iimbalo zabo bangandithetheliyo, nize nibe nobulumko, bantwana bam; kuba abaninzi benu abanalwazi kangangokuba aninakukwazi ukwahlula ubuxoki kwinyaniso. Kuba anindibambi njengentloko yenu kodwa nibambe amazwi athethwa ngabaprofeti bobuxoki bakaIzebhele, abasebenza ngeendlela zikaBhileham, belandela umvuzo wokungalungisi. Kuba iLizwi likaThixo liphilile, linamandla, libukhali ngaphezu kwalo naliphi na ikrele elintlangothi-mbini, lihlaba lide lahlule umphefumlo nomoya, namalungu nomongo, yaye liyakwazi ukugweba iingcinga neenjongo zentliziyo. Buyelani kwiLizwi lam elinyulu likaThixo, kuba uBawo uyalikhusela unanamhla.

Qondani indlela yam yokusebenza, bantu bam, nize nifunde ngam. Andikhethanga lizwi linye ukuba limele isibhalo sam, yaye andikhethanga lizwi lidala ukuba lilawule amazwi am nokuqinisekisa ubunyaniso bawo. Hayi, ngeendlela zikaBawo (uBawo akadingi mcebisi kwizicwangciso zakhe) nakubulumko bukaBawo wakhetha amazwi amaninzi avela kumaxesha amaninzi, kwiindawo, nakwiinkcubeko ezivumelana zodwa. Khangelani kwiByzantine, bantwana bam, yaye ningawathandabuzi amazwi am, nize ningene nzulu kwimibhalo endiyigcinileyo, nize nizithobe kumazwi am. Aniyalelwanga na ukuba nifunde ukuze nizibonakalise nivunyiwe? Kuba abantwana beli hlabathi kwisizukulwana sabo balumkile kunabantwana bokukhanya: Kuba balumkile ebubini nasenkohlisweni, yaye abenzi nto ingaphaya kokuba, ukubulala, nokutshabalalisa.

UBawo ulikhusile ilizwi lakhe elinyulu likaThixo, kwaye liphambi kwenu kanye, kodwa niyalichasa ukuze nizonwabise ngeminqweno yenu yobuvila ngeenguqulelo ekulula ukuzifunda, iinguqulelo ezonakeleyo endingazithumanga okanye endingazibhalanga. Kutheni nifulathela la mazwi? Ebubhanxeni benu bokungazi, aniyiqondi into yokuba uSathana uzenza nkqu neZibhalo Ezingcwele. Uyakwenza oko ngendlela efanayo nomkhohlisi onganyanisekanga anokukopela ngayo ngokususa amakhonkco kwityathanga ngalinye ngexesha ukuze enze ityathanga libe lifutshane kungekho mntu uqaphelayo. Ayisuswa kancinci kancinci na imida? Kuba nguwuphi umthiyeli obeka umgibe emehlweni entaka? Utshintshe amagama, wasusa amacandelo neendinyana ezipheleleyo, wongeza amagama, kwaye ukwenzile konke oko ngenjongo yokumanya zonke iinkolo phantsi kwentshukumo yokuhlanganisa zonke iinkolo ukuze aphantsise bonke kuKrestu wakhe wobuxoki, ohamba phakathi kwenu ekuhleni, oza kutyhilwa kungekudala.

Ndiza kuliphosa irhamncwa nomprofeti wobuxoki bephila edikeni elivutha umlilo nesulfure ukuze bafumane ilifa labo, ilifa uBawo alilungiselele uMtyholi nezithunywa zakhe. Kuba uya kubanika ilizwe labo nelizwe elifanele iminqweno yabo emibi abayinqwenela nina, bantwana bam. Anifundanga okanye nilibele na ukuba amahlelo obuGnostiki azitshintsha iincwadi zam kwakamsinya nje emva kokuba ezifumene? Kwaye nicinga ukuba akunakwenzeka kumaxesha enu, xa ulwazi lwande kangaka?

Phandani ilizwi lam elinyulu, nibe nokholo lokuba ndiyakwazi ukunika abaguquleli abanyanisekileyo. Kholwani ngamazwi endiwanikileyo; kholwani ngamazwi eLizwi likaThixo. Musani ukuba zizidenge kangangokuba nicinge ukuba nawuphi na umbhalo ugqibelele; anditshongo na kuni ukuba nifundisise ukuze nizibonakalise ukuba namkelekile? Kodwa bantu bam, xa nifunda, nivumela iintshaba zomnqamlezo ukuba zitolike amazwi am ukuze nikhohliswe ninike imvume kwindlu yesikhungu kaSathana. Ninika imvume yenu ngokungazi kunye nokuvuma okuthuleyo kuSathana nabantwana bakhe abahluthe imihlaba yam engcwele, kodwa okwexeshana elithile, kwaye inkathazo kaYakobi iya kubahlamba ibasuse elizweni, kwaye abalulamileyo baya kulidla ilifa umhlaba. Kholwani kum, uLizwi owenziwa inyama, ukuba ndiniphe inyaniso; kwaye inyaniso ikwiZibhalo zam eziNgcwele, kuba zithetha ngam.

Niya kuwala na amazwi am, bantu bam? Niya kukhetha ukungabakholelwa abathunywa endibathumileyo? Kuba ndithume abaninzi! Uninzi lwenu luya kuqhubeka nokuphambana kwenu, ningazikhathazi nokuzikhathaza ngokuqinisekisa amazwi esi siprofeto, kwaye niya kwenza oko kube yingozi kuni nelahleko yomvuzo wenu. Kwakamsinya nje emva kokuba umthinteli esusiwe noMtshakazi wam ethathiwe, baya kuhamba ngesantya esikhulu ukubulala yonke into naye nabani na ongumKristu. Baya kuyitshayela iNkonzo yobuKristu emhlabeni ukuba bebengakwazi, kwaye baya kubatshutshisa babulale abangcwele bam ngeendlela ezininzi, kwaye abo banqula igama leNkosi baya kusindiswa.

Guqukelani kum ngoku, nihlambulule iintliziyo zenu kumazwi abo obuxoki. Nizifumbalele amazwi okufa nentshabalalo ngokungazi kwenu ngokupheleleyo ngembali yenu. Ndinicela umngeni ukuba nifunde imbali yeencwadi endingazibhalanga eniziphakamisa ngaphezu kwamazwi am. Fundani ukuba babengoobani na ababhali bokwenene, kuba babhala ubuxoki obukhulu kwiincwadi zabo, kuba niya kubazi ngeziqhamo zabo. Uninzi lwezi ncwadi zenziwe umgangatho kwiisemina zenu; phandani, kuba kuyinyaniso! Ukuba niya kukhuthala kwezi zinto nibambelele kum, ndiya kunikhokelela kuyo yonke inyaniso, kwaye ekugqibeleni niya kuyiqonda imfihlelo yobuThixo eniyiyo. Kodwa kwanaleyo mfihlelo inkulu yenziwe yanto yexabiso eliphantsi ezingqondweni nasezintliziyweni zenu ngobuxoki babo; loo mfihlelo inkulu ngumntu omnye omtsha othathwe kumaJuda nakwiiNtlanga ngokufanayo, apho kungekho ludonga lokwahlula, kuba olo donga luphelisiwe kum.
