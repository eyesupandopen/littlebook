Ukhetho LungeLwakho!

Phulaphulani amazwi oMsindisi wenu, iNkosi noKumkani wenu Bantwana bam, nithobe iindlebe zenu Emazwini eNkosi yenu, uYesu Krestu olilungisa, kuba ndingunyana wobulungisa kwaye ndiphakama ebuqaqawulini bam, kwaye nikhule njengamathole esitalini eluxolweni nokhuseleko oluthile, kwaye ndinikhulisele injongo yasezulwini eza kutyhilwa ngokupheleleyo kuni, nina banyanisekileyo nabanyuliweyo bam. Yabonani! Ndim ovavanya iintliziyo zabantu ukuze nazi ngokuqinisekileyo ukuba nikholo ngokwenene na, okanye nilahlekisiwe kwaye nithatyathwe zizono zenu nenkohliso enamandla yangoku ethiwe qipu ihlabathi.

Kuba ndim lowo uYahweh abelane naye ngetrone yakhe, uKrestu Yesu iNkosi evusiweyo nenikwe amandla, kwaye ndiyiNkosi yeeNkosi noKumkani wooKumkani kwaye yabona iNgonyama yeSizwe sakwaYuda isiza emafini ukwamkela abanyanisekileyo bam kum ukuze bathatyathelwe emazulwini kumtshato weMvana, kuba uMtshakazi wam uzilungisile. Nezibane zakhe zizele, kwaye uzicandile iintambo zezibane zakhe, kwaye ulindele ukubonakala kwam njengentombi enobulumko, kwabo ndibahlambulule ngokupheleleyo kwaye abanasiphako phambi kwetrone kaThixo. Kwanabo bangathinjwanga ngobuqhetseba bukaBharabhas, kuba uBharabhas uzisa imvukelo enogonyamelo, imfazwe nentlekele, kuba ngoku uyintloko enye phakathi kweentloko ezininzi ezisebenza ngokubambisana, kodwa uya kuvela njengoMoshiach wobuxoki, kwaye unyulwe ngabantu belizwe ukuba alawule isinagoge kaSathana. Kuba uyindlalifa yalowo ookhokho babo bamkhethayo, kwaye bazilungiselela ukumamkela njengoKumkani wabo.

Kodwa nina, O Cawa yaseLawodike, ngoku kufika amaxesha enu enkangala nomgwebo. Kuba nizikhethele omnye umsindisi njengoKumkani wenu, kwanalowo ukufika kwakhe kuvakaliswa yimiqondiso nemimangaliso yobuxoki kwaye ngoku unikhokele ngobugqi nokukhafula. NdinguYeshua HaMashiach kwaye ndinithengile ngegazi lam elihlawulelayo, elingenasono, kwaye ndim obeya kuniphilisa, anidale ngokutsha kumoya wam, kuba ndenziwe umoya ophilisa kuba uYahweh umisele bonke ubukhulu kuNyana wakhe, kwanalowo ungumphumo wemvana kaThixo neNgonyama yeSizwe sakwaYuda! Kwaye ngobulungisa ndiya kugweba ndenze imfazwe kuba imvana kaThixo neNgonyama yeSizwe sakwaYuda zizalaniswe kum, uKrestu Yesu, kwanaye uYeshua HaMashiach kuba ndinguye!

Ndisaza kulugqibezela usindiso lwenu, O Cawa yaseLawodike, kodwa anikalungeli kungena eBukumkanini bam ngeli xesha kuba niphambukile kwinjongo yenu enye kuKrestu! Ndiya kunibonisa impazamo yenu, kwaye ninalo ithuba lokuguquka kuzo zonke izono, kodwa anisenalo ixesha eliseleyo lokuphikisana nelizwi leNkosi yenu, kuba esi sono sibi sesinye sezininzi uSathana azisebenzisileyo ukunibopha ekuzeni ngokupheleleyo kum ngenguquko ukuze ndinihlambulule ngokupheleleyo ngegazi lam elihlawulelayo ezonweni zenu.

Yabonani! Amaxesha eeNtlanga aphelile kwaye umnquma wasendle owaxhunyelelwa phakathi kwazo, kwaye kunye nazo wabelana ngengcambu nokutyeba koMnquma, ngoku uza kunqunyulwa. Kuba niwatyhalile Amazwi kaKrestu Yesu, kuba ndanixelela ngokucacileyo ukuba ningaziqhayisi ngamasebe awawa ekungakholweni: kodwa ukuba niyaziqhayisa, aniyithwali ingcambu, kodwa ingcambu inithwele nina. Ndim iNgcambu kaYese, kwaye ndim omiyo njengophawu lwethemba kuwo onke amaxesha, kwanalowo uzisa usindiso neendlela zikaYahweh emhlabeni kwaye ndiya kulawula ilifa lam kwitrone kaDavide ngobulungisa kwaye ndiwuthengile umhlaba ngegazi lam elingenasono kwaye ndiya kuthatha oko kungokwam ngokufanelekileyo ngamandla ezandleni zabahlali abangekho mthethweni, kwanabo bawileyo.

Ndim lowo unike ubabalo nenceba emhlabeni, kuba ixesha lonke lobabalo ndalinikwa nguYahweh losindiso lweeNtlanga. Kuba iinjongo zikaYahweh kwaSirayeli zazisoloko zikukuba iiNtlanga zisikelelwe kwaye zifike elusindisweni ngokuthobela kukaSirayeli. Kodwa ekuhambeni kwexesha baba nekratshi, baba namabhongo, kwaye banqunyulwa kumnquma wemvelo ekungakholweni. Kodwa nina, Cawa yam yaseLawodike, kwananina niyisibonelo salo hlobo lunye lwekratshi, ukungakholwa, nobumfama obaboniswa ngamasebe emvelo anqunyulwa ekungakholweni, kwaye ngoku niza kunqunyulwa kumnquma wemvelo. Kwaye ngoku ndiza kuqhubela phambili ndigqibezele umsebenzi wam kumnquma wemvelo, uSirayeli isizalwana sam nabantu bam, kwanabo bonke endibakhethileyo ukuba bandamkele njengeNkosi yabo ukuze ndibadale ngokutsha njengomntu omnye omtsha kwaye imfihlelo kaThixo igqitywe, kwanokugqitywa kwemisebenzi yam yokuhlawulela yosindiso.

Kwaye nina, Cawa yam yaseLawodike, nilushiyile uthando lwenu lokuqala kwaye nisezandleni zomkhohlisi enifunge kuye ukunyaniseka. Nizikhethele uBharabhas omtsha njengoKumkani wenu kwaye nimkhabile uYesu waseNazarete njengeNkosi yenu kwaye nisezandleni zomnye umsindisi wobuxoki. Kwanalowo uzenza umsindisi weBhabheli eyimfihlakalo, othetha kwaye enze njengomnye wenu, kodwa uthetha njengenamba. Kuba iBhabheli eyimfihlakalo iphila kamnandi nakubutyebi obukhulu bezinto eziphathekayo, ulonwabo nobutyebi.

Kwaye iBhabheli eyimfihlakalo ilinxilisile lonke ihlabathi ngobunyhukunyhuku bobuhenyu obukwindeb' yayo yegolide, kodwa zinjalo iindlela zabawileyo, abanikezela obona bunyhukunyhuku bubi, kwaye niyabusela ngokuzithandela nangolonwabo olukhulu kuba nimfushwe yindeb' yegolide kubunyhukunyhuku obugqithisileyo enibusela ngokungcolileyo. Kuba neCawa yam yaseLawodike ayisahlanganeli kwizibhalo zam ezingcwele kodwa kwiindibano zikaBharabhas, enithembele kuye. Kwaye ke nikhetha umsindisi omtsha kuba nithanda ihlabathi nezinto zehlabathi kwaye aninakuyicinga into yokuba ningalahlekelwa zizinto zenu ezixabisekileyo, ngaphandle kokuba ndiya kuthatha lonke ulonwabo kuni kwingqumbo yam esondelayo, kwaye nangoku ilahleko zenu ziyanda!

Kuba niyimfama kwaye ninenkohliso ekratshini lenu lokuba uYahweh anganikezela omnye umsindisi ngaphandle kwam, uYesu waseNazarete, kuba ndiyimbewu ethenjisiweyo yomfazi kwaye uDan lithole lengonyama ekungekho Mesiya uya kuvela kuye. Ndiveni kwaye nindive kakuhle, O cawa yaseLawodike, kuba ngoku lixesha lokuguquka kwenu kwisono sentliziyo yenu okanye ngoku lixesha endiya kunishiya emlilweni wocikido kwaye bonke ababiza egameni leNkosi kude kube sekupheleni baya kusindiswa kwaye abaninzi abavuma igama lam ngokungathembeki baya kuwa ekulibalekeni, bechithe ithuba labo kuhlobo lobuthixo olukhanyela amandla alo: Nina baseLawodike, ndim amandla alo kwaye kum uYahweh ubeke bonke ubukhulu, kwaye nindikhanyele mna, iNkosi noMsindisi wenu, kwaye ndiya kunikhafulela emlonyeni wam ngaphandle kokuba niguquke ngoku!

Ngexesha lam lobabalo, usindiso lwasasazeka phakathi kweentlanga, kwaye isizwe emva kwesizwe sabelana kwaye sathatha inxaxheba kwingcambu yomnquma wemvelo. Kwaye ngoku, iintlanga azisayi kuba luphawu losindiso kwaye ixesha lazo kuNyana lafika ngokwaliwa kwesizalwana sam nabantu bam kuba bakhwaza ekungakholweni besithi khululela kuthi uBharabhas! Kodwa njengoko iimfihlelo zikaYahweh zatyhilwa ngabapostile bam abangcwele nabaprofeti, ndimthintelile uBharabhas kwaye andimkhululanga ngokupheleleyo, kodwa ndavumela kuphela imfihlelo yobugwenxa ukuba iqhubeke. Kwaye ngoku kufike amaxesha apho ndiya kumkhulula ngokupheleleyo uBharabhas njengoko umthinteli wam esusiwe kwaye njengoko abanyanisekileyo bam, ababiziweyo, nabanyuliweyo bethatyathelwa emazulwini apho ndibalungiselele khona amakhaya abo asisigxina, kwaye baya kwambathiswa ngelinen ecikizekileyo engenabala engenakuze idyojwe okanye ingcoliswe.

Ngaba nimfama kangaka kwihlabathi lenu, kwaye nizithulu kangaka ekuveni kangangokuba anisaliva ilizwi lam O Cawa yaseLawodike? Nikholelwe ubuxoki obuziswe yimfihlelo yobugwenxa, kwaye nivumele igwele elinetyhefu kwiBhayibhile zenu ezonakeleyo elizise ukonakala okugqithisileyo kwiiCawa zenu okungcolise iintliziyo zenu ngeentsomi ekungakholweni okuneempazamo. Nivumele ubuxoki babakhafuli bexesha lokwahlulwa ukuba banithakazele ngemibhalo yobuxoki namazwi axokayo. Anisaziqondi izibhalo, kwaye nizifumbalele iinkathazo ezinkulu eziya kunihlela ngequbuliso. Lonke iLizwi likaThixo linyulu kwaye lenzelwe ukuthotyelwa kokholo nokwakhiwa kwabamkeli. Qondani Amazwi amaLizwi am kwaye niqonde amaxesha eniphila kuwo.

Kuba ndenza uYohane uMtyhili wabhala: Ndabona irhamncwa liphuma elwandle, lineentloko ezisixhenxe, neempondo ezilishumi, naphezu kweempondo zalo izithsaba ezilishumi, naphezu kweentloko zalo igama lokunyelisa.

Kwaye kwakhona uYohane uMtyhili wabhala: Ndabona umfazi ehleli phezu kwerhamncwa elibomvu, lizele ngamagama onyeliso, lineentloko ezisixhenxe, neempondo ezilishumi. Kwaye umfazi wayambethe ezimfusa nezibomvu, ehonjiswe ngegolide, namatye anqabileyo neeperile, ephethe indebe yegolide esandleni sakhe, izele ngamasikizi nobunyhukunyhuku bobuhenyu bakhe.

Kwaye kwakhona: Kwaye iimpondo ezilishumi owazibonayo phezu kwerhamncwa, ezi ziya kumthiya umfazi olihenyukazi, zimshiye, zimenze aze, zitye inyama yakhe, zimtshise ngomlilo. Kuba uThixo ubeke ezintliziyweni zabo ukuba bafeze intando yakhe, bavumelane, banike ubukumkani babo kwirhamncwa, de amazwi kaThixo azaliseke. Kwaye umfazi, owambonayo, nguloo Mzi mkhulu olawula ookumkani bomhlaba.

Bantwana bam, qondani ukuba irhamncwa linokubhekisa kubantu ngabanye nakwizikumkani, fundani ukuze nifunde. Irhamncwa elithwele ihenyukazi liya kulitshabalalisa ihenyukazi kwaye alisayi kuba phantsi kolawulo lwehenyukazi, kuba lilawule ookumkani bomhlaba ngobuhenyu balo. Ngoko ke irhamncwa eliza kuphuma elwandle liya kuvelisa uMahdi wabo olindelekileyo, kodwa liya kuyitshabalalisa ngokupheleleyo iBhabheli eyimfihlakalo, kuba uThixo ulinikele ihenyukazi elikhulu ezandleni zeentshaba zalo esele zilithiye ngokugqithisileyo.

Phambi kokuba irhamncwa elivela elwandle nerhamncwa elivela emhlabeni liphakame ngokupheleleyo eBukumkanini benamba, baninzi abalawuli abamanyene ngenjongo yokunika amandla nolawulo lwabo kwinamba, kuba uThixo ukubeke ezintliziyweni zabo ukuba bakwenze. Kwaye baya kuyitshabalalisa iBhabheli eyimfihlakalo. Ucinga ukuba uzazi kakuhle izibhalo zam ezingcwele kuba ukholelwe iingcali zakho zezakwalizwi ezixokayo? Qondani kwaye nive Amazwi eNkosi yenu!

Kwakhona, ndenza uYohane uMtyhili wabhala: Kwaye irhamncwa endalibonayo, lalinjengeNgwe, neenyawo zalo zinjengebhere, nomlomo walo unjengomlomo wengonyama: kwaye inamba yalinika amandla ayo netrone yayo, negunya elikhulu. Kwaye ndabona enye yeentloko zalo ngokungathi yenzakele yafa, kodwa inxeba layo elibulalayo laphola, kwaye lonke ihlabathi lamangaliswa lalandela irhamncwa.

Bantwana bam, amazwi kaYahweh anobunzulu obuneenkalo ezininzi, ekungekho mntu unokuwazi kakuhle. Irhamncwa elichaziweyo bubukumkani obuya kuvela ekutshatyalalisweni kwehenyukazi, kwaye kulungelelwaniso oludala kuya kuvela ulungelelwaniso olutsha! Limele irhamncwa lolwandle, kuba liya kuvela njengoMahdi wabo, kwaye limele uhlobo lobukumkani aya kulawula ngalo ulwandle. Kutheni ningawahloniphi amazwi eLizwi lam? Akuthi irhamncwa elivela elwandle ngokwalo lenzakala, kuba nguwuphi umntu oneentloko ezisixhenxe ngokoqobo? Kodwa enye yeentloko zalo ngokungathi yenzakele yafa. O, zizidenge, neentliziyo ezicothayo ukukholwa kuko konke okuthethwe ngabaProfeti! NGOKUNGATHI yenzakele yafa. Amazwi "ngokungathi" kufanele akunqande, kodwa anisakholelwa nakwelona lincinci lamazwi am, kwaye ndiliLizwi elenziwe inyama. Akucaci na kuni, NGOKUNGATHI, sisaci esidala? Akucaci na kuni ukuba "kwanga" kukusetyenziswa kwemo yokucingela? Akucaci na kuni ukuba kukufinyezwa kokuthi, ngokungathi kunjalo?

Kodwa hayi, nikholelwa ubuxoki benamba, kuba uBharabhas uthetha njengenamba, kodwa ayinguye uBharabhas, kodwa lirhamncwa elivela emhlabeni. Kwaye ngoku lonke ihlabathi liya kumangaliswa lirhamncwa ngenxa yemiqondiso nemimangaliso yalo yobuxoki, kwaye liya kwenza eminye imiqondiso nemimangaliso emininzi yobuxoki, kwaye alikaphakami ngokupheleleyo kuba ihenyukazi alikatshiswa ngomlilo, kodwa ngoku lityhiliwe ngexesha lalo. Uthetha njengenamba, esithi "NguThixo kuphela owamsindisayo kwinxeba lakhe lentloko" kwaye obu bubuxoki obunyelisayo. UYahweh, uThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade, undithumile mna Yesu waseNazarete njengomsindisi wehlabathi. Mna noBawo simunye kubunye nasenjongweni kwaye uYahweh akamsindisanga uBharabhas kwaye nam andimenzanga, uKrestu Yesu. Nikhohlisiwe kwaye nathinjwa kwaye nabanjiswa kwinkohliso enkulu, kwaye ndithi kuni guqukani nibuyele kuthando lwenu lokuqala kwaye nikwenze oko ngoku, kuba akukho xesha!

Kuba ndim ovavanya iintliziyo, kwaye ndim ovavanya bonke endibabizela elusindisweni, kuba ndicikida bonke emlilweni wocikido! Yabonani! Ndim ohleli njengomcikidi nomhlanjululi wesilivere: kwaye ndihlanjulula oonyana bakaYahweh, ndibacikide njengegolide nesilivere, ukuze banikele eNkosini umnikelo ngobulungisa. Aniqondi na ukuba ndiya kunenza ababingeleli nookumkani phambi koBawo? Nangona isipho sam somoya oyingcwele, enizalwa ngaso ngokutsha ngembewu engenakonakala sigqibelele kwaye asidingi kucikidwa, niphuma ku-Adam ngegazi kwaye kufuneka nivumele ngokupheleleyo umsebenzi wam wenziwe ebomini benu, ngaphandle kokuba imbewu yam iwile emhlabeni onamatye.

Ngoku kufika amaxesha omgwebo kwaye amaxesha obabalo agqityiwe. Nangona abaninzi beya kuhleka amazwi am kwaye bajike ekungakholweni, sele kusaziwa nguYahweh isiphumo. Kuba uYahweh usoloko engqina eluntwini iindlela zalo ezonakeleyo kwaye esohlwaya uluntu ngesono, esazi eluthandweni lwakhe nobulumko obungunaphakade ukuba uya kusindisa abanye. Kwaye njengoko uYahweh wayenza lukhuni intliziyo kaFaro, uya kwenza lukhuni iintliziyo zabaninzi abevayo kodwa bangaphulaphuli. UYahweh uya kuvavanya umntu ngokuphindaphindiweyo. Kwaye abanye baya kuba njengoFaro kwaye benze iintliziyo zabo zibe lukhuni ngakumbi ekungakholweni kodwa abanye baya kushiya iindlela zikaFaro baphakamele kubomi obungunaphakade kuKrestu Yesu, kodwa ngomlilo wocikido.

Qondani ukuba abo bakhetha uBharabhas baya kuzenza abathathi-nxaxheba kulwaphulo-mthetho lukaBharabhas, kwaye abo bala ukubiza egameni lam kude kube sekupheleni baya kugwetywa bonke kuba babeyoliswa kukungabikho kokusesikweni. Kuba umsindisi wenu omtsha ayingomsindisi kodwa lirhamncwa elirhalayo. Kwaye inkqubo yakhe nobukumkani bakhe benziwe ngabo benzakalisa abantwana. Aniziqondi na iindlela zeNamba? Kuba abo balandela iindlela zenamba baxhaphaza abantwana. Babingelela abantwana kuMoleki, bafuna umthombo wabo wobutsha egazini labantwana abathuthunjisiweyo, baxhaphaza abantwana ukuze bazanelise ngokugulayo, kwaye baqhelisela ukubetha umbungu esibelekweni ukuze babulale abancinane phambi kokuzalwa, kwaye banikhokelele ngokufihlakeleyo kwiindlela zabo, kuba zinjalo iindlela zeenkokeli zeBhabheli eyimfihlo kunye nobukumkani benamba. Kwaye ngoku kufika umgwebo kaThixo ngezi zinto nezinye ezininzi. Kuba ukuba beningazibonanga ezi zinto beningayi kuba nasono, kodwa ngoku niyabona kwaye isono senu sihleli.

Anifundanga na? Itsho iNkosi, Kwavakala ilizwi phezulu, isijwili nesililo esikrakra. URakeli, elilela abantwana bakhe, wala ukuthuthuzelwa ngabantwana bakhe, kuba babengekho. Kuba nisilele kumsebenzi enawunikwayo, kwaye nathenjwa ngeemfihlelo zam kwaye nema njengokukhanya kosindiso nophawu lwethemba. Kodwa ngoku nizimanye nabo ndanithuma ukuba nime ngokuchasene nabo. Kwaye ngoku kufika amaxesha endiya kunishiya kwikamva lenu.

Ndiveni kwaye nindive kakuhle nonke eniya kulala ilizwi likaYeshua HaMashiach, kwanelizwi likaKrestu Yesu: aninaxesha! Ndiyanicebisa ukuba nindive eluthandweni lwam ngani bantwana bam kwaye niguquke ngoku! Kuba umlilo wocikido uza kuwa ngokupheleleyo phezu kwenu, kwaye uya kulihlambulula ilizwe kulo lonke ubukumkani benamba kwaye umhlaba awusayi kuba phantsi kweendlela zikaSathana kwakhona kwaye ubukumkani bakhe abuyi kuphinda buvuke, kuba ndiyiNkosi yeeNkosi noKumkani wooKumkani, kwaye ndiya kubatshabalalisa ngokupheleleyo abawileyo. Bonke abala ukuguquka baya kuba nesabelo sabo echibini lomlilo kwaye baya kwabelana ngesohlwayo somtyholi neengelosi zakhe kwelo chibi likhulu lesulfure evuthayo. Ngokunjalo, bonke abaguqukayo ngokuphuma esonweni nangokubiza egameni lam kude kube sekupheleni baya kusindiswa banikwe umvuzo omkhulu ongunaphakade kum.
