Ubungqina Obubini: IBandla
Emisebenzini kaYohane uMbhaptizi
Matshi 02, 2024

Vukani!

Vukani! Vukani! Vukani! Bantu bam, vukani, kuba mna, iNkosi uYesu Krestu, ndibizela abantu bam kum. Musani ukulichasa ilizwi leNkosi xa ndinibizayo ndinipha inceba, uxolelo, noxolo. Alukho uxolo kweli hlabathi; nangona baya kuthi uxolo nokhuseleko, musani ukubakholelwa. Alukho uxolo ngaphandle kwam, uYesu Krestu. Vukani! Vukani abazalwana benu! Abaninzi baya kuwa ngasekhohlo nangasekunene, kodwa akuyi kusondela kuni ukuba nithembekile kwilizwi lam, ilizwi leNkosi.

UMtshakazi wam uya kusiwa kumagumbi angaphakathi amazulu enoxolo nokhuseleko, njengoko bezihlambile iingubo zabo egazini lam, igazi leMvana! Abo bandilahlileyo balibala idini likaYesu Krestu, iPasika egqibeleleyo, ngenxa yezono zoluntu njengento engenaxabiso abayi kuba nalo uxolo. Guqukani nifulathele izono zenu, kuba lilo eli ilixa lokugqibela. Musani ukuzinikela kwakhona ekuqiqa nasekungakholweni, kuba akukho mntu unokuma phambi kwam, uYesu Krestu, ngaphandle kokuba uhlambe iingubo zakhe egazini lemvana wazihlambulula esonweni.

Phumani phakathi kwakhe, bantu bam, kuba ihenyukazi elikhulu liya kugwetywa ngequbuliso kwaye kungabikho lunyango. Ixesha lembandezelo kaYakobi selisondele, kwaye umgwebo omkhulu nentlekele ziya kufika emhlabeni. Ninendlela yokuphuncuka kanye emilebeni yenu ukuba niya kundivuma mna, uYesu waseNazarete, njengeNkosi yenu nikholwe ukuba uThixo wandivusa kwabafileyo. Zixabiseke kangaka na izono zenu kuni? Zibaluleke ngokwaneleyo na iziyolo zesono ukuba nibeke esichengeni unaphakade ebumnyameni obungaphandle? Niyalinqwenela na ichibi lomlilo? Nqulani iNkosi isenokufunyanwa, nize nibize kum ndisenokuva, kuba kusondele ilixa ekungekho mntu unokusebenza ngalo.

Nangona iNkosi iya kuthumela abaprofeti bayo ngexesha lembandezelo kaYakobi, niya kuma phi xa iNkosi ihlutha abantu bayo? Ngubani oya kunikhonza xa iBandla lisusiwe phakathi kwenu? Kuya kubakho indlala yesibhalo elizweni, kwaye kuya kufuneka nimfumane uKrestu ngokunyamezela kude kube sekuphefumleni kwenu kokugqibela, ukuba nisikelelwe ngokwaneleyo ukuba nifike apho. Guqukani nibuyele eNkosini! Phumani kuye, bantu bam, nahlukane naye. Ndimi emnyango ndiyankqonkqoza.

Umoya wesiprofeto wehlele phezu kwabantu bam kwihlabathi liphela, njengoko ndathembisayo kwisibhalo esingcwele.

IZenzo 2:16-21

Hayi, le yinto eyathethwayo ngomprofeti uYoweli, esithi,

Kuya kuthi ke ngemihla yokugqibela, utsho uThixo, Ndiya kuthulula kuMoya wam phezu kwayo yonke inyama; Oonyana benu neentombi zenu boprofeta, Abafana benu babone imibono, Namadoda enu amakhulu aphuphe amaphupha;

Ewe, naphezu kwabakhonzi bam, naphezu kwabakhonzazana bam, Ndiya kuthulula ngaloo mihla kuMoya wam, boprofeta. Ndiya kunika izimanga ezulwini phezulu, Neemiqondiso emhlabeni phantsi: Igazi, nomlilo, nesiqhume somsi; Ilanga liya kujika libe mnyama, Nenyanga ibe ligazi, Phambi kokuba ifike yona imini enkulu, ebonakalayo, yeNkosi. Kuya kuthi ke, bonke abasukuba belinqula igama leNkosi, basindiswe.
