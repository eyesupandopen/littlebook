Kwabam Abathembekileyo Abalindele Ngenyameko INkosi Yenu Engunaphakade!

Yivani amazwi eNkosi yenu engunaphakade, uYeshua HaMashiach, nize kum nentliziyo zenu zikulungele ukwamkela nokulindela umvuzo wenu esandleni sam, kuba inene ixesha lifikile ngoku. Kwanelo xesha kudala lilindelwe xa ndihla ezulwini ngesidumo esikhulu, ngelizwi lesiphatha-zithunywa nangeXilongo likaThixo (kuba elo xilongo liya kuvakala) baze abafileyo bekuKristu bavuswe bengenakonakala, nani nina baphilayo niseleyo niya kunyukela emafini apho niya kwambatha ukungafi, kwaye niya kuhlala ninjalo, nikunye neNkosi yenu engunaphakade ngonaphakade kanaphakade.

NdinguNkosi yeeNkosi noKumkani wooKumkani, kwaye ndingulowo walithengayo ihlabathi ngegazi lam lentlawulelo. Inene, inene, ndayihlawula intlawulo yezono zoluntu ukuze uluntu luxolelaniswe noYahweh, kuba mna, Yesu waseNazarete, ndisisivumelwano senu soxolo esenza abantu baxolelaniswe noThixo, yiyo loo nto ndenza uPawulos wabhalela abaseKorinte esithi: Ngoko ke singabathunywa bakaKristu, ngokungathi uThixo uyanibongoza ngathi, siyanicenga endaweni kaKristu, ukuba nixolelaniswe noThixo. Kuba wamenza isono ngenxa yethu, yena wayengasazi isono, ukuze thina senziwe ubulungisa bukaThixo kuye.

Ngoko ke, bantwana bam, ndinenze ubulungisa bukaThixo ngendlela kaKristu kuni, ithemba lozuko, kwaloo moya wobunyana obungunaphakade enikhala ngawo nithi, ABHA, Bawo, kwaye ngoko nizalwa ngokutsha njengoonyana bakaThixo abangonaphakade kwaye nitywinwe ngaloo moya ungcwele wesithembiso. Qondani ukuba itywina likaYahweh alinakwaphulwa, alinakuguqulwa, kwaye bonke abo batywinwe ngalo bakhuselekile ngonaphakade, ngoko ke qondani ukuba oko uYahweh akubhalileyo wakuvakalisa akukho mntu unokukuguqula. Ngubani ke onganixhwila ezandleni zam? Ngubani onokumoyisa uThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade, uYahweh, onguYise weziKhanyiso? Kutheni ke nisoloko nixhalaba nikhathazeka ngokuba ndiya kunilahla? Kanti, nguwuphi kuni ongabalahlayo abantwana bakhe, ngokungathi loo nto yinto elula emehlweni eNkosi noMsindisi wenu, uYesu waseNazarete.

Inene, inene, izipho nobizo lukaThixo azinakuguqulwa, kwaye abo banikwe isipho sobomi obungunaphakade ngokuvuma uYesu njengeNkosi ngeli lixa bethemba ukuba uThixo wandivusa kwabafileyo, banikwa ubomi obungunaphakade kwaye benziwa ubulungisa bukaThixo kuKristu Yesu iNkosi yabo. Ke ukuba nawuphi na umntu wenza eso sivumo ngentliziyo engenakholo, ndiyalubamba itywina likaThixo ophilayo ukuze ndibatsalele ngokupheleleyo kum ukuze bafikelele ekuguqukeni okupheleleyo ezonweni, hleze imbewu yam iwele emhlabeni olityeni. Ke ukuba nawuphi na umntu akanamoya kaKristu, akangowakhe. Ndingulowo uvavanya aze alinge iintliziyo zabantu ukuze bahlanjululwe emlilweni wam wokuhlanjululwa kuba ndingulowo uhleli ephethe isifutho evuselela amadangatye okuhlanjululwa kuba ndingumkhandi ofutha amalahle ukuze nifikelele kwilifa lenu elipheleleyo kum, uKristu Yesu, ngoko hlalani nithobekile kwiinzame zam kwaye ningawuchasi umtsalane waloo moya wobunyana ozelwe ngaphakathi kwenu ozisa ubulungisa nobungcwele bokwenene. Kwaye ngoko ndiyanicenga bantwana bam ukuba nihambe ngokomoya ukuze ningazalisekisi iinkanuko zenyama.

Kanti, uninzi lwenu luvumela iintliziyo zenu zinihlupha ngokungeyomfuneko ngokucinga ukuba itywina likaThixo linokwaphulwa bubuyatha bomntu. Ndiyanibuza, zithandwa zam? Ingaba u-El Shaddai, kulwazi lwakhe lwangaphambili, wayengayibonanga into yokuba wayethumela mna, Yesu waseNazarete, ukuba ndikhulule aboni ababeza konela kwakhona emva kosindiso? Ingaba uYahweh, uThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade ongadingi mcebisi nangona amashumi amawaka ngamashumi amawaka ejikeleze itrone yakhe, nosebenza zonke izinto ngokwecebo lentando yakhe, wayengakuqondi ukungafezeki kuka-Adam owonakeleyo, kwanabo bonke abantu abazelwe kumhlaba obomvu abathatha inxaxheba egazini lika-Adam?

Hayi bantwana bam, akunakwenzeka ukuba uYahweh angaziqondi ezi zinto kwaye unilungiselele ngazo zonke iindlela waninika zonke iindlela ukuze inkululeko yenu epheleleyo ifike ngokupheleleyo kuni zithandwa zam. Ngoko hambani emthethweni wokholo nangendlela yomoya wam ozelwe ngaphakathi kwenu kwaye nihambe ngokomoya wobomi obukuKristu Yesu. Kuba kuKristu Yesu akukho nto iluncedo nokuba kukwaluka okanye ukungaluki, kodwa yindalo entsha. Kwaye bonke abahamba ngokwalo mgaqo, uxolo malube phezu kwabo, nenceba, naphezu koSirayeli kaThixo. Aniqondi na ukuba olilungisa uya kuphila ngokholo? Ngoko qondani ukuba akukho namnye kwabathembekileyo bam ofezekileyo ngeli xesha, hayi, de kube yinkululeko yabo epheleleyo. Kwaye bonke abakhululweyo abatywinwe ngaloo moya ungcwele wesithembiso baya kona bonke, nokuba kuyaziwa okanye kungaziwa, imini yonke. Ngoko ke, zihlanjululeni egazini lam lentlawulelo mihla le. Yizani kum nivuselele ubudlelwane benu nam kwaye nibugcine bomelele, zithandwa zam. Vumani izono zenu kum mihla le, kwaye nihlale nithobekile kwilizwi lam elizolileyo xa ndisiza kuni ukunohlwaya ngobulungisa. Sele ndisondela kuni kwaye ndilapha kanye phambi kwenu ndinikhuthaza kwaye ndigcina iindlela zenu zicacile ukuze nikwazi ukuhamba loo ndlela imxinwa nincinanana enibizelwe ukuyihamba. Kwaye qhubekani nilangazelela loo mini yenkululeko yenu epheleleyo apho niya kuma nifezekile phambi koYahweh ngokwakhe nambethe ubulungisa endiya kuninika bona ngokupheleleyo kuba ndim, uKristu Yesu, oya kunambathisa ilinen emhlophe ecocekileyo, engenabala, nasici, nambimbi, okanye nayiphi na into enjalo.

Ngoko ke, qondani bantwanana bam, ukuba umgwebo ufikile emhlabeni, kwaloo mgwebo mkhulu waxelwa kwangaphambili ngabaprofeti bamandulo ekufike ngoku ixesha lokuzaliseka kwawo. Iinimba zokuzala azipheli emhlabeni ngoku njengoko umgwebo ulapha kwaye abakhululweyo bam baya kufikelela kwinkululeko yabo epheleleyo. Kwaye kuya kuba njalo ukuba iziganeko ziya kuza, iliza emva kweliza. Iliza ngalinye likhulu kwaye linamandla ngakumbi kwintshabalalo yalo kunelokugqibela de ngequbuliso nithathelwe kukhuseleko kwaye ngequbuliso iBhabheli eyiMfihlelo ingabikho. Iliza ngalinye lizisa umgwebo kwaye iliza ngalinye libetha ngamandla nangengxolo enkulu kunelokugqibela. Kwaye zinjalo izigwebo zam nezilumkiso zam emhlabeni, kuba ndiza njengengonyama yesizwe sakwaYuda kwaye ngobulungisa ndiza kugweba ndenze imfazwe.

Kwaye kuya kuba njalo ukuba phambi kokuba intlekele yokugqibela ifike kuya kwenzeka ngomyalelo wam ukuba abathembekileyo bam abalele kuKristu baya kuvuswa emangcwabeni abo kwaye abathembekileyo bam abaphilayo nabaseleyo baya kuqokelelwa kunye nabo kwaye niya kuya ezulwini lamazulu, apho niya kuma nonke phambi kweso sihlalo sokugweba sikaKristu ukuze nonke namkele ngenxa yezinto ezenziwe emzimbeni. Bonke abathathwayo ukuya kwiBema yam abayi apho ukuze bagwetywe ngenxa yezono zabo kuba namkela ukuxolelwa kwezono ngomhla womntu onesono kwanangalo mhla xa umntu onesono elawula umhlaba olawulo lwakhe lusekupheleni. Kuba umhlaba ngowam, uKristu Yesu, nokuzala kwawo, kwaye ndiyasondela kwaye ndiya kuthatha ngamandla ilifa lam kwabo bahleli ngokungekho mthethweni kwaye umgwebo wabo opheleleyo awulibazisi. Zithandwa zam, qondani ukuba nisukile ekufeni naya ngqo kubomi obungunaphakade kum, uKristu Yesu iNkosi yenu. Kwaye ndingulowo unihlanjulele ngokupheleleyo esonweni ngegazi lam lentlawulelo kuba ndiyiPasika yenu egqibeleleyo endizinikele kuYahweh uThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade, kanye, konke, ngonaphakade kanaphakade ngenxa yezono zoluntu: ngoko ke, akusekho mbingelelo ngenxa yezono.

Ngoko ke, oonyana bakaThixo baya kusondela kwisihlalo sam sokugweba ukuze ndigwebe imisebenzi yabo ukuze bonke bamkele ngoko bakwenzileyo, nokuba kulungile okanye kubi. Kwaye ngoko nonke niya kuzisa amadini obomi benu obukhululweyo emhlabeni eniwanikele enkonzweni yeNkosi yenu engunaphakade. Njengoko kubhaliwe, kuya kwenziwa njalo, kanye njengoko kubhaliwe: Kuba akukho mntu unokubeka esinye isiseko, ngaphandle kweso sibekiweyo, onguYesu Kristu. Kwaye ukuba nawuphi na umntu wakha phezu kwesi siseko, igolide, isilivere, amatye anqabileyo, umthi, ifula, okanye iindiza, Umsebenzi womntu ngamnye uya kubonakaliswa: kuba loo mini iya kuwutyhila, kuba uya kutyhilwa ngomlilo: kwaye umlilo uya kuvavanya umsebenzi womntu ngamnye ukuba unjani. Ukuba umsebenzi womntu, awakhe phezu kwawo, uhleli, uya kufumana umvuzo. Ukuba umsebenzi womntu utshile, uya kulahlekelwa, kodwa yena uya kusindiswa: nangona kunjalo ngokungathi kungomlilo.

Kuya kuba njalo zithandwa zam ukuba ndiya kunivuza ngokupheleleyo ngenkonzo yenu kum kwaye izinto ezitshiswe njengomququ yimililo yam yokugweba niya kulahlekelwa kwaye ngezo zinto zimelana nemililo yam yokugweba, kwanemisebenzi endininike yona ukuba niyenze, umvuzo omkhulu uya kuba ngowenu. Kuba kubhaliwe: Iliso alikabonanga, nendlebe ayivanga, kungangenanga nasentliziyweni yomntu, izinto uThixo azilungiselele abo bamthandayo. Kwaye unjalo ke umgangatho welifa lenu nomvuzo wenu, ukuba akukho namnye umntu emhlabeni oqonda ngokupheleleyo ubukhulu nozuko lwelifa loonyana bakaThixo abangonaphakade abazelwe ngokutsha ngokomoya kaKristu, enithi ngako nizalwe ngokutsha nibe ngoonyana bakaThixo ngonaphakade kanaphakade.

Ngoko qondani zithandwa zam ukuba usuku lwenkululeko yenu epheleleyo lusondele kunokuba nicinga, kwaye lukhawuleza lusiza kuni kanye ngalo mzuzwana kwaye olo suku luza kuzaliseka kungekudala, kungekudala kunokuba nilindele. Qondani ukuba akusekho xesha liseleyo lokulumkisa, akusekho xesha liseleyo lokucenga, akusekho xesha liseleyo lokuqiqa neBhabheli eyimfihlelo kuba umgwebo wayo ugqityiwe kwaye awunakuguqulwa. Hayi, amaxesha ayo okuguquka achithwa, kwaye yawatyeshela ngokupheleleyo amazwi abaprofeti bam nabapostile isithi entliziyweni yayo njengoko yayigculela amazwi am “Ndihleli njengokumkanikazi kwaye andingomhlolokazi, ingomso liya kuba njenganamhlanje kwaye namhlanje kuyafana nayizolo.”

Kanti ngeyure enye intlekele yayo iya kuwela phezu kwayo ngokupheleleyo, kwaye ayisayi kuphinda ivuke. Kodwa endaweni yesizwe esakha saba sikhulu esasihlonipha iindlela zikaYahweh nalapho abantu babelandela uYesu waseNazarete kwaye bengenazintloni zokuthanda abamelwane babo kuya kusala inkangala apho iBhabheli eyiMfihlelo iya kutshatyalaliswa ngeyure enye kuba yomelele iNkosi uThixo oyigwebayo. Kwaye njengoko loo ntlekele isondela ngakumbi zithandwa zam, kunjalo nangenkululeko yenu epheleleyo, usindiso, umvuzo, novuyo olungunaphakade, ngoko musani ukukhathazeka ngomgwebo osondelayo waboni ongeyowenu ukuba niphuma ngokupheleleyo ezonweni zenu.

Kwaye ngoko ke elo qabaza lelihlo liyasondela apho ngomzuzwana omnye niya kuzifumana nimi kwigumbi letrone likaYahweh ngokwakhe apho iitrone zenu zihonjisiwe kwaye apho nihleli njengookumkani nababingeleli abangonaphakade ngelifa leNkosi yenu engunaphakade, eniya kulawula ngalo iintlanga ngentonga yentsimbi kuba kunikwe mna njalo, kwaye ndiya kunika abo boyisayo. Ngoobani aboyisi bam? Kwanabo baphuma ngokupheleleyo esonweni ngokuguquka ngokupheleleyo nokuzilahla izono zabo. Kwaye ndim uKristu Yesu owoyisileyo amaqhinga omtyholi ngokupheleleyo ngonaphakade kanaphakade, emshiya elutshaba oluyinkangala noloyisiweyo ngokupheleleyo oluvuselelwe amadangatye esohlwayo angunaphakade kwaye alungele ukwamkela, kuba linjalo ilifa labo kwaye bafanelekile.

Ngoko ke, ndilungise indlela enamandla kubo bonke abeza kum ngokupheleleyo ukuze boyise ngokupheleleyo amaqhinga omtyholi ngendlela efanayo nam. Ngoko yizani kum ngokupheleleyo ukuze ndinikhokele kuloo ndlela imxinwa nincinanana eya kusindiso lwenu olupheleleyo. Yivani ilizwi lam nina batyibilikileyo nawa endleleni kuba umntu olilungisa uwa kasixhenxe, aze avuke kwakhona, ngoko vukani O' madoda kaThixo nize kum ngokupheleleyo ukuze ndinidibanise kwakhona nomvuzo wenu ngokunihlambulula egazini lam lentlawulelo ukuze nime nambethe ngokupheleleyo ubulungisa bam. Qondani ukuba aboyisi bam baya kudla ilifa lazo zonke izinto kwaye akukho nto ilungileyo iya kubanjwa kubo. Kwaye ngoko aboyisi bam, baya kukhanya ngonaphakade, kwanjengokuqaqamba kwesibhakabhaka: nani nina nijikise abaninzi babuyela ebulungiseni, niya kukhanya njengeenkwenkwezi, ngonaphakade kanaphakade. Ngoko ke ngoku niyazi le nto bantwana bam ukuba ngoku lixesha lokushiya iinkathazo zalo mhlaba nize niphumle kum kwaye nihlale ninyulu njengentombi enyulu egazini lam lentlawulelo.

Ngoko ke, zithandwa zam yimani niqine phambi kwentlekele esondelayo, kuba niyalubona olo suku loyikekayo lusondela, kwaye ndingulowo uvule amehlo abathobekileyo entliziyweni ukuze niyibone inyaniso yehlabathi lenu kwaye ningoyiswa yinkohliso enamandla engeyoyenu. Qondani ukuba mna, uYeshua HaMashiach, ndingulowo wenza iimfama zibone, ngoko ke, yizani kum ukuze ndithambise amehlo enu ngesithambiso samehlo kwaye nibone ngokuyinxenye eli hlabathi lingendawo njengoko linjalo ngokwenene eniphila kulo. Ngoko musani ukuwoyika umgwebo waboni kuba loo mgwebo ayingowenu zithandwa zam, hayi umgwebo ayingowabakhululweyo kuba nisindisiwe kwingqumbo yeMvana nakwingqumbo kaThixo, ngoko ke yimani niqine nikhuselekile elusindisweni lwenu zithandwa zam kuba usuku lusondele kwaye lukhawuleza lusiza kuni ngakumbi ngomzuzwana.

Ewe elo xesha losuku lwenkululeko yenu epheleleyo lifikile phezu kwenu zithandwa zam kwaye elo xesha lingunaphakade sele liza kufika kwaye lisondela kuni ngakumbi nangakumbi ngomzuzwana. Ndiqinisekisile emimoyeni yenu, ndibonise imiqondiso phezulu nemiqondiso emhlabeni, ndenze imimangaliso emazulwini ndade ndakhupha iintlekele emgwebeni phezu kwaboni kwaye ndavusa abo baleleyo ukuze baqonde ukuba ngoku lixesha lenkululeko epheleleyo yabantwana bakaThixo uSomandla, kwanomtshakazi wam nomzimba wam.

Musani ukuzimanya nabo kwiindlela zabo zokungakholwa, kuba bafuna ukubanga nokuchaza imiqondiso nemimangaliso emininzi eboniswa emazulwini apho uninzi lwayo luzotywe ngomnwe kaYahweh ngokwakhe. Niyilibele iGenesis zithandwa zam? Kuba kubhaliwe: Waza wathi uThixo, Makubekho izikhanyiso esibhakabhakeni sezulu, zokwahlula imini nobusuku, kwaye mazibe zezemiqondiso, namaxesha, nemihla neminyaka. Kwaye mazibe zizikhanyiso esibhakabhakeni sezulu zokukhanyisa phezu komhlaba: kwaba njalo. Ngoko ke, qondani ukuba uYahweh ulawula ngokupheleleyo kwaye akukho nto ivunyelweyo kwindalo yakhe angayivumeliyo, kwaye angayaziyo. Ngoko ke, musani ukuzimanya nabo kwiziqiqo zabo zokungakholwa zokuba uluntu ngoku ngandlel' ithile lulawula kwaye lusebenzisa amazulu, nangona uluntu ngokuqinisekileyo luzama ukumoyisa uThixo kwaye iinzame zalo zihlala ziphelela kwilize.

Kanti, abawileyo bayazama ukufihla imiqondiso yam nezaziso ezininzi uYahweh azibeke emazulwini zomtshato womyeni nomtshakazi wakhe. Ngoko thuthuzelekani eluzukweni lukaThixo, nisazi ukuba nantoni na abawileyo abavunyelweyo ukuyenza isebenza kuphela iinjongo ezingunaphakade zikaThixo ongunaphakade kuba zinjalo iindlela zakhe ukuba neentshaba zakhe ziyalithobela ilizwi lakhe. Kodwa ningaphazami uYahweh ulawula ngokupheleleyo kwaye akukho nto yenzekayo angayivumeliyo ngobabalo lwakhe, inceba, nomgwebo wakhe ongunaphakade ekugqibeleni phezu kwabangaguqukiyo nabawileyo. Kwanaloo mgwebo mkhulu waxelwa kwangaphambili ngoku oza kuqhambuka emhlabeni ngendlela enobundlobongela.

Kwaye olo suku luphezu kwenu zithandwa zam kwaye NDIYEZA KUNINA! Nangona abo bahleli ngokungekho mthethweni bebeya kufuna ukufihla uzuko lwam, andiyi kwabelana ngozuko lwam nomnye kwaye ngoko iinzame zezinto ezibuthathaka nezililize azinamsebenzi wokufihla ukubonakala kukaKristu Yesu kumtshakazi wam nomzimba wam. Hayi, andinakuthintelwa okanye ndifihlwe nakancinane kwaye ihlabathi lonke liya kwazi ngokubonakala kwam, nangona abawileyo beya kufuna ukufihla ukubonakala kwam ngemfazwe nohlaselo lwabo lwasemzini lobuxoki. Qondani ukuba akukho bantu basemzini abavela kwamanye amahlabathi ajikelezayo obuxoki kodwa ngabawileyo abavalelweyo emazulwini ngonaphakade abazenzisayo kuba bonke bangabazenzisi abeza kukhohlisa nokulahlekisa.

Qondani ukuba ukubonakala kwam akufani nokuza kwam kwesibini kwaye kuyimfihlelo efihliweyo ngaphakathi kwimfihlelo enkulu kaThixo endayityhilayo, mna, uYeshua HaMashiach, kumpostile wam uPawulos. Ukuza kwam kwesibini kuye kwaziwa kwalindelwa iminyaka ngoku kwaye kuza novuko lwabalungileyo. Ekubonakaleni kwam ndiza kuqokelela abangcwele bam kum ukuze ndibavuze kwaye nditshate umtshakazi wam kwaye ndidle nabandwendwe bam abahloniphekileyo. Kusekubonakaleni kwam apho ndiya kuxhwila kum abathembekileyo abeza kum ngokuguquka okupheleleyo ndibathabathele kukhuseleko lwamazulu de kudlule umgwebo emhlabeni, kuba amaxesha omgwebo afikile phezu kwehlabathi lonke kwaye ngakumbi phezu kwabawileyo.

Qondani ukuba ukuza kwam kwesibini kulapho ndiza nabakhululweyo bam kunye ne-144,000 yam, nomkhosi wonke wezulu ukuze nditshabalalise ubukumkani bukaSathana ngokupheleleyo apho intshabalalo yobukumkani bakhe ayisayi kuphinda ibuyele. Kwaye ekuzeni kwam kwesibini bonke baya kubona kwaye bonke baya kwazi kwaye akukho nkohliso inokukhohlisa nabani na kulo msitho. USathana ayingokumkani kwaye abawileyo abangoothixo kodwa ngabazenzisi abazenzisayo. Kwaye uSathana uya kubotshwa aze aphoswe enzonzobileni iminyaka eliwaka ukuze angaphindi azikhohlise iintlanga kwaye de akhululwe ngexesha lakhe elifutshane kwaye uya kuhlala apho ebotshelelwe ngetyathanga elikhulu kwintuthumbo noloyiko olukhulu lwelifa lakhe kwelo chibi lomlilo nesulfure angasayi kuze abaleke kulo. Kwaye emva kwexesha lakhe elifutshane uya kuphoswa ngokupheleleyo kwelo chibi livuthayo elinukayo lesulfure evuthayo elivutha awona madangatye ashushu aluhlaza. Kwaye ngoko ke kuya kwenzeka umgwebo wetrone emhlophe enkulu, ngokuqinisekileyo phambi kokuba uYahweh adale izulu elitsha nomhlaba omtsha, aze enze zonke izinto zibe ntsha ekugqityweni okungunaphakade nokugqibeleleyo.

Kwaye kubhaliwe kunjalo, kuya kwenzeka kungekudala ukuba ndihle ezulwini ngesimemelelo esikhulu, ngelizwi lesiphatha-zithunywa, nangexilongo likaThixo, kwaye eso sandi sixilongo asilibazisi, kwaye lixilongo lokugqibela lexesha lam lobabalo, njengoko ihlabathi lijikela ekujonganeni nomgwebo walo osemnyango nonzima. Kwaye ndiya kubahlanganisa abanyulwa bam kuzo zone iimbombo zomhlaba, nkqu nabo bonke abalele ukufa bekuKristu, ewe, bonke abalele ukufa bekuKristu betywinwe nguloo moya ungcwele wesithembiso. Kwaye iintlungu zokuzala ezingapheliyo ziya kuvulela umhlaba ukuba uzale abo balele ukufa, kodwa ekungenakwenzeka ukuba ukufa kubabambe nomzuzwana omnye. Kwaye nina ke abahleliyo besaphila niya kunyakulwa emoyeni kunye nabo, ukuze nindikhawulele emafini, kwaye ngephanyazo leso niya kubona.

Ewe, niya kubona ngawenu amehlo ubuqaqawuli obungunaphakade boThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade, uYise weziKhanyiso onqwenela ubukho benu, nolungiselele lo mzuzu ngonaphakade kuba nanisoloko nisenhliziyweni kaYehova. Kwaye niya kundibona mna, uMsindisi wenu ongunaphakade nowaphakade, owazinikela ngokupheleleyo kwintlungu njengoko ndazincamayo ukuze niphile phambi koYise weziKhanyiso, owazala izikhanyiso zehlabathi. Ewe, niya kundibona ngawenu amehlo, nina nonke eninokholo kwiNkosi yenu ezukisiweyo, eningekayiboni ngawenu amehlo.

Niya kubona ngawenu amehlo ubuqaqawuli bam obupheleleyo uYehova andinike bona kwilifa lam, endiya kwabelana ngalo ngokupheleleyo naboyisi bam, nendiya kuvuza ngalo bonke abeza kum benemisebenzi emelana novavanyo lomlilo. Inene, inene, niya kubona ngawenu amehlo umkhosi wasezulwini, abaninzi bawo abathunyelweyo ukuba balungiselele abo baziindlalifa zosindiso olungunaphakade. Kwaye niya kuma nigqibelele ngokupheleleyo, nidalwe ngokutsha kwimfezeko yasezulwini, kwaye ngokuqinisekileyo niya kuma nimangalisiwe koko isiphelo senu sanaphakade siya kunizisela kona, noko ubutyebi bobuqaqawuli belifa lam kwabangcwele buthetha kona kuni.

Qondani zithandwa zam, (nina nonke eningabuthandiyo ubomi benu kwada kwasekupheleni) nani eniphume ezonweni zenu ngenguquko epheleleyo: Isiphelo senu sanaphakade sisiqalo senu sanaphakade, apho niya kuma kumzuzwana wenu wokuqala wobomi obungunaphakade, kwaye akukho nto iya kuphinda inonakalise nakancinane. Kuba niya kudalwa ngokutsha nifezekile kwimfezeko engcwele, kwaye soze niphinde nonakaliswe kuba niya kudalwa ngokutsha ningenakonakala.

Kwaye ngonaphakade kanaphakade niya kukhonza uYehova, kwaye ngonaphakade kanaphakade niya kuba nam naphi na apho ndiya khona. Kwaye ndiya kunibuyisela konke okudliwe yinkumbi nako konke uSathana akunibeleyo. Niya kubuyekezwa ngokupheleleyo ngemisebenzi yenu nomgudu wenu wothando eniwubonakalisileyo egameni lam, ngokuthi nilungiselele abangcwele kwaye nisalungiselela. Siya kudla okunonileyo kwelizwe ngexesha lolawulo lwam lweminyaka eliwaka, apho niya kulawula nam njengookumkani nababingeleli bam, kwaye kuya kuba lulawulo lobulungisa ngonaphakade kanaphakade.
