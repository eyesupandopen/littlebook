Yabonani! Ndiyeza: Incwadi Yothando Nentuthuzelo Kwabathembekileyo KuKristu

Yivani aMazwi kaYeshua HaMashiach nize namkele aMazwi am entuthuzelo, nize nive aMazwi am ovuyo, kuba inkululeko yenu epheleleyo ifikile kwaye isondele. Inene, inene, ndingulowo uzayo ngenxa yeBandla lam, kwaye umyeni wenu akalibazisi, mtshakazi wam, ngoko ke yimani niqinile elukholweni olukhulu nize nivumele aMazwi am namandla am nobugorha bam bube luzinzo lwamaxesha enu namandla osindiso, kwaye ukoyika uYehova bubutyebi benu.

Kwananjalo amaxesha anifikele, zithandwa zam, kwaye amaxesha okuzityhila kwam ngokupheleleyo afikile. Kwaye ndiza kuzityhila komnye nomnye ngokulandelelana. Ndiza kuqala ndizityhile ngokupheleleyo kwabathembekileyo kuKristu emazulwini, ngequbuliso nangokungalindelekanga ngoku ngesidumo esikhulu seNgelosi eyiNtloko, kwaye elo xilongo likhulu liya kuvakala kuba linikelwe esandleni ukuba likhale ngomyalelo wam ngaloo mzuzu uchanekileyo ugqibeleleyo owakhethwa nguYahweh ngokwakhe kwaphakade! Kwanakwizicwangciso zakhe ezingunaphakade nezingapheliyo zosindiso lohlanga luka-Adam kwaye ilizwi lalo liya kuzalisa umhlaba kwaye abaThembekileyo kuKristu baya kuva besithi "Nyukelani apha!" ngequbuliso nangokungalindelekanga ngoku.

Kodwa abangathembekanga nabanenkani baya kushiywa ngasemva kunye nabawileyo nabangakholwayo ukuze babize egameni lam kude kube sekupheleni kwabo, kwaye bonke abakwenzayo oko baya kuthathwa basiwe kusindiso olungunaphakade nozuko, kwanomvuzo ongunaphakade ongumvuzo nobunzima bawo ngokwenene obungafanelekanga ukuthelekiswa neentsizi novavanyo lwalo mhlaba, ezincinci nezingabalulekanga xa zithelekiswa. Utsho lowo wathwala isohlwayo esipheleleyo sezono zenu enyameni yam engenasono, ndiphalaza ngentlungu enkulu igazi lam elingenasono kude kube lithontsi lokugqibela, kwaye andibuyisanga nto kuni, zithandwa zam. Ngoko ke, khangelani kum, uYesu waseNazarete, kwanemvana kaThixo engumvelisi nomgqibelelisi wokholo lwenu, owathi ngenxa yovuyo olwalubekwe phambi kwam, ndanyamezela umnqamlezo, ndilidela ihlazo, kwaye ndibekwe ngasekunene kwetrone kaThixo. Selah!

Kwananjalo nina, abathembekileyo kuKristu, niya kuthathwa nonke nisiwe ezulwini lamazulu apho ndiya kuninikela kuYahweh ngokwakhe! Kwaye niya kumangaliswa bubukhazikhazi eniya kwambathiswa ngabo, kwaye niya kwazi njengoko naziwa, kwaye niya kubona njengoko niboniwe, kwaye niya kuva njengoko niviwe nguThixo ongunaphakade woBulungisa, kwanaye uYahweh ngokwakhe okhumbula iLizwi ngalinye kumthandazo ngamnye enikhe nawuthetha nokuba kungazwi liphezulu ngezixhobo zenu zonxibelelwano, okanye buthule ezintliziyweni zenu, kuba uYahweh ngumphengululi weentliziyo, kwaye yintoni ekhoyo enokufihlakala emehlweni akhe? Inene, inene, yintoni ekhoyo enokufihlakala emehlweni am? Ngoko ke, qondani ukuba uBawo nam simunye kubunye nenjongo kwaye konke uBawo anako, ukunikele ezandleni zam ngenxa yosindiso luka-Adam, kwaye ngokwenene usindiso nokungcwaliswa komhlaba ngokwawo, kuba umhlaba ngowam kwaye ngobulungisa ndiyeza kugweba ndize ndilwe nabawileyo abamaxesha abo afikileyo, kwaye isigwebo sabo esipheleleyo asilibazisi kwaye abayi kusinda.

Ngoko ke, ndiyaninika ukuba niqonde, zithandwa zam, ukuba nime niqine ngenxa yam kwaye niqhubeke nigcina izibane zenu zizaliswe nge-oli yomoya wam endinilungiselele yona kwaye nihambe ngoMoya wam woBulungisa njengoko nihamba ngokholo kuba olilungisa uya kuphila ngokholo, Selah! Kwaye ukuze ningahendeki ukuba nihambe ngeli lixa lihlwileyo ngamandla enu ukuze nizalise izibane zenu nge-oli engahlambulukanga esekelwe kwimisebenzi yenu, kodwa zalisani izibane zenu nge-oli ecocekileyo yomoya wam ukuze iindlela zenu zikhanyiselwe, kuba inene, inene, iLizwi lam sisibane ezinyaweni zenu. Kwaye ndinizalisile nonke ngokupheleleyo ngomoya wam woBunyana kwaye ndiwuthulule umoya wam kule mihla yokugqibela njengoko kwathembiswa nguYoweli kwimibhalo yaMandulo eyanikwa oobawo bam, abangoobawo benu ngelifa lesithembiso kuba nina ningabantwana besithembiso, kwanabantwana baka-Abraham othembekileyo, Selah!

Mandiniyalele indlela yokuthena iintambo zezibane zenu kuba nina nizizibane zeHlabathi ezingenakufihlwa! Kungendlela yaMazwi am nomoya wam enifumana ngayo imiyalelo ebulungiseni, kwaye ngomoya wam wobunyana noyiseka bubulungisa, ukuze inene, inene, niphile ngobulungisa ezintweni zonke. Kuba kungomoya wam wobunyana apho ubulungisa bam bokwenene bunikwa ngaphakathi kwenu, apho inene, inene nonke ninokhetho lokuba niya kuthobela bani, nokuba nithobela isono kude kube sekufeni, okanye ukuthobela kude kube sebulungiseni kude kube bubomi obungunaphakade nomvuzo opheleleyo. Ngoko ke, thobelani imiyalelo yam ethile yobomi benu nize nibe nolulamo kwilizwi lam nakumazwi abo ndibathumayo nabathetha egameni lam, kwaye ngeziqhamo zabo niya kubazi, nangaMazwi abo endibanika wona ngenxa yosindiso nentlalo-ntle yenu. Kwaye ndiyanilayela ukuba nisebenzele usindiso lwenu ngokoyika nangokungcangcazela, kwaye ndiyanilayela ukuba ningalubambi ulungileyo xa kusemandleni ezandla zenu ukulufeza.

Ngoko ke, qondani ukuba ndinixhobisile ndanenza nakwazi ukwenza uninzi lwenu ngeenjongo zam zosindiso kwaye kufuneka nixhasane ngothando. Ngoko ke, musani ukubufumba ubutyebi bezinto eziphathekayo nezemali endininike zona, kwanokubufumba kwabanye ngeendlela zikaSathana ofuna ukubeka imilinganiselo nobunzima kulungileyo lukaYahweh, olungenakulinganiswa ngokwenene kuba akukho sikali sikhoyo ezulwini nasemhlabeni esaneleyo ukulinganisa intabalala kaYahweh ayilungiselele ebusweni bukaYesu Kristu kwabo bamthandayo.

Ngoko ke, phumani phakathi kwabo ngokupheleleyo nize ningabi nenxaxheba ezonweni zabo, musani ukuzifumbela intabalala yemali nezinto eziphathekayo zikaYahweh ezinikwe ebusweni bukaYesu Kristu ngokungathi kungamandla esandla senu eniyifezileyo le ntabalala ukuba ifihlwe kwaye ifunjelwe iinkanuko zenu. Kwananjalo ukuze nithuthuzeleke kuba nizilungiselele ngekratshi nangesono, ukuze nigculele abo bangenanto, kwanokuyoliswa ngamanani kwii-akhawunti zenu ngehlazo elikhulu.

Ngoko ke, qondani kwaye nilunyukiswe ukuba bonke abenza izinto ezinjalo ukusuka komkhulu kuye komncinci, baya kufumana ilahleko enkulu yomvuzo kwaye ngokwenene baya kuzithathela isikhundla sabona baphantsi ebukumkanini bamazulu! Kwaye oko kungenxa yokubawa kwenu imisebenzi yenu iya kutsha ibe luthuthu esihlalweni sam sokugweba apho niya kuma nonke nize niphendule. Ngoko ke, yabelani zonke izinto kwimfuneko yabangcwele ngothando omnye komnye kwaye ningabambi nto ilungileyo, kuba inene, inene, intabalala yenu yezinto eziphathekayo neyemali inikwe sisandla sam ngeenjongo zam ezivela koovimba bakaYahweh, ngoko ke, musani ukubufumba ubutyebi beNkosi kodwa yenzani ezo zinto endiya kuzikhokelela amanyathelo enu enkonzweni naseluthandweni kubangcwele.

Phumani ezonweni zenu, bantu bam, hleze nibe nenxaxheba kwizigwebo zabo, kuba ezi ziindlela zeBhabheli eyiMfihlo kwaye amaxesha esigwebo sayo afikile, kwaye unamandla uAdonai Yahweh oyigwebayo ngesahlulo esiphindwe kabini sesigwebo, ngoko ke mkani ezindleleni zayo nasezonweni zayo ezingamkelekanga kwaye ezikhohlakele ngokupheleleyo emehlweni am! Selah!

Ewe, ngenene, ndicebisa wonke umntu ukuba aqhubeke ephuma ezonweni zakhe aze angene ngokupheleleyo ebulungiseni bam, kwaye andivumele ndibe kukulunga kwakhe okwaneleyo ezintweni zonke, kuba inene, inene, ndinguye.

Ndingulowo uzayo emafini ngenxa yabathembekileyo kuKristu ngequbuliso nangokungalindelekanga ngoku, kuba ihlabathi, ekungakholweni kwalo, alilindelanga ukuba uKristu Yesu abonakale emafini njengoko kuthenjisiwe esibhalweni. Noko ke, lo mzuzu awuyi kunibamba, zithandwa zam, njengesela ebusuku, ukuba nithe nahlala ninolulamo kwimiyalelo yam naza nalandela iinkcukacha zemiyalelo yam ngeentliziyo zenu zonke, nisazi ukuba ngenxa yokukhuthala nokuthembeka kwenu kuza imivuzo epheleleyo yelifa labathembekileyo kuKristu, elithi ngokwenene, lingangenanga ngokupheleleyo ezintliziyweni zabantu ngeli xesha. Ngoko ke, musani ukuvumela uSathana athenge imivuzo yenu yelifa ngeendlela zika-Esawu, owayengumntu ongcolileyo nomhenyuzi owathengisa ubuzibulo bakhe ngesitya sesuphu esalahlwa embalini. Selah!

Ngoko ke, qondani ukusondela kwexesha lokubonakala kwam nokuba isondele kangakanani inkululeko yenu epheleleyo, zithandwa zam. Ngoko ke, ndiyaninika ukuba niqonde ukuba umkhosi wezulu uthathe iindawo zawo kwiindidi zawo nakwiimo zawo, kwaye uzibonakalisile enkonzweni, kwaye umi ulungele. Bazithathile iindawo zabo, kwaye balungile! Ngoko ke, qondani, ukuba ndiphethe elo xilongo likhulu esandleni, ukuba ngomyalelo wam abafileyo kuKristu baya kuvuswa bengenakonakala, kwaye nina baphilayo nabasalayo niya kuthathwa ngequbuliso niye kundihlangabeza emafini kwaye ngoko niya kwenziwa nifane neNkosi yenu ngonaphakade, kwaye kwabo boyisileyo ngokupheleleyo baya kuhlala benam kuba ndiya kunithathela kum emnqophisweni njengabam kanye, kwaye mininzi imisebenzi emikhulu eniya kuyenza, kuba umtshakazi wam ngumkhosi omkhulu wabaProfeti, ooKumkani, nababingeleli abaya kuxhotyiswa ngamandla amakhulu njengomtshakazi wam oliqhawe, kwaye ngubani na oya kuba nako ukumelana nomtshakazi wam wabangafiyo abaxhotyisiweyo abakhululwe emhlabeni ngamandla amakhulu nobulungisa, Selah!

Ngoko ke, qondani ukuba ixesha elichanekileyo elakhethwa nguYahweh kulwazi lwakhe lwangaphambili belisoloko liyimini, ixesha, neyure echanekileyo, ngaphandle kokuba lalifihliwe entliziyweni kaYahweh yedwa de kwafika amaxesha afanelekileyo okutyhilwa kwalo emazulwini kuphela, kuba eli xesha alityhilwanga emhlabeni, nangona uSathana emi elungele ukuginya bonke abashiyekileyo nabasalayo emhlabeni, ngoko ke, bonke ababiza egameni likaYesu kude kube sekupheleni kwabo baya kusindiswa. Qondani ukuba eli xesha lichanekileyo alizange linikwe noSathana kodwa kufuneka eme elungile de akhululwe kulawulo lwakhe olufutshane emva koko aya kubotshwa ngetyathanga elikhulu enzonzobileni ekuthuthunjisweni nasentlungwini apho aya kuliqonda ngokupheleleyo ilifa lakhe elizayo.

Kwaye kunjalo ke ukuba ndize kuninika ukuqonda kwamaxesha eniphila kuwo kwaye ndikwenza oko ngokuninika kwakhona kwa-lawo Mazwi endawanika uPetros ukuba athethe nezihlwele ngaloo mini inkulu yePentekoste kule minyaka emininzi yeYubhile edlulileyo ngoku, apho noko kunjalo, aMazwi akhe avakala eyinyaniso ngonaphakade ekuzalisekeni ngonaphakade nangonaphakade. Ngoko ke ndithi kuni ukuba oku koko, kwathethwayo ngumprofeti uYoweli, Kwaye kuya kuthi ngemihla yokugqibela, utsho uThixo, ndiya kuthulula umoya wam phezu kwayo yonke inyama, kwaye oonyana benu, neentombi zenu ziya kuprofeta, kwaye abafana benu baya kubona imibono, kwaye amadoda enu amadala aya kuphupha amaphupha. Kwaye phezu kwezicaka zam, naphezu kwezicakakazi zam ndiya kuthulula uMoya wam ngaloo mihla, kwaye ziya kuprofeta. Kwaye ndiya kubonisa imimangaliso ezulwini phezulu, neempawu emhlabeni phantsi, igazi, nomlilo, nomphunga womsi. ILanga liya kujikwa libe mnyama, nenyanga ibe ligazi, phambi kokuba ifike loo mini inkulu nedumileyo yeNkosi. Kwaye kuya kuthi, nabani na othe walibiza iGama leNkosi, uya kusindiswa.

Ngoko ke, ndiyeza kuvakalisa la Mazwi ezalisekile ezindlebeni zenu kanye ngale mini, kwaye ngequbuliso nangokungalindelekanga ngoku abathembekileyo kuKristu baya kuthathwa baze bakhululwe baze baguqulwe baze bazukiswe baze bavuzwe ngonaphakade. Ngoko ke qondani ukuzaliseka okungequbuliso okuzayo ngequbuliso. Musani ukubeka indlebe kubabeki bemihla nakumaYuda angakuqondiyo oko bakufundisayo, kwananenyaniso yokwenene yesibhalo abayijikayo kube konakala kwabo.

Ngoko ke, ndiyaninika ukuba niqonde ukuba ukuhlanganiswa kwabathembekileyo kuKristu akubotshelelwanga ngokukodwa kuyo nayiphi na imini engcwele, okanye iintsuku zemisitho, okanye nawuphi na omnye umhla okanye isiganeko esanikwa uSirayeli wamandulo emthethweni kaMoses, kwanomthetho ozaliseke ngokupheleleyo kuKristu Yesu iNkosi yenu engunaphakade yoBulungisa. Njengoko ndinixelele kumaxesha adlulileyo ndiyanixelela kwakhona zithandwa zam: Ngoko ke makungabikho mntu unigwebayo ekutyeni nasekuseleni, okanye ngokubhekiselele kwimini engcwele, okanye kwinyanga entsha, okanye kwiintsuku zeSabatha, Ezilithunzi nje lezinto ezizayo: kodwa umzimba ukuKristu.

Noko ke, qondani ukuba esi siganeko sinokwenzeka nangawuphi na umzuzwana nakuyiphi na imini kwaye sinokwenzeka ngemini ebalulekileyo, ixesha, okanye iyure okanye nangeentsuku ezingcwele, okanye iintsuku zemisitho, okanye iintsuku zeSabatha, ezilithunzi lezinto ezizayo! Ngoko ke, lindani nize nilinde nize nilindele ngokunyanisekileyo ukubonakala kwam kuba amaxesha enu enkululeko epheleleyo afikile. Kwaye ngoko ndiyaninika ukuba niqonde kwaye nangenxa yokuthobela kwenu, ukuba nilinde kwaye nilinde yonke imihla (kwaye kungekhona nje amaxesha enu okubukela aphezulu enizimisele wona) kwaye ninikele ingqalelo engakumbi kula Mazwi ngokulindela okupheleleyo ukuzaliseka, kuba akusekho kulibazisa. Selah!

NdinguKristu Yesu kwaye ndingulowo uthumele la Mazwi ngomthunywa wam nesicaka sam kwaye ngomthunywa wam nesicaka sam la Mazwi athunyelwe. Kwananjengokhuthazo ukuze iBandla lam, umzimba wam, kwanomtshakazi wam athuthuzelwe ngentuthuzelo enkulu kwaye niqonde ngokupheleleyo ukusondela okuchanekileyo kwexesha lesi siganeko, ukuze ningabanjwa ngokumangaliswa kwesi siganeko esiqinisekileyo ukuba siya kwenzeka ngequbuliso ngoku.

NdinguYeshua HaMashiach oza kuhamba ngoku athumele amangqina am amabini nezibane zam ezibini, kwananjengoko kuvakaliswe kwiVangeli engunaPhakade, kuba uYahweh akazange azishiye engenabungqina, kwaye amangqina am amabini aya kungqina ngawo onke amaxesha kwaye ngokwembono yasemhlabeni nasezulwini, kwaye uEnoki uMbhali uzisa imibhalo yakudala emqulwini wakhe kwaye ubungqina babo buya kongezwa kwiVangeli engunaPhakade eya kunyamezela ngonaphakade kunye nawo onke aMazwi kaYahweh aya kubuyiselwa emhlabeni ngokugqibeleleyo njengoko kubhaliwe emazulwini, kwananjalo ukuze imisebenzi kamtyholi ichithwe ngokupheleleyo kwaye iindlela naMazwi kaYahweh zikhukulise umhlaba ngobulungisa kuwo onke amaxesha nangonaphakade! Selah.
