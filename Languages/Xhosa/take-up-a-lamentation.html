Thabathani Isililo

Amazwi iNkosi indinike wona, ndiwavakalisa elubala: Yivani iLizwi leNkosi!

Thabathani isililo; inene, thabathani isililo ngenxa yabo basabambelele kweli hlabathi liwileyo. Kuba bathi ezintliziyweni zabo, "Yintoni enokundihlela? Njengoko bekunjalo mandulo, kuya kuqhubeka ngokungenasiphelo; kwaye mna nabam siya kuqhubeka siphila kamnandi, kwaye siya kuzifumbela iimpahla zeli hlabathi nobutyebi bemali, eziya kusisindisa ngexesha leentswelo." Nithi ezintliziyweni zenu, "Silumkile, kwaye siphumelele; intlekele ayisayi kusihlela, njengoko ingasoze isihlele, kuba uThixo wethu akayi kulivumela isizwe sethu siwe; kuba silumkile kwaye sinamandla." Kanti anilumkanga, kodwa nilumkile ngokwenu ukuzicingela; kwaye ndinike iintshaba zenu uloyiso phezu kwenu.

Owu, mntu, ngubani na oya kukusindisa kwiyure yakho yomgwebo esondela kwihlabathi liphela? Iinkokeli zakho ziya kukusindisa na? Inene, anditsho, njengoko unyana wentshabalalo esiza ngakuni kwaye sele ehamba elubala phakathi kwenu, ekhohlisa abaninzi ngamazwi amnandi neentetho ezimnandi. Imali yakho iya kukusindisa na? Hayi, bonke ubutyebi bakho buya kuthiwa abunaxabiso kwangoko, kwaye akuyi kubakho mbuyekezo yemisebenzi yobomi bakho oyifumbe ngokubawa. Owu, akwaba ubungazishiyanga iindlela zeNkosi kwaye ubungamthandile ummelwane wakho kwaye ubuncedile abasweleyo ngobuninzi bakho.

Kodwa hayi, nibufumba kanye obo buninzi uThixo aninike bona ukuze anelise umphefumlo wenu. Inene, ndininikile abaninzi benu okungaphezulu kokwaneleyo kuni nasekusikeleleni ummelwane wenu; kodwa hayi, nithi, Ndiya kuzigcinela obu buninzi bukhulu kuba ngokuqinisekileyo uThixo undisikelele ngenxa yokulunga kwam namandla am. Yabonani, ndinixelela into ingekenzeki, ukuze ngeyure yenu yentlekele nikhumbule ilizwi lam namazwi omlomo wam endiwaphefumlele kubaprofeti bam. Kuba andibathumi ukuba bathethe okwabo, kodwa ndibathuma njengesilumkiso kuni, ukuze nisindise umphefumlo wenu ngokuguqukela kum, iNkosi uYesu Krestu, onako ukunisindisa ngokugqibeleleyo. Kodwa nithi entliziyweni yenu, Usindiso? Andidingi kusindiswa, njengoko ndimangalisa ngendlela endazalwa ngayo. Kanti izono zenu livumba elibi ezimpondweni zikaBawo, kwaye ilizwe libolile ngenxa yobugwenxa benu. Anazi na ukuba nazalwa nifile kwizigqitho nasezonweni, ukonakalisa kanye indalo yenu?

Nangona bendiya kuninika indalo entsha, ecocekileyo, umbingelelo wam niwubala njengongenaxabiso kwaye ninamathele kwinyama yenu ebolileyo.

Thabathani isililo ngenxa yabantu besizwe senu esakha saba sikhulu abalilahlileyo ilizwi lam. Kuba usizi olukhulu nentlungu ziya kunihlela ngesiquphe. Kuya kubakho ukuzamazama okukhulu, kwaye umkhosi wam onamandla wasezulwini uya kunisa intshabalalo enkulu phezu kwehenyukazi elikhulu, kwaye aliyi kuphinda livuke, nangona intsalela iya kusindiswa. Kwanasentlekeleleni yenu nentshabalalo esondelayo ngokuqinisekileyo ndiya kuthumela abaprofeti bam kuni. Niya kubazi ngeziqhamo zabo, ngamazwi, nangeempiliso; iimpiliso ezininzi nemimangaliso ndiya kuyenza ngezandla zabo. Kanti, nangona kunjalo abaninzi baya kutshabalala bafe ezonweni zabo, kodwa abalulamileyo baya kulidla ilifa umhlaba; kuba ilifa lam likum, lilungele ukwabiwa kwabathembekileyo. Ndingayolula ixesha elingakanani isandla sam kubantu abaphikayo abamelene nam neendlela zam kwaye ngenkohliso yabo bazibhengeza bengamalungisa phambi koThixo benxibe iingubo zabo ezimdaka?

Guqukani! Lilani nenze isijwili ngenxa yezono zenu, kwaye nikhalaze kum ukuze ndinisindise. Iicawa zenu ezininzi zingenwe ziingcuka eziphangayo ezishumayela kuni amazwi aya kunisa esiphelweni senu, kwaye sekunjalo aniyi kuguquka kuzo niguqukele kum ngobuso nentliziyo evulekileyo, njengoko bendiya kunisindisa kwiingxaki zenu kwaye ndigcine abantwana benu bekhuselekile kwimikhosi yomubi, ehamba elubala ezitalatweni zenu kwaye sele iseba kakhulu kuni. Iinkanuko zabo aziyi kwaneliswa de zithathe yonke into kuni kwaye zinqunqe abaninzi. Kodwa abo babiza egameni lam baya kusindiswa ukuba banyamezela kwada kwaba sekupheleni.

Kutheni nizenza lukhuni iintliziyo zenu ngokuchasene neNkosi yemikhosi? Kutheni nisaxoka kwiintliziyo zenu, nisithi, "Akukho ntlekele; njengoko bekunjalo izolo, kuya kuba njalo nanamhlanje, kwaye ingomso liza nokufanayo?" Nina bagxeki niya kuvuna umvuzo weendlela zenu zokungakholwa, kwaye abo bandilahlileyo baya kuziswa kwintshabalalo epheleleyo nakwingxaki enkulu. Yivani amazwi am, owu sizukulwana esikrexezayo nesibi. Yivani amazwi am kwaye nithobe iindlebe zenu kuwo ukuze nive lonke unobumba endiya kuwuthetha. Ukuzamazama okukhulu kuya kusidabula isizwe senu enisixabise kakhulu, kwaye kukuzamazama okukhulu kuya kuza intshabalalo enkulu. Ndiya kuzenza kube senkangala izixeko zenu ezakha zaba zikhulu kwaye ndiya kuzisa namanzi ukuba agubungele iinkalo zenu zonxweme ezinesono; kuba aniyithandi iNkosi kodwa ningabathandi beziyolo ngaphezu kokuba ningabathandi bakaThixo.

Andinayolo ekugwetyweni kwenu, njengoko ubabalo lwam nenceba yam zande kuni ixesha elide ngoku. Abaninzi kwabo bawungcolisileyo umnqophiso kaNowa baya kugutyungelwa ngamanzi ngokwabo ngenxa yokungcolisa umnyama ongcwele wemibala emininzi uBawo awawunika njengesibambiso. Umlilo uya kunina ngesandla seentshaba zenu, kwaye abaninzi baya kutshabalala emalangatyeni nakwityhefu elandelayo. Ilizwe lenu liya kudatshulwa, kwaye intlekele enkulu iya kuba sisidlo senu sangokuhlwa, kuba niyilahlile iNkosi yeeNkosi noKumkani wooKumkani.

Omubi sele engenisile uMkhosi wakhe oBomvu ezixekweni zenu, ezidolophini, nasezilalini. Bahamba phakathi kwenu, beceba intshabalalo yenu; kwaye balindele imiyalelo yabo nokumka komthinteli wam, endiya kumkhulula kungekudala. Baya kutshayela batshabalalise kwaye boyise okuseleyo. Ayiphelelanga apho, kodwa baya kuyibanga njengeyabo, kwaye baya kuthimba abaninzi benu babathwalele ezinkampini zabo abasele bezilungisile. Abaninzi baya kunqunyulwa iintloko ngenxa yegama lam, kwaye ndiya kubavuza kakhulu xa behlala bethembekile kwada kwaba sekupheleni. Akuyi kubakho kukhanya; kuya kubakho iibhulorho eziwileyo neendlela ezigqithukileyo. Ngubani oya kuninika ukutya namanzi xa isizwe senu silele senkangala singasayi kuphinda sivuke? Kodwa ndisaya kusindisa intsalela, kwaye intsalela ndiya kuyisindisa, kuba nditshilo!

Musani ukoyika, bantu bam nabathembekileyo bam. Ndiya kunixhwila phakathi kwenu ngephanyazo leso ndinise endlwini kaBawo, apho ndinilungiselele indawo netafile ukuze sidle kunye, bantwana bam. Ndilangazelela ubukho benu, kwaye kungekudala niya kubona ubuso bam ngamehlo enu, kwaye niya kuba nomvuzo wokholo lwenu kum kuba ninokholo entliziyweni yenu xa ningekaboni nangamehlo enu ubuqaqawuli bam, nobuqaqawuli bukaBawo, omkhulu kunam. Kwaye ndinibizile ndanikhetha njengabam, kwaye anisoze nimke kum, njengoko niya kuhlala ninayo iNkosi yenu nomkhululi wenu. Yazini ukuba ndiya kunivuza kakhulu kwaye ndinithululele iintsikelelo eningenakuziqonda ngeli xesha. Hlalani nithembekile kwada kwaba sekupheleni, kuba okwexeshana elifutshane ezi zinto ziya kwenzeka; inene, ixesha lihambile, kwaye ubusuku sele buwile emhlabeni.

NdinguKumkani wooKumkani neNkosi yeeNkosi, kwaye ndinikwe igama elingaphezu kwawo onke amagama, ukuze ngegama likaYesu Krestu onke amazulu nomhlaba agobe idolo.

NdinguMthetheli woxolo, kwaye ngaphandle kwam akukho luxolo.

Ndingulowo unike ukuphumla, ukuze niphumle kum nifumane ikhusi elubabalweni lwam.

Ndingulowo uhamba phakathi kweziphatho zezibane ezisixhenxe, olawula iingelosi zikaBawo, kwaye zonke izinto ezulwini nasemhlabeni zisezandleni zam, njengoko uBawo wazinika mna kuloyiso lwam phezu komubi de iintshaba zam zenziwe isihlalo seenyawo zam.

NdinguNkosi yenu nomkhululi wenu, kwaye ndingumalusi olungileyo othweswe lonke uzuko, kwaye ndiphethe ubomi obungunaphakade ezandleni zam kwabo baya kuzithoba bacele.

Vakalisani ukuba ndiyiNkosi! Musani ukoyika, bantwana bam abancinane, njengoko ingamaxesha okugqibela kwaye isihlwele esikhulu siya kusindiswa kwimbandezelo ezayo. Nabani na obiza egameni likaYesu Krestu uya kusindiswa, kwaye ndiya kubaphilisa ndibavuze ngokuthembeka kwabo. Inene, ndihamba phakathi kwenu, kwaye yeyiphi into enkulu eyicwangcisileyo iNkosi angayixelelanga abaprofeti bayo phambi kokuba yenzeke? Ndinixelela ngokucacileyo, phulaphulani ilizwi lam, njengoko kusele ixesha elincinane kakhulu phambi kwesandi sexilongo lokugqibela. Kwanazo iingelosi zimi zilungele emazulwini phezulu, zilindele ukuvuthelwa kwexilongo! Intlawulelo yenu epheleleyo isemnyango wenu, ngoko musani ukoyika kwaye ninamathele kum, kuba ubomi benu bufihliwe kum kwaye sele buza kutyhilwa.
