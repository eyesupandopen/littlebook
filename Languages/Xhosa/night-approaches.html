Busondele Ubusuku

ILizwi leNkosi lehla phezu kwam.

Busondele ubusuku apho kungekho mntu unokusebenza. Izihlabo ziya kuphela, iilwimi ziya kuthi cwaka, izipho ezizeBandleni zonke ziya kuphela xa uMtshakazi wam exhwilwa. Kuze kubekho umhla wobumnyama apho kuya kubakho indlala elizweni, indlala yeLizwi lam iya kubakho. Kanti naxa kunjalo iingelosi zam nabaprofeti babanyulwa baya kuhlala bethetha endaweni yam. Kusondele ixesha apho amarhamncwa asendle aya kuhlasela aze atshabalalise abaninzi. Umhla weNkosi uxeliwe kwiLizwi lam eliNgcwele, yaye kungekudala kuya kuba njalo. Umgwebo uya kuqhambuka ngequbuliso; ngoko ke, guqukani ezonweni zenu nize kum, iNkosi uYesu Krestu, kuba ndizihlawulele izono zenu, yaye ninokuba mhlophe njengekhephu phambi kwam nilambethe ilinen ecikizekileyo ukuba ningazilahla nje iingubo zenu. Guqukelani kum ngenguquko. Zithobeni phambi kweNkosi kusekho ukukhanya kwemini, kuba kungekudala ubusuku buya kungena yaye akukho mntu unokusebenza. Kanti nakulawo maxesha, nabani na onqula igama leNkosi uya kusindiswa, ngokungathi uphuma emlilweni.
