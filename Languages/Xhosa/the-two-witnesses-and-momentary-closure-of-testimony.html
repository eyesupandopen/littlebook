Okukrakra Okumnandi
Janyuwari 13, 2025

Amangqina Amabini Nokuvalwa Okwexeshana Kobungqina

Kusuka kuYahweh, MNA NDINGUYE OMKHULU:

Yabonani! Ndiza kunixelela ngamangqina am amabini, iziphatho zam zezibane ezibini nemithi yam yeminquma emibini, ndize ndinike iimfama ukubona ngokutyhila iimfihlelo ebezigcinwe ziyimfihlelo ehlabathini kubulumko bam, ukuze nazi ngokuqinisekileyo izinto ezizayo nokuba indlela eya elusindisweni mayicace kubo bonke abafundayo nabanokholo lokuthobela la Mazwi eNdaba Ezilungileyo Ezingunaphakade.

Yabonani! NDINGU Yahweh, yena kanye owadala amazulu nomhlaba ngeenjongo zam ezingunaphakade nozuko lwam, kwaye NDINGUYE oza kubuyisela indalo ekonakaleni, ukuze iindlela zikaYahweh zilungise kungekudala zizalise umhlaba ngobulungisa ebusweni bukaYesu Krestu. Kwaye ngoko iindlela zam ziya kuwugubungela umhlaba kuBukumkani beMinyaka Eliwaka bukaKrestu Yesu, uBukumkani apho kungekho nto imbi iya kungena khona, uBukumkani apho umhlaba ulawulwa ngobulungisa, kwaye uxolo lokwenene nokubuyiselwa kuya kuzalisa umhlaba, kwaye abathembekileyo bazo zonke izizukulwana baya kuhamba apho ngoxolo olugqibeleleyo nokuzola kuba iindlela zikaYahweh ziya kuwugubungela umhlaba ngobulungisa. Lixesha apho uSathana abotshwe ekuthuthunjisweni nasekoyikeni okukhulu noloyiko lwelifa lakhe elizayo lesohlwayo esingunaphakade, apho aya kubona irhamncwa nomprofeti wobuxoki bephoswa emalangatyeni esohlwayo esingunaphakade betshiswa, kuba bonke bafanelekile, kodwa uya kuzalisekisa iinjongo zam ezingunaphakade zobulungisa phambi kokuba abotshwe aze aphoswe emlilweni ngonaphakade angaphinde aphume, kwaye ndiya kubeka itywina kwaye abasayi kuphinda bakhunjulwe.

Kwaye ndiya kunika (kwaye ndikonwabele) ukuphumla kosuku lweSabatha naboyisi bam abangamalungisa nabo bonke abavunyelweyo ukungena kuBukumkani beMinyaka Eliwaka bukaKrestu Yesu! (Kuba iminyaka eliwaka injengosuku emehlweni am nosuku lunjengeminyaka eliwaka kwaye iminyaka eliwaka yiminyaka eliwaka.) Kwanabo bonke abaya kube bebuthiwe baza bavuswa kuzo zonke izizukulwana, ezangoku nezadlulayo, kuKrestu Yesu; kwangaphambi kokuba uSathana aphoswe emalangatyeni, phambi kokuba ixesha lakhe elifutshane lokugqibela lifike. Ekupheleni kwalo loo nyoka indala iya kubotshwa ngonaphakade ize iphoswe emlilweni, ingaze iphume kwisohlwayo sayo esingapheliyo kumalangatye ashushu esulfure evuthayo. Kuba uSathana nabo bonke abalandela ubuxoki bakhe abanamandla ngokwenene emehlweni am anaphakade! Ngubani onokumoyisa uYahweh, MNA NDINGUYE OMKHULU, nangona bexoka beqhayisa? Okanye ngubani onokuguqula intando yam engunaphakade nezibhengezo zemigwebo ezimiselwe ukuwa ngokupheleleyo xa umthinteli wam esusiwe ngokukhawuleza ngoku kwisandi sokuvuthelwa kwexilongo elinikwe ukuba likhale ngexesha elichanekileyo neligqibeleleyo endilikhethileyo kwicebo lam elingunaphakade ukusukela kumaphakade adlulileyo. Kwanomzuzu osekufuphi ukwenzeka apho isithembiso sobulungisa sidibana nokuzaliseka okungunaphakade kumaxesha angoku.

Kuba NDINGUYE, MNA NDINGUYE OMKHULU, yena kanye owazi isiphelo kwasekuqaleni nesiqalo kwasekupheleni. Ngoko ke, ndiza kutyhila iinkalo zecebo lam elingunaphakade losindiso eliye lahlala liyimfihlelo, izinto ezisafihliweyo kwiMfihlelo kaThixo ekufuphi ukuba igqitywe ngobulungisa obungunaphakade. Kwanjengoko kwakubhalwe nguYohane uMtyhili njengoko wayebhala ngokuthembekileyo aMazwi awawanikwayo nguKrestu Yesu! Kwaye kubhaliwe ukuba ngemihla yelizwi leNgelosi yesixhenxe, xa iya kuqala ukuvuthela ixilongo, kwanemfihlelo kaThixo iya kugqitywa, njengoko eyixelele abakhonzi bakhe abaProfeti.

Noko ke, baninzi abaye baphulukana nenyaniso yala Mazwi ngabakhafuli abaxokayo ababhatalwa likhaka elibomvu ukuze bagqwethe izibhalo zam ezingcwele ngobuxoki be-dispensational nebeZionist kunye neemfundiso zobuxoki zokuthatha indawo, ubuxoki obunikezelwe ngobuqhetseba ngabawileyo ngabantu abakhohlakeleyo ukuze bakhohlise iintliziyo zabathobekileyo nabaphantsi. Kwaye baya kukhohlisa abanyulwa bam kanye ukuba abanyulwa bam bakhetha ukuwatyeshela aMazwi am obulungisa ngekratshi becinga ukuba abasoze bakhohliswe ngenxa yokunyulwa kwabo. Noko ke bonke abaphosisa ngolo hlobo ngokwala inyaniso bekhetha ubuxoki babo baya kukhohliswa. Ngoko ke, qondani bantu bam ukuba ixilongo lesixhenxe asiloxilongo linye elinikwe ukuba likhale ukuphelisa amaxesha am obabalo nokuqokelela abathembekileyo kuKrestu, ngoko musani ukukhohliswa!

Noko ke, inyaniso kaYahweh imi ngonaphakade kwaye ayinakuguqulwa, kwaye iLizwi lam alinakuphulwa, kwaye iLizwi lam liya kufezekisa oko ndikujongileyo kuye nabani na iLizwi lam elithunyelwe kuye. Akukho namnye onokumisa amacebo am anaphakade! Akukho namnye onokumisa uKrestu Yesu, apho icebo lam elingunaphakade losindiso lixhomekeke ngokupheleleyo. Kwanaye endimnike amandla ngaphezu kwazo zonke izidalwa kwindalo yam ndamnika ulawulo olupheleleyo phezu kwendalo yam de iintshaba zakhe zenziwe isihlalo seenyawo zakhe, ngelo xesha uya kunikezela uBukumkani obuthotyiweyo kuAdonai Yahweh.

Noko ke, ngenyaniso engunaphakade, alukho usuku lokuphela kolawulo olulungileyo lukaYeshua HaMashiach, oya kulawula ngobulungisa ngonaphakade kanaphakade, kunye nomtshakazi wakhe oya kuqhubeka naye ngonaphakade kanaphakade kwizikhundla zabo zegunya nemiyalelo yenkonzo. Kwaye oko kungenxa yokuba bamamkela uYesu waseNazarete njengeNkosi yabo engunaphakade ngamaxesha obabalo nenceba, kwaye bamthanda ngokupheleleyo ngokuthobela aMazwi akhe, kwanangawo la Mazwi abaya kube bezoyisile ngokupheleleyo zonke izinto zobukumkani beentshaba ngonaphakade.

Kuba ngenyaniso engunaphakade, ingubo kaMelkitsedeke ihleli ngokuqinileyo emagxeni kaYeshua HaMashiach anaphakade, ayisayi kuze isuke! Kuba ngonaphakade kanaphakade uKumkani woBulungisa uya kulawula ngonaphakade kunye nomyalelo wakhe wabaBingeleli, ooKumkani, nabaProfeti abaya kuzalisa umhlaba ngeendlela ezilungileyo nolwazi lukaYahweh, kude kube ngamaxesha apho iziprofeto ziya kuphela ngonaphakade kuba ndiya kwenza ikhaya lam nabantu ezulwini elitsha nasemhlabeni omtsha oza kudalwa kungekudala ngobulungisa nobungcwele bokwenene! Kwanendalo apho ulwazi lokulungileyo nokubi luya kuphela ngonaphakade kwaye kuya kufunyanwa kuphela ubulungisa nothando noxolo ngonaphakade kungaze kusuke okanye kuncitshiswe okanye konakaliswe nakancinane. Ngokwenene kuba ukonakala kuya kuphoswa emalangatyeni ngonaphakade.

Noko ke, kunjalo ukuba abawileyo baye bagubungela baza basasaza ubuxoki babo phezu kwendalo yam, bade babusasaza ngokungapheliyo kwiitekhnoloji zabo zesihogo, ukuze umntu wemvelo alawulwe aze alawulwe ngamaza abo kunye neetekhnoloji zesihogo. Kwanemifanekiso elawula ingqondo neguqula indlela yokuziphatha, iimbali ezimbi, namakhubalo asasazwa kwizibuko zabo ezimnyama apho abaninzi bahleli ebumnyameni bekhwelwe yimifanekiso nobuxoki babo. Kwaye ngenene, abawileyo baye bathakatha izihlwele ngeetyhefu ne-pharmakeia edluliswa njengeyeza, kangangokuba batyhefiswa baze babulawa ngayo. Noko ke, iityhefu zabo neetekhnoloji nezibuko zabo ziya kutshatyalaliswa ngonaphakade, kwaye usasazo lwabo luya kuphela ngonaphakade, abaphosa ngalo amakhubalo abo baze bavuse iinkanuko neminqweno yomntu wemvelo esonweni esikhulu kwaye iitekhnoloji zabo ezingenaxabiso ziya kuphela, zingaphinde zibekho.

Noko ke, kunjalo, ukuba ndabavumela abawileyo ukuba bagubungele indalo yam ngobuxoki babo, ukuze bonke abaya kulandela iindlela zabo ezicekisekayo bakhethe ukuhlala kwilifa labo elingunaphakade lesulfure evuthayo kuba babeyoliswa kukungabikho kobulungisa ngokona kuYahweh, MNA NDINGUYE OMKHULU, nakwabamsulwa kwindalo yam, ababaphathise kakubi. Kwaye kubulumko bam obungunaphakade nothando lwam ngendalo yam, ndiye ndavumela ngamnye ukusuka kuAdam ukuba akhethe ukuba uya kukhonza bani na: nokuba sisono esisa ekufeni, okanye ukuthobela ubulungisa obusa kubomi obungunaphakade.

Zinjalo iindlela zikaYahweh, kuba iindlela zam ziindlela zobulungisa, uxolo, nothando! Kwanothando olungunaphakade nolungapheliyo, apho yonke indalo yam, eyenziwe ngumkhosi wezidalwa ezahlukeneyo nezenziwe ngokumangalisayo zazo zonke iintlobo neenjongo, iya kuphila ngemvisiswano yobulungisa nothando. Kuba indalo yam ijongwe njengelaphu elinxibeleleneyo lobomi elilawulwa ngobulungisa nothando ukuze kuxhamle ngokupheleleyo kwaye kuzukiswe zonke izidalwa zam, apho kungekho namnye umntu oya kuze axhamle ngeendleko nelahleko yomnye, kodwa bonke baya kwabelana ngokukhululekileyo ngobuninzi obungapheliyo obukwiindawo zam zokugcina ezilungiselelwe ukunceda nokuzukisa abo bathobela imiyalelo yam elungileyo ngeentliziyo zabo ngothando kuYise wabo ongunaphakade, endithi ngokwenene, NDINGUYE!

Noko ke, kunjalo, ukuba NDINGU MNA NDINGUYE OMKHULU kwaye andibuxhasi okanye andivumelani nobuxoki babo, kwaye andiyi kuvumela ubuxoki babo buqhubeke. Kunjalo, ndiyabhengeza, ukuba mna, Yahweh, MNA NDINGUYE OMKHULU onguThixo ongunaphakade woBulungisa ndiya kutyhila ngokupheleleyo yonke inkalo yobukumkani beentshaba obumiselwe ukuvuka ngexesha elifutshane kakhulu inamba elinikwe ulawulo, kwaye iya kuba nolawulo olulinganiselweyo kakhulu. Kwaye ngokunjalo, ndiya kubatyhila abawileyo abazifihle phakathi kwendalo yam, abaza kuncothulwa baze batyhilwe kwihlazo labo elingunaphakade ngokupheleleyo phambi kokuba baphoswe emalangatyeni esohlwayo ngonaphakade, apho impethu yabo ingafi. Kwaye isibhengezo sam siya kuphuma, kuba oko kubhengeziweyo ebuqaqawulini nasemandleni etrone kaYahweh, kwanaye MNA NDINGUYE OMKHULU, akunakuze kuguqulwe okanye kutshintshwe, kwaye kuya kwenzeka kanye njengoko kubhengeziwe. Selah!

Noko ke, kunjalo, ukuba andizange ndibafihle ngokupheleleyo abawileyo, kwaye sele ndiqalisile ukubatyhila ngokupheleleyo kwaye isigqubuthelo siyancipha. Kwanaso isigqubuthelo sokufihla endibafihle ngaso emehlweni oluntu, kwaye umthinteli uya kususwa owenziwe ngeendawo ezahlukeneyo zokuthintela eziya kususwa ngokupheleleyo xa uMikayeli enikwa umyalelo xa ixilongo lokugqibela likhala ngokukhawuleza ngoku. Kwaye uya kuwa umkhenkce, nomhlaba uya kuguqulwa, kwaye baya kuba nemfazwe yabo enkulu kwaye abaninzi baya kutshabalala ngayo, njengoko kubhaliwe kwaxelwa kwangaphambili nguYohane uMtyhili kubungqina bakhe obungunaphakade. Kwaye ndiyaninika ukuba niqonde ukuba ngexesha lokuhlanganisana kwabathembekileyo kuKrestu ndiya kusisusa eso sigqubuthelo sokufihla okuyinxalenye ngokukhawuleza nangamandla njengoko ndakrazula isigqubuthelo setempile kubini ukusuka phezulu kuye ezantsi nto leyo eyaphawula ukuphela kwenkonzo kaLevi nokubuyiselwa ngokupheleleyo kukaMelkitsedeke. Ukuze ndisuse ubumfama obuyinxalenye kubantwana bakwaSirayeli ngonaphakade, ndingaphinde ndibuyele kumavukuthu am.

Kwaye oku kuya kwenzeka ngokukhawuleza nangokungalindelekanga kuba ihlabathi alilindelanga ukuba uKrestu Yesu akhanyise imimandla yomhlaba nezibhakabhaka ngokupheleleyo ngozuko lwakhe olungunaphakade kula maxesha eentlungu zokubeleka eziphakamileyo, kwanjalo, kuba ihlabathi ngokwenene alinankolo. Kwaye kwabo bangavunyelwanga ukungena emazulwini xa olo cango lukhulu luvalwa, abaninzi abangaboni abakhohlakeleyo baya kutshabalala baze bathathwe emgwebeni ngonaphakade, kwanabo bamalileyo uYesu waseNazarete ngokupheleleyo ngonyeliso olukhulu nekratshi kwisaphetha sam esingcwele, nabanye abaninzi abanesono esifanayo ekwaleni kwabo okunentiyo abaya kuwa ngonaphakade kumgwebo ongqongqo ngenxa yobubi ababenzileyo. Kwabona bubi bunye abaqhubeka bezenza ngokuzinikela okukhulu, kwaye abayi kusinda.

Qondani ukuba bonke abafuna ukwaphula imida yendalo nemiyalelo yendalo yam ngokweqa loo mida nemiyalelo, bade bafune ukuziguqula babe ngamasikizi (ngeendlela zabawileyo) nabo baya kuwa ngokunjalo kumgwebo ongunaphakade: Kuba esi sisono esingenakuxolelwa, kwanonyeliso oluchasene noYahweh onguMoya oyiNgcwele, befuna ukuzitshintsha kumfanekiso kaYahweh abadalwa ngawo ekuqaleni. Ngoko ke, zilahleni iintsomi zabo zamaJesuit eziye zaguqula ubuthixo bokwenene baba ziintsomi ezaziswa nguNimrodi ngabathathu bakhe bokuqala abangangcwele! Kuba abathathu abangcwele boYise, uNyana, nomoya oyingcwele baye bonakaliswa kwintsingiselo nenjongo yabo yantlandlolo, kodwa inyaniso kaYahweh ayinakufihlwa zizinto ezibuthathaka nezingento nangona bexoka! Noko ke, uYesu waseNazarete nguYahweh obonakaliswe enyameni, kwaye umoya wam ongcwele wobunyana uyabazalisa ngokupheleleyo abathembekileyo kuKrestu abayinxalenye yokwenene yobuthixo kulawulo lwabo noKrestu Yesu phantsi koMyalelo kaMelkitsedeke njengabaBingeleli nooKumkani ngonaphakade! Ngoko ke, qondani ukuba ningoobani kuKrestu bantwana bam kuba abawileyo bafuna ukuyifihla inyaniso ngani ngeentsomi zabo zobuxoki.

Noko ke, abathembekileyo kuKrestu baye baphila baza bafa kwaye basahamba ebusweni bomhlaba ngokholo olukhulu, kwaye lo mzuzu umangalisayo nozuko awuyi kubafumana bengazi kuba baya kukuqonda ukuthethwa kweso sidumo sikhulu, kwaye baya kuliva ilizwi lexilongo lokugqibela esele linikiwe esandleni kunye nemiyalelo epheleleyo yokuba likhale xa umyalelo unikwa nguKrestu Yesu, osoloko eyenza ngokugqibeleleyo intando kaAdonai Yahweh. Abathembekileyo kuKrestu baya kuvunyelwa ukungena kolo cango lukhulu lusiya emazulwini ekungekho mntu emhlabeni unokuluvala okanye aluvule, nokuba balele okanye baphila ebusweni bomhlaba ngexesha lokubizwa kwabo!

Kwaye inkxwaleko enkulu iya kubafumana abangathembekanga abamvumayo uYesu waseNazarete ngentliziyo engenankolo, bade bala ukuphila ubomi benguquko kuba babezithanda izono zabo. Kuya kwenzeka ukuba abo bakhetha ukuqhubeka esonweni, abathi ngeentliziyo ezingathobeliyo neenkani bamvuma uYesu njengeNkosi kodwa baqhubeka kwimisebenzi yenyama baya kushiyelwa kumlilo womsuli wokutshatyalaliswa kwenyama, ukuze basinde kwiziyolo zesono nokuba basinde kwinyama yabo ebakhuthuzelele kwiinkanuko zayo. Kwaye abaninzi baya kufumana usindiso ngokubiza uYesu waseNazarete de baphefumle okokugqibela, kuba bekusoloko kuyimfuneko kubathembekileyo, ukuba umntu afunyanwe ethembekile de aphefumle okokugqibela, ukuba akayi kuba ngomnye wabambalwa abathathwa bephila besiya kukhuseleko lwamazulu ngamaxesha abo okuthembeka. Selah!

Kwaye imfazwe yabo iya kuqhuma iqhubeke ngexesha layo elimisiweyo kwaye eluthuthwini lwentshabalalo yabo baya kuvuka. Inamba, irhamncwa eliphuma elwandle nerhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni elikwangumprofeti wabo wobuxoki liya kuvuka ngonyeliso olukhulu ukuze libe nolawulo lwalo olukhohlakeleyo kodwa olufutshane ngenceba. Noko ke, uYahweh, MNA NDINGUYE OMKHULU, akazange azishiye engenabungqina, njengoko kungqinwa ngumkhosi wamangqina kwizibhalo zam ezingunaphakade nezingcwele ezigcinwe ngokugqibeleleyo emazulwini. Kwaye njengoko kunjalo, ubungqina bam obuninzi bulahlekile ngenxa yexesha nokungakhathalelwa ngabo babenikwe uxanduva lokubakhathalela. Qondani iminyaka bantwana bam kwaye nilahle ubuxoki babo be-dispensational bobuqhetseba. Qondani ukuba indalo ayinazigidi zeminyaka ubudala ngokobuxoki babawileyo. Indalo iphantsi kweminyaka engamawaka amathandathu ubudala kwaye ndiwahlule la maxesha abe ngamaphakade. Kwakukho ixesha lesiphithiphithi esilinganiselweyo ngenxa yabawileyo abangcolisa ihlabathi elalikhona ngamasikizi abo, kwanamasikizi lawo atshatyalaliswa ngokupheleleyo emgwebeni owaphela ngonogumbe omkhulu ngemihla kaNowa.

Noko ke, uNowa waqhubeka ephila emva kukazamcolo, wasinda ephilile eMkhombeni kunye nenzala yakhe (nezilwanyana nendalo) ezanikwa indawo yokuqhubeka kuba uNowa wayeyindoda elilungisa nomlawuli olilungisa ngokommiselo kaMelkitsedeke, owathi isambatho sahlala emagxeni akhe de umkhonzi wam waya kuphumla ngoxolo de avuswe kungekudala ngoku ebulungiseni. Ekuhambeni kwexesha uMoses wakhutshwa ephila emanzini oMnayile, waza waphakama ngamandla amakhulu waza wangenisa ixesha lam leTorah, naxa uSirayeli wenziwa isizwe esikhulu nesinamandla esisisithunzi sezinto ezizayo.

Kwaye ngexesha elifanelekileyo nelichanekileyo elimisiweyo uYesu waseNazarete wazalwa yintombi enyulu waza waphakama waba yimvana kaThixo, wada wazincama waba yintlawulelo engunaphakade yesohlwayo sikaMoses, isidima endasamkelayo ngonaphakade ukuze kuxolelwe izono ngonaphakade, kubo bonke abangcwaliswe ngaso, nakwiTestamente yakhe eNtsha yobulungisa awayiqinisayo wayitywina ngonaphakade uKristu Yesu ngegazi lakhe elingenasono, ngoko ke, akusekho mfuneko yombingelelo wesilwanyana. Inene, uYesu waseNazarete ngumongo wobulungisa kuwo onke amaxesha, isithuba sexesha esaphela ngokoyiswa kwabawi ngonaphakade, isithuba sexesha esabhalwa ngamazwi anaphakade athi “KUGQITYIWE!”

Laza ke ixesha lam lobabalo laqalisa ngaloo mini yePentekoste xa ndamnika uKristu Yesu umoya wam ongcwele woBunyana awawuphalazayo ngokomyalelo wam wokuhlawulela bonke abakhetha ukuba ngoonyana bakaThixo abanikwe ngumoya woBunyana ngala maxesha. Kwaza ke kwabakho ukuzalwa kweBandla likaKristu Yesu elanikelwa ngenxa yobabalo nenceba kwiintlanga, nangaphambi kokuba kufike umgwebo ongqongqo nowokugqibela. Kwanamaxesha enceba nobabalo apho izizwe ezilahlekileyo zendlu kaSirayeli zazinokuhlanganiselwa kuSirayeli kaThixo, kunye naye nabani na ovela ezintlangeni owayebiziwe waza wasabela ngokwenza uYesu waseNazarete iNkosi yakhe engunaphakade. Nabantu bam ababesasazwe ngesohlwayo nababhadula emhlabeni njengabahambi kumazwe angaziwayo, nabo bonke abavela ku-Adam abamkela usindiso ngala maxesha baya kuba yinxalenye kaSirayeli kaThixo ngonaphakade, bangaze bemke kwimiyalelo yabo engunaphakade yokukhonza uKristu Yesu iNkosi yabo enyukileyo!

Ngoko ke qonda ukuba ihlabathi elikhoyo ngoku lifikelele esiphelweni salo sanaphakade kwaye eli hlabathi likhoyo lingendawo alisoze liphinde libekho ukuze likhathaze abo ndibathandayo ngonaphakade. Ngoko ke ixesha lam lobabalo liyaqhubeka de kube ngumzuzwana wokugqibela apho kuvakala khona ixilongo lokugqibela lexesha lobabalo esele linikiwe ukuba livuthelwe. Ekupheleni kwenkathazo kaYakobi, ekwayiveki yamashumi asixhenxe kaDaniyeli, iveki yeminyaka apho usuku ngalunye lungunyaka kwiveki yeentsuku ezisixhenxe, buya kumiselwa uBukumkani beMinyaka eliwaka bukaYeshua HaMashiach ukuze kube lusuku lokuphumla, ekupheleni kwalo kuya kuba yiminyaka esixhenxe samawaka. Ngoko ke, qonda ukuba kuya kube kukho amaxesha amathathu eminyaka engamawaka amabini lilinye, kunye nosuku lwesabatha lweminyaka eliwaka oluya kwenzeka ngesiphelo esingcwele nesilungileyo apho indalo yam ihlawulelwa ngokupheleleyo esonweni, ingaphindi yone.

Ngoko ke waqonde amaxesha ophila kuwo, kuba iimfihlelo neendlela zikaYehova ziye zalahleka ngenxenye ngenxa yezono zabantu kwabo bahleli emhlabeni, zifihlwe nangeenkohliso zabawi.
