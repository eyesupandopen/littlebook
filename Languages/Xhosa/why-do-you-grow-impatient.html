Kutheni Niphelelwa Ngumonde Nje?

Yizani, thobani iintliziyo neendlebe zenu nive aMazwi eNkosi yenu, bandla lam nkqu noMtshakazi wam. Kutheni nisuswe ngokukhawuleza nje ethembeni lenu lokubonakala kwam, ngokungathi andizi? Ngokungathi izibhengezo zikaYahweh zingatshintshwa? Ingaba uThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade, uYahweh, uwalityhile na amadinga akhe? Ingaba ndiwalityhile na amazwi namadinga am? Inene, inene, ndithi hayi! Kwaye bonke abathi uYahweh, uThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade, kunye nam, uYeshua HaMashiach, iNkosi enkulu yeeNkosi noKumkani wooKumkani, unobuyatha, bamele baguquke kwangoko. Celani uxolelo bantwana bam kwaye niqhubeke nizilungiselela kuba ndiza kubonakala ngequbuliso, kwaye niya kumangaliswa yindlela esiya kwenzeka ngokukhawuleza ngayo isiganeko esikhulu esilandelayo kwicebo likaYahweh lokuhlangula uluntu, kuba siya kwenzeka ngequbuliso nangokungalindelekanga kuba kutshiwo njalo. Kuba kuya kwenzeka ukuba ndivuse abafileyo kuKrestu kuqala kwaye nina baphilayo nabasalayo niya kuthathwa niye kundihlangabeza emafini, kwaye kuya kwenzeka ngokuqhwanyaza kweliso.

Kutheni niphelelwa ngumonde nje? Kutheni abanye benu benza ngathi izinto zibuyele kwindlela ezaziyiyo? Kucacile ukuba akukho kubuyela kwiindlela zakudala, kuba ihlabathi elikhoyo ngoku liya kuhlangabezana nentlekele enkulu kwaye liya kutshintshwa ngonaphakade, kwaye liya kutshintsha ngakumbi ngexesha lenkathazo kaYakobi kwaye umhlaba uya kuba njengasemihleni kaNowa. Xa umthinteli esusiwe akuyi kubakho nto yokugcina imikhosi yobubi kude, nkqu nemikhosi yabo bawayo abaya kuhlasela konke okwam, kuba uYahweh unike inamba amandla okulwa nabangcwele nokuboyisa. Abaninzi baya kuza kum ngokutshatyalaliswa kwenyama yabo, kwa le nyama bayithandayo nesabakhonkxileyo. Kuba bonke abanqula igama leNkosi baya kusindiswa, kwaye umkhonzi akamkhulu kunenkosi yakhe.

Nangona ndinikezele ngokusinda kwintshabalalo ezayo kwabathembekileyo, abaninzi abakulungelanga kodwa baya kusindiswa kwaye basebenze injongo enkulu kuBukumkani bam obuzayo, kuba ndiya kulawula kwitrone kaDavide kwaye ndabelana ngetrone kaBawo, kwaye nani niya kwabelana ngetrone yam nina bathembekileyo bam. Kwabo bangakulungelanga baya kubandezeleka kakhulu ngenxa yegama lam, kwaye baya kubandezeleka njengam, uYesu waseNazarete, ndabandezelekayo ukuze ndikhusele usindiso loluntu kwaye ngokubethelelwa kwam emnqamlezweni ndishiye umzekelo wokuthobela kude kube sekufeni ukuze bonke bawulandele. Kwaye ngeembandezelo ezinkulu iindimbane ezininzi ziya kusindiswa zize zifumane umvuzo omkhulu kum Krestu Yesu, zide zikhonze noYahweh etempileni yakhe. Umvuzo omkhulu ulindele bonke abanqula igama lam, kwaye abo banqula igama lam kude kube sekupheleni baya kusindiswa, nkqu nabo bangabathandiyo ubomi babo kude kube sekupheleni. Kuba eli hlabathi liyatshabalala kwaye liya kutshatyalaliswa kwaye lohlwaywe ngokupheleleyo phambi kokuba uBukumkani bam bobulungisa bumiselwe emhlabeni.

Ngoko ke musani ukutyhafa Bantwana bam, kuba amadinga am ngu-ewe no-amen kwaye ndiya kumxhwila umtshakazi wam ndimse emazulwini njengoko kuthenjisiwe, kwaye akukho namnye umntu omiselwe usindiso olungunaphakade nguYahweh phambi kokuba kusekwe iziseko zomhlaba, oya kuxhwilwa ezandleni zam ezingunaphakade kwaye usindiso lwenu lukhuselekile kum, Krestu Yesu, hlalani nithembekile kuphela kwaye nihlale kuKrestu kude kube sekupheleni. Oku bekusoloko kuyimfuneko evela kuYahweh, ukuba umntu afunyanwe ethembekile ezindleleni zakhe. Kwaye mna, Yeshua HaMashiach, ndizisa iindlela zikaYahweh kwaye abo bafunda ngam bafunda iindlela zikaYahweh, kuba ndimnye ngobunye nangenjongo noBawo. Kungekudala niya kuba banye nathi, akubhalwanga na ukuba nenziwe inxalenye yobuThixo obungunaphakade, kuba linjalo ilifa lenu kwaye unjalo umvuzo wam koonyana bakaThixo abathembekileyo abafunyenwe bethembekile kum. Kuba kum, Krestu Yesu, kuhlala konke ukuzala kobuThixo ngokwasemzimbeni, kwaye niya kwabelana kwilifa lam ngenxa yokuthembeka kwenu, kwaye niya kwabelana kwitrone yam njengooKumkani nababingeleli kwaye uBukumkani bam abunaziphelo.

Ngoko yimani niqine ngenxa yam kule mizuzwana yokugqibela yeli phakade, kwaye niqonde ukuba ixesha liphelile. Ewe, ixesha liphelile lokuba iBhabheli eyimfihlakalo iguquke. Amaxesha am obabalo nenceba aphelile, nangona inceba nobabalo zihleli nam ngonaphakade. Ngoku kuza umgwebo omkhulu emhlabeni, nakwizizwe nkqu nasemhlabeni. Kodwa umgwebo awuyi kuwa uze utshayele bonke abaphilayo, kodwa uya kuwa uqokelelane. Umgwebo phezu komgwebo, itywina emva kwetywina, ixilongo emva kwexilongo, isitya emva kwesitya, usizi emva kosizi, nesibetho emva kwesibetho. Kwaye ngenxa yoko abantu abaninzi, abaninzi, abaninzi kakhulu baya kufumana usindiso kum, Yesu waseNazarete, kuba ndiyiMvana yePasika egqibeleleyo yalo lonke ixesha, kwaye akusekho mfuneko yombingelelo. Kodwa kuni, Mtshakazi wam othembekileyo, amaxesha okulinda aphelile.

Akusayi kubakho kulibaziseka, kwaye akusayi kubakho kulinda, kwaye niya kufikelela kumvuzo wenu opheleleyo nakwilifa lenu kum. Kwaye nangona abanye beya kulahlekelwa, iliso alikabonanga, nendlebe ayikeva, kungangenanga nasentliziyweni yomntu, izinto uYahweh azilungiselele abo bamthandayo. Kwaye kunjalo zithandwa zam ezithembekileyo, nilubonisile uthando lwenu kum, Krestu Yesu, nothando lwenu ngoYahweh kuba niyithobele imiyalelo yam ngokholo kolu suku lungendawo. Nangona ningandibonanga ngamehlo enu, ningalubonanga nozuko lukaYahweh olungunaphakade ngamehlo enu nihleli nithembekile kum kude kube sekupheleni, ngoko ke kukho umvuzo omkhulu enigcinelwe wona emazulwini, ngoko musani ukutyhafa!

Qhubekani nomonde, Bantwana bam, kuba ndiyasondela. Musani ukuphulaphula abagxeki abanihlekayo kuba ukugxeka kwabo kuza kujika kungekudala kube lusizi olukhulu, kwaye abaninzi abagxekayo baya kuthi cwaka njengoko ubomi babo buphela. Kuba zinjalo izigwebo ezizayo. Kwabo bazalileyo iingalo zam ezoluliweyo balibala igazi lam lentlawulelo njengento engcolileyo bazibala njengabangafanelanga ubomi obungunaphakade kuBukumkani bam obuzayo, kwabona baya kudla ilifa lobumnyama nokufa ababekuthanda kakhulu babenqwenela kwaye abaninzi baya kuwa ngokuzithandela ekufeni kwesibini.

Kuba eluthandweni olukhulu lukaBawo, nkqu uYahweh uThixo ongunaphakade kuba akukho namnye onjengaye, akayi kunyanzela namnye kwizidalwa zakhe ukuba amlandele aze amthande. Bonke banikwe inkululeko yokuzikhethela kwaye bonke baya kufumana umvuzo wokhetho lwabo. Kuba injalo imithetho kaYahweh. Abo bathanda ububi, abathanda iziyolo zesono kwaye bathanda ububi obubangelwa zizono zabo kwabanye ababandezeleka ngokungekho sikweni ngenxa yeenkanuko zabo neendlela zabo ezigwenxa baya kudla ilifa leendlela zabo ezimbi zibuyele ezifubeni zabo kwaye sinjalo isohlwayo sabo siya kuba kwaye sonke isono siya kuhlawulelwa ngonaphakade. Kodwa kwabo bamkela isivumelwano soxolo sikaYahweh, mna Yesu waseNazarete, baya kudla ilifa lobomi obungunaphakade noxolo nomvuzo nothando novuyo olungunaphakade ebukweni bam nasebukweni bukaYahweh ngokwakhe, kuba kungekudala indawo yokuhlala kaYahweh iya kuba phakathi kwabantu kuba kubhaliwe njalo. Kwaye ndiyasondela ukuze ndinixhwile ndinise kwigumbi letrone likaYahweh ngokwakhe, apho sele nihleli khona, kwaye izithsaba zenu ziyanilindela.

Musani ukukholelwa kubuxoki babo bantwana bam, ndiyeza kuni, hlalani niqinile omnye umzuzwana okanye emibini, kuba ukubonakala kwam kusondele kwaye kuya kwenzeka nangawuphi na umzuzwana ngoku, kwaye ndiya kugqitha kulindelo lwenu. Zilungiseleleni kwaye nizihlambulule egazini lam lentlawulelo. Qhubekani nobomi benu benguquko kwaye niqhubeke nokubethelela iindlela zenu zenyama, kuba aziveli kum kwaye akukho nto imbi iya kungena eBukumkanini bam kwaye akukho nto imbi iya kuma ebukweni bukaYahweh. Ndim umbingelelo wenu ongunaphakade kwaye ndinenze naba ngabangenabala okanye umbimbi kwaye akukho bala kwabathembekileyo bam kuba igazi lam liyanihlambulula ngokuqhubekayo njengoko nivuma izono zenu enguqukweni, nangona inyama yenu yonakele ngokupheleleyo.

Yizani kum ngentliziyo evulekileyo kwaye ndiya kunibonisa ngothando iintsilelo zenu ukuze nivume izono zenu kum, kwaye ngokwenjenjalo ndiya kunihlambulula kwaye ndiya kuqhubeka ndiniphilisa ngokokuba nisisidalwa esitsha kum, Krestu Yesu. Kwaye inene, inene bonke abo batywinwe ngomoya wam ongcwele wesithembiso, nokuba ngumYuda okanye weNtlanga, (nokuba likhoboka okanye ukhululekile) baya kudalwa ngokutsha ngokupheleleyo njengomntu omnye omtsha kum Krestu Yesu kwaye baya kuma enkululekweni ezukisiweyo ngonaphakade! Kuba ndingumoya opha ubomi, kuba wenjenjalo uBawo ukundenza. Kwaye ndiyile nto uMkhulu onguMNA andenze yona ngonaphakade. Kwaye ngoku nani niya kwenziwa nifane nam kumzimba ozukisiweyo, umzimba owenziwe njengomvuzo oya kubonakalisa impumelelo yenu ekumeleni kwam ngolu suku lungendawo. Akukho namnye umzamo wenu oya kushiywa ungavuzwanga: Kuba uThixo akanantswela-bulungisa, ukuba angawulibala umsebenzi wenu, nombulaleko wothando, enalubonisayo ngeGama lakhe, ekubeni nakhonzayo kwabaNgcwele, nisakhonza.

Ubuni benu bokwenyani bufihliwe kum, nkqu noMsindisi wenu noMesiya. Kuya kuba ngonaphakade ninam, kwaye niya kuba nengqondo enye nentliziyo enye nam Yeshua HaMashiach nkqu noYahweh, kuba u-El Shaddai Omkhulu, nkqu uThixo uSomandla uya kuba yinto yonke kuyo yonke into kuba iinjongo zakhe ziya kuzalisekiswa kuni, nina bam babiziweyo, abanyuliweyo nabathembekileyo. Ndiyeza kuni, Mtshakazi wam, nikulungele na? Ninayo na i-oyile ezibhaneni zenu? Nizihlambe ngokupheleleyo na egazini lam lentlawulelo? Niyekile na ukuhlambana iinyawo?

Phaphani! Khangelani! Phulaphulani! Phaphani nilindele ukubonakala kwam kwaye nikhuthazane kuba uSathana ebenokukufihla ukubonakala kwam ukuba loo nto ibinokwenzeka. Khangelani kum, umbhali nomgqibelelisi wokholo lwenu, kuba ngokuqhwanyaza kweliso niya kutshintshwa nize nifaniswe nam ngonaphakade. Phulaphulani aMazwi am kwaye nilungele ukubonakala kwam kwaye ngaphezu kwako konke thobelani ngenxa yoThando ngoYahweh nangam ukuze nigcine iLizwi lam elingcwele ngentliziyo enyulu, nisazi ukuba inkululeko yenu ayisasondeli kodwa ilapha.

Ndimnye ngobunye nangenjongo noBawo, kwaye kungekudala niya kuba banye nathi.
