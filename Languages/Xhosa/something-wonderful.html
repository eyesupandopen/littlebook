Emisebenzini kaYohane uMbhaptizi
Matshi 15, 2024

Into Emangalisayo

ILizwi leNkosi liphezu kwam, khangelani ilizwi leNkosi!

Khangelani! Ndenza into emangalisayo phambi kwenu, yaye uninzi lwenu luyala ukubona!

Khangelani! Ndenza umsebenzi omkhulu phakathi kwenu oza kugqitywa kungekudala!

Khangelani! Ndenza umsebenzi omkhulu kuni, enisindiswa niphiliswe ngawo!

Ndenze ngaphezu kokulungisa iinxalenye ezimbalwa ezaphukileyo. Ndenze ngaphezu kokunidibanisa kwakhona, apho imithungo namanxeba akhoyo. Ndibenze bagqibelela kum abanyulwa nabathembekileyo bam, mna Krestu ophilayo, yaye ndinidale ngokutsha. Yazini ukuba ubomi benu bufihliwe kum, yaye xa ndibonakala, nani niya kubonakala nam ebuqaqawulini yaye ukusukela ngoko niya kuba nam ngonaphakade. Ningumtshakazi wam yaye ningumzimba wam; andinibizanga na ngegama? Andinenzelanga ndlela na yokuba nibuyele kuYihlo, uYise wezikhanyiso?

Ndingulowo wayesaziwa nguBawo phambi kokuba umhlaba uqalise, owathunywa wazalelwa usindiso lwenu, owabethelelwa emnqamlezweni, enikela ngethontsi lam lokugqibela legazi ngenxa yezono zenu. Akukho nto ishiyiweyo kwaye akukho nto ingenzekanga, kwaye oko kuthethiweyo kuya kwenziwa kuze kugqitywe ngokupheleleyo. Ndinidale ngomoya wam ongcwele awathi uBawo wawunika ukuba uwuthululele phezu kwabapostile bam ngaloo mini yakudala yePentekoste, xa babexela imisebenzi emangalisayo kaThixo ngeelwimi ababengaziqondi nabo. Ndibizile, yaye nangona abaninzi bendele, intsalela yobabalo nonyulo isabele, yaye ndenze umsebenzi omkhulu kuyo oza kutyhilwa kungekudala. Inene, xa uzijonga esipilini, awuziboni ukuba ungubani na kanye. Ndinenze naba namandla ebuthathakeni benu, yaye ekubonakaleni kwam nani niya kubonakala. Niya kufana nam, yaye niya kuhlala ninam ngonaphakade. Ndingamazibulo kwabafileyo, yaye isivuno sisondele kuni. Xa ndiya kubonakala, niya kunyukela phezulu nindikhawulele, yaye niya kuba sendlwini kaBawo xa ndinixhwila ndinisa ekhayeni lenu emazulwini.

Inene, inene, kwiLizwi lam elingcwele (njengoko ndiliLizwi elenziwa inyama elingcwele nelihamba phakathi kwenu), ewe, kwiLizwi lam elingcwele, anifundanga na ukuba umgwebo uqala ngendlu kaThixo? Ndiza kugqiba umsebenzi kwabanyulwa bam, abangamalungisa, nabakhethiweyo, apho niya kudalwa ngokutsha ngokupheleleyo kum nize nibe njengam, kuba nifaniswe nomfanekiso woKuphela koZelweyo unyana kaThixo. Umvuzo wam unam, yaye andiyi kunigweba ngenxa yezono zenu; andizihlawulelanga na? Ngokwamkela kwenu ubuNkosi bam nokuzifanisa nam, nixolelwe yaye ningamalungisa kum, kuba ndahlawula ixabiso eloyikekayo elalingafanelanga ndilihlawule njengoko ndingenasono. Hayi, ndathabatha izono zenu, ubugwenxa benu, ubugqwirha benu, nengqumbo nesohlwayo kuni ukuze nimanyane noYise noNyana. Hayi, ndiya kugweba imisebenzi yenu eyenziweyo emhlabeni.

Yaye kunye nomgwebo wam kukho umvuzo omkhulu, yaye imisebenzi emininzi iya kusinda eyigolide, isilivere, namatye anqabileyo. Inene, iinkuni, ifula, neendiza ziya kutsha nje ngomququ, yaye niya kulahlekelwa, nangona niya kusindiswa ngokungathi kuphuma emlilweni. Kuba umlilo wam kuni ngumlilo womnyibilikisi, endiye ndazihlambulula ngawo imiphefumlo yenu njengoko umkhandi wegolide ehlambulula igolide yakhe, esusa inkunkuma. Yimani niqine nize nizole kum. Ngubani na lowo umele anikhathaze, nangona inene nitshutshiswa yaye ninyamezela imbandezelo? Musani ukoyika, banditshiyile nam yaye bayani tshiya nani. Noko ke, ekuhlangulweni kwenu niya kuthandwa kakhulu ebuqaqawulini endiya kunambathisa ngabo ngaloo mini isondeleyo nesondela ngokukhawuleza. UBawo ulangazelela ukunibona phambi kwakhe kwigumbi lakhe letrone, apho izihlalo zenu zinilindileyo, kuba uBawo unihlalise ezulwini. Aningabo abanyulwa bakhe, oonozakuzaku bakhe, uzuko lwakhe, isithsaba, nebhaso elixabiso lingenakulinganiswa? Inene, anixabiseki.

Umgwebo uza kuwela phezu kweBhabhiloni Enkulu kungekudala, yaye uya kuba ngumgwebo owothusayo, kodwa uwufanele. Owu, mntu, ndiya kukunyamezela kude kube nini na? Usuke ekubeni luhlanga lweengcwele olwaluyisibane ehlabathini ukuba luze kum, waba luhlanga lwamahenyukazi akrexezayo. Ndibahlule abantu bam kuni, yaye olo lwahlulo sele luza kugqitywa njengoko eli phakade lobabalo lisondela esiphelweni. Owu, mntu, kutheni ulandela omubi, wamkela izinyobo zakhe ezingenaxabiso ngaphezu kwam, mna Krestu ongunaphakade, kuko konke ukubengezela nozuko lwam.

UBawo wanika ixesha lokuba zonke iintlanga ziguquke, yaye abantu abaninzi, nkqu nesihlwele esikhulu, basamkele isicelo sam kubabalo lwam olusindisayo oluthululelwe phezu kwabo.

Noko ke wena, owu, mntu wemvelo owalayo izinto zomoya nomdelayo ummelwane wakhe, uya kunyamezela umgwebo omkhulu phezu komhlaba wakho, yaye iBhabhiloni Enkulu ayisayi kuphinda ibekho. Bhabhiloni, usuke waba luhlanga lwamahenyukazi adlala indima nezikhaka zawo zingaphezu kweentloko zawo njengedonki yasendle elishushu lilindele umdluli olandelayo. Nizidyobhile ngenyama yasemzini, niziyolisile nazithanda izono zenu, yaye umgwebo ongunaphakade uya kuwela phezu kwabo bonke abaneentliziyo ezilukhuni ababala idini lam njengento elula nengenaxabiso yokujongwa. Nisuke naba luhlanga lwabenzi bobubi nabaphuli beminqophiso abatshintsha abafazi njengeengubo ezindala bafake ezintsha de le ntsha idyobheke. Amazwi enu amakhulu eniwathethileyo ngoBawo aya kuphenduliswa. Iingcinga zenu ezingenaxabiso ziya kubekwa phambi kwenu, yaye niya kuphendula. Kodwa ukuphendula kwenu akusayi kuze kwanele ukuba nime nilungile phambi kwam. Ubukho benu kanye bonakalisiwe, yaye ekuphambaneni kwenu nikhethe inyama yenu ebolayo, kuba njengoko watshoyo uBawo, niya kufa ngenene. Noko ke, kusabonakala kungaqhelekanga kuni ukuba ekufeni niya kufa. Nangona ukuphelelwa kwenu ngokupheleleyo kungakhange kwenzeke kwangoko, benisifa ukususela kusuku lokuphefumla kwenu kokuqala. Umgwebo uvakaliswe phezu kohlanga lwenu, yaye namazulu akakhwazi na into enye? Ndiya kuwutshabalalisa ngokupheleleyo umhlaba wenu, yaye iinqaba zenu ezinkulu ziya kuwa, nobumnyama obukhulu buya kugubungela umhlaba. Iindonga zenu sele zigqojozwe, yaye izigidi zizele kuni ukuba zithathe kuni oko ndibanike kona, kuba ndiya kutyebisa isandla seentshaba zenu emgwebeni wenu. Ixesha lenu seliza kuphela, yaye niluhlanga olubotshiweyo phambi kwam, nilindele iingelosi zam ukuba zize zigwebe. Niya kuwa kakhulu njengoko amanzi esiza phezu kwenu, njengoko ukushukuma kusiza phezu kwenu, njengoko imikhosi ikhululwa, yaye umhlaba wenu uya kucandwa kubini. Indlala iya kuzinza phezu kwehlabathi, yaye imfazwe iya kufika ehlabathini lonke njengoko irhamncwa liphuma eluthuthwini kumayelenqe alo acetywe ixesha elide.

Kunikwe ixesha lokuguquka, yaye abaninzi baphendule ngokholo, inceba enkulu endiyinike phambi kokuba umgwebo wenziwe. Andinayolo kule ntshabalalo izayo, kodwa nanamhlanje, abaninzi baphila esonweni esikhulu, bade baxhaphaze nabantwana benu, yaye anikhathali nokukhathala lo gama imali yenu ikufuphi. Ndiya kuthatha bonke ubutyebi, izinto ezimnandi, ulonwabo, nolonwabo kuni. Niya kushwabana phambi kwamazulu, nifuna iqhekeza lokutya elingayi kufunyanwa ngabaninzi. Noko ke, abo banqula igama leNkosi baya kusindiswa.

Bantwana bam abancinane, liyure lokugqibela lexesha lokugqibela kweli phakade lobabalo. Ndithumele abaprofeti bam emsebenzini womhlobo wam othandekayo uYohane uMbhaptizi. Ababanga lizwi elidandulukayo entlango na? Asingabo abelusi bam, ababizayo nabafuna intsalela yomhlambi wam olahlekileyo? Inene, ndinesinye isibaya nalo, yaye ixesha losindiso lwaso lisondele, njengoko uYakobi eya kusindiswa nentsalela kaYakobi iya kundamkela njengeNkosi yayo, njengoko bonke abanyulwa besiba mntu mnye omtsha. Ndithumele abaninzi kuwo wonke umhlaba, bevakalisa ukufika kwam.

Nangona abaninzi kubantu bam bekhohlisiwe yaye bedikidiki, umoya wam uya kuvuka kwabaninzi, yaye abaninzi baya kubuyela kum. Ukubonakala kwam kusondele. Yimani niqine nize nikhwaze ukufika kwam nikhwaze namazwi am osindiso kubo bonke. Ixesha selisondele, yaye kukho ukuzola enzulwini njengoko imikhosi yasezulwini ilindele ukuvuthelwa kwexilongo elikufuphi kunokuba unokucinga. Noko ke, kusele ixesha elifutshane kakhulu, yaye abaninzi ngakumbi baya kusindiswa, ngoko musani ukutyhafa.

Ndingulowo walusa ibandla lam yaye ndanika izipho ebantwini.

NdinguYehova omkhulu nonamandla, owaxhelwayo kwasekusekweni kwehlabathi.

Ndingulowo wanikela ngobomi bakhe ngenxa yomhlambi, uKrestu ophilayo awamvusayo uThixo kwabafileyo.

NdinguLizwi elenziwa inyama elihleli phakathi kwenu.

Ndingunyana okuphela kwamzeleyo kaThixo oze enyameni, nkqu noMesiya onamandla kaSirayeli kaThixo.

Ndikufuphi, yaye ukubonakala kwam kusondele.

Ndingumgwebi olilungisa ofanele ukuwavula amatywina.

NdinguMvana kaThixo, owaxhelwa ngenxa yezono zabantu bam.

Ndingumsindisi ongenasono osindisa ngokugqibeleleyo.

Ndingumntu oqhelene nosizi neentsizi ezininzi, kodwa uvuyo lwam luhleli lugcwele.

NdinguYehova ophilayo, umalusi olilungisa.

Ndingulowo kwaprofethwa ngaye ngabo bonke abaprofeti yaye ndinezitshixo zokufa nezesihogo, yaye uBawo uzibeke zonke izinto ezulwini nasemhlabeni ezandleni zam ezinamandla.
