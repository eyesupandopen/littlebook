Ubuso bewotshi Buphelelwe Ngamanani Awileyo

Undilungele na, Mtshakazi wam? (Kuba ndiyeza) Ukulungele na? Uphulaphule na? Ujonge na? Kuba ndiyeza kuwe, ngoko musa ukutyhafa ube nethemba elikhulu nolindelo, kuba zona kanye iindyebo zeZulu zilindele abathembekileyo bam kwaye umvuzo wakho usesandleni sam! Kodwa yeha kubo bonke abandikhanyele ngokungakholwa okungenalukholo, mna kanye, uYesu waseNazarete! Kwaye ke umhla womgwebo wenu uphezulu kwenu, o wena hengekazikazi elikhulu elihleli phezu kwamanzi amaninzi, kwaye ixesha lakho elimisiweyo lifikile.

Intshabalalo yesiquphe iphezu kwakho kwaye iintshaba zakho zizilungisile, kodwa usaphila kamnandi nangona uluhlanga olusemakhonkcweni phambi kwam, uKristu Yesu. Ixesha lakho elangeni lenzeka kuba okwexeshana ooYihlo balandela uNyana kaThixo baza bagcina amazwi am. Kodwa ixesha leBandla lam lifikelele esiphelweni kuba narhweba ngeendyebo zeemfihlelo zam, kwaye kwanentlalo-ntle nothando lwabantu bam nizithengisile ngenxa yenzuzo emdaka nodumo phambi kwabantu.

Nazithengisa ngokubawa okubi nokunganyaniseki, kwaye nindilahlile, mna kanye, uYesu Kristu, owafela izono zehlabathi. Kodwa nilalile ilizwi lam, niyalile isohlwayo sam, niyalile isiluleko sam, niyalile oonozakuzaku bam, niyalile abaprofeti bam, niyalile abapostile bam, kwaye nindalile mna, uYesu waseNazarete, ngoko ke nimkhanyele uYahweh, kwaye ngoku ndiza kunikhanyela nani, o wena Bhabhiloni eyimfihlo. Kwaye ke umgwebo uqala eNdlwini kaYahweh!

Kuba xa beninokuba noxolo nokhuseleko, endaweni yoko nakhetha ukulandela ngokungaboni abo balonakalisileyo uhlanga lwenu. Kwanomdiza wamaEdom ongcolisa imihlaba, nosikhathazileyo isizwe ukusukela oko okhohlakeleyo wasasaza amanyala akhe kwindalo kaYahweh.

Ngoko ke yazini, ukuba mna, mna kanye uKristu Yesu, ndiya kuyitshabalalisa ngokupheleleyo iBhabhiloni eyiMfihlakalo ngomlilo kwaye ngeyure enye intshabalalo yenu iya kufika, kuba kubhaliwe kwaye oko akuthethileyo uYahweh akukho mntu unokukuguqula. Owu, nina banquli-zithixo abazizidenge bomhlaba weBhabhiloni eyiMfihlo, ukuba beninokuqonda imeko yenu. Ukuba beningathobela iindlebe zenu kumazwi am kuba ndibizile, ndacenga, ndabongoza, ndakhuthaza, ndakhwaza, ndabonisa imiqondiso phezulu nemiqondiso emhlabeni kwaye endaweni yokwambathisa intliziyo yenu ngamalaphu okuzila, endaweni yokuhlala eluthuthwini lwenguquko endaweni yoko nizonwabisa ngokungathi nibukele amaqhinga omlingo.

Yeha kwabo bonwatyiswa yimiqondiso kaYahweh esibhakabhakeni ebhengeza umgwebo wabo, kwaye yeha kabini kuwe Bhabhiloni eyiMfihlakalo, kuba unesabelo esiphindwe kabini somgwebo osele uza kukhululwa, kwaye awuyi kubaleka okanye uchache. Bhabhiloni eyiMfihlo ungumhlaba wokwaneliseka kwangoko, kwaye awunabulumko kwaphela. Uzihendile iintlanga ngeendlela zakho zobubi nokuyekelela, kwaye uzinxilisile ngamanyala ohenyuzo lwakho. Aniziqondi iindlela zam, kuba ngamnye kuni uhamba ngeendlela zakhe njengoko ngobusathana besenza nantoni na abayithandayo, yenza oko ukuthandayo ngumthetho wabo wonke wobusathana kwaye ke abangenamthetho abayi kuba yanto.

Aniziqondi iindlela zam, kuba iindlela zam ziindlela zikaYahweh, kwaye mna ndiyindlela, inyaniso, nobomi, kuba akukho mntu uza kuBawo ngaphandle kwam kwaye ukuba nindibonile mna nimbonile noBawo. Kodwa nizivalile iingqondo zenu, nizivalile iindlebe zenu, niziqaqadekisile iintliziyo zenu, kwaye nivule imilomo yenu ngokungakholwa okunyelisayo, nide ninyelise nomnqophiso woxolo eniwunikiweyo nguYahweh. Kuba mna Yesu Kristu, ndiluxolo lwenu!

Kodwa hayi, endaweni yokundenza iNkosi endaweni yoko nisebenzisa igama lam elingcwele, mna kanye, uYesu Kristu, ngokulifumana njengelinye ilizwi lokubonisa amanyala ezintliziyweni zenu. Kodwa aninakulihlambela igama lam, kuba uYahweh undinike igama elingaphezu kwawo onke amagama, ukuze ngegama likaYesu Kristu onke amadolo aguqe, ezulwini nasemhlabeni. Kwaye niya kuhlawulela onke amazwi angenamsebenzi, kwaye amaxesha enu afikile. Kwaye ke ngoku, ekubeni nizivalele kwimingxuma yenu nicinga ukuba ningazifihla kumgwebo kaYahweh, ngoku ndiza kuvala umnyango wokungena emazulwini kwabangathembekanga njengoko ndixhwila abathembekileyo bam kunye nam emazulwini kuba umyeni uyasondela.

Ngobudenge nicinga ukuba yonke into ibuyele esiqhelweni, kwaye imfazwe ezayo inokubukelwa kumabonakude njengezinye iimfazwe ezaziliwa ngokungekho sikweni ziinkokeli zenu. Njengoko niye nanwatyiswa kukufa nentshabalalo yezinye iintlanga, ngokunjalo nohlanga lwenu luya kutshatyalaliswa ngokupheleleyo, kwaye ukufa nentshabalalo yenu kuya kubonelela ngembuyekezo kwabo batshatyalaliswe ngemvume yenu.

Ngobudenge nicinga ukuba ningayilungiselela ingqumbo ezayo ngokuphila ngomhlaba, kwindawo ethile ekude nempucuko, ngaphandle kokuba niya kutshatyalaliswa ngokupheleleyo, nibanjwe, nibulawe, nizingelwe, nilambe, nigule, kwaye izibetho ezininzi ziya kuwela bonke abo bandikhanyelayo mna Yeshua HaMashiach, kuba ukundikhanyela mna kukukhanyela uThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade, uYahweh ngokwakhe kwaye akuyi kubakho nyango. Ngoko balekelani kum ngoku!

Ngobudenge aniqondi ukuba iimpondo ezilishumi zicebisene nerhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni nerhamncwa eliphuma elwandle ezingekaphakanyiselwa kumandla apheleleyo, kwaye uhlanga lwenu olwalukhe lwaba lukhulu lunikelwe ngokupheleleyo ezandleni zazo ukuze lutshatyalaliswe ngokupheleleyo. (Kwaye ngentshabalalo epheleleyo yohlanga lwenu amarhamncwa aya kuphakama!) Kuba uThixo ukufakile ezintliziyweni zazo ukuzalisekisa iinjongo zakhe.

Ningqingwe ngokupheleleyo ziinqanawa zeentshaba zenu, nivumele izigidi ngezigidi zeMikhosi eBomvu, kwanomkhosi werhamncwa ukuba ungene kwimihlaba yenu. Nibavumele ukuba benze izenzo zokonakalisa, bazitshabalalisile iindlela zenu zokubonelela, batshabalalise ukutya kwenu, batshabalalise uqoqosho lwenu, benze iinkampu zokubulala, ukuthuthumbisa nokubulala nabani na ongahambelani neenjongo zabo. Qondani ukuba nangoko nabani na obiza egameni lam, mna kanye, uYesu Kristu, kude kube sekupheleni uya kusindiswa. Abo benza ukhetho lwabo lokwala lube lolokugqibela baya kuhlala ngonaphakade neziphumo zokungakholwa kumadangatye amakhulu alungiselelwe umtyholi neengelosi zakhe, kuba akukho olunye ukhetho.

Ndiyaluleka bonke abafunda la mazwi ukuba basondele kum. Yekani iingxoxo zenu ezingenantsingiselo kwaye niyeke ukuxabana kwenu okungenantsingiselo phakathi kwenu kwaye nishumayele ivangeli yosindiso njengoko ibhaliwe esibhalweni kwaye nishumayele ilizwi lam, kwanamazwi amazwi am kule mizuzwana yokugqibela. Wathethe phakathi kwenu, kwaye nihlale nikulungele ukunceda abo basweleyo. Yekani ukugweba abazalwana noodade wenu kwaye endaweni yoko nincedane ngothando ukuze nime elubabalweni nasencebeni endiyinikeleyo, kuba yanele.

Vumani niguquke ezonweni zenu kwaye nenjenjalo ngoku, aninalo ixesha! Ndenzeni mna, uYesu waseNazarete, iNkosi yenu, kuba ixesha liphelile. Zani kum ngoku, kuba akukho amanye amagama anikiweyo osindiso, ngaphandle kwelam, uYesu Kristu. Zani kum ngoku kwaye nimke ebubini kwaye ndiya kunamkela kum ukuba nithe nandivuma njengeNkosi ngentliziyo ezele lukholo nisazi ukuba ndivusiwe kwabafileyo ukuze ndingaphindi ndife. Ndolule iingalo zam ezivulekileyo kwihlabathi elifayo kangangeeYubhili ezininzi ngoku kwaye amaxesha obabalo nenceba asekupheleni, nangona ubabalo nenceba zihlala zinam kuba ndiyiNkosi enobabalo.

Kwaye ke ngoku, nilindile kule mizuzwana yokugqibela, o wena Bhabhiloni eyimfihlakalo. Kuba kuya kwenzeka ngequbuliso. Ngequbuliso umlilo uya kuwa, ngequbuliso amanzi aya kugubungela iziketi zenu eziphakanyiswe ngaphezu kwezinqe zenu, o wena hengekazikazi elikhulu, ngequbuliso ukushukuma okukhulu kuya kuza phezu kwenu kwaye kunikrazule, ngequbuliso umyalelo uya kunikwa kwaye izigidi ziya kusasazeka kuwo wonke umhlaba ukubulala nawuphi na umntu ophilayo ongahambelani neenjongo zabo. Abayi kuba nanceba, abanazisola, abanakucengwa, abanavelwano, kodwa bakhonza uSathana kwaye baya kuzalisekisa iinjongo abazinikiweyo ukuba bazizalisekise, kuba baya kuzalisekisa ingqumbo kaThixo kwiBhabhiloni eyiMfihlakalo kwaye ayisayi kuphinda iphakame.

Yabonani! Amaxesha alapha. Guqukani ngoku nisakwazi ukuphefumla, kuba kuya kwenzeka ngequbuliso. Yivani amazwi am kwaye niqonde: Umthinteli kuphela kwamandla agcina obu bubi bungaveli, kwaye ixesha enicinga ukuba ninalo lixesha nje elivunyelweyo lokulungiselela nokuhambisa iziqwenga endaweni yazo kwibhodi yehlabathi kwaye la maxesha akhonza iinjongo zikaYahweh.

Emva koko ngequbuliso ndiya kuqokelela abathembekileyo eBandleni lam ukuze ndidibane nam emafini kwaye ndiya kumxhwila uMtshakazi wam ndimse emagumbini akhe, kuba endlwini kaBawo zininzi iindawo zokuhlala kwaye ndaya kukulungiselela indawo Mtshakazi wam kwaye amazulu akulungele!

Kwaye ngequbuliso, xa ubutyebi obuxabisekileyo bukaBawo bugcinwe ngokukhuselekileyo kwindlu yobuncwane yamazulu (kuba kuya kwenzeka ngokuqhwanyaza kweliso) yonke iyod kunye nalo lonke ilahle leyod ziya kuzaliseka, kwanalo lonke ilizwi endilithethileyo ngabaprofeti bam bokwenyani nabapostile, kuba baninzi kwaye abaninzi abangabam bathetha ngazwi-nye kwaye bathetha ngokwezibhalo zam. Qondani oku, abo bawakhanyelayo amazwi acacileyo esibhalweni malunga nokuqokelelwa kweBandla lam ukuze lidibane nam emafini, kwabo bakhanyelayo baya kukhanyelwa nabo kwaye bathathe indawo yabo ngokuzithandela engqumbeni xa into enjalo ibingeyomfuneko, kodwa bangaphi kuni abaya kulikhanyela ngenkani ilizwi lam nithanda abo banikiti-kitayo iindlebe zenu? Kodwa, kuya kuba njalo.

Ukuba kukho nabani na ongathethiyo ngokwamazwi am, kuba mna Kristu Yesu, ndiliLizwi elenziwe inyama, musani nokumnqwenelela impumelelo kuba abawaqondi kakuhle amazwi am kuba abaprofeti bam abasokuze baphikisane nesibhalo kuba andinakuziphikisa. Ndingamazwi kanye kaYahweh enziwe inyama, kwaye ndinguYeshua HaMashiach, iMvana kanye kaThixo eyeza kucoca izono zoluntu.

Ndiyeza njengeNgonyama yesizwe sakwaYuda kwaye ingqumbo yam ishushu emhlabeni, kuba ngobulungisa ndiyagweba kwaye ndenza imfazwe. Guqukani nina bakhohlakeleyo bomhlaba kuba ukwala kwenu kuya kuba kokunaphakade. Ekupheleni kweminyaka esixhenxe yengxaki kaYakobi iHlabathi liya kuphumla kuba kuya kubakho usuku lokuphumla kuba iminyaka eliwaka ifana nosuku nje emehlweni kaYahweh. Kwaye iNgonyama iya kulala neMvana kuba iinjongo zam zeMvana kaThixo ziya kuzalisekiswa yiNgonyama yesizwe sakwaYuda. Ixesha liphelile.

Mna Yeshua HaMashiach ndithumele la mazwi ngesicaka sam, kwaye ngesicaka sam la mazwi athunyelwe!
