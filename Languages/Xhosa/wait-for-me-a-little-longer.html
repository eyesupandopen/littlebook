Ndilindeni ixesha elingephi: Andiyi kulibazisa

Ndilindeni ixesha elingephi, bantwana bam, kwaye andiyi kulibazisa. Andisokuze ndinishiye okanye ndinilahle, kuba niliqula leso likaBawo nequla leso lam kanye. NinguMtshakazi wam, kwaye nguwuphi na umyeni oshiya umtshakazi wakhe kumgwebo waboni?

Yazini ukuba mna, iNkosi uYesu Krestu, ndiza kubahlangula abantu bam. Anifundanga na kwiZibhalo Ezingcwele? UBawo akamthabathanga na uEnoki wamsa emazulwini? UBawo akamthabathanga na uEliya wamsa emazulwini? UBawo akabakhululanga na abantwana bakwaSirayeli eYiputa? Akamhlangulanga na uLote eSodom ngaphambi kokuba umlilo uwele kwezo zixeko, uzitshabalalise zonke? Kangakanani na ke ngoku endiya kumhlangula umtshakazi wam endimthenge ngegazi lam ezandleni zomgwebo oza kudlula kweli hlabathi? Niya kude kube nini na nithabathela ubudenge kum, iNkosi yenu, nakuBawo, uThixo wenu?

Ndiniphe ubomi obungunaphakade, kwaye kukho umvuzo omkhulu olondolozelwe abathembekileyo ezulwini, ngoko musani ukoyika, kuba ilifa lenu liza kuzalisekiswa. NdinguMalusi olilungisa ohlangula umhlambi kwiingcuka eziphangayo. Kwakwezi ngcuka ziya kuva kungekudala zisithiwa, "Mkani kum, nina benzi bobubi; andizanga ndinazi, nangona nithi, Nkosi, Nkosi!" NdinguMalusi othembekileyo ongalahlekelwanga namnye uBawo andinike yena, ngaphandle konyana wentshabalalo owandingcatshayo ngezicucu zesilivere ezingamashumi amathathu kwaye isibhalo sazalisekiswa ngokwenene sisiphoso sakhe nokwala kwakhe. Ziziphi izifundo ezikhoyo kuni, bantwana bam, ngaphandle kokuba aninakulahleka esandleni sam kwaye akukho namnye unako ukunixutha kum?

Musani ukuwoyika umgwebo wabenzi bobubi, nina bathembekileyo bam, kwaye yazini ukuba ingqumbo kaBawo ayiyi kunichukumisa. Nguwuphi na utata phakathi kwenu onganikela nomnye wabantwana bakhe ezandleni zotshaba? Nangona esi sisono esiqhelekileyo phakathi kwabantu bomhlaba, akukho sithunzi sakujika kuYise wezikhanyiso! Ngoko musani ukoyika nibe seluxolweni. Yekani imisebenzi yenu nize niphumle kum. Phangelani imisebenzi yam nize nifumane ukuphumla kum, iNkosi uYesu Krestu, owazinikela njengedini lezono zabo bonke kwaba kanye naphakade.

Owu, nina bantwana bomhlaba, izono zenu ziphambi kwam, kwaye niziqwebele ingqumbo enkulu nomgwebo! Endaweni yokuqwebela ilifa leentsikelelo, niqwebe ingqumbo phezu kwengqumbo ngalo mhla mkhulu uza kunigubungela. Owu, amazwi enu angenamsebenzi eniwathethileyo, nisithi, "Iphi na iNkosi; iyintsomi nje engazanga ibekho kwaye ingomso liya kufana nanamhlanje nezolo." Umgwebo wenu uya kunigubungela ngequbuliso, kwaye iziganeko ziya kuhamba ngokukhawuleza kangangokuba niya kulahleka ekubulalekeni. Amazwi enu amabi neendlela zenu ezimbi ziya kunizisela intshabalalo enkulu ngomzuzwana. Konke enikuxabisileyo kuya kususwa kuni, nabo bonke ubutyebi benu buya kunyamalala ezandleni zenu njengoko ndibunikele ezandleni zabo banithiyileyo, kuba nindithiye ngaphandle kwesizathu.

Ingqumbo yeMvana iza kuthululwa phezu kwenu, kwaye iinimba zokusikwa zisondelelene ngoku, zisondele kangangokuba niziphazamisa nengqumbo yeMvana. Yazi, wena mntu usisidenge, ukuba ekuzekeni kwam kade umsindo ndiyanilumkisa ngokufa kwenu, kwaye nisahlekisa ningakholwa. Kungekudala kakhulu ndiza kususa umnqandi phakathi kwenu, kwaye ndiza kususa uMtshakazi wam phakathi kwenu, kwaye intshabalalo epheleleyo iya kuza phezu kwenu ngequbuliso. USathana uhambise imikhosi yakhe neziqwenga zakhe endaweni, ekulungele ukukhupha umsindo wakhe opheleleyo nengqumbo yakhe phezu kwenu, kwaye ndininikele ezandleni zakhe. Isizwe senu esikhulu sicinyiwe kwaye siphawulelwe intshabalalo epheleleyo; owu, ukuba beningawaqonda amazwi athi, iBhabhiloni eyimfihlelo. Ukanti lisekho ixesha lokuguquka, kodwa uninzi lwenu aluyi kwenjenjalo ngeendlela zenu ezineenkani neentliziyo ezilukhuni zovukelo lobuqhetseba.

Umgwebo wenu uya kuza phezu kwenu ngeendlela ezimbini eziphambili: ngaphakathi nangaphandle. Ihenyukazi elikhulu liya kukrazulwa libe ziziqwenga; liya kudliwa ngamanzi litshiswe ngomlilo ligutyungelwe yindyikitya yokufa nendlala. Ewe, wena mntu wasemhlabeni, ngubani na oya kukuhlangula esandleni sam? Akukho mntu uzayo ukukusindisa. Intshabalalo yenu isondele. Imikhosi ebomvu yangena esizweni senu kanye phantsi kweempumlo zenu, kwaye anizange nikhathalele nje ukuba nizonwabisa kwaye nityebile ngobutyebi bexeshana obungazange bube bobenu kwasekuqaleni, kuba umhlaba yoweNkosi nenzaliseko yawo. Anizitywinelanga nje intshabalalo yenu, kodwa nithengise abantwana benu nabazukulwana benu kwintshabalalo epheleleyo, kwaye nisahlekisa. Inzala yenu ilahlekile. Aniyi kuhlekisa ixesha elide njengoko zonke iindlela zenu ezimbi nezigwenxa ziya kunigubungela elusizini, ekuthuthunjisweni, nasentshabalalweni, nangona isihlwele esikhulu siya kuhlanjululwa ngembandezelo.

Bonke abo bajikela kum baze babize egameni lam kwaye bangabathandi ubomi babo kude kube sekupheleni, ndiya kubavuza ngonaphakade! Abasokuze baphinde babandezeleke, kodwa ukubandezeleka kwenu akusokuze kuphele, nina bakhohlakeleyo. Kodwa nina, nina bantu bendalo abamgatya uThixo, abavakalise imfazwe nxamnye noMdali wenu kwaye nanqwenela ukuvukela mna neendlela zam. Intshabalalo yenu iya kuza ngequbuliso kwaye ngaphandle kwesiqabu. Nibe sisizwe samahenyukazi, kwaye kufaneleke kangakanani na ukuba izizwe zenu zibizwe njengehenyukazi kwiZibhalo Ezingcwele. Imikhosi iya kudla okuseleyo kwesizwe senu esakha saba sikhulu nesinamandla, kwaye ukuhlanjululwa okukhulu kuya kuza phezu kwelizwe, nesohlwayo esikhulu siya kunidla ngenxa yeendlela zenu ezimbi.

Nithathele yonke into ngenxa yeendlela zenu zokuvukela neentliziyo ezilukhuni; kwaye nangona ndazihlawulela izono zenu, nisalile isicelo sam, nikhetha endaweni yoko ukuzihlawulela ngokwenu, kwaye niya kuzisola ngonaphakade. Xa umnqandi esusiwe, intshabalalo ekhawulezileyo iya kuba liqabane lenu; kwaye nibingelele abantwana benu kwisiphelo esifana nesenu, nangona abaninzi baya kusindiswa. Kwanabanye babantwana benu baya kujikela kum, kwaye ndiya kubamkela eluzukweni, nangona nanibathintela kum, kwaye nina niya kungena entshabalalweni kwelo chibi likhulu lilindele uMtyholi nezithunywa zakhe.

Bantwana bam abancinane, musani ukoyika. Qhubekani nime nithembekile kum apha kulo mzuzu wokugqibela, ewe, umzuzu wenu wokugqibela kweli hlabathi lifayo. Ndinibizile, ndinikhethile, ndinikhethe ngesandla ngenxa yothando lwenu ngam nokuthembeka kwenu, kwaye ubutyebi obukhulu bunilindile endlwini kaBawo. Niya kusiwa kukhuseleko nomvuzo omkhulu; nangona abanye baya kulahlekelwa, bonke abanyulwa bam baya kusindiswa ngokupheleleyo nangokugqibeleleyo.

Gxilani kum, thethanani nam; ndisondele kunomphefumlo wenu olandelayo. Yazini, banyulwa bam, ukuba ndiyanilwela; kwaye nangona intshutshiso nembandezelo zininzi, niya kusindiswa engqumbeni. Ngoko ke, zihlanjululeni ngokuphuma kweli hlabathi nize nilahle iindlela zalo ezimbi. Hlambululani izandla zenu ezonweni zalo nize nimke ebugwenxeni balo, kuba nabani na osebhedini yehenyukazi uya kubandezeleka. Zihlanjululeni egazini lam lentlawulelo nize nizihlambe ngokupheleleyo. Yabonani, ndinitywinile ngetywina likaBawo, kwaye ndiya kunihlawulela ngokupheleleyo ndinidale ngokutsha ngokomfanekiso wam. Ewe, niya kuba ngumntu omtsha kwaye niyindalo entsha kum, kwaye kungekudala kuya kubakho izulu elitsha nomhlaba omtsha, kuba uBawo wenza zonke izinto zibe ntsha. Ngoko yimani niqinile nize nazi ukuba uthando lwam ngumlilo ovuthayo, kwaye nixatyiswe kakhulu kwaye nilangazelelwa. Kungekudala niya kuba nam.
