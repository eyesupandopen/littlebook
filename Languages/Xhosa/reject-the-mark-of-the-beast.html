Yalani Uphawu Lwerhamncwa!

Yivani ilizwi lobulumko niguqe ngamadolo, nive ilizwi eliphilayo likaYeshua HaMashiach, kuba mna ndiyiNgonyama yesizwe sakwaYuda, yaye ndinguKumkani wooKumkani neNkosi yeeNkosi! U-El Shaddai, uThixo uSomandla, undiphakamisile kakhulu waza wandinika igama elingaphezu kwawo onke amagama. Ukuze ngegama likaYesu onke amadolo aguqe, ezinto ezisemazulwini, nezinto ezisemhlabeni, nezinto eziphantsi komhlaba: Kwaye zonke iilwimi zivume ukuba uYesu Krestu uyiNkosi, kube luzuko kuYise wezikhanyiso, uYahweh, uThixo ongunaphakade nongunaphakade, kuba akukho namnye onjengoBawo wethu!

Yivani ilizwi leNkosi yenu niziqonde izinto esele ziqhubeka nezinto eziza kwenzeka kungekudala, kuba yabonani ndingulowo uthetha ngezinto ezizayo ngokungathi sele zenzekile kuba ngokuqinisekileyo iNkosi yenu enkulu, mna Krestu Yesu oyena ubalaseleyo, andiyi kwenza nto, ngaphandle kokuba ndityhile iimfihlelo zam kubakhonzi bam abaProfeti. Kutheni na niphulaphula amazwi amaninzi kangaka aphikisanayo, yaye kutheni na nijinga phakathi kwezimvo ezimbini? Anditshongo na ukuba abaprofeti bam bathetha ngazwinye yaye abasokuze bawaphikise amazwi am angcwele? Kuba mna ndiliLizwi elenziwa inyama elihlala phakathi kwenu ngoMoya wam oyiNgcwele ozelwe ngaphakathi kwenu ngonaphakade, kuba wonke umphefumlo ozelwe ngokutsha utywinwe ngaloo moya ungcwele wesithembiso oluhlobo lwam olungenakonakala nophawu lukaThixo ophilayo alunakwaphulwa. Kwaye usuku lokuhlangulwa kwelifa elithengiweyo lusemnyango kwaye inkululeko yenu epheleleyo ilapha.

Utsho uYehova wemikhosi, musani ukuweva amazwi abaprofeti abaprofeta kuni, abanifundisa amampunge: bathetha umbono wentliziyo yabo, kungekhona ophuma emlonyeni weNkosi. Yizani kum bantwana bam kuba intambiso enayifumanayo kum, ihleli kuni: kwaye anidingi mntu ukuba anifundise: Kodwa, njengoko yona loo ntambiso inifundisa zonke izinto, iyinyaniso, kwaye ayibobuxoki: kwaye kanye njengoko inifundisile, niya kuhlala kum. Bantwana bam hlalani kum nithelekise amazwi akhe nabani na othi uthetha egameni lam nesibhalo nize nindicele ukuba ndiqinisekise emoyeni wenu, ukuba mhlawumbi niya kuliphulaphula ilizwi lam kuba abaninzi benu baneendlebe ezilukhuni, kwaye abaninzi benu bathanda amazwi obuxoki achukumisa iindlebe zenu ukuze nibonakale nilumkile kodwa endaweni yoko nilandele amazwi obuxoki ezidenge ezikhukhumeleyo.

Yabonani! Ndithetha ngamaxesha embandezelo kaYakobi ekulungele ukuba iqhambuke kuwo wonke umhlaba. Kuba umgwebo sele uqalile yaye awunakunqandwa, kuba oko akutshoyo uYahweh akukho mntu unokukuguqula! Iinyembezi ezininzi, ezininzi ndizikhalile ngenxa yomgwebo weBhabheli eyimfihlakalo kwaye ndithumele abaprofeti bam iminyaka emininzi, emininzi ngoku kwaye ndithumele nabasemzini abavela kumazwe angaziwayo ukuba bangqine kwaye baprofete ngemigwebo eyayisondela kodwa ngoku ilapha.

Kuba kuya kuba njalo kuba kubhaliwe kwathiwa: Iimpondo ezilishumi owazibonayo phezu kwerhamncwa, ezi ziya kumthiya umfazi olihenyukazi, zimshiye elubhacweni, eze, zidle inyama yakhe, zimtshise ngomlilo. Kuba uThixo uzifakile ezintliziyweni zabo ukuba benze ukuthanda kwakhe, bavumelane, banike irhamncwa ubukumkani babo, ade azaliseke amazwi kaThixo. Waza umfazi, owambonayo, nguloo mzi mkhulu ulawula ookumkani bomhlaba.

Wevani amazwi am, umgwebo weBhabheli eyimfihlakalo sele uqalile. Iimpondo ezilishumi zilungiselele uhlaselo lwazo olothusayo kwaye uhlaselo lwazo luyasondela kwaye lusemnyango wenu. Kuba namajelo enu eendaba afuna ukunilalisa buthongo kwaye abaninzi bahleli ebumnyameni bengayiboni inyani besithi: Ndihleli njengoKumkanikazi kwaye andinguye umhlolokazi kwaye andiyi kubona zila. Ingomso liya kuba njenganamhlanje, kuba namhlanje kunjengayizolo. Kangangoko ezukisa yena, ahlale eyoliswa, mnike kangako intuthumbo nosizi: Ngako oko izibetho zakhe ziya kufika ngamini-nye, ukufa, nosizi, nendlala, kwaye uya kutshiswa ngomlilo: kuba uYahweh omgxekayo, nguThixo uSomandla kwaye akukho mkhulu kwaye akukho nto inokunqanda isandla esinamandla sika-El Shaddai!

Kwaye ke ilanga liya kuphuma kungekudala ngosuku lwentlekele eningayilindelanga, kwaye olo suku lusondele kunokuba nicinga. Kwaye olo suku luya kuzisa intshabalalo epheleleyo kwiBhabheli eyimfihlakalo, kuba imiselwe isahlulo esiphindwe kabini somgwebo. Izono zakhe nobuhenyu bakhe buninzi kwaye isohlwayo sakhe asilibazisi kwaye isohlwayo esikhulu siya kuwela phezu kwabangaguqukiyo kanye njengoko iBandla lam elithembekileyo kunye noMtshakazi wam othembekileyo baya kuqokelelwa kum ngequbuliso emafini kwaye basindiswe kwingqumbo, kwanenkathazo enkulu yeveki yamashumi asixhenxe kaDaniyeli. IBhabheli eNkulu iwile, iwile! Wevani amazwi am kuba ndithetha ngokungathi sele kwenzekile kuba akukho nto iya kutshintsha, icothise, ithintele, okanye imise imigwebo kaYahweh ekuweni emhlabeni.

Ngeyure enye iya kutshiswa ngomlilo, ezinye zezixeko zayo ziya kutsha, ezinye zezixeko zayo ziya kukhukuliswa ngogonyamelo, iinqaba zayo ziya kuwa zaphuke zibe ziziqwenga ezininzi, iibhulorho zayo ziya kuwa, kwaye kuya kubakho ukushukuma okukhulu. Umhlaba uya kukrazulwa ngogonyamelo kwaye umhlaba omninzi uya kugutyungelwa ngamanzi anogonyamelo. Iziketi zakhe ziya kugutyungelwa ukusuka empuma nasentshona ngamaza amakhulu kwaye phezulu embindini kuqhube amaza nokushukuma okunogonyamelo. Oko kuseleyo kuya kuba yinkqantosi kwaye akasayi kuphinda avuke, kuba esi sizwe samazwe amaninzi nezixeko ezininzi asisayi kuphinda sivuke kwaye kuya kuba kukhulu ukuwa kwaso! Kuba umhlaba yoweNkosi nokuzala kwawo, kwaye zonke iinkalo zempembelelo yawo embi ziya kucinywa ngonaphakade ezizweni ngexesha leveki yamashumi asixhenxe kaDaniyeli, kuba ndiya kuwucoca umhlaba ngengqumbo yam. Mna, Krestu Yesu, iNgonyama yesizwe sakwaYuda, ndiwathethile la mazwi, kwaye amalungiselelo okuzaliseka kwawo sele eqalile kwaye aya kuzaliseka ngequbuliso ngeyure enye.

Kwaye ke imfazwe iya kuqhambuka kuwo wonke umhlaba njengenxalenye yohlaselo olothusayo kwiBhabheli eyimfihlakalo, kuba oku kucwangcisiwe kwaye kulungelelaniswe iminyaka emininzi ngoku, kuba imfihlelo yobugwenxa ibisebenza ukusukela kwiintsuku zakudala kwaye iya kufikelela ekugqityweni okupheleleyo xa umthinteli esusiwe njengoko abathembekileyo bam bethathwa basiwa emazulwini. Imfazwe iya kuwela kuwo wonke umhlaba kwaye iya kufikela bonke abantu nazo zonke iintlanga, kuba le yimfazwe yesithathu enjalo, njengoko abawileyo befuna ukutshabalalisa iindlela ezindala zehlabathi kwaye batshintshe zonke izinto ngolungelelwano lwabo olutsha, kwanolungelelwano lweNamba. Irhamncwa eliphuma elwandle nerhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni liya kuvuka ngomsindo omkhulu, kwaye liya kulwa nabangcwele, kuba kunikwe lona ukuba loyise abangcwele. Kuya kuba njalo ke ukuba bonke abangabathandi ubomi babo babize egameni leNkosi kude kube sekupheleni baya kusindiselwa kuvuyo olungunaphakade kude kube ngonaphakade kanaphakade. Kwaye mna Yesu waseNazarete ndishiye umzekelo ongunaphakade wokuthobela kude kube sekufeni, kuba ukuthobela kwam uYahweh kwakukukufa komnqamlezo njengoko ndabandezeleka ndaza ndahlawulela izono zoluntu njengemvana kaThixo kwaye yabonani ndiyeza njengeNgonyama yesizwe sakwaYuda kwaye andiyi kunqandwa okanye ndithintelwe!

Kwaye ke abaprofeti bam ababini abayimithi yomnquma emibini nezibane ezibini ezimi phambi koThixo womhlaba baya kuprofeta iinyanga ezingama-42 bembathiswe iingubo ezirhwexayo, kuba bathathwa basiwa emazulwini ngamaxesha akudala. Kwaye ukuba kukho umntu oya kubenzakalisa, umlilo uphuma emlonyeni wabo, utshabalalise iintshaba zabo: kwaye ukuba kukho umntu oya kubenzakalisa, kufuneka abulawe ngale ndlela. Aba banamandla okuvala izulu, banqande imvula ngeentsuku zokuprofeta kwabo: kwaye banamandla phezu kwamanzi ukuwajika abe ligazi, nokubetha umhlaba ngazo zonke izibetho, rhoqo njengoko bethanda.

Obabo bubungqina obungunaphakade, kwaye baya kuvakalisa ivangeli engunaphakade, kwaye baya kuthetha ngezicwangciso zikaYahweh kuwo onke amaxesha, kwaye baya kundithethela mna Krestu Yesu, kwaye iimfihlelo ezininzi baya kuzityhila kwaye baya kuguqula iintliziyo zabaninzi zibuyele kuYise wezikhanyiso ongenaguquguquko nasithunzi sokujika. Baya kungqina ngam mna Yeshua HaMashiach kuba ndim lowo uYahweh amthumele kwaSirayeli ukuze azalisekise izithembiso ezenziwa kooyise kwaye ndinguye uMesiya wokwenene ngoku nangonaphakade kwaye ubukumkani bam abunakuphela! Kuba ngawaphi amaxesha apho uYahweh azishiye engenabungqina? AKUKHO xesha uYahweh azishiye engenabungqina, kwaye bonke abamiselwe usindiso olungunaphakade baya kusindiselwa kubomi obungunaphakade novuyo olungunaphakade, kuba ukuzaliseka kwazo zonke izinto sele kuqalile.

Kwaye ke irhamncwa eliphuma elwandle liya kuvuka kunye nerhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni, elikwangumprofeti wobuxoki. Kwaye liya kuvula umlomo walo lithethe izinyeliso ezikhulu ngokuchasene noYahweh ukunyelisa igama lakhe nomnquba wakhe nabo bahlala ezulwini. Kwaye kuya kunikwa lona ukuba lilwe nabangcwele kwaye liboyise kwaye liya kunikwa amandla okulawula yonke imizalwana, nolwimi, nohlanga kuba liya kukhokela ihlabathi. Irhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni liya kulawula umhlaba njengoMoshiach wabo wobuxoki kwaye liya kuba ngumakhi wetempile yabo. Irhamncwa eliphuma elwandle liya kulawula ngokwemithetho yabo yobuNoahide enyelisayo njengoko befuna ukuphelisa nantoni na enento yokwenza noThixo wokwenene nonyana wakhe okuphela kwamzeleyo uYesu Krestu ebusweni bomhlaba.

Irhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni liya kubangela ukuba umhlaba unqule irhamncwa, elalinexeba elibulalayo elaphiliswayo. Kwaye liya kwenza imimangaliso emikhulu ukukhohlisa abo bahlala emhlabeni, kuba liya kuvunyelwa ukuba lenze imiqondiso emininzi yobuxoki nemimangaliso, kwaye liya kubangela ukuba umlilo uhle emhlabeni emehlweni abantu kwaye liya kukhokelela bonke abantu ukuba bamangaliswe ngabo. Kodwa lingumchasi, kwaye liyaziphakamisa ngokuchasene nako konke okubizwa ngokuba nguThixo, okanye okunqulwayo: ukuze lihlale njengoThixo eTempileni kaThixo, lizibonakalisa ukuba linguThixo.

Kwaye liya kwenza umfanekiso werhamncwa kwaye liya kubangela ukuba bonke banqule umfanekiso eliya kuwenza. Kwaye liya kuvunyelwa ukuba linike umoya kumfanekiso werhamncwa ukuze umfanekiso werhamncwa uthethe kwaye uya kubangela ukuba bonke abangawunquliyo umfanekiso werhamncwa babulawe. Kwaye liya kwenza bonke, abazizityebi nabangamahlwempu, abakhululekileyo namakhoboka, ukuba bamkele uphawu lwalo esandleni sabo sasekunene okanye emabunzini abo.

Kwaye ke ndiphinda ndithi kwabo bahlala emhlabeni ukuze bonke beve:

UKUBA KUKHO UMNTU ONQULA IRHAMNCWA NOMFANEKISO WALO, AMKELE UPHAWU LWALO EBUNZINI LAKHE, OKANYE ESANDLENI SAKHE, YENA UYA KUSELA KWIWAYINI YENGQUMBO KATHIXO, ETHULULWE INGENAMXUBE ENDEBENI YOMSIDO WAKHE, KWAYE UYA KUTHUTHUNJISWA NGOMLILO NESULUFURE, PHAMBI KWEENGELOSI EZINGCWELE, NAPHAMBI KWEMVANA: KWAYE UMSI WENTUTHUMBO YABO UNYUKA NGONAPHAKADE KANAPHAKADE. KWAYE ABANAKUPHUMULA EMINI NASEBUSUKU, ABO BANQULA IRHAMNCWA NOMFANEKISO WALO, NAYE NABANI NA OWAMKELA UPHAWU LWEGAMA LALO.

Ilihlabathi elikhoyo ngoku aliyi kuphinda libekho, kwaye ubukumkani obusakhulayo beNamba buya kugungxulwa ngokupheleleyo, njengoko ubukumkani bukaSathana businga esiphelweni sabo sokugqibela, kuba bangabahlali-mthethweni kwaye abangookumkani. Kwaye eli lihlabathi abaninzi abaya kuzifumana bekulo, nkqu nabo basindayo kwiintlekele ezizayo nemfazwe. Musani ukulwamkela uphawu bantu bam, kuba baya kuza beninika uxolo, baya kuza beninika ukhuseleko, baya kunixelela ukuba konke kungenxa yokulunga kwenu. Baya kusungula oko bakubiza ngokuba ngubhubhane kuba sele becebile ukweyisela ihlabathi ukuba liyagula. Kwaye zonke iindidi zezifo nezibetho ziya kwanda kuba zinjalo iziphumo zomkhweli-hashe ozayo kwaye baya kukhwela!

Baya kunikela ngempilo nokuphiliswa kwizigwebo zikaThixo, endaweni yokuba umntu aguquke ebubini bakhe. Baya kunikela ngokungeniswa kuluntu lwabo kwizigwebo ezoyikekayo eziza kuqhambuka emhlabeni. Kuba mna Krestu Yesu ndiya kunixelela ngaphambi kokuba kwenzeke ukuze niyazi inyaniso yamazwi am kwaye nikwazi ukuhamba kumanzi anobungozi apho abaninzi baya kuzifumana bekubo, nkqu nabo banesibindi sokubiza egameni lam kude kube sekupheleni, kuba bona baziindlalifa zosindiso olungunaphakade. Kuba bonke ababiza kuYesu waseNazarete njengeNkosi yabo, nkqu nabo bonke ababiza egameni lam elinamandla kwaye bengabathandi ubomi babo kude kube sekupheleni, baya kusindiselwa kubomi obungunaphakade.

Anifundanga na? Aba ngabo baphuma embandezelweni enkulu, bazihlamba iingubo zabo, bazenza mhlophe egazini leMvana. Ngenxa yoko, baphambi kwetrone kaThixo, kwaye bamkhonza imini nobusuku eTempileni yakhe: kwaye lowo uhleli eTroneni uya kuhlala phakathi kwabo. Abayi kuphinda balambe, abayi kuphinda banxanwe, nelanga aliyi kubatshisa, nabuphi na ubushushu. Kuba iMvana, ephakathi kwetrone, iya kubondla, kwaye iya kubakhokelela kwimithombo ephilayo yamanzi: kwaye uThixo uya kosula zonke iinyembezi emehlweni abo.

Nangona kunjalo, nangona isihlwele sezihlwele siya kusindiswa kwiveki yamashumi asixhenxe kaDaniyeli kwaye usindiso luya kuza kwaSirayeli kuba intsalela isaya kuguqa kum, Yeshua HaMashiach, (Kuba ndinguye uMesiya wokwenene apho imvana kaThixo iya kunika indlela kwiNgonyama yeSizwe sakwaYuda, kwaye zombini ziya kuzalisekiswa ngemvisiswano engunaphakade kum, kuba kum Krestu Yesu iNgonyama iya kulala phantsi ngoxolo nemvana.) okulusizi, kwaye ngokungeyomfuneko, uninzi lwehlabathi luya kumangaliswa kwaye lulandele aba balawuli banjengezilwanyana, kuba baya kuthakatha abaninzi. Ngobugqwirha babo nokuthakatha kwabo, baya kuhenda ihlabathi ukuba lamkele ityhefu yabo, kwaye baya kubatyhefa ngonaphakade abo bathanda ihlabathi ngobugqi babo, kuba uphawu lwerhamncwa luya kuzifihla njengeyeza. Nkqu nokulunywa yinyoka apho inkqubo yabo sele isunguliwe kwaye yavavanywa, kwaye izihlwele sele ziqheliselwe ukwamkela uphawu lwabo kwaye umsebenzi wabo wembewu yemfazwe waba yimpumelelo.

Kwaye ke inkqubo yabo ilungele ixesha eliphambili, kwaye balungiselele amanqaku abo oluntu kwaye bonke abahlala ezixekweni zabo nakuluntu lwabo baya kulawulwa. Kuba kuya kufuneka bathathe uphawu lwerhamncwa ukuze bathathe inxaxheba kuluntu lwabo lobusathana, nkqu nobukhosi beNamba. Abo bathatha uphawu baya kutshintshwa ngonaphakade. Imfuzo yabo kanye iya kutshintshwa, kuba baya kuzixuba nembewu yabantu: kodwa abayi kunamathelana, njengoko intsimbi ingaxutywa nodongwe. Uphawu lwabo olunjengerhamncwa, nkqu nokulunywa yinyoka, luya kubatshabalalisa ngokupheleleyo bonke abathatha ityhefu yabo kwaye akukho kusinda.

Nkqu nabo bonke abathatha uphawu baya kutshintshwa ngonaphakade, kwaye baya kuzifumana beyinxalenye yenkqubo yabo, kwaye baya kudityaniswa bubuchwepheshe kwaye iingqondo zabo aziyi kuphinda zibe zezabo. Baya kulawulwa kwaye baya kuba njengabahambi abafileyo, baya kuba ngamakhoboka ngonaphakade kwingqondo edibeneyo yenkqubo yabo, nkqu nobuchwepheshe babo kuba baya kudityaniswa ne-intanethi yezinto ezizayo. Baya kuphulukana nokuzikhethela kwabo, kwaye abayi kuba nazimvakalelo kwaye nantoni na kaThixo iya kucinywa ngonaphakade kubo kuba baya kuzitshintsha babe kwimeko engenakuhlangulwa, kwaye baya kuzidibanisa ngokupheleleyo nangonaphakade nabawileyo kwaye ngonaphakade baya kuba ngabenamba kwaye baya kuya kwichibi elikhulu lomlilo ngonaphakade. Kwaye kuya kuba njalo isiphelo abazikhethele sona.

Kuba isigqibo sabo sokugqibela asinakurhoxiswa. Kwaye izibetho zabo nezilonda nezifo zabo ziya kuba ninzi, kwaye nangona baya kuthenjiswa impilo noxolo, impilo noxolo abayi kuze babe nazo. Kuba uSathana uzama ukusebenzisa ubugqi bakhe ukulwa ingqumbo nezigwebo zikaThixo nokutyhefa nokubulala izihlwele, kodwa abayi kuba nasiqabu kwaye abo bathatha inxaxheba kubugqi bakhe abayi kuze basinde. Abo bathatha uphawu baya kukhanya phantsi kwezibane zabo ezimnyama, kwaye izibane zabo ziya kulayina izitrato zezixeko zabo abazilungiselele amakhoboka abo. Qonda ukuba aba baya kuba ngabahambi abaqalekisiweyo abangenaxolelo nabangenakuze baphile, kuba aba ngabo amagama abo angabhalwanga eNcwadini yoBomi yeMvana, exhelweyo kwasekusekweni kwehlabathi. Kwaye akukho sindiso luya kuze lufunyanwe phakathi kwabo okanye kuluntu lwabo, kuba ukuhamba phakathi kwabo nokujoyina uluntu lwabo kuya kufuna ngokupheleleyo uphawu lwerhamncwa.

Amangqina am amabini aya kungqina de amaxesha obungqina bawo agqitywe kuba kunikwe iinyanga ezingama-42. Kwaye kuya kunikwa irhamncwa elinyuka liphuma enzonzobileni ukuba lilwe nabo, kwaye liya kuboyisa, libabulale, nkqu nemithi yam yeminquma emibini, nezibane ezibini xa begqibile ubungqina babo. Kwaye izidumbu zabo ziya kulala esitratweni sesixeko esikhulu, esithi ngokomoya sibizwe ngokuba yiSodom neYiputa, apho neNkosi yenu yabethelelwa khona. Kwaye abangendawo baya kwenza umbhiyozo omkhulu kwaye banikane izipho ngenxa yokufa kwabo. Kwaye emva kweentsuku ezintathu enesiqingatha uMoya wobomi ovela kuThixo uya kungena kubo: kwaye baya kuma ngeenyawo zabo, kwaye uloyiko olukhulu luya kuwela phezu kwabo bonke abababonayo. Kwaye beva ilizwi elikhulu livela ezulwini, lisithi kubo, nyukelani apha. Kwaye banyukela ezulwini ngefu, kwaye iintshaba zabo ziya kubabona benyukela emazulwini kubomi obungunaphakade kwaye ngonaphakade baya kutshintshwa. Kwaye iNgelosi yesixhenxe iya kuvakala, kwaye kuya kubakho amazwi amakhulu ezulwini, esithi, Ubukumkani beli hlabathi bube bubukumkani beNkosi yethu, noKrestu wayo, kwaye uya kulawula ngonaphakade kanaphakade.

(Banoyolo abafileyo abafela eNkosini, ukususela ngoku, ewe, utsho uMoya, ukuze baphumle emisebenzini yabo, kwaye imisebenzi yabo iyabalandela.)

Kwaye ke imbandezelo enkulu kunayo yonke iya kuqala, nangona imbandezelo ihleli ikhona ngexesha leveki yamashumi asixhenxe kaDaniyeli. Kwaye itempile yabo iya kugqitywa kwaye isikizi lesiphanziso liya kuzalisekiswa nguMoshiach wabo wobuxoki. Kwaye uya kuqinisekisa umnqophiso nabaninzi iveki enye: kwaye phakathi evekini uya kubangela ukuba umbingelelo nomnikelo uphele, kwaye ngenxa yokunwenwa kwezikizi, uya kuyenza ibe yintlango, kude kube sekuphelisweni okumiselweyo kuya kuthululwa phezu kwentlango.

Aya kuba ngawona maxesha amnyama bantwana bam, kuba mna Yeshua HaMashiach ndiyanixelela ezi zinto ngaphambi kokuba zenzeke, kwaye zibhaliwe njengesilumkiso kubo bonke. Kwaye ke abam abali-144,000 baya kuza kunceda abo ngebabhubhile nababiza egameni lam. Ndiya kubakhokela inyathelo nenyathelo, kwaye baya kundenzela imisebenzi emininzi enamandla. Kuba aba ngabo bathathwayo ekuhlanganisweni kwabangcwele kum, Krestu Yesu, emoyeni ekukhaleni kwexilongo lokugqibela kwaye ixilongo sele liza kukhala.

Aba ngabathembekileyo bam, nkqu nabo kungafunyanwanga buqhinga kubo, kuba bazihlambulule njengentombi enyulu egazini leMvana endalinikela ngonaphakade njengombingeleli wabo omkhulu, kuba ndingumbingeleli omkhulu ngonaphakade ngokohlobo lukaMelkitsedeke. Aba ngumtshakazi wam, kwaye baya kundilandela naphi na apho ndiya khona. Yabonani! Ndiya kubenza babe namandla, kwaye akukho nto yakha yabonwa emhlabeni enokuthelekiswa nezakhono endiya kubambathisa ngazo. Baya kwambathiswa ngelinen ecikizekileyo, emhlophe, engenabala kwaye izakhono zabo ziya kubonakalisa impumelelo yabo kwaye baya kuba nezakhono ezikhulu kuba bangabathembekileyo ababiziweyo nabakhethiweyo nkqu nangaphambi kokusekwa kwehlabathi nguYahweh ngezi njongo.

Abam abali-144,000 baya kuhla eNtabeni iZiyon kwaye ndiya kubakhokela. Baya kuthatha abo ndibakhokelela kubo baye entlango ukuze bafihlwe kubumnyama obupheleleyo nentshutshiso yobukumkani beentshaba. Baya kukhokelelwa kukhuseleko kwizibetho ezisixhenxe kuba kwezi zigwebo kukho ubunzima bengqumbo kaThixo eza kuthululwa esihlalweni serhamncwa nokuthuthumbisa ubukumkani benamba kuba sikhulu isohlwayo sabo. Kwaye abali-144,000 baya kubakhokelela kukhuseleko, baya kubaphilisa kumanxeba abo, baya kubanceda kwaye babanike ithemba elikhulu lokubiza egameni leNkosi kude kube sekupheleni ukuba kuyimfuneko. Kuba ukuba bendingazifinyezanga iintsuku, ngekungasindanga nyama. Kwaye ke baya kugqibezela oko ndiya kubakhokelela kuko.

Kwaye xa amaxesha ezalisekile kuya kuba njalo, kanye njengoko kubhaliwe kwaye njengoko kubhaliwe makwenzeke: Kwaye ndabona izulu livulekile, kwaye yabona ihashe elimhlophe, kwaye lowo wayehleli phezu kwalo wayebizwa ngokuba nguThembekile noNyaniso, kwaye ngobulungisa uyagweba kwaye enze imfazwe. Amehlo akhe ayenjengelangatye lomlilo, kwaye entloko yakhe kwakukho izithsaba ezininzi, kwaye wayenegama elibhaliweyo, elalingaziwa mntu ngaphandle kwakhe. Kwaye wayambethe isambatho esifakwe egazini, kwaye igama lakhe libizwa ngokuba, NguLizwi likaThixo. Kwaye imikhosi eyayisezulwini yamlandela ikhwele amahashe amhlophe, yambethe ilinen ecikizekileyo, emhlophe necocekileyo. Kwaye emlonyeni wakhe kuphuma ikrele elibukhali, ukuze ngalo abethe iintlanga: kwaye uya kuzilawula ngentonga yentsimbi: kwaye unyathela isixovulelo sewayini sobushushu nengqumbo kaThixo uSomandla. Kwaye unesambatho sakhe, nasethangeni lakhe igama elibhaliweyo, UKUMKANI WOOKUMKANI, NENKOSI YEENKOSI.

Kwaye ke mna, nkqu noYesu waseNazarete, nkqu neMvana kaThixo, nkqu noKrestu Yesu okwayiNgonyama yeSizwe sakwaYuda ndiya kuza kugweba ndenze imfazwe neNamba kwaye bonke ubukumkani bayo buya kutyunyuzwa ngokupheleleyo bungaphindi buvuke. Kwaye irhamncwa nomprofeti wobuxoki baya kuphoswa bephila echibini lomlilo kwaye uSathana uya kubotshwa ngekhonkco elikhulu enzonzobileni iminyaka eliwaka kwaye ke ulawulo lwam lweminyaka eliwaka luya kuqala kwaye ulawulo lwam luya kuba sebubulungiseni. Yabonani! Ndizisa umtshakazi wam nomkhosi wookumkani nababingeleli endiya kulawula nabo umhlaba ngobulungisa kwaye ndiya kulawula iintlanga ngentonga yentsimbi. Ngoko yibani nethemba elikhulu bantwana bam kuba ukupheliswa kwezinto zonke kusondele kwaye eli xesha leminyaka esixhenxe yenkathazo enkulu, nkqu nenkathazo kaYakobi liya kulandelwa lulawulo lwam olulungileyo kwaye uYahweh uya kuba yinto yonke kuyo yonke into.

Kwaye ndabona izulu elitsha, nomhlaba omtsha: kuba izulu lokuqala, nomhlaba wokuqala udlule, kwaye akwabikho lwandle. Kwaye mna Yohane ndabona isiXeko esingcwele, iYerusalem entsha isihla ivela kuThixo ezulwini, ilungiswe njengomtshakazi ohlotshiselwe umyeni wakhe. Kwaye ndeva ilizwi elikhulu livela ezulwini, lisithi, Yabonani, uMnquba kaThixo unabantu, kwaye uya kuhlala nabo, kwaye baya kuba ngabantu bakhe, kwaye uThixo ngokwakhe uya kuba nabo, abe nguThixo wabo.
