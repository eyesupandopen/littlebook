Ingcaciso “Yemisebenzi Ka”

Ubungqina BukaYesu Bungumoya Wesiprofeto

Yabonani, ndinityhilela imfihlelo yencwadana. Kuba incwadi inikiwe, kwaye umoya wesiprofeto bubungqina bukaYesu. Ubungqina bam ngoYesu baqala ebomini bam xa iNkosi uYesu Krestu yandibizela kubulungiseleli nenkonzo ngowe-1996 ndisahlala kwisiXeko saseNew York. Ndandisabele ubizo lweNkosi lokuguquka ngokuya kwisifundo seBhayibhile sasekhaya kwaye ndingumfundi kwenye yeeklasi zabo. Ngenye ingokuhlwa ndandihleli kwindawo yokutyela eLong Island City nomhlobo wam olungileyo, waza wandicela ukuba ndityhile iBhayibhile yam kuYohane 4, waza waqalisa ukufunda oku kulandelayo nam ndifunda kunye naye:

Yohane 4:35-38

Anitsho na nina ukuthi, Kusasele iinyanga zozine, kufike ukuvuna? Yabonani, ndithi kuni, Phakamisani amehlo enu, nikhangelè amasimi; ngokuba asele emhlophe, alungele ukuvunwa.

Novunayo wamkela umvuzo, abuthele isiqhamo kubomi obungunaphakade; ukuze kùvuyisane ndawonye ohlwayelayo nonovunayo.

Kuba kulo liyinene elo lizwi lithi, Omnye uhlwayela, omnye avune.

Mna ndanithuma ukuvuna oko ningabulalekanga kuko; abanye babulaleka, naza nina nangena ekubulalekeni kwabo.

Njengoko umhlobo wam wayefunda ezi vesi, la mazwi abonakala ngathi ayatsiba ephuma ephepheni aza angena ngqo enzulwini yomphefumlo wam. Kwavutha umlilo entliziyweni yam, yaza ke iNkosi yandithuma enkonzweni yayo ngowe-1996. Ndabuyeka ngokoqobo bonke ubomi bam nayo yonke into endandiyenza ukuze ndingene kubulungiseleli. Ndaba yinkokeli kwicawa enkulu encinane, yaye kamva, xa ndafumanisa indlela ababekhohlakele ngayo ngokwenene, ndaphuma ndaza andizange ndibuyele ngowe-2008. Ndatsala nzima iminyaka embalwa njengoko ndandizama ukuhamba noKrestu, kodwa izono zam zandibambezela. Ngowe-2013 ubomi bam badilika ndaza ndazitshabalalisa, kwaye yayiyingozi kaloliwe ehamba kancinci eyayisele inethuba isenzeka. Yaza ke iNkosi yandithabatha yandohlwaya kakhulu yaza yandityakatya amaxesha amaninzi de ndabuyela ezingqondweni ngoJulayi 7, 2020, xa ndaguquka ngokupheleleyo ezonweni zam.

INkosi yandilungiselela olu hambo ngokundinika imibono emininzi, imiqondiso, imimangaliso, iimphiliso ezininzi, kwaye ndikhuphé oomoya abaninzi ngokomyalelo weNkosi, kwaye iNkosi indinike isiqinisekiso esikhulu sobunyaniso bobulungiseleli nobizo lwam. Ngaloo ndlela ndibuyiselwe eNkosini yam, ndibuyiselwe kuYahweh, ndibuyiselwe kumfazi wam nonyana wam nomvuzo wam ongunaphakade.

Ngaxesha lithile ekupheleni kowe-2023, kwenzeka into emangalisayo kum. Amaxesha amaninzi ndilala kudlala isibhalo kwiTV yam nakwisandisi-lizwi. Ngale ngokuhlwa, ndalala kudlala iTestamente eNdala kancinane kakhulu. Ndothuswa ndavuka ebuthongweni ezinzulwini zobusuku xa isandisi-lizwi sam, ebesithe cwaka, sakhwaza kakhulu kungaziwa isizathu. Akukho mntu wayenyusile ivolyum, kwaye iremote yayilele ingachukunyiswanga kwitafile yam esecaleni kwebhedi. Ezi vesi zilandelayo zikaHezekile zadlala ngoxa ndandihleli apho ndingakholelwa:

Hezekile 3:17-22

Nyana womntu, ndikwenze umlindi wendlu kaSirayeli; ke ngoko live ilizwi emlonyeni wam, ubaxelele isilumkiso esivela kum.

Ekuthini kwam kongendawo, Uya kufa ngenene; wena ke ungamlumkisi, ungathethi, umlumkise ongendawo ngendlela yakhe engendawo, ukuze aphile: yena ongendawo uya kufa ngobugwenxa bakhe; ke lona igazi lakhe ndiya kulibiza esandleni sakho.

Ke ukuba wena umlumkile ongendawo, aze angabuyi ebubini bakhe, nasendleleni yakhe embi, yena uya kufa ngobugwenxa bakhe; ke wena uwuhlangule umphefumlo wakho.

Kananjalo, xa ilungisa libuya ebulungiseni balo, lenze ubugqwetha, ndibeke isikhubekiso phambi kwalo, liya kufa: ngenxa yokuba ungamkhange umlumkise, uya kufa esonweni sakhe, nobulungisa bakhe abenzileyo abuyi kukhunjulwa; ke lona igazi lakhe ndiya kulibiza esandleni sakho.

Noko ke, ukuba wena umlumkile olilungisa, ukuze ilungisa lingoni, laza alona, liya kuphila ngenene, ngokuba lilumkisiwe; nawe uwuhlangule umphefumlo wakho.

Saba phezu kwam apho isandla sikaYehova.

Kwaye kunjalo kanye. Nangona ngelo xesha ndandingaqondi ukuba iNkosi indithuma njengomprofeti wexesha lokuphela, ndandiza kufumanisa kungekudala.

NgoMatshi 2, 2024, ndaqalisa ukuchwetheza imiyalezo yesiprofeto koko iNkosi yakuchaza kum njengemisebenzi kaYohane uMbhaptizi. Oku kwaqhubeka ukusukela ngoMatshi 2 ukuya ku-Epreli 5, 2024, kwaye ngelo xesha ndafumana imiyalezo yesiprofeto engama-20. Yonke imiyalezo yayijikeleze umgwebo kwiBhabhiloni eyiMfihlakalo, ibizela abangcwele enguqukweni, kwaye isazisa ukubonakala okukufuphi kukaYesu Krestu ngokwakhe, njengoko eza kubonakala emoyeni nangaliphi na ixesha ngoku kwabathembekileyo ebandleni lakhe. Eyona nto ndandiyicinga kukuba isiprofeto sango-Epreli 5 sesokugqibela. Ndandiphazama. Umyalezo olandelayo wesiprofeto wenzeka ngo-Epreli 10, 2024, yaza iNkosi yandixelela ukuba indithuma emsebenzini kaPetros wokondla nokunyamekela umhlambi.

Uhlobo nomxholo wemiyalezo lwatshintsha ukusukela ngoko, nangona isenazo iinxalenye ezivela kwezingamashumi amabini zokuqala, kwaye uninzi lwale miyalezo luyadibana. Ngo-Epreli 21, 2024, uhlobo nomxholo wemiyalezo lwaphinda lwatshintsha, yaza iNkosi yandixelela ukuba indithuma emsebenzini wompostile uPawulos njengomphathi weemfihlelo zikaThixo. Ngaloo ndlela umxholo wemiyalezo waphinda watshintsha kwaye wakhelwe phezu kwemiyalezo yangaphambili kwaye uyadibana. Le miyalezo yaqhubeka ngendlela efanayo de kwangoMeyi 16, 2024, xa umxholo waphinda watshintsha. Ndaxelelwa ukuba ndithunyelwa emisebenzini kaYohane uMtyhili (igama endingazange ndilive okanye ndilisebenzise), kwaye ngoMeyi 16 zaqala ezo ziprofeto.

Ngaloo ndlela umsebenzi wezandla zeNkosi ubonakaliswa kubizo endilunikwe lona, kwaye indithume apho ndingabulalekanga khona; amanye amadoda abulaleka, ndaza mna ndangena ekubulalekeni kwawo. Asikokuba la madoda anamandla kaThixo akazange azigqibe iindlela zawo, kodwa kukuba le misebenzi iqhubekile ukususela kwakudala xa uYesu Krestu wayewayalela kwimisebenzi awayithumayo. Ezi zahlulo zazingaziwa kum xa ndaqala ukuprofeta, kwaye njengoko wandixelelayo, ndandingena emisebenzini yomntu ngamnye. Andizange ndiyenze loo ntlonipho de ndatyhilelwa nguYeshua.

Ubizo nobulungiseleli ebomini bam buzalisekisiwe. Amanye amadoda abulaleka, yaza yandithuma emisebenzini yawo, ndaza ndabulaleka apho. Ndithunyiwe emsebenzini kaYohane uMbhaptizi ukuba ndivakalise ukuza kweNkosi. Ndithunyiwe emsebenzini kaPetros ukuba ndaluse ndondle umhlambi. Ndithunyiwe emsebenzini wompostile uPawulos njengomphathi weeMfihlelo zikaThixo. Ndithunyiwe emsebenzini kaYohane uMtyhili ukuba ndibize abali-144,000 kwaye ndibonise iziganeko ezizayo.

Ngokwam, andifanelekanga ukuzithelekisa nayo nayiphi na kula madoda makhulu kaThixo kwaye andifanelekanga ngokwam ukuba ndithethe nombuliso olula kuye nabani na kuni. Kodwa iNkosi indenze ndafaneleka, kwaye ndithunyiwe kule misebenzi ngokufanayo. Bonke ubomi bam bukhokelele kule miyalezo, kwaye onke amazwi aya kufumana amehlo, iindlebe, neentliziyo zabo athunyelwe kubo. Ukuba iNkosi ifuna ndithethe ngakumbi, ndiya kwenjenjalo. Ngaloo ndlela ndiya kuyigqiba indlela yam nemisebenzi endiyinikiweyo ukuba ndiyigqibe. Kwaye ngale ngcaciso, umsebenzi wezandla notyikityo lweNkosi uYesu Krestu lutyhiliwe kule miyalezo yesiprofeto endiyinikiweyo. Ndiyigqibile imisebenzi andithume yona. Ngaloo ndlela ndilindele eNkosini.
