Kwabanyanisekileyo KuKrestu: Zilungiseleleni Kwaye Nizilungiselele Ukuba Nihlanganiswe, Umyeni Wenu Usondele!

Yivani, yivani iLizwi likaYeshua HaMashiach nithobe iintliziyo zenu ekuthobeleni isandi samaZwi am! Kunjalo ke, ukuba abaninzi kubantu bam bajikele ngasekunene kwaye abaninzi ngakumbi bajikele ngasekhohlo, bemka kuKrestu Yesu iNkosi yabo, kanye njengoko ndisiza kunihlanganisela kum ukuba nithe nahlala nithembekile kwilizwi lam ekuthobeleni konke amaZwi am. Kuba, inene, inene, ndiyiNkosi yenu yokwenene, uKumkani, uMbingeleli oMkhulu, noMesiya! Ewe, ndinibizela nonke ezingalweni zam ezinothando ukuba nihlanganiselwe kum ngesiquphe nangokungalindelekanga ngoku, nkqu nabo bonke abalindileyo ngokuthobela bekholwa ngokupheleleyo, kwaye ngakumbi bonke abo baye bawa kule nkohliso eyayijongwe nguHeylel Ben Shachar ukukhohlisa iintliziyo zabangenabuqili, nokutyumza ukulindela kwenu kobulungisa kwenkululeko elungileyo nepheleleyo, nkqu nenkululeko yenu epheleleyo eya kufikela abathembekileyo kuKrestu, ngesiquphe nangokungalindelekanga ngoku!

Nkqu namazwi obuxoki anikwe ngabaprofeti bobuxoki abamisela imihla, nkqu bethetha ubuxoki egameni likaYesu Krestu, nkqu bebanga ukuba bathetha endaweni yam! Kanti ke abawathethi amaZwi am, bengavelisi siqhamo sam, yaye abandazi, kwaye ndiya kuthi kubo bonke ababanga izinto ezinjalo ngobuxoki ngaloo mini inkulu: “Mkani kum nina benzi bobubi, andizanga ndinazi!” Kunjalo ke, ukuba abaninzi kwabathembekileyo kuKrestu abalibandla lam umzimba wam, nkqu nomtshakazi wam ngoku uhleli edanile, kanye njengoko uMyeni wakhe esondela njengoko kwabhengezwayo, kwavunywayo kwaza kwathenjiswa! Kanti ke abaninzi kuni ngoku bahleli cwaka ngokungathi ukuhlangana okukhulu kwabathembekileyo kuKrestu kunokucinywa, okanye kurhoxiswe nguHeylel Ben Shachar, kuba inene, inene, nguye owacwangcisa ngobuxoki ukumka kwabathembekileyo kuKrestu kwaye abaninzi kwabathembekileyo bam bawatya amazwi akhe obuxoki ngokubawa ngokungathi la mazwi obuxoki avela kuKrestu Yesu ngokwakhe! Yivani amaZwi am bandla lam mzimba wam nkqu nomtshakazi wam! Aniliziva ilizwi lam? Aniwazi amaZwi am? Kuba akuyi kudlula neyod enye, okanye isuntswana leyod de zonke izinto zizaliseke KANYE njengoko kubhaliwe kwathenjiswa.

Kanti ke kunjalo, ukuba abaninzi kuni ngoku bahleli betyhafile, neentliziyo ezaphukileyo ekungathobeleni kwenu, kangangokuba ngoku nihleli nityhafile niphantsi kwamazwi abagxeki nabahleki, abasigwebo sabo siya kubafikela ngesiquphe nangokungalindelekanga ngoku kwaye abaninzi, abaninzi kwaba baya kuwela esigwebeni sanaphakade, kwaye abasayi kuze banikwe iwonga lokunqula igama likaYesu de kube sekupheleni kwabo, kuba usindiso ngokufela ukholo lilungelo newonga elinikwa abo baziindlalifa zobomi obungunaphakade nabuya kufumana umvuzo omkhulu ngokuma kwabo, kwaye ndiya kubamkela kum ngonaphakade nangonaphakade eluthandweni lwam ngabo, kanti ke nguMtshakazi wam othembekileyo oya kuthi anconywe ngabo bonke ngaloo mini! Ngoko ke, yimani niqine Mtshakazi wam nizityabise ngamaZwi am, nkqu namaZwi eNdaba Ezilungileyo ezingunaPhakade endizinike emhlabeni ukuba ndinilungiselele zithandwa zam, ngoko ke bophelelani amaZwi obungqina bomthunywa wam nomkhonzi wam ngokuqinileyo ezintliziyweni zenu, nisazi ukuba la maZwi acocekileyo anikwa ngqo nguYahweh, ebusweni bukaKrestu Yesu, kwaye ndiyawakhulula ngokuqonda ngomyalelo wam njengoko ndiwatyhila ngokugqibeleleyo nangokuchanekileyo entliziyweni yomthunywa wam, nkqu nalapho ndiwahlwayele khona, nkqu agcinelwe ukuba akhululwe ngomyalelo wam, kwaye inene amaZwi am anikwe ngenyaniso yonke nangokuchaneka. Selah.

Inene, inene, njengoko kunjalo: UHeylel Ben Shachar, ngentiyo enkulu, ufuna ngenkuthalo ukubhukuqa ukholo lwabo bonke abanokholo olunokubhukuqwa ngamazwi akhe obuxoki neenkohliso zakhe zobuqhetseba, nkqu nabo bonke abanokholo olungekho ngokupheleleyo phezu kwelo litye lokwenene lesiseko, nkqu uKrestu Yesu, kodwa endaweni yoko bagxile ekungathobeleni kumazwi obuxoki abantu abanesono, abangamaziyo uKrestu Yesu bengandikhonzi nakancinane, kodwa endaweni yoko bekhonza uHeylel Ben Shachar oxokayo. Kwaye ngamazwi abo obuxoki neziqhamo zokungalungisi nifanele nibazi; kodwa abaninzi kwabathembekileyo bam baseneendlebe ezirhawuzelayo eninqwenela ukuba zikilitwe ngamazwi obuxoki nawokuthomalalisa okungekuko, nkqu namazwi obuxoki uKrestu Yesu angawathumanga, engawaxhasi, kwaye andisayi kuze ndithume nelinye ilizwi lobuxoki eliphikisana nezibhalo zam eziNgcwele, kwaye andisayi kuze ndithethe nelinye ilizwi elichasene nesibhalo esele simisiwe endisinikileyo, kuba inene, inene, ndiliLizwi likaYHVH, uMkhulu onguMNA ndenziwe inyama, kwaye ndenziwe umoya ophilisa, kwaye andisayi kuze ndiziphikise nakancinane, nangona bexoka. Ngoko ke, ndiyanilawula ngegunya lonke ukuba nibambe amaZwi am ngokuqinileyo ezintliziyweni zenu ngokuzinikela okutsha okunetyuwa ukuze nizibuyise ekungathembekini kwenu, nkqu nokuba nonke nina enikholelwe kula mazwi obuxoki niguquke niwavume kum, ukuze ndinihlambulule ngokupheleleyo egazini lam lentlawulelo kwaye nilungiselelwe ngokupheleleyo ukubonakala kwam okufikayo kuni ngesiquphe nangokungalindelekanga ngoku, kanye ngeli xesha lonke ihlabathi lirhoxa kuKrestu Yesu, obengabasindisa ngokugqithisileyo. Kanye njengoko berhoxa ngokuzinikela ngokupheleleyo kumfanekiso womfel'ukholo wobuxoki owenzelwe waza wanikwa ukuphawula abo baya kufumana usindiso ngomlilo wocikido, kuba esi sisiqalo semfazwe nxamnye nabangcwele esele iza kukhululwa ngokupheleleyo ukuze kusindiswe abangathobeliyo abasakhotyokiswe sisono, ukuze inyama iwele entshabalalweni, ukuze inene umoya usindiswe, Selah.

Inene inene, ndinguKrestu Yesu, nkqu uYeshua HaMashiach, kwaye ndiza kuthomalalisa amanxeba abangathobeliyo phakathi kwabathembekileyo bam, nkqu nabo bonke abaya kuzivumela ukuba bajikwe kumaZwi am eNdaba Ezilungileyo ezingunaPhakade, nkqu namaZwi anikwe ngabapostile bam nabaprofeti ekuqaleni kwesi sikhathi sobabalo, nkqu namaZwi okwenene awatyhilayo iimfihlelo zam ngamaxesha azo okutyhilwa, nkqu namaZwi ayinyaniso nanamhlanje njengoko ayenjalo xa aqala ukunikwa ndim ezindlebeni zabo babekhona, kodwa ngakumbi abafundi bam apho kwavela khona abapostile bam: Lumkani: lindani, nithandaze: kuba anilazi ixesha.

Kanti ke abaninzi kuni bayawavumela amazwi obuxoki aphikisana namaZwi am okwenene esithembiso, nkqu namazwi enzelwe ukubhukuqa ukholo lwenu ngokwakha ulindelo lobuxoki kumhla endingawunikelanga, nkqu nolo lindelo luthi ke lutyunyuzwe ngenjongo yokunitshonisa kulindelo olungazalisekanga ngabo banikhathazayo, nkqu nabo bonke abeza kuni ngokunganyaniseki okukhulu benika ubuxoki obuchasene nesibhalo, nkqu namazwi obuxoki anikwe ngabo bathetha ngokuchasene namaZwi am, nkqu nabo baprofeta ngobuxoki egameni lam.

Kanti ke, abaninzi phakathi kwebandla lam umzimba wam nkqu nomtshakazi wam bayaqhubeka bevumela aba balusi bobuxoki ukuba babhukuqe ulindelo lwabo lokuba uMyeni wabo usondela ngomhla abazigqibela wona, nkqu nesiprofeto sobuxoki esichasene noko sele kubhengeziwe nguYHVH, uMkhulu onguMNA, kwaye nkqu nanguKrestu Yesu ngomyalelo ka-Adonai-YHVH kuba ndisoloko ndisenza ukuthanda kwalowo wandithumayo. Kuba ndiliLizwi elenziwe inyama elihlala ngokwenene phakathi kwenu nkqu nakuni ngaloo moya ungcwele wobunyana, ozalisa ngokupheleleyo izibane zeHlabathi ezambathiswe ngamandla avela phezulu! Sekunjalo abaninzi kuni ngokuzithandela bakhetha ukuhlala ebumnyameni nkqu bengawahoyi wona kanye amaZwi anokucima iintolo ezivuthayo zomngendawo, eninzi yenu ngokobuyatha enizikhethelayo. Inene, inene, ndingulowo uhleli ephethe isifutho esandleni nkqu evuselela amadangatye okuhlanjululwa, ukuze inene inene, nonke nilungiselelwe ukubonakala kweNkosi yenu, kuba ndiyeza kuni Zithandwa zam, kwaye ngubani okhoyo onokundinqanda? Ndabuza, ndabuza kwakhona, NDITHI NGUBANI!

Kanti ke, kunjalo. Ukuba uMtshakazi wam ngoku uhleli edakumbile edidekile, nkqu ebambekile kumazwi abo banyotyozayo nabakhamisayo! Kwaye ke ndiyanilawula ukuba niwale la mazwi obuxoki, nkqu nabo bonke abaya kubeka ukholo lwabo nethemba labo kumagqwirha obuxoki anyotyozayo nakhamisayo nathetha ngokuchasene namaZwi am obomi, kuba nditshilo ngokucacileyo esibhalweni, kwaye isibhalo asinakwaphulwa: Ukuba akukho mntu emhlabeni owazi usuku, ixesha, okanye iyure yokuhlangana okukhulu kwabathembekileyo kuKrestu okumiselwe ukwenzeka ngesiquphe nangokungalindelekanga ngoku. Kwaye ndiniyalile nonke ukuba nigcine ulindelo lweentliziyo zenu neengqondo zenu zigxile ngokupheleleyo kwiNkosi yenu engunaPhakade yoBulungisa esondela ngokuqinisekileyo kuni Zithandwa zam, nkqu nazo zonke iintombi zam ezinyulu ezizilungisileyo iintambo zezibane zazo, zaza zazalisa izibane zazo ngeoli ecocekileyo yomoya wam, ukuze inene inene nilungiselelwe ukubonakala koMyeni wenu kwaye nilungiselelwe ngokupheleleyo nilindile nilungile kunye neendwendwe zethu. Nkqu nabo bonke ababiziweyo, abanyuliweyo, nabamenyiweyo kuloo mtshato mkhulu woMyeni kumtshakazi wakhe, nkqu lona olo suku oluza kuni nonke ngesiquphe nangokungalindelekanga ngoku.

Ngoko kutheni uMtshakazi wam ehleli edakumbile elusizini ngokungathi uMyeni wenu unikreqile waza wanixabela ecaleni kwendlela, nikreqiwe kwaye neentliziyo zaphukile kulindelo lwenu? Hayi, bathembekileyo bam ezi zinto asizizo ezenu, kodwa ziya kunikwa njengesigwebo kwabangathembekanga, nabo bonke abasala isimemo seNkosi yabo engunaPhakade yoBulungisa sokuzilungiselela ngokwemiyalelo yam, ekuthobeleni konke okunothando kwilizwi nemiyalelo yeNkosi yabo!

Ngoko ke, ndiza kunikhumbuza kwakhona ngala maZwi anikwa kumaxesha amandulo kuBantwana beSithembiso, nkqu uSirayeli ngokwenyama ngamaxesha okutyhilwa kwabo, nkqu namaZwi amileyo unanamhlanje zithandwa zam, ngoko ke, yivani amaZwi eNkosi yenu namkele umyalelo wam ngobulali bonke, ukuthobeka, ukuthobeka kwentliziyo, nokulungela kwengqondo. Yivani nithobele: Kwaye ukuba ucinga entliziyweni yakho, Siya kulazi njani ilizwi angalithethanga uYehova? Xa uMprofeti ethetha ngeGama leNkosi, ukuba loo nto ayilandeli ingenzeki, yiyo loo nto angayithethanga uYehova, kodwa uMprofeti uyithethe ngokuzigwagwisa: ngoko ke akuyi kumoyika.

Kanti ke, abaninzi kuni baye bawalibala la maZwi anikwe njengemida nezikhuselo kwiingcuka eziphangayo, ezisigwebo sazo ngokuqinisekileyo singalibazisiyo. Ngoko ke, thelekisani ngenkuthalo amazwi akhe nabani na obanga ukuba uthetha endaweni kaKrestu Yesu, okwanguYeshua HaMashiach ngenkuthalo kumaZwi am amisiweyo anikwe kwaye akhuselwe kwizibhalo ezingcwele, nkqu namaZwi angcwele anikwe kumaxesha amandulo nguAdonai-YHVH, uMkhulu onguMNA, nkqu namaZwi angcwele anikwe kumaxesha angoku nguAdonai-YHVH noKrestu Yesu, okwanguYeshua HaMashiach.

Ngoko ke, Zithandwa zam, yivani amaZwi eNkosi yenu engunaphakade yoBulungisa, nkqu namaZwi anikwe ngezandla zam njengoko ndiwatyhila entliziyweni yomthunywa wam nomkhonzi wam onamandla kwaye ngezandla zam nangokuthanda kwam la maZwi ayatyhilwa ngokuqonda entliziyweni yomthunywa wam nomkhonzi wam onamandla apho mna, Krestu Yesu ndinika la maZwi entuthuzelo, nkqu namaZwi athuthuzelayo okuthuthuzela abathembekileyo kuKrestu, nkqu neentombi zam ezinyulu ezizilungiselele ngala maZwi anikwe ukuba akhululwe emhlabeni kuba ahlala ethembekile kuKrestu ekuthobeleni konke nkqu nokuba nonke nilungisiwe kwaye nilungiselelwe nilindile ecaleni kwendlela nezibane zenu zizaliswe, neentambo zenu zezibane zilungisiwe, nkqu nonke nina enithathe umlilo wam naza nazilayita izibane zenu ekuthobeleni kobulungisa kumaZwi am, kuba ndiyasondela kuni Zithandwa zam.

Kunjalo ke, uMyeni uyasondela kuMtshakazi wakhe kwaye umhlaba ngoku ngesiquphe uya kugutyungelwa bubumnyama obukhulu, kuba andiyi kwabelana ngobuqaqawuli bam nomnye, kwaye njengoko ubumnyama bobumnyama bugubungela umhlaba, ubuqaqawuli nokukhanya kokubonakala kwam kuya kukhanyisa umhlaba kanye njengoko umbane uphuma empuma usiya entshona, kwaye ngesiquphe nangokungalindelekanga ngoku iBandla lam, uMzimba wam, nkqu noMtshakazi wam uya kuzifumana emi ezulwini lamazulu, nkqu emi kwigumbi letrone likaYHVH, uMkhulu onguMNA, nkqu nomzuzwana wexesha uAdonal-YHVH awulindileyo kususela koonaphakade abadlulileyo, nkqu nexesha elide kangangokuba aninakuliqonda ngeli xesha. Kwaye nonke benifihliwe emandleni akhe nakwintliziyo yakhe emangalisayo yothando nokukhanya, nkqu nalapho wanigcina nikhuselekile kwimfihlelo yakhe enkulu kunazo zonke nkqu neMfihlelo eNkulu kaYahweh, uMkhulu onguMNA, kwaye inene inene amazulu alungisiwe kwaye sele evuya ngokufika kwenu ngesiquphe ngoku, nkqu nangaphambi kokuba nifike. Kuba nkqu neengelosi ezingcwele nezidalwa ezimangalisayo zamazulu zilindele ngolangazelelo ukubonakala kwenu nobukho benu Zithandwa zam.

Ngoko ke, phumzisani iingqondo neentliziyo zenu ngokupheleleyo eluthandweni lwam ngani bantwana bam, kwaye ningavumeli amazwi obuxoki anikwe ngabaprofeti bobuxoki abanyotyozayo nabakhamisayo nabamisela imihla yomtshato wam waseGalili ukuba abhukuqe ukholo lwenu, ninganenzi lusizi entliziyweni ngokungathi ndiya kumkreqa uMtshakazi wam othandekayo ozilungiselele ekuthobeleni okunothando kwilizwi loMyeni wakhe. Kwaye ke ndiyanilawula zithandwa zam ukuba nigxininise intliziyo yenu yonke, umphefumlo, ingqondo, namandla enu kumaZwi eNkosi yenu engunaPhakade yoBulungisa, nkqu namaZwi am angcwele esibhalo endiwatyhilileyo kuloo ncwadana ivuliweyo endiyivulileyo ezandleni zomthunywa wam nomkhonzi wam, kuba incwadana yesityhilelo isahluko se-10 iqulethe amazwi eendudumo ezisixhenxe kunye nevangeli engunaphakade ngokuyinxenye, kwaye la ngamaZwi anikwe ukukhokelela abantu bam kwiimvuno zembandezelo kaYakobi nkqu neveki yamashumi asixhenxe kaDaniyeli. Nkqu namaZwi atywinwe agcinwa eyimfihlo ngomyalelo kaYHVH, uMkhulu onguMNA, kuba inene, inene, amazwi nelizwi leendudumo ezisixhenxe liyathetha, kwaye ezi ndudumo zisixhenxe kukusebenza kwencwadi yesityhilelo, nkqu neveki yamashumi asixhenxe kaDaniyeli eya kukhokelela ekutshatyalalisweni okupheleleyo nokupheleleyo kobukumkani bukaHeylel Ben Shachar ngonaphakade, kwaye umhlaba uya kulawulwa ngoBulungisa, nkqu nobulungisa apho kungekho nto imbi iya kungena kubukumkani bam beminyaka eliwaka, ngoko phumla ngethemba elikhulu kula maZwi ethemba nesithembiso sanaphakade Zithandwa zam.

NdinguKrestu Yesu, kwaye ndiza kuzisa la maZwi okohlwaya, okukhalimela, nothando kwabathobelayo, nabangathobeliyo ukuze inene, inene, ningaweli kwicala elingathobeliyo lekrele lam lomoya, nkqu nokuba niya kubalelwa njengabafanelekileyo ukuba nihlanganiswe nabathembekileyo ngesiquphe nangokungalindelekanga ngoku. Kuba nkqu nakule mizuzu yokugqibela phambi kokuba eso sikhalo sinamandla sivakale, naphambi kokuba eso sixhobo sinamandla sivakale ngamandla nangokungalindelekanga ngoku, ndisashukuma kwaye ndibangele ukushukuma okukhulu kufike phezu kwabantu bam, nkqu nokuba ndingabashukumisa abangathembekanga ukuze babe nokholo ekuthobeleni kobulungisa, okanye bawele emlilweni wocikido ekungathobeleni kwabo, kanye phambi kwaloo mzuzu we-kairos udibana nexesha eliqhelekileyo eliza kunifumana nonke ngesiquphe nangokungalindelekanga ngoku. Kanye njengoko ihlabathi lililela umsindisi walo wobuxoki, nkqu nomfel'ukholo wobuxoki endingamthumanga, kodwa owanikwa nguBharabhas endaweni yoko, kanti ke abaninzi ngoku balandela lo mfel'ukholo wobuxoki, nkqu belahlekiswe kwilizwi likaKrestu Yesu okwanguYeshua HaMashiach, Selah.

Kunjalo ke, ukuba ndilungiselela abo baya kushiywa ngokupheleleyo emlilweni wocikido, nkqu nabo bonke abaya kunikwa ithuba lokunqula igama likaYesu Krestu de kube sekupheleni kwabo, kuba bonke abathanda ubomi babo kungekhona de kube sekupheleni ngaloo ndlela baya kufumana usindiso lwanaphakade oluthi inene inene, aluthelekiseki nentlungu abaya kuyinyamezela ngenxa yegama lam.

Zilungiseleleni Bandla lam Mzimba wam nkqu noMtshakazi wam, zilungiseleleni nizilungiselele ngokwamaZwi am angcwele nezibhalo zeNdaba Ezilungileyo ezingunaPhakade nindilinde zithandwa zam kuba ndiyeza kuni kwaye aniyi kudana kulindelo lwenu. Ngoko ke, yamkelani ukukhalinyelwa kwam nokohlwaywa kwam nithobele amaZwi am nezilumkiso zam kuba ndiyeza kuni Zithandwa zam, kwaye andisayi kuze ndishiye abathembekileyo kuKrestu emlilweni wocikido, okanye esigwebeni saboni esingesiso esenu.

Zilungiseleleni Zithandwa zam, ekuthobeleni konke nasekulindeleni, kwaye nizintywilisele kumaZwi eNdaba Ezilungileyo ezingunaPhakade, niwabambe ezingqondweni nasezintliziyweni zenu niqonde ubuncwane besibhalo endibunike emhlabeni ukunigcina kula maxesha okutyhila, nkqu nokutyhila endakuqala ngamaxilongo amaninzi alungisiweyo kumhlambi wam omncinci avakele ngokuthembekileyo, nkqu lawo maxilongo alungisa indlela yomthunywa wam nomkhonzi wam osisitya esinyuliweyo kwiNkosi yakhe engunaPhakade, nkqu uKrestu Yesu, okwanguYeshua HaMashiach, nkqu nalapho ndityhila khona amaZwi am kwisitya sam esibekelwe bucala esivumelana kwaye sizinikele ngokupheleleyo kwiinjongo endimthume zona, kwaye kungomyalelo wam namandla am apho iminwe yakhe ifumana khona izitshixo nkqu nokutyhila la maZwi angcwele endimnike wona ukuba awakhululele emhlabeni okokuqala, nkqu namaZwi alungiselelwe ukufumana amehlo, iindlebe, neentliziyo zabo bonke abathunyelwe kubo, Selah.

Ngoko ke, yivani isandi selizwi lam kwesi sibhengezo singcwele esinikwe ukutsalela abathembekileyo kumyalelo wam, nokunilungisa nonke ekuthobeleni isandi saloo mbande, ukuze nihlanganiselwe kum ngomyalelo wam.

Yabonani! Ndiyasayina esi sibhengezo nale leta ngesandla somthunywa wam nomkhonzi wam, kodwa isayinwe ngegama lam kanye ngothando lonke negunya,

uYeshua HaMashiach, iNkosi engunaPhakade yoBulungisa.
