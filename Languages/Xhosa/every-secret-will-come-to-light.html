Epreli 12, 2024

Yonke Imfihlelo Iya Kuvela Ekukhanyeni!

Anifundanga na? Yonke imfihlelo iya kuvela ekukhanyeni. Anifundanga na? Yonke into efihliweyo iya kutyhilwa. Anifundanga na? Yonke injongo yentliziyo iya kuza phambi kwam. Lonke ulwimi luya kuvuma kum, itsho iNkosi elungileyo noKumkani olungileyo, olawula ngobulungisa. Owu, bantu bam, kutheni nikhetha isiphoso sika-Adam no-Eva, abathi kwasekuqaleni bazama ukufihla ihlazo labo ngemisebenzi yabo, kuba bathunga amagqabi omkhiwane bazenzela iimfaskoti. Kanti, uThixo wakubona okwenzekileyo kwaye wayesazi ukuba baya kukhubeka ngaphambi kokuba abadale. Waza wenzani uBawo kubo? WABENZELA iingubo zezintsu waza wabanxibisa ngaphandle kwemisebenzi yabo.

Kutheni nicinga ukuba ningazifihla izono zenu kum, bantwana bam? Ndinibona nonke kwaye ndinazi ngcono kunokuba nizazi nina. Yekani ukumamela abelusi abalahlekileyo abashumayela omnye uYesu; ngokwenene kwimiqolomba eyaphukileyo banikela ngamanzi adikidiki kwibandla elidikidiki elisongelwe kukunyaniseka kobuxoki nakoko kubizwa ngokuba kukucinga okuhle. Banixokisa ngobuKrestu bokuzinceda, benixelela iinyaniso ezingekho mgceni ezinjengokuba uThixo unithanda ninjalo. Ngoxa uthando lukaBawo lungapheliyo kwaye lushushu kakhulu kubantwana bakhe, ubuKrestu bokwenene bushumayela inguquko noxolelo lwezono, izono ezivunywayo ngokunyaniseka ngentliziyo emsulwa ekhokelela elusindisweni nasekuphileni. Kanti, abaninzi eBandleni lam bayazithethelela kwaye bazigwebele izono zabo. Bazithethelela kwiintanda-bulumko zabo baze bafune indawo yokuphumla yobuxoki, bejongela phantsi abazalwana babo ngekratshi lokuzenzisa njengoko besonga ubugwenxa babo kukuzithethelela kwabo kobuxoki. Asikokwazi kukaSathana oku xa wanikela isiqhamo esalelweyo kunina wabo bonke abaphilayo? Ayiyiyo le imisebenzi yenyama?

Ndinibuza umbuzo; nonke niyayazi impendulo. Uyintoni umvuzo wesono? Isibhalo esingcwele sithi lowo mvuzo uyintoni? Asikokufa na? Kanti, uninzi lwenu luvakalelwa kukuba izono zenu zithethelelekile kwaye ziyinxalenye yenu noko kunenza nahluke, njengoko nilusebenzisa kakubi ubabalo lwam. Ekufeni, niya kufa. Nangona ninobomi obungunaphakade kum, kwaye ndiya kunixolela nonke kwaye andiyi kunilahla ukuba nihleli kum, ekufeni niya kufa.

Ukususela ekuphefumleni kwenu kokuqala, niyatshabalala ngenxa yendalo yesono engaphakathi kwenu.

Xa niqhubeka esonweni kwaye nihlwayelela inyama, niya kuvuna ukonakala ngalo lonke ixesha, kuba ekufeni niya kufa. Isono ekugqibeleni siya kubulala yonke inkalo yobomi benu kwanenyama yenu kude kube sekuphefumleni kwenu kokugqibela ukuba niyaqhubeka. Asikuko oko kuphela, kodwa niya kulahlekelwa ngumvuzo wenu nime neentloni emazulwini esihlalweni sam sokugweba njengoko iinkuni, ifula, neendiza enazixabisayo zitsha zibe lilize phambi kwenu, kwaye niya kulahlekelwa njengoko ndiya kuwuhluba umvuzo wenu ndiwunike omnye, kuba ndingumgwebi olilungisa. Kutheni niza kum ngenguquko nize nibuyele emhlanzweni wenu? Nifeza ntoni? Aniqondi na ukuba aninakukhonza mna noSathana ngaxeshanye? Ukuba nenza isono, ningabakhonzi besono de nize kum nicoceke, kwaye lonke ulwimi luya kuvuma! Kutheni ninqwenela ukubuyela kwizinto ezibuthathaka nezililize? Into eziya kuhlala ziyenza kukufuna ukunibulala njengoko ziniqweqwedisa njengexhoba.

Yazini ukuba imimoya emibi iyaqhayisa ngabantwana bam ekwaziyo ukubabamba. Kutheni nikhetha ukuzisa ihlazo egameni lenu nize nizenze intlekisa? Nilulibele uthando lwenu lokuqala, kwaye uninzi lwenu lubandakanyeka kubugqwirha bobuKrestu nakubuKrestu bokuzinceda, uhlobo lokholo olungenam, kuba andiyi kungena kwibandla laseLawodike kodwa ndiya kuma emnyango ndinkqonkqoze. Ndithi kuni ngokucacileyo, ukucinga kwenu okuhle akulawuli nto, kwaye nikhohlisiwe.

Ngaba iintywenka zesono zexesha elifutshane zibaluleke kangako kuni? Aniqondi na ukuba ndiya kunivuza kakhulu ngezinto zasezulwini ngeenkathazo nezilingo zenu? Ndiya kubuyekeza oko nikuncamayo ngenxa yam egameni lam. Buyelani kuthando lwenu lokuqala, bantwana bam, nize kum niphume eBhabheli. Kuba imihla yomfazi omkhulu ohenyuzayo ibaliwe, kwaye akuyi kubakho nyango kuye; ngoko ke, phumani kuye, bantu bam, nahlukane ukuze ningabelani naye kwizibetho zakhe.ahlukanani nenkanuko nemisebenzi yenyama. Kuba konke okusehlabathini, inkanuko yenyama, nenkanuko yamehlo, nekratshi lobomi, asiveli kuBawo, kodwa livela ehlabathini kwaye ihlabathi liyadlula.

Balekani umbulo kwaye ningabelani nabo kwihlazo neziphumo zabo. Bantwana bam, kufana nokuba nenza isenzo ngokwenu xa nifumana uyolo kubugwenxa nasekonakaleni. Lahlanani neendlela zenu zokubawa, kuba ndiya kubohlwaya abo baziqwebela ubutyebi, kwaye kungekudala imali yenu iya kuba lilize. Tyhalelani kumoya wam, kuba umoya wam bubomi noxolo.ahlukanani nobubi nize ningoni! Vunani isiqhamo somoya kum, kuba ndininikile amandla phezu kwezono zenu ukuba niya kuza kum nicoceke. Nicinga ukuba ningafihla into ekujongeni kwam? Ndinikwe amandla okubona, kwaye mna noBawo simunye kubunye nasenjongweni. Akunakwenzeka ukuzifihla, kwaye akunakwenzeka ukufihla izono zenu kum. Uninzi lwenu alunangcamango yokuba nikhangeleka njani na njengezilima xa nizenza ngamalungisa ngokungathi aninasono, kanti amehlo enu atsha yinkanuko evuthisa izinqe zenu.

Qondani ukuba isela lisebenzisa izinto ezininzi ezimbi namaqhinga ngokuchasene nani. Ukuba nilandela intando yam yokuxhathisa nize niguqukele kum, ndiluncedo olusoloko lukho ngexesha lengxakeko kwaye ndiya kuninceda, ndiya kunikhusela, ndiya kunihlambulula ndenze indlela yokubaleka izilingo ezininzi enijongene nazo, kodwa kufuneka nixhathise ngako konke okuni. Akuthethi nto xa nisithi nixolile nize nibuyele ngqo emhlanzweni wenu, kodwa ningancama ukubona inja isenza okufanayo, kanti ngekratshi lenu nicinga ukuba nahlukile, ngoko musani ukuziphatha njengenja yasendle.

Isono sisono. Indlela ekuphela kwayo yokoyisa isono kungokwegazi lam lentlawulelo nangokuphumla kum. Umoya endiwuthululele phezu kwenu uya kuninika amandla. Ndidale indalo entsha kuni, kwaye ukuba nithe nacela, ndiya kudala intliziyo ecocekileyo kuni.

Ngoko yithuliseni imilomo yenu emdaka, valani amehlo enu anenkanuko ngomthandazo, nahlukane nobugwenxa, kwaye ndiya kunihlambulula. Ewe, ndiya kunihlambulula ngokupheleleyo nibe mhlophe njengekhephu elitsha xa nivuma kwaye nitshintsha niguquke kwiindlela zenu ezimdaka. Bonke endibathandayo, ndiyabakhalimela; kwaye bonke endibathandayo, ndiyabathethisa. Nguwuphi ubawo ongamthethisiyo owakhe umntwana? Nangona abaninzi besenjenjalo, ezi asizondlela zam, kwaye ezi asizondlela zikaBawo. Ekubeni uThixo esebenzelana nani njengabantwana, uya kunothethisa njengabantwana; kuba ukuba akanithethisi, aningobantwana bakhe kodwa ningabantwana basemzini.

Kodwa ningabantwana bakhe, ngoko bekani indlebe kumazwi am ninyamezele ukuthethiswa, nithobele izilumkiso, kuba ubomi nolonwabo lulindile. Akukho kuthethiswa kubonakala kuvuyisa ngelo xesha, kodwa kuya kukhokelela kwiziqhamo zoxolo zobulungisa. Yizani kum, kwaye ndiya kunihlambulula kwiindlela zenu ezindala, kwaye ndiya kunifundisa iindlela zam ukuze nihambe ngobulungisa nangoxolo. Ncedani abazalwana benu nibe ngumzekelo wenguquko, kuba ekumeni kwenu niya kuqhawula amatyathanga amaninzi nemixokelelwane emininzi, kwanemixokelelwane eniyibambe ngezandla zenu nize niyibambelele ngokuqinileyo. Ndinicebisa ukuba niyiyeke yonke. Isono sikhokelela ebukhobokeni, kwaye sihlawula ukufa; ngoko ke, phumani kuye, ukuze ningabelani naye kwizigwebo zakhe.

Ndingcwele, ngoko yibani ngcwele nani.

Ndingumgwebi olilungisa.

Ixesha lifutshane, bantwana bam, kwaye iikhosi zenu ziya kugqitywa kungekudala. Musani ukucinga ukuba ukungalungisi kuya kungena ebukumkanini bam, okanye ukuba niya kuma phambi kwam ezonweni zenu nithwale umvuzo. Nguabo boyisayo kuphela abaya kuma nam. Niya kusindiswa, kodwa kutheni ningafuna ukulahlekelwa bubukhulu endinilungiselele bona, kuba aninakuyiqonda nokuyiqonda into eniyincamayo ngenxa yeenkanuko zenu.

Yizani kum, kuba ndityebile ngenceba.

Yizani kum, kuba ubabalo lwam luya kunixhasa.

Yizani kum, kuba ndim ohamba phakathi kweziphatho zezibane ezisixhenxe kwaye ndim oya kulondoloza umvuzo wenu ukuba nithe neza kum ngokuthobeka, nilahla ikratshi lenu elingenaxabiso. Ndiyanithanda, bantwana bam, kwaye kungekudala niya kuba nam. Yabonani! Ndimi emnyango ndinkqonkqoza okwexeshana nje elifutshane. Ndinixelela ezi zinto kungekuba ndinigweba, kodwa ukuze nibe ne-oyile ezibaneni zenu kwaye nibe ndilungele xa ndifika. Ndiza ngexesha eningalilindelanga, kwaye ndiza kungekudala kunokuba ningacinga. Ixesha liphelile phantse.
