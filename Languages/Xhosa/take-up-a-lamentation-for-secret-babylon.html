Yenzani isililo ngeBhabheli eyimfihlo!

Yivani amazwi am kwaye nive ilizwi lalowo udandulukayo entlango! Ndiyabiza, ndiyabiza, niyandiva na? Thulisani iilwimi zenu ezizele sisono niguqe ngeedolo niguquke kwaye nive ilizwi lam elingunaphakade ngoku nisakwazi, kuba uthando lwam ngani lukhulu, kwaye ndingulowo uchitha iinyembezi ezininzi ngentliziyo ebuhlungu ngenxa yoko kuya kwenzeka kwabo bangalihoyiyo ilizwi lam. Kuba nakulo mzuzu wokugqibela bonke abasabelayo bayamenywa kubomi obungunaphakade. Ngoko ndivumeni njengeNkosi yenu, kuba ndinguYesu waseNazarete, kwaye ndingulowo uThixo ongunaphakade amthumileyo ukuba anihlangule phambi kokuba intlekele nomgwebo zifike. Kutheni nizibala njengabangafanelanga ubomi obungunaphakade? Ngaba niya kukhetha amadangatye entshabalaliso kunamasimi oxolo anendyebo apho niya kufumana idlelo elingunaphakade?

Kuba njengokuba umenzi-bubi owabethelelwa ecaleni kwam eKalvari uya kuba nam eparadesi, ngokunjalo ndiya kunisindisa ngokupheleleyo ukuba niya kundenza iNkosi yenu ngoku! AKUKHO XESHA LOKUMOSHAKALA. Kuba ndafela ngaphandle kwamasango aseYerusalem naboni njengoko ndathatha indawo yenu emgwebeni, kwaye niya kusindiswa ukuba nithe naguqukela kum ngenguquko ngoku kanye. Ndithi kuni inene, inene: La mazwi akafikanga ezindlebeni zenu ngengozi, kwaye kungekudala kuya kubakho intshabalaliso enkulu kwaye abaninzi abayi kusinda kwaye ukwala kwabo kuya kuhlala ngonaphakade kumalangatye angunaphakade abekelwe bonke abakhetha ukunamathela ezonweni zabo baze bahlale bemanyene nabawileyo.

Ukuzimanya nemvukelo kaSathana, nokuba kungenxa yokungazi, kukuthatha inxaxheba ngokuzithandela ezonweni zabo, ngoko ke bonke abakwenzayo baya kuthwala umgwebo wabo ngonaphakade. Ngaba niya kulwamkela unikelo lwam, nisazi ukuba uThixo wandivusa kwabafileyo ukuze ndingaphindi ndife kwaye nisazi ukuba ndinguloo Nkosi enkulu yeeNkosi noKumkani wooKumkani? Guqukani ngoku nisakwazi, kuba uYahweh ukulungele ukuvala umnyango womkhombe, kwaye akukho xesha kwaye nangawuphi na umzuzwana ndiya kuxhwila abathembekileyo ndibase ezulwini, nkqu nabo bonke abasindisiweyo engqumbini ngedini lam elisindisayo nelingunaphakade. Ngoko ndivakaliseni, mna Yesu waseNazarete, njengeNkosi yenu kwaye nazi ukuba ndiphila ngonaphakade kuba uThixo undivusile kwabafileyo ukuze ndingaphindi ndife.

Bantwana bam, phulaphulani kwaye niqhubeke niguqa ngeedolo ngenguquko eqhubekayo kwaye nive ilizwi leNkosi yenu uYesu Krestu, yena kanye lowo uYahweh amthume njengemvana kaThixo. Yivani amazwi am, Bandla lam nkqu noMtshakazi wam, Kuba njengombingeleli wenu omkhulu ongunaphakade ngokohlobo lukaMelkitsedeke ndizinikele njengePasika yenu egqibeleleyo, kwaye ndanikezela ngegazi lam elingenasono kude kube lithontsi lokugqibela ngenkululeko yenu epheleleyo nesesikweni kwimeko yenu yangoku yokuwa kwaye inkululeko yenu epheleleyo ilapha, ngoko vuyani!

Kwaye bonke abaye bandamkela baza balivuma igama lam elingunaphakade njengeNkosi yabo baya kuba nobomi obungunaphakade. Kuba ndinguYesu waseNazarete iNkosi yenu engunaphakade, kwaye ndenze bonke abavumileyo igama lam kula maxesha obabalo nenceba oonyana bakaThixo abangunaphakade. Yabonani, uthando olunjani uBawo aninike lona, lokuba nibizwe ngokuba ngoonyana bakaThixo: ngenxa yesi sizathu ihlabathi alinazi, kuba alindazi mna, Krestu Yesu iNkosi yeeNkosi noKumkani wooKumkani. Zintanda, ngoku ningoonyana bakaThixo, kodwa akukabonakali into eniya kuba yiyo: kwaye niyazi ukuba xa ndithe ndabonakaliswa, niya kufana nam: kuba niya kundibona njengoko ndinjalo.

Bantwanana bam, qondani ukuba ndiza kubonakaliswa nangawuphi na umzuzu ngoku njengoko ukubonakala kwam emafini kulapha. Kuba mna, Krestu Yesu, ndiya kuhla ezulwini ndikhwaza, ngelizwi lesiphatha-zithunywa, nangexilongo likaThixo: kwaye abafileyo kuKrestu baya kuvuka kuqala. Emva koko nina baphilayo, nabasalayo, niya kuxhwilwa kunye nabo emafini, ukuze nihlangabeze iNkosi yenu engunaphakade emoyeni: kwaye niya kuhlala ninjalo neNkosi yenu ngonaphakade. Ngaba niya kuwavumela la mazwi ukuba anithuthuzele, bantwana bam? Ngaba nimvumele uSathana ukuba anenze niwakhanyele la mazwi acacileyo abhalelwe ukuba wonke umntu awabone? Ngaba niya kukhetha ukuhlala phakathi kwabagwetyiweyo ngenkani yokuvukela? Ukukhanyela esi siganeko sibhalwe ngokucacileyo esibhalweni ngesandla soMpostile wam uPawulos kukundikhanyela mna kuba ndiza kusindisa abathembekileyo engqumbini kuba zinjalo izithembiso zikaYahweh ezityhilwe kwimfihlelo enkulu kaThixo ebhalwe nguPawulos kwiiminyaka emininzi eyadlulayo.

Ndiyanicebisa: musani ukundikhanyela, kwaye ningene emkhombeni kungekabi mva kakhulu. Kuba bonke abakhetha ukuhlala kwimigwebo ezayo baya kuza kum ngokubiza egameni lam kude kube sekupheleni, kwaye abaninzi baya kubandezeleka kude kube sekuphefumleni kwabo kokugqibela. Kodwa baya kuvukela kubomi obungunaphakade nomvuzo omkhulu novuyo olungunaphakade! Kwaye ngoko bonke ababizelwe kunaphakade baya kufika kwilifa labo elingunaphakade, kuba akukho mntu unokunixhwila nkqu noyena mncinane kuni ezandleni zam ezingunaphakade. Ngoko yivani amazwi am kwaye nihlale kwingqiqo enye ekum, Krestu Yesu, kuba ndiliLizwi likaThixo elenziwe inyama kwaye ndisoloko ndithetha inyaniso. Yekani ukumamela amazwi amaninzi angenalukholo akhanyela amazwi am okanye niya kuzifumana nishiywe phakathi kwawo xa into enjalo ingeyomfuneko.

Kuba nina basindisiweyo bam, nkqu nani ngoku ninguBantwana boThixo ophilayo, nkqu abakaYahweh ngokwakhe ngendlela nangommangaliso wokuzalwa okutsha nisindiswe ngonaphakade kwaye nizelwe ngokutsha ngembewu engenakonakala. Noko ke, ningabaphambukeli nabahambi kumazwe asemzini, kwaye abaninzi benu baphakathi kwemida yaloohenyukazi mkhulu, nkqu namazwe akwimixokelelwane alindele umgwebo wawo opheleleyo nentshabalaliso: Kuba isahlulo esiphindwe kabini somgwebo sivakaliswe ngeBhabheli eyimfihlo kwaye kuya kuba njalo. Kuba ngeliphi ixesha uYahweh angabakhululanga abalungileyo emgwebeni, kuba akukho mntu unokuthi uYahweh unobuyatha, kwaye ubungqina bukaLote bungqina oku kude kube namhlanje. Nkqu nakumazwe anenyanyekayo aseSodom naseGomora uYahweh wathumela iiNgelosi zakhe ukuba zihlangule le ndoda inye ilungileyo eyayihleli emasangweni esixeko sotshaba.

Kwaye ngokunjalo ndiya kulihlangula iBandla lam elithembekileyo, nkqu nabo bonke endibatywinileyo ngaloo moya ungcwele wesithembiso ukususela ngomhla wePentekoste ekuzalweni kweBandla lam okubhalwe kubungqina beZenzo! (Anifundanga na?) Kwaye bonke abathe bafa bethembekile kuKrestu ukususela ekuqaleni kwexesha leBandla lam nabo bonke abaphilayo nabasalayo baya kuqokelelwa kunye ngokuqhwanyaza kweliso kwaye ngonaphakade baya kuba nam! Kuba ndingulowo uzayo ngenxa yabam abathembekileyo, ababiziweyo, nabanyuliweyo, nkqu noMzimba wam neBandla lam nkqu noMtshakazi wam. Ndiya kubakhulula abathembekileyo, nkqu nabo bahambe ngokomoya wam nabo bonke abaguqukileyo ezonweni zabo baza bandenza iNkosi ngentliziyo ezele lukholo kuba bangoSirayeli kaThixo! Kwaye siya kuba sikhulu esi siganeko sizayo kwaye kuya kuba kukhulu ukushukuma kwaye ngeemiqondiso ezininzi ukusondela kwam kuvakalisiwe kwaye kusavakaliswa kuwo onke amazulu ngeemiqondiso phezulu neemiqondiso emhlabeni ngezantsi. Kwaye kuya kuba njalo ukuqokelelwa kweentlahlela ukuze bonke babone kwaye bakhangele bemangalisiwe!

Kwaye ngoko ilizwi lam lisahleli lihlokoma kweli hlabathi liwileyo, lifumana amehlo, iindlebe, neentliziyo zabo bonke abo lithunyelwe kubo. Uyaliva na ilizwi likaYeshua HaMashiach, uyalazi na ilizwi lam? Uya kuwamkela na amazwi am? Ungandiva na njengoko ndithetha nentliziyo yakho? Kuba njengoko kubhaliwe: iintliziyo zaba bantu zityebile ngenxa yokutya kakhulu, neendlebe zabo zinzima ukuva, namehlo abo bawavalile, hleze babone ngamehlo abo, beve ngeendlebe zabo, baqonde ngentliziyo yabo, baguquke, ndibaphilise. Abaninzi kwaba baye balaliswa ngamazwi anenyanyekayo kodwa athuthuzelayo omnye uYesu, kodwa qinisekani akukho wumbi.

Kuba nangaluphi na ulwimi igama lam libizwa ngalo, mna Yesu waseNazarete, nkqu noYeshua HaMashiach, ndiya kuva kwaye ndiya kusindisa ngokupheleleyo bonke ababiza egameni lam kude kube sekupheleni. Kuba amazwi am obulumko, nkqu neendaba ezilungileyo ezingunaphakade zityhiliwe kwaye zihlokoma indlela yazo kuwo wonke umhlaba. Kwaye amazwi am ayabafumana abo athunyelwe kubo, kuba anjalo amazwi oThixo oPhezukonke, nkqu noYahweh, kuba alikho nelinye iLizwi likaThixo elibuyayo lilambatha, kwaye aya kufezekisa oko ajongene nako kwaye asikelelekile amehlo enu, kuba ayabona: neendlebe zenu, kuba ziyeva neentliziyo zenu zizele lukholo, kwaye ndilufake uthando lukaThixo ezintliziyweni zenu kanye apho nikhala khona nithi Abha Bawo! Ningoonyana bakaThixo kwaye akubonakali ukuba niya kuba yintoni, kodwa xa ndibonakala niya kubona ngokupheleleyo kwaye ubomi benu bufihliwe kum, Krestu Yesu, kwaye ubomi benu sele buza kutyhilwa ngokupheleleyo kwaye ngoku lixesha lokutyhilwa.

Yabonani! IBhabheli eyimfihlo ihleli emixokolelweni njengoko iimpondo ezilishumi zigqibezela amalungiselelo azo entshabalaliso yayo epheleleyo. Inene, inene, kwiBhabheli eyimfihlo kwaye kangangeminyaka emininzi ngoku ndithume abapostile bam, ndithume abaprofeti bam, ndithume abavangeli bam, ndithume abefundisi bam, ndithume abafundisi bam, ndithume abafundi bam abathembekileyo nabazalwana kwaye ndithumele nabahlolokazi bebodwa, kwaye ndiwuthulule ngokupheleleyo umoya wam kule mihla yokugqibela yobabalo nenceba!

Kodwa endaweni yokuba iintlanga zihlele kwilaphu elirhwexayo naseluthuthwini ziyazonwabisa ngeemiqondiso nezilumkiso ezinikwe ngomgwebo ozayo njengoko zithatha imifanekiso neevidiyo zokonwaba kwazo. Baye bazigatya izithunywa zam ezazisa ukukhanya kweendaba zam ezilungileyo zosindiso, baye bagculela baza bahlekisa ngabaprofeti bam, baye bahlasela abavangeli bam, baye bonakalisa abefundisi bam, kwaye batshutshisa baza balalanisa abafundisi bam.

Imikhosi yenamba enkulu ebomvu iyakungqinga wena Bhabheli eyimfihlo kwaye iphakathi kwemida yakho ngokupheleleyo. Kuba nkqu neenkokeli zakho ezingamaZiyonisti ezingcatshayo zikuthengisile ngeqhekeza lesonka kwiintshaba zakho kwaye nokuqhubela phambili amayelenqe azo obusathana. Kwaye u-El Shaddai uwavumele la madoda aphambeneyo ukuba aqhubeke, nkqu nokukufaka ezintliziyweni zawo ukufezekisa iinjongo zakhe. Unamandla u-El Shaddai okugwebayo, wena henyukazi elibomvu elihleli ngokuzidla liyoliswa nangona uhlutyiwe lonke uzuko, uhlutyiwe zonke izinto ezimnandi, uhlutyiwe nkqu namakhaya akho kuba ndinike amakhaya akho nemihlaba yakho ezandleni zeentshaba zakho. Nkqu nasezandleni zerhamncwa nomprofeti wobuxoki, kuba basebenza kunye ukuze bakutshabalalise ngokupheleleyo emva kwesigqubuthelo sexhego elisisidenge. Kuba irhamncwa sele liza kumphosa phantsi umfazi olihenyukazi elikhulu elo irhamncwa lidiniweyo kukumthwala, kwaye iinjongo zalo zifezekisiwe. Ngoko ke, uya kutshiswa ngokupheleleyo, aze atshatyalaliswe ngokupheleleyo ngeyure enye kwaye intshabalaliso yakhe ayilibazisi.

Kuba imida yabo yemfazwe, nkqu nemida yabo ebomvu enikwe ngokungekho nyani ukuba ingawelwa, ibekiwe yaze yawelwa ngokuphindaphindiweyo nangenjongo. Musani ukukholelwa kubuxoki babo, okanye kwizizathu zabo zobuxoki kuba bafuna ukubonakala bengamalungisa kwimfazwe yabo ezayo kwaye irhamncwa nomprofeti wobuxoki abangekavuki ngokupheleleyo baya kuvuka ngokupheleleyo eluthuthwini baze bathi umgwebo kaThixo wehla ukuze ukhonze iinjongo zabo, ngoko musani ukukholelwa nakwelona lincinane kubuxoki babo kwaye umgwebo wabo wokugqibela awulibazisi.

Kuba baya kuqhubeka baze benze izono ezimbi nangakumbi kuneBhabheli eyimfihlo, kwaye banetyala lokukhokelela iBhabheli eyimfihlo ekulahlekeni ngokupheleleyo nasekuyitshabalaliseni ngokupheleleyo, kuba uThixo ukufakile ezintliziyweni zabo ukufezekisa intando yakhe. Basebenza ngokubambisana kwaye bonke abo babonakala beziintshaba basebenza kunye emfihlekweni njengoko bedlala ngokweskripthi sabo kwaye bedlala iindima zabo kwinqanaba lehlabathi, kwaye baya kubulala bonke abo bakwimiyalelo yabo abakhetha ukungaziqhelanisi kwaye badlale iindima zabo ngokwaneleyo ukuze bafezekise iinjongo zenamba. Kuba inamba inika amarhamncwa amandla ayo netrone yayo, negunya elikhulu, Kwaye iimpondo ezilishumi zifumana amandla njengookumkani iyure enye nerhamncwa kwaye aba banangqiqo-nye, kwaye baya kunika amandla abo namandla abo kwirhamncwa kuba banikwe njalo nguYahweh ukuba bafezekise. Qaphelani bantwana bam kwaye nifunde kum kwaye nilandele iindlela zam, kuba iindlela zam ziindlela zikaYahweh, uThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade!

Qondani kwaye niphulaphule, kuba la marhamncwa angcatshayo nookumkani abakhohlakeleyo bangabemiyalelo yeNamba abaye balawula iBhabheli eyimfihlo kwimibutho yabo eyimfihlo ukususela ekuqaleni kwayo. Indoda ezenzisayo nezenza ngathi, nkqu nendoda engendawo, ihamba ngomlomo ogwenxa. Ewe bazenza abanye kuba bonke bangabazenzisi, kwaye befihliwe phakathi kwazo zonke iintlanga ukhula luhlwayelwe. Benza imiqondiso ngamehlo abo: bakhomba ngeenyawo zabo: bayalela ngeminwe yabo. Izinto ezirheletyo zisezintliziyweni zabo: bacinga ububi ngamaxesha onke, baze bavuse iingxabano. Ngoko ke, intshabalaliso yabo iya kufika ngokukhawuleza: baya kutshatyalaliswa ngequbuliso bengenakuchacha. Ezi zinto zintandathu uYehova uyazithiya: ewe, umphefumlo wakhe uyazicekisa ezisixhenxe: Amehlo aqhankqalazayo, ulwimi oluxokayo, nezandla eziphalaza igazi elimsulwa, intliziyo ecinga amayelenqe akhohlakeleyo, iinyawo ezikhawulezayo ukubalekela ebubini, Ingqina elixokayo elithetha ubuxoki, nalowo uvusa iingxabano phakathi kwabazalwana.

Aniboni na? Ngaba niyala ukuzazi iisimboli zabo ukuze niqhubeke nihleli ekungazini ngokuzithandela ukuba iinkokeli zenu zezabawileyo? Aniqondi na ukuba abazonwabisi benu ngabawileyo? Aniqondi na ukuba abanjengani ngemvelo, kuba abaninzi baziguqule ngonaphakade ekuphambaneni kwabo kwaye abanye bangukhula ukususela ekuzalweni kwaye uMtyholi nguYise wabo? Noko ke nikhetha ukuhlala ngokuzithandela ebumnyameni, nize niziyolise nize nizibhixe ezonweni zenu njengoko niqengqeleka eludakeni neehagu eziniholele kumasango esihogo kanye apho abaninzi beqhayisa ngokungazi ngamampunge nangekratshi. Kungoko ndithe encwadini yam eya kwabase-Efese endayinika uPawulos: Vuka wena uleleyo, uvuke ekufeni, kwaye mna, Krestu Yesu, ndiya kukunika ukukhanya! Lumkani ngoko ukuba nihambe ngokulumka: ningabi njengezidenge, kodwa nibe njengabalumkileyo. Kwaye ngoko ndibaveza aba baphathi bakhohlakeleyo beBhabheli eyimfihlo kwabam ababiziweyo abanyuliweyo nabathembekileyo ukuze ningabi lixhoba lenkohliso yabo kwaye niphume ngokupheleleyo kuyo kwaye nahlukane nazo zonke iindlela zenu kwaye ningabelani ngezibetho zayo.

Yivani amazwi am kwaye niqonde ukuba kwenzeka ntoni ngoku, kuba uhlaselo lwabo olothusayo luyaqhubeka. Ndithetha ngoku neBhabheli eyimfihlo nangona isisithulu, isisimumu, kwaye iyimfama kwaye ibotshelelwe ngokupheleleyo emixokolelweni ngenxa yomgwebo wayo. Njengoko belulungiselela uhlaselo lwabo olothusayo iminyaka emininzi ngoku, kwaye baye baqinisekisa intshabalaliso yeBhabheli eyimfihlo ngobukrelekrele, nezinye iindlela zenkohliso, kuba nkqu namanqaku ayo emali ayinto nje isithembiso esingenanto samatyala enziwe ezandleni zeenkokeli zenu ezikhonza abatshintshi bemali kwaye iBhabheli eyimfihlo ithengiselwe ezandleni zeentshaba zabo ngezi ndlela zobuqhetseba. Aniyiqondi na ibhanki yogcino-mali oluyinxalenye kunye nemali ye-fiat? Fundani ukuze nifunde.

Andikuniki mzekelo na xa ndagxotha abatshintshi bemali etempileni? Noko ke, endaweni yokubagxotha, niyabamema ukuba beze kudla isidlo sangokuhlwa neentsapho zenu, kwaye niye nabeka abantwana benu nabazukulwana benu njengesibambiso ukuze nifezekise iinkanuko zenu zokubawa kwaye ngoko inzala yenu ithengisiwe ukuze iqinisekise intshabalaliso yenu ukuze niphile iiyolo zesono. Wena, Bhabheli eyimfihlo, uyababulala abantwana bakho abangekazalwa ngolonwabo olukhulu nangekratshi kwaye ababulali babamsulwa banomgwebo wabo omisiweyo kwaye umgwebo wakho awulibazisi. Nangona abambalwa abaseleyo bebaleka benyukela emkhombeni phambi kokuba u-El Shaddai avale umnyango, abaninzi baya kuzifumana beshiywe emgwebeni kuba baya kukhetha endaweni yoko ukuhlala ngaphandle behlekisa kwaye bephila kamnandi.

Ngesiquphe, ngeyure enye intshabalalo yakho iya kufika ngequbuliso kwaye abayi kusinda. Kuba izinikele ekukhonzeni iintshaba zayo zanaphakade, ngoku ziya kuzisa intshabalalo yayo engunaphakade kuba ayisayi kuphinda ivuke iBhabheli eyiMfihlo. Owu Bhabheli eyiMfihlo, kuba uthengise abantwana bakho njengesibambiso samatyala akho ukuze uqhubeke neziyolo zesono (kuba nemali yakho ingumrhumo), kuba ubulele abamsulwa, nokunye okuninzi okwenzileyo. Ngoku ndiza kukunikela ngokupheleleyo ezandleni zabo kwaye noYehova ukumisele isahlulo esiphindwe kabini somgwebo phezu kwakho Bhabheli eyimfihlo. Kwaye abaninzi babantwana bakho baya kuthunyelwa kwiinkampu zabo zokusebenza nezokufundiswa ngokutsha kwaye abaninzi baya kusiwa kumazwe angaphandle kuba inamba ivunyelwe ukuba ibange umhlaba nabantu abandilahlileyo mna, Yeshua HaMashiach, bekhetha uBharabhas umesiya wabo wobuxoki. Kwaye abaninzi baya kuthatha uphawu lwabo loburhamncwa kwaye bathengise imiphefumlo yabo nenzala yabo kwisohlwayo sanaphakade esivela kubukho bukaThixo ongunaphakade nongunaphakade ngenxa yeziyolo zesono zexesha elifutshane.

Yenzani isililo ngabantu baseBhabheli eyiMfihlo, nani nihleli ebumnyameni nasekungazini ngokupheleleyo kwentshabalalo yenu ezayo. Phambi kwamehlo enu kanye nangemvume yevoti yenu nangokudangala kwenu, isizwe senu sonke siye saqhaqhwa phambi kwamehlo enu. Anisenabo ubuchule okanye izixhobo zokulwa imfazwe, kuba mna, noKrestu Yesu, ndiyihluthe imikhosi yenu yamandla ndayinikela ezandleni zeentshaba zenu. Izixeko zenu ezazikade zizikhulu ngoku zihleli kumanxuwa olwaphulo-mthetho nentlupheko, eniqhayisa ngayo nina nabazobi benu ngekratshi elikhulu. AmaEdom kudala ayisusa imizi-mveliso yawo kumazwe enu, kwaye niswele amandla okuvelisa enaninayo kwangaphambili kwanemathiriyeli ekrwada yokuvelisa oko nikudingayo ukuze nilwe iimfazwe zenu ezingapheliyo.

Kuba nisuka emfazweni niye emfazweni, kwaye niwatshabalalise ngokupheleleyo amazwe, angaphandle nasekhaya. Kuba kuwe, Bhabheli eyiMfihlo, kuphume isibetho seMikhosi eBomvu, sisasaza kwihlabathi lonke igwele elibomvu leNamba enkulu ebomvu, kuba ukhonze abawileyo ngasese kwasekuqaleni kwaye uyaqhubeka unanamhla. Bhabheli eyiMfihlo, uyinika ngokuzithandela imvume yakho kubaphathi bakho bakaSathana, abazazi kakuhle izicwangciso zabo kwaye besazi kakuhle ukuba bakwinkqubo yokutshabalalisa ngokupheleleyo isizwe sakho esasisakuba sikhulu kunye nawe. Kuba unetyala legazi labangcwele, kwaye igazi labafeli-nkolo liyakhala emhlabeni lifuna umgwebo wakho kwaye ukuba uYehova ebengayi kukugweba bekungafuneka aguquke kwiintlanga azigwebileyo kumaxesha adlulileyo, kwanabo basebenza njengesilumkiso somgwebo wakho ozayo ongawuhoyiyo ngokuphandle.

Kuvela kuwe, Bhabheli eyiMfihlo, intshabalalo eyaziswa kumazwe angaphandle yiMikhosi eBomvu njengoko amaEdom azithandekisa kubasebenzi ngegwele lawo elinetyhefu, kwanencwadi yawo enyelisayo exhaswe ngemali yiKhaka eliBomvu. Kuba iinkosi eziphangayo zamaxesha adlulileyo, kwanabaphathi bakho boshishino bezizukulwana zangaphambili, (kwanabo uthengise kubo inzala yakho) ngabo abanoxanduva lwesibetho semikhosi ebomvu. IBhabheli eyiMfihlo ngomnatha wayo wamatyala iye yaxhasa ngemali kwaye yaxhasa iMikhosi eBomvu kwiinzame zayo kwasekuqaleni, kwaye iBhabheli eyiMfihlo ibe yinxalenye yomnatha wabo kwasekuqaleni njengoko iKhaka eliBomvu lazimisa kwihlabathi lonke. Kwanomphefumlo ovuthayo wenamba ovela kwiKhaka eliBomvu, kuba ngabo abawileyo abahamba phakathi kwenu.

EBhabheli eyimfihlo kwaqeqeshwa iinkokeli zabo "zenguqu" emfihlekweni kuba ihleli phezu kwamanzi amaninzi, kwaye zathunyelwa ngasese kumazwe angaphandle, nakwaGogi noMagogi kwaye iinguqu zabo zazizikhulu, zinobundlobongela, zinenkohlakalo kwaye ngoku zigubungele umhlaba.
