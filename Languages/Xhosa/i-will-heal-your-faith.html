Ubungqina Obubini: Icawa
KwiMisebenzi kaPawulos uMpostile
Meyi 02, 2024

Ndiya Kulunyanga Ukholo Lwakho, itsho iNkosi Enkulu!

Yabonani! Mna, ewe mna, uKrestu Yesu iNkosi evukileyo nomSindisi wehlabathi, ndinombuzo kuni, mtshakazi wam. Ingaba niyakundiphendula ninyanisekile? Ingaba niyakwazi ukundinika impendulo efanelekileyo kwingxelo yenyaniso? Ngoko ke, ndiyabuza kuni: Xa uNyana woMntu esiza, nicinga ukuba, ndiyakufumana ukholo emhlabeni? Ngokucacileyo lo ngumbuzo ongafuni mpendulo, kodwa ukuba bekufuneka nime ngokobizo lwam ukuze niphendule, ibiyakuthini impendulo yenu? Beningandixelela ngokholo lwenu nokuba luhle kangakanani na? Beningandixelela ngeengcinga zenu ezakhayo, enicinga ngobuyatha ukuba nilawula ihlabathi ngeengcinga zenu? Aniqondi na ukuba ezona mfundiso zisisiseko elukholweni lwam, nkqu nokholo lukaYesu Krestu, kwanazo ezo mfundiso zisisiseko ziye zenziwa umgunyathi zaza zachazwa ngokutsha njengobuxoki? Kodwa nina ngobuyatha nithi: "Owu, uThixo soze ayivumele loo nto yenzeke!" Endibuza ngayo ukuba, nimazi kakuhle kangakanani na uThixo? Ningabacebisi bakhe? Niyakuma nize niyalele uYahweh? Niyakuma nize niyalele mna, uYeshua HaMashiach, ukuba ndenze intando yenu? Kanti uninzi lwenu ngobuyatha luzama ukwenza kanye loo nto, kuba nikhohlisiwe ngendlela ebuhlungu kakhulu.

Nivumele abaprofeti bobuxoki, kwa loo mungu uSathana awutyalileyo kwiicawa zakhe zobuxoki (ezingezizo ezam), kwabona baprofeti bobuxoki awayethetha ngabo umntakwethu uYuda njengoko ndandiphefumlela amazwi am entliziyweni yakhe nanjengoko ndandishukumisa umoya wakhe ngaphakathi kuye ngempembelelo engcwele: Yeha kubo, kuba bahambe ngendlela kaKayin baza balandela ngokubawa isiphoso sikaBhileham ukuze bafumane umvuzo, baza batshabalala ekuchaseni kukaKora. Aba bobuxoki bahamba phakathi kwenu ekuhleni, yaye uninzi lwenu luyabalandela, nicinga ukuba nilandela mna, kuba bangumgunyathi, yaye bazama ukuxelisa ilizwi lam ukuze abangenabuqili phakathi kweegusha zam babalandelele kwisiphoso esikhulu, abakuso. Kuba yintoni enye awayithethayo uYuda ngaba bangento yanto? Aba ngamabala ezidlweni zenu zothando, xa besidla nani, bezondla bengenaloyiko: ngamafu angenamanzi, aphetshethwa yimimoya, yimithi eneziqhamo ezibunayo, engenasiqhamo, efileyo kabini, encothulwe ngeengcambu. Ngamaza olwandle anomsindo, akhupha amagwebu ehlazo lawo, ziinkwenkwezi ezibhadulayo, ezigcinelwe ubumnyama obukhulu ngonaphakade.

Ndiyazi ukuba umgunyathi uyakholisa kwabanye benu, kuba abanye benu abandazi ngokwenene kodwa bazama ukunqula uYahweh nokundilandela mna, iNkosi yenu uYesu Krestu, ngeemfundiso zevangeli yempumelelo. Ningaphazami: Ivangeli yempumelelo bubuxoki, yaye andizange ndizinikele njengemvana yenu yePasika ukuze niqokelele ubutyebi kuni: Kuba ukuthanda imali yingcambu yabo bonke ububi: nto leyo abathi abanye, beyinqwenela, baphambuka elukholweni, baza bazihlabagula ngeentlungu ezininzi. Uninzi lwenu lulandele enye yeevengeli ezininzi zomgunyathi, kodwa akukho yimbi ivangeli. Uninzi lwenu lulandele omnye wooKrestu abaninzi bomgunyathi, kodwa akukho wumbi uKrestu ngaphandle kwam, uYeshua HaMashiach. Ndithi kuni ngokucacileyo, nikhohlisiwe, yaye uninzi lwenu lutshintshe ukholo lokwenene lukaYesu Krestu ngokholo lobuxoki. Ukholo lobuxoki nokholo oluxokayo apho igama elithi ukholo (andininikanga na iLizwi lokwenene loKholo?) alusathethi kuthemba, inkolelo, nokuzinza kuYahweh, uYihlo, nakum, uYesu Krestu iNkosi yenu, kodwa liye lachazwa ngokutsha kwizinto ezigqithisileyo ezingenaxabiso nakwiintsomi nakwiingcinga zomlingo zobugqi. Nivumele abangento yanto banilahlekise, yaye iicawa zenu ziye zangenelelwa ngabakaSathana, kwa loo mungu umtyholi awawutyalayo phakathi kwenu.

Oku kugqithisa kuhla kuye kwiingcinga ezakhayo nezingakhayo, yaye nina ngobuyatha nicinga ukuba nantoni na enicamanga ngayo ixesha elide iyakwenzeka, ngokungathi uThixo ubeke amandla akhe nenceba yakhe kwabo banokholo olukhulu lobuxoki, apho niyalela izinto ngeengcinga zenu. Aniqondi na ukuba bonke abenza izinto ezinjalo ezingento yanto bayabanjwa baze barhintyelwe baze bathinjwe yiminqweno yabo naba buxoki, nkqu nokubotshelelwa ekusileleni, kuba ngokungazi nilandela iindlela zikaSathana? Qondani ukuba zazizithunywa zezulu eziwileyo, ewe, ooSathana nabalindi abazibandakanya nabo, abafundisa uluntu ubugqwirha, ukusika iingcambu, amakhubalo, nokuthakatha; yaye amadoda amaninzi akhohlakeleyo angenelele kwicawa yam yaseLawodike yaye athimbe abaninzi njengexhoba abakholelwe kubuxoki bawo.

Oku kwenzekile kuba abaninzi kwicawa yam yaseLawodike bayala ukuma balwe ngobulungisa ukuze noyise ububi ngokulungileyo! Mandincede niqonde inyaniso. Akukho namnye kuni onamandla nawuphi na okanye isakhono ngokwakhe, kuba kusebuthathakeni benu xa ekugqibeleni niqonda ubuyatha bemizamo yenu. Ke, amaxesha amaninzi, niyaqonda ukuba xa nithembele kum, uKrestu Yesu, nize nilandele imiyalelo yam endleleni endinikhethele yona, ukuba nithembele kum nasemandleni am yaye ninokholo olupheleleyo kum. Kuba ndiyakungqina kuni nonke ngendlela yam, xa nihlala nithobekile kwilizwi lam nize nijike xa ndinixelela, ukuba kwimizamo yenu nasemandleni enu aninto yanto ngaphandle kokhozo lwesanti entlango, elahlekileyo zizandyondyo, yaye andifanga ukuze ndihlangule isanti kodwa umhlaba obomvu ka-Adam, kuba ndingum-Adam wokugqibela, yaye ndingumoya ophilisa. Noko ke, kum uKrestu Yesu, ninamandla yaye aninakunqandwa, kuba ninobu butyebi ezityeni zodongwe, yaye ndiyakunika imimangaliso emininzi, imiqondiso, izimanga nezinto ezinjalo ngezandla zenu xa ninokholo lobudlelwane kum, uYesu Krestu, endaweni yokholo kuni. Ncedani nindivumele ndinikhokele, nize nibuyeke ubuxoki babo bawayo, kuba ngezenzo zabo ingqumbo enkulu iqokelelwe nxamnye nabo ngomhla wengqumbo, apho isiphelo sabo sityiwa ngumlilo nesulfure evuthayo.

Qondani indlela le nkohliso eyafunyanwa ngayo, kuba kwiiYubhili zakutshanje kwabakho ukukhanyiselwa kobuxoki apho abo uluntu lwenu lubabala njengabakhulu bamgatya uThixo bekhetha ukuqiqa kwabo okungenamsebenzi. Bade benza isithixo sokuqiqa ngexesha lovukelo lwabo; aniyazi na imbali? La maqula angenamanzi aneefilosofi zawo ezonakeleyo nezingento yanto amchasile uThixo aza afundisa ngempazamo ukuba uThixo wayengumbukeli nje ongenzi nto ongenamdla ngokwenene eluntwini kodwa obonelela ngako konke ekude aze avumele uluntu luzifumanele, inceba kaThixo isiya kowona nozakuzaku unamandla phakathi kwabo. Emva koko, ekuhambeni kwexesha, imiphefumlo emininzi engazinzanga, kwa loo mungu kaSathana, yangenela icawa yam yaseLawodike ukuze igqibezele ubuxoki bayo ngamaxesha afanelekileyo. Baningenela ngamazwi amnandi neentetho ezintle baza bafundisa amandla okucinga ngomlingo kuMtshakazi wam, yaye abaninzi phakathi koMzimba wam babalandela ngokubawa, kuba ukubawa kwabo kwavuswa.

Aba nyana bakaSathana bakhohlakeleyo baxuba ngokufihlakeleyo ubugqwirha kwiimfundiso zam baza baphumelela ukungcolisa iLizwi lam elingcwele ngegwele, ngaphandle kokuba iLizwi lam alinakungcoliswa ziintsomi zabo, kuba ubuxoki babo bumi phezu kobuxoki. Aniqondi na ukuba igwele elincinane liyayibilisa yonke intlama? Aniqondi na ukuba igwele likaHerode negwele labaFarisi ayizizo zodwa iindlela zonakalo? Kuba nithakathiwe ligwele lobugqwirha bobuKrestu! Ngoko ke, GUQUKANI! Kuba ndathini kumaKorinte ngesandla sikaMpostile uPawulos? Kukhupheni ngoko igwele elidala, ukuze nibe yintlama entsha, njengoko ningenagwele. Kuba noKrestu iPasika yethu ubingelelwe ngenxa yethu. Yivani amazwi am! Ngoko la marhamncwa, la maqula angenamanzi, kwa lo mungu ungenaxabiso nonetyhefu washumayela waza waqala awabo amahlelo obuKrestu obuxoki, iinkonzo nkqu, eba abaninzi kwabo babeya kubalandela kwiCawa yokwenene, apho ndikhathalela uMtshakazi wam ngothando, ubabalo nenceba, ebakhokelela kude nam ukuya kwiindlu zonqulo zobuxoki ezinikezelwe ngokufihlakeleyo kuBhahali, kuba isizwe senu yiBhabhiloni efihlakeleyo!

Ngokungathi oku kwakungonelanga, abanozakuzaku abaninzi abanamandla nabazizityebi bahlawula imali eninzi baza bachitha umgudu omkhulu bebeka ubuxoki babo kwiiBhayibhile zobuxoki. Bakhupha imibhalo yase-Aleksandriya, yobuGnostiki, nto leyo eyabangela ukuba iCawa yam ibe neengqondo ezimbini isuke ekugxileni okunye kwemibhalo endayigcina ivela eAntiyokwe, nkqu nezinye iiBhayibhile zokufunda apho obu buxoki bongezelelwa emacaleni. Ezi ncwadi ke zaziswa kwii-seminari zenu ezonakeleyo zaza zaba ziiBhayibhile zokufunda eziphambili zaba baprofeti bobuxoki abaye bashumayela ubuxoki ngobuxoki ukusukela ngoko, yaye baya kuhlawula ngokupheleleyo. Ukusukela ngelo xesha, amazwe enu azele ziicawa zobuxoki ezikhokelwa ziingcuka eziphangayo, yaye ziseninzi iigusha zam ezicinga ngempazamo ukuba zinokuhlala neengcuka. Iicawa zenu zobuxoki zizindlu zokunyelisa; zizindlu zokonwabisa apho zicula iindumiso kuSathana kanye phambi kwenu, yaye nina aniswele ukuqonda kokomoya ukuze nibone. Zininzi iinkosi phakathi kobukumkani bukaMtyholi, yaye zicula iNkosi, Nkosi kuzo, ngaphandle kokuba xa zisithi kum Nkosi, Nkosi, ndiyakuthi, Mkani kum, nina benzi bobubi kuba andizange ndinazi.

Ukwenza izinto zibe mbi ngakumbi, bathatha ivangeli yempumelelo enyelisayo baza bayiguqula ngakumbi ngokungenisa isayikholoji yokuzinceda, yaye ndithini na ngemiba enjalo: Lumkani hleze umntu eze anithimbe ngefilosofi nenkohliso elilize, ngezithethe zabantu, nangemimiselo yehlabathi, hayi emva koKrestu. Ezi cawa zobuxoki zonakele azinaxabiso lokuhlangula, yaye ezininzi zisekwe ngabakhi abakhohlakeleyo abakhululekileyo kum, ilitye lembombo eliyintloko, yaye zonke izakhiwo zabo ziya kuwa kwisanti ehambayo. Aba ngabakhi abanyelisayo nabachweli bamatye bobuxoki nabakhi bezitena bobuxoki abalilahlileyo ilitye lembombo eliyintloko, yaye abananyaniso kubo! Bade babonise iimpawu zabo koko nikubiza ngokuba zizindlu zonqulo, apho banigculela khona ekuhleni. Yivani umzekeliso endinawo kuni, kuba uyinyaniso yaye uya kuzisa ukukhanyiselwa kokwenene emiphefumlweni yenu: Umntu olitshijolo, umntu okhohlakeleyo, uhamba ngomlomo ogwenxa. Uqhwanyaza ngamehlo akhe, uthetha ngeenyawo zakhe, ufundisa ngeminwe yakhe.

Ubugwenxa busentliziyweni yakhe, uceba ububi ngalo lonke ixesha, uhlwayela iyantlukwano. Kwaye banjalo kwaye benza njalo phambi kwamehlo enu, kodwa aninakubona, kuba nimfanyekiswe ziimfundiso zabo zobuxoki neendlela ezigwenxa.

Bantwana bam, phumani phakathi kwabo nahlukane nabo; kuba luluphi na ulwalamano analo uKrestu noBheliyali. Ningabi nabudlelane nemisebenzi yobumnyama engenasiqhamo, yaye zahlukaniseni ngokupheleleyo nabo. Kuba nina, cawa yam yaseLawodike, nkqu nani nidikidiki yaye aninamqolo, yaye ezi asizondlela zam nakancinane, kuba ndabagxotha abatshintshi bemali eTempileni ngenzondelelo yam ngoYahweh. Buyelani kum, uYesu Krestu, umalusi wokwenene nogqirha wemiphefumlo yenu, nize nifunde ngam. Kuba izivumo ezakhayo neengcinga ezakhayo ezingenaxabiso asizozam, kodwa bubuxoki obusebezelwa kuni ngabapostile bobuxoki, kwa bona abalungiseleli bakaSathana abaziguqule baba ngabalungiseleli bobulungisa. Balekelani kum, bantwanana bam, kuba ixesha liphelile, yaye nina ngokungazi nicinga ukuba ninalo lonke ixesha ehlabathini, yaye nina ninemizuzwana nje embalwa yokuguquka nize nibuyele kum ngokuthobeka ukuze ndisenokunibekela indawo etafileni yam nokuze ndisenokunivulela umnyango emazulwini ukuze ningene kulonwabo lweNkosi yenu!

Kuba uninzi lwenu lucinga ukuba luya kuqokelelwa kum xa luphila obu buxoki naxa lusitya ubumdaka abanondla ngabo abaprofeti bobuxoki, ngokunyelisa egameni lam. Yazini ukuba andibathumanga yaye amazwi abo asingawo awam, yaye ukuba anahlukani nabo, kuya kufuneka nizibonakalise kum kuvavanyo olukhulu novavanyo ngexesha lembandezelo kaYakobi, efikele ehlabathini lonke yaye isondele ekukhululweni! Khangelani kum emoyeni wenu; gxilani, kuba niyayiva imihelo emoyeni wenu, kuba ndinibizela ekhaya yaye uMtshakazi wam sele eza kuzala. Yabonani, ndinishiyela ileta endayinithumela yona, nina baseLawodike, kuba kula mazwi kukhanyiswe indlela yokubuyela kum; yaye ukuba nithe naphulaphula, ndiya kunibuyisela umvuzo wenu, yaye niya kudla nam etafileni yam emazulwini endiyilungiselele uMtshakazi wam.

Nakwisithunywa sebandla eliseLawodike, bhala uthi: Utsho (uAmen) ingqina elithembekileyo neliyinyaniso, isiqalo sezidalwa zikaThixo. Ndiyayazi imisebenzi yakho, ukuba akubandi yaye akushushu. Ndingathanda ukuba ububanda okanye ushushu. Ngoko ke ngenxa yokuba uphakathi kwazo zombini, yaye akubandi yaye akushushu. Ndiya kukukhafula emlonyeni wam: kuba usisityebi yaye wandile ngeempahla, yaye akuswele nto, yaye akwazi ukuba ulusizi yaye uhlwempuzekile, umbi, uyimfama yaye uhamba ze. Ndikucebisa ukuba uthenge kum igolide evavanywe emlilweni, ukuze ube sisityebi, nesambatho esimhlophe, ukuze wambathiswe, ukuze ubunqunu bakho obumdaka bungabonakali, uze uthambise amehlo akho ngesithambiso samehlo, ukuze ubone. Bonke endibathandayo, ndiyabakhalimela ndize ndibohlwaye. Ngoko ke, shisekela, uze uguquke. Yabona, ndimi emnyango ndiyankqonkqoza. Ukuba umntu uthe weva ilizwi lam waza wavula umnyango, ndiya kungena kuye, ndidle naye, naye adle nam. Lowo woyisayo ndiya kumnika ukuba ahlale nam esihlalweni sam, njengoko nam ndoyisayo ndaza ndahlala noBawo, esihlalweni sakhe.

Yabonani!

Ndingulowo uhamba phakathi kweziphatho zezibane ezisixhenxe, ngoko buyelani kum, hleze ndisuse isiphatho sakho sesibane kuwe.

Ndingumalusi olungileyo owazinikela ngenxa yomhlambi.

Ndingulowo uya kulawula umhlaba kwitrone kaDavide aze azibethe iintlanga ngentonga yentsimbi.

Ndingulowo ufanelekileyo ukuba athathe incwadi aze aqhawule amatywina.

Ndingulowo uyalela isithunywa esinyuka empumalanga, sinetywina likaThixo ophilayo, kuba ziya kutywina abayi-144,000 bam, kwa abakhonzi bakaThixo ophilayo.

Ndingulowo uya kuma eNtabeni yeZiyon nabayi-144,000 bam, yaye baya kucula ingoma entsha ekungekho mntu unokuyifunda.

NdinguNkosi yeeNkosi noKumkani wooKumkani, yaye ndiza kulwa nookumkani bomhlaba, kuba ixesha labo lifikile.

Ndingulowo uzayo kuMtshakazi wam, kuba ukubonakala kwam kusondele. Zilungiseleleni, Mtshakazi wam!
