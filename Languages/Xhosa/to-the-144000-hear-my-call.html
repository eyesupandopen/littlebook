Kuma-144,000: Yivani Ubizo Lwam!

O yivani, yivani, zizwe zam ezilishumi elinambini, ilizwi loMesiya wenu, iNkosi, uKumkani noMbingeleli Omkhulu, mna lo, uYeshua HaMashiach, uNyana woMntu awamvusayo uYahweh kwabafileyo. Kuba:

NdinguMesiya wenu wokwenene, ovela kwitrone kaDavide, kwaye ndiza kulawula iLizwe leDinga ndisetrone yakhe ngenxa kaYahweh, uThixo ongunaphakade, kuba wathembisa ooyihlo ngolo hlobo. Ngoko ke qondani ukuba ndiyiNgonyama yeSizwe sakwaYuda, kwaye akukho kumkani okanye mprofeti unikweyo ovela kwaDan, apho kuvela khona umprofeti wobuxoki.

NdiyiNkosi yenu, kuba uYahweh undenze iNkosi yeeNkosi, kwaye ndinithengile ngegazi lam njengePasika yenu egqibeleleyo, endayinikela ngenxa yezono zeeNtlanga namaYuda ngokufanayo, ukuze nobabini nibe mntu mnye omtsha kum, uKrestu Yesu. Kuba uYahweh undinike igama elingaphezu kwawo onke amagama waza wandenza ndabelane naye ngeTrone yakhe ngenxa yokuthobela kwam. Ngoko ke ubuNkosi bam bunikwe njengesibane sosindiso, kuba ndenziwe umoya odlisayo, kwaye nguYahweh kuphela omkhulu kunam, kuba unguThixo ongunaphakade.

NdinguKumkani wenu, kuba uYahweh undenze uKumkani wooKumkani, kwaye ndiza kulawula umhlaba ngobulungisa. Yabonani! Ndizisa uMtshakazi wam, oza kulawula nam, kuba ndibenze ookumkani nababingeleli phambi koYahweh ongunaphakade, ekungekho namnye onjengaye. Kuba kulawulo lwam lwewaka leminyaka oluthenjisiweyo, ndiza kuba ngoyena unegunya eliphezulu, kwaye ndiza kuthumela ookumkani bam nababingeleli bam ukuba balawule kwilifa labo elikum; kuba ndibadale ngokutsha kum, ndibathathile kumaYuda nakwiiNtlanga ngokufanayo ndabenza umntu omnye omtsha, ndabadala ngokutsha kugqibelelo. Ndimnye ngobunye nangenjongo noBawo, uYahweh, kwaye kwiTrone yakhe mkhulu kunam.

Ndingumbingeleli wenu omkhulu ngonaphakade ngokohlobo lukaMelkitsedeke, kwaye ndanikela ngegazi lam elihlawulelayo njengedini xa ndazinikela njengePasika yenu egqibeleleyo nefezekileyo. Kuba amaHebhere anikela ubungqina bawo xa ndacela uMpostile uPawulos ukuba athethe nawo egameni lam (wayengowakwaBhenjamin): Kobeka phi na ke ukugqithisela kokuhle igazi likaKrestu, owathi ngoMoya ongunaphakade wazinikela kuThixo engenabala, ukuzihlambulula izazela zenu emisebenzini efileyo, ukuze nikhonze uThixo ophilileyo? Ngoko ke idini lam ngam ngokwam lelokukanye ngonaphakade, kwaye akukho Mesiya wumbi.

Nangona indlu yesikhungu kaSathana iza kwamkela umesiya wabo wobuxoki nomprofeti wabo wobuxoki okwangumakhi wetempile olirhamncwa (kuba ulirhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni). Kuba nina niyitempile kaThixo ophilileyo, kwaye akusekho mfuneko yadini lezilwanyana kwakhona, kuba ndilidini lokugqibela, iPasika egqibeleleyo, engenasono, kwaye imisebenzi yam igqibelele kwaye ifezekile. Ngoko ke, akusekho mfuneko yanto enjalo kwakhona, kodwa kuphela kukuba nize kum nezono zenu niguqukile, kwaye ndiza kunihlambulula kwimisebenzi yenu efileyo yenyama. Kuba kwabaseRoma kubhaliwe kwathiwa: Kuba umthetho woMoya wobomi obukuKrestu Yesu undikhulule emthethweni wesono nokufa. Kuba ndinikhulule ngolo hlobo kum.

Qondani, zizwe zam nabantu bam, ukuba amaxesha eeNtlanga ayaphela. Amaxesha eeNtlanga enzeka ngenxa yobulukhuni beentliziyo zooyihlo. Kuba wathini uPawulos kwabaseZenzo ngalo mbandela: Kwakuyimfuneko ukuba ilizwi likaThixo lithethwe kuni kuqala; kodwa ekubeni nilityhalela kude, nizigweba njengabangafanelekanga bubomi obungunaphakade, yabona, sijikela kwiiNtlanga.

Ndiyanicebisa, bantu bam, musani ukwenza iimpazamo ezifanayo kwaye musani ukuphila kwizono zooyihlo, kodwa guqukani kwizono zenu niguquke nakwizono zooyihlo, nize kum kwaye ndiza kunamkela, kuba ningabantwana bedinga. Kwaye yonke iyod okanye isuntswana leyod liza kuzalisekiswa; kuba oko kuthenjiswe nguYahweh akunakwenzeka ukuba kutshintshwe, kuba akunakwenzeka ukuba uYahweh axoke. Ngoko ke, ndiyinzaliseko yezithembiso ezininzi, kuba ndinguMesiya wenu wokwenene, umfanekiso kaThixo ongunaphakade. Kuba ndingunyana wakhe ekuphela kwamzeleyo, iMvana kaThixo awayichazayo uYohane uMbhaptizi ngonaphakade. Kuba wathi: Yabonani iMvana kaThixo, esusa isono sehlabathi!

Ngoko ke ndajikela kwiintlanga kuba ilizwe lam landala, nasemva kokuba ndifele izono zabo ndaza ndavuswa. Kuba ndathumela abapostile bam nabaprofeti bam kubo, kwaye abaninzi basindiswa. Kuba ekuqaleni kwexesha lofefe, ndabathumela eTempileni nakwiindlu zesikhungu, kwaye abaninzi bajikela kum baza bandamkela njengeNkosi yabo. Ngoko ke ndiyanibizela ukuba niphiliswe ebuNkosini bam, kuba ndinikhululele kuBawo wam ukuze nani nibe ngabantwana baKhe ngomoya wam endiza kunitywina ngawo. Ngoko ke amaxesha eeNtlanga aphelile, kuba ngoku nabo bandalile bakhetha omnye uYesu abamnqulayo njengothixo, ngaloo ndlela bezenza abanquli-zithixo; kuba uSathana wabalinga ngolo hlobo. Kuba mnye kuphela uThixo, uYahweh ongunaphakade, kwaye mnye kuphela uMesiya wokwenene, mna lo, uYeshua HaMashiach.

Kuba balahlekelwa ziimfihlelo zam, abayihoya imibono yam neenjongo zesibhalo sam esingcwele. Balahlekelwa ngumkhondo wesicwangciso sikaBawo sokuhlangula, kwaye ngokuqhayisa bayawala amazwi am endawabhala kwabaseRoma. Kuba uPawulos wathi: Ndithi ke, UThixo ubahlahlele na abantu bakhe? Nakanye! Kuba nam ndingumSirayeli, wembewu ka-Abraham, wesizwe sakwaBhenjamin. UThixo akabahlahlelanga abantu bakhe, ababazi ngaphambili. Kwaye kwakhona: Yintoni ke? Oko akufunayo uSirayeli akakufumananga; kodwa abanyuliweyo bakufumene, baze abanye benziwa iimfama. Ngoko ke unyulo lwaluluqinisekile kwabo ndibanyulileyo phakathi kwezizwe; kuba abapostile bam bonke babelizwe nedinga, ngoko ke ndabathumela kwiintlanga, apho iinkokeli zenu zonqulo zinyelisa ngobuxoki, zibabiza ngobuxoki. Kwanezo nkokeli zobuxoki nabalusi abangenaxabiso bayakukhanyela ukuvuswa kwam, kuba ndivusiwe kwabafileyo, kwaye andisayi kuphinda ndife ngonaphakade.

Kuba yintoni enye ethethwa ngabaseRoma ngalo mbandela: UDavide uthi, Isithebe sabo masibe sisibatha, nomgibe, nesikhubekiso, nomvuzo kubo: Amehlo abo makabe mnyama ukuze bangaboni, nomhlana wabo uwugobe ngonaphakade. Ndithi ke, Bakhubekile na ukuze bawe? Nakanye! Kodwa ngokuwa kwabo usindiso lufikile kwiiNtlanga, ukuze zibakhweletise.

Kuba andithandi, bazalwana, ukuba ningayazi le mfihlelo, hleze nibe nobulumko ngokwenu; ukuba ubumfama ngenxalenye buhlele uSirayeli, kude kungene inzaliseko yeeNtlanga. Ngoko ke ndiyavakalisa ukuba inzaliseko yeeNtlanga ifikile, kwaye iimfihlelo zikaThixo ziza kubuyiselwa kuSirayeli ngenxa yokuthobela kokholo. Kuba ndisaza kumsindisa uYakobi, kwaye intsalela ethembekileyo iza kujikela kum ngokuthembeka, ngokholo olukhulu!

Ngoko ke ndize kubangela umona ezintliziyweni zenu ngam, uYeshua HaMashiach, nangoYahweh uThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade owandithumayo, eningasamnquliyo ngokwazi, kuba isigqubuthelo sobumfama siza kugubungela nabani na olandela umthetho kaMoses. Kuba ndathini kwabaseKorinte ngalo mbandela: AsinjengoMoses, owasibekayo isigqubuthelo ebusweni bakhe, ukuze abantwana bakwaSirayeli bangakwazi ukujonga ngokuqinileyo esiphelweni sento epheliswayo: Kodwa iingqondo zabo zenziwa iimfama: kuba kude kube namhlanje kusekho isigqubuthelo esifanayo esingasuswanga ekufundweni kweTestamente eNdala; eso sigqubuthelo sisuswa kuKrestu. Kodwa kude kube namhlanje, xa kufundwa uMoses, isigqubuthelo sisentliziyweni yabo.

Noko ke, xa ithe yajikela eNkosini, isigqubuthelo siya kususwa.

Kuba ndiwuzalisekisile umthetho kaMoses, kuba nina nooyihlo anikwazi ukuwuthobela ngokugqibeleleyo ngenxa yesono esisegazini lenu. Kuba mna, uYeshua HaMashiach, ndiwuzalisekisile umthetho ngokupheleleyo, kwaye umthetho ugcinwa ngokugqibeleleyo kuphela ngokholo kum, uYesu Krestu. Nibalelwa ubulungisa ngokuzifanisa nam ngokupheleleyo, ngoko ke olilungisa uya kuphila ngokholo. Ngoko ke ndiza kusisusa isigqubuthelo ezintliziyweni zenu ukuze nindibone mna, uYeshua, uMesiya wenu wokwenene, kuba andinilahlanga! Kwaye ndiza kuni kumnqophiso omtsha, kungekhona kubudala boncwadi. Kuba ndiwuqinisekisile umnqophiso wam omtsha ngegazi lam, ngokwentando nezithembiso zikaYahweh, ngenxa yenkululeko yenu epheleleyo. Kwaye ndiza kunikhokelela kwilifa lenu, kuba izithembiso zikaThixo ngu-ewe no-amen. Akukho neyod okanye isuntswana leyod eliya kusilela, kuba konke sekuzakuzalisekiswa; kwaye ngexesha elifanelekileyo, inene, ndiza kuzalisekisa konke, kuba ndithunywe nguYahweh ukuba ndikwenze oko.

Ngoko ke iiNtlanga zijikile kum njengenja ebuyela emhlanzweni wayo, kwaye ixesha lifikile lokuba ndiziyekele kuko. Kuba kwixesha lam lofefe, akukho namnye umntu uBawo amnyulele usindiso oshiyekileyo, kwaye akukho namnye onokubaxutha esandleni sam. Kuba abo bandivumayo mna, uYesu, njengeNkosi yabo, benokholo ezintliziyweni zabo lokuba uThixo wandivusa kwabafileyo, batywinwa ngomoya oyingcwele wesithembiso, kwaye iinjongo zikaBawo zizalisekisiwe kum ngenxa yabo. Ngoko ke baya kuhlala benam ngonaphakade.

Kuba umnquma utyhila inyaniso. Kwaye ndathini malunga nomnquma kwileta kaPawulos eya kwabaseRoma: Ukuba amanye amasebe aphuliwe, waza wena, ungumnquma wasendle, wamiliselwa phakathi kwawo, waza wabelana nawo ngengcambu nokutyeba komnquma; Musa ukuzigwagwisa ngamasebe. Kodwa ukuba uyazigwagwisa, akuwuthwali wena ingcambu, kodwa ingcambu ithwele wena.

Ngoko ke benze iimpazamo baza bathatha izono zooyihlo baza bajika kum baya kwizithixo zabo, kwaye abasayi kuphinda balive ilizwi lam. Nangona ukuba bathe balibiza igama leNkosi, baya kusindiswa abo bangabathandiyo ubomi babo kude kube sekupheleni.

Kuba yintoni enye ethethwa bubungqina bamaRoma esibhalweni njengesilumkiso kwiintlanga: Uya kuthi ke, Amasebe aphuliwe, ukuze mna ndimiliselwe. Kulungile; ngenxa yokungakholwa aphuliwe, kwaye wena umi ngokholo. Musa ukuziphakamisa, kodwa yoyika: Kuba ukuba uThixo akawaconganga amasebe endalo, lumka hleze angakusindisi nawe. Yabona ke ububele nobukhali bukaThixo: kwabo bawayo, ubukhali; kodwa kuwe, ububele, ukuba uqhubeka ebubeleni bakhe: kungenjalo nawe uya kunqunyulwa. Kwaye nabo, ukuba abahlali ekungakholweni, baya kumiliselwa: kuba uThixo unamandla okubamilisela kwakhona. Kuba ukuba wena unqunyulwe kumnquma wasendle ngokwendalo, waza wamiliselwa ngokuchasene nendalo kumnquma olungileyo: kobeka phi na ke ukugqithisela kokuba aba, abangamasebe endalo, bamiliselwe kumnquma wabo?

Kuba andithandi, bazalwana, ukuba ningayazi le mfihlelo, hleze nibe nobulumko ngokwenu; ukuba ubumfama ngenxalenye buhlele uSirayeli, kude kungene inzaliseko yeeNtlanga. Ngoko ke lonke uSirayeli uya kusindiswa: njengoko kubhaliwe, Kuya kuphuma eZiyon uMhlanguli, aze asuse ukungahloneli Thixo kuYakobi.

Ngoko ke, zihlobo zam, amaxesha eeNtlanga ayaphela, kwaye utshintsho olukhulu luyenzeka, apho ufefe lwam luza kujika lube ngumgwebo ngoko nangoko. Kwaye nangoku iziqwenga zivunyelwe ukuba zibekwe endaweni yazo ngesandla sikaSathana, njengoko eza kuma, nangona eza kutyunyuzwa ngokupheleleyo, kwaye ndiza kubuchitha ngokupheleleyo ubukumkani bakhe; kuba akangokumkani.

Ngoko ke ngoku kufike ixesha lokuxhunyelelwa kwamasebe endalo kumthi womnquma kwakhona, kuba iinjongo zikaYahweh zizalisekisiwe kubabalo nenceba ebingafanelekanga eyaboniswa kwiiNtlanga (kuba ubabalo nenceba kaThixo azifanelekanga kuye nawuphi na umntu), kuba kwanolo babalo nenceba zasetshenziselwa ukufumana iigusha ezilahlekileyo zendlu kaSirayeli. Kuba anifanelanga ukuba ningayazi imbali yenu, ukuba nithe nazibeka phantsi iiTalmud zenu zaseBhabhiloni ezinyelisa igama likaThixo naza nafunda ngam; kuba andiyithumelanga loo ncwadi, kwaye kufuneka niguquke kwezo mfundiso nize nizilahle iinkohliso zikaSathana. Buyelani kwilizwi lam elinyulu likaThixo nimke ezonweni zooyihlo, kuba ndiya kunihlawulela ukuba nithe naliva ilizwi lam.

Kuba andithunyelwanga mntu ngaphandle kwiigusha ezilahlekileyo zendlu kaSirayeli. Zaye ke izizwe ezilishumi zachithachitheka phakathi kweentlanga ukusukela ekuthinjweni kwazo ngama-Asiriya kulo lonke eli xesha lidlulileyo. Ziye zazulazula zaza zabhadula emhlabeni njengabahambi nabaphambukeli. Ukusuka e-Asiriya zaya kumazwe asemantla, eSitiya, naseYurophu, zaza ekugqibeleni zaya kwelaseMelika, apho ndazenzela ikhaya elinoxolo okwethutyana. Ndaza ndabiza bonke abanokuvela ezintlangeni ukuba beze elusindisweni lwam. KwanamaSirayeli amaninzi asindisiwe aza ahlangulwa kunye nabaninzi abavela ezintlangeni, ababengayi kuba nalo ithemba. Kwaye isihlwele siye sasabela, ngoko ke amaxesha eeNtlanga aphelile, kuba alisekho ixesha. Kodwa qondani ukuba bonke abanqula igama leNkosi baya kusindiswa ukuba bathembekile kwada kwaba sekupheleni, kuba ndiya kubamkela kungakhathaliseki ukuba baphuma kwesiphi isizwe.

Kunjalo ke, ukuba inkathazo kaYakobi iyehla phezu komhlaba neentlanga, kuba lixesha lokuba umgwebo omkhulu wehle phezu komhlaba. Zaye ke iinyamakazi ngamandla enamba ziya kuphakama; kodwa musani ukuthatha uphawu lwayo kwaye ningaweli kwiinkohliso zayo. Kuba sele izithandekisile kubaphathi besizwe senu, kwanakwindlu yesikhungu kaSathana ngeendlela zayo zama-Edom. Kwaye sele ithetha unyeliso olukhulu, kuba ilungiselelwa injongo yayo yokwenene.

Kuba isikizi lesiphanziso liyeza. Kuba yintoni endayenza ukuba uLuka wam othembekileyo ayibhalele onke amaxesha: Xa nithe nayibona iYerusalem ingqongwe yimikhosi, yazini ke ukuba isiphanziso sayo sikufuphi. Ngelo xesha abo baseYuda mababalekele ezintabeni; nabo baphakathi kwayo mabaphume; nabo basemaphandleni mabangangeni kuyo. Kuba le yimihla yempindezelo, ukuze zonke izinto ezibhaliweyo zizaliseke.

Kwaye kuya kuba njalo ngexesha elifanelekileyo, kwaye ndiya kubakhusela abam, kuba ndinithuma njengabaprofeti bam, abalusi, abaphilisi, nabalungiseleli. Kuba ndinibizela enkonzweni, kwanokuzalisekisa ubizo lwenu lokwenene enalunikezwa ngooyihlo ngesithembiso nguYahweh ngokwakhe, kwaye ngesandla sam ziya kuzalisekiswa. Kuba uYahweh ubeke isihlalo sam sobukhosi etroneni yaKhe, kwaye ndabelane ngetrone yam noMtshakazi wam, abahleli benam. Ngoko ke akukho mkhulu kunam emhlabeni; akukho namnye kubaprofeti benu bobuxoki onokuthelekiswa nam, kuba bonke babuthathaka, bayahlwempuzekile kwaye bathunyelwe ukukhohlisa nokulahlekisa, kuba bangabalusi abangenaxabiso.

Yazini, bantu bam, ukuba utshaba lunibekele imigibe emininzi, ngoko landelani ukhokelo lwam kwaye niya kuyiphepha yonke. Kuba kungekudala zonke iintlobo zolonwabo ziya kususwa emhlabeni. Abaninzi abayi kufumana nkqu neqhekeza, kwaye abayi kuba nayo netafile abanokutyela kuyo kodwa baya kuba ziimbacu emhlabeni ngenxa yokholo lwabo kum.

Musani ukuzivumela ukuba nihendwe yimana yabo yobuxoki eya kufuna uphawu lwenyamakazi ukuze nifumane ukungena kuluntu lwabo olugwenxa. Kuba uphawu aluyi kuxolelwa, kwaye bonke abawela kulo mgibe abayi kukhululwa; kuba lusisigxina kubo bonke abenza olo khetho lokugqibela, kuba baya kuba batywine ikamva labo kwaye elo kamva lelokugqibela. Ngoko musani ukuhendwa sisibambiso, kungenjalo niya kuphelela emnatheni nize nibanjiswe ngokusisigxina emhlathini wenu.

Ndinithuma ukuba niqokelele umhlambi wam, kwaye ndiya kunikhokelela kuwo ndize ndiwukhokelele kuni, kwaye niya kuwalusela mna, kuba ndingumalusi oyintloko. Aniyi kulandula olunye uhlanga okanye ilizwe okanye nasiphi na esinye isizwe, kuba isithembiso sikaYahweh simi, kwaye bonke abanqula igama leNkosi baya kusindiswa. Musani ukuba neentamo ezilukhuni njengooyihlo, kwaye nithobeke kwilizwi lam. Kuba ooyihlo bandala, ngoko vumani izono zooyihlo nezakho ngenguquko kum, kwaye ndiya kunixolela ndize ndinigalele uthando lwam nobabalo lwam, kuba ndiya kwenza imilambo yamanzi aphilayo iphume esiswini senu. Ndinixhobisile ukuba nenze imimangaliso emikhulu. Kwaye ndinithuma kubazalwana benu ukuba nibajikele kum, kuba intsalela isaza kusindiswa; kwaye ndiya kusindisa uYakobi esandleni seentshaba zabo, kuba nditshilo kwaye kuya kuzalisekiswa.

Mna, uYeshua HaMashiach uYahweh amthumileyo, ndinguye uMesiya wokwenene kaSirayeli nehlabathi. Kwanam ndiya kuthumela abaprofeti bam ababini, kwanabo uYahweh wabaxwilayo emhlabeni ngamaxesha abo: bathathwa ngenjongo enjalo, kwaye baya kuthunyelwa ngokunjalo. Ndinezinto ezininzi endifuna ukuzithetha kuni kwaye ezo zinto ziya kuthethwa, kwaye ndiya kunilungiselela imisebenzi yenu, kwaye niya kundenzela imisebenzi emikhulu nenamandla, kwanemisebenzi eningazange nicinge ukuba inokwenzeka. Kuba amaxesha amabi kakhulu ahlala evelisa amadoda amakhulu kaYahweh, kwanamadoda angenakwaphulwa, kuba niya kuphumla emandleni am, kwaye andinakwaphulwa. Kuba uYahweh undenze njalo, kwaye nam ndiya kunenza njalo. Ngoko ke yivani ilizwi lam, kuba ndiyabiza kwaye ndiya kuthumela iingelosi zam ukuba zitywine i-144,000 ngetywina likaYahweh.

Ndiya kunigcina nikhuselekile kwinyamakazi nesikizi layo; ndiya kunifihla, kwaye niya kondliwa ngamazulu kwanaxa nifuna, ngoko musani ukoyika. Musani ukoyikela ubomi benu, kuba umvuzo omkhulu ngowenu ngenkonzo nokuthembeka, kwaye ndiya kunivuza ngokuma kwenu.

Ndingulowo uya kutywina i-12,000 kwisizwe ngasinye ngaphandle kwesakwaDan nesakwaEfrayim.

Ndingulowo uya kutyumza ithole lengonyama eliya kuvela kwaDan.

Ndingulowo uya koyisa inyamakazi nomprofeti wobuxoki, kwaye ndiya kubaphosa bephila kwichibi elikhulu lomlilo.

Ndingulowo uya kukhokelela abantu bam kumadlelo aluhlaza ngabalusi bam, kuba ndingumalusi oyintloko.

Ndingulowo uya kuzalisekisa izithembiso ezenziwa koobawo, kuba ndithunyelwe ngenxa yoko.

Ndingulowo uya kutyumza intloko yeeSathana, kuba iinkosana zalo mhlaba ziya kuhlawula.

Ndingulowo uya kulawula etroneni kaDavide, kuba ubukumkani bam abunakuphela.

Ndingulowo uzayo emafini, kwaye ndiya kuza kwisizwe sam ndize ndithathe ulawulo lwam, kwaye andiyi kunqandwa.

Mna, ewe mna, Yeshua HaMashiach, ndithumele la mazwi kwabo ndibabizayo. Ngoko ke yivani amazwi am ngokuthobela, nize nazi ukuba ndiya kuwukhulula umhlaba nomhlaba wonke kwaye ndiya kulawula ngobulungisa. Ngoko ke yivani ubizo lwam, kuba ndiyeza kuni kwaye ndiya kunifundisa iindlela zam kwaye ndiya kunikhokelela kuyo yonke inyaniso. Ngoko musani ukuyala imibhalo yam, kuba iimfundiso zikaThixo ziya kubuyiselwa kuSirayeli, kwaye umhlaba uya kwazi ukuba ukho uThixo kwaSirayeli, uYahweh, uThixo omkhulu ongunaphakade, kuba akukho wumbi, kwaye akukho unjengaYe. Shalom! Shalom! Ngoko ke hlalani eluxolweni niphile, kuba ndiya kuniphilisa nonke ngokupheleleyo nina enibeka ukholo lwenu kuYeshua HaMashiach.
