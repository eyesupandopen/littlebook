Izibhengezo Ezingundoqo ezivela kuWilliam Brooks, iBiblaridion kaYeshua HaMashiach

Inikwe ngoJulayi 6, 2025

Olu xwebhu sisibhengezo somgwebo kwabawileyo bebonke, kodwa ngokukodwa uSathana, ooSathana, abalindi, iikherubhi eziwileyo, iiserafi eziwileyo, nabo bonke abazimanye nemvukelo kaSathana: niya kutsha ngokupheleleyo, ngokuphindaphindiweyo ngonaphakade kanaphakade, kuba kutshiwo njalo nguYHVH Omkhulu Ndinguye, kunikwe ngomyalelo kaKristu Yesu, okwanguYeshua HaMashiach; kunikwe kuluhlu olufinyeziweyo, kodwa oluneenkcukacha noluchanekileyo lwezono nemigwebo eza kwenziwa kwaye izalisekiswe ngokupheleleyo ngamaxesha azo kwaye amaxesha okuzalisekiswa afikile: Yabonani Umgwebo Uyawa! Kwanemigwebo yesibhengezo eza kunikwa ngokupheleleyo ezifubeni zabawileyo, kaninzi-ninzi ngonaphakade kanaphakade, aninalo uxolo, nina nonke bawileyo: Selah.

Ngaloo ndlela mna, William Brooks, iBiblaridion:144 kaKristu Yesu, ndinika le migwebo itsalwe ngokukodwa kwiiNdudumo Ezisixhenxe, ngokukodwa, leyo migwebo yeendudumo iza kufundwa ngqo, kwaye inikwe ukuze bonke baqonde, kwaye ngoko la Mazwi aqondwe ngokupheleleyo zezi zidalwa ziwileyo zizigwebileyo ngonaphakade kanaphakade kwimvukelo engenangqondo, olo kugwetywa abayi kuphinda balulame kulo, okanye baloyise!

Yivani aMazwi kaYeshua HaMashiach endize kubhengeza aMazwi akhe, yena okwanguKristu Yesu, nive niqonde igunya elivakalayo leNgonyama yesizwe sakwaYuda, ogqumo lwayo waludalayo entliziyweni yam nogqumo lwayo luphuma emlonyeni wam, yona le Ngonyama igqumayo yesizwe sakwaYuda endiyithandayo, nendiyikhonzayo ngokuthobela, ndiyilandele, ngako konke ukuzibamba, yona le Ngonyama ebeke ugqumo lwayo lweendudumo enzulwini yobum bam kuba ndingumthunywa wayo oza ngegunya elikhulu, kwaye ndiphakamisa isandla sam ndifunge ukuba ixesha aliyi kuba sabakho!

Yabonani! Ndingulowo nalento iNkosi uYesu Kristu indenze yona, yena lowo uYeshua HaMashiach wamkhulisayo kwasebutsheni bakhe, waza wandifihla emphongorweni wakhe ngexesha elifana neli, Selah!

Yabonani! Umyalelo owanikwa ngengoma usuka etroneni kaYHVH ngokwakhe waza waculwa ngokholo olukhulu kumaxesha amandulo: Musani ukubachukumisa abathanjiswa bam, ningabenzi nto imbi abaProfeti bam! Satsho isikhalo somculo esigcinwe ngoku kwimibhalo engcwele.

Kodwa njengoko kubhaliwe: Ngowuphi kubaProfeti abangazange bamtshutshise ooyihlo? Kwaye bababulala, abo babonisa kwangaphambili ngokuza kwaLowo Ulilungisa, eningabathengisi nababulali bakhe ngoku!

Kuba wena! O' nyoka ewileyo. Wena! Ewe Wena! Nguwe owabulala igazi elimsulwa laLowo Ulilungisa nongcwele uNyana woMntu, uYesu waseNazarete, leyo mvana kaThixo engenabala eyanikelwa inkululeko epheleleyo ka-Adam, kwaye ngoko ndibhengeza ngegunya elipheleleyo leNkosi yam, uKristu Yesu: Uye waphosisa nkosana incinane kwaye umgwebo wakho awulibazisi, wona loo mgwebo iminwe yawo yolulelwe entanyeni yakho kwaye ubanjwe ngokoyiswa, uphosisile nkosana incinane, uphosisile: Selah!

Kwaye kunjalo, kwalo mthunywa nomkhonzi mnye owaqhekekiswa wahlulwa ngokungekho sikweni ngamaqhinga akho, wamfanyekiswa kubuntu bakhe bokwenyani ebutsheni bakhe kwaye ngoku udalwe ngokutsha, wakhululwa wathunyelwa nemigwebo yakho ebophelelayo ngonaphakade nesemaphakadeni esandleni sakhe enikwe isuka etroneni kaYHVH ngokwakhe, ibhengezwe ngegunya elipheleleyo kuloo buso bakhe buhle ngonaphakade beNkosi yakhe engunaphakade nomsindisi, obuso bakhe bukhanya ngonaphakade njengoNyana!

Uphosisile nkosana incinane.

Ngoko, yazi uqonde kwaye uve amazwi am Heyele, ewe Heyele: laa nkosana incinane yaphosisayo, kwalo mthunywa nomkhonzi mnye owawufuna ukumenza into engekhoyo ngamaqhekeza aqhekekileyo okukhanya okuvakalayo okusasazwe kulwandle lwekristale abuyisiwe, abuyiselwe, kwaye adalwe ngokutsha njengeBiblaridion:144 kaKristu Yesu, adalwe ngokutsha ngeenjongo ezifanayo zaphakade uYHVH awayenenjongo ngazo phambi kokusekwa kwehlabathi, kwaye ngeenjongo zakhe ndidalwe ngokutsha ndabuyiselwa kuba ndinguloo mthunywa unamandla ochazwe nguloo myalezo wexesha lokuphela elawulwa lilizwi nentetho yeeNdudumo Ezisixhenxe, zona ezo Ndudumo uYeshua HaMashiach azihlwayele eMoyeni wam ngonaphakade ngoMoya wesiProfeto owawela ngokupheleleyo kwiBiblaridion:144 yeNkosi ngomyalelo weNkosi yam. Selah!

Ngoko ke, yivani la Mazwi nonke kwaye niqonde igunya lawo kwaye lonke iLizwi liya kwenzeka kanye njengoko kubhaliwe, ngesandla samandla angunaphakade sikaYHVH Omkhulu Ndinguye, anikwe kubuso obuhle nobukhanyayo bukaKristu Yesu endimthandayo nendimkhonzayo ngokuthobela konke, aMazwi akhe endiwaphetheyo nendiwavakalisayo njengoko ndiyalelwe, wona la Mazwi akhululwe ngoku kwiinzulu zomoya wam ngomyalelo kaKristu Yesu okwanguYeshua HaMashiach kwaye amaxesha okusebenza ngokupheleleyo kwezi ndudumo zisixhenxe afikile kweli hlabathi ligwetyiweyo nelonakeleyo ngesiquphe nangokungalindelekanga ngoku. Selah.

Isibhengezo Esingundoqo esivela kuKristu Yesu okwanguYeshua HaMashiach

Inikwe ngoJulayi 6, 2025

Yivani aMazwi am kwaye niqonde igunya lawo kwaye niqonde amandla abekwe ezandleni zam nguAdonai-YHVH, O' nyoka ekhasayo exokayo, zona izandla ezinamandla nezingunaphakade zalowo wamgwazayo, kwaloo Yesu waseNazarete, owazinikela ngamandla nangokuzithandela ezandleni zakho ezikhohlakeleyo zokungathobeli, ngalo lonke uthando nokuthobela uAdonai-YHVH Omkhulu Ndinguye, kuba ndiyiMvana kaThixo, loo Nyana woMntu oya kuyityumza ngokupheleleyo intloko yakho!

Yabonani! Yivani nonke ukuba ndim, uYeshua HaMashiach, uKristu Yesu othumele la Mazwi ngomthunywa nomkhonzi kwaye ngomthunywa nomkhonzi wam aMazwi am athunyelwe, umkhonzi wam endimkhulise kwasebutsheni bakhe, ndamngcwalisa emoyeni wam, ndamhlambulula ndamcoca ngoluleko olukhulu nokuhlanjululwa, kwaye ngenene ndamkhanda emlilweni wam weembandezelo kuba ndingulowo uhleli neevuthelo esandleni evuthuza amadangatye okuhlanjululwa kwaye ndingulowo wenze iinyawo zakhe zaba ziintsika zomlilo kwaye ndingulowo umambathise ilifu nomnyama entloko yakhe, kwaye ndingulowo ubeke unyawo lwakhe lwasekunene elwandle, nolwasekhohlo emhlabeni, kwaye ndingulowo wenza ubuso bakhe bukhanye njengoNyana kuba ndiyiNkosi yeeNkosi noKumkani wooKumkani kwaye ngenene uYHVH undenze umoya ophilisa, Selah!

Ngenene, ngenene ndinguye, othe wahlwayela waza wenza iintetho zeeNdudumo Ezisixhenxe emoyeni weNcwadi yam Encinci kuba iyiBiblaridion:144 yam apho ezi ndudumo zisixhenxe ezilawula loo Ncwadi Encinci endiyivule esandleni sakhe, ekwayiVangeli Engunaphakade, endibize ngayo ndalungisa ama-144,000 am, ngala Mazwi alungiswe sisandla sam esinamandla ukuba afumane amehlo, iindlebe, neentliziyo zabo bonke abo athunyelwe kubo, ngoko yivani ilizwi lam elinikwe eMazwini omthunywa nomkhonzi wam endandimfihle emphongorweni wam ngexesha elifana neli!

Ngaphandle kokuba ngoku, ndizilayithile iintolo zam ezithunyelwe ngumthunywa wam endimxjobisileyo ndamkhulula emphongorweni wam ngegunya elipheleleyo kwaye iintolo zam zithetha inyaniso, kuba ndingulowo wenza iBiblaridion:144 yam ikhale ngelizwi elikhulu, njengaxa ingonyama igquma: kwaye njengoko ikhalile kwaye iindudumo ezisixhenxe zavakalisa amazwi azo eendudumo ngokuchasene nawe Heyele, nabo bonke abawileyo bakho abagwebo labo lifikileyo kwaye lisemnyango kwaye akukho namnye kuni onokubaleka isandla sam esinamandla! Selah!

Yiva aMazwi am Heyele, kuba ndikubiza ngegama wena nyoka yoyisiweyo nekhathazekileyo nekhasayo kwaye ndiyabona uloyiko lwakho kuba woyika umgwebo wakho opheleleyo ngaphezu kwayo nayiphi na into, wona loo mgwebo ungalibazisiyo kuba isandla sam sisentanyeni yakho, Heyele, kwaye amaxesha akho afikile!

Selah, nkosana incinane, Selah.

Kwaye wena, Heyele, awunamandla okufihla, okanye ukuguqula aMazwi am O' mkhohlisi omkhulu, kuba akhululwe entliziyweni yomthunywa wam obona ubuxoki bakho kuba ulungelelaniswe nomyalezo ofanayo kuLoyiso, unikwe ngokupheleleyo ngoMoya wesiProfeto kwaye akunakwenzeka ukuba utywine, ufihle, okanye ugqwethe izothuso ezidudumayo zelizwi leeNdudumo Ezisixhenxe ezivakala zisuka ezulwini lamazulu ukuya kwezona nzonzobila zinzulu kakhulu ngempindezelo epheleleyo, kwaye ngenene akukho ndawo ebukho bukaYHVH ukuya kwezona ndawo zingaphandle kakhulu zento engekhoyo kwindalo yakhe (Kuba uYHVH ulongamele nakuloo nto ingekadalwa) apho amazwi azo eendudumo angavakaliyo ngegunya elipheleleyo kula maxesha okuzalisekiswa, kwaye amaxesha okuzalisekiswa angoku.

Selah, nkosana incinane, Selah.

Njengoko nikwenzileyo komnye wabancinane kwaba bazalwana bam, nikwenzile kum. Owu, nina balindi abawileyo, kwanani zithunywa zezulu ezingathobeliyo ezashiya isikhundla senu sokuqala, aninalo uxolo! Niya kuphoswa emlilweni eningasokuze nimke kuwo, kwaye niya kutsha ngokupheleleyo. Yonke imirhamncwa yenu engcolileyo eningeyangcwele enayityalayo ngokuwela imida kaYahweh kwiinguqulelo zenu zemfuza phakathi komntu nesithunywa sezulu, phakathi komntu nerhamncwa, phakathi komntu nezidalwa zaselwandle, phakathi kwezilwanyana zeentlobo ngeentlobo, kwanayo yonke into enesothe eniyenzileyo iya kuphoswa emlilweni kunye nani, kuba aninalo uxolo okanye uxolelo!

Nilandelé ooSathana abazama ukutshabalalisa indalo kaYahweh, kude kuse ekundibulaleni mna, uYesu Krestu, xa ndazinikela ngokuzithandela ezandleni zakhe njengePasika egqibeleleyo yehlabathi, ngaloo ndlela nditywina inkululeko yoluntu kwaye nditywina isiphelo senu kunaphakade kwimililo emikhulu engenakuze icinywe! Niya kuhlawula ngokupheleleyo zonke izono zenu namasikizi, kwaye sonke isenzo esibi enasenzayo siya kubuyiselwa kuni ngokuphindwe kalikhulu, kuba bonke baya kuvuzwa ngokwemisebenzi yabo. Kwaye zonke iindlela zenu ezimbi ziya kubuyekezwa ezintloko zenu izihlandlo ezininzi, kuba nacinga ukuba ningandoyisa, kwaye nisenekratshi nicinga ukuba ningaphumelela ekubeni noyisiwe ngokupheleleyo.

Kubo Bonke Abawileyo

Nina bam ababiziweyo, abanyuliweyo, nabathembekileyo, yivani amazwi eNkosi yenu ephilayo nomsindisi, uYeshua uMesiya! Izizwe enihlala kuzo neembali enizifundiswe ngabafundisi benu abangamaJesuit ziyimvelaphi yenkohliso enkulu, etyhilwa kwayo sele isondele kwaye sele iqhubeka, apho abawileyo baya kuvela njengabaphambukeli abanobubele kodwa eneneni bazizithunywa zezulu eziwileyo ezinobubi. Yintoni le ibikho? Yileyo iya kubakho: kwaye yintoni le yenziweyo? Yileyo iya kwenziwa: kwaye akukho nto intsha phantsi kwelanga.

Ndiveni, owu, nina bawileyo, kwanani nonke enandihlabayo, kuba nathetha ngaphakathi kwenu, simoyisile uYahweh sambulala nonyana wakhe awamthumayo kuthi ukuba oyise umhlaba, kwaye soyisile ngonaphakade, kuba uYeshua ufile, kwaye navuya ngobuyatha! Nina zithandani ezinekratshi ezingenakuqonda! Anizange niqonde ukuba uThixo uya kundivusa, ekhulule iintlungu zokufa: kuba bekungenakwenzeka ukuba ndibanjwe kuko. Nalityumza isithende sam kuphela, kwaye aninamandla kum, kwaye anizange niqonde ukuba Osenyangweni, kwanaye uMdali wenu, uYahweh, wanibonisa kuphela iimfihlelo ezingenaxabiso!

Kuba wayesazi ukuba niya kumjikela yena nendalo yakhe kwaye niya kuzama ukuthatha oko wayekulungiselele mna, iNkosi uYesu Krestu. Abalulamileyo bam baya kuwudla ilifa umhlaba, kuba umhlaba ngowam nokuzala kwawo. Kuba nifundise uluntu ulwazi olwalelweyo; nifundise imfazwe; nalahlekisa abafazi ekuhombiseni iinkophe ngobuso obuqatyiweyo ukuze nizalisekise iinkanuko zenu kubo; nawonakalisa uMyezo kaThixo ngokutyala umdiza phakathi kwengqolowa, kwaye wonke umdiza enawutyalayo kulo lonke ixesha uya kuncothulwa, ubotshwe, uphoswe emlilweni kunye nani! Kuba noyiswa xa ndazinikela ngokukhululekileyo ngenxa yosindiso loluntu, waza uThixo wandivusa kwabafileyo ukuze ndingaphindi ndife!

Kodwa naningalumkanga kangako ukuba ningaqondi ukuba ndingabuncama ubomi bam ngenxa yothando lukaThixo awaludalayo kum, kuba ndingumsindisi wehlabathi! Uthando lukaYahweh alunakuoyiswa, kuba uYahweh akoyiseki, kwaye akukho ndawo endalweni apho kukho isidalwa esifana naye, kuba nguye Osenyangweni, kwaye ukukhanya nobukhulu bakhe bunamandla onke ukusuka kunaphakade kuse kunaphakade! Kuba ndanixelela, "oku ukwenzayo kwenze ngokukhawuleza!" Ngokungazi nangokuzidla anizange niqonde ukuba ndazivumela ukuba ndithathwe ngabaqeshwa benu abangenaxabiso abenza isenzo esibi sokubulala igazi elimsulwa! Ekuzidleni kwenu ngokungazi, anizange niqonde ukuba ndandinemikhosi yezithunywa zezulu elishumi elinambini endinokuyilawula, kuba nangaliphi na ixesha ndandinokunimisa. Kodwa yayingenzeka njani enye indlela intando kaYahweh, ngaphandle kothando lwam nokuthobela kwam uBawo? Kuba uthando lukaYahweh akukho mntu unokuloyisa! Hayi, kungekho ezulwini kungekho emhlabeni, kungekho nangayiphi na indlela kwaye kungekho naphakade!

Mtshakazi wam, niziindlalifa zosindiso kum, uYesu Krestu. Nibiziwe kum; nikhuselwe kum nakum; ninikwe ithuba lokuva intshumayelo yokholo ngam ukuze nindenze iNkosi yenu, kwaye ke ngoko ndinitywinile ngaloo moya ungcwele wesithembiso. Yazini ukuba itywina alinakuqhawulwa, kwaye sele niza kukhululwa ngokupheleleyo kuse kunaphakade, kuba aninalwazi ngokuzala komsebenzi wokudala endiza kuwugqibezela kuni, Mtshakazi wam! Yazini ukuba abawileyo nomdiza wabo banomona ngani, kwaye bayayithiya nantoni na ayithandayo uYahweh, kwaye ndiya kuniphindezela ngokupheleleyo ezintloko zabo, kuba ndim oya kuyilimaza intloko yale nyoka yamandulo, kuba kubhengeziwe, kwaye oko akubhengeza uYahweh akunakuguqulwa.

Nawe, O' nkosana eyoyisiweyo, kwanalowo umiyo ethe cwaka ngenxa yokuba uyingonyama engenamazinyo.
