Ukuvuka Kwabathathu Abangengcwele!

Ngaloo ndlela ixesha lifikelele esiphelweni yaye ukubonakala kwam kuyasondela! Ngoku ndiza kunixelela ngoluhlu lweziganeko eziza kwenzeka kungekudala, ngoko ke thobelani ukuva ilizwi loMesiya wenu, uYeshua HaMashiach, kwanezwi leNkosi yeeNkosi, uKumkani wooKumkani, uMesiya, nombingeleli omkhulu ngonaphakade ngokohlobo lukaMelkitsedeke. Mhlawumbi ikratshi lenu lesizwe liya kuvala iintliziyo zenu kum, Owu Bhabhiloni Eyimfihlakalo: kwanezi United States. Kuba nicinga ukuba nihleli njengokumkanikazi yaye akukho ntlekele iya kuniwela, nize nithi kuni, Andingomhlolokazi! Kanti intshabalalo yenu iya kufika ngeyure enye yaye anisoze niphinde nivuke kwakhona okanye ubunkokeli benu buphinde buyihluthe umhlaba kaYahweh kwakhona, kuba isahlulo esiphindwe kabini somgwebo sivakaliswe nguYahweh, kwaye oko akutshoyo uYahweh akukho mntu unokukuguqula.

Khawufan' ucinge, ukuba ubunokwenza njalo, ngehlabathi elingenabo ubuKristu nezizwe zamaKristu. Akusayi kubakho Cawa kwaye ubuKristu buya kuba ngokungekho mthethweni kwihlabathi liphela kwaye ixesha leCawa lifikelele esiphelweni, kwane ixesha lam lobabalo elavakaliswa ngabapostile bam nabaprofeti ngenkulungwane yokuqala, yaye batyala imbewu apho nikwaziyo ukufumana indawo yokusabela kula maxesha enkathazo, kwaneLizwi lam elingcwele neemfihlakalo zikaThixo! Kubalulekile ukutolika ezi ziganeko ngolwimi oluchanekileyo olwasetyenziswa nguYohane uMtyhileli, kuba wathi: Ndaye ndema phezu kwentlabathi yolwandle: ndabona irhamncwa liphuma elwandle, lineentloko ezisixhenxe, neempondo ezilishumi, naphezu kweempondo zalo izithsaba ezilishumi, naphezu kweentloko zalo, igama lonyeliso. Kwakhona: Ndabona elinye irhamncwa liphuma emhlabeni, yaye lalineempondo ezimbini njengemvana, yaye lathetha njengenamba. Kwakhona: inamba yalinika amandla ayo, nesihlalo sayo, negunya elikhulu. Kuya kuba njalo ke ukuvuka kwabathathu abangengcwele, irhamncwa eliphuma elwandle, irhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni elikwangumprofeti wobuxoki noMoshiach wobuxoki, nenamba ebanika igunya elikhulu.

Akukho mchasi-Kristu mnye, nangona oomoya bomchasi-Kristu bephumele ehlabathini yaye bekhohlisa abaninzi, kodwa ngamarhamncwa amabini nenamba. Elinye lokulawula ulwandle, okanye iintlanga zeeNtlanga, nelinye lokulawula imihlaba engcwele, eliphuma kwisizwe sakwaDan yaye lingumakhi wetempile olirhamncwa. Irhamncwa eliphuma elwandle liya kulawula iintlanga, okanye amagoyim ngemithetho kaNowa, yaye sele ikhona, yaye yomibini ingumchasi-Kristu ngokwendalo. Umchasi-Kristu uchasene nam, uKristu Yesu kwaye endaweni kaKristu Yesu ngokunjalo. Kuba khangelani! Imfihlakalo yobugwenxa sele iqhubekile kakhulu yaye uSathana ulindele ukumka kwenu, Bandla lam, kwanoMtshakazi wam. Uya kulawula esebenzisa inkolo edibanisayo, edityanisiweyo yaye uya kuthi ungumMahdi wabo. Ukudityaniswa kweenkolo kuya kubamanyanisa, phantsi kobuxoki bokuba zonke iindlela zikhokelela kuthixo omnye. Ngaphandle kokuba, mna Yesu Kristu uMesiya wokwenene andiyi kuvunyelwa, yaye andinayo inxaxheba kubo kwaye intshabalalo yabo ayilibazisi. Ngoko ke ukudityaniswa kweenkolo zabo kuya kukhokelela kuluntu lwabo olulirhamncwa apho uphawu luya kunyanzeliswa ezintlangeni ukuze zithathe inxaxheba kuluntu lwabo. Musani ukuthatha uphawu, kuba nabani na owenzayo uya kwabelana ngechibi lomlilo nerhamncwa nomprofeti wobuxoki. Kwaye bonke abathatha uphawu baya kutshintshwa ngokwemfuza ngonaphakade. Anifundanga na? Kwaye apho wabona intsimbi ixutywe nodongwe oludakayo, baya kuzixuba nembewu yabantu: kodwa abayi kunamathelana, njengokuba intsimbi ingaxutywa nodongwe. Abo bathatha uphawu baya kuba likhoboka lenkqubo evukayo kwaye baya kuba likhoboka lengqondo edibeneyo eya kwenzeka ngenxa yokuxutywa kwenyama nethekhinoloji.

Ulwandle lumele iintlanga zeeNtlanga. Imithetho yobuxoki kaNowa sele isitsho ukuba ubuKristu bubuthixo yaye imisela ukunqunyulwa kwentloko njengesohlwayo. Le mithetho ikhona yaye iya kunyanzeliswa ngonya. Kwaye abo banqula igama leNkosi kude kube sekupheleni baya kusindiswa, kuba kubhaliwe kwathiwa: UThixo wozisula zonke iinyembezi emehlweni abo: kungabi sabakho kufa, nasijwili, nakukhala, kungabi sabakho ntlungu: ngokuba izinto zokuqala zidlule. Lowo uhleli etroneni, wathi, Yabona, izinto zonke ndizenza ntsha. Wathi kum, bhala: ngokuba la mazwi ayinyaniso, athembekile. Kuya kuba njalo ke ikamva lenu, zingcwele zam zembandezelo, kuba ndisaya kumsindisa uYakobi nabo bonke abanqula igama lam elinamandla ngalo naluphi na ulwimi abanqula ngalo.

Irhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni, elikwangumprofeti wobuxoki, liya kulawula uSirayeli, yaye liya kulawula iHlabathi lisemhlabeni wesithembiso, kodwa hayi abantu bakaThixo, hayi uSirayeli kaThixo kuba ninguSirayeli kaThixo, kwanoonyana bOsenyangweni abatywinwe ngetywina likaThixo Osenyangweni, yaye itywina likaYahweh alinakwaphulwa. Kwaye isihlwele esikhulu siya kuphuma kwiNkathazo kaYakobi kwaye siya kuthatha iindawo zaso ezifanelekileyo phakathi koSirayeli kaThixo, kuba bobabini umYuda neNtlanga baya kusindiswa kwaye baya kuba ngumntu omnye omtsha kuba ndiya kubadala ngokutsha kum ekuvukeni kwabalungisileyo.

Bantwana bam, iNdlu yesikhungu kaSathana ilungiselele ulawulo lwayo yaye abanazintloni, nina nizizilwanyana ezinemilenze emibini kubo, kuba balandela iTalmud yaseBhabhiloni enyelisayo, incwadi evela kuSathana hayi kuYahweh, kuba uSirayeli wamfulathela uYahweh kudala xa bandalayo mna, uYeshua waseNazarete, yaye ndisaya kubasindisa abantu bam. Olu luhlobo olufanayo lomdiza apho ndaxelela ooyise babo xa ndandibaxelela ngokucacileyo: Nina ningaboyihlo uMtyholi, neenkanuko zoyihlo niya kuzenza: ebesoloko engumbulali kwasekuqaleni, akemi enyanisweni, ngokuba akukho nyaniso kuye. Xa ethetha ubuxoki, uthetha okwakhe: kuba ulixoki, noyise wabo. Qondani Bantwana bam ukuba uDan lithole lengonyama kwaye uDan akammelwanga kuma-144,000. Lo mdiza ulindele ngolangazelelo umakhi wetempile yawo, yaye sele umamkela njengokumkani wawo. Irhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni lelo lizisa uphawu lwerhamncwa. Kwanomkhankaso wembewu yemfazwe, osele uzanyiwe waza wagqityeleliswa ukuze usasazwe kwaye unyanzeliswe ngokufa kwabo bangavumiyo. Umhlaba umele ilizwe lesithembiso.

Bantwana bam, yekani ukukholelwa kubuxoki babo bangathetheliyo mna, kuba andibathumanga. Abaprofeti bam bokwenene bathetha ngazwinye, yaye baninzi, yaye abaphikisani, kwanazibhalo, kuba ndiliLizwi elenziwa inyama. La marhamncwa mabini akhohlakeleyo akawafumani amandla awo komnye ubawo wecawa owonakeleyo, kodwa kwiNamba ngokwayo, kuba iNamba nguMtyholi. Qondani ukuba ubuKristu abuyi kuba nandima kwaphela kuba zonke iicawa zobuxoki zigwetyiwe kwaye zitshatyalalisiwe kuba umgwebo uqala endlwini kaThixo, kwaye iBandla lam lokwenene, kwaneAbabiziweyo bam Abathembekileyo nabanyuliweyo baya kuthathwa bayiswe emazulwini ukuze bafumane imivuzo yabo kwaye baqale unaphakade wabo. Kwaye kunikwe inamba ukuba ilawule, kwaye abaphambukeli baya kubonakala, kodwa abangobaphambukeli kodwa zizithunywa zezulu eziwileyo apho iNamba iyintloko yazo. Uninzi lwazo luye lwagcinwa ngamatyathanga ukususela kwimihla kaNowa kodwa ngoku zikhululwe ngenxa yomgwebo wazo wokugqibela ongaleleyo.

Irhamncwa eliphuma elwandle, kwane elo igama lalo liguqulelwe esiNgesini lisuka kwisiHebhere: Umbani uwe ezulwini: funda ukuze ufunde. Kuba akanqweneli bafazi, akayihloneli nayiphi na ithixo: kuba uya kuzikhulisa ngaphezu kwako konke. Irhamncwa eliyinamba eliphuma elwandle, nerhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni liya kuvuka eluthuthwini njengamagorha ngokutshabalalisa iBhabhiloni Eyimfihlo, ihenyukazi elikhulu, yaye onke asebenza ngokubambisana. Bayayithiya iMelika nayo nantoni na enxulumene negama likaYesu Kristu, kodwa baya kuguqa ngamadolo ngokufanayo emgwebeni wabo, apho bangasoze baphinde bachache. Ngoko ke, isibhalo asinakuqondwa kulungelelwaniso lwangoku lweentlanga nomhlaba ngokwawo. Kuba uYahweh ulungiselela ukuzisa ukushukuma okukhulu emhlabeni emgwebeni kwaye imida iya kuzotywa ngokutsha. Le yimfazwe yabo yokugqibela yokutshabalalisa ulungelelwaniso oludala, nokungenisa ulungelelwaniso lukaSathana oluya kulawula uluntu ngerhamncwa eliphuma elwandle nerhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni elikwangumprofeti wobuxoki. Iimpondo ezilishumi, ezingookumkani abalishumi abaya kufumana amandla okulawula iyure enye, kuba kunikwe njalo, ziya kunika amandla azo nemvume yazo kwesi sithathu silirhamncwa.

Yabonani! Ndiza kuninika uluhlu olusisiseko lweziganeko ukuze nazi amaxesha eniphila kuwo. Ezinye zezi ziganeko zenzeka ngokulandelelana, kwaye ezinye zenzeka ngaxeshanye, kwaye zimi ngolu hlobo!

  • IBhabhiloni Eyimfihlakalo iyatshatyalaliswa njengoko imfazwe yehlabathi iqhambuka emhlabeni, kwaye nangoku bayayilungiselela imikhosi yabo kwaye bacwangcisa uhlaselo olulungelelaniswe kakuhle kwiBhabhiloni Eyimfihlakalo. Musani ukukholelwa kwiinkokeli zenu, nakumajelo eendaba, kuba ziyipulpiti yamaxoki ayinxalenye yohlaselo.
  • Irhamncwa eliphuma elwandle, nerhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni, nenamba ziya kuvuka eluthuthwini lwemfazwe.
  • La marhamncwa mabini akhohlakeleyo aya kuma echasene namangqina ama-2 aya kuprofeta iinyanga ezingama-42, ngelo xesha aya kubulawa aze anyukele emazulwini ngeentsuku ezintathu enesiqingatha kwaye kuya kuba njalo ukuvuka kwawo.
  • Njengoko itempile isakhiwa, imfazwe nxamnye nezingcwele iyaqhubeka iinyanga ezingama-42 kwaye uphawu lwerhamncwa luyamiselwa, lulawulwe kwaye lunyanzeliswe phantsi kwentlungu yokufa. Emva koko kufike inguqu, xa isikizi lesiphanziso lisenziwa kwaye irhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni liya kufuna ukubulala bonke abaya kuza kum, kwanoYeshua HaMashiach ngeli xesha.
  • Irhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni lizibhengeza lingoThixo lize lijikele abantu ukuze libabulale, kuba liyabangcatsha abantu ngendlela efanayo noAntiyokasi Epifanesi.
  • Ndakhangela, yaye, yabona, iMvana yema phezu kwentaba yeZiyon, yaye inayo ikhulu elinamashumi amane anesine lamawaka, libhalwe iGama likaYise emabunzini alo. (Banoyolo abafileyo, abo bafela eNkosini, kususela ngoku, utsho uMoya, ukuze baphumle emisebenzini yabo, nemisebenzi yabo iyabalandela.)
  • Ama-144,000 am aya kukhusela abantu bam aze abase kwiindawo zokhuseleko ukuze bamelane nesaqhwithi njengoko ilizwe lihlanjululwa kuko konke ukungalungisi, kwane umdiza okhohlakeleyo. Kuba ukuba bendingayinqumlanga imihla bekungayi kusinda nyama, ngoko ke ndiya kukhusela abaninzi entlango. Oku kuya kwenzeka ngenxalenye ukuze insalela yoluntu isindiswe.
  • Ama-144,000 eza emhlabeni enxibe iingubo endiya kuwathungela ngokukhethekileyo, yaye awanakwaphulwa yaye aya kuba namandla kwaye akukho nto yotshaba iya koyisa, kwaye ngaloo ndlela iinjongo zikaYahweh ziya kuzaliseka.

NdinguYeshua HaMashiach kwaye ndithumele la mazwi ngomkhonzi wam, kwaye ngomkhonzi wam amazwi am athunyelwe.

Khangelani emazulwini Bandla lam elithembekileyo kuba ndiyasondela kwaye ndiya kunisa endlwini kaBawo apho ndinilungiselele indawo kwaye nilawula nam njengooKumkani nababingeleli, oboBukumkani bam bungenasiphelo, ngoko ke zithuthuzeleni ngala Mazwi.
