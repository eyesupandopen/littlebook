NdinguYehova Ongunaphakade Wobulungisa!

Yabonani! Yivani amazwi kaYesu Krestu nize ninikele iingcinga zenu, noluse iintliziyo zenu neendlebe zenu ekuthobeleni amazwi kaYeshua HaMashiach, yaye ndiya kunika abalumkileyo ubulumko ukuze indoda elumkileyo phakathi kwenu ifunde ukoyika uYehova nize nifunde ukungcangcazela phambi kweNkosi yemikhosi, kuba wenjenjalo uYehova, uMvelinqangi Omkhulu, ukundenza.

Ungubani na wena, mntu wenyama, ukuba uphakamise isithende sakho nxamnye noYehova, uMvelinqangi Omkhulu, yena onguNyangele, yena oyiNkosi yooMoya? Ungubani na wena mntu wenyama ukuba uziphakamise ngekratshi eliqhayisayo nxamnye noYesu waseNazarete onguKrestu Yesu, yena ogama lakhe lingaphezu kwawo onke amagama, yena athe uYehova, uMvelinqangi Omkhulu, wabelana naye ngetrone yakhe, yena ophakanyiswe ngaphezu kwako konke ngaphandle koYehova ngokwakhe, zide iintshaba zam zenziwe isihlalo seenyawo zam? Yaye ubukumkani bam nolawulo lwam alunasiphelo, yaye ndiya kubunikezela kuYise weziKhanyiso obukumkani obuthotyiweyo, ukuze uAdonai Yehova abe yinto yonke kuzo zonke!

Ingaba into eyenziweyo ingathi na kulowo uyenzileyo, undenzele ntoni na ukuba ndibe nje? Ingaba isigaqa sodongwe esenziwe izitya zembeko singathi na kumbumbi, undenzele ntoni na ukuba ndibe nje? Undinike imbeko engaka nje ngenxa yani? Ndifuna ukuba yenye into, endaweni yoko ndiya kuthatha imbeko yabo! Ngaba ukhona na umbumbi ongayi kuyitshabalalisa indalo enjalo ekhohlakeleyo enokuthi imvukele, ifune nokumbulala umbumbi kunye nezinye iindalo zakhe; ngokungathi into enjalo inokwenzeka?

Kanti zinjalo iindlela ezihlekisayo neziphambeneyo zabo bawayo abaye bamvukela umdali wabo ngokushiya imiyalelo nemisebenzi yabo engcwele nebekekileyo ababeyinikiwe ukuba bayizalisekise ngobulungisa, iinjongo kanye ezanikezelwa nguYehova ngokwakhe ngomhla awadala ngawo oonyana bakaThixo. Kwanazo iingelosi ezingcwele awazenzayo uYehova ngaphambi kokuba enze umhlaba, ekubhalwe ngazo kuYobhi xa wayesenza umhlaba: Iinkwenkwezi zokusa zacula kunye, nabo bonke oonyana bakaThixo baduma bevuya! Yaye ngenene baduma bevuya ngenxa yemimangaliso abayibonayo ezandleni zikaYehova xa wayedala umhlaba. Kwaye zazenjalo neengelosi eziwileyo, kwanabo oonyana bakaThixo ekuqaleni badalwa bazukiswa banikwa namandla ngeenjongo zabo, waza uYehova wazambathisa ngembeko wazenza zagqwesa ngobulumko ukuze zimthande uYehova ngokuthobela ngokugqibeleleyo ilizwi lakhe, yaye ngenene banikwa isimo sokuqala esizukileyo abadalelwa kuso ekuqaleni, de bavukela ngeenkanuko zeentliziyo zabo lwaza ubugwenxa lwafunyanwa kubo.

Kwaye benjenjalo ooSathana ekuqaleni bevukela umdali wabo, kwanabo babekade bengqonge itrone kaYehova, kwanetroni yakhe ngoku ingqongwe ngamawaka-waka ali-10,000 aphindwe kali-10,000, kwaye uYehova usengumongami yaye akadingi mcebisi. Kanti kubhaliwe kanje ngale namba inkulu ibomvu, inentloko ezisixhenxe, neempondo ezilishumi, nezithsaba ezisixhenxe ezintlokweni zayo, ukuba umsila wayo warhola isahlulo sesithathu seenkwenkwezi zezulu, waziphosa emhlabeni: zaye zanjalo ke ooSathana kwanasesithathwini seengelosi ezalandela imvukelo yabo zaphoswa emhlabeni, kwanakwimihla apho ukonakala kweengelosi kwawa ezulwini ngesono nemvukelo, zaza zaba bubumnyama obupheleleyo emva kwezono zazo, yaye zinjalo nanamhlanje.

Yaza ke inyoka yasondela kunina wabo bonke abaphilayo eMyezweni wase-Eden kwiminyaka emininzi eyadlulayo, yaza yamqhatha uEva ukuba athathe kwantoni leyo uYehova awayebayalele ukuba bangayithathi, kuba ngomhla ababesidla ngawo kumthi wokwazi okulungileyo nokubi, kwangalo mhla babeya kufa. Nangona kunjalo, uEva wathatha enyokeni njengoko wayeqhathekile yinkohliso yayo waza wadla, waza wanika umyeni wakhe esi siqhamo salelweyo waza uAdam wadla ngokuzithandela, yaye ekufeni kwabo bafa njengoko kwakuthenjisiwe, yaye uluntu luye lwaba phantsi kokufa ukususela ngoko ngenxa yesono esifunyenwe egazini lakhe. Kuba uAdam wonakele ngonaphakade esonweni, yaye ngenxa yale njongo uNyana woMntu wabonakaliswa ukuze nditshabalalise imisebenzi kamtyholi ndize ndizise inkululo kuAdam owileyo kuba ndingumAdam wokugqibela uYehova amenze umoya ophilisa. Inene, inene, ndiya kubadala ngokutsha bonke abamkela isipho sam sosindiso babe yindalo entsha nelungileyo kuba yile nto iziswa liTestamente lam Elitsha.

Ngaloo ndlela uAdam noEva bagxothwa eMyezweni wase-Eden, baza batshintshwa kwimo emangalisayo eqaqambileyo ababedalwe ngayo buqaqawuli obupheleleyo nguYehova ngokwakhe, yaye phantsi kolawulo lwesono nobubi obuziswayo baye babhadula emhlabeni besohlwaywa. Yaye ngemihla kaYerede abaLindi behla phezu kweNtaba yeHermon, kwanabo babebanjwe ziinkanuko zeentliziyo zabo kuba babenkanukela iintombi zabantu, benqwenela nenzala kuzo, yaye ngeenkanuko zeentliziyo zabo abaqhathwa ngazo ngooSathana bazibophelela kwiziqalekiso ezifanayo, kwanakwisifungo sabo esingcwele esithe sajika saba sisiqalekiso esingunaphakade esiya kuzalisekiswa kwelo chibi likhulu lesulfure evuthayo, apho impethu yabo ingafiyo.

Baza ke oonyana bakaThixo bangena kwiintombi zabantu, baza bazithabathela abafazi kubo bonke ababakhethayo. Abanye abafazi baya ngokuzithandela, bezinikela ngokupheleleyo kwizono zabo bawayo (abaya kuthwala umgwebo wabo), yaye abanye bathathwa ngenkani baza baba ngamaxhoba abaLindi ngeenkanuko zabo ezimbi. Laye lalinjalo ke ihlabathi elalikhona ngoko, kwanalo ihlabathi elatshatyalaliswa ngunogumbe omkhulu owenzeka ngemihla kaNowa, elatshatyalaliswa ngenxa yamasikizi amabi enziwa ngabalindi, ababenzelwe ukutshatyalaliswa koluntu ukuze uluntu lungaze lube nalo ilizwe elalinqwenelwa ngabo bawayo yaye ngoku babotshelelwe emgwebeni.

Kuba bakhetha intshabalalo yabo nentuthumbo engunaphakade ukuze bafumane izinto ababezinqwenela, ezingezizo ezabo ukuba bazithathe, yaye ezo zinto bazenzayo, yaye basafuna ukuzithatha ngenkani. Nangona kunjalo, izenzo zabo nezicwangciso zabo ziya kuba lilize, yaye abasayi kuze bayifumane kwakhona into abayibiza ngokuba lixesha labo legolide, kodwa endaweni yoko baya kuba nomgwebo onzima, oqatha nongunaphakade. Kuba ngenyaniso engaguqukiyo: Umhlaba yoweNkosi nokuzala kwawo, yaye mna ndinguYehova Ongunaphakade Wobulungisa owathenga umhlaba ngegazi lam elihlawulelayo, yaye ndiza kuwuthatha ngenkani oko kungokwam ngokufanelekileyo, yaye ngubani na oya kuba nako ukumelana namandla am amakhulu?

Njengoko kubhaliwe ngam, kuya kwenziwa njalo: Yabonani, iNgonyama yesizwe sakwaYuda, iNgcambu kaDavide, yoyisile ukuba iyivule incwadi, nokuba izikhulule izitywina zayo ezisixhenxe. Ngoko ke, qondani ngokupheleleyo ukuba ndingulowo ufanelekileyo ukukhulula izitywina kuba itayitile yomhlaba yeyam! Yaye njengoko kubhaliwe ngam kuya kwenziwa njalo kuba ndifanelekile ukuyithabatha incwadi, nokuvula izitywina zayo: kuba ndaxhelwa, ndaza ndahlawulela bonke abaya kuza kusindiso olungunaphakade kuYehova ngegazi lam elingenasono, yaye ndanihlawulela kuyo yonke imizalwane, neelwimi, nabantu, neentlanga: yaye akukho namnye onokunixhwila ezandleni zam ezingunaphakade! Ngoko ke, ngubani na enifanele nimoyike?

Ngaloo ndlela abafazi abathathwe ngoonyana bakaThixo, (kwanabo balindi bawayo) bakhawula baza babazalela iingxilimbela ezinezothe ezazikhohlakele kude kube sekuzoleni kweentliziyo zazo. Yaye babekholose beqhayisa ngobukhulu namandla abo, kodwa emgwebeni kaYehova kubo ngenxa yobubi abona ngabo uluntu, wayibophelela imimoya yabo emhlabeni njengesohlwayo kuba benziwa emhlabeni ngokomfanekiso wenyama enesono yaye imimoya yabo yayingunaphakade ngokwendalo, inikwe ngooyise ababotshelelwe ukuba bazalisekise iinkanuko zabo. Yaye njengoko abaNefili babesifa kwimigwebo yabo evela kuYehova ngokwakhe, imimoya yabo yahlulwa nemizimba yabo ekufeni kwabo, yaye baqhubeka, bebotshelelwe emhlabeni njengesohlwayo. Ngoko ke, bayalamba banxane kodwa abanakuthatha kutya namanzi. Ngelixa babekhe banemizimba emikhulu nenamandla ababeyisebenzisa ukulawula abemi bomhlaba, benziwa bangabi namandla ngaphandle kokuba bakwazi ukuthatha umzimba abahlala kuwo njengezifunxi-gazi zeedemon, kuba baye baba njalo. Qonda, ukuba ukususela kwimihla kaNowa ngenxa yokungenelela kukaNowa, isithoba kwishumi labo sigcinwe enzonzobileni kodwa sele siza kukhululwa emhlabeni njengomgwebo.

Kanti, ibinjalo imvukelo yabo bawayo ukususela kanye kuloo mhla ubugwenxa bafunyanwa ezintliziyweni zabo. Ukuphambana nobugeza kuzalise iintliziyo zabo bawayo kwimvukelo yabo; kunye nomntu wenyama, owazimanya nabo ngokwala ukusuka kwimeko yakhe yokuwa neyesono: ukuba bonke bafuna ukuba yinto enye ngaphandle kwaleyo uYehova ayijongileyo kuko konke ukugqibelela okulungileyo. Endaweni yokuthobela umdali wabo owayeza kuqinisekisa ukuzaliseka kwabo okugqibeleleyo, uvuyo, ulonwabo, noxolo; owayeza kuhlala ebanika ngokugqibeleleyo eluthandweni lwakhe ngendalo yakhe, endaweni yoko bafuna baza balandela iinkanuko zeentliziyo zabo yaye bafuna ihlabathi labo ngaphandle koYehova, apho banokuphila khona iinkanuko zeentliziyo zabo baze bazonwabise kwimvukelo yabo kwiziyolo zesono ngeendleko zendalo kaYehova. Kwaneminqweno yesono nobubi abangasoze bayifumane okanye bayiphumeze.

Kwangokunjalo, umntu wenyama uzimanye nemvukelo yabo ngaphandle kokuba umntu wenyama uzalelwe ekungazini nasebumnyameni ngeenjongo zakhe zokwenene nozuko olunokwenzeka aluthembisileyo uYehova kwabathobelayo, yaye umntu wenyama uye walandela ngokuzithandela iindlela zabo bawayo, nangona, abo bawayo bezinikele ekutshatyalalisweni kuka-Adam, nto leyo uAdam angayaziyo ngokuzithandela kwiinkanuko zakhe, kwanale nto azibophelela kuyo ngolindelo lobudenge kubuxoki babo obuninzi nobubi. Kwanangendlela yobuxoki, abaninzi baya kukhokelelwa kwidike lomlilo ngoxa eso sohlwayo sasingenzelwanga bona.

Kanti, le mvukelo iqhubekile kule minyaka ngoku, kwanemvukelo edlalwa kwimfazwe yembewu phakathi kweendalo ezilungileyo zikaYehova namasikizi angalunganga enziwe lutshintsho lwemfuza lwabakhohlakeleyo, yaye abangoothixo nangona bezenzisa. Yaye anjalo amaxesha eniphila kuwo, wena mntu, ovukela ngokungathobeli okunentiyo kwiindlela zomdali wakhe, kwanokuzimanya ngokungazi nabo bazinikele ngokupheleleyo ekufeni kwenu nasekutshatyalalisweni okungunaphakade abakunqwenelayo.

Ngaloo ndlela ndivakalisa umhla, mna Yeshua HaMashiach, ndivakalisa ukuba umhla weNkosi ufikile yaye sele uza kuqhambuka kuwo wonke umhlaba ngomgwebo omkhulu onogonyamelo yaye iintlekele ezininzi ngoku ziza kukhululwa emhlabeni, kwanazo iintlekele nemigwebo eyaxelwa kwangaphambili mandulo amaxesha ayo ngoku afikileyo. Ngoko ke, qondani ukuba ndingubani na owenza esi sibhengezo ngamandla nangetrone kaYehova ngokwakhe, kuba ndingulowo ufanelekileyo ukuyithabatha incwadi nokuyivula, nokukhulula izitywina ezikuyo, yaye ndiya kwenza kanye loo nto, yaye ngubani na onokunqanda isandla sam esinamandla?!

Qonda, wena mntu wenyama, kwanawena ungamala ngokunentiyo umdali wakho, ukuba amaxesha akho afikile. Yaye uthando oluninzi nobabalo novelwano lunikiwe kula maxesha omgwebo, kuba bonke abo banqula igama likaYesu waseNazarete de kube sekupheleni kwabo baya kusindiswa yaye ubunzima bomvuzo wabo abufanelanga kuthelekiswa neentsizi abaya kuzinyamezela ngenxa yegama lam. Qonda, wena mntu wenyama, ukuba ndiya kukhokela amawaka am ali-144,000 anamandla okuhlangula uYakobi yaye ndiya kufihla intsalela enamandla yakwaSirayeli eya kuqhubeka iphila kubuKumkani bam beminyaka eliwaka kwindawo elungiswe nguYehova entlango, ukuze babe nesiqhamo bande bonwabele ukutyeba kwelizwe kubukumkani bam beminyaka eliwaka obuza kufika kungekudala emhlabeni ekuzalisekisweni kwesithembiso, umnqophiso, netestamente.

Kuya kuba njalo kumntu wenyama, ukuba baya kwamkela imvana kaThixo njengomsindisi wabo okanye baya kuba bazikhethele umgwebo wabo ongunaphakade kwelo chibi lomlilo elivutha ngonaphakade kuba akukho nto yimbi banokuyikhetha, yaye akukho ndawo yimbi banokuya kuyo kuba uYehova akasayi kuze avumele ububi buthathe butshabalalise indalo yakhe yaye imihla yabo yokuvukela ngokuzithandela iyaphela.

Ndingumvana kaThixo, onikelwe ngenxa yezono zehlabathi.

NdinguNgonyama Yesizwe SakwaYuda, yaye ngobulungisa ndiza kugweba ndenze imfazwe.

Ndingulowo kuye imvana kaThixo neNgonyama Yesizwe SakwaYuda zimanyene ngokupheleleyo kwiinjongo zam ezingunaphakade nolawulo njengoKumkani wooKumkani neNkosi yeeNkosi, ngoko ke ngubani na onokumelana noYeshua HaMashiach ongcwele ka-Adonai, kwanalowo uYehova amthumileyo ukuba enze umgwebo ongunaphakade kwabo bawayo nakubo bonke abakhetha ukuzimanya neendlela zabo.

Ngoko ke ndiyabacebisa abathembekileyo kuKrestu ukuba bahlale bethembekile kuba imini yokuhlangulwa kwenu ifikile kwaye isondele kuni. Khangelani kuphela kwiNkosi yenu engunaPhakade yoBulungisa kuba abawileyo bawugutyungele umhlaba ngesikhukula esikhulu sobuxoki neenkohliso ezenzelwe ukukhohlisa nabanyuliweyo ukuba kunokwenzeka. Ngoko ke, qondani ukuba kuthunyelwe abaprofeti abaninzi abaxokayo abaprofeta ngeendlela zikaBhileham nabathathwe yimimoya kaIzebhele nabathetha ngemimoya yomchasi-Krestu abafundisa omnye uYesu omi endaweni kaYesu Krestu onguYeshuwa HaMashiyakhi ezintliziyweni zabakhohlisiweyo, emchasa. Bonke abathathwa yinkohliso enjalo baya kuthwala isigwebo sabo ukuba bayala ukuguquka kwiindlela zabo xa bejamelene neNkosi yabo emilonyeni yabanyanisekileyo kuKrestu. Inene, inene, abanye abaprofeti abaxokayo banyanisekile kwaye bakhohlisiwe kwaye banokufikelelwa ngenyaniso ethethwa ngemilebe yothando, kodwa yinyaniso kuphela eya kubakhulula kwaye yinyaniso ecacileyo yezono zabo kunye neziprofeto zobuxoki ukuze bakwazi ukuzilungisa ngokuguquka.

Kukwakho nabo baprofeta ngenxa yemali, nenzuzo emdaka, ukuze bafumane udumo nengqwalasela baze bazinze ngokupheleleyo kwiimpahla zeli hlabathi likhohlakeleyo. Nokuba ngubani na oprofeta ngobuxoki, mkhuze uze uwachithe amazwi akhe, nkqu namazwi ayilwe ngabawileyo ukuze abambise nabanyuliweyo engozini yenkohliso ukuba kunokwenzeka. Kutheni nibaleka ngapha nangapha, Bandla lam elithembekileyo? Andinikanga myalelo na, andinicebisanga na, andinixhobisanga na ukuba nibe ngaboyisi bam? Kwanabo boyisa yonke imiba yobukumkani beentshaba ngendlela efanayo nam ndoyisa ngayo? Ngoko ke, yizani kuKrestu Yesu kwaye ndiya kuqinisekisa ezintliziyweni zenu abaprofeti bam bokwenene kuba abaprofeti bam bokwenene bathetha bemanyene nentliziyo yeNkosi yabo eliLizwi elenziwe inyama. Ngoko ke, uKrestu Yesu akanakuze aziphikise izibhalo, kuba ndinjalo mna!

Kwabo bacinga ngekratshi ukuba bandimele kodwa bathathwe bubuxoki: Kutheni nibaleka ngapha nangapha, nifuna umntu wokunikholisa iindlebe zenu, nide nifune noosiyazi ababhibhizayo nabakhamisayo abaya kunixelela kanye le nto bafuna niyive ukuze bavuse iinkanuko ezintliziyweni zenu ezikhohlakeleyo njengoko nibaleka inguquko nisiya ezingalweni zabakhohlisi abathunyelwe ukukhohlisa. Nibaleka ngapha nangapha kuba anilihoyi ilizwi likaYeshuwa HaMashiyakhi, kuba niyayala inyaniso ebeya kunikhulula, kuba niyala ukufunda ngam ngokubamba iLizwi likaYahweh njengowona mgangatho wenu uphezulu nowukuphela kwawo. Ngoko ke, ndiyabacebisa bonke abacinga ukuba baprofeta ngoKrestu Yesu, nkqu nabo bonke abacinga ukuba bathethela uYahweh, uMkhulu onguMkhulu, ukuba bazilumkele hleze bawele ebumnyameni obungunaphakade nakwisigwebo esingunaphakade kuba bonke abazimanya nabawileyo ngendlela efanayo baze bale inguquko amagama abo aya kucinywa encwadini yobomi ukuba bayawala umlilo womcoci abaya kushiyelwa kuwo ukuba bayaqhubeka nezono zabo, kuba alisekho ixesha.

Ngoko ke ndiyabacebisa kwaye ndiyabalumkisa abo ndibathandayo ukuze bakuphephe ukukhohliswa okunamandla okungenzelwanga bona ukuba bahamba ngokuthobela iNkosi yabo, kwaye ndiyabacebisa kwaye ndiyabalumkisa abo ndibathandayo ukuze bangabanjiswa yinkohliso enkulu leyo sele ilawula umhlaba. Ndiyabacebisa bonke abaya kuva la mazwi ukuba bemke ebubini kwaye bemke koosiyazi ababhibhizayo nabakhamisayo kwaye nisuse iindawo zenu etafileni kaIzebhele kwaye niyale imivuzo kaBhileham. Qondani ukuba nabani na oziprofeto zakhe ziphikisana nesibhalo uthetha ubuxoki ngohanahaniso. Qondani ukuba uYahweh, uMkhulu onguMkhulu, noYeshuwa HaMashiyakhi (okwanguKrestu Yesu) abasoze baziphikise izibhalo. Ngoko ke, abo bathetha bechasa isibhalo bathetha bechasa uYahweh uMkhulu onguMkhulu kwaye bathetha bechasa noYeshuwa HaMashiyakhi okwanguKrestu Yesu.

Qondani kakuhle ukuba ndingubani nokuba uYahweh wandidalela ukuba ndibe ngubani. NdiliLizwi likaYahweh elenziwe inyama elihlala kuni zithandwa zam, kwaye andinakuze ndiziphikise okanye ndixoke, kuba andinasono kwaye ndiya kuhlala ndingenasono ngonaphakade. Mqondeni uYise wezikhanyiso ongenaguquguquko nasithunzi sakujika, yena kanye enimele nina! Qondani ukuba akunakwenzeka ukuba uAdonayi Yahweh aziphikise kuba akunakwenzeka ukuba uAdonayi Yahweh axoke. Kanti, kutheni nizithoba ngokobudenge iindlebe zenu kwabo badlula ngokunyonyoba ebusuku? Niya kuhenyuza na zithandwa zam, ekubeni nifanele nibe ngabathembekileyo nabathobelayo kwiNkosi enye, engumsindisi wenu ongunaphakade, yena kanye ozayo ukuxhwila abathembekileyo kuKrestu abase ezulwini nangawuphi na umzuzwana ngoku?

Ngoko ke qondani, ukuba amasango esihogo akayi koyisa abathembekileyo kuKrestu abaliBandla lam lokwenene kuba kwabhalwa kwathenjiswa njalo mandulo, kwaye kusabhalwe kwathenjiswa ngoku, kuba amazwi kaYahweh noYeshuwa HaMashiyakhi ngamazwi acocekileyo anjengesilivere, evavanywe ezikweni lomhlaba, ecikidwe kasixhenxe. Ewe, abawileyo abanamandla ngokuchasene nabathembekileyo kuKrestu kwaye ndiya kubangela ukuba abathembekileyo bam banyathele phantsi kweenyawo nayiphi na imiba yobukumkani beentshaba, nkqu nokunyathela abo bafuna ukunqanda okungenakunqandwa.

NdinguKrestu Yesu kwaye ngubani onokumelana namandla nam obukhosi endiwamkele kuYahweh ngokwakhe!

Qondani ukuba mna noBawo simunye kubunye nasenjongweni, kwaye ndisoloko ndisenza ukuthanda kukaBawo! Ngoko ke, akukho mntu unokumelana nam, kwaye akukho nanye into phakathi kwabawileyo enokundoyisa, nokuba kungobuqhetseba, okanye ngamandla, okanye ngobuninzi, okanye ngeteknoloji yabo engenaxabiso! Kwaye ndingulowo waqinisekisa ukoyiswa kwabo okungunaphakade eKalvari xa ndathetha amazwi athi, kugqityiwe. Qondani ukuba nimanyene ngokupheleleyo noKrestu Yesu kwaye ndiyanenza nime ngamandla nangobukhosi bam! Ngoko ke, ngubani onokunichasa zithandwa zam? Ngubani enifanele nimoyike? Yivani amazwi am bantwana bam kwaye niqonde ukuba andisokuze ndinishiye okanye ndinilahle esandleni seentshaba zenu kuba nizoyisile kwaye nibe ngabathobelayo kwiimvavanyo ezininzi nezohlwayo ezinikwe sisandla sam esisindisayo!
