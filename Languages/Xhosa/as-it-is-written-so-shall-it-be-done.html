Ingqina eliMbaxa: uSirayeli
Okukrakra Okumnandi
Septemba 7, 2024

Njengoko Kubhaliwe, Kuya Kwenziwa Kanjalo!

Zihlanganiseni nize nive amazwi eNkosi yenu, uYeshua HaMashiach, bantwana bam, kuba mna ndiyiNkosi yeeNkosi noKumkani wooKumkani, yaye ngobulungisa ndiya kuza kugweba ndenze imfazwe. Ngoko ke ziqondeni iixesha eniphila kuzo neziganeko ezizayo, nibeke iindlebe zenu kwisandi selizwi lam, nize nive amazwi eNkosi yenu engunaphakade nengunini-nanini, uKrestu Yesu ovusiweyo nozuko! Ngoku ndiza kuthetha ngezinto ezizayo evekini kaDaniyeli yamashumi asixhenxe, ndiya kuzenza zicace iindlela zenu, yaye ndiya kolula ubabalo lwam olusindisayo ngezandla zama-144,000 am. Kuba ayeza kusindisa abantu bam entshabalalweni, yaye ayeza kubafihla abantu bam engqumbini, ingqumbo kaThixo eyenzelwe abawileyo nabo bazinxulumanya ngonaphakade nabo nobukumkani babo ngokwamkela uphawu lwabo, kwaloo lulwano lwenyoka eyirhamncwa ekungekho nyango lalo.

Ukanti kunjalo ukuba ndiya kumxhwila uMtshakazi wam ndimse emazulwini, yaye ndiya kuwabeka uphawu ama-144,000 am avela kwezi zizwe zilandelayo, ama-12,000 kwisizwe ngasinye kwezi zilandelayo: uYuda, uRubhen, uGadi, uAshere, uNafetali, uManase, uSimeyon, uLevi, uIsakare, uZebhulon, uYosefu, noBhenjamin. Ngoko ke liveni ilizwi lam!

Yaye baya kuba ngumkhosi ongenakunqandwa, ewe, baya kuba ngumkhosi ongenakwaphulwa. Kuba ubomi babo bobukanaphakade, obungenakonakaliswa bububi. Imizimba yabo ayinakonakaliswa, kuba akukho ntshabalalo inokubabamba abakhululweyo; kuba uloyiso phezu kokufa nentshabalalo banalo ngonaphakade kanaphakade. Baya kudalwa ngokutsha njengabantwana bokukhanya, yaye baya kwambathiswa ngozuko olungunaphakade, yaye baya kuba nezakhono neetalente ezizodwa ezibhalwe kubo kanye eziya kubonakalisa impumelelo yabo eyenza yaye iqulethe izakhono endiya kuzidala ngaphakathi kwabo. Kuba mna, Krestu Yesu, ndingumcoci ohleli ephethe isivuthelo ngesandla, evuselela amadangatye okuhlanjululwa, ukuze ndinidale nonke ngokutsha kum, Krestu Yesu iNkosi yenu enyukileyo noMsindisi; yaye nabani na owoyisayo, uya kuzidla ilifa zonke izinto.

Ngoko ke ndinika ukuqonda ngeziganeko eziya kwenzeka kungekudala, yaye ezinye iziganeko zenzeka ngokulandelelana ngoxa ezinye zenzeka ngaxeshanye, kodwa ndiya kuzidwelisa ukuze nikwazi ukwahlula amaxesha eniphila kuwo, nokuze nina zingcwele zam zembandezelo ningathinjwa yinkohliso enamandla eyigubungeleyo umhlaba yaye abaninzi baya kukhohliswa baze bagwetywe. Kuba abawileyo baya kuzibonakalisa baze baxoke amaxesha amaninzi, yaye baya kuthembisa ngobomi kodwa abanamandla okunika ubomi, yaye abanakunika ubomi; kodwa ilizwi ngalinye abalithethayo lisekelwe kubuxoki obukhokelela ekufeni kwesibini, yaye yintshabalalo engunaphakade kuphela efunyanwa ngabo balandela iindlela zabawileyo nabo bonke abamkela ubuxoki babo obumdaka njengenyaniso. Kodwa lulo olo ugwebo oluze ehlabathini, lokuba abantu bathanda ubumnyama kunokukhanya yaye abantu bathanda ubuxoki kunenyaniso, kuba uluntu lundikhanyele mna, Yeshua HaMashiach, uYesu Krestu, lukhetha umsindisi wobuxoki; kodwa inyaniso ikuKrestu kuphela, yaye bonke abanye ngamaxoki nabaqhetseki.

Ngoko ke izinto ezizayo zimi ngolu hlobo, yaye ezinye iziganeko zenzeka ngaxeshanye yaye ezinye zenzeka ngokulandelelana yaye ziyafuneka ukuze kwenzeke ezinye iziganeko. Yaye iziganeko ezininzi azidweliswanga kodwa ziya kwenzeka ngexesha lazo. Ngoko ke into abanye abayibiza ngokuba kukuxhwilwa, okanye ukuhlanganiselwa kwabo bonke abakuKrestu eNkosini yabo, sisiganeko sokuqala esibangela zonke ezinye iziganeko ngexesha lembandezelo kaYakobi, ekwabizwa ngokuba yiveki kaDaniyeli yamashumi asixhenxe, kuba iya kuba yiveki yeminyaka esixhenxe, apho usuku ngalunye lungunyaka. Ngoko ke abathembekileyo eBandleni lam, elinguMzimba wam noMtshakazi wam, baya kuthathwa xa mna, Krestu Yesu, ndisihla emazulwini ndidanduluka, nangelizwi lesiphatha-zithunywa nangeXilongo likaThixo. Kuba ixilongo lokugqibela liya kuvakala, apho abafileyo kuKrestu baya kuvuselwa kubomi obutsha; ngoko nina baphilayo nabasalayo niya kuxhwilelwa emafini kunye nabo, yaye kuya kwenzeka kanye njengoko kubhaliwe, yaye kuya kwenzeka ngolandelelwano olunikiweyo ngokuqhwanyaza kweliso.

Ngoko ke qondani, nina bathembekileyo bam, nina endinithanda ngothando olungunaphakade nolungapheliyo, ukuba niya kuzifumana ngequbuliso nangoko nangoko kwigumbi letroni likaYahweh ngokwakhe, apho ndiya kuninikela ningenabala okanye imibimbi okanye naziphi na izinto ezinjalo kuYise wezikhanyiso, yaye nina nizizikhanyiso zehlabathi. Yaye ngoko nangoko niya kwazi njengoko naziwa, yaye niya kundibona mna, Yeshua HaMashiach, yaye niya kumbona uYahweh, uThixo Omkhulu Ongunaphakade, ngamehlo enu okokuqala, yaye niya kumangaliswa kakhulu koko nikubonayo nozuko eniya kwambathiswa ngalo. Yaye ngonaphakade niya kuba kumanyano olungunaphakade noYise, noNyana, yaye ngonaphakade siya kuba njalo.

Ngamaxesha okuhlanganiswa kwezingcwele, umnqandi uya kususwa, yaye umgwebo uya kuwela ngokupheleleyo phezu kwendlu kaThixo. Yaye abo bandivumelayo ukuba ndibacoce emlilweni womcoci baya kuma kuba bahlanjululwe egazini lemvana yaye baya kuma ebulungiseni endibanike bona, kuba mna ndiyiNkosi Elungileyo engumoya onika ubomi. Yaye abo balalayo ilizwi lam baza bahlala benenkani kwintando yam baza bala ukuphuma esonweni bangene ebulungiseni baya kushiyelwa ukucocwa okuqhubekayo ngamaxesha embandezelo kaYakobi, yaye izihlwele ezininzi ziya kufumana usindiso olungunaphakade, umvuzo, nobukhazikhazi, yaye ngonaphakade ziya kuphila yaye uzuko olukhulu luya kuba lolwazo ngonaphakade. Yaye ngoko ke amabandla omgunyathi aseleyo aya kutshatyalaliswa njengoko iNamba, irhamncwa elivela elwandle, nerhamncwa elivela emhlabeni ziya kuhamba ngokukhawuleza ziye kulwa nezingcwele eziseleyo zize zizoyise. Yaye kuya kuba njalo.

Xa umnqandi esusiwe, iBhabheli Eyimfihlo iya kutshatyalaliswa njengoko kubhaliwe. Ewe, ngeyure enye isiphelo sayo siya kuba sesokugqibela, yaye usizi olukhulu nentshabalalo ziya kuwudla umhlaba, yaye kuya kubakho ukuzamazama okukhulu, yaye kuya kucaca ehlabathini ukuba umhla weNkosi ufikile, nangona abawileyo beya kuxoka baze bazibonakalise njengabaphambukeli abanobubele, yaye baya kuzama ukukugquma ukubonakala kwam kwabathembekileyo ngemfazwe nohlaselo lobuxoki lwabaphambukeli. Ngoko ke ningazikholelwa iinyani zabo, bantwana bam, kuba iinyani zabo zijolise ekunikhokeleleni kwintshabalalo engunaphakade ebekelwe abawileyo, ngoko ke ningazibandakanyi nabo.

Ngoko ke amatywina aya kukhululwa yaye abakhweli-mahashe abane baya kukhululwa phezu kwehlabathi, yaye abantu abaninzi, abaninzi kakhulu baya kutshabalala ngenxa yoko. Yaye kubukumkani beBhabheli Eyimfihlo obuhleli phezu kwamanzi amaninzi, nabo baya kutshatyalaliswa baze bancitshiswe, yaye amaxhoba aya kukhonza inkqubo yerhamncwa elizayo nelokugqibela; yaye yeyokugqibela kuba mna, Krestu Yesu, ndiya kubutshabalalisa ngokupheleleyo ndize ndibuchithe ubukumkani bukaSathana, bungaphinde buvuke. Ngoko ke ihlabathi liya kulandela irhamncwa, yaye abaninzi baya kujoyina ubukumkani babo bobusathana. Yaye imvelo embi eluntwini, eyaziswa sisono sika-Adam xa uEva wathatha kwinyoka waza wadla, iya kukhululwa ngokupheleleyo, yaye uluntu luya kuba nempambano epheleleyo ezintliziyweni zalo, yaye abaninzi baya kuzinxulumanya ngonaphakade nerhamncwa ngokwamkela nokuphatha uphawu lwerhamncwa. Ningaluthathi uphawu lwayo, kuba bonke abaluthathayo baya kuphoswa bephila ngonaphakade kwelo chibi lomlilo wesulfure, elivutha awona madangatye ashushu aluhlaza.

Kuthuthu lwemfazwe kuya kuvela ubukumkani babo bokugqibela, ewe, kuthuthu lolawulo oludala, kuba kwisiphithiphithi abasibangelayo kuya kuvela ulawulo lwabo olutsha, ulawulo lweNamba. Ngoko ke baya kufuna ukumanyanisa ihlabathi lonke phantsi kwebhanile yabo, yaye baya kudibanisa zonke iinkonzo zehlabathi baze bandikhanyele ngokupheleleyo mna, Yeshua HaMashiach, uYesu Krestu, ongayi kuvunyelwa phakathi kwabo. Yaye inene, inene, andizange ndibe nenxaxheba okanye isabelo nabo. Ngoko ke baya kuhamba ngesantya esikhulu nangenkohlakalo enkulu emfazweni yabo yokulwa nezingcwele, yaye ihlabathi liya kujikela ngokupheleleyo kwiingcwele zembandezelo, yaye bonke aboyisayo ngokubiza egameni lam kude kube sekupheleni baya kuzukiswa ngonaphakade ngomvuzo ongunaphakade nobukhazikhazi obungunaphakade, yaye mkhulu umvuzo wabo, yaye abo boyisayo baya kuzidla ilifa zonke izinto.

Njengoko uluntu lwabo olutsha luvela, kuya kuvela nemithi yam emibini yomnquma, iziphatho zam zezibane ezibini ezimi phambi koThixo womhlaba. Baya kunikwa amandla amakhulu, yaye baya kuprofeta iinyanga ezingama-42 bembathiswe ngamalaphu okuzila; yaye ukuba kukho umntu oya kubenzakalisa, kuya kuphuma umlilo emlonyeni wabo, ubatshise iintshaba zabo: yaye ukuba kukho umntu oya kubenzakalisa, umele abulawe ngale ndlela. Aba baya kuba namandla okuvala izulu, ukuze linganethi ngeentsuku zokuprofeta kwabo: yaye baya kuba namandla phezu kwamanzi ukuwajika abe ligazi, nokubetha umhlaba ngazo zonke izibetho, rhoqo njengoko beya kuthanda. Yaye iya kuba nkulu intiyo yehlabathi ngakubo. Yaye bemi bechasene nemithi yam emibini yomnquma kuya kuba lirhamncwa nomprofeti wobuxoki, yaye omnye uya kwalatha komnye, yaye baya kuzizukisa, yaye zininzi izinto zokunyelisa abaya kuzithetha, yaye mininzi imiqondiso yobuxoki nemimangaliso abaya kuyenza phambi kwabantu.

La marhamncwa aya kwenza imimangaliso emikhulu ukuze akhohlise abo bahleli emhlabeni, kuba kuya kuvunyelwa ukuba enze imiqondiso emininzi yobuxoki nemimangaliso, yaye aya kulawula ngamandla afanayo. Irhamncwa elivela emhlabeni liya kubangela ukuba umlilo uhle emhlabeni phambi kwabantu, yaye liya kukhokelela bonke abantu ukuba bamangaliswe yiyo ngemiqondiso emininzi yobuxoki nemimangaliso. Yaye liya kwenza umfanekiso werhamncwa lize libangele ukuba bonke banqule umfanekiso eliya kuwenza. Yaye kuya kuvunyelwa ukuba linike umoya kumfanekiso werhamncwa ukuze umfanekiso werhamncwa uthethe uze ubangele ukuba bonke abangawunquliyo umfanekiso werhamncwa babulawe. Yaye liya kwenza bonke, abazizityebi nabangamahlwempu, abakhululekileyo namakhoboka, ukuba bamkele uphawu lwayo esandleni sabo sasekunene okanye emabunzini abo.

UKUBA KUKHO UMNTU ONQULA IRHAMNCWA NOMFANEKISO WALO, AZE AMKELE UPHAWU LWALO EBUNZINI LAKHE, OKANYE ESANDLENI SAKHE, YENA UYA KUSELA KWIWAYINI YENGQUMBO KATHIXO, ETHULULWE INGENAMXUBE KWIKOMITYI YOMSINDWANO WAKHE, YAYE UYA KUTHUTHUNJISWA NGOMLILO NESULPHURE, PHAMBI KWEZITHUNYWA EZINGCWELE, NAPHAMBI KWEMVANA: YAYE UMSI WENTUTHUMBO YABO UNYUKA NGONAPHAKADE KANAPHAKADE. YAYE ABANAKUPHUMULA EMINI OKANYE EBUSUKU, ABO BANQULA IRHAMNCWA NOMFANEKISO WALO, NABANI NA OWAMKELA UPHAWU LWEGAMA LALO.

Yaye ngeli xesha itempile yabo iya kwakhiwa, naxa idini lezilwanyana lingasafuneki kuba mna ndiyiMvana kaThixo yaye ndazincama ngenxa yezono zoluntu njengombingeleli omkhulu ngonaphakade ngokohlobo lukaMelkitsedeke. Intlawulelo yenziwe ngenxa yezono zoluntu kanye, konke, ngonaphakade egazini lam elihlawulelayo, elingenasono. Ngoko ke, akusekho dini ngenxa yesono.

Ngoko ke xa imithi yam emibini yomnquma igqibile ubungqina bayo ekupheleni kweenyanga ezingama-42, irhamncwa elinyuka liphuma emngxunyeni ongenasiphelo liya kulwa nabo lize libulale iziphatho zam zezibane ezibini, kuba kunikwe lona ukuba likwenze oko. Yaye izidumbu zabo ziya kuhlala zifile iintsuku ezintathu enesiqingatha ezitratweni, kanye njengoko ihlabathi libhiyozela lize litshintshisane ngezipho ngenxa yokufa kwabo. Yaye kuya kuvela ilizwi elikhulu livela emazulwini lithi, Nyukani nize apha! Yaye baya kuvuselwa kubomi obutsha, yaye baya kwamkelwa emazulwini phambi kweentshaba zabo.

Yaye baya kuyigqibezela itempile yabo yaye umgibe uya kubekwa, kuba nguwuphi umthiyeli obeka umgibe phambi kwentaka? Kuba irhamncwa elivela emhlabeni ngulo ubeka umgibe elizweni, liya kurhwebesha abaninzi kumnatha walo wenkohliso, yaye liya kubangcatsha abantu belizwe. Yaye loo mntu wesono uya kutyhilwa, unyana wentshabalalo, Ongumchasi, yaye uya kuziphakamisa ngaphezu kwako konke okubizwa ngokuba nguThixo, okanye okunqulwayo: ukuze ahlale njengoThixo eTempileni kaThixo, ezibonakalisa ukuba unguThixo. Yaye itempile yabo iya kugqitywa, yaye isikizi lesiphanziso liya kufezwa nguMoshiach wabo wobuxoki. Yaye uya kuqinisekisa umnqophiso nabaninzi iveki enye: yaye phakathi evekini uya kubangela ukuba idini nomnikelo uphele, yaye ngenxa yokusasazeka kwezikizi; uya kulenza kube senkangala, kude kube sekupheleni okumiselweyo kuya kuthululwa phezu kwesiphanziso.

Ngoko ke eyona mbandezelo inkulu iya kwenzeka, imbandezelo engazange ibonwe ngaphambili emhlabeni, yaye inene, ayisayi kuphinda ibekho. Khangelani! Mna, uYeshuah HaMashiach, andiyi kubashiya abantu bam kwintshabalalo engequbuliso, yaye ndisaya kumsindisa uSirayeli, elo ntsalela linamandla eliya kundamkela njengoMesiya wokwenene, kuba uYahweh undidalele oko. Ngoko ke ama-144,000 am aya kunceda abo bebeya kutshabalala nababiza egameni lam. Ndiya kubakhokela inyathelo nenyathelo, yaye ama-144,000 am aya kundenzela imisebenzi emininzi enamandla.

Kuba aba ngabo bathathwayo ekuhlanganisweni kwabangcwele kum, uKrestu Yesu, esibhakabhakeni ekukhaleni kwexilongo lokugqibela, kwaye ixilongo sele liza kukhala. Aba ngabanyanisekileyo bam, kwanabo kwakungafunyanwanga buqhetseba kubo, kuba bazihlambulule njengentombi enyulu egazini leMvana, endayinikela ngonaphakade njengombingeleli wabo omkhulu, kuba mna ndingumbingeleli omkhulu ngonaphakade ngokohlobo lukaMelkitsedeke. Aba nguMtshakazi wam, kwaye baya kundilandela naphi na apho ndiya khona. Yabonani! Ndiya kubenza babe namandla, kwaye akukho nto yakha yabonwa emhlabeni enokuthelekiswa nobuchule endiya kubambathisa ngabo. Baya kwambathiswa ilinen ecikizekileyo, emhlophe, engenabala, kwaye ubuchule babo buya kuba yimbonakaliso yezenzo zabo, kwaye baya kuba nobuchule obukhulu; kuba banyanisekile, babiziwe, kwaye bakhethiwe, kwanangaphambi kokusekwa kwehlabathi nguYehova ngezi njongo.

Ama-144,000 am aya kuhla eNtabeni iZiyon, kwaye ndiya kubakhokela. Baya kuthatha abo ndibakhokelela entlango ukuze bafihlwe kubumnyama obukhulu nakwintshutshiso yobukumkani beentshaba. Baya kukhokelelwa kukhuseleko kwizibetho ezisixhenxe, kuba kwezi zigwebo kukho ubungqongqo bengqumbo kaThixo eza kuthululwa esihlalweni serhamncwa kwaye kuthuthumbise ubukumkani beNamba, kuba sikhulu isohlwayo sabo. Kwaye ama-144,000 aya kubakhokelela kukhuseleko; aya kubaphilisa kumanxeba abo; aya kubanceda kwaye abanike ithemba elikhulu lokunqula igama leNkosi, kude kube sekupheleni ukuba kuyimfuneko. Kuba ukuba bendingayinqumlanga imihla, bekungayi kusinda nanye inyama. Kwaye ngoko baya kugqibezela oko ndiya kubakhokelela kuko.

Kwaye njengoko abathathu abangangcwele besiya kutshabalalisa abo bandamkelayo njengoMesiya wabo, ngokunjalo ingqumbo epheleleyo kaThixo iya kuzalisekiswa ezintlokweni zabo nakubukumanini babo ngokupheleleyo; inene, inene, izitya ezisixhenxe ezizaliswe yingqumbo kaThixo; kwaye isigwebo esikhulu nosizi ziqulethwe ezityeni zabo, kwaye kuya kwenzeka kanye njengoko kubhaliwe:

Isithunywa sokuqala siya kuthulula isitya saso emhlabeni, kwaye abo bamkela uphawu lwabo olungangcwele baya kabethwa ngezilonda ezibi nezibuhlungu ngesitya esiya kuthululwa sisithunywa sokuqala.

Kwaye isithunywa sesibini siya kuthulula isitya saso, kwaye iilwandle ziya kuba njengegazi lomntu ofileyo, kwaye zonke izidalwa zaselwandle eziphilayo ziya kutshabalala.

Kwaye isithunywa sesithathu siya kuthulula isitya saso, sibangele ukuba imilambo nemisinga ijikwe ibe ligazi, kuba baphalaze igazi labangcwele nabaprofeti, kwaye ngoko kufanelekile ukuba banikwe igazi balisele.

Inene inene, isithunywa sesine siya kuthulula isitya saso elangeni, kwaye siya kunikwa ukuba sithuthumbise abantu ngobushushu bomlilo, kwaye abantu baya kutshiswa bubushushu kwaye bathethe izinto ezinkulu nezinyelisa uThixo wasezulwini.

Kwaye isithunywa sesihlanu siya kuthulula isitya saso etroneni yerhamncwa, kwaye ubukumkani balo buya kuba mnyama ngakumbi, ngamandla amakhulu, kwaye baya kuzingcungcuthekisa iilwimi zabo ngenxa yosizi.

Kwaye isithunywa sesithandathu siya kuthulula isitya saso phezu komlambo omkhulu i-Ewufrathe, kwaye amanzi awo aya koma, ukuze kulungiswe indlela yooKumkani baseMpuma.

Kwaye isithunywa sesixhenxe siya kuthulula isitya saso emoyeni: kwaye kuya kuphuma ilizwi elikhulu eTempileni yasezulwini etroneni, lisithi, Kugqityiwe. Kwaye kuya kubakho amazwi, neendudumo, nemibane, kwaye kuya kubakho inyikima enkulu yomhlaba, enjengoko ingazange ibekho okoko abantu babekho emhlabeni, inyikima yomhlaba enamandla angako. Kwaye isixeko esikhulu siya kwahlulwa sibe ziinxalenye ezintathu, kwaye izixeko zeentlanga ziya kuwa. Kwaye ziya kuba njalo ezi zinto.

Ngoko ke qondani, bantwana bam, ukuba uYehova uya kubafihla abakhe kwanangexesha lembandezelo kaYakobi, kwanangexesha lembandezelo enkulu intsalela iya kusindiswa iphile kwaye ihlale ikhuselekile entlango, kwanendawo elungiswe nguThixo ngokwakhe, ukuze babondle apho iwaka elinamakhulu amabini anamashumi amathandathu eentsuku. Kwaye bekungayi kusinda nanye inyama ukuba imihla ibinganqunyulwanga. Kwaye abawileyo baya kuzihlanganisela ukulwa noKumkani wooKumkani neNkosi yeeNkosi, kwaye kubhaliwe njalo kwaye kuya kwenziwa njalo.

Ndabona izulu livulekile, nanko ihashe elimhlophe, nalowo wayekhwele kulo wayebizwa ngokuba nguNyanisekileyo noNyaniso, kwaye ngobulungisa uyagweba kwaye alwe imfazwe. Amehlo akhe ayenjengelangatye lomlilo, kwaye entlokweni yakhe kwakukho izithsaba ezininzi, kwaye wayenegama elibhaliweyo, elalingaziwa mntu ngaphandle kwakhe. Kwaye wayambethe isambatho esifakwe egazini, kwaye igama lakhe libizwa ngokuba, Lilizwi likaThixo. Kwaye imikhosi eyayisezulwini yamlandela ikhwele amahashe amhlophe, yambethe ilinen ecikizekileyo, emhlophe necocekileyo. Kwaye emlonyeni wakhe kuphuma ikrele elibukhali, ukuze ngalo abethe iintlanga: kwaye uya kuzilawula ngentonga yentsimbi: kwaye unyathela isixovulelo sewayini sobushushu nengqumbo kaThixo uSomandla. Kwaye unesambatho sakhe, kwaye ethangeni lakhe kubhaliwe igama, UKUMKANI WOOKUMKANI, NENKOSI YEENKOSI.

Kwaye ngoko isithunywa sam siya kuhla sivela emazulwini, sinesitshixo somngxuma ongenamkhomo kunye netyathanga elikhulu apho siya kubopha uSathana emngxunyeni ongenamkhomo iminyaka eliwaka kwaye sibeke itywina ukuze angaphindi akhohlise iintlanga iminyaka eliwaka. Ekupheleni kolawulo lwam lweminyaka eliwaka, uSathana uya kukhululwa enzonzobileni okwexeshana elifutshane.

Yabonani! Kanye njengoko uYohane uMtyhili wabhala, kuya kuba njalo: Ndabona iitrone, bahlala phezu kwazo, kwaye banikwa isigwebo: ndabona imiphefumlo yabo babenqunyulwe iintloko ngenxa yobungqina bukaYesu, nangenxa yelizwi likaThixo, nababengamnqulanga irhamncwa, nomfanekiso walo, bengamkelanga uphawu lwalo emabunzini abo, okanye ezandleni zabo; kwaye baphila balawula noKrestu iminyaka eliwaka. Kodwa abanye abafileyo abaphindanga baphila de kwaphela iminyaka eliwaka. Olu luvuko lokuqala. Unoyolo kwaye ungcwele lowo unesabelo kuvuko lokuqala: kwabanjalo ukufa kwesibini akunamandla, kodwa baya kuba ngabaBingeleli bakaThixo, nabakaKrestu, kwaye baya kulawula naye iminyaka eliwaka.

Kwaye ekupheleni kolawulo lwam lweminyaka eliwaka uSathana uya kukhululwa entolongweni yakhe okwexeshana elifutshane ukuze abahlanganise njengentlabathi yolwandle. Kwaye uya kuphuma ayokukhohlisa iintlanga ezikwiimbombo ezine zomhlaba, uGogi noMagogi, ukuze abahlanganisele emfazweni: inani labo linjengentlabathi yolwandle. Kwaye baya kunyuka kububanzi bomhlaba, bajikeleze inkampu yabangcwele, nesixeko esithandekayo: kwaye umlilo uya kuhla uvela kuThixo ezulwini, ubatshise. Kwaye umtyholi owabakhohlisayo uya kuphoswa echibini lomlilo nesulfure, apho kuya kubakho irhamncwa nomprofeti wobuxoki, kwaye baya kuthuthunjiswa imini nobusuku ngonaphakade kanaphakade.

Kwaye ngoko kuya kufika uvuko lwabangemalungisa, kuba kubhaliwe njalo nguYohane uMtyhili: Ndabona itrone enkulu emhlophe, nalowo wayehleli kuyo, owathi ubuso bakhe babaleka umhlaba nezulu; kwaye akufunyanwanga ndawo yabo. Ndabona abafileyo, abancinci nabakhulu, bemi phambi koThixo; kwaye iincwadi zavulwa: kwavulwa nenye incwadi, eyiNcwadi yoBomi: kwaye abafileyo bagwetywa ngezo zinto zazibhaliwe ezincwadini, ngokwemisebenzi yabo. Kwaye ulwandle lwakhupha abafileyo ababekulo; kwaye ukufa nelabafileyo bakhupha abafileyo ababekubo: kwaye bagwetywa umntu ngamnye ngokwemisebenzi yakhe. Kwaye ukufa nelabafileyo baphoswa echibini lomlilo. Oku kukufa kwesibini. Kwaye nabani na ongafunyanwanga ebhaliwe eNcwadini yoBomi waphoswa echibini lomlilo.

Kwaye kubhaliwe njalo kwaye kuya kwenziwa njalo, ukuba kukho ukuhlanganisana apho abafileyo kuKrestu baya kuvuswa kwaye abo baphilayo nabasalayo baya kuthathwa kunye nabo emafini, kwaye oku kuya kwenzeka kanye phambi kokuqala kwembandezelo kaYakobi. Emva koko ekupheleni kwembandezelo kaYakobi kuya kwenzeka uvuko lwamalungisa, olwenziwe ngabangcwele bexesha lembandezelo kunye nabo beTestamente eNdala abafela elukholweni, bephile ngokwezithembiso. Kwaye njengoko kubhaliwe kuDaniyeli, kuya kuba njalo: Kodwa hamba indlela yakho kude kube sekupheleni: kuba uya kuphumla, kwaye ume kwisabelo sakho ekupheleni kweentsuku. Kwaye ngoko ukuphela kweentsuku kukugqitywa kweveki yamashumi asixhenxe kaDaniyeli nasekuqaleni kolawulo lwam lweminyaka eliwaka.

Kwaye ekupheleni kolawulo lwam lweminyaka eliwaka nasemva kokuba uSathana ephoselwe kwilifa lakhe lokugqibela kwelo chibi lomlilo elivutha ngonaphakade, kuya kwenzeka uvuko lwabangemalungisa, kwanaloo mgwebo wetrone enkulu emhlophe uya kwenzeka, kwaye nabani na ogama lakhe lingafunyanwanga libhaliwe eNcwadini yoBomi uya kuphoswa echibini lomlilo.

Kwaye ngoko mna, kwanuYesu waseNazarete, kwanayiMvana kaThixo, kwanuKrestu Yesu, okwayiNgonyama yesizwe sakwaYuda, ndiya kuza kugweba ndilwe neNamba, kwaye bonke ubukumkani bayo buya kutyunyuzwa ngokupheleleyo, bungaphindi buvuke. Kwaye irhamncwa nomprofeti wobuxoki baya kuphoswa bephila echibini lomlilo, kwaye uSathana uya kubotshwa ngetyathanga elikhulu emngxunyeni ongenamkhomo iminyaka eliwaka, kwaye ngoko ulawulo lwam lweminyaka eliwaka luya kuqala, kwaye ulawulo lwam luya kuba sebulungiseni. Yabonani! Ndizisa uMtshakazi wam kunye nomkhosi wookumkani nababingeleli endiya kulawula nabo umhlaba ngobulungisa, kwaye ndiya kulawula iintlanga ngentonga yentsimbi. Ngoko yibani nethemba elikhulu, bantwana bam, kuba ukugqitywa kwezinto zonke kusondele, kwaye eli xesha leminyaka esixhenxe lembandezelo enkulu, kwanembandezelo kaYakobi, liya kulandelwa lulawulo lwam olulungileyo, kwaye uYehova uya kuba yinto yonke kuyo yonke into.

Kwaye ngoko ndininikile ithemba elikhulu, bantwanana bam, kwanokuba ninamathelise iingqondo zenu kuvuyo olubekwe phambi kwenu kuba iintlungu zembandezelo kaYakobi azithelekiseki kwaye azifanelekanga ukuba zikhankanywe xa kuthelekiswa novuyo lwanaphakade olubekwe phambi kwenu. Yabonani! Kubhaliwe nguYohane uMtyhili: Aba ngabo baphuma kwimbandezelo enkulu, bahlambe iingubo zabo, bazenza mhlophe egazini leMvana. Ngenxa yoko, baphambi kwetrone kaThixo, kwaye bamkhonza imini nobusuku eTempileni yakhe: kwaye lowo uhleli eTroneni uya kuhlala phakathi kwabo. Abayi kuphinda balambe, abayi kuphinda banxanwe, nelanga aliyi kubatshisa, nabuphi na ubushushu. Kuba iMvana, ephakathi kwetrone, iya kubondla, kwaye iya kubakhokelela kwimithombo ephilayo yamanzi: kwaye uThixo uya kosula zonke iinyembezi emehlweni abo. Baya kuba ngabaBingeleli bakaThixo, nabakaKrestu, kwaye baya kulawula naye iminyaka eliwaka.

Kwaye ngoko lilo ithemba lenu, zithandwa zam, ukuze ninyamezele kude kube sekupheleni, kwaye elenu lithemba lanaphakade, kuba u-El Shaddai, uThixo uSomandla, uya kudala izulu elitsha nomhlaba omtsha apho kuhlala ubulungisa, kwaye niya kunikwa ukufikelela kweso sixeko singcwele silungiswe saza sahonjiswa nguYehova ngokwakhe njengomtshakazi olungiselelwe umyeni, kwaye niya kutya kumthi wobomi. Kwaye njengoko kubhaliwe: Lowo woyisayo, uya kuzidla ilifa zonke izinto, kwaye ndiya kuba nguThixo wakhe, kwaye yena uya kuba ngunyana wam.

Yabonani! NdinguYeshua HaMashiach, kwaye ndithumele amazwi am ngomkhonzi wam, kwaye ngomkhonzi wam amazwi am athunyelwe.

Yabonani! Ivangeli engunaphakade ityhiliwe yathunyelwa ngexesha layo elifanelekileyo, kwaye la mazwi aya kugcinwa, kwaye aya kufumana amehlo, iindlebe, neentliziyo zabo bonke abo athunyelwe kubo.

Yabonani! Gcinani umlindo wenu kwaye ningazishiyi izikhundla zenu, bantu bam, kuba amaxesha afikile.

Yabonani! Amaxesha ngoku!
