Yivani amazwi kaYeshua HaMashiach, onguKrestu Yesu, nize niyiqonde intetho yam ukuze niqonde imvelaphi yokwenyani, uhlobo, nenjongo yeBhabhiloni Efihlakeleyo. Kuba, inene, lilo iLizwe leNyoka eneeNtsiba, kwayona iAmeruKa, lona ilizwe elirhubuluza ngobuqhetseba, elitya abemi balo, lixhele abamsulwa, libingelele ngotata nomama ngobuninzi obukhulu ukusukela kumaxesha amandulo, kude kube ngoku. Lona ilizwe eliqalekisiweyo elingcolisa abemi balo ngobuqhetseba, nangobuninzi beentsalela ezingcolisayo nezonakalisayo zamadini anegazi nankohlakalo avela kumaxesha adlulileyo angcwatywe kulo lonke olu hlanga lungcolileyo kwiinduli ezininzi zemifanekiso, izithethe, ukutshiswa kweTofete, negazi lamadini eliqukuqela ngokukhululekileyo. Lona elo gazi linyakamise umhlaba walo apho umhlaba ukhala ucela ubulungisa ngegazi eliye lachithwa, nelisachithwa ngobuninzi obukhulu. Yona le mihlaba ingcolileyo ikhala ngamandla nangokuvakalayo isiya emazulwini icela ubulungisa ngalo mzuzwana, lo gama umhlaba ugxadazela kakhulu njengendoda enxilileyo ekulungele ukuwa, ifuna umgwebo wobulungisa ongenakulibazisa.

Nawe Bhabhiloni Efihlakeleyo, ewe wena: Ulilizwekazi elingcwele nelingcolileyo, ilizwe elamiwa mva kwiintsuku zikaNowa, ilizwe elanikezelwa kuHeylel Ben Shachar neendlela zenyoka, ilizwe elinyakanyiswe ligazi labamsulwa ngezithethe nezenzo zenzala engcolileyo yamarhamba nabangcolisi abahamba bayongcolisa kumaxesha amandulo. Lona ilizwe eligcinwe ngobuqhetseba ngezandla ezifihliweyo zabo bawayo, lona elagcinelwa laza lajongelwa ukuba libe yiAtlantis yabo eNtsha, lona uhlanga oluyilwe lwanikezelwa ukuze kuvele irhamncwa eliphuma elwandle nerhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni. Lona uhlanga oluzenza ngathi lungamaKristu, kodwa eneneni luhlanga lwabakhonzi bakasathana abafihlakeleyo abazinzisa baza bahlala kamnandi kumaziko alo angcolileyo nanangcolisayo (ukusuka kwelona lincinci ukuya kwelona likhulu) kwaye ngala maziko alawulwa yimibutho efihlakeleyo ezinekele kuHeylel Ben Shachar (abambiza ngobuxoki nangokungazi ngokuba nguLusifa), bezicingela ngokungazi ukuba bangabakhanyayo nabakhanyiselweyo, kwanakwinkohliso nasekuqhatheni kwabo apho bona ngokwabo babotshelelwe ngonaphakade kwizifungo zabo ezingcwele, beqhatha, kodwa ngalo lonke elo xesha beqhathwa ngabo babotshelelwe kubo ngonaphakade njengonyana besono kwabo bawayo ngonaphakade.

Makwazeke, ukuba iBhabhiloni Efihlakeleyo yiUnited States Of America, eyayibizwa mandulo ngokuba yiAmeru-Ca, iLizwe leNyoka eneeNtsiba, eye yarhubuluza yaziguqula yaba leliya henge elikhulu elihleli phezu kwamanzi amaninzi kwaye luhlanga olwenziwa, ukuze irhamncwa eliphuma elwandle nerhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni zivuke eluthuthwini lwalo ngamaxesha azo okutyhilwa. Lona uhlanga olukrexezayo, lona uhlanga lokuhenyuza, uhlanga ngokwenene olulihenge elikhulu elizibanga lingumlandeli olilungisa kaYesu waseNazarete; kodwa eneneni luhlanga olunabakhi balo abakhululekileyo kwiLiwa leSiseko lokwenyani, lona elo litye lembombo elikhulu abakhubeka kulo abangakholwayo, lona uYesu waseNazarete onguYeshua HaMashiach.

Inene, iAmeruKa iye yaba yile United States yanamhlanje, kwaye luhlanga olwasekwa zizandla zabo ezingcwele nezifihliweyo, lona ilizwe lemibutho efihlakeleyo enikezelwe kwinyoka ngezinto ezifihlakeleyo; kwaye njengoko amandla oMnqamlezo oyiRozi ahlasela iAmeruKa, kwasekwa nesigqubuthelo sabo sokugqibela seAtlantis eNtsha. Lona icebo elalifihlwe ixesha elide elaliya kudibanisa lize lihlanganise la mahlelo enyoka kwihlabathi liphela ngokutshabalalisa nokubingelela abaninzi abavela kudidi lwababingeleli bezi mpucuko ziwileyo, nabaninzi abavela kwezi mpucuko ngokwazo ababeya kufuna ngenkani nangobuyatha ukumisa yona le nyoka babezinikele kuyo ngezifungo ezininzi ezinegazi ukuba bayikhonze ngokupheleleyo, bengayiqondi imeko yesidalwa sokwenyani abazibophelele kuso, nangona, iintsalela zaba bantu zikule mihlaba nanamhlanje.

Ngoko ke uyiAmeruKa: Kuba ndithetha nelizwekazi ukusuka emantla ukuya emazantsi nokusuka empuma ukuya entshona elilizwe elinikezelwe kuloo nyoka indala ineeNtsiba eye yangcolisa yaza yonakalisa le mihlaba ngeenjongo zayo, njengoko ubukhosi buye bavuka bawa kwimida yalo, kwanobukhosi obuninzi obungabhalwanga benyoka, lona uhlanga lwenyoka enentsiba nebhabhayo olunikezelwe kwimibutho elawulwa ngabafazi nedemokhrasi apho izihlwele ezikhohlakeleyo zinokulawula phantsi kwempembelelo kaHeylel Ben Shachar. Ngaphezu koko, inzala yakhe yamarhamba iye yayingcolisa ngokupheleleyo imihlaba yeBhabhiloni efihlakeleyo, elilizwe elamiwa njengoko umntu wayebhadula emhlabeni ukusuka apho uMkhombe wema khona kwiNtaba yeArarati ngelo xesha lonke. Ukusuka apho, abantu bayalelwa nguYahweh, uMkhulu onguMkhulu, bebotshelelwe phantsi komnqophiso kaNowa ukuba bazisasaze baze bahlakazeke baze bawuzalise kwakhona umhlaba ngobulungisa bomnqophiso; nangona inzala kaHam endaweni yoko yazihlanganisa ngokungeva okukhulu ukuze izenzele igama eLizweni laseShinare, isithi: Yizani, masizakhele inqaba eyomeleleyo ukuze sizigcine, kwaye ngokwenene silwe namazulu kuba uNimrodi wazenza ingcibi enamandla phambi kweNkosi, ingcibi enamandla yabantu awayebazingela ukuze adlale.

Baza abantu beza kwilizwe lenyoka eneeNtsiba bevela entshona, behamba ngasempuma bewela ibhulorho yomhlaba yaseBering njengoko isaziwa namhlanje, kwaye beza ngolwandle bevela empuma besiya entshona bewela iAtlantiki, ngeendlela ezahlukeneyo nezininzi zorhwebo nohambo beza kule mihlaba igcinelwe nguHeylel Ben Shachar iinjongo zakhe ezimbi. Lona ilizwe elabangwa ngabo bawayo ngokupheleleyo kwihlabathi elalikhona ngoko, lona ilizwe apho bangcwaba iimfihlo ezininzi abaya kuvunyelwa ngokukhawuleza nangokungalindelekanga ukuba bazifumane, kanye njengoko umthinteli wam esusiwe kwaye njengoko ulawulo lwabo olufutshane luvunyelwe usindiso lukaYakobi, kwaye kananjalo ukuze bawe kumgwebo ovuthayo ngokwabo njengoko lo mcimbi uqhubeka ukusuka kwiintsuku zikaNowa, ukuze batshatyalaliswe ngokupheleleyo, kuba amaxesha abo bawayo afikelele esiphelweni, kwaye ndim uKrestu Yesu, yona iNgonyama yeSizwe sakwaYuda eya kutyumza intloko yale nyoka yamandulo ekugqityweni komgwebo.

Kwaye kunjalo ukuba uKanan wafumana oko abalindi bakungcwabayo, kwaye wakhokelwa ngokweenkanuko zentliziyo yakhe njengoko wahlasela waza waba ilizwe elalingelolakhe ukuba alihlale ngokwaphula umnqophiso kaNowa. Kodwa wayenqwenela iintsuku zakudala ezazitshatyalaliswe kuloo nogumbe mkhulu apho ihlabathi elalikhona ngoko latshabalala ngenxa yokuba uSathana wayehenda abalindi abawayo ukuba bazidibanise nabafazi ukuze bavelise inzala yabo eqalekisiweyo. Yona le nzala ingeyonto ngaphandle kwemigqakhwe nabalahliweyo, lona ukulinganisa ubomi apho amanyala abo abotshelelwe ngonaphakade ukuba enze iinkanuko ezimbi nezibolileyo zooyise, kwaye ukwenza iinkanuko zooyise babotshelelwe ngonaphakade. Ngoko ke, qondani ukuba lo mungu mkhohlakeleyo, ongamasikizi, amanyala ngokupheleleyo, awunangcambu nasebe ezulwini, emhlabeni, naphantsi komhlaba kwaye uya kuncothulwa ngokupheleleyo, ngonaphakade kanaphakade, phantsi kwelanga ngokwesithembiso somnqophiso sika-Adonai-YHVH uMkhulu onguMkhulu! Kwaye baya kubotshwa batshiswe kuloo mlilo mkhulu wembuyekezo apho impethu yabo ingafiyo kwaye bonke abaphoswa emalangatyeni baya kwamkela izono zabo izihlandlo ezininzi ezifubeni zabo kumgwebo ovuthayo ongunaphakade nobulungisa abangasoze bawubaleke.

Kwaye kunjalo, ukuba uHeylel Ben Shachar, yona inkosana encinci eyoyisiweyo, lona mqhayisi mkhulu nomngcolisi wako konke okumsulwa, wahamba waya kutyala umungu wakhe kwisitiya sikaYahweh, uMkhulu onguMkhulu, sona eso sitiya satyalwa xa uElohim wathi makubekho ukukhanya kwabakho ukukhanya. Kuba ekuqaleni, uElohim wenza amazulu nomhlaba, waza umhlaba waba yinto engenamimo nalilize xa ubumnyama beza phezu kobunzulu. Obo bumnyama yayinguHeylel Ben Shachar nesinye kwisithathu seengelosi zakhe ezalandela imvukelo yakhe zaza zaphoswa emhlabeni ngehlazo elikhulu, nangokoyiswa. Kwaye nguHeylel Ben Shachar, yona inkosana encinci eyoyisiweyo eyakhala ngehlazo nangokoyiswa njengoko yaphoswa emhlabeni isuka kwizulu lamazulu ngomsindo omkhulu, igcinelwe umgwebo wokugqibela novuthayo emlilweni waloo mini mkhulu, yona le mililo ivutha ngawona malangatye ashushu namsulwa aluhlaza alungele ukwamkela bonke abazibandakanye nemvukelo kaSathana ukusuka kwabancinci ukuya kwabakhulu kubukumkani babo bawayo.

UElohim wathi "Makubekho ukukhanya kwabakho ukukhanya!" Emva koko uElohim wamisa, umhlaba owamiselwa ngokutsha ngomyalelo wakhe kuphela, nangamazwi akhe kuphela, nangecebo lakhe kuphela indalo yabunjwa, yenziwa, yadalwa. Ngamazwi akhe anamandla nanaphakade namandla necebo lakhe kuphela. Kuba uYHVH, uMkhulu onguMkhulu, akadingi mcebisi nangona itrone yakhe ijikelezwe ngamawaka alishumi aphindwe ngamawaka alishumi, kwaye akukho sidalwa sinjengoThixo ongunaphakade woBulungisa, nangona bexoka kwaye bezenza ngendlela elusizi! Kwaye wenziwa njalo umhlaba nako konke okuqulethwe kuwo kwaye loo mhlaba wanikwa uAdam, kuba umntu wenzelwa umhlaba, nomhlaba wenzelwa umntu, owadalwa ngokwenene ngokomfanekiso womdali wakhe, uYHVH - uMkhulu onguMkhulu!

Kanti loo nyoka inobuqili yalihlasela ilifa lika-Adam ngamaqhinga ayo, lona uhlaselo nokwaphulwa okwenziwa ngokupheleleyo eKalvari, xa mna, Yesu waseNazarete, ndathetha amazwi anaphakade, "Kugqityiwe!"; kwaye kunjalo, ukuba ndiluthengile uluntu, umhlaba, nako konke okukuwo ngegazi lam lentlawulelo. Kwaye ngoko ke abo bawayo baye bahamba bayongcolisa baze baguqule indalo kwimfezeko yayo yantlandlolo eyadalwa ngayo. Ngoko ke, qondani ukuba aba bawayo bayaqhayisa ukuba bangoothixo kanti bonke bangabazenzisi abazenzisayo. Selah.

Ngoko ke, yivani amazwi kaYeshua HaMashiach, uKrestu Yesu njengoko ndisiza kutyhilela ibandla lam, umzimba wam, nomtshakazi wam inyaniso malunga naloo mihlaba yonakeleyo nengcolileyo apho uninzi lwenu lwazalelwa khona, luhlala khona. Kuba ooyihlo babhadula emhlabeni besohlwaywa phantsi kuka-Adonai-YHVH, uMkhulu onguMkhulu, kwaye kwakungaphantsi kwesohlwayo esikhulu apho uYahweh wazikhupha izizwe zasemantla zakwaSirayeli phakathi kweentlanga ephethe incwadi yoqhawulo-mtshato. Apho, zihlakazekile phakathi kweentlanga ziye zabhadula zaza zagcinwa, zaza zalondolozwa ukusukela ngoko, kwaye ezininzi zazo ngoku ziyinxalenye kaSirayeli kaThixo, ngonaphakade kanaphakade! Zigcinwe ngumfundisi wazo, kuba zabambelela kumthetho kaMoses njengoko zazihamba zisiya kwimihlaba yasemantla emva kokuba zoyisiwe ngokungeva ngezandla zama-Asiriya. Ngoko ke izizwe zasemantla zaqhawulwa umtshato zaza zaphoselwa ezintlangeni, kodwa zazisele zisasazekile ekuthinjweni xa amaBhabhiloni oyisa aza ohlwaya uBukumkani baseMazantsi, bona abangevayo phakathi koobawo abavela kwizizwe zakwaYuda noBhenjamin nabanye, abenza uBukumkani bakwaYuda ngeentsuku zakudala. Bona abo baba yiYudiya xa i-Idumea noYuda zadityaniswa ngencam yekrele.

Kwaye ngoko ke iigusha ezilahlekileyo zeNdlu kaSirayeli ziye zasasazwa phakathi kweentlanga ngenxa yokungeva kwazo, nangona intsalela isoloko ihleli kwilizwe lesithembiso unanamhla. UBukumkani baseMantla basasazwa phakathi kweentlanga zasemantla, kumazwe kaGogi noMagogi, kulo lonke elaseYurophu, naseMerika baya khona. Kwaye ngoko ke abazalwana babo kunye namalungu esizwe esinye avela kuBukumkani baseMazantsi nawo oyiswa ngamaBhabhiloni. Kodwa, babuya bayokwakha kwakhona itempile yesibini kwaye ekuhambeni kwexesha basasazwa phesheya ngeentsuku zikaVespasian noTitus, kwaye kwakhona ngexesha lemvukelo ekhokelwa nguSimon Bar Kokhba, xa abaninzi babantu bam basasazwa ngakumbi kumazwe asemazantsi nasempuma yeYerusalem ukuya emantla e-Afrika naseAleksandriya yaseYiputa nakwezinye iindawo ezininzi baye babhadula ekohlwayweni kwabo. Kodwa nabo baye balondolozwa nguMoses, nangona abaninzi bebukhobokeni buka-Esawu, noAshkenazi, kwaye abaninzi babo bakhohlisiwe kwaye balindele uMahdi wabo, endaweni kaYeshua HaMashiach onguMesiya wabo wokwenyani, nomhlanguli kaSirayeli othenjiswe mandulo ngu-Adonai-YHVH, Selah.

Kodwa, njengoko kubhaliwe, kunjalo ngokwenene, ukuba abantu bam abaninzi abazi kwaye baphantsi kobumfama obuyinxalenye kwiziqu zabo zokwenyani nakwizazisi zabo. Bade bancothulwe ingcambu kuYahweh, uMkhulu onguMkhulu, apho umnquma wendalo uthatha inxaxheba ekutyebeni kwengcambu, yona le ngcambu inguYehovah-YHVH, uMkhulu onguMkhulu.

Kodwa, abaninzi babantu bam baye bazivumela ukuba bajikwe ngokungazi omnye komnye ngobuxoki nentiyo kwaye kubangele ukungazi okunzulu ngemvelaphi yabo yokwenyani, kanye njengoko baye bafundiswa ukuba bakholelwe kubuxoki bale nzala yabapristi benyoka kulo naluphi na uhlanga abazinzise kulo, yona leyo nzala yabapristi benyoka abakhonza uHeylel Ben Shachar, loo serafi ivuthayo newileyo egcinelwe imililo yesohlwayo esingunaphakade nomgwebo apho imililo ithwele esona sohlwayo sikrakra kule nyoka ikhohlakeleyo eye yazingcolisa ngemvukelo engenangqondo, ide ibanjwe kwimeko eyoyisiweyo newileyo angasoze achache kuyo.

Kwaye ngoko ke abantu belizwe lam nabazalwana bam baye bafundiswa ukuba bathiyane ngeendlela ezininzi zobuxoki ezilungiselelwe ukufundisa izihlwele. Kwaye kubhaliwe kwathiwa: Ezi zinto zintandathu uyazithiya uYehova; ewe, zixhenxe ezingamasikizi emphefumlweni wakhe: amehlo aqwayingayo, ulwimi oluxokayo, nezandla eziphalaza igazi elimsulwa, intliziyo ecinga amayelenqe amabi, iinyawo ezikhawulezayo ukubalekela ebubini, ingqina elixokayo elithetha ubuxoki, nalowo uhlwayela ingxabano phakathi kwabazalwana.

Kunjalo ke, ukuba abantwana boBukumkani baseZulwini baye bafundiswa ngobuqhetseba, ngenkohliso, nangentiyo eninzi ukuba bona ngokwabo bangabathimbi babazalwana babo ngexesha lorhwebo lwamakhoboka olwawela iAtlantiki. Apho batyhola abantwana boBukumkani baseZulwini ngezono zomungu owaba abantwana koomama nootata, nowaba ootata noomama nabantwana kumazwe angengawabo; abathi emva koko bathengiswa ebukhobokeni eAmeruKa, (nakwezinye iindawo) ngentiyo enkulu kulo mungu ngabamelwane babo ababezizizwe ezingabamelwane ezazisilwa ezabathengisa ezandleni zala madoda akhohlakeleyo kumaxesha amandulo. Ngoko ke qondani ukuba ezi zenzo zimbi zibhalwe ngokugqibeleleyo emazulwini kwaye ubulungisa nembuyekezo ziya kuna ngokukhawuleza kubemi bomhlaba abasafuna intshabalalo kaYakobi; endaweni yoko intshabalalo yabo iya kubafikela ngokukhawuleza nangokungalindelekanga ngoku, Selah.

Kwaye kunjalo, ngemihla yakudala, umnombo kaKanan wazonakalisa ngokuzidibanisa nabawileyo, kwanangemihla yakudala, kwaye njengoko babezingcolisa esonweni bazinikela ngokupheleleyo kwabawileyo Ezweni laseShinare, naphantsi komthunzi waloo nqaba inde yaseBhabheli, yona kanye inqaba uYHVH, MNA NDINGUYE OMkhulu, awayidilizayo ekoyisweni kwabo.

Kuba kubhaliwe kwathiwa: Emva kokuba umhlaba wonakele ngamanzi ngemihla kaNowa, umhlaba wahlulwa ngamaqashiso elifa phakathi koonyana bakaNowa ngemihla kaPelege. Kwaye iinkosana zikaNimrodi zacebisana kunye noFuti, uMitsrayim, uKushe, noKanan neentsapho zabo ukuba bakhe isixeko sabo kwaye kuso kubekho inqaba eyomeleleyo, inqaba ende ukuze bakwazi ukulwa namazulu kwaye bazihlanganise ukuze bazenzele igama ngokuvukela umthetho kaYahweh wokuba baqhame bande ukuze baphinde bawuzalise umhlaba. Kodwa zinjalo iindlela zomntu wemvelo ongakholwayo, othi, ngeenkanuko zakhe nekratshi lakhe akhonze isono aze ngoko akhonze uyise wesono onguMtyholi, ngendlela yokungathobeli kukaYahweh, MNA NDINGUYE OMkhulu, ngenxa yesono esisenyameni yomntu wemvelo.

Owu, mntu, umqonde kakubi umdali wakho, ucinga ukuba yinto elula ukuphikisana noYHVH, uMkhulu onguMNA. Nizivumele ngobuyatha ukuba nikholelwe ukuba uluntu lunokuwatyeshela amazwi omdali walo xa enikwa kumgwebo obhengeziweyo phambi kokuba kufike intlekele, kwanentlekele eya kuvala abangathobeliyo nabanentliziyo elukhuni kumgwebo ongunaphakade.

Kuba lilungelo nembeko enikwe abo baya kunqula igama likaYesu kude kube sekupheleni kwabo, kwaye kungekhona imbeko ebekelwe abangathobeliyo ngenkani nangeenkani abaza kuthathwa emgwebeni ngequbuliso nangokungalindelekanga ngoku, kuba imigwebo kaYahweh, uMkhulu onguMNA, ijoliswe, kwaye ilinganiswe ngeenkcukacha ezigqibeleleyo kwisidalwa ngasinye esiwela phantsi kwemigwebo enjalo. Kwaye le migwebo iya kuwa kanye njengoko imisebenzi yabo ibinjalo, ngoko ke kufuneka ungcangcazele kakhulu wena mntu nokuba ungubani na kwaye uphi na onokuphakamisa isithende sakhe ngolo hlobo.

NdinguKristu Yesu, kwanalowo uzayo emafini, kwananjengoko kwathenjiswa kwimibhalo yam engcwele, kwananjengoko kubhaliwe esibhalweni.

NdinguYesu uKristu, kwanalowo uphethe elo xilongo linamandla esandleni, kwaye ngelo xesha kanye isidumo esikhulu, nesandi somculo esilizwi laso elinamandla abathembekileyo kuKristu baya kuhlanganiswa.

NdinguMyeni wenu, zithandwa zam, kwaye ndiyasondela kumtshakazi wam kuba uBawo unike umyalelo wokuba ndiye kuhlanganisa umtshakazi wam omhle nothembekileyo, neendwendwe zethu.

Ndiyeza kuni zithandwa zam, kwaye ndiya kubahlanganisa bonke abathembekileyo kuKristu kum ngelo xesha kanye eligqibeleleyo elinikiweyo nelikhethiweyo nelagqitywa nguYahweh, uMkhulu onguMNA endilithobelayo icebo lakhe losindiso, kwaye ndililawule ngegunya lonke, kuba ndimnye ngobunye nangenjongo noMkhulu onguMNA, kwaye ndinako ukuthanda kwam endisenokuya ndikulandelise. Nangona kunjalo, ndikwangulowo usoloko esenza ukuthanda kukaBawo ngentobelo yonke yobulungisa, kuba ndinguloo nyana womntu!

Kunjalo ke. Zilungiseleleni Mtshakazi wam, kwanani nonke bathembekileyo. Kuba aninalo ixesha, kwaye sele nahluliwe kwabo baya kusala kumlilo wokucikida, kwaye ngakumbi kwabo baya kuwa kulo mgwebo uphindwe kabini ungenayo, oya kuwa kanzima nangonaphakade phezu kweAmeruKa, kwanelizwe lenyoka enentsiba, kwanelo henge likhulu elihleli phezu kwamanzi amaninzi. Kuba lo mhlaba uya kuhlanjululwa ngokupheleleyo ngomgwebo ovuthayo kuko konke oko uHeylel Ben Shachar akwenzileyo ukuze awudyobhe kwaye awungcolise, kwaye ngenene ngenene, zonke iindlela zakhe ezingalunganga nayo yonke loo nzala yamarhamba emlandelayo iya kususwa emhlabeni ngomgwebo ovuthayo ngonaphakade.

Ngoko ke, lungiselelanani kwaye nizilungiselele. Aninalo ixesha. Ndiniyalele ukuba nizifihle bantu bam. Kwanobuxoki nentsomi yobuyatha engayi kwenzeka!

Ngoko ke, lungiselelanani kwaye nizilungiselele zithandwa zam, kuba ndiyeza kuni. Kwaye andiyi kunishiya kumgwebo waboni kwaye nonke niya kuzifumana ngequbuliso nimi kwigumbi letrone likaYHVH, apho umkhosi wezulu ulindele ubukho benu ngozuko olukhulu, umvuzo, uvuyo, nobomi obungunaphakade obunikezelwe ngokupheleleyo nguAdonai-YHVH, ebusweni bukaYesu Kristu, ngqo kwimizimba yenu engunaphakade yozuko eniza kuma kuyo nihlangulwe ngokupheleleyo.

Nikulungele na Zithandwa zam?

Kuba esandleni sam, kuxilongo. Ixilongo elikhazimlisiweyo. Ixilongo elizukileyo. Ixilongo elikhethekileyo. Ixilongo elilungiselelwe iindlebe zabathembekileyo, elikhazimlisiweyo licocekile. Kwanexilongo elilungiselelwe isandi esiya kuqondwa kuphela ngabathembekileyo kuKristu, kwanabo bonke endibalungiselele ukuva nokwamkela amaza alo aguqula ubomi aza kuniguqula nonke ngequbuliso nangokungalindelekanga ngoku, kwaye niya kuguqulwa ngonaphakade nonke, ningaze nonakale okanye niguqulwe kule mfezeko imangalisayo endiya kunidala ngokutsha nonke ngamandla amakhulu nangokokugqibela ngoku. Kwaye niya kumangaliswa ngonaphakade ngendalo entsha enamandla eniya kwenziwa kuyo nonke ngamandla amakhulu nangcwele nangobulungisa nangokudala ngonaphakade.

Amen, makwenziwe njalo ngelo xesha ligqibeleleyo lomyalelo othenjisiweyo.
