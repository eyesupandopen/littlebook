Ngoku Lixesha

Lixesha, bantwana bam, lixesha. Lixesha lokuba nigoduke, xa kuvakala ixhilongo ibandla lam lihlanganiswe, apho uYahweh alindele khona ngovuyo entliziyweni yakhe ukufika kwenu ngesiquphe. Lixesha lokuba nigoduke, xa isandi sexhilongo sibiza abantwana bOsenyangweni ukuba baye apho zihlala khona zityhutyha khona izithunywa zezulu. Lixesha bantwana bam lokuba ndinihlanganise ndinisusa kwiimbombo zone zomhlaba, kwanabo bonke abalele ukufa bekuKristu baya kuvuka kuqala, nani niphilayo niseleyo niya kuxwilelwa phezulu kunye nabo emafini. Kuba mna Kristu Yesu ndiyiNkosi noMsindisi wenu ophilayo, kwaye ndinibizela ekhayeni lenu emazulwini apho imivuzo yenu ilindele khona, apho ndinilungiselele indawo endlwini kaBawo, kuba endlwini kaBawo zininzi iindawo zokuhlala. Ndinibizela ekhaya ukuba nime phambi kweNkosi yenu, ukuze bonke bamkele izinto ezenziweyo besesemzimbeni, nokuba zilungile nokuba zimbi, ukuze bonke bamkele umvuzo wabo eNkosini, kwaye bonke bathathe iindawo zabo kwimiqolo yasezulwini elungiselelwe umntu ngamnye kuni.

Yabonani! NdinguYeshua HaMashiach, kwaye ndingumyeni osondelayo ukuba ahlanganise umtshakazi wam kuba amaxesha omtshato wethu afikile, apho ndiya kungena kumnqophiso ongunaphakade nabanyulwa bam, kwanabo bazilungisileyo, kwaye ndibahlambile egazini lam lentlawulelo kuba beze kum ngokupheleleyo baza banikela iintliziyo zabo zonke kum eluthandweni nasekunzinikeleni. Nangona bengakhange bahambe ngokugqibeleleyo, kwaye abaninzi boyisa isono esibi, bazihlamba ngokupheleleyo egazini lemvana, kwaye ngoku bemi phambi kwam behlanjululwe ngokupheleleyo ezonweni zabo kwaye banyulu njengeentombi ezingazange zenza umbulo kwaye zazigcina kude nomubi. Yabonani! Aboyisi bam endibaxolele kuzo zonke izono kwaye ndagalela i-oli ezibaneni zabo kuba beze kum ngamaxesha abo entswelo kwaye ndababonisa indlela yokuthena iintambo zezibane zabo kwaye ndabaphilisa ndabahlukanisela mna, kwanabo bonke uYahweh abanyule kwangaphambi kokusekwa kwehlabathi ukuba babe ngumtshakazi wam kwaye babe nam ngonaphakade, kwaye baya kulawula nam njengooKumkani nababingeleli abangonaphakade kuba babizwa njalo, bangcwalisiwe kwaye banyulwe kum, mna Kristu Yesu iNkosi yabo evusiweyo.

Kwaye abayi-144,000 bam baya kuguqulwa batywinwe kwaye badalwe ngokutsha ngokupheleleyo ngeenjongo ababizelwe zona nabalungiselelwe ukuzifezekisa. Inene, inene, wonke umphefumlo okholiweyo kwigama lam elinamandla waza wandenza iNkosi yawo engunaphakade uya kuguqulwa ngokupheleleyo aze adalwe ngokutsha njengesidalwa esitsha, kwaye baya kubunjwa njengam. Kwaye abasayi kuphinda bone, nokuba kungokuzidla okanye ukungahloneli Thixo kwaye abasayi kuphinda baqalekiswe, kwaye akusayi kuphinda kubekho ukugula okanye intlungu, okanye ukufa okanye usizi oluya kuze lubachukumise kwakhona kuba izinto zangaphambili zidlule kwaye ukukhanya kwenene ngoku kuyakhanya. Kwaye ndim, mna Kristu Yesu, oza kuhamba ngoku azityhile kuYakobi kwaye nabani na obiza igama lam elinamandla kude kube sekupheleni uya kusindiswa, kwanabo bonke abemi bomhlaba abamiselwe ubomi obungunaphakade kwaye kuya kubakho isihlwele esikhulu esiya kundamkela njengoMesiya waso kwaye ndiya kube ndibasindise ngokugqibeleleyo.

Ndiyeza kuni, zithandwa zam, kwaye amaxesha ngala ngoku. Ndilinde ixesha lonke lofefe kwaye ndisindise abaninzi, kwaye abaninzi baya kuvuselwa kubutsha bobomi kum, mna Kristu Yesu iNkosi yabo engunaphakade, kwaye abafileyo bekuKristu baya kuvuka kuqala, kwaye ixhilongo lokugqibela lilungile ngoku. Imiqondiso emininzi imimangaliso nezimanga zigubungele umhlaba ngobuhle bazo kwaye imiqondiso ayipheli kwihlabathi liphela ngoku eyazisa ukubonakala kwam, eyazisa umtshato weMvana kumtshakazi wayo othembekileyo, kwaye namagumbi esidlo emazulwini alungisiwe kwaye akulungele.

Kwaye kunjalo, umbhiyozo wasezulwini ulindele abathembekileyo bam, ababiziweyo, nabanyuliweyo, kwaye ngoku lixesha lokuba baphumle ngonaphakade emisebenzini yabo abayisebenzileyo emhlabeni ezindlwini zabo zodongwe, kwaye basebenze enyameni njengabasebenzi kunye nam kwaye bahlanganisa baza bazisa abaninzi kum, iNkosi uYesu Kristu ukuze bamkele usindiso olungunaphakade ngonaphakade. Kuba nangona inyama yabo yayonakele esonweni nguSathana, ndibanike injongo ndabazibiza ukuba bayishiye ngonaphakade inkohlakalo ababegwetyelwe yona sisono.

Kwaye ke banabo obu butyebi ezityeni zodongwe bokuba amandla kaThixo aphila ahlale kubo, kwaye benza imisebenzi emininzi enamandla egameni lam, kwaye ndim owabanika amandla okuba babe ziindlalifa zosindiso neendlalifa zobomi obungunaphakade kwaye ndibahlangule ndabanika eso siqinisekiso saphakade sokuzalwa ngokutsha kwaye ndibatywinile bonke ngaloo moya ungcwele wesithembiso, kwanelo tywina lesithembiso elingenakuqhawulwa. Kwaye ngoku lixesha lokuhlawulela ngokupheleleyo ilifa elithengiweyo, kwanabo bonke abandenze iNkosi ngentliziyo ezaliswe lukholo besazi ukuba uYahweh wandivusa kwabafileyo ukuze ndingaphindi ndife. Ngoku ngamaxesha okuhlangulwa kwabo ngokupheleleyo apho baya kwamkela ngokupheleleyo isiqinisekiso sabo esifunyenwe kum ekuzalweni ngokutsha kukaKristu kuni ithemba lozuko!

Yabonani! Yivani ilizwi lam niqonde amazwi am kwaye nazi ukuba ikamva lenu elizukileyo alikho kwiminyaka ezayo, kwaye alimiselwanga ukuba lifike kwiiYubhili ezizayo, kodwa liphezu kwenu zithandwa zam kwaye ngesiquphe ngomzuzwana ongunaphakade niya kuguqulwa nonke kwaye amaxesha otshintsho ngala ngoku. Niya kuzifumana ngesiquphe niguqulwe naba sebuqaqawulini obungunaphakade, kwaye ndiya kunambathisa iingubo ezingenabala ezingenakho ukungcoliswa. Ngesiquphe niya kuzifumana nimi kwigumbi letrone likaYahweh, kwanakubukhazikhazi obungunaphakade nozuko lukaBawo weziKhanyiso, kwaye niya kuthabatha inxaxheba ngonaphakade kwindalo yakhe engunaphakade kuba ningoonyana boThixo ophilayo, kwanabo bonke abathathwe ku-Adam bahlangulwa kumhlaba obomvu.

Kwaye yayiligazi lam lentlawulelo elaphalazwa emhlabeni eGolgotha elaqinisekisa usindiso lwenu kanye, ngonaphakade njengoko ndancama ubomi bam ngamazwi athi "Kugqityiwe!" Kwaye mna Kristu Yesu, ndingunaphakade iNkosi yeeNkosi noKumkani wooKumkani ovusiweyo wanyukela ezulwini kuba uYahweh wandivusa kwabafileyo ukuze ndingaphindi ndife! Ndingu-Adam wokugqibela, kwaye ndingunyana womntu ohlangule oonyana babantu ababiza igama lam waza wabenza oonyana bakaThixo abangonaphakade abaya kuhlala ngonaphakade kubuqaqawuli obungunaphakade nobukhazikhazi obungunaphakade kwaye ngonaphakade uYahweh uya kuzukiswa kubo, nabo kuYahweh kuba kum, mna Yeshua HaMashiach, kuhlala konke ukuzala ngonaphakade, kwaye uYahweh uya kuba yinto yonke kuyo yonke into kwaye ngonaphakade siya kuhamba njengesidalwa esinye esitsha nomntu omnye omtsha. Kwaye nina, ababiziweyo bam abanyuliweyo nabathembekileyo, nihlangulwe ngonaphakade, kwaye sele niza kuqonda ubomi benu bokwenene kuba ubomi benu bungunaphakade. Ngoko qondani ukuba nifile, kwaye ubomi benu bufihliwe nam, mna Kristu Yesu, kuYahweh. Xa mna, Kristu Yesu, ongubomi benu, ndibonakala, nani niya kubonakala nam ebuqaqawulini. Anazi na ukuba ubuntu benu bokwenene bufihliwe kum, mna Kristu Yesu?

Ngoko kutheni nidakumbile banyulwa bam, abathembekileyo bam, iBandla lam kwanaye noMtshakazi wam? Ndiyeza kuni kwaye, yabonani, ndiyasondela kuni. Kwaye ndiya kunihlanganisa ngesiquphe kum. Kwaye njengoko umhlaba ujikeleza usinga emgwebeni nasemfazweni, amazulu aya kuvuya bubukho benu kwaye kuya kubakho umbhiyozo omkhulu xa oonyana bakaThixo bemanyene ngonaphakade neNkosi yabo yeeNkosi noKumkani wooKumkani kubuqaqawuli obungunaphakade nakubutsha bobomi obuya kuba yinxalenye yabo bonke. Kwaye akusayi kuphinda kubekho ukwahlukana phakathi kwabo bahlangulweyo ngegazi leMvana noYise wabo noThixo wabo.

Kuba ngoku lixesha uYahweh alikhethileyo lokuqala ukuzityhila ngokupheleleyo kwindalo yakhe kwaye ukuzityhila kwakhe ngokupheleleyo kuqala ngendlu kaThixo, kwaye sele eza kuzityhila ngokupheleleyo kubanyulwa bakhe nabathembekileyo, kwanazo iintlahlela, kuba abo ndibahlanganisela kum ngesandi sexhilongo lokugqibela baziintlahlela zabafileyo. Kwaye ngoku lixesha lokuba bazi ngokupheleleyo, njengoko nonke naziwa ngokupheleleyo ndim noBawo. Kwaye ke ndiyakwazisa ukubonakala kwam kwaye ndiyanicebisa nina bathembekileyo bam ukuba niqhubeke nizilungiselela ukubonakala kwam, kuba ngoku ngamaxesha njengoko umgwebo uqala endlwini kaThixo. Kwaye ngesiquphe, kuya kwenzeka.

Yabonani! Musani ukoyika izaqhwithi ezizayo ezibonwa ngabaninzi benu zihlangana: izaqhwithi zemfazwe, izaqhwithi zentlekele esondelayo, izaqhwithi zeentlekele zendalo eziza kwenzeka ngokuqinisekileyo. Qondani ukuba akukho nto iya kunenzakalisa nangayiphi na indlela, nangona abaninzi behlala ezihendweni nasemfazweni yokomoya eyoyiswa ngokupheleleyo kum, mna Kristu Yesu. Yabonani! Ixesha lokubonakala kwam kwabathembekileyo bam lifikile kwaye njengoko iinimba zingapheli kwaye ngesiquphe baya kuhlanganiswa baxwilelwe kude nomgwebo ongekho sesabo.
