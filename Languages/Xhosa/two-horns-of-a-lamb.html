Kanti ke, yintlekele yamaxesha enu leyo yokuba umntu wemvelo unokhetho lokufumana intlawulelo epheleleyo noxolelo olupheleleyo lwezono aze ale endaweni yoko, ekhetha ububi nomgwebo wakhe ongunaphakade. Kwaye oku kungenxa yokuba uninzi lwabantu bemvelo, kwanabantu abangenawo umoya wam ongcwele wobunyana, bayala ukwamkela ukuba bakwimeko yesono neyonakeleyo, kwaye ke ngoko bemi ngekratshi eliphakamileyo bebanga ukuba imisebenzi yabo engenamsebenzi ilungile endaweni yoko, kwanamabango angayi kuma kwimigwebo ezayo, kwaye angamkelekanga emehlweni am kuba akukho mntu unokuza kuYahweh, Mna Mkhulu Ndinguye, ngaphandle kokuba eze ngam, uYesu waseNazarete!

Umthetho uwa kwiBhabhiloni Eyimfihlo, kanye njengoko kwathenjiswayo, nangenxa yokuba izilumkiso zam ezininzi azihoywanga, nangenxa yokuba aMazwi am athunyelwe ngabathunywa bam nabaprofeti ukuze kusindiswe abo ngoku bakhethe umgwebo nentshabalalo yabo ngokungeva okuvukelayo alahlwa bucala njengenkunkuma engenaxabiso. Kwabhalwa ke ngoko, kwaye kuyenzeka ngoku: Kwaye iimpondo ezilishumi owazibonayo phezu kwerhamncwa, ezi ziya kumthiya umfazi ohenyuzayo, zimenze kube senkangala, aze, zitye inyama yakhe, zimtshise ngomlilo. Kuba uThixo ubeke ezintliziyweni zabo ukuba bazalisekise intando yakhe, bavumelane, banikele ubukumkani babo kwirhamncwa, de azaliseke amazwi kaThixo.

Kwaye ke kuyenzeka ukuba umgwebo uwele kwiBhabhiloni Eyimfihlo, kwaye iziyolo zayo zithathwe kuyo ngomgwebo. Kwaye kungekudala ngoku kuya kuzaliseka ngokupheleleyo njengoko kubhaliwe nguYohane uMtyhileli owabhala ngokuthembekileyo aMazwi anikwe sisandla sam: Kuba ngeyure enye ubutyebi obungaka benziwe isiziba. Ngoko ke, ubutyebi neziyolo ziyasuswa emhlabeni kwaye aziyi kubuya de bufike ubukumkani bam bewaka leminyaka kwaye oku kwenziwa ngomgwebo nesohlwayo kwabo babenkanukela ezi zinto zimnandi ngenjongo yokuba bafikelele enguqukweni ukuba bangaguquka ngokholo olupheleleyo ezintliziyweni zabo kuYesu waseNazarete ngokubiza egameni lam ngelahleko yobomi babo ukuze bafumane usindiso kwihlabathi eligwetyiweyo nelifayo eliya kuthi, inene, inene, lifikelele kwintshabalalo epheleleyo nomgwebo kwaye akukho namnye uya kusinda ngaphandle kobabalo nenceba kaYahweh enikwe ebusweni bukaKristu Yesu uKumkani ovusiweyo nonyukileyo Wobulungisa!

Noko ke, uYahweh uthembekile kuba wazibophelela ngomnqophiso noNowa ukuba akayi kuphinda awutshabalalise umhlaba ngesiquphe ngamanzi, kodwa umhlaba uya kuthathwa ngomlilo kumgwebo wokugqibela omiselwe ukuwa ngokupheleleyo uqale ngendlu kaThixo. Kuba nakula maxesha omgwebo wokugqibela, ubabalo nenceba nosindiso ziya kufunyanwa sisihlwele sezihlwele esiya kubona impazamo yeendlela zaso kwaye esiya kubiza egameni likaYesu ukuze sisindiswe; kwaye kuSirayeli oya kwamkela uYeshua HaMashiach njengoMesiya wakhe wokwenyani kwaye abaninzi baya kunikwa ukungena kubukumkani bam bewaka leminyaka apho kuya kufunyanwa ubulungisa kuphela. Kuba ndinguKumkani Wobulungisa kwaye ngobulungisa ndiza kugweba ndenze imfazwe!

Ngoko ke, qondani ukuba uYahweh ungumlilo odlayo, kwaye mna ndinguKristu Yesu ohleli ephethe isifutho esandleni evuselela amadangatye okuhlanjululwa ukuze ubutyebi bukaYahweh (obungabantu bakhe) buhlanjululwe kuyo yonke inkunkuma ukuze bume phambi kweNkosi noMsindisi wabo ongunaphakade bengambathiswanga ngemisebenzi yabo engenamsebenzi yokungalungisi, kodwa baya kuma phambi kwam bembathiswe ngelinen emhlophe, ecocekileyo, engenabala kuba bamkela ubulungisa obunikwe sisandla sam kwiTestamente Entsha endiya kuyisebenzisa ukulawula umhlaba ngobulungisa, kwanomTestamente wengqibelelo ongayi kuze uphele. Kwaye kwabo bamkela usindiso ngale ndlela kuya kuthiwa ngabo ukuba inene ababuyithandanga ubomi babo kwada kwasekupheleni kwaye endaweni yoko bakhetha ukumka kweli hlabathi libi ngeembeko zokufela ukholo, kwanakwiimbeko zabo endazincamela njengemvana kaThixo ukuze bonke abakhetha uYesu uKristu njengomsindisi wabo baphile ngonaphakade ngengqibelelo phambi koYahweh Mna Mkhulu Ndinguye! Ngoko ke, qondani ukuba umkhonzi akamkhulu kunenkosi yakhe.

Kwaye kunjalo ukuba umgwebo uqala endlwini kaThixo kwaye inkathazo kaYakobi imiselwe ukuba iqhambuke kuwo wonke umhlaba ngokupheleleyo, kwakamsinya nje bakuba behlanganisiwe abathembekileyo kuKristu, aze ke amatywina akhululwe, kwaye amaxilongo aya kukhaliswa kwaye ingqumbo yeMvana iya kuwa ngokupheleleyo emhlabeni kuba iNgonyama yesizwe sakwaYuda, ingcambu kaDavide, yoyisile ukuba iyivule incwadi, nokukhulula amatywina ayo asixhenxe ngokuthobela okulungileyo kwintando nesicwangciso esingunaphakade sosindiso esinikwe nguYahweh kwiMfihlelo kaThixo kwabo baka-Adam abaya kwamkela usindiso olulungileyo nakumgwebo waboni abaya kususwa ebusweni bomhlaba kwimigwebo elungileyo ezayo.

Kuba ngenene ukuba iinkosana zeli hlabathi zaziqondile iMfihlelo Enkulu kaThixo endayityhilela umpostile uPawulos xa ndandimfundisa, kwaye wabhala ngokuchanekileyo ukuze kuthotyelwe ubulungisa kubo bonke abangcwaliswe ngayo: ukuba iinkosana zeli hlabathi zazingasoze zayibethelela emnqamlezweni iNkosi yozuko ukuba zaziyiqondile iMfihlelo Enkulu kaThixo. Kuba ngokuzinikela kwam ngokungenatyala ezandleni zabo ezinentiyo, kwintiyo yabo yoburhamncwa babingelela bengazi imvana kaThixo eligazi elimsulwa! Ngokwenjenjalo bahlawulela uAdam ngokupheleleyo kubo bonke abakhetha ukubiza egameni lam ukuze basindiswe, ngaloo ndlela bebaleka ukonakala okungunaphakade egazini lika-Adam okwabangelwa yinyoka eMyezweni wase-Eden kulo lonke eli xesha lidlulileyo. Kanti kunjalo, ukuba ooSathana kwiinkanuko zabo ezinentiyo nezingazi nto bancedisa ekuziseni usindiso luka-Adam abafuna ukumtshabalalisa ebusweni bomhlaba! Ngaphandle kokuba boyiswa ngokupheleleyo zizenzo zabo ekungazini kwabo ubulumko bukaYahweh obungunaphakade, kwanaye Mna Mkhulu Ndinguye, ababefuna ukumoyisa ngobuqhetseba, kwanento engenakwenzeka engayi kuze yenzeke kuba uYahweh ubabamba abanobuqhetseba kumgwebo ongunaphakade abangayi kuze bawubaleke.

Ngoko ke, qondani ukuba ndingulowo uvuselela amadangatye okuhlanjululwa ukuze bonke abaya kuza kuYahweh ebusweni bukaYesu Kristu bafumane usindiso ngokupheleleyo kwaye beze kum ngokupheleleyo ukuze badalwe ngokutsha ngokupheleleyo ngamaxesha abo afanelekileyo nangolungelelwano. Kwaye kunjalo ukuba ixesha lifikile lokuhlanganisa abathembekileyo kuKristu, kwanentlahlela kwabafileyo, kwaneso sivuno sikhulu serhasi esiza kwenzeka ngesiquphe ngoku kuba amaxesha esivuno afikile kwintsimi yobabalo lukaYahweh apho isivuno esinamandla nesilungileyo siya kuqokelelwa koovimba bakaYahweh kwaye umququ uya kubotshwa utshiswe ngonaphakade emlilweni ongacimekiyo wesohlwayo, itsho iNkosi Yesivuno!

Kunjalo, ngexesha lokubhalwa koku ukuhlanganiswa okukhulu kwabathembekileyo kuKristu akukenzeki kwaye abaninzi babathembekileyo bam basokola ukuba bangalahli ithemba ekubonakaleni okukufuphi kweNkosi yabo ngenxa yokungabi namonde nezilingo ezininzi. Ngoko ke, mna, Kristu Yesu, ndiyaninika ukuba niqonde ukuba ukuhlanganiswa kwabathembekileyo kuKristu sele kuqhubeka kwaye akunakunqandwa okanye kulibaziswe, kwaye ke qondani ukuba oko kuthe kwabhengezwa kwaza kwamiselwa etroneni nasemandleni kaYahweh akunakuguqulwa, okanye kutshintshwe nakancinane kwaye kuya kwenzeka kanye njengoko kwaxelwa kwangaphambili kwaye kanye ngexesha eligqibeleleyo elikhethwe nguYahweh ngokwakhe, kuba nantoni na ayimisela injongo uYahweh iya kwenzeka ngengqibelelo epheleleyo kuba lunjalo uzuko lukaYahweh, Mna Mkhulu Ndinguye!

Phumani phakathi kwabo nahlukane, kwaye ke ndiyanilumkisa kwakhona kuba lixesha lokumka kwiinkathalo zeli hlabathi libi kuba ukumka kwenu kusondele. Ndiyanicebisa ukuba niphile ixesha lenu eliseleyo, elidlulayo ngobulungisa ukuze nibe nilungele ilizwi leNkosi yenu ukuze ndinithume ukuba nikhonze ummelwane wenu enifanele nimthande, kwanangendlela enithanda ngayo nina ukuze nihlwayele imbewu yobulungisa ezintliziyweni zabo kwaye nizise abasalelayo eMkhombeni phambi kokuba olo cango lukhulu luvulekileyo emazulwini luvalwe ngokuqhwakraza ngomgwebo. Ndiyanicebisa ukuba niphile ixesha eliseleyo ngobulungisa ukuze nibe nilungele ilizwi leNkosi yenu kwaye ukuze nive eso sidumo sinamandla xa kukhala ixilongo lokugqibela lokuhlanganisa abathembekileyo kuKristu. Kwaye ukubonakala kwam kuya kubonwa kwaziwe kwihlabathi liphela kuba njengoko umbane uphuma empuma, ubonakale entshona, kuya kuba njalo nokufika koNyana woMntu. Kanti kuya kuba njalo xa ndazityhila kuPawulos endleleni eya eDamasko ukuba abo bamkelweyo emazulwini baya kuliva ixilongo kwaye basiqonde isandi salo kwaye abaninzi baya kubona uzuko lwam luqhambuka esibhakabhakeni. Noko ke, uninzi aluyi kukwazi ukwahlula eso sidumo sinamandla kwaye lungasiqondi isithetho esinikwe ngaso, kwaye ke baya kusala.

Ngoko ndiyaninika ukuba niqonde ukuba ngexesha lokubonakala kwam ukukhanya kozuko lwam kuya kuzalisa amazulu kwaye uzuko lwam luya kubonwa kwaye ihlabathi liya kwazi liqonde okomzuzwana ukuba uKristu Yesu ubonakele kuba abathembekileyo kuKristu baya kuthathwa ngesiquphe bayiswe kukhuseleko lwamazulu apho baya kuma phambi kweNkosi yabo, bade badalwe ngokutsha kubulungisa obungunaphakade ngonaphakade! Kwaye abo bashiyekileyo ababeqinisekile ukuba baya kwamkelwa ekubonakaleni kwam baya kulila, babhomboloze, bawele kuxinzelelo olukhulu nolukrakra. Kwananjalo, njengoko ubunzima bemeko yabo nekamva labo buzinza kubo. Emva koko abaninzi babo baya kuzinikela ngokupheleleyo ekubizeni egameni lam nokuba kwenzeka ntoni na kwaye babize egameni lam kude kube sekupheleni, kwaye baya kuvuswa babe luzukweni olungachazekiyo kuba ubunzima bomvuzo wabo ongunaphakade abufanelekanga ukuthelekiswa neentsizi ezifunyanwa kweli hlabathi libi, itsho iNkosi yenu engunaphakade eyaxhonywa ngokuzithandela esibondeni ukuze nisindiswe.

Kwananjalo, ndiya kuphefumlela isigqibo esingenakuqhawulwa ngaphakathi kubo sokunyamezela iimvavanyo nezilingo eziya kube zifikile ngokupheleleyo emhlabeni, kwanemivavanyo nezilingo ezisathintelweyo nezitywiniweyo ngoku. Noko ke, baya kwenza imisebenzi emininzi enamandla yenkonzo nothando kubantu babo kula maxesha kwaye ndiya kunika imimangaliso emininzi enamandla ukuze basindiswe kwaye abaninzi baya kunyamezela iimvavanyo neembeko zokufela ukholo kula maxesha, ngaloo ndlela befumana ilifa elibaluleke ngonaphakade nomvuzo ongakhange ungene ngokupheleleyo ezintliziyweni zabantu ngeli xesha. Kwaye ke ndixelela bonke abaya kufunda la Mazwi eGospile Engunaphakade ukuba bangajongi ngasekhohlo okanye ngasekunene kodwa bagcine ingqwalasela yabo kuYesu uKristu oya kunomeleza ukuba ninyamezele ukuze abaninzi ngakumbi beze elusindisweni ngomzekelo wenu nobungqina benu.

Nangona kunjalo, kuya kuba njalo ukuba inamba, irhamncwa elivela elwandle, nerhamncwa elivela emhlabeni (elingumprofeti wobuxoki) onke aya kuvuka ngexesha lawo, kwanaxa ekhululwa ngumthinteli ukuba enze oko anikwe kona ukuba akufeze ngexesha elifutshane analo, kwaye akasayi kuphinda avuke ngolo hlobo. Kwaye ngenkohliso enkulu uya kubanga ngokwenyani ukuba uvukile kwabafileyo, kuba irhamncwa elivela emhlabeni ngokuqinisekileyo liya kwenza ngathi linenxeba entloko kwaye intlekele enkulu iya kulandela ngenxa yoko, kanye njengoko kwaxelwa kwangaphambili nguYahweh kwiMvana Eneempondo Ezimbini. Qondani ukuba baya kuzifihla ngexesha lemfazwe yabo yentlekele abayilwayo ngoku eya kukhululwa ngokupheleleyo xa umthinteli esusiwe kwaye imfazwe yabo iya kuzalisekiswa ngayo yonke intshabalalo eya kuziswa yimfazwe yabo. Kuba kwisiphithiphithi esipheleleyo kuya kuvela ucwangco lwabo olutsha kwaye irhamncwa elivela emhlabeni liya kuxoka libange ukuba livukile kwabafileyo ukuze kusindiswe uSirayeli nokucoca ihlabathi ebubini, kwanobubi abaya kubuzisa ngokupheleleyo bona.

Noko ke ndinazisa ukuze niyiqonde inkohliso eziswa zezi zilo zingendawo ukuze ningabanjiswa yiyo. Ngoko ke, qondani ukuba abaweyo bafuya abantu njengomfuyi weenkomo efuya iinkomo zakhe. Bayadibanisa baze benze izinto zabo ezizizothe baze bathi bangothixo ngenxa yokudibanisa imbewu ekungafanelanga idityaniswe, kuba uYahweh wadala ubomi ngokohlobo lwabo kwaye bekusoloko kuyinjongo kaYahweh ukuba enze irhamncwa lomhlaba ngokohlobo lwalo, neenkomo ngokohlobo lwazo ukuze kugcinwe ubunyulu nozuko lwesidalwa ngasinye asidalileyo uYahweh eluthandweni lwakhe olulungileyo lwezidalwa zakhe. Noko ke, abaweyo bayona kuzo zonke iindidi zendalo kaYahweh njengoko befuna ukudibanisa, ukutshatisa, nokwenza iikopi zeentlobo ngeentlobo zezidalwa, ukuze bazithi bangothixo, kanti bonke bangabakhohlisi abazenzisayo.

Ngoko ke, qondani ukuba abaweyo ngabo bafuna ukuzixuba nembewu yabantu kodwa abayi kunamathelana, njengoko intsimbi ingaxutywa nodongwe. Qondani ukuba abaweyo baye bavelisa iitekhnoloji ezininzi zobuxoki namaqhinga okulawula nokukhohlisa abo bahleli emhlabeni, kwanabo bonke abonwabela ukungalungisi. Qondani ukuba baye bavelisa izinto ezininzi ezizizothe ezenzelwe ukutshabalalisa umntu wemvelo ebusweni bomhlaba. Qondani ukuba abagcini baye benza kwakhona izinto zabo ezizizothe, kwaye qondani ukuba abaweyo baye bafunda ukukopa nokwenza iikopi zobomi obuphefumlelweyo emathatheni oluntu kuba uluntu lwadalwa ngokomfanekiso kaYahweh.

Ngoko ke, qondani ukuba nasiphi na isizothe esenziwe ngabaweyo asenziwanga ngokomfanekiso kaYahweh kodwa sisilinganiso sobomi, kwaye asenziwanga ngokupheleleyo kuhlobo luka-Adam kwaye ke ngoko, asinakuhlangulwa okanye lufumane usindiso kwaye lwenzelwe intshabalalo epheleleyo neyonke ka-Adam nazo zonke izizothe ezenziwe ngendlela efanayo zibotshelelwe ukuzalisekisa iinkanuko zooYise abaya kuzenza ngokuqinisekileyo. Loo nto igoso ayinakwenziwa ithe tye kwaye abayi kuba nangcambu nasebe kwaye bonke baya kuya kumgwebo ongunaphakade ngenxa yobubi ababenzileyo.

Qondani ngakumbi, ukuba irhamncwa eliphuma emhlabeni liyeza kulawula linobuhlungu balo esandleni, kwanolo lulumo lwenyoka lwanaphakade olukwaluphawu lwerhamncwa.
