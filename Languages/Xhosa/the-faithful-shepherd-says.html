Utsho uMalusi Othembekileyo

Umalusi othembekileyo uthi kwabathembekileyo: Inene, inene, ndithi kuni, mkani kubo ubugwenxa ningoni. Kuba umvuzo wam ndinawo, wokumnika umntu wonke ngokwemisebenzi yakhe. Ndinibizile eluthandweni lukaBawo ukuze nibe nobomi, bube bobunaphakade. Ndinibizele ukuba nihambe eBukumkanini bam, nindikhonze, nihlale emhlabeni ngobulungisa. Ndininikile ubabalo lwam ukuze nikhulele kum, iNkosi yenu uYesu Krestu. Musani ukutyhafa zizohlwayo zam, kuba bonke endibathandayo, ndiyabakhalimela, ukuze ubabalo lwam olusindisayo luhlumele ezintsunguzini zobuntu benu, ukuze nenze imisebenzi emikhulu, imisebenzi yeentsikelelo, imisebenzi yokuphilisa, nokuba nithwale isiqhamo esininzi kum, bantu bam.

Kuba uBawo unguThixo onothando, kwaye uthando lukaBawo alunakoyiswa. Kum, iNkosi elilungisa, kumi konke ukuzala andinikwe nguBawo. Uzinikele zonke izinto ezandleni zam, yaye njengoko uFaro wamphakamisayo uYosefu waza wanikela ezandleni zakhe lonke elaseYiputa, ngokunjalo noBawo undiphakamisile waza wanikela zonke izinto ezulwini nasemhlabeni ezandleni zam. Undinike igama elingaphezu kwawo onke amagama, ukuze ngegama likaYesu onke amadolo agobe, asemazulwini nasemhlabeni, nazo zonke iilwimi zivume ubuNkosi bam, ezulwini nasemhlabeni.

Ndiniphe ubomi obungunaphakade, yaye ndinenzele indlela yokubaleka entlango nokubaleka loo mini inkulu yengqumbo isondelayo, ngoko ke musani ukutyhafa! Musani ukudinwa kukwenza okulungileyo, kodwa yimani phambi kwam, kwaye ndiya kuqhubeka ndinitsalela kubukho bam. Nihleli ezulwini kwaye niya kuba nenxaxheba kwitrone yam njengoko nam ndinenxaxheba kwitrone kaBawo. Ndineenqwelo ezininzi neentsikelelo ezininzi enizibekelweyo, bantu bam abathembekileyo. Yimani phambi kwam nize nibe ngabafezekileyo kum. Hlukanani nezono zenu nize nivumele amaxesha okuhlaziya aphephethe umququ ebomini benu. Kum kukho ubomi obungunaphakade, yaye kum kukho indalo entsha, umntu omtsha, uKrestu ngokwakhe kuni, ithemba lozuko! Hlukanani nobubi nize nihlukane nesono. Qhubekani nindivakalisa phakathi kwabahedeni, kuba kusekho abantu ekufuneka basindiswe kweli lixa lokugqibela. Qhubekani nivakalisa igama lam, kuba alikho elinye igama phantsi kwezulu abanokusindiswa ngalo abantu. Thuthuzelekani kukuphumla endininike kona, nize nibeke imithwalo yenu ezinyaweni zam; kuba umthwalo wam kulula, nedyokhwe yam ilula.

Kwabathembekileyo (abo boyisileyo isono nobunzima ngegazi lam lentlawulelo) kwabathembekileyo, ndithi: Ndinomvuzo omkhulu eniwubekelweyo endlwini kaBawo. Iliso alikabonanga, nendlebe ayikavanga, yaye akukhange kungene ezintliziyweni zabantu izinto abazilungiselelweyo. Ndiya kunivuza kakhulu ngeenqwelo ezininzi, ngoko ke yimani ngenxa yam nize nithembeke kwilizwi lam nakumazwi am; kuba kuloo mazwi niya kufumana ubomi. Andinguye na uLizwi owenziwa inyama waza wahlala phakathi kwenu?

Ndingumgwebi olilungisa.

Ndingumalusi olungileyo owalusa umhlambi.

Ndiyimpiliso yenu, yaye ndiya kuniphilisa ngokupheleleyo.

Ndazalwa ngumfazi ndenziwa inyama, nangona kungekho sono kum.

Ndabethelelwa emnqamlezweni ngenxa yezono zenu, yaye inene, akukho mntu wabuthathayo ubomi bam, kodwa ndabuncama ngokuzithandela ngenxa yothando endinalo ngoonyana neentombi zikaBawo.

Ndavuswa ekufeni ngomhla wesithathu, kuba kwakungenakwenzeka ukuba ukufa kundibambe. UBawo waziqhawula iimbophelelo zokufa ngamandla ovuko lwam.

Ndiyintlahlela yabafileyo.

Kungekudala niya kundihlangabeza esibhakabhakeni, yaye iintlungu zokufa ziya kubakhulula abo baleleyo, nize nina baphilayo nabaseleyo nithatyathwe kunye, kwaye ngonaphakade niya kuba nam. NdinguNyana kaThixo, yaye esandleni sam kukho umvuzo wam wabathembekileyo bam. Yimani niqine, bantwana bam, ningatyhafi. Yomelelani eNkosini nasemandleni amandla ayo, kuba nguBawo kuphela omkhulu kunam. Ndizivile izicelo zenu, yaye ndinamkele njengabam.

Yibani nomonde niqhubeke nindisebenzela. Kuba kungekudala niya kuba sekhaya nam, kwaye ngonaphakade siya kuba kwishishini likaBawo wethu. Yomelelani nibe nemihlali. Ndiyazi ukuba uninzi lwenu luyayibona imbandezelo nentlekele isondela, yaye uninzi lwenu luyibonile imikhosi yonkohlakalo iwela imida yenu. Musani ukuboyika! Musani ukuboyika, nangona bebeya kunibhubhisa ukuba bebengakwazi, kodwa abekho apha ngenxa yabathembekileyo bam, njengoko ukubaleka okukhulu nendibano enkulu isaza kubasindisa abathembekileyo bam kwilixa lokulingwa nesilingo eliza kufikela ihlabathi lonke. Phumulani eluthandweni lwam, nisazi ukuba andisokuze ndinishiye okanye ndinilahle, kanye njengoko noBawo engazange andishiye okanye andilahle kwilixa lam lokuzincama. Kuba usindiso lwenu lukhuselekile kum; ngoko ke, tyhalelani kum, njengoko ndiya kunomeleza nangakumbi kunokuba ndenzile. Inkulu iMfihlelo yobuthixo!
