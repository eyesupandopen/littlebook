Ufakazi Ombaxa: Israyeli
Okubabayo Okumnandi
September 7, 2024

Njengoba Kulotshiwe, Kanjalo Kuyokwenziwa!

Zibutheni ndawonye nizwe amazwi eNkosi yenu, uYeshua HaMashiach, bantwana bami, ngokuba ngiyiNkosi yamakhosi noMbusi wababusi, futhi ngokulunga ngizoza ukuzokwahlulela nokulwa. Ngakho-ke qondisisani izikhathi eniphila kuzo nezigameko ezizayo, nibeke izindlebe zenu ekuzweni kwezwi lami, nizwe amazwi eNkosi yenu yaphakade nengunaphakade, uKristu Jesu ovusiweyo nokhazinyulisiweyo! Manje ngizokhuluma ngezinto ezizayo phakathi nesonto lamashumi ayisikhombisa likaDaniyeli, ngenze izindlela zenu zicace, futhi ngizonweba umusa wami wosindiso ngezandla zabayizi-144,000 bami. Ngokuba bayeza ukuzosindisa abantu bami ekubhujisweni, futhi beza ukuzobafihla abantu bami olakeni, olakeni lukaNkulunkulu oluqondiswe kwabawiweyo nalabo abazinamathisela kubo nasembusweni wabo ingunaphakade ngokwamukela uphawu lwabo, lokho kulunywa yinyoka eyisilo okungekho khambi lakho.

Nokho kunjalo ukuthi ngiyomhlwithela ezulwini uMlobokazi wami, futhi ngiyobeka uphawu kwabayizi-144,000 bami abavela ezizweni ezilandelayo, abayizi-12,000 esizweni ngasinye kulezi ezilandelayo: kwaJuda, kwaRubeni, kwaGadi, kwa-Asheri, kwaNafetali, kwaManase, kwaSimeyoni, kwaLevi, kwa-Isakare, kwaZabuloni, kwaJosefa, nakwaBenjamini. Ngakho yizwani izwi lami!

Futhi bayoba yibutho elingenakunqandeka, yebo, bayoba yibutho elingenakwephulwa. Ngokuba impilo yabo ngeyaphakade, engenakonakaliswa nayinkohlakalo. Imizimba yabo ayinakubhujiswa, ngokuba akukho kubhujiswa okungabamba abahlengiweyo; ngokuba ukunqoba ukufa nokubhujiswa banakho ingunaphakade kuze kube phakade. Bayodalwa kabusha njengabantwana bokukhanya, bembethe inkazimulo yaphakade, futhi bayoba namakhono ahlukile neziphiwo ezibhalwe khona kanye kubo ezoyibonakalisa impumelelo yabo ehlanganisa futhi ibe yisibonelo samakhono engiyowadala phakathi kwabo. Ngokuba mina, Kristu Jesu, ngingumcwebisi ohlezi nephephetha esandleni, ngivuthisa amalangabi okuhlanzwa, ukuze nginidale nonke kabusha kimi, uKristu Jesu iNkosi yenu enyukile noMsindisi; futhi lowo onqobayo, nguyena oyodla ifa lazo zonke izinto.

Futhi ngakho-ke ngininika ukuqonda izigameko ezizokwenzeka maduzane, ezinye izigameko zenzeka ngokulandelana kanti ezinye zenzeka kanyekanye, kodwa ngizozibala ukuze nihlukanise izikhathi eniphila kuzo, futhi ukuze nina bangcwele bami bosizi ningathathwa njengempango yinkohliso enamandla esisibekele umhlaba futhi abaningi bayokhohliswa bathathwe ekwahlulelweni. Ngokuba abawiweyo bayoziveza futhi bayoqamba amanga kaningi, futhi bayothembisa ukuphila kodwa abanamandla okunikeza ukuphila, futhi abakwazi ukunika ukuphila; kodwa wonke amazwi abawakhulumayo agxile emangeni aholela ekufeni kwesibili, futhi ukubhujiswa kwaphakade kuphela okuyotholwa yilabo abalandela izindlela zabawiweyo nabo bonke abamukela amanga abo anyanyekayo njengeqiniso. Kodwa lokhu kuyukulahlwa okufikile emhlabeni, ukuthi abantu bathanda ubumnyama kunokukhanya nabantu bathanda amanga kuneqiniso, ngoba isintu singalile mina, uYeshua HaMashiach, uJesu Kristu, bakhetha umsindisi wamanga; kepha iqiniso likuKristu yedwa, futhi bonke abanye bangabaqambimanga nabakhohlisi.

Ngakho-ke izinto ezizayo zimi kanje, ezinye izigameko zenzeka kanyekanye kanti ezinye zenzeka ngokulandelana futhi ziyadingeka ukuze ezinye izigameko zenzeke. Futhi izigameko eziningi azibalwanga kodwa ziyofika ngesikhathi sazo. Futhi lokho abanye abakubiza ngokuthi ukuhlwithwa, noma ukubuthelwa ndawonye kwabo bonke abakuKristu eNkosini yabo, kuyisigameko sokuqala esizovusa zonke ezinye izigameko ngesikhathi sosizi lukaJakobe, esibizwa nangokuthi isonto lamashumi ayisikhombisa likaDaniyeli, ngokuba kuyoba yisonto leminyaka eyisikhombisa, lapho usuku ngalunye luwunyaka. Ngakho-ke abathembekileyo eBandleni lami, elinguMzimba wami noMlobokazi wami, bayothathwa lapho mina, Kristu Jesu, ngehla ezulwini ngimemeza, nangezwi lengelosi enkulu nangecilongo likaNkulunkulu. Ngokuba icilongo lokugcina liyokhala, ngaleso sikhathi abafileyo kuKristu bayovuswa babe sebusha bokuphila; khona-ke nina eniphilayo nisele niyohlwithwa emafwini kanye nabo, futhi kuyokwenzeka njengoba kulotshiwe, nangokulandelana okunikiwe kuyokwenzeka ngokuphazima kweso.

Ngakho-ke qondani, bathembekile bami, nina enginithanda ngothando olungunaphakade nolwaphakade, ukuthi niyozithola ngokuzumayo nangokuphazima kweso esihlalweni sobukhosi sikaYahweh uqobo lwakhe, lapho ngiyonethula ningenabala nangenamibimbi nangenazinto ezinjalo kuBaba wezinkanyiso, futhi nina niyizinkanyiso zezwe. Futhi ngokuphazima kweso niyokwazi njengoba naziwa, futhi niyongibona mina, uYeshua HaMashiach, futhi niyobona uYahweh, uNkulunkulu Omkhulu Waphakade, ngamehlo enu okokuqala, futhi niyomangala kakhulu ngalokho enikubonayo nangenkazimulo eniyokwembathiswa yona. Futhi ingunaphakade niyoba senhlanganweni yaphakade noBaba, neNdodana, futhi ingunaphakade siyoba ndawonye.

Ngezikhathi zokubuthwa kwabangcwele, umvimbeli uyosuswa, futhi ukwahlulela kuyokwehlela ngokugcwele phezu kwendlu kaNkulunkulu. Futhi labo abangivumelile ukuba ngibacwebise emlilweni womcwebisi bayokuma ngoba bahlanziwe ngegazi leWundlu futhi bayokuma ekulungeni engibanike kona, ngoba ngiyiNkosi Elungileyo engumoya onika ukuphila. Futhi labo abalenqaba izwi lami bahlala enkanini entandweni yami futhi benqaba ukuphuma esonweni bangene ekulungeni bayosala ukuze bacwengwe ngokwengeziwe ngezikhathi zosizi lukaJakobe, futhi izixuku eziningi ziyothola insindiso yaphakade, umvuzo nobukhazikhazi, futhi ziyophila ingunaphakade nenkazimulo enkulu iyoba ngeyazo ingunaphakade. Kanjalo futhi amabandla omkokotelo asele ayobhujiswa njengoba uDrako, isilo sasolwandle, nesilo somhlaba beyoshesha ukulwa nabangcwele abasele futhi babanqobe. Futhi kuyoba njalo.

Lapho umvimbeli esusiwe, iBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo liyobhujiswa njengoba kulotshiwe. Ngesikhathi esisodwa isiphetho salo siyoba ngesokugcina, nosizi olukhulu nokubhujiswa kuyodla umhlaba, futhi kuyoba nokuzamazama okukhulu, futhi kuyocaca emhlabeni ukuthi usuku lweNkosi selufikile, nakuba abawiweyo beyoqamba amanga bazethule njengezidalwa zasemkhathini ezinomusa, futhi bayozama ukumboza ukubonakala kwami kwabathembekileyo ngempi nokuhlasela kwamanga kwezidalwa zasemkhathini. Ngakho-ke ningawakholwa amanga abo, bantwana bami, ngokuba amanga abo ahlose ukuniyisa ekubhujisweni kwaphakade okubekelwe abawiweyo, ngakho-ke ningazihlanganisi nabo.

Futhi kanjalo izimvu zamanothi ziyothukululwa nabagibeli bamahhashi abane bayokhululwa phezu kwezwe, futhi abantu abaningi kakhulu bayobhubha ngenxa yalokho. Futhi embusweni weBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo ohlezi phezu kwamanzi amaningi, nalaba bayobhujiswa bancishiswe, futhi impango iyosebenzela uhlelo lwesilo olusukayo nolokugcina; futhi lungolokugcina ngoba mina, Kristu Jesu, ngiyowubhubhisa ngokuphelele ngiwuchithe umbuso kaSathane, ungaphinde uvuke. Ngakho-ke umhlaba uyomangala ngesilo, futhi abaningi bayojoyina umbuso wabo wobusathane. Futhi isimo esibi esintwini, esalethwa yisono sika-Adamu lapho u-Eva ethatha enyokeni wadla, siyobe singenazo nhlobo izithiyo, futhi isintu siyoba nobuhlanya obugcwele ezinhliziyweni zaso, futhi abaningi bayozihlanganisa nesilo ingunaphakade ngokwamukela nokuthatha uphawu lwesilo. Ungaluthathi uphawu lwaso, ngoba bonke abakwenzayo bayophonswa bephila echibini lomlilo wesibabuli, elivutha amalangabi aluhlaza ashisa kakhulu.

Emilothweni yempi umbuso wabo wokugcina uyovuka, yebo emilothweni yohlelo lwakudala, ngoba esiphithiphithini abasidalayo kuyovela uhlelo lwabo olusha, uhlelo lukaDrako. Futhi bayofuna ukuhlanganisa umhlaba wonke ngaphansi kwesibhengezo sabo, futhi bayohlanganisa zonke izinkolo zomhlaba futhi bayongiphika ngokuphelele mina, uYeshua HaMashiach, uJesu Kristu, ongenakuvunyelwa phakathi kwabo. Futhi ngeqiniso, ngeqiniso, angikaze ngibe nayingxenye noma sabelo nabo. Ngakho-ke bayohamba ngejubane elikhulu nangonya olukhulu empini yabo emelene nabangcwele, futhi umhlaba uyophendukela ngokuphelele kwabangcwele bosizi, futhi bonke abanqobayo ngokubiza egameni lami kuze kube sekupheleni bayokwaziswa ingunaphakade ngomvuzo waphakade nobukhazikhazi obungunaphakade, futhi mkhulu umvuzo wabo, futhi labo abanqobayo bayodla ifa lazo zonke izinto.

Njengoba umphakathi wabo omusha uvela, kanjalo nezihlahla zami ezimbili zomnqumo ziyovela, nezinti zami ezimbili zezibani ezimi phambi kukaNkulunkulu womhlaba. Bayonikezwa amandla amakhulu, futhi bayoprofetha izinyanga ezingama-42 bembethe indwangu yesaka; futhi uma umuntu efuna ukubalimaza, umlilo uyophuma emlonyeni wabo, uqothule izitha zabo: futhi uma umuntu efuna ukubalimaza, kumele abulawe ngale ndlela. Laba bayoba namandla okuvala izulu, ukuze lina lingani ezinsukwini zokuprofetha kwabo: futhi bayoba namandla phezu kwamanzi ukuwaphendula abe yigazi, nokushaya umhlaba ngazo zonke izinhlupho, kaningi ngangokuthanda kwabo. Futhi inzondo yomhlaba ngabo iyoba nkulu. Futhi kuyoma isilo nomprofethi wamanga bephikisana nezihlahla zami ezimbili zomnqumo, futhi omunye uyokhomba komunye, futhi bayozidumisa, futhi bayokhuluma izinto eziningi ezihlambalazayo, futhi bayokwenza izibonakaliso eziningi zamanga nezimangaliso emehlweni abantu.

Lezi zilo ziyokwenza izimangaliso ezinkulu ukukhohlisa abakhileyo emhlabeni, ngokuba kuyovunyelwa zona ukuba zenze izibonakaliso eziningi zamanga nezimangaliso, futhi ngamandla afanayo ziyobusa. Isilo somhlaba siyokwenza ukuba umlilo wehle emhlabeni emehlweni abantu, futhi siyohola bonke abantu ukuba bamangale ngazo ngezibonakaliso eziningi zamanga nezimangaliso. Futhi siyokwenza umfanekiso wesilo futhi senze bonke bakhonze umfanekiso esiyowenza. Futhi kuyovunyelwa sona ukuba sinike umoya emfanekisweni wesilo ukuze umfanekiso wesilo ukhulume futhi wenze ukuba bonke abangakhonzi umfanekiso wesilo babulawe. Futhi siyokwenza bonke, abacebile nabampofu, abakhululekile nezigqila, ukuba bamukele uphawu lwaso esandleni sabo sokunene noma emabunzini abo.

UMA UMUNTU EKHONZA ISILO NOMFANEKISO WASO, FUTHI AMUKELA UPHAWU LWASO EBUNZINI LAKHE, NOMA ESANDLENI SAKHE, YENA LOWO UYOPHINDA APHUZE EWAYININI LOLAKA LUKANKULUNKULU, ELITHULULWE LINGAXUTSHIWE ENDEBENI YOLAKA LWAKHE, FUTHI UYOHLUSHWA NGOMLILO NESIBABULI, PHAMBI KWEZINGELOSI EZINGCWELE, NAPHAMBI KWEWUNDLU: FUTHI INTUTHU YOKUHLUSHWA KWABO IYENYUKA KUZE KUBE PHAKADE NAPHAkade. FUTHI ABANAKUPHUMLA EMINI NASEBUSUKU, ABONA ABAKHONZA ISILO NOMFANEKISO WASO, NANOMA NGUBANI OWAMUKELA UPHAWU LWEGAMA LASO.

Futhi phakathi nalesi sikhathi ithempeli labo liyakwakhiwa, yize umhlatshelo wezilwane ungasadingeki ngoba ngiyiWundlu likaNkulunkulu futhi ngazinikela ngenxa yezono zesintu njengompristi omkhulu kuze kube phakade ngokohlelo lukaMelkisedeki. Ukubuyisana sekwenziwe ngezono zesintu kanye, kwaphela, naphakade ngegazi lami elihlawulayo nelingenasono. Kanjalo-ke, akusekho mhlatshelo osele ngenxa yesono.

Ngakho-ke lapho izihlahla zami ezimbili zeminqumo seziqedile ubufakazi bazo ekupheleni kwezinyanga ezingama-42, isilo esikhuphuka kwalasha siyokulwa nazo, sibulale izinti zami ezimbili zezibani, ngokuba kunikiwe kuso ukufeza lokho. Izidumbu zazo ziyakuba zifile izinsuku ezintathu nengxenye emigwaqweni, umhlaba wonke uthokoza, uphana ngezipho ngokufa kwazo. Khona-ke kuyofika izwi elikhulu livela emazulwini lithi, Kwenyukelani lapha! Ziyovuselwa ekuphileni okusha, zamukelwe emazulwini phambi kwezitha zazo.

Bayophelelisa ithempeli labo bese ugibe luyabekwa, ngoba yimuphi umthiyi obeka ugibe inyoni isibona? Ngokuba isilo esivela emhlabeni nguye obeka ugibe ezweni, uyokhohlisa abaningi ngobunono bakhe bokukhohlisa, futhi uyokhashela abantu bezwe. Lowo muntu wesono uyokwambulwa, yona indodana yokulahlwa, engumphikisi, futhi iyoziphakamisa ngaphezu kwakho konke okuthiwa uNkulunkulu, noma okukhonzwayo: ize ihlale njengoNkulunkulu eThempelini likaNkulunkulu, iziveza ukuthi inguNkulunkulu. Ithempeli labo liyopheleliswa, nesinengiso sencithakalo siyofezwa nguMoshiach wabo wamanga. Futhi uyokuqinisa isivumelwano nabaningi isonto elilodwa: futhi phakathi nesonto uyokwenza ukuba umhlatshelo nomnikelo kunqamuke, nangenxa yokwanda kwezinengiso; uyokwenza kube yincithakalo, kuze kube yilapho ukubhujiswa okunqunyiweyo sekuthululelwe phezu kwencithakalo.

Kanjalo-ke kuyokwenzeka usizi olukhulu kakhulu, usizi olungakaze lubonwe emhlabeni ngaphambili, futhi impela, olungeke luphinde lube khona. Bhekani! Mina, uYeshuah HaMashiach, ngeke ngishiye abantu bami ekubhujisweni okungazelelwe, futhi ngisazokwelekelela u-Israyeli, yona leyo nsali enamandla eyongamukela njengoMesiya weqiniso, ngokuba uYahweh ungidalele lokho. Futhi kanjalo abayizi-144,000 bami bayofika ukuzosiza labo abebengabhubha, abakhuleka egameni lami. Ngiyobahola isinyathelo ngesinyathelo, futhi abayizi-144,000 bami bayokwenza imisebenzi eminingi yamandla ngenxa yami.

Ngokuba yilabo abathathwa ekubuthweni kwabangcwele kimi, Kristu Jesu, emoyeni lapho kukhala icilongo lokugcina, futhi icilongo selizokhala. Laba bayabantu bami abathembekile, labo okungafunyaniswanga kubo ubuqili, ngokuba bazihlambululile njengentombi emsulwa egazini leWundlu, engalinikela phakade njengompristi wabo omkhulu, ngokuba mina ngingumpristi omkhulu kuze kube phakade ngokohlelo lukaMelkisedeki. Laba banguMakoti wami, futhi bayongilandela nomaphi lapho ngiya khona. Bhekani! Ngiyobenzela amandla, futhi akukaze kubonwe lutho emhlabeni olungaqhathaniswa namakhono engiyobembathisa wona. Bayokwembathiswa ilineni elihle, elimhlophe, nelingenabala, futhi amakhono abo ayomela izimpumelelo zabo, futhi bayoba namakhono amakhulu; ngokuba bathembekile, babiziwe, futhi bakhethiwe, kusukela ngaphambi kokusekelwa komhlaba nguYahweh ngalezi zinhloso.

Abayizi-144,000 bami bayokwehlela eNtabeni iSiyoni, futhi ngiyobahola. Bayothatha labo engibaholela kubo, baye ehlane ukuze bafihlwe kude nobumnyama obukhulu nokushushiswa kombuso wezitha. Bayoholwa bayiswe ekuphepheni ezinhluphweni eziyisikhombisa, ngokuba kulezi zahlulelo kukhona ubunzima bolaka lukaNkulunkulu oluzothululelwa esihlalweni sobukhosi sesilo nokuhlupha umbuso kaDrako, ngokuba sikhulu isijeziso sabo. Futhi abayizi-144,000 bayobaholela ekuphepheni; bayobaphilisa ezilondeni zabo; bayobasiza futhi babanike ithemba elikhulu lokukhuleka egameni leNkosi, kuze kube sekupheleni uma kudingeka. Ngokuba ukube angizifushanisanga izinsuku, bekungeke kusinde muntu. Kanjalo-ke bayofeza lokho engiyobaholela kukho.

Futhi njengoba laba abathathu abangcwele behamba beyobhubhisa labo abangamukelayo njengoMesiya wabo, kanjalo nolaka oluphelele lukaNkulunkulu luyofezeka emakhanda abo nasembusweni wabo ngokuphelele; impela, impela, izitsha eziyisikhombisa ezigcwele ulaka lukaNkulunkulu; futhi ukwahlulela okukhulu nosizi kuqukethwe ezitsheni zabo, futhi kuyokwenzeka njengoba kulotshiwe:

Ingelosi yokuqala iyothulula isitsha sayo emhlabeni, futhi labo abamukela uphawu lwabo olungcwele bayoshaywa ngezilonda ezibuhlungu nezimbi ngesitsha esiyothululwa yingelosi yokuqala.

Ingelosi yesibili iyothulula isitsha sayo, futhi izilwandle ziyoba njengegazi lomuntu ofile, nazo zonke izidalwa zasolwandle eziphilayo ziyobhubha.

Ingelosi yesithathu iyothulula isitsha sayo, yenze imifula nemifudlana iphenduke igazi, ngokuba bachithile igazi labangcwele nabaprofethi, ngakho-ke kufanelekile ukuba banikwe igazi baliphuze.

Impela impela, ingelosi yesine iyothulula isitsha sayo elangeni, futhi liyonikezwa amandla okuhlupha abantu ngokushisa komlilo, futhi abantu bayosha ngokushisa bakhulume izinto ezinkulu nezihlambalazayo ngoNkulunkulu wasezulwini.

Ingelosi yesihlanu iyothulula isitsha sayo esihlalweni sobukhosi sesilo, futhi umbuso waso uyoba mnyama kakhulu, ngamandla amakhulu, futhi bayoluma izilimi zabo ngenxa yosizi.

Ingelosi yesithupha iyothulula isitsha sayo phezu komfula omkhulu i-Ewufrathe, futhi amanzi awo ayokoma, ukuze kulungiswe indlela yaMakhosi aseMpumalanga.

Ingelosi yesikhombisa iyothulula isitsha sayo emoyeni: futhi kuyophuma izwi elikhulu eThempelini lasezulwini esihlalweni sobukhosi, lithi, Sekwenzekile. Futhi kuyoba khona amazwi, nokuduma kwezulu, nemibani, nokuzamazama komhlaba okukhulu, okunjengokungakaze kube khona selokhu kwaba nabantu emhlabeni, ukuzamazama komhlaba okunamandla kangako. Umuzi omkhulu uyokwehlukaniswa ube yizingxenye ezintathu, nemizi yezizwe iyowa. Kanjalo-ke lezi zinto ziyokwenzeka.

Ngakho-ke qondani, bantabami, ukuthi uYahweh uyobafihla abakhe ngisho naphakathi kosizi lukaJakobe, ngisho naphakathi kosizi olukhulu insali iyosindiswa iphile futhi iyohlala iphephile ehlane, yona leyo ndawo elungiswe nguNkulunkulu uqobo, ukuba babondle khona izinsuku eziyinkulungwane namakhulu amabili namashumi ayisithupha. Futhi bekungeke kusale muntu ephila ukube izinsuku azifushanisiwe. Futhi abawiweyo bayozibuthela ndawonye ukuyolwa neNkosi yaMakhosi noMbusi wabaBusi, futhi kanjalo kulotshiwe futhi kanjalo kuyokwenziwa.

Ngabona izulu livulekile, bheka, ihhashi elimhlophe, futhi lowo owayehlezi phezu kwalo wayebizwa ngokuthi oThembekileyo noQinisileyo, futhi ngokulunga uyahlulela futhi ulwa. Amehlo akhe ayenjengelangabi lomlilo, nasekhanda lakhe kwakukhona imiqhele eminingi, futhi enegama elilotshiweyo, elingaziwa muntu ngaphandle kwakhe. Wayembethe ingubo ecwiliswe egazini, futhi igama lakhe kuthiwa, uLizwi likaNkulunkulu. Amabutho ayesemazulwini amlandela ngamahhashi amhlophe, embethe ilineni elicolekileyo, elimhlophe nelihlanzekileyo. Emlonyeni wakhe kuphuma inkemba ebukhali, ukuze ngayo ashaye izizwe: futhi uyozibusa ngentonga yensimbi: futhi unyathela isikhamo sewayini sentukuthelo enkulu nolaka lukaNkulunkulu uMninimandla onke. Futhi unegama elilotshiwe engutsheni yakhe, nasethangeni lakhe, elithi, INKOSI YAMAKHOSI, NOMBUSI WABABUSI.

Kanjalo-ke ingelosi yami iyokwehla ezulwini, inesihluthulelo sakwalasha neketanga elikhulu lapho iyobopha khona uSathane kwalasha iminyaka eyinkulungwane futhi ibeke uphawu ukuze angabe esakhohlisa izizwe iminyaka eyinkulungwane. Ekupheleni kokubusa kwami iminyaka eyinkulungwane, uSathane uyokhululwa kwalasha isikhashana.

Bhekani! Njengoba uJohane uMambuli waloba, kanjalo kuyokuba: Ngabona izihlalo zobukhosi, bahlala phezu kwazo, banikwa ukwahlulela: ngabona imiphefumulo yalabo abanqunywa amakhanda ngenxa yobufakazi bukaJesu, nangenxa yezwi likaNkulunkulu, nababengakhonzanga isilo, nomfanekiso waso, futhi bengamukelanga uphawu lwaso emabunzini abo, noma ezandleni zabo; baphila babusa noKristu iminyaka eyinkulungwane. Kodwa abanye abafiweyo abaphindanga baphila kuze kuphele iminyaka eyinkulungwane. Lokhu kungukuvuka kokuqala. Ubusisiwe futhi ungcwele lowo onesabelo ekuvukeni kokuqala: phezu kwabo ukufa kwesibili akunalo amandla, kodwa bayoba ngabapristi bakaNkulunkulu, nabakaKristu, futhi bayobusa naye iminyaka eyinkulungwane.

Ekupheleni kokubusa kwami iminyaka eyinkulungwane, uSathane uyokhululwa etilongweni lakhe isikhashana ukuze abaqoqe babe njengesihlabathi solwandle. Futhi uyophuma ayokhohlisa izizwe ezisemagumbini amane omhlaba, uGogi noMagogi, ukuze abaqoqele empini: inani labo linjengesihlabathi solwandle. Futhi bayokhuphukela ebubanzini bomhlaba, bazungeze inkambu yabangcwele, nomuzi othandekayo: futhi umlilo uyokwehla uvela kuNkulunkulu ezulwini, ubadle. Udeveli owabakhohlisa uyophonswa echibini lomlilo nesibabule, lapho kuyoba khona isilo nomprofethi wamanga, futhi bayohlushwa imini nobusuku kuze kube phakade naphakade.

Kanjalo-ke kuyofika ukuvuka kwabangakulungile, ngokuba kulotshiwe nguJohane uMambuli: Ngabona isihlalo sobukhosi esikhulu esimhlophe, nalowo ohlezi kuso, obusweni bakhe umhlaba nezulu kwabaleka; akwabe kusatholakala ndawo yabo. Ngabona abafileyo, abancane nabakhulu, bemi phambi kukaNkulunkulu; izincwadi zavulwa: kwavulwa enye incwadi, eyiNcwadi Yokuphila: futhi abafileyo bahlulelwa ngezinlto ezazilotshiwe ezincwadini, ngokwemisebenzi yabo. Ulwandle lwakhipha abafileyo ababekulo; ukufa neHayidese bakhipha abafileyo ababekubo: bahlulelwa yilowo nalowo ngokwemisebenzi yakhe. Ukufa neHayidese baphonswa echibini lomlilo. Lokhu kungukufa kwesibili. Futhi noma ubani ongatholakalanga elotshiwe eNcwadini Yokuphila waphonswa echibini lomlilo.

Kanjalo kulotshiwe futhi kanjalo kuyokwenziwa, ukuthi kukhona ukubuthana lapho abafileyo kuKristu bayovuswa nalabo abaphilayo nabasalayo bayothathwa kanye nabo emafwini, futhi lokhu kuyokwenzeka ngaphambi nje kokuqala kosizi lukaJakobe. Khona-ke ekupheleni kosizi lukaJakobe kuyokwenzeka ukuvuka kwabalungile, okwakhiwa ngabangcwele bosizi nalabo beTestamente Elidala abafa ngokukholwa, bephile ngokwezithembiso. Futhi njengoba kulotshiwe kuDaniyeli, kuyoba njalo: Kodwa hamba indlela yakho kuze kube sekupheleni: ngokuba uyophumula, ume esabelweni sakho ekupheleni kwezinsuku. Ngakho-ke ukuphela kwezinsuku kungukuphela kwesonto lamashumi ayisikhombisa likaDaniyeli nasekuqaleni kokubusa kwami iminyaka eyinkulungwane.

Ekupheleni kokubusa kwami iminyaka eyinkulungwane futhi ngemva kokuba uSathane eseyophonswa efeni lakhe lokugcina kulelo chibi lomlilo ovutha njalo, kanjalo-ke kuyokwenzeka ukuvuka kwabangakulungile, lowo mahlulelo omkhulu esihlalweni sobukhosi esimhlophe uyokwenzeka, futhi noma ngubani ogama lakhe lingatholakali lilotshiwe eNcwadini Yokuphila uyophonswa echibini lomlilo.

Kanjalo-ke mina, uJesu waseNazaretha, iWundlu likaNkulunkulu, uKristu Jesu, oyiNgonyama yesizwe sakwaJuda, ngiyofika ukuzokwahlulela nokulwa noDrako, futhi wonke umbuso wakhe uyochotshozwa ngokuphelele, ungaphinde uvuke. Isilo nomprofethi wamanga bayophonswa bephila echibini lomlilo, futhi uSathane uyoboshwa ngeketanga elikhulu kwalasha iminyaka eyinkulungwane, futhi kanjalo ukuphatha kwami iminyaka eyinkulungwane kuyoqala, futhi ukubusa kwami kuyoba sekulungeni. Bhekani! Ngiletha uMakoti wami nebutho lamakhosi nabapristi engiyobusa nabo umhlaba ngokulunga, futhi ngiyobusa izizwe ngentonga yensimbi. Ngakho yibani nethemba elikhulu, bantabami, ngokuba ukuphela kwazo zonke izinto sekusondele, futhi lesi sikhathi seminyaka eyisikhombisa yosizi olukhulu, usizi lukaJakobe, siyolandelwa ukubusa kwami okulungile, futhi uYahweh uyoba yikho konke kukho konke.

Kanjalo-ke ngininikile ithemba elikhulu, bantwana bami abancane, ukuze nigxilise izingqondo zenu enjabulweni ebekwe phambi kwenu ngoba izinhlupheko zosizi lukaJakobe azifaniswa futhi azifanele ukukhulunywa ngazo maqondana nenjabulo yaphakade ebekwe phambi kwenu. Bhekani! Kulotshiwe nguJohane uMambuli: Laba yibo abaphuma osizini olukhulu, bahlanza izingubo zabo, bazenza zaba mhlophe egazini leWundlu. Ngakho-ke, baphambi kwesihlalo sobukhosi sikaNkulunkulu, bamkhonze imini nobusuku eThempelini lakhe: futhi lowo ohlezi esihlalweni sobukhosi uyohlala phakathi kwabo. Abasayikulamba futhi, abasayikoma futhi, nelanga aliyikubashisa, nakho ukushisa. Ngokuba iWundlu, eliphakathi kwesihlalo sobukhosi, liyobondla, futhi liyobaholela emithonjeni yamanzi aphilayo: futhi uNkulunkulu uyokwesula zonke izinyembezi emehlweni abo. Bayoba ngabapristi bakaNkulunkulu, nabakaKristu, futhi bayobusa naye iminyaka eyinkulungwane.

Kanjalo-ke liyithemba lenu, zithandwa zami, ukuba nikhuthazele kuze kube sekupheleni, futhi okwenu kuyithemba laphakade, ngoba u-El Shaddai, uNkulunkulu uMninimandla onke, uyodala izulu elisha nomhlaba omusha okuhlala kuwo ukulunga, futhi niyonikezwa ilungelo lokungena kulowo muzi ongcwele olungiswe futhi wahlotshiswa nguYahweh uqobo njengomakoti olungiselelwe umkhwenyana, futhi niyodla esihlahleni sokuphila. Futhi njengoba kulotshiwe: Lowo onqobayo, uyodla ifa lazo zonke izinto, futhi ngiyoba nguNkulunkulu wakhe, yena abe yindodana yami.

Bhekani! NginguYeshua HaMashiach, futhi ngithumele amazwi ami ngenceku yami, futhi ngeNceku yami amazwi ami athunyelwe.

Bhekani! Ivangeli laphakade selambuliwe futhi lathunyelwa ngesikhathi salo esifanele, futhi lawa mazwi ayolondolozwa, futhi ayofinyelela emehlweni, ezindlebeni, nasezinhliziyweni zabo bonke labo athunyelwe kubo.

Bhekani! Gcinani ukulinda kwenu ningasuki ezikhundleni zenu, bantu bami, ngokuba izikhathi sezifikile.

Bhekani! Izikhathi yimanje!
