Izahlulelo Ezigcwele NgeBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo: Zimenyezelwe NgeSonto Juni 22, 2025. Zonke izingcaphuno zithathwe Encwadini Encane yeSambulo Isahluko 10 esesambuliwe kusukela ngoMashi 2, 2024, kuya kuMashi 20, 2025. Wonke Amazwi engiwaphiwe nguYeshuwa HaMashiya noYHVH, uMINA OMKHULU ngoMoya Wesiprofetho onguwukufakaza kukaJesu Kristu. Selah!

Izahlulelo ezilandelayo zivela Encwadini Encane yeSambulo Isahluko 10, ephinde ibe yiVangeli Laphakade leSambulo Isahluko 14. Ukwahlulela iBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo sekumenyezelwe amajubili amaningi neminyaka Encwadini yeSambulo. Nokho, Ukuduma Okuyisikhombisa Nencwadi Encane manje sekwambuliwe futhi kwamenyezelwa, futhi le ncwadi enamandla ivuliwe esandleni sami nasezandleni zabo bonke abafunda lesi simemezelo esivela Emhlambini Omncane, obizwe nguYeshuwa HaMashiya ngenjongo nesikhathi esinjengalesi! Selah!

Okulandelayo yizahlulelo ezimemezelwayo ezithathwe Encwadini Encane ezizokwenzeka ngokuqinisekile ngokuzumayo nangokungalindelekile manje. Kulesi sikhundla, ngisebenza njengesithunywa sokwahlulela esithunyelwe ukumemezela izahlulelo ezivela esihlalweni sobukhosi sikaYHVH, ezinikezwe ebusweni bukaJesu Kristu, engimkhonzayo. Sicela niqaphele, ezinye zalezi zahlulelo seziqhubeka kanti ezinye zizokwenzeka maduze, kodwa zonke zizogcwaliseka njengoba kumenyezelwe futhi isahlulelo seBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo sizosebenza njengesexwayiso kuzo zonke izizwe, intuthu yokuhlushwa kwalo izokhuphuka njengesimemezelo esilungileyo sezahlulelo ezilungileyo zikaKristu Jesu, kanye noYHVH uMINA OMKHULU!

Makwaziwe, ukuthi namuhla, mhlaka-22 Juni 2025 ngekhalenda likaGregory (ikhalenda elifanayo elisetshenziswa yiBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo), ukuthi iNkosi yami Eyenyuka uKristu Jesu, uYeshuwa HaMashiya, iyangiyala ukuba ngifunde lezi zahlulelo ngeBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo, yiwo la maMelika AseMelika, ukuze noma ngubani aphenduke kusenomzuzwana wesikhathi esidlulayo, nokuba isahlulelo simenyezelwe ngaphambi kokuba umlilo wehle. Selah!

Lesi simemezelo siphelele futhi lezi akuzona izahlulelo ezintsha kodwa zakhululwa ngokugcwele kusukela ngoMashi 2, 2024, kuya kuMashi 20, 2025. Ngakho-ke, ngidlulisela noma ngubani Encwadini Encane yeSambulo isahluko 10 ezixhumanisweni ezinikeziwe. Sicela uqaphele: Lezi zahlulelo ziqhamuka ngokuqondile Ekudumeni kwesi-5 futhi lokhu kufundwa kuzoqala ngokuthi Abangalungile Ngeke Balidle Ifa Lombuso KaNkulunkulu, Ukuduma kwesi-5 nokwesi-6, okunikezwe nguYeshuwa HaMashiya kanye noYHVH uMINA OMKHULU!

Sicela uqaphele, lokhu kufundwa kusebenza njengesimemezelo esigcwele sokwahlulela iBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo okunikezwa NguMhlambi Omncane ngaphambi kokuba Ukwahlulela kwehle ngokugcwele. Ngiyisithunywa seNkosi nenceku yayo kulokhu futhi ngingumenzeli wezobulungiswa othunyiwe futhi wagunyazwa nguYHVH, ebusweni bukaYeshuwa HaMashiya futhi yibona engibakhonzayo ngokugcwele ehhovisi lami.

Sicela uqaphele, lokhu kufundwa kuphinde kube yinkomba yemicimbi ezokwenzeka maduze, futhi lokhu kwenziwa ngenhloso yokuthi abantu beNkosi, abakholekileyo kuKristu, noMlobokazi kaKristu ozohlwitshwa ngokuzumayo manje. Kunikeziwe ukuze sazi futhi sikwazi ukumemezela ukuphela kusukela ekuqaleni, ngisho kungakenzeki. Lezi zigameko zizokwenzeka ncamashi njengoba kunikeziwe nguKristu Jesu nangesandla sikaYHVH, uMINA OMKHULU, okuvela kuye zonke izinto ngegama elinamandla negunya likaKristu Jesu, onguYeshuwa HaMashiya.

Lokhu kuthathe isikhathi nomzamo ukukuhlanganisa, futhi kusebenza njengokufundwa kwesahlulelo okufingqiwe ngeBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo, kanye nomhlahlandlela wezehlakalo ukuze uMlobokazi weqiniso kaKristu, Abakholwayo kuKristu, nabayi-144,000 bangadukiswa yizilingiso ezishibhile. YiNcwadi Encane ezobusa izikhathi zokugcina ngoKuduma Okuyisikhombisa osekukhululiwe manje, hhayi abaprofethi bamanga abazibeka bona bekhuluma umbhedo ovela ezinhliziyweni zabo. Selah!

1Abangalungile Ngeke Balidle Ifa Lombuso KaNkulunkulu!Ephreli 19, 2024
Kusuka kuYeshuwa HaMashiya:

Ndodana yami, uma uyakwamukela amazwi ami, ufihle imiyalo yami kuwe; ukuze ubeke indlebe yakho ekuhlakanipheni, unikele inhliziyo yakho ekuqondeni. Ningazenzi lukhuni izinhliziyo zenu, njengasekucunuleni, ngosuku lokulingwa ehlane: futhi nazi ukuthi mina, iNkosi uJesu Kristu, nginani njalo futhi ngikhona njalo ezimpilweni zenu njengoba nibambelela kimi nasensindisweni yami, ngoba ngiyinhloko yoMzimba, futhi ngiyiNkosi yenu ethembekile, Mlobokazi wami othandekayo. Ngoba nginilungisele indawo nekusasa, futhi kunomvuzo omkhulu kwabami ababiziweyo, nabakholekileyo, nabakhethiweyo.

Anginibizelanga ekulahlekelweni nasekushayweni, futhi anginibizelanga ehlazweni nasekuzigwebeni; anifundanga yini ukuthi akukho kulahlwa kulabo abakuKristu Jesu, abangahambi ngokwenyama kodwa ngokomoya? Ngakho kholwani amazwi ami! Anginibizelanga ukuba niphile ethunzini lezono zenu ezindala, noma niphile ethunzini lezenzo zenu zobusha, kodwa nginibizele ukuba nife ezindleleni zenu ezindala eziyisono futhi nithathe ubusha bempilo kimi njengoba nilahla inyama yenu, njengoba nilahla izono zenu nihambe ebuntshani bempilo kimi. Bhekani! Ngenza konke kube kusha! Akulona yini ithemba lenu leli?

Bantwana bami, nginikhiphile ekonakaleni kwaleli zwe elifayo lamanje, futhi nginikhululile emandleni obumnyama nganidlulisela embusweni wami, umbuso kaNkulunkulu, oholwa yimi, iNkosi yamakhosi noMbusi wababusi, okunguYahweh onguNkulunkulu, ngoba akekho omunye. Nginibizele enkazimulweni nasebuhleni kimi, futhi nginihlengile embusweni kaSathane, umbuso wobumnyama, nganingenisa eMbusweni wami, lapho kukhona ukukhanya! Ngakho-ke, vuka wena oleleyo, uvuke kwabafileyo, futhi mina, uJesu Kristu, ngizokunika ukukhanya.

Anifundanga yini ezincwadini engazithumela kuMlobokazi wami, engazithumela kini, lona kanye iBandla lami? Anazi yini ukuthi abangalungile abayikulidla ifa lombuso kaNkulunkulu? Ningakhohliswa: nazifebe, nazikhonzi zezithombe, naziphingi, nazihlobongi, nazesilisa ezilala namadoda, namasela, nabahahayo, nazidakwa, nazithuki, nabaphangi, abayikulidla ifa lombuso kaNkulunkulu. Futhi babenjalo abanye benu: kodwa senihlanziwe, kodwa seningcwelisiwe, kodwa senenziwe abalungileyo ngegama leNkosi uJesu, nangoMoya kaNkulunkulu wethu.

Aniziqondi yini izimiso zami, o, bandla elisivivi laseLawodikeya? Anginibizelanga ukuba nibuyele enyameni yenu, futhi anginibizelanga ukuba niqhubeke ngezindlela zenu ezingalungile. Cabangani, niqondisise; lowo ocabanga ukuthi umi, makaqaphele angawi! Ziqapheleni nina, nemfundiso: qhubekani kukho: ngokuba ngokwenza lokhu, uyakuzisindisa wena, nalabo abakuzwayo. Qondani, bantwanyana bami abasivivi, ukuthi anikwazi ukuphila impilo yokuyekelela phakathi kokuhle nokubi, nicabanga ukuthi nilungile phambi kwami, lapho nikhetha okubi okuncane kunokubili emigidini yenu yokukhetha engingayivumeli noma engiyisekelayo.

Phumani kuyo, bantu bami, ngoba angihlinzekanga ngesixazululo sezombusazwe, futhi angininikanga neze umholi wezwe ozokwenza noma yini ibe nkulu futhi. Nikhohlisiwe, futhi nilungiselelwa ukulandela nokuvumela indodana yokulahlwa ehamba obala phakathi kwenu, nakuba ingakambulelwa okwamanje. Kodwa nina enicela ukuqonda niyokwazi ukuthi ingubani, ukuze nigweme inkohliso yayo. Cha, ukuyekelela akusona esenu, ngoba ningabantwana bami nabafowethu, futhi angiyizuzanga insindiso yenu ngokunikela ngokuphila kwami njengenhlawulo yabaningi ngokuyekelela noSathane; ngokuba ukuyekelela kuwukuhlanganyela nezinhlu zabo. Ngakho ningalenzi iphutha: Noma ningimela ekulungeni nasebungcweleni beqiniso, noma senihlangene nezinhlu zikaSathane embusweni wakhe ofayo, ngakho Phendukani!

Nizuzani ngokuyekelela nobubi? Aniqondi yini ukuthi kufanele ninqobe okubi ngokuhle? Ngakho-ke yimani niqinile kimi niyeke ukuphenduphenduka njengenhlanzi ephume emanzini. Abaningi benu basapheshulwa yiyo yonke imimoya yezimfundiso nobuqili bezandla, lapho beqamele khona ukukhohlisa. Mina, uKristu Jesu, ngikhulumile, futhi ngeke ngithuliswe. Abaningi benu baziyekelele kanye nebandla lami, futhi ngiyanibuza: koze kube nini ninqikaza phakathi kwemibono emibili? Uma iNkosi ingoNkulunkulu, mlandeleni: kodwa uma kunguBali, mlandeleni yena. Kalula nje, anikwazi ukuyekelela nikhethe noma yiluphi uhlobo lobubi nicabange ukuthi ningimela; lapho nikhetha okubi, nikhetha imithombo yalobo bubi njengenkosi yenu nomphathi wenu nibe yisigqila sesono. Anazi yini ukuthi lowo enizinikela kuye njengezinceku ukuba nimlalele, niyizinceku zakhe enimlalelayo: noma ezesono kuze kube sekufeni, noma ezokulalela kuze kube sekulungeni?

Ngizweni futhi ngizweni kahle, Bandla lami; yazini ukuthi ngimile ngokunengeka emikhubeni egcwele esizweni senu nasemhlabeni wonke, eniyekelele futhi nayivumela ukuba ingene eBandleni lami ukuze ihluphe abantwanyana bami. Njengasezinsukwini zikaNowa, kuyakuba njalo. Izinsuku zikaNowa kwakuyisikhathi lapho umhlaba wawubuswa yizinengiso ezimbi ezenziwe yizingelosi eziwileyo, inzalo yazo kanye, inzalo yobubi eyenzelwe ukubhujiswa kwenu ngokuphelele: futhi ziyobuya, ngokuba bhekani, ngiletha imidondoshiya ukugcwalisa ulaka lwami. Bakwenza lokhu ngokususa nokwephula imingcele yemvelo uBaba ayibekayo ukuze ukuthula nokulunga kubuse, kodwa ngokususa nangokuwela ngokungafanele yona kanye le mingcele esobala emvelweni ngokwawo, uBaba ayimisileyo, abaningi benu bazingcolisile ngokuvuma kwenu ububi obugcwele ezikhathini zenu. Into eyake yaba khona, yiyona eyoba khona; nalokho okwenziwe, yikho okuyokwenziwa; akukho lutho olusha ngaphansi kwelanga. Anifundanga yini, o, nina bokukholwa okuncane? Ukususa imingcele kaNkulunkulu uqobo kubanga uhlanya, nokugula, nokudideka kwazo zonke izinto. Abaningi benu bazitika ngokugula kwengqondo, becabanga ukuthi indoda ingaba ngowesifazane nowesifazane abe yindoda, futhi niholela abantwana benu enkolweni yenu. Abaningi benu baya emasontweni aholwa ngabefundisi bamanga abangongqingili abavulekile nabazidlayo ekuguleni kwabo, futhi nicabanga ukuthi bakhuluma egameni lami? Yazini lokhu: uma nisesimweni esinjalo sokona, ngizoninikela ezinkanukweni enizifisa ngokungenanhlonipho, futhi niyovuna izithelo zezindlela zenu ezingcolile.

Bhekani! Ngenxa yalokho uNkulunkulu wabanikela ezinkanukweni eziyichilo: ngokuba nabesifazane babo bashintsha ukusetshenziswa kwemvelo bakusebenzisela okuphambene nemvelo: Kanjalo namadoda, eshiya ukusetshenziswa kwemvelo kowesifazane, asha enkanukweni yawo omunye komunye, amadoda elala namadoda enza okuyichilo, amukela kuwo umvuzo ofanele wephutha lawo. Kodwa cha, akwanele ukuholwa yizihlobongi namanga azo; nivumela abantwana benu ukuba bayekelelwe baze banqunywa amalungu phambi kwami. Kunemiphumela emikhulu ezayo ngalezi zenzo, futhi ngeke zishiywe zingajezisiwe. Kodwa nisaqhubeka ebuhlanyeni benu, futhi uma ningaphenduki, ngizonikhafula emlonyeni wami.

Wozani kimi ngokuphenduka, nihlale ngendwangu yesaka nomlotha, ukuze ngazi ubuqotho bokuphenduka kwenu, nikhale futhi nililele izono zenu ukuze ningakhokhi intengo ngokwahlulelwa. Abaningi benu bazikhukhumeza kangangokuba nizama ukungenza iNkosi nisenezinhliziyo ezingenalo ukholo, ngokuba ngenhliziyo umuntu ukholwa kuze kube sekulungeni. Ngakho-ke yibani nokholo ukuze nisindiswe; ukholo lwenu luyokhonjiswa kubo bonke ngokulahla kwenu isono nokuphuma ezindleleni zenu ezingcolile. Ababi bahamba nxazonke, lapho abantu ababi kakhulu bephakanyiswa. Kodwa nisaqhubeka nivumelana nezindlela zabasodomeli, futhi nisiza ekulethweni kobubi obukhulu ezweni, kuze kube seqophelweni lokuthi nivotela abahlukumezi bezingane ukuba bahole amazwe enu. Nintula ngisho nezinga eliphansi kakhulu lokuqonda, futhi anilandeli indlela yami; futhi uma niqhubeka, ngizoninikela ngokuphelele ezinkanukweni zenu eziyichilo ukuze kubhujiswe inyama ukuze umoya usindiswe.

Ake nginivule amehlo, nina eningafuni ukwazi futhi niyizimpumputhe ngenhloso, nina enihleli ekungcoleni nasebumnyameni, nizigingqa odakeni. Anikwazi ukunika imvume yenu ebubini nicabange ukuthi ningikhonza, ngokuba ngamelana nobubi nesilingo kwaze kwaba iconsi lokugcina legazi eligeleza livela kimi liya emhlabathini obomvu, ngokuba ngingowokugcina u-Adamu futhi ngenziwe umoya ophilisayo. Abaningi benu baphuphuthekiswe ngamazwi amahle nenkulumo emnandi futhi ninikeza imvume yenu ephelele ezigebengwini nakubantu abesabekayo abaxhaphaza izingane. Bahlukumeza izingane, bahlukumeza izingane ngenxa yezinkanuko zabo ezimbi: Ngokuba kanjalo luyeza ulaka lukaNkulunkulu ngeBhabhiloni Eyimfihlakalo, kodwa abaningi benu bayaqhubeka nokunikeza imvume yenu kulezi zilwane zasendle ezigcinelwe imililo yesihogo saphakade engeke icishwe.

Phumani kuyo, bantu bami, kungenjalo niyohlanganyela ezinhluphekweni zayo nasesahlulelweni esesabekayo esiyehlelayo; futhi nginixwayisile. Ngokuba kunakuhlanganyela kuni uKristu noBeliyali? Uma nihlanganyela emangeni ayo, niyohlanganyela ezinhluphekweni zayo; ngakho-ke zahlukaniseni ngokuphelele ngezwi nangezenzo, futhi ningavumelani nayo nakancane. Ningaphili impilo yokunganaki, yokuyekelela; ngokuba ngiyobajezisa bonke abenza kanjalo. Nicabanga ukuthi ngenxa yokuthi ninokuphila okuphakade anikwazi ukulahlekelwa kakhulu nemiphumela? Ngizoniphuca imivuzo yenu ngiyinike abanye, futhi niyoba abancane kunabo bonke embusweni wezulu uma niqhubeka, ngokuba usho kanje uYeshuwa HaMashiya, uMkhwenyana, kuMlobokazi wami!

Kusuka kuYahweh, uNkulunkulu Omkhulu Waphakade:

O, nina eningelusizo lwalutho enilimaza indalo yami, nina eningelusizo lwalutho enizidayisele kuSathane, nina eningelusizo lwalutho enenze uSathane uNkulunkulu wenu, nina eningelusizo lwalutho enenqaba iNdodana yami uJesu Kristu nensindiso engiyilungiselele ngegama lakhe, nina eningelusizo lwalutho enivumele abantwana benu ukuba bashe emlilweni kaMoloki. Ngizoninika izwe enilifisayo, futhi nginilungiselele izwe elingenami, izwe elingenayo iNdodana yami uKristu Jesu, izwe elinechibi elikhulu lomlilo nesibabuli elenzelwe nina kuphela.

Ngizoninika ubumnyama enibufisa kangaka, ngoba ukukhanya kwami ngeke kube khona lapho, futhi niyochitha ingunaphakade nihlukene nami, uYahweh, uNkulunkulu omkhulu okhazimulayo, ongenkazimulo nokukhanya kwakhe kungabonwa nyama, futhi okhona kusukela phakade kuze kube phakade. Nina bahlubuki, nina ababi enicekele phansi indalo yami kusukela ekuqaleni ngenxa yezinkanuko zenu nenzondo nomona, yazini ukuthi nizibangele isijeziso esikhulu nokuhlushwa engeke niphinde nelapheke kukho, nakubo bonke abadukiswa ngamanga enu benqaba uJesu Kristu, benqaba ukuphenduka emangeni enu beze eqinisweni lami.

Nicabanga ukuthi ukuphambanisa izindlela zami nokususa imingcele yami kuyinto elula, kodwa niyoyiqonda intukuthelo yami ngokushesha nje lapho ilangabi lokuqala linithinta, futhi niyogwinywa ngumlilo nesibabuli engeke niphunyuke kukho. Ngaphezu kwalokho, nibulale, nadlwengula, naphanga, nahlukumeza, nalimaza, nabulala, nacekele phansi, futhi nahlanganyela emacaleni amabi kakhulu, naze nafisa nenyama engaziwa; futhi nizihlanganise nezingelosi eziwileyo, ngakho-ke ngizonihlanganisa nechibi lomlilo engililungiselele uDeveli nezingelosi zakhe, futhi ngeke niphunyuke. Aninakho ukuthula.

Ngiyokhipha ukwahlulela okunobulungiswa phezu kwenu, ngoba nginguNkulunkulu wobulungiswa, futhi nginguNkulunkulu wempindiselo. Nokho, niyoma ngokuzikhukhumeza niphikisane nami, nethule icala lenu, nicabanga ukuthi ngizokwesaba izindlela zenu zobusathane zokuthi: yenza okuthandayo, kuyoba umthetho wonke. Aninakho ukuthula. Ngiyophindisela, futhi nithi nginguNkulunkulu onothando (engiyilo ngempela), kodwa bese nilindela ukuba ngingakhiphi ubulungiswa kulabo abangcolise indalo yami, kulabo abahlukumeza futhi babulala iNdodana yami ezelwe yodwa ngenkathi izinikela ibe yisihlengo sabo bonke; yebo, nilindela ukuba ngingakhiphi ubulungiswa phezu kwenu ngenxa yezindlela zenu ezingelusizo lwalutho lapho nibulale, nahlukumeza, futhi nakhubaza abangenacala futhi nalambisa abancane bami ngenxa yezinhloso zenu.

Ngempela, uSathane uqobo lwakhe ubeyocekelela phansi konke okungokwami ngenxa yokuhaha kwakhe, futhi noma kunjalo nizama ukungisola ngobuwula ekwahluleleni kwami okulungileyo lapho ngiza ukuvikela abami. Izinsuku zenu zokudukisa abancane bami sezizophela. Bhekani! Isilo senu nomprofethi wamanga bayobhujiswa ngokuphelele, futhi ngiyobaphonsa bephila kulelo chibi elikhulu elivutha umlilo nesibabuli; bayolinda nina ukuba nihlanganyele nabo efeni lenu.

Ukwahlulela okukhulu kuphezu kwakho, o, nkosana yobumnyama; umbuso wakho nabo bonke abakulandelayo sebezocekelwa phansi ngokuphelele. Ngoba anibona onkulunkulu, futhi anikwazi ukuzikhulula nina eningelusizo lwalutho esandleni sami, futhi ngizonibopha ebumnyameni obukhulu enibufisayo, yebo ngisho nakwalasha ojulile ngizonibopha ngeketanga elikhulu engeke niligqabule. Yebo, kwalasha niyokuba khona usuku olulodwa, ngoba usuku olulodwa kuNkulunkulu lunjengeminyaka eyinkulungwane, neminyaka eyinkulungwane injengosuku olulodwa. Futhi nangaleso sikhathi ngizonikhulula isikhashana ukuze nilinge futhi nibuthe abokugcina balabo abanyanyekayo abayolandela nina nezindlela zenu. Futhi ngiyobe senginiqeda nonke ngomlilo, nezihlalo zobukhosi zokwahlulela ziyobekwa, futhi ngiyonahlulela nonke nina benzi bokubi, futhi niyoya esijezisweni saphakade kude nobuso beNkosi. Niyosha phambi kwezingelosi naphambi kweWundlu, futhi bayonibuka nihlupheka futhi nijeziswa, ngoba nonile kubo bonke, futhi niyokhokhela ngokugcwele izono zenu kuze kube phakade.
