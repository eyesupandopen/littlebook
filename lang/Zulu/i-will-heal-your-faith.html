Emisebenzini KaPawulu Umphostoli
Meyi 02, 2024

Ngizokuphilisa Ukholo Lwakho, kusho iNkosi Enkulu!

Bhekani! Mina, yebo mina, uKristu Jesu iNkosi evusiwe nomSindisi wezwe, nginombuzo kini, makoti wami. Ingabe nizongiphendula ngeqiniso? Ingabe nizokwazi ukunginikeza impendulo efanele irekhodi leqiniso? Ngakho-ke, ngiyanibuza: Lapho iNdodana yomuntu ifika, nicabanga ukuthi, ngiyothola ukholo emhlabeni? Ngokusobala lona ngumbuzo ongadingi mpendulo, kodwa ukube bekufanele nime ekubizeni kwami ukuze niphendule, ibizothini impendulo yenu? Benizokhuluma nami ngokholo lwenu nokuthi luyisimangaliso kangakanani? Nizongitshela ngemicabango yenu emihle, enicabanga ngobuwula ukuthi nilawula izwe ngayo? Aniqapheli yini ukuthi nezimiso eziyisisekelo kakhulu okholweni lwami, ngisho ukholo lukaJesu Kristu, nalezo zimiso eziyisisekelo ziguquliwe zaba ngamanga? Kodwa nina nithi ngobuwula: "O, uNkulunkulu ngeke akuvumele lokho kwenzeke!" Ngokwalokho ngiyanibuza, nimazi kangakanani uNkulunkulu? Ningabeluleki bakhe yini? Nizoma niqondise uYahweh? Nizoma niqondise mina, uYeshua HaMashiach, ukuba ngenze intando yenu? Nokho abaningi benu ngobuwula bazama ukwenza lokho kanye, ngoba nikhohlisiwe ngendlela ebuhlungu kakhulu.

Nivumelile abaprofethi bamanga, wona kanye ukhula uSathane alutshala emabandleni akhe amanga (angasiwo awami), bona kanye laba baprofethi bamanga umfowethu uJuda akhuluma ngabo njengoba ngangiphefumulela amazwi ami enhliziyweni yakhe futhi njengoba ngangithinta umoya wakhe phakathi kwakhe ngogqozi olungcwele: Maye kubo, ngokuba bahambe endleleni kaKhayini, bagijimela ngokuhaha iphutha likaBalami ukuze bathole umvuzo, futhi babhubha ekugxekweni kukaKora. Laba bamanga bahamba obala phakathi kwenu, futhi abaningi benu bayabalandela, becabanga ukuthi balandela mina, ngoba bangabakhohlisi, futhi bazama ukulingisa izwi lami ukuze abangenalwazi phakathi kwezimvu zami babalandele ekuphambukeni okukhulu, abakukho. Ngoba yini enye uJuda ayisho ngalaba abangenalusizo? Laba bayizisihla emidlalweni yenu yothando, lapho bedla nani, bezondla bengenakwesaba: bangamafu angenamanzi, apheshulwa yimimoya, izihlahla ezinezithelo ezibunayo, ezingenazithelo, ezifile kabili, ezisishuliwe ngezimpande. Amagagasi olwandle avuthayo, akhipha ihlazo lawo, izinkanyezi ezizulayo, ezigcinelwe ubumnyama obukhulu kuze kube phakade.

Ngiyazi ukuthi umkhohlisi uyakholisa kwabanye benu, ngoba abanye benu abangazi ngempela kodwa bazama ukukhonza uYahweh nokungilandela mina, iNkosi yenu uJesu Kristu, ngezimfundiso zevangeli lenala. Ningaphazami: Ivangeli lenala lingamanga, futhi angizange ngizinikele njengewundlu lenu lePhasika ukuze niqongelele ingcebo: Ngoba uthando lwemali luyimpande yakho konke okubi: okwathi abanye belangazelela, baduka okholweni, bazigwaza ngezinhlungu eziningi. Abaningi benu balandele elinye lamavangeli amaningi amanga, kodwa alikho elinye ivangeli. Abaningi benu balandele omunye waboKristu abaningi bamanga, kodwa akekho omunye uKristu ngaphandle kwami, uYeshua HaMashiach. Ngithi kini ngokusobala, nikhohlisiwe, futhi abaningi benu bashintshe ukholo lweqiniso lukaJesu Kristu ngokholo lwamanga. Ukholo lwamanga nokholo olukhohlisayo lapho igama elithi ukholo (angininikanga yini iZwi leqiniso loKholo?) lingasasho ukuthemba, ukukholwa, nokunamathela kuYahweh, uYihlo, nakimi, uJesu Kristu iNkosi yenu, kodwa selichazwe kabusha ngokwedlulele okungenamsebenzi nezinganekwane nomcabango womlingo wobuqaba. Nivumele abangenalusizo banidukise, futhi amabandla enu angenwe ngabakhonzi bakasathane, ukhula olubi uSathane alutshala phakathi kwenu.

Lokhu kwedlulela kugxila emicabangweni emihle nemibi, futhi nina ngobuwula nicabanga ukuthi noma yini enizindla ngayo isikhathi eside izokwenzeka, kube sengathi uNkulunkulu ubeke amandla nomusa wakhe kulabo abanokholo lwamanga olukhulu, lapho niyala khona izinto ngemicabango yenu. Aniqondi yini ukuthi bonke abenza lezi zinto eziyize bayabanjwa futhi bathiyeke futhi bathunjelwe ezinkanukweni zabo nakulawa manga, baze baboshelwe ekuhlulekeni, ngoba nilandela izindlela zikaSathane ngokungazi? Qondani ukuthi kwakuyizingelosi eziwile, yebo, oSathane nabalindi abahlangana nabo, abafundisa isintu ubuthakathi, ukusika izimpande, iziphonso, neziphithiphithi; futhi abantu abaningi ababi bangene ebandleni lami laseLawodikeya futhi bathumba abaningi njengempango abakholelwa emangeni abo.

Lokhu kwenzekile ngoba abaningi ebandleni lami laseLawodikeya bayenqaba ukuma balwe ngokulunga ukuze ninqobe okubi ngokuhle! Ake nginisize niqonde iqiniso. Akekho noyedwa kini onamandla nekhono ngokwakhe, ngoba kusebuthakathakeni benu lapho ekugcineni niqaphela khona ubuze bemizamo yenu. Khona-ke, izikhathi eziningi, niyaqonda ukuthi lapho nithembela kimi, uKristu Jesu, futhi nilandela iziqondiso zami endleleni enginikhethele yona, ukuthi nithembela kimi nasemandleni ami futhi ninokholo oluphelele kimi. Ngoba ngizofakazela kini nonke ngendlela yami, lapho nihlala nithobekile ezwini lami futhi niphenduka lapho nginitshela khona, ukuthi emizamweni nasemandleni enu anilutho ngaphandle kohlamvu lwesihlabathi ogwadule, olulahlekile neziphepho, futhi angifanga ukuze ngisindise isihlabathi kodwa umhlaba obomvu ka-Adamu, ngoba ngingu-Adamu wokugcina, futhi ngingumoya onika ukuphila. Nokho, kimi Kristu Jesu, ninamandla futhi aninakuvinjwa, ngoba ninaleli gugu ezitsheni zobumba, futhi ngizoninika izimangaliso eziningi, izibonakaliso, nezimangaliso nezinto ezinjalo ngezandla zenu lapho ninokholo lobudlelwano nami, uJesu Kristu, esikhundleni sokukholwa kini. Ngicela ningivumele nginihole, niyeke amanga alabo abawileyo, ngoba ngezenzo zabo ulaka olukhulu luqongelelwa kubo ngosuku lolaka, lapho isiphetho sabo siqedwa ngomlilo nesibabule esivuthayo.

Qondani ukuthi le nkohliso yafinyelelwa kanjani, ngoba kumaJubili amuva nje kwaba khona ukukhanyiselwa kwamanga lapho labo umphakathi wenu obathatha njengabakhulu benqaba uNkulunkulu bevuna ukucabanga kwabo okuyize. Baze benza isithombe sokucabanga ngesikhathi soguquko lwabo; aninalwazi yini ngomlando? Le mithombo engenamanzi enefilosofi yabo eyonakele neyize ayimvumelanga uNkulunkulu futhi yafundisa ngephutha ukuthi uNkulunkulu wayemane engumbukeli ongenzi lutho ongenandaba ngempela nesintu kodwa ohlinzeka ngakho konke esekude futhi evumela isintu ukuba sizitholele, umusa kaNkulunkulu uya kumthakathi onamandla kunabo bonke. Khona-ke, ngokuhamba kwesikhathi, imiphefumulo eminingi engazinzile, yebo, ukhula lukaSathane, lwangena ebandleni lami laseLawodikeya ukuze luqedele amanga alo ngezikhathi ezifanele. Bangena kini ngamazwi amahle nenkulumo emnandi futhi bafundisa amandla okucabanga komlingo kuMakoti wami, futhi abaningi phakathi koMzimba wami babalandela ngobuhili, ngoba kwavuswa ukuhaha kwabo.

Lawa madodana amabi kaSathane ahlangana ngobuqili nobuthakathi ezimfundisweni zami futhi aphumelela ukungcolisa iZwi lami elingcwele ngemvubelo, ngaphandle kokuthi iZwi lami alikwazi ukungcoliswa yiziqambo zabo, ngoba amanga abo ami phezu kwamanga. Aniqondi yini ukuthi imvubelo encane ibilisa inhlama yonke? Aniqondi yini ukuthi imvubelo kaHerode nemvubelo yabaFarisi akuzona zodwa izindlela zokonakala? Ngoba niloyiwe yimvubelo yobuthakathi bobuKristu! Ngakho-ke, PENDUKANI! Ngoba ngathini kwabaseKorinte ngesandla soMphostoli uPawulu? Hlanjululani-ke imvubelo endala, ukuze nibe yinhlama entsha, njengoba ningenamvubelo. Ngoba noKristu iPhasika lethu uhlatshiwe ngenxa yethu. Yizwani amazwi ami! Ngakho lezi zilwane eziwubulwane, le mithombo engenamanzi, ngisho nokhula olungenalusizo nolunobuthi lwashumayela futhi lwasungula amahlelo alo amanga obuKristu, ngisho nezinkolo zamanga, lweba abaningi balabo ababengabalandela eBandleni leqiniso, lapho nginakekela khona uMakoti wami ngothando nomusa nesihe, ngibahola besuka kimi baya ezindlini zamanga zokukhonza ezinikezelwe ngasese kuBhali, ngoba isizwe senu yiBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo!

Kwaba sengathi lokhu akukubi ngokwanele, abathakathi abaningi abanamandla nabacebile bakhokha imali eningi futhi basebenzisa umzamo omkhulu ukubeka amanga abo emaBhayibhelini amanga. Bakhipha imibhalo yase-Alexandria, yobuGnostic, okwabangela ukuba iBandla lami libe nengqondo ephindwe kabili lisuke ekugxileni okukodwa kwemibhalo engayilondoloza isuka e-Antiyokiya, ngisho namaBhayibheli athile okubhekisela kuwo lapho lawa manga engezwa emakhoneni. Lezi zincwadi zabe sezifakwa ezikoleni zenu zenkolo ezonakele futhi zaba amaBhayibheli okufunda ayisisekelo alaba baprofethi bamanga abashumayele amanga ngamanga kusukela lapho, futhi bazokhokha ngokugcwele. Kusukela ngaleso sikhathi, amazwe enu agcwele amabandla amanga aholwa yizimpisi ezihahayo, futhi nokho ziningi kakhulu izimvu zami ezicabanga ngephutha ukuthi zingazihlanganisa nezimpisi. Amabandla enu amanga ayizindlu zokuhlambalaza; ayizindlu zokuzijabulisa lapho becula khona izindumiso kuSathane phambi kwenu, futhi nintula ukuqonda okungokomoya ukuze nibone. Baningi amakhosi phakathi kombuso kaDeveli, futhi bacula iNkosi, iNkosi kuwo, ngaphandle kokuthi lapho bethi kimi Nkosi, Nkosi, ngiyothi, Dedani kimi, nina benzi bobubi ngoba angikaze nginazi.

Ukwenza izinto zibe zimbi kakhulu, bathatha ivangeli lenala elihlambalazayo futhi baliguqula ngokwengeziwe ngokufaka i-psychology yokuzisiza, futhi ngithini mayelana nezindaba ezinjalo: Qaphelani funa umuntu eze anitshontshe ngezimfundiso eziyize nenkohliso, ngamasiko abantu, nangemithetho yezwe, hhayi ngoKristu. Lawa mabandla amanga onakele angenanani lokuhlenga, futhi amaningi asungulwe ngabakhi ababi abakhululekile kimi, itshe legumbi eliyinhloko, futhi zonke izakhiwo zabo zizowela esihlabathini esigudlukayo. Laba ngabakhi abahlambalazayo nababazi bamatshe bamanga nabakhi bezitini bamanga abalile itshe legumbi eliyinhloko, futhi abanalo iqiniso kubo! Baze babukise ngezimpawu zabo ezindlini zenu zokukhonza, lapho benigcona khona obala. Yizwani isaga enginiphathele sona, ngoba siyiqiniso futhi sizoletha ukukhanya kweqiniso emiphefumulweni yenu: Umuntu ongenamsebenzi, umuntu omubi, uhamba ngomlomo ophambeneyo. Uqhwanyaza ngamehlo akhe, ukhuluma ngezinyawo zakhe, ufundisa ngeminwe yakhe.

Ukuphambana kusenhliziyweni yakhe, usungula ububi njalo, uhlwanyela ukungezwani. Kanjalo-ke kunjalo futhi benza kanjalo phambi kwamehlo enu, kodwa anikwazi ukubona, ngoba niphuphuthekiswe yizimfundiso zabo zamanga nezindlela zabo eziphambeneyo.

Bantwana bami, phumani phakathi kwabo nizihlukanise; ngoba uKristu unobudlelwane buni noBheliyali. Ningabi nabudlelwane nemisebenzi yobumnyama engatheli, futhi nihlukane nabo ngokuphelele. Ngoba nina, bandla lami laseLawodikeya, nani nisivivi futhi aninamgogodla, futhi lezi akuzona izindlela zami nakancane, ngoba ngaxosha abashintshi bemali eThempelini ngentshiseko yami ngoYahweh. Buyelani kimi, uJesu Kristu, umalusi weqiniso nodokotela wemiphefumulo yenu, nifunde kimi. Ngoba ukuvuma okuhle nokucabanga okuhle okungenamsebenzi akusikho okwami, kodwa ngamanga anihutshelwa ngabaphostoli bamanga, zona kanye izikhonzi zikaSathane eziziguqule zaba yizikhonzi zokulunga. Gijimelani kimi, bantwanyana bami, ngoba isikhathi siphelile, futhi nina ngokungazi nicabanga ukuthi ninaso sonke isikhathi emhlabeni, futhi ninemizuzu embalwa nje yokuphenduka nibuyele kimi ngokuthobeka ukuze ngisanibekela indawo etafuleni lami futhi ngisanivulela umnyango emazulwini ukuze ningene enjabulweni yeNkosi yenu!

Ngoba abaningi benu bacabanga ukuthi nizobuthelwa kimi ngenkathi niphila lawa manga nalapho nidla ukungcola abaprofethi bamanga abanondla ngakho, ngokuhlambalaza egameni lami. Yazini ukuthi angibathumanga futhi amazwi abo awasiwo awami, futhi uma ningasuki kubo, kuzodingeka nizibonakalise kimi ekulingweni nasekuhlolweni okukhulu ngesikhathi sosizi lukaJakobe, olufikele umhlaba wonke futhi seluzodedelwa! Bhekani kimi emoyeni wenu; gxilani, ngoba ningezwa imihelo yokubeletha emoyeni wenu, ngoba ngiyanibizela ekhaya futhi uMakoti wami usezozala. Bhekani, nginishiyela incwadi enganithumela yona, nina maLawodikeya ami, ngoba kulawa mazwi kukhanyiswe indlela yokubuyela kimi; futhi uma nizolalela, ngizobuyisela umvuzo wenu kini, futhi niyodla nami etafuleni lami emazulwini engililungiselele uMakoti wami.

Nakuso isithunywa sebandla eliseLawodikeya, bhala: Usho kanje (u-Ameni) ufakazi othembekileyo noqinisileyo, ukuqala kwezidalwa zikaNkulunkulu. Ngiyayazi imisebenzi yakho, ukuthi awubandi futhi awushisi. Ngingathanda ukuba ububanda noma ushisa. Ngakho-ke ngenxa yokuthi uphakathi kwakho kokubili, awubandi futhi awushisi. Ngizokukhafula emlonyeni wami: ngoba ucebile futhi wande ngezimpahla, futhi awusweli lutho, futhi awazi ukuthi ulusizi futhi uyahlupheka, umpofu, uyimpumputhe futhi unqunu. Ngikweluleka ukuba uthenge kimi igolide elicwengisisiwe emlilweni, ukuze ucebe, nezingubo ezimhlophe, ukuze wembathiswe, ukuze ubunqunu bakho obungcolile bungabonakali, futhi ugcobe amehlo akho ngamafutha okugcoba, ukuze ubone. Bonke engibathandayo, ngiyabasola futhi ngibashaye. Ngakho-ke, shiseka, uphenduke. Bheka, ngimi emnyango ngingqongqoza. Uma umuntu ezwa izwi lami avule umnyango, ngizongena kuye, ngidle naye, naye adle nami. Onqobayo ngizomnika ukuba ahlale nami esihlalweni sami, njengoba nami nganqoba ngahlala noBaba, esihlalweni sakhe.

Bhekani!

Nginguye ohamba phakathi kwezinti zezibani eziyisikhombisa, ngakho-ke buyelani kimi, funa ngisuse uthi lwesibani lwenu kini.

Ngingumalusi omuhle owazinikela ngenxa yomhlambi.

Nginguye oyobusa umhlaba esihlalweni sobukhosi sikaDavide futhi ngishaye izizwe ngenduku yensimbi.

Nginguye ofanele ukuthatha incwadi futhi aphule izimpawu.

Nginguye oqondisa ingelosi enyuka empumalanga, enophawu lukaNkulunkulu ophilayo, ngoba bazobeka uphawu kwabayi-144,000 bami, zona kanye izinceku zikaNkulunkulu ophilayo.

Nginguye oyokuma eNtabeni iZiyoni nabayi-144,000 bami, futhi bazocula iculo elisha okungekho muntu ongakwazi ukulifunda.

NgiyiNkosi yamakhosi noMbusi wababusi, futhi ngiyeza ukulwa namakhosi omhlaba, ngoba isikhathi sawo sesifikile.

Nginguye ozayo ngomakoti wami, ngoba ukubonakala kwami kuseduze. Zilungiseleleni, Makoti wami!
