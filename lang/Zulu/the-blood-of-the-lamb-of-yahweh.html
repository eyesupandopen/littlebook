Ubufakazi Obukabili: Israyeli
Okubabayo Okumnandi
Disemba 3, 2024

Igazi LeWundlu likaYahweh

Wozani eNkosini yenu yaphakade, nizwe amazwi Ami, nina zihlobo zami, nithobise izinhliziyo zenu ngokulalela amazwi kaYeshua HaMashiach. Ngoba Mina, Yeshua HaMashiach, ngizokwenza ohlakaniphile phakathi kwenu, O' Israyeli, ahlakaniphe kakhulu nakakhulu lapho nilalela iziyalezo zami, ukuze namukele ukuhlakanipha nolwazi olwengeziwe, ngoba ukuhlakanipha nolwazi kuyoba ukusimama kwezikhathi zenu namandla ensindiso; ukwesaba iNkosi kuyingcebo yenu.

Ngakho-ke, qondisisani izikhathi eniphila kuzo ukuze nihambe ngokucophelela, futhi ukuze izinhloso zami zensindiso zifinyelele ekupheleleni okugcwele kini enibizwe ngegama lami, yebo, nina O' Israyeli, nakuba okwamanje ningangazi futhi ningaphili ngezindlela zami, ngoba u-Israyeli ubuswa u-Esawu no-Ashkenazi, kanti kubekwe ngokusobala ukuthi omdala uyokhonza omncane, futhi akekho u-Esawu noma u-Ashkenazi onelungelo lobuzibulo befa likaJakobe. Kodwa uJakobe wamala uYeshua HaMashiach, nami ngaphendukela ezizweni okwesikhashana ukuze kugcwaliseke lokho u-Israyeli angazange akulalele ngokuphelele, ukuthi izizwe ziyobusiswa ngo-Israyeli, nokuthi ensalini yezizwe ezilahlekileyo kuyosindiswa insali.

Noma kunjalo, lapho niphendula izinhliziyo zenu ngokulalela izwi lemiyalo kaMesiya wenu weqiniso, niyobuyiselwa njengesizwe esikhethekile kuYahweh, nakuba okhokho benu bafulathela uYahweh kudala. Yebo, bafulathela uYahweh lapho bengala mina, uJesu waseNazaretha, onguYeshua HaMashiach, nangezinsuku zokuphatha kukaPontiyu Pilatu eJudiya, lapho ngakhishwa abapristi babo abakhulu neSanihedrini, nganikelwa ezandleni zabantu abayizoni, ukuba benze lezo zinto umthetho owawubavimbela ngokusobala ukuba bazenze; kodwa ngenzondo yabo ngami nangezinto zonke zikaYahweh, bakwenza lokho ngokushesha ngobubi obukhulu nonya. Kanjalo-ke insindiso ka-Adamu ifezwa ngezandla zezitha zakhe, ukuze noma ngubani okholwayo abize igama likaJesu waseNazaretha, onguYeshua HaMashiach, asindiswe ngokuphelele.

Ngakho-ke kungenxa yokuthi ngiyiWundlu likaYahweh; kungenziwa kanjani ukuhlawulelwa kwezono zesintu, ngaphandle kokuba igazi lami elingenasono lichithwe njengomhlatshelo ukukhokha isamba esingenakulinganiswa ngenani ngempela. Qondisisani ukuthi nakuba umuntu wemvelo eziqhenya ngokuzidla okukhulu ngokuthi uzenzile futhi uzimele ezintweni zonke, umuntu wemvelo uwile esimweni esiyisoni nesidabukisayo sokungalungi nehlazo elifanele.

Kodwa-ke, ngomusa wami nangesihe sami nangokuzinikela kwami eGolgotha, lapho ngazinikela kuYahweh njengeWundlu likaNkulunkulu, lapho ngazinikela ngobuhlungu obukhulu, lapho iconsi lami lokugcina legazi ligelezela eGolgotha, kwaze kwaba sekuphefumuleni kwami kokugcina lapho ngiphimisa amazwi athi, "Kuphelile." Ngangcwatshwa, ngavuka futhi ngosuku lwesithathu njengokwemibhalo; njengoba uYahweh ayethembisile ukuthi uzokwenza, ngoba akunakwenzeka ukuthi ukufa kubambe igazi elingenasono. UYahweh wangivusa, futhi ngemva kwalokho ngokushesha, ngangena endaweni engcwelengcwele emazulwini uqobo, lapho ngazethula njengompristi omkhulu kuze kube phakade kuYahweh, uMINA ENGINGUYE omkhulu, owamukela lowo mhlatshelo ingunaphakade. Njengoba-ke ninompristi omkhulu kangaka, ongenile ezulwini, uJesu, iNdodana kaYahweh, masibambelele siqinise ekuvumeni kwethu. Ngoba aninampristi omkhulu, O' Israyeli, ongenakuzwelana nobuthakathaka benu, kodwa owalingwa ezintweni zonke ngendlela efanayo, nokho engenasono.

Ngakho ngiyabuza, O' Israyeli, yini ephelile? Ngakho ngiphendula ngokusobala: Isivumelwano sami esisha, esiyisivumelwano saphakade, iTestamente lami Elisha! Ngoba isivumelwano siqinisekiswa lapho abantu sebefile: ngoba asinamandla uma lowo owasenzayo esaphila; futhi cishe zonke izinto ngaphansi komthetho zihlanjululwa ngegazi, futhi ngaphandle kokuchithwa kwegazi akukho ukuthethelelwa kwezono. Ngendlela efanayo neTestamente Elidala, lapho uMose esekhulumile yonke imiyalo kubantu, ngokomthetho, wathatha igazi lamathole nezimbuzi, namanzi noboya obunsomi nehisopi, wafafaza incwadi nabantu bonke, ethi, "Leli yigazi lesivumelwano uNkulunkulu animisele sona."

Nokho, isivumelwano sakuqala, iTestamente likaMose langaphambili, lalingahloselwe neze ukuba isiphetho sohlelo lukaYahweh lokuhlenga isintu, futhi no-Israyeli wayengafanele neze ukuba ahlangane ngokuzidla, akhe amaqembu ahlukene ngokwahlukana okuningi okungadingekile nokubulalanayo. Kunalokho, u-Israyeli wayehloselwe ukuba yisibani sensindiso kwabezizwe, ukuze izizwe zibone ukuthi kukhona uNkulunkulu kwa-Israyeli, futhi ukuze izizwe zithelekele kwa-Israyeli zifunde ngoYahweh, njengoba kwakulotshiwe ezikhathini zasendulo.

Ngakho-ke, qondisisani isimo sesikhashana sikaMose njengoba sasenziwa kwa-Israyeli ezikhathini ezingaphambi kweTestamente lami Elisha, futhi niqonde ukuthi uMose uphila ngokugcwele kuMesiya wenu weqiniso, oNgcwele kaNkulunkulu, owaphila ngokuphelele ngokukaMose, nowanikela ngempilo yakhe kanye, kwaphela, kuze kube phakade, ukukhokha isijeziso sikaMose ngenxa yenu. Njengoba kulotshiwe, ngakho-ke, qondisisani iTestamente lami Elisha, O' Israyeli: Ngokuba uMthetho, njengoba unethunzi lezinto ezinhle ezizayo, hhayi umfanekiso uqobo lwezinto, awusoze ngaleyo mihlatshelo abayinikelayo unyaka nonyaka njalo, wangcwelisa abasondelayo. Ngoba, ukuba bekungenjalo, ibingeke yini iyekwe ukunikelwa, ngoba abasondelayo, sebehlanjululiwe kanye, bebengasenakunembeza wezono? Kodwa kuleyo mihlatshelo, kukhona ukukhunjulwa kwezono minyaka yonke.

Ngoba akunakwenzeka ukuthi igazi lezinkunzi nezimbuzi lisuse izono. Ngakho-ke, bantu bakithi, uMose wayekwazi kuphela ukuhlanjululwa kwezono okwesikhashana, kodwa izono eziningi ngaphansi kukaMose zasala, lapho labo ababenza izinto ezinjalo babenqunywa kubantu kuze kube phakade ngenxa yezono zabo. Ngakho-ke, uMose wayeyisithunzi sezinto ezizayo, lezo zinto ezigcwaliseke ngokugcwele emhlatshelweni wami, ngoba igazi lami elingenasono lihlawulela zonke izono, kanye, kwaphela, naphakade, ukuze bonke abavela ku-Adamu bakwazi ukuthola insindiso ngalo. Kodwa kulabo abamukela uphawu lwesilo, ngeke babe nokuthethelelwa naphakade.

Ngakho kulotshiwe ngoYeshua HaMashiach, ukuthi ngempilo yami nokulalela kwami u-Adamu angabuyiselwa ku-Elohim, uMdali wakhe waphakade nolungileyo. Yingakho, lapho engena ezweni, uthi, "Umhlatshelo nomnikelo awuwufunanga, kodwa umzimba ungi ordainedle. Eminikelweni yokushiswa, naseminikelweni yezono, awuthokozanga." Ngase ngithi, "Bheka, ngiyeza (Ekuqaleni kwencwadi kulotshiwe ngami) ukuthi ngenze intando yakho, O Nkulunkulu." Ngenhla, lapho ethi, "Umhlatshelo nomnikelo, neminikelo yokushiswa, neminikelo yezono awuyifunanga, futhi awuthokozanga ngayo (okunikelwa ngokoMthetho)," Wayesethi, "Bheka, ngiyeza ukwenza intando yakho, O Nkulunkulu," ususa okokuqala, ukuze amise okwesibili. Ngaleyo ntando siyangcweliswa, ngomnikelo womzimba kaJesu Kristu owenziwa kanye. Futhi wonke umpristi wakwaLevi umi nsuku zonke ekhonza, futhi evame ukunikela uhlobo olulodwa lomnikelo, olungenakususa izono naphakade: Kodwa lo muntu, emva kokunikela umhlatshelo owodwa wezono, uhlezi ingunaphakade ngakwesokunene sikaNkulunkulu, Futhi kusukela manje ulindile, kuze kube yilapho izitha zakhe zenziwa isenabelo sezinyawo zakhe. Ngoba ngomnikelo owodwa ubangcwelisile kuze kube phakade labo abangcweliswayo ngawo.

Ngoba noYahweh uyasifakazela: ngoba emva kokuba eshilo ngaphambili, "Lesi yisiVumelwano engiyosenza nabo emva kwalezo zinsuku," kusho u-Elohim, "Ngizobeka imithetho yami ezinhliziyweni zabo, nasemingqondweni yabo ngiyoyibhala. Nezono zabo nobubi babo angisayikukhumbula." Manje, lapho kukhona ukuthethelelwa kwalezi zinto, akusekho mnikelo wesono. Futhi nimi esikhathini sokungazi kwesizwe lapho okhokho benu kudala bamala lowo oyiWundlu lePhasika eliphelele likaYahweh uqobo, lapho uYahweh alithanda kangaka izwe, waze wanikela ngeNdodana yakhe ezelwe yodwa, ukuze noma ngubani okholwa egameni likaYeshua HaMashiach, uJesu Kristu, lowo muntu asindiswe ngokuphelele. Ngakho-ke ngiyanibiza ukuba nisuke ekungazini kwenu nasebubumpumputheni benu obuyingxenye, O' Israyeli, ngisho nangokwala nokuchitha ngokuphelele ukuhlakanipha okuhlambalazayo kuka-Esawu okuqukethwe kwiTalmud yakhe engcolile, bese nibuyela emazwini ahlanzekile kaYahweh, lawo mazwi alondolozwe ohlwini lwemibhalo yaseByzantine, i-Textus Receptus njengoba ilondolozwe embhalweni kaStephens.

Ngakho-ke, qondisisani ukuthi ukwala uYeshua HaMashiach kusho ukuthini kini Israyeli kuze kube phakade, ukuze niqonde ubucayi balokho kwala. Ngoba uma nona ngamabomu emva kokuba senamukele futhi navuma lelo qiniso, akusekho mhlatshelo wezono, kodwa ukulindela okwesabekayo kokwahlulelwa, nomlilo onamandla, oyodla abaphikisi. Lowo owadelela umthetho kaMose, wafa ngaphandle kwesihe ngofakazi ababili noma abathathu: Nicabanga ukuthi uyakufanelekela isijeziso esingakanani, lowo onyathela iNdodana kaNkulunkulu, futhi ebala igazi leTestamente Elisha njengeto elingcwele. Ngakho-ke, yamukelani umhlatshelo wami, nivume izono zenu kimi, ukuze nginithethelele, ngoba ngithembekile futhi ngilungile ukunithethelela izono zenu, ukuze nihlanjululwe egazini lami elihlawulelayo, futhi nivumelane amaphutha enu omunye komunye.

Ngakho-ke, ngiza kini ngomusa wami nesihawu ukuze nginisuse ebumpumputheni benu obuyingxenye, O' Israyeli, ukuze nisengathola insindiso ngokulalela izwi lami, futhi ukuze nisuke ekwaleni kokhokho benu okugxilile kuze kube phakade kwiTalmud yenu engcolile nasenkolo yenu engcolile u-Esawu ayenzele ukubhujiswa kwenu, kodwa eninamathela kuyo sengathi ukuhlakanipha kwakhe kwanikezwa uYahweh uqobo, hhayi uSathane. O' Israyeli, zwanini izwi lami elisindisayo, namukele uthando lwami ngani, nize kimi ngezinhliziyo zenu ezigcwele, zivulekile futhi zilungele ukwamukela, ukuze nigcwaliswe ngenkazimulo yonke nobuhle bonke.

Ngininika ukuqonda ukuthethelelwa okuphelele nokubuyiselwa okulethwa yigazi lami elihlawulelayo kubo bonke abangamukelayo njengeNkosi yabo esindisayo, futhi njengoMesiya wabo weqiniso, ngoba nginguye. Futhi njengoba u-Adamu wadalwa, wabunjwa, wenziwa engenasono nasekupheleleni okugcwele kwaze kwaba usuku awona ngalo ngamabomu, kanjalo nokukhokhelwa kwesono sika-Adamu kudinga igazi elingenasono, imfuneko engenakwenzeka kubo bonke abahlala emhlabeni ekonakaleni ukuba bayihlinzeke. Futhi ethandweni lukaYahweh, wahlinzeka ngobuphelele obunjalo negazi elingenasono emzimbeni weNdodana yakhe ezelwe yodwa, enikelwe njengomhlatshelo wezono zika-Adamu, ngakho-ke akusekho mhlatshelo wesono.

Nginguye owazinikela ngokugcwele ngenxa yezono zika-Adamu owileyo.

Nginguye owazinikela ngokugcwele ekuhlawulelweni kwaphakade ukuze bonke abazinikela ensindisweni yaphakade bahlanjululwe ngakho.

Nginguye onguMpristi wenu Omkhulu kuze kube phakade ngokohlelo lukaMelkisedeki, lowo owazinikela njengePhasika eliphelele kanye, konke, naphakade.

NginguYeshua HaMashiach, lowo onguMesiya wenu weqiniso, futhi ukubusa kwami akupheli.

NginguKristu Jesu, futhi ngithumele amazwi ami ngesithunywa sami nenceku yami, nangomthunywa wami nenceku yami amazwi ami athunyelwe.

Ngakho-ke, qondisisani ukuthi ukugcwaliseka kwazo zonke izinto sekufikile, O' Israyeli, futhi ngiyanibizela kimi ngeVangeli Laphakade elinikezwa ngezikhathi ezifanele zokwambulwa, nezikhathi zokwambulwa yimanje. Ngoba kusukela ekuqaleni uYahweh wathi enyokeni ensimini ngezikhathi lapho isono singena ezweni, "Ngizobeka ubutha phakathi kwakho nowesifazane, naphakathi kwenzalo yakho nenzalo yakhe; wena uyomlimaza isithende, yena uyokuchoboza ikhanda."

Ngakho-ke ekuhambeni kwesikhathi uDavide, esengumfana ongumelusi, owaba yiNkosi yakwa-Israyeli ngaphambi kokuba umbuso uhlukaniswe; owaba futhi umuntu ngokwenhliziyo kaYahweh, wavikela u-Israyeli wabulala lowo mFilisti omkhulu, uGoliyati waseGati, owayengukhula, inzalo yamaRefayimi! Ngakho uDavide, umfana ongumelusi, waphuma waya empini noGoliyati waseGati njengomelusi, enenduku, isikhwama nendwayimane, eya empini elwa nomdondoshiya weqhawe, kodwa uDavide waya empini ngegama likaYahweh, amaFilisti ayemdelela, uDavide wema walwa nezitha zika-Israyeli ukuze impi ka-Elohim inqobe.

Njengoba itshe elivela endwayimaneni kaDavide labangela ukuba lo mdondoshiya ongcwele awele phansi ebhakuza, uDavide wahosha inkemba yalo mdondoshiya wamnquma ikhanda. Futhi njengoba kulotshiwe endulo: "UDavide wathatha ikhanda lomFilisti, waliletha eJerusalema, kodwa izikhali zakhe wazibeka etendeni lakhe." Ngakho uDavide wangcwaba ugebhezi lukaGoliyati waseGati ngaphandle kwezingange zaseJerusalema. Nokho, emhlatshelweni wami njengeWundlu likaNkulunkulu, ngakhishelwa ngaphandle kwezingange zedolobha, nalapho eGolgotha ngabethelwa, indawo yogebhezi. Futhi njengoba ngangibethelwa, bangibethela kulowo muthi onamahhabhula, balimaza isithende sami ngezipikili zabo lapho bengibethela kulowo muthi. Ngakho ukunqoba kwatholakala lapho ku-Adamu, lapho isithende sami silinyazwa eKalvari, lapho ikhanda likaSathane lachotshozwa ngokufanayo, njengoba igazi lami lageleza liletha ukunqoba phezu kwenzalo yenyoka kuze kube phakade. Ngakho-ke, ngendlela efanayo umfana ongumelusi anqoba ngayo inzalo kaSathane, kanjalo noMelusi Omkhulu wanqoba uSathane kuze kube phakade emhlatshelweni wami wothando engawenzela izono zika-Adamu.

Nokho, njengoba ngangifa ngobuhlungu obukhulu njengesigebengu esivamile (ubugebengu engimsulwa kubo) ngaphandle kwamasango alowo muzi ongcwele, igazi lami elingenasono laphuma emzimbeni wami lapho, limelele ngokugcwele umuntu oyisoni womhlabathi obomvu efinyelela ekuhlenzeni okuphelele. Futhi njengoba igazi lami lacwilisa umhlabathi obomvu endaweni yogebhezi, eKalvari, ikhanda lenyoka lalinyazwa ingunaphakade, futhi ekuphethweni kwazo zonke izinto lowo drago omdala uyoboshwa aphonswe phakade emlilweni wesijeziso lapho isibungu sabo singafi, ngoba lunjalo ifa lakhe.

Futhi nginguye ozayo ukubhubhisa ngokuphelele umbuso wezitha ukuze udrago aboshwe avalelwe kwalasha iminyaka eyinkulungwane, ukuze kubuswe ukubusa kwami okulungile emhlabeni. Ekupheleni kwalokho, uyokhululwa okwesikhashana sakhe esifushane, lapho eyobutha khona ukuhlubuka kwakhe kokugcina. Futhi lapho behlasela inkambu yabangcwele, umlilo uyokwehla uvela kuYahweh uqobo, ubadle ngokuphelele, ngaleso sikhathi uSathane uyophonswa emlilweni waphakade ovutha ingunaphakade ngesibabule esibolayo.

Ngalokho, ngininika ukuqonda, O' Israyeli, ukuthi isijeziso sikaMose sikhokhelwe ngokugcwele ngomhlatshelo kaYeshua HaMashiach onguJesu Kristu ngolimi lwesiNgisi, ukuze niqonde ukuphelela komhlatshelo wami nobuphelele obufezwe yigazi lami elihlawulayo njalo elachithwa lanikelwa ngenxa yezono zika-Adamu. Qondisisani ngokugcwele, O' Israyeli, ukuthi ukube abawileyo babeyiqondile imfihlakalo kaYahweh, babengeke neze bambethele uMninimandla wenu weNkazimulo! Ngoba ngokubethelwa kwami, lapho inyoka yalinyaza khona isithende sami okwesikhashana, ikhanda lenyoka manje selilinyazwe ingunaphakade, ukulimala okungeke kuphinde kululame kukho.

Ngakho-ke, O' Israyeli, zwanini amazwi ami niqonde uthando lwami ngani, nokuthi ngiyanibiza kulesi sikhathi sokugcina. Ngakho-ke qondisisani inkulumo yami ngoba kulotshiwe: "UNkulunkulu wokuthula owavusa kwabafileyo uYeshua HaMashiach, umalusi omkhulu wezimvu, ngegazi lesivumelwano saphakade, aniphelelise kuyo yonke imisebenzi emihle, ukuba nenze intando yakhe, esebenza kini lokho okuthandekayo emehlweni akhe ngoJesu Kristu, okukuye makube udumo kuze kube phakade naphakade. Amen."
