Ufakazi Omabili: Ibandla
Emisebenzini KaPetru
Ephreli 10, 2024

Yimani Isibindi!

Bantwanyana bami, yimani isibindi; angithi ngimnqobile omubi, nganihlengela ngegazi lami uqobo? Kungani kufanele nidangale? Kungani izinhliziyo zenu zibuhlungu; nicabanga ukuthi nginikhohliwe? Cha, esikhundleni sokuthembela ekuqondeni kwenu, funani ukuhlakanipha kwami, okuvela phezulu; ngokuba niyoholwa ukuhlakanipha kokuhlonipha uNkulunkulu, ngakho-ke ningakushiyi! Ukuhlakanipha akusamemezi yini emigwaqweni? Ngakho-ke ningesabi, yimani isibindi, ngokuba mina nginqobile nani nizonqoba, ngegazi lami uqobo. Vumelani ukuthula kwami kududuze izinhliziyo zenu, vumelani namazwi ami adambise imiphefumulo yenu, ngokuba nginani.

Ubani oyonihlukanisa nothando lwami? Ingozi, noma inkemba, noma indlala iyonihlukanisa yini? Ngithi cha, bhekani nazi ukuthi nivikelekile kimi. Yazini ukuthi nginibeke uphawu lukaBaba, futhi lolo phawu alunakwephulwa noma luhlutshulwe kini, ngakho-ke hlalani kimi, iNkosi yenu, amandla, nomhlengi wenu. Bhekani! Nganithenga ngegazi lami uqobo, nganibiza ngamagama, nganibeka uphawu ngokokukholwa kwenu egameni lami, futhi noma ubani okholwa kulo uyosindiswa ngokukholwa, ngokuba olungileyo uyophila ngokukholwa! Gcinani ithemba lenu liphila, lilinike amandla, nezelezele ukukhuthazela othandweni nasenkolweni yenu, ngokuba nisindiselwe ethembeni. Ithemba elikhulu nokulindela okukhulu kungokwenu, ngokuba ngikushilo.

Kungani nilahle ithemba futhi nidabukile? Bhekani! Nginqobile, futhi masinyane nizoba nami, futhi nizongibona ngamehlo enu, nizokwazi njengoba naziwa.

Bhekani! Isho iNkosi enkulu, iNkosi enamandla eyiNkosi yamakhosi. Bhekani kimi osizini nasezinkingeni zenu. Ngiyazazi izinhliziyo zenu, futhi ngazi kahle ukuthi niyangithanda futhi nilangazelela ukuba nami njengoba nami ngilangazelela ukuba nani; kuphela ningadangali. Ngiphenduleni kulokhu, isikhathi esifushane esisele ekulindeni kwenu â€” singafaniswa yini nephakade nami, makoti wami? Cha, ukuhlupheka kwenu nosizi lwasemhlabeni akufanele neze kuqhathaniswe nenkazimulo engizoyambula kini, ngokuba niyoba njengami, futhi niyongibona njengoba nginjalo, ngakho-ke ningavumeli izinhliziyo zenu zikhathazeke. Ziqondeni izikhathi zenu futhi nihlale nilungile, ngokuba ukuvela kwami kuyokwenzeka ngokuzuma, futhi kuyodlula okulindele.

Bhekani, bantwana bami! Umhlaba uyokwazi ukuthi ngiyanithanda nokuthi ningabami, futhi uSathane uyodalula inkohliso enkulu ukuze akhohlise ababhubhayo, kodwa ngeke isondele kini. Cha, ukwahlulelwa akusona esenu, kanjalo nentukuthelo yami. Qondani, bantwana bami, ukuthi kimi, nilungile, hhayi ngokulunga kwemisebenzi yenu, kodwa ngokulunga enginiyembathise kona. Ningazilahli izingubo enginiyembathise zona, futhi ningawucimi umoya wami. Nakuba kuyizikhathi zokuphela, ngiyothulula umoya wami, amadodana namadodakazi enu ayoprofetha, namakhehla enu ayophupha amaphupho. Kusekhona umsebenzi okufanele wenziwe, futhi isenzo sami sokugcina kulezi zikhathi zomusa siyodlula usuku lwePhentekoste lapho ngaqala ukuthululela khona kwabakhethiweyo bami umoya engawamukela kuBaba. Imisebenzi emikhulu isezandleni zenu futhi kusekhona abaningi abasazokwazi ukusindiswa, ngakho-ke yenzani umsebenzi wami nikhonze abantwana bami nifune izimvu zami ezilahlekile. Celani kimi ngizonithuma, njengoba ngininikile amandla amakhulu futhi ngiphile izipho kubantu. Ubani ongamelana nani, nakuba ninaleli gugu ezitsheni zobumba? Kungani nisangabaza mina? Ebubuthakathakeni benu amandla ami enziwa aphelele kini. Ngakho-ke, chichimani emsebenzini weNkosi, bantwana bami.

Hlolani iZwi lami eliNgcwele, ngokuba njengoba ngavusa abafileyo, nani ningenza okufanayo. Gcobani amehlo enu ngomuthi wamehlo ukuze nibone ukuphulukiswa okuningi okunamandla engakwenza ukuze ngikhulule abathunjwa lapho ngifika kwabami; nani ningenza okufanayo. Angishongo yini ukuthi okholwa yimi, naye uyokwenza yona le misebenzi, nemisebenzi emikhulu kunale uyoyenza ngoba mina sengiye kuBaba wami noYihlo wenu, nakuNkulunkulu wami noNkulunkulu wenu: Nina ningamakhosi nabapristi phambi kwami futhi nihlezi ezulwini, ngakho-ke kungani nisangabaza? Yibani nokukholwa okungangohlamvu lwesinaphi, ngokuba iyimbewu encane kakhulu ekhiqiza isihlahla esikhulu, kanjalo nani niyoba njalo uma niqhubeka niqinile kimi.

Kungani nidida amazwi ami futhi ningayinaki imininingwane enkulumweni yami? Angiqambi manga: ningakwenza lokho enginithume kona ukuba nikufeze uma nihlala nithembekile. Khulumani iZwi lami ngegunya elikhulu nangesibindi. Yishoni kulezo zintaba, Phonselwani olwandle! Amazwi ami ayiqiniso nokuphila, ngakho-ke ningawakholwa amanga abelusi abaphambukayo abathi aninakukwenza lokho enginitshela kona. Thathani isifundo. Nifuna ukuphulukisa abagulayo? Yalelani ukugula kusuke egameni lami, nisho lawo mazwi ngokukholwa kimi nangokukholwa emazwini engizoninika wona. Khulumani ukuba kube njalo, ngokuba ukuphila nokufa kusemandleni olimi; yeka ukuba namandla kwezilimi zenu njengoba nikhuluma amandla kaNkulunkulu futhi niyalela okungenzeki.

Khulumani ngesibindi. Khulumani ngokuzethemba. Khulumani ngokukholwa, ningangabazi lutho; ngokuba nginithumile futhi ngininikile amandla. Kungani nicela umoya ukuba usuke egameni lami ngokwesaba ezindebeni zenu? Yalelani usuke egameni lami ngesibindi sonke nangokuzethemba konke, ngokuba mkhulu lowo okini kunalowo osezweni. Bhekani! Imimoya emibi iyanesaba futhi iyashwabana emazwini enu, ngakho kungani nesaba izinto ezibuthakathaka nezingenamsebenzi esevele zinqotshiwe futhi zilindele umlilo? Ningesabi, kodwa kholwani kuphela!

Hambani niye phambili egameni lami nenze imisebenzi yami emikhulu. Khumbulani uthando lwenu lokuqala futhi niphumule ekuphumuleni engininike kona. Yekani imisebenzi nemithwalo yenu, nithathe ijoka lami phezu kwenu; ngokuba ijoka lami lilula, nomthwalo wami ulula. Yenzani imisebenzi enginilungisele yona nihambe kuyo, ngokuba ngokwenza kanjalo niyosindisa abaningi emalangabini, nezithelo ziyokwandela i-akhawunti yenu. Isikhathi siseduze, makoti wami, nokuphela kohambo lwenu kuseduze, kuseduze kunalokho enikucabangayo. Kodwa ngizweni, bantwana bami; ngizweni, makoti wami! Lamulelani kusesekhona isikhathi. Phulukisani abagulayo iNdodana isakhanya empilweni yenu, ininika amandla ami.

Funani izimvu zami ezilahlekile; zibizeni, ngokuba ziningi ezisazokwazi ukusindiswa kule mizuzwana yokugcina yokuvuselela. Ngokuba masinyane nizofika efeni lenu kimi, futhi nizobona impilo yenu ephelele kimi; ngokuba izimpilo zenu zifihliwe kimi, futhi lapho ngivela, kanjalo nokuphila okuphelele uBaba aninike kona kimi kuzovela. Ngiyanithanda kakhulu, bantwanyana bami, ngakho-ke thandani abalahlekile ngendlela efanayo. Ningabahluleli noma nidelele usizi lwabo, ngokuba banqotshwe uSathane njengoba nani naninjalo; ngakho-ke khumbulani ukukhululwa engakwenza kini nikunike abaswele. Nakuba abaningi beyoniphikisa, ningami kuze kube buthene izimvu zami, ngokuba masinyane kakhulu ukolweni wami uyobuthelwa ngokuphepha esibayeni sami. Labo abaphika ukuvela kwami bayangiphika mina futhi baphika nezwi lami; ngakho-ke ziqiniseni ezwini lami niyeke ukukholwa amasela, ngokuba amasela nawo angabaqambi-manga.

Nginguye owakhuthazela kwaze kwaba sekuphefumuleni kokugcina, elwa nesono ngegazi lami elingenasono.

Nginguye owavuswa ngosuku lwesithathu wenyukela kuBaba.

Nginguye owahamba phakathi kwabafundi nabaphostoli bami izinsuku ezingamashumi amane, wabonwa nangaphezu kwabazalwane abangamakhulu amahlanu ngasikhathi sinye, futhi wabonwa wathintwa ngabaningi, ngokuba umoya awunanyama nagazi.

Nginguye owathathwa, besabuka, wenyukela emafini.

Nginguye okukhanya kwakhe kukhanya ukudlula ilanga, ngokuba ngibonakalisa inkazimulo kaBaba.

Sengizokwambula inkazimulo enkulu kini.

Nginani njalo, kuze kube sekupheleni kwesikhathi.
