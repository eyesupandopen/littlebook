“amadodana kaNkulunkulu amemeza ngenjabulo” — yebo, izingelosi zazikhona ekudalweni, futhi ziyazi izimfihlo eziningi zasezulwini ezingafanele zaziwe ngabantu, ngokuba abantu sebefune iminyaka eminingi ezinkanukweni zabo zamandla nenkazimulo.

Lolu ulwazi lwezingelosi eziwileyo, bantwana bami, anifundanga yini? Noma nisakholwa amanga ama-Edomi abawahlwanyele phakathi kwenu? Kodwa uGenesise uthini ngaleyo ndaba? Kwakukhona imidondoshiya emhlabeni ngalezo zinsuku: kanjalo nangemva kwalokho, lapho amadodana kaNkulunkulu angena emadodakazini abantu, awazalela abantwana; wona aba ngamadoda anamandla, ayengawamandulo, amadoda adumileyo. Baningi benu abaqeda ukubekezela kwami ​​bekholelwa inganekwane eyiphutha yokuthi amadodana kaNkulunkulu ayengakaSeti, futhi ashada namadodakazi amabi abantu futhi azala imidondoshiya ebubini. Isintu besinesono selokhu kwaba nokuwa, futhi kungakhathaliseki ukuthi bangaki abantu ababi abazalanayo, inzalo yabo ngeke ibe yimidondoshiya emikhulu kakhulu. Cha. Leyo yinganekwane ehlose ukukhohlisa, ngokuba ingxenye yempumelelo kaSathane ibangelwa yimfihlo yakhe yokungathembeki, futhi uhluliwe, ngakho impumelelo yakhe yesikhashana iphele ngokwehluleka okukhulu futhi ngeke abaleke echibini lakhe. Ningamesabi, kodwa qondani amazwi eZwi lami kumongo wawo webhayibheli, futhi ningabi ngabangenangqondo nabangazi iqiniso.

Amadodana kaNkulunkulu bangabalindi kulo mongo, izingelosi ezazithunyiwe ukusiza isintu kodwa kunalokho zashiya isikhundla sazo sokuqala, ngokuba zakhanukela amadodakazi abantu. Ngokuba ngamtshelani uJuda ukuba amemezele egameni lami? Futhi izingelosi ezingagcinanga isikhundla sazo sokuqala, kodwa zashiya indawo yazo yokuhlala, uzigcinele emaketangeni aphakade ngaphansi kobumnyama, kuze kube sekwahlulelweni kosuku olukhulu. Phindani nifunde futhi ningabi ngabangakholwayo, kodwa kholwani kuphela. Amadodana kaNkulunkulu yizingelosi eziwile ezazingcolisa nabesifazane; abaningi balaba besifazane bathathwa futhi bahlukunyezwa, nakuba abanye babo bakwenza ngokuzithandela futhi bayothwala isahlulelo sabo.

Inzalo abayivezile ekungcoleni kwabo, yebo, lokhu kuhlangana okungcwele kwaveza imidondoshiya, izidalwa ezimbi ezenzelwe ukubhujiswa kwenu. Nizilimaza kakhulu ngokuthembela kancane ohlwini lwenu lwezincwadi ezingama-66 kuphela. Kukhona izinhla eziningi zezincwadi futhi uhla lwenu lwamaProthestani lwezincwadi ezingama-66 aluqukethe zonke iziprofetho ezake zanikezwa kodwa luqukethe zonke izinto eziphathelene nokuphila nokumesaba uNkulunkulu kimi Kristu Jesu. Fundani izincwadi zika-1 Enoke noJasheri namaJubili. Akuyona imibhalo kini kodwa ngokuqinisekile kufundwa okunconyiwe okusikiselwe futhi okucashunwe emibhalweni kanye eniyithandayo. Ningalawulwa ukucabanga ukuthi yizincwadi ezivunyelwe ohlwini oluthile kuphela ezizo zonke iziprofetho ezake zanikezwa. Fundani kimi.

Nokho, uNkulunkulu walibhubhisa izwe elalakhiwe ngaleso sikhathi, ngokuba uthini umngane wami uPetru: Ngokuba lokhu bayakuziba ngamabomu, ukuthi ngezwi likaNkulunkulu amazulu ayekhona kusukela kudala, nomhlaba umi uphuma emanzini, nasemanzini, Ngalokho izwe langaleso sikhathi, ligcweliswe ngamanzi, labhubha. Nangezinsuku zikaNowa uBaba wathumela uzamcolo omkhulu owasula zonke izinengiso zabo ezimbi, ngokuba benza izinengiso eziningi ezaletha incithakalo enkulu emhlabeni. Baphula uhlelo lwemvelo lokuphila futhi bafaka ukungazinzi ku-DNA uqobo lwesintu, nomuntu wahlanganiswa nesilo nesilo sahlanganiswa nenhlanzi futhi uhlobo olulodwa lwesilo lwahlanganiswa nolunye uhlobo lwesilo kwaze kwatholakala izinengiso ezifiselekayo. Khona-ke izinengiso zabhubhisa isintu. ngakho uBaba wazibhubhisa, wasindisa imiphefumulo eyisishiyagalombili iphila. Fundani amazwi ami niqonde umhlaba wenu, ngokuba abaningi benu bakholwa amanga enkathi yokukhanyiselwa kwamanga engavumanga lutho olungokomoya futhi eyasiza ekudidiseni izixuku ngamanga azo angenakukholwa okuthi into ebonakalayo yasemhlabeni iyona yodwa ebalulekile. Ngezinjongo zethu sizolandela uhlanga lukaHamu, ngokuba kuHamu kwavela izinhlanga zegazi labaqalekisiwe, ngisho uNimrode ovela kwaKushe naseGibhithe noma eMizrayimi, namaKhanani amabi ehla kuKhanani omubi. Babebabi kangaka futhi be pervasive kangangokuthi wakwazi ukuthelela izwe laseKhanani ngezinengiso zakhe ezisanda kwenziwa, lapho uSathane asebenza khona ngenxa yeziphambeko zakhe. Aniwufundanga yini umbiko omubi owanikezwa yizinhloli eziyishumi kweziyishumi nambili ezathunyelwa ezweni laseKhanani nguMose ukuyolihlola? Ngokuba uJoshuwa noKalebi kuphela abakhuluma amazwi okholo, kodwa umbiko omubi owanikezwa wawuyiqiniso elingokoqobo, ngaphandle kokuthi kwakuwumbiko onesono, ongenakukholwa owaphika amazwi kaBaba uvuna lokho abakubona ngamehlo abo, ngokuba uNkulunkulu mkhulu kunamehlo anoma ubani, bantwanyana bami. Fundani umbiko kuDuteronomi: Kodwa amadoda akhuphuka naye, athi, Asikwazi ukukhuphukela kubantu, ngokuba banamandla kunathi. Ngokuba bathini abaHluleli ngale ndaba: Futhi uJehova wayenoJuda, waxosha abakhileyo entabeni, kodwa wehluleka ukuxosha abakhileyo esigodini, ngoba babenezinqola zensimbi.

Futhi kanjalo umbhalo wagcwaliseka futhi izizwe zamaKhanani zisabakhathaza abantwana bakaNkulunkulu kuze kube namuhla. Kodwa isiphetho siyafana njalo: Zibhubhisa ngamabomu izizwe ezizingenisile (uNkulunkulu uzenze ababaleki nezihambi emhlabeni). Bakhulume amanga amakhulu futhi badayisa wona lawo manga njengeqiniso. Banqobe amazwe enu, futhi anibaboni nokubabona, ngokuba niphunjuphutshelwe yizindlela zabo ngoba abaningi benu bayekelele kubo, ngisho nesinagoge likaSathane.

Buyelani eZwini lami elicwebileyo, ningawanaki amazwi nezindlela zabase-Edomi. Yekani ukuphakamisa amazwi ayisono ngaphezu kweZwi likaBaba eliNgcwelengcwele. Ngaleyo ndlela ngamelana nabo kwa-Israyeli eminyakeni engaba ngu-2000 edlule, futhi ukuma kwami kulotshiwe emavangelini ukuze nani nihambe njengoba ngahamba mina. Kusukela kuGenesise 3:15 kube khona impi yenzalo phakathi kukaNkulunkulu noSathane, futhi inzalo kaSathane uqobo ilwela ubukhulu, kodwa ayisoze yabuphatha. Sengilunqobile ukunqoba, bantabami, futhi ngininikile amandla, bantabami, phezu kwazo zonke izinhlobo zobubi, futhi akukho lutho oluyonivimba; ngoba mina, Jesu Kristu, ngiwunqobile umbuso wesitha. Melananani nabo niyeke ukukhuthaza izindlela zabo ezimbi. Phumani phakathi kwabo ngoba bazenze bafana nani ncamashi. Anikholwa yini umfanekiso wami kakolweni nokhula? Waphinda wabeka omunye umfanekiso kubo, ethi; Umbuso wezulu ufana nomuntu owahlwanyela imbewu enhle ensimini yakhe: Kodwa lapho abantu belele, isitha sakhe safika sahlwanyela ukhula phakathi kukakolweni, sahamba. Bantabami, ngininika umlando wenu ngamafuphi, ngoba umlando wesintu ulandeleka kusukela kuNowa kuya ku-Adamu, futhi uNkulunkulu wenza zonke izizwe ngegazi linye. Bantabami, isikhathi sesicishe saphela, kodwa lokho akusho ukuthi umsebenzi wenu usuphelile. Ngisabiza abantabami ukuba bangimele futhi bangikhonze. Ngoba nginenze izikhonzi ezikwaziyo zeTestamente Elisha egazini lami. Anifundanga yini lapho ngitshela abalandeli bami ukuthi uma bethi kule ntaba 'suka uphonswe olwandle', futhi uma bengangabazi, bayoba nezinto abaziyalayo? Ngiyakweluleka ukuba ubuyele emuva ivesi elilodwa, ngoba leyo ndaba iqala ngokuthi 'kholwani kuNkulunkulu' (ngoba kuNkulunkulu akukho okungenzeki) futhi aninakwenza lutho ngaphandle kwami, bancane bami.

Baningi benu abafundiswe ngabase-Edomi amanga aniholela ezindleleni zabo. Kodwa uma ninokholo kuNkulunkulu, niyoyala okungenzeki, futhi kuzodingeka nenze lokho kanye emsebenzini enginithuma kuwo. Ngiyanithuma, bantabami, ukuba nibuthe izimvu eziseleyo, ngoba abaningi bayoza kimi kule mizuzu yokugcina futhi angisoze ngabaphika, kodwa ngisabafuna ngentshiseko. Vumelani imilomo yenu ngokholo ningimele nikhonze abantabami.

Sengiyanitshelile ukuthi niwasebenzisa kanjani amandla ami, kodwa aniyilaleli imiyalo yami, ngakho-ke ngizophinda ngitshele abakhohlwayo phakathi kwenu. Kungani nicela umoya ngokwesaba ukuba ushiye umuntu ngezindebe ezithintithayo nisebenzisa igama lami, uJesu Kristu, ngendlela yokungakholwa? Ningaliphathi ngeze igama lami! Yalani imimoya egameni lami ngokholo olukhulu futhi nikholwe emazwini engizoninika wona, futhi imimoya emibi iyosuka ngamandla ami asebenza ngani!

Yishoni kuleyo ntaba, 'suka uphonswe olwandle', phulukisani abagulayo, khiphani imimoya, vusani abafileyo niyale okungenzeki, ngoba ngininikile amandla okwenza kanjalo.

Anifundanga yini ukuthi abokuqala bayoba ngabokugcina, nabokugcina babe ngabokuqala? Yazini, bantabami, ukuthi ngisebenza ngamandla amakhulu kunalawo engasebenza ngawo ekhulwini lokuqala, futhi sengithulule umoya wami ngokugcwele emhlabeni wonke. Ngubani phakathi kwenu onesibindi sokungimela? Sokukhuluma amazwi engininika wona egameni lami? Ngubani phakathi kwenu onokholo lokungahlehli lapho ebhekene nombiko omubi kodwa abhekane nobubi ngqo? Ngoba mkhulu lowo okini kunalowo osezweni. Ningazithobi kwabaseKhanani, ngoba baqalekiselwe ukuba ngaphansi kwenu. Ngaphezu kwalokho, ningabambeki ezindabeni zozalo lwezizwe, ngoba ngithunyelwe kuzo zonke izizwe ukuze ngisindise abanye, futhi ngokwenza kanjalo, ngisindisa u-Israyeli weqiniso, ngoba uhlakazekile phakathi kwezizwe, futhi nina nonke ningu-Israyeli weqiniso kaNkulunkulu!

Ngimeleni nikhulume egameni lami, ngoba isikhathi sesicishe saphela. Ingabe uyoba ngomunye walabo abasondela esihlalweni sami sokwahlulela bephethe izinkuni, utshani, nezinhlanga ngalolo suku olukhulu olusondelayo? Noma uyonethula kimi igolide, isiliva, namatshe ayigugu, ngoba abantu bami bayingcebo yami. Khululani abathunjiweyo nisebenze nami kule mizuzu yokugcina. Futhi ngaphandle kokuphikisa, inkulu imfihlakalo yokwesaba uNkulunkulu: UNkulunkulu wabonakaliswa enyameni, walungisiswa eMoyeni, wabonwa yiZingelosi, washunyayelwa kwabezizwe, wakholwa emhlabeni, wamukelwa enkazimulweni.

Bantabami, nginguZwi owenziwa inyama, futhi mina noBaba simunye. Ngendlela efanayo, yibani munye nathi ngokungilalela kwenu, ngoba maduzane nizofana nami namukelwe enkazimulweni, ngoba ngiyizithelo zokuqala kwabafileyo, futhi ngizonethula niyibandla elikhazimulayo, elingenabala, nambimbi, nanoma yini enjalo; kodwa ukuba libe ngcwele futhi lingasoleki. Isikhathi sesicishe saphela, ngakho ninganqikazi. Sebenzani nami kule mizuzu yokugcina, futhi ninganqikazi ukuvula imilomo yenu. Ngizobaletha kini, ngakho yenzani imisebenzi yami niyeke eyenu. Ngenzo yezandla zenu, ngiyokwenza imisebenzi eminingi enamandla, kuphela ningangabazi!
