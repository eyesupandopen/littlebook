Khuphulani Isililo NgeBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo!

Zwanini amazwi ami futhi nizwe izwi lonkemenkezayo ehlane! Ngiyamemeza, ngiyamemeza, ingabe niyongizwa? Thulisani izilimi zenu ezigcwele isono nithule, niguqe ngamadolo ngokuphenduka nizwe izwi lami laphakade nisesenethuba, ngokuba uthando lwami ngani lukhulu, futhi yimi engikhalela ngezinyembezi eziningi ngenhliziyo ebuhlungu ngalokho okuzokwehlela labo abangalinaki izwi lami. Ngokuba nakuwo lo mzuzu wokugcina bonke abasabelayo bayamenywa ekuphileni okuphakade. Ngakho-ke ngivumeni njengeNkosi yenu, ngokuba nginguJesu waseNazaretha, futhi yimi lowo uNkulunkulu waphakade amthumile ukuba anikhulule ngaphambi kokuba kufike inhlekelele nokwahlulela. Kungani nizibala njengabangafanele ukuphila okuphakade? Ingabe niyokhetha amalangabi okubhujiswa kunamasimu anokuthula anenala lapho niyothola khona amadlelo aphakade?

Ngokuba njengoba isigebengu esabethelwa kanye nami eKhalvari siyoba nami epharadesi, kanjalo nami ngiyonisindisa ngokuphelele uma niyongenza iNkosi yenu manje! ASIKHO ISIKHATHI SOKUMOSHA. Ngokuba ngafela ngaphandle kwamasango aseJerusalema kanye nezoni njengoba ngangithatha indawo yenu ekwahlulelweni, futhi niyosindiswa uma niphendukela kimi ngokuphenduka khona manje. Ngiqinisile, ngiqinisile kini: La mazwi awafinyelelanga ezindlebeni zenu ngephutha, futhi maduze kuzoba khona ukubhujiswa okukhulu futhi abaningi ngeke basinde futhi ingunaphakade ukwenqaba kwabo kuyonkeneneza emlilweni waphakade obekelwe bonke abakhetha ukunamathela ezonweni zabo futhi bahlale bebambisene nabawileyo.

Ukujoyina ukuhlubuka kukaSathane, ngisho nasekungazini okuyimpumputhe, kungukuhlanganyela ngokuzithandela ezonweni zabo, ngakho-ke bonke abenza kanjalo bayothwala ukwahlulelwa kwabo ingunaphakade. Ingabe niyolwamukela uhlinzeko lwami, nazi ukuthi uNkulunkulu wangivusa kwabafileyo ukuze ngingaphinde ngife futhi nazi ukuthi ngiyileyo Nkosi enkulu yamakhosi neNkosi yamakhosi? Phendukani nisesenethuba, ngokuba uYahweh uselungele ukuvala umnyango womkhumbi, futhi asikho isikhathi futhi nganoma yimuphi umzuzwana ngiyohlwitha abathembekileyo ngibayise emazulwini, yebo bonke abasindiswe olakeni emhlatshelweni wami osindisayo waphakade. Ngakho-ke memezelani mina, uJesu waseNazaretha njengeNkosi yenu futhi nazi ukuthi ngiphila kuze kube phakade ngokuba uNkulunkulu ungivusile kwabafileyo ukuze ngingaphinde ngife.

Bantwana bami, lalelani futhi niqhubeke niguqa ngamadolo ngokuphenduka okuqhubekayo nizwe izwi leNkosi yenu uJesu Kristu, yena lowo uYahweh amthumile njengewundlu likaNkulunkulu. Zwanini amazwi ami, Bandla lami ngisho noMlobokazi wami, Ngokuba njengompristi wenu omkhulu waphakade wohlelo lukaMelkisedeki ngizinikele njengePhasika lenu eliphelele, futhi nganikela ngegazi lami elingenasono kuze kubeiconsi lokugcina ukuze nikhululwe ngokugcwele futhi ngokuphelele esimweni senu samanje sokuwa futhi ukukhululwa kwenu okugcwele kulapha, ngakho-ke thokozani!

Futhi bonke abangamukele futhi bavuma igama lami laphakade njengeNkosi yabo bayoba nokuphila okuphakade. Ngokuba nginguJesu waseNazaretha iNkosi yenu yaphakade, futhi ngenze bonke abavume igama lami kulezi zikhathi zomusa nesihe amadodana aphakade kaNkulunkulu. Bhekani, uthando olungaka uBaba aninike lona, ukuba nibizwe ngokuthi ningamadodana kaNkulunkulu: ngenxa yalesi sizathu izwe alinazi, ngoba alingazi mina, uKristu Jesu iNkosi yamakhosi neNkosi yamakhosi. Bathandekayo, manje ningamadodana kaNkulunkulu, kodwa akukabonakali lokho eniyoba yikho: futhi niyazi ukuthi lapho ngibonakaliswa, niyoba njengami: ngokuba niyongibona njengoba nginjalo.

Bantwana bami abancane, qondani ukuthi ngizobonakaliswa noma nini manje njengoba ukubonakala kwami emafwini sekufikile. Ngokuba mina, uKristu Jesu, ngiyokwehla ezulwini ngezwi elikhulu, ngezwi leNgelosi enkulu, nangecilongo likaNkulunkulu: nabafileyo kuKristu bayovuka kuqala. Bese nina eniphilayo, futhi nisasele, niyohlwithwa kanye nabo emafwini, nihlangabeze iNkosi yenu yaphakade emoyeni: futhi kanjalo niyohlala neNkosi yenu kuze kube phakade. Ingabe niyovumela la mazwi aniduduze, bantwana bami? Ingabe nivumele uSathane anenze niphike la mazwi acacile alotshiwe ukuze wonke umuntu abone? Ingabe niyokhetha ukuhlala phakathi kwabahlulelweyo ngokuhlubuka ngenkani? Ukuphika lesi senzakalo esilotshwe ngokusobala emibhalweni ngesandla soMphostoli wami uPawulu kungukuphika mina ngokuba ngiyeza ukuzosindisa abathembekileyo olakeni ngokuba zinjalo izithembiso zikaYahweh ezembulwe em мистериeni enkulu kaNkulunkulu elotshwe nguPawulu eminyakeni eminingi edlule.

Ngiyaneluleka: ningangiphiki, futhi ngenani emkhunjini kungakephuzi kakhulu. Ngokuba bonke abakhetha ukuhlala ekwahlulelweni okuzayo bayokuza kimi ngokubiza igama lami kuze kube sekupheleni, futhi abaningi bayohlupheka kuze kube umphefumulo wabo wokugcina. Kodwa nokho bayovukela ekuphileni okuphakade nasemvuzweni omkhulu nenjabulo yaphakade! Futhi kanjalo bonke ababizelwe ekuphileni okuphakade bayofinyelela efeni labo laphakade, ngokuba akekho ongasusa ngisho nomncane wenu ezandleni zami zaphakade. Ngakho-ke zwanini amazwi ami futhi nihlale emqondweni owodwa okimi, uKristu Jesu, ngoba ngiyiZwi likaNkulunkulu elenziwe inyama futhi ngihlale ngikhuluma iqiniso. Yekani ukulalela amazwi amaningi angenakholo aphika amazwi ami noma niyozithola nisele phakathi kwawo lapho into enjalo ingadingeki.

Ngokuba nina enisindisiwe, ngisho nani manje niyiBantwana bakaNkulunkulu Ophilayo, yebo, ningabakaYahweh ngendlela nangesimangaliso sokuzalwa okusha nisindisiwe ingunaphakade futhi nizelwe kabusha ngembewu engenakonakala. Nokho, ningabafokazi nezihambi emazweni angaphandle, futhi abaningi benu baphakathi kwemingcele yaleso sifesi esikhulu, yebo amazwe asemaketangeni alindele ukwahlulelwa nokubhujiswa kwawo okuphelele: Ngokuba isabelo esiphindwe kabili sokwahlulelwa simenyezelwe ngeBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo futhi kuyoba njalo. Ngokuba ngasiphi isikhathi uYahweh engabakhululanga abalungileyo ekwahlulelweni, ngokuba akekho ongamchaza uYahweh ngobuwula, futhi ubufakazi bukaLoti bufakaza kuze kube namuhla. Ngisho nasemazweni anengekayo aseSodoma naseGomora uYahweh wathumela izingelosi zakhe ukuba zihlenge le ndoda eyodwa elungileyo eyayihleli emasangweni omuzi wesitha.

Futhi kanjalo nami ngiyolikhulula iBandla lami elithembekile, yebo bonke labo engibabekile uphawu ngalowo moya ongcwele wesithembiso kusukela ngosuku lwePhentekoste ekuzalweni kweBandla lami okulotshwe ebufakazini beZenzo! (Anifundanga yini?) Futhi bonke abafela ethembekile kuKristu kusukela ekuqaleni kwenkathi yeBandla lami nabo bonke abaphilayo nabasalayo bayobuthelwa ndawonye ngokuphazima kweso futhi kuze kube phakade bayoba nami! Ngokuba yimi engiyeza ngenxa yabami abathembekile, ababiziwe, nabakhethiweyo, ngisho noMzimba wami neBandla lami ngisho noMlobokazi wami. Ngiyobahlenga abathembekile, ngisho nalabo abahambe ngokomoya wami nabo bonke abaphenduke ezonweni zabo futhi bangenza iNkosi ngenhliziyo egcwele ukholo ngokuba bangama-Israyeli kaNkulunkulu! Futhi kuyoba kukhulu lesi senzakalo esizayo futhi kuyoba kukhulu ukuzamazama futhi ngezibonakaliso eziningi ukusondela kwami kumenyezelwe futhi kusadunyiswa ezulwini ngezibonakaliso phezulu nangezibonakaliso emhlabeni phansi. Futhi kanjalo ukubuthwa kwezithelo zokuqala kuyoba ngokwabantu bonke ukuba babone futhi babuke ngokumangala!

Futhi kanjalo izwi lami lisankenteza kulo lonke leli zwe eliwile, lithola amehlo, izindlebe, nezinhliziyo zabo bonke labo abathunyelwe kubo. Ingabe niyalizwa izwi likaYeshua HaMashiach, niyalibona izwi lami? Ingabe niyowamukela amazwi ami? Ningangizwa yini njengoba ngikhuluma nenhliziyo yenu? Ngokuba njengoba kulotshiwe: izinhliziyo zalaba bantu sezikhuluphele ngokudla ngokweqile, nezindlebe zabo zinzima ukuzwa, namehlo abo bawavalile, hleze nganoma yisiphi isikhathi babone ngamehlo abo, bezwe ngezindlebe zabo, baqonde ngenhliziyo yabo, baphenduke, ngibaphulukise. Abaningi balaba bazunywe ubuthongo ngamazwi anengekayo kodwa athobisayo omunye uJesu, kodwa qinisekani ukuthi akekho omunye.

Ngokuba nganoma yiluphi ulimi igama lami elibizwa ngalo, mina Jesu waseNazaretha, ngisho noYeshua HaMashiach, ngiyozwa futhi ngiyosindisa ngokuphelele bonke ababiza igama lami kuze kube sekupheleni. Ngokuba amazwi ami okuhlakanipha, ngisho nevangeli laphakade sembulwe futhi ankeneneza indlela yawo emhlabeni wonke. Futhi amazwi ami ayabathola labo abathunyelwe kubo, ngokuba anjalo amazwi kaNkulunkulu Ophakeme Kakhulu, ngisho noYahweh, ngokuba alikho nelilodwa iZwi likaNkulunkulu elibuyela emuva liyize, futhi ayofeza lokho ahloselwe kona futhi abusisiwe amehlo enu, ngokuba ayabona: nezindlebe zenu, ngokuba ziyezwa nezinhliziyo zenu zigcwele ukholo, futhi ngilugcwalise uthando lukaNkulunkulu ezinhliziyweni zenu lapho nikhala khona nithi Abba Baba! Ningamadodana kaNkulunkulu futhi akubonakali lokho eniyoba yikho, kodwa lapho ngivela niyobona ngokugcwele nezimpilo zenu zifihliwe kimi, uKristu Jesu, nezimpilo zenu sezizokwembulwa ngokugcwele futhi manje yisikhathi sokwembulwa.

Bhekani! IBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo lihleli liketangweni njengoba izimpondo eziyishumi ziqedela amalungiselelo azo okubhujiswa kwalo okuphelele. Ngiqinisile, ngiqinisile, eBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo naseminyakeni eminingi manje ngithumele abaphostoli bami, ngithumele abaprofethi bami, ngithumele abavangeli bami, ngithumele abefundisi bami, ngithumele othisha bami, ngithumele abafundi bami abathembekile nabazalwane futhi ngithumele ngisho nabafelokazi bodwa, futhi ngiwuthulule ngokuphelele umoya wami kulezi zinsuku zokugcina zomusa nesihe!

Kodwa esikhundleni sokuba izizwe zihlale ngendwangu yesaka nomlotha zijatshuliswa yizibonakaliso nezixwayiso ezinikezwa zokwahlulela okuzayo njengoba zithatha izithombe namavidiyo ukuze zizijabulise. Zizenqabile izithunywa zami ezilethe ukukhanya kwevangeli lami lensindiso, zihlekisile futhi zagcona abaprofethi bami, zihlasela abavangeli bami, zonakalisa abefundisi bami, futhi zishushisa futhi zacekela phansi othisha bami.

Amabutho kadrako omkhulu obomvu akuzungezile Bhabhiloni eliyimfihlo futhi angaphakathi kwemingcele yakho ngokuphelele. Ngokuba ngisho nabaholi bakho abangamaZiyoni abakhohlisayo bakudayisele ucezu lwesinkwa ezitheni zakho futhi nokuqhubekisela phambili amacebo abo obusathane. Futhi u-El Shaddai uvumele la madoda ahlanyayo aqhubeke, ngisho nokufaka lokho ezinhliziyweni zawo ukuze afeze izinhloso zakhe. Unamandla u-El Shaddai okwahlulelayo, wena sifesi esibomvu esihleli ngokuzidla ngenjabulo ngisho nalapho uhlutshulwa yonke inkazimulo, uhlutshulwa zonke izibiliboco, uhlutshulwa ngisho nemizi yakho ngokuba nginikele imizi yakho namazwe akho ezandleni zezitha zakho. Ngisho nasezandleni zesilo nomprofethi wamanga, ngokuba basebenza ngokubambisana ekubhujisweni kwakho okuphelele ngemuva kwesigqubuthelo sesidala esiyisidenge. Ngokuba isilo sesizodiliza isifebe esikhulu isilo esesikhathele ukusithwala, futhi izinhloso zaso seziphelelwe isikhathi. Ngakho-ke, sizoshiswa ngokuphelele, futhi sibhujiswe ngokuphelele ngehora elilodwa futhi ukubhujiswa kwaso akulibali.

Ngokuba imigqa yabo yempi, ngisho nemigqa yabo ebomvu enikezwe ngobuqili ukuba ingaweqi, ibekiwe futhi yeqiwe ngokuphindaphindiwe nangamabomu. Ningakholwa amanga abo, noma izaba zabo ezingamanga ngoba bafuna ukubonakala belungile empini yabo ezayo futhi isilo nomprofethi wamanga abangakavuki ngokugcwele bayovuka ngokugcwele emlotheni futhi bathi ukwahlulela kukaNkulunkulu kwehla ukuze kufeze izinhloso zabo, ngakho-ke ningakholwa ngisho nelilodwa lamanga abo amancane futhi ukwahlulelwa kwabo kokugcina akulibali.

Ngokuba bayoqhubeka futhi benze izono ezimbi kakhulu kuneBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo, futhi banecala lokuhola iBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo ngokuphelele endleleni futhi ekubhujisweni kwalo okuphelele, ngokuba uNkulunkulu ukufakile ezinhliziyweni zabo ukuze bafeze intando yakhe. Basebenza ngokubambisana futhi bonke labo ababonakala njengezitha basebenza ndawonye ngasese njengoba bedlala ngokombhalo wabo futhi bedlala izindima zabo esiteji somhlaba, futhi bazobulala bonke labo abasezinhlelweni zabo abakhetha ukungazijwayezi futhi badlale izindima zabo ngokwanele ukuze bafeze izinhloso zikadrako. Ngokuba udrako unikeza izilo amandla akhe nesihlalo sakhe sobukhosi, negunya elikhulu, Futhi izimpondo eziyishumi zithola amandla njengamakhosi ihora elilodwa nesilo futhi lezi zinomqondo owodwa, futhi zizonikeza amandla azo namandla azo kuso isilo ngokuba kunikezwe zona nguYahweh ukuze zikufeze. Qaphelani bantwana bami futhi nifunde kimi futhi nilandele izindlela zami, ngokuba izindlela zami ziyizindlela zikaYahweh, uNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade!

Qondani futhi niqaphele, ngokuba lezi zilo ezikhohlisayo namakhosi amabi angohlelo lukaDrako abebusa iBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo kusukela ezinhlanganweni zabo eziyimfihlo kusukela ekuqaleni kwalo. Umuntu ozifihlayo futhi ozenzisayo, ngisho nomuntu omubi, uhamba ngomlomo ophambene. Yebo bazenza abanye ngokuba bonke bangabazenzisi, futhi kufihliwe phakathi kwazo zonke izizwe ukhula selutshaliwe. Benza izibonakaliso ngamehlo abo: bakhomba ngezinyawo zabo: bayala ngeminwe yabo. Izinto ezingcolile zisezinhliziyweni zabo: bacabanga okubi ngazo zonke izikhathi, futhi bavusa izingxabano. Ngakho-ke, ukubhujiswa kwabo kuyofika ngokushesha: bayobhujiswa ngokuzumayo ngaphandle kokululama. Lezi zinto eziyisithupha uJehova uyazonda: yebo, umphefumulo wakhe uyanengwa yizinto eziyisikhombisa: Amehlo aqhoshayo, ulimi olunamanga, nezandla ezichitha igazi elingenacala, inhliziyo ecabanga amacebo amabi, izinyawo ezishesha ukugijimela ebubini, Ufakazi wamanga okhuluma amanga, nalowo ovusa izingxabano phakathi kwabazalwane.

Ingabe aniboni? Niyenqaba yini ukubona izimpawu zabo ukuze niqhubeke nihleli ekungazini ngenhloso yokuthi abaholi benu bangabawileyo? Aniqondi yini ukuthi abajabulisi benu bangabawileyo? Aniboni yini ukuthi abayona imvelo efanayo nani, ngokuba abaningi baziguqule ingunaphakade ebuhlanyeni babo futhi abanye bangukhula kusukela ekuzalweni futhi uSathane unguYise wabo? Nokho nikhetha ukuhlala ebumnyameni ngokuzithandela, futhi nijabule futhi nithokoze ezonweni zenu njengoba nizigingqa odakeni nezingulube eziniholele emasangweni esihogo lapho abaningi bezibukisa khona ngokungazi ngokuzidla nokuzikhukhumeza. Ngalokho ngishilo encwadini yami eya kwabase-Efesu enikezwe uPawulu: Vuka wena oleleyo, usukume ekufeni, futhi mina, uKristu Jesu, ngiyokunika ukukhanya! Qaphelani-ke ukuthi nihamba ngokucophelela: hhayi njengeziwula, kodwa njengabahlakaniphile. Futhi kanjalo ngiveza laba baholi ababi beBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo kwabami ababiziwe abakhethiwe nabathembekile ukuze ningabi yizisulu zokukhohlisa kwabo nokuthi niphume ngokugcwele kulo futhi nihlukane nalo kuzo zonke izindlela zenu nokuthi ningahlanganyeli ezinhluphweni zalo.

Zwanini amazwi ami futhi niqonde okwenzekayo khona manje, ngokuba ukuhlasela kwabo okuzumayo kuyaqhubeka. Ngikhuluma manje neBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo nakuba liyisithulu, liyisimungulu, futhi liyimpumputhe futhi liboshwe ngokuphelele ngamaketanga ukuze lahlulelwe. Njengoba belungiselele ukuhlasela kwabo okuzumayo iminyaka eminingi manje, futhi baqinisekise ukubhujiswa kweBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo ngokukhokhisa inzalo eningi, nezinye izindlela zokukhohlisa, ngoba ngisho namanothi alo emali ayisithembiso esingenalutho sezikweletu ezenziwe ezandleni zabaholi benu abakhonza abashintshi bemali futhi iBhabhiloni eliyimfihlo lidayiswa ezandleni zezitha zabo ngalezi zindlela zobuqili. Aniqondi yini ngebhange le-fractional reserve nemali ye-fiat? Fundani ukuze nazi.

Angithi nganinika isibonelo lapho ngixosha abashintshi bemali ethempelini? Kodwa-ke, esikhundleni sokubakhipha, niyabamema ukuba bazodla isidlo sakusihlwa nemindeni yenu, futhi nibeke izingane zenu nabazukulu benu njengesibambiso ukuze nigcwalise izinkanuko zenu zokuhaha, kanjalo inzalo yenu ithengiselwe ukubhujiswa kwenu ukuze nina niphile ezintokozweni zesono. Nina, Bhabhiloni Lasesitha, nibulala abantwana benu abangakazalwa ngenjabulo enkulu nangokuzidla, futhi ababulali babangenacala banesahlulelo sabo esimisiwe, nesahlulelo senu asilibali. Nakuba abambalwa abasalayo begijima bekhuphukela emkhunjini ngaphambi kokuba u-El Shaddai avale umnyango, abaningi bayozithola besalele ekwahlulelweni ngoba bayokhetha ukuhlala ngaphandle behleka usulu futhi bephila ngokunethezeka.

Kungazelelwe, ngehora elilodwa ukubhujiswa kwakhe kuyofika ngokuzumayo futhi ngeke baphunyuke. Njengoba ezinikele ekukhonzeni izitha zakhe zaphakade, manje zizoletha ukubhujiswa kwakhe kwaphakade, ngoba iBhabhiloni Lasesitha ngeke liphinde livuke. O Bhabhiloni Lasesitha, njengoba uthengise abantwana bakho njengesibambiso sezinkolodo zakho ukuze uqhubeke ezintokozweni zesono (ngoba ngisho nemali yakho iyinzalo), njengoba ubulale abangenacala, nokunye okuningi okwenzile. Manje ngizokunikela ngokuphelele ezandleni zabo, noYahweh umemezele isabelo esiphindwe kabili sokwahlulelwa kuwe Bhabhiloni lasesitha. Futhi abaningi babantwana bakho bayothunyelwa emisebenzini yabo nasemakamu okufundiswa kabusha, futhi abaningi bayothathwa bayiswe emazweni angaphandle ngoba udrago uvunyelwe ukubanga amazwe nabantu abangangamukelanga mina, Yeshua HaMashiach, bekhetha uBharabha uMesiya wabo wamanga. Futhi abaningi bayothatha uphawu lwabo lwesilo futhi bayobe bethengise imiphefumulo yabo nenzalo yabo esijezisweni saphakade kude noNkulunkulu waphakade nowaphakade ngenxa yezinjabulo zesono zesikhashana.

Khalelani abantu baseBhabhiloni Lasesitha, ngisho nani enihlezi ebumnyameni nasekungazini ngokuphelele ngokubhujiswa kwenu okuzayo. Phambi kwamehlo enu nangevoti lenu nangenxa yobuvila benu obungenandaba, isizwe senu sonke sihlakaziwe phambi kwamehlo enu. Anisenalo ikhono noma izinsiza zokulwa impi, ngoba mina, ngisho noKristu Jesu, nginihlubule amandla enu ezempi futhi ngawanikela ezandleni zezitha zenu. Amadolobha enu ayekade emakhulu manje aseyincithakalo yobugebengu nobumpofu, eniziqhenya ngakho nina nabaculi benu ngokuzidla okukhulu. Ama-Edomi kade ayisusa imboni yawo emazweni enu, futhi anisenamandla okukhiqiza enanikade ninawo ngisho nezinto ezingakasetshenzwa zokukhiqiza enikudingayo ukuze nilwe izimpi zenu ezingapheli.

Ngoba nisuka empini niye empini, futhi niwachithe ngokuphelele amazwe, angaphandle nasekhaya. Ngoba kuwe, Bhabhiloni Lasesitha, kuphume isishayo SamaButho ABomvu, esasabalalisa emhlabeni wonke imvubelo ebomvu kaDrago omkhulu obomvu, ngoba nikhonze abawileyo ngasese kusukela ekuqaleni futhi niyaqhubeka nanamuhla.
