Ubufakazi Obumbaxa: Israyeli
Okubabayo Okumnandi
Novemba 23, 2024

IThempeli Lika-Esawu

Lalelani, lalelani! O' Israyeli izwi likaYeshua HaMashiach, iNdodana ezelwe yodwa kaYahweh, uMINA OMKHULU! Ukuze nginixwayise ngezinto ezizayo, futhi ukuze niqapheliswe kabanzi ngogibe uBaraba azolubeka ukuze ningabanjwa kanye nabangakholwa; izinto ezabhalwa ngokwengxenye ngaphambili eVangelini Laphakade. Nokho ngiza ukuzonitshela ngogibe isicabucabu esilubeka ezweni lesithembiso nokulenza licace ukuze ningabhujiswa amacebo aso. Futhi ngifisa ukwenza izindlela zenu zicace futhi nginigcine ezindleleni zokulunga, zona kanye izindlela zikaYahweh, ozindlela zakhe ziphila ngokuphelele kuYeshua HaMashiach uMesiya wenu weqiniso ohlala elalela intando kaYahweh ngokuphelele. Futhi ukuze nigcine unyawo lwenu kuleyo ndlela eqondile nemincane, lapho ngiyogcina khona izinyawo zenu ziqinile uma niqhubeka nokungilandela ngokholo olulalelayo. Ngoba olungileyo uyophila ngokholo!

Ngakho-ke qondani ukuthi isikhathi sesifikile, njengoba kumemezele futhi kwamenyezelwa lapho ngiyobutha khona ngokuzuma abathembekileyo kuKristu ngibayise emazulwini kungakhathaliseki ukuthi bayaphila noma bafile; futhi njengoba abangcwele abathembekile bethathwa ngokuzuma, incithakalo ezumayo iyofikela iBhabhiloni Eyimfihlo ehleli phezu kwamanzi amaningi, angabantu, nezixuku, nezizwe, nezilimi. Futhi njengoba umlotha wempi ubhebhetheka futhi uhlelo lwamanje lwezizwe lucekeleka phansi futhi luhlelwa kabusha ukuze luvumelane nezinjongo zazo, uhlelo lokugcina lwesilo salowo owileyo luyovuka ngokugcwele.

Nokho, isilo sasolwandle nesilo sasemhlabeni badlale indima yabelusi abalungileyo abeza ukuzokwelusa izimvu ezilahlekile zeBhabhiloni Eyimfihlo ngezithembiso eziningi eziyize; zona kanye izithembiso ezingamanga ekugcineni eziletha ukufa nembubhiso. Futhi bazenza izitha ngemuva kwesifihla-buso sesidlova esidala esidungekile abacasha ngaso nesidlova esikhona ukuze sithwale icala lobugebengu babo; futhi balawula konke ukunyakaza kwaso futhi babhale wonke amazwi aso. Ngoba le nkohliso enkulu inikezwa njengesahlulelo phezu kweBhabhiloni Eyimfihlo; ukuze inkohliso enamandla iyithathe ngokugcwele ngoba imboze umhlaba ngamanga ayo inenkomishi yegolide esandleni sayo, igcwele amanyala nokungcola kobufebe bayo.

Nokho lezi zilo ezimbili ezimbi ziphange iBhabhiloni Eyimfihlo, nangaphambi kokuba kufike ukubhujiswa kwayo, futhi zithathe amandla ayo ezempi nengcebo yayo ngezinjongo zikadrako ohlelweni lwazo lokugcina lwesilo oluzayo. Ngoba impi ezayo iyogwinya iBhabhiloni Eyimfihlo, futhi ngempela iyobhujiswa ngehora elilodwa, ngoba kumemezele kanjalo uYahweh, uMINA OMKHULU. Nokho, lokhu kuyingxenye yecebo futhi kuhleliwe ohlelweni lukaYahweh lokuhlenga (ukuthi izitha zakhe zilalele izwi lakhe) ngakho-ke isahlulelo seSifebe esikhulu simenyezelwe, futhi sabhalwa, futhi kuyoba kanjalo kuze kube phakade, futhi asisayikuphinde sivuke. Noma kunjalo, bayohlukanisa umhlaba futhi kuzoba nokuzamazama okukhulu, futhi baklame ukubhujiswa kwaso ukuze babuthe imithombo abayikhanukayo engcwatshwe emhlabathini. Yona kanye imithombo abayidingayo ukuze banikeze amandla futhi bandise ubuchwepheshe balowo owileyo; lobo kanye ubuchwepheshe umhlaba okhangwe futhi oklonyeliswe yibo manje ekukhohlisweni kwawo okunamandla, ugcinwe uthunjiwe emisinga yabo nasekuqondisweni kwabo lapho ukhuthazwa futhi ulawulwa khona.

Futhi kubantu abayosala ngemva kwencithakalo ezayo yeBhabhiloni Eyimfihlo; isilo sasemhlabeni nesilo sasolwandle bangenise ibutho labahlaseli nababulali emingceleni yayo abamanani abo anjengesifo esikhulu sezinkumbi, futhi bayokhululwa ngophawu lwabo! Ngoba lawa amabutho esilo abuthelwe ukubhujiswa kweBhabhiloni Eyimfihlo. Abaningi balaba bavela emazweni kadrako nasemazweni ebhere, futhi bayokwenza njengoba iBhabhiloni Eyimfihlo yenza kubo ngasese emijubili edlule: Bayobulala yonke indoda, owesifazane, nengane ngaphandle kokuzisola ongafaneli izinjongo zabo futhi abaningi abavunyelwe ukuhlala bephila bayophoqelelwa emakamu abo ezigqila ukuba basebenze ezimayini zabo kuze kube sekufeni kwabo. Futhi bayokhandleka futhi basebenze kuze kube sekuphefumuleni kwabo kokugcina; ngoba konke iBhabhiloni Eyimfihlo ekwenze ngasese kwabanye manje sekuyokwenziwa kuyo obala njengesahlulelo nesijeziso. Ngoba iBhabhiloni Eyimfihlo idakwe yigazi labangcwele, negazi labafelukholo bakaJesu ebafelise ngokobuningi obukhulu emazweni angaphandle; kukho konke lokho izishaya isifuba ngokuthi nayo ikhonza uJesu waseNazaretha. Kodwa eqinisweni ikhonza abawileyo futhi izinjongo zayo manje seziphelile, futhi bakhanukela ukubhujiswa kwayo ukuze bamboze ubugebengu babo futhi ukuze abelusi bayo abanonwayo babonakale belungile lapho bevuka ukuze bafihle ugibe lwabo.

Nokho, abasoze bazifihla kuYahweh, noma kuYeshua HaMashiach futhi ubugebengu babo bulotshwe ngokuphelele emazulwini futhi zonke izono zilotshwe ngemininingwane ephelele kanye nemicabango nezinhloso zezinhliziyo zabo ezingcolile. Futhi isilo sasolwandle nesilo sasemhlabeni bangamanyala amabi adalelwe ukubhujiswa kwesintu. Lezi zilo ezimbi zingabahlukumezi bezingane, futhi ziqoqa futhi zisebenzise izidumbu zabancane ezizibulele ukwenza amanyala azo ngakho konke ezikubhubhisayo kunikelwa njengomhlatshelo kunkulunkulu wazo. Ngoba bathatha iThabenekeli likaMoloki, nenkanyezi kankulunkulu wabo uRemfani, izifanekiso abazenzela zona, ukuze bazikhonze, nokunye okuningi kakhulu abakwenzile okuyokwambulwa ngokugcwele ngezikhathi ezifanele zokwambula ngoba yonke imfihlo iyovela obala.

Ngakho kuyoba ukuthi isilo sasemhlabeni siyobonakala sivuka njengokungathi kwabafileyo; size sisho ukuthi sivusiwe kwabafileyo, khona kanye enhliziyweni yomhlaba ngoba ukufa akukwazanga ukusibamba! Futhi siyoba nokuvuka kwaso kwamanga futhi silethe ukuthula kwamanga emhlabeni ukuqeda impi yaso, futhi siyovela sengathi size ukuzosindisa isintu. Futhi abawileyo bayozembula esintwini njengabasizi abanomusa abaphakeme kakhulu ngokuhlakanipha futhi abathuthukile ezindleleni zobuchwepheshe, ezokwelapha, nokuthula.

Bayoveza izibonakaliso nezimangaliso eziningi zamanga, futhi bayothi labo ababuthelwe kuKristu embuthanweni omkhulu futhi bathathwa baya ekuphepheni kwamazulu ngaphambi kokuba kwehle inhlekelele enkulu; ukuthi nalabo bathathwe ngendluzula yisitha esizishaya esingesakwamanye amaplanethi. Sona kanye isitha esingumfokazi esinobutha esiyobuya ukuzobhubhisa isintu, futhi bayohlanganyela umuntu wemvelo ukuba ahlangane nabo ukuze azisindise kulobu bubi ababucabangayo endaweni yabo yonke yokuzenzisa yamhlaba yokuzenzisa ejikelezayo njalo eze elandayo lobuphuma-ze.

Kuyimfundiso yamanga yezinkanyezi abawileyo abayicabanga futhi bayisebenzisa ngaphambili enkathini yabo yegolide yasendulo, leyo nkathi yegolide eyaphela ezandleni zikaYahweh ngozamcolo omkhulu ezinsukwini zikaNowa, okuyimibhalo yakhona eqoshwe emibhalweni engcwele yasendulo. Futhi amanga ayo ayophonswa phansi ngoba inkohliso yabo enkulu iyokwambulwa ngokugcwele ukuze bonke babone laba bazenzisi abazenzisayo ukuthi bangobani ngempela ngoba bayizidalwa eziwileyo ezivela endaweni yasezulwini esezide zazonakalisa ekuvukeleni okungenanjongo ekulungeni kukaYahweh ukuze zizuze ifa lokubhujiswa kwazo ezizenzele lona efeni lazo lesibabule esivuthayo ezingasoze zabaleka kulo.

Ngakho-ke isilo sasemhlabeni siyovuka kubonakale sengathi sivela kwabafileyo futhi ngezibonakaliso nezimangaliso eziningi zamanga siyovela, siphethe igatsha laso lomnqumo esandleni. Futhi siyovuka njengewundlu elinezimpondo ezimbili njengoba livuka ngamandla agcwele negunya likadrako kanye nesilo sasolwandle, sona kanye esikhethwe ngudrako nesilo sasemhlabeni ukuze kubuse izizwe ukuze sibuse ulwandle lwezizwe ngokwemithetho yabo yaseNowa ehlambalazayo neqothula uhlanga. Nokho izifundazwe zaso ezimbili azisoze zaba yisixazululo ngoba yimina engiyohlanganisa u-Israyeli kuze kube phakade ukuze angaphinde ahlukaniswe abe imibuso emibili.

Ngakho-ke bayosebenzisa izimiso zabo ezimbi nemiphumela equkethwe emthethweni kaNowa ukulwa nabangcwele nokubanqoba, ngaphandle kwanoma ngubani obiza igama leNkosi kuze kube sekupheleni uyosindiswa, nalabo ababiza uJesu waseNazaretha njengeNkosi yabo esindisayo eyobasindisa ngokuphelele uma bengapheli amandla osizini lwabo. Ngakho-ke isilo sasolwandle siyokhululwa phezu kwalokho okusele kwezizwe abayobe bezibhubhisile, nomlotha wazo owawuyizizwe abake bazibusa ngenkohliso enkulu. Futhi bayobonakala bevuka njengefeniksi enkulu emlotheni nasemalangabini empi esabhebhetheka ngenkohliso yabo. Futhi kuyoba yimpi enkulu, yona kanye enkulu kunazo zonke izimpi ezintathu ezinjalo abazibhebhethekisile nabazilawulile; ukuze udrako avuke ngokugcwele ngesikhathi sakhe nezilo zakhe ezimbi, ezinengekayo ayoba nokubusa kwakhe okufushane ngazo, angaphinde abuse.

Ngoba imfihlakalo yobubi ibisebenza kuyo yonke iminyaka; lapho isifebe esikhulu esihleli phezu kwezintaba eziyisikhombisa, sona kanye leso sifebe esikhulu esibomvu sokuhlambalaza esikhonza indlovukazi yezulu nesifundisa omunye uJesu njengengxenye kathathu wabo wobuqaba. Lezo zimpande zazo ezigxilile eBhabhiloni lasendulo lapho uNimrode aqala khona izinganekwane zakhe nendlovukazi yezulu. Ifa labo nabapristi nezizukulwane zabo zilandela uhlu lwazo lwabawileyo ababusa iBhabhiloni lasendulo ngonkulunkulu babo abathathu abangcwele ababasebenzisela ukuhleka usulu ubunkulunkulu beqiniso, nenzalo yesithembiso yowesifazane. Lezi zenzo nemikhuba yobuqaba kubuse umhlaba ngasese kakhulu lapho abapristi abaqalisiwe babawileyo bekhonza ilanga ngasese kodwa bethi bayiNhlangano KaJesu; futhi kanjalo ibandla labo lokuzenzisa lamanga nelihlambalazayo liye lacindezela futhi lashushisa umhlaba ngezinsuku imfihlakalo yobubi isakazeka ngasese. Ngakho-ke, ukubhujiswa kwabo akulibali futhi izikhathi zabo sezifikile!

Nokho lezi zilo ezimbili ezimbi, ezisebenza ngokubambisana manje ukuze kubhujiswe ngokuphelele iBhabhiloni eyimfihlo, isilo sasolwandle nesilo sasemhlabeni; zona kanye lezi zibili ebezingabaphathi ababusayo beBhabhiloni Eyimfihlo futhi ezibuse ngezithembiso eziningi ezinkulu neziyize lapho laba belusi ababili abanonwayo beluse khona izimvu ezilahlekile zeBhabhiloni Eyimfihlo bethembisa ukuchuma kwezinto ezibonakalayo okungamanga nengcebo kulabo ababelusile. Futhi ezandleni zabo nangamaqhinga abo ngokuqinisekile bahlelile futhi bazokwenza lokho okunikezwe ezandleni zikadrako ukuze kukufeze.

Ngoba iBhabhiloni Eyimfihlo, sona kanye leso sifebe esikhulu esihleli phezu kwamanzi amaningi siyisizwe esenziwa ukuze lezi zilo ezimbili ezimbi zivuke emlotheni waso ngezikhathi zazo zokwambulwa. Siyisizwe esiphingayo, isizwe sobufebe, isizwe esiyisifebe esikhulu ngempela esizibiza ngomlandeli olungileyo kaJesu waseNazaretha. Kodwa eqinisweni siyisizwe abakhi baso abakhululekile EDwaleni Lesisekelo leqiniso, lona lelo litshe legumbi elikhulu abangakholwa abakhubeka kulo, uJesu waseNazaretha onguye futhi onguYeshua HaMashiach. Ngakho-ke laba bakhi ababi bazikhulule kulolo chibi lomlilo lapho impethu yabo ingafi khona ngokwala itshe eliyinhloko legumbi nedwala lokukhubekisa futhi ngeke baphunyuke!

Nokho abawileyo bebelokhu benezinhloso ezifanayo engqondweni futhi amacebo nezinjongo zabo zihlala zifana. Ngoba bahlose ukumbhubhisa ngokuphelele u-Adamu emhlabeni, futhi bahlose ngokugcwele ukumbhubhisa uJakobe ngaphansi kwelanga ukuze baqhubeke nokuhlala njengabahlali abahlala ngokungemthetho nabathumbi bomhlaba abangalungile ukuze babe nenkathi yabo yegolide abayikhanukayo. Kodwa qondani ukuthi inkathi yabo yegolide kwakuyisikhathi lapho u-Adamu ekhungathekiswe futhi ebuswa umbuso womhlaba wonke wamanyala amabi owawenzelwe ukubhujiswa okuphelele kwesintu, futhi amanyala abo azobuya! Kodwa abayikuba nayo inkathi yegolide kodwa kuphela amathemba aphukile nesahlulelo esinzima njengoba umbuso wabawileyo ubhujiswa ngokuphelele phambi kwamehlo abo ngesandla sami esinamandla!

Futhi lokho abakubiza ngenkathi yegolide kwakuyisikhathi lapho uhlu lokulunga lukaSeti luzifihla ehlane emphakathini wabo omubi emhlabeni owawukhona ngaleso sikhathi. Ukuze uhlu lozalo lukaSeti luhlale lumsulwa ekungcolisweni kofuzo futhi inzalo ethenjisiwe yowesifazane (engiyiyo), ifike njengoba kwathenjiswa endulo nguYahweh uqobo. Bhekani! UYahweh umemezele ukuthi uzoletha Iziqhwaga ukuze zigcwalise intukuthelo yakhe, futhi kuyoba njalo. Nokho, ngakho-ke umbuso wabo omubi uyovuka futhi ubuse umhlaba wonke futhi bayoletha ububi obuningi, futhi amanyala amaningi ayokhululwa ngale kweqhwa nasenhliziyweni yomhlaba futhi kwalasha kuyovulwa.

Kuyoba nobubi obukhulu emhlabeni futhi nakuba beyovuka ukuze balethe ukuthula kwamanga; impi iyohlala ibusa ukubusa kwabo ngoba ukuthula kuzothathwa emhlabeni njengesahlulelo ekususweni komvimbeli wami. Futhi ukuthula abasoze baba nakho ngoba uYahweh wamemezela endulo ukuthi abawileyo abanakho ukuthula ngoba bonile isintu ezinkanukweni zabo ezimbi nenzondo yabo yazo zonke izinto ezenziwe nguYahweh. Futhi bayomisa ukuthula kwamanga ezweni futhi isilo sasemhlabeni siyofika ngokugcwele emandleni aso, futhi siyobusa njengewundlu elinezimpondo ezimbili elikhuluma njengodrako.

Ngokuba iwundlu lika-Esawu, yebo isilo sasemhlabeni esinguye futhi noBharabha, sikhethiwe njengomakhi wabo wethempeli lobusilo futhi sinikelwa njengewundlu likadrako kuMoshiach wabo wamanga nenkosi yabo yokuzenzisa. Ngiqinisile, ngiqinisile, uyohlanganisa u-Esawu no-Ashkenazi nabo bonke abahlanganyela nabo ngaphansi kwesibhengezo senkolo ababengayazi oYise babo, nezindlela zabo zeTalmud ziyobusa umhlaba. Bayozithobisa izizwe ngonya olukhulu ngaphansi kweNoahide futhi bayobusa izizwe ngesihluku esikhulu; futhi isilo sasemhlabeni siyophoqa bonke, abancane nabakhulu, abacebile nabampofu, abakhululekile nabayizigqila, ukuba bamukele uphawu lwaso ezandleni zabo zokunene noma emabunzini abo ukuze kungabikho muntu ongakwazi ukuthenga noma ukuthengisa, ngaphandle kwalowo onophawu, noma igama lesilo, noma inombolo yegama laso. Ngoba uletha ukulunywa yinyoka okungekho muntu ongalulama kukho futhi bonke abahlanganyela kukho bayoziguqula ingunaphakade lapho kuleso siguquko bengeke babe khona ukuthethelelwa, futhi bayohlanganisa insimbi nobumba, kodwa ngeke banamathelane. Okugobile akunakuqondiswa futhi bonke abazenza bagobe ngaleyo ndlela bayoba nesabelo sabo kulelo chibi elikhulu lomlilo wesibabule samalangabi angacimeki lapho isibungu sabo singafi khona.

Ngakho isilo sasemhlabeni esingumprofethi wabo wamanga noMoshiach wamanga siyokwenza izimemezelo zaso futhi siyakwakha ithempeli laso ngoba sithunyiwe njengomakhi wethempeli wobusilo ozakhele imibhoshongo eminingi embusweni waso ezinikele egameni laso, futhi siyokhuluma ngaso ngokuzithakasela okukhulu size simemezele ukuthi sona ngokwaso unguNkulunkulu. Ngoba siza ukwenza izithembiso eziningi ezinkulu esingenamandla okuzigcwalisa, futhi siza ukukhuluma izinto eziningi ezinkulu nezihlambalazayo, futhi siyokwenza kanjalo ngoba kunikelwe kuso ukuba sikwenze. Ngoba singumakhi nonjiniyela ozakhele imibhoshongo eminingi nezindawo zokungcebeleka, futhi ngempela sihlezi phezu kombuso waso esizinikele odumweni lwaso; ngoba sihlale sizidumisa ngoba zinjalo izindlela zikadrako onguYise waso wangempela.

Njengoba ithempeli laso lakhiwa futhi liqedwa phakathi kwemikhosi emikhulu nezithembiso eziningi ezihlambalazayo nezingamanga, kanjalo liyokwenza izimemezelo eziningi zamanga esikhundleni salo njengomprofethi wamanga futhi ukubusa kwalo kuyoba nolaka nonya. Nokho, liyobeka isicupho salo kodwa imuphi umthiyi obeka ugibe emehlweni enyoni? Ngobuqili obukhulu lubeka ugibe lwalo futhi ngesithembiso esikhulu liyothembisa ukunikezela ithempeli lalo kubantu bezwe futhi ngesasasa elikhulu u-Esawu no-Ashkenazi bayozigubha bezungeze iwundlu lika-Esawu, futhi bayozenzisa ukuthi babuyisela izindlela zeTorah, kodwa baletha izindlela zokufa ezibhalwe encwadini yabo yokufa okuhlambalazayo, iTalmud yabo. Bhekani! Isilo sasemhlabeni singumbulali nomholi wokuvukela uYahweh ngezindlela zikaBharabha. Siyokhaphela bonke labo esibenza izithembiso ezinkulu kubo futhi ukukhashelwa kwaso kuyoba ngaphandle kokuzisola nangenzondo enkulu esiyizuze kuYise waso, usathane, ozinkanuko zakhe siboshelwe ebubini ukuba sizifeze.

Nokho injalo imvelo yesicabucabu eseluke umhlaba ngokuphelele ngolwembu lwaso. Isicabucabu sihlala silindele inyamazane yaso ukuba ifike olwembeni lwaso bese sigxuma sithathe isinyathelo sokubamba, ukukhubaza nokusongela inyamazane yaso ukuze sidle kancane kancane impilo yezithunjwa zaso; njengoba zingeniswa kancane kancane ngokugcwele ububi ezibanjwe ngabo. Nokho izixuku ziyokuza zivela kude naseduze lapho zizwa ubizo lwaso; futhi isimemo sokunikezelwa nokuhlanzwa kwethempeli laso siyozwakala emhlabeni wonke futhi abaningi abenqaba uMesiya wabo weqiniso bayothathwa esicuphweni saso bashabalale ngakho. Nokho ngeke kubonakale kuyisicupho kulabo esibathakathile, kodwa kuyimvuselelo kaMose eholwa yiSanhedrin yabo futhi buswa ngamasiko nokuhlakanipha kuka-Esawu okuqukethwe encwadini yabo engcwele ehlambalazayo. Futhi iqembu labapristi bamanga abangesibo abakwaLevi ngoba uLevi utholakala ngokugcwele kuMpristi Ophakeme kaMelkisedeki, ngoba mina, Yeshua HaMashiach nginguMpristi wenu Ophakeme kuze kube phakade ngokohlelo lukaMelkisedeki, ngokuba uYahweh umemezele kanjalo.

Nokho njengoba ngafundisa iqiniso ngesikhathi sami lapho ngiza kwabakithi njengeWundlu likaNkulunkulu; lapho ngenqatshwa ngabakithi, nokho ngafundisa iqiniso ngokufanayo, ngakho-ke, O' Israyeli awunazaba. Nakuba, othandweni lwami ngani nangezithembiso nezivumelwano zasendulo, ngiza ukunitshela futhi ukuze uJakobe angawi ku-Esawu.

Ngokuba kulotshiwe emibhalweni engcwele: Ngalokho, lapho niyakubona isinengiso sencithakalo, esakhulunywa ngaso nguDaniyeli umPhrofethi, simi endaweni engcwele, (ofundayo makaqonde). Khona-ke abaseJudiya mababalekele ezintabeni. Osephahleni lwendlu, makehle, angathathi lutho endlini yakhe: Nalowo osensimini, angabuyeli emuva ukuyothatha izingubo zakhe. Maye kwabakhulelweyo, nakwabancelisayo ngalezo zinsuku. Kodwa khulekani ukuthi ukubaleka kwenu kungabi sebusika, noma ngeSabatha: Ngokuba ngaleso sikhathi kuyoba khona ukuhlupheka okukhulu, okunjengako okungakaze kube khona kusukela ekuqaleni komhlaba kuze kube yilesi sikhathi, cha, futhi akusoze kwaba khona. Futhi ngaphandle kokuba lezo zinsuku zifinyezwe, bekungeke kusindiswe nyama: kodwa ngenxa yabakhethiweyo, lezo zinsuku ziyofinyezwa.
