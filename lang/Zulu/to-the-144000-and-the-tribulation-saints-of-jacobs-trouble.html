Kwabayi-144,000 Nakwabangcwele Bosizi Lwesikhathi Sokuhlupheka KukaJakobe

Kwabayi-144,000 nakwabangcwele bosizi lwesikhathi sokuhlupheka kukaJakobe: Bhekani! Ngiyanitshela izinto eziningi zingakenzeki ukuze ohlakaniphileyo phakathi kwenu, ozwayo izwi lami, ande ukuhlakanipha kakhulu, futhi nazi isiqiniseko samazwi ami. Ngoba uSathane uzozama ukusitha ukuhamba kwabathembekileyo eBandleni lami (ngoba ngizobahlwithela ekuhambeni kwabo emhlabeni ngibayise emazulwini) ngempi nangokuvela okungamanga kwezidalwa ezibizwa ngokuthi ezivela kweminye imihlaba, ezingeyilutho ngaphandle kwezingelosi eziwileyo eziziveza emhlabeni ebubini obuningi obugxilile emangeni azo. Ngoba ziyinkohliso.

Ziqambe amanga ngendawo yonke okuthiwa ikhona; ngoba ayikho indawo yonke ehlale ikhula yobuze obungenamgomo, kodwa ngaphezu komhlaba kukhona izulu lamazulu, yebo (izulu eliphakeme kakhulu) lapho uYahweh, uNkulunkulu ophakeme kakhulu, ehlezi esihlalweni sakhe sobukhosi, kodwa uyokwenza indawo yakhe yokuhlala nabantu. Nokho ukhona yonke indawo futhi uyikho konke kubo bonke ngaphakathi koMzimba kaKristu. Emhlabeni kuhlala iparele lakhe eliyigugu elikhulu, yebo labo abakhethwa emaphakadeni adlule enhliziyweni kaYahweh uqobo. Ngakho-ke umhlaba ungowenkosi nokugcwala kwawo; ngoba mina, Jesu waseNazaretha, ngiwuthengile umhlaba ngegazi lami elihlengayo.

Fundani i-cosmology yenu yangempela (fundisani ukufunda) futhi nikholwe incazelo namazwi emiBhalweni Engcwele mayelana nendalo, ngoba kunjengoba uYahweh echazile emBhalweni, futhi akunakwenzeka ukuba uYahweh aqambe amanga. Cha, azikho izidalwa ezivela kweminye imihlaba ngomqondo amanga azo awuchazayo, kodwa kunezingelosi eziwileyo, ezihlubukayo, yebo uSathane nombuso wakhe abaqambe amanga futhi bakhohlisa umhlaba ukuze bonke abathokoza ngokungalungi balahlwe.

Baluke izinganekwane eziningi eziklanywe ngobuhlakani zaba yindwangu yamanga, futhi balawule zombili izinhlangothi zezindaba zabo kusukela ekuqaleni njengoba beqhubeka nezinhlelo zabo. Ngoba balawula umqondo oyisisekelo futhi balawula umqondo ophikisanayo, futhi bahlanganisa imisebenzi yabo emibi isinyathelo ngesinyathelo, futhi inkohliso yabo inkulu! Kodwa ukukhanya kweMibhalo Engcwele kuveza ubuqili, kodwa umuntu uyalenqaba usizo lukaNkulunkulu kuze kumenyezelwe ukwahlulela bese kufika inhlekelele. Khona-ke isintu sifuna ukuphenduka lapho sekwephuzile ngemva kokuba ukwahlulela sekudlulile, futhi labo abaphila ngaleyo ndlela ngeke babalekele ukwahlulelwa kwabo. Ngoba noma yini emenyezelwe nguYahweh akekho ongangayiguqula!

Abaningi bayonqotshwa yizinkohliso ezizayo ezizovela empini yomhlaba nasenhluphekweni esizofikela umhlaba. Ngoba bayoziveza esintwini njengezidalwa ezinomusa ezivela emihlabeni ethuthukile ukuze zabelane ngolwazi lwazo olunomusa. Kodwa kumane kuwulwazi lokuhle nokubi oluphinde lwapakishwa ukuze luhambisane nezinjongo zabo. Bayoba nobuchwepheshe obuphakeme futhi babonakale bethuthuke kakhulu, kude nomuntu wemvelo. Kodwa intuthuko yabo ikububi kuphela, futhi abaveli kulo mhlaba kodwa badalwa nguYahweh ekuqaleni kwendalo emazulwini. Babekhona lapho uYahweh edala umhlaba. UBaba ekwazini kwakhe kwangaphambili wayazi ukuthi bazokwenza ubuqili ngakho wagcina imfihlakalo yenhliziyo yakhe eyigugu ifihlekile kulabo ayazi ukuthi bazoletha imbubhiso embi endalweni yakhe.

Ngisabiza umuntu wemvelo ekulungeni, kodwa umuntu wemvelo ukhetha ukugula, isono, nobumnyama; ngakho ngizokunika isintu lokho esikufisa kakhulu. Njengoba umuntu wemvelo enza okubi futhi ethanda ubumnyama, ngizomnika ubumnyama obungenazithiyo.

Ngoba okwamanje, umuntu wemvelo ngokuzidla umi emseni wami esebenzisa isihawu njengesizathu sokwenezela isono esonweni, esikhundleni sokwamukela isivumelwano sokuthula sikaYahweh, yebo mina, uYeshua HaMashiach, owanikeza igazi lami elingenasono ngokulalela uYahweh ukuze kube yinkokhelo yazo zonke izono zesintu zaso sonke isikhathi. Ungenzi iphutha: nginguMesiya weqiniso, futhi konke okunye kuyizilingiso ezingabizi ezingenamsebenzi.

USathane wahlubuka ngokushesha ngemva kokuqala kwendalo futhi wangcolisa indalo kaBaba ngendlela engenamahloni, embi, enenzondo, neyonakalisayo; kodwa kwenziwa ngobuqili obukhulu nangezibonakaliso ezimangalisayo eziyinkohliso, ngisho nobuchwepheshe obuthuthukile obukhanga izinzwa. Futhi njengembuyiselo bafuna ukukhonzwa nokulalelwa okuqinile kwezindlela zabo, kodwa izindlela zabo zihlale ziletha ukufa nokubhujiswa kwesintu, ngoba zakhelwe lokho.

Akukho lutho olusha ngaphansi kwelanga, bantabami. Labo okuthiwa onkulunkulu bomlando wasendulo nenzalo yabo enyanyekayo okuthiwa yi-demigods bazobuya, futhi njengasezinsukwini zikaNowa, kuyoba njalo. Ngoba bakhululwa ezibophweni zabo nasekuhlushweni ukuze bamukele ukwahlulelwa kwabo okuphelele, futhi ngizophindisela, isho iNkosi uJesu Kristu, futhi uYahweh uzobaphindisela ngokugcwele.

USathane nezingelosi zakhe eziwileyo benqaba ukuguqa ngedolo emthethweni kaYahweh futhi esikhundleni salokho bazama ukweba umhlaba esintwini ngenkohliso enkulu. USathane uze wazama ukweba ifa lami kuBaba weziKhanyiso! Kodwa akekho onganikina ngisho nomncane wenu ezandleni zami, futhi akekho ongangiphuqa ifa lami; ngoba linikezwe nguYahweh uqobo, futhi noma ngubani onqaba ukuguqa ngedolo kimi, uJesu Kristu, uhlubuka emthethweni kaYahweh, ejoyina abawileyo kuze kube phakade.

Ngakho ngithi kini, ningakholwa ngamanga kaCopernicus, owayengumJesuit noFreemason owayekhonza uSathane. Wavele wafundisa kabusha amazwi ezazi zefilosofi zasendulo zamaGreki ezingamaqaba. Ngakho-ke i-cosmology yenu eyamukelwe yacatshangwa ngabakhonzi belanga namaqaba ngokusekelwe emangeni kaSathane, yebo amaJesuit ahlambalazayo aye akhohlisa izinhlelo zenu zemfundo ngokufunda okuphikisana nokufunda, ngenhloso egcwele yokuba indawo yabo yonke engamanga igxiliswe ezingqondweni zabantu ngamanga apakishwe njengemfundo. Esikhundleni salokho, kwenzeke ukufundiswa okungenangqondo, futhi umhlaba njengoba unjalo ngempela awuqondwa yilabo abaphila emhlabeni.

Manje isiteji sesilungiselelwe inkohliso enamandla kakhulu, futhi amanga asevele akhiwe, ngoba izisekelo zawo zingububi nenkohliso enkulu. Lezi zinkohliso zigxilile emfundweni yenu yamaJesuit. Ngoba umhlaba wonke ukhohlisiwe ngamanga awo; ngisho nabakhethiweyo kakhulu bebengakhohliswa uma kungenzeka. Ngakho-ke ugibe selulungele ukugxuma, futhi ukuphela kwento elubambile umvimbeli, ozosuswa ngokuzumayo nangesikhathi esifushane kakhulu. Khona-ke impi enkulu izoqhuma emhlabeni wonke njengoba abathembekileyo bami bebuthelwa kimi emazulwini. Khona-ke emlotheni kuyovuka isilo nomprofethi wamanga, abayoba ngumgogodla wenkohliso ezayo.

Ngakho-ke ningakholwa amanga abo, nina ziqhwaga zami, ngoba niyisizwe esikhethekileyo futhi nginibizile futhi nganibeka uphawu lweNkulunkulu Ophilayo, ukuze intukuthelo kaNkulunkulu inganithinti. Ngizokuthuma njengeziqhwaga zami. Niyoba ngamazwi ami amaningi akhamuluka ehlane nomaphi lapho nithunyelwa khona, futhi nginithumela ukuba niqoqe izimvu zami ezilahlekileyo ezisele.

Ngoba isixuku esikhulu siyophuma osizini lukaJakobe, futhi niyokwenza imisebenzi emikhulu futhi niyalele okungenakwenzeka, ngoba ngiyonika izibonakaliso eziningi ezinamandla, izimangaliso, nezimangaliso eziyokwenziwa ngezandla zenu. Niyokwenza izimangaliso ezinkulu zasendulo ezilotshwe emiBhalweni Engcwele, yebo ngamandla ka-Eliya, ngaphandle kokuthi niyokwenza imisebenzi emikhulu futhi enamandla kakhulu kunalokho okulotshwe ngaye wasendulo.

Ngoba imibhalo ithi lowo okholwa kimi, uYeshua HaMashiach, ngeke enze imisebenzi engayenza emhlabeni kuphela enkonzweni yami eJudiya njengewundlu likaNkulunkulu, kodwa niyokwenza imisebenzi emikhulu kunaleyo engayenza mina, ngoba ngizokwenza izibonakaliso ezinkulu, izimangaliso, nezimangaliso ngezandla zenu njengoba niyala okungenakwenzeka ngokukholwa kimi. Ngakho bhekani kimi njalo. Futhi gcwalisani amehlo enu kimi, ngoba ngizonihola isinyathelo ngesinyathelo. Ukuhamba kwenu kuyoba okokuhlakanipha, futhi niyoba nolimi lwabahlakaniphileyo, futhi ngiyoba ngamandla enu nensindiso yenu, ngoba kimi, Kristu Jesu, kuhlala konke ukugcwala.

Ngakho-ke ngiyaniyeluleka nonke ukuthi maduzane imali iyoba yize, futhi imali eyamukelekayo kuphela iyodinga uphawu ukuze nifinyelele ezimpahleni zomhlaba. Ningaluthathi uphawu, ngoba kungcono ukufa kule mpilo (ningazithandi izimpilo zenu kuze kube sekupheleni) kunokuthatha uphawu futhi niphonswe echibini lomlilo laphakade elilungiselelwe uDeveli nezingelosi zakhe, ngoba izikhathi zabo sezifikile; kuphela ningazihlanganisi nabo. Ngoba bayothembisa izithembiso ezibonakala zizinkulu futhi ziyigugu, kodwa amazwi abo angamanga, futhi ubuthi busemazwini abo, nezithembiso zabo zingamanga futhi zigcwele zonke izinhlobo zezinto ezingcolile. Ngeke bakwazi ukuletha izithembiso zabo, ngoba ukwahlulelwa kwabo kuyeza; abanakho ukuthula, futhi ukuthula kuyosuswa ngokuphelele emhlabeni.

Ububi besintu buyobe bungavinjwe ngokuphelele; futhi kwabaningi, uma umvimbeli esusiwe, bayozinikela ingunaphakade ebuhlanyini nobubi. Ngaphandle kokuthi ohlala ngenkemba kumelwe abulawe ngenkemba, futhi ngokuqinisekile bayowa kwesobunxele nakwesokudla ngenani elikhulu, kodwa ngeke kufike kini njengoba nihamba ngenxa yami; kuphela ningazithandi izimpilo zenu kuze kube sekupheleni futhi nilandele isibonelo sami sokuzidela nenkonzo yokuzidela. Ngoba umvuzo omkhulu uyoba ngowenu ngokulalela nokwethembeka.

Ngakho-ke izinkanuko nobubi besintu kuyobe kungavinjwe ngokuphelele, noDeveli nezingelosi zakhe bayobe bengavinjwe ngokuphelele, nazo zonke izinengiso zabo ezimbi ziyobe zingavinjwe ngokuphelele, futhi bayoza njengabasizi basendulo. Inkohliso yabo ijule kakhulu futhi inezimpande ezijulile ezizosishulwa ngokuphelele maduze. Ababona onkulunkulu, futhi ababona abanomusa abathuthukile, kodwa bangabakhohlisi, futhi bazingcolise ngazo zonke izindlela. Ngakho-ke bayobe bengavinjwe ngokuphelele, futhi ukugcwala kombuso kaSathane kuyokhululwa kwalasha, ngoba uyovulwa.

Lokhu kuyokwenziwa ukuze kuboniswe umuntu wemvelo ukuthi ukhethe ini futhi anikezwe ithuba lokugcina lokuphenduka; ngoba ububi ngeke busafihlwa futhi buvinjwe, futhi yonke imfihlo iyovela obala. Ngisho namanga enkohliso yabo enkulu nezinkohliso ezinamandla ayodalulwa ukuze wonke umuntu abone, ngoba uYahweh ukumemezele emiBhalweni Engcwele, futhi lokho uYahweh akumemezelayo akekho ongangakuguqula.

Ngakho-ke ukuhlukaniswa kokugcina kuyokwenzeka, futhi abaningi babazithele ngabandayo engibathandayo bayoshiyelwa ezilingweni nasekuhlolweni okuzayo, ukuze basindiswe. Ngoba asisekho isikhathi, futhi umusa wami nesihe ngeke kusakhishwa ngendlela efanayo, futhi asisekho isikhathi sezindlela zokuba mlaza. Wonke umuntu kuyodingeka enze ukukhetha; futhi abesabayo nabangakholwayo bayobe bekhethe uSathane nombuso wakhe lapho bebengangikhetha mina, yebo uJesu Kristu, iNdodana kaNkulunkulu eze ngenyama futhi ngizinikele njengePhasika eliphelele lazo zonke izono zesintu kanye kuphela. Abaningi basazonenqaba isandla sami sosizo ngesikhathi sokudinga okukhulu, ngoba usizo lwami luwusizo lwaphakade futhi olungunaphakade lomvuzo omkhulu, isihe, umusa, nothando.

Nokho, iningi lomhlaba liyokholwa amanga futhi liyomangala ngesilo, lithi, ngubani onjengesilo? Bayozivumela kalula ukuba balunywe wulunywe lwenyoka olunobuthi obungeke bulungiswe, ngoba luhlala njalo. Ukuba umuntu akhethe ububi ezikhathini zokukhetha kuyoba ukukhetha kwaphakade, futhi labo abenza lokho kukhetha bayoma ekwahlulelweni okukhulu.

Labo abakhetha ububi ngokuzithandela bayolandisa ngayo yonke imicabango emibi, wonke amazwi amabi, nayo yonke imisebenzi emibi abayenzile ngobubi emhlabeni, ngoba izincwadi ziyovulwa futhi ngeke kube khona isihawu. Futhi akekho umuntu oyokwazi ukuma ngokulunga kwakhe siqu, ngoba umuntu akanakho ukulunga kwakhe siqu; ngoba inyoka yabakhohlisa kusukela ekuqaleni, futhi izinsuku zayo zokukhohlisa sezizophela. Ngakho-ke uYahweh wangithuma mina, iNdodana yakhe ezelwe yodwa, njengesivumelwano sokuthula kumuntu wemvelo, futhi umuntu wemvelo wenqabile ukuthula kwaphakade ngoba umuntu wemvelo uthanda ububi.

Ukwahlulela okukhulu sekuzokhishwa embusweni kaSathane. Lokhu kwahlulela kuyoba kubi kakhulu, futhi izigameko ezenziwa ekwahluleleni ziyoba zimbi kakhulu. Usizi luyokwenezelwa phezu kosizi nokuhlushwa kuyokwenezelwa phezu kokuhlushwa kubo bonke abenqaba ukuphenduka emisebenzini yabo emibi. Ngoba abaningi ngeke baphenduke emisebenzini yezandla zabo, ukuba bangakhonzi amademoni, nezithombe zegolide, nesiliva, nethusi, netshe, nokhuni: okungeke kubone, noma kuzwe, noma kuhambe: Futhi ngeke baphenduke emisebenzini yabo yokubulala, noma ekuthakatheni kwabo, noma ebufebeni babo, noma ekwebini kwabo.

Ngoba abawileyo ababona onkulunkulu, kodwa bangabaqambimanga abazenzisayo, abalingisi, nabakhohlisi. Baletha ukufa nokubhujiswa kuphela kulabo abalandelayo, futhi bonke abakhohlisiwe bayodumala kakhulu, ngoba okulindelekile kubo kuyonyamalala kulelo chibi lomlilo elikhulu lapho kungekho themba khona. Futhi mina, Kristu Jesu, ngiyowuhlakaza ngokuphelele umbuso kaSathane ekwahlulelweni okukhulu, ngoba ukwahlulela okukhulu kuyokhishwa kubo bonke, futhi ngiyiNgonyama yesizwe sakwaJuda.

Ngakho-ke uphawu ngalunye luyokwengeza ukwahlulelwa phezu kokwahlulelwa, futhi icilongo ngalinye liyokwengeza ukwahlulelwa phezu kokwahlulelwa, usizi ngalunye luyokwengeza usizi phezu kosizi, futhi ngesitsha ngasinye kuza intukuthelo ephelele kaNkulunkulu, yebo intukuthelo phezu kwentukuthelo. Umbuso kaSathane uyohlupheka kakhulu, futhi umbuso wakhe uyobhujiswa ngokuphelele futhi uchithwe ngokuphelele.

Ngoba bayozibutha futhi bazihlele ngokumelene nokuza kweNkosi, ngoba ngizofika ngokuqinisekile. Kusukela ekuqaleni kosizi lukaJakobe, kuyoba nezinsuku eziyisikhombisa zeminyaka, ngoba iviki lamashumi ayisikhombisa likaDaniyeli liyogcwaliseka ngokubhujiswa kokungalungi nangensindiso ka-Israyeli. Ngoba ngizohlanza u-Israyeli ebubini obuthumbe izwe.

Ngoba akumina uYahweh noma mina ngokwami, uYeshua HaMashiach, ngithume noma ngigunyaze ababusi babo, ngoba babusa ngokungalungi futhi ukubulawa kwabo kwabantu abaningi kuyisinengiso, futhi abantu bami bakhokha inani lezimpi zabo. Nokho abaningi beBandla lami bakholwa amanga abo obuZiyoni, ngakho-ke qonda ukuthi angiyena umZiyoni, ngoba bangabaphakanyisi bamaJuda, futhi bangukhula, imbewu kaSathane uqobo; futhi ngizobasiphula ezweni, ngoba bathathe ifa lami, futhi ngiyeza ukuzothatha okungokwami. Ngoba mina, Yeshua HaMashiach, yebo mina, ngiphethe itayitela lomhlaba; ngoba ngawuthenga ngegazi lami elingenasono, futhi nginezikhiye zokufa nesihogo. Futhi ngikhokhele izono zesintu, futhi noma ngubani obiza igama leNkosi uyosindiswa.

Ngakho umhlaba ungowami nokugcwala kwawo, futhi ngizohlanza izwe ebubini babo, futhi isinagoge likaSathane liyobhujiswa ngokuphelele, futhi ngakho ngizoletha isivumelwano esisha emhlabeni futhi ngizobusa ngokulunga. Ngoba njengoba umhlaba uba mnyama futhi uba mubi ngokwengeziwe umzuzwana nomzuzwana, ukubuya kwami kwesibili kusondela ngokwengeziwe umzuzwana nomzuzwana.

Yibani nethemba elikhulu ngokufika kwami; ngoba ngizofika futhi ngizogqashuka emazulwini namabutho ezulu, yebo ibutho elingafani nelinye, ngoba ibutho elinjengelami alikaze libe khona, ibutho elingenakunqotshwa elakhiwe yilabo abaphila ukuphila okuphakade futhi abangeke babulawe noma banqotshwe. Ngoba nginikwe amandla nguYahweh ngenjongo enjalo, futhi ngizobe ngibanike amandla futhi ngibavuze ngokwethembeka kwabo nangothando lwabo kimi negama lami, yebo uJesu Kristu! Ngoba noma ngubani othanda futhi ekhonza uYeshua HaMashiach uyathanda futhi ukhonza noYahweh, ngoba akekho ophakeme kunoNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade ka-Israyeli! Noma ngubani ophika iNdodana, lowo aka naye uBaba; futhi nginguJesu Kristu ozelwe yedwa kaBaba owazinikela ngezono zabo bonke, futhi ngingumpristi ophakeme kuze kube phakade ngokohlelo lukaMelkisedeki.

Futhi ngizomnqoba ngokuphelele udrago, isilo, nomprofethi wamanga. Ngoba isilo nomprofethi wamanga bayophonswa bephila kulelo chibi elivuthayo elivutha amalangabi aluhlaza okwesibhakabhaka ashisa kakhulu angenakucishwa futhi lapho kungeke kube khona ngisho nomzuzwana wokuphumula. Kodwa yonke imisebenzi yabo emibi iyobuyiselwa kubo ingunaphakade. Konke ukuhlupheka abakubangele kuyobuyiselwa ingunaphakade, ngoba uYahweh uyobuyisela yonke imisebenzi emibi kuze kufike esilinganisweni esincane kunazo zonke ezifubeni zabo ezimbi, futhi bayokhokha ingunaphakade ngamacala abo.

Ngakho-ke uSathane uyoboshwa kwalasha iminyaka eyinkulungwane, ngoba kunjalo kumemezele uYahweh. Mina, uYeshua HaMashiach, ngizobusa umhlaba ngokulunga usuku olulodwa. Ngoba kuYahweh usuku lunjengeminyaka eyinkulungwane neminyaka eyinkulungwane injengosuku.
