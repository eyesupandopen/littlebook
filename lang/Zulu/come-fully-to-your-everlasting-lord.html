Ufakazi Ombaxa: Israyeli
Okubabayo Okumnandi
Okthoba 8, 2024

Zanini Ngokupheleleyo Kuyo INkosi Yenu Yaphakade!

Yizwani, yizwani! Yizwani izwi leNkosi yenu, nizwe amazwi kaYeshua HaMashiach, nithobise izinhliziyo zenu ekulaleleni intando yeNkosi yenu, nivumele amazwi ami aduduze imiphefumulo yenu. Ngakho-ke ngiyanibuza umbuzo? Niyalalela na? Lapho iNdodana yomuntu ifika, ngiyofumana ukholo emhlabeni na? Ngiqinisile, ngiqinisile, ngiyofumana ukholo phakathi kwabathembekileyo bami engiyobasusa ngibayise ekuphepheni kwamazulu ngenxa yokuthembeka kwabo, kodwa iningi labo abasemhlabeni abanakholo. Ngalokho-ke ngiyaqhubeka nginixwayisa nina zithandwa zami ukuba nisuke ezonweni zaboni, niphume phakathi kwezindlela zabo, nahlukane, hleze nihlanganyele ekwahlulelweni kwabo.

Nokho, kunjalo ukuthi abantu abaningi kangaka basondela kimi ngokungaqotho ngezindebe zabo, kanti izinhliziyo zabo zikude nami. Ngalolo suku abaningi bayothi kimi, Nkosi, Nkosi, nami ngiyothi kubo, Sukani kimi, angizange nginazi! Qondani ukuthi akwanele ukumemezela ukuthi ngiyiNkosi ngenhliziyo engenakholo, kodwa labo abangenza iNkosi ngempela yilabo abaphendukayo ezonweni zabo baziyeke, bafune ukuphila ngokulunga phambi kwami. Anazi yini ukuthi mina, Jesu waseNazaretha, ngingathanda kakhulu inhliziyo yenu yonke othandweni nasekuzinikeleni kunemihlatshelo yezindebe zenu? Yebo, ngingathanda isihawu, hhayi umhlatshelo, ngoba ngokwengxenye kungenxa yesihawu sami, ukubanjwa kolaka olufanelekile, kubonakaliswa uthando lwami, lona uthando lukaYahweh uNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade! Kodwa amazwi ami esixwayiso anakekelwa yilabo abangithandayo nabaphendule ngokuphenduka othandweni lwami ngabo, njengoba ngizembulile kubo ukuze basindiswe. Kunjalo-ke, uthando lwami lwamukelwa phakathi kwalabo abanezinhliziyo ezithambile ezwini lami, yebo, kubo bonke abangithandayo nalabo engibayalile ukuba bafunde ngami, labo engibaholayo ezindleleni zami zensindiso.

Kungani nivumela izinhliziyo zenu zinihluphe, nina bancane bami? Abaphostoli nabaprofethi bami abazange baxwayise ngalolu suku kusukela ekuntweleni kokusa kwesikhathi na? Abaprofethi basendulo abazange bashumayele yini ngeviki likaDaniyeli lamashumi ayisikhombisa elizayo ngokwahlulela okukhulu emhlabeni na? Ngakho-ke, qondani, niphila ezinsukwini ezingaphambi kokuba ukuhlupheka kukaJakobe kugqashuke ngokupheleleyo emhlabeni wonke, futhi ngalezi zinsuku kuye kwabikezelwa izinhlekelele eziningi, izibonakaliso, izimangaliso, nezimangaliso ezinkulu, ngisho nokwahlulela, futhi manje sekukhululiwe, pho nimangalelani na? Lezi yizixwayiso zokugcina ngezinga elincane zalokho okuzayo emhlabeni wonke lapho abathembekileyo bami sebesukile baya kimi emazulwini besuka emhlabeni, njengoba ngibabutha ndawonye njengamathole esibaya, futhi bayothola ukuphepha okuphelele nokwaneliseka lapho bemi emizuzwini yabo yokuqala bezelwe kabusha kuze kube phakade, lapho bengasoze babhujiswa, noma bahlupheke, noma bakhuthazelele ukugula, kodwa bayoba nenjabulo ephelele nokuzwana okuphelele noYahweh, nami-ke Yeshua HaMashiach, futhi ngiyobanika umvuzo omkhulu nefa elikhulu, ngoba umvuzo wabo usesandleni sami esinamandla.

Baningi kakhulu abathembekileyo bami asebenziwe imvubelo yezimfundiso zamanga zevangeli lenala elishunyayelwa ngokungafanele ngabaprofethi bamanga nabafundisi abaqamba amanga kubantu bami, bebatshela ukuthi inhlekelele ayisoze yabathinta inqobo nje uma becabanga imicabango ejabulisayo. Le mithombo engenamanzi ishumayela omunye uJesu, ayazi mina, Yeshua HaMashiach, futhi angibathumanga. Hlaziyani njalo amazwi alabo abathi bakhulumela mina nemibhalo, futhi lapho bephikisana nemibhalo kulapho bekhuluma khona okuvela ezinhliziyweni zabo ukuze bazuze ngokuhaha kwabo.

Ngiqinisile, ngiqinisile, kunezikhonzi eziningi zamanga ezihlwanyele amanga phakathi kwamabandla ami, futhi ivangeli lamanga lenala liholela endleleni ebanzi yokubhujiswa lapho amasango esihogo eyovuleka kakhulu kulabo abalandela kuze kube kulelo chibi lomlilo elivutha amalangabi ashisa kakhulu aluhlaza okwesibhakabhaka, alungiselelwe abawileyo nalabo ababalandelayo, ngakho-ke ningakhohliswa ngamanga abo! Angikhulumi ngabaprofethi bami abathembekileyo okungenzeka baphosise izwi kimi, noma labo abenza iphutha eliqotho bazilungise. Qondani ukuthi akekho kubaprofethi bami oprofetha ngokugcwele, kodwa bonke abaprofethi bami baprofetha ingxenye, futhi abasoze baphikisana nemibhalo lapho bekhulumela mina ngempela, uKristu Jesu, ngoba ngiyizwi likaNkulunkulu elenziwa inyama elihlala phakathi kwenu, nakuba ngingasaziwa ngokwenyama.

Bancane bami, ningakhohliswa ngoba kuleli zwe kuhlale kukhona ukushushiswa nosizi, ikakhulukazi manje ekupheleni kombuso kaSathane njengoba abawileyo beza ekwahlulelweni futhi belungiselela ukwenza lokho abavunyelwe ukukufeza ekwahlulelweni kwabo, ngoba ngisho nezitha zikaYahweh ziyalilalela izwi lakhe. Anifundanga yini kwabaseRoma lapho ngiyalela uPawulu ezindleleni zami na? Ngubani oyosihlukanisa nothando lukaKristu na? Usizi, noma ukucindezeleka, noma ukushushiswa, noma indlala, noma ubunqunu, noma ingozi, noma inkemba na? (Njengoba kulotshiwe, ngenxa yakho sibulawa usuku lonke, sithathwa njengezimvu zokuhlatshwa.) Cha, kuzo zonke lezi zinto singabanqobi abakhulu ngaye owasithandayo. Ngoba ngiyaqiniseka ukuthi nakufa, nakuphila, nazingelosi, nababusi, namandla, nazinto zamanje, nazinto ezizayo, nakuphakama, nakujula, nanoma iyiphi enye indalo, akusoze kwasihlukanisa nothando lukaNkulunkulu olukuKristu Jesu iNkosi yethu.

Ngalokho abakhethiweyo bami bahlale behlupheka ngezinto eziningi ngenxa yegama lami, futhi kuye kwaba nesixuku esikhulu sabafelukholo esikhathini sami somusa abanikela ngezimpilo zabo enkonzweni yokuqhubekisela phambili ivangeli lensindiso, lona leli vangeli elisindise imiphefumulo yenu ngokholo kuJesu waseNazaretha ngoba ngokwengxenye kwakungenxa yomsebenzi wabafelukholo bami ukuthi ivangeli lensindiso lagcwala umhlaba. Nokho, abaningi benu benziwe imvubelo ngemvubelo yezimfundiso ezahlukahlukene zamanga zokuthi “yibize futhi uyithathe” eseligcwele iBandla lami laseLawodikeya.

Lezi zimfundiso ezimbi aziveli kimi, futhi imicabango yenu ayifezi lutho ngaphandle kwenu ngaphandle kokuthi nikhulume amazwi ami ngezindebe zokholo lapho nginika izibonakaliso, izimangaliso, nezimangaliso ukuba zenziwe ngezandla zenu. Ngakho anikwazi ukuzicabangela niphume ezinkingeni nasekushushisweni, futhi ababiziweyo, abakhethiweyo, nabathembekileyo bami abakhululekile enhlekeleleni. Kushiwo kuphi eZwini likaNkulunkulu ukuthi noma ngubani okholwa kimi ngeke ahlupheke ngosizi, noma ukucindezeleka, noma ukushushiswa, noma indlala, noma ubunqunu, noma ingozi, noma inkemba na? Ngokuphambene nalokho, ezinye noma zonke lezi zinto ziqinisekisiwe kubo bonke abaphila ngokuthembeka kimi, ngakho ngiyaneluleka ukuba nilahle amazwi abo ngoba amazwi abo ayinganekwane eqanjwe ngobuqili ethunyelwe ngabawileyo ukuze baguqulwe ukholo lwabakhethiweyo uqobo, ukuba into enjalo ibingenzeka. Nokho, qondani ukuthi bonke abathembekileyo bami abangilandelayo ngenhliziyo yabo yonke bayasindiswa olakeni lweWundlu nasolakeni lukaNkulunkulu, futhi bayoyiswa ekuphepheni esihlalweni sobukhosi sikaYahweh ngaphambi kokuba ukwahlulela kuwele ngokuphelele emhlabeni.

Kunjalo-ke bantwana bami ukuthi abaningi benu bayohlupheka ngezinhlekelele ezahlukahlukene nokushushiswa okulethwa ngabawileyo, futhi abaningi bayolahlekelwa yinzuzo yaleli zwe kuleli phethelo ngaphambi kokuba ngibuthe abathembekileyo bami. Ngomzuzwana ngamunye odlulayo, ukubonakala kwami kusondela kakhulu. Ngakho ngiyanikhuthaza, zithandwa zami, ukuba ningimele futhi nibambelele othandweni lwami njengamandla enu akhuthazayo okukhuthazelela lokho okudingekayo ukuze nilethe insindiso kwabalahlekileyo njengoba nibaholela eMkhunjini ngaphambi kokuba kugadle inhlekelele.

Qondani lokho engakunika uPawulu ukuba akulobele abaseKolose: Manje ngiyajabula ekuhluphekeni kwami engikuhluphekayo ngenxa yenu, futhi ngigcwalise lokho okusele ezinhluphekweni zikaKristu enyameni yami ngenxa yomzimba wakhe, okuyibandla, engenziwa isikhonzi salo ngokomyalo kaNkulunkulu, lowo myalo engawunikwa nina, ukuze ngigcwalise izwi likaNkulunkulu. Kunjalo, zithandwa zami, ukuthi lapho nihlupheka ngenxa yegama lami, lapho-ke niyaqhubeka futhi nihlanganyele ekuhluphekeni kwami engakukhuthazelela eKalvari. Nakuba ngingashiyanga lutho lungenziwanga ensindisweni yenu, futhi ngiyiPhasika eliphelele nelipheleleyo lazo zonke izikhathi, nginibizile ngamunye wenu ukuba nikhuthazelele izinto eziningi ngenxa yegama lami, ngakho-ke kubaleleni ekuthokozeni kodwa! Futhi yazini bantwana bami ukuthi iso alibonanga, nendlebe ayizwanga, futhi akungenanga ezinhliziyweni zabantu izinto uNkulunkulu azilungiselele labo abamthandayo. Ngakho-ke, gijimani, gijimani ukuze nizuze! Ngoba uNkulunkulu akayena ongalungile, ukuba akhohlwe umsebenzi wenu, nokukhandleka kothando, enikubonise egameni lakhe, ngokuthi nikhonze abaNgcwele, futhi nisakhonza.

Baningi kakhulu kini abaye badukiswa kulokho ebenikulindele kangangokuthi anisaqapheli ukuthi izikhathi eziningi ababiziweyo, abakhethiweyo, nabathembekileyo bami bakhuthazelela ngokuzithandela ubunzima, ukuthotshiswa, ukulahlekelwa, nokunye okuningi, futhi umphumela wokugcina wokukhuthazela kwabo ezinhluphekweni zikaKristu uyinsindiso kwabalahlekileyo nomvuzo omkhulu nenxephezelo kulabo abazinikela ngaleyo ndlela. Anifundanga yini eZenzo lapho uPawulu noSila bakhuthazelela ubunzima obukhulu eMakedoniya, beshaywa kanzima baphonswa ejele ngokungafanele, baboshwa ngokuqinile ezibondeni eFilipi na?

Nokho ngathumela ukuzamazama komhlaba ukuze ngibakhulule ezibophweni zabo, futhi kanjalo umgcini-jele waseFilipi nendlu yakhe bathola insindiso ngokukhuthazela kukaPawulu noSila. Futhi kwabaningi balabo abangilandelayo, bayokhuthazelela ubunzima nokushushiswa nosizi. Kodwa kuzo zonke lezi zinto ningabanqobi abakhulu, futhi ngiyalwela nina bantwana bami, uma nje nihlala nilalela izwi lami futhi nilandela intando yami ngenhliziyo yenu yonke, futhi ngiyonenza ninqobe zonke izinto uma nje nihlala nithembekile ezwini lami futhi ningivumele ngiqondise izinyathelo zenu, ngoba insindiso yenu isekulaleleni uMsindisi wenu waphakade.

Ngakho-ke ngiyaneluleka, zithandwa zami, ukuba ningimele futhi ninike uthando lwami izwe elifayo nelilahliwe elisemaketangeni njengoba ukwahlulela sekuzowela ngokuphelele. Nizongivumela yini nginisize ninqobe ukudangala ukuze nibambe ithemba lenu engqondweni? Niwakhohliwe yini amazwi ami, zithandwa zami? Manje kuhlala ukholo, ithemba nothando, kodwa okukhulu kunakho konke uthando. Ngalokho nginithuma ukuba nithande abalahlekileyo, ngoba izikhathi eziningi kungubuhle bukaNkulunkulu obuhola abantu ekuphendukeni.

Ngakho-ke, ngikhonzeni emiphakathini yenu, nikhulekele labo abalahlekelwe yikho konke ngoba ngenkathi besaphefumula kungase kube ukuthi ukulahlekelwa kwaleli zwe kuyobenza bazuze okuphakade konke. Shumayelani ezonini nilethe insindiso kwabalahlekileyo, futhi khumbulani ukuthi ningamanxusa ami zithandwa zami, nigcwalise ubizo lwenu kimi, nihlale niqinile kulesi sikhathi sokugcina. Ngakho-ke nginithuma ukuba nithande omakhelwane benu futhi ninikeze ukudla nosizo kulabo abaswele. Ngisho isiphuzo samanzi noma ipuleti lokudla, nikezani noma yini eningayinika ukusiza labo abahluphekayo ningangabazi lutho. Futhi uma kwenzeka nihlupheka kanye nabalahlekileyo, bathandeni ngokuphelele nibonise ubufakazi bami namandla, ningikhulumele ngezindebe zokukholwa ezisuka enhliziyweni egcwele ukholo, ithemba, nothando!

Ngakho ngiyanibuza, niyesaba yini ukusiza izoni? Aniqondi yini ukuthi lapho ngithunyelwe kuphela ezimvini ezilahlekileyo zendlu yakwa-Israyeli, ngachitha isikhathi sami nabathelisi nezoni na? Nikhohliwe yini ukuthi nake naphila izimpilo zomkhuba wesono na? Nikhohliwe yini ukuthi ngingukuphela komuntu ongenasono, iWundlu likaNkulunkulu? Ngakho-ke abahlengiweyo beNkosi mabasho kanjalo, labo engibahlengile esandleni sesitha. Thandani, thandani, thandani kuze kube sekuphefumuleni kwenu kokugcina, ngoba kungcono ukufa ukhonza othandweni lukaKristu kunokuba ninyenyezwe ngokwesaba ngenxa yemizamo edabukisayo yezinto ezibuthakathaka nezimpofu, engizinqobile ngokuphelele futhi nganikeza ukunqoba kubo bonke abangenza iNkosi yabo yaphakade. Ngakho ningathandi izimpilo zenu kuze kube sekupheleni, nilandele isibonelo sami senkonzo engenakwesaba phambi kobubi nokufa okunqotshwe ngokuphelele uthando lwaphakade lukaNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade, u-El Shaddai!

Ungesabi, ngoba nginawe; ungapheli amandla, ngoba nginguNkulunkulu wakho. Ngiyokomeleza, ngikusize, futhi ngiyokusekela ngesandla sokunene sokulunga kwami. Ngakho wozani kimi bantwana bami, niphumule othandweni lwami ngani, futhi ngizoduduza imiphefumulo yenu, ngiphilise amanxeba enu, futhi ngizoniqinisa ebuntekentekeni benu ukuze nibe nokokunika labo abanidingayo, ngoba nithunyelwe kanjalo. Ngoba yinina manxusa ami eningakhethelwe futhi nabizelwe enkazimulweni yoMbuso wami kuphela, kodwa futhi nokuhluphekela igama lami.

Ngiqinisile, ngiqinisile, ningabahlanganyeli bokuhlupheka kwami, landelani isibonelo sami kuphela, ningapheli amandla ehoreni lenu lokuphelelwa yithemba. Ngoba nginani isinyathelo ngasinye sendlela, futhi yimi, iNkosi uJesu Kristu, engihlanza imiphefumulo yenu emlilweni wami wokucwenga, nakuba niyizitsha zobumba ezandleni zombumbi, imililo yokucwenga iyokwenza ubuso benu bube njengegetshe ukuze ningephulwa emandleni ami, nokho nisesebuthakathakeni benyama yenu. Nokho, kunjalo ukuthi ebuthakathakeni benyama enesono uthando lwami lupheleliswa kini njengoba nginisondeza kimi futhi nginilungiselela ngokuqhubekayo izinhloso zenu zaphakade. Ngakho-ke, kuyinto elungile ukuba abahlengiweyo bahlupheke ngenxa yegama lami ngoba nganikela ngakho konke engiyikho emhlatshelweni obuhlungu ukuze niphile phambi kukaYahweh, uNkulunkulu omkhulu onamandla onke. Qondani ukuthi ezilingweni nasekuhluphekeni kwenu anilethi nje umusa wami wensindiso kulabo abaswele, kodwa futhi nisebenzela insindiso yenu siqu ngokwesaba nangokuthuthumela ukuze nginilethe emvuzweni wenu ophelele, kanjalo-ke ithemba lenu selizogcwaliseka!

Mhlawumbe nike nezwa ngokukhuthazela kukaJobe? Indlela uJobe ayeyindoda ethembekile eyafikelwa yizikhathi ezinzima futhi eyahlaselwa ngokungafanele nguSathane. Mhlawumbe nike nezwa ngokuphela kukaJobe, ngoba uJobe wayengeyena umuntu ongalungile owanqotshwa ngububi, kodwa wayeyindoda elungile eyathola isihawu, eyanqoba ububi ngokuhle ngokulandela amazwi kaYahweh nokuhlala ekulungeni ngisho naphambi kokushushiswa okubi. Kanjalo uYahweh wambuyisela uJobe ngokuphindwe kabili konke okwakuthathwe kuye ngokungafanele, futhi uYahweh wamnxephezela uJobe ngezinkinga zakhe nokushushiswa. Bhekani, sibabala njengababusisiweyo abakhuthazelayo. Nizwile ngokubekezela kukaJobe, futhi nikwazi ukuphela iNkosi eyakwenza. Ngoba iNkosi inesihe esikhulu futhi inomusa.

Ngakho kusele ukholo, ithemba nothando, futhi okukhulu kunakho konke uthando. Ngakho qondani zithandwa zami ukuthi kwabathembekileyo ithemba selizogcwaliseka ngokuphelele njengoba nihlwithelwa ekuphepheni kwamazulu ekwahlulelweni okuzayo. Yizwani izwi lami, niqonde uthando lwami ngani nina babiziweyo, abathembekileyo, nabakhethiweyo bami, wona umzimba wami nomakoti wami! Ngakho-ke, phumulani othandweni lwami ngoba ngisho nasebunzimeni obukhulu uthando lwami luhlala luqinile futhi alunakunqotshwa, ngoba ngimunye ebumbanweni nasenhlosweni noBaba, futhi uthando lwaphakade lukaYahweh engilunika bonke abayizindlalifa zokuphila okuphakade nabo bonke abeza kuJesu waseNazaretha ukuze basindiswe.

Qondani ukuthi akekho umuntu ongeza kuYahweh ngaphandle kokuba eze ngami, uKristu Jesu. Kunjalo-ke ukuthi uthando lukaYahweh alunakunqotshwa ngoba uYahweh akanakunqotshwa, ngakho-ke nami anginqotshwa, ngoba nginqobile. Futhi ngiyiNkosi yaphakade yensindiso, yena lowo uYahweh amenze iNkosi yamakhosi noMbusi wababusi, futhi unginike igama elikhulu kunanoma iliphi igama, ukuba ngegama likaJesu Kristu wonke amadolo aguqe ezulwini nasemhlabeni nangaphansi komhlaba. Qondani ukuthi abawileyo bayanesaba nina zithandwa zami, futhi ukwesaba okukhulu kubamba izinhliziyo zabo ngenxa yokwahlulelwa kwabo okuzayo okungalibali.

Ngiyaninika umyalo futhi ngiyanikhuthaza, bantwana bami, ukuba nithathe uthando lwami niliyise kuleli zwe eligulayo nelifayo nelahlulelwe kulesi sikhathi sokugcina ngaphambi kokuba inhlekelele iwele ngokuphelele. Ngithi kini, philisani abagulayo, vusani abafileyo, hlanzani abanochoko, khiphani amadimoni, ngoba okholwa kimi imisebenzi engayenza emhlabeni naye uyoyenza, nemisebenzi emikhulu impela ngoba sengiye kuBaba wethu. Hambani ngothando olukhulu nokuthobeka, nihlangabezane nalabo abaswele ngamandla kaKristu engizowavuselela ngokunamandla kini. Ngoba kukuphi kini, zitsha zami zobumba, lapho kuhlala khona amandla obukhulu bukaNkulunkulu okuKristu okukini. Khulumani nezinkinga enibhekene nazo ngokholo. Yibani nokholo kuYahweh, nokholo kuYeshua HaMashiach, nithi kuleyo ntaba: SUKA UPHONSWE OLWANDLE! Futhi njengoba nenza kanjalo ngokuthembeka, ngizonika izibonakaliso eziningi, izimangaliso, nezimangaliso ukuba zenziwe ngezandla zabathembekileyo.

Yibani nesibindi futhi nibe namaqhawe, ngoba ngiyiNgonyama yesizwe sakwaJuda enilwelayo. Ngiyanincenga futhi ngiyanicindezela ngothando lukaNkulunkulu olukuKristu Jesu iNkosi yenu ukuba nikhuthazelele ngokuzithandela igama lami futhi nilethe abalahlekileyo ekhaya kuBaba wethu ngisho nakulesi sikhathi sokugcina. Ngiyanincenga nina bakhethiweyo bami, bekani isibonelo ngoba ningabathembekileyo nabakhethiweyo bami enginibumbile osizini, ukuze ebuthakathakeni nasekonakaleni kwenyama yenu nibonakalise amandla kaNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade, uYahweh uqobo, njengoba nihamba enkonzweni nasegunyeni elikhulu niya kulabo enginithuma ukuba nibakhonze.

Nginani isinyathelo ngasinye sendlela.

Ngimunye ebumbanweni nasenhlosweni noBaba.

NgiyiNkosi yaphakade yensindiso.

Ngingulowo uYahweh amenzé iNkosi yamakhosi noMbusi wababusi.

NgiyiNgonyama yesizwe sakwaJuda enilwelayo.

Mina, Yeshua HaMashiach, ngithumele lawa mazwi ngenceku yami, futhi ngenceku yami amazwi ami athunyelwe ukuze kulalelwe futhi kukhuthazwe bonke labo la mazwi abafinyelelayo. Futhi amazwi ami azofinyelela emehlweni, ezindlebeni, nasezinhliziyweni zalabo athunyelwe kubo. Imani niqine zithandwa zami ngoba umvuzo wenu usemnyango wenu, kuphela ningapheli amandla. Phumulani othandweni lwami, niphumule emisebenzini yami, seniyekile eyenu, manje yisikhathi sokuhlengwa kwenu, futhi manje yisikhathi sensindiso yenu ephelele, futhi ngiyeza ngani zithandwa zami. Ngizweni, nizwe namazwi ami, ngeke nginishiye futhi ngeke nginilahle nina bathembekileyo bami abakuKristu, bonke labo abalalela izwi lami. Bhekani emazulwini, ngoba ukubonakala kwami sekuzogqashuka esibhakabhakeni ngesikhathi nangehora eningalilindele. Ngiyeza ngani nina bathembekileyo bami, nikulungele na?
