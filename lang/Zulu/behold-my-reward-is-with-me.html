Ubufakazi Obumbaxambili: IBandla
Emisebenzini KaPawulu Umphostoli
May 15, 2024

Bhekani, Umvuzo Wami Unami!

Kuwe Makoti wami othembekileyo, othandekayo, ongithande ngenhliziyo yakho yonke. Uyinhlamvu yeso lami, futhi masinyane ngiyeza kini, ngokushesha kunokuba nicabanga. Ngokukholeka kwenu, kukhona umvuzo omkhulu emazulwini eniwulungiselelweyo. Bhekani! Iso alikabonanga, nendlebe ayikezwanga, futhi akukangenanga enhliziyweni yomuntu, izinto uNkulunkulu azilungiselele labo abamthandayo. Kunjalo-ke namaqiniso amangalisayo aphathelene nawe, sithandwa sami, ngokuba niyokwabelana nami ngefa lami njengokufakaza kokuthembeka kwenu esihlalweni sami sokwahlulela, yona kanye leyo Bema enkulu, lapho ngiyokwahlulela khona imisebenzi yalowo nalowo wababiziweyo nabakhethiweyo nabakholwayo bami.

Kulabo abamisebenzi yabo isha, bayakulahlekelwa, kepha bona uqobo bayakusindiswa, kunjengokungathi kungomlilo; kanti kulabo abamisebenzi yabo imiyo, bayakwamukeliswa ingcebo eningi, ngokuba usuku seluyokumemezela lokho maduzane. Ngokuba lokhu akusiwona umqondo ongaziwa kini, kodwa ngulowo enawufunda ebufakazini babaseKorinte encwadini yesibili kaPawulu: Ngokuba thina sonke simelwe ukubonakaliswa phambi kwesihlalo sokwahlulela sikaKristu, ukuze yilowo nalowo amukele okwenziwe esemzimbeni, njengalokho akwenzileyo, noma kuhle noma kubi. Ngakho-ke, njengoba sazi ukwesabeka kweNkosi, siyancenga abantu; kodwa senziwe sabonakala kuNkulunkulu, futhi ngiyethemba ukuthi, senziwe sabonakala nakunembeza benu.

Ngokuba unembeza wakho uyofakazelana namazwi akho lapho maduzane uzolandisa ngawe phambi koMahluleli olungileyo, mina kanye, uJesu Kristu. Abanye bayokuma phambi kwami bethule imisebenzi yabo eyoshiswa ngumlilo wocwengisisayo, okuyoba namahloni nokulahlekelwa. Abanye bayokuma beqondile phambi kwami, bezihlanze ngegazi lami lokuhlawulela, lapho niyongethulela khona imisebenzi enganinika yona ukuba niyenze. Ngokuba ukungena ekuphumuleni kwami kusho ukuphumula emisebenzini yenu. Kanjalo-ke kulabo abaphumule emisebenzini yami kukhona umvuzo omkhulu kwabakholwayo. Kodwa nonke niyosindiswa, futhi akekho noyedwa kini ohambe ngokuphelele phambi kwami, kanjalo usuku luyokumemezela lokho. Ungesabi, ngiyokuvuza ngokupheleleyo, ngokuba yonke imisebenzi emikhulu ongenzele yona ngasese iyokwenziwa ibonakale, kanjalo usuku luyokumemezela lokho.

Kungani izinhliziyo zenu zikhathazwa yilawa mazwi? Anifundanga yini encwadini yokuqala kaJohane, lapho ngamenza wabhala? Ngokuba uma inhliziyo yethu isilahla, uNkulunkulu mkhulu kunenhliziyo yethu, futhi wazi zonke izinto. Bathandekayo, uma inhliziyo yethu ingasilahli, khona-ke sinokuzethemba kuNkulunkulu. Futhi futhi: Uma sithi asinasono, siyazikhohlisa, neqiniso alikho kithi. Uma sizivuma izono zethu, uthembekile, futhi ulungile ukusithethelela izono zethu, nokusihlambulula kukho konke ukungalungi. Ngakho, vumani izono zenu kimi nsuku zonke futhi kaningi ngendlela enithanda ngayo, nizigeze egazini lami lokuhlawulela.

Ngokuba ngingumkhulumeli wenu, futhi nathengwa ngomhlatshelo wami njengePhasika eliphelele ukuze ngiqede izono, ngenze ukubuyisana ngenxa yobubi, futhi ngilethe ukulunga okuphakade. Bheka, Makoti wami! Izikhathi zokubuyiselwa seziseduze, ngokuba uPetru waprofetha ngalesi sikhathi lapho elapha indoda eSangweni Elihle Lethempeli: Futhi uyothuma uJesu Kristu, owashunyayelwa kini ngaphambili. Yena amazulu kumelwe amamukele, kuze kufike izikhathi zokubuyiselwa kwazo zonke izinto. Manje sezifikile izikhathi zokubuyiselwa, Makoti wami omuhle, ngokuba nimsulwa phambi kwami futhi nihlanjululiwe kukho konke ukungalungi ngegazi leWundlu, ngokuba ngiyiWundlu likaNkulunkulu! Ngakho-ke, vumani izono zenu ngokwethembeka.

Ngakho-ke ukwahlulela, ukubuyisela, ukulahlekelwa, nomvuzo omkhulu konke kuqala endlini kaNkulunkulu. Ngokuba maduzane, niyobuthaniselwa ndawonye emafwini ningihlangabeze emoyeni, ngokuba ngiyokwehla ezulwini ngokumemeza, nangezwi lengelosi enkulu, bese kukhala icilongo lokugcina! Qhubekani nibheke amazulu nigcine, ngokuba uNkulunkulu wenze izibonakaliso eziningi ezinamandla emazulwini, ememezela ngisho nomshado womkhwenyana nomakoti wakhe, yebo, isivumelwano saphakade phakathi kwami nomakoti wami, esizokwenzeka maduze. Ngakho vumani izono zenu ningicele ukuba nginikhombise izono enizikhohliwe ukuze nizivume nazo; futhi ngaphezu kwakho konke, qinani othandweni lukaNkulunkulu, ngokuba uthando lukaNkulunkulu lusibekela izono eziningi. Ngakho-ke ningesabi, ngoba akunakwenzeka ukuba nizivume zonke.

Ngokwahlulela: ngoba nisukile ekufeni nangena ekuphileni kimi, iNkosi yenu evusiweyo uJesu Kristu. Ngokuba ngadela ukuphila kwami; akekho owakuthatha. Ngakunikela ngokuzithandela futhi ngingaphinda ngikwenze uma kudingeka. Kodwa asikho isidingo sento enjalo, ngokuba ngatshelani amaHeberu: Ngoba akunakwenzeka ukuba igazi lezinkunzi nelezimbuzi lisuse izono. Kodwa lo muntu, emva kokuba enikele umhlatshelo owodwa ngenxa yezono kuze kube phakade, wahlala phansi ngakwesokunene sikaNkulunkulu. Makoti wami, angiyukunahlulela ngokwezono zenu; ngokuba bonke abakuKristu balungisisiwe, yebo bangcwelisiwe, benziwa balunga kimi, uKristu Jesu, oyophila abuse phakade, ngokuba umbuso wami awunaphelo. Ngakho-ke nikhululiwe ezonweni zenu; ngakho-ke, aniyikuba sekwahlulelweni kwezoni kodwa ekwahlulelweni kwabalungileyo, ngokuba ukwahlulela kuqala endlini kaNkulunkulu.

Ngokubuyisela: ngokuba ngiyobuyisela, isho iNkosi. Baningi kini abashiye izindlu, amazwe, imindeni futhi badela izinto eziningi kakhulu; futhi abaningi phakathi koMakoti wami badela ngisho nokuphila kwabo, bangabafelukholo bami. Ngakho-ke kuyoba khona ukubuyiselwa kwezinto ezideliweyo ngisho nezilahlekileyo. Ngokuba kwaba yini isiphetho sikaJobe: Futhi iNkosi yaphendula ukuthunjwa kukaJobe, lapho ekhulekela abangane bakhe: futhi iNkosi yanika uJobe okuphindwe kabili kunalokho ayenakho ngaphambili. Nezwi likaJakobe umfowethu liyasho futhi: Bhekani, sibabiza ngokuthi bajabulile abakhuthazelayo. Nizwile ngokubekezela kukaJobe, futhi nibonile isiphetho seNkosi: ukuthi iNkosi inesihe esikhulu nomusa omkhulu. Unjalo-ke noYihlo, futhi nginjalo nami, ngokuba ngingowoMkhulu onguNGINGUYE!

Ngokulahlekelwa: ngokuba abaningi bayolahlekelwa, ngokuba ngishilo mayelana nesihlalo sami sokwahlulela kwabaseKorinte: Ngokuba thina sonke simelwe ukubonakaliswa phambi kwesihlalo sokwahlulela sikaKristu, ukuze yilowo nalowo amukele okwenziwe esemzimbeni, njengalokho akwenzileyo, noma kuhle noma kubi. Futhi abaseKorinte bayafakaza futhi: Umsebenzi womuntu ngamunye uyokwenziwa ubonakale. Ngokuba usuku luyokumemezela, ngokuba luyokwambulwa ngomlilo, nomlilo uyovivinya umsebenzi womuntu ngamunye ukuthi unjani. Uma umsebenzi womuntu usha, uyolahlekelwa: kodwa yena uqobo uyosindiswa: nokho kunjengokungathi kungomlilo.

Ngomvuzo omkhulu: ngokuba abaseKorinte bakhuluma futhi: Iso alikabonanga, nendlebe ayikezwanga, futhi akukangenanga enhliziyweni yomuntu, izinto uNkulunkulu azilungiselele labo abamthandayo. Bathembekile nabakhethiweyo bami, aninakukuqonda ukugubha okukhulu namadili enilungiselelwe wona nguBaba emazulwini; ngokuba niza ekugujweni, futhi ingcebo enkulu ibekelwe nina emazulwini. (Ngokuba nibuyela ekhaya kimi, uKristu Jesu, nakuBaba wethu, uYahweh, uNkulunkulu waphakade.) Futhi futhi: uma umsebenzi womuntu uma, awakhile phezu kwawo, uyokwamukela umvuzo. Nginemivuzo eminingi kakhulu engizonipha yona, nefa lami linami; ngakho-ke ningesabi, ngokuba niyokwabelana ngefa lami; futhi lapho ngikhona, nani niyokuba khona, ngokuba izikhathi zokwehlukana seziphelile.

Imiqhele yomvuzo negunya ilindele nina. Ngibale eminye emibhalweni: umqhele ongokonakali, umqhele wokwethaba, umqhele wokulunga, umqhele wenkazimulo, nomqhele wokuphila.

Kulabo abagijime umjaho, ngisho nezinkambo zenkonzo engibabekele zona, bayokwamukela umqhele ongokonakali: ngokuba niyobe nizithobile emoyeni wami, futhi niyobe nizikhuze njenganoma yimuphi umgijimi. Kulabo abawine umjaho ngokomthetho kukhona umqhele ongokonakali.

Kukhona umqhele wokwethaba. Ngokuba liyini ithemba lethu, noma injabulo, noma umqhele wokwethaba? Akusini yini phambi kweNkosi yethu uJesu Kristu ekufikeni kwayo? Ngokuba, nina niyinkazimulo nenjabulo yethu. Kanjalo-ke siyoba nenjabulo enkulu, futhi nonke niyoba senkazimulweni nikhazinyuliswe kimi! Kulabo abasebenze ngokwethembeka ukuguqula nokukhulisa abafowenu kukhona umqhele wokwethaba.

Kukhona umqhele wokulunga kulabo abaqeda izinkambo zabo nakulabo abathanda ukubonakala kwami. Ngakho-ke qhubekani nibheke phezulu, ngokuba ngiseduze futhi nginalindelo elikhulu ngokubonakala kwami. Ngokuba ukulangazelela ukubonakala kwami nakho kuletha umvuzo omkhulu. Kulabo abalangazelela ukubonakala kwami kukhona umqhele wokulunga.

Kukhona umqhele wenkazimulo kulabo abondle umhlambi kaNkulunkulu. Ngokuba uPetru wabhala: Yalusani umhlambi kaNkulunkulu ophakathi kwenu, niwengamele, kungengokucindezelwa, kodwa ngokuzithandela: kungengokwenzuzo engcolileyo, kodwa ngomqondo olungileyo. Kulabo abangazibusi phezu kwefa likaNkulunkulu futhi abangashukunyiswa yinzuzo engcolileyo futhi abangikhonze ngokwethembeka ngokuthobeka nangenhliziyo efanele yothando kukhona umqhele wenkazimulo.

Kukhona umqhele wokuphila engiyowunika abafelukholo bami, kulabo abahlupheka ngokuhlushwa nokushushiswa ngenxa yegama lami nakulabo ababekezelele ngesineke amaqhinga namasu kasathane. Kulabo abanqobe lezi zinto ngokwethembeka kukhona umqhele wokuphila.

Niyokwenziwa futhi nifane nami, u-Adamu wokugcina futhi niyofana nami, futhi niyongibona njengoba nginjalo, kanjalo nani niyokuba njalo. Bhekani! Ngizodala kabusha imizimba emisha kini engafani nanoma yini eningayicabanga. Bhekani amarekhodi emibhalweni lapho ngabonakala khona kwabathembekile bami ngaphambi kokuba nginyukele emazulwini. Ngizowulungisa lo mzimba ngokukhethekile kini, futhi uyophatha amakhono enu, impumelelo yenu; futhi niyomangala kakhulu.

Aniqondi yini lokho engenza uPawulu akubhalela abaseKorinte? Kukhona nemizimba yasezulwini, nemizimba yasemhlabeni: Kepha inkazimulo yabasezulwini ingenye, nenkazimulo yabasemhlabeni ingenye. Kukhona inkazimulo eyodwa yelanga, nenye yenyanga, nenye yezinkanyezi: ngokuba inkanyezi iyahluka kwenye inkanyezi ngenkazimulo. Kunjalo nokuvuka kwabafileyo: ihlwanyelwa ekonakaleni, ivuswa ekungonakalini. Bantwana bami, niyoba namakhono amaningi eningakaze niwaphuphuthe. Yize ngamunye wenu eyohluka komunye, nonke niyohamba ngokuphelele kimi, ngokuba ngizonenza amakhosi nabapristi phambi kukaNkulunkulu wethu!

Kuyoba nezinto eziningi kakhulu eningasayikuthwaliswa zona. Ngeke kusaba khona ukugula, ngeke kusaba khona isono kini, ngeke kusaba khona ukufa kini, ngeke kusaba khona ukwesaba, akukho ukukhathazeka; ngokuba nonke niyodalwa kabusha ngokupheleleyo kimi, uKristu Jesu iNkosi. Ngakho-ke ngithi kini, Makoti wami, yibani ngabanqobi, ngokuba ngizonika imivuzo yabanqobi, futhi ingaphezu kwemicabango namaphupho enu amakhulu. Ngokuba umuthi wokuphila, owawuthela izithelo eziyishumi nambili, uthela isithelo sawo inyanga ngayinye: namaqabunga omuthi ayengawokuphulukisa izizwe. Futhi ngeke kusaba khona isiqalekiso, kodwa isihlalo sobukhosi sikaNkulunkulu, neseWundlu siyokuba kuso, nezinceku zakhe ziyomkhonza. Futhi bayobona ubuso bakhe, negama lakhe liyokuba emabunzini abo. Futhi ngeke kube khona ubusuku lapho, futhi abadingi isibani, noma ukukhanya kwelanga, ngokuba iNkosi uNkulunkulu iyabanika ukukhanya, futhi bayobusa kuze kube phakade naphakade. Ngakho-ke ningesabi, Makoti wami othembekile, umvuzo omkhulu sekuseduze ukuba wenzeke kini. Ngokuba uNkulunkulu akasiye ongalungile, ukuba akhohlwe umsebenzi wenu nomshikashika wothando, eniwubonisile egameni lakhe, ngokuba nikhonzile abaNgcwele, futhi niyakhonza.

Ngiyeza kini, ngokushesha kunokuba nicabanga.

Ngingumkhulumeli wenu.

Nginguye futhi nani niyokuba njalo, ngokuba niyongibona njengoba nginjalo.

Ngiyeza, nomvuzo wami unami kwabakholwayo.

Nginguye oyokunika ifa lenu ngokugcwele, ngokuba linikelwe kimi.

Nginguye oyokunika imizimba emisha esikhundleni somzimba wesono.

Nginguye, ngokuba ngingowoMkhulu onguNGINGUYE!

Bhekani ukubonakala kwami emafwini, njengoba izibonakaliso ziyongimemezela ngaphambi kokuba ukukhanya kwami kugqame esibhakabhakeni. Mina, uYeshua HaMashiach, ngithumele lawa maZwi.
