Ufakazi Ombaxa: Israyeli
Okubabayo Nokumnandi
uMasingana 06, 2025

Uhlelo LukaMelkhizedeki: Ukudluliselwa Kwesambatho, Ukumiswa Kwalo, Nokubuyiselwa Kwaso

Kusuka kuYahweh, uMINA OMKHULU ENGINGUYE:

Zwanini Amazwi kaYahweh uNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade futhi ningivumele nginikeze ukuhlakanipha kohlakaniphileyo phakathi kwenu. Ngalokho-ke ngininika ukuqonda ngoHlelo lukaMelkhizedeki, lolo hlelo uKristu Jesu amiselwe kulo njengoMpristi oMkhulu, ngisho nangokohlelo lukaMelkhizedeki kuze kube phakade! Nokho kunjalo ukuthi ngenxa yezono zesintu, izindlela zami zikhohlakele, nezivumelwano eziningi zilahliwe, nobumnyama obukhulu nezindlela zokungazi sezithathe umhlaba ngenxa yamanga alabo abawayo. Futhi lokhu kungenxa yamanga enziwa ngamabomu nalabo abafuna ukucindezela iqiniso ezindleleni zabo zokungalungi nasemangeni abo okukhohlisa lapho umhlaba uthathwe khona inkohliso yabo enkulu esefike emhlabeni wonke. Ngisho inkohliso ngokwengxenye ecabanga ukuthi konke ukuphila kuvela kokuqhuma okukhulu okucatshangwayo okunikezwe ukufundisa isintu amanga alowo owileyo ozama ukufihla uYahweh, uMINA OMKHulu ENGINGUYE, emhlabeni engiwudalile ngesandla sami.

Futhi ngiyiNkosi yemiMoya, futhi nginguMdala weziNsuku, ngempela, ngempela nginguye owayekhona ngaphambi kokuba izinsuku zenziwe futhi nginguye owadala izingelosi ezingcwele okulotshwe ngazo ukuthi: Wenza imimoya izithunywa zakhe, nezikhonzi zakhe ilangabi lomlilo, ngisho nalapho indalo yadalwa futhi yakhiphwa engcebweni yenhliziyo yami yaphakade esebenza zonke izinto ngokwesiluleko sentando yami. Ngoba ngeqiniso ngangazi ukuthi u-Adamu wayezona ngokuzithandela ngisho nangaphambi kokuba u-Adamu one nokuthi ukonakala kwakuzowa ezulwini kubantu balabo abawayo. Ngakho-ke, ngahlela icebo lami laphakade lokuhlengwa ngokugcwele kuka-Adamu elibekwe ngokuphelele ebusweni bukaJesu Kristu onguYeshuwa HaMashiach eTestamenteni lakhe Elisha lokulunga neqiniso!

Futhi ngokufana nomfanekiso ka-Adamu wokugcina isintu siyohlengwa sibe senjabulweni yaphakade nokuthula, lapho siyogcwalisa khona ngothando izinhloso zaphakade zikaYahweh, uMINA OMKHULU ENGINGUYE. Ngeqiniso, indalo yami yadalwa futhi yenziwa ngothando ukuze indalo yami ize kimi ngothando ngakho konke engibanike khona othandweni lwami ngabo. Futhi ukuze indalo yami ihambe nami isandla nesandla ezinhlosweni zami zaphakade ekugcwalisekeni kwentando yami yaphakade, futhi bona bayoba nenjabulo ephelele negcwele, ukuthula, nokuphila okuphakade ngisho nalapho beholelwa ekuthuleni ngesandla soMalusi wami oMkhulu, uJesu waseNazaretha, okungekho muntu ongasondela ngaphandle kwakhe kuYahweh, uMINA OMKHULU ENGINGUYE, kusukela manje, ngoba ngishilo kanjalo.

Futhi labo abawayo basakaze amanga abo ebusweni bendalo yami, umphumela waba ukuthi umhlaba manje ugcwele izinkohliso zabo, lapho belalele khona ukuze bakhohlise. Ngisho nezinkohliso ezifike ngokugcwele ngenxa yemfihlakalo yobubi eyasinda kuzamcolo kaNowa ngolwazi lwamanga lwabawayo abalunikeza umuntu wemvelo ukuze abhujiswe ngokuphelele. Akunandaba, ngoba ngithumela bonke labo abathokoza ngokungalungi inkohliso enamandla ukuze bonke balahlwe abenqaba ulwazi lweqiniso bethanda amanga alabo abawayo. Nokho kunjalo, ukuthi umuntu wemvelo ngokungazi ufuna ukuzimela ngecala lakhe lokuzenzela ukulunga phambi kokubuka kwami kwaphakade nokulungileyo, ngakho-ke, qondani ukuthi ayikho inyama elungisisiwe emehlweni ami ngenxa yokonakala egazini lenu ngenxa yesono enyameni. Ngoba ukuphila kwenyama kusegazini!

Ngenxa yalesi sizathu indodana kaYahweh yabonakaliswa kubakhileyo bomhlaba, ukuze ngomhlatshelo wokuhlenga wewundlu likaNkulunkulu, u-Adamu ahlengwe ngensindiso ephelele ekwahlulelweni kwakhe okuzayo kubo bonke abalalela isivumelwano sami sokuthula phakathi kwami nesintu onguJesu waseNazaretha owenziwe waba umoya ophilayo ngamandla esandla sami. Futhi-ke, ngeNgonyama yesiZwe sakwaJuda, engiyithumela emhlabeni ngokulunga ukuze yahlulele futhi ilwe, ngaleyo ndlela indalo iyobuyiselwa ngenkani isuswa ezandleni zabahlali abahlala behlubuka futhi befuna ukumisa intando yami yaphakade, okuyinto engenakwenzeka phakade, ngoba ngubani okhona ongamelana nesandla sami esinamandla? Futhi ngobuningi babo, nobubi babo, namacebo abo, namaqhinga abo, ngisho nasebuchwephesheni babo obungenamsebenzi (abaziqhayisa ngabo) abawayo banqotshwe ngokuphelele ngomhlatshelo wewundlu likaNkulunkulu, elanikela ngegazi lalo elingenasono njengoMpristi oMkhulu phakade ngokoHlelo lukaMelkhizedeki ngokulalela okuphelele kuye owamthumayo, ngisho uYahweh, uMINA OMKHULU NAPHAKADE ENGINGUYE!

Futhi umbuso wakhe awusoze waba nasiphelo, ngoba ngokulunga konke nobungcwele beqiniso uwuthengile umhlaba ngegazi lakhe lokuhlenga ukuze ukubusa okulungileyo kufike emhlabeni ngesikhathi sako nokuba sijabulele usuku lokuphumula (ngoba iminyaka eyinkulungwane kuYahweh injengosuku nosuku lunjengeminyaka eyinkulungwane emehlweni ami), ngisho usuku lokuphumula lwesabatha ngaphambi kokuba leyo nyoka endala iphonswe phakade emalangabini osizini oluphelele nolwaphakade ingaphinde ikhunjulwe futhi ngaphambi kokuba leso sihlalo sobukhosi esikhulu esimhlophe simiswe. Futhi kungomsebenzi wompristi omkhulu woHlelo lukaMelkhizedeki ukuthi u-Adamu uhlengiwe ekonakaleni ngoJesu waseNazaretha owanikela ngomhlatshelo wakhe waphakade ebuhlungwini obukhulu ukuze akhokhe isijeziso sikaMose ukuze imithetho yami yaphakade yaneliswe ngayo indalo yami yaphakade ebuswa ngokuphelele ngensindiso elungileyo.

Futhi ngempela kuyokwenzeka ukuthi ngiyodala izulu elisha nomhlaba omusha ngoba izulu lokuqala, nomhlaba wokuqala kuyodlula, futhi ngeke kusaba khona ulwandle; ngeke kusaba khona oyoke anikhathaze phakade. Futhi kuyokwenzeka ngokuzuma manje ukuthi abanqobi bami bayodla ifa lazo zonke izinto, futhi ngiyoba uNkulunkulu wabo, bona babe ngamadodana ami. Kepha abesabayo nabangakholwayo, nabanyanyekayo nababulali, nabaphingi nabathakathi, nabakhonza izithombe, nabo bonke abaqamba amanga bayoba nesabelo sabo echibini elivutha umlilo nesibabuli, okungukufa kwesibili.

Ngakho-ke, ngubani okufanele nimesabe bantwana bami, ngoba nenziwe abantwana bakaNkulunkulu ophilayo ngemisebenzi kaYeshuwa HaMashiach onguJesu Kristu, ngisho yena engimnike amandla ngaphezu kwakho konke endalweni yami, ngaphandle kwasesihlalweni sami sobukhosi lapho ngigcina khona igunya lami, ngakho-ke ngubani ongamelana nesandla esinamandla sikaKristu Jesu, iNkosi evusiweyo neyenyukileyo? Ngempela, ngempela, njengoba bekunjalo, futhi kunjalo, kuyoba njalo phakade naphakade ukuthi uYahweh noYeshuwa HaMashiach munye ebunye nasenhlosweni futhi ngimnike amandla ngokugcwele kukho konke ukulunga futhi nguye engimnike ukuphatha phezu kwayo yonke impi yasezulwini, futhi ubusa ngokulalela okuphelele entandweni yami yaphakade. Ngalokho ngininika ukuqonda ubuqili obufike emhlabeni, ngisho amanga alabo abawayo abangafuna ukufihla iqiniso lensindiso yenu neqiniso leNkosi yenu yaphakade, uKristu Jesu!

Ngakho-ke, qondani ukuthi abafundisi benu abangamaJesuit babangele ubumpumputhe ezinhliziyweni zenu ngokufihla iqiniso lendalo yami nangokunifihlela umlando wenu weqiniso, ukuze nizulazule ebumnyameni nigcwalisa izinkanuko zabo ezimbi, ngisho nokuba amajuba ami ahlanganyele nabo efeni labo laphakade lokusha nokugingqika kwesibabuli ngoba injalo inhloso yabo. Futhi kunjalo ukuthi uYahweh, uMINA OMKHULU ENGINGUYE, angikaze ngizishiye ngingenabo ubufakazi. Futhi ngesivumelwano ngasinye engisinikile, nangeTestamente ngalinye engilinikile futhi ngalamukela, angikaze ngizishiye ngaphandle kobufakazi obucacile bemibhalo nesiqondiso esinikezwe ngamazwi abaprofethi bami basendulo, engibamisile. Futhi kunikezwe nangamazwi abaprofethi beTestamente Elisha uYeshuwa HaMashiach abamisile abazomkhonza njengabapristi bakhe abanamandla namakhosi!

Ngoba lunjalo uHlelo lukaMelkhizedeki, ngisho uhlelo lwaphakade olunikezwe isintu, lapho ukubusa u-Adamu okwakufanele akwenze emhlabeni kwakungokwakhe okwakufanele kubuswe nguye omiswe njengompristi omkhulu, inkosi, nomprofethi! Ngoba lwalunjalo (futhi lunjalo) uHlelo lukaMelkhizedeki olunikezwe isintu, ngisho nokuba isintu sibe nokubusa okulungileyo naphezu kwaleyo nyoka endala eyakhohlisa u-Eva ngobuqili bayo. Futhi ngaleyo ndlela uHlelo lukaMelkhizedeki lwaqhubeka, ngisho luhleli phezu kuka-Adamu kusukela ekuqaleni owanikwa lona, kodwa ekufeni kwabo bagcina befile futhi uhlelo lukaMelkhizedeki lwadluliselwa phambili isikhashana, ezinsukwini kusukela ku-Adamu kuya kuJakobe.

Futhi-ke eMhlabeni owawukhona ngaphambi kukazamcolo omkhulu uHlelo lukaMelkhizedeki lwasuka ku-Adamu, lwaya kuKayinani, ku-Enose, ku-Enoke, kuMethusela, kuNowa futhi kanjalo uHlelo lukaMelkhizedeki lwadlula ezinsukwini zangaphambi kukazamcolo omkhulu. Nokho, abafundisi benu abangamaJesuit bayibhalile kabusha imibhalo, futhi benza ubuqili obuningi ukuze bafihle iqiniso lomlando wangempela ka-Adamu ngisho nalapho bezama ukwenza isintu sibe yinhlayiyana yothuli endaweni yabo yamanga ecatshangwa ukuthi yavela ekuqhumeni okukhulu endaweni ethile esikhathini sabo esidlule esicatshangwayo.

Ngoba amaJesuit ashumayela omunye uJesu, ngisho uJesu owenziwe ngomfanekiso kaNimrode waseBabiloni, owayengumbusi wokuqala wohlobo lwakhe ngemva kukazamcolo. Futhi nakuba ababusi bami abalungileyo abamisiweyo kusukela endulo ngaphansi koHlelo lukaMelkhizedeki banikeza isintu ukulunga esikhundleni sesono, ukuhlupheka, ukufa, nesijeziso saphakade. Noma kunjalo, umuntu wemvelo uvame ukukhetha isono, ukuhlupheka, ukufa, nesijeziso saphakade. Noma kunjalo, bonke endalweni yami abanikezwe inkululeko yokuzikhethela bakhululekile ukuzikhethela ukuthi bazophila kuYahweh ngoKristu Jesu yini, noma bakhethe isijeziso sabo saphakade ngenxa yobumnandi besono besikhashana. Futhi kulotshiwe ukuthi bonke abasemhlabeni owawukhona ngaleso sikhathi ngaphandle kwemiphefumulo eyisishiyagalombili basindiswa bephila ngenxa kaNowa, owayeyindoda elungileyo eyabusa umhlaba ngokoHlelo lukaMelkhizedeki isikhashana ngoba ekufeni wafa.

Ngakho-ke, qondani izindlela zalabo abawayo, ukuthi ngenhliziyo yabo yonke bafuna ukuhlanekezela, ukuqamba amanga, nokufihla iqiniso emajubeni ami, ngisho nalabo engibamiselwe ukuphila okuphakade. Futhi kunjalo ukuthi njengoba icebo lami lokusindisa u-Adamu lalembulwa kancane kancane, ngathumela imibhalo engcwele ngomoya wesiprofetho owahlala phezu kwabo, ngisho nomoya wesiprofetho owagcina uzelwe emadodaneni kaNkulunkulu ngokuzalwa okusha okunikezwe eTestamenteni Elisha likaYeshuwa HaMashiach, ngisho nakuye engithokoza ngaye njalo. Futhi njengoba ngembula icebo lami kancane kancane, nganikeza nemibhalo engcwele kancane kancane, futhi uKristu Jesu unikeze imibhalo engcwele eyamukelwe esandleni sami, ngisho yena osebenza zonke izinto ngokwesiluleko sentando yami.

Futhi-ke labo abawayo basebenzela ukonakalisa iqiniso, ngisho ngendlela efanayo naleyo abazonakalisa ngayo bona phakade naphakade. Ngoba bayibhalile kabusha imibhalo, banikeze amaBhayibheli okufunda amanga, banikeze nezichazamazwi namakhonkodensi amanga lapho abaningi bekhohliswa khona ngezincazelo ezingcolile, njengokungathi uYahweh akanakho ukuhlinzeka abahumushi abanekhono bokuhumusha iZwi lami ngoba iZwi lami alinakwephulwa noma lifihlwe, nakuba bezama. Ngempela, kuwudumo lukaYahweh ukufihla indaba nodumo lwamaKhosi ukuyiphenya! Ngakho-ke, nginiyala ukuba niphenye amaqiniso kaYahweh ebusweni bukaYeshuwa HaMashiach!

Ngakho qondani ukuthi labo abakhonza leso sifipha esikhulu, ngisho nabafundisi benu abangamaJesuit, bakhethe ingubo ekhethwe ngokucophelela yamanga ahlobene kancane lapho belalele khona ukuze bakhohlise. Bafihlile ubufakazi obuningi obungcwele futhi babhale kabusha amaBhayibheli amaningi besebenzisa imibhalo yamanga futhi benza amanga amaningi mayelana neqiniso lendalo yami. Futhi ekwazini kwami kwangaphambili nasezahlulelweni zami ngamvumela lowo owileyo ukuba enze kanjalo, ngoba ukube imibhalo yami engcwele yayigadwe futhi yanakekelwa kahle njengoba kwakuyaliwe futhi kuphathisiwe, ubumpumputhe obunjalo babungeke bufezwe. Kodwa zinjalo izindlela zomuntu onesono futhi zinjalo izindlela zalabo abawayo abadukisa isintu ezinkanukweni zaso ezihlala zivutha. Noma kunjalo, iZwi lami alinakwephulwa futhi liyofeza leyo nto elithunyelwe ukuyifeza.

Futhi kulotshiwe kuJasheri ukuthi ngemva kukazamcolo omkhulu isambatho sikaMelkhizedeki sahlala kuNowa futhi ekugcineni sadluliselwa kuShemi owayengowesithupha olandelanayo ukugqoka isambatho futhi ngokuhamba kwesikhathi uhlelo lukaMelkhizedeki lwadluliselwa ku-Abramu owathi ngokuhamba kwesikhathi waba nguBaba wezizwe eziningi, futhi ezindlalifeni zakhe zesithembiso ziya izithembiso zefa ukuze zigcwaliseke ngoba indodana yesigqilakazi ngeke ibe yindlalifa yezithembiso zoMbuso kaNkulunkulu kodwa abalungileyo abaphila ngokukholwa, njengoba u-Abrahama othembekileyo wayephila ezinsukwini zasendulo.

Ngakho-ke, nginazisa incazelo kaMelkisedeki okusho ukuthi iNkosi Yokulunga. Futhi kanjalo imvelaphi yegama iyisiHebheru esihunyushelwe esiNgisini lapho uMelek esho iNkosi khona bese uZedek esho ukulunga, okunenjongo eyinikiweyo nehlosiweyo yokuba yiNkosi Yokulunga. Kanjalo-ke la makhosi okulunga abusile indalo yami, ngisho nabantu abalungileyo ababelindele uMesiya wabo, yena lowo owayezozalwa esizweni sakwaJuda.

Futhi njengoba kulotshiwe kumaHebheru ngesandla sikaPawulu (owayeyisikhonzi sezimfihlakalo zikaKristu Jesu) wabhala wathi: Ngoba lo Melkisedeki wayeyiNkosi yaseSalema, uMpristi kaNkulunkulu Osenyangweni, owahlangana no-Abrahama, lapho ebuya ekubulaleni amakhosi, wambusisa: Owanikwa futhi ngu-Abrahama okweshumi kwakho konke: okokuqala ngokuhunyushwa uyiNkosi Yokulunga: emva kwalokho, futhi uyiNkosi yaseSalema, okungukuthi, iNkosi yokuthula, engenayise, engenanina, engenazihlobo, engenasiqalo sezinsuku zakhe, engenakuphela kokuphila: kodwa ufaniswa neNdodana kaNkulunkulu, futhi uqhubeka enguMpristi kuze kube phakade. Kanjalo-ke isembatho sikaMelkisedeki sadluliselwa ku-Abrahama othembekileyo ekulondolozweni kwaso, uhlelo olwalulondolozelwe futhi luhloselwe uJesu Kristu onguYeshuwa HaMashiyakhi.

Nokho, abaningi benu bakholwe amanga abafundisi benu bamaJesuit abaphumphuthekise abaningi benu ngemfundo ephikisana nemfundo yokuthi la mavesi akhuluma ngoJesu Kristu uqobo lwakhe hhayi ngoHlelo lukaMelkisedeki uqobo. Ngoba kusobala, uJesu waseNazaretha unoYise, uYahweh uMina Enginguye Omkhulu, futhi kusobala uJesu waseNazaretha unonina, uMariya waseNazaretha yaseGalile owathi makube kimi njengezwi lakho, isibonelo saphakade sokholo esinikwe ukuba abathembekileyo bazi izindlela zokholo abahamba ngazo. Ngoba kusobala ukuthi uJesu waseNazaretha unezihlobo okunguye umhlengi wazo, kungani ningawahloniphi amaZwi ami acacile anikezwe emibhalweni ahloselwe ukuniqondisa ngokuphepha kude namanga abo enkohliso? Kepha abaningi benu bayawakholwa la manga kunokucaca nokunemba kwamaZwi ami.

Nokho, kulotshiwe ukuthi uShemi wahlala ethembekile kuNowa, ngoba uHamu wasishiya ngokuphelele isivumelwano sikaNowa wakhohlwa izindlela zikaYahweh, futhi uHamu nabazukulu abaningi bakaNowa bazama ukwakha umuzi wabo omkhulu nombhoshongo lapho umbuso kaNimrode ongakulungile wavuka ukubusa umhlaba ngalezo zinsuku ngokungalungi, ngokwezindlela zabawileyo ayebakhonza, nakuba ngasebenza kaningi ukunika uNimrode ithuba lokubuyela kimi ngokuphenduka, ithuba alenqaba ingunaphakade naphakade.

Nokho kulotshiwe ngokucacile kuJashere mayelana no-Abrama noShemi owadlulisela isembatho ku-Abrama lapho ebulala uKedorlawomere namabutho akhe: Kwathi esebuya ekushayeni lawo makhosi, yena nabantu bakhe badabula iSigodi saseSidimi lapho amakhosi ayelwe khona. UBera inkosi yaseSodoma, kanye nabanye abantu bakhe ababenaye, baphuma emigodini yetela ababewele kuyo, bayohlangabeza u-Abrama nabantu bakhe. U-Adonisedeki (noma uMelkisedeki) inkosi yaseJerusalema, onguye uShemi, waphuma nabantu bakhe eyohlangabeza u-Abrama nabantu bakhe, benesinkwa newayini, bahlala ndawonye esigodini saseMeleki. U-Adonisedeki (noma uMelkisedeki) wabusisa u-Abrama, futhi u-Abrama wamnika okweshumi kwakho konke ayekuphathe empangweni yezitha zakhe, ngoba u-Adonisedeki (noma uMelkisedeki) wayengumpristi phambi kukaNkulunkulu.

Nokho kulotshiwe kuJashere: Izinsuku zikaSara zaba yiminyaka eyikhulu namashumi amabili nesikhombisa, wafa, futhi u-Abrahama wenza isililo esikhulu nesinzima, wenza imikhuba yokulila izinsuku eziyisikhombisa. Bonke abakhileyo ezweni bamduduza u-Abrahama no-Isaka indodana yakhe ngenxa kaSara. Lapho izinsuku zokulila kwabo sezidlulile u-Abrahama wamukisa indodana yakhe u-Isaka, waya endlini kaShemi no-Ebere, ukuyofunda izindlela zeNkosi nemiyalo yayo, u-Abrahama wahlala lapho iminyaka emithathu. Kanjalo-ke isembatho sikaMelkisedeki sadlulela ku-Isaka owadlulisela ilungelo lobuzibulo komncane, ngoba u-Esawu wadelela izinto zikaNkulunkulu wathengisa ubuzibulo bakhe ngesitsha sesobho: Isinqumo uYahweh asamukele ingunaphakade.

Nokho kulotshiwe kuJashere mayelana noHlelo lukaMelkisedeki: Emva kwalokhu uShela indodana ka-Arapakishadi wafa ngalowo nyaka, okungumnyaka weshumi nesishiyagalombili wokuphila kukaJakobe no-Esawu; zonke izinsuku zokuphila kukaShela zaba yiminyaka engamakhulu amane namashumi amathathu nantathu, wafa. Ngaleso sikhathi u-Isaka wathuma indodana yakhe encane uJakobe endlini kaShemi no-Ebere, wafunda imiyalo yeNkosi, uJakobe wahlala endlini kaShemi no-Ebere iminyaka engamashumi amathathu nambili, u-Esawu umfowabo akahambanga, ngoba wayengathandi ukuya, wahlala endlini kayise ezweni laseKhanani.

Nokho kulotshiwe kuJashere mayelana noHlelo lukaMelkisedeki, ukuthi ngokuhamba kwesikhathi mina, uYahweh uMina Enginguye Omkhulu, ngembulela uJakobe ukuthi uhlelo lukaMelkisedeki lwaluzohlukaniswa lube yizikhundla ezintathu njengokwamaZwi alotshwe kuJashere ukuze nazi iqiniso lamaZwi ami: URubeni waba nomona ngonina uLeya ngenxa yalokhu, wagcwala intukuthelo, wasukuma ngokuthukuthela kwakhe wayongena etendeni likaBiliha wasusa khona ukhukho lukayise. Ngaleso sikhathi isabelo sobuzibulo, kanye nezikhundla zobukhosi nobupristi, kwasuswa emadodaneni kaRubeni, ngokuba wayengcolise ukhukho lukayise, ubuzibulo banikwa uJosefa, ubukhosi kuJuda, nobupristi kuLevi, ngokuba uRubeni wayengcolise ukhukho lukayise.

Kanjalo kwaba njalo futhi kulotshiwe ukuthi imithwalo yemfanelo equkethwe oHlelweni lukaMelkisedeki yabekelwa eceleni esikhundleni sokuqondisa kweTestamente likaMose, ngaphansi kweTestamente lakhe eliDala elanikeza izikhundla ezintathu ezihlukene nezicacile zale misebenzi engcwele, kanjalo u-Israyeli waba nohlu lozalo lwaMakhosi, waba nesiKhundla sobupristi bamaLevi, waba nesikole sabaprofethi, isikole esifaniswe nesikole sabaprofethi basendulo, kusukela ezinsukwini zangaphambi kukazamcolo.

Kanjalo imithwalo yemfanelo nezinhlelo zoMpristi, iNkosi, noMprofethi konke kwadlula kwa-Israyeli ngokohlu lozalo lwabo olubhalwe emibhalweni engcwele, kwathi ngokuhamba kwesikhathi, kwafika umuntu ogama lakhe linguJohane owayezalwa nguZakariya owayengowohlu lozalo lobupristi lukaZadoki. Kanjalo kwafanela uJohane uMbhapathizi ukugcwalisa konke ukulunga lapho ebhapathiza iwundlu likaNkulunkulu emfuleni iJordani lapho uJesu abhapathizwa khona, waphuma masinyane emanzini. Bheka, amazulu avuleka kuye, uJohane wabona umoya kaNkulunkulu wehla njengejuba, wehlela phezu kwakhe. Bheka, kwavela izwi ezulwini, lithi: Lo uyiNdodana yami ethandekayo, engithokozile ngayo.

Kanjalo uJesu waseNazaretha wagcotshwa waba ngumpristi kuze kube phakade ngokohlelo lukaMelkisedeki, futhi zonke izikhundla zabuyiselwa ngaphansi kwesambatho salobu bupristi baphakade, manje naphakade.

Ngoba uHlelo lukaMelkisedeki luwuhlelo lobupristi, isembatho salo esingeke sisuke kuYeshuwa HaMashiyakhi, ngoba uvusiwe ukuze angafi futhi!

Ngoba uHlelo lukaMelkisedeki luwuhlelo lwaMakhosi lokubusa okulungileyo kwendalo yami.

Ngoba uHlelo lukaMelkisedeki luwuhlelo lwabaProfethi abakhuluma futhi bafundise umlando, izindlela, nekusasa lezinhlelo zokuphelela zikaYahweh ezinikezwe ebusweni bukaJesu Kristu.

NGINGU-YHVH, ngisho noMina Enginguye Omkhulu, futhi ngithumele la maZwi ngesithunywa sami nenceku yami, futhi ngesithunywa sami nenceku yami la maZwi athunyelwe. Ngeqiniso, esithunyweni sami nasencekwini yami ekhonza ngaphansi kukaKristu Jesu iNkosi yayo yaphakade, nangomoya wesiprofetho uYahweh noKristu Jesu bambulele isithunywa sethu nenceku yethu iVangeli Laphakade lokulalela nokusindiswa kwabo bonke laba okuthunyelwe kubo la maZwi. Futhi amazwi e-Vangeli Laphakade azothola amehlo, izindlebe, nezinhliziyo zabo bonke okuthunyelwe kubo.

NGINGU-YHVH, yena owadala, wenza wathumela iNdodana yami ezelwe yodwa, uYeshuwa HaMashiyakhi onguJesu uMesiya ukuze kusindiswe bonke abazalwa ngu-Adamu bamukele inkululeko ephelele uma labo ababiziwe bephendula umsindisi wabo lapho ebiza. Ngakho-ke, qondani ukuthi yimina, u-YHVH, uMina Enginguye Omkhulu owembula uJesu waseNazaretha kwabalahlekileyo, ukuze uMalusi wabo oMkhulu athole abuyise zonke izimvu zami ezilahlekileyo ezinqolobaneni zami zokuphepha nesiphephelo ingunaphakade naphakade.

NGINGU-YHVH, yena owahlukanisa ubupristi baphakade bukaMelkisedeki baba yizikhundla ezintathu ezihlukene nezicacile zeNkosi, uMpristi, noMprofethi ngaphansi kukaJakobe ezikhathini zakhe.

NGINGU-YHVH, yena obuyisele uBupristi baphakade bukaMelkisedeki kuKristu Jesu, okulotshwe ngaye ukuthi: Ngoba kusobala ukuthi iNkosi yenu yavela kwaJuda, isizwe uMose angakhulumanga lutho ngaso, mayelana noBupristi. Futhi kuyinto esobala nakakhulu, ngoba ngokufana noMelkisedeki, kuvuke omunye uMpristi, ongamenziwanga umpristi ngokomthetho womyalo wenyama wasendulo, kodwa ngamandla okuphila okungapheliyo. Ngoba uyafakaza kanje: Wena unguMpristi kuze kube phakade, ngokohlelo lukaMelkisedeki. Ngoba umyalo owandulelayo uchithiwe, ngenxa yobuthakathaka bawo, nokungabi nasizo kwawo.

Ngoba uMthetho awenzanga lutho luphelele, kodwa ukungeniswa kwethemba elingcono kwenza kwaphelela, okusondeza ngalo kuYahweh. Futhi njengoba kungeyona into engenasifungo (ngoba laba benziwa abapristi ngaphandle kwesifungo: Kodwa lokhu kwenziwa ngesifungo ngalowo owathi kuye, uYahweh ufunge, ngeke aphenduke, Wena unguMpristi kuze kube phakade, ngokohlelo lukaMelkisedeki) Ngokunjalo uJesu wenziwa isiqinisekiso seTestamente elingcono. Phakathi kwabo abaningi benziwa abaPristi, ngoba babengeke baqhubeke, ngenxa yokufa kwabo. Kodwa lo muntu, ngoba ehlala kuze kube phakade, unobuPristi, obungeke budlule komunye. Ngakho-ke, unamandla futhi okubasindisa ngokuphelele labo abeza kuNkulunkulu ngaye, njengoba ehlala njalo, ukubancengela.

Ngakho-ke, nginazisa uHlelo Lwaphakade lukaMelkisedeki neqiniso ngoYeshuwa HaMashiyakhi, onguJesu Kristu, ngoba ngaphansi kwesambatho sakhe saphakade sompristi omkhulu ngokohlelo lukaMelkisedeki, uKristu Jesu uzoqhelisa futhi angcwelise ibutho elikhulu lamakhosi nabapristi ngaphansi koHlelo lukaMelkisedeki, kanjalo uMbuso Wezulu uzobuswa ngesandla saphakade seNkosi yabo Yaphakade Yokulunga, abazobusa ngaphansi kwayo ngokupheleleyo ngokulunga nobungcwele beqiniso kuze kube phakade, ngoba mayelana noMbuso kaKristu Jesu, akukho ukuphela.
