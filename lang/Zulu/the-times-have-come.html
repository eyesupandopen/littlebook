Izikhathi Zifikile!

Kusuka kuYeshua HaMashiach:

Bhekani! Izikhathi zokwehlukaniswa ziyasondela ekupheleni, nokubonakala kwami sekucishe kwafika manje. Kuseduze kakhulu ngisho nezingelosi nempi yezulu balindele ukukhala kwecilongo. Sengizelulele izandla zami ezweni elifayo iminyaka eminingi manje, futhi abaningi basamukele isivumelwano sokuthula uBaba wami, uYahweh, asithumela esintwini, yebo, mina, Kristu Jesu, ngokuba ngingukuthula kwenu. Kodwa abaningi kakhulu balunqabile uthando lwami nomusa kaBaba wami nesihe esesitheliwe emhlabeni.

Labo abaphawuliwe nabahlengiwe yibo abakhethwa kusukela ekusekelweni komhlaba. Ngoba uBaba wami wanazi kusukela phakade kuze kube phakade, futhi nanilokhu nisenhliziyweni yakhe, njengoba nami ngibe senhliziyweni yakhe, ngokuba mina, Jesu waseNazaretha, ngiyindodana kaYahweh ezelwe yodwa. Kodwa ngemisebenzi yami ephelele njengePhasika eliphelele nangokulalela koZelwe Yedwa, amadodana amaningi, amaningi kaNkulunkulu azalwe kabusha kuze kube phakade, athathwe emadodaneni abantu, ngokuba ngiyiNdodana yoMuntu!

Lezi zikhathi zomusa zaziyimfihlakalo efihlwe enhliziyweni kaYahweh angazange ayitshele muntu waze wayambulela mina, futhi mina ngiyambulile kubaphostoli bami abangcwele nabaprofethi futhi ngalondoloza iZwi lami elingcwele ezindlebeni nasezinhliziyweni zabakhethiweyo nabathembekileyo. Ngokuba nina, Mlobokazi wami, niyingxenye yemfihlakalo enkulu kaNkulunkulu, futhi izimpilo zenu (ezifihliwe kimi) sezizokwambulwa. Futhi ngizoklomelisa abathembekileyo ngayo yonke imisebenzi yabo yokuthembeka abayenzile ngokholo olukhulu kimi, Jesu Kristu.

Futhi kanjalo ngisindise izixuku ezikhulu ngegazi lami lokuhlawulela, ngokuba igazi lami lalingenasono futhi kuze kube namuhla anginaso isono, ngokuba angisoze ngona. Labo abaphile izimpilo zabo ngokuthembeka kuKristu bayokwamukela umvuzo omkhulu, futhi bayokhanya njengezinkanyezi ezulwini, futhi inkazimulo enkulu ingeyabo, ngokuba abazange bazidumise bona ngokungathi bangabantu abakhulu. Kodwa badumise mina, Yeshua HaMashiach, futhi ngizobethula kuBaba wami bengenabala noma umbhinco, nezingubo zabo zizoba mhlophe qhwa futhi azisoze zangcoliswa. Futhi bonke bayophila futhi abasoze bona futhi, kungaba ngokungamesabi uNkulunkulu noma ngokuzidla. Futhi bayodunyiswa kimi.

Ngokuba ngingumalusi omkhulu, futhi ngihole umhlambi wami emadlelweni aluhlaza kuze kube yizikhathi zokuphela, ezifika ngokushesha emhlabeni, ngokuba asisekho isihlabathi esisele ukuba sidlule engilazini. Futhi ngizoklomelisa bonke, kusukela komncane kuze kube komkhulu, ngendlela eningeke niyiqonde ngalesi sikhathi; kodwa lokho kuqonda kusemnyango wenu, ngokuba nizothathwa niye emazulwini maduzane kakhulu. Futhi kanjalo uMlobokazi wami uzothathelwa enkazimulweni, lapho ngizokwahlulela khona imisebenzi yakhe futhi ngihlenge ngokuphelele yonke indoda, owesifazane, nomntwana. Ngizobavuza kakhulu nangezinto ezincane kakhulu ezenziwe ekungikhonzeni. Ngokuba uNkulunkulu akalona ongalungile, ukuba akhohlwe umsebenzi wenu nokukhandleka kothando, enikubonisile ngeGama lakhe, ngokuba nikhonze abaNgcwele, futhi nisakhonza.

Ungesabi, Mlobokazi wami, ngokuba uNkulunkulu akahluleli njengoba umuntu ahlulela, ngokuba umuntu akanabulungisa futhi unokukhetha abantu. Umuntu udumisa labo umuntu ababona bebalulekile, futhi umuntu wemvelo uvame ukunganaki ukuthobeka nobuhle ekhetha abazidlayo nabazifezile. Ngokuba umuntu wemvelo ukhala ngokuthi abalwe olungile phambi kukaNkulunkulu, kodwa umuntu wemvelo akakwazi ukunqoba isimo sakhe sesono futhi uyenqaba ukukholelwa ukuthi wonakele, aze aphike neNkosi yamakhosi, engamsindisa.

Futhi kanjalo ukuzidla kwandulela ukubhujiswa, nomoya oziphakamisayo wandulela ukuwa. Esikhundleni sokukhothamisa izinhliziyo zabo emazwini ami nasekuhlakanipheni nothando engiluthele emhlabeni, umuntu wemvelo ukhetha ukulandela izinkanuko zakhe, ngokuba izindlela zomhlaba zakhiwe nguSathane ukulinga nokuvusa lezo zinkanuko, ngisho nemivuzo yokungalungi. Futhi kanjalo izikhathi zabeZizwe ziyeza ekupheleni, futhi sekuyisikhathi sokuhlupheka kukaJakobe; ngokuba ngizokuhlenga u-Israyeli, ngisho nabakhethiweyo bami abathembekileyo, abayoza kimi ngokuphenduka ezonweni zabo nasezonweni zawoyise abangalahlayo. Futhi njengoba kulotshiwe, makwenzeke kanjalo.

Oh, muntu wemvelo, kungani usakhetha izono zakho futhi unganaki umusa wami nensindiso ngokungathi yinto elula? Akekho uYahweh noma mina, Kristu Jesu, onokuthokoza ezahlulelweni ezizayo nasekwesabeni okukhulu okuzodedelwa maduze. Kodwa uNkulunkulu akakhethanga le ngxenye yecebo lakhe ukuze abe nonya endalweni yakhe: ngokuba uNkulunkulu uluthando, futhi uNkulunkulu ungukukhanya, futhi akukho bumnyama kuye nhlobo. Ukuhlupheka okukhulu okuzayo kwenzelwe ukunika isintu ithuba lokugcina ngaphambi kokuba kwephuze kakhulu kuso. Isixuku esikhulu sizosindiswa osizini olukhulu, ngisho nalabo ababambelela egameni lami kuze kube sekuphefumuleni kwabo kokugcina futhi bengazithandi izimpilo zabo kuze kube sekupheleni.

Muntu wemvelo, unenkani kakhulu, futhi awulaleli, futhi ngokobuwula uyenqaba umusa wami, futhi ngisathandaza okufanayo: Baba, bathethelele, ngokuba abakwazi abakwenzayo. Kulabo abasalele osizini olukhulu, ngisho nalabo abenqabile isipho sami sokuthula, abaningi bayolahlekelwa babhujiswe, ngokuba bayinqabile iNkosi uJesu Kristu. Nokho, abaningi bayosindiswa, ngisho bonke abakhuleka egameni leNkosi bayosindiswa. Futhi kanjalo izilingo zomlilo sezizoqala, ngokuba ukwahlulela kuyasondela.

Ukwahlulela okukhulu kuyeza embusweni kaSathane! Futhi wena, muntu wemvelo, ngisho nasekungakholweni kwakho okunenkani, uyenqaba ngisho nesicelo esilula. Ngokuba ezikhathini zami zomusa, insindiso ibimahhala futhi ilula, futhi okwakufanele umuntu akwenze kwakuwukuvuma ukuthi uJesu uyiNkosi futhi akholwe ukuthi uNkulunkulu wangivusa kwabafileyo. Futhi manje sekuzoba nendlala yazo zonke izinto ezinhle ezweni, ngisho neZwi lami eliNgcwele. Isikhathi esikhulu kakhulu sosizi siyasondela, futhi uSathane ngokobuwula ulungiselela ukuma kwakhe, aze acabange ngokuzidla ukuthi anganqoba ukuze yena nezingelosi zakhe babe nezwe labo, izwe elingenaye uNkulunkulu, ngoba izindlela zabo zisontekile. Ngiqinisile, lokho okuzenze kwasonteka ngeke kuqondiswe, futhi akukho ukuthethelelwa noma ukuthula kuSathane nezingelosi zakhe, noma enzalweni yabo.

Ngingunyana kaNkulunkulu oweza enyameni.

NgiyiNgonyama yesizwe sakwaJuda.

Nginguye oyobusa izizwe ngentonga yensimbi.

Nginguye oyovula izimvu zakhe maduzane kakhulu.

Nginguye ozayo ngomlobokazi wami.

Nginguye oyohlangana nani emoyeni.

Nginguye enginguye, futhi icilongo selizokhaliswa.

Mina, Yeshua HaMashiach, ngithumele la Mazwi.

Kusuka kuYahweh, uNkulunkulu Omkhulu Ophakade:

Manje kufika ukwahlulela kukaNkulunkulu waphakade, yebo, uYahweh, ngokuba ngithumela iNdodana yami, futhi asisekho isikhathi. Oh, nina eningelusizo lwalutho enanicabanga ukuthi ninganqoba uYahweh, yebo, uNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade! Oh, nina eningelusizo lwalutho enacabanga ngobuwula ukuthi ninganqoba iNdodana yami ezelwe yodwa, uYeshua HaMashiach. Nizibekelele ulaka lwaphakade, futhi intukuthelo yami inkulu ngani, ngokuba nginguNkulunkulu onomhawu. Ngokuba kusukela ekuqaleni nimelene nezinhlelo zami, futhi nikhathazile, nacindezela, nashushisa, nahlukumeza, futhi nabhubhisa indalo yami. Aninakho ukuthula.

Ngizweni kahle, ngokuba ngimemezela okuzokwenzeka kini, futhi lokho uYahweh akumemezelayo akekho ongakuguqula, nakanye, naphakade. Futhi kanjalo ukwahlulela kwenu kuyeza. Ngizothumela iNdodana yami ukuba iqoqe abakhethiweyo bami nabathembekileyo, yebo, amadodana ka-Adamu asebengamadodana kaNkulunkulu. Nehlulekile, oh, wena nyoka engelusizo lwalutho, eshelela ngokungathembeki nangamanga. Ngokuba anibona onkulunkulu, futhi anisoze nazikhulula esandleni sami. Ngokuba ngizobuyisela!

Ngizobuyisela kini yonke imisebenzi emibi nabo bonke ubuhlungu nokuhlupheka enikubangele abangenacala, futhi niyosha phakade emalangabini ashisayo kakhulu aluhlaza, nomusi onoshevu oyityhefu uyoba umoya wenu, futhi ngenhlupheko enkulu niyoba phakade. Ngokuba nangenza ngabhubhisa indalo yami, ngisho umhlaba njengoba wawunjalo ngaphambi kukazamcolo kaNowa, ngenxa yenzalo yenu engelusizo lwalutho enayenzela ukubhujiswa kwesintu; futhi ngenxa yalezi zinto, ngizonibhubhisa efeni lenu lomlilo.

Futhi nazama ukubhubhisa umhlaba ngemva kukazamcolo kaNowa, ngisho nenza izinengiso zenu futhi, kodwa ngeke zibe nampande nagatsha; ngokuba lonke ukhula luyosishulwa, luboshwe, luphonswe emlilweni. Futhi kanjalo ngizobhubhisa umhlaba ngokwahlulela komlilo, ngokuba ngathembisa ukuthi angisoze ngaphinda ngikhukhulise umhlaba wonke futhi. Kodwa ngomlilo ukwahlulela kwami kuyeza, ngokuba ngingumlilo oqothulayo, futhi intukuthelo yami iyabila kwababi. Futhi ngenxa yalezi zinto, ngizonibhubhisa emlilweni oshisayo kakhulu nesibabule esivuthayo.

Ngizweni kahle, ngokuba ngimemezela okuzokwenzeka kini, ngisho nina balindi abangelusizo lwalutho enashiya isimo senu sokuqala. Aninakho ukuthula, futhi nakuba nginikhululile ezibophweni zenu isikhashana, ngikwenze lokho kuphela ukuze nikhonze izinhloso zami. Bese kufika ukwahlulela okukhulu phezu kwenu, futhi aninakho ukuthula. Ngokuba nangenza ngikhukhulise umhlaba ngenxa yezinengiso zenu. Ningobani nina ukuba nidlale indima yomdali endalweni kaYahweh? Ningobani nina ukuba ningcolise insimu kaNkulunkulu ngezindlela zenu ezingcolile? Nenza abantwana benu bezifebe nabalahliwe ngokuzingcolisa nabesifazane, futhi bayohlanganyela efeni lenu lomlilo. Ngokuba ngamtshelani uMalaki: Ngokuba bheka, usuku luyeza oluyovutha njengesithando somlilo, futhi bonke abazidlayo, yebo, nabo bonke abenza okubi, bayoba amabibi, futhi usuku oluzayo, luyobashisa, kusho iNkosi Sebawoti, futhi ngeke lubashiyele mpande nagatsha. Bhekani lolo suku luyasondela ngempela, futhi nonke nizokhokha ngokwezindlela zenu ezimbi.

Ngokuba nginike abantu abafazi ukuze bangasweli lutho, abafazi bokuba abangane nokubazalela abantwana. Kodwa nehlela eNtabeni iHermoni, naze nashiya isikhundla senu sokuqala senkazimulo, futhi nizigcolisile nabesifazane, futhi aninakho ukuthula noma ukuphumula. Njengoba ngatshela u-Enoke umbhali ngenkathi yecala lenu: Aninakho ukuthula: isigwebo esinzima siphumele kini sokunibopha: Futhi ngeke nibe nokubekezelelwa noma isicelo esinikezwayo kini, ngenxa yokungalungi enikufundisile, nangenxa yayo yonke imisebenzi yokungamesabi uNkulunkulu nokungalungi nesono enikubonisile kubantu. Futhi nisezibophweni niyoya njengoba niphonswa echibini lomlilo.

Nalimala indalo yami ngenxa yezinkanuko zenu, ngisho nithatha ngenkani abaningi enabakhetha njengabafazi benu, futhi nizokhokha. Nihlangene noSathane, ngisho nezikhulu ezazihlele ukulwa nami, futhi nakhetha amanga ama-seraphim. Ngakho-ke, niyohlangana nabo echibini elikhulu lomlilo laphakade, futhi niyosha phakade, futhi anisoze naphumula. Niyilimazile indalo yami futhi nabulala abaningi kangaka, futhi aninandaba nezimpilo engizidalile. Ngakho-ke, angizukuninaka, futhi nginidalile, futhi manje kufika ukwahlulela kwenu nokubhujiswa kwenu okuzayo.

Ngithi koSathane, abalindi, amakherubi awileyo, ama-seraphim awileyo, ngisho nabo bonke abajoyine ukuvukela kukaSathane: niyosha ngokuphelele, kaninginingi kuze kube phakade; ngokuba ngizonibopha futhi nginiphonse emlilweni lapho impethu yenu ingafi khona, ngisho namalangabi abekelwe nina eningelusizo lwalutho ayovutha amalangabi ashisayo kakhulu ngenxa yenu, ngokuba nifanelekile. Ngokuba ngizokwenza kini lokho kanye enikwenzile kwabanye. Bonke ubuhlungu, usizi, ukuzisola, ukuhlupheka, ngisho zonke izenzo ezinengekayo ziyobuyiselwa kini kuze kube phakade, futhi ngeke niphunyuke. Ngizonibeka obala phambi kwezizwe futhi nginiveze njengabakhohlisi eniyibo, futhi bayothi konkulunkulu abancane (abangesibo onkulunkulu): Ingabe nawe usubuthakathaka njengathi? Ingabe usufana nathi?

Ngizobuyisela ngokuphelele izindlela zenu ezimbi ezifubeni zenu, futhi niyokhokha phakade. Niyophonswa niphila emalangabini, futhi niyosha phambi kweWundlu, futhi izingelosi nabo bonke abalungileyo enibonile bayokwamukela kimi okuphindaphindiwe kunalokho enikuthathile kubo. Ngokuba lapho senibuyiselwe okubi kwenu, ngiyobe ngibuyisele abakhethiweyo bami nabathembekileyo ngomvuzo omkhulu oyodlula lokho enikuthathe kaningi ngaphezu kwezenzo zenu ezingelusizo lwalutho.

Futhi kuyoba njalo. Ngizodala izulu elisha nomhlaba omusha lapho kuhlala khona ukulunga, futhi abantwana bami bayohlala nami, futhi ngizokwakha ikhaya lami phakathi kwabantu, futhi kuyoba njalo. Kodwa nina niyosha ezindleleni zenu ezimbi, futhi zonke izinto ezimbi enizenzile ziyobuyiselwa kini phakade. Futhi ngizobeka uphawu, futhi niyokhohlakala, futhi ngeke nikhunjulwe. Futhi bonke bayohlala ngokuthula, futhi anisoze naba nokuthula.

Bheka, isikhathi siphelile, futhi ngithumela iNdodana yami ngomlobokazi wayo, yebo, uJesu Kristu oLungileyo! Ngokuba kuye ngithokozile kakhulu! Futhi bonke abasindisile futhi wabaphulukisa ezindleleni zenu ezingelusizo lwalutho, ngisho nalabo enibamangalela ngokungafanele, bayophumula kimi futhi babe nokuthula okuphelele, injabulo, nothando. Ngokuba ngizonenza njengabo futhi ngibenze babe nenkazimulo enkulu kunalokho enake naba yikho, futhi aninawo umbuso, ngokuba anibona amakhosi.

Maye kulabo abakhileyo emhlabeni, ngokuba ulaka olukhulu luphezu kwenu. Ngokuba nimlahle umdali wenu futhi nithanda ukufa. Ngokuba ukungilahla kuwukuthanda ukufa, futhi ekufeni niyokufa. Intukuthelo yami iyashisa ngani, futhi ngizothatha yonke injabulo, yonke intokozo, ngisho nezidingo zenu eziyisisekelo ngeke zaneliseke. Ngokuba lapho nginithela ngomusa nangesihe, nakhetha esikhundleni salokho ukusebenzisa umusa njengethuba lokuhlangana nabangelusizo lwalutho, ngisho nombuso kaSathane, lapho naningaphunyuka khona. Futhi manje abaningi bayobhubha, amagama abo angalotshwanga eNcwadini Yokuphila kusukela ekuqaleni. INdodana yami, uJesu Kristu, iyasondela, futhi izohlwitha umlobokazi wayo iye emazulwini, futhi ngizokususa umvimbeli. Bheka! Ngithumela imidondoshiya ukufeza ulaka lwami! Nathatha umusa wami nesihe ngeze futhi naphila izimpilo zenjabulo yesono, futhi ngizobuyisela.

Ngokuba lapho naningaba nokuthula kuze kube phakade, ngisho nokuphila okuphakade kwenjabulo egcwele nenjabulo, nakhetha ukufa kwenu, futhi ngizoninika lokho enikufisayo. Nifuna umhlaba wobubi nobumnyama; ngizoninika lokho kanye enikufisayo, futhi ngizonibuyisela ngabo bonke ubuhlungu enibubangele abanye ngenkathi niphila esonweni esikhulu. Ngizokwephuca yonke injabulo, futhi niyodla ukungcola kwezindlela zenu. Ukwahlulela okukhulu kuphezu komhlaba, futhi ngeke kusaba nokulibala.

Bheka! Bonke abakhuleka egameni leNkosi bayosindiswa. Ngokuba isixuku esikhulu sizosindiswa osizini oluzayo, futhi ngizobamukela kimi. Ngokuba kulotshiwe: Laba yibo abaphuma osizini olukhulu, futhi bahlanza izingubo zabo, bazenza zaba mhlophe ngegazi leWundlu. Ngakho-ke baphambi kwesihlalo sobukhosi sikaNkulunkulu, futhi bamkhonza imini nobusuku eThempelini lakhe: futhi lowo ohlezi esihlalweni sobukhosi uyohlala phakathi kwabo. Abasayikulamba futhi, abasayikoma futhi, nelanga aliyikukhanya phezu kwabo, nanoma yikuphi ukushisa. Ngokuba iWundlu eliphakathi kwesihlalo sobukhosi liyobondla, futhi liyobaholela emithonjeni yamanzi aphilayo: futhi uNkulunkulu uyosula zonke izinyembezi emehlweni abo.

Ngiyaninxusa nonke ukuba nikhuleke egameni likaJesu Kristu, uMesiya weqiniso, ngisho indodana kaNkulunkulu ezelwe yodwa, ngokuba uyeza, futhi ulaka lweWundlu nolaka lukaYahweh sekuzodedelwa.

NginguYahweh, uNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade.

Ngingu-El Shaddai, uNkulunkulu Somandla.

NginguJehova Jireh.

Ngingu-Elohim omkhulu, uNkulunkulu ka-Israyeli.

Nginguye owathuma indodana yakhe ezelwe yodwa ukuba ihlenge labo ababeyohlangulwa.

Nginguye owavusa uKristu Jesu kwabafileyo futhi wamnika igama eliphezu kwawo onke amagama, ukuze onke amadolo aguqe futhi zonke izilimi zivume.

NGINGUYE OMKHULU ENGINGUYE, futhi ulaka lwami seluzogcwaliseka maduze, futhi akekho omkhulu, futhi ngikhulumile.

Mina, Yahweh, uNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade, ngithumele amazwi engiwathumele, futhi ngithumele la mazwi. Ngithumele indodana yami, uYeshua HaMashiach, futhi uthumele amazwi akhe kwabathembekileyo. Isikhathi siphelile.
