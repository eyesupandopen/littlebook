Ngokuba Olungileyo Uyakuphila Ngokukholwa!

Ngokuba olungileyo uyakuphila ngokukholwa! Vuka, uvuse nabazalwane bakho, bandla lami laseLawodikeya elisivivi, elikhohliwe izimfihlakalo zami; ngokuba ihora selihambile nesikhathi sesicishe saphela. Wozani kimi, bantwana bami, ngezandla ezivulekileyo nenhliziyo evulekileyo (ngokuba izingalo zami nenhliziyo yami kuvulekele nina); wozani kimi ngenhliziyo ethambileyo nelungele ukwamukela amazwi ami. Ngokuba wathini umfowethu uJakobe? Ngakho-ke lahlani konke ukungcola, nokubi okuchichimayo, namukele ngobumnene izwi elitshaliweyo, elinamandla okusindisa imiphefumulo yenu. Bantwana bami, yibani ngabalandeli bami, ngokuba ngimnene, ngithobekile ngenhliziyo, namukele ngobumnene iZwi elitshaliweyo, engilibeke phakathi kwenu ukuze nibe namehlo okubona nezindlebe zokuzwa. Phendulelani izinhliziyo zenu kimi, ngokuba ngiyindlela, neqiniso, nokuphila, akekho oza kuBaba ngaphandle kwami, ngokuba intando kaBaba yembulwe kanjalo ngokusobala, wenzile kanjalo.

Kungani nivumela izinhliziyo zenu zinihluphe, bantwana bami? Kungani nisawaphika amazwi ami futhi nidukisa abanye ephutheni lenu? Ngokuba nina, bandla lami laseLawodikeya, nikhohliwe ukuthi ningobani kimi, uKristu Jesu, futhi nilahlekelwe yizimfihlakalo zami, nakuba nganiphathisa zona. Ngokuba ngiyiNkosi eyanithenga ngegazi lami uqobo; ngiyiNkosi yamakhosi neNkosi yamakhosi eyazuza ifa lomhlaba kuBaba wami, futhi ngizokwabelana ngefa lami nabathembekileyo nabakhethiweyo; ngizokwabelana ngefa lami nawe, Makoti wami! Ngakho-ke ungesabi, ngokuba noma umpofu, ucebile kimi, ngokuba uBaba unikele ezandleni zami zonke izinto. Oh, bandla laseLawodikeya, usuphenduke owesifazane ongenakukholwa wokuyekelela.

Cha, aninandaba nokudla okuqinileyo kwezwi lami, futhi aningilandeli ngenhliziyo yenu yonke; anifundanga yini iSaga? Ukuqonda okuhle kuzuza umusa, kepha indlela yabangakholwa ilukhuni. Nezindlela zokuyekelela enizikhethile zilukhuni; izindlela zenu zokungakholwa akuzona ezami, futhi nizama ukuhamba endleleni engininike yona ngonyawo olulodwa endleleni nolunye emseleni, kepha nibe nesibindi sokungisola, mina, uYeshua HaMashiach, lapho niwela emseleni njengoba niholiwe. Ngokuba uhambo lwenu lunokuwa okuningi, futhi ithemba lami ngani lisewukuthi ukuwa kwenu kunjengokuwa kwabalungileyo: Ngokuba olungileyo uwa kasikhombisa, aphinde asukume, kepha ababi bayakuwela ebubini.

Anginibizelanga ekuweleni ebubini, bantwana bami, ngisho nani, bandla lami laseLawodikeya, ngokuba angikanilahli ezikhathini zokulingwa okwamanje. Ngokuba bhekani! Ngisemi emnyango ngingqongqoza ngoba ngeke neze ngihlanganyele ezindleleni zenu ezisivivi, zokuyekelela, kodwa nginibizela ekulungeni. Nginibizela ekumeni ngokuqina ekulungeni, ngokuba abelusi abaphambukayo banikhohlisile, beningenisa ezindleleni zikaBalami; futhi abaningi benu baprofetha amanga ngomoya kaJezebeli, onganciphisa amazwi abaprofethi bami beqiniso futhi afise ukubulala abaprofethi engibangcwelisile kimi, ngokuba yimi owanika abantu izipho. Ngokuba ngabhalela elaseGalathiya lapho bekhubeka njengoba nikhubeka nani: Ngiyamangala ukuthi nisuke masinyane kangaka kuye owanibizela emseni kaKristu, niye kwelinye ivangeli: Okungelona elinye. Yebo, fundani lokho futhi, ngokuba alikho elinye iVangeli futhi alikho elinye igama elinikiweyo abantu abayakusindiswa ngalo ngaphandle kwelami, uJesu Kristu. Kungani niphambukiswa kimi, bantwana bami abancane? Anginondli futhi anginakekeli yini, ngokuba ngingumalusi omuhle owanikela ngokuphila kwakhe ngenxa yezimvu. Kodwa amazwi engingawakhulumanga, eniwafisa ngokulangazela niwalandele, ayakikitaza izindlebe zenu; ngokuba lowo muzwa usuphenduke uthando lwenu lokuqala ezindleleni zenu zokuzikhukhumeza; ngokuba nizigcinela amanga njengokungathi ayiqiniso, futhi nilahlekelwe yikho konke ukwahlukanisa.

Nikhohliwe yini ngomlando oyisisekelo webandla? Nivilaphe kakhulu ukuba nifunde? Ingabe lesi sihloko asizikizi izindlebe zenu ngokwanele ukuba nizwe amazwi ami futhi nibe nokukholwa kuwo? Ngokuba umuntu ngeke aphile ngesinkwa sodwa kodwa NGAWO WONKE amazwi aphuma emlonyeni kaNkulunkulu! Ibandla lami lazalwa ngosuku lwePhentekoste futhi kulotshiwe encwadini yezEnzo ukuze nikufunde. Ngokuba ibandla lami laqala kwa-Israyeli, eJudiya, nakuba bengawaqondanga amazwi engawakhuluma ngoPetru ngalolo suku olukhazimulayo lapho ngethulela umoya ongcwele phezu kwabakhethiweyo bami, njengoba bekhuluma ngezinye izilimi njengoba umoya wawubanika ukuba bakhulume! Ngokuba wathini uPetru esixukwini eJerusalema ngalolo suku lapho engiprofethela? Ngokuba isithembiso singesenu, nesabantwana benu, nesabo bonke abakude, ngisho nabaningi njengalabo iNkosi uNkulunkulu wethu eyakubabiza. Bantwana bami, nina niyilabo abakude, ngokuba abaningi benu abangesibo abakwa-Israyeli ngokwenyama, kodwa nginithathile ezizweni ngomusa wami ukuze nime emseni wami, ngokuba nibiziwe.

Anifundanga yini? Kepha manje kuKristu Jesu, nina enake nikude, senenziwe saba seduze ngegazi likaKristu. Kodwa ezindlini zenu zokukhonza ezonakele enizinikela ngokuhlambalaza egameni lami (nakuba ngingeke ngingene kodwa ngiyakuma emnyango ngingqongqoze), nicabanga ukuthi namukele umthetho kaMose kimi, futhi ekuzidleni kwenu aniqondi ukuthi uMose wayengowakwa-Israyeli, futhi kwakungukwa-Israyeli iTorah eyanikezwa ukuba iyigcine, umthwalo abahluleka kuwo; ngokuba akekho umuntu okwaziyo ukugcina umthetho kaMose ngenxa yenkohlakalo inyoka eyayibangela enyameni yenu, ngokuba ulwazi lwayo luhlala lonakalisa, futhi inkokhelo yesono ingukufa. Ngokuba abaseKorinte bayafakaza ngamazwi ami: Ongusenzile sibe yizikhonzi ezifanelekileyo zesivumelwano esisha, kungengezohlamvu, kodwa ezomoya; ngokuba uhlamvu lubulala, kepha umoya uyaphilisa. Nibizelwe ukuhamba emoyeni, nakuba abaningi benu befisa ngokuyiphutha uhlamvu lomthetho, okungesilona olwenu, kodwa olugcwaliseka kimi.

Ngiphenduleni kulokhu: Umoya weza kini ngokugcwalisa kwenu iTorah? Noma weza ngokugcwalisa kwami okulungileyo kweTorah njengePhasika lenu eliphelele? Ngokuba bukhala buthini ubufakazi baseGalathiya? Lokhu kuphela ngingathanda ukukufunda kini, namukela umoya, ngemisebenzi yoMthetho, noma ngokuzwa kokukholwa? Futhi uPhawuli uphinda uthi kwabaseGalathiya: Angiwuchithi umusa kaNkulunkulu; ngokuba uma ukulunga kuza ngoMthetho, khona-ke uKristu wafela ize. Aniqondi yini ukuthi ekuqaleni kwenkathi kwakukhona ibandla lamaJuda nebandla labeZizwe? Nakuba ibandla lami lazalelwa eJudiya, uPhawuli wathi kwabaseGalathiya futhi ngakulondoloza lapho: Ngokuba lowo owasebenza ngempumelelo kuPetru ebuPhostolini bokusoka, lowo muntu waba namandla kimi kwabeZizwe. Ngokuba ngathuma uPetru kuqala kwabeZizwe, futhi kulotshiwe ezEnzweni lapho ngitshela uPetru: Lokho uNkulunkulu akuhlanzileyo, ungakubizi ngokuthi kungcolile. Ngokuba lapho ngithuma uPetru kuKorneliyu, ubufakazi obulandelayo babhalwa: Futhi labo bokusoka abakholwayo, bamangala, bonke abeza noPetru, ngoba naphezu kwabeZizwe kwathululwa isipho somoya oyingcwele. Ngiyanibuza lokhu, nina baseLawodikeya, ingabe uKorneliyu wathola insindiso ngokulandela iTorah (ngokuba wayeyindoda ezinikele)? Noma wasamukela isithembiso ngokukholwa? AbaseKorinte banokuthile abakushoyo ngale ndaba lapho uPhawuli ebabhalela: Okuyilona enisindiswa ngalo futhi, uma nibambelela kulokho engakushumayela kini, ngaphandle kokuthi nikholwe ngeze. Insindiso yeza ngokushunyayelwa kokukholwa hhayi ngohlamvu lomthetho, ngokuba ukuzibeka ngaphansi komthetho kaMose, ngisho nokukholwa kwenu kuyize.

Ngakho-ke abaseRoma bathi: Kodwa manje ukulunga kukaNkulunkulu ngaphandle koMthetho sekubonakalisiwe, kufakazelwa uMthetho nabaProfethi. Ngisho nokulunga kukaNkulunkulu, okungokukholwa kuJesu Kristu kubo bonke, naphezu kwabo bonke abakholwayo: ngokuba awukho umehluko: Ngokuba bonke bonile, futhi basilalelwe inkazimulo kaNkulunkulu. Ngokuba ngalitshela ibandla lami kwabaseGalathiya lapho uPhawuli abhalela ngokulalela: Ngokuba kuJesu Kristu, ukusoka akusizi lutho, nokungasoki, kodwa ukukholwa okusebenza ngothando. Futhi kwabaseGalathiya uPhawuli waphinda wabhala: Ngokuba kuKristu Jesu ukusoka akusizi lutho nokungasoki, kodwa isidalwa esisha. Ngakho-ke insindiso ayinikwa ngemisebenzi yomthetho, futhi insindiso yenu ayiphilwa futhi ayifezeki ngemisebenzi yomthetho. Ngokuba kwabhalelwa abaseRoma: Bhekani, ngibeka eSiyoni itshe lokukhubekisa, nedwala lokuwisa, futhi noma ngubani okholwa kulo, akayikujabha. Bantwana bami, ngiyilo lelo litshe lokukhubekisa nedwala lokuwisa, ngokuba nginguJesu waseNazaretha, iWundlu likaNkulunkulu impela, futhi ngiwugcwalisile umthetho.

O, nina baseLawodikeya, nicabanga ukuthi nina nodwa enikhubeka kulolu daba? Ngithi cha. Ngokuba ningahlanganisa izincwadi zami ngezigaba ezimbili eziyisisekelo, ngaphandle kokuthi zombili zibhalelwe umuntu oyedwa omusha kuKristu Jesu. Ngokuba izincwadi zami zibhalelwe amabandla abeZizwe, futhi ezinye zibhekiswe emabandleni amaJuda, futhi ngaleyo ndlela uPetru wabhala ngesambulo seMfihlakalo engayembulela uPhawuli: Njengoba nakuzo zonke izincwadi zakhe, ekhuluma kuzo ngalezi zinto, okukhona kuzo izinto ezinzima ukuziqonda, lezo abangafundile nabangazinzile abazihlanekezelayo, njengoba benza neminye imiBhalo, kuze kube sekubhujisweni kwabo.

Ngakho-ke isimo senu asisisha, ngokuba akukho silingo esinihlasele ngaphandle kwesivamile kubantu: kodwa uNkulunkulu uthembekile, ongeke avume ukuba nilingwe ngaphezu kwamandla enu; kodwa kanye nesilingo uyokwenza indlela yokuphunyuka, ukuze nibe namandla okusithwala. Ingabe niyoyithatha indlela yokuphunyuka uNkulunkulu anilungisele yona emseni nasesihawini sami ukuze nibaleke kulabo abanganiphuca umvuzo wenu ngokunibangela ukuba niwe emseni? Ingabe niyawenqaba amazwi ami ngenxa yabaFundisi bamaJuda abamemezela ngokungazi ukuthi kufanele nigcine iTorah ukuze nisindiswe? Niyiziwula kangangokuthi nicabange ukuthi, njengoba seniqalile umthetho wokuphila emoyeni kaJesu Kristu, manje seninikelwe emthethweni wesono nokufa? Kuthiwani ngomthetho? Bantwana bami, ngizonitshela. Ningaphethi ngobuwula ukuthi ngiyizwi lamanga eliniholela ephutheni, ngokuba abaningi benu abalazi izwi lami futhi aningazi, nakuba nginibiza! Ningacabangi ukuthi ngize ukuzochitha umthetho noma abaprofethi. Angizanga ukuzochitha, kodwa ukuzogcwalisa. Ngokuba ngiqinisile ngithi kini, Kuze kudlule izulu nomhlaba, akusoze kwadlula neyodi elilodwa noma ucezwana lweyodi emthethweni, kuze kugcwaliseke konke.

Nginguye owagcwalisa umthetho kaMose, futhi izimiso zawo zigcinwa ngokulalela amazwi ami omusa nesihe ezincwadini zami nangekholwa likaJesu Kristu. Nicabanga ngokungazi ukuthi ngenxa yokuthi ngiyisigcwaliso somthetho ngikhuthaza noma ubani ukuba aphule imithetho kaNkulunkulu, kungaba umthetho kaMose noma imithetho yokuziphatha kaNkulunkulu. Ngokuba ngabatshelani labo ababehlala eKorinte? Bandla lami laseLawodikeya, engilithandayo, anibona abokuqala ukubhekana nalesi silingo, ngakho-ke ngabhalisa uPhawuli: Ongusenzile sibe yizikhonzi ezifanelekileyo zesivumelwano esisha, kungengezohlamvu, kodwa ezomoya; ngokuba uhlamvu lubulala, kepha umoya uyaphilisa. Kodwa uma ukukhonza kokufa okulotshiweyo, nokubazwe ematsheni, kwakukhazimula, kangangokuthi abantwana bakwa-Israyeli abakwazanga ukubuka ubuso bukaMose ngokuqinile, ngenxa yenkazimulo yobuso bakhe, leyo nkazimulo eyayizosuswa: Kangakanani ukukhonza komoya, ngeke kube nenkazimulo enkulu kunalokho? Ningenzi iphutha, bantwana bami, uhlamvu lubulala ngenxa yesono esisenyameni yenu esilahlayo lolo hlamvu olufanayo, ngisho nomthetho kaMose: Ngakho-ke umthetho ungcwele, nomyalo ungcwele, ulungile, futhi muhle. Ingabe lokho okuhle, kwenziwa ukufa kimi? Makube kude. Kodwa isono, ukuze sibonakale siyisono, sisebenza ukufa kimi ngalokho okuhle: ukuze isono ngoMyalo sibe nesono ngokweqile. Futhi ngakho-ke ngatshela abaseGalathiya ngoPhawuli: Angiwuchithi umusa kaNkulunkulu; ngokuba uma ukulunga kuza ngoMthetho, khona-ke uKristu wafela ize. Bantwana bami, niphambukisiwe, futhi nganitshela ukuthi iwayini elisha kufanele lifakwe ezitsheni ezintsha, ngakho-ke yimani niqine emseni enginikhulule ngawo mina, uKristu Jesu.

Bantwana bami, anikwazi ukwephula imithetho kaNkulunkulu lapho nihamba emoyeni, ngokuba akunakwenzeka. Nakhiwe phezu kwesisekelo sabaphostoli nabaprofethi, uJesu Kristu ngokwakhe eyitshe legumbi eliyinhloko. Futhi kwashiwo kumaHebheru: Ngokuba ngiyakuba nomusa ekungalungini kwabo, nezono zabo nobubi babo angisayikukhumbula. Ngokuthi uthi, isivumelwano esisha, usenze esokuqala sibe sidala. Manje lokho okubolayo nokugugayo sekulungele ukunyamalala. Bantwana bami, ukulunga akuveli ngokugcina umthetho, ngokuba kuyisipho esitholwa ngokukholwa kimi, uKristu Jesu. Futhi ngenxa yokuthi nenziwe abalungileyo kimi, nikhululiwe emthethweni wesono nokufa.

Ngokuba kuwukuhlambalaza ukuphetha ngokuthi ukuhamba emoyeni wokuphila kuyisono ngandlela thize, ngokuba noma ngubani okholwa kanjalo uthatha umhlatshelo wami wezono zabantu njengento engcolile. Ngokuba ngimkhulu kunoMose, ngokuba uMose ngokwakhe uthi: INkosi uNkulunkulu wakho iyakukuvusela umProfethi phakathi kwakho, kubafowenu, onjengami, uyakumzwa yena. Yizwani izwi lami, bantwana, nilalele inkulumo yami, ngokuba anginenzi nibe abangenamthetho, ngokuba amadodana kaSathane angenamthetho, hhayi amadodana kaNkulunkulu! Ngokuba kwabaseRoma uPhawuli wabhala ngokusobala: Ngakho-ke manje akukho ukulahlwa kulabo abakuKristu Jesu, abangahambi ngokwenyama, kodwa ngokomoya. Ngokuba umthetho womoya wokuphila, kuKristu Jesu, ungenzile ngakhululeka emthethweni wesono nokufa.

Ngokuba lokho umthetho ongekwenze, ngokuthi wawubuthakathaka ngenyama, uNkulunkulu ethuma iNdodana yakhe, ngesimo senyama enesono, futhi ngenxa yesono walahla isono enyameni: Ukuze ukulunga komthetho kugcwaliseke kithi, esingahambi ngokwenyama, kodwa ngokomoya. Futhi abaseRoma baphinda bakhuluma: Ukuze amemezele, ngithi, ngalesi sikhathi ukulunga kwakhe: ukuze abe olungileyo, nomthetheleli walowo okholwa kuJesu. Kuphi ukuzibonga, pho? Kukhishiwe. Ngamuphi uMthetho? Owemisebenzi? Qha, kodwa ngoMthetho wokukholwa. Ngakho-ke, siphetha, ukuthi umuntu uthethelelwa ngokukholwa, ngaphandle kwemisebenzi yoMthetho. Ngokuba ngithé: Ngokuba kuwo kuvezwa ukulunga kukaNkulunkulu kusukela ekukholweni kuye ekukholweni; njengoba kulotshiwe, Olungileyo uyakuphila ngokukholwa.

Kepha abaningi benu babekelana imithwalo engafanele ngokuthi nikholwe abaprofethi bakaSathane abathunyelwe ukunithiyela ekugcineni iTorah ngokungemthetho njengemfuneko yensindiso kanye nokuphila insindiso; futhi abanye benu bahlambalaza bathi ngawugcwalisa umthetho ngomhlatshelo wami ukuze manje nikwazi ukugcina iTorah. Ngakolunye uhlangothi, uqinisile, ngoba ukuhamba emthethweni womoya wokuphila kuwukugcina iTorah ngokuphelele ngokuzihlanganisa kwenu nami; ngokuba ibandla lami lakhiwe ngokuqinile ekulungeni nasebungcweleni beqiniso kimi, uKristu Jesu, itshe legumbi eliyinhloko. Niyayigcina iTorah ngokulalela ukukholwa okukimi. Kungani nikhetha izwi labaFundisi bamaJuda kunelizwi lami elingcwele? Kungani nibeka imithwalo engafanele kubazalwane benu, anifundanga yini? Ngokuba ngase ngitshelile abaseKolose: Ngakho-ke makungabikho muntu onahlulelayo ekudleni, noma ekuphuzeni, noma maqondana nanoma yiluphi usuku olungcwele, noma olweNyanga eNtsha, noma olwamaSabatha Okuyisithunzi sezinto ezizayo, kepha umzimba ungowoKristu.

Bantwana bami, angiwulahli umthetho; ngiveza kuphela okusobala ukuthi ngenxa yesono esisenyameni yenu anisoze nakwazi ukugcina iTorah ngaphandle kokukholwa kwenu kimi, ngokuba olungileyo uyakuphila ngokukholwa! Kuyini ukukholwa kimi, ningabuza? Ukukholwa kimi kwakhelwe phezu kokuthemba amazwi ami nokuqonda ukuthi nihlanganiswe nami kuwo wonke amazinga, ngokuba niyibandla lami engiligeze ngegazi lami elingenasono; ngokuba ukuthethelelwa kwezono akunakwenzeka ukukuthola ngomhlatshelo wezilwane. Kholwani kimi, ngokuba ngokukholwa kimi iTorah iyagcinwa, ngokukholwa kimi umthetho uyagcwaliseka kini, futhi nilungile, nithetheleliwe, futhi ningcwelisiwe! Lapho ngivuswa ebusha bokuphila, nani navuswa; futhi lapho ngenyuselwa esandleni sokunene sikaBaba, nani nahlaliswa ezulwini. Ngokuba abase-Efesu bayafakaza: Kepha uNkulunkulu ocebile ngesihe, ngenxa yothando lwakhe olukhulu asithanda ngalo, Noma sase sifile ezonweni, wasiphilisa kanye noKristu (ngomusa nisindisiwe), Futhi wasivusa kanye, wasihlalisa kanye ezindaweni zasezulwini kuKristu Jesu. Kufanele niqonde ukuthi umthetho kaMose akusona isivumelwano senu, ngokuba esenu yisivumelwano esisha esiqiniswe ngegazi lami futhi sakhokhelwa ngokuhlupheka kwami.

Ngakho-ke lalelani ukukholwa, bantu bami, futhi ningabe nisaba sivivi. Khethani ukuphila kimi, uJesu Kristu, ngokuba nabizelwa lokho. Yenzani imisebenzi eminingi emikhulu engininike yona, ngisho nakulo mzuzu wokugcina. Ngidinga ukuba niqonde ukuthi ngaphandle kwami, aninakwenza lutho, futhi nigcina zonke izici zeTorah ngokukholwa kimi. Ngaphezu kwalokho, abaFundisi bamaJuda abanamagama-angcwele baligcwalisile ibandla lami, bebeka imithwalo engafanele kulabo abafuna insindiso nakulabo abangabami. UBaba wanginika igama elingaphezu kwawo wonke amagama, ukuze ngegama likaJesu Kristu wonke amadolo aguqe. Nicabanga ukuthi ngingabeka umthwalo ongafanele ezizweni ezikhuluma izilimi eziningi ukuba zisebenzise igama lami, uYeshua HaMashiach, ngesiHebheru kuphela? Ngizweni futhi ningizwe kahle, ngokuba ngiyeza, futhi ngizobhekana nabangalaleli nalabo abanikhathazayo.
