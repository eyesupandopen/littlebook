Okubabayo Nokumnandi
Disemba 10, 2024

Ukwahlulela Kuqala Endlini KaNkulunkulu!

Zwanini amazwi kaYeshua HaMashiach, bantu bakithi, ukuze niqonde izindlela zami futhi nize kuMesiya wenu weqiniso, ngoba ngiyanibiza, O Israyeli, ingabe nisazolizwa izwi lami? Ngakho-ke, thobekisani izindlebe zenu ngokulalela futhi nisoke izinhliziyo zenu, ukuze ohlakaniphileyo phakathi kwenu aqhubeke nokuhlakanipha kakhulu uma elalela imfundiso yami futhi ethobela iziyalezo zami. Ngoba ngiza ukuzonitshela ngalabo abayothatha uphawu lwesilo, ukuze niqonde isiqalo sabo kuze kube sekupheleni kwabo, futhi ningabanjwa ngogibe lwalo.

Ngakho-ke, qondani izikhathi eniphila kuzo futhi niqonde ukwehlukaniswa kokugcina osekuzoqala masinyane nje lapho abakholwayo bami kuKristu sebebuthelwe kimi emafwini ngokuzuma nangokungalindelekile manje. Kunjalo-ke futhi kuyoba njalo ukuthi ngizokwehla ngokuzuma nangokungalindelekile ezulwini ngiye emafwini ngiyobutha abakholwayo kuKristu lapho icilongo likaNkulunkulu likhala ngokumemeza okukhulu ngezwi lengelosi enkulu. Khona-ke abafileyo kuKristu bayovuka kuqala, nalabo abaphilayo nabasalayo bayobuthelwa emafwini ukuyohlangabeza iNkosi yabo yaphakade emoyeni, kanjalo bayoyiswa ekuphepheni kwamazulu futhi bayohlala nayo phakade, baguqulelwe ukufana neNkosi yabo yaphakade yokulunga!

Nokho, ukubonakala kwami ngeke kwenzeke ngasese njengoba amaBhayibheli okufunda angamanga efundisa ngokuyiphutha, futhi ngeke kubekhona isikhathi esisha samanga esizokwehla emhlabeni sehlukanise u-Israyeli neBandla lami leqiniso, ngoba abakholwayo kuKristu Jesu bangama-Israyeli kaNkulunkulu futhi bayothatha izindawo zabo phakathi kwezinhlelo nezizwe emazulwini futhi bangumuntu munye omusha. Kunjalo kulotshiwe encwadini kaPawulu kwabaseGalathiya: Ngokuba kuKristu Jesu akusizi lutho ukusoka, noma ukungasoki, kodwa isidalwa esisha. Futhi bonke abahamba ngalo mthetho, ukuthula makube phezu kwabo, nomusa, naphezu kuka-Israyeli kaNkulunkulu. Ngakho-ke, qondani ukuthi nginguye oyokwenza u-Israyeli angabe esaba umbuso ohlukene phakade naphakade. Ngakho-ke, yalahleni amanga abefundisi be-dispensationalism, izangoma zamaZiyoni, namukele iqiniso likaYeshua HaMashiach!

Ngakho ngininika ukuqonda ukuthi izinkathi ezihlukene nokuhlwithwa ngasese yimibono eqanjwe ngoSathane, lapho befuna ukubamba abangenalwazi futhi bathathe abamnene nabathobekileyo enhliziyweni ngobuqili, ngezinganekwane eziklanywe ngobuhlakani nezindaba ezingamanga. Nokho, ukubonakala kwami kuyokwenzeka ncamashi njengoba kulotshiwe futhi kuyobonwa yibo bonke, futhi umhlaba uyokwazi ukuthi uKristu Jesu ubonakele njengoba kwathenjiswa kubangcwele bakhe abakholwayo, eshiya ngisho nabasivivi ukuze kubhujiswe inyama yabo yesono ebathumbile esonweni, futhi kuyokwenziwa kanjalo.

Nokho, abakholwayo kuKristu bayobuthelwa njengoba kulotshiwe emibhalweni eNkosini yabo yaphakade! Futhi ngeke ngiphikwe ngayo impahla yami engiyithengile, futhi ngizobahlwitha ngamandla bonke abangabami ngokwethembeka, noma belele enhliziyweni yomhlaba noma bephakathi kwabaphilayo ebusweni bomhlaba: Bayobuthelwa kimi emoyeni ngokufanayo. Nokho, abawiweyo bayoqamba amanga ngokuhlasela kwabo okungamanga kwabafokazi lapho beyothi abafokazi bathathe labo ababuthelwe kuKristu, futhi impi enkulu iyoqubuka emhlabeni wonke, futhi abawiweyo bayoqamba amanga kaningi, kodwa ningakholwa nelilodwa izwi abalishoyo. Ngoba ukubuthwa kwabakholwayo kuKristu kunikezwa njengethemba nesiqiniseko sokuthi ukuza kwami kwesibili sekusondele ekupheleni kwesonto lamashumi ayisikhombisa likaDaniyeli, okuyisonto leminyaka eyisikhombisa lapho usuku ngalunye lwesonto lubalwa njengonyaka.

Nokho, abasivivi ngeke basahlala besivivi ngoba izikhathi zokwehlukaniswa kokugcina ziyobe sezikhululeke ngokuphelele lapho umvimbeli wami esusiwe futhi iqhwa liwa. Ngokunjalo nabasivivi bayofinyelela ezinqumweni zabo, lapho abanye bayovutha bhe ngokuzinikela kwabo nokuqiniseka eNkosini yabo uJesu Kristu, kanti abanye bayobanda njengeqhwa elisozowa olwandle, bezikhethela ukubhujiswa kwabo kwaphakade.

Noma kunjalo, mina, Kristu Jesu, ngiyokhela ukuzimisela okungenakunqotshwa kubo bonke abakhetha ukuphila okuphakade kuJesu waseNazaretha, futhi ngiyobe ngibaphefumulele ukuzimisela okungenakwephulwa ukuba babize iNkosi yabo uJesu kuze kube sekupheleni, kanjalo bayofika emvuzweni wabo waphakade, lapho ukuhlupheka kungafanele ukuqhathaniswa nesisindo saphakade nenkazimulo yemivuzo eminingi yaphakade enikezwa labo abanqobayo; futhi ngiyokwenza labo abangabanqobi bami banqobe ngendlela efanayo neNkosi yabo, ngisho nalapho nganqoba abawiweyo ngendlela efanayo phakathi nomhlatshelo wami njengeWundlu likaNkulunkulu.

Kuyokuba njalo-ke, ukuthi abaningi ababengazinikela izinhliziyo zabo eNkosini yabo uJesu Kristu kuze kube sekupheleni, esikhundleni salokho bayobandela iNkosi yabo yaphakade futhi ngokudabukisayo bayofulathela ukuphila okuphakade baye emlilweni waphakade wesijeziso, ngoba bayobe sebemlahle ngokuphelele uJesu waseNazaretha ngenxa yezinjabulo zesono, bazikhethele ukuhlushwa kwabo kwaphakade, lapho babengakhetha injabulo yaphakade ebukhoneni bukaYahweh ingunaphakade. Kanjalo abaningi abake baba nokukhetha kwaphakade kokuba ngamadodana kaNkulunkulu ingunaphakade, esikhundleni salokho bayofulathela ukuze bajoyine abalahliweyo abahambayo abayowa ingunaphakade ekufeni kwesibili.

Noma kunjalo, bonke bakhululekile ukukhetha, ngakho-ke abanye bayokhetha ngokuhlakanipha futhi abaningi bayokhetha ngobuwula. Ngakho-ke ngenani ngesango elincane: ngoba liyisango elibanzi, nendlela ebanzi eholela ekubhujisweni: futhi baningi abangena ngalo. Ngoba isango lincane, nendlela iminyene eholela ekuphileni, futhi bambalwa abathola indlela eholela ingunaphakade ezimpilweni zokulunga okungapheliyo, futhi akukho okunye ukukhetha okukhona. Ngakho-ke, khethani ukuphila ukuze niphile, nina nenzalo yenu!

Nokho, ngiza ukuzonixwayisa, O Israyeli, ukuze bonke bezwe iziyalezo zami futhi balalele izixwayiso zami, bonke abaseduze nabakude nabahlakazekele phesheya, ngoba ukukhetha okufanayo kokwehlukaniswa kokugcina kuhlala kunjalo kubo bonke abozalo luka-Adamu abahlala emhlabeni, kungakhathalekile isizwe, noma uhlanga, noma ulimi abazalwa kulo. Kuyokuba njalo-ke ukuthi lapho abakholwayo bami kuKristu bethathwa emhlabeni bayiswa emazulwini ukuze bafihlwe olakeni oluzayo futhi baklonyeliswe ngokugcwele ngokuqeda izifundo zabo ngokulalela okuthembekileyo kuKristu Jesu iNkosi yabo evusiwe futhi yenyukela; umhlaba uyoguqiswa ngamadolo ngokungakholwa lapho ngibonakala nangembubhiso ezumayo elandelayo.

Kunjalo, ngoba ngizokhombisa inkazimulo yami ngokungalindelekile ekubonakaleni kwami okuzumayo emafwini, futhi ukukhanya kwami kuyokhanyisa amazulu, futhi bonke bayobona inkazimulo yeNkosi igcwalisa imikhawulo ngokukhanya okukhazimulayo, futhi abaningi bayothathwa ngokuzuma, umhlaba uyozamazama, uqhaqhazele, futhi uthuthumele ebukhoneni bukaKristu Jesu, okukhanya kwakhe abaningi bayofuna ukufihla ubuso babo kukho. Ngokuba njengombani uphuma empumalanga, ubonakale nasentshonalanga, kuyokuba njalo nokuza kweNdodana yomuntu.

Isixuku esikhulu, ngisho nabakholwayo kuKristu, siyonyamalala ngokuzuma nangokungalindelekile singashiyi mkhondo emhlabeni, ngoba ukuphila kwabo emhlabeni enyameni eyonakele sekuphelile futhi izifundo zabo bayobe sebeqedile. Ngakho-ke, bayoyiswa enkazimulweni yamazulu, lapho bayokhazinyuliswa khona ingunaphakade futhi baklonyeliswe ingunaphakade. Bhekani! Bayokwenziwa bafane neNkosi yabo yaphakade yokulunga, futhi bayokwembathiswa ilineni elimhlophe elicolekileyo elingenabala, elingenakungcoliswa noma lonakaliswe. Bayonikezwa umvuzo omkhulu ngokwethembeka kwabo nemisebenzi yokulunga abayenzile egameni lami, ngisho imisebenzi engabanika yona ukuba bayifeze ukuze kube yinsindiso, inzuzo, nodumo lwabo.

Abaningi bayothwala imivuzo engakangeni ngokugcwele ezinhliziyweni zabantu ngalesi sikhathi. Nakuba kunjalo, abanye bayolahlekelwa lapho imisebenzi yabo ishiswa ngumlilo womcwebisi ukuze wonke amakhoba asuswe ezimpilweni zabo ebumneneni baphakade, futhi ibala lemisebenzi emibi lisuke kubo ingunaphakade. Qondani, ukuthi akekho noyedwa kini ohambe ngokupheleleyo phambi kwami, ngakho-ke, abanye bayolahlekelwa, abanye bayolahlekelwa bamukele umvuzo, kanti abanye bayomukela umvuzo abangabanqobi bami abayonqoba ngendlela efanayo nami nganqoba abawiweyo, futhi ngihlezi ngakwesokunene sikaYahweh uqobo, uMINA Mkhulu waphakade. Nokho, bonke abathathiweyo nababuthelweyo bayoba nokuphila okuphakade ingunaphakade futhi bayophila izimpilo zokulunga ingunaphakade, ngoba bamiswe kanjalo.

Ngokunjalo, njengoba abakholwayo bethathwa, nabaseleyo bayobona ubucayi besimo sabo, isixuku esikhulu siyokwamukela iqiniso nezinhlupheko okumelwe bazikhuthazelele, abaningi bayokhala balile ngokuphenduka, abaningi bayophila izimpilo zokuzisola zothando nokuzidela, abaningi bayolahlekelwa yizimpilo zabo othandweni nasenkonzweni komakhelwane babo abayobe besesimweni esibucayi nabo, isixuku esikhulu siyokuza kimi ngokubiza igama likaJesu Kristu oyiNkosi yabo yaphakade kuze kube sekupheleni!

Umhlaba uyozamazama futhi uqhaqhazele, imbubhiso enkulu iyoqubuka emhlabeni wonke, njengoba impi ezumayo, isifo esiwumshayabhuqe, ukungabi nabulungisa, indlala nokufa kuyobanga imbubhiso enkulu emhlabeni, umhlaba uyoguqulwa esimweni sawo samanje ngokuzamazama komhlaba okuningi okukhulu, abaningi bayobhubha ngokuzuma bengenakhambi. Nokho abaningi abengeziwe bayosala bephila futhi basinde ezinhlekeleleni ezizayo, abaningi bayosalele izinhlupheko zosizi lukaJakobe olwehlele umhlaba ukuze kuvivinywe abakhileyo kuwo, ngisho nokusindisa bonke abasazofika ensindisweni kuYeshua HaMashiach.

Ngakho-ke, qondani ukuthi kunezivuno eziningana zika-Adamu. Isivuno sokuqala sebhali esolibo singesabakholwayo bami kuKristu, ngisho nabo bonke abahambe ngokwethembeka ngomoya wami ezikhathini zami zomusa nesihe, ngisho nalabo abahlakulele isithelo somoya ezimpilweni zabo ngokulalela amazwi ami njengoba behamba emoyeni wami wobudodana, ababekwe uphawu ngalo kuze kube usuku olukhulu lokuhlengwa. Laba ngabakholwayo kuKristu kanye nabanqobi bami nomakoti wami nezinkulungwane zami eziyi-144 engizozithumela ukusindisa uJakobe esandleni sika-Ashkenazi no-Esawu.

Bese kuzoqala isivuno sikakolweni lapho ukolweni uvuthwa emasimini ukhothama ngokulalela ngenxa yesisindo sesithelo esikhiqiziwe, ukhula lona lungenasithelo luyoma luqonde ngokwenqaba, ukubhula okukhulu kuyokwenzeka, ukolweni wami uyobuthelwa ekuphileni okuphakade, ukhula lona luyoboshwa, luhlanganiswe, lushiswe emlilweni waphakade wesijeziso. Ngakho-ke qondani ukuthi bonke abenqaba ukuvunwa ngokulalela ekubhuleni kwami bayohlanganyela nalolu khula olubi efeni labawiweyo lamangilangabi aphakade elenzelwe ngokukhethekile abangalaleli nabahlubukayo, futhi ngeke baphunyuke. Kanjalo kuyokuba kwabangakholwayo nabangenalo ukholo abalahle isivumelwano sokuthula sikaYahweh, ngisho noJesu waseNazaretha obengabasindisa ngokupheleleyo.

Kuyokuba kanjalo ukuphela kombuso kaSathane, bantu bakithi, isikhathi sokwehlukaniswa nezivuno esilondoloza abalungileyo ingunaphakade futhi sinikele abangalungile esijezisweni saphakade efeni labawiweyo, isiphetho esigcinelwe bonke abaqhubeka nokungalaleli nabahlala ekuhlubukeni koSathane, nabaGadileyo, nabo bonke abalandela izindlela zabo ezimbi. Laba bayolwa neWundlu, iWundlu liyobanqoba: ngoba liyiNkosi yamakhosi, noMbusi wababusi: nalabo abangakulo, ababiziweyo, nabakhethiweyo, nabathembekileyo.

Ngakho-ke, qondani ukuthi bayovuka emlotheni wempi, isilo sasolwandle, isilo sasemhlabeni, nodrako! Bayovuka emlotheni wembubhiso enkulu abayibangele bona. Nokho, bayosola imbubhiso yabo abayibangele ezitheni ezingabafokazi, nakumaKristu, nakuzo zonke izinto eziphathelene noJesu waseNazaretha, kanjalo uKristu uyokwenziwa isigebengu embusweni wabo wobuSathane. Ngiqinisile, ngiqinisile, mina, Kristu Jesu, angina nxenye nalutho nabo, nokho bonke ababiza igama leNkosi yabo uJesu kuze kube sekupheleni bayosindiswa.

Ngakho-ke bayolwa nabangcwele futhi babanqobe ngonya olukhulu oluyobuyiselwa kubo ngaphandle kwesihe ingunaphakade. Kunjalo, isixuku esikhulu sezixuku siyosindiswa ekubhujisweni kwaphakade ngokubhujiswa kwenyama yabo yesono ebavalele ekugqilazweni yisono. Ngakho-ke ngiyosusa zonke izinjabulo emhlabeni futhi ngephule imilingo nobukhunkuli ababi abakubeke phezu kwabo, ukuze bavuke ebuthongweni babo baphile ngokulunga phambi kwami njengoba bephuma esonweni ngokuphenduka okuphelele nokulahla umbuso kaSathane, ababewusekela ngaphambili ngokuvuma kwabo ekungalalelini.

Nokho, kanjalo nabawileyo bayoziveza lapho bezenza abafokazi abavela kweminye imihlaba engamanga ezungezayo engekho ngendlela efanayo neyabafundisi babo abangamaJesuit abafundise ngayo izixuku ngokufunda okuphikisana nokufunda. Kangangokuthi amanga abawileyo akholakale ngenkohliso enkulu kulabo abathokoza ngokungalungi. Ngaleyo ndlela isiteji sesilungiselwe laba bakhohlisi ukuba baziveze ngoba bonke bangabazenzisi abazenzisayo.

Bazokwenza sengathi bangabasizi abanomusa abeze ukuzosindisa u-Adamu ekubhujisweni kwakhe, okuyilapho empeleni, ukubhujiswa kwakhe kuyodalula ukuthi bona bakuhlele ngobuqili obukhulu nangayo yonke inkohliso nangezibonakaliso eziningi zamanga nezimangaliso. Ngoba bazoletha ubuchwepheshe babo nobuqili babo kanye nezinengiso zabo, nomgodi ongenakugcwala uyovulwa ngokuphelele. Ububi ngeke busavinjelwa futhi zonke izinhlobo zobubi ziyobusa emhlabeni ngesikhathi sokuhlupheka kukaJakobe. Ngisho nangenxa yokuthi umuntu wemvelo ulangazelela kakhulu ububi nobumnyama, kangangokuthi uYahweh manje uzonikela umuntu wemvelo ngokuphelele embusweni kaSathane ukuze abaningi basindiswe, kunjengokungathi ngomlilo. Lokhu kungenxa yokuthi isintu sibi nezenzo zaso zimbi, okuyibubi obuthandwa yisintu ekuhlubukeni kwaso kanye noSathane. Ngakho-ke, isintu siyonikelwa ngokuphelele kuSathane osuvele ehlela isiphetho saphakade sika-Adamu, nakuba amacebo abo eyoba yize futhi bayothola isahlulelo esinzima nesinokhahlo kuphela, ngoba bakufanele lokho.

Ngakho-ke bayovela nezixazululo zabo kanye nobuchwepheshe babo, futhi bayovela bezenza abafokazi abavela kweminye imihlaba yokuzenzisa engekho. NoBaraba uyovuka, isilo esivela emhlabeni, futhi uyovuka ephethe ukulunywa yinyoka esandleni. Futhi uyokwenza bonke, abancane nabakhulu, abacebile nabampofu, abakhululekile nabagqilaziwe, ukuba bamukele uphawu esandleni sabo sokunene noma emabunzini abo. Futhi ukuze kungabikho muntu ongase athenge noma athengise, ngaphandle kwalowo onophawu noma igama lesilo noma inombolo yegama laso. Naku ukuhlakanipha. Lowo onokuqonda makabale inombolo yesilo: ngoba iyinombolo yomuntu, futhi inombolo yaso ingamakhulu ayisithupha namashumi ayisithupha nesithupha.

Ngakho-ke, qondani okubhalweyo nalokho uMoya kaYahweh akusho ngokusobala kulezi zinsuku zokugcina, bantu bakithi, ukuze niqonde ingozi yezikhathi eniphila kuzo: Uma umuntu ekhonza isilo nomfanekiso waso, futhi amukela uphawu lwaso ebunzini lakhe, noma esandleni sakhe, Yena lowo uyophuza iwayini lolaka lukaNkulunkulu, yebo, iwayini elicwebile, elithelwa esitsheni solaka lwakhe, futhi uyohlushwa ngomlilo nesibabule phambi kweziNgelosi ezingcwele, naphambi kweWundlu. Futhi intuthu yokuhlushwa kwabo iyokwenyuka phakade: futhi ngeke babe nakuphumula imini nobusuku, labo abakhonza isilo negama laso.

Nokho, bayovuka ngokufanayo, isilo esivela olwandle nesilo esivela emhlabeni, nodrako, futhi bayovuka ekubuseni kwabo okungangcwele futhi bayobusa umhlaba ngokungalungi nangobubi futhi akukho lutho oluyovinjelwa ezifisweni zezinhliziyo zabo ezimbi. Futhi ngaleyo ndlela bayothembisa izinto eziningi ezinkulu nezinamandla. Futhi bayogubuzezela futhi bahlobise ukulunywa yinyoka ngenkohliso enkulu nangamanga amaningi, futhi bayosho izithembiso ezingamanga ezizungeze lesi sihlungu okungekho muntu ongaphunyuka kuso uma esesithathile. Ngakho-ke, bonke abathatha uphawu lwesilo bayobe sebeziguqule unomphela, kube uguquko abangeke baphunyuke kulo, ngoba lokho okugobile akunakuqondiswa.

Futhi ngaleyo ndlela bayothembisa ukuphila okuphakade, futhi bayothembisa impilo ephelele nokukhululeka ezifweni, futhi bayothembisa ukuzwana okuphelele nobuchwepheshe babo abayothi buhlosele ukuthula, nempilo nokuphulukiswa. Futhi bonke abahlanganyela ekulunyweni yinyoka bayoguqulwa unomphela esithombeni sikaYahweh abadalwa ngaso, ngaleyo ndlela bazenze abalahliweyo abahambayo abayoba yizigqila zikanomphela ezinkanukweni zikadrako. Ngoba empeleni uphawu lwesilo lunikezelwa ukubhujiswa kuka-Adamu ukuze abawileyo babe nenkathi yabo yegolide emhlabeni bebodwa nezinengiso zabo futhi bengenaye u-Adamu abamzondayo. Imiklamo engenakwenzeka ngempela ngoba ngeke babe nayo inkathi yegolide kodwa isahlulelo esinokhahlo nesinzima esikhundleni salokho.

Ngeqiniso, ngeqiniso, ngokwamukela ukulunywa yinyoka bayobe sebenikele inkululeko yabo yokuzikhethela ekuzithobeni kudrako ngoba bayozihlanganisa nenzalo yabantu: kodwa ngeke bahlangane omunye nomunye, njengoba insimbi ingenakuxutshwa nobumba. Nokho bayoziguqula ngokufanayo ngokulunywa yinyoka nangobuchwepheshe obuqukethwe kuyo. Futhi bayozithola bexhunywe kwinethiwekhi yezinto ezizayo, lapho beyolawula futhi baphathe khona izigqila zabo ukuba zenze intando kadrako ngokulalela njengoba zihamba ngomqondo owodwa wama-zombie abangeke baphunyuke kuwo futhi lapho bonke ababanjwe khona beyolawulwa ngokuphelele ngamaza nemifanekiso yabo.

Nokho, udrako uyazi ukuthi unesikhathi esifushane kuphela, futhi ngeqiniso, isikhathi esifushane nesifinyeziwe kuphela anaso. Labo abamukela ukulunywa yinyoka, ngisho nophawu lwesilo, bayosheshe bazithole bekhashelwe futhi bayosheshe baqonde ukuthi bakhashelwe ngezithembiso eziningi zamanga, kodwa bayoba yizigqila zokufeza izinkanuko zikadrako futhi bayokwenza ububi obuningi njengoba belawulwa futhi beyalwa ngobuchwepheshe babo, okuyinto ukubaleka kuyo kuyoba yinto engenakwenzeka.

Futhi kuyoba kanjalo ukuphila kwalabo abalahla uJesu uKristu ngaleyo ndlela ngoba ukujoyina umphakathi wabo kuyodinga ukuthatha uphawu lwabo lwesilo futhi bonke abakwenzayo abasoze bathethelelwa futhi ngeke baphunyuke. Ngoba isikhathi sokuba abalungileyo bahlukaniswe ngokuphelele kwabangakulungile sesifikile, okuyibubi abalungileyo ababenqabayo. Futhi sekuyisikhathi sokuba abangakholwayo nabangenalo ukholo bajoyine ngokuphelele izinhlu zabahlubukayo ngoba bonke abenqaba uYeshua HaMashiach abanayo enye inketho ngoba bonke abenqabayo futhi abangafuni ukukhetha, bona labo ngokungalaleli kwabo kokuhlubuka nokwenqaba, bayobe ngokuzenzakalelayo bekhethe lelo chibi lomlilo elivutha ungunaphakade ngesibabule esigingqikayo.
