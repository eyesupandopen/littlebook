Ngiletha Izidlakela Ukuba Zigcwalise Intukuthelo Yami!

Bhekani, ziyeza! Ngiletha izidlakela ukuba zigcwalise intukuthelo yami! Yizwani amazwi ami, nina enakhileyo emhlabeni, wona amazwi kaYahweh, uNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade. Ngokuba njengoba kwakunjalo ezinsukwini zikaNowa, kuyokuba njalo futhi: Kwakukhona izidlakela emhlabeni ngalezo zinsuku, kusukela ezinsukwini zikaJarede kuze kube ezinsukwini zikaNowa zadalwa futhi zazulazula emhlabeni ngokubi okukhulu. Futhi njengoba kwakunjalo, kuyokuba njalo futhi, nezidlakela ziyobuya. Ezinsukwini zikaJarede abalindi behlela entabeni iHerimoni, babengamakhulu amabili sebebonke ngezinsuku zikaJarede behla bevela emazulwini phezulu. Base bezibopha ngeziqalekiso ezifanayo, sona isifungo sabo abazibopha ngaso esesiphenduke isiqalekiso saphakade kubo bonke ababoshwe ngaso nabangeke baphunyuke kuso, base beyenga futhi badukisa isintu esonweni esikhulu.

Abalindi bahendwa oSathane, ngokwezinkanuko zezinhliziyo zabo, base bewela esilingweni besuka esimweni sabo sokuqala esikhazimulayo, futhi babenokuphila okuphakade emazulwini, ngokuba babedalwe kanjalo. Nokho bawela phansi, njengezinkanyezi eziwayo ukuwa kwabo kwenzeka, futhi bahlangana noSathane kuze kube phakade. Ngakho oSathane bayenga abesifazane abavumayo futhi babafundisa ukuyenga abalindi ngokwezinkanuko zezinhliziyo zabo, futhi abanye abesifazane bazinikela ngokuzithandela kubalindi, futhi ukwahlulelwa kwabo bayokuthwala. Abanye bathathwa ngendluzula ngoba abalindi bagumbuqela abaholi babo basemhlabeni basendulo, base beya bathatha abesifazane ngendluzula kumadoda abo nasemindenini yabo, ngakho abalindi bathatha bonke ababakhethayo baba omkabo, futhi bahlukumeza abaningi, futhi kulo mbimbi ongcwele kwazalwa izidlakela, izidlakela ezinkulu zasendulo. Ngoba abalindi baqhubeka bonakalisa ufuzo lwesintu. Kanjalo-ke bona ezilwaneni, ezinyonini, nasezinhlanzini, nasezinhlotsheni zonke zenyama bazihlanganisa zodwa nenye, ngokungalaleli okunenzondo nokuzithandela emikhawulweni engayibekayo, mina Yahweh, uNkulunkulu Omkhulu Waphakade! Kusukela ekuqaleni ngadala umhlaba nembewu ngayinye ngokohlobo lwayo, kanjalo kweqiwa imikhawulo okwakungafanele neze yeqiwe, futhi ukweqa imikhawulo yemvelo ebekwe uYahweh kuwukuletha uhlanya, ukuhlupheka, nokufa endalweni kaYahweh, futhi bonke abakwenzayo bayothwala ukwahlulelwa kwabo. Impilo yakhelwe njengendwangu exhumene, lapho yonke impilo ixhumene ngokuthula nangokuzwana, lapho uthando lubusa usuku futhi bonke befuna inhlalakahle yabanye hhayi eyabo kuphela. Ngisho nendalo lapho kungekho khona inkanuko nesifiso esibi esiziveza ekufeni nasekubhubhiseni, futhi kuyoba njalo kuze kube phakade naphakade, nezikhathi zokugcwaliseka sezifikile.

Ngakho-ke ezine zezingelosi eziyinhloko zamazulu, oMikayeli, Uriyeli, Rafaeli, noGabriyeli, baletha icala ngalokho ababekwenzile ozakwabo bakudala emhlabeni, kanjalo kwamenyezelwa ukwahlulelwa futhi kwenziwa ngokushesha kubo bonke abathintekayo nasemhlabeni uqobo, ngoba izwe langaleso sikhathi lalonakele ngokuphelele. AmaNefilimi, abantwana babalindi, enziwa alwe wodwa kuze kube sekufeni ezimpini eziningi ezinkulu phakathi kwezizwe eziphikisanayo, futhi lokho kungenxa yokuthi ngasusa ukuthula phakathi kwawo, futhi alwa aze afa futhi abulalana njengoba oyise (abalindi) baphoqeleka ukubuka ukubhujiswa kwezidalwa zabo ezinengekayo ababezidalele ukubhujiswa kwesintu. Kusukela lapho, abalindi baboshwa esijezisweni nasekwahlulelweni futhi bagcinwa behlukene isikhathi esiyizizukulwane ezingamashumi ayisikhombisa. Ngakho ngendlela efanayo engaphoqeleka ukubuka indalo yami engangiyithanda kakhulu ibhujiswa yizinengiso ezimbi, ngakho ngenza izidalwa ezimbi zibhubhisane esijezisweni koyise bazo, kanjalo ngaphindisela, ngoba ngilungile ekwahluleleni kwami njalo kuze kube phakade, futhi nginguNkulunkulu wokwahlulela okulungileyo okungehluleki, futhi okungalibali ukugcwaliseka.

Ngakho ngaletha uzamcolo omkhulu emhlabeni, futhi kwaba yisigameko esinobudlova uzamcolo ngezinsuku zikaNowa. Imithombo yotwa olukhulu yaqhuma futhi amanzi agqashuka ngobudlova emhlabeni uqobo, futhi amafasitela ezulu avulwa, okuvumela amanzi angaphezu komkhathi ukuba akhukhule umhlaba, ngisho nangaphezu kwezintaba eziphakeme kakhulu, futhi yaminzisa yonke impilo emhlabeni, ngaphandle kwemiphefumulo eyisishiyagalombili eyasindiswa iphila emkhunjini, ngoba uNowa watholakala elungile phambi kwamehlo ami. Ngenxa yalesi sizathu ngathatha uNowa ngamyala ukuba enze umkhumbi ngokweziqondiso ezanikezwa yisandla sami. Ngaphinde ngaletha kuye izilwane ezazizosindiswa ziphila, kanjalo uNowa wathembeka ekwenzeni konke ayeyalwe ngakho, futhi uzamcolo wafika njengoba kwakubikezelwe futhi kwamenyezelwa, ngoba lokho engikumemezelayo, mina Yahweh uNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade, akekho ongakushintsha noma akuvimbele nakancane!

Ngakho u-Enoke, umbhali wasendulo, uchaza isiphetho salezi zidlakela kanje, ngoba mina, Yahweh uqobo, ngamemezela kanjalo: Manje, izidlakela, ezidalwe ngomoya nenyama, ziyobizwa ngokuthi imimoya emibi phezu komhlaba, futhi emhlabeni kuyoba yindawo yazo yokuhlala. Imimoya emibi iphume emizimbeni yazo; ngoba zazalwa ngabantu, kubalindi abangcwele umsuka wazo nesiqalo sazo; ziyoba yimimoya emibi emhlabeni, futhi ziyobizwa ngokuthi yimimoya emibi. Ngokuqondene nemimoya yasezulwini, ezulwini kuyoba yindawo yayo yokuhlala, kodwa ngokuqondene nemimoya yomhlaba eyazalelwa phezu komhlaba, emhlabeni kuyoba yindawo yayo yokuhlala. Futhi imimoya yezidlakela iyahlukumeza, icindezele, ibhubhise, ihlasele, ilwe, futhi yenze umonakalo emhlabeni, futhi idale inkathazo: ayidli kudla, kodwa nokho iyalamba futhi yome, futhi idale amacala. Futhi le mimoya iyovukela abantwana babantu nabesifazane, ngoba iphume kubo.

Ngakho-ke ngesikhathi esifanele, uNowa wehlela eNtabeni i-Ararati, lapho umkhumbi waphumula khona njengoba amanzi ayebohla. Ngenza isivumelwano noNowa, futhi ngokuhamba kwesikhathi abazukulu bakaNowa baqala ukuphambuka esivumelwaneni engasenza noNowa, futhi ngenxa yalokho imimoya emibi yaqala ukubahlasela. Ngakho-ke le mimoya, eyayiningi, yehlula abantwana bamakhosana kaNowa, ngakho uNowa wabanxusela, futhi ngabopha okuyisishiyagalolunye kokuyishumi kwale mimoya emibi kwalasha, lapho ibigcinwe khona kusukela ngaleso sikhathi kuze kube namuhla. Ngakho ngakhipha umyalo, futhi uNowa wafundiswa yonke imithi yabo, futhi wafundiswa ukuyenga, ukuze uNowa abelaphe ngamakhambi omhlaba. Ngakho ingxenye eyodwa kweziyishumi yale mimoya yavunyelwa ukuba ihlale ikhululekile, ukuze idukisele ebubini amadodana abantu ababeyokhanuka amanga ayo. Ngakho imimoya esele eyayiboshelwe ukwahlulelwa kwalasha iyokhululwa ngezikhathi zokuhlupheka kukaJakobe, lapho kwalasha kuvulwa ngokugcwele futhi okuyisishiyagalolunye kokuyishumi kwabo ababoshiweyo kuyokhululwa ukuze basebenze izindlela zabo ezimbi nezonyanyekayo njengengxenye yokwahlulelwa komuntu wemvelo okulapha, futhi kuzogqashuka kuwo wonke umhlaba ngokuzumayo nangokubhujiswa okukhulu!

Kanjalo uKhanani, umzukulu kaNowa uNowa amqalekisa ngenxa yobubi bakhe, wenza isono esikhulu (futhi) esebenzisa ulwazi lwezingelosi eziwileyo, futhi ezindleleni zikaSathane wahamba ngokuzithandela, ayeholwa kuzo ngokuzithandela, futhi wenza ezinye izinengiso ngemva kukazamcolo kaNowa. Futhi lezi zinengiso zahlala emazweni kaShemi ayenikezwe ngenkatho yefa, uKhanani oqalekisiweyo awathatha futhi wazebela wona, futhi babiza izwe lakhe elintshontshiwe ngokuthi izwe laseKhanani. Inzalo yakhe eminingi eyisinengiso yayiyizidalwa ezishintshwe ufuzo ezazigcwele ngokuphelele emazweni esithembiso, engathumela kuwo uMose ukuba abuthe abantwana bakwa-Israyeli emazweni aseGibhithe ukuze bathathe ifa labo kwabayinengiso. Kanjalo uJoshuwa walwa impi enkulu nalezi zinengiso njengoba kulotshiwe emibhalweni engcwele, futhi bathatha kulezi zinengiso ifa labo elifanele, futhi kubhalwe kanjalo njengesithunzi sezinto ezizayo. Ngoba ngendlela efanayo indodana yami, uYeshua HaMashiach, engimenze iNkosi yamakhosi neNkosi yamakhosi, izokuza ukuzothatha ifa layo kwabahlala ngokungemthetho nabaphangi, ngoba ukuhlala kwabo akukho emthethweni. Ababona makhosi futhi abanambuso, kodwa ichibi elikhulu elivutha umlilo nesibabule liyifa labo laphakade.

Nokho, u-Israyeli akazange athembeke ngokugcwele obizweni lwakhe, futhi insali yezidlakela yavunyelwa ukuba isale. Kodwa ngisho nasekungalalelini kwabo ngiye ngazibamba lezi zinengiso futhi ngabhubhisa eziningi zazo emhlabeni kusukela ngalezo zikhathi. Futhi ngiye ngazixosha eziningi zazo ngaphansi komhlaba, futhi eziningi zazo zaya emazweni angaziwa ngale kweqhwa lapho isintu singazulazuli khona ngokukhululeka. Qondani ukuthi isintu asiwuqondi umhlaba esenziwe kuwo nesiphila kuwo, ngoba isintu sidla amanga angamampunge athengiswa njengeqiniso, aphinde ahlanganiswe njengemfundo, ngakho isintu sihlala singazi ngokuzithandela ukuthi ngezwi likaNkulunkulu amazulu ayekhona endulo, nomhlaba umi uphuma emanzini nasemanzini: Ngalokho izwe langaleso sikhathi, ligcwaliswe ngamanzi, labhubha: Kodwa amazulu nomhlaba, akhona manje, ngalo lona lelo zwi agcinelwe umlilo kuze kube usuku lokwahlulelwa nokubhujiswa kwabantu abangamesabi uNkulunkulu.

Ngakho-ke ngokuhamba kwesikhathi ngasusa izidlakela emhlabeni ngomusa wami nozwelo lwami nesifiso sami sokusindisa isintu ekubhujisweni, futhi izinsalela zazo zihlakazekile emhlabeni wonke futhi zingcolisa indalo yami njengobufakazi bokuba khona kwazo. Ngisho nezinsalela zempucuko yazo yasendulo zisemi njengobufakazi bokuthi zake zabusa umbuso wezizwe emhlabeni ngesikhathi senkathi yazo ebizwa ngokuthi yinkathi yegolide, ngezinsuku zikaNowa ngaphambi kukazamcolo. Amanxiwa azo amakhulu ahlakazekile njengobufakazi obumelene nazo, futhi khona kanye ukuba khona kwala manxiwa kumi njengobufakazi obumelene namanga kaSathane uqobo, ofuna ngenkuthalo ukufihla iqiniso ukuze abe nokuhlasela kwakhe kokuzumayo ngokugcwele. Ngakho-ke ngitshela labo abanezindlebe zokuzwa namehlo okubona nenhliziyo yokufunda okuzokwenzeka ngaphambi kokuba kwenzeke, ukuze izinhliziyo zabo zingehluleki ngokumangala nokungakholwa, kodwa ukuze bazi iqiniso futhi balungele lokho okuzayo ukuze basindiswe, bonke abakhuleka egameni likaJesu Kristu kuze kube sekupheleni kwabo.

Ngakho kwamenyezelwa futhi sekwenzekile ekugcwalisekeni kwezikhathi, ukuthi ngakhulula abalindi emajele abo lapho babeboshwe khona behlushwa futhi bejeziswa, baboshwe behlukene bodwa. Futhi bakhululwa ngemva kokuba izikhathi zokuboshwa kwabo seziphelile: izizukulwane ezingamashumi ayisikhombisa. Umlando awukwazi ukukufihla ngokugcwele ukukhululwa kwabo, futhi izimpi eziningi nezinhlekelele nezinye izigameko zenzeka emhlabeni wonke ngokukhululwa kwabo, futhi baziphonsa empumalanga, futhi bazihlanganisa nokuhlubuka kukaSathane, ngokugcwele, futhi, ngoba bazibopha kanjalo kuze kube phakade esifungweni sabo esiqalekisiwe, futhi echibini lomlilo izifungo zabo ziyogcwaliseka kuze kube phakade, ngoba baboshelwe esijezisweni sikaSathane nasefeni lakhe kuze kube phakade.

Ngakho-ke bazilungiselele ngesikhathi abasinikiwe nangendlela elinganiselwe amadunyule ambalwa manje. Ngokuqinisekile baphinde bakhiqiza izinengiso zabo, futhi emfihlakalweni bayazilungiselela futhi balindele ukususwa komvimbeli lapho bezophinde bazame ukwephula imingcele engiyibekele bona, ngoba bahlale behlubuka. Futhi nakuba beboshelwe ukufeza izinjongo zika-El Shaddai, ngoba nginguNkulunkulu uMninimandla onke, bazofuna ukuphinda basungule inkathi yabo yegolide futhi bazithathele umhlaba, okuyinto engenakwenzeka; ngoba umhlaba ungowenkosi nokugcwala kwawo, futhi itayitela lomhlaba lithengwe yindodana yami, uYeshua HaMashiach, futhi linikezwe ezandleni zakhe ezinamandla njengefa laphakade, futhi uyolihlanganyela ngokugcwele nabathembekileyo, ababiziweyo, abakhethiweyo, nabahlengiweyo.

Ngakho-ke oSathane nabalindi bazilungisile, futhi balindele ngenjabulo ukususwa komvimbeli, ngoba kulokho kususwa umbuso wabo ukhululwa ngokugcwele, futhi umhlaba uyophinde ube njengasezinsukwini zikaNowa. Bheka! Ngikhipha umyalo, futhi ngiyabaletha: izidlakela ziyeza ukuzogcwalisa intukuthelo yami, zijabula ngesikhathi esisodwa futhi zithuka, futhi ngiyozivumela ukuba zikhululwe phezu komhlaba futhi ukuze ngifeze izinjongo zami. Ziyogqashula ngokuzumayo lapho zicashe khona, futhi zicashe emizini yazo ngaphansi komhlaba, futhi zicashe ngaphandle kweqhwa elizoshintshwa maduze, futhi emhlabeni wabantu ziyovunyelwa ukuba zihlasele, ziphange, futhi zinqobe, futhi uDrako uyozihola ngoba ziyingxenye yombuso wakhe. Kodwa isikhashana nje, isonto lamashumi ayisikhombisa likaDaniyeli, isonto leminyaka eyisikhombisa ziyokhululwa lapho usuku ngalunye luwunyaka, kanjalo kuyoba yiminyaka eyisikhombisa. Futhi ziyodla kakhulu, futhi izinhliziyo zabantu ziyobehluleka ngenxa yobuso bazo obusabekayo nobukhulu bazo obukhulu uma ziqhathaniswa nomuntu wemvelo.

Bhekani, Nginguye Omkhulu enginguye, engithumele amazwi ami ngenceku yami, futhi ngenceku yami amazwi ami athunyelwe. Ngakho nginitshela izinto ngaphambi kokuba zenzeke ukuze abantu bami balunge futhi baqonde okuzayo, futhi babe nokuqonda njengoba lezi zinto zizokwenzeka maduze. Futhi bonke abakhuleka egameni likaYeshua HaMashiach, uJesu Kristu ngolimi lwesiNgisi, bayosindiswa.

Ngakho-ke intukuthelo enkulu nokwahlulela okukhulu njengoba kwathenjiswa endulo manje sekuzofika ngokugcwele phezu komhlaba, futhi izikhathi sezikhona manje, kusho uNkulunkulu Omkhulu Waphakade! Inkathi yonke yomusa ivangeli lensindiso nemfihlakalo enkulu kaNkulunkulu (eyembulwa kuMphostoli uPawulu) ligcwalise umhlaba futhi liye ezingxenyeni ezikude kakhulu zomhlaba. Futhi nakuba abaningi, abaningi, abaningi basindiswa, bonke abeza kuJesu waseNazaretha ngokuphenduka okuphelele, bemenza iNkosi yabo yaphakade, izikhathi sezifikile zokugcwaliseka ngoba isintu siphinde samlahla umdali waso. Futhi mina, El Shaddai, uNkulunkulu uMninimandla onke, nginguye engiye ngavimba ububi obuningi ukuze izindlalifa zensindiso zifike ekuphileni okuphakade kuKristu Jesu, iNkosi yazo eyenyukile. Manje ngimemezela lezi zinto ngaphambi kokuba zenzeke, ngoba nginguYahweh, Omkhulu enginguye! Ngakho-ke batshele ukuthi uMkhulu enginguye umemezela ngokuqinile ukuthi ekubuthweni kwabantwana bami abathembekile endodaneni yami, lapho bevuswa ukuyihlangabeza emoyeni futhi njengoba umvimbeli esusiwe, ukuthi umbuso kaSathane wonke uyokhululwa phezu komhlaba futhi umhlaba uyoshintshwa.

Ngimemezela ekwahluleleni abakhileyo emhlabeni ukuthi ngiyokhulula umbuso kaSathane wonke, futhi zonke izinengiso zabo ziyogqashuka kuwo wonke umhlaba, futhi yonke leyo mimoya engayibopha kwalasha ngezinsuku zikaNowa iyokhululwa phezu komhlaba, ngoba izikhathi zokuhlukaniswa kokugcina nokwahlulela kokugcina zifikile emhlabeni, futhi usuku lokwahlulela okukhulu selumisiwe. Ngakho-ke umbuso kaSathane uyonikwa ukubusa phezu komhlaba, ngoba ngokobulungiswa bami, isintu besifisa kanjalo ukubuswa oSathane, sikholelwa amanga akhe okuthi unguNkulunkulu onomusa nomkhanyisi oletha ukukhanya, kangangokuthi manje sengizonika isintu lokho esikufisa kakhulu.

Futhi abawileyo bakhululwe ngokugcwele ukuze bahlulelwe, futhi baphinde benza izinengiso zabo. Futhi baboshelwe emhlabeni kanye nodrako, ngoba sinjalo isikhathi sabo, futhi bayozenza abafokazi, izidalwa ezinomusa ezivela kwamanye amazwe. Nokho mina, Yahweh uNkulunkulu Omkhulu Waphakade, ngifunga ngegama lami, ngoba ngeke ngifunge ngophakeme kunami, ukuthi akukho mazwe ajikelezayo ngamanga endaweni engapheli engenalutho, kodwa ngaphezu komhlaba kukhona amazulu, izulu lamazulu eliyizulu eliphakeme kunawo wonke, lapho uNkulunkulu oPhezukonke ehlezi khona ngobukhosi obungunaphakade esihlalweni sakhe sobukhosi futhi akukho okungacasha emehlweni ami.

Bheka! Sebezenza baziwe, nezinto zabo namabhola azo zizungezile, futhi umhlaba ushintsha ngokushesha okukhulu manje. Futhi njengoba umuntu wemvelo manje esenqabile isivumelwano sami sokuthula, indodana yami ezelwe yodwa, uJesu waseNazaretha, kanjalo manje ngiyenqaba umuntu wemvelo futhi ngiyomshiya ukuba abuswe ngokugcwele yizitha zakhe, futhi zona zilangazelela ukubhujiswa kwakhe. Nokho, ukwenqaba kwami akugcwele futhi akusikho okokugcina, ngoba isixuku esikhulu siyosindiswa ngesikhathi sokuhlupheka kukaJakobe, bonke labo abakhuleka egameni leNkosi, uJesu Kristu, kuze kube sekupheleni. Ngoba insindiso ngesikhathi sokuhlupheka kukaJakobe ayinakutholakala ngenye indlela. Ngakho ukuhlukaniswa kokugcina kuzoqala, futhi bonke engibamisele ekuphileni okuphakade kusukela ngaphambi kokusekelwa komhlaba bayofika ekuphileni okuphakade, futhi ngiyobavusela ekuphileni okusha, futhi bayoba nomvuzo omkhulu.

Ngakho yizwani amazwi ami futhi nigxilise izinhliziyo zenu emazwini ami esixwayiso nesimemezelo, ngoba izikhathi zabeZizwe seziphelile manje, futhi intukuthelo nokwahlulela nokwembulwa okukhulu sekuphezu komhlaba, ngoba mina, El Shaddai, uNkulunkulu uMninimandla onke, nginguye ohlezi phezulu, futhi akukho okufihliwe emehlweni ami, futhi manje sengizothuthela ekusindiseni ngokugcwele bonke abayizindlalifa zensindiso njengoba ngibahlukanisa nalabo abamiselwe ukubhujiswa kwaphakade. Kanjalo ukolweni uyohlukaniswa nokhula kuze kube phakade, futhi bonke labo abayingxenye yokuhlubuka kukaSathane bayonikezwa ukwahlulelwa, futhi izikhathi zokwahlulelwa kwabo sezizogqashuka kuwo wonke umhlaba.

Yizwani Amazwi Ami futhi niqonde ubunzima balokho okuzayo, ngoba usuku lweNkosi luwusuku lobumnyama, futhi ngubani ongamela?

Mina, Yahweh, uNkulunkulu Omkhulu Waphakade, ngimemezela lezi zinto ngaphambi kokuba zenzeke, futhi asisekho isikhathi. Noma ngubani ovumayo, makeze azophuza mahhala emanzini okuphila okuphakade, futhi isimemo sami sokuphila okuphakade simi, futhi bonke abakhuleka egameni leNkosi kuze kube sekupheleni bayosindiswa.

Mina, Yahweh, uNkulunkulu Omkhulu Waphakade, ngithumele la Mazwi ngenceku yami, futhi ngenceku yami la Mazwi athunyelwe!

Izikhathi sezikhona manje!
