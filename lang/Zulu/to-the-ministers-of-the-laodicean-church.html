Kuzo Izikhonzi Zebandla LaseLawodikeya: Yizwani Amazwi Ami!

Makoti wami nebandla lami, ngizweni othandweni lwami ngani, bantwana bami, futhi nithobele izwi lami izindlebe zenu. Yizwani amazwi ami, O zikhonzi zami zaseLawodikeya, nani kanye eninginike izipho. Ngokuba nginike abantu izipho, futhi nginike abanye baba abaphostoli, nabanye abaprofethi, nabanye abavangeli, nabanye abelusi, nabafundisi. Ngininike lezi zipho ukuze kupheleliswe abangcwele, kube ngumsebenzi wenkonzo, kwakhiwe umzimba kaKristu. Ngoba ngathini kubaphostoli nabafundi bami emibhalweni yezindaba ezinhle zikaJohane: Bhekani, ngizothumela isithembiso sikaBaba phezu kwenu. Kodwa hlalanini emzini waseJerusalema, nize nembathiswe amandla avela phezulu.

Futhi ezEnzweni zabaPhostoli bami kulotshiwe ukugcwaliseka kwalesi sithembiso: Kwathi selufikile ngokugcwele usuku lwePhentekoste, babendawonye nganhliziyonye. Kwase kuzwakala ngokuzuma umsindo ovela ezulwini onjengowokuvunguza komoya onamandla, wagcwala indlu yonke ababehlezi kuyo. Kwabonakala kubo izilimi ezihlukene njengomlilo, zahlala phezu komunye nomunye wabo. Bagcwaliswa bonke ngomoya ongcwele, baqala ukukhuluma ngezinye izilimi, njengoba umoya wabanika ukuba baphimisele. Futhi ukusungulwa kwebandla lami kulotshwe kanjalo emibhalweni ukuze nazi ukugcwaliseka kwalesi sithembiso esikhulu sezindaba ezinhle, sokuthi nginigcwalisile ngamandla avela phezulu, ngisho nasekuzalweni okusha, ngisho nakimi, uKristu Jesu iNkosi yenu. Futhi ngiyinhloko yomzimba, ibandla: ngiyisiqalo, izibulo kwabafileyo, ukuze ezintweni zonke ngibe ngongaphambili: Ngokuba kwathandeka kuBaba ukuba kimi kuhlale konke ukugcwala.

Zikhonzi zami zaseLawodikeya, ukugcwala kwami akuhlali emabandleni enu enziwe ngezithombe zenu, ngoba ngimi emnyango ngingqongqoza! Zikhonzi zami zaseLawodikeya, angizigodlanga ngobugovu bonke ukugcwala kukaBaba okukimi, kodwa ngathulula umoya wami nganenza naba namandla, futhi nginibeke uphawu ngalowo moya ongcwele wesithembiso. Akukho okungaphula lolo phawu, nakuba ningahamba niye ekuhlubukeni uma nikhetha kanjalo. Kodwa uphawu lukaBaba, uYahweh uNkulunkulu waphakade, alunakwephulwa; ngokuba akunakwenzeka ukwephula uphawu lukaNkulunkulu, futhi niqinisekile kimi uma nithembekile kimi, ngokuba izithembiso zami zingu-yebo no-amen!

Zikhonzi zami zaseLawodikeya, izipho zami kini azinakwa ngenxa yezindlela zenu zokusivivi, futhi niwucimile umoya engininike wona, niyenqaba ukuvula izipho engininike zona, futhi nivumela izipho zami zihlale zingavuliwe ekunganakini nasebudedengwini. Lokhu akuyona indaba encane okufanele ithathwe kalula, ngokuba kuyisono esibi kakhulu esibalelwa kini, futhi anilaleli izwi lami. Aniwahloniphi amazwi ami, futhi anizihloniphi noma anizifundisi izindlela zami. Ngoba ngathini uPawulu akulobele umholi osemncane webandla lami elalisanda kutshalwa, uThimothewu: Ungasideleli isipho esikuwe, owasinikwa ngesiprofetho, kanye nokubekwa izandla zabadala. Zijwayeze lezi zinto, uzinikele kuzo, ukuze kubonakale indlela ozuza ngayo ezintweni zonke.

O, ukube beniwafundile amazwi ami ngokuqonda! O, ukube beningakhumbula amazwi ami ngokholo, kodwa aniqondi futhi aninakholo! O, ukube beningalalela amazwi ami, ngokuba ukungilalela mina, iNkosi uJesu Kristu, kungukulalela umthombo wokuphila, futhi eziswini zenu kuyogeleza imifula yamanzi aphilayo, ngokuba ngininikile umoya wami. Kodwa esikhundleni sokufunda amazwi ami, nigqolozele izikrini zenu ekungalalelini okungenangqondo; nisuka esikrinini niye kwesinye, nijatshuliswa yisono! Futhi niyiziphukuphuku kangangokuthi nicabanga ukuthi ukungalaleli okungenangqondo kuwukholo olusebenzayo kimi, futhi nikhohlisiwe. Nizishaye indiva izipho engininike zona, ngisho nomuntu omusha, futhi esikhundleni salokho nichithe isikhathi nizijwayeza imvelo yenu yobuntu obudala ngeze njengoba nifuna ukwanelisa inyama yenu.

Esikhundleni sokuzijwayeza ezindabeni zikamoya, nizijwayeza ngobuze imizimba yenu eyonakele futhi ebolayo, ngokuba ukuzivocavoca umzimba kusiza isikhashana nje, futhi imizimba yenu iyabhubha. Ngokuba izwi lami liyankenteza kuze kube namuhla kusukela encwadini kaPawulu eya kwabase-Efesu: Echithile enyameni yakhe ubutha, nomthetho wemiyalo equkethwe ezimisweni, ukuze azenzele yena kubo bobabili, umuntu munye omusha, enze kanjalo ukuthula. Ngizweni, bantwana bami, ngoba ngiyanithanda futhi ngikhathalela imiphefumulo yenu, nani kanye baseLawodikeya bami abasivivi. Ngokuba nikhetha ukuhamba kumuntu wenu omdala nezindlela zakhe, ngisho nenyama yenu eyonakele, ngokuba niyitshengisa yonke intando yenu inyama yenu. Kunalokho, kufanele nigxilise izingqondo nezinhliziyo zenu kimi nasezwini lami lonke: Ngokuba umuntu ngeke aphile ngesinkwa sodwa, kodwa ngalo lonke izwi eliphuma emlonyeni kaNkulunkulu. Ngokuba ngokulalela obala amazwi ami (ngisho neZwi likaNkulunkulu, ngokuba ngiyiZwi elenziwa inyama futhi ngihlala phakathi kwenu nakini), nihamba emoyeni hhayi enyameni.

Ngokuba ngitshele ibandla laseKorinte lokhu okulandelayo: Kodwa thina sonke, sibuka ngobuso obungamboziwe njengasesibukweni inkazimulo yeNkosi, siyaguqulwa sibe sesona isimo, sisuka enkazimulweni siye enkazimulweni, njengokungathi kungomoya weNkosi. Bantwana bami, izincwadi zami ebhandleni lami (ngisho nezincwadi zothando engazithumela kuMakoti wami) ziyisibuko sobuntu benu beqiniso, ngokuba ubuntu benu beqiniso bufihliwe em misterini omkhulu kaNkulunkulu. Ngokulalela amazwi nemiyalo yami nizohamba emoyeni, futhi anisoze nanelisa inyama yenu eyonakele. Yonke imibhalo kufanele ihlonishwe futhi ifundwe, kodwa nina niyimfihlakalo eyayifihliwe kuNkulunkulu futhi manje isiyembuliwe, futhi kufanele nifunde lokho engakwembulela uPawulu ukuze niqonde indalo entsha enginenza yona kimi, uKristu Jesu.

Zikhonzi zami zaseLawodikeya, kukhona iphutha elikhulu phakathi kwenu. Senilahlekelwe yiyo yonke inhlonipho ngamazwi ami, futhi nishaya indiva izipho engininike zona kangangokuthi aniselaphi kodwa niguqulela ibandla lami kwi-pharmakeia nommese; ngokuba ukube beningilalele, bengizovumela ukuba kwenziwe izimangaliso ngezandla zenu. Kodwa nihlonipha amazwi abelusi abahlubukile ngaphezu kwamazwi kaYeshua HaMashiach, futhi ibandla lami laseLawodikeya likhokha inani lokungalaleli kwenu; ngokuba anisenandaba nalo, kodwa esikhundleni salokho nilivumela ukuba libune esonweni esingenamandla. Abelusi abahlubukile bahlonzwa ngamazwi engawanika uJohane encwadini yakhe yokuqala: Bantwanyana, sekuyisikhathi sokugcina: futhi njengoba nizwile ukuthi uMphikukristu uyeza, ngisho namanje baningi oMphikukristu, ngalokho siyazi ukuthi sekuyisikhathi sokugcina. Baphuma kithi, kodwa babengesibo abakithi: ngokuba ukube babengabakithi, babeyohlala nathi ngaphandle kokungabaza: kodwa baphuma ukuze babonakaliswe, ukuthi babengesibo bonke abakithi.

Aniqondi yini ukuthi labo abalawulwa yimimoya yomphikukristu bethula omunye uJesu esikhundleni somalusi weqiniso, mina uqobo, uKristu Jesu? Ngokuba lezi yizitha zesiphambano sikaKristu, futhi zimelene nami, futhi zizibeka esikhundleni sami, zifuna ukuthatha igunya lami, okungenakwenzeka. Lezi zingene emabandleni enu, ezikoleni zenu zobufundisi, ezikhungweni zenu ezahlukahlukene, futhi zilawula amabandla enu ngokuvuma kwenu ukusebenza ngaphansi kwe-501(c)(3), ngokuba bonke abangaphansi kwesivumelwano esinjalo bavalwe imilomo ohulumeni bezwe. Ama-501(c)(3) enu akhombisa ukuhaha kwenu inzuzo engcolile, ngokuba nizithengisile ngobufebe ngaphansi kwezindlela zikaBalami, futhi ninikeza amazwi amanga ngemali ukuze nizigodlele yonke imali abancane bami abaninike yona ekuphingeni kwenu okukhululekile enteleni! Senibe ngumngane wezwe, futhi izwe liyeza kunoma yini enamathiselwe egameni lami lapho umvimbeli esesusiwe. Nintula ukuhlakanipha kokomoya futhi aniboni! Nintula ukunakekela ukuvikela umhlambi kulezi zimpisi ezihahayo, ngokuba niyazazisa iziqu ezinamathiselwe emagameni enu, iziqu enginganinikanga zona; kepha niyazifisa. Ngokuba nganitshela ukuba nifise ngokulangazela izipho ezinhle kakhulu: Futhi nokho nginikhombisa indlela enhle kakhulu: indlela enhle kakhulu eniyenqabayo ezindleleni zenu zobuvila, zokusivivi.

Anisashumayeli isono nokuphenduka esonweni, kodwa nishumayela izinganekwane ezizwakala zimnandi ezingesizo ezami, futhi izinganekwane zenu zakhiwe ngamazwi amanga futhi ziholela ezindleleni ezingamanga; ngokuba anilutho ngaphandle komxoxi wezindaba obhucunga izindlebe zamavila. Ngikhuluma nani, baphostoli bami: ngokuba amehlo enu amnyama, futhi anisaboni ukukhanya kwami, futhi nimemezela amanga! Ngikhuluma nani, baprofethi bami: ngokuba nikhulumela omunye uJesu, futhi nishaya indiva amazwi ami, futhi aningazi, futhi niprofetha ngekusasa elingamanga! Ngikhuluma nani, bavangeli bami: Ngokuba nivangela izizwe ngezimfundiso zamanga zamadimoni, futhi nizenza ziphuze amanzi angcolile, ngokuba nizigcwalisa ngemimoya engcolile! Ngikhuluma nani, belusi bami: Ngokuba ninakekela izinkanuko zenu, futhi nenze isisu senu unkulunkulu wenu ngemfucuza yenu yefilosofi yokuzisiza; siphukuphuku, ngeke welaphe izimvu zami ngolimi lwamanga! Ngikhuluma nani, bafundisi bami: Ngokuba anisasifundisi amazwi ahlanzekile evangeli lami, kodwa nifundisa amazwi okuyekelela kobuhlangano, futhi kukhona iphutha elikhulu phakathi kwenu nonke, zikhonzi zami zaseLawodikeya. Nonke ninesifo esisodwa enifana ngaso: Anisayihloniphi imiyalo eseZwini lami, futhi anisasihloniphi ngisho namazwi ngamanye emibhalweni yami.

Nicabanga ngokuzidla ukuthi ningabahumushi ngoba niyakwazi ukukhetha incazelo ezichazamazwini zezwe nasezinkombeni zezwe ukuze nifeze izinhloso zenu: Izincwadi engingazibhalanga, ngokuba ngibhala imibhalo ngabaprofethi bami, ngokuba umoya wesiprofetho uwubufakazi bukaJesu! Abahumushi abakhuluma ulimi olulodwa, futhi seniphenduke inhlekisa! Esikhundleni salokho, nihlonipha amazwi abelusi abahlubukile, ngisho nabazenzisi bokhula abangenamsebenzi. Bangena emabandleni enu ukuze bazenze nina (ngokuba nina ningukolweni ebengingawubuthela esibayeni sami ukube benisaphendukela kimi). Kodwa esikhundleni salokho bangukhula, abazalwane bamanga abaphenduka baba yizikhonzi zamanga ezinidukisayo. Bayisitshalo somkokotelo nesinoshevu!

Laba ababi abasaphumi phakathi kwebandla lami ukuze babonakaliswe, kodwa niyabamema futhi nibaphoqe ukuba badle nani futhi nihloniphe amazwi abo amanga nezilimi zabo eziqambayo ngaphezu kwamazwi ebenginganinika wona, ngisho namazwi kaNkulunkulu Ophilayo, ngisho namazwi kaYahweh! Niyathengisa ngamazwi ami ukuze nibe nayo yonke inkazimulo nodumo okungokwami, iNkosi yenu uJesu Kristu. Ngiqinisile, ninomvuzo wenu, futhi ngizowuhlwitha umvuzo wenu ngiwunike omunye owayethembekile esikhundleni senu. Ngokuba anisenakungikhulumela mina, futhi sekufanele ngize ebandleni lami njengezwi lomemeza ehlane, ngokuba ningivalele umnyango ebusweni, nakuba ngisemi ngingqongqoza! Kukhona ihlazo elibi nokudelela phezu kwenu ngaphandle kokuba niphenduke!

Uma ningayinaka amazwi ami futhi ningalizwa izwi lami, uma niqhubeka ezindleleni zenu zokusivivi, ngizonishiya osizini lukaJakobe; ngokuba ngasho ngaphambili kini, baseLawodikeya bami, ngizonikhafula emlonyeni wami ngaphandle kokuba niphenduke! Futhi ngaphinda ngasho maqondana nalowo mprofethikazi wamanga uJezabeli lapho ngibhalela iThiyathira, ngokuba ngathini kuJohane uMambuli?

Nokho, nginezinto ezimbalwa engimelene nawe ngazo, ngokuba uyamyekela lowo wesifazane uJezabeli, ozibiza ngoMprofethikazi, ukuba afundise futhi ayenge izinceku zami ukuba zifebe, futhi zidle izinto ezihlatshwe izithombe. Futhi ngamnika isikhathi sokuphenduka ebufebeni bakhe, futhi akaphendukanga. Bheka, ngizomphonsa embhedeni, nalabo abaphinga naye, osizini olukhulu, ngaphandle kokuba baphenduke ezenzweni zabo.

Aniwafundile yini la mazwi? Ningazidla kangangokuthi nicabange ukuthi uYahweh noma uYeshua HaMashiach bangabakhethi buso babantu? Nicabanga ukuthi ngiyazihlonipha iziqu enizinika zona ukuze nizilengise phezu kwamakhanda abantu bami, njengoba nizenza amakhosi phezu kwefa likaNkulunkulu?

Mfundisi lokhu, Bhishobhi lokhuya; ukube beningazishayanga indiva izipho zami, isipho senu besiyonenzela indawo phambi kwabantu abakhulu futhi beningeke nizidinge iziqu zenu ezingenamsebenzi, ngokuba bengiyonihlonipha futhi nani beniyonihlonipha, kodwa ningihlazile! Kukhona iphutha elikhulu phakathi kwenu, futhi intukuthelo yami ivuthela nina, ngokuba nehlulekile emiyalweni engininike yona futhi esikhundleni salokho senibe ngabahlubuki, ngisho nihola abancane bami niye emgodini lapho nimise khona amatende enu. Nikhetha amanzi angcolile emgodini wenu ngaphezu kwamanzi ami ahlanzekile omoya ongcwele okuzothi bonke abaphuza kuwo bangaphinde bome!

Ninikeza amanzi angcolile, anukayo kubantwana bami emithonjeni ephukile, futhi intukuthelo yami iphezu kwenu! Ngizokwephuca umvuzo wakho ngiwunike omunye, ngoba njengoba ngishilo ngawe: Ngempela, unomvuzo wakho. Unomvuzo wakho wezihlalo zakho ozikhethele zona zokuzihlonipha, futhi unomvuzo wakho weziqu ezingenamsebenzi, futhi unomvuzo wakho wenzuzo engcolile, futhi uzoba nomvuzo wentukuthelo yami ngaphandle kokuba uphenduke ubuyele kimi ngokuthobeka. Ngokuba umvuzo wentukuthelo yami ululaka, futhi lapho ungilethela ukhuni lwakho, utshani, nezinhlanga, kuyosha kube umlotha phambi kwabo bonke, futhi niyotholakala niyizikhonzi zamanga!

Kukhona iphutha elikhulu phakathi kwenu nonke, nani kanye, baphostoli bami, baprofethi, bavangeli, belusi, nabafundisi! Anisazibambi ebudlelwaneni obusindisayo nami, uJesu Kristu, kodwa ningiyekile; ngokuba ngiyinhloko yomzimba, futhi kufanele ningibambe ngaphezu kwakho konke! ANGEKE nikhonze mina uma ningalazi ngisho nezwi lami. Anizwa, futhi ninenkinga yaphakade ezindlebeni ngaphandle kokuba niphenduke, ngokuba ngizophulukisa izindlebe zenu emimoyeni yobuthongo ukuze nizwe izwi lami, ngokuba ngikhuluma nani! Ngaphandle kokuthi ningibambe njengenhloko yenu futhi nihloniphe amazwi ami, ngizonishiya ningaboni nasemgodini nalabo abaniholile. Aniboni, futhi ninenkinga yaphakade emehlweni ngaphandle kokuba niphenduke, ngokuba bengizophulukisa amehlo enu emimoyeni yobuthongo ukuze nifunde amazwi ami nibone ubuso bami: kodwa kuseyilokhu ukulala okuncane, kuseyilokhu ukusonga izandla.

Senibe inhlekisa nehlazo, ngokuba izindlela zenu zokusivivi zihlambalaza igama lami elingcwele. Akukhona nje ukuthi aningibambi mina, Kristu Jesu, njengenhloko yenu (ngokuba ukube benenza kanjalo, beningakhuluma ngami), kodwa niyaqhubekela phambili ekuhlubukeni ngokuhlambalaza amazwi ami angcwele, ngokuba ngiyiZwi elenziwa inyama! Ngiyanibuza, anizifundile yini izixwayiso zami? Ngokuba ngiyafakaza kuwo wonke umuntu ozwa amazwi esiprofetho sale ncwadi, Uma umuntu engeza kulezi zinto, uNkulunkulu uyokwengeza kuye izinhlupho ezilotshwe kule ncwadi. Futhi uma umuntu esusa emazwini encwadi yalesi siprofetho, uNkulunkulu uyosusa ingxenye yakhe eNcwadini Yokuphila, nasemzini ongcwele, nasezintweni ezilotshwe kule ncwadi. Niyiziphukuphuku, anizwa, futhi aniboni kangangokuthi aniqondi ukuthi leso sixwayiso angisinikanga nje ngencwadi enkulu yokwembula kwami, iSambulo! Kodwa leso sixwayiso sisebenza kuyo yonke imibhalo engcwele! Ngokuba Izaga zami zimemeza kakhulu emigwaqweni kubo bonke abanezindlebe zokuzwa: Onke amazwi kaNkulunkulu ahlanzekile futhi acwebile; ngokuba uyisihlangu kubo bonke abathembela kuye. Ngakho-ke ungabeki lutho emazwini akhe, funa akusole utholakale unamanga.

Bafundisi bami baseLawodikeya, enenze ubungane nezwe. Bafundisi bami baseLawodikeya, enizenza amakhosi phezu kwefa likaNkulunkulu, nishumayela ukuthi niyinto enkulu. Bafundisi bami baseLawodikeya, eningawunakekelanga umoya wami engiwudalile phakathi kwenu. Bafundisi bami baseLawodikeya, enizinika iziqu ezingasho lutho nezibizo enizihlanganisa namagama enu. Bafundisi bami baseLawodikeya: Yizwani amazwi ami ningiphendule, ngokuba zonke izilimi ziyovuma!

Ngubani oninike igunya lokushintsha amazwi ami, ngisho neZwi leNkosi engilinike ngezithunywa zami? Izindlela zenu, nina bafundisi baseLawodikeya abavilaphayo, azizintsha, ngokuba kwathi lapho nje izincwadi zami zisakazwa emabandleni ami asanda kuzalwa alotshwe encwadini yezEnzo nakwezinye izindawo, amahlelo obuGnostic eholwa ngabaprofethi bamanga bobuGnostic ashintsha amazwi ami abhala kabusha imibhalo, ngokuba baphuma eBandleni lami ukuze bonke bazi ukuthi bangabelusi abahlubukayo!

Niyovuna umvuzo wezindlela zabo, ngokuba nizihlanganise nabo, niphila izindlela zabo, futhi nibonakalisa imimoya engcolile kaJezebeli, umprofethikazi wamanga enimbiza ngokuthi ungcwele! Ngiyayibona imisebenzi yenu emibi, futhi ilotshiwe emazulwini ngemininingwane ephelele emelene nani! Kufanele nikhulume amazwi engininika wona, futhi kufanele niwakhulume ngokwethembeka, ngobuqotho, ngaphandle kokushintsha nelilodwa igama lesiphi isiprofetho! Angiziphikisi, ngokuba nginitshelile: Khuthalelani ukuziveza ningenasici kuNkulunkulu, niyisisebenzi esingenamahloni, esehlukanisa izwi leqiniso ngokulunga. Amazwi amaningi ashintshiwe emibhalweni yami eminyakeni edlule, futhi uma nizofunda futhi nihloniphe amazwi ami engiwanikile, ngizonihola ngenise kulo lonke iqiniso, ngokuba ngimnikile kanjalo uMduduzi.

Ngizokukhombisa, uma nizolalela amazwi amaningi enginishiyele wona. Landelani imibhalo engayinika e-Antiyokiya, ngokuba ingamazwi ofakazi abaningi, futhi ofakazi bami abaqambi manga futhi abaphikisani. Bhekani kwi-Textus Receptus nigweme amaBhayibheli abahlubukayo: fundani ukuze niqonde! Kodwa nina, bafundisi bami baseLawodikeya, aniwahloniphi amazwi ami, futhi nishintsha amazwi ami ukuze ahambisane nezinjongo zenu ezingcolile, nifundisa abantu bami ukuze baqhubeke esonweni, ngisho nokubaholela ekujuleni kwezindlela zenu ezingcolile! Kukhona iphutha elikhulu phakathi kwenu, futhi anilandeli izindlela zami, ngokuba niyazidla futhi ninezinhliziyo ezilukhuni, futhi ngizoninikela ekubhujisweni kwenyama ngaphandle kokuba niphenduke nishintshe izindlela zenu. Ngokuba mina, Jesu Kristu, ngithumele lawa mazwi! Ningawashalazeli, futhi niphenduke ngesaka nangomlotha. Phendukani obala emabandleni enu nivume izono zenu kulabo enibadukisile, ngokuba ngizolususa uthi lwenu lwesibani. Phendukani nihambe ngokulunga, futhi ngizobuyisa isibani senu nelangabi elisanda kukhanyiswa, ngokuba yimina engizokhela umlilo womoya wami emphefumulweni wenu.

Buyelani othandweni lwenu lokuqala, ngokuba niyangithanda, kodwa kungengokwazi. Ningaphikisani namazwi ami, ngokuba ngiyeza masinyane kunalokho enikucabangayo, ngokuba ngiza njengesela ebusuku. Ngisho nasephutheni lenu, nicabanga ukuthi isela ebusuku ngumuntu ongena ngefasitela njengesigebengu. Zithutha! Oh, ukube beniziqonda izindlela zamaHeberu. Isela ebusuku kwakungumpristi omkhulu owayefika engamenyezelwanga ukuzobona ukuthi abapristi bakhe bathembekile emisebenzini yabo yasethempelini, ngokuba eminye imisebenzi yayiqhubeka ubusuku bonke kuze kuqedwe leyo misebenzi ngokomthetho. Uma umpristi omkhulu ethola umpristi ongaphansi kwakhe elele esemsebenzini, wayephonsa ilahle elishisayo ezingutsheni zakhe, ukuze umpristi azithintithe izingubo zakhe ezivuthayo agijime aphume ethempelini enqunu enamahloni. Ningalali lapho ngifika, kungenjalo niyowa phambi kwami ​​niqunu ninamahloni amakhulu angacabangeki, ngokuba ngiyoba namahloni ngani nginiphuce wonke umvuzo. Ukuyala kwami ​​kuphezu kwenu nesandla sami sinzima phezu kwenu, futhi ngaphandle kokuba niphenduke, niyoba senkingeni enkulu kakhulu ebeningayigwema.
