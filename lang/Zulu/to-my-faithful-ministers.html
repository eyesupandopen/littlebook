Ezikhonjini Zami Ezikholekileyo: Ngininika Igunya!

Ezikhonjini zami ezikholekileyo nakulabo abaphumile eLawodikeya!

Makoti wami, yizwa izwi lami uze kimi, ukuze ngikwazi ukududuza amanxeba akho ngikuqinise ngamandla ami. Ungesabi! Intukuthelo yami ayikho phezu kwakho, ngoba ngiyakuthi kuwe: Kuhle kakhulu, nceku enhle nethembekileyo; ubukholekile ezintweni ezimbalwa, ngiyakukubeka phezu kwezinto eziningi: ngena enjabulweni yenkosi yakho. Ngoba nginamazwi aqinile, amazwi okusola, kwabangalaleli; kodwa lokho kusola okuqondile akubhekiswe kuwe ngoba ukholekile, nakuba kufanele usawaqaphela amazwi ami, funa uwe.

Makoti wami othembekile, uyinhlavu yeso lami, futhi akukho ukulahlwa kuwe, ngoba ukuKristu, futhi wakhe izimpilo zakho phezu kwami, uJesu Kristu, lona kanye itshe legumbi eliyinhloko abalakha amanga abalilahla. Qhubeka uhlale ukholekile kimi, ngoba ngiyeza kuwe ngamandla amakhulu, ngokuba ukuphela kwesikhathi kuseduze. Ngiyanithuma ngamandla amakhulu nasemisebenzini eningakaze nizicabangele yona, ngoba beningazi ukuthi nazalelwa emfihlakalweni kaNkulunkulu ekuhefuzeni kwenu kokuqala, kodwa nikhule kakhulu emseni wami, futhi manje niyabona. Ngoba umusa wami ungumusa onamandla ongenakunqotshwa, nesihe sami singesaphakade naphakade.

Bhekani! kusho uYeshua HaMashiach. Lalelani mina niqophe amazwi ami ezibhebheni zenyama zezinhliziyo zenu. Ngoba ngiyanithuma; yebo, ngininika igunya ngala mazwi, nalabo engibanika igunya bayothola isiqinisekiso esingenakuphikwa sentando yami evuthayo. Nginithuma emisebenzini yabakhulu bami, imisebenzi abayiqala eminyakeni eminingi yamajubili edlule, futhi leyo misebenzi iyaqhubeka kuze kube namuhla. Ngoba incwadi yezEnzo ayikaqedwa ukubhalwa kuze kube yilapho uMakoti wami esenami. Khona-ke niyokuqonda ngokugcwele ezincwadini ezisezulwini, ngokuba izenzo zenu ngazinye zilotshiwe lapho, futhi sonke siyojabula ngokukholwa kwenu phambi kukaYahweh uqobo, ngokuba yena uyajabula nathi futhi ukini nonke.

Bhekani! Ngithuma abanye benu enkonzweni kaPawulu njengabaphathi bezimfihlakalo zami nanjengabaphostoli bezizwe. Bhekani! Ngithuma abanye benu emoyeni ka-Eliya ukuze nimelekelelane noJezebeli, umphrofethikazi wamanga, futhi niprofethe ngami, uKristu Jesu! Bhekani! Ngithuma abanye benu emsebenzini kaFiliphu umvangeli, lapho ngiyakuniyisa ezindaweni ngezindawo namazwi evangeli lami ezindebeni zenu, enziwe ngokwezidingo zabangasindisiwe, ngokuba niyobasindisa ngokushumayela isiphambano! Bhekani! Ngithuma abanye benu emsebenzini kaPetru, ngoba welusa umhlambi wami wawupha izwi likaNkulunkulu elimsulwa. Ngoba izikhonzi zaseLawodikeya eziyilawayo zondla umhlambi wami ngokudla okungenamsoco, namazwi angenamandla okukholwa. Bhekani! Nginenze naba abafundisi ukuze nifundise niqondise abantu bami ezindleleni zokulunga, ngokuba niyobafundisa njengoba ngihola! Ngoba niyobelusa njengoba ngihola! Ngoba niyobavangela njengoba ngihola! Ngoba abaprofethi bami bayokhuluma amazwi engibanika wona, engashintshiwe encazelweni nasenhliziyweni! Ngoba abaphostoli bami bayokhanyisa ukukhanya kwezindaba zami ezinhle ezikhazimulayo zemfihlakalo enkulu kaNkulunkulu, ukuze isisitho sikaMose sisuswe ezinhliziyweni zenu. Ngoba ubufakazi baseKorinte buyamemeza kakhulu! Kodwa kuze kube namuhla, lapho kufundwa uMose, isisitho sisekhona ezinhliziyweni zabo. Nokho, lapho isiphendukela eNkosini, isisitho siyosuswa. Manje iNkosi ingulowo moya, nalapho uMoya weNkosi ekhona, kukhona inkululeko. Kodwa thina sonke, ngobuso obungamboziwe sibuka njengasesibukweni inkazimulo yeNkosi, siyaguqulwa sibe isithombe esifanayo, sisuka enkazimulweni siye enkazimulweni, njengalokhu kunjalo ngomoya weNkosi. Mina, Kristu Jesu, ngisusa isisitho senu!

Bhekani, ngiyanithuma kule mizuzu yokugcina ngaphambi kokuhlangana kwenu nami ekubonakaleni kwami. Ngidinga ukuba nizwe izwi lami nilandele iziyalezo zami, njengoba nenza ngokukholwa, Makoti wami othembekile, ngokuba nithembekile! Ningahambi emsebenzini enginithuma kuwo ngokuqonda kwenu. Ningahambi emsebenzini enginithuma kuwo ngamandla enu. Ningahambi emsebenzini enginithuma kuwo ngekhono lenu. Ningahambi emsebenzini enginithuma kuwo ngamandla enu. Yizwani amazwi ami ngenhliziyo yenu yonke! Ngokuba mina, yebo mina, Kristu Jesu iNkosi ekhazinyulisiwe, ngithi kini: Ngingukuqonda kwenu, ngingamandla enu, futhi ngingamandla enu, futhi ngiyikhono lenu: kuphela ningangabazi!

Yenzani imisebenzi engiyonihola ukuba niyifeze, futhi ninakekele abantu engibanithuma kini. Niyozithoba yini kubantu abasezingeni eliphansi? Niyohlala, nishumayele, nisindise, futhi nihlanganyele nabangenamakhaya? Ngokuba ngithuma abanye benu ngaleyo njongo. Niyoncenga izikhonzi zaseLawodikeya ngokuthobeka njengomuntu ebengayoncenga uBaba? Ngokuba zinjalo izindlela zami. Niyokhuluma amazwi engininika wona, ngisho ningawaqondi ngokugcwele noma ningavumelani nawo? (Qinisekisani amazwi ami ngemibhalo.) Ngoba ngiyanithuma ukuba nenze intando yami, hhayi eyenu. Niyokwelusa umhlambi ngamandla okuphulukisa omlilo emoyeni wenu engiyowubhebhezela kini? (Futhi lapho umalusi omkhulu eyovela, niyokwamukela umqhele wenkazimulo ongabuni.) Yenzani imisebenzi yami, Makoti wami, niyeke eyenu imisebenzi, ngoba asisekho isikhathi, nezibani zizocima maduze.

Qondani, ngiyanithuma emsebenzini wami, futhi niyovuna umvuzo omkhulu uma nithembekile, ngoba kakade ninengcebo eningi ebekelwe amazulwini, futhi izindlu eziningi endlini kaBaba zilungele nina. Ngokuba inkinga kaJakobe iyizinsuku eziyisikhombisa zeminyaka eyisikhombisa, lapho unyaka ngamunye ubalwa njengosuku. Kunjalo nomshado woMkhwenyana noMakoti wakhe. Ngokuba kuyoba nezinsuku eziyisikhombisa zeminyaka eyisikhombisa emazulwini, lapho unyaka ngamunye ubalwa njengosuku, futhi niyohlala ninami, Makoti wami. Bese siyagibela!

Hambani ngezindlela zenu nihambe ngendlela enginiholela kuyo. Zithobeni ngothando nokuthobeka omunye komunye. Nakekelani insimu yami, ngoba ngitshale insimu yokuphila okuphakade leyo omubi angenakuyisiphula noma ayibhubhise. Niyokudla kuso kanye isihlahla sokuphila ahluleke ukunigcina kude naso, futhi akukho ukuphila okuchichimayo echibini lomlilo likaSathane, lapho eya khona, kodwa isijeziso kuphela, ukuzisola, ihlazo, nokuhlushwa. Lezi zinto azizona ezenu, Makoti wami, nokufa kwesibili akunakho amandla phezu kwenu, ngoba nisukile ekufeni naya ekuphileni kimi, yebo kuJesu Kristu, umsindisi wenu neNkosi yenu.

Ngakho hambani lapho nginithuma khona, nisebenze nabantu enginithuma kubo, nengibathumela kini. Ngoba abezi kini ngephutha, kodwa ngoba sengiqalile ukubaqondisa kini. Philani imfihlakalo yami niphile njengoba benza EzEnzweni, ngokuba niyisahluko sokugcina esibhalwayo njengoba niphilela mina, futhi maduze nizobona lapho nisemazulwini lapho senginihlwithele kimi, ngokuba ningabami! Ningumakoti wami, iBandla lami elikhazimulayo, engiyolenza libe elami, lingenabala noma umbombo, nanoma yini enjalo: kodwa ukuba nibe ngcwele futhi ningenasici, ngokuba nginihlanzile egazini lami lokuhlawulela!

Ngiyanibiza, Makoti wami, futhi nginibizela ezinkonzweni eningakaze niziphuphuthe ukuthi ningazifeza, ngokuba izikhonzi zaseLawodikeya zazama ukunihlalisa ebumnyameni. Ningabalekeli amandla engiwabhebhezelayo kini, ngoba isandla seNkosi, yebo isandla sami sesibusiso, siphezu kwenu. Ningazibalekeli izinkonzo zami engizibhebhezelayo emoyeni wenu. Ngoba ngizobhebhezela ilangabi elikhulu emoyeni wenu, Makoti wami, umlilo ozoncibilikisa zonke izinto ezinibambezelayo. Ungesabi! Hambani ngamandla ami, ngoba izikhonzi zaseLawodikeya ezingenakukholwa zizamile ukuniqinisekisa ukuthi aninamandla, futhi kunemiphumela emibi kakhulu kubo uma bengaphenduki.

Bhekani! Nginithuma emisebenzini lapho abanye behluleke khona, futhi niyohamba esikhundleni sabo, ngoba nithembekile: Kuphela bhubhisa ukuziqhenya kwenu, funa nibe yisisulu sobubi njengabo. Ningabambi izilimi zenu, ngoba ngizithukulile. Khulumani amazwi engininike wona, nihambe phambi kwami ngamandla amakhulu. Nicabanga ukuthi ngingumbandlululi wabantu? Ngoba manje isikhathi sezimangaliso ezikhethekile, njengoba ngaloba EzEnzweni: UNkulunkulu wenza izimangaliso ezikhethekile ngezandla zikaPawulu: Kangangokuthi emzimbeni wakhe kwalethwa kubagulayo amaduku noma amaphinifa, futhi izifo zasuka kubo, nemimoya emibi yaphuma kubo. Makoti wami wokholo olukhulu, qedani ngami imisebenzi enginibizela kuyo, ngoba siphelelisa izinhloso zami kini, futhi ilanga seliyashona enkathini yami yomusa, futhi maduze ubusuku buyowela ngokugcwele emhlabeni, ngoba niyahamba. Bhekani! Mina, Jesu Kristu, ngiyokwenza izimangaliso eziningi ezinamandla, ngisho nezimangaliso ezikhethekile ngesandla senu, ngoba lokhu kungukunikezwa igunya kokugcina okukhulu kweBandla lami ngaphambi kokuba nihlwitshwe ngenkazimulo.

Hambani, ngokuthula, futhi ngiyonihola. Yazini ukuthi niyophikiswa kakhulu ngumngane, umndeni, nesitha ngokufanayo. Lezi yizikhathi zokuhlukaniswa lapho ngithena abangakholwa ngihlanza isihlahla sami somnqumo ezindleleni zabo zokungakholwa, ngoba abazilungiselelanga umkhwenyana, futhi bayothatha izindawo zabo nezintombi eziyiziwula. Futhi ngihlanza abathembekile bami esonweni ukuze bakhiphe amagatsha amasha athela izithelo eziningi. Ningesabi, nicabange ukuthi anifanele izimangaliso. Ngokuba ngiyavuma, ngokwenu akekho kini ongenza izimangaliso; kodwa ngiyonika izibonakaliso eziningi, izimangaliso, nezimangaliso ezandleni zabathembekile bami. Hamba uthi ezintabeni, suswa uphonswe olwandle, futhi ungangabazi kodwa ube nokholo kuYahweh, nakimi, uYeshua HaMashiach.

Asikho isando somoya esingashaywa, ngakho yekani imizamo yenu. Ngokuba ningahamba phezu kwamanzi uma nigcina amehlo enu kimi futhi ningangabazi futhi ningabheki kwesokunxele noma kwesokudla kodwa nigxilise amehlo enu kimi, futhi aniyokushona. Yalani imimoya, ngokuba ingaphansi kwami ngakho-ke ingaphansi kwenu. Yalani imimoya ukuba iphume; ningaceli, kodwa yalani! Ningayivumeli ikhulume, kodwa yithuliseni; ningakhulumi nayo, ngokuba amazwi ayo anobuthi; kodwa yixosheleni ehlane lapho ingenayo indlu, futhi maduze izophonswa emlilweni. Phulukisani abagulayo! Khulumani nokugula egameni lami, nikutshele ukuba kuhambe; ningesabi, ngokuba ngiyoninika amazwi. Ungesabi! Akudingeki nikhulume amazwi abushelelezi; khulumani nje amazwi okholo engizowaphefumulela izinhliziyo zenu futhi ningangabazi.

Bhekani! Hambani phambili ngenxa yami, Makoti wami, ningangabazi lutho. Nginimemela emisebenzini engiyiqedayo, lapho iningi lenu seliyizisebenzi ezikanye nami. Kusekhona imiphefumulo okufanele isindiswe, yebo leyo uSathane azama ukuyifihla. Ngiyobakhulula ezibophweni zakhe ngemisebenzi yenu eminingi enamandla nezenzo zobuqhawe esiyokwenza ndawonye. Hambani phambili, Makoti wami, ningangabazi lutho, ngoba nginilungiselele izimpilo zenu zonke ngale mizuzwana embalwa yamandla nokulalela, futhi niqeqeshwe kahle yimina, nalabo engibathumele ukuba banakekele imiphefumulo yenu.

Makoti wami othembekile, ngiyakuthanda, futhi uyinjabulo yami nokuthokoza kwami. Ngiyamomotheka ngawe nsuku zonke, nokho awungiboni ngamehlo akho, kodwa usenokholo. Ngikhala ngezinhlungu zakho, nakuba ngikhala buthule izikhathi eziningi. Ngizwa ukukhala kwakho ucela usizo, futhi ngingumsizi okhona njalo ngesikhathi sokudinga. Ngiyakuthanda futhi ngiyakunakekela, futhi uyinhlavu yeso lami, futhi maduze sizoba ndawonye. Ngiyazi ukuthi ukhathele, ngoba ube qotho eZwini lami nasegameni lami, ngakho ngiyokuqinisa ngamandla kaNkulunkulu ongenamkhawulo nophakade, yebo uYahweh, ngokuba akekho onjengaye! Ungapheli amandla, ngokuba noma inyama yakho ingaba buthakathaka, ngiyokuqinisa ngamandla ami, ngokuba mina, Yeshua HaMashiach, ngikushilo!

Ngiyeza kini ngamandla amakhulu.

Nginithuma ngamandla amakhulu.

Ngiyanibiza, Makoti wami, ngenise ezinkonzweni eningazange nazi ukuthi ninazo kuze kube manje.

Ngithumela abanye benu ukuba baqedele imisebenzi yabangakholwa, futhi niyoba nomvuzo wabo.

Ngithena abangakholwa.

Ngihlanza abathembekile bami ezonweni zenu ukuze nithele izithelo eziningi.

Ngingumsizi okhona njalo ngesikhathi sokudinga.

Hamba indlela yakho, ume eThempelini, ukhulume nabantu onke amazwi alokhu kuphila. Ngokuba nina niyithempeli likaNkulunkulu ophilayo!
