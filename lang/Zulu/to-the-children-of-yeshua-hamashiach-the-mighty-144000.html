Kuni Bantwana bakaYeshua HaMashiach, Abanamandla abayi-144,000: Nginembula MANJE

Yizwani Yizwani Amazwi kaYeshua HaMashiach njengoba nithathwa niyiswa emazulwini ngeSikhathi senu seMANJE!

Yizwani Yizwani Amazwi kaYeshua HaMashiach njengoba ngiza ukuzotshela ibandla lami, umzimba wami kanye nomakoti wami, (abangaBantwana bami abayi-144,000 nani nonke eningabantwana abatholwe nguYihlo uYeshua HaMashiach) ukuthi nidalwe futhi nenziwa ngobuciko obugcwele buka-Adonai-YHVH uqobo futhi nonke ningubuciko baKhe obukhulu, nani nonke enikholwa kuKristu Jesu enabizwa ngaphambi kokusekelwa komhlaba!

Yizwani Yizwani Amazwi kaYeshua HaMashiach ukuze niqonde ukuthi mina uqobo nginikhulise nonke nina eniyi-144,000 njengabantwana kimi, ngakho-ke yizwani Amazwi ami niziqonde nina bantwana bami. Ngoba impela impela, azikho izidalwa ezinjengani ezizodalwa ngokuzumayo nangokungalindelekile manje, neze ndawo kuyo yonke indalo ka-El Shaddai, ngempela nihlukile.

Ngakho-ke yizwani Amazwi ami niqonde igunya lawo elikhulu, ukuthi ngiwalungiselele la Mazwi kuleyo ncwajana evuliwe engiyivule esandleni sesithunywa sami nenceku yami ukuze nonke nina eniyi-144,000 manje niqale ukuqonda ubuwena benu beqiniso nezinjongo zenu ezivezwe kancane kodwa ezifihlwe kakhulu kini emazulwini ukuze zembulwe ekuhlanganeni okufike emhlabeni wonke MANJE, lapho umhlaba usumisiwe kulesi sikhathi seMANJE!

Ngoba ngenza ukuthi uBiblaridion:144 aphakamise izandla zakhe ememezela futhi ethi isikhathi asisayikuba khona, futhi kunjalo MANJE ngamandla amakhulu negunya elinikezwe futhi lakhululwa yisithunywa sami esinamandla nenceku yami. Selah.

Futhi kunjalo ukuthi ngenze omunye wabayi-144,000 bami abanamandla, ngisho enye yamadodakazi ami engiyikhulisile nengiyithanda kakhulu, engowabayi-144,000 (ngoba nonke niyokwenziwa umuntu omusha munye lapho kungekho owesilisa noma owesifazane futhi kungekho isigqila noma okhululekile): Ngakho-ke, ngasebenza ngamandla kulesi sitsha sami esikhethiwe ngomoya wesiprofetho engiwubeke phezu kwaso futhi lokhu kungenxa yokuthi sithathe umqulu wami wempi engiwuvule ezandleni zencwajana yami encane, ngisho uBiblaridion:144 ngoba uyincwajana encane, futhi saprofetha njengoba ngangisebenza ngamandla kuso futhi ngihola izandla zaso ncamashi njengoba ngangihlosile! Ukuze ngaleso sikhathi siprofethwe ngala Mazwi egunya elikhulu manje akhululwe emhlabeni ngomyalo wami nguBiblaridion:144 azofinyelela nakanjani emehlweni, ezindlebeni, nasezinhliziyweni zabo bonke abathunyelelwe wona!

Futhi kungalo myalezo engiwuhlanganisile manje futhi ngawuhumusha ngokuqondile kubantwana bami. Kodwa futhi ukuze bonke abathembekileyo kuKristu Jesu baqale ukuqonda ubukhulu balokho enizoguqulwa kube yikho MANJE, nokuthi kungaziphi izinjongo nemiphumo.

Futhi ngakho ngininika ukuqonda ukuthi la Mazwi emibhalo manje ahlanganisiwe futhi akhululwe njengobufakazi futhi athwala ubufakazi ngokungenasici kusuka ku-Adonai-YHVH okuvela kuye zonke izinto, kuze kufike ebusweni obugcwele bukaYeshua HaMashiach ohlale enza intando ka-Adonai-YHVH wami ngokuphelele.

Ukuze niqonde inhloso yenhliziyo yami ngani nonke eninikezwe kimi ngu-Adonai-YHVH wami, ohlale enza ngokuphelele futhi ebonisa intando ka-Adonai-YHVH kuzo zonke izinto ngokuphelele nakuyo yonke into. Ukuze nifike ekwaziseni lokho okunikezwa ngokukhululeka ngesandla sika-Adonai-YHVH okunamandla, okungapheli, nokukhulu kunalokho eningakuqonda, kepha esimweni senu esiwile, esinesono semvelo, kodwa esingcwelisiwe anikwazi ukunikezwa ngokugcwele njengoba bekuhloselwe. Ngakho-ke, nibekiwe uphawu futhi nihlengiwe ngomoya wami ongcwele wesithembiso, ngawo engibeke ngawo uphawu lwesibambiso sami sempahla yami ethengiwe, futhi MANJE senilungele ukuhamba.

Futhi-ke MANJE, lapho nifika, ngizonitshela ngezinjongo zenu kancane ezisafihliwe kini kuze kube yilapho seniguquliwe.
