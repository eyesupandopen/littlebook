Ufakazi Ombili: Israyeli
Okubabayo Okumnandi
September 02, 2024

Ngenkohliso Balwa Impi!

Bhekani! Yimuphi umthiyi obeka ugibe emehlweni enyoni? Kodwa ngeqiniso, ngeqiniso, kubekelwa ugibe abantu bezwe lesithembiso, futhi impi isemhlabeni njengoba ulaka nokwahlulela sekuzokwehlela. Zibutheleni ezwini likaYeshua HaMashiach nizwe amazwi ami. Bhekani! Ngiyanitshela ngezinto ezizayo zingakenzeki, ukuze amazwi ami abe yisixwayiso kubo bonke abathunyelwe kubo. Ngoba injalo imvelo yamazwi okuphila engikhuluma ngawo, ngokuba mina, Yeshua HaMashiach, ngiyiZwi likaNkulunkulu elenziwa inyama, futhi ngihlala phakathi kwenu, ngokuba nina niyithempeli likaNkulunkulu ophilayo, futhi mina ngingumpristi omkhulu phakade ngokohlelo lukaMelkisedeki.

Ngeqiniso, ngeqiniso, amazwi ami azofinyelela emehlweni, ezindlebeni, nasezinhliziyweni zalabo abathunyelwe kubo, futhi azolondolozwa phakathi kwezimpi nezinkathazo ezizayo, ngaphandle kokuthi kuyoba khona ukusweleka kwemibhalo emhlabeni wonke ngoba ngenkathi umhlaba wawuniselwa kakhulu ngamazwi kaYahweh, uNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade, umhlaba wawamukela lawo mazwi isikhashana. Kodwa manje umhlaba usenqabile amazwi kaYahweh ngenxa yamanga kaDrago, ngakho-ke manje iZwi likaNkulunkulu liyoba yindlala, futhi kuyoba khona indlala yeZwi likaNkulunkulu, ngokuba zinjalo izahlulelo ezizayo. Nokho, wonke umphefumulo ophilayo obiza egameni leNkosi kuze kube sekupheleni uyosindiswa, ngisho nalabo abangakuthandi ukuphila kwabo kuze kube sekugcineni.

Kodwa yizwani niqonde amazwi ami, nazi niqonde ukuthi amazwi ami azolondolozwa, futhi azodalulwa emhlabeni, nezikhathi zokwembulwa sezifikile. Ngakho-ke mina, Yeshua HaMashiach, ngithumela ivangeli laphakade lezikhathi zosizi olukhulu, ngisho usizi lukaJakobe, ukuze isixuku esikhulu sisindiswe ngokungathi kungomlilo. Futhi kanjalo ivangeli laphakade selizokwembulwa ngesikhathi salo, nobufakazi busazobhalwa, namazwi kaYahweh ayogeleza njengemifula yamanzi aphilayo, futhi abhalelwe ukulalelwa yibo bonke abanezindlebe zokuzwa namehlo okubona nenhliziyo yokufunda. Futhi amazwi kaYahweh angamazwi aphakade okungekho muntu ongawalahla, awonakalise, noma awathulise, ngokuba umuntu owenza kanjalo uyozilahla yena, kodwa amazwi kaNkulunkulu waphakade ami phakade.

Bhekani! Izikhathi zosizi lukaJakobe sezifikile, nezikhathi sezifikile zokwembulwa kwazo zonke izinto, ngokuba uYahweh ukhethe lesi sikhathi ukuzembula kuyo indalo yakhe, ngisho nasebusweni bendodana yakhe ezelwe yodwa, uJesu Kristu. Ngokuba mina nginguJesu Kristu, iNkosi yamakhosi neNkosi yamakhosi enyukile, futhi ezandleni zami kunikezwe itayitela lomhlaba ngoba ngawuthenga ngegazi lami elingenasono, nomvuzo wami unami. Futhi ngizokwabela ifa lami phakathi kwabathembekile, futhi lowo onqobayo uyodla ifa lazo zonke izinto esandleni sami njengomvuzo wokuthembeka kwabo, ukuze bakhombise phakade inkazimulo yaphakade kaYahweh. Futhi uYahweh uyobakhazimulisa, nalabo abazithobile ekuthobekeni, ekulaleleni, nasekuphendukeni bayophakanyiswa enkazimulweni yaphakade futhi bakhanye njengezinkanyezi emkhathini, futhi inkazimulo yabo iyokhanya kuze kube phakade. Ngakho-ke ngiyanikhuthaza, zithandwa zami, ukuba nizwe izwi lami niguqe izinhliziyo zenu ekulaleleni.

Ngizweni, futhi ngizweni kahle; impi isifikile, neBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo liboshelwe ukubhujiswa; futhi ukwahlulelwa nokujeziswa kwalo akulibali kodwa sekuseduze. Futhi njengoba ngihlwitha iBandla lami, uMzimba wami, ngisho noMlobokazi wami ngimsusa enhlekeleleni ngimusa ekuphepheni kwamazulu, umvimbeli uyosuswa, futhi bayoba nokuhlasela kwabo okungazelelwe, futhi isikhathi sokuhlasela kwabo yimanje. Amakhosi ayishumi ahlangene odwendweni, futhi ngempela abeke izandla zawo ekulungeni; ahlelile, alungiselele, futhi amboze umhlaba ngamanga awo, ukuze abonakale elungile ekubulaweni kwezigidi ngezigidi eziyowela osizini nasezinhlungwini zempi yawo.

Futhi yisinagoge likaSathane uqobo elilawula umhlaba manje, futhi azifihla ezweni lesithembiso njengoba isicabucabu sizifihla emehlweni empango yaso kuze kube yilapho impango ingena olwembeni lwaso. Ngeqiniso, ngeqiniso, baluke ulwembu lwabo emhlabeni wonke, futhi babambe amakhosi omhlaba ezindebeni zabo zenkohliso, ngokuba ngenkohliso balwa impi, futhi nokubulala, ukufumbathisa, ukusabisa, ukuphanga, nezinye izenzo eziningi ezimbi. Ngokuba izindlela zabo ziphambene ngokuphelele, ngoba uSathane ubeka abaphansi kunabo bonke nalabo abonakele ngokuphelele njengabaholi bomhlaba ukuze balawuleke kalula futhi basetshenziswe ngokuphelele ngukhula uSathane alutshale phakathi kwamasimu kakolweni kaYahweh. Futhi izikhathi zokuvuna kokugcina ziyasondela lapho ukolweni uyoqoqelwa enqolobaneni yaphakade kaYahweh ngokuvuna okuzayo, futhi ukhula lukaSathane luyosishulwa ngokuphelele, luboshwe, luphonswe luphila kulelo chibi elivuthayo lesibabule esivutha amalangabi aluhlaza okwesibhakabhaka efeni lawoyise; futhi likusasa labo, ngakho-ke ningabesabi.

Kodwa kunjalo, isicabucabu sibusa ulwembu lwaso njengoba sifuna ukulawula umhlaba; ngokuba umhlaba unikwe ezandleni zikadrago ngendlela elinganiselwe isikhashana, ngisho isonto lamashumi ayisikhombisa likaDaniyeli, okuyizikhathi zakhona manje. Futhi kanjalo abathembekile eBandleni lami bayothathwa bayiswe emazulwini emvuzweni omkhulu, futhi bayokwamukela ifa labo, futhi umkhwenyana uyohlangana nomlobokazi wakhe esivumelwaneni saphakade, futhi bayoba nami kuze kube phakade nomaphi lapho ngiya khona. Futhi kanjalo itayitela lomhlaba engiliphethe ezandleni zami elivalwe uphawu okwamanje selizosulwa uphawu, futhi izimpawu zizothukululwa, futhi bazokhalisa amacilongo abo, futhi izitsha zizothululwa ngolaka. Futhi ukwahlulelwa okukhulu nokubhujiswa kuyogubuzela umhlaba, futhi inhlekelele iyokwehlela umhlaba wonke njengoba impi yokugcina yokuhlanganisa izizwe ngaphansi kokulawulwa kukaSathane iqhubeka, ukuze isilo nomprofethi wamanga bavuke babuse umhlaba ngonya olukhulu, futhi kuyoba njalo. Bayomemezela impi kuzo zonke izinto zikaNkulunkulu weqiniso, futhi ubuKristu buyoba semthethweni, futhi kunikwe izilo ukuba zilwe nabangcwele zibanqobe, futhi abaningi bayolahlekelwa yizinhloko zabo ngenxa yegama lami, futhi bonke abenza kanjalo bayothola ukuphila kwabo phakade emvuzweni omkhulu nobukhazikhazi baphakade, futhi bayophila ukuphila kwaphakade kokuthula, futhi bayojabulela ukunotha kwezwe.

Nokho, njengoba umhlaba ulethwa emadolweni awo angenakholo ekuzithobeni ebubini obumsulwa ngempi enkulu, unya, nokubhujiswa, isilo esivela olwandle nesilo esivela emhlabeni siyovuka ngokugcwele, yize okwamanje benza sengathi bayizitha ekukhohliseni nasekukhohliseni kwabo kwamanje ukubhubhisa iBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo. Qondani ukuthi ubaba wabo munye, ngokuba bangabakaSathane uqobo, ngisho nalowo mbhangqwana onengekayo udrago awukhethile ukufeza izinjongo zakhe. Futhi kanjalo isilo esivela olwandle nesilo esivela emhlabeni siyovuka, futhi siyoletha ukuthula phakathi kwezizwe, ngaphandle kokuthi kuyoba ukuthula kwamanga. Qondani ukuthi akukho ukuthula ngaphandle kwesivumelwano sokuthula esinikezwe nguYahweh ngezono zomhlaba, ngokuba mina, uJesu waseNazaretha, ngiyinkosi yokuthula futhi ngiletha ukuthula kwaphakade, futhi ngokulunga ngiyobusa umhlaba kusukela ezweni lesithembiso esihlalweni sobukhosi sikaDavide, ngokuba mina ngiyindlalifa yakhe efanele.

Futhi kanjalo bayovuka. Impi yezwe yesithathu iyovuswa phakathi kwalabo abakhothama ekhubhini labo elimnyama futhi bayokwenza impi emhlabeni njengoba befuna ukuqeda noma yini ephathelene negama likaJesu Kristu. Ngaphandle kokuthi labo abakhothama ekhubhini labo elimnyama bayolethwa ngokugcwele ngaphansi kokulawulwa yisilo esivela olwandle, esiyobahola njengoMahdi wabo. Futhi labo abasemazweni esithembiso bayoba nenkosi yabo yamanga, umprofethi wamanga, uMoshiach wamanga, futhi uyoba ngumakhi wethempeli lasezilwaneni, futhi isilo esivela emhlabeni siyobahola kanjalo ngenkohliso enkulu. Futhi labo abagqoka ikhubhu elimnyama emabunzini abo bayoletha ukuthula nalabo abakhothama ekhubhini labo elimnyama, futhi umhlaba uyohlangana njengoba belandela lezi zilo futhi bethi phakathi kwabo, ngubani onjengesilo? Futhi ngubani ongalwa nesilo? Nokho mina, Kristu Jesu, oyiNkosi yamakhosi neNkosi yamakhosi, ngizoza ukuzokwahlulela nokwenza impi, futhi ngiyosibhubhisa ngokuphelele isilo nomprofethi wamanga, futhi bayophonswa bephila kulelo chibi elikhulu elivutha umlilo nesibabule lapho beyolinda khona uDrago ukuba ahlangane nabo ngesikhathi esimisiwe.

Futhi kanjalo bayokwenza izithembiso ezinkulu zokuthula nokuphepha, futhi bayoqoqa labo abalahlekile phakade ekubhujisweni kwabo. Bayothembisa umphakathi omusha nohlelo olusha oluzodinga uphawu lwabo lwesilo ukuze babambe iqhaza, ngakho-ke ningaluthathi uphawu. Ngophawu lwabo bayothembisa ikhambi esifweni esiwumshayabhuqe futhi bathembise impilo nokuphila isikhathi eside, futhi bayozicabangela ikusasa elihle, futhi bayokwenza izithembiso eziningi zamanga kulabo abayobhubha ngokuthatha uphawu lwabo lwesilo.

Futhi ngakho-ke ngiphinda ngithi kulabo abahlala emhlabeni, ukuze bonke bezwe:

UMA UMUNTU EKHONZA ISILO NOMFANEKISO WASO, FUTHI AMUKELE UPHAWU LWASO EBUNZINI LAKHE, NOMA ESANDLENI SAKHE, YENA UYOPHUZISWA IWAYINI LOLAKA LUNKULUNKULU, ELITHULULWE LINGAXUTSHIWE ENDEBENI YOLAKA LWAKHE, FUTHI UYOHLUSHWA NGOMLILO NESIBABULE, PHAMBI KWEZINGELOSI EZINGCWELE, NAPHAMBI KWEWUNDLU: FUTHI INTUTHU YOKUHLUSHWA KWABO IYENYUKA KUZE KUBE PHAKADE NAPHAkade. FUTHI ABANAKUPHUMULA EMINI NASEBUSUKU, ABKHONZA ISILO NOMFANEKISO WASO, NANOMA NGUBANI OWAMUKELA UPHAWU LWEGAMA LASO.

Kodwa qondani, bantwana bami, ukuthi umphakathi wabo ngeke ube nokuthula, futhi izithembiso zabo ziyisiyengo sokuyenga abangazi ogibeni lwabo; ngoba yimuphi umthiyi obeka ugibe emehlweni enyoni? Nokho izinyoni eziningi ziyobanjwa ogibeni lwabo, ngisho nabo bonke labo amagama abo angalotshwanga eNcwadini Yokuphila. Qondani ukuthi bonke abathatha uphawu bayoguqulwa phakade, futhi bayobe benze ukuhlambalaza uYahweh, ngokushintsha i-DNA yabo futhi kanjalo bonakalise isithombe sikaNkulunkulu umuntu adalwe ngaso, futhi bonke abenza kanjalo abanakuthethelelwa, ngakho-ke ningaluthathi uphawu lwabo. Kungcono nilahlekelwe ukuphila kwenu kwasemhlabeni ukuze nginisindise phakade futhi nithole ukuphila okuphakade kimi, uYeshua HaMashiach, ngokubiza egameni lami kuze kube sekupheleni kunokuba nizame ngobuwula ukusindisa ukuphila kwenu kwasemhlabeni futhi nilahlekelwe yinoma yiliphi ithuba lokuphila okuphakade ekufeni kwesibili kwamalangabi aphakade lapho impethu yabo ingafi. Ngakho-ke khethani ngokuhlakanipha!

Umhlaba oyovuka emlotheni wempi uyoba umhlaba wesifo esiwumshayabhuqe, ukufa, indlala, ukungcola, ukungalungi, unya, uhlanya, nosizi olukhulu. Yonke injabulo iyosuswa emhlabeni, kodwa isilo esivela emhlabeni siyophoqa uphawu lwaso emhlabeni, ngisho nokulunywa yinyoka, futhi bonke abamelanayo bayolahlekelwa ukuphila kwabo. Futhi lokho kuyisinqumo abaningi okuyodingeka basenze ukuze bathole ukuphila okuphakade ensindisweni, futhi bonke abakhuleka egameni leNkosi kuze kube sekupheleni bayosindiswa. Nokho, umphakathi wabo omusha awuyikulethela ukuthula, futhi bonke abamukela uphawu bayoba yingxenye yomqondo owodwa, futhi bayohlanganiswa nobuchwepheshe, futhi bayoba izigqila zentando kadrako, futhi bayomkhonza, futhi bayongena kulelo chibi elikhulu lomlilo, futhi lokho kungukufa kwesibili.

UDrako, isilo sasolwandle, nesilo sasemhlabeni bayohola umfelandawonye wamakhosi, futhi bayolwa nabo bonke abafuna ukuphila ngezindlela zikaYahweh, futhi bayosola mina, Yeshua HaMashiach, ngezinhlekelele ezehlela umhlaba. Ekwahluleleni nasekuhlubukeni okukhulu bayohlambalaza igama likaNkulunkulu wezulu nendodana yakhe ezelwe yodwa, futhi bayokwenza izimangalo ezinkulu zokuhlambalaza. Isilo sasolwandle nesilo sasemhlabeni bayozihlela ngokumelene nofakazi bami ababili nezinti zami ezimbili zezibani futhi bayomelana nabo ngezibonakaliso eziningi zamanga nezimangaliso, futhi bayokhuluma amazwi amaningi okuhlambalaza. Futhi lapho ofakazi bami ababili nezinti zami ezimbili zezibani sebeqedile ubufakazi babo, isilo esikhuphuka emgodini ongenamkhawulo siyolwa nabo, sibanqobe, sibabulale. Nokho ezinsukwini ezintathu nengxenye bayovuswa, futhi izwi elikhulu elivela ezulwini liyothi kubo, Yenyukelani lapha. Futhi bayokwenyukela ezulwini ngefu, nezitha zabo ziyobabona.

Futhi kanjalo isilo sasemhlabeni, yebo, yena oza nophawu lwakhe lwesilo esandleni, yebo, yena oyoba umprofethi wabo wamanga noMoshiach wamanga, uyofika ukuzobusa izwe lesithembiso. Futhi usezithandekisile kakade kubabusi bezwe, ngisho nabaholi besinagoge likaSathane, ngoba uhlanganyela nabo ezizukulwaneni zabo zegazi ezonakele, futhi uyaziqhenya ngohlu lozalo lwakhe, futhi uDani uyingonyama encane okungeke kuphume kuyo uMesiya. Futhi kanjalo uyokwakha ithempeli labo njengoba befuna ngobuwula ukuphinda bamisa umthetho kaMose, izinto ezigcwaliseke ngokupheleleyo emhlatshelweni wami wokuzinikela njengeWundlu likaNkulunkulu, ngoba nginguye owazinikela njengePhasika eliphelele ngenxa yezono zesintu. Kodwa yimuphi umthiyi obeka ugibe emehlweni enyoni?

Ngiqinisile, ngiqinisile, bayobeka ugibe eSiyoni, futhi bayofuna ukuthatha abezwe, ngoba uSathane ufuna ukubhubhisa wonke umphefumulo wokugcina wohlanga luka-Adamu, yebo, uhlanga lwesintu; futhi ukube izinsuku zokuhlupheka kukaJakobe bezingafinyezwanga, bekungeke kusale nyama iphila. Kodwa kanjalo isigcawu siyolungiswa ngethempeli labo lesithathu, lapho kuyokwenzeka khona isinengiso sencithakalo. Futhi kanjalo ngiye ngaxwayisa: Ngakho-ke, lapho nibona isinengiso sencithakalo, esakhulunywa ngumprofethi uDaniyeli, simi endaweni engcwele (ofundayo makaqonde). Khona-ke abaseJudiya mababalekele ezintabeni. Osemuva kwendlu, makehle, angathathi lutho endlini yakhe: Nalowo osensimini, angabuyeli emuva ukuyothatha izingubo zakhe. Futhi maye kwabakhulelweyo, nakwabancelisayo ngalezo zinsuku. Kodwa khulekani ukuthi ukubaleka kwenu kungabi sebusika, noma ngeSabatha: Ngoba ngaleso sikhathi kuyoba khona ukuhlupheka okukhulu, okunjengako okungakaze kube khona kusukela ekuqaleni komhlaba kuze kube yilesi sikhathi, cha, futhi akusoze kwaba khona.

Qondani ukuthi lesi sikhathi siseduze futhi siyokwenzeka ngemva kwezinyanga zokuqala ezingu-42 zokuhlupheka kukaJakobe. Phakathi nezinyanga zokuqala ezingu-42, ofakazi ababili bayoprofetha, futhi bayokwakha ithempeli labo lapho into enjalo ingasadingeki; ngoba mina, Jesu waseNazaretha, ngiyiPhasika eliphelele lezono zesintu kuze kube phakade. Nokho, lezi zilwane eziyiziwula zimelene nami, futhi isilo sasemhlabeni siyozama ukuzibeka njengoMoshiach wabo wamanga, futhi lokhu kuwugibe lwabo.

Bayonxenxa labo bezwe abasala bengamukeli mina, Yeshua HaMashiach, uMesiya wabo weqiniso, futhi abaningi bayogijima bazibuthele kuMoshiach wabo wamanga, kodwa ngokungazi bamlandela ekubhujisweni kwabo. Ngoba isilo sasemhlabeni nguye obeka ugibe ezweni, futhi siyoyenga abaningi engxubevangeni yaso yenkohliso; khona-ke isicabucabu siyogxumela impango yaso, futhi siyokhaphela abantu bezwe. Anifundanga yini? Ngoba kanjalo, kulotshiwe: Makungabikho muntu onikhohlisa nganoma iyiphi indlela: ngoba lolo suku aluyikufika, ngaphandle kokuba kufike ukuhamba kuqala, nokuthi lowo muntu wesono adalulwe, yebo, indodana yokulahlwa, Engumphikisi, futhi iyoziphakamisa ngaphezu kwakho konke okubizwa ngokuthi uNkulunkulu, noma okukhonzwayo: kangangokuthi ihlezi njengoNkulunkulu eThempelini likaNkulunkulu, izibonakalisa ukuthi inguNkulunkulu. Ngakho-ke qondani ukuthi ukuhamba kuqala kulapho ngiyobuthela khona abathembekile beBandla lami kimi ukuba sihlangane nami emoyeni futhi ngaleso sikhathi umvimbeli uyosuswa futhi lowo muntu wesono uyokwambulwa ngesikhathi sakhe.

Kodwa uYahweh uke wazishiya nini izindlalifa zensindiso ekubhujisweni okuphakade, futhi ngikumemezela obala kungakenzeki: Ukuthi mina, Yeshua HaMashiach, ngisazokonga insali kaJakobe, futhi abaningi bezwe bayongamukela njengoMesiya wabo. Futhi ngiyobakhulula. Bhekani, kulotshiwe: Ngabona, bheka, iWundlu limi entabeni iSiyoni, futhi kanye nalo kukhona abayizi-144,000 benegama likaYise libhalwe emabunzini abo. Futhi kuyoba njalo ukuthi ngiyoza ukuzokhipha insali ekubhujisweni, futhi ngiza nebutho elingenakunqandwa eliyohamba isinyathelo ngesinyathelo njengoba ngiliholela ekuholeni abancane bami ekuphepheni. Mhlawumbe ungazibuza ukuthi bangobani abayizi-144,000 futhi ngamuphi umgomo bangilandela eNtabeni iSiyoni?

Abayizi-144,000 bakhethwe uYahweh ngaphambi kokusekelwa komhlaba isikhathi esinjengalesi. Bakhandwe osizini, futhi bayoba namakhono ahlukile namandla engiyobembathisa wona ezingutsheni zabo ezintsha engiyobanika zona, ngoba izingubo zabo zithungelwe bona ngokukhethekile futhi ziyoba nemisebenzi yabo nezimfanelo. Bayothathwa ekubuthweni kwabathembekile kodwa babekelwe eceleni izinhloso zami, yebo, izinhloso zikaYahweh noYeshua HaMashiach. Bafika phakathi nokuhlupheka okukhulu ukuzohola u-Israyeli ekuphepheni kodwa baqale bathathwe ukuze bashade noKristu, ngoba nginguMyeni, futhi bona banguMlobokazi wami, ababiziweyo, abakhethiweyo, abathembekileyo, nabeqiniso, yebo, labo engibenze baba msulwa njengentombi egazini lami lokuhlawulela. Futhi yini, Mlobokazi wami, eniyosebenzela izinhloso zikaYahweh ngomyalo wami ngezikhathi zokuhlupheka okukhulu kakhulu.

Bheka, kulotshiwe! Futhi owesifazane wabalekela ehlane, lapho enendawo ayilungiselwe uNkulunkulu, ukuba bamondle khona izinsuku eziyinkulungwane, namakhulu amabili, namashumi ayisithupha. Futhi kuyoba njalo ukuthi ngiyolungiselela indawo yokuphephela ehlane abantu bami, ngoba ngisazokonga uJakobe ezinkingeni zabo, futhi abaningi bezwe lesithembiso bayophendukela kimi, Yeshua HaMashiach, onguMesiya wabo weqiniso. Futhi ngiyobasindisa ogibeni abayobekelwa lona, futhi kanjalo ngixwayisa bonke ngaphambi kokuba kwenzeke ukuba bahlale kude nogibe futhi bangabalandeli. Ningakholwa yizibonakaliso zabo zamanga nezimangaliso, futhi ningawakholwa amazwi abo okuhlambalaza, kodwa khulekani egameni leNkosi, yebo, uYeshua HaMashiach, onguJesu Kristu ngolimi lwesiNgisi, ngoba nganoma yiluphi ulimi umuntu abiza ngalo igama lami, ngisho nabo bayosindiswa lapho bekhuleka egameni lami kuze kube sekupheleni.

Futhi kanjalo ngiza ukuzokwazisa abantu bami ngogibe abayobekelwa lona ngumprofethi wamanga oza nophawu lwakhe lwesilo esandleni. Futhi kanjalo ngitshela abantu bami ngokusobala ngaphambi kokuba kwenzeke lokho okuzokwehlela umhlaba ngaphambi kokuba kwenzeke. Impela, iNkosi uNkulunkulu ayiyikwenza lutho, kodwa yambula imfihlo yayo ezincekwini zayo abaprofethi. Futhi ngiyoza ukuzokhipha abaningi kwa-Israyeli abebeyobhubha futhi ngibaholele ehlane endaweni abayilungiselwe uYahweh, futhi lapho bayondliwa, badliswe, futhi banakekelwe kuze kudlule usizi. Ngoba kuyokwenzeka ekupheleni kokuhlupheka kukaJakobe, futhi kanjalo kulotshiwe futhi kuyokwenziwa kanjalo:

Ngase ngibona izulu livulekile; bheka, nanto ihhashi elimhlophe, nophakeme phezu kwalo ubizwa ngokuthi oThembekileyo noQinisileyo, futhi ngokulunga uyahlulela futhi alwe. Amehlo akhe ayenjengelangabi lomlilo, nasekhanda lakhe kwakukhona imiqhele eminingi, futhi enegama elilotshiwe, elingaziwa muntu ngaphandle kwakhe. Futhi wembethe ingubo ecwiliswe egazini, futhi igama lakhe libizwa ngokuthi, Izwi likaNkulunkulu. Futhi amabutho ayesemazulwini amlandela ngamahhashi amhlophe, embethe ilineni elicolekileyo, elimhlophe nelihlanzekileyo. Futhi emlonyeni wakhe kuphuma inkemba ebukhali, ukuba ngayo ashaye izizwe: futhi uyozibusa ngentonga yensimbi: futhi unyathela isikhamo sewayini sentukuthelo nolaka lukaNkulunkulu uMninimandla onke. Futhi unalo engutsheni yakhe, nasethangeni lakhe igama elilotshiwe, INKOSI YAMAKHOSI, NOMBUSI WABABUSI.

NginguYeshua HaMashiach, futhi ngithumele la maZwi ngenceku yami, futhi ngenceku yami la mazwi athunyelwe. Futhi mina, Yeshua HaMashiach, nginguye owembule ivangeli laphakade ngesikhathi salo, futhi izikhathi zokwembula sezik manje.

Bheka! Izikhathi zokwembula sezifikile emhlabeni, futhi ngimemezela ukuthi ukuhlupheka kukaJakobe, yebo, isonto lamashumi ayisikhombisa likaDaniyeli, sekufikile. Ngokushesha ngiyohlwitha abathembekile emhlabeni ngibayise ekuphepheni kwamazulu, futhi ngokushesha umvimbeli uyosuswa, futhi ngokushesha inhlekelele enkulu, ukwahlulela, nosizi kuyokwehlela abakhileyo emhlabeni. Futhi bonke labo abamiselwe ukuphila okuphakade bayofinyelela ensindisweni kimi, Kristu Jesu, iNkosi evusiwe yaphakade. Futhi umbuso kaSathane uyoshaywa ngokuphelele futhi ubhujiswe, ungaphinde uvuke.

Nginguye oza ukuzokwahlulela nokulwa, futhi ngiza njengeNgonyama yesizwe sakwaJuda, futhi insali kaJakobe ngiyoyisindisa, futhi ngiyobusa umhlaba ngokulunga embusweni wami weminyaka eyinkulungwane, futhi ngiletha ibutho elikhulu lamakhosi nabapristi ukuzobusa umhlaba ngokulunga ngomyalo wami.

NginguYeshua HaMashiach, iNkosi enkulu yamaKhosi noMbusi wabaBusi, ngakho yizwani amaZwi ami, ngoba izikhathi zokugcwaliseka kwawo sezifikile.
