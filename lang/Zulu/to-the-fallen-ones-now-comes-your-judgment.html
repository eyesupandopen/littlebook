Izimemezelo Eziyisihluthulelo ezivela kuWilliam Brooks, iBiblaridion kaYeshua HaMashiach uqobo

Kunikezwe ngoJulayi 6, 2025

Lombhalo uyisahlulelo sesimemezelo phezu Kwabawileyo bebonke, kodwa ikakhulukazi uSathane, Osathane, abalindi, amakherubi awileyo, amaserafi awileyo, yebo bonke abahlanganyele ekuhlubukeni kukaSathane: nizakusha ngokuphelele, kaninginingi kuze kube phakade, ngoba kumemezele kanjalo uYHVH onguMINA OMkhulu, kunikezwe ngomyalo kaKristu Jesu, onguYeshua HaMashiach futhi; kunikezwe ohlwini olufinqiwe, kodwa olunemininingwane futhi olunembile lwamacala nezahlulelo okuzomele zenziwe futhi zigcwaliseke ngokugcwele ezikhathini zazo futhi izikhathi zokugcwaliseka sezifikile: Bhekani Ukwahlulela Kuwa! Ngisho nezahlulelo zesimemezelo okuzomele zinikezwe ngokugcwele ezifubeni zabawileyo, ngisho izikhathi eziningi kuze kube phakade naphakade, aninakho ukuthula, nina nonke eniwileyo: Selah.

Futhi-ke mina, William Brooks, iBiblaridion:144 kaKristu Jesu uqobo, nginikeza lezi zahlulelo ezithathwe ngokuqondile Emidlalweni Eyisikhombisa, okuyizahlulelo ezinjengokuduma okufanele zifundwe ngokuqondile, futhi zinikezwe ukuze bonke baziqonde, futhi-ke laMazwi aqondwe ngokugcwele yilezi zidalwa eziwileyo uqobo ezizilahlele ingunaphakade naphakade ekuhlubukeni okungenangqondo, ukulahlwa abangasoze balulame kukho, noma bakunqobe!

Zwanini aMazwi kaYeshua HaMashiach engiza ukuzomemezela aMazwi akhe, yena onguKristu Jesu futhi, futhi nizwe futhi niqonde igunya elikhulu leNgonyama yesizwe sikaJuda, ukubhonga kwayo akudala enhliziyweni yami futhi ukubhonga kwayo kuphuma emlonyeni wami, yona leyo Ngonyama ebhongayo yesizwe sikaJuda engiyithandayo, futhi engiyikhonza ngokulalela futhi engiyilandelayo ngempela, kukho konke ukuzithiba, yona leyo Ngonyama ebeke ukubhonga kwayo okunjengokuduma ekujuleni kobuyimina ngoba ngiyisithunywa sayo esiza ngegunya elikhulu, futhi ngiphakamisa isandla sami ngifunga ukuthi isikhathi asisayikuba khona!

Bhekani! Ngingulokho nalowo iNkosi uJesu Kristu engenze ngaba yikho, yena uYeshua HaMashiach amkhulisa kusukela ebusheni bakhe, futhi wamfihla emphongolweni wakhe isikhathi esinjengalesi, Selah!

Bhekani! Umyalo owanikezwa ngehubo usuka esihlalweni sobukhosi sikaYHVH uqobo futhi-ke wahutshwa ngokholo olukhulu ezikhathini zasendulo: Ningathinti abagcotshiweyo bami, ningoni Abaprofethi bami! Kwasho isikhalo somculo esilondolozwe manje emibhalweni engcwele.

Nokho njengoba kulotshiwe: Yimuphi kubaProfethi obaba benu abangamzingelanga? Futhi bababulele, abakhombisa ngaphambili ngokuza kwaLowo Olungileyo, enimngcatshileyo nababulali bakhe manje!

Wena! Nyoka ewileyo. Wena! Ngisho nawe! Nguwe obulele igazi elingenacala laLeyo Ndodana yoMuntu elungileyo nengcwele, uJesu waseNazaretha, lelo wundlu likaNkulunkulu elingenabala elanikelwa ukuhlengwa okuphelele kuka-Adamu, futhi-ke ngimemezela ngegunya eligcwele leNkosi yami, uKristu Jesu: Uhlulekile nkosana encane futhi ukwahlulelwa kwakho akulibali, khona lokho kwahlulelwa okuminwe yakhona yelulelwe entanyeni yakho futhi ubambekile ekunqotshweni, uhlulekile nkosana encane, uhlulekile: Selah!

Futhi kunjalo-ke, sona kanye leso sithunywa nenceku eyaphulwa futhi yahlukaniswa ngokungafanele ngobuqili bakho, yaphucwa ubuyona bangempela ebusheni bayo futhi manje isidaliwe kabusha, futhi yakhululwa futhi yathunyelwa nezahlulelo zakho ezingunaphakade nezaphakade esandleni ezinikezwe zivela esihlalweni sobukhosi sikaYHVH uqobo, zamiswa ngegunya eligcwele kulobo buso bakhe obuhle obungunaphakade beNkosi yakhe enguNaphakade nomsindisi, obuso bakhe bukhanya ingunaphakade njengeNdodana!

Uhlulekile nkosana encane.

Ngakho-ke, yazi futhi uqonde futhi uzwe amazwi ami Heyele, ngisho nawe Heyele: leyo nkosana encane ehlulekile, sona leso sithunywa nenceku owawufuna ukuyichitha ibe yizicucu zokukhanya okukhulu ezihlakazeke olwandle oluyikristalu sekuqoqiwe, kwabuyiselwa, futhi kwadalwa kabusha njengeBiblaridion:144 kaKristu Jesu, yebo kwadalwa kabusha ngezinhloso ezifanayo zaphakade uYHVH ayezihlosile ngaphambi kwezisekelo zoMhlaba, futhi ngenxa yezinjongo zakhe ngidalwe kabusha futhi ngabuyiselwa ngoba ngiyisona leso sithunywa esinamandla esichazwe yilo myalezo wesikhathi sokuphela onjengokuduma olawulwa yizwi nokukhuluma kweMidlalo Eyisikhombisa, yona leyo Midlalo uYeshua HaMashiach ayihlwanyele eMoyeni wami kuze kube phakade ngoMoya wokuProfetha owehle ngokugcwele phezu kweBiblaridion:144 yeNkosi ngomyalo weNkosi yami. Selah!

Ngakho-ke, yizwani laMazwi nani nonke futhi niqonde igunya lawo futhi iZwi ngalinye liyokwenzeka njengoba nje kulotshiwe, ngesandla saphakade namandla kaYHVH onguMINA OMkhulu, kunikezwe ebusweni obuhle nobukhanyayo bukaKristu Jesu engimthandayo futhi engimkhonza ngokulalela konke, aMazwi akhe engiwaphethe futhi engiwamemezela njengoba kuyaliwe, wona laMazwi asekhululiwe manje ekujuleni komoya wami ngomyalo kaKristu Jesu onguYeshua HaMashiach futhi izikhathi zokuqaliswa ngokugcwele kwale midlalo eyisikhombisa zifike phezu kwalo Mhlaba owahlulelwe futhi owonakele ngokuzuma nangokungalindelekile manje. Selah.

Isimemezelo Esiyisihluthulelo esivela kuKristu Jesu onguYeshua HaMashiach

Kunikezwe ngoJulayi 6, 2025

Yizwa aMazwi ami futhi uqonde igunya lawo futhi uqonde amandla abekwe ezandleni zami ngu-Adonai-YHVH, nyoka enwabuzelayo eqamba amanga, izandla ezinamandla nezaphakade zalowo owamgwazayo, wona lowo Jesu waseNazaretha, owazinikela ngamandla nangokuzithandela ezandleni zakho ezimbi zokungalaleli, ngalo lonke uthando nokulalela ku-Adonai-YHVH onguMINA OMkhulu, ngoba ngiyiWundlu likaNkulunkulu, yona leyo Ndodana yoMuntu eyochoboza ikhanda lakho ngokuphelele!

Bheka! Yizwa munye futhi nizwe nonke ukuthi nginguye, uYeshua HaMashiach, uKristu Jesu othumele laMazwi ngesithunywa nenceku futhi ngesithunywa sami nenceku yami kuthunyelwe aMazwi ami, inceku yami engiyikhulise kusukela ebusheni bayo, futhi ngayingcwelisa emoyeni wami, futhi ngayihlanza futhi ngayihlambulula ngokushaywa nokuhlanzwa okukhulu, futhi ngempela ngayikhanda emililweni yami yokuhlupheka ngoba nginguye ohlezi nezivunguvungu esandleni evuselela amalangabi okuhlanzwa futhi nginguye owenze izinyawo zayo zaba yizinsika zomlilo futhi nginguye oyembathise ngefu nothingo phezu kwekhanda layo, futhi nginguye obeke unyawo lwayo lwesokunene olwandle, nolwesokhohlo emhlabeni, futhi nginguye owenza ubuso bayo bukhanye njengeNdodana ngoba ngiyiNkosi yamakhosi neNkosi yamakhosi futhi ngempela uYHVH ungenze umoya ophilayo, Selah!

Ngeqiniso, ngeqiniso nginguye, othele futhi wenza amazwi eMidlalo Eyisikhombisa emoyeni weNcwadi Yami Encane ngoba iyiBiblaridion:144 yami lapho le midlalo eyisikhombisa elawula leyo Ncwadi Encane engiyivule esandleni sayo, okuyiVangeli laphakade futhi, engibize futhi ngalungisa ngalo abayi-144,000 bami, yebo ngalaMazwi ahlelwe yisandla sami esinamandla ukuze athole amehlo, izindlebe, nezinhliziyo zabo bonke labo ethunyelwe kubo, ngakho yizwa izwi lami elinikezwe emazwini esithunywa sami nenceku yami engangiyifihle emphongolweni wami isikhathi esinjengalesi!

Ngaphandle kwamanje, ngikhanyise imicibisholo yami ethunyelwe yisithunywa sami engisihlomisile futhi ngasikhipha emphongolweni wami ngegunya eligcwele futhi imicibisholo yami ikhala iqiniso, ngoba nginguye owenza iBiblaridion:144 yami ikhale ngezwi elikhulu, njengalapho ingonyama ibhonga: futhi njengoba ikhalile futhi imidumo eyisikhombisa yakhipha amazwi ayo anjengokuduma ngokumelene nawe Heyele, nabo bonke abakho abawileyo abakwahlulelwa kwabo sekufikile futhi kuseduze futhi akekho noyedwa kini ongabaleka esandleni sami esinamandla! Selah!

Yizwa aMazwi ami Heyele, ngoba ngikubiza ngegama nyoka ehluliwe neyanqotshwa futhi enwabuzelayo futhi ngiyabona ukwesaba kwakho ngoba wesaba ukwahlulelwa kwakho okugcwele ngaphezu kwanoma yini, khona lokho kwahlulelwa okungalibali ngoba isandla sami sisentanyeni yakho, Heyele, futhi izikhathi zakho sezifikile!

Selah, nkosana encane, Selah.

Futhi wena, Heyele, awunawo amandla okufihla, noma ukuguqula aMazwi ami mkhohlisi omkhulu, ngoba akhululwe enhliziyweni yesithunywa sami esibona amanga akho ngoba sihambisana nomlayezo ofanayo Ekunqobeni, owanikezwa ngokugcwele ngoMoya wokuProfetha ogcwele futhi akunakwenzeka ukuba uvale, ufihle, noma uhlanekezele ukushaqeka okunjengokuduma kwezwi leMidlalo Eyisikhombisa okuzwakala kusuka ezulwini lamazulu kuya ezigodini ezijulile zotwa olujulile ngayo yonke impindiselo, futhi ngempela ayikho indawo phambi kobukhona bukaYHVH kuze kube semikhawulweni engaphandle kakhulu yalutho endalweni yakhe (Ngoba uYHVH ungumbusi ngisho nalokho okusazodalwa) lapho amazwi abo anjengokuduma engazwakali ngegunya eligcwele kulezi zikhathi zokugcwaliseka, futhi izikhathi zokugcwaliseka yimanje.

Selah, nkosana encane, Selah.

Isimemezelo Esiyisihluthulelo Esivela ku-Adonai-YHVH

Sikhishwe ngoJulayi 6, 2025

Lalelani, lalelani nonke, umsindo wezwi lami eliduma njengezulu, yebo mina Jehovah-YHVH ofanayo futhi nonguye osindise umnqumo wasendle ngemiSebenzi kaKristu Jesu, yebo, omhlatshelo waKhe ngiwamukele kuze kube phakade!

Bhekani! IWundlu likaNkulunkulu elisusa izono zezwe!

Bhekani! IBhubesi lesiZwe sakwaJuda elithunyelwe ngokulunga ukuze lahlulele futhi lilwe impi!

Bhekani! Isithunywa sami esinikezwe umsebenzi, sathunyelwa, salulama futhi sadalwa kabusha nguKristu Jesu, futhi esizoguqulwa ngokugcwele kungazelelwe manje lapho kukhala lelo cilongo elinamandla elinikezwe esandleni ukuba likhale kungalindelekile manje, ngakho phumulani emazwini ami bantwana bami futhi nibe nokuthula, Selah!!

Futhi ngempela izwe selibonile iWundlu likaNkulunkulu futhi isixuku esikhulu nesinamandla sifike ensindisweni ngendlela yomnqumo wasendle, futhi ngiphulile amagatsha kumnqumo wasendle amukela isivumelwano sami sokuthula ngokukholwa, futhi ngiwaxhumele kumnqumo wemvelo osazokwambulwa ngokugcwele, yebo, u-Israyeli kaNkulunkulu, lowo Israyeli onamandla kaNkulunkulu ohlanzwe futhi wacwengwa yisandla sami esinamandla, sona isandla sikaKristu Jesu owabathanda futhi wazinikela waba umhlatshelo ngenxa yezono zika-Adamu, Selah!

Futhi ngempela, sekuyisikhathi sokuba baqoqwe ngaphansi kwesandla esinamandla sikaKristu Jesu ngomyalo wami, ngoba inkathi yokuhlengwa kwenu isiphezu kwenu, O madodana anamandla ka-Adonai-YHVH, yona leyo nkathi esezohlangana ngokulandelana kwesikhathi ne-kairos yami yezinkathi, eqalwe yi-rhema yami futhi lapho kukhala lelo cilongo elinamandla elinikezwe esandleni manje, ukubiza nokuqoqa kungazelelwe bonke engibabizela egumbini lami lesihlalo sobukhosi futhi sengizobaqoqela ngokugcwele ezulwini lamazulu ngaleso sikhathi esiqondile nesiphelele esagxiliswa enhliziyweni yami ngaphambi kokusekelwa koMhlaba.

Bhekani! Ukuhlengwa kwenu sekufikile, Bantwana baka-Adonai-YHVH, bonke labo abamukele uKristu Jesu ngokulalela njengeNkosi yenu! Futhi lelo cilongo ngokuqinisekile selizokhala kungazelelwe manje njengoba kwathenjiswa emibhalweni yami engcwele! Niyalalele yini bantwana bami lokho kumemeza nokukhala okukhulu kwecilongo elinamandla elizokhala kungalindelekile manje yiNkosi noMsindisi wenu, uKristu Jesu?

Noma nilalele amazwi alabo abanyenyezayo nabakhulumela phansi, nalabo uKristu Jesu angabathumanga futhi angabanikanga umsebenzi?

Selah.

Ngempela, impi yasezulwini imi ilindile ngoba umyalo usukhishiwe futhi lelo cilongo lokugcina elinikezwe esandleni yisandla sami kuKristu Jesu uqobo lwakhe, onguYeshua HaMashiach, futhi nguyena oyokhipha umyalo wami ne-oda lami ngaleso sikhathi esiphelele (leso sikhathi esesivele siphonsa isithunzi saso esinamandla phezu kwendalo yami, esibangela abawileyo ukwesaba okukhulu) ngoba uKristu Jesu uhlale enza intando kaYise, yebo, u-Adonai-YHVH: Selah!

Ngoba. NginguMdala wezinsuku, Yena okhona nobusayo, futhi ngingumdala ngisho nangaphambi kokuba izinsuku zenziwe, futhi nginguYE oyohlala engumbusi phakade naphakade lapho izinsuku emhlabeni ziphela, ngoba ukulandelana kwezikhathi kuyogcina kuphelile kube yinkathi yaphakade nengunaphakade yokulunga engenakuphela, futhi lelo yithemba labo bonke abalungileyo engilithembise phakade naphakade, Selah.

Futhi ngempela, nginguye oyokwenza kwenzeke ngokuvumelana namacebo ami aphakade nezinjongo engizisebenzele ngeseluleko sentando yami; futhi nakuba ngizungezwe yizinkulungwane eziyishumi eziphindwe ngezinkulungwane eziyishumi zezidalwa ezimangalisayo neziphelele engizidalile ngesandla sami, eziphakeme ekuhlakanipheni nasekulungeni, futhi ezihlale zilalela ngenxa yothando izwi lami. Ngoba othandweni lwami ngiyabanakekela ngokuphelele futhi ngihlinzeka ngazo zonke izidingo zabo ngisho kungakabi bikho isidingo, ukuze ngibakhazimulise futhi bona bangikhazimulise othandweni lwabo nasekukhanyeni kwabo okukhazimulayo, ngoba ngibembathise izimilo ezikhanyayo phakade neziphelele. Selah.

Futhi nginguYE othumela la Mazwi ngesithunywa sami nenceku yami uKristu Jesu ayivusile kusukela ebusheni bayo, wayigcina ebubini obukhulu ekuphepheni nasemandleni esandla sakhe esinamandla, futhi ngempela lowo omubi akamthintanga!

Ngoba nginguYE ongakwazi ngokoqobo ukufeza konke futhi konke ngokushesha nangaphandle komzamo ngamandla ami! Kodwa, nginguye futhi uNkulunkulu waphakade wokuLunga ongumdali wenu, futhi njengomdali ngidala izidalwa ngamacebo ami aphakade nezinjongo zokufeza intando yami yaphakade nengunaphakade yokulunga.

Ngakho-ke, nginguYE odalile, walungisa, wanika umsebenzi futhi wathumela isithunywa sami nenceku yami ebuyele ngokwethembeka kuYeshua HaMashiach ngokujeziswa okukhulu, nangokuphenduka, futhi ngesandla sayo lezi zimemezelo zithunyelwa zivela esihlalweni sami sobukhosi, kuya kuKristu Jesu, enhliziyweni yesithunywa sethu esinamandla nenceku yethu enegunya eligcwele lokwahlulela nelokumemezela elinikezwe yisandla sami ngendlela kaKristu Jesu, ngisho nangokukhuluma kwemidumo eyisikhombisa enikezwe ngqo enhliziyweni yakhe ngendlela yoMoya wesiProfetho ngoba la Mazwi atshalwe ebuntwini bakhe ngomyalo wami ukuba akhululwe ngomyalo kaYeshua HaMashiach ukuze kungabikho Zwi elonakaliswa yizono nokungalaleli kwalabo abashiye ubizo lwabo, abakwehlulela kwabo kungalibali okuzowela ngokugcwele kungazelelwe manje kuqala endlini kaNkulunkulu, okuzokwenzeka kungalindelekile manje, Selah!

Ngakho makwaziwe, ukuthi lezi zahlulelo zinikezwe ukuba zifundwe ngalolu suku, ngosuku lwekhalenda likaGregory olungcolile lwangoJulayi 6, 2025.

Futhi la Mazwi azothola amehlo, izindlebe nezinhliziyo zabo bonke labo athunyelwe kubo, ikakhulukazi izinhliziyo ezingalaleli nezingcolile zabawileyo abafikelwe yizahlulelo zabo. Yebo abawileyo kanye nezinto ezibuthakathaka nezihluphekile ezibalandelayo futhi abazifungile ukulandela uHeylel. Yebo uHeylel, lowo okhala ngokwehlulwa lapho ephonswa emhlabeni yisandla sami saphakade ngehlazo elikhulu nokwehlulwa, lowo Heylel owabhujiswa ngokuphelele futhi wehlulwa yiWundlu likaNkulunkulu elamemezela ngokunqoba njengoba elenga ngokunqoba okukhulu kuleso sigxobo esinzima ngalawo Mazwi aphindaphindeka phakade athi: SEKUPHELILE.

Futhi kanjalo-ke mina, Adonai-YHVH, ngiyamemezela ukuthi nginguye othumela la Mazwi ngendlela kaKristu Jesu, anikezwe futhi amemezelwa yileso sithunywa esinamandla seSambulo Isahluko 10 engisithumele ngomyalo kaKristu Jesu, yena lowo ochazwe kwiSambulo Isahluko 10 ngalowo myalezo otshalwe enhliziyweni yakhe anikezwe wona ngokugcwele nguMoya wesiProfetho, nasesandleni sakhe kunencwajana evuliwe. Selah.

Isendlalelo

Lezi zahlulelo zithathwe kwiMidumo Eyisikhombisa embulwe eNcwajaneni yeSambulo Isahluko 10, okuyiVangeli laphakade leSambulo isahluko 14; embulwe kusukela ngoMashi 2, 2024, kuya kuMashi 20, 2025. Lezi zimemezelo ziyizahlulelo ezinikezwe ukuba zifundwe ngaphambi kokuba ukwahlulela kuwele ngokugcwele, okuzonikezwa ngokugcwele ezifubeni zabawileyo phakade naphakade ngoba esijezisweni sabo abanakuphumula, noma ukuthula, noma umusa: ngoba ukuphumula, ukuthula, nomusa abakwazanga, abakubonakalisanga, futhi abakunikanga.

Ungumkhohlisi nomqambimanga oyingcweti ofundise abantu izinto eziningi uYahweh ayengahlosile, ngoba lezi yizindlela zokufa. Indalo kaYahweh ayenzelwanga ukukhohliswa ukuze kutholakale amandla nenzuzo yomuntu siqu noma njengendlela yokubalekela ukwahlulela kukaNkulunkulu, noma ukusungula izindlela nomyalelo womuntu siqu. Ngoba uSathane wonakalise isintu kusukela ekuqaleni, futhi wonakalise abalindileyo abawileyo abahlanganyela ekuhlubukeni kwabo.

Oh, wena sitha esikhulu sakho konke ukulunga, yebo nina boSathane: izono zenu zokuqala zalotshwa ngu-Enoke umbhali kudala kangaka, futhi zisekhona kuze kube namuhla, ngoba izono zenu zalotshwa kanjalo. Ngakho ngizofingqa, ngoba nenze lezi zinto, nokunye okuningi nikwenzile: Nadukisa onke amadodana kaNkulunkulu, nawahlisela emhlabeni, nawadukisa ngamadodakazi abantu. Nanikeza amadodana angcwele kaNkulunkulu iseluleko esibi, nawadukisa kangangokuthi ayangcolisa imizimba yawo ngamadodakazi abantu. Nikhombise abantwana babantu onke amagalelo okufa, nadukisa u-Eva, futhi nakhombisa izikhali zokufa emadodaneni abantu: ihawu nejazi lensimbi, nenkemba yokulwa, nazo zonke izikhali zokufa kubantwana babantu. Futhi kusukela esandleni senu zahamba zamelana nalabo abahlala emhlabeni kusukela ngalolo suku kuze kube phakade.

Nina boSathane nafundisa abantwana babantu okubabayo nokumnandi, futhi nabafundisa zonke izimfihlo zokuhlakanipha kwabo. Nafundisa isintu ukubhala nge-inki nephepha, futhi ngalokho abaningi bona kusukela phakade kuze kube phakade kuze kube namuhla. Ngoba abantu babengadalelwanga injongo enjalo, ukunikeza isiqinisekiso sokholo lwabo oluhle ngepeni ne-inki. Ngoba abantu badalwa njengezingelosi, ngenhloso yokuba baqhubeke bemsulwa futhi belungile; futhi ukufa, okubhubhisa konke, kwakungeke kubabambe, kodwa ngalolu lwazi bayabhubha, futhi ngala mandla kudla abantu.

Nina boSathane nakhombisa abantwana babantu konke ukushaya okubi kwemimoya namadimoni, nokushaywa kombungu esibelethweni, ukuze udlule, nokushaywa komphefumulo, ukulunywa yinyoka, nokushaywa okwenzeka ngesikhathi sokushisa kwasemini, indodana yenyoka egama layo linguTabââ'ĕt. Ngoba nacele ngisho noMikayeli ukuba anikhombise igama elifihliwe, ukuze nilibize esifungweni, ukuze labo bathuthumele ngaphambi kwalelo gama nesifungo abaveza konke okwakuyimfihlo kubantwana babantu.

Ngoba nabangela abalindileyo ukuba bajoyine ukuhlubuka kwenu njengoba naqhubeka nokonakalisa isintu.

Ngoba nishushisile futhi nahluphe izizwe eziyi-12 emhlabeni wonke njengoba nifuna ukubhujiswa kwazo. Nebe abantwana komama, nathatha abantwana emindenini nabathuthela emhlabeni wonke, nabe seniqamba amanga kubo nabenza bakholwa izinganekwane zamaJesuit mayelana nomlando wabo weqiniso, izinhloso, nomsuka. Akunandaba, nehlulekile, ngoba mina, Yeshua HaMashiach, ngizobaxhumanisa ngokugcwele nesikhathi sabo esidlule, isizwe namagugu eqiniso, futhi ngizokwenza bonke abayizindlalifa zensindiso babe umuntu munye omusha, yebo, kimi, uKristu Jesu.

Ngobuqili nokubhujiswa okukhulu, nifihle ngobunono ubuchwepheshe benu, naze naqamba amanga okuthi abantu basendulo babengenalwazi futhi bengenabo ubuchwepheshe. Ngoba nakhombisa abasendulo izimfihlakalo zenu ezingenamsebenzi, futhi akukho lutho olusha ngaphansi kwelanga. Ngakho-ke nifihla iqiniso mayelana nokukhohlisa kwenu indalo, naze nabafihlela nalabo enibayengayo ngenkohliso yenu enkulu. Niphinde naletha amaqhinga enu omlingo futhi, njengoba nilungise isiteji ukuze nizifanise nabafokazi. Futhi ngokuhlasela kwenu kwamanga kwabafokazi nizokwenza bonke bamangale ngezibonakaliso nezimangaliso zenu zamanga, ngisho nalabo amagama abo angabhalwanga eNcwadini Yokuphila kusukela ekusekelweni komhlaba. Kodwa mina, Yeshua HaMashiach, ngizoniveza obala ngokugcwele, futhi yonke imfihlo efihliwe iyovela ekukhanyeni.

Bhekani! Ngizothatha abathembekile bami ngibayise emazulwini, ngoba ngiza uMlobokazi wami futhi ngeke ngivinjwe. Futhi mina, Yeshua HaMashiach, ngiza ukuzokwahlulela nokulwa impi futhi ngilethe ukwahlulela okukhulu emhlabeni, futhi ngeke niphunyuke.

Mina, Yeshua HaMashiach, Nkosi yamakhosi neNkosi yamakhosi, ngithumele la mazwi ngenceku yami, futhi ngenceku yami amazwi ami athunyelwe, futhi konke kuzogcwaliseka.

Kusuka kuYahweh, uNkulunkulu Omkhulu Waphakade: Umdumo Wesibili!

Oh, nina boSathane, nikhohlise indalo yami ukuba ilandele izibonakaliso nezimangaliso zenu zamanga, futhi intukuthelo yami iyashisa phezu kwenu. Abantu bami bazohlukaniswa ngokuphelele nokhula lwenu enilutshale phakathi kukakolweni wami ensimini yami yomusa; futhi iNdodana yami, uKristu Jesu, izobutha ukolweni wami esiphahlukeni sami, futhi kanjalo iNkosi yokuvuna iyobutha, ibophe futhi ishise ukhula lwenu. Ukwahlulela okukhulu kuphezu kwenu, futhi ngeke niphunyuke esandleni sami. Kusukela ekuqaleni, nanyenya futhi nazifihla phakathi kwabantwana bami, naze nonakalisa abancane bami kusukela ekuqaleni ngamanga nenkohliso yenu.

Anibona onkulunkulu, nalabo abalandela inkohliso yenu nezinkolelo zokunqoba ababona onkulunkulu nabo. Anikwazi ukudala, ngoba nintula amandla, futhi angikaze nginidale ngamakhono anjalo ngoba ngangazi ukuthi nizohlubuka ngaphambi kokuba nidalwe; futhi ekulungeni kwami nasothandweni lwami nganidala ngokulunga, futhi naniphelele ngosuku enadalwa ngalo. Ekuziqhenyeni kwenu okungenamsebenzi niyaziqhayisa ngobukhulu benu, kodwa aninabo ubukhulu, futhi ngizoniveza obala ngokuphelele ngibe yinkohliso eniyiyo. Ngizonibeka phambi kwamaKhosi, futhi azobona ukuthi aninamandla kangakanani. Indodana yami, uYeshua HaMashiach, yaninqoba ngokuphelele eKalvari, futhi aninathemba.

Nivunyelwe ukwenza lokho engikuvumelayo kuphela, njengoba niyisitha esehluliwe. Neba isenzo somhlaba ngobuqili benu nangamanga ku-Adamu, futhi manje indodana yami ezelwe yodwa, yebo uYeshua HaMashiach, unqobile ngenxa yothando lwakhe ngami nangendalo yami; futhi umhlaba ungowakhe, yebo, iNkosi yamakhosi neNkosi yamakhosi, ngoba ngimemezele kanjalo. Ngoba wathenga umhlaba nesintu ngegazi lakhe elingenasono, futhi labo abakhetha ukuphila bazohlengwa. Yonke imisebenzi yenu engenamsebenzi iyochithwa, futhi bonke abakhetha ukuphila bayophuma emangeni nasekukhohliseni kwenu baye ebuntsha bokuphila, futhi ngizokwenza abathembekile babe bakhazimulayo kunani nina enake naba njalo. Ngoba nehlulekile, oh zikhulu ezibuthakathaka nezihluphekile zomhlaba, ngisho zonke izikhulu ezisehlelweni lenu zehlulekile kanye nani, oh nyoka enyenyayo, eqamba amanga.

Manje kufika isikhathi sokwahlulelwa kwenu nokubhujiswa ngokuphelele kombuso wenu, ngisho nombuso eniwakhile emizamweni yenu yokuzama ukungifihla, mina Yahweh, endalweni yami. Ngeke niphunyuke. Ngeke niphinde ningene emazulwini, ngoba niphonswe phansi emhlabeni ukuba ningaphinde nibuye, futhi indawo yenu ayisekho emazulwini. Niboshelwe emhlabeni enawunxanela, owawungeyona owenu ukuba niwuthathe. Yonke imisebenzi yenu iyochithwa ezimpilweni zalabo enazama ukubabhubhisa (nasemhlabeni uqobo); bayohlala phakade ngokuthula nokuphepha, futhi bayoba nenjabulo ephelele, futhi ngizohlala phakathi kwesintu futhi ngibe uNkulunkulu wabo.

Kodwa wena, oh nyoka enyenyayo, awunamandla emehlweni ami, futhi awukwazi ukwenza lutho ukungivimba. Ezinsukwini eziyisithupha kusukela ekuweni komuntu, ngoba usuku olulodwa luyiminyaka eyinkulungwane emehlweni ami, anifezanga lutho ngaphandle kokwahlulelwa kwenu, ukulahlwa, ukubhujiswa, nokuhlushwa kwenu nabo bonke abalandelayo. Ngoba nizoboshwa niphonswe kulelo chibi elikhulu elivuthayo lapho ngenzele khona amalangabi ashisayo kakhulu, ngisho namalangabi aluhlaza okwesibhakabhaka, futhi nizosha phakade, futhi ngizonibuyisela yonke imisebenzi emibi enake nayenza, izikhathi eziningi. Nibulele izixuku ngezixuku, futhi nizokhokhela wonke umphefumulo olahlekile, futhi anisoze naphunyuka. Nizokwazi zonke izono enazenza, futhi anisoze naphunyuka ekwahlulelweni, futhi niyohlushwa phakade ngenxa yezono enazenzayo. Nizokwazi kahle izono zenu neziphambeko zenu ekuhlushweni kwenu, futhi labo enibazondayo bayoba nokunqoba kokugcina phezu kwenu. Futhi bonke abangalungile enake nabayenga ukuba bone futhi bazibophezele ekujoyineni ukuhlubuka kwenu, ngisho nalabo amagama abo angalotshwanga eNcwadini Yokuphila, bayohlanganyela ekuhlushweni kwenu. Nonke nizokhokhela sonke isono esisodwa enasenzayo, ngoba nonile kuYahweh nasendalweni yami, futhi ngizobuyisela. Ngoba ekulungeni kwami ngizobuyisela izono zenu, izenzo, nezindlela ezimbi ngokugcwele ezifubeni zenu izikhathi eziningi naphakade. Futhi mina, Yahweh, uNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade, ngifunga lezi zinto ngegama lami, ngoba angeke ngifunge ngomkhulu kunami.

Nizofeza izinjongo zami, futhi enondweni lwenu nasekubhujisweni kwenu nizoqhuba abakhethiweyo bami kimi; ngoba ngizobahola ngesandla sikaYeshua HaMashiach nebutho lakhe lamadoda anamandla, ngoba bazobutha izimvu zami ezisele. (Bhekani! Izinkulungwane eziyi-144 ezithunyelwe nguYeshua HaMashiach zizokwenza imisebenzi emikhulu phambi kobubi benu obunyanyekayo.) Futhi akekho noyedwa engimmisele ukuphila okuphakade oyowela emaqhingeni enu, ngoba abasoze bahlwitshwa esandleni sami, futhi akekho ongbahlwitha ezandleni zeNdodana yami: Ngoba ngimnike igama eliphezu kwawo onke amagama, futhi uza ngamandla kaYahweh uqobo, ngoba ngabelane naye ngesihlalo sami sobukhosi. Futhi uyolichoboza ngokuphelele ikhanda lenu. Nehlulekile, nkosana encane, futhi isikhathi senu sesifikile.

Mina, Yahweh, uNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade, ngithumele la mazwi ngenceku yami, futhi ngenceku yami amazwi ami athunyelwe, futhi ayogcwaliseka. Ngoba uYahweh, uNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade, umemezele kanjalo, futhi akukho okungaphula izimemezelo zami. Ngingu-El Shaddai!

Inkosi yenu enkulu ngizoyinqoba nomprofethi wenu wamanga ngizomnqoba, futhi bazophonswa bephila echibini lomlilo elivutha isibabule; futhi lapho bazolinda nina ukuba nihlanganyele nabo, futhi lapho niyogwinywa umlilo nesibabule ingunaphakade, futhi ukwahlulelwa kwenu sekuzoqala!

Kusuka kuYeshua HaMashiach: Ukuduma Kokuqala!

Ngokuba uSathane wonakalise isintu kusukela ekuqaleni, futhi wonakalise ababheki abawile abahlanganyela ekuhlubukeni kwabo. Ngokuba nenza ababheki ukuba bahlanganyele ekuhlubukeni kwenu njengoba naqhubeka nokonakalisa isintu.

Kanjalo izono zababheki zalotshwa ngu-Enoke umbhali, futhi ngizofingqa kanje: Bafundisa abantu ukwenza izinkemba, nemimese, nezihlangu, nezivikelo zesifuba, futhi babazisa ngezinsimbi zomhlaba nobuciko bokuzisebenza, namasongo, nemihlobiso, nokusetshenziswa kwe-antimony, nokuhlobisa amehlo, nazo zonke izinhlobo zamatshe ayigugu, nazo zonke izinhlobo zokudaya. Bafundisa ukuthakatha, nokusika izimpande, ukuxazulula ukuthakatha, ukufundwa kwezinkanyezi, izinkanyezi, ulwazi ngamafu, izibonakaliso zomhlaba, izibonakaliso zelanga, nenkambo yenyanga.

Kanjalo ngenxa yokonakala kwenu namanga enu, ningcolise indalo kaYahweh ngezindlela zenu ezimbi nezinengiso ezihlambalazayo. Nidukisile isintu futhi nona kuYahweh, futhi nenze kabi esintwini, ngisho nona ezinyonini, nasezilwaneni, nasezihuquzelayo, nasezinhlanzini, futhi izono zenu ziletha ukwahlulelwa okukhulu eningeke nilulame kukho. Ningcolise indalo kaYahweh ngobuchwepheshe benu nobuqili benu, futhi nenza isintu samangala ngamaqhinga enu namanga enu. Ngokuba uYahweh wanibonisa kuphela izimfihlakalo ezingenamsebenzi zendalo yakhe futhi wagcina izimfihlakalo zakhe eziyigugu kakhulu zifihliwe enhliziyweni yakhe kude nokukhaphela kwenu.

Nina enikhohlakele nafundisa izindlela zenu ezingenamsebenzi esintwini njengoba banda ebusweni bomhlaba, futhi naletha usizi olukhulu nokubhujiswa ngamaqhinga enu amabi nobuchwepheshe benu. Ngokuba anizi ngaphandle kokweba, ukubulala, nokubhubhisa. Nafundisa ukuphatha umoya, ngokuba niyisikhulu samandla omoya, ngokuba niphatha i-aithēr. Ngokuba lapho uKayini ona, wakha umuzi; futhi nifundisile isintu izimfihlo zenu ezingenamsebenzi nobuqili benu. Le mizi yayisetshenziselwa ukulawula nokuqila kanye nokuletha ukubhujiswa kwesintu, njengoba nayisebenzisela ukwenza izinengiso zenu zokubhujiswa.

Ngobuqili obukhulu nangokubhujiswa okukhulu, nibufihlile ngobuhlakani ubuchwepheshe benu, ngisho nokutshela amanga okuthi abantu basendulo babengenalwazi futhi bengenabo ubuchwepheshe. Ngokuba nabonisa abasendulo izimfihlakalo zenu ezingenamsebenzi, futhi akukho okusha ngaphansi kwelanga. Kanjalo nifihla iqiniso ngokuphathelene nobuqili benu endalweni, ngisho nikufihlela nalabo enibayengayo ngenkohliso yenu enkulu. Niphinde naletha amaqhinga enu omlingo futhi, njengoba nilungise isiteji ukuze nizishaye abafokazi. Futhi ngokuhlasela kwenu kwamanga kwabafokazi nizokwenza bonke bamangale ngezibonakaliso zenu zamanga nezimangaliso, ngisho nalabo amagama abo angabhalwanga eNcwadini Yokuphila kusukela ekusekelweni komhlaba. Kodwa mina, Yeshua HaMashiach, ngizonembula ngokuphelele, futhi zonke izimfihlo ezifihliwe ziyovela obala.

Ngizweni futhi ningizwe kahle, ngokuba ngimemezela lokho okuzokwenzeka kini, ngisho nani babheki abangenamsebenzi enashiya isikhundla senu sokuqala. Aninakho ukuthula, futhi nakuba nginikhululile ezibophweni zenu isikhashana, ngikwenze lokho kuphela ukuze nifeze izinhloso zami. Bese kuba nokwahlulelwa okukhulu phezu kwenu, futhi aninakho ukuthula. Ngokuba nenza ukuba ngikhukhulise umhlaba ngenxa yezinengiso zenu. Ningobani nina ukuba nizidlalele umdali endalweni kaYahweh? Ningobani nina ukuba ningcolise insimu kaNkulunkulu ngezindlela zenu ezingcolile? Nenza abantwana benu besihlahla nabalahliwe ngokuzingcolisa ngabesifazane, futhi bayohlanganyela efeni lenu lomlilo. Ngokuba ngamtshelani uMalaki: Ngokuba bhekani, usuku luyeza oluyovutha njengeziko, nabo bonke abaziqhenyayo, yebo, nabo bonke abenza okubi, bayoba yizinhlanga, nosuku oluzayo luyobashisa, usho uJehova Sebawoti, lungabashiyeli mpande nagatsha. Ngokuba nginike abantu abafazi ukuze bangasweli lutho, abafazi bokuba abangane nokubazalela abantwana. Kodwa nina nehla eNtabeni iHerimoni, ngisho nashiya isikhundla senu sokuqala senkazimulo, futhi nizigcolisile ngabesifazane, futhi aninakho ukuthula noma ukuphumula. Futhi nasezibophweni niyohamba njengoba niphonswa echibini lomlilo.

Nilimazile indalo yami ngenxa yezinkanuko zenu, ngisho nathatha ngenkani abaningi enabakhetha njengabafazi benu, futhi niyokhokha. Ngakho-ke, nizohlanganyela nabo echibini elikhulu lomlilo waphakade, futhi nizosha ingunaphakade, futhi anisoze nathola ukuphumula. Ngakho-ke, nami ngeke nginindabe, futhi nginidalile, futhi manje kufika ukwahlulelwa kwenu nokubhujiswa kwenu okuzayo.

Ngithi koSathane, kubabheki, kuma-cherubim awileyo, kuma-seraphim awileyo, ngisho nakubo bonke abahlanganyele ekuhlubukeni kukaSathane: nizosha ngokuphelele, kaninginingi kuze kube phakade; ngokuba ngizonibopha nginiphonse emlilweni lapho isibungu sabo singafi khona, ngisho nasemalangabini abekelwe nina eningenamsebenzi azokushisa kakhulu ngenxa yenu, ngokuba nikufanele.
