Ukuduma 1 & 2

Kuni Nina Abawileyo: Manje Sekufika Ukwahlulelwa Kwenu!

Kusuka kuYeshua HaMashiach:

Nina enibiziwe, enakhethwa, nabathembekile, yizwani amazwi eNkosi yenu ephilayo nomsindisi, uYeshua uMesiya! Izizwe enihlala kuzo kanye nemilando eniyifundiswe ngabafundisi benu abangamaJesuit iyisizinda senkohliso enkulu, okwembulwa kwayo ngokugcwele kuseduze futhi sekuvele kuqhubeka, lapho abawileyo beyovela njengabafokazi abanomusa kodwa eqinisweni bayizingelosi eziwile ezinonya. Yini leyo ebikhona? Yileyo ezoba khona: futhi yini leyo eyenziwe? Yileyo eyokwenziwa: futhi ayikho into entsha ngaphansi kwelanga.

USathane unikele ngolwazi lwakhe okuthiwa luthuthukile kusukela ekuqaleni, ngisho nolwazi lwakhe lokuhle nokubi. Uyingcweti yokukhohlisa nomqambimanga ofundise izinto eziningi esintwini uYahweh ayengahlosile, ngoba lezi yizindlela zokufa. Indalo kaYahweh ayenzelwanga ukuba isetshenziswe ngobuqili ukuze kutholakale amandla nenzuzo yomuntu siqu noma njengethuluzi lokubalekela ukwahlulela kukaNkulunkulu, noma ukusungula izindlela nomthetho womuntu siqu. Ngoba uSathane wonakalisile isintu kusukela ekuqaleni, futhi wonakalisile abalindi abawileyo abahlanganyela naye ekuhlubukeni kwabo.

Hawu, wena sitha esikhulu sakho konke ukulunga, ngisho nani boSathane: izono zenu zokuqala zalotshwa ngu-Enoke umbhali kwaso sonke lesi sikhathi esedlule, futhi zimi kuze kube namuhla, ngoba izono zenu zalotshwa kanjalo. Ngakho-ke ngizofingqa, ngoba nenze lezi zinto, futhi nokunye okuningi nikwenzile: Nadukisa onke amadodana kaNkulunkulu, nabehlisela emhlabeni, nabadukisa ngamadodakazi abantu. Nanikeza amadodana angcwele kaNkulunkulu iseluleko esibi, nawadukisa aze angcolisa imizimba yawo ngamadodakazi abantu. Nikhombise abantwana babantu onke amagalelo okufa, nadukisa u-Eva, nakhombisa amadodana abantu izikhali zokufa: isihlangu nejazi lensimbi, nenkemba yokulwa, nazo zonke izikhali zokufa kubantwana babantu. Futhi kusukela esandleni senu ziqhubekele kwabahlala emhlabeni kusukela ngalolo suku kuze kube phakade.

Nina boSathane nafundisa abantwana babantu okubabayo nokumnandi, nanibafundisa zonke izimfihlo zokuhlakanipha kwabo. Nafundisa isintu ukubhala ngoyinki nephepha, futhi ngenxa yalokho abaningi bona kusukela ephakadeni kuze kube phakade nakuze kube namuhla. Ngoba abantu babengadalelwanga injongo enjalo, yokunikeza isiqinisekiso sokholo lwabo oluhle ngepeni noyinki. Ngoba abantu badalwa njengezingelosi, ngenhloso yokuba baqhubeke bemsulwa futhi belungile; nokufa, okubhubhisa konke, kwakungeke kubabambe, kodwa ngalolu lwazi bayabhubha, futhi ngala mandla kudla abantu.

Nina boSathane nakhombisa abantwana babantu zonke izinhlupho ezimbi zemimoya namademoni, nezinhlupho zombungu esibelethweni, ukuze ushone, nezinhlupho zomphefumulo, ukulunywa yinyoka, nezinhlupho ezehlela ngokushisa kwasemini, indodana yenyoka egama layo linguTabââ’ĕt. Ngoba naze nacela uMikayeli ukuba anikhombise igama elifihliwe, ukuze niliphimisele esifungweni, ukuze labo abembula konke okwakuyimfihlo kubantwana babantu bathuthumele phambi kwalelo gama nesifungo.

Ngoba nabangela ukuba abalindi bajoyine ukuhlubuka kwenu njengoba naqhubeka nokonakalisa isintu. Kanjalo izono zabalindi zalotshwa ngu-Enoke umbhali, futhi ngizofingqa kanje: Bafundisa abantu ukwenza izinkemba, nemimese, nezihlangu, nezivikelo zesifuba, futhi babazisa ngezinsimbi zomhlaba nobuciko bokuzisebenza, namasongo, nemihlobiso, nokusetshenziswa kwe-antimony, nokuhlobisa amankophe, nazo zonke izinhlobo zamatshe ayigugu, nazo zonke izidayi zombala. Bafundisa ukuthakatha, nokusika izimpande, ukuxazulula ukuthakatha, ukufundwa kwezinkanyezi, izinkanyezi, ulwazi lwamafu, izibonakaliso zomhlaba, izibonakaliso zelanga, nenkambo yenyanga.

Kanjalo ngenxa yezonakalo namanga enu, ningcolise indalo kaYahweh ngezindlela zenu ezimbi nezinengiso eziyinhlamba. Nidukisile isintu futhi nonile kuYahweh, futhi nonile isintu, ngisho nenza okubi ezinyonini, nasezilwaneni, nakwezihuquzelayo, nakwezinhlanzi, futhi izono zenu ziletha ukwahlulelwa okukhulu eningeke nisinde kukho. Ningcolise indalo kaYahweh ngobuchwepheshe nokukhohlisa kwenu, futhi nabangela isintu ukuba simangale ngamaqhinga namanga enu. Ngoba uYahweh wanikhombisa kuphela izimfihlakalo ezingelusizo zendalo yakhe futhi wagcina izimfihlakalo zakhe eziyigugu kakhulu zifihliwe enhliziyweni yakhe kude nokukhaphela kwenu.

Nina enikhohlakele nafundisa izindlela zenu ezingelusizo esintwini njengoba banda ebusweni bomhlaba, futhi naletha usizi olukhulu nembubhiso ngamasu nobuchwepheshe benu obubi. Ngoba anizi ngaphandle kokweba, ukubulala, nokubhubhisa. Nafundisa ukukhohlisa umoya, ngoba niyisikhulu samandla omoya, ngoba nikhohlisa i-aithēr. Ngoba lapho uKhayini ona, wakha umuzi; futhi nifundisile isintu izimfihlo namasu enu angenamsebenzi. Le mizi yayisetshenziselwa ukulawula nokukhohlisa nokuletha ukubhujiswa kwesintu, njengoba nayisebenzisela ukwenza izinengiso zenu zokubhujiswa.

Nibangele isizwe sivukele isizwe ezimpini nasezimpini ezingapheli, futhi nibangele abantu bami ukuba bone, futhi uYahweh uhlakazile Izizwe zakwa-Israyeli emimoyeni emine. Akunandaba, inhloso yenu embi iqhubekisele phambili izinjongo zikaYahweh, ngoba ngokuhlakazeka kwabo kuzofezeka ukuqoqwa kwezimvu ezisele. Futhi ngizothumela ofakazi bami ababili ukuba baprofethe izinsuku eziyi-1260 bembethe izindwangu zamasaka. Kuyo yonke imizamo yenu yokubhubhisa abantwana bakaYahweh nehlulekile kabi, futhi anikwazanga ukubona ukuthi imizamo yenu iqhubekisele phambili izinjongo zikaYahweh, ngoba nezitha zakhe ziyalalela izwi lakhe.

Ngoba nihlushile futhi naphazamisa izizwe eziyi-12 kuwo wonke umhlaba njengoba nifuna ukubhujiswa kwazo. Nebile abantwana komama babo, futhi nathatha abantwana emindenini nabathuthela kuwo wonke umhlaba, base niqamba amanga kubo futhi nibenza bakholwe izinganekwane zamaJesuit mayelana nomlando wabo weqiniso, izinjongo, nomsuka. Akunandaba, nehlulekile, ngoba mina, uYeshua HaMashiach, ngizobaxhumanisa ngokugcwele nesikhathi sabo esedlule, isizwe kanye nefa labo leqiniso, futhi ngizokwenza bonke abayizindlalifa zensindiso babe muntu munye omusha, ngisho nakimi, uKristu Jesu.

Ngobuqili nembubhiso enkulu, nifihle ngobuhlakani ubuchwepheshe benu, naze naqamba amanga okuthi abantu basendulo babengenalwazi futhi bengenabo ubuchwepheshe. Ngoba nakhombisa abantu basendulo izimfihlakalo zenu ezingelusizo, futhi ayikho into entsha ngaphansi kwelanga. Kanjalo nifihla iqiniso mayelana nokukhohlisa kwenu indalo, naze nabafihlela nalabo eniyobayenga ngenkohliso yenu enkulu. Niphinde naletha amaqhinga enu omlingo futhi, njengoba nisethe isiteji ukuze nizifanise nabafokazi. Futhi ngokuhlasela kwenu okungamanga kwabafokazi niyobangela ukuba bonke bamangale ngezibonakaliso namanga enu, ngisho nalabo amagama abo angabhalwanga eNcwadini Yokuphila kusukela ekusekelweni komhlaba. Kodwa mina, uYeshua HaMashiach, ngizoniveza obala ngokuphelele, futhi yonke imfihlo efihliwe iyovela ekukhanyeni.

Bhekani! Ngizohlwitha abathembekile bami ngibayise emazulwini, ngoba ngiza kuMlobokazi wami futhi angiyukumiswa. Futhi mina, uYeshua HaMashiach, ngiza ukuzokwahlulela nokulwa nokuletha ukwahlulelwa okukhulu emhlabeni, futhi aniyikubaleka.

Mina, uYeshua HaMashiach, iNkosi yamakhosi neSilo samasilo, ngithumele lawa mazwi ngenceku yami, futhi ngenceku yami amazwi ami athunyelwe, futhi konke kuyogcwaliseka.

Kusuka kuYahweh, uNkulunkulu Omkhulu Ophakade:

Hawu, nina boSathane, nikhohlisile indalo yami ukuba ilandele izibonakaliso namanga enu, futhi intukuthelo yami ishisa phezu kwenu. Abantu bami bayokwehlukaniswa ngokuphelele nokhula lwenu enilutshale phakathi kukakolweni wami ensimini yami yomusa; futhi iNdodana yami, uKristu Jesu, izobutha ukolweni wami esibayeni sami, kanjalo iNkosi yokuvuna izobutha, ibophe futhi ishise ukhula lwenu. Ukwahlulelwa okukhulu kuphezu kwenu, futhi aniyikusinda esandleni sami. Kusukela ekuqaleni, nanwabuzela futhi nazifihla phakathi kwabantwana bami, naze nonakalisa nabancane bami kusukela ekuqaleni ngamanga nenkohliso yenu.

Anibona onkulunkulu, nalabo abalandela inkohliso yenu nemibono yenu yokunqoba ababona onkulunkulu. Anikwazi ukudala, ngoba aninawo amandla, futhi angizange nginidale ngamakhono anjalo ngoba ngangazi ukuthi niyohluza ngaphambi kokuba nidalwe; futhi ebulungiseni nothando lwami nganidala ekulungeni, futhi naniphelele ngosuku enadalwa ngalo. Ngokuzidla kwenu okungenamsebenzi niyazigqaja ngobukhulu benu, kodwa aninabo ubukhulu, futhi ngizoniveza obala ngokugcwele ukuthi ningabakhohlisi abanjani. Ngizonibeka phambi kwamakhosi, futhi ayonibona nokuthi aninamandla kangakanani. Indodana yami, uYeshua HaMashiach, yaninqoba ngokuphelele eKalvari, futhi aninalo ithemba.

Nivunyelwe ukwenza lokho engikuvumelayo kuphela, njengoba niyisitha esehluliwe. Neba itayitela lomhlaba ngobuqili nangamanga enu ku-Adamu, futhi manje indodana yami ezelwe yodwa, ngisho uYeshua HaMashiach, ininqobile othandweni lwayo ngami nangendalo yami; futhi umhlaba ungowayo, ngisho iNkosi yamakhosi neSilo samasilo, ngoba ngimemezelile. Ngoba yawuthenga umhlaba nesintu ngegazi layo elingenasono, futhi labo abakhetha ukuphila bayohlengwa. Yonke imisebenzi yenu engenamsebenzi iyochithwa, futhi bonke abakhetha ukuphila bayophuma emangeni nasenkohlisweni yenu bangene ebuntshani bokuphila, futhi ngizokwenza abathembekile babe nodumo olukhulu kunalokho enake naba yikho. Ngoba nehlulekile, o zikhosana ezibuthakathaka nezimpofu zomhlaba, ngisho nazo zonke izikhulu ezisehlelweni lenu zehlulekile kanye nani, wena nyoka enwabuzelayo, eqamba amanga.

Manje sekufika isikhathi sokwahlulelwa kwenu nokubhujiswa okuphelele kombuso wenu, ngisho nombuso eniwakhile emizamweni yenu yokuzama ukungifihla, ngisho noYahweh, endalweni yami. Aniyikubaleka. Anisayikungena emazulwini, ngoba niphonswe emhlabeni ningaphinde nibuye, futhi indawo yenu ayisekho emazulwini. Niboshelwe emhlabeni enawukhanukelayo, owawungesiwo owenu ukuba niwuthathe. Yonke imisebenzi yenu iyochithwa ezimpilweni zalabo enizame ukubabhubhisa (nasemhlabeni uqobo); bayohlala phakade ngokuthula nokuphepha, futhi bayoba nenjabulo ephelele, futhi ngiyohlala phakathi kwesintu futhi ngiyoba uNkulunkulu wabo.

Kodwa wena, wena onwabuzelayo, awunamandla ekubukeni kwami, futhi awukwazi ukungenza lutho ukungimisa. Ezinsukwini eziyisithupha selokhu kwawa umuntu, ngoba usuku olulodwa luyiminyaka eyinkulungwane emehlweni ami, anifezanga lutho ngaphandle kokwahlulelwa kwenu, ukulahlwa, ukubhujiswa, nokuhlukunyezwa kwenu nabo bonke abalandelayo. Ngoba niyoboshwa futhi niphonswe kulelo chibi elikhulu elivuthayo lapho nginenzele khona amalangabi ashisayo kakhulu, ngisho namalangabi aluhlaza okwesibhakabhaka, futhi niyosha phakade, futhi ngiyoniphindisela wonke umsebenzi omubi enake nawenza, izikhathi eziningi. Nibulele izixuku ngezixuku, futhi niyokhokhela yonke imiphefumulo elahlekile, futhi anisoze naphunyuka. Niyokwazi zonke izono enazenza, futhi anisoze naphunyuka ekwahlulelweni, futhi niyohlushwa phakade ngenxa yezono enazenza. Niyokwazi kahle izono namacala enu ekuhlushweni kwenu, futhi labo enibazondayo bayoba nokunqoba kokugcina phezu kwenu. Futhi bonke abangalungile enake nabayenga ukuba bone futhi bazinikele ekuhlubukeni kwenu, ngisho nalabo amagama abo angalotshwanga eNcwadini Yokuphila, bayohlanganyela ekuhlushweni kwenu. Niyokhokhela sonke isono esisodwa enasenza, ngoba nonile kuYahweh nasendalweni yami, futhi ngiyophindisela. Ngoba ebulungiseni bami ngiyophindisela izono zenu, izenzo, nezindlela ezimbi ngokugcwele ezifubeni zenu izikhathi eziningi naphakade. Futhi mina, Yahweh, ngisho uNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade, ngifunga lezi zinto ngegama lami, ngoba ngeke ngifunge ngomkhulu kunami.

Niyosebenzela izinjongo zami, futhi ngenzondo nembubhiso yenu niyosunduza abakhethiweyo bami kimi; ngoba ngiyobahola ngesandla sikaYeshua HaMashiach nebutho lakhe lamadoda anamandla, ngoba bayobutha izimvu zami ezisele. (Bhekani! Abayi-144,000 abathunywe nguYeshua HaMashiach bayokwenza imisebenzi emikhulu phambi kobubi benu obunengekayo.) Futhi akekho noyedwa engimmisele ukuphila okuphakade oyowela emacebweni enu, ngoba abasoze bahlwitshwa esandleni sami, futhi akekho ongbahlwitha ezandleni zeNdodana yami: Ngoba ngimnike igama eliphezu kwawo onke amagama, futhi uza ngamandla kaYahweh uqobo, ngoba ngihlanganyele naye isihlalo sami sobukhosi. Futhi uyochoboza ikhanda lenu ngokuphelele. Wehlulekile, sikhosana esincane, nesikhathi sakho sesifikile.

Mina, Yahweh, uNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade, ngithumele lawa mazwi ngenceku yami, futhi ngenceku yami amazwi ami athunyelwe, futhi ayokwenzeka. Ngoba uYahweh, uNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade, usho kanjalo, futhi akukho okungaphula izimemezelo zami. Ngingu-El Shaddai!
