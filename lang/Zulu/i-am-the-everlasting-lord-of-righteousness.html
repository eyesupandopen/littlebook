uMasingana 01, 2025

NginguNkulunkulu Waphakade Wokulunga!

Yizwani! Lalelani Amazwi kaJesu Kristu, nithobise imicabango yenu, nisoke izinhliziyo nezindlebe zenu ekulaleleni Amazwi kaYeshua HaMashiach, mina-ke ngizokwabela abahlakaniphileyo ukuhlakanipha, ukuze ohlakaniphileyo phakathi kwenu afunde ukwesaba iNkosi, nifunde ukuthuthumela phambi kweNkosi yamabandla, ngokuba uYahweh, uMvelinqangi Omkhulu, ungidalile kanjalo.

Ungubani wena, muntu wemvelo, ukuba uphakamise isithende sakho ngokumelene noYahweh, uMvelinqangi Omkhulu, yena onguMdala Wezinsuku, yena oyiNkosi Yemimoya? Ungubani wena muntu wemvelo ukuba uziphakamise ngokuzigqaja ngokumelene noJesu waseNazaretha onguKristu Jesu, yena ogama lakhe liphezu kwawo onke amagama, yena uYahweh, uMvelinqangi Omkhulu, abelane naye ngesihlalo saKhe sobukhosi, yena ophakanyiswe ngaphezu kwakho konke ngaphandle kukaYahweh uqobo lwaKhe, zize zenziwe izitha zami isenabelo sezinyawo zami? Umbuso wami nokubusa kwami akusoze kwaphela, futhi ngiyakunikela uMbuso othotshisiwe kuBaba Wezibani, ukuze u-Adonai Yahweh abe yikho konke kukho konke!

Ingabe into ebunjiweyo ingasho yini kulowo oyibumbileyo ukuthi, 'Ungenzele ntoni kanje?' Ingabe isigaxa sobumba esenziwe izitsha zokuhlonipheka singasho yini kumbumbi ukuthi, 'Ungenzele ntoni kanje? Kungani unginike udumo olungaka? Ngifuna ukuba enye into, ngizothatha udumo lwabo esikhundleni salokho!' Ingabe ukhona yini umbumbi ongayibhubhisi indalo embi kangaka engahlubuka esandleni sakhe, ize ifune ukubhubhisa nombumbi uqobo, kanye nezinye izidalwa zombumbi; sengathi into enjalo ingenzeka?

Nokho, zinjalo izindlela eziyisiphukuphuku nezihlanyayo zalabo abawileyo abahlubuke kuMdali wabo ngokushiya imiyalo nemisebenzi yabo engcwele nehloniphekile ababeyinikiwe ukuba bayifeze ngokulunga, imisebenzi eyayinikwe nguYahweh uqobo lwaKhe ngosuku adala ngalo amadodana kaNkulunkulu. Ngisho nezingelosi ezingcwele uYahweh azenza ngaphambi kokuba adale umhlaba, okubhalwe ngazo kuJobe lapho edala umhlaba: Izinkanyezi zokusa zahlabelela kanyekanye, nawo onke amadodana kaNkulunkulu amemeza ngenjabulo! Futhi amemeza ngenjabulo ngempela ngenxa yezimangaliso nezimangaliso azibona ezandleni zikaYahweh lapho edala umhlaba. Zazinjalo-ke nezingelosi eziwileyo, ngisho namadodana kaNkulunkulu ekuqaleni adalwa enkazimulo namandla ngenxa yezinjongo zawo, uYahweh wazembathisa udumo wazenza zivelele ekuhlakanipheni ukuze zithande uYahweh ngokulalela okuphelele izwi laKhe, futhi ngempela zanikwa isimo sokuqala esikhazimulayo ezadalelwa sona, zaze zahlubuka ezinkanukweni zezinhliziyo zazo, kwafunyanwa ububi kuzo.

Kanjalo-ke oSathane bahlubuka ekuqaleni ngokumelene noMdali wabo, nalabo ababekade bezungeza isihlalo sobukhosi sikaYahweh, isihlalo saKhe sobukhosi esisazungezwe yizinkulungwane eziyishumi eziphindwe ngezinkulungwane eziyishumi, nokho uYahweh usenguMbusi futhi akadingi meluleki. Nokho kulotshiwe kanje ngalodrako omkhulu obomvu, onamakhanda ayisikhombisa, nezimpondo eziyishumi, nemiqhele eyisikhombisa emakhanda akhe, ukuthi umsila wakhe wadonsa ingxenye yesithathu yezinkanyezi zezulu, waziphonsa emhlabeni: kanjalo-ke oSathane ngisho nengxenye yesithathu yezingelosi ezalandela ukuhlubuka kwazo baphonswa phansi emhlabeni, ngisho nangezinsuku lapho ukonakala kwezingelosi kuwa ezulwini ngesono nokuhlubuka, baba ngubumnyama obukhulu emva kwezono zabo, futhi banjalo nanamuhla.

Kanjalo-ke inyoka yasondela kunina wabo bonke abaphilayo eNsimini yase-Edeni eminyakeni eminingi edlule, yamkhohlisa u-Eva ukuba adle khona lokho uYahweh ayebayalele ukuba bangakudli, ngoba ngosuku ababeyodla ngalo esihlahleni solwazi lokuhle nokubi, ngalolo suku babeyokufa nokufa. Noma kunjalo, u-Eva wathatha enyokeni njengoba ayekhohliswe yinkohliso yayo wadla, wanika nomyeni wakhe lesi sithelo esenqatshelwe, u-Adamu wadla ngokuzithandela, base befa njengoba kwakuthenjisiwe, isintu saba ngaphansi kokufa kusukela lapho ngenxa yesono esatholakala egazini lakhe. Ngoba u-Adamu wonakele ingunaphakade esonweni, nangenxa yale njongo iNdodana yoMuntu yabonakaliswa ukuba ngichithe imisebenzi kasathane, ngilethe ukukhululwa ku-Adamu owileyo, ngoba ngingowokugcina u-Adamu uYahweh amenzile umoya onika ukuphila. Ngiqinisile, ngiqinisile, ngiyakudala kabusha bonke abamukela isipho sami sensindiso babe yisidalwa esisha nesilungileyo, ngokuba iTestamente lami Elisha liletha lokho.

Ngakho u-Adamu no-Eva baxoshwa eNsimini yase-Edeni, bashintshwa esimweni esikhanyayo esimangalisayo ababedalwe ngaso enkazimulweni ephelele nguYahweh uqobo lwaKhe, futhi ngaphansi kokubuswa yisono nobubi obulethwa yiso, bazulazula emhlabeni ekuyalweni kwabo. Futhi ezinsukwini zikaJerede abaLindi behla eNtabeni iHerimoni, labo ababethunjwe yizinkanuko zezinhliziyo zabo ngoba babekhanuka amadodakazi abantu, befisa nenzalo kuwo, nasezinkanukweni zezinhliziyo zabo abalingwa ngazo oSathane, bazibopha ngeziqalekiso ngokufanayo, ngisho nasesifungweni sabo esingcwele esesiphenduke isiqalekiso saphakade esiyogcwaliseka kulelo chibi elikhulu lomlilo ovuthayo wesibabule lapho impethu yabo ingafi khona.

Kanjalo-ke amadodana kaNkulunkulu angena emadodakazini abantu, azithathela omkabo kubo bonke abakhethayo. Abanye besifazane baya ngokuzithandela, bezinikela ngokugcwele ezonweni zalabo abawileyo (abayothwala ukwahlulelwa kwabo), kanti abanye bathathwa ngendluzula futhi bahlukunyezwa ngesihluku abaLindi ezinkanukweni zabo ezimbi. Kanjalo-ke kwakunjalo izwe langaleso sikhathi, izwe elabhujiswa uzamcolo omkhulu owenzeka ezinsukwini zikaNowa, elachithwa ngenxa yezinengiso ezimbi ezenziwa abalindi, ababedalelwe ukubhubhisa isintu ukuze isintu singalokothi sibe nomhlaba abawileyo ababewukhanukela, futhi manje sebeboshiwe ekwahlulelweni.

Ngoba bakhetha ukubhujiswa kwabo nokuhlushwa kwaphakade ukuze bathole izinto ababezikhanuka, okungezona ezabo ukuba bazithathe, futhi lezo zinto bazithatha, futhi basazama ukuzithatha ngenkani. Noma kunjalo, izenzo nezinhlelo zabo ziyophelela ezeni, futhi abasoze bayithola leyo nkathi yabo yegolide, kodwa bayothola isahlulelo esinokhahlo nesinzima nesaphakade esikhundleni salokho. Ngoba kuyiqiniso elingaguquki: Umhlaba ungowoMhlengi kanye nokugcwala kwawo, futhi mina nginguNkulunkulu Waphakade Wokulunga owathenga umhlaba ngegazi lami elihlengayo, futhi ngiza ukuzothatha ngenkani lokho okungokwami ngokufanelekile, futhi ubani oyokwazi ukumelana namandla ami amakhulu?

Njengoba kulotshiwe ngami, kuyokwenzeka kanjalo: Bhekani, iNgonyama yesizwe sakwaJuda, impande kaDavide, inqobile ukuvula incwadi, nokuxazulula izimpawu zayo eziyisikhombisa. Ngakho-ke, qondisisani ngokugcwele ukuthi nginguye ofanele ukuxazulula izimpawu ngoba itayitela lomhlaba lingelami! Futhi njengoba kulotshiwe ngami, kuyokwenzeka kanjalo ngoba ngifanele ukuthatha incwadi, nokuvula izimpawu zayo: ngokuba ngahlatshwa, futhi ngihlengile bonke abayokuza ensindisweni yaphakade kuYahweh ngegazi lami elingenasono, futhi nganihlengela kuyo yonke imindeni, nezilimi, nabantu, nezizwe: futhi akekho noyedwa ongaphuca omunye wenu ezandleni zami zaphakade! Ngakho-ke, ubani okufanele nimesabe?

Ngakho-ke abesifazane abathathwe amadodana kaNkulunkulu, (abaLindi abawileyo) bakhulelwa, babazalela iziqhwaga eziyisinengiso ezazikhohlakele ekujuleni kwezinhliziyo zazo. Babeqhosha ngobukhulu namandla abo, kodwa ekwahluleleni kukaYahweh phezu kwabo ngenxa yobubi abona ngabo isintu, wabopha imimoya yabo emhlabeni njengesijeziso ngoba babedalwe emhlabeni ngesimo senyama enesono, futhi imimoya yabo yayingunaphakade ngokwemvelo, inikwe oYise ababeboshelwe ukufeza izinkanuko zabo. Ngakho-ke njengoba lawa maNefili afa ekwahlulelweni kwawo okuvela kuYahweh uqobo, imimoya yawo yahlukaniswa nemizimba ekufeni kwawo, yaqhubeka, iboshelwe emhlabeni njengesijeziso. Ngakho-ke, alambile futhi omile kodwa awakwazi ukuthatha ukudla noma amanzi. Njengoba ayekade enemizimba emikhulu nenamandla ayeyisebenzisela ukubusa abakhileyo bomhlaba, enziwa angenamandla ngaphandle kokuba akwazi ukuthatha umzimba womunye umuntu ahlale kuwo njengamadayimoni angama-parasite, ngoba sekunjalo. Qondisisani ukuthi kusukela ezinsukwini zikaNowa, ngenxa yokunxusa kukaNowa, izingxenye eziyisishiyagalolunye kweziyishumi zazo zigcinwe kwalasha kodwa sezizokhululwa emhlabeni ekwahlulelweni.

Nokho, sekunjalo ukuhlubuka kwabawileyo kusukela osukwini uqobo lokutholakala kobubi ezinhliziyweni zabo. Ukuhlanya nobuhlanya kugcwele izinhliziyo zabawileyo ekuhlubukeni kwabo; kanye nomuntu wemvelo, owazihlanganisa nabo ngokwenqaba ukusuka esimweni sakhe esiwileyo nesinesono: bonke bafuna ukuba yinto enye kunaleyo uYahweh ayihlosile ekulungeni okuphelele. Esikhundleni sokulalela uMdali wabo obezobaqinisekisa ngokugcwaliseka okuphelele, intokozo, injabulo, nokuthula; obezobahlinzeka ngokuphelele othandweni lwaKhe ngesidalwa saKhe, esikhundleni salokho bafuna futhi balandela izinkanuko zezinhliziyo zabo, bafuna izwe labo ngaphandle kukaYahweh, lapho bengaphila khona izinkanuko zezinhliziyo zabo, bajabulele ukuhlubuka kwabo ezintokozweni zesono ngokulimaza isidalwa sikaYahweh. Ngisho nezifiso ezingcolile nezimbi abangeke bazizuze noma bazifinyelele.

Ngokufanayo, umuntu wemvelo uzihlanganisile nokuhlubuka kwabo ngaphandle kokuthi umuntu wemvelo uzalwa ekungazini nasebumnyameni ngezinjongo zakhe zangempela nenkazimulo engaba khona uYahweh ayethembise abalalelayo, futhi umuntu wemvelo uzilandele ngokuzithandela izindlela zabawileyo, yize abawileyo bezibophezele ekubhujisweni kuka-Adamu, okuyinto u-Adamu engayinakile ngokuzithandela ezinkanukweni zakhe, ngisho nalapho ezibopha khona ngokulindela kobuwula emangeni abo amaningi nanobubi. Ngisho nangendlela yalawo manga, abaningi bayoholwa bayiswe echibini lomlilo, yize leso sijeziso sasingahloselwe bona.

Nokho, lokhu kuhlubuka kuqhubekile kuze kube manje eminyakeni edlule, impi ekhona phakathi kwenzalo efanele kaYahweh nezidalwa eziyisinengiso ezenziwe ukuguqulwa kofuzo lwabakhohlakele, futhi ababona onkulunkulu nakuba bezenzisa. Zinjalo-ke izikhathi eniphila kuzo, wena muntu, ohlubuka ngokungalaleli okugcwele inzondo ezindleleni zoMdali wakho, ujoyina ngokungazi labo abazibophezele ngokugcwele ekufeni kwenu nasekubhujisweni kwaphakade abakufisayo.

Ngakho-ke ngimemezela usuku, mina, uYeshua HaMashiach, ngimemezela ukuthi usuku lweNkosi selufikile futhi seluzoqala emhlabeni ngokwahlulela okunodlame olukhulu, futhi izinhlekelele eziningi manje sezizodedelwa emhlabeni, ngisho nezinhlekelele nokwahlulela okwabikezelwa kusukela kudala, izikhathi zako sezifikile. Ngakho-ke, qondisisani ukuthi ngingubani mina owenza lesi simemezelo ngamandla nasesihlalweni sobukhosi sikaYahweh uqobo lwaKhe, ngoba nginguye ofanele ukuthatha incwadi nokuyivula, nokuxazulula izimpawu zayo, futhi ngizokwenza lokho kanye, futhi ubani ongavimba isandla sami esinamandla?!

Qonda, wena muntu wemvelo, nawe omphikayo ngentukuthelo uMdali wakho, ukuthi izikhathi zenu sezifikile. Futhi uthando olukhulu nomusa nobubele kunikiwe kulezi zikhathi zokwahlulela, ngoba bonke ababiza igama likaJesu waseNazaretha kuze kube sekupheleni kwabo bayosindiswa, futhi isisindo somvuzo wabo asifanele ukuqhathaniswa nokuhlupheka abayokukhuthazelela ngenxa yegama lami. Qonda, wena muntu wemvelo, ukuthi ngizohola amaqhawe ami ayizi-144,000 ukuze ngikhulule uJakobe, futhi ngisazofihla insali enamandla yakwa-Israyeli eyohubeka iphila iye eMbusweni wami weminyaka eyinkulungwane endaweni elungiswe nguYahweh ehlane, ukuze ithele, yande, ijabulele ukukhuluphala kwezwe eMbusweni wami weminyaka eyinkulungwane ozofika maduze emhlabeni ukuze kugcwaliseke isithembiso, isivumelwano, netestamente.

Kuyoba njalo kumuntu wemvelo, ukuthi bayokwamukela iWundlu likaNkulunkulu njengoMsindisi wabo, noma bayobe bezikhethele ukwahlulelwa kwabo kwaphakade kulelo chibi lomlilo elivutha njalo, ngoba akukho okunye abangakukhetha, futhi akukho ndawo enye abangaya kuyo ngoba uYahweh akasoze avumela ububi bunqobe futhi bubhubhise indalo yaKhe, futhi izinsuku zabo zokuhlubuka ngokuzithandela sezizophela.

NginguMhlengi womhlaba, onikelwe ngenxa yezono zawo.

NginguMhlengi wesizwe sakwaJuda, futhi ngokulunga ngiyeza ukuzokwahlulela nokulwa impi.

Ngingulowo uMhlengi noMahluleli abahlangene ngokugcwele kuye ezinhlosweni zami zaphakade nasekubuseni kwami njengeNkosi yamakhosi noMbusi wababusi, ngakho-ke ubani ongamelana noYeshua HaMashiach ongcwele ka-Adonai, yena uYahweh amthumile ukuba enze ukwahlulela kwaphakade phezu kwabawileyo nabo bonke abakhetha ukulandela izindlela zabo.

Ngalokho-ke ngeluleka abakholwayo kuKhristu ukuba bahlale bethembekile, ngoba usuku lwabo lokuhlengwa selufikile futhi selusondele kakhulu. Mababheke kuphela eNkosini yabo Engunaphakade Yokulunga ngoba abawileyo basigcwalisile umhlaba ngezikhukhula ezinkulu zamanga nobuxoki obuhloselwe ukukhohlisa nabakhethiweyo uqobo uma bekungenzeka. Ngakho-ke, yiqondeni into yokuthi kuthunyelwe abaprofethi abaningi bamanga abaprofetha ngezindlela zikaBhalami, futhi abathathwe yimimoya kaJezebeli, nabakhuluma ngemimoya kamphikukristu efundisa ngomunye uJesu, ome ngamanga esikhundleni sikaJesu Kristu futhi emelene naye, onguYeshuwa HaMashiach ezinhliziyweni zabakhohlisiweyo. Bonke abakhohliseka kanjalo bayokwamukela ukwahlulelwa kwabo uma benqaba ukuphenduka ezindleleni zabo lapho beqondiswa yiNkosi yabo ngemilomo yabakholwayo kuKhristu. Ngiqinisile, ngiqinisile, abanye abaprofethi bamanga banobuqotho kodwa bakhohlisiwe futhi bangafinyelelwa ngeqiniso elikhulunywa ngezindebe zothando, kodwa iqiniso kuphela eliyobakhulula, neqiniso eliqondile lezono zabo nokuprofetha kwabo kwamanga, ukuze bakwazi ukuzilungisa ngokuphenduka.

Bese kuba khona labo abaprofetha ngenxa yemali, nenzuzo engcolile, ukuze bathole udumo nokunakwa futhi bazitike ezintweni zaleli zwe elibi. Kungakhathaliseki ukuthi ngubani oprofetha amanga, mkhuze futhi uwaphike amazwi akhe, ngisho namazwi aklanywe ngabawileyo ukubamba nabakhethiweyo uqobo enkohlisweni uma kungenzeka. Kungani nigijima niye le nale, Bandla lami elithembekile? Anginilayezanga yini, nganeluleka futhi nganinika amandla okuba ngabanqobi bami? Ngisho nalabo abanqoba zonke izici zombuso wesitha ngendlela efanayo nami enganqoba ngayo? Ngakho-ke, wozani kuKristu Jesu, futhi ngiyoqinisa ezinhliziyweni zenu abaprofethi bami beqiniso ngoba abaprofethi bami beqiniso bakhuluma ngokuvumelana nenhliziyo yeNkosi yabo eliyiZwi elenziwa inyama. Ngakho-ke, uKristu Jesu ngeke aphikisane nemibhalo, ngoba nginguye!

Kulabo abacabanga ngokuzidla ukuthi bangakimi kodwa bemukele amanga: Kungani nigijima niye le nale, nifuna umuntu ozonincenga ezindlebeni, nize nifune nabathakathi abahlonyuzayo nabakhulumela phansi abazonitshela lokho ncamashi enifuna ukukuzwa ukuze kuvuswe izinkanuko ezinhliziyweni zenu ezimbi njengoba nibalekela ukuphenduka niwele ezingalweni zabakhohlisi abathunyelwe ukukhohlisa. Nigijima niye le nale ngoba anilinaki izwi likaYeshuwa HaMashiach, ngoba nenqaba iqiniso elalizonikhulula, ngoba nenqaba ukufunda kimi ngokubamba iZwi likaYahweh njengezinga lenu eliphakeme nelukuphela kwalo. Ngakho-ke, ngeluleka bonke abacabanga ukuthi baprofetha ngoKristu Jesu, ngisho nabo bonke abacabanga ukuthi bakhulumela uYahweh, uMina Omkhulu Enginguye, ukuba baziqaphele funa bawele ebumnyameni obungunaphakade nasekwahlulelweni okuphakade ngoba bonke abahlanganyela nabawileyo ngendlela efanayo futhi benqabe ukuphenduka amagama abo azosulwa encwadini yokuphila uma benqaba umlilo womcwebisi abayosala kuwo uma beqhubeka ezonweni zabo, ngoba asisekho isikhathi.

Ngakho-ke ngiyeluleka futhi ngiyaxwayisa labo engibathandayo ukuze bagweme inkohliso enamandla engahloselwe bona uma behamba ngokulalela iNkosi yabo, futhi ngeluleka futhi ngiyaxwayisa labo engibathandayo ukuze bangabanjwa inkohliso enkulu manje eseyengamele umhlaba. Ngeluleka bonke abayozwa lawa mazwi ukuba basuke ebubini futhi basuke kubathakathi abahlonyuzayo nabakhulumela phansi futhi basuse izindawo zabo etafuleni likaJezebeli futhi benqabe imivuzo kaBhalami. Yiqondeni into yokuthi noma ngubani oziprofetho zakhe ziphikisana nemibhalo ukhuluma amanga ngokuzenzisa. Yiqondeni into yokuthi uYahweh, uMina Omkhulu Enginguye, noYeshuwa HaMashiach (ophinde abe nguKristu Jesu) abasoze baphikisana nemibhalo. Ngakho-ke, labo abakhuluma ngokumelene nemibhalo bakhuluma ngokumelene noYahweh uMina Omkhulu Enginguye futhi bakhuluma ngokumelene noYeshuwa HaMashiach ophinde abe nguKristu Jesu.

Qondisisani kahle ukuthi ngingubani nokuthi uYahweh ungidalele ukuba ngibe yini. NgiyiZwi likaYahweh elenziwe inyama elihlala kini zithandwa zami, futhi angisoze ngaziphikisa noma ngaqamba amanga, ngoba anginasono futhi ngiyohlala ngingenasono. Mqondeni uBaba Wezinkanyiso okungekho kuye ukuguquguquka noma isithunzi sokuphenduka, lowo kanye okumele nisebenzelane naye! Qondisisani ukuthi akunakwenzeka ukuba uAdonai Yahweh aziphikise ngoba akunakwenzeka ukuba uAdonai Yahweh aqambe amanga. Nokho, kungani nilalela ngobuwula labo abadlula ngokunyenya ebusuku? Niyozifeza yini izithandwa zami, ekubeni kufanele nithembeke futhi nilalele iNkosi eyodwa, engumsindisi wenu waphakade, yona kanye esezofika ukuzohlwitha abakholwayo kuKhristu ibayise emazulwini noma nini manje?

Ngakho-ke yiqondeni, ukuthi amasango esihogo ngeke anqobe abakholwayo kuKhristu abaliBandla lami leqiniso, ngoba kulotshiwe kanjalo futhi kwathenjiswa kusukela endulo, futhi kusenjalo kulotshiwe futhi kuyathenjiswa ngezikhathi zamanje, ngoba amazwi kaYahweh noYeshuwa HaMashiach angamazwi ahlanzekile njengesiliva, elivivinywe esithandweni somlilo somhlaba, elicwengisiswe kasikhombisa. Yebo, abawileyo abanamandla kubakholwayo kuKhristu futhi ngiyokwenza abathembekile bami banyathele ngaphansi kwezinyawo zonke izici zombuso wesitha, baze banyathele nalabo abangafuna ukumisa okungenakuvinjwa.

NginguKristu Jesu, ngubani ongamela amandla ami namandla engiwamukele kuYahweh uqobo!

Qondisisani ukuthi mina noBaba simunye ebunye nasenhlosweni, futhi ngihlale ngenza intando kaBaba! Ngakho-ke, akekho ongamela mina, futhi ayikho into phakathi kwabawileyo enganqoba mina, kungaba ngobuqili, noma ngamandla, noma ngobuningi, noma ngobuchwepheshe babo obungenamsebenzi! Futhi nginguye owaqinisekisa ukunqotshwa kwabo kwaphakade eKhalvari lapho ngiphusha amazwi athi, sekuphelile. Qondisisani ukuthi nihlonzwa ngokugcwele noKristu Jesu futhi nginenza nime ngokuqina emandleni ami namandla ami! Ngakho-ke, ngubani ongamela nina zithandwa zami? Ngubani okufanele nimesabe?
