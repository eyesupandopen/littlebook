Epreli 24, 2024

Yizwani Amazwi E Nkosi: Imfihlakalo Enkulu Yembuliwe!

UYeshua HaMashiach uthi kuMlobokazi wakhe: Lalelani nizwe amazwi ami, nina bakhethiweyo bami, niqonde inkulumo yami, ngoba inkulu imfihlakalo yokukholwa! Abaningi benu abanawo umqondo wemfihlakalo enkulu kaNkulunkulu, kodwa isenhliziyweni kanye nasesisekelweni sabalandeli beNdlela, ngokwemfundiso nangokwenza: Iyisizinda sobuKristu enginidalele kuso kimi, futhi niyisidalwa esisha kuKristu Jesu, ngoba mina, Jesu Kristu, ngenze njalo.

Kwabaningi benu, imfihlakalo enkulu kaNkulunkulu isilahlekile ngenxa yesikhathi namasiko nobuqili obunobuqili lapho belindele ukukhohlisa. Ningesabi, bantwana bami, yizwani amazwi ami futhi nizwe inkulumo yami, ngoba ngizonichazela yona lapha kule mizuzu yokugcina yalesi sikhathi. Qondani, bantwana bami, ukuthi ukudideka okuningi namanga amaningi kungeniswe ebandleni lami yizimpisi ezihahayo nezimfundiso zazo zamanga. Bahlukanise iZwi lami (noMzimba wami) ngokungafanele ngomqondo wezinkathi ezihlukene zokuphatha kukaNkulunkulu (dispensationalism), futhi lokhu kungamanga amakhulu alethwe yilabo abakha izindlu zabo esihlabathini, hhayi kimi, itshe legumbi eliyinhloko, ngoba ngaphandle kwami akekho umuntu ongakha.

Isihlabathi abakhetha ukusenza itshe legumbi lezakhiwo zabo ngamaBhayibheli akhohlakele aholela ebunye bezinkolo ngezinjongo umuntu wesono anazo enhliziyweni yakhe, izinjongo zokulawula nokufa nokubhujiswa. Isihlabathi abasisebenzisela ukwakha senziwe ngemibhalo eyonakele, imibhalo yesiGreki edluliselwe isuka e-Alexandria. Landelani imibhalo yezinhlu zase-Antiyokiya, iByzantine, futhi lokhu kuholela kwiTextus Receptus, isitha esikhulu saseRoma. Azikho izinkathi ezihlukene zokuphatha kukaNkulunkulu, futhi uNkulunkulu akahlukanisiwe, nakuba ngizochaza umqondo wezinkathi njengoba uvela ezincwadini zami. UNkulunkulu usebenzelene nesintu esebenzisa izikhathi; fundisisani ukuze nifunde. Ngakho-ke qondani, lokhu kuhlukaniswa akuveli kimi kodwa kwaklanyelwa ukonakalisa iBandla lami kusukela ekuqaleni.

Imfihlakalo enkulu ifihliwe kini, bantwana bami, kodwa ayifihliwe ngokuphelele, ngoba ngayembulela uMphostoli uPawulu eminyakeni eminingi yamaJubili edlule. Ifihlwe ngamanga, inkohliso, izimfundiso ezingalungile ezingaveli kimi; kodwa ifihliwe obala, futhi isela lingathanda ukuthi ningalokothi niyiqaphele, kodwa iyindaba esobala. Ngoba lokho okwethusa uSathane kakhulu ngeBandla lami ukuthi uyazi ukuthi akanamandla okunilawula lapho nihamba emandleni ami aphelele nasebudlelwaneni obusobala nami. Ngakho-ke, njengoba engeke aphule uphawu lukaNkulunkulu engilubeke phezu kwenu, futhi akekho ongakwazi ukunihlutha ezandleni zami noma ezandleni zikaBaba, uzama ukuniminyanisa ebumpofwini. Hhayi ubumpofu bezinto ezibonakalayo; iBandla lami akufanele neze likhuthaze ivangeli lokuchuma elingalungile ngoba aliveli kimi, nakuba abaningi benu bekholelwa kulawo manga. USathane unifisela ubumpofu obungokomoya ukuze anifihlele ubuwena benu bangempela futhi aniyenge ukuba nikholelwe ukuthi niyizicathulo ezingenamandla, enizimisele ukuphakade ukuphendula esinye isihlathi nokubaleka njengegwala, okuyindlela yokusebenzisa kabi imiyalo yami esetshenziselwa ukulawula amadodana ami ukuba angahambi emandleni awo eqiniso. Cha, angikuthumanga ukuba uphendule esinye isihlathi ukuze ube yisisulu futhi unqotshwe okubi; ngikuthumele ukunqoba okubi ngokuhle nokudiliza imisebenzi kaDeveli, ngoba nami ngathunyelwa kanjalo, futhi nami nginithumele kanjalo. Kodwa qondani: impindiselo ngeyami, ngakho-ke ningaziphindiseli, kodwa phendulani esinye isihlathi lapho kufanele khona ngenxa yokusindiswa komunye futhi ninikeze indawo olakeni, ngoba imiphefumulo eminingi isindisiwe ngale ndlela.

Qondani: banikhohlisile ngamanga abo, futhi babeka la manga aphikisana neqiniso lami eduze kwemibhalo engcwele. Bakwenza lokho emaphethelweni amaBhayibheli okufunda; bakwenza lokho besebenzisa izincazelo ezingalungile; bakwenza lokho ngobuqili ngokuhumusha kabi izincazelo ezithile zesiGreki ezisekelwe esikweni lasendulo lobuqaba hhayi eqinisweni leZwi likaNkulunkulu eliNgcwele. Ningasebenzisa le mithombo ngezinye izikhathi, kodwa qaphelisisani futhi niyeke ukuvumela umuntu wemvelo azame ukunichazela izindaba zikamoya; ngoba umuntu wemvelo akanakuqonda izindaba zikamoya, ngoba ziwubuwula kuye. Ngakho-ke, yazini ukuthi abaningi balaba bakhohlisi abahlanekezela umbhalo abanakho ukholo eZwini likaNkulunkulu futhi abawufanele umsebenzi abazinike wona ngoba abanawo umoya kaKristu futhi abaveli kimi; ngoba ukube babevela kimi, ngabe ngibaholele eqinisweni. Nginguye onibeke uphawu lokuhlengwa okuphelele emandleni omoya wami ongcwele.

INkosi uJesu Kristu ithi kuMlobokazi wayo othandekayo: Ngidinga ukuba niqonde injongo yenu yangempela, futhi uma ningayiqondi imfihlakalo enkulu njengoba ihlobene nani, khona-ke ngeke nihambe ekugcwaleni kwamandla enginibizele ukuba nihambe kuwo, kodwa imizamo yenu iyoba buthaka njengoba kunjalo kwabaningi benu. Kuyangizwisa ubuhlungu futhi kudabukise inhliziyo yami ukubona ukuthi abantwana bami abaziqondi bona uqobo futhi abanaso isifiso sokucwaninga. Kufanele nihlale nicwaninga amazwi ami ngokukhuthala konke; kodwa izimfundiso zamanga zenza abaningi bangabi nandaba, ngoba akukho kudla okwakha umzimba kuzo. Ngoba zingamanga kaSathane, kodwa abaningi baphila ngokudla okusheshayo neziphuzo ezinoshukela, futhi iningi alinawo amandla okudla ukudla engikunike khona nokuphuza isiphuzo engikunike khona, ngoba ngokwenza kanjalo anisoze naphinde nilambe noma nome futhi.

Ngakho yizwani amazwi ami, Mlobokazi wami onganandaba; sekuyisikhathi sokuvukela obizweni lwenu lwangempela futhi nenze imisebenzi engiyilungiselele nina, futhi niyazi ukuthi ngizokwenza izimangaliso eziningi ngezandla zenu njengoba nihamba ngenxa yami ngokholo nangolwazi lweqiniso. Ngoba ngathini enkonzweni yami kwa-Israyeli emuva ngesikhathi ngihamba obala eJudiya? Ngoba umlando kaLuka uthini ngalolu daba: Wathi kubo, Nina nikunikiwe ukwazi imfihlakalo yombuso kaNkulunkulu, kodwa kulabo abangaphandle, zonke lezi zinto zenziwa ngemifanekiso. Ngakho-ke qondani, abaphostoli bami bakhethwa ukuba bamukele imfihlakalo enkulu kaNkulunkulu engayembula kubo. Bayibhala phansi, futhi ngiyivikele imibhalo ukuze nazi iqiniso lale ndaba.

Abaningi benu balalela abaJuda abangafuna ukunibuyisela ngaphansi koMthetho (Torah). Kuyathakazelisa ukuqaphela ukuthi ukube kwakungeyona imfihlakalo enkulu kaNkulunkulu, iningi lenu belingeke lazi ngoMthetho; ngoba awunikezwanga ezizweni zabahedeni kodwa kwa-Israyeli wasendulo ukuba uwugcine, ube ngumfundisi wabo ukuba ubalethe kimi. Ngoba mina ngingukugcwaliseka komthetho, futhi labo ababambelela ngentshiseko emthethweni kaMose bafuna ukudumisa inyama yabo nokuhamba ekulungeni kwabo siqu, futhi abanawo umqondo wokuthi uMthetho ugcinwa ngokugcwele nangokucophelela ngokuzihlanganisa kwenu nami. Ngoba yimina ngedwa owakwazi ukugcina umthetho, futhi mina, uJesu waseNazaretha, ngigcwalise izimfuneko ezilungile zomthetho. Ngaqinisa impumelelo yami ngegazi lami, futhi ngabe senginihlanza emanzini agelezayo omoya wami njengoba nibhaphathizelwe kimi.

Ngoba ngathuma uPawulu ukuba atsheleni abaseKorinte? Ngoba ngoMoya munye sonke sabhapathizelwa emzimbeni munye, noma singamaJuda noma singabahedeni, noma siyizigqila noma sikhululekile; futhi sonke senziwe ukuba siphuze uMoya munye. Uma nihamba nigcina umthetho, niyohluleka futhi niyolahlekelwa ubizo lwenu, njengoba ngifingqe umthetho emiyalweni emibili emikhulu: Woyithanda iNkosi uNkulunkulu wakho ngayo yonke inhliziyo yakho, nangawo wonke umphefumulo wakho, nangayo yonke ingqondo yakho, nangawo onke amandla akho: lo ngumthetho wokuqala. Owesibili uyafana nawo, uthi, Wothanda umakhelwane wakho njengalokhu uzithanda wena. Yazini, bantwana bami, ukuthi ayikho imiyalo emikhulu kunale, ngoba kule miyalo kugcinwa yonke imiyalo.

Qondani: Ngikuchaza konke lokhu ezincwadini engazithumela emabandleni ami ezikhathini ezidlule. Ngakho-ke qondani ukuthi ngiyembule imfihlakalo enkulu kaNkulunkulu, futhi abaprofethi basendulo bacwaninga ngokukhuthala futhi babuza ngale mfihlakalo ababeyazi, kodwa kwakunganikezwanga bona ukuba bayazi. Ngifisa sengathi ningaqonda ukuthi nakuba ikhava yamaBhayibheli enu ithi “Ngcwele,” akungcwele ngokwawo, ngoba kuyikhava nje yencwadi. Okwenza iBhayibheli libe ngcwele ukuthi izincwadi abazibophele ziqukethe uMbhalo oNgcwele othunyelwe ngumoya ebantwaneni boPhezukonke, othunyelwe nguYahweh, uNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade, ngoba akekho onjengaye. Ngakho-ke yizwani amazwi ami angcwele ngawo wonke umphefumulo wenu! Ngoba uPetru wathini ngani, bantwana bami? Lolusindiso abaprofethi abaphenya ngalo, futhi bacwaninga ngokukhuthala, abaprofetha ngomusa owawuzokuza kini, bephenya ukuthi yisikhathi sini, noma isikhathi esinjani uMoya kaKristu owawukubo wawusho, lapho wawufakaza kusengaphambili izinhlupheko zikaKristu, nenkazimulo eyayizolandela.

Bantwana bami, ngokungazi niphila isibambiso senkazimulo ezolandela, futhi ifa lenu eligcwele selizokwembulwa kini kimi, uKristu Jesu, ngoba maduze nizohlwithwa futhi nikhazinyuliswe ngokuphelele. Ngakho iyini le mfihlakalo? Futhi niyitholaphi? Ngizonitshela, ngoba ngiyiNkosi uJesu Kristu, owelusa umhlambi, futhi nginguMyeni woMlobokazi wami, futhi niphila imfihlakalo enkulu kaNkulunkulu, nakuba abaningi benu behamba ebumnyameni kulolu daba. Ningesabi, ngizoninika ukuqonda, futhi kwabahlakaniphile nabathobekile ngizonika ukuhlakanipha. Kodwa kulabo phakathi kwenu abazowachitha amazwi ami, nikwenza lokho niphikisana nami; futhi ningaba ngakimi noma niphikisane nami, akukho okuphakathi nendawo. Ngoba ngineluleka nonke ukuba nifunde kimi, nizwe amazwi ami futhi ningalenqabi izwi lami, ngoba ngininike ikusasa nenjongo kimi. NginguMalusi omuhle: uMalusi omuhle unikela ngokuphila kwakhe ngenxa yezimvu, futhi izimvu zami ziyalizwa izwi lami, ngakho yekani ukulandela abaqashiwe abazenza mina!

Bhekani! Umoya uthi: Kunezimfihlakalo eziningana ngaphakathi kweMfihlakalo Enkulu kaNkulunkulu! Ngizoqala ngeMfihlakalo Enkulu, bese ngikhuluma ngezinye izimfihlakalo ezikuyo. Ngaphambi kokuba siqhubekele phambili, susani inkungu yomqondo wezinkathi ezihlukene ezibukweni zenu futhi nivule amehlo enu ukuze nibone: Amavangeli njengoba niwazi, imisebenzi kaMathewu, uMarku, uLuka, noJohane, abalulekile kubo bonke. Lezi zincwadi zibonisa ukugcwaliseka kwami komthetho ngokugcwele, kungashiywanga lutho lungenziwanga. Ziyangembula ekuzeni kwami kwa-Israyeli. Ngokulalela amavangeli amane njengoba aziwa, nifunda ukuhamba njengoba ngahamba, ngoba niyalwe kanjalo. Nokho, akuwona wodwa amavangeli; cha, akuzona zodwa izindaba ezinhle eziphathelene nami.

Ngoba nganika izindaba ezinhle zeMfihlakalo Enkulu kaNkulunkulu kuMphostoli uPawulu ezincwadini zakhe eziya ebandleni, ezithunyelwe yimi kuye ukuze nizembulelwe nina. Ingabe nizohleka usulu ngalo mbono? Ingabe nicabanga ukuthi ngishumayela elinye ivangeli nokuthi ngiyizwi lamanga? Ingabe senijwayele ukulandela amazwi abaqashiwe kangangokuthi anisalazi izwi lami? Kulabo abangahleka usulu, kufanele nilalele izwi labaseGalathiya lapho uPawulu ethi: Kodwa ngiyanitshela, bazalwane, ukuthi iVangeli elashunyayelwa yimi, aliveli kumuntu. Ngoba angilamukelanga kumuntu, futhi angilifundiswanga, kodwa ngesambulo sikaJesu Kristu. UPawulu wakhuluma ngokwethembeka iqiniso engamnika lona, kodwa abaningi benu bayawenqaba amazwi engamnika wona, baze banciphise namazwi ngezisho ezinjengokuthi: Oh, lokho kwabhalwa nje uPawulu, noma, lezo yizincwadi zikaPawulu nje; akuwona amazwi kaKristu ngoba awabhalwanga ngokubomvu! Siphukuphuku! Unephutha; vula amehlo akho ekukhanyeni naseqinisweni lami! Nganika uPawulu ivangeli lemfihlakalo. Ingabe niyiziphukuphuku kangangokuthi nikholelwa ukuthi kunemisebenzi emine kuphela emifushane equkethe iVangeli lami?

Ngicela nizwe amazwi kaKristu Jesu, bantwana bami, ngoba ngizofundisa abazimisele. Ngoba inkulu iMfihlakalo yobuNkulunkulu kanye nevangeli elikuyo, ngoba ngamemezela izindaba ezinhle zemfihlakalo. Kodwa ngeke nginishiye nezwi lofakazi oyedwa kuphela; wonke amazwi mawaqiniswe ngomlomo wofakazi ababili noma abathathu. Ngoba incwadi engayithumela kwabaseRoma ingathanda ukukhuluma nani: Manje kuye onamandla okunimisa ngokwevangeli lami, nangokushumayelwa kukaJesu Kristu, ngokwesambulo semfihlakalo, eyayigcinwe iyimfihlo kusukela ekuqaleni komhlaba. Izindaba ezinhle zikaPawulu ziyisambulo semfihlakalo eyayigcinwe iyimfihlo kusukela ekuqaleni komhlaba, njengoba noMathewu, noMarku, noLuka, noJohane beyizindaba ezinhle zokuza kwami kokuqala kwa-Israyeli.

NginguNkosi othembekileyo futhi amazwi ami ayiqiniso, ngakho ngiboleke indlebe yakho futhi ikakhulukazi inhliziyo yakho, ngoba ngenhliziyo umuntu ukholwa kuze kube sekulungeni. Ngicela ukuba nibe nokholo olungangohlamvu lwesinaphi, ngoba leyo mbewu iyokhula kakhulu kini ngokunakekelwa okufanele. Ngoba uPawulu wathini kwabaseKorinte: Kepha sikhuluma ukuhlakanipha kukaNkulunkulu okuyimfihlakalo, ukuhlakanipha okufihliweyo uNkulunkulu akumisa ngaphambi kwezikhathi, kube yinkazimulo yethu. Okungazange kwaziwe yilowo nalowo kubabusi balelizwe, ngokuba uma bebekwazi, bebengayikumbethela iNkosi yenkazimulo. Kubhalwe ngokusobala, bantwana bami, futhi akudingi ncazelo, kodwa ngizochaza kancane.

Niyawaqonda yini lawa mazwi, bantwana bami? Niyakuqonda yini ukuthi niyigugu kangakanani, njengoba uNkulunkulu wayenibhekile kusukela ngaphambi kokudalwa komhlaba, futhi njengoba engunaphakade, nani ninjalo, ngoba benilokhu niphambili emqondweni wakhe kusukela ngaphambi kwesikhathi, futhi uniphile ukuphila okuphakade kimi, uKristu Jesu? Izikhathi zenu nezimpilo zenu zimisiwe nguYahweh, uNkulunkulu omkhulu futhi omuhle, okukhanya kwakhe kungekho nyama engakubona. Ukuba uSathane wayewazi amacebo kaBaba, wayengeke angibethelwe, ngoba ekulaleleni kwami kwaze kwaba sekufeni nasekuvukeni kwami kwapheleliswa amandla amnqobayo futhi uchithekile ngokuphelele. Wayengeke alifune ibandla elinamandla elinikwe amandla ngumoya wami futhi engenza ngalo imisebenzi emikhulu ngezandla zabo.

Lokho kuyazwakala yini kini? Yekani ukukhathazeka kwengqondo ngoba izithunywa zami zinitshela into eningafuni ukuyizwa, futhi nqobani ukuzidla kwenu, ngoba ngizohola abathobekileyo kuphela futhi ngiyomelana nabazidlayo. Yizwani amazwi ami futhi niwavumele angene ekujuleni kwemiphefumulo yenu; ngoba nginidalile kimi, futhi ninamandla, futhi niyisimangaliso; ngoba nihlanziwe ngegazi lami, lona kanye igazi leWundlu. Ngoba ngiyinhloko yoMzimba wami owenza imisebenzi eminingi emikhulu kubantu bami engisababiza, kuphela bambelelani eNkosini yenu, uYeshua HaMashiach, ngenhliziyo eyodwa nangobunye benjongo, ngoba ngiyiNkosi yamakhosi.

Ufakazi othembekileyo, nozalwa kuqala kwabafileyo uthi kwabathembekileyo nabakhethiweyo bakhe! Kwakukhona ibandla lasendulo e-Efesu, futhi kubo kwembulwa ngokugcwele iMfihlakalo Enkulu kaNkulunkulu encwadini eyathunyelwa nguPawulu evela kimi, ngoba wayengazikhulumeli yena. Ngizochaza kancane, kodwa yazini ukuthi ngilindele ukulalela kwenu ngokukholwa kimi, uJesu waseNazaretha, futhi kulowo onikwe okuningi kulindelwe okuningi. Ngaphambi kokuqhubeka, ngifuna nicabangele okulandelayo okuvela eKolose, ngoba ngayithumela nencwadi kwabaseKolose: Ngoba nafa, nokuphila kwenu kufihliwe noKristu kuNkulunkulu. Lapho uKristu, ongukuphila kwethu, eyobonakala, khona-ke nani niyobonakala naye ekukhazimuleni.

Bantwana bami, nafa lapho ngifa, futhi nizifela nsuku zonke ngokubala imvelo yomuntu omdala njengefileyo; ngoba nabethelwa nami, futhi ayikho enye indawo emibhalweni lapho niyothola khona isibuko sobuyena benu beqiniso, ngoba ayikho enye ingxenye yamavangeli eyembulayo ngaphandle kwevangeli leMfihlakalo Enkulu engayembulela iBandla lami. Nakuba nibona lufifi njengasesibukweni, lokho sekuzoshintsha maduzane, futhi niyokwazi njengoba nani naziwa. Zigxiliseni eBhayibhelini leMfihlakalo Enkulu kaNkulunkulu, ngoba ibandla lami laseLawodikeya lilahlekelwe yizimfihlakalo zami, ngoba iningi lenu anivamile ukufunda izincwadi zami eziya eBandleni ngoba abelusi abaphambukayo banidukisele kwenye indawo nifuna amazwi abomvu. Bantwana bami, ngiyazi ukuthi niyangithanda, ngakho-ke ngithandeni ngokwazi, ningahambi ngenkohliso.

Ngakho-ke ngiyanibuza, ithini incwadi eya kwabase-Efesu? Ngoba bhekani, imfihlakalo enkulu ikhona! Esesazisile imfihlakalo yentando yakhe, ngokokuthokoza kwakhe okuhle, ayekumisile kuye, Ukuthi ekuhleleni kokugcwala kwezikhathi, ahlanganise ndawonye zonke izinto kuKristu, kokubili okusezulwini, nokusemhlabeni, kuye kanye. Nginazisile imfihlakalo yentando kaYahweh, ayeyifihlile kuye, ngoba akadimeli. Ningacabangi ngencazelo yomgunyathi enayifunda kubaprofethi bamanga kumanothi abo angenamsebenzi aphathelene nezikhathi zokuphatha, ngoba uSathane uyicacisa kabusha ngobuqili imiqondo yemibhalo ukuze ivumelane nentando yakhe namacebo akhe angaphumeleli.

Le mfihlakalo yanikezwa uPawulu, futhi wayengumphathi owaphathiswa ukuyabela iBandla lami, nani futhi uma ninezindlebe zokuzwa namehlo okubona. Ngoba ivangeli leMfihlakalo Enkulu lanikezwa uPawulu, futhi ngamthuma endleleni eya eDamaseku. Ekugcwaleni kwezikhathi, uBaba uyohlanganisa ndawonye zonke izinto ezisezulwini nezisemhlabeni kimi, uKristu Jesu. Ngeke kusaba khona ukwahlukana, ngeke kusaba khona ukwehlukana phakathi kukaNkulunkulu nabantwana bakhe, ngeke kusaba khona ubutha phakathi komJuda noWezizwe, ngoba nonke nizelwe ngokusha futhi niyisidalwa esisha kimi, uKristu Jesu. Ingabe lawa mazwi mahle kakhulu kini? Ingabe amazwi ami enza niphikisane nawo ngoba nikholwa incazelo yabelusi abaphambukayo ngawo?

Fundani kimi ngokuthobeka ukuze niqonde. Futhi, kwabase-Efesu uPawulu wabhala amazwi engamnika wona: Ngenxa yalesi sizathu mina Pawulu, isiboshwa sikaJesu Kristu ngenxa yenu nina Bezizwe, Uma nizwile ngokuphathiswa komusa kaNkulunkulu, engakuphiwe mina ngani: Ukuthi ngesambulo wangazisa imfihlakalo. Futhi, ningakhubeki ngamazwi abaprofethi bamanga, njengoba ukuphathiswa komusa kaNkulunkulu kwakuwukukhonza kwakhe eBandleni, ngoba yena (nabanye) bakhethwa njengabaphathi bezimfihlakalo zikaNkulunkulu.

Futhi futhi: Okwathi kwezinye izikhathi kungaziswanga emadodaneni abantu, njengoba manje sekwembulwe kubaPhostoli bakhe abangcwele nabaProfethi ngoMoya, Ukuthi abaZizwe babe yizindlalifa kanye nabo, nabomzimba munye, nabahlanganyeli besithembiso sakhe kuKristu, ngeVangeli. Yebo, fundani kahle futhi niphinde nifunde: abaZizwe abakholwa egameni lami elingcwele, uJesu Kristu, bayizindlalifa kanye nabo futhi bangomzimba munye nalabo bakwa-Israyeli abangenze iNkosi, ngoba akukho mehluko kimi.

Futhi futhi: Imfihlakalo efihliweyo kusukela ezikhathini, nasezizukulwaneni, kodwa manje isibonakalisiwe kwabangcwele bakhe, Okwathi kubo uNkulunkulu wathanda ukubazisa ukuthi buyini ubukhazikhazi benkazimulo yale mfihlakalo phakathi kwabezizwe, onguKristu kini, ithemba lenkazimulo. Yebo, nizelwe ngokusha ngomoya wami, futhi umoya wami uhlala unomphela kubantwana bami, ngoba ungumoya wobundodana, inkokhelo yefa lenu, futhi lowo moya uhlanganisa wonke amakhono enginiphile wona, ngoba ngiphile izipho kubantu. Nenziwe ngomfanekiso wami, futhi nidalwe kabusha kimi; ngoba uBaba unizele kimi, ngoba ngingumoya ophilisayo.

UKristu Jesu owenyuka uthi: Bantwanyana bami, duduzezwani kakhulu kimi, ngoba insindiso yami, uma isizuziwe ngokungenza iNkosi yenu ngokukholwa nokuphenduka, ayinakulahleka noma ichithwe; ngoba ngubani kini ongaxazulula ukudalwa kwemvelo kwezici zenu kubantwana benu, nakuba bengalahlekelwa yifa uma benibalahla. Insindiso isipho esinikezwa nguBaba wami ngami, uYeshua HaMashiach, futhi izipho nobizo lukaNkulunkulu azinakuzisola. Manje, ngizoninika imvuthuluka yokulandela ohambweni lwenu lokuya ezimfihlakalweni eziqukethwe yiMfihlakalo Enkulu kaNkulunkulu, ngakho-ke fundisisani ukuze nizibonakalise ningabaswelekayo kuNkulunkulu, isisebenzi seZwi, nihlukanisa kahle izwi leqiniso, ukuze ningabi namahloni phambi kwami ekufikeni kwami.

Kukhona imfihlakalo kwabaseRoma, ecabangela ubumpumputhe bengxenye ka-Israyeli. Kukhona imfihlakalo yokuhlangana komlobokazi wami, abanye abakubiza ngokuthi ukuhlwitshwa, futhi lokho kuseKorinte (nakwezinye izindawo); ngoba ngomzuzwana, ngokucwayiza kweso, icilongo lokugcina liyokhala, futhi ukukhala kwalo kuphezu kwenu. Bese kuba khona imfihlakalo yendoda nomfazi beba nyamanye, futhi lokhu kuyimfihlakalo enkulu: kodwa ngikhuluma ngoKristu nebandla, futhi lokho ningakuthola kwabase-Efesu. Ngoba ngizosusa ubumpumputhe kwa-Israyeli lapho bephendulela izinhliziyo zabo kimi. Ngoba ngiyohlangana noMlobokazi wami kimi, ngoba nginguMkhwenyana. Ngingulowo eniyoshada naye, ukuze sibe munye phakade kimi nakuBaba wami, ngoba uNkulunkulu uyikho konke kukho konke. Inhliziyo yami iphumule kini, bantwana bami, futhi maduzane nizongibona ubuso nobuso; futhi ningadideki, nginibizela kimi, zintombi zami ezihlakaniphile, futhi ngiyeza ngani maduzane kakhulu, futhi ngilangazelela ukunibona nonke lapho siyoba ubuso nobuso, futhi niyokwazi, njengoba naziwa.

Ngingulowo onibekele uphawu lokuhlengwa ngokugcwele emandleni omoya wami ongcwele. Ngingukugcwaliseka komthetho.
