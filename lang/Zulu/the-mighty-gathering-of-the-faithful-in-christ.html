UMbuthano Omkhulu Wabakholwayo KuKristu

Yizwani amazwi eNkosi yenu yaphakade, bandla lami, umzimba wami, yebo nomakoti wami, bese nithobisa izindlebe zenu ngokulalela amazwi kaYeshua HaMashiach. Yizwani izwi lami nize eNkosini yenu ngezinhliziyo ezilungele ukwamukela amazwi ami, ukuze induduzo ifike phezu kwenu ngokugcwele, ngoba izikhathi zokududuzwa nokuhlengwa okuphelele ziphezu kwenu zithandwa zami, ngalokho-ke lungani!

Kunjalo-ke, mina Jesu waseNazaretha, yena futhi onguYeshua HaMashiach ngolimi lwesiHeberu, ngiza ukuzonilungisa zithandwa zami ngoba asisekho isikhathi futhi ukubuthwa kwabakholwayo kuKristu sekuzoqala ngokuzuma nangokungalindelekile manje. Ngakho-ke ningesabi zithandwa zami, niqonde ukuthi izahlulelo zalabo abenqabile izixwayiso zami akuzona ezenu, futhi niyosindiswa ngokuphelele olakeni oluzayo! Ngiqinisile, ngiqinisile, ngoba nikholwe futhi namukela insindiso nokuthethelelwa kwezono ekuzalweni okusha ngokweSivumelwano sami Esisha ngezikhathi zomusa nesihe, nisindisiwe nakanjani olakeni oluzayo!

Nokho, kunjalo, ukuthi imiphefumulo eminingi engazinzile nengaqotho ekhuluma amanga ngokuzenzisa futhi ihlanekezela amazwi ami kube ngesahlulelo nokubhujiswa kwayo, yona ethi ngobuqili nangokuzidla kwenyama iyona ingamandla amakhulu kaYahweh, idukise bonke abezwayo nabalalela amanga ayo akhulunywa ezinhliziyweni ezikhohlisayo. Ngalokho, qondani izithembiso zami engazembulela uPawulu lapho ngimfundisa Imfihlakalo Enkulu kaNkulunkulu eyembulwe ngokuphelele ezincwadini zakhe, yebo imfihlakalo iningi lenu elingakayiqondi. Noma kunjalo, wafakaza futhi waqinisekisa ngolaka lukaYahweh wathi: Kepha uNkulunkulu ubonakalisa uthando lwakhe kithina, ngokuthi lapho siseyizoni, uKristu wasifela. Kakhulu-ke manje, njengoba sesilungisisiwe ngegazi lakhe, siyosindiswa olakeni ngaye. Ngoba uma siseyizitha, sabuyisaniswa noNkulunkulu ngokufa kweNdodana yakhe, kakhulu-ke sesibuyisaniwe, siyosindiswa ngokuphila kwayo!

O, nina bokukholwa okuncane, ngiyakubekezelela kuze kube nini, zithandwa zami? Ngakho ngiza ukunipha induduzo ngoba angisoze nganishiya noma nganilahla, nonke nina enikuKristu, futhi ngempela ngiyobekezelelana nani kuze kube phakade ngoba niyoba nami kuze kube phakade futhi nibe njengami kuze kube phakade. Sela.

Ngakho-ke, qondani ukuthi bonke abaphila ngokukaNkulunkulu bayohlushwa ngandlela thize, futhi lelo bhubesi elingenamazinyo lisazulazula lifuna elingamshwabadela, ngakho-ke melananani nalo ngokuphelele bese liyabaleka. Nokho, amasango esihogo ngeke alehlule iBandla lami noma abakholwayo eBandleni lami abalalela izwi lami nabangibeka amandla ngomoya wami wobuNdodana odalwe ngaphakathi kwabo bonke abangenze iNkosi ngenhliziyo egcwele ukholo, futhi nibekiwe uphawu lweNkosi Engunaphakade Yokulunga, yebo uYahweh Omkhulu Nginguye, futhi lolo phawu alunakwephulwa futhi aninakuhlwithwa ezandleni zami, noma beqamba amanga!

Ngakho-ke, qondani ukuthi nisindisiwe olakeni, futhi nisindisiwe kulo lonke ulaka. Ngakho qondani ukuthi ulaka lweWundlu aluhloselwe abalalelayo, kodwa luhloselwe ukuqala isahlulelo sokugcina kwabawileyo nabo bonke abahlangana ngokugcwele nabo noma abasala nabo, futhi luhloselwe ukuletha insindiso yokugcina kwabangalaleli nenkani abayolalela iVangeli Laphakade ngezikhathi zabo zosizi nenhlekelele ngoba bazishaya indiva izixwayiso zami ngezikhathi zesixwayiso esesiphelile manje, futhi esikhundleni sezixwayiso, manje sekumi izimemezelo!! Nokho, bonke abakholwayo kuKristu, yebo bonke labo abamukelwe ngokugcwele njengamaDodana aphakade kaYahweh futhi manje abaxhunywe esihlahleni somnqumo semvelo seSizwe sikaNkulunkulu sase-Israyeli lapho kuhlala khona konke ukugcwala abekho ngaphansi kolaka lweWundlu, noma ulaka lukaYahweh, Sela!

Ngoba ulaka lweWundlu luza kuqala futhi luyowela kanzima kakhulu kwabangalaleli nabangathembekile bendlu kaNkulunkulu, kungakhathaliseki ukuthi bangabakwa-Israyeli noma abezizwe. Ngoba lokhu kungumusa onikezwe ngesithembiso sezivumelwano zasendulo osathenjisiwe futhi obekelwe ukugcwaliseka kwaso sonke isikhathi naphakade naphakade eSivumelwaneni sami Esisha sokugcwaliseka okuphelele! Ulaka lweWundlu luza lapho ngithukulula izimpawu emqulwini wami wefa, yebo itayitela lomhlaba kanye nefa lami elikhulu engilinikwe ezandleni zami ezinamandla nguYahweh uqobo ukuze ngihlanganyele nabanqobi bami ngoba kuzo zonke izinto nginqobile! Ofakazi ababili bayovuka ngesikhathi sabo futhi benze izimangaliso ezinkulu nezinamandla, futhi bayokwembula izambulo eziningi, futhi bayofakaza ngobufakazi obuningi basendulo ngezikhathi zabo ezimisiwe zokwembula, futhi bayoyala izinhlupho eziningi, izimangaliso, izibonakaliso, nezimangaliso ngamazwi nemiyalo yezinhliziyo zabo ezigcwele ukholo nemilomo yabo egcwele amandla! Sela.

Futhi, ngalesi sikhathi kunikezwe udrago, isilo sasolwandle, nomprofethi wamanga ukuba balwe nabangcwele futhi babanqobe. Nokho, labo abangakuthandi ukuphila kwabo kuze kube sekupheleni nabo bonke abakhuleka egameni likaJesu kuze kube sekupheleni kwabo bayothola insindiso yaphakade nefa elikhulu laphakade elinesisindo esingafanele ukuqhathaniswa nokuhlupheka kwabo abayokukhuthazelela ngenxa yegama lami. Futhi mina, Yeshua HaMashiach, ngiyohola abangu-144,000 bami abanamandla ukuze ngisindise uJakobe, yebo leyo nsali enamandla eyongamukela njengoMesiya wayo, yebo bonke abakwa-Israyeli abangabohlu lwegazi lwesithembiso futhi abayohlukaniswa futhi bathathwe bephila bayiswe ehlane endaweni abayilungiselelwe nguYahweh lapho beyohlala khona befihliwe ngokuphepha osizini olukhulu kunawo wonke olwake lwehlela umhlaba, futhi ngempela izikhathi zezinkathazo ngeke ziphinde zehlele umhlaba, ngoba ushilo kanjalo uYahweh, Omkhulu Nginguye!

Ngakho-ke, qondani amazwi ami futhi nilalele izwi lami zithandwa zami, niqonde ukuthi nisindisiwe olakeni oluzayo, nakuba nisemhlabeni nonke futhi nisalwa izimpi eziningi ezilwiwayo kulezi zikhathi zokugcina zempi yembewu kaGenesise 3:15 esaqhubeka nanamuhla phakathi kukaYahweh, namazulu, nosathane abathumbe abantu ababi emhlabeni ukuba benze intando yabo ngothando lwemali namanga amaningi akhohlisayo nathembisayo agcina eholela echibini lomlilo lapho isibungu sabo singafi khona. Noma kunjalo, ngibanqobile futhi zonke izimpi ziyophela ngamandla amakhulu nesandla esinamandla seNgonyama yesizwe sakwaJuda, lapho ngiza ngokulunga ukuze ngahlulele futhi ngilwe! Sela.

Yebo, nisindisiwe olakeni futhi amasango esihogo ngeke anehlule! Ngakho-ke, zinikeleleni kuKristu Jesu iNkosi yenu yaphakade, yebo yena onihlengile esandleni sesitha, yebo zinikeleleni eNkosini yenu ngoba izikhathi zokwethulwa kwenu ezulwini sezisondele. Yebo isikhathi sesithembiso saphakade esifihliwe enhliziyweni kaYahweh esesembuliwe ngokugcwele, yebo isikhathi lapho isithembiso saphakade nokulindela kuhlangana nokugcwaliseka nokwethulwa ngoba, mina, Kristu Jesu, iNkosi yenu yaphakade yokulunga ngiyokwethula abakholwayo kuKristu kuYahweh, Omkhulu Nginguye, esigodlweni sakhe sobukhosi ezulwini lamazulu.

Futhi NGIYOKWETHULA abalalelayo, bengenabala noma isici, noma ibala, noma yini enjalo kodwa ngiyonembathisa nonke ngelineni elimhlophe elicolekileyo nelingenabala futhi kanjalo izindlu zenu ziyokuma kuze kube phakade, ngoba izingubo zokugqoka, nezingubo zokwembatha, nezindlu konke kubhekise emizimbeni yenu evusiweyo nepheleleyo eniyozithola ngokushesha nimi kuyo kuze kube phakade lapho ngidala kabusha abakholwayo kuKristu ngokuphazima kweso kulokho kumemeza okukhulu kweNgelosi Eyinhloko nalelo cilongo elimnandi lokuphelelisa liyokhala, liqede ngokusemthethweni izikhathi zami zoMusa nesihe, okuyophawula ukuphela okuphelele kwezikhathi zabezizwe, nakuba, bonke abakhuleka egameni likaJesu waseNazaretha kuze kube sekupheleni bayosindiswa ngokuphelele.

Kanjalo-ke ngiza ukuzodalula labo abanikhathazayo, yebo bonke labo abaningi benu abasanaka amazwi abo anomdlavuza, yebo amazwi amanga anikezwa ngenkohliso ezinhliziyweni ezimbi ezibuswa yizinkanuko zenyama yabo. Yebo labo abahlanekezela amazwi amsulwa evangeli lami ngezinganekwane nezindaba ezingcwele ezingamanga, ezihloselwe ngokuphelele ukubhujiswa kwabo bonke abangakholwa la mazwi angenakukholwa. Zithandwa zami, gxilisani ukunaka kwenu okuphelele kimi naseZwini lami ngoba ngiyisiqiniseko sezikhathi zenu namandla ensindiso, futhi ngubani ongamelana neNgonyama yesizwe sakwaJuda!?

Bhekani! Ngivikele amazwi ami ahlanzekile ohlwini lwemibhalo yaseByzantium futhi mina Yeshua HaMashiach, kanye no-Adonai Yahweh, sembule amazwi ethu eVangelini Laphakade, yebo kuleyo Ncwajana Yokulunga ephinde yasungula amaqiniso amaningi phakathi kwabakholwayo kuKristu, ngakho-ke, qhathanisani ngokukhuthala wonke amazwi avela kunoma yimuphi umuntu othi ukhulumela uYeshua HaMashiach, noma uYahweh, Omkhulu Nginguye namazwi ami ahlanzekile aqukethwe emibhalweni futhi nifunde ukuze nizibonakalise nifanelekile!

Ngoba noma isiphi isiprofetho esake sanikezwa nguYeshua HaMashiach noma u-Adonai Yahweh, ngeke siphikisane namaqiniso asunguliwe ayembulwe ezikhathini zasendulo noma ezikhathini zamanje. Lahla amanga abo futhi uqonde ukuthi abakholwayo kuKristu ngeke babekezelele ubumnyama obukhulu njengezinhlupho zaseGibhithe, futhi ngeke babe khona emhlabeni lapho kuvulwa kwalasha, futhi iBandla lami ngeke linqotshwe ngamasango esihogo, ukuze licashe emizini yalo njengamagwala angenamandla phezu kobubi! Qondani, inhlamba yabo bonke abasho lezi zinto ngeke ihambe ingavuzwanga kubo bonke abenqaba ukuphenduka ebuwuleni obunjalo.

Qonda futhi, abangu-144,000 bakhethwe nguYahweh ngaphambi kwezisekelo zomhlaba ngesikhathi esinjengalesi. Bakhandwe osizini futhi bonke banamakhono ahlukile namandla. Bayothathwa ekuhlwithweni kwabakholwayo kodwa babekelwe eceleni izinhloso zikaYeshua HaMashiach futhi bayobekwa uphawu ngokufanele emabunzini abo emazulwini. Bafika kamuva emhlabeni ngesikhathi sosizi olukhulu ukuze baholele u-Israyeli ekuphepheni. Kodwa kuqala bayiswa ukuyoshada noMyeni wabo ngoba bangumakoti wami, ababiziweyo, abakhethiweyo, abathembekileyo, nabeqiniso. Yebo labo mina, Kristu Jesu, engibenze baba msulwa njengentombi egazini lami lokuhlawulela. Ngakho-ke, noma ubani okhuluma okuphambene nala mazwi akangazi, futhi akakhulumi egameni lami! Sela!

Ngakho-ke qonda, ukuthi amasango esihogo ngeke anqobe abakholwayo kuKristu abayiBandla lami leqiniso ngoba kulotshiwe futhi kwathenjiswa kanjalo endulo, futhi kusabhalwe futhi kwathenjiswa ngezikhathi zamanje, ngoba amazwi kaYahweh noYeshua HaMashiach angamazwi ahlanzekile njengesiliva, elihlolwe esithandweni somlilo somhlaba, elicwengisiswe kasikhombisa. Yebo, abawileyo abanamandla phezu kwabakholwayo kuKristu futhi ngiyobangela ukuba abakholwayo bami banyathele ngaphansi kwezinyawo noma iyiphi ingxenye yombuso wezitha, banyathele ngisho nalabo abangafuna ukumisa okungenakuvinjelwa.

Ngakho-ke, ngiyaniyala ukuba nibe nokholo eNkosini yenu yaphakade futhi nivumele uNkulunkulu waphakade wokulunga anifake amandla angaphakathi ebusweni beNkosi yenu, uKristu Jesu! Hamba ngokholo olukhulu kuYeshuah HaMashiach, onguJesu waseNazaretha ngoba nginike amandla ukuze ninyathele ngaphansi kwezinyawo zonke izici zombuso wezitha. Ngakho-ke, qondani, ukuthi nezingelosi eziwileyo zingaphansi kwezinyawo zami ngokwehlulwa okuphelele, futhi uma ungahlangana noSathane uqobo lwakhe ubungadinga kuphela ukuphendula njengoba uMikayeli aphendula lapho ephikisana ngesidumbu sikaMose, ethi ngokholo olunamandla, INkosi ikukhuze Sathane! Futhi ngiyokhuza, futhi ngibophe noma yibaphi kwabawileyo abangazama ukunilimaza noma ukunivimba ekwenzeni intando yalowo onithumile, uma niqhubeka nokuhamba ngokwethembeka okholweni njengoba nikhuluma amazwi engizowapha okholweni.

Ngakho-ke ngiyaniyala zithandwa zami ukuba nizilungiselele, ngoba ukuhamba kwenu sekuseduze! Futhi ngiza ukuzonixwayisa ukuba nihlale nihlukene neBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo kuzo zonke izinto nangezindlela eziningi njengoba ngizonihola ukuze ningcweliswe futhi nihlukaniswe. Lalelani amazwi eVangeli Laphakade futhi nivumele iqiniso lami lonke elembuthwe kulezi zikhathi zokwembulwa libe yisibani ezinyaweni zenu ukuze indlela yenu ikhanyiselwe, ngoba kunendlela eyodwa kuphela, leyo ndlela emincane neluqotho eholela ekuphileni okuphakade kuKristu Jesu iNkosi yenu yaphakade!

Zilungiseleleni, makoti wami, nithenge amafutha kimi ukuze izibani zenu zigcwale! Amafutha ami atholakala emazwini ami sithandwa sami, yebo kulawo mazwi eniwafundayo manje futhi ngininike inqwaba yamazwi okududuza, nokugcina, nokuniqondisa kukho konke ukulunga lapho lowo omubi enganithinti khona!

Zilungiseleleni, zithandwa zami, ngoba kuhlale kuyisikhathi sokunquma izintambo zenu, futhi niyozinquma izintambo zenu ngokuphenduka ezonweni futhi nithele izithelo zomoya njengoba ninqoba imisebenzi yenyama ngomoya wami ongcwele ozelwe ngaphakathi kini ngokugcwele, futhi ongasoze wasuswa uma usuzimpande ezinhliziyweni zabantu ngobungcwele bonke nokulunga. Gcinani izinhliziyo zenu zithambile othandweni lwami nemiyalo yami, ukuze nigcinwe niphephile ngokugcwele futhi nivikelekile kukho konke okubi entendeni yezandla zami zaphakade.

Zilungiseleleni, O' Bakholwayo kuKristu, niqonde ukusondela kwehora lokugcina lapho ukubuthwa kwabakholwayo kuKristu kwenzeka, kunihlwitha nonke nikuse ekuphepheni okuphelele nasemvuzweni wamazulu osekuzogubuzela umhlaba ekubonakaleni kwami okukhazimulayo. Njengoba inkazimulo yami ikhanya njengombani ovela empumalanga uye entshonalanga, abaningi benu bayobona ukukhanya kokufika kwami.

Khona-ke, ngiqinisile, ngiqinisile, lapho ukumemeza okukhulu kweNgelosi Eyinhloko kugqashuka emhlabeni wonke, njengoba lokho kuqhuma kwecilongo elinamandla kumenyezelwa emhlabeni wonke, abafileyo kuKristu bayozalwa emhlabeni ngamandla amakhulu; khona-ke nina eniphilayo nenisasele niyohlwithwa kanye nabo ukuze nihlangabeze iNkosi yenu yaphakade yokulunga emoyeni, futhi ngokushesha niyozithola nembethe inkazimulo ephelele nemvelo ekhanyayo kakhulu nekhazimulayo, nimi ezulwini lamazulu. Niyobona ngokugcwele, niyozwa ngokugcwele, niyokwazi njengoba naziwa, futhi abaningi benu bayophinde bahlangane nabathandiweyo benu futhi bonke bayojabula!

Nina eniphilayo nenisasele niyoyiqonda isigameko enizithola senigubuzela ngokushesha futhi niyozwa amazwi athi "Yenyukelani Lapha!" futhi niyozizwa nidonswa ngamandla futhi ngokushesha phezulu ekukhanyeni kwenkazimulo yami futhi imihelo iyophela njengoba abantwana baphakade bakaYahweh bezelwa emazulwini lapho beyokuma khona ngenkazimulo enkulu nomvuzo waphakade ngokokwethembeka kwabo.

Yimina, Kristu Jesu, iNkosi yaphakade yokulunga engithumele la mazwi ngesithunywa sami esinamandla nenceku yami, futhi ngesithunywa sami esinamandla nenceku yami amazwi ami athunyelwe, ukuze abakholwayo kuKristu balunge ngokugcwele futhi balindele ukusondela koMyeni wabo, osesondela kumakoti wakhe! Sela.

Zilungiseleleni, O' Bakholwayo kuKristu, ngoba ihora lokuhlengwa kwenu okuphelele selizokuthatha ngokuzuma nangokungalindelekile likususe kuzo zonke izinkathazo eziyowela emhlabeni ngesahlulelo esikhulu nesesabekayo ngoba izikhathi zesahlulelo zifikile futhi kufanele zigcwaliswe, kuphela nihlukanise ngokuphelele ezintweni zonke ngezwi nangesenzo kwabangakholwayo ukuze ningahlanganyeli ezahlulelweni zabo, futhi ningahlanganyeli ezinhluphekweni zabo.

Zilungiseleleni, Makoti wami, futhi nize ngokuphelele kimi ngokuphenduka okuphelele futhi nisuke kubo bonke ububi ukuze izibani zenu zigcwale nezintambo zenu zinqunywe futhi ukuze nilungele uMyeni wenu osondele ngokuqinisekile futhi ihora lohambo lwami lisondela ekupheleni! Sela!

Ebandleni lami, emzimbeni wami, ngisho nakumakoti wami, ngithi izikhathi zenu zokuhlengwa okuphelele zifikile, ngakho-ke, lindelani mina futhi nibe nilungile futhi nibambelele ebufakazini engibunikile ngoba lobu bufakazi buyonisekela futhi bunigcine kulowo omubi oza ngokumelene nani ngempela ngenzondo enkulu nolaka olungahloselwe nina. Qondani, ukuthi ngiyakhuza futhi ngiyabavimba abawileyo ngaphezu kwalokho enikuqaphelayo futhi bavinjelwe kuze kususwe umvimbeli wami. Ngakho-ke, zigcineni nihlukene nezixuku ezahlulelwe futhi ningahlanganyeli emibuthwanweni yazo ngoba sezivele ziletha isiphithiphithi nembubhiso kulabo abayonikezwa ngokugcwele esahlulelweni ngokuzuma njengoba umvimbeli wami esusiwe. Ngakho-ke, ningazesabi izinto abazishoyo, futhi ningazesabi izinto abazenzayo futhi ningazesabi izinto abazihlelayo nabazidlayo ukuthi bayozenza ngokuqinisekile, ngoba lezi zinto akuzona ezabakholwayo kuKristu, ngakho-ke, ningabesabi.

Ngubani ongamelana neNgonyama yesizwe sakwaJuda nalo lonke ulaka namandla ami!? Yizwani amazwi ami futhi ningizwe kahle, nonke nina eniwileyo kusukela komncane kuye komkhulu. Mina, Jesu Kristu, yebo uKristu Jesu, nginguye osho esihlalweni sobukhosi nasemandleni kaYahweh, Omkhulu Nginguye, ukuthi nginguye ozayo, futhi ngokuqinisekile ngiyothatha bonke labo abahlengwe ngokugcwele ngegazi lami lokuhlawulela futhi babekwe uphawu lophawu lukaNkulunkulu ophilayo, yebo uYahweh, Omkhulu Nginguye, futhi izikhathi zabo zifikile. Ngakho-ke, angiyenqatshwa futhi imizamo yenu yokungimelana iyodilika ngokuphelele emakhanda alabo enibathumayo ukuzama. Ngubani phakathi kwenu ongamelana namandla ami!? Ngubani?!

Intando kaYahweh, Omkhulu Nginguye mayenziwe phakade, kanye, konke naphakade! Bheka, ngiyasondela, futhi bonke abafuna ukuvimbezela bayokwehlulwa ngokwehlulwa okuphelele nesahlulelo abangeke baphunyuke kuso, phakade naphakade. Sela!
