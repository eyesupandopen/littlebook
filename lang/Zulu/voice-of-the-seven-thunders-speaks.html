1Kusuka kuYeshua HaMashiach: Ukuduma Kokuqala!

Nina enibiziweyo, enakhethiweyo, nabethembekileyo bami, zwanini Amazwi eNkosi yenu ephilayo nomsindisi, uYeshua uMesiya uqobo! Izizwe enihlala kuzo kanye nemilando eniyifundiswe abafundisi benu abangamaJesuit kuyisizinda senkohliso enkulu, okwembulwa kwayo okuphelele sekuseduze futhi sekuvele kuqhubeka, lapho abawileyo beyovela njengabafokazi abanomusa kodwa eqinisweni bayizingelosi eziwile ezinonya. Yini leyo ebike yaba khona? Yileyo eyoba khona: futhi yini leyo eseyenziwe? Yileyo eyokwenziwa: futhi ayikho into entsha ngaphansi kwelanga.

USathane unikele ngolwazi lwakhe okuthiwa luthuthukile kusukela ekuqaleni, ngisho nolwazi lwakhe lokuhle nokubi. Uyingcweti yokukhohlisa nomqambimanga ofundise isintu izinto eziningi uYahweh ayengahlosile, ngoba lezi yizindlela zokufa. Indalo kaYahweh ayenzelwanga ukuba isetshenziswe kabi ukuze kutholakale amandla nenzuzo yomuntu siqu noma njengendlela yokubalekela ukwahlulela kukaNkulunkulu, noma ukumisa izindlela nomthetho womuntu siqu. Ngoba uSathane wonakalisile isintu kusukela ekuqaleni, futhi wonakalisile abalindi abawileyo abahlanganyela ekuhlubukeni kwabo.

O, wena sitha esikhulu sakho konke ukulunga, nina boSathane uqobo lwenu: izono zenu zokuqala zalotshwa ngu-Enoke umbhali sonke lesi sikhathi esadlulayo, futhi zimi kuze kube namuhla, ngoba izono zenu zalotshwa kanjalo. Ngakho-ke ngizofingqa, ngoba nenze lezi zinto, nokunye okuningi nikwenzile: Nadukisa onke amadodana kaNkulunkulu, nawaehlisela emhlabeni, nawadukisa ngamadodakazi abantu. Nanikeza amadodana angcwele kaNkulunkulu iseluleko esibi, nawadukisa aze angcolisa imizimba yawo ngamadodakazi abantu. Nikhombise abantwana babantu onke amagalelo okufa, nadukisa u-Eva, futhi nakhombisa izikhali zokufa emadodaneni abantu: isihlangu nejazi lensimbi, nenkemba yokulwa, nazo zonke izikhali zokufa kubantwana babantu. Futhi kusukela esandleni senu ziye zaqhubekela phambili melawanabo abahlala emhlabeni kusukela ngalolo suku kuze kube phakade.

Nina boSathane nafundisa abantwana babantu okubabayo nokumnandi, futhi nabafundisa zonke izimfihlo zokuhlakanipha kwabo. Nafundisa isintu ukubhala nge-inki nephepha, futhi ngenxa yalokho abaningi bonile kusukela ephakadeni kuze kube phakade nakuze kube namuhla. Ngoba abantu abadalelelwanga injongo enjalo, yokunikeza isiqinisekiso sokholo lwabo oluhle ngepeni ne-inki. Ngoba abantu badalwa njengezingelosi ncamashi, ngenhloso yokuba baqhubeke bemsulwa futhi belungile; futhi ukufa, okubhubhisa konke, kwakungeke kubabambe, kodwa ngalolu lwazi bayabhubha, futhi ngala mandla kudla abantu.

Nina boSathane nakhombisa abantwana babantu zonke izinhlupho ezimbi zemimoya namademoni, nezinhlupho zombungu esibelethweni, ukuze ushone, nezinhlupho zomphefumulo, ukulunywa yinyoka, nezinhlupho ezenzeka ngokushisa kwasemini, indodana yenyoka egama layo linguTabââ'ĕt. Ngoba nacela noMikayeli ukuba anikhombise igama elifihliwe, ukuze niliphakamise esifungweni, ukuze labo abembula konke okwakuyimfihlo kubantwana babantu bathuthumele phambi kwalelo gama nesifungo.

Ngoba nabangela ukuba abalindi bahlanganyele ekuhlubukeni kwenu njengoba naqhubeka nokonakalisa isintu. Kanjalo-ke izono zabalindi zalotshwa ngu-Enoke umbhali, futhi ngizofingqa kanje: Bafundisa abantu ukwenza izinkemba, nemimese, nezihlangu, nezivikelo zesifuba, futhi babazisa izinsimbi zomhlaba nobuciko bokuzisebenza, namasongo, nemihlobiso, nokusetshenziswa kwe-antimoni, nokuhlobisa izinkophe, nazo zonke izinhlobo zamatshe ayigugu, nazo zonke izinhlobo zodayi. Bafundisa ukuthakatha, nokusika izimpande, ukuxazulula ukuthakatha, ukufundwa kwezinkanyezi, izinkanyezi, ulwazi lwefu, izibonakaliso zomhlaba, izibonakaliso zelanga, nenkambo yenyanga.

Ngakho-ke ngenxa yokonakala kwenu namanga, ningcolise indalo kaYahweh ngezindlela zenu ezimbi nangamanyala enu ahlambalazayo. Nidukisile isintu futhi nonile kuYahweh, futhi nonile isintu, ngisho nona ezinyonini, nasezilwaneni zasendle, nasezilwaneni ezihuquzelayo, nasenhlanzini, futhi izono zenu ziletha ukwahlulelwa okukhulu eningeke nisinde kukho. Ningcolise indalo kaYahweh ngobuchwepheshe benu nokukhohlisa, futhi nabangela isintu ukuba simangale ngezibonakaliso zenu zamanga nobuxoki. Ngoba uYahweh wanikhombisa kuphela izimfihlo ezingelusizo zendalo yakhe wagcina izimfihlo zakhe eziyigugu kakhulu zifihliwe enhliziyweni yakhe kude nokukhaphela kwenu.

Nina enikhohlakele nafundisa izindlela zenu ezingelusizo esintwini njengoba zanda ebusweni bomhlaba, futhi naletha usizi olukhulu nembubhiso ngamasu enu amabi nobuchwepheshe. Ngoba anizi ngaphandle kokweba, ukubulala, nokubhubhisa. Nafundisa ukukhohlisa umoya, ngoba niyinkosana yamandla omoya, ngoba nikhohlisa i-aithēr. Ngoba lapho uKhayini ona, wakha umuzi; futhi nifundise isintu izimfihlo zenu ezingelusizo nokukhohlisa. Le mizi yayisetshenziselwa ukulawula nokukhohlisa nokuletha imbubhiso yesintu, njengoba nayisebenzisa ukwenza amanyala enu okubhubhisa.

Nibangele ukuba isizwe sivukele isizwe empini nasekuxabaneni okungapheli, futhi nabangela abantu bami ukuba bone, futhi uYahweh usakaze Izizwe zakwa-Israyeli emimoyeni emine. Akunandaba, inhloso yenu embi iqhubekisele phambili izinhloso zikaYahweh, ngoba ngokusakazeka kwazo kuzofezeka ukubuthwa kwezimvu ezisele. Futhi ngizothuma ofakazi bami ababili ukuba baprofethe izinsuku ezingu-1260 bembethe indwangu yesaka. Kuyo yonke imizamo yenu yokubhubhisa abantwana bakaYahweh nehlulekile kabi, futhi anikwazanga ukubona ukuthi imizamo yenu iqhubekisele phambili izinhloso zikaYahweh, ngoba nezitha zakhe zilalela izwi lakhe.

Ngoba nishushisile futhi nahluphe izizwe ezingu-12 kuwo wonke umhlaba njengoba nanifuna ukuzibhubhisa. Nebile abantwana komama, futhi nathatha abantwana emindenini nabathuthela kuwo wonke umhlaba, base nibaqambela amanga futhi nibenza bakholelwe izinganekwane zamaJesuit mayelana nomlando wabo weqiniso, izinhloso, nomsuka. Akunandaba, nehlulekile, ngoba mina, Yeshua HaMashiach, ngizobaxhumanisa ngokugcwele nesikhathi sabo esedlule, isizwe kanye nefa labo leqiniso, futhi ngizokwenza bonke abayizindlalifa zensindiso babe umuntu munye omusha, kimi uqobo, Kristu Jesu.

Ngobuqili nembubhiso enkulu, nibufihlile ngobuhlakani ubuchwepheshe benu, naze naqamba amanga okuthi abantu basendulo babengenalwazi futhi bengenabo ubuchwepheshe. Ngoba nakhombisa abasendulo izimfihlo zenu ezingelusizo, futhi ayikho into entsha ngaphansi kwelanga. Kanjalo-ke nifihla iqiniso mayelana nokukhohlisa kwenu indalo, nize nilifihle kulabo eningabayenga ngenkohliso yenu enkulu. Senibuye nazethula futhi izimanga zenu zomlingo, njengoba nilungise isiteji ukuze nizishaye abafokazi. Futhi ngokuhlasela kwenu kwamanga kwabafokazi niyokwenza bonke bamangale ngezibonakaliso nezimangaliso zenu zamanga, ngisho nalabo amagama abo angabhalwanga eNcwadini Yokuphila kusukela ekusekelweni komhlaba. Kodwa mina, Yeshua HaMashiach, ngizokwambula ngokuphelele, futhi yonke imfihlo efihliwe iyovela obala.

Bhekani! Ngizohlwitha abethembekileyo bami ngibayise emazulwini, ngoba ngizela uMlobokazi wami futhi ngeke ngivinjwe. Futhi mina, Yeshua HaMashiach, ngiza ukuzokwahlulela nokulwa nokuletha ukwahlulelwa okukhulu emhlabeni, futhi ngeke nibaleke.

Mina, Yeshua HaMashiach, iNkosi yamakhosi neNkosi yamakhosi, ngithumele la mazwi ngenceku yami, futhi ngenceku yami amazwi ami athunyelwe, futhi konke kuzogcwaliseka.

2Kusuka kuYahweh, uNkulunkulu Omkhulu Waphakade: Ukuduma Kwesibili!

O, nina boSathane, nikhohlise indalo yami ukuba ilandele izibonakaliso nezimangaliso zenu zamanga, futhi intukuthelo yami isha phezu kwenu. Abantu bami bazohlukaniswa ngokuphelele nokhula lwenu eniluhlwanyele phakathi kukakolweni wami ensimini yami yomusa; futhi iNdodana yami, uKristu Jesu, izobutha ukolweni wami esiphahlini sami, kanjalo iNkosi yokuvuna izobutha, ibophe futhi ishise ukhula lwenu. Ukwahlulelwa okukhulu kuphezu kwenu, futhi ngeke nibaleke esandleni sami. Kusukela ekuqaleni, nanihuquzela futhi nizifihla phakathi kwabantwana bami, naze nonakalisa nabancane bami kusukela ekuqaleni ngamanga nenkohliso yenu.

Anibona onkulunkulu, nalabo abalandela inkohliso yenu nemibono yenu yokunqoba ababona onkulunkulu. Anikwazi ukudala, ngoba aninawo amandla, futhi angikaze nginidale ngamakhono anjalo ngoba ngangazi ukuthi niyohluza ngaphambi kokuba nidalwe; futhi ngobulungiswa nothando lwami nganidala ngokulunga, futhi naniphelele ngosuku enadalwa ngalo. Ngokuzidla kwenu okungenalusizo niyazigabisa ngobukhulu benu, kodwa aninabo ubukhulu, futhi ngizoniveza obala ngokugcwele ukuthi ningabakhohlisi. Ngizokwendlala phambi Kwamakhosi, futhi azobona ukuthi aninawo amandla. Indodana yami, uYeshua HaMashiach, yaninqoba ngokuphelele eKhalvari, futhi aninathemba.

Nivunyelwe ukwenza lokho engikuvumelayo kuphela, njengoba niyisitha esehluliwe. Neba itayitela lomhlaba ngobuqili benu namanga ku-Adamu, futhi manje indodana yami ezelwe yodwa, uYeshua HaMashiach uqobo, ininqobile othandweni lwayo ngami nangendalo yami; futhi umhlaba ungowayo, iNkosi yamakhosi neNkosi yamakhosi uqobo, ngoba ngikumemezele. Ngoba yawuthenga umhlaba nesintu ngegazi layo elingenasono, futhi labo abakhetha ukuphila bayokhululwa. Yonke imisebenzi yenu engenasidingo iyochithwa, futhi bonke abakhetha ukuphila bayophuma emangeni nasekukhohliseni kwenu baye ebuntshani bokuphila, futhi ngizokwenza abathembekileyo babe nenkazimulo kunani enake naba nayo. Ngoba nehlulekile, o nina zikhosana ezibuthakathaka nezimpofu zomhlaba, ngisho nazo zonke izikhosana ohlwini lwenu zihlulekile kanye nani, o wena nyoka ehuquzelayo, enamaqhinga.

Manje sekufika isikhathi Sokwahlulelwa kwenu nembubhiso ephelele yombuso wenu, ngisho nombuso eniwakhile emizamweni yenu yokuzama ukungifihla, mina Yahweh, endalweni yami. Ngeke nibaleke. Ngeke niphinde ningene emazulwini, ngoba niphonswe emhlabeni ningeke niphinde nibuye, futhi indawo yenu ayisekho emazulwini. Niboshelwe emhlabeni enawukhanukelayo, owawungewona owenu ukuba niwuthathe. Yonke imisebenzi yenu iyochithwa ezimpilweni zalabo enazama ukubabhubhisa (nasemhlabeni uqobo); bayohlala phakade ngokuthula nokuphepha, futhi bayoba nenjabulo ephelele, futhi ngiyohlala phakathi kwesintu futhi ngibe nguNkulunkulu wabo.

Kodwa wena, o wena ohuquzelayo, awunawo amandla emehlweni ami, futhi awukwazi ukungivimba. Ezinsukwini eziyisithupha selokhu kwawa umuntu, ngoba usuku olulodwa luyinkulungwane yeminyaka emehlweni ami, anizuzanga lutho ngaphandle kokwahlulelwa kwenu, ukulahlwa, imbubhiso, nokuhlushwa kwenu nabo bonke abalandelayo. Ngoba niyoboshwa niphonswe kulelo chibi elikhulu elivuthayo lapho nginenzele khona amalangabi ashisayo kakhulu, ngisho amalangabi aluhlaza okwesibhakabhaka, futhi niyosha phakade, futhi ngiyoniphindisela wonke umsebenzi omubi enake nawenza, izikhathi eziningi. Nibulele izixuku ngezixuku, futhi niyokhokhela wonke umphefumulo olahlekile, futhi ngeke niphunyuke. Niyozazi zonke izono enazenza, futhi ngeke niphunyuke ekwahlulelweni, futhi niyohlushwa phakade ngenxa yezono enizenzile. Niyozazi kahle izono namacala enu ekuhlushweni kwenu, futhi labo enibazondayo bayoba nokunqoba kokugcina phezu kwenu. Futhi bonke abangalungile enake nabayenga ukuba bone futhi bazinikele ekuhlubukeni kwenu, ngisho nalabo amagama abo ayengakaze abhalwe eNcwadini Yokuphila, bayohlanganyela ekuhlushweni kwenu. Niyokhokhela sonke isono esisodwa enisenzile, ngoba nonile kuYahweh nakwindalo yami, futhi ngiyophindisela. Ngoba ngobulungiswa bami ngiyophindisela izono zenu, izenzo, nezindlela ezimbi ngokugcwele ezifubeni zenu izikhathi eziningi futhi kuze kube phakade. Futhi mina, Yahweh, ngisho noNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade, ngifunga lezi zinto ngegama lami, ngoba angeke ngifunge ngomkhulu kunami.

Niyosebenzela izinhloso zami, futhi ngenzondo nembubhiso yenu niyosunduza abakhethiweyo bami kimi; ngoba ngiyobahola ngesandla sikaYeshua HaMashiach nebutho lakhe lamadoda anamandla, ngoba bayobutha izimvu zami ezisele. (Bhekani! Abayi-144,000 abathunywe nguYeshua HaMashiach bazokwenza imisebenzi emikhulu phambi kobubi benu obunyanyekayo.) Futhi akekho noyedwa engimmisele ukuphila okuphakade oyowela emacebweni enu, ngoba angeke bahlwithwe esandleni sami, futhi akekho ongebahlwitha ezandleni zeNdodana yami: Ngoba ngimnike igama eliphezu kwawo onke amagama, futhi uza ngamandla kaYahweh uqobo, ngoba ngabelane naye ngesihlalo sami sobukhosi. Futhi uyolichoboza ngokuphelele ikhanda lenu. Nehlulekile, nkosana encane, futhi isikhathi senu sesifikile.

Mina, Yahweh, uNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade, ngithumele la mazwi ngenceku yami, futhi ngenceku yami amazwi ami athunyelwe, futhi ayokwenzeka. Ngoba uYahweh, uNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade, ukumemezele kanjalo, futhi akukho okungaphula izimemezelo zami. Ngingu-El Shaddai!


Izikhathi ZifikileUkuduma 3 & 4 (Meyi 16, 2024)

3Kusuka KuYeshua HaMashiach: Ukuduma Kwesithathu

Bhekani! Izikhathi zokuhlukaniswa ziyaphela, nokubonakala kwami sekucishe kwafika manje. Kuseduze kakhulu, ngisho nezingelosi nebutho lezulu balindele ukukhala kwecilongo. Sengelulele izandla zami ezweni elifayo iminyaka eminingi manje, futhi abaningi basamukele isivumelwano sokuthula uBaba, uYahweh, asithumela esintwini, yebo, mina, Kristu Jesu, ngoba ngingukuthula kwenu. Kodwa abaningi kakhulu balunqabile lolu phawu lwami nomusa kaBaba nesihe sakhe esithululwe emhlabeni.

Labo abaphawuliwe nabahlengiwe yilabo abakhethwa kusukela ekusekelweni komhlaba. Ngoba uBaba wayenazi kusukela phakade kuze kube phakade, futhi nanilokhu nisenhliziyweni yakhe, njengoba nami ngibe senhliziyweni yakhe, ngoba mina, Jesu waseNazaretha, ngiyindodana kaYahweh ezelwe yodwa. Kodwa ngemisebenzi yami ephelele njengePhasika eliphelele nangokulalela koZelwe Yedwa, amadodana amaningi, amaningi kaNkulunkulu azalwe kabusha kuze kube phakade, athathwe emadodaneni abantu, ngoba ngiyiNdodana yoMuntu!

Lezi zikhathi zomusa zaziyimfihlakalo efihliwe enhliziyweni kaYahweh angayitshelanga muntu waze wayembulela kimi, futhi mina ngayembulela abaphostoli bami abangcwele nabaprofethi futhi ngalondoloza iZwi lami elingcwele ezindlebeni nasezinhliziyweni zabakhethiweyo nabathembekileyo. Ngoba nina, Makoti wami, niyingxenye yemfihlakalo enkulu kaNkulunkulu, futhi izimpilo zenu (ezifihliwe kimi) sezizokwambulwa. Futhi ngizoklomelisa abathembekile ngayo yonke imisebenzi yabo yokwethembeka abayenze ngokholo olukhulu kimi, Jesu Kristu.

Futhi ngalokho ngisindise izixuku ezinkulu ngegazi lami lokuhlawulela, ngoba igazi lami lalingenasono futhi nanamhlanje angisoni, ngoba angisoze ngona. Labo abaphile izimpilo zabo ngokwethembeka kuKristu bazokwamukela umvuzo omkhulu, futhi bazokhanya njengezinkanyezi ezulwini, futhi inkazimulo enkulu ingeyabo, ngoba abazange bazidumise bona ngokungathi bangabakhulu. Kodwa badumise mina, Yeshua HaMashiach, futhi ngizobethula kuBaba bengenabala noma umbhinco, nezingubo zabo zizoba mhlophe qwa futhi azisoze zangcola. Futhi bonke bayophila futhi ngeke baphinde bone, kungaba ngokungamesabi uNkulunkulu noma ngokuzidla. Futhi bazodunyiswa kimi.

Ngoba ngingumalusi omkhulu, futhi ngiholele umhlambi wami emadlelweni aluhlaza kuze kube yizikhathi zokuphela, ezifika ngokushesha emhlabeni, ngoba alisekho isihlabathi esisele ukudlula engilazini. Futhi ngizoklomelisa bonke, kusukela komncane kuze kube komkhulu, ngendlela eningeke niyiqonde ngalesi sikhathi; kodwa lokho kuqonda kusemnyango wenu, ngoba nizothathwa niye emazulwini maduzane nje. Kanjalo uMakoti wami uzothathelwa enkazimulweni, lapho ngizokwahlulela khona imisebenzi yakhe futhi ngihlenge ngokuphelele wonke umuntu wesilisa, owesifazane, nomntwana. Ngizobavuza kakhulu nangezinto ezincane kakhulu ezenziwe ngenkonzo yami. Ngoba uNkulunkulu akalona ongalungile, ukuba akhohlwe umsebenzi wenu nomshikashika wothando, eniwubonisile ngeGama lakhe, ngokuthi nikhonze abaNgcwele, futhi nisakhonza.

Ungesabi, Makoti wami, ngoba uNkulunkulu akahluleli njengomuntu, ngoba umuntu akalungile futhi unobandlululo. Umuntu udumisa labo umuntu ababona bebalulekile, futhi umuntu wemvelo cishe uhlale enganaki ukuthobeka nobuhle evuna abazidlayo nabazifezile. Ngoba umuntu wemvelo ukhala ngokuthi abalwe olungile phambi kukaNkulunkulu, kodwa umuntu wemvelo akanakunqoba isimo sakhe sesono futhi uyenqaba ukukholelwa ukuthi wonakele, aze aphike iNkosi yamakhosi, engamsindisa.

Kanjalo ukuzidla kwandulela ukubhujiswa, nomoya oziphakamisayo wandulela ukuwa. Esikhundleni sokuthobela izinhliziyo zabo emazwini ami nasekuhlakanipheni nothando engilukhukhumeze emhlabeni, umuntu wemvelo ukhetha ukulandela izinkanuko zakhe, ngoba izindlela zomhlaba zakhiwe nguSathane ukuyenga nokuvusa lezo zinkanuko, ngisho nemivuzo yokungalungi. Kanjalo izikhathi zabeZizwe seziyaphela, futhi sekuyisikhathi sokuhlupheka kukaJakobe; ngoba ngizohlawulela u-Israyeli, ngisho nabakhethiweyo bami abathembekile, abazokuza kimi ngokuphenduka ezonweni zabo nasezonweni zawoyise abangilahlayo. Futhi njengoba kulotshiwe, makwenzeke kanjalo.

O, muntu wemvelo, kungani usakhetha izono zakho futhi ungawunaki umusa wami nensindiso ngokungathi yinto elula? Akekho uYahweh noma mina, Kristu Jesu, onenjabulo ezahlulelweni ezizayo nasekwesabeni okukhulu okuzodedelwa maduze. Kodwa uNkulunkulu akakhethanga le ngxenye yecebo lakhe ukuze abe nonya endalweni yakhe: ngoba uNkulunkulu uluthando, noNkulunkulu ungukukhanya, futhi kuye akukho bumnyama nhlobo. Ukuhlupheka okukhulu okuzayo kwenzelwe ukunika isintu ithuba lokugcina ngaphambi kokuba kwephuze kakhulu. Isixuku esikhulu sizosindiswa osizini olukhulu, ngisho nalabo ababambelela egameni lami kuze kube sekuphefumuleni kwabo kokugcina futhi bengazithandi izimpilo zabo kuze kube sekupheleni.

Muntu wemvelo, unenkani kakhulu, awulaleli, futhi uyawenqaba umusa wami ngobuwula, futhi ngisakhuleka okufanayo: Baba, bathethelele, ngoba abakwazi abakwenzayo. Kulabo abasalele ekuhluphekeni okukhulu, ngisho nalabo abenqabile isipho sami sokuthula, abaningi bayolahlekelwa babhujiswe, ngoba bayinqabile iNkosi uJesu Kristu. Nokho, abaningi bayosindiswa, ngisho bonke abakhuleka egameni leNkosi bayosindiswa. Kanjalo izilingo zomlilo sezizoqala, ngoba ukwahlulela kuyasondela.

Amazwi ami okwahlulela awaqondiswe kuwe kodwa angawokukududuza, ngoba ngikusindisile entukuthelweni esezokhululwa, futhi ngizokufihla endaweni ephephile nasemvuzweni omkhulu, futhi uyoba nami kuze kube phakade. Ngoba ngikukhethile, ngoba ngikubizile, futhi uyigugu lami elikhulu kunawo onke, futhi uyigugu likaYahweh elikhulu kunawo onke; ungisho iparele lenani elikhulu, ngoba nginikele ngakho konke ngenxa yakho. Ngeluleka uMlobokazi wami ukuba afune ukuhlakanipha futhi eze kimi ukuze athole ukuhlakanipha, ngoba ngizokufundisa. Ngoba abanye benu balandele ezinye zezindlela zalaba baphrofethi bamanga; phendukani kuphela ngokuphenduka eNkosini yenu, futhi ngizonamukela.

Ngoba banikhohlisile ngobuthakathi bobuKristu, futhi nikholwe amanga amaningi enathengiselwa wona, nisho nitshelwa ukuthi ayiqiniso, kodwa angamanga anengekayo. Le mithombo engenamanzi izamile ukunidonsa kimi, futhi lokho akunakwenzeka, ngakho ungesabi, ngoba angisoze ngenqatshwa! Ngizoqhubeka nokunikhulula ukuze nikhululeke ngempela, futhi angisoze ngathuliswa! Ngilwela nina ngezindlela eziningi kakhulu eningazazi. Ngoba nonke naphawulwa ngaphambi kokuba nizalwe, ngoba uBaba wanazi ngisho nisesiswini somama wenu, futhi walinda ngamunye wenu isikhathi eningeke nasiqonde, futhi nihlale nisenhliziyweni yakhe. Naphawulwa ekuzalweni, futhi izingelosi zami zanigcina ngomyalo wami futhi zanilinda zanivikela, ngoba zithunyelwe kanjalo. Ngabe lokhu kuyanimangaza? Niyokulalela yini lokho okushiwo amaHebheru? Kodwa yikuphi kulezi zingelosi ake wathi, hlala ngakwesokunene sami, ngize ngenze izitha zakho zibe yisenabelo sezinyawo zakho? Akuzona zonke yini imimoya ekhonzayo, ethunyelwe ukuba ikhonze labo abayoba yizindlalifa zensindiso?

Mlobokazi wami, nina niyizindlalifa zensindiso kimi, Jesu Kristu. Nibiziwe kimi; nivikelwe kimi futhi ngami; ninikezwe ithuba lokuzwa ukushunyayelwa kokholo mayelana nami ukuze ningenza iNkosi yenu, futhi ngakho-ke nginibeke uphawu ngalowo moya ongcwele wesithembiso. Yazini ukuthi uphawu alunakwephulwa, futhi senizohlengwa ngokugcwele kuze kube phakade, ngoba anazi ngobukhulu bomsebenzi wokudala engizowufeza kini, Mlobokazi wami! Yazini ukuthi labo abawile nokhula lwabo banomhawu ngani, futhi bayakuzonda konke lokho okuthandwa nguYahweh, futhi ngiyoniphindisela ngokugcwele emakhanda abo, ngoba yimi engizolimaza ikhanda laleyo nyoka yasendulo, ngoba kumemezele, futhi lokho okumemezelwa nguYahweh akunakuguqulwa.

Akekho onganihlukanisa nami; abukho ububi obungangephula lolo phawu; ningabami futhi ningabakaBaba, ngoba konke lokho uBaba anginika khona ngiyokubuyisela kuNkulunkulu ukuze sibe yikho konke kukho konke! Futhi unginike zonke izinto ezulwini nasemhlabeni, ngaphandle kwasesihlalweni sakhe sobukhosi mkhulu kunami!

Futhi mina, Kristu Jesu, ngimemezela la mazwi kini, futhi yimi, Jesu Kristu, omemezela ukwahlulelwa ezitheni zenu, futhi ngizokufeza kuze kube yimali encane yokugcina. Mlobokazi wami, uthando lwami ngani alucimeki, futhi nginilungisele indawo, ngoba endlini kaBaba kukhona izindlu eziningi! Ngakho maduze kakhulu niyogana nami kuze kube phakade, futhi kanjalo niyohlala nami, futhi ekugcineni niyokuqonda ubukhulu benjongo yenu kimi enadalelwa ukuyifeza!

Ngoba kimi, uYahweh unginike ikusasa nenjongo, ngisho nenjongo yezikhathi isizokwambulwa kini, ngoba ngiyeza! Futhi angisoze ngenqatshwa. Bhekani ukubonakala kwami. Bhekani ukukhanya kokufika kwami, ngoba sekuzokusa phezu komhlaba njengoba ngivela emafwini ngokumemeza okukhulu, ngezwi lengelosi enkulu, nangephondo likaNkulunkulu, ngoba nginitshela ngeqiniso ukuthi ngisho nezingelosi zilinde lelo phondo elizokhaliswa, futhi sezilungele! Lungelani mina nibheke, niqaphe, njengoba niyaliwe; ngoba maduze nizobona mina nenkazimulo yami uBaba angembathise yona, ngoba kanjalo nami ngizonembathisa nina, ngoba niyoba njengami, futhi niyongibona njengoba nginjalo.
