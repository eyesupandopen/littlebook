Novemba 20, 2024

IWundlu NeNgonyama Kuba Munye!

Wozani kuYeshua HaMashiach, zihlobo zami, niqonde umsebenzi womhlengi wenu, ngokuba ngingumhlengi wenu oyisihlobo. Ngoba yimi engisazohlenga insali kaJakobe ukuze iphile phambi kuka-MINA ENGINGUYE omkhulu, uYHVH, uNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade nongunaphakade. Futhi ukubusa kwami nombuso wami ngeke kube nesiphelo, ngakho-ke, lalelani Amazwi eNkosi yenu yaphakade yeSithembiso, uYeshua HaMashiach, onguJesu Kristu, futhi onguKristu Jesu, ngokuba u-MINA ENGINGUYE omkhulu ungenzile kanjalo kuze kube phakade.

Futhi ngeqiniso uYahweh unginikile igama eliphezu kwawo onke amagama ukuze ngegama likaJesu onke amadolo aguqe, ezulwini nasemhlabeni ngisho nangaphansi komhlaba. Ngoba nginguye lowo uYahweh anikele kuye zonke izinto ezulwini nasemhlabeni ezandleni zami ezinamandla ukuze kugcwaliseke zonke izinto. Ngakho-ke, O' Israyeli, yizwa izwi lami ngokulalela kothando ngenxa yothando engikukhombise lona ngokukufela esikhundleni sakho osizini olukhulu okwakungekhona okwami ukuluthwala. Nokho, othandweni lwami ngani, ngathatha isikhundla senu ukuze nginihlenge esijezisweni sikaMose, isijeziso eningenakuzikhokhela sona ngenxa yesono esisenyameni yenu. Ngempela, ngathunyelwa ezweni ngenjongo yokuhlenga u-Adamu.

Ngoba uMose ulungile, ungcwele futhi uqotho, futhi uMose uhlala ngempela kuMesiya wenu weqiniso neNkosi yaphakade yokuLunga, ngokuba nginjalo. Futhi nginguye owagcina umthetho kaMose ngokuphelele ngothando lwami nokulalela kwami uYahweh owangihola othandweni lwakhe olungapheli ngohlanga luka-Adamu olwonelwe lwanqotshwa abawileyo, ngakho-ke umusa nomusa kunikiwe ngenala enkulu ukuze kubambezeleke ubukhulu obugcwele bokwahlulela nolaka ukuze kwenzeke ukwehlukaniswa okuphelele kokugcina phakathi kukakolweni kaYahweh nokhula lwabawileyo, futhi kanjalo bonke abayizindlalifa zokuphila okuphakade kuKristu Jesu bayohlengwa ngokukhetha kwabo ukuzithoba kuYeshua HaMashiach, onguKristu Jesu, futhi onguJesu waseNazaretha.

Futhi uYahweh wangithuma ezizweni, futhi ngenkonzo yezizwe ezilahlekile zakwa-Israyeli ngahamba ngoba ngenqatshwa yizihlobo zami ngokungakholwa okungenalukholo, ngisho nabapristi abakhulu kanye neSanhedrini abakhonza uDrako ngasese ngoba bangabaka-Esawu ozihlanganise enzondweni yakhe ngomfowabo uJakobe no-Ashkenazi kaJafete, ukuze baziqoqele izinto ezingezami futhi balimaze uJakobe ekhanda okokugcina, okuyisenzo uYahweh angeke asivumele, isenzo azosenqaba ngesandla sami esinamandla nesilalelayo njalo. Ngakho-ke, zihlobo zami, zithobeni ngokulalela izwi lami ukuze niphile phambi kwami, futhi niphile phambi kukaYahweh okungekho muntu ongasondela kuye ngaphandle kukaJesu Kristu ngoba ukhethe kanjalo uYahweh ohlelweni lwakhe lwaphakade lokuhlenga nokuhlakanipha. Ngakho-ke qondani ukuthi ngaphandle kukaMesiya wenu weqiniso, uYeshua HaMashiach, nisahlezi nifile eziphambekweni nasezonweni engazinqoba lapho ngikhokha ngokugcwele isijeziso sikaMose kuze kube phakade nakubo bonke abakhetha ukwamukela umhlatshelo wami.

Futhi kunjalo-ke ukuthi uMose uhlala kuYeshua HaMashiach, futhi nginguye lowo uMose ayala ukuba nimlalele ngokugcwele, ngakho-ke yizwani amazwi ami nisoke izinhliziyo zenu nezindlebe zenu ukuze nizwe inkulumo yami. Ngoba izinhliziyo zenu sezikhuluphele ngokungalaleli, futhi nigcwalise izindlebe zenu ngokuhlakanipha kuka-Esawu nangcolisa izinhliziyo zenu ngamanga abo enhlamba eTalmud. Qondani ukuthi uMose uphelele, ulungile futhi ungcwele futhi uma noma yimuphi umuntu ethi umthetho kaMose uqalekisiwe, lowo muntu makabe oqalekisiwe ngenxa yenhlamba yakhe, ngisho nangomthetho kaMose awuqalekisileyo. Ngakho-ke, nginazisa ukuthi umthetho wanikwa ukuze ulahle isono enyameni, ngokuba lapho kungekho mthetho isono asibalwa ngokomthetho, nakuba isono sikhona enyameni kunjalo nje. Ngakho-ke, ngemisebenzi yomthetho kaMose akukho nyama eyolungisiswa emehlweni kaYahweh: ngokuba ngomthetho kaMose kuza ulwazi lwesono.

Nokho, njengoba ngenza uPawulu abhalele iBandla lami enkathini yami yoMusa, manje ukulunga kukaNkulunkulu kubonakalisiwe ngaphandle komthetho kaMose, kunobufakazi bomthetho nabaPhrofethi. Oh, zikhulumi zami zangaphambili eseniwile esabelweni senu sangaphambili enasabelwa sona isizwe senu esake saba sikhulu nguYahweh uqobo ngoba nanifanele nibe yisizwe esikhethekile esihlukile nezizwe! Kodwa ningalahlile njengoba benza okhokho benu ekuhlubukeni kwenu okunhliziyo lukhuni ngokumelene nezinjongo namacebo aphakade kaNkulunkulu uSomandla, u-El Shaddai. Futhi kunjalo ngokwenqaba kwenu Amazwi ami anikezwe evangelini lensindiso lapho ngembula imfihlakalo enkulu kaNkulunkulu kubaphostoli bami abangcwele nabaprofethi senifana nabadinga ubisi, futhi anikwazi ukugaya ukudla okuqinile ngoba niseyizinsana ezingenaqondisiso oluphelele, nakuba nizishaya abahlakaniphileyo nezinkanyiso kodwa nikhanyiselwe ngamanga abawileyo ukuze niduze abantu ezindleleni zenu ezingafundile.

Ngakho-ke, qondani zihlobo zami, ukuthi olungileyo uyophila ngokukholwa nokuthi ngemisebenzi yomthetho akukho nyama eyolungisiswa! Kulotshiwe emibhalweni engcwele enikezwe ngezandla zikaPawulu, owayengumBenjamini olalelayo owayengalaleli futhi enenkani ekwenqabeni kwakhe uJesu waseNazaretha, njengani nani: Khona-ke labo abangabokukholwa, babusiswa kanye no-Abrahama othembekileyo. Ngokuba bonke abangabemisebenzi yomthetho, bangaphansi kwesiqalekiso somthetho: ngokuba kulotshiwe, uqalekisiwe wonke umuntu ongaqhubeki ezintweni zonke, ezilotshiwe encwadini yoMthetho, ukuba azenze.

Nokho, ngenxa yobubi, ubuqili, namaqhinga enyoka ngosuku u-Adamu awa ngalo phezulu; isono sesibuse enyameni kusukela lapho futhi isimo esonakele sika-Adamu akusilo ikhaya lenu, futhi anihloselwe ukuchitha iphakade ezindlini zobumba okuyizindlu ezibuyela othulini lomhlaba lapho isiqu senu sathathwa khona. Ngakho-ke, uYahweh ungenzile umoya ophilayo, futhi ngingu-Adamu wokugcina. Futhi nginguye owenziwe isibonelo sokuphila okuphakade ukuze nonke nidalwe kabusha ngokuphelele nangobunye, njengomuntu munye omusha. Ngoba ngeke kube nezinhlobo ezimbili zabantu phakade kodwa kube yinye ngoba isimo sika-Adamu sizobhubha kuze kube phakade kanye nenkohlakalo esenyameni eyonakele.

Ngakho-ke, qondani ukuthi abawileyo banganifihlela iqiniso ebumnyameni benu obuyingxenye nasekuziqhenyeni kwenu, iqiniso elizonikhulula ngokuphelele enkululekweni engifuna ukunikhulula ngayo. Ngakho-ke nginazisa, O' Israyeli, (engimthandayo) ukuthi akekho umuntu olungisiswa ngemisebenzi yomthetho kaMose emehlweni kaYahweh, kusobala: ngokuba olungileyo uyophila ngokukholwa. Futhi umthetho awusiwo owokukholwa: kodwa umuntu oyokwenza lezo zinto, uyophila kuzo. Futhi mina, uYeshua HaMashiach, nginihlengile esiqalekisweni somthetho, ngenziwa isiqalekiso ngenxa yenu, ngokuba kulotshiwe, uqalekisiwe wonke umuntu olenga esihlahleni. Ngakho-ke, O' Israyeli, wamukelani umhlatshelo wami ukuze nina nenzalo yenu niphile futhi nithole ithuba lokungena embusweni wami weminyaka eyinkulungwane onikezwe njengokuphumula kosuku lweSabatha ezinhlelweni zaphakade zikaYahweh zokuhlenga isintu, ngokuba iminyaka eyinkulungwane injengosuku nosuku lunjengeminyaka eyinkulungwane emehlweni kaYahweh, eninokwenza naye, O' Israyeli.

Ngakho-ke, hlukani nalabo abaniqambela amanga futhi nihlukanise naleyo ncwadi yamanga enhlamba ethunyelwe abawileyo ukuze banikhohlise futhi banibophe ephutheni ukuze nibhubhe ngokuphelele. Sukani emangeni enyoka niphume phakathi kwabo, nihlukane, O' Israyeli, futhi ngizonamukela nginilondoloze niphile ekubhujisweni kweviki lamashumi ayisikhombisa likaDaniyeli, usizi lukaJakobe oluzayo ukuhlanza umhlaba ezandleni zabenzi bokubi ngokwahlulela okukhulu nokulungileyo.

Ngoba izikhathi zokwehlukaniswa zifika ngokugcwele emhlabeni lapho uJakobe, ifa lakhe eliyisithembiso labantwana besithembiso abaphila ngokukholwa, eyokwahlukaniswa ngokuphelele naphakade nomfowabo ongalaleli, u-Esawu ofuna ukweba lokho akuthengisa ngokungakholwa okungenalukholo ngoba wayengenanhlonipho noma ukholo ezithembisweni ezithembekile ezenziwa ngokukholwa nguYahweh ku-Abrahama othembekileyo, kodwa kunalokho wayekhathalela kakhulu izinto zomhlaba engumphingi nomuntu ongahloniphi owathengisa ifa lakhe laphakade nobuzibulo ngesinkwa esincane.

Ngakho ngiyanazisa ngekusasa enginibizela ukuba nihlanganyele kulo futhi engizonilondoloza kulo ekuphepheni kwamandla ami uma nizithoba ekubuseni nasegunyeni lami, ngokuba ngokwenza kanjalo ngizonibuyisela ngokugcwele kuYahweh, uBaba wezinkanyiso okungekho kuye ukuguquguquka nasithunzi sokuphenduka. Futhi kulotshiwe ezithembisweni zasendulo futhi kuyoba njalo zihlobo zami engingumhlengi wazo oyisihlobo!

Futhi kuyokwenzeka ekupheleni kosizi lukaJakobe ukuthi ngiyomisa umbuso wami ngokulunga emhlabeni, futhi akukho nto embi eyongena kuwo. Qondani ukuthi ngiyoletha isiphelo saphakade embusweni kaSathane futhi udrako ngeke aphinde avuke abuse futhi isilo sasolwandle nesilo sasemhlabeni siyophonswa siphila kulelo chibi elikhulu lomlilo wesibabule ovuthayo, ogubhazayo oshisa kakhulu ngamalangabi aluhlaza futhi kulelo chibi elinukayo lesijeziso bayophonswa kuze kube phakade, lapho impethu yabo ingafi ngokuba bakufanele lokho.

USathane uqobo uyothathwa aboshwe ngeketanga elikhulu lokuhlushwa nesijeziso lapho eyolinda ngokwesaba nangosizi lokubhujiswa kwakhe okuzayo, okuyifa ayokwabelana ngalo nabo bonke abamlandelayo. Ngeqiniso, ngeqiniso, kwalasha uyohlala evalelwe futhi eboshiwe isikhathi esiyiminyaka eyinkulungwane, ekupheleni kwaso uyokhululwa ukuze abuthe labo abayobe behlubukile bajoyina izindlela zakhe, ukuhlubuka kwakhe kokugcina ukuze ububi bubhujiswe kuze kube phakade ekwahlulelweni kokugcina. Futhi ekupheleni kwesikhashana sakhe lapho esebahlanganisile njengesihlabathi solwandle bemelene nekamu labangcwele kububanzi bomhlaba, umlilo uyokuwa uvela kuYahweh phezulu uyobashisa ubaqede bonke, futhi uSathane uyoboshwa phakade aphonswe efeni lakhe laphakade kulawomalangabi avuthayo aphakade esijeziso futhi lokho kwahlulela okukhulu kwesihlalo sobukhosi esimhlophe kuyoqala.

Futhi kulelo chibi elivuthayo lesijeziso elishisa amalangabi okuhlushwa ashisayo, ngisho nasekugoneni kwalo okuvuthayo okuphakade uSathane uyokhokha ngokuphelele sonke isono ake wasenza izikhathi eziningi, futhi zonke izenzo ezimbi zilotshwe ngokuphelele emazulwini futhi zizonikezwa emalangabini esijeziso njengefa lakhe laphakade. Impela, zonke izidalwa ezimlandele zazibophezela emkhankasweni wakhe zizobhekana nesijeziso esifanayo futhi ngeke zibe nomusa ngoba azibonisanga umusa, ngakho-ke akukho musa oyonikwa kuze kube phakade. Futhi njengoba benze kwabanye, futhi njengoba belimaze indalo kaYahweh kanjalo kuyobuyiselwa emakhanda abo, njengoba kwathenjiswa endulo ngoba asikho isithembiso esisodwa sikaYahweh esiyoke siwele phansi singagcwalisekanga. Ngeqiniso, ngeqiniso bonke abenqaba umnikelo wami wensindiso bayophonswa emlilweni futhi akukho ukukhetha muntu.

Nokho, isiphetho sabawileyo abazikhethele sona ekuhlubukeni kwabo okubi ngokumelene noYahweh nendalo yakhe akusona esenu zihlobo zami, uma ningamukela njengoMesiya wenu. Cha, ngokuphambene nalokho, kunemivuzo eminingi nezibusiso enibekelwe zona eniyoziqaphela ngokugcwele embusweni wami engiyowubusa ngokulunga okuphakade. Kuyoba neNkosi eyodwa yamakhosi neNkosi yamakhosi eyobusa umhlaba ngokulunga kwaphakade, kusukela eJerusalema, kusukela esihlalweni sobukhosi sikaDavide engiyindlalifa yaso yaphakade! Futhi kumakoti wami othembekileyo engizomthathela yena esivumelwaneni saphakade ngiyogcoba futhi ngingcwelise ibutho elinamandla lamaKhosi nabaPristi ngokokuthembeka kwabo futhi bayobusa ngokulunga ngokuvumelana okuphelele neNkosi yabo yamakhosi neNkosi yamakhosi futhi siyobusa njengabanye.

Futhi abantu bezwe nabantu bezizwe bayokhanda izinkemba zabo zibe amakhuba futhi izizwe ngeke zisaphinde zilwe, nabantu bomhlaba ngeke bafunde ukulwa nokulwa embusweni wami ongcwele. Njengoba kulotshiwe ngabaprofethi basendulo: Impisi iyohlala newundlu, ingwe ilale nezinyane lembuzi, nethole, nengonyama, nesilwane esikhulupheleyo ndawonye, nomntwana omncane uyobahola. Inkomo nebhere kuyoklaba: amazinyane azo ayolala ndawonye: nengonyama iyodla utshani njengenkunzi yenkomo. Futhi umntwana oncelayo uyodlala emgodini wenyoka, nomntwana olunyuliweyo uyobeka isandla sakhe emgodini webululu. Khona-ke akekho oyolimaza noma abhubhise kuyo yonke intaba yobungcwele bami: ngokuba umhlaba uyogcwala ulwazi lweNkosi, njengamanzi asibekela ulwandle.

Futhi ngalolo suku kuyophuma amanzi okuphila eJerusalema, ingxenye yawo iye olwandle lwaseMpumalanga, nengxenye yawo iye olwandle olukude kakhulu, futhi kuyoba njalo, ehlobo nasebusika. Futhi iNkosi iyoba yiNkosi phezu komhlaba wonke: ngalolo suku kuyoba neNkosi eyodwa, neGama layo liyoba linye. Izwe lonke liyophendulwa libe yithafa kusukela eGeba kuya eRimoni, ngaseNingizimu yeJerusalema, futhi liyophakanyiswa, lihlale endaweni yalo: kusukela esangweni likaBenjamini kuya endaweni yesango lokuqala, kuze kube sesangweni legumbi, nakusukela embhoshongweni waseHananeli, kuze kube sezikhamweni zewayini zeNkosi. Futhi abantu bayohlala kulo, futhi ngeke kusaba khona ukubhujiswa, kodwa iJerusalema liyohlala ngokulondeka.

Ngakho-ke qondani, nina bakwa-Israyeli, lokho engizokwenza kubo bonke abangithandayo ngokulalela ngenhlonipho imiyalo yami yaphakade yokuphila nokuthula enginiyala ngayo. Qondani-ke ukuthi ukuthanda uYahweh kungukugcina imiyalo yakhe, futhi imiyalo kaYahweh iyala ukulalelwa kweNdodana kaYahweh, enginguye mina; ngakho-ke, qabulani iNdodana funa ngithukuthele nife endleleni.

Ngoba owami umbuso lapho izinhloso zami zaphakade ziyohlangana khona ziphelele, zifezeke ngokugcwele lapho iWundlu liyoba munye neBhubesi.

Ngoba ngiyiWundlu likaNkulunkulu, elanikelwa ngenxa yezono zesintu, igazi lalo elingumhlatshelo wokubuyisana waphakade, lapho abangalungile benziwa mhlophe njengeqhwa ebumneneni obungcwele.

Ngoba ngiyiBhubesi lesizwe sakwaJuda elinilwelayo, nina bakwa-Israyeli, futhi eliyozichitha ngokuphelele izitha zenu njengoba kulotshiwe ngami; impindiselo ngeyami futhi ngiyophindisela, isho iNkosi yamakhosi neNkosi yamakhosi!

Ngakho-ke, qondani izinhloso zami nokuthi uYahweh umthumele bani ukuba anikhulule, futhi ziningi izici ezinhlosweni zami zensindiso. Futhi ngingophakeme kunabo bonke, ngisho lowo owanizuzela insindiso yaphakade uma nikhetha ukwamukela isandla sami sothando futhi namukele isivumelwano sami esisha.

Ngoba kungesivumelwano sami esisha engasiqinisa ngegazi lami, esesiyiTestamente lami Elisha manje, lapho umhlaba uyobuswa ngokulunga! Futhi ulwazi ngoYahweh luyogcwala umhlaba futhi inkazimulo yami iyokhanya isuka eJerusalema ukuze izizwe zibuthene zingene emasangweni angcwele alowo muzi ongcwele, ukuze zifunde ngoYahweh futhi ziphile ngezindlela zakhe emhlabeni.

Embhusweni wami ngeke kusaba khona usana lwezinsuku, noma ixhegu elingazigcwalisanga izinsuku zalo; ngoba umntwana uyokufa eneminyaka eyikhulu ubudala; kodwa isoni esineminyaka eyikhulu ubudala siyoqalekiswa. Futhi kuyoba ukuthi lowo ophilayo aze abone iminyaka eyikhulu uyoba njengosana nje.

Ngisho kanjalo, umhlaba uyobuyiselwa enkazimulweni yawo yakuqala ngaphambi kokuba abawiwe bangcolise umhlaba ngezinengiso zabo lapho behlela eNtabeni iHermoni ukwenza isifungo sabo esiqalekisiwe ukuze baphile izinkanuko zabo esonweni esikhulu nasekuhlubukeni emiyalweni yokuphila ababedalwe bephelele kuyo. Ngeke babe khona embusweni wami; futhi umhlaba ngeke ubuswe ngobudlova obunenzondo lapho abanamandla besinda khona, ngoba umbhedo onjalo ungukuphambana nemithetho kaYahweh uSathane ahlala efuna ukuyiguqula ukuze kubuse incithakalo.

Futhi yimina, Yeshua HaMashiach, oyobusa izizwe ngenduku yensimbi ngezandla zamakhosi amaningi nabaPristi engiyobathwesa imiqhele ngibamisele enkonzweni ephakeme kakhulu, futhi bona bayizithelo zokuqala zabafileyo, futhi abafi futhi abasoze bafa futhi, futhi umvuzo wabo awupheli, ngoba beza kimi ngokuzithandela ezinsukwini zomusa futhi ngabahlanganisela kimi emzimbeni wami owadalwa kabusha njengomuntu oyedwa omusha, kungakhathaliseki ukuthi babengamaJuda noma abeZizwe, noma babeyizigqila noma bekhululekile. Bayoba namakhono amaningi neziphiwo abazinikiwe ngenxa yokwethembeka kwabo ngoba bangabanqobi bami abanqoba zonke izinto ngegazi leWundlu.

Ngoba yiWundlu likaNkulunkulu elanqoba abawiwe eKhalvari kuze kube phakade futhi yiBhubesi lesizwe sakwaJuda eliyoyehlula ngokuphelele indalo kuYahweh, ngisho nokuthi uYahweh abe yikho konke kukho konke lapho ngiyokwethula kuye indalo ehluliwe ngoba kumele ngibuse kuze kube yilapho zonke izitha zami zenziwe isenabelo sezinyawo zami, futhi yiBhubesi lesizwe sakwaJuda eliyonyathela zonke izici zombuso kaSathane ngaphansi kwezinyawo zami ezinamandla naphakade.

Kungakho-ke, ngingulowo iWundlu eliyohlangana naye ekuthuleni okuphelele nasebunyeni neBhubesi ngoba izinhloso zami zaphakade ziyokwenziwa kuze kube phakade futhi akukho lutho futhi akekho ongamela uYeshua HaMashiach futhi izikhathi zami sezifikile lapho ngiyoziveza khona kubakhileyo bomhlaba. Bhekani, ngiyeza ngamafu, futhi wonke amehlo ayongibona: yebo, ngisho nalabo abangigwazayo; futhi yonke imindeni yomhlaba iyolila phambi kwami, kunjalo, Amen.

NginguYeshua HaMashiach osezoveza yena emhlabeni futhi umhlaba wonke uyokwazi ukuthi kukhona uNkulunkulu ezulwini futhi izindlela zikaYahweh ziyogcwala emhlabeni ngokulunga okungcwele.

NginguKristu Jesu, iNkosi yenu eyenyuka yavuka kwabafileyo, iNkosi, umPristi Omkhulu, noMesiya!

Ngingulowo othumele amazwi ami angcwele ngesithunywa sami nenceku yami futhi ngesithunywa sami nenceku yami athunyelwe amazwi ami angcwele! Ngoba isithunywa sami esinamandla sibuyele ngokwethembeka obizweni lwaso lwaphakade ngemva kokuyalwa kakhulu, ukuphenduka, nokuhlanzwa egazini lami lokubuyisana; futhi ngisamukele kimi othandweni lwami kwabaphendukayo kungakhathaliseki ukuthi bangobani abeza kimi ngenhliziyo ediliziwe. Futhi esithunyweni sami ngembula iNcwadi Encane uJohane uMambuli ayeyalwe ukuba ayivale ukuze yembulwe ngesikhathi sayo, le ncwadi equkethe izwi lemidumo eyisikhombisa, konke okuyiVangeli Laphakade, futhi umemezele amazwi ami ngokwethembeka njengoba ayeyaliwe, ubufakazi uJohane uMambuli abuloba futhi wabufakaza.

Ngingulowo ongenakhethe, ngisho uYeshua HaMashiach, futhi bonke abezayo kimi besuka ohlwini luka-Adamu ngaleyo ndlela ngiyobamukela, ngaphandle kwalabo abathatha uphawu lwesilo ngobuwula, noma abazishintsha ngaleyo ndlela. Ngiqinisile, ngiqinisile bonke abeza kimi ngaleyo ndlela abaphendukela ekuphileni okulungile bayoklonyeliswa kuze kube phakade futhi isisindo somvuzo wenu singesaphakade.

Futhi kunjalo zihlobo zami, ukuthi insindiso iyisipho sokuphila okuphakade, engasizuza mina Yeshua HaMashiach ngokunamathela kwami ngokwethembeka emthethweni kaMose nangomhlatshelo wami waphakade wami njengeWundlu likaNkulunkulu elingenasono neliphelele. Ngakho-ke, bonke abeza kimi ngokuphenduka okuphelele nokuthobeka nokulalela ngiyobabuyisela kimi nakuYahweh futhi ngiyobanika imisebenzi eminingi elungile uYahweh angathanda ukuba bayifeze ezinhlosweni zakhe zaphakade. Ngakho-ke, isipho sokuphila okuphakade sisezandleni zami kubo bonke abacelayo ngobumnene nangokuphenduka futhi abeza kimi ezikhathini ezinikezelwe ukuphenduka.

NginguYeshua HaMashiach, futhi ngiyanithanda bantu bakithi futhi ngiyeza ukuzonitakula futhi ngiyosindisa abalalelayo phakathi kwenu ngokuphelele, ngakho-ke lalelani amazwi kaMINA ENGINGUYE omkhulu ngokulalela uMesiya wenu weqiniso, okuyilapho iWundlu lihamba khona neBhubesi, ngoba ngiqinisile, nginguye.

Bhekani! Insindiso yakwa-Israyeli isondele futhi iWundlu lihlabelela ngokubambisana neBhubesi elibhongayo livikela ngolaka amawundlu alo, ngakho-ke, zilungiseleleni nina bakwa-Israyeli ngoba ukukhululwa kwenu kufikile.

Ngizweni othandweni lwami ngani, bantu bakithi, ngoba uMesiya wenu weqiniso useduze!
