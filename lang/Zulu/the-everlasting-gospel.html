Ubufakazi Obumbaxambili: Israyeli
Okubabayo Nokumnandi
uNcwaba 04, 2024

IVangeli Laphakade!

Zwanini amazwi kaYeshua HaMashiach, nithobele izindlebe zenu ngokulalela. Kanjalo-ke izinkathi zonyaka ziyashintsha, nosuku selufikile lapho inhlekelele iyokwehlela umhlaba kungazelelwe. Ngokuphazima kweso ngiyobuthela abathembekileyo bami kimi emafwini, futhi kungazelelwe incithakalo iyokwehlela emhlabeni wonke, futhi ngokuphazima kweso umhlaba eniwaziyo uyoguqulwa unomphela, ungasoze wabuyela kulesi simo esonakele nesinesono. Nakuba ukuphela kokubusa kukaSathane okugcwele isono sekuseduze, lolo suku aluyikufika ngokugcwele kuze kubhujiswe onke amandla, negunya likaDrako ekufikeni kwami kwesibili lapho ngiyofika khona ukuzobusa umhlaba ngokulunga namabutho asezulwini, yebo amabutho amakhulu asezulwini okuyothi phakathi kwawo kube khona izithelo zokuqala ezivela kwabafileyo, futhi ngiyozibuthela kimi.

Ngoba labo abayoba nami babiziwe, futhi bakhethiwe, bathembekile futhi baqinisile. Futhi bazibonakalise bethembekile ezintweni ezimbalwa, ngakho-ke ngiyobenza ababusi phezu kokuningi, bangene enjabulweni yeNkosi yabo. Bayoba yimpi engenakunqotshwa nephakade engeke neze inqotshwe noma imiswe, futhi bayonyathela ngokoqobo zonke izici zoMbuso wezitha kube yincithakalo ephelele nokunqotshwa kwazo zonke izinto ezimbi. Ngoba impindiselo ngeyami, isho iNkosi, futhi ngiyophindisela. Futhi mina, Yeshua HaMashiach, ngiyiNkosi yamakhosi noMbusi wababusi, futhi ngokulunga ngiyeza ukuzokwahlulela nokulwa impi.

Ngakho-ke yizwani amazwi ami niqonde ithemba layo yonke indalo, ngoba ngisho nendalo ilindele ngokulangazela ukubonakaliswa kwamaDodana kaNkulunkulu! Ngoba indalo yabekwa ngaphansi kokuyize, kungengokuthanda kwayo, kodwa ngenxa yalowo owayibeka ngaphansi kwethemba: Ngoba indalo uqobo lwayo iyokhululwa ekugqilazweni kokonakala, iye enkululekweni ekhazimulayo yabantwana bakaNkulunkulu. Kanjalo-ke mina, Jesu Kristu, ngiyokhulula indalo ngokulunga futhi ngiyophulukisa umhlaba, futhi ngokulunga ngiyobusa. Futhi ngiyochitha lonke igunya nokubusa kwesitha, nombuso kaSathane awusoze waphinde uvuke.

Njengoba kulotshiwe, kuyokuba njalo, kanjalo-ke ngenza ukuba uJohane umAmbulikamshini alobe: Ngase ngibona izulu livulekile, bheka, nanto ihhashi elimhlophe, nohlezi phezu kwalo ubizwa ngokuthi oThembekileyo noQinisileyo, futhi ngokulunga uyahlulela alwe nempi. Amehlo akhe ayenjengelangabi lomlilo, nasekhanda lakhe kwakukhona imiqhele eminingi, futhi wayenegama elilotshiweyo, elingaziwa muntu ngaphandle kwakhe. Wayembethe ingubo ecwiliswe egazini, negama lakhe kuthiwa, uLizwi likaNkulunkulu. Futhi amabutho ayesemazulwini amlandela egibele amahhashi amhlophe, embethe ilineni elicolekileyo, elimhlophe nelihlanzekileyo. Nasemlonyeni wakhe kwaphuma inkemba ebukhali, ukuze ngayo ashaye izizwe: futhi uyozibusa ngentonga yensimbi: futhi unyathela isikhamo sewayini sentukuthelo enkulu nolaka lukaNkulunkulu uMninimandla onke. Futhi unegama elilotshiwe engutsheni yakhe, nasethangeni lakhe, elithi, INKOSI YAMAKHOSI, NOMBUSI WABABUSI. Kanjalo-ke ithemba lokubuya kwami kwesibili, futhi lolo suku luyasondela kakhulu ngomzuzwana nomzuzwana odlulayo.

Bhekani! Ngalolo suku ngiyoletha amaqhawe ami ngoba lelo yifa lawo, ibutho elingafani nelinye, ngoba ibutho elinjengelami alikaze libe khona, ibutho elingenakunqotshwa elakhiwe ngabahlengiweyo, yebo labo ababezalwe befile eziphambekweni nasezonweni kodwa manje abazophila ukuphila okuphakade nabangeke babulawe noma banqotshwe. Ngoba nginikwe amandla nguYahweh ngenjongo enjalo, futhi ngiyobe sengibanikile amandla ngibavuza ngokuthembeka kwabo nangothando lwabo kimi negama lami, yebo uJesu uKristu! Ngoba lowo othanda futhi okhonza uYeshua HaMashiach uthanda futhi akhonze noYahweh, ngoba akekho omkhulu kunoNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade ka-Israyeli! Ngalolo suku olusondelayo niyobona amazulu evulekile, futhi niyobona mina, Jesu Kristu, ngihola ngiphuma emazulwini lelo butho elikhulu nelinamandla lasezulwini, futhi kuyoba yimpi engafani nenye, nanini! Ngoba uMlobokazi wami uyoba nami.

Futhi ngiyosichoboza nya isilo nomprofethi wamanga nawo onke amabutho abo nazo zonke izinengiso zabo abazenze ngendlela enyanyekayo, kanjalo-ke bayobhujiswa ngokukhanya kokufika kwami nangaleyo nkemba ebukhali ezinhlangothini zombili ephuma emlonyeni wami, futhi abayikuma kodwa bayobhujiswa ngokuphelele. Futhi isilo nomprofethi wamanga bayophonswa bephila kulelo chibi elivuthayo lomlilo nesibabule futhi abasoze bakhululwa ekuhlushweni kwabo, ngoba bazenze bafaneleka. Kodwa kuqala umhlaba kumele uhlanzwe ngokuhlupheka kukaJakobe futhi abaningi bayoza kimi, yebo izixuku eziningi, nawo wonke umphefumulo obiza egameni leNkosi kuze kube sekupheleni uyosindiswa ngokuphelele, futhi no-Israyeli kumele abuthelwe ngoba ngisazokusindisa insali kaJakobe esazofika kuMesiya wabo weqiniso nowaphakade, ngoba mina nginguYeshua HaMashiach futhi ngiyeza njengokwezithembiso koBaba ezamenyezelwa endulo nguYahweh uqobo, futhi mina ngiyikho ukugcwaliseka kwazo futhi nesikhathi sami sesifikile.

Ngakho-ke kulabo abalile igama lami elisindisayo, yebo uJesu waseNazaretha, ngiyaneluleka ukuba nizwe amazwi ami niqonde izikhathi eniphila kuzo nigijimele ekuzwakaleni kwezwi lami nibize egameni lami khona manje nivume ukuthi ngiyiNkosi yenu, ngoba aninaso isikhathi sokuchitha. Njengoba nje kwakukhona labo ababengamazibulo ekuzalweni okusha ekuzalweni kwebandla lami ngosuku lwePhentekoste njengoba kulotshiwe emibhalweni, kanjalo-ke kuyoba khona nabokugcina abayofika ekuzalweni okusha kulesi sikhathi somusa, yebo bonke abafika ensindisweni njengoba ukuthululwa kwami komoya ongcwele kulesi sikhathi sami somusa nesihe kufinyelela esiphelweni.

Kanjalo-ke ukuswela kwazo zonke izinto zobunkulunkulu manje kuzokwehlela emhlabeni futhi isono manje ngeke sibe nazithiyo njengoba ngisusa umvimbeli wami lapho ngibutha iBandla lami. Umuntu wemvelo uliphikile izwi lami, umuntu wemvelo uwulahlile umusa wami, umuntu wemvelo uwulahlile umhawu wami, umuntu wemvelo usenqabile isimemo sami sokulunga, futhi umuntu wemvelo ungiphikile mina Jesu waseNazaretha, ngakho-ke umuntu wemvelo umphikile uYahweh uNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade owangithuma, futhi manje ngizonika umhlaba ubumnyama ababufisa kangaka.

Kodwa qondani ukuthi abaningi bayokwenza imisebenzi emikhulu ngenxa yami, nofakazi bami ababili, yebo izinti zami ezimbili zezibani, bayoprofetha, futhi ngiyothuma abayizi-144,000 bami ukuba basindise abaningi, ngakho-ke ningesabi! Insindiso iyofezwa, ngisho nangokubhujiswa kwenyama, futhi izixuku ezinkulu ziyofika ensindisweni ngalesi sikhathi. Ngoba ngeqiniso, uYahweh akakaze futhi ngeke azishiye engenabufakazi!

Izikhathi zabeZizwe zithele izithelo eziningi phakathi kwalabo abebeyohlala bengenathemba ngaphandle kokuba babe yingxenye yezithembiso ezenziwa koBaba, yebo labo abaphila ngokukholwa eNdodaneni kaNkulunkulu eyabathanda yazinikela ngenxa yensindiso yabo. Ngoba njengoba kulotshiwe: Olungileyo uyophila ngokukholwa! Kanjalo-ke ngokukholwa kimi, uKristu Jesu, sebengabantwana bokukholwa, yebo abantwana bokukholwa kuka-Abrahama. Ngoba u-Abrahama wakholwa uNkulunkulu ngaphambi kokuba kunikezwe uphawu lokusoka, kwabalelwa kuye kube ngukulunga, njengoba noMose efakaza! Kanjalo-ke u-Abrahama unguyise wabo bonke abakholwayo nalabo abenziwe balunga ngokukholwa kuJesu Kristu oLungileyo, ngoba mina ngingolungileyo nomenzi walabo abalungileyo bonke abakholwa egameni lami elinamandla!

Nokho abaningi basebenzise umusa wami nesihe sami njengemvume yokona, bejabulela futhi beziqhayisa ngezindlela zabo ezinesono nezichithayo, baze bafundisa nabantwana babo ukuba baphile izindlela zabo ezinesono, kanjalo-ke izizukulwane zimbi ezindleleni zazo nezindlela zazo ziyanengeka, kanjalo abantu ababi nabakhohlisi sebeqhubeka beba babi kakhulu. Ningakhohliswa, uma umuntu ehlwanyela enyameni lowo muntu uyovuna ukonakala enyameni. Futhi bonke abenqaba isipho sami sokuphila okuphakade esikhundleni salokho bakhethe ukonakala kuka-Adamu bayovuna isithelo salokho konakala ekwahlulelweni kwaphakade, ngoba ukwahlulela sekufikile nezikhathi zokwembula sezifikile.

Ngeqiniso, ngeqiniso, izikhathi zokwahlulela okukhulu okwaprofethwa ngazo endulo ngu-Enoke umbhali (ngoba ubufakazi bakhe banikelwa kulesi sizukulwane), zifikile emhlabeni njengoba kulotshiwe futhi bonke bayolandisa. Labo abamukele isipho sami sensindiso nabatholakala bethembekile kuKristu ekubonakaleni kwami bayoguqulwa unomphela, kungakhathaliseki ukuthi balele kuKristu noma baphilile futhi bahlala kuKristu, labo abakuKristu ekubonakaleni kwami bayothathwa bayiswe ezulwini lamazulu. Ukuba kuKristu kungukuhamba ngomoya wami onamandla, yebo umthetho womoya wokuphila! Futhi labo abaphila ngokulunga ngaphambi kokuzalwa kweBandla lami nakubangcwele bosizi lukhulu kuwukuvuka kwabalungileyo, lapho abalungileyo beyovukela ebuntshani bokuphila emvuzweni omkhulu, waphakade nonaphakade. Ekupheleni kokubusa kwami kweminyaka eyi-1000 kuyoba khona ukwahlulela esihlalweni sobukhosi esikhulu esimhlophe, futhi bonke labo abaphila futhi bajabulela ukungalungi nesono bayovuzwa ngokwemisebenzi yabo, futhi abaningi bayowela ekufeni kwesibili ngoba bakhethe kanjalo.

Kanjalo-ke izikhathi zokwembula zifikile, nevangeli laphakade selambulwe ngesikhathi salo, yebo incwadi encane esesandleni sengelosi yami enamandla manje isiyambulwe, nokukhuluma kwemidumo eyisikhombisa sekumenyezelwe! Ngoba lo msebenzi ungumsebenzi waphakade, futhi uwumvuthwandaba wezivumelwano nezithembiso zasendulo uba yizivumelwano nezithembiso ezintsha, yebo iTestamente lami Elisha engiliqinisile ngegazi lami. Ngoba mina Yeshua HaMashiach, yebo uJesu Kristu oLungileyo oyiNkosi yamakhosi noMbusi wababusi, ngingulowo onikelwe ukugcwalisa zonke izithembiso nazo zonke izivumelwano.

Ngoba uJohane umAmbulikamshini waloba lokho akubona, kanjalo waloba: Ngase ngibona enye ingelosi enamandla yehla ivela ezulwini, yembethe ifu, uthingo lwenkosazana lwaluphezu kwekhanda layo, nobuso bayo bunjengelanga, nezinyawo zayo zinjengezinsika zomlilo. Futhi yayiphethe incwadi encane ivuliwe esandleni sayo: yabeka unyawo lwayo lwesokunene olwandle, nolwesobunxele emhlabeni. Futhi futhi uJohane umAmbulikamshini wamemezela: Ngase ngibona enye ingelosi indiza emkhathini wezulu, inevangeli laphakade, ukulishumayeza labo abahlezi emhlabeni, nasezizweni zonke, nasemindenini yonke, nasezilimini zonke, nakubantu bonke, isho ngezwi elikhulu, yesabani uNkulunkulu, nimnike inkazimulo, ngoba ihora lokwahlulela kwakhe selifikile: nimkhonze lowo owenza izulu nomhlaba, nolwandle, nemithombo yamanzi.

Kanjalo kulotshiwe kanjalo kwenziwe. Ivangeli laphakade selizokwembulwa ngesikhathi salo nencwadi encane ayiseyona imfihlakalo. Ingelosi enamandla eyembethe ifu nothingo lwenkosazana oluphezu kwekhanda layo nobuso bayo obunjengelanga nezinyawo zayo ezinjengezinsika zomlilo. Uthingo lwenkosazana luphawu olunikezwa esivumelwaneni sikaNowa uYahweh asenza noNowa ngemuva kukazamcolo sokuthi ngeke aphinde akhukhule umhlaba wonke. Ifu nezinsika zomlilo zimele isivumelwano sikaNkulunkulu noMose, esakhelwe phezu kwezivumelwano zangaphambili. Ngoba lapho uMose esekhulumile yonke imiyalo kubo bonke abantu ngokomthetho, wathatha igazi lamathole nelezimbuzi, namanzi, noboya obubomvu, nehisopi, wafafaza kokubili incwadi, nabo bonke abantu, kanjalo iTestamente Elidala laqiniswa!

Futhi njengoba kulotshiwe kumaHebheru: Ngoba uma igazi lezinkunzi nelezimbuzi, nomlotha wesithokazi ufafazwa phezu kwabangcolileyo, lingcwelisa ukuze kuhlanzwe inyama: Kakhulu kangakanani-ke igazi likaKristu, owazinikela ngaphandle kwebala kuNkulunkulu ngoMoya waphakade, liyohlanza unembeza wenu emisebenzini efileyo ukuze nikhonze uNkulunkulu ophilayo? Futhi ngenxa yalesi sizathu ungumlamuleli wetestamente elisha, ukuze ngokufa, kube yisihlengo seziphambeko ezaziphansi kwetestamente lokuqala, labo ababiziweyo bamukele isithembiso sefa laphakade.

Futhi njengoba kulotshiwe kunjalo kugcwalisekile, bathembekile bami, kanjalo neTestamente Elisha. Futhi njengoba kungu-El Shaddai owala ukuba ukukhanya kukhanye ebumnyameni ekuqaleni, ngendlela efanayo uNkulunkulu uMninimandla onke ukhanyisile ezinhliziyweni zethu ukunikeza ukukhanya kolwazi lwenkazimulo kaNkulunkulu, ebusweni bukaJesu Kristu. Kunjalo-ke, esivumelwaneni sami esisha esinamandla amakhulu, njengoba ngasiqinisa ngegazi lami elingenasono, kugcwaliseka zonke izivumelwano zazo zonke izikhathi, ngoba ngasho eKalvari ekuphefumuleni kwami kokugcina: Sekuphelile. Kanjalo isivumelwano esisha saqiniswa ngegazi lami saba yiTestamente Elisha!

Kanjalo kimi, uKristu Jesu, izivumelwano zasendulo zigcwaliseka ngenkazimulo yobuso obuvulekile, yebo ebusweni boMsindisi noMesiya wenu waphakade, yebo mina Yeshua HaMashiach. Futhi le ngelosi uJohane umAmbulikamshini ayichazayo yayinonyawo olulodwa olwandle nolunye emhlabeni ngoba insindiso yami ivulekele bobabili labo abavela olwandle lwezizwe, (abeZizwe) nalabo abavela ezweni lesithembiso yebo bonke abangenzile iNkosi yabo yaphakade, yebo abaningi njengoba iNkosi uNkulunkulu wethu iyobabiza! Kanjalo-ke ivangeli lami laphakade liyothola amehlo, izindlebe, nezinhliziyo zabo bonke elithunyelwe kubo, futhi abaningi bayofika ensindisweni ngoba lonke izwi likaNkulunkulu liyaphila futhi lihlala njalo, futhi alikho nelilodwa izwi likaYahweh eliyoke lihluleke noma lidlule kuze kugcwaliseke konke. Ngoba kuYahweh zonke izithembiso zinguyebo no-amen, futhi konke kuzogcwaliseka, futhi mina, Kristu Jesu, ngazinikela ekuhlushweni okubuhlungu ukuze ngivale isivumelwano sami esisha ngegazi lami elingenasono, kanye kuze kube phakade, futhi itestamente elisha liyophila kuze kube phakade.

Ngakho yizwani amazwi ami niqonde inkulumo yami, yebo izwi leNkosi noMesiya wenu. Izikhathi zifikile bantwana bami futhi amazwi ami agcwele umhlaba, nabaprofethi bami nezikhonzi bami basibekele imikhawulo yomhlaba, futhi kuzo zonke izilimi, nezilimi zomdabu, nezizwe ivangeli lami lensindiso lizwakele, yebo kuze kube semikhawulweni yomhlaba. Kunjalo, futhi ngiyamemezela: Izindaba zami ezinhle zihlale zakhelwe phezu kwesisekelo somthetho nabaprofethi futhi mina ngiyitshe legumbi eliyinhloko lapho lonke ithempeli likaNkulunkulu ophilayo lisekelwe khona! Bantwana bami, nina niyithempeli likaNkulunkulu ophilayo!

Kanjalo ngininika ukuqonda ukuthi kunezingxenye eziningi zevangeli likaKristu! Ningaba yiziwula kangako ukukholwa ukuthi kunamavangeli amane kuphela? Futhi kunezinye izinto eziningi engazenza, ezingathi, uma bezingalotshwa zonke ngazinye, ngicabanga ukuthi ngisho nomhlaba uqobo ubungeke uziqukathe izincwadi ebezizolotshwa. Kunjalo-ke kukhona izindaba ezinhle zokufika kwami kokuqala, kukhona izindaba ezinhle zemfihlakalo kaNkulunkulu engayembulela uPawulu uMphostoli, kukhona ivangeli laphakade elisiholela emvuthwandabeni wazo zonke izinto. KuYahweh akukho ukuphela kwezindaba ezinhle!

Ngakho-ke nginibuza umbuzo? Ningabeluleki bakaYahweh? Izazi zenu zenkolo eziqamba amanga nezikhonzi zenu ezisivivi ziyakwazi yini ukweluleka uYahweh? Ungubani wena, muntu, ukuba unqume ukuthi uYahweh ukhuluma nini futhi kanjani, nokuqukethwe nemvelo yamaZwi akhe, noma ukuthi ukhetha ukuthumela amazwi akhe kubani? Kunjalo-ke. Amazwi kaNkulunkulu ayoqhubeka egeleza, nabo bonke ubufakazi buyoqedwa, nezincwadi ziyolotshwa kuze kube phakade ngoba iZwi likaNkulunkulu liyaphila futhi lihlala phakade!

Futhi ngiyanibuza, ingabe iTestamente Elisha likaKristu Jesu lalisulile iTestamente Elidala nezivumelwano ezindala? UNkulunkulu makube kude! Lonke iZwi likaNkulunkulu lihlelekile futhi konke kuyogcwaliseka kanye, konke, naphakade! Kunjalo-ke imvelo kaNkulunkulu ongaguquki! Esihlalweni sakhe sobukhosi kunezinkulungwane eziyishumi izikhathi eziyizinkulungwane eziyishumi kodwa akadingi meluleki. UYahweh, uNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade, wenza zonke izinto ngokweluleka kwentando yakhe futhi unguNkulunkulu waphakade! Kodwa uzembulile izinhlelo zakhe, yebo intando yakhe yaphakade nengunaphakade ngezigaba. Ungabuza ukuthi kungani uYahweh enza into enjalo? Awukafundi yini lokho uPawulu uMphostoli akuloba lapho ngimenza waloba? Kodwa sikhuluma ukuhlakanipha kukaNkulunkulu okuyimfihlakalo, yebo ukuhlakanipha okufihliwe, uNkulunkulu akuhlele ngaphambi kwezwe, kube yinkazimulo yethu. Okungaziwa yinoma yisiphi isikhulu saleli zwe: ngoba ukube babekwazi, babengeke bambethele iNkosi yenkazimulo.

Kanjalo uNkulunkulu waphakade, yebo uYahweh ongadingi meluleki nasekuhlakanipheni kwakhe kwaphakade wembula izindlela zakhe namaZwi ngendlela ayikhethayo ekugcwalisekeni kwezinhlelo zakhe zaphakade zokuhlenga isintu. Qondani zithandwa zami, yebo Mlobokazi wami, ukuthi benisezinhliziyweni zikaYahweh kusukela ngaphambi kokudalwa kwezwe futhi nifihlwe ngobuhlakani enhliziyweni kaBaba futhi kufihliwe kini lokho eniyoba yikho ekubonakaleni kwami, ngoba nifile nokuphila kwenu kufihliwe kimi uKristu Jesu. Futhi lapho ngivela, nani niyovela nami enkazimulweni! Bantwanyana bami senizodalwa ngokusha ngokuphelele kimi futhi niyomangala ngalokho uYahweh akulungiselele labo abamthandayo. Ngoba njengoba kunjalo akekho umuntu emhlabeni oke waqonda ngokugcwele ukuthi amadodana kaNkulunkulu ayoba yini ekuhlenjweni kwawo okuphelele, futhi senizokuthola ngoba usuku selufikile nokubonakala kwami kuseduze.

Ngeluleka oyedwa ngeluleka bonke, yebo uKristu Jesu iNkosi yenu yaphakade! Yamukelani amaZwi ami nizitholele ukuhlakanipha nokuqonda, ngoba ubuntu benu beqiniso buyimfihlakalo yaphakade efihliwe kuNkulunkulu uqobo esezokwembulwa ngokugcwele. Maduzane kakhulu nizozithola nimi egumbini lesihlalo sobukhosi likaYahweh, yebo uBaba wethu, futhi ulindele ngokulangazela ukuba khona kwenu njengamanje, yebo kusukela emaphakadeni adlule. Futhi amahholo amakhulu ezulwini alungiselelwe ukufika kwenu namatafula asedekiwe nomshado woMyeni noMlobokazi wakhe useduze futhi umlobokazi wami usezilungisile futhi ngiyobamukela kimi futhi bayohlala nami phakade esivumelwaneni saphakade futhi bayokwenza imisebenzi eminingi emikhulu engakaze ingene ezinhliziyweni zabantu ngalesi sikhathi.

Kanjalo uYahweh uNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade unihlelele futhi wanilungiselela izinto eziningi, futhi mina, Kristu Jesu iNkosi ephakanyisiwe futhi enyukile, nginomvuzo wenu esandleni sami yebo abathembekileyo bami. Futhi ibutho lasezulwini lilindele ngokulangazela ukuba khona kwenu futhi manje senizoya emvuzweni wasezulwini nowaphakade. Ngakho ningesabi ngoba izimpilo zenu manje sezizokwembulwa futhi nganoma yisiphi isikhathi manje icilongo lokugcina lizokhala, nilungele yini ukubonakala kwami bantwanyana bami? Ngoba kusondele kunalokho enikucabangayo nesikhathi esiphakathi kwaleso sikhathi namanje asibalulekile futhi sikhonza izinhloso zaphakade zikaYahweh uNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade!

Anazi yini? noma anizwanga yini, ukuthi uNkulunkulu waphakade, iNkosi, udale imikhawulo yomhlaba? akadangali, futhi akakhathali, futhi akukho ukuphenywa kokuqonda kwakhe.
