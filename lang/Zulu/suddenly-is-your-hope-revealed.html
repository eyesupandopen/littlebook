Ngokuphazima Kweso Ithemba Lenu Liyembulwa!

Buthanani nizwe izwi leNkosi yenu, lona izwi likaJesu waseNazaretha ngokuba yimi owazinikela kanye, ngokuphelele, naphakade njengeWundlu lePhasika elipheleleyo kuYahweh ngenxa yezono zenu, yena uNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade, njengoMpristi wenu Ophakeme kuze kube phakade ngokohlelo lukaMelkisedeki ukuze nithole ukuthethelelwa kwezono. Wozani kimi, nilethe izindlebe zenu nezinhliziyo zenu ukuze nilalele ngokukholwa okuphelele emazwini ami okukholwa! Yebo ngiyanicela ukuba nilalele, kungabi nje ukuzwa imisindo yamazwi ami kodwa nilalele ngezinhliziyo zenu ukuze niqonde inhloso yami nithole induduzo othandweni enginithanda ngalo, nokuba namukele ngobumnene izwi elixhunywe kini, elinamandla okusindisa imiphefumulo yenu.

Ngokuba ngikhuluma nabakholwayo eBandleni lami, wona umzimba wami, yena umakoti wami, bona bonke engisengizobabuthela kimi ekuphepheni kwamazulu esihlalweni sobukhosi sikaYahweh. Ngokuba yimi onakekela imiphefumulo yenu nonakekela ukukhula kwenu okuqhubekayo ukuze nikhule njengesitshalo esilungileyo phambi kwami, ngokuba yimi uYahweh anginikeze ukuba ngibe ngumlimi nomnakekeli wensimu yakhe engcwele kakhulu yemiphefumulo yabahlengiweyo. Ngokuba nina niyiparele lenani elikhulu, naphakathi kwengcebo eningi kaYahweh niphakathi kwezinto ezingenakuqhathaniswa, okungekho tshe elingaphendulwanga ensindisweni yenu futhi akukho mzamo owagwenywa ukuze nithenwe, ninakekelwe, futhi nethulwe kuYahweh, uYihlo uSomandla, njengentombi emsulwa engenabala, noma umbimbi, nanoma yini enjalo. Ngakho ningesabi, futhi ningavumeli ukungabaza ngisho nakwelincane lamazwi ami!

Ngakho ngiyanibuza, zithandwa zami, kungani izinhliziyo zenu zinihlupha? Kungani ninikina amakhanda enu sengathi nithwele ihlazo? Ingabe nivumele oSathane bangene ezinhliziyweni zenu bahlebe amazwi amanga enduduzo aveza ukungakholwa kuphela, njengoba befuna ukudabula nokuklebhula konke okungcwele? Niyozithathela yini leyo nyoka yasendulo ngobuqili bayo obuqamba amanga njalo nenkohliso yayo eqhubekela phambili, ezonyenyezela ezindlebeni zenu wona lawo mazwi amanga ayochitha ukholo lwenu, njengoba ngempela sekwenzekile kwabaningi kangaka abavumele izingqondo zabo ukuba zonakaliswe ekugxileni okukuphela kwakho kimi, uKristu Jesu iNkosi yenu?

Ngokuba akekho omunye oyoninakekela, bonke abanye bangabazenzisi abazenzisayo, futhi akekho omunye uJesu ngaphandle kwezilingiso ezingatheni nezingelusizo nezemali-mbumbulu ezenzelwe ukukhohlisa. Ngakho ngiyanicela ukuba ningakushayi indiva lokhu kuqonda enginganinika khona ukuze niqhubeke nime njengophawu lokukhanya ngisho nakule mizuzu yokugcina ngaphambi kokuba umhlaba eniwaziyo unyamalale futhi uguqulwe unomphela ususwe ekungamesabini uNkulunkulu kwamanje uye ekwahlulelweni kwaphakade okuyokwehlela manje njengesijeziso saphakade phezu kwabangamesabi uNkulunkulu abafuna ukuthatha ifa lami. Ngokuba umhlaba ungowami nokugcwala kwawo, futhi ngiyiNkosi enkulu yaphakade yamakhosi neNkosi yamakhosi, yona iNkosi yenu elungileyo futhi yimi engizothatha ifa lami ezandleni zezigebengu ezingenalo ilungelo futhi ezingeyona amakhosi futhi ezingebona onkulunkulu futhi ezingebona abafokazi abavela kweminye imihlaba kodwa abawileyo abazenzisayo ngokuba bonke bangabazenzisi abazenzisayo. Futhi izinsuku zabo ziyokwenziwa isiphelo saphakade efeni labo laphakade emalangabini ashisa kakhulu aluhlaza.

Ngokuba imihelo manje ayiphezi, futhi kunezibonakaliso eziningi phezulu nezibonakaliso eziningi emhlabeni ezibonakala njalo nezingapheli. Lezi zinikezwa njengesimemezelo nokuba nibe nethemba, ngakho ningazideleli futhi ningazichitheli ngendlela yabaklolodayo! Umsebenzi wezandla zikaYahweh owenziwe ngezandla zezingelosi zakhe ezinamandla, futhi ngempela odwetshwe ngomunwe kaNkulunkulu uqobo ngezinye izikhathi, ngisho nangomyalo wakhe waphakade, umemezela lezi zigameko zasezulwini ezisondelayo, ngokuba usuku selufikile futhi phezu kwenu zithandwa zami, ngakho ningesabi! Izikhathi sezifikile zokubuthwa kwabakholwayo bami ukuba bahlangane nami emafwini njengoba ngehla ezulwini ngezwi lengelosi enkulu nangecilongo elikhulu likaNkulunkulu, ngakho kholwani amazwi ami ngokuba anguyebo no-amen!

Ubani futhi yini ekhona okufanele inikhathaze bantabami? Niyesaba yini ukuthi umkhwenyana ngeke afike athathe isandla somakoti wakhe esivumelwaneni saphakade njengoba kwathenjiswa endulo? Ingabe izwi likaNkulunkulu seliphenduke ize? UNkulunkulu makube kude! Njengoba kulotshiwe futhi kwamenyezelwa, ngokuqinisekile kuyokwenzeka ukuthi ngokuphazima kweso ngizonihlwitha olakeni nginiyise ekuphepheni kwamazulu lapho ngiye khona ukunilungisela indawo, ngokuba endlini kaBaba kukhona izindlu eziningi. Kanjalo niyohlala nami njalo, kanjalo niyoba ngamakhosi nabapristi phambi kukaYahweh uNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade, ngokuba uhlose futhi walungisa kanjalo ezimpilweni zenu, futhi ubani ongamisa izinhloso namalungiselelo kaNkulunkulu waphakade?

Nginitshela iqiniso, akukho lutho futhi akekho ongangena ngisho nakancane ezinhlosweni zika-El Shaddai, futhi akekho ongama (ngisho nangokuphikisa okuncane kakhulu) ngaphandle kokuthuliswa ngokwesaba emandleni aphelele, enkazimulweni nasekuthuthumeleni okuvela lapho uYahweh enyakaza kancane esuka esihlalweni sakhe sobukhosi saphakade, futhi lonke izulu nomhlaba kuyazamazama phambi kobukhona bakhe obunamandla. Ngokuba nakuba uYahweh ezifihlile ngobumnene, ngothando nangozwelo izinsuku eziningi manje (ngokuba usuku lunye lunjengeminyaka eyinkulungwane emehlweni kaNkulunkulu waphakade) manje yisikhathi uYahweh asikhethile lapho ezokwembula khona yena endalweni yakhe, lapho bonke beyokwazi ukuthi kukhona uNkulunkulu ezulwini, futhi ngokuqinisekile bayoqonda ukuthi njengoba kunjalo phezulu, kuyoba njalo nangezansi emhlabeni ngokuba uYahweh unguNkulunkulu wazo zombili izulu nomhlaba futhi ubusa ngokuphelele phezu kwendalo yakhe futhi akekho omunye ngaphandle kwakhe ngamandla, ngenkazimulo nangamandla. (Futhi abawileyo babanjwe ukwesaba okuphakade) Ngakho ningesabi Bantwana boPhezukonke ngokuba uNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade, yena uYahweh uqobo, nguyena onilwelayo futhi nguyena oyokhulula, ahlenga, futhi aguqule izimpilo zenu zibe yinkazimulo yaphakade ngokuphazima kweso.

Ngakho kungani niphelelwa isineke? Ingabe nizizwa sengathi iNkosi yenu yaphakade nomkhwenyana unishiyile, makoti wami? Ingabe umsindisi wenu waphakade uwushiyile umzimba wakhe, wawushiya ungenakhanda? UNkulunkulu makube kude ukuba into enjalo icatshangwe. Yiluphi udaba uYahweh alimemezele endalweni yakhe njengolusondelayo olungazange lwenzeke ngemva kwalokho? Ingabe amazwi kaNowa awela emhlabathini owomile njengezithembiso eziyize lapho wayengumshumayeli wokulunga iminyaka eyikhulu namashumi amabili emhlabeni wangaleso sikhathi? Cha! Kodwa ngokuphazima kweso nangodlame olukhulu amanzi adedelwa ngomzuzwana futhi enyuka ngaphezu kwezintaba eziphakeme kakhulu emhlabeni ezinsukwini ezalandela.

Ngakho nginiyala ukuba ningaphinde nijoyine izinhlu zalabo abangazi ngokuzithandela. Bakhetha ngokuzithandela ukungazi ukuze bazibe ukuthi ngezwi likaNkulunkulu amazulu ayekhona endulo, nomhlaba umi ngaphandle kwamanzi nasemanzini: Ngalokho umhlaba wangaleso sikhathi, wakhukhulwa ngamanzi, wabhubha: Kodwa amazulu nomhlaba, akhona manje, ngalo lona lelo zwi agcinelwe umlilo, alondolozelwe usuku lokwahlulelwa nokubhujiswa kwabantu abangamesabi uNkulunkulu. Ngalokho ngimemezela futhi ukuthi usuku lomlilo nokwahlulelwa nokubhujiswa kwabantu abangamesabi uNkulunkulu selufikile, njengoba kwathenjiswa endulo, kanjalo kuyokwenzeka ngokuphazima kweso.

Ngakho ngiyanibuza futhi, bantwanyana bami, uNkulunkulu ungumuntu yini ukuba aqambe amanga? Mina, uJesu Kristu olungileyo, angithembekile yini ukuze nginikhohlwe kodwa ngininike ithemba elingamanga? Nginikhohliwe yini futhi ngaya kodoba? Ngiyanibuza zithandwa zami, ngalinikela yini iconsi lami lokugcina legazi ebuhlungwini esiphambanweni ukuze nginishiye lapho nginithembise izithembiso ezinkulu kakhulu neziyigugu, zona izithembiso ezinikezwe nguYahweh uqobo em misterini wakhe waphakade, yona imfihlakalo enkulu kaNkulunkulu?

UNkulunkulu makube kude ukuba izinto ezinjalo zicatshangwe, ngakho hlonzani imicabango emibi kaSathane ayiphonsa kini, niyenqabe niyikhuze, bese nithatha indawo yaleyo micabango engenangqondo nemibi ngemibhalo engcwele, zona izithembiso zikaYahweh ezinguyebo no-amen. Kodwa ngazo zonke izikhathi futhi ikakhulukazi ngezikhathi ezinje kulotshiwe kanjalo uPawulu wabhalela abaseKorinte egameni lami: Kodwa ngiyesaba hleze njengoba inyoka yakhohlisa u-Eva ngobuqili bayo, kanjalo izingqondo zenu zonakaliswe ekugxileni okuphelele okukuKristu. Ngalokho nginiyala bantabami, nina enginithanda kakhulu nengikulangazelelayo, ukuba nigcine ukugxila kwenu okuphelele kimi, nasemazwini ami, ngokuba mina ngiyizwi likaNkulunkulu elenziwe inyama futhi lonke izwi likaNkulunkulu liyogcwaliseka ngempela, futhi akukho nelilodwa kwelincane lamazwi ami eliyohluleka alakhulunywa futhi laphefumulelwa ngalowo Moya oNgcwele omkhulu waphakade, onguYahweh, uNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade!

Ngalokho nginiyala bantabami, nina enginithanda kakhulu nengikulangazelelayo, nina eniyinjabulo nomqhele wami: phumulani kimi niphulule emisebenzini yami niyeke eyenu. Ngokuba kulotshiwe: Kusekhona-ke ukuphumula kwabantu bakaNkulunkulu. Ngokuba lowo ongenile ekuphumuleni kwakhe, naye uyekile emisebenzini yakhe, njengoba uNkulunkulu enza kweyakhe. Ngakho khuthalelani ukungena ekuphumuleni kwami, funa umuntu awele ngemuva kwesibonelo esifanayo sokungakholwa. Bantabami khuthalelani ukuyeka imisebenzi yenu nenze imisebenzi engininika yona ukuba niyifeze, ningenciki ekuqondeni kwenu kodwa kuzo zonke izindlela zenu ningamukele mina, uKristu Jesu iNkosi yenu enyukile, futhi niyothola ukuphumula phakathi kwamagquma aluhlaza amadlelo nokuphepha, ikakhulukazi ngaphansi kokunakekelwa nokuvikelwa kwengalo yami enamandla, ngokuba nginguYeshua HaMashiach onguMalusi wenu omkhulu osazobonakala!

Ngakho ngiyanibuza, zithandwa zami, niyobuyela yini ukuyohlala phakathi kweziwula eziklolodayo eziklolodayo futhi zifuna ukuniyengela ezindleleni zazo zokungakholwa? Niyowaziba yini amazwi ami ngisho nezibonakaliso eziningi, izimangaliso nezimangaliso eziningizungezile? Ngobuwula nicabanga ukuthi ningaqhubeka niphile njengalabo abangazi ngenhlekelele nokwahlulelwa okuzayo? Niyobuyela yini ekuhlaleni phakathi kwabahamba bejulile, abenqaba ukuphenduka bakhethe kunalokho ukwahlulelwa okuqinile nesijeziso? Nginitshela ngokusobala uma nizama niyozithola nifana neziwula eziqhubeka zidlula izixwayiso zami futhi zijeziswe ngokungenasidingo, kanjalo senixwayisiwe.

Qondani ukuthi umhlaba eniphila kuwo manje awufani nanjengoba wawunjalo ngisho emasontweni ambalwa edlule, futhi uyaqhubeka nokushintsha ngezinga elikhulayo. Qondani ukuthi akekho kini owake waphila emhlabeni okhona manje, ngokuba umhlaba eniphila kuwo manje uboshelwe ekwahlulelweni kodwa uvunyelwe ukukhipha izimemezelo ezikhazimulayo zomshado ozayo emazulwini lapho mina Kristu Jesu ngiyoshada khona unomphela nomakoti wami othembekile kanjalo bayohlala nami njalo. Futhi njengoba umakoti wami neBandla lami behlwithwa ngokuphazima kweso, khona-ke ukubhujiswa okungazelelwe kuyokwehlela bonke abaklolodayo nabantu ababi abanihleka usulu bethi: Kusasa kuyofana nanamuhla, nanamuhla kufana nezolo, futhi angiyikubona nhlekelele futhi angiyena umfelokazi kodwa ngiyindlovukazi ebusa phezu kwazo zonke izinto! Ngaphandle kokuthi ukubhujiswa kwabo kuyobafikela ngokuphazima kweso njengowesifazane omihelo, futhi ngeke baphunyuke.

Ngakho phumani phakathi kwabo nibe ngabahlukile kuzo ZONKE izinto nakuzo zonke izindlela zenu. Aninakuba nasabelo noma ingxenye nabo noma niyohlanganyela ezinhluphekweni zabo. Qondani ukuthi izigameko sezivele ziqhubeka, ngisho nezigameko eziyothinta izoni, kodwa ngizohlinzeka ngokuphepha kwabakholwayo bami ukuze lokhu kwahlulelwa kungani limazi bantabami. Ngokuba le mihelo engapheli ihloselwe ukuvusa abokugcina abalele nokuhlukanisa abokugcina abazogibela umkhumbi ngaphambi kokuba inhlekelele yehle ngokuphazima kweso futhi isikhathi siphele. Anisakwazi ukuphila izimpilo zokunganaki futhi kufanele nihambe ngokuhlakanipha okukhulu ngakho lalelani izixwayiso zami niqonde ukuthi ngininike ukuhlakanipha okukhulu nokuqonda ukuze nikwazi ukuqonda izikhathi eniphila kuzo, ngisho naphakathi kwalabo abalele ubuthongo obukhulu enhlekeleleni esondelayo futhi abayohlupheka ngenxa yalokho.

Ngakho qondani ukuthi isikhathi esisele asibalulekile futhi sisebenzela izinhloso zikaYahweh kuphela ngaphambi kokuba ukwahlulelwa kwehle ngokuphazima kweso. Yebo, isitha sinikezwa ithuba lokulungiselela ukuhlasela iBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo ngokuba lokho sinikwe kona nguYahweh ukuba sikwenze. Kodwa qondani ngokuphelele ukuthi uSathane uyisitha esehluliwe esiboshelwe emhlabeni ukuze ahlulelwe futhi uvunyelwe kuphela ukuqhubekisela phambili izinhloso zikaYahweh futhi uyohlala athule alinde umvimbeli ukuba asuswe futhi akukho lutho angalwenza ngaphezu kwalokho akuvunyelwe, ngakho ningesabi ukubhonga kwalelo bhubesi elingenamazinyo ngokuba isikhosana esincane siboshwe ekunqotshweni! Ngokuba ngeqiniso, abawileyo bayanesaba futhi bayaqonda futhi bayazi ukuthi labo abake babahlukumeza yibona abayoletha ukwahlulelwa nesijeziso esilungileyo ezifubeni zabo kuze kube phakade naphakade. Kanjani? Anazi yini ukuthi niyokwahlulela izingelosi?

Ngakho ngiyanibuza zithandwa zami, niyowenqaba yini manje umusa wami nomhawu wami owelulelwe kwabalahlekileyo, ngisho nalapho ukwahlulelwa nenhlekelele kuhlelelwe ukwehlela ngokuphazima kweso? Senikhohliwe yini iminyaka eminingi yomusa nomhawu enginganinika yona ngokungafanele ukuze nigcwalise izono zenu ngaphambi kokuphendukela ekulungeni? Niyobenqabela yini labo abafuna ukugibela umkhumbi ukuze sihlangane umzuzwana ngaphambili? Ngiqinisekile ngokungcono ngani, bantabami, ngakho ngicela ukubekezela kwenu nokubekezela, ngisho nangaphambi kokuba inhlekelele yehle ngokuphazima kweso.

Uma kukhona phakathi kwenu ocabanga ukuthi unesizathu sokuletha ubulungiswa phezu kukaSathane nombuso wakhe, nginesizathu esikhulu kunaleso esake sangena ezinhliziyweni zenu. Ngokuba ngubani kini olwe noSathane, kuze kube seconsini lenu lokugcina legazi njengoba nanilenga ngokuzithandela kuleso sigxobo esimaholoholo ebuhlungwini obukhulu. Ngokuba ngathwala ngokuzithandela isiphambano sami futhi ngazinikela ekunqobeni kwaphakade njengoba ngangonelwa ezandleni zabenzi bokubi, njengoba ngathatha indawo yenu ekwahlulelweni ebuhlungwini obukhulu ngaphandle kwamasango phakathi kwezoni ezinengekayo kakhulu. Igazi lami lalingenacala futhi akukho bubi obungabekwa ngokufanele ku-akhawunti yami. Kodwa othandweni lwami ngoBaba wami nothando lwami ngani, nganikela ngokuphila kwami ngokuzithandela futhi ngingaphinda ngikwenze ngomzuzwana, nakuba into enjalo ingadingeki. Ngakho ngisho nasebuhlungwini benu bamanje landelani isibonelo sami sokukhuthazela nazi ukuthi ngokuphazima kweso niyongena ekukhululweni kwaphakade!

Ngakho ngiyanicela ukuba niqhubeke niqinile kuze kube sekupheleni, sona isiphelo lapho nithathwa khona ngokuphazima kweso niye emazulwini emvuzweni waphakade nasenhlosweni yaphakade, nokho abafileyo kuKristu bayovuka kuqala futhi niyohlangana nabo ngokuphazima kweso emkhosini waphakade. Ngokuba izinhloso zaphakade enabizelwa zona, nakhethwa, nananyatheliswa, nagcinelwa zona ziyogcwalisa izinhloso zaphakade zikaNkulunkulu oPhezukonke futhi konke sekuzokwembulwa kini bantabami, ngakho khanyani ngenxa yami njengezibani zami khona manje kule nkathi yokugcina yokuma ngaphambi kokuba inhlekelele engazelelwe ishaye umhlaba. Futhi ngiyobaletha kini ukuze nibalethe kimi, ngokuba nisengamanxusa ami kuze kube yilapho nithathwa ngokuphazima kweso niye emazulwini. Futhi ngokuphazima kweso kuyokwehlela inhlekelele enkulu.

Qondani ukuthi isikhathi sesiphelile futhi akukho lutho olungabuyisela emuva izigameko ezizayo nezizihlelayo ngokumelene nabakhileyo emhlabeni, ngokuba kukhulu ukwahlulelwa okuzayo kwezoni. Ngakho ngiyanicela futhi ngiyanicindezela ngaso sona leso sihawu, umusa nomhawu uYahweh anibonise wona ebusweni bukaJesu Kristu ukuze nisindiswe. Ningahlala nithembekile kuze kube sekupheleni? Niyozivumela yini ukungena ekuphumuleni kwami kwaphakade ngaphandle kokuyeka ngomzuzu wokugcina? Niyogxilisa yini inhliziyo yenu yonke nokunaka emazwini ami niqonde ukuthi ngokuphazima kweso kuyoshintsha unomphela futhi ngokuphazima kweso sekuyimanje.

Asisekho isikhathi esisele sokuba iBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo liphenduke futhi asisekho isikhathi enkathini yami yomusa nomhawu ngokuba isikhathi sesiphelile. Futhi kuyo yonke inkathi kunesikhathi nenhloso futhi ekuphelelisweni kwezikhathi ukuhlengwa okungazelelwe kuyokwehlela abahlengiweyo ngakho mabasho abahlengiweyo bomhlaba abahlengile esandleni sesitha. Futhi ningalivumeli isela ukuba lintshontshe injabulo yenu ngokuba yimuphi umakoti olilela usuku lomshado wakhe, lona usuku lomshado olukhazimulayo, olungcwele okungekho muntu ongalonakalisa noma aluphazamise.

Ngakho duduzekani emazwini ami enduduzo niyeke ukuphila njengalabo abaphonswe phansi ngehlazo necala. Ngokuba nina makoti wami, niyilabo engibenze bangabi nabala futhi bangabi nasici phambi kwesihlalo sobukhosi sikaYahweh uqobo, futhi niyizithelo zokuqala kwabafileyo. Nakuba labo kini abaphilayo futhi abasele bengeke balizwe ukufa, nazalwa nifile ezonweni naseziphambekweni. Futhi niyobe nisukile ngokuqondile ekufeni (lapho ngokufa beniyokufa), naya ngqo ekuphileni kwaphakade lapho seniphila isibambiso sako, sona uphawu olukhulu lukaYahweh enananyatheliswa ngalo kulolo suku lokuhlengwa olungazelelwe. Ngokuba nizelwe ngokusha ngembewu engenakonakala futhi ningamadodana kaNkulunkulu oPhezukonke, yebo amadodana kaNkulunkulu uSomandla!

Ngakho-ke, duduzekani nibe nokuthula nisebenze nami kulo mzuliswano wokunqoba wezikhathi. Ngokuba akukaze kube nesikhathi emhlabeni esinje futhi ngeke kuphinde kube nesikhathi esinje futhi. Yebo isikhathi lapho amadodana aphakade kaNkulunkulu elindele ngokwazi ukuphefumula kwawo kokuqala kokuphila kwaphakade njengezinsana zaphakade ebukhoneni baphakade bukaNkulunkulu waphakade lapho eyoshada neNkosi yawo yaphakade esivumelwaneni saphakade, futhi... ngokuphazima kweso, kuyoba njalo!

Yimi owazinikela kanye, ngokuphelele, naphakade njengeWundlu lePhasika elipheleleyo.

Sengizobuthela abakholwayo kimi ekuphepheni kwamazulu esihlalweni sobukhosi sikaYahweh.

Yimi onakekela imiphefumulo yenu nonakekela ukukhula kwenu okuqhubekayo ukuze nikhule njengesitshalo esilungileyo.

Yimi uYahweh anginikeze ukuba ngibe ngumlimi nomnakekeli wensimu yakhe engcwele kakhulu yemiphefumulo yabahlengiweyo.

NgiyiNkosi enkulu yaphakade yamakhosi neNkosi yamakhosi, yona iNkosi yenu elungileyo.

Yimi engizothatha ifa lami ezandleni zezigebengu ezingenalo ilungelo.

Ngiyizwi likaNkulunkulu elenziwe inyama futhi lonke izwi likaNkulunkulu liyogcwaliseka ngempela.

NginguYeshua HaMashiach onguMalusi wenu omkhulu osazobonakala!

Mina, Yeshua HaMashiach, ngithumele lawa mazwi ngenceku yami, futhi ngenceku yami lawa mazwi athunyelwe!

Ngakho ngiyanikhuthaza zithandwa zami ukuba ningapheli amandla nihlale niqinile kuze kube sekupheleni okungazelelwe okuyosusa unomphela isikhathi senu emhlabeni esimweni senu samanje. Ngokuphazima kweso, nangokuphazima kweso niyozithola nimi esihlalweni sobukhosi sikaYahweh enjabulweni yaphakade futhi niyokuqonda ngokugcwele futhi nazi ngisho njengoba niqondwa ngokugcwele futhi naziwa. Kanjalo isithembiso siyohlangana nokugcwaliseka enjabulweni yaphakade nasenkazimulweni yaphakade enilindele futhi konke kuyogcwaliseka kini ngokuphazima kweso, futhi kuyokwenzeka ngokuphazima kweso. Hlalani kimi bantabami ngokuba nginithanda kakhulu futhi anginamahloni okuthi ningabafowethu.
