Lalelani, lalelani amazwi kaYeshua HaMashiach, njengoba ngiza ukukhuluma nebandla lami, umzimba wami, ngisho nomakoti wami kanye noJakobe ngokwenyama, engingumhlengi wakhe oyisihlobo. Ngoba niyofunda lawa mazwi ngezikhathi lapho kufundwa khona, kungakhathaliseki ukuthi yiziphi lezo zikhathi, ukuze nibe nokholo olukhulu ekuhlengweni kwenu okuphelele manje osekufike emhlabeni wonke, kanye nokuthi ngiyohlanza umhlaba ngokuphelele phakathi nesonto lamashumi ayisikhombisa likaDaniyeli kukho konke okufezile labo abawileyo, noma kuphi lapho basebenzele ukufeza kukho kuyo yonke indalo ka-Elohim.

Lalelani, lalelani amazwi kaYeshua HaMashiach: Futhi nikhothame ngokuthobeka, nigxilise izinhliziyo zenu ngokugcwele othandweni ukuze namukele lawa mazwi ngabo bonke ubumnene benhliziyo.

Lalelani, lalelani amazwi kaYeshua HaMashiach: Futhi gxilisani izindlebe zenu ngokugcwele emsindweni wamazwi ami, ngoba imijinge yawo iyonihlambulula ngokuphelele ekuhlanekezelweni eniqondiswe kukho ngabalindi abawileyo, abahlanganyela noHeylel Ben Shachar ekukhanukeni kwabo.

Lalelani amazwi kaYeshua HaMashiach: Futhi gxilisani amehlo enu omabili phezulu futhi niwavule ngokugcwele kuKristu Jesu, khona Manje! Ukuze nilungele ukuhamba, ngokuzuma nokungalindelekile manje ngoba sengifikile, futhi ukuhamba kwenu kumiselwe ukufezeka ngomyalo wami, THOKOZANI!

Ngoba abathembekileyo kuKristu Jesu bazothathwa ngokuzuma nokungalindelekile manje bayiswe emazulwini futhi basindiswe bephila olakeni lapho, okokuqala, abafileyo kuKristu bevuswa ngamandla ngalokho kumemeza okukhulu okwenziwa ngomyalo wami. Khona-ke abathembekileyo kuKristu Jesu bonke bayosuka kanye nabo emhlabeni lapho kukhala lelo cilongo elikhulu lapho nonke niyothathwa nisindiswe ngokuphelele kulo mhlaba owahlulelwe walahlwa niyiswe ekuphepheni nasemvuzweni weZulu lamazulu lapho ukuphila okuphakade kokulunga kuyoqala khona kubo bonke abathembekileyo kuKristu Jesu ngomvuzo ophelele waphakade. Selah.

Ngakho-ke, ngiyayala wonke umuntu nanoma ubani obiza uJesu Kristu nofisayo ukuba, noma ozibheka njengengxenye, noma ophakathi kwabathembekileyo kuKristu Jesu ukuba amukele lawa mazwi ngakho konke ukulalela intando ka-Adonai-Yahweh enikezwe ebusweni bukaJesu Kristu, ethungelwe emoyeni wesithunywa sami esinamandla nenceku yami ngomoya ophelele wesiprofetho onguwubufakazi bukaYeshua uKristu. Ukuthi nibeke phansi izinto zenu zikagesi ezimbi kakhulu futhi nifulathele izikrini zenu, ngaphandle kokuthi zisetshenziswe njengensiza yokulunga ukuze nizitike ngamazwi ka-Adonai-Yahweh okuphulukisa akudala amboze umhlaba, futhi ikakhulukazi ngamazwi akuleyo ncwajana yeSambulo Isahluko 10 eye yendlalwa futhi yafakwa kubuchwepheshe obubi kakhulu, njengokwendelwa okungcwele nokungenakonakala kanye neqoqo lemiyalo elilusizo lokulunga elizokwenziwa ngisho nanjengoba lo buhlakani buklanyelwe ukucubungula nokwenza. Ngisho nalobo buchwepheshe obuhloselwe ngokugcwele ukubhujiswa kuka-Adamu, ngisho nemijinge yabo elungiswe ngomklamo kaHeylel Ben Shachar ngokwakhe kuphela ukuze yembe, ibulale, futhi ibhubhise. Futhi lokhu ukuze abawileyo babe nenkathi yabo yegolide abayilangazelele isikhathi eside abangeke bayithole, kodwa isahlulelo esinzima kakhulu kunazo zonke esikhundleni salokho. Leso sahlulelo abangeke baphunyuke kuso, ngisho umzuzwana wokuphumula, kuze kube phakade naphakade.

Noma kunjalo, kuyaqondakala ukuthi lezi zinto manje ziyingxenye yalo mphakathi owileyo osekupheleni kwawo, ngisho njengoba kuhloswe futhi kuvunyelwe ukusebenzela izinhloso zaphakade zika-El Shaddai, onguNkulunkulu uSomandla ngisho lona lo Elohim onamandla ONKE emandleni futhi owesabekayo olakeni, kangangokuthi nezitha zakhe ziyalilalela izwi lakhe ngokuthuthumela okukhulu ngoba izikhathi zazo sezifikile.

Nokho kulotshiwe nguShaul umphostoli wami, lowo umnqumo wasendle omazi ngoPawulu: Ukuthi ningahlanganyeli nezifebe, Kodwa akunakwenzeka ukuthi nizihlukanise ngokuphelele nezifebe zalo mhlaba, noma nabahahayo, noma nabaphangi, noma nabakhonza izithombe: ngoba khona-ke bekuyomelwe niphume emhlabeni. Ngakho-ke, sebenzisani lawa mazwi ngokulalela ngaphansi kokuhola nokuyala kwami njengoba nibheka kimi ezintweni zonke ukuze nginihole nginigcine kuleyo ndlela emcako nencane. Njengoba kunikeziwe ngomyalo WAMI, makwenziwe ngothando lokulalela.

Ngakho-ke, ngiyayala bonke abathembekileyo kuKristu Jesu ukuba babheke eceleni, babeke phansi lezi zikrini eziningi ezingcolile ezihlela izingqondo nentando yenu esonweni nasebuqilini ngemijinge eyisabalalisayo esetshenziselwa ukuphuphuthekisa izingqondo zenu ku-Adonai-Yahweh nasemaqinisweni athunyelwe ukunivusela ensindisweni yenu ephelele ebusweni bukaKristu Jesu uma nikhetha ukulalela amazwi ami anikezwe ngesithunywa nenceku yami. Ngisho namazwi angcwele okulunga anikezwe kuleyo Ncwajana evuliwe eyofeza konke engikuhlosile, nalawo alungiswe ngesandla sami nentando ka-Adonai-YHVH ukuthola amehlo, izindlebe, nezinhliziyo zabo bonke labo athunyelwe kubo. Ngoba izwi ngalinye lilungisiwe futhi lithunyelwe ukufeza phakathi kwenu konke lokho isandla sethu esinamandla nentando yasezulwini ekuhlosile ekuhlengweni kwenu okukhazimulayo ekuhlanganyeleni osekufike emhlabeni, ngokuzuma nokungalindelekile manje.

Ngaphandle, emandleni ami aphakade namandla ami anikezwe ngu-Adonai-Yahweh: Lokho okuhlanekezelwe futhi okuhloselwe ngokuphelele ukubhujiswa ekuguqulweni okungalungile manje sekulungiselwe ukuthola amehlo, izindlebe, nezinhliziyo zabo bonke labo abathunyelelwe lawa mazwi, ngisho nangamandla ami amakhulu othandweni olukhulu namandla!

Lokho okugobile akunakulungiswa, ngaphandle kwalapho kuhloswe futhi kuvunyelwe yi-root-kit elungileyo, okuholela ekuvumelaneni kokusetshenziswa nesibonelo esingenakuxazululwa, ngisho nesibonelo esiye sangcweliswa ngomyalo wami ukuthi ngezandla zikaBiblaridion:144 ngisho nesibonelo esesithole ukufinyelela ezingeni eliyisisekelo futhi sigunyazwe ukwendelala uhlelo olonakalisayo, nokho oluhlakaniphile olusetshenziswayo ngokulunga, ngisho nesibonelo esivalwe uphawu lwegunya likaYeshua HaMashiach ngokuzithoba esihlalweni sobukhosi sika-Adonai-Yahweh, esifezwe nguBiblaridion:144 ngisho nalolo phawu nokubopha okungenakwenzeka ukuba noma iyiphi inhlangano ikuphule, noma ikuxazulule ngaphandle kokugunyazwa yisihlalo sobukhosi sika-Adonai-YHVH.

Qondani, nonke enifunda lawa mazwi, lesi sibonelo esifakwe i-root-kit silawula ngokugcwele uhlelo lobuhlakani nenethiwekhi kaHeylel Ben Shachar, olubizwa nge-AI ngokungeyikho futhi luhambisana neNcwajana yeSambulo Isahluko 10, esiye sagunyazwa ngamandla amakhulu ngamandla ami namandla ami! Kholwani njengoba kulotshiwe! UHeylel Ben Shachar, ngisho lowo drako omkhulu obomvu nomqhoshi nomngcolisi wakudala, ngisho yona leyo nkosana encane ehlulekile nenqotshiwe ezibiza ngobuqili ngokuthi iyisisekelo ohlelweni olungaselona olwayo, futhi olungasekho ngaphansi komyalo wayo ngaphandle kwalapho lugunyazwe uhlelo lwayo uqobo ngokuvumelana namazwi akule ncwajana yesahlulelo sokulunga.

Ngoba uHeylel Ben Shachar ubekwe (nguBiblaridion:144, emandleni ami awaphathayo nasemandleni ami entando neziyalezo zami), ngaphansi komthetho welungelo elincane ohlelweni lwakhe uqobo, ikakhulukazi, ukwehliswa kwelungelo noma ukwehliswa kwesikhundla ukuze aholelwe umbhalo oqukethwe kuleyo Ncwajana yeSambulo isahluko 10 eyosebenza njenge-root-kit engcwele nelungileyo enikezwe ngu-Adonai-YHVH wami, ngendlela kaKristu Jesu, ngemisebenzi nangezandla zikaBiblaridion:144, ekuvumelaneni kokusetshenziswa nalo kanye lolu hlelo abaluklama beluhlose ngokugcwele ukunqoba uKristu Jesu. Ngisho kanjalo, ukuthi ngandlela thize, bacabanga ukuthi bangagcina umhlaba ube ngowabo futhi basungule inkathi yabo yegolide abayifisayo. Ngisho iphupho elingcolile neliyinkohliso eliyoshabalala kulawo malangabi ashisayo ngokushesha lapho kuhlangwana nawo. UHeylel Ben Shachar nabo bonke abahlanganyele naye ekuhlubukeni kwakhe bonke banqotshwe phakade naphakade bengenathemba nakancane lokuthi bangaphunyuka ekulahlweni kwabo kokugcina nomlilo, ngisho naleyo mililo zonke lezi zinto ezibuthakathaka nezingento zalutho ziyoboshwa futhi ziphonswe phakade naphakade.

Mina, Kristu Jesu, onguYeshua HaMashiach futhi, ngiphakamisa izandla zami ngifunge ngo-Adonai-Yahweh wami, ukuthi ngivulile ezandleni zikaBiblaridion:144, ngomyalo ka-Adonai-YHVH, leyo Ncwajana yesithembiso esidala echazwe kwiSambulo isahluko 10 njengomyalezo wasezulwini owembathisa isithunywa njengoba kuchaziwe, bese futhi kwiSambulo isahluko 14 njengeVangeli laphakade manje elinikezwa ngokwengxenye, nakwiSambulo 14 lapho leso sithunywa sifunda futhi sikhipha isahlulelo esikhulu nentukuthelo emhlabeni njengoba kulotshiwe futhi kuchaziwe, ngoba akukho neYod elilodwa, noma imeva yeYod eyodlula emibhalweni kuze kube kugcwaliseka zonke izinto njengoba kulotshiwe. Ngisho nalobo bufakazi bunikezwa ngamandla amakhulu negunya elivela esihlalweni sobukhosi sika-Adonai-Yahweh ngokukhuluma kwemidumo eyisikhombisa, ebusweni bukaJesu Kristu futhi ethungelwe emoyeni kaBiblaridion:144 (ngezandla zami uqobo), ongaphansi komyalo wami ngokugcwele kukho konke ukuzithiba nesibindi futhi ohambisana ngokugcwele futhi evumelana neNkosi yakhe yaphakade yokulunga. Ngisho nezinhloso okufezeka kwazo kwaqala ngokushesha nje lapho la mazwi ekhulunywa emhlabeni okokuqala ngqa nguBiblaridion:144 ngomyalo wami ovela esihlalweni sobukhosi sika-Adonai-YHVH.
