Isikhalo Sabangenacala!

Thathani isililo, nikhale, nibubule ngenxa yokuhlatshwa kwabangenacala. Ngoba abenzanga cala, abaphilanga zimpilo, futhi abenzanga sono, ngokuba ukumuka kwabo kungakafiki isikhathi kwavela ekuphepheni kwezisu zonina, futhi bengakayitholi ingqayizivele yokuthatha umoya wabo wokuqala, abasekho!

Thathani isililo, nikhale, nibubule ngenxa yezimpilo ezilahlekile zabangenacala, negazi labo elichithekile liyakhala emazulwini lifuna ubulungiswa. Bhekani! Umhlaba uyazamazama, uyajikajika njengoba ingcindezi ikhula, ngokuba isisindo sokuhlatshwa kwabangenacala sikhulu!

Thathani isililo nangenxa yabantwana bakaRaheli, ngokuba itsho kanje iNkosi; Kwezwakala izwi eRama, isililo nokukhala okukhulu: uRaheli ekhalela abantwana bakhe, wala ukududuzwa ngabantwana bakhe, ngokuba babengekho. Noma nicabanga ukuthi lokhu kuyinto elula, yiqondani ukuthi lezi zono azinakushaywa indiva, nesisindo sesijeziso sazo sikhulu ngokwedlulele, futhi ngempela ukwahlulelwa kuphezu kwabo bonke abenze lezi zono futhi benqaba isipho sami sokuthula!

Zwanini amazwi ami, bantu bami, nithobele izinhliziyo zenu emazwini ami, nizwe ukuhlakanipha kwami, ngokuba ukwahlulelwa akusibo obenu. Qondani usizi olukhulu nezinyembezi ezibuhlungu zeNkosi noMsindisi wenu, uJesu waseNazaretha! Ngoba ngezinyembezi eziningi, ngiye ngakhalela njalonjalo ukuhlatshwa kwabangenacala. Phumani phakathi kwakhe, bantu bami, ningivumele ngichithe ngokuphelele imisebenzi kasathane ezimpilweni zenu kulokhu, nakuzo zonke izindaba, bantwana bami. Ngokuba uMoya wami akasafiki ukunilahla ngecala lesono, ngokuba nenziwe ningukulunga kukaNkulunkulu kimi, ngenxa yokuthi namukele isipho sami sokuphila okuphakade nesipho sami sokulunga, enenziwa ngaso abalungileyo, ngisho nokulunga kukaNkulunkulu kimi ngegazi lami lokuhlawulela nangomoya kaNkulunkulu Ophilayo, ngisho umoya wobudodana enabekwa ngawo uphawu kuze kube usuku lokuhlengwa, futhi niyisidalwa esisha kimi. Ngakho ningesabi!

Nidalwe ekulungeni nasebungcweleni beqiniso, futhi lapho uMoya wami ongaphakathi unilahla ngecala, yimina Kristu Jesu onilahla ngecala lokulunga. Yebo, nginilahla ngecala lokulunga ukuze niphile ngokulunga, ngokuba niyikulunga kukaNkulunkulu, futhi kumelwe niqhubeke nokubulala inyama, nihambe ngoMoya wami, ngokuba ngakho-ke manje akusekho ukulahlwa kulabo abakuKristu Jesu, abangahambi ngokwenyama kodwa ngokukaMoya! Ngakho-ke yiqondani ukuthi ukwahlulelwa akusibo obenu, ngokuba wonke umphefumulo uYahweh awuthethelele kimi Kristu Jesu ngeke eze ekwahlulelweni ngenxa yezono, kodwa uyosuka ekufeni aye ekuphileni okuphakade, ngokuba namukele uJesu waseNazaretha, ngisho nesivumelwano sokuthula sikaYahweh kuso sonke isintu, naphakade kubo bonke abamukela umhlatshelo wami!

Wozani kimi nivume nezono enizibangele ukuba nizikhohlwe, niphaphamele ekulungeni, ningoni. Wozani kimi nivume amaphutha enu, ukuze nginihlanze kulo lonke ibala ngegazi lami lokuhlawulela, nokuba imisebenzi kasathane isulwe ngokuphelele naphakade ezimpilweni zenu. Kungani nibambelela ngenkani ezonweni zenu? Kungani nifuna ukuthula ngokulala? Ningamfihlela yini uNkulunkulu ophilayo lapho nivale amehlo enu? Ninamathela yini enganekwaneni yentshe, nifuna ukulingisa le nganekwane? Anginibizelanga ukuba niphile inganekwane yentshe, noma ukungcwaba amakhanda enu esihlabathini, noma ukusibekela izono zenu ngenjabulo nangokukhohlwa. Ngokuba intshe ifaka ikhanda layo emhlabathini ukuze inakekele amaqanda ayo angakazalwa, hhayi ukucasha eqinisweni. Kodwa nginibizele ukuba nibhekane nezono zenu zesikhathi esedlule, nizivume kimi, ukuze nginiphilise, ngichithe imisebenzi kaSathane ezimpilweni zenu, ngokuba ufuna ukunibopha ezonweni zakhe, anivivimbele phakade ngobuwula!

Ngakho-ke, qhubekani nivume obala izono zenu namaphutha enu omunye komunye, niqonde ukuthi njengoba nenza kanjalo, izitha zenu ziyathuthumela, ngokuba zibona ubufakazi obungenakuphikwa bokusindiswa kwenu okuqhubekayo, nobufakazi obungenakuphikwa bokubhujiswa kwazo okusondelayo nokwahlulelwa kwaphakade kulelo chibi elikhulu lapho amalangabi ahlanzekile aluhlaza okwesibhakabhaka eyovutha phakade, nesibungu sazo singafi! Ngakho phumani phakathi kwazo, nihlukaniswe, niqonde ukuthi ninokuthethelelwa kwezono. Njengoba niqhubeka ebuhlotsheni benu obusindisayo nami, Kristu Jesu, ngizokuhlanza ngokuphelele kuzo zonke izono zenu, ukuze ngizenzele ibandla elikhazimulayo, elingenabala, nambimbi, nanoma yini enjalo, kodwa ukuze nibe ngcwele, ningasoleki phambi kwami ​​othandweni. Futhi ukuze nembathiswe ngelineni elicolekileyo, ngokuba injalo ikusasa lenu eliseduze. Ngakho yiqondani ukuthi kimi, Kristu Jesu, akukho ukulahlwa noma ukwahlulelwa kini bantwana bami, namazwi ami aqinile awasiwo awenu, kodwa ngiyaneluleka ukuba nifunde ngami, niqonde ubukhulu balokho enginihlengile kukho, ngokuba nihlengiwe ekwahlulelweni.

Futhi yazini ukuthi sonke isenzo silotshiwe emazulwini kuso sonke isidalwa ngaphansi kwezulu esingaphansi kokwahlulelwa okukhulu. Futhi ngibonile, ngaloba amazwi nemisebenzi yabaprofethi bamanga, ngisho nalabo abazenza abakhuluma egameni lami, abavumele ukuhlatshwa kwabangenacala ngenxa yezindlela zabo ezimbi. Ngokuba laba yimithombo engenamanzi, ngisho nalabo abazifebe zabo zaholela ekuhlatshweni kwabangenacala. Abathi, esikhundleni sokuphenduka ezifebeni zabo nasebuhlotsheni babo, babulala abangakazalwa ukuze baqhubeke nokuzitika emhlambini wami esikhundleni sokondla umhlambi wami, futhi bayobhekana nolaka olukhulu ngamacala abo, ngokuba akunakwenzeka ukuba izimpisi ezihahayo zicashe epulpiti ekubukeni kwami ​​kwaphakade, futhi bayothwala isahlulelo sabo bonke abangaphenduki kulezi zenzo!

Ngakho-ke ngikhuluma kuwe muntu wemvelo, futhi ngikhuluma nawe Bhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo. Ngokuba unecala legazi labangenacala, ngisho nalabo obathathe ekuphepheni kwezisu zonina. Usongile ukushaywa kombungu ngezizathu ezingamanga nokuzithethelela okwenza isilingo sakho sobugovu sokubulala abangenacala sibe yinto evamile njengokuvakashela udokotela, futhi imitholampilo yakho yokubulala iyiphunga elibi emakhaleni kaBaba, nephunga elibi emakhaleni ami. Ngokuba igazi labangenacala liyakhala lisuka emhlabeni lize lifike esihlalweni sobukhosi sikaYahweh ezulwini lamazulu obona konke, ngisho nani nonke enifuna ukucasha ekubukeni kwakhe kwaphakade. Futhi mina Yeshua HaMashiach ngithunyiwe nguBaba wami ekwahlulelweni, futhi ngiyiNgonyama yesizwe sakwaJuda, ngokuba nilahlile iWundlu likaNkulunkulu, manje sekufika isahlulelo senu. Futhi ngizowuhlanza ngokuphelele umhlaba ezindleleni zenu zokubulala, futhi niyodla ukungcola kwezindlela zenu ezimbi, ngokuba uYahweh umemezele isabelo esiphindwe kabili sokwahlulelwa phezu kwenu, oh Bhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo.

Ngokuba nibulele abangenacala, nicabanga ngokungazi ukuthi lesi senzo esinyantisayo siyinto elula. Nithengise ngabantwana benu abangakazalwa ukuze nithole inzuzo engcolile, ukuze nithuthukise imisebenzi yenu, ngisho nemisebenzi yenu eyize ngokuphelele, ngokuba yonke iyophela, nemizamo yenu yasemhlabeni eyize eniyazisa kakhulu iyonyamalala ekwahlulelweni, ingaphinde ikhunjulwe. Ngokuba ninikele ngokuzithandela ngabantwana benu abangakazalwa emhlatshelweni ongaziwa kuMoloki, futhi nibulala abantwana benu abangakazalwa ngokuziqhenya okukhulu. Ngakho-ke kuyoba njalo, ngokuba ukuziqhenya kwandulela ukuwa, nomoya ozidlayo wandulela ukubhujiswa, nokwahlulelwa kwenu nokubhujiswa kwenu akulibali, kodwa kuphezu kwenu, futhi sekuvele kuqhubeka, nemihelo isingenamkhawulo manje.

Kodwa esikhundleni sokuphenduka, esikhundleni sokuza kimi, uJesu waseNazaretha, ngokuthobeka lapho nginiphile ukuphila okuphakade nokuthethelelwa kwezono nemiphumela evela ekwahlulelweni okuhambisanayo, nikhethe esikhundleni salokho izono zenu nesijeziso senu saphakade. Nizibophezele, nakhetha esikhundleni sami uJesu waseNazaretha, uBaraba, uMoshiach wenu wamanga. Esikhundleni sikaYahweh uNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade, nikhethe udrako njengonkulunkulu wenu, futhi manje ngizoninikela kubo, ngokuba nihlanganyele nabo ezindleleni zabo ngokuzikhukhumeza nokuziqhenya kwenu, futhi manje niyodla ukungcola kwezindlela zenu.

Ninikele ngegazi labangenacala ema-altare okuzanelisa, futhi nichitha imibungu yenu eshayiwe kube sengathi iyizidwedwe ezingcolile ezilahlelwa emigqonyeni kadoti wezokwelapha, futhi nibale umoya wokuphila osegazini njengento engcolile, futhi ukwahlulelwa kwenu kuphezu kwenu. Kodwa abenu uqobo, ngisho nabantwana benu abangakazalwa, babephume esitayeleni semfashini yenu ehlala ishabalala, nezimpilo zabo zaziyisithiyo kini ekuzicwiliseni ngokuphelele ezintokozweni zezono.

Niyathethelela, nifuna igazi labangenacala emibonweni yenu yezombangazwe yokubulala, futhi nazi ukuthi nihlanganyele nabawileyo ekuhlubukeni kwabo, ngokuba kwakungoSathane abafundisa ukushaywa kombungu esiswini, ngisho nezinqubo zenu eziyigugu zokubulala eniziqhenya ngokuzivikela nangokuzibukisa ngazo. Ngakho-ke igazi labangenacala liyakhala lisuka emhlabeni, nesisindo sezono zenu senze umhlaba wantengantenga njengendoda edakiwe, futhi kuyoba kukhulu ukuzamazama, kube kukhulu nokuwa!
