Ubufakazi Obumbaxa: Israyeli
Okubabayo Okumnandi
Julayi 27, 2024

Nqabani Uphawu Lwesilo!

Zwanini izwi lokuhlakanipha, niguqe ngamadolo, nizwe izwi eliphilayo likaYeshua HaMashiach, ngokuba ngiyiNgonyama yesizwe sakwaJuda, futhi ngiyiNkosi yamakhosi noMninimandla onke! U-El Shaddai, uNkulunkulu uSomandla uqobo, ungiphakamisile kakhulu wanginika igama eliphezu kwawo onke amagama. Ukuze ngegama likaJesu onke amadolo aguqe, ezintweni ezisezulwini, nasezintweni ezisemhlabeni, nasezintweni ezingaphansi komhlaba: Nokuba zonke izilimi zivume ukuthi uJesu Kristu uyiNkosi, kube yinkazimulo kaYise wezinkanyiso, uYahweh uqobo, uNkulunkulu waphakade nongunaphakade, ngokuba akekho onjengoBaba wethu!

Zwanini izwi leNkosi yenu niqonde izinto esezivele zenzeka nezinto ezizokwenzeka maduze, ngokuba bhekani nginguye okhuluma ngezinto ezizayo sengathi sezenzekile, ngokuba ngokuqinisekile iNkosi yenu enkulu, mina Kristu Jesu ophakeme kunabo bonke, ngeke ngenze lutho, ngaphandle kokuba ngembule izimfihlo zami ezincekwini zami abaProfethi. Kungani nilalela amazwi amaningi kangaka aphikisanayo, futhi kungani nixhuga phakathi kwemibono emibili? Angishongo yini ukuthi abaprofethi bami bakhuluma ngazwi linye futhi ngeke baphikisane namazwi ami angcwele? Ngokuba ngiyiZwi elenziwa inyama elihlala phakathi kwenu ngomoya wami ongcwele ozelwe ngaphakathi kini kuze kube phakade, ngokuba yonke imiphefumulo ezelwe kabusha ibekwe uphawu ngalowo moya ongcwele wesithembiso oluyimbewu yami engonakali, futhi uphawu lukaNkulunkulu Ophilayo alunakwephulwa. Futhi usuku lokuhlengwa kwefa elithengiwe selusondele, nokuhlengwa kwenu okuphelele kulapha.

Usho kanje uYehova Sebawoti, ningawalaleli amazwi abaprofethi abaprofetha kini, abanifundisa okuyize: bakhuluma umbono wezinhliziyo zabo, hhayi ophuma emlonyeni weNkosi. Wozani kimi bantwana bami ngoba ukugcotshwa enikwamukele kimi kuhlala kini, futhi anidingi muntu onifundisayo: Kepha, njengoba ukugcotshwa okufanayo kunifundisa ngezinto zonke, futhi kuyiqiniso, futhi akusilo manga: futhi njengoba kunifundisile, niyakuhlala kimi. Bantwana bami hlalani kimi niqhathanise amazwi anoma ngubani othi ukhulumela mina nemibhalo, ningicele ukuba ngiqinisekise emoyeni wenu, uma mhlawumbe niyolilalela izwi lami ngoba abaningi benu banezindlebe ezilukhuni, futhi abaningi benu bathanda amazwi amanga abakitazayo ezindlebeni zenu ukuze nibonakale nihlakaniphile, kepha kunalokho nilandele amazwi amanga ezithutha ezizidlayo.

Bhekani! Ngikhuluma ngezikhathi zokuhlupheka kukaJakobe osekuzoqala emhlabeni wonke. Ngokuba ukwahlulela sekuvele kuyaqhubeka futhi akunakuvinjwa, ngokuba lokho uYahweh akushoyo akekho ongakuguqula! Izinyembezi eziningi ngizikhalile ngokwahlulelwa kweBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlakalo futhi ngithumele abaprofethi bami iminyaka eminingi kakhulu manje futhi ngithumele nabafokazi abavela emazweni angaziwa ukuba bafakaze futhi baprofethe ngokwahlulela okwakusondela kodwa manje sekulapha.

Ngokuba kuyakuba njalo njengoba kulotshiwe: Izimpondo eziyishumi owazibona phezu kwesilo, lezi ziyakuzonda isifebe, zisenze incithakalo, zisihambise ze, ziyakudla inyama yaso, zisishise ngomlilo. Ngokuba uNkulunkulu ufake ezinhliziyweni zazo ukuba zenze intando yakhe, nokuba zivumelane, zinike umbuso wazo isilo, kuze kugcwaliseke amazwi kaNkulunkulu. Futhi owesifazane, owambonayo, yilowo muzi omkhulu obusa phezu kwamakhosi omhlaba.

Zwanini amazwi ami, ukwahlulelwa kweBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo sekuvele kuyaqhubeka. Izimpondo eziyishumi sezilungiselele ukuhlasela kwazo okungalindelekile futhi ukuhlasela kwazo kuyasondela futhi kuseminyango yenu. Ngokuba ngisho nabezindaba benu bafuna ukunilalisa ubuthongo futhi abaningi bahleli ebumnyameni bengaboni iqiniso bethi: Ngihleli njengeNdlovukazi futhi angiyena umfelokazi futhi ngeke ngibone ukulila. Kusasa kuyakuba njenganamuhla, ngokuba namuhla kunjenayizolo. Ngokulingana nokuzidumisa kwaso, nokuhlala ebunandini, ngokulinganayo sinikeni ukuhlushwa nosizi: Ngakho-ke izinhlupho zaso ziyakuza ngosuku olulodwa, ukufa, nosizi, nendlala, futhi siyakushiswa ngomlilo: ngokuba uYahweh osilahlayo, unguNkulunkulu uSomandla futhi akekho omkhulu futhi akukho okungavimba isandla sika-El Shaddai esinamandla!

Futhi kanjalo ilanga liyophuma maduze ngosuku lwenhlekelele eningayilindele, futhi lolo suku luseduze kunalokho enikucabangayo. Futhi lolo suku luyoletha ukubhujiswa okuphelele phezu kweBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlakalo, ngokuba linengxenye ephindwe kabili yesahlulelo esimemezelelwe kulo. Ubugebengu balo nobufebe balo buningi futhi isijeziso salo asilibali futhi isijeziso esikhulu siyowela phezu kwabangaphenduki njengoba iBandla lami elithembekile ngisho noMlobokazi wami othembekile bayobuthelwa kimi ngokuzumayo emafwini futhi basindiswe olakeni, ngisho nasekuhluphekeni okukhulu kweviki likaDaniyeli lamashumi ayisikhombisa. IBhabhiloni Elikhulu liwile, liwile! Zwanini amazwi ami ngokuba ngikhuluma sengathi sekuvele kwenzekile ngokuba akukho okuyoguqula, kwehlise ijubane, kuvimbe, noma kumise izahlulelo zikaYahweh ekuweni emhlabeni.

Ngehora elilodwa liyoshiswa ngomlilo, eminye imizi yalo iyosha, eminye imizi yalo iyogcwala amanzi ngodlame, imibhoshongo yalo iyowa iphuke ibe yizicucu eziningi, amabhuloho alo ayowa, futhi kuyokuba nokuzamazama okukhulu. Umhlaba uyodabuka ngodlame futhi umhlaba omningi uyogqitshwa emanzini anolaka. Imiphetho yalo iyogcwaliswa ngamagagasi amakhulu avela empumalanga nasentshonalanga, naphakathi kwamagagasi agijimayo nokuzamazama okunodlame. Okusalayo kuyoba yihlane futhi ngeke liphinde livuke, ngokuba lesi sizwe sezifundazwe eziningi nemizi eminingi ngeke siphinde sivuke futhi ukuwa kwaso kuyoba kukhulu! Ngokuba umhlaba ungowenkosi nokugcwala kwawo, futhi zonke izici zethonya lawo elibi ziyosulwa phakade ezizweni phakathi neviki likaDaniyeli lamashumi ayisikhombisa, ngokuba kanjalo ngiyohlanza umhlaba ngentukuthelo yami. Mina, Kristu Jesu, iNgonyama Yesizwe SakwaJuda, ngimemezele lawa mazwi, futhi ukulungiselela ukugcwaliseka kwawo sekuvele kuyaqhubeka futhi kuyogcwaliseka ngokuzumayo ngehora elilodwa.

Kanjalo-ke impi iyoqhuma emhlabeni wonke njengengxenye yokuhlaselwa okungalindelekile kweBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo, ngokuba lokhu kuhleliwe futhi kwahlelwa amajubhili amaningi manje, ngokuba imfihlakalo yokona ibisebenza kusukela ezinsukwini zasendulo futhi iyofinyelela ekufezekeni okuphelele lapho umvimbeli esusiwe njengoba abathembekileyo bami bethathwa bayiswe emazulwini. Impi iyowela phezu komhlaba futhi iyehlele phezu kwabo bonke abantu nazo zonke izizwe, ngokuba lena impi yesithathu enjalo, njengoba abawileyo befuna ukubhubhisa izimiso ezindala zomhlaba futhi bashintshe zonke izinto ngohlelo lwabo olusha, ngisho uhlelo lukaDrako. Isilo esivela olwandle nesilo esivela emhlabeni kuyovuka ngolaka olukhulu, futhi bayokulwa nabangcwele, ngokuba kunikiwe kubo ukuba banqobe abangcwele. Ngakho kuyoba ukuthi bonke abangazithandi izimpilo zabo futhi babize igama leNkosi kuze kube sekupheleni bayosindiselwa enjabulweni yaphakade kuze kube phakade naphakade. Futhi mina Jesu waseNazaretha ngishiye isibonelo saphakade sokulalela kuze kube sekufeni, ngokuba ukulalela kwami uYahweh kwaze kwaba sekufeni kwesiphambano njengoba ngahlupheka futhi ngakhokhela izono zesintu njengewundlu likaNkulunkulu, futhi bhekani ngiyeza njengeNgonyama Yesizwe SakwaJuda futhi ngeke ngivinjwe noma ngithiywe!

Kanjalo-ke abaprofethi bami ababili abayizihlahla ezimbili zomnqumo nezinti zezibani ezimbili ezimi phambi kukaNkulunkulu womhlaba bayoprofetha izinyanga ezingama-42 bembethwe indwangu yamasaka, ngokuba bathathwa baya emazulwini ezikhathini zasendulo. Futhi uma kukhona umuntu ofuna ukubalimaza, umlilo uphuma emilonyeni yabo, uqothule izitha zabo: futhi uma kukhona umuntu ofuna ukubalimaza, kufanele abulawe ngale ndlela. Laba banamandla okuvala izulu, bemisa imvula ezinsukwini zokuprofetha kwabo: futhi banamandla phezu kwamanzi ukuwaphendula abe yigazi, nokushaya umhlaba ngazo zonke izinhlupho, kaningi ngendlela abafuna ngayo.

Obabo ubufakazi baphakade, futhi bayomemezela ivangeli laphakade, futhi bayokhuluma ngamacebo kaYahweh azo zonke izikhathi, futhi bayokhulumela mina Kristu Jesu, futhi izimfihlo eziningi bayozembula futhi bayophendula izinhliziyo zabaningi kuYise wezinkanyiso okungekho kuye ukuguquguquka nanoma isithunzi sokuphenduka. Bayofakaza ngami Yeshua HaMashiach ngokuba yimi uYahweh angithumele kwa-Israyeli ukuzogcwalisa izithembiso ezenziwa koYise babo futhi nginguMesiya weqiniso manje naphakade futhi umbuso wami awunasekupheleni! Ngokuba yiziphi izikhathi ezake zaba khona lapho uYahweh ezishiye engenabufakazi? Akukaze kube nesikhathi lapho uYahweh ezishiye engenabufakazi, futhi bonke abamiselwe insindiso yaphakade bayoza bazosindiselwa enjabulweni yaphakade naphakade, ngokuba ukugcwaliseka kwazo zonke izinto sekuvele kuyaqhubeka.

Futhi kanjalo isilo esivela olwandle siyovuka kanye nesilo esivela emhlabeni, naye ongumprofethi wamanga. Futhi uyovula umlomo wakhe ekhuluma ukuhlambalaza okukhulu ngoYahweh ukuze ahlambalaze igama lakhe nedokodo lakhe nalabo abahlala ezulwini. Futhi kuyonikezwa kuye ukuba alwe nabangcwele futhi abanqobe futhi amandla ayonikezwa kuye ukuba abuse yonke imindeni, nezilimi, nesizwe ngokuba uyohola umhlaba. Isilo esivela emhlabeni siyobusa nomhlaba njengoMoshiach wabo wamanga futhi siyoba ngumakhi wethempeli labo. Isilo esivela olwandle siyobusa ngokwemithetho yaso ye-Noahide ehlambalazayo njengoba befuna ukuqeda noma yini ephathelene noNkulunkulu weqiniso nendodana yakhe ezelwe yodwa uJesu Kristu ebusweni bomhlaba.

Isilo esivela emhlabeni siyokwenza umhlaba ukhonze isilo, esinexeba elibulalayo elapholiswa. Futhi siyokwenza izimangaliso ezinkulu ukukhohlisa labo abahlala emhlabeni, ngokuba kuyovunyelwa ukuba senze izibonakaliso eziningi zamanga nezimangaliso, futhi siyokwenza umlilo wehle emhlabeni emehlweni abantu futhi siyoholela bonke abantu ukuba bamangale ngabo. Kodwa ungumphikisi, futhi uziphakamisa ngaphezu kwakho konke okuthiwa uNkulunkulu, noma okukhonzwayo: kangangokuba uhlezi njengoNkulunkulu eThempelini likaNkulunkulu, ezibonakalisa ukuthi unguNkulunkulu.

Futhi uyokwenza umfanekiso wesilo futhi uyokwenza bonke bakhonze umfanekiso ayowenza. Futhi kuyovunyelwa kuye ukuba anike umoya kumfanekiso wesilo ukuze umfanekiso wesilo ukhulume futhi wenze bonke abangafuni ukukhonza umfanekiso wesilo babulawe. Futhi uyokwenza bonke, abacebile nabampofu, abakhululekile nezigqila, ukuba bamukele uphawu lwaso esandleni sabo sokunene noma emabunzini abo.

Ngakho-ke ngiphinda ngithi kulabo abahlala emhlabeni ukuze bonke bezwe:

UMA KUKHONA UMUNTU OKHONZA ISILO NOMFANEKISO WASO, AMUKELE UPHAWU LWASO EBUNZINI LAKHE, NOMA ESANDLENI SAKHE, YENA LOWO UYOPHINDA APHUZE EWAYININI LOLAKA LUKANKULUNKULU, ELITHULULWE LINGAXUTSHIWE ENDEBENI YENTUKUTHELO YAKHE, FUTHI UYOHLUSHWA NGOMLILO NESIBABULE, PHAMBI KWEZINGELOSI EZINGCWELE, NAPHAMBI KWEWUNDLU: FUTHI INTUTHU YOKUHLUSHWA KWABO YENYUKELA PHEZULU KUZE KUBE PHAKADE NAPHAKADE. FUTHI ABANAKUPHUMULA EMINI NOMA EBUSUKU, LABO ABAKHONZA ISILO NOMFANEKISO WASO, NANOMA NGUBANI OWAMUKELA UPHAWU LWEGAMA LASO.

Izwe elikhona manje ngeke liphinde libe khona, nombuso kaDrako okhulayo uyoketulwa ngokuphelele, njengoba umbuso kaSathane usondela ekupheleni kwawo, ngoba bangabahlali abangekho emthethweni futhi abasiwo amakhosi. Leli yizwe abaningi abayozithola bekulo, ngisho nalabo abayosinda ezinhlekeleleni nasezimpini ezizayo. Ningalwamukeli uphawu bantu bami, ngoba bayofika bethembisa ukuthula, bayofika bethembisa ukuphepha, bayonitshela ukuthi konke kungenxa yokuhle kwenu. Bayokwethula lokho abakubiza ngezifo eziwumshayabhuqe ngoba sebehlele ukukholisa umhlaba ukuthi uyagula. Futhi zonke izinhlobo zezifo nezinkathazo ziyokwanda ngoba leyo yimiphumela yabagibeli bamahhashi abazayo futhi bayogibela!

Bayothembisa impilo nokuphulukiswa ekwahluleleni kukaNkulunkulu, kanti kunalokho umuntu kufanele aphenduke ebubini bakhe. Bayothembisa ukungena emphakathini wabo usuka ekwahluleleni okwesabekayo osekuzogqashuka emhlabeni wonke. Ngoba mina, Kristu Jesu, ngizonitshela ngaphambi kokuba kwenzeke ukuze nazi ubuqotho bamazwi ami futhi nikwazi ukuhamba emanzini ayingozi lapho abaningi bayozithola bekhona, ngisho nalabo abanesibindi sokubiza igama lami kuze kube sekupheleni, ngoba bayizindlalifa zensindiso yaphakade. Ngoba bonke ababiza uJesu waseNazaretha njengeNkosi yabo, ngisho bonke ababiza igama lami elinamandla futhi abangakuthandi ukuphila kwabo kuze kube sekupheleni, bayosindiselwa ekuphileni okuphakade.

Anifundanga yini? Laba yibo abaphume osizini olukhulu, futhi bahlanza izembatho zabo, bazenza zaba mhlophe egazini leWundlu. Ngakho-ke, baphambi kwesihlalo sobukhosi sikaNkulunkulu, bamkhonza imini nobusuku ethempelini lakhe: nalowo ohlezi esihlalweni sobukhosi uyohlala phakathi kwabo. Abasayikulamba, abasayikoma, nelanga alisayikubashisa, nakushisa okunye. Ngoba iWundlu eliphakathi kwesihlalo sobukhosi liyobondla, libaholele emithonjeni yamanzi aphilayo: noNkulunkulu uyokwesula zonke izinyembezi emehlweni abo.

Nokho, nakuba isixuku esikhulu kakhulu siyosindiswa evikini lamashumi ayisikhombisa likaDaniyeli futhi insindiso iyofika kwa-Israyeli ngoba insali iyokhothama kimi, Yeshua HaMashiach, (Ngoba nginguMesiya weqiniso lapho iwundlu likaNkulunkulu liyovulela indlela iNgonyama yesizwe sakwaJuda, futhi kokubili kuyopheleliswa kimi ngokuzwana okuphakade, ngoba kimi Kristu Jesu iNgonyama iyolala ngokuthula newundlu.) ngokudabukisayo, nangaphandle kwesidingo, iningi lomhlaba liyomangala lilandele laba babusi abanjengezilo, ngoba bayothakatha abaningi. Ngobuthakathi babo nemilingo, bayoyenga umhlaba ukuba wamukele ubuthi babo, futhi bayobafaka ushevu unomphela labo abathanda umhlaba ngemilingo yabo, ngoba uphawu lwesilo luyofihliswa njengomuthi. Ngisho nokulunywa yinyoka okuyisimiso sabo esesivele sethuliwe futhi sahlolwa, futhi izixuku sezivele zijwayeziswe ukwamukela uphawu lwabo nomkhankaso wabo wembewu yempi kwaba yimpumelelo.

Ngakho-ke uhlelo lwabo selulungele isikhathi esifanele, futhi sebelungise amaphuzu abo omphakathi futhi bonke abahlala emadolobheni abo nasemphakathini wabo bayolawulwa. Ngoba kuyodingeka bamukele uphawu lwesilo ukuze babambe iqhaza emphakathini wabo wobusathane, wona umbuso kaDrako. Labo abamukela uphawu bayoshintshwa unomphela. Ufuzo lwabo kanye luyoshintshwa, ngoba bayozihlanganisa nenzalo yabantu: kodwa abayikunamathelana, njengoba insimbi ingaxutshwa nobumba. Uphawu lwabo oluyisilo, ngisho nokulunywa yinyoka, luyoqothula ngokuphelele bonke abathatha ubuthi babo futhi akukho ukuphunyuka.

Ngisho bonke labo abamukela uphawu bayoshintshwa unomphela, futhi bayozithola beyingxenye yohlelo lwabo, futhi bayohlanganiswa ubuchwepheshe nezingqondo zabo ngeke zisaba ngezabo. Bayolawulwa futhi bayoba njengabafileyo abahambayo, bayoba yizigqila zaphakade zengqondo ehlangene yohlelo lwabo, ngisho nobuchwepheshe babo ngoba bayohlanganiswa ne-inthanethi yezinto ezizayo. Bayolahlekelwa yintando yabo, futhi ngeke babe nemizwa futhi noma yini kaNkulunkulu iyosulwa kubo ingunaphakade ngoba bayozishintsha babe sesimweni esingenakuhlengeka, futhi bayozihlanganisa ngokuphelele nangonaphakade nalabo abawileyo futhi bayoba ngabakadrako ingunaphakade futhi bayoya echibini elikhulu lomlilo kuze kube phakade. Kanjalo-ke kuyoba yisiphetho abazikhethele sona.

Ngoba ukuphetha kwesinqumo sabo akunakuhlehliswa. Futhi izifo zabo namanxeba nokugula kuyoba kuningi, futhi nakuba beyothembiswa impilo nokuthula, impilo nokuthula ngeke babe nakho. Ngoba uSathane uzama ukusebenzisa ubuthakathi bakhe ukuze alwe nolaka nokwahlulela kukaNkulunkulu futhi afake ushevu abulale izixuku, kodwa ngeke babe nampumuzo futhi labo abahlanganyela emilingweni yakhe ngeke baphunyuke. Labo abamukela uphawu bayokhanya ngaphansi kwezibani zabo ezimnyama, futhi izibani zabo ziyokhanyisa imigwaqo yemizi yabo abayilungiselele izigqila zabo. Qondani ukuthi laba kuyoba ngabalahliweyo abahambayo abangenatsetheleliso nabangeke baphinde balulame, ngoba laba ngabamagama abo angabhalwanga eNcwadini Yokuphila YeWundlu, elahlatshwa kusukela ekusekelweni komhlaba. Futhi akukho nsindiso eyoke ibe khona phakathi kwabo noma emphakathini wabo, ngoba ukuhamba phakathi kwabo nokuzihlanganisa nomphakathi wabo kuyodinga nakanjani uphawu lwesilo.

Ofakazi bami ababili bayofakaza kuze kuphele izikhathi zobufakazi babo izinyanga ezingama-42 ezinikiwe. Kanjalo-ke kuyonikezwa isilo esikhuphuka emgodini ongenamkhawulo ukuba silwe nabo, futhi siyobanqoba, sibabulale, yona iminqumo yami emibili, nezinti zezibani ezimbili lapho sebeqedile ubufakazi babo. Futhi izidumbu zabo ziyolala esitaladini somuzi omkhulu, okuthiwa ngokomoya yiSodoma neGibhithe, lapho neNkosi yenu yabethelwa khona. Futhi ababi bayokwenza umgubho omkhulu futhi baphe izipho omunye komunye ngenxa yokufa kwabo. Futhi emva kwezinsuku ezintathu nengxenye uMoya wokuphila ovela kuNkulunkulu uyongena kubo: futhi bayokuma ngezinyawo zabo, nokwesaba okukhulu kuyokwehlela bonke abababona. Futhi bezwa izwi elikhulu livela ezulwini, lithi kubo, khuphukelani lapha. Futhi benyukela ezulwini ngefu, futhi izitha zabo ziyobabona benyukela emazulwini ekuphileni okuphakade futhi bayoshintshwa ingunaphakade. Futhi ingelosi yesikhombisa iyokhalisa, futhi kuyoba namazwi amakhulu ezulwini, ethi, Imibuso yaleli zwe isibe yimibuso yeNkosi yethu, nekaKristu wayo, futhi iyobusa kuze kube phakade naphakade.

(Babusisiwe abafileyo abafela eNkosini, kusukela manje, yebo, usho uMoya, ukuze baphumule emisebenzini yabo, nemisebenzi yabo iyabalandela.)

Kanjalo-ke usizi olukhulu kunawo wonke olwake lwaba khona luyoqala, nakuba usizi luqhubeka phakathi neviki lamashumi ayisikhombisa likaDaniyeli. Futhi ithempeli labo liyophela futhi isinengiso sencithakalo siyofezwa nguMoshiach wabo wamanga. Futhi uyokuqinisa isivumelwano nabaningi isonto elilodwa: futhi maphakathi nesonto uyokwenza umhlatshelo nomnikelo kunqamuke, futhi ngenxa yokusabalala kwezinengiso, uyokwenza kube yincithakalo, kuze kube yilapho ukuphethwa okunqunyiwe sekuthululelwe phezu kwencithakalo.

Lezi kuyoba yizikhathi ezimnyama kakhulu bantwana bami, ngoba mina Yeshua HaMashiach nginitshela lezi zinto ngaphambi kokuba zenzeke, futhi zibhalwe njengesixwayiso kubo bonke. Futhi kanjalo abayizi-144,000 bami bayofika bezosiza labo abebengabhubha nalabo ababiza igama lami. Ngiyobahola isinyathelo ngesinyathelo, futhi bayokwenza imisebenzi eminingi enamandla kimi. Ngoba laba yibo abathathwayo ekuqoqweni kwabangcwele kimi, Kristu Jesu, emoyeni lapho kukhala icilongo lokugcina futhi icilongo selizokwakhaliswa.

Laba ngabakholekileyo bami, ngisho nalabo okungatholakalanga kubo ubuqili, ngoba bazihlanze njengentombi emsulwa egazini leWundlu engalinikela kuze kube phakade njengompristi wabo omkhulu, ngoba ngingumpristi omkhulu kuze kube phakade ngokohlelo lukaMelkisedeki. Laba ngumakoti wami, futhi bayongilandela nomaphi lapho ngiya khona. Bhekani! Ngizobenzela babe namandla, futhi akukaze kubonakale emhlabeni okungaqhathaniswa namakhono engiyobembathisa wona. Bayokwembathiswa ilineni elicolekileyo, elimhlophe, elingenabala futhi amakhono abo ayoveza izimpumelelo zabo futhi bayoba namakhono amakhulu ngoba bangabakholekileyo ababiziwe nabakhethiweyo ngisho nangaphambi kokusekelwa komhlaba nguYahweh ngenxa yalezi zinhloso.

Abayi-144,000 bami bayokwehla eNtabeni iSiyoni futhi ngiyobahola. Bayothatha labo engibaholela kubo babayise ehlane ukuze bafihlwe ebumnyameni obukhulu nasekushushisweni kombuso wezitha. Bayoholwa bayiswe ekuphepheni ezifweni eziyisikhombisa ngoba kulezi zahlulelo kukhona ubukhulu bolaka lukaNkulunkulu oluzothululelwa esihlalweni sesilo futhi kuhlushwe umbuso kadrako ngoba sikhulu isijeziso sabo. Futhi abayi-144,000 bayobaholela ekuphepheni, bayobaphulukisa amanxeba abo, bayobasiza futhi babanike ithemba elikhulu lokubiza igama leNkosi kuze kube sekupheleni uma kudingeka. Ngoba ukuba angizifinyezanga izinsuku, bekungeke kusale nyama iphila. Kanjalo-ke bayophelelisa lokho engiyobaholela kukho.

Futhi lapho izikhathi seziphelile kuyoba njalo, njengoba kulotshiwe futhi njengoba kulotshiwe makwenzeke kanjalo: Futhi ngabona izulu livulekile, bheka ihhashi elimhlophe, futhi lowo owayehlezi phezu kwalo wayebizwa ngokuthi othembekileyo noqinisileyo, futhi ngokulunga uyahlulela futhi alwe impi. Amehlo akhe ayenjengelangabi lomlilo, nasekhanda lakhe kwakunemiqhele eminingi, futhi enegama elilotshiweyo, elingaziwa muntu ngaphandle kwakhe. Futhi wayembethe isambatho esicwiliswe egazini, negama lakhe libizwa ngokuthi, Izwi likaNkulunkulu. Futhi amabutho ayesemazulwini amlandela ngamahhashi amhlophe, embethe ilineni elicolekileyo, elimhlophe nelihlanzekileyo. Futhi emlonyeni wakhe kuphuma inkemba ebukhali, ukuze ngayo ashaye izizwe: futhi uyozibusa ngentonga yensimbi: futhi unyathela isikhamo sewayini solaka nentukuthelo kaNkulunkulu uSomandla. Futhi esembathweni sakhe, nasethangeni lakhe unegama elilotshiweyo, INkosi Yamakhosi, NomBusi WababusI.

Kanjalo-ke mina, ngisho uJesu waseNazaretha, ngisho iWundlu likaNkulunkulu, ngisho uKristu Jesu onguNgonyama yesizwe sakwaJuda ngiyoza ukuzokwahlulela nokulwa noDrako futhi wonke umbuso wakhe uyochotshozwa ungunaphakade ungaphinde uvuke. Futhi isilo nomprofethi wamanga bayophonswa bephila echibini lomlilo futhi uSathane uyoboshwa ngeketanga elikhulu emgodini ongenamkhawulo iminyaka eyinkulungwane futhi kanjalo ukubusa kwami kweminyaka eyinkulungwane kuyoqala futhi ukubusa kwami kuyoba ngokulunga. Bhekani! Ngiletha umakoti wami kanye nenqwaba yamakhosi nabapristi engiyobusa nabo umhlaba ngokulunga futhi ngiyobusa izizwe ngentonga yensimbi. Ngakho-ke yibani nethemba elikhulu bantwana bami ngoba ukupheliswa kwazo zonke izinto sekusondele futhi lesi sikhathi seminyaka eyisikhombisa yosizi olukhulu, ngisho usizi lukaJakobe luyolandelwa ukubusa kwami okulungileyo futhi uYahweh uyoba yikho konke kukho konke.

Futhi ngabona izulu elisha, nomhlaba omusha: ngoba izulu lokuqala, nomhlaba wokuqala kwase kudlulile, futhi kwakungasekho lwandle. Futhi mina Johane ngabona uMuzi ongcwele, iJerusalema elisha wehla uvela kuNkulunkulu ezulwini, ulungiswe njengomakoti ohlotshiselwe umyeni wakhe. Futhi ngezwa izwi elikhulu livela ezulwini, lithi, Bheka, iTabernakele likaNkulunkulu linabantu, futhi liyohlala nabo, futhi bayoba ngabantu bakhe, noNkulunkulu uqobo uyoba nabo, abe nguNkulunkulu wabo.

Mina, Yeshua HaMashiach, ngithumele lawa Mazwi ngenceku yami, futhi ngenceku yami lawa Mazwi athunyelwe!
