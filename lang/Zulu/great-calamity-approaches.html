Mashi 11, 2024

Usizi Olukhulu Luyasondela

Izwi leNkosi lafika kimi; ngicela nilizwe.

Yizwani izwi lami, isho iNkosi yamaBandla! Bantu bami, phendukelani kimi, ngoba usizi olukhulu seluzofikela isizwe senu. Usizi olukhulu lusemnyango wenu, futhi usizi olukhulu luzofika ogwini lwenu. Phendukelani kimi ningesabe, isho iNkosi yamaBandla. UNkulunkulu usekhiphe isahlulelo phezu kweMelika ngezindlela ezimbili. Kuzoba khona ukwahlulela okuvela ngaphakathi futhi kuzoba khona ukwahlulela okuvela ngaphandle. Ngaphakathi kuzoba nokuzamazama okukhulu, ukuzamazama okukhulu, futhi iMelika iyokuwa kakhulu ngokuzamazama okukhulu. Ngaphandle, amabutho akhe afike ukuzokweba nokubulala nokuchitha konke okusendleleni yawo. Akukho sihe ezinhliziyweni zawo. Anomqondo munye. Futhi azothatha inala yenu kini; azothatha izimpahla zenu kini; azothumba inzalo yenu; futhi nendlala enkulu izofika ezweni lenu. Lowo ongabulawa yimpi uyobulawa yinhlekelele; lowo ongabulawa yinhlekelele uyobulawa yizilwane zasendle; lowo ongabulawa yizilwane uyobulawa yindlala. Nokho, naphakathi kosizi lwenu, noma ngubani obiza igama leNkosi uyosindiswa.

Futhi ngiyosindisa abantu bami, kunjalo nangosizi olukhulu nangomlilo womcwebisi. Wozani kimi, bantu bami, kusesekhona isikhathi. Wozani kimi, iNkosi uJesu Kristu, ngoba ngiyiNkosi elungileyo. NgiyiNkosi yamakhosi. NginguMalusi omuhle, futhi akekho onokuhlwitha izimvu zami esandleni sami. Wozani kimi! Wozani kimi kusesekhona isikhathi, nivume izono zenu; nivume igama lami elinamandla njengeNkosi, ngoba alukho usindiso kwelinye igama. Ngoba mina, uJesu Kristu, ngimiselwe usindiso lwenu engaluthenga ngegazi lami. Wozani kimi, yebo, wozani kimi othandweni lwami enginalo ngani. Ningangiphikisi, kodwa wozani kimi, ukuze ngisanisindisa ngininike ukuphila okuphakade, ukuze ngibusise inzalo yenu. Wozani kimi, bantu bami, ningazenzi lukhuni izinhliziyo zenu, njengoba okhokho benza ehlane. Ngoba iNkosi uNkulunkulu wenu imemezele isahlulelo esikhulu phezu kwesizwe senu, ngoba izono zaso ziningi futhi zethusa.

Yebo, ngikhokhele lezo zono nazo zonke izono zesintu, kodwa umnikelo wami nezingalo zami ngeke kuhlale kweluliwe njalo. Ngosuku losizi, usindiso luyotholakala ngokuhlupheka okukhulu nosizi olukhulu engiyoluletha emhlabeni ukuhlola izinhliziyo zabantu, futhi lowo okhuthazelelayo kuze kube sekupheleni uyosindiswa. Ningalandeli umuntu wesono. Ningaluthathi uphawu lwakhe futhi ningawuthathi umuthi wakhe, ngoba ukulunywa yinyoka kungaphakathi kwawo, futhi anisoze naphulukiswa ebuthini bawo. Noma ngubani othatha uphawu lwesilo uyoba nesabelo sakhe echibini lomlilo kanye nesilo nomprofethi wamanga. Akulotshiwe yini eZwini lami eliNgcwele? Anifundanga yini? Anizwanga yini? Buyelani othandweni lwami nize kimi, bantwana bami, ngoba nginibiza kulelihora lokugcina, ngoba usizi olukhulu lusemnyango wenu.

Ngizweni, isho iNkosi yamaBandla:

Ngoba nginguye, iNdodana yoMuntu eyabethelwa ngaphansi kukaPontiyu Pilatu, eyafela izono zenu yavuswa ngosuku lwesithathu. Ngavuswa futhi ngakhazimuliswa.

NgiyiNkosi yamakhosi noMbusi wababusi.

NgiyiNdodana yoMuntu, iNkosi elungileyo, iNkosi yeqiniso ethembekileyo neqinisileyo, futhi akekho noyedwa kini ongahlwithwa ezandleni zami.

NginguMalusi omuhle, futhi akekho onokuhlwitha izimvu zami esandleni sami.

Konke uBaba anginike khona, ngiyokwethula kuye, futhi ngifisa ukunethula kuBaba ningenasici, nasisihla, nasici, nembethe ilineni elimhlophe elicwiliswe egazini lami. Okuwukuphela kwendlela umuntu angama ngayo phambi kwami wukwamukela umhlatshelo wami wezono zenu. Ningalibali nezoni! Ningalibali; ningalibali! Phumani phakathi kwazo nizihlukanise nezono zazo. Vumani izono zenu kimi nivume nezono zawokhokho benu kimi, ngoba zidlulela kini. Philani impilo engininike yona, nihambe endleleni enginibekele yona, njengoba nginibizile ngamagama. Ningaphambukeli kwesokunxele noma kwesokudla, kodwa qaphelisisani ninikele umqondo wenu owodwa emazwini ami. Nokho, abaningi bayosenqaba isimemo sami, futhi abaningi bayobhubha. Kholwani yimi, ngoba uthando lukaNkulunkulu luyosindisa bonke ababiza igama leNkosi. Kholwani yimi, nemifula yamanzi aphilayo iyogeleza ivela ezibilinini zenu njengoba izono zenu zigezwa zihlanzeka nomoya wenu wenziwa uphelele. Wozani kimi kusesekhona isikhathi; vumani igama lami; ngenzeni iNkosi, ngoba alukho usindiso kwelinye igama elinikezwe ngaphansi kwezulu abantu abangasindiswa ngalo.
