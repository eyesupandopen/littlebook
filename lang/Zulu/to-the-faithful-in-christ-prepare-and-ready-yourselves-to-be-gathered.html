Kwabakholwayo kuKristu: Lungisani futhi Nizilungiselele Ukubuthelwa, uMyeni wenu Usondela!

Yizwani, yizwani iZwi likaYeshua HaMashiach futhi nithobise izinhliziyo zenu ngokulalela umsindo wamaZwi ami! Kunjalo-ke, ukuthi abaningi babantu bami baphambukele kwesokudla nabaningi kakhulu baphambukela kwesokunxele, baze basuka kuKristu Jesu iNkosi yabo, njengoba nje ngiza ukuzonibuthela kimi uma nihlala nithembekile ezwini lami ngokulalela okuphelele amaZwi ami. Ngoba, ngiqinisile, ngiqinisile, ngiyiNkosi yenu yeqiniso, iNgonyama, uMphristi Ophakeme, noMesiya! Yebo, nginibizela nonke ezingalweni zami zothando ukuba nibuthelwe kimi kungazelelwe nangalindelekile manje, bonke labo abalinda ngokulalela ngokukholwa okuphelele, futhi ikakhulu bonke labo abahlubukile balandela le nkohliso ehloswe nguHeylel Ben Shachar ukukhohlisa izinhliziyo zabangenabuqili, nokuchoboza okulindele nina kokulunga kokukhululwa okulungile nokuphelele, ngisho nokukhululwa kwenu okuphelele okuzofikela abathembekileyo kuKristu, kungazelelwe nangalindelekile manje!

Ngisho namazwi amanga anikezwa ngobuxoki ngabaprofethi bamanga ababeka izinsuku, baze bakhulume ngobuxoki egameni likaJesu Kristu, baze bathi bakhulumela mina! Kodwa abakhulumi amaZwi ami, futhi abathetheli isithelo sami, futhi abangazi, futhi ngiyakusho kubo bonke abasho kanjalo ngobuxoki ngalolo suku olukhulu: "Sukani kimi nina benzi bokubi, angizange nginazi!" Kunjalo-ke, ukuthi abaningi babathembekileyo kuKristu abayibandla lami umzimba wami, ngisho nomakoti wami manje bahleli bedabukile bedumele, njengoba nje uMyeni wabo esondela njengoba kumenyezelwe, kwashiwo futhi kwathenjiswa! Kodwa abaningi benu manje bahleli bethule sengathi ukubuthwa okukhulu kwabathembekileyo kuKristu kungamiswa, noma kukhanselwe nguHeylel Ben Shachar, ngoba ngiqinisile, ngiqinisile, nguyena owahlela ngobuxoki ukuhamba kwabathembekileyo kuKristu futhi abaningi babathembekileyo bami badla amazwi akhe amanga ngobuhwaba sengathi la mazwi amanga avela kuKristu Jesu uqobo! Yizwani amaZwi ami bandla lami mzimba wami ngisho nomakoti wami! Anilizwa yini izwi lami? Aniwaboni yini amaZwi ami? Ngoba ngeke kudlule ngisho ne-yod elilodwa, noma uphaphe lwe-yod kuze kugcwaliseke konke NGOKUQONDILE njengoba kulotshiwe futhi kwathenjiswa.

Nokho kunjalo, ukuthi abaningi benu manje bahleli bedangele, futhi bephukile izinhliziyo ekungalalelini kwenu, kangangokuthi manje nihleli nidangele ngaphansi kokulawulwa ngamazwi abahleki nabaklolodayo, ukwahlulelwa kwabo okuyobafikela kungazelelwe nangalindelekile manje futhi abaningi, abaningi balaba bayowela ekwahlulelweni okuphakade, futhi ngeke baphinde banikwe udumo lokubiza igama likaJesu kuze kube sekupheleni kwabo, ngoba insindiso ngokufela ukholo iyilungelo nodumo olunikezwa labo abayizindlalifa zokuphila okuphakade nokungapheliyo abayothola umvuzo omkhulu ngokuma kwabo, futhi ngiyobamukela kimi phakade naphakade othandweni lwami ngabo, kodwa uMlobokazi wami othembekileyo oyothakaselwa yibo bonke ngalolo suku! Ngakho-ke, qinani Mlobokazi wami futhi nizifake usawoti emaZwini ami, ngisho namaZwi Evangeli Laphakade engilinike emhlabeni ukuba nginilungiselele zithandwa zami, ngakho-ke bophelani amaZwi obufakazi besithunywa sami nenceku yami ngokuqinile ezinhliziyweni zenu, nazi ukuthi la maZwi ahlanzekileyo anikezwa ngokuqondile nguYahweh, ebusweni bukaKristu Jesu, futhi ngiyawadedela ngokuqonda ngomyalo wami njengoba ngiwambula ngokuphelele nangokunembile enhliziyweni yesithunywa sami, ngisho nalapho engiwahlwanyele khona, ngisho nokulondolozwa ukuze kudedelwe ngomyalo wami, futhi ngempela amaZwi ami anikezwa ngalo lonke iqiniso nokunemba. Selah.

Ngiqinisile, ngiqinisile, njengoba kunjalo: UHeylel Ben Shachar, ngenzondo enkulu, ufuna ngenkuthalo ukuketula ukholo lwabo bonke labo ukholo lwabo lungaketulwa ngamazwi akhe amanga nenkohliso yakhe yobuqili, ngisho nabo bonke labo ukholo lwabo lungemi ngokugcwele kulelo dwala leqiniso lesisekelo, uKristu Jesu, kodwa kunalokho kugxile ekungalalelini emazwini amanga abantu abayizoni, abangamazi uKristu Jesu futhi abangangikhonzi nakancane, kodwa kunalokho uHeylel Ben Shachar wamanga. Futhi ngamazwi abo amanga nezithelo zokungalungi kufanele nibazi; kodwa abaningi babathembekileyo bami basenezindlebe ezilumayo enifisa ukuba zikitazwe ngamazwi amanga nanokuthoba okungamanga, ngisho namazwi amanga uKristu Jesu angawathumelanga, futhi engawavumelanga, futhi angisoze ngathumela nelilodwa izwi lamanga ukuphikisa imibhalo yami eNgcwele, futhi angisoze ngakhuluma nelilodwa izwi eliphikisana nombhalo osunguliwe engiwunikile, ngoba ngiqinisile, ngiqinisile, ngiyiZwi lika-YHVH, uMINA ENGINGUYE Omkhulu owenziwa inyama, futhi ngenziwe umoya ophilayo, futhi angisoze ngaziphikisa nakancane, nakuba beqamba amanga. Ngakho-ke, ngiyaniyala ngalo lonke igunya ukuba nibambe amaZwi ami ngokuqinile ezinhliziyweni zenu ngokuzinikela okusanda kufakwa usawoti ukuze nizisize ekungathembekini kwenu, ukuze nonke enikholwe la mazwi amanga niphenduke niwavume kimi, ukuze nginihlambulule ngokugcwele egazini lami lokuhlawulela futhi ukuze nilungiswe ngokugcwele ukubonakala kwami okusondele phezu kwenu kungazelelwe nangalindelekile manje, lapho lonke izwe lihlehla kuKristu Jesu, obengabasindisa ngokuphelele. Ngisho njengoba behlehla ngokuzinikela okuphelele esithombeni somfel'ukholo wamanga owaklanywa futhi wanikezwa ukuphawula labo abayothola insindiso ngomlilo womcwebisi, ngoba lokhu kuyisiqalo sempi emelene nabangcwele esezokhululwa ngokugcwele ukuze kusindiswe abangalaleli abasasebugqilini besono, ukuze inyama iwele ekubhujisweni, ukuze ngempela umoya usindiswe, Selah.

Ngiqinisile ngiqinisile, nginguKristu Jesu, ngisho uYeshua HaMashiach, futhi ngiza ukuzoduduza amanxeba abangalaleli phakathi kwabathembekileyo bami, ngisho nabo bonke abangazivumela ukuba baphendulwe emaZwini ami Evangeli Laphakade, ngisho namaZwi anikezwa ngabaphostoli bami nabaprofethi ekuqaleni kwalesi sikhathi somusa, ngisho namaZwi eqiniso adalula izimfihlakalo zami ngezikhathi zawo zokwembulwa, ngisho namaZwi ayiqiniso nanamuhla njengoba ayenjalo lapho eqala ukunikezwa yimina ezindlebeni zalabo ababekhona, kodwa ikakhulukazi abafundi bami abavela kubo abaphostoli bami: Qaphelani: lindani, nikhuleke: ngoba anazi ukuthi isikhathi siyini.

Nokho abaningi benu bavumela amazwi amanga aphikisana namaZwi ami eqiniso esithembiso, ngisho namazwi aklanyelwe ukuketula ukholo lwenu ngokwakha okulindelwe okungamanga osukwini engingalunikezanga, ngisho nokulindela okuchotshozwa ngenhloso ukuze kuniminzise ekulindeleni okungagcwalisekile yilabo abanganikhathaza, ngisho nabo bonke labo abeza kini ngokungathembeki okukhulu benikeza amanga aphikisana nombhalo, ngisho namazwi amanga anikezwa yilabo abakhuluma ngokuphambene namaZwi ami, ngisho nalabo abaprofetha ngamanga egameni lami.

Nokho, abaningi phakathi kwebandla lami umzimba wami ngisho nomakoti wami bayaqhubeka nokuvumela laba belusi bamanga ukuba baketule okulindelekile kwabo ukuthi uMyeni wabo usondela ngosuku abazinqumela lona, ngisho nesiprofetho samanga esiphambene nalokho okusekumenyezelwe ngu-YHVH, uMINA ENGINGUYE Omkhulu, futhi ngisho nanguKristu Jesu ngomyalo ka-Adonai-YHVH ngoba ngihlala ngenza intando yalowo ongithumile. Ngoba ngiyiZwi elenziwa inyama elihlala ngempela phakathi kwenu ngisho nakini ngalowo moya ongcwele wobudodana, ogcwalisa ngokugcwele izibani Zomhlaba ezigqokiswe amandla avela phezulu! Noma kunjalo abaningi benu bakhetha ngokuzithandela ukuhlala ebumnyameni baze bangawanaki wona amaZwi angacima imicibisholo evuthayo yababi, eningi lenu eliyikhetha ngobuwula. Ngiqinisile, ngiqinisile, nginguye ohlezi nephephelo esandleni aze aphephethe amalangabi okuhlanzwa, ukuze ngempela ngempela, nonke nilungiselelwe ukubonakala kweNkosi yenu, ngoba ngiyeza ngani Zithandwa zami, futhi ubani okhona ongivimba? Ngabuza, futhi ngiyabuza futhi, NGITHI UBANI!

Nokho, kunjalo. Ukuthi uMlobokazi wami manje uhlezi edangele ngokudideka, ebambeke emazwini alabo abakhonkonthayo nabahlebayo! Ngakho-ke ngiyaniyala ukuba naliqhelelanise nala mazwi amanga, ngisho nabo bonke ababeka ukholo lwabo nokwethemba kubathakathi bamanga abakhonkonthayo nabahlebayo nabakhuluma ngokuphambene namaZwi ami okuphila, ngoba ngishilo ngokusobala emibhalweni, futhi umbhalo awunakwephulwa: Ukuthi akekho muntu emhlabeni owazi usuku, isikhathi, noma ihora lokubuthwa okukhulu kwabathembekileyo kuKristu okuhlelelwe ukwenzeka kungazelelwe nangalindelekile manje. Futhi nginiyale nonke ukuba nigcine ukulindela kwezinhliziyo zenu nemiqondo yenu kugxile ngokugcwele eNkosini yenu Yaphakade Yokulunga esondela ngokuqinisekile ngenxa yenu Zithandwa zami, ngisho nazo zonke izintombi zami ezihlakaniphile ezilungise izintambo zazo, futhi zagcwalisa izibani zazo ngamafutha ahlanzekileyo omoya wami, ukuze ngempela ngempela nilungiselelwe ukubonakala koMyeni wenu futhi ukuze nilungiswe ngokugcwele futhi nilinde nilungile kanye nezihambi zethu. Ngisho nabo bonke ababiziweyo, abakhethiweyo, nabamenyiweyo kulowo mshado omkhulu woMyeni kumakoti wakhe, ngisho lolo suku oluqonde phezu kwenu nonke kungazelelwe nangalindelekile manje.

Pho kungani uMlobokazi wami ehlezi edangele ngokudabuka sengathi uMyeni wenu unishiyile futhi unishiye nime eceleni kwendlela nilahliwe, nishiyiwe futhi nephukile izinhliziyo ekulindeleni kwenu? Cha, bathembekile bami lezi zinto akuzona ezenu, kodwa zizokunikezwa ekwahlulelweni kwabangathembekile, nabo bonke abenqaba isimemo seNkosi yabo Yaphakade Yokulunga sokuzilungiselela ngokwemiyalo yami, ngakho konke ukulalela kothando ezwini nemiyalelo yeNkosi yabo!

Ngakho-ke, ngiza ukuzonikhumbuza futhi ngala maZwi anikezwa ngezikhathi zasendulo Ezinganeni Zesithembiso, ngisho no-Israyeli ngokwenyama ngezikhathi zokwembulwa kwazo, ngisho namaZwi amiyo kuze kube namuhla zithandwa zami, ngakho-ke, yizwani amaZwi eNkosi yenu futhi namukele imfundiso yami ngabo bonke ubumnene, ukuthobeka, ukuphansi kwenhliziyo, nokulungela komqondo. Yizwani Nilalele: Futhi uma ucabanga enhliziyweni yakho, Sizokwazi kanjani izwi iNkosi engalikhulumanga? Lapho uMprofethi ekhuluma Egameni leNkosi, uma into ingalandeli noma ingenzeki, leyo yinto iNkosi engayikhulumanga, kodwa uMprofethi uyikhulume ngokugabadela: ngakho-ke ngeke umesabe.

Nokho, abaningi phakathi kwenu baye bakhohlwa la maZwi anikezwa njengemingcele neziqaphi zokuvikela ezimpisini ezihahayo, ukwahlulelwa kwazo ngokuqinisekile kungalibali. Ngakho-ke, qhathanisani ngokukhuthala amazwi anoma ubani othi ukhulumela uKristu Jesu, naye onguYeshua HaMashiach ngokukhuthala namaZwi ami asunguliwe anikeziwe futhi avikelwe emibhalweni engcwele, ngisho namaZwi angcwele anikezwa ngezikhathi zasendulo ngu-Adonai-YHVH, uMINA ENGINGUYE Omkhulu, ngisho namaZwi angcwele anikezwa ngezikhathi zamanje ngu-Adonai-YHVH noKristu Jesu, naye onguYeshua HaMashiach.

Ngakho-ke, Zithandwa zami, yizwani amaZwi eNkosi yenu yaphakade Yokulunga, ngisho namaZwi anikezwa ngezandla zami njengoba ngiwembula enhliziyweni yesithunywa sami esinamandla nenceku futhi ngezandla zami nentando yami la maZwi ayembulwa ngokuqonda enhliziyweni yesithunywa sami esinamandla nenceku lapho mina, uKristu Jesu nginikeza la maZwi enduduzo, ngisho namaZwi athobayo okududuza abathembekileyo kuKristu, ngisho nezintombi zami ezihlakaniphile ezizilungisile ngala maZwi anikezwe ukuba akhululwe emhlabeni kubathembekileyo kuKristu ngakho konke ukulalela ukuze nonke nilungiswe futhi nilungiseke futhi nilinde eceleni kwendlela izibani zenu zigcwele, nezintambo zenu zilungisiwe, ngisho nani nonke enithathe umlilo wami nakhanyisa izibani zenu ngokulalela okulungileyo emaZwini ami, ngoba ngiyasondela ngenxa yenu Zithandwa zami.

Kunjalo-ke, uMyeni uyesondela eMlobokazini wakhe futhi umhlaba manje uzogubuzelwa ubumnyama obukhulu, ngoba ngeke ngabelane ngenkazimulo yami nomunye, futhi njengoba ubumnyama obunzima busibekela umhlaba, inkazimulo nokukhanya kokubonakala kwami kuzokhanyisa umhlaba njengoba unyazi luphuma empumalanga luye entshonalanga, futhi kungazelelwe nangalindelekile manje iBandla lami, uMzimba wami, ngisho noMlobokazi wami bazozithola bemi ezulwini lamazulu, bemi egumbini lesihlalo sobukhosi sika-YHVH, uMINA ENGINGUYE Omkhulu, ngisho nomzuzwana wesikhathi u-Adonal-YHVH awulinda kusukela ephakadeni elidlule, ngisho nesikhathi esidala kangangokuthi aninakusiqonda ngalesi sikhathi. Futhi nonke benifihliwe emandleni akhe nenhliziyo emangalisayo yothando nokukhanya, ngisho nalapho anigcina khona nivikelekile emfihlakalweni yakhe enkulu kunazo zonke ngisho Imfihlakalo Enkulu kaYahweh, uMINA ENGINGUYE Omkhulu, futhi ngempela ngempela amazulu alungisiwe futhi asejabulile ngokufika kwenu okungazelelwe manje, ngisho nangaphambi kokuba nifike. Ngoba ngisho nezingelosi ezingcwele nezidalwa ezinhle kakhulu zasemazulwini zilindele ngokulangazela ukubonakala kwenu nobukhona benu Zithandwa zami.

Ngakho-ke, phumuzani imiqondo nezinhliziyo zenu ngokugcwele othandweni lwami ngani bantwana bami, futhi ningavumeli amazwi amanga anikezwa ngabaprofethi bamanga abakhonkonthayo nabahlebayo nababeka izinsuku zomshado wami waseGalile ukuba aketule ukholo lwenu, futhi anganenzi nidabuke enhliziyweni sengathi ngizolahla uMlobokazi wami othandekayo ozilungisile ngokulalela kothando ezwini loMyeni wakhe. Ngakho-ke ngiyaniyala zithandwa zami ukuba nigxilise yonke inhliziyo yenu, umphefumulo, ingqondo, namandla enu emaZwini eNkosi yenu Yaphakade Yokulunga, ngisho namaZwi ami angcwele ombhalo engiwambulile kuleyo ncwajana evuliwe engiyivulile ezandleni zesithunywa sami nenceku, ngoba incwajana yesambulo isahluko 10 iqukethe amazwi ezinduma eziyisikhombisa kanye nevangeli laphakade ngokwengxenye, futhi lawa ngamaZwi anikezelwe ukuhola abantu bami ezivunweni zokuhlupheka kukaJakobe ngisho neviki lamashumi ayisikhombisa likaDaniyeli. Ngisho namaZwi abevaliwe futhi agcinwe eyimfihlo ngomyalo ka-YHVH, uMINA ENGINGUYE Omkhulu, ngoba ngiqinisile, ngiqinisile, amazwi nezwi lezinduma eziyisikhombisa liyakhuluma, futhi lezi zinduma eziyisikhombisa ziwukusebenza kwencwadi yesambulo, ngisho neviki lamashumi ayisikhombisa likaDaniyeli elizophetha ekubhujisweni okuphelele nokuphelele kombuso kaHeylel Ben Shachar phakade naphakade, futhi umhlaba uzobuswa Ngokulunga, ngisho nokulunga lapho kungekho nto embi eyongena embusweni wami weminyaka eyinkulungwane, ngakho phumulani ngethemba elikhulu kula maZwi ethemba nesithembiso saphakade Zithandwa zami.

NginguKristu Jesu, futhi ngiza ukuletha la maZwi okusola, okukhuza, nothando kwabalalelayo, nabangalaleli ukuze ngempela, ngempela, ningaweli ohlangothini lokungalaleli lwenkemba yami yomoya, ukuze nibalwe njengabafanele ukubuthelwa nabathembekileyo kungazelelwe nangalindelekile manje. Ngoba ngisho nakule mizuzu yokugcina ngaphambi kokuba lelo khwelo elinamandla lizwakale, nangaphambi kokuba lelo cilongo elinamandla likhale ngamandla nangokungalindelekile manje, ngisanyakazisa futhi ngibangele ukunyakaziswa okukhulu ukuba kufike phezu kwabantu bami, ukuze nginyakazise abangathembekile ukuze babe abathembekile ngokulalela okulungile, noma bawele emlilweni wokucwengwa ekungalalelini kwabo, ngaphambi nje kwaleso sikhathi se-kairos sihlangane nesikhathi sokulandelana okuzonifica nonke kungazelelwe nangalindelekile manje. Njengoba nje umhlaba ulilela umsindisi wawo wamanga, ngisho nomfel'ukholo wamanga engingamthumelanga, kodwa owanikezwa nguBaraba esikhundleni salokho, kodwa abaningi kangaka manje balandela lo mfel'ukholo wamanga, beze badukiswa ezwini likaKristu Jesu naye onguYeshua HaMashiach, Selah.

Kunjalo-ke, ukuthi ngilungiselela labo abayosala ngokugcwele emlilweni wokucwengwa, ngisho nabo bonke abazonikezwa ithuba lokubiza igama likaJesu Kristu kuze kube sekupheleni kwabo, ngoba bonke abathanda ukuphila kwabo kuze kube sekupheleni ngaleyo ndlela bayothola insindiso yaphakade ngempela ngempela, engalinganiswa nokuhlupheka abayokukhuthazelela ngenxa yegama lami.

Zilungiseleleni Bandla lami Mzimba wami ngisho noMlobokazi wami, lungisani futhi nizilungiselele ngokwamaZwi ami angcwele nemibhalo yeVangeli Laphakade futhi ningilinde zithandwa zami ngoba ngiyeza kini futhi anisoze nadumazeka ekulindeleni kwenu. Ngakho-ke, yamukelani ukusola kwami nokuyala kwami futhi nilalele amaZwi ami nezixwayiso zami ngoba ngiyeza kini Zithandwa zami, futhi angisoze ngashiya abathembekileyo kuKristu emlilweni wokucwengwa, noma ekwahlulelweni kwezoni okungekona okwenu.

Zilungiseleleni Zithandwa zami, ngakho konke ukulalela nokulindela, futhi nizicwilise emaZwini eVangeli Laphakade, futhi niwabambe ezingqondweni nasezinhliziyweni zenu futhi niqonde ingcebo yombhalo engiyinike emhlabeni ukunigcina kulezi zikhathi zokwembulwa, ngisho nokwembulwa engakuqala ngamacilongo amaningi alungisiwe emhlambini wami omncane azwakale ngokwethembeka, ngisho namacilongo alungisa indlela yesithunywa sami nenceku eyisitsha esikhethiweyo seNkosi yaso Yaphakade, uKristu Jesu, naye onguYeshua HaMashiach, ngisho nalapho ngembula khona amaZwi ami esitsheni sami esihlukanisiwe esivumelana futhi sizibophezele ngokugcwele ezinhlosweni engimthume zona, futhi kungomyalo wami namandla ami ukuthi iminwe yakhe ithole okhiye ukuze yembule la maZwi angcwele engiwanike kuye ukuba awadedele emhlabeni okokuqala ngqa, ngisho namaZwi ahlelelwe ukuthola amehlo, izindlebe, nezinhliziyo zabo bonke labo athunyelwe kubo, Selah.

Ngakho-ke, yizwani umsindo wezwi lami kulesi simemezelo esingcwele esinikezelwe ukudonsela abathembekileyo emyalweni wami, nokunihlelela nonke ekulaleleni umsindo walelo cilongo, ukuze nibuthelwe kimi ngomyalo wami.

Bhekani! Ngiyasayina lesi simemezelo nencwadi ngesandla sesithunywa sami nenceku, kodwa kusayinwe ngegama lami siqu ngalo lonke uthando negunya,

uYeshua HaMashiach, iNkosi Yaphakade Yokulunga.
