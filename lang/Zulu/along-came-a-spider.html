Ufakazi Ombaxa: Israyeli
Okubabayo Okumnandi
Novemba 25, 2024

Kwase Kufika Isicabucabu

Yizwani, yizwani, mazwi ami O Israyeli, nithobise izinhliziyo zenu ngokulalela umsindo wezwi lami, namukele ngokuthobeka amazwi kaYeshuwa HaMashiach, ukuze ngamazwi eNdaba eNhle yaphakade nizuze insindiso nifinyelele ekuphileni okuphakade kuMesiya wenu weqiniso, olwa ngenene ngenxa yenu ngothando. Ngakho-ke ngize ukuzonixwayisa, nina eningabakwaJakobe nabo bonke abafunda futhi bamukele amazwi eNdaba eNhle yaphakade ngokukholwa okusindisayo, ukuze babe nokukholwa kuYeshuwa HaMashiach, onguye futhi uJesu Kristu, futhi babe nokukholwa kuYahweh, uMINA OMKHULU, lowo iNdaba eNhle yaphakade eyayifihliwe enhliziyweni yakhe kuze kube yizikhathi zokwembulwa kwayo. Ngakho-ke uJohane uMambuli wavalela amazwi emidumo eyisikhombisa ayeyizwile. Ngakho ngize ukuzonixwayisa ngesicabucabu esiluke ubulembu baso, sabamba izwe ekusingatheni kwaso; njengoba nje sizondla ngegazi labanye, ukuze nigweme izindwangu zaso eziningi, ningabanjwa ekusingatheni kwaso okubulalayo.

Ngakho-ke, thobisani izinhliziyo zenu ngokulalela, nizwe amazwi kaMesiya wenu weqiniso, uYeshuwa HaMashiach! Ngokuba ngeqiniso ngize ukwenza ohlakaniphileyo phakathi kwenu ahlakaniphe kakhulu, ukuze nikwazi ukuhlukanisa izikhathi eniphila kuzo, futhi niziqonde izitha zenu nemvelo yazo nezindlela zazo zamanga; ngoba zihamba ngobunono obukhulu nangokucasha, futhi zilwa ngenkohliso. Ngoba bonke bangabazenzisi abazenzisayo, nesicabucabu sihamba ngobunono obukhulu nangokucasha kuze kubanjwe isisulu sesicabucabu obulembwini baso, isicabucabu esiyohlale sisisebenzisela izinhloso zaso ezimbi.

Ngakho-ke, ngize ukuzonixwayisa ngesicabucabu nokunikhombisa izindlela zaso, ukuze nigweme ubuthi baso futhi nizigcine kude nokusingatha kwaso okukhubazayo nokubulalayo. Kodwa yisiphi isicabucabu esake sazenzela umbuso nesizwe? Futhi yisiphi isicabucabu esake sajoyina umfelandawonye wabanqotshiweyo, sizimisele ukwenza ingxenye yaso ebubini bokuhlubuka? Futhi yisiphi isicabucabu esake sathatha ubuyena balabo ababanjwe obulembwini baso? Nokho sinjalo isicabucabu engize ukuzonixwayisa ngaso, futhi ngeqiniso, ngeqiniso, isicabucabu singesinye sezingxenye eziningi zombuso wezitha, futhi siyisicabucabu esinobuthi nesiyingozi ngempela.

Ngoba isicabucabu siluka ubulembu baso ngokucasha nangenkohliso. Ngisho nobulembu benkohliso nobulembu besikweletu isicabucabu siyaluka, futhi ubulembu baso manje bumboze umhlaba wonke ekusingatheni kwaso kokucasha. Cabanga ngesicabucabu endaweni yaso yemvelo, siluka ubulembu baso ngokucasha ezindaweni lapho singabamba khona isisulu saso, ngisho nalapho isisulu saso sihlala singanakile obulembwini baso. Futhi lapho isisulu saso silwela ukuzikhulula obulembwini lapho sasibhajwe khona singazi, sizibopha kakhulu, isicabucabu siyasukuma sijove ubuthi baso ngenkathi sisonga isisulu saso obulembwini baso. Bese isicabucabu sigcina isisulu saso ngenjongo yaso siqu, sizondle kancane kancane ngesisulu saso kuze kungasali lutho, sishiye isisulu saso siwugwadule ngokuphelele kuze kube yilapho singasekho ngempela.

Nokho zinjalo izindlela zesicabucabu semvelo esidalelwe izinjongo zikaYahweh, ngaphandle kokuthi ngubani kini owake wabona isicabucabu sisebenza ngokubambisana nombuso nesizwe sezinye izicabucabu; kepha abawiweyo bahlanganise izicabucabu zabo kulezi zikhathi zokuphela. Ngakho-ke ngize ukuzonixwayisa ngabantu abavela kuJafete, owazala uGomere, kwathi kuGomere kwavela u-Ashkenazi, kwathi ku-Ashkenazi kwaba yisizwe ngoba u-Ashkenazi usetafuleni lezizwe ngohlelo lwasendulo, ngisho nasezinsukwini lapho abantu babephila ngaphansi kwesivumelwano sikaNowa, leso sivumelwano isintu esasilahla; nokho kwakukhona u-Abrama othembekileyo owayefuna izindlela zikaYahweh lapho umhlaba uphinda uhlubuka kumdali wawo. Nokho, nakuba umuntu wemvelo wasenqaba isivumelwano sikaYahweh; uYahweh uthembekile ezithembisweni zakhe ngokunjalo. Futhi asikho nesinye isithembiso sesivumelwano uYahweh asenza noNowa (owayengumuntu olungileyo emehlweni kaYahweh) esake sawela phansi singagcwalisekanga, futhi konke kuyoba u-yebo no-amen kuze kube phakade ngoba uYahweh uhlale ethembekile ngokuphelele!

Ngemuva kokuba umhlaba ugcwele amanzi ngezinsuku zikaNowa, umhlaba wahlukaniswa ngenkatho yefa phakathi kwamadodana kaNowa ngezinsuku zikaPelegi, ngakho uJafete wangena efeni lakhe emazweni asenyakatho. Futhi abantu babenolimi olulodwa, namadodana kaHamu abuthanela ezweni elaba yiBabiloni. Futhi izikhulu zikaNimrode zelulekana noPhuti, noMisirayimi, noKushe, noKhanani nemindeni yabo ukwakha idolobha labo kanye nombhoshongo oqinileyo kuwo, ngisho nombhoshongo ophakemeyo ukuze balwe namazulu nokuthi bazibuthanisele ndawonye ukuze bazenzele igama ngokuhlubuka emyalweni kaYahweh wokuthi bathethelele futhi bande ukuze baphinde bagcwalise umhlaba. Kodwa zinjalo izindlela zomuntu wemvelo ongenakholo, lowo ezinkanukweni zakhe nasekuziqhenyeni kwakhe ekhonza isono, ngalokho ekhonza uBaba wesono; yena udeveli, ngendlela yokungalaleli kukaYahweh kwesintu ngenxa yesono enyameni yomuntu wemvelo.

Futhi enzalweni kaNimrode eyakhula ebubini kuthiwa komubi kuphuma ububi obuningi. Ngakho uNimrode wangcolisa umhlaba ngezindlela zabanqotshiweyo, kwathi kubathakathi bakhe nezangoma kwavela izikole zemfihlakalo zasendulo lapho abathakathi babanqotshiweyo bafundisa khona izimfihlakalo zabo ezingelusizo kulabo abayophonswa emalangabini kuze kube phakade ezonweni zoSathane, futhi bayohlanganyela nabo efeni labo laphakade. Futhi uNimrode wavuka embusweni wokuqala ngemva kukazamcolo kaNowa, lapho uNimrode ayebusa khona umhlaba ngokungalungi; ngisho nokubusa abanqotshiweyo abafuna ukuzitholela kona ngokugcwele emhlabeni, kodwa ngeke babe nenkathi yegolide kodwa isahlulelo esinzima.

Ngakho uYahweh ekwahluleleni kwakhe wabangela ukuba umhlaba uvuleke ugwinye ingxenye yesithathu yombhoshongo wabo, nomlilo wehla uvela ezulwini wadla enye ingxenye yesithathu yombhoshongo, kwathi ingxenye yesithathu yavunyelwa ukuba isale njengesikhumbuzo ngabo. Ngakho-ke igama lawo libizwa ngokuthi yiBabeli, ngoba uYahweh waphithanisa khona ulimi lomhlaba wonke: futhi kusukela lapho uYahweh wabahlakaza emhlabeni wonke. Futhi izizwe eziningi nabantu benqaba futhi bakhohlwa uYahweh owangena esivumelwaneni noNowa, ngakho bakhohlwa isivumelwano uNowa asenza noYahweh ngokwefa labo nangokwezilimi ababezikhuluma bakhohlwa isivumelwano sikaYahweh noNowa, futhi baphendukela ezindleleni zabanqotshiweyo ngoba akukho okunye abangakukhetha. (Nokho u-Abrama waba ngu-Abrahama futhi olungileyo uyophila ngokukholwa!)

EBabiloni uNimrode wenza ububi obuningi, wenza izithixo eziningi wazibiza ngonkulunkulu, wenza nobuthathu bakhe obuhlambalazayo, obungcwele obuthathu, obuhloselwe ukuhlekisa ngobunkulunkulu beqiniso nenzalo ethenjisiwe yowesifazane nokudukisa amadodana abantu ekungalungini. Ngakho-ke uNimrode wamisa izikole zakhe zemfihlakalo njengoba ulwazi lwabawiweyo lwaluqala ukusabalala futhi lumboze umhlaba, futhi isintu saphinda sashintsha iqiniso likaYahweh laba ngamanga, lakhonza futhi lakhonza indalo kunomdali obusisiwe kuze kube phakade, Amen!

Futhi njengoba abantu babesakazwa bevela eBabiloni, kanjalo nolwazi olungamanga nezindlela zabawiweyo zasakazeka nabo, ngisho nazo zonke izenzo zabo ezinengekayo nezihlambalazayo zalabo abadla inyama enegazi nalabo abaphuza igazi lika-Adamu lapho impilo yenyama ihlala khona; izenzo ezinengekayo nezinengekayo ngokuphelele uYahweh azinqabele kanye, konke, naphakade ngoba u-Adamu wadalwa ngomfanekiso kaYahweh futhi impilo yenyama isegazini. Nokho bayaqhubeka ngezindlela zabo ezinengekayo nezihlambalazayo kuze kube namuhla, futhi imikhuba yabo nemikhosi yabo igcinwe kulabo abakhonza amadimoni futhi bakhothamele izithixo eziyizimungulu, futhi lezi yizindlela zesicabucabu, nokho isicabucabu sizenza uJakobe olungileyo ongenasabelo noma inkatho kuye.

Ngakho izindlela zobubi zagxila ezindleleni zabantu bezizwe futhi. Kwathi kuGomere kwavela u-Ashkenazi, kwathi ku-Ashkenazi ngokuhamba kwesikhathi kwavela isizwe esikhulu sabantu ababa yimizulane ababehlala futhi bezulazula emathafeni ase-Eurasia nasezindaweni ezizungezile, lapho benza khona umbuso wabo. Lowo mbuso wakhula ngokungalungi futhi wawa ezitheni zabo ngokunjalo. Izitha zabo zabahlasela ngenxa yokungalungi kwabo, futhi esikhundleni sokwamukela uJesu waseNazaretha nokusuka ebubini, bazenza uJakobe, kanti kwaJakobe kwakungakaze kube nesizwe sika-Ashkenazi kaJafete. Ngakho-ke bamphika uJesu waseNazaretha ngoba bangabakayise udeveli, futhi izinkanuko zakhe zibophezeleke ukuzenza kuze kube phakade.

Futhi kubantu base-Ashkenazi, ababezindlela zabo zimbi futhi zikhohlisayo, kwavela isizwe, kodwa sasingesona ngokuphelele sohlanga luka-Adamu, kodwa sasiwukhula olubi olutshalwe phakathi kukakolweni kaYahweh, olwazona unaphakade ekungalungini. Yilabo abazalwa nguyise udeveli ngoba imbewu kadeveli isekhona, nakuba ngizoyisiphula ngokuphelele futhi ngiyiphonse esithandweni somlilo njengoba uYahweh ememezele. Kwavela ku-Ashkenazi isicabucabu, kodwa sasingesona isicabucabu semvelo, kodwa sasiyisizwe sezicabucabu ezinengekayo, eziguqulwe emfanekisweni kaYahweh umdali wazo, eziboshelwe unaphakade ezinkanukweni zikaYise udeveli, ezizokwenza izinkanuko zakhe. Ngakho izicabucabu zasakazeka yonke indawo, zibaleka ezandleni zezitha zokungalungi ezamelana nezindlela zesicabucabu. Ngakho u-Ashkenazi wamphika uYeshuwa HaMashiach, angenasabelo noma inkatho kuye.

Ngakho-ke ngeqiniso isintu sidukiswe ngabawiweyo kusukela u-Adamu awa esimweni sakhe sokungabi nasono adalwa kuso ensimini yase-Edeni, futhi ukonakala kwasakazeka emhlabeni ngohlu lozalo lukaKhayini. Lokho konakala abalindi abawiweyo bakuqhubekisela phambili lapho behlela eNtabeni iHerimoni ngezinsuku zikaJarede. Ngakho-ke uYahweh wahlamba ukonakala kwabo ebusweni bomhlaba kulowo zamcolo omkhulu ngezinsuku zikaNowa, kodwa abawiweyo baphinde babuyisela izindlela zabo ngemva kukazamcolo, ngoba uYahweh wavumela isintu ukuba sizikhethele. Ngoba ngeqiniso, ngeqiniso, uYahweh unikeze konke akudalileyo inkululeko yokuzikhethela ekudalweni kwakhe ukuthi bayophila yini ngezindlela zikaYahweh noma bakhethe ukukhonza oSathane ekuhlubukeni kwabo, okuyikuhlubuka osekusondele ekupheleni. Ngoba mina, ngisho uYeshuwa HaMashiach, nginguye oyochoboza ikhanda lenyoka, ngisho noyochitha umbuso wayo ngokuphelele ukuze ungalokothi uvuke futhi.

Kunjalo futhi yiqiniso engalifundisa mayelana nombuso wezulu lapho ngifika kwa-Israyeli njengeWundlu likaNkulunkulu ngesikhathi sami enyameni eJudiya. Ngoba kulotshiwe kanje: Umbuso wezulu ufana nomuntu owahlwanyela imbewu enhle ensimini yakhe. Kodwa ngenkathi abantu belele, kwafika isitha sakhe, sahlwanyela ukhula phakathi kukakolweni, sahamba. Kwathi lapho isiqu sesimile, sathela izithelo, kwavela nokhula. Khona-ke abakhonzi bomninindlu beza, bathi kuye, Nkosi, awuhlwanyelanga yini imbewu enhle ensimini yakho? Luvelaphi-ke ukhula? Wathi kubo, Umuntu onomona okwenzile lokhu. Base bethi kuye abakhonzi, Uyafuna yini ukuba sihambe siyolubutha? Kodwa wathi, Cha, funa kuthi lapho niyobutha ukhula, nisiphule kanye nalo ukolweni. Kuyekeni kukhule ndawonye kuze kube isikhathi sokuvuna, ngesikhathi sokuvuna ngiyakusho kubavuni, Buthani kuqala ukhula, nilubophe izinyanda ukuze lushiswe: kodwa buthani ukolweni enqolobaneni yami.

Kunjalo ngoba ukolweni ukhothama ngokulalela, kuyilapho ukhula lumi luqondile ngobubi obunqabayo. Selah!

Kodwa kunjalo ukuthi izikhathi zokubutha sezifikile, futhi ngizobutha ukolweni wami enqolobaneni kaYahweh ngoba isikhathi sokuvuna izithelo zokuqala sesifikile. Ngakho kuyokwenzeka ukuthi ngiyolusiphula ngokuphelele ukhula ngilubophe, luyophonswa emlilweni ekwahlulelweni kwalo. Ngakho qondani umhlaba eniphila kuwo, ngisho nempi enazalelwa kuyo. Ngisho nempi yembewu eye yaqhubeka kusukela ensimini yase-Edeni lapho uYahweh amemezela khona impi enyokeni nakubawiweyo ngenxa yobubi babo nangokona ingadi kaYahweh. Leyo mpi iphetha ngokunqotshwa ngokuphelele kwabo bonke abahlubuke ngaleyo ndlela nabawiweyo, futhi ilindelwe yifa labo lesibabule esivuthayo.

Futhi uYahweh wamemezela kanje: Ngiyakubeka ubutha phakathi kwakho nowesifazane, naphakathi kwenzalo yakho nenzalo yakhe; wena uyomlimaza isithende sakhe, yena uyochoboza ikhanda lakho. Ngakho-ke isithende sami salinyazwa ekubethelweni kwami lapho ngazinikela njengePhasika eliphelele njengeWundlu likaNkulunkulu ngokuzithandela ezandleni zezitha zami ezinonya. Futhi ngiyinzalo ethenjisiwe yowesifazane eyochoboza ngokuphelele ikhanda likaSathane, futhi ngiyokwenza izitha zami isenabelo sezinyawo zami, ngoba ngiyiNgonyama yesizwe sakwaJuda futhi ngiza ngokulunga ukuze ngahlulele futhi ngilwe!

Ngakho-ke, qondani ukuthi impi yembewu inqotshwe ngokugcwele kuKristu Jesu, njengoba ngalobisa uPetru eminyakeni eminingi edlule: Nizelwe kabusha, hhayi ngembewu ebolayo, kodwa ngengaboliyo, ngezwi likaNkulunkulu eliphilayo nelimiyo kuze kube phakade. Futhi ngaphinda ngalobisa uJohane uMambuli encwadini yakhe: Noma ngubani ozelwe nguNkulunkulu akenzi isono: ngoba imbewu yakhe ihlala kuye, futhi akanakona, ngoba uzelwe nguNkulunkulu. Kulokhu abantwana bakaNkulunkulu bayabonakaliswa, nabantwana bakadeveli: noma ngubani ongenzi ukulunga akayena owakaNkulunkulu, nalowo ongamthandi umfowabo. Ngakho-ke, bonke ababekwe uphawu kuKristu abasoze bonakaliswa ngaleyo ndlela, futhi abanakona lapho beshintshwa khona ophawini lukaNkulunkulu ophilayo, yena uYahweh abangabakhe. Ngakho-ke, ningavumeli ubuwula obunjalo bunikhathaze bokuthi labo abazelwe kabusha ngomoya wami ongcwele wokuba ngamadodana futhi babekwe uphawu lukaYahweh bangahlwitshwa esandleni sami esinamandla nganoma iyiphi indlela. Ngakho-ke, kulabo abazuze insindiso, insindiso yenu ivikelekile.

Ngakho-ke izindlela zesicabucabu ngizozembula, futhi ngizosembula isicabucabu ngokugcwele, nakuba izicabucabu zithanda ukuhamba ngokucasha okukhulu, ngizoziveza izindlela zazo. Ngoba lezi zicabucabu eziguquliwe yilezo ezithatha ubuyena balabo ezibahlelele futhi zibabulale. Lezi yizicabucabu ezichuma onya olukhulu futhi zithanda ukuhlukumeza izisulu zazo ngaphandle kwesinye isizathu ngaphandle kokwanelisa izinkanuko zikaYise eziboshelwe ukumkhonza futhi eziyinzalo yakhe. Lezi zicabucabu eziguquliwe engikhuluma ngazo ziyizigebengu, namasela, nababulali, nabazenzisi (futhi zinecala lokunye okuningi kakhulu) ezingasoze zaneliseka futhi ezifisa ukufa kuka-Adamu ukuze zibe nenkathi yazo yegolide kuze kube phakade kanye nabawiweyo. Ngakho-ke izicabucabu zihambile emhlabeni wonke futhi zaluka izindwangu zazo ndawonye ngokucasha okukhulu nemfihlo.

Ngisho nobulembu obukhulu besikweletu ngoba isicabucabu siyachuma futhi sizicebisile ngegolide elizuzwe ngenzalo engalungile. Isicabucabu sizifihlile phakathi kukaJakobe futhi sazenza sengathi singuJakobe, futhi benze ukuzifihla okukholisayo. Ngoba isicabucabu asikwazi ukuzibonakalisa njengesicabucabu esibi, esiguquliwe; ngoba yimuphi umthiyi obeka ugibe emehlweni enyoni? Kodwa isicabucabu siyazifihla futhi sishintshe ukubukeka kwaso nezenzo zaso ukuze sihlangane nalabo esifisa ukubadla. Futhi lapho isicabucabu siqala ukuluka ubulembu baso phakathi kwalabo abavumele isicabucabu indawo yokuluka phakathi kwesizwe sabo ngokunganaki; isicabucabu sibe siletha ezinye izicabucabu eziza ukuzohlanganyela naso ekunqobeni kwaso okubi noma ngendlela yokhula oluningi oluchanyuselwa esakeni lamaqanda esicabucabu olugijimela ukuyoluka izindwangu zalo siqu nokuqhubeka nomjikelezo wokufa. Ngakho u-Adamu uboshiwe futhi usongwe yilezi zicabucabu ezimbi. Futhi kancane kancane nangenjabulo enkulu bayabamba futhi badle u-Adamu, nangenkohlakalo enkulu nobulwane isicabucabu sifuna umhlaba wonke ube ngowaso noYise bazo ababoshelwe ezinkanukweni zabo.

Ngoba enkohlisweni yaso, izicabucabu zase-Ashkenazi zizihlanganise no-Esawu, ngeTalmud, ngoba eminyakeni eminingi edlule bamukela iTalmud njengenkosi nombusi wabo, kodwa ababona abaphendukeli beqiniso bakaMose, futhi abalandeli indlela kaYeshuwa HaMashiach, kodwa bayazenzisa. Kwakungabakwa-Esawu ababulali bami, futhi ngokuhlakanipha kwamanga kuka-Esawu, ngisho namasiko abo ahlambalazayo abantu abawaphakamisa ngaphezu kwezindlela zikaYahweh, baye bangcolisa uJakobe futhi babamba umfowabo ka-Esawu izikhathi eziningi. Ngisho nokho ngathi kubo ngesikhathi sami enyameni njengeWundlu likaNkulunkulu: Nina ningabakayihlo udeveli, futhi izinkanuko zikayihlo niyozifeza: ubelokhu engumbulali kusukela ekuqaleni, futhi akahlalanga eqinisweni, ngoba alikho iqiniso kuye. Lapho ekhuluma amanga, ukhuluma okwakhe: ngoba ungumqambimanga, noyise wawo.

Nokho isicabucabu asikaze sisuke ezindleleni zabanqotshiweyo, futhi isicabucabu sisahlanganyela futhi siyazithokozisa ngemikhuba yaso emibi. Ngoba isicabucabu sidla izingane esizisongile njengesisulu saso futhi siphuza igazi lazo kwabancane esibahlukumezayo ekusingatheni kwaso. Ngoba isicabucabu sigcwalisa izinkanuko zaso ezinengekayo kwabancane abasuka esizweni sabo ababathumba ngokucasha okukhulu njengoba abancane bebanjwa obulembwini baso. Futhi yisiphi isicabucabu esake saba khona esiluka ubulembu futhi simbe ngaphansi komhlaba. Ngoba ubulembu besicabucabu buhamba nangaphansi komhlaba futhi ubulembu baso busibekela indawo enkulu, nombuso womhlaba wonke isicabucabu siwufuna. Ngakho-ke isicabucabu sihamba ngenkohliso enkulu ngoba zilwa ngenkohliso.

Nokho laba bazenzisi abazihlanganise nabawileyo ingunaphakade nabayinzalo yabo ngempela; bayazenzisa ukuba nguJakobe futhi babanga ngephutha izwe lesithembiso, abangenangxenye nankatho kulo kodwa bangabazenzisi abazenzisayo. Futhi ezindleleni zabo kuvela isicupho isilo esiphuma emhlabathini esiyosibekela uJakobe ngoba ngendlela yeTalmud ka-Esawu u-Ashkenazi uhlangene no-Esawu, futhi bayohlanganiswa kakhulu yimvu kadrako, yona imvu yabo yamanga enezimpondo ezimbili. Ngisho nemvu engeyona eyemvelo lapho olunye uphondo lubusa ulwandle ngokukaNowahide nolunye uphondo lubusa izwe lesithembiso ngokuhlambalaza okukhulu nenkohliso ngoba iluphondo oluncane lukaDaniyeli, futhi kanjalo umhlaba uyobuswa ngombuso wabo isikhashana. Ngoba bayohlanganisa onke amabutho nezigaba zobubi ngaphansi kombuso wabo, futhi bayowabuthela ndawonye empini ngalolo suku olukhulu, lapho beyophonswa phansi ngobudlova ingunaphakade nasekujezisweni okuphakade nasekwahlulelweni beyongena khona ingunaphakade.

Nokho izindlela zesicabucabu ziyizindlela zokubamba inkunzi, nokuqola, nokubulala, nenkohlakalo nezindlela eziningi ezimbi isicabucabu esizifundisayo nesiziphilayo ngoba isicabucabu sihlale sifuna ukwenza ezinye izicabucabu eziyizingane zesihogo ngokuphindwe kabili kunaso. Futhi kanjalo izicabucabu ziyadansa zizigubha ngokuqothula kwazo uhlanga lwabantu bezwe, zize zidanse zibambene ngezandla ngenkathi zidansa ngenjabulo ngokufa nokubhujiswa kwezisulu zazo eziningi zazo ezingabesifazane nezingane nezinsana; ngakho-ke ukubhujiswa kwazo akuphuzi. Nokho bathathe abaholi bomhlaba futhi babambe amakhosi omhlaba ngokuvuma nangokunqoba, futhi sebephantse bacindezela umhlaba wonke ezindlini zazo eziningi nasezindlini zazo umhlaba uboshiwe futhi unikezelwe ezandleni zikadrako ukuze wahlulelwe futhi ubhujiswe osekucishe kwafika ngokugcwele. Nokho isicabucabu siyakhala kakhulu ngenkathi sidla isisulu saso ukuze sihlale sibonakala siyisisulu esihluphekayo esishushiswayo, kanti eqinisweni yisicabucabu esishushisayo nesihlukumeza u-Adamu.

Ngakho-ke, lalelani izwi lami bantu bakithi, nani nonke enifunda la mazwi nizihlukanise nesicabucabu nezindlu zaso, ngoba noma ngubani ophenduka ekubhujisweni kwakhe abize igama leNkosi, uJesu waseNazaretha onguYeshuwa HaMashiach onguMesiya weqiniso wakwa-Israyeli; impela, impela lowo muntu uyosindiswa.

Impela, impela, ngiyanikhuluma nonke ngamunye ukuba niyilahle ngokuphelele futhi niyenqabe iTalmud yabo ehlambalazayo eyincwadi yobubi obukhulu, yongcola, nenkohliso; incwadi ebhaliwe ngu-Esawu futhi yamukelwa yiSinagoge LikaSathane, okuyisinagoge okuhlala kulo ububi nongcola kuphela. Ngoba isicabucabu siyiSinagoge LikaSathane, esizalwa yisizwe sika-Ashkenazi kaJafete abangenangxenye nankatho efeni likaSheme, futhi ikakhulukazi elelikaJakobe olungileyo engiyosindisa kuye insali enamandla.

Ngalokho, phumani phakathi kwabo nahlukane nabo, niphendukele kuYeshuwa HaMashiach ukuze nifunde ngami futhi nifunde ukuphila izindlela zokulunga zikaYahweh engiyizindlela zakhe nengiyiZwi lakhe elenziwe inyama, ngoba uMose uphila ngokugcwele kuYeshuwa HaMashiach naseTestamenteni lami Elisha kukhona zonke izithembiso nezivumelwano zasendulo, izithembiso eziyogcwaliseka ngokuphelele nangesikhathi esifanele ekubuseni kwami kweminyaka eyinkulungwane noMbuso wami engiyowumisa ngokulunga emhlabeni.

NginguYeshuwa HaMashiach onguMesiya weqiniso wakwa-Israyeli, futhi impela ngiyiNkosi yenu, uMphathi, uMpristi Ophakeme, noMesiya wenu ingunaphakade futhi uMbuso wami nokubusa kwami akusoze kwaba nasiphelo.

Ngingulowo ozayo ukuzoxwayisa abantu bakithi ngale nkohliso enkulu nokubhujiswa osekugubungele umhlaba ngendlu yaso ngoba izicabucabu sezeluke izindlu zazo emhlabeni wonke manje.

NginguKristu Jesu, futhi ngithumele la Mazwi ngesithunywa sami nenceku yami futhi ngesithunywa sami nenceku yami la mazwi athunyelwe.

NginguJesu uKristu, onguYeshuwa HaMashiach futhi owembule Amazwi Evangeli Laphakade kanye noYahweh uqobo lwakhe ezikhathini zokwambula manje. Zona izikhathi zokwambula ngaphambi kokuba ukuhlupheka kukaJakobe kuqale ngokugcwele ukuze uJakobe asindiswe.

Ngakho-ke, qondani ubucayi bamaZwi ami futhi ningabi ngabangakholwayo kodwa kholwani kuphela ubufakazi bami futhi nizwe Amazwi ami nisuke ekungakholweni nasebukhungetheleni obunenkani ngoba manje yizikhathi zokubuyela kuYahweh ngendlela kaYeshuwa HaMashiach nasezikhathini zokuhlupheka lezo zikhathi sezifikile.

Futhi bonke abeza kuMesiya wabo Ophakade bangamukele mna neTestamente lami Elisha bayozuza insindiso ngokwezigaba zabo. Labo ababiza igama leNkosi uJesu Kristu ngenhlonipho yokufela ukholo bayovuswa ekuvukeni kwabalungileyo futhi isisindo somvuzo wabo waphakade siyodlula kakhulu izinkinga zabo nobunzima babo engiyobaqinisa ukuba bakunqobe njengoba noYahweh angiqinisa ukuba nginqobe.

Nalabo abavela kuJakobe engiyobafihla ehlane ezikhathini zokuhlupheka okukhulu kunakho konke; emva kwalokho bayothathwa bephila bayiswe eMbusweni wami weminyaka eyinkulungwane ukuze bathele izithelo bande, futhi siyophila izindlela zikaYahweh ngoba umhlaba uyobuswa ngokulunga, futhi akukho nto embi eyongena kuwo.

Futhi yimina, uYeshuwa HaMashiach, omemezela ukugcwala kwezikhathi ukuze umhlaba uzale labo abalele kuKristu futhi ukhulule labo abaphilayo nabasalayo ekubonakaleni kwami nabatholwa bethembekile kuKristu, ngoba impela, impela izikhathi sezingamanje. Selah!
