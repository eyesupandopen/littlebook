Meyi 03, 2024

Ngininikile Ithemba Elikhulu!

Wozani kimi nizwe amazwi ami, Makoti wami, ngoba ngilapha ukuzothoba imiphefumulo yenu, ngiduduze izinhliziyo zenu, futhi nginenze niphelele kimi, uKristu Jesu. Ningadangali ngamazwi ami okulaya, ngoba engimthandayo ngiyamkhuza, ukuze ubizo lwenu nokukhethwa kwenu kuqiniseke kimi, uMyeni, uKristu Jesu. Bantwana bami, bekani izindlebe zenu ezwini lami nizwe amazwi ami, ngoba anginibizelanga ekwahlulelweni nasekulahlweni, kodwa nginimemezele nilungile kimi, iNkosi yenu uJesu Kristu. Anizwanga yini lokho uPawulu akutshela abaseKorinte encwadini yakhe yokuqala? Manje kumi ukukholwa, ithemba nothando, yebo, lezi zintathu; kodwa okukhulu kunakho konke uthando. Ngakho-ke uthando lukhulu kunalezi ezintathu, ngoba uthando lukaYahweh alunakunqotshwa. Uthando lukaYahweh luyisibopho esihlanganisayo. Luyisikhuthazo esikhulu, futhi ukuphana njengoba nani naphana kungukulingisa uNkulunkulu!

Baningi benu abalahlekelwe ithemba, ngisho nethemba lenu kimi, uJesu Kristu, ngoba ngininikile ithemba elikhulu, bantwana bami. Qondani ukuthi isela linganidida linidukise, kodwa uNkulunkulu akasiye umbhali wokudideka, kodwa owokuthula njengakuwo onke amabandla abangcwele. USathane uzamile ukufiphaza izimiso zami engazinikeza esambulweni sami seMfihlakalo Enkulu kaNkulunkulu kuMphostoli uPawulu eminyakeni eminingi yeJubili eyedlule. Bantwana bami, ithemba lenu yiwona mqondo onigcina nikhuthazekile ukuze nibekezelele izilingo eziningi nezinhlupheko eninazo kulokhu kuphila. Ngoba ngaphandle kwethemba lenu, benizoba njengoba uPawulu asho kwabaseKorinte: Uma sinethemba kuKristu kulokhu kuphila kuphela, singabantu abakhohlakele kunabo bonke. Kodwa manje uKristu uvusiwe kwabafileyo, waba yizithelo zokuqala zalabo abaleleyo. Bantwana bami, ngininike ithemba elikhulu ngethemba lenu, ngoba ngithe: nina eniphilayo nenisasele niyothathwa emafwini, njengoba abafileyo kuKristu beyovuka kuqala, futhi nonke niyoguqulwa, ngoba niyofana nami ingunaphakade futhi uMakoti wami uyoba nami kuze kube phakade.

Ngoba liyini ithemba lenu kuKristu, bantwana bami? Akusona yini isithembiso sokuhlengwa okuphelele nokubuyiselwa, lapho niyobona khona izulu nobukhosi nenkazimulo kaYahweh ngamehlo enu? Ithemba lenu akusilo yini ikusasa lapho niyongikhonza khona embusweni wami wokulunga, ngoba njengoba benithembekile ezintweni ezimbalwa, ngizonenza nibe ngababusi phezu kwezinto eziningi. Ithemba lenu akusona yini isithembiso sokuphila okuphakade lapho niyodla khona esihlahleni sokuphila nihambe ezitaladini zaseJerusalema Elisha, lapho ukukhanya kukaYahweh nokukhanya kukaYeshua HaMashiach kuyokhanya phakade ngokukhazimula okukhulu? Ithemba lenu akusiyona yini inkazimulo yemiqhele eminingi nemivuzo ngokuthembeka kwenu nothando lwenu kimi, uKristu Jesu? Ithemba lenu akusilo yini ithemba lempilo ephelele, kungabikho ukugula noma isifo? Ithemba lenu akusilo yini ukuthi ngiyobeka izitha zenu ngaphansi kwezinyawo zenu ngize ngenze nesinagoge likaSathane likhothame ezinyaweni zenu? (Ngoba bayokwazi ukuthi nginithandile!) Ithemba lenu akusilo yini elenjabulo yaphakade phambi kukaYahweh uqobo? Ngoba uyokwenza indawo yakhe yokuhlala phakathi kwabantu, futhi ngeke kusaba nombuso ohlukene, kodwa uMbuso kaNkulunkulu kuphela, ngoba akekho omkhulu kunoYahweh; cha, akekho ongafaniswa nobukhazikhazi bukaNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade, uBaba wethu!

Jabulani ngekusasa lenu, bantwana bami, ngoba ngininikile ikusasa nenjongo engcwele kimi, iNkosi yenu ekhanyayo! Ithemba lenu akulona yini ithemba elikhulu lapho nisindiswa olakeni kimi? Ngoba elenu yilona ithemba elikhulu kunawo onke, ithemba okukhulunywa ngalo yisithunywa sami esikhethiwe uMphostoli uPawulu, isikhonzi seMfihlakalo Enkulu kaNkulunkulu? Ngoba ngambulela uPawulu lokho akubhalela abaseKolose: Yebo imfihlakalo ebifihliwe ezizukulwaneni nasezizukulwaneni, kodwa manje isibonakalisiwe kubangcwele bakhe, UNkulunkulu afuna ukubazisa kubo ukuthi iyini ingcebo yenkazimulo yale mfihlakalo phakathi kwabezizwe, okunguKristu phakathi kwenu, ithemba lenkazimulo. Elenu liyithemba elikhazimulayo elifanele inkosi, ngoba ngizonenza nibe amakhosi nabapristi phambi kukaNkulunkulu wethu; ngoba ngingumpristi omkhulu kuze kube phakade ngokohlelo lukaMelkisedeki, futhi ngeke kube khona omunye.

Bantwana bami abancane, ningawesabi amanga esitha, ngoba besinganiqeda amandla ngobuze ukube besingakwazi; kodwa sihlangabezene nomlingani waso kimi futhi ngisinqobile ngokuphelele, futhi ngiyeza ukuzobhubhisa umbuso waso, ngoba ngathunyelwa ukuchitha imisebenzi kaDeveli, futhi ngakho-ke nginithumile! Inyoka yasendulo nombuso wayo wonke abanathemba. Bayazi ukuthi bagwetshwele ukubhujiswa kwaphakade nesijeziso, lapho beyosha emalangabini ashisayo kakhulu aluhlaza njengoba bebhukuda esibabule esivuthayo kuze kube phakade. Banomona ngani futhi bangenza noma yini ukuze babe yinina, ngakho ningawalaleli amazwi abo. Ngizwe, Makoti wami; uSathane uyokwenza noma yini ukuze anidukise emvuzweni wenu okimi, uKristu Jesu. Uyokwenza noma yini ukufihla ithemba lenu engininike lona, ngakho-ke izindlela zakhe kulesi sigaba kaningi zingukwehlisa isithunzi somqondo ngokulingisa iqiniso nangolimi lwakhe lwamanga.

Ningamesabi, kodwa sukani emangeni akhe nigcine ithemba lenu leqiniso engqondweni, ngoba anifundanga yini? Umoya uqobo lwawo uyafakaza, nomoya wethu, ukuthi singabantwana bakaNkulunkulu. Futhi uma singabantwana, siyizindlalifa, izindlalifa zikaNkulunkulu, nezindlalifa kanye noKristu: uma kunjalo ukuthi sihlupheka naye, ukuze sikhazinyuliswe kanye naye. Ngoba ngiyacabanga ukuthi, izinhlupheko zalesi sikhathi samanje, azifanele ukuqhathaniswa nenkazimulo eyokwambulwa kithi. Bantwana bami, ukube beniqonda ubukhulu bokuthi kusho ukuthini ukuba yindlalifa kanye noKristu, kodwa nizokwazi maduzane njengoba nani naziwa. Aniqondi yini ukuthi uNkulunkulu unginikile zonke izinto ezulwini nasemhlabeni, futhi ifa lami liyifa elikhulu kunawo onke? Bantwana bami, anizazi nina uqobo lwenu lwangempela, ngoba ubuwena benu bangempela bufihliwe kimi, futhi lapho ngizovela, nani niyovela nami enkazimulweni!

Ningesabi, futhi niqine othandweni nasekukholweni kwenu, ngoba ithemba lenu lizonenza nihambe ngokukhuthazela okukhulu; ngoba ithemba lami nenjabulo uBaba ayibeka phambi kwami kwangenza ngafezani? Ngoba amaHebheru athi: Sibheke kuJesu uMqalisi noMphelelisi wokukholwa kwethu, owathi ngenxa yenjabulo eyabekwa phambi kwakhe, wabekezelela isiphambano, edelela ihlazo, futhi uhlezi ngakwesokunene sesihlalo sobukhosi sikaNkulunkulu. Anginishiyelanga yini isibonelo, futhi angibanga yini uhlobo okufanele nilulandele? Aniqondi yini? Ukuthi okholwa kimi ngeke enze kuphela imisebenzi engayifeza ngesikhathi sokuza kwami kokuqala, kodwa imisebenzi emikhulu kunaleyo engayenza? Ngoba ngiye kuBaba futhi ngininikile amandla futhi nginimela ngawo onke amandla ami, ngoba nginamandla, ngoba uNkulunkulu ungizuzise kanjalo ekulaleleni kwami.

Bantu bami, lawa mazwi kufanele anikhuthaze futhi aninike amandla okuma, ngoba ngibeke injabulo enkulu phambi kwenu! Ningawakholwa amanga esitha mayelana nethemba lenu, ngoba lingazama ukunishiya ningenathemba nigxotshwe ngaphansi kwezinyawo, futhi ngisho nasebuthakathakeni benu niyenziwa nibe namandla kimi, iNkosi yenu uJesu Kristu! Manje ngizofingqa ikusasa lenu, Makoti wami, ngakho bekani izindlebe zenu emazwini omlomo wami, ngoba angamazwi okukholwa, angamazwi othando, futhi amazwi ami azovuselela ithemba lenu eqinisweni, ngoba ithemba elikhulu liseqinisweni engininike lona!

Okokuqala, qondani ukuthi nginibizile; nginisindisile emoyeni wami wamandla nobuntwana; nginenze isidalwa esisha kimi, uKristu Jesu! Ngenxa yomhlatshelo wami njengePhasika eliphelele, bonke abakholwa kimi ngokukholwa okusindisayo okuvela ezinhliziyweni zabo nabo bayosindiswa olakeni kimi. Bantwana bami, qondani ukuthi lawa mazwi amangalisa kakhulu kubantu abaningi, ngoba izono zabo zibashaya zibafake ecaleni, ekulahlweni, nasehlazweni, futhi lokhu kwenzeka ngoba kaningi benqaba ukuzihlanza egazini lami elihlawulayo, futhi bayakushaya indiva ukuvuma izono zabo, futhi bayakushaya indiva ubudlelwano babo nami, isikhulu sokuthula, ngoba insindiso ivela ekukholweni kobudlelwano kimi, iNkosi enkulu!

Baningi benu abangaqondi ukuthi nginilungisisile, futhi ngenxa yalokho kulungisiswa okukimi, nisindisiwe olakeni, ngoba ngatshela abaseRoma: Kakhulu-ke, njengoba manje sesilungisisiwe ngegazi lakhe, siyosindiswa olakeni ngaye. Ngiphenduleni lokhu, Makoti wami engizomlanda, kungani nilufisa usuku lweNkosi? Kungani nilalele izikhonzi zikaSathane ezizifihla njengezikhonzi zami lapho zithi kufanele nibekezelele usizi lukaJakobe ngisho nosizi olukhulu olwenzeka engxenyeni yokugcina yesonto lamashumi ayisikhombisa likaDaniyeli? Ziniqambela amanga futhi niyazikholwa. Zikhuluma nemvelo yenu yesono, zikutshela ukuthi anilusizo lwalutho kanti niyiparele lenani elikhulu; zikutshela ukuthi nifanele ulaka olukhulu kimi, futhi ziyaqamba amanga! Zikutshela ukuthi akufanele nilindele ukukhululwa osizini, futhi zicaphuna kabi iZwi lami elingcwele. Ngoba ngeqiniso ngatshela uPawulu lokhu okulandelayo ukuba afundise uThimothewu: Ukushushiswa, izinhlupheko ezafika kimi e-Antiyokiya, e-Ikoniyu, eListra, yeka izinhlupheko engazibekezelela: kodwa kuzo zonke iNkosi yangikhulula. Yebo, futhi bonke abazophila ngokuhlonipha uNkulunkulu kuKristu Jesu, bayohlushwa. Futhi ngeqiniso, uSathane uzoshushisa bonke abaphila izimpilo zokuhlonipha uNkulunkulu, futhi kuzo zonke izinhlupheko zakhe ngizonikhulula lapho niphendukela kimi ezinkingeni zenu nihlale nithembekile ethembeni lenu. Qondani umehluko, ngoba uSathane udide indaba: uSathane uzoshushisa bonke abaphila ngokuhlonipha uNkulunkulu, futhi lokho kuwukushushiswa; futhi kaningi kufanele nibekezelele ulaka lwakhe, ngoba ekunqotshweni kwakhe unentukuthelo enkulu. Kodwa ningakwesabi ukubhonga kwakhe, ngoba ngayala uPetru, ngathi: Qondani, nilinde: ngoba isitha senu udeveli njengengonyama ebhongayo uyahambahamba, efuna angamgwinya: Melanani naye niqine ekukholweni, nazi ukuthi izinhlupheko ezifanayo zenzeka kubazalwane benu abasemhlabeni.

Qondani, bantwana bami, ukuthi ulaka oluzayo akulona ulaka lukaSathane kodwa ulaka lukaNkulunkulu nolaka lweWundlu oluhloselwe uSathane. Ngoba uSathane uyigwala elazishushumbisa kuyo yonke indalo ukuze lizame ukuzifihla olakeni aluqongelele, licabanga ukuthi lingamnqoba uYahweh ezindabeni ezinjalo; kodwa izinhloso zikaBaba zishiwo kanje: Mnumzane, awuhlwanyelanga yini imbewu enhle ensimini yakho? Luvelaphi-ke ukhula? Wathi kubo, Isitha senze lokhu. Izinceku zathi kuye, Uyafuna yini ukuthi siye siyoluhlakula? Kodwa wathi, Cha: hlezi nithi nisahlakula ukhula, nisiphule kanye nalo nokolweni. Makukhule kokubili kuze kube isikhathi sokuvuna: futhi ngesikhathi sokuvuna, ngiyothi kubavuni, Buthani kuqala ukhula, nilubophe ngezinyanda ukuba lushiswe: kodwa buthani ukolweni enqolobaneni yami. Kanjalo ukolweni uyobuthelwa enqolobaneni, kodwa ukhula nabo bonke abalutshalayo nabaluniselayo bayophonswa bephila kulelo chibi elikhulu lomlilo nesibabule uBaba wami alilungiselele uDeveli nezingelosi zakhe, kodwa ayikho ingxenye yolaka lukaNkulunkulu engokwenu, nakuba uSathane ezama ukuzivikela ngani futhi ezofuna ukunithumba njengempango. Ningesabi, ngoba ulaka akulona olwenu. Ngiyaqonda ukuthi imiphefumulo eminingi engazinzile iyawuhlanekezela lo mfanekiso, ikutshela ukuthi niyobekezelela ulaka nabo, kodwa bakwenza lokho ngokunganaki ukuhleleka okucacile kombhalo ngale ndaba, futhi bazama ukuniphuphuthekisa ngokucaphuna kabi umfanekiso wami.

Qondani ukuthi ngizonithatha ngokushesha nginiyise emazulwini, ngoba ngilungiselele indawo yokuphepha nomvuzo kuMakoti wami; ngoba endlini kaBaba ngizonifihla futhi kukhona izindlu eziningi. Ngakho yebo, ngizonibuthela kimi ekubonakaleni kwami, osekuzokwenzeka. Ngoba ngasho kwabaseThesalonika: Ngoba iNkosi uqobo iyakwehla ezulwini ngokumemeza, ngezwi leNgelosi eyinhloko, nangecilongo likaNkulunkulu: futhi abafileyo kuKristu bayovuka kuqala. Khona-ke thina esiphilayo, nesisasele, siyothathwa kanye nabo emafwini, ukuhlangabeza iNkosi emoyeni: futhi kanjalo siyohlala neNkosi kuze kube phakade. Ngakho-ke, duduzanani ngalawa mazwi. Ngiyanibuza, Makoti wami oqanjelwe amanga, niyowenqaba manje amanga kaSathane nibambelele emazwini ami nithole induduzo kuwo niduduze abafowenu? Ngoba sekuzoba njalo maduze, futhi ngilangazelela ubukhona benu, ngoba nginithanda kakhulu, ngoba nginguMyeni wenu.

Uma senithathiwe emhlabeni, usizi lukaJakobe luzogqashuka emhlabeni wonke futhi ukubhujiswa okukhulu kuzofika ngokuzumayo, kodwa akusona esenu, njengoba niyobe nithathwe niyiswe emazulwini. Bese niyokuma phambi kwesihlalo sami sokwahlulela ukuze nginivuze ngokwemisebenzi yenu. Futhi kwabathembekile kunomvuzo omkhulu nemiqhele eminingi, futhi niyogqokiswa indlu enginenzele yona, yebo, indlu eyokwakhelwa nina ngokukhethekile futhi izoqukatha izimpumelelo zenu eziningi, engizoyinika amakhono amaningi, ngoba unjalo umzimba omusha enginilindele wona. Akukho ukugula, akukho buhlungu, akukho lusizi, futhi akusekho siqalekiso, bantwana bami, ngoba isiqalekiso siyophendukela emakhanda abaqalekisiweyo. Kanjalo niyobe nise mazulwini kuleso sikhathi seminyaka eyisikhombisa, yebo, isonto lamashumi ayisikhombisa likaDaniyeli. Bese, niyogibela nami kanye nayo yonke impi yasezulwini, ngoba kuprofethiwe futhi kuyoba njalo: Ngabona izulu livuliwe, bheka, ihhashi elimhlophe, futhi ohlezi phezu kwalo wayebizwa ngokuthi othembekileyo noqinisileyo, futhi ngokulunga uyahlulela futhi uyalwa. Amehlo akhe ayenjengelangabi lomlilo, futhi ekhanda lakhe kwakukhona imiqhele eminingi, futhi wayenegama elibhalwe, elingaziwa muntu ngaphandle kwakhe. Wayegqoke ingubo ecwiliswe egazini, futhi igama lakhe libizwa ngokuthi, Izwi likaNkulunkulu. Futhi amabutho ayesemazulwini amlandela phezu kwamahhashi amhlophe, egqoke ilineni elicolekileyo, elimhlophe nelihlanzekileyo.

Bantwana bami, elenu liyikusasa lenkazimulo engenakulinganiswa, futhi ngeke niswele lutho, ngoba nizibonakalisile kimi, futhi anidingi ukuhlolwa okuqhubekayo; ngakho kungani kungadingeka ukuthi nibekezelele usizi lukaJakobe? Ngoba inhloso yalo wukusindisa uJakobe, ngoba insali iyosindiswa. Kodwa qinisekani, labo abaphika ukubonakala kwami emizamweni yabo yobuzenzisi yokuchitha ithemba lenu langempela, yebo, bona kanye labo belusi abaphambukayo, bayobekezelela usizi lukaJakobe, futhi bayosindiswa uma bebiza igama lami futhi bethembekile kuze kube sekupheleni. Kodwa ngeke babe nandawo etafuleni lami, ngoba ukuphika ukubonakala kwami kuwukuphika mina, uMyeni, lapho ngiza ukulanda uMakoti wami, futhi sengizokwenza lokho kanye.

Bhekani ukukhanya kokubonakala kwami! Lalelani, ngoba nizozwa izwi lami kanye nokukhala okukhulu kwecilongo lokugcina; ngoba nakuba ngiyiWundlu likaNkulunkulu, ngiza njengeNgonyama yakwaJuda, futhi wonke umbuso kaSathane ubambeke ngokwesaba mina, uKristu Jesu, ngoba izikhathi zawo sezifikile, futhi amalangabi abaselwe ashisa kakhulu. Kodwa ngeke kusondele kini, ngoba nginilungiselele umvuzo omkhulu nefa elikhulu, Makoti wami. Fundani amazwi ami encwadini enkulu yesAmbulo, ngoba ngizonikhombisa umbono wekusasa lenu: Futhi nxazonke zeSihlalo sobukhosi kwakukhona izihlalo ezingamashumi amabili nane, naphezu kwezihlalo ngabona amadoda amadala angamashumi amabili nane ehlezi, egqoke izingubo ezimhlophe, futhi emakhanda awo babenemiqhele yegolide. (futhi futhi) Futhi lapho lezo zilo zinika inkazimulo, nodumo, nokubonga kuye ohlezi esihlalweni sobukhosi, okhona kuze kube phakade, Lawo madoda amadala angamashumi amabili nane awa phansi phambi kwalowo ohlezi esihlalweni sobukhosi, akhuleka kuye okhona kuze kube phakade, futhi aphonsa imiqhele yawo phambi kwesihlalo sobukhosi, ethi, Ufanelwe, Nkosi, ukwamukela inkazimulo, nodumo, namandla: ngoba wena wadala zonke izinto, futhi ngenxa yentokozo yakho zikhona, futhi zadalwa.

Bantwana bami, ningalesabi ikusasa lenu, kodwa bhekani kimi ngokulangazela okukhulu, ngoba maduzane nje niyobe nise mazulwini niphonsa imiqhele yenu phambi kwesihlalo sobukhosi sikaNkulunkulu ophakeme kakhulu!

Ngingumahluleli olungileyo futhi maduze nizokwamukela umvuzo omkhulu eBema lami.

NginguMyeni kumakoti wami.

Ngiza ukuzohlwitha uMakoti wami!

NginguNgonyama Yesizwe SakwaJuda futhi umhlaba usuzoqonda.

Ngingulowo onikwe ubukhulu ezulwini nasemhlabeni, ngoba igama lami likhulu kunawo onke amagama ngaphandle kukaYahweh.

NginguNdodana kaNkulunkulu owafika enyameni ngenxa yezono zesintu, futhi ngavuswa ngosuku lwesithathu ngamukelwa emazulwini.

Ngingulowo oyobusa ngokulunga emhlabeni, noMakoti wami uyobusa nami.

Ngingulowo onibizele enkazimulweni nasebuhleni, ngakho-ke ningadangali!

Nginguye.
