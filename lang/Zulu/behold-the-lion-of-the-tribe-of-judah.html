Bhekani! Ibhubesi Lesizwe SakwaJuda!

Vimbululani izindlebe zenu, ningivumele ngivule amehlo enu, O Israyeli, niphaphame ebumpumputheni benu obuyingxenye nizwe izwi likaMesiya wenu weqiniso, lona iBhubesi Lesizwe SakwaJuda, ngokuba nginguye. Ngakho-ke qondisisani amazwi kaYeshua HaMashiach, yena eninokwenza naye, O Israyeli, ngokuba nginguye.

Ngiqinisile, ngiqinisile, zwanini amazwi ami bazalwane bami ukuze niqonde izikhathi eniphila kuzo, nokuba niqonde ithemba lokubizwa kwenu elibuyela ezweni lesithembiso, ngokuba ngiyithemba lokubizwa kwenu, futhi ngiyanibiza, O Israyeli, ukuze niqonde lokho okuzayo kanye nolaka oluzokhululwa kulezi zikhathi eniphila kuzo. Futhi ngiyiBhubesi Lesizwe SakwaJuda, futhi ngeza ngokulunga ukuze ngahlulele futhi ngilwe, ngakho-ke qondisisani ubukhulu Bamazwi ami, wona Amazwi alowo eninokwenza naye ngokuba uYahweh ungenzile ukuba ngibe njalo.

Ngokuba ngiyilolo dwala lokukhubekisa, lona lelo litshe elikhulu lokukhubeka lapho bonke abangakholwayo bayokhubeleka khona, ngokuba abalungileyo abasaphili ngoMose kodwa abalungileyo bayophila ngokukholwa! Ngokuba uMose uphila kuMesiya wenu weqiniso, yena owagcina ngokupheleleyo wona lowo mthetho okungenakwenzeka ukuba u-Israyeli awugcine, ngisho nabo bonke abakwa-Adamu ukuba bawugcine ngenxa yesono enyameni yenu eyonakele. Futhi nginguye owakhokha isijeziso sikaMose lapho ngazinikela njengewundlu likaNkulunkulu kuYahweh njengomhlatshelo wokubuyisana waphakade, ngokuba isijeziso sezoni kwakungesona esami ukusikhokha, kodwa othandweni lwami ngoYahweh, nasothandweni lwami ngesintu ngazikhokha izijeziso zenu ngokugcwele. Ngisho nezijeziso zokuhlushwa okukhulu, nobuhlungu, nokufa ngazikhokha ngokugcwele, futhi uYahweh wamukele umhlatshelo wami kuze kube phakade.

Ngakho-ke qondani, ulaka LweWundlu seluzovulwa futhi amacilongo sekuzoshaywa, futhi bhekani, izitsha zentukuthelo yolaka lukaNkulunkulu ziyasondela ngokuqinisekile futhi zisemnyango wenu, ngakho-ke, yizwani izwi lami ukuze nina nabantwana benu niphile futhi nibaleke ugibe uBaraba asebenzela ukulubekela abantu bami, lona iwundlu likadrako elikhethwe u-Esawu njengoMoshiach wabo, ongumakhi wethempeli oyisilo. Futhi uyovuka njengewundlu elinezimpondo ezimbili, futhi uza ukuzohlukanisa amazwe kakhulu kubanikazi abafanele abasuswe ngokubulawa kwabo kohlanga, ukwabela izihlali ezingemthetho ezivela ku-Esawu ezenze isimangalo samanga, kanye nabahlaseli abathunyelwe udrako abayinzalo ka-Ashkenazi hhayi ekaJakobe kodwa abazihlanganise no-Esawu ngezindlela zabo zeTalmud.

Futhi nginguye uYahweh amthuma njengewundlu likaNkulunkulu, njengesivumelwano sokuthula esiphelele, njengePhasika lenu eliphelele ngisho nomhlatshelo wokugcina wezono zesintu kanye, konke, naphakade futhi akusekho mhlatshelo wezono. Futhi ngeza kwabami ngothando nokuthula, labo abenqaba umhlatshelo wami, kodwa ngithunyiwe njengePhasika eliphelele ngokulalela intando kaYahweh, futhi ngiyiwundlu likaNkulunkulu, uYahweh alinikeza ngokuphelele ngenxa yezono zenu, ngakho-ke yizwani izwi lami O Israyeli niqonde ukuthi ngeke ngisabuya emhlabeni njengewundlu likaNkulunkulu, nokuthula kuzosuswa emhlabeni ekwahluleleni okufikile. Ngisho nokwahlulela okuzokhululwa ngokuzuma kuqala endlini kaNkulunkulu ngoba ukwahlulela kuqala endlini kaNkulunkulu.

Ngokuba abakholwayo kuKristu bayothathwa bayiswe emazulwini, futhi abasivivi bayoshiyelwa emlilweni wocwengayo ukuze kubhujiswe inyama, ukuze umoya usindiswe kodwa kungathi ngomlilo. Futhi izixuku eziningi, eziningi ziyokuza kuJesu waseNazaretha zivela kuzo zonke izizwe nezinhlanga nezilimi futhi ngiyozihlambulula ngokulunga futhi ngiyozamukela kimi futhi ngiyozidala kabusha ngokulunga nobungcwele beqiniso futhi umvuzo wazo awunakufaniswa nokuhlupheka eziyokukhuthazelela, ngoba isisindo somvuzo wazo siyodlula kakhulu okulindelwe abantu futhi phakade ziyohlala ngokuthula okuphelele nobungcwele nokulunga kweqiniso.

Ngokuba kulotshiwe ngabo futhi kuyoba njalo: Laba yibo abaphuma osizini olukhulu, futhi bahlanza izingubo zabo ezinde, futhi bazenza zaba mhlophe izingubo zabo ezinde egazini leWundlu. Ngakho-ke, baphambi kwesihlalo sobukhosi sikaNkulunkulu, futhi bamkhonza imini nobusuku ethempelini lakhe, nalowo ohlezi esihlalweni sobukhosi uyohlala phakathi kwabo. Abasayikulamba, abasayikoma, nelanga alisayikushaya phezu kwabo, nakho ukushisa. Ngokuba iWundlu, eliphakathi kwesihlalo sobukhosi, liyobabusa, futhi liyobaholela emithonjeni ephilayo yamanzi, noNkulunkulu uyokwesula zonke izinyembezi emehlweni abo.

Ngakho-ke qondani ukuthi nginguye oqedile imisebenzi yami phakathi kwabezizwe nezikhathi zami zomusa nesihe sezifike ekupheleni, nakuba umusa nesihe kuhlezi nami phakade, futhi uYahweh ngokwakhe umemezele ukuthi uyoba nomusa kulowo ayoba nomusa kuye, ngakho umusa nesihe kuhlala phakade ngempela. Ngiqinisile, ngiqinisile, ngaya kwabezizwe njengodingisiwe owenqatshwa ngabakithi abathathwe u-Esawu ekuthunjweni kwamasiko akhe ahlambalazayo afuna ukonakalisa ngawo Amazwi kaYahweh aphakade nangunaphakade, okuyinkohlakalo engenakwenzeka. Nokho baqamba izinkolelo zabo zesihogo ngezandla zababhali babo ababhala amasiko abantu ababi encwadini okuthiwa ingcwele, kodwa ingaba kanjani incwadi yenhlamba ibe ngcwele? Ngiqinisile, ngiqinisile, maye kulabo ababiza ubumnyama ngokuthi ukukhanya nokukhanya ngokuthi ubumnyama, ngokuba ukwahlulelwa kwabo akuphuzi!

Futhi izikhathi zami phakathi kwabezizwe sezifike esiphelothweni ngoba bawele ezonweni ezifanayo oYihlo abawela kuzo lapho benqaba iwundlu likaNkulunkulu uYahweh alithumela kubo bengakufanele njengesivumelwano sokuthula saphakade ezizweni, okwathi isixuku esikhulu safinyelela ensindisweni. Manje labo ababengaba abantwana besithembiso phakathi kuka-Israyeli kaNkulunkulu bazigqajisile ngamagatsha emvelo wona awomuthi womnqumo wemvelo abaxhunyelelwa kuwo lapho amagatsha emvelo enqunywa ekungakholweni okufanayo.

Nokho bathathwa emthini womnqumo wasendle, umuthi ongenathemba nasithembiso futhi kulabo abeza kimi bangenza iNkosi yabo yaphakade yokulunga ezikhathini zokuthula, umusa, nesihe manje bamukelwe kwa-Israyeli kaNkulunkulu futhi bahlanganyela ngokugcwele ezithembisweni ezenziwa koYise futhi abaningi balaba bayongikhonza phakade njengomzimba wami futhi ngiyobathatha kimi njengomakoti wami futhi ngiyobeka uphawu lwami kwabayi-144,000 abazihlanze egazini lami lokubuyisana njengabezintombi ezingangcoliswanga yisifebe esikhulu. Futhi ngensindiso yabezizwe izizwe zabusiswa futhi ngempela labo bezizwe ezilahlekile abalalela izwi lami bafinyelela ensindisweni eNkosini yabo futhi bayophila phakade phakathi kwezizwe kwa-Israyeli kaNkulunkulu! Ngakho qondani, ngiyosindisa abanye babo bonke abavela ku-Adamu nomaphi lapho azulazula khona emhlabeni ezinsukwini zakhe zokujeziswa.

Ngokuba uYahweh ungenzile ngaba umoya ophilayo, futhi angisaziwa ngokwenyama. Ngiqinisile, ngiqinisile, nginguye manje osenqume umuthi womnqumo wasendle ngenxa yokungakholwa kwawo nangenxa yokuthi baphule isivumelwano sami, sona iTestamente Lami Elisha. Futhi lokho kungenxa yokuthi kufanele babe neNkosi eyodwa, mina, uKristu Jesu iNkosi yamakhosi neNkosi yamakhosi futhi ebukhosini bami kukhona ithemba labo lodwa. Nokho bazithathele ithemba lamanga kuBaraba futhi ngokobuwula babeka amathemba abo enkosini yamanga eyahlasela Amasonto abo ngamathemba amanga okuchuma ngokwezinto ezibonakalayo, ngisho uMsindisi wamanga othembisa ukusindisa iBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo kodwa ngasese osebenzela ukubhujiswa kweBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo. Yena lowo mzenzisi oyisilo esivela emhlabeni esiyovuka njengoMoshiach wamanga ka-Esawu, iwundlu likadrako eliyovuka linezimpondo ezimbili. Ngalokho iSonto lami elingenelwe laseLawodikeya liphambukile futhi manje liyavumelana nokufa kwalo, ngokuba uBaraba uletha ukufa kuphela, futhi uyosibhubhisa ngokuphelele leso sifebe esikhulu, iBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo, ukwahlulelwa kwalo sekufikile ngenxa yobubi baso obukhulu nenkohlakalo esonakalise ngayo umhlaba ngobufebe baso.

Ngakho-ke ngokuvukela bazikhethele yena oletha ukubhujiswa kwabo njengomsindisi wabo wokuzenzisa, ngoba iSinagoge LikaSathane lingabazenzisi abazenzisayo bonke. Ngakho-ke fundani, O Israyeli, lokho uBaraba asezokufeza ngoba ubodayisele ezandleni zezitha zabo, futhi uyokwenza sengathi ulimele ekhanda bese ezifihla emhlabeni ezinhlekeleleni esezofika. Futhi uyovuka ngenkohliso enkulu njengoMoshiach wamanga kwa-Israyeli ngoba bayoba nempi yabo, impi yabo yesithathu enjalo, futhi iyoba yimbi kunazo zontathu. Kunjalo, ngokuba kulotshiwe kanjalo futhi kuyokwenziwa kanjalo: Ngabona isilo siphuma olwandle, sinekhanda eziyisikhombisa, nezimpondo eziyishumi, naphezu kwezimpondo zaso imiqhele eyishumi, naphezu kwekhanda laso igama lenhlamba. Isilo engasibona sasinjengengwe, nezinyawo zaso zinjengebhere, nomlomo waso unjengomlomo weBhubesi: udrako wasinika amandla aso nesihlalo saso sobukhosi, negunya elikhulu. Ngabona elinye lamakhanda aso sengathi lilimele laze lafa, kodwa inxeba laso elibulalayo laphola, futhi wonke umhlaba wamangala walandela isilo.

Kanjalo-ke amazwi emibhalweni ayaveza ubuqili bakhe, wona Amazwi engawanika uJohane uMambuli eminyakeni eminingi edlule. Futhi kuyobonakala sengathi uBaraba uzolimala ekhanda, kodwa uyoqamba amanga akhohlise umhlaba oyokholwa ngokugcwele izimpawu nezimangaliso zakhe zamanga ngokuba unikwe lokho ukuze adonsele kuye bonke abathokoza ngokungalungi ngenkohliso enamandla, ngoba ungumngcolisi nombhubhisi wabo. Futhi ezandleni zakhe uletha ukuluma kwenyoka, lona lowo mjovo wesilo, futhi bayohlanganisa insimbi nobumba nakuba bengayikunamathelana, nokho iwundlu likadrako liyovuka kanjalo. Futhi liyovuka phezu kohlelo lwesilo lokugcina lowo owileyo, oluyoba umbuso wabo wokugcina wokubusa umhlaba usezweni lesithembiso. Nokho ngeke livuke lodwa, kodwa liyovuka kanye nesilo esivela olwandle, futhi bayobusa umfelandawonye wabo wamakhosi anikele amandla awo aphelele negunya kudrako ngoba bonke bakhonza udrako futhi bazinikele ngokuphelele kuye ngoba unguYise, yena owatshala ukhula lwakhe olubi phakathi kukakolweni kaYahweh, futhi ngiyolusiphula ngokuphelele ngiluphonse emlilweni.

Kanjalo-ke iwundlu likadrako liyovuka ngenkohliso ephelele nezimpawu eziningi zamanga nezimangaliso liyozibonisa ukuze lifakazele iziprofetho zalo zamanga, futhi liyokwenza izimangalo eziningi zokuzigqaja nezenhlamba ngalo, lize lisho ukuthi lona ngokwalo linguNkulunkulu. Futhi isilo esisodwa siyokhomba kwesinye futhi bayoba nokubusa okuhlangene, ngisho nangokwezinhlelo zikadrako ayobusa ngazo umbuso wakhe omubi isikhashana. Futhi bayokwenza zonke izinto zobuKristu zibe ezingekho emthethweni, futhi bayovimbela imibhalo engcwele, futhi kunikelwe ezandleni zabo ukuba balwe nabangcwele babanqobe, futhi bayokwenza kanjalo. Nokho bayohlanganisa izinkolo zomhlaba ukuze bonke bakhonze amakhiyubhu abo amnyama ngoba ezinyaweni zikaSathane bahlangene ngokuvukela kokugcina futhi ngeke babaleke esandleni sami esinamandla, isho iMesiya lenu laphakade, uYeshua HaMashiach!

Nokho enzondweni yabo yazo zonke izinto eziphathelene noKristu Jesu bayobangela ukuba insindiso ifike esixukwini esikhulu abayobabulala njengabafelâ€™ ukholo, labo abayobiza egameni likaJesu waseNazaretha kuze kube umphefumulo wabo wokugcina futhi ngiyobaqinisa ngibanike ithemba elikhulu, futhi bayokuza kimi ngokugcwele, bonke labo abayizindlalifa zensindiso yaphakade, futhi akekho onamandla okubahlwithela ezandleni zami futhi bonke labo amagama abo alotshiwe encwadini yokuphila yewundlu bayosindiswa. Ngakho-ke ningakhathazeki ngabathandekayo benu ababonakala belahlekile, ngoba abaningi babo basezandleni zami ezinamandla futhi ngisazobaletha ensindisweni kuKristu Jesu futhi nganoma yiluphi ulimi ababiza ngalo igama lami elinamandla ngiyobezwa!

Nokho, kuyoba njalo ukuthi iwundlu lamanga elinezimpondo ezimbili liyovuka libuse umhlaba lisuka ezweni lesithembiso u-Esawu afuna ukulibiza njengelakhe, kodwa izimangalo zakhe zingamanga futhi ilungelo lobuzibulo likaJakobe liqinisekile kuze kube phakade, lona lelo lungelo lobuzibulo u-Esawu alithengisa ngesinkwa esincane. Ngokuba u-Esawu uzihlanganise nabawileyo futhi uzihlanganise no-Ashkenazi abazithathele amasiko nemikhuba yamaKhanani amabi nokho baqamba amanga bathi bangabakwa-Israyeli kanti abasibo. Ngokuba kwakunguKhanani owahawukela izindlela zabawileyo ngemva kukazamcolo omkhulu, ngaleyo ndlela eguqula inzalo yakhe ingene ekulahlweni kwaphakade ngoba bonke abaziguqula ngokofuzo emfanekisweni kaNkulunkulu abadalwa ngawo abanako ukuthethelelwa ngoba lezi yizindlela zikaSathane u-Esawu azibhala ingxenye yeTalmud yakhe ehlambalazayo lapho kuvela khona inkohliso yakhe.

Kanjalo u-Ashkenazi, ongokaJafete, uhlangene no-Esawu ezimangalweni zakhe zamanga zefa, lapho befuna ukweba ifa likaJakobe kubantwana besithembiso. Nokho, imizamo enjalo iyize futhi ngeke ime ngoba nginguye uYahweh anikele zonke izinto kuye ezandleni zami ezinamandla nekhonokhono. Ngakho-ke qondani ukuthi nginguye owazuza zonke izinto ezathenjiswa uJakobe futhi nginguMesiya wenu weqiniso, futhi ezandleni zami kukhona imivuzo yenu O Israyeli nasezandleni zikaBaraba kulindele ukubhujiswa kwenu kwaphakade, ngakho-ke ningamhlanganyeli!

Ngakho-ke qondani ukuthi imivuzo yesithembiso eyenziwa koYise basendulo ihlezi iqinile ezandleni zami futhi akekho onamandla okweba lokho okungokwami ngokufanelekile ngokuba lawo ngamandla engiwanikiwe ngu-MINA NGINGUYE omkhulu futhi ngiyonika leli fa kubantwana besithembiso, abalungileyo abaphila ngokukholwa eNdodaneni kaYahweh, eyabathanda yazinikela ngenxa yensindiso yaphakade yabantwana besithembiso, ngakho-ke O Israyeli ngamukeleni njengoMesiya wenu ukuze nina nabantwana benu niphile futhi nichume endleleni. Futhi ngiyonigcina nginilondoloze ukuze nifinyelele esabelweni senu esigcwele sefa embusweni wami weminyaka eyinkulungwane osemnyango wenu. Ngakho yizwani ubizo lwami, O Israyeli! Ngokuba ngiyanibiza ukuba nizinikele esandleni sami esinamandla ukuze nani nibe abantwana besithembiso ngoba uMose ugcinwe ngokugcwele ngokukholwa kimi, uYeshua HaMashiach, ngakho-ke, phumulani emisebenzini yami niyeke eyenu.

Kanjalo u-Ashkenazi uhlangene no-Esawu ezimangalweni zakhe zamanga, lapho befuna ukweba ifa likaJakobe kubantwana besithembiso. Ngakho-ke qondani ukuthi nginguye owazuza zonke izinto ezathenjiswa uJakobe, futhi nginguMesiya wenu weqiniso, futhi ezandleni zami kukhona umvuzo wenu O Israyeli nasezandleni zikaBaraba kulindele ukubhujiswa kwenu. Ngakho-ke, qondisisani isinqumo okufanele nisenze, nalowo eninokwenza naye, ngoba nginguye othunywe uYahweh ukuze nisindiswe, yena lowo uMose ayala ukuba nimlalele, O bazalwane bami abangalaleli.

Kanjalo njengoba kulotshiwe ngabo kuyogcwaliseka ngesandla sami esinamandla, ngoba uYahweh wembula iqiniso ngo-Esawu kubaprofethi bakhe ezinsukwini zokona kwabo okwakukuningi. Ngokuba kulotshiwe kusukela endulo: Ngenxa yonya lwakho kumfowenu uJakobe, ihlazo liyokusibekela, futhi uyonqunywa phakade. Lapho umi ngaphesheya, ngosuku abafokazi abathumba ngalo impahla yakhe, nabafokazi bangena emasangweni akhe, benza inkatho ngeJerusalema, nawe wawunjengomunye wabo.

Ngakho qondani O Israyeli ukuthi ukuhlukana kokugcina kukaJakobe no-Esawu sekufikile phezu kwenu, futhi ngeza ukuzohlanza izwe lesithembiso ezandleni zabawileyo futhi u-Esawu uyosishulwa ngokuphelele, futhi ngo-Esawu kulotshiwe: Indlu kaJakobe iyoba umlilo, nendlu kaJosefa ibe ilangabi, nendlu ka-Esawu ibe njengezinhlanga, bayobasa kuzo bazidle: futhi ngeke kube khona nsali yendlu ka-Esawu: ngokuba iNkosi ikhulumile. Ngakho-ke qondani nina baka-Esawu enifunda Amazwi ami, lokho uYahweh akumemezele akekho ongakuguqula futhi ukuphela kwenu kuyoba okokugcina futhi niyosishulwa ngokuphelele ningabe nisaba khona futhi.

Ngoba ngiyiNgonyama yesizwe sakwaJuda futhi ngokulunga ngiyeza ukuzokwahlulela nokulwa. Ngakho-ke, yizwani amazwi aMi, nina bakwa-Israyeli, futhi nizifihle osukwini olusezonifinyelela. Zifihleni kumuntu wesono oza endleleni yenu, ngoba uBharabha uyeza nokufa kwaphakade ezandleni zakhe, okunikelwa njengekhambi eliyisimangaliso elithembisa ngokungamanga ukunika ukuphila okuphakade. Kodwa ngeke babe nokuphila ekufeni kwesibili, kodwa kuphela ukubhujiswa okuqhubekayo emalangabini ashisayo kakhulu esijeziso, lapho impethu yabo ingafi.

Nginguye ozokwephula izimpawu zomqulu wami wefa, sona kanye isenzo somhlaba engiwuthengile ngegazi lami lokuhlawulela esandleni sikaYahweh uqobo. Futhi ngiyeza ukuzosiphula izimpande abahlala ngokungemthetho nabo bonke abathunyelwe ngabawileyo, kanye nabawileyo uqobo, nezikhathi zabo sezisondele ekupheleni. Ngakho-ke qondisisani okuzayo, Israyeli, ngoba bayomisa umbuso wabo futhi bayobonakala benqobile, futhi bayokhombisa izibonakaliso eziningi zamanga nezimangaliso ukuze bakhohlise bonke abathokoza ngokungalungi. Bayocabanga ukuthi sebemise ukuqala kwenkathi yabo yegolide abanxanela ukuyimisa, ngoba bangabakaYise udeveli, futhi udeveli ungudrako omkhulu, ngakho-ke, bayokwenza izinkanuko zikadrako.

Kodwa, ngeke babe nankathi yegolide, kodwa kuphela isahlulelo esinzima nesiqinile, isahlulelo esiyoqala ngokushesha njengoba abakholwayo bami kuKristu bebuthelwa kimi emafwini njengoba ngibonakala, okuyinto umhlaba oyoyibona. Kodwa, bayoqamba amanga batshele umhlaba ukuthi izitha ezingaziwa zintshontshe abaningi emhlabeni futhi ziyobuya, futhi umhlaba kumele ulungele ukulwa nalabo bahlaseli bokuzenzisa. Kodwa eqinisweni bazilungiselela impi yalolo suku olukhulu, usuku lokubuya kwami kwesibili, usuku lapho izinyoni zomkhathi ziyodla kakhulu inyama yamakhosi abo awileyo omhlaba, futhi kuyoba njalo.

Futhi njengoba izahlulelo zanda, njengoba izimpawu zephulwa (abagibeli bamahhashi bayogibela) namacilongo eyokhala, kuyokwenzeka njengoba kulotshiwe nguJohane uMambuli, futhi nangokuvumelana naMazwi eVangeli laphakade elambuliwe ngesikhathi salo ukuze u-Israyeli alungiselelwe usuku losizi lwakhe futhi alungiselelwe usuku losindiso lwakhe, olungaphuzi. Abaningi bayohlambalaza uNkulunkulu wezulu ngenxa yezahlulelo ezizobehlela, futhi abaningi ngeke baphenduke, futhi iningi liyothatha ujovo lwabo lwesilo futhi lizihlanganise nobuchwepheshe abangaqondi, ngoba ubuchwepheshe babo bungobokubhujiswa kuka-Adamu, ngakho-ke zifihleni, nina bakwa-Israyeli.

Futhi abaprofethi bami ababili, izinti zami ezimbili zezibani, bayovuka baprofethe izinto eziningi ezigcinwe ziyimfihlo ezinhliziyweni zabantu, futhi bayokwambula okuningi okwalahleka emibhalweni yasendulo, futhi iqiniso liyokwaziwa, nabawileyo bayodalulwa ngokugcwele, no-Edomi uyokwenziwa obala lapho u-Esawu avela khona, nabo bonke abazihlanganise naye emthethweni bayodalulwa, futhi iqiniso liyokwaziwa. Futhi bayomemezela iVangeli laphakade, nobufakazi babo buyonezelwa emibhalweni engcwele kuze kube phakade naphakade. Futhi noma ngezikhathi zesahlulelo umusa wami ubusa ngokukhulu, ngoba esikhundleni sokwahlulela okusheshayo okwakungagwinya izwe labaphilayo, umusa nendawo yokuphenduka kuyonikezwa ngaphambi kokuba isahlulelo sokugcina siwele. Ngakho-ke, abaningi bayokuza eNkosini yabo yaphakade Yokulunga, nami ngiyobamukela kimi futhi ngibagcine kuze kube phakade ngenjongo yaphakade kaYahweh abasindiselwe ukuyifeza.

Futhi lapho sebeqedile ubufakazi babo, isilo esiphuma kwalasha, siyolwa nabo, sibanqobe, sibabulale, futhi izidumbu zabo ziyolala emigwaqweni izinsuku ezintathu nengxenye. Ekupheleni kwaleso sikhathi bayovukela ekuphileni okusha njengoba bezwa lelo zwi elinamandla livela ezulwini eliyothi phambi kwabo bonke abakhona, "Khuphukelani lapha!" Futhi bayovuswa bathathwe bayiswe emazulwini bebonwa yibo bonke.

Futhi njengoba ithempeli liqedwa, iwundlu likadrako liyosondela ukunikezela ithempeli, kodwa liyobeka isinengiso sencithakalo endaweni engcwele futhi lenze umhlatshelo unqamuke maphakathi nesonto, lapho liyozimemezela linguNkulunkulu futhi liphendukele abantu. Futhi igazi liyogeleza kakhulu, nalabo abenqabile aMazwi ami futhi bazihlanganisa noBharabha, abaningi babo bayobulawa njengoba kulotshiwe: Futhi ingelosi yaphonsa isikela layo elibukhali emhlabeni, yanquma amavini esivini somhlaba, yawaphonsa kuleso sikhamo sewayini esikhulu sentukuthelo kaNkulunkulu. Futhi isikhamo sewayini sanyathelwa ngaphandle komuzi, futhi igazi laphuma esikhamweni sewayini, laze lafika ematomini amahhashi, ebangeni lamastadiya ayinkulungwane namakhulu ayisithupha.

Ngakho-ke, qondani ugibe enibekelwe lona, Israyeli, futhi nisuke ezindleleni zika-Esawu niye ezingalweni zikaMesiya wenu weqiniso, futhi ngiyosindisa insali ephila kimi. Futhi kuyokwenzeka ukuthi ngiyofika emhlabeni eNtabeni iSiyoni nabayizinkulungwane zami eziyi-144, futhi ngiyonihola nginiyise endaweni ehlane elungiswe nguYahweh, lapho niyonakekelwa futhi nigcinwe niphila entukuthelweni yalolo suku olukhulu. Ngoba kuyokwenzeka njengoba kulotshiwe: Abafileyo abafela eNkosini, babusisekile ngokugcwele. Yebo, usho kanje uMoya: ngoba bayaphumula emisebenzini yabo, nemisebenzi yabo iyabalandela.

Futhi njengoba umhlambi wami uthathwa uyiswa ensimini yawo yokuphepha, ukuthukuthela okugcwele kwentukuthelo kaNkulunkulu kuyothululwa ngokugcwele phezu kombuso wesilo naphezu kwabo bonke abahlala emhlabeni ngokungalungi, futhi kuyokwenzeka njengoba kulotshiwe; nobuhlungu obukhulu, nemiphumela emibi iyokwehlela labo abazihlele balwa neNgonyama yesizwe sakwaJuda, futhi bayobekezelela isijeziso esikhulu nokuhlushwa ngenxa yezitsha zentukuthelo ezizothululwa emhlabeni, futhi kuyoba njalo.

Ngakho-ke, qondisisani ukuthi ngingubani, nina bakwa-Israyeli, ngoba anisamazi uYahweh, futhi anilandeli izindlela zakhe, kodwa ngokungazi nikhonza onkulunkulu bamanga okhokho benu ababengabazi, ngezindlela zika-Esawu owazihlanganisa nabawileyo nomfelandawonye abazihlanganise nencwadi yabo yamanga ehlambalaza kakhulu lapho babhala khona amasiko abantu ngaphezu kweqiniso lamaZwi aphakade kaYahweh okulunga, ngalokho bekhonza futhi bakhonze indalo esikhundleni soMdali, obusisiwe kuze kube phakade. Yebo no-Ameni!

Nginguye oyochoboza ngokuphelele ikhanda likadrako, inyoka endala, uSathane uqobo, futhi ngeke alulame enxebeni lakhe, futhi umbuso wakhe ngeke uphinde uvuke ngoba akayona inkosi, futhi isijeziso sakhe sinzima kakhulu ngoba ngesandla sakhe indalo yonakaliswa ekuqaleni futhi abaningi bathathwe ngamaqhinga akhe.

NgiyiNkosi yamakhosi neNkosi yamakhosi, intukuthelo yami inkulu, nesahlulelo sami sesifikile, nezikhathi zeviki lamashumi ayisikhombisa likaDaniyeli sezizogqashuka emhlabeni ngokuzumayo, njengoba ngizumayo abakholwayo kuKristu kimi, ekuphepheni kwesihlalo sobukhosi sikaYahweh lapho beyokwazi njengoba baziwa, futhi umkhwenyana uyozithathela umakoti wakhe ozilungiselele umkhwenyana wakhe ngokuhlanzwa okukhulu nokuhlawulelwa ngegazi elihlawulela njalo lewundlu.

Ngakho-ke, qondisisani ukuthi nginguye oyolungisa amaphutha amaningi ngokwahlulela, futhi nginguye okulotshiwe ngaye ukuthi impindiselo ngeyami, isho iNkosi, futhi ngiyobuyisela. Ngakho ngiyosiphula u-Esawu ngokuphelele ezweni lesithembiso, futhi nginguye olondoloza ifa likaJakobe ezandleni zami ezinamandla engizibekele abantwana besithembiso, izithembiso abantwana benyama abayophikwa kuzo ngokugcwele.

Ngakho-ke, qondisisani amazwi aMi, nina bakwa-Israyeli, futhi niqonde lokho engiyokufeza, ngoba kulotshiwe kanje ngo-Esawu futhi kuyofezeka kanjalo: Kodwa wena (Esawu) kwakungafanele ubheke usuku lomfowenu, ngosuku enziwa ngalo umfokazi, futhi kwakungafanele uthokoze ngabantwana bakwaJuda, ngosuku lokubhujiswa kwabo: kwakungafanele ukhulume ngokuziqhenya ngosuku losizi lwabo. Kwakungafanele ungene esangweni labantu bami ngosuku lokubhujiswa kwabo, futhi kwakungafanele ubheke usizi lwabo ngosuku lokubhujiswa kwabo, noma ubeke izandla empahleni yabo ngosuku lokubhujiswa kwabo. Futhi kwakungafanele ume ezimpambanweni zomgwaqo ukuze ubanqume, labo ababalekayo, futhi kwakungafanele uvale insali yabo ngosuku losizi. Ngoba usuku lweNkosi LUKHONA, futhi luphezu kwabo bonke abezizwe: njengoba wenzile, kuyokwenziwa kuwe: umvuzo wakho uyobuyela ekhanda lakho.
