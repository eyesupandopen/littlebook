Into Enhle Ngendlela Engavamile

IZwi leNkosi liphezu kwami, bhekani izwi leNkosi!

Bhekani! Ngenza into enhle ngendlela engavamile phambi kwenu, kodwa abaningi benu bayanqaba ukubona!

Bhekani! Ngenza umsebenzi omkhulu phakathi kwenu ozopheleliswa maduzane!

Bhekani! Ngenza umsebenzi omkhulu phakathi kwenu, enisindiswa futhi niphiliswe ngawo!

Ngenze okungaphezu kokulungisa izingcezu ezimbalwa eziphukile. Ngenze okungaphezu kokunihlanganisa futhi, lapho kusabonakala khona imithungo nezibazi. Mina, Kristu ophilayo, ngenze abakhethiweyo nabathembekileyo bami baphelele kimi, futhi nginidalile kabusha. Yazini ukuthi izimpilo zenu zifihliwe kimi, futhi lapho ngivela, nani niyovela nami ekukhazimuleni futhi kusukela lapho niyoba nami kuze kube phakade. Ningumakoti wami futhi ningumzimba wami; anginibizanga yini ngamagama? Anginenzelanga yini indlela yokubuyela kuYihlo, uYise wezinkanyiso?

Nginguye owayaziwa nguBaba ngaphambi kokuba umhlaba ube khona, owathunywa wazalelwa insindiso yenu, owabethelwa, wanikela ngethonsi lokugcina legazi lakhe ngenxa yezono zenu. Akukho okushiywe ngaphandle futhi akukho okungaphelele, futhi lokho osekushiwo kuyokwenziwa kupheleliswe ngokupheleleyo. Nginidalile ngomoya wami ongcwele uBaba awupha ukuba uthululwe phezu kwabaphostoli bami ngalolo suku lwePhentekoste olwadlula kudala, lapho bekhuluma ngemisebenzi emangalisayo kaNkulunkulu ngezilimi bona ngokwabo ababengaziqondi. Ngibizile, futhi nakuba abaningi bengangamukelanga, insali yomusa nokukhethwa isabelile, futhi ngenze umsebenzi omkhulu kuyo osuzokwambulwa. Ngeqiniso, lapho nizibuka esibukweni, aniziboni ingempela yenu. Nginenze naba namandla ebuthakathakeni benu, futhi ekubonakaleni kwami, nani niyovela kanjalo. Niyofana nami, futhi niyoba nami kuze kube phakade. Ngingulibo lwabafi, futhi isivuno sesisondele kini. Lapho ngivela, niyokwenyuka nihlangabeze mina, futhi niyoba sendlini kaBaba lapho nginihlwitha nginiyisa ekhaya lenu emazulwini.

Ngeqiniso, ngeqiniso, eZwini lami elingcwele (njengoba nginguZwi elenziwa inyama ongcwele futhi ohamba phakathi kwenu), yebo, ezwini lami elingcwele, anifundanga yini ukuthi ukwahlulela kuqala endlini kaNkulunkulu? Sengizophelelisa umsebenzi kubakhethiweyo bami, abalungileyo, nabakhethiweyo, lapho niyodalwa kabusha ngokuphelele kimi futhi niyoba njengami, ngoba nifaniswe nomfanekiso weNdodana ezelwe yodwa kaNkulunkulu. Umvuzo wami unami, futhi ngeke nginahlulele ngenxa yezono zenu; angizikhokhelanga yini vele? Ngokwamukela ubukhosi bami nokuzihlanganisa nami, nithethelelwe futhi nilungile kimi, ngoba ngakhokha inani elisabekayo elalingesilona elami ukulikhokha njengoba ngingenasono. Cha, ngathatha izono zenu, ububi, ukuthakatha, nolaka nesijeziso sakho ukuze nihlanganiswe noYise neNdodana. Cha, ngizokwahlulela imisebenzi yenu eyenziwe emhlabeni.

Futhi ngokwahlulela kwami kunomvuzo omkhulu, futhi imisebenzi eminingi eyigolide, isiliva, namatshe ayigugu iyosinda. Ngeqiniso, ukhuni, utshani, nezinhlanga kuyosha kube ngumquba, futhi niyolahlekelwa, nakuba niyosindiswa njengokungathi kungomlilo. Ngoba umlilo wami kini ungumlilo womcwebisi, engicwebise ngawo imiphefumulo yenu njengoba umkhandi wegolide ecwebisa igolide lakhe, esusa amanyela. Qiniselani nithule kimi. Ngubani ongakuhlupha, nakuba ngeqiniso nishushiswa futhi nikhuthazelela usizi? Ungesabi, bangizonda mina nani bayanizonda. Nokho, ekukhululweni kwenu niyodunyiswa kakhulu enkazimulweni engizonembathisa yona ngalolo suku oluseduze nolusondela ngokushesha. UBaba ulangazelela ukunibona phambi kwakhe esigodlweni sakhe sobukhosi, lapho izihlalo zenu zilindele khona, ngoba uBaba unihlalise ezulwini. Aningebona yini abakhethiweyo bakhe, amanxusa akhe, inkazimulo yakhe, umqhele wakhe, nomklomelo wenani elingenakulinganiswa? Ngeqiniso, anilinganiswa nalutho.

Ukwahlulela kuzowela phezu kweBhabhiloni Elikhulu maduzane, futhi kuyoba ukwahlulela okwesabekayo, kodwa kufanelekile. O, muntu, ngizokubekezelela kuze kube nini? Usukile esizweni sabangcwele esasiyisibani ezweni ukuba lize kimi, waba yisizwe sezifebe ezipingayo. Ngihlukanisile abantu bami kini, futhi ukuhlukana sekuzopheleliswa njengoba lesi sikhathi somusa siphela. O, muntu, kungani ulandela omubi, wamukela izifumbathiso zakhe ezingenamsebenzi kunami, uKristu waphakade, kukho konke ubukhazikhazi nenkazimulo yami.

UBaba wanika zonke izizwe isikhathi sokuphenduka, futhi abantu abaningi, isixuku esikhulu, samukela isipho sami emuseni wami wensindiso owathululwa phezu kwabo.

Kodwa wena, o, muntu wemvelo onqaba izinto zomoya futhi odelela umakhelwane wakhe, uyokuthola ukwahlulelwa okukhulu ezweni lakho, futhi iBhabhiloni Elikhulu ngeke liphinde libe khona. Bhabhiloni, usuphenduke isizwe sezifebe ezidlala indima izingubo zazo ziphezu kwamakhanda njengembongolo yasendle esesikhathini ilindele odlulayo olandelayo. Nizangcolisile ngenyama engaziwa, nijabulele futhi nathanda izono zenu, futhi ukwahlulelwa kwaphakade kuzowela phezu kwabo bonke abanenhliziyo elukhuni ababheka umhlatshelo wami njengento elula nengenamsebenzi. Usuphenduke isizwe sabenzi bokubi nabaphuli bezivumelwano abashintsha abafazi njengezingubo ezindala bathathe ezintsha kuze kungcole lezo ezintsha. Amazwi enu amakhulu eniwakhulume ngokuphambene noBaba wami anizolandisa ngawo. Imicabango yenu engenamsebenzi iyobekwa phambi kwenu, futhi niyolandisa. Kodwa ukulandisa kwenu ngeke kwanele ukuba nime nilungile phambi kwami. Ukuba khona kwenu sekonakele, futhi ebuhlanyini benu nikhethe inyama yenu ebolayo, ngoba njengoba uBaba wathi, niyokufa nokufa. Nokho, kusabonakala kungajwayelekile kini ukuthi ekufeni niyokufa. Nakuba ukuphela kwenu okuphelele kungazange kwenzeke ngaso leso sikhathi, benifa kusukela ngosuku lokuphefumula kwenu kokuqala. Ukwahlulelwa sekumenyezelwe esizweni senu, futhi amazulu awamemezi yini okufanayo? Ngizobhubhisa ngokuphelele izwe lenu, nemibhoshongo yenu emikhulu iyowa, nobumnyama obukhulu buyosibekela umhlaba. Izindonga zenu sezivele zibhoboziwe, futhi izigidi zize kini zizothatha kini lokho engibanike khona, ngoba ngizokhuluphelisa isandla sezitha zenu ekwahlulelweni kwenu. Isikhathi senu sesicishe siphelile, futhi niyisizwe esiboshiwe phambi kwami, nilindele izingelosi zami ukuba zize ngokwahlulela. Niyowa kakhulu njengoba amanzi efika phezu kwenu, njengoba ukuzamazama kufika phezu kwenu, njengoba amabutho ekhululwa, futhi izwe lenu liyodabuka phakathi. Indlala iyohlala phezu komhlaba, nempi iyofika emhlabeni wonke njengoba isilo sivuka emlotheni ngamaqhinga aso ahlosiwe isikhathi eside.

Kuye kwanikezwa isikhathi sokuphenduka, futhi abaningi baphendulile ngokholo, umusa omkhulu engiwunikezile ngaphambi kokuba kwenziwe ukwahlulela. Angithokozi ngokubhujiswa okuzayo, kodwa ngisho namanje, abaningi baphila esonweni esikhulu, baze bahlukumeze nezingane zenu, futhi aninandaba inqobo nje uma imali yenu iseduze. Ngizothatha yonke ingcebo, izibiliboco, injabulo, nenjabulo kini. Niyoshwabana phambi kwamazulu, nifuna ucezu lokudla abaningi abangeke baluthole. Nokho, labo ababiza egameni leNkosi bayosindiswa.

Bantwanyana bami, yiwashi lokugcina lesikhathi sokugcina kulesi sikhathi somusa. Ngithumele abaprofethi bami emsebenzini womngane wami othandekayo uJohane Umbhapathizi. Abaphendukanga yini izwi elimemeza ehlane? Ababona yini abelusi bami, ababizayo nabafuna izimvu zami zokugcina ezilahlekile? Ngeqiniso, nginesinye isibaya futhi, nesikhathi sensindiso yaso sesisondele, njengoba uJakobe ezosindiswa futhi insali kaJakobe izongamukela njengeNkosi yayo, njengoba bonke abakhethiweyo beba umuntu omusha munye. Ngithumele abaningi emhlabeni wonke, bememeza ukufika kwami.

Nakuba abaningi babantu bami bekhohlisiwe futhi besivivi, umoya wami uyovuka kwabaningi, futhi abaningi bayobuyela kimi. Ukubonakala kwami sekusondele. Yimani niqine nimemeze ukufika kwami ​​nimemeze namazwi ami ensindiso kubo bonke. Isikhathi sesicishe sifike, futhi kunokuthula ekujuleni njengoba amabutho asezulwini elindele ukushaywa kwecilongo okusondele ukudlula lokho eningakucabanga. Nokho, kusele isikhathi esifushane kakhulu, futhi abaningi abengeziwe basazosindiswa, ngakho-ke ningadangali.

Nginguye owelusa ibandla lami futhi wanika abantu izipho.

NginguNkosi omkhulu nonamandla, owahlatshwa kusukela ekusekelweni komhlaba.

Nginguye owanikela ngempilo yakhe ngenxa yomhlambi, uKristu ophilayo uNkulunkulu amvusile kwabafileyo.

NgiyiZwi elenziwa inyama elihlala phakathi kwenu.

NgiyiNdodana ezelwe yodwa kaNkulunkulu eze enyameni, uMesiya onamandla ka-Israyeli kaNkulunkulu.

Ngiseduze, nokubonakala kwami kuseduze.

Ngingumahluleli olungileyo ofanele ukuvula izimpawu.

Ngiyiwundlu likaNkulunkulu, elahlatshwa ngenxa yezono zabantu bami.

Ngingumsindisi ongenasono osindisa ngokuphelele.

Ngingumuntu owazi usizi olukhulu nosizi, nokho injabulo yami ihlala igcwele.

NginguNkosi ophilayo, umalusi olungileyo.

Ngingulowo okuprofethwe ngaye yibo bonke abaprofethi futhi nginezikhiye zokufa nesihogo, futhi uBaba ubeke zonke izinto ezulwini nasemhlabeni ezandleni zami ezinamandla.
