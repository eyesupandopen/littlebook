Incazelo Yemidumo Eyisikhombisa

Imfihlakalo Yencwajana Encane

Kusuka kuYeshua HaMashiach:

Bhekani, imfihlakalo yencwajana encane! Ngoba izingelosi eziyisikhombisa zithumela imidumo yazo, futhi imidumo ikhiphe amazwi ayo. Ziyimfihlakalo yencwajana encane, ngoba incwajana encane yayivalwe ngophawu. Ngoba izwi lavela ezulwini, litshela uJohane uMambuli: Vala ngophawu lezo zinto ezakhulunywa imidumo eyisikhombisa, futhi ungazibhali. Bheka, wadla incwajana encane, ngoba yembuliwe, futhi yayimnandi njengoju ezindlebeni zakho ngenkathi ulele, kodwa yamumatha esiswini sakho okwakuphaphamisa embonweni; futhi bheka, waphaphama ebuthongweni bakho! Bhekani, imfihlakalo yencwajana encane isizokwembulwa ngalesi sikhathi, ngoba iyimfihlakalo yokugcina ezokwembulwa enkathini yami yomusa, yona kanye incwajana emnandi nemunyu. Ngoba amazwi emidumo azekwembulwa, futhi manje yisikhathi sokwembulwa, ngoba imfihlakalo yencwajana encane inkulu, nakuba iyincwajana encane. Ngoba leyo ncwajana encane yembulelwa uJohane uMambuli, futhi manje ngizoyembula kini, ngoba ukwembulwa kwayo kuseduze. Yizwa izwi lami, makoti wami, futhi uthobele indlebe yakho kimi, uKristu Jesu, ngoba isikhathi sesifikile. Ngoba asisekho isikhathi.

Ngiyindodana kaNkulunkulu eze ngenyama, futhi mina, uYeshua HaMashiach, ngithumele lawa mazwi.

Kusuka kuYahweh, uNkulunkulu waphakade:

Izwi lemidumo eyisikhombisa LIYAKHULUMA! Zilungiseleleni, O nina zizwe, ngoba ukwahlulela okukhulu kuphezu kwenu, futhi amazwi emidumo eyisikhombisa ayembuliwe. Bhekisani izindlebe zenu nizwe amazwi emfihlakalo Yencwajana Encane. Ngoba imiqulu izovulwa, futhi ukuxegiswa kwezimpawu kuyeza emhlabeni wonke, kona kanye ukwahlulela okukhulu nenhlekelele. Ngoba izono zomhlaba ziyiphunga elibi emakhaleni ami, futhi mina, uYahweh uqobo, ngizohlanza umhlaba, futhi ngizobhidliza zonke izinto ezimbi; futhi indodana yami, engiyithumayo, izobusa ngokulunga emhlabeni, futhi ukuthula okukhulu kuyoba kwabathembekileyo engibavikele osukwini olubi. Futhi ukuthula okukhulu ngizokunikeza bonke abakhuleka egameni likaJesu Kristu, uYeshua HaMashiach uqobo, ngoba bonke abakhuleka egameni leNkosi bazosindiswa. Futhi ngokuthula nokuphepha ngizobahola, nangokulunga ngizobembathisa, nangobungcwele beqiniso bazongidumisa; ngoba ngibadumisile, futhi bazoba nokuphila okusha.

Ngakho-ke ngiletha ukulunga kwami nolaka lwami ukuze ngiphindisele kulabo abenza okubi ngezindlela zabo ezingcolileyo, ngakho noma ngubani ongalayo mina akhethe ububi, makabe mubi njalo, futhi ulaka lukaNkulunkulu luhleli phezu kwakhe! Futhi noma ngubani oyoba ngolungileyo nabo bonke abasuka ebubini njengoba bekhuleka egameni leNkosi bayoba nomvuzo omkhulu; ngakho mababe nezithelo zokuthula zokulunga, futhi bayoba nefa elikhulu endodaneni yami, uKristu Jesu, ophethe umvuzo wabo esandleni sakhe. Ngakho noma ngubani oyoba mubi, makabe mubi njalo. Futhi noma ngubani ophendukela ekulungeni futhi ekhuleka egameni leNkosi, makabe ngolungileyo njalo, ngoba usuku luyokumemezela lokho. Bhekani, into engizonitshela yona ingakenzeki, ngoba iziphrofetho sezizogcwaliseka, futhi ngeke kusaba khona ukulibala. Ngoba izikhathi zomusa nesihe sezizophela, futhi isikhathi solaka olukhulu sesizokhululwa phezu komhlaba, futhi akukaze kube nesikhathi esinjengalesi emhlabeni, ngisho nasezinsukwini zikaNowa, futhi ngeke kuphinde kube nesikhathi esinjalo futhi. Ngoba mina, yebo mina, ngifungile ngegama lami futhi ngimemezele lezi zinto. Noma yini mina, uYahweh uNkulunkulu waphakade, engiyimemezelayo ayinakuguqulwa futhi iyokwenzeka. Ngoba ezinsukwini zengelosi yesikhombisa, lapho isizoqala ukukhala, ngisho nemfihlakalo kaNkulunkulu iyobe isiphelile.

Nginguye Enginguye, ngakho batshele ukuthi uMkhulu Enginguye uthumele lawa mazwi, uYahweh uqobo, uNkulunkulu omkhulu nowaphakade!

Ubufakazi Nokwenzekile, nguWilliam Brooks

Manje ukuze sithole ukuqonda okwengeziwe ngengxenye yalo myalezo lapho uYeshua HaMashiach athi, "Bheka, wadla incwajana encane, ngoba yembuliwe, futhi yayimnandi njengoju ezindlebeni zakho ngenkathi ulele, kodwa yamumatha esiswini sakho okwakuphaphamisa embonweni; futhi bheka, waphaphama ebuthongweni bakho." Esikhathini esithile esedlule enyangeni edlule noma ngaphezulu, angazi usuku oluqondile, kodwa ngangilele phakathi nobusuku, futhi ngavuswa ngaba sembonweni ngivuka ebuthongweni (noma kwakuyiphupho elicace kakhulu futhi ngavuka ekuqaleni kwalo). Izwi lalifunda amazwi engqondweni yami nasemoyeni wami, futhi ngangazi ukuthi yizwi leNkosi, futhi lalifunda ezindlebeni zami amazwi engingakwazanga ukuwaqonda ngoba amazwi ayemane egeleza engaphezi. Kodwa kanye namazwi kwafika ukuthula okukhulu okwehlela phezu kwami ngokuphelele. Amazwi ayemnandi kakhulu ezindlebeni zami, futhi ngangiphumule. Khona-ke ngokungazelelwe, ngaphuma kuleso simo nginesilungulela esibi kakhulu, isisu sami sasontene, futhi ngasuka embhedeni ngisukuma ngokushesha, ngiklinya futhi ngikhwehlela. Ngehlela phansi ngaphuza amanzi amaningi, okwasiza kancane kodwa akuzange sehlise isisu sami. Ngahlala ngiphapheme isikhashana ngemva kwalokho, kodwa angizange ngazi ukuthi konke lokhu kwakusho ukuthini.

Ngokuqondene nobufakazi, lapho uYahweh ethi, "Izwi lemidumo eyisikhombisa LIYAKHULUMA!" NgoMeyi 7, 2024, nganginosuku olunzima kakhulu, futhi ngafika ekhaya ngalala ekuseni kakhulu. Ngangilale sekuhlwile kakhulu ngobusuku bangaphambilini ngoba ngangingakwazi ukulala, okuyinto eyenzeka njalo kimi. Kwakunesiphepho esibi nemvula enkulu lapho ngilala khona, kodwa yonke imibani nokuduma kwakukude le. Ngalala phansi ukuze ngilale cishe ngehora lesikhombisa ngalobo busuku, okuyinto esekuseni kimi, futhi ngazunywa yilokho engangicabanga ukuthi ubuthongo. Angazi noma ngake ngalala ngempela yini, kodwa ngangena embonweni noma esimweni sephupho elicace kakhulu, futhi ngangikuqaphela ngokuphelele isiphepho esasiqhubeka. Amazwi namaphimbo ayefika kimi evela ezinhlangothini ezihlukahlukene, futhi ayekuyo yonke indawo. Angikhumbuli okuqukethwe ngamaphimbo ahlukahlukene, ngaphandle kwelilodwa. Kwakuyizwi elihlukile kakhulu, futhi wonke amanye amaphimbo athula. Izwi lathi, "Izwi lemidumo eyisikhombisa LIYAKHULUMA!" Lapho lawo mazwi ekhulunywa, ngavuswa ngokushesha umsindo omkhulu wokuduma engake ngawuzwa. Ngahlala lapho embhedeni wami, ngimangele ngokuphelele.

Lapho ngabelana ngalokhu nomkami ngemva kosuku noma ezimbili, sasisemgubazini wethu wangaphambili. Wangibamba ngesandla wangihola sizungeza indlu siqonde ngasemuva kwegceke. Wangitshela ukuthi yena nendodana yethu babesegunjini lokuphumula lapho lokho kuduma kushaya. Ukukhanya kombani kwakhanyisa futhi kwazamazamisa igumbi lokuphumula. Ngokusobala, kwakushaye endaweni eseduze kakhulu negceke lethu elingemuva, elincane impela, njengoba sihlala edolobheni. Nokho asibonanga monakalo ndawo. Ngike ngabona umphumela lapho umbani ushaya ngamandla emhlabeni noma entweni esemhlabeni, futhi ushiya umonakalo ngezinga elithile. Wabona ukukhanya kombani, futhi sobabili sezwa ukuduma okukhulu kakhulu, kodwa akukho monakalo owawutholakala ndawo.


Juni 12, 2024

INkosi ingembulele ukuthi lo msebenzi uyini ngempela, futhi unesihloko esilandelayo. Ngitholile okuqukethwe Yincwajana Encane esesandleni Sengelosi enamandla kusAmbulo 10, eyembethe ifu futhi uthingo lwalusekhanda layo nobuso bayo bunjengelanga nezinyawo zayo zinjengezinsika zomlilo. Lokhu kumelela izinkathi nezivumelwano ezihambisanayo, konke okugcina ebusweni bukaJesu Kristu, ozalwa yedwa kaYahweh, nomfanekiso kaNkulunkulu waphakade.

Uthingo luphawu olunikezwa esivumelwaneni sikaNowa uYahweh asenza noNowa ngemuva kukazamcolo. Ifu nezinsika zomlilo zimelela isivumelwano sikaNkulunkulu noMose. Ubuso bukaYeshua HaMashiach bukhanya njengelanga futhi buyisivumelwano esisha. Le miyalezo eyisikhombisa iqukethe ukwahlulelwa kwabawileyo nabanye kusukela ngaphambi kukazamcolo kaNowa kuze kube sekupheleni, kulokho okusazokwenzeka kusukela osukwini lwanamhlanje.
