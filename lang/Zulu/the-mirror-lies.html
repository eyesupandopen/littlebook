Okubabayo Okumnandi
uZibandlela 5, 2024

Isibuko Siqamba Amanga!

Sibuko, sibuko eludongeni, ngitshele ngezwe lapho izikrini zande khona zikhanga bonke! Ngokuphinda amanga ashiwo kaningi, ngezithombe zamanga ezingapheli ukuze ezqondweni zabo babumbe inkohliso enkulu ngokuzijabulisa okuyisiko ukuze izimvu zidukiswe yimicabango abayiphethe ezqondweni zabo! Noma kunjalo, bahlatshwa umxhwele yizinkanuko zenyama, nezinkanuko zamehlo nokuzidla kwempilo okubhebhethekiswayo futhi kuvuswe olwazini lwamanga olunikezwa njengemfundo noma ngabe amanga abo athengiswa ngobuqili kuphi. Ngaleyo ndlela, izindlebe ziyakitazwa futhi ziduduzwe ngamaza aphindaphindwayo ukuze abazidlayo neziwula balawulwe futhi baqondiswe. Futhi ngokuhamba kwesikhathi bathole ukuthi ngokusebenzisa imilolozelo elawula ingqondo, ukuziphatha kwesintu kuyashintshwa ngokufanele futhi esipelini sabo uyaboshwa!

Nokho linjalo izwe ohlala kulo, O' Israyeli, izwe elele ngokuphelele ebubini, izwe elithunjiwe futhi lahlatshwa umxhwele ngokuphelele ngendwangu yamanga anikezwa ukuhlela izixuku ezindabeni zabo zokubulala ezinikezwa manje zingaphezi ukuze kubhujiswe u-Adamu. Izibuko, zibukiswa yonke indawo, kodwa ekubukeni kwesibuko iqiniso alikho lapho, ngoba iqiniso alibonwa ngamehlo abukele lapho ngoba izibuko zabo ziyakhohlisa ukuze zakhe iqiniso lamanga elenziwe ngobuchwepheshe babawileyo obenzelwe ukugqilaza isintu emangeni abo ukuze u-Adamu afe ingunaphakade.

Izibuko ezimnyama, zibukiswa yonke indawo futhi zibekwe ukuze zibukwe nomaphi lapho amehlo angazulazula khona, ukuze izibuko zitshele amanga afanayo ahlanganiswe ngomklamo ononya, ukuze umuntu wemvelo ajatshuliswe yisono nokufa futhi athathekiswe yizilingo eziningi nezinkulu ezipakishwe njengokuhlakanipha nokuchuma kodwa esikhundleni salokho zibangele ukufa nokubhujiswa ngoba alikho iqiniso ezithombeni zamanga abazinikezayo. Izibuko ezimnyama, zibukiswa futhi ziphethwe esandleni lapho isithombe nesithombe sakha uhlanya nenkohliso enamandla ezweni, lapho iqiniso lingahlukaniswa khona namanga, lapho bonke abathokoza ngokungalungi bengayi kubalekela kodwa bayakufa phakade emalangabini okubhujiswa okuphakade, isijeziso ababoshelwe kuso phakade futhi ayikho indawo abangabalekela noma bacashe kuyo lapho kungatholakala khona ukugwema ukwahlulelwa.

O', bezwe behlabelela! Lalela uzwe izwi lezinhlenhlenhle eziningi zihlabelela ngazwi linye, zihambisana nezithombe zamanga ezivumelanisiwe ezinikezwa yizibuko zabo ezimnyama njalo. Zihlabelela eduze kakhulu nezingoma zezinyoni zangempela kodwa eqinisweni eyazo ingoma yomgunyathi etshela amanga ukuze amazwi azo ahambisane nobuqotho bamanga ezinkohlisweni zabo abazama ukuzifihla. Nokho lezi zinhlenhlenhle zamanga azinazo izingoma zazo kodwa zikopisha futhi ziphinde izingoma zezinyoni zangempela ngaphandle kokubhala noma ukuqonda amagama ezingomeni ezizihlabelelayo, ngakho-ke izithombe ezisakazwa ezibukweni ezimnyama eziyizikrini ziyahambelaniswa ukuze amanga aphindwe futhi izixuku zikhubazeke ngalokho. Ngisho nemibono engamanga neqiniso lamanga lapho umhlaba wonke uthathwe emangeni abawileyo, iqiniso lamanga elifikile emhlabeni eliyokwanda kuphela lapho ngisusa umvimbi wami lapho ngibuthela abathembekile kuKristu ekuphepheni kwamazulu ngokungazelele nangokungalindelekile manje.

Ngakho-ke, yizwani amazwi kaYeshua HaMashiach niqonde izwe eniphila kulo nenkohliso evunyelwe ukubamba umhlaba ngokwanga kwayo kwamanga. Ngisho nenkohliso enikezwe ekwahlulelweni lapho abawileyo bevunyelwe ukuba nokubusa kwabo, futhi lokho kungenxa yokuthi umuntu wemvelo ufisa ukujoyina oSathane ekuhlubukeni kwabo ngenxa yenkokhelo yobubi, ukuze athokoze ngokungalungi kube ngukufa kwakhe, ngakho-ke umuntu wemvelo manje uzonikezwa ezandleni zezitha zakhe, kodwa noma kunjalo isixuku siyakusindiswa ngomlilo womcwebisi. Futhi ukuhlukaniswa okuqhubekayo nokuphelele kuyokwenzeka ngesikhathi sokuhlupheka kukaJakobe, ukuhlukaniswa lapho insali yakwa-Israyeli ikhululwa ekugqilazweni kuka-Esawu futhi ikhululelwe embusweni wami weminyaka eyinkulungwane ozomiswa maduze emhlabeni!

Nokho, umuntu wemvelo ukhetha iqiniso lamanga labawileyo lapho befuna ukucasha kuYahweh (okuyinto ukufihlakala kungenakwenzeka), kodwa kunjalo inkohliso enamandla abayisabalalise emhlabeni wonke lapho bonke abenqaba uJesu waseNazaretha, uYeshua HaMashiach, begqilazwa ngayo. Ngakho-ke, yizwani Amazwi kaYeshua HaMashiach, ukuze niqonde izwe eniphila kulo nenkohliso enamandla egone umhlaba esandleni sayo. Qonda, O' Israyeli, ukuthi mina, uYeshua HaMashiach onguKristu Jesu, nginguye oyosusa ubumpumputhe obuyingxenye yenu lapho niphendulela izinhliziyo zenu ekulaleleni okuphelele kwezwi lami, elikhulunywa ezindebeni ezithembekile zabathembekile abaphila ngokukholwa eNdodaneni kaYahweh, ngoba olungileyo uyophila ngokukholwa!

Yini leyo ebike yaba khona? Yileyo eyoba khona: futhi yini leyo eseyenziwe? Yileyo eyokwenziwa futhi: futhi akukho lutho olusha ngaphansi kwelanga. Kukhona yini, lapho umuntu angathi, Bheka lokhu, kusha? Kwase kukhona kakade ezikhathini zakudala ezazikhona ngaphambi kwethu. Akukho ukukhunjulwa kwalesi sikhathi sangaphambili, futhi ngeke kube khona ukukhunjulwa kwesikhathi sakamuva esiyoba khona, nalabo abayokuza ngemva kwalokho. Impela, impela, kuyofika isikhathi lapho abawileyo bengeke bakhunjulwe, noma bafike engqondweni futhi izimfihlakalo zabo zamanga ziyophela phakade lapho bephonswa emalangabini.

Ngakho-ke, qondani amanga asendulo anikezwa ngabawileyo esintwini ezweni elalikhona ngaphambi kukazamcolo omkhulu kaNowa, wona lawo manga aphinde apakishe futhi asetshenziselwe ukubhubhisa isintu futhi. Ngakho-ke qondani, abawileyo baletha ubuchwepheshe babo nokukhohlisa kwabo nezibonakaliso zamanga nezimangaliso ababezisebenzisa ukuthakatha nokubhubhisa isintu ezinsukwini zikaJarede. Futhi umhlaba uyobuyela njengoba kwakunjalo ezinsukwini zikaNowa futhi kuyoba njalo nasekuhluphekeni kukaJakobe, isikhathi emhlabeni esibuswa yizitha zikaYahweh. Ngisho nalowo drago omkhulu wasendulo manje oboshelwe emhlabeni ukuze ahlulelwe, nezinengiso eziningi ziyokhululwa ezweni labantu; njengoba uYahweh ememezele ukuthi uyoletha imidondoshiya ukuze igcwalise ulaka lwakhe.

Ngoba lokhu ngamabomu abanakile, ukuthi ngezwi likaNkulunkulu amazulu ayekhona kusukela kudala, nomhlaba umi ngaphandle kwamanzi, nasemanzini, lapho izwe elalikhona ngaleso sikhathi, ligcwele amanzi ngodlame, labhubha. Kodwa amazulu nomhlaba okhona manje, ngeZwi elifanayo agciniwe, abekelwe umlilo kuze kube usuku lokwahlulelwa, nokulahlwa kwabantu abangamesabi uNkulunkulu.

Ngosizi, izixuku eziningi, eziningi emhlabeni ngamabomu azinakile ukuthi izwe elalikhona ngaleso sikhathi uYahweh waligcwalisa ngamanzi ngodlame futhi labhubha ngenxa yamaNefili nezinengiso zawo eziningi; nangenxa yabawileyo abazenza onkulunkulu lapho bonakalisa izindlela zesintu emhlabeni ngezimfihlakalo zabo ezingenamsebenzi ezifundiswa esintwini ukuze zibhubhise sona. Ngakho yizwa iqiniso, uqonde Amazwi ami, futhi uzwe izwi likaYeshua HaMashiach ukuze uqonde iqiniso futhi ukuze ungabanjwa ngobuchwepheshe babo nokuthi ungashintshwa luphawu lwesilo ube yisinengiso esigcinelwe amalangabi esijeziso saphakade.

Ngoba ubuchwepheshe babo yilokho okwake kwenziwa ngaphambili, futhi kukhona manje, futhi kuyofika ngokugcwele futhi okokugcina. Impela, ubuchwepheshe babo buyodalulwa ngokugcwele njengoba beqhubeka nokwethula uhlelo lwabo lokugcina lwesilo, nazo zonke izibonakaliso zamanga nezimangaliso ezinikezwa labo ababhubhayo. Kanjalo udrago uyovuka abuse umhlaba ngenkohliso enkulu ngendlela yewundlu elinezimpondo ezimbili.

Futhi abawileyo bayoziveza njengezidalwa ezinomusa ezivela emazweni ajikelezayo ngamanga emkhathini wabo wamanga wamazwe ajikelezayo ngamanga asetshenziswe ezinsukwini zakudala. Qonda ukuthi ngaphezu komhlaba kukhona amazulu nomkhathi kanye nezulu lamazulu lapho uYahweh ehlezi esihlalweni sakhe sobukhosi, futhi impela, impela uYahweh ubusa phezu kwayo yonke indalo yakhe ayidala eyedwa ekuqaleni ngokweseluleko sentando yakhe nangezinjongo zakhe zaphakade.

Ngakho-ke, funda ukuze uqonde izwe lakho njengoba linjalo ngempela futhi uqonde ukuthi amapulanethi ayizinkanyezi ezizulazulayo ezinikezwe njengezibonakaliso nezinkathi zonyaka futhi akuwona amanye amazwe anezinye izinhlobo zokuphila, ngakho-ke, lahla amanga abawileyo. Laba okuthiwa ngabafokazi yilabo abahlubuka kuYahweh kusukela ekuqaleni futhi bangabaphandle komhlaba ngomqondo wokuthi imvelaphi yabo isemazulwini ngosuku abadalwa ngalo ekulungeni okuphelele nguYahweh uqobo. Nokho, ekuhlubukeni kwabo bawa ekupheleleni kwabo kwasekuqaleni, futhi baziguqula ingunaphakade, futhi abayikuphunyuka. Nokho umuntu wemvelo ukhetha la manga ukuze afeze izinkanuko zenyama yakhe ebolile kukho konke ukona nokungalungi, futhi bonke abahamba kule ndlela ebanzi yokubhujiswa bayowela emalangabini aphakade ngalokho.

Kanjalo bayovela, bezenza abanakekela izindaba zesintu futhi bayoziveza ngokugcwele lapho umvimbi esusiwe njengoba iBandla lami libuthelwa kimi emafwini. Bayovela bethi beze ukuvikela isintu ekuhlaselweni nokusindisa isintu empini futhi bayoza namanga amaningi apakishwe kahle futhi azwakala emnandi. Nokho, izimpondo eziyishumi zinomqondo owodwa futhi zizonika amandla azo negunya esilwaneni! Futhi izimpondo eziyishumi yizo eziyozonda isifebe, futhi zisenze incithakalo futhi zihambe ze, futhi zidle inyama yaso, futhi zisishise ngomlilo empini yazo yezwe yesithathu esezogubuzela umhlaba. Nokho bayovela nobuchwepheshe babo ezandleni, kodwa baletha kuphela izixazululo zamanga nezindlela zamanga nenkohliso eziholela ekwahlulelweni kwaphakade nasekulahlweni kwabo bonke abajoyina ukuhlubuka kwabo.

Nokho, kanjalo bayovuka, isilo esivela olwandle nesilo esivela emhlabeni, futhi bayovuka njengewundlu elinezimpondo ezimbili elikhuluma njengodrago, iWundlu likaDrago u-Esawu azikhethele lona ukuze abuse umhlaba ngokungalungi. Ngisho nokubusa lapho izizwe zibuswa ngonya yi-Ashkenazi nge-Noahide ngesilo esivela olwandle nesilo esivela emhlabeni esibusa izwe nomhlaba ngeTalmud ka-Esawu ngenkohliso enkulu njengoba ebekela uJakobe ugibe lwakhe.

Qonda ukuthi isilo esivela olwandle sizihlukanisile neBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo ukuze sizihlukanise namacala aso amaningi, futhi bayogcoba igama laso futhi babonakale bemmangalela. Kodwa qonda ukuthi isilo esivela olwandle nesilo esivela emhlabeni bavela ezizukulwaneni ezifanayo ezinengekayo futhi basebenza ngokubambisana, iqembu lezilo ezimbili, ukuze bakhonze futhi banelise izinjongo zikanodrago zokukhanuka. Isilo esivela olwandle siyovuka ngesikhathi saso futhi siyovuka sibe yiqhawe labo elithenjisiwe, uMahdi wabo abamlindele njengamanje. Futhi uyofihla imvelo yakhe yangempela nobubi bakhe, nakuba imizamo enjalo iyize ngoba sonke isono ake asenza silotshwe ngokuphelele emazulwini. Nokho, uyokhohlisa labo abamlandelayo ngezithembiso zakhe eziyize.

Futhi njengoba bevuka emlotheni wempi, bayozihlanganisa obala nodrago nabawileyo okungababo kakade, futhi bayoshesha ukuzimisa embusweni wabo wokugcina, asebevele bebeke uhlaka lwawo, ngisho nangaphambi kokuba iBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo libhujiswe ngendlela yemfihlakalo yobubi. Nokho, bayofuna ukwakha lokho osekuke kwaba khona njengoba abawileyo befuna ukusungula inkathi yabo entsha yegolide, inkathi yegolide abangeke babe nayo kodwa ukwahlulelwa okunzima esikhundleni salokho. Noma kunjalo, bayovuka ngamandla amakhulu futhi isilo esisodwa siyokhomba esinye futhi bayofakazelana, omunye ngomunye nomunye ngomunye, futhi bayokhuluma izinto eziningi ezinkulu nehlazo futhi balethe izibonakaliso eziningi zamanga nezimangaliso.

Noma kunjalo, kanjalo bayovuka njengokulotshiwe. Futhi iWundlu elinezimpondo ezimbili liyosebenza ngokubambisana nodrago futhi bayohlanganisa zonke izinto zibe yinto eyodwa emphakathini wabo omusha. Bayohlanganisa izinhlelo zabo zezimali, bayohlanganisa ohulumeni babo, bayobopha zonke izinto zomphakathi wabo omusha ndawonye futhi bahlangane njengento eyodwa ohlelweni lwabo olusha. Bayohlanganisa izinkolo zabo eziyimfihlakalo ezihlambalazayo zasendulo zibe yinkolo entsha ezobonakala njengenkolo yoYise babo abakhonza amakhiyubhu abo amnyama, nokho bayohlanganisa izinkolo zabo kwenye inkolo yamanga oYise babo ababengayazi kodwa amakhiyubhu abo ayohlala. (Nokho, ngeke ahloniphe uNkulunkulu woyise, noma izifiso zabesifazane, noma anakekele noma yimuphi uNkulunkulu: ngoba uyozikhulisa ngaphezu kwakho konke).

Qonda ukuthi bazokwenzani, O' Israyeli, ngoba bayolwa nabangcwele futhi babanqobe, futhi bonke abakhuleka egameni likaJesu waseNazaretha onguYeshua HaMashiach kuze kube sekupheleni bayosindiswa, ngoba kuthenjisiwe futhi kulotshiwe. Bayosola labo abakhuleka egameni likaJesu ngamacala abo futhi uBaraba uyokhaphela abangcwele baseBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo ngoba abaningi babo bengazi banikele amaBandla abo esilwaneni esivela emhlabeni, esisazofika ngesikhathi sokuloba lokhu. Futhi uzenze iNkosi yabo futhi wathengisa amaBhayibheli ngegama lakhe, nokho uvuma kalula ukuthi akasiye umKristu, nakuba abaningi abayiziwula bekholelwa ukuthi unjalo.

Futhi njengoba umhlaba uphuma esandleni futhi uwela empini enkulu, nasosizini, nendlala, nokungabi nabulungiswa nokufa bayosola zonke izinto zikaKristu ngezinkinga zabo abazibangele bona futhi bayothi abafokazi bathathe abathembekile ababuthene kuKristu, kanti eqinisweni ngiyobe ngibabuthele egumbini lesihlalo sobukhosi sikaYahweh emshadweni womkhwenyana nomakoti wakhe nokuthi bamukele ifa labo futhi bakhazinyuliswe phakade. Kodwa emhlabeni bayotshela amanga amaningi futhi bayokwakha izinto eziningi, eziningi ezibukweni zabo ezimnyama besebenzisa ukukhohlisa kwabawileyo nobuchwepheshe babo futhi bayosakaza izibonakaliso eziningi zamanga nezimangaliso ezikrinini zabo okuzokholelwa kalula yizixuku.

Qonda ukuthi labo abahlala emadolobheni abo nalabo abathatha uphawu lwesilo bayoguqulwa ingunaphakade futhi bayohlanganisa insimbi nobumba kodwa ngeke banamathelane. Labo abathatha uphawu lwabo bayoshintshwa futhi babe yizigqila zomqondo wabo wesidleke kanye ne-inthanethi yezinto ezizayo futhi bayoba sebugqilini bokwenza intando kadrago, lapho beyobe bezibophe khona ingunaphakade ngoba bayobe bezishintshile esithombeni sikaYahweh abadalwa ngaso. Futhi bonke abenza izinto ezinjalo abasoze bathethelelwa, ngoba lokho okugobile akunakulungiswa.

Ngakho-ke, qondani ukuthi umphakathi wabo omusha uzokwakhelwa ngokuphelele enkohlisweni nasemangeni, futhi abakhileyo bomhlaba bayohlukaniswa ngokuphelele ekukhohlisweni nomhlaba weqiniso kanye neqiniso abahlala kulo. Umhlaba wonke uzoboshwa enkohlisweni enkulu futhi izixuku ziyomangala ngesilo, zithi, ubani onjengesilo, futhi ubani ongalwa naso? Futhi abaningi, abaningi bayowela emalangabini ngokuwela emangeni aso, futhi laba bantu abamagama abo angalotshwanga encwadini yokuphila kusukela ekusekelweni komhlaba bayowa phakade ngoba bayobe bemlahlile ngokuphelele uYahweh ngokulahla uJesu Kristu onguYeshua HaMashiach.

Lokhu kulahla kuyoba okokugcina futhi ngeke bathenjiswe intethelelo ngoba babale amaZwi ensindiso ababengawasindisa ngokuphelele njengento engcolile futhi baphetha ngokuthi iqiniso likaNkulunkulu lingamanga, yebo, isono abangeke baphinde balulame kuso. Kodwa kanjalo kuyoba ukwahlukana kokugcina kwezikhathi ukuthi kungangeni lutho olubi eMbusweni wami weminyaka eyinkulungwane kodwa kuphela labo abeza kuYeshua HaMashiach onguJesu Kristu ngokuphenduka ngezikhathi zensindiso ngoba akekho umuntu ongeza kuYahweh ngelinye igama, futhi ayikho enye indlela, ngoba mina ngiyindlela eyiqiniso nephilayo.

Lezi yizikhathi eniphila kuzo, we Israyeli, futhi anikwazi ukuhlala emizini yabo futhi anikwazi ukujoyina umphakathi wabo ngoba ukujoyina bona kudinga uphawu futhi bonke abamukela uphawu lwesilo abasoze bathenjiswa intethelelo. Noma kunjalo, isintu sifundiswe ngemfundo yaso eyigugu kodwa engamanga, imfundiso ethengiswa njengemfundo, futhi izixuku sezilungiselelwe ngayo, futhi zizokwamukela amanga abo futhi iningi labantu bemvelo lizozihlanganisa nabawileyo kuze kube phakade.

Nokho kuyokuba njalo, ukuthi bonke abakholwa amanga ngokuzithandela futhi bathokoze ngokungalungi bangaboshelwa esijezisweni saphakade ngonaphakade ngokuzihlanganisa nabawileyo, futhi bonke abazihlanganisa nabawileyo bayokwabelana ngokugcwele ngefa labo elinikezwa ngaphandle kwesihe ngoba ababonakalisanga sihe kodwa inzondo enonya kuphela kuzo zonke izinto zikaYahweh.

NginguKristu Jesu onguJesu Kristu futhi onguYeshua HaMashiach futhi ngiza njengeNgonyama yesizwe sakwaJuda futhi ngokulunga ngiyeza ukwahlulela nokulwa impi.

Nginguye oyobhubhisa inkohliso enkulu neqiniso elingamanga labawileyo, futhi indalo iyokhululwa ingene enkululekweni ekhazimulayo yeqiniso nokulunga, ingene enkululekweni ekhazimulayo yamadodana kaYahweh.

Nginguye obiza abakwa-Israyeli, futhi ngempela bonke abantwana baka-Adamu, ukuba beze ensindisweni ukuze babuyisane ngokuphelele noYahweh ngendlela yegazi leWundlu, ngoba nginguye.

NginguKristu Jesu iNkosi yenu eyenyuka nevusiweyo futhi ngenziwe umoya onika ukuphila ukuze bonke abeza kimi ngenhliziyo evulekile nobuso obuvulekile bafike ngokugcwele ensindisweni eNkosini yabo yaphakade!

NginguYeshua HaMashiach, kanye noYahweh Omkhulu onguNGINGUYE, engithumele amaZwi e-Vangeli laphakade ngesithunywa sami esinamandla nenceku yami enqobe zonke izinto ngokuza ngokugcwele eNkosini yayo yaphakade noMsindisi ngokuthobeka nokuphenduka okulusizi, futhi ngesithunywa sami esinamandla nenceku yami amaZwi e-Vangeli laphakade athunyelwe ukuze alalelwe yibo bonke labo la maZwi athunyelwe kubo.

Ngakho-ke, qondani izikhathi ezikhohlisayo eniphila kuzo lapho umhlaba wonke uzothathwa uhambe ekusanganeni nasekukhohlisweni futhi ububi buyodedelwa ngokuphelele, kodwa isixuku esikhulu kakhulu siyothola insindiso yaphakade ngokubiza igama leNkosi yabo uJesu kuze kube sekupheleni. Ngokunjalo, mina, uYeshua HaMashiach onguMesiya weqiniso kaJakobe, ngiyosindisa insali enamandla yakwa-Israyeli eyothathwa futhi inikwe ukungena eMbusweni wami weminyaka eyinkulungwane lapho zonke izithembiso ziyoba ngu-yebo no-amen!

Ngalokho, we Israyeli, lahlanini ubuchwepheshe babo futhi ningaqhubeki nokuthatheka yizibuko zabo ezimnyama, izikrini zabo lapho abantu sebezigqilile njengamanje njengoba bechitha izimpilo zabo ezeni besuka esikrinini beya kwesinye njengoba bedonswa futhi bejabuliswa yisono. Iqiniso elingamanga elibonakala lingokoqobo kodwa eqinisweni, liyindaba engamanga futhi alilutho ngaphandle kwezinganekwane ezilukwe ngobuhlakani ezihloselwe ukubhubhisa isintu.

Ngakho-ke, phumani phakathi kwabo futhi nihlukane nabo ezintweni zonke. Zihlanzeni egazini lami lokuhlawulela ngokuvuma zonke izono zenu kimi futhi nizihlanze ezwini lami elingcwele, amazwi kaYahweh, ngoba ngiyiZwi elenziwa inyama elihlala phakathi kwenu futhi ngempela, ngaphakathi kini ngomoya wami wesithembiso uma nilalela amaZwi ami.

Ngiyanithanda bantu bakithi, futhi nginilwela ngempela futhi ngiyonikhulula ngokuphelele uma nje ningapheli amandla ezinkingeni zenu kodwa niphumule emandleni ami, ngoba ngiza ngegunya eligcwele likaYahweh Omkhulu onguNGINGUYE owabelane ngesihlalo sakhe sobukhosi kuze kube yilapho izitha zami zenziwa isenabelo sezinyawo zami, futhi ukubusa kwami akusoze kwaphela, futhi ngiyosindisa abalalelayo ngokuphelele othandweni lwaphakade nensindiso, futhi niyophinde nibe yisizwe esikhethekile, we Israyeli, Selah no-Amen!
