Ubufakazi Obukabili: Kwa-Israyeli
Okubabayo Nokumnandi
Novemba 7, 2024

IWundlu Elinezimpondo Ezimbili

Okuvela ku-YHVH: Mina Enginguye Omkhulu!

Bhekani! Inkohliso enkulu ivele eBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo futhi ithumbe umhlaba ngengalo yayo ebulalayo! Sondelani ukuze niqonde inkohliso enkulu, nina enakhileyo emhlabeni, futhi niqonde izikhathi eniphila kuzo, ngoba isifikile inkohliso enkulu ejatshuliswa umhlaba ngoba umhlaba wabo uyisiteji nabaholi babo bangabalingisi, yebo, abazenzisi abenza sengathi. Ngakho-ke manje ngizokwembula iqiniso mayelana nesekisi labo abalisebenzisela ukuthakatha izixuku.

Xwayani! Inkohliso enkulu ifike phezu kwenu inezibonakaliso nezimangaliso eziningi zamanga, futhi le nkohliso enkulu ihloselwe kanzima ukubhujiswa kukaJakobe, yebo, izinhloso u-Esawu azigcine ziyimfihlo kuyo yonke iminyaka, kodwa manje zizokwembulwa futhi zidalulwe ukuze bonke babone. Njengoba iNdodana yami, uJesu waseNazaretha, yatshela oYise babo eJudiya eminyakeni edlule: Nina ningabakaYihlo usathane, nezinkanuko zikaYihlo niyozifeza. Ngakho-ke ngiza kini nina bakwa-Israyeli ukuze ningathathwa yile nkohliso enkulu, ngoba niyisisulu esiqondiwe (nakuba umhlaba uyozamazama) ngoba yimuphi umthiyi obeka ugibe obala inyoni isibona?

Futhi yimina, uYahweh, Mina Enginguye Omkhulu, engiza ukuzoxwayisa abantu bami ngalawa Mazwi engiwembulile futhi engiwathumele ngesithunywa sami nenceku yami egcotshwele lezi zinhloso. Ngakho-ke ngiza kulabo bezizwe, noma beseduze noma bekude, noma besezweni noma behlakazekile phesheya, futhi ngizobutha yonke insali yami kuMesiya noMalusi wabo Oyinhloko, uYeshuwa HaMashiach.

Ngoba nguye oyohola u-Israyeli amsondeze ekuphepheni ngezandla zabangu-144,000 bami abanamandla, ngoba uJesu waseNazaretha ngimenze iNkosi yamakhosi noMbusi wababusi! Futhi nguye oyobusa umhlaba ngokulunga phakathi nokubusa kwakhe kweminyaka eyinkulungwane esihlalweni sobukhosi sikaDavide kwa-Israyeli asizuzile. Ngoba ngathembisa kanjalo koYihlo, futhi yimina engifeza sonke isithembiso engisenzayo ngokuphindaphindiwe ngokulunga kwami okuphakade, ukuze konke kube ngu-yebo no-amen, futhi nginguNkulunkulu Ongunaphakade Wokulunga, ongapheli amandla!

Nokho, sekunjalo ukuthi udrako uyovuka ngokugcwele, nesilo sasolwandle sizifihlile ukuze sivuke ngokugcwele phakathi kwalabo esiyobahola, futhi siyovuka emlotheni ngesikhathi saso esimisiwe. Siphangile futhi sahlela ukubhujiswa okuphelele kweBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo, sisebenzisana nesilo sasemhlabeni ukuhlela nokwenza ukubhujiswa kwabo, futhi ngibavumele ukuba bakufeze. Umhlaba uthakathiwe yisifebe esikhulu futhi awusakwazi ukucabanga umhlaba ongenayo ingcindezelo yesifebe esikhulu esicindezele ngayo abantu bomhlaba futhi sonakalisa indalo yami ngokungcola kobufebe baso okunikezwa umhlaba ngaleyo ndebe yegolide esesandleni saso.

Ngakho-ke, ngiza ukuzonixwayisa, nina bakwa-Israyeli, ngenkohliso enkulu esezophonsa umhlaba empini nasophithiphithini olukhulu, inkohliso abayijwayeze besebenzisa ubuchwepheshe babo ngoba bonke bangabazenzisi abenza sengathi, futhi ngenkohliso yabo umprofethi wabo wamanga uyongena ezweni njengoMoshiach wamanga ka-Esawu.

Futhi u-Esawu uyafihla ubuso bakhe phakathi kuka-Israyeli, azenze sengathi ungu-Israyeli njengoba efuna ukuzihlanganisa phakathi kuka-Israyeli, efihla imvelo yakhe yangempela kulabo azenza bona. (Bangukhula phakathi kukakolweni, futhi iNkosi Yokuvuna iyobasiphula ngokuphelele.) Ngakho-ke udrako uyavuka ngoba ihora lakhe, futhi uKristu Jesu uyosusa ngokuzumayo abakholwayo kuKristu emhlabeni (abaphilayo nabafileyo), bese ukwahlulela kufika ngokugcwele eBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo, leso sifebe esibomvu okwakhulunywa ngaso nguJohane umAmbululi eminyakeni eminingi edlule.

Futhi nginguMdala Wezinsuku, iNkosi Yemimoya, futhi ngiyafakaza ukuze bonke baqonde inkulumo yami: Into eyake yaba khona iyobuye yenzeke, nento eyake yenziwa iyophinde yenziwe, futhi akukho lutho olusha ngaphansi kwelanga. Ngakho-ke abawiweyo basebenzele ukubhubhisa u-Adamu emhlabeni kusukela ekuqaleni ukuze bazithathele wona, insimu yami yokuphila lapho nginakekela futhi ngikhulisa imiphefumulo yabantu ukuze ngiyihlenge ekonakaleni ngoKristu Jesu, engimdalile njengo-Adamu wokugcina. Futhi kukuMesiya wenu, nina bakwa-Israyeli, lapho u-Israyeli kaNkulunkulu eyobuthelwa khona ngokugcwele futhi ahlengwe njengomuntu omusha oyedwa, owenziwe ngendlela efanayo neNkosi noMhlengi wenu, owathenga ukuhlengwa kwenu ngegazi lakhe lokuhlawulela.

Ngakho-ke qondani nina bakwa-Israyeli ukuthi uJesu waseNazaretha unguYeshuwa HaMashiach onguKristu Jesu futhi (qondani ukugcizelelwa kwamagama akhe ngoba insindiso yenu iphelele), ngakho lalelani aMazwi akhe ngokulalela ngenxa yemiphefumulo yenu, ngoba anisenaso isikhathi sokuphikisana noMdali wenu, futhi anisenaso isikhathi sokwenqaba uMesiya wenu weqiniso.

Futhi kulotshiwe njengoba ngishilo, futhi ngakho ngenza ukuba uYeshuwa HaMashiach embulele umphostoli wakhe uPawulu ngaye: Ngalokho, u-Elohim wamphakamisa kakhulu, wamnika igama eliphezu kwawo onke amagama, Ukuze ngegama likaJesu amadolo onke aguqe, awasezulwini, nawasemhlabeni, nawangaphansi komhlaba, Futhi ukuze zonke izilimi zivume ukuthi uJesu Kristu uyiNkosi, kube yinkazimulo kaNkulunkulu uYise. Nanjalo-ke, mina YHVH, Enginguye Omkhulu, ngiphinde ngiqinisekise ubuqiniso balawa Mazwi ukuze ukholo lwenu lunganyakaziseki.

Inkohliso enkulu isifikile futhi abaningi bayobanjwa kuyo, nabaningi bayowela emalangabini, bonke labo abamagama abo angalotshwanga eNcwadini YeWundlu Yokuphila kusukela ekuqaleni. Ngoba mina, El Shaddai, nginguyena owazi isiqalo nesiphetho, futhi nakuba nginika wonke umphefumulo ithuba lokuphila impilo yokulunga phambi kwami, ngiyazi ukuthi obani abayokhetha ukugcwalisa izono zabo ngokugcwele kulabo abayoza kuKristu ukuze badalwe kabusha ekulungeni, futhi ukwahlulela kwami kuphelele ekulungeni kwami.

Kodwa nina bakwa-Israyeli, aninakho ukulunga ngoba niyamala uNkulunkulu Ongunaphakade Wokulunga, ngoba niyamala iNdodana yami ezelwe yodwa engayithumela ukuba ibe yintethelelo yezono njengewundlu likaNkulunkulu, ngakho-ke izono zenu zisahleli. Ngakho-ke ngiyaniyeluleka, nina bakwa-Israyeli, lalelani aMazwi aLowo Onguye Omkhulu kulesi sikhathi sokugcina futhi namukele umhlatshelo wami!

Yebo, Israyeli, nganilungisela umhlatshelo ophelele ngoba u-Adamu wonakala esephelele, futhi ukuphila kwenyama kusegazini. Ngakho-ke isono sidluliswa ngegazi elingukuphila kwenyama kusuka kuYise kuya enzalweni yakhe, noma ngabe ingowesilisa noma owesifazane, bazuza ukonakala ngoba u-Adamu wonakele ingunaphakade. Emuseni nasekuthandeni kwami okuphakade, ngalungiselela lokho okungenakwenzeka kunoma yisiphi esinye isidalwa: ngadala futhi nganikela ngeNdodana yami ezelwe yodwa, uMose ayala ukuba niyilalele, ngenxa yezono zabo bonke abantu, noma ngabe bangamaJuda noma abezizwe, noma ngabe bagqilaziwe noma bakhululekile.

Nokho kunjalo, Israyeli, ukuthi izitha zenu zifisa ukubhujiswa kwenu ngoba zifisa ukuba nenkathi yazo yegolide futhi, inkathi yegolide abangeke baphinde bayizuze, ngoba esikhundleni senkathi yabo yegolide yangaphambi kukazamcolo, egcwele isono ngamabomu, lapho kwakuzulazula khona zonke izinhlobo zezinengiso, bayothola ukwahlulelwa okunzima nesijeziso njengoba umbuso wabo ubhujiswa ngokuphelele. Futhi kuyokwenzeka maduze ukuba isilo nomprofethi wamanga baphonswe bephila echibini elivuthayo lomlilo nesibabule, futhi uSathane aboshwe iminyaka eyinkulungwane ekuhlushweni nasek wesabeni okukhulu ifa lakhe elizayo emalangabini aphakade esijeziso lapho impethu yabo ingafi.

Futhi udrako uboshelwe emhlabeni ukuze angabe esangena emazulwini kuze kube phakade, kodwa ugcinwe ngaphansi komkhathi eboshiwe emhlabeni ukuze ahlulelwe njengoba ayefisa, futhi udrako unolaka olukhulu ekunqotshweni kwakhe, ngoba unqotshwe ngokuphelele ezandleni zikaYeshuwa HaMashiach, oyimbewu ethenjisiwe kanina wabo bonke abaphilayo, ngoba ngathembisa kanjalo ku-Adamu no-Eva othandweni lwami ngabo. Ngakho-ke uvunyelwe kuphela ukufeza izinhloso zami zaphakade ngaphambi kokuba athunyelwe emalangabini ingunaphakade, futhi ngizobeka uphawu, futhi ngeke baphinde bakhunjulwe noma baphathwe ngoba linjalo ikusasa lenu lesithembiso lapho nikhululwa khona kubacindezeli benu kuze kube phakade, futhi niyophila ngokuthula okuphakade nokuphelele uma nje niguqa ngamadolo kuMesiya wenu weqiniso, uYeshuwa HaMashiach, ngoba ayikho enye indlela!

Ngakho-ke manje ngizoninika ukuqonda izinto esezizokwenzeka, futhi ngokuqinisekile ugibe olubekwe ngumthiyi luzokwembulwa, ugibe olubekwe ngudrako ofuna ukubamba amajuba ami ayigugu, abantu bami abakhethekile engizibekele bona. Futhi yimina owenza ijuba likaNowa labuya neqabunga lomnqumo emlonyeni walo, futhi ngendlela efanayo, yimina engithumele isivumelwano sami sokuthula saphakade ebusweni bukaJesu Kristu, uMesiya wenu ovusiwe, nina bakwa-Israyeli!

Ngakho-ke, qondani izikhathi eniphila kuzo. ISinagoge LikaSathane lihamba ngezibonakaliso nezimangaliso eziningi zamanga, futhi ngobuqili bayalwa. Benza amasiko abo obala, afihliwe kancane njengokuzijabulisa nezindaba nemibuthano yezombangazwe. Sebeqedile inqubo yabo yokukhetha, lapho u-Esawu esezikhethele iwundlu lakhe phakathi kweSinagoge LikaSathane ngenkohliso yabo yokuhlambalaza abafuna ukubamba ngayo. Kodwa isilo sasemhlabeni akusilo iwundlu likaNkulunkulu abalihleka usulu ngobuwula nangokuhlambalaza, kodwa umprofethi wabo wamanga abazikhethele yena manje, onezimpondo ezimbili njengewundlu, kodwa okhuluma njengodrako.

Ngakho ngenza iNdodana yami, uJesu Kristu Olungileyo, yembula encwadini ethi *Okukhethayo Kungokwakho* (fundisisa ukuze ufunde) lapho uBaraba ethola isilonda ekhanda sengathi wayesezofa, futhi leso senzakalo sasinencazelo eminingi angayembulanga ngaleso sikhathi, kodwa kwakukulo mhlangano, umkhosi wakhe wokukhetha lapho bethi khona, uYahweh ngokwakhe wambhola imbobo endlebeni wamenza inceku yami yaphezulu. Ngakho-ke, ngifunga ngegama lami, YHVH Enginguye Omkhulu, ukuthi angimenzanga inceku yami eboshelweyo, futhi angimnikanga omunye uMesiya ngaphandle kweNdodana yami ezelwe yodwa, uYeshuwa HaMashiach, okunguye kuphela iwundlu likaNkulunkulu engiyoke ngilithumele. Ngakho-ke umzamo wokumbulala wasetshenziswa njengesinye sezibonakaliso nezimangaliso eziningi zamanga lapho lo mzenzisi enza sengathi. Futhi lo mzenzisi akasiye owemvelo yami noma umhlambi wami, futhi uyisitha sami.

Nokho, abaningi eBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo ababezenza sengathi uJesu Kristu uyiNkosi yabo manje sebeyishiyile iNkosi yabo engaba yiyo ngenxa kaBaraba, okhuluma njengodrako kodwa eyiwundlu elinezimpondo ezimbili. Kepha abaningi kulabo abenze uBaraba iNkosi yabo bayosala emlilweni wokucwengwa ngoba insindiso yabo yayingaphelele, ngenxa yokuthi abezi kuKristu ngokugcwele ngezinhliziyo ezigcwele ukholo nokuphenduka, kodwa beza nezinkanuko ezivezwa yivangeli lenkqubela phambili. Ngoba ISinagoge LikaSathane lonakalise imibhalo yabo engcwele ngemvume yabo, futhi manje sebengamaZiyoni. Nokho iningi lale miphefumulo efanayo liyonikezwa udumo lokufela ukholo ngenxa yensindiso yalo uma likhetha ukwamukela insindiso ngokubiza igama leNkosi yabo uJesu Kristu kuze kube sekupheleni, futhi ngiyobamukela phakade naphakade, nokuhlupheka kwabo ngeke kuqhathaniswe nenkazimulo ezokwembulwa kubo.

Ngakho, lo mashiya wamanga uyahamba futhi uyashumayela ekhuluma njengodrako futhi uyazenzisa futhi usho izinto eziningi ezinkulu nezihlambalazayo nangaphambi kokuba avuke ngokugcwele, futhi uyothi uyavuka. Qondani bantu bami ukuthi lo mprofethi wamanga ophinde abe yisilo sasemhlabeni esikhonza iSinagoge likaSathane manje usekhethiwe njengewundlu labo. Futhi kuyokwenzeka ukuthi ikhanda lakhe libonakale sengathi lilimele lafa, kodwa inxeba lakhe elibulalayo lapholiswa, futhi umhlaba wonke uyomangala ulandele isilo. Ngakho-ke lo mkhohlisi uyovuka.

Ekuvukeni kwakhe uyothi uzinikele njengewundlu likaNkulunkulu eletha ukuthula kwa-Israyeli njengejuba labo elibaphathela ihlamvu lomnqumo! Futhi hhayi u-Israyeli kuphela, kodwa umhlaba wonke futhi uyobonakala sengathi manje usebuyile ukuzoletha ukuthula, kodwa izindlela zokuthula akazazi futhi ekwahlulelweni kweBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo kuyoqubuka impi enkulu. Ngoba umoya wamanga wenduduzo ovela kudrako usuzinze emhlabeni, futhi mina ngize ngathumela ukukhohlisa okunamandla ukuze bonke labo abathokoza ngokungalungi bakholwe amanga ukuze bonke abathokoza ngokungalungi balahlwe. Ngoba lapho bethi ukuthula nokulondeka, khona-ke ukubhujiswa okuzumayo futhi ngeke baphunyuke.

Futhi abaningi eBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo, ngisho nalabo abakholwa amanga akhe futhi balandele amazwi akhe amahle nezinkulumo ezimnandi, bayathunjwa futhi babhajwe ukuthula kwamanga lapho becabanga ukuthi izimpilo zabo ezimnandi nezigcwele isono zizoqhubeka lapho namuhla kunjengayizolo futhi nakusasa kuyoba njenganamuhla, futhi abaningi bayokhamuluka, balile, futhi bakhale ngokuthi ithemba labo manje selithathiwe kubo, futhi bayolwa nabantu bakubo baze bafe futhi ngaso sonke leso sikhathi ukuhlasela kwabo okuzumayo kuyokhululwa ngokugcwele, ngisho nalapho umvimbeli wami esuswa emhlabeni futhi iBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo liyowela esahlulelweni esiphindwe kabili ngehora elilodwa njengoba kwehlela izinhlekelele eziningi.

Futhi iSinagoge likaSathane liyoba nempi yalo abaqothule ngayo abangenacala enkohlisweni yabo ukuze bashoshozele futhi labo abakhonza amabhokisi abo amnyama bayozikhandla ekubulaleni, ngisho nokubulala okulethelwe ngenhloso ngenkohliso enkulu futhi bayovuka emlotheni. Futhi abawileyo bayoziveza ngokugcwele benikeza isintu usizo olubonakala lunomusa kodwa izindlela zabo nobuchwepheshe babo kuhloselwe ukubamba bonke abangabathola efeni labo emalangabini esijeziso, abangeke baphunyuke kuwo futhi lapho bayokwamukela izikhathi eziningi ngaphezulu ngaso SONKE isono abasenzileyo futhi ubulungiswa buyokwenziwa. Kodwa yimuphi umthiyi obeka ugibe emehlweni enyoni?

Ngakho-ke ngininika ukuqonda, ukuthi isahlulelo seBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo sesilungisiwe futhi bami belindile. Kodwa kuqala leli wundlu lamanga elinezimpondo ezimbili liyokwenza sengathi lilungile kanti empeleni lingumalusi onukayo, olunye lwezimpondo ezimbili zokubusa, ngisho nophondo oluncane lukaDaniyeli. Futhi liyobonakala sengathi lizame ukusindisa iBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo ngokususa inkohlakalo yalo kodwa eqinisweni lingumkhohlisi omkhulu owabamba abaningi abayobasusa manje futhi abasole ngenkohlakalo yakhe aphoqe ngayo abaningi ukuba bazithobe. Futhi manje konke okungahambisani nezinhloso zabo, noma abangafakaza mathupha ngobubi babo kuyosuswa ngezandla zalo malusi onukayo futhi kusolwe ngezono abasazenza futhi abaholela abanye kuzo, futhi ngeke baphunyuke.

Futhi, qondani nokuthi abaningi abayizinhlangothi emacaleni abo futhi abangukhula olunjengalaba belusi abanukayo bayongena ezindaweni zabo zokucasha futhi bacashe enhliziyweni yomhlaba olakeni oluzayo futhi bayobhubhisa bonke ubufakazi bezimpilo ababeziphila eBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo ngenkohliso enkulu, futhi ngeke baluphunyuke ulaka oluzayo! Nokho, kunjalo-ke ukuthi izono zabo ziyodalulwa ngokuzenzisa yilo mprofethi wamanga futhi uyobonakala elungile futhi eqotho, ngisho nomuntu othunywe nguNkulunkulu ukuba asindise u-Israyeli ngakho-ke ugibe selulungele ukudubula. Nokho, zonke izenzo zabo ngizibhalile ezincwadini emazulwini, futhi mina mathupha ngikhumbula yonke imininingwane futhi ngiyazi wonke amacala abo futhi ngiyobadalula futhi bayokhokha phakade naphakade ngezono zabo ezinyanyekayo.

Ngakho-ke isilo sasolwandle sizilungiselela ukuvuka njengeMahdi yabo futhi isilo sasemhlabeni sizilungiselela ukuvuka njengewundlu lamanga ngoba yiliphi iwundlu elinezimpondo ezimbili futhi elikhuluma njengodrako. Nokho u-Esawu uzilungiselele ukumemukela kuye ezweni afuna ukuleba futhi bayosishulwa ngokuphelele ngesandla esinamandla sikaYeshuwa HaMashiya engimnike amandla, ngisho nokwabelana ngesihlalo sami sobukhosi neNdodana yami ethandekayo futhi iyochoboza ngokuphelele ikhanda lenyoka futhi zonke izitha zayo ziyokwenziwa isenabelo sezinyawo zayo ngoba abawileyo yibo abenqaba ukuguqa ngamadolo ngomyalo wami ekuhlubukeni kwabo futhi yibo abahlasela indalo yami ngobuqili nobubi futhi bayokhokha kuze kube phakade.

Ngakho-ke ngifunga ngegama lami futhi ngithi, mina YHVH uMINA OMKHULU NGIKHONA, ngiyafunga ukuthi okuwukuphela kukaMesiya engake ngamthuma kwa-Israyeli futhi engiyoke ngimthume ngempela yindodana yami ezelwe yodwa, uYeshuwa HaMashiya okhokho benu bamala nani enimalayo ngenxa yamanga abo enawazuza ezinhliziyweni zabo ezilukhuni adluliselwa phansi kwiTalmud yenu ehlambalazayo. Ngakho-ke ngiza kini othandweni lwami ngani O' Israyeli ukuze nginidalulele ngokugcwele ugibe enilubekelwe ngu-Esawu osafuna ukubhujiswa kukaJakobe.

Isilo sasemhlabeni sinihangile njengomprofethi wenu wamanga futhi siyovuka size kini njengewundlu lenu lomhlatshelo elizinikele ngenxa ka-Israyeli futhi siyokwenza sengathi linguMoshiya wenu weqiniso. Kanti empeleni singcoliswe ngu-Esawu futhi akukho Moshiya oyoke avele kwaDani. Impela, sizele ukubhujiswa kukaJakobe kodwa ugibe elulubekayo luyobamba labo abalulandelayo ngokuzithandela ngakho-ke bayothathwa phakade ngamanga alo futhi bayokwamukela uphawu lwalo eliyofuna ukuliphoqa emhlabeni ukuze libe yisigqila futhi libhujiswe.

Ngakho ngithi kini, O' Israyeli, guqani ngamadolo nilalele uMesiya wenu weqiniso owafika kini njengewundlu likaNkulunkulu, okhokho benu bamala ngenxa ka-Esawu. Guqani ngamadolo eNkosini yenu yamakhosi kanye neNkosi yamakhosi futhi namukele insindiso yami ukuze nginikhulule esiqalekisweni somthetho ngoba akekho umuntu olungileyo oza kimi ngoMose, ngaphandle komuntu uKristu Jesu engimshintshe phakade, futhi ngokwenyama akasaziwa. Ngoba, ngimkhazimulisile ngendlela efanayo noFaro amkhazimulisa ngayo uJosefa futhi wabelana naye ngesihlalo sakhe sobukhosi, ngaphandle kokuthi esihlalweni sobukhosi wayemkhulu kunoJosefa futhi wagcina igunya lakhe.

Ngoba:

MINA nginguye okhona kusukela phakade kuze kube phakade futhi akekho omkhulu kunoMINA OMKHULU NGIKHONA!

MINA nginguye othumele la maZwi kubantu bami abathandekayo ukuze ngize kini ngisuse ubumpumputhe benu obuyingxenye lapho izinhliziyo zenu ziphendukela ngokugcwele kuMesiya wenu! Ngisho noMesiya wenu weqiniso, uKristu Jesu iNkosi evusiweyo nekhazinyulisiweyo!

MINA nginguNkulunkulu waphakade Wokulunga ongapheli amandla, futhi ngiyabuza, kukhona yini okunzima kakhulu ukuba isandla sami esinamandla sikwenze?
