Izahlulelo Zesimemezelo
Nhlangulana 16, 2025

Qaphelani! Isifinyezo Sezahlulelo Zesimemezelo NgeBhabhiloni Elifihlekile Ngaphambi Kokuba Ukwahlulela Kwehle Ngokugcwele

Izahlulelo ezilandelayo zivela Encwadini Encane YesAmbulo Isahluko 10, okuyiyo futhi iVangeli Laphakade. Ukwahlulelwa kweBhabhiloni Elifihlekile sekumenyezelwe iminyaka eminingi namajubili. Okulandelayo yizahlulelo zesimemezelo ezithathwe Encwadini Encane ezizokwenzeka ngokuqinisekile ngokushesha nangokungalindelekile manje. Kulesi sikhundla, ngisebenza njengesithunywa sokwahlulela esithunyelwe ukumemezela izahlulelo ezivela esihlalweni sobukhosi sikaYahweh, ezinikezwe ebusweni bukaJesu Kristu, engimkhonzayo.

Makwaziwe, ukuthi namuhla mhlaka 16 kuNhlangulana, 2025 ngekhalenda likaGregory (ikhalenda elifanayo elisetshenziswa yiBhabhiloni Elifihlekile) ukuthi iNkosi yami Enyukile uKristu Jesu, uYeshua HaMashiach uqobo, uyangiyala ukuba ngifunde lezi zahlulelo ngeBhabhiloni Elifihlekile, yebo, leli zwe laseMelika, ukuze noma ubani aphenduke kusenomzuzwana wesikhathi esifushane, nokuba ukwahlulela kumenyezelwe ngaphambi kokuba umlilo wehle. Selah!

Lesi simemezelo asiphelele ngoba lezi akuzona izahlulelo ezintsha kodwa sezikhululwe ngokugcwele kusukela mhlaka 2 kuNdasa, 2024, kuya kumhlaka 20 kuNdasa, 2025. Iningi lalezi zivela kuYeshua HaMashiach, kunesigaba esisodwa esivela kuYahweh, uMINA NGINGuye Omkhulu. Manje ngizoqala ukumemezela.

1Bhekani! Ukwahlulela Kuyeza Futhi Izikhathi Sezikhona Manje!Nhlangulana 4, 2024 (Izwi: Yeshua HaMashiach)

Lapho ukuhlangana kwenzeka, umvimbeli uyasuswa, futhi incithakalo enkulu iyogubuzela umhlaba, ngokuzumayo nangokuphazima kweso. Ngokuba lapho umvimbeli esuswa, akukho okuyosala ukuvimbela ububi emhlabeni kanye nezinkanuko nezifiso ezimbi zesintu. Impi enkulu iyogqashuka emhlabeni wonke, futhi umfowabo uyophinde avukele ukubulala umfowabo, futhi incithakalo yabo iyoba nkulu. Izizwe eziningi ziyowa njengeBhabhiloni Lemfihlakalo, yebo, leso sifebe esikhulu esihleli phezu kwamanzi amaningi, siyokwenziwa incithakalo ngehora elilodwa. Lowo muzi omkhulu, yebo, leso sizwe seMelika, siyobhujiswa ngokuphelele; ngokuba sizuzele isabelo esiphindwe kabili sokwahlulelwa, futhi siyakufanelekela.

Ngokuba saphila ngokuzitika esonweni esikhulu; sihleli ngokuzidla okukhulu sicabanga ukuthi asisoze sawa futhi izinto ziyoqhubeka, izolo njenganamhlanje, nakusasa kuya ngokufana nokungaguquki. Futhi isifebe esikhulu sonakalisile umhlaba ngenkomishi yegolide esesandleni saso, egcwele amanyala nokungcola kobufebe baso. Ngakho-ke ukwahlulelwa kwaso kuyofika ngokuzumayo. Umlilo omkhulu uyogubuzela imizi yaso lapho imicibisholo iwa; ukuzamazama okukhulu kuyokwenzeka, futhi leso sizwe siyohlukana phakathi nendawo. Izingxenye zaso ezisogwini ziyokhukhulwa kakhulu, futhi asisoze saphinde savuka, ngokuba izono zaso zinkulu nokwahlulelwa kwaso kukhulu.

2Zwanini Amazwi Ami Futhi Niqonde Izikhathi Eniphila Kuzo!Nhlaba 23, 2024 (Izwi: Yeshua HaMashiach)

Zwanini amazwi ami, bantu bami, nina nonke enginibizayo, futhi niqonde izikhathi eniphila kuzo. Ngokuba izwe enilaziyo manje liyophela, futhi esikhundleni salo kuyosala amazwe amaningi ayizincithakalo, yebo, umlotha namanxiwa eBhabhiloni Lemfihlakalo, ngokuba impi isifikile. Ngokuba lapho ngihlwitha uMlobokazi wami ngimusa emazulwini, inhlekelele enkulu iyogwinya iBhabhiloni Lemfihlakalo, futhi liyobhujiswa ngehora elilodwa, futhi ngehora elilodwa liyobhujiswa, futhi akukho okungashintsha isiphetho salo, ngokuba uYahweh umemezele kanjalo. Ngokuba wena, Bhabhiloni Elifihlekile, wenqabile izwi lami futhi wangibeka eceleni, mina Jesu Kristu Olungileyo, ngenxa yenjabulo yesono. Ngokuba ngisho nezixwayiso ezinkulu ezinikezwe phezu kwesizwe sakho emazulwini zavele zaba yintokozo ezinhliziyweni zenu ezingakholwa nasemehlweni enu anenkanuko, futhi kini kwaba olunye usuku njengoba naniphila ngokuzitika ezonweni zenu eziniholela ekubhujisweni kwenu. Kwakufanele niphenduke! Ngokuba seniphenduke isizwe sabakhonzi bezithombe, isizwe sabahlaseli, isizwe esidla amanga futhi esithanda izinkohliso zaso, isizwe esigcwalise umhlaba ngamanyala enu; futhi izizwe ziphuza enkomishini yenu yegolide egcwele amanyala nokungcola kobufebe benu.

Futhi-ke lokho kuphela kuyofika ngokugcwele lapho ngiqoqa abathembekileyo beBandla lami kimi emafwini nalapho ukuhamba kwabo kuzamazamisa umhlaba wonke. Ngokweqiniso kuyozamazamisa umhlaba, futhi ukuzamazama okukhulu kuyogwinya iBhabhiloni Elifihlekile, futhi liyodabuka phakathi nendawo lapho abangcwele bami besuka. Liyoshiswa ngokuphelele ngomlilo ovela esibhakabhakeni lapho imicibisholo iwa. Izingubo zalo ziyogutshuzelwa ngamanzi avela empumalanga nasentshonalanga! Ngokuba ingelosi enamandla iyothatha itshe elinjengelitshe elikhulu lokugaya, iliphonse olwandle, ithi, Kanjalo ngodlame lowo muzi omkhulu iBhabhiloni uyophonswa phansi, futhi awusoze waphinde watholakala. Ngokuba kulotshiwe kanjalo futhi kuyokwenziwa kanjalo! Ngokuba kukhulu ukwahlulelwa kwalo, futhi ukwahlulela sekuqalile endlini kaNkulunkulu. Amabandla alo ahlubukile ayobhujiswa, ngokuba ngiwahlulele onke angenami, yebo, onke amabandla omgunyathi akhuluma amanga amakhulu ngobuzenzisi. Bhabhiloni Lemfihlakalo, oh, ukube wawuphendukele kimi, Jesu Kristu, washiya izindlela zakho ezimbi, ngabe ngikusindisile, futhi angithokozi mina noYahweh ngezahlulelo esezizokwehliswa.

Oh, Bhabhiloni Elifihlekile, amabhuloho akho ayowa; imigwaqo yakho iyogumbuqelwa, iphuke, ingabe isahambeka; imigudu yakho yokuhlinzeka isivele idilika futhi maduze izoba yincithakalo. Awusoze walethulelwa miphako, ngokuba akekho oyokusiza, futhi uyoba wedwa ngehora lakho lokuchithwa, oh, sifebe esikhulu. Ngeke ube nenjabulo, futhi labo abasindayo bayozithola belwela nje ukuphuza amanzi. Indlala iyogubuzela izwe. Futhi isifo esiwumshayabhuqe esikhulu siyokwehlela, ngisho nezilwane ziyohlasela, ngokuba izikhathi sezifikile.

Amabutho abomvu uhulumeni wakho ongathembekile owawavumela ukuba angene emingceleni yakho ayobulala wonke amadoda, abesifazane, nezingane abangahambisani nezinjongo zawo. Ngokuba bayobulala futhi bagqilaze bonke ababatholayo. Bangamabutho esilo aqoqwe emagumbini amane omhlaba ngale njongo, futhi isilo esifihliwe nomprofethi wamanga ofihliweyo bawahlangene.

Abanalo uzwelo, abanasihe, abanamusa, abanako ukuzisola, futhi bazonda kakhulu abakhileyo beBhabhiloni Lemfihlakalo ngenxa yezono ezimbi abaholi benu abazenza emazweni abo. Futhi sonke leso sikhathi abaningi benu babegqugquzela izimpi zenu ezingenabulungiswa njengoba nanibuka ngokujabula komabonakude benu, njengoba nanithokoza kakhulu ezonweni zabaholi benu. Ninikeze imvume yenu kubaholi benu abazono zabo nazivumela ngokuvota, ngakho-ke niyohlanganyela ekwahlulelweni kwabo, ngokuba nizenzile ukuba nifanelekele isabelo esiphindwe kabili sokwahlulelwa, futhi siyonifica ngokuzumayo. Futhi-ke uMlobokazi wami uyovela esihlalweni sami sokwahlulela, futhi abanye bayolahlekelwa, abanye bayothwala umvuzo omkhulu, futhi bonke bayokwamukela ngokugcwele isibambiso asebevele besamukele: Ngokuba ngiyobanika imizimba emisha, engenasono, engasoze yonakala!

3Ebandleni Lami, Umzimba Wami, Yebo, UMlobokazi Wami: Zwanini Amazwi E Nkosi Yenu!Nhlaba 13, 2024 (Izwi: Yeshua HaMashiach)

Bayovela esiteji somhlaba ngamandla amakhulu, futhi umfelandawonye wamakhosi ayishumi uyobusa. Ngisho namanje, umfelandawonye usuvele uhlele amabutho awo ngasese ukuze uhlasele iBhabhiloni Elifihlekile ngasese. Bazilungiselele ngasese, futhi ngokuqinisekile bahlele ukuhlasela, futhi sebelihlaselile iBhabhiloni Elifihlekile ngokusobala ngokwesahlulelo esimenyezelwe nguNkulunkulu esizolehlela, lapho lingasoze liphinde livuke. Ngokuba ububi balo bufinyelele phambi koPhezukonke, nezono zalo phambi kwesihlalo sobukhosi esikhulu sasezulwini, yebo, phambi kukaNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade, ngokuba akekho omkhulu kunaye; futhi sekuphinyiselwe isahlulelo ngalo.

Ngiletha ulaka olukhulu eBhabhiloni Elifihlekile. Isikhathi siyinto eningasenayo. Anisavunyelwe ukuvilapha, kungenjalo niyozithola osizini lukaJakobe, lapho ngangiyokulungisela indawo etafuleni lami ngikwenza umlobokazi wami, kodwa ungethembeki usezingalweni zomunye, uSathane uqobo! Ngakho-ke yizani kimi ukuze nginibuyisele emvuzweni wenu. Akwanele ukucasuka niphume; kumele niphenduke kulobu bubi niguqule izindlela zenu nivume lezi zono kimi, futhi ngiyonihlanza ngegazi lami elihlawulayo ngisule amabala kini ngobumsulwa bami obuphelele, ngokuba ngihlanzekile futhi izingubo zami zimhlophe njengeqhwa elisha. Buyelani kimi futhi niyoba nami phakade; phumani nize ezingalweni zami ezivulekile, ngokuba ngilapha ukunitshela ukuthi aninaso isikhathi sokumosha.

4Maye Kini, Belusi Abahlubukileyo!Nhlaba 4, 2024 (Izwi: Yeshua HaMashiach)

Ngiletha ulaka olukhulu eBhabhiloni Elifihlekile. Isikhathi siyinto eningasenayo. Anisavunyelwe ukuvilapha, kungenjalo niyozithola osizini lukaJakobe, lapho ngangiyokulungisela indawo etafuleni lami ngikwenza umlobokazi wami, kodwa ungethembeki usezingalweni zomunye, uSathane uqobo! 5Abalungileyo Abayikulidla Ifa Lombuso KaNkulunkulu! (Ukuduma kwesihlanu nesithupha)Mbasa 19, 2024 (Izwi: Yeshua HaMashiach, Isigijimi esikabili)

Ake ngivule amehlo enu, nina eningafuni ukubona futhi niyizimpumputhe, nina enihleli ekungcoleni nasebumnyameni, nizingiqinga odakeni. Anikwazi ukunikeza imvume yenu ebubini bese nicabanga ukuthi nikhonza mina, ngokuba mina ngamelana nobubi nesilingo kwaze kwaba yilaphoiconsi lokugcina legazi liphuma kimi laya emhlabathini obomvu, ngokuba ngingu-Adamu wokugcina futhi ngenziwe umoya ophilayo. Abaningi benu baphuphuthekiswe ngamazwi amahle nezinkulumo ezinhle futhi banikeza imvume yabo egcwele ezigebengwini nakubantu ababi abaxhaphaza izingane. Bahlukumeza izingane, bazihlukumeze ngenxa yezinkanuko zabo ezimbi: Ngoba lufika kanjalo ulaka lukaNkulunkulu eBhabhiloni Lemfihlakalo, kodwa abaningi benu bayaqhubeka nokunikeza imvume yabo kulezi zilwane zasendle ezibekelwe imililo yaphakade yesihogo engasoze yacinywa. Phumani kulo, bantu bami, kungenjalo niyohlanganyela ezinhluphekweni zalo nasekwahlulelweni okwesabekayo okuzayo phezu kwalo; futhi nginixwayisile. Ngokuba uKristu uhlangene ngani noBeliyali? Uma nihlanganyela emangeni alo, niyohlanganyela ezinhluphekweni zalo; ngakho-ke zahlukaniseni ngokuphelele ngezwi nangesenzo, futhi ningavumelani nalo nakancane. Ningaphili impilo engenandaba, enokuyekelela; ngokuba ngiyobajezisa bonke abenza kanjalo. Nicabanga ukuthi ngenxa yokuthi ninokuphila okuphakade ngeke nibhekane nokulahlekelwa okukhulu nemiphumela? Ngizokwaphuca imivuzo yenu ngiyinike abanye, futhi niyoba abancane kunabo bonke embusweni wezulu uma niqhubeka, ngoba usho kanje uYeshua HaMashiach, uMyeni, kuMlobokazi wami!

6Mlindi, Vuka!Mbasa 5, 2024 (Izwi: Yeshua HaMashiach)

Ngiyeza, futhi angiyikuthula, futhi intukuthelo yami iyashisa ngeBhabhiloni Elifihlekile, yebo, iBhabhiloni Elifihlekile elidayisa abancane njengempahla ezandleni zabantu ababi, abayomiswa. Kungcono ukube abazalwanga kunokuba bazalwe benze izono ezingenamthetho nezimbi ezinganeni. Kungcono ukuba itshe lokusila lalengiswe entanyeni yabo, futhi baphonswe olwandle; kodwa babekelwe umlilo waphakade, futhi ubulungiswa buyokwenziwa maduze, njengoba ulaka lweWundlu seluzogubuzela umhlaba wonke futhi usizi lukaJakobe lukhululwe lapho umvimbeli esuswa. Kodwa wena, oh, Bhabhiloni Elifihlekile elikhulu, ungcolisile uthingo lukaBaba lwemibala eminingi alunikela kuNowa nakubo bonke abantu njengesithembiso sokuthi akayikuphinde abhubhise umhlaba wonke ngamanzi, futhi uyosigcina izwi lakhe. Kodwa wena, oh, Bhabhiloni Lemfihlakalo, uyochithwa ngehora elilodwa.

Umlilo omkhulu uyokwehlela; ukuzamazama okukhulu kuyokugudluza; imibhoshongo yakho iyowa; ingqalasizinda yakho iyonyamalala ibe yincithakalo, futhi amagagasi amakhulu ayogubuzela izingxenye zakho ezisogwini, futhi ngiyokuvuza ngokwemisebenzi yakho, futhi kukhulu ukwahlulela okumelene nawe. Ngokuba wake waba yisibani sethemba nokukhanya emhlabeni isikhashana, kodwa ukhethe ukuhaha, ubugovu, nenkohlakalo njengabalingani bakho, oh, sifebe esikhulu! Ngubani oyokusindisa kimi nasentukuthelweni yami nolaka lwami olusondelayo olushisayo phezu kwakho? Umi uboshiwe ngamaketanga, ulindele ukwahlulelwa kwakho kokugcina, okuyobonakala emhlabeni wonke, futhi ukwesaba okukhulu kuyokwehlela abaningi.

Bhekani! Ziningi izizwe uBaba azigumbuqelile ekwahluleleni, ngokuba uphakamisa esinye ehlise esinye, ngokuba uYahweh unobukhosi futhi akekho omkhulu kunoNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade omela futhi asindise abakhe emlilweni obekelwe ababi. Bhekani! Izizwe ezakha imibuso emikhulu zisakhona, kodwa ngendlela encishisiwe, kodwa ziyaqhubeka. Kodwa wena, oh, sifebe esikhulu, uzidayisile ngezithandwa eziningi ngenxa yokuhaha okubi nenjabulo yesono. Ulahlekisile abantu bami kimi emhlabeni wonke ngokungcola okunikeze izizwe ukuba zikudle zikuphuze, futhi uyophonswa phansi ngokuphelele, futhi awusoze waphinde waba khona. Futhi emlotheni wakho ngeke kuvuke isizwe esikhulu, kodwa insali iyosindiswa. Isandla sesitha sisentanyeni yakho, futhi ngilethe izinsalela emingceleni yakho. Ziyophanga, zithathe impahla, zithathe okusele.

Ubani oyokusiza? Ngokuba umphikile umakhelwane wakho, futhi akukho ukwesaba iNkosi ezinhliziyweni zenu. Uthe, angisiye umfelokazi, kodwa ungumfelokazi, uyimpumputhe, futhi uyisithulu phambi kwami. Alukho usindiso kuwe ngenxa yobufebe bakho bokuphinga obenzile nezwe; ngakho-ke, ngiyokuletha encithakalweni ephelele. Izehlakalo ezizayo ziyokwenzeka ngokushesha okukhulu; bese uzamcolo omkhulu uvala izingubo zakho. Umlilo uyokushisa ngokuphelele uhlanze abenzi bokubi ezweni, futhi abathunjwa bami bayokhululwa, futhi akukho okungamisa lokho osekuzokwenzeka. NginguNkosi uJesu Kristu, futhi nginitshela ngaphambi kokuba kwenzeke, futhi ngimemezela okuzokwenzeka sengathi sekuvele kwenzekile, ngokuba ngokuqinisekile lawa mazwi anjalo, futhi akukho okungawaguqula, njengoba iziphrofetho ziyogcwaliseka. Yimani niqine, bantu bami, njengoba ngizohlwitha umlobokazi wami; ngokuba nginezinhlelo nezinjongo ngaye embusweni wami ozayo.

7Angikunilungisisanga yini?Epreli 1, 2024 (Izwi: uYeshua HaMashiach)

Labo abangazehlukanisi nalo bayohlanganyela ekwahlulelweni kwalo, ngakho-ke kungani nisakhetha ukufa kunokuphila engininike khona, okwakhokhelwa ngegazi lami elingenasono? Impi isizofika ngokugcwele ezweni nasesizweni senu, futhi nakuba isivele iqhubeka — yebo, impi engamenyezelwanga abayilwa nani — maduze izogqashuka emhlabeni wonke njengoba izimpawu zikhululwa. Impi enkulu nokushushiswa okukhulu kuyofika emhlabeni njengoba bezama ukubhubhisa noma yini kaNkulunkulu noKristu wakhe lapho bezogcina bememezele obala impi engcwele kini. Bayothumba abaningi, futhi abaningi bayothathwa, futhi labo abayosindiswa bayoba nokukhetha okukodwa kuphela: ukukhuthazela kuze kube sekupheleni futhi bangakuthandi ukuphila kwabo kuze kube sekupheleni futhi babize igama lami kuze kube sekupheleni.

Abaholi benu banikhaphelile futhi bayonikela ngabaningi komubi ezimpini ezizovutha maduze. Isizwe senu siyohlaselwa ngaphakathi nangaphandle. Amabutho abomvu asevele elapha, futhi alindele imiyalelo yawo; ngoba ahlela ukuhlasela ngokuzumayo, futhi abaningi bayothathwa. Amabutho omubi ayothatha konke ezweni ukuze kube okwawo nakulabo abangangamukeli. Bayokweba izwe lenu, imizi yenu, inzalo yenu; futhi abaningi bayolahlekelwa ukuphila kwabo ekungakholweni. Impi iyeza ivela ngaphandle njengoba izizwe eziningi zihlangene melawanayo; futhi zinemvume yabaphathi benu, futhi abaphathi benu basebenzela ukufa kwenu. Bafuna izwe labo, izwe elinikezelwe kuSathane lapho bengalandela ngokugcwele ububi obuhlala kubo, futhi akukho okuyovimbela izifiso zabo ezimbi, njengoba umvimbeli eyobe esehambile.

Bayoshisa iBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo ngomlilo, futhi noma ngubani ongahlulwa ngamalangabi uyohlulwa yindlala; noma ngubani ongahlulwa yindlala uyohlulwa yisifo; noma ngubani ongahlulwa yinkemba uyokwethulwa esigxotsheni ngenxa yegama lami, futhi abaningi bayonqunywa amakhanda njengoba lisho iZwi lami Elingcwele. Futhi labo abafel' ukholo ngenxa yegama lami bayoba nomvuzo omkhulu, futhi ngiyosula zonke izinyembezi emehlweni abo. Yazini, bantabami, ukuthi zonke izibiliboco nenjabulo yesono abantu ababi abazazisa ngakho kuyoshwamalala, futhi usizi nokuhlupheka kuyothatha indawo yakho. Ubumnyama buyosibekela izwe, futhi kuyoba khona ukuswelakala kweZwi lami, futhi izwi lomkhwenyana nezwi lomakoti ngeke lisazwakala kulo.

8Nqabani Uphawu Lwesilo!Julayi 27, 2024 (Izwi: uYeshua HaMashiach)

Yizwani izwi lokuhlakanipha, niguqe ngamadolo, nizwe izwi eliphilayo likaYeshua HaMashiach, ngokuba ngiyiNgonyama yesiZwe sakwaJuda, futhi ngiyiNkosi yamakhosi noMbusi wababusi! U-El Shaddai, uNkulunkulu uSomandla, ungiphakamisile kakhulu wanginika igama eliphezu kwawo onke amagama. Ukuze ngeGama likaJesu onke amadolo aguqe, ezintweni ezisezulwini, nezintweni ezisemhlabeni, nezintweni ezingaphansi komhlaba: Futhi ukuze zonke izilimi zivume, ukuthi uJesu Kristu uyiNkosi, kube inkazimulo kaBaba weziKhanyiso, uYahweh, uNkulunkulu waphakade nowaphakade, ngokuba akekho onjengoBaba wethu!

Zwanini izwi leNkosi yenu niqonde izinto esezivele zenzeka nezinto ezizokwenzeka maduze, ngokuba bhekani yimina okhuluma ngezinto ezizayo sengathi sezenzekile ngokuba ngokuqinisekile iNkosi yenu enkulu, mina Kristu Jesu ophakeme, ngeke ngenze lutho, ngaphandle kokuba ngembule izimfihlo zami ezincekwini zami abaProfethi.
