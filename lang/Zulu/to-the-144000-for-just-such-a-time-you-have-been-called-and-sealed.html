Kwabayizi-144,000: Nibizelwe Futhi Nabekwa Uphawu Ngenxa Yesikhathi Esifana Nalesi Nje

Ngimeleni ningilandele isibonelo sami, ngoba athini amaHebheru mayelana nokuma kwami ngakuYahweh? Yena owathi ngenxa yenjabulo eyayibekwe phambi kwakhe, wakhuthazelela isiphambano, edelela ihlazo, futhi uhlezi ngakwesokunene sesihlalo sobukhosi sikaNkulunkulu. Ngakho-ke othandweni lwami ngoBaba, olungenakunqamuka, nasekulaleleni kwami okuvela othandweni, nasothandweni lwami ngesintu kanye nesifiso sami sokuzidela ukuze nginisindise, ngakhuthazelela isijeziso esesabekayo, ubuhlungu obukhulu, nokuhlupheka futhi ngakwenza ngokuzithandela, ngenjabulo enhliziyweni yami. Ngangazi ukuthi ngomhlatshelo wami nokufa kwami, abaningi bayokwenziwa abalungileyo nabaphilayo ngamandla okuvuka kwami ngoba uYahweh uqobo lwakhe wangivusa kwabafileyo, ngokuba akunakwenzeka ukuthi ukufa kungibambe. Futhi ngawona lawo mandla, nani ngokunjalo niyovuselwa ebuntshani bokuphila, ngoba ngiyikhokhile intengo ngokunikela ngegazi lami elingenasono njengenhlawulo yabo bonke abazokholwa egameni lami, futhi noma ngubani obiza igama leNkosi uyosindiswa, labo abangakuthandi ukuphila kwabo kuze kube sekupheleni!

Futhi manje ngiyanicela, nina bathembekileyo nabakhethiweyo bami, ngisho nezihlobo zami, ukuba ningimele futhi ningazihoxisi. Ngoba ngasho ngokusobala encwadini yami eya kumaHebheru: Manje olungileyo uzophila ngokukholwa: kodwa uma ekhona ohlehlayo, umphefumulo wami awuyikuthokoza ngaye.

Lokho makucace, nikuphinde nikufunde, futhi ningabuyeli emuva eTorah, ngoba iTorah igcinwa ngokukholwa kimi; ngakho-ke, olungileyo uzophila ngokukholwa. Nina nihlanganiswe nami, futhi yimina onilungisileyo ngemisebenzi yami yokuhlawulela nangegazi lami elingenasono.

Abaningi kakhulu kini badonsa kanzima ngomqondo wokuthi ngathatha indawo yenu ekulahlweni emthini ngenxa yezono engingazenzanga, futhi ngakwenza lokho ngokuzithandela nangenjabulo ukuze ngilethe insindiso kumuntu wemvelo owonakele, ukuze ngibahlawulele kuYahweh, ngisho uNkulunkulu wenu noYihlo wenu, ngisho uBaba wezibani okungekho kuye ukuguquguquka nasithunzi sokuphenduka. Ngokuba uYahweh akaguquki; unjalo izolo, namuhla, naphakade!

UnguNkulunkulu ofanayo okhokho benu abavumelana naye esivumelwaneni, ngisho naseNtabeni iSinayi lapho uMose amukela khona umthetho kuYahweh uqobo. Futhi nakuba umthetho ungcwele, nomyalo ungcwele, ulungile, futhi muhle, isintu asikwazanga neze ukuphila ngokwezindinganiso zawo ngoba umthetho wanikezwa ukuze ulahle isono enyameni, futhi nonke nizalwa nifile eziphambekweni nasezonweni ngoba isono sisenyameni yenu. ITorah ayinikezwanga abalungileyo kodwa abangalungile, ukuze isono sivele njengesono enyameni. ITorah yanikezwa njengomfundisi wenu ukuze inilethe kimi, ngisho uKristu Jesu, iNkosi yamakhosi ekhuphukile kanye neNkosi yamaNkosi!

Ngakho-ke ngazithwalela ngokuzithandela imiphumela yesono, ngokuba ngazalelwa lokho ukuze ngihlenge isintu esiqalekisweni somthetho. Ngokuba umthetho wawungaqalekisiwe; yebo, mawume umthetho futhi wonke umuntu makaqalekiswe oqalekisa umthetho kaMose, ngoba angizanga ukuzochitha umthetho, kodwa ukumisa umthetho futhi ngidlule ngisho nomthetho kaMose. Uthi ukuhlambalaza?

Ubufakazi bamaHebheru bumi njengomlamuleli phakathi kwethu: Ngokuba uma igazi lezinkunzi nelezimbuzi, nomlotha wesithokazi esisafufazwa kwabangcolileyo, lingcwelisa kuze kube sekuhlanzekeni kwenyama: Kakhulu kangakanani-ke igazi likaKristu, owathi ngoMoya ongunaphakade wazinikela engenabala kuNkulunkulu, liyakuhlanza unembeza wenu emisebenzini efileyo ukuze nikhonze uNkulunkulu ophilayo?

Njengoba nimthanda uMose (njengoba nami ngenza), nginiyala ukuba nilalele ngothando iziyalezo zikaMose; ngokuba wathi: UYahweh u-Elohim wenu uyakuphakamisela umPhrofethi phakathi kwenu, kubafowenu, onjengami; uyomlalela yena. Ngakho yizwani izwi likaYeshua HaMashiach! Nina nihlanziwe esonweni nakuzo zonke izono enizenzayo ngegazi lami elingenasono lokuhlawulela enganikela ngalo kuYahweh njengenhlawulo yokuphila kwenu, futhi senginihlenge ngisho nasesandleni sesitha. Ningoni! Futhi uma umuntu ona, vumani izono zenu kimi, ngokuba yimina onihlanza ngegazi lami lokuhlawulela.

Angizihlawulelanga izono zenu kanye nemiphumela yazo ngembuzi yokulahlela, futhi angizange nginikele ngewundlu lonyaka wokuqala; ngokuba ngiyilo iWundlu likaNkulunkulu lonyaka wokuqala, futhi yimina owazinikela ngaphandle kwebala nangaphandle kwesici, ngisho ongcwele, omsulwa, nonikezelwe kuYahweh uqobo (ngisho uBaba wami) ngenxa yezono zenu kanye kuphela, ngisho okwaphakade. Ngahlawulela izono zenu ngegazi lami elingenasono; ngakho-ke, akusekho mhlatshelo wesono. Futhi ukungamukeli mina, uYeshua HaMashiach, ngisho isivumelwano sokuthula esiphelele sikaYahweh esintwini, kuwukwenqaba uYahweh futhi uhlanganyele ekuhlubukeni kukaSathane, ngoba uSathane uhlubuka ehlelweni likaNkulunkulu kusukela ekuqaleni.

Ngoba ngatshela amaHebheru: Ngokuba uma sona ngenhloso emva kokuba sesamukele ulwazi lweqiniso, akusekho mhlatshelo wezono. Ngakho ukona ngamabomu ngokwenqaba igazi lami lokuhlawulela, futhi kanjalo wenqabe isivumelwano sokuthula esithunyelwe nguYahweh, kubi kakhulu, futhi akusekho mhlatshelo. Ngokuba kulotshiwe kumaHebheru: Lowo owedelela umthetho kaMose wafa ngaphandle kwesihe ngaphansi kofakazi ababili noma abathathu: Nicabanga ukuthi, uyofanelwa yisijeziso esingakanani esibi kakhulu, lowo onyathelé ngaphansi kwezinyawo iNdodana kaNkulunkulu, wabala igazi lesivumelwano, ahlanzwa ngalo, njengento engcolileyo, futhi wadelela uMoya womusa?

Ngakho-ke nginikhuthaza nonke ukuba namukele umhlatshelo wami futhi niqonde ukuthi ayikho eminye imihlatshelo edingekayo futhi munye kuphela uMesiya weqiniso, ngisho mina, uYeshua HaMashiach. Futhi kulotshiwe ngami: Kodwa lo muntu, emva kokuba enikele umhlatshelo owodwa ngenxa yezono kuze kube phakade, wahlala phansi ngakwesokunene sikaNkulunkulu; Kusukela lapho elindele kuze kube yilapho izitha zakhe zenziwa isenabelo sezinyawo zakhe. Ngokuba ngomnikelo owodwa uphelelisile labo abangcweliswayo kuze kube phakade.

Ngakho-ke qondani ukuthi nginihlanzile kuzo zonke izici eziphathelene nesono nemiphumela yaso. Nina ningcweliswe kimi; nilungiswe kimi; nenziwe ukulunga kukaNkulunkulu kimi; futhi nginihlawulele ngokuphelele kimi, ngisho mina, uYeshua HaMashiach, uYahweh amthumele njengoMesiya wenu. Futhi qondani ukuthi abasekho abaPristi abakhulu bamaLevi, nobuPristi buka-Aroni sebadlula, ngokuba babuyisithunzi sezinto ezizayo. Ngoba ngingumpristi wenu omkhulu kuze kube phakade ngokohlelo lukaMelkisedeki.

Ngoba umthetho awenzanga lutho oluphelele: kodwa wawuyisingeniso sethemba elingcono, esisondeza ngalo kuYahweh. Futhi futhi: UmPristi oMkhulu onjalo wasifanele thina ongcwele, ongenacala, ongenabala, ohlukanisiwe nezoni, nowenziwe waba mkhulu kunezulu. Futhi futhi: Ngokuba umthetho wenza abantu babe ngabapristi, abanobuthakathaka: kodwa izwi lesifungo elafika selokhu kwanomthetho, lenza indodana ibe ngumpristi, ophelele kuze kube phakade. Anifundanga yini ukuthi imibhalo ithini ngami? UYahweh wafunga futhi akayikuzisola: UngumPristi kuze kube phakade ngokohlelo lukaMelkisedeki.

Qondani ukuthi akukho okushodayo emhlatshelweni wami, futhi niphephile kimi, uKristu Jesu. ITorah igcinwa ngokukholwa kimi, uYeshua HaMashiach, futhi akunakwenzeka ukuba nephule noma yimuphi umthetho kaNkulunkulu ngokuhamba kimi, uKristu Jesu. Ngokuba nihamba emthethweni woMoya wokuphila, futhi nginikhululile emthethweni wesono nokufa. Ngakho-ke kholwani kimi, ngoba olungileyo uzophila ngokukholwa. Ngemisebenzi eminingi emikhulu enginibizele yona, futhi niyolekelela ukuletha insindiso kwabaningi. Likhulu ithemba nomvuzo enibekelwe wona emazulwini ngokuma kwenu nokuthembeka kwenu.

Ngokuba ngiyanithuma, nibekwe uphawu emabunzini enu ngophawu lukaYahweh uqobo, ezweni elizokunizonda ngokuphelele, futhi akumangalisi, ngoba izwe langizonda kuqala, futhi inceku ayinkulu kunenkosi yayo. Nina ningofakazi bami nabaprofethi bami nabaphulukisi bami nabahlinzeki bami. Ngiyanithuma ebumnyameni obukhulu kakhulu obuke basibekele indalo kaYahweh. Ngenxa yesikhathi esifana nalesi nibiziwe, nakhethwa, nanikwa uphawu, futhi lezi zikhathi ziyoba ezonakele kakhulu nezimbi kunazo zonke izwe elake lazibona, ngisho kakhulu kunezinsuku zikaNowa. Phakathi kwabo ngiyanithuma, futhi ngithi kini ningesabi! Ningesabi izinsongo zabo; ningesabi lokho umuntu angenzani kini; ningesabi ubuso babo; ningesabi imimoya ebanika amandla; ningesabi isilo nomprofethi wamanga; futhi ningesabi uSathane uqobo: Ngisho lowo drago omkhulu. Ngoba niyonyathela zonke izici zombuso kaSathane, futhi akukho lutho oluyonivimba uma nihlala nithembekile ezwini lami futhi ningivumele nginiqondise isinyathelo ngesinyathelo. Ngokuba ngeqiniso, niyizisebenzi kanye nami kulesi sikhathi sokuhlupheka kukaJakobe, futhi ngininike amandla.

Nina ninamandla futhi ninegunya, futhi ngiyanithuma ngokuthobeka nangokuzithoba. Ngiyanithuma ukuba nigcine umthetho ngokukholwa kwenu kimi, ngakho-ke thandani uYahweh ngenhliziyo yenu yonke, nangomphefumulo wenu wonke, nangengqondo yenu yonke, nangamandla enu onke, futhi nithande umakhelwane wenu njengoba nizithanda. Ubani umakhelwane wenu? Bonke abadinga usizo lwenu enginithuma kubo, futhi anginivimbeli ngemingcele yezizwe. Hambani niyokwelusa umhlambi wami, ngoba ngingumelusi oMkhulu ophethe umvuzo wenu esandleni sami ngenxa yokuthembeka. Hambani niyokunakekela umhlambi wami, ninakekele izilonda zawo, niwondle, niwuphilise, niwuhole, futhi ngaphezu kwakho konke niwulethise kimi; ngokuba ngokwenu anilutho, kodwa kimi, nina ningamadoda ami anamandla, aqinile kaNkulunkulu. Bafundiseni ukubiza igama lami kuze kube sekupheleni, ngokuba noma ubani owenzayo uyosindiswa.

Gcinani amehlo enu emklomelweni nasenjabulweni ebekwe phambi kwenu, futhi niqhubekele phambili emgomweni womklomelo wobizo oluphezulu lukaNkulunkulu kuKristu Jesu. Ngokuba umvuzo wenu uyoba mkhulu, futhi niyomangala ngalokho enibekelwe khona emazulwini. Futhi umvuzo wami awuwona owesikhashana kodwa ungunaphakade, futhi imivuzo yenu ayiyikubhubha kodwa iyosebenza njengefa laphakade ekugcwalisekeni kwezithembiso uYahweh azenza kokhokho; yonke i-yod noma imeva ye-yod iyogcwaliseka. Aniyikuqonda ubukhulu bemisebenzi yenu kuze kube yilolo suku lomvuzo, kodwa iMibhalo icacile mayelana nesithelo semisebenzi yenu.

Ngokuba uJohane uMambuli waloba ngokwethembeka lokho engamkhombisa khona: Emva kwalezi zinto ngabona, bheka, isixuku esikhulu, okungekho muntu ongasibala, sazo zonke izizwe nezinhlanga, nabantu, nezilimi, simi phambi kwesihlalo sobukhosi, naphambi kweWundlu, sembethe izingubo ezinde ezimhlophe, siphethe amagatsha esundu ezandleni zaso. Futhi bamemeza ngezwi elikhulu, bethi, Insindiso ivela kuNkulunkulu wethu, ohlezi esihlalweni sobukhosi, nakulo iWundlu. Ngoba niyosiza ukusindisa nokukhonza isixuku esikhulu, futhi imivuzo yenu yokwethembeka iyoba mikhulu.

Futhi niyongilandela nomaphi lapho ngiya khona, futhi nina niyizithelo zokuqala ezithengwe kubantu, futhi niyohlabelela iculo elisha phambi kwesihlalo sobukhosi elingafundwa yinina kuphela. Niyobusa nami eMbusweni wami iminyaka eyinkulungwane futhi niyoba nami phakade. Ngokuba eyenu injabulo yaphakade, ngokuba niyoba nami eMbusweni wami emhlabeni lapho ngibusa umhlaba ngokulunga esihlalweni sobukhosi sikaDavide, futhi uMbuso wami awunaphelo. Futhi akukho nto embi eyongena, futhi ngeke kusaba khona nhlupheko, futhi owami ngumbuso wokulunga lapho iwundlu nengonyama kuyolala phansi ndawonye ngokuthula. Futhi ekupheleni kokubusa kwami ​​kweminyaka eyinkulungwane, uSathane uyokhululwa isikhashana ukuze aqoqe ukuhlasela kwakhe kokugcina, futhi umlilo uyokwehla uvela kuYahweh ubaqothule ngokuphelele. USathane uyophonswa echibini lomlilo, angaphinde avuke, futhi uYahweh uyokwakha izulu elisha nomhlaba omusha, futhi uyokwakha indawo yakhe yokuhlala phakathi kwabantu, ngakho niyohlala eJerusalema Elisha, ngisho lelo dolobha elingcwele elakhiwe nguYahweh uqobo. Futhi uYahweh uqobo ushilo: Lowo onqobayo uyodla ifa lazo zonke izinto; futhi ngiyoba nguNkulunkulu wakhe, yena abe yindodana yami.
