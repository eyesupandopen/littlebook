Ubufakazi Obubili: Israyeli
Okubabayo Nokumnandi
Julayi 15, 2025

Bhekani! Ukukhetha Kungokwenu | Izilo Zembuliwe | Izimemezelo Zesiprofetho Ziyamenyezelwa

Lalelani amazwi oMsindisi wenu, iNkosi neSilo, Bantabami, nithobele amazwi eNkosi yenu, uJesu Kristu olungileyo, ngokuba ngiyiNdodana yokulunga futhi ngiyavela ngenkazimulo yami, nani nikhulile njengamathole esibayeni ngokuthula nokulondeka okukhulu, futhi nginikhulisele injongo yasezulwini esezokwembulwa ngokugcwele kini, Bathembekileyo nabakhethiweyo bami. Bhekani! Nginguye ovivinya izinhliziyo zabantu ukuze nazi ngokuqinisekileyo ukuthi nisekholweni ngempela yini, noma nikhohlisiwe futhi nithunjwe yizono zenu kanye nenkohliso enamandla yamanje ethiye umhlaba wonke.

Ngokuba nginguye uYahweh ahlukanisele naye isihlalo sakhe sobukhosi, uKristu Jesu iNkosi evusiweyo nenikwe amandla, futhi ngiyiNkosi yamakhosi neSilo samasilo, bhekani iNgonyama yesizwe sakwaJuda iyeza isemafini ukuzamukela abathembekileyo bami ukuze bayiswe emazulwini emshadweni weWundlu, ngokuba uMlobokazi wami usezilungisile. Izibani zakhe zigcwele, futhi uselungisile izintambo zezibani zakhe, futhi ulindele ukubonakala kwami njengentombi ehlakaniphileyo, labo engibahlambulule ngokuphelele futhi abangenacala phambi kwesihlalo sobukhosi sikaNkulunkulu. Yebo, labo abangathathwanga ngobuqili nguBaraba, ngokuba uBaraba uletha udlame, impi kanye nenhlekelele, ngokuba okwamanje uyinhloko eyodwa phakathi kwezinhloko eziningana ezisebenzisana ngokubambisana, kodwa uyovuka abe nguMoshiach wamanga, futhi ukhethiwe ngabantu bezwe ukuba abuse isinagoge likaSathane. Ngokuba uyindlalifa yalowo oyise babo abamkhetha, futhi bazilungiselela ukumamukela njengeSilo sabo.

Kodwa nina, oh Bandla laseLawodikeya, manje sekufika izikhathi zenu zokuchithwa nokwahlulelwa. Ngokuba nizikhethele omunye umsindisi ukuba abe yiSilo senu, yena kanye lowo okufika kwakhe kumenyezelwa ngezibonakaliso nezimangaliso zamanga, futhi okwamanje oniholile ngezingoma zokulumba. NginguYeshua HaMashiach, futhi nginithengile ngegazi lami elihlawulayo, elingenasono, futhi nginguye obengazonenza niphelele, futhi nginidale kabusha ngomoya wami, ngokuba ngenziwe umoya ophilayo ngokuba uYahweh unikile iNdodana yakhe bonke ubukhulu, yena kanye ongukupheleliswa kwewundlu likaNkulunkulu neNgonyama yesizwe sakwaJuda! Ngokulunga ngizokwahlulela futhi ngilwe impi ngokuba iwundlu likaNkulunkulu neNgonyama yesizwe sakwaJuda sekupheleliswe kimi, uKristu Jesu, uYeshua HaMashiach, ngokuba nginguye!

Ngisazophelelisa insindiso yenu, oh Bandla laseLawodikeya, kodwa anikalungeli ukungena eMbusweni wami ngalesi sikhathi ngoba niphambukile enjongweni yenu eyodwa kuKristu! Ngizonikhombisa iphutha lenu, futhi ninalo nakanjani ithuba lokuphenduka kuzo zonke izono, kodwa anisenaso isikhathi sokuphikisana nezwi leNkosi yenu, ngokuba lesi sono esibi singesinye seziningi uSathane azisebenzisile ukunibopha ekuzeni kimi ngokuphelele ekuphendukeni ukuze nginihlambulule ngokuphelele ngegazi lami elihlawulayo ezonweni zenu.

Bhekani! Izikhathi zabeZizwe seziphelile futhi umnqumo wasendle owaxhunyelelwa phakathi kwabo, futhi ohlanganyela nabo empandeni nasekunoneni komnqumo, manje usuzonqunywa. Ngokuba niwashaye indiva amazwi kaKristu Jesu, ngokuba ngasho ngokusobala ukuba ningaziqhayisi ngamagatsha awela ngokungakholwa: kodwa uma niziqhayisa, aniyithwele impande, kodwa impande inithwele nina. Ngiyimpande kaJese, futhi nginguye omiyo njengophawu lwethemba lazo zonke izikhathi, yena oletha insindiso nezindlela zikaYahweh emhlabeni futhi ngizobusa ifa lami esihlalweni sobukhosi sikaDavide ngokulunga, futhi ngiwuthengile umhlaba ngegazi lami elingenasono futhi ngizothatha lokho okungokwami ngokufanelekileyo ngamandla ezandleni zabahlali ngokungemthetho, labo kanye abawileyo.

Nginguye onike umusa nesihe emhlabeni, ngokuba ngaphiwa nguYahweh inkathi yonke yomusa ukuze kusindiswe abeZizwe. Ngokuba izinhloso zikaYahweh kwa-Israyeli zazihlale ziwukuthi abeZizwe babusiseke futhi bafinyelele ensindisweni ngokulalela kuka-Israyeli. Kodwa ngokuhamba kwesikhathi baba nokuzidla, nokuziqhayisa, futhi banqunywa emthini womnqumo wemvelo ngokungakholwa. Kodwa nina, Bandla lami laseLawodikeya, nina kanye niyisibonelo salolu hlobo olufanayo lokuzidla, ukungakholwa, nobumpumputhe obaboniswa ngamagatsha emvelo anqunywa ngokungakholwa, futhi manje nani nizokunqunywa emthini womnqumo wemvelo. Futhi manje ngizoya phambili ukuqedela umsebenzi wami emthini womnqumo wemvelo, u-Israyeli kanye izihlobo zami nabantu bami, labo bonke engibakhethile ukuba bangamukele njengeNkosi yabo ukuze ngibadale kabusha njengomuntu oyedwa omusha nokuba imfihlakalo kaNkulunkulu ipheleliswe, ukupheleliswa kanye kwemisebenzi yami ehlawulayo yensindiso.

Futhi nina, Bandla lami laseLawodikeya, nishiyile uthando lwenu lokuqala futhi nisezingalweni zomkhohlisi enimfungele ukwethembeka kuye. Nizikhethele uBaraba omusha njengeSilo senu, futhi nimlahla uJesu waseNazaretha njengeNkosi yenu, nisezingalweni zomunye umsindisi wamanga. Lowo kanye ozishaya umsindisi weBhabhiloni eliyimfihlakalo, okhuluma futhi enze njengomunye wenu, kodwa ekhuluma njengodrako. Ngokuba iBhabhiloni eliyimfihlakalo liphila ngokunethezeka nangobuningi bezinto ezibonakalayo, injabulo nengcebo.

Futhi iBhabhiloni eliyimfihlakalo lenze umhlaba wonke wadakwa ngokungcola kobufebe obusesitsheni salo segolide, kodwa zinjalo izindlela zabawileyo, abanikela ngokungcola okubi kakhulu, futhi nina nikuphuza ngokuzithandela nangenjabulo enkulu ngoba niphuphuthekiswe yisitshe segolide ukuba ningakuboni ukungcola okukhulu enikudla ngokungcolileyo. Ngokuba ngisho neBandla lami laseLawodikeya alisahlangani lizungeze imibhalo yami engcwele kodwa emibuthanweni kaBaraba, enithembele kuye. Ngakho-ke nikhetha umsindisi omusha ngoba nithanda umhlaba nezinto zomhlaba futhi aninakuyicabanga into yokuthi ningalahlekelwa yizibiliboco zenu eziyigugu, ngaphandle kokuthi ngizothatha zonke izinjabulo kini ngentukuthelo yami esondelayo, futhi manje ukulahlekelwa kwenu kuyanda!

Ngokuba niyizimpumputhe futhi nikhohlisekile ekuzidleni kwenu ngokuthi uYahweh anganikeza omunye umsindisi ngaphandle kwami, uJesu waseNazaretha, ngokuba ngiyimbewu ethenjisiweyo yowesifazane kanti uDani uyibhubesi elincane okungeke kuvele kulo uMesiya. Ngizweni kahle, oh bandla laseLawodikeya, ngokuba manje kungaba ukuphenduka kwenu ekoneni kwezinhliziyo zenu noma yisikhathi lapho ngizonishiya emlilweni wokucwenga futhi bonke abakhuleka egameni leNkosi kuze kube sekupheleni bayosindiswa, futhi abaningi abavuma igama lami ngokungakholwa bayowa banyamalale, bechithe ithuba labo ohlotsheni lobunkulunkulu oluphika amandla alo: Nina baseLawodikeya, ngingamandla alo futhi kimi uYahweh ubeke bonke ubukhulu, kanti nina ningiphikile, iNkosi noMsindisi wenu, futhi ngizonikhafula emlonyeni wami ngaphandle kokuba niphenduke manje!

Ngesikhathi senkathi yami yomusa, insindiso yasakazeka phakathi kwezizwe, futhi izizwe ngezizwe zabelana futhi zahlanganyela empandeni yomnqumo wemvelo. Manje, izizwe ngeke zisaba yisibani sensindiso, nesikhathi sazo eNdodaneni saza ngokulahlwa kwezihlobo zami nabantu bami, ngokuba bamemeza ngokungakholwa bethi, “Sikhululele uBaraba!” Kodwa njengoba izimfihlakalo zikaYahweh zembulwa ngabaphostoli bami abangcwele nabaprofethi, ngimbambile uBaraba futhi angimkhululanga ngokuphelele, kodwa ngavumela kuphela imfihlakalo yobubi ukuba iqhubeke. Manje sekufikile izikhathi lapho ngizomkhulula khona uBaraba ngokugcwele njengoba umbambi wami esusiwe futhi abathembekileyo bami, ababiziweyo, nabakhethiweyo beyiswa emazulwini lapho ngibalungiselele khona amakhaya abo aphakade, futhi bayokwembathiswa ilineni elihle elicolekileyo elingasoze langcola noma lacatshwa.

Ngabe niphuphutheke kangaka emhlabeni wenu, futhi ninezindlebe ezingezwa kangangokuthi anisalizwa izwi lami oh Bandla laseLawodikeya? Nikholwe amanga alethwe yimfihlakalo yobubi, futhi nivumele imvubelo enobuthi ingene emaBhayibhelini enu onakele alethe ukonakala okuphelele emaBandleni enu okungcolise izinhliziyo zenu ngezinganekwane ekungakholweni okuyiphutha. Navumela amanga ezangoma zesikhathi ukuba anilumbe ngemibhalo yamanga nangamazwi amanga. Anisaqondi umbhalo, futhi nizibizele izinkathazo ezinkulu ezizonehlela ngokuzumayo. Lonke iZwi likaNkulunkulu lihlanzekile futhi lenzelwe ukulalela kokukholwa nokwakha abamukeli. Qondani amazwi amaZwi ami futhi niqonde izikhathi eniphila kuzo. Ngokuba ngenza uJohane uMambuli ukuba abhale: “Ngabona isilo siphuma olwandle, sinezinhloko eziyisikhombisa nezimpondo eziyishumi, naphezu kwezimpondo zaso kunemiqhele eyishumi, naphezu kwezinhloko zaso igama lokuhlambalaza.”

Futhi uJohane uMambuli wabhala futhi: “Ngabona owesifazane ehlezi phezu kwesilo esibomvu klebhu, sigcwele amagama okuhlambalaza, sinezinhloko eziyisikhombisa nezimpondo eziyishumi. Owesifazane wayembethe okunsomi nokubomvu klebhu, ehlotshiswe ngegolide namatshe ayigugu namapharele, ephethe isitsha segolide esandleni sakhe, sigcwele amanyala nokungcola kobufebe bakhe.”

Futhi futhi: “Nezimpondo eziyishumi owazibona phezu kwesilo, zona ziyosizonda isifebe, zisichithe, zisishiye sinqunu, zidle inyama yaso, zisishise ngomlilo. Ngokuba uNkulunkulu ufake ezinhliziyweni zazo ukuba zifeze intando yakhe, zivumelane, zinike umbuso wazo esilweni, kuze kugcwaliseke amazwi kaNkulunkulu. Nowesifazane owambonayo uyilowo muzi omkhulu obusa amakhosi omhlaba.”

Bantabami, qondani ukuthi isilo singasho abantu ngabanye kanye nemibuso, fundani ukuze nifunde. Isilo esithwele isifebe siyosichitha isifebe futhi ngeke sisaba ngaphansi kokubuswa yisifebe, ngokuba sesibuse amakhosi omhlaba ngobufebe baso. Ngakho isilo esizophuma olwandle siyoveza uMahdi wabo olindelwe, kodwa futhi siyoyichitha ngokuphelele iBhabhiloni eliyimfihlakalo, ngokuba uNkulunkulu unikela isifebe esikhulu ezandleni zezitha zaso esezivele zisizonda kakhulu.

Ngaphambi kokuba isilo sasolwandle nesilo sasemhlabeni kuvele ngokugcwele eMbusweni kadrako, baningi ababusi abahlangene ngenhloso yokunika amandla nokubusa kwabo kudrako, ngokuba uNkulunkulu ukufakile ezinhliziyweni zabo ukuba bakwenze lokho. Futhi bayoyichitha iBhabhiloni eliyimfihlakalo. Nicabanga ukuthi niyazi kahle imibhalo yami engcwele ngoba nikholwe izazi zenu zenkolo eziqamba amanga? Qondani nizwe amazwi eNkosi yenu!

Futhi, ngenza uJohane uMambuli ukuba abhale: “Isilo engasibona sasifana nengwe, nezinyawo zaso zinjengezebhere, nomlomo waso unjengomlomo wengonyama: udrako wasinika amandla aso, nesihlalo saso sobukhosi, negunya elikhulu. Ngabona enye yezinhloko zaso sengathi ilinyazwe yafa, kodwa inxeba layo elibulalayo laphola, umhlaba wonke wamangala walandela isilo.”

Bantabami, amazwi kaYahweh anokujula okunezinhlangothi eziningi, okungekho muntu ongakuqonda ngokugcwele. Isilo esichazwayo ngumbuso ozovuka ekubhujisweni kwesifebe, futhi ohlelweni oludala kuzovuka uhlelo olusha! Simele isilo sasolwandle, ngokuba siyovuka njengoMahdi wabo, futhi simele uhlobo lobukhosi azobusa ngalo ulwandle. Kungani ningawahloniphi amazwi eLizwi lami? Akusho ukuthi isilo ngokwaso sasolwandle salinyazwa, ngoba yimuphi umuntu ngempela onezinhloko eziyisikhombisa? Kodwa enye yezinhloko zaso sengathi ilinyazwe yafa. Oh, ziwula, nezinhliziyo ezinensa ukukholwa konke abakukhulumile abaProfethi! "SENGATHI" ilinyazwe yafa. Amazwi athi "sengathi" bekufanele anivimbe, kodwa anisakholwa ngisho namazwi ami amancane, futhi mina ngiyiLizwi elenziwa inyama. Akucaci yini kini, ukuthi "SENGATHI" yisisho esidala? Akucaci yini kini ukuthi "sasi" kusebunzimeni? Akucaci yini kini ukuthi ukufushanisa kwelithi, "kunjengokungathi kunjalo"?

Kodwa cha, nikholwa amanga kadrako, ngokuba uBaraba ukhuluma njengodrako, kodwa akayena uBaraba, kodwa uyisilo sasemhlabeni. Manje wonke umhlaba uyomangala ngesilo ngenxa yezibonakaliso nezimangaliso zaso zamanga, futhi siyokwenza ezinye izibonakaliso nezimangaliso zamanga eziningi, futhi asikaveli ngokugcwele ngoba isifebe asikashiswa ngomlilo, kodwa manje sembuliwe esikhathini saso. Ukhuluma njengodrako, ethi “uNkulunkulu yedwa nguye owamsindisa enxebeni lakhe lasekhanda” futhi lawa ngamanga ahlambalazayo. UYahweh, uNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade, ungithumile mina Jesu waseNazaretha njengoMsindisi womhlaba. Mina noBaba simunye ebunye nasenjongweni, futhi uYahweh akamsindisanga uBaraba futhi nami, Kristu Jesu, angimenzanga kanjalo. Nikhohlisiwe, nathathwa, futhi nathiywa enkohlisweni enkulu, futhi ngithi kini phendukani nibuyele othandweni lwenu lokuqala futhi nikwenze lokho manje, ngokuba asikho isikhathi!

Ngokuba nginguye ovivinya izinhliziyo, futhi nginguye ohlola bonke engibabizela ensindisweni, ngokuba ngihlanza bonke emlilweni wokucwenga! Bhekani! Nginguye ohlezi njengomcwengi nomhlambululi wesiliva: futhi ngihlanza amadodana kaYahweh, ngiwacwenga njengegolide nesiliva, ukuze anikele eNkosini umnikelo ngokulunga. Aniqondi yini ukuthi ngizonenza abapristi namakhosi phambi kukaBaba? Nakuba isipho sami somoya ongcwele, enizalwa ngaso kabusha ngembewu engonakaliyo siphelele futhi asidingi kucwengwa, nivela ku-Adamu ngegazi futhi kufanele nivumele umsebenzi wami ufezeke ngokugcwele ezimpilweni zenu, ngaphandle kokuthi imbewu yami iwele endaweni enamadwala.

Manje sekufika izikhathi zokwahlulela futhi izikhathi zomusa seziphelile. Nakuba abaningi beyohleka amazwi ami futhi bahlehle ngokungakholwa, sekwaziwa kakade nguYahweh umphumela. Ngokuba uYahweh uhlale efakazela esintwini izindlela zaso ezonakele futhi asole isintu ngesono, azi ethandweni lwakhe nasekuhlakanipheni kwakhe kwaphakade ukuthi uyosindisa abanye. Futhi njengoba uYahweh wenza lukhuni inhliziyo kaFaro, kanjalo uzokwenza lukhuni izinhliziyo zabaningi abezwayo kodwa bangalaleli. UYahweh uyophinda avivinye umuntu kaninginingi. Abanye bayoba njengoFaro futhi benze izinhliziyo zabo zibe lukhuni ngokwengeziwe ekungakholweni kodwa abanye bayosuka ezindleleni zikaFaro bavukele ekuphileni okuphakade kuKristu Jesu, kodwa ngomlilo wokucwenga.

Qondani ukuthi labo abakhetha uBaraba bayozenza abahlanganyeli emacaleni kaBaraba, futhi labo abenqaba ukubiza igama lami kuze kube sekupheleni bonke bayolahlwa ngoba babenamajabulo ekungalungini. Ngokuba umsindisi wenu omusha akayena umsindisi kodwa uyisilo esihahayo. Uhlelo lwakhe nombuso wakhe kwakhiwe yilabo abalimaza abantwana. Aniziqondi yini izindlela zikaDrako? Ngokuba labo abalandela izindlela zikadrako bahlasela abantwana. Banikela ngabantwana kuMoloki, bafuna umthombo wabo wobusha egazini labantwana abahlukunyeziwe, bahlukumeza abantwana ukuze bazanelise ngokugula kwabo, futhi benza umkhuba wokushaya umbungu esibelethweni ukuze babulale abancane ngaphambi kokuzalwa, futhi baningenele ngasese ezindleleni zabo, ngokuba zinjalo izindlela zabaholi beBhabhiloni eliyimfihlakalo kanye nombuso kadrako. Manje sekufika ukwahlulela kukaNkulunkulu ngalezi zinto nokunye okuningi. Ngokuba ukube anizibonanga lezi zinto, beningeke nibe nasono, kodwa manje niyabona futhi isono senu sihlezi.

Anifundanga yini? Usho kanje uJehova, “Kwezwakala izwi phezulu, isililo nokukhala okubabayo. URaheli ekhalela abantwana bakhe, wala ukududuzwa ngabantwana bakhe, ngokuba babengekho.” Ngokuba nehlulekile emsebenzini enawunikezwayo, futhi nathenjwa ngezimfihlakalo zami futhi nema njengokukhanya kwensindiso nophawu lwethemba. Kodwa manje senihlangene nalabo enganithuma ukuba nime nabo. Ngakho manje sekufika izikhathi lapho ngizonishiyela ekusaseni lenu.

Ngizweni kahle nonke eningalahla izwi likaYeshua HaMashiach, izwi likaKristu Jesu: aninaso isikhathi! Ngiyaneluleka ukuba ningizwe othandweni lwami ngani bantabami futhi niphenduke manje! Ngokuba umlilo wokucwenga usuzonehlela ngokugcwele, futhi uzohlanza izwe kuwo wonke umbuso kadrako, futhi umhlaba ngeke usaba ngaphansi kwezindlela zikaSathane futhi ngeke uphinde uvuke umbuso wakhe, ngokuba ngiyiNkosi yamakhosi neSilo samasilo, futhi ngizobabhubhisa ngokuphelele abawileyo. Bonke abenqaba ukuphenduka bayoba nengxenye yabo echibini lomlilo futhi bahlanganyele esijezisweni sikadeveli nezingelosi zakhe kulelo chibi elikhulu lesibabule esivuthayo. Ngokunjalo, bonke abaphendukayo ngokuphuma esonweni bebiza igama lami kuze kube sekupheleni bayosindiswa futhi banikwe umvuzo omkhulu waphakade kimi.

Bhekani! Manje yisikhathi sokubuthwa kwabathembekileyo, futhi manje ngizoshiya labo abenqabayo kuBaraba, ngokuba sebenzenzile ukukhetha kwabo.

Ngokuba Abamukelanga Uthando Lweqiniso, Ukuze Basindiswe

(Futhi kuyokwenzeka ukuthi ikhanda laso liyobonakala sengathi lilimele lafa, kodwa isilonda saso esibulalayo sapholiswa, futhi izwe lonke liyomangala lilandele isilo. Futhi kanjalo lo mkhohlisi uyovuka.)

Futhi abaningi eBhabhiloni Elifihlekile, ngisho nalabo abakholwa amanga alo futhi balandele amazwi alo amahle nezinkulumo zalo ezinhle, bayathunjwa futhi babhajwe ukuthula kwamanga lapho becabanga ukuthi izimpilo zabo ezimnandi nezigcwele isono zizoqhubeka, lapho namuhla kunjengayizolo nekusasa liyofana nanamhlanje.
