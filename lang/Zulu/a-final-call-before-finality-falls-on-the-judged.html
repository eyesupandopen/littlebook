Ubizo Lokugcina Ngaphambi Kokuba Isiphetho Sehlele Abahlulelweyo

Bhekani! NginguYahweh, uNGINGUYE OMKHULU, futhi ngiza ukuthumela amazwi ami othando kanye nobizo lokugcina kulabo ababhekene nesiphetho sokwahlulelwa uma benqaba ubizo lwami lokugcina. Nakuba bonke abakhalaza ngegama likaJesu kuze kube sekupheleni beyothola insindiso enkulu yaphakade nomvuzo omkhulu, abantu abaningi kakhulu bayowela ekwahlulelweni ngenxa yezono zabo nobulukhuni bezinhliziyo zabo. Ngoba, nginguYE osola abangalungile ukuba baphenduke, futhi labo abenqaba ukuphenduka ngiyaqhubeka nokubasola kuze kube yilapho izinhliziyo zabo sezilukhuni ngokuphelele bese bethathwa ekwahlulelweni.

Ngakho-ke, ngiza ukunikeza lo myalezo ngothando lwami ngani, futhi ngithumele umusa wami onganifanele ezweni elahlulelwe futhi emizamweni yami yokuthambisa izinhliziyo zenu ezingaphenduki ukuze namukele insindiso; noma kuthi ngenzondo nokuhlubuka kwenu nishiywe esimweni sokwenqaba kokugcina, senivuthiwe futhi senilungele ukwahlulelwa kokugcina. Qondani, asisekho isikhathi ezikhathini zami zomusa nesihe sempilo yonke yokuphenduka ngenxa yezinhlelo zami zensindiso yaso sonke isintu esiyofika ensindisweni ephelele ngezikhathi zaso nangokulandelana kwaso kulabo abayokwamukela uthando lwami ngabo. Ngakho-ke, izikhathi zenu yimanje futhi ubizo lokugcina luyathunyelwa manje. Selah.

Yizwani, O' Yizwani izwi likaNkulunkulu Omkhulu Waphakade Wokulunga, uYahweh, uNGINGUYE OMKHULU, onguNkulunkulu Waphakade Wokulunga futhi niqonde incazelo yenkulumo yami, futhi nazi ukuthi izwi lami linjengomsindo wamanzi amaningi agijimayo naphilayo, futhi niqonde ukuthi izwi lami liduma njengomsindo wemidumo eminingi, (ngisho nemidumo yemidumo) entukuthelweni yami yokulunga ngabakhileyo emhlabeni abahlala kuwo ekungalalelini okukhulu nasekungalingini okukhulu nasesonweni.

Bhekani! NginguNGINGUYE ENGINGUYE, ngisho noNGINGUYE OMKHULU, futhi ngidalile amazulu nomhlaba nakho konke okukuwo, futhi emklamo wami wokulunga ngenza amazulu nomhlaba kwaphelela ezindleleni zami zokuphelela, ngisho ngiwadala ekupheleleni okuvumelanayo nokulungileyo lapho zonke izidalwa zami zazizohlala ekupheleleni okuvumelanayo nasothandweni nasenkonzweni ngothando komunye nomunye. Futhi othandweni lwabo ngoYise nomdali wabo, babeyolalela izwi lami nemiyalo yami ezimpilweni zabo ukuze ngempela baziqonde bona njengoba babedaliwe futhi baqonde nezindlela zami. Nokho, ububi batholakala kuHeyleli, yena owawa ekupheleleni okukhazimulayo, yena owadalwa ngobukhazikhazi obukhulu nobesabekayo, ukuze awele emhlabeni, ehliswe isithunzi esonweni nasebumnyameni ukuze ahlale esesimweni senyoka eqalekisiweyo ngenxa yokungalaleli kwamaSerafi. Selah.

Ngenxa yeqiniso laphakade, izindlela zami ziphakeme kunezindlela zomuntu, futhi nginguYE ozungeze isihlalo sami sobukhosi ngezinkulungwane eziyishumi eziphindwe ngezinkulungwane eziyishumi, ngisho nesibalo esingenakubalwa sezingelosi eziphumelele ekuhlakanipheni, emandleni, nasenkotweni, futhi ezithanda uYahweh nezithanda ukufunda izindlela zikaYahweh, uNGINGUYE OMKHULU ngakho konke ukulalela okushisekayo nokuthembekileyo njalo: Selah.

Ngeqiniso, leqiniso elikhulu nelaphakade, ngibadalile benamandla futhi bekhazimula futhi ngabembathisa ubukhazikhazi obukhanyayo, nokulunga kwaphakade, nodumo lwaphakade. Ngeqiniso, leqiniso elikhulu nelaphakade, abakaze baswele noma badinge ngisho nasezindabeni ezincane kakhulu; futhi bangikhonza mina nendalo yami ngokugcwele ezinhliziyweni zabo zothando ngoYise nomdali wabo, u-Elohimi, ekulaleleni kwabo uKristu Jesu.

(Yena lowo, uYahweh, uNGINGUYE OMKHULU NAPHAKADE abeke zonke izinto ezulwini nasemhlabeni ezandleni zakhe ezinamandla naphakade nezilalelayo.)

Ngakho-ke yazini iqiniso laphakade ukuthi uKristu Jesu uyiNkosi yamakhosi noMbusi wababusi futhi nguyena oyoletha ukuthula emazulwini kanye nasemhlabeni futhi nguyena oyobuyisela esandleni sami esikhazimulayo (othandweni lwakhe ngoYise noMdali wakhe, u-Elohimi, ngisho noNGINGUYE OMKHULU) uMbuso othotshisiwe ekupheleni kwazo zonke izinto lapho izandla zakhe ezinamandla ziyobe seziqedile zonke izinto ezibhaliwe nezabikezelwa mayelana neNkosi Yaphakade Yokulunga! Ngisho nalapho leyo Ndodana yoMuntu isiyiqedile yonke into ngamandla amakhulu, nangothando olukhulu. Ngisho nangaleso sikhathi, ngiyobe sengifeze ngamandla ami ngezandla zakhe ezinamandla nangomhlatshelo zonke izinto ezinikezwe ezinhlelweni zami zaphakade nasezimfihlakalweni ukuze azifeze.

Ngakho-ke, nginazisa ukuba niqonde, ukuthi nginguYE okwazi ngamandla onke nobukhosi ukuqonda, ukufeza, ukwazi ngokugcwele, nokulawula yonke imininingwane ephathelene nayo yonke ingxenye yendalo yami kungakhathaliseki ukuthi inkulu kangakanani noma ibonakala inamandla kangakanani kuze kufike emininingwaneni emincane kakhulu, futhi akukho okungafihlwa emehlweni ami! NginguYE ongakwazi ukufeza zonke izinto khona manjalo ngamandla ami naphakade. Nokho, nginguYE futhi osebenza zonke izinto ngokweluleka kwentando yami futhi ongadingi meluleki, ngakho-ke, izinhlelo zami nezimfihlakalo zizogcwaliseka ngokuphelele kulezi zikhathi zokugcwaliseka kwazo, ngisho izikhathi ZAMANJE.

Nokho, othandweni lwami ngendalo yami nasesifisweni sami sabantwana nomndeni, ngidala izidalwa nabantu abathile (abane inkululeko yokuzikhethela) ngezinhloso zami ezithile zaphakade ezinikezwa abalalelayo enkonzweni enkulu nasodumweni, futhi ngibanikeza amandla ngokufanele. Ngakho-ke, ngiyakweluleka ukuba ufunde izindlela zami ensindisweni noma ukhethe amalangabi okubhujiswa kuze kube phakade. Kodwa yazi, O' muntu owileyo, unokhetho okuzothi ngokuzuma kushintshe kube wabe unokhetho, futhi bonke abayobe bekhethe okubi ekugcineni lapho kufika isiphetho, abasoze baphunyuka ekwahlulelweni kwabo ingunaphakade naphakade. Selah.

Ungubani wena, O' muntu owileyo, wena ozihlanganise nokuhlubuka okungenangqondo nobuwula nobuhlanya kwabawileyo kukho konke ukungalungi, ukuhaha, inkanuko, nokonakala ukuze uziphakamise ngokuzidla melawanana noNkulunkulu Omkhulu Waphakade okudalile ngezinhloso zami nenjabulo yami?

Ungubani wena, O' muntu owileyo ukuthi ekungazini nasekanukweni yakho ungalokotha uphakamise isithende sakho melawanana noNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade Wezulu Lamazulu (YENA owakunika ukuphila) ngezindlela zosathane? Ngakho-ke, qonda izinhloso zami nenhloso yami ngoba ngithumela laMazwi KUWE njengobizo lwami lokugcina kwabaningi benu abacabanga ngobuwula ukuthi banesikhathi eningasenaso, futhi ngisho ngininika amazwi esixwayiso othandweni lwami olungapheli ngani ngokunikhumbuza futhi ngezimemezelo zokwahlulelwa nosizi esezinikeziwe eniye naziziba ngokuphelele ezenziwe nguKristu Jesu esihlalweni sobukhosi nasemandleni kaYahweh, futhi zamemezelwa nguYahweh uqobo, ngisho noNGINGUYE OMKHULU!

(Aniqondi yini ukuthi izimemezelo zami azisoze zashintshwa?)

Nokho, niziba ngisho nokukhuluma kwemidumo eyisikhombisa engayembulela, futhi ngayenza uJohane uMambuli wayivala ngesikhathi sakhe kuze kube izikhathi ezifanele zokwembulwa lezo zikhathi ezingamanje. (Futhi kuzoqhubeka kuze kube yilapho ubufakazi babafakazi bami ababili buphethwa ekwahlulelweni kokugcina, ngaphambi kokuba usizi olukhulu kunawo wonke lwehlele ngokuphelele abangalungile, Selah.) Le midumo leyo eyamenyezelwa nguYeshua HaMashiach noYahweh, uNGINGUYE OMKHULU, ivela esihlalweni sami sobukhosi esinamandla sokumemezela nokwahlulela, futhi manje inikezwa ngesandla sesithunywa sami esinamandla engithumela ngaso la Mazwi othando nezixwayiso eziqinile nezesabekayo, ukuze bonke abangase beze beze, ngaphambi kokuba ukwahlulelwa kwehle ngokugcwele futhi kungabe kusaba khona isikhathi!

Ngakho-ke, uma nifisa ukubalekela ukwahlulelwa kwenu nokubhujiswa kwaphakade, ngiyaniyala ukuba nenze uJesu abe yiNkosi yenu ngokuphelele nangokungananazi. Ngoba ukwahlulela kwami kuyawehla, futhi kuyowela kunoma ngubani okuthunyelwe kuye, khona lokho kwahlulela kokulahlwa okungokwaphakade ngokuphelele naphakade. Ngakho-ke, ngiyeluleka bonke abangakavumi uJesu Kristu njengeNkosi ngenhliziyo egcwele ukholo ukuba bakwenze lokho manje, bazi ukuthi nginguYE owamvusa kwabafileyo ngamandla amakhulu kakhulu ukuze angabe esafa futhi aqhubeke efeni lakhe ingunaphakade naphakade. Nokho, iningi elikhulu liyowela ekwahlulelweni okukhulu naphakade njengoba nje kubhaliwe emibhalweni eNgcwele yasendulo nasemibhalweni eNgcwele yamanje.

Kodwa ngiza ukunikeza izixwayiso zami noma kunjalo, ngisho nangemva kokuba izimemezelo zokwahlulelwa kwenu kokugcina sezimenyezelwe futhi seziqhubeka. Ngoba izindlela zami ziyizindlela zomusa nesihe nothando futhi ngeqiniso laphakade, ngifisa ukuthi kungabhubhi muntu kodwa bonke abantu beze ensindisweni yaphakade nasekubuyiselweni, nakuba iningi liyokwenqaba isicelo sami futhi lizikhethele ukuhlushwa nokwahlulelwa kwalo kwaphakade esikhundleni salokho. Nakuba ngiza ukuxwayisa abantu, azikho izixwayiso ezinikezwayo eziyoshintsha ukwahlulela kwami, noma isikhathi sakho, noma imiphumela yakho, noma izinhlelo zami zaphakade zokuhlenga izithelo zami zokuqala, ngisho ngibahlwitha ukuze ngigcwalise ifa labo laphakade ngaphambi kokuba kwehle usizi. Selah.

Futhi ngithumela lo myalezo wothando nesixwayiso othandweni lwami kumuntu wemvelo omubi, ngisho nangothando olufanayo engithumela ngalo imiyalezo yothando nesixwayiso kubantwana bami abamukele uJesu Kristu ngokulalela; ngoba ngenza ukuba line phezu kwabalungile nabangalungile ngokufanayo. Ngenxa yothando lwami ngani, ngiza ukunikeza ubizo lwami lokugcina ngaphambi kokuba usizi luhlasele luvela esihlalweni sami sobukhosi samandla aphakade, ukwahlulela, nesimemezelo lapho izwi lami elidumayo lisaduma ngentukuthelo enkulu nelungileyo. Kunjalo, ngoba NIYENQABA ukungizwa nakuba ngiye ngaxwayisa ngaxwayisa ama-jubili amaningi kakhulu manje! Ngizweni futhi ningizwe kahle ngoba izikhathi zenu zokubhujiswa kwaphakade zizothunyelwa ukuze zibophe ekwahlulelweni bonke abathanda futhi benze amanga, nabathokoza ngokungalungi, futhi uma ningangamukeli kulezi zikhathi zomusa, niyowela ekwahlulelweni kwaphakade ngoba ayikho enye indlela.

Nokho, ekuzidleni kwenu okuyimpumputhe nobulukhuni benhliziyo, niyamlahla umuntu oyedwa engamthuma ukuze nithole insindiso yenu ephelele, ngisho noJesu waseNazaretha, leyo Ndodana encekwini kaYahweh engiyenzile yaba umoya onika ukuphila, ngisho ngayenza yaba ngaphansana kukaYahweh uqobo ekuvukeni kwami kwabafileyo nasekukhazinyulisweni kukaKristu Jesu! Ngoba ngiphakeme futhi ngingumbusi phezu kwayo yonke indalo yami futhi ngeqiniso laphakade nelingaguquki, nginguYE othumela imvula phezu kwabalungile nabangalungile ngokufanayo othandweni lwami ngendalo yami, futhi ezindleleni zami akukho ukukhetha ubuso babantu ekwahluleleni kwami okulungile endalweni yami.

Ngakho-ke, qondani ukuthi ukuba nokhetho lobuso kungenye yezindlela eziningi ezingcolile zabawileyo, ngakho ngiza ukuxwayisa ngisho nesoni esibi kakhulu phakathi kwabakhileyo emhlabeni ngaphambi kokuba ukwahlulelwa kwabo kufike ngokugcwele, ukuze bakwazi ukubaleka emizuzwaneni embalwa esele futhi edlulayo yomusa ngaphambi kokuba usizi luhlasele. Ngakho ngiyeluleka bonke abezwayo noma abafunda la Mazwi ukuba baphendukele kuJesu waseNazaretha ngokushesha, bengangabazi lutho ngoba esixukwini esikhulu ngeke kube nelinye ithuba, ngoba ezonweni zabo ezimbi nasekudeleleni kwabo okukhulu zonke izinto eziNgcwele, bayowela ekwahlulelweni kwaphakade, ngisho begcinwe ekuhlushweni lapho beyolinda khona ukwahlulelwa kwesihlalo sobukhosi esimhlophe esikhulu, lapho bengeke bame kodwa kunalokho bayoboshwa futhi badluliselwe emalangabini esijeziso lapho izono zabo zizonikelwa emalangabini ukuze kutholakale impindiselo egcwele yobulungisa kuze kube phakade.

Nokho, wena, O' muntu owileyo, ungahlala ezindleleni zakho ezingalungile, ngisho unqotshwe ngokuphelele ngabawileyo ekungazini kwakho nasebumpumputheni obubangelwa amanga abo, ngisho amanga owathandayo ngoba uyazithanda izindlela zamanga akho. Ngakho-ke, ngithumele emhlabeni inkohliso enkulu nenamandla ukuze nonke nilahlwe enithokozela ukungalungi futhi nenqabe uJesu waseNazaretha, uYeshua HaMashiach. Futhi njengoba nibhampaza ekungazini ngezindlela zenu eziyimpumputhe nezinhliziyo ezilukhuni, njengoba nihleli ebumnyameni ekunganakini nasesonweni esikhulu, njengoba nihamba ngeze niziqongelelela ulaka olukhulu nemiphumela yaphakade ekungalalelini kwenu, ngisazophakamisa izwi lami, ngisho ngithumela la Mazwi njengobufakazi obumelene nalabo abayokwenqaba, ngisho ngethemba lokuthi ningaphendukela kuKristu Jesu, ukuze nginisindise ekwahlulelweni kwabawileyo: Selah.

O' Muntu wemvelo, ngisho nani nonke bantwana baka-Adamu abangahlengiwe nabangaphenduki, ngisho nani nonke enginiyalayo emhlabeni othandweni lwami ngani! Nokho nikholwe amanga ayisiphukuphuku kakhulu eninikwe wona njengemfundo ekufundisweni kwenu ngabawileyo. Imfundo yokufa enikezwa ezikhungweni zenu ezonakele nezonakalisayo zokufa lapho izindlela zongqingili manje zibusa njengoba ziphawula futhi zilungiselela abantwana benu uqobo ukuze badle abangenacala enkanukweni yabo ebolile. Nokho, nithathekile kakhulu yizinjabulo zesono, futhi nisindwa kakhulu ukuhaha kwenu kokuqongelela ingcebo eyengeziwe, kangangokuthi ngokuzithandela ninikela abantwana benu ngezindlela zikaBali ngomthamo wesinkwa esingcolile. Ngakho-ke, ngivumele abawileyo ukuba bafake ushevu ekudleni kwenu, futhi ngibanikile inala yenu ephelele ukuze udrago, isilo sasolwandle, nesilo somhlaba kugcwalise izinhloso zami, ngoba nezitha zami ziyalalela izwi lami.

Ngoba lokho okuthiwa yimfundo yenu kungamanga futhi konakele ngomklamo futhi lokhu kungenxa yokuthi niyalulahla ulwazi lweqiniso nezindlela zika-Elohimi umdali wenu, esikhundleni salokho nikhetha ukudakiswa yizibuko zenu ezimnyama eniziphethe ngokwethembeka okukhulu esandleni njengoba nisuka esikrinini uye kwesinye ekungazini okukhulu. Nokho ninqotshwe ngokuphelele futhi naphuphuthekiswa yinjabulo eshibhile nedlulayo njengoba izingqondo zenu ziguqulwa ngamakhemikhali ngomklamo ngemishini enizibopha ngayo esonweni. Futhi ulwazi olungamanga lubanga ukuzidla futhi ukuzidla kwandulela ukubhujiswa okukhulu. Qonda, O' Muntu, ukuthi ulwazi lwakho alunakukusindisa ekwahlulelweni kwami, futhi ukwahlulelwa kwabawileyo manje sekuzowela. Nokho, wena, O' Muntu wemvelo uyisiphukuphuku kakhulu ukungaqondi ukuthi abawileyo bakufundise amanga abo, ukuze benze abantu abaningi ngangokunokwenzeka bahlanganyele nabo efeni labo lesibabule esivuthayo, esibolile, nesigingqikayo. Ngisho isijeziso esinzima esenzelwa udeveli nezingelosi zakhe, hhayi u-Adamu. Selah.

Nokho, ngokuvuma kwenu ngokuzithandela niyaziqhenya kakhulu ngempumelelo yenu yezwe engenamsebenzi lapho nibutha ngokuhaha iphepha lenu lokuzenzisa elingenamsebenzi namadijithi enu okuzenzisa angenamsebenzi kuma-akhawunti okuzenzisa angenamsebenzi azokwephucwa ngokuzuma manje ekwahlulelweni, ngenxa yokuthi lezi yizindlela zika-Esawu owathengisa ubuzibulo bakhe ngomthamo wesinkwa. Nokho, ngokuvuma kwenu ngokuzithandela nivumela abawileyo ukuba bathathe imali kini ngokusatshiswa ngodlame ukuze nikhokhele ukufundiswa kwabantwana benu ngezindlela zokufa. Ukuze bonke abafundiswe ekungazini nasebumpumputheni obunjalo bakhonze ngokuzithandela abawileyo becabanga ukuthi benzela uNkulunkulu uqobo umusa omkhulu ezonweni zabo, kube sengathi uYahweh kufanele asuke endalweni yakhe nasebukhosini bakhe ngenxa yokuzidla kwabo okungenamsebenzi, okunesono. Okungenzeki okungenakwenzeka, ngoba nginguNGINGUYE.

Futhi ezindleleni zenu zokungalungi ezinenhliziyo elukhuni senilahlekelwe ngisho nezindlebe zenu futhi nime niyizithulu, izimungulu, nezimpumputhe ezindleleni zenu ezingalungile ezinehlise njengesigqila sesono ukuze nenelise izinkanuko zabawileyo, enibakhonzayo ezindleleni zenu ezingenankolo. Nokho, inceku ayihlali endlini kuze kube phakade uma ikhetha ngokulalela ukungena ngomnyango owodwa kuphela oya emazulwini engiyoke ngiwuvulele isintu: uJesu Kristu onguJesu Mesiya, ogama lakhe linguYeshua HaMashiach, lowo mnyango omkhulu nowodwa ovulekile wensindiso owake wavulwa ngesandla sami kwabathembekile ngokulunga kuKristu Jesu. Selah!

NGINGUYE, futhi ngithumele la Mazwi othando kwabalahliwe ngesithunywa sami nenceku yami, nangesithunywa sami nenceku yami ngithumele la Mazwi kulabo abazokwahlulelwa maduze.

Qondani nonke enizwa la Mazwi, ukuthi lesi akusona isimemezelo sokugcina sokwahlulelwa, esiletha isiphetho kumuntu wemvelo. Ngokuphambene nalokho, lo myalezo usebenza ukuxwayisa abangalaleli nabangalungile baseBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo kanye nabo bonke labo la Mazwi athunyelwe kubo, (futhi abanye bayofika ensindisweni kule mizuzwana yokugcina) kodwa futhi njengobufakazi bokugcina besixwayiso obunikezwa ngaphambi kokuba ukwahlulelwa kwehle ngokugcwele eBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo.

Qondani, ukuthi amazwi ami azothola amehlo, izindlebe, nezinhliziyo zalabo athunyelwe kubo, futhi awukho umzamo ovela kwabawileyo, amanyala abo, noma ubuchwepheshe babo obungenamsebenzi nobungazi obungamisa lokho uNGINGUYE OMKHULU ahlose ukukufeza.

Futhi ngaleyo ndlela ngisayina incwadi yami yothando yomuntu siqu kwabahlulelwe ngethemba lokuthi ngokulalela amazwi ami athunyelwe othandweni lwami ngabo bangase babalekele ukwahlulelwa kwabo ngaphambi kokuba leso sandla sokugcina sishaye lowo mzuzwana wokugcina.

Futhi ngisayina incwadi yami yothando ngoThando Olukhulu Nasegunyeni Laphakade!

NginguNGINGUYE ENGINGUYE, futhi nginguYahweh, uNGINGUYE OMKHULU NAPHAKADE.
