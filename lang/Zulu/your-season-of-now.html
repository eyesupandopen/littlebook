Inkathi Yakho kaMANJE

MANJE kuza uYeshua HaMashiach ukuba anitshele izizathu zezinqumo zami, ukuze nizwe ngokugcwele amaZwi ami ukuze niqonde izizathu eziholele kule nkathi kaMANJE! Ukuze niqonde imiphumela yokuba iwe ngehora elilodwa nesilo ngokungazelele nangokungalindelekile kulesi sigaba sokugcina sikaMANJE, nokuthi ningalungela ngokuphelele ukuhlangana okugcwele okuzodilika kulo mhlaba ngokwahlulela okugcwele komlilo njengoba abakholwayo kuKristu Jesu bemuka masinyane. Selah.

Gxilisani ngokugcwele izinhliziyo zenu, umphefumulo wenu, ingqondo yenu, namandla enu konke ekulaleleni uKristu Jesu MANJE njengoba lemihelo yokubeletha iphela ngokungazelele MANJE. Lapho nizohlwithwa khona ngokungazelele nangokungalindelekile manje, niyiswe esihlalweni sobukhosi sika-Adonai-YHVH.

Kunjalo-ke, mina Kristu Jesu, ngiza ukuzonitshela ngani nina bakholwayo bami kuKristu Jesu ukuze niqonde izinhloso zika-Adonai-YHVH wenu enginihlukanisele zona kulo mhlaba oyisigwegwe nogwegwile ngaphambi kokuba ukwahlulela kwehle.

Nina niyibandla lami, umzimba wami, ngisho nomakoti wami: Ngakho-ke, qondani ukuthi ningobani kuKristu Jesu. Ngoba nginiyala nonke MANJE, ukuba nigxilise izingqondo zenu ngokuphelele ezintweni ezingaphezulu, hhayi ezintweni ezisemhlabeni, okumele zibhubhe ekwahlulelweni. Ngoba nifile, futhi ukuphila kwenu kufihliwe noKristu Jesu ku-Adonai-YHVH. Lapho uKristu Jesu, ongukuphila kwenu, eyobonakala, khona-ke nani niyobonakala nami enkazimulweni.

Qondani nonke nina bakholwayo kuKristu Jesu ukuthi nginiyalile NONKE ukuba niphume emisebenzini yenyama futhi nisuke ezindleleni zeBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo eziholele ekwahlulelweni kwalo okuphindwe kabili komlilo, okuzodlulela kwezinye izizwe eziyizifebe ngokwentengiselwano nobuhwebo bazo.

Nokho abaningi benu ngokwenqaba ngenkani imiyalelo yami, echazwe ngamagama esimanje manje hhayi ngezilimi ezihunyushiwe, kodwa niyaqhubeka nihlezi ebumnyameni ngokungalaleli obala njengokungathi amaZwi ami angakhethwa ezindleleni zenu zokuba vithi. Aninazaba.

Lapho, esikhundleni sokukhonza iNkosi uJesu Kristu owathenga ukuphila kwenu esandleni sezitha zenu zaphakade, esikhundleni salokho nisebenzisa ukuhlengwa kwenu njengethuba lenyama futhi ngokuzenzakalelayo nahlala nikhonza labo abawileyo emisebenzini yenyama ngobuzenzisi bokuhlubuka okufanayo.

Qondani, nonke nina enizitika ngalawa maza angangcwele ezonweni zenu zenyama ekuluthekeni kwe-dopamine okuhlobene nemidlalo, ukuthi ngenxa yokungalaleli kwenu nobulukhuni benhliziyo benu obuthuka uKristu Jesu, ngeke ningene eMbusweni Wezulu ngalesi sikhathi! Futhi lokhu akungenxa yesenzo sokudlala umdlalo, kodwa imidlalo eniyidlalayo. Gwema imisebenzi yenyama ngazo zonke izindlela, funda ukuze wazi.

Esikhundleni salokho nizozithola ningalitholanga ifa futhi ninikelwe ngokuphelele emisebenzini yenyama ngesikhathi sosizi lukaJakobe. Kuleyo misebenzi yenyama kufanele niphenduke futhi nihlale nisesimweni sokuphenduka nokulungela njengoba kuyaliwe ukuze nginikhulule ngokugcwele ekwahlulelweni okunokhahlo okungahloselwe abalalelayo abakholwayo kuKristu Jesu. Kodwa okwabangalaleli nabanenhliziyo elukhuni abaMANJE benqaba isicelo sami sokugcina ngokuzibeka ngaphansi kwalokhu kwahlulela okufanayo. Selah.

Qondani, ningasebenzisa lezi zinsiza eziyingxenye ebalulekile yomphakathi wenu owileyo ngezinhloso zokulunga nokuphulukiswa nobunjalo obuphelele kanye nokufunda ubufakazi bami ngamaZwi Ami aqukethwe lapha kule ncwajana evuliwe engiyivulile esandleni sesithunywa sami nenceku. Ukuze nihambe ngokulunga phambi kwami ​​ngakho konke ukulalela kothando. Zonke izinto ngokulinganisela! Sukani ezonweni zenyama! Hhayi ukuthi ningalinganisela izono zenu zenyama, nakuba, abaningi benu benza lokho kanye. Dedani kukho konke okubonakala kukubi futhi nihlole zonke izinto, nibambelele kokuhle. Abalungile ngeke balidle ifa lombuso ka-Adonai-YHVH, nixwayiswe ngokugcwele MANJE.

Ukudlala imidlalo ngokwako akusona isono; nomqondo wako awusona isono. Nokho, ukuvuma ngokuzithandela imisebenzi yenyama njengoba abanye benza izono zabo ngenkathi nijatshuliswa ngezenzo zabo kuyafana nokwenza lezi zono mathupha nina. Akunandaba ukuthi iyiphi indlela yokungcola: Kungaba ithelevishini, izincwadi, amathebulethi, omakhalekhukhwini, nokunye okunjalo: Ngiyanitshela ngokusobala: Bonke labo abazitika ngokungcola bayoshiywa phakathi kwabangcolile, futhi lokho kuyathembiswa MANJE.

Qondani, nonke nina enisukile eBandleni laseLawodikeya nifuna ukubalwa phakathi kwabakholwayo kuKristu Jesu, njengoba kufanele. Kufanele nisuke ezindleleni zenu zokuba vithi zokungalaleli ngokwengxenye amaZwi ami futhi ninikele ngenhliziyo yenu yonke, umphefumulo, ingqondo namandla ngokunakekela okukhulu ukuze niqiniswe phakathi kwabakholwayo kuKristu Jesu. Ngoba akwanele ukushiya amabandla enu aseLawodikeya futhi nishintshe indawo yenu ukuze niqale impilo entsha kanye/noma ubuyena ngaphandle kokuphenduka okuphelele kuzo zonke izono zenu, anikwazi neze ukuqala impilo entsha ngoba lezi yizindlela zalabo abawileyo. Uma kukhona noyedwa kini ocabanga ngaphakathi kwenu ebubini benu ukuthi ngingadela lo mklomelo ngenxa yalesi sono, ngempela anikakulungeli ukungena eMbusweni Wezulu ngalesi sikhathi.

Qondani: Nonke nina abangakholwa kuKristu Jesu enifunda lawa maZwi: Kungaba niza kimi MANJE, nihlale ekuphendukeni okuphelele, lapho nivuma izono zenu kimi nixolise futhi niyeke ngokushesha, noma niyoshiyelwa emlilweni wokucwenga. Nginiyala ukuba nihlale ngokwethembeka ngaphansi kwegazi lami lokuhlawulela elizonihlanza ngokuphelele, kungenjalo, ningawanaki amaZwi ami nihlale ngaphansi kokulawulwa yila maza anesono futhi nibhekane nokwahlulelwa kwezoni okungahloselwe nina.

Abaningi benu bathanda kakhulu izinsiza zenu kangangokuthi anisamhoyi noKristu Jesu ingasaphathwa eyokuphendulela ukunaka kwenu kimi ukuze nisindiswe nina. Abanye benu bavumela lezi zinsiza eziqamba amanga ukuba zinitshele ukuthi amaZwi ami angcwele anikezwe kule Ncwajana yokwahlulela okulungile ayiqiniso ngokwengxenye, noma amanga aluhlaza anibopha eziphonweni nasebuqilini bazo lapho isazi esisodwa singavumelani nesinye, kuyilapho iningi lenu lingezona izazi ngakho ningazi kanjani? Noma ukuthi noma ngabe uyisazi sezazi, kunezinye izazi eziningi ezingavumelani, ngakho-ke, ubani ongalazi iqiniso. Kanjalo-ke, amazwi athi "bahlala befunda bengakwazi ukufinyelela olwazini lweqiniso" ami njengesilahlo saphakade sabo bonke abathanda ukwenza nokuphila amanga abo, noma amanga abanye ngokufanayo.

Zwanini amaZwi ami, ayikho imiklomelo eyingxenye futhi awekho amaholo anqanyuliwe futhi awekho ama-akhawunti okulondoloza embusweni Wezulu ngoba lezi zinto zenziwe zanyamalala phakade kini MANJE ekukhululweni kwenu okuphelele. Ngaphezu kwalokho, qondani, ekupheleni kwesonto lamashumi ayisikhombisa likaDaniyeli lezi zinto ziyobe ziphelile ekwahlulelweni komlilo okunikezwe u-Adonai-YHVH ngomyalo kaKristu Jesu kuze kube phakade.

Okuzokwenzeka kwabangakholwayo kulula: Ngeke bamukelwe efeni laphakade MANJE kodwa bazoshiywa nabahlulelweyo ukuze bazibonakalise bethembekile ngokufela igama lami. Uma bezithola befanele lolu dumo. Noma kunjalo, kubo bonke abanikezwa lolu dumo niyozuza insindiso uma nenqaba ukuyeka. Ngakho ngiyanincenga nonke othandweni lwami oluvutha njalo ngani: Phumani ngokuphelele kukho konke ukungalaleli MANJE futhi niyeke ngokushesha ukungayinaki imiyalelo yami ye-rhema kule ncwajana evuliwe ukuze nisuke ngokuphelele ezindleleni zeBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo, nifunde futhi niqonde amaZwi ami ningicele ukuba nginikhombise izimpilo zenu, nginithanda phakade naphakade!

Nina niyibandla LAMI: I-Ekklesia yami ebizelwe ngaphandle eqoqelwe futhi yavalwa ngezinhloso zami kulezi zikhathi zomusa eseziphelile MANJE. Abakholwayo kuKristu Jesu bathathwa kwa-Israyeli kuqalwa eJudiya ezinsukwini zikaPontius Pilatu, base beya kuzo zonke izizwe ngokufanayo ukuze ngempela lawo magatsha omnqumo wasendle MANJE axhunywe esihlahleni somnqumo semvelo kuze kube phakade naphakade. Lapho nixhunywe khona ku-Israyeli ka-Adonai-YHVH ngomyalo ka-Adonai-YHVH uqobo, okwenziwe ngezandla zami ezinamandla ngoba abanqobi bami abathembekile MANJE sebefakazelwe bethembekile kuKristu Jesu, THOKOZANI!

Hlalani Kimi, zithandwa zami, futhi ningavumeli izandla zenu zihlale zingcolile uma kwenzeka zingcola. Vumani izono zenu MANJE, ngilapha nani. Ngizozisusa kini nginihlanze ngokuphelele.

Kunjalo-ke, mina Yeshua HaMashiach, ngiza ukuzonitshela izizathu zemiphumela yenkathi yami yezizathu ezichaza izenzo zami, ukuze nidle le Ncwajana evuliwe engiyivulile esandleni sesithunywa sami nenceku, yebo iBiblaridion:144, nakuba imnandi emlonyeni futhi ibaba esiswini. Ukuze niqonde intando yami ezimpilweni zenu kuzo zonke izindlela zenu, nokuthi niqonde le nkathi yokugcina emisiweyo kaMANJE enikezwe ukuze nonke nisuke ngokuphelele ezonweni zenyama, noma niyohlala emlilweni wami wokucwenga ukuze nibize egameni lami kuze kube sekupheleni, noma intando yenu iyowela ekwahlulelweni kwaphakade echibini lomlilo uma nihlubuka ngokuphelele. Ngoba ayikho enye inketho, yilesi-ke isiphetho.

UBiblaridion:144 ungumuntu olungileyo ngokulunga kwami ​​ngokwami ​​okunikezwe emoyeni wami ohlanzayo nasekuyalweni okukhulu nasekuhlanzweni ebuhleni bezindlela ZAMI zika-Adonai-YHVH wakhe, okunikezwe ngezandla zikaYise uYeshua. Futhi ngalezi zindlela, utholakala ethembekile futhi engumnqobi onamandla owanqoba hhayi uHeylel Ben Shachar kuphela, (owamhlasela ngokuqondile), kodwa nalaba balindi abawileyo abangangcwele ababenikezwe inkululeko futhi bamhlasela nabo. Nokho ngomusa wami nesihe sami nomlilo wami wokucwenga uBiblaridion:144 uwe kasikhombisa, kanye nakasikhombisa futhi, kodwa, usabuya ngokwethembeka ukuze ame ngezinyawo zakhe eqinile ngomyalo wami, futhi ezinikele kakhulu enhliziyweni kukho konke ukulalela okulungile engalindele lutho oluhle njengembuyiselo, nokho ehamba ngokukholwa okuphelele njengoba mina ngedwa ngimphakela ngezandla zabanye.

Mina, Kristu Jesu, enginguYeshua HaMashiach, ngahlukanisa uBiblaridion:144 enkonzweni yami engayimisa eBandleni lami lapho ngambeka khona ukuba ngimfundise izindlela zami nendlela yokukhonza ngempela umuntu wakubo ngothando, ngibaxolela esikhundleni sokuhlasela lapho kuqala isilonda sangaphakathi futhi ngempela azisekho izilonda ezisele kuBiblaridion:144 kodwa ungufakazi oqotho ezintweni zonke. Ngamfundisa nokuhlala ematafuleni esidlo sakusihlwa esihlelekile nokufinyelela kwabaphansi kakhulu emigwaqweni engemuva. Ngabe sengimthatha ngamfihla kini nonke ukuze ngimlanze emlilweni wami wokucwenga kukho konke okwenziwa kuye nokumlanza ezonweni zakhe ezaziningi futhi zimbi.

Qondani ukuthi izono awela kuzo zabangelwa yilokho okwenziwa kuye nodadewabo ngokungemthetho ngamakhemikhali yilezi zandla zabalindi abawileyo beseyizinceku ngaphambi kweminyaka yokuziphendulela. Lezi zinto zenziwa kuye ngokungemthetho ezinhlelweni zokusebenza kwengqondo eziyimfihlo nezikhethiwe ezifakwe yila balindi abangangcwele ngaphakathi kwalezi zikole zemfihlakalo ezibizwa ngokungeyikho ngokuthi imfundo yomphakathi, okuyizikhungo ezingcolile ezisabalele kulo lonke iBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo. Wadakwa kakhulu ngezidakamizwa esemncane futhi waphoqwa, wabe esevuma futhi wabamba iqhaza ekuqeqeshweni kwabo ngemvume yakhe yobusha yobuwula. Lapho athola khona umusa ngenxa yobusha bakhe nokubhujiswa komndeni wakhe ngenxa yamathonya amabi angaphandle.

Eqinisweni wanikelwa kulaba balindi abangangcwele nguyise owayekholelwa emangeni abo okuthi wayezenziwa indoda engcono nephumelelayo ngaphansi kokunakekelwa kwabo futhi babezosebenza njengomfowabo omkhulu ukumxazulula emphakathini futhi wayezobona amamaki amahle ngenxa yalokho. Bamhlukanisa ngezindlela eziningana ezazivunyelwe kulolu chungechunge lwezehlakalo futhi bamqeqesha emakhonweni amaningana obuthakathi kanye nobuciko bokulwa obathuntubezwa futhi bavinjwa ngokulunga ngenxa yezinhloso zami.

Noma kunjalo, u-Adonai-YHVH wayelwazi kahle lolu chungechunge lwezehlakalo kusukela ngaphambi kokusekelwa komhlaba futhi esikhundleni sokudonsa uBiblaridion:144 ezandleni zabo, esikhundleni salokho wamvumela ukuba akhuthazelele lokho kanye kuphela okwakufanela izinhloso ezicacile zika-Adonai-YHVH wami kuYeshua HaMashiach ukuze kuqiniswe futhi kwakhiwe lesi sitsha esinamandla sokwahlulela kwesiphathimandla nempi. Nokho, mina, Kristu Jesu, ngazifihla kubo bonke abathintekayo kodwa ngahlala naye sonke isikhathi. Noma kunjalo, wenziwa waba yingozi futhi waba nekhono lokukhonza kwakhe okuhloselwe uHeylel Ben Shachar, owalethwa kuye yibo laba balindi abawileyo ababalulwe kulobu bufakazi. Othandweni lwami ngaye ngimgcwalise ngothando nobubele nokumvikela futhi ngamphulukisa, ngisho nokudala kabusha ingqondo yakhe othandweni lwami ngaye. Ngeqiniso, ungumalusi othembekileyo ofakazele iZwi lakhe kimi ngoba ubonisile ukuthi ngingathembela kuye ngempela futhi.

Ngakho lokhu kwavunyelwa ukubakhombisa bonke ukuthi akunakwenzeka ukuba noma yimuphi wabo ahlwithe noma ngubani ezandleni ZAMI EZINAMANDLA futhi ukuze noBiblaridion:144 abelane nami ekuhluphekeni kwami futhi uhluphekile kakhulu futhi uhluphekile isikhathi eside, nokho usamile ngokulalela eceleni kwami engalindele lutho njengembuyiselo.

Ngakho-ke, kwavunyelwa ukuba aqeqeshwe buthule nangasese ezindleleni zabo zobuqili nezempi, kodwa aqashwe isandla sami ngoba ngamvala uphawu uBiblaridion:144 esebusha ngaphambi kokuba angene emfundweni yomphakathi, lapho yena nodadewabo bahlonzwa futhi baqondiswa kulezi zikole zemfihlakalo ngenxa yalokho okubizwa ngokuthi ukuzwakala kwabo kwesiphiwo, okwakungukuthi empeleni kuwuphawu lomoya ongcwele olwalubekwe phezu kwezimpilo zabo yisandla sami kanye namakhono emvelo aklanywe u-Adonai-YHVH kulezi zitsha zombili, ezikhethiwe futhi zigcinelwe izinhloso zami. Kanjalo-ke, laba balindi abawileyo bamamukela ngenjabulo futhi abazange bongaye imali emizamweni yabo yokuphula lolo phawu olungcwele lokulunga engimvale ngalo. Kuyo yonke imizamo yabo bonke bahluleka kabuhlungu njengoba kufakazelwa yile ncwajana evuliwe nalokho okuzofezwa ngezandla zikaBiblaridion:144 ekwahlulelweni kwalaba balindi abawileyo okuMANJE.

Kufanele niqonde ukuthi wayevela emndenini owawuhlonziwe, okuyinto evamile emazweni ase-AmeruKa, futhi abaningi benu bavela ezimweni ezifanayo.

Udade wabo, owahlukunyezwa esemncane ngaphambi kokuba afinyelele eminyakeni yokuziphendulela, uzokuma endlini yami ngenxa yezinto azibekezelela esemncane ngenxa yalezi zithunywa eziwileyo ezifanayo, nangenxa yalokho ayekuphoqelelwa ngokungenalwazi ngonya esemncane, okwamlimaza kakhulu kangangokuba akazange alulame, waze akabe esakhathalela ukuphila ngoba wazisola ngokulahlekelwa ingane yakhe eyathathwa kuye ngokungemthetho esonweni.

Ngakho-ke, sengibaxolele bonke abeza kimi kulolu daba bezofuna intethelelo ngaphansi kwegazi lami elihlawulayo, ngoba eKhalvari ngakhokhela nezono lezi zithunywa eziwileyo ezaziyozidala kanye nazo zonke izinto ezithanda ukuzenza ngamakhonsathi azo nezidakamizwa ezithinta ingqondo.

Qondani ukuthi umusa nomhawu kuboniswa ngale ndlela ukuze kungabikho muntu owela ekwahlulelweni ngenxa yezinhloso zika-Adonayi-YHWH, ngaphandle kwalapho ukwahlulelwa kumfanele ngenxa yobulukhuni benhliziyo yakhe. Futhi ekulungeni okuphelele nasebulungiseni obuphelele akukho ukukhetha ubuso kulezi zinqumo, kodwa ukwahlulela okuphelele okunikezwa ngu-Adonayi-YHWH okusekelwe eWundlwini lika-Adonayi-YHWH okuyogcwaliseka ngokuphelele ngeNgonyama yeSizwe sakwaJuda. Ngakho-ke, duduzelelani kakhulu ngabathandekayo benu enicabanga ukuthi balahlekile kodwa empeleni baphawuliwe, balele emhlabathini bezovuswa ngaphambi kwenu nina eniphilayo nisasele. Sela.

Ngakho-ke, uzokuma endlini yami ngokufanayo ngoba ezinkingeni nasezishayweni zakhe waphenduka ngokuphelele emizuzwini yakhe yokugcina, futhi kulezo zenzo ngemicabango nezinhloso zenhliziyo, uthethelelwe futhi uyofeza izinhloso zakhe zaphakade ngokufanayo. Wayazi kahle kamhlophe ukuthi nganginaye ngoba ngamembulela isimo sami esikhanyayo ukuze ngimkhuthaze futhi ngimazise ukuthi aphumule, ukuthi wayemane ekhungathekiswe futhi efakwe ushevu ngabantu ababi abathunyelwe yilezi zithunywa eziwileyo ezifanayo ezizowela ekwahlulelweni kwaphakade ekupheleni kwesonto lamashumi ayisikhombisa likaDaniyeli, futhi izikhathi zazo sezifikile ngempela.

Ngakho-ke, qondani futhi nikhuthazeke kakhulu ngoba ngizohawukela engimhawukelayo, ngenxa yokuthi izindlela zami ziyizindlela zika-Adonayi-YHWH wami ohlale ecebile ngomusa nangomhawu futhi ohlale emi ukuze aphakamise labo abanqotshiwe. Ngisho nalabo okuthi ezinhliziyweni nasemiqondweni yabo bangafuni lutho ngaphandle kokunqoba zonke izinto, kodwa izimpilo zabo zibonakale zinqanyulwa. Noma kunjalo, akunakwenzeka ukuthi noma yimuphi wenu ahlwithwe ezandleni zami, kungakhathaliseki ukuthi izinto zingabonakala zinjani.

Qondani ukuthi ngingubani: ngiyiwundlu lika-Adonayi-YHWH futhi bonke ngaphandle kwamadodana okungalaleli bangaphenduka, ngoba inceku ayihlali endlini yesono kuze kube phakade kodwa iNdodana ihlala. Ngenxa yale njongo, iNdodana ka-Adonayi-YHWH ibonakalisiwe ukuze ngikhulule ngokuphelele futhi ngichithe noma yimuphi umkhondo ebusweni bomhlaba wayo yonke imisebenzi kaHeylel Ben Shachar. Kunjalo-ke: Ngamandla omoya wami ongcwele wesithembiso, enginibumba ngawo nonke, ngiyayichitha imisebenzi kaHeylel Ben Shachar ezimpilweni zenu, kungakhathaliseki ukuthi nihlaselwa futhi niboshwe ngayiphi indlela.

Ngakho-ke, qondani, ukuthi mina Jesu waseNazaretha, engiphinde ngibe yiwundlu lika-Adonayi-YHWH, ngeza ukuzosindisa izoni, iningi lenu eliyizikhulu zazo. Kunjalo-ke, ukuthi u-Adonayi-YHWH ukhethe ukwenza izibonelo ngani nonke ngomusa nomhawu wakhe awuthele ngobuningi emhlabeni ngoKristu Jesu, ophinde abe nguYeshuwa HaMashiyakhi. Kanjalo-ke, ngezinye izikhathi ngiyehlukanisa futhi ngiphakamise izibonelo phakathi kwabathembekileyo bami ukuze nonke nazi ubuhle baphakade buka-Adonayi-YHWH, u-El Shaddayi Omkhulu!

Kunjalo-ke, bantwana bami abalahlekileyo senibuyele ku-Adonayi-YHWH wenu ngokulalela kothando futhi nahlala nithembekile kunoma yimuphi umsebenzi noma imisebenzi enayinikwa yizandla zami. Ngakho-ke, MANJE ngizoniphakamisa nonke nginiqondise ngqo egumbini lesihlalo sobukhosi sika-Adonayi-YHWH!

Futhi le United States of AmeruKa iyisizwe esinqunyiwe impande MANJE esiyowela esahlulelweni esinzima nesaphakade esiphindwe kabili esingeke sisiphunyuke noma silulame kuso. Nokho, ngaphambi kokuba ihora elilodwa nesilo likhululwe ngokugcwele ngokucwayiza kweso lokho okungamemeza, futhi lelo cilongo liyokhaliswa MANJE. Ngoba ngeke ngishiye abathembekileyo kuKristu emlilweni wokucwenga. Nokho, bonke labo abasalele emlilweni wokucwenga abalahliwe kodwa bayosala ngemuva ngezinjongo zami zensindiso eziyofezeka kubo, ngakho-ke, qhubekani nokunxusela kakhulu.

Manje-ke kunjalo, lobu bufakazi bufika ekupheleni ngoba le nkathi kaMANJE ifikile futhi manje isilengiswe ngokugcwele phezu komhlaba ngomyalo ka-Adonai-YHVH onikezwe kuKristu Jesu onguYeshua HaMashiach futhi. Hhayi ukuthi umlomo wesithunywa sami esinamandla nenceku yami usuvaliwe manje futhi hhayi ukuthi noYeshua HaMashiach ngeke esakhuluma futhi. Kodwa ukuthi le nkathi kaMANJE izowa emhlabeni ngokuzuma nangokungalindelekile futhi ngokuphelele manje ngehora elilodwa nesilo.

Ngakho-ke, hlalani nilungile futhi nizifihle kule ncekelelo ezayo eniyibona ihlangana obala nemininingwane eyofezeka ncamashi imenyezelwe manje kule Ncwadi Encane YokuLunga futhi ngiyayala bonke labo abatholwa yila maZwi ukuba bazicwilise kula maZwi alungiselelwe ukuthola amehlo, izindlebe nezinhliziyo zabo bonke labo athunyelwe kubo.

Futhi-ke, ngiza ngalesi sikhathi sikaMANJE ukuze nilungiselwe ukungena emaZulwini ngakho-ke ngiyaniyala nonke ukuba niye emakamelweni enu oMshado futhi nikhulumisane nami kulesi siphetho. Ngoba MANJE yisikhathi enithathwa ngaso, futhi MANJE yisikhathi lapho sinomzuzwana owodwa owengeziwe wokuba niphume ngokugcwele ezonweni zenu ukuze ninqobe kuzo zonke izinto futhi namukele umvuzo wenu ophelele esandleni sami esilangazelelayo.

NginguKristu Jesu onguYeshua HaMashiach futhi ngithumele la maZwi ngesithunywa sami esinamandla nenceku yami futhi ngesithunywa sami esinamandla nenceku yami la maZwi athunyelwe.

Ngisho nokuthi nibe nokulindela okukhulu komvuzo ovela esandleni sami ngakho konke enikwenzile ngokwethembeka enkonzweni. Futhi ukuze niqhubeke nibone futhi niqonde izihe zami eziyonikwa labo eziyonikwa bona ngomyalo ka-Adonai-YHVH. Ukuze kungabikho muntu ovalwe uphawu ngesandla sikaKristu Jesu ongahlwithwa noma ukuthi omunye wenu angakhululwa ezandleni zami. Lokho akunakwenzeka neze. Futhi-ke ngize ukuzokhombisa lezi zinto kumuntu ka-Biblaridion:144 owaboniswa futhi kumuntu kaShawuli, lowo umnqumo wasendle ombiza ngoPawulu, oyohlala ngakwesokunene sami ngoba unikezwa isandla sokunene sobuhlobo, futhi-ke yilokho okushiwo yilelo binzana emaBhayibhelini enu.

Hlalani nilungile futhi nihlale ngaphansi kwegazi lami lokuhlawulela kuzo zonke izinto nangazo zonke izindlela. Gxilisani ukunaka kwenu ngokugcwele kimi manje futhi niqonde ukuthi kufanele niphume kulo mhlaba ngenhliziyo nangenhloso ngoba uzowa ngokuphelele nangokuzuma ngehora elilodwa nesilo futhi isikhathi yiMANJE.
