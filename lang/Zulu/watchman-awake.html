Ephreli 05, 2024

Mlindi, vuka!

Mlindi, vuka, ubhince ukhalo lwakho ngeqiniso, ugqoke isivikelo sesifuba sokulunga!

Mlindi, vuka, uzithathele isigqoko sensindiso nenkemba kaMoya, eyiZwi likaNkulunkulu!

Mlindi, vuka, izinyawo zakho zembathiswe ukulungela iVangeli lokuthula!

Yala abantu bami ungathuli, njengoba ngikhulule ulimi lwakho! Lwela abantu bami, uxwayise abakhethiweyo bami, ngokuba uyokwamukela umvuzo womprofethi uma nje ukhuluma amazwi engizokunika wona. VUKA! Angishongo yini kubantu bami ukuba balindele ukuza kwami? Izikhathi nezinkathi azibonakali yini futhi azikho phezu kwenu? Ngakho-ke, bhekani ukubonakala kwami, ngokuba sekusondele. Bhekani izibonakaliso nezimangaliso esibhakabhakeni, ngokuba uBaba wami wamisa imizila yazo kusukela ekuqaleni, nosuku lubekiwe. Bhekani ukukhanya kwami esibhakabhakeni njengoba icilongo selizokhaliswa; bhekani, ngikushilo.

Bantu bami, hlolani iZwi lami nibheke emiBhalweni yami eNgcwele ukuze niqonde ukuthi ngizomhlwibela umakoti wami ekwahlulelweni kwababi. Ngizovikela umakoti wami nomzimba wami; ngumuphi umuntu owake wazonda inyama yakhe, kunalokho akayondli futhi ayinakekele? Bhekani, qaphelani, naka izinkathi, nazi ukuthi izikhathi sezifikile nosuku luyosa ngokuzuma njengelanga elikhulu eliphuma ngokuzuma emkhathizwe. Ngeke ngiphikwe, futhi ngeke ngivinjwe. Nakuba isela liyozama, liyobhonga njengengonyama; kodwa aliyilutho ngaphandle kwengonyama engenamazinyo. Aliyukungivimba, futhi ngeke ngincishwe impahla yami, ngokuba ngiwuthengile umhlaba futhi ikakhulukazi abantu bami ngegazi lami elingenasono. Negama lami limi ngaphezu kwakho konke ngaphandle kwelikaBaba, omkhulu kunami; futhi ngeke ngiphikwe, ngithiywe, noma ngivinjwe.

Ngiyeza, futhi ngeke ngithule, intukuthelo yami iyavutha ngeBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo, yebo, iBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo elihweba ngabancane libanikele ezandleni zabantu ababi, abazomiswa. Kungcono ukube abazalwanga kunokuba bazalwe benze izono ezingenamthetho nezibuhlungu ezinganeni. Kungcono ukube kwaboshelwa itshe lokusila entanyeni yabo, baphonswe olwandle; kodwa bagcinelwe umlilo waphakade, futhi ubulungiswa buzokwenziwa masinyane, njengoba intukuthelo yeWundlu isizogubuzela wonke umhlaba nosizi lukaJakobe luqala lapho umvimbeli esusiwe.

Ziqiniseni, bantu bami, nime niqine kimi ngenhliziyo eyodwa nangenjongo eyodwa. Lahla izinto zezwe enizifisile, nimise mina, iNkosi uJesu Kristu, njengesifiso senu; ngokuba ngiyinqaba yenu eqinile, idwala lokuvikela, nomqaphi wenu wasemuva. Ngubani oyovukela nina ongamelana nesandla sami esinamandla? Abakhethiweyo bami nabathembekileyo basezandleni zami ezeluliwe, futhi akekho ongabahlwitha kimi. Ningabami, futhi niphephile kimi; ngakho-ke phumulani kimi emisebenzini yenu, ngokuba izikhathi zomvuzo ziyofika ngokuzuma. Umvuzo wokwahlulela ababi usufikile, nomvuzo wabathembekileyo bami uphezu kwabakhethiweyo bami, njengoba ngizobavuza ngefa labo, ngokuba ngibenze bafaneleka.

O, wena drago ongenamsebenzi, ongenamazinyo; wena nyoka yasendulo: isikhathi sakho sesifikile nokwahlulelwa kuphezu kwakho. Nakuba ucabanga ngokuzidla ukuthi uyonqoba, ngizothatha konke okubuthele ezandleni zakho zobusela, nalabo abakulandelayo ekuhaleni kwabo bazokwabelana nawe ngefa lakho echibini lomlilo, lapho wonke umuntu eyovuzwa ngokwemisebenzi yakhe ngokuphindwe kaningi. Intukuthelo kaNkulunkulu iyohlala phezu kwakho kuze kube phakade, futhi ngeke uphumule.

Isijeziso esikhulu siyoba ngumduduzi wakho, intuthu ebabayo yesibabule esivuthayo iyohlala emakhaleni akho, futhi uyokhokha kuze kube phakade. Intuthu yokuhlushwa kwakho iyokhuphuka phakade, nabo bonke abakulandelayo kusukela komncane kuze kube komkhulu bayoba nefa nomvuzo wabo kanye nawe emalangabini ashisayo aluhlaza okwesibhakabhaka ayokumboza phakade njengoba uyoboshwa uphonswe ekudalelweni kwakho. Kuyoba nokukhala okukhulu nokugedla amazinyo. Futhi nicabanga ukuthi ningamelana noYahweh noKristu wakhe, kodwa niyokwenziwa ize nibe yinhlekisa yaphakade ekuhlushweni kwenu okungapheli, futhi niyovalelwa phakade. Ngeke niphinde nilimaze indalo yami, futhi niyokhohlwa ngokuphelele, futhi ngeke nikhunjulwe.

Ngakho bantu bami, makoti wami othembekileyo engimthanda kakhulu, yini eniyoyesaba? Intukuthelo yami nentukuthelo kaBaba wenu akuyona eyenu futhi ngeke yehlele phezu kwenu, futhi ngeke nibe sengozini yokufa kwesibili. Ngakho ningesabi njengoba ngizothatha impindiselo ephelele ezitheni zenu, ngokuba impindiselo ngeyami, isho iNkosi! Anazi yini ukuthi niyokwahlulela izingelosi? Anazi yini ukuthi niyokwahlulela umhlaba? Ngakho zihlanzeni kimi, ngokuba igazi lami lokuhlawulela kuphela eliyonenza nifaneleke ukuma phambi kwami enkazimulweni. Bopha izwi lami ezinhliziyweni zenu, nigcine imibhalo yami emabunzini enu, niphumule emazwini ami asendulo; ngokuba angukuphila kini, nokuphila kwenu kufihliwe kimi, futhi ngiyokwembula!

O, Bhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo, ukube wawuqondile umnikelo wokuthula uBaba wami akuthumele wona, manje usungilahlile mina, uJesu waseNazaretha, futhi kwenzekile ngokwemibhalo engcwele ukuthi abantu ababi nabakhohlisi bayoqhubeka beba babi kakhulu. Kodwa wena, o, Bhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo elikhulu, ungcolise uthingo lwezulu lukaBaba wami olunemibala eminingi alwunika uNowa nabo bonke abantu njengesethembiso sokuthi ngeke aphinde abhubhise wonke umhlaba ngamanzi, futhi uyoligcina izwi lakhe. Kodwa wena, o, Bhabhiloni Eliyimfihlakalo, uyochithwa ngehora elilodwa.

Umlilo omkhulu uyokwehlela phezu kwakho; ukuzamazama okukhulu kuyokugudluza; imibhoshongo yakho iyokuwa; ingqalasizinda yakho iyonyamalala ibe yincithakalo, namagagasi amakhulu ayogubuzela ugu lwakho, futhi ngizokuvuza ngokwemisebenzi yakho, nokwahlulelwa okukhulu kumelene nawe. Ngokuba wake waba yisibani sethemba nokukhanya emhlabeni isikhashana, kodwa ukhethe ukuhaha, ubugovu, nenkohlakalo njengabalingani bakho, wena sifakazi esikhulu! Ngubani oyokusindisa kimi nasentukuthelweni yami nasolaka lwami olusondelayo oluphezu kwakho? Umi uboshiwe, ulindele ukwahlulelwa kwakho kokugcina, okuyobonakala emhlabeni wonke, nokwesaba okukhulu kuyofikela abaningi.

Bheka! Ziningi izizwe uBaba wami aziketulile ekwahluleleni, ngokuba umisa esinye ahlise esinye, ngokuba uYahweh unobukhosi futhi akekho omkhulu kunoNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade omela abakhe futhi abasindise emlilweni obekelwe ababi. Bheka! Izizwe ezakha imibuso emikhulu zisekhona, kodwa ngendlela encishisiwe, kodwa ziyaqhubeka. Kodwa wena, sifakazi esikhulu, uzidayisile nezithandani eziningi ngenxa yokuhaha okubi nenjabulo yesono. Uholele abantu bami kude nami emhlabeni wonke ngenxa yobubi obunikeze izizwe ukuba zidle ziphuze, futhi uyochithwa ngokuphelele, ngeke uphinde ube khona. Nasemlotheni wakho ngeke kuvele isizwe esikhulu, kodwa insali iyosindiswa. Isandla sesitha sisentanyeni yakho, futhi ngilethe izinsalela emingceleni yakho. Ziyophanga, zihlwithe, zithathe okusele. Ngubani oyokusiza?

Ngokuba umphikile umakhelwane wakho, futhi akukho ukwesaba iNkosi ezinhliziyweni zakho. Uthe, angisiye umfelokazi, kodwa ungumfelokazi, uyimpumputhe futhi uyisithulu phambi kwami. Alukho usindiso lwakho ngenxa yokuphinga kwakho okwenzile nomhlaba; ngakho-ke, ngizokulethela incithakalo ephelele. Izenzakalo ezizayo ziyokwenzeka ngokushesha okukhulu; bese kuba nokhukhula olukhulu oluyogubuzela iziketi zakho. Umlilo uyokushisa ngokuphelele futhi uhlanze ababi ezweni, nabathunjwa bami bayokhululwa, futhi akukho okungavimba lokho osekuzokwenzeka. NginguNkosi uJesu Kristu, futhi nginitshela ngaphambi kokuba kwenzeke, futhi ngimemezela lokho okuzokwenzeka sengathi sekwenzekile, ngokuba ngokuqinisekile lawa mazwi anjalo, futhi akukho okungawaguqula, njengoba iziphrofetho ziyogcwaliseka.

Qinisani, bantu bami, njengoba ngizomhlwibela umakoti wami; ngokuba nginezinhlelo nezinjongo ngaye embusweni wami ozayo. Uyohlala enami njalo, futhi ngeke ahluke kimi. Ngiyamthanda futhi ngiyamnakekela othandweni oluphelele nozwelo. Ngizokwenza ube namandla enkazimulweni yami, futhi uyokwenza imisebenzi emikhulu ebufeni bakho obuzayo, ngakho ungesabi. Yazi ukuthi inhliziyo yami iyaphuka, futhi ngidabukile ngabantu bami abangazilungiselelanga. Abanye bayosala ngemuva ngoba unkulunkulu waleli zwe uphuphuthekise izingqondo zabo, futhi abaningi abakalungi. Ungesabi, nakuba uyongikhuthazelela kakhulu, nonke niyosindiswa nina eninophawu lukaNkulunkulu ophilayo. Ngizoninakekela uma ningaliphiki igama lami, uma, futhi ningisebenzela emsebenzini engininike wona.

Niyokwenza imisebenzi emikhulu uma nje ningicela ukuba nginithume. Nginibizile futhi nginithanda kakhulu, nalabo ababiza igama lami bakhuthazele kuze kube sekupheleni bayosindiswa bavuswe enkazimulweni enkulu futhi bavuzwe kuze kube phakade ngokuma kwenu, njengoba nginithanda futhi ngikusho ezindlebeni zenu. Kukhona izivuno eziningana futhi ngasinye ngesikhathi saso, ngakho ungesabi; ngokuba ezikhathini ezinzima kuhlale kunikezwa abantu abakhulu bakaNkulunkulu ukwelusa umhlambi, futhi nginibizile. Izingelosi zami ziyobeka uphawu kwabayizi-144,000, futhi abanye benu bayobasiza emisebenzini nasekuthunyweni kwabo engibanike khona. Ningesabi, ngokuba ngiyokwenza izibonakaliso, izimangaliso, nezimangaliso eziningi ngezandla zenu, ngakho phumulani othandweni lwami niphumule emandleni ami; ngokuba nginani futhi ngeke nginishiye noma nginilahle.

Niyokwazi ukuthi ngimhlwibile umakoti wami, futhi ngizobe ngibuthele abathembekileyo kimi ukuze ngiveze ukuphenduka kwabaningi ezweni elifayo. Ngakho-ke vumelani umhlaba udlule ningahlanganyeli enhlekeleleni nasekudalelweni kwawo ngokuba akukona okwenu uma nisazongcwelisa iNkosi ezinhliziyweni zenu futhi niphile ngokwahlukaniselwe mina. Ngokuba ububi obukhulu buyasondela, futhi abukaze buvele ngale ndlela, futhi ngeke buphinde buvele, bantwana bami abancane, ngokuba ngikumemezelile. Tholani induduzo endleleni enginibekele yona, nigcine izinyawo zenu ziqinile endleleni. Ngiyoba nani futhi nginiphe ukuthula okudlula ukuqonda, futhi niyoba nokuthula phakathi kwezwe lesiphithiphithi, ukuthula okuyonigcina uma ningaphambukeli kwesobunxele noma kwesokudla.

Ningaluthathi uphawu, futhi ningakholelwa amanga. Bayonikeza ukuthula nempilo nokuphepha kulabo abathatha uphawu, futhi bayonitshela ukuthi ningena ezweni elisha lokuthula nokuchuma nokuphepha. Ningawakholwa amanga enyoka, ngokuba inkosi yabo ngeke iligcine izwi layo. Lowo muntu wesono unomgomo owodwa kuphela, futhi lowo uthi: chitha.

Alikho iqiniso kuye, futhi uSathane uqobo uyolawula izintambo zakhe. Ngeke kube khona ukuthula okuvela kuye, kodwa lonke izwi liyoba ngamanga athobayo, aklanyelwe ukuhola umhlaba ekubhujisweni okuphelele. Futhi ukube angizifinyezanga izinsuku, akukho nyama eyayosinda esishayweni ayosiletha ezweni, ngokuba uyophiwa amandla okuqedela izinhloso zami isikhashana. Bese eboshwa ngamaketango anzima obumnyama kwalasha ukuze akhululwe isikhashana ukukhohlisa umhlaba okokugcina. Lapho esebabuthele kuye, bayohlasela lowo muzi omkhulu, nomlilo ovela kuNkulunkulu uyobabhubhisa bonke. Bese kuba nokwahlulelwa kokugcina, yebo, ukwahlulelwa kwesihlalo sobukhosi esikhulu esimhlophe kuyofika kubo bonke, futhi bayoba ngaphandle kwekhambi. Nginitshela lezi zinto ukuze nginiphe ithemba futhi ngilungise abantu bami, ngokuba iMfihlakalo Enkulu kaNkulunkulu nayo iyofezwa.

Nginguyena indlela, neqiniso, nokuphila.

Ngiyinkosi yamakhosi noMbusi wababusi.

Nginguyena ofanele ukuvula izimpawu, futhi izikhathi sezifikile.

Nginguyena ovivinya izintambo zabakhethiweyo bami ukuze nginihlanze futhi nginigeze ngegazi lami lokuhlawulela.

NgiyiNdodana kaNkulunkulu eyafika ngenyama, yabethelwa, yavuswa ngosuku lwesithathu, yabonwa ngabaningi, yamukelwa ezulwini phambi kwamehlo abo.

Ngiyindodana kaDavide, futhi ngizobutha insali kaJakobe ngaphansi kwezingalo zami zokududuza namandla.

Nginguyena oyogcwalisa yonke intando kaBaba.

Sengizokwenza izitha zami isenabelo sezinyawo zami, futhi maye kulabo abangiphikisayo, ngokuba inkulu intukuthelo yeWundlu.

Nginguyena oyothobisa indalo kaBaba wami futhi anikele zonke izinto esandleni sakhe, ukuze uNkulunkulu abe yikho konke kukho konke.

Nginguyena ombuso wakhe ungowaphakade naphakade, futhi ngiyonika abakhethiweyo bami ukuba badle emthini wokuphila futhi baphuze emifuleni yamanzi aphilayo, futhi ngeke niphinde nilambe noma nome, futhi ngiyosula zonke izinyembezi emehlweni enu.

Mina, iNkosi, ngikushilo, futhi ngiyokwenza kube njalo. Isikhathi sesifikile, ngakho lindelani ukubonakala kwami, njengoba kungekho okungavimba isandla sami esinamandla.
