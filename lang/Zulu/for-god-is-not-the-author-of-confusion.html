Ngokuba UNkulunkulu Akasiye Owesiphithiphithi!

Ngokuba uNkulunkulu akasiye owesiphithiphithi, kodwa ngowokuthula, njengakuwo onke amabandla abangcwele! Fundani lawa mazwi nililalele izwi lami. Thobani indlebe yenu, nizwe amazwi abahlakaniphileyo, nibeke inhliziyo yenu olwazini lwami. Mhlawumbe kufanele nilandele isibonelo sami ensimini lapho ngathandaza ngikhathazekile ngawa phansi phambi kukaBaba ngomthandazo nangokuthobeka, wangiqinisa ukuze ngamukele indebe yolaka yezono zenu uqobo, engazithwalela nina, ngokuba nginamandla okuzithwala. Ngikhuluma nabazidlayo eBandleni lami, futhi ngikhuluma nokwahlukana okuningi phakathi kwenu. Pho, wahlulelelani umfowenu na? Nokuthi wamenzelani ize umfowenu na? Ngokuba sonke siyakuma phambi kwesihlalo sokwahlulela sikaKristu. Akulotshiwe yini encwadini engayithumela kubalandeli beNdlela eRoma? Kodwa uthini uZakariya ngaleyo ndaba: Kepha benqaba ukulalela, bamhudulela emuva ihlombe, bavala izindlebe zabo ukuze bangezwa. Anifundanga yini u-Isaya, nina bantwanyana bami? Ngokuba bathini abaprofethi basendulo? Ngokuba u-Isaya uyaphinda uthi: Ngokuzwa niyakuzwa, ningaqondi; nokubona niyakubona, ningaboni.

Bantwana bami, nginikhombisile indlela enhle kakhulu, kodwa abaningi benu bawe emseni futhi ukuzidla kwenu okukhulu kunenze naziphakamisa ngokweqile. Nokho, amazwi ami engawanika uMphostoli uPawulu kwabaseGalathiya asho ngokusobala: Ngokuba uma umuntu ezitshela ukuthi uyinto, engento yalutho, uyazikhohlisa. Bantwanyana bami, ngiyazi ukuthi nishiseka ngenhliziyo ukungikhonza, futhi ngiyazi ukuthi niyangithanda, kodwa ngezinye izikhathi hhayi ngokokolwazi. Aniqondi yini ukuthi uNkulunkulu uyamelana nabazidlayo kodwa abathobekileyo uyabanika umusa? Ngakho-ke, zithobeni phansi kwesandla sikaNkulunkulu esinamandla, ukuze aniphakamise ngesikhathi esifaneleyo. Ngokunjalo, nina bantu abasha, zithobeni kubadala benu. Yebo, nonke zithobeleni omunye nomunye, nembathiswe ukuthobeka. UNkulunkulu uyamelana nabazidlayo kodwa abathobekileyo uyabanika umusa. Ningacabangi ukuthi amazwi ami ayaphindaphinda, ngokuba ngomlomo wofakazi ababili noma abathathu makuqiniswe izwi lonke. Qondani, bantwana bami, aninakungikhonza ngenyama yenu: Ngokuba konke okusezweni, inkanuko yenyama, nenkanuko yamehlo, nokuzidla kokuphila, akuveli kuBaba, kodwa kuvela ezweni. Bandla lami, zithobeleni omunye nomunye ngengqondo embethe ukuthobeka, ngokuba kungaba yimina oninika amazwi okukhuluma nogqozi lokunifundisa, nakuba abaningi benu beyobeka eceleni izwi lothobekileyo esikhundleni sedumela nomuntu othile. Yibani abalandeli bakaNkulunkulu, bantwana bami, ngokuba ngeqiniso uNkulunkulu akabheki ubuso bamuntu.

Bhekani! Kuyinkazimulo kaNkulunkulu ukufihla into: kodwa udumo lwamakhosi wukuphenya indaba. Qondani ukuthi ngiyakunenza nibe ngamakhosi nabapristi phambi kukaNkulunkulu wethu noBaba wethu, ngakho-ke qondani izisho zabahlakaniphileyo phakathi kwenu: uNkulunkulu akaninikanga yini izindlebe ezimbili nomlomo owodwa? Anifundanga yini? Ngulowo nalowo kini unesihlabelelo, unemfundiso, unolimi, unesambulo, unokuhumusha: Zonke izinto mazenziwe ukwakha! Kodwa cha, niyaluma futhi nidlane ngokuzidla kwenu; ngokuzidla kwenu nihlasela labo abazama ukunifundisa, futhi senibe ngabangafundiseki. Niyaphikisana, nibange, niphikisane, futhi nilwe omunye nomunye esikhundleni sokucabangela ngokuthobeka lokho okushiwo omunye. Ngakolunye uhlangothi, anizidla kangako yini ukuthi nicabange ukuthi ngenxa yokuthi amazwi aphuma ezindebeni zenu ayiqiniso ngoba nina niwashilo? Zithobeleni omunye nomunye. Bambani ulimi lwenu nithathe amazwi abafowenu niwayise emibhalweni ukuze nihlole konke nibambelele kokuhle. Vulani izindlebe zenu nilalele ngokugcwele nicabangele, ngokuba ngokwenza kanjalo ngiyonenza nihlakaniphe ekuthobekeni kwenu, ngakho-ke ningahlakaniphi ngokuzicabangela kwenu. Ngiyeluleka wonke umuntu phakathi kwenu, zwe amazwi ami. Futhi lawo mazwi athini? Ngokuba ngithi, ngomusa engiwunikiweyo, kuwo wonke umuntu ophakathi kwenu, ukuba angazicabangi ngokweqile kunalokho okufanele akucabange, kodwa acabange ngokuhluzeka, njengokuba uNkulunkulu abele umuntu ngamunye isilinganiso sokholo.

Cha, imani, cabangelani amazwi engisanda kuninika wona, vulani amaBhayibheli enu nifunde lawa mazwi futhi. Wezwani niwakholwe, ngokuba kufanele nizicabangele kakhulu, ngokuba nginivusile kanye nani nganihlalisa kanye nani ezindaweni zasezulwini kimi, uKristu Jesu. Kodwa ngasikhathi sinye kufanele nicabange ngokuhluzeka, ngokuba uNkulunkulu unike umuntu ngamunye phakathi kwenu isilinganiso esifanayo sokholo, ngakho umoya owanikezwayo awumkhulu noma mncane kubafowenu, kodwa kunikwe ngamunye wenu umusa, ngokwesilinganiso sesipho sikaKristu. Ngalokho uthi: Lapho enyukela phezulu, wathumba ukuthunjwa, wanika izipho kubantu. Nginike abanye babe abaphostoli; nabanye babe abaprofethi; nabanye babe abavangeli; nabanye babe abelusi, nabafundisi: Ukuze kupheleliswe abangcwele, kube ngumsebenzi wokukhonza, kwakhiwe umzimba kaKristu. Zithobeleni omunye nomunye nilahle ukuzidla kwenu okungenamsebenzi njengoba kubanga amaqembu phakathi kwenu. Kungani ningaqondi ukuthi akufanele kube nokwahlukana phakathi kwenu, bantwana bami. Umzimba kaKristu wahlukaniswe yini? Ngathini kwabaseKorinte?

Manje ngiyanincenga, bazalwane, ngegama leNkosi yethu uJesu Kristu, ukuba nonke nikhulume into eyodwa, nokuba kungabikho ukwahlukana phakathi kwenu; kodwa ukuba nihlangane ngokupheleleyo emqondweni owodwa, nasekwahluleleni okukodwa. Anibona yini munye kimi, iNkosi uJesu Kristu? Kodwa ninamaqembu amaningi kangaka phakathi kwenu, futhi niyaluma futhi nidlane ngenxa yokungaboni ngaso linye okuncane kakhulu. Yazini ukuthi emaqenjini enu nasekuhlebeni nithokozisa uSathane, futhi uyaziqhenya ngobuze bemizamo yenu esetshenziselwa ukulwa phakathi kwenu ngoba abaningi benu abanakho ukuthobeka. Futhi izindlebe zenu zivaliwe ukuze ningezwa amazwi omunye nomunye, futhi nivale amehlo enu, futhi anamatheliswe avalwa ngokuzidla nokungakholwa, ngokuba abaningi benu bacabanga ukuthi banalo lonke iqiniso. Futhi aniyishayi mkhuba amazwi abafowenu, futhi aniyishayi mkhuba wona kanye amazwi engibanike wona nina, futhi ngokuzidla kwenu okukhulu aninakho ukuthobeka okuyisisekelo kokuzwa nokucabangela. Ngokuba uma umuntu engayizwa into, kungubuwula nehlazo kuye. Futhi futhi, kungani nenza ize abafowenu, ngokuba sonke kumelwe sime phambi kwesihlalo sokwahlulela sikaKristu? Ngiyakweluleka, Bandla lami, ukuba nilahle ukuzidla kwenu nezindlela zokuzikhukhumeza nize kimi ningumfundi weZwi lokuphila, ngokuba mina ngiyiZwi likaNkulunkulu elenziwe inyama.

Ngakho lalelani ngomphefumulo wenu wonke ninake imisindo yezindlebe zenu niqonde amazwi ami. Ngakho qondani ukuhleleka kwezigameko okunikezwe eZwini lami, ngokuba abaningi benu bakhohlisiwe yizimpisi ezihahayo enizilandela ngokungazi, ngokuba nilalele abelusi abaphambukayo. Nina ningobani ukuba nishumayele ukuthi akukho ukubuthana kweBandla lami kimi, ukuthi ngeke ngihlangane neBandla lami emoyeni lapho ngiza ngokumemeza okukhulu nezwi lengelosi enkulu nangecilongo likaNkulunkulu? Ngokuba icilongo liyokhala futhi abafileyo kuKristu bayovuka kuqala futhi nina eniphilayo nisalayo aniyukubandulela, futhi nonke niyokhwishwezelwa emafwini ukuze nihlangane nami futhi niyokuba nami kuze kube phakade. Ingabe lawa mazwi mahle kakhulu ukuba ningawakholwa? Noma nizokholwa amazwi abelusi abaphambukayo abangamaJuda ngaphezu kwezwi leNkosi nomsindisi wenu, uKristu Jesu? Kungani ningenalo ukholo eZwini lami noma kuwona kanye amazwi akha iZwi lami? Aniqondi yini ukuthi iyini imfihlakalo yebhayibheli noma inhloso yayo?

Ngizokutshela: Imfihlakalo yinto egcinwe iyimfihlo kodwa eyaprofethwa ngayo eyembulwa ngesikhathi esifanelekile. Futhi kungani uBaba enza into enjalo, uyabuza? Awuwufundanga yini umbiko wami? Kodwa sikhuluma ukuhlakanipha kukaNkulunkulu okuyimfihlakalo, ngisho nokuhlakanipha okufihliweyo uNkulunkulu akuhlela ngaphambi kwezwe, kube yinkazimulo yethu. Okungazange kwaziwe yizikhulu zaleli zwe: ngokuba ukuba babekwazi, babengeke bambethele iNkosi yenkazimulo. Nina ningobani ukuba nidelele amacebo nezindlela zikaBaba? Ngokuba akadingi meluleki, nakuba amashumi ezinkulungwane ngamashumi ezinkulungwane ezungeza isihlalo sakhe sobukhosi.

Ngakho-ke dunduzanani ngamazwi ami, ngokuba nisindisiwe olakeni kimi. Bese, ezindleleni zenu zokungakholwa, niphikisana ngesikhathi salesi sigameko. Nakuba abanye benu bethi ngephutha akukho ukubuthana, abaningi benu baphikisana ngesikhathi sokwenzeka kwaso ngokuphathelene nosizi lukaJakobe. Ngizokwenza kucace kini uma nizolalela, nakuba abaningi benu bezophika ngokunenkani lesi sigameko, ngisho bephika nami futhi baqinisekise ngokuphelele isiphetho sabo sokuthatha indawo yabo phakathi kwezintombi eziwubuwula. Ngokuba lithini iZwi lami?

Manje siyanincenga, bazalwane, ngokufika kweNkosi yethu uJesu Kristu, nangokuhlangana kwethu kuyo, Ukuba ninganyakaziswa masinyane emqondweni wenu, ningakhathazwa nangomoya, nangazwi, nangancwadi, sengathi ivela kithi, kungathi usuku lukaKristu selufikile. Makungabikho muntu onikhohlisayo nganoma iyiphi indlela: ngokuba lolo suku aluyikufika, ngaphandle kokuba kufike kuqala ukuhlubuka, nokuthi lowo muntu wesono adalulwe, yona indodana yokulahlwa. (Bhekani kwabaseByzantine, bantwana bami, ngokuba amaBhayibheli amaningi akhohlakele avela emibhalweni yobuGnostic, imibhalo mbumbulu enikezwe abantu abayizoni. Aniwuqondi yini umehluko phakathi kombhalo webhayibheli nenguqulo yeBhayibheli?) Kuyoba khona ukuhlubuka okholweni, futhi labo abenqaba ukuhlubuka okholweni bayosuka emhlabeni bayohlangana nami emoyeni.

Ngokuba kulapha umfanekiso wezintombi eziyishumi. Izintombi ezinhlanu ezihlakaniphileyo zathenga uwoyela zalungisa izintambo zazo futhi zazilungele lapho umkhwenyana efika, futhi ezinhlanu zazingalungile futhi zanganaka ukuthenga uwoyela, zizama esikhundleni salokho ukuthatha ezidingweni zabanye. Izintombi ezinhlanu ezazilungisiwe zaqhubeka nomkhwenyana, futhi ezinhlanu eziwubuwula zacela ukungeniswa ngemva kwalokho zatshelwa, sukani kimi, angikaze nginazi. Ningawucimi umoya wami kini obungaqinisekisa lawa mazwi. Qondani ukuthi indodana yokulahlwa ayinakungena emandleni futhi umbuso kaSathane ogcwele awunakuvuka kwalasha kuze kususwe umvimbeli, futhi njengoba nisindiswe olakeni, umvimbeli uyosuswa lapho ngibuthela uMlobokazi wami kimi emafwini lapho ehlangana nami emoyeni.

Khona-ke ekupheleni kweminyaka eyisikhombisa yosizi lukaJakobe kuza uvuko lwabalungileyo. Bhekani! Ngase ngibona izihlalo zobukhosi, bahlala phezu kwazo, banikwa ukwahlulela; ngabona nemiphefumulo yalabo ababenqunywe amakhanda ngenxa yobufakazi bukaJesu, nangenxa yezwi likaNkulunkulu, nababengakhulekelanga isilo, nomfanekiso waso, bengamukelanga uphawu lwaso emabunzini abo, noma ezandleni zabo; baphila, babusa noKristu iminyaka eyinkulungwane. Kodwa abanye abafileyo abaphindanga baphila kwaze kwaphela iminyaka eyinkulungwane. Lolu wuvuko lokuqala. Ubusisiwe, ungcwele, lowo onesabelo ovukweni lokuqala; phezu kwabo ukufa kwesibili akunakuba namandla, kodwa bayakuba ngabapristi bakaNkulunkulu nabakaKristu, babuse naye iminyaka eyinkulungwane.

Anisakholwa yini amazwi ami? Ngeqiniso, abaningi benu bayonamathela ngobulukhuni ephutheni labo nasemazwini abelusi abaphambukayo, futhi abaningi benu ababengabalekela ulaka lukaNkulunkulu oluza ukuzama umhlaba ngesikhathi sosizi lukaJakobe, bakhetha esikhundleni salokho ukukhuthazelela ulaka olukhulu lapho sengikuvivinyile futhi ngikuzamile, ngaphandle kokuthi niyokwenqaba ngenkani ukubonakala kwami, futhi niyobambelela olakeni olungahloselwe nina. Angikutshelanga yini ukuba uphume kulo ukuze ungahlanganyeli ezinhluphweni zalo?

Nokho, kuyoba khona olunye uvuko. Ngabona isihlalo sobukhosi esikhulu esimhlophe, naye owayehlezi phezu kwaso, obuso bakhe umhlaba nezulu kwabaleka kuso, akwatholakalanga ndawo kubo. Ngabona abafileyo, abancane nabakhulu, bemi phambi kukaNkulunkulu; zavulwa izincwadi; kwavulwa nenye incwadi, eyincwadi yokuphila; abafileyo bahlulelwa ngezalotshwa ezincwadini, njengokwemisebenzi yabo. Nolwandle lwakhipha abafileyo ababekulo; nokufa neHayidese kwakhipha abafileyo ababekukho; bahlulelwa umuntu ngamunye njengokwemisebenzi yakhe, ngokuba lolu wuvuko lwabangakulungile bantu bami.

Kungani ningaqondi ukuthi ukubuthana kweBandla lami ukuhlangana nami emafwini ekubonakaleni kwami kwehlukile ekubuyeni kwami noMlobokazi wami namabutho asezulwini? Kungani ningaqondi ukuthi ukubuthana kweBandla lami kuyimfihlakalo eyembulwe nguMphostoli uPawulu kumongo weMfihlakalo Enkulu kaNkulunkulu. Ngakho-ke kuyimfihlakalo phakathi kwemfihlakalo, futhi ayizange yembulwe ngokugcwele emadodaneni abantu kwaze kwaba yembulwa kuPawulu owaloba phansi, futhi ngiyigcinele nina.

Nokho, kunezibonelo eziningana zikaNkulunkulu ethatha abantu ebasa emazulwini ngezinhloso ezithile. U-Enoke wemuka. U-Eliya wemuka. Mina ngemukela emazulwini ngenkathi abaphostoli bami bebukele ngokumangala. UFiliphu wathathwa endaweni ethile wayiswa kwenye njengoba uBaba wamthatha ngenjongo yami. Kanjalo-ke umlobokazi wami uyobuthwa kimi emafwini ukuze ayiswe emazulwini ukuba agane mina futhi akhonze izinjongo zami zokuhlenga.

Aniqondi yini ukuthi uMlobokazi wami uyoba ngamakhosi nabapristi phambi kukaNkulunkulu wami noBaba futhi uyobusa nami embusweni wami ozayo futhi uyoba nami phakade? Kungani nephuza kangaka ukukholwa amazwi ami? Nithakathiwe yini nalinyaziswa abelusi abaphambukayo? Noma kunjalo, abaningi benu bayoba yisiprofetho esizigcwalisayo futhi niyozibophela ekuhlubukeni ngokuhlubuka emiyalweni yami, ikakhulukazi imfundiso yokuthi iBandla lami liyobuthwa kimi. Kufanele nibe namahloni nina enihlanganisa le mfundiso nabaprofethi bamanga emlandweni wakamuva, nimemezela ukuhlwithwa okuyimfihlo okungamanga okuxhumene nezinye izimfundiso zamanga zezikhathi zenkambo.

Qondani ukuthi kunezikhathi emugqeni wesikhathi sikaNkulunkulu hhayi izikhathi zenkambo, futhi akuyona umqondo ofanayo. Zikhandleni ukuba nibe ngabavunyiweyo, niyeke ukuba ngamavila engqondo. Yekani ukuncika nokufunda ukuhumusha kwabanye abantu ngalokho okulotshiweyo, niphile ngokuthi kulotshiwe: Ngokuba anidingi muntu onifundisayo. Nokho, abaningi benu bayoklolodela benqabe iseluleko sami. Ngaphandle kokuthi le nto ngeke yenziwe ngasese futhi uSathane uzokwethula inkohliso enkulu yokumboza lesi sigameko ngoba sizokwaziwa umhlaba wonke. Kuzoba nokuzamazama okukhulu, futhi umhlaba wonke uzoshintsha kusuka ezweni enilaziyo, futhi lowo muntu wesono uyokwembulwa futhi avuke emlotheni oshiywe ngemuva ngabagibeli bamahhashi abane. Sekuyisikhathi sokuzilungisa, Mlobokazi wami, futhi nikwenze lokho ngokushesha, ngokuba bhekani ukubonakala kwami sekusondele!

NginguNdodana yoMuntu eyaprofetwa ngu-Enoke, uNkulunkulu amthatha wamyisa emazulwini.

Nginguye owaguqulwa isimo wakhuluma noMose no-Eliya ngokumuka kwami, futhi uNkulunkulu wathatha u-Eliya wamyisa emazulwini ngesikhathi sakhe.

Nginguye owathathwa efwini njengoba ngamukelwa emazulwini kwaze kwaba yizikhathi zokubuyiselwa.

Nginguye owathumela uFiliphu kumthenwa wase-Ethiopia ngaphambi kokuba uNkulunkulu amyise kwenye indawo emhlabeni.

Nginguye owahlwitha uPawulu wamyisa ezulwini lesithathu futhi wambonisa izinto ezingakhulumeki ayengenakukhululeka ukuzabelana.

Nginguye oyokwehla emazulwini ngokumemeza okukhulu ngibuthe uMlobokazi wami ukuba ahlangane nami emoyeni.

Nginguye oyovusa ofakazi bami ababili babe baphile ngemva kokuba befile izinsuku ezintathu nengxenye, futhi ngiyobathatha ngibayise emazulwini.

Nginguye ongu-Alfa no-Omega, ngokuba ngiyisiqalo nesiphelo, futhi bhekani! Ngiza ngokushesha kunokuba nicabanga, ngakho-ke lungelani ukuhlangana nami noma nihlubuke.

Noma ngubani obiza igama leNkosi kuze kube sekupheleni uyosindiswa.
