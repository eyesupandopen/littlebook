Ubufakazi Obukabili: Israyeli
Okubabayo Okumnandi
Novemba 3, 2024

O Israyeli, NGIYAnibiza!

Okuvela kuYahweh, UNkulunkulu Omkhulu Waphakade:

Zwanini Amazwi kaYahweh, uMINA OMkhulu, nicabange ngombuzo wami. Kungani amagundane ebaleka endlini ewohlokayo, bantabami? Impendulo esobala ngeyokusinda kwawo, ngaphandle kwamagundane abaleka eBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo enza kanjalo ukuze afihle amacala awo, futhi ukuze akwazi ukuyiphanga ngokuphelele ukuze abe nokubusa okufushane engiwanika khona. Njengoba umuntu wemvelo ethanda ubumnyama, manje ngizomnika ubumnyama abuthanda kangaka. Nokho, ngiqophile (futhi ngisazoqopha) zonke izenzo zazo zonke izidalwa endalweni yami ezinenkululeko yokuzikhethela ngoba kulowo onikwe okuningi, kudingeka okuningi.

Futhi akukho okufihliwe emehlweni ami, futhi akukho okufihliwe ekuboneni kwami, futhi nginguYE obonayo, oqophayo, nokhumbulayo ZONKE izenzo ezimbi ezake zenziwa endalweni yami. Futhi akukho okungenzeka ngingakwazi futhi ngingakuvumeli! Emehlweni ami, yonke imicabango yembuliwe. Emehlweni ami yonke inhloso yenhliziyo yembuliwe futhi akukho lutho noma muntu owadaliwa noma owenziwa ongekho ngaphansi kwesandla sami esinamandla, ngokuba nginguNkulunkulu Waphakade wokulunga okumele nisebenzelane naye, O Israyeli, futhi ngiyanibona ngokuphelele, nakuba nikhetha ukungangilaleli ngobumpumputhe obuyingxenye ngenxa yobulukhuni bezinhliziyo zenu.

Ngoba, lapho ngiza kini ngomuntu okuwukuphela kukaMesiya engake ngamthuma nokuphela kukaMesiya engiyoke ngimthume, ngenqatshwa ngokuphelele ngoYihlo. Ngoba, nginguYE owadala uYeshua HaMashiach enyameni ukuze kusindiswe bonke ababiza egameni lakhe elinamandla, enina nimphika ngokungazi ngisho nangokuzidla nangokuhlambalaza nimbiza ngesithixo sabezizwe. O Israyeli, baningi benu abazihlanganise neSinagoge likaSathane futhi manje nilindele ngabomvu uMoshiach wenu wamanga u-Esawu amlungiselele ukuhlatshwa kukaJakobe.

Ngakho-ke zwanini izwi likaMINA OMKHULU ngokuba izwi lami elidumayo, laphakade liyanibiza kulesi sikhathi sokugcina futhi ngithumela iNdodana yami kini futhi ukuze ngihlenge uJakobe kanye nensali eyamukela iNdodana yami Yaphakade, uYeshua HaMashiach, iyosindiswa ngesandla sakhe esinamandla futhi ayikho enye inketho yensindiso. Futhi ngeke kuphinde kube nenye inketho noma igama elinikeziwe lensindiso ngoba ngimnike igama elingaphezu kwawo wonke amagama, ukuze ngegama likaJesu waseNazaretha wonke amadolo aguqe. Futhi labo abenqabayo, bayokwenziwa ukuba baguqe eBhubesini Lesizwe SakwaJuda engilithumela emhlabeni ukuze lahlulele futhi lilwe ngisho nangokulunga kwaphakade, futhi yilo eliyoletha yonke indalo yami ngaphansi kokuthobeka okulungileyo futhi liyobusa ngentonga yensimbi nokwahlulela, umusa, nesihe kuhlala nalo kuze kube phakade. Qondani ukuthi ibanzi indlela eya ekubhujisweni futhi amagama amaningi nonkulunkulu bamanga bafuna ukuniyisa efeni labo lechibi lomlilo elivutha njalo, elivutha isibabule ngamalangabi ashisa kakhulu esijeziso.

O Israyeli, naze nayiphula kanjani inhliziyo kaYahweh ngokungalaleli kwenu futhi inhliziyo yami isaniphukela kusukela ephakadeni eladlulayo lapho ngangazi khona ngokwazi kwami kusengaphambili ukuthi ohlelweni lwami lokuhlenga oYihlo babeyomphika uJesu waseNazaretha bekhetha uBaraba, owayengumbulali nomholi wezihlubuki ezivukelayo ezweni ngaleso sikhathi. Kanjalo ngamenza ukuba aphendukele kwabezizwe, futhi bamuzwa isikhashana, nokho ubuphumputhe obuyingxenye benzeka ku-Israyeli ukuze insali ka-Israyeli isengathola insindiso ngesikhathi esifanele. Qondani ukuthi abaningi baseJudiya bamenza iNkosi yabo futhi bathola insindiso yaphakade kwa-Israyeli kaNkulunkulu futhi bayoba nendodana yami kuze kube phakade. Futhi nginguYE othume uJesu waseNazaretha phakathi kwezizwe ukuze ahlenge abafowenu abahlakazekela phesheya ngenxa yokuhlubuka kwabo, ngisho nalabo ababengenathemba futhi abaningi bezizwe bathole insindiso yaphakade futhi manje baxhunywe ezizweni kuze kube phakade. Ngakho-ke qondani O Israyeli ukuthi lokho uMINA Waphakade akuhlanzileyo ningakubizi ngokuthi kungcolile noma kungahlanzekile!

Kodwa nina O Israyeli, nayiphika futhi nayibulala indodana yami uJesu Kristu nayinikela ezandleni zabantu abayizoni ababezimisele ukuyiyeka ihambe ingenasici. Kodwa lwalungadlula kanjani olunye uhlelo lwami lwensindiso yaphakade ngaphandle kokuba kuhlangatshezwane nezimfuneko zemithetho yaphakade nengaguquki kaYahweh, okuyimithetho engayibeka ukuze ukuhleleka okulungile kwayo yonke indalo yami kubuswe ngobulungiswa obuphelele. Kanjalo uJesu waseNazaretha wazinikela njengeWundlu likaNkulunkulu njengoMpristi wenu Omkhulu waphakade ngokohlelo lukaMelkisedeki ukuze akhokhe isijeziso esabekwa uMose ngenxa yezono neziphambeko zesintu futhi ahlenge isintu ngokuphelele esiqalekisweni somthetho. Ngoba uMose weza njengomfundisi wesikole sika-Israyeli ukuze athathe futhi alondoloze u-Israyeli kuYeshua HaMashiach futhi uselikhokhele ngokugcwele ihlengo lenu, futhi nakuba ningabafundi abahlubukayo insali iyosabela ekusoleni kwami ngisho nasekuhluphekeni okuzayo kini phakathi nesonto lamashumi ayisikhombisa likaDaniyeli elisemnyango wenu futhi maduzane lizokhahlela livule iminyango yenu ekukhetheni kwaphakade.

Ngakho-ke qondani ukuthi nimphikile bani, O Israyeli, engimthanda ngothando lwaphakade, ngokuba nina niyilabo engathembisa oYihlo ezivumelwaneni eziningi ezikhathini ezidlule ukuthi kulezi zikhathi zokwahlulela ngizonilondoloza ngenxa yabo, futhi nginguNkulunkulu Waphakade Wokulunga ongaguquki! Futhi nginguYE okhumbula ukunxusa okuningi okwenziwa oYihlo ngenxa yenu futhi konke ukunxusa ngiyakukhumbula izwi nezwi futhi ngizohlonipha isicelo sabo kulabo kini abazimisele nabalalelayo. Futhi ngisho namanje iNdodana yami, eniyizonda ngokungazi, iyaninxusela othandweni lwayo lwaphakade ngezihlobo zayo ngoba uYeshua HaMashiach unguMhlengi ongumhlobo ka-Israyeli, yena kanye othenge umhlaba ngegazi lakhe lokuhlawulela futhi unihlengile ezandleni zesitha uma nikhetha ukwamukela. Nakuba, abaningi phakathi kwenu badle ifa lothando lwenu lweGibhithe koYihlo, ningazivumeli ukuba niwele kuze kube phakade ehlane labangenakukholwa.

Ngoba uYeshua HaMashiach nguye oyosindisa insali kuJakobe eyofika kuye ngokuphelele, ngisho naphakathi kwezilingo zesonto lamashumi ayisikhombisa likaDaniyeli. Ngakho-ke qondani izikhathi eniphila kuzo futhi niphaphame ebuphumputheni benu obuyingxenye ngomyalo wami lapho niphendukela kuYeshua HaMashiach, engimthumile ngenxa yensindiso yenu njengoMesiya wenu (lapho ningasayikuphuphuthekiswa ubulukhuni bezinhliziyo zenu). Ngoba niyophendukela endodaneni yokulunga futhi iyonamukela yona uqobo, futhi iyoniyisa endaweni ephephile inifihle ekubhujisweni kwenu ezandleni zikaMoshiach wamanga ka-Esawu.

Ngakho-ke qondani ukuthi ninendaba nobani ngoba nginguNkulunkulu wezivumelwano eziningi, futhi nginguNkulunkulu wezithembiso eziningi, futhi nginguNkulunkulu Waphakade Wokulunga oza futhi ukuzobiza abantu bami ukuba babuyele kumalusi wabo omkhulu engimthumele insindiso ka-Adamu. Futhi kunezici eziningi zokuthi ngingubani, futhi igama ngalinye linokubaluleka kwalo mayelana noNkulunkulu Waphakade. Ngakho-ke ngizweni futhi ningizwe kahle niqonde uYahweh, onibizela kimi ukuze nisengathola insindiso. Kanjalo ngiyanikhumbuza futhi nginifundise njengezinsana, lapho empeleni kungani okufanele nibe ngabafundisi, O Israyeli! Ngakho-ke fundani ngoNkulunkulu wenu Waphakade Wokulunga!

Ngingu-YHVH Rapha! INkosi Umphilisi Wenu!

Ngingu-YHVH Nissi! Ngoba ngiyisibhengezo senu!

Ngingu-YHVH Shalom! Futhi ngithumele uYeshua HaMashiach njengesivumelwano senu sokuthula phakathi kwenu nami!

Ngingu-YHVH Raah! Ngokuba nginguYE ohlinzeke umalusi wami ophelele, ngisho noYeshua HaMashiach!

Ngingu-YHVH Tsidkenu! NginguNkosi Yokulunga!

Ngingu-YHVH Shammah! Ngoba ngiyiNkosi uNkulunkulu wenu okhona phakade ngenxa yendalo yami.

Futhi amanye amagama amaningi nezimfanelo nginazo, ngakho zwanini izwi lami elibhongayo njengokuduma kwamazulu, O Israyeli, ngoba nginguNkulunkulu wawoyihlo enimkhonza ngamanga ngenkani.

NginguNkulunkulu ka-Abrahama enimfulathele!

NginguNkulunkulu kaJakobe enimshiyile naya ezingalweni zomunye, futhi nigijimele konkulunkulu benu bamanga ngisho niziqhenya ngokuhlubuka kwenu. Qondani Israyeli ukuthi nisezinziphoni ZeSinagoge likaSathane ngisho nalabo abathi bangabakwa-Israyeli abaqamba amanga futhi abangesibo.

Zwanini izwi lami nina bakwa-Israyeli, nize kuYeshua HaMashiach kulesi sikhathi sokugcina ngoba nginibiza othandweni lwami ngani futhi nginelulela umusa nesihe sami kulesi sikhathi. Kodwa niyisizwe esinenkani, futhi inkani nobulukhuni bezinhliziyo zoyihlo nizenzé okwenu, futhi nimlahle uNkulunkulu woyihlo enikhonza ngendlela engafundisiwe nengamanga ngoba abantu bami babhujiswa ngenxa yokuswela ulwazi, ngakho-ke yizani kuYeshua HaMashiach nifunde ngaye ukuze nisengathola insindiso esandleni sakhe esinamandla.

O Israyeli, yeka ukuphuka kwenhliziyo yami ngenxa yenu, ngoba nganithatha njengesitshalo esithambile ehlane eliyinyumba sesizoshabalala futhi sibune ingunaphakade, nganinika ukuphila nganenza isizwe esikhethekile kimi. Ngenza izivumelwano eziningi noyihlo, ngisho nezivumelwano abahluleka ukuzigcina ngenxa yesono nenkohlakalo esegazini lika-Adamu. Kodwa MINA NGIYIHUJE uNkulunkulu wenu okhumbulayo futhi ogcinile izithembiso nezivumelwano zami futhi ngisazogcwalisa zonke izithembiso nesivumelwano esake senziwa esintwini ngisho nasebusweni bukaYeshua HaMashiach, oyiNdodana yami ethandekayo uJesu Kristu.

O Israyeli, niyoqhubeka nokungakholwa nokungalaleli yini? Niyohlala phakade nihlanganyela nezitha zenu esijezisweni sazo saphakade? Ngakho-ke kunjalo ukuthi anilalelanga futhi nihambe ekungabonini ngokwengxenye, futhi ngisusile isizwe senu kini futhi anisaholwa nguYAHWEH uNkulunkulu oMkhulu waphakade. Kodwa ekuhlubukeni kwenu niholwa isandla sezitha zenu ezenza sengathi zinguJakobe kanti empeleni zingu-Esawu. Qondani ukuthi bonke abalahla uYeshua HaMashiach bafulathele uYAHWEH baphendukela konkulunkulu bamanga nokungakholwa futhi abaningi bayathakatha futhi baboshelwe emabhokisini abo amnyama abawakhonzayo ngokungazi ekungakholweni. Ngakho-ke qondani, bonke labo abadukiswa nguMoshiach wabo wamanga noma uMahdi wabo wamanga ezinkolweni zabo zamanga ezingaholeli kuYAHWEH noma ekuphileni okuphakade, bayothola ukubhujiswa kwaphakade kubo bonke abakhetha ukuhlala kuzo.

O Israyeli, wozani kimi ngeNdodana yami uYeshua HaMashiach onguJesu waseNazaretha, enimalayo njengoba benza oyihlo ngaphambi kwenu. Kodwa ningabhubhi ezonweni zoyihlo, namukele uthando lwami nomhlatshelo engawenza esikhundleni senu, umhlatshelo okwabusiswa ngawo sonke isizukulwane sika-Adamu futhi abaningi abavela emazweni nasezizweni beza eNkosini yabo ngokuphenduka futhi bayabusiswa futhi bahlengwa ingunaphakade. Futhi qondani ukuthi Nginguye owabeka zonke izinto ezandleni zikaKristu Jesu ezivusiwe, ezinamandla neziguquliwe, ngoba Nginguye owamenza umoya onika ukuphila futhi ukubusa nombuso wakhe ngeke kube nasiphelo!

Ngakho-ke, zonke izivumelwano zami kusukela ku-Adamu kuya kuMose, ngisho neTestamente likaMose manje sekuzogcwaliseka ebusweni bukaJesu Kristu ngeSivumelwano sami Elisha esiyiTestamente Elisha elinikezelwe ukugcwaliseka kwazo zonke izinto kusukela ku-Adamu kuya kuKristu. Ngoba labo abathola ukuphila okuphakade kumele bazithobe baguqe ngedolo eNdodaneni yami ezelwe yodwa, engithokoza ngayo njalo. Ngakho-ke wozani kimi ebusweni bukaYeshua HaMashiach, owathi ngegazi lakhe elingenasono waqinisa iSivumelwano sakhe Elisha, esiyiTestamente Elisha, engalamukela ngokugcwele futhi ngalihlela ngaphambi kokusekelwa komhlaba. Futhi ngizothuma abayizi-144,000 bami, beholwa nguYeshua HaMashiach, ukuze nisindiswe futhi bayonifihla endaweni enginilungisele yona futhi lapho nizonakekelwa nisindiswe ku-Esawu olungiselela ukunibhubhisa.

Nokho u-Esawu, ocindezela abantu bami, uyalishiya iBhabhiloni leMfihlakalo ngoba bahlose ukumboza izono zabo. Isono amagundane ashiya indlu ewayo angenacala laso. Ngakho-ke, bayozama ukungcwaba ubugebengu babo ngisho namafosholo abo anesono abawasebenzisela ukumboza ubugebengu babo futhi ngisazobadalula, kusho uJehova Sebawoti. Ngoba baliphangile ngokuphelele iBhabhiloni leMfihlakalo njengoba nje ngikuvumele ukuba kwenzeke esahlulelweni sami esiphindwe kabili kuleso sifebe esikhulu! Futhi kanjalo bazibuthile zonke izikhwepha zempi zeBhabhiloni leMfihlakalo futhi bayoqhubeka nokuphanga kuze kubhujiswe iBhabhiloni leMfihlakalo ngokushesha ngehora elilodwa futhi ngeke liphinde livuke. Qondani ukuthi asikho isikhali esakhiwe noma esenziwe ngani esiyophumelela uma nisabiza uMesiya wenu weqiniso, uYeshua HaMashiach!

Ngakho-ke qondani ukuthi iSinagoge likaSathane linesimo sokwesaba uNkulunkulu ngokubukeka kwalo kwangaphandle, kodwa bangabakaYise udeveli futhi izinkanuko zikaYise bayozenza ngoba u-Esawu wonakalisa inzalo yakhe ingunaphakade. Lolu khula olubi, ngisho nabaholi benu ababonakala belungile bangukhula olubi futhi abaningi bangabahlukumezi bezingane abadla abancane futhi abadué ezonweni zaseSodoma neGomora, futhi badla izingane, futhi ababona abelusi abahle kodwa banomhlwenga. Futhi udrako onguYise wabo nguye obaholayo, yena lowo ohlinzeke ngabelusi ababili abanomhlwenga ukuba babuse beluse umhlaba ngokungalungi. Ngisho nesilo sasolwandle esiyovuka njengoMahdi wabo nesilo sasemhlabeni, esingumMoshiach wabo wamanga futhi siyokuza ukuzobusa izwe futhi ngempela bayobusa umhlaba ngokungalungi.

Futhi ibala lomhlwenga wabo lingcolisa isiqu sabo sonke, futhi insindiso angeke bayithole. Ngoba bonke abaziguqula ngokofuzo ngaleyo ndlela noma abazalwa ngabawiweyo abanayo insindiso ngoba benziwe ngomfanekiso kadrako futhi bangalowo mfanekiso ngokuphelele. Okugwegwile akunakuqondiswa, futhi zonke izinengiso zabawiweyo zingenza okubi kuphela ngoba zenzelwe ukuletha ukubhujiswa kwesintu, futhi ngabe baphumelela ukube angibamisanga ezinsukwini zikaNowa.

Nokho bayovuka futhi, ngoba ngiletha imidondoshiya ukuze igcwalise ulaka lwami! Futhi iSinagoge likaSathane lisebenzela ukumboza yonke imikhondo yokuthi bangobani ngempela ngaphambi kokuba kufike inhlekelele futhi inkohliso yabo igcine enhlekeleleni ngoba abawiweyo bavunyelwe ukubhubhisa iBhabhiloni leMfihlakalo. Nokho sonke isono siyonikezwa ngokugcwele ezifubeni zabo futhi ngakho-ke, akunakwenzeka ukucasha kuYAHWEH neNdodana yami uYeshua HaMashiach futhi ulaka lwethu luyobathola ngisho bezama ukucasha enhliziyweni yomhlaba. Akekho noyedwa kulabo abangaphansi kwesahlulelo esikhulu ongabalekela ichibi lomlilo, ngaphandle kwalabo ababhalwe encwadini yokuphila yewundlu futhi akekho ongabalekela izono zakhe ngaphandle kwalabo insindiso ehloselwe bona ngisho nalabo abamukela isivumelwano sami sokuthula. Akunandaba ukuthi zingaki izizwe abazibhubhisayo nokuthi bacasha kude kangakanani emhlabeni, ngeke babalekele impindiselo nolaka abazibuthile futhi ngiyobabuyisela ngokugcwele kusho uNkulunkulu oPhakade.

Ngoba ngiyoguqula umhlaba usuke ekuhlelekeni kwawo kwamanje uye enkazimulweni yawo yasekuqaleni, futhi abahlengiweyo bomhlaba bayohamba kuwo ngokuphepha. Futhi kanjalo lokho okukhona, kuyoguqulwa kube yilokho okuzoba khona, futhi ngeke babaleke, ngisho nabo bonke abashiyelwe esahlulelweni lapho kuyokwenzeka khona izinhlekelele eziningi, neqhwa liyoncibilika, nezizwe ziyoguqulwa, nomhlaba uyobuyiselwa enkazimulweni yawo yangaphambili. Inkazimulo abayiguqula abawiweyo bayenza inkohlakalo ukuze ivumelane nezinkanuko zabo.

Futhi nginguYAHWEH, uNkulunkulu oMkhulu waphakade, yena owadala amazulu nomhlaba, yena okusukela phakade kuze kube phakade, nangentando neseluleko sami ngakhuluma kube khona izinto ezikhona ngisho nokwakungekho. Ngakho-ke zwanini izwi lami, O Israyeli, ngisho nani nonke enifuna ukuziba amaZwi ami ngenkani yobulukhuni benhliziyo. Isahlulelo sesizofika ngokugcwele emhlabeni futhi ngiyodalula bonke abakwa-Esawu ukuthi bangobani ngempela, futhi ngiyodalula ngokugcwele oYise babo ababoshelwe phakade ezinkanukweni zabo.

Bhekani! Labo bakwa-Esawu bathi balungile bona, becabanga ngokungazi ukuthi bangabantu bami abakhethiweyo, noMoshiach wabo wamanga, lolo phondo oluncane, uyothi yena ngokwakhe, unguNkulunkulu! (Akekho onjengoYAHWEH, uNkulunkulu oMkhulu waphakade noSomandla!) Futhi abawiweyo abanakuzikhulula ezandleni zami ezinamandla onke, futhi uYeshua HaMashiach uyochoboza ikhanda likaSathane ngokuqinisekile njengoba ngathembisa u-Adamu. Azikho izinzulu emhlabeni lapho bengacasha khona, nakuba bezama, futhi manje baboshelwe emhlabeni ngisho nangaphansi komkhathi abangenakubalekela kuwo. Abukho obunye ubuntu abangabuthatha lapho ngingeke ngibabone ukuthi bangobani ngempela. Azikho izimo abangazithatha lapho ngingabazi futhi ngibabone ngokugcwele, kuze kufike ezinhliziyweni zabo ezingcolile. Futhi wonke umuntu wokugcina wabawiweyo kusukela komncane kuye komkhulu nabo bonke abalandela izindlela zabo ezimbi bayoba nefa labo laphakade kulawo malangabi esijeziso kulelo chibi lomlilo elivutha ingunaphakade lapho impethu yabo ingafi.

Ngakho-ke banganani isililo, O Israyeli, ngenxa yalabo benu abenqaba ngenkani isimemo sami sokuphila okuphakade, ngisho nabo bonke abayonquma ukulandela abawiweyo nabo bonke abayozihlanganisa nodrako nabo bonke abangabaka-Adamu ababenethuba lokusindiswa kodwa bakhetha izono zabo eziyobaholela ekufeni kwesibili uma bengabizi egameni likaYeshua HaMashiach ukuze basindiswe.

Ngakho-ke ngizochaza izinketho enisele nazo manje. Ningamukela uMesiya wenu weqiniso, uYeshua HaMashiach engimthumile, futhi niyosindiselwa emvuzweni nasenkazimulweni yaphakade, okungefaniswe nezilingo nokushushiswa kwenu. Noma uma umuntu eqhubeka noma ehlanganyela nodrako ohlelweni lwabo olusha, uyobe enqabile ukuphila okuphakade futhi esikhundleni salokho wamukele ngokugcwele ukulahlwa kwakho ngenxa yezono zenyama, futhi uyosithwala ngokufanele isahlulelo sakho lapho unxuswa ukuba ubaleke ngokulalela, okuyobe ukwenqabile.

Noma ngubani othatha uphawu lwesilo, uyobe eseguqule ingunaphakade umfanekiso kaYAHWEH adalwe ngawo, emshiya esesimweni esingenakuhlengeka. Ngakho-ke, ngiyanincenga ukuba nikhethe ukuphila ukuze niphile ingunaphakade othandweni nasezibusisweni zomdali wenu ezivezwa ngokugcwele ebusweni bukaJesu Kristu, uYeshua HaMashiach! Ngaphandle kwalokho, niyohlanganyela nabawiweyo efeni labo lesibabule esivuthayo kulelo chibi lomlilo laphakade, lapho isijeziso nempindiselo yezono kuwukuphela kwephakade eniyoke nilazi.

Qondani, ukuthi inhloso yami akukhona ukuba nonya noma ukungabi nobulungisa endalweni yami, kodwa imithetho yami yobulungisa ayiguquki futhi ayinakuguqulwa. Futhi kanjalo indalo iyabubula ikhala kakhulu kimi ngaphansi kwesisindo sezono zabawiweyo nezono zesintu, futhi isikhathi sokuhlanza nokuhlanza umhlaba ngokwahlulela ebubini sesifikile; nakuba imbewu yokuhlubuka iyosala kodwa ngeke kuphinde kuvuke umbuso wezitha. Nakuba uSathane eyozama ukuhlasela kwakhe kokugcina esikhathini sakhe esifushane ngaphambi kokuba aphonswe ingunaphakade emlilweni ngoba akayona inkosi, kodwa isihlalo sakhe sobukhosi siyoba amalangabi angenakucimeka obumsulwa obushisa kakhulu, futhi ngeke babe nesihe.

Ngoba ithini imithetho yami yaphakade ngabangalaleli, ngakho khumbulani ukuthi inkokhelo yesono ingukufa, kanjalo u-Adamu wona waqeda ukuphila kwakhe enkokhelweni yesono, okungukufa. Nokho iminikelo yokushiswa nemihlatshelo yasendulo ayenele ukuhlenga ngokugcwele indodana yami yolahleko, ngakho-ke umuntu ophelele ongenasono nguyena kuphela umhlatshelo owamukelekayo ngoba u-Adamu wayephelele futhi engenasono lapho ona.

Ngakho-ke mina, uYAHWEH oMkhulu Nginguye, ngithumele uYeshua HaMashiach njengoba kwathenjiswa njengewundlu likaNkulunkulu elachitha igazi lalo elingenasono ngokuzithandela ukuze likhokhe isijeziso sikaMose ngokuchitha igazi lalo elingenasono ebuhlungwini obukhulu ukuze isintu sihlangulwe esiqalekisweni somthetho, ngisho nabo bonke abakhetha ukuba yindalo entsha kuKristu Jesu ingunaphakade ngoba lo Kristu Jesu unguYeshua HaMashiach!

NGINGUYAHWEH, uNkulunkulu oMkhulu waphakade, futhi NGIYILOKHO ENGIYIKHO.

Nginguye othumele uJesu Kristu, uYeshua HaMashiach, ukuze kusindiswe indalo yami ngakho-ke mzweni futhi nilalele amaZwi akhe njengoba uMose ayala.

Nginguye othumele la maZwi ngesithunywa sami nenceku yami iNdodana yami uKristu Jesu eyithumileyo futhi yayivusela inhloso enjalo ngomyalo wami ngoba uYeshua HaMashiach wenza njalo intando kaYise futhi simunye ebumbanweni nasenhlosweni.

NGINGUNKULUNKULU oPhilayo onibizayo, O Israyeli, ukuze bonke abamiselwe ukuphila okuphakade beze ekuphepheni komalusi wabo omkhulu.

Ngakho-ke zwanini amazwi ami O Israyeli nize eNdodaneni yami ngokuphenduka okuphelele futhi namukele umhlatshelo wayo, ngoba ngeqiniso uma nilahla ubizo lwayo akusekho mhlatshelo wesono kodwa isahlulelo saphakade ngoba niyobe nikhethe ngokuzithandela nangunaphakade okubi kunokulunga, futhi ngiyonika bonke abenza ukukhetha okunjalo imiphumela egcwele yesinqumo sabo, nokho qondani ukuthi bonke bakhululekile ukukhetha.

Nginguye oyokwenza zonke izinto zibe zintsha!

NGINGUYISE wezinkanyiso oyokwakha indawo yakhe yokuhlala nabantu emazulwini amasha nasemhlabeni omusha engiyowudala ngokugcwele ekulungeni futhi akukho lutho olubi oluyoke lungene kuwo. Futhi ngiyonika lelo dolobha elingcwele iJerusalema Elisha kubanqobi bami nabo bonke abeza kuYeshua HaMashiach, onguJesu Kristu futhi bayodla umuthi wokuphila futhi bayongena kulelo dolobha elingcwele neliphelele futhi ngiyoba uNkulunkulu wabo, futhi bayoba abantu bami ingunaphakade, Yebo no-Amen.

Yanganini iNdodana, funa ithukuthele futhi nibhubhe endleleni.

Izikhathi yimanje.
