Ubufakazi Obumbaxambili: Ibandla
Emisebenzini KaJohane Umbhabhadisi
Mashi 02, 2024

Phaphama!

Phaphama! Phaphama! Phaphama! Bantu bami, phaphama, ngoba mina iNkosi uJesu Kristu ngibizela abantu bami kimi. Ningaliphikisi izwi leNkosi lapho nginibiza futhi ngininika umusa, intethelelo, nokuthula. Akukho ukuthula kulo mhlaba; noma beyokhamuluka bethi ukuthula nokuphepha, ningabakholwa. Akukho ukuthula ngaphandle kwami, uJesu Kristu. Phaphama! Phaphazisani abafowenu! Abaningi bayakuwa ngakwesobunxele nangakwesokudla, kodwa lokho ngeke kusondele kini uma nihlala nithembekile ezwini lami, izwi leNkosi.

UMakoti wami uyothathelwa emakamelweni angaphakathi amazulu ngokuthula nangokuphepha, njengoba bezihlanze izingubo zabo egazini lami, igazi leWundlu! Labo abangangamukeli futhi bethatha umhlatshelo kaJesu Kristu, iPhasika eliphelele, lezono zesintu njengento engento, ngeke babe nokuthula. Phendukani nifulathele izono zenu, njengoba leli kuyihora lokugcina. Ningabe nisazinikela ekuzithetheleleni nasekungakholweni, njengoba kungekho muntu ongema phambi kwami, uJesu Kristu, ngaphandle kwalabo abahlanze izingubo zabo egazini leWundlu futhi bazihlanza ezonweni.

Phumani phakathi kwalo, bantu bami, ngoba isifebe esikhulu siyokwahlulelwa masinyane futhi kungabikho khambi. Isikhathi sokuhlupheka kukaJakobe sesiseduze, futhi ukwahlulelwa okukhulu nenhlekelele kuyofika emhlabeni. Ninendlela yokuphunyuka ezindebeni zenu uma nivuma mina, uJesu waseNazaretha, njengeNkosi yenu futhi nikholwa ukuthi uNkulunkulu wangivusa kwabafileyo. Ingabe izono zenu ziyigugu kangaka kini? Ingabe injabulo yesono ibaluleke ngokwanele ukuba nifake engcupheni iphakade ebumnyameni obungaphandle? Niyalifisa yini ichibi lomlilo? Bizani iNkosi isatholakala, futhi nikhuleke kimi ngesikhathi ngisanizwa, ngoba ihora liyasondela lapho kungekho muntu ongasebenza khona.

Nakuba iNkosi iyothumela abaprofethi bayo ngesikhathi sokuhlupheka kukaJakobe, niyokuma kuphi lapho iNkosi ihlwitha abantu bayo? Ubani oyokhonza nina lapho iBandla lisuswa phakathi kwenu? Kuyoba khona ukusweleka kwemiBhalo ezweni, futhi kuyodingeka nimthole uKristu ngokukhuthazela kuze kube sekuphefumuleni kwenu kokugcina, uma nibusiseke ngokwanele ukufika lapho. Phendukani nibuyele eNkosini! Phumani kulo, bantu bami, futhi nahlukane nalo. Ngimi emnyango ngiyangqongqoza.

Umoya wesiprofetho wehlele phezu kwabantu bami emhlabeni wonke, njengoba ngangethembisile emiBhalweni engcwele.

IzEnzo 2:16-21

Kodwa yilokhu okwashiwo ngomprofethi uJoweli;

Kuyothi ezinsukwini zokugcina (isho iNkosi) ngiyothulula uMoya wami phezu kwayo yonke inyama: amadodana enu namadodakazi enu ayoprofetha, nezinsizwa zenu ziyobona imibono, nabadala benu bayophupha amaphupho:

Naphezu kwezinceku zami nezincekukazi zami ngiyothulula ngalezo zinsuku uMoya wami, futhi ziyoprofetha: Futhi ngiyoveza izimangaliso ezulwini phezulu, nezibonakaliso emhlabeni phansi: igazi, nomlilo, nesitunzi somusi: Ilanga liyophenduka ubumnyama, nenyanga ibe yigazi, ngaphambi kokuba lufike usuku olukhulu noludumileyo lweNkosi. Futhi kuyokwenzeka ukuthi noma ubani obiza egameni leNkosi uyosindiswa.
