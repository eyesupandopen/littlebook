Okthoba 28, 2024

Isiqinisekiso Esiyisiprofetho ngu-J. Leland Earls

Yizwani amazwi ami, bantu bami; lalelani lokho enginazisa khona kuleli hora. Ngokuba angizi yini kini manje njengesithunywa esinamandla ukunembulela lokho obekufihliwe eminyakeni eminingi nasezizukulwaneni ngezizukulwane? Ngokuba manje ngizimisele ukwembulela labo abanezindlebe zokuzwa lokho engikulungiselele umzimba wami kulezi zinsuku zokuphela. Nicabanga ukuthi ngizonishiya ningenasiqondiso nolwazi lwezinjongo zami ngani kulezi zinsuku zokuphela? Nicabanga ukuthi anginako yini ukukhetha izithunywa ezikhethekile enginganembula ngazo umyalezo wami nenjongo yami kini ekuphethweni kwezinto zonke? Yizwani amazwi ami: Ngiyaphinda ngithi, lalelani lokho engikukhuluma kini kuleli hora. Ngokuba manje ngiphethe incwajana ezandleni zami, futhi ngenza okuqukethwe yile ncwajana kwaziwe yizinceku zami ezikhethwe ngokukhethekile.

Futhi ithini? Ithi lokhu. Bantu bami, ngiza kini ngomyalezo wami wezinsuku zokuphela nezinjongo, okuyoba ukuphethwa kwakho konke enginilungiselele khona kakade izizukulwane ngezizukulwane eziningi. Futhi ngizokwenza ukuba nifike ekugcwalisekeni kwalokho engikulungiselele, yebo, ukugcwaliseka kwalokho engakuphetha esiphambanweni lapho ngathulula igazi lokuphila kwami ngenxa yenu, ukuze ngiqinise kini iSivumelwano saphakade, isivumelwano sokuphelela kwabantu bami; isivumelwano esihlanganisa konke ukugcwala kwenkazimulo namandla ami kulabo abalungiselelwe ngokukhethekile ukwamukela.

Ngokuba ngithi kini abambalwa, yebo, insali, bayolungiselelwa ukwamukela lesi sambulo senkathi yokuphela nenjongo, engiyenza yaziwe manje. Ngoba sinamandla kakhulu ukuba samukelwe ngaphandle kweqembu elilungisiwe, yebo, "umhlambi omncane," oyokwamukela ukugcwala kwesivumelwano. Ngokuba niyohamba kulezi zinsuku zokugcina ekugcwalisekeni kwalokho enginilungiselele khona. Ngokuba khona manje ngininika isambulo salokho okwakunanyathelisiwe, kwaqiniswa, kwenziwa kwatholakala futhi kwalunga lapho igazi lokuphila kwami ligeleza eKalvari. Ngokuba akulotshiwe yini ukuthi ngakhuluma amazwi athi, "Kuphelile"? Kwakuphelile ini? Ukugcwala kwesivumelwano sami kwakuphelile; ngaleso sikhathi kwase kwenziwe ngokuphelele ukukhululwa okuphelele kuwo onke amandla esitha, nokungena okuphelele kwakho konke engikulungele futhi ngikwazi ukukudlulisela, kuhlanganise nokuqedwa kokufa. Yebo, amandla aphelele okunqoba yonke imisebenzi yesitha, nenkazimulo ephelele eyalungiswa kusukela ekusekelweni komhlaba kulabo abakulungele ukwamukela nokungena.

Kodwa akulotshiwe yini futhi ukuthi bamkhawulela oNgcwele ka-Israyeli? Bantabami, Nginguye oNgcwele ka-Israyeli! Kodwa abantu bangikhawulele. Baqinisa izinhliziyo zabo ukuze bakholwe amanga ngaleyo ndlela behluleka ukuzuza ngokugcwele konke engakulungisa futhi ngakunamathelisa, ngakuqinisa, esiphambanweni saseKalvari. Yebo, ngithi kini zonke izinto zazilungile ngaleso sikhathi. Kodwa ngangingabatholaphi abantu ababelungile—ababelungiselelwe ukukholwa ngokugcwele? Yebo, akutholakalanga muntu, ngaphandle kwalabo abafana noJoshuwa noKalebi. Onendlebe, makezwe! Ngokuba nalapha ngiyabona ukuthi izindlebe zenu zinzima, futhi angikwazi ukunembulela ngokugcwele ngalesi sikhathi ukuthi kusho ukuthini lokhu, kodwa nizokuqonda kungakapheli izinsuku eziningi.

Kodwa manje ngizokwembula ngendlela enikulungele ukuyamukela. Ngokuba incwajana iqukethe okuningi kwalolu suku nesikhathi. Kodwa kufanele ngiyembule kancane kancane, ngoba khona manje abaningi benu banjengezingane ezisanda kulumulwa obisini futhi abakalungeli ukudla okuqinile. Abanye bazokuqonda kabanzi lokho engikukhulumayo. Ngakho-ke ake nginilungise futhi ngisoke izinhliziyo zenu ngokuphelele ukuze namukele lo myalezo wezinsuku zokuphela. Ngokuba lowo ophiwe okuningi, kudingeka okuningi kuye. Nifuna ukwazi okuqukethwe yile ncwajana na? Khona-ke lungisani izinhliziyo zenu ukwamukela. Ngokuba kancane kancane izokwembulwa kini njengoba nilungela ukwamukela.

Ngokuba khona manje ngilungisa isizwe esiyolithatha ngokugcwele izwe elilungisiwe—esiyowela amagagasi aseJordani—esiyohleka amabutho kadrako—yebo, yena okufika kwakhe kunezibonakaliso nezimangaliso zamanga nokusebenza kwakhe kufana namanzi agijimayo aseJordani. Cabangani ngalokhu, bantu bami! Ngokuba akulotshiwe yini ukuthi udrako uyokuza njengesikhukhula esinamandla sokufa nokubhujiswa? Futhi lowo drako ofanayo akayikuza yini emelene nabantu bami ngesikhukhula samanga nenkohliso kulo suku?

Kodwa aniyikunqotshwa, ngokuba ngiyokwenza ukuba amanzi avinjwe ngenkathi niwela ngesandla sami esinamandla ukuze nithathe izwe enililungiselwe. Ngokuba, ngempela, leli yizwe elihle. Futhi abantu bami sebehlale isikhathi eside ehlane lokuchithakala okungokomoya. Ngithi kini 'iminyaka engamashumi amane yokuvivinywa' isiphethiwe, futhi ngokwesibili abantu bami beza eZweni Lesithembiso. Khona manje, nginilungiselela ukuwela ngamandla angokomoya. Lalelani nicabange ngalokho engikushoyo kini. Ngokuba incwajana engiyiphethe ezandleni zami iqukethe zonke izimfihlo nezimfihlakalo engifisa ukuba nizazi kulezi zinsuku.

Oh bantu bami, angilindanga yini ngokulangazela leli hora ukuze nginembulele lokho obekufihliwe? Akulona yini leli hora elilindwe yiwo wonke umlando? Akukhona yini lokhu okwakhulunywa ngabaprofethi, nakuba bona bengakuqondanga? Oh bantu bami, nikucabangile yini udumo nenkazimulo eninikwe yona yokuphila kuleli hora nokuhlanganyela kulo mkhonzo wokuphela kwesikhathi? Oh, sengathi ningavuswa ebuthongweni benu ukuze nibone inkazimulo elungiselelwe leli hora. Kungani nizothi enhliziyweni yenu isikhathi asikafiki? Kungani nizolinda ningangeni ngenhliziyo yenu yonke nomphefumulo wenu wonke? Ngithi kini, MANJE ISIKHATHI SOKUNGIFUNA NGENHLIZIYO YENU YONKE, ukuze ngikhulume nani ekamelweni elingaphakathi lenhliziyo yenu futhi nginazise iseluleko sami saleli hora. Ningalaleli labo abafuna ukunidumaza futhi baniphufumise kulokho engikukhulumayo.

Baphi labo abanesibindi SOKUPHUMA bangene emanzini obunzima novivinyo FUTHI BAHAMBE BEZA KIMI NGOKUKHOLWA, bazi ukuthi ngiyakwazi ukuniphakamisa nokunisekela kuleli hora naphakade, ukuze nihambe ngokunqoba phezu kwamanzi osizi nesiphithiphithi, okuyovivinya bonke abantu kulo suku? Hambani, ngithi, nigxilise amehlo enu kimi, ningabheki kwesokudla noma kwesobunxele; ningabheki labo abanizungezile noma amanzi anesiphithiphithi angafuna ukunigwinya.

Ngokuba ngithi kini anisoze nafike ekubizweni nasekuvukeni kwenkathi yokuphela kuze kube yilapho nivuma ngokholo ukuphuma esikebheni sokunethezeka nokuphepha kwenu kwamanje ningene EKUGELEZENI KWENHLOSO YAMI YAPHEZULU, ningavumeli amagagasi okungabaza nokwesaba aninqobe, futhi ningavumeli labo abakhetha ukuhlala esikebheni banivimbe. Kodwa WOZANI BANTU BAMI, ngokuba ngiyoba nalabo abalokothayo ukuhamba phezu kwamanzi alokho okungakaze kwenziwe ngaphambili, futhi nihlangane nami njengoba ngiza kini ngamandla nangenkazimulo kulo suku.

Ababonanga yini ukuthi kwakungumzimba ohamba phezu kwamanzi hhayi umoya njengoba babecabanga? Futhi uPetru, emzimbeni wakhe, akahambanga yini phezu kwamanzi? Kanjalo, bantu bami, akusiwo umoya kuphela kodwa UMZIMBA WENYAMA oyolethwa kimi kulo suku ukwenza izenzo ezimangalisayo phezu kwamanzi okudideka nokuhlupheka kwabantu. Anisiwo yini UMZIMBA kaKristu; aniyona inyama yami namathambo ami? Kanjalo niyoba munye nami kulo suku; kanjalo siyohlanganyela ndawonye PHEZU kwamanzi ukuze siqhubekele phambili kulo suku, isho iNkosi. Futhi kanjalo niyoba sezinsukwini ezizayo, isho iNkosi, UMZIMBA WABANQOBI—labo abanqobile konke ukungcola kwemizwa okusekujuleni kwamanzi esitha futhi abahamba ngokunqoba ngamandla okunqoba.

Bantu bami, yizwani amazwi ami! Ama-asidi esiswini awulona yini uhlobo lwemisebenzi yami phakathi kwenu? Ningathola ukondliwa kulokho okungesona isinkwa nakulokho okunganelisi? Ngithi kini, ukusebenza kukaMoya wami kubalulekile ukuze namukele lokho okunika ukuphila. Ngokuba angikwazi yini ukuthatha lokho okubalulekile eZwini lami ngikwenze kungene ebuntwini benu ukuze nithole lokho kondliwa enikudingayo? Kodwa kungani nidla lokho, okungelona izwi lami? Kungani nifuna lokho enazi ukuthi ngeke kwanelise ubujamo benu obungaphakathi?

Ngithi kini, uma ningeke ningifune ngokuphelele futhi nivumele uMoya wami unike amandla lokho enikungenisayo emizimbeni yenu, niyozithola nisesimweni esingokomoya esifana nalesi esichazwe lapha. Imisebenzi yomoya wami iyadingeka. Ngaphandle kwalokho, nithatha kuphela lokho okufile futhi okungeke kwenze lutho ukuletha ukuphila okungokomoya ebuntwini benu obungaphakathi. Ngakho-ke, qaphelani lokho enikufundayo, ngoba kuyiqiniso esibonelweni salokho okwenzekayo kubantu bami namuhla. Ngoba akunakwenzeka, esimweni samanje somhlaba webandla, ukuba ngisebenze ngoMoya wami ngendlela yokuthi iZwi lami liyoletha ukuphila nempilo nokukhululwa kubo bonke abadingayo. Ngakho-ke, ngiyaphinda ngithi, ngifuneni futhi nginiphe lokho okudingekayo ukuze niphile impilo engokomoya enamandla negcwele, isho iNkosi uJesu Kristu.

Umyalezo Wesiprofetho Onikezwe ngu-J. Leland Earls 1923-1991, ngesinye isikhathi phakathi nenkathi yeMpi Yomshoshaphansi, eyenzeka cishe ngonyaka ka-1962. Kuthathwe kule webhusayithi elandelayo: Isithunywa Esinamandla Nencwajana


Amazwana kaWilliam Brooks:

Sekukabili manje, ngithinta umphathi wale webhusayithi onakekela imisebenzi kaMnu. Earls kodwa lutho impendulo, ngakho-ke manje ngingacabanga kuphela ukuthi ngeke esaphendula. Ngaphandle kwalokho, anginalutho engingalusho nganoma yibaphi abantu abathathe umthwalo wokuba abaphathi bemisebenzi yakhe, futhi lokhu akusona neze isithunzi emisebenzini yesiprofetho eyenziwa nguMnu. Earls namazwi akhe asamile nanamuhla. INkosi ingumahluleli wethu sonke, ngakho-ke kukuKristu lapho sima noma siwa khona, futhi uKristu uyakwazi ukusimisa!

Nokho, ukuze kucace, iwebhusayithi engiyixhumanise inezimfundiso eziningi nezikhundla engingahambisani nazo futhi engingazeseki, futhi angivumelani ngokweBhayibheli nokunye kwalokho okulotshiwe. Izinto eziningi engizishoyo azibhalwanga nguMnu. Earls, kodwa kungakhathaliseki ukuthi ubani ubhala ini, funda ukuze uzibonakalise wamukelekile kuNkulunkulu, isisebenzi esingadingi ukuba namahloni, esihlukanisa ngokufanele izwi leqiniso.

Futhi ngizothatha leli thuba ngikusho futhi lokhu. Noma yini noma ubani ayishoyo lapho ethi ukhulumela uNkulunkulu, ngokwesiprofetho noma ngenye indlela, thatha lawo mazwi uwahambise emibhalweni futhi ikakhulukazi eNkosini ukuze uthole isiqinisekiso nokuqonda. Akusizi ukungibuza ngobuqiniso bomunye umprofethi. Ngeke ngiphendule ngoba angeke ngibhajwe ogibeni lokukhuluma kabi nganoma yimuphi umprofethi weqiniso kaNkulunkulu, futhi anginaso isikhathi sokuphambukisa ukunaka kwami ukuze ngifakazele imisebenzi yomunye umuntu. Singobani thina ukuba sahlulele inceku yomunye umuntu? Zima noma ziwa enkosini yazo, futhi kanjalo sikhonza iNkosi yethu uJesu Kristu. Ngakho-ke, iNkosi iyona okufanele iqinisekise noma iphike ubuqiniso banoma ubani othi ukhuluma egameni leNkosi. Uma iNkosi ikhetha ukukhuluma ngomuntu othile, ngiyakuthembisa ukuthi banobudlelwano obuqinile noKristu, futhi wazi okuningi ngabo kunalokho abazazi khona. Kuse eNkosini!
