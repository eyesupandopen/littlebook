Qinanini Futhi Nizihlambulule

INkosi ethembekileyo ithi kwabakhethiweyo nabathembekileyo bayo: Qinani futhi nizihlambulule ngokubeka phansi nokuvuma izono zenu. Phumani kulo, bantu bami, nihlukane nezono zalo ukuze ningahlanganyeli ekwahlulelweni nasezinhluphekweni zalo. Niyabuza ukuthi ngubani lona? Akusilo yini iBhabhiloni Elikhulu, okufike ukwahlulelwa kwalo? Akusilo yini isifebe esikhulu esidakwe yigazi labangcwele? Ngizokunikeza ukuphepha nokuthula, futhi ngizokuhola ngikuyise esiphephelweni endlini kaBaba kuze kuphele lesi sikhathi.

NginguNkulunkulu uJesu Kristu, owanibizela enkazimulweni nasebuhleni, ngakho-ke ningadangali ekwenzeni okuhle. Ngininikile izwi lokungikhulumela nokumemezela ukufika kwami okuseduze. Kodwa abaningi eBandleni lami balahlekelwe yizwi labo futhi baphelelwa ukholo ekulindeleni lokho uBaba anibekela kona eZwini lakhe. Ungabi ngongakholwayo kodwa kholwa kuphela. Bhekani izibonakaliso zokuza kwami, njengoba uBaba ezimemezele emazulwini futhi sezaziwa kahle. Kungani nisalokhu nephuza ukukholwa? Thembani kimi, bantwana bami, ngoba ngiyabasiza; yebo, ngithena lonke igatsha elingatheli isithelo kini. Yethembani eBukhosini bami phezu kwenu futhi niphushele ensindisweni yami njengoba nisebenzela ukusindiswa kwenu ngokwesaba nangokuthuthumela. Phendukelani kimi ngomkhuleko futhi nicele ukuba nginikhombise izono zenu ukuze nizivume kimi futhi nizinqobe ngegazi lami. Ngakho-ke ningesabi, ngoba insindiso yenu iseduze; yebo, usuku selucishe lwaqedela ukuzungeza kwalo kokugcina enkathini yami yomusa.

Phendukelani kimi nize kimi njengoba nginilungiselele indawo etafuleni lami futhi nginilungiselele indawo yokukhosela endlini kaBaba ukuze nibalekele isikhukhula sezinhlekelele esizayo. Ziyasondela kulabo abahlala emhlabeni, futhi ukwahlulelwa okukhulu sekuzowela emhlabeni wonke. Ningalilahli igama lami! Ngiyazi ukuthi nikhathele kulelihora lokugcina, kodwa phumulani kimi nithole amandla kimi futhi nimemezele ukufika kwami okuseduze. Anginikanga usuku noma ihora okufanele nililindele, ngoba benizokwakha izimpilo zenu zonke kulowo mcimbi. Kodwa ningazikhohlisi: usuku nehora kuyaziwa emazulwini ngalesi sikhathi, nezingelosi zami zimi ngomumo, zilindele ukukhaliswa kwecilongo lapho ngizobutha khona labo abalele emhlabeni kuqala, nalabo abaphilayo nabasalayo bayohlangana nabo ukuze bangihlangabeze emoyeni, futhi ngizoniyisa efeni lenu uBaba alifihlile kimi. Kuphela ningadangali.

Kuzokwenzeka ngokuphazima kweso, lapho abaningi benu bezobe bematasa nosuku lwabo, futhi ngaso leso sikhathi niyokuma phambi kwami lapho nginethula kuBaba; futhi siyalulangazelela lolo suku njengoba sobabili silangazelela ubukhona benu. Impela bengingavela ngishumayele kwabaningi, futhi ubani ongathi angikaze ngenze lokho ngezinye izikhathi? Ingabe niyomemezela lokho iNkosi engakwenza? Noma niyobeka umkhawulo emandleni ami naseBukhosini bami ngokuntula kwenu ukholo? Akwenzeki! Nokho, nginenze izisebenzi ezikanye nami futhi nganifaka emsebenzini uBaba anginike wona ukuba ngiwufeze, futhi yazini ukuthi kuwudumo nenjabulo enginiphe yona ukuze nithole insindiso, umvuzo, nokuphelela.

Njengoba nikhonze abanye ngegama lami, angikhonzanga yini nani? Yiphani ngesandla esivulekile futhi ningagcini ulwazi lokufika kwami, nakuba abaningi beyomelana futhi niyobalwa njengabahlanyayo. Amanga esitha ayoncibilika phambi kwamazwi enu agcwele amandla uma nje nikhulumela mina futhi nimemezela ukuvela kwami futhi nimemezele ukwahlulelwa okuzayo, njengoba ngisho nakule mizuzwana yokugcina abanye beyofika ensindisweni. Abaningi abakalungi okwamanje futhi bayowenqaba amazwi enu; bathandeni noma kunjalo, njengoba isixuku esikhulu siyosindiswa ezinhluphekweni nasekuhluphekeni okukhulu, futhi yazini ukuthi amazwi enu angasebenza njengesibani sokukhanya nethemba ezinhluphekweni abangabhekana nazo.

Oh, nina enihlala emhlabeni enenqabile ubizo lwami futhi nazibala njengabangafanele isipho sami sensindiso nakho konke esikuletayo: Ukwahlulelwa okukhulu kumemezele phezu komhlaba futhi sekucishe kwaba phezu kwenu, nakuba niziqambela amanga, nithi, "Nginezinhlelo ezinhle ngami futhi kusasa kuyofana nanamuhla!" Siphukuphuku! Aniqondi ngisho nokushintsha kwezinkathi zonyaka, njengoba amazulu ememezela umshado womakoti wami nami emazulwini, namazulu eshintsha esuka emseni eya ekwahlulelweni phambi kwamehlo enu. Inhlekelele enkulu iyeza phezu kwenu maduzane, futhi abaningi bayokufa ezonweni zabo. Akuyona intando yami ukuba nibhubhe, kodwa niyenqaba umhlatshelo wami ongenasono ngenxa yenu obungabasindisa ngokuphelele.

Umhlaba eniwazi namuhla uyonyamalala phambi kwamehlo enu, nazo zonke izinjabulo zesono kule mpilo ngeke zisaba abangane benu; kodwa niyozingelwa futhi nijahwe yibutho lezitha zenu ezikhanuka ukubhujiswa kwenu futhi zilangazelele impahla yenu. Kuzoba nokuzamazama okukhulu ngaphansi kwezinyawo zenu, nomhlaba uyoqhekezeka, futhi abaningi bayowela emhlabeni. Abaholi benu sebevele banikhaphele, nakuba ngobuwula nisabheke kubo, nizama ukunquma ivoti lenu. Imibhoshongo yenu iyowa, namabhuloho enu ayodilika, nengqalasizinda yenu iyonyamalala phambi kwamehlo enu, futhi akukho lutho oluyophinde lufane.

Niyobalekela kuphi lapho seniyilahlile iNkosi engcwele, mina, iNkosi uJesu Kristu? Ubani oyosindisa nina manje esikhathini senu sokwahlulelwa? Oh, kodwa nithi, angifanele ukwahlulelwa, usho ukuthini? Anizange yini nilahle uMdali wenu lapho engithumele kini ngenxa yomusa wakhe osindisayo, umusa osindisayo enizibala njengabangafanele ukuwuthola? Kungani ningilahlile? Yiliphi icala engilenze kini? Nganikela ngethonsi lami lokugcina legazi ebuhlungwini obukhulu nasosizini ukuze niphile phambi kwami, kodwa namanje anikholwa ngofakazi bami engibathume ngegama lami.

Ngisho nasekunixwayiseni ngenhlekelele yenu esondelayo, mina noBaba asinayo injabulo kulokho osekuzokhululwa. Kodwa njengoba benijatshuliswe yisono, manje seniyogwinywa niphila yimiphumela enizigcinele yona. Njengoba benikhanuka imali, impahla, nodumo lwezezimali, anenzanga lutho ngaphandle kokunqwabelanisa ulaka etafuleni lenu lokudlela, futhi niyodla usizi nobuhlungu. Ngizoninika ubumnyama enibuthanda kakhulu. Ubumnyama buyowela phezu kwezwe lenu futhi bugubungele umhlaba, ubumnyama obukhulu kangangokuthi ilanga lasemini liyobonakala sengathi licinyiwe. Nithi kimi, yisiphi isono enginaso esiletha ulaka lwakho; asenzanga yini imisebenzi eminingi emikhulu ngegama lakho?

Ngeqiniso, nenze imisebenzi eminingi, kodwa akuyona imisebenzi engininike yona; cha, seniphenduke abaprofethi bamanga, niprofetha amazwi avela enyameni yenu echezukile. Cha, nenza imisebenzi yenu ukuze nithole inkazimulo yenu, njengoba nenze izisu zenu unkulunkulu wenu. Nibambe amazwi amahle nenkulumo emnandi yabantu ababi ngaphezu kweZwi lami elingcwele. Angisilo yini iZwi elenziwa inyama? Ngabe nadla mina isinkwa sokuphila futhi nabalekela ukubhujiswa, ningaphinde nilambe. Ngabe naphuza emifuleni yamanzi aphilayo ageleza evela kimi; yebo, ngabe naphuza umoya wami futhi aniphinde nome. Kodwa ekungenqabeni kwenu, niyongena endlaleni enkulu nasekucindezelekeni, nakuba kuze kube yilo mzuzu nisacabanga ukuthi ngeke kufike phezu kwenu ngokuphazima kweso. Umnotho wenu ubhujisiwe, futhi ingcebo eniyikhanukile iyoba yize ngaso leso sikhathi. Nibeke ukholo lwenu kubabusi balobu bumnyama bamanje futhi nathanda amanga kaSathane ngaphezu kokukhanya kweqiniso lami elihlala likhona.

Ngakho-ke, niyofuna indawo etafuleni labo, bese nithola ukuthi ayikho indawo enibekelwe yona. Bayoba nokuningi okukhulu abazigcinele kona, futhi niyokwazi ukukuthola kuphela ngokuthatha uphawu lwesilo. Nakuba isilo singakambuleki ngokugcwele esikhathini saso, ngixwayisile ngaso futhi ngasikhomba kwabami abathembekileyo ukuze bangakhohliswa ngamazwi aso amahle nenkulumo emnandi, nakuba ngalesi sikhathi singenawo umbuso, okungenani okwamanje. Ningaluthathi uphawu lwaso, njengoba bonke abaluthathayo bayoba nesabelo sabo kanye nesilo nomprofethi wamanga echibini lomlilo, nomusi wokuhlushwa kwabo uyokwenyuka kuze kube phakade.

Nixwayisiwe, futhi nakuba ngisafuna ukunikhulula ekubhujisweni kwenu, nisalokhu nenqaba ngobulukhuni ubizo lwami. Ngakho-ke, ngizoninika isithelo sezindlela zenu.

Niyobhekana nendlala enkulu, impi enkulu, imbubhiso enkulu. Amabutho abomvu ayonijaha, anihluphe, futhi abhubhise abaningi benu; futhi kuyokwenzeka ukuthi nizungezwe yizingozi nembubhiso. Lowo ongathunjelwa ekuhlushweni nasekufeni ekugcineni uyokuwa ngenkemba; lowo ongawi ngenkemba uyohlangana nezilwane zasendle, eziyobulala abaningi; lowo ongabhubhi ngezilwane zasendle uyobulawa yindlala, nezindlela zokufa ziyokwanda ezweni.

Usizi olukhulu, inhlekelele, imbubhiso kuyofika kini ngokuphazima kweso, namagagasi amakhulu olwandle ayofika phezu kwesizwe senu. Anisayikuphinde nibe yisizwe esinamandla, kodwa insali isayosindiswa. Noma ubani obiza igama leNkosi uyosindiswa, kodwa lowo othanda impilo yakhe kuze kube sekupheleni uyoba nengxenye yakhe echibini lomlilo, futhi niyohlanganyela ekuhlushweni nasekujezisweni kukaSathane ngoba nibambelele ezweni lakhe elifayo kuze kube sekuphefumuleni kwenu kokugcina kwenkani, futhi ngokuqinisekile niyobhubha naleli zwe elifayo. Phendukelani kimi kusesekhona isikhathi ukuze nginisindise nina nemindeni yenu enhlekeleleni ezayo. Njengoba isixuku sathola insindiso ngosuku lwePentekoste eminyakeni eminingi edlule emizuzwini yokuqala yale nkathi, kanjalo nesixuku siyosindiswa emizuzwini yokugcina yale nkathi, futhi isimemo sami sisamile isikhashana esifushane.

Ububi obuzayo ngeke buhloniphe abesifazane noma izingane noma asebekhulile noma abagulayo. Cha, lobo bubi buyodla konke okusemandleni abo bukubuyisele ekubhujisweni okukhulu nasosizini. Gijimani nize kimi kusesekhona isikhathi, njengoba iwashi selizoma, futhi ngeke kusaba khona isikhathi. USathane uzophonswa emhlabeni maduzane, nombuso wakhe uzokhululwa ngokugcwele emhlabeni wenu, futhi unolaka olukhulu ngoba uyazi ukuthi unesikhathi esifushane. Phendukelani kimi nisesenethuba lokubalekela ububi obuzayo.

NginguNkulunkulu wamakhosi neNkosi yamakhosi.

Ngingulowo ohamba phakathi kwezinti zezibani eziyisikhombisa futhi omyala izingelosi eziyisikhombisa zamabandla ayisikhombisa.

Ngingulowo omyala amabutho ezulu, njengoba uBaba ebeke umyalo wawo ezandleni zami.

NginguLizwi elenziwa inyama owahlala phakathi kwenu.

NginguMpande kaJese, nombuso wami awunaphelo.

NginguNdodana kaNkulunkulu owafika enyameni ukuhlawulela izono zabo bonke, ukuba nje bonke bamukele.

Labo abamukele umusa wami futhi bebethembekile bayobusa nami embusweni wami futhi bahlanganyele esihlalweni sami sobukhosi, njengoba nginenze amakhosi nabapristi phambi kukaNkulunkulu wethu. Kukhona umvuzo omkhulu eniwugcinelwe wona emazulwini; qinani kuphela nithembeke eminye imizuzu embalwa.
