UMalusi Othembekile Uthi: Nifana Nezintshebe Ezidukileyo!

O, bantu bami, nifana nezimvu ezilahlekile, futhi ngamunye wenu uphambukele endleleni yakhe, izindlela enginganenzelanga zona, kodwa nilandela izindlela zabaprofethi bamanga abaqamba amanga futhi bafunge ngegama lami elingcwele, futhi ngeke bayekwe bangajeziswa. Ngiyanixwayisa ngokuqinile, bantu bami, futhi ngibiza izulu nomhlaba ukuba kufakaze ngamazwi ami: UNGABALI amazwi esiprofetho anikezwe yimi, iNkosi uJesu Kristu, njengento engcolile; kodwa kunalokho, hlolani wonke amazwi engiwanikezayo. Uma nenqaba, kuyisono esibalelwa kini.

Ngiyanitshela ngaphambi kokuba ngiqhubeke, abaningi benu bazovimba izindlebe zabo futhi bavale amehlo abo emazwini engizowanikeza, futhi ngiyaneluleka ukuba ningashwabani ngokwesaba. Abaningi benu bazowa ngaphansi kwengcindezi yabangane nemindeni, ingcindezi evela emasontweni omgunyathi nasezikhungweni zezwe, becabanga ukuthi izifundiswa zezwe zingimelele ekubeni ulwazi lwezwe lungekaSathane uqobo lwakhe, futhi ulwazi lwakhe lokuhle nokubi luhlala luletha ukufa, ngoba wahlela kanjalo amazwi akhe ukuba afeze lokho. Ningabi phakathi kwalabo abenqaba amazwi ami, kodwa lalelani ngomphefumulo wenu wonke futhi nicabangele engizokusho, ngoba amazwi ami angukuphila, futhi azoqinisa ukubizwa nokukhethwa kwenu.

Kukuphi eZwini lami elingcwele lapho nibona khona into efana nobuKristu beZiyoni? Aniqondi yini ukuthi abaholi babo bayisinagoge likaSathane? Yebo, kukhona abangene ngokunyenya kwa-Israyeli abangesibo abakwa-Israyeli futhi abangesinagoge likaSathane, futhi baqamba amanga, bethi bangamaJuda kodwa bengesiwo! Nina, Bandla lami, ningu-Israyeli kaNkulunkulu, futhi anithathi ndawo yamunye, futhi lena akuyona imfundiso yokuthatha indawo, kodwa yiZwi lami elingcwele. Ziboneleni nani! Cha! Angikhulumi ngokuphikisana nabami, nakuba kukhona phakathi kwenu abahlakaniphile ngokuzicabangela kwabo abazosho lokho kanye.

Hlolani uhlu lozalo lwami, njengoba ngazalwa kokhokho, futhi isizinda sami siqinile; futhi uma kukhona phakathi kwenu oshisekela u-Israyeli, mina ngingaphezulu, ngoba nganikela ngokuphila kwami ngenxa yabo! Obani umama nabafowethu? Ngoba noma ngubani owenza intando kaNkulunkulu ungumama wami nomfowethu, anifundanga yini? Nginihlukanisela: okuhle nokubi, njengoba abaningi benu bentula ukuqonda kweqiniso okungokomoya futhi baphuphuthekiswe amanga ababi. Anifundanga yini ukuthi uNkulunkulu ngezinye izikhathi ufeza izindlela zakhe ngesandla sezitha zakhe, ngoba ngisho nezitha zakhe ziyalalela izwi lakhe? Yebo, u-Israyeli wamiswa kabusha ngo-1948, futhi ngithanda abaningi ezweni futhi ngizobasindisa; kodwa u-Israyeli wamiswa kabusha ngabantu ababi, futhi abaholi babo banezinhloso ezimbi ezifihliwe. Akusona esenu, bantwana bami, ukuba nibe nesandla kule ngxenye yomsebenzi kaBaba ngalesi sikhathi, nakuba nizosiza esikhathini esizayo eMbusweni wami; kodwa kukimi ukusindisa uJakobe, hhayi kini!

Nidukiswe ngamazwi amahle nangezinkulumo ezibushelelezi futhi nakhohliswa yiqhinga elihlakaniphile. Niloyiwe yimimoya kaJezebeli, esebenza ngezindlela zikaBalami. Yazini ukuthi ngizokwephula ukubamba kwayo phezu kwenu futhi ngivule amehlo nezindlebe zenu uma nje niphendukela kimi ngokuthobeka nangokuzimisela ukuza eZwini lami futhi nifunde kimi ngaphandle kwemibono yenu eniyifunde kule mimoya yamanga eprofetha ngabaprofethi bayo bamanga. Imimoya kaJezebeli ekhuluma amanga amakhulu ngabaprofethi bayo bamanga, ngisho nezibonakaliso nezimangaliso zamanga, futhi iprofethela inzuzo yobugovu ngomoya kaBalami, le mimoya ingene ngokunyenya emasontweni enu, futhi nidla ukungcola okuvela ezandleni zayo.

Nikhohliswe yisinagoge likaSathane ukuba ningene esivumelwaneni samanga nalo, isinagoge likaSathane. Abanye eBandleni lami abathembekile emazwini nasemiyalweni yami ngoba bakholwe amanga akhulunywe ngegama lami, behlanganyela nabo ebumpumputheni babo, okuyinto efanele. Ningazivumeli nitholakale phakathi kwabo lapho ngifika; angishongo yini, phumani kulo nahlukane, ngoba uKristu uhlangene ngani noBeliyali? Gezani udaka emehlweni enu ngegazi lami lokuhlawulela futhi niwavule ukuze nibone ukukhanya kweqiniso engizoninika kona, njengoba amehlo enu embozwe ngodaka, futhi anikwazi ukuwavula ngaphandle kwami.

Njengoba kulotshiwe, uNkulunkulu ubanike umoya wokulala, amehlo ukuze bangaboni, nezindlebe ukuze bangezwa kuze kube namuhla, futhi ngamazwi amahle nangezinkulumo ezibushelelezi nihlanganyele nabo ebumpumputheni babo, ningagcini nje ngokungaziboni izinhloso zami ngo-Israyeli kodwa futhi niphuphuthekiswe ekubizweni kwenu kweqiniso kimi, uKristu Jesu, iNkosi yenu! Uma niphuphuthekile, anikhululekile ukufunda nokuqonda amazwi ami, futhi anihambi emoyeni. Ngakho-ke, landelani amazwi ami, bantu bami; ngoba nakuba naningazi ngokwenyama, anisekho ngazi ngokwenyama. Ngoba sengiguquliwe ngaba umoya ophilayo, onike umoya wokuphila ku-Israyeli kaNkulunkulu, wona umoya wokuba ngamadodana enikhala ngawo nithi ABBA, Baba!

Manje iNkosi ingulowo Moya: futhi lapho uMoya weNkosi ukhona, kukhona inkululeko; ngakho-ke, yimani niqinile enkululekweni uKristu anikhulule ngayo futhi ningabuyi niboshelwe ejokeni lobugqila. Thobani izindlebe zenu futhi niphendule izinhliziyo zenu ukuzwa iZwi lami, futhi ningalenqabi! UMoya uyaphilisa; inyama ayisizi ngalutho. Amazwi engiwakhuluma kini angumoya futhi angukuphila. Ngakho-ke, ningazifanisi no-Israyeli ngokwenyama, futhi niphume emangeni akhe ezombangazwe, kodwa nifaniswe nomoya wami owenziwe ngomfanekiso kaNkulunkulu!

Aniqondi yini? Amanga abo avunyelwe ukulungiselela indodana yokulahlwa, ukuze bonke balahlwe labo abathokoza ngokungalungi, amagama abo angalotshiwe eNcwadini Yokuphila kusukela ekusekelweni komhlaba. Lalelani amazwi ami okusola futhi ningazenzi lukhuni izinhliziyo zenu, ngoba amazwi ami azonisindisa osizini oluzayo futhi azonihlukanisa nalabo abalwa nami futhi abangalahlile cishe iminyaka engu-2000 manje. Futhi nakuba ubumpumputhe ngokwengxenye benzekile ku-Israyeli, insali kaJakobe izophendukela kimi futhi yamukele ukulunga okungaveli emthethweni kodwa ngokukholwa kimi. Hlanzani imicabango yenu futhi nizithintithe amanga ezimpisi abawanyenyeze ngokukhohlisa ezindlebeni zenu, bekhuluma amanga ngegama lami, ekubeni ngingabathumanga.

Kukhona ububi obukhulu phezu kwezwe lami, futhi ngizofika ngizosindisa. Kukhona ububi obukhulu phezu kukaJakobe ngoba bazivumele ukuba baguqulwe ngabaqambi-manga base-Edomu. Anikholwanga yini umbiko wami wokuthi isinagoge likaSathane likhona futhi lingokoqobo? Yazini ukuthi bahamba phakathi kwenu! Aniqondi yini ukuthi oyimpumputhe akanakuholela oyimpumputhe, futhi u-Israyeli uphuphuthekiswe ngokwengxenye kuze kungene ukugcwala kwabeZizwe? Ngisazosindisa insali ethembekile kuJakobe, futhi ngibeka uphawu kwabayizi-144,000. Futhi bazongenzela imisebenzi emikhulu, futhi ngizobathuma ngapha nangale phakathi nosizi lukaJakobe, futhi bazosindisa abaningi, futhi bayokwenza imisebenzi eminingi emikhulu, futhi ngizobaholela ukuholela abanye, njengoba kungekho enye indlela eya kuBaba ngaphandle kwami, iNkosi uJesu Kristu, uMesiya abamlahlayo.

Kodwa nina, o, maKristu anamuhla, nina bantwana bami abasivivi, nicabanga ukuthi nazi kangcono ngoba nifunde imibhalo yabo engeniswe ngokunyenya phakathi kwenu. Futhi nikholwe umbiko wabo wamanga, umbiko wamanga ovela esinagogeni lase-Edomu likaSathane, ihlelo lokwakha ithempeli, bona ababhubhisi uSathane abathumile ukuba banqobe umhlaba. Futhi bahola isizwe eningasikhonza esikhundleni sikaBaba uma nilandela izinganekwane zabo ezakhiwe ngobuhlakani, bezibiza ngokuthi bangabantu abakhethiweyo bakaNkulunkulu futhi benikholisa ukuba nibabusise ekubeni bezonibhubhisa ngethuba lokuqala. Yazini ukuthi angithumelanga isixazululo sezombangazwe, futhi ohulumeni bomhlaba bakhonza uSathane, obathenge ngenkokhelo yokungalungi.

Anifundanga futhi aniqondanga yini ukuthi yimina ngedwa isixazululo, futhi ngiyiNkosi yeqhawe ethunywe nguYahweh uqobo ukuba anqobe umbuso ngokulunga futhi achithe konke ukubusa negunya likaSathane? Yebo, ngizokwephula ukubusa kwakhe kube yizicucu, kungabe kusaphinde kululame, futhi ngizolimaza ikhanda lakhe eliqamba amanga njengoba kwaprofethwa, futhi uzosha phakade emalangabini ashisayo aluhlaza okwesibhakabhaka, futhi uzobhukuda esibabule esivuthayo kulelo chibi elikhulu ngalolo suku olukhulu, futhi ngeke aphinde alulame kodwa uzobhunjiswa futhi ashe phakade. Yazini ukuthi akukho lunya olungenabulungiswa ekwahluleleni kukaBaba, kodwa kuwumvuzo ofanele wezenzo zikaSathane siqu nezifiso zakhe siqu ngabantwana bakaBaba nendalo, ngakho uBaba uzomnika lokho anifisela khona ekwahluleleni kwakhe.

Anifundanga yini? INkosi iyophendula, ithi kubo: Ngiqinisile ngithi kini: Ngenxa yokuthi nikwenzile komunye walaba abancane babafowethu, nikwenzile kimi. Kungani nilandela abaholi bezombangazwe bamanga njengokungathi nilandela mina? Ngizonitshela ukuthi kungani ukuze niqonde ngokugcwele: Abaningi benu balandela amanga asemaBhayibhelini enu okucwaninga engingawabhalanga, futhi angibathumanga labo abahlanganisa imibhalo okuthiwa eye-"Christian-Zionism". Ayikho into enjengobuKristu beZiyoni, ngoba angisiye umsekeli wobukhulu bamaJuda. Ukube ngangiyiZiyoni, ngangingeke ngiphendukele kwabeZizwe, futhi izizwe zangamukela isikhashana. Cha, basebenzisa iZiyoni ukungenisa indodana yokulahlwa ngethempeli labo abazolakha ngokungazi egameni likaBaba, futhi abamhloniphi uBaba ngoba bayangilahla mina, Jesu Kristu. Akukhona nje ukuthi ngeza kwabami futhi ngalahlwa ngabami, kodwa abaningi babo basangilahla kuze kube namuhla. Kodwa nina, o, bandla laseLawodikeya, nicabanga ukuthi ningasheshisa ukugcwaliseka kwesiprofetho ngokulandela ubuhlanya babo; ngoba isinagoge likaSathane lifuna umhlaba walo, umhlaba ongenani, bantwana bami. Anginisindisanga yini kubo?

Yazini ukuthi uSathane wazama ukuntshontsha ifa lami kimi, ngoba umhlaba ungowaseNkosini nokugcwala kwawo. USathane ufuna umhlaba ongenaye uNkulunkulu, umhlaba okhululekile kuNkulunkulu, umhlaba ongenami, itshe legumbi eliyinhloko, nomhlaba lapho kusinda khona abanamandla njengoba beqothula umhlaba kubo bonke ngaphandle kwembewu kaSathane uqobo, abayiyo. U-Israyeli useminyakeni eyizinkulungwane manje ungenwe ngokunyenya yimibhalo yabo yaseBabiloni (yebo, bahlela amasiko omlomo adluliselwa phansi) engilahlayo, iNkosi uJesu Kristu, okuyinto engenakwenzeka ukuyifinyelela ngoba ngeke ngiphikwe. Futhi umhlaba usuzoqonda intukuthelo namandla ami, ngoba ngimkhulu kunabo bonke ngaphandle kukaBaba, futhi akunakwenzeka ukungimisa.

Ngilinde ngokubekezela nangokubhekakade, ngivumele igama lami lihlanzwe futhi umhlatshelo wami wokuhlawulela ubalwe njengento engcolile ukuze uMlobokazi wami asindiswe, wona uMlobokazi owayefihliwe ezikhathini zasendulo em misterini omkhulu kaNkulunkulu, imfihlo engayembula ngokugcwele kuMphostoli uPawulu. Futhi ningafunda imibhalo yakhe ukuze niqonde, ngoba akazikhulumelanga yena, kodwa wamemezela ivangeli leMisteri omkhulu kaNkulunkulu engamnika wona ukuba awembulele mina. Akekho ozothatha okungokwami, ngoba ngikukhokhele ngegazi lami lokuhlawulela, futhi ngizobuyisela kwabenza okubi.

USathane ufuna umhlaba ongenani, bantwana bami, kodwa abaningi benu ngokobuwula banikela ngemali yenu ukusekela isinagoge likaSathane nezinhlelo zalo ezingcwele ezweni lami naphezu kwezinsali zesizwe sakwa-Israyeli, isizwe lapho ngizobusa khona umhlaba esihlalweni sobukhosi sikaDavide embusweni wami ozokwembulwa maduze. Nisekela ukufa kwenu, nicabanga ukuthi niqhubekisela phambili ivangeli. Kungani ningakwazi ukuqonda ukuthi ukufa nokubhujiswa kanye nababusi ababulala abantu bonke AKUKHO kimi kodwa kuyimbewu kaSathane uqobo; yebo, uyise wabo nguDeveli, uYise waManga, uSathane uqobo. Yazini ukuthi uyisitha esehluliwe, futhi wazi ngokwesaba ukuthi izinsuku zakhe zimbalwa, futhi wesaba ikusasa lakhe ngaphezu kwanoma yini, ngoba wazi ukuthi ngeke kube khona ukukhululeka esijezisweni sakhe, ngoba uNkulunkulu uqobo lwakhe umemezela kanjalo! Bantwana bami, anikwazi ukulandela uSathane nikhonze mina. Ngoba akakho umuntu ongakhonza amakhosi amabili: ngoba uyakuzonda enye, athande enye; noma uyakunamathela kwenye, adelele enye. Ngakho-ke khethani ukuthi nizokhonza bani, ngoba angikaze ngimisele iBandla lami ukuba libe nanoma yini ehlangene nalokho okuthiwa yi-"Christian-Zionism", futhi leyo nhlangano ayiveli kimi, kodwa kuSathane, futhi uyisebenzisela ukungenisa indodana yokulahlwa. Abaningi benu balahlekelwe incazelo yamazwi ami, becabanga ukuthi “anti” kusho ukuphikisana ngokuqinile kuphela. Cha, umuntu wesono uthunyelwa ngobuhlakani esikhundleni sami kubantu abangalahlile, kodwa ngizomsindisa uJakobe ngosizi olukhulu, futhi angizukuhlanza izwe lami lefa kuphela, kodwa ngizohlanza umhlaba wonke ebubini nasesonweni. Qondani ukuthi ngizomsindisa uJakobe ngezithembiso uBaba azenza kokhokho babo, futhi akukhona ngokufaneleka noma ngokulunga kwabo, ngoba ayikho inyama elungileyo emehlweni ami.

Ngiyaneluleka ukuba nibeke phansi imibhalo yalabo abangakhulumeli mina, futhi nihlakaniphe, bantwana bami; ngoba abaningi benu abanalwazi kangangokuba anikwazi ukuhlukanisa amanga eqinisweni. Ngoba aningibambi njengenhloko yenu kodwa nibambe amazwi akhulunywe ngabaprofethi bamanga bakaJezebeli, abasebenza ngezindlela zikaBalami, belandela umvuzo wokungalungi. Ngoba iZwi likaNkulunkulu liyaphila, linamandla, futhi libukhali kunanoma iyiphi inkemba esika nhlangothi zombili, lihlaba kuze lehlukanise umphefumulo nomoya, amalunga nomnkantsha, futhi liyakwazi ukwahlulela imicabango nezinhloso zenhliziyo. Buyelani eZwini lami elihlanzekile likaNkulunkulu, ngoba uBaba uyalivikela kuze kube namuhla.

Zwanini indlela yami yokwenza, bantu bami, nifunde ngami. Angikhethanga izwi elilodwa ukuba limele imibhalo yami, futhi angikhethanga izwi elidala kunawo wonke ukuba libuse amazwi ami futhi liqinisekise ubuqiniso bawo. Cha, ngezindlela zikaBaba (uBaba wami akadingi meluleki ezinhlelweni zakhe) nasekuhlakanipheni kukaBaba wakhetha amazwi amaningi avela ezikhathini eziningi, ezindaweni eziningi, nasemasikweni amaningi avumelana wodwa. Bhekani kwabaseByzantine, bantwana bami, ningangabazi amazwi ami, futhi nigxile emibhalweni engiyilondolozile, futhi nizithobe emazwini ami. Aniyalelwanga yini ukuthi nifundisise ukuze nizibonakalise ningabamukelekileyo? Ngokuba abantwana baleli zwe bahlakaniphile esizukulwaneni sabo kunabantwana bokukhanya: Ngokuba bahlakaniphele ebubini nasenkohlisweni, futhi abenzi lutho ngaphandle kokweba, ukubulala, nokubhubhisa.

UBaba wami ulivikele izwi lakhe elihlanzekile likaNkulunkulu, futhi lilapha phambi kwenu, kodwa niyalenqaba ukuze ninakekelwe ezifisweni zenu zokuvilapha ngezinguqulo ezifundeka kalula, izinguqulo ezikhohlakele engingazithumelanga futhi engingazibhalanga. Kungani nizofulathela la mazwi? Ebumpofu benu bobuwula, anizwisisi ukuthi uSathane wenza ngisho nemiBhalo Engcwele inkohliso. Ukwenza lokho ngendlela efanayo nomkhohlisi ongathembekile angakhohlisa ngayo ngokususa izixhumanisi ochungechungeni ngasinye ngasinye ukuze enze uchungechunge lube lufushane kungaboni muntu. Imingcele ayisuswa kancane kancane yini? Ngoba yimuphi umthiyi obeka ugibe emehlweni enyoni? Ushintshe amagama, wasusa izigaba eziphelele namavesi, wengeza amagama, futhi ukwenzile konke lokho emzamweni wokuhlanganisa zonke izinkolo ngaphansi komzamo we-ecumenical wokuthobisa bonke kuKristu wakhe wamanga, ohamba phakathi kwenu obala, ozokwambulwa maduze.

Ngizophonsa isilo nomprofethi wamanga bephila echibini elivutha umlilo nesibabule ukuze bafike efeni labo, ifa uBaba wami alilungisele uDeveli nezingelosi zakhe. Ngokuba uzobanika izwe labo kanye nezwe elifanele izifiso zabo ezimbi abazifisela nina, bantwana bami. Anifundanga noma nikhohliwe ukuthi amahlelo obuGnostic ashintsha izincwadi zami ngokushesha nje lapho ezithola? Futhi nicabanga ukuthi ngeke kwenzeke ezikhathini zenu, lapho ulwazi lwandile?

Funanani izwi lami elihlanzekile, futhi nibe nokholo lokuthi ngiyakwazi ukunikeza abahumushi abathembekile. Kholwani emazwini engiwanikezile; kholwani emazwini eZwi likaNkulunkulu. Ningabi yiziwula kangangokuthi nicabange ukuthi noma yimuphi umbhalo uphelele; angishongo yini kini ukuthi nifundisise ukuze nizibonakalise ningabamukelekileyo? Kodwa bantu bami, lapho nifunda, nivumela izitha zesiphambano ukuba zichaze amazwi ami ukuze nikhohliswe ukuba ninikeze imvume esinagogeni likaSathane. Ngokungazi ninikeza imvume yenu nokuvuma okuthulile kuSathane nabantwana bakhe abathathe amazwe ami angcwele, kodwa okwesikhashana, futhi ukuhlupheka kukaJakobe kuzobasusa ezweni, nabathobekileyo bayodla ifa lomhlaba. Kholwani kimi, iZwi elenziwa inyama, ukuba nginiphe iqiniso; futhi iqiniso lisemiBhalweni yami eNgcwele, ngoba ikhuluma ngami.

Niyawenqaba yini amazwi ami, bantu bami? Niyokhetha yini ukungazethembi izithunywa engizithumile? Ngokuba ngithume eziningi! Abaningi benu bazophikelela ebuhlanyini benu, bengazihluphi ngisho nokuqinisekisa amazwi alesi siprofetho, futhi niyokwenza lokho kube yingozi nokulahlekelwa umvuzo wenu. Ngokushesha nje lapho umvimbeli esusiwe noMlobokazi wami ethathiwe, bazoshesha kakhulu ukubulala konke nanoma ubani ongumKristu. Bazosusa ubuKristu emhlabeni uma bebengakwazi, futhi bazoshushisa futhi bafezele abangcwele bami ngezindlela eziningi, futhi labo abakhuleka egameni leNkosi bayosindiswa.

Phendukelani kimi manje, nihlanze izinhliziyo zenu emazwini abo amanga. Nizibizele amazwi okufa nokubhujiswa ngokungazi ngokuphelele umlando wenu. Ngininika inselelo yokufunda umlando wezincwadi engingazibhalanga eniziphakamisa ngaphezu kwamazwi ami. Fundani ukuthi ababhali babengobani ngempela, ngokuba babhala amanga amakhulu ezincwadini zabo, ngokuba niyobazi ngezithelo zabo. Eziningi zalezi zincwadi zenziwe izinga elijwayelekile ezikoleni zenu zemfundiso yenkolo; funani, ngokuba kuyiqiniso! Uma nikhuthele kulezi zindaba futhi nibambelele kimi, khona-ke ngizonihola kulo lonke iqiniso, futhi niyogcina niqonde imfihlakalo yobuNkulunkulu eniyiyo. Kodwa ngisho nemfihlakalo enkulu yehliswe isithunzi ezingqondweni nasezinhliziyweni zenu ngamanga abo; leyo mfihlakalo enkulu ingumuntu omusha oyedwa othathwe kumaJuda nabeZizwe ngokufanayo, lapho kungekho khona udonga lokwehlukanisa, ngokuba lolo donga lwasuswa kimi.

Ngibusisa labo ababizwe ngegama lami, futhi nakuba ngenza ukuba line phezu kwabalungileyo nabangalungile ngokufanayo, zonke izibusiso zikimi, uKristu Jesu iNkosi yenu, iNkosi yeqiniso yamakhosi noMbusi weqiniso wamakhosi. Ngakho-ke, phendukelani kimi ngokuphenduka, ngokuba nithathe amanga kaJezebeli njengeqiniso levangeli, futhi nizibizele ulaka ngokwenza kanjalo; ngokuba ngiyokuvuza ngisho nabantwana bami ngokwemisebenzi yabo, nakuba ngingeke nginahlulele ngezono zenu.

Ngingulowo amazulu okumelwe amamukele kuze kufike izikhathi zokubuyiselwa kwazo zonke izinto.

Ngingulowo owabhapathizwa nguJohane uMbhapathizi lapho umoya wehla njengejuba kuzwakala izwi likaBaba ngokwakhe.

Ngingulowo owafika kwabakhe futhi owenqatshwa, umuntu wosizi owakhokha inani eliphakeme kunawo wonke ngenxa yenu nonke.

Ngimkhulu kunabo bonke ngaphandle kukaBaba wami, uYahweh.
