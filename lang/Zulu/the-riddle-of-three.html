Ubufakazi Obumbaxambili: Israyeli
Okubabayo Nokumnandi
Septhemba 5, 2024

Imfumbe Yezinto Ezintathu!

Lalelani izwi likaYeshua HaMashiach, bathandwa bami, bese niza kimi ukuze nithole induduzo futhi nithole ukuphumula othandweni lwami ngani, nina bancane bami. Ngoba nithembekile ezintweni ezimbalwa, manje ngizonenza umbusi phezu kokuningi njengoba ningena enjabulweni yeNkosi yenu. Ukwahlulelwa akusikhona okwenu, nina abathembekileyo bami; ngisho nabo bonke abakuKristu nabo bonke abalele kuKristu bayovuselwa ekuphileni okusha ngokungazelele nangokuphazima kweso. Ngoba ngeqiniso, ngiyokwehla ezulwini ngihuba, ngezwi lengelosi enkulu, nangecilongo likaNkulunkulu: nabafileyo kuKristu bayovuselwa ekuphileni okungenakonakala, nani eniphilayo nisasele niyohlwithwa kanye nabo emafwini, kanjalo niyohlala ninjengeNkosi yenu yaphakade. Niyoguqulwa ngokushesha kusukela esimweni senu samanje esiwileyo nibe yisidalwa esisha esihlengwe ngokugcwele sokungonakali, ngisho nidalwe kabusha njengomuntu munye omusha, nanjengoba nginjalo nami, nani niyoba njalo; futhi njengoba kulotshiwe, kuyokwenziwa kanjalo ngokungazelele.

Kungani izinhliziyo zenu zizikhathaza, bantabami? Kungani nivumela amazwi ommangaleli anikhathaze aze anenze nicabange ukuthi alukho uhlwitho, noma ukuthi anifanele umusa wami osindisayo? Ngiyaniyeluleka, bantabami, ukuthi niqhubeke nihambe ngokukholwa hhayi ngokubona; futhi yimi onenze nafaneleka, ngisho nabo bonke abakuKristu futhi abanobudlelwane obusindisayo neNkosi yabo elungileyo, ngakho-ke ningangabazi! Nakuba ningangibonanga ngamehlo enu enyama, nazi kahle ukuthi ngiyiNkosi yenu ephilayo nomsindisi wenu, futhi kimi kukhona ukugcwala kokuphila nokukhululwa. Ngiqinisile, ngiqinisile, lowo okholwa yimi ngeke aphinde alambe noma ome futhi.

Ngoba ngiyisinkwa sokuphila, nabo bonke abadlayo ngeke baphinde balambe; futhi yimi okuphuma kimi imifula yamanzi aphilayo ngobuningi nobusha bokuphila, nalowo ophuzayo kuyo ngeke aphinde ome. Kodwa nonke niyokwenziwa niphile empilweni ephelele, ekuvumelaneni okuphelele, nasebunyeni obuphelele noYahweh, neNkosi yenu ephilayo nomsindisi wenu, ngoba nginguKristu Jesu, owanithenga ngegazi lami elihlawulayo, futhi ngeqiniso aninakulinganiswa nentengo. Kanjalo-ke kwaba yinkokhelo engenakulinganiswa nentengo engayinikeza ngaphakade ngami njengewundlu likaNkulunkulu, ngakho-ke ngaletha umhlatshelo wami ngenxa yenu njengompristi omkhulu naphakade ngokohlelo lukaMelkisedeki futhi ngakhokha isihlengo senu ngegazi lami elingenasono.

Kanjalo kuze kube phakade ukuthi asisekho isidingo somhlatshelo wesono, ngoba uma igazi lezinkunzi nelezimbuzi, nomlotha wesithokazi, kufafazwa phezu kwabangcolileyo, kungcwelisa mayelana nokuhlanzwa kwenyama, kakhulu kangakanani-ke igazi likaKristu, owazinikela ngokwakhe ngaphandle kwecala kuNkulunkulu ngoMoya waphakade, liyohlambulula unembeza wenu emisebenzini efileyo, ukuze nikhonze uNkulunkulu ophilayo? Futhi lokho umhlatshelo wezilwane owawungenakukufeza, igazi lami elihlawulayo lakwazi ukukufeza kanye naphakade, njengoba ngenzile inhlawulo yezono zesintu kanye naphakade, ngisho nakubo bonke abakhetha ukwamukela insindiso egameni lami, ngoba alikho elinye igama elinikezwe ngaphansi kwezulu abantu abangasindiswa ngalo. Ngakho-ke ningawakholwa amanga abo, ngoba udrako, isilo, nomprofethi wamanga bayovuka, futhi bayofuna ukuhlanganisa zonke izinkolo zomhlaba zamanga ngaphansi kwesibhengezo esisha, ngaphandle kokuthi uJesu Kristu ngeke avunyelwe phakathi kwabo, kungaba ngegama noma ngesenzo, futhi banikiwe ukuba balwe nabangcwele futhi babanqobe. Ngiqinisile, ngiqinisile, anginayo ingxenye noma isabelo nabo, futhi yimi engiyobabhubhisa ngokuphelele, ngoba ngiza ngokulunga ukuze ngahlulele futhi ngilwe.

Manje ngikhuluma nalabo abayokuza kimi ngesikhathi sosizi lukaJakobe, ngisho nabangcwele bosizi abayokuza emvuzweni omkhulu nasefeni laphakade eNkosini yabo yaphakade noMesiya, yena uYeshua HaMashiach. Futhi kulotshiwe kanjalo njengeqiniso laphakade, ngakho ngisho phakade naphakade ukuthi ningene ngesango elincane: ngoba isango libanzi, nendlela ibanzi eholela ekubhujisweni: futhi baningi abangena ngalo, Ngoba isango lincane, nendlela incane eholela ekuphileni, futhi bambalwa abayitholayo. Ngakho ngiyaniyala, nina bancane bami, ukuthi nihlale endleleni eqondile nencane futhi nigweme ukubhujiswa kweziwula, ngisho nalabo amagama abo angalotshwanga eNcwadini Yokuphila kusukela ekusekelweni komhlaba, ngoba bathanda izindlela zokubhujiswa, futhi bathanda ukungalungi, futhi bakhetha inkohliso enamandla kunokulunga neqiniso.

Ngoba bayogijima baye ngapha nangapha, futhi impi enkulu iyeza emhlabeni, nembubhiso enkulu isondela futhi iseduze; ingozi, isexwayiso esiphuthumayo esifana no-911, nosuku lusondela kakhulu futhi selufikile phezu kwenu. Futhi bayovuka emlotheni wempi, futhi emlotheni wempi bayoziveza esintwini futhi, nesintu siyomangala ngabawileyo ngokwesaba okukhulu ngenxa yamaqhinga abo abebelokhu bethakatha ngawo abalahlekileyo kusukela ekuqaleni. Bayohlanganisa izinkolo zabo zamanga, futhi bayonitshela amanga amaningi akhulunywa ngemilomo yemimoya yamanga yomphikukristu, ngoba baningi abaphikukristu abaphumele ezweni.

Uyini umphikukristu, bantabami? Umphikukristu uphikisana noKristu, umsindisi weqiniso noMesiya, futhi uthatha nendawo kaKristu; ngoba nginguYeshua HaMashiach, futhi akekho omunye. Ngakho amanga abo asevele agcwele emhlabeni njengoba sebelungiselele izixuku ukuba zilalele laba abathathu abangcwele, yebo udrako, isilo sasolwandle, nesilo sasemhlabeni, esingumprofethi wabo wamanga. Kodwa uJohane wabhalani ngale ndaba encwadini yakhe yokuqala? Anginibhalelanga ngoba ningalazi iqiniso, kodwa ngoba niyalazi, nokuthi akukho manga avela eqinisweni. Ubani umqambimanga ngaphandle kwalowo ophikayo ukuthi uJesu unguKristu? Ungumphikukristu, lowo ophika uYise, neNdodana. Noma ubani ophika iNdodana, lowo akanaye uYise; kodwa lowo ovuma iNdodana, unaye noYise.

Kunjalo-ke, ukuthi mina, uJesu waseNazaretha, ngiyindlela, neqiniso, nokuphila; futhi akekho neyodwa oza kuYahweh ngaphandle kokuba eze ngami. Ngiwumnyango owodwa, futhi ngiyindlela eyodwa eya kuYahweh, uNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade; ngoba uYahweh ushilo kanjalo futhi uYahweh uhlelile wanquma kanjalo kusukela ngaphambi kokusekelwa komhlaba ngokwazi kwakhe kwaphambili nangokuhlakanipha kwakhe. Ubani kini ongameluleka uYahweh, uNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade? Nakuba izinkulungwane eziyishumi eziphindwe ngeziyishumi zizungeze isihlalo sakhe sobukhosi, akadingi meluleki; futhi nguYahweh, uNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade, osebenza zonke izinto ngokweluleka kwentando yakhe ukuze nibe ludumo lwenkazimulo yakhe nina enaqale nathembela kuKristu.

Ngakho-ke qondisisani inkohliso ezayo eseyifikile emhlabeni. Ngoba izinceku zikaSathane zikhuluma ziphikisana neqiniso, futhi zimemeza zithi zonke izinkolo zomhlaba ziholela kunkulunkulu ofanayo kungakhathaliseki ukuthi zivelaphi, kungakhathaliseki izinkolelo zazo, zithi wonke amanye amagama anikezwe ngabawileyo ayiqiniso futhi aholela kunkulunkulu weqiniso, kodwa ziholela kudrako kuphela. Ngiqinisile, ngiqinisile ngithi kini, ningawakholwa amanga abo, futhi ningabafiseli ngisho nenhlanhla labo abakholelwa ebuhleni obunjalo. Ngoba isango lokubhujiswa libanzi, eliholela kulelo chibi elikhulu lomlilo ovuthayo, futhi uSathane ufuna ukuphusha abaningi ngangokunokwenzeka endleleni yakhe eya ekubhujisweni kwaphakade, ngoba liyilolo ifa lakhe, futhi injalo inzondo yakhe ngani nonke. Ningawakholwa amanga abo okubumbana kwezinkolo, futhi nibaleke ekuhlanganeni kwabo okungcwele, ngoba isiphetho sakho ngamalangabi ashisa kakhulu aluhlaza okwesibhakabhaka njengoba isibabule esivuthayo sigingqika futhi sisha phakade, futhi akekho oyophunyuka kulabo ababoshiwe baphonswa emlilweni.

Qondisisani, bantabami, ukuthi wonke amagama abo aholela kunkulunkulu ofanayo, yena uSathane, ngoba ababona onkulunkulu kodwa bangabazenzisi abazenzisayo. Ngakho-ke bayophakamisa amafulegi abo okwehlukahlukana, futhi bayothi amagama amaningi aholela kunkulunkulu ofanayo. Futhi ngakolunye uhlangothi baqinisile ngoba ziningi izindlela eziholela kunkulunkulu walo mhlaba, okwahlulelwa kwakhe sekufikile nokujeziswa nokubhujiswa kwakhe kungalibali. Futhi ngakolunye uhlangothi, baqamba amanga emhlabeni futhi bafuna ukuyenga bonke abayolandela izindlela zabo zokubhujiswa ukuba bangene emphakathini wabo omusha oyovuka emlotheni wempi ozodinga uphawu ukuze kungene kuwo.

Ningaluthathi uphawu lwabo, ngoba bonke abaluthathayo baqalekisiwe phakade futhi baguqulwe unomphela. Futhi baqamba amanga amabi kakhulu bethi wonke amagama aholela kuNkulunkulu weqiniso, yena uYahweh, onguNkulunkulu waphakade ka-Israyeli, ngoba uYahweh wazikhethela uJakobe ukuze nibe yisizwe esikhethekile kuye. Ngakho-ke uSathane uhlale efuna ukulingisa izindlela zikaNkulunkulu oyedwa weqiniso, yena u-El Shaddai, onguNkulunkulu uMninimandla onke! Ngakho-ke wenze izinkolo eziningi zokulingisa eziklanyelwe ukulingisa izindlela zikaYahweh, uNkulunkulu waphakade waphakade, futhi zonke lezi zinkolo zokulingisa zigcina ekubhujisweni kwaphakade kulabo ababanjwe kanjalo. Ngoba yimuphi umcuphi obeka ugibe emehlweni enyoni?

Ngakho-ke sebevele banikeza izibusiso kubo bonke, ngisho nasezinkolweni zonke, futhi sebevele befuna ukuhlanganisa zonke izinkolo ngaphansi kwezibhengezo zabo zokuvumelana okuhlangene, ngoba zenziwa kanjalo izindlela zabo zokuphatha ingqondo. Ngakolunye uhlangothi bethula umbono oyisisekelo; ngakolunye uhlangothi bethula umbono ophikisanayo; futhi ngokuphuma ekungqubuzaneni okulawulwayo baletha ukuhlanganiswa kwabo, ngoba bafuna ukuhlanganisa wonke amasiko, izizwe, nezihlobo nezilimi emphakathini wabo kasathane ukuze babhubhise bonke abalandela izindlela zabo zokubhujiswa. Ngakho-ke qondisisani, lokho okugobile akukwazi ukuqondiswa, namazwi abo amahle nezinkulumo zabo ezinhle zenzelwe ukukhanga imizwa yabantu nenkanuko nokuzwelana kwamanga abayokubiza ngobungcwele. Qondisisani ukuthi isintu ngokwaso sonakele ngokuphelele futhi asikwazi ukuziqondisela indlela yaso, futhi akukho okuhle esintwini, kodwa isintu sonakaliswa ukungalaleli kuka-Adamu ikakhulukazi ngamanga namaqhinga enyoka, ekhanda layo engiyolichoboza ngokuqinisekile, ngoba uYahweh umemezele kusukela ekuqaleni ukuphela kwaleyo nyoka eqamba amanga edla uthuli lomhlaba.

Bekulokhu kunjalo, kunjalo, futhi kuyoba njalo, ukuthi akukho okusha ngaphansi kwelanga. Futhi njengoba umhlaba usuka emseni uya ekwahlulelweni, inhlekelele engazelele isizowa, futhi iyokwenzeka ngokungazelele. Ngakho-ke ngiyeluleka bonke abafunda la mazwi ukuba baqonde ukuthi la mazwi awanitholanga ngengozi, kodwa athunyelwe nguYahweh uqobo lwakhe ngendlela yendodana yakhe ezelwe yodwa, yena uYeshuah HaMashiach, akhulunywe ngazwi linye yizinceku zami abaprofethi. Futhi yimi, uJesu waseNazaretha, yena uYeshua HaMashiach, owakhokha inani laphakade lensindiso yenu nensindiso yabo bonke abasazofika ekuphileni okuphakade othandweni lwami olungapheliyo nolwaphakade uYahweh angidalele lona ukuze ngiligcwalise. Ngithi kini okufanayo nalokho engikusho kusukela ekuqaleni: ukuthi mina, uJesu Kristu, yena uYeshua HaMashiach, ngiyindlela, neqiniso, nokuphila, futhi akekho umuntu oza kuYise ngaphandle kwami; futhi alikho elinye igama elinikeziwe emhlabeni, nanini, okumelwe abantu basindiswe ngalo, nawo wonke amanye amagama kungakhathaliseki ukuthi ukulingisa kuqinisekisa kangakanani awaholeli kuNkulunkulu weqiniso, yena uBaba wezibani okungekho kuye ukuguquguquka noma isithunzi sokuphenduka. Ngakho-ke qondisisani, nina bancane bami, ukuthi niyizibani zezwe uYahweh azizele, ngisho enizidale kabusha kimi, uKristu Jesu iNkosi elungileyo yaphakade, futhi ngenziwe umoya onika ukuphila.

Bhekani, nginikhombisa imfumbe eyonikeza ukuhlakanipha kwabahlakaniphileyo futhi eyovimba iziwula nabangafundile ukuba bajoyine labo ababoshelwe ekubhujisweni ezindleleni zabo. Okuthathu, okubili, okukodwa: futhi ezinkolweni eziningi zomhlaba kunezintathu eziphambili eziyoholela kweyodwa yamanga engcwele, futhi lezi ezintathu zibonakala ziyizitha futhi sezilwe izimpi eziningi. Nokho, kulezi ezintathu, ezimbili ziyizimfihlo ziyizimbangi, futhi eyodwa iyosebenzisa enye, futhi zombili ziholela ekubhujisweni. Ngoba kulezi ezintathu, ezimbili ziyophendukela ngonya kolunye, yebo leyo engcwele abayizondayo kodwa abanqotshwe yiyo ngokuphelele. Kanjalo-ke ongcwele uyoletha ukwahlulelwa endlini kaNkulunkulu kuqala, ngoba ukwahlulelwa kuqala endlini kaNkulunkulu futhi usuku lokwahlulela selufikile.

Nakuba ongcwele enqobile, ezimbili kwezintathu ziyophendukela ekubhubhiseni eyodwa, kodwa lokho kubhujiswa akunakwenzeka, futhi eyodwa isivele ithole ukunqoba okuphelele. Kodwa inkohliso iyoba kanjalo ukuthi ezimbili kwezintathu ziyophendukela kolunye futhi zenze izinsolo zamanga emilonyeni yofakazi abaningi bamanga, futhi ziyolwa nabangcwele zibanqobe. Futhi ngenhlamba eningi yamanga bayasabalalisa emhlabeni, kodwa eyodwa, yebo ongcwele kaNkulunkulu, isivele ibanqobile futhi iyolichoboza ikhanda labo ngokuphelele. Kodwa lezi ezimbili ziyobonakala zinqoba phezu kwalena abafuna ukuyibhubhisa, futhi kulezi ezimbili ziyolwa nalena eyodwa, nakuba ukubhujiswa kwazo kokugcina kungalibali. Ngakho-ke ugibe luyobekelwa umhlaba, ukuthi ngobudlova obukhulu nempi bayozihlanganisa ukuze okuthathu, okubili, okukodwa kube okubili, okukodwa, okuthathu ngoba bayozihlanganisa babe inkolo eyodwa yamanga eholwa ngabathathu abangcwele, yebo udrako, isilo sasolwandle, nesilo sasemhlabeni, esingumprofethi wabo wamanga.

Ngakho-ke nginikhombisa impendulo yale mfumbe yami yezinto ezintathu. Lezi zintathu yizinkolo ezintathu eziphambili ezibusa umhlaba wamanje; futhi kulezi zintathu, yinye kuphela eyiqiniso, yebo ongcwele kaNkulunkulu; ngoba mina, uYeshua HaMashiach, ngingongcwele kaNkulunkulu onqobile omubi futhi oyiNkosi yamakhosi noMbusi wababusi. Anginakunqotshwa, futhi nginikwe igama eliphezu kwawo onke amagama, ukuze egameni likaJesu Kristu, yena uYeshua HaMashiach (ngoba ngingumsindisi wabezizwe namaJuda ngokufanayo) wonke amadolo aguqe, kokubili ezulwini nasemhlabeni ngisho nangaphansi komhlaba. Kanjalo izinkolo ezimbili zamanga zivela ziphikisana kodwa zizamile ukuzithandekisa kweyodwa, futhi ngeqiniso uSathane ulingise ibandla lami leqiniso ngesifebe samanga esidumisa futhi sikhonze indlovukazi yezulu. Ngakho-ke bafuna umfelandawonye wamanga futhi bahlanganise amanga amaningi eqinisweni, ngaphandle kokuthi amanga neqiniso akuhlangani: kungaba unamanga noma unalo iqiniso: ayikho enye inketho.

Futhi njengoba ibandla lami leqiniso liyiswa emazulwini futhi umvimbeli uyasuswa, kanjalo ibandla lokulingisa liyobhujiswa, kanjalo nalena eyodwa iyasuswa ngokuphelele. Izinkolo ezimbili eziseleyo ziyovuka ngokunqoba kwamanga, futhi ziyoba nokuthula kwamanga, futhi ziyofuna ubunye bamanga ukuze zihlanganise abalahlekileyo, zize zinikeze ukuphila okuphakade, impilo ephelele, nosuku olusha udrako ayolunikeza. Futhi uyohlanganisa lezi zimbili zibe inkolo okhokho babo ababengayazi kodwa iyobonakala ifana ngoba isintu asinaki umlando waso wangempela. Futhi ngalokhu kuhlanganiswa kokubhujiswa bayoya emlilweni waphakade, ngoba bonke abahlanganyela nabo yilabo abathokoza ngokungalungi, futhi hhayi lokho kuphela, kodwa nalabo abathanda futhi abakholelwa futhi abenza futhi abakhonza amanga ngoba izindlela zabo zingamanga futhi ziphambene futhi izindlela zabo ziholela ekufeni kwesibili.

Ngakho-ke bayodukisa umhlaba, oyomangala ngabo, uthi, ubani onjengesilo? Futhi isilo esisodwa siyonikwa amandla esinye isilo, nesilo sasolwandle siyobusa phezu kwabo ngokokubusa kwaso, nesilo sasemhlabeni siyobusa umhlaba ngokokubusa kwaso, futhi bayobusa umhlaba ngokungalungi. Futhi kulezi ezimbili ezisele, ziyohlangana zibe munye. Ngaphandle kokuthi, izindlela zabo zingukufa, futhi lapho kugibe lubekwe, ngoba yimuphi umcuphi obeka ugibe emehlweni enyoni? Bonke abalandela izindlela zabo zokubumbana kwezinkolo bayohlanganiswa, kuphela efeni labawileyo; futhi bonke abazihlanganisa ngokulunywa yinyoka yabo bayohlanganyela efa lowileyo kulelo chibi elikhulu lesibabule esivuthayo lapho besha phakade futhi impethu yabo ingafi. Ngakho ukubumbana kwabo kwezinkolo kugcwele izingibe ezifihlwe ngobuhlakani, ngoba yimuphi umcuphi obeka ugibe emehlweni enyoni?

Ngakho-ke ngiyeluleka bonke abafunda lawa mazwi nabo bonke abanamehlo okubona nezindlebe zokuzwa nenhliziyo yokuqonda, ukuthi insindiso nokuphila okuphakade kanye noNkulunkulu weqiniso, uYahweh uqobo lwakhe, akutholakali kwelinye igama ngaphandle kwegama lendodana ezelwe yodwa kaNkulunkulu, uJesu Kristu, onguYeshua HaMashiach; futhi ngingumsindisi wesintu sonke, ngisho nesizwe sika-Adamu okungesegazi laso sonke isizwe sabantu esihlanganyela ngokugcwele. Futhi nganoma yiluphi ulimi, futhi kunoma yisiphi isizwe, bonke bangaza ngokukhululeka bamukele isipho sami sensindiso yaphakade ngokubiza egameni lami elingcwele kuze kube sekupheleni. Ngokuba ngiyiNkosi nomsindisi wenu, kuphela ningalokothi nivumele igama lami lisuke ezindebeni zenu, futhi ngizonisindisa ngokupheleleyo.

Ningahlanganyeli ekubumbaneni kwezinkolo kwabo, ngokuba abazi ukuthi bakhonza ini, futhi bakhanukiswe ngamanga azobaholela ekubhujisweni kwabo kwaphakade. Kanjalo-ke insindiso ayitholakali kwelinye igama, futhi alikho elinye igama elinikiwe lapho isintu singasindiswa khona nalapho isintu singasondela kuYahweh, ngokuba ongenayo iNdodana akanaye noYise. Futhi ngakho ngizosekela ngiqinise bonke ababiza egameni lami elingcwele kuze kube sekupheleni. Futhi niyongenzela imisebenzi emikhulu, futhi nginithuma ukuba nithande iNkosi uNkulunkulu wenu ngenhliziyo yenu yonke, nangomphefumulo wenu wonke, nangengqondo yenu yonke, nangamandla enu onke futhi nithande umakhelwane wenu njengalokhu nizithanda nina futhi nisizane nithandane ngezikhathi ezimbi kakhulu esezizogubuzela umhlaba.

Ngakho-ke kuthathu, kubili, kunye, lapho ababili beyovukela oyedwa, futhi ababili abaseleyo bayohlangana babe munye futhi bayobuswa ngabathathu abangcwele, abayobusa labo amagama abo ayengalotshwanga eNcwadini Yokuphila kusukela ekusekelweni komhlaba. Kodwa ningahlanganyeli nabo, bangcwele bami; ngokuba kungcono ukulahlekelwa ukuphila kwenu ukuze nikuthole kunokuthanda ukuphila kwenu kodwa nilahlekelwe ukuphila kwenu kwaphakade ekubhujisweni okuphakade. Bhekani! Ukuhlukaniswa kokugcina sekuzoqala futhi izikhathi sezingamanje. Ningabesabi, bantwanyana bami, kodwa thembelani eNkosini yenu yaphakade nomsindisi. Ngingumsizi waphakade ngezikhathi zosizi, futhi ngisazokusindisa isixuku sezixuku osizini lukaJakobe, futhi ngisazokusindisa insali ka-Israyeli, futhi akekho onganihlwitha ezandleni zami.

Qondani ukuthi ithemba lenu liyithemba laphakade, futhi anginiyekanga ningenathemba, futhi nginishiyele isibonelo saphakade. Ngokuba kulotshiwe ngami kwabaseHebheru: Sibheke kuJesu, umqalisi nomphelelisi wokukholwa kwethu, owathi ngenxa yentokozo eyayibekwe phambi kwakhe, wakhuthazelela isiphambano, edelela ihlazo, futhi uhlezi ngakwesokunene sesihlalo sobukhosi sikaNkulunkulu. Futhi ngakho qondani ukuthi isibonelo sami sokulalela sinanela njengesibonelo sokuqinisa ukholo lwenu ukuze nikhuthazele kuze kube sekupheleni, njengoba nami ngakhuthazelela kuze kube sekupheleni lapho ngisho lawo mazwi okugcina esiphambanweni: Sekuphelile. Kanjalo-ke uNkulunkulu wangivusa kwabafileyo ngokuba kwakungenakwenzeka ukuthi ukufa kungibambe, ngokuba elami igazi elingenacala.

Futhi ngakho ngininike ithemba elikhulu, bantwanyana bami, kangangokuthi nigxilise izingqondo zenu entokozweni ebekwe phambi kwenu ngoba izinhlupheko zosizi lukaJakobe azifaniswa futhi azifanele ukukhulunywa ngazo maqondana nentokozo yaphakade ebekwe phambi kwenu. Bhekani! Kulotshiwe nguJohane uMambuli: Laba yibo abaphuma osizini olukhulu, futhi bahlanza izivatho zabo, bazenza zaba mhlophe egazini leWundlu. Ngalokho-ke baphambi kwesihlalo sobukhosi sikaNkulunkulu, futhi bamkhonza imini nobusuku ethempelini lakhe: futhi lowo ohlezi esihlalweni sobukhosi uyohlala phakathi kwabo. Abasayikulamba, abasayikoma, nelanga alisayikubashisa, nakushisa kunoma kukuphi. Ngokuba iWundlu, eliphakathi nesihlalo sobukhosi, liyobondla, futhi liyobaholela emithonjeni ephilayo yamanzi: futhi uNkulunkulu uyokwesula zonke izinyembezi emehlweni abo. Bayokuba ngabaPristi bakaNkulunkulu, nabakaKristu, futhi bayobusa naye iminyaka eyinkulungwane.

Lelo-ke ithemba lenu, zithandwa zami, ukuze nikhuthazele kuze kube sekupheleni. Futhi elenu liyithemba laphakade ngoba u-El Shaddai, uNkulunkulu uMninimandla onke, uyodala izulu elisha nomhlaba omusha lapho kuhlala khona ukulunga, futhi nizonikwa ukungena kulowo muzi ongcwele olungiswe wahlotshiswa nguYahweh uqobo njengomakoti elungiselelwe umkhwenyana, futhi niyodla esihlahleni sokuphila. Futhi njengoba kulotshiwe: Lowo onqobayo, uyodla ifa lazo zonke izinto, futhi ngiyoba nguNkulunkulu wakhe, yena abe yindodana yami.

Mina, Yeshua HaMashiach, nginguye othumele amazwi ami ngenceku yami, futhi ngenceku yami amazwi ami athunyelwe ukuze alalelwe yibo bonke abafundayo. Futhi ngembule ivangeli laphakade, ngokuba izikhathi zokwembulwa kwalo sezingamanje.

Nginguye ozayo ngalabo abakuKristu futhi ngiyokhulula abathembekileyo ebandleni lami emlilweni womcwebisi naselakeni oluzayo.

Nginguye ohlukanisa ukolweni emakhuleni, futhi akekho noyedwa kini ongahlwithwa esandleni sami.

Ngimeleni, bangcwele bami bosizi, futhi wozani kimi, o Israyeli, ngokuba nginguYeshua HaMashiach, uMesiya wenu weqiniso, futhi ngininike ithemba elikhulu namandla amakhulu. Futhi abaningi benu bayongenzela imisebenzi enamandla, futhi akekho noyedwa kini oyohlala engaklonyelisiwe, ngokuba ngisho nezinto ezincane kakhulu enizenzayo ngezikhathi zosizi olukhulu ziletha umvuzo waphakade, inkazimulo yawo izinhlupheko zenu ezingafanele nokukhulunywa ngazo; ngakho bizani egameni lami elinamandla, bantu bami! Insindiso nomvuzo omkhulu kusesandleni sami kulabo abanqobayo, ngokuba kunjalo uYeshua HaMashiach ethembisa abathembekileyo.

Bhekani, izikhathi sezingamanje!
