Bhekani! Ngiyeza: Incwadi Yothando Yokududuza Abakholwayo KuKristu

Zwanini aMazwi kaYeshua HaMashiach, namukele aMazwi ami okududuza, nizwe namazwi ami enjabulo, ngokuba ukukhululwa kwenu okuphelele sekufikile futhi kuseduze. Ngiqinisile, ngiqinisile, nginguye ozayo ukuzolanda iBandla lami, nomkhwenyana wenu akalibali makoti wami, ngakho-ke yimani niqinile okholweni olukhulu, nivumele aMazwi ami namandla ami amakhulu kube yikuqina kwezikhathi zenu namandla ensindiso, nokumesaba uJehova kube yingcebo yenu.

Kanjalo izikhathi phezu kwenu zithandwa zami, nezikhathi zokuzembula kwami ngokugcwele sezifikile. Futhi ngiyozembula komunye nomunye ngokulandelana. Ngiyozembula kuqala ngokugcwele kwabathembekileyo kuKristu emazulwini, kungazelelwe nangokungalindelekile manje kulokho kumemeza okukhulu kweNgelosi Enkulu, nalelo cilongo elinamandla liyokhala ngoba linikelwe esandleni ukuba likhale ngomyalo wami ngaleso sikhathi esifanele esikhethwe nguYahweh uqobo emaphakadeni adlule! Ngisho nasezinhlelweni zakhe zaphakade zensindiso zohlanga luka-Adamu, nezwi lalo liyogcwala umhlaba, nabathembekileyo kuKristu bayozwa bethi “Yenyukelani lapha!” kungazelelwe nangokungalindelekile manje.

Kodwa abangathembekile nabanenkani bayosala nabawileyo nabangakholwayo ukuba babize igama lami kuze kube sekupheleni kwabo, futhi bonke abakwenzayo bayomukelwa ensindisweni nasenkazimulweni yaphakade, ngisho umvuzo waphakade ofanele nesisindo sawo kungafanele neze kuqhathaniswe nokuhlupheka nezilingo zalo mhlaba, okuyizinto ezincane nezingasho lutho uma kuqhathaniswa. Usho kanje lowo owahlupheka ngesijeziso esiphelele sezono zenu enyameni yami engenasono, ngachitha kabuhlungu obukhulu igazi lami elingenasono kwaze kwaba iconsi lokugcina, futhi angigodlanga lutho ngani zithandwa zami. Ngakho-ke, bhekani kimi, uJesu waseNazaretha, iWundlu likaNkulunkulu onguMqalisi noMphelelisi wokholo lwenu, owathi ngenxa yentokozo eyayibekwe phambi kwami, ngakhuthazelela isiphambano, ngalidelela ihlazo, futhi ngihlezi ngakwesokunene sesihlalo sobukhosi sikaNkulunkulu. Selah!

Kanjalo nina, enikholwayo kuKristu, niyothathwa nonke niye ezulwini lamazulu lapho ngiyonethula khona kuYahweh uqobo! Futhi niyomangala ngobukhazikhazi eniyobe nembethe bona, niyokwazi njengoba naziwa, niyobona njengoba niboniwe, niyozwa njengoba nizwiwe nguNkulunkulu waphakade wokulunga, nguYahweh uqobo okhumbula wonke aMazwi kuwo wonke umthandazo enake nawusho, noma ngomlomo ngezitho zenu zokukhuluma, noma buthule ezinhliziyweni zenu, ngoba uYahweh ungumphenyi wezinhliziyo, futhi yini ekhona engafihlwa emehlweni akhe? Ngiqinisile, ngiqinisile, yini ekhona engafihlwa emehlweni ami? Ngakho-ke, qondani ukuthi uBaba wami nami simunye ebunye nasenhlosweni futhi konke uBaba anakho, ukunikele ezandleni zami ukuze kube yinsindiso ka-Adamu, futhi ngempela kube yinsindiso nokungcweliswa komhlaba uqobo, ngoba umhlaba ungowami futhi ekulungeni ngiyeza ukuzokwahlulela nokulwa nabawileyo abasezikhathini zabo sezifikile, nokwahlulelwa kwabo okuphelele akulibali futhi ngeke baphunyuke.

Ngakho-ke, nginika nina ukuqonda zithandwa zami ukuba ningimele ngokuqinile niqhubeke nokugcina izibani zenu zigcwele amafutha omoya wami engininike wona, nihambe ngawo nangomoya wami wokulunga njengoba nihamba ngokholo ngoba olungileyo uyophila ngokholo, Selah! Futhi ukuze ningalingeki ukuhamba kulesi sikhathi esesihambile ngamandla enu ukuze nigcwalise izibani zenu ngamafutha angahlanzekile ngokusekelwe emisebenzini yenu, kodwa gcwalisani izibani zenu ngamafutha ahlanzekile omoya wami ukuze izindlela zenu zikhanyiswe, ngoba ngiqinisile, ngiqinisile, iZwi lami liyisibani ezinyaweni zenu. Futhi nginigcwalise nonke ngokugcwele ngomoya wami wobuNdodana futhi ngithulule umoya wami kulezi zinsuku zokugcina njengoba kwathenjiswa nguJoweli emibhalweni yasendulo eyanikezwa oBaba bami, abangoBaba benu ngefa lesithembiso ngoba nina ningabantwana besithembiso, ngisho nabantwana baka-Abrahama othembekile, Selah!

Ngivumeleni nginiyale ukuthi nizilungisa kanjani izintambo zezibani zenu ngoba nina niyizibani zomhlaba ezingenakufihlwa! Kungendlela yamazwi ami nomoya wami enithola ngayo imiyalo ekulungeni, nangomoya wami wobundodana niyalahlwa icala ngokulunga, ukuze ngiqinisile, ngiqinisile, niphile ngokulunga ezintweni zonke. Ngoba kungomoya wami wobundodana ukulunga kwami kweqiniso kunikezwa ngaphakathi kini, lapho ngiqinisile, ngiqinisile, nonke ninokuzikhethela ukuthi nizolalela bani, noma nilalele isono kuze kube sekufeni, noma ukulalela kuze kube sekulungeni kuze kube sekuphileni okuphakade nasemvuzweni ogcwele. Ngakho-ke, lalelani imiyalelo yami eqondile yezimpilo zenu, nibe mnene ezwini lami nasemazwini alabo engibathumayo nabakhuluma egameni lami, nangezithelo zabo niyobazi, nangaMazwi abo engibanika wona ensindisweni nenhlalakahle yenu. Futhi ngiyaniyala ukuba nisebenzele insindiso yenu ngokwesaba nangokuthuthumela, futhi ngiyaniyala ukuba ningagodli okuhle lapho kusemandleni ezandla zenu ukukwenza.

Ngakho-ke, qondani ukuthi ngininikile amandla futhi ngenza abaningi benu bakwazi ukufeza izinhloso zami zensindiso nokuthi kufanele nisekelane othandweni. Ngakho-ke, ningabunqongeleli ubuhle bezinto ezibonakalayo nezezimali engininike bona, ngisho nokunqongela abanye ngezindlela zikaSathane ofuna ukubeka izisindo nezilinganiso ebuhleni bukaYahweh, obungenakulinganiswa ngempela ngoba asikho isikali ezulwini noma emhlabeni esanele ukukala inala kaYahweh ayilungiselele ebusweni bukaJesu Kristu kulabo abamthandayo.

Ngakho-ke, phumani phakathi kwabo ngokuphelele, ningahlanganyeli ezonweni zabo, ningazinqongeleli inala kaYahweh yemali nezinto ezibonakalayo enikezwe ebusweni bukaJesu Kristu sengathi ngamandla esandla senu niwuzuzile lo ncebo ukuze ifihlwe inqongelelwe izinkanuko zenu. Ukuze nithole induduzo ngokuthi niziprovidele ngokuzidla nangokona, ukuze niklolodele labo abangenalutho, ngisho nokujabulela izinombolo kuma-akhawunti enu ngehlazo elikhulu.

Ngakho-ke, qondani futhi nixwayiswe ukuthi bonke abenza izinto ezinjalo kusukela komkhulu kuya komncane, bayolahlekelwa kakhulu umvuzo wabo futhi bayozithathela bona isiqu sokuba ngabaphansi kunabo bonke embusweni wezulu! Futhi lokho kungenxa yokuthi ngenxa yobugovu benu imisebenzi yenu iyosha ibe ngumlotha esihlalweni sami sokwahlulela lapho nonke niyoma khona nilandise. Ngakho-ke, yabelanani ngazo zonke izinto ezidingekayo kwabangcwele ngokuthandana, ningagodli lutho oluhle, ngoba ngiqinisile, ngiqinisile, inala yenu yezinto ezibonakalayo neyemali inikezwe ngesandla sami ngenxa yezinhloso zami eziphuma ezinqolobaneni zikaYahweh, ngakho-ke, ningabunqongeleli ubuhle beNkosi kodwa yenzani lezo zinto engiyoqondisa kuzo izinyathelo zenu ekukhonzeni nasekuthandeni abangcwele.

Phumani ezonweni zenu bantu bami, hleze nihlanganyele ekwahlulelweni kwazo, ngoba lezi yizindlela zeBhabhiloni Eliyimfihlo nezikhathi zokwahlulelwa kwalo sezifikile, futhi unamandla u-Adonai Yahweh olihlulela ngesabelo esiphindwe kabili sokwahlulela, ngakho-ke sukani ezindleleni nasezonweni zalo ezingamukelekile futhi ezimbi kakhulu emehlweni ami! Selah!

Yebo, ngeqiniso, ngeluleka wonke umuntu ukuba aqhubeke nokuphuma ezonweni zakhe angene ngokuphelele ekulungeni kwami, futhi angivumele ngibe ukwanela kwakhe ezintweni zonke, ngoba ngiqinisile, ngiqinisile, ngikhona.

Nginguye ozayo emafwini ukuzolanda abathembekileyo kuKristu kungazelelwe nangokungalindelekile manje, ngoba umhlaba, ekungakholweni kwawo, awulindele ukuba uKristu Jesu abonakale emafwini njengoba kuthenjisiwe emibhalweni. Nokho, lesi sikhathi ngeke sinifice zithandwa zami njengesela ebusuku, uma nihlala ninokuthobeka emiyalweni yami nilandela imininingwane yemithetho yami ngezinhliziyo zenu zonke, nazi ukuthi ngokukhuthala nokwethembeka kwenu kuza imivuzo ephelele yefa labathembekileyo kuKristu, okuthi ngempela, kungakaze kungene ngokugcwele ezinhliziyweni zabantu ngalesi sikhathi. Ngakho-ke, ningavumeli uSathane athenge imivuzo yenu yefa ngezindlela zika-Esawu, owayengumuntu ongahloniphi noziphethe kabi owathengisa ubuzibulo bakhe ngesitsha sesobho esalahlwa ngesikhathi sesomiso. Selah!

Ngakho-ke, qondani ukusondela ngesikhathi kokubonakala kwami nokuthi kuseduze kangakanani ukukhululwa kwenu okuphelele, zithandwa zami. Ngakho-ke, ngininika ukuqonda ukuthi ibutho lasezulwini selithathe izindawo zalo ngokuhleleka nokwakheka kwalo, futhi bazethule ukuba bakhonze, futhi balindile. Bathathe izindawo zabo, futhi balindile! Ngakho-ke, qondani ukuthi ngiphethe lelo cilongo elinamandla esandleni, ukuthi ngomyalo wami abafileyo kuKristu bayovuswa bengenakonakala, futhi nina eniphilayo nisasele niyothathwa kungazelelwe nihlangane nami emafwini, kanjalo nonke niyokwenziwa njengeNkosi yenu kuze kube phakade, futhi labo abanqobe ngokugcwele bayoba nami njalo ngoba ngiyonithathela mina ngesivumelwano njengabami siqu, futhi niyokwenza imisebenzi eminingi enamandla, ngoba umakoti wami uyibutho elinamandla labaPhrofethi, amaKhosi, nabaPristi abayonikwa amandla amakhulu njengomakoti wami oyiqhawe, futhi ngubani okhona oyokwazi ukumelana nomakoti wami wabantu abanganakufa abanikwe amandla abakhululwe emhlabeni ngamandla amakhulu nokulunga, Selah!

Ngakho-ke, qondani ukuthi isikhathi esiqondile esakhethwa nguYahweh ekwazini kwakhe kwangaphambili besilokhu siwusuku oluqondile, isikhathi nehora, ngaphandle kokuthi sasifihliwe enhliziyweni kaYahweh kuphela kwaze kwafika izikhathi ezifanele zokwembulwa kwaso emazulwini kuphela, ngoba lesi sikhathi asivezwa emhlabeni, nakuba uSathane elungele ukudla bonke abasele emhlabeni, ngakho-ke, bonke ababiza igama likaJesu kuze kube sekupheleni kwabo bayosindiswa. Qondani ukuthi lesi sikhathi esiqondile asizange sinikezwe noSathane kodwa kufanele alunge aze akhululwe ukuba abuse isikhashana emva kwalokho uyoboshwa ngeketanga elikhulu kwalasha ekuhlushweni nasezinhlungwini lapho eyokuqonda khona ngokugcwele ifa lakhe elizayo.

Kanjalo ngiyeza ukuzoninika ukuqonda ngezikhathi eniphila kuzo futhi ngikwenza lokho ngokuninika futhi wona lawo mazwi engawanika uPetru ukuba akhulume nezixuku ngalolo suku olukhulu lwePentekoste eminyakeni eminingi yamaJubili edlule manje, lapho nokho, amazwi akhe esezwakala njalo eqinisile ngokugcwaliseka kwaphakade. Ngakho ngithi kini lokhu yilokho okwakhulunywa ngumprofethi uJoweli, Futhi kuyothi ezinsukwini zokugcina, usho uNkulunkulu, ngiyothulula umoya wami phezu kwayo yonke inyama, namadodana enu, namadodakazi enu ayoprofetha, nezinsizwa zenu ziyobona imibono, namadoda enu amadala ayophupha amaphupho. Futhi phezu kwezinceku zami, naphezu kwezincekukazi zami ngiyothulula uMoya wami ngalezo zinsuku, futhi ziyoprofetha. Futhi ngiyobonisa izimangaliso ezulwini phezulu, nezibonakaliso emhlabeni phansi, igazi, nomlilo, nomusi wentuthu. Ilanga liyophenduka ubumnyama, nenyanga ibe yigazi, ngaphambi kokuba lufike usuku olukhulu noludumileyo lweNkosi. Futhi kuyothi, noma ngubani obiza iGama leNkosi, uyosindiswa.

Ngakho-ke, ngiyeza ukuzomemezela la mazwi egcwalisekile ezindlebeni zenu namuhla, nokuthi kungazelelwe nangokungalindelekile manje abathembekileyo kuKristu bayothathwa, bakhululwe, bashintshwe, bakhazinyuliswe futhi bavuzwe kuze kube phakade. Ngakho qondani ukugcwaliseka okungazelelwe okuzofika ngokushesha okungaka. Ningalaleli ababeka izinsuku nabaJudaizer abangaqondi lokho abakufundisayo, noma iqiniso langempela lemibhalo abalihlanekezela kube yikulimala kwabo.

Ngakho-ke, nginazisa ukuba niqonde ukuthi ukubuthwa kwabathembekileyo kuKristu akuboshelwe ngokuqondile kunoma yiluphi usuku olungcwele, noma izinsuku zemikhosi, noma olunye usuku noma umcimbi owanikezwa u-Israyeli wasendulo emthethweni kaMose, lowo mthetho ogcwaliswe ngokuphelele kuKristu Jesu iNkosi yenu yaphakade yokulunga. Njengoba ngasho kini ezikhathini ezidlule ngiyaphinda ngisho kini zithandwa zami: Ngakho-ke makungabikho muntu onilahla ngecala ngokudla nokuphuza, noma mayelana nosuku olungcwele, noma ukwethwasa kwenyanga, noma izinsuku zesabatha, okuyizithunzi nje zezinto ezizayo: kodwa umzimba ukuKristu.

Nokho, qondani ukuthi lesi senzakalo singenzeka nganoma yimuphi umzuzwana nanoma yiluphi usuku futhi kungenzeka kakhulu ukuthi senzeke ngosuku olubalulekile, isikhathi, noma ihora noma ngisho nangezinsuku ezingcwele, noma izinsuku zemikhosi, noma izinsuku zeSabatha, eziyisithunzi sezinto ezizayo! Ngakho-ke, lindani nilinde, futhi nilindele ngokulangazela ukubonakala kwami ngoba izikhathi zenu zokukhululwa okuphelele sezifikile. Futhi ngakho nginazisa ukuba niqonde futhi ngenxa yokulalela kwenu, ukuthi nilinde nilinde nsuku zonke (hhayi nje izikhathi zenu zokulinda eziphakeme enizibekele zona) nokuthi ninake kakhulu la mazwi ngokulindela okugcwele ukugcwaliseka, ngoba akusekho ukulibala. Selah!

NginguKristu Jesu futhi yimina engithumele la mazwi ngesithunywa sami nenceku yami, nangayo leyo nceku yami la mazwi athunyelwe. Njengesikhuthazo ukuze iBandla lami, umzimba wami, ngisho noMakoti wami baduduzwe ngenduduzo enkulu nokuthi niqonde ngokugcwele ukusondela kwesikhathi salesi senzakalo, ukuze ningaficwa ngokumangala yilesi senzakalo esiqinisekile ukuthi sizokwenzeka kungazelelwe manje.

NginguYeshua HaMashiach manje ozohamba ayothumela ofakazi bami ababili nezinti zami zezibani ezimbili, njengoba kumenyezelwe kwiVangeli Laphakade, ngoba uYahweh akakaze azishiye engenabufakazi, futhi ofakazi bami ababili bayofakaza ngazo zonke izikhathi ngokombono wasemhlabeni nowasezulwini, futhi u-Enoke uMbhali uletha imibhalo yasendulo esikhwameni sakhe futhi ubufakazi babo buyonezelwa kwiVangeli Laphakade eliyohlala kuze kube phakade kanye nawo wonke amazwi kaYahweh ayobuyiselwa emhlabeni ngokuphelela okuqondile njengoba kulotshiwe emazulwini, ukuze imisebenzi kasathane ichithwe ngokuphelele nokuthi izindlela namazwi kaYahweh agcwale umhlaba ngokulunga kuze kube phakade naphakade! Selah.
