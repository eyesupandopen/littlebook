Ufakazi Omabili: Kwa-Israyeli
Okubabayo Nokumnandi
Novemba 13, 2024

O Israyeli! Yiliphi iWundlu enizolikhetha?

Zwanini amaZwi kaYahweh, uMINA ENGINGUYE Omkhulu, nigijimele ekuzwakaleni kwamaZwi ami ukuze namukele ukuhlakanipha, nalowo ohlakaniphile phakathi kwenu ande ngokuhlakanipha.

Bhekani! Ngiza ukunitshela ngezinto ezizayo ukuze nazi isiqiniseko samaZwi ami, nokuba nifinyelele ekusindisweni kwenu okupheleleyo kuYeshua HaMashiach, onguye ngempela uMesiya wenu weqiniso, iNkosi, uSomandla, noMpristi oMkhulu kuze kube phakade ngokohlelo lukaMelkhizedeki.

Ngalokho, ngiyaniyala ukuba nimlalele ngenhliziyo yenu yonke nokuba nisoke izinhliziyo zenu ekulaleleni! Ngoba, nguYeshua HaMashiach, yena kanye uJesu Kristu ngolimi lwesiNgisi, owanikelwa kungekona nje ukusindiswa kuka-Israyeli kuphela, kodwa ngisho nokusindiswa kwaso sonke isizwe sika-Adamu, ngoba bonke abakhuleka egameni leNkosi kuze kube sekupheleni bayakusindiswa, noma bengama-Israyeli noma bengabezizwe, futhi uYeshua HaMashiach uyakusindisa insali ka-Israyeli nasohlangeni luka-Adamu eyakungena embusweni weminyaka eyinkulungwane.

Kanjalo-ke, mina ngalungiselela lokho okwakungenakwenzeka ukuba isizukulwane sika-Adamu sizilungiselele sona ekusindisweni kwaso, ngoba isizukulwane sika-Adamu sasingathola ukuhlengwa okuphelele kuphela ngegazi lewundlu. Ngoba yimina, uYahweh, uMINA ENGINGUYE Omkhulu, owanilungiselela iPhasika lenu elipheleleyo kanye, manje, naphakade; nomhlatshelo wakhe wamukelekile kuze kube phakade, ngalokho, akusekho sidingo somhlatshelo, O Israyeli.

Njengokulotshiwe kumaHeberu, ngisho futhi manje kini: Ngokuba uma igazi lezinkunzi, nelezimbuzi, nomlotha wesithokazi esisafufuzwa kwabangcolileyo, lingcwelisa kuze kube sekuhlanjululweni kwenyama, kakhulu kangakanani igazi likaKristu, owazinikela kuNkulunkulu engenabala ngoMoya waphakade, liyakuhlanza unembeza wenu emisebenzini efileyo, ukuze nikhonze uNkulunkulu ophilayo? Kanjalo ngiyakweluleka, O Israyeli, wamukele umhlatshelo wami ngoba akusekho mhlatshelo osele ngenxa yesono!

Ngakho-ke qondani Israyeli, ukuthi akwenzeki muntu ukuba asondele kuNkulunkulu Waphakade Wokulunga esebenzisa uMose, ngoba ngenzile uYeshua HaMashiach waba yindlela, neqiniso, nokuphila, okungekho muntu oza kuYahweh ngaphandle kokuba eze ngoYeshua HaMashiach!

Futhi ngiyalile ukulalela kwenu izwi lakhe, ngisho nangeThora lapho izwi likaMose likhala khona nanamuhla njengofakazi omelene nani nasezindleleni zenu zokungafundi nokungakholwa, esikhundleni sokulalela nifuna ngokungazi ukumisa ithempeli elenziwe ngumuntu, njengoba nidukiswa ngu-Esawu! Ngoba nilahlile ithempeli elakhiwe ngumdali wenu, ithempeli elenziwe ngaphandle kwezandla, lona kanye ithempeli elenziwe nguYahweh ngokwakhe ku-Israyeli kaNkulunkulu, lapho uJesu Kristu eyitshe legumbi eliyinhloko. Ngalokho, yekani imisebenzi yenu nophumule emisebenzini kaMesiya wenu weqiniso, ngizoninika iSabatha.

Ngakho ngiyaniyala O Israyeli, futhi yimina owanikeza wona kanye amaZwi akhulunywa nguMose koYihlo eSinayi, maZwi lawo engiyala ukulalelwa kwawo njalo naphakade, ngisho nasekungalalelini kwenu kwenkani nobulukhuni benhliziyo. Futhi uMose wathi ukuze bonke balalele: INkosi uNkulunkulu wenu iyakunivusela umPhrofethi onjengami, phakathi kwenu, kubafowenu: yena niyakumzwa!

Kanti uMose wayengumlamuleli weSivumelwano sokuqala (esiqoshwe ngomunwe wami eSinayi) esanikezwa ukulahla isono enyameni, Sivumelwano leso esikhanya manje ebusweni bukaJesu Kristu, yena uYeshua HaMashiach onguNkosi noMesiya wenu weqiniso! Nokho uMthetho kaMose uyisithunzi sezinto ezizayo, zona zigcwalisekile kuKristu Jesu, futhi ngimenze waba umoya ophilayo, futhi ngokwenyama uJesu waseNazaretha akasaziwa!

Njengoba kwenziwa ngesandla sami esinamandla, wona kanye umunwe kaYHVH uMINA ENGINGUYE omkhulu, ngaqopha izimiso zami ezibhebheni zamatshe eSinayi lapho nganikeza khona uMose izimiso zami ukuze u-Israyeli aphile phambi kwami. Nokho, lokho okwanikezwa u-Israyeli eSinayi kwakuyukulahlwa kwesono enyameni ukuze u-Israyeli abe nomfundisi ozobaholela kuMesiya wabo weqiniso, yena uYeshua HaMashiach engimthumele ukusindiswa kwenu okupheleleyo.

Kanjalo-ke, umthetho kaMose muhle, ulungile, uphelele, futhi ungcwele, futhi uma kukhona umuntu othuka umthetho kaMose makahlulelwe ngawo ngobukhulu obumiselwe ngaphansi kwesiqalekiso nesijeziso somthetho kaMose. Kodwa lokho okuhle, nokulungile, nokuphelele, nokungcwele kwaletha ukufa kubo bonke abalahlwe kuwo ngenxa yesono enyameni, ngoba lapho kungekho mthetho isono asibalwa, ngakho-ke umthetho kaMose unikwe ukwembula nokulahla isono enyameni.

Kodwa kungomoya womthetho hhayi ngohlamvu lwawo lapho isintu sithola khona umusa othandweni lwami ngaso, ngoba uhlamvu lomthetho luletha ubunzima (Anifundanga yini ukuthi ngingathanda umusa kunomhlatshelo?). Ngalokho, othandweni lwami ngabantwana bakwa-Israyeli engihlose ukubasindisa uma nilalela uYeshua HaMashiach, ngamisa umhlatshelo ngoba ngaphandle kokuchitheka kwegazi akukho ukuthethelelwa kwezono. Ngalokho, ngesandla esiqaphile sikaMose, abantu bami baye bagcinwa kwaze kwafika uKristu, nakuba abaningi benu baphendukele konkulunkulu bamanga futhi nithanda ama-cube enu amnyama, izinto ezinengeka kakhulu emehlweni ami, O Israyeli, ngakho-ke sukani ngokushesha!

Ngakho qondani intukuthelo yami ngani O Israyeli, ngoba nginguYHVH, uMINA ENGINGUYE omkhulu, yena onguNkulunkulu uSomandla Waphakade Wokulunga ongakhathali, futhi akukho lutho olukhulu kakhulu ukuba isandla sami esinamandla singakwenza. Futhi yimina, u-El Shaddai, uNkulunkulu uSomandla, owadala indalo ngokuhlakanipha kwami kwaphakade, futhi ngamandla amakhulu omoya wami zonke izinto zibambene ngokulalela izwi lami.

Ngingu-Elohim wenu, lowo ozungezwe yizinkulungwane eziyishumi zezinkulungwane eziyishumi ezizungeze isihlalo sami sobukhosi, yebo, ibutho elikhulu lezingelosi zezinceku ezikhonzayo, lezo zinceku eziyilangabi lomlilo elidlula zonke ngokuhlakanipha futhi zidalwe ngokuphelele nangobuhle obukhulu njengoba ngizidalile othandweni lwami ngazo! Futhi othandweni lwazo ngami, zilalela ngokuphelele intando yami yaphakade, futhi nginikeze uYeshua HaMashiach ukubusa phezu kwazo zonke.

Nokho, mina YHVH uMINA ENGINGUYE Omkhulu, yena ozungezwe yibutho lezingelosi elingenakubalwa, ANGIDINGI MELULEKI ekuhlakanipheni kwami kwaphakade, futhi phezu kwendalo yami nginobukhosi obuphelele futhi ngenza ZONKE izinto ngokweluleka kwentando yami, futhi phakathi kwezihlakaniphi ezinkulu zaphakade akekho ohlakaniphe ukudlula uMINA ENGINGUYE omkhulu! Ngakho yizwani izwi lami nithulise izilimi zenu uma nikhetha ukuphila okuphakade kunamalangabi angenakucimeka esijeziso O Israyeli, ngoba lokho kuyisinqumo okufanele senziwe ngumuntu ngamunye.

Futhi ngokuhlakanipha kwami kwaphakade ngathumela uJesu waseNazaretha kwa-Israyeli, ngisho nalapho iJudiya ibuswa nguPontiyu Pilatu, ngisho nalapho abapristi abakhulu neSanihedrini bemangalela ngokungafanele, futhi ngezandla ezimbi iWundlu likaNkulunkulu labulawa futhi labethelwa esiphambanweni, ukuze umzimba wesono obopha u-Adamu ubhujiswe kubo bonke abaphila ngokulalela ngokukholwa, ngoba olungileyo uyakuphila ngokukholwa! Futhi nginguye, u-El Shaddai (uNkulunkulu uSomandla) obeke zonke izinto ezulwini nasemhlabeni ezandleni ezinamandla zikaKristu Jesu, yena uYeshua HaMashiach, futhi uyibhubesi lesizwe sakwaJuda engilithumayo ukwahlulela nokulwa, nokusindiswa kwabathembekile nabalalelayo. Ngalokho, yizwani amaZwi ami ngokulalela uMesiya wenu weqiniso, ngoba akekho omunye!

Ngakho-ke qondani intukuthelo yami ngani O Israyeli, ukuthi ebumpumputheni benu obuyingxenye enabuzuzayo koYihlo abanenhliziyo elukhuni, labo izwi labo leTalmud enilihlonipha kakhulu kuno-Elohim wenu. Yebo, nibambelele emasikweni ezihlakaniphi zenu ezibizwa kanjalo nangaphezu kwamaZwi kaMINA ENGINGUYE Omkhulu, ongumdali waphakade nowaphakade wakho konke okudaliweyo! Ngoba ngoYHVH zonke izinto zadalwa, ezisezulwini, nase mhlabeni, izinto ezibonakalayo nezingabonakaliyo: noma kuyizihlalo zobukhosi, noma imibuso, noma amakhosi, noma amandla, zonke izinto zadalwa yimina, futhi zadalelwa mina, futhi ngingaphambi kwazo zonke izinto, futhi kuYHVH zonke izinto zimi.

Nokho, nicabanga ukuthi ningabeluleki bami futhi nizibiza ngokungenangqondo ngokuthi ningabantu bami abakhethiweyo kanti nenqaba amaZwi ami ngokungalaleli okungenakukholwa? Oh Israyeli usuhlukile waya ezithombeni eziyizimungulu zenhliziyo nasemazwini amanga ka-Esawu, oneseluleko sakhe enisithanda ngaphezu kukaNkulunkulu Waphakade Wokulunga, u-Elohim wenu! Futhi niyakhubeka ebumpumputheni obuyingxenye ngoba kuMose izinhliziyo zenu zivaliwe. Nokho, lapho inhliziyo yenu isiphendukela kuMesiya wenu weqiniso, isisitho siyakususwa, futhi esikhundleni sobumpumputhe obuyingxenye niyakubona!

Ungubani wena, O Israyeli, ukuba uzibeke njengomeluleki kaYahweh? Nokho, esikhundleni samaZwi kaYahweh anikezwe ebusweni bukaYeshua HaMashiach, nikholwe ngamanga adluliselwe emasikweni abadala benu ababeyizigqila zika-Esawu, okuyimibhalo yakhe enizibophe ngayo ku-Esawu ngendlela efanayo, naku-Esawu niboshiwe! Futhi ngabhala ngokusobala ukuba uMose anikeze oYihlo eSinayi: Noma yini enginiyala ngayo, qaphelani ukuyenza: ningezeleli kuyo, futhi ninganciphisi kuyo. Futhi ngaphinda ngakubhalisa ekuhlakanipheni kukaSolomoni, okuyikho ukuhlakanipha okumi phakade: Izwi lonke lika-Elohim lihlanzekile: uyisihlangu kulabo abamethemba. Ungafaki lutho emazwini akhe, funa akusole, utholakale unamanga.

Oh Israyeli, ukube bewungakhothamisa izinhliziyo zakho ekulaleleni amaZwi anikezwe insindiso yakho nenjabulo yaphakade, ukube ubungaphinda ulalele amaZwi oMdali wakho! Futhi nginguye obengakubuyisela futhi akulethe phakathi kwesibaya saphakade esingaphansi kwesandla esinamandla soMelusi wami oMkhulu engimnike zonke izinto! Yena uYeshua HaMashiach, owelusa u-Israyeli kaNkulunkulu weqiniso ngokuphelele othandweni lwakhe lokulalela izwi lami, isibonelo enisidelelayo. Ngoba uYeshua HaMashiach nguyena kuphela umuntu owake walalela ngokuphelele intando kaYHVH, uMINA ENGINGUYE omkhulu engithokozayo ngaye njalo, ngakho-ke ngimenze waba umoya ophilayo, ngakho-ke yizwani lowo engimthumele ngenxa yenu ngoba akekho omunye.

Futhi nizenzile lukhuni izinhliziyo zenu njengeTshe Lesisekelo elithunjwe yiSinagoge likaSathane ngoba nilahlile amazwi eseluleko sami anikezwe ebusweni bukaKristu Jesu, enimlahla ngokungazi, ngaleyo ndlela nilahla ithemba lenu elilodwa lokubuyiselwa nensindiso kuze kube phakade. Futhi ngakho nima niyizimpumputhe, nihamba-ze, futhi ninamahloni esonweni senu, nicabanga ngokungazi ukuthi senibe ngabeluleki bakaYHVH, uMINA ENGINGUYE Omkhulu!

Ngakho ngininika ukuqonda ebumpumputheni benu obuyingxenye ukuthi nikhubekile etsheni lami legumbi eliyinhloko abakhi benu abalilahlile, lona idwala lami lesikhubekiso. Ngoba uYeshua HaMashiach uyitshe legumbi eliyinhloko, isisekelo sangempela, yena uJesu waseNazaretha enimlahlayo ngokungalaleli ngenkani nakuba nganikhethela mina njengesizwe esikhethekile, futhi inhliziyo yami iyadabuka.

Zwana, zwana, O Israyeli uqonde ukuthi nginguye oqopha umthetho wami waphakade, isibonakaliso sentando yami yaphakade ohlelweni lwami lwaphakade lokuhlengwa engalwenza ngokweluleka kwentando yami yaphakade nephelele ezibhebheni zamatshe ukuze u-Israyeli alalele eSinayi! Ngakho-ke, nginguye oyobhala umthetho wami ezibhebheni zenyama zezinhliziyo zenu ngoba othandweni lwami ngani, ingxenyana encane yamandla ami iyakwazi ukuqopha umthetho wami ezibhebheni zenu zamatshe, futhi ngomusa wami nomhawu obonakaliswe ebusweni bukaYeshua HaMashiach, ngisazophahlaza izibhebhe zenu zamatshe zibe yibumba lombumbi ukuze nidalwe kabusha ngokulunga nobungcwele beqiniso, njengoba ngafunga ezivumelwaneni zami koYihlo ukuthi ngiyokwenza.

Ngalokho, O Israyeli, ngiyaniyala ukuba nilalele izwi likaYeshua HaMashiach ngoba akukho nsindiso kwelinye igama elinikeziwe ngaphandle kukaJesu Kristu oLungileyo, enimubiza ngokungazi njengesithixo sezizwe ngoba niyizigqila zika-Esawu!

Nokho kulotshiwe kanjalo futhi sekwenzekile kanjalo: Kusukela ku-Abrahama kuya ku-Isaka, lapho uJakobe no-Esawu bezalwa nguye, isandla sikaJakobe sabamba kuqala isithende sika-Esawu. Ngokuba u-Esawu ungukuphela komhlaba, noJakobe ungukuqala kwalowo olandelayo. Isandla somuntu siphakathi kwesithende nesandla. Kodwa kunjalo ukuthi manje ngizokwehlukanisa u-Esawu noJakobe ngoba u-Esawu uyiSinagoge likaSathane eliwukhula olubi olugcwele izwe lesithembiso futhi, futhi u-Israyeli usephinde wagcwala amaKhanani.

Futhi njengoba kulotshiwe, kuyokwenziwa kanjalo, kusho uMINA ENGINGUYE Omkhulu: Ngokuba bheka, usuku luyeza, luyakusha njengeziko, nabobonke abazidlayo, yebo, nabobonke abenza okubi bayakuba yizinhlanga; nosuku oluzayo luyakubashisa, isho iNkosi Sebawoti, ukuthi aluyikubashiyela mpande nagatsha. Kepha kini enesaba igama lami, iLanga lokulunga liyakuphuma, linokuphulukisa emaphikweni alo, niyakuphuma, nikhule njengamathole esibayeni.

Futhi njengoba uYeshua HaMashiach eqoqa abathembekile ebandleni, umzimba wakhe, nomakoti wakhe, manje ngizohambisa uYeshua HaMashiach ukuba ahlukanise ngokokugcina phakathi kuka-Esawu noJakobe, futhi uJakobe uyakusindiswa ngisho nasezilingweni zeviki lamashumi ayisikhombisa likaDaniyeli. Ngalokho, makungabikho mhlobongi, noma umuntu ongcolileyo njengo-Esawu, owathengisa ubuzibulo bakhe ngesidlo esisodwa. Ngokuba niyazi ukuthi kamuva futhi lapho ezakudla ifa lesibusiso, waliwa; ngokuba akatholanga ndawo yokuphenduka, nakuba wasifuna leso sibusiso ngezinyembezi.

Kanjalo isintu siphakathi kwesandla sikaJakobe nesithende sika-Esawu, ngaphandle kokuthi u-Esawu ufuna ukunika uJakobe inxeba ekhanda lokugcina, engingeke ngikuvumele. Kanjalo-ke, bonke abakhileyo emhlabeni abashiyelwe osizini lukaJakobe ukuze babekezelele umlilo wokucwenga bayakuba ngabakaJakobe ngoJesu waseNazaretha, abangabantwana befa, noma bayakwaliwa phakathi kuka-Esawu owathengisa ubuzibulo bakhe ngesiqephu sesinkwa. Ngalokho, umuntu wemvelo uyakubiza igama likaJesu waseNazaretha kuze kube sekupheleni kwakhe ukuze avuswe abe ngowakwa-Israyeli kaNkulunkulu, noma uyakuthengisa ifa lakhe elingaba khona nelaphakade ngesiqephu sesinkwa esinikezwa ngesandla sikaBaraba, ngoba uBaraba uyakuhola iSinagoge likaSathane futhi uyimvu kadrako.

Nokho, ugibe luyakubekwa ezweni ukuze lubambe uJakobe unomphela, futhi uBaraba uletha inkohliso enkulu ngokubusa kwakhe, nobubi obuningi uyakwenza ezweni naselwandle. Nokho, kuyakuba njalo ukuthi njengoba imililo yempi ivutha futhi isha, uBaraba uyakuvuka, aze athi uvusiwe ukuze agcwalise izinhloso zikaNkulunkulu, futhi uyakusho izinto eziningi ezihlambalazayo nezimbi. Futhi uyakuletha igatsha lakhe lomnqumo futhi uyakuzibiza ngoNowa, ophilayo ukusindisa umhlaba ekwahlulelweni okuhloselwe abantu ababi.

Ngoba isilo esivela emhlabeni esingumprofethi wabo wamanga siyakulethela isixazululo saso ngoba siwundlu olunezimpondo ezimbili olukhuluma njengodrako. Futhi ukubusa kwakhe kuyoba nezinhlangothi ezimbili njengoba eyakufuna ukuhlanganisa sonke isintu ngaphansi kokubusa kukadrako, ongusathane uqobo lwakhe. Ngokuba uBaraba akezi ukuzobusa yedwa nakuba ezothi ungunkulunkulu, kodwa uletha isivumelwano sokuhlukanisa lapho izimpondo ezimbili ziyakubusa ulwandle nomhlaba, futhi kulezi zimbili enye iyimpondo encane kaDaniyeli. Kodwa ngithembisile ukuthi u-Israyeli ngeke aphinde ahlukaniswe, futhi kule mibuso yakhe emibili ngeke kube khona isixazululo, kodwa uYeshua HaMashiach uyakuhlanganisa u-Israyeli ngokuphelele naphakade.

(Kodwa kuyakwenzeka kanjalo, zithandwa zami, ngakho-ke qondani lokho engisazokwenza ngoYeshua HaMashiach: Ngiyakuthatha abantwana bakwa-Israyeli phakathi kwabezizwe, lapho besiye khona, ngibaqoqe nxazonke, ngibalethe ezweni labo. Ngiyakubenza babe isizwe sinye ezweni, ezintabeni zakwa-Israyeli, futhi inkosi eyodwa iyakuba yinkosi kubo bonke; futhi ngeke babe yizizwe ezimbili, futhi ngeke bahlukaniswe babe yimibuso emibili kusukela manje kuqhubeke. Futhi ngeke baphinde bangcoliswe ngezithombe zabo, nangezinengiso zabo, nangeziphambeko zabo; kodwa ngiyakubasindisa kuzo zonke izindawo zabo zokuhlala, lapho bonile khona, ngibahlanze; kanjalo bayakuba ngabantu bami, nami ngibe nguNkulunkulu wabo.)

Esinye isilo esivela olwandle, okulotshiwe ngaso: Ngokuba akakhathaleli abesifazane, futhi akahloniphi nkulunkulu: ngokuba uyakuzikhulisa ngaphezu kwakho konke. Ngiyanibuza manje ngithi anibone ukuthi umuntu uyaba nemihelo yokubeletha yini? Nokho umkakhe ubonakala engowesifazane onezingane, nakuba zingezona izingane zakhe. Futhi isilo esivela olwandle siyakuvuka sihlangene ngokuphelele nenjongo yesilo esivela emhlabeni, futhi bazenza izitha ezibonakala zehlukene ngemibono nangenkolo.

Kodwa bayaqamba amanga futhi bayazenzisa, futhi uyise wabo ngulowo drako owenze lezi zilo ezimbili ezimbi ngenjongo yakhe embi, yokubhubhisa u-Adamu ukuze antshontshe umhlaba unomphela ube ngowakhe, futhi akunakwenzeka ukuthi afeze amacebo akhe amabi ngoba umhlaba ungowenkosi nokugcwala kwawo, usho kanje uYHVH, uMINA ENGINGUYE Omkhulu!

Ngokuba uBaraba uletha izifundazwe ezimbili eziyohlanganiswa ngaphansi kukadrako, futhi bayoba nokuthula kwamanga njengoba bethembisa ukuphila okuphakade nempilo nokuthula uma nje niyothatha uphawu lwabo nihlanganyele nabo njengoba behlanganisa insimbi nobumba kodwa ngeke banamathelane. Qondisisani, nonke nina eningacabanga ukuzihlanganisa naphakade nabawileyo ngophawu lwabo ukuthi bayokhuluma amanga amaningi bethi kuwumuthi oyisimangaliso oyonika ukuphila nempilo futhi uphelele ngokumangalisayo ekwakhiweni kwawo!

Kodwa bonke abathatha ushevu wabo bayoziguqula ingunaphakade, uguquko okungekho ukululama kulo. Ngokuba ukuhlambalaza umfanekiso kaYHVH u-Adamu adalwa ngawo kuwukuzihlanganisa nezono zabawileyo abazenza onkulunkulu bengesi bona, futhi bonke abenza kanjalo abanakho ukuthethelelwa futhi abanakho ukuthula, futhi bayongena kulelo chibi lomlilo elingacimekiyo ingunaphakade ngoba bayobe bekhethe kanjalo.

Nokho, njengoba abathathu abangcwele bevuka, kanjalo nabafakazi bami ababili bayovuka, futhi bayoprofetha izinsuku eziyi-1260 bembethe indwangu yesaka futhi bayonikeza ubufakazi obuningi. Bayofika kwa-Israyeli bafakaze ngoNkulunkulu Oaphakade Wokulunga futhi bayofakaza ngamandla ngoYeshua HaMashiach, uJesu waseNazaretha, okungekho muntu ongeza ku-El Shaddai ngaphandle kwakhe! Nokho, abathathu abangcwele bayomemezela uJesu Kristu njengesigebengu futhi bayokwenza imibhalo engcwele, lapho uYeshua eyiZwi elenziwa inyama, ibe engekho emthethweni. Nokho, wona lowo abafuna ukumenza abe ongekho emthethweni uyochoboza amakhanda abo ekunqotshweni okuphakade.

Ngakho-ke, ezinsukwini zobufakazi babo uma intando yanoma yimuphi umuntu kuwukubalimaza, umlilo uyophuma emilonyeni yabo ushwabadele izitha zabo: ngokuba uma intando yanoma yimuphi umuntu kuwukubalimaza, khona-ke kumele balinyazwe ngendlela efanayo, futhi uma intando yanoma yimuphi umuntu kuwukubabulala, nabo kumele babulawe ngendlela efanayo. Laba banamandla okuvala izulu, ukuze lingani ezinsukwini zokuprofetha kwabo, futhi banamandla phezu kwamanzi ukuze bawaphendule abe yigazi, futhi bashaye umhlaba ngazo zonke izinhlobo zezinhlupho, kaningi ngangokuthanda kwabo. Futhi ziyoba njalo izinsuku zobufakazi babo.

Futhi bayokhuluma ngezinto eziningi futhi bayomemezela iVangeli Elaphakade, lona leli elambuliwe ngesikhathi salo sokugcwaliseka kwazo zonke izinto ebusweni bukaJesu Kristu. Futhi bayonikeza obabo ubufakazi futhi bayokwambula izinto eziningi phakathi nezinsuku zobufakazi babo futhi konke kuyobhalwa ingunaphakade. Ekupheleni kwezinsuku zabo kumele babulawe futhi izidumbu zabo ziyolala esitaladini somuzi omkhulu, ngokomoya obizwa ngokuthi yiSodoma neGibhithe, lapho neNkosi yenu yabethelwa khona, futhi bayojabula kakhulu banikane izipho ngokufa kwabo. Ekupheleni kwezinsuku ezintathu nengxenye bayozwa izwi elikhulu lithi khuphukelani lapha futhi bayovuswa bephila ingunaphakade futhi baphinde bathathwe bayiswe emazulwini, kanjalo ubufakazi babo buyobe buphelile.

Ngakho-ke qonda inkohliso efikile emnyango wakho Israyeli, futhi uqonde ukukhetha okumele ukwenze. Uyokwamukela uBaraba njengoMoshiach wenu wamanga futhi uthathe uphawu lwakhe, ngaleyo ndlela uhlanganyele no-Esawu njengoba unikezela ngobuzibulo bakho ukuze uthole ucezu lwesinkwa ingunaphakade, noma uyothobisa izinhliziyo zenu futhi nilalele izwi likaYahweh uNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade, uMINA NGINGUYE omkhulu ebusweni bukaYeshua HaMashiach ofana noKristu Jesu engamvusa kwabafileyo futhi wenyukela emazulwini, futhi ngamkhazimulisa ngenkazimulo yaphakade, futhi ukubusa kwakhe noMbuso wakhe ngeke kube nakuphela, futhi akekho umuntu ongasondela kuYHVH nganoma yiliphi elinye igama elinikiweyo, kungaba sezulwini noma emhlabeni noma ngaphansi komhlaba kuze kube phakade naphakade. Ngokuba kuKristu bonke bayokwenziwa baphile futhi benziwe babe muntu munye omusha ingunaphakade ngoba uKristu Jesu ungu-Adamu wokugcina.

Futhi bayolwa nabangcwele futhi babanqobe ngoba banikiwe amandla okwenza kanjalo. Kodwa ngeke banqobe u-Israyeli, cha, insali ka-Israyeli iyosindiswa lapho bephendukela kuYeshua HaMashiach ngezinhliziyo zabo zonke.

Ngakho-ke yizwani amazwi ami O' Israyeli ukuze niphile phambi kwami, futhi ngiyonibuthela kimi ngaphansi kwegunya elinamandla likaMesiya wenu weqiniso, uYeshua HaMashiach. NGINGU-YHVH, uMINA NGINGUYE omkhulu, futhi nginibiza nina Israyeli ukuze insali ethembekileyo ize kuYeshua HaMashiach ukuze ayisindise ogibeni uBaraba alwenzayo ezweni ukuze kubhujiswe uJakobe ngokuphelele, imbubhiso engingeke ngiyivumele.

Futhi bonke abakwa-Israyeli ababiza uYeshua HaMashiach bayophinde babone futhi babone ngokugcwele, nasebusweni bukaJesu uKristu, uMesiya wenu waphakade!
