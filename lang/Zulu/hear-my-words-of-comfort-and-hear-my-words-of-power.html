Ubufakazi Obumbaxambili: IBandla
Emisebenzini KaPawulu UmPhostoli
May 08, 2024

Yizwani Amazwi Ami Enduduzo Futhi Yizwani Amazwi Ami Amandla!

Zwanini amazwi eNkosi, wona amazwi kaYeshua HaMashiach. Wozani kimi, Makoti wami; wozani kimi senilungele, ngokuba ngiyeza kini. Phumulani kimi, nina bantwanyana bami, nihlale ezinyaweni zami. Yizwani amazwi ami enduduzo, futhi yizwani amazwi ami amandla, ngokuba nginiphile umduduzi, nakulowo mduduzi amandla amakhulu. Ngizweni nikholwe amazwi ami, thobani ukuzidla kwenu ukuze nizwe amazwi ezithunywa zami, ngokuba zithobekile ngenhliziyo. Angithumelanga kini abanamandla baleli zwe; angithumelanga kini abacebe kakhulu nabadumile (nakuba bezenzisa). Cha, nginithumelele labo izwe elibalahlele kude ngokudelela ngoba bakhulumela mina futhi umoya wami ukubo futhi awunakususwa.

Ningamangali ngokuthi izwe liyanizonda, ngokuba izwe lazonda mina kuqala, futhi inceku ayinkulu kunenkosi yayo. Uma bezonde mina, kanjalo-ke bazonizonda nani. Kodwa ningobani nina ukuba nesabe lokho umuntu angenzakona kini? Ningobani nina ukuba nihlale ebumnyameni nasekuthuthumeleni kanti nginiholele kuBaba wami noBaba wenu, kuNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade, ngokuba akekho omunye! Mina, yebo mina, Kristu Jesu, nginihlalise ezindaweni zasezulwini, futhi bonke abasezulwini balindele ngokulangazela okukhulu ukufika kwenu okuseduze, Makoti wami.

Ngokuba noNkulunkulu omkhulu waphakade, uBaba wethu, uYahweh uqobo (okhona kusukela kwaphakade kuze kube phakade) ulindele nina ngokulangazela okukhulu! Ngokuba maduzane kakhulu nizovela phambi kwesihlalo sakhe sobukhosi ngenkazimulo enkulu, futhi niyohlaliswa egumbini lakhe lesihlalo sobukhosi, ngokuba niyohlanganyela esihlalweni sami sobukhosi. Izingelosi ziyanilangazelela nazo ngenjabulo, bantwana bami, ngokuba kukhona injabulo ebukhoneni bazo nalapho kuphenduka isoni esisodwa. Ngokuba kunjalo: Kepha nina nifikile entabeni yaseSiyoni, nasemzini kaNkulunkulu ophilayo, iJerusalema lasezulwini, nasezinkulungwaneni ezingenakubalwa zezingelosi. Ngisho nezinye zalezi zingelosi ziyizikhonzi zalabo abazodla ifa lensindiso, futhi lokho kuhlanganisa nani, Makoti wami. Futhi mina, yebo uKristu Jesu, nami ngilangazelela ubukhona benu phambi kwami. Ngokuba ngiyanithanda futhi nginilangazelele, futhi maduze sizohlanganiswa kuze kube phakade ngomshado weWundlu. NginguMkhwenyana olangazelela uMakoti wakhe, futhi ngiyeza kini! Nginilungiselele izinto eziningi, futhi amagugu amaningi nemivuzo yokwethembeka inilindile. Maduze nizofika ezeni ifa lenu eligcwele kimi, futhi niyoba naphakade lonke ukuze nibone ubukhulu nobukhazikhazi balokho enginiqoqela kukho ukuze ngikufeze, nalokho engiyokwenza ngani, ngokuba niyisidalwa esisha, umuntu omusha, odalwe kimi! Futhi niyongenzela izinto eziningi kakhulu, hhayi kuphela eMbusweni wami wokuLunga lapho ngiyobusa umhlaba ngokulunga usuku olulodwa! Kodwa ikakhulukazi emazulwini amasha nasemhlabeni omusha ubukhulu benu buyokhanya, futhi niyoba nami kuze kube phakade. Ngokuba usuku olulodwa eNkosini lunjengeminyaka eyinkulungwane, neminyaka eyinkulungwane injengosuku olulodwa. Nginombuzo kini, zithandwa zami, yebo mina, Jesu Kristu, iNkosi yamakhosi neNkosi yamakhosi! Ngizweni nicabangele amazwi ami, ngokuba amazwi eNkosi angamazwi ahlanzekileyo: njengesiliva elihlolwe esithandweni somhlaba, elihlanjululwe kasikhombisa. Kungani nizenza abangenalutho? Ngiyaqonda ukuthi nithobekile futhi niphansi enhliziyweni, futhi lokho kuvela emvelweni entsha engayidala phakathi kwenu, ngokuba umuntu wemvelo uhlale ezigqaja. Kodwa kungani nizibheka njengabangenalutho, nizibuza ukuthi kungani uNkulunkulu engafuna ukusebenzelana nani, nokuthi kungani nginganinika insindiso kanti kukhona abangcono? Kungani nihlala lapho ekucindezelekeni, nizilahla lapho nona? Kungani ningazithinti uthuli? (Uthuli luyoba ukudla kwenyoka.) Sukumani! Phakamani! Vukani! Yebo, nonke niyona kusukela ekuseni kuze kushone ilanga. Ingabe lokhu kuyinto uYahweh angayinakanga ekwazini kwakhe kwangaphambili? Kuyini-ke? Ukhohliwe yini uNkulunkulu ukuthi niyona ngemva kwensindiso? Makube kude! Yebo, uNkulunkulu makaqiniswe, kodwa wonke umuntu abe ngumqambimanga! Ngokuba ecebweni lakhe lokuhlenga, ngisho nezinhlelo zakhe eziphathelene nami, ngisho nemfihlakalo enkulu kaNkulunkulu, zonke lezi zinto zahlanganiswa ndawonye ngomusa nesihe; yamukelani kuphela umusa wami nesihe sami, ngokuba abaningi benu esikhundleni salokho bayazishaya.

Bantwana bami, ngalikhokha inani lezono zenu. Lapho nihamba ngokulunga nangokwethembeka bese nona ngoba inyama yenu yonakele, vele nivume isono kimi nizihlanze ngegazi lami elihlengayo, ngokuba mina ngiyinkululeko yenu futhi nginihlengile. Abahlengiweyo beNkosi mabasho kanjalo: labo abahlengile esandleni sesitha. Akukho lutho eningakwenza ukuze nenezele emisebenzini yami eqediwe, ngokuba imisebenzi eqedwe ngokuphelele idingani okunye? Lapho ngithi esiphambanweni ekuphefumuleni kwami kokugcina kuleyo ndlu yomhlabathi obomvu, ngangigqokile ngaleso sikhathi: Sekuphelile, angishiyanga lutho lungenziwanga. Futhi lapho uNkulunkulu amukela umhlatshelo ophelile, ophelele, makungabikho muntu ozama ukwengeza lutho; ngokuba leyo yimisebenzi yobuze, ngisho nokuzidla kwenu okucashile nobuze benu, ngokuba emhlabeni kukhona ubuze obukhulu. Ubuze bezinto eziyize, usho umShumayeli: ubuze bezinto eziyize, konke kuyize. Ngokuba ubuze buyakhuluma, futhi buniqambela amanga, buthi ananele futhi umhlatshelo wami awuphelele.

Ngakho-ke, kufanele nenze okuthile okwengeziwe ukuze nifakazele ukufaneleka kwenu lapho niphenduka phambi kwami. Ngiqinisile ngithi, izithelo ezifanele ukuphenduka ziyamukeleka, kodwa hhayi ukuzilahla, hhayi ukuzizonda, hhayi imicabango yokuzehlula yokucindezeleka. Ngokuba lapho nizibandakanya kulezi zenzo ezingelusizo zenyama, ngaphandle kokwazi nibiza iqiniso ngamanga futhi nizama ukubuyisanisa inyama yenu nomoya. Bantwana bami, lokho okuzelwe yinyama kuyinyama nalokho okuzelwe ngumoya kungumoya. Ngokuba inyama ikhanuka okuphambene nomoya, nomoya okuphambene nenyama: futhi lezi zinto ziyaphikisana: ukuze ningakwazi ukwenza izinto eningathanda ukuzenza. Ngokuba ngomhlatshelo wami nibuyiseniwe nami, iNkosi yenu uJesu Kristu, futhi nginibuyisene noNkulunkulu, ngokuba injalo imfihlakalo enkulu kaNkulunkulu eniyingxenye yayo. Kungani ningabaza amazwi ami? Tshela inyama yenu ukuba ithule, futhi nithulise amazwi aphikisanayo nemfundiso yami eqinile! Ngokuba nginilungiselele ubukhulu. Ngininike amandla emoyeni wami wobudodana enikhala ngawo nithi, ABHA, Baba. Ngininike amandla futhi nginenze nakwazi ukungenzela izinto ezinkulu; futhi ngaphezu kwakho konke, nginithumile kimi, ngisho nasezincwadini zami zothando eziya kuMakoti wami. Ngisho nezincwadi engenza uPawulu azibhalele iBandla lami zichaza lokhu ngenkulumo ecacile ukuze niqonde nikholwe. Ngokuba ngathini eBandleni lami laseKorinte ngesandla sikaPawulu? Ngakho-ke, uma umuntu ekuKristu, uyisidalwa esisha: izinto ezindala zidlulile, bheka, konke sekube kusha. Futhi zonke izinto zivela kuNkulunkulu, osibuyisene naye ngoJesu Kristu, futhi wasinika inkonzo yokubuyisana. Bantwana bami, lapho nihamba emoyeni wami futhi nihlala nithembekile emiyalweni yami, nginidala kabusha kimi.

Ngokuba umuntu omdala womhlabathi obomvu uyabhubha, futhi bobabili umJuda nowezizwe bayokwenziwa babe muntu munye omusha kimi, ngisho nangomoya wami wobudodana, ngokuba ngiyosindisa abanye babo bonke. Manje, uma kukhona umuntu ongenawo umoya kaKristu, lowo akayena owakhe. Kuye futhi emva kokuba nikholwe, naphawulwa ngalowo moya ongcwele wesithembiso, Oyisibambiso sefa lethu, kuze kube sekuhleni kwempahla ethengiweyo, kube ludumo lwenkazimulo yakhe. Ngakho hambani ebuntwini obusha bempilo niqonde ukuthi maduzane kakhulu impahla yami ethengiweyo izokwamukela ngokugcwele (ninesibambiso somoya wami), futhi niyogqokiswa imizimba emisha, imizimba engonakele, imizimba enamandla ngobubanzi nangekhono, ngisho izindlu ezifanele inkosi! Ngokuba niyoba ngamakhosi nabapristi phambi kukaNkulunkulu wethu. Ngakho-ke nginibuyisene noNkulunkulu, futhi akusekho butha, futhi ngininikile inkonzo yokubuyisana. Oh, kodwa kungani ngingenza into enjalo, ningabuza? Lalelani ngizonitshela.

Kanjalo-ke abaseKorinte bayaqhubeka nobufakazi babo: Okungukuthi, uNkulunkulu wayekuKristu, ebuyisanisa izwe naye, engababaleli izono zabo, futhi usiphathise izwi lokubuyisana. Manje-ke singamanxusa kaKristu, sengathi uNkulunkulu uyanincenga ngathi; siyanithandaza esikhundleni sikaKristu, ukuthi nibuyisane noNkulunkulu. Bantwana bami, ningamanxusa ami, futhi niphethe isikhundla esihlonipheke kakhulu, ngokuba nimmele mina. Ngokuba ukuba yinxusa lami kusho ukumela iNkosi yamakhosi neNkosi yamakhosi ezweni elifayo, futhi ngingumpristi omkhulu kuze kube phakade ngokohlelo lukaMelkisedeki. Ngakho-ke, ngicela ningizwe: Nginenze amanxusa ami. Nginibuyisene noNkulunkulu uMninimandla onke, u-El Shaddai, ngokuba izinjongo zakhe ziyokwenziwa! Ngininikile izwi lokubuyisana, ngisho namazwi obuciko afanele amanxusa ami, ngokuba iZwi likaNkulunkulu livela kwaphakade kuze kube phakade. Futhi nginike ngamunye wenu inkonzo yokubuyisana. Lokhu kusho ukuthi ngigunyaze wonke umphefumulo ozelwe kabusha ngomoya wami onamandla njengezikhonzi zami ezinamandla, futhi isitha siniyesaba kakhulu futhi sizobaleka kini lapho nihamba emandleni ami, nakuba sizozama ukunihlebela amazwi amanga futhi sibhonge njengengonyama. Kodwa umlomo waso onukayo awunamazinyo, ngokuba aphulwa ngamandla okuvuka kwami, futhi ngeke silulame. Ungayinaki leyo ngonyama engenamazinyo, futhi ugxile ezwini lami, ngisho eMazwini eLizwi lami.

Kulesi sikhundla esikhulu segunya namandla (ngokuba nisebenza egameni lami futhi igama lami likhulu kunamanye onke amagama, futhi nguYahweh kuphela omkhulu!) nginibeke njengezikhonzi zami namanxusa ami, futhi nginilungisile, nganihlomisa, nganigqokisa amandla amakhulu! Bantwana bami, aniyiqondi imiyalo yamaZwi ami, ngokuba abelusi abahlubukayo baqasha izikhonzi zaseLawodikeya ukuze ziniqambele amanga mayelana namandla nesipho esidalwe kini ekuzalweni okusha. Ngokuba uPawulu wachaza ubukhosi bamandla anikezwe emoyeni wobudodana odalwe kini. Ngokuba watshela abaseKorinte: Ezintweni zikamoya, bazalwane, angithandi ukuba ningazi. Futhi futhi: Kukhona iziphiwo ezihlukahlukene, impela, kodwa munye umoya. Futhi futhi: Kodwa ukubonakaliswa koMoya kunikezwa wonke umuntu, ukuze kusize. Ningabi abangenalwazi ezindabeni zikamoya; qondani umehluko phakathi kwesiphiwo nokubonakaliswa, ngokuba akuwona amazwi afanayo embhalweni.

Nginike isiphiwo somoya ongcwele, futhi kuleso siphiwo kukhona ukubonakaliswa. Ngokuba lithini iZwi lami: Kodwa ukubonakaliswa komoya, kunikezwa wonke umuntu ukuze kusize.

Izikhonzi zenu ezisivivi zaseLawodikeya zinanele amanga uSathane awashilo eminyakeni edlule, amanga okwenza nikholwe ukuthi aninamandla, namanga okuzama ukufihla amandla enu eqiniso kimi, ngokuba umoya wenu unguKristu kini, ithemba lenkazimulo! Ngakho ukubonakaliswa kwami komoya kubalwe kanje: izwi lokuhlakanipha, izwi lolwazi, ukholo, iziphiwo zokuphilisa, ukwenza izimangaliso, ukuprofetha, ukuhlukanisa omoya, izinhlobo ezihlukahlukene zezilimi, ukuhunyushwa kwezilimi. Ngaphambi kokuba siqhubeke, bekani phansi izincazelo zenu zezwe nezichazamazwi zenu zezwe nezinkomba zenu zezwe, futhi niyeke ukuzivumela ukuba ziniqambele amanga ukuze zichaze kabusha umbhalo ngenjongo kaSathane.

Kuyo yonke imimoya kuwo wonke umuntu kukhona la mandla okubonakalisa amandla avela phezulu! Kodwa konke lokhu kusebenza ngalowo moya ofanayo, owabela umuntu ngamunye ngokuthanda kwakhe. Bantwana bami, kungani nicabanga ukuthi lezi yiziphiwo kanti umbhalo uthi ziyizibonakaliso? Baniyele amanga; fundani amazwi ami nibe nokholo emazwini abahumushi bami abawakhethileyo, ngokuba nganginabo. Nina ninawo onke la makhono, futhi uma nifuna ukuwabonakalisa, ngizowasebenza kini ukuze ngikhulule abathunjwa ekubanjweni kukaSathane ngibayise ekukhanyeni okukhazimulayo kwaphakade. Unganaki noma ubani okhuluma okuphikisanayo nala mazwi, futhi nifundisekeni eZwini lami. Yekani ukukhathazeka ngokuthi lezi zinto zisebenza kanjani, ngokuba azisebenzi ngamazwi obuciko nemicabango emihle. Zisebenza ngokwesidingo, ngesifiso somuntu okufanele abe nentando yokwenza intando yami, futhi ikakhulukazi ngokukholwa kimi. Ngokuba ngiyokwenza imisebenzi emikhulu ngezandla zenu, futhi nginithumile, makoti wami, ngokuba lokhu kungukuthunywa kokugcina.

Nginike isiphiwo somoya ongcwele, futhi kuleso siphiwo kunezinye iziphiwo eziningi, ngokuba bathini abase-Efesu: Kodwa kumuntu ngamunye wethu kunikezwe umusa, ngokwesilinganiso sesiphiwo sikaKristu. Kungakho ethi: Lapho enyukela phezulu, wahola ukuthunjwa ekuthunjweni, wanika iziphiwo kubantu. Futhi futhi kwabaseRoma: Ngokuba ngithi, ngomusa engiwuphiweyo, kuwo wonke umuntu ophakathi kwenu, ukuba angazicabangi kakhulu kunalokho okufanele akucabange, kodwa acabange ngokuqonda, njengoba uNkulunkulu abele umuntu ngamunye isilinganiso sokukholwa. Ngakho qondani ukuthi kufanele nizicabangele kakhulu ngokuba nidalwe kabusha ngokulunga nobungcwele beqiniso ngisho nokubonisa inkazimulo kaNkulunkulu uqobo, uBaba wezinkanyiso, ngokuba nina niyizinkanyiso zezwe. Kodwa cabangani ngokuqonda, ngokuba anibakhulu kunabafowenu nodadewenu abazelwe ngomoya ofanayo nani: Ngokuba nonke ninala makhono, futhi nonke ninalezi ziphiwo.

Ngokuba nginike iziphiwo kubantu: Futhi nginike abanye abaphostoli: nabanye abaprofethi: nabanye abavangeli: nabanye abelusi, nabafundisi: Ukuze kupheleliswe abangcwele, umsebenzi wenkonzo, ukwakhiwa komzimba kaKristu. Ziningi izinkonzo engizinike uMakoti wami, futhi ngezenu uma nifisa. Celani kimi ngizosebenza lezi zinkonzo kini, kodwa kufanele nibe nesifiso. Ngakho-ke zikhuthaleleleni iziphiwo ezinhle kakhulu: Kodwa ngisanikhombisa indlela enhle kakhulu. Leyo ndlela enhle kakhulu, bantwana bami, uthando lukaNkulunkulu olungenakunqotshwa. Kufanele nizikhuthalelele iziphiwo ezinhle kakhulu, ngokuba ngininikile amandla. Kodwa ukukhonza ngothando lukaNkulunkulu, ukunikela ngemihlatshelo yezindebe zenu nokudumisa kwenhliziyo yenu ethembekileyo ngothando, kuphakeme kakhulu. Phanini ngesihle njengoba namukele ngesihle. Hambani kuleli gunya ngoba nithanda uNkulunkulu futhi nithanda mina, Jesu Kristu, futhi niphane ngakho konke enikunikiweyo, futhi hambe phambili, ningangabazi lutho.

Makoti wami, ngakho-ke nginithumile ukuze niqedele le misebenzi, futhi maduze izifundo zenu zizophela, futhi lapho nginiqoqela kimi emafwini, niyokwazi, njengoba ngangazi esiphambanweni, futhi niyokwazi ukusho (njengoba ngake ngasho): Sekuphelile. Maduze niyongena enjabulweni yeNkosi yenu!

Ngiyeza kini maduzane kakhulu.

Ngilangazelela ubukhona benu phambi kwami.

NginguKristu Jesu futhi nginidale kabusha kimi.

Ngingumpristi omkhulu kuze kube phakade ngokohlelo lukaMelkisedeki. Nginguye onike iziphiwo kubantu.

Nginguye oninike inkonzo namazwi okubuyisana.

Nginguye onithumele njengamanxusa ami athembekile.

Nginguye enginguye, ngakho-ke batshele ukuthi uNginguye onithumile.

uNGINGUYE ungithumile kini.

Nginguye onithumayo kulesi sikhathi sokugcina.
