Mashi 14, 2024

Zwanini Izwi Lami!

Izwi leNkosi lehlela phezu kwami. Zwanini izwi lami, nina bantwanyana bami, nize ezingalweni zami ezimukelayo. Yazini ukuthi uthando lwami ngani alunakunqamuka futhi alunakunqotshwa, ngoba luwuthando lukaBaba ngokuba unike abantwana bakhe isimo sakhe, futhi kimi kuhlala konke ukugcwala. Nginibizile ngamagama enu futhi nginimemile ukuba nime phambi kwami. Izimvu zami ziyalizwa izwi lami, njengoba zilazi izwi lomalusi wazo. Anikalizwa yini izwi lami? Nizivalile yini izindlebe zenu ezwini lami? Ngikhala kakhulu ezitaladini nasemizileni; ngize ngavakashela namajele enu futhi ngaletha insindiso kwabahlukunyeziwe nabakhohliwe.

Hawu, muntu, ungishiyelani uJehova wakho? Kungani ufulathele izindlela zami? Nokho, uyothi, angifulathelanga izindlela zakho; ngingulowo iNkosi emmbizile. Ngeqiniso, ngabiza, wasabela isikhashana, isikhathi sobusha ngenjabulo enkulu. Abanye benu bayiswa lapho kuhlala khona izimpisi, futhi abaningi benu badukisiwe ngamazwi amahle, izinkulumo ezinhle, nezilimi eziqamba amanga esezizothuliswa maduze.

Khumbulani uthando lwenu lokuqala, niphenduke; vumani izono zenu kimi, njengoba ngihlale ngikulungele ukuhlanza izono zenu ngegazi lami lokuhlawulela.

Zwanini izwi lami, bantwana bami, njengoba amazwi amaningi ezwakala efana futhi oKristu bamanga abaningi bazama ukulingisa izwi lami. Ngeqiniso, ngiyalikhathalela futhi ngeluse ibandla lami ngesandla sami, ukuze nginethule kuNkulunkulu nihlanzekile njengentombi egqoke ilineni elicolekileyo elimhlophe, amabala ezono zenu ethethelelwe, esusiwe, futhi ekhohliwe, njengoba ngiwaphonse ekujuleni okukhulu, angaphinde avuke. Kodwa, abaningi benu baphazamisekile futhi bathakathiwe yilo mhlaba omubi wamanje. Bahlasela ngemilingo yabo ngokulunywa yinyoka, kodwa qondani ukuthi noma inyoka ikuluma, akukho monakalo oyokwehlela, ngoba uphawu lukaBaba alunakwephulwa. Gxilisani ukunaka kwenu kimi, iNdodana yakhe ezelwe yodwa, ayinikela ngokuzithandela ukuze ihlawulele izono zenu ukuze siphinde sihlangane, singaphinde sihlukane. Ake ngikuphulukise; ake ngikuphulukise ngokuphelele futhi ngisuse izibazi zakho ukuze ube uphilile futhi uphelele phambi kwami. Landelani izindlela zami futhi nilandele iZwi lami, ngoba:

NginguLizwi owenziwa inyama ohlala phakathi kwenu.

Ngingukudla kokuphila, futhi noma ubani odla kukho akasayikulamba futhi.

Ngingulowo ombuso wakhe ungesiwona owalomhlaba, nomvuzo wami unami kwabakholekile nabakhethiweyo bami.

Ngingulowo ohamba phakathi kwezinti zezibani eziyisikhombisa futhi oyala izingelosi eziyisikhombisa zeBandla.

Ngingcwele kaNkulunkulu.

Ngingumkhulumeli wenu.

Ngingulowo ovivinya izinhliziyo zabantu.

Ngihlale ngikulungele ukuhlanza izono zenu ngegazi lami lokuhlawulela.

Ngingumahluleli olungileyo.

NginguKristu uqobo owabikezelwa kusukela ekuqaleni.

NginguNdodana kaNkulunkulu owafika esenyameni.

Ngingulowo owanikela ngegazi lami elingenasono ngenxa yezoni, ukuze zizuze ifa lokuphila okuphakade.

NginguNdodana kaNkulunkulu owafika esenyameni.

Ngingumalusi omuhle.

Ngingulowo oweza kwabakhe futhi wenqatshwa ngabakhe.

NginguKristu uqobo, okhethwe nguBaba kusukela ekusekelweni komhlaba.

Nginguye Enginguye.

Bantwana bami, nginitshela ngeqiniso ukuthi sengelulele isandla sami emhlabeni ofayo amaJubili amaningi manje, futhi isandla sami ngeke sihlale seluliwe ngomusa nesihe, nakuba umusa nesihe kuhlala kunami. Ngibize abaningi, futhi abaningi benqabile. Nokho, labo engibabizile futhi ngabakhetha futhi ngabangcwelisa futhi ngabageza futhi ngabahlambulula futhi ngabaphilisa, bona kanye labo bayokuma phambi kwesihlalo sami sokwahlulela futhi bathwale umvuzo omkhulu ngokukholeka kwabo.

Nakuba nginemisebenzi eminingi okusamele niyifeze, futhi nakuba nenze okuningi ngenxa yami negama lami, ngifisa inhliziyo yenu ephelele ngaphezu kwemisebenzi yenu. Ngifisa ukuba niphendukele kimi futhi nisuke ezonweni zenu. Zani kimi nihlanzekile, njengoba iNkosi yenu ilungile inesandla sayo sokuphilisa eseluliwe ukuze nginenze nibe mhlophe njengeqhwa elisha. Nakuba izandla zami zabhobozwa, igazi lami lokuhlawulela laphuma kuzo nasemzimbeni wami kuze kubeiconsi lokugcina. Nganikela ngakho konke ukuze niphile phambi kukaBaba wethu kuze kube phakade.

Ngeqiniso, angihlawulelanga isono esisodwa; ngeqiniso, angihlawulelanga izono ezimbalwa; ngeqiniso, angihlawulelanga izono eziningi, kodwa ngahlawulela zonke izono zaso sonke isikhathi uma nizosabela kimi lapho ngithinta izinhliziyo zenu ngoMoya wami onamandla. Aniqondi yini ukuthi anginaso isifiso sokunilahla? Kukhona onaleso sifiso futhi ofisa ukubhujiswa kwenu futhi onimangalela imini nobusuku phambi kukaBaba, nakuba ngingumkhulumeli wenu. Uphonswe phansi futhi unqotshiwe; kodwa, kungani niphakamisela isela endaweni ephakeme ezinhliziyweni zenu ekubeni nginokunqoba okuphelele nokuphelele phezu kwakhe nombuso wakhe osuzophela maduze? Thulani nibe nokuthula nize kimi ngenhliziyo ethobekile. Anazi yini ukuthi uma benizahlulela nina, beningeke nahlulelwe? Celani kimi ukuba nginikhombise impilo yenu, njengoba ngizokwembula izinto eziningi kini ukuze nivume amaphutha enu futhi niphiliswe.

Ningesabi, nina bantwanyana bami, ngoba akumina onilahlayo. Lalelani unembeza wenu futhi ningalichithi izwi lami ngokuzithethelela. Lo mhlaba usuzophonswa phansi, futhi njengoba lo mhlaba uguqulwa ingunaphakade, niyophakanyiselwa emazulwini phezulu, ukuze nginethule kuBaba, ukuze nibone ifa lenu futhi namukele imivuzo yenu yokukholeka. Sengilangazelele usuku lapho sizobonana emazulwini ukuze ekugcineni nembulelwe impilo yenu yeqiniso njengoba nisindile kule mpilo yesikhashana yobubi, isono, nobubi. Ngingulowo ovivinya izinhliziyo zabantu, hhayi ukuthi nginilahle, kodwa ukuze ngomlilo womcwebisi nihlanzwe ezindleleni zenu ezindala futhi nazi izindlela zami zothando, ukukhanya, injabulo, ukuthula, ukubekezela, nobumnene.

Zwanini ukusola kwami ​​futhi namukele ukuyala kwami ​​ngoba uBaba wenu uniphatha njengabantwana, futhi yimuphi umntwana oyiwayo engathandwa nguBaba ongakhuzwa futhi ayalwe. Ukuyala kwami ​​kuthela izithelo zokuthula zokulunga, njengoba ukuyala kwami ​​kuwukukuhlanza ngokuphelele ezonweni zenu uma nizolalela amazwi ami futhi nilandele izwi lami. Hambani, nime, nikhulume onke amazwi alempilo. Abanye basazofika ensindisweni kulelihora lokugcina, futhi kuphela egameni likaJesu Kristu, igama lami elinamandla lapho kukhona insindiso, ngoba alikho elinye igama elinikezwe insindiso. Igama lami elinamandla limi liqinile, lilindele ukuba nibalethe kimi, ukuze ngilethe abaphendukayo kuBaba njengosana olusanda kuzalelwa ephakadeni. Nginothando olukhulu ngani, futhi njengoba uBaba engiphile, ngizonika abakholekile nabakhethiweyo bami.

NginguNdodana kaNkulunkulu owafika esenyameni, ngingenasono futhi ngimsulwa kuze kube phakade. Ngokunjalo, ngingulowo owanikela ngegazi lami elingenasono ngenxa yezoni ukuze zizuze ifa lokuphila okuphakade. Anisibo yini nina, abakholekile nabakhethiweyo bami, abaphakathi kwabazuza inkazimulo yaphakade? Kungani nisathule sengathi nilindele amazwi afanele. Khulumani! Vula umlomo wakho ukhulume, njengoba ngizoqondisa inkulumo yakho ukuba isize ubuhlungu obukhulu abasabuzwa ababiziweyo bami, futhi abaningi basazosabela. Hlanganyelani nami emsebenzini wami kulelihora lokugcina ngaphambi kokuba ngizamazamise izisekelo zomhlaba futhi ngiwudabule ezindaweni eziningi nokwahlulela okukhulu kugubungele umhlaba. Ngizonibuyisela lo mhlaba eMbusweni wami ozayo, kodwa kuqala umbuso wababi uzobhujiswa futhi uthathwe ngokwahlulelwa ukuze uboshwe kwalasha, futhi bayazi ukuthi ichibi elishisa bhe elizochitha kulo iphakade liyabalindela.

Ngizozichitha nya izihlalo zabo zamandla; ngizoyiqothula nya yonke imibuso yabo; ngizoyishisa nya ibe umlotha ngomlilo ongacimekiyo, njengoba nami, iNkosi ethembekile, nginitshelile ngaphambi kokuba kwenzeke. Endlini kaBaba kukhona izindlu eziningi, futhi ubukhulu nobukhazikhazi obukhulu bunilindele. Ngakho-ke, makungabikho muntu onikhohlisa ngomvuzo wenu futhi nihlale nithembekile kimi. Gcinani ukugxila kwenu kunye kimi, iNkosi yaphakade, iNkosi yenkazimulo. Ngingumalusi omuhle; nginguKristu uqobo okhethwe nguBaba kusukela ekusekelweni komhlaba. Ngingulowo oweza kwabakhe futhi wenqatshwa ngabakhe, nakuba ngokuvivinywa okukhulu uJakobe eyosindiswa ngesandla sami esinamandla. Ukuphela kwazo zonke izinto sekusondele, nokuphela kwenkosana yalomhlaba kuseduze. Nakuba eyokhululwa isikhashana, ukwahlulelwa kwakhe kwaphakade kuyalandela, lapho engeke akhululwe khona futhi ngeke kube khona ikhambi. Buyelani kimi, khulumani nami, nikhonze ngenxa yami, njengoba uBaba enginike zonke izinto, futhi umvuzo wami ngani ugcinwe emazulwini kwabakholekile nabakhethiweyo bami futhi uqinisekile njengophawu lukaBaba enginibeke uphawu ngalo. Sekusele imizuzu embalwa kuphela yesikhathi somusa, futhi ukwahlulela kwami ​​kuyofika ngokuzumayo njengoba ulaka lweWundlu lufika emhlabeni. Lungisani izinhliziyo zenu futhi nilungiselele, ngoba isikhathi siseduze.
