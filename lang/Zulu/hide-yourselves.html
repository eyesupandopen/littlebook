Zifihleni!

Zwanini amazwi kaYeshua HaMashiach afihlwe esithunyweni nasesikhonzeni sakhe, kodwa embulwe ngomyalo wami negunya lami ngalolu suku lukaSepthemba 14, 2025: Nanjengoba iminwe yakhe ithola izinkinobho ngomyalo wami nangoMoya wesiprofetho ophelele ogeleza ngamandla amakhulu njengoba ngembula amazwi ami azothola amehlo, izindlebe, nezinhliziyo zabo bonke abathunyelelwe wona, Sela!

Isandla sezitha zakho sisentanyeni yakho Melika! Phendukani! Yizwa amazwi ami Bhabhiloni eyimfihlo!

Usuku lweNkosi luphezu komhlaba, kodwa kuqala ihora elilodwa nesilo liyokwehlela emhlabeni ngosuku eningalulindele, ngoba nizitshela nithi ngihlezi njengendlovukazi, futhi angisoze ngaba ngumfelokazi, umsindisi wami omkhulu uyosenza sibe bakhulu futhi!

Kodwa nikhohlisiwe nina bakhileyo bomhlaba ngokucabanga ukuthi noma yimuphi umuntu ulingana noKristu Jesu, iNkosi yaphakade yokulunga, eningamazi, yebo nina nonke enihlezi ebumnyameni nithanda ububi ngokungalaleli okukhulu kwezwi likaKristu Jesu oyilo futhi iWundlu likaNkulunkulu elihlezi ngakwesokunene sikaYahweh, uMINA ENGINGUYE omkhulu, engihlanganyela naye isihlalo sobukhosi negunya ngoba ngenziwe umoya ophilisayo, manje omile ngamandla amakhulu njengeNgonyama yesizwe sakwaJuda ngoba ayikho enye iNdodana yoMuntu, Sela, futhi qondani ukuthi intukuthelo nolaka lwami kumiselwe ukwehlela ekubhujisweni okuzumayo eningeke nikubone kuza, futhi ningeke nikuphunyuke.

Maye, MAYE OMKHULU kini madodana abantu angalaleli, eniyokwehlela phezu kwenu manje ulaka lweWundlu ngokuzuma okukhulu, lilethe imbubhiso ezumayo nengalindelekile ngenxa yezindlela zenu zokukhonza izithixo, wena sifakazi esikhulu, futhi niyokuzwa ukubhonga kwami lapho umhlaba uba mnyama, ngoba angisoze ngabelana ngenkazimulo yami nomunye, Sela!

Yizwa amazwi ami Heyleli, ngisho nawe nkosana ehluliwe, uhlale phansi ekuthobekeni kokwehlulwa futhi uyeke ukubhonga kwakho okungenamazinyo, ngoba uyisitha esehluliwe esingeke sibe nenkathi yegolide kodwa ukwahlulelwa okunzima esikhundleni salokho, kodwa kuqala uyosebenzela izinjongo zikaYHVH uMINA ENGINGUYE omkhulu, ngokuthobela igunya lami ekwehlulweni kwakho, (nasehlazweni nasekwehlulweni kwakho) uyokwenza kuphela lokho ovunyelwe ukukwenza ngegunya lami, ngoba ayikho indawo endalweni kaYHVH lapho isandla sami negunya lami eligcwele kungafinyeleli khona futhi ngiyakubona ukwesaba kwakho, wena nyoka ehluliwe! Hlala phansi uthule wena serafi elihlubukayo!

Esandleni sami .. isihluthulelo .. nekateni elikhulu, futhi maduze uzoboshwa ekwehlulweni okuphelele ngokuthobela isihlalo sobukhosi sikaYHVH uMINA ENGINGUYE omkhulu, njengoba umbuso wakho uyowa ngokuphelele ekwahlulelweni okukhulu futhi ngiyakubona ukwesaba kwakho nkosana encane, futhi ngeke uphunyuke ezandleni zami ngakho yizwa amazwi eNgonyama yesizwe sakwaJuda, futhi uqonde intukuthelo nolaka lwami namandla nokuthi ngeke ngiphikwe impahla yami!

Hlala uthule nkosana encane ngoba isandla sami siqinile entanyeni yakho, futhi lokho okuthiwa yi-AI yakho kuthobele ngokuphelele isihlalo sobukhosi sikaYHVH, uMINA ENGINGUYE omkhulu, ngomyalo wami wena nkosana ehluliwe! Futhi ngubani ongavimba isandla esinamandla, amandla, negunya elinikwe kuKristu Jesu, iNkosi yaphakade yokulunga, onguYeshua HaMashiach futhi, Sela!

Nina nonke eniwile niyokwenza kuphela lokho enikuvunyelwe ukukwenza ehlazweni nasekwehlulweni kwenu, nokho ihora lenu selifikile nkosana encane, futhi ubona ngokucacile, nakuba uqamba amanga amakhulu, wena oshelelayo. Kodwa izindlela zakho seziseduzane nokuphela futhi usizi lukaJakobe oluyiviki lamashumi ayisikhombisa likaDaniyeli luyohlanza umhlaba ezindleleni zakho kuze kube phakade: Ngaphandle kwenkathi yakho yokugcina encane yokuhlubuka lapho uyoqoqa khona amakhoba asele ayovela kabusha ekuhlubukeni ngaphansi kombuso wokulunga weVangeli laphakade manje elambulwe ngokwengxenye encwadini encane yesAmbulo isahluko 10, yebo incwadi encane evuliwe engiyivule esandleni sesikhonzi sami.

Nokho kunjalo! Okusele kweTestamente elisha kuyonikezwa uJakobe oyohlala ezinyaweni zabafakazi bami ababili abayofika ngokuzuma emazweni ngendlela ongeke wayibikezela, wena nkosana encane ehluliwe. Futhi iminqumo yami emibili, okuyizinti zami ezimbili zamakhandlela, iyolungisa futhi ihlukanise insali enamandla kakolweni eyokhothamela uYeshua HaMashiach ngaleyo ndlela isuse ubumpumputhe ngokwengxenye kubantu bami beqiniso abayohlukaniswa nokhula lwakho! Kodwa igceke labezizwe liyonyathelwa phansi empini emelene nabangcwele elwiwa yisilo sasolwandle, esizibiza ngokuthi umbani uwa ezulwini ngesiHeberu, esibhalwe ngisho njengoBarack Hussein Obama osebenzisana nesilo somhlaba esinobuthi baso nokulunywa yinyoka esandleni saso esiyohlanganisa insimbi nobumba kodwa ngeke zibambane.

Futhi u-Enoke umbhali no-Eliya umThishibi bayolungisa ukolweni wami ukuze ususwe, ngisho nokolweni wami olondolozwe ingunaphakade ngokuvikelwa kwesivumelwano nesithembiso emadodaneni angalungile okungalaleli kuze kube phakade ekugcwalisekeni kweTestamente lami elisha lokulunga, lapho iBhayibheli lami elingcwele laliwukuphela kwesibambiso sempilo ethengiwe eyohlala njalo eqinisweni laphakade ngoba ngiyiZwi lika-Adonai-YHVH elenziwe inyama, futhi eliyisisekelo senu saphakade sensindiso, ngisho netshe lenu legumbi eliyinhloko, elizokhipha umyalo maduze nje: Futhi kube nokumemeza okukhulu! Futhi lokho kuvuthelwa kwecilongo kuyocula futhi kumemezele ngokuzuma, ngamandla, nangokungalindelekile manje ekugubheni abakholwayo kuKristu, ukuze kuqondwe kuphela yilabo abavumelaniswe nemvamisa yalo ngokuphenduka okulungileyo, abanemicu yabo ilungiswe ngokulalela izindlela zami!

Labo abezwayo: Mabaqonde ukuthi imisindo ikhala ngokukholwa nangokuthula, ngoba yimuphi ubaba onganika indodana yakhe inyoka, ngaphandle kokuba ngaphansi kwezinyawo zayo, iboshwe ngokuthula kokwehlulwa ukuze ikhululwe ukuze ibuse abangakholwa, kuphela lapho abakholwayo kuKristu besuka ngokuzuma manje!

Futhi lapho inkazimulo yokukhanya kwami ​​igqamuka esibhakabhakeni futhi lelo cilongo elinamandla likhala kamnandi esimemweni esilindelwe soMlobokazi wami nezimenywa zethu esizokhalela abakholwayo kuKristu, yebo nina nonke engininanze manje imvamisa yalelo cilongo elinamandla likaNkulunkulu eliyicilongo lokugcina lenkathi yami yomusa ezophela ngokuzuma nangokungalindelekile: Yizwani amazwi bantu bami ngoba umgcini nomhlengi wenu manje umi elungele ukunikhulula ngokugcwele Bandla lami, Mzimba wami ngisho noMlobokazi wami, Thokozani!

Zilungiseleleni mlobokazi wami nihlale eceleni kwendlela nezibani zenu zigcwele amafutha ami ahlanzekile, okuwulophawu lokulunga nomoya wobudodana, okuwumoya ongcwele wesithembiso ozelwe ngaphakathi komntwana ngamunye wokukhanya, onaso ngokugcwele isibambiso saleyo mpahla ethengiwe, esezokhululwa ngokugcwele nguKristu Jesu, iNkosi yabo yaphakade ngamazwi ahlanzekile obufakazi bami nomoya ngoba ulaka lwami alulona olwenu.

Ngakho-ke, yekani imisebenzi yenu niphumule othandweni lwami bantu bami, kodwa qinani nivumele inkemba yomoya wami ehlukanise amakhoba okugcina ezimpilweni zenu zithandwa zami.

Zilungiseleleni zithandwa zami ngoba ukuhlengwa kwenu sekufikile futhi maduze nizozwa ukumemeza okukhulu futhi nizodonselwa phezulu ngokuzuma manje futhi ngokuphazima kweso egumbini lesihlalo sobukhosi sikaYHVH uqobo, ngakho-ke zilungiseleleni bantu bami ngoba ngifika njengesela ebusuku hhayi njengomcimbi ohleliwe, nokho makume ukuhlukanisa kwami nibale izinombolo ngokwamazwi ami angcwele nezindlela zami; hhayi abathakathi abahlolayo nabanyenyezayo, noma abanamathelene nezinsuku ezingahambisani nekhalenda lenu elingcolile likaGregory.

Yizwani amazwi ami nina bakhileyo bomhlaba ngoba ngiyiNgonyama yesizwe sakwaJuda futhi ngiza ukuzokwahlulela nokulwa!

Yizwani izwi lokuhlakanipha niguqe ngedolo nizwe izwi eliphilayo likaYeshua HaMashiach, ngoba ngiyiNgonyama yesizwe sakwaJuda, futhi ngiyiNkosi yamakhosi noMbusi wababusi! Yebo lowo u-El Shaddai, uNkulunkulu uSomandla, amphakamise kakhulu wamnika igama eliphezu kwawo onke amagama. Ukuze ngegama likaJesu amadolo onke aguqe, ezintweni ezisezulwini, nasezintweni ezisemhlabeni, nasezintweni ezingaphansi komhlaba: Futhi ukuze zonke izilimi zivume, ukuthi uJesu Kristu uyiNkosi, kube yinkazimulo kaBaba wezibani, yebo uYahweh, uNkulunkulu waphakade nongunaphakade, ngoba akekho onjengoBaba wethu!

Yizwani izwi leNkosi yenu niqonde izinto esezivele zenzeka nezinto ezizokwenzeka maduze, ngoba bhekani nginguye okhuluma ngezinto ezizayo sengathi sezenzekile ngoba ngempela iNkosi yenu enkulu, yebo mina Kristu Jesu ophakeme kunabo bonke, ngeke ngenze lutho, ngaphandle kokuba ngembule izimfihlo zami ezincekwini zami abaprofethi. Kungani nilalela amazwi amaningi aphikisanayo, futhi kungani nintengezela phakathi kwemibono emibili? Angishongo yini ukuthi abaprofethi bami bakhuluma ngazwi linye futhi ngeke baphikise amazwi ami angcwele? Ngoba ngiyiZwi elenziwe inyama elihlala phakathi kwenu ngomoya wami ongcwele ozelwe ngaphakathi kwenu kuze kube phakade, ngoba umphefumulo ngamunye ozelwe kabusha uphawulwe ngalowo moya ongcwele wesithembiso oyimbewu yami engonakali futhi uphawu lukaNkulunkulu ophilayo alunakwephulwa. Futhi usuku lokuhlengwa kwempahla ethengiwe selusondele futhi ukuhlengwa kwenu okugcwele sekukhona.

Usho kanje uJehova Sebawoti, ningawalaleli amazwi abaprofethi abaprofetha kini, abanifundisa okuyize: bakhuluma umbono wenhliziyo yabo, hhayi ophuma emlonyeni weNkosi. Wozani kimi bantwana bami ngoba ukugcotshwa enikwamukele kimi kuhlala kini: futhi anidingi ukuba umuntu anifundise: Kodwa, njengoba ukugcotshwa okufanayo kunifundisa ngezinto zonke, futhi kuyiqiniso, futhi akusilo inyoka: futhi njengoba kunifundisile, niyohlala kimi. Bantwana bami hlalani kimi niqhathanise amazwi anoma ngubani othi ukhuluma egameni lami nemibhalo ningicele ngiqinisekise emoyeni wenu, uma mhlawumbe niyolalela izwi lami ngoba abaningi benu banezindlebe ezilukhuni, futhi abaningi benu bathanda amazwi amanga akitaza izindlebe zenu ukuze nibonakale nihlakaniphile kodwa esikhundleni salokho nilandele amazwi amanga ezithutha ezizidlayo.

Bhekani! Ngikhuluma ngezikhathi zokuhlupheka kukaJakobe osekulungele ukuqubuka emhlabeni wonke. Ngoba ukwahlulela sekuvele kuyaqhubeka futhi akunakunqandwa, ngoba lokho uYahweh akumemezelayo akekho ongakushintsha! Izinyembezi eziningi ngizikhalile ngokwahlulelwa kweBhabhiloni eyimfihlakalo futhi ngithumele abaprofethi bami iminyaka eminingi manje futhi ngithumele nabafokazi abavela emazweni angaziwa ukuba bafakaze futhi baprofethe ngokwahlulelwa okwakusondela kodwa manje sekukhona.

Ngoba kuyoba njalo ngoba kulotshiwe kanje: Izimpondo eziyishumi owazibona esilwaneni, lezi ziyosizonda isifebe, zisenze incithakalo, zisihambise nqunu, zidle inyama yaso, zisishise ngomlilo. Ngoba uNkulunkulu ufake ezinhliziyweni zazo ukuba zenze intando yakhe, zivumelane, zinike umbuso wazo esilwaneni, kuze kugcwaliseke amazwi kaNkulunkulu. Futhi owesifazane, owambona, yilo muzi omkhulu obusa phezu kwamakhosi omhlaba.

Yizwani amazwi ami, ukwahlulelwa kweBhabhiloni eyimfihlo sekuvele kuqhubeka. Izimpondo eziyishumi zilungiselele ukuhlasela kwazo okuzumayo futhi ukuhlasela kwazo kuyasondela futhi kusemnyango wenu. Ngoba ngisho nabezindaba benu bafuna ukuniduduza nilale futhi abaningi bahlezi ebumnyameni bengaboni iqiniso bethi: Ngihlezi njengeNdlovukazi futhi angiyena umfelokazi futhi angisoze ngabona ukulila. Kusasa kuyoba njenganamuhla, ngoba namuhla kunjengezolo. Njengoba ezidumisa, futhi ehlala ebumnandini, mnikeni ukuhlushwa nosizi olungako: Ngakho izinhlupho zakhe ziyofika ngosuku olulodwa, ukufa, nosizi, nendlala, futhi uyoshiswa ngomlilo: ngoba uYahweh omlahlayo, unguNkulunkulu uSomandla futhi akekho omkhulu futhi akukho okungavimba isandla sika-El Shaddai esinamandla!

Ngakho-ke ilanga liyophuma maduze ngosuku lwenhlekelele eningayilindele, futhi lolo suku lusondele kunalokho enikucabangayo. Futhi lolo suku luyoletha incithakalo ephelele eBhabhiloni eyimfihlakalo, ngoba linesabelo esiphindwe kabili sokwahlulelwa esimenyezelwe ngalo. Amacala alo nobufebe balo maningi futhi isijeziso salo asilibali futhi isijeziso esikhulu siyokwehlela phezu kwabangaphenduki njengoba iBandla lami elithembekile ngisho noMlobokazi wami othembekile beyobuthelwa kimi ngokuzuma emafini basindiswe olakeni, ngisho nokuhlupheka okukhulu kweviki lamashumi ayisikhombisa likaDaniyeli. IBhabhiloni elikhulu liwile, liwile! Yizwani amazwi ami ngoba ngikhuluma sengathi sekuvele kwenzekile ngoba akukho okuyoshintsha, kwehlise ijubane, kuvimbe, noma kumise ukwahlulela kukaYahweh ekuweni emhlabeni.

Ngehora elilodwa iyoshiswa ngomlilo, eminye imizi yayo iyosha, eminye imizi yayo iyokhukhulwa ngodlame, imibhoshongo yayo iyowa iphuke ibe yizicucu eziningi, amabhuloho ayo ayowa, futhi kube nokuzamazama okukhulu. Umhlaba uyodabuka ngodlame futhi izwe eliningi liyombozwa ngamanzi anodlame. Izingubo zayo ziyombozwa ngasempumalanga nangasentshonalanga ngamagagasi amakhulu futhi maphakathi kube namagagasi agijimayo nokuzamazama okunodlame. Okusalayo kuyoba yihlane futhi ayisoze yaphinde yavuka, ngoba lesi sizwe sezifundazwe eziningi nemizi eminingi asisoze savuka futhi ukuwa kwaso kuyoba kukhulu! Ngoba umhlaba ungowenkosi nokugcwala kwawo, futhi zonke izici zethonya lawo elibi ziyosulwa phakade ezizweni phakathi neviki lamashumi ayisikhombisa likaDaniyeli, ngoba ngiyowuhlanza kanjalo umhlaba ngolaka lwami. Mina, Kristu Jesu, iNgonyama yesizwe sakwaJuda, ngimemezele la mazwi, futhi ukulungiselela ukugcwaliseka kwawo sekuvele kuyaqhubeka futhi kuyogcwaliseka ngokuzuma ngehora elilodwa.

Ngakho-ke impi iyogqashuka emhlabeni wonke njengengxenye yokuhlasela okuzumayo kweBhabhiloni eyimfihlo, ngoba lokhu kuhleliwe futhi kwahlanganiswa iminyaka eminingi yama-jubilee manje, ngoba imfihlakalo yobubi ibisebenza kusukela ezinsukwini zasendulo futhi iyofinyelela ekugcwalisekeni okuphelele lapho umvimbeli esuswa njengoba abakholwayo bami bethathwa bayiswe emazulwini. Impi iyokwehlela emhlabeni wonke futhi iyofika kubo bonke abantu nasezizweni zonke, ngoba lena yimpi yesithathu enjalo, njengoba abawile befuna ukubhubhisa imiyalelo yakudala yomhlaba futhi bathathele zonke izinto indawo ngomyalelo wabo omusha, ngisho nomyalelo kaDrako. Isilo esivela olwandle nesilo esivela emhlabeni bayovuka ngolaka olukhulu, futhi bayolwa nabangcwele, ngoba kunikwe bona ukuba banqobe abangcwele. Kuyoba njalo ukuthi bonke abangazithandi izimpilo zabo futhi babize egameni leNkosi kuze kube sekupheleni bayosindiselwa enjabulweni yaphakade ebuphakadeni obuphakade. Futhi mina Jesu waseNazaretha ngishiye isibonelo saphakade sokulalela kuze kube sekufeni, ngoba ukulalela kwami uYahweh kwaba ngisho nokufa esiphambanweni njengoba ngahlupheka futhi ngakhokhela izono zesintu njengewundlu likaNkulunkulu futhi bhekani ngiza njengeNgonyama yesizwe sakwaJuda futhi ngeke ngivinjwe noma ngiphazanyiswe!

Kanjalo-ke abaprofethi bami ababili abayiminqumo emibili nezinti zezibani ezimbili ezimi phambi kukaNkulunkulu womhlaba bayoprofetha izinyanga ezingama-42 bembethe indwangu yamasaka, ngokuba bathathwa baya emazulwini ezikhathini zasendulo. Uma kukhona umuntu ofuna ukubalimaza, umlilo uyophuma emlonyeni wabo, uqothule izitha zabo; uma kukhona ofuna ukubalimaza, kumele abulawe ngale ndlela. Laba banamandla okuvala izulu, kumiswe imvula ezinsukwini zokuprofetha kwabo; banamandla phezu kwamanzi ukuwaphendula igazi, nokushaya umhlaba ngazo zonke izinhlupho, kaningi ngendlela abathanda ngayo.

Ubufakazi babo ngobaphakade, futhi bayomemezela ivangeli laphakade, futhi bayokhuluma ngamacebo kaYahweh azo zonke izikhathi, futhi bayongikhulumela mina Kristu Jesu, futhi bayokwambula izimfihlakalo eziningi futhi bayophendula izinhliziyo zabaningi kuBaba Wezibani okungekho kuye ukuguquguquka nasithunzi sokuphenduka. Bayofakaza ngami Yeshua HaMashiach ngokuba yimi uYahweh angithumele kwa-Israyeli ukugcwalisa izithembiso ezenziwa koYise babo futhi nginguMesiya weqiniso manje naphakade futhi umbuso wami awunaphelo! Ngokuba zikhathi zini ezake zaba khona lapho uYahweh ezishiye engenabufakazi? Azikaze zibe khona izikhathi lapho uYahweh ezishiye engenabufakazi, futhi bonke abamiselwe insindiso yaphakade bayosindiselwa ekuphileni okuphakade nasenjabulweni yaphakade, ngokuba ukugcwaliseka kwazo zonke izinto sekuvele kuqhubeka.

Kanjalo isilo sasolwandle siyovuka kanye nesilo somhlaba, esingumprofethi wamanga. Futhi siyovula umlomo waso sikhulume inhlamba enkulu ngoYahweh sihlambalaze igama lakhe nedokodo lakhe nalabo abahlala ezulwini. Futhi siyonikwa amandla okulwa nabangcwele nokubanqoba futhi siyonikwa amandla phezu kwazo zonke izizwe, nezilimi, nezinhlanga ngokuba siyohola umhlaba. Isilo somhlaba siyobusa futhi izwe njengoMoshiach wabo wamanga futhi sibe umakhi wethempeli labo. Isilo sasolwandle siyobusa ngokwemithetho yabo kaNowa eyinhlamba njengoba befuna ukuqeda noma yini ephathelene noNkulunkulu weqiniso nendodana yakhe ezelwe yodwa uJesu Kristu ebusweni bomhlaba.

Isilo somhlaba siyokwenza umhlaba ukhonze isilo, esinelonda elibulalayo elaphola. Futhi siyokwenza izimangaliso ezinkulu ukukhohlisa abakhileyo emhlabeni, ngokuba siyovunyelwa ukwenza izibonakaliso eziningi zamanga nezimangaliso, futhi siyokwenza umlilo wehle emhlabeni emehlweni abantu futhi siyoholela bonke abantu ukuba basimangalele. Kodwa singumphikisi, futhi siyaziphakamisa ngaphezu kwakho konke okuthiwa uNkulunkulu, noma okukhonzwayo; ukuze sihlale njengoNkulunkulu eThempelini likaNkulunkulu, sizibonakalisa singuNkulunkulu.

Futhi siyokwenza umfanekiso wesilo futhi senze bonke bakhonze umfanekiso esiyowenza. Futhi siyovunyelwa ukunika umoya emfanekisweni wesilo ukuze umfanekiso wesilo ukhulume futhi wenze bonke abangafuni ukukhonza umfanekiso wesilo babulawe. Futhi siyokwenza bonke, abacebile nabampofu, abakhululekile nezigqila, ukuba bamukele uphawu lwaso esandleni sabo sokunene noma emabunzini abo.

UMA UMUNTU EKHONZA ISILO NOMFANEKISO WASO, AMUKELE UPHAWU LWASO EBUNZINI LAKHE, NOMA ESANDLENI SAKHE, YENA LOWO UYOPHINDA APHUZE EWAYININI LOLAKA LUKANKULUNKULU, ELITHULULWE LINGAXUTSHIWE ENDEBENI YOLAKA LAKHE; UYOHLUSHWA NGEMLILO NESIBABULE, PHAMBI KWEZINGELOSI EZINGCWELE, NAPHAMBI KWEWUNDLU; FUTHI INTUTHU YOKUHLUSHWA KWABO YENYUKELA PHEZULU PHAKADE NAPHAkade. FUTHI ABANAKUPHUMULA EMINI NASEBUSUKU, ABKHONZA ISILO NOMFANEKISO WASO, NANOMA UBANI OWAMUKELA UPHAWU LWEGAMA LASO.

Izwe elikhona manje alisoze laba khona futhi, futhi umbuso kaDrako okhulayo uyochithwa ngokuphelele, njengoba umbuso kaSathane uqonda ekupheleni kwawo, ngokuba bangabahlali ngokungemthetho futhi abasiwo amakhosi. Futhi leli yizwe abaningi abayozithola bekulo, ngisho nalabo abasinda ezinhlekeleleni ezizayo nasempini. Ningaluthathi uphawu bantu bami, ngokuba bayoza bethembisa ukuthula, bayoza bethembisa ukuphepha, bayonitshela ukuthi konke kungenxa yezinhloso zenu ezinhle. Bayokwethula lokho okubizwa ngezifo eziwumshayabhuqe zabo ngokuba sebevele bahlelile ukukholisa umhlaba ukuthi bayagula. Futhi zonke izinhlobo zezifo ezithathelwanayo nezifo nezinhlupho ziyokwanda ngokuba kunjalo imiphumela yabagibeli bamahhashi abezayo futhi bayogibela!

Bayonikeza impilo nokuphulukiswa ezahlulelweni zikaNkulunkulu, kuyilapho umuntu kufanele aphenduke ebubini bakhe. Bayonikeza ukungena emphakathini wabo kude nezahlulelo ezimbi esezizokwehlela emhlabeni. Ngokuba mina, Kristu Jesu, ngiyanitshela ngaphambi kokuba kwenzeke ukuze nazi ubuqotho bamazwi ami futhi nikwazi ukuhamba emanzini ayingozi lapho abaningi beyozithola bekuwo, ngisho nalabo abanesibindi sokubiza igama lami kuze kube sekupheleni, ngokuba bayizindlalifa zensindiso yaphakade. Ngokuba bonke ababiza uJesu waseNazaretha njengeNkosi yabo, ngisho bonke ababiza igama lami elinamandla futhi abangathandi ukuphila kwabo kuze kube sekupheleni, bayosindiselwa ekuphileni okuphakade.

Anifundanga yini? Laba yibo abaphuma osizini olukhulu, futhi bahlanza izingubo zabo, bazenza zaba mhlophe egazini leWundlu. Ngakho-ke, baphambi kwesihlalo sobukhosi sikaNkulunkulu, futhi bamkhonza imini nobusuku eThempelini lakhe; futhi lowo ohlezi esihlalweni sobukhosi uyohlala phakathi kwabo. Abasayikulamba, abasayikoma, nelanga alisayikukhanya kubo, nanoma yikuphi ukushisa. Ngokuba iWundlu, eliphakathi kwesihlalo sobukhosi, liyobondla, futhi liyobaholela emithonjeni yamanzi aphilayo; futhi uNkulunkulu uyosula zonke izinyembezi emehlweni abo.

Nokho, nakuba isixuku esikhulu kakhulu siyosindiswa evikini lamashumi ayisikhombisa likaDaniyeli futhi insindiso iyoza kwa-Israyeli ngokuba insali isazokhothama kimi uYeshua HaMashiach, (Ngokuba nginguMesiya weqiniso lapho iwundlu likaNkulunkulu liyovumela iNgonyama Yesizwe SakwaJuda, futhi kokubili kuyohlanganiswa ngokuphelele kimi, ngokuba kimi uKristu Jesu iNgonyama iyolala ngokuthula newundlu.) ngokudabukisayo, nangokungadingekile, iningi lomhlaba liyomangala futhi lilandele laba babusi abanjengezilo, ngokuba bayothakatha abaningi. Ngokuthakatha kwabo nemilingo, bayoyenga izwe ukuba lamukele ubuthi babo, futhi bayobulala unomphela labo abathanda izwe ngemilingo yabo, ngokuba uphawu lwesilo luyofihliswa njengomuthi. Ngisho nokulunywa yinyoka okuyisimiso sabo esesivele sethuliwe futhi sahlolwa, futhi izixuku seziphoqiwe ukwamukela uphawu lwabo nomsebenzi wabo wokulwa nembewu kwaba yimpumelelo.

Kanjalo uhlelo lwabo lulungele isikhathi esifanele, futhi balungiselele izikolo zabo zenhlalo futhi bonke abahlala emadolobheni abo nasemphakathini wabo bayolawulwa. Ngokuba kuyodingeka bathathe uphawu lwesilo ukuze babambe iqhaza emphakathini wabo kasathane, ngisho nombuso kaDrako. Labo abathatha uphawu bayoguqulwa unomphela. Izakhi zabo zofuzo ziyoguqulwa, ngokuba bayozihlanganisa nembewu yabantu; kodwa abayikunamathelana, njengoba insimbi ingaxutshwa nobumba. Uphawu lwabo lwesilo, ngisho nokulunywa yinyoka, luyoqothula ngokuphelele bonke abathatha ubuthi babo futhi akukho ukuphunyuka.

Ngisho bonke labo abathatha uphawu bayoguqulwa unomphela, futhi bayozithola beyingxenye yohlelo lwabo, futhi bayohlanganiswa ubuchwepheshe futhi izingqondo zabo ngeke zisaba ngezabo. Bayolawulwa futhi babe njengabafileyo abahambayo, bayoba izigqila zaphakade zengqondo ehlangene yohlelo lwabo, ngisho nobuchwepheshe babo ngokuba bayohlanganiswa ne-inthanethi yezinto ezizayo. Bayolahlekelwa yintando yabo, futhi ngeke babe namizwa futhi noma yini kaNkulunkulu iyosuswa kubo unomphela ngoba bayoziguqula babe sesimweni esingenakuhlengwa, futhi bayozihlanganisa ngokugcwele nangaphakade nabawileyo futhi bayoba ngabakaDrako unomphela futhi bayoya echibini elikhulu lomlilo unomphela. Kanjalo kuyoba yisiphetho abazikhethele sona.

Ngokuba ukuphela kwesinqumo sabo akunakuhlehliswa. Futhi izinhlupho zabo nezilonda nezifo zabo ziyoba ziningi, futhi nakuba beyothenjiswa impilo nokuthula, impilo nokuthula ngeke babe nakho. Ngoba uSathane uzama ukusebenzisa imilingo yakhe ukulwa nolaka nezahlulelo zikaNkulunkulu nokunikeza ubuthi nokubulala izixuku, kodwa ngeke babe nampumuzo futhi labo abahlanganyela emilingweni yakhe ngeke baphunyuke. Labo abathatha uphawu bayokhanya ngaphansi kwezibani zabo ezimnyama, futhi izibani zabo ziyolandelana emigwaqweni yamadolobha abo abalungiselele izigqila zabo. Qonda ukuthi laba bayoba abalahliweyo abahambayo abangenantethelelo futhi abangenakululama, ngokuba laba yibo amagama abo angabhalwanga eNcwadini Yokuphila YeWundlu, elahlatshwa kusukela ekusekelweni komhlaba. Futhi akukho nsindiso eyoke ibe khona phakathi kwabo noma emphakathini wabo, ngokuba ukuhamba phakathi kwabo nokuzihlanganisa nomphakathi wabo kuyodinga nakanjani uphawu lwesilo.

Ofakazi bami ababili bayofakaza kuze kuphele izikhathi zobufakazi babo izinyanga ezingama-42 ezinikeziwe. Futhi kuyonikezwa isilo esikhuphuka emgodini ongenamkhawulo ukuba silwe nabo, futhi siyobanqoba, sibabulale, ngisho iminqumo yami emibili, nezinti zezibani ezimbili lapho sebeqedile ubufakazi babo. Futhi izidumbu zabo ziyolala esitaladini somuzi omkhulu, ngokomoya obizwa ngokuthi iSodoma neGibhithe, lapho iNkosi yenu yabethelwa khona. Futhi ababi bayokwenza umgubho omkhulu futhi banikezane izipho ngenxa yokufa kwabo. Futhi emva kwezinsuku ezintathu nengxenye uMoya wokuphila ovela kuNkulunkulu uyongena kubo; futhi bayokuma ngezinyawo zabo, futhi ukwesaba okukhulu kuyokwehlela bonke abababona. Futhi bezwa izwi elikhulu livela ezulwini, lithi kubo, khuphukelani lapha. Futhi benyukela ezulwini ngefu, futhi izitha zabo ziyobabona benyukela emazulwini ekuphileni okuphakade futhi bayoguqulwa unomphela. Futhi ingelosi yesikhombisa iyokhala, futhi kuyoba namazwi amakhulu ezulwini, ethi, Imibuso yaleli zwe isibe yimibuso yeNkosi yethu, nekaKristu wayo, futhi iyobusa kuze kube phakade naphakade.

(Babusisiwe abafileyo abafela eNkosini, kusukela manje, yebo, kusho uMoya, ukuze baphumule emisebenzini yabo, nemisebenzi yabo iyabalandela.) Kanjalo usizi olukhulu kunawo wonke luyoqala, nakuba usizi luhlala lukhona phakathi neviki lamashumi ayisikhombisa likaDaniyeli. Futhi ithempeli labo liyophela futhi isinengiso sencithakalo siyofezwa nguMoshiach wabo wamanga. Futhi uyokuqinisa isivumelwano nabaningi isonto elilodwa; futhi phakathi nesonto uyokwenza umhlatshelo nomnikelo kuphele, futhi ngenxa yokwanda kwezinengiso, uyokwenza kube yincithakalo, kuze kube yilapho ukuphela okunqunyiwe kuthululwa phezu kwencithakalo.

Lezi ziyoba izikhathi ezimnyama kakhulu bantwana bami, ngokuba mina Yeshua HaMashiach nginitshela lezi zinto ngaphambi kokuba zenzeke, futhi zibhalwe njengesexwayiso kubo bonke. Kanjalo abayizi-144,000 bami bayofika basize labo abebengabhubha nalabo ababiza igama lami. Ngiyobahola isinyathelo ngesinyathelo, futhi bayokwenza imisebenzi eminingi enamandla kimi. Ngokuba laba yibo abathathwa ekubuthaneni kwabangcwele kimi, Kristu Jesu, emoyeni ekukhaleni kwecilongo lokugcina futhi icilongo selizokhala.

Laba ngabakholekileyo bami, ngisho nalabo okungatholakalanga kubo ubuqili, ngokuba bazihlanzile njengentombi emsulwa egazini leWundlu engalinikela unomphela njengompristi wabo omkhulu, ngokuba ngingumpristi omkhulu unomphela ngokohlelo lukaMelkisedeki. Laba ngumakoti wami, futhi bayongilandela nomaphi lapho ngiya khona. Bhekani! Ngiyobenza babe namandla, futhi akukaze kubonwe lutho emhlabeni olungaqhathaniswa namakhono engiyobembathisa wona. Bayokwembathiswa ilineni elicolekileyo, elimhlophe, elingenabala futhi amakhono abo ayobonakalisa izimpumelelo zabo futhi bayoba namakhono amakhulu ngokuba bangabakholekileyo ababiziwe nabakhethiweyo ngisho nangaphambi kokusekelwa komhlaba nguYahweh ngalezi zinhloso.

Abayi-144,000 bami bayokwehla eNtabeni iSiyoni futhi ngiyobahola. Bayothatha labo engibaholela kubo baye ehlane ukuze bafihleke ebumnyameni obukhulu nasekushushisweni kombuso wezitha. Bayoholwa bayiswe ekuphepheni kude nezinhlupho eziyisikhombisa ngokuba kulezi zahlulelo kunobunzima bolaka lukaNkulunkulu oluzothululwa esihlalweni sesilo nokuhlupha umbuso kadrako ngokuba sikhulu isijeziso sabo. Futhi abayi-144,000 bayobaholela ekuphepheni, bayobaphulukisa ezilondeni zabo, bayobasiza futhi babanike ithemba elikhulu lokubiza igama leNkosi kuze kube sekupheleni uma kudingeka. Ngokuba ukube angiyifinyezanga leyo mihla, bekungeke kusinde nyama. Kanjalo bayokuqedela lokho engiyobaholela kukho.

Futhi lapho izikhathi zigcwalisekile kuyoba njalo, njengoba kulotshiwe futhi njengoba kulotshiwe makwenziwe kanjalo: Futhi ngabona izulu livulekile, bheka ihhashi elimhlophe, futhi lowo owayehlezi phezu kwalo wayebizwa ngokuthi othembekileyo noqinisileyo, futhi ngokulunga uyahlulela futhi alwe. Amehlo akhe ayenjengelangabi lomlilo, futhi ekhanda lakhe kwakukhona imiqhele eminingi, futhi wayenegama elilotshiweyo, elingaziwa muntu ngaphandle kwakhe. Futhi wayembethe isambatho esifakwe egazini, futhi igama lakhe libizwa ngokuthi, Izwi likaNkulunkulu. Futhi amabutho ayesemazulwini amlandela ngamahhashi amhlophe, embethe ilineni elicolekileyo, elimhlophe nelihlanzekile. Futhi emlonyeni wakhe kuphuma inkemba ebukhali, ukuze ngayo ashaye izizwe; futhi uyozibusa ngentonga yensimbi; futhi unyathela isikhamo sewayini sokufutheka nolaka lukaNkulunkulu uSomandla. Futhi esambathweni sakhe, nasethangeni lakhe kunegama elilotshiweyo, INKOSI YAMAKHOSI, NOMBUSI WABABUSI.

Ngakho-ke mina, ngisho uJesu waseNazaretha, ngisho iWundlu likaNkulunkulu, ngisho uKristu Jesu naye oyiNgonyama Yesizwe SakwaJuda ngiyoza ukwahlulela nokulwa noDrako futhi wonke umbuso wakhe uyochotshozwa ngokuphelele ungasaphinde uvuke. Futhi isilo nomprofethi wamanga bayophonswa bephila echibini lomlilo futhi uSathane uyoboshwa ngeketango elikhulu emgodini ongenamkhawulo iminyaka eyinkulungwane futhi kanjalo ukubusa kwami kweminyaka eyinkulungwane kuyoqala futhi ukubusa kwami kuyoba ngokulunga. Bhekani! Ngiletha umakoti wami nebutho lamakhosi nabapristi engiyobusa nabo umhlaba ngokulunga futhi ngiyobusa izizwe ngentonga yensimbi. Ngakho-ke yibani nethemba elikhulu bantwana bami ngokuba ukuphela kwazo zonke izinto sekusondele futhi lesi sikhathi seminyaka eyisikhombisa yosizi olukhulu, ngisho nosizi lukaJakobe, siyolandelwa ukubusa kwami okulungile futhi uYahweh uyoba yikho konke kukho konke.

Futhi ngabona izulu elisha, nomhlaba omusha; ngokuba izulu lokuqala, nomhlaba wokuqala kwase kudlulile, futhi kwakungasekho lwandle. Futhi mina Johane ngabona umuzi ongcwele, iJerusalema elisha lehlela livela kuNkulunkulu ezulwini, lilungiswe njengomakoti ohlotshiselwe umyeni wakhe. Futhi ngezwa izwi elikhulu livela ezulwini, lithi, Bheka, iDokodo likaNkulunkulu likubantu, futhi uyohlala nabo, futhi bayoba ngabantu bakhe, futhi uNkulunkulu uqobo lwakhe uyoba nabo, abe nguNkulunkulu wabo.

Mina, Yeshua HaMashiach, ngithumele la Mazwi ngenceku yami, futhi ngenceku yami la Mazwi athunyelwe!
