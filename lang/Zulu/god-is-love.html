Ubufakazi Obumbaxambili: Ibandla
Emisebenzini KaPawulu Umpostoli
Epreli 30, 2024

UNkulunkulu Uluthando!

Bantabami, qondisisani isimo senu sangempela, ngokuba esenu yiso kanye isimo esiphiwe nguNkulunkulu kimi, uKristu Jesu, ngokuba uNkulunkulu uluthando! Nokho, uthando lukaNkulunkulu alufani nothando lomuntu ngendlela efanayo nezindlela zikaNkulunkulu ezingefani nezindlela zomuntu; kodwa nifunda izindlela zikaNkulunkulu kimi, ngokuba ngingumfundisi nomalusi wenu. Ngokuba uNkulunkulu walithanda izwe kangaka, waze wanikela ngeNdodana yakhe ezelwe yodwa: ukuba yilowo nalowo okholwa yiyo angabhubhi, kodwa abe nokuphila okuphakade. Futhi kulesi senzo sikaNkulunkulu sokunikela ngami, uJesu Kristu, iNdodana yakhe ezelwe yodwa ngenxa yezono zesintu, uthando lwakhe lubonakaliswa ngokupheleleyo kimi. Ngokuba ngivela kuBaba futhi nginesimo esifanayo nomoya ofanayo, ngokuba uNkulunkulu unguMoya, futhi abamkhonzayo kufanele bamkhonze ngoMoya nangeqiniso, ngokuba mina noBaba simunye emoyeni, ebunye, nasenhlosweni.

Qondisisani ukuthi uthando lukaNkulunkulu alufani nothando lomuntu, ngokuba uthando lomuntu luvame ukuba nemibandela, futhi umuntu owileyo uvame ukuveza uthando lwakhe ekunikezeni nasekwamukeleni umusa, futhi uthando lomuntu luphendukela ngaphakathi ukuze kuzuzwe umuntu othandayo, ngokuba lunjalo uthando lomuntu. Uthando lukaNkulunkulu izikhathi eziningi lubonakaliswa ekunikezeni ngokukhululekile ukuze kuzuzwe omunye, nasekukhonzeni umuntu wakini ngaphandle kwembuyiselo ngezinye izikhathi, nasekuvakasheleni abafelokazi, nasekuphulukiseni abagulayo, nasekwakheni uMzimba kaKristu. Ngakho ngiyanicela, bantabami, ukuba nifunde ngami uKristu Jesu, ngokuba lowo ongibonile mina ubone uBaba, futhi nakuba ningakangiboni ngamehlo enu, maduzane kakhulu nizongibona.

Ngokuba ubusisiwe lowo ongabonanga kodwa ekholwa, futhi ningibonile emoyeni naseqinisweni, ningibonile nasezindleleni zami nangothando engiluthumelayo nangomsebenzi izikhonzi zami eziwenzela mina ngenxa yenu. Ngakho niyangazi futhi ningibonile, futhi lapho nihamba emoyeni kaKristu ozelwe ngaphakathi, nihamba ngempela othandweni, ngokuba sinjalo isimo sami engininike sona, futhi unjalo noBaba onginike isimo sakhe: Bhekani, lunjani uthando uBaba asiphe lona, ukuba sibizwe ngokuthi singamadodana kaNkulunkulu: ngakho izwe alisazi, ngokuba alimazanga yena. Futhi futhi kusukela encwadini yokuqala kaJohane: Lolu uthando, kungengukuba thina samthanda uNkulunkulu, kodwa ukuba yena wasithanda, wathuma iNdodana yakhe ibe yinhlawulo yezono zethu. Bathandekayo, uma uNkulunkulu wasithanda kanjalo, nathi sifanele ukuthandana.

Bantwanyana bami, ningabantwana bakaNkulunkulu ophilayo, uYahweh uqobo, ngokuba ngendlela efanayo uYahweh atshala ngayo insimu ngasempumalanga e-Edeni, utshale imbewu yomoya wobudodana kini, ngokuba ngingumlimi wakhe onakekela imiphefumulo yenu. Ngokuba nidalwe kabusha kimi, futhi nidalwe othandweni, nothando lukaNkulunkulu lusesimweni senu sangempela. Qondisisani ukuthi ukuhlengwa kwenu okuphelele kusemnyango lapho nizokwazi khona njengoba naziwa. Makoti wami, kuningi kakhulu okufanele ngikuchazele kona nokufundisa kona nokwambula kona ngani nangokuthi yiziphi izinhloso eningcweliselwe ukuzifeza, ngokuba kube yimfihlakalo enhliziyweni kaBaba kusukela ngaphambi kokudalwa, futhi maduze nizokuqonda.

Pho ngubani ongahlukanisa nina nothando lukaNkulunkulu? Usizi, noma ukucindezeleka, noma ukushushiswa, noma indlala, noma ubunqunu, noma ingozi, noma inkemba na? Qha, ningabanqobi ngokwedlulele kimi, uKristu Jesu, futhi uthando lwami lufinyelela ekugcwalisekeni okuphelele kini, Makoti wami, ngokuba izikhathi sezisondele futhi maduze nizokwazi. Uthando lukaBaba alunakunqotshwa, ngokuba akanakunqotshwa, ngokuba uNkulunkulu uluthando, futhi uthando luyisimo sikaYahweh sangempela ngokuba uNkulunkulu uluthando. Enye yezinkohliso eziningi zikaSathane azisebenzisayo ukuze anidukise iwukubonisa uthando njengobuthakathaka, obungenakuzibophezela, obuzikhonzayo nobuyize: Ngokuba lezi yizindlela zomuntu wemvelo.

Ngeqiniso, uSathane uyaphika, ngokuba nguye owenziwe ize, futhi uthando lukaBaba alunakunqotshwa, ngokuba akekho onjengaye! Kimi uthando lwakhe lwapheleliswa enyameni, ngokuba kwaba ngothando lwami ngoBaba nangani, Makoti wami, lapho ngazinikela khona njengenhlawulo engenasono; ngokuba nithandwa kakhulu. Futhi yazini ukuthi niyinhlamvu yeso likaYahweh, nani, Makoti wami, niyinhlamvu yeso lami, futhi ngothando nganihlengela esandleni sesitha ngokupheleleyo, kungasweleki lutho, ngegazi lami eliyinhlawulo. Mabasho abahlengiweyo beNkosi, labo ebahlengile esandleni sesitha!

Bantabami, njengoba uNkulunkulu enithanda kangaka, khona-ke kufanele nizihloniphe ngokwamukela uthando lwakhe nokwamukela amazwi akhe ngani kanye nokukhululwa okukhulu uBaba akwenza emhlatshelweni wami njengePhasika lenu eliphelele. Abaningi benu abakuqondi ukuphelela kwendalo enginenze yona kimi, uKristu Jesu, nasemoyeni engiwudalile ngaphakathi kini, ngokuba lowo moya unamandla, futhi uluthando; ngokuba yisimo sikaBaba sangempela, ngokuba uYahweh uluthando. Bhekani, alukho uthando olukhulu kunalolu, ukuba umuntu anikele ngokuphila kwakhe ngenxa yabangane bakhe. Futhi nganikela ngokuphila kwami ngenxa yabangane bami, futhi ngokwenza kanjalo ngenze nina naba abafowethu ngenza nina naba ngamadodana kaNkulunkulu: Bhekani, uthando olunjani uBaba asinike lona, ukuba sibizwe ngokuthi singamadodana kaNkulunkulu: ngenxa yalesi sizathu leli zwe alinazi, ngokuba alimazi yena.

Zwanini aMazwi ami enganika uJohane encwadini yakhe yokuqala, futhi wezwani kahle. UYahweh unikeze umusa omkhulu kunayo yonke kini omelene nommangaleli nomelene nalabo abanilahlayo, futhi wenze izinsolo zabo zibe yize ngani, ngokuba nithengiwe ngegazi lami elingenasono, futhi nikhululiwe kuwo wonke amacala emsebenzini wami wokuhlawulela. Anifundanga yini lokho engakutshela amaRoma eminyakeni eminingi edlule? Ubani oyomangalela abakhethiweyo bakaNkulunkulu? NguNkulunkulu olungisisayo: Ngubani olahlayo? NguKristu owafa, yebo, kunalokho owavuswa kwabafileyo, ongakwesokunene sikaNkulunkulu, osincengelayo nathi. Anibuzwanga yini ubufakazi bamaHeberu, lapho izwi lawo lifakaza ngobupristi bami obungaguqukiyo? Ngalokho unamandla okusindisa ngokupheleleyo labo abeza kuNkulunkulu ngaye, njengoba ephila njalo ukubancengela.

Zwanini lawa mazwi, futhi mawazike phakathi enhliziyweni yenu, futhi zwanini izwi enganinika lona ibandla laseRoma: Ngokuba wamenza waba yisono ngenxa yethu, yena ongazanga sono, ukuze thina senziwe ukulunga kukaNkulunkulu kuye. Ingabe lawa mazwi amangalisa kakhulu kini? Nizozivumela yini ukuqonda, noma nizovala izindlebe zenu ngamasiko enu angenamsebenzi nangobufakazi bamanga obungenamsebenzi enibambelele kubo, obunikezwe ngabaprofethi bamanga abangenamsebenzi abenzelwe ukunidukisa? Ingabe kuyinto elukhuni kakhulu ukwamukela ukuthi nenziwe ukulunga kukaNkulunkulu kimi, uKristu Jesu?

Ngidinga ukuba nithule impela emqondweni nasenhliziyweni yenu futhi nivumele izwi lami lithulise umoya wenu nizwe amazwi ami. Thambisani izinhliziyo zenu nibhidlize ukuzidla kwenu; ngokuba nginokuthile engizokutshela kona okuzonikhulula ekugqilazweni uma nje ningakholwa amazwi athile engizokunika wona futhi. Ngokuba izwi lamaRoma likhuluma nani njengoba nifunda, ngakho fundani niqonde; nasekuqondeni, amukelani iqiniso ngokuma kwenu kimi, uKristu Jesu. Futhi amazwi ami yilawa: Ngakho-ke manje akusekho ukulahlwa kulabo abakuKristu Jesu, abangahambi ngokwenyama, kodwa ngokukaMoya. Ngokuba umthetho kaMoya wokuphila, kuKristu Jesu, ungikhululile emthethweni wesono nokufa. Musani ukuvumela amehlo enu adlule phezu kwamazwi, kodwa fundani ngalinye lawo kancane nangokholo, ukholo kimi, ngokuba ngikhulume lawa mazwi phezu kwenu, bantabami; futhi mina, uKristu Jesu, nginikhululile kukho konke ukulahlwa kimi. Aniqondi yini ukuthi nenziwe ukulunga kukaNkulunkulu? Ngiqondeni futhi nizwe izwi lami ninake amazwi ami: Ngubani onilahlayo? NguKristu owafa, yebo, kunalokho owavuswa kwabafileyo, ongakwesokunene sikaNkulunkulu, osincengelayo nathi.

Bantabami, anginibizelanga kimi ekulungeni nasebungcweleni beqiniso ukuze nginilahle, futhi kufanele niqonde iqiniso: Aninalo ilungelo lokuzilahla, futhi abaningi benu bayakwenza lokho, futhi izimpilo zenu zihlukunyezwa yileli zondo enilibekwe yisitha. Ngivumeleni ngigqabule la maketanga amanga esitha, nizwe amazwi ami niwakholwe, ngokuba ayiqiniso, futhi abhalelwe nina ukuze nikhululeke ngempela kimi. Ngokuba nginikhululile kukho konke ukulahlwa, futhi isitha nommangaleli wabazalwane uphonswe phansi: Manje sekufikile insindiso, namandla, nombuso kaNkulunkulu wethu, namandla kaKristu wakhe: ngokuba ummangaleli wabafowethu uphonswe phansi, lowo owabamangalela phambi kukaNkulunkulu wethu imini nobusuku.

Qondani, bantabami, ukuthi nilungile, futhi ukuzilahla kuwukunganaki amazwi ami uBaba aninike wona; ngakho musani ukungakholwa, kodwa bethembani kakhulu amazwi ami, ngokuba ayiqiniso, futhi abukho ubufakazi obuneqiniso obake banikezwa. Qondani umehluko phakathi kokuzilahla nomoya wami osebenza kini ukunisola ngesono; ngokuba ngamtshelani uJohane encwadini yakhe yokuqala: Ngokuba uma inhliziyo yethu isilahla, uNkulunkulu mkhulu kunenhliziyo yethu, futhi wazi zonke izinto. Bathandekayo, uma inhliziyo yethu ingasilahli, khona-ke sinesibindi kuNkulunkulu. Aniqondi yini ukuthi umoya wami engiwudalile ngaphakathi komunye nomunye wenu, Makoti wami, empeleni unisola ngezono zenu ngisho senizelwe kabusha kimi, uKristu Jesu, iNkosi yenu?

Anifundanga yini ubufakazi bami obulotshwe ezindabeni ezinhle zikaJohane mayelana nomduduzi? Futhi lapho efika, uzosola izwe ngesono, nangokulunga, nangokwahlulela. Ngesono, ngokuba abakholwa yimi. Ngokulunga, ngokuba ngiya kuBaba, futhi aningisaboni. Ngokwahlulela, ngokuba umbusi waleli zwe wahlulelwe. Bantabami, umduduzi wanisola ngesono, futhi niphendukele kimi, nasekuzalweni okusha nasemandleni omoya engiwudalile ngaphakathi kini, wona lowo moya wobudodana, uKristu uqobo kini ithemba lenkazimulo, nginenze ukulunga kukaNkulunkulu kimi. Ngakho-ke, anisolaswa ngesono, kodwa nisolwa ngokulunga lapho nona, ngisho nalapho niphendukela ezindleleni zenu ezindala, ngokuba inyoka iyakuthanda ukudukisa abantu bami bese iletha usongo phambi kukaBaba naphambi kwami ngemisebenzi yayo engamesabi uNkulunkulu, futhi ngingumncengeli wenu.

Ngokuba futhi, ubufakazi bukaJohane encwadini yakhe yokuqala bukhuluma ngale ndaba: Bantwanyana bami, lezi zinto nginilobela zona, ukuze ningoni. Futhi uma umuntu ona, sinommeli kuBaba, uJesu Kristu olungileyo: Futhi yena uyinhlawulo yezono zethu: futhi hhayi ngezethu kuphela, kodwa nangezono zezwe lonke. Bantabami, ngingummeli wenu, futhi nilungile kimi, futhi nilungile kuze kube phakade, ngokuba ukulunga kuzelwe esimweni senu sangempela. Ngakho yibani nenhliziyo ethambile kimi, futhi ngizonibonisa izono namaphutha enu, ngisho nemikhuba yenu emibi nezindlela zenu zangaphambili ezimbi enilingeka ukuba nibuyele kuzo ngezinye izikhathi. Ngokuba lapho inhliziyo yenu inisola, phendukelani kimi nivume izono zenu, ngokuba uthini umbhalo: Kodwa uma sihamba ekukhanyeni, njengoba yena esekukhanyeni, sinobudlelwano omunye nomunye, negazi likaJesu Kristu iNdodana yakhe liyasihlanza kuzo zonke izono. Uma sithi asinaso isono, siyazikhohlisa, neqiniso alikho kithi. Uma sivuma izono zethu, uthembekile, futhi ulungile ukusithethelela izono zethu, nokusihlanza kukho konke ukungalungi.

Bantabami, nginahlulele njengabalungileyo kimi, futhi uYahweh unikhululile kuwo wonke amacala kimi; kungani ningazivumeli ukukholwa amazwi ami anomusa nothando? Ngokuba uthando lukaNkulunkulu lubonakaliswa kuwo kanye nothando lwami ngoMakoti wami kanye nezwe lonke, ngokuba noma ngubani ofisayo angeza, kodwa wozani ngaphambi kokuba umnyango uvalwe ngokuba isikhathi sesiphelile. Buyelani kimi nsuku zonke nizihlanze ngegazi lami lokuhlawulela, ngokuba umhlatshelo wami uphelele futhi ungowesikhathi esisodwa naphakade: Ngokuba amaHeberu asho ngokusobala ngami: Yena oyinkazimulo yenkazimulo, nomfanekiso oqoshiwe womuntu wakhe, nothwala zonke izinto ngezwi lakhe elinamandla, usezihlanze yena ngokwakhe izono zethu, futhi uhlezi ngakwesokunene soBukhosi ezindaweni eziphakeme. Bantabami, qondani ukuthi umduduzi uphinde wasola izwe ngokwahlulela, ngokuba umbusi waleli zwe wahlulelwe, nesigwebo sakhe sifanelekile. Kodwa kuze kube manje ufuna ukunidonsela phansi ekulahlweni kwakhe okufanelekile, ngakho uza ukuzonilahla; ngakho musani ukulalela amazwi akhe nichithe amanga akhe.

Bantwanyana bami, hambani othandweni lukaNkulunkulu kimi, uKristu Jesu iNkosi yenu. Thandani uBaba ningithande ngokugcina imiyalo yami, ngokuba ithini iNcwadi yokuQala kaJohane? Lowo othi, ngiyamazi, kodwa engagcini imiyalo yakhe, ungumqambimanga, neqiniso alikho kuye. Kodwa noma ngubani ogcina izwi lakhe, kuye ngempela uthando lukaNkulunkulu lupheleliswe: ngalokhu siyazi ukuthi sikuye. Lowo othi uhlala kuye, naye ufanele ukuhamba kanjalo, njengoba yena wahamba. Ngiyaniyala, Makoti wami, ukuba niqhubeke nihambe njengoba ngahamba futhi nigcine imiyalo yami, ngokuba ngangenza njalo intando kaBaba, futhi nani kufanele nizame ukwenza njalo intando yami, ngokuba eyami yintando kaYahweh. Ekugcineni imiyalo yami kuzodingeka niphendukele kubazalwane, ngokuba ithini iNcwadi yokuQala kaJohane? Ngalokhu siyazi ukuthi siyabathanda abantwana bakaNkulunkulu, lapho sithanda uNkulunkulu sigcina imiyalo yakhe. Ngokuba lolu uthando lukaNkulunkulu, ukuba sigcine imiyalo yakhe, nemiyalo yakhe ayinzima. Zwanini izwi lami nilandele amazwi ami kuze kube sekupheleni kokulalela, futhi niqhubeke nithanda abazalwane benu nikhonze othandweni, hhayi ngenzuzo engcolile; ngokuba umvuzo wenu usezandleni zami, hhayi ezandleni zalabo enibakhonzayo.

Makungabikho lutho olwenziwa ngombango noma ngokuzidla; kodwa ekuthobekeni kwengqondo yilowo nalowo makathathe omunye njengomkhulu kunaye.

Ngingumfundisi nomalusi wenu.

Ngingukulunga kukaNkulunkulu, futhi nginenze naba ngukulunga kukaNkulunkulu.

Ngingulowo olungisisiwe ezindleleni zakhe.

Anginasono, futhi nginilungisisile kimi, uKristu Jesu.

Ngingulowo ongcweliswe njalo naphakade, ngokuba ungowami uMbuso kaNkulunkulu, futhi nginingcwelisile ngomoya wobudodana.

Ngingulowo owanikela ngethonsi lakhe lokugcina legazi esiphambanweni ukuze nginihlanze kuzo zonke izono, ngokuba asikho isono emoyeni wobudodana engininike wona.

Ngingumncengeli wenu.

Ngingummeli wenu.

Yimani nilindile, Makoti wami, ngokuba izikhathi zokubuyiselwa sezisondele, futhi ukubonakala kwami kusondele kunokuba nicabanga.
