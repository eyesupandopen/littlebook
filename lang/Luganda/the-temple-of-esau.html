Omujuzi Omulala: Yisirayiri
Ekiwoomerezza Ekikaawa
Novemba 23, 2024

Yeekaalu ya Esawu

Muwulire, muwulire! Ggwe Yisirayiri, eddoboozi lya Yeshua HaMashiach, ye Mwana omu yekka owa Yahweh, Nze Omunene Aliwo! Nkyo nno nsasire okubalabula ku bintu ebigenda okujja, era mutegeerezebwe nate ekitego Baraba ky'anaatega muleme kusibibwa wamu n'abatakkiriza; ebyo byawandiikibwako edda mu njiri etaggwaawo. Naye nzize okubategeeza ekitego ky'ennabbubi ky'eteeka mu nsi ey'ekisuubizo n'okukitangaaza muleme kuzikirizibwa enkwe zaabwe. Era njagala okugolola amakubo gammwe n'okubakuuma mu makubo ag'obutuukirivu, ge makubo ga Yahweh, era nga amakubo ge gatuukiridde mu Yeshua HaMashiach, Masiya wammwe ow'amazima, bulijjo agondera ddala byonna Yahweh by'ayagala. Era nate mulyoke musimbule ebigere byammwe ku kkubo eryo eggolokofu era effunda, era nja kusigaza ebigere byammwe nga binywevu bw'onoogenda mu maaso ng'onngoberera mu kukkiriza okugonda. Kubanga omutuukirivu anaabanga mulamu lwa kukkiriza!

Kale, mutegeere nti ekiseera kituuse, nga bwe kyalangirirwa era ne kitegeezebwa lwe nja okukung'aanyiza mu kaseera katono abeesigwa mu Kristo mu ggulu, oba nga balamu oba bafu; era abatuukirivu abeesigwa bwe banaaba baggyiddwawo amangu, okuzikirira okw'amangu kulyoke kutuuke ku Babulooni Ekyama atuula ku mazzi amangi, be bantu, n'ebibinja by'abantu, n'amawanga, n'ennimi. Era evvu ly'olutalo bwe linaaba ligenda okuggwaawo n'entegeka y'amawanga ey'omu kiseera kino ng'eserebye era ng'eddiddwaawo okutuukana n'ebigendererwa byabwe, enteekateeka y'ensolo ey'omulabe ey'enkomerero eneegolokokera ddala.

Naye, ensolo eva mu nnyanja n'ensolo eva mu nsi bizannye omulimu gw'abasumba abalungi abajja okusumba endiga ezaabula ez'e Babulooni Ekyama n'ebisuubizo bingi ebitagasa; kwe kugamba, ebisuubizo eby'obulimba ebireeta okufa n'okuzikirira. Era befuula abalabe emabega w'ekifaananyi ky'omusajja omukadde agendedde mu byalo gwe beekwese emabega era omusajja oyo gy'ali okubeerako omusango gw'ebikolwa byabwe ebibi; era bafuga buli ky'akola era ne bamulagirira buli kigambo ky'ayogera. Kubanga okubuzaabuzibwa kuno okw'amaanyi kuweereddwa nga musango ku Babulooni Ekyama; okubuzaabuzibwa okw'amaanyi kulyoke kumumalire ddala kubanga abisse ku nsi n'obulimba bwe ng'alina ekikopo kya zzaabu mu mukono gwe, nga kijjudde emizizo n'obugwagwa bw'obwenzi bwe.

Naye ensolo zino zombi embbi zinyaze Babulooni Ekyama, nga n'okuzikirira kwe tekunnatuuka era zitutte amaanyi ge ag'eby'olutalo n'obugagga bwe olw'ebigendererwa bya Gguluddene mu nteekateeka yaabwe ey'ensolo ey'enkomerero egenda okujja. Kubanga olutalo olugenda okujja lunaamegga Babulooni Ekyama, era ddala alizikirizibwa mu ssaawa emu, kubanga bwe kityo bwe kyalangirirwa Yahweh, ye Nze Omunene Aliwo. Naye, kino kiri ku nkwe era kitereddwa mu nteekateeka ya Yahweh ey'okununula (abalabe be balyoke bagondere eddoboozi lye) era bwe kityo omusango gw'Omwenzi omukulu gulangiriddwa, era guwandiikiddwa, era bwe gutyo bwe gunaakolebwanga emirembe gyonna, so taddengayo kugoloka. N'olwekyo, bajja kwawulamu ensi era wajja kubaawo okukankana okw'amaanyi, era enkwe z'okuzikirizibwa kwe bazitegese balyoke bakung'aanye eby'obugagga bye beegomba ebiziikiddwa mu ttaka. Kwe kugamba eby'obugagga bye beetaaga okukozesa n'okugaziya tekinologiya w'omulabe; gye basirisa ensi kati n'okugisiikuula mu kubuzaabuza kwabwe okw'amaanyi, ne bagikuuma nga nkwate ku mayengo gaabwe n'okukyukakyuka kwabwe kwe bakuma n'okufugira.

Ne ku bantu abanaasigalawo ng'okuzikirira okugenda okujja okwa Babulooni Ekyama kuwedde; ensolo eva mu nsi n'ensolo eva mu nnyanja bayingizza eggye ly'abalumbaganyi n'abattabaganyi mu nsalo zaayo ab'omuwendo gwabwe guli ng'ekibonyoobonyo eky'amaanyi eky'enzige, era bajja kusumululwa ku kabonero kaabwe! Kubanga gano ge magye g'ensolo agakung'aanyiziddwa olw'okuzikirizibwa kwa Babulooni Ekyama. Abasinga ku bano bava mu nsi za gguluddene ne mu nsi za ddubu, era bajja kukola nga Babulooni Ekyama bwe yabakolako mu kyama mu myaka egiyise: Bajja kutta buli musajja, mukazi, na mwana awatali kusaasira atatuukana na bigendererwa byabwe n'abasinga ku abo abanaakkirizibwa okusigala nga balamu bajja kukakibwa okuyingira mu nkambi zaabwe ez'abaddu okukola mu birombe byabwe okutuusa lwe banaafa. Era bajja kukola nnyo n'okufuba okutuusa ku mukka gwabwe ogw'enkomerero; kubanga byonna Babulooni Ekyama by'akoze abalala mu kyama kati bijja kumukolebwa lwatu nga musango n'ekibonerezo. Kubanga Babulooni Ekyama atamidde omusaayi gw'abatuukirivu, n'omusaayi gw'abajulirwa ba Yesu b'ajulizza obungi mu nsi endala; mu kiseera kyonna ng'ayogerayogerako nti ye yennyini aweereza Yesu Omunazaaleeti. Naye mu mazima aweereza abagwa era ebigendererwa bye kati biweddeko, era beegomba okuzikirizibwa kwe balyoke babikke ku bikolwa byabwe ebibi n'abasumba baabwe abalwadde balyoke balabike ng'abatuukirivu bwe banaagoloka balyoke bakweke ekitego kyabwe.

Naye tebasobola kwekwasa Yahweh, newaakubadde Yeshua HaMashiach era ebikolwa byabwe ebibi biwandiikiddwa mu bugumu mu ggulu era buli kibi kiwandiikiddwa mu bujjuvu awamu n'ebirowoozo n'ebigendererwa by'emitima gyabwe egifudde. Era ensolo eva mu nnyanja n'ensolo eva mu nsi mizizo mibi egyakolebwa olw'okuzikirizibwa kw'omuntu. Ensolo zino embbi zikabasanya abaana abato, era zikung'aanya ne zikozesa emirambo gy'abaana abato be zisse okukola emizizo gyazo era bwe kityo byonna bye zizikiriza biweebwayo nga ssaddaaka eri katonda waazo. Kubanga baatwala Eweema ya Moloki, n'emmunyeenye ya katonda waabwe Remufani, ebifaananyi bye baakola, okubisinza, era bingi nnyo bye baakola ebijja okubikkulwa mu bujjuvu mu biseera eby'okubikkulirako ebituufu kubanga buli kyama kijja kujja mu musana.

Bwe kityo bwe kinaaba nti ensolo eva mu nsi erirabika ng'egolokoka okuva mu ntebe; nga yeeyita nti azuukidde mu bafu, ye mutima gwennyini ogw'ensi kubanga okufa tekwasobodde kumukwata! Era ajja kubeera n'okuzuukira kwe okw'obulimba era n'aleeta emirembe egy'obulimba ku nsi okumalawo olutalo lwabwe, era ajja kulabika ng'ajja kulokola abantu. Era abagwa bajja kwebikkulira abantu ng'abayambi abalungi abasinga ennyo mu magezi era abakulu mu makubo ga tekinologiya, eddagala, n'emirembe.

Bajja kuleeta obubonero n'ebyamagero bingi eby'obulimba, era bajja kugamba nti abo abakungaanyiziddwa mu Kristo ku lukuŋŋaana olunene ne batwalibwa mu kifo eky'obukuumi eky'omu ggulu nga tewannabaawo kutuukawo kw'akabi akanene; nti n'abo baatwalibwa ku mpaka omulabe ow'ebweru w'ensi omulowooze. Kwe kugamba, amaanyi g'aggaliraya ag'ekirabe agajja okudda nate okuzikiriza abantu, era bajja kukungaanya omuntu ow'obutonde okwegatta nabo alyoke yeerokole okuva mu bubi buno bwe balowooza mu bwengula bwabwe obw'obulimba obw'ensi ez'obulimba ezitakyukakyuka mu bbanga eritagambika erigaziwa bulijjo.

Kwe kugamba okusoma okw'obulimba okw'obwengula abagwa kwe baalowooza ne bakozesa edda mu mulembe gwabwe ogwa zzaabu ogwa kera, omulembe ogwa zzaabu ogwatuuka ku nkomerero yaagwo ku mikono gya Yahweh mu mataba amanene mu biseera bya Nuuwa, ebikwata ku ebyo byawandiikibwa mu biwandiiko ebitukuvu eby'edda. Era obulimba obwo bujja kusuulibwa wansi kubanga okubuzaabuza kwabwe okw'amaanyi kujja kwalibwa mu bujjuvu bonna balyoke balabe abeefuusa bano abeefuusa be baani ddala kubanga bitonde ebyagwa okuva mu bbanga ery'omu ggulu abazze beeyonoona mu bujeemu obutagasa okuva mu butuukirivu bwa Yahweh n'okutuusa kati balyoke basikire okuzikirira kwabwe kwe beekoledde mu busika bwabwe obw'okwokya sulfuriki mwe batayinza kuva.

Era bwe kityo ensolo eva mu nsi ejja kugoloka ng'eva mu bafu era n'obubonero n'ebyamagero bingi eby'obulimba by'ejja okulabika, nga n'ettabi lye ery'omuzeyituuni liri mu mukono gwe. Era ajja kugoloka ng'omwana gw'endiga alina amayembe abiri bw'agoloka mu maanyi n'obuyinza obujjuvu obwa gguluddene n'ensolo eva mu nnyanja, ye eyalondebwa gguluddene n'ensolo eva mu nsi olw'okufuga amawanga alyoke afuge ennyanja y'amawanga okusinziira ku mateeka gaabwe aga Noowa agavvoola era agatta abantu. Naye amawanga ge abiri tegajja kuba ntegeka kubanga nze nnina okugatta Yisirayiri emirembe gyonna aleme kuddamu kugabanyizibwamu bwakabaka bubiri.

Era bwe kityo bajja kukozesa amateeka gaabwe amabi n'ebivaamu ebiri mu Noowa okulwanyisa abatuukirivu n'okubawangula, okuggyako buli anaakoowoola erinnya lya Mukama okutuusa ku nkomerero alirokolebwa, be abo abakoowoola Yesu Omunazaaleeti nga Mukama waabwe omulokozi ajja okubalokola mu bujjuvu bwe batanafa mu nnaku zaabwe. Era bwe kityo ensolo eva mu nnyanja ejja kusumululwa ku ebyo ebisigaddewo ku mawanga ge bajja okuba nga bazikirizza, era ku vvu lyaago kwaliko amawanga ge baafugako edda mu bukuusa obw'amaanyi. Era bajja kulabika ng'abagolokoka ng'akanyonyi akanene akava mu vvu n'ennimi z'omuliro ezikyanyooka okuva mu bukuusa bwabwe. Era lujja kuba lutalo lunene, lwe lusinga obunene ku ntalo essatu ezo ze baasasamaza era ne bazirwanirira; gguluddene alyoke agoloke mu bujjuvu mu kiseera kye n'ensolo ze embbi enkyamu z'ajja okubeera nazo n'okufuga kwe okw'akaseera obuseera, aleme kuddamu kufuga.

Kubanga ekyama ky'obujeemu kibadde kikola mu myaka gyonna; omwenzi omukulu atuula ku nsozi musanvu, ye mwenzi omukambwe omukulu ow'okuvvoola asinza nnabagereka w'eggulu era ayigiriza Yesu omulala ng'ekitundu ky'obusatu bwabwe obw'abakaafiiri. Ye emirandira gye gisibuka mu Babulooni eya kera Nimuloodi gye yatandikira enganikyo ze ne nnabagereka w'eggulu. Ab'obusaserdooti n'ab'olulyo lwe balondoola olulyo lwabwe okutuuka ku bagwa abaafuga Babulooni eya kera n'abasatu baabwe abatali batukuvu be bakozesa okuvoola obwakatonda obw'amazima, n'ezzadde erisuubiziddwa ery'omukazi. Enkola n'emikolo bino eby'abakaafiiri bifuze ensi mu kyama eky'amaanyi abasaserdooti abaatandikibwawo ab'abagwa gye basinza enjuba mu kyama naye ne beeyita nti be Kibiina kya Yesu; era bwe kityo ekkanisa yaabwe ey'obulimba era evvoola eteesezza okujooga n'okuyigganya ku nsi mu nnaku ekyama ky'obujeemu lwe kyasaasaana mu kyama. N'olwekyo, okuzikirizibwa kwabwe tekulwaawo n'ebiseera byabwe bituuse!

Naye ensolo zino zombi embbi, ezikolera awamu kati olw'okuzikirizibwa okujjuvu okwa Babulooni ekyama, ensolo eva mu nnyanja n'ensolo eva mu nsi; zino zombi ezibadde emitwe egifuga Babulooni Ekyama era ezifugye n'ebisuubizo bingi ebinene ebitagasa abasumba bano ababiri abalwadde bye basumbye endiga ezaabula ez'e Babulooni Ekyama nga basuubiza obugagga obw'ebintu n'obungi obw'obulimba abo be basumba. Era mu mikono gyabwe n'oku nkwe zaabwe ddala bategeese era bajja kutuukiriza ebyo ebiweereddwa mu mikono gya gguluddene okutuukiriza.

Kubanga Babulooni Ekyama, ye mwenzi omukulu atuula ku mazzi amangi, lye ggwanga eryakolebwa ensolo zino zombi embbi zirongooke okuva mu vvu lyayo mu biseera byabyo eby'okubikkulwa. Kwe kugamba, eggwanga eryenzi, eggwanga ery'obwenzi, eggwanga eriri ddala omwenzi omukulu eyeeyita omugoberezi omutuukirivu owa Yesu Omunazaaleeti. Naye mu mazima lye ggwanga abazimbi baalyo abatalina nkolagana na Lwazi lw'Omusingi olw'amazima, ye jjinja eryo eddene ery'oku nsonda abatakkiriza kwe beesittala, ye Yesu Omunazaaleeti era ye Yeshua HaMashiach. Era bwe kityo abazimbi bano ababi beefudde abatalina bulokozi era beewaddeyo mu nnyanja ey'omuliro awali envunyu yaabwe etafa olw'okugaana ejjinja eddene ery'oku nsonda n'olwazi olw'okwesittalako so tebajja kuwona!

Naye abagwa bulijjo babadde n'ebigendererwa bye bimu mu birowoozo era n'entegeka zaabwe n'ebigendererwa byabwe bisigala bye bimu. Kubanga balina ekigendererwa eky'okuzikiriza ddala Adamu okuva ku nsi, era balina ekigendererwa eky'okuzikiriza ddala Yakobo okuva wansi w'enjuba n'okutuusa kati balyoke bagende mu maaso ng'abasenze n'abanyazi abatali batuukirivu ab'ensi balyoke babeere n'omulembe gwabwe ogwa zzaabu gwe beegomba. Naye tegeera nti omulembe gwabwe ogwa zzaabu gwali kiseera Adamu lwe yawambibwa era n'afugibwa obwakabaka obw'ensi yonna obw'emizizo emibi egyakolebwa olw'okuzikirizibwa okujjuvu okw'abantu, era emizizo gyabwe gijja kudda! Naye tewajja kubaawo mulembe gwa zzaabu gwonna gwe bajja kubeera nagwo wabula essuubi erisaasaanye n'omusango omukakali ng'obwakabaka bw'abagwa buzikiriziddwa ddala mu maaso gaabwe n'omukono gwange ogw'amaanyi!

N'omulembe gwabwe gwe bayita ogwa zzaabu gwali kiseera olunyiriri olutuukirivu olwa Seeti lwe lweekweka mu ddungu okuva mu bantu baabwe ababi mu nsi eyaliwo mu kiseera ekyo. Ekyo kireme kuggwaawo olulyo lwa Seeti lusigale nga lulongoofu okuva mu kwonooneka kw'ebika n'ezzadde erisuubiziddwa ery'omukazi (nze), lisobole okujja nga bwe lyasuubizibwa edda Yahweh yennyini. Laba! Yahweh alangiridde nti Ajja kuleeta Abagambamamba okutuukiriza obusungu bwe, era bwe kityo bwe kiriba. Naye bwe kityo obwakabaka bwabwe obubi bujja kugoloka ne bufuga ensi yonna n'ebibi bingi bye bajja okuleeta, era emizizo mingi gijja kusumululwa okuva emitala w'omuzira n'okuva mu mutima gw'ensi era obunnya bujja kuggulwawo.

Wajja kubaawo obubi obungi ku nsi era newaakubadde nga bajja kugoloka okuleeta emirembe egy'obulimba; olutalo lujja kufuga bulijjo okufuga kwabwe kubanga emirembe gigenda kuggyibwa ku nsi mu musango ku kuggyibwawo kw'omuziyiza wange. Era emirembe tebajja kugifuna kubanga Yahweh yalangirira dda nti abagwa tebalina mirembe kubanga baayonoona abantu mu kwegomba kwabwe okubi n'obukyayi bw'ebintu byonna ebyakolebwa Yahweh. Era bajja kuteekawo emirembe egy'obulimba ku nsi era ensolo eva mu nsi ejja kujja mu maanyi ge yonna, era ejja kufuga ng'omwana gw'endiga alina amayembe abiri ayogera ng'oguluddene.

Kubanga omwana gw'endiga owa Esawu, era nga ye nsolo evudde mu nsi era ye Baraba, alondeddwa okuba omuzimbi wa yeekaalu yaabwe ey'ekisolo era aweereddwayo ng'omwana gw'endiga ow'ogusota eri Masiya waabwe omulimba n'omukama waabwe ow'obulimba. Mazima ddala, ajja kugatta Esawu ne Asikenazi n'abo bonna ababeegattako wansi w'ebbendera y'eddiini bakitaabwe gye baali tebamanyi era enkola zaabwe ez'omu Talumudi zijja kufuga ensi. Bajja kukkakkanya amawanga n'obukambwe wansi wa Noowaidi era bajja kufuga amawanga n'obukambwe obungi; era ensolo evudde mu nsi ejja kuleetera bonna, abato n'abakulu, abagagga n'abaavu, ab'eddembe n'abaddu okuteekebwako akabonero ku mukono gwabwe ogwa ddyo oba mu byenyi byabwe, omuntu yenna aleme okugula oba okutunda, okuggyako oyo alina akabonero, oba erinnya ly'ensolo, oba omuwendo gw'erinnya lyayo. Kubanga aleeta okulumwa kw'omusota okutali muntu n'omu gw'ayinza kuwona era bonna abakwetabamu bajja kuba beekyusizza emirembe gyonna, nga ku kukyuka okwo tebaliba na kisonyiwo, era bajja kutabula ekyuma n'ebbumba, naye tebijja kwegattanga. Ekyakyamye tekiyinza kugololwa era bonna abeekyamya mu ngeri eyo bajja kufuna omugabo gwabwe mu nnyanja eri ey'omuliro ogwaka n'ekibiriiti ogutazikira, omusiriŋŋanyi gwabwe mwe gatafiira.

N'olwekyo, ensolo evudde mu nsi, era nga ye nnabbi waabwe omulimba ne Masiya waabwe omulimba, ejja kulangirira ebyayo era ejja kuzimba yeekaalu yayo kubanga yatumibwa ng'omuzimbi wa yeekaalu ow'ekisolo, eyezimbiiridde ebiminaala bingi mu bwakabaka bwe ebyatuumibwa erinnya lye, era ajja kwogerangako yekka ng'eyeegulumiza, era nga yeerangirira nti ye Katonda. Kubanga ajja kusuubiza ebintu bingi ebinene by'atalina buyinza kutuukiriza, era ajja kwogera ebintu bingi ebinene era eby'okuvvoola, era bw'atyo bw'ajja okukola kubanga ky'ekyo kye yaweebwa okutuukiriza. Kubanga muzimbi era mukulaakulanya eyezimbiiridde ebiminaala n'ebifo eby'okuwummuliramu bingi, era mazima ddala atuula waggulu ku bwakabaka bwe bw'eyeeweereredde yekka mu kitiibwa kye; kubanga bulijjo yeetendereza kubanga ezo ze nkola z'ogusota, Kitaawe ow'amazima.

Nga yeekaalu ye ezimbibwa n'eggwa mu mbaga nnene n'ebisuubizo bingi eby'obuvvoola n'obulimba, bw'atyo bw'ajja okulangirira eby'obulimba bingi mu offiisi ye ey'obunnabbi obulimba era okufuga kwe kujja kuba kwa bukambwe n'obukambwe. Naye, ajja kuteega omutego gwe naye omutambyi ki atega omutego ng'ekinyonyi kiraba? Mu bukuusa obungi atega omutego gwe era n'ebisuubizo ebinene ajja kusuubiza okuwaayo yeekaalu ye eri abantu b'omu nsi era n'essanyu lingi Esawu ne Asikenazi bajja kwekulisa okwetooloola omwana gw'endiga owa Esawu, era bajja kwefuula ng'abazzaawo enkola za Tora, naye nga baleeta nkola za kufa ezikodeddwa mu kitabo kyabwe eky'okufa eky'obuvvoola, ye Talumudi yaabwe. Laba! Ensolo evudde mu nsi mutemu era mukulembeze w'okujeemera Yakuwa mu nkola za Baraba. Ajja kulyamu olukwe bonna b'asuubiza ebisuubizo bye ebinene era okulyamu olukwe kwe tekujja kuba na kwejjusa era n'okukyawa okungi kwe yasikira okuva eri Kitaawe, sitaani, okwegomba kwe kusibiddwa mu butali butuukirivu okutuukiriza.

Naye obwo bwe butonde bw'ennyenje eyaluka ensi yonna mu lutimbe lwayo. Ennyenje etuula n'erinda omuyiggo gwayo okugwa mu lutimbe lwayo n'eryoka eddukira okugukwata, okugulemaza n'okuguzinga mu kawuuzi kalyo esobole okulya obulamu bw'abasibe bayo mpola; nga bwe bamirwa mpola mpola obubi bwonna bwe baateegerwamu. Naye ebibiina by'abantu bijja kuva wala na kumpi nga bawulira okuyita kwe; n'okuyitibwa kwe okw'okuwaayo n'okutukuza yeekaalu ye kujja kuwulirwa mu nsi yonna era abangi abagaana Masiya waabwe ow'amazima bajja kukwatirwa mu mutego gwe ne bazikirira omwo. Naye tegujja kulabika ng'omutego eri abo b'alogojjedde, wabula okuzuukira kwa Musa okukulemberwa Sanhedrin yaabwe era nga kufugibwa ennono n'amagezi ga Esawu ebiri mu kitabo kyabwe eky'obuvvoola ekitatukuvu. N'ekibinja kya bakabona abalimba abatali ba Leevi kubanga Leevi asangibwa mu bujjuvu mu Kabona Asinga Obukulu owa Melkizeddeeki, kubanga nze, Yesuwa HaMasiya, ndi Kabona wammwe Asinga Obukulu emirembe gyonna ng'entegeka ya Melkizeddeeki bw'eri, kubanga bw'atyo Yakuwa bwe yalangirira.

Naye nga bwe nnayigiriza amazima mu kiseera kyange bwe nnajja eri abange ng'Omwana gw'Endiga gwa Katonda; bwe nnagaanibwa ab'omu nsi yange, naye nnayigiriza amazima era bwe gatyo, kyenvudde, ggwe Isirayiri tolina kya kwewolereza. Wadde, mu kwagala kwange eri ggwe n'okusinziira ku bisuubizo n'endagaano ez'edda, nzize okukubuulira omulundi omulala era Yakobo aleme okugwa eri Esawu.

Kubanga kyawandiikibwa mu biwandiiko ebitukuvu nti: Bwe muliraba eky'omuzizo eky'okuzikiriza, ekyayogerwa nnabbi Danyeri, nga kiyimiridde mu kifo ekitukuvu (asoma ategeere). Kale abo abali mu Buyudaaya baddukire ku nsozi. Ali waggulu ku nnyumba, takka wansi, okuggyamu kintu kyonna mu nnyumba ye: N'oyo ali mu nnimiro, tadduka kudda nate okutwala engoye ze. Era ziribasanga abali embuto, n'abonsa mu nnaku ezo. Naye musabe okudduka kwammwe kuleme okuba mu kiseera ky'obutiti, newakubadde ku ssabbiiti: Kubanga mu kiseera ekyo kuliba okubonaabona okungi, okutali kwabangawo okuva ensi lwe yatondebwa okutuusa ku kiseera kino, nedda, so tekulibaawo nate. Era singa ennaku ezo tezaali zikendeezeddwa, tewandibaddewo muntu n'omu awona: naye olw'abalonde, ennaku ezo zijja kukendeezebwa.

Nga bwe kyawandiikibwa, bwe kityo bwe kirikolebwa era Baraba ajja kuteega omutego gwe gw'ajja okuba ng'akwese mu magezi. Kubanga abagwira banoonya okuzikiriza olulyo lwa Adamu lwonna okuva ku nsi. Kyebaliva balwanyisa abatukuvu ne babawangula, era Esawu awamu ne Asikenazi banoonya okuteega Yakobo, Yakobo asobole okugwa ku kiwundu ky'oku mutwe kye banoonya okumaliriza Yakobo. Tegeera nti singa si kisa na kusaasira bya Yakuwa eyewaayo mu kwagala kwe okutaggwaawo eri Isirayiri mu bisuubizo bingi n'endagaano ez'edda okuwonya n'okununula Yakobo mu mikono gy'abalabe be; mwenna mwandizikiridde mu mikono gy'abasatu abatali batukuvu! Naye ate, Yakuwa mwesigwa emirembe gyonna eri buli kisuubizo ky'eyali akoze! Era nze, Yesuwa HaMasiya, ye oyo eyagula obulokozi bwammwe n'okubonaabona kwange nzekka, ndi mwesigwa bulijjo n'emirembe gyonna okutuukiriza buli kisuubizo kya buli ndagaano eyakolebwako, era mazima ddala buli kisuubizo ekirikoleddwa emirembe gyonna era n'olwekyo tewali kisuubizo n'ekimu ekyakolebwako Yakuwa oba Yesuwa HaMasiya ekirigwa wansi nga tekituukiridde naye byonna bijja kutuukirizibwa mu bujjuvu, emirembe gyonna.

Kyendiva njija okuyamba Isirayiri, beebo ab'oku Yakobo abaligondera okufuga kwange nga Masiya waabwe mu nteekateeka ya Yakuwa ey'emirembe gyonna ey'obulokozi Yakuwa gy'awadde olw'obulokozi bwa Yakobo; kubanga mazima ddala, singa ennaku tezaali zikendeezeddwa tewandibaddewo muntu n'omu awona. Kubanga abagwira bwe batyo bwe bagenderera, nti bandizikirizza abatukuvu, ate ne bazikiriza Yakobo, ate okuva awo ne bazikiriza bonna abasigaddewo abazaalibwa mu lulyo lwa Adamu; ensi n'eryoka eba yaabwe mu bujjuvu mu nteekateeka zaabwe ez'obulimba ezitatuukirira.

Ate olwo okubonaabona okusinga obunene okutabangako kulizingiramu ensi n'omusaayi gujja kukulukuta ku nsi mu bungi. Era Baraba ajja kutta bangi ebweru w'ekibuga ky'ajja okuba ng'azingizza amagye ge baleme okudduka; era bwe kityo bwe kyawandiikibwa nti: Balina omukisa abafu abafiira mu Mukama, okuva kaakano, weewaawo, Omwoyo bw'ayogera nti bawummule okuva mu mirimu gyabwe, n'ebikolwa byabwe bibagoberera.

Kale, okubonaabona okusinga obunene okutabangako kujja kubaawo, n'okubonaabona okutali kwati kwali kulabikangako ku nsi, era mazima ddala, tekulibaawo nate. Laba! Nze, ye Yesuwa HaMasiya, sijja kuleka Yakobo okuzikirizibwa amangu ago, era nja kulokola Isirayiri, ye ns remnant eyo ey'amaanyi erinkkiriza nga Masiya waabwe ow'amazima, kubanga bw'atyo Yakuwa bwe yantonda okuba. Era n'olwekyo abange 144,000 bajja kuyamba abo abandyazikiridde era abakoowoola erinnya lyange. Nja kubakulembera ntuuko ku ntuuko, n'abange 144,000 bajja kunkolera ebikolwa bingi eby'amaanyi, n'ebikolwa ebisinga obukulu ku bye nnakola mu kiseera kyange mu mubiri ogutaliiko kibi ng'omwana gw'endiga gwa Katonda.

Kubanga bano beebo abakung'aanyizibwa mu kukung'aanya kw'abatukuvu gye ndi, Kristo Yesu, mu bbanga ku ddoboozi ly'ekkondeere erisembayo, n'ekkondeere liri kumpi kuvuga. Bano be beesigwa bange, beebo abataliimu bukuusa, kubanga beetukuzizza ng'omuwala atamanyi musajja mu musaayi gw'Omwana gw'Endiga; gwe nnawaayo emirembe gyonna nga kabona waabwe omukulu, kubanga nze kabona omukulu emirembe gyonna ng'entegeka ya Melkizeddeeki bw'eri. Bano be Mugole wange, era bajja kungoberera wonna w'endaga. Laba! Nja kubafuula ba maanyi, era tewali kintu kyonna ekyali kirabiddwako ku nsi ekiyinza kugeraageranyizibwa ku busobozi bwe nja okubambaza. Bajja kwambazibwa bafuta, enjeru, etaliiko bbala, n'obusobozi bwabwe bujja kwolesa ebyo bye batuuseeko, n'obusobozi obungi bwe bajja okuba nabwo; kubanga beesigwa, bayite, era balonde, okuva ne mu kutondebwa kw'ensi Yakuwa lwe yabategekera ebyo.

Abange 144,000 bajja kukka ku Lusozi Sayuuni, era nze nja kubakulembera. Bajja kutwala abo be mbakulemberera mu ddungu okukwekebwa okuva mu kizikiza ekikutte n'okuyigganyizibwa kw'obwakabaka bw'abalabe. Bajja kukulemberwa mu kifo eky'obulokozi okuva mu busungu bwonna obw'ekiruyi kya Katonda obuli mu bibya musanvu; kubanga mu bibya bino mwe muli obukambwe bw'ekiruyi kya Katonda ekigenda okuyiibwa ku ntebe y'ensolo n'okubonyaabonya obwakabaka bw'Ogusota, kubanga ekibonerezo kyabwe kinene. N'abantu 144,000 bajja kukulembera Yakobo mu bulokozi; bajja kubawonya ebiwundu byabwe; bajja kubayamba n'okubawa essuubi eddene okukoowoola erinnya lya Mukama, okutuusa ne ku nkomerero bwe kinaaba kyetaagisizza. Kubanga singa saakendeeza nnaku, tewandibaddewo muntu awona. Bwe batyo bajja kumalako ekyo mwe ndibakulemberera. Era Yakobo ajja kukwekebwa mu bulokozi okuva ku lunaku lw'oluyo olwo olunene, ye luyo olunene olw'e Alumagedoni olw'okuzikirizibwa kwabwe okutayinza kubawonya.

N'olwekyo, ggwe ow'oluganda lwange, tegeera ebiseera by'olimu n'obulimba bw'oyolekedde obulizingiramu ensi yonna; obulimba obulimira amalamu mu musango abo abakyawa Yakuwa n'okumugaana, nga kiva mu kubonerezebwa okunene olw'enkola zaabwe ezitali za bukkiriza.

Nze Mwana gw'Endiga gwa Katonda eyaweebwayo okuggyawo ebibi by'ensi mu butuukirivu.

Nze Mpologoma ey'omu kika kya Yuda era mu butuukirivu njija okusala omusango n'okulwana olutalo.

Nze oyo ajja okuwonya ensigalira ey'amaanyi okuva mu Yakobo erisigala nga nnamu okuyingira mu bwakabaka bwange obw'emyaka olukumi, Obwakabaka Kabaka wammwe ow'bakabaka ne Mukama w'abakama bw'ajja okuteekawo.

Nze Yesuwa HaMasiya, omuzzukulu omusuubize ow'omukazi eyakolebwa wansi w'amateeka n'aweebwayo ng'ekiweebwayo olw'ekibonerezo kya Musa n'olw'okusoniyibwa ebibi n'okusoniyibwa okujjuvu n'okununulibwa kw'abo bonna abajja gye ndi nga beenenyezza mu bujjuvu.

Nze oyo eyatumye ebigambo bino ng'abiyisa mu mubaka wange n'omuweereza wange afunye Enjiri Ey'emirembe n'emirembe, ye Katabo Akatono ako akaategeezebwako Yokaana Omubikkulirwa eyagambibwa nti siba ebyo ebyayogeddwa okubwatuka omusanvu, so tobiwandiika, era omwo mwe muli obulokozi bwa Yakobo obubikkuliddwa!

Mazima ddala, nze Yesuwa HaMasiya ntegeeza nti Ebigambo by'Enjiri Ey'emirembe n'emirembe biweereddwa era bibikkuliddwa omukono gwange nzekka, n'omukono gwa Yakuwa yennyini, ye Nze Nzuuno Omukulu! N'Ebigambo bino eby'Enjiri Ey'emirembe n'emirembe ebiweereddwa bya kwesigama era bya mazima era bibadde bikwekeddwa mu mutima gwa Yakuwa yennyini okutuusa ekiseera ekituufu eky'okubikkulibwa kwabyo; era biwandiikiddwa mu butuufu nga bwe byaweebwa omubaka wange ow'amaanyi eyalongoosebwa mu kubonerezebwa kungi ne mu musaayi gwange ogununula, era alangiridde Ebigambo bino nga bwe byaweebwa, mu bwesigwa, olw'okugondera Ebigambo bino byonna kwe bigendereddwamu. Ebigambo by'Enjiri Ey'emirembe n'emirembe bijja kukuumibwa mu mataba n'entalo ebigenda okujja era bijja kutuuka ku maaso, amatu, n'emitima gy'abo be bitumiddwa. Seera!

Kyova ogondera Ebigambo bya Masiya wo ow'amazima, ye Yesuwa HaMasiya, ggwe Isirayiri, oddire eri Yakuwa Nze Nzuuno Omukulu ng'onkkiriza nga Masiya wo ow'amazima kubanga, mazima ddala tolina kiseera kisigaddewo kya kuwakana na mutonzi wo newakubadde Masiya wo eyaweebwa mu kwagala olw'obulokozi bwo era mazima ddala obulokozi bw'abo bonna okuva ku Adamu abakkiriza ekiweebwa kyange eky'obulamu obutaggwaawo.

Laba! Ebiseera eby'okutuukirizibwa by'ebintu byonna bituuse n'ebiseera byange eby'ekisa n'okusaasira biweddewo era mu kifo kyabyo obusungu n'omusango kaakano bijja kuyimirirawo. Abo bonna ab'oku Isirayiri abakkiriza okubonerezebwa n'okugondera eddoboozi lyange, abo bennyini be balokolebwa nga balamu okuyingira mu bwakabaka bwange obw'emyaka olukumi; mwe bajja okuyimirira ng'abantu ab'enjawulo eri Yakuwa era ekigendererwa kya Isirayiri eky'amazima ng'eky'obugagga eky'enjawulo gy'ali okusinga abantu bonna era kiribaawo nga bwe kyasuubizibwa edda era ndireeta ekibinja ky'Abakabaka n'Abakabona abalifuga ku kiragiro kyange okuva ku ntebe ya Dawudi n'okuva mu kibuga ekyo ekitukuvu ekinene ensi ejja kufugibwa Yesuwa HaMasiya mu butuukirivu.
