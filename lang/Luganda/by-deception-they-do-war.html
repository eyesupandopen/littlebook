Mujulizi Omubiri: Isirayiri
Ekiwoomerera Ekikaawa
Ssebutemba 02, 2024

Mu Lukwe Bakola Entalo!

Laba! Ani omutunzi w’ennyonyi atega omutego gwe ng’ekinyonyi kiraba? Naye mazima, ddala, omutego gutegekeddwa eri abantu b’ensi ensuubize, era olutalo luli ku nsi ng’obusungu n’omusango bitegekeddwa okugwa. Mwekuŋŋaanyize eri eddoboozi lya Yeshua HaMashiach muwulire ebigambo byange. Laba! Mbategeeza eby’okujja nga tebinnabaawo, ebigambo byange biryoke bibeere okwewanisa eri bonna be bitumiddwa. Kubanga eyiyo y’engeri y’ebigambo by’obulamu bye njogera, kubanga nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, ndi Kigambo kya Katonda ekyefuula omubiri, era ntuula munda mu mmwe, kubanga mmwe yeekaalu ya Katonda omulamu, era nze kabona asinga obukulu emirembe n’emirembe ng’engeri ya Melkizeddeeki bweri.

Mazima, ddala, ebigambo byange bijja kutuuka ku maaso, amatu, n’emitima gy’abo be bitumiddwa, era bijja kukuumibwa okuyita mu ntalo n’ebizibu ebigenda okujja, okuggyako nga wajja kubaawo enjala y’ebyawandiikibwa mu nsi yonna kubanga ensi bwe yafukirirwa nnyo ebigambo bya Yahweh, ye Katonda omunene ow’emirembe n’emirembe, ensi yakkiriza ebigambo ebyo okumala akaseera. Naye kaakano ensi egaanyi ebigambo bya Yahweh n’esinga okulonda obulimba bw’Oguwoomu, n’olwekyo kaakano Ekigambo kya Katonda kijja kuba kikalu, era wajja kubaawo enjala y’Ekigambo kya Katonda, kubanga egyo gye misango egijja. Naye, buli mmeeme ennamu ekoowoola erinnya lya Mukama okutuusa ku nkomerero erirokolebwa, ne abo abataagala bulamu bwabwe okutuusa ku nkomerero.

Naye muwulire mutegeere ebigambo byange, mumanye era mutegeere nti ebigambo byange bijja kukuumibwa, era bijja kubikkulirwa ensi, n’ebiseera eby’okubikkulirwa bituuse. N’olwekyo nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, ntuma enjiri ey’emirembe n’emirembe ey’ebiseera eby’okubonaabona okungi, kwe kubonaabona kwa Yakobo, ekibiina eky’ebibiina kiryoke kirokoke ng’ekiyise mu muliro. Era n’olwekyo enjiri ey’emirembe n’emirembe etuuse okubikkulibwa mu kiseera kyayo, n’obujulizi bukyagenda kuwandiikibwa, n’ebigambo bya Yahweh bijja kukulukuta ng’emigga gy’amazzi amalamu, era biwandiikiddwa olw’okugondera kw’abo bonna abalina amatu okuwulira n’amaaso okulaba n’omutima okuyiga. Era ebigambo bya Yahweh bigambo bya lubeerera omuntu yenna by’atasobola kubula, kwonoona, wadde okusirisa, kubanga omuntu bw’akola bw’atyo yeerabira, naye ebigambo bya Katonda ow’emirembe n’emirembe binywevu emirembe gyonna.

Laba! Ebiseera eby’okubonaabona kwa Yakobo bituuse, n’ebiseera bituuse eby’okubikkulirwa kw’ebintu byonna, kubanga Yahweh alonze ekiseera kino okweyanjulira ekitonde kye, ne mu maaso g’omwana we omu yekka, Yesu Kristo. Kubanga nze ndi Yesu Kristo, ye Mukama ow’abakama eyalinnyisibwa era Kabaka w’abakabaka, era mu mikono gyange ekyapa ky’ensi mwe kiweereddwa kubanga nakigula mu musaayi gwange omutali kibi, n’empeera yange eri nange. Era nja kugabira abeesigwa omugabo gwange, n’oyo awangula alifuna byonna okuva mu mukono gwange ng’empeera olw’obwesigwa bwe, balyoke balage emirembe n’emirembe ekitiibwa kya Yahweh eky’olubeerera. Era Yahweh alibawa ekitiibwa, n’abo abeetoowazizza mu bwetoowaze, mu kugondera, ne mu kwenenya baligulumizibwa mu kitiibwa eky’olubeerera era balimasamasa ng’emmunyeenye ez’oku ggulu, era n’olwekyo ekitiibwa kyabwe kirimulisa emirembe n’emirembe. Kale mbakubiriza, baagalwa bange, okuwulira eddoboozi lyange n’okukkakkanya emitima gyammwe mu kugondera.

Mumpulire, era mumpulire bulungi; olutalo lutuuse, ne Babulooni Ekyama lusibiddwa okuzikirizibwa; n’omusango gwakyo n’ekibonerezo kyakyo tebirwawo wabula biri kumpi. Era bwe nkwakula Ekkanisa yange, Omubiri gwange, era n’Omugole wange okuva mu kabi okudda mu by’obulamu mu ggulu, omuzibi aliggibwawo, era balifuna okulumbibwa kwabwe okw’amangu, n’ekiseera ky’okulumbibwa kwabwe kye kino. Bakabaka ekkumi bakunsizza bombi, era ddala bateeseteese emikono gyabwe; bateeseteese, beetegese, era babisse ku nsi obulimba bwabwe, balyoke balabike ng’abatuukirivu mu kutta obukadde n’obukadde obuligwa mu nnaku n’obulumi obw’olutalo lwabwe.

Era lye kuŋŋaaniro lya Sitaani lyennyini erifuga ensi kaakano, era beekweka mu nsi ensuubize ng’ennabbubi bwerikwekera omuyiggo gwayo okutuusa omuyiggo lwe gugwa mu mutimba gwayo. Mazima, ddala, baluse omutimba gwabwe mu nsi yonna, era bakutte bakabaka b’ensi mu mitimba gyabwe egy’obulimba, kubanga mu lukwe mwe bakolera olutalo, era n’obutemu, okugulirira, okukanga, okunyunyunta, n’ebibi ebirala bingi. Kubanga amakubo gaabwe goonoonefu ddala, kubanga Sitaani ateeka abasinga obubi n’abo aboonoonefu ddala ng’abakulembeze b’ensi balyoke bafugibwe mangu era bakozesebwe ddala ebisagazi Sitaani bye yasiga mu nnimiro za Yahweh ez’engano. N’ebiseera eby’okukungula okw’enkomerero bisemberedde omuliganyulwa eŋŋaano ne zikuŋŋaanyizibwa mu ggaliiro lya Yahweh ery’olubeerera, n’ebisagazi bya Sitaani birisimbulibwawo ddala, ne bisibibwa, ne bisuulibwa nga biramu mu nnyanja ey’omuliro ey’ekibiriiti eyaka n’ennimi z’omuliro ez’ebbuli ezisinga okwaka mu busika bwa bakitaabwe; era bwetyo bwe buli obulamu bwabwe obw’omu maaso, kale temubatiina.

Naye bwekityo bwekiri, ennabbubi efuga omutimba gwayo bwe yeeyongera okulwana okufuga ensi; kubanga ensi eweereddwa mu mikono gy’oguwoomu mu ngeri entono okumala akaseera akatono, y’esabbiiti ya Danyeri ey’amakumi musanvu, ebiseera byayo bye bino. Era n’olwekyo abeesigwa mu Kkanisa yange balitwalibwa mu ggulu mu mpeera ennene, era balifuna omugabo gwabwe, n’awasa omugole alikwatagana n’omugole we mu ndagaano ey’olubeerera, era emirembe n’emirembe baliba nange wonna we ndigenda. Era n’olwekyo ekyapa ky’ensi kye nkutte mu mikono gyange ekisaanidde kaakano okusaanyizibwawo, era obubonero bulisaanizibwawo, era balifuuyira amakondeere gaabwe, n’ebibya birifukibwamu busungu. Era omusango omunene n’okuzikirira biribuna ensi, n’akabi kaligwa mu nsi yonna ng’olutalo olw’enkomerero olw’okukwataganya amawanga agali wansi w’obufuzi bwa Sitaani lwe lugenda mu maaso, ensolo n’omulanuzi ow’obulimba balyoke bagoloke bafuge ensi n’obukambwe obungi, era bwe kirituukirira. Balirangirira olutalo ku bintu byonna ebya Katonda ow’amazima, n’Obukristaayo buliba bwa bumenyi bwa mateeka, era nsibiddwa ensolo zirwane n’abatukuvu era zibawangule, era bangi balifiirwa emitwe gyabwe ku lw’erinnya lyange, na buli akola bw’atyo alifuna obulamu bwe obw’olubeerera mu mpeera ennene n’ekitiibwa eky’olubeerera, era baliba n’obulamu obw’emirembe obw’olubeerera, era balirya ku masavu g’ensi.

Naye, ensi bw’ereetebwa ku maviivi gaayo agatali ga bwesigwa mu kujeemulukuka eri obubi obw’omwanguka okuyita mu lutalo olunene, obukambwe, n’okuzikirira, ensolo evva mu nnyanja n’ensolo evva mu ttaka birigolokokera ddala, wadde nga kaakano befuula balabe mu bukuusa n’obulimba bwabwe obw’okuzikiriza Babulooni Ekyama. Mutegeere nti kitaabwe ye omu, kubanga be ba Sitaani bennyini, era ye kibinja ekyo eky’omuziro oguwoomu kye yalonda okutuukiriza ebigendererwa bye. N’olwekyo ensolo evva mu nnyanja n’ensolo evva mu ttaka birigoloka, era birireeta emirembe mu mawanga, okuggyako nga mirembe gya bulimba. Mutegeere nti tewali mirembe ebweru w’endagaano y’emirembe eyaweebwa Yahweh olw’ebibi by’ensi, kubanga nze, Yesu Omunazaaleeti, ndi mulangira w’emirembe era ndeeta emirembe egy’olubeerera, era mu butuukirivu nja kufuga ensi okuva mu nsi ensuubize okuva ku ntebe ya Dawudi, kubanga nze musika we omutuufu.

Kale n’olwekyo baligoloka. Olutalo lw’ensi yonna olw’okusatu luliyiting’ana mu abo abavunnama ku kiyungu kyabwe ekiddugavu era balirwana olutalo ku nsi nga banoonya okuzikiriza ekintu kyonna ekikwatagana n’erinnya Yesu Kristo. Okuggyako abo abavunnama ku kiyungu kyabwe ekiddugavu abalireetebwa ddala wansi w’obufuzi bw’ensolo eva mu nnyanja, eribakulembera nga Mahdi waabwe. N’abo abali mu nsi ensuubize baliba ne kabaka waabwe ow’obulimba, omulanuzi ow’obulimba, Moshiach ow’obulimba, era aliba muzimbi wa yeekaalu ey’ekisolo, era ensolo evva mu ttaka bw’etyo eribakulembera mu bulimba obungi. N’abo abayambala ekiyungu ekiddugavu ku byenyi byabwe balireeta emirembe n’abo abavunnama ku kiyungu kyabwe ekiddugavu, era ensi erikwatagana nga bagoberera ensolo zino ne boogera bokka na bokka nti, Ani afaanana ng’ensolo? Era ani ayinza okulwana n’ensolo? Naye nze, Kristo Yesu, ali Kabaka w’abakabaka era Mukama w’abakama, nja kujja okusala omusango n’okulwana olutalo, era nja kuzikiriza ddala ensolo n’omulanuzi ow’obulimba, era balisuulibwa nga balamu mu nnyanja eno ennene eyaka n’omuliro n’ekibiriiti gye balirindirira Oguwoomu okubegattako mu kiseera ekyateekebwawo.

Kale balisuubiza ebisuubizo ebinene eby’emirembe n’obutebenkevu, era balikuŋŋaanya abo ababuze emirembe n’emirembe okutuuka ku kuzikirizibwa kwabwe. Balisuubiza entabaganya empya n’entegeka empya eriyagala akabonero kaayo ak’ekisolo okwetabamu, kale totwala kabonero. N’akabonero kaabwe balisuubiza eddagala erivvuunula kawumpuli era balisuubiza obulamu obulungi n’obulamu obuwanvu, era balibeera n’ebiseera eby’omu maaso ebirungi bye balowooza, era balisuubiza ebisuubizo bingi eby’obulimba eri abo abalizikirira olw’okutwala akabonero kaabwe ak’ekisolo.

Kale nate ndigamba abo abali ku nsi, bonna balyoke bawulire:

BWANA OMUNTU YENNA ASINZA ENSOLO N’EKIFANAANYI KYAYO, N’AKKIRIZA OKUBEEKAKO AKABONERO KAYO MU KYENYI KYE, OBA MU MUKONO GWE, OMUNTU OYO ALINYWA KU MVINYO GW’OBUSUNGU BWA KATONDA, OGUTABUDDWAMU MU KIKOMPE KY’OBUSUNGU BWE, ERA ALIBONYEBONYEZABWA N’OMULIRO N’EKIBIRIITI, MU MAASO GA BAMALAYIKA ABATUKUVU, NE MU MAASO G’OMWANA GW’ENDIGA: N’OMUKKA GW’OKUBONYABONYEZWA KWABWE GUNYIIKA EMIREREMBE N’EMIREMBE. ERA TEBAWUMULA MISANA NA KIRO, ABASINZA ENSOLO N’EKIFANAANYI KYAYO, NA BULI AKKIRIZA AKABONERO K’ERINNYA LYAYO.

Naye mutegeere, abaana bange, nti ekibiina kyabwe tekinabanga na mirembe, era ebisuubizo byabwe nsoloopyo okusendasenda abatali beegendereza mu katego kaabwe; kubanga omutegesi w’ebinyonyi atega atya omutego mu maaso g’ekinyonyi? Naye ebinyonyi bingi birikwatibwa mu mutego gwabwe, era bonna amannya gaabwe abatassibwa mu Kitabo ky’Obulamu. Mutegeere nti bonna abalikkiriza akabonero balikyusibwa emirembe gyonna, era baliba bavvodde Katonda Yahweh, nga bakyusizza endagabutonde yaabwe (DNA) ne balyoka boonoona ekifaananyi kya Katonda mwe baatonderwamu omuntu, era bonna abakola ekyo tebalisonyiyibwa, kale temukikkirizanga akabonero kaabwe. Kirungi okufiirwa obulamu bwammwe obw’oku nsi njookubalokola emirembe n’emirembe era mulyoke mufune obulamu obutaggwaawo mu nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, nga mukoowoola erinnya lyange okutuusa ku nkomerero okusinga okugezaako mu busiru okutaasa obulamu bwammwe obw’oku nsi ne mufiirwa omukisa gwonna ogw’obulamu obutaggwaawo mu kufa okw’okubiri okw’ennimi z’omuliro ezitazikira awaliba n’oluwuka lw’omwo olutafa. Kale mulonde n’amagezi!

Ensi eneegolokoka okuva mu vvu ly’entalo eneebanga nsi ya kawumpuli, okufa, enjala, okwonooneka kw’obutonde, obutali bwenkanya, obukambwe, eddalu, n’okubonaabona okungi ennyo. Essanyu lyonna liriggyibwa ku nsi, naye ensolo eva mu nsi erikaka akabonero kaayo ku nsi, kwe kugamba okulumwa kw’omusota, era bonna abalijjeemera balifiirwa obulamu bwabwe. Era okulonda okwo kwe kungi kwe kulina okukola okufuna obulamu obutaggwaawo mu bulokozi, era bonna abalikoowoola erinnya lya Mukama okutuusa ku nkomerero balilokolebwa. Naye, ekibiina kyabwe ekipya tekinareeta mirembe, era bonna abalikkiriza akabonero baliba kitundu ku birowoozo ebya wamu, era balitabikibwamu ne tekinologiya, era baliba baddu b’okwagala kwa Gguluddene, era ye gwe baliweereza, era mu nnyanja ennene ey’omuliro omwo mwe baligenda, era okwo kwe kufa okw’okubiri.

Gguluddene, n’ensolo eva mu nnyanja, n’ensolo eva mu nsi balikulira ekibiina ky’abassekabaka, era balirwana ne bonna abanoonya okubeera mu makubo ga Yahweh, era balinvunaana, Yeshua HaMashiach, olw’ebikangabwa ebirigwa ku nsi. Mu musango ne mu bujeemu obunene balivvoola erinnya lya Katonda w’eggulu n’omwana we eyazaalibwa omu yekka, era balyogera eby’okuwaayiriza ebinene. Ensolo eva mu nnyanja n’ensolo eva mu nsi biriyimirira wamu okuziyiza abajulirwa bange ababiri n’ebikondo byange eby’ettaala ebibiri era biribawakanya n’obubonero n’ebyamagero bingi eby’obulimba, era biryogera ebigambo bingi eby’okuwaayiriza. Era abajulirwa bange ababiri n’ebikondo byange eby’ettaala ebibiri bwe balimala okutegeeza kwabwe, ensolo eriva mu bunnya obutakoma erirwana nabo, n’ebawangula, n’ebatta. Naye mu nnaku ssatu n’ekitundu balizuukizibwa, era eddoboozi eddene eriva mu ggulu liribagamba nti, Mulinnye wano. Nabo balirinnya mu ggulu mu kire, era abalabe baabwe balibatunuulira.

Era bwe kityo ensolo eriva mu nsi, ye wuuyo ajja n’akabonero kaayo ak’ensolo mu ngalo, ye wuuyo aliba nnabbi waabwe omulimba ne Moshiach waabwe omulimba, alijja okufuga ensi ey’ekisuubizo. Era yeeyanjudde dda eri abafuzi b’ensi, ye abakulembeze b’ekkuŋŋaaniro lya Sitaani, kubanga assa ku musaayi gwabwe ogw’olulyo olwonoonese, era yeenyumiriza mu lulyo lwe, era Ddaani ye mwana gw’empologoma so tewali Masiya alivaamu. Era bwe kityo alizimba yeekaalu yaabwe nga banoonya mu busiru okuddamu okussawo amateeka ga Musa, ebintu ebyatuukirira mu butuukirivu obutaggwaawo mu ssaddaaka yange bwe nneewaayo ng’Omwana gw’endiga gwa Katonda, kubanga nze nneewaayo okuba Okuyitako okutuukiridde olw’ebibi by’abantu. Naye omutegesi w’ebinyonyi atega atya omutego mu maaso g’ekinyonyi?

Mazima, mazima, balitega omutego mu Sayuuni, era balinoonya okukwata ab’omu nsi, kubanga Sitaani anoonya okuzikiriza buli mmeeme y’omu kika kya Adamu, ye kika ky’omuntu; era singa ennaku z’okubonaabona kwa Yakobo tezaasalibwako, tewandisigaddewo mubiri mulamu. Naye bwe kityo siteegi eneetegekebwa ne yeekaalu yaabwe ey’okusatu, awali ekizikiza eky’omuzizo we kiribaawo. Era bwe ntyo bwe nnalabula: Kale bwe muliraba ekizikiza eky’omuzizo ekyayogerwa nnabbi Danyeri nga kiyimiridde mu kifo ekitukuvu, (oyo asoma ategeere). Abo abali mu Buyudaaya ne baddukira ku nsozi. Oyo aliba ku nnyumba waggulu, takkanga kutwala kintu kyonna mu nnyumba ye: N’oyo aliba mu nnimiro, takyukanga kutwala byambalo bye. Era ziribasanga abali embuto n’abayoza mu nnaku ezo. Naye musabenga okudduka kwammwe kuleme kubeera mu biseera bya ttoggo, newakubadde ku lunaku lwa Ssabbiiti: Kubanga mu kiseera ekyo wajja kubaawo okubonaabona okunene, okutali kwabangawo okuva ku lubereberye lw’ensi okutuusa kati, nedda, so tekulibaawo nate.

Mutegeere nti ekiseera kino kiri kumpi era kiribaawo oluvannyuma lw’emyezi 42 egisooka egy’okubonaabona kwa Yakobo. Mu myezi 42 egisooka, abajulirwa ababiri baliragulanga, era balizimba yeekaalu yaabwe so nga ekintu ekyo tekikyetaagisa; kubanga nze, Yesu Omunazaaleeti, nze Okuyitako okutuukiridde olw’ebibi by’abantu emirembe n’emirembe. Naye ensolo zino ezitafaayo ziyimiridde okuziyiza nze, era ensolo eva mu nsi eneefuba okweteekawo ng’omulimba waabwe Moshiach, era ogwo gwe mutego gwabwe.

Balisendasenda ab’omu nsi abakyanganidde, Yeshua HaMashiach, Masiya waabwe ow’amazima, era bangi balidduka ne beekuŋŋaanya eri Moshiach waabwe omulimba, naye mu butamanya bamugoberera mu kuzikirira kwabwe kwennyini. Kubanga ensolo eva mu nsi y’oyo atega omutego mu nsi, era alisendasenda bangi mu mutembo gwe ogw’obulimba; olwo nannansi alibuuka ku muyiggo gwe, era aliryamu olukwe abantu b’ensi. Temwasomangako? Kubanga bwe kityo bwe kyawandiikibwa nti: Omuntu yenna aleme okubalimba mu ngeri yonna: kubanga olunaku olwo terulituuka, okuggyako nga wazzeewo okuggyibwawo okusooka, n’omuntu oyo ow’ekibi n’abikkulibwa, ye mwana w’okuzikirira, Oyo omuwakanyi, era eyeegulumiza okusinga byonna ebyogerwako nga Katonda, oba ebivunnamirwa: n’alyoka atuula nga Katonda mu Yeekaalu ya Katonda, nga yeeyolesa nga ye Katonda. Kale mutegeere nti okuggyibwawo okusooka kwe nze we ndikuŋŋaanyiriza abakkiriza mu Kkanisa yange gyendi okunsisinkana mu bbanga era mu kiseera ekyo omuziyiza aliggyibwawo era omuntu oyo ow’ekibi alibikkulibwa mu kiseera kye.

Naye ddi Yahweh lwe yalekawo abasika b’obulokozi okutuuka ku kuzikirira okutaggwaawo, era nkirangirira lwatu nga tekinnabaawo: Nti nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, nja kulokola ensigalira ya Yakobo, era bangi ab’omu nsi balinkkiriza ng’omasiya waabwe. Era ndibanunula. Laba, kyawandiikibwa: Ne ntunula, era, laba, Omwana gw'endiga yali ayimiridde ku lusozi Sayuuni, naye ng’ali wamu n’abantu 144,000 nga balina Erinnya lya Kitaawe nga liwandiikiddwa ku byenyi byabwe. Era bwe kityo bwe kiriba nti nja kununula ensigalira mu kuzikirira, era njija n’eggye eritasobola kuziyizibwa eriritambulira ddala nga mbaluŋŋamya okuluŋŋamya abaana bange abato mu kifo ekirungi. Oboolyawo oyinza okwebuuza abo 144,000 be baani era lwa nsonga ki gye bangoberera ku Lusozi Sayuuni?

Abo 144,000 baalondebwa Yahweh emisingi gy’ensi nga tenassibwawo olw’ekiseera ng’ekyo. Baaweebwa mu kubonaabona, era baliba n’obusobozi obw’enjawulo n’amaanyi ge ndibambaza mu byambalo byabwe ebipya bye ndibawa, kubanga ebyambalo byabwe bitungiddwa bwannannyini ku lwabwe era birimu ebintu bye baakola n’engeri zaabwe. Balitwalibwa mu kukuŋŋaana kw’abakkiriza naye ne bassibwawo ku bbali olw’ebigendererwa byange, ye bigendererwa bya Yahweh ne Yeshua HaMashiach. Bajja mu kiseera ky’okubonaabona okunene okukulembera Isirayiri okubatuusa mu kifo ekirungi naye okusooka batwalibwa okuwasa Kristo, kubanga nze Ow’embaga, era be Mugole wange, abayite, abalonde, abeesigwa, era ab’amazima, be abo be nnafuula abalongoofu ng’omuwala embeerera mu musaayi gwange ogutangirira. Era ye mmwe, Mugole wange, abaliweereza ebigendererwa bya Yahweh ku kiragiro kyange mu biseera by’okubonaabona okusinga obunene.

Laba, kyawandiikibwa! Omukazi n'addukira mu ddungu, gye yalina ekifo Katonda kye yamutegekera, bamuliisirizeyo ennaku lukumi mu bibiri mu nkaaga. Kale bwe kiriba nti nja kuteekateeka ekifo eky’okwekwasa mu ddungu eri abantu bange, kubanga nja kulokola Yakobo mu kubonaabona kwabwe, era bangi ab’oku nsi ey’ekisuubizo balikyukira nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, Masiya waabwe ow’amazima. Era ndibalokola mu katego akalibategerwa, era bwe ntyo ndabula bonna nga tekinnabaawo okwewala omutego n’obutabagoberera. Temukkiririzanga bubonero bwabwe na byamagero byabwe eby’obulimba, so temukkiririzanga bigambo byabwe eby’okuwaayiriza, naye mukoowoolenga erinnya lya Mukama, ye Yeshua HaMashiach, ye Yesu Kristo mu lulimi Olungereza, kubanga mu lulimi lwonna omuntu lw’akoowoolamu erinnya lyange, nabo balilokolebwa bwe balikoowoola erinnya lyange okutuusa ku nkomerero.

Era bwe ntyo njija okutegeeza abantu bange omutego ogulibategerwa nnabbi omulimba ajja n’akabonero ke ak’ensolo mu ngalo. Era bwe ntyo ntegeeza abantu bange lwatu nga tekinnabaawo ebigenda okujja ku nsi nga tebinnabaawo. Mazima, Mukama Katonda talikola kigambo, wabula abikkulira abaweereza be bannabbi ekyama kye. Era nja kununula bangi mu Isirayiri abandyazikiridde ne mbakulembera okubayingiza mu ddungu mu kifo Yahweh ky’abategekedde, era omwo mwe baliriisirizibwa, ne balundibwa, ne balabirirwa okutuusa okubonaabona lwe kuliggwaawo. Kubanga kiribaawo ku nkomerero y’okubonaabona kwa Yakobo, era bwe kityo bwe kyawandiikibwa era bwe kirikolebwa:

Ne ndaba eggulu nga libikkuse, era laba, embalaasi enjeru, n’oyo eyali agituddeko yayitibwa Omwesigwa era Ow'amazima, era mu butuukirivu asala omusango era alwana. Amaaso ge gaali ng’olulimi lw'omuliro, ne ku mutwe gwe kwaliko engule nnyingi, era yalina erinnya eriwandiikiddwa, eritamanyiddwa muntu yenna wabula ye yekka. N’ayambala ekyambalo ekyanyikibwa mu musaayi, n’erinnya lye liyitibwa, Kigambo kya Katonda. N’amagye agaali mu ggulu ne gamugoberera ku mbalaasi enjeru, nga bambadde bafuta enjeru ennungi. Ne mu kamwa ke ne muva ekitala eky’obwogi, alyoke akitwale okukuba amawanga: n’abafugisa n’omuggo ogw’ekyuma: n’alinnyirira essogolero ly’obukambwe n’obusungu bwa Katonda Omuyinza w'ebintu byonna. Era ku kyambalo kye, ne ku kisambi kye kuliko erinnya eriwandiikiddwa, Kabaka Wa Bassekabaka, Era Mukama Wa Bakama.

Nze Yeshua HaMashiach, era ntumye Ebigambo bino okuyita mu muweereza wange, era okuyita mu muweereza wange ebigambo bino biweerezeddwa. Era nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, nze abikkudde enjiri eteriggwaawo mu kiseera kyayo, era ebiseera by’okubikkula bituuse.

Laba! Ebiseera by’okubikkula bituuse ku nsi, era ntegeeza nti okubonaabona kwa Yakobo, ye wiiki ya Danyeri ey’amakumi musanvu, etuuse. Amangu ago nja kusikula abeesigwa okuva ku nsi okubatwala mu kifo ekirungi eky’omu ggulu, era amangu ago omuziyiza aliggyibwawo, era amangu ago akabi akanene, omusango, n’ebikangabwa birigwa ku abo abali ku nsi. Era bonna abalagirwa obulamu obutaggwaawo balijja mu bulokozi mu nze, Kristo Yesu, Mukama eyazuukira emirembe gyonna. N’obwakabaka bwa Sitaani bulibonerezebwa ddala ne buzikirizibwa, ne butaddamu kugoloka.

Nze wuuyo ajja okusala omusango n’okulwana, era njija ng’Empologoma y’ekika kya Yuda, era ensigalira ya Yakobo ndigirokola, era ndifuga ensi mu butuukirivu mu bwakabaka bwange obw’emyaka olukumi, era ndeeta eggye ddene ery’abakabaka n’abakabona okufuga ensi mu butuukirivu ku kiragiro kyange.

Nze Yeshua HaMashiach, Kabaka Omukulu Wa Bassekabaka era Mukama Wa Bakama, kale wuliriza Ebigambo byange, kubanga ebiseera by’okutuukirizibwa kwabyo bituuse.
