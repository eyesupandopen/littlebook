Laba! Nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, njija okubuulira Ekkanisa yange, omubiri gwange, era n’omugole wange ebintu ebigenda okujja ku nsi. Bwe kityo mulyoke mutegeere amakulu g’ebintu ebigenda okubaawo, era muleme okuzibizibwa amaaso ku bukulu bwabyo. Kubanga, abo abagwa baagala okubuzaabuza bonna n’obulimba bwabwe obw’obulimba era ensi gye mwali mumanyi eri mu kukyusibwa okuva ku nsi gye mwali mumanyi era tekyali y’emu era ejja kugenda mu maaso n’okukyusibwa okuva ku ntegeka yaayo ey’edda eyateekebwawo okuva ku mataba ga Nuuwa.

Laba! N’olwekyo, mutegeere ebiseera mwe muli n’obukulu bw’ebintu ebijja. Era n’ebintu ebijja okusalibwako mu butuukirivu, kubanga singa ennaku ez’okubonaabona kwa Yakobo tezaasalibwako, mazima ddala, tewandisigaddewo mubiri mulamu ku nsi era byonna byandizise. Mutegeere nti, ku kuvuga kw’akagombe ak’enkomerero akagenda okuvuga, era akagombe ako ak’enkomerero ak’ekiseera kyange eky’ekisa ekyateekebwa mu mikono gyange n’ekiragiro ekituukiridde eky’okuvuga mu kiseera ekituukiridde ekibali kumpi mu bujjuvu, baagalwa bange, nti ensi ejja kuweebwayo mu bujjuvu mu mikono gy’ogusota mu kufuga kwagwo okumpi okw’ekisa.

Era kino kubanga omuntu ow’obutonde akkirizza kyeyagalire okufuga okuweereddwa bakabaka ekkumi, abagolokoka mu bulimba obunene okuva mu nnyumba ekkumi ez’edda, abafuze ensi mu bulimba obunene n’obukumpanya ku lw’abo abagwa okuva lwe baatandika okugolokoka ku kugwa kwa Babeli n’omunaala gwagwo mu nnaku za Nimuloodi. Abo abagwa bawadde ensolo zaabwe okufuga ku kiragiro ky’ogusota, abakumpanyizza n’ebisuubizo ebitaliimu n’okwawukana okungi, ne bawamba amawanga abakulembeze b’ensi yonna kaakano baweereze ogusota mu bujjuvu, era abo abagaana battiddwa, oba bajja kuttibwa, emikono gyabwe egikwekeddwa oba bajja kugwa mu bujjuvu olw’olutalo lwabwe olugenda okujja olw’okuwamba ensi yonna.

Naye bw’etyo bw’eri ensi mwe muli n’ebiseera ebituuse ku nsi mu bubi obunene n’obulimba obutuukiridde. Kubanga nga bwe kiri, ensi kaakano egaanyi endagaano yange empya ey’obutuukirivu eyateekebwako omukono era n’ekakasibwa mazima ddala omusaayi gwange omutaliiko kibi gwe nnawaayo okusasula mu bujjuvu omutango gwa Musa. Bwe kityo, omuntu eyagwa yandibadde n’eky’okulondawo oba okukkiriza okusonyiyibwa ebibi n’obulokozi obw’obwa Katonda n’okuzzibwawo eri Yakuwa, NZE NDI ALIWO Omunene, ng’ayita mu Yesu Omunazaaleeti oba omuntu eyagwa yandironzeewo okusigala ng’agudde era ng’aweereza okwegomba kw’abo abagwa, ng’akkiriza omusango gwonna ku buli kibi, na buli kikolwa ekitari kya butuukirivu kye bakola mu butali butuukirivu, ka kibe katono kitya. Mutegeere nti bonna abalondawo okusigala nga baweereza abo abagwa bajja kwegatta nabo mu bujjuvu mu kubonerezebwa kwabwe mu nnyanja ey’omuliro ennene eyo, era omusango omukakali era omunene ogutatategekerwa muntu, naye bonna abalondawo okusigala n’abo abagwa era bajja kwegatta n’abo abagwa kubanga tewali kulondawo kulala!

Naye, okugaana endagaano yange empya tekuggyawo maanyi n’omugaso gw’endagaano yange empya kubanga endagaano yange empya ndagaano ya lubeerera era ndagaano ya lubeerera ejja kusigalawo emirembe na mirembe era n’amaanyi gonna aga Kabona Asinga Obukulu emirembe gyonna ng’Omuwendo gwa Merukizeddeeki bwe guli, era ejja kusigalawo era n’ebeerawo emirembe na mirembe ng’Endagaano ey’Obutuukirivu ey’Obutuukirivu ey’okufuga kwa Yakuwa okutaliiko nkomerero era okutuukiridde era okw’obutuukirivu. Era ekitonde ky’ebintu ebiri mu ggulu, ne ku nsi, era n’ebiri wansi w’ensi era tewali wa kwekweka okufuga kwange okw’obutuukirivu we kutalina maanyi matuukiridde ku nkomerero ya ssabbiiti ya Danyeri ey’amakumi musanvu, era nga y’enjala ya Yakobo, kati eyetegese mu bujjuvu mu kwegatta kw’obulumi bw’okuzaala era omwana ajja kuzaalibwa!

Era omwana omutukuvu ow’ebibala ebibereberye eby’obutuukirivu mu makungula ga sayiri amatuukiridde agagenda okubaawo mu bujjuvu amangu ago era mu ngeri etasuubirwa kaakano, era ekiseera eky’emirembe na mirembe eky’okutuukirizibwa abafu mu Kristo we bajja okuzaalibwa okuva mu mutima gw’ensi gye bawummulidde mu mirembe, n’abeesigwa mu Kristo abalamu era abasigaddewo bajja kuva ku maaso g’ensi nabo okunsisinkana mu bbanga era bwe mutyo mwenna mujja kutwalibwa mu ggulu ery’amagulu g’amagulu mwe mujja okuyimirira mu maaso ga Mukama wammwe ow’Obutuukirivu ow’Emirembe n’Emirembe okuweebwa obusika bwammwe obujjuvu! Era nja kubaleeta nga temuli bbala oba kamogo oba bbala oba mukunya oba kintu kyonna eky’engeri eyo eri Yakuwa, NZE NDI ALIWO Omunene, ddala eyabazaala mwenna n’omwoyo gwange ogw’Obwana mwe mwateekerwa akabonero olunaku luno olw’okununulibwa!

Era mwenna mujja kutwala ebifo byammwe mu Isiraeri ya Katonda, kubanga oba mwazaalibwa mu Bayudaaya, oba mu bika bya Isiraeri ebyabula, okuva mu Bamawanga: mu Kristo Yesu temujja kuba nate ku lw’olunyiriri lw’omusaayi gw’oku nsi wabula ekitonde ekipya, omuntu omupya, era abatondeddwa obupya mu bujjuvu mu butuukirivu obutuukiridde mu Kristo Yesu era mujja kwewuunya ekitiibwa eky’emirembe na mirembe kye mujja okwambazibwa. Kubanga abo abali mu Kristo bajja kukolebwa era ne babumbibwa mu ngeri y’emu nga Kristo Yesu era abawangula bange bajja kusikira ebintu byonna mu ngeri y’emu nga bwe nnawangula ne nsikira ebintu byonna okuva mu mukono gwa Yakuwa, NZE NDI ALIWO Omunene, era bwe butyo bwe buli obusika bw’abeesigwa! N’olwekyo, mutegeere nti mu kutemya kw’eriiso okw’amangu ago mwenna mujja kusumululwa emirembe gyonna okuva ku musaayi ogw’obulwadde ogwa Adamu, era okuva ku Adamu ow’enkomerero: Yakuwa gw’afuuse omwoyo oguwa obulamu ajja okubatonda obupya mwenna mu ngeri y’emu nga Mukama wammwe ow’Obutuukirivu ow’emirembe n’emirembe, Seera!

N’olwekyo, nja kukola omulimu omutuukiridde era omujjuvu mu mmwe baagalwa bange, era okusinziira ku kuwuliza kwammwe okw’obwesigwa eri obutuukirivu mu biseera byange eby’ekisa n’okusaasira. Kubanga ekisa n’okusaasira bigwa ng’enjuba mu kiseera kino eky’okwegatta, wano ku ntandikwa y’okubonaabona kwa Yakobo. Kubanga okwegatta kwe njogerako kwe kwegatta kw’obutuukirivu, okukomekkerezebwa n’abeesigwa mu Kristo nga bawuddwamu mu bujjuvu n’abo abali ku nsi mu butali butuukirivu, bwe batwalibwa mu bulamu obw’omu ggulu mu kutemya kw’eriiso amangu ago era mu ngeri etasuubirwa kaakano.

Naye, omulimu guno ogw’obutuukirivu bwe gutuukirizibwa mu bujjuvu, era wajja kubaawo okwegatta kw’obubi ku nsi omuzibi wange bw’aliggibwawo. Kubanga waliwo ebigambo ebiwerako ebiziyiza abo abagwa obutaba na kwegatta kwe baagala, naye abeesigwa mu Kristo bwe batwalibwa ebigambo byonna ebiziyiza bijja kuggibwawo n’omuzibi wange ne Mikayiri ajja kuyimirira n’eggyawo n’omuzira gujja kugwa, n’ensi ejja kukyusibwa n’amaanyi ereme kuba n’amakubo ag’edda era ne Nefulimu baabwe ab’omuziro abakolebwa nate bajja kusumululwa. Laba! Ndeeta ebikonge okutuukiriza obusungu bwange!

Bwe kityo, ensi ejja kuweebwayo mu mikono gy’ogusota okumala akaseera katono, era ekiseera eky’omusango omukakali abali ku nsi gye bajja okufuna mu bujjuvu obubi n’ekizikiza kye beegomba era kye baagala, okuggyako tewali kiseera kya zaabu kye bajja kuba nakyo wabula omusango omukakali era ogw’emirembe na mirembe. Era abagwa bajja kuba n’olutalo lwabwe lwe bajja okujeemuliramu ensi mu bujjuvu, mu bubi obutaziyiziddwa, n’obubi obutaziyiziddwa, n’omwenzi oyo omunene omumyufu ajja kugwa mu nnimi z’omuliro ez’omusango nga Babulooni Ekyama kisalirwa omusango mu bujjuvu era ne kizikirizibwa ku kugenda kw’abeesigwa mu Kristo, nga tekigolokoka nate! Mutegeere nti bajja kwekweka mu bibuga byabwe eby’omu ttaka ne mu bifo eby’obwama, ne mu nkambi, ne mu bifo eby’okwekweka, naye bangi ku bo tebajja kuwona wabula bajja kuziikibwa mu nsi mu musango omujjuvu, era bajja kusigala nga babonyaabonyezebwa mu kufa okutuusa ku musango ogwo ogw’entebe enjeru ennene era tebajja kuwona.

Awo okubikkulirwa n’okutegeera bijja kugwa mangu ku nsi nti omusango n’obusungu bituuse ku nsi, era ani ajja okuyinza okuyimirira? Naye, bonna abakoowoola erinnya lya Yesu okutuusa ku nkomerero yaabwe bajja kujja mu bulokozi obujjuvu era obw’ekitiibwa era abantu abasigaddewo ab’amaanyi okuva mu Isiraeri bajja kukkiriza Yeshua HaMashiach nga Masiya waabwe ow’amazima era bajja kulokolebwa nga balamu mu bwakabaka bwange obw’emyaka olukumi. N’olwekyo mutegeere okwawukana okw’enkomerero okugenda okujja.

Kubanga abantu abasigaddewo ab’obutuukirivu aba Yakobo bajja kwawulibwa ku Esawu ne Ashekenazi, era bajja kulokolebwa nga balamu olw’okubikkulirwa okujja okutuukirizibwa abajulirwa bange ababbiri, Enoka Omuwandiisi ne Eriya Omutisubi.

Kubanga ab’ekivvi n’abangi ku abo abaasooka okugaana Yesu Omunazaaleeti bajja kulekebwa mu muliro ogw’okulongoosa era bajja kwawulibwa ku abo abawuniikirira oluvannyuma lw’ensolo era bajja kusooka okuwangulwa ensolo eva mu nnyanja mu lutalo lwayo ku batukuvu abasigaddewo; abajja okuwangula ensolo eva mu nnyanja nga bakoowoola Yesu nga Mukama waabwe okutuusa ku nkomerero yaabwe era mu kitiibwa ekinene mwe bajja okugenda emirembe gyonna!

Kubanga ab’omuziro n’abatali batuukirivu bajja kwawulibwa mu bujjuvu ku batuukirivu era bajja kuteekebwako akabonero emirembe gyonna n’okulumwa kw’omusota okuli mu mukono gw’ensolo eva mu nsi okujja okukozesebwa okuteega mu bujjuvu bonna abaagala obubi era abasanyukira obutali butuukirivu, era tebajja kuwona.

Era bwe kityo bwe kiri, nti ebiseera eby’okubikkulirwa bitiise ensi yonna kaakano, era byonna bijja kubikkulirwa mu bujjuvu mu biseera byabyo ebituufu. Ku kukuŋŋaana kw’abeesigwa mu Kristo okugabibwa n’okuleekaana okw’amaanyi n’eddoboozi lya malayika omukulu n’akagombe ako ak’enkomerero akajja okuvuga, abo abaatwalibwa mu ggulu tebajja kuba bakyalaba mu ndabirwamu etalaga bulungi naye bajja kutegeera ebintu byonna mu bujjuvu era nga bwe bimanyiddwa mu bujjuvu. Era bajja kulaba n’amaaso gaabwe, era bajja kuwulira n’amatu gaabwe, era bajja kulaba n’okutegeera mu bujjuvu ekitiibwa kya Yakuwa NZE NDI ALIWO Omunene, era ne Kristo Yesu Mukama waabwe ow’Obutuukirivu ow’Emirembe n’Emirembe era kijja kuba kiseera ki ekirungi ng’omugole omusajja yeegatta mu bujjuvu n’omugole we mu ndagaano ey’emirembe n’emirembe! Era emmeeza ziteeseteese n’ebisenge by’embaga biri mu nteekateeka!

Era bwe kityo bwe kiri, nti ebiseera eby’okubikkulirwa bijja kujja ku nsi yonna kaakano, era buli kimu ku bwakabaka bwa Setaani n’ebiragiro by’obutali butuukirivu bijja kwanjulwa mu bujjuvu era ne bireetebwa mu lwatu omusango bwe gujja okugwa, kubanga eggigi eriwawula lijja kuggibwawo mangu ago era n’amaanyi nga eggigi eryali mu yeekaalu eryawula ekifo ekitukuvu ennyo ku kifo ekitukuvu ekitono bwe lyayulibwa okuva waggulu okutuuka wansi n’omukono ogw’amaanyi ogwa Yakuwa yennyini, bwe nnayogera ekigambo ekyo eky’obuwanguzi obw’emirembe na mirembe, KIWEDDE!

N’olwekyo, mutegeere ebiseera mwe muli era mweteekereteekere baagalwa bange. Mugende mu maaso n’okwenenya era mwesabire omusaayi gwange ogutangirira ku bulamu bwammwe n’obulamu bw’abo be mwagala mu kuwolereza, era bwe mutyo mulyoke mubeere n’okusonyiyibwa okujjuvu okw’ebintu byonna n’okusonyiyibwa ebibi era abangi bajja kulokolebwa olw’okuwolereza kwammwe okw’obutuukirivu. Mutegeere nti kaakano ke kaseera okwawukana mu bujjuvu n’engeri z’ensi eno era n’ebyo ebikwata ku nsi eno embi kubanga amangu ago era mu ngeri etasuubirwa ebiseera byammwe eby’okubeera mu nsi eno ey’ebibi bijja kuba ku nkomerero ey’emirembe n’emirembe, era temujja kuddamu kufugibwa bubi na butali butuukirivu. N’olwekyo mugende mu maaso n’okuva mu bo mubeere beeroboze mu bintu byonna, wadde nga, nsaba mugende mu maaso n’emirimu gyammwe, nga mukolera ddala Mukama nga muva mu mitima gijjudde okukkiriza nga mukola obutuukirivu okutuusa lwe mutaba ku nsi amangu ago!

Era n’olwekyo njija okubuulira Ekkanisa yange, omubiri gwange, era n’omugole wange nti ebiseera eby’okununulibwa kwabwe bituuse n’ebiseera eby’okujaguza okujjuvu n’obusika n’empeera bitegekeddwa okuwaniriza abeesigwa mu Kristo mu ggulu ku kiragiro kyange kaakano, kubanga Yakuwa awadde ebintu byonna mu mikono gyange egy’amaanyi okutuukiriza, era bulijjo nkola Kitange by’ayagala era bwe kityo mulyoke mununulibwe mu butuukirivu obujjuvu mulyoke nammwe bulijjo mukole Kitammwe by’ayagala mu butuukirivu obutuukiridde!

N’olwekyo, mu ngeri y’emu okukyusibwa kuno okw’obukulembeze bwe kujja okubaawo mu kuzikirizibwa okunene n’olutalo ng’abo abagwa bwe bakola ekyo ekiweereddwa mu mikono gyabwe okutuukiriza ku musango omujjuvu ogwa Babulooni Ekyama atudde ku mazzi amangi agali abantu, n’ebibinja by’abantu, n’amawanga, n’ennimi; era nga bawamba amawanga okufuga kwabwe okutali kutukuvu kwe nja okukomya emirembe gyonna ku nkomerero y’okubonaabona kwa Yakobo.

Nti mu ngeri y’emu, ku kujja kwange okw’okubiri, bwe nja okudda ku nsi n’eggye ery’omu ggulu n’abeesigwa mu Kristo era n’Omugole wange, nti mu butuukirivu nja kusala omusango ne ntabaaza ne nzikiriza emirembe na mirembe n’obukambwe n’amaanyi obufuzi bwonna n’obuyinza n’obufuzi bw’ogusota!
