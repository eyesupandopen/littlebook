Omujulirwa Omulundi Ogw'okubiri: Isirayiri
Okukaawa n'Okuwooma
Okitobba 13, 2024

Gye Muli Mmwe Abange Abeesigwa Abagumiikiriza nga Mulindiridde Mukama Wammwe Ataggwaawo!

Muwulire ebigambo bya Mukama wammwe ow’emirembe gyonna, ye Yeshua HaMashiach, era mujje gye ndi n’emitima gyammwe emyetegefu okukkiriza n’okusuubira empeera yammwe okuva mu mukono gwange, kubanga mazima ekiseera kituuse. Weewaawo, akaseera ako akaalindirirwa ennyo lwe nzikka okuva mu ggulu n’okwogerera waggulu n’eddoboozi, n’eddoboozi lya malayika omukulu era n’ekkondeere lya Katonda (kubanga ekkondeere eryo linaafuuyibwa) n’abafu abali mu Kristo balizuukizibwa nga tebalivunda nammwe abali abalamu abalisigalawo mulitwalibwa mu bire gye mulyambala obutafa, era bwe mutyo munaabeeranga ne Mukama wammwe ataggwaawo emirembe n’emirembe, nga mufaanana naye.

Nze Mukama wa Bakama era Kabaka wa Bakabaka, era nze nnagula ensi mu musaayi gwange ogwatangirira. Mazima ddala, nnasasula omuwendo gw’ebibi by’omuntu omuntu asobole okutabagana ne Yahweh, kubanga Nze, Yesu Omunazaaleeti, nze ndagaano yammwe ey’emirembe abantu gye bayita okutabagana ne Katonda, kyennava ndeetera Pawulo okuwandiikira Abakkolinso nti: Kale ffe tuli babaka ku lwa Kristo: ng’era Katonda ababegayirira ku bwaffe, tubasaba ku lwa Kristo, mutabagane ne Katonda. Kubanga oyo ataalina kibi, yamufuula ekibi ku lwaffe, ffe tulyoke tufuuke obutuukirivu bwa Katonda mu ye.

Kyeriva, mmwe abaana bange, mbafudde butuukirivu bwa Katonda okuyita mu Kristo ali mu mmwe, essuubi ly’ekitiibwa, ye mwoyo ogwo ogw’obwana obutaggwaawo mwe mukaabira nti ABBA, Kitange, era bwe mutyo ne muzaalibwa omulundi ogw’okubiri nga muli baana ba Katonda ab’emirembe gyonna era ne muteekebwako akabonero ak’omwoyo oyo omutukuvu ow’ekisuubizo. Mutegeere nti akabonero ka Yahweh tekasobola kumenyeka, newankubadde okukyusibwa, era bonna abateekebwako akabonero ako balina obukuumi obutaggwaawo, era bwe mutyo mutegeere nti ekyo Yahweh ky’awandiise n’akyogera tewali asobola kukikyusa. Kale ani asobola okubaggya mu ngalo zange? Ani asobola okuwangula Katonda omukulu ow’emirembe gyonna, ye Yahweh, Kitaffe w’ebitangaala? Kale lwaki mweeraliikirira ne mutya nti nja kubasuula? Naye, ani ku mmwe eyandisuudde abaana be, ng’ekintu ekyo kiyitibwa kya buseere mu maaso ga Mukama wammwe era Omulokozi wammwe, ye Yesu Omunazaaleeti.

Mazima ddala, ebirabo n’okuyitibwa kwa Katonda tebyejjusibwa era abo abaaweebwa ekirabo eky’obulamu obutaggwaawo nga baatula nti Yesu ye Mukama, ate nga bakkiririza nti Katonda yanzuukiza mu bafu, baweebwa obulamu obutaggwaawo era bafuulibwa butuukirivu bwa Katonda mu Kristo Yesu Mukama waabwe. Naye omuntu yenna bw’atula okwatula okwo n’omutima ogutalina kukkiriza, nnimma akabonero ka Katonda omulamu ndyoke mbaleetere ddala gye ndi beenenye ebibi byabwe byonna, oboolyawo ensigo yange yagwa ku ttaka ery’amayinja. Naye omuntu yenna bw’aba talina mwoyo gwa Kristo, oyo si wuwe. Nze ngezesa era nkema emitima gy’abantu balyoke balongoosebwe mu muliro gwange ogw’okulongoosa kubanga nze ntuula n’ekiwuuwo mu ngalo nga nkuuma omuliro ogw’okulongoosa kubanga nze muweesi awuuwa amanda mulyoke mutuuke ku busika bwammwe obujjuvu mu nze, Kristo Yesu, kale mubeere bawombeefu eri amagezi gange era temugaana kusikibwa kwa mwoyo ogwo ogw’obwana ogwazaalibwa mu mmwe oguleeta obutuukirivu n’obutukuvu obw’amazima. Era mbegayirira mmwe abaana bange mutambulirenga mu mwoyo muleme okutuukiriza okwegomba kw’omubiri.

Naye, bangi ku mmwe muleka emitima gyammwe okubatyemula awatali nsonga nti akabonero ka Katonda kasobola okumenyeka olw’obusiru bw’omuntu. Mbagalwa bange, mbabuuza? El Shaddai mu kumanya kwe okw’emabega, yasuulirira ekya nti yali antuma nze, Yesu Omunazaaleeti, okununula aboonoonyi abandyonoonyeyo oluvannyuma lw’obulokozi? Yahweh, Katonda omukulu ow’emirembe gyonna ateetaaga muwi wa magezi newankubadde ng’emitwalo n’emitwalo gyetoolodde entebe ye ey’obwakabaka, era akola byonna ng’okuteesa kw’okwagala kwe bwe kuli, teyategeera butatuukirivu bwa Adamu eyeeyonoona, ye muntu yenna eyazaalibwa mu ttaka emmyuufu agabana ku musaayi gwa Adamu?

Nedda baana bange, tekiyinzika nti Yahweh yandiremyeddwa okutegeera bino era abategedde mu ngeri yonna n’awa buli nkola erunaku lwammwe olw'okununulibwa olujjuvu okubajjirira mu bujjuvu, mmwe bagalwa bange. Kale mutambulire mu tteeka ly’okukkiriza era nga muyita mu mwoyo gwange ogwazaalibwa mu mmwe era mutambulirenga ng’omwoyo gw’obulamu bwe guli mu Kristo Yesu. Kubanga mu Kristo Yesu okukomolebwa tekugasa kintu, newankubadde obutakomolebwa, wabula ekitonde ekiggya. Era bonna abatambulira mu tteeka lino, emirembe gibeerenga ku bo, n’okusaasira, ne ku Isirayiri wa Katonda. Temutegeera nti omutuukirivu anaabanga mulamu lwa kukkiriza? Kale mutegeere nti tewali muntu wange mwesigwa atuukiridde mu kiseera kino, nedda, okutuusa ku kununulibwa kwabwe okujjuvu. N’abanunule bonna abateekebwako akabonero ak’omwoyo oyo omutukuvu ow’ekisuubizo balyonoona, ka babe nga bamanyi oba nga tebamanyi, okumala olunaku lwonna. N’olwekyo mwerongooseze mu musaayi gwange ogutangirira buli lunaku. Mujje gye ndi mukuumenga enkolagana yammwe nange era mugikuumenga nga nnywevu, mbagalwa bange. Mwatulenga ebibi byammwe gye ndi buli lunaku, era musigalenga nga muli bawombeefu eri eddoboozi lyange eddogoofu bwe njija gye muli okubanenya olw’obutuukirivu. Nze mbadduukiridde dda era ndi awo mu maaso gammwe nga mbazizzaamu amaanyi era nga nkuuma amakubo gammwe nga matereevu mulyoke musobole okutambulira mu kkubo eryo eggololofu era effunda lye mwayitirwa okutambuliramu. Era mweyongere okwesunga olunaku olwo olw’okununulibwa kwammwe okujjuvu lwe muliyimirira nga mutuukiridde mu maaso ga Yahweh yennyini nga mwambadde obutuukirivu bwe nja okubawa mu bujjuvu kubanga nze, Kristo Yesu, nze nja okubambaza bafuta, olugoye olweru olutukuvu olutalina bbala, oba kaamogo, oba kkunyira, newankubadde ekintu kyonna ekifaanana bwe kityo.

N'olwekyo mutegeere, mmwe abato bange, nti omusango gutuuse ku nsi, ye musango ogwo omunene ogwalagulibwa edda bannabbi aba edda, era ekiseera kyagwo eky’okutuukirizibwa kituuse. Obulumi obw’okuzaala tebukoma ku nsi kaakano kubanga omusango guli wano era abanunule bange balituuka ku kununulibwa kwabwe okujjuvu. Era bwe kityo ebigenda okubaawo bijja kujja, mayengo ku mayengo. Buli jjengo lisinga obunene n’amaanyi mu kuzikiriza kwalyo okusinga eddiridde okutuusa lwe mutwalibwa mu kifo eky’obukuumi mu kaseera katono, ne Babulooni ow’Ekyama n’atabeerawo. Buli jjengo lireeta omusango era buli jjengo likuba n’amaanyi mangi era n’eddoboozi ddene okusinga eddiridde. Era bwe gityo emisango gyange n’okulabula kwange eri ensi, kubanga njija ng’empologoma ey’ekika kya Yuda era mu butuukirivu njija okusala omusango n’okulwana olutalo.

Era bwe kityo bwe kinaaba nti akabi ak’enkomerero nga tekannatuuka, ku kiragiro kyange kiribaawo nti abeesigwa bange abaagalamidde mu Kristo balizuukizibwa okuva mu ntaana zaabwe n’abeesigwa bange abali abalamu abalisigalawo balikung’aanyizibwa wamu nabo ne mu ggulu ery’eggulu mwe muligenda, gye muliyimirira mwenna mu maaso g’entebe eyo ennene ey’omusango gwa Kristo mulyoke muweebwe empeera olw’ebyo bye mwakola nga muli mu mubiri. Bonna abatwalibwa okusemberera Bema wange tebajja eyo kusimbibwa musango lwa bibi byabwe kubanga mwakkiriza okusonyiyibwa ebibi mu kiseera ky’omuntu omwonoonyi, ye lunaku luno omuntu omwonoonyi lw’afuga ensi, era okufuga okwo kuli ku nkomerero. Kubanga ensi yaange, Kristo Yesu, n’okujjula kwayo, era nsembera era nja kuggyawo obusika bwange ku ng’aanyi okuva ku basenze n’omusango gwabwe omujjuvu tegulwawo. Mbagalwa bange, mutegeere nti muvudde mu kufa ne muyingira butereevu mu bulamu obutaggwaawo mu nze, Kristo Yesu Mukama wammwe. Era nze mbanaazizza mu bujjuvu okuva mu kibi mu musaayi gwange ogutangirira kubanga nze Okuyitako kwammwe okutuukiridde eyeezaalayo eri Yahweh Katonda omukulu ow’emirembe gyonna, omulundi gumu, byonna, n’emirembe gyonna olw’ebibi by’omuntu: n’olwekyo, tewakyali ssaddaaka ya bibi.

N'olwekyo, abaana ba Katonda balisemberera entebe yange ey’omusango nsobole okusala omusango gw’ebikolwa byabwe buli muntu alyoke aweebwe empeera olw’ebyo by’akoze, ka bibe birungi oba bibi. Era bwe mutyo mwenna mulireeta ssaddaaka z’obulamu bwammwe obwanunulibwa ku nsi bwe mwawaayo mu kuweereza Mukama wammwe ow’emirembe gyonna. Nga bwe kyawandiikibwa, bwe kityo bwe kirikolebwa, nga bwe kyawandiikibwa: Kubanga tewali muntu ayinza kuteekawo musingi mulala, okuggyako ogwateekebwawo, ye Yesu Kristo. Era omuntu yenna bw’azimba ku musingi guno, zaabu, ffeeza, amayinja ag’omuwendo, embaawo, essubi, oba ebisambu, Omulimu gwa buli muntu gulirabisibwa: kubanga olunaku luligulaga, kubanga gulibikkulibwa n’omuliro: n’omuliro gulikema omulimu gwa buli muntu nga bwe guli. Omulimu gw’omuntu gwonna gw’azimbiddeko bwe gunaasigalangawo, anaafunanga empeera. Omulimu gw’omuntu gwonna bwe gunaayokyebwanga, anaafunanga okufiirwa, naye ye yennyini alirokolebwa: naye ng’ayitidde mu muliro.

Bwe kityo bwe kiriba mbagalwa bange nti nja kubasasula mu bujjuvu olw’okuweereza kwammwe gye ndi n’ebintu ebyokeddwa ng’ebisusunku n’omuliro gwange ogw’omusango mulifuna okufiirwa ate ebyo ebinaagumira omuliro gwange ogw’omusango, ye mirimu gye nabawa okukola, empeera ennene eneeba yammwe. Kubanga kyawandiikibwa nti: Eriiso teryalaba, so n’okutu tekyawulira, so n’ebyo tebyayingira mu mutima gw’omuntu, ebyo Katonda bye yategekera abamwagala. Era bwe gityo obusika bwammwe n’empeera yammwe bwe biri, nti tewali muntu ku nsi ategeera mu bujjuvu obukulu n’ekitiibwa eby’obusika bw’abaana ba Katonda ab’emirembe gyonna abazaalibwa omulundi ogw’okubiri ng’omwoyo gwa Kristo bwe guli, mwe muzaalibwa omulundi ogw’okubiri nga muli baana ba Katonda emirembe gyonna.

Kale mutegeere mbagalwa bange nti olunaku lwammwe olw’okununulibwa olujjuvu luli kumpi nnyo okusinga bwe mulowooza, era lubaddukira mu kiseera kino ddala era olunaku olwo lunaatera okutuukirizibwa, mangu okusinga bwe musuubira. Mutegeere nti tewakyali kiseera kya kulabula, tewakyali kiseera kya kwegayirira, tewakyali kiseera kya kuteesa ne Babulooni ow’ekyama kubanga omusango gwe gwa nkomerero era tegusobola kukyusibwa. Nedda, ebiseera bye eby’okwenenya byayonoonebwa, era yasuulirira ddala amaloboozi ga bannabbi bange n’abatume bange ng’agamba mu mutima gwe ng’asekerera ebigambo byange nti “Ntuula nga kabaka omukyala so siri nnamwandu, enkya eneeba nga leero era leero y’emu ne jjo.”

Naye mu ssaawa emu akabi ke kalimugwako mu bujjuvu, so taddemu kugolokoka. Naye mu kifo ky’eggwanga eddene eryali lissa ekitiibwa mu makubo ga Yahweh era abantu gye baagobereranga Yesu Omunazaaleeti ne batakwatibwa nsonyi kwagala baliraanwa baabwe, walisigalawo ddongo Babulooni ow’Ekyama w’alizikirizibwa mu ssaawa emu kubanga Mukama Katonda amusala omusango alina amaanyi. Era ng’akabi ako bwe kasembera, mbagalwa bange, n’okununulibwa kwammwe okujjuvu, obulokozi, empeera, n’essanyu ery’emirembe gyonna bwe biri, kale temweraliikirira olw’omusango ogusemberera ogw’aboonoonyi ogutali gwammwe singa muva mu bibi byammwe mu bujjuvu.

Era okutemya okwo kw’eriiso kusembera, mu kaseera katono we muneesanga nga muyimiridde mu kisenge eky’entebe ya Yahweh yennyini entebe zammwe ez’obwakabaka we ziteekeddwa n’okwambazibwa obulungi era gye mutuula nga bakabaka ab’emirembe gyonna era bakabona olw’obusika bwa Mukama wammwe ow’emirembe gyonna, mwe mulifugira amawanga n’omuggo ogw’ekyuma kubanga bwe gityo bwe gyaweebwa nze, era bwe ntyo bwe nja okuwa abo abawangula. Bawanguzi bange be baani? Beebo abaava mu kibi mu bujjuvu nga beenenya mu bujjuvu ne beegaana ebibi byabwe. Era nze Kristo Yesu nze yawangula enkwe za sitaani mu bujjuvu era emirembe gyonna, ne mmuleka ng’omulabe omuzikirize era omuwangule ddala enninniro z’omuliro ez’okubonerezebwa kwe zigootaanyizibwa era nga zeetegefu okumukkiriza, kubanga obwo bwe busika bwabwe era bwe batyo bwe bagwanidde.

N’olwekyo, nkolonze ekkubo eddene eri bonna abajja gye ndi mu bujjuvu okuwangula mu bujjuvu enkwe za sitaani mu ngeri y’emu nange. Kale mujje gye ndi mu bujjuvu nsobole okubakulembera mu kkubo eryo eggololofu era effunda okutuuka ku bulokozi bwammwe obujjuvu. Muwulire eddoboozi lyange mmwe abaserengeta ne mugwa mu kkubo kubanga omuntu omutuukirivu agwa emirundi musanvu, n’agolokoka nate, kale mugolokoke mmwe abasajja ba Katonda mujje gye ndi mu bujjuvu mbasobozese okuddamu okubakwataganya n’empeera yammwe nga mbanaaza mu musaayi gwange ogutangirira mulyoke muyimirire nga mwambadde mu bujjuvu obutuukirivu bwange. Mutegeere nti abawanguzi bange balisikira byonna so tewali kirungi kiribaggibwako. Era bwe kityo abawanguzi bange, balyaka emirembe gyonna, n’okwaka ng’okw’ebbanga: nammwe abakyusizza abangi okujja mu butuukirivu, mulyaka ng’emmunyeenye, emirembe n’emirembe. Kale kaakano nga mumanyi kino baana bange nti kaakano kye kiseera okuleka okweraliikirira kw’ensi eno ne muwummulira mu nze ne musigala nga muli balongoofu ng’omuwala mu musaayi gwange ogutangirira.

N’olwekyo, mbagalwa bange munywerere mu maaso g’akabi akajja, kubanga mulaba olunaku olwo olw’entiisa nga lusembera, era nze nzigudde amaaso g’abawombeefu mu mutima mulyoke mulabe amazima g’ensi yammwe so temugwa mu kulimbibwa okw’amaanyi okutali kwammwe. Mutegeere nti nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, nze aleetera abazibe b’amaaso okulaba, n’olwekyo, mujje gye ndi mbasiige eddagala mu maaso mulyoke mulabe ekitundu ky’ensi eno embi nga bwe kiri ddala mwe mubeera. Kale temutya musango gwa boonoonyi kubanga omusango ogwo si gwammwe mbagalwa bange, nedda omusango si gwa banunule kubanga mulokoddwa okuva ku busungu bw’Omwana gw’endiga n’okuva ku busungu bwa Katonda, n’olwekyo munywerere nga muli bukuumi mu bulokozi bwammwe mbagalwa bange kubanga olunaku luli kumpi era lubaddukira buli kaseera.

Weewaawo, akaseera ako ak’okununulibwa kwammwe okujjuvu kubatuuseeko mbagalwa bange era akaseera ako ak’emirembe gyonna kali kumpi era kajja gye muli buli kaseera. Nkakasizza mu myoyo gyammwe, nnoonyezza obubonero waggulu n’obubonero mu nsi, ndeese eby’amagero mu ggulu era nsumuludde n’obutyabaga mu kusala omusango ku boonoonyi n’okuzuukusa abo abeebase balyoke bategeere nti kaakano kye kiseera eky’okununulibwa okujjuvu kw’abaana ba Katonda Ayinzabyonna, ye mugole wange n’omubiri gwange.

Temwegattananga nabo mu makubo gaabwe ag'obutakkiriza, kubanga banoonya okwezza n'okunnyonnyola obubonero ebingi n'ebyamagero ebiragibwa mu ggulu, ebingi ku byo byakubibwa n'olunwe lwa Yahweh yennyini. Mwerabidde Olubereberye, abaagalwa bange? Kubanga kyawandiikibwa nti: Katonda n'agamba nti, Wabeewo eby'okuva mu bbanga ery'eggulu, okwawulamu emisana n'ekiro; era bibeerenga bya bubonero, n'ebiro, n'ennaku n'emyaka. Era bibeerenga bya kutangaaza mu bbanga ery'eggulu okuwa omusana ku nsi: bwe kityo bwe kyaba. N'olwekyo, mutegeere nti Yahweh y'afuga byonna era tewali kintu kyonna ekikkirizibwa ku kitonde kye nga takikkirizza, era nga takimanyi. N'olwekyo, temwegattanga nabo mu kutebereza kwabwe okw'obutakkiriza nti omuntu kaakano afuga era ayinza okukyusa ebiri mu ggulu, newaakubadde ddala omuntu agezaako okuvuganya Katonda, naye enfuba ze zonna zikoma mu butaliimu.

Naye, abo abaagwa bagezaako okukisa obubonero bwange n'ebirango ebingi Yahweh bye yateeka mu ggulu eby'embaga y'omugole omusajja n'omugole we omukazi. N'olwekyo, musanyukire ekitiibwa kya Katonda, nga mumanyi nti kyonna abo abaagwa kye bakkirizibwa okukola, kiweereza bigendererwa bya Katonda ataggwaawo eby'olubeerera, kubanga amakubo ge bwe gaty' n'abalabe be ne bawulira eddoboozi lye. Naye temubula, Yahweh y'afuga byonna era tewali kibaawo nga takikkirizza mu kisa kye, mu kusaasira kwe, ne mu musango gwe ogw'enkomerero ogw'olubeerera ku abo abateenenya n'abaagwa. Okutuusa ne ku musango ogwo omunene ogwalagulwa edda ennyo oguli kumpi okutyoboola ensi yonna n'amaanyi amangi.

Era olunaku olwo lubatuuseeko, abaagalwa bange, era NZE NZIJA OKUBANONA! Newaakubadde abamenyi b'amateeka bandinoonyezza okukisa ekitiibwa kyange, ekitiibwa kyange sirikigabanako na mulala, n'olwekyo enfuba z'ebintu ebinafu eby'obwavu tezirina mugaso mu kukisa kulabika kwa Kristo Yesu eri omugole wange n'omubiri gwange. Nedda, siyinza kuziyizibwa wadde okukisibwa n'akatono, era ensi yonna erimanya okulabika kwange, newaakubadde abo abaagwa balinoonya okukisa okulabika kwange nga bayita mu ntalo n'okulumika kwabwe okulimba okw'ebiwanga by'ebweru. Mutegeere nti tewali biwanga bya bweru ebiva mu nsi endala ezikyuka mu bukyamu, wabula abo abaagwa abagobebwa mu ggulu emirembe n'emirembe abeefuula, kubanga bonna befuula, abajja okukyusa n'okubuza.

Mutegeere nti okulabika kwange tekufaanana na kujja kwange okw'okubiri era kya kyama ekikisiddwa munda mu kyama ekinene ekya Katonda, nze Yeshua HaMashiach, kye nabikkulira omutume wange Pawulo. Okujja kwange okw'okubiri kumanyiddwa era kusuubirwa okuva edda, era kujjana n'okuzuukira kw'abatuukirivu. Ku kulabika kwange, njija kukuŋŋaanya abatukuvu bange gyendi, mbatuuseeko empeera yange era mpase omugole wange era nnywe n'abagenyi bange ab'ekitiibwa. Ku kulabika kwange kwe ndisimbula n'entwala gyendi abeesigwa abajja gyendi nga beenenyezza ddala ne mbatwala mu ggulu aw'emirembe okutuusa omusango ku nsi lwe guliggwaawo, kubanga ebiseera by'omusango bituuse ku nsi yonna naddala ku baagwa.

Mutegeere nti okujja kwange okw'okubiri kwe kujja n'abanunule bange era n'abange emitwalo kkumi n'ena mu enkumi nnya (144,000), n'eggye lyonna ery'omu ggulu okuzikiriza obwakabaka bwa Sitaani ddala, okuzikirizibwa okwo obwakabaka bwe bwe butaliddayo kuddamu. Era ku kujja kwange okw'okubiri buli muntu aliraba era alimanya, era tewali bukuusa buyinza kubula muntu yenna ku kikolwa kino. Sitaani si kabaka era n'abaagwa si bakatonda, wabula befuula abeefuula. Era Sitaani alisibwa era asuulibwe mu bunnya butaakoma okumala emyaka lukumi, aleme kukyusa mawanga nate, okutuusa lw'alisumululwa okumala akaseera ke akatono, era eyo gye walisigalira ng'asibiddwa mu njegere ennene ng'ali mu kubonyaabonyezebwa n'okutya okw'entiisa olw'obusika bwe mu nnyanja eyaka omuliro n'ekibiriiti, mw'ataliddayo kuva. Era oluvannyuma lw'akaseera ke akatono, alisuulibwa ddala mu nnyanja eyo eyaka emirembe n'emirembe ey'ekibiriiti ekiwunya ennyo ekyaka ennimiro ezisinga okwokya eza bbulu. Era olwo omusango gw'oku ntebe ey'obwakabaka enjeru ennyo ne gubaawo, mazima ddala nga Yahweh tannatonda ggulu ppya n'ensi nshya, n'afuula ebintu byonna bipya mu kutuukirizaganyizibwa okw'olubeerera era okutuukiridde.

Era bwe kityo bwe kyawandiikibwa, era bwe kityo bwe kiribaawo mu bbanga ttono nti ndikka okuva mu ggulu n'okwogerera waggulu n'eddoboozi lya malayika omukulu n'akagombe ka Katonda, era okuvuga okwo okw'amaanyi okw'akagombe tekulwawo, era ge makondeeka g'akagombe k'ekiseera kyange eky'ekisa, ng'ensi ekyuka okusanga omusango gwayo ogugijidde era omukakali. Era ndikuŋŋaanya abalonde bange okuva ku nsonda ennya ez'ensi, ye, bonna abaafa nga bali mu Kristo, yee bonna abaafa nga bali mu Kristo abassibwako akabonero k'omwoyo oyo omutukuvu ow'ekisuubizo. Era n'obulumi bw'okuzaala obutakoma bulireka ensi n'ezaala abo abaafa, naye abo okufa bwe kutayinza kubakwata na ddakiika emu. Era olwo mmwe abali abalamu ne musigalawo, mulitwalibwa mu bbanga nabo okunsisinkana mu bire, era mu kusekeenyangerera kw'eriiso muliraba.

Yee, muliraba n'amaaso gammwe ekitiibwa eky'olubeerera ekya Katonda omunene ow'emirembe gyonna, ye Kitange w'ebitangaala, eyeegomba okubeerawo kwammwe era eyategeka akaseera kano okuva emirembe gyonna kubanga mubadde mu mutima gwa Yahweh. Era mulindaba, Omulokozi wammwe ow'emirembe gyonna eyeezaayo yenna mu nnaku ennyingi bwe nneewaayo nga ssaddaaka mulyoke mubeere balamu mu maaso ga Kitange w'ebitangaala eyazaala ebitangaala by'ensi. Yee, mulindaba n'amaaso gammwe, ye, mmwe mwenna abalina okukkiriza mu Mukama wammwe ow'ekitiibwa gwe mutannalaba na maaso gammwe.

Muliraba n'amaaso gammwe ekitiibwa kyange eky'amaanyi Yahweh kye yampa mu busika bwange, bwe ndigabana ddala n'abawanguzi bange, era bwe ndisembera abo bonna abajja gyendi n'emirimu egiyita mu kugezesebwa kw'omuliro. Mazima ddala, ddala, muliraba n'amaaso gammwe eggye ly'omu ggulu, abangi ku bo abaatumibwa okuweereza abo abasika b'obulokozi obw'olubeerera. Era muliyimirira nga mutuukiridde ddala, nga mukoleddwa bupya mu butuukirivu obw'omu ggulu, era muliyimirira ddala ne mwewuunya enkomerero yammwe ey'olubeerera kye neetegeera, n'obugagga bw'ekitiibwa ky'obusika bwange mu batukuvu kye butegeeza gye muli.

Mutegeere abaagalwa bange, (ye mwenna abataganza bulamu bwammwe okutuuka ku nkomerero) ye mmwe abavudde mu bibi byammwe nga mweenenyezza ddala: Enkomerero yammwe ey'olubeerera y'entandikwa yammwe ey'olubeerera, mwe muliyimirira mu ddakiika yammwe esooka ey'obulamu obutaggwaawo, era tewali kiddayo kubonoonako n'akatono. Kubanga mulikolebwa bupya abatuukiridde mu butuukirivu obutukuvu, era temuliddayo kwonoonwa nate, kubanga mulikolebwa bupya abatavonooneka.

Era emirembe n'emirembe muliweereza Yahweh, era emirembe n'emirembe mulibeera nange wonna we ndigenda. Era ndibaddiza byonna enzige bye zaalya n'ebyo byonna Sitaani bye yababba. Mulisasulwa ddala olw'emirimu gyammwe n'okutegana kwammwe okw'okwagala kwe mwalaga eri erinnya lyange, bwe mwaweereza abatukuvu era ne muweereza. Tulirya ku masavu g'ensi mu kiseera ky'obufuzi bwange obw'emyaka olukumi, mwe mulifugira nange nga bakabaka bange era bakabona bange, era kuliba kufuga kwa butuukirivu emirembe n'emirembe.

Nze oyo eyabanaazaako ddala ekibi mu musaayi gwange ogwatangirira.

Nze Okuyitako kwammwe okutuukiridde eyeezaayo nze nzekka eri Yahweh Katonda omunene ow'emirembe gyonna.

Nze oyo eyazibula amaaso g'abawombeefu mu mutima.

Nze omuweesi abikutula amanda g'omuliro mulyoke mutuuke ku busika bwammwe obujjuvu mu nze, Kristo Yesu.
