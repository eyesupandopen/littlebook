Omujulirwa Omulamba: Isirayiri
Ekikaawa n'Ekiwoomu
Novemba 17, 2024

Laba! Empologoma y'omu Kika kya Yuda!

Zibula amatu go, nange nkwanzjulire amaaso go, ggwe Isirayiri, era zuukuka okuva mu buzibe bwo obw'ekitundu owulire eddoboozi lya Masiya wo ow'amazima, ye Empologoma y'omu Kika kya Yuda, kubanga ddala Nze wuuyo. Kale tegeera ebigambo bya Yeshua HaMashiach, ye wuuyo gw'olina naye ensonga, ggwe Isirayiri, kubanga ddala Nze wuuyo.

Mazima ddala, wulira ebigambo byange mmwe ab'oluganda lwange mulyoke mutegeere ebiseera bye mulimu, era mulyoke mutegeere essuubi ly'okuyitibwa kwammwe eribakomyawo mu nsi ey'ekisuubizo, kubanga Nze ssuubi ly'okuyitibwa kwammwe, era Nze mbayita, ggwe Isirayiri, mulyoke mutegeere ebigenda okujja, era n'obusungu obugenda okusumululwa mu biseera bino bye mulimu. Era Nze Empologoma y'omu Kika kya Yuda, era mu butuukirivu nzijja okusala omusango n'okulwana, kale tegeera obukakali bw'Ebigambo byange, byo Ebigambo by'Oyo gw'olina naye ensonga, kubanga Yakuwa yankola bw'atyo.

Kubanga Nze lwe Lwazi olwesittaza, lwe Lwazi olwo lwennyini olw'esittalo abatatya Katonda bonna kwe banaagwira, kubanga omutuukirivu takyabeerawo ku bwa Musa naye omutuukirivu aliba mulamu lwa kukkiriza! Kubanga Musa mubeeramu mu Masiya wammwe ow'amazima, ye oyo eyakwata obutiribiri amateeka ago yennyini Isirayiri g'atasobola kukwata, era na buli muntu wa Adamu yenna okugakwata olw'ekibi ekiri mu mibiri gyammwe egyayononeka. Era Nze wuuyo eyasasula omutango gwa Musa bwe nneewaayo ng'Omwana gw'endiga gwa Katonda eri Yakuwa okuba ssaddaaka ey'okutangirira emirembe gyonna, kubanga omutango gw'aboonoonyi tegwali gwange kusasula, naye mu kwagala kwange eri Yakuwa, n'okwagala kwange eri abantu nnasasula emitango gyammwe gyonna. N'emitango egy'okubonyaabonyezebwa ennyo, n'obulumi, n'okufa nnagisasula gyonna era Yakuwa yakkiriza ssaddaaka yange emirembe n'emirembe.

Kale tegeera, obusungu bw'Omwana gw'endiga bunaatera okusumululwa, n'amakondeere gategekeddwa okufuuwebwa, era laba, ebibya by'ekiruyi eky'obusungu bwa Katonda ddala kisemberedde era kituuse ku mulyango gwammwe, kyemunaava muwulira eddoboozi lyange, mmwe n'abaana bammwe mulyoke mubeere abalamu muwone omutego Baraba gw'atega abantu bange, ye mwana gw'endiga gw'omusota Esawu gwe yeerondedde okuba Masiya waabwe, era nga ye muzimbi w'ekisolo ow'omu yeekaalu. Era ajja kugolokoka ng'omwana gw'endiga alina amayembe abiri, era ajja kugabanya ensi okusingawo okuziggya ku bannannyinizo ab'ensonga abaagobebwawo olw'okuttibwa kw'ekika kyabwe, okugabira abasenze ab'ekivve okuva eri Esawu abaagirekaanya mu bukyamu, n'abalumbaganyi abaatumibwa omusota abazzukulu ba Asikenazi so si ba Yakobo naye ne beegatta ku Esawu n'empisa zaabwe ez'omu Talmud.

Era Nze wuuyo Yakuwa gwe yatuma ng'omwana gw'endiga gwa Katonda, ng'endagaano ey'emirembe etuukiridde, ng'Okuyitako kwammwe okutuukiridde era ssaddaaka ey'enkomerero olw'ebibi by'abantu omulundi gumu, eri bonna, emirembe n'emirembe so tekuli ssaddaaka ndala esigaddewo olw'ebibi. Era nnajja eri abange mu kwagala n'emirembe, abo bennyini abaagaana ssaddaaka yange, naye nnasindikibwa ng'Okuyitako okutuukiridde mu kuwulira okwagala kwa Yakuwa, era Nze mwana gw'endiga gwa Katonda, Yakuwa gwe yateekerateekera ebibi byammwe mu butuukirivu, kyemunaava muwulira eddoboozi lyange ggwe Isirayiri mutegeere nti sijja kukomawo nate ku nsi ng'omwana gw'endiga gwa Katonda, era emirembe gigenda kuggyibwa ku nsi mu musango ogujjidde. Gwe musango ogugenda okusumululwa amangu ago nga gusoose ku nnyumba ya Katonda kubanga omusango gutandikira ku nnyumba ya Katonda.

Kubanga abeesigwa mu Kristo balitwalibwa mu ggulu, n'abakayabaga balirekerwa omuliro ogulongoosa okuzikirizibwa kw'omubiri, omwoyo gulyoke gulokolebwe kyokka ng'ayise mu muliro. Era bangi nnyo, ebibiina by'abantu bingi birijja eri Yesu Omunazaaleeti okuva mu mawanga gonna n'ebika n'ennimi, nange ndibalongoosa mu butuukirivu era ndibaaniriza gyendi ne mbazza obuggya mu butuukirivu n'obutukuvu obw'amazima era empeera yaabwe terina kye bagigeraageranya n'okubonaabona kwe baliyitamu, kubanga obuzito bw'empeera yaabwe bulisinga nnyo ebyo abantu bye basuubira era balibeerawo emirembe gyonna mu mirembe emitukuvu n'obutukuvu n'obutuukirivu obw'amazima.

Kuba kyabawandiikibwako era bwe kityo bwe kiriba: Bano be bava mu kibonyoobonyo ekinene, ne boza engoye zaabwe enjeru mu musaayi gw'Omwana gw'endiga. Kyebava babeera mu maaso g'entebe ya Katonda, ne bamuweereza emisana n'ekiro mu Yeekaalu ye, n'oyo atuula ku ntebe alibeera mu bo. Tebaliddamu kulumwa njala, newakubadde ennyonta, so n'enjuba teribookya, newakubadde ebbugumu lyonna. Kubanga Omwana gw'endiga ali wakati mu ntebe, ye alibafuga, era alibakulembera okubatuusa ku nzizi z'amazzi amalamu, era Katonda alisiimuula amaziga gonna mu maaso gaabwe.

Kale tegeera nti Nze wuuyo eyamaliriza emirimu gyange mu b'amawanga era ebiseera byange eby'ekisa n'okusaasira biweddewo, newankubadde ekisa n'okusaasira biri nange emirembe gyonna, era Yakuwa yennyini yalangirira nti alisaasira gw'alisaasira, kale ekisa n'okusaasira ddala bya lubeerera. Mazima ddala, nnagenda eri ab'amawanga ng'omuwambe agaanyibwa abange abaatwalibwa Esawu mu buddu bw'ennono ze ez'okuvvoola ze banoonya okwonooneramu Ebigambo bya Yakuwa eby'olubeerera, okwo kwonooneka okutasoboka. Naye ne bafabiriza enzikiriza zaabwe ez'omuziro ng'emikono gy'abawandiisi baabwe bwe gyateeka ennono z'abantu ababi mu katabo akeeyita akatukuvu, naye akatabo k'okuvvoola kayinza kutya okuba akatukuvu? Mazima ddala, zibasanze abo abayita ekizikiza omusana n'omusana ekizikiza, kubanga omusango gwabwe tegulwawo!

N'ebiseera byange mu b'amawanga biweddewo kubanga bagudde mu bibi Kitaabwe by'eyagwaamu bwe baagaana omwana gw'endiga gwa Katonda Yakuwa gwe yabatumira nga tebagwanidde ng'endagaano ey'emirembe ey'olubeerera eri amawanga, mu go mwe mwava ekibiina ekinene eky'abantu abaafuna obulokozi. Era kati abo abandisobodde okufuuka abaana b'ekisuubizo mu Isirayiri wa Katonda beekuluntazza ku matabi ag'obutonde, ge matabi ag'omuzeyituuni ogw'obutonde kwe baagattibwa bwe baamenyebwawo mu kutakkiriza kuli kumu.

Naye baagibwa ku muzeyituuni ogw'omu nsiko, omuti ogutalina ssuubi newakubadde ekisuubizo, era ku abo abajja gyendi ne bamfuula Mukama waabwe ow'obutuukirivu ow'emirembe gyonna mu biseera eby'emirembe, eby'ekisa, n'okusaasira kati bafuuliddwa abaana mu Isirayiri wa Katonda era bakwata kinene mu bisuubizo ebyakolebwa ne Bakitaffe era bangi ku bano balimpeereza emirembe gyonna ng'omubiri gwange era ndibeetwalira okuba omugole wange era nditeeka akabonero ku 144,000 bange abeelongosezza mu musaayi gwange ogutangirira nga balongoofu ng'abawala abatannayonoonebwa malaya omukulu. Era mu kulokoka kw'ab'amawanga amawanga gaweebwa omukisa era ddala abo ab'ebika ebyabula abaawulira eddoboozi lyange bafunye obulokozi mu Mukama waabwe era balibeerawo emirembe gyonna mu bika mu Isirayiri wa Katonda! Kale tegeera, ndirokola abamu ku bonna okuva mu Adamu wonna we yatambulira ku nsi mu nnaku ze ez'okubonerezebwa.

Kubanga Yakuwa yanfuula omwoyo oguwa obulamu, era ng'omubiri bwe guli sikyamanyibwa. Mazima ddala, Nze wuuyo asazeewo kati omuzeyituuni ogw'omu nsiko olw'okutakkiriza kwabwe n'olw'okumenya endagaano yange, ye Ndagaano yange Empya. Era ekyo n'okuba nti balina okuba ne Mukama omu, Nze, Kristo Yesu Mukama wa bakama era Kabaka wa bakabaka era mu bufuzi bwange mwe muli essuubi lyabwe lyokka. Naye beetwalidde essuubi eddamu mu Baraba ne bateeka mu busirusiru essuubi lyabwe mu mukama omulimba eyalumba Ekanisa zaabwe n'amasuubi amalamu agaagala eby'obugagga, omulokozi omulimba asuubiza okulokola Babulooni ow'Ekyama naye nga mu kyama akolera kuzikirizibwa kwa Babulooni ow'Ekyama. Ye mulimba oyo yennyini ekisolo ekyava mu nsi ekirigolokoka ng'omulimba Masiya wa Esawu, ye mwana gw'endiga gw'omusota aligolokoka ng'alina amayembe abiri. Bw'etyo Ekanisa yange eyalumbibwa eya Lawodikiya yakyama era kati ekkiriziganya n'okufa kwayo, kubanga Baraba aleeta kufa kwokka, era alizikiririza ddala omulaya oyo omukulu, ye Babulooni ow'Ekyama, omusango gwe gujje olw'obubi bwe obungi n'obwononefu bw'ayonoonye nakyo ensi mu bwenzi bwe.

Bwe batyo mu bujeemu beerondedde oyo yennyini abaleetera okuzikirira okuba omulokozi waabwe ow'okulimba, kubanga Ekkuŋŋaniro lya Setaani balimba bonna abeerimbalimba. Kale yiga, ggwe Isirayiri, ekyo ddala Baraba ky'anaatera okutuukiriza kubanga abaguzizza mu mikono gy'abalabe baabwe, era alirimba okufuna ekiwundu ku mutwe gwe n'ekweeka mu ttaka okwewala ebibonyoobonyo ebinaatera okugwa. Era aligolokoka mu bulimba obungi ng'omulimba Masiya eri Isirayiri kubanga balifuna olutalo lwabwe, lwe lwabwe olw'okusatu, era lwe lulisinga obubi ku zonsatule. Bwe kityo, kubanga bwe kyawandiikibwa era bwe kirikolebwa: Ne ndaba ekisolo nga kiva mu nnyanja, nga kirina emitwe musanvu, n'amayembe kkumi, ne ku mayembe gaakyo engule kkumi, ne ku mutwe gwakyo erinnya ery'okuvvoola. N'ekisolo kye nnalaba kyali kifaanana ng'engo, n'ebigere byakyo ng'ebya eddubu, n'akamwa kaakyo ng'akamwa k'Empologoma: n'omusota ne gukiwa amaanyi gaagwo n'entebe yaagwo, n'obuyinza obungi. Ne ndaba omutwe gwakyo gumu nga gufudde olw'ekiwundu, naye ekiwundu kyakyo eky'okufa ne kiwona, n'ensi yonna ne yeewuunya ne bagoberera ekisolo.

Bwe kityo ebigambo ebiri mu byawandiikibwa bibikkula obulimba bwe, byo Ebigambo bye nnawa Yokaana Omubikkulirwa emyaka mingi egiyise. Era kirirabika nga Baraba alifuna ekiwundu ku mutwe, naye alirimba era anaddagaza ensi erikkiriza ddala obubonero bwe n'ebyamagero bye eby'obulimba kubanga bwe kityo bwe kyamuwebwa okusika bonna abasanyukira obutali butuukirivu gy'ali mu bulimba obw'amaanyi, kubanga ye mwononefu waabwe era omuzikiriza waabwe. Era mu mikono gye aleeta okulumwa kw'omusota, okwo kucumita okw'ekisolo, era balitabula ekyuma n'ebbumba newankubadde tebirigattagana, naye bw'atyo omwana gw'endiga gw'omusota bwe guligolokoka. Era aligolokokera ku sistemu y'ekisolo ey'enkomerero ey'oyo eyagwa, obwakabaka bwabwe obw'enkomerero obulifuga ensi okuva mu nsi ey'ekisuubizo. Naye taligolokoka yekka, wabula aligolokoka wamu n'ekisolo eky'omu nnyanja, era balifuga omukago gwabwe ogw'abakabaka abawadde omusota amaanyi gaabwe gonna n'obuyinza kubanga bonna basinza omusota era gy'ali gye beewaddeyo ddala kubanga ye Kitaabwe, ye oyo eyasiga omuddo gwe omubi mu ŋŋaano ya Yakuwa, nange ndigukuulira ddala ne ngusuula mu muliro.

Bw'atyo omwana gw'endiga gw'omusota bwe guligolokoka mu bulimba obujjuvu n'obubonero n'ebyamagero bingi eby'obulimba by'aliraga okukakasa obunnabbi bwe obw'obulimba, era alyogera eby'okwekuluntaza n'okuvvoola bingi ku ye n'okwefuula nti ye Katonda. N'ekisolo ekimu kiriraga ku kirala era balifugira wamu, ddala ng'entegeka z'omusota bwe ziri z'alifugisiza obwakabaka bwe obubi okumala akaseera katono. Era balifuula ebintu byonna eby'Ekikristaayo okuba ebitemanyizibwa mateeka, era baligaana ebiwandiiko ebitukuvu, era kiweereddwa mu mikono gyabwe okulwana n'abatukuvu n'okubawangula, era bwe batyo bwe balituukiriza. Naye baligatta amadiini g'ensi yonna bonna balyoke basinze 'black cubes' zaabwe kubanga ku bigere bya Setaani gye beegattidde mu bujeemu obw'enkomerero era tebaliwona mukono gwange ogw'amaanyi, bw'ayogera Masiya wammwe ow'emirembe gyonna, ye Yeshua HaMashiach!

Naye mu bukyayi bwabwe eri ebintu byonna ebikwata ku Kristo Yesu balireetera obulokozi okujja ku kibiina ekinene ky'abantu be balitta, abo abalikoowoola erinnya lya Yesu Omunazaaleeti okutuusa ku mukka gwabwe ogw'enkomerero nange ndibawa amaanyi ne mbawa essuubi eddene, era balijja gyendi ddala, abo bonna abasikira obulokozi obw'emirembe, so tewali ayinza okubakwakkula mu mikono gyange naabo bonna amannya gaabwe ge gawandiikiddwa mu kitabo ky'obulamu eky'omwana gw'endiga balilokolebwa. Kale temweraliikirira baagalwa bammwe abalabika ng'ababuze, kubanga bangi ku bo bali mu mikono gyange egy'amaanyi era nkyalibaleeta mu bulokozi mu Kristo Yesu era mu lulimi lwonna lwe balikoowoola erinnya lyange ery'amaanyi ndibawulira!

Naye, bwe kiriba nti omwana gw'endiga omulimba alina amayembe abiri aligolokoka okufuga ensi okuva mu nsi ey'ekisuubizo Esawu gy'anoonya okwekaanya ng'eyiye, naye okwekaanya kwe kwa bukyamu era obusika bwa Yakobo bwa nkalakkalira emirembe gyonna, bwe busika obwo Esawu bwe yatunda olw'emmere entono. Kubanga Esawu yeegasse ku abo abaagwa era yeegasse ku Asikenazi abeetwalidde emikolo n'empisa z'Omukanani omubi naye ne balimba ne beekaanya nti baava mu Isirayiri so si bwe kiri. Kubanga Kanani ye yayagala ennyo emize gy'abo abaagwa oluvannyuma lw'amataba amangi, bwe yatyo n'akyusa emirembe gye gyonna okugituusa mu kubonerezebwa okw'emirembe kubanga bonna abeekyusa mu buzaale okuva mu kifaananyi kya Katonda kye baatondebwamu tebalina kisonyiwo kubanga ezo ze ngeri za Setaani Esawu ze yakodifayinga mu kitundu mu Talmud ye ey'okuvvoola omulimba gwe mwe gusibuka.

Bwe batyo Asikenazi, ava mu Yafeesi, yeegasse ne Esawu mu kwekaanya kwe okw'obulimba, mwe banoonyeza okubba obusika bwa Yakobo okuva mu baana b'ekisuubizo. Naye, okufuba okwo kwa bwereere era tekuliyimirirawo kubanga Nze wuuyo Yakuwa gw'awadde ebintu byonna mu mikono gyange egy'amaanyi era egisobola. Kale tegeera nti Nze wuuyo eyasikira ebintu byonna ebyasuubizibwa Yakobo era Nze Masiya wammwe ow'amazima, era mu mikono gyange mwe muli empeera yammwe ggwe Isirayiri era mu mikono gya Baraba okuzikirira kwammwe okw'emirembe gyonna kwe kulindiridde, kyemunaava mutamwegattako!

N'olwekyo, mutegeere nti empeera z'ebisuubizo ebyakolebwa eri Bakadde baffe ab'edda zinywezezza mu mikono gyange, era tewali n'omu ayinza kubba ebyange eby'ensonga, kubanga bwe butyo obuyinza bwe nnaweebwa NZE ALIWO omukulu, era obusika buno nja kubuwa baana ba kusuubiza, be batuukirivu abalamu olw'okukkiriza mu Mwana wa Yakuwa, eyabaagala ne yeewaayo olw'obulokozi obutaggwaawo obw'abaana b'ekisuubizo. N'olwekyo, ggwe Isirayiri, nzikiriza nga Masiya wo, ggwe n'abaana bo mube balamu era mulongoose mu kkubo. Era nja kubakuuma n'okubalinda mulyoke mutuuke ku mugabo gwammwe omujjuvu ogw'obusika mu bwakabaka bwange obw'emyaka olukumi obuli ku mulyango gwammwe. N'olwekyo, wulira okukoowoola kwange, ggwe Isirayiri! Kubanga nkukoowoola mweweeyo wansi w'omukono gwange ogw'amaanyi, nammwe mulyoke mufuuke abaana b'ekisuubizo, kubanga Musa akuumibwa mu bujjuvu olw'okukkiriza mu nze, Yeshua HaMashiach. N'olwekyo, muwummulire mu bikolwa byange, muleke ebyammwe.

Kale nno Asikenazi yeegasse ne Esawu mu bye beewanako eby'obulimba, bye banoonyeza okubba obusika bwa Yakobo okuva ku baana b'ekisuubizo. Naye, okufuba okwo kwa bwereere era tekulinywera, kubanga nze oyo Yakuwa gw'awadde ebintu byonna mu mikono gyange egy'amaanyi era egisobola. N'olwekyo, mutegeere nti nze eyasikira ebintu byonna ebyasuubizibwa Yakobo, era nze Masiya wammwe ow'amazima, era mu mikono gyange mulimu empeera yammwe, ggwe Isirayiri, ne mu mikono gya Baraba mulimu okuzikirira kwammwe. N'olwekyo, mutegeere okulonda kwe mulina okukola, n'oyo gwe mulina naye ensonga, kubanga nze eyatumibwa Yakuwa olw'obulokozi bwammwe, ye oyo Musa gwe yalagira okugondera, mmwe ab'oluganda lwange abatagondera.

Kale, nga bwe kyawandiikibwa ku bo, bwe kityo bwe kiribatuukirizibwako n'omukono gwange ogw'amaanyi, kubanga Yakuwa yabikkulira bannabbi be amazima ga Esawu mu nnaku z'ebyonoono byabwe ebibadde ebingi. Kubanga kyawandiikibwa edda nti: Olw'obukambwe bwo eri muganda wo Yakobo, ensonyi zirikubikkako, era olizikirizibwa emirembe gyonna. Bwe wayimirira ku luuyi olulala, ku lunaku abagenyi lwe baatwala ebintu bye, n'abagenyi ne bayingira mu miryango gye, ne bakuba akalulu ku Yerusaalemi, nawe wali ng'omu ku bo.

N'olwekyo, mutegeere ggwe Isirayiri nti okwawukana okw'enkomerero okwa Yakobo ne Esawu kutuuse ku mmwe, era nzija kwoza ensi ey'ekisuubizo okuva mu mikono gy'abagwi, era Esawu alisimulibwa ddala n'emiramwa, era ku Esawu kyawandiikibwa nti: Era ennyumba ya Yakobo eriba muliro, n'ennyumba ya Yusufu eriba nnimi z'omuliro, n'ennyumba ya Esawu eriba ng'ebisusunku; era balibookyamu ne babookya; so tewaliba nsige ya nnyumba ya Esawu: kubanga Mukama y'akyogedde. Kale mutegeere mmwe ab'omu lulyo lwa Esawu abasoma Ebigambo byange, Yakuwa ky'alangiridde tewali n'omu ayinza kukikyusa era enkomerero yammwe eriba ya lubeerera era mulisimulibwa ddala n'emiramwa so temuliddamu kubeerawo.

Kubanga nze Mpologoma ey'omu kika kya Yuda era mu butuukirivu nzija kusala musango n'okulwana olutalo. N'olwekyo, wuliriza Ebigambo byange ggwe Isirayiri mwekweke olunaku olubatuuseeko. Mwekweke omusajja ow'ekibi ajja gye muli, kubanga Baraba ajja n'okufa okutaggwaawo mu mikono gye ng'akuweereddwa ng'obujjanjabi obw'ekyamagero obusuubiziddwa mu bulimba okuwa obulamu obutaggwaawo. Naye tebaliba na bulamu bwa kubeeramu mu kufa okw'okubiri wabula okuzikirizibwa okw'olubeerera mu nnimi z'omuliro ezisinga okwokya ez'okubonerezebwamu, awalulimi lwabwe we lutafiira.

Nze oyo alimenya obubonero ku muzingo gwange ogw'obusika, ye ekyapa ky'ensi kyennyini kye nnagula mu musaayi gwange ogutangirira okuva mu mukono gwa Yakuwa yennyini. Era nzija kusimbula abasenze ab'empaka n'abo bonna abagwi be baatuma, era n'abagwi bennyini n'ebiseera byabwe biweddeko. Kale, mutegeere ebigenda okujja, Isirayiri, kubanga baliteekawo obwakabaka bwabwe era balirabika ng'abawanguzi era baliraga obubonero n'ebyamagero bingi eby'obulimba balyoke balimbe bonna abasanyukira obutali butuukirivu. Era balilowooza nti batandiseewo emyaka gyabwe egya zaabu gye beegwanyiza okuteekawo, kubanga ba Kitaabwe omulyolyomi, era omulyolyomi ye gujwa guli omunene, n'olwekyo, okwegomba kwa gujwa kwe balituukiriza.

Naye, tebaliba na myaka gya zaabu wabula omusango omukakali ennyo, ogwo omusango ogulijja mangu abaana bange abeesigwa mu Kristo nga bakuŋŋaanyizibbwa gye ndi mu bire bwe ndirabika ensi kw'erirabira. Naye, balirimba ne bagamba ensi nti abazigu abava mu ggulu babbye bangi okuva ku nsi era nti balidda n'ensi erina okweteekateeka okulwanyisa abalumbaganyi abo abeefuula. Naye mu mazima beetegekera olutalo lw'olunaku olwo olunene, lwe lunaku lw'okujja kwange okw'okubiri, lwe lunaku ennyonyi ez'omu bbanga lwe ziriira ennyama y'abakabaka baabwe abagwa ab'oku nsi era bwe kityo bwe kiriba.
