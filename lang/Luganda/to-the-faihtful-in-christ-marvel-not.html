Ekikaawa Ekirimu Obuwoomu
Gatonnya 15, 2024

Eri Ab’Amazima Muri Kristo: Temwewuunya!

Temwewuunya, baganda bange, temwewuunya, newankubadde ensi eno ebakyawa. Mumanye nti ensi yansooka kunkyawa nga tennabakyawa mmwe, baagalwa bange, era omuweereza tasinga mukama we. N’olwekyo, mutegeere ebiseera bye mulimu era musembere ddala mu mitima gyammwe eri Yeshua HaMashiach, kubanga mbayita okusemberera ennyo, mmwe ekkanisa yange, omubiri gwange, era n'omugole wange, era mbayita okusemberera n'okusingawo mu biseera bino ebigenda okubakulembera okutwalibwa mu ggulu, mulyoke muwangule ebintu byonna mu bujjuvu muri Kristo.

Era bwe mutyo mu kibwatukira era nga temusuubira, mujja kutwalibwa mu ggulu, kubanga njija mu ssaawa gye mutasuubira. Laba! Njija ng'omubbi ekiro! Naye ng'ennaku za Nuuwa bwe zaali, bwe kityo n'okujja kw'Omwana w'omuntu bwe kuliba. Kubanga ng'ennaku ezaaliwo oluberyeberye lw'amataba bwe zaali, baalyanga ne banywa, ne bawasa, ne bafumbiza, okutuusa olunaku Nuuwa lwe yayingira mu lyato, so tebaamanya kintu kyonna, okutuusa amataba lwe gajja, ne gabatwala bonna; bwe kityo n'okujja kw'Omwana w'omuntu bwe kuliba.

Kale mbawadde amagezi, baagalwa bange, okwawukana n'okweraliikirira kw'ensi eno, nga mumanyi nti okugenda kwammwe okuva mu nsi eno embi kuli kumpi. Naye, kityo bwe kiri nti mukyalina obulamu bwe muteekwa okubeerawo, era mulina okweyongerayo okutuusa lwe mutalibaawo, kubanga bwe mulitwalibwa mu ggulu mu kibwatukira n'omukono gwange ogw’amaanyi ogw’obulokozi, emirimu gyammwe gijja kuba giwedde ddala era obulamu bwammwe ku nsi bujja kuba bukomye mu kiseera kino. Kale mbawa amagezi, baana bange abato, mubeere mu bulamu bwammwe nga mugondera mu bujjuvu eddoboozi lya Mukama wammwe era Omulokozi wammwe, ye Kristo Yesu Mukama wammwe eyalinnya mu ggulu, nange ndibaleetera okuwangula byonna mu bujjuvu!

N’olwekyo, bwe mubeera mu bulamu bwammwe, mumpe byonna byammwe mu kwemalira n'ebiruubirirwa by'emitima gyammwe kubanga nze kumala kwammwe mu byonna. Temulowooza kwogera mu mitima gyammwe nti muli balungi era muli bagagga lwa maanyi g’emikono gyammwe, wadde olwa ddiguli z'amatendekero ezimu ku mmwe ze musinga okutwala ng'ez'omuwendo okusinga okumanya Yahweh, era obulimba obwatundibwa gye muli mu kifo ky'amazima mu misomo gyammwe egy'obulimba egyazimbibwa kw'ago, naye mmwe mu busirusiru ne mwegulumiza mu busobozi bwammwe obw'okulowoozebwa bwe mwafuna bwe mutyo. Kale mbagamba nate, muve wakati mu bo mweyawule mu bintu byonna, oba muligabana nabo mu misango gyabwe n'ebibonerezo byabwe ekintu ekyo bwe kitaba kya nfunza olw'okuba mu biseera byonna eby'okakano mbadde mbayita era mbeegayirira okujja gye ndi mu bujjuvu mu kwagala kwange eri mmwe baana bange. Kale muve ku malala agatalina mugaso okumanya kwe kuleeta!

N’olwekyo, mbategeeza nti kyonna kye mufunye mu bulamu buno ekikwata ku bintu by'ensi eno kiweereddwa olw'okusiima n'okwagala okulungi n'ekisa kya Yahweh, Nze Aliwo Omukulu, ayagala okusinga byonna okuba abalungi n'okuba n'obulamu obulungi ng'emmeeme yammwe bw'eri. Naye, temuba basiru nnyo okulowooza nti mwekoze mwekka oba nti mu bitendo byammwe eby'ensi mukkirizibwa mu maaso ga Yahweh, Nze Aliwo Omukulu wadde Yesu Kristo, kubanga tewali mubiri gwonna gutuukirira mu maaso gaffe. Kale mutegeere nti abo abeegulumiza mu ngeri eyo bajja kufiirwa nnyo olw'amalala gaabwe, ag'amazima gakulembera okuzikirira okw'amaanyi.

Ku lwammwe, mbeegayirira muve mu nsi eno n'enneyisa yaayo yonna etasaana ya kwe tuukiriza, okwekoza, okweteza etasaana n'okwenyumiriza kw'omubiri. Kubanga, mazima ddala, singa ekisa kya Yahweh, Nze Aliwo Omukulu, tekyajjudde mu kwagala kwe okutaggwaawo eri mmwe, era waakiri aw'okwagala kwe n'ekitangaala kye ebitaggwaawo byamasidde mu mitima gyammwe ne mu bulamu bwammwe mu maaso ga Yesu Kristo: mwenna mwandibadde nga Sodoma ne Ggomola so temwandibaddewo kikolo wadde ettabi, n'olwekyo okwenyumiriza kwammwe kubeere muri Kristo. Naye, omukisa ogwo si gwammwe, baagalwa bange, n'olwekyo, mu kunjagala kwammwe mugondere eddoboozi lyange mulyoke muwangule ebintu byonna kubanga bwe muliba abawulize eri eddoboozi lyange, ndibaleetera okuwangula!

Kale bwe kityo bwe kiri, era bwe kityo bwe kiriba, nti abawanguzi bange balina empeera nnyingi, era n'abo abawangudde mu ngeri y'emu nange, era empeera yaabwe y'eneeba esinga obunene. Naye, bangi ku beesigwa bange muri Kristo bakemebwa ne bakwasibwa, nga bwe kiba, okuddayo eri abafu! Era mu maaso g'ebiragiro byange bye nawa Pawulo emyaka mingi egiyise mu bbaluwa ye eri Abefeeso, era bwe kityo yawandiika, era bwe ntyo mbajjukiza baagalwa bange: N'olwekyo ayogera nti, Ggwe eyeebase, zuukuka, oyimuke ove mu bafu, ne Kristo anaakuwa omusana. Mukeke nno okutambulanga n'amagezi, si nga abasiru, naye ng'ab'amagezi, nga mununula akaseera: kubanga ennaku mbi. N'olwekyo, temuba basiru, naye mutegeere Katonda by'ayagala.

Mutegeera Mukama wammwe by'ayagala, baana bange abato? Ye mutima gwammwe gwonna? Oba mweheereza mwekka mu kwesiga kw'emitima gyammwe, nga munoonya eby'obugagga ebirala okwongera ku byammwe buli lunaku? Mutaddewo emitima gyammwe mu kuweereza ebibiina, oba kkampuni z'abantu aboonoonyi ku lw'okunoonya ssente ezitali nongoofu, era mu kiseera ng'abantu be ssente ze bakung'aanya ne batereka mu bubi bo bennyini zijja kulangirirwa nga ezitali za mugaso mu maaso gaabwe? Oba mweyongeddeyo mu kuwulira eddoboozi lyange era ne mufumiitiriza bulungi buli Kigambo kye mbawa ku lw'obulokozi bwammwe? Bwe muneeyongera okuwulira eddoboozi lyange ndibaleetera okuwangula ebintu byonna, n'olwekyo, temuggwaamu maanyi!

Nkyabeegayirira, mmwe mwenna ab'amazima muri Kristo n'abo bonna abanaafuuka ab'amazima muri Kristo okweyongera okunkyukirira kubanga mbayita okusemberera ennyo n'okusingawo ndyoke njijjuze okwagala kwange eri mmwe okuba n'amaanyi n'okubawanirira mu byonna, n'olwekyo, temutya, wadde okumalira ebiseera mu kwenenya. N'olwekyo, mweyongere okuba abanyiikivu mu kwagala kwammwe n'okwesiga emitima gyammwe eri Mukama wammwe Yesu Kristo, baana bange abato, kubanga tewakyali biseera bya kumala! Era ekkondeere bwe lirivuga muliba mu bawala abasiru abalirekebwa mu muliro ogulongosa, oba mulisangibwa mu bawala ab'amagezi abalitwalibwa mu ggulu n'omugole waabwe buli ssekonda kati. Muliba baani era muliyimirira wa bwe ndirabika mu kibwatukira buli ssekonda kati?

Mujja kunkkiriza mbatereke amafuta mu ttaala zammwe mulyoke muyimirire ku ntebe yange ey'omusango? Mujja kutereeza obutambi bwammwe obulungi nga mugondera amagezi gange? Oba mulironze mu busirusiru okusigala nga mwekumala mwekka nga muyimiridde mu butuukirivu bwammwe obw'okwekoza ne musigala nga muyimiridde mu bawala abasiru? Era bwe mba mbategekedde ebirungi, singa muba bawulize mu bujjuvu eri eddoboozi lyange. Kale, mbawa amagezi, mmwe ab'amazima bange, mweyongere okujja gye ndi n'okwagala kwonna n'obuwulize ndyoke mbagulumize mu ggulu erisinga obugulumivu mulyoke mwake ng'emmunyeenye ez'omu bbanga olw'ebyo bye mukoze mu buwulize mu kuweereza Mukama wammwe Ataggwaawo ow'obutuukirivu!

Kale, mbabuuza mmwe mwenna abamanyi eddoboozi lyange, era mmwe abayitiddwa amannya, era abakoowoola erinnya lyange: Mutunula ddala? Amaaso gammwe mazibule ddala eri ensi ng'eri leero? Mutegeere nti ensi mwe muli kati si y'emu ng'eyaliwo n'emyezi mukaaga egiyise, era ensi yeeyongera okukyuka mu ngeri ey'amaanyi mu maaso gammwe, newankubadde bangi ku mmwe mukyalowooza nti obubonero n'eby'amagero bya bujja mu butakkiriza obutali bwa ddiini nga munoonya kuddayo kwebaka. N’olwekyo, mwekebere mwekka n'entegeka zammwe ne Mukama wammwe ataggwaawo ndyoke nkwate emitindo gyammwe era mbakuume obutatuukibwako kabi mu kwagala kwange n'ekisa kyange.

Kale bwe kityo bwe kiri, obulimba bw'ogusota kati bujjudde ku nsi okusinga bwe bwali nga gugusota gunnaba kugobebwa; kubanga ogusota gugobeddwako, gugobeddwako ku nsi era gusibiddwa wansi w'ebbanga okusalirwa omusango, era gumanyi nti gulina akaseera katono n'obusungu bwakyo bunene nnyo! Era gujja kuleeta olutalo n'entalo zaakyo ziriba nnene, naye ogusota ogwo omukadde teguliba na bukulu bwonna okuggyako omuliro ogwaka n'amazzi ag'ekibiriiti agakulukuta agakwata ennyo ennyiriri z'ekibonerezo.

Kale njija gye muli baagalwa bange okubagumya okweyongera okusemberera omutima gwange olw'obuwulize bwammwe eri Ebigambo byange, era ku bisenge by'omutima gwange amannya gammwe gonna amajjuvu gawandiikiddwa mu kwagala kwange eri mmwe omwo, okutaggwaawo n'okw'emirembe gyonna. Weewaawo, mu kunjagala kwange, (mbamanyi buli omu ku mmwe okusinga bwe mwemanyi mwekka) mbaweerera kumpi nnyo n'omutima gwange bulijjo era naddala mu biseera byammwe eby'okunakuwala n'okugezesebwa kwe muyitamu ddala. (Nkungaanyizza buli zziga lyo mu ccupa yange ey'amaziga ndyoke nkusasule buli limu) Era mu ntalo eziriwo mu lutalo luno olw'ensigo olugenda mu maaso, oluwangulwa muri Kristo era olulwanyisa abagudde; mutegeere nti entalo zammwe ziwangulwa mu bujjuvu mu Mukama wammwe ataggwaawo! N’olwekyo, muwummule mu Mukama wammwe ow'emirembe gyonna, ye Kristo Yesu, era mulekere awo emirimu gyammwe egy'obwereere ne muwummuliramu ddala, era mumalire emirimu gy'obutuukirivu gye mbawadde okukola mulyoke muwangule abalabe bammwe nange ndibawa Ssabbiiti! Seera!

N’olwekyo, mutegeere ennaku ze mulimu kubanga tewali n'omu ku mmwe eyali abeeredde mu nnaku zino ezituuse ku nsi mu kiseera kino eky'enkomerero, newankubadde zirabika mu ngeri ey'obulimba ng'ezifaanana n'ezo ze mwamanyi. Kubanga ensi eri mu kukyuka era mazima ensi ekyusibwa, era mazima ddala, ensi ejja kweyongera okukyuka n'amaanyi okusinziira ku bigendererwa n'entegeka za Yahweh.

Mutegeere nti ekiseera eky'okukyuka okw'amaanyi kituuse ku nsi, era n'ekiseera ensi w'etereezebwa n'okutegekebwa nate ebibeerawo n'obutyabaga n'emisango okujja mu kuzzaawo ensi eyaliwo edda mu nnaku za Nuuwa! Era ebibeerawo n'emisango bijja kubeerawo nga bannabbi aba edda bwe baalagulwa okubeerawo mu bujjuvu mu kiseera kya Yakobo. N'olwekyo mutegeere nti emisango mingi gisuubiddwa okutandika okugwa ne mu kiseera kino eky'okukyuka kubanga tekyandisobose mu ngeri yonna okuba nti ebintu bibeerawo mu ngeri endala.

Era bwe kityo bwe kyawandiikibwa, era bwe kityo bwe kirikolebwa, bwe ndikungaanya ab'amazima muri Kristo amangu ago era mu kibwatukira kati omusibi bw'aliggibwawo, n'omuzira guligwa, n'obubonero buliggulwawo, n'amakondeere galivuga! Nti abajulirwa bange ababiri baliyimuka abaliragira omuliro okuva mu ggulu, abalikuba ensi n'ebibonerezo bya buli ngeri enfunda nnyingi nga bwe baagala, era abalireetera obutatonda ku nsi mu nnaku z'obunnabbi bwabwe ku kiragiro kyabwe, naye bangi baligaana okwenenya! Mutegeere nti baliziyiza ogusota, n'ensolo n'omunnabbi ow'obulimba era omuntu yenna bw'anaayagala okubalumya, omuntu oyo ateekwa okulumizibwa mu ngeri y'emu. Omuntu yenna bw'anaayagala okubatta omuntu oyo ateekwa okuttibwa mu ngeri y'emu. Bino bye bikondo byange eby'ettaala ebibiri n'emiti gyange ebiri egy'emizeyituuni era ebintu bingi, bingi bye biribikkulwa mu biseera by'okubikkulwa kwabyo n'obujulizi bwabyo buliwandiikibwa emirembe gyonna.

Bino bye biseera bye mulimu, baana bange abato, era bino bye biseera bye mulina okweyongera okugumiikiriza nga musemberera ennyo mu by'ayagala ku lw'obulamu bwammwe kubanga omwo mwe muli obukuumi bwammwe n'obulokozi. Mutegeere nti mwakulira ng'ennyana ez'omu kisibo mu biseera byange eby'ekisa n'okusaasira ebyaweebwa olw'obulokozi bw'ebika ebyabula ebyasaasaanyizibwa mu mawanga era bwe kityo, amawanga ne gaweebwa omukisa ne gayanirizibwa okwagala, okusaasira, n'ekisa era bangi ne bafuna obulokozi bwe batyo. Naye kati ebiseera bikyuse nnyo, era mbalagira okutambulanga n'amagezi AMANGI, era mubeere ab'amagezi ng'emisota ate nga temuliiko kabi ng'amayiba kubanga ebiseera bino bya kabi mu ngeri ey'obulimba.

Kubanga ogusota kati gusibiddwa ku nsi n'abawaggulu abagudde beeyongera amaanyi buli ssekonda, naye tebalina maanyi gonna okuziyiza eddoboozi lyange ery'amaanyi n'amaanyi gange gonna n'obuyinza! N’olwekyo, mbanenya ne mbaziyiza ku lwammwe okusinga bwe muyinza okutegeera mu kiseera kino era ntadde ebintu byonna eby'obwakabaka bw'omulabe wansi w'ebigere byammwe mulyoke mubirinnyirire bwe kinaaba kyetaagisa. N’olwekyo, temubatya kubanga nawangudde byonna, era n'obwakabaka bwa Setaani bwonna; era nammwe bwe mutyo bwe mulisinza, bwe mulisigala nga muli beesigwa eri Ebigambo byange eby'okuyigiriza bye nawadde era bye mpa olw'obuwulize bwammwe.

Temwewuunya, baagalwa bange, era n'abagudde bwe beebikkulira ensi, temwewuunya n'akatono! Kubanga ennaku za Nuuwa zituuse! Era n'ennaku Setaani ne bamalayika be we bagobebwa ku nsi, ne bataddayo nate mu ggulu gye bagobeddwamu kati emirembe gyonna mu buwangulwa obujjuvu. Era n'ennaku abalabi we basumululwa okuva mu makomera gaabwe mwe baasibibwa ne bawulibwa omu ku mulala mu kubonyaabonyezebwa okw'amaanyi olw'ebikolobero byabwe eby'omuzizo bye baakola ku Yahweh n'ekitonde kye mu nnaku ez'edda era mu kuwangulwa okujjuvu baasibibwa okuva mu bujeemu bwabwe n'ebirayiro bye beerayirira okutuukiriza. Naye, ebirayiro byabwe birituukirizibwa emirembe gyonna bwe birisibibwa ne bisuulibwa mu miriro gy'ekibonerezo mu nnyanja eyo ennene ey'ekibiriiti ekirokerera omusota gwabwe we gutafiira.

Nga nate, ennaku zikomyewo nga mu biseera bya Nuuwa, abalabi bwe baaserengeta ku Lusozi Kerumooni mu nnaku za Yeredi. Weewaawo, ennaku zikomyewo, abatabaazi kati nga bakolaganira ddala n'obwakabaka bw'ensi n'abafuzi baabwe ab'ensi yonna be beekwese emabega, abakwatagana nabo mu ntegeka zaabwe ez'okuleeta okuzikirira okw'amaanyi n'akatyabaga n'entalo, amangu ddala ng'omuziyiza aggyiddwawo, obubi bwabwe bwe buliba nga busumuluddwa mu bujjuvu. Yee, bateereddwa ku nsi, era omusibizi bw'aliggibwawo, obwakabaka bwa Setaani obujjuvu bulisumululwa omuzira bwe guligwa, n'obunnya butigulwe, n'emiryango gyabwe giramuke nate. Laba! Ndeeta ebikonge okutuukiriza obusungu bwange!

Temwewuunya, baagalwa bange, nga n'abatabaazi bamulisa waggulu wansi w'ebbanga n'ebitangaala byabwe n'obukunkumuli bwabwe, kubanga kati beeyoleseza mu bifaananyi bye batoola era n'ebyuma byabwe kati bya ddembe okubuuka, era obulimba n'okutabuka okungi bye baleese ku maaso g'ensi n'ebyuma byabwe, ne mu kiseera eky'okumaliriza okulumwa kw'okuzaala, okugenda okumalirizibwa mu kabenje mu bugwi obw'amangu ennyo kati. Kale omuntu ow'omubiri yeewuunya n'ateebereza n'anoonya ensonga ku eby'obwakatonda ebifuuse ebya bulijjo nate.

Yee, abantu mu butakkiriza bwabwe bajeemera amaanyi ga Yakuwa agalagirwa n'ekigendererwa nti abantu bazuukuke mu butuukirivu era bafune obulokozi. Mu kifo ky'okuwa Yakuwa ekitiibwa olw'emirimu gy'emikono gye n'obubonero obulabula ku musango ogunaatera okubaawo; abantu bo bawa ekitiibwa omuntu n'ebyuma bye ebitagasa bye yafuna okuva eri abatabaazi: kyekiva omusango omukakali ennyo gujja! Temuba na mugabo wadde akatono mu kuteebereza kwabwe okutaliimu kukkiriza, era mugende mu maaso n'okugulumiza Yakuwa olw'ebikolwa bye eby'obulokozi ebyaweebwa mu nteekateeka ze ez'olubeerera ez'okulokola Adamu mu maaso ga Yesu Kristo, ye Adamu ow'oluvannyuma.

N'olwekyo mbawa okutegeera: ka bubeere ki ekireeta enkyukakyuka mu ggulu oba ku nsi, oba enkyukakyuka mu bbanga ly'ensi, oba omuntu yenna ayinza okukyusa ekitonde kya Yakuwa mu ngeri yonna: Mbalagira okutegeera mu bujjuvu nti Yakuwa ye afuga ebitonde bye mu bujjuvu era ekintu kyonna ekibaawo kibaawo lwa kukkirizibwa kwa Yakuwa, era nga akimanyi bulungi, era okutuukiriza enteekateeka ze ez'olubeerera ez'obulokozi eri omwana we omujaaga Adamu. Tegeera nti Yakuwa aleka abatali batuukirivu, ab'emitima emikakanyavu, n'abatagondera okubeera mu bulamu bwabwe obw'ebibi balyoke bajjuze ebibi byabwe mu bujjuvu era omusango gwabwe ogunaatera okubaawo tebagenda kugwewona, okuggyako nga bajja eri Yesu Kristo mu kwenenya okujjuvu nga obudde bukyaliwo.

Kwe kugamba, Yakuwa aleka abatali batuukirivu okwonoona balyoke basasulwe mu bujjuvu mu musango, nga bafuna empeera ejjuvu ey'ebikolwa byabwe ebibi ku mitwe gyabwe emirembe gyonna ne lubeerera mu nnimi z'omuliro gw'okubonerezebwa, wadde nga kino tekyetaagisa era kyandiwonye mu bulokozi olw'okugondera obutuukirivu. Mu kisa kya Yakuwa, n'okusaasira kwe mu nteekateeka ye ey'olubeerera ey'okulokola Adamu; Yakuwa ayagala waleme kubaawo muntu n'omu abula mu nnimi z'omuliro, naye abantu bonna baaweebwa eddembe ly'okweronderawo okukola ekibi, oba okweronderawo obutuukirivu mu maaso ga Yesu Kristo alitonda abajja eri obulokozi bonna okuba omuntu omu omuggya, era balibeerawo emirembe n'emirembe.

Kale abatabaazi balisumululwa mu bujjuvu mu musango ku bantu ab'oku nsi abatagondera olw'okugaana erinnya lya Yeshua HaMashiach ye Yesu Kristo! N'olwekyo, tegeera nti bonna abagaana Yesu Kristo okuba Omutonzi, oba ababeerawo mu butakkiriza mu butagondera biragiro byange balirekebwa wansi w'okufugibwa kwabwe okw'ettima n'obufuzi bwabwe mwe bategekera okusaanyaawo Adamu ku maaso g'ensi mu ttima n'obujjuvu; mu nteekateeka zaabwe ez'edda mwe bagenderera okubbira ensi mu mikono gyange egy'amaanyi, n'enkola ezo ezitasoboka kubatuukako mu bulalu bwabwe obw'obujeemu.

Naye, abantu batambulira ddala butereevu mu mutego gwabwe, nga n'okunnyonnyola bwe bannyonnyola obubonero obuwaabwa olw'omusango gwabwe ogunaatera okubaawo gwe bakyagaana okukkiriza nti gujja mu kuteebereza kwabwe okutaliimu kukkiriza okwava mu kusoma kwabwe okw'obulimba mwe bafuukira abazibe b'amaaso ddala ku nsi ey'amazima gye babeeramu, ensi eyatondebwa Yakuwa Nze Ndi Asinga, abantu gwe baagala okwegaana mu kusoma kwabwe okw'obulimba okwategekebwa Abayesuiti okw'okuyiga okulwanyisa okuyiga. Bwe mbabuulira eby'ensi ne mutakkiriza, munaakkiriza mutya, bwe nnaababaguliranga eby'omu ggulu? Kubanga tewali muntu alinnya mu ggulu, wabula oyo eyaserengeta okuva mu ggulu, ye Mwana w'omuntu ali mu ggulu. Muliyeetoowaza emitima gyammwe ne mukkiriza Ebigambo by'Omwana w'omuntu? Oba mulinywerera ku bifaananyi by'emitima gyammwe n'obulimba bw'abasiru abasome abayogera nga bawakanya butereevu Ebigambo ebyaweebwa Yakuwa mu maaso ga Yesu Kristo?

Mu kuteebereza kw'abantu okutaliimu kukkiriza basiru basiru banoonya okuddamu eby'obulimba eri obubonero n'eby'amagero by'abatabaazi eby'obulimba. Okuddamu n'abatabaazi bennyini bye bawa oluusi n'oluusi eri bonna abagaana Ebigambo bya Adonai Yakuwa, Ebigambo ebyaweebwa okugonderwa n'omukono gw'ekkubo ery'amazima era eddamu, ekkubo Nze Kristo Yesu lye ndi ddala. N’olwekyo, temwegatta nabo mu ntegeerera yaabwe etalimu kukkiriza n’akatono. Era mbawa okutegeera nti, ensi ya El Shaddai eyagiwa Yeshua HaMashiach, Kabaka wa Bakabaka era Omutonzi w’Abatonzi; n’olwekyo, ddala, ensi ya Mukama n’okujjuza kwayo! Era tewali kitonde kyonna ekiyinza okuggya omugabo gwange mu mikono gyange egy’amaanyi, era njija kutwala omugabo gwange ku lwa maanyi okuva ku basenze abagenda mu musango ogw’olubeerera ogutayinza kubawonya. Naye beekutte ku ssuubi lyabwe ery’obulimba nti mu bungi bwabwe obunaasumululwa, bayinza okuwangula Yeshua HaMashiach, ssuubi eryo lye ndibbetenta mu maanyi g’obuyinza bwange obw’okuva eri Adonai Yakuwa yennyini, ow’entebe eyagabanyizibwa ne Yesu Kristo.

Temugwa mu nkwe zaabwe baagalwa bange era temugwa mu kulimbibwa okw’amaanyi n’omusango ogutali gwammwe. Kubanga mwakulira ng’ennyana ez’omu kiyumba mu biseera eby’emirembe n’obutebenkevu omusibizi bwe yasibanga era ekisa n’okusaasira ne bibeera ku nsi wadde nga okuyigganyizibwa kwabanga kungi. Era kati emirembe n’obutebenkevu bikendedde ku nsi era nga byonna biweddewo, wadde ng’omusibizi akyasiba. Era abatabaazi bagenda mu maaso n’enteekateeka zaabwe n’ebigendererwa byabwe mwe banoonyeza okuteekawo emirembe gyabwe egya zaabu egizziddwa buggya, naye balifuna musango mukakali gwa maanyi gwokka.

N’olwekyo, temuyitangamu mu biseera bino baagalwa bange era nja kugenda mu maaso okubakulembera nnamutindo ku nnamutindo okubasembeza ku layini yammwe ey’enkomerero gye mbalindiridde era gye mbakubira engalo nga muwomerera layini z’enkomerero ez’emirimu gyammwe egy’obuweereza egyaweebwa omukono gwange olw’ekitiibwa kyammwe, obulokozi, n’empeera! N’olwekyo, temubatya omugole wange kubanga mmwe muwangula, temutya omubiri gwange kubanga empeera ennene yammwe era ab’ekikumi kimu mu nkaaga n’enkumi nnya balokola emirimu egy’amaanyi ku nsi okusinga ne gye nnakola mu biseera byange eby’obuweereza ku nsi ng’omwana gw’endiga gwa Katonda Pontio Piraato bwe yali omufuzi w’e Buyudaaya!

Kubanga ng’ennaku ezaakulembera amataba bwe zaali, baalyanga era baanywanga, baawasanga, era baawanyisizanga, okutuusa olunaku Nuuwa lwe yayingira mu lyato, so tebaamanya, okutuusa amataba lwe gajja, ne gabatwala bonna, n’okujja kw’Omwana w’omuntu bwe kuliba bwe kutyo. Ababiri baliba mu nnimiro, omu alitwalibwa, omulala alirekebwa. Abakazi babiri baliba basera ku lubengo: omu alitwalibwa, omulala alirekebwa. Mutunule n’olwekyo: kubanga temumanyi ssaawa ki omukama wammwe gy’alijjiramu. Mube bakakafu ku kino, nti singa nnyinimu yali amanyi ekisisimuka ki omubbi ky’ajja, yanditunudde, so si kuleka nnyumba ye kusimibwamu. N’olwekyo nammwe mubeetegefu: kubanga mu ssaawa gye mutalowooleza, Omwana w’omuntu alijja.

Ani olwo omuweereza omwesigwa era omugezi, mukama we gw’afudde omufuzi w’ennyumba ye, okubawanga emmere mu biseera byayo? Alina omukisa omuweereza oyo, mukama we gw’alisanga ng’akola bw’atyo. Mazima, mbagamba nti, alimufuula omufuzi w’ebintu bye byonna. Naye omuweereza omubi oyo bw’alyogera mu mutima gwe nti, omukama wange alwawo okujja, n’atandika okukuba banne, n’okulya, n’okunywa n’abatamiivu, omukama w’omuweereza oyo alijja ku lunaku, lw’atamulindirira, ne mu ssaawa gy’atamanyi, n’amusalako, n’amuwa omugabo gwe n’abannanfusi: walibaawo okukaaba, n’okuluma obujiji.

N'olwekyo mbawa okulabula nate, kubanga bonna be njagala mbawa okulabula, na bonna be njagala mbanenya, na bonna be njagala mbakangavvula era bonna be njagala mbakuba emirundi mingi kubanga Yakuwa abayisa ng'abaana, era kitaawe ki atakanga abaana be, oba ate temuli baana naye muli bizinga era abajeemu. N'olwekyo, mugumiikirize okukangavvulwa kwange era muwulire Ebigambo byange eby'okunenya n'okukangavvula mulyoke muwangule abatabaazi mu bintu byonna mu bulamu buno mwe banoonyeza okubatega, mulyoke muleme kujja mu busika bwammwe obujjuvu bwe mbategekedde mu mukono gwange ogw'amaanyi. Obusika obutayinza kubula bwe munaabanga abagonvu mu bujjuvu eri ebiragiro byange bye mulina okukwata mu kwagala kwammwe eri Yakuwa, n'eri Yeshua HaMashiach.

Mutegeere okwagala kwange gye muli mmwe ekkanisa yange, omubiri gwange, n'omugole wange era mumanye nti mbayita okusemberera gye ndi kubanga mangu nnyo kati nja kusereka okuva mu ggulu n'eddoboozi ly'omutume omukulu ne n'ekkondeere lya Yakuwa, era ekkondeere linaavuga n'eddoboozi eryo linaayogerera waggulu era olunaku, ekiseera, n'essaawa y'okulabika kwange kati birangiriddwa mu ggulu era binaatera okutuukirira ku nsi, mu bugwi n'okutamanyirwa nga bwe kyasuubizibwa. Era bonna abalindirira era abalina okwagala okulabika kwange baliba n'empeera ennene era balifuna n'engule ey'obutuukirivu olw'okusuubira kwabwe kubanga abo abalina okusuubira okwo beebo abafudde mu bujjuvu eri ensi eno embi, era baliba bawangudde mu bujjuvu olw'amaanyi, obuyinza, n'obutuukirivu by'Omutonzi waabwe ow'olubeerera ow'ekitiibwa!

Nze Yesu Omunazaaleeti, abayita okusemberera gye ndi mmwe ekkanisa yange, omubiri gwange, n'omugole wange.

Nze Yesu Kristo, abayita okusemberera mu biseera bino nga temunnatwalibwa mu mpeera z'obusika bwammwe bye nja okubawa mangu mu ggulu nga mumaze okutwalibwa.

Nze Kristo Yesu, ddala, ddala ye mmwe mu bintu byonna, n'olwekyo muwummulire mu mirimu gyange n'obutuukirivu bwange era munsabe mu bwetoowaze era muve mu nkola zonna ez'okwewaana ez'okwemalirira, kubanga munaatera okugattibwa mu bujjuvu n'Omugole wammwe alibawa emirembe n'emirembe obungi n'obugagga bw'ebintu byonna mu busika bwammwe mu Kristo.

Nze Yeshua HaMashiach, ayita era abegayirira okujja gye ndi mu bujjuvu mu kwagala kwange gye muli mmwe abaana bange, obulokozi bwammwe obujjuvu bulyoke butuukirire mu bujjuvu mu biseera bino eby'okununulibwa okujjuvu eri abeesigwa mu Kristo, oba nga bawummudde mu mutima gw'ensi nga beebase mu mirembe mu butuukirivu, oba nga balamu era basigadde ku maaso g'ensi ku kulabika kwange, ekiseera kye kituuse.

N'olwekyo, tegeera obukulu n'obwangu bw'ebiseera bino kubanga tewakyali kiseera kya butagondera era ddala kituuse kumpi mu bujjuvu kati, nti ku kulabika kwange oyo omwesigwa mu Kristo alitwalibwa mu butebenkevu mu ggulu gye baliba n'empeera ennene era baliba bawonyezeddwa okuva mu busungu obujja, n'omusango ogunaatera okugwa ku nsi mu kabenje akanene. Era bwe kityo ku kulabika kwange oyo atali mwesigwa era atali mu Kristo alirekebwa mu muliro ogwoza, gye baliwonyezebwa bwe banaakoowoola erinnya lya Yesu Omutonzi waabwe okutuusa ku nkomerero yaabwe era tewali kirala kyonna ekisigaddeyo eri abo ab'amawanga abagaanye obulokozi bwabwe obujjuvu mu kiseera eky'okulabika kwange.

N'olwekyo, baagalwa bange njija kubagamba nate, temukyuka ku kkono, wadde ku ddyo naye mukuumire ebigere byammwe ku kkubo eryo eggolokofu era erifunda gye ndeka abawombeefu n'ab'emitima emitoowavu okuba n'obuwanguzi obujjuvu nga bawangula abatabaazi n'amaanyi gange amangi, n'obutuukirivu bwe nnawa obwereere eri abagonvu. N'olwekyo, mukuumire amaaso gammwe ku Mufuzi wammwe ow'olubeerera ow'obutuukirivu mulyoke mujje mu mpeera yammwe ejjuvu era mulyoke muwummulire mu bujjuvu mu mirembe n'okubudaabudibwa bye ndeeta nga mbafuyirira mu mirembe n'okubudaabudibwa mu kwagala kwange gye muli. Nti ne mu maaso g'okuyisibwa obubi n'okugezesebwa muyinza okuyimirira nga muli ba maanyi emirembe n'emirembe mu kumenyeka kwammwe n'okubonyaabonyezebwa kwammwe nga muwummulira mu maanyi gange agatasobola kuwangulwa.

Nze Yeshua HaMashiach, awamu ne Yakuwa, Nze Ndi Asinga, abatumye Ebigambo by'Enjiri ey'olubeerera n'omubaka wange ow'amaanyi era omuweereza wange eyawandiise n'okusoma Ebigambo bino mu bwesigwa olw'okugonderwa n'obulokozi bw'Ebigambo bino byonna bye bitumiddwa. Era n'omubaka wange ow'amaanyi era omuweereza wange Ebigambo by'Enjiri ey'olubeerera bitumiddwa. Mazima, mazima, Ebigambo by'Enjiri ey'olubeerera birikuumibwa okuyita mu matigga n'emisango egijja eginaatera okubwatuka ku nsi yonna. Tegeera nti obujulizi obulala buliweebwa era buwandiikibwe era bukubibwe mu bitabo mu ggulu era Ebigambo bya Adonai Yakuwa birijjuza ensi mu butuukirivu bwonna n'obutukuvu obw'amazima!

Njija mangu nnyo eri abeesigwa mu Kristo, era ddala ebiseera byabwe bituuse! Ebiseera ebibala ebisooka eby'amakungula lwe birituukiriza okusuubizibwa n'empeera mu maaso ga Yakuwa yennyini (ekiseera Yakuwa ky'ayagadde okuva mu biseera eby'edda), ne Yeshua HaMashiach n'eggye eringi erya bamalayika abatalina muwendo abalina essanyu mu maaso gaabwe amangi omuntu omu yekka bw'afuna obulokozi obw'olubeerera. Naye, bwe kiriba bwe kityo bwe nnaayita abeesigwa mu Kristo okudda eka mu mpeera yaabwe ey'olubeerera balitwala ebifo byabwe mu bika kubanga be Isiraeri wa Katonda era balijjuza amadaala g'ebiragiro byabwe n'eggye eddene erya bakabaka n'abakulu be nnaatikkira engule ne mbatukuza olw'obuweereza obw'olubeerera mu bwakabaka bwange obw'olubeerera, obutalina nkomerero.

Nze Yeshua HaMashiach, era Omutonzi w’Amakungula, era mazima ddala, ebiseera by’amakungula eby’ebibala ebisooka bituuse, era n’ekiseera ky’empeera z’ebibala ebisooka by’amakungula era nakyo kituuse.
