Ekiseera Kyo Ekya KAATI

KAATI Yeshua HaMashiach ajja okubategeeza ensonga zange, mulyoke muwulire mu bujjuvu Ebigambo byange mutegeere ensonga ezireeseewo ekiseera kino ekya KAATI! Mulyoke mutegeere eby'okuddako ebirigwa mu ssaawa emu n’ensolo mu ngeri ey'ekibwatukira era etasuubirwa mu nkomerero eno eya KAATI, era mulyoke mweteekereteekere mu bujjuvu okwegatta okw’enkomerero okunaakutukira mu nsi eno mu musango ogw’omuliro ogw’amaanyi, ng’abeesigwa mu Kristo Yesu bwe bagenda okuggyibwawo amangu. Seera.

SSA n’obwegendereza omutima gwo, obulamu bwo, amagezi go, n'amaanyi go mu kuwulira okujjuvu eri Kristo Yesu KAATI ng'obulumi bw’okuzaala buno bukomye mu kibwatukira KAATI. Lw’oliggyibwawo mu ngeri ey'ekibwatukira era etasuubirwa kaakati, n'otwalibwa mu kifo eky’entebe y'obwakabaka eya Adonai-YHVH.

Kale bwe kityo, nze Kristo Yesu, nzija okubategeeza ebikwata ku mmwe bennyini abeesigwa bange mu Kristo Yesu mulyoke mutegeere ebigendererwa bya Adonai-YHVH wammwe bye nnabawulamu mu nsi eno enkyamu era empotofu ng’omusango teguggye.

Mmwe muli kkanisa yange, omubiri gwange, era n’omugole wange: Kale, mutegeere kye muli mu Kristo Yesu. Kubanga mbalagira mwenna KAATI, okuteeka ebirowoozo byammwe byonna ku bintu ebiri waggulu, so si ku bintu ebiri ku nsi, ebigenda okuzikirizibwa mu musango. Kubanga muli bafu, n'obulamu bwammwe bukwekeddwa ne Kristo Yesu mu Adonai-YHVH. Kristo Yesu, ye bulamu bwammwe, bw'alirabika, nammwe mulirabikira wamu nange mu kitiibwa.

Mutegeere mwenna abeesigwa mu Kristo Yesu nti nnalagira MWENNA okuva mu bikolwa by'omubiri n'okuva mu makubo ga Babulooni ow’Ekyama agaleeseewo omusango gwe ogw'omuliro omulundi ebiri, ogugenda okusaasaana ne mu mawanga amalala amakaba okusinziira ku buseegu n’obusuubuzi bwago.

Naye bangi ku mmwe mwagaana n’obukakanyavu ebiragiro byange, ebinnnyonnyoddwa kaakano mu bigambo eby’omulembe so si okuvvuunula ennimi, naye ne musigala nga mutudde mu kizikiza mu butawulira obw’olwatu ng’Ebigambo byange bya kwesalirawo mu ngeri yammwe ey’ekivuguto. Temulina kya kwewolereza.

Awo, mu kifo ky'okuweereza Mukama waffe Yesu Kristo eyagula obulamu bwammwe okuva mu mukono gw'abalabe bammwe ab'emirembe gyonna, mmwe mwakozesa okununnulibwa kwammwe ng'omukisa eri omubiri, era ne musigala nga muweereza abatukuvu mu bikolwa by'omubiri mu bunnanfuusi obw'obujeemu bwe bumu.

Mutegeere, mmwe mwenna abeegombesa mu bintu bino ebitatukuvu mu bibi byammwe eby'omubiri mu kwesiba ku mizannyo, nti olw'obutawulira bwammwe n'emitima emikakanyavu eri Kristo Yesu, temuliyingira mu Bwakabaka bwa Katonda mu kiseera kino! Era kino si lwa kikolwa kya kuzannya muzannyo, wabula emizannyo gye muzannya. Mwewale ebikolwa by'omubiri ku buli ngeri, muyige okumanya.

Mu kifo ky'ekyo, mulisanga nga musuuliddwa ebbali era nga muweereddwayo ddala eri ebikolwa by'omubiri mu kiseera ky'okubonaabona kwa Yakobo. Mu bikolwa ebyo eby'omubiri mulina okwenenya ne musigala nga mwenenyezza era nga mweteeseteese nga bwe nnalagira, nsobole okubanunula mu bujjuvu okuva mu musango omukakali ogutategekerwa bawulize abeesigwa mu Kristo Yesu. Naye eri abatakkiriza n’ab’emitima emikakanyavu abagaana KAATI okwegayirira kwange okw’enkomerero ne bagwa mu musango guno bwe batyo. Seera.

Mutegeere nti, muyinza okukozesa ebikozesebwa bino ebiri obulamu mu bantu bammwe abagwa olw'ebigendererwa eby'obutuukirivu, n'okuwonya, n'okubeera abalamu obulungi, n'okusoma obujulizi bwange okuyita mu Bigambo byange ebiri mu katabo kano akatono akaggule, ke nzigudde mu mukono gw'omubaka wange n'omuweereza wange. Mulyoke mutambulire mu maaso gange mu butuukirivu mu kwagala okujjuvu. Ebintu byonna mu kigero! Mwabulire ebibi by'omubiri! Si lwa kuba nti mwandigerozze ebibi byammwe eby'omubiri, wadde nga, bangi ku mmwe bwe mukola bwe mutyo. Mwewale buli kirabika ng'ekibi era mukeme buli kintu, nga munyweza ekirungi. Abatali batuukirivu tebalisikira bwakabaka bwa Adonai-YHVH, mulabuddwa mu bujjuvu KAATI.

Okuzannya emizannyo kwennyini si kibi; newankubadde endowooza eno. Naye, okukkiriza n'obwegendereza ebikolwa by'omubiri ng'abalala bwe bakola ebibi byabwe ng'osanyusibwa ebikolwa byabwe, kye kimu n'okukola ebibi bino mu muntu yennyini. Ka bibeere mu ngeri ki ey’obugwagwa: Ku ttivi, mu bitabo, ku ssimu, n’ebirala: Mbagamba lwatu nti: Buli muntu yenna eyeegombesa mu bugwagwa alirekebwa mu bagwagwa, era ekyo kisuubiziddwa KAATI.

Mutegeere, buli omu ku mmwe eyava mu Kkanisa y'e Lawodikiya ng'anoonya okubalibwa mu beesigwa mu Kristo Yesu, nga bwe kirina okubeera. Mulina okuva mu ngeri yammwe ey’ekivuguto ey'obutawulira obw'ekitundu eri Ebigambo byange, ne muwaayo omutima gwammwe gwonna n'obulamu bwammwe n'amagezi gammwe n'amaanyi gammwe mu kwegendereza okungi, mulyoke muganyulwe mu maanyi mu beesigwa mu Kristo Yesu. Kubanga tekimala kuva mu kkanisa zammwe ez'e Lawodikiya ne mukyusa ekifo kyammwe okutandika obulamu obupya oba omuntu omupya awatali kwenenya kujjuvu mu bibi byammwe byonna, temuyinza butereevu kutandika bulamu bupya kubanga ezo ze ngeri z'abagwa. Bwe wabaawo muntu yenna alowooza mu mutima gwe mu butali butuukirivu bwe nti nsobola okuwaayo empeera eno olw'ekibi kino, mazima ddala toteekateeka kuyingira mu Bwakabaka bwa Katonda mu kiseera kino.

Mutegeere: Mwenna abatali beesigwa mu Kristo Yesu abasoma Ebigambo bino: Oba mujja gye ndi KAATI, ne musigala nga mwenenyezza mu bujjuvu, nga mwatula ebibi byammwe gye ndi ne mwetonda ne mukomawo amangu, oba mulirekebwa mu muliro ogulongosa. Mbalagira okusigala nga muli beesigwa wansi w'omusaayi gwange ogutangirira ogunaabalongoosa mu bujjuvu, oba obutafaayo ku Bigambo byange ne musigala wansi w'obufuzi bw'ebintu bino eby'ekibi ne mwesanga omusango gw'aboonoonyi ogutabategekerwa.

Bangi ku mmwe mwagala nnyo ebikozesebwa byammwe n’okutuuka ku ssinga temukyamanyi Kristo Yesu, ate okudda eri nze olw'obulokozi bwammwe. Abamu ku mmwe mukkiriza ebikozesebwa bino ebirimba okubagamba nti Ebigambo byange ebitukuvu ebiri mu Katabo kano Akatono ak'emisango emitukuvu bituufu ekitundu, oba bulimba ddala obubasiba mu malogo gaabyo n'obukujjukujju, omukenkufu omu bw'awakana n'omulala, ate nga bangi ku mmwe si bekenkufu, kale ani ayinza okumanya? Oba ne bw’oba mukenkufu wa bekenkufu, waliwo abakugu abalala bangi abataikiriziganya, n'olwekyo, ani ayinza okumanya amazima? Kale, Ebigambo "bayiga bulijjo naye ne batasobola kutuuka ku kumanya amazima" bityo bisigalawo ng'okusalirwa omusango okw'emirembe gyonna okw'abo bonna abagala okukola n'okubeera mu bulimba bwabwe, oba obulimba bw'abalala bwe bumu.

Muwulire Ebigambo byange, tewali mpeera za kitundu wadde emisaala egikendeezeddwako wadde amakaawunta g’okutereka mu bwakabaka bwa Katonda kubanga ebintu bino biggibwawo emirembe gyonna eri mmwe KAATI mu kununulibwa kwammwe okujjuvu. Ate era mutegeere nti, ku nkomerero ya ssabbiiti ya Danyeri ey’amakumi musanvu ebintu bino biriba bikomekkerezeddwa mu musango ogw’omuliro oguliweebwa Adonai-YHVH ku kiragiro kya Kristo Yesu emirembe gyonna n’emirembe.

Ekiribaawo eri abatali beesigwa kyawangu: Tebalitwalibwa mu busika obw'emirembe gyonna KAATI naye balirekebwa n'abasalirwa omusango okulaga nti beesigwa mu kuttirwa erinnya lyange. Singa basanga nti bagwanidde ekitiibwa kino. Naye, eri bonna abaliweebwa ekitiibwa kino, mulituuka ku bulokozi singa mugaana okuggwaamu amaanyi. N’olwekyo mbasaba mwenna mu kwagala kwange okutayaka: Muve mu butawulira bwonna KAATI era mukomye amangu ddala okusuulirira ebiragiro byange ebya rhema ebiri mu katabo kano akatono akaggule, mulyoke muve mu bujjuvu mu makubo ga Babulooni ow’Ekyama, musome mutegeere Ebigambo byange era munsabe mbalage obulamu bwammwe, mbagala emirembe gyonna n’emirembe!

Mmwe muli Kkanisa YANGE: Ekklesia yange eyayitibwa, eyakuŋŋaanyizibwa n'eteekebwako akabonero olw'ebigendererwa byange mu biseera bino eby'ekisa ebikomekkerezeddwa KAATI. Abeesigwa mu Kristo Yesu baggyibwa mu Isirayiri okutandikira mu Buyudaaya mu nnaku za Pontiyo Piraato, ne mu mawanga gonna era nti ddala amatabi g’omuzeyituuni ogw’omu nsiko KAATI galina okusimbibwa mu muzeyituuni ogw’obutonde emirembe gyonna. Gye musimbibwa mu Isirayiri ya Adonai-YHVH ku kiragiro kya Adonai-YHVH Yennyini, nga kituukirizibwa emikono gyange egy'amaanyi kubanga abawanguzi bange abeesigwa KAATI balangiriddwa nti beesigwa mu Kristo Yesu, MUSANYUKE!

Musigale mu NZE, baagalwa bange, so temukkiriza mikono gyammwe kusigala nga micaafu singa gicaafuwala. Mwatule ebibi byammwe KAATI, ndi wano nammwe. Nja kubibaggyako ne mbalongoosa mu bujjuvu.

Kale bwe kityo, nze Yeshua HaMashiach, nzija okubategeeza ensonga z'ebigoberera eby'ekiseera kyange, mulyoke mulye Akatabo kano Akatono akaggule ke nzigudde mu mukono gw'omubaka wange n'omuweereza wange, Biblaridion:144, newankubadde kawoomu mu kamwa naye kakaawa mu lubuto. Mulyoke mutegeere kye njagala mu bulamu bwammwe mu ngeri zammwe zonna, era mulyoke mutegeere ekiseera kino eky'enkomerero ekya KAATI ekyayimirizibwawo mulyoke mwenna muve mu bujjuvu mu bibi by'omubiri, oba mulisigala mu muliro gwange ogulongosa okukaabirira erinnya lyange okutuusa ku nkomerero, oba muligwa mu musango ogw'emirembe gyonna mu nnyanja ey'omuliro singa mujeema mu bujjuvu. Kubanga tewali kusalawo kulala, kuno kwe kuli.

Biblaridion:144 musajja mutuukirivu lwa butuukirivu bwange bwokka obuweereddwa mu mwoyo gwange ogulongoosa ne mu kubonererezebwa kungi n'okulongoosebwa mu butuukirivu bw'engeri ZANGE eza Adonai-YHVH we, obuweereddwa emikono gya Kitaawe Yeshua. Era mu ngeri ezo, asangiddwa nga mwesigwa era omuwanguzi ow'amaanyi eyawagula si Heylel Ben Shachar yekka, (eyamulumba obutereevu), naye n'abalabi bano abatono abatukuvu abaweereddwa eddembe ne bamulumba nabo. Naye mu kisa kyange n'okusaasira kwange n'omuliro gwange ogulongoosa Biblaridion:144 agudde emirundi musanvu, n'emirundi emirala musanvu, naye, akyakomawo n'obwesigwa okuyimirira ku bigere bye ng'alina amaanyi mangi ku kiragiro kyange, era ng'omutima gwe gunywevu mu kuwulira okw'obutuukirivu bwonna nga tasuubira kusimibwa, naye ng'atambulira mu kukkiriza okujjuvu nga nze nzekka bwe mmulabirira okuyita mu mikono gy'abalala.

Nze, Kristo Yesu, era ye Yeshua HaMashiach, nnawula Biblaridion:144 okuva mu buweereza obwalagirwa mu Kkanisa yange gye nnamuteeka okumuyigiriza engeri zange n'engeri y'okuweereza muntu munne mu kwagala, ng'abasonyiwa mu kifo ky'okubalumba ng'ekisiraanye ky'obuntu kizuukusiddwa era mazima ddala tewali bisiraanye ebisigaddemu mu Biblaridion:144 naye mujulirwa mulamu mu bintu byonna. Nnamuyigiriza n'okutuula ku mmeeza ez'ekitiibwa n'okutuukirira abasinga okuba ab'omuwendo omutono mu makubo ag'emabega. Oluvannyuma nnamuggyawo ne mmukweka mmwe mwenna nsobole okumulongoosa mu muliro gwange ogulongoosa okuva mu byonna ebyamukolebwa n'okumulongoosa okuva mu bibi bye ebyali ebingi era eby'entiisa.

Mutegeere nti ebibi bye yagwamu byava ku ebyo ebyamukolebwa mu ngeri embi ye ne mwannyina mu bukyemika n'emikono gy'abalabi bano abagwa nga bakyali bato nga tebannatuuka ku myaka gya vunaanyizibwa. Ebintu bino byamukolebwa mu ngeri embi mu nkola ez'ekyama era ez'okulondamu ez'eby'omwoyo ezaateekebwa abalabi bano abatono mu masomero gano ag'ekyama agayitibwa emisomo gya lukale, ebitongole ebyo eby'obugwagwa ebisaasaanidde mu Babulooni ow’Ekyama. Yali atamiiziddwa nnyo mu buto bwe n'awalirizibwa, oluvannyuma n'akkiriza n'anyigira mu kutendekebwa kwabwe n'okukkiriza kwe okw'obusirusiru okw'obuvubuka. Gye yafunira okusaasira olw'obuto bwe n'okuzikirizibwa kw'amaka ge okuva mu mikutu emibi egy'ebweru.

Mazima ddala, yaweebwayo eri abalabi bano abatono kitaawe eyakkiririza mu bulimba bwabwe nti yandifuuse omusajja omulungi era omuwanguzi wansi w'okulabirira kwabwe era nti bandibadde ng'omukulu we okumuzza mu bantu era nti yandirabye eby'okuganyulwa ebirungi. Baamwawulamu engeri nnyingi ezaalemererwa mu lugendo luno ne bamutendeka mu ngeri nnyingi ez'eby'omwoyo n'emikono emingi ebyakomekkerera era ne biteekebwako akabonero mu butuukirivu olw'ebigendererwa byange.

Naye, Adonai-YHVH yamanya bulungi olunyiriri luno lw'ebintu okuva emisingi gy'ensi nga teginateekebwawo era mu kifo ky'okuggya Biblaridion:144 mu mikono gyabwe, yamukkiriza okugumira ekyo kyokka ekyali kitukana n'ebigendererwa bya Adonai-YHVH wange mu Yeshua HaMashiach okunyweza n'okuweesa ekibya kino eky'amaanyi eky'omusango n'olutalo. Naye, nze Kristo Yesu, nneekweka eri bonna abeenyigiramu naye ne nsigenako naye ebbanga lyonna. Naye, yafuulibwa wa kabi era omusobola olw'obuweereza bwe obwali butegekeddwa eri Heylel Ben Shachar, obwaleetebwa gy'ali abalabi bano abagwa abali mu bujulizi buno. Mu kwagala kwange nnamujjuzamu okwagala n'okusaasira n'okulabirira ne mmuwonya, ne ntonda obwongo bwe obupya mu kwagala kwange eri ye. Mazima ddala, musumba mwesigwa eyalaga Ekigambo kye gye ndi kubanga alaze nti nsobola okumwesiga nate.

N'olwekyo kino kyakkirizibwa okubalaga bonna nti tekisoboka muntu yenna okukwakkula OMUNTU YENNA mu mikono gyange EGY'AMAANYI era n'okugabana Biblaridion:144 mu kubonaabona kwange era abonyeebonye nnyo era abonyebonye ebbanga ddene, naye akyayimiridde n'obuwulize ku ludda lwange nga tasuubira kantu konna.

N’olwekyo, kyakkirizibwa nti atendekebwe mu kasirise ne mu kyama mu ngeri zaabwe ez'okulwana, naye nga nkuumiddwa omukono gwange kubanga nateeka akabonero ku Biblaridion:144 mu buto bwe nga tannayingira mu misomo gya lukale, ye ne mwannyina gye baalondobolwa ne baluŋŋamizibwa mu masomero gano ag'ekyama olw'ekitone kyabwe ekiyitibwa resonansi, ekyali mazima akabonero k'omwoyo omutukuvu akaateekebwa ku bulamu bwabwe omukono gwange era n'obusobozi obw'obutonde obwakolebwa Adonai-YHVH mu bibya bino byombi, abalondeddwa era abaterekeddwa olw'ebigendererwa byange. N'olwekyo, abalabi bano abagwa baamukkiriza n'essanyu era ne batatereka nsimbi mu kufuba kwabwe okumenya akabonero ako akatukuvu ak'obutuukirivu ke nnamuteekako akabonero. Mu kufuba kwabwe kwonna baalemwa nnyo nga bwe kiragibwa mu katabo kano akatono akaggule n'ekigenda okutuukirizibwa emikono gya Biblaridion:144 mu musango gw'abalabi bano abagwa oguliwo KAATI.

Mutegeere nti yava mu maka agaali galuŋŋamiziddwa ekintu ekya bulijjo mu nsi za AmeruKa, era bangi ku mmwe muva mu mbeera ze zimu.

Mwannyina, eyakosebwa mu buto bwe nga tannatuuka ku myaka gya vunaanyizibwa, aliyimirira mu nnyumba yange olw'ebintu bye yayitamu mu buto bwe mu mikono gy'abalabi bano abagwa, n'olw'ebyo ebyamukakibwako mu butamanya mu buto bwe ebyamulumya ennyo n'atasuuka, n'okutuusa lw'atakyayagala kubeera mulamu kubanga yeenenya olw'okufiirwa omwana we eyamusikulwako mu ngeri etali ya butuukirivu mu kibi.

N’olwekyo, nsonyiye bonna abajja gye ndi mu nsonga eno nga banoonya okusonyiyibwa wansi w’omusaayi gwange ogutangirira, kubanga ku Kalvari nnasasulira ebibi abalabi bano abagwa bye bandireetedde era n’ebintu byonna bye baagala okukola n’ebivvulu byabwe, n’eddagala erikosa ebirowoozo.

Mutegeere nti ekisa n'okusaasira biragibwa mu ngeri eno, waleme kubaawo n'omu agwa mu musango ku lw'ebigendererwa bya Adonai-YHVH, okuggyako nga omusango gwamutegekebwa olw'obukakanyavu bw'omutima gwe. Era mu butuukirivu obujjuvu n'obwenkanya obujjuvu, tewali kusosola mu bantu mu kusalawo kuno, wabula omusango omutuukiridde ogusalibwa Adonai-YHVH okusinziira ku mwana gw'endiga gwa Adonai-YHVH ogulituukirizibwa mu bujjuvu Empologoma y'Ekika kya Yuda. N'olwekyo, musanyukire nnyo abooluganda lwa mmwe be mulowooza nti baabula, so nga mu butuufu bateekebwako akabonero, beebase mu ttaka okuzuukizibwa nga temunaba mmwe abakyali abalamu era abalisigalawo. Seera.

N'olwekyo, ajja kuyimirira mu nnyumba yange mu ngeri y'emu kubanga mu bizibu bye ne mu kubonerezebwa kwe, yeenenya mu bujjuvu mu ddakiika ze ez'enkomerero era mu bikolwa ebyo n'ebirowoozo n'ebigendererwa by'omutima gw'obulamu bwe, asonyiyiddwa era ajja kutuukiriza ebigendererwa bye eby'olubeerera mu ngeri y'emu. Yamanya bulungi nti nnali naye kubanga namubikkulira obutonde bwange obw'ekitangaala okumusanyusa n'okumutegeeza awummule bulungi kubanga yali alemereddwa era n'atondozebwa abantu ababi abaatumibwa abaserikale abo abagudde abagenda okugwa mu musango ogw'olubeerera ku nkomerero ya wiiki ya Danyeri ey'amakumi musanvu, era ebiseera byabwe bituuse ddala.

N'olwekyo, mutegeere era muzirike nnyo kubanga nja kusaasira gwennayagala okusaasira kubanga amakubo gange ge makubo ga Adonai-YHVH wange bulijjo alina okusaasira n'ekisa ekingi era bulijjo ayimirira okusitula abo abalemererwa. N'abo abamu mitima gyaabwe n'ebirowoozo byabwe nga tebalina kirala kye baagala okusinga okuwangula ebintu byonna, naye obulamu bwabwe ne bumala gasalibwawo mangu. Wadde kiri kityo, tekiyinzika kubaggya mu mikono gyange, ka mbe kifaanane kitya.

Mutegeere gwennina: Nze mwana gw'endiga gwa Adonai-YHVH era bonna okuggyako abaana b'obutawulize bayinza okwenenya kubanga omuweereza tasigala mu nnyumba ya kibi lubeerera wabula omwana. Olw'ekigendererwa kino, Omwana wa Adonai-YHVH yalabika nsumulule mu bujjuvu era nzikirize buli kabonero konna okuva ku maaso g'ensi emirimu gyonna gya Heylel Ben Shachar. Era bwekityo: Mu maanyi g'omwoyo gwange omutukuvu ogw'ekisuubizo, mwe mbabumbiramu mmwenna, nsumulula emirimu gya Heylel Ben Shachar okuva mu bulamu bwammwe mu nkola yonna gye mulumbibwamu era gye musibibwamu.

N'olwekyo, mutegeere nti, nze Yesu Omunazaaleeti era ali omwana gw'endiga gwa Adonai-YHVH, nnajja okulokola aboonoonyi, nga bangi ku mmwe muli bakulu. Era bwekityo, Adonai-YHVH alonze okubafuula mmwenna ekyokulabirako mu kusaasira kwe n'ekisa kye ky'afukidde ku nsi mu bungi okuyita mu Kristo Yesu, era ye Yeshua HaMashiach. Era n'olwekyo, njaawula ne nnyimiriza ebyokulabirako oluusi n'oluusi mu beesigwa bange, mmwe mwenna mulyoke mumanye obulungi obw'olubeerera obwa Adonai-YHVH, ye El Shaddai Omukulu!

Era bwekityo, abaana bange aboonoonefu mukomyewo eri Adonai-YHVH wammwe mu kugondera okw'okwagala era ne musigala nga muli beesigwa mu mulimu gwonna oba emirimu gyonna gye mwakwasibwa emikono gyange. N'olwekyo, KA KANO njja kubagulumiza mmwenna butereevu mu kisenge ky'Entebe y'Obwakabaka ya Adonai-YHVH!

Mutegeera obukulu bw'okugulumizibwa kuno?

Era kino lwa kuba nti tekinayingira mu mitima gy'abantu ebintu ebitegekeddwa era ebibalindiridde mu mikono gyange, mwasaanidde okusanyuka ennyo mu kifo ky'okulabirira ennaku zammwe ez'omwezi kubanga nnina ku nsi dda mu kiseera kino ekya kaakano.
