Ekikaawa Ekirimu Obuwoomu
Novembba 6, 2024

Bwe Baligamba Nti Mirembe n’Obutebenkevu!

Okuva eri Yeshua HaMashiach, ye Kristo Yesu:

Muwulire Ebigambo bya Yeshua HaMashiach, Yahweh gwe yatuma okuba Masiya wammwe, Kabaka, Mukama wammwe, era Kabona Asinga Obukulu emirembe gyonna okusinziira ku Ntegeka ya Merukizeddeeki, era mukotamye emitima gyammwe mu kuwulira Ebigambo byange kubanga nzijja kulabula abeesigwa mu Kkanisa yange, era n’Omugole wange, (n’abo bonna abalina amatu okuwulira ka babe kumpi oba wala) omulundi omulala ku bulimba obw’amaanyi obuliwo kati obukutte ensi yonna, era obugenda kwongera okweyongera omugya bw’aliggibwawo amangu ago mu ngeri etategeerekese! Saaligamba nti omutunzi w’ennyonyi ategera wa ennyonyi gy’erina okumulabira? Mmwe abalina okukkiriza okutono!

Noolwekyo mbategeeza nate ekyo kye nnayigiriza ab’oluganda lwange bwe nnajja mu Isirayiri ng’Omwana gw’endiga gwa Katonda, kale muwulire Ebigambo byange! Kubanga balibaawo bakristo ab’obulimba, ne bannabbi ab’obulimba, abalaga obubonero obukulu n’ebyamagero, n’okutuusa singa kisoboka, ne balimba n’abalonde bennyini. Laba, mbategeezezza dda. Noolwekyo bwe balibagamba nti, Laba, ali mu ddungu, temugendayo: Laba, ali mu bifo eby’ekyama, temukikkiriza. Kubanga ng’okumyansa bwe kuva ebuvanjuba, ne kulabikira ebugwanjuba, bwe kutyo n’okujja kw’Omwana w’Omuntu bwe kuliba! Noolwekyo mbategeeza nti okujja kwange kuli kumpi era kujja mu ngeri etategeerekese era ey’amaanyi, era sijja kugaanibwa bintu byange bye nnagula n’omusaayi gwange mwennyini, ye mugole wange omwesigwa n’omubiri gwange, ye kkanisa yange ennesigwa gye njagala n’okwagala okutaggwaawo! Muli bange!

Kale bwe kityo, baagalwa bange, bakristo ab’obulimba bagolokose era bangi babaddewo. Naye kati ekiseera ky’ogusota kituuse n’ennaku ya Yakobo etuuse era bonna abasigaddewo aba Adamu ku nsi bateekwa okukola okusalawo okw’olubeerera. Kubanga ogusota guweereddwa okufuga ensi wamu n’abasumba baagwo ababili abakyafu, ye nsolo eva mu nnyanja n’ensolo eva ku nsi, era ye nnabbi waabwe ow’obulimba. Muli mwetegeeza obutagwa mu bulimba obw’amaanyi nga mugondera eddoboozi lyange? Muligezaako okugoberera ensi mu bulimba bwayo n’ebigambo byayo eby’obulimba okutuuka ku kuzikirira okw’olubeerera nga temuwulira ddoboozi lyange, ne bwe mba nzize okubalabula ku bulimba obw’amaanyi obuliwo kati obukutte ensi? Mukkirizza emitima gyammwe n’okukkiriza kwammwe okusensensebwa obulimba obw’amaanyi bwe muweereddwa olw’essuubi ery’obulimba?

Noolwekyo tewali kiyinza kukyusa birango bya musango Yahweh yennyini bye yawa ku Babulooni ow’Ekyama, n’omusango ogugabiddwa mu mikono gyange egy’amaanyi okusumulula abeesigwa bwe bakuŋŋaanyizibwa n’omugya bw’aliggibwawo, era kijja kubaawo mangu ago nga bwe kyasuubizibwa! Emisango egyo gyalangirirwa mu kulabula ab’omu Babulooni ow’Ekyama, okulabula kwe baasuulirira ddala nga bwe baali basekerera mu butakkiriza. Era n’okulabula kwange okungi ennyo okwaweebwa mu mimwa gya bannabbi bange abangi abaabuulira mu bwesigwa Ebigambo bino eby’okulabula okumala emyaka emingi kati. Okulabula okwo kwe nnakomya era mu kifo kyabyo kati waliwo ebirango, ebirango bya Yahweh, n’ebya Yeshua HaMashiach, ebirango ebitasoboka kintu kyonna kuziyiza oba okukyusa. Kubanga ebyalangirirwa mu maanyi n’entebe ya Yahweh tewali ayinza kubikyusa, kubanga bwe zityo engeri z’Omulokozi Katonda ow’Obutuukirivu, akola byonna okusinziira ku kuteesa okw’okwagala kwe.

Kale mbagamba mmwe abeesigwa bange, nga bwe nnagamba Simooni Peetero mu ssaawa y’okuwonga kwange: Simooni, Simooni, laba, Setaani akwegombye, okukusengejja ng’eŋŋaano. Naye nkusabidde, okukkiriza kwo kuleme okuggwaawo: kale bw’olimala okukyuka, onyweze baganda bo. Noolwekyo nzize gye muli mu ssaawa eno ey’enkomerero (kubanga essaawa kumpi okuggwaako) okubalabula mu ngeri y’emu mulyoke mwesonyiwe mu kkubo era mbalagira mmwe abayaka ng’ebitangaala by’Ensi okunyweza baganda bammwe n’okunywezagana mwekka na mwekka n’Ebigambo bino mu kwagala okw’okukkiriza n’okugonda mulyoke mwenna musobole okwekuuma n’okwesonyiwa mu kkubo, kale mugondere eddoboozi lyange mutambuliremu.

Laba! Kyawandiikibwa nti: Era ku lw’ensonga eno Katonda alibasindikira obulimba obw’amaanyi, balyoke bakkirize obulimba: balyoke bonna basalirwe omusango abataakkiriza mazima, naye ne basanyukira obutali butuukirivu. Muligwa mu bulimba buno obutali bwammwe, baagalwa bange? Mazima, mazima, ngamba ekkanisa yange, muliddukira mu mikono gy’abalimba bano? N’abo abatuniddwa okubazikiriza ogusota gwennyini? Noolwekyo, baagalwa bange, mweyongere okuva mu bo mweyawule! Temwenyigira mu bikolwa byabwe ebibi n’ebisuubizo byabwe kubanga bangi basikiriziddwa obulogo bwabwe n’obufumu bwabwe, n’oyo gwe balonze kati ayogera ng’ogusota, n’okukola ebisuubizo ebikulu n’emboozi ennene ku ye yennyini era bangi baligoberera amakubo ge amabi era bangi balilimbwa ne bategebwa ne batwalibwa, kale mukyukire eddoboozi lyange muwulire ebigambo byange muddukire gye ndi baagalwa bange, temukyukira ku kkono, newaakubadde ku ddyo era nja kubakuuma bulungi mu bibatu byange eby’olubeerera ebitayinza kuggyibwamu muntu yenna mu maanyi gange amangi.

Kale lwaki mukkiriza emitima gyammwe okubatyemula, mmwe abeesigwa bange era ab’amazima, n’abo be njagala n’okwagala okutaggwaawo, n’abo bonna ogusota gwe gunoonya okuteega mu bulimba n’omusango ogutali gwammwe. Noolwekyo, temwegatta ku bulimba bwabwe era muve mu bo mweyawule! Ate mmwe abakyuse obulungi okuva mu bulimba obw’amaanyi, mbalagira obutaddayo n’akatono. Laba! Yahweh, Katonda Omukulu ow’Olubeerera, newaakubadde Yeshua HaMashiach, tatumanga oba tatuumanga ntegeka za byabufuzi newaakubadde abafuzi b’ensi okugonjoola ebizibu by’ensi mu kiseera kino. N’ebizibu ebijja mu musango ku abo abali ku nsi okuva eri Yahweh yennyini, omusango ogutayinza kuwonwa muntu yennyini, okuggyako abeesigwa mu Kristo Yesu. Mutegeere bulungi ekyo kye nnawandiisa Pawulo mu b’e Efeso mu bbaluwa gye nnamuwandiisa ku lwange eri ekkanisa yange ey’omukwano:

Eky’enkomerero, baganda bange, mube banywevu mu Mukama waffe, ne mu maanyi g’obuyinza bwe. Mwambale ebyokulwanyisa byonna ebya Katonda, mulyoke muyinzenga okuyimirira eri enkwe z’omulyolyomi. Kubanga tetumeggana na musaayi na mubiri, wabula n’abakulu, n’ab’obuyinza, n’abafuga ensi ab’ekizikiza ky’omu kiseera kino, n’emyoyo emibi egiri mu bifo ebya waggulu. Kale mutegeere baagalwa bange nti ebigambo byange tebikyuka, kale lwaki nze, Kristo Yesu Mukama wammwe eyalinnya mu ggulu, nnandibasindikidde omufuzi w’ensi, oba omufuzi oba kabaka ayinza okuziyiza emisango egigenda okujja? Yahweh, ow’okwagala kwe kwe ntuukiriza mu bujjuvu era atayinza kulimba, ayinza atya okwekontana mu ntegeka ze ez’olubeerera eziweereddwa nze mu bujjuvu okuzituukiriza?

Kubanga abafuzi b’ensi bonna baawa okusiima kwabwe n’amaanyi gaabwe eri ogusota, kubanga bwe kityo bwe baweebwa okukola kubanga n’abalabe ba Yahweh bawulira eddoboozi lye era balituukiriza okwagala kwe okubaweereddwa mu kiseera kino ekimpi ennyo ekigenda okugwa ku nsi n’obusungu obungi! Kubanga ekiseera ky’obusungu bw’Omwana gw’endiga n’obusungu bwa Katonda Ayinzabyonna kituuse era tewali kiyinza kuziyiza, newaakubadde okulemesa n’akatono ekyo ekigenda okusumululwa amangu ago nga maze okukuŋŋaanya abeesigwa bange mu ggulu. Noolwekyo njogerera waggulu! Ani ayinza okuyimirira eri okwagala okw’olubeerera okwa Yahweh, Nze Nzuuno omukulu?!

Naye, mutegeere nti mu kiseera kyange eky’ekisa nnalagira Ekkanisa yange okusabira abafuzi baabwe b’ensi, nga mmanyi nti abafuzi baabwe b’ensi baali babi mu buzaale bwabwe era nti okusaba kw’abatukuvu obubi bwandiziyiziddwa nate (era omugya wange akyaziya), era abafuzi bano baandisobodde okulonda ekyandikoledde obulungi n’obulamu obulungi eri abo abaagala Katonda, era gwe gwali omulembe gwange ogw’ekisa, wadde nga okuyigganyizibwa kwali kungi. Era wadde nga okusaba okw’amaanyi okw’okwegayirira okw’omutuukirivu kugasa nnyo, mutegeere nti tewali bakulembeze ba nsi baweereddwa kulokola nsi okutuusa lwe ndijja n’eggye eddene ery’abakabaka ab’olubeerera n’abakabona abalifuga ensi mu butuukirivu ku kiragiro kyange kubanga nze Mukama wa bakama era Kabaka wa bakabaka era nze Kabona Asinga Obukulu emirembe gyonna okusinziira ku Ntegeka ya Merukizeddeeki.

Mutegeere mu bujjuvu era awatali kukkiriza, tewali bakulembeze ba nsi abayinza okuleeta emirembe ku nsi. Tewali bakulembeze ba nsi abayinza okuleeta obutebenkevu ku nsi. Tewali bakulembeze ba nsi abayinza okuzzaawo obugagga ku nsi. Wadde nga balisuubiza emirembe n’obutebenkevu n’obugagga obuzzeemu, mutegeere nti tebalina maanyi kuwa bintu bye basuubiza, wadde nga baligaba eby’obugagga by’ensi ebitagasa eri abo abakkiriza akabonero k’ensolo, n’empeera y’obutali butuukirivu egenda okubaggyibwako mu bujjuvu mu musango! Kale temubagoberera kubanga bonna abagoberera oluvannyuma balyetaagibwa okukkiriza akabonero k’ensolo era abo abakikola baba baweereddwayo emirembe n’emirembe mu nnimi z’omuliro ezitagwaawo, ez’ekibonerezo, n’ensi n’eby’obugagga byayo ebitagasa biggwaawo. N’ogusota guligolokokera ddala n’ensolo zaagwo, n’ensolo eva ku nsi eteeseeteese obusagwa bwayo obuterekebwa mu kulumwa kw’omusota okuyingiza obusagwa bwayo n’ekyuma kiriteekebwa wamu n’ebbumba, naye tebirikwatagana.

Bwe kityo bwe kyawandiikibwa era ne kiteekebwa mu bujulizi bw'okubikkulirwa kwange, ddala Okubikkulirwa kwa Yesu Kristo, bonna abalina amaaso okulaba n'amatu okuwulira, n'omutima omugonvu okutegeera, balyoke basobole okukimanya. Bwe kityo Yokaana n'awandiika nti: Malayika n'aŋŋamba nti, Kiki ekikwewuunyisizza? Nja kukubuulira ekyama ky'omukazi, n'eky'ensolo emusitula, erina emitwe omusanvu n'amayembe ekkumi. Ensolo gye walabye yaliwo, era nga taliwo; era egenda kuva mu bunnya obutakoma, egende mu kuzikirira: n'abo abatuula ku nsi balyewuunya, amannya gaabwe gaatandika butawandiikibwa mu kitabo ky'obulamu okuva ku lubereberye lw'ensi, bwe baliraba ensolo eyaliwo, era nga taliwo, naye nga weeri.

N'olwekyo, mmwe abaana bange abato, mukkirize okuyiga era mbawa okutegeera, newaakubadde obwakabaka bwa Ggulugu tesobola okugolokoka mu bujjuvu (kubanga omuzibi agenda kuziyiza okutuusa omuzibi lw'ataliziyiza nate, abeesigwa mu Kristo bwe balitwalibwa mu ggulu), abagudde bakkirizibwa okwetegekera okulumbagana kwabwe okw'amangu ddala, era Katonda aweereza abo ababeera ku nsi mu butawulize obulimba obw'amaanyi. Naye ebintu bino si byammwe beesiwa bange era abalonde, era nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, ntegeeza era ntegeeza amazima gano n'ebigambo bino bye mpadde omubaka wange n'omuweereza wange okusomebwa olw'okubudaabuda n'obuwulize, byonna bye bigambo bino bye biteekeddwawo. Bwe kityo mbabuuza abo ku mmwe abandyagadde obulimba buno okusinga amazima gange ag'olubeerera, lwaki mwewuunya? Mujja kukyalondako obulimba bw'abasumba abalina ebikakampa okusinga amazima n'okubudaabuda omwoyo gw'obunnabbi bye guleeta?

N'olwekyo, mbawa okutegeera nti abasumba bano ababiri abalina ebikakampa, abaliwo era abataliwo, naye abajja okubeerawo MU BUJJUVU, tebannagolokoka mu bujjuvu, era bajja kugolokoka mu bujjuvu okuva mu vvu n'ebifunfugu by'enteekateeka enkade, n'enteekateeka yaabwe empya ejja kugolokoka mu bujjuvu! Kati beeyonoonedde Masiya waabwe ow'obulimba, ye Balaba, era ajja kuzimba yeekaalu yaabwe, era ensolo evudde mu nnyanja ekyali mu mbeera ya muto, era balituuka, era balireeta okuzikirizibwa kwa Babulooni Ekyama, kubanga mu bukuusa be balwanira, era bwe butonde bwaabwe.

Era batumiddwa nnabbubi eyalumba ensi, era nnabbubi ye yamulonze okukulembera ensi bwe zirigolokoka mu bujjuvu. Kubanga Ssinagoga ya Sitaani ye yakola okulonda kwayo n'eronze Masiya waayo omupya ow'obulimba, era Babulooni Ekyama eddemu n'ewa Ggulugu olukusa lwayo, n'olwekyo bajja kuweebwayo mu bujjuvu mu mikono gya Ggulugu abawa amaanyi okutuukiriza obusungu n'omusango, omusango oguweerezeddwa ng'omugabo ogw'emirundi ebiri okuva eri Yakuwa yennyini, ogw'omusango ogutalwawo era oguli okumpi, era bwe batyo beeyonoonedde okuzikirira kwabwe.

N'olwekyo Babulooni Ekyama kati erangirira nti ebiseera byayo eby'omu maaso tebijja kubaamu musango gwa Yakuwa nga banoonya okudduka nga banyoomye mu makubo gaabwe agatalina kukkiriza. Kubanga Babulooni Ekyama alukiddwa mu bujjuvu mu lutimbe lwa nnabbubi, era tasobola kudduka, era Babulooni Ekyama ali mu njegere ng'alindirira omusango gwe ogw'enkomerero. Era bajja kweyongera okunyaga, okubbisa, okutta, okunyaga n'okutwala n'amaanyi obugagga bwe n'ezzadde lye, kubanga bwe batyo bwe baweereddwa okutuukiriza, era ettale lirisigalawo we balinyagira ensi okweyongera ku by'obugagga bye beegomba ennyo.

Awo okuzikirizibwa okw'amangu ddala kuligwawo, era mu ssaawa emu alizikirizibwa. Ndamu nate ŋŋamba nti olutalo lujja kubalukawo amangu mu nsi yonna, n'omuliro gujja kugwa, era bajja kuweereza bbomu nnyingi okumuzikiriza. Ekibuga kye ekisinga obunene kijja kwokya, n'amayengo amanene ag'okuzikiriza galirengera ennyanja ye ey'ebuvanjuba n'ey'ebugwanjuba. Ensi ejja kukankana n'amaanyi, era ejja kwabulwaamu wakati, era awali omutima gw'ensi omugagga, amazzi galisigalawo agajja okugyawulaamu ebbiri, era ajja kugwa mu bujjuvu mu mugabo gwe ogw'emirundi ebiri ogw'omusango. Ddala omusango gwe beeyonoonera bwe baanyoomye eddoboozi lyange lye nnatuma ng'anyoolera mu batume bange n'abannabbi n'abaweereza abakulu n'abato: mazima ddala abanyoomye omu n'abonna okutuusa ku kuzikirira kwe, so taliddawo nate kugolokoka. N'olwekyo ensi eweereddwa mu mikono gya Ggulugu, era ajja kuwa obufuzi bwe obw'omuliro eri abasumba be abalina ebikakampa be yagolokosa olw'ebigendererwa bye, era boonooneddwa emirembe gyonna.

N'olwekyo, temugwa mu bukuusa bwe ensolo evudde mu nsi bw'eleeta kubanga ayogera nga Ggulugu. Temugoberera abo ab'omu Babulooni Ekyama abalindirira omusango gwabwe, kubanga omusango gwe ddala gujja kujja mu ssaawa emu, era amangu ago tajja kubawo! Kubanga abo abandisobodde okugoberera Yesu Omunazaaleeti nga Mukama waabwe kati basendeddwa mu mikono gy'omulimba kubanga baagala okubeera mu bulimba bwabwe, nga balowooza nti nga bwe kiri leero, bwe kyali jjo, era mazima ddala kabaka ono omukulu ajja kumpitiriza nate! Naye, ggwe Babulooni Ekyama, toliddamu kuba mukulu nate, n'ebibi byo bisigalawo, era toliwonye musango gwo ogukuli ku ggwe.

Era nze Yeshua HaMashiach, ddala Kabaka wa Bakabaka era Mukama wa Bakama atawadde kabaka yenna ku nsi mu kiseera kino kubanga tewali bufuzi bulala Yakuwa bwe yateekawo, okuggyako Obwakabaka bw'Eggulu, obwo, nze Yeshua HaMashiach ndi Kabaka wa Bakabaka era Mukama wa Bakama! Kale mutegeere nti bonna abeewaddeyo eri omulimba ono ne baleka Mukama waabwe era omulokozi waabwe Yesu Kristo balirekerwa omuliro ogw'okulongoosebwa kubanga muli mu mikono gya Kristo ow'obulimba, era ye Masiya waabwe ow'obulimba. N'olwekyo okwawukana okw'enkomerero kuzze mu mirembe gyange egy'ekisa, era bangi balimbibbwa ne batwalibwa ng'omunyago gw'ensolo evudde mu nsi. Era newaakubadde bangi bajja kuva mu bukuusa bwe ne bayingira mu bulamu obutaggwaawo, kijja kwetaagisa ekibonyoobonyo kya Yakobo okubamenya mu bulimba obw'amaanyi singa banyoomye okuyita kwange okw'okwenenya amangu ago mu ddakiika zino ezisembayo.

N'olwekyo mbawa okutegeera nti Yakuwa awadde ekkondeere ery'enkomerero okuvuga, ddala ekkondeere lyange ery'enkomerero mu mirembe gyange egy'ekisa, era ekkondeere lirivuga. Kubanga ekiseera kituuse era kiri ku mmwe, mmwe abanjagala, lwe ndiserengeta okuva mu ggulu n'oluyoogaano, n'eddoboozi lya malayika omukulu n'ekkondeere lya Katonda, n'abafu mu Kristo balizuukira olubereberye, n'abo ku mmwe abali mu Kristo balibegattako mu bbanga, era bwe mutyo mulibeera nange emirembe gyonna, mmwe abanjagala, era sirireka beesiwa bange eri omuliro ogw'okulongoosebwa kubanga okulongoosebwa kwabwe nnakumalira mu kwagala kwange gye bali kubanga bajja gye ndi kyeyagalire, ddala bwe nnabikkulirwa gye bali.

N'olwekyo mutegeere, mmwe abanjagala, nti essaawa etuuse Ggulugu okugolokoka mu bujjuvu, n'olutalo n'omusango okujja mu bujjuvu, n'ensolo evudde mu nnyanja n'ensolo evudde mu nsi ziyinze okugolokoka mu bujjuvu ne Ggulugu waazo. Singa saafunza nnaku, tewandisigadde muntu mulamu, n'olwekyo okulumbagana kwabwe okw'amangu ddala kujja kujja mu bujjuvu ku nsi, era bajja kuzikiriza ddala Babulooni Ekyama. Kale temugwa mu bulimba bwabwe n'obulimba nga beeyanza era ne boogera lwatu mu maaso g'emisango gya Yakuwa n'obusungu bw'Omwana gw'endiga nti "emirembe n'obutebenkevu" "emirembe n'obutebenkevu"! Kubanga nze Yeshua HaMashiach ajja okuggyawo emirembe n'obutebenkevu ku nsi okutuusa ku nkomerero ya ssabbiiti ya Danyeri ey'amakumi musanvu lwe nditeekawo obwakabaka bwange mu bujjuvu ku nsi. N'olwekyo, temugwa mu bulimba bwabwe.

Era nze oyo atuma Ebigambo bino ng'abiyisa mu mubaka wange n'omuweereza wange, era mu mubaka wange n'omuweereza wange Ebigambo bino mwe bitumiddwa.

Laba! Ekiseera kituuse Mukama wo okumasamasa ekitiibwa kye n'obukulu bwe mu bbanga lyonna bonna okulaba, era ndikuŋŋaanya abeesigwa bange bonna mu Kristo, ddala okuva ku nkomerero z'ensi, nga ndabika mu bbanga okukuŋŋaanya abeesigwa mu Kristo, n'okulabika kwange kulirabika mu ggulu nga laddu bw'eva ebuvanjuba, n'erabikira ebugwanjuba, so siriggaanibwa!

Nze Yeshua HaMashiach ajja okujja okusala omusango n'okulwana olutalo ku nsi, era obwakabaka bwange tebulibaako nkomerero.

Nze oyo gwe nze omukulu gwe yandeeta era ebiseera bituuse.

Nze Mpologoma ey'omu kika kya Yuda era ebiseera byange bituuse.

Byonna biribeerawo emirembe n'emirembe, Yee ne Amiina!

Okuva eri Yakuwa Katonda omukulu Ataggwaawo:

Wulira, Wulira ebigambo bya Katonda Ataggwaawo era ow'Emirembe n'Emirembe! Ebiseera by'okubonaabona kwa Yakobo bituuse ku nsi n'ebiseera by'amakungula bituuse. Awo amangu ago era mu ngeri etasuubirwa omwana wange, Kristo Yesu, era ye Yeshua HaMashiach, alirabikira mu bire, abafu n'abalamu mu Kristo ne bagolokoka okuva ku nsi mu butafa n'obutavunda ne bayimirira nga batondeddwa busa okutuusa emirembe gyonna, ddala bwe baliyimirira mu ntebe yange ey'obwakabaka, ddala ekiseera kye nneegombye era kye nnalindiridde mu mirembe egiyise.

N'olwekyo, mwewale Yeshua HaMashiach kubanga mpadde ekiragiro, era ekiseera ekyo ddala kisemberedde, n'olunaku n'essaawa entuufu kati bimanyiddwa mu ggulu, newaakubadde nga tebinnabikkulirwa mu bujjuvu ku nsi. N'olwekyo ebiseera by'okufuuwa ekkondeere eryo erisembayo bituuse, n'ebibala ebibereberye birikuŋŋaanyizibwa, n'omugole aliyegatta n'omugole we omwesigwa mu ndagaano eteriggwaawo, era balituukiriza ebigendererwa byange eby'olubeerera, n'Endagaano Empya eya Yeshua HaMashiach erinywezebwa mu bujjuvu emirembe n'emirembe, kubanga ebisuubizo byange byonna Yee ne Amiina!

Abeesigwa mu Kristo baliyimirira ku ntebe y'omusango ya Mukama waabwe ne baweebwa olw'emirimu gyabwe, oba mirungi oba mibi, naye bonna balina obulamu obutaggwaawo. Era bangi balitwala empeera ennene ezitannayingira mu mutima gwa muntu mu bujjuvu ebintu bye mbategekedde. Kubanga Kristo Yesu ajja kubafuula Bakabaka ne Bakabona mu maaso g'entebe yange, era balisikira mu bujjuvu entebe zaabwe n'obuyinza okuva eri Yeshua HaMashiach mu busika bwe obwo bwe nnamuwa okugabana n'abeesigwa, era abayite, era abakakasiddwa, era ab'amazima.

N'olwekyo ntegeeza abatuula ku nsi okuwulira n'okugondera mu bujjuvu eddoboozi lya Yeshua HaMashiach kubanga ajja kusala musango n'okulwana olutalo, era okwawukana okw'enkomerero kusemberedde. Naye okusooka ebiseera bya ssabbiiti ya Danyeri ey'amakumi musanvu birituukirizibwa, era Yesu Kristo, era ye Yeshua HaMashiach, ajja ku nsi okulokola ensigalira ya Yakobo kubanga NZE GWE NDI, ddala YHVH Katonda Omukulu Ataggwaawo, era ani alinga nze? Tewali anfaanana, era mazima ddala tewalibaawo.

NZE oyo eyawa enteekateeka zange ez'olubeerera ze nnakweka mu mutima gwange okutuusa lwe zaabikkulirwa eri Omwana wange, Kristo Yesu, n'Ekyama Ekinene Ekya Katonda kibikkuddwa era kinaatera okutuukirizibwa. Era mpadde Enjiri eteriggwaawo eri Omwana wange Kristo Yesu ne ngibikkula naye eri omubaka wange omulonde eyakomyewo mu buwesigwa eri okuyitibwa kwe okw'olubeerera, era ajja kutuukiriza ebigendererwa byange eby'olubeerera wansi wa Kabaka wa Bakabaka ne Mukama wa Bakama, gw'ali owuwe mu bujjuvu.

NZE NZE OMUKULU, n'olwekyo bagambe nti NZE OMUKULU y'atumye Ebigambo bino, era ebiseera bya kaakano.
