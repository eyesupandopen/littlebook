Obujulirwa Obubiri: Isirayiri
Okukaawa n'Okuwooma
Julaayi 15, 2025

Laba! Okulonda Kwo | Ensolo Zibikkuddwa | Ebirangiriro by'Obunnabbi Bitangaziddwa

Muwulirize ebigambo by'Omulokozi wammwe, Mukama wammwe era Kabaka wammwe, mmwe Baana bange, era mutege amatu eri Ebigambo bya Mukama wammwe, ye Yesu Kristo omutuukirivu, kubanga nze mwana wa butuukirivu era ngolokoka mu kitiibwa kyange, era mukoze ng'ennyana ez'omu kisibo mu mirembe n'obutebenkevu, era mbakuzizza olw'ekigendererwa eky'omu ggulu ekigenda okubikkulwa mu bujjuvu mu mmwe, mmwe abeesigwa bange era abalonde. Laba! Nze ngezesa emitima gy'abantu, mulyoke mumanye n'obukakafu oba ddala muli mu kukkiriza, oba mwalimbibwa ne mutwalibwa ebibi byammwe n'obulimba obw'amaanyi obuliwo kati obuwambye Ensi yonna.

Kubanga nze oyo Yakuwa gwe yagabana naye entebe ye ey'obwakabaka, ye Kristo Yesu, Mukama eyafuuka omuzukize era ow'amaanyi, era nze Mukama wa bakama ne Kabaka wa bakabaka, era laba Empologoma ey'omu kika kya Yuda ejja mu bire okwaniriza abeesigwa bange gyendi, balyoke batwalibwe mu ggulu olw'embaga y'Omwana gw'endiga, kubanga Omugole wange yeeteeseteese. N'ettaala ze zijjudde, era alongooseza entambi ze, era alindiridde okujja kwange ng'omuwala omuzaana omugezi, abo be natukuza mu bujjuvu era abataliiko kamogo mu maaso g'entebe ya Katonda. Abo abatakyusibwa bukujjukujju bwa Balaba, kubanga Balaba aleeta okujeemera okw'effujjo, entalo n'akabi, kubanga mu kiseera kino ye mutwe gumu mu mitwe egiwerako egikolera awamu, naye ajja kugolokoka nga Moshiach ow'obulimba, era alondeddwa abantu b'omu nsi okufuga ekkuŋŋaanirizo lya Sitaani. Kubanga ye musika w'abo bajjajjaabwe be baalonda, era beeteeseteese okumwaniriza nga Kabaka waabwe.

Naye ggwe, Ggwe Ekanisa y'e Lawodikiya, kaakano obudde bwo obw'okuzikirizibwa n'omusango butuuse. Kubanga mwerondedde omulokozi omulala okuba Kabaka wammwe, ye oyo ajja n'obubonero n'ebyamagero eby'obulimba era abakulembezezza kati n'obulogo n'okusamira. Nze Yeshua HaMashiach era nabagula n'omusaayi gwange ogw'omuwendo, ogutaliiko kibi, era nze ayinza okubafuula abalamu obulungi, n'okubatonda obuggya mu mwoyo gwange, kubanga nfuuliddwa omwoyo oguwa obulamu kubanga bwe kityo Yakuwa kye yalagira nti obukulu bwonna bube ku Mwana we, ye oyo okutuukirira kw'omwana gw'endiga owa Katonda n'Empologoma ey'omu kika kya Yuda! Era mu butuukirivu nja kusala omusango n'okulwana kubanga omwana gw'endiga owa Katonda n'Empologoma ey'omu kika kya Yuda bituukiridde mu nze, Kristo Yesu, ye Yeshua HaMashiach kubanga bwe ntyo bwe ndi!

Nja kumalayo okubalokola, ggwe Ekanisa y'e Lawodikiya, naye temweteeseteese kuyingira mu Bwakabaka bwange mu kiseera kino kubanga mwakyuka okuva ku kigendererwa kyammwe eky'omutima gumu mu Kristo! Nja kubalaga ensobi yammwe, era ddala mulina omukisa okwenenya ebibi byonna, naye temulina kiseera kyonna kisigaddewo kya kuwakana na ddoboozi lya Mukama wammwe, kubanga ekyo kibi kikakali kimu ku bingi Sitaani by'akozesezza okubasiba obutajja gyendi mu kwenenya ndyoke mbatukuze mu musaayi gwange ogutangirira okuva mu bibi byammwe.

Laba! Ebiseera by'Abamawanga bikomye era n'omuzeyituuni ogw'omu nsiko ogwasibibwamu guli mu bo, era wamu nabo ne gugabana ku kikolo ne ku masavu g'omuzeyituuni, kati gugenda kutemebwako. Kubanga mwanyooma Ebigambo bya Kristo Yesu, kubanga nabagamba lwatu obutekwansasira matabi agaagwa mu butakkiriza: naye bw'okwewaanira, tossanidde kikolo, wabula ekikolo kye kikusanira. Nze Kikolo kya Yese, era nze oyo ayimiridde ng'akabonero k'essuubi ery'ebiseera byonna, ye oyo aleeta obulokozi n'amakubo ga Yakuwa ku nsi era nja kufuga obusika bwange okuva ku ntebe ya Dawudi mu butuukirivu era nagula ensi mu musaayi gwange ogutaliiko kibi era nja kutwala ekyo ekyange ku lwa nsonga n'amaanyi okuva mu mikono gy'abeesenza, abo abagwa.

Nze oyo eyawa ekisa n'okusaasira ku nsi, kubanga Yakuwa yampa emirembe gyonna egy'ekisa olw'obulokozi bw'Abamawanga. Kubanga ebigendererwa bya Yakuwa mu Isirayiri bulijjo byali nti Abamawanga baali ba kuweebwa omukisa ne bajja eri obulokozi okuyita mu kugonda kwa Isirayiri. Naye oluvannyuma lw'ekiseera baafuuka ab'amalala, era ab'ekivve, era ne batemebwa ku muzeyituuni ogw'obutonde mu butakkiriza. Naye ggwe, Ekanisa yange ey'e Lawodikiya, nawe oli kifaananyi kya kika kye kimu eky'amalala, obutakkiriza n'obuzibe bw'amaaso obwalagibwa amatabi ag'obutonde agaatengebwa mu butakkiriza, era nawe kati ogenda kutemebwako ku muzeyituuni ogw'obutonde. Era kaakano nja kukyuka okumaliriza omulimu gwange ku muzeyituuni ogw'obutonde, ye Isirayiri ow'oluganda lwange n'abantu bange, be abo bonna be nnalonda okunzikiriza ng'Oweekitiibwa waabwe ndyoke mbatonde ng'omuntu omu omujja era nti ekyama kya Katonda kimalirizibwe, kwe kugenda mu maaso kw'emirimu gyange egy'okutangirira egy'obulokozi.

Naawe, Ekanisa yange ey'e Lawodikiya, waleka okwagala kwo okwasooka era oli mu mikono gy'omulimba gwe walayirira obwesigwa. Mwerondedde Balaba omujja okuba Kabaka wammwe era mwagaana Yesu Omunazaaleeti okuba Mukama wammwe era muli mu mikono gy'omulokozi omulala ow'obulimba. Oyo eyeefuula omulokozi wa Babulooni ow'ekyama, ayogera era akola ng'omu ku mmwe, naye ayogera ng'ogusota. Kubanga Babulooni ow'ekyama abeera mu bulamu obw'effujjo n'obugagga obungi, essanyu n'obugagga.

Era Babulooni ow'ekyama atamiize ensi yonna obucaafu bw'obwenzi obuli mu kikompe kye ekya zzaabu, naye bwe zityo engeri z'abo abagwa, abawa obucaafu obusinga obubi, era mmwe mubunywa kyeyagalire n'essanyu lingi kubanga muzibiddwa amaaso ekikompe ekya zzaabu obutamanya bucaafu bwe munywa mu bucaafu. Kubanga n'Ekanisa yange ey'e Lawodikiya tekyakuŋŋaanira ku byawandiikibwa byange ebitukuvu wabula ku nkuŋŋaana za Balaba, gwe mwesiga. Era bwe mutyo mwerondera omulokozi omujja kubanga mwagala ensi n'ebintu eby'omu nsi era temusobola kutegeera kirowoozo nti mwandifiiriddwa eby'omuwendo byammwe eby'omuwendo, okuggyako nti nja kubaggyako amasanyu gonna mu busungu bwange obujja, era na kati okufiirwa kwammwe kweyongera!

Kubanga muzibe bwa maaso era mulimbibwa mu malala gammwe nti Yakuwa yandibawadde omulokozi omulala atali nze, Yesu Omunazaaleeti, kubanga nze nze ezzadde erayirirwa ery'omukazi ate Ddaani ye mwana w'empologoma atavaamu Masiya. Mumpulire era mumpulire bulungi, ggwe ekanisa y'e Lawodikiya, kubanga kati lwe lunaku lw'okwenenya kwammwe okuva ku kibi ky'emitima gyammwe oba kati lwe lunaku lwe nja okubaleka mu muliro ogutukuza era bonna abakoowoola erinnya lya Mukama okutuusa ku nkomerero balilokolebwa era bangi abaayatula erinnya lyange mu buteesigwa baligwira ddala mu kusaanawo, nga basizza omukisa gwabwe ku ngeri ey'okutya Katonda eyagaana amaanyi gaakyo: Mmwe ab'e Lawodikiya bange, nze maanyi gaakyo era mu nze Yakuwa mw'assa obukulu bwonna, era mmwe mwannenyeza, Mukama wammwe era omulokozi wammwe, era nja kubasesema okuva mu kamwa kange okuggyako nga mwenenyezza kaakano!

Mu kiseera kyange eky'ekisa, obulokozi bwasaasaana mu mawanga, era eggwanga n'eggwanga byagabana ne bigabana ku kikolo ky'omuzeyituuni ogw'obutonde. Era kati, amawanga tegakyaliwo ng'ettaala y'obulokozi era ekiseera kyabwe mu Mwana kyajja lwa kugaana kwa baluganda lwange n'abantu bange kubanga baayogerera waggulu mu butakkiriza nti tuteere Balaba! Naye ebyama bya Yakuwa bwe byabikkulwa abatume bange abatukuvu ne bannabbi, nazwiyiza Balaba era saamuleka kutwalibwa mu bujjuvu, wabula nakkiriza ekyama ky'obutali butuukirivu okugenda mu maaso. Era kati ebiseera bituuse lwe nja okuleka Balaba mu bujjuvu ng'omuziyiza wange aggyiddwawo era ng'abeesigwa bange, abayite, n'abalonde bange batwaliddwa mu ggulu gye mbategekedde ennyumba zaabwe ez'olubeerera, era balyambazibwa bafuta empanvu etaliiko bbala etasobola kucaafuwazibwa oba kutonnyebwako.

Muzibe bwa maaso nnyo eri ensi yammwe, era muzibe bwa matu nnyo n'obutakyawulira ddoboozi lyange ggwe Ekanisa y'e Lawodikiya? Mukkirizza obulimba obwaleetebwa ekyama ky'obutali butuukirivu, era mukkirizza ekizimbulukusa eky'obutwa okuyingira mu Baibuli zammwe ezonooneddwa ezireese okwononeka okutuukiridde mu Makanisa gammwe okukosezza emitima gyammwe n'enfumo mu butakkiriza obw'ensobi. Mwakkiriza obulimba bw'abalogo ab'ebigambo eby'emirembe okubaloga n'ebiwandiiko eby'obulimba n'ebigambo eby'obulimba. Temukyategeera byawandiikibwa, era mwerundidde ebizibu bingi ebigenda okubatuukako amangu ago. Buli Kigambo kya Katonda kirongoofu era kigendereddwamu okugondera okukkiriza n'okuzimba abo abakiweereddwa. Tegeera Ebigambo by'Ebigambo byange era otegeere ebiseera by'obeeramu. Kubanga nalagira Yokaana Omubikkulirwa okuwandiika nti: Ne ndaba ensolo eva mu nnyanja, erina emitwe musanvu, n'amayembe kkumi, ne ku mayembe gaayo engule kkumi, ne ku mitwe gyayo erinnya ly'okuvvoola.

Era nate Yokaana Omubikkulirwa n'awandiika: Nalaba omukazi ng'atudde ku nsolo emmyufu, ejjudde amannya g'okuvvoola, ng'erina emitwe musanvu, n'amayembe kkumi. N'omukazi yali ayambadde olugoye olwa kakobe n'olumyufu, era ng'ayonjeddwa zzaabu, n'amayinja ag'omuwendo ne luulu, ng'alina ekikompe ekya zzaabu mu mukono gwe, ekijjudde emizizo n'obucaafu bw'obwenzi bwe.

Era nate: N'amayembe kkumi ge walaba ku nsolo, ago galyakyawa omwenzi, ne gamufuula matongo, ne gamwambula obwereere, ne galya ennyama ye, ne gamwokya omuliro. Kubanga Katonda yateeka mu mitima gyabwe okutuukiriza okwagala kwe, n'okukkiriza, n'okuwa obwakabaka bwabwe ensolo, okutuusa ebigambo bya Katonda lwe birituukiririra. N'omukazi, gwe walaba, kye Kibuga ekyo ekinene ekifuga bakabaka b'ensi.

Baana bange, mutegeere nti ensolo esobola okutegeeza abantu ssekinnoomu era n'obwakabaka, muyige okumanya. Ensolo esitula omwenzi ejja kuzikiriza omwenzi era tejja kubeera wansi w'omwenzi nate, kubanga afuze bakabaka b'ensi mu bwenzi bwe. Kale ensolo eneegolokoka okuva mu nnyanja ejja kufulumya Mahdi gwe basuubira, naye era ejja kuzikiriza ddala Babulooni ow'Ekyama, kubanga Katonda awaddeyo omwenzi omukulu mu mikono gy'abalabe be abamukyawa ddala.

Ensolo ey'oku nnyanja n'ensolo ey'oku nsi nga tezinaba kugolokoka mu bujjuvu mu Bwakabaka bw'ogusota, waliwo abafuzi bangi abali wamu mu kigendererwa okuwa amaanyi gaabwe n'obufuzi bwabwe eri ogusota, kubanga Katonda akiteese mu mitima gyabwe okukituukiriza. Era balizikiriza Babulooni ow'ekyama. Olowooza nti omanyi bulungi ebyawandiikibwa byange ebitukuvu kubanga okkirizza abasomesa bo ab'obulimba? Tegeera era owulire Ebigambo bya Mukama wo!

Nate, nalagira Yokaana Omubikkulirwa okuwandiika: N'ensolo gye nnalaba, yali efaanana ng'engo, n'ebigere byayo ng'ebya ddubu, n'akamwa kaayo ng'akamwa k'empologoma: n'ogusota ne gugiwa amaanyi gaagwo n'entebe yaagwo, n'obuyinza bungi. Ne ndaba omu ku mitwe gyayo ng'afumitiddwa okufa, naye ekiwundu kye eky'okufa ne kiwona, ensi yonna ne yewuunya n'egoberera ensolo.

Baana bange, ebigambo bya Yakuwa birimu obuzito obw'ebika ebiwerako, omuntu yenna by'atasobola kumanya. Ensolo ennyonnyoloddwa bwakabaka obujja okugolokoka okuva mu kuzikirizibwa kw'omwenzi, era okuva mu ntegeka enkadde enjja ejja kugolokoka! Kikiikirira ensolo ey'omu nnyanja, kubanga ajja kugolokoka nga Mahdi waabwe, era kikiikirira obwakabaka bw'ajja okufuga ennyanja. Lwaki temussa kitiibwa mu bigambo by'Ekigambo kyange? Tekigamba nti ensolo yennyini ey'oku nnyanja ye yafuna ekiwundu, kubanga muntu ki ddala alina emitwe musanvu? Naye omu ku mitwe gyayo NG'ALINA ekiwundu eky'okufa. Mmwe abasiru, era ab'emitima emigonvu okukkiriza byonna Bannabbi bye baayogera! NG'ALINA ekiwundu eky'okufa. Ebigambo "ng'alina" byandibakomezza, naye temukyakkiriza na katono ku bigambo byange, era nze ndi Kigambo ekyafuuka omubiri. Tekibategeerekese, NG'ALINA, nti muganda muzire ogw'edda? Tekibategeerekese nti "were" (ng'alina) ngeri ya kwogera etali ya bulijjo? Tekibategeerekese nti kumpi okugamba nti, nga bwe kyandibadde bwe kityo?

Naye nedda, mukkiriza obulimba bw'ogusota, kubanga Balaba ayogera ng'ogusota, naye si ye Balaba, wabula ensolo eva ku nsi. Era kaakano ensi yonna ejja kwewuunya olw'ensolo olw'obubonero bwe obw'obulimba n'ebyamagero, era ajja kukola obubonero obulala bungi obw'obulimba n'ebyamagero, era tanaba kugolokoka mu bujjuvu kubanga omwenzi tannaba kwokebwa muliro, naye kati abikkuddwa mu kiseera kye. Ayogera ng'ogusota, ng'agamba nti "Katonda yekka ye yamulokola okuva mu kiwundu kye eky'oku mutwe" era kuno kulimba kwa kuvvoola. Yakuwa, Katonda omukulu ataggwaawo, yantuma nze Yesu Omunazaaleeti okuba omulokozi w'ensi. Nze ne Kitange tuli bumu mu bumu n'ekigendererwa era Yakuwa teyalokola Balaba wadde nze Kristo Yesu. Mwalimbibwa ne mutwalibwa ne mutegebwa mu bulimba obunene, era mbagamba nti mwenenye muddeyo eri okwagala kwammwe okwasooka era mukikole kati, kubanga tewali kiseera!

Kubanga nze ngezesa emitima, era nze ngezesa bonna be mpita eri obulokozi, kubanga ntukuza bonna mu muliro ogutukuza! Laba! Nze ntudde ng'omutekuza era omulongoosa wa ffeeza: era ntukuza abaana ba Yakuwa, ne mbalongoosa nga zzaabu ne ffeeza, balyoke baweeyo eri Mukama ekiweebwayo mu butuukirivu. Temutegeera nti nja kubafuula bakabona ne bakabaka mu maaso ga Kitange? Wadde ng'ekirabo kyange eky'omwoyo omutukuvu, mwe muzaalibwa obuggya mu nsigo etevunda kituukiridde era tekyetaaga kulongoosebwa, mmwe muli ba zzika lya Adamu mu musaayi era mulina okukkiriza omulimu gwange okutuukirizibwa mu bulamu bwammwe, okuggyako ng'ensigo yange egudde ku lwazi.

Kati bujja obudde bw'omusango n'ebiseera by'ekisa bikomekkerezeddwa. Wadde nga bangi bajja kunyooma ebigambo byange ne bakyuka mu butakkiriza, Yakuwa amanyi dda ebigenda okubaawo. Kubanga bulijjo Yakuwa akakasa abantu amakubo gaabwe amabi era anenya abantu olw'ekibi, ng'amanyi mu kwagala kwe n'amagezi ge ag'emirembe n'emirembe nti alirokola abamu. Era nga Yakuwa bwe yakakanyaza omutima gwa Falaawo, bwe kityo bw'ajja okukakanyaza emitima gya bangi abawulira naye ne batawuliriza. Yakuwa ajja kugezesa omuntu emirundi n'emirundi. Era abamu baliba nga Falaawo ne bakakanyaza emitima gyabwe nate mu butakkiriza naye abamu baliva mu makubo ga Falaawo ne bagolokoka okutuuka ku bulamu obutaggwaawo mu Kristo Yesu, naye okuyita mu muliro ogulongoosa.

Mutegeere nti abo abalonda Balaba balyefuula abagabana mu misango gya Balaba, n'abo abagaana okukoowoola erinnya lyange okutuusa ku nkomerero bonna balisalirwa omusango kubanga baasanyukira obutali butuukirivu. Kubanga omulokozi wammwe omujja si mulokozi wabula nsolo ey'ettima. N'entegeka ye n'obwakabaka bwe bikolebwa abo abalumya abaana. Temutegeera makubo ga Gusota? Kubanga abo abagoberera amakubo g'ogusota balya abaana. Bawaayo abaana eri Moloki, banoonya oluzzi lwabwe olw'obuvubuka mu musaayi gw'abaana ababonyebonyebwa, basobya ku baana olw'okwemanyisa kwabwe okubi, era bakola ekikolwa eky'okutta omwana ali mu lubuto okutta abato nga tebannaba kuzaalibwa, era babakulembezezza mu nkukutu mu makubo gaabwe, kubanga bwe gityo amakubo g'abakulembeze ba Babulooni ow'Ekyama era n'obwakabaka bw'ogusota. Era kati gujja omusango gwa Katonda olw'ebintu bino n'ebirala bingi. Kubanga singa temwalaba bintu bino temwandibadde na kibi, naye kati mulaba era ekibi kyammwe kisigalawo.

Temusomanga? Bw'atyo bw'ayogera Mukama nti, Eddoboozi lyawulirwa waggulu, okukungubaga n'okukaaba amaziga. Laakeeri, ng'akaabira abaana be, yagaana okubudaabudibwa olw'abaana be, kubanga tebaaliwo. Kubanga mwagaana obuvunaanyizibwa obwabaweebwa, era mwesigibwa ebyama byange ne muyimirira ng'ekitangaala ky'obulokozi n'akabonero k'essuubi. Naye kati mwegasse ku abo be nnabatuma okulwanyisa. Era kati bujja obudde bwe nja okubaleka mu biseera byammwe eby'omu maaso.

Mumpulire era mumpulire bulungi mmwe mwenna abagaana eddoboozi lya Yeshua HaMashiach, eddoboozi lya Kristo Yesu: temulina kiseera! Mbasaba mummpulire mu kwagala kwange eri mmwe baana bange era mwenenye kati! Kubanga omuliro ogulongoosa gwagala okubagwako mu bujjuvu, era gulirongoosa ensi okuva mu bwakabaka bwonna obw'ogusota era ensi tejja kuba wansi w'amakubo ga Sitaani nate era n'obwakabaka bwe tebujja kugolokoka nate, kubanga nze Mukama wa bakama ne Kabaka wa bakabaka, era nja kuzikiriza ddala abo abagwa. Bonna abagaana okwenenya balifuna omugabo gwabwe mu nnyanja ey'omuliro era baligabana ekibonerezo kya Setaani ne bamalayika be mu nnyanja eyo ennene ey'omuliro ogw'ekibiriiti. Mu ngeri y'emu, bonna abeenenya nga bava mu kibi ne bakoowoola erinnya lyange okutuusa ku nkomerero balilokolebwa era baliweebwa empeera ennene ey'emirembe n'emirembe mu nze.

Laba! Kati bwe budde obw'okukuŋŋaanya abeesigwa, era kati nja kuleka abo abagaana eri Balaba, kubanga bakoze okulonda kwabwe.

Nze mwana wa butuukirivu era ngolokoka mu kitiibwa kyange.

Nze ngezesa emitima gy'abantu.

Nze oyo Yakuwa gwe yagabana naye entebe ye ey'obwakabaka.

Nze Mukama wa bakama ne Kabaka wa bakabaka.

Nze Yeshua HaMashiach era nabagula n'omusaayi gwange ogw'omuwendo, ogutaliiko kibi!

Nze ayinza okubafuula abalamu obulungi, n'okubatonda obuggya mu mwoyo gwange.

Nze oyo ayimiridde ng'akabonero k'essuubi ery'ebiseera byonna.

Nze oyo eyawa ekisa n'okusaasira ku nsi.

Nze maanyi gaakyo era mu nze Yakuwa mw'assa obukulu bwonna.

Nze ngezesa emitima gy'abantu.

Nze ngezesa bonna be mpita eri obulokozi.

Nze ntudde ng'omutekuza era omulongoosa wa ffeeza: era ntukuza abaana ba Yakuwa.

Nze Mukama wa bakama ne Kabaka wa bakabaka, era nja kuzikiriza ddala abo abagwa.

Nze oyo NZE NDI ow'amaanyi gwe yantuma era nzize kusala musango na kulwana.

Laba! Nze Yeshua HaMashiach ntumye ebigambo bino okuyita mu muweereza wange, era okuyita mu muweereza wange ebigambo bino bitumiddwa!

Abo abatakyuka ku Masiya ono ow'obulimba balisanga obulokozi mu muliro ogulongoosa, oba baligwira ddala mu kusaanawo okw'emirembe n'emirembe: Okulonda kuli mu mikono gyammwe!

Kubanga Tebaalina Kwagala kwa Mazima, balyoke Balokoke

(Era kiribaawo nti omutwe gwe gulirabika ng’ogulina ekiwundu eky’okufa, naye ekiwundu kye eky’okufa ne kiwona, era ensi zonna ziriraba ne zeewuunya, ne zigoberera ensolo. Bw’atyo omulimba ono aligolokoka.)
