Omusango Gutandikira mu Nnyumba ya Katonda!

Muwulire ebigambo bya Yeshua HaMashiach, mmwe abantu b'omu nsi yange, mulyoke mutegeere amakubo gange era mujje eri Masiya wammwe ow'amazima, kubanga mbakoowoola, ggwe Isiraeri, onaddayo okuwulira eddoboozi lyange? N’olwekyo, mutege amatù gammwe mu buwulize era mukomole emitima gyammwe, omuntu omugezi ali mu mmwe alyoke yongere okuba omugezi ennyo bw’anaafuuka omuwulize eri okuyigiriza kwange era omukkakkamu eri ebiragiro byange. Kubanga nzize okubategeeza ebikwata ku abo abalikkiriza akabonero k'ensolo, mulyoke mutegeere entandikwa yaabwe okutuuka ku nkomerero yaabwe, era muleme okutegulwa ne mutwalibwa olw'ekyo.

N'olwekyo, mutegeere ebiseera mwe muli era mutegeere okwawukana okw’enkomerero okugenda okutandika amangu ddala ng'abeesigwa bange mu Kristo bakuŋŋaanyiziddwa gye ndi mu bire amangu ago mu ngeri etasuubirwa. Bwe kityo bwe kiri era bwe kityo bwe kiriba nti nja kuserengeta okuva mu ggulu okutuuka mu bire mu ngeri etasuubirwa okukuŋŋaanya abeesigwa mu Kristo, ekkondeere lya Katonda bwe lirivuga n'omwanguka omunene n’eddoboozi ly’omumalayika omukulu. Abafu mu Kristo ne balyoka basooka kuzuukira, n'abo abalamu abalisigalawo ne bakuŋŋaanyizibwa mu bire okusisinkana Mukama waabwe ow'emirembe n'emirembe mu bbanga, ne batwalibwa mu ggulu mu mirembe, era bwe batyo bwe balibeera naye emirembe gyonna era ne bafuulibwa okufaanana nga Mukama waabwe ow'obutuukirivu ow'emirembe n'emirembe!

Naye, okwabika kwange tekuliba mu kyama nga Baibuli z'okunoonyereza ez'obulimba bwe ziyigiriza, so tekulibaawo kwawula kwa bulimba okuserengeta ku nsi okwawula Isiraeri ku Kkanisa yange ey'amazima, kubanga abeesigwa mu Kristo Yesu be Isiraeri wa Katonda era balitwala ebifo byabwe mu mitendera n'ebika by'omu ggulu era be muntu omu omuggya. Bwe kityo bwe kyawandiikibwa mu bbaluwa ya Pawulo eri Abagalatiya nti: Kubanga mu Kristo Yesu okukomolebwa tekuwagasa kintu, newakubadde obutakomolebwa, naye ekitonde ekiggya. Era bonna abatambulira mu tteeka lino, emirembe gibeerenga ku bo, n'okusaasira, ne ku Isiraeri wa Katonda. N’olwekyo, mutegeere nti nze oyo alireetera Isiraeri obutaddamu kuba bwakabaka obwawuddemu emirembe gyonna. N’olwekyo, mugaane obulimba bw'abalogo Abaziyooni abayigiriza eby’okwawula, era mukkirize amazima ga Yeshua HaMashiach!

N’olwekyo mbawa okutegeera nti eby'okwawula n'okutwalibwa mu kyama bye bintu ebyazulibwa Sitaani, mw’anoonyeza okutega abatalina magezi n’okutwala abakkakkamu n’abawombeefu mu mutima n’obukujjukujju ng'akozesa enfumo ezitungiddwa n'amagezi n’emboozi ez'obulimba. Naye, okwabika kwange kulibaawo ddala nga bwe kyawandiikibwa era kulirabibwa bonna n'ensi erimanya nti Kristo Yesu alabise nga bwe yasuubiza abatuukirivu be abeesigwa, n'aleka n'abannyogoga eri okuzikirizibwa kw'omubiri gwabwe ogw'ekibi ogubasibye mu kibi, era bwe kityo bwe kirikolebwa.

Naye, abeesigwa mu Kristo balikuŋŋaanyizibwa nga bwe kyawandiikibwa mu byawandiikibwa eri Mukama waabwe ow’emirembe n’emirembe! Era siriganyibwa mu ngeri yonna okuggyibwako obutaka bwange bwe nnagula, era nja kusikula n'amaanyi abo bonna abali abange mu bwesigwa, oba nga beebase mu mutima gw’ensi oba nga bali mu balamu ku maaso g'ensi: Balikuŋŋaanyizibwa gye ndi mu bbanga mu ngeri y'emu. Naye, abagudde balirimba n’olulumba lwabwe olw’obulimba olw'abava mu bintonde ebirala gye baligamba nti abava mu bintonde ebirala be baatwala abo abakuŋŋaanyiziddwa mu Kristo era olutalo olunene lulibalukawo mu nsi yonna, era abagudde balirimba emirundi mingi, mmwe temukkirizanga kigambo kyonna kye boogera. Kubanga okukuŋŋaanyizibwa kw'abeesigwa mu Kristo kuweereddwa ng'essuubi n'obukakafu nti okujja kwange okw'okubiri kuli kumpi okujja ku nkomerero ya ssabbiiti ya Danyeri ey'amakumi musanvu, eyo ye ssabbiiti y'emyaka musanvu buli lunaku lwa ssabbiiti nga lubalibwa ng'omwaka.

Naye, abannyogoga tebalisigala nga bannyogoga kubanga ebiseera by’okwawukana okw'enkomerero biriba tebirina kye biziyiza omuzibi wange bw’aliggibwawo n’omuzira ne gugwa. Era mu ngeri y’emu abannyogoga balituuka ku kusalawo kwabwe, abamu gye balifuuka abookya ennyo mu kwewaayo kwabwe n’obukakafu eri Mukama waabwe Yesu Kristo, ate abamu ne bafuuka abannyogovu ng’omuzira ogugenda okugwa mu nnyanja, ne beeronderawo okuzikirira kwabwe okw’emirembe n’emirembe.

Wabula, Nze, Kristo Yesu, nja kukuma omuliro ogutazikire mu abo bonna abalironze obulamu obutaggwaawo mu Yesu Omunazaaleeti era nja kuba nteekamu amaanyi agatasobola kumenyeka okukoowoola Mukama waabwe Yesu okutuusa ku nkomerero, era bwe batyo balituuka ku mpeera yaabwe ey'emirembe n'emirembe ebibonobono gye bitasaana kugeraageranyizibwa n'obuzito obw'ekitiibwa obw'empeera ennyingi ez'emirembe n'emirembe eziweebwa abo abawangula; era nja kuleetera abo abali abawanguzi bange okuwangula mu ngeri y'emu nga Mukama waabwe, nga nange bwe nnawangula abagudde mu ngeri y'emu mu kiseera ky'okuweebwayo kwange ng'Omwana gw'endiga gwa Katonda.

Era bwe kirityo, nti bangi abandyandisobodde okwewaayo emitima gyabwe eri Mukama waabwe Yesu Kristo okutuusa ku nkomerero, balifuuka bannyogovu eri Mukama waabwe ow'emirembe n'emirembe era mu nnaku nnyingi balikyuka okuva mu bulamu obutaggwaawo okudda mu muliro ogutazikira ogw'okubonerezebwamu kubanga baliba bagaanidde ddala Yesu Omunazaaleeti olw’essanyu ly'ekibi era balyeronderawo okubonyaabonyezebwa kwabwe okw'emirembe n'emirembe so nga bandyandisobodde okulonda essanyu ery'emirembe n'emirembe mu maaso ga Yakuwa emirembe gyonna. N'abangi abaali balina okulonda okw'emirembe n'emirembe okufuuka Abaana ba Katonda emirembe gyonna, balikyuka okwegatta ku batambula abakolimirwa abaligwa emirembe gyonna okutuuka ku kufa okw'okubiri.

Naye, bonna balina eddembe ly’okulonda, n’olwekyo abamu balironda n’amagezi ate abamu bangi ne balonda n'obusirusiru. N'olwekyo muyingirire mu mulyango omufunda: kubanga omulyango mugazi, n'ekkubo ddamuwa erigenda mu kuzikirira: era bangi abaliyitamu. Kubanga omulyango mufunda, n'ekkubo ffuuti erigenda mu bulamu, era batono abalizuula ekkubo erigenda emirembe gyonna mu bulamu obw'obutuukirivu obutakoma era tewali kulonda kulala kuliiwo. N’olwekyo, londà obulamu, ggwe n’ezzadde lyo mulyoke mubeere abalamu!

Naye, nzize okukulabula, ggwe Isiraeri, bonna balyoke bawulire ebiragiro byange era bagonderere okulabula kwange, bonna abali okumpi n'abo bonna abali ewala era abasaasaanye, kubanga okusalawo okwo okw’enkomerero okw'okwawukana kusigalirawo abo bonna aba Adamu ababeera ku nsi, ka babeere ba ggwanga ki, oba kika ki, oba lulimi ki mwe bazaalibwa. Era bwe kirityo nti abeesigwa bange mu Kristo bwe baliggibwa ku nsi okutwalibwa mu ggulu okukwekebwa okuva ku busungu obugenda okujja n’okuweebwa empeera mu bujjuvu olw’okumaliriza empaka zaabwe mu buwulize obw'obwesigwa eri Kristo Yesu Mukama waabwe eyazuukira era n'alinnya mu ggulu; nti ensi erifukamizibwa mu butakkiriza obutali bwa bwesigwa ku kwabika kwange n'okuzikirira okw'amangu ago okuddako.

Bwe kityo, kubanga nja kulaga ekitiibwa kyange mu ngeri etasuubirwa mu kwabika kwange okw'amangu ago mu bire era okumasamasa kwange kulimulisa eggulu era bonna baliraba ekitiibwa kya Mukama nga kijjula enjuyi zonna z'eggulu mu kumasamasa okw'ekitiibwa era bangi balitwalibwa mangu ago, n'ensi eriyuguumizibwa n'ekankana n'etengattenga ku maaso ga Kristo Yesu, bangi gwe balinoonya okukweka amaaso gaabwe ku kumasamasa kwe. Kubanga ng'okumyansa bwe kuva ebuvanjuba, ne kulabikira ebugwanjuba, bwe kutyo n'okujja kw'Omwana w'omuntu bwe kuliba.

N'ekibiina ekinene, abeesigwa mu Kristo, balibula mangu ago mu ngeri etasuubirwa nga tewali luba lwasigaddewo ku nsi, kubanga obulamu bwabwe ku nsi mu mubiri oguvunda buba bukomye era empaka zaabwe baliba bazimalirizza. N’olwekyo, balitwalibwa mu kitiibwa eky’omu ggulu, gye baligulumizibwa emirembe gyonna era ne baweebwa empeera emirembe gyonna. Laba! Balifuulibwa okufaanana nga Mukama waabwe ow’Obutuukirivu ow'emirembe n'emirembe, era balyambazibwa bafuta enjeru ennamu ezitayinza kuyonooneka wadde okuvunda. Baliweebwa empeera nnyingi olw’obwesigwa bwabwe n’ebikolwa byabwe eby’obutuukirivu bye baakola mu linnya lyange, ebikolwa bye nnabawa okutuukiriza olw’obulokozi bwabwe bo, omukisa, n’ekitiibwa.

Era bangi balitwala empeera ezitannayingira mu mitima gy'abantu mu bujjuvu mu kiseera kino. Newakubadde, abamu balifuna okufiirwa ebikolwa byabwe bwe biryokebwa omuliro ogulongoosa, ebisusunku byonna biryoke biggibwe mu bulamu bwabwe mu buyonjo obw'emirembe n'emirembe, n'ebbala ly'ebikolwa ebibi biryoke libaggibweko emirembe gyonna. Mutegeere nti tewali n'omu ku mmwe eyatambulira mu bujjuvu mu maaso gange, n'olwekyo, abamu balifiirwa, abamu balifiirwa n’afuna empeera, ate abamu balifuna empeera abali abawanguzi bange abaliwangudde mu ngeri y'emu nga bwe nnawangula abagudde, era nze ntuula ku mukono ogwa ddyo gwa Yakuwa yennyini, Oyo Omukulu era ow'emirembe n'emirembe NZE NDI. Naye, bonna abalitwalibwa n'abakuŋŋaanyizibwa baliba n'obulamu obutaggwaawo emirembe n'emirembe era balibeera n'obulamu obw'obutuukirivu emirembe gyonna kubanga bwe batyo bwe balagirwa.

Mu ngeri y’emu, abeesigwa bwe balitwalibwa, n’abo abalisigalawo nabo balitegeera obuzito bw’embeera yaabwe era ekibiina ekinene kirikkiriza amazima n’ebigezo bye bateekwa okugumira, era bangi balikaaba n’okwowonga nga beenenyezza era bangi balibeera mu bulamu obw'okwenenya obw'okwagala n'okwewaayo, era bangi balifiirwa obulamu bwabwe mu kwagala n'okuweereza baliraanwa baabwe abaliba mu mbeera enzibu era ekibiina ekinene kirijja gye ndi nga bakoowoola erinnya lya Yesu Kristo ye Mukama waabwe ow'emirembe n'emirembe okutuusa ku nkomerero!

N'ensi eriyuguumizibwa n'ekankana, n'okuzikirira okunene kulibalukawo mu nsi yonna, ng'olutalo olw'amangu ago ne kawumpuli, n'obutali bwenkanya, n'enjala n'okufa birireetawo okuzikirira okunene ku nsi era ensi erikyusibwa okuva ku mbeera yaayo ey'omu kiseera kino olw'omusisi omungi omunene era bangi balifa mangu ago awatali ddagala. Naye bangi abalala balisigala nga balamu era balisigala mu kubonaabona okugenda okujja, era bangi balisigala mu bigezo by'okubonaabona kwa Yakobo okujja ku nsi okukema abo ababeera ku nsi, okusobola okulokola abo bonna abalijja okufuna obulokozi mu Yeshua HaMashiach.

N’olwekyo, mutegeere nti waliwo amakungula agawerako aga Adamu. Okukungula kwa ssayiri okw’ebibala ebibereberye kwa beesigwa bange mu Kristo, abo bonna abaatambulira mu bwesigwa mu mwoyo gwange mu biseera byange eby'ekisa n'okusaasira, abo abaalima ebibala by'omwoyo mu bulamu bwabwe olw'obuwulize eri Ebigambo byange nga batambulira mu mwoyo gwange ogw'obwana gwe baateekerwako akabonero okutuusa ku lunaku olunene olw'okununulibwa. Bano be beesigwa mu Kristo era be bawanguzi bange n’omugole wange ne 144,000 bange be ndituma okulokola Yakobo mu mukono gwa Asikenazi ne Esawu.

Oluvannyuma kulitandika okukungula kw’eŋŋaano, eŋŋaano gye zengerera mu nnimiro ne zikutama mu buwulize olw’obuzito bw’ebibala ebibaliddwa, n’omuddo omubi nga gulimu ebitali bibala ne guyimirira butereevu mu bujeemu n’okuwuula okunene ne kubaayo, n’eŋŋaano zange ne zikuŋŋaanyizibwa mu bulamu obutaggwaawo, n’omuddo omubi ne gusibwa, ne gusibwa mu nnyumba, ne gwokebwa mu muliro ogutazikira ogw'okubonerezebwamu. N’olwekyo mutegeere nti bonna abagaana okukungulwa mu buwulize ku gguuliro lyange balyegatta ku muddo omubi guno mu musika gw'oyo eyagwa ogw'ennimi z'omuliro ez'emirembe n'emirembe ezikoleddwa n'ekigendererwa eri abatawulize n'abajeemu, so tebaliwona. Era bwe kityo abataalina kukkiriza n'abataali beesigwa bwe baligenda abagaanidde endagaano ya Yakuwa ey'emirembe, ye Yesu Omunazaaleeti eyandyandibalokodde mu bujjuvu.

Noolwekyo enkomerero y'obwakabaka bwa Setaani bw'eriba, ab'omu nsi yange, era kiriba kiseera kya kwawula n'okukungula okukuumira abatuukirivu emirembe gyonna n'okuwaayo abatali batuukirivu eri okubonerezebwa okw'olubeerera mu busika bw'abo abaagwa, ddala omukisa ogwategekerwa bonna abagenda mu maaso n'obutawulira era abagenda mu maaso n'obujeemu bwa Setaani, n'Abalabi, na bonna abagoberera amakubo gaabwe amabi. Abo balirwana n'Omwana gw'endiga, era Omwana gw'endiga alibawangula: kubanga ye Mukama w'abakama, era Kabaka w'abakabaka: na bali abali ku ludda lwe, abayite, n'abalonde, n'abeesigwa.

N'olwekyo, mutegeere nti okuva mu vvu ly'entalo mwe baligolokoka, ye nsolo eva mu nnyanja, n'ensolo eva mu nsi, n'ogusota! Baligolokoka okuva mu vvu ly'okuzikiriza okunene kwe beereetera bennyini. Naye, balivumirira okuzikiriza kwe beereetera ku bagwira ab'ebweru, n'Abakristaayo, na byonna ebikwata ku Yesu Omunazaaleeti, era bwe kityo Kristo alifuulibwa omujeemu mu bwakabaka bwabwe obwa Setaani. Mazima, mazima, Nze, Kristo Yesu, sirina kitundu kyonna wadde omugabo nabo, naye bonna abakoowoola erinnya lya Mukama waabwe Yesu okutuuka ku nkomerero balilokolebwa.

Bwe batyo balirwanyisa abatukuvu era balibawangula n'obukambwe obunene obulibaddizibwa gye bali awatali kusaasira emirembe gyonna. N'olwekyo, ekibiina kinene eky'ebibiina kingi kirilokolebwa okuva mu kuzikirira okw'olubeerera olw'okuzikirizibwa kw'omubiri gwabwe ogw'ekibi ogwabali gusibye mu busibe bw'ekibi. Kale nno ndiggyawo essanyu lyonna ku nsi era ndimenya obulogo n'obufumu omubi by'abateekako balyoke bazuukuke okuva mu tulo twabwe okubeera abatuukirivu mu maaso gange nga bava mu kibi nga beenenyezza ddala era nga beegaanyi obwakabaka bwa Setaani, bwe baali bawagidde edda olw'okukkiriza mu butawulira.

Era bwe batyo abo abaagwa balyeyoleka nga beefuula abagwira abava mu nsi endala ezetooloola mu bukyamu ezitaliwo mu ngeri y'emu nga ababayigiriza Abajesuiti bwe baayigiriza enkuyanja y'abantu okuyita mu kuyiga okwakira okuyiga. N'okutuusa nti obulimba bw'abo abaagwa buyinzwe okukkirizibwa mu bulimba obunene abo abasanyukira obutali butuukirivu. Kale nno olunyiriri lutegekeddwa abalimbye bano okweyoleka kubanga bonna befuula abafuuyi.

Balyeefuula abayambi ab'ekisa abajja okulokola Adamu okuva mu kuzikirira kwe ye, ddala, okuzikirira kwe kuliba kulabisizza nti be baakukola mu kyama ekinene n'obulimba bwonna n'obubonero obw'obulimba bungi n'ebyamagero. Kubanga balireeta tekinologiya zaabwe n'obukujjukujju bwabwe n'emizizo gyabwe era olukonko luliggulwawo ddala. Ekibi tekiriba kikomereddwa era buli kika kya kibi kirifuga ensi mu kiseera ky'okubonaabona kwa Yakobo. Era kubanga omuntu ow'obutonde yeegomba nnyo ekibi n'ekizikiza, Yahweh kaakano aliwaayo ddala omuntu ow'obutonde mu bwakabaka bwa Setaani n'okutuusa nti abangi balyoke balokolebwe, ddala ng'okuyita mu muliro. Era kino lwa kuba nti omuntu mubi era n'ebikolwa bye bibi era omuntu omubi abyagala mu bujeemu bwe ne Setaani. N'olwekyo, omuntu aliweebwayo ddala eri Setaani abapanga edda enkomerero ya Adamu ey'olubeerera, wadde ng'entegeka zaabwe zirifuuka za busa era okusalirwa omusango okw'amaanyi era okukakali kwe baliba nakwo kubanga be bakisaanira.

Bwe batyo balirabika n'ebigonjoolo byabwe ne tekinologiya zaabwe mu ngalo zaabwe era balirabika nga beefuula abagwira abava mu nsi endala ez'obufuuyi ezitaliwo. Era Balaba aligolokoka, ye nsolo eva mu nsi era agolokoka n'obulogo bw'omusota mu ngalo. Era alireetera bonna, abato n'abakulu, abagagga n'abaavu, ab'eddembe n'abaddu, okuteekebwako akabonero ku mukono gwabwe ogwa ddyo oba ku byenyi byabwe. Era nti tewali muntu ayinza kugula wadde okutunda, okuggyako oyo alina akabonero oba erinnya ly'ensolo oba omuwendo gw'erinnya lyayo. Wano we wali amagezi. Alina okutegeera abale omuwendo gw'ensolo: kubanga gwe muwendo gw'omuntu, n'omuwendo gwayo ye lukaaga mu nkaaga mu mukaaga.

N'olwekyo, mutegeere ebiri mu mawandiiko era n'Omwoyo gwa Yahweh ky'alangirira ddala mu biseera bino eby'enkomerero ab'omu nsi yange mulyoke mutegeere akabi k'ebiseera bye mulimu: Buli muntu asinzizza ensolo n'ekifaananyi kyayo, era ateekebwako akabonero kaayo ku kyenyi kye, oba ku mukono gwe, Oyo y'alinywa ku mwenge gw'obusungu bwa Katonda, weewaawo, ku mwenge omulongoose, ogufukibwa mu kikompe ky'obusungu bwe, era alibonerezebwa mu muliro n'ekibiriiti mu maaso g'Abamalayika abatukuvu, ne mu maaso g'Omwana gw'endiga. N'omukka gw'okubonyaabonyezebwa kwabwe gulinnyuka emirembe n'emirembe: so tebaliwummula emisana n'ekiro, abasinza ensolo n'erinnya lyayo.

Naye, baligolokoka kye kimu, ye nsolo eva mu nnyanja n'ensolo eva mu nsi, n'ogusota era baligolokoka mu bufuzi bwabwe obutatukuvu era balifuga ensi mu butali butuukirivu n'obubi era tewaliba kikomye ku kwegomba kw'emitima gyabwe emibi. Bwe batyo balisuubiza ebintu bingi ebinene era eby'amaanyi. Era balibikka era balinyiriza obulogo bw'omusota mu bulimba obunene n'obulimba obungi n'ebisuubizo eby'obulimba bye balyogerera obusagwa buno obutaliiko n'omu ayinza kubwewala ng'amaze okubukkiriza. N'olwekyo, bonna abakkiriza akabonero k'ensolo baliba beekyusizza emirembe gyonna, ddala enkyukakyuka gye batasobola kwewala kubanga ekikoonyeko tekiyinza kugololwa.

Era bwe batyo balisuubiza obulamu obutaggwaawo, era balisuubiza obulamu obutuukiridde n'eddembe okuva mu ndwadde era balisuubiza okutabagana okutuukiridde ne tekinologiya zaabwe ze baligamba nti zigendereddwamu emirembe, n'obulamu n'okuwonya. Era bonna abakkiriza obulogo bw'omusota balikyusibwa emirembe gyonna okuva mu kifaananyi kya Yahweh mwe baatonderwa, bwe batyo ne beeyita abatambula abakolimirwe abaliba abaddu emirembe gyonna eri okwegomba kw'ogusota. Kubanga mu mazima akabonero k'ensolo kaweebwayo olw'okuzikiriza Adamu abaagwa balyoke babeere n'emyaka gyaabwe egya zzaabu ku nsi bokka n'emizizo gyabwe nga tewali Adamu gwe bakyawa. Ddala entegeka ezitasoboka kutuukirizibwa kubanga tewaliba myaka gya zzaabu gye baliba nayo wabula okusalirwa omusango okukakali era okw'amaanyi mu kifo kyagyo.

Mazima, mazima, okukkiriza obulogo bw'omusota baliba beewaddeyo okukkiriza kwabwe okw'eddembe mu kujeemulukuka eri ogusota kubanga balyetabula n'ezzadde ly'abantu: naye tebaligattibwa omu n'omulala, ng'ekyuma bwe kitayinza kwetabula na bbumba.
