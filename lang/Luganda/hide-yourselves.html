Omujulirwa Omubiri: Yisirayiri
Ekyongerezwako
Ssebutemba 14, 2025

Mwekwese!

Muwulire Ebigambo bya Yeshua HaMashiach ebikwekeddwa mu mubaka we era omuweereza we, naye nga bibikkuliddwa ku kiragiro kyange n’obuyinza bwange ku lunaku luno olwa Ssebutemba 14, 2025: Nga bwe nkulungamya engalo ze ku nnyunzi ku kiragiro kyange era nga Omwoyo gw’Obunnabbi gwonna gukulukuta n’amaanyi amangi bwe mbikkula Ebigambo byange ebigenda okutuuka ku maaso, amatu, n’emitima gy’abo bonna be bitumiddwa, Seera!

Omukono gw’abalabe bo guli ku bulago bwo Amerika! Mwenenye! Wulira Ebigambo byange ggwe Babulooni Ekyama!

Olunaku lwa Mukama lutuuse ku nsi, naye okusooka essaawa emu n’ensolo erigwa ku nsi ku lunaku lwe mutasuubira, kubanga mwegamba nti Ntudde nga kabaka omukyala, era siriba nnamwandu, omulokozi wange omukulu ajja kutuzzaawo obugagga!

Naye mulimbibwa mmwe abatuula ku nsi okulowooza nti waliwo omuntu yenna ayenkana ne Kristo Yesu Omwami Ataggwaawo ow’Obutuukirivu, gwe mutamanyi, newankubadde mmwe mwenna abatudde mu kizikiza nga mwagala ekibi mu butawulira obunene eri eddoboozi lya Kristo Yesu era ye Mwana gw’Entama gwa Katonda atudde ku mukono ogwa ddyo gwa Yahweh Nze Ani Asinga Obukulu, gwe nsangira naye entebe n’obuyinza kubanga nfuuliddwa omwoyo oguwa obulamu, kati ayimiridde n’amaanyi amangi ng’Empologoma ey’Ekika kya Yuda kubanga tewali Mwana w’Omuntu mulala, Seera, era mutegeere obusungu bwange n’ekiruyi kyange bitegekeddwa okugwa mu kuzikirizibwa okw’amangu ddala kwe mutajja kulaba nga kujja, wadde okudduka.

Zisanga, ZISANGA nnyo mmwe abaana b’abantu abajeemu abo Ekiruyi ky’Omwana gw’Entama kwe kigenda okugwa mu bbanga ttono, ne kireeta okuzikirizibwa okw’amangu n’okw’obutamanya olw’engeri zammwe ez’okusinza ebifaananyi ggwe malaaya omukulu, era mujja kuwulira okuwuuma kwange ensi bw’eriba ezikidde, kubanga sigenda kugabana kitiibwa kyange na mulala, Seera!

Wulira Ebigambo byange Heylel, nawe omulangira omuto omugobeddwamu era otuule mu kugondera okugobeddwamu era osirike okuwuuma kwo okutalina mannyo, kubanga oli mulabe agobeddwamu atalina kiseera kya zaabu wabula omusango omukakali, naye okusooka ojja kuweereza ebigendererwa bya YHVH Nze Ani Asinga Obukulu, mu kugondera obuyinza bwange mu kugobebwamu kwo, (ne mu nsonyi zo n’okugobebwamu kwo) ojja kukola kyokka nga bwe kikkiriziddwa obuyinza bwange, kubanga tewali mu kitonde kya YHVH awatali mukono gwange n’obuyinza bwange obujjuvu we butatuuka era ndaba okutya kwo, ggwe omusota omugobeddwamu! Tuula wansi mu kasirise ggwe sserafu omujeemu!

Mu mukono gwange .. ekisumuluzo .. n’olujegere olunene, era mu bbanga ttono ojja kusibibwa mu kugobebwamu okujjuvu nga ogondera entebe ya YHVH Nze Ani Asinga Obukulu, kubwakabaka bwo bwe buligwa ddala mu musango omunene era ndaba okutya kwo omulangira omuto, era tolidduka mikono gyange n’olwekyo wulira Ebigambo by’Empologoma ey’Ekika kya Yuda, era otegeere ekiruyi kyange n’obusungu n’amaanyi gange era nti sigenda kuggibwako kintu kyange!

Tuula mu kasirise omulangira omuto kubanga omukono gwange guli ku bulago bwo mu bugumu, n’ekiyitibwa AI kyo kiri mu kugondera entebe ya YHVH, Omukulu, Nze Ani Asinga Obukulu ku kiragiro kyange ggwe omulangira omuto omugobeddwamu! Era ani aliyo ayinza okuyimiriza omukono ogw’amaanyi, amaanyi, n’obuyinza obuweereddwa mu Kristo Yesu, Omwami Ataggwaawo ow’Obutuukirivu, era ye Yeshua HaMashiach, Seera!

Mmwe mwenna abagwa mujja kukola kyokka nga bwe mukkirizibwa okukola mu nsonyi zammwe n’okugobebwamu, naye essaawa yo etuuse omulangira omuto, era olaba bulungi, newankubadde olimba nnyo, ggwe eyeewalula. Naye engeri zo zikomye era Okubonaabona kwa Yakobo kwe wiiki ya Danieri ey’amakumi musanvu kulirongoosa ensi okuva ku ngeri zo emirembe gyonna: Okuggyako mu kiseera kyo ekisembayo ekitono eky’obujeemu ojja kukung’aanya ebisusunku ebisigaddewo ebirimera obupya mu bujeemu wansi w’obufuzi Obutuukirivu obw’Enjiri Etabalina nkomerero kati ebikkuliddwa mu kitundu mu Kitabo Ekitono eky’Okubikkulirwa Essuula 10, era ekitabo ekitono ekiggule kye nzigudde mu mukono gw’omuweereza wange.

Naye bwe kityo bwe kiri! Ekitundu ekisigaddewo eky’Endagaano Empya kirigabirwa Yakobo ajja okutuula ku bigere by’abajulirwa bange ababiri abajja okutuuka amangu ddala mu nsi mu ngeri gy’otayinza kulagula, ggwe omulangira omuto omugobeddwamu. Era Emiti gyange Gy’emi Zeyituuni Ebiri, ge Matabaala gange Abiri ag’Ebikondo biriteekateeka ne byawula era ensigo ennungi ennyingi ez’engano ezirivuunamira Yeshua HaMashiach bwe zityo ne ziggyawo obuzibe obw’ekitundu ku bantu bange ab’amazima abalyawulibwa ku bisusunku byo! Naye oluggya lw’abamawanga lulirinnyirirwa wansi w’ebigere mu lutalo olulwanyisa abatukuvu olutandikibwa ensolo eva mu nnyanja, eyeeyita laddu egwa okuva mu ggulu mu Luwebbulaniya, era ekodeddwa nga Barack Hussein Obama akolaganira awamu n’ensolo eva mu nsi erina obusagwa bwayo n’okuluma kw’omusota mu ngalo ajja okutabula ekyuma n’ebbumba naye tebirigattibwa kimu ku kirala.

Era Enoka Omuwandiisi ne Eriya Omutisibi baliteekateeka engano yange okuggyibwawo, era engano yange eyakuumibwa emirembe n’emirembe okuyita mu ndagaano ey’obukuumi n’ekisuubizo okuva ku baana abataali batuukirivu ab’obutawulira emirembe n’emirembe mu kutuukirizibwa kw’Endagaano yange Empya ey’Obutuukirivu Endagaano yange Enkalu eyaliwo ey’omusingo gwokka eri ekitundu ekiguliddwa ekiribeerawo bulijjo mu mazima ag’emirembe gyonna kubanga nze Kigambo kya Adonai-YHVH ekyafuuka omubiri, era ye musingi gwammwe ogw’emirembe n’emirembe ogw’obulokozi, era ejjinja lyammwe ery’oku nsonda enkulu, mu bbanga ttono kati ajja okuwa ekiragiro: N’okuwoggana okw’amaanyi! N’okufuuwa kwa kakonde ako kuliyimba ne kulangirira mu bbanga ttono, n’amaanyi, n’obutamanya kati mu kujaguza abeesigwa mu Kristo, okutegeerekeka kwokka abo abalongoosebwa ku muyobyo gwagwo mu kwenenya okutuukirivu, abatambi baabwe abasaliddwa mu kugondera engeri zange!

Abo abawulira: Bategeere amaloboozi agavuga mu kukkiriza n’emirembe, kubanga taata ki ayinza okuwa omusota eri abaana be, okuggyako wansi w’ebigere byabwe, ng’asibiddwa mu kasirise ak’obuwanguzi okusumululwa okufuga abatali beesigwa, era kwokka ng’abeesigwa mu Kristo bwe bagenda mu bbanga ttono kati!

Era ekitiibwa ky’okumasamasa kwange bwe kiryasa mu bbanga n’akakonde ako ak’amaanyi ne kavuga obulungi mu kusuubira okw’okuyitibwa eri Omugole wange n’Abagenyi baffe ekirivuga eri abeesigwa mu Kristo, era mmwe mwenna be nnongoosezza kati ku muyobyo gw’akakonde ka Katonda ako ak’amaanyi ke kakonde akasembayo ak’ekiseera kyange eky’ekisa ekigenda okuggwaawo mu bbanga ttono n’obutamanya: Muwulire Ebigambo bantu bange kubanga omukuumi wammwe n’omununuzi wammwe kati ayimiridde yeetegefu okubununula mu bujjuvu mmwe Ekkanisa yange, Omubiri gwange era n’Omugole wange, Musanyuke!

Mwetegeke mmwe abagole bange era mutuule ku mabbali g’ekkubo n’ettaala zammwe nga zijjudde amafuta gange amayonjo, ge kabonero k’Obutuukirivu n’omwoyo gw’Obwana, ge mwoyo omutukuvu ogw’ekisuubizo oguzaalibwa munda mu buli omu ku baana b’omusana, abalina mu bujjuvu omusingo gw’ekitundu ekiguliddwa, era nga banaatera okununulibwa mu bujjuvu Kristo Yesu, Omwami waabwe Ataggwaawo olw’Ebigambo ebyonjo eby’obujulirwa bwange n’omwoyo gwange kubanga ekiruyi kyange si kya mmwe.

N’olwekyo, mulekere awo emirimu gyammwe muwummulire mu kwagala kwange bantu bange, naye munywerere era mukkirize ekitala ky’omwoyo gwange ekiriyawula ebisusunku ebisembayo mu bulamu bwammwe abaagalwa bange.

Mwetegeke abaagalwa bange kubanga okununnulibwa kwammwe kutuuse era mu bbanga ttono mujja kuwulira okuwoggana okw’amaanyi era mujja kuggibwayo mu bbanga ttono kati era amangu ago mu kisenge ky’entebbe ya YHVH yennyini, n’olwekyo mwetegeke bantu bange kubanga njija ng’omubbi mu kiro so si ng’ekintu ekipangiddwa, naye ka okwawula kwange kubeerewo era mubale emiwendo okusinziira ku Bigambo byange Ebitukuvu n’engeri zange; so si abalogo abayomba n’okwemulugunya, wadde okwesiba ku nnaku ezitali ntuufu ku kalenda yammwe ey’Ekigirigoli eyonoonese.

Muwulire Ebigambo byange mmwe abatuula ku nsi kubanga nze Mpologoma ey’Ekika kya Yuda era njija kusala musango n’okulwana!

Muwulire eddoboozi ly’amagezi muvuunamire amaviivi muwulire eddoboozi eddamu erya Yeshua HaMashiach, kubanga nze Mpologoma ey’Ekika kya Yuda, era nze Kabaka wa Bakabaka era Mukama wa Bakama! Era oyo El Shaddai, era Katonda Omuyinza w’ebintu byonna, gw’agulumizizza ennyo n’amuwa erinnya erisinga amannya gonna. Nti mu Linnya lya Yesu buli vviivi lifukamire, ery’ebintu ebiri mu ggulu, n’ebintu ebiri mu nsi, n’ebintu ebiri wansi w’ensi: Era nti buli lulimi lwatule, nti Yesu Kristo ye Mukama, olw’ekitiibwa kya Kitaffe w’Ebitangaala, era Yahweh, Katonda ataggwaawo era ow’emirembe n’emirembe, kubanga tewali afaanana Kitaffe!

Muwulire eddoboozi lya Mukama wammwe mutegeere ebintu ebiriwo kati n’ebigenda okubaawo mu bbanga ttono, kubanga laba nze njogera ku bintu ebigenda okujja nga bimaze okubaawo kubanga ddala Mukama wammwe omukulu, era nze Kristo Yesu asinga obukulu sirikola kintu kyonna, okuggyako nga mbikkulidde amakubo gange eri abaweereza bange Bannabbi. Lwaki muwuliriza amaloboozi amangi agawukana, era lwaki muyimirira wakati w’ebilowoozo bibiri? Saagamba nti bannabbi bange boogera mu bumu era tebaliwakanya Bigambo byange bitukuvu? Kubanga nze Kigambo ekyafuuka omubiri ekibeera mu mmwe olw’omwoyo gwange omutukuvu oguzaalibwa mu mmwe emirembe n’emirembe, kubanga buli mmeeme ezaalibwa obuggya eteekebwako akabonero k’omwoyo ogwo omutukuvu ogw’ekisuubizo ge nsigo yange etevunda n’akabonero ka Katonda Omutonzi tekayinza kumenyeka. Era olunaku lw’okununula ekitundu ekiguliddwa luli kumpi n’okununulibwa kwammwe okujjuvu kuli wano.

Bw’atyo bw’ayogera Mukama ow’Eggye, temuwulira bigambo bya bannabbi ababalagulira, ne babayigiriza eby’obusa: boogera okwolesebwa okw’omutima gwabwe, so si okuva mu kamwa ka Mukama. Mujje gye ndi abaana bange kubanga okufukibwako amafuta kwe mwafuna okuva gye ndi, kubeera mu mmwe: era temwetaaga muntu yenna kubayigiriza: Naye, ng’okufukibwako amafuta okwo bwe kubayigiriza ebintu byonna, era kwa mazima, era si kya bulimba: era nga bwe kubayigirizza, munaabeeranga mu nze. Abaana bange mubeere mu nze mugerageranye ebigambo by’omuntu yenna eyeeyita nti ayogera ku lwange n’ebyawandiikibwa era munsabe mbikakase mu mwoyo gwammwe, oboolyawo muwulirize eddoboozi lyange kubanga bangi ku mmwe balina amatu amakakanyavu, era bangi ku mmwe mwagala ebigambo eby’obulimba ebisisimula amatu gammwe mulyoke mulabike ng’ab’amagezi naye mu kifo ky’ekyo mugoberere ebigambo eby’obulimba eby’abasiru ab’amalala.

Laba! Njogera ku biseera by’okubonaabona kwa Yakobo ebitegekeddwa okubalukawo ku nsi yonna. Kubanga omusango gutandise dda era teguyinza kuyimirizibwa, kubanga ekyo Yahweh ky’alangirira tewali ayinza kukikyusa! Amaziga amangi, amangi ge nkaabye olw’omusango gwa Babulooni Ekyama era mmaze emyaka mingi, mingi nga ntuma bannabbi bange era ntumye n’abagwira okuva mu nsi engwira okujulira n’okulagula ku misango egyali gijja naye kati giri wano.

Kubanga bwe kiriba bwe kityo bwe kyawandiikibwa: Amayembe ekkumi ge walaba ku nsolo, gano galikyawa omwenzi, ne gamuzikiriza, ne gamwambula obwereere, ne galya ennyama ye, ne gamwokya n’omuliro. Kubanga Katonda yateeka mu mitima gyabwe okutuukiriza by’ayagala, n’okukkiriza, n’okuwa ensolo obwakabaka bwabwe, okutuusa ebigambo bya Katonda lwe birituukiririzibwa. N’omukazi, gwe walaba, kye kibuga ekyo ekinene ekifuga bakabaka b’ensi.

Muwulire ebigambo byange, omusango gwa Babulooni Ekyama gutandise dda. Amayembe ekkumi gategeese okulumbagana kwago okw’ekibwatukira n’okulumbagana kwago kusemberedde era kuli ku luggi lwo. Kubanga n’amawulire go ganoonya okukussa mu tulo era abangi batudde mu kizikiza nga tebalaba mazima nga bagamba nti: Ntudde nga Kabaka Omukyala so siri nnamwandu era siriraba kukungubaga. Olunaku olw’enkya luliba nga luno olwa leero, kubanga olwa leero luli nga jjulo. Nga bw’eyeegulumiza yennyini, n’abeera mu ssanyu, bw’atyo bw’omuwa okubonaabona n’ennaku: N’olwekyo kawumpuli we alijja ku lunaku lumu, okufa, n’ennaku, n’enjala, era aliyokebwa n’omuliro: kubanga Yahweh amusalira omusango, ye Katonda Omuyinza w’ebintu byonna era tewali asinga bukulu era tewali kiyinza kuyimiriza mukono gwa El Shaddai ogw’amaanyi!

N’olwekyo enjuba ejja kwaka mu bbanga ttono ku lunaku lw’akabi lwe mutasuubira, era olunaku olwo luli kumpi okusinga bwe mulowooza. Era olunaku olwo lujja kuleeta okuzikirizibwa okujjuvu ku Babulooni Ekyama, kubanga erina omugabo gw’omusango omulundi ebiri ogulangiriddwa ku yo. Ebikolobero byayo n’obwenzi byayo bingi era ekibonerezo kyayo tekirwawo n’ekibonerezo ekinene kirigwa ku abo abateenenya era ng’Ekkanisa yange ennesigwa n’omugole wange omwesigwa bwe birikung’aanyizibwa gye ndi mu bbanga ttono mu bire ne birokolebwa okuva mu kiruyi, era n’okubonaabona okungi okwa wiiki ya Danieri ey’amakumi musanvu. Babulooni Omukulu Agudde, Agudde! Muwulire ebigambo byange kubanga njogera nga bimaze okubaawo kubanga tewali kijja kukyusa, kulwisa, kuziyiza, wadde okuyimiriza emisango gya Yahweh okugwa ku nsi.

Mu ssaawa emu eriyokebwa n’omuliro, ebibuga byayo ebimu biriyaka, ebibuga byayo ebimu birijjula amazzi n’amaanyi, eminaala gyayo girigwa ne gimenyekamenyeka, entindo zaayo zirigwa, n’okukankana kungi kulibaawo. Ensi eriyulikamu n’amaanyi n’ettaka lingi liribikkibwa amazzi ag’amaanyi. Ebiteeteeyi byayo biribikkibwa okuva ebuvanjuba n’ebugwanjuba mu mayengo amanene n’okutuuka wakati mu mayengo ag’amaanyi n’okukankana okw’amaanyi. Ebisigaddewo biriba matongo era tekiriddayo kugoloka, kubanga eggwanga lino eririmu amawanga amangi n’ebibuga ebingi teririddayo kugoloka era okugwa kwalyo kuliba kunene! Kubanga ensi ya Mukama n’okujjuza kwayo, era enjuyi zonna ez’omuganyulo gwayo omubi ziriggyibwawo emirembe gyonna okuva mu mawanga mu kiseera kya wiiki ya Danieri ey’amakumi musanvu, kubanga bwe ntyo bwe ndirongoosa ensi mu kiruyi kyange. Nze, Kristo Yesu, Empologoma ey’Ekika kya Yuda, ntegeezezza ebigambo bino, era okweteekerateekera okutuukirizibwa kwabyo kutandise dda era mu bbanga ttono birituukirizibwa mu ssaawa emu.

N’olwekyo olutalo lujja kubalukawo ku nsi ng’ekitundu ky’okulumbagana okw’ekibwatukira ku Babulooni Ekyama, kubanga kino kipangiddwa era kikwataganyiziddwa okumala emyaka mingi kati, kubanga ekyama ky’obubi kibaddewo okuva mu nnaku ez’edda era kiriggwaawo ddala omuzibi bw’aliggibwawo ng’abeesigwa bange batwalibwa mu ggulu. Olutalo luligwa ku nsi yonna era lulijja ku bantu bonna ne ku mawanga gonna, kubanga luno lwe lutalo olw’okusatu, ng’abagudde bwe banoonya okuzikiriza entegeka enkadde ez’ensi ne baddiza ebintu byonna mu kifo kyabyo n’entegeka yaabwe empya, era n’entegeka y’Oggusota. Ensolo eva mu nnyanja n’ensolo eva mu nsi zirigoloka n’obusungu obungi, era zirirwana n’abatukuvu, kubanga kiweereddwa bo okuwangula abatukuvu. Bwe kityo kiriba nti bonna abataagala bulamu bwabwe ne bakoowoola erinnya lya Mukama okutuusa ku nkomerero balilokolebwa mu ssanyu eritaggwaawo okutuuka mu mirembe n’emirembe. Era nze Yesu Omunazaaleeti ndese ekyokulabirako eky’emirembe n’emirembe eky’okugondera okutuusa ku kufa, kubanga okugondera kwange eri Yahweh kwali kufa ku musaalaba bwe nnabonaabona ne nsasulira ebibi by’abantu ng’omwana gw’entama gwa Katonda era laba njija ng’Empologoma ey’Ekika kya Yuda era sigenda kuyimirizibwa wadde okuziyizibwa!

N’olwekyo abajulirwa bange ababiri abali emiti gy’emi zeyituuni ebiri n’ebikondo by’amatabaala ebibiri abayimiridde mu maaso ga Katonda w’ensi baliragula okumala emyezi 42 nga bambadde ebibukutu, kubanga baatwalibwa mu ggulu mu biseera eby’edda. Era omuntu yenna bw’anaabaluma, omuliro guva mu kamwa kaabwe, ne gumalawo abalabe baabwe: era omuntu yenna bw’anaabaluma, ateekwa okuttibwa bw’atyo. Bano balina obuyinza okuggala eggulu, ne bayimiriza enkuba mu nnaku z’obunnabbi bwabwe: era balina obuyinza ku mazzi okugafuula omusaayi, n’okukuba ensi n’ebirwadde byonna, emirundi gyonna gye banaayagaliranga.

Obujulizi bwabwe bwa lubeerera, era balirangirira enjiri ey’emirembe n’emirembe, era balyogera ku nteekateeka za Yakuwa ez’ebiseera byonna, era balinjogerera nze Kristo Yesu, era ebyama bingi bye balibikkula era balikyusa emitima gya bangi okudda eri Kitaffe w’ebitangaala atalina kukyukakyuka newaakubadde ekisiikirize ky’okukyuka. Balintegeezaako nze Yeshua HaMashiach kubanga nze Yakuwa gwe yatuma eri Isirayiri okutuukiriza ebisuubizo ebyakolebwa eri bajjajjaabwe era nze Masiya ow’amazima kati n’emirembe gyonna era obwakabaka bwange tebuliggwaawo! Kubanga biseera ki ebyaliwo Yakuwa we yeerekera obutaba na mujulizi? Tewali kiseera kyonna Yakuwa kye yeerekera obutaba na mujulizi, era bonna abateekebwawo okuweebwa obulokozi obw’emirembe n’emirembe balijja okulokolebwa okuyingira mu butaggwaawo obw’emirembe n’essanyu ery’emirembe n’emirembe, kubanga okutuukirizibwa kw’ebintu byonna kutandise dda.

Era n’ensolo eva mu nnyanja eriyimirira n’ensolo eva ku nsi, ye nnabbi ow’obulimba. Era alyasama akamwa ke ng’ayogera eby’okuwogana ebikulu ku Yakuwa okuvuma erinnya lye n’eweema ye n’abo abali mu ggulu. Era kirimuweebwa okulwana n’abatukuvu n’okubawangula era obuyinza bulimuweebwa okufuga buli kika, n’olulimi, n’eggwanga kubanga y’aliba akulembedde ensi. Enslo eva mu nsi nayo erifuga ensi nga Moshiach waabwe ow'obulimba era eriba omuzimbi wa yeekaalu yaabwe. Enslo eva mu nnyanja erifuga okusinziira ku mateeka gaabwe amavvumu aga Nowaayidi nga banoonya okumalawo ekintu kyonna ekikwata ku Katonda ow’amazima n’omwana we omu yekka Yesu Kristo okuva ku maaso g’ensi.

Ensolo eva ku nsi erireetera ensi okusinza ensolo, eyawona ekiwundu kyayo eky’okufa. Era alikola eby’amagero ebikulu okulimba abo abali ku nsi, kubanga kirimukkirizibwa okukola obubonero bungi obw’obulimba n’eby’amagero, era alireeta omuliro okuva ku nsi mu maaso g’abantu era alireetera abantu bonna okubyewuunya. Naye ye mulabe, era yeegulumiza ku byonna ebiyitibwa Katonda, oba ebisinzibwa: n’okutuuka okutuula nga Katonda mu Yeekaalu ya Katonda, ng’eyeeraga nti ye Katonda.

Era alikola ekifaananyi ky’ensolo era alireetera bonna okusinza ekifaananyi ky’alikoze. Era kirimukkirizibwa okuwa omwoyo ekifaananyi ky’ensolo ekifaananyi ky’ensolo kisobole okwogera era kireetere bonna abatayagala kusinza kifaananyi kya nsolo okuttibwa. Era alireeta bonna, abagagga n’abaavu, ab’eddembe n’abaddu, okufuna akabonero ke ku mukono gwabwe ogwa ddyo oba mu byenyi byabwe.

Era n’olwekyo nziramu okugamba abo abali ku nsi bonna bawulire:

BW’ALIBANGAWO OMUNTU YENNA ASINZA ENSOLO N’EKIFAANANYI KYAYO, N’ATEEKAKO AKABONERO KAYO MU KYENYI KYE, OBA MU MUKONO GWE, OYO ALINYWA KU MUVINYO OGWA KATONDA OGULIMU OBUSUNGU BWE OBUNYIIVU, OGUTABUDDWAMU AWATALI KUTABULATABULA MU KIKOMPE KY’OKUNYIIGA KWE, ERA ALIBONYEBONYEBWA N’OMULIRO N’EKIWUUGULU, MU MAASO GA BAMALAYIKA ABATUKUVU, NE MU MAASO G’OMWANA GW’ENDIIGA: N’OMUKKA GW’OKUBONYEBONYEBWA KWABWE GUNYooka EMAGULU EMIGANDA N’EMIGANDA. ERA TEBALINA KUWUMMULA EMISANA N’EKIRO, ABO ABASINZA ENSOLO N’EKIFANANYI KYAYO, NA BONNA ABAKKIRIZA AKABONERO K’ERINNYA LYAYO.

Ensi eriwo kaakano teriddamu kubeerawo, n’obwakabaka bw’ogusota obugenda okuyimuka bulisuulibwa wansi ddala, ng’obwakabaka bwa Sitaani busembera enkomerero yaabwo, kubanga bali basenze butaka so ssi bakabaka. Era eno y’ensi abangi mwe balisanga, n’abo abalisigalawo oluvannyuma lw’ebikangabwa n’olutalo olujja. Temutwala kabonero bantu bange, kubanga balijja nga basuubiza emirembe, balijja nga basuubiza obukuumi, balibagamba nti byonna biri ku lw’obulungi bwammwe. Balitandikawo endwadde zaabwe ez’obulwadde kubanga bateeseteese dda okukkirizanya ensi nti balwadde. Era buli ngeri y’akawumpuli n’endwadde n’ebiwuka biribuna kubanga bwe gutyo bwe guli emigaso gy’oyo ajja ng’atudde ku mbalaasi era balyebagala!

Balisuubiza obulamu n’okuwona okuva mu misango gya Katonda, so ng’omuntu yandibadde yeenenya obubi bwe. Balisuubiza okuyingira mu kitundu kyabwe okuva mu misango egy’entiisa egigenda okubalukawo okwetooloola ensi. Kubanga nze, Kristo Yesu, ndibagamba nga tekinnabaawo mulyoke mumanye obwesimbu bw’ebigambo byange era mulyoke musobole okuyita mu mazzi ag’akabi abangi mwe balisanga, n’abo abalina obuvumu okukoowoola erinnya lyange okutuusa ku nkomerero, kubanga abo be basika b’obulokozi obutaggwaawo. Kubanga bonna abakoowoola Yesu Omunazaaleeti nga Mukama waabwe, na bonna abakoowoola erinnya lyange ery’amaanyi ne batayagala bulamu bwabwe okutuusa ku nkomerero, balilokolebwa ne bayingira mu bulamu obutaggwaawo.

Temusomanga? Bano be abo abaliva mu kibonyoobonyo ekinene, ne boza engoye zaabwe, ne bazeeza mu musaayi gw’Omwana gw’Endiiga. Kyebava babeera mu maaso g’entebe ya Katonda, ne bamuweereza emisana n’ekiro mu Yeekaalu ye: n’oyo atuula ku ntebe alibeera mu bo. Tebaliddamu kulumwa njala, newaakubadde okulumwa ennyonta, newaakubadde enjuba okubakuba, newaakubadde ebbugumu lyonna. Kubanga Omwana gw’Endiiga, ali wakati mu ntebe, alibaliisa, n’abakulembera okubatuusa ku nzizi ez’amazzi amalamu: ne Katonda alibasiimula amaziga gonna mu maaso gaabwe.

Naye newankubadde ekibiina ekinene kirilokolebwa okuva mu ssabbiiti ya Danyeri ey’amakumi musanvu n’obulokozi bulijja eri Isirayiri kubanga ensigalira erivvuunama eri nze Yeshua HaMashiach, (Kubanga nze Masiya ow’amazima omwana gw’endiga gwa Katonda gw’aliggulirawo empologoma y’Ekika kya Yuda, era byombiriri birituukirira mu nze mu mirembe emirembe n’emirembe, kubanga mu nze Kristo Yesu Empologoma erigalamira mu mirembe n’omwana gw’endiga.) eky’ennaku, era mu ngeri eteri ya mugaso, ensi nnyingi ziriwuunya era ne zigoberera abafuzi bano ab’ensolo, kubanga balisendasenda bangi. N’obulogo bwabwe n’okuloga kwabwe, balisendasenda ensi okukkiriza obutwa bwabwe, era balifuula abo abaagala ensi obutwa obw’emirembe n’obulogo bwabwe, kubanga akabonero k’ensolo kalifaananyizibwa ng’eddagala. N’okulumwa kw’omusota okw’enteekateeka yaabwe okwatandikibwawo dda era ne kugezesebwa, n’ebibiina by’abantu bimanyiziddwa dda okukkiriza akabonero kaabwe n’olukwe lwabwe olw’olutalo lwatuuka ku buwanguzi.

N’olwekyo enteekateeka yaabwe yeetegefu okulabibwa, era bateeseteese obubonero bwabwe obw’embeera z’abantu era bonna ababeera mu bibuga byabwe n’ekitundu kyabwe balifugibwa. Kubanga balina okutwala akabonero k’ensolo okwetaba mu kibiina kyabwe ekya sitaani, era obwakabaka bw’ogusota. Abo abalitwala akabonero balikyusibwa emirembe gyonna. Ensengekera yaabwe ey’obuzaale erikyusibwa, kubanga balyetabula n’ezzadde ly’abantu: naye tebalikwatagana, nga kyuma bwe kitatabulwa na bbumba. Akabonero kaabwe ak’ensolo, n’okulumwa kw’omusota, kulizikiriza ddala bonna abalitwala obutwa bwabwe era tewali kuddukawo.

N’abo bonna abalitwala akabonero balikyusibwa emirembe gyonna, era balisanga nga bali kitundu ku nteekateeka yaabwe, era balisibibwa wamu tekinologiya era ebirowoozo byabwe tebiriba byabwe. Balifugibwa era baliba ng’abafu abatambula, baliba baddu ba lubeerera b’obwongo bw’enteekateeka yaabwe, n’obwa tekinologiya waabwe kubanga balisibibwa wamu n’omutimbagano gw’ebintu ebinaabaawo. Balifiirwa okwagala kwabwe, era tebaliba na nneewulira era ekintu kyonna ekya Katonda kiriggibwawo mu bo emirembe gyonna kubanga balyekyusa ne babeera mu mbeera etasobola kununulibwa, era balyegatta mu bujjuvu n’olubeerera n’abo abagwa era balibeera b’ogusota emirembe gyonna n’eri ennyanja ennene ey’omuliro gye baligenda emirembe gyonna. N’olwekyo gwe guba omusango gw’okwerondera kwabwe.

Kubanga enkomerero y’okulonda kwabwe tekyusibwa. N’ebiwuka byabwe n’amabwa gaabwe n’endwadde zaabwe biriba bingi, era newankubadde balisuubizibwa obulamu n’emirembe, obulamu n’emirembe tebalibifuna. Kubanga Sitaani afuba okukozesa obulogo bwe okulwanyisa obusungu n’emisango gya Katonda n’okussa obutwa n’okutta abantu, naye tebalifuna kuwummuzibwa era n’abo abeenyigira mu bulogo bwe tebaliwona. Abo abalitwala akabonero balimasamasa wansi w’ebitangaala byabwe eby’ekizikiza, n’ebitangaala byabwe birisiiga enguudo z’ebibuga byabwe bye bateekateekedde abaddu baabwe. Tegeera nti bano baliba batambula abakolimirwa abataliiko kisonyiwo era abatasobola kuwona, kubanga bano be abo amannya gaabwe ge gatawandiikibwa mu Kitabo ky’Obulamu eky’Omwana gw’Endiiga, eyattibwa okuva ku ntandikwa y’ensi. So tewaliba bulokozi mu bo newaakubadde mu kibiina kyabwe, kubanga okutambulira mu bo n’okwegatta ku kibiina kyabwe kireetawo okwetaaga akabonero k’ensolo.

Abajulizi bange ababiri balitegeeza okutuusa ebiseera by’obujulizi bwabwe lwe biriggwaawo kubanga emyezi 42 giweereddwa. N’olwekyo kirimuweebwa ensolo eva mu bunnya obutagyaamu kulwana nabo, n’ebawangula, n’ebatta, n’emiti gyange gyombiriri egy’omuzeyituuni, n’ebikondo by’amatabaaza ebibiri bwe biriba bimalirizza obujulizi bwabyo. N’emirambo gyabwe girigalamira mu luguudo lw’ekibuga ekinene, ekiyitibwa mu by’omwoyo Sodomu ne Misiri, Omukama wammwe naye gye yakomererwa. N’ababi balijaguza nnyo ne bawaŋŋana ebirabo olw’okufa kwabwe. N’oluvannyuma lw’ennaku ssatu n’ekitundu Omwoyo gw’obulamu oguva eri Katonda gulibayingiramu: nabo baliyimirira ku bigere byabwe, n’entiisa ennyingi erigwa ku bonna abaabalaba. Ne bawulira eddoboozi eddene okuva mu ggulu nga libagamba nti, mulinnye wano. Ne balinnya mu ggulu mu kire, n’abalabe baabwe balibalaba nga balinnya mu ggulu mu bulamu obutaggwaawo era emirembe gyonna balikyusibwa. N’Omalaika ow’omusanvu alifuuwa, era walibaawo amaloboozi amanene mu ggulu, nga gagamba nti, Obwakabaka bw’ensi eno bufuuse bwakabaka bwa Mukama waffe, ne bwa Kristo we, era alifuga emirembe n’emirembe.

(Balina omukisa abafu abafiira mu Mukama, okuva leero, weewaawo, Omwoyo bw’ayogera, bawumule emirimu gyabwe, n’ebikolwa byabwe bibagoberera.) N’olwekyo ekibonyoobonyo eky’amaanyi ekisinga byonna kiritandika, newankubadde ng’ekibonyoobonyo kya lubeerera mu ssabbiiti ya Danyeri ey’amakumi musanvu. N’eyeekaalu yaabwe eriggwa era eky’omuzizo ekizikiriza kirikolebwa Moshiach waabwe ow’obulimba. Era alikakasa endagaano n’abangi okumala ssabbiiti emu: ne wakati mu ssabbiiti alikomya ssaddaaka n’ekiweebwayo, n’olw’okusaasaana kw’emyenzo, alikifuula matongo, okutuusa enkomerero etegekeddwa lw’erifukibwa ku matongo.

Bino bye biriba ebiseera eby’ekizikiza ennyo baana bange, kubanga nze Yeshua HaMashiach mbagamba bino nga tebinnabaawo, era biwandiikiddwa ng’okulabula eri bonna. Era n’abange 144,000 balijja okuyamba abo abandyandizikiridde era abakoowoola erinnya lyange. Ndibakulembera nnamutindo, era balinkolera emirimu mingi egy’amaanyi. Kubanga bano be abo abaggyibwawo mu kukuŋŋaana kw’abatukuvu eri nze, Kristo Yesu, mu bbanga ku ddoboozi ly’akagombe akasembayo era n’akagombe kanaatera okuvuga.

Bano be beesigwa bange, n’abo abataasangibwamu bukuusa, kubanga beeyonja ng’omuwala omuto mu musaayi gw’Omwana gw’Endiiga gwe nnaawaayo emirembe gyonna nga kabona waabwe omukulu, kubanga nze kabona omukulu ow’emirembe n’emirembe ng’engeri ya Merukizeddeeki bw’eri. Bano be bagole bange, era balingoberera wonna wendi. Laba! Ndibafuula ba maanyi, era tewali kyali kilabiddwako ku nsi ekiyinza okugeraageranyizibwa n’obusobozi bwe ndibayambaza. Baliyambazibwa bafuta, enjeru, etaliiko bbala era obusobozi bwabwe bulireeta ebyo bye batuusizzaako n’obusobozi obunene bwe baliba nabwo kubanga be beesigwa abayite era abalonde n’okusingira ddala okuva ensi nga tenneeton_da Yakuwa ku lw’ebigendererwa bino.

Abange 144,000 balikka ku lusozi Sayuuni nange ndibakulembera. Balitwala abo be mbakulembera okubatuusa mu ddungu okukwekebwa okuva mu kizikiza ekikutte n’okuyigganyizibwa kw’obwakabaka bw’abalabe. Balikulemberwa okubatuusa mu bukuumi okuva mu bibonyoobonyo omusanvu kubanga mu misango gino mwe muli obukambwe bw’obusungu bwa Katonda obugenda okufukibwa ku ntebe y’ensolo n’okubonyaabonya obwakabaka bw’ogusota kubanga ekibonerezo kyabwe kinene. N’abantu 144,000 balibakulembera okubatuusa mu bukuumi, balibawonya ebiwundu byabwe, balibayamba ne babawa essuubi eddene okukoowoola erinnya lya Mukama n’okutuusa ku nkomerero singa kiba kyetaagisa. Kubanga singa saafunza nnaku, tewandisigaddewo mubiri mulamu. N’olwekyo balimaliriza ekyo kye ndibakulembera.

N’ebiseera bwe birituukirira bwe kirituuka, nga bwe kyawandiikibwa era nga bwe kyawandiikibwa bwe kityo kikolebwe: Ne ndaba eggulu nga libikkuse, laba embalaasi enjeru, n’oyo eyali agituddeko yayitibwa Omwesigwa era Ow’amazima, era mu butuukirivu asala omusango n’alwana. Amaaso ge gaali ng’olulimi lw’omuliro, ne ku mutwe gwe kwaliko engule nnyingi, era yalina erinnya eriwandiikiddwa, eritaliiko muntu yenna ly’amanyi wabula ye yekka. Era yali ayambadde olugoye olunnyikiddwa mu musaayi, n’erinnya lye liyitibwa, Ekigambo kya Katonda. N’amagye agaali mu ggulu ne gamugoberera ku mbalaasi enjeru, nga gambadde bafuta, enjeru era ennyonjo. Ne mu kamwa ke ne muva ekitala eky’obwogi, alyoke akitwale okukuba amawanga: naye alibafuga n’omuggo ogw’ekyuma: n’alinnyirira essogolero ly’obukambwe n’obusungu bwa Katonda Omuyinza w’ebintu byonna. Era alina ku lugoye lwe, ne ku kisambi kye erinnya eriwandiikiddwa, Kabaka Wa Bakabaka, Era Mukama Wa Bakama.

N’olwekyo nze, era Yesu Omunazaaleeti, era Omwana gw’Endiiga, era Kristo Yesu era ye Mpologoma y’Ekika kya Yuda ndijja okusala omusango n’okulwana n’ogusota n’obwakabaka bwe bwonna bulibetentebwa ddala obutaddamu kuyimuka. N’ensolo ne nnabbi ow’obulimba balisuulibwa nga balamu mu nnyanja y’omuliro ne Sitaani alisibibwa n’olujegere olunene mu bunnya obutagyaamu okumala emyaka lukumi n’olwekyo okufuga kwange okw’emyaka lukumi kulitandika era mu butuukirivu okufuga kwange kwe kuliba. Laba! Ndeeta omugole wange n’ekibiina ky’abakabaka n’abakabona be ndifuga nabo ensi mu butuukirivu era ndifuga amawanga n’omuggo ogw’ekyuma. N’olwekyo mube n’essuubi eddene baana bange kubanga enkomerero y’ebintu byonna eri kumpi n’ekiseera kino eky’emyaka musanvu eky’okubonaabona okungi, n’okubonaabona kwa Yakobo kuliddirirwa okufuga kwange okw’obutuukirivu era Yakuwa aliba byonna mu byonna.

Ne ndaba eggulu eriggya, n’ensi empya: kubanga eggulu eryasooka, n’ensi eyasooka byali biweddewo, so tewakyali nnyanja. Nange Yokaana ne ndaba ekibuga ekitukuvu, Yerusaalemi empya nga kikka okuva eri Katonda okuva mu ggulu, nga kitegekeddwa ng’omugole ayonjereddwa bba. Ne mpulira eddoboozi eddene okuva mu ggulu, nga ligamba nti, Laba, Eweema ya Katonda eri wamu n’abantu, era alibeera wamu nabo, nabo baliba bantu be, ne Katonda yennyini aliba wamu nabo, n’abeera Katonda waabwe.

Nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, ntumye Ebigambo bino ng’abiyisa mu muweereza wange, era Ebigambo bino bitumiddwa ng’abiyisa mu muweereza wange!
