Omujulirwa Omulamba: Ekkanisa
Mu Biwandiiko by'Omutume Pawulo
May 06, 2024

Eri Abaweereza Bange Ab'esigwa: Mbatuma!

Eri abaweereza bange ab'esigwa n'abo abavudde mu Lawodikiya!

Ggwe Omugole wange, wuliriza eddoboozi lyange ojje gyendi, ndyoke nkukuumuulule ebiwundu byo era nkugumye mu maanyi gange. Totya! Obusungu bwange tebuli ku ggwe, kubanga nja kukugamba nti: Kale, omuddu omulungi era omwesigwa; obadde mwesigwa mu bintu bitono, nja kukufuula mufuzi wa bintu bingi: yingira mu ssanyu lya mukama wo. Kubanga nnina ebigambo ebikakali, ebigambo bya kubonereza, eri abatagondera; naye okubonereza okwo tekukukwatako ggwe kubanga oli mwesigwa, wadde ng'olina okussaayo omwoyo ku bigambo byange, oleme okugwa.

Ggwe Omugole wange omwesigwa, oli mboni ya liiso lyange, era tewali kusinza ku ggwe, kubanga oli mu Kristo, era ozimbye obulamu bwo ku nze, Yesu Kristo, ejjinja lyennyini ery'oku nsonda abazimbi ab'obulimba lye baagaana. Weeyongere okuba omwesigwa mu nze, kubanga njija gy'oli n'amaanyi mangi, kubanga enkomerero y'emirembe eno erikumpi. Nkutuma n'amaanyi mangi era ne mu bikolwa bye waterowoozangako, kubanga tewali okimanyi nti wazaalibwa mu kyama kya Katonda okuva ku mukka gwo ogwasooka, naye okuzze nnyo mu kisa kyange, era kaakano olaba. Kubanga ekisa kyange kisa kya maanyi ekitaayinza kuwangulwa, n'okusaasira kwange kwa lubeerera.

Laba! Bw'atyo bw'ayogera Yeshua HaMashiach. Mpuliriza era owandiike ebigambo byange ku mmeeme zo. Kubanga nkutuma; yee, nkutuma n'ebigambo bino, era abo be ntuma bajja kufuna okukakasibwa okutaliiko kubuusabuusa okw'okwagala kwange okungi. Nkutuma mu mirimu gy'abakulu bange, emirimu gye baatandika emyaka mingi egiyise, era emirimu egyo gigenda mu maaso n'okutuusa leero. Kubanga ekitabo ky'Ebikolwa tekinnaggwa kuwandiikibwa okutuusa Omugole wange lw'aliba nange. Olwo nno lwe ne munaategeera obujjuvu okuva mu bitabo ebiri mu ggulu, kubanga ebikolwa byammwe byawandiikibwamu, era fenna tujja kusanyukira obwesigwa bwammwe mu maaso ga Yahweh yennyini, kubanga asanyukira wamu naffe era ali mu mmwe mwenna.

Laba! Abamu ku mmwe mbatuma mu buweereza bwa Pawulo ng'abawanika b'ebyama byange era ng'abatume eri amawanga. Laba! Abamu ku mmwe mbatuma mu mwoyo gwa Eriya okulwanyisa Yezebereezi, nnabbi w'obulimba, era n'okunlangirira nze, Kristo Yesu! Laba! Abamu ku mmwe mbatuma mu mulimu gwa Firipo omuvubuuzi w'enjiri, gye nja okukuggyanga mu kifo ekimu n'nkutwala mu kirala ng'olina ebigambo by'enjiri yange ku mimwa gyo, ebisaanira ebyetaago by'abatalokoka, kubanga ojja kubalokola ng'obuulira omusalaba! Laba! Abamu ku mmwe mbatuma mu mulimu gwa Peetero, kubanga yalunda ekisibo kyange n'akiwaliriza ekigambo kya Katonda ekirongoofu. Kubanga abaweereza abagayaavu ab'e Lawodikiya baliisa ekisibo kyange emmere etali ya mugaso, ebigambo by'obutakkiriza ebitarina maanyi. Laba! Mbafulizza abayigiriza mulyoke muyigirize era mulagirire abantu bange mu makubo g'obutuukirivu, kubanga mujja kubayigiriza nga nze bwe nkuŋŋaanya! Mujja kubalunda nga nze bwe nkuŋŋaanya! Mujja kubavubuulira nga nze bwe nkuŋŋaanya! Kubanga bannabbi bange bajja kwogera ebigambo bye mbawa, nga tebikyusizza makulu wadde omwoyo! Kubanga abatume bange bajja kumulisa ekitangaala ky'amawulire gange amalungi ag'ekyama kya Katonda, ekiremba kya Musa kilyoke kiggibwe ku mitima gyammwe. Kubanga omujulirwa w'e Kkolinso aleekaana nnyo! Naye n'okutuusa leero, Musa bw'asomebwa, ekiremba kiba ku mitima gyabwe. Naye bwe kinaakyukira eri Mukama, ekiremba kijja kuggibwawo. Era Mukama ye mwoyo ogwo, era Omwoyo gwa Mukama w'ali, we waba eddembe. Naye ffe fenna, nga tutunuulira mu ndabirwamu ekitiibwa kya Mukama n'amaaso agali obweru, tukyusibwa okufaanana naye, okuva mu kitiibwa okudda mu kitiibwa, nga bwe kiri ku bw'omwoyo gwa Mukama. Nze, Kristo Yesu, nziggyawo ekiremba kyo!

Laba, nkutuma mu ddakiika zino ezisembayo nga temunnakung'aanyizibwa gyendi ku kulabika kwange. Neetaaga owulirize eddoboozi lyange era ogoberere ebiragiro byange, nga bw'okola n'obwesigwa, Omugole wange omwesigwa, kubanga oli mwesigwa! Togeza kugenda mu mulimu gwe nkutumamu ng'osinziira ku kutegeera kwo. Togeza kugenda mu mulimu gwe nkutumamu ng'osinziira ku maanyi go. Togeza kugenda mu mulimu gwe nkutumamu ng'osinziira ku busobozi bwo. Togeza kugenda mu mulimu gwe nkutumamu ng'osinziira ku maanyi go gennyini. Wuliriza ebigambo byange n'omutima gwo gwonna! Kubanga nze, yee nze, Kristo Yesu Mukama ow'ekitiibwa, nkugamba nti: Nze kutegeera kwo, nze maanyi go, nze busobozi bwo: kyokka tobuusabuusa!

Kola emirimu gye nja okukulemberamu okutuukiriza, era ofe ku bantu be nkutumira. Onoogondera abantu ab'omutindo ogwa wansi? Onootuula n'abatalina maka, n'obabuulira, n'obalokola, era n'obeera nabo? Kubanga abamu ku mmwe mbatuma lwa nsonga eyo. Onooyogera n'abaweereza b'e Lawodikiya n'obuwombeefu ng'omuntu bw'ayogera ne kitaawe? Kubanga amakubo gange bwe gaty'o. Onooyogera ebigambo bye nkuwa, ne bwe kuba nga tobitegeera bulungi oba tobikkiriziganya nabyo? (Kakasa ebigambo byange n'ebyawandiikibwa.) Kubanga nkutuma okukola by'njagala, so si by'oyagala ggwe. Onoosumba ekisibo mu maanyi g'okuwonya ag'omuliro mu mwoyo gwo gwe nja okukoleeza mu ggwe? (Era omusumba omukulu bw'alilabika, muliweebwa engule ey'ekitiibwa etawotoka.) Kola emirimu gyange, Omugole wange, era oleke emirimu gyo, kubanga tewakyali kiseera, era ettaala ziri kumpi okuzikira.

Tegeera, nkutuma mu mulimu gwange, era ojja kungula empeera nnene bw'onooba omwesigwa, kubanga olina edda eby'obugagga bingi ebiterekeddwa mu ggulu, n'amayumba mangi mu nnyumba ya Kitange gakutegekeddwa. Kubanga ekibonyoobonyo kya Yakobo nnaku musanvu ez'emyaka musanvu, nga buli mwaka gubalibwa ng'olunaku. N'embaga y'Omugole n'Omwasibwe bw'etyo bw'eba. Kubanga walibaawo ennaku musanvu ez'emyaka musanvu mu ggulu, nga buli mwaka gubalibwa ng'olunaku, era ojja kubeeranga nange emirembe gyonna, Omugole wange. Olwo nno ne tutambula!

Genda mu makubo go era genda mu kkubo lye nkulembeddemu. Mugonderaganenga mu kwagalana n'obuwombeefu. Lunda olusuku lwange, kubanga nasimba olusuku lw'obulamu obutaggwaawo omubi lw'atayinza kusimbula wadde okuzikiriza. Ojja kulya ku muti gw'obulamu yennyini gw'alemeddwa okukuziyiza, era tewali bulamu bungi mu nnyanja y'omuliro gya Setaani, mw'agenda, wabula mulimu kubonerezebwa, kwejjusa, nsonyi, na kubonyaabonyezebwa. Ebyo si bibyo, Omugole wange, era okufa okw'okubiri tekulina buyinza ku ggwe, kubanga ovudde mu kufa n'oyingira mu bulamu mu nze, yee Yesu Kristo, omulokozi wo era Mukama wo.

N'olwekyo genda gye nkutuma, era okolere wamu n'abantu be nkutumira, era be nkutumako. Kubanga tebajja gy'oli lwa butanwa, wabula kubanga natandika dda okubalagirira gy'oli. Beera mu kyama kyange era beera nga bwe baabeeranga mu Bikolwa, kubanga ggwe ssuula esembayo ewandiikibwa nga bw'obeererawo ku lwange, era mu bbanga ttono ojja kulaba bw'onoobeera mu ggulu nga nkututte gyendi, kubanga oli wange! Oli mugole wange, Ekkanisa yange ey'ekitiibwa, gye nja okwefuulira, nga tolina bbala wadde enkunyalala, oba ekintu kyonna ekiringa ekyo: naye obeere mutukuvu era nga tolina kya kunenyezebwa, kubanga nkulongoosezza mu musaayi gwange ogutangirira.

Nkuyita, Omugole wange, era nkuyita okuyingira mu buweereza bwe waterowoozangako nti osobola okutuukiriza, kubanga abaweereza ab'e Lawodikiya baagezaako okukutuula mu kizikiza. Toddira maanyi ge nkoleeza mu ggwe, kubanga omukono gwa Mukama, yee omukono gwange ogw'omukisa, guli ku ggwe. Toddira buweereza bwange bwe nkoleeza mu mwoyo gwo. Kubanga nja kukoleeza omuliro omunene mu mwoyo gwo, Omugole wange, omuliro ogujja okusaanuusa byonna ebikuziyiza. Totya! Tambulira mu maanyi gange, kubanga abaweereza ab'e Lawodikiya abatali beesigwa bagezezzaako okukukakasa nti tolina maanyi, era waliwo ebibonerezo eby'amaanyi eri bo singa tebeenenya.

Laba! Nkutuma mu mirimu abalala gye baalemwamu, era ojja kugenda mu kifo kyabwe, kubanga oli mwesigwa: Kyokka zikiriza amalala go, oleme okugwa mu bubi nga bo bwe bagwamu. Tosirika, kubanga nsumuludde ennimi zo. Yogera ebigambo bye nkuwadde, era ondeetere amaanyi mangi. Olowooza nti ndi musosoze mu bantu? Kubanga kino kiseera kya byamagero eby'enjawulo, nga bwe nnawandiika mu Bikolwa: Era Katonda yakolera ebyamagero eby'enjawulo mu mikono gya Pawulo: N'okutuusa lwe baatwaliranga abarwadde ebitambaala oba ebifaananyi ebyava ku mubiri gwe, endwadde ne zibavaako, n'emyoyo emibi ne gibavaamu. Ggwe Omugole wange ow'okukkiriza okungi, ntuukiriza emirimu gye nkuyitira, kubanga tukomekkereza ebigendererwa byange mu ggwe, n'enjuba egwa ku mirembe gyange egy'ekisa, era ekiro kijja kugwa ku nsi yonna, kubanga ogenda. Laba! Nze, Yesu Kristo, nja kukola ebyamagero bingi eby'amaanyi, n'ebyamagero eby'enjawulo mu mukono gwo, kubanga kuno kwe kutumibwa okusembayo eri Ekkanisa yange nga temunnatwalibwa mu kitiibwa.

Genda, n'emirembe, era nja kukukulembera. Manya nti ojja kuziyizibwa nnyo mikwano gyo, ab'omu maka go, n'abalabe bo. Bino bye biro eby'okwawukana nga nsalawo abatali beesigwa era nga ndongoosa omuzeyituuni gwange okuva mu makubo gaabwe ag'obutakkiriza, kubanga tebeeteeseteese kulinda mugole, era bajja kutwala ebifo byabwe n'abawala abasirusiru. Era ndongoosa abeesigwa bange okuva mu kibi balyoke baleete amatabi amaggya agabala ebibala bingi. Totya, ng'olowooza nti tosaana byamagero. Kubanga nzikiriziganya, ku bwammwe mwennyini tewali n'omu ayinza kukola byamagero; naye nja kuwa obubonero bungi, ebyewuunyo, n'ebyamagero mu mikono gy'abeesigwa bange. Genda ogambe ensozi, muggibwewo era musuulibwe mu nnyanja, so tobuusabuusa naye beera n'okukkiriza mu Yahweh, ne mu nze, Yeshua HaMashiach.

Tewali nnyondo ya bya mwoyo ya kukuba, n'olwekyo lekera awo okufuba kwo. Kubanga oyinza okutambulira ku mazzi bw'onoonyweza amaaso go ku nze n'otobuusabuusa era n'ototunula ku kkono oba ku ddyo naye amaaso go gabeere ku nze, so tojja kubbira. Lagira emyoyo, kubanga gindimu obuyinza n'olwekyo gili mu buyinza bwo. Lagira emyoyo okugenda; togisaba, wabula giragire! Togikkiriza kwogera, wabula gisirise; toyogera nagyo, kubanga ebigambo byagyo bwa butwa; wabula gigobe mu ddungu gye gitalina nnyumba, era mu bbanga ttono gijja kusuulibwa mu muliro. Wonya abarwadde! Yogera n'obulwadde mu linnya lyange, era obugambe okugenda; totya, kubanga nja kukuwa ebigambo. Totya! Tekikwetaagisa kwogera bigambo birungi; yogera bugambo bigambo bya kukkiriza bye nja okussa mu mitima gyo era tobuusabuusa.

Laba! Genda ku lwange, Omugole wange, nga tewali ky'obuusabuusa. Nkuyita okuyingira mu mirimu gye mmaliriza, abamu ku mmwe gye muli dda bakozi bannange. Wakyaliwo emmeeme ez'okulokola, n'ezo Setaani z'agezaako okukweka. Nja kuzimenya okuva mu njegere ze okuyita mu bikolwa byo eby'amaanyi ebingi bye tujja okukolera awamu. Genda, Omugole wange, nga tewali ky'obuusabuusa, kubanga nkutegekedde obulamu bwo bwonna olw'obudde buno obutono obw'amaanyi n'obuwulize, era otendekeddwa bulungi nze, n'abo be nnatuma okukufaako.

Ggwe Omugole wange omwesigwa, nkwagala, era oli ssanyu lyange n'okwesunga kwange. Nkumwenyera buli lunaku, naye tondaba n'amaaso go, era okyalina okukkiriza. Nkaabira obulumi bwo, wadde nga nkaabira mu kasirise enfunda nnyingi. Mpulira okukaaba kwo okw'obuyambi, era ndi muyambi aliwo bulijjo mu kiseera eky'okwetaaga. Nkwagala era nkufaako, era oli mboni ya liiso lyange, era mu bbanga ttono tujja kubeera wamu. Mmanyi nti okooye, kubanga obadde mwesigwa eri Ekigambo kyange n'erinnya lyange, n'olwekyo nja kukugumya mu maanyi ga Katonda atalina kikomo era ow'emirembe gyonna, yee Yahweh, kubanga tewali amufaanana! Tozirika, kubanga wadde omubiri gwo guyinza okuba omunafu, nja kukugumya mu maanyi gange, kubanga nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, nkitegeezezza!

Njija gy'oli n'amaanyi mangi.

Nkutuma n'amaanyi mangi.

Nkuyita, Omugole wange, okuyingira mu buweereza bwe wabade tomanyi nti olina okutuusa kati.

Abamu ku mmwe mbatuma okumaliriza emirimu gy'abatali beesigwa, era mujja kufuna empeera yaabwe.

Nsalawo abatali beesigwa.

Ndongoosa abeesigwa bange okuva mu bibi byammwe mulyoke mubale ebibala bingi.

Ndi muyambi aliwo bulijjo mu kiseera eky'okwetaaga.

Genda mu kkubo lyo, oyimirire mu Yeekaalu, oyogere n'abantu ebigambo byonna eby'obulamu buno. Kubanga mmwe muli yeekaalu ya Katonda omulamu!
