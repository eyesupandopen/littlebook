Obujulizi obw'Ebiri Mujulizi: Ekkanisa
Mu Mirimu gya Yokaana Omutume Omubatiza
Maaki 15, 2024

Ekintu Eky'ekitalo

Ekigambo kya Mukama kiri ku nze, laba ekigambo kya Mukama!

Laba! Nkola ekintu eky'ekitalo mu maaso gammwe, era bangi ku mmwe mugaana okulaba!

Laba! Nkola omulimu ogw'amaanyi mu mmwe ogugenda okutuukirira mu bbanga ttono!

Laba! Nkola omulimu ogw'amaanyi mu mmwe, ogubalokola n'okubafuula abalamu obulungi!

Nkoze kisinga ku kugatta ebitundu ebimenyese. Nkoze kisinga ku kubazzaamu amaanyi nate, awalabikira ennyingo n'enkovu. Abantu bange abalonde n'abeesigwa mbafudde balamu mu nze, Kristo omulamu, era mbatonze bupya. Mmanye nti obulamu bwammwe bukwekeddwa mu nze, era bwe ndirabika, nammwe mulirabika nange mu kitiibwa era okuva olwo mulibeera nange emirembe gyonna. Mmwe mugole wange era mmwe muli mubiri gwange; ssaabakowoola mannya? Ssaabakolera kkubo lyakudda eri Kitammwe, Kitaa'ffe w'ebitangaala?

Nze nnali mmanyiddwa Kitange ensi n'etennabaawo, eyatumibwa n'azaalibwa olw'okulokolebwa kwammwe, eyakomererwa, n'awaayo buli ttondo ly'omusaayi gwange olw'ebibi byammwe. Tewali kyasigalayo era tewali kyatakolebwa, era ekyayogerebwa kirikolebwa era kirimalirizibwa ddala. Nnabatonda n'omwoyo gwange omutukuvu Kitange gwe yawa okufukibwa ku batume bange ku lunaku olw'edda olwa Pentekote, bwe baayogeranga eby'ekitalo bya Katonda mu nnimi ze batategeeranga. Nnayita, era wadde nga bangi banjeemera, ensigalira y'ekisa n'okulondebwa yanannyitaba, era nabakoleramu omulimu ogw'amaanyi ogugenda okubikkulibwa. Mazima, bwe mutunula mu ndabirwamu, temulaba buzima bwe muli. Nabafuula ba maanyi mu bunafu bwammwe, era bwe ndirabika bwe mutyo bwe mulirabika. Muliba nga nze, era bulijjo mulibeera nange. Nze bibala ebisooka eby'abafu, era n'amakungula gali kumpi nammwe. Bwe ndirabika, mulisitulibwa okunsisinkana, era mulibeera mu nnyumba ya Kitange bwe ndibanyakula okubatwala mu maka gammwe ag'omu ggulu.

Mazima, mazima, mu Kigambo kyange ekitukuvu (kubanga nze kigambo ekyefuula omubiri omutukuvu era atambulira mu mmwe), weewaawo, mu kigambo kyange ekitukuvu, temwasomanga nti omusango gutandikira ku nnyumba ya Katonda? Ndi kumpi okumaliriza omulimu mu bantu bange abalonde, abatuukirivu, era abalokole, omulibakolebwamu obupya mu nze ne muba nga nze, kubanga mufuuliddwa okufaanana n'omwana omu yekka owa Katonda. Empeera yange eri nange, era siribasalira musango lwa bibi byammwe; saabisasulira dda? Olw'okukkiriza kwammwe Obukulu bwange n'okweyagaliramu nange, musonyiyiddwa era muli batuukirivu mu nze, kubanga nnasasula omuwendo omunene ogutaali gwange okusasula kubanga ndi mutukuvu. Nedda, nnabaggyako ebibi byammwe, obutali butuukirivu, obulogo, n'obusungu n'ekibonerezo okuva ku mmwe, mulyoke mugattibwe ne Kitange n'Omwana. Nedda, ndisalira omusango ku mirimu gyammwe gye mwakolera ku nsi.

Era n'omusango gwange gulimu empeera nnene, era emirimu mingi girisigalawo egya zaabu, ffeeza, n'amayinja ag'omuwendo. Mazima, emiti, essubi, n'ebisasiro biryaka ng'ebisusunku, era mulifuna okufiirwa, newankubadde mulirokolebwa ng'okuyita mu muliro. Kubanga omuliro gwange guli gwa kulongoosa, mwe nnalongoosa emyoyo gyammwe ng'omuweesi wa zaabu bw'alongoosa zaabu we, ng'aggyamu ebicaafu. Munywerere era mubeere bateefu mu nze. Ani oyo alina okubalumbuula, newankubadde mazima muyigganyizibwa era mugumiikiriza okubonaabona? Temutya, bankyawa era nammwe babakyawa. Naye, mu kununulibwa kwammwe mulijja okusiimibwa ennyo mu kitiibwa kye ndibayambaza ku lunaku olwo oluli okumpi era olusembera amangu. Kitange aludde ng'ayagala okubalaba mu maaso ge mu kisenge kye eky'obwakabaka, entebe zammwe gye zibalindiridde, kubanga Kitange abatudde mu bifo eby'omu ggulu. Mmwe si be yalonda, si babaka be, si kitiibwa kye, engule, n'omuwendo ogutayinza kubalika? Mazima, temulina muwendo.

Omusango gugenda kugwa ku Babulooni Omukulu mu bbanga ttono, era guliba musango gwa ntiisa, naye gwagugwanidde. O' omuntu, ndikugumiikiriza kutuusa ddi? Mwavudde mu ggwanga ly'abatukuvu eryali ng'ettaala eri ensi okujja gye ndi, ne mufuuka eggwanga ly'abakaaba ab'obwenzi. Nnaawudde abantu bange okuva mu mmwe, era okwawukana kuli kumpi okutuukirira ng'emirembe gino egy'ekisa giggwaawo. O' omuntu, lwaki ogoberera omubi, n'okkiriza enguzi ze ezitaliiko mugaso okusinga nze, Kristo ataggwaawo, mu kutangaala kwange kwonna n'ekitiibwa.

Kitange yawa ebiseera amawanga gonna okwenenya, era abantu bangi, n'ekibiina kinene, bakkiriza ekyange kye nnawa mu kisa kyange ekirookola ekibafukiddwako.

Naye ggwe, o' omuntu ow'obutonde agaana ebintu by'omwoyo n'anyoomoola muliraanwa we, olijja okufuna omusango omunene ku nsi yo, era Babulooni Omukulu talibaawo nate. Babulooni, ofuuse ggwanga lya bakaaba abazannya omulimu n'engoye zaabwe waggulu w'emitwe gyabwe ng'endogoyi enkazi ey'omu nsiko mu bbugumu erindirira omuyise addako. Mwefuulidde abacaafu n'emibiri egitali gyammwe, mwasanyukira era mwagala ebibi byammwe, era omusango ogutaggwaawo guligwa ku bonna abalina emitima emikakanyavu ababala okusaddaaka kwange ng'ekintu ekiwewufu era ekitaliiko mugaso okulaba. Mufuuse ggwanga lya bakola bibi n'abamenya endagaano abakyusa abakyala ng'engoye enkadde ne baggyako empya okutuusa empya lw'ecaafuwala. Ebigambo byammwe ebinene bye mwayogera ku Kitange birivunaanibwa. Ebirowoozo byammwe ebitaliiko mugaso biriteekebwa mu maaso gammwe, era muliwaayo okubivunaanibwa. Naye okwewoza kwammwe tekulimalawo kubayimirizaawo nga mutuukirivu mu maaso gange. Okubaawo kwammwe kwonoonese, era mu bulalu bwammwe mwerondera omubiri gwammwe oguvunda, kubanga nga Kitange bwe yagamba, mazima mulifa. Naye, kikyabewuunyisa nti mu kufa mulifa. Newankubadde okufa kwammwe okujjuvu tekwaliwo mangu ago, mubadde mufa okuva ku lunaku lwe mwassa omukka gwammwe ogwasooka. Omusango gwategeezeddwa ku ggwanga lyammwe, era n'eggulu teryogera kye kimu? Ndirizikiriza ddala ensi yammwe, n'ebizimbe byammwe ebiwanvu birigwa, n'ekizikiza ekikutte kiribikka ku nsi. Bbugwe wammwe baamumenya dda, era obukadde bujja okubaggyako ekyo kye nnabawa, kubanga ndigejjesa omukono gw'abalabe bammwe mu musango gwammwe. Ebiseera byammwe biri kumpi okuggwaawo, era muli ggwanga erisibe mu maaso gange, eririndirira bamalayika bange okujja mu musango. Muligwa nnyo ng'amazzi bwe gajja ku mmwe, ng'okukankana bwe kujja ku mmwe, ng'amagye bwe gasumululwa, n'ensi yammwe eriyulibwamu ebitundu. Enjala erigwa ku nsi yonna, n'olutalo lujja ku nsi yonna ng'ensolo eva mu vvu mu nteekateeka zaabwe ezaakolebwa edda.

Ebiseera biweereddwa okwenenya, era bangi baddidde mu kukkiriza, ekisa ekinene kye nnawa ng'omusango tegunnatuukirizibwa. Sirina ssanyu mu kuzikirira okugenda okujja, naye ne kaakano, bangi babeera mu kibi ekinene, ne balya n'abaana baabwe bennyini, era temufaayo okutuusa ssente zammwe bwe ziba kumpi. Ndiribaggyako eby'obugagga byonna, eby'okulya ebirungi, essanyu, n'obulungi. Mulikala mu maaso g'eggulu, nga munoonya akatundu k'eby'okulya abangi ke bataliyinza kufuna. Naye, abo abalikoowoola erinnya lya Mukama balirokolebwa.

Baana bange abato, lino lye ssawa esembayo ey'ebiseera ebisembayo mu mirembe gino egy'ekisa. Nnatumye bannabbi bange mu mulimu gwa mukwano gwange omwagalwa Yokaana Omutume Omubatiza. Tebaafuukanga ddoboozi erikaabira mu ddungu? Si be basumba bange, abayita era abanoonya abasembayo mu kisibo kyange ekibuzze? Mazima, nnina ekisibo kirala, era ebiseera by'okulokolebwa kwabwe bituuse, nga Yakobo alirokolebwa n'ensigalira ya Yakobo erinkkiriza nga Mukama waabwe, nga bonna abalonde bafuuka omuntu omu omupya. Nnatumye bangi okwetooloola ensi, nga balangirira okujja kwange.

Newankubadde bangi ku bantu bange balimbibwa era bali bbugumu, omwoyo gwange gulizuukuka mu bangi, era bangi balidda gye ndi. Okulabika kwange kuli kumpi. Muyimirire nga munywevu era mulangirire okujja kwange era mulangirire ebigambo byange eby'obulokozi eri bonna. Ebiseera biri kumpi okutuuka, era waliwo essirise ku buziba ng'eggye ly'omu ggulu lirindirira okufuuwa kw'ekkondeere okuliraanye okusinga bwe mulowooza. Naye, wasigaddewo akaseera katono nnyo, era n'abalala bangi balyokolebwa, kale temuggwaamu maanyi.

Nze oyo alunda ekkanisa yange era eyawa abantu ebirabo.

Nze Mukama omukulu ow'amaanyi, eyattibwa okuva ku ntandikwa y'ensi.

Nze oyo eyawaayo obulamu bwe olw'ekisibo, Kristo omulamu Katonda gwe yazuukiza mu bafu.

Nze Kigambo ekyefuula omubiri abeera mu mmwe.

Nze mwana omu yekka owa Katonda eyajja mu mubiri, ye Masiya ow'amaanyi owa Isirayiri wa Katonda.

Ndi kumpi, n'okulabika kwange kutuuse.

Nze mulamuzi omutuukirivu asaanidde okusumulula obubonero.

Nze mwana gw'endiga gwa Katonda, eyattirwa ebibi by'abantu bange.

Nze mulokozi atalina kibi alokola okutuuka ku nkomerero.

Nze musajja amanyi obunnaku n'ennaku nnyingi, naye essanyu lyange lisigala lijjudde.

Nze Mukama omulamu, omusumba omutuukirivu.

Nze oyo eyalangirirwa bannabbi bonna era nnina ebisumuluzo by'okufa n'emagombe, era Kitange yateeka ebintu byonna mu ggulu ne ku nsi mu mikono gyange egy'amaanyi.
