Okulabula okw’Obwangu kwa 911!

Okulabula okw’Obwangu kwa 911! Okulabula okw’Obwangu kwa 911! Okulabula okw’Obwangu kwa 911!

Bw’atyo bw’alangirira Mukama wo ow’olubeerera! Mazima, mazima, nnakoowoola! Mazima, mazima, nnakuba ebiwoobe! Mazima, mazima, nnakungubaga! Mazima, mazima, nnalabula! Kale eddoboozi lya Yeshua HaMashiach lyalangirira emisango egigenda okujja nga kulabula eri bonna abaalina amatu okuwulira n’amaaso okulaba n’omutima okutegeera. Naye abasigaddewo bange bawulidde eddoboozi lyange ne beeteekereteekera okujja kwa Mukama waabwe era Omulokozi waabwe ow’olubeerera, Omwana gw’endiga gwa Katonda era Empologoma ey’ekika kya Yuda, era mu butuukirivu njija okusala omusango n’okulwana olutalo.

Laba! Ensi ya Mukama n’okujula kwayo, era Nze, Kristo Yesu, nnina ekyapa ky’ensi, kubanga bwe busika bwange. Era nnawaayo omuwendo mu musaayi gwange ogw’okutangirira, ensi ereme okuzikiririra ddala mu kibi n’obutali butuukirivu, kubanga Yahweh yakuumye ekitonde kye bonna abayite era abalonde, n’abo bonna abazaalibwa mu ttaka eddemyu abayitiddwa eri obulamu obutaggwaawo, balyoke bazaalibwe omulundi ogw’okubiri mu nsigo etevunda, era balyeyimirira mu kisenge ky’Entebe ya Yahweh ng’abaana abakazaalwa, n’abo abazaaliddwa omulundi ogw’okubiri mu butuukirivu obw’olubeerera okutuuka mu mirembe gyonna ku kiseera ekigereke, era ekiseera kituuse kati! Wadde tebaliba baana bato, wabula bakabaka ab’amaanyi ab’olubeerera era bakabona, kubanga obwo bwe busika bwabwe, era mu ngeri eyo gye balina okuweererezaamu Yahweh wansi w’obufuzi bwange; kubanga Nze Mukama wa Bakama era Kabaka wa Bakabaka, era Nze kabona wo omukulu ow’olubeerera emirembe gyonna ng’engeri ya Merukizeddeeki bw’eri, n’obufuzi bwange tebulina nkomerero.

Wulira eddoboozi lyange, owulire mu mitima gyammwe mu kuwuliza, era mutege amatu gammwe eri amagezi, era muwulire ebigambo by’Awasa omugole, ggwe Omugole wange! Mazima, njogera n’abeesigwa, era nsaba emitima gyammwe mu kuwuliza okujjuvu eri ebigambo byange. Kubanga ebiseera by’okulabula biggwaayo era binaatera okukomekkerezebwa, n’obulumi bw’okuzaala tebukoma kati. Kubanga Nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, Nze Adamu ow’enkomerero era nfuuse omwoyo oguleeta obulamu era nkyusiddwa emirembe gyonna, era ng’omubiri bwe guli simanyiddwa nate.

Mazima, mazima, nnakolebwa mu kifaananyi ky’omubiri ogw’ekibi, naye mu musaayi ogw’ekibi gwa Adamu sirina mugabo wadde enkizo, so ssirangako na kusaasaaniramu mu kuvunda kw’omubiri omwonoonefu, ogw’ekibi. Nze Adamu ow’enkomerero, era ng’omuntu bw’akolebwa mu kifaananyi kya Adamu ku kuzaalibwa kwe okw’obutonde, era ng’ekibi n’okufa bwe byajja olw’obutawulira bwa Adamu, bwe kityo obutuukirivu n’obulamu obutaggwaawo biritondebwa mu bujjuvu munda mu mmwe ku lwange, Kristo Yesu, amangu ago nga nkuŋŋaanya abeesigwa gyendi. Ku mazima amakulu, Nze Adamu ow’enkomerero, era nfuusiddwa omwoyo oguwa obulamu, era mu butuukirivu n’obutukuvu obw’amazima mwe mulizaalibwa omulundi ogw’okubiri okutuuka mu mirembe gyonna.

Era newankubadde nga bonna abazaaliddwa omukazi (okuggyako Omwana gw’endiga gwa Katonda) basaasaaniramu mu bujjuvu mu kuvunda okwasigibwa mu mubiri Setaani olw’obutawulira bwa Adamu, bwe kityo bonna abazaaliddwa omulundi ogw’okubiri mu nsigo yange etevunda balisaasaaniramu mu bujjuvu mu butuukirivu n’obutukuvu obw’amazima. Kubanga buli omu ku mmwe alitondebwa buto mu butuukirivu, nga talina kibi, era nga mutuukirivu ddala ng’abaana ba Katonda ab’olubeerera era ab’emirembe gyonna, be ndissaawo nga bakabaka bange era bakabona bange era be ndyetwalira ng’omugole wange mu ndagaano entukuvu ey’olubeerera, era balibaawo nange emirembe gyonna, nga tebalivaawo.

Naye, omwana wa Katonda bwe yatyo bwe yalabika ndyoke nzikirize emirimu gya Setaani, kubanga Setaani ye yajja ng’akola ekibi okuva ku lubereberye. Yahweh yennyini ye Kitange, kubanga yantonda lwa nsonga ng’eyo, okuba Adamu ow’enkomerero, era oyo omuntu omupya gw’alitondebwa mu kifaananyi kye. N’olwekyo tegeera nti Nze, Kristo Yesu, nze mutwe, era ndibafuula bantu bapyapyã kubanga muli mubiri gwange, era mwenna muliba n’omulimu omukulu gwe mwatonderwa. Tegeera nti wadde natwala ekifaananyi ky’omubiri ogw’ekibi, ebibi bya bajjajjammwe tebyampitirako mu musaayi. Kubanga Yahweh yennyini yantonda mu butuukirivu n’obutukuvu obw’amazima, era nti Katonda yalabikira mu mubiri; kubanga andabye nze alabye Kitange, naye ng’omubiri bwe guli simanyiddwa nate.

Era bwe ntyo ne mpeebwayo olw’okununulibwa kwammwe n’okulokolebwa. Andabye nze alabye Kitange, era Nze ne Kitange tuli bumu mu bumu ne mu kigendererwa, okuggyako nga mu ntebe ye Yahweh mukulu okunsinga, kubanga tewali afaanana nga Katonda omukulu ow’emirembe gyonna! Naye mu lubuto nnakuliramu mu ngeri y’emu ng’omuntu yenna, naye mu lubuto temubaayo kuyita kwa musaayi okuva eri nnyina okudda eri omwana; era bwe ntyo ne ntwalibwa okuba omutukuvu ne nzaalibwa nga sirina kibi ng’omwana gw’endiga gwa Katonda, era bwe ntyo ne nneewaayo ng’Okuyitako okutuukiridde ng’ono ye kabona omukulu owa Yahweh okusinziira ku ntegeka ya Merukizeddeeki ey’olubeerera olw’okusoniyibwa ebibi eri bonna abalonda okukkiriza ekiweebwayo kyange eky’obulamu obutaggwaawo.

Era kaakano njija ng’empologoma ey’ekika kya Yuda kubanga nate ngaaniddwa abo be nnakyukira. Era nga bwe nnagaanibwa Isiraeri, abange bennyini be nnatumibwa eri, era olw’ekyo ne nkyukira Abamawanga, bwe batyo kaakano Abamawanga banganze, era kaakano nkomawo eri Isiraeri, era nkomawo mu nsi yange ey’obusuubize, kubanga nja kulokola abasigaddewo mu Yakobo ndyoke ndokoleko abamu ku bonna. Era okuva ku ntebe ya Dawudi ensi erifugibwa okuva mu nsi y’obusuubize, eyasuubizibwa edda Yahweh, era ekyo Yahweh kye yasuubiza kirirongoosebwa mu bujjuvu! Era nze oyo eyagabibwa olw’okutuukirizibwa kwa byonna.

Laba! Ebiseera bituuse kati, ebiseera bituuse kati, okuzaalibwa okupya lwe kulituukirira mu bujjuvu ne kutuukirira mu Nze, Mukama waffe Yesu Kristo, era ndituukiriza ebigendererwa bya Yahweh eby’olubeerera eby’edda, bye yateekateeka mu biseera ebyayita (n’ebiseera eby’edda ennyo bye mutasobola kutegeera mu kiseera kino) kubanga bulijjo mwakwekebwa mu mutima gwa Yahweh. Naye, ebigendererwa bya Yahweh eby’olubeerera kaakano biteekeddwawo okutuukirizibwa, era okukungula ebibala ebisooka kutuuse, n’essaawa etuuse ku mmwe. Kubanga ensi kaakano efuuse ng’omukazi alumwa okuzaala, eyeeteeseteese okuzaala, n’obulumi bwe n’obulumi bw’okuzaala tebukoma kati, n’okununulibwa kwe kusemberedde, n’essaawa y’okuzuukira n’okukuŋŋaanyizibwa etuuse.

Kubanga kijja kubaawo mu kaseera katono nga Nze, Kristo Yesu, nserengeta okuva mu ggulu n’oluyoogaano, n’eddoboozi lya Malayika Omukulu, n’ekkondeere lya Katonda, n’ekkondeere eryo ery’enkomerero lirivuga, era ku ddoboozi eryo okujja kwange kulimansuka mu bbanga n’okumasamasa okungi (ekitiibwa kyange kwe kwaka okw’olubeerera), n’ensi erimanya nti waliwo Katonda mu ggulu n’ensi eritegeera nti olunaku lwa Mukama lutuuse ku bo. Era bwe kityo ensi erizaala abo bonna abeebakidde mu Kristo era abaddayo mu nfuufu y’ensi, n’emyoyo gyabwe ne giddyayo eri Katonda, n’emmeeme zaabwe zibadde ziwumudde bulungi okuva ku lunaku lwe baaziba amaaso gaabwe okwebaka mu ttaka eddemyu. Kubanga omusajja omukadde ow’ettaka eddemyu alibumbulukuka mu bupya obw’obulamu, n’ensi eriggyayo ku kiragiro kyange abo abebase mu Kristo, nammwe abakyali abalamu era abasigaddewo mulinyagulibwa mu bire nabo okusisinkana nange mu bbanga. Era kiribaawo mu kaseera katono, era mwenna mulizaalibwa mu kitiibwa eky’olubeerera ne mutondebwa buto mu bujjuvu ng’ekitonde ekipya eky’amaanyi ag’olubeerera n’ekitiibwa ebirukiddwa mu byambalo byammwe ebyeru ennyo bye mulisanga amangu ago nga mwambadde, era ebiseera bituuse kati.

Zisanga abali mu nsi. Zisanga, zisanga, zisanga nnyingi abali mu nsi. Zikusanga, ggwe omuntu ow’obutonde, kubanga oganye okuyita kwange. 911 okweraliikirira nnalabula, ne nkoowoola, era kaakano olunaku lutuuse ku ggwe, era okyali mu butamanya butamanya eri okuzikirizibwa kwo okunaatera okujja. Ggwe, omuntu ow’obutonde, oyonoonye amaloboozi g’abo be nnatuma, era nnatumye amaloboozi mangi gy’oli, era oganze abatume bange n’abanaabi bange n’ababaka bange; n’abaana nabo wabagaana, kubanga mu mimwa gy’abawere mwe nnatuukiriza ettendo, n’abato balose ebirooto ne baweebwa okwolesebwa, era balangirira n’obwesigwa okujja kwange! Era bwe kityo kiriba nti okuzikirizibwa okw’amangu ago kuligwa n’okulabula kulikoma. Kubanga kuno si kulabula wabula kulangirira kubanga ebiseera by’okulabula binaatera okukoma mu kaseera katono, era mu kifo ky’okulabula okutuukirizibwa kuliyimirirawo, n’okuzikirizibwa okw’amangu ago kuligwa mu kaseera katono, era toliwonye, ai abali mu nsi abagaana omutonzi waabwe.

Omuntu ow’obutonde, ova mu kibi n’odda mu kibi mu makubo go amagwagwa ag’ekibi, era wadde nga nfukidde ensi ekisa kyange n’okusaasira kwange olyoke owone omusango gwo ogunaatera okujja, mu kifo ky’ekyo olonze okwetikka omusango gwo n’akabi, era bwe kityo bwe kiriba. Era bwe kityo kaakano omusango gutandikira ku nnyumba ya Katonda, era bwe kityo bwe kiriba. Babulooni ow’ekyama, era eggwanga eryo eddene erya Amerika, kaakano liyimiridde nga lisibiddwa enjegere, liri bwereere, era lyaavu era lirindiridde okuzikirizibwa kwalyo okw’enkomerero.

Olw’ebibi bye, obwenzi, obwenzi, emizizo, n’obugwagwa obungi n’obubi bwonna bw’ayonoonye ensi, kaakano agenda kwesanga n’okuzikirizibwa kwe. Era omwenzi omukulu kaakano aweereddwayo ddala mu mikono gy’abalabe be, naye azibiikiriza azibiikiriza okutuusa lwe walibaawo azibiikiriza okuzibiikiriza nate, era mu kaseera katono azibiikiriza aliggibwawo ku kukuŋŋaanyizibwa okukulu kw’abatukuvu bange! Era mu kiseera ekyo azibiikiriza lw’aliggibwawo, baliteeka mu nkola okulumba kwabwe okw’ekyama kwe bateeseteese era kwe bateeseteese okumala emyaka mingi kati, kubanga bwe kityo bwe baweereddwa mu mikono gyabwe okutuukiriza, era Babulooni ow’ekyama alizikirizibwa ddala mu ssaawa emu.

N’amayembe ekkumi galizikiriza omwenzi omukulu kubanga bakyawa omwenzi, era balimufuula matongo era bwereere ne balya ennyama ye ne bamwokya n’omuliro. Era okukankana okungi kulijja ku nsi, era eriyulibwa, n’amazzi galibikka ennyanja zaayo, n’amazzi galibikka omutima gwayo, era eriyawulibwamu. Kubanga bwe kityo bwe kyawandiikibwa nti: ebibi bye bituuse mu ggulu, era Katonda ajjukidde obutali butuukirivu bwe. Mumusasule, nga naye bw’abasasudde, era mumuwe emirundi ebiri okusinziira ku bikolwa bye: ne mu kikompe ky’ajjuza gye muli, mmwe mujjuze emirundi ebiri. Ng’oggyeeko okwegulumiza kwe n’okubeera mu ssanyu, bwe mutyo mumuwe okubonyaabonyezebwa n’ennaku: kubanga ayogera mu mutima gwe nti, ntuula nga ndi nnabagereka, so siri nnamwandu, so siriraba kunakuwala. Kyeliva amagombe ge gajja ku lunaku lumu, okufa, n'ennaku, n'enjala, era aliyokebwa n'omuliro: kubanga oyo Katonda amunenya, ye Mukama ow'amaanyi. Awo malayika ow’amaanyi n’asitula ejjinja ng’olubengo olunene, n’alisuula mu nnyanja, ng’agamba nti, Bwe lityo ekibuga ekyo ekinene Babulooni lwe lirisuulibwa n’amaanyi, so tekirirabika nate.

Era bwe kityo kiriba nti eggwanga lino eddene edda, lye nnunula abangi, kaakano liritwalibwa mu musango, nga n’Ekkanisa yange n’Omugole wange bwe batwalibwa mu ggulu, gye mbategekedde ekifo eky’obulokozi n’obubudamo gye balifunira obusika bwabwe. Era Babulooni ow’ekyama talibaawo nate, n’ekibuga kye ekikulu ennyo ekisitudde omutwe mu kwerowooza nti tewali kijja kukyuka kirigwa ddala wamu nakyo, ne kitaddamu kuddawo.

Mazima, mazima, ebibuga bye byonna eby’ekibi era eby’omuzizo birigwa, n’eminara gyabyo girigwa, n’emiro miro egy’amaanyi n’okuzikirizibwa birifuula amakubo gaabyo obutayitikamu. Olwo amagye amamyuufu, n’amagye g’ensolo g’aleese olw’okuzikirizibwa kwayo, galisumululwa ku ekyo ekisigaddewo, era galirya ennyama yaayo. Era bwe kityo kiriba nti buli alikoowoola erinnya lya Mukama alirokolebwa. Naye, abo abasigaddewo batundiddwa mu buddu olw’obuli bw’amagoba kubanga abakulembeze ba Babulooni ow’ekyama balidde mu nkwe abantu baayo era babawaddeyo ng’omusingo, era okusasula kaakano kutuuse.

Mutegeere ebyabaddewo, abantu bange, era mujjukire ebigambo byange. Abasumba abalwadde olukuku baakolaganira okumuzikiriza; omusumba omu omulwadde olukuku afuga mu kyama yeeyambisa omukadde omuwulize nga kalabalaba, ate omusumba omulala omulwadde olukuku alabika ng’ayigganyizibwa omukadde omuwulize akolaganira wamu n’omusumba omulwadde olukuku afuga mu kyama. Kubanga befuula ng’abalabe mu kiseera kino, naye bonna bava mu kisibo kimu ekirwadde olukuku, nga batuukiriza ebigendererwa by’ogusota omunene, era bwe ntyo bwe nnagamba Ekkanisa yange, Omubiri gwange, era n’Omugole wange! Era n’olwekyo abasumba abalwadde olukuku baawaddeyo Babulooni ow’Ekyama mu mikono gy’abalabe be, kubanga bwe batyo bwe baatumibwa.

Baamwambudde omwenzi obwereere, kubanga bwe batyo bwe baaweebwa okukola, kubanga n’abalabe ba Yakuwa bagondera eddoboozi lye. Mutegeere nti baagenderera okuleka amaanyi ge ag’eby’entalo mu nsi z’ebweru ne bawa amayembe ekkumi ebyama bye balyoke bafuge n’amaanyi. Ge mayembe ekkumi agaliwa ensolo amaanyi gaago, kubanga bwe batyo bwe baaweebwa okukola.

Omwenzi omukulu alwanye entalo ezitakoma era ezitaliiko nsa, ezikoleddwa okumalako amaanyi ge. Babulooni ow’Ekyama, nga kya kugenderera, yatandika olutalo n’Eddubu alyoke amaleko amaanyi ge n’obuyinza bwe, ne kiba nti kati asigadde bwereere era nga taliiko kuwomererwa, era kati aweereddwayo mu mikono gy’ogusota omumyufu ng’omunyago. Era buli kitundu ky’obwakabaka bwe obutudde ku mazzi amangi kyalirifooka munyago mu mikono gyabwe. Mutegeere nti bali beetegefu era bajja kusitukirawo mangu okukola mu bumu okulumba kwabwe okw’ensi yonna, era balirumba ku njuyi zonna mu kuzikiriza okw’amangu, era baliba n’obuwanguzi obw’akaseera katono nnyo omusango nga tegunnaba kugwa ku nsi mu busungu obungi.

Mutegeere nti ensi kati efugibwa Ogusota, era kati asibiddwa ku nsi ng’alindirira omusango gwe, era bwe kityo obwakabaka bwe bulizikirizibwa ddala era bubonerezebwe (kubanga si kabaka), era Setaani alisibibwa n’omujegere omunene mu bunnya obutakoma okumala ekiseera ekyalagirwa, kubanga emyaka olukumi giweereddwayo ng’olunaku olw’okuwummula eri abanunule, era tuliba ne ssabbiiti nnyingi. Era n’olwekyo amagonde ge ganyoola ne gabuna ku bakabaka b’ensi ng’ennyanga bw’ewamba omuyiggo gwayo, era mu ngeri y’emu nnabbubi bw’aluka olutimba lwe; bw’atyo bw’akwata omuyiggo gwe, naabo bonna abakwatibwa bateresebwa eri ennyanja ey’omuliro ennene eyo.

Kubanga nnabbubi atuula mu mpuku ye, era nnabbubi yeekwese mu nsi ey’ensuubizo. Era bwe zityo engeri za Setaani, kubanga engeri za Setaani za butiitiizi. Yeetolontose ku lubuto lwe era agezezzaako okwekwasa mu nnimiro ya Yakuwa, naye tewali kikisiddwa mu maaso ga Yakuwa, era tewali kikisiddwa mu maaso gange, kubanga nze Yeshua HaMashiach, y’ensigo eyasuubizibwa ey’omukazi. Era nnaabeta omutwe gw’omusota, era kiriba ekiwundu eky’olubeerera, ky’atalyerabira.

Naye bwe kityo bwe kiri nti omuigo gwe gugabana ku bubi bwe, era n’olwekyo nnabbubi aluse ensi mu lutimba lwe okuyita mu bubi obungi, era nnabbubi kati ali mwetegefu, kubanga essaawa ye etuuse. Era n’olwekyo nnabbubi kati atambuza amagulu ge amangi n’ateeka ebintu ku kimeeza mu bifo byabyo, bisobole okubeera ebyetegefu. Asenze ensi mu lutimba lwe, ng’asuubiza mu bulimba olunaku olupya; era okunafuwa kw’ekiseera kyabwe eky’obulungi kye banoonya okuba nakyo, naye tebaliba na kiseera kyabwe kya bulungi, wabula ennyanja ennene ey’omuliro eyaka ennyiriri eza bbulu ezisinga okwokya gwe musika gwabwe.

Era n’olwekyo nnabbubi abiyisa ku lutimba lwe n’abiteeka mu bifo byabyo nga byetegefu. Nnabbubi ye sinagoga ya Setaani, eyayingirira ensi zange ez’obusika n’ensuubizo, era balisimbulibwawo ddala ne bazikirizibwa, era tebaliba na kikolo wadde ezzadde. Naye baliweereza ebigendererwa bya Yakuwa eby’olubeerera okumala akaseera katono, kubanga ekibiina ekinene ennyo kirirokolebwa mu kuzikirira mu wiiki ya Danyeri ey’amakumi musanvu. Buli muntu alikoowoola erinnya lya Mukama alirokolebwa, bw’atyo bw’ayogera Mukama Omutuukirivu, ye Yeshua HaMashiach, ye Yesu Kristo mu lulimi Olungereza; era mu lulimi lwonna lwe balikoowoola, baliwulirwa ne balokolebwa ne bazuukizibwa mu bulamu obuggya, era mu bwakabaka bwange obutuukirivu mwe baliyingira mu mpeera ey’olubeerera n’ekitiibwa eky’olubeerera.

N’olwekyo, bwe kityo bwe kiri, okuzikirizibwa kwa Babulooni ow’Ekyama kati kuli kumpi, era abeesigwa bange bagenda kuggyibwawo. Era nze, Kristo Yesu, sikyajja nate eri abatuula ku nsi nga mbalabula kubanga nabalabula nnyo era mu ngeri nnyingi ne bangaanira ku buli nsonga; era bwe kityo bwe kiri, kubanga bwe kyawandiikibwa. Naye, wadde nga Yakuwa amanyi byonna eby’emabega, Yakuwa akyalabula abo abagenda okutwalibwa mu musango ku mpererwe yaabwe eteekwa okubatuukako, era mu kwagala kwe yantuma olw’obulokozi bwabwe, era abangi ne balokolebwa.

Naye kati banjeemye ne basisinkana omulokozi waabwe ow’obulimba, kubanga kati bagoberera Balaba, era kati balonzeewo Ogusota okuba katonda waabwe. Era n’olwekyo sikyajja nate kulabula muntu wa bulijjo wabula okuyigiriza n’okuteekateeka abantu bange balyoke babeere beetegefu olw’okugenda kwabwe, kubanga tewakyali kiseera. Yakuwa, wadde ng’amanyi bino byonna okuva edda n’edda, era mu butuukirivu bwe n’okwagala kwe, alabudde abateneenya, wadde ng’amanyi nti abangi tebagenda kwenenya mu kiseera kino, naye ekibiina ekinene ennyo kirirokolebwa okuyita mu muliro ogwoza ogw’okubonaabona kwa Yakobo. Era bonna abalikoowoola erinnya lya Mukama okutuusa ku nkomerero balilokolebwa!

Naye abangi bankyukidde, era gy’oli ggwe, Omugole wange n’Ekkanisa yange n’Omubiri gwange, gye nzijja kati okukuŋŋaanya n’okukuteekateeka olw’okugenda kwo mu ggulu, kubanga kati bye biseera era ebiseera bye bino. Mutegeere, baana bange, nti guno si gwe mukyali nsi gye mwali mumanyidde okubeeramu, n’ennaku ez’edda kati ziyiseewo, n’olunaku lwa Mukama lutuuse. N’olwekyo mbagumya era mbalagirira mmwe mwenna abali mu Kkanisa yange, ye abangi bonna abaliwulira eddoboozi lyange okujjuza ettaala zaabwe amafuta ge nateekateeka n’okusalako entambi zammwe nga bwe nnalagira, kubanga olunaku lw’okununulibwa kwammwe lutuuse. Mazima ddala, sikyalabula naye ndi wano okulanga mu lwa Yakuwa, Katonda omunene ow’olubeerera, nti ebiseera bituuse; era ekyo Yakuwa ky’alangirira tewali muntu ayinza kukikyusa, wadde okukikyusa katono, era byonna birituukirira nga bwe byawandiikibwa.

N’olwekyo, baana bange, mutegeere ebiseera mwe muli kubanga temukyasobola kubeerawo mu bulamu obutaliiko nsa. Ebituukawo bitandise dda era ebiseera bya kabi. Mwekweke, baana bange abato, mu misango egitali gyammwe, era mutegeere nti n’amazzi gatandise okukaawa. Mbasaba, baana bange, okusigala mu bukuumi bwe nnabateerawo era muve mu bo kati mwawukane ddala muleme okugabana ku bibonyoobonyo byabwe. Bino bye njogerako si byammwe, baana bange, wabula biweereddwa okuzuukusa abasembayo ku abo abalijja eri obulokozi Yakuwa nga tannaba kuggalirawo oluggi lw’eryato ku kukuŋŋaana kw’Ekkanisa yange ku kkondeere erisembayo, era basigaddemu batono nnyo.

N’olwekyo muwulirize eddoboozi lyange, baana bange, muveemu, muveemu, muveemu mu ye. Omulanga ogw’amangu ogwa 911, kubanga olunaku luli kumpi. Ebigwa bitalaze bisumuluddwa dda ku nsi, naye nteeseewo obukuumi n’emirembe eri abo abalindirira okwogerera kwange, era ekkondeere liri kumpi okufuyibwa. N’olwekyo, musigale mu bukuumi bw’amawuliro ge nnabateekerateekera, era akabi akatagendereddwamu mmwe kalibayitawo mu ngeri y’emu malayika w’okufa bwe yayita ku baana ba Isirayiri mu Misiri. Okuggyako nti, mu kaseera katono temuliba ku nsi, era temuliddayo kuba mu mbeera egudde, kubanga okunulibwa kwammwe tekukyali kumpi, wabula kubatuuseeko ddala. N’olwekyo, musigale nga muli beetegefu.

Njogera n’abeesigwa era nsaba emitima gyammwe mu kugonda okujjuvu eri ebigambo byange.

Ebigambo bino mbitumye okuyita mu muweereza wange, era okuyita mu muweereza wange ebigambo bino bitumiddwa.

Mpagira bonna abawulira ebigambo bino okuwuliriza ddala okuyigiriza kwange n’okutegeera nti kati kye kiseera era mu kaseera katono okutuukirira kulijja kujja. Nneetaaga emitima gy’abeesigwa bange mu bujjuvu, n’olwekyo mweyongere okwetukuza mu musaayi gwange ogutangirira era mwatule ebibi byammwe gye ndi nsobole okubatukuza mu bujjuvu mu musaayi gwange ogutangirira. Temulimbibwanga bya kuwulira byammwe, kubanga Babulooni ow’Ekyama muzibe wa maaso era kiggala wa matu era nga yeegendereza obutamanya musango gwe oguli kumpi, ogutandise dda era ogutasobola kuyimirizibwa. N’olwekyo, temugabananga ku buzibe bwe obw’amaaso muleme okubonaabona ku bibonyoobonyo bye, era mwawukane mu bo mubeere beetegefu mu bujjuvu olw’okulabika kwa Mukama wammwe ow’olubeerera.
