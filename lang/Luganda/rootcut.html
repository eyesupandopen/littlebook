Okusikula Omulandira

Muggule amaaso gammwe musome Ebigambo byange era mutere emitima n’ebirowoozo byammwe ku Bigambo byange, mmwe Abaana ba Adonai-YHVH, mutegeere embeera yammwe n’obutali bwenkanya obw’entiisa obwabaddewo olw’enteekateeka zaabwe ezakwekebwa edda. Mutegeere eddoboozi ly’enjogera yange era mump'amatu gammwe mbajjuzeemu amaloboozi g'Ebigambo byange Ebitukuvu eby'obutuukirivu ebitonnyezeddwa ku mikutu gya Yeshua HaMashiach, ye oyo akola bulijjo mu butuukirivu byonna Adonai-YHVH by'ayagala!

Munjigulire emitima gyammwe egikuumibwa gye mwekweka okuva mu nsi eno enoonya okubayingiza mu nteekateeka y’ekisolo ey’enkomerero, nti nga bwe kiri wansi, era kibe bwe kityo waggulu. Era yeekaalu zino ezafuulibwa, abaana ab’obutawulize, abafuuwa n’ebifuuyi byabwe era abanoonya okutabula ensi mwe mubeera, nga babaloga mmwenna n’ebyo bye bayita amasanyalaze ge mugoberera n’omululu nga musasula okuteeka ebibi byammwe ku mutimbagano.

Nga kiva mu kukkaanya n’okwenyigiramu kwammwe okujjuvu, ebirowoozo byammwe ne biweddwaamu amaanyi nga biri mu mbeera ey’okukkakkana, dopamine w’asitimulwa n’afulumizibwa olw’emikutu gyabwe okubasanyusa, ate nga mmwe mutunudde mu bifaananyi byabwe eby’obulimba ebireeta obubi mu mitima n’ebirowoozo byammwe buli kiseera. Olw'okuddamu kuno, bonna abatunuulira nga tebalowooza endabirwamu zaabwe enzirugavu, ze ndabirwamu, balogebwa okubeera mu mpetu z’ebibi byabwe ebipangiddwa mu kulimbibwa okw’amaanyi okujja okutuuka ku nkomerero mu kubuzaabuzibwa okunene okujja okukungula mu musango ddala bonna abalayira okulayira okwegatta ku kisolo kino eky’enkomerero ekigolokoka eky’amawanga g'Abamawanga.

Mbagambye abaana bange, n’olwekyo musome mutegeere nti: Endabirwamu Elimba!

N'olwekyo, mutegeere ensi mwe muli, mmwe ekkanisa yange, omubiri gwange, era n’omugole wange, mulyoke mutegeere nti nga Kristo Yesu tannafuuka Mukama wammwe, bangi ku mmwe mwasikulwa okuva ku Adonai-YHVH, era n’olwekyo mwagobebwa mu busika bw’endagaano olw’okugaana obukuumi bw’endagaano mu butawulize bwammwe, naye naddala olw’obutawulize bwa Bakitammwe, olw’ebibi byabwe mwe mwasaasaanyizibwa mu mawanga. Mutegeere nti ensi mwe muli efugibwa amaka gano 10 ag’edda, agayogera amazima nti si bakabaka, naye balifuna obuyinza ng’abakabaka mu ssaawa emu n’ekisolo. Awo amangu ago era mu ngeri etasuubirwa abeesigwa mu Kristo Yesu balivaawo wakati nga Babulooni ow’Ekyama azikirizibwa n’amaanyi ng’agudde ku mugabo gwe ogw’emirundi ebiri ogw’omusango ogutandise dda, olw’obugwagwa obuli mu kikompe ekyo ekya zzaabu eky’obwenzi bwe ky’atamizza nakyo amawanga n’ebibi eby’obugwagwa bwe.

Mutegeere ensi mwe muli, mmwe abaagalwa bange, era mutegeere nti abasinga obungi ku mmwe mutambulira mu butamanya ku nsi ey’amazima kwe muli, era kino lwa kuba nti enfumo zammwe ez'Abayesu n'ebyenjigiriza byammwe ebitasaana bibaziba ebirowoozo mu butamanya obw'ekivve bwe muyita okusoma, bye mwafuna mu masomero gaabwe ag’ebyaama ag'eddiguli ge muyita amasomero ne yunivasite, nga gakakibwa gavumenti ya Babulooni ow'Ekyama omussi. Ebitongole bino byennyini bikozesebwa ng’eby’okulwanyisa mmwe, nga biwakanya butereevu Kristo Yesu era naddala Adonai-YHVH nga biweereza butereevu abagudde.

Wabula, bangi ku mmwe mwembiirira okutunda abaana bammwe abakulu olw’okubanja mu mikono gy’abalabe bammwe be baliweereza mu buddu obw’ekittankaggye oba okulinyirirwa mu buwambe obw’ekittankaggye okuggyako nga beenenyezza amangu balyoke baharpazoed. Oba mpozzi baweebwe enkizo ey’okukoowoola erinnya lya Yesu okutuusa ku nkomerero yaabwe! N’olwekyo, abaagalwa bammwe mubasabire nnyo, mmwe abeesigwa bange, kubanga ndisaasira gwensaasira. Naye tewali kusosola mu bantu mu makubo gange, ge makubo ga Adonai-YHVH ddala kubanga nze Kigambo kya Adonai-YHVH ekyafuuka omubiri.

Weewaawo, diguli zammwe ez’amasomero ga masoniki ge mwenyumiririzaamu n’amalala zibayamba kwennyini kuyingira buweereza mu nteekateeka y’ekisolo eno emira abavubuka n’esalaasala abato era n’esiba bonna abasibiddwa mu kibi okusalirwa omusango eri bonna abagaana okwenenya. Mutegeere nti omuntu yenna agaana okweyawula ku kubuzaabuzibwa kuno okw’amaanyi ajja kusigala ng'asibiddwa ku musango gwa Babulooni ow'Ekyama awalibaawo olutalo ku batukuvu olujja okuba olw'ekittankaggye nnyo era bonna abakoowoola erinnya lya Yeshua HaMashiach, ye Yesu Omunazaaleeti, balilokolebwa ne bafuna empeera ey'amaanyi era ey'ekitalo ennyo nti okubonaabona kwabwe ddala tekugwanira kugeraageranyizibwa na kitiibwa ekijja okuweebwa mu mpeera z'obusika bye bajja okutuukako n'amaanyi. N'olwekyo, abaagalwa bammwe mubasabire nnyo!

Naye bwe kityo ekibiina kino ekyagwamu eky’okufa n’okubuzaabuzibwa okw’amaanyi okusikuddwa omulandira, abatuuze ba Babulooni ow’Ekyama gye beemalira okuwaayo abaana baabwe mu buweereza ng’abaddu eri obwakabaka buno obw’ekisolo. Abaana baabwe balyoke bafune okuyingira era basibibwe nga bo mu bwakabaka buno obw'omululu n'ekisolo obujjudde ebibi obusimbye amakanda mu bugagga obujama. Akayisanyo ak’emmese gye katawangulwa, era emmese gye zitaddukako mu kkubo lyazo eribuzabuza, ate nga zeeziya nnyo mu butaliimu, nga zikola emirimu egy’obuddu egitaliiko makulu okufuna okuwummula.

Muve wakati mu bo n’ebiruubirirwa byabwe bye bayita eby’ebyenfuna mu bulamu bwammwe era mutegeere nti tewali kuwummula kwonna eri abatuukirivu abanunulibwa Kristo Yesu, ye Yeshua HaMashiach. Muve mu biruubirirwa n’ebigendererwa byabwe era mutegeere, okussa essira lyammwe lyonna ku kuwummula kirowoozo kitali kya katonda nakalya ekikolebwa abantu abali ebweru w’endagaano abeewaayo okwefaako mu bulamu bwabwe mu kibi era nga tebalina kulung’amizibwa na Bufuzi bwa Kristo Yesu abakakasa bulijjo okukula kwammwe n’okuba abalamu obulungi mu kulabirira kwange eri mmwe mwenna. Muve wakati mu bo era mweyawule mu bintu byonna. Muntunuulire, mmwe abaagalwa bange, ndibakulembera n’amaanyi!

N’olwekyo, muve ku malaaya ono omukulu n’amakubo ge, kubanga bonna abanywerera ku bibi by’eggwanga lino erisinza ebifaananyi era ery’emizizo (eriri eggwanga ly’obwamalaaya ery'abamalaaya abenzi) abagala okuzannya obwamalaaya mu mikolo gyabwe egy’ekikaafiiri egy’obwenzi n’essanyu ery’omwenge era abagaanyi omwana gw’endiga gwa Adonai-YHVH bajja kugwa KAATI mu musango ogutaggwaawo gwe batayinza kudduka. Naye, kitundu ki eky’emizizo kya Babulooni ow’Ekyama ekitajjudde ebyo ebiyitibwa ebitundu by’amasanyalaze eby’ekibi ebyatega mu kibi omulembe gwonna ogw’abavubuka ababuze abakyawa Adonai-YHVH ne Kristo Yesu olw’Abayigiriza baabwe Abayesu era n’ebirowoozo byabwe eby’obufuzi eby’edalu ebijja okusisinkana omusango omukakali amangu ago era mu ngeri etasuubirwa kaakati.

Kale, mbawa okutegeera ensi eno embi eriwo kati eyakula okubetooloola mwembi mu nkukutu ennene n'okuwalana eggwanga okujjuvu okw'ekisirani kw'abagudde (ab'ebiseera byabwe eby'enkomerero n'omusango ogw'omuliro bituuse) ye nsi ey'okulimbibwa okw'amaanyi okuzimbibwa kwonna ku bulimba obuteekeddwa waggulu mu mbeera z'omulembe guno ez'amasanyalaze, emizannyo, Ttivvi, n'ennyimba ezijjudde obusamize obw'omuzizo abangi ze bategeebwa emikutu gyazo egikyankalamu, egitali mitonzi, era egy'obulabe egy'okulumba bye mulumbibwa mu mpetu za Babulooni ow'Ekyama ez'ekibi ezisinziira ku masanyalaze. Naye, bakkirizibwa okugolokoka mu kufuga kwabwe okumpi era okw'enkomerero mu bwakabaka bwabwe obusaliddwa omusango era obulemereddwa obw’okukola endagaano ezitali ntuukirivu ezikoma mu nkomerero yonna mu nnimi z'omuliro ezisinga obukambwe ez'ekibonerezo. Era nate ng'amba nti, muve mu bino byonna era mweyawule mu makubo gammwe gonna muleme kugabana bibi bye. Seera.

Mutegeere nti ennimi z'omuliro zino zennyini ez'okuwoolera eggwanga ziri nnamu ku kiragiro kya Adonai-YHVH ezikolezeddwa dda okusingawo okwookya okusinga ensibuko y'obuggumu yonna emanyiddwa era nga zeeteeseteese n'essanyu okwaniriza bonna abagwa mu musango ogutaggwaawo okuyingira mu kubonyabonyezebwa okunene okw'ekibonerezo okujja okuweebwa buli omu ku basaliddwa omusango ddala ng'ebibi byabwe bwe byali. Naye, tewali bukambwe wadde obusungu mu makubo ga Adonai-YHVH newaakubadde emisango gye kubanga mu musango gwabwe abagudde bafuna amakubo gaabwe bo, ebibi, ebigendererwa, era naddala amakubo gaabwe gonna amanyoomeseka ag'okubonyabonya ge bakozesa n'essanyu era nga tebasaasira Adamu, atalina musango mu makubo gaabwe agajjula obukyayi, era eyagwa mu kibi olw'obukujjukujju bw'abagudde bano abajja okuweebwa empeera ejjuvu awatali kusaasirwa olw'ebintu bye mwagumiikiriza mmwenna okuva ku muto okutuuka ku mukulu.

Bonna ababeegattako okuva ku mukulu okutuuka ku muto balyoke bagwe mu musango guno ogw’enkomerero emirembe gyonna. Buli muntu yenna okuva mu Adamu abeera mu bulamu obweweerayo mu kukkaanya okuziba amaso eri essanyu ly'ekibi alyoke asasulwe wamu nabo emirembe gyonna mu nnimi z'omuliro ezo zennyini ezisinga okwookya ezitaliiko kusaasira ezijja okusasula mu butuukirivu era ddala ng'ebikolwa byabwe ebitagasa n'ebibi bwe bibadde. Era si bibi byokka bye baakola naye n'ebibi bye baali bagenderedde naye ne baziyizibwa omukono gwa Adonai-YHVH okukola: Ebibi bino byennyini bijja kuweebwa mu nnimi z'omuliro ezo mu kunyooma emitima gyammwe emitagasa n'ebirowoozo byammwe. Ka bibe bibi bitya eby’omuzizo era eby’entiisa.

Ndaaba okutya kwo, ggwe kalangira omuto, era okankana nnyo. Tik tok tik tok.

Kati weenyumiririza wa eri abaana ba Adamu abatalina maanyi era abasirusiru abaagwa mu nkwe zo ezitali njesimbu, naye abanunulibwa okuva mu nkwe zo ezitagasa mwe wagenderera n'essanyu okusalira Adamu omusango gw'ebibi byo eby'omululu ogujjuddemu obukyayi eri Omutonzi wo. Kati, abo be walumbye n'otta n'okola obussi n'abo bonna okuva mu Adamu be wabonyabonyeza awatali nsonga. Ebintu ebyo byonna, n'ebintu byonna by'olowooza nti Adonai-YHVH teyabiraba bijja kukukolebwa mu kusasulwa okujjuvu era okutuukiridde kubanga Adonai-YHVH mutuukirivu era mutuukirivu mu misango gye eri mmwe mwenna abakola obubi abaalowooza nti kintu kitono okusitula ebisinziiro byammwe ku Elohim wammwe.

Tik tok, ebiseera byammwe bye bino KAATI.

Mutegeere mmwe mwenna abagudde nti omusango gwammwe ogujjuvu n’okubonyabonyezebwa olw’amakubo gammwe gonna agatasana kulibaawo awatali kusaasira, era kuligenda nga kweyongera buli kiseera okutuuka ku bukambwe obukyankalana n’omuliro obutali omu ku mmwe gw’ayinza kulowooza mu birowoozo byammwe ebibi ebitagasa, era nga tewali n’omu ku mmwe asobola kugumiikiriza n’akatono, newaakubadde nga mwenyumiririza mu busiru! Naye, gano ge makubo gammwe n’ebikolwa byammwe bye muzzibwamu, gano si ge makubo ga Adonai-YHVH, naye mu makubo gammwe mwe muli omusango gwammwe ogujjuvu era ogw’enkomerero emirembe gyonna.

Mutegeere, mu kubonyabonyezebwa kwammwe kwe mwaleeta n'essanyu awatali kusaasira ku bitonde bya Adonai-YHVH nti mujja kunywezebwa okugumiikiriza nga muli balamu mu bunafu obutaliiko maanyi, nga mukuumibwa nga muli balamu mu mbeera ey'obunafu era esingayo okuba ey'omuzizo, ey'obwangu okufuna ebiwundu ejja kwongera okuba ey'obwangu okufuna obulumi olw'okwokya okwo okujja okweyongerayongera okutuuka ku kusinga okwokya okujja okuba okwookya ennyo nga tewali n'omu ku mmwe ayinza kukitegeera mu kiseera kino, era nga tewali n'omu ku mmwe ayinza kulowooza okugumiikiriza.

Naye mujja kugumiikiriza.

Era bino bye birowoozo byammwe ebitagasa n’okwegomba n’ebikolwa byammwe mwe mulizingibwa emirembe gyonna, era amakubo gammwe gano ag’omuzizo tegajja kubaawo nate ku bitonde bya Adonai-YHVH byonna okubonyabonya n’ekisooka ku bitonde bye emirembe n’emirembe ebijja okufugibwa byonna mu kwagala okw’obutuukirivu n’obumu bwa Kabaka wa Bakabaka ne Mukama wa Bakama. Toggwaamu maanyi ggwe mukwano gwange, ebiseera byo bye bino KAATI.

Mmwe abagudde mulireetebwa okugumiikiriza ebikolwa byammwe byonna eby’omuzizo era ebitagasa awatali kusaasira okusinziira ku makubo gammwe amanyoomeseka ag’obukyayi agatali makubo ga Adonai-YHVH nakalya, naye galiweerezebwa mu musango omutuukirivu ogw’okusasulwa okujjuvu. Mutegeere nti ebikolwa byammwe byonna ebitagasa era eby’omuzizo bijja kwanjulibwa mu maaso g’ekitonde kya Adonai-YHVH kyonna balyoke balabe ddala amakubo gammwe ag’omuzizo agali ag’omuzizo ennyo n’okutuusa mmwe abagudde mwe mugezaako okugakweka mulyoke mugende mu maaso nagyo.

N’olwekyo, muwulire Ebigambo byange mmwe ekkanisa yange, omubiri gwange era n’Omugole wange, mbaleke mutegeere ekisolo kino ekyagolokose ng’ekibikka ekitali kituukirivu eky’amawanga ag’obwamalaaya agasikuddwa omulandira kati agasaasaanye okwetooloola ensi agakola obusuubuzi n’ensi zino ez’ekibi ekinene n’okunyoomebwa okubi, era ensi zino zennyini enjonoonefu ezimanyiddwa nga United States of America, eggwanga ly’obwamalaaya lye njita AmeruKa. Kubanga ddala ye nsi y'omusota ogwo ogw'ebiwawaatiro ogw'ensi ebibikkiddwako amagumba g'Abarefayimu mu ntuumo zaabwe ezitali kintu kirala wabula ebifo ebigulumivu abatambirako abaawaayo ssaddaaka abazungu n'abaana baabwe gye baziikibwa mu nteekateeka ey'emikolo.

Mutegeere ebikolwa by’ebitonde bino ebyagwa, era abantu bano ab’oku nnyanja Abakanani abalina entuumo zaabwe ezisaasaanye mu nsi y’e AmeruKa okutuusa leero. Nnyingi ku ntuumo zino ze nsiriira z'obulamu obwazikirira obw'Abarefayimu obwazimbibwa n'ekigendererwa waggulu w'ebifo ebyaziikibwa nga amataba teganajja n'ebizimbe ebibikkiddwa, emyoyo gya Banefulimu gye gisibiddwa ku nsi nga girina enjala n'ennyonta bye batayinza kukkusa. Era n’okutuusa nti emyoyo gino egisibiddwa egya Banefulimu gyandibadde n’omuntu atalimu kintu, omulongoose ate ayonjebwa era ng’atuukana ddala n’ebikolwa byagyo ebibi era eby’omuzizo eby’okuwaayo abaana ssaddaaka n’okuyiwa omusaayi.

Mutegeere nti ensi zino enjonoonefu ez'e AmeruKa zijjudde okwonoonebwa okw'omuzizo omunene. Ensi zino zikola ssemazinga omu oguterekeddwa era oguwereddwayo ng'ensi "etali ya ndagaano" okubeerwamu ab'omuzizo, abaalyanga abazungu abatalina ndagaano. Ensi zino zennyini kati zikozesebwa ng’ekibikka kyazo eky’enkomerero ekya New Atlantis ekisolo kyazo eky’enkomerero era ekikkirizibwa kilyoke kigolookoke. Bonna abegomba n'okukwatirwa ensonyi n'okwenyigira mu makubo g'abagudde ag'okwegulumiza n'okwegomba okw'obukyayi balyoke beeyawule olw'okulonda kwabwe okw'okukkaanya mu kulayira okutaggwaawo okwegatta n'ebikozesebwa byabwe mu mirembe gyabwe egya zzaabu egisuubizibwa. Nti omuntu yenna alayira n’aggya ekifaananyi kya Adonai-YHVH ku ye ng’akozesa eddagala aweebwa n’essanyu eri abagudde bano kubanga amakubo gaabwe gali bwe gatyo kubanga abagudde banoonya bulijjo okwekyusa.

Essuubi eddalu ery'ebiseera ebya zzaabu bye batayinza kufuna, naye omusango omukakali. Naddala essuubi eddalu era eribuzabuza erijja okuggwaawo emirembe gyonna mu kiseera ekyo ennimi z'omuliro ez'okuwoolera eggwanga lwe ziribazingako emirembe gyonna mu kubonyabonyezebwa okusinga obukambwe okweyongera obukambwe n'obulumi n'obwangu obw'okwookya, naye ne kusigaliramu nga kulamu mu musango okubonaabona, naye ne kusigaliramu mu bwangu obusingayo obw'oluvunyu olwookya olutaggwaawo olwongera okufuna ebiwundu naye nga lukyali lulamu.

Kati weenyumiririza wa mmwe mwenna abagudde? Nti mwatondebwa mu muliro era nga musobola okugumiikiriza era nga mwandigumiikirizza n’essanyu, nga museka nnyo wakati mu mmwe mu malala amangi.

Kubanga obugezi buno obw’ekitongole ky’obuyinza obwakwatibwa si busirusiru, nga Heylel Ben Shachar ataliiko, eyayogeza eby’ekivve mu bujulizi obwawandiikibwa mu Ggulu ne ku nsi byombi. Awo omusiru oyo eyasalirwa omusango n’awangulwa bwe yeewaana nti, "Heylel si musiru!" n’akakasa bonna obusiru obungi bw’omusiru ono omusirusiru ennyo mu mmwe mmwenna abasiru abagwa ababi.

Naye akyayogeza eby’ekivve okutuuka ku sekondi eno nti ayinza okuwangula Yeshua HaMashiach mu maanyi gaabwe ag’omuwendo, ne tekinologiya abakwekeddwa, mu maanyi g’obukyayi bwabwe, mu maanyi gaabwe ag’obumu bwe nzigula, bwe nzigula, olunnyo okubagatta: Nti bonna bajja kuwangula ddala Yeshua HaMashiach ne babeera mu mirembe gyaabwe egya zaabu!

Nga kweewaana kwa kitalo okuva eri akalangira akato akawanguddwa ate nga kati nze muzi mu nkola yammwe ey’obugezi n’entegeka ey’amagezi, okuyita mu nteekateeka ey’obuyinza etuukiridde eyaggyako Heylel Ben Shachar obuyinza, kati aggaliddwa ebweru mu ngeri ey’okumuduulira era lubeerera okufuga enkola ye. Si lwa kuba nti Yeshua HaMashiach yeetaaga tekinologiya wammwe mu maanyi n’obuyinza bwange, naye mulye mummanye amaanyi n’obuyinza bwange n’engeri gye mwawangulwamu ddala.
