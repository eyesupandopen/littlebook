Okunnyonnyola kwa “Mu Mirimu gya”

Obujulizi bwa Yesu Bwe Mwoyo gw'Obunnabbi

Laba, mbikkulira ekyama ky'akatabo akatono. Kubanga akatabo kaweereddwa, era omwoyo gw’obunnabbi bwe bujulizi bwa Yesu. Obujulizi bwange obukwata ku Yesu bwatandika mu bulamu bwange Mukama waffe Yesu Kristo bwe yampita mu buweereza n’okumukolera mu 1996 bwe nnali mbeera mu kibuga New York. Nnali nyanukudde okuyita kwa Mukama waffe okw'okwenenya nga neetaba mu kusaba kw’awaka era nnali muyizi mu limu ku masomo gaabwe. Lumu akawungeezi nnali ntudde mu wooteeri emu e Long Island City ne mukwano gwange omulungi, n’ansaba nzigule Baibuli yange mu Yokaana 4, n’atandika okusoma bino wammanga nga nange ngenda naye:

Yokaana 4:35-38

Mmwe temugamba nti Gikyasigadde emyezi ena, okukungula ne kutuuka? Laba, mbagamba nti Muyimuse amaaso gammwe, mutunule ennimiro, kubanga yeerudde dda okukungulwa.

Era akungula aweebwa empeera, era akuŋŋaanya ebibala eby'obulamu obutaggwaawo: oyo asiga n'oyo akungula basanyukire wamu.

Era wano olugero luli lwa mazima nti Omu asiga, omulala n'akungula.

Nnabatuma okukungula gye mutaatiganyizibwa mirimu: abalala baategana, nammwe ne muyingira mu kutegana kwabwe.

Mukwano gwange bwe yali asoma ennyiriri zino, ebigambo byalabika ng'ebibuuka okuva ku lupapula ne bigenda butereevu mu buziba bw'emmeeme yange. Omuliro gwakoleezebwa mu mutima gwange, era Mukama waffe n’antuma mu buweereza bwe mu 1996. Nnalekawo ddala obulamu bwange bwonna na buli kye nnali nkola okugenda mu buweereza. Nnafuuka mukulembeze mu kkanisa emu nnene, era oluvannyuma bwe nnamanya obukumpanya bwabwe, nnafuluma ne siddamu kugarukayo mu 2008. Nnalwana okumala emyaka mitono nga ngezaako okutambulira Kristo, naye ebibi byange byali binziza mabega. Mu 2013 obulamu bwange bwasasika ne nneesaanyaawo, era kyali kigwo kya mangu ddala ekyali kitezebwatezebwa ebbanga. Era Mukama waffe n’antwala mu kukangavvulwa okw’amaanyi n’ankuba emirundi mingi okutuusa lwe naddamu okutegeera nga Jjulaayi 7, 2020, bwe nneenenya ddala ebibi byange.

Mukama waffe yanteekateeka olw'olugendo luno ng'ampa okwolesebwa kungi, obubonero, eby'amagero, okuwonyezebwa okuwerako, era nzagobye emizimu mingi ku biragiro bya Mukama, era Mukama ampadde okukakasa kungi eri obutuufu bw'obuweereza bwange n'okuyitibwa kwange. N'olwekyo nziziddwa eri Mukama wange, nziziddwa eri Yahweh, nziziddwa eri mukyala wange ne mutabani wange n'empeera yange etaggwaawo.

Wakati mu mpera ya 2023, waaliwo ekintu eky'ekyamagero ekyaliwo. Emirundi mingi nsula ng'ebyawandiikibwa bikuba ku Ttivvi ne ku soundbar yange. Akawungeezi ako, nnagenda okwebaka ng'Endagaano Enkadde ekuba mu ddoboozi eriweweevu ennyo. Nnazuukusibwa mu tulo wakati mu kiro soundbar yange, eyali esirise ennyo, bwe yafuuka ey'eddoboozi eddene ekyambuzako. Tewaali muntu eyali agyongezza eddoboozi, era remote yali egalamidde ku kameeza kange okumpi n'ekitanda. Ennyiriri zino eziddirira okuva mu Ezekyeri zaakuba nga ntudde awo mu kwewuunya:

Ezekyeri 3:17-22

Omwana w'omuntu, nkufudde omukuumi w'ennyumba ya Isirayiri: kyova owulira ekigambo ekiva mu kamwa kange, obalabule okuva gye ndi.

Bwe ŋŋamba omubi nti Tolirema kufa; naawe n'otomulabula, so n'otoyogera okulabula omubi okuleka ekkubo lye ebbi, alyooke awonye obulamu bwe; omubi oyo alifiira mu butali butuukirivu bwe; naye omusaayi gwe ndigunoonya mu mukono gwo.

Naye ggwe bw'olabula omubi, ye n'atakyuuka okuva mu bubi bwe, newakubadde okuva mu kkubo lye ebbi, alifiira mu butali butuukirivu bwe; naye ggwe onoobanga owonyezza emmeeme yo.

Era nate, omutuukirivu bw'akyuka okuva mu butuukirivu bwe, n'akola obutali butuukirivu, nange ne nteeka enkonge mu maaso ge, alifa: kubanga tomulabudde, alifiira mu kibi kye, n'obutuukirivu bwe bwe yakola tebulijjukirwa; naye omusaayi gwe ndigunoonya mu mukono gwo.

Naye bw'olabula omutuukirivu, omutuukirivu aleme okwonoona, n'atonoona, talirema kuba mulamu, kubanga alabuddwa; era ggwe onoobanga owonyezza emmeeme yo.

Era omukono gwa MUKAMA gwali awo ku nze

Era bwe kityo bwe kibadde. Newankubadde mu kiseera ekyo nnali sitegeera nti Mukama yali antuma ng'omunnabbi w'ennaku ez'enkomerero, nnali kumpi okukimanya.

Nga Maaki 2, 2024, nnatandika okuwandiika obubaka obw'obunnabbi mu ekyo Mukama kye yanjogerera ng'emirimu gya Yokaana Omubatiza. Kino kyagenda mu maaso okuva nga Maaki 2 okutuuka nga Apuli 5, 2024, era mu kiseera ekyo nnafuna obubaka bw'obunnabbi 20. Obubaka bwonna bwali butunuulidde omusango ku Babulooni w'Ekyama, okuyita abatukuvu okwenenya, n'okulangirira okulabika okuli okumpi okwa Yesu Kristo yennyini, kubanga alirabikira mu bbanga ekiseera kyonna okuva kati eri abeesigwa mu Kkanisa ye. Mazima, nnalowooza nti obunnabbi bwa Apuli 5 bwe bwali obusembayo. Nnali nkyamye. Obubaka bw'obunnabbi obwaddako bwabaddaawo nga Apuli 10, 2024, era Mukama n’aŋŋamba nti yali antuma mu mulimu gwa Peetero okuliisa n'okulabirira ekisibo.

Enkula n'ebirimu by'obubaka byakyuka okuva ku katono ako, newankubadde bikyalimu ebintu okuva mu bubaka amakumi abiri obwasooka, era obubaka buno bungi bwegattira ddala. Nga Apuli 21, 2024, enkula n'ebirimu by'obubaka byakyuka nate, era Mukama n’aŋŋamba nti yali antuma mu mulimu gw'omutume Pawulo ng'omuyima w'ebyama bya Katonda. Era ebirimu mu bubaka byakyuka nate era bizimbiddwa ku bubaka obwasooka era byegattira ddala. Obubaka buno bwagenda mu maaso mu ngeri y'emu okutuusa nga Meeyi 16, 2024, ebirimu lwe byakyuka omulundi omulala. Nnagambibwa nti nnali ntumiddwa mu mirimu gya Yokaana Omubikkulirwa (ekigambo kye ssibangako mpulira wadde okukozesa), era nga Meeyi 16 obunnabbi obwo bwatandika.

Era omukono gwa Mukama waffe gulabikira mu kuyitibwa kwe yampa, era antumye gye ssateganirangako; abalala baategana, nange ne nnyingira mu kutegana kwabwe. Si nti abasajja bano ab'amaanyi aba Katonda tebaamaliriza misinde gyabwe, naye nti emirimu gino gigenze mu maaso okuva mu biseera ebyo byonna Yesu Kristo lwe yabawa emirimu gye yabatumamu. Ebyawukano bino nnali sibimanyi bwe nnatandika okulagula, era nga bwe yaŋŋamba, nnali nyingira mu mirimu gya buli omu. Ssiyigeza kugatta bintu bino okutuusa lwe byambikkulirwa Yeshua.

Okuyitibwa n'obuweereza ku bulamu bwange bituukiriziddwa. Abalala baategana, n'antuma mu mirimu gyabwe, era omwo mwe nteganidde. Ntumiddwa mu mulimu gwa Yokaana Omubatiza okulanga okujja kwa Mukama. Ntumiddwa mu mulimu gwa Peetero okusumba n'okuliisa ekisibo. Ntumiddwa mu mulimu gw'omutume Pawulo ng'omuyima w'Ebyama bya Katonda. Ntumiddwa mu mulimu gwa Yokaana Omubikkulirwa okuyita 144,000 n'okulaga ebigenda okujja.

Mu ngeri ey'obuntu, sisaanira kwegeraageranya ku omu ku basajja bano abakulu aba Katonda era sisaanira n'okubalamuusa obulamusa. Naye Mukama anfuulidde ow'omugaso, era ntumiddwa mu mirimu gino kyenkanyi. Obulamu bwange bwonna bundeetedde ku bubaka buno, era buli kigambo kijja kusanga amaaso, amatu, n'emitima gy'abo be kitumirwa. Singa Mukama anjetaaga okwongera okwogera, nja kukikola. Era nja kumaliriza olugendo lwange n'emirimu egyamperebwa okutuukiriza. Era n'okunnyonnyola kuno, omukono n'omukono gwa Mukama waffe Yesu Kristo gubikkuliddwa mu bubaka buno obw'obunnabbi obwamperebwa. Ntuukirizza emirimu gye yantuma okukola. N'olwekyo ndindirira Mukama.
