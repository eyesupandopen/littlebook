Mu Maaso g'Essaawa Mwererevu Kubanga Ennamba Ziguddeewo

Muli beetegefu ku lwange, Omugole wange? (Kubanga nzija) Muli beetegefu? Muwuliriza? Mutunula? Kubanga nzija ku lwammwe, n’olwekyo temuggwaamu maanyi era mube n’essuubi n’okunsuubira okungi, kubanga eby’obugagga byennyini eby’omu Ggulu birindiridde abeesigwa bange era empeera yammwe eri mu mukono gwange! Naye zisanze bonna abanzigaanyi mu butakkiriza obutali bwa bwesigwa, nze Yesu Omunazaaleeti! Era n’olwekyo olunaku lw’omusango gwammwe lutuuse ku ggwe, ggwe malaya omukulu atuula ku mazzi amangi, n’ekiseera kyo ekyalagirwa kituuse.

Okuzikirizibwa okw’amangu kukutuuseeko era abalabe bo beeteeseteese, naye okyali mulamu mu ssanyu newankubadde oli ggwanga eriri mu masamba mu maaso gange, Kristo Yesu. Ekiseera kyo eky’okumasasamala kyaliwo kubanga okumala akaseera Bakitaammwe baagoberera Omwana wa Katonda ne bakwata Ebigambo byange. Naye emyaka gy’Ekkanisa yange gikomekkerezeddwa kubanga mwatunda eby’obugagga by’ebyama byange, era n’emirembe n’okwagala kw’abantu bange mwabisuubula olw’essente ennyingi n’ettendo mu maaso g’abantu.

Mwetunda mu mululu n’obutali bwesigwa, era mwanzira nze, Yesu Kristo, eyafiirira ebibi by’ensi. Naye mwagaana ekigambo kyange, mwagaana okunenya kwange, mwagaana okuteesa kwange, mwagaana ababaka bange, mwagaana bannabbi bange, mwagaana abatume bange, era mwanzira nze, Yesu Omunazaaleeti, n’olwekyo mwagaana Yakuwa, era kaakano nange nja kubagaana, ggwe Babulooni ow’ekyama. Era n’olwekyo omusango gutandikira ku Nnyumba ya Yakuwa!

Kubanga bwe mwali musobola okufuna emirembe n’obutebenkevu, mmwe mwalondawo okugoberera mu buzibe bw’amaaso abo abaayonoona eggwanga lyammwe. N’ab’omu Edomu omusiri gw’omuyonga ogutta ensi, era abatayaganya ensi okuva omubi lwe yasaasaanya emizizo gye mu bitonde bya Yakuwa.

N’olwekyo, mumanye nti nze, nze Kristo Yesu, nja kumalawo ddala Babulooni w’Ekyama n’omuliro era mu ssaawa emu okuzikirizibwa kwo kujja kutuuka, kubanga kyawandiikibwa era ekyo Yakuwa ky’alangiridde tewali asobola kukikyusa. Ai mmwe abasala ebifaananyi ab’omu nsi ya Babulooni w’Ekyama, singa mwategeera embeera yammwe. Singa mwatega amatu gammwe eri ebigambo byange kubanga nkoowodde, neegayiridde, neesabye, neesengeredde, neeyogerera waggulu, ne ndaga obubonero waggulu n’obubonero mu nsi era mu kifo ky’okwambaza omutima gwammwe ebibukutu, mu kifo ky’okutuula mu vvu ery’okwenenya mmwe musanyusibwa ng’abalaba eby’obufumu.

Zisanze abo abasanyusibwa n’obubonero bwa Yakuwa mu bwengula obulangirira omusango gwabwe, era zikubiseemu emirundi ebiri, ggwe Babulooni w’Ekyama, kubanga olina omugabo gw’omusango omulundi ebiri ogugenda okuteebwa, era toliwanduka wadde okuddamu okutereera. Babulooni w’Ekyama, oli nsi y’okumatizibwa amangu, era toli wa magezi nnyo. Wasendasenda amawanga mu makubo go amabi n'okukkiriziganya, era wabatamisa n'obugwagwa bw'obwenzi bwo. Totegeera makubo gange, kubanga buli omu ku mmwe atambulira mu makubo ge ye kennyini nga bwe bakola mu ngeri ya sitaani kyonna kye baagala, kola ky’oyagala lye tteeka lyabwe lyonna erya sitaani era n’olwekyo abataliiko mateeka tebatuuka ku kintu kyonna.

Totegeera makubo gange, kubanga amakubo gange ge makubo ga Yakuwa, era nze kkubo n’amazima n’obulamu, kubanga tewali muntu ajja eri Kitange okuggyako ng’ayita mu nze era bw’oba ondabye oba olabye Kitange. Naye mwaggala ebirowoozo byammwe, mwaziba amatu gammwe, mwakakanyaza emitima gyammwe, era mwasumulula emimwa gyammwe mu butakkiriza obuvvoola, nga muvuma n’endagaano ey’emirembe Yakuwa gye yabawa. Kubanga nze Yesu Kristo, nze mirembe gyammwe!

Naye nedda, mu kifo ky'okunfuula Mukama wammwe, mukozesa erinnya lyange ettukuvu, nze Yesu Kristo, mu bukyamu ng’ekigambo ekirala eky’okulaga obugwagwa obuli mu mitima gyammwe. Naye temusobola kuvumaganya linnya lyange, kubanga Yakuwa yampa erinnya erisinga amannya gonna, nti mu linnya lya Yesu Kristo buli vviivi lyonna lifukamire mu ggulu ne ku nsi. Era mujja kusasulira buli kigambo ekitagasa, n’ebiseera byammwe bituuse. Era kaakano, okuva bwe mweggalidde mu mpuku zammwe nga mulowooza nti musobola okwekweka omusango gwa Yakuwa, kaakano nja kuggalawo oluggi oluyingira mu ggulu eri abatali beesigwa nga nkwakula abeesigwa bange ne mbatwala mu ggulu kubanga omugole asembera.

Mu busirusiru mulowooza nti byonna bizzizzaawo obulamu, era nti olutalo olugenda okujja luyinza okulabirwa ku TV ng’entalo endala abakulembeze bammwe ze baalwana mu butali bwa nsonga. Nga bwe mwasanyusibwa n’okufa n’okuzikirira kw’amawanga amalala, n’eggwanga lyammwe bwe lityo lijja kuzikirizibwa ddala, era okufa kwammwe n’okuzikirira kwammwe kujja kuwa empeera abo abaazikirizibwa n’okukkiriza kwammwe.

Mu busirusiru mulowooza nti musobola okwetegekera obusungu obugenda okujja ng’obeera ku ttaka, mu kifo ekiri wala n’abantu, wabula mujja kuzikirizibwa ddala, okukwatibwa, okuttibwa, okuyigganyizibwa, okufa enjala, okulwala, n’ebikwekweto bingi bijja kutuuka ku bonna abanzigaana nze Yeshua HaMashiach, kubanga okunzigaana kwe kwegaana Katonda omunene ow’emirembe n’emirembe, Yakuwa yennyini era tewaliba ddagala. N’olwekyo mudduke mujje gyendi kaakano!

Mu busirusiru temutegeera nti amayembe ekkumi gateesezza n’ensolo eva mu nsi n’ensolo eva mu nnyanja abatannatuuka ku maanyi gaabwe gonna, era nti eggwanga lyammwe eddene eryali eddene liweereddwayo ddala mu mikono gyabwe okuzikirizibwa ddala. (Era ng’eggwanga lyammwe bwe lirizikirizibwa ddala ensolo zirigolokoka!) Kubanga Katonda akiteese mu mitima gyabwe okutuukiriza ebigendererwa bye.

Mwetooloddwa ddala ebyombo by'abalabe bammwe, mukkirizza bukadde na bukadde b'Amagye Amamyufu, n'eryo eggwanga ly'ensolo okuyingirira ensi zammwe. Mwabakkirizza okukola ebikolwa eby'okwonoonayo, baayonoonye enkolagana zammwe ez'okubawa ebintu, baayonoonye emmere yammwe, baayonoonye eby'enfuna byammwe, baateekawo enkambi okutta, okubonyaabonya n'okutta omuntu yenna atagoberera bigendererwa byabwe. Tegeerera nti ne mu kiseera ekyo omuntu yenna akoowoola erinnya lyange, nze Yesu Kristo, okutuusa ku nkomerero alirokolebwa. Abo abalondawo okugaana okw’enkomerero bajja kubeera n’ebiva mu butakkiriza emirembe n’emirembe mu nnimi z’omuliro ezategekerwa omulyolyomi n’abaserikale be, kubanga tewali kulondawo kulala.

Nsaba bonna abasoma ebigambo bino okusemberera gyendi. Mulekere awo okuwakana okutaliiko nsa era mulekere awo okweyogereraganako mu mmwe mwekka naye mubuulire enjiri y’obulokozi nga bwe yawandiikibwa mu byawandiikibwa era mubuulire ekigambo kyange, n’ebigambo by’ebigambo byange mu ssekonda zino ezisembayo. Mbibuulire mu mmwe mwekka, era mube beetegefu bulijjo okuyamba abo abali mu bwetaavu. Mulekere awo okusalira baganda bammwe ne bannyina mmwe omusango naye muyambagane mu kwagalana okuyimirira mu kisa n'okusaasira bye nnawa, kubanga bimala.

Mwatule era mwenenye ebibi byammwe era mukikole kaakano, temulina biseera! Munfuule nze, Yesu Omunazaaleeti, Mukama wammwe, kubanga obudde buweddewo. Mujje gyendi kaakano, kubanga tewali mannya malala gaweebwa kulokolebwa, okuggyako eryange, Yesu Kristo. Mujje gyendi kaakano muve mu butali butuukirivu nange nja kubaaniriza bwe muba mwandyatula nti nze Mukama okuva mu mutima ogujjudde okukkiriza nga mukimanyi nti nzuukidde mu bafu okutafa nate. Ngolodde emikono gyange eri ensi efa okuva emyaka mingi n’ebiseera by’ekisa n’okusaasira biri kumpi okuggwaawo, newankubadde ekisa n’okusaasira biri nange bulijjo kubanga nze Mukama ow’ekisa.

Era kaakano, mulindirira mu ssekonda zino ezisembayo, ggwe Babulooni w’ekyama. Kubanga kijja kubaawo mangu ddala. Mangu ddala omuliro gujja kugwa, mangu ddala amazzi gajja kubikka ku ngoye zo ezisituliddwa waggulu w'ekiwato kyo, ggwe malaya omukulu, mangu ddala okukankana okw'amaanyi kujja kukujjira ne kukwawulamu, mangu ddala ekiragiro kijja kuweebwa era obukadde bujja kusensera mu nsi okutta buli muntu yenna atagoberera bigendererwa byabwe. Tebalina kusaasira, tebalina kwenenya, tebasobola kwegayirirwa, tebalina kisa, naye baweereza Sitaani era bajja kutuukiriza ebigendererwa ebyabaweebwa okutuukiriza, kubanga bajja kutuukiriza obusungu bwa Katonda ku Babulooni w’Ekyama era tewaliddamu kugolokoka nate.

Laba! Ebiseera bituuse. Mwenenye nga mukyayinza okussa omukka, kubanga kijja kubaawo mangu. Muwulire ebigambo byange mutegeere: omuziiyizi ye maanyi gokka agaziyiza obubi buno, n'ekiseera kye mulowooza nti mulina kye kiseera kyokka ekikkirizibwa okuteekawo n'okutambuza ebitundu mu kifo kyabyo ku lubaawo lw'ensi yonna era ebiseera bino biweereza ebigendererwa bya Yakuwa.

Oluvannyuma mangu ddala nja kukuŋŋaanya abeesigwa mu Kkanisa yange okusisinkana nange mu bire era nja kunyakula Omugole wange okumutwala mu bisenge bye, kubanga mu nnyumba ya Kitange mulimu amayumba mangi era nnagenda okubategekera ekifo mmwe Omugole wange era eggulu libategekedde!

Era amangu ago, eby’obugagga bya Kitange eby’omuwendo bwe biriba biterekeddwa obulungi mu tterekero ly’omu ggulu (kubanga kijja kubaawo mu kubonaabona kw’eriiso) buli yodi na buli ggwa lya yodi birituukirira, na buli kigambo kye njogedde nga nkiyita mu bannabbi bange ab’amazima n’abatume, kubanga bangi era abangi abali ku lwange boogera wamu era boogera okusinziira ku byawandiikibwa byange. Kino mukitegeere, abo abagaana Ebigambo ebyangu mu byawandiikibwa ebikwata ku kukuŋŋaanyizibwa kw’Ekkanisa yange okunsisinkana mu bire, abo bennyini abajeemera nabo bajja kugaana era batwale ekifo kyabwe kyeyagalire mu busungu so nga tekyali kya ngerekera, naye mmwe bameka abajja okugaana n’obukakanyavu eddoboozi lyange okusinga abo ababasiiwasiiwanya amatu? Naye, bwe kityo bwe kiriba.

Omuntu yenna bw’atoogera ng’ebigambo byange bwe biri, kubanga nze Kristo Yesu, nze Kigambo eyafuuka omubiri, temumwagaliza na mirembe kubanga tawawula bulungi Bigambo byange kubanga bannabbi bange tebayinza kwegattika na byawandiikibwa kubanga nze siyinza kwegattika nze nzennyini. Nze bigambo bya Yakuwa ebyafuuka omubiri, era nze Yeshua HaMashiach, omwana gw’endiga gwa Katonda yennyini eyajja okulongoosa ebibi by’abantu.

Nzija ng'Empologoma ey'omu kika kya Yuda era obusungu bwange bubuguma ku nsi, kubanga mu butuukirivu nze nsala omusango era ndwana. Mwenenye mmwe ababi ab’oku nsi kubanga okugaana kwammwe kujja kuba kwa lubeerera. Ku nkomerero y'emyaka omusanvu egy'okubonaabona kwa Yakobo Ensi ejja kuba mu mirembe kubanga walibaawo olunaku olw'okuwummula kubanga emyaka lukumi luli ng'olunaku lumu mu maaso ga Yakuwa. Era Empologoma ejja kugalamira wamu n’Omwana gw’endiga kubanga ebigendererwa byange eby’Omwana gw’endiga wa Katonda bijja kutuukirizibwa n’Empologoma ey’omu kika kya Yuda. Obudde buweddewo.

Nze Yeshua HaMashiach ntumye ebigambo bino okuyita mu muweereza wange, era okuyita mu muweereza wange ebigambo bino bitumiddwa!
