Ekiro Kiri Kumpi

Ekigambo kya Mukama Katonda kyajjira ku nze.

Ekiro kituuka omuntu yenna lw'atasobola kukola. Obunnabbi bulikoma, ennimi ziriggyibwawo, ebirabo byonna ebiri mu Kkanisa biriggyibwawo ng'Omugole wange avutuliddwa. Olwo luliba lunaku lwa kizikiza, era wajja kubaawo enjala mu nsi, enjala y'Ekigambo kyange. Naye ne mu biro ebyo bamalayika bange n'abannabbi b'abalonde bange bajja kwogera mu linnya lyange. Obudde busembera ebisolo by'omu nsiko lwe birirumba era bangi balizikirira. Olunaku lwa Mukama lwalangirirwa mu Kigambo kyange Ekitukuvu, era mu bbanga ttono lujja kutuukirira. Omusango gwange gujja kwabika mangu ddala; kyemunaava mwenenya ebibi byammwe gye ndi, nze Mukama wammwe Yesu Kristo, kubanga nnasasulira ebibi byammwe, era muyinza okweruka okutukula ng'omuzira mu bafuta ne mu langi ennungi singa mwandyegayiridde ebyambalo byammwe. Mukyukire gyendi nga mwenenyezza. Mwetoowaze mu maaso ga Mukama ng'obudde bukyali bwa misana, kubanga ekiro kigenda kugwa mangu ddala omuntu yenna n'atasobola kukola. Naye ne mu biro ebyo, buli muntu alikoowoola erinnya lya Mukama alirokolebwa, naye nga ayise mu muliro.
