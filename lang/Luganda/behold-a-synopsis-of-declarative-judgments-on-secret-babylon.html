Emisango Egirangiriddwa
Jjuuni 16, 2025

Laba! Olukalala lw'Emisango Egirangiriddwa ku Babulooni ow'Ekyama nga Omusango Omujjuvu tegunnagwa

Emisango egiddirira giva mu Kitabo Ekitono eky'Okubikkulirwa Essuula 10, era ye Njiri ey'Emirembe n'Emirembe. Omusango ku Babulooni ow'Ekyama gumaze okulagirwa okumala amayubiri n'emyaka mingi. Egiddirira y'Emisango egirangiriddwa egiggyiddwa mu Kitabo Ekitono era egijja okutuukirira ddala mu bbanga ttono era mu ngeri etasuubirwa. Mu ngeri eno, nkola ng'omubaka w'obulamuzi atumiddwa okulangirira emisango okuva ku ntebe ya Yakuwa, egigabiddwa mu maaso ga Yesu Kristo, gwe nsinza.

Kimanyibwe nti leero, Jjuuni 16, 2025 ku Kalenda ya Gulegori (kalenda y'emu Secret Babylon gy'akozesa) nti Mukama wange eyasitula Kristo Yesu, ye Yeshua HaMashiach, ankirizza okusoma emisango gino ku Babulooni ow'Ekyama, ye Amagwanga ga Amerika Agatagata, kisobozese omuntu yenna okwenenya nga obudde obutono obusigaddeyo tebunnaggwaawo, era n'omusango gulangirirwe nga omuliro tegunnaba kugwa. Seera!

Okulangirira kuno si kwe kukoma kubanga gino si misango mipya, wabula gyateebwa mu bujjuvu okuva nga Maaki 2, 2024, okutuuka nga Maaki 20, 2025. Eisinga obungi ku gino giva eri Yeshua HaMashiach, nga kuliko ekitundu ekimu ekiva eri Yakuwa, NZE NDI ASINGA OBUNENE. Kaakati nja kutandika okulangirira.

1Laba! Omusango Gujja n'Ebiseera Bituuse!Jjuuni 4, 2024 (Eddoboozi: Yeshua HaMashiach)

Ng'okukuŋŋaana kubaawo, omuzibi aggyibwawo, n'okuzikirira okunene kuligwira ensi, amangu ago era mu kaseera kamu. Kubanga omuzibi bw'aggyibwawo, tewaliba kisigalawo kuziyiza bubi mu nsi n'okwegomba n'okwagala okubi okw'abantu. Entalo ennene zijja kubalukawo okwetooloola ensi, era muganda w'omuntu ajja kuddamu okugolokokera okutta muganda we, n'okuzikirira kwabwe kujja kuba kunene nnyo. Amawanga mangi galigwa nga Babulooni ow'Olubye, ye malayika omukazi asitudde ku mazzi amangi, ajja kufuulibwa matongo mu ssaawa emu. Ekibuga ekyo ekinene, ye ggwanga eryo ery'Amagwanga ga Amerika Agatagata, lijja kuzikirizibwa ddala; kubanga yafuna omugabo gw'omusango ogw'emirundi ebiri, era asaanidde.

Kubanga yabeera mu maanyi mu kibi ekinene; atuula mu malala amangi ng'alowooza nti tayinza kugwa era nti ebintu bijja kweyongera, eggulo nga leero, n'enkya nga bwe kirikiri era nga tebikyuka. Era malayika omukazi omukulu ayonoonye ensi n'ekikopo kya zzaabu ekiri mu mukono gwe, ekijjudde eby'emizizo n'obugwagwa bw'obwenzi bwe. N'olwekyo omusango gwe gujja kugwa mangu ago. Omuliro omunene gujja kwokya ebibuga bye ng'emizigo gigwa; okukankana okunene kujja kubaawo, n'eggwanga eryo lijja kwabizibwamu wakati. Ennyanja zaalyo zijja kujjula amazzi, era terijja kuddamu kugolokokera, kubanga ebibi byalyo binene n'omusango gwalyo munene.

2Muwulire Ebigambo Byange era Mutegeere Ebiseera mwe Muli!Meeyi 23, 2024 (Eddoboozi: Yeshua HaMashiach)

Muwulire ebigambo byange, abantu bange, mmwe mwenna be nkoowoola, era mutegeere ebiseera mwe muli. Kubanga ensi gye mumanyi kaakati ejja kuggwaawo, ne mu kifo kyayo walisigala amatongo mangi, ye vvu n'ebifunfugu bya Babulooni ow'Olubye, kubanga olutalo luzze. Kubanga bwe nnaanyaga Omugole wange okumutwala mu ggulu, akabi akanene kajja kubikka Babulooni ow'Olubye, n'azikirizibwa mu ssaawa emu, era mu ssaawa emu ajja kuzikirizibwa, so tewali kiyinza kukyusa mpewo ye, kubanga Yakuwa bw'atyo bwe yalangirira. Ggwe, Babulooni ow'Ekyama, wagaana eddoboozi lyange n'onteeka nze, Yesu Kristo Omutuukirivu, ku bbali olw'essanyu ly'ekibi. Kubanga n'okulabula okw'amaanyi okwaweebwa ku ggwanga lyo mu ggulu kwakola ng'eky'okusanyusa emyoyo gyammwe egitali mikkiriza n'amaaso gammwe ageegomba, era gye muli lwali lunaku lwa bulijjo bwe mwabeeranga mu maanyi mu bibi byammwe ebibatwala mu kuzikirira kwammwe. Mwandibadde mwenenya! Kubanga mufuuse eggwanga ly'abasinza ebifaananyi, eggwanga ly'abalwanyisa, eggwanga erirya obulimba era eriyagala okulimbibwalimbibwa kwalyo, eggwanga erijjuzza ensi eby'emizizo byalyo; n'amawanga ganywa ku kikopo kyalyo ekya zzaabu ekijjudde eby'emizizo n'obugwagwa bw'obwenzi bwalyo.

N'olwekyo okwo okukoma kujja kutuukirira ddala bwe nnaakuŋŋaanya abeesigwa b'Ekkanisa yange gyendi mu bire era okugenda kwabwe bwe kulikankanya ensi yonna. Kujja kukankanya ensi mu bujjuvu, n'okukankana okunene kujja kubikka Babulooni ow'Ekyama, n'ayanjulwamu wakati ng'abatukuvu bange bagenda. Ajja kwokebwa ddala n'omuliro okuva mu ggulu ng'emizigo gigwa. Engoye ze zijja kubikkibwako amazzi okuva ebuvanjuba n'ebugwanjuba! Kubanga malayika ow'amaanyi ajja kusitula ejjinja ng'olubengo olunene, n'alisuula mu nnyanja, ng'ayogera nti, N'amaanyi mangi bwe kityo ekibuga ekyo ekinene Babulooni bwe kirisuulibwa wansi, so tekirirabika nate. Kubanga bwe kityo bwe kyawandiikibwa era bwe kityo bwe kirikolebwa! Kubanga omusango gwe munene, n'omusango gutandikidde ku nnyumba ya Katonda. Ekkanisa ze ez'obujeemu zijja kuzikirizibwa, kubanga nzigisalidde zonna ezitali zange, era ekkanisa zonna enkola-ya-ngabirano ezogera obulimba obunene mu bunnanfuusi. Babulooni ow'Olubye, singa wankyukira nze, Yesu Kristo, okuva mu makubo go amabi, nandikulokodde, era nze ne Yakuwa tetulina ssanyu lyonna mu misango eginaatera okuteebwa.

Ggwe Babulooni ow'Ekyama, engege zo zijja kugwa; enguudo zo zijja kusendebwasendebwa, okumenyekamenyeka, n'obutayitikamu; enkolagana z'obugabuzi bwo zisaasaanikira dda era zijja kuba matongo mangu. Tewaliba mpeke zijja kukutuusibwako, kubanga tewaliba ajja kukuyamba, era ojja kuba wekka mu ssaawa yo ey'okuzikirira, ggwe malayika omukazi omukulu. Toliba na ssanyu, naabo abalisigalawo balisanga nga balwanira okufuna amazzi ag'okunywa. Enjala ejja kubikka ensi. N'akawumpuli akanene kajja kukukwata, n'ensolo zijja kulumba, kubanga ebiseera bituuse.

Amagye amamyuufu gavumenti yo enjeemu ge yakkiriza okuyingira mu nsalo zo gajja kutta buli musajja, mukazi, na mwana atagyamu makulu gy'egali. Kubanga gajja kutta n'okufuula abaddu bonna ge balisanga. Ye magye g'ensolo eyakung'aanyizibwa okuva ku nsonda ennya ez'ensi olw'ekigendererwa kino, n'ensolo ekwekeddwa n'omulanzi w'obulimba akwekeddwa babikung'aanyizza.

Tebalina kisa, tebalina kusaasira, tebalina ekisa, tebalina kwenenya, era bakyawa ddala abatuuze ba Babulooni ow'Olubye olw'ebibi eby'entiisa abakulembeze bo bye baakola ku nsi zaabwe. Naye bangi ku mmwe mwajagulizanga entalo zammwe ezitali za ntuukirivu nga mulaba mu kusanyuka ku ttivi zammwe, nga musanyukira ebibi by'abakulembeze bammwe. Mwaganyizza abakulembeze bammwe be mwakakasa ebibi byabwe mu kulonda, n'olwekyo mujja kugabana nabo mu musango gwabwe, kubanga mwefuulidde abasaanidde omugabo gw'omusango ogw'emirundi ebiri, era gujja kubatuukako amangu ago. N'olwekyo Omugole wange ajja kulabika ku ntebe yange ey'omusango, abamu balifuna okufiirwa, abamu balitwala empeera ennene, era bonna balifuna mu bujjuvu ekyo kye baasooka okufuna: Kubanga nja kubawa emibiri emipya, egitaliiko kibi egitayinza kuvunda!

3Eri Ekkanisa Yange, Omubiri Gwange, Ye Mugole Wange: Muwulire Ebigambo bya Mukama Wammwe!Meeyi 13, 2024 (Eddoboozi: Yeshua HaMashiach)

Balijja ku siteegi y'ensi n'amaanyi amangi, n'omukago gw'abakabaka ekkumi gulifuga. Ne kaakati, omukago gumaze dda okuteekateeka amagye gaagwo mu kyama okulumba Babulooni ow'Ekyama mu kyama. Beeteeseteese mu kyama, era ddala bateeseteese okulumba, era balumbye Babulooni ow'Ekyama obulungi okusinziira ku musango ogwalangirirwa Katonda ogugenda okumugwako, so nga tajja kuddamu kugolokokera. Kubanga obutali butuukirivu bwe butuuse mu maaso g'Oyo Asinga Obukulu, n'ebibi bye mu maaso g'entebe ennene ey'eggulu, era mu maaso ga Katonda omunene ow'emirembe n'emirembe, kubanga tewali amusinga; n'omusango gumaze okumusalirwa.

Ndeeta obusungu obunene ku Babulooni ow'Ekyama. Obudde bwe bumu ku bintu bye mutakyalina. Temusobola kuba bagayaavu nate, kubanga mulisanga muli mu kutegana kwa Yakobo, lwe nandibadde mbateereddewo ekifo ku mmeeza yange ne mbawasa ng'omugole wange, naye muli mu mikono gy'omulala mu buteesigwa, ye Setaani! Kale mujje gyendi nsobole okubaddiza empeera yammwe. Tekimala kufuluma mu bunyiivu; mulina okwenenya obubi buno ne mukyusa amakubo gammwe ne mwatula ebibi bino gyendi, nange nja kubanaaza mu musaayi gwange ogutangirira ne mbakuhhaanyako amabala mu butukuvu bwange obutuukiridde, kubanga ndi muyonjo n'engoye zange njeru ng'omuzira omupya. Mudde gyendi era mujja kuba nange emirembe gyonna; mufulume mujje mu mikono gyange egigoloddwa, kubanga ndi wano okubagamba nti temulina budde bwa kunoonya.

Ndeeta obusungu obunene ku Babulooni ow'Ekyama. Obudde bwe bumu ku bintu bye mutakyalina. Temusobola kuba bagayaavu nate, kubanga mulisanga muli mu kutegana kwa Yakobo, lwe nandibadde mbateereddewo ekifo ku mmeeza yange ne mbawasa ng'omugole wange, naye muli mu mikono gy'omulala mu buteesigwa, ye Setaani! 5Abatali Batuukirivu Tebaligabana Bwakabaka bwa Katonda! (Okubwatuka 5 ne 6)Apuli 19, 2024 (Eddoboozi: Yeshua HaMashiach, Obubaka obw'emirundi ebiri mu Meseji)

Leka nzigule amaaso gammwe, mmwe abatamanyi era abazibe b'amaaso ku bugenderevu, mmwe abatuula mu kazambi n'ekizikiza, nga mwekulukuunya mu ttosi. Temusobola kuwa kukkiriza kwammwe eri obubi ne mulowooza nti munsinza, kubanga naziyiza obubi n'okukemebwa okutuusa ku ttondo ly'omusaayi erisembayo lye lyakulukuta okuva mu nze okugwa ku ttaka eddungi, kubanga nze Adamu ow'oluvannyuma era nfuuliddwa omwoyo oguzza obulamu. Bangi ku mmwe muzibiddwa amaaso n'ebigambo ebirungi n'emboozi ennungi ne muwa okukkiriza kwammwe okujjuvu eri abamenyi b'amateeka n'abantu ababi abatigomya abaana. Batulugunya abaana, babonyaabonya abaana olw'okwegomba kwabwe okubi: Kubanga bwe gutyo obusungu bwa Katonda bwe bujja ku Babulooni ow'Olubye, naye bangi ku mmwe mukyagenda mu maaso n'okuwa okukkiriza kwammwe eri ensolo zino ez'effujjo eziteekeddwawo olw'omuliro gw'omuziro ogutayinza kuzikizibwa. Mufulume mu ye, abantu bange, kubanga muligabana naye mu bibonyoobonyo bye n'omusango ogw'entiisa ogugenda okumugwako; era mbalabudde. Kubanga Kristo alina mukwano ki ne Beriali? Bwe mugabana mu bulimba bwe, mujja kugabana mu bibonyoobonyo bye; kale mweyawule ddala mu kigambo ne mu kikolwa, so temukkiriziganya nabo n'akatono. Temubeera mu bulamu obutaliiko nsonga, obuli mu kukkiriziganya; kubanga nja kubonereza bonna abakola bwe batyo. Mulowooza nti kubanga mulina obulamu obutaggwaawo temusobola kufuna kufiirwa kunene n'ebivaamu? Nja kubaggyako empeera zammwe ne nzigabira abalala, era mujja kuba abasembayo wansi mu bwakabaka bw'eggulu bwe mugenda mu maaso, kubanga bw'atyo bw'ayogera Yeshua HaMashiach, Omuwasi, eri Omugole wange!

6Omukuumi, Zuukuka!Nga 5 Ogwokuna, 2024 (Eddoboozi: Yeshua HaMashiach)

Nzija, era sijja kusirika, n'obusungu bwange bubuguumiridde ku Babulooni ow'Ekyama, weewaawo, Babulooni ow'Ekyama asuubula abaana abato okubafuula eby'amaguzi mu mikono gy'abasajja ababi, abagenda okukomezebwa. Kyandibadde kirungi eri bo singa tebaazaalibwa okusinga okuzaalibwa ne bakola ebibi ebitali mu mateeka era eby'entiisa ku baana. Kyandibadde kirungi olubengo lwe lw'ensigo lwe lwasibibwa ku bulago bwabwe, ne basuulibwa mu nnyanja; naye baterekeddwa muliro ogutazikira, n'obwenkanya bunaatera okukolebwa, kubanga obusungu bw'Omwana gw'endiga bunaatera okubuna ensi yonna n'akabi ka Yakobo ne katandika ng'omuziyiza amaze okuggyibwawo. Naye ggwe, Ayi Babulooni ow'Ekyama omukulu, oyonoonyeyoonyoonyedde omusoke gwa Kitange ow'amabala amangi gwe yawa Nuuwa n'abantu bonna ng'ekiragaano nti yali tajja kuddamu kuzikiriza nsi yonna na mazzi, era ajja kukwata ekigambo kye. Naye ggwe, Ayi Babulooni ow'Ekyama, ojja kuzikirizibwa mu ssaawa emu.

Omuliro omunene gujja kukujjira; okukankana okunene kujja kukusaliza; ebigo byo bijja kugwa; ebizimbe byo bijja kubula mu matongo, n'amayengo amanene gajja kubikka ennyanja zo, era nja kukusasula okusinziira ku bikolwa byo, n'omusango omunene gukuliiko. Kubanga waliwo lwe wali olutadocero olw'essuubi n'omusana eri ensi okumala akaseera katono, naye olonze omululu, okwekkaanya, n'obuli bw'enguzi okuba eby'okwebaka nabyo, Ayi malaaya omukulu! Ani ajja kukulokola ku nze n'obusungu bwange n'okujja kw'obusungu bwange obukuli okumpi? Oyimiridde mu njegere, ng'olindirira omusango gwo ogw'enkomerero, ogujja okulabikira ensi yonna, n'okutya okungi kujja kujja ku bangi.

Laba! Amawanga mangi Kitange ge yasuula mu musango, kubanga ateekawo limu n'aggyawo eddala, kubanga YAHWEH ye afuga byonna era tewali asinga Katonda omukulu ow'emirembe n'emirembe ayimirira era alokola ababe okuva mu muliro ogwaterekerwa ababi. Laba! Amawanga agaakola obwakabaka obunene gakyaliwo, naye mu ngeri etali ya maanyi, naye geeyongeddeyo. Naye ggwe, Ayi malaaya omukulu, weeyonoonye n'abagalwa bangi olw'omululu omubi n'essanyu ly'ekibi. Osiigudde abantu bange okuva ku nze mu nsi yonna olw'obugwagwa bwe waweerezza amawanga okulya n'okunywa, era ojja kusuulibwa ddala wansi, era toliddamu kubaawo. Era okuva mu vvu lyo tewajja kugolokokamu ggwanga ddene, naye ensigalira ejja kulokolebwa. Omukono gw'omulabe guli ku bulago bwammwe, era ndeese abasezi mu nsalo zammwe. Bajja kunyaga, okunyaga, n'okutwala ebisigaddewo.

Ani ajja kukuyamba? Kubanga weegaanyi muliraanwa wo, era tewali kutya Mukama mu mitima gyammwe. Ogambye nti, Nze siri nnamwandu, naye oli nnamwandu era muzibe w'amaaso era kiggala mu maaso gange. Tewali bulokozi gy'oli olw'obwenzi bwo bwe wakola n'ensi; kyenva nkuleetera okuzikirira okw'olubeerera. Ebigenda okubaawo bijja kwanguwa nnyo era nnyo; olwo amazzi amangi gajja kubikka engoye zo. Omuliro gujja kukwokya ddala era gujja kulongoosa abakola ebibi okuva mu nsi, n'abasibe bange bajja kuteebwa, era tewali kiyinza kuziyiza ekigenda okubaawo. Nze Mukama Yezu Kristu, era nkubuulira nga tekinnabaawo, era ndangirira ekigenda okubaawo ng'ekimaze okubaawo, kubanga ebigambo bino bya mazima, era tewali kiyinza kubikyusa, kubanga obunnabbi bujja kutuukirira. Munywerere, bantu bange, kubanga nja kutwala omugole wange; kubanga nnina enteekateeka n'ebigendererwa ku ye mu bwakabaka bwange obujja.

7Saakutuukiriza?Nga 1 Ogwokuna, 2024 (Eddoboozi: Yeshua HaMashiach)

Abo abatagattulukukako bajja kugabana naye mu musango gwe, kati lwaki okyalonda okufa okusinga obulamu bwe nkuwadde, obwasasulirwa mu musaayi gwange ogutalina kibi? Olutalo lunaatera okujja mu nsi yo ne mu ggwanga lyo, era newankubadde nga lutambula dda — weewaawo, olutalo olutalangirirwa lwe balwana naawe — mangu ddala lujja kusasaanira ensi yonna ng'obubonero buteebwa. Olutalo olunene n'okuyigganyizibwa okunene bijja kujja ku nsi nga bagezaako okuzikiriza ekintu kyonna ekya Katonda ne Kristu we nga bajja kulangirira olutalo olutukuvu ku mmwe. Bajja kukwata bangi, era bangi bajja kutwalibwa, n'abo abajja okulokolebwa bajja kuba n'eky'okulonda kimu kyokka: okugumiikiriza okutuusa ku nkomerero n'obutayagala bulamu bwabwe okutuusa ku nkomerero n'okukoowoola erinnya lyange okutuusa ku nkomerero.

Abakulembeze bammwe babavuddeko era bajja kuwaayo bangi eri omubi mu ntalo ezinaatera okubalukawo. Eggwanga lyammwe lijja kulumbibwa okuva munda n'ebweru. Amagye amamyuufu gali wano dda, era galindirira ebiragiro byago; kubanga gategeka okulumba okw'amangu, era bangi bajja kutwalibwa. Amagye g'omubi gajja kutwala buli kintu okuva mu nsi ku lwabwe n'okuva ku abo abanjegaana. Bajja kubba ettaka lyammwe, amayumba gammwe, n'obuzzukulu bwammwe; n'obulamu bungi bujja kusaanirawo mu butakkiriza. Olutalo lujja kuva bweru kubanga amawanga mangi geegasse okukulwanyisa; era balina olukusa lw'abakulembeze bammwe, n'abakulembeze bammwe bakolera kufa kwammwe. Baagala ensi yaabwe, ensi eweereddwayo eri Setaani mwe bayinza okugoberera mu bujjuvu ebibi ebibatuulamu, era tewali kijja kuziyiza okwegomba kwabwe okubi, kubanga omuziyiza ajja kuba amaze okugenda.

Bajja kwokya Babulooni ow'Ekyama n'omuliro, era yenna atagenda kufa na nnimi z'omuliro ajja kufa na njala; yenna atagenda kufa na njala ajja kufa na kawumpuli; yenna atagenda kufa na kitala ajja kuweebwayo ku kalimankonde olw'erinnya lyange, era bangi bajja kutemebwako emitwe ng'Ekigambo kyange Ekitukuvu bwe kirangirira. N'abo abafiirira erinnya lyange bajja kufuna empeera nene, era nja kusangula amaziga gonna mu maaso gaabwe. Mmanye, baana bange, nti eby'okulya ebirungi byonna n'amasanyu g'ekibi abasajja ababi bye beekuumira bijja kubula mu bbanga, n'okubonaabona n'okulumwa bijja kuyimirira mu bifo byabyo. Ekizikiza kijja kubikka ensi, era wajja kubaawo enjala y'Ekigambo kyange, n'eddoboozi ly'omugole n'eddoboozi ly'omugole omusajja tebiriddamu kuwulirwa mu ye.

8Gaana Akabonero k'Ensolo!Nga 27 Ogwomusanvu, 2024 (Eddoboozi: Yeshua HaMashiach)

Muwulire eddoboozi ly'amagezi muvunnamire amaviivi muwulire eddoboozi eddamu erya Yeshua HaMashiach, kubanga nze Mpologoma y'Ekika kya Yuda, era nze Kabaka w'Abakabaka era Mukama w'Abaami! El Shaddai, ye Katonda Omuyinza w'ebintu byonna, ankuulumizizza nnyo n'ampa erinnya erisinga amannya gonna.
