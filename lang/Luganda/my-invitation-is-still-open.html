Obujulirwa Obubiri: Ekkanisa
Mu Bikoleddwa bya Yokaana Omutume
Maaki 07, 2024

Okuyitibwa kwange kukyaguddewo

Ekigambo kya Mukama kyajjira ku nze, era nkibaleetera nga ssigasseeko wadde okuggyawo kyonna.

Laba! Okuyitibwa kwange kukyaguddewo! Mujje mwenna, era ajja yenna asobola okujja, kubanga nze Mukama wammwe Yesu Kristo ntegekeddwa okuba olwazi lwammwe olw’okwekuumirako era ndi kigo kyamwe kya maanyi. Tewali linnya ddala eriri wansi w’eggulu eryaweebwa abantu mwe bayinza okulokokerera, okuggyako erinnya lyange, Yesu Kristo. Nze jjinja essuulirwemu ery’oku nsonda eryo lye bagaana, nga balowooza nti bali ba ddembe. Eddembe terisangibwa walala wonna okuggyako mu nze, Kristo Yesu. Lwaki muleka abakozi ab’empeera okubabuza? Lwaki muleka abataliiko kye bagasa okubonoonako obwetowaze obusangibwa mu nze? Amakubo mangi gatuusa ku kuzikirira. Naye nze kkubo, n’amazima, n’obulamu, ekkubo ettuufu erituusa eri Kitange erireeta obulokozi n’eddembe: obulokozi kubanga mbagabira ekisonyiwo ky’ebibi okuyita mu musaayi gwange ogununula, n’eddembe kubanga omwana ajja kubafuula ba ddembe ddala.

Lwaki mukooye, abantu bange? Lwaki muzirika? Ssaabawummuza? Ssaawangula byonna, ne mbakolera ekkubo mulyoke mugoberere obuwanguzi bwange era mugabane ku kubonaabona kwange? Okuyita mu kukkiriza okwewaayo kwange kwe nneewaayo kyokka mwe muyinza okununulibwa okuva mu kuzikirira okw’amazima. Okuyita mu musaayi gwange gwokka mwe mufunira ekisonyiwo ky’ebibi, era okuyita mu mubiri gwange ogwamenyebwa mwe muweebwa okuwonyezebwa, era nja kubawa omubiri omuggya ku lunaku olwo, ee, eri abeesigwa bange n’abalonde. Bw’atyo Mukama wammwe bwe yabateekerateekera. Nakola ekkubo awataali kkubo: ekkubo mu ddungu erikomekkerezebwa n’okugulumizibwa kwammwe, bwe munaalekanga okwonoona.

Ensi eno yonoonese, okumanya kwayo kwonoonese, sayansi waayo wa bwereere, era olutimba lw’obulimba lutegera abantu bange, ne lubaggya mu kkubo lye nnabalondera. Temutegeera nti nabayita? Nti nabalonda? Temutegeera nti muteekwa okufiira obulamu bwammwe obwa bulijjo mulyoke mufune obulamu mu nze, kubanga obulamu bwammwe obw’amazima bukwekeddwa mu nze, Mukama omwesigwa? Bwe ndirabika, nammwe bwe mutyo bwe mulirabika mu kitiibwa; kyemunaava mungoberera n’omutima ogumu. N’ebirowoozo ebitabuddemu, mujje gye ndi mu kukkiriza okw’obuwombeefu. Kyetaagisa kukkiriza kyenkanankana n’akaweke ka kaladaali, era omuti gujja kukula okusinga gyonna.

Ebiseera by’Abamawanga binaatera okuggwaako, naye Yakobo ajja kulokolebwa nga n’okuyita mu muliro. Mu muliro ogwokya ennyo ensi eno mw’eneesalirwa omusango. Mazima, omulangira w’ensi eno asalidwa omusango era mulabe awanguddwa. Omusango gumusaliddwa, obwakabaka bwe, abagoberezi be, n’ensi gye yagezaako okunyaga Kitange. Mazima, ayagala okubabbako obusika bwammwe, kubanga nabateerawo ekkubo mulyoke mugabane ku kitiibwa ky’entebe yange ey’obwakabaka nga nze bwe ngabana ku kitiibwa ky’entebe ya Kitange ey’obwakabaka. Nagenda okubateekerateekera ekifo. Temutegeera nti waliwo amayumba mangi mu nnyumba ya Kitange? Waliwo ekigambo kyonna ekizibu gye ndi? Ebisuubizo byange byabalema? Oba mwabuzizibwa okuva ku kigendererwa eky’omutima ogumu kye nabawa? Lwaki mukyalonda amakubo gammwe mwekka?

Mwenenye, mmwe abaana bange! Mwatuukirire ebibi byammwe gye ndi era n’ebibi bya bajjajjammwe, kubanga nja kubawulira mu kiseera ekirongoose. Nja kubagulumiza; nja kubawa obujja bw’obulamu mu nze. Mujje gye ndi mufune ebyambalo ebya bafuta ennungi! Mujje gye ndi mufune erinnya eriggya lye nja okubawa ku jjinja lye nnabakolera. Tewaba muntu ababoola okuva mu mpeera yammwe. Okuzikirira okungi n’akabi kali ku mulyango gwammwe. Ensi eneetera okukyuka okuyingira mu bubi obutaliiko nkomo. Olukonko lugenda kuggulwawo, n’obwakabaka bwa Setaani obujjuvu bujja kusumululwa. Nja kukankanya eggulu, nja kukankanya ennyanja, okukankana okunene kujja kujja ku nsi. Omwana w’okuzikirira atambulira mu mmwe ng’omusege mu ddiba ly’endiga, ng’alindirira obwakabaka bwe obwa setaani okujja mu lujjudde alyoke abikkulwe mu kiseera kye. Temumugoberera!

Munddukireko, abaana bange, kubanga obusungu obunene n’okuzikirira okunene bijja kujjira ku ggwanga lyammwe, era amawanga mangi gajja kukankanizibwa wansi mangu ddala. Lwandibadde lulungi singa mwankyukirira ne mutegeera ebiseera byammwe. Mu kuzikirira okugenda okujja omuwazi w’ebifaananyi mw’ajja okugolokokera, kubanga asendasenda abangi n’olulimi olulimba era ajja kulaga eby’amagero eby’obulimba ebingi n’obubonero obulimba. Ne kaakano amagye ge gayingidde mu bibuga byammwe ne gajjuza enguudo zammwe. Temubatya. Abamu ku bo bajja kufuna obulokozi. Ebisusunku bijja kuweebwayo mu muliro. Ekizibu ekinene kijja kujja ku nsi eno amangu ago. Amangu ago akabi kaabwe kajja kubagwako, so tebaliwona. Ye ssaawa esembayo. Temuba bagabana ku bibonyoobonyo bye; mufulume mu ye, abantu bange, kubanga omwenzi omukazi omukulu asalidwa omusango, n’akabi ke kajja kumujjira mu ssaawa emu. Abasuubuzi abakulu abaasuubulanga mu bulogo bwe bajja kugwa mu nnaku nnyingi, n’omukka gw’okwokya kwe gujja kuba gwa kulabibwa bonna. Abasuubuzi b’ensi bajja kukaabirako akabi ke nga bali wala.

Naye mbategekedde entegeka, abantu bange. Obulamu bwammwe bukwekeddwa mu nze. Wadde nga mbagezesezza okumala ekiseera, mbalongoosezza nga zzaabu mu muliro gw’omulongoosa. Mufulume mu ye! Muzuukuke okutuukirira obutuukirivu so temwonoona. Mwetukuze mu musaayi gwange ogutangirira. Mukaabe era muwowoggane olw’okwonoona kwammwe; mukyuke okuva mu bibi byammwe n’omutima gwammwe gwonna. Nze nno ngezesa emitima gy’abantu; tewali kintu kyonna kye muyinza kunkweka. Mujje gye ndi awatali bulimba. Mujje gye ndi mu bw’esimbu n’olwatu, era nja kulya nammwe era nja kubateera ekifo ku mmeeza yange. Mangu ddala Omugole ajja kukung’aanyizibwa, amangu okusinga bwe muyinza okulowooza.

Omugole wange bw’alinyukulibwa mu bbanga, omuzibi ajja kusumululwa, so tewaliba kintu kyonna ekibi ekiriziyizibwa ku muntu owa bulijjo. Era bajja kugoberera mu lwatu okwegomba n’ebibi ebiri mu mitima gyabwe; kyekiriva kibaleetera okusalirwa omusango omunene, era teguliggibwawo. Munkyukirire, Yesu Kristo! Mukaabirire erinnya lyange nga nkyasobola okusangibwa! Ne mu kiseera ky’olunaku olukulu olw’okuzikirizibwa, erinnya lyange lijja kuleetera obulokozi abo abalikaabirira. Mujje gye ndi kaakano era mufulume mu ye, kubanga obudde bunaatera okuggwaawo. Amagye g’omu ggulu geeteeseteese, galindiridde ekkondeere.

Nze jjinja essuulirwemu ery’oku nsonda eryo lye bagaana, nga balowooza nti bali ba ddembe.

Nze kkubo, n’amazima, n’obulamu: ekkubo ettuufu erituusa eri Kitange erireeta obulokozi n’eddembe.

Nze nno ngezesa emitima gy’abantu; tewali kintu kyonna kye muyinza kunkweka.

Ebikolwa 2:16-21

Naye kino kye kyayogerwa nnabbi Yoweeri, nti,

Era olulituuka ku nnaku ez’oluvannyuma, Katonda bw’agamba nti, Ndiffuka ku Mwoyo gwange ku bonna abalina omubiri: ne batabani bammwe ne bawala bammwe baliragula, n’abalenzi bammwe baliraba okwolesebwa, n’abakadde bammwe baliroota ebirooto:

Era n’oku baddu bange n’oku bazaana bange ndifukira ku Mwoyo gwange ku nnaku ezo; era baliragula. Era ndiraga eby’amagero waggulu mu ggulu, n’obubonero wansi ku nsi; omusaayi, n’omuliro, n’olufu lw’omukka: Enjuba erifuuka ekizikiza, n’omwezi gufuuke omusaayi, olunaku olukulu olw’ekitiibwa olwa Mukama nga terunnajja. Era olulituuka buli alikaabirira erinnya lya Mukama alirokoka.
