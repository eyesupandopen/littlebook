Abajulirwa Ababiri: Isirayiri
Okukaawa Okw’obuwomi
Julaayi 05, 2024

Lwaki Mweraliikirira?

Mujje, muvuunamise emitima gyammwe n'amatu gammwe muwulire ebigambo bya Mukama wammwe, Ekkanisa yange era n'Omugole wange. Lwaki muggyiddwa mangu nnyo mu ssuubi lyammwe ery'okulabika kwange, ng'alaba sizze kujja? Ng'alaba ebirangiriro bya Yakuwa biyinza okukyusibwa? Katonda omunene ow'emirembe n'emirembe, Yakuwa, yeerabidde ebisuubizo bye? Nze nneerabidde ebigambo byange n'ebisuubizo byange? Mazima, mazima, mbagamba nti nedda! Era bonna abawa Yakuwa, Katonda omunene ow'emirembe n'emirembe, nange, Yesuwa HaMasiya, Mukama wa bakama era Kabaka wa bakabaka, obusirusiru, balina okwenenya amangu ddala. Musabe okusonyiyibwa abaana bange era mweyongere okweteekateeka kubanga ndirabika mu kaseera katono, era mulisamaalirira engeri ekintu ekinene ekiddako mu nteekateeka ya Yakuwa ey'okununula abantu gye kijja okutuukirira, kubanga kiribaawo mu kaseera katono era nga tekisuubirwa, kubanga bwe kityo bwe kyalangirirwa. Kubanga kiribaawo nti ndizuukiza abafu mu Kristo okusooka, nammwe abali abalamu ne musigalawo mulitwalibwa okunsisinkana mu bire, era kiribaawo mu kusekeenyaze eriiso.

Lwaki mweraliikirira? Lwaki abamu ku mmwe mweraga nti ebintu bizzeeyo mu mbeera ey'edda? Mazima ddala tewali kuddayo mu ngeri ez'edda, kubanga ensi eno eririwo kati erisisinkana akabi ak'amaanyi era erikyusibwa emirembe gyonna, era erikyuka nnyo mu kiseera ky'okubonaabona kwa Yakobo era ensi eriba ng'omu nnaku za Nuuwa. Omuzibi bw'aliggibwawo tewalibaayo kigiziyiza bibi, n'amagye g'abagwi agalumba byonna ebyange, kubanga Yakuwa awadde ogusota amaanyi okulwana n'abatukuvu n'okubawangula. Abangi balijja gye ndi okuyita mu kuzikirizibwa kw'omubiri gwabwe, n'omubiri gwennyini gwe baagala era ogukyabasiba. Kubanga bonna abakoowoola erinnya lya Mukama balirwokoka, era omuweereza tasinga mukama we.

Newankubadde mbadde mpawa okuwonyezebwa okuzikirizibwa okujja eri abeesigwa, abangi tebeeteeseteese naye balirwokoka era baliba n'omugaso munene mu Bwakabaka bwange obujja, kubanga ndifuga okuva ku ntebe ya Dawudi era nnyinako omugabo ku ntebe ya Kitange, era nammwe muliba n'omugabo ku ntebe yange mmwe abeesigwa bange. Abo abateeteeseteese balibonaabona nnyo ku lw'erinnya lyange, era balibonaabona nga nze, Yesu Omunazaaleeti, bwe nnabonaabona okunyweza okulokoka kw'abantu era okuyita mu kukomererwa kwange ku musalaba ndese ekyokulabirako eky'obuwulize okutuusa okufa eri bonna okugoberera. Era okuyita mu kubonaabona okungi, ebibiina bingi birirwokoka ne bifuna empeera nene mu nze Kristo Yesu, era n'okuweereza Yakuwa mu yeekaalu ye. Empeera nnene erindiridde bonna abakoowoola erinnya lyange, n'abo abakoowoola erinnya lyange okutuusa ku nkomerero balirwokoka, n'abo abataganya bulamu bwabwe okutuusa ku nkomerero. Kubanga ensi eno eggwaawo era erizikirizibwa era eribonerezebwa ddala Obwakabaka bwange obw'obutuukirivu nga tebunassibwawo ku nsi.

Kale temuggwaamu maanyi Abaana bange, kubanga ebisuubizo byange ye yee ne amiina era ndisikula omugole wange okumutwala mu ggulu nga bwe nassuubiza, era tewali muntu n'omu ku abo abaalondebwa okufuna obulokozi obw'emirembe n'emirembe okuva eri Yakuwa emisingi gy'ensi nga tenassibwawo, alinyagulibwa mu mikono gyange egy'emirembe era obulokozi bwammwe bunywevu mu nze, Kristo Yesu, musigale beesigwa era musigale mu Kristo okutuusa ku nkomerero. Kino kibadde kya mukalulu okuva eri Yakuwa, nti omuntu asangibwe nga mwesigwa mu makubo ge. Nange, Yesuwa HaMasiya, ndeeta amakubo ga Yakuwa n'abo abayiga ku nze bayiga amakubo ga Yakuwa, kubanga ndi omu mu bumu ne mu bigendererwa ne Kitange. Mu bbanga ttono muliba omu naffe, tekyawandiikibwa nti mufuuliddwa ekitundu ky'Obwakatonda obw'emirembe n'emirembe, kubanga obwo bwe busika bwammwe era eyo ye mpeera yange eri abaana abeesigwa ba Katonda abaasangibwa nga beesigwa mu nze. Kubanga mu nze, Kristo Yesu, mutuulamu okujjula kwonna okw'Obwakatonda mu mubiri, era muligabana obusika bwange olw'obwesigwa bwammwe, era muligabana entebe yange nga Bakabaka n'Abakabona era Obwakabaka bwange tebulina nkomerero.

Kale munywerere ku lwange mu busekendi buno obw'enkomerero obw'emirembe gino, era mutegeere nti obudde buweddewo. Yee, obudde buweddewo Babulooni ow'Ekyama okwenenya. Ebiseera byange eby'ekisa n'okusaasira biweddewo, newankubadde okusaasira n'ekisa biri nange emirembe gyonna. Kati gujja omusango omunene ku nsi, ne ku mawanga n'ensi yonna. Naye omusango teguligwa ogulimalawo abalamu bonna, naye guligwa ne gweyongerayongera. Omusango ku musango, akabonero ku kabonero, ekkondeere ku kkondeere, ekibya ku kibya, zisanze ku zisanze, n'ekirwadde ku kirwadde. Era olw'ekyo abantu bangi, bangi nnyo, balijja mu bulokozi mu nze, Yesu Omunazaaleeti, kubanga nze ndi Mwana gw'endiga gw'Okuyitako atuukiridde ow'ebiseera byonna, era tewakyali kwetaaga ssaddaaka. Naye eri mmwe, Omugole wange omwesigwa, ebiseera eby'okulinda biweddewo.

Tewaliba kulwawo kuddamu, era tewaliba kulinda kuddamu, era mulituuka ku mpeera yammwe ejjudde n'obusika bwammwe mu nze. Era newankubadde abamu balifuna okufiirwa, eriiso teryalaba era okutu tekuwulidde, so tekuyingidde mu mutima gw'omuntu, ebintu Yakuwa bye yateekerateekera abo abamwagala. Bwe kityo bwe kiri abaagalwa bange abeesigwa, mulaze okwagala kwammwe eri nze, Kristo Yesu, n'okwagala kwammwe eri Yakuwa kubanga mwawulira amateeka gange olw'okukkiriza mu lunaku luno olubi. Newankubadde temundabangako n'amaaso gammwe gennyini, so temulabangako kitiibwa kya Yakuwa eky'emirembe n'amaaso gammwe gennyini, musigadde beesigwa mu nze okutuusa ku nkomerero, olw'ekyo waliwo empeera nnene eyabateekerwa mu ggulu, kale temuggwaamu maanyi!

Mweyongere okugumiikiriza, Abaana bange, kubanga nsembera. Temuwuliriza abasekerezi ababasekerera kubanga okusekerera kwabwe mu bbanga ttono kulifuuka okunakuwala okungi, era abasekerezi bangi balisirika ng'obulamu bwabwe bukomye. Kubanga gy'emiba emisango egijja. Abo abagaanyi emikono gyange egigoloddwa ne balowooza nti omusaayi gwange ogutangirira kintu kitakyalina muwendo era ne beerowooza nga tebasaanira bulamu bwa lubeerera mu Bwakabaka bwange obujja, abo bennyini balisikira ekizikiza n'okufa bye baagala ennyo n'okwegomba era abangi baligwa kyeyagalire mu kufa okw'okubiri.

Kubanga mu kwagala kwa Kitange okungi, Yakuwa Katonda ow'emirembe kubanga tewali alinga ye, talikaka n'omu ku bitonde bye okumugoberera n'okumwagala. Bonna baweebwa eddembe ly'okulonda era bonna balifuna empeera y'okulonda kwabwe. Kubanga gye miba amateeka ga Yakuwa. Abo abaagala obubi, abaagala essanyu ly'ekibi n'okwagala obubi ebibi byabwe bye bireetera abalala ababonaabona awatali nsonga olw'okwegomba kwabwe n'amakubo gaabwe amakyamu balisikira amakubo gaabwe amabi ne gadda mu bifuba byabwe era okwo kwe kuliba okubonerezebwa kwabwe era buli kibi kirisasulwa emirembe gyonna. Naye abo abakkiriza endagaano y'emirembe eya Yakuwa, nze Yesu Omunazaaleeti, balisikira obulamu obutaggwaawo n'emirembe n'empeera n'okwagala n'essanyu eritagwawo mu maaso gange ne mu maaso ga Yakuwa yennyini, kubanga mu bbanga ttono ekifo Yakuwa ky'abeeramu kiriba mu bantu kubanga bwe kityo bwe kyawandiikibwa. Nange nsembera okubanyagulayo okubaleeta mu kisenge ky'entebe ya Yakuwa yennyini, gye mutudde dda, era engule zammwe zibalindiridde.

Temukkiriza bulimba bwabwe abaana bange, njija kubaggyawo, musigale banywevu akaseera akatono oba obubiri, kubanga okulabika kwange kuli kumpi era kulibaawo mu kiseera kyonna kati, era ndisinga ebyo bye musuubira. Mweteeketeeke era mwetukuze mu musaayi gwange ogutangirira. Mweyongere okubeera n'obulamu obw'okwenenya era mweyongere okukomerera amakubo gammwe ag'omubiri, kubanga si gange era tewali kintu kibi kiriyingira mu Bwakabaka bwange era tewali kintu kibi kiriyimirira mu maaso ga Yakuwa. Nze ndi ssaddaaka yammwe ey'emirembe n'emirembe era mbafudde abataliiko bbala wadde enkanyanya era tewali bbala ku beesigwa bange kubanga omusaayi gwange gubanaaza bulijjo bwe mwatuukirira ebibi byammwe mu kwenenya, newankubadde omubiri gwammwe gwonoonese ddala.

Mujje gye ndi n'omutima omuggule era ndibalaga mu kwagala obutali butuukirivu bwammwe mulyoke mwatuukirire ebibi byammwe gye ndi, era bwe mukola bwe mutyo ndibanaaza era ndyeyongera okubafuula abatuukiridde nga bwe muli ekitonde ekipya mu nze, Kristo Yesu. Era mazima, mazima, bonna abateekwako akabonero ak'omwoyo gwange omutukuvu ogw'ekisuubizo, oba Omuyudaaya oba Omunnamawanga, (oba muddu oba wa ddembe) balitondebwa buto bonna ng'omuntu omu omupya mu nze Kristo Yesu era balijimirira mu ddembe erigulumizibbwa emirembe n'emirembe! Kubanga nze ndi mwoyo oguwa obulamu, kubanga Kitange bwe yantonda. Nange ndi oyo YE NZE omunene gwe yantonda emirembe n'emirembe. Era kati nammwe mulifaananyizibwa nze mu mubiri ogugulumizibbwa, omubiri ogukoleddwa ng'empeera oguliraga ebyo bye mwatuukako mu kuyimirira kwammwe ku lwange mu lunaku luno olubi. Tewali n'emu ku nteeka zammwe eteriweebwa mpeera: Kubanga Katonda si mutali mutuukirivu, yeerabire omulimu gwammwe, n'okuteekawo okwagala, kwe mwalaga eri Erinnya lye, kubanga mwaweereza Abatukuvu, era mukyabaweereza.

Obuntu bwammwe obw'amazima bukwekeddwa mu nze, Omununuzi wammwe era Masiya. Emirembe n'emirembe mulibeera nange, era muliba n'omwoyo gumu n'omutima gumu nange Yesuwa HaMasiya era ne Yakuwa, kubanga El Shaddai Omunene, Katonda Ayinzabyonna aliba byonna mu byonna kubanga ebigendererwa bye birituukiririzibwa mu mmwe, abayite bange, abalonde, n'abeesigwa. Nzijja ku lwammwe, Mugole wange, mweteeseteese? Mulina amafuta mu ttaala zammwe? Mwenyezedde ddala mu musaayi gwange ogutangirira? Mwalekeraawo okunaazaganako ebigere?

Mutunuulire! Mulabe! Muwulire! Mutunuulire okulabika kwange era muzzeŋŋanamu amaanyi kubanga Setaani yandyekwese okulabika kwange singa ekyo kyandisobose. Mutunuulire nze, omutandisi n'omutuukiriza w'okukkiriza kwammwe, kubanga mu kusekeenyaze eriiso mulikyusibwa ne mufaananyizibwa nange emirembe n'emirembe. Muwulire ebigambo byange era mwetegekere okulabika kwange ate okusingira ddala mubeerenga bawulize olw'Okwagala Yakuwa nange okukwata Ekigambo kyange ekitukuvu mu butukuvu bw'omutima, nga mumanyi nti okununulibwa kwammwe teku kyali kusembera naye kutuuse.

Ndi omu mu bumu ne mu bigendererwa ne Kitange, era mu bbanga ttono muliba omu naffe.

Nze ndi Mwana gw'endiga gw'Okuyitako atuukiridde ow'ebiseera byonna.

Nsembera. Temuwuliriza abasekerezi ababasekerera.

Nzijja ku lwammwe, musigale banywevu akaseera akatono oba obubiri.

Nze ndi ssaddaaka yammwe ey'emirembe.

Nze ndi mwoyo oguwa obulamu, kubanga Kitange bwe yantonda.

Nze ndi oyo YE NZE omunene gwe yantonda emirembe n'emirembe.
