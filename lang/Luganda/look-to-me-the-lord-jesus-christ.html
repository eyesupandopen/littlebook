Omujulirwa Omulamba: Ekkanisa
Mu Bikoleddwa bya Yokaana Omutume
Ogw'okusatu 28, 2024

Muntunuulire nze, Mukama wammwe Yesu Kristo!

Abaana bange, mwolesemu amaaso gammwe muntunuulire nze, Mukama wammwe Yesu Kristo. Muzuukuke mu tulo mukomewo gyendi; mukomewo gyendi, kubanga emikono gyange gigooleddwa eri mmwe, abalonde bange. Mangu ddala mujja kuba nange, kubanga essaawa esigaddeyo eddakiika ey'enkomerero n'ebiseera by'ekisa binaatera okuggwaako, era ndikituusa ku nkomerero yaakyo etuukiridde. Muzuukuke mu tulo, nange ndibaggyako amalogero ag'ebiroogo by'ensi eno ku maaso gammwe. Mukaabe era muwowoggane olw'ebibi byammwe n'okugaana kwammwe okujja gyendi, era mumanye nti mbayita mu ddakiika eno ey'enkomerero. Mbayita mudde gyendi, okwagala kwammwe okwasooka, wadde nga muleese abalala mu mitima gyammwe.

Mubadduke bonna, kubanga befuula abo be batali; yee, be misuma gya mu ndiga, nammwe mwagattama mu kukwatibwa kwabyo okw'ekivve. Temusiroobera, baana bange, naye muzuukuke era munninde, kubanga obubonero bwange bumaze okuba mu ggulu era njija ng'omubbi mu kiro. Kiki kye ndisanga nga nkomyeewo okutwala Ekkanisa yange? Ndisanga n'okukkiriza kwonna? Olowooza, ggwe ekkanisa y'e Lawodikiya, oli wa luguuguumo, era njagala obe munnyogovu oba wa kwokya. Mudde gyendi omulundi omulala, nange ndibayonja okubaggya mu tulo, era ndiwonya ebiwundu ebyabalumya ababi. Sirina kwagala kubawanika kuva mu kamwa kange, kale mujje omulundi omulala gyendi, nange ndibaaniriza. Mumanye nti okwagala kwange gye muli tekufa era bulijjo kwa maanyi era tekukyuka.

Tewali mulala wabula nze ayinza okubalokola, tewali mulala wabula nze ayinza okubafuula abatuukiridde n'okuwummuza, tewali mulala wabula nze ayinza okubakuuma n'okubawa obulamu obutaggwaawo, tewali mulala wabula nze ayinza okubalokola mu bibi byammwe, kubanga tewali linnya ddala lyaweebwa abantu mwe bayinza okulokolerwa. Nze ssuubi lyammwe eritaggwaawo eyabatonda obuggya mu kuzaalibwa kwange okupya kwe nabafukirako. Mumanye nti muli baana ba Kitange, era mulina obusika obw'amaanyi olw'obwesigwa bwammwe. Naye, wadde nga ndisalira emisango emirimu gyammwe okufuna empeera oba okufiirwa, muvudde mu butali butuukirivu ne muyingira mu bulamu obutaggwaawo olw'okukkiriza kwammwe mu nze n'obufuzi bwange obutaggwaawo. Laba! Nabafuula bakabaka ne bakabona mu maaso ga Kitange, kubanga nze kabona asinga obukulu emirembe gyonna okusinziira ku mutindo gwa Merukizeddeeki.

Ani oyo ayinza okweyimusa okubalwanyisa? Kubanga okujja okulwanyisa baganda bange kwe kujja okulwanyisa nze, nange ndisasula, bw'ayogera Mukama ow'ekitiibwa omukulu ow'oku ggulu! Ebintu byonna mu ggulu ne ku nsi byateekebwa mu mikono gyange, kale mumanye nti nnina obuyinza obusukkulumu, era tewali muntu mukulu kundiko okuggyako Kitange, eyangulumiza okusinga byonna. Kale ani oyo gwe mulina okutya? Okuyigganyizibwa n'okubonaabona tebibafuula b'amaanyi mu nze? Si bwe kityo nti amaanyi gammwe bwe gagenda, mba awo okubazzaamu amaanyi mu bunafu bwammwe?

Kale munywerere era mubeere b'amaanyi mu nze! Lwaki mubuusabuusa ebigambo byange? Naye, mubeere n'okukkiriza mu nze era mwogere ebigambo bye mbawa, kubanga ebigambo bino bya maanyi era bya nkizo singa munaabyogera mu kukkiriza. Mwogere n'obwesige n'obuyinza, era mulagire ebintu eby'obwavu mu linnya lya Yesu Kristo, erinnya lyange ery'amaanyi erisinga gonna. Mangu ddala mujja kundaba n'amaaso gammwe, era okubeerawo kwammwe mu ggulu kwegwanyizibwa nnyo nze ne Kitange, yee, ne Yesu ne Yahweh. Muli bya bugagga bya Kitange ebisinga obunene, era temuliiko muwendo. Kubanga wa walala omusaayi ogutalimu kibi we gwateekebwawo olw'ebibi byammwe okuggyako mu nze?

Olowooza, mmwe ababi abatuula ne musekerera, nga mulowooza nti mulina obuwanguzi obunene. Ndittaanya enteekateeka zammwe ez'obubi, era mujja kusobola kutuukiriza ekyo kyokka Kitange ky'asazeewo nti muyinza okukola okusinziira ku nteekateeka ze ze yateekawo. Muli banafu era baavu era abawanguddwa ddala. Olowooza, ggwe omuntu ow'obutonde, lwaki okyalonda amakubo amabi ag'ababi ababakyamya mu bukumpanya? Okusanyuka kw'ekibi kusaana okwawukanibwa okutaggwaawo n'ekitiibwa kya Katonda ow'emirembe n'emirembe? Wa w'ojja okufuna obulamu ebweru wange? Ani ajja okubawonya okuggyako oyo gwe mukyagaana? Ekisa kyange n'okusaasira kwange tebyaziyiza mukono gw'omulabe?

Ekintu kyonna ekirungi kye mulina kyava eri Kitaffe ow'ebitangaala, atalina kukyuka kwonna wadde ekisiikirize ky'okukyuka. Naye nedda, mwagaana ekisa kyange ne mukozesa okusaasira kwange okweyagala n'okweyagaza mu bubi bwammwe. Laba! Okuzikirizibwa okunene kujja kubatuukako. Mulyeddwaamu olukwe abakulembeze bammwe bennyini, abateekateeka okuzikirizibwa kwammwe era abakkirizza amagye amamyuufu okuyingira. Okuzikirizibwa n'obubi obunene bijja kubatuukako amangu ago, era bijja kujja nga nkwakula omugole wange okuva wakati mu makubo gammwe amabi. Olwo okuzikirizibwa okw'amangu ago kujja kubakwata, kubanga omutegesi w'obutimba atega butimba ki mu maaso g'ennyonyi? Nedda, omutego ogutegekeddwa obulungi gweteeseteese okutogoloka, era gujja kubaawo ng'omuziyizi wange aggyiddwawo, so temujja kuwona.

Olwo emitima gyammwe gijja kusaanuuka munda mu mmwe n'amaanyi gammwe gadduke nga mulaba n'amaaso gammwe ebitonde ebinaatera okusumululwa okuva mu buziba bw'ensi okubajjirako. Ndiggulawo olunnyo, kubanga nnina obuyinza ne ku lwo; kubanga nnina ebisumuluzo by'okufa n'emagombe, era tewali ayinza okubinkwakula. Singa mwategeera nti temulina kintu kyonna ku nsi, era byonna biri bya kisa na kusaasira, kubanga okwagala kwa Katonda kuleeta abantu okwenenya. Naye nedda, mu mululu mukung'aanya obulungi bwa Katonda eri mmwe mwennyini ne mutta muliraanwa wammwe mu kifo ky'okumwagala nga bwe mweyagala, nga bwe nnalagira. Katonda bwe yatyo yagala ensi, n'okuwaayo n'awaayo Omwana we omu yekka, Yesu Omunazaaleeti, okubatabaganya naye mu kwagala okuyita mu nze.

Naye mu malala gammwe n'amakubo g'ensi, mukalubizza emitima gyammwe okumpinga nze n'obulokozi bwange. N'olwekyo, okuzikirizibwa okutaliiko kya kulabirako kujja kutuuka ku ggwanga lyammwe mu ssaawa emu, era Babulooni Ekikwabuzi tekiribaawo nate. Yee, Babulooni omunene, ebyama byo bijja kwanjulwa abantu bonna babirabe, naabo abakwewumbako bajja kubonerezebwa n'omusango gwe gumu.

N'olwekyo, olowooza, ggwe omuntu agaana Katonda, weetoowaze ojje gyendi kati! Essaawa yo ey'okwetaaga eriwo kati. Mudde gyendi; mbeegayiridde, era mbayita ku mannya! Muve mu nsi eno efa, kubanga mulimu obulokozi mu nze singa munaakkiriza.

Sirina ssanyu mu musango gwammwe, era Kitange ayagala mubeere balamu mu maaso ge, era mu kwagala kwe yawadde ebitonde bye omukisa okwerondera: nteeka mu maaso gammwe leero obulamu n'okufa, n'olwekyo, mulonde obulamu mmwe n'ezzadde lyammwe mulyoke mubeere abalamu! Kale mujje gyendi nga tekunnayitawo era mumanye nti ensi eno embi gye mwewumbako ejja kuzikirizibwa ddala, era kijja kuyita mu busungu bw'Omwana gw'endiga. Katonda talisalira musango abakola ebikolwa ebibi abaasse abaana be, abalumazizza n'okutyoboola abatalina musango, abaasse omwana omu yekka owa Katonda? Wadde nga nze nnawaayo obulamu bwange ku lwammwe mu kwagala era tewali muntu yaggyawo bulamu bwange, waliwo omusango n'obwenkanya obunene obugenda okuweerezebwa nze. Kubanga nnagula ensi mu musaayi gwange ne ssaddaaka ey'okutangirira, era yange. Nditeekawo obwakabaka obw'obutuukirivu n'obutukuvu obw'amazima, omusege n'omwana gw'endiga we birigalamira awamu, awataliba kintu kibi kyonna kiyingira. N'olwekyo, muve mu bibi byammwe, nga bwe mbagala, era nkoowoola n'omukka gwange ogusembayo nga akayinja akasembayo k'omusaawa tekannagwa. Era kanaatera okutuuka.

Omwoyo n'Omugole bagamba nti, jjangu!

Mbayita mu ddakiika eno ey'enkomerero.

Mbayita mudde gyendi.

Nze ssuubi lyammwe eritaggwaawo eyabatonda obuggya mu kuzaalibwa kwange okupya kwe nabafukirako.

Nze kabona asinga obukulu emirembe gyonna okusinziira ku mutindo gwa Merukizeddeeki.

Ndi wano okubazzaamu amaanyi mu bunafu bwammwe.

Nkoowoola n'omukka gwange ogusembayo nga akayinja akasembayo k'omusaawa tekannagwa, era kanaatera okutuuka.

Nze Ndi nga bwe Ndi, era Nze Ndi Asinga Obukulu yantumye.
