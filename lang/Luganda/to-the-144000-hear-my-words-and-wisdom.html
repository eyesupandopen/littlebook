Mu Biwandiiko bya Yokaana Omubikkulirwa
Meeyi 25, 2024

Abantu 144,000: Muwulire Ebigambo byange n'Amagezi!

Muwulire ebigambo byange era mutege amatu eri amagezi gange, kubanga nze, Mukama wammwe Yesu Kristo, njakwogera nammwe bwe munaawulirangayo ebigambo byange. Kubanga nja kuteeka akabonero ku bantu bange 144,000; temusomanga? Era nalaba malayika omulala ng'ava ebuvanjuba, ng'alina akabonero ka Katonda omulamu: n'ayogerera n'eddoboozi ddene eri bamalayika abana abo abaweebwa okulumya ensi n'ennyanja, ng'agamba nti, Temulumya nsi, newaakubadde ennyanja newaakubadde emiti, okutuusa lwe tulimala okuteeka akabonero ku bawereza ba Katonda waffe ku byenyi byabwe. Bwe ntyo nja kutuma abo be nja okuteekako akabonero, era mujja kunkolera ebikolwa eby'amaanyi, era mujja kutambulira mu maanyi mangi. Naye kino tekijja kuba nga mu mulembe ogw'ekisa, ababuulizi bwe baayigirizanga okuva mu biwandiiko ebitukuvu nga bali ku katuti. (Abamu baayogera bya bulimba ate abamu ne bankolera ebikolwa eby'amaanyi mu Makanisa gaabwe.) Omulembe gw'ekkanisa gunaatera okuggwaako, n'omulembe gwange ogw'ekisa gwakumpi okukomekkerezebwa, era bwe kityo ebiseera eby'okugezesebwa bijja kutandika, n'okutuusa ku nnaku za Yakobo.

Era bwe ntyo nja kubatuma okulabirira ekisibo kyange ekijja okusaasaanyizibwa ennyo n'okugezesebwa n'ebibonyoobonyo ebingi. Kubanga ennaku zijja kuba mbi, nammwe mujja kwakaayakana ng'omusana era muyimirire ng'empagi mu bantu abasaasaanye. Noolwekyo mbatuma okwagala muliraanwa wo n'okumufaako. Kubanga mmwe abeesigwa eri okuyitibwa kwange mujja kugondera amateeka ge nawa, si mateeka ga Musa. Kubanga nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, natuukiriza amateeka ga Musa, era Torah ligonderwa lwa kukkiriza nze, kubanga omutuukirivu ajja kuba mulamu lwa kukkiriza.

Bwe kityo bwe kiriba: Yagalanga Mukama Katonda wo n'omutima gwo gwonna, n'obulamu bwo bwonna, n'amagezi go gonna, n'amaanyi go gonna, era yagalanga muliraanwa wo nga bwe weeyagala wekka. Kubanga bw'onookolanga bino, tolirema kulokola bulamu bwo kuzikirira, era n'obw'abo be nkutumyeeko. Bino si bye biseera bya bulamu buwanvu na kugaggawala, kubanga ennaku ezo zaweddewo ku nsi okutuusa lwe ndijja okufuga ensi mu butuukirivu. Oyo atayagala bulamu bwe okutuusa ku nkomerero, alirokolebwa era aliba mulamu emirembe gyonna.

Muli balamu mu biseera by'entalo, n'obusungu obungi, n'okubikkulirwa okungi okwa byonna. Kubanga Katonda omunene ow'emirembe n'emirembe, ye Yahweh, tajja kwekweka nate ku nsi; kubanga ajja kufuluma mu kifo kye, era antuma nze, ye Yeshua HaMashiach, okutuukiriza obusungu bwe n'okutuukiriza ebisuubizo bye yakola ne Bajjajjaffe. Naye okusooka, omusango gujja kugwa, n'obusungu obungi bujja kugwa ku nsi, era temujja kusobola nate okwenyigira mu ntegeka yaabwe. Kubanga okwenyigira mu ntegeka yaabwe ejja okuva mu vvu kujja kwetaagisa buli muntu okukkiriza akabonero kaabwe. Bw'oneegattanga nabo, ojja kugabana nabo mu kuzikirira kwabwe okw'emirembe mu nnyanja ey'omuliro, era ennyanja eyo yategekebwa Katonda eri Setaani ne bamalayika be, kale temubeegattako.

Temujja kufuna mirembe gyonna mu nsi yaabwe ejja okuyimuka, wabula obubi bwokka obutagambika, kubanga nja kuba nzigyewo omuzibi. Era bwe mutaabe mu kibiina kyabwe, temujja kuba n'omukago gwonna nabo, kubanga bajja kutta bonna abawakanya. Kubanga akabonero kajja kukyusa abo bonna abakakkiriza emirembe gyonna nga kakyuusa obuzaale bwabwe, kubanga omuntu yakolebwa mu kifaananyi kya Yahweh, newaakubadde nga Setaani anoonya okuvoola ekifaananyi ekyo mu ngeri etakyuka okuyita mu ddagala lye ly'asuubula mu bulimba obungi. Bajja kubasuubiza obulamu obuwanvu, emirembe, n'okugaggawala, naye obulimba obwo buba butego bwokka. Kubanga akabonero bwe kalikkirizibwa, tewali kijja kudda mabega kukyusa kyafaanana, kubanga Yahweh bw'atyo bwe yalangirira!

Kubanga omuweereza tasinga mukama we, era mbayita okukola emirimu gye nja okubakulemberamu ekkubo lyonna, era nga bwe kyali mu nnaku za Nuuwa, bwe kityo bwe kiriba. Bwe ntyo nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, natuukiriza ekigendererwa kyange ng'omwana gw'endiga gwa Katonda, kubanga nawaayo omusaayi gwange ogutalimu kibi eri Yahweh ku lw'okwesalirawo kwange era olw'okwagala okutuukiridde okwaka mu mutima gwange.

Bwe ntyo olw'essanyu eryateekebwa mu maaso gange, nagumiikiriza okubonaabona okungi, okutulugunyizibwa, n'ennaku (n'okutuusa awatali nsonga) kubanga neetikka ebibi byonna eby'abantu. Era nasasulira buli kibi, mu bujjuvu, era nakikola kyeyagalire. Kale muntunuulire nze, Yesu, eyatandika era atuukiriza okukkiriza kwammwe, olw'essanyu eryateekebwa mu maaso gange, eyagumiikiriza omusalaba, n'anyooma ensonyi, era ntuula ku mukono ogwa ddyo ogw'entebe ya Katonda. Kale mugoberere ekyokulabirako kyange okutuuka ku buwanguzi obunene, kubanga ne mu ddungu nja kubalaga ekkubo eritereevu.

Kale mutegeere nti, tewajja kubaawo mirembe, era mbatuma okuweereza mu biseera by'entalo n'okubonaabona okungi. Kubanga ogusota gujja kulwanyisa abatukuvu era gujja kubawangula, kubanga bwe gutyo bwe gwaweebwa amaanyi okukola. N'abo abajja okukoowoola erinnya lya Mukama balimulokoka, kubanga abo abataayagala bulamu bwabwe okutuusa ku nkomerero balibusanga emirembe gyonna mu nze, Kristo Yesu. Kubanga natangirira ebibi by'abantu n'omusaayi gwange ogutalimu kibi, era ndi kabona asinga obukulu emirembe n'emirembe ng'engeri ya Merukizeddeeki bw'eri, eyawaayo omusaayi gwe ogutalimu kibi eri Yahweh omulundi gumu gwokka. N'olwekyo, temuddayo mu makubo amakadde, newaakubadde mu nnono zaabwe, newaakubadde mu Talmud yaabwe, newaakubadde mu Torah; kubanga Torah ligonderwa mu bujjuvu lwa kukkiriza nze, Yesu Kristo.

Bwe kityo okuyita mu kubonaabona okungi, obulumi obungi, okunyigirizibwa okungi, abatukuvu bange we bajja okwakaayakana, n'empeera yaabwe eriba nnene, kubanga empeera yange nnene. Kubanga ekisuubizo kigamba ki: Bano be bava mu kubonaabona okungi, ne booleza ebyambalo byabwe, ne babyeruza mu musaayi gw'Omwana gw'endiga. Kyebava babeera mu maaso g'entebe ya Katonda, ne bamuweereza emisana n'ekiro mu Yeekaalu ye: n'oyo atuula ku ntebe alituula mu bo. Tebaaluma njala nate, so tebaaluma nnyonta nate, so n'enjuba teeryaka ku bo, newaakubadde okwokya kwonna. Kubanga Omwana gw'endiga ali wakati mu ntebe, ye alibaliisa, era alibakulembera okubatuusa ku nzizi ez'amazzi amalamu: ne Katonda alisiimuula amaziga gonna mu maaso gaabwe.

Era bwe ntyo nja kutuma abo be nnayise. Mbayita okwagala Katonda n'omutima gwammwe gwonna, obulamu bwammwe bwonna, amagezi gammwe gonna, n'amaanyi gammwe gonna, era mbatuma okwagala muliraanwa wammwe nga bwe mweyagala mwekka. Kubanga okuwona kujja kuva mu bigambo ebiwa obulamu Kitange by'awadde okugonderwa abantu bonna. Noolwekyo yagalanga muliraanwa wo, mweteekerenganenga, muyambaganenga era muwaniriraganenga; kubanga abantu bange bajja kusaasaanyizibwa. Bajja kukuŋŋaanira mu bibiina mu ddungu, era newaakubadde nga mu ddungu temujja kuba mirembe, nja kuwa abantu bange obubudamo era nja kubawa ebyetaago byabwe.

Era bwe ntyo nja kutuma abo be nnayise. Mbatuma okulagira ebitasoboka nga mutambulira mu kwagala n'amaanyi amangi, ge maanyi ge nja okubambaza. Kubanga mujja kuntambulira ekkubo lyonna mu kugonda, nga mwesiga nze, Kristo Yesu, ajja okubakulembera n'omwoyo gwange; n'okufukibwako amafuta kwe mulina kujja kubaleetera okutambulira mu buwanguzi. Naye kiribaawo ng'ekyawandiikibwa bwe kigamba nti: Balina omukisa abafiira mu Mukama, okuva kaakano, weewaawo, Omwoyo bw'agamba, balyoke bawummule emirimu gyabwe, n'ebikolwa byabwe bibagoberere.

Mujja kutambula ekkubo lyonna, era nja kubakulembera mu ngeri eyo. Kubanga nze bubudamo bwammwe n'ekigo kyammwe, ye Masiya wammwe ow'amazima gwemwesiga. Era newaakubadde abantu lukumi bajja kugwa ku ludda lwo, n'abantu mutwalo ku mukono gwo ogwa ddyo: naye tebirikusemberera. Kubanga mwateekebwako akabonero okutuukiriza ebigendererwa byange, n'empeera ennene n'ekisuubizo mbibawadde okubawanirira n'okubazzaamu amaanyi, kubanga mbatuma mu mirimu Kitange gye yantegekera, era bwe ntyo mbatuma ng'abaweereza bange era bannabbi bange.

Kale bwe mugenda okulagira ebitasoboka, mumanye nti mujja kulagira bingi n'omwoyo ogwazaalibwa mu mmwe. Kale mulagire ensozi ziggyibwewo zisuulibwe mu nnyanja, nga mulina okukkiriza mu Yahweh. Kale mulagire emyoyo emibi giveewo nga mwogera ebigambo eby'amaanyi n'okukkiriza okungi mu Yahweh. Kale mulagire endwadde zonna n'obubenje bigende nga mwogera ebigambo ebigwanidde okugonderwa (ne byonna) nga mwogera ebigambo eby'amaanyi amangi n'okukkiriza okungi mu Yahweh. Kubanga ku lw'entegeka n'ebigendererwa bya Yahweh nze natumibwa, ne mu ntegeka n'ebigendererwa ebyo mbatuma, era mujja kunkolera ebikolwa eby'amaanyi. Kubanga Yahweh y'ali mu byonna n'omwoyo gwange, gwe nja okuteeka ku balonze.

Kubanga mu nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, okujulira kwonna mwe kubeera, kubanga Kitange yampa ebintu byonna mu ggulu ne ku nsi, ndyoke ntuukirize byonna. Bwe ntyo Yahweh y'ali mu byonna, era mulina omukisa okuva eri Kitange n'eri Omwana we, kubanga Musa bwe yatyo bwe yayigiriza: Mukama Katonda wo alikuyimusiza nnabbi mu ggwanga lyo, mu baganda bo, alifaanana nze, ye gwe munaawuliranga. Nze nnabbi oyo Musa gwe yayogerako (eyazuukizibwa mu kika kya Yuda), era ndi wa muwendo nnyo okusinga awo; kubanga Kitange yasalawo nti nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, mbeere n'obukulu mu byonna, ne mu nze okujulira kwonna kubeerenga. Nze nnabbi oyo Musa gwe yayogerako, eyazuukizibwa mu bafu olw'ekigendererwa ekyo.

Bwe ntyo nja kubalabirira n'abo be nja okubatumako. Kubanga mujja kuba musana ogwaka, n'okubeera mu lwatu mu nsi enzibuwavu era etali nnywevu. Mujja kubuulira byonna ebinkwatako, Mukama wammwe era omulokozi wammwe, kubanga nze mwana gw'endiga gwa Katonda, ye Pasika etuukiridde. Tewali kulokoka mu linnya eddala lyonna wabula eryange, ye Yesu Kristo Omutuukirivu.

Abamu ku mmwe nja kubatwala mu bifo eby'enjawulo, era mujja kuba boogezi bange, bannabbi, era abaweereza. Kijja kubetaagisa okukebera emyoyo gy'abo be musanga era mutambulire mu magezi mangi; kubanga ensi gye mumanyi ejja kuddizibwawo n'ensi etalina mateeka ey'ebifo ebizise bingi, era abamu ku mmwe bajja kutumibwa okuweereza abo abali mu bifo ebizise, era mujja kutegeera ebiragiro byange, era mujja kukola ebikolwa ebinene.

Bwe ntyo mutegeere nti oluvannyuma lw'entalo n'okuzikirizibwa okujja okubaawo nga mmaze okukuŋŋaanya abeesigwa bange (n'okuggyawo omuzibi), wajja kubaawo akavuyo n'okuzikirizibwa kungi. Abantu bajja kudduka eno n'eri, era bangi bajja kuzikirira mu butakkiriza obutagambika, era bajja kuyigga n'okukwata n'okutta bangi. Era eri abangi, eddoboozi lye bajja okusooka okuwulira terijja kuba lyange, newaakubadde nga eddoboozi eryo lijja kuwa obulokozi obw'obulimba. Nedda, n'ensolo ejja okuyimuka n'amaanyi amangi okuva ku gusota ejja kuyita bangi mu bulimba okubatuusa ku nkomerero yaabwe ey'emirembe etaliiko ssuubi lyonna.

Bonna bajja kwefuula, kubanga be bafuulifu abanene, era bajja kwefuula nti be Masiya ow'amazima, n'abagoberezi baabwe beeyite nti bakiikirira Masiya ow'amazima ne Katonda ow'amazima; naye saabatuma, kale temugoberera eby'okulabirako byabwe eby'essuubi eby'obulimba. Kubanga bajja kuwaayo okuyingira mu ntegeka yaabwe empya okuzimba ensi yaabwe empya; naye okuyingira, omuntu alina okukkiriza akabonero. N'abo abakkiriza akabonero, okuyingira kujja kuweebwa mu nnimi z'omuliro eza bbululu mu nnyanja ey'omuliro eyaka n'ekibiriiti. Kubanga bonna abakkiriza akabonero bajja kugabana mu muliro ogutazikira era bajja kuba bawayisizza obulamu obutaggwaawo ku lw'akafunfugu k'omugaati mu makubo ga Esawu.

Kijja kubetaagisa okusaaliza amaloboozi gonna okuggyako eryange n'okuva ku abo bonna abangaana. Kijja kwetaagisa amagezi amangi, obukugu, n'obukujjukujju okuwonawo, era nja kubawa olulimi lw'abagezigezi musobole okumanya engeri y'okuddamu buli muntu. Kubanga nja kubategeereza eddoboozi lyange, newaakubadde nga ddoboozi ttono, eriwulirwa mu kasirise. Mujja kumanya eddoboozi lyange, kubanga nja kukakasa eddoboozi lyange gye muli emirundi mingi, kale temuba batakkiriza wabula mubeere n'okukkiriza okungi mu bigambo byange eby'ekisuubizo.

Nja kubayigiriza byonna, era mujja kumanya engeri y'okukola emirimu gye mbatuma okukola. Eddoboozi lyange ddoboozi ettuukiridde, ye ddoboozi ly'oyo akaabira mu ddungu, era njogera okuyita mu ddoboozi ly'oyo akaabira mu ddungu; kubanga bwe batyo bwe baatumibwa, be bannabbi bange n'abaweereza bange abaakulembedde okubalaga amakubo n'engeri zammwe. Mbategekedde emirimu mingi egy'amaanyi, era mujja kukola n'amaanyi.

N'olwekyo, mumanye, mmwe ebika byange, ab'oluganda, n'abaana b'ekisuubizo: ebikungulwa ddala bingi, naye abakozi batono; kale musabe Mukama w'ebikungulwa, asindike abakozi mu bikungulwa bye. Mugende mu makubo gammwe: Laba, mbatuma ng'abaana b'endiga wakati mu misege. Kubanga nze Mukama w'ebikungulwa, era newaakubadde nga abakozi babaddenga batono, waliwo empeera nnene n'amagoba ag'emirembe n'ekitiibwa olw'omulimu gwe mujja okukola. Kubanga mujja kungula empeera n'ebibala bingi mu nze; kyokka temuggwaamu maanyi mu kubonaabona n'emitawaana gyammwe. Kuno kwe kwawukana okusembayo, era bonna abaalondebwa okuweebwa obulamu obutaggwaawo okuva ensi nga tenatondebwa bajja kulabika, era tebajja kubula, kubanga tewali asobola kubaggya mu ngalo zange.

Bwe kityo ebikolwa byammwe bijja kugoberera okutuuka mu bulamu obutaggwaawo, ne mu bikolwa byammwe eby'obuzira mujja kugulumiza Yahweh, eyantuma nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, nga Masiya wammwe ow'amazima. N'olw'ebikolwa byammwe eby'amaanyi bingi n'ebigambo eby'omunnyo eby'amazima bye mujja okwogera, mujja kulangirira okudda kwange okuli okumpi era mugende mu mirimu gya Yokaana Omubatiza okuteekateeka okujja kwa Mukama: kale mulangirire okudda kwange, kubanga sirirema kutuukiriza bigambo byange eby'ekisuubizo. Kubanga mazima ddala ku nkomerero y'okubonaabona kwa Yakobo, n'okutuusa ku nkomerero ya ssabbiiti y'emyaka esembayo, ye ssabbiiti ya Danyeri ey'amakumi musanvu, nja kudda n'eggye ly'omu ggulu okuteekawo Obwakabaka bwange ku Nsi, ye Bwakabaka bwa Katonda; n'obufuzi bwange obw'emyaka olukumi bujja kutandika, ye nfuga ey'obutuukirivu, obutukuvu, n'emirembe ng'empologoma egalamira wamu n'omwana gw'endiga!

Mbayita okukola emirimu gye nja okubakulemberamu ekkubo lyonna.

Mbayita okwagala Katonda n'omutima gwammwe gwonna, obulamu bwammwe bwonna, amagezi gammwe gonna, n'amaanyi gammwe gonna, n'okwagala muliraanwa wammwe nga bwe mweyagala mwekka.

Mbatuma okulagira ebitasoboka nga mutambulira mu kwagala okungi n'amaanyi amangi.

Nze bubudamo bwammwe n'ekigo kyammwe, ye Masiya wammwe ow'amazima gwemwesiga.

Mbatuma mu mirimu Kitange gye yantegekera, era bwe ntyo mbateekawo ng'abaweereza bange era bannabbi bange.

Mbatuma, era mujja kunkolera ebikolwa eby'amaanyi.

Nze nnabbi oyo Musa gwe yayogerako, eyazuukizibwa mu kika kya Yuda olw'ekigendererwa ekyo.

Ndi wa muwendo nnyo okusinga awo, kubanga mu nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, okujulira kwonna mwe kubeera.

Nze mwana gw'endiga gwa Katonda, ye Pasika etuukiridde.
