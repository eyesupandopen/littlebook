Enkola y'Okufuna Obunnabbi: Amagezi ge Nkzesa Okufuna Obubaka bw'Obunnabbi

Nga gwakubiri, Ogw'okusatu, 2024, Omukama waffe Yezu Kristu yatandika okumpa obubaka obw'obunnabbi obukwata ku kujja Kwe okuli okumpi okutwala Ekkanisa Ye n'emiramwa emirala, mbugabane n'abantu. Obunnabbi buno bwajja gye ndi mu ngeri Bayibuli gy'ennyonnyola ng'omuntu "omukono gwa Mukama" bwe guba gutuddeko. Gye ndi, kuno kuba kuwulira buzito bwa maanyi mu mwoyo gwange, nga tebugenda kuvaako okutuusa lwe ndimala okutuusa obubaka. Kristu Ateeka ebigambo oba ennyingo ntono mu birowoozo byange ze sisobola kwerabira wadde okwejjako, era emirundi mingi nneesindikira obubaka ku ssimu nneme okwerabira. Bwe ntuula ne ntandika okuwandiika, Mukama ddala Andaga Ebigambo mu mwoyo gwange nga bwe mpandiika: Yee, oluusi mubaamu okulaba Ebigambo kwennyini, ate emirundi mingi mpulira ebigambo nga bwe mpandiika, si mu ngeri ya kuwulira na matu, wabula mu mwoyo gwange, era nkiwulira mu ngeri eteganingika.

Bwe nsobya ne mpandiika ebirowoozo byange, ebigambo biyimirira okutuusa lwe nggyamu kye mbadde nnyongezzaako. Bwe nneebuusaamaanyo ne nzigyamu ennyingo Kristu gy'abadde ayagala esigale, ebigambo biyimirira okutuusa lwe nzizaayo olunyiriri nga bwe lwabadde, era olunyiriri olwo teruva mu birowoozo byange okutuusa lwe nkikola. Mu biro bino, Mukama abadde Ampa obubaka obw'enjigiriza ey'ebuziba, era abadde Antwala mu byawandiikibwa ebitali bimu. Ateeka ennyingo mu mwoyo gwange nange ne ngiwandiika; olwo ne nnoonya ennyingo eyo mu Bayibuli yange, ne nzuula nti ennyingo eyo eri mu kitundu ky'ekyawandiikibwa. Awo w'Annonkoma nti njulize ebyawandiikibwa ebyetaagisa, nange ne mbikopa ne mbiteeka mu lugero, olwo ebigambo ne byeyongerayo amangu ago. Nkyewuunya nti Ebigambo Mukama by'Ampa biba bituukagana bulungi n'ebitundu by'ebyawandiikibwa by'Ampa okujuliza, era ekyawandiikibwa kye njuliza kigenda bulungi n'olugero mu ngeri gye sisobola kutuukako nzekka. Kino sikikozeeko mu bulamu bwange, okutuusa kati. Kitera okuba nti simanya bujjuvu bwa bubaka okutuusa lwe nziramu okukebera empandiika n'obubonero bw'okuwummuza. Awo we ndibusomera nate, Mukama n'Andagira okukolamu enkyukakyuka entonotono, mpozzi okwongeramu olunyiriri olw'okunnyonnyola, mpozzi okuggyamu ekigambo n'okukizzaawo ekirala, n'ebirala. Olwo obuzito ne buvaako ne mmanya nti maze.

Mbegayiridde okimanye nti mu bubaka buno bwonna obw'obunnabbi, sikyusizzaamu kintu kyonna kikulu, n'akamu, n'omulundi n'ogumu; era tewabangawo nkyukakyuka ya maanyi mu bigendererwa, n'akamu, n'omulundi n'ogumu. Obunnabbi obuwanvuko butera okutwala essaawa ng'emu oba etannaweza okuwandiika, ate omulimu gw'okubulongoosa guyinza okwongerako essaawa endala emu oba bbiri. Byonna mbikebera n'ebyawandiikibwa okukakasa nti sirina kye noonoonye mu kutegeera kwange okw'obuntu. Okutuusa kati sikirinako, naye okuba omwerufu: Ebigambo bino bingi biba bireetere okutegeera kwange bwe mbigamba, era neetaaga okudda mu byawandiikibwa okuyiga ebisingawo okusinziira ku ekyo kye mfunye. Obubaka tebwakontana na byawandiikibwa n'akamu, newankubadde busoomooza okutegeera kwange okw'ebyawandiikibwa, era nange ndi muyizi. Kaakano, ndi muweereza era mmaze kumpi emyaka 30 mu byawandiikibwa era nkoze ennyigiriza nnyingi, n'okubuulira, n'ebirala. Ka mbabuulire kino okuva mu bunanyikivu bwange obw'emyaka: Kisoboka te nze okuwandiika ekintu kyonna ekiri ku mutendera guno n'obunuvu obuli bwe gutyo mu kiseera ekimpi bwe kityo nga nkozesa amaanyi n'okutegeera kwange. Kintwalira ennaku nnyingi, oluusi wiiki oba okusingawo, okutegeka okubuulira okuwanvu era okw'obunuvu, so nga kino kyawukana, kyawukana ddala. Kino tekiva gye ndi era kiri waggulu w'okutegeera kwange. Omwoyo gw'obunnabbi gunguddeko, kubanga Obujulizi bwa Yezu gwe mwoyo gw'obunnabbi.
