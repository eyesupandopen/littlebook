Abajulirwa Ababiri n'Okukomekkereza okw'Akaseera Obuseera Obujulirwa

Okuva eri Yakuwa, NZE ALIWO Omukulu:

Laba! Nzidde okukubuulira ku bajulirwa bange ababiri, ebikondo byange eby'ettabaaza ebibiri n'emiti gyange egy'emiziyituuni ebiri, era n'okuzibula amaaso g'abazibe b'amaaso nga mbikkulira ebyama ebyakuumibwa nga bya kyama okuva ku nsi mu magezi gange, n'olwekyo olyoke omanye obukakafu bw'ebigenda okujja, era n'ekkubo erituuka ku bulokozi liyisibwe mu ttale eri bonna abasoma era abalina okukkiriza okugondera Ebigambo bino eby'Enjiri ey'Emirembe n'Emirembe.

Laba! NZE Yakuwa, ye ogwo eyatonda eggulu n'ensi olw'ebigendererwa byange eby'emirembe n'emirembe n'ekitiibwa kyange, era NZE oyo ajja okuzzaawo ekitonde okuva mu kuvunda, n'olwekyo amakubo amatuukirivu aga Yakuwa ganaatera okujjula ensi mu butuukirivu mu maaso ga Yesu Kristo. Era bwe kityo amakubo gange gajja kusasaanira ensi mu Bwakabaka bwa Kristo Yesu obw'Emyaka Olukumi, ddala Obwakabaka omutagenda kuyingiramu kintu kyonna kibi, ddala Obwakabaka ensi mwe efugirwa mu butuukirivu, n'emirembe egya nnamaddala n'okuzzaawo ebintu bijja kujjula ensi, n'abeesigwa ab'emirembe gyonna bajja kugitambuliramu mu mirembe egy'olubeerera n'obutebenkevu kubanga amakubo ga Yakuwa gajja kubikka ku nsi mu butuukirivu. Ddala ekiseera Setaani w'anaasibirwa mu kubonyaabonyezebwa n'entiisa ey'akattiro n'okukankana olw'obusika bwe obugenda okujja obw'okubonerezebwa okw'emirembe n'emirembe, w'ajja okulabira ensolo n'omunnabbi ow'obulimba nga basuulibwa mu nnimi z'omuliro ez'okubonerezebwa okw'emirembe n'emirembe ne bookebwa, kubanga bonna basaanidde bwe batyo, naye era ajja kutuukiriza ebigendererwa byange eby'emirembe n'emirembe eby'obutuukirivu nga tannasibwa na kusuulibwa mu muliro emirembe gyonna obutaddamu kuvaayo, n'ateekawo akabonero era tebajja kuddamu kujja mu birowoozo.

Era nja kuwa (era n'okunyumirwa) okuwummula kw'olunaku lwa Ssabbiiti n'abawanguzi bange abatuukirivu n'abo bonna abakkirizibwa okuyingira mu Bwakabaka bwa Kristo Yesu obw'Emyaka Olukumi! (Kubanga emyaka olukumi giri ng'olunaku olumu mu maaso gange n'olunaku ng'emyaka olukumi, n'emyaka olukumi ye myaka olukumi.) Ddala abo bonna abanaaba bakuŋŋaanyiziddwa ne bazuukizibwa okuva mu mirembe gyonna, egya kaakano n'egya edda, mu Kristo Yesu; ddala nga Setaani tannasuulibwa mu nnimi z'omuliro, nga akaseera ke akatono ak'enkomerero tekannatuuka. Ku nkomerero yaabyo omusota ogwo omukadde gujja kusibibwa emirembe n'emirembe ne gusuulibwa mu muliro, obutaddamu kuva mu kibonerezo kye ekitakoma mu nnimi z'omuliro ezisinga okwokya eza sulfure ayaka. Kubanga Setaani n'abo bonna abagoberera obulimba bwe tebalina maanyi mu maaso gange ag'emirembe n'emirembe! Ani ayinza okuwangula Yakuwa, NZE ALIWO Omukulu, wadde nga balimba ne beewaanawaana? Oba ani ayinza okukyusa okwagala kwange okw'emirembe n'emirembe n'okulangirira kwange okw'emisango egitegekeddwa okugwa mu bujjuvu omuggyawo wange bw'aliggibwawo amangu ago kati ku ddoboozi ly'ekkondeere erirangiriddwa okufuuwebwa mu kiseera ekituufu ddala kye nnalonda mu ntegeka yange ey'emirembe n'emirembe okuva emirembe gyonna egiyise. Ddala akaseera mu budde akaali okumpi ennyo okutuukirira, ekisuubizo ekituukirivu we kisisinkanira okutuukirizibwa okw'emirembe n'emirembe mu biseera bya kaakano.

Kubanga NZE NDI, NZE ALIWO Omukulu, ye ogwo amanyi enkomerero okuva ku ntandikwa n'entandikwa okuva ku nkomerero. Kyenva njija okubikkula enjuyi z'entegeka yange ey'obulokozi ey'emirembe n'emirembe eyasigala nga ya kyama, ddala ebintu ebikyali ebikwekeddwa mu Kyama kya Katonda ekigenda okuggwaawo mu butuukirivu obw'emirembe n'emirembe. Ddala nga bwe kyawandiikibwa Yokaana Omubikkulirwa bwe yawandiika n'obwesigwa Ebigambo bye yaweebwa Kristo Yesu! Era bwe kityo kyawandiikibwa nti mu nnaku ez'eddoboozi lya Malayika ow'omusanvu, bw'alitanula okufuuwa ekkondeere, ddala n'ekyama kya Katonda kiriggwaawo, nga bwe yalangirira eri abaweereza be Bannabbi.

Naye, bangi nnyo baazibizibwa amaaso ku mazima g'Ebigambo bino abafumu ab'ebitundu abalimba abaasaasulwa engabo emmyufu okwonoona empandiika zange entukuvu n'obulimba obw'ebitundu n'obw'Abaziwini n'enjigiriza ez'okuddizaawo ez'obulimba, ddala obulimba obwaweebwa mu bukodyo abagwi okuyitira mu bantu ababi okulyazaamaanya emitima gy'abawombeefu n'abaavu. Era bajja kulyazaamaanya abalonzi bange bennyini singa abalonzi bange basalawo obutafaayo ku Bigambo byange eby'obutuukirivu mu malala nga balowooza nti tebayinza kulyazaamaanyizibwa olw'okulondebwa kwabwe. Naye bonna abasobya mu ngeri eyo nga bagaana amazima ne basalawo obulimba bwabwe bajja kulyazaamaanyizibwa. N'olwekyo, mutegeere, mmwe abantu bange, nti ekkondeere ery'omusanvu si lye kkondeere lyerimu erigenda okufuuwebwa okukomekkereza ebiseera byange eby'ekisa n'okukuŋŋaanya abeesigwa mu Kristo, kale temulyazaamaanyizibwanga!

Wabula, amazima ga Yakuwa ganywevu emirembe n'emirembe era tegayinza kukyusibwa, n'Ekigambo kyange tekiyinza kumenyebwa, n'Ekigambo kyange kijja kutuukiriza ekyo kye ŋŋenderera eri omuntu yenna Ekigambo kyange gw'ekitumirwa. So tewali n'omu ayinza kuziyiza ntegeka zange ez'emirembe n'emirembe! So tewali n'omu ayinza kuziyiza Kristo Yesu, alimu entegeka yange ey'obulokozi ey'emirembe n'emirembe mw'etuulira mu bujjuvu. Ddala oyo gwe nnawa amaanyi okusinga buli kitonde kyonna mu kitonde kyange ne mmuwa okufuga okujjuvu okufuga ekitonde kyange okutuusa abalabe be lwe balifuusibwa entebbe y'ebigere bye, mu kiseera ekyo w'aliyolekera Obwakabaka obugonzezeddwa eri Adonai Yakuwa.

Naye okuva ku mazima ag'emirembe n'emirembe, tewali nkomerero y'okufuga okutuukirivu okwa Yeshua HaMashiach, ajja okufuga mu butuukirivu emirembe gyonna, ddala n'omugole we ajja okweyongera okubeera naye emirembe n'emirembe mu bifo byabwe eby'obuyinza n'entegeka zaabwe ez'okuweereza. Era ekyo lwa kuba nti baafuna Yesu Omunazaaleeti nga Mukama waabwe ow'emirembe n'emirembe mu biseera eby'ekisa n'okusaasira, era ne bamwagala mu bujjuvu mu kugondera Ebigambo bye, ddala Ebigambo ebyo bye banaaba bawangudde byonna eby'obwakabaka bw'abalabe emirembe gyonna.

Kubanga okuva ku mazima ag'emirembe n'emirembe, omunagiro gwa Melkizeddeeki gutudde kunywevu ku bibegabega bya Yeshua HaMashiach eby'emirembe n'emirembe, obutaggibwaawo! Kubanga emirembe n'emirembe Kabaka w'Obutuukirivu ajja kufuga emirembe n'emirembe n'entegeka ye ey'Abakabona, Bakabaka, ne Bannabbi abajja okujjaza ensi amakubo amatuukirivu n'okumanya kwa Yakuwa, ddala ne mu biseera obunnabbi we bulikomera emirembe gyonna kubanga nja kussaawo ennyumba yange mu bantu mu ggulu eriggya n'ensi empya egenda okutondebwa mu butuukirivu n'obutukuvu obwa nnamaddala! Ddala ekitonde okumanya okulungi n'okubi we kulikomera emirembe gyonna n'obutuukirivu bwokka n'okwagala n'emirembe biribaawo emirembe gyonna obutaddamu kugendawo wadde okusaanuuka oba okukendeera oba okwononebwa n'akatono. N'ekirala, kubanga okuvunda kulisuulibwa mu nnimi z'omuliro emirembe n'emirembe.

Naye, bwe kityo bwe kiri, abagwi baabikka era ne basaasaanya obulimba bwabwe ku kitonde kyange ne babusaasaanya awatali kulekeraawo ku tekinologiya zaabwe ez'omuziro, n'olwekyo omuntu ow'omubiri ayinze okufugibwa n'okukozesebwa amaanyi n'amasannyalaze gaabwe ne tekinologiya zaabwe ez'omuziro. Ddala ebifaananyi ebifuga ebirowoozo n'ebikyusa empisa, engero embi, n'amalogo ebisaasaanyizibwa ku ndabirwamu zaabwe enzirugavu abangi gye batuula mu kizikiza nga bafuumitiddwa ebifaananyi byabwe n'obulimba bwabwe. Era mu mazima, abagwi baloga abantu n'obutwa ne pharmakeia ebiyisibwa ng'eddagala, n'olwekyo ne batirimbulwa ne bakendeezebwa. Naye, obutwa bwabwe ne tekinologiya zaabwe n'endabirwamu zaabwe bijja kuzikirizibwa emirembe gyonna, n'ebisawe byabwe bijja kukoma emirembe gyonna, mwe balogera ne babalumbalumba okwegomba n'okwagala kw'omuntu ow'omubiri mu kibi ekinene ne tekinologiya zaabwe ezitaliiko mugaso zijja kuggwaawo, obutaddamu kubeerawo.

Naye, bwe kityo bwe kiri, nti nnakkiriza abagwi okubikka ku kitonde kyange n'obulimba bwabwe, n'olwekyo bonna abandyagoberedde amakubo gaabwe ag'omuziro bayinze okusalawo okusigala mu busika bwabwe obw'emirembe n'emirembe obw'ekibiriiti ekyaka kubanga baasanyukira obutali butuukirivu nga boonoona Yakuwa, NZE ALIWO Omukulu, n'abatalina musango mu kitonde kyange, be baayisa obubi. Era mu magezi gange ag'emirembe n'emirembe n'okwagala kwange eri ekitonde kyange, nzikirizza buli omu okuva ku Adamu okulonda gw'ajja okuweereza: oba ekibi okutuusa okufa, oba okugondera obutuukirivu okutuusa obulamu obutaggwaawo.

Ago ge makubo ga Yakuwa, kubanga amakubo gange ge makubo g'obutuukirivu, emirembe, n'okwagala! Ddala okwagala okw'emirembe n'emirembe okutakoma, ekitonde kyange kyonna, ekirimu ebibinja by'ebitonde eby'enjawulo era ebikoleddwa mu ngeri ey'ekitalo eby'engeri n'ebigendererwa byonna, we kiribeerera mu kubeera obumu n'okwagala okutuukirivu. Kubanga ekitonde kyange kyategekebwa ng'oluwuzi olulabika obulungi olw'obulamu olufugibwa mu butuukirivu n'okwagala olw'omuganyulo n'ekitiibwa ekijjuvu ekya buli kimu ku bitonde byange, awatali kitonde na kimu ekijja okuganyulwa ku lwa munne okufiirwa, naye byonna bijja kugabana ku bungi obutakoma obuli mu mawanika gange obwategekerwa omuganyulo n'ekitiibwa ky'abo abagondera amateeka gange amatuukirivu okuva mu mitima gyabwe mu kwagala Kitabwe ow'emirembe n'emirembe, oyo ddala, NZE NDI!

Naye, bwe kityo bwe kiri, nti NZE NDI NZE ALIWO Omukulu era siwagira wadde okukkiriziganya n'obulimba bwabwe, so sijja kukkiriza bulimba bwabwe kweyongera. Kale bwe kityo, ntegeeza, nti nze, Yakuwa, NZE ALIWO Omukulu ali Katonda Ataggwaawo ow'Obutuukirivu nja kwanika mu bujjuvu buli ludda lwonna olw'obwakabaka bw'abalabe obugenda okugoloka mu kaseera akatono ennyo ogusota lwe guweereddwa okufuga, era gujja kuba n'okufuga okukomye ennyo. Era mu ngeri y'emu, nja kwanika abagwi abeekweka mu kitonde kyange, abagenda okusimbulibwawo n'okwanikibwa mu nsonyi zaabwe ez'emirembe n'emirembe mu bujjuvu nga tebannasuulibwa mu nnimi z'omuliro ez'okubonerezebwa emirembe gyonna, olusiriŋŋanyi lwabwe gye lutafa. Era bwe kityo okulangirira kwange kujja kugenda mu maaso, kubanga ekyo ekirangiriddwa mu kitiibwa n'amaanyi g'entebe ya Yakuwa, ddala NZE ALIWO Omukulu, tekiyinza kukyusibwa, era kijja kutuukirira ddala nga bwe kyalangirirwa. Sela!

Naye, bwe kityo bwe kiri, nti sirikwekangako bagwi mu bujjuvu, era ntandise dda okubaanika mu bujjuvu n'olutimbe luno lugenda luyuzibwa. Ddala olutimbe olwo olw'okukweka lwe nnabakwekamu mu maaso g'abantu, n'omuggyawo ajja kuggyibwawo ogulimu obubonero obw'enjawulo obw'okuziyiza obuliggibwawo mu bujjuvu Mikayiri bw'aliweebwa ekiragiro ekkondeere ery'enkomerero bwe lirivuga amangu ago kati. Era bwe gutyo omuzira guligwa, n'ensi ejja kukyusibwa, era bajja kuba n'olutalo lwabwe olunene n'abangi bajja kuzikirira, ddala nga bwe kyawandiikibwa era ne kiragulwa Yokaana Omubikkulirwa mu bujulirwa bwe obw'emirembe n'emirembe. Era bwe ntyo mbawa okutegeera nti mu kiseera eky'okukuŋŋaanyizibwa kw'abeesigwa mu Kristo nja kuggyawo olutimbe olwo olw'okukweka okutonotono mu ngeri ey'amangu ddala era ey'amaanyi nga bwe nnayuzamu olutimbe lwa yeekaalu wakati okuva waggulu okutuuka wansi ekyalaga enkomerero y'okuweereza kwa Leevi n'okuzzaawo okujjuvu kwa Melkizeddeeki. N'olwekyo ndyoke ngyewo obuzibe bw'amaaso obutonotono ku baana ba Isirayiri emirembe gyonna, obutaddamu kukomawo ku mayiba gange.

Era kino kijja kubaawo mu bbanga ttono era mu ngeri etasuubirwa kubanga ensi tesuubira Kristo Yesu okwakaayakanya ebyeggulu n’eggulu lyennyini n’ekitiibwa Kye eky’emirembe gyonna mu biseera bino eby’okulumwa okw’enkomerero; era bwe kityo, kubanga ddala ensi tebagikkiriza. Era ku abo abaligaanibwa okuyingira mu ggulu omulyango ogwo omunene bwe guliggalawo, bangi ku bo ababi ennyo balizikirira era balitwalibwa mu musango ogw’emirembe gyonna, wadde abo abaagaana Yesu Omunnazaaleeti mu bujjuvu mu kuvvoola okungi n’amalala eri omutego Gwange omutukuvu, n’abalala bangi abali n’ekibi kye kimu mu kugaana kwabwe okw’obujeemu abaligwa emirembe gyonna mu musango omukakali olw’ebibi bye baakola. Wadde ebibi by’ebyo byennyini bye beeyongera okukola mu kwewaayo okungi, so tebaliwona.

Mutegeere nti bonna abanoonya okumenya ensalo n’entegeka z’obutonde Bwange ez’obutonde nga basala ensalo n’entegeka ezo, nga banunula n’okwenunula okufuuka emizizo (mu makubo g’abagwi) nabo bennyini baligwa mu musango ogw’emirembe gyonna: Kubanga kino kye kibi ekisonyiwibwa, wadde okuvoola Mukama Katonda ali Omwoyo Omutukuvu, okunoonya okwekyusa okuva mu kifaananyi kya Mukama Katonda kye baatondebwa olubereberye. N’olwekyo, mwegaane enfumo zaabwe ez’Abayesuwiiti ezaakyusa Obwakatonda obw’amazima ne zifuuka enfumo ezaleetebwa Nimuloodi n’abasatu be abatali batukuvu ab’olubereberye! Kubanga abasatu abatukuvu aba Kitaffe, Omwana, n'omwoyo omutukuvu baayonooneddwa okuva ku makulu gaabyo ag’olubereberye n’ekigendererwa, naye amazima ga Mukama Katonda tegayinza kukwekebwa bintu binafu era eby’obwavu wadde nga birimba! Naye, Yesu Omunnazaaleeti ye Mukama Katonda eyeyoleka mu mubiri, era omwoyo gwange omutukuvu ogw'Obwana gujjuza mu bujjuvu abeesigwa mu Kristo abali ddala ekitundu ky'Obwakatonda mu kufuga kwabwe ne Kristo Yesu wansi w’Entegeka ya Merukizeddeeki nga Bakabona n’Abakabaka emirembe gyonna! N’olwekyo, mutegeere kyemuli mu Kristo mmwe abaana bange kubanga abagudde banoonya okukweka amazima agabakwatako n’enfumo zaabwe ezitali za mazima.

Naye, abeesigwa mu Kristo babeereddewo era baafa era bakyali ku maaso g’ensi mu kukkiriza okungi, era akaseera kano ak’ekitalo era ak’ekitiibwa tekayinza kubatuukako mu butamanya kubanga balitegeera okwogerera kw’oluyoogaano olwo olunene, era balitegeera eddoboozi ly’ekkondeere erisembayo erimaze okugabibwa mu ngalo n’ebiragiro ebijjuvu okufuuwa ekiragiro bwe kiriweebwa Kristo Yesu, bulijjo akola mu bujjuvu bya Adonai Mukama Katonda by’ayagala. Abeesigwa mu Kristo balikkirizibwa okuyingira mu luggi olwo olunene okuyingira mu ggulu omuntu yenna ku nsi tayinza kuggalawo wadde okuggulawo, oba nga beebase oba balamu ku maaso g’ensi mu kiseera ky’okuyitibwa kwabwe!

Era okubonaabona okungi kulituuka ku batali beesigwa abaayatula Yesu Omunnazaaleeti okuva mu mutima ogutali mwesigwa, nga bagaana n’okubeera obulamu obw’okwenenya kubanga baayagala ebibi byabwe. Kirituuka nti abo abaalondawo okweyongera mu kibi, abo abaayatula Yesu nga Mukama naye ne beeyongera mu bikolwa by’omubiri, balirekebwa omuliro ogulongoosa olw’okuzikiriza omubiri, n’olwekyo balyoke bawone essanyu ly’ekibi era balyoke bawone omubiri gwabwe ogubasibye mu kwegomba kwagwo. Era bangi balijja mu bulokozi nga bakoowoola Yesu Omunnazaaleeti okutuusa ku mukka gwabwe ogusembayo, kubanga bulijjo kyetaagibwa eri abeesigwa, omuntu okusangibwa nga mwesigwa okutuusa ku mukka gwe ogusembayo, singa taba omu ku batono ennyo abatwalibwa nga balamu mu bulamu obw’omu ggulu mu biseera byabwe eby’obwesigwa. Seera!

Era olutalo lwabwe luliryoka lutabaala era lweyongere okumala ekiseera kyalyo ekigereke era okuva mu vvu ly’okuzikirizibwa kwabwe mwe baligolokokera. Ogusota, ensolo eva mu nnyanja n’ensolo eva mu nsi naye ye nabbi waabwe ow’obulimba zirigolokoka mu kuvvoola okungi okuba n’okufuga kwabyo okw’obukambwe naye okw’ekisa okumpi. Naye, Mukama Katonda, Nze Aliwo Omukulu, talekerangawo mujulirwa, nga bwe kyategeezebwa ekibinja ky’abajulirwa mu mpandiika Zange ez’emirembe n’emirembe era entukuvu ezaakuumibwa mu bujjuvu mu ggulu. Era nga bwe kiri, obujulizi bwange bungi bwasaanawo olw'ebiseera n'obutafaayo bw'abo abaweebwa obuvunaanyizibwa bw'okubulabirira. Mutegeere ebiro mmwe abaana bange era mwegaane obulimba bwabwe obw'obulimba obw'okugabanyaamu. Mutegeere nti obutonde tebukaddiye myaka bukadde na bukadde okusinziira ku bulimba bw'abagwi. Obutonde buweza emyaka nga kanaana era ebiro bino mbigabyeemu emirembe. Waaliwo emirembe gy'akavuyo olw'abagwi abaayonoona ensi eyaliwo mu kiseera ekyo n'emizizo gyabwe, era emizizo egyo gyazikirizibwa mu bujjuvu mu musango ogwaggweerako amataba amanene mu nnaku za Nuuwa.

Naye, Nuuwa yasigalawo ng’akyali mulamu mu mataba, n’awonyezebwa ng’akyali mulamu ku lyato wamu n’ezzadde lye (n’ensolo n’ebitonde) ebyaweebwa ekifo okweyongera kubanga Nuuwa yali musajja mutuukirivu era omufuzi omutuukirivu oluvannyuma lw’Entegeka ya Merukizeddeeki, eyali ku bibegabega bye omwambalo ne guwummula okutuusa omuweereza wange lwe yagenda mu kuwummula kwe okw’emirembe okutuusa lwe yazuukusibwa mangu nnyo kati mu butuukirivu. Mu kiseera kye, Musa yaggyibwawo ng’akyali mulamu mu mazzi g’omugga Kiyira, n’agolokoka n’amaanyi mangi n’aleeta emirembe gyange egy’amateeka, wadde Isiraeri lwe yafuulibwa eggwanga eddene era ery’amaanyi ekiri ekisiikirize ky’ebintu ebigenda okujja.

Era mu kiseera ekituufu era ekituufu ekyategekebwa Yesu Omunnazaaleeti yazaalibwa omuwala embeerera n’agolokoka n’afuuka omwana gw’endiga gwa Katonda, n’ewaayo ye yennyini okuba ettaulo ery’emirembe gyonna olw’ekibonerezo kya Musa, era ye ssaddaaka gye nnakkiriza emirembe gyonna olw’okuggibwawo kw’ebibi emirembe n’emirembe, eri bonna abatukuzibwa olw’ekyo, wadde mu Ndagaano ye Empya ey’obutuukirivu Kristo Yesu gye yakakasa n’agiteekako akabonero emirembe gyonna mu musaayi gwe ogutaliiko kibi, n’olwekyo, tewakyali kwetaaga kwa ssaddaaka ya nsolo. Mazima, Yesu Omunnazaaleeti ye ntabiro y’obutuukirivu mu biseera byonna, era ye kaseera mu biseera akaaggweera mu kuggyibwawo kw’abagwi emirembe gyonna, era ye kaseera akakwatibwa n’ebigambo eby’emirembe gyonna nti, “KIWEDDE!”
