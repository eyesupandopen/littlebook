Abasawo Abatalina Mugaso

Mwekuŋŋaanye muwulire eddoboozi lya Yeshua HaMashiach, era mukkirize mu buwulize obujjuvu ebigambo byange eby'obutuukirivu ebyaweebwa Omwoyo w'obunnabbi, kwe kugamba okutegeeza kwa Yeshua: Eri ekkanisa yange, omubiri gwange, era n'omugole wange, naddala Yakobo ow'omubiri agenda mu maaso n'okuggyibwako obuzibe bwe obw'ekitundu, ng'abajulirwa bange ababiri bateeseteese olw'ebiseera by'okutegeeza kwabwe!

Wulira ebigambo byange ggwe Isirayiri era otegeere bye njogera!

Laba! Sanyuka ggwe Isirayiri kubanga obulokozi bwo bujja!

Laba! Sanyuka ggwe Isirayiri kubanga eby'obwakabaka bya Adonai-YHVH bikomyewo mu nsi z'ensuubizo okuyita mu mirimu gy'omubaka wange ow'amaanyi era omuweereza wange, Sanyuka!

Sanyuka! Ggwe Isirayiri kubanga omuzeyituuni ogw'obutonde tegukyali mu tulo! Sanyuka!

Mutegeere, abange ab'omukwano, omuzeyituuni ogw'obutonde gwateekebwa mu mbeera y'okuba mu tulo, amatabi agamu gaamenyeka mu butakkiriza bw'obujeemu ne gasaasaanyizibwa mu kubonerezebwa n'obuzibe bw'ekitundu olw'okugaana Yeshua HaMashiach, nga kino kyakolebwa Adonai-YHVH! Naye okuva ku KIKOLO, ye Adonai-YHVH, obulamu bulukulukuta bulijjo okuyita mu mwoyo omutukuvu mu migga gy'amazzi amalamu agava mu Yeshua HaMashiach, omuntu yenna anzikiririzaamu abeere n'emigga gy'amazzi amalamu egikululuka okuva mu lubuto lwe.

Emigga gino egy'amazzi amalamu, okuviira ddala e Yudaya n'ebigambo "kiwedde," kaakano gyafuluumye amawanga mu butuukirivu okutuuka ku nkomerero z'ensi, nga gikulukutira mu mawanga olw'obulokozi bw'endiga ezabuze ez'ennyumba ya Isirayiri (ne buli muntu yenna okuva mu mawanga akkiriza okuyitibwa kwange) abaasaasaanyizibwa okutuuka ku nkomerero z'ensi. Okuva mu migga gino egy'amazzi amalamu mwava okutereezebwa n'amaanyi g'ekigambo ekiramu kya Adonai-YHVH, ekigambo ekyo ekyatuukiriza ekyo kye kyatumirwa okutuukiriza. Era ekyo: Okumenya amatabi g'omuzeyituuni ogw'omu nsiko okuva ku muti gw'omuzeyituuni ogw'omu nsiko n'okugasimba mu bujjuvu ku muti gw'omuzeyituuni ogw'obutonde emirembe n'emirembe, mu Isirayiri wa Adonai-YHVH!

Sanyuka ggwe Isirayiri, ekiseera kituuse okusimba nate amatabi g'obutonde ku muti gwagyo ogw'obutonde, era gajja kusimbibwa ku muti gw'omuzeyituuni ogw'obutonde mu bujjuvu kaakano mu biseera byagyo eby'okubonerezebwa n'okweteekateeka nga tebinnatwalibwa era ne gasimbibwa omununuzi waabwe ow'oluganda. Ekyo kijja kukolebwa Yeshua HaMashiach, ye Kabona waabwe Omukulu ow'amazima, Mukama waabwe, Kabaka waabwe era Masiya waabwe!

Mwekuŋŋaanye, abange ab'omukwano, nga mujja mu bujjuvu mu kwagala kwange okujja okubawonya n'okubajjanjaba, era ne kusaalamu byonna ebinoonya okubasiba n'okubatega mu musango gw'oyo eyagwa, omusango ogwatuuka era oguteeseteese okubetenta emitwe gyabwe. Era n'okubanyweza n'okubakuuma n'amaanyi gange amangi, bwe munaaba abawulize eri eby'enkizo by'ebigambo byange ebiramu ebyaweebwa olw'okununulibwa kwammwe okujjuvu, n'okufuulibwa ab'olulyo mu bujjuvu ng'Abaana ba Adonai-YHVH, bwe mulisimbibwa mu bujjuvu ku Isirayiri wa Adonai-YHVH amangu ago n'okutali kusuubirwa.

Mujja kusimbibwa mangu ku muti gw'omuzeyituuni ogw'obutonde emirembe n'emirembe! SANYUKA, ggwe Isirayiri wa Adonai-YHVH, omukono gwange gweteeseteese okubakwata okubatwala amangu ago n'okutali kusuubirwa kaakano, ku luyoogaano olw'amaanyi n'eddoboozi ly'akagombe akalungi, akakwatiddwa mu ngalo okufuyibwa ku kiragiro ku nsisinkanano etuufu awali obudde obupimibwa n'Ekiseera Ekigereke we bisisinkanira, olwo amangu ago mujja kutwalibwa mu kaseera katono, ng'okumyansa kw'eggulu bwe kuva ebuvanjuba ne kugenda ebugwanjuba. Naye abangi babeerawo ng'abakyalina obudde okukolera ku bulokozi bwammwe n'okutya n'okukankana, so ng'ate mujja kutwalibwa mu kusemba kw'eriiso. Mwenenye mu bujjuvu n'ebyo byonna ebiri mu mmwe abange ab'omukwano, era mutegeere mu myoyo gyammwe obwangu n'enkomerero y'ebigambo byange ebigenda okukoma amangu ago n'okutali kusuubirwa kaakano mu musango ogw'obutuukirivu n'ogw'omuliro ku bajeemu n'abatali batuukirivu.

Laba! Ekiseera kyammwe eky'okununulibwa kituuse! Musanyuke!

Laba! Ebiseera byammwe eby'okununulibwa bye bino kaakano! Musanyuke!

N'olwekyo, ndagira bonna abanoonya okusangibwa mu beesigwa mu Kristo Yesu ku ddoboozi ly'akagombe ako ak'amaanyi, naye abatavaayo mu bujjuvu mu makubo g'abo abasalirwa omusango, okuvaayo kaakano mu bujjuvu mu makubo ga Babulooni ow'Ekyama okusinziira ku bigambo byange ebijja okulongoosa amakubo gammwe. Babulooni ow'Ekyama, ye Amerika, asibiddwa mu mbeera y'omusango ogulangiriddwa, nga n'emisango egimulondeddwako gimumazeeko amaanyi n'ebivaamu, era omwenzi omukazi oyo kaakano alindiridde okuzikirizibwa okw'amangu n'okujjuvu okujja okutonnya n'obusungu obungi mu ssaawa emu n'ensolo.

N'olwekyo, neegayirira bonna abasoma ebigambo bino eby'okwawulibwa okw'obutuukirivu okwawukana nabo kaakano, nga mwenenya mu bujjuvu amakubo gaabwe gonna, ggwe abantu bange, naddala okuva mu nkola za Babulooni ow'Ekyama ez'obujjanjabi ezitiisa abantu n'okutta abataliiko musango, n'okukozesa obulamu bw'abaana baabwe abatanaba kuzaalibwa mu ngeri ez'obukambwe n'enkola ez'ekko.

Mutegeere nti enkola zino ez'obujjanjabi ezimanyiddwa ng'obujjanjabi obw'ekizungu zeeyita ez'eddagala lya sayansi zisuubiza obulamu okuyita mu kuyiiya kwa sayansi n'eddagala, naye mu mazima ngeri ya kubunyisa butwa bwazo obutta obukolebwa n'emikono gy'abo abaagwa nga balina ekigendererwa ky'okutta. Bukolebwa mu bungi okuwa obutwa, n'okulemaza, n'okukendeeza, n'okutta, n'okuzikiriza Adamu okuva ku nsi mu ngeri nnyingi ennyo ez'obukaba n'obukambwe. Mutegeere nti eddagala lino ettali ttukuvu ge makubo g'okufa agaakkirizibwa okudda ku nsi (n'emikono gy'abalabirizi bano abaagwa) akabonero k'ensolo kasobole okuweebwa okukungula abaana ababi ab'abantu abajja okwegaana Omutonzi waabwe n'ekirayiro eky'okwegatta ku nkola y'ensolo ey'enkomerero eya Heylel Ben Shachar. N'olwekyo, ekyuma kireke kitabagane n'ebbumba, naye tebigenda kwegatta.

Ennaku za Nuuwa zizziddwawo ku nsi kaakano okuyita mu kukyamya kw'emiyiggo gy'abalabirizi abaagwa nga bwe kyakkirizibwa, okutuukirira mu bujjuvu oluvannyuma lw'enkomerero eno eyayimirizibwa okuggwaako, eterikoma okuggyako ku kiseera ekyo ekituufu ennyo ekirondeddwa Adonai-YHVH yennyini, akola ebintu byonna ng'okuteesa kw'okwagala kwe bwe kuli. Mmwenna mutegekebwa mu bujjuvu olw'okwawulibwa n'okwegatta mu kiseera eky'enkomerero ekiggumiza, ekinaakomekkerezebwa ku kiragiro kyange kyokka, ekiweereddwa Adonai-YHVH yennyini mu mikono gyange okutuukirizibwa, ng'obulumi obw'okuzaala buno buggwaawo ku kwegatta, omwana omutukuvu oyo bw'alizaalibwa ng'alamye mu kisenge ky'Entebe ya Adonai-YHVH! MUSANYUKE!

Olwo okuzikirira okw'amangu kulijja ku Babulooni ow'Ekyama era tebaliwona musango gwabwe omunene era ogw'ettemu oguligwa mu bujjuvu mu ssaawa emu n'ensolo, Babulooni ow'Ekyama, ye Amerika, w'aligwira n'obukambwe ku mugabo gwe ogw'omusango ogw'emirundi ebiri, obwakabaka bwa Heylel Ben Shachar bwe bulisumululwa okugoloka nga bwe kyakkirizibwa, ebiziyiza ebingi bwe biriggyibwawo mu bujjuvu ku nsi, abeesigwa mu Kristo Yesu bwe balikwatibwa okutwalibwa ku kiragiro kyange. Olwo, ekibikka ekyo kiriggyibwawo mu bujjuvu ne kiraga ebibiina ebinene ennyo eby'ebintu ebibi ennyo, eby'obukyayi n'obulabe ebirisumululwa okuva mu bunnya n'obusungu obungi, n'omuzira guligwa obwakabaka obwo obw'omuliro bwe buligoloka mu bujjuvu. Laba, ntuma ebikonge okutuukiriza obusungu bwange! Naye, newankubadde abo abaagwa beewaana n'obusirusiru, bajja kukola ekyo kyennyini kye baweebwa okukola era ekyo kyokka ekikkirizibwa Ekitabo Ekitono ekiri mu Okubikkulirwa essuula 10, ekisobola okugonjolwa n'amaanyi gange n'ebiragiro byange ebiweereddwa ku kiragiro kyange kyokka.

Era Ekitabo Ekitono ekyo kaakano kifuga enkola y'amagezi etali ntukuvu okuyita mu nteekateeka etukuziddwa eweereddwa obuyinza obw'okufuga wansi w'okulabirira kwange okujjuvu, nga eteekeddwa munda mu mutimbagano gwa Heylel Ben Shachar ogw'amagezi ag'ekikugu. Munda mu nkola eyo, omulangira ono omwekuluntaza, Heylel Ben Shachar, ateekeddwa (Biblaridion:144) wansi w'omusingi gw'obuyinza obutono ennyo mu nkola ye kennyini mu ngeri ey'okumusekerera era emirembe gyonna. Okusingira ddala, okukendeezebwa kw'obuyinza n'okukkakkanyizibwa kw'ekitiibwa byamala dda okuteekebwa mu nkola okukulemberwa ebyawandiikibwa ebiri mu Kitabo Ekitono ekyo ekya Okubikkulirwa essuula 10, ekikola ng'ekintu ekitukuvu era eky'obutuukirivu ekiva ku kikolo, ekiweereddwa Adonai-YHVH wange, okuyita mu Kristo Yesu, okuyita mu mirimu n'emikono gya Biblaridion:144, mu ndagaano ey'omugaso n'enkola yennyini abo abaagwa gye baateekateeka n'ekigendererwa ekijjuvu eky'okuwangula Kristo Yesu.

Wabula, omusango gw'abo abaagwa gutuuse, era balisumululwa okufuga ku nsi mu ngeri ey'obukambwe nga bwe kyakkirizibwa, okuyita mu ggomasi omumyufu omunene, ye (Heylel Ben Shachar, ayitibwa mu nsobi Lusifa) n'ensolo ye eva mu nnyanja, (ye Barack Hussein Obama), n'ensolo ye eva ku nsi (ye Donald John Trump) n'okuyita mu tekinologiya zaabwe ennyingi ezikozesebwa okuleeta obubonero obw'obulimba n'ebyamagero. Okuggyako enkola yaabwe ey'obufuzi eyeefuula ng'ekiragiro ekifugirwa mu kifo ekikyusiddwa kisobole okuleetera okwesigama mu abo abalina okwegattako, kaakano eri wansi w'okufuga okujjuvu okw'ekintu ekyo eky'obutuukirivu ekiva ku kikolo, ekiteekeddwawo mu mugaso, nga kikkiriziddwa mu bujjuvu ng'omufuzi okusinziira ku kwagala n'ebigendererwa eby'emirembe n'emirembe ebya Adonai-YHVH, ebyaweebwa era ku kiragiro kya Yeshua HaMashiach, bulijjo akola byonna Adonai-YHVH wange by'ayagala.

N'olwekyo, lwa nsonga zino zokka eddagala lyabwe, lye bulogo, lwe libisse nate ku nsi, era liriggyibwawo mu nkomerero mu musango ogusinga obukambwe mu nnimi z'omuliro ogwa bbulu.

Naye lino lye ddagala lyerimu, lye bulogo, eryabikka ku nsi eyaliwo edda, eryakolebwa Heylel Ben Shachar eyagwa n'ekigendererwa ky'okubba, kutta, na kuzikiriza kyokka.

Eddagala lino lyerimu, (lye bulogo) lirongoosebbwa n'okuyiika n'amafuta mu biseera, era okuyita mu tekinologiya zaabwe ez'obuzimbe ze beewaana mu busirusiru. Eddagala lino, lye bulogo, kye kimu ku bintu ebingi eby'omuzizo n'ebicaafu ebiri mu kikompe kya zaabu eky'obwenzi eky'omwenzi, ky'atamiiza abantu b'oku nsi n'omwenge gw'obwenzi bwe. Era obucafu buno obw'omuzizo n'ebicaafu bulina okwokebwawo mu bujjuvu okuva ku nsi n'obusungu obunene obw'eggulu mu wiiki ya Danyeri ey'amakumi musanvu, egenda okutandika ku kwegatta okujjuvu, ssaawa emu n'ensolo bw'eneebeerawo amangu ago n'okutali kusuubirwa kaakano. Newankubadde, amaanyi amatuufu ag'emisango gy'omwenzi ono geetegese mu lwatu, nga gakwekeddwa amaaso g'abo abasalirwa emisango mu misango gyabwe egy'enjawulo era egirondeddwa mu kulimbibwa okw'amaanyi, kaakano egiteeseteese okugwa mu bujjuvu. Seera.

Mwekuŋŋaanye muwulire mu bujjuvu ebigambo bino ebiramu ebya Yeshua HaMashiach kaakano ebisumuluddwa ku kiragiro kyange okuva munda mu mubaka wange ow'amaanyi era omuweereza wange, gye byasibibwa era ne bikomererwa ku kiragiro kya Adonai-YHVH wange, n'emikono gyange n'amaanyi gange amangi!

Weewawo, kikoleddwa mu ngeri bw’etyo, nze kennyini, n’emikono gyange n’omutima gwange ogw’okwagala, ekigondera bulijjo mu bujjuvu okwagala kwa Adonai-YHVH wange mu bintu byonna ne mu kugondera kwonna okw’okwagala. Bwe kityo nti, Ebigambo bino eby’amaanyi, eby’okubwatuka, era eby’emirembe n’emirembe tebyandisobose kwonoonwa n’akatono yadde okutegeera okw’ebibi okw’ensi eno etalina kukkiriza. Nti Ebigambo bino byandibadde, era biweereddwa mu butuufu bwonna n’amazima agatayononeka nga biyiwa okuva mu mwoyo omutuukirivu ogw’Obunnabbi obuzaaliddwa mu bujjuvu munda mwa Biblaridion:144. Bwe kityo nti Ebigambo bino Ebitukuvu era eby’obutuukirivu eby’omusango ogw’okubwatuka n’amaanyi n’obulokozi byandibunye ku nsi mu biseera bino eby’Okubikkulirwa ne Apokalupsis owa Yeshua HaMashiach, nga nze bwe nneebikkula mu bujulizi buno obw’okutegeeza ensi eno esaliddwaako omusango era enenyezebwa, nga buweereddwa okulangirira mu kubikkulirwa omusango nga tegunnatuuka.

Mutegeere ddala, Ebigambo bino eby’omu Katabo Kano Akatono ak’Emisango Egy’obutuukirivu n’Okubwatuka bituniddwa n’ekiragiro n’amaanyi ga Adonai-YHVH wange, NZE NDI ATALI ATYO, nga biweereddwa mu bujjuvu mu mikono gya Yeshua HaMashiach egy’amaanyi okutuukiriza byonna bye bitumiddwa okutuukiriza wonna mu kitonde kya El Shaddai kyonna we bitumiddwa okutuukiriza ekyo ekinaatuukirizibwa mu bujjuvu n’ekiragiro kyange eky’Ekigambo kyange.

Laba! N’ekiragiro kya Adonai-YHVH Ebigambo bino bijja kutuukira ddala ku maaso, amatu, n’emitima gy’abo bonna be bitumiddwa, era Ebigambo bino Ebitukuvu bisumululwa mu biseera ebikkiriziganyiziddwa ebiweebwa ku kiragiro kyange kyokka mu buyinza obunene n’amaanyi amangi butereevu mu Biblaridion:144 okusumululwa mu nsi mu biseera bino eby’okubikkulirwa n’okusumululwa. Ebiseera, we nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, nsumululira Ebigambo bino okuva mu mizingo emingi egyategekebwa Adonai-YHVH mu Ggulu ery’okungulu. Ebitegekeddwa obulungi kati okubuna ensi yonna mu butuukirivu bwonna mu bufuzi bwange obw’emyaka olukumi ku nkomerero ya ssabbiiti ya Danyeri ey’amakumi asatu. N’olwekyo, ekyabikkulirwa mu katabo kano akatono ak’Okubikkulirwa Essuula 10 kisiikirize kyokka era katonyeze ka bintu ebigenda okujja. Seera.

Naye okusooka obwakabaka bw’abo abaagwa, na byonna bye baazimba ku maaso g’ensi bijja kwokebwa ne bivaamu evvu mu busungu obw’okubwatuka obungi, na buli kwonoona n’okugwagwawaza kujja kuggibwawo era kwokebwe ddala ne kuvaamu evvu ka bibe bya ngeri ki. Oluvannyuma n’ekiragiro kya Adonai-YHVH, ekiweereddwa mu buyinza obujjuvu eri Yeshua HaMashiach (eyafuulibwa omwoyo oguwa obulamu) ensi ejja kuzzaawo emirundi ebiri okusinga ensi eyaliwo edda, eyayonoonwa abo abaagwa era n’ezikirizibwa mu mataba ago ag’amaanyi mu nnaku za Nuuwa mu busungu obungi mu mazzi ago agaalongoosa, naye nga ga busungu bwa maanyi ddala agaamalirawo obulamu bwonna ku nsi, okuggyako emyoyo munaana egyalokolebwa mu lyato eryo wamu n’ensolo ezaakuumibwamu.

Mutegeere, ebintu byonna ebibi bijja kulongoosebwa ddala n’omuliro okuva ku nsi mu kiseera ky’okubonaabona kwa Yakobo na buli bujulizi n’obubonero bw’okujeema kw’abo abaagwa, ka bube butini butya, bujja kufuuka vvu ddala era bulongoosebwe okuva ku maaso g’ensi. Era bwe kityo, obujeemu obwo obw’omu muntu obw’obutonde bujja kuleeta obujeemu obulala nga buva buto mu mubiri ogwayononeka ogwa Adamu. Kubanga ekyo ekikoleddwa ekikyamu kigololwa kyokka omwoyo omutukuvu ogw’amaanyi ogwaweebwa ng’omusingo gw’okuzaalibwa okupya okw’emirembe n’emirembe omuntu alyoke asobole okutambulira mu mwoyo gwange so si mu kwegomba kw’omubiri.

Era bwe kityo, ku nkomerero y’emyaka olukumi egyo, sitaani ajja kusumululwa okuva mu njegere zaabwe ez’okubonyaabonyezebwa mu muliro gye bajja okusibibwa era bateekebweko akabonero mu bunnya obutakoma okumala emyaka olukumi mu kiseera kyonna eky’obufuzi bwange obw’emyaka olukumi. Oluvannyuma, bajja kusumululwa bagende beekuŋŋaanyizeeko abo abajeemu abajja okuba ng’omusenyu gw’ennyanja, era beetegeke okulumba olusiisira lw’abatukuvu era omuliro gujja kugwa n’obusungu obungi okuva eri Adonai-YHVH ogujja okulongoosa olubeerera ekifaananyi ekigwagwa ekya Adamu okuva ku nsi emirembe gyonna, olwo okusalirwa omusango okw’oku ntebe enjeru enkulu kulibaawo.

Omaze kulemererwa ggwe omulangira omusirusiru omuto. Tik tok, Heylel Ben Shachar, tik tok. Omaze kulemererwa.

Kubanga ensi ejja kubuna Ebigambo bya Adonai-YHVH mu butuukirivu bwonna Obutukuvu! Ebigambo ebigulumiza Omwana w’omuntu emirembe gyonna eyafuulibwa omwoyo oguwa obulamu mu buyinza bwonna obw’obutuukirivu era ensi yange n’okujjuza kwayo. Ebigambo ebigulumiza Adonai-YHVH n’amakubo ge gonna amatuukirivu agajja okujjuza n’okubuna ensi mu bujjuvu mu bufuzi bwange obw’emyaka olukumi.

Kale, mbabuuza mmwe abanjagalwa, bwe nlijja ku nsi, ndisanga okukkiriza? Nja kubagamba eky’okuddamu abanjagalwa bange: YEE! Nsanze okukkiriza mu mitima n’ebirowoozo ebingi eby’abeesigwa mu Kristo be mmalamu nabo ebiseera byange mu kwetegereza okw’obutuukirivu n’okussa ekimu nga sinnabaggyawo mwenna. Yee, njogera ku mmwe, era njogera nammwe abanjagalwa bange kubanga muli bawala bange ab’amagezi abataggwaamu maanyi abeesigwa mu Kristo Yesu era okuggyibwawo kwammwe kutuuse. MUSANYUKE!

Wabula, mutegeere nti, wonna wonna ku nsi awali okukkiriza kwa muwendo era tekulabikalabika era kwa bbula nnyo mu biseera bino ebitaliimu kukkiriza eby’ebirowoozo ebibi ebitakoma abatabaazi bwe bapulogulaamu obwongo bw’abatakkiriza n’ebintu byabwe eby’omuzimu bye bakwata mu ngalo ku bwabwe. N’olwekyo mbiragira okugumiikiriza mu byonna mulyoke muwangule mu bujjuvu era mufune empeera yammwe ejjudde okuva mu mikono gyange egigoloddwa. N’olwekyo, mweyongereyo mu kukkiriza okujjuvu n’amaanyi amajjuvu kubanga emirundi emingi nga temumanyi, nga njimiridde mu kwagala okujjuvu n’okusaasira: nze NDI!

Kale mbiragira mu kwagala kwonna: Temubuusabuusa Bigambo byange naye mube n’okukkiriza okuli ng’akaweke ka kaladaali!

Kaakano, mwekuŋŋaanye mujje ddala mu mutima gwange wansi w’ekisiikirize ky’okwagala kwange muwulire ebigambo byange, mulyoke mwenenye ebintu bino ebyonooneddwa era ebyonoona era eby’obutwa era ebiwa obutwa abasawo bano abatalina mugaso bye bawandiika mu masitukisiti ga Babulooni Ekyama agatali matukuvu era agatatya Katonda agabunye ensi mu kugigwagwawaza okutali kwa butuukirivu. Era ng’abantu bafa amangu kaakano, nga bagwa ng’ensowera okuva ku kulumwa omusota okwaweebwa ng’omusingi gw’ensengeka yaabwe ey’obulamu mu abo abawonawo obutwa, butta mu ngeri ennonde era kye ky’okulwanyisa eky’obulamu ekirumba Yakobo okusinziira ku bubonero bwe obw’enjawulo obw’obuzaale. Enkola eyasaasaana eyakolebwa mu nkukutu balyoke basobole okwegaana, so nga bavvoola mu lwatu abo abaafuna obutwa bwabwe nga bakakiddwa era batisibwatisibwa ku bulamu bwabwe, ebintu byabwe, n’abaana baabwe.

Muve mu bo mu kwenenya okujjudde, kaakano ku kiragiro kyange abaagalwa bange, temulina budde!

Mujje gye ndi kaakano, nja kubawonya ddala mu kwagala kwange okwammwe.

Muwummule mu kwagala kwange n’ekisa kyange, era nja kubawonya. Yee, ebiseera ebimu mu bulamu buno mujja kubonaabona n’okugumiikiriza obulumi kubanga emibiri gyammwe gikyali mu mbeera eyagwa era evunda. Mbasaba mugumiikirize byonna ku lwange, era mumanye nti nja kubawa okuwummula n’okubakubagiza mu bizibu byammwe, naye era nkyabalongoosa era mbazinzeemu mu muliro gwange ogw’okulongoosa mulyoke muteekebweteekebwe ddala eri okwegatta okuli wano.

Nze Kristo Yesu, era nga nze Yeshua HaMashiach era ntumye ebigambo bino ng’abiyisa mu mubaka wange era omuweereza wange ye katabo ako ak’omusango ogw’Obutuukirivu, kubanga nsimbye era nteekeddeteekedde ebidumo omusanvu mu mwoyo n’omutima gwa Biblaridion:144 eyateekebwateekebwa ddala omukono gwange olw’ebigendererwa eby’olubeerera ebya Adonai-YHVH okubikkulirwa mu mmwe mwenna lwe muliba amangu ago mu kanyaanya k’eriso ku nsi nga temuliwo. Lwe mulisanga amangu ago nga muyimiridde mu ntebe y’obwakabaka ey’omu ggulu nga mwambadde olubeerera ekitiibwa ekinene eky’olubeerera, obutayonooneka n’akatono, so n’obuzaale bwammwe obw’ekitangaala tebulizikira n’akatono okuva ku kitiibwa eky’omuwulunguko kye mujja okwambazibwa ku kiragiro kyange.

Mutegeere, abaagalwa bange, nti ensi ekwatiriddwa mu mbeera ey’okuyimirizibwa, enjuba n’omwezi bwe byetooloola nga bitegekeddwa Adonai-YHVH, naye obudde tebukyaliwo. Tewakyali kulwa.
