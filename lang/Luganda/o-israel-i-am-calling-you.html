Omujulirwa Omulamba: Isirayiri

Novemba 3, 2024

Ggwe Isirayiri, Nze NKUYITA!

Okuva eri Yahweh, Katonda Omukulu Ataggwaawo:

Wuliriza Ebigambo bya Yahweh, Nze Nzuuno omukulu era olowooze ku kibuuuzo kyange. Lwaki emmese zidduka okuva mu nnyumba egwa, ggwe mwana wange? Eddamu eritayinza kubuusibwabuusibwa lya kuba nti bakikola lwa kwetaasa, okuggyako emmese ezidduka okuva mu Babulooni Ekyama zikikola okukweka ebyonoono byabyo, era n'okulyangayo ddala ku byayo basobole okufuna obufuzi obw'akaseera akatono bwe mbadde mbategekedde. Kubanga omuntu ow'obutonde bw'ayagala ekizikiza, kaakano ng'enda kumuwa ekizikiza ky'ayagala ennyo. Naye, nnawandiika (era nja kuddamu okuwandiika) buli kikolwa kya buli kitonde mu butonde bwange ekirina eddembe ly'okwerondera kubanga oyo aweereddwa ebingi, naye by'anenyezebwa bingi.

Era tewali kikwekeddwa mu maaso gange, era tewali kikwekeddwa we ndabira, era Nze ndi oli alaba, awandiika, era ajjukira BULI kikolwa ekibi ekyakolebwako mu butonde bwange. Era tewali kiyinza kubaawo nga sikimanyi era nga sikikkirizza! Mu maaso gange, ebirowoozo byonna biteekebwa bweru. Mu maaso gange buli kigendererwa ky'omutima kiteekebwa bweru era tewaliwo oba tewali muntu eyatondebwa oba eyakolebwa atali wansi w'omukono gwange ogw'amaanyi, kubanga nze Katonda Ataggwaawo ow'obutuukirivu gw'olina okukolagana naye, ggwe Isirayiri era nkumanyi bulungi, wadde ng'olondamu okunjeemera mu buzibe bw'amaaso obw'ekitundu olw'obukakanyavu bw'emitima gyammwe.

Kubanga, bwe nnajja gy'oli mu muntu w'oMasiya omu yekka gwe nnatumako era Masiya omu yekka gwe nja okutuma, baajjajjabo baamugaana ddala. Kubanga, NZE nze eyatonda Yeshua HaMashiach mu mubiri olw'obulokozi bwa bonna abakoowoola erinnya lye ery'amaanyi, gwe mutamanyi ne mumugaana n'amalala n'okumuvvoola nga mumuyita ekifaananyi eky'amawanga. Ggwe Isirayiri, bangi ku mmwe mwegasse ku Ssinagoga ya Setaani era kaakano mulindiridde n'essuubi Moshiach wammwe ow'obulimba Esawu gw'ategekedde okutta Yakobo.

Kale wuliriza eddoboozi lya NZE NZUUNO OMUKULU kubanga eddoboozi lyange eddivuuma, ery'olubeerera likuyita mu kiseera kino eky'enkomerero era ntuma Omwana wange gy'oli omulundi omulala okulokola Yakobo n'ensigalira ekkiriza Omwana wange Ataggwaawo, Yeshua HaMashiach, erirokolebwa n'omukono gwe ogw'amaanyi era tewali kulondawo kulala kwonna kwa bulokozi. So tekulibaawo kulondawo oba linnya ddala lyonna liweereddwa lwa bulokozi kubanga nnamuwa erinnya erisinga amannya gonna, nti mu linnya lya Yesu Omunazaaleeti buli vviivi lyonna lifukamire. Ate abo abagaana, balifukamirizibwa eri Empologoma y'Ekika kya Yuda gwe ntuma ku nsi asobole okusala omusango n'okulwana mu butuukirivu obutaggwaawo, era ye alireeta obutonde bwange bwonna wansi w'okugondera okw'obutuukirivu era alifuga n'omuggo ogw'ekyuma n'omusango, ekisa, n'okusaasira biri naye emirembe gyonna. Tegeera nti ekkubo ly'okuzikirira ggwanvu era amannya amangi ne bakatonda ab'obulimba banoonya okukutwala mu musika gwabwe ogw'ennyanja y'omuliro eggyako, egyaka n'ebibanja by'omuliro ogusinga okwookya ogw'okubonerezebwa.

Ggwe Isirayiri, nga wayonoona omutima gwa Yahweh mu butawulize bwo era omutima gwange gukyayonooneka ku lulwo okuva emirembe n'emirembe gye nnamanya mu kumanya kwange okw'emabega nti mu nteekateeka yange ey'okununula baajjajja bo baali ba kugaana Yesu Omunazaaleeti ne basaba Bababba, eyali omussi era omukulembeze w'okwekalakaasa okw'obujeemu mu nsi mu kiseera ekyo. Bwe ntyo nnamuleka n'akyukira ab'amawanga, ne bamuwuliriza okumala akaseera, naye obuzibe bw'amaaso obw'ekitundu bwatuuka ku Isirayiri ensigalira ya Isirayiri esobole okujja mu bulokozi mu kiseera ekituufu. Tegeera nti bangi okuva mu Buyudaaya baamufuula Mukama waabwe ne bafuna obulokozi obutaggwaawo mu Isirayiri wa Katonda era baliba n'omwana wange emirembe gyonna. Era NZE ndi oli eyatuma Yesu Omunazaaleeti mu mawanga okununula baganda bo abaasaasaanyizibwa olw'obujeemu bwabwe, n'abo abaali tebalina ssuubi era bangi okuva mu mawanga bafunye obulokozi obutaggwaawo era kaakano basibiddwako emirembe gyonna mu bika. Kale tegeera ggwe Isirayiri nti ekyo NZE NZUUNO Ataggwaawo kye nnalongoosa tokiyitanga kya bulijjo oba ekitali kirongoofu!

Naye ggwe Isirayiri, ggwe wagaana n'otta omwana wange Yesu Kristo n'omuwabula mu mikono gy'abasajja aboonoonyi abaali bamaliridde okumuta mu butaliiko musango bwe. Naye nteekateeka yange ey'obulokozi obutaggwaawo yandituukiridde etya okuggyako nga ebisaanyizo by'amateeka agataggwaawo era agatakyuuka ga Yahweh bituukiriziddwa, amateeka ge nnateekawo olw'entegeka entuukirivu ey'obutonde bwange bwonna okufugibwa mu bwenkanya obutuukiridde. Bwe ntyo Yesu Omunazaaleeti ne yeewaayo ng'Omwana gw'endiga gwa Katonda nga Kabona wo Omukulu ow'emirembe n'emirembe ng'engeri ya Merukizeddeeki bwe yali okusasula ekibonerezo Musa kye yalagira olw'ebibi n'ebyonoono by'omuntu n'okununula omuntu ddala okuva mu kikolimo ky'amateeka. Kubanga Musa yajja ng'omusomesa wa Isirayiri okutwala n'okukuuma Isirayiri ku lwa Yeshua HaMashiach era asasulidde ddala okununulibwa kwo, era wadde nga muli bayizi bajeemu, ensigalira ejja kwanukula okukangavvula kwange n'ekizibu ekibajjira mu wiiki ya Danyeri ey'amakumi musanvu eri ku mulyango gwammwe era etasaangudde kuguggulawo mulyango gwammwe eri okulondawo okw'olubeerera.

Kale tegeera gw'owagaanidde, ggwe Isirayiri, gwe njagala n'okwagala okutaggwaawo, kubanga ggwe gwe nnasuubiza baajjajja bo mu ndagaano nnyingi mu biseera ebyayita nti mu biseera bino eby'omusango nnandikukuumye ku lwabwe, era nze Katonda Ataggwaawo ow'Obutuukirivu atakyuka! Era NZE NDI oli ajjukira okwegayirira okungi baajjajja bo kwe baakola ku lwammwe era buli kwegayirira nkujjukira kigambo ku kigambo era nja kussa ekitiibwa mu kusaba kwabwe ku lw'abo ku mmwe abakkiriza era abawulize. Era ne kaakano Omwana wange, gw'okyaye mu butamanya, abawolereza mu kwagala kwe okutaggwaawo eri oluganda lwe kubanga Yeshua HaMashiach ye mununuzi w'oluganda lwa Isirayiri, ye yagula ensi n'omusaayi gwe ogw'okutangirira era yabakombola okuva mu mikono gy'omulabe singa mulondawo okukkiriza. Wadde nga, bangi mu mmwe mwasikira okwagala kwammwe okwa Misiri okuva eri baajjajja bammwe, temukkirizanga kugwa emirembe n'emirembe mu ddungu ly'abatakkiriza.

Kubanga Yeshua HaMashiach ye akyali ow'okulokola ensigalira okuva eri Yakobo erimujjira ddala, ne mu biseera eby'okugezesebwa kwa wiiki ya Danyeri ey'amakumi musanvu. Kale tegeera ebiseera by'olimu era ozuukuke okuva mu buzibe bwo obw'ekitundu ku kiragiro kyange bw'onookyukira Yeshua HaMashiach, gwe nnatuma olw'obulokozi bwo nga Masiya wo (nga tokyalina kuzibizibwa maaso lwa bukakaanyavu bwa mitima gyammwe). Kubanga olookyukira omwana w'obutuukirivu era alikwaniriza gy'ali, era alikutwala mu kyama n'akukweka okuzikirizibwa okuva mu mikono gya Moshiach wa Esawu ow'obulimba.

Kale tegeera oyo gw'olina okukolagana naye kubanga NZE ndi Katonda ow'endagaano nnyingi, era NZE ndi Katonda ow'ebisuubizo ebingi, era NZE Ndi Katonda Ataggwaawo ow'Obutuukirivu ajja omulundi omulala okuyita abantu bange okudda eri omusumba waabwe omukulu gwe nnatuma olw'obulokozi bwa Adamu. Era waliwo enjuyi nnyingi ez'engeri gye ndi, era buli linnya lirina amakulu gaalyo agakwata ku Katonda Ataggwaawo. Kale mpulira era mpulira bulungi era otegeere Yahweh, akuyita gyendi osobole okufuna obulokozi. Bwe ntyo nkujjukiza era nkuyigiriza ng'abaana abawere, so nga ddala ggwe wandibadde omusomesa, ggwe Isirayiri! Kale yiga ebikwata ku Katonda wo Ataggwaawo ow'Obutuukirivu!

N'amannya n'engeri endala nnyingi bye nnina, kale wulira eddoboozi lyange eriwuluguma ng'okubwatuka kw'eggulu mu ggulu, ggwe Isirayiri, kubanga NZE ndi Katonda wa baajjajja bo gw'osinza mu bukyayi mu bukakanyavu.

NZE Ndi Katonda wa Ibulayimu gw'olekedde!

NZE Ndi Katonda wa Yakobo n'okutuusa gw'ovuddemu okugenda mu mikono gy'omulala, era oddukidde eri bakatonda bo ab'obulimba nga weenyumiririza mu bujeemu bwo. Tegeera Isirayiri nti oli mu ngalo z'Essinagoga lya Setaani n'abo abagamba nti ba Isirayiri ate nga balimba so ssi be bo.

Wuliriza eddoboozi lyange ggwe Isiraeri, era jangu eri Yeshua HaMashiach mu kiseera kino eky'enkomerero kubanga nkuyita mu kwagala kwe nkulinamu era nkugololerako ekisa kyange n'okusaasira kwange mu kiseera kino. Naye oli ggwanga jjenjevu era obujjenjevu n'obukakanyavu bwa bakitaawo wabifuula bibyo, era wagaana Katonda wa bakitaawo gw'osinza mu butamanya n'obulimba kubanga abantu bange bazikirira lwa kubulwa kumanya, n’olwekyo jangu eri Yeshua HaMashiach oyige ku ye olyoke ofune obulokozi mu mukono gwe ogw'amaanyi.

Ggwe Isiraeri, omutima gwange nga gwesika ku lulwo kubanga nakutwala ng'ekimera ekito mu ddungu eggumba nga kyeteeseteese okuzikirira n'okuwotoka emirembe gyonna, ne nkuwa obulamu era ne nkufuula abantu bange ab'enjawulo. Nayogeragana endagaano nnyingi ne bakitaawo, endagaano ze baatayinza na kuzikwata olw'ekibi n'okuvunda okuli mu musaayi gwa Adamu. Naye NZE Mukama Katonda wo ajjukira era eyakwata amasuubizo gange n'endagaano zange era nja kutuukiriza buli kisuubizo n'endagaano ebyakolebwa eri abantu, era nga mbiyisa mu maaso ga Yeshua HaMashiach, ye Mwana wange omwagalwa Yesu Kristo.

Ggwe Isiraeri, ojja kusigala nga toli mwesigwa era nga toli muwulize? Ojja kwegatta emirembe gyonna ku balabe bo mu kibonerezo kyabwe eky'olubeerera? Era bwe kityo, wali ojeemu era n'otambulira mu buzibe bw'amaaso obw'ekitundu, era nakuggyako eggwanga lyo era tokyakulemberwa Yahweh Katonda Omukulu Ataggwaawo. Naye mu bujeemu bwo okulemberwa omukono gw'abalabe bo abeefuula era abeenyigira mu kisaanikizo nga bali Yakobo so nga mu mazima be Esawu. Tegeera nti bonna abagaana Yeshua HaMashiach bavudde ku Yahweh ne badda eri bakatonda ab'obulimba n'obutakkiriza era bangi balogeddwa ne basibibwa mu masanduku gaabwe amaddugavu ge basinza mu butamanya nga tebakkiriza. Kale tegeera, bonna abakyamizibwa Moshiach waabwe ow'obulimba oba Mahdi waabwe ow'obulimba mu ddiini zaabwe ez'obulimba ezitatwala muntu eri Yahweh newaakubadde eri obulamu obutaggwaawo, bajja kufuna okuzikirizibwa okw'olubeerera bonna abalonda okusigala omwo.

Ggwe Isiraeri, jangu gyendi ng'oyita mu Mwana wange Yeshua HaMashiach era ye Yesu Omunazaaleeti, gw'ogaana nga bakitaawo bwe baamugaana. Naye tozikiririra mu bibi bya bakitaawo, era kkiriza okwagala kwange ne ssaddaaka gye nawaayo ku lulwo, ssaddaaka Adamu yenna gy'aweebwa omukisa era bangi okuva mu nsi n'amawanga ne bajja eri Mukama waabwe nga beenenyezza era baweebwa omukisa era banunulibwa emirembe gyonna. Era tegeera nti NZE eyateeka ebintu byonna mu mikono gya Kristo Yesu egy'amaanyi egizuukidde era egifuuliddwa emipya, kubanga NZE eyamufuula omwoyo oguwa obulamu era okufuga kwe n'obwakabaka bwe tebiriggwaawo!

N'olwekyo, endagaano zange zonna okuva ku Adamu okutuuka ku Musa, era n'Endagaano ya Musa kati egenda kutuukirizibwa mu maaso ga Yesu Kristo olw'Endagaano yange Empya, ye Ndagaano yange Empya eweereddwa okutuukiriza ebintu byonna okuva ku Adamu okutuuka ku Kristo. Kubanga abo abafuna obulamu obutaggwaawo bateekwa okwegondeza ne bafukamira omwana wange omu yekka gwenzadde, gwe nsanyukira bulijjo. Kale jangu gyendi mu maaso ga Yeshua HaMashiach mu musaayi gwe ogutalimu kibi mwe yakakasa Endagaano ye Empya, ye Ndagaano Empya, gye nakkiriza ddala era ne ngiteekateeka emisingi gy'ensi nga tenassibwawo. Era nja kutuma emitwalo kkumi n'ena n'enkumi nnya (144,000), nga bakulemberwa Yeshua HaMashiach, olw'obulokozi bwammwe era bajja kubakweka mu kifo kye mbategekedde era eyo mujja kulabirirwa era mulokolebwe nga muli balamu okuva eri Esawu eyetegekera okubazikiriza.

Naye Esawu, ajooja abantu bange, adduka Babulooni Ekikwabuzi kubanga bagenderera okubikka ku bibi byabwe. Ekibi emmese ezidduka ennyumba egwa kye zitaliiko. N'olwekyo, bajja kugezaako okuziika ebyonoono byabwe era n'ebijiiko byabwe eby'ebibi bye baakozesa okubikka ku byonoono byabwe era nja kubabikkula, bw'ayogera Mukama ow'Eggye. Kubanga bamaze okunyaga Babulooni Ekikwabuzi ddala nga bwe nakkiriza kibaawo mu mugabo gwange ogw'emirundi ebiri ogw'omusango ku malaya oyo omukulu! Era bwe batyo beekungaanyirizza amaanyi ga Babulooni Ekikwabuzi gonna ag'amagye era bajja kweyongera okunyaga okutuusa Babulooni Ekikwabuzi lw'erizikirizibwa mu ssaawa emu era teriddamu kuyimuka. Tegeera nti tewali ky'okulwanyisa kyonna ekikolebwa oba ekibumbibwa okukulwanyisa ekirigasa singa oyita erinnya lya Masiya wo ow'amazima, ye Yeshua HaMashiach!

Kale tegeera nti Ekkuŋŋaaniro lya Sitaani lirina ekifaananyi ky'obutiibwa Katonda mu ndabika yaabwe ey'ebweru, naye ba kitaabwe sitaani era okwegomba kwa kitaabwe kwe bajja okukola kubanga Esawu yayonoona omuzzukulu gwe emirembe gyonna. Omuti guno omubi, n'abakulembeze bammwe abalabika ng'abatuukirivu gwe muti omubi era bangi bakabassanya ba baana abato abayiiggira ku bavubuka era abaakyamira mu bibi bya Sodoma ne Gomola, era bayiiggira ku baana, so si basumba balungi wabula ba magenge. Era ogusota ogwo oguli kitaabwe gwe gubakulembera, gwe gumu ogwawa abasumba ab'amagenge babiri okufuga n'okulunda ensi mu butali butuukirivu. Era ensolo evà mu nnyanja ejja okugolokokera ng'e Mahdi waabwe n'ensolo evà mu nsi, era ye Moshiach waabwe ow'obulimba era ajja kufuga ensi era ddala bajja kufuga ensi mu butali butuukirivu.

N'ebbala ly'amagenge gaabwe lisiiga mu mubiri gwabwe gwonna, era obulokozi tebayinza kubufuna. Kubanga bonna abeekyusa mu buzaale mu ngeri eyo oba abazaalibwa mu abo abaagwa tebalina bulokozi kubanga bakolebwa mu kifaananyi ky'ogusota era ekifaananyi ekyo kye balimu mu byonna. Ekyakyama tekiyinza kugololwa, na buli muzizo gw'abo abaagwa guyinza kukola bubi bwokka kubanga baakolebwa okuleeta okuzikirira kw'abantu, era bandibadde bawangudde singa saabakomya mu nnaku za Nuuwa.

Naye era bajja kugolokoka nate, kubanga ndeeta abanene okutuukiriza obusungu bwange! N'Ekkuŋŋaaniro lya Sitaani likolerawo okubikka ku buli kabonero k'abo be bali ddala akabi nga tekannagwirawo n'obulimba bwabwe ne butuuka ku kuzikirira kubanga abo abaagwa bakkirizibwa okuzikiriza Babulooni Ekikwabuzi. Naye buli kibi kijja kuweebwa mu bifuba byabwe era n'olwekyo, tekisoboka kwekwasa Yahweh n'omwana wange Yeshua HaMashiach era obusungu bwaffe bujja kubasanga nga bagezaako okwekwasa mu mutima gw'ensi. Tewali n'omu ku abo abali wansi w'omusango omunene ayinza okuwona ennyanja y'omuliro, wabula abo abawandiikiddwa mu kitabo ky'obulamu eky'omwana gw'endiga era tewali n'omu ayinza okuwona ebibi bye wabula abo obulokozi be bwagendererwa, beebo abakkiriza endagaano yange ey'emirembe. Kaakano amawanga ameka ge bazikiriza era kaakano beekweka wala mu nsi, tebajja kuwona kuwalana gwange n'obusungu bwe beekungaanyirizza era nja kubasasula mu bujjuvu, bw'ayogera Katonda Ataggwaawo.

Kubanga nja kukyusa ensi okuva ku ntegeka yayo ey'omu kiseera kino okudda ku kitiibwa kyayo eky'olubereberye, era abanunule b'ensi bajja kutambuliramu nga mirembe. Kale ekiriwo, kijja kukyusibwa ne kifuuka ekiribaawo, era tebajja kuwona, abo bonna abalisigalawo okusalirwa omusango awalibaawo emitawaana n'obutyabaga bingi, n'omuzira gulisaanuuka, n'amawanga galikyuka, n'ensi erizzibwawo mu kitiibwa kyayo eky'edda. Ekitiibwa abo abaagwa kye baakyusa ne bakifuula obuvundu okusanyusa okwegomba kwabwe.

Era nze Yahweh, Katonda Omukulu Ataggwaawo, ye gwe watonda eggulu n'ensi, ye gwe wava emirembe n'emirembe, era mu kwagala kwange n'okuteesa kwange mwe nnayogereramu ebintu ebiriwo okuva mu ekyo ekitabaddeewo. Kale wuliriza eddoboozi lyange, ggwe Isiraeri, era na mmwe mwenna abanoonya okusaaliza Ebigambo byange mu bukakanyavu obw'omutima. Omusango gunaatera okujja mu bujjuvu ku nsi era nja kubikkula Esawu yenna abo be bali ddala, era nja kubikkula ddala bakitaabwe be beegomba era be basibibwako emirembe gyonna.

Laba! Abo ab'omu lulyo lwa Esawu beewaana obutuukirivu bwabwe, nga balowooza mu butamanya nti be bantu bangeabalonde, era Moshiach waabwe ow'obulimba, ejjembe eryo ettono, ajja kwewaana nti ye, kennyini, ye Katonda! (Tewali alinga Yahweh, Katonda Omukulu Ataggwaawo era Ayinza Byonna!) N'abo abaagwa tebayinza kwerokola mu mikono gyange egy'amaanyi gonna, era Yeshua HaMashiach ajja kubetenta ddala omutwe gwa Sitaani nga bwe nnasuubiza Adamu. Tewali buziba mu nsi mwe bayinza okwekwasa, newakubadde nga bagezaako, era kati basibiddwa ku nsi wansi w'ebbanga lye batayinza kuwona. Tewali kimanyiso kirala kye bayinza kwetikkawo we siribaggulirawo be baani. Tewali bifaananyi bye bayinza okwetikkawo bye siribategeerera ne mbiraba mu bujjuvu, okutuukira ddala ku mitima gyabwe egifudde. Era buli omu ku abo abaagwa okuva ku muto okutuuka ku mukulu ne bonna abagoberera amakubo gaabwe amabi bajja kufuna obusika bwabwe obw'olubeerera mu nnimi z'omuliro ezo ez'okubonerezebwa mu nnyanja eyo eyaka emirembe n'emirembe omuwuka gwabwe mwe gutafiira.

Kale mukube ekiwoobe, ggwe Isiraeri, ku lwammwe abagaana mu bukakanyavu okuyitibwa kwange okujja mu bulamu obutaggwaawo, abo bonna abalisalawo okugoberera abo abaagwa n'abo bonna abalyegatta ku gusota n'abo bonna ab'omu lulyo lwa Adamu abaalina omukisa gw'okulokolebwa naye ne balondawo ebibi byabwe ebiribatwala mu kufa okw'okubiri bwe batakoowoola linnya lya Yeshua HaMashiach okulokolebwa.

Kale nja kunnyonnyola ebironde bye mulina kati. Mulina okukkiriza Masiya wammwe ow'amazima, Yeshua HaMashiach gwe nnatuma, era mulirokolebwa ne muyingira mu mpeera n'ekitiibwa eby'olubeerera, ebizibu byammwe n'okuyigganyizibwa kwammwe bye bitasaana kugeraageranyizibwa nakyo. Oba omuntu yenna bw'agenda mu maaso n'okwegatta ku gusota mu nteekateeka yaagwo empya, ojja kuba ogaanye obulamu obutaggwaawo n'okkiriza mu bujjuvu okusalirwa kwo omusango olw'ebibi by'omubiri, era ojja kusitula mu butuukirivu omusango gwo bwe wagayirirwa okuwona ng'ogondera, ky'ojja kuba ogaanye.

Omuntu yenna anaateekebwako akabonero k'ensolo, ajja kuba akyusizza emirembe gyonna ekifaananyi kya Yahweh kye yatondebwamu, n'amuleka mu mbeera etayinza kununulibwa. N'olwekyo, mbegayirira mulonde obulamu mulyoke mubeere mu bulamu obutaggwaawo mu kwagala n'emikisa by'omutonzi wammwe ebiri mu maaso ga Yesu Kristo, ye Yeshua HaMashiach! Oba si ekyo, mujja kwegatta ku abo abaagwa mu busika bwabwe obw'ekkibiriiti ekiriko omuliro mu nnyanja eyo ey'omuliro ey'olubeerera, okubonerezebwa n'okuweebwa empeera y'ebibi gye mirundi gyokka gye mulimanya.

Tegeera, nti ekigendererwa kyange si kuba mukambwe wadde obutaba mwangu eri ekitonde kyange, naye amateeka gange ag'obwenkanya tegakyuka era tegayinza kukyusibwa. Kale ebitonde bisinda era binkaabira nnyo wansi w'obuzito bw'ebibi by'abo abaagwa n'ebibi by'abantu, n'ebiseera by'okulongoosa n'okutukuza ensi mu musango okuva mu bubi bituuse; wadde ng'ensigo z'obujeemu zijja kusigalawo naye obwakabaka bw'abalabe tebuddemu kuyimuka. Wadde nga Sitaani ajja kugezaako okulumba kwe okusembayo mu kiseera kye ekitono nga tanasuulibwa mu muliro emirembe gyonna kubanga si kabaka, naye entebe ye ejja kuba nnimi z'omuliro ezitazikizika ez'obulongoofu obusinga okwookya, era teziriba na kusaasira.

Kuba amateeka gange ag'olubeerera galagira ki eri abatagondera, kale jjukira nti empeera y'ekibi kwe kufa, era bwe kityo Adamu yayonoona n'amalira obulamu bwe mu mpeera y'ekibi, ke kufa. Naye ebiweebwayo ebyokebwa ne ssaddaaka ez'edda tebyali bimala kununula mwana wange omujaajaamya, kale omusajja atuukiridde atalina kibi ye ssaddaaka yokka ekkirizibwa kubanga Adamu yali atuukiridde era nga talina kibi bwe yayonoona.

Kale Nze, ye Yahweh Omukulu NZE NDI, ntumye Yeshua HaMashiach nga bwe nnasuubiza ng'omwana gw'endiga owa Katonda eyafiirwa omusaayi gwe ogutalimu kibi ku bwammwe okusasula ekibonerezo kya Musa ng'afumita omusaayi gwe ogutalimu kibi mu bulumi obw'entiisa abantu balyoke banunulibwe okuva mu kikolimo ky'amateeka, abo bonna abalonda okufuuka ekitonde ekipya mu Kristo Yesu emirembe gyonna kubanga ono Kristo Yesu ye Yeshua HaMashiach!

NZE Yahweh, Katonda Omukulu Ataggwaawo, era NZE NDI.

NZE nnatuma Yesu Kristo, ye Yeshua HaMashiach, olw'obulokozi bw'ekitonde kyange kale mumuwulire era mugondere Ebigambo bye nga Musa bwe yalagira.

NZE nnatumye Ebigambo bino okuyita mu mubaka wange n'omuweereza wange omwana wange Kristo Yesu gwe yalagira n'amuyimusa olw'ekigendererwa ekyo ku kiragiro kyange kubanga Yeshua HaMashiach bulijjo akola bya Kitaawe by'ayagala era tuli bumu mu bumu ne mu kigendererwa.

NZE Katonda Omulamu akuyita, ggwe Isiraeri, abo bonna abalagirwa okufuna obulamu obutaggwaawo balyoke bajje mu bukuumi bw'omusumba waabwe omukulu.

Kale wuliriza ebigambo byange ggwe Isiraeri ojje eri omwana wange ng'okwenenyezza mu bujjuvu okkirize ssaddaaka ye, kubanga mu mazima bw'onoomugaana okuyita kwe teribaayo ssaddaaka ndala ya kibi wabula omusango ogw'olubeerera kubanga ojja kuba olonze mu bwagazi n'emirembe gyonna obubi okusinga obutuukirivu, era nja kuwa abo bonna abakola ekironde ekyo ebiva mu kusalawo kwabwe mu bujjuvu, naye tegeera nti bonna balina eddembe okulonda.

NZE ndifuula ebintu byonna bipya!

NZE Kitaffe w'Ebitangaala alibeera n'abantu mu ggulu eriggya n'ensi empya bye nditonda mu bujjuvu mu butuukirivu so tewalibaayo kintu kyonna kibi eky'okuyingiramu. Era nja kuwa ekibuga ekyo ekitukuvu Yerusaalemi Empya abawanguzi bange n'abo bonna abajja eri Yeshua HaMashiach, era ye Yesu Kristo era balirya ku muti gw'obulamu era balifuna okuyingira mu kibuga ekyo ekitukuvu era ekituukiridde era nze ndiba Katonda waabwe, nabo baliba bantu bange emirembe n'emirembe, Yee era Amiina.

Nywegera Omwana, aleme okusunguwala n'ozikirira okuva mu kkubo.

Ebiseera bye bino.
