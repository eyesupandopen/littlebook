Omujulirwa Omubiri: Ekkanisa
Mu Bikorwa bya Yokaana Omutume Omubatiza
Gwakusatu 12, 2024

Omusumba Omwesigwa Agamba

Omusumba omwesigwa agamba abeesigwa nti: Mazima ddala, ddala mbagamba nti, mwabulire ebitali bya butuukirivu era temwonoona. Kubanga empeera yange nnayo okuwa buli muntu okusinziira ku bikolwa bye. Mbayise mu kwagala kwa Kitange mulyoke mubeere n'obulamu era mubeere nabwo emirembe gyonna. Mbayise okutambulira mu Bwakabaka bwange, okunweereza n'okubeera ku nsi mu butuukirivu. Mbawadde ekisa kyange mulyoke mukulire mu nze, Mukama wammwe Yesu Kristo. Temuggwaamu maanyi olw'okukangavvula kwange, kubanga bonna be njagala mbanenya, ekisa kyange ekirokuza kilyoke kisimbe amakanda mu buziba bw'obuntu bwammwe mulyoke mukole ebikolwa eby'amaanyi, ebikolwa eby'omukisa, ebikolwa eby'okuwonya, era mulyoke mubalire mu nze ebibala ebingi, mmwe abantu bange.

Kubanga Kitange ye Katonda ow'okwagala, era okwagala kwa Kitange tekuwangulwa. Mu nze, Mukama omutuukirivu, mwe mubeera okujjudde kwonna Kitange kwe yampa. Ebintu byonna yabiteeka mu mikono gyange, era nga Falaawo bwe yagulumiza Yusufu n'ateeka mu mikono gye Misiri yonna, ne Kitange bw'atyo bwe yangulumiza n'ateeka ebintu byonna ebiri mu ggulu ne ku nsi mu mikono gyange. Yampa erinnya erisinga amannya gonna, erinnya lya Yesu buli vviivi liryoke lifukamire, ery'omu ggulu n'ery'oku nsi, na buli lulimi lulyoke lwatule Obukama bwange, mu ggulu ne ku nsi.

Mbawadde obulamu obutaggwaawo, era mbakutte akkubo mulyoke mudduke okuva mu ddungu era mudduke olunaku olwo olukulu olw'obusungu olujja, kale temuggwaamu maanyi! Temukoowa okukola obulungi, naye muyimirire mu maaso gange, nange nja kweyongera okubasembeza mu maaso gange. Mutudde mu bifo eby'omu ggulu era muligabana ku ntebe yange ey'obwakabaka nga nze bwe ngabana ku ntebe ya Kitange. Nnina engule nnyingi n'emikisa mingi egibategekerwa, mmwe abantu bange abeesigwa. Muyimirire mu maaso gange mube nga mutuukiridde mu nze. Mwabulire ebibi byammwe era mukkirize ebiseera eby'okuzza obuggya bifuuweyo omuwempe okuva mu bulamu bwammwe. Mu nze mwe muli obulamu obutaggwaawo, era mu nze mwe muli ekitonde ekipya, omuntu omupya, ye Kristo ali mu mmwe, essuubi ery'ekitiibwa! Mwabulire ekibi era mwabulire okwonoona. Mweyongere okunjatula mu bamawanga, kubanga bakyaliyo abantu ab'okulokola mu ssaawa eno esembayo. Mweyongere okulangirira erinnya lyange, kubanga tewali linnya eddala wansi w'eggulu abantu mwe bayinza okulokokerera. Musanyukire ekiwummulo kye mbawadde, era emitego gyammwe mugiteeke ku bigere byange; kubanga omutwalo gwange mwangu, n'ekikoligo kyange kiwewufu.

Abeesigwa (abo abawangudde ekibi n'emitawaana olw'omusaayi gwange ogwatangirira) mbagamba nti: Nnina empeera nnene ey'omuwendo eyibategekerwa mu nnyumba ya Kitange. Eriiso terirabanga, n'okutu tekuwuliranga, so tekyayingiranga mu mitima gy'abantu, ebintu ebyabategekerwa. Nja kubasasula nnyo n'engule nnyingi, kale munyimirirewo era mube beesigwa eri eddoboozi lyange n'ebigambo byange; kubanga mu bigambo ebyo mwe mulisanga obulamu. Si nze Kigambo eyafuuka omubiri n'abeera mu mmwe?

Nze mulamuzi omutuukirivu.

Nze musumba omulungi alunda ekisibo.

Nze kuwonyezebwa kwammwe, era nja kubawonya ddala ddala.

Nnazaalibwa omukazi ne nfuuka omubiri, newankubadde tewali kibi mu nze.

Nnakomererwa ku musaalaba olw'ebibi byammwe, era mazima, tewali muntu yaggyawo bulamu bwange, wabula nze kennyini nnabuwaayo olw'okwagala kwe nnina eri batabani ne bawala ba Kitange.

Nnazuukizibwa ku lunaku olw'okusatu, kubanga tekyasoboka lufu kunswaza. Kitange yamenya enjegere z'olumbe n'amaanyi g'okuzuukira kwange.

Nze kibala ekisooka eky'abafu.

Mbiro mujja kunsisinkana mu bbanga, era obulumi bwennyini obw'olumbe bujja kuta abo abeebakidde, ate mmwe abakyali abalamu abasigaddewo mulitwalibwa wamu, era emirembe n'emirembe mulibeera nange. Nze Mwana wa Katonda, era mu mukono gwange mulimu empeera yange eri abeesigwa bange. Muyimirire nga mugumu, mmwe abaana bange, era temuggwaamu maanyi. Mube b'amaanyi mu Mukama ne mu maanyi g'obuyinza bwange, kubanga Kitange yekka yaansinga obukulu. Mpulidde okusaba kwammwe, era mbakkirizza ng'abange.

Mugumiikirize era mweyongere okunkolera. Kubanga embiro mujja kubeera ewange eka, era emirembe n'emirembe tulibeera ku mirimu gya Kitaffe. Mube b'amaanyi era musanyuke. Nkimanyi nti bangi ku mmwe mulaba ekibonyoobonyo n'akabi ebisembera, era bangi ku mmwe mulabye amagye g'omubi nga gasomoka ensalo zammwe. Temubatya! Temubatya, newankubadde nga bandibazikirizza singa babadde basobola, naye tebajja lwa beesigwa bange, kubanga okudduka okukulu n'okukuŋŋaana okukulu bijja kulokola abeesigwa bange okuva mu kiseera eky'okugezesebwa n'ekikemo ekigenda okujja ku nsi yonna. Muwummulire mu kwagala kwange, nga mumanyi nti sijja kubaleka so sijja kubasuulirira, nga Kitange bwe yataleka so teyansuulirira mu ssaawa yange ey'okuweebwayo. Kubanga obulokozi bwammwe bukuumibwa mu nze; kyemunaava mweyongera okujja gye ndi, kubanga nja kweyongera okubazzaamu amaanyi okusinga bwe mbadde. Ekikulu ky'ekyama eky'okutya Katonda!
