Bw'atyo bw'ayogera Mukama, Olw'okwonoona kwa Isiraeri emirundi esatu, weewaawo, n'ena, sirikkyusa kibonerezo kyakyo: kubanga baatunda omutuukirivu olw'effeeza n'omwavu olw'engatto. Eby'okusooka

Ekiwandiiko kino musango ogusaliddwa ku Babulooni Ekyama nga Juni 26, 2025, nga bwe kyalagirwa Yeshua HaMashiach ne YHVH. Emisango gino giggiddwa mu Kitabo Ekitono eky'Okubikkulirwa Essuula 10, era ye Njiri Ey'emirembe n'emirembe ey'Okubikkulirwa essuula 14; ekyabikkulirwa okuva nga Maaki 2, 2024, okutuuka nga Maaki 20, 2025. Giweereddwa Yeshua HaMashiach ne YHVH okuyita mu Mwoyo gw'Obunnabbi. Ebirangiriro bino bikola ng'okulabula okw'okwenenya ng'omusango omujjuvu tegunnagwa, era bikola ng'ekiragiriro eky'ebigenda okubaawo mu bbanga eritali lya wala.

Kimanyibwe, nti mu kifo kino, nkola ng'omubaka w'amateeka era omubaka atumiddwa okuva ku ntebe y'obwakabaka bwa Yahweh yennyini, wansi w'obuyinza bwa Kristo Yesu, era ye Yeshua HaMashiach, gwe mpeereza. Nze wuuyo era ekyo Mukama kye yanzimba, era nze Biblaridion we, akatabo ke akatono, ddala omubaka ayogerwako mu bubaka obukaawa n'obuwoomu era mu ngalo zange mulimu akatabo akatono akabikkule. Seera. Sizze ku buyinza bwange oba lwa kwagala kwange, era siseetisse, naye ntumiddwa n'Ebigambo Mukama bye yasiga mu Mwoyo gwange n'omukono gwe ogw'amaanyi okuteebwa ng'ayagadde era ng'akooye okusomebwa n'okulangirirwa eri Ekkanisa, n'eri Isiraeri, naye era n'eri ensi eno ejjudde ebibi era esaliddwa omusango era efa, era naddala okuweebwa mu bifuba by'abagwi kubanga ebiseera byabwe bituuse. Seera!

Ekirangiriro Ekikulu ekiweereddwa Yeshua HaMashiach, era ye Kristo Yesu

(Juni 26, 2025)

Muwulire, Muwulire mwenna munaŋŋanamu era mukotamye amaaso gammwe, amatu gammwe, n'emitima gyammwe mu buwulize eri Ebigambo bya Adonai-YHVH, ne Yeshua HaMashiach, era ye Kristo Yesu. Muwulire Ebigambo byange! Bino wammanga bye birangiriro ebitereevu ebikwata ku Babulooni Ekyama, etegeerekeseka nga United States of America, n'okubikkululwa kw'ebigambo by'ennaku z'enkomerero okuva eri Yeshua HaMashiach, naddala Yahweh Nze Ani Asinga, kubanga wa maanyi Adonai YHVH asalira omusango omwenzi omukulu era omusango gwe gutuuse!

Era kimanyibwe, nti emisango gino gitumiddwa okuyita mu Yeshua HaMashiach era ye Kristo Yesu, ng'eyita mu Mwoyo gw'Obunnabbi, oguteekeddwa mu mutima gw'omubaka wange, era omuweereza wange gwe natuma era ne mmutuma, era ne mmukweka mu ky'emisaale kyange okutuusa ekiseera ng'ekino, Seera!

Muwulire Ebigambo byange mmwe Omubiri gwange, Ekkanisa yange, era n'Omugole wange! Muwulire eddoboozi lyange era mwesibire ku maloboozi g'Ebigambo byange era muwulire obuyinza bwabyo nga buvuga mu nsi eno, kubanga ensi ya Mukama n'okujjuza kwayo! Muwulire Ebigambo byange mmwe ab'amaanyi bange 144,000 era mutegeere okwogera kwange era mweteekereteekere okulabika kwa Mukama wammwe, Kabaka wammwe, Kabona Asinga Obukulu, ne Masiya wammwe! Kubanga mujja kutwalibwa mu ggulu akakondera ako akanaatera okufuuwebwa bwe kalivuga, ddala akakondera ako ak'amaanyi ke nkwatidde mu ngalo zange ez'amaanyi okukavuga amangu ago n'obutasuubirwa kati, Seera! N'olwekyo, mweteekereteeke mmwe abawala bange ab'amagezi, kubanga nzija okubaggyawo, era ani ayinza okunziza, NZE NGA MBADDE NJOGEDDE ANI!

Naawe, Ggwe omulangira omuwangulwe, ggwe ayimiridde mu busirise obw'okuwangulwa kubanga oli mpologoma etalina mannyo. Wulira Ebigambo byange Ggwe Sitaani era otegeere okwogera kwange, kubanga sigenda kugaanibwa bintu byange era tolina maanyi wadde amannyo okunziza, n'olwekyo, nkukangavvudde Sitaani, nze Mukama wa bakama ne Kabaka wa bakabaka, kubanga okkirizibwa kukola ekyo kyokka ekikkiriziddwa wansi w'omukono gwange ogw'amaanyi n'obuyinza, ebyaweebwa Adonai-Yahweh yennyini, n'olwekyo, musirike era mutuule nga mukangavvuddwa eddoboozi lyange, kubanga bulijjo mujeemu, naye muwanguziddwa, era ebiseera by'omusango gwammwe bituuse, era temujja kugwonona! Seera, gwe akalangira akatono, Seera.

Laba! Nze Yeshua HaMashiach, ddala Mukama wa bakama omu era Kabaka wa bakabaka, kubanga nze Kristo Yesu, ddala Empologoma y'Ekika kya Yuda atuma Ebigambo bino okuyita mu mubaka wange era omuweereza wange, era okuyita mu mubaka wange n'omuweereza wange Ebigambo byange mwe bitumiddwa, Seera!

Ddala Ebigambo ebiweereddwa okuyita mu Mwoyo gw'Obunnabbi nga biyita mu mubaka wange era omuweereza wange Ebigambo bino gwe byatumiddwa okulabula, era ne mu mimwa n'emikono gy'Ekisibo kyange Ekitono! Seera.

Laba! Nkwatidde akakondera mu ngalo, okuweebwa okuvuga ku kiragiro kyange era ku kabonero kange n'oluyoogaano olw'amaanyi!

N'ensi ejja kuzikira ng'ekitangaala ky'okulabika kwange kimasamasa ku nsi, kubanga sigabana kitiibwa kyange na mulala!

Laba, nzija! Laba, nzija! Laba! Nzija! Wuliriza ebirangiriro byange era owulire okwogera kwange era okankane Ggwe Babulooni Ekyama. Kubanga ekitangaala kyange bwe kityasaganya ekizikiza ekikutte, kubanga ekizikiza tekiyinza kuwangula kitangaala kyange wadde ebitangaala by'ensi, ebizzibwamu amaanyi Kristo Yesu era bitumiddwa n'obuyinza obungi Kitange w'Ebitangaala era ebiseera byammwe eby'okununulibwa okujjuvu bituuse. N'olwekyo, muntunuulire, nze Kristo Yesu era amaaso gammwe mugatunule waggulu era mugagule nga mutunuulidde Mukama wammwe Yesu Kristo mu bweteefuteefu n'obuwulize obujjuvu. Entegeka zammwe muzirongoose, kubanga kijja kuba kati nti amangu ago ku nsi, temukyaliwo.

Era bwe ntyo mbalagira mmwe abaagalwa bange, mujjuze ettaala zammwe, era musale entambi zammwe era mutwale omuliro gwange mwakire ettaala zammwe ziryoke zaake okwemulisa mu Bigambo byange, era mutegeere nti mulina kulindirira ku mabbali g'ekkubo, kubanga Awasa Omutonzi asembere okujja olw'Omugole we, n'abagenyi baffe. Seera.

Seera! Baagalwa bange, Seera, mmwe amayiba gange, kubanga nkwagala nnyo era nzija era essaawa etuuse ku mmwe.

Biteekeddwaako omukono n'omukono gwange ogw'amaanyi, era mu kwagala kwange nze kennyini era biweereddwa mu ngalo z'Omubaka wange butereevu okuva gye ndi okugenda mu maaso, amatu, n'emitima gy'abo Ebigambo bino be bitumiddwa. Ddala Ebigambo bye ntuma eri Ekkanisa yange, Omubiri gwange, era n'Omugole wange, naddala eri abange 144,000 kubanga ebiseera byammwe eby'okuteekebwako akabonero bituuse, era oluyimba oluggya lwe nditonda munda mu mmwe omuntu yenna lw'atayinza kuyiga. Seera.

Okulangirira Okukulu okuva eri Adonai-YHVH, Oyo Nze Ani Asinga Obukulu

(Ntenvu 26, 2025)

Laba! Muwulire ebigambo byange mmwe abali mu nsi, ebigambo bya YHVH, Nze Ani asinga obukulu, mutegeere nti obusungu bwange n’ekiruyi kyange biri ku mmwe abali ku nsi, era singa si lwa ssaddaaka gye nnakkiriza emirembe gyonna, omulundi gumu obutaddamu, y’eyo Paasika etuukiridde eyaweebwayo kabona omukulu ow’olubeerera ng’eri mu kitiibwa kya Merukizeddeeki, oyo Omwana w’omuntu eyawaayo omusaayi gwe ogutalimu kibi, era nga ye Mwana gw’endiga gwa Katonda, mazima mwandibadde nga Sodoma ne Ggomola era omusango gwammwe gwandibadde gwa mangu nnyo era gwa kibwatukira, Seera!

Laba Kaakano! Tunula! Tunula olabe oyo gwe ntuma, oyo agenda okuyimuka n’obusungu obungi ng’aleeta omusango ogw’amangu ku Babulooni eky’ekyama, era nga kibwatukira abeesigwa mu Kristo nga tebakiali ku nsi! Oyo Omwana w’omuntu ow’amaanyi gwe nfuula omuzzukusa, era mu mikono gye egy’amaanyi mwe nateeka ebintu byonna okubituukiriza, era ebiseera eby’okutuukiriza bituuse era weebiri kaakano! Seera.

Wabula, omusango guteekwa okusooka mu nnyumba ya Adonai-YHVH era gujja kugwa mu bukakali mu busungu bw’Omwana gw’endiga obugenda okubwatukira ensi mu kukankana okw’amaanyi n’omusango omunene, era nga n’eŋŋaano yange bw’eneewulibwa n’eyawulibwa ku ngano mu kiseera ky’okubonaabona kwa Yakobo, n’ekisinga obukulu, eŋŋaano yange bwe n’etyo bw’eneeyawulibwako emirembe gyonna n’obutaddamu okuva ku ngano z’omusota, Seera!

Kubanga kaakano bwe biseera by’amakungula, n’ebiseera by’amakungula bituuse! Laba! Tunula! Nze namala dda okulanga era okutuma Omwana wange, oyo Omwana w’omuntu era ye Mukama w’amakungula; ng’alina n’ekkondeere ery’amaanyi mu mukono gwe, nga bwe kyalangirirwa edda mu Njiri ey’olubeerera!

Era mu kasera ako kennyini akateereddwawo emirembe gyonna mu mutima gwange ogw’olubeerera (kubanga akasera ako kaali kalagiddwa dda mu mutima gwange ogukola ebintu byonna ng’okuteesa kw’okwagala kwange bwe kuli), mu mutima gwange omwo mwe nnakweka ekyama kya Yave!

Laba ebiseera bituuse era tusemberedde wonnawo ekiseera n’omwaka we bisisinkanira, ekivaamu abeesigwa mu Kristo okuyimirira nga bazaaliddwa bupya mu ntebe yange ey’obwakabaka, nga bambadde ekitiibwa eky’amaanyi era nga bazaaliddwa bupya mu bulamu obw’olubeerera obw’empeera eteriggwaawo, essanyu n’ekitiibwa olw’okwagala kwabwe n’obwesigwa bwe baalaga Kristo Yesu mu biseera byabwe eby’okugezesebwa. Seera!

N’olwekyo, mumanye nti olunaku lwa Mukama luli kumpi okubagwako mu maanyi, mmwe abali mu nsi, mmwe mwenna abalowooza nti kintu kitono okugaana endagaano yange ey’emirembe, ye Yesu Omunazaaleeti, oyo Mwana gw’endiga gwa Katonda, oyo abawolereza okutuusa ku ssekendi eno mmwe mwenna, era n’amaanyi mangi nze gye nnakkiririzaamu ebigambo bye eby’okubawolereza ng’akaloosa akalungi ak’essaddaaka, akakkirizibwa ddala era akakkirizibwa YHVH Nze Ani Asinga Obukulu emirembe n’emirembe! Seera!

Oba mwenna mwandibadde nga Sodoma ne Ggomola era mwenna mwandibadde kijjukizo ekiggale eky’emisango egiyise, kale mutegeere okwagala kwange kubanga Nze kwagala era Nze Kitange w’ebitangaala, era ndokola n’omukono ogw’amaanyi, ye mukono ogw’amaanyi ogw’omwana gw’endiga gwa Katonda, era ye Mpologoma y’omu kika kya Yuda ewuluguma ng’ewolereza abo b’eyagala, Amiina.

N’olwekyo, kitegeereke, era babuulire nti Nze Ani Asinga Obukulu ye yantuma ebigambo bino ng’abiyisa mu mubaka wange ow’amaanyi era omuweereza wange, era okuyita mu mubaka wange ow’amaanyi n’omuweereza wange ebigambo byange mwe byayita!

NZE ANI atuma ebigambo byange, ebigambo bya Nze Ani Asinga Obukulu, nga mbiyisa mu Kristo Yesu era ye Yeshua HaMashiach, nga mbiyisa mu Mwoyo gw’obunnabbi, omusango gulyoke gulangirirwe mu bujjuvu, n’omusango ogw’ekibwatukira nga tegunnaba kugwa.

Kale babuulire nti Nze Ani Asinga Obukulu, NZE ANI atuma ebigambo bino mu kifo ky’omusango ogw’ekibwatukira, ebigambo eby’ekisa era ebiwa obulamu eri abawulize abanaakoowoola erinnya lya Yesu nga ye Mukama waabwe, okutuusa ku nkomerero yaabwe, Seera!

Nze Adonai YHVH, era NZE ANI atuma ebigambo bino ebiweereddwa ng’obujulizi okubalumiriza, Ggwe Babulooni eky’ekyama, ggwe malaaya omukulu ow’amawanga amangi, ezo Amagwanga Amagatte ezirigwa mu kibwatukira mu musango ogw’emirundi ebiri kaakano ku kiragiro kya Kristo Yesu era ye Yeshua HaMashiach.

N’olwekyo, nywegera omwana aleme kusunguwala n’ozikirira okuva mu kkubo. Tolina budde bwa kuwakana, wabula obw’okugondera amangu, n’olwekyo Seera mangu weenenye, kubanga tolina budde!

Okufunza

Kitegeereke nti Babulooni eky’ekyama ye Amagwanga Amagatte ga Amerika, ye malaaya omukulu atuula ku mazzi amangi era eggwanga eryakolebwa, ensolo eva mu nnyanja n’ensolo eva mu nsi ziryoke ziyimuke okuva mu vvu lyayo mu biseera byabyo eby’okubikkulirwa. Eggwanga ery’obwenzi, eggwanga ery’obwamalaaya, eggwanga mazima ddala ye malaaya omukulu eyeeyita omugoberezi omutuukirivu owa Yesu Omunazaaleeti. Naye mu mazima ye ggwanga abazimbi baalyo abaatagattibwa ku Lwazi lw’Omusingi olw’amazima, olwo olwazi olw’oku nsonda olw’amaanyi abatalina kukkiriza kwe beesittala, ye Yesu Omunazaaleeti era ye Yeshua HaMashiach.

Emisango n’Ebikolwa Ebibi

Operation War-seed / Okukulaakulanya Akabonero k’Ensolo

  1. Ggwe Babulooni eky’ekyama, n’obulogo bwo n’amasasiro go wasendasenda ensi okukkiriza obutwa, omusingi n’eky’okulwanyisa eky’obutonde eky’enjawulo kye baawa obutwa abo abaagala ensi n’obusamize bwabwe ne bagaana okwenenya kubanga nali naabawonyezza!
  2. Tegeera nti Operation War-seed n’okuluma kwayo okw’omusota tebyali kabonero ka nsolo, naye akabonero k’ensolo kajja kukwekebwa mu ddagala era kirayiro kikakafu eky’obwesigwa okukkiriza tekinologiya waabwe ajja okusangulawo emirembe gyonna erinnya lya Yave okuva mu oyo akkiriza.
  3. N’okuluma kwennyini okw’omusota okwa sisitiimu yaabwe eyamala dda okuteekebwawo n’okugezesebwa, era n’enkuyanja y’abantu yamala dda okuyigirizibwa okukkiriza akabonero kaabwe era n’olukwe lwabwe olwa Operation War-seed lwatuuka ku buwanguzi.
  4. Okuva gy’oli Babulooni eky’ekyama, mwe mwava tekinologiya bino eby’omuzimu, era olw’obulimba bwo n’obukumpanya bwo enkuyanja y’abantu yatiibwa obutwa era omusango gwo tegweraliikirira.

Okukkiriza ekibi

  1. Bangi ku mmwe muli ba malala nnyo kubanga mugezaako okunfuula Mukama wammwe ate nga mukyalina omutima ogutalina kukkiriza, kubanga n’omutima omuntu gw’akkiririzaamu okutuuka ku butuukirivu. Kale mube n’okukkiriza mulyoke mulokolebwe; okukkiriza kwammwe kuliragibwa eri bonna nga mwegayirira ekibi ne muva mu makubo gammwe amakyafu.
  2. Ababi batambulira ku njuyi zonna, abasajja abasinga obubi bwe bagulumizibwa. Naye mukyakkaanya n’amakubo g’omuliisamaanyi, era muyamba okuleeta ekibi ekinene mu nsi, okutuuka n’okulonda abakwasi b’abaana okukulembera ensi zammwe. Temulina kutegeera na kutono n’akatono, era temugoberera kkubo lyange; era bwe munyiikira, nja kubawaayo ddala mu kwegomba kwammwe okw’obugwagwa okuzikirizibwa kw’omubiri omwoyo gulyoke gulokolebwe.
  3. Leka nzigule amaaso gammwe, mmwe abatayogera era abazibe b’amaaso ku lwammwe, mmwe abatudde mu kazambi n’ekizikiza, nga mwekulukuunya mu ttosi. Temuyinza kukkaanya na kibi ne mulowooza nti mumpeereza, kubanga naziyiza ekibi n’okukemebwa okutuusa ettondo ly’omusaayi erisembayo lye lyankulukuta ku ttaka eddungi, kubanga nze Adamu ow’enkomerero era nfuuse mwoyo omuzzukusa.
  4. Bangi ku mmwe muziibiddwa amaaso n’ebigambo ebirungi n’emboozi ennungi ne muwa okukkiriza kwammwe okujjuvu abamenyi b’amateeka n’abantu ababi ennyo abalya ku baana. Bakwata abaana, babonyaabonya abaana olw’okwegomba kwabwe okubi: Kubanga bwe gutyo obusungu bwa Katonda bwe bujja ku Babulooni eky’ekyama, naye bangi ku mmwe munyiikira okuwa okukkiriza kwammwe eri ensolo zino eziteeberezebwa eziterekerwa omuliro ogw’olubeerera ogutazikire.
  5. Mufulume mu ye, abantu bange, oba muligabana naye mu bibonyoobonyo bye n’omusango ogw’entiisa ogugenda okumujjira; era nabalabula. Kubanga Kristo alina kutabagana ki ne Beriali? Bwe mugabana naye mu bulimba bwe, muligabana naye mu bibonyoobonyo bye; kale mweyawule ddala mu kigambo ne mu kikolwa, so temukkiriziganya nabo n’akatono. Temubeera mu bulamu obw’obutali na kufaayo, obujjudde okwekiriranya; kubanga nja kubonereza bonna abakola bwe batyo. Mulowooza nti olw’okuba mulina obulamu obutaggwaawo temuyinza kufuna kufiirwa kunene na bizibu? Nja kubaggyako empeera zammwe ne nzigabira abalala, era muliba abasinza obutono mu bwakabaka bw’eggulu bwe munyiikira, kubanga bw’atyo bw’ayogera Yeshua HaMashiach, Omuwasi, eri Omugole wange!
  6. Kitegeereke! Abalabe ba Babulooni eky’ekyama tebalina kusaasira, tebalina kusaasira, tebalina kisa, tebalina kwenenya, era bakyawa ddala abantu b’omu Babulooni eky’ekyama olw’ebibi eby’entiisa bye yakola ku nsi zaabwe. Ate mu kiseera ekyo kyonna bangi ku mmwe mwajagulizanga entalo zammwe ezitali za nsonga nga mulaba n’essanyu ku televizoni zammwe, nga musanyukira nnyo ebibi by’abakulembeze bammwe. Muwadde okukkiriza kwammwe abakulembeze bammwe be mwasemba ebibi byabwe ng’omululu, era bwe mutyo muligabana nabo mu musango gwabwe, kubanga mwefuulidde abasaanira omugabo gw’omusango ogw’emirundi ebiri, era gujja kubabwatukira.
  7. Laba! Abakulembeze b’ensi yonna bakolera wamu mu kyama, era bonna baweereza Setaani. Temulina bakulembeze abalondeddwa, naye mubadde muweebwa okulonda okw’obulimba kubanga temwalondangako abo be mulowooza nti mulonda n’akalulu, naye babaleetera mulonde balyoke bafune okukkiriza kwammwe, n’okukkiriza kwammwe kibi kubanga temuyinza kulonda na bugenderevu ekibi ekitono ne munyimirirawo ku lwange mu kiseera kye kimu, ekyo kirabiriro.
  8. Babulooni eky’ekyama, ggwe okkiriza n’okwagala abafuzi bo aba Setaani, abamanyi obulungi enteekateeka zaabwe era abamanyi obulungi nti bali mu kussawo okuzikiriza okujjuvu eggwanga lyo eddene edda ne ggwe.
  9. Kubanga ggwe Babulooni eky’ekyama mu bwamalaaya bwo n’obwenzi bwo, osula n’omulabe.
  10. Kubanga bwe gutyo bwe guli, ggwe wabeera mu by’okweyagala ate abakulembeze bo nga bazikiriza amawanga amalala n’okukkiriza kwo.
  11. Kubanga bwe gutyo bwe guli, Babulooni eky’ekyama, ggwe wajaguza abakulembeze bo nga baleeta akabi ku mbalama z’amawanga agatakoma mu ntalo zaabwe ezitali za nsonga ze wasanyukirangamu ng’olaba ku televizoni.
  12. Kubanga ye Sinagoga ya Setaani eyalonze n’eronka Moshiach waabwe omupya ow’obulimba era Babulooni eky’ekyama eddemu okuwa okukkiriza kwayo eri ogusota.
  13. Nga biri mu maaso go ממש, era olw’okukkiriza okw’akalulu ko n’olw’obugayaavu bwo obw’ekito, eggwanga lyo lyonna lyagattuluddwa mu maaso go ממש.

Ekikopo kya Zaabu: Obwenzi n’Obwamalaaya, n’ebirala.

  1. Kimanyibwe nti omusango gugenda kugwa ku Babulooni Ekikulu mangu, era gujja kuba musango gwa ntiisa, naye kigugwanidde. Ggwe Amerika, ndikugumiikiriza kutuuka ddi? Wava mu ggwanga ly’abatukuvu eryali ng’ekitara ekimulisa ensi okujja gyendi, n’ofuuka eggwanga ly’abakazi abenzi ab’obwenzi.
  2. Njawudde abantu bange okuva gy’oli, era okwawukana kuno kuli kumpi okutuukirizibwa ng’emirembe gino egy’ekisa giggwaako. Ggwe Amerika, lwaki ogoberera omubi, n’okkiriza enguzi ze ezitaliiko mugaso n’onsuulawo, nze Kristo ow’emirembe n’emirembe, mu kitiibwa kyange kyonna n’ekifaananyi kyange? Era malaaya omukulu ayonoonye ensi n’ekikopo ekya zzaabu ekiri mu mukono gwe, ekijjudde eby’emizizo n’obugwagwa bw’obwenzi bwe.
  3. Naye ggwe, ayi Amerika, agaana eby’omwoyo era anyooma baliraanwa be, kaakano ojja kugumiikiriza omusango omunene ku nsi yo, era Babulooni Ekikulu tekiriddayo kubaawo. Babulooni, ofuuse eggwanga ly’abakazi abenzi abazannya omuzannyo n’ebiteeteeyi byammwe waggulu w’emitwe gyammwe ng’endogoyi enkazi ey’omu nsiko ey’omukwano ng’erindiridde omuyise addako.
  4. Mwefuulidde abananfu n’omubiri omugenyi, mwesiimye era mwagadde ebibi byammwe, era omusango ogw’emirembe n’emirembe gujja kugwa ku abo bonna ab’emitima emikakanyavu ababala ssaddaaka yange ng’ekintu ekyangu era ekitaliiko mugaso okutunuulira.
  5. Mufuuse eggwanga ly’abakozi b’ebibi n’abamenyi b’endagaano abakyusa abakyala ng’engoye enkadde okufuna empya okutuusa empya lw’egwaawuka. Ebigambo byammwe ebikulu bye mwogedde okuwakanya Kitange bijja kuvunaanibwa.
  6. Kubanga olwali ekirannamo ky’essuubi n’ekitara eri ensi okumala akaseera katono, naye mulonze omululu, okwekkaanya, n’obukenuzi okuba bannaŋŋanda bammwe, ggwe malaaya omukulu!
  7. Naye ggwe, ggwe malaaya omukulu, weekyaye n’abagalwa bangi olw’omululu ogw’ebbeeyi n’essanyu ly’ekibi.
  8. Okuzikirira okungi kuli ku malaaya, Babulooni ekikulu, eyatamiiza ensi n’obwenzi bwe n’obukaba bwe, kubanga abadde n’abagalwa bangi. Abo abatagenda kwesasuzaako bajja kugabana naye mu musango gwe, kale lwaki mujja kukyasinziika kufa okusinga obulamu bwe nnabawa, obwasasulirwa mu musaayi gwange ogutaliiko kibi?
  9. Kubanga mufuuse eggwanga ly’abasinza ebifaananyi, eggwanga ly’abalwanyisa entalo, eggwanga erirya obulimba era eriraga omukwano mu kulimbibwa kwabwe, eggwanga erijjuzza ensi eby’emizizo byammwe; era amawanga ganywa ku kikopo kyammwe ekya zzaabu ekijjudde eby’emizizo n’obugwagwa bw’obwenzi bwammwe.
  10. Era malaaya omukulu ayonoonye ensi n’ekikopo ekya zzaabu ekiri mu mukono gwe, ekijjudde eby’emizizo n’obugwagwa bw’obwenzi bwe.
  11. Musensense amawanga mu makubo gammwe ag’obubi n’okukkiriziganya, era mubatamizza n’obugwagwa bw’obwenzi bwammwe.
  12. Kubanga muva mu lutalo ne muyingira mu lutalo, era musaasanyizza ddala ensi, ez’ebweru n’ez’awaka.
  13. Malaaya omukulu eyalwana n’amawanga gonna ge yatumibwa okuwangula kaakano asibiddwa, era talina buyambi era yeeteeseteese okuwangulwa mu bujjuvu.

Okugaana Endagaano Empya / Okwegatta ku Abo Abaagwa

  1. Ggwe, Babulooni eky’Ekyaama: weegaanyi muliraanwa wo, era tewali kutya Mukama mu mitima gyammwe. Ogambye nti, Nze siri nnamwandu, naye oli nnamwandu era muzibe w’amaaso era kiggala mu maaso gange.
  2. Waleese abantu bange okubaggya gyendi mu nsi yonna olw’obugwagwa bwe waawa amawanga okulya n’okunywa, era ojja kusuulibwa ddala, era toliddayo kubaawo.
  3. Kubanga ggwe, Babulooni eky’Ekyaama, weegaanyi eddoboozi lyange n’onteeka, nze Yesu Kristo Omutuukirivu, ku bbali olw’essanyu ly’ekibi. Kubanga n’okulabula okukulu okwaweebwa ku ggwanga lyo mu ggulu kwanguyiza kuleeta ssanyu eri emitima gyo egitali mukkiriza n’amaaso go ag’okwegomba, era gy’oli lwali lunaku lulala lwa bulijjo nga weeyagala mu bibi byo ebikuleetera okuzikirira kwo. Wandinenyezza!
  4. N’amakanisa go gonna ag’obujeemu gajja kuzikirizibwa era malaaya oyo omukulu agenda kusuulibwa wansi ne Nnabagereka w’eggulu ajja kwokebwa! Laba! Mbasalidde omusango bonna abatali bange, era n’amakanisa gonna amakyamu agogera obulimba obungi mu bunnanfuusi. Omusango Gugwa!
  5. Naye muzzibye ebirowoozo byammwe, muzibye amatu gammwe, mukakanyazizza emitima gyammwe, era mwasamizza emimwa gyammwe mu butakkiriza obw’okuvvoola, nga muvvoola n’endagaano ey’emirembe Yahwe gye yabawa. Kubanga nze Yesu Kristo, nze mirembe gyammwe!
  6. Naye, mu kifo ky’okunfuula Mukama wammwe, mukozesa erinnya lyange ettukuvu, nze Yesu Kristo, mu bukyamu ng’ekigambo ekirala eky’okulaga obugwagwa obuli mu mitima gyammwe.
  7. Mu busirusiru mulowooza nti muyinza okwetegekera obusungu obugenda okujja nga mubeera ku nsi, awali awantu awayawukanye ku bannaŋŋanda, wabula mujja kuzikirizibwa ddala, okukwatibwa, okuttibwa, okuyiggibwa, okulumwa enjala, okukwatibwa endwadde, era kawumpuli mungi ajja kugwa ku abo bonna abanjeegaana nze Yeshua HaMashiach, kubanga okunjeegaana kwe kwegaana Katonda omukulu ow’emirembe n’emirembe, ye Yahwe yennyini era tewalibaawo ddagala.
  8. Mwenenye! Mufulume mu bo era mubeere baawule! Nze, ye Mukama Yesu Kristo mu lulimi Olungereza ne Yeshua HaMashiach mu Luwebbulaniya, saaletawo kigonjoolo kya byabufuzi era tewali mukulembeze w’ensi ajja kuyimuka kubanga saawaayo, era abo abakkiriza obulala balimbibwa nnyo.
  9. Lwaki mukyanoonya abakulembeze b’ensi mu kifo kyange, Kristo Yesu? Kubanga nze ndi Mukama wammwe, Kabaka wammwe, Masiya wammwe, ne Kabona wammwe Asinga Obukulu Yahwe gwe yawa, era nagula ensi n’obulamu bw’abanunule mu musaayi gwange ogw’okutangirira ogutaliiko kibi.
  10. Mwakyame ne muyingira mu makubo ga Baali era mufuuse nga Setaani bw’abakulembedde, kubanga musukkiridde mu nneeyisa yammwe ey’obutakkiririza mu kintu kyonna. Kkola ky’oyagala ye mateeka gaabwe gonna ge mwefuulidde agammwe.
  11. Mazima, mazima, eri Babulooni eky’Ekyaama n’okumala emyaka mingi egy’essanyu kaakano ntumye abatume bange, ntumye bannabbi bange, ntumye abavumbuzi bange, ntumye abasumba bange, ntumye abayigiriza bange, ntumye abayigirizwa bange abeesigwa n’abooluganda era ntumye ne bannamwandu mu bwavu bwabwe, era nfuse ddala omwoyo gwange okutuuka ku nkomerero mu nnaku zino ezisembayo ez’ekisa n’okusaasira!
  12. Naye mu kifo ky’amawanga okutuula mu bibukutu n’evvu, basanyusibwa obubonero n’okulabula okuweebwa okw’omusango ogugenda okujja nga bakuba ebifaananyi ne vidiyo okusanyusa kwabwe.
  13. Bagaanyi ababaka bange abaaleeta ekitangaala ky’enjiri yange ey’obulokozi, basekereredde era banyooma bannabbi bange, balumbye abavumbuzi bange, bayonoonye abasumba bange, era bayigganyizza n’okukema abayigiriza bange.
  14. Muli bazibe b’amaaso? Mugaana okutegeera obubonero bwabwe musobole okweyongera okutuula mu butamanya obw’okwesalirawo nti abakulembeze bammwe ba mu baagwa? Temutegeera nti abasanyusa bammwe ba mu baagwa?
  15. Temukimanyi nti tebali ba mbeera y’emu nammwe, kubanga bangi beekyusizza emirembe gyonna mu bulalu bwabwe era abamu nsolo okuva lwe baazaalibwa era Setaani ye kitaabwe?
  16. Naye mulondawo okutuula mu kizikiza ky’okwesalirawo, ne mweraga era ne mwoota mu bibi byammwe nga mwetooloola mu ttosi n’embizzi ezibakulembedde okutuuka ku miryango gy’omuliro omunene ennyo abangi gye beeraga mu bukyamu mu malala n’amalala.
  17. Olw’okuba yeewaayo mu kuweereza abalabe be ab’emirembe n’emirembe, kaakano bajja kuleetawo okuzikirira kwe okw’emirembe n’emirembe kubanga Babulooni eky’Ekyaama tekiriddayo kuyimuka.
  18. Laba! Ekiseera kyonna abakulembeze ba Babulooni eky’ekyama balimbye amawanga, nga bagamba nti malaaya omukulu aweereza Katonda, era ayogerera nze n’eyeeyita Omukristaayo, n’okutuusa lwe yaweereza mu kyama abaagwa okuva lwe yatandika.
  19. Babulooni eky’Ekyaama, ebiseera byo bituuse, n’ennaku zo zikomekkerezeddwa. Nze, Yesu Omunazaaleeti, nkayiye amaziga mangi nnyo olw’okuzikirira kwo okuli okumpi era ntumye obubonero waggulu, n’ebyamagero mu bbanga, n’obubonero ku nsi era n’amataba amangi ag’amangu ddala gabaddewo mu kiseera ekituufu era wakati mu ye era Babulooni eky’Ekyaama n’akyaziba amatu ge mu bujeemu obw’obukakanyavu.
  20. Naye mu kifo ky’eggwanga eddene eryali lissa ekitiibwa mu makubo ga Yahwe era abantu gye baagobereranga Yesu Omunazaaleeti era nga tebakwatibwa nsonyi kwagala baliraanwa baabwe, wajja kusigalawo eddungu Babulooni eky’Ekyaama w’erisuulibwa mu ssaawa emu kubanga Mukama Katonda amusalira omusango wa maanyi.
  21. Olw’okuba omuntu ow’obutonde ayagala ekizikiza, kaakano nja kumuwa ekizikiza ky’ayagala ennyo.
  22. Ne misango ki egyalangirirwa mu kulabula abantu b’omu Babulooni eky’Ekyaama, okulabula kwe baasuulirira ddala nga banyoomye mu butakkiriza. Era n’okulabula kwange okungi ennyo okwaweebwa mu mimwa gya bannabbi bange abangi abalangiridde mu bwesigwa Ebigambo bino eby’okulabula okumala emyaka mingi ennyo kaakano.
  23. Era bwe kityo Babulooni eky’Ekyaama kaakano kirangirira eby’omu maaso byakyo okuba nga bya ddembe okuva mu musango gwa Yahwe nga banoonya okwewonya nga banyoomye mu makubo gaabwe agatalina kukkiriza.
  24. Era n’omusango gwe beesalirawo bwe baasuulirira eddoboozi lyange lye nnatuma ng’eyita mu batume bange ne bannabbi n’abaweereza abakulu n’abato: mazima asuuliridde omu ne bonna okutuusa lw’anafa era talyadda nate kuyimuka.
  25. Kubanga abo abandisobodde okugoberera Yesu Omunazaaleeti nga Mukama waabwe kaakano bawoowoddwamu mu mikono gy’omulimba kubanga baagala okubeera mu kulimbibwa kwabwe, nga balowooza nti nga bwe kiri leero, bwe kyali jjo, era mazima ddala kabaka ono omukulu ajja kumpumuza nate!
  26. Naye mu mazima aweereza abaagwa n’ebigendererwa bye kaakano biggwaako, era beegomba okuzikirizibwa kwe basobole okubikka ku misango gyabwe era abasumba baabwe abagwagwa basobole okulabika ng’ab’empisa ennungi bwe bayimuka basobole okukweka omutego gwabwe.
  27. Era ensolo eva mu nnyanja n’ensolo eva mu nsi by’emizizo emibi ebyatondebwa okuzikiriza abantu. Kubanga baatwala Eweema ya Moloki, n’emmunyeenye ya katonda waabwe Remphan, ebifaananyi bye baakola, okubisinza, era bingi nnyo bye baakola ebijja okubikkulwa mu bujjuvu mu biseera ebituufu eby’okubikkula kubanga buli kyama kijja kujja mu kitangaala.

Okusanyusibwa Obubi / Pharmakeia - Eddagala / Essanyu ly'Ekibi

  1. Ggwe Amerika: Mu busirusiru bwammwe mulowooza nti byonna bizzewo nga bwe byali, era nti olutalo olujja luyinza okulabirwa ku ttivi nga entalo endala abakulembeze bammwe ze baalwana mu ngeri etali ya nsonga. Nga bwe mwasanyukira okufa n'okuzikirira kw'amawanga amalala, era bwe kityo eggwanga lyammwe bwe linaazikirizibwa ddala, era okufa kwammwe n'okuzikirira kwammwe kuliba kyakuliyirira abo abaazikirizibwa n'okukkiriza kwammwe.
  2. Ekiseera kyammwe eky'okwaka kyajja kubanga okumala akaseera, Bajjajjammwe baagoberera Omwana wa Katonda ne bakwata Ebigambo byange. Naye emirembe gy'Ekkanisa yange giggwaako kubanga mwatunda eby'obugagga by'ebyama byange, era n'emirembe n'okwagala kw'abantu bange mwabisuubula olw'obugagga obw'obugwenyufu n'ettendo mu maaso g'abantu.
  3. Kubanga Babulooni ow'Ekyama yakulembera amawanga mu kibi ekinene n'obulimba okuyita mu bulogo bwe n'obusamize bwe, n'ebyo bye yayita eby'okwesanyusa eby'emikolo gye, era n'obusamize bwe obw'omuziro bwe yayisa ng'eddagala ate nga mu mazima busagwa bwa kufa.
  4. Mweteeka mu bbugumu ery'omululu n'obutali bwesigwa, era mwannabulira, ne nze Yesu Kristo, eyafiirira ebibi by'ensi. Naye mwagaana ekigambo kyange, mwagaana okunenya kwange, mwagaana okuteesa kwange, mwagaana ababaka bange, mwagaana bannabbi bange, mwagaana abatume bange, era mwannakana nze, Yesu Omunazaaleeti.
  5. Ggwe ggwe omusirusiru asinza ebifaananyi by'ensi ya Babulooni ow'Ekyama, singa wanditegedde embeera yo. Singa wanditeeze amatu go eri ebigambo byange kubanga nnakoowoola, neegayirira, ne nsaba, ne nkubiriza, ne njogera n'eddoboozi ddene, ne ndaga obubonero waggulu n'obubonero mu nsi, naye mu kifo ky'okwambaza omutima gwo ebibukutu, mu kifo ky'okutuula mu vvu ery'okwenenya, ggwe wasanyusibwa ng'alabira ku bukuusa bwa ddagala.
  6. Awataali kumanya mwawaayo abaana bammwe abatannazaalibwa okwongera okuzimba obwakabaka bw'ekisolo, era abaagwa baakkiriza ssaddaaka zammwe. Baakozesa omusaayi gw'abataliiko musango nga bwe baakola ebbanga lyonna mu kukola eby'emiziro byabwe, era nga bwe kyali mu nnaku za Nuuwa bwe kiriba. Kubanga oyo aziyiza bw'aliggibwawo, olwo mulitegeera obunene obw'ekitalo n'omuwendo gw'eby'emiziro byabwe, kubanga balisumululwa awatali kuziyizibwa kwonna.

Obulamu obw'Ebibi obunyumira / Amalala / Emitima Emikakanyavu

  1. Kubanga yabeera mu kibi ekinene mu ngeri enyumira; atuula mu malala amangi ng'alowooza nti tasobola kugwa era ebintu birigenda mu maaso, jjo nga leero, n'enkya nga bwe kiriba bulijjo era tekikyuka.
  2. Naye mu malala gammwe n'enkola zammwe ez'ensi, mwakakanyaza emitima gyammwe okung'eemera n'okulokoka kwange.
  3. Ggwe, Babulooni ow'Ekyama, singa wanditegedde ekiweebwayo eky'emirembe Kitange kye yakutumira, naye kaakano wannakana, nze Yesu Omunazaaleeti, era kibaddewo okusinziira ku mawandiiko amatukuvu nti abantu ababi n'abawenzi balyeyongera okwonooneka ennyo.
  4. Okuzikirizibwa okw'amangu kuli ku mmwe era abalabe bammwe beeteeseteese, naye mukyabeera mu binyuma wadde nga muli ggwanga erisibiddwa mu njegere mu maaso gange, nze Kristo Yesu.
  5. Ggwe Babulooni ow'Ekyama, oli nsi y'okumatizibwa amangu, era toli mugezi n'akatono.
    6. Obusaalizi bw'eggwanga lyammwe buyinza okuziba emitima gyammwe gyendi, ggwe Babulooni ow'Ekyama: ne mmwe Amerika. Kubanga mulowooza nti mutuula nga nnabagereka era tewali kabi kali kabatuukako, era mwegamba nti, Ssi nnamwandu!
  6. Kubanga n'amawulire gammwe geegayirira okubasendasenda okwebaka era abangi batuula mu kizikiza nga tebalaba mazima nga bagamba nti: Ntuula nga Nnabagereka era siri nnamwandu era siriraba kukungubaga.
  7. Enkya eriba nga leero, kubanga leero liri nga jjo. Nga bw'eyeegulumiza, era n'abeera mu ssanyu, era bwe mutyo mumuwe okubonyaabonyezebwa n'ennaku.
  8. Kubanga mu ngeri eyo, mwabeera mu binyuma ng'abakulembeze bammwe bazikiriza amawanga amalala n'okukkiriza kwammwe era n'ensimbi zammwe, muli na musango nga omuzimbakase w'omu Edomu gwe mwawaayo ezzadde lyammwe olw'essanyu ly'ebibi.
  9. Kaakano okusalirwa omusango kwo kujja kutuukiridde, ggwe Babulooni ow'Ekyama, era oli kyakulabirako eri bonna, naye weekuumira mu butamanya n'obuzibe bw'amaaso ng'ogamba mu mitima gyo nti akabi tekajja kukutuukako.

Ebikolwa ebibi ebikolebwa ku bataliiko musango

  1. Ggwe Babulooni ow'Ekyama asuubula abaana abato n'abawa mu mikono gy'abantu ababi, abagenda okukomezebwa. Kyandibadde kirungi ne batabaawo okusinga okuzaalibwa ne bakola ebibi eby'obujeemu n'obukambwe ku baana.
  2. Naye ggwe, ggwe Babulooni omunene, wavumisa omutayimbwa gwa Kitange ogw'amabala amangi gwe yawa Nuuwa n'abantu bonna ng'ekiragaano nti taliddamu kuzikiriza nsi yonna na mazzi, era ajja kukwata ekigambo kye. Naye ggwe, ggwe Babulooni ow'Ekyama, ojja kusaanyizibwawo mu ssaawa emu.
  3. Era nkwogerera ggwe omuntu ow'obutonde, era nkwogerera ggwe Babulooni ow'Ekyama. Kubanga oli na musango gw'omusaayi gw'abataliiko musango, n'abo be waggya mu by'okwerinda by'enda za bamaama baabwe. Wabikka okukuba kw'ekisu mu kuwoza okw'obulimba n'ensonga ezaawula ezifuula ekikemo kyo eky'omululu eky'okutta abataliiko musango ng'ekintu ekya bulijjo ng'okugenda ewa omusawo, era amaduuka go ag'obutemu gavunya bubi mu nnyindo za Kitange, era gavunya bubi mu nnyindo zange. Kubanga omusaayi gw'abataliiko musango gukaabira okuva mu nsi okutuuka ku ntebe ya Yakuwa mu ggulu ery'eggulu alaba byonna, n'abo bonna abanoonya okwekweka okuva mu kutunuulira kwe okw'olubeerera.
  4. Kubanga mwatta abataliiko musango, nga mulowooza mu butamanya nti ekikolwa kino eky'omuziro kintu kitono. Mwatunda abaana bammwe abatannazaalibwa olw'obugagga obw'obugwenyufu mulyoke mukule mu mirimu gyammwe, n'emirimu gyammwe egy'obwereere kubanga gyonna gijja kuggwaawo era n'ebyo bye mwagala ennyo eby'ensi eby'obwereere byonna bijja kusaanawo mu musango ne bitaddamu kujjukirwa. Kubanga mwawaayo abaana bammwe abatannazaalibwa mu ssaddaaka eri Moloki nga temumanyi, era mutta abaana bammwe abatannazaalibwa n'amalala amangi.
  5. Mwasaddaaka omusaayi gw'abataliiko musango ku byoto by'okwemalira, era mwayiwa ebisuse byammwe ebikubiddwa ng'ebiwero eby'obugwagwa ebisuuliddwa mu bintu by'eddagala eby'obucaafu, era mwabala omwoyo gw'obulamu oguli mu musaayi nga kintu kitatukuvu era omusango gwammwe guli ku mmwe. Naye abaana bammwe bennyini, n'abaana bammwe abatannazaalibwa, baali tebakyali mu misono gyammwe egiggwaawo, era obulamu bwabwe bwali buzito ku mmwe okweteeka mu ssanyu ly'ebibi.
  6. Muwoza era musaba omusaayi gw'abataliiko musango mu nfumintiriza zammwe ez'eby'obufuzi ez'obutemu, era nga mumanyi mwegatta n'abaagwa mu bujeemu bwabwe, kubanga Sitaani ze zaayigiriza okukuba ekisu mu lubuto, n'enkola zammwe ez'obutemu ez'omuwendo ze munyumirirwamu ennyo okukuuma n'okweraga. Era omusaayi gw'abataliiko musango gukaabira okuva mu nsi n'obuzito bw'ebibi byammwe buleetedde ensi okuzunga ng'omutamiivu, era okukankana kuliba kunene n'okugwa kuliba kunene! Babulooni Omukulu Agudde, Agudde!
  7. Kubanga n'amawulire gammwe geegayirira okubasendasenda okwebaka era abangi batuula mu kizikiza nga tebalaba mazima nga bagamba nti: Ntuula nga Nnabagereka era siri nnamwandu era siriraba kukungubaga.
  8. Enkya eriba nga leero, kubanga leero liri nga jjo. Nga bw'eyeegulumiza, era n'abeera mu ssanyu, era bwe mutyo mumuwe okubonyaabonyezebwa n'ennaku.
  9. Mwakyamyizibwa ne muyingira mu makubo g'Abakanani, kubanga nabo baawaayo abaana baabwe nga ssaddaaka, n'amalala n'essanyu lingi, nga nammwe bwe mukyakola! Naye mu malala gammwe mulowooza nti temufaanana na abo ab'edda abasirusiru era abatali bayigirize era nti enkola zammwe zisinga ku z'abasavage bano ab'edda, naye mukola kye kimu.
  10. Era Babulooni ow'Ekyama taddemu kubaawo, n'ekibuga kye ekisinga obunene ekisitudde mu malala ng'ekirabika nti tewali kijja kukyuka kirigwa wamu nakyo ddala, nga tekiriddayo kugolokoka.
