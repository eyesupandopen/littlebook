Omujulirwa Omulamba: Ekkanisa
Mu Biwandiiko by'Omutume Pawulo
Nakasenene 30, 2024

Katonda ye Kwagala!

Baana bange, mutegeere obuzaale bwammwe obw'amazima, kubanga obwammwe bwe buzaale Katonda bwe yabawa mu nze, Kristo Yesu, kubanga Katonda ye kwagala! Naye, okwagala kwa Katonda tekufaanana na kwa muntu, nga n'amakubo ga Katonda bwe gatali ga muntu; naye muyigira amakubo ga Katonda mu nze, kubanga nze ndi musomesa wammwe era omusumba wammwe. Kubanga Katonda bwe yatyo bwe yayagala ensi, n'awaayo Omwana we eyazaalibwa omu yekka, buli muntu amukkiriza aleme okubula, naye afune obulamu obutaggwaawo. Ne mu kikolwa kino eky'okuwaayo nze, Yesu Kristo, Omwana we eyazaalibwa omu yekka olw'ebibi by'abantu, okwagala kwe kulabisibwa mu nze mu ngeri etuukiridde. Kubanga ndi wa Kitange era ndi wa buzaale bumu era omwoyo gwe gumu, kubanga Katonda Mwoyo, era abo abamusinza bateekwa okumusiniza mu mwoyo ne mu mazima, kubanga nze ne Kitange tuli bumu mu mwoyo, mu bumunye, ne mu kigendererwa.

Mutegeere nti okwagala kwa Katonda tekufaanana na kwa muntu, kubanga okwagala kw'omuntu kulina obukwakulizo, era omuntu eyagwa y'atera okulaga okwagala kwe mu kuwa n'okufuna ebirungi, era okwagala kw'omuntu kukyukira munda okuganyula oyo ayagala, kubanga bwe kutyo okwagala kw'omuntu bwe kuli. Okwagala kwa Katonda emirundi mingi kulabikira mu kuwa kyereere okuganyula omulala, n'okuweereza munno awatali kisuubira musimbi ebiseera ebimu, n'okukyalira bannamwandu, n'okuwonya abalwadde, n'okuzimba Omubiri gwa Kristo. Noolwekyo mbabuuliriza, baana bange, okuyigira ku nze Kristo Yesu, kubanga andabyeko aba alabye ne Kitange, era newakubadde temunandaba na maaso gammwe, mu bbanga ttono mujja kundaba.

Kubanga alina omukisa oyo atalabye naye n'akkiriza, era mwandebera mu mwoyo ne mu mazima, era mwandebera mu makubo gange ne mu kwagala kwe ntuma ne mu mulimu abaweereza bange gwe bankolera ku lwammwe. Noolwekyo mummanyi era mwandebera, era bwe mutambulira mu mwoyo gwa Kristo oguzaalibwa munda, muba mutambulira ddala mu kwagala, kubanga bwe butyo obuzaale bwange bwe nabawa, era ne Kitange bwe yawa obuzaale bwe gyendi: Laba, okwagala okw'ekika Kitange ky'eyatuwa, okuyitibwa abaana ba Katonda: ensi kyeva etatumanya, kubanga teyamumanya ye. Era nate okuva mu bbaluwa ya Yokaana ey'olubereberye: Muno mwe muli okwagala, si lwa kuba nga ffe twayagala Katonda, naye kubanga ye yatwagala, n'atuma Omwana we okuba ekiweebwayo olw'ebibi byaffe. Abaagalwa, oba nga Katonda yatwagala bwe yatyo, naffe kitugwanira okwagalananga.

Baana bange abato, muli baana ba Katonda omulamu, ye Yahweh, kubanga nga Yahweh bwe yasimba olusuku ebuvanjuba mu Adeni, bw'atyo yasiga ensigo z'omwoyo gw'obuzaaliranwa mu mmwe mwennyini, kubanga nze ndi mulimi we alabirira emmeeme zammwe. Kubanga mwatondebwa buggya mu nze, era mwatondebwa mu kwagala, era okwagala kwa Katonda kuli mu buzaale bwammwe mwennyini. Mutegeere nti okunululwa kwammwe okujjuvu kuli ku mulyango, lwe mulimanya nga nammwe bwe mwamanyibwa. Omugole wange, nnina bingi eby'okukunnyonnyola n'okukuyigiriza n'okukubikkulira ebikukwatako n'ebigendererwa bye mwatukuzibwa okutuukiriza, kubanga kyabadde kyama mu mutima gwa Kitange okuva nga n'ensi tennatondebwa, era mu bbanga ttono mujja kutegeera.

Kale ani alibawula ku kwagala kwa Katonda? Okubonyaabonyezebwa, oba ennaku, oba okuyigganyizibwa, oba enjala, oba obwereere, oba akabi, oba ekitala? Nedda, muli basinga abawanguzi mu nze, Kristo Yesu, era okwagala kwange kuleetebwa ku nkomerero etuukiridde mu mmwe, Omugole wange, kubanga ebiseera bituuse era mu bbanga ttono mujja kumanya. Okwagala kwa Kitange tekusobola kuwangulwa, kubanga ye tawanguzika, kubanga Katonda ye kwagala, era okwagala bwe buzaale bwa Yahweh yennyini kubanga Katonda ye kwagala. Obulimba bwa Setaani bungi bw'akozesa okubakyamya mulimu n'okulaga okwagala ng'okunafu, okutali kwekomerere, okwefaako, n'okutagasa: Kubanga ago ge makubo g'omuntu ow'obutonde.

Mazima, Setaani yeerimbika, kubanga ye yafuulibwa atagasa, era okwagala kwa Kitange tekusobola kuwangulwa, kubanga tewali alinga ye! Mu nze okwagala kwe kwatuukirizibwa mu mubiri, kubanga olw'okwagala kwange eri Kitange n'eri mmwe, Omugole wange, neeyo nze nzekka ekiweebwayo ekitaliiko kibi; kubanga mwagalwa nnyo. Era mumanye nti muli mpeke ya liiso lya Yahweh, nammwe, Omugole wange, muli mpeke ya liiso lyange, era mu kwagala nabawanula mu mukono gw'omulabe mu ngeri etuukiridde, awatali kikendeezeddwa, mu musaayi gwange ogutangirira. Abo abanunulibwa Mukama boogere bwe batyo, abo be yanunula okuva mu mukono gw'omulabe!

Baana bange, kasita Katonda abagala bw'atyo, kale kibagwanira okukkiriza okwagala kwe n'okukkiriza ebigambo bye ebibakwatako n'okuwonyezebwa okw'amaanyi Kitange kwe yakola mu ssaddaaka yange nga Ppaasika yammwe etuukiridde. Abangi ku mmwe temutegeera butuukirivu bwa kitonde kye nabafuula mu nze, Kristo Yesu, ne mu mwoyo gwe natonda munda mu mmwe, kubanga omwoyo ogwo gwa maanyi, era gwe kwagala; kubanga bwe buzaale bwa Kitange yennyini, kubanga Yahweh ye kwagala. Laba, tewali muntu alina kwagala kusinga kuno, omuntu okuwaayo obulamu bwe olw'emikwano gye. Era nze nnawaayo obulamu bwange olw'emikwano gyange, era mu kukola ekyo nabafuula baganda bange era nabafuula baana ba Katonda: Laba, okwagala Kitange kwe yatuwa, okuyitibwa abaana ba Katonda: ensi kyeva etatumanya, kubanga teyamumanya ye.

Muwulire Ebigambo byange bye nnawa Yokaana mu bbaluwa ye ey'olubereberye, era mubiwulire bulungi. Yahweh abawadde ekisa ekisinga obukulu okubataasa ku muwaabi ne ku abo ababasalira omusango, era okuwaaba kwabwe yakufuula okutaliiko nsonga ku mmwe, kubanga mwagulibwa n'omusaayi gwange ogutaliiko kibi, era mwesonyiyibwa emisango gyonna mu mulimu gwange ogutangirira. Temusomangako kye nnagamba Abaruumi emyaka gya Yubiri mingi egiyise? Ani alivaayo okuwaabira abalonde ba Katonda? Ye Katonda ababala obutuukirivu: Ani abasalira omusango? Ye Kristo eyafa, weewaawo era eyazuukira, ali ku mukono ogwa ddyo gwa Katonda, era atuwolereza. Temuwulidde bujulizi bwa Abebbulaniya, eddoboozi lyabwe gye lijulira obwakabona bwange obutakyuka? Kyava ayinza okubalokola okutuukirira ddala, abo abajja eri Katonda ku bubwe, kubanga mulamu ennaku zonna okubawolereza.

Muwulire ebigambo bino, era mubiweese okuyingira ddala mu buziba bwammwe, era muwulire eddoboozi lye nnawa ekkanisa y'e Rooma: Kubanga yamufuula ekibi ku lwaffe, ye ataalina kibi, ffe tulyoke tufuuke obutuukirivu bwa Katonda mu ye. Ebigambo bino bibasinga obukulu? Mujja kwekkiriza okutegeera, oba mujja kuziba amatu gammwe mu nnono zammwe ezitagasa n'obujulizi obw'obulimba bwe mukutteko, obuweereddwa bannabbi ab'obulimba ab'okubakyamya? Kintu kizibu nnyo okukkiriza nti mwafuulibwa obutuukirivu bwa Katonda mu nze, Kristo Yesu?

Nneetaaga musirike ddala mu birowoozo byammwe ne mu mitima gyammwe era muleke eddoboozi lyange likakkanye omwoyo gwammwe era muwulire ebigambo byange. Mugonze emitima gyammwe era mumenyeemenye amalala gammwe; kubanga nnina kye njagala okubagamba ekijja okubasumulula mu busibe bwe munaaba mwakkirizza ebigambo bye nnaatera okubawa nate. Kubanga eddoboozi ly'Abaruumi lyogera nammwe nga musoma, kale musome mutegeere; era mu kutegeera, mukkirize amazima g'embeera yammwe mu nze, Kristo Yesu. N'ebigambo byange bye bino: Kale kaakano tewali kusalirwa musango kwonna ku abo abali mu Kristo Yesu, abatambula si nga omubiri bwe gugamba, naye nga omwoyo bwe gugamba. Kubanga etteeka ery'omwoyo gw'obulamu, mu Kristo Yesu, linsumuludde okuva mu tteeka ery'ekibi n'ery'okufa. Amatama gammwe galeme kulaba bukubi ku bigambo, naye musome buli kimu mpola n'okukkiriza, okukkiriza mu nze, kubanga nze njogedde ebigambo bino ku mmwe, baana bange; era nze, Kristo Yesu, mbasumuludde okuva mu kusibibwa kwonna mu nze. Temutegeera nti mwafuulibwa obutuukirivu bwa Katonda? Muntegeere era muwulire eddoboozi lyange era muteeke omwoyo ku bigambo byange: Ani abasalira omusango? Ye Kristo eyafa, weewaawo era eyazuukira, ali ku mukono ogwa ddyo gwa Katonda, era atuwolereza.

Baana bange, saabaita mu butuukirivu n'obutukuvu obw'amazima okubasalira musango, era muteekwa okutegeera amazima: Temulina buyinza kweesalira musango, era abangi ku mmwe mukikola, n'obulamu bwammwe bubonyaabonyezebwa olw'okwekyawa kuno omulabe kw'abateekako. Munkkirize menye enjegere zino ez'obulimba ez'omulabe, era muwulire ebigambo byange era mubikkirize, kubanga ga mazima, era gawandiikiddwa ku lwammwe mulyoke mubeere ab'eddembe ddala mu nze. Kubanga mbasumuludde okuva mu kusibibwa kwonna, n'omulabe era omuwaabi w'abooluganda asuuliddwa wansi: Kaakano obulokozi butuuse, n'amaanyi, n'obwakabaka bwa Katonda waffe, n'obuyinza bwa Kristo we: kubanga omuwaabi w'abooluganda asuuliddwa wansi, eyabawaabiranga mu maaso ga Katonda waffe emisana n'ekiro.

Mutegeere, baana bange, nti muli batuukirivu, era okweesalira omusango kwe kunyooma ebigambo byange Kitange by'abawadde; noolwekyo temuba batakkiriza, naye mubeere n'obwesige obungi mu bigambo byange, kubanga ga mazima, era tewali bujulizi bwa mazima businga obwo obuweereddwa. Mutegeere enjawulo eri wakati w'okweesalira omusango n'omwoyo gwange ogukola mu mmwe okubanenya olw'ekibi; kubanga kiki kye nnagamba Yokaana mu bbaluwa ye ey'olubereberye: Kubanga omutima gwaffe bwe gutusalira omusango, Katonda asinga omutima gwaffe obukulu, era amanyi byonna. Abaagalwa, omutima gwaffe bwe gutatusalira musango, tuba n'obuvumu eri Katonda. Temutegeera nti omwoyo gwange gwe natonda munda mu buli omu ku mmwe, Omugole wange, ddala gubanenya olw'ebibi byammwe ne bwe muba nga mwamala okuzaalibwa obuggya mu nze, Kristo Yesu, Mukama wammwe?

Temusomangako bujulizi bwange obwawandiikibwa mu njiri ya Yokaana ku bikwata ku Mubeezi? Era bw'alijja, alinenya ensi olw'ekibi, n'olw'obutuukirivu, n'olw'omusango. Olw'ekibi, kubanga tebanzikiriza. Olw'obutuukirivu, kubanga ŋŋenda eri Kitange, so temulindaba nate. Olw'omusango, kubanga omulangira w'ensi eno asalidwa omusango. Baana bange, omubeezi yabannenya olw'ekibi, ne mukyukira gye ndi, ne mu kuzaalibwa obuggya ne mu maanyi g'omwoyo gwe natonda munda mu mmwe, ye mwoyo ogw'obuzaaliranwa, ye Kristo mu mmwe essuubi ly'ekitiibwa, nabafuula obutuukirivu bwa Katonda mu nze. Noolwekyo, temukyanenyesebwa lwa kibi, naye munenyesebwa lwa butuukirivu bwe mwonoona, ne bwe mukyukira amakubo gammwe amakadde, kubanga omusota gwagala nnyo okukyamya abantu bange n'olyoka guleete okuwaaba mu maaso ga Kitange ne mu maaso gange olw'ebikolwa byagwo ebitasaana, era nze ndi muwolereza wammwe.

Kubanga nate, omujulizi Yokaana mu bbaluwa ye ey'olubereberye ayogera ku nsonga eno: Baana bange abato, bino mbibawandiikira, muleme okwonoona. Era omuntu bw'ayonoona, tulina omuwolereza eri Kitange, Yesu Kristo omutuukirivu: Era ye kiweebwayo olw'ebibi byaffe: so si byaffe byokka, naye n'olw'ebibi by'ensi yonna. Baana bange, nze ndi muwolereza wammwe, era muli batuukirivu mu nze, era muli batuukirivu emirembe gyonna, kubanga obutuukirivu buzaaliddwa mu buzaale bwammwe mwennyini. Noolwekyo mubeere n'omutima omugonvu gye ndi, nange ndibalaga ebibi byammwe n'ensobi zammwe, n'empisa zammwe embi n'amakubo gammwe amabi ag'edda ge mukemebwa okuddamu ebiseera ebimu. Kubanga omutima gwammwe bwe gubanenya, mukyukire gye ndi mwatule ebibi byammwe, kubanga ekyawandiikibwa kigamba kitya: Naye bwe tutambulira mu musana, nga ye bw'ali mu musana, tuba n'omukwano wakati waffe, n'omusaayi gwa Yesu Kristo Omwana we gutunaazaako ebibi byonna. Bwe twogera nti tetulina kibi, tweerimba, n'amazima tegali mu ffe. Bwe twatula ebibi byaffe, ye mwesigwa, era mutuukirivu okutusonyiwa ebibi byaffe, n'okutunaazaako obutali butuukirivu bwonna.

Baana bange, mbaze obutuukirivu mu nze, era Yahweh abasonyiye emisango gyonna mu nze; lwaki temwekkiriza kukkiriza bigambo byange eby'ekisa n'okwagala? Kubanga okwagala kwa Katonda kulabikira mu byo era n'okwagala kwange eri Omugole wange era n'ensi yonna, kubanga yenna ayagala ajje, kyokka jja ng'oluggi terunnaggulwawo kubanga obudde buweddeyo. Mudde gyendi buli lunaku mwenaaze mu musaayi gwange ogutangirira, kubanga ssaddaaka yange etuukiridde era ya mulundi gumu era ya lubeerera: Kubanga Abebbulaniya boogera lwatu ku nze nti: Ye, okumasamasa kw'ekitiibwa kye, n'ekifaananyi ekyoleka obuntu bwe, era ng'asitula ebintu byonna n'ekigambo kye eky'amaanyi, bwe yamala okutunaazaako ebibi byaffe ye yennyini, n'atuula ku mukono ogwa ddyo ogw'Obukulu mu bifo ebya waggulu ennyo. Baana bange, mutegeere nti omubeezi era anenyezza ensi olw'omusango, kubanga omulangira w'ensi eno asalidwa omusango, era ekibonerezo kye kimugwanira. Naye okutuusa ku ddakiika eno anoonya okubawalula mu kusibibwa kwe okumugwanira ennyo, noolwekyo ajja okubasalira omusango; kale temuwuliriza bigambo bye era mugaane obulimba bwe.

Baana bange abato, mutambulire mu kwagala kwa Katonda mu nze, Kristo Yesu mukama wammwe. Mwagale Kitange era munyagale nga mukwata amateeka gange, kubanga Eky'olubereberye kya Yokaana kigamba kitya? Ayogera nti, Mmumanyi, so takwata mateeka ge, ye mulimba, n'amazima tegali mu ye. Naye buli akwata ekigambo kye, mu ye okwagala kwa Katonda kutuukirizibwa ddala: ku kino kwe tutegeerera nti tuli mu ye. Ayogera nti abeera mu ye, naye agwanidde okutambulanga bwe yatyo, nga ye bwe yatambulanga. Mbalagira, Omugole wange, okweyongera okutambulanga nga nze bwe nnatambulanga n'okukwata amateeka gange, kubanga nnateranga okukola byonna Kitange by'ayagala, era nammwe bwe mutyo bwe musaana okufubanga okukola byonna bye njagala, kubanga ebyange bye bya Yahweh. Mu kukwata amateeka gange kijja kubetaagisa okukyukira abooluganda, kubanga Eky'olubereberye kya Yokaana kigamba kitya? Ku kino kwe tutegeerera nti twagala abaana ba Katonda, bwe twagala Katonda ne tukwata amateeka ge. Kubanga kuno kwe kwagala kwa Katonda, ffe okukwata amateeka ge, n'amateeka ge si mazito. Muwulire eddoboozi lyange era mugoberere ebigambo byange okutuuka ku nkomerero y'obuwulize, era mweyongere okwagala abooluganda bammwe n'okuweereza mu kwagala, si lwa magoba matono; kubanga empeera yammwe eri mu mikono gyange, so si mu mikono gy'abo be muweereza.

Waleme okubaawo kintu na kimu ekikolebwa olw'empaka oba olw'okwejalabya; naye mu kwetoowaza okw'omwoyo buli muntu alowoozenga munne okuba amusinga obulungi.

Nze musomesa wammwe era omusumba wammwe.

Ndi wa Kitange era ndi wa buzaale bumu era omwoyo gwe gumu.

Nze butuukirivu bwa Katonda, era nabafuula obutuukirivu bwa Katonda.

Nze oyo abalibwa obutuukirivu mu makubo ge.

Nze sirina kibi, era mbaze obutuukirivu mu nze, Kristo Yesu.

Nze oyo atukuzibwa bulijjo era emirembe gyonna, kubanga Obwakabaka bwa Katonda bwange, era nabatukuza n'omwoyo gw'obuzaaliranwa.

Nze oyo eyawa ettondo lye ery'omusaayi erisembayo ku musaalaba ndyoke mbanaazeeko ebibi byonna, kubanga tewali kibi mu mwoyo gw'obuzaaliranwa gwe nabawa.

Nze muwolereza wammwe.

Nze ey'owolereza wammwe.

Mubeere beetegefu, Omugole wange, kubanga ebiseera eby'okuzzaawo biriko kumpi, n'okulabika kwange kuli kumpi okusinga bwe mulowooza.
