Omujulirwa Ow'okubiri: Isirayiri
Ekikaawa Ekirimu Obuwoomu
Agusito 04, 2024

Enjiri Eteriggwaawo!

Muwulire Ebigambo bya Yeshua HaMashiach era mutege amatu gammwe mu buwulize. Kale nno ebiseera bikyuka, n'olunaku lutuuse akatyabaga lwe kaligwa ku nsi kibwatukira. Kibwatukira lwe ndikung’aanyiza abeesigwa bange gyendi mu bire, era kibwatukira okuzikirira lwe kuligwa ku nsi yonna, era kibwatukira ensi gye mumanyi lwe erikyusibwa emirembe gyonna, n'etadda nate mu mbeera eno eyonooneddwa era ey'ebibi. Newankubadde enkomerero y'obufuzi bwa Setaani obw'ebibi eriraajanira, olunaku olwo terulijja kutuuka mu bujjuvu okutuusa amaanyi gonna, n'obuyinza bw'Oggwanga bwe biriggibwawo ku kujja kwange okw'okubiri lwe ndijja okufuga ensi mu butuukirivu n'abalwanyi ab'omu ggulu, ye, amagye amakulu ag'omu ggulu nga mu go mulimu ebibala ebisooka mu bafu era ndibakuŋŋaanyiza gyendi.

Kubanga abo abaliba nange bayitibwa, era balonde, era beesigwa era ba mazima. Era beeraze nga beesigwa mu bintu bitono, kale ndibafuula bafuzi ku bingi era bayingira mu ssanyu lya Mukama waabwe. Baliba ggye eritaliiko kuzikirira era lya lubeerera eritasobola kuwangulwa newankubadde okuziyizibwa mu ngeri yonna, era balirinnyirira ddala ebifaananyi byonna eby'Obwakabaka bw'abalabe mu kuzikirizibwa okw'olubeerera n'okugondera ebintu byonna ebibi. Kubanga okuwalana eggwanga kwange, bw'atyo Mukama bw'agamba, nange ndisasula. Era nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, ndi Mukama wa bakama era Kabaka wa bakabaka, era mu butuukirivu njija kusala musango na kulwana.

Kale muwulire ebigambo byange era mutegeere essuubi ly'ebitonde byonna, kubanga n'ebitonde birindirira n'okwesunga okubikkulibwa kw'abaana ba Katonda! Kubanga ebitonde byateekebwa wansi w'obwereere, si lwa kwagala, wabula olw'oyo eyabigonza mu ssuubi: Kubanga ekitonde kyennyini kirinunulibwa okuva mu busibe bw'okuvunda, okuyingira mu ddembe ery'ekitiibwa ery'abaana ba Katonda. Kale nze, Yesu Kristo, ndinunula ebitonde mu butuukirivu era ndiwonya ensi era mu butuukirivu mwe ndifugira. Era ndiggyawo obuyinza bwonna n'obufuzi bw'omulabe, n'obwakabaka bwa Setaani tebulidda kuddawo nate.

Nga bwe kyawandiikibwa, bwe kityo bwe kiriba, era bwe ntyo bwe nnawandiisa Yokaana Omubikkulirwa: Ne ndaba eggulu nga libikkuse, era laba embalaasi enjeru; n'oyo eyali agituddeko ayitibwa Mwesigwa era ow'Amazima, era mu butuukirivu asala omusango era alwana. Amaaso ge gaali ng'olulimi lw'omuliro, ne ku mutwe gwe kwaliko engule nnyingi; era yalina erinnya eriwandiikiddwa, eritannategeerwa muntu yenna wabula ye yekka. Era yali ayambadde olugoye olunnyikiddwa mu musaayi: n'erinnya lye liyitibwa, Kigambo kya Katonda. N'amagye agaali mu ggulu ne gamugoberera ku mbalaasi enjeru, nga bambadde bafuta enjeru ennungi. Ne mu kamwa ke ne muva ekitala eky'obwogi, kirwanise amawanga: era alibafuga n'omuggo ogw'ekyuma: era alinnyirira essogolero ly'effervensi n'obusungu bwa Katonda Omuyinza w'ebintu byonna. Era ku lugoye lwe ne ku kisambi kye kuliko erinnya eriwandiikiddwa, KABAKA WA BAKABAKA, ERA MUKAMA WA BAKAMA. Kale lye ssuubi ery'okujja kwange okw'okubiri, era olunaku olwo lusemberera buli sekonda eyitawo.

Laba! Ku lunaku olwo ndireeta abalwanyi bange kubanga bwe butyo obusika bwabwe, ye, eggye eritaliiko ngeri ndala, kubanga eggye nga lyange teryaliwo, eggye eritasobola kuwangulwa erikolebwa abanunule, ye, abo abaazaalibwa nga bafu mu kusobya n'ebibi naye kati abaliba n'obulamu obutaggwaawo era abatasobola kuttibwa newankubadde okuwangulwa. Kubanga mpeereddwa amaanyi ga Yakuwa olw'ekigendererwa ekyo, era ndiba mpeereddwa amaanyi era mbaweereddwa empeera olw'obwesigwa bwabwe n'okunjagala n'erinnya lyange, ye, Yesu Kristo! Kubanga buli anjagala era aweereza Yeshuah HaMashiach ajagala era aweereza ne Yakuwa, kubanga tewali asinga Katonda omukulu ataggwaawo ow'e Isirayiri! Ku lunaku olwo olusemberera muliraba eggulu nga libikkuse, era mulindaba, Yesu Kristo, nga nkuulembera okuva mu ggulu eggye eryo eddene era ery'amaanyi ery'omu ggulu, era liriba ggye eritaliiko ngeri ndala, emirembe gyonna! Kubanga Omugole wange aliba nange.

Era ndizikiriza ddala ensolo n'omunnabbi ow'obulimba n'amagye gaabwe gonna n'emizizo gyabwe gyonna gye baakola mu muzizo era bwe batyo bwe balizikirizibwa n'okumasamasa kw'okujja kwange n'ekitala ekyo eky'obwogi ekiwagamira ebbali zombi ekiva mu kamwa kange, so tebaliyimirira naye balizikirizibwa ddala. N'ensolo n'omunnabbi ow'obulimba balisuulibwa nga balamu mu nnyanja eyaka omuliro n'ekibiriiti so tebalisumululwa ku kubonerezebwa kwabwe, kubanga beesaanyizza. Naye okusooka ensi erina okulongoozebwa mu kibonyoobonyo kya Yakobo era bangi balijja gyendi, ye, ebibiina bingi era buli mmeeme eneekoowoola erinnya lya Mukama okutuusa ku nkomerero erirokolebwa okutuukira ddala, era Isirayiri erina okukung'aanyizibwa kubanga nkyali n'okulokola ensigalira okuva mu Yakobo abakyali n'okujja eri Masiya waabwe ow'amazima era ataggwaawo, kubanga nze Yeshua HaMashiach era njija ng'ebisuubizo eri Bakadde bwe byalangirirwa edda Yakuwa yennyini, era nze kutuukirizibwa kwabyo n'ekiseera kyange kituuse.

Kale abo abagaanyi erinnya lyange erirokola, ye, Yesu Omunazaaleeti, mbategeeza okuwulira ebigambo byange n'okutegeera ebiseera mwe muli n'okuddukira ku ddoboozi lyange n'okukoowoola erinnya lyange kaakano n'okunjatula nga Mukama wammwe, kubanga temulina kiseera kya kusasaanya. Nga bwe waaliwo abo abaasooka okuzaalibwa mu kuzaalibwa okupya ku kuzaalibwa kw'ekkanisa yange ku lunaku lwa Pentekooti nga bwe kyawandiikibwa mu byawandiikibwa, bwe kityo bwe walibawo ab'enkomerero abalijja mu kuzaalibwa okupya mu mirembe gino egy'ekisa, ye, bonna abajja mu bulokozi ng'okuyiwa kwange okw'omwoyo omutukuvu mu mirembe gyange egy'ekisa n'okusaasira bwe kugenda ku nkomerero.

Kale obwavu bw'ebintu byonna ebya Katonda kati buligwa ku nsi n'ekibi kati tekiriba na nkomerero nga nziggyawo omuzibi wange bwe nkuŋŋaanya Ekkanisa yange. Omuntu ow'obutonde agaanyi eddoboozi lyange, omuntu ow'obutonde agaanyi ekisa kyange, omuntu ow'obutonde agaanyi okusaasira kwange, omuntu ow'obutonde agaanyi okuyitibwa kwange okw'obutuukirivu, n'omuntu ow'obutonde anjeemye nze Yesu Omunazaaleeti era kyava agaana Yakuwa Katonda omukulu ataggwaawo eyantuma, era kaakano nja kuwa ensi ekizikiza kye beegomba ennyo.

Naye mutegeere nti bangi balinkolera emirimu egy'amaanyi, n'abajulirwa bange ababiri, ye, ebiterekezo byange ebibiri biriragulwa, era ndituma 144,000 bange okulokola bangi, kale temutya! Obulokozi bulituukirizibwa, ne bwe kiba nga kuyita mu kuzikirizibwa kw'omubiri n'ebibiina binene birijja mu bulokozi mu kiseera kino. Kubanga mu mazima, Yakuwa taze era talirekawo bujulizi!

Ebiseera by'Abamawanga bizzadde ebibala bingi mu abo abandyandisigadde nga tebalina ssuubi okuggyako nga bayingiziddwa mu bisuubizo ebyakolebwa eri Bakadde, ye, abo ababeerawo olw'okukkiriza kw'Omwana wa Katonda eyabaagala n'e yeewaayo olw'obulokozi bwabwe. Kubanga nga bwe kyawandiikibwa: Omutuukirivu aliba mulamu lwa kukkiriza! Era bwe kityo olw'okukkiriza mu nze, Kristo Yesu, bafuuse baana ba kukkiriza, ye, baana ba kukkiriza kwa Ibulayimu. Kubanga Ibulayimu yakkiriza Katonda nga kabonero k'okukomolebwa tekannaba kuweebwa, ne kibalerwa okuba obutuukirivu, nga ne Musa bw'ategeeza! Kale Ibulayimu ye kitaawe w'abo bonna abakkiriza n'abo abafuuliddwa abatuukirivu olw'okukkiriza mu Yesu Kristo Omutuukirivu, kubanga nze mutuukirivu era ntuukiriza abo bonna abakkiriza erinnya lyange ery'amaanyi!

Naye bangi nnyo bakozesezza ekisa kyange n'okusaasira kwange ng'olukusa lw'okwonoona, nga basanyuka era nga beewaanirira mu makubo gaabwe ag'ebibi n'okuzikiriza, nga n'okuyigiriza abaana baabwe okubeera mu makubo gaabwe ag'ebibi era n'emirembe mibi mu makubo gaabwe n'amakubo gaabwe ga muzizo era n'abantu ababi n'abasendasenda beeyongedde okuba ababi ennyo. Temulimbibwanga, omuntu bw'asiga mu mubiri, omuntu oyo alikungula okuvunda mu mubiri. Era bonna abagaana eky'okulaba kyange eky'obulamu obutaggwaawo ne balondawo okuvunda kwa Adamu balikungula ebibala by'okuvunda okwo mu kusala omusango okutaggwaawo, kubanga omusango gutuuse n'ebiseera by'okubikkulibwa bituuse.

Mazima ddala, mazima ddala, ebiseera by'omusango omunene ebyalagurwa edda Enoka omuwandiisi (kubanga obujulizi bwe bwaweebwa eri omulembe guno), bituuse ku nsi nga bwe kyawandiikibwa era bonna baliyanjula. Abo abakkiriza eky'okulaba kyange eky'obulokozi era abasangibwa nga beesigwa mu Kristo ku kulabika kwange balikyusibwa emirembe gyonna, oba nga baafa mu Kristo oba nga balamu era basigala mu Kristo, abo abali mu Kristo ku kulabika kwange balitwalibwa mu ggulu ly'amagulu. Okuba mu Kristo kwe kutambulira mu mwoyo gwange ogw'amaanyi, ye, etteeka ly'omwoyo gw'obulamu! Era abo abaabeerawo mu butuukirivu nga Ekkanisa yange tennazaalibwa n'eri abatuukirivu ab'ekibonyoobonyo lye zzuukira ly'abatuukirivu, abatuukirivu lwe balizuukira mu bupya bw'obulamu mu mpeera ennene, etaggwaawo era ey'olubeerera. Ku nkomerero y'obufuzi bwange obw'emyaka 1000 kulibawo omusango gw'entebe ey'obwakabaka enjeru era bonna abaabeerawo ne basanyukira mu butali butuukirivu n'ebibi balisasulwa okusinziira ku bikolwa byabwe era bangi baligwa mu kufa okw'okubiri kubanga bwe batyo bwe balondeddewo.

Kale ebiseera by'okubikkulibwa bituuse, n'enjiri eteriggwaawo ebikkuddwa mu kiseera kyayo ye, akatabo akatono mu mukono gwa malayika wange ow'amaanyi kati kabikkuddwa n'okwogera kw'okubwatuka omusanvu kulangiriddwa! Kubanga omulimu guno mulimu gwa lubeerera, era lye ggwanika ly'endagaano n'ebisuubizo eby'edda okuyingira mu ndagaano n'ebisuubizo eby'obupya, ye, Endagaano Yange Empya gye nnakakasa mu musaayi gwange. Kubanga nze Yeshua HaMashiach, ye, Yesu Kristo Omutuukirivu ali Kabaka wa bakabaka era Mukama wa bakama, nze eyalondebwa okutuukiriza ebisuubizo byonna n'endagaano zonna.

Kubanga Yokaana Omubikkulirwa yawandiika kye yalaba, era n'awandiika nti: Ne ndaba Malayika omulala ow'amaanyi ng'akka okuva mu ggulu, ng'ayambadde ekire, n'omusoke gwali ku mutwe gwe, n'amaaso ge gaali ng'enjuba, n'ebigere bye ng'empagi z'omuliro. Era yalina mu mukono gwe akatabo akatono akagule: n'ateeka ekigere kye eky'obwa ddyo ku nnyanja, n'ekya kkono ku nsi. Ate Yokaana Omubikkulirwa n'alangirira nti: Ne ndaba Malayika omulala ng'abuuka mu bbanga, ng'alina enjiri eteriggwaawo, okugibuulira abo abatuula ku nsi, ne buli ggwanga, n'ekika, n'olulimi, n'abantu, ng'ayogera n'eddoboozi ddene nti, mutye Katonda, era mumugulumize; kubanga ekiseera ky'omusango gwe kituuse: era mumusinze eyakola eggulu, n'ensi, n'ennyanja, n'ensulo z'amazzi.

Kale bwe kyawandiikibwa era bwe kityo bwe kikolebwa. Enjiri eteriggwaawo ezze okubikkulibwa mu kiseera kyayo n'akatabo akatono tekakyali kya maama. Malayika ow'amaanyi ayambadde ekire n'omusoke gwali ku mutwe gwe n'amaaso ge gaali ng'enjuba n'ebigere bye ng'empagi z'omuliro. Omusoke ge kabonero akaaweebwa mu ndagaano ya Nuuwa Yakuwa gye yakola ne Nuuwa oluvannyuma lw'amataba nti taliddayo kusaanyaawo nsi yonna. Ekire n'empagi z'omuliro bikiikirira endagaano ya Katonda ne Musa, nga zizimbiddwa ku ndagaano ezaasooka. Kubanga Musa bwe yamala okwogera buli kiragiro eri abantu bonna okusinziira ku mateeka, n'addira omusaayi gw'ennyana n'embuzi, n'amazzi, n'ebyoya eby'effulungu, n'ezobu, n'amansira ekitabo n'abantu bonna era bwe yatyo Endagaano Enkadde n'ekakasibwa!

Era nga bwe kyawandiikibwa mu Abaebbulaniya: Kubanga oba ng'omusaayi gw'ente ennume n'ogw'embuzi ennume, n'evvu ly'ente enduusi erimansibwa ku abo abatalongoofu, litukuza okutuusa ku kulongoosa omubiri: Omusayi gwa Kristo, eyawaayo yekka awatali bbala eri Katonda okuyita mu Mwoyo ataggwaawo, teguusinge nnyo kulongoosa omwoyo gwammwe okuva mu bikolwa ebifu okuba nga muweereza Katonda omulamu? Era olw'ensonga eno ye mutabaganya w'endagaano empya, okuyita mu kufa, olw'okununula okusobya okwali wansi w'endagaano esooka, abo abayitibwa balyoke bafune ekisuubizo ky'obusika obutaggwaawo.

Kale nga bwe kyawandiikibwa bwe kityo bwe kituukiriziddwa abeesigwa bange, era bwe ntyo Endagaano Empya. Era nga bwe kiri nti El Shaddai ye yalagira omusana okwaka okuva mu kizikiza ku lubereberye, mu ngeri y'emu Katonda Omuyinza w'ebintu byonna ayase mu mitima gyaffe okuwa omusana ogw'okumanya ekitiibwa kya Katonda, mu maaso ga Yesu Kristo. Bwe kityo, mu ndagaano yange empya erina amaanyi amangi, okuva lwe nnagikakasa n'omusaayi gwange ogutalina kibi, zituukiriziddwa endagaano zonna ez'ebiseera byonna, kubanga nnagamba ku Kalvari mu kussa kwange okusembayo nti: Kiwedde. Era bwe ntyo endagaano empya n'ekakasibwa omusaayi gwange okuyingira mu Ndagaano Empya!

Kale mu nze, Kristo Yesu, endagaano ez'edda zituukiriziddwa n'ekitiibwa eky'amaaso agagule, ye, mu maaso g'omulokozi wammwe ataggwaawo era Masiya, ye, nze Yeshuah HaMashiach. Era malayika ono Yokaana Omubikkulirwa gw'annyonnyola yalina ekigere kimu ku nnyanja n'ekimu ku nsi kubanga obulokozi bwange bugule eri abo abava mu nnyanja y'amawanga, (Abamawanga) n'abo abava mu nsi ey'ekisuubizo ye, bonna abamfuula Mukama waabwe ataggwaawo, ye, bangi nga Mukama Katonda waffe bw'aliyita! Kale enjiri yange eteriggwaawo erizuula amaaso, amatu, n'emitima gya bonna be tumiddwa era bangi balijja mu bulokozi kubanga buli kigambo kya Katonda kiramu era kya lubeerera so tekuli kigambo kya Yakuwa kirirema newankubadde okuyitawo okutuusa byonna lwe birituukiririzibwa. Kubanga ne Yakuwa ebisuubizo byonna ye ne amiina era byonna birituukiririzibwa era nze, Kristo Yesu, nneewaayo mu kubonerezebwa okw'entiisa okukakasa endagaano yange empya mu musaayi gwange ogutalina kibi, omulundi gumu n'emirembe gyonna n'endagaano empya eribeerawo okutuuka mu butaggwaawo obutaggwaawo.

Kale muwulire ebigambo byange era mutegeere okwogera kwange, ye, eddoboozi lya Mukama wammwe era Masiya. Ebiseera bituuse abaana bange n'ebigambo byange bijjudde ensi n'abannabbi bange n'abaweereza bange babunye enkomerero z'ensi, ne mu nnimi zonna, n'ennimi, n'amawanga enjiri yange ey'obulokozi ewuliddwa, ye, okutuuka ku nkomerero z'ensi. Kale bwe kiri, era bwe ntyo bwe ndangirira: Amawulire gange amalungi gazimbiddwa bulijjo ku musingi gw'amateeka n'abannabbi era nze jjinja essuuti ery'oku nsonda kwe yeekaalu yonna eya Katonda omulamu lw'ewummulira! Baana bange, mmwe muli yeekaalu ya Katonda omulamu!

Kale mbawa okutegeera nti waliwo ebitundu bingi mu njiri ya Kristo! Muli basirusiru nnyo okukkiriza nti waliwo enjiri nnya zokka? Era waliwo n'ebintu birala bingi bye nnakola, ebyo, singa biwandiikibwa buli kimu, ndowooza nti n'ensi yennyini teyandisaanye ebitabo ebyandiwandiikiddwa. Kale bwe kiri nti waliwo amawulire amalungi ag'okujja kwange okusooka, waliwo amawulire amalungi ag'ekyama kya Katonda kye nnabikkulira Pawulo Omutume, waliwo enjiri eteriggwaawo etutuusa ku ggwanika ly'ebintu byonna. Eri Yakuwa tewali nkomerero ya mawulire malungi!

Kale nkubuuza ekibuuzo? Ggwe muwi wa magezi wa Yakuwa? Abafumbo bo ab'obulimba n'abaweereza ab'ekivuvu basobola okuwa Yakuwa amagezi? Ggwe ani, ai omuntu, okusalawo ddi era ngeri ki Yakuwa mw'ayogereramu n'ebiri mu bigambo bye n'obulamu bwabyo, oba ani gw'alonda okutumira ebigambo bye? Kale bwe kiri. Ebigambo bya Katonda birigenda mu maaso n'okukwata era obujulizi bwonna buliggwaawo n'ebitabo biriwandiikibwa mu butaggwaawo kubanga Ekigambo kya Katonda kiramu era kya lubeerera!

Kale nkubuuza, Endagaano Empya eya Kristo Yesu yasangula Endagaano Enkadde n'endagaano enkadde? Katonda agireme! Buli Kigambo Katonda kirongoofu era byonna birituukiririzibwa ne bwe kiba omulundi gumu, byonna, n'emirembe n'emirembe! Kale ye ngeri ya Katonda atakyuka! Okwetooloola entebe ye ey'obwakabaka kuliko emirundi lukumi emirundi emirongo kkumi naye teyeetaaga muwi wa magezi. Yakuwa, Katonda omukulu ataggwaawo akola ebintu byonna ng'agezesa okwagala kwe era ye Katonda ataggwaawo! Naye abikkudde enteekateeka ze, ye, okwagala kwe okw'olubeerera n'okutaggwaawo mu bitundutundu. Lwaki Yakuwa yakola ekintu ng'ekyo oyinza okubuuza? Tomaze kusoma Pawulo Omutume kye yawandiika bwe nnamuwandiisa? Naye twogera amagezi ga Katonda mu kyama, ye, amagezi amakise, Katonda ge yali asazeewo ensi nga tennabaawo, olw'ekitiibwa kyaffe. Ekyo tewali n'omu ku bafuzi b'ensi eno eyamanya: kubanga singa baakimanya, tebandikomereredde Mukama w'ekitiibwa.

Kale Katonda ataggwaawo, ye, Yakuwa ateetaaga muwi wa magezi era mu magezi ge ag'olubeerera abikkula amakubo ge n'Ebigambo bye mu ngeri gy'alonda mu kutuukirizibwa kw'enteekateeka ze ez'olubeerera ez'okununula abantu. Mutegeere abanjagalwa bange, ye, Omugole wange, nti mubadde mu mutima gwa Yakuwa okuva ebitonde nga tebinnabaawo era mukisiddwa mu bukwakkulizo mu mutima gwa Kitange era kibakisiddwa kye mulifuuka ku kulabika kwange, kubanga muli bafu n'obulamu bwammwe bukisiddwa mu nze Kristo Yesu. Era bwe ndirabika, bwe mutyo bwe mulirabika nange mu kitiibwa! Baana bange abato muli kumpi okutondebwawo mu bujjuvu mu nze era mulisamaalirira ekyo Yakuwa ky'ategese abo abamwagala. Kubanga nga bwe kiri tewali muntu ku nsi ategedde mu bujjuvu ekyo abaana ba Katonda kye balifuuka ku kununulibwa kwabwe okujjuvu, era muli kumpi okukizuula kubanga olunaku lutuuse n'okulabika kwange kuli kumpi.

Mpa amagezi omu era mpa amagezi bonna, ye, Kristo Yesu Mukama wammwe ataggwaawo! Mukkirize Ebigambo byange era mufune amagezi n'okutegeera, kubanga obuntu bwammwe obw'amazima kya maama kya lubeerera ekikisiddwa mu Katonda yennyini ekiri kumpi okubikkulibwa mu bujjuvu. Mulisanga mangu nnyo nga muyimiridde mu kisenge ky'entebe ey'obwakabaka eya Yakuwa, ye, Kitaffe, era yeegomba n'okwesunga okubaawo kwammwe kaakano, okuva mu butaggwaawo obwayita. N'ebisenge ebinene mu ggulu bitegekeddwa okutuuka kwammwe n'emmeeza ziteekeddwa n'embaga y'Omugole ku Mugole we eri kumpi n'omugole wange yeeteeseteese era ndibakkiriza gyendi era emirembe gyonna balibeera nange mu ndagaano eteriggwaawo era balikola emirimu mingi egy'amaanyi egitannayingira mu mitima gy'abantu mu kiseera kino.

Kale Yakuwa Katonda omukulu ataggwaawo era ataggwaawo ategese era akutegekedde ebintu bingi, era nze, Kristo Yesu Mukama agulumiziddwa era ayambuse nnina empeera yo mu mukono gwange n'eri abeesigwa bange. N'eggye ly'omu ggulu lyegomba okubaawo kwammwe era mu mpeera ey'omu ggulu era eteriggwaawo kaakano mwe muligenda. Kale temutya kubanga obulamu bwammwe kati buli kumpi okubikkulibwa era mu sekonda yonna kaakano ekkondeere erisembayo lirivuga, mwetegefu okulabika kwange abaana bange abato? Kubanga liri kumpi okusinga bwe mulowooza n'ekiseera ekiriwo wakati w'ekyo ne kaakano tekyetaagisa era kiweereza ebigendererwa ebitaggwaawo ebya Yakuwa Katonda omukulu ataggwaawo!

Tomanyi? oba towuliranga, nti Katonda ataggwaawo, Mukama y'atonda enkomerero z'ensi? tazirikira, so takowa so tewali kunoonya kutegeera kwe.

Nze njogera ku bintu ebigenda okujja nga bwe byali biwedde okubaawo.

Nze nkubayise okuyingira mu bulamu obutaggwaawo era nkukubye akabonero ka Katonda ataggwaawo!

Nze jjinja essuuti ery'oku nsonda ekizimbe kyonna kwe kiwummulira.

Nze Yeshua HaMashiach era nkyali n'okulokola Yakobo!

Nze mutuukirivu era ntuukiriza abo bonna abakkiriza erinnya lyange ery'amaanyi!

Laba! Ntumye Ebigambo bino omuddu wange era omuddu wange ebigambo bino gwe biweerezeddwa!

Mwekuumenga balongoofu abeesigwa bange, so temukkirizanga mikono gyammwe kugwaamu! Mugende mu maaso n'okunaaba mu musaayi gwange ogutangirira mulyoke muyimirire mu maaso gange nga mutondeddwa buto era mulongoofu ng'omuwala kubanga mbanaazizza mu musaayi gwange ogutangirira. Mutunule mu ggulu! Obubonero bwange buweereddwa mu nsi yonna kaakano era buli mu ggulu era buli ku nsi yonna n'obulumi bw'okuzaala tebukoma. Okununulibwa kwammwe okujjuvu kutuuse, muyimirire bunyimirivu era mube beesigwa okutuusa ku nkomerero, kubanga enkomerero yammwe ya lubeerera eya butaggwaawo erindirira!
