Labba! Okusalirawo Okujjuvu era Okutayonooneka ku Babulooni ey'Ekyama: Kulangiriddwa mu bujjuvu nga Omusango teginnagwa mu bujjuvu

Omusango Omujjuvu ku Babulooni ey'Ekyama: Gulangiriddwa ku Ssande nga Ogwekuminebiri 22, 2025. Ebyagiddwa byonna biva mu Kitabo Ekitono ekya Okubikkulirwa Essuula 10 ekibikkuddwa okuva nga Ogwokusatu 2, 2024, okutuuka nga Ogwokusatu 20, 2025. Ebigambo byonna byaweebwa nze okuva eri Yeshua HaMashiach ne YHVH, Nze Aliwo Omukulu ng'ebiyita mu Mwoyo gw'Obunnabbi oguli obujulirwa bwa Yesu Kristo. Sela!

Emisango egiddako giva mu Kitabo Ekitono ekya Okubikkulirwa Essuula 10, era kye Kitabo ky'Enjiri ey'Emirembe n'Emirembe ey'Okubikkulirwa Essuula 14. Omusango ku Babulooni ey'Ekyama gumaze okulabgulirwa emyaka mingi mu Kitabo ky'Okubikkulirwa. Naye, Okubwatuka Omusanvu n'Akatabo Akatono kati bibikkuddwa era akatabo kano ak'amaanyi kagule mu mukono gwange ne mu mikono gy'abo bonna abasoma okulangirira kuno okuva mu Kisibo Ekitono, abayite Yeshua HaMashiach ku lw'ekigendererwa n'ekiseera nga kino! Sela!

Okuddako kwe kusalirawo okulangirirwa okuggyiddwa mu Katabo Akatono okugenda okutuukirira amangu ddala mu ngeri etasuubirwa. Mu ngeri eno, nkola ng'omubaka omulamuzi atumiddwa okulanga emisango okuva ku ntebe ya YHVH, nga giweereddwa mu maaso ga Yesu Kristo, gwe mpeereza. Mwattu manya nti, emisango gino egimu gitandise dda ate egimu gigenda kubaawo mu bbanga ttono, naye gyonna gijja kutuukiririzibwa nga bwe kyalangirirwa era omusango ku Babulooni ey'Ekyama gujja kubeera kulabula eri amawanga gonna era omukka gw'okubonyaabonyezebwa kwayo gujja kwambuka ng'okulangirira okw'obutuukirivu okw'emisango emituukirivu egya Kristo Yesu, era ne YHVH Nze Aliwo Omukulu!

Kimanyibwe nti leero nga Ogwekuminebiri 22, 2025 ku kalenda ya Girigoli (kalenda y'emu Secret Babylon gy'ekozesa) nti Mukama wange eyalinnyisibwa Kristo Yesu, ye Yeshua HaMashiach, anningiridde okusoma emisango gino ku Babulooni ey'Ekyama, ze Zino United States of America, buli muntu asobole okwenenya nga wakyaliwo akaseera akatono, era omusango gulangirirwe nga omuliro tegunnaba kugwa. Sela!

Okulangirira kuno kujjuvu era gino si misango mipya wabula gyafulumizibwa mu bujjuvu okuva nga Ogwokusatu 2, 2024, okutuuka nga Ogwokusatu 20, 2025. N'olwekyo, njuliza buli muntu mu Kitabo Ekitono ekya Okubikkulirwa Essuula 10 ku nkungaanya eziweereddwa. Mwattu manya: Emisango gino giva naddala mu Kubwatuka okw'Okutaano era okusoma kuno kujja kutandika na "Abatali Batuukirivu tebalisikira Bwakabaka bwa Katonda", Okubwatuka Okw'Okutaano n'Okw'omukaaga, okwaweebwa Yeshua HaMashiach era ne YHVH Nze Aliwo Omukulu!

Mwattu manya nti okusoma kuno kulangirira musango mujjuvu ku Babulooni ey'Ekyama okuweereddwa Ekisibo Ekitono nga Omusango Omujjuvu tegunnaba kugwa. Nze ndi mubaka wa Mukama era omuweereza we mu nsonga eno era ndi mubaka omulamuzi atumiddwa era ayanjuliddwa YHVH, mu maaso ga Yeshua HaMashiach era be bo be mpeereza mu bujjuvu mu ofiisi yange.

Mwattu manya nti okusoma kuno era kulagirira ebigenda okubaawo mu bbanga ttono, era kino kikolebwa n'ekigendererwa nti abantu ba Mukama, abeesigwa mu Kristo, n'Omugole wa Kristo ajja okutwalibwa amangu ddala. Tuweereddwa tusobole okumanya n'okulangirira enkomerero okuva ku ntandikwa, nga tekinnabaawo. Ebintu bino bijja kubaawo ddala nga bwe byaweebwa Kristo Yesu era n'omukono gwa YHVH, Nze Aliwo Omukulu, okuva gy'ebintu byonna biva mu linnya n'obuyinza obw'amaanyi bya Kristo Yesu, era ye Yeshua HaMashiach.

Kino kitutte akaseera n'amaanyi okukitegeka, era kikoze ng'okusoma okusalira omusango okw'ekifuulannenge ku Babulooni ey'Ekyama, era n'entabiro y'ebigenda okubaawo Omugole wa Kristo ow'amazima, Abeesigwa mu Kristo, n'abakikumi kimu mu nkaaga mu enkumi nnya obutakyamyizibwa bannabbi ab'obulimba. Ke Katabo Akatono akajja okufuga ennaku ez'enkomerero okuyita mu Kubwatuka Omusanvu kati okusumuluddwa, so si bannabbi ab'obulimba abeesindika boogera eby'obusiru okuva mu mitima gyabwe. Sela!

Mwattu manya: Kino kigendereddwamu okuba nga kalagirizi k'okuyiga eri abeesigwa musobole okumanya ebigenda okujja era ffenna tuwummulire mu Kristo era twesigame ku bigambo BYE so si ebigambo bya bannabbi ab'obulimba abatali bamanyi Mukama waffe, era abatayogera ku lulwe n'akatono.

1Abatali Batuukirivu Tebalisikira Bwakabaka bwa Katonda!Ogwokuna 19, 2024
Okuva eri Yeshua HaMashiach:

Mwana wange, bw'onookkiriza ebigambo byange, n'okweka amateeka gange gy'oli; n'otega okutu kwo eri amagezi, n'oteeka omutima gwo ku kutegeera. Temukakanyaza mitima gyammwe, ng'omu kukema, ku lunaku olw'okukemebwa mu ddungu: era mumanye nti nze, Mukama wammwe Yesu Kristo, mbeera nammwe bulijjo era mbeerawo bulijjo mu bulamu bwammwe bwe munnywererako nze n'obulokozi bwange, kubanga nze mutwe gw'Omubiri, era ndi Mukama wammwe omwesigwa, mmwe Mugole wange omwagalwa. Kubanga mbategekedde ekifo n'ebiseera eby'omu maaso, era waliwo empeera nnene eri abayite bange, abeesigwa, n'abalonde.

Saabayitira kufiirwa na kubonerezebwa so saabayitira nsonyi na kwejjusa; temwasomanga nti tewali kusalirwa musango kw'abo abali mu Kristo Yesu, abatambula nga tebagoberera mubiri wabula omwoyo? N'olwekyo mukkirize ebigambo byange! Saabayitira kudda mu byonoono byammwe eby'edda, so saabayitira kugenda mu maaso n'amakubo gammwe agatali matuukirivu, wabula mbayitidde okufiira amakubo gammwe amabi amakadde ne mutwala obulamu obuggya mu nze bwe mwambula omubiri gwammwe, bwe mwambula ebibi byammwe ne mutambulira mu bulamu obuggya mu nze. Laba! Nfuula byonna bipya! Lino si lye ssuubi lyammwe?

Baana bange, mbaggye mu bubi bw'ensi eno efa, era mbawonyezza mu maanyi g'ekizikiza ne mbayingiza mu bwakabaka bwange, obwakabaka bwa Katonda, obukulemberwa nze, Kabaka wa bakabaka era Mukama wa bakama, Yahweh gw'ali Katonda, kubanga tewali mulala. Mbayitidde mu kitiibwa n'obulungi mu nze, era mbawonyezza okuva mu bwakabaka bwa Setaani, obwakabaka bw'ekizikiza, ne mbayingiza mu Bwakabaka bwange, awalimu omusana! Kale, ggwe yeebase zuukuka, ogolokoke mu bafu, era nze, Yesu Kristo, nnaakuwa omusana.

Temwasomanga mu bbaluwa ze natumira Omugole wange, ze nnabatumira, Ekkanisa yange yennyini? Temumanyi nti abatali batuukirivu tebalisikira bwakabaka bwa Katonda? Temulimbibwanga: abenzi, newakubadde abasinza ebifaananyi, newakubadde abasambazi, newakubadde abasiyazi, newakubadde abeeyisa obubi n'abantu bannaabwe, Newakubadde ababbi, newakubadde ab'omululu, newakubadde abatamiivu, newakubadde abavumi, newakubadde abanyazi, tebalisikira bwakabaka bwa Katonda. Era bwe mutyo abamu ku mmwe bwe mwali: naye munaazibwa, naye mutukuzibwa, naye muweebwa obutuukirivu mu Linnya lya Mukama waffe Yesu, ne mu Mwoyo gwa Katonda waffe.

Temutegeera biragiro byange, ggwe kkanisa y'e Lawodikiya ebuguma? Saabakuyitira kudda mu mubiri gwammwe, so saabayitira kugenda mu maaso n'amakubo gammwe agatali matuukirivu. Lowooza, era wefumiitirize; oyo alowooza nti ayimiridde, yeegendereze aleme kugwa! Mwekuume mwekka, n'enjigiriza: mugende mu maaso mu byo: kubanga bw'onookola bw'otyo, olyerokola ggwe wennyini, n'abo abakuwulira. Tegeera, mmwe abato bange ababuguma, nti temusobola kubeerawo nga mukolagana n'obulungi n'obubi, nga mulowooza nti muli batuukirivu mu maaso gange, bwe mulonda obubi obutono mu bubi obubiri mu mikolo gyammwe egy'okulonda gye saagenderera wadde okuwagira.

Mufulume mu ye, mmwe abantu bange, kubanga SIBAWEEREDDEYO kigambo kya byabufuzi, era ssaweerayo n'akatono omukulembeze w'ensi okufuula ekintu kyonna ekikulu nate. Mulimbibwa, era mutegekebwa okugoberera n'okukkiriza omwana w'okuzikirira atendera mu mmwe obulungi, newankubadde tannaba kubikkulibwa. Naye mmwe abasaba okwawula mujja kumanya ye ani, musobole okwewala obulimba bwe. Nedda, okukkiriziganya si kwa mmwe, kubanga muli baana bange era baganda bange, era saabawangulira bulokozi bwe nnawaayo obulamu bwange okuba ennunula ya bangi nga nzikiriziganya ne Setaani; kubanga okukkiriziganya kwe kwegatta ku bibinja byabwe. N'olwekyo temukola nsobi: Obanga munnyimirirawo mu butuukirivu n'obutukuvu obw'amazima, oba mwagatta ku bibinja bya Setaani mu bwakabaka bwe obufa, kale MWENENYE!

Kiki kye mufuna bwe mukkiriziganya n'obubi? Temutegeera nti muteekwa okuwangula obubi n'obulungi? Kale munywerere mu nze muleme okukyukaakyuka ng'ekyennyanja ekiri ku lukalu. Abangi ku mmwe mukyapeepeetwa buli mpewo y'enjigiriza n'obukodyo, mwe balindirira okulimba. Nze, Kristo Yesu, njogedde, era sijja kusirisibwa. Abangi ku mmwe mwefuulidde amakubo gammwe n'ekkanisa yange, era mbabuuza nti: mulimpa okutuusa ddi wakati w'ebirowoozo bibiri? Bw'aba Mukama ye Katonda, mumugoberere: naye bw'aba Baali, mumugoberere ye. Mu butuufu, temusobola kukkiriziganya ne mulondawo engeri yonna ey'obubi ne mulowooza nti munnyimiriddeko; bwe mulonda obubi, muba mulonze ensibuko y'obubi obwo okuba mukama wammwe era ne mufuuka muddu w'ekibi. Temumanyi nti oyo gwe mwegabira okuba abaddu okumugondera, muli baddu be gwe mugondera: oba bwa kibi okutuusa ku kufa, oba bwa kugonda okutuusa ku butuukirivu?

Mumpulire era mumpulire bulungi, mmwe Ekkanisa yange; mumanye nti nnyimiridde mu kunyooma ebikolwa ebiri mu ggwanga lyammwe ne mu nsi yonna, bye mukkiriziganyizza ne mubikkiriza mu Kkanisa yange okulumya abato bange. Nga mu nnaku za Nuuwa bwe byali, bwe bityo bwe biriba. Ennaku za Nuuwa zaali kiseera ensi we yafugibwanga emizizo emibi egyakolebwa bamalayika abagwa, ezzadde lyabwe lyennyini, ezzadde ly'obubi eryakolebwa okubazikiriza ddala: era balidda, kubanga laba, ndeeta ebiremu okutuukiriza obusungu bwange. Kino baakituukako nga baggyawo era nga bamenya ensalo z'obutonde Kitange ze yateekawo obulungi n'obutuukirivu bisobole okubeerawo, naye olw'okuggyawo n'okusala mu ngeri etali ya mpisa ensalo z'ennyini ezeeyoleka mu butonde, Kitange ze yateekawo, abangi ku mmwe mwefuulidde amakubo gammwe mwe mukkiriza obubi obuli mu biseera byammwe. Ekintu ekyabangawo, kye kiriba: n'ekyo ekikolebwa, kye kirikolebwa; so tewali kigambo kipya wansi w'enjuba. Temwasomanga, mmwe abalina okukkiriza okutono? Okuggyawo ensalo za Katonda yennyini kireeta eddalu, n'obulwadde, n'okutabula eby'ebintu byonna. Abangi ku mmwe musanyukira obulwadde bw'omu mutwe, nga mulowooza nti omusajja asobola okufuuka omukazi n'omukazi okufuuka omusajja, era mukulembera abaana bammwe mu kulowooza kwammwe okw'obulimba. Abangi ku mmwe mugenda mu makanisa agakulemberwa abaminsani ab'obulimba abasiyazi ab'olwatu era ab'ekivve mu bulwadde bwabwe, era mulowooza nti boogera ku lwange? Kino mukimanye: bw'oba mu mbeera eyo ey'ekibi, nnaabawaayo eri okwegomba kwe mwegomba ennyo, era mujja kungula ebibala by'amakubo gammwe agatali malongoofu.

Laba! Ku lw'ensonga eno, Katonda yabawaayo eri okwegomba okw'ensonyi: kubanga n'abakazi baabwe baakyusa enkozesa yaabwe ey'obutonde ne bakola ekitali kya butonde: Era n'abasajja nabo bwe batyo, ne baleka enkozesa ey'obutonde ey'omukazi, ne babumbujja mu kwegomba kwabwe omu ku munne, abasajja n'abasajja nga bakola ekitasaana, ne bafuna mu bo empeera ey'ensobi yaabwe eyali esaanidde. Naye nedda, tekimala kukulemberwa abasajja abakyakazi n'obulimba bwabwe; mukkiriza abaana bammwe okukyamizibwa era n'okumenyebwamenyebwa mu maaso gange. Waliwo ebibonerezo binene ebitegekeddwa ku lw'ebikolwa bino, era tebijja kusonyiyibwa. Naye mukyagenda mu maaso n'eddalu lyammwe, era okuggyako nga mwenenyezza, nnaabaserengula okuva mu kamwa kange.

Mujje gyendi nga mwenenyezza, mutuuule mu bibukutu n'evvu, mmanye obwenkanya bw'okwenenya kwammwe era mukaabe amaziga n'okuwuubaala olw'ebibi byammwe muleme kusasula muwendo mu kusala omusango. Abangi ku mmwe mulina amalala mangi kubanga mugezaako okunfuula Mukama wammwe nga mukyalina omutima ogutalina kukkiriza, kubanga omuntu akkiriza n'omutima okutuuka ku butuukirivu. Kale mube n'okukkiriza mulyoke mulokoke; okukkiriza kwammwe kuliragibwa eri bonna nga mwegaana ekibi ne muva mu makubo gammwe amacaafu. Ababi batambulira ku njuyi zonna, abasajja abasinga obubi bwe bagulumizibwa. Naye mukyakkiriza amakubo g'omusodoma, era muyamba okuleeta obubi obunene ku nsi, okutuuka ku ddaala lye mulondera abakwasi b'abaana okukulembera ensi zammwe. Temulina wadde eddaala erisooka ery'okwawula, era temugoberera kkubo lyange; era bwe muligenda mu maaso, nnaabawaayo ddala eri okwegomba kwammwe okw'ensonyi okuzikiriza omubiri omwoyo gulyoke gulokoke.

Ka nzigule amaaso gammwe, mmwe abatamanyi era abazibe b'amaaso ku bwammwe, abatuula mu bucaafu n'ekizikiza, nga mwerindiggula mu bitosi. Abangi ku mmwe muzibye amaaso n'ebigambo ebirungi n'emboozi ennungi ne muwa okukkiriza kwammwe okujjuvu eri abamenyi b'amateeka n'abantu ababi abalya abaana. Bakwata abaana, babatulugunya olw'okwegomba kwabwe okubi: Kubanga bwe gutyo obusungu bwa Katonda bwe bujja ku Babulooni ey'Eby'obusamize, naye abangi ku mmwe mukyagenda mu maaso n'okuwa okukkiriza kwammwe eri ensolo zino ez'omu nsiko eziteekeddwawo eri omuliro ogutazikira ogw'emirembe n'emirembe.

Mufulume mu ye, mmwe abantu bange, oba muligabana naye kawumpuli we n'omusango omubi ogumujjira; era mbalabudde. Kubanga Kristo alina ntegeeragana ki ne Beliyaali? Bwe mugabana naye obulimba bwe, muligabana naye kawumpuli we; kale mweyawule ddala mu kigambo ne mu kikolwa, so temukkiriziganya nabo n'akatono. Temubeerawo nga temufaayo, nga mukolagana; kubanga nnaabonereza bonna abakola bwe batyo. Mulowooza nti olw'okuba mulina obulamu obutaggwaawo temusobola kufuna kufiirwa kunene na bibonerezo? Nnaabaggyako empeera zammwe ne nzigaba, era muliba abasinga obutono mu bwakabaka obw'omu ggulu bwe muligenda mu maaso, kubanga bw'atyo bw'ayogera Yeshua HaMashiach, Omugole, eri Omugole wange!


Okuva eri Yakuwa, Katonda Omukulu Ataggwaawo:

Lwaki mmwe abataagasa abakola obubi ku kitonde kyange, mmwe abataagasa abeetunze eri Setaani, mmwe abataagasa abafuuse Setaani katonda wammwe, mmwe abataagasa abagaana omwana wange Yesu Kristo n'obulokozi bwe nateekateeka mu linnya lye, mmwe abataagasa abalese abaana bammwe bbo okwokebwa mu muliro gwa Moloki. Nja kubawa ensi gye mwagala, era mbaterekerere ensi etaliimu nze, ensi etaliimu mwana wange Kristo Yesu, ensi erimu ennyanja ennene ey’omuliro n’ekibiriiti nga yammwe yokka.

Nja kubawa ekizikiza kye mwagala ennyo, kubanga omusana gwange tegujja kubeerayo, era mujja kumala emirembe n’emirembe nga mwawukanye nange, Yakuwa, Katonda ow’ekitiibwa ekingi, ogw’ekitiibwa n’okumasamasa kwe tewali mubiri gufa gwe gusobola kulaba era abaawo okuva emirembe gyonna n’emirembe gyonna. Mmwe abajeemu, ababi abazikirizza ekitonde kyange okuva ku lubereberye mu kwegomba kwammwe n’obukyayi n’obuggya, mutegeere nti mwekuŋŋaanyirizza ekibonerezo n’okubonyaabonyezebwa okunene kwe mutajja kuwonako, n’eri bonna abakyamizibwa obulimba bwammwe n’okugaana Yesu Kristo, ne mugaana okuva mu bulimba bwammwe okudda eri amazima gange.

Mulowooza nti okwonoona amakubo gange n’okuggyawo ensalo zange kintu kitono, naye mujja kutegeera obusungu bwange olulimi lw'omuliro olusooka bwe lulibakwatako, era mujja kubikka omuliro n’ekibiriiti bye mutajja kuwonako. Ate era, mwatta, ne mukwata, ne munyaga, ne musobya ku bantu, ne mukola obubi, ne muzikiriza, era ne mwetaba mu bikolwa eby’omuzizo ennyo, nga mwegomba n’emibiri egitali gya bulijjo; era mwegasse ku bamalayika abagwa, nange nja kubagattira ku nnyanja y’omuliro gye nateekera Omulyolyomi ne bamalayika be, era temujja kuwona. Temulina mirembe.

Nja kubasalira omusango, kubanga ndi Katonda ow’obwenkanya, era ndi Katonda awalanira eggwanga. Naye, mujja kunnyimirirawo n’amalala, nga mwerwanako, nga mulowooza nti nja kutya amakubo gammwe aga Setaani agagamba nti: kola ky'oyagala, lye lijja okuba etteeka lyonna. Temulina mirembe. Nja kusasula, era mugamba nti ndi Katonda wa kwagala (era bwe ntyo), naye ne musuubira nti sijja kusalira musango abo aboonoonye ekitonde kyange, abo abaabonyaabonya ne batta omwana wange omu yekka eyazaalibwa bwe yeewaayo okuba ekinunulo kya bonna; yee, musuubira nti sijja kubasalira musango olw’amakubo gammwe amataagasa bwe mwatta, ne mubonyaabonya, ne mulemaza abataliiko musango era ne mulumya abaana bange abato olw'ebigendererwa byammwe.

Mazima, Setaani yennyini yandizikirizza byonna ebyange olw’omululu gwe, naye era ne mugezaako okunnanga obusirusiru mu misango gyange egy’obutuukirivu bwe njija okukuuma abange. Ennaku zammwe ez’okukyamya abaana bange abato zinaatera okuggwaawo. Laba! Ensigo yammwe n'omunnabbi w'obulimba bijja kuzikirizibwa ddala, era nja kubasuula nga balamu mu nnyanja ennene eyaka omuliro n’ekibiriiti; bajja kubalindirira okwegatta nabo mu busika bwammwe.

Omusango omunene guli ku ggwe, ai omulangira w'ekizikiza; obwakabaka bwo na bonna abakugoberera binaatera okuzikirizibwa ddala. Kubanga mmwe si bakatonda, era temusobola kwerokola mmwe abataagasa okuva mu mukono gwange, era nja kubasiba mu kizikiza ekikutte kye mwagala ennyo, n'obuziba obusinga nja kubasibiramu n’olujegere olunene lwe mutasobola kumenya. Yee, mu bunnya mujja kubeeramu olunaku lumu, kubanga olunaku lumu eri Mukama lulinga emyaka lukumi, n’emyaka lukumi gilinga olunaku lumu. Era n'oluvannyuma nja kubasumulula akaseera katono mulyoke mukeme era mukuŋŋaanye abo bonna ab’omuzizo abasembayo abandyagoberedde mmwe n'amakubo gammwe. Era n'oluvannyuma nja kubookya mwenna n’omuliro, n'entebe ez'omusango zijja kuteekebwawo, era nja kusala omusango gwammwe mwenna abakola obubi, era mujja kugenda mu kibonerezo ekitaggwaawo okuva mu maaso ga Mukama. Mujja kwokera mu maaso ga bamalayika ne mu maaso g'omwana gw'endiga, era bajja kubalaba nga mubonyaabonyezebwa era nga mubonererezebwa, kubanga mwabasobyako bonna, era mujja kusasulira ddala ebibi byammwe emirembe n'emirembe.

Naye omuntu yenna akoowoola erinnya lya Mukama okutuusa ku nkomerero alirokolebwa, k’abe Muyudaaya oba Mumawanga, kubanga obulokozi tebusibiddwa ku nsalo za nsi.

Ggwe, Babulooni eky’Ekyaama, entindo zo zirigwa; enguudo zo zirivulungulwa, ziryatikamu, era teziriyitikamu; engeri z’otambuzaamu eby’amaguzi zitandise dda okusaanawo era zijja kusigala matongo. Tewali byakweyambisa birikuweerezebwa, kubanga tewali alijja kukuyamba, era olibeera wekka mu ssaawa yo ey’okwonoonebwa, ggwe malaaya omukulu. N’akawumpuli akanene kalikukwata, era n’ensolo zenyini zirirumba, kubanga ebiro bituuse. Amagye amamyuufu gavumenti yo enkyamu ge yakkiriza okuyingira mu nsalo zo galitta buli musajja, mukazi, na mwana atagasa ntegeka yaabwe. Kubanga balitta ne basiba mu buwambe bonna be balisanga. Ge magye g’ensolo egakung’aanyiziddwa okuva mu nsonda ennya ez’ensi olw’ekigendererwa ekyo, era ensolo ekeesereese n’omunnabbi ow’obulimba omukweke be babakung’aanyizza.

Tebalina kisa, tebalina kusaasira, tebalina buyambi, tebalina kwenenya, era bakyayidde ddala abantu ba Babulooni eky’Ekyaama olw’ebibi eby’entiisa abakulembeze bammwe bye baakola ku nsi zaabwe. Ate nga mmwe abasinga obungi mwajagulizanga entalo zammwe ezitali ntuukirivu bwe mwazirabiranga mu kusanyuka ku televizoni zammwe, bwe mwasanyukiranga ennyo ebibi by’abakulembeze bammwe. Mwakakasa abakulembeze bammwe be mwasiima ebibi byabwe mu kulonda, era bwe mutyo muligabana nabo mu musango gwabwe, kubanga mwefuulidde abasaana okufuna omugabo gw’omusango ogw’emirundi ebiri, era gulibatuukako amangu ago.

N’olwekyo Omugole wange alirabikira ku ntebe yange ey’omusango, era abamu balifuna okufiirwa, abamu balitwala empeera ennene, era bonna balifuna mu bujjuvu ekyo kye baali bafunyeeyo nga kitandikwa: Kubanga nnaabawa emibiri emiggya, egitaliimu kibi egitalivunda n’akatono! Era obubonero bulisumululwa, kubanga newankubadde okubonaabona n’ennaku bibunye ku nsi okumala emyaka mingi kati, si bwe busungu bwa Katonda newankubadde obw’omwana gw’endiga. Abasinga obungi ku mmwe mu kusirusiru mulowooza nti mulina okugumiikiriza obusungu bwa Katonda, nga mu kwerowooza mulowooza nti muyinza okugumira ebigenda okujja. Mutegeere nti nnalokola Ekkanisa yange okuva mu busungu, kubanga nnajja eri abange n’eri Abamawanga mu mulembe ogw’ekisa ng’endagaano ey’emirembe, nga mpawa emirembe n’okutabaganyizibwa ne Kitange ow’Ebitangaala, ye El Shaddai! Era abo be nnalokola ku lwabwe baakkiriza ekisa kyange ekitasaana, kale mugoberere ekyokulabirako kyabwe!

Kale okuva mu vvu ly’omuvuzi w’embalaasi n’okuva mu kuzikirira okunene muliva ensolo n’omunnabbi ow’obulimba, kubanga ekyaama ky’obujeemu kikola dda. Kubanga Babulooni eky’Ekyaama aweereddwayo mu mikono gyabwe n’omukago ogukolebwa, ye amayembe ekkumi galimuzikiriza, kubanga be bakabaka ekkumi abawa byonna byabwe eri ogusota, ensolo, n’omunnabbi ow’obulimba. Kubanga omuzibi bw’aliggibwawo, baligolokoka mu bujjuvu ng’Ebyawandiikibwa bwe bigamba; kubanga okuva mu kavuyo muva entegeka yaabwe empya, ye entegeka y’Ogusota, kubanga lye ly’amaanyi gaabwe n’obuyinza bwaabwe, kubanga bw’atyo bw’aweereddwa mu ngeri etali ya lubeerera okumala akaseera akatono ennyo. Ogusota gulina obusungu bungi era gulitambulira mu mwoyo gwa Amani okufuula ebintu byonna ebya Kristo ebitali mu mateeka, era omuntu yenna ayogerwako wamu n’erinnya lyange, ye Yesu Kristo, aliyiggibwa era attibwe. N’abo abataganza bulamu bwabwe okutuusa ku nkomerero balirokolebwa era bafune empeera nnene. Kale mutereke ebirowoozo byammwe n’emitima gyammwe ku ssanyu eribateereddwa mu maaso, kubanga mu ngeri ng’eyo nnabalokola mu kubonaabona okw’entiisa okwali okwammwe, wabula nga nze nneeyagalira ne ntwaala ekibonerezo kyammwe ne nsasulira mu bujjuvu ebibi by’abantu omulundi gumu ogw’olubeerera, ye eby’ebiro byonna.

8Laba! Omusango Gujja n'Ebiro Bituse!June 4, 2024

Kubanga bwe mwali muyinza okuba n’emirembe n’obutebenkevu, mmwe mwasalawo okugoberera mu buzibe bw’amaaso abo abaayonoona eggwanga lyammwe. N’ab’omu nnyumba ya Edomu, omuziro ogwasaasaanira ensi, era abatawaanyizza ensi okuva omubi lwe yasaasaanya eby’emizizo bye mu bitonde bya Yahweh.
