Eby’okwanjula

Amalangirira gano gakola ng'okulabula okw'okwenenya omusango ogujjuvu nga tegunnagwa, era gakola ng'ekiragiro eri ebyo ebigenda okubaawo mu bwangu.

Kimanyibwe nti, mu kifo kino, nkola ng'omubaka w'amateeka era omubaka atumiddwa okuva ku ntebe y'obwakabaka bwa Yahweh yennyini, wansi w'obuyinza bwa Kristo Yesu, era ye Yeshua HaMashiach, gwe mpeereza. Nze kyonna Mukama kye yanzimba okuba, era nze Biblaridion we, akatabo ke akatono, ddala omubaka alagibwa n'obubaka obukaawa naye nga buwooma era mu mukono gwange mulimu akatabo akatono akagule. Selah. Sajja na buyinza bwange oba kweyagalira kwange, naye ntumiddwa n'Ebigambo Mukama bye yasiga mu Mwoyo gwange n'omukono gwe ogw'amaanyi, okubitegeeza ng'ayagadde era ng'akoowodde, bisomebwe era birangirirwe eri Ekkanisa, ne Isiraeri, n'eri ensi eno ejjudde ebibi era esaliddwa omusango era efa, naddala okuteekebwa mu bifuba by'abo abagwa kubanga ekiseera kyabwe kituuse. Selah!
