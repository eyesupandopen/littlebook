Mu Biwandiiko bya Yokaana Omubikkulirwa
Ntenvu 20, 2024

Eri Abantu 144,000: Muwulire Okuyita Kwange!

Wulira, Wulira, ggwe ebika byange ekkumi n'ebibiri, eddoboozi lya Masiya wammwe, Mukama, Kabaka era Kabona Asinga Obukulu, ye nze kennyini, Yeshua HaMashiach, Omwana w'Omuntu Yahweh gwe yazuukiza mu bafu. Kubanga:

Nze Masiya wammwe ow'amazima, asibuka ku ntebe y'obwakabaka bwa Dawudi, era okuva ku ntebe ye gye ndifugira Ensi y'Ekisuubizo ku lwa Yahweh, Katonda ataggwaawo, kubanga bwe yasuubiza bajjajjammwe. Kale mutegeere nti nze Mpologoma ey'Ekika kya Yuda, so tewaweebwa kabaka oba nnabbi yenna ava mu Ddaani, mwe muva nnabbi ow'obulimba.

Nze Mukama wammwe, kubanga Yahweh yanfuula Mukama wa Bakama, era nabagula n'omusaayi gwange mwennyini ng'Embaga yammwe ey'Okuyitako etuukiridde, eyaddiririra ebibi by'Abamawanga n'Abaayudaaya okufaanagana, mmwe mwenna mubeere omuntu omu omuggya mu nze, ye Kristo Yesu. Kubanga Yahweh yampa erinnya erisinga amannya gonna, era n'ansanyukirwamu Entebe Ye olw'obuwulize bwange. Kale Obukama bwange buweereddwa ng'akasulu ka kalambe ak'obulokozi, kubanga nfuuliddwa omwoyo oguwa obulamu, era Yahweh yekka y'ansinga obukulu, kubanga Ye Katonda ataggwaawo.

Nze Kabaka wammwe, kubanga Yahweh yanfuula Kabaka wa Bakabaka, era ndifuga ensi mu butuukirivu. Era laba! Ndeeta Omugole wange, alifuga nange, kubanga mbafudde bakabaka era bakabona mu maaso ga Yahweh ataggwaawo, atalina amufaanana. Kubanga mu kufuga kwange okw'ekisuubizo okw'emyaka olukumi, nze nnaaba n'obuyinza obusinga obukulu, era ndituma bakabaka bange ne bakabona bange okufuga mu busika bwabwe obuli mu nze; kubanga nabatonda buto mu nze, nga mbajja mu Baayudaaya n'Abamawanga okufaanagana ne mbafuula omuntu omu omuggya, nga bafuuliddwa buto mu butuukirivu. Ndi omu mu kwegatta n'ekigendererwa ne Kitange, Yahweh, era mu Ntebe ye ansinga obukulu.

Nze kabona wammwe asinga obukulu emirembe n'emirembe ng'engeri ya Merukizeddeeki bw'eri, era nawaayo omusaayi gwange ogutangirira ng'ekiweebwayo bwe nnawaayo omubiri gwange ng'Embaga yammwe ey'Okuyitako etuukiridde era enzijuvu. Kubanga Abebbulaniya bajulira bwe nalagira Omutume Pawulo okubabuulirira ku lwange (yali wa Benyamini): Omusaayi gwa Kristo, eyawaayo omubiri gwe okuyitira mu Mwoyo ataggwaawo nga talina bbala eri Katonda, tegusingako nnyo okutukuza omwoyo gwammwe okuva mu bikolwa ebifu okusobola okuweereza Katonda omulamu? Era n'olwekyo, ekiweebwayo kyange eky'omubiri gwange kiri kya mulundi gumu emirembe gyonna, so tewali Masiya mulala.

Wadde ng'ekkuŋŋaaniro lya Sitaani lijja kwaniriza masiya waabwe ow'obulimba ne nnabbi ow'obulimba era omuzimbi wa yeekaalu ow'ekisolo (kubanga ye kisolo ekiva mu nsi). Kubanga mmwe muli yeekaalu ya Katonda omulamu, so tewakyali kwetaaga kwa ssaddaaka ya nsolo nate, kubanga nze ssaddaaka esembayo, ye Mbaga ey'Okuyitako etuukiridde, etalina kibi, era emirimu gyange gijjudde era gituukiridde. N'olwekyo, tewakyaliwo kwetaaga kwa kintu ekyo nate, wabula mujje gyendi n'ebibi byammwe nga mwenenyezza, nange ndibatukuza okuva mu bikolwa byammwe ebifu eby'omubiri. Kubanga mu Abaruumi kyawandiikibwa nti: Kubanga etteeka ly'Omwoyo gw'obulamu obuli mu Kristo Yesu linsumuludde okuva mu tteeka ly'ekibi n'ery'okufa. Kubanga bwe ntyo bwe mbasumuludde mu nze.

Mutegeere, mmwe ebika byange n'abantu bange, nti ebiseera by'Abamawanga biri kumpi n'okuggwaawo. Ebiseera by'Abamawanga byajjawo olw'obukakanyavu bw'emitima gya bajjajjammwe. Kubanga kiki Pawulo kye yayogera mu Bikolwa ku nsonga eno: Kyali kya kwetaagisa ekigambo kya Katonda okusooka okubuulirwa mmwe: naye kubanga mukisudde wala nammwe, ne mweramula nti temusaana kufuna bulamu butaggwaawo, laba, tukyukidde Abamawanga.

Mbawa amagezi, mmwe abantu bange, temukola nsobi ze zimu so temubeera mu bibi bya bajjajjammwe, wabula mwenenyezebibi byammwe bennyini era mwenenyeze n'ebibi bya bajjajjammwe, mujje gyendi nange ndibaaniriza, kubanga muli baana b'ekisuubizo. Era buli yodi oba thorn ya yodi erituukirira; kubanga ekyo Yahweh kye yasuubiza tekisoboka kukyusibwa, kubanga tekisoboka Yahweh okulimba. N'olwekyo, nze kutuukirizibwa kwa bisuubizo bingi nnyo, kubanga nze Masiya wammwe ow'amazima, ye kifaananyi kya Katonda ataggwaawo. Kubanga nze mwana we omu yekka gw'eyazaala, ye Mwana gw'endiga gwa Katonda Yokaana Omubatiza gwe yategeeza emirembe gyonna. Kubanga yagamba nti: Laba Omwana gw'endiga gwa Katonda, aggyawo ekibi ky'ensi!

Era bwe ntyo nakyukira amawanga kubanga ensi yange yanjegaana, ne bwe nnali maze okufiirira ebibi byabwe n'okuzuukizibwa. Kubanga nabatumira abatume bange ne bannabbi bange, era bangi ne balokolebwa. Kubanga ku ntandikwa y'emirembe gy'ekisa, nabatuma mu Yeekaalu ne mu makungaaniro, era bangi ne bankyukira ne bannyaniriza nga Mukama waabwe. Era bwe ntyo mbayita okufuulibwa abalamu mu Bukama bwange, kubanga mbanunudde eri Kitange n'okutuusa mmwe okuba abaana Be okuyitira mu mwoyo gwange gwe ndibateekako akabonero. Era n'olwekyo ebiseera by'Abamawanga biweddeyo, kubanga kati nabo banjeemye ne basisa Yesu omulala gwe basinza nga katonda, bwe batyo ne befuula abasinza ebifaananyi; kubanga bwe batyo Sitaani bwe yabakema. Kubanga waliwo Katonda omu yekka, Yahweh ataggwaawo, era waliwo Masiya omu ow'amazima, ye nze, Yeshua HaMashiach.

Kubanga baabula amakubo gange ag'ekyama, ne balonzalonza okwolesebwa kwange n'ebigendererwa by'ekyawandiikibwa kyange ekitukuvu. Baabula entegeka ya Kitange ey'okununula, era mu malala bagaana ebigambo byange bye nawandiisa eri Abaruumi. Kubanga Pawulo yagamba nti: Kale njogera nti, Katonda yasudde abantu be? Nedda ddala. Kubanga nange ndi Muyisirayiri, ow'ezzadde lya Ibulayimu, ow'ekika kya Benyamini. Katonda teyasudde bantu be, be yamanya edda. Ate nate: Kale nno? Isirayiri teyafuna ekyo ky'anoonya; naye abalonde baakifuna, abalala ne bazibizibwa amaaso. Era bwe ntyo okulonda kwali kunywevu eri abo be nnalonda okuva mu bika; kubanga abatume bange bonna baali ba mu nsi era aba ekisuubizo, era bwe ntyo nabatuma eri amawanga, abo abakulembeze bammwe ab'eddiini be bavvoola mu bulimba, nga babayita ab'obulimba. Abakulembeze abo ab'obulimba n'abasumba abataliiko mugaso era beegaana n'okuzuukira kwange, kubanga nzuukiziddwa mu bafu, so ssikyadda nate kufa emirembe n'emirembe.

Kubanga kiki ekirala Abaruumi kye boogera ku nsonga eno: Dawudi agamba nti, Emmere yaabwe efuuke ekyambika, n'omutego, n'ekyesittaza, n'empeera gye bali: Amaaso gaabwe gazibirire baleme okulaba, n'emigongo gyabwe gikutame ennaku zonna. Kale njogera nti, Beesittadde balyoke bagwe? Nedda ddala: naye okugwa kwabwe kwe kwaleetera ab'amawanga obulokozi, balyoke bakwatirwe obuggya.

Kubanga saagala, abooluganda, obutamanya kyama kino, muleme okwerowooza okuba abagezigezi; nti obuzibe bw'amaaso obw'ekitundu bubadde ku Isirayiri, okutuusa okujjuza kw'ab'amawanga lwe kulituuka. Era bwe ntyo ndangirira nti okujjuza kw'Abamawanga kutuuse, era amakubo ga Katonda ag'ekyama galiddizibwa Isirayiri olw'obuwulize obw'okukkiriza. Kubanga nkyali ndokola Yakobo, era ensigalira ennesigwa eneekyukira gyendi mu buwulize, mu kukkiriza okungi!

Era bwe ntyo nzize okubakwatiza obuggya mu mitima gyammwe ku lwange, Yeshua HaMashiach, ne Yahweh Katonda omukulu ataggwaawo gwe mukyasinza awatali kumanya, kubanga ekiremba ky'obuzibe bw'amaaso kijja kubikka ku muntu yenna agoberera amateeka ga Musa. Kubanga kiki kye nnagamba Abakkolinso ku nsonga eno: So si nga Musa, eyateeka ekiremba ku maaso ge, abaana ba Isirayiri baleme okutunuulira enkomerero y'ekyo ekiggibwawo: Naye ebirowoozo byabwe byazibizibwa amaaso: kubanga okutuusa leero ekiremba kye kimu kikyaliwo nga tekiggiddwawo mu kusoma Endagaano Enkadde; ekiremba ekyo kiggibwawo mu Kristo. Naye ne leero, Musa bw'asomebwa, ekiremba kiba ku mutima gwabwe.

Naye bwe kirikyukira Mukama, ekiremba kiriggibwawo.

Kubanga ntuukirizza amateeka ga Musa, kubanga mmwe newakubadde bajjajjammwe temusobola kutuukiriza bugondero butuukiridde olw'ekibi ekiri mu musaayi gwammwe. Kubanga nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, ntuukirizza amateeka mu bujjuvu, era amateeka gagonderwa butuukirivu kokka olw'okukkiriza okuli mu nze, Yesu Kristo. Mubalibwa abatuukirivu olw'okwegatta kwammwe okujjudde nange, era bwe kityo omutuukirivu aliba mulamu lwa kukkiriza. Era bwe ntyo ndiggyawo ekiremba ku mitima gyammwe musobole okundaba, Yeshua, Masiya wammwe ow'amazima, kubanga sibalesewo! Era njija gyemuli mu ndagaano empya, so si mu bukadde bw'ebbaluwa. Kubanga nakakasa endagaano yange empya mu musaayi gwange mwennyini, okusinziira ku kwagala n'ebisuubizo bya Yahweh, olw'okununulibwa kwammwe okujjudde. Era ndibakulembera okutuuka ku busika bwammwe, kubanga ebisuubizo bya Katonda ye yee ne amiina. Tewali n'akatono ka yodi oba thorn ya yodi erirema, kubanga byonna biri kumpi kutuukirizibwa; era mu kiseera kyabyo, ddala, ndituukiriza byonna, kubanga bwe ntyo bwe nnatumibwa Yahweh okukola.

Era bwe ntyo Abamawanga banvuddeko ng'embwa eddidde ebisesemye byayo, n'ekiseera kituuse okubalekawo. Kubanga mu mirembe gyange egy'ekisa, tewali muntu n'omu Kitange gwe yalonda olw'obulokozi eyasubwa, so tewali ayinza okubaggya mu mukono gwange. Kubanga abo abanjatula, nze Yesu, nga Mukama waabwe, n'okukkiriza mu mitima gyabwe nti Katonda yanzuukiza mu bafu, baateekebwako akabonero n'omwoyo omutukuvu ow'ekisuubizo, era ebigendererwa bya Kitange bituukiridde mu nze ku lwabwe. Era bwe batyo banaabeeranga nange emirembe gyonna.

Kubanga omuzeeyituuni gubikkula amazima. Era kiki kye nnagamba ku muti gw'omuzeeyituuni mu bbaluwa ya Pawulo eri Abaruumi: Era oba ng'amatabi agamu gakutuddwawo, naawe, ng'oli muti gw'omuzeeyituuni ogw'omu nsiko, ne weekattako mu go, ne weegatta nago ku kir kore n'amasavu g'omuti gw'omuzeeyituuni; Teweenyumiririzanga ku matabi. Naye bw'oneenyumiririza, si ggwe ositula ekikolo, naye ekikolo ggwe.

Era bwe ntyo bakola ensobi ne batwala ebibi bya bajjajja mmwe ne banvaako ne badda ku bifaananyi byabwe, so tebakyawulira ddoboozi lyange. Naye bwe banaakowoola erinnya lya Mukama, balilokolebwa abo abataganya bulamu bwabwe okutuuka ku nkomerero.

Kubanga kiki ekirala omujulirwa w'Abaruumi kye yayogera mu kyawandiikibwa ng'alabula amawanga: Olwo ojja kugamba nti, Amatabi gaakutulwawo, nze ne nsibibwako. Weewaawo; olw'obutakkiriza gaakutulwawo, naawe oyimiridde lwa kukkiriza. Tobeera na birowoozo bikuluntaza, naye otyenga: Kubanga oba nga Katonda teyasonyiwa matabi ga buzaale, weekuumenga nawe aleme obutakusonyiwa. Kale laba ekisa n'obukakali bwa Katonda: ku abo abaagwa, obukakali; naye gy'oli, ekisa, bw'onoonywereranga mu kisa kye: oba naawe olikutulwawo. Nabo, bwe batasiigalanga mu butakkiriza, balisibibwako: kubanga Katonda ayinza okubasibako nate. Kubanga oba nga ggwe wakutulwa ku muti gw'omuzeeyituuni oguli ogw'omu nsiko ku buzaale, n'osibibwako mu ngeri eteri ya buzaale ku muti gw'omuzeeyituuni omulungi: bano, abali amatabi amatuufu, tebalisinga nnyo okusibibwako ku muti gwabwe ogw'omuzeeyituuni?

Kubanga saagala, abooluganda, obutamanya kyama kino, muleme okwerowooza okuba abagezigezi; nti obuzibe bw'amaaso obw'ekitundu bubadde ku Isirayiri, okutuusa okujjuza kw'ab'amawanga lwe kulituuka. Era bwe kityo Isirayiri yenna alirokolebwa: nga bwe kyawandiikibwa nti, Mu Sayuuni muliva Omulokozi, era aliggyawo obutatya Katonda ku Yakobo.

Era bwe kityo, mmwe ab'oluganda lwange, ebiseera by'Abamawanga biri kumpi n'okuggwaawo, era waliwo enkyukakyuka ennene eteekwa okubaawo, ekisa kyange we kijja okufuukirawo omusango. Era ne kaakano ebitundu bikirizibwa okuteekebwa mu kifo kyabyo n'omukono gwa Sitaani, kubanga ajja kuyimirirawo, newakubadde ajja kusaanyizibwawo ddala, era ndimenyaamenya obwakabaka bwe; kubanga si kabaka.

Era kati kituuse ekiseera eky'okusiba amatabi amatuufu nate ku muti gw'omuzeeyituuni, kubanga ebigendererwa bya Yahweh bituukiridde mu kisa n'okusaasira okutalina kya kusaanawo ebyalagirwa Abamawanga (kubanga ekisa kya Katonda n'okusaasira bye bya buli muntu), kubanga n'ekisa ekyo n'okusaasira byakozesebwa okuzuula endiga ezabula ez'ennyumba ya Isirayiri. Kubanga temusaana butamanya byafaayo byammwe, bwe muteeka wansi ebiwandiiko byammwe ebya Talimudi y'e Babulooni evvoola ne muyiga ku nze; kubanga ssatumanga kitabo ekyo, era mwetaaga okwenenya enjigiriza ezo ne mwegaana obulimba bwa Sitaani. Mudde eri ekigambo kyange ekitukuvu ekya Katonda muve ku bibi bya bajjajjammwe, kubanga ndibanunula bwe munaawulira eddoboozi lyange.

Kubanga ssatumibwa wabula eri endiga ezabula ez'ennyumba ya Isirayiri. Era bwe ntyo ebika ekkumi byasaasaanyizibwa mu mawanga okuva mu buwambe bwabyo obw'Abasuuli emabega ennyo. Byatambulatambula ne bitambula ku nsi ng'abatambuze n'abasuze. Okuva e Busuuli baagenda mu nsi z'obukiikakkono, mu Scythia, ne Bulaaya, era oluvannyuma mu Amerika, gye nabateerawo ekifo eky'emirembe okumala akaseera. Era nayita buli muntu yenna ayinza okuva mu mawanga okujja eri obulokozi bwange. N'Abayisirayiri bangi baalokolebwa era banunulibwa awamu n'abangi ab'omu mawanga, abandi tebandibadde na ssuubi. Era ekibiina kinene kyayanukula, era bwe ntyo ebiseera by'Abamawanga biweddeyo, kubanga tewakyali kiseera. Naye mutegeere nti bonna abakoowoola erinnya lya Mukama balilokolebwa bwe baba abeesigwa okutuuka ku nkomerero, kubanga ndibaaniriza awatali kufaayo ku ggwanga lye baba bavaamu.

Era bwe kityo, emitawaana gya Yakobo gijja ku nsi ne ku mawanga, kubanga kiseera kya musango munene okugwa ku nsi. Era bwe ntyo ebisolo n'amaanyi ga ggomasi birigolookoka; naye temutwala kabonero ke so temugwa mu bubbi bwe. Kubanga yeegattise dda n'abafuzi b'eggwanga lyammwe, ye nnyumba ya Sitaani n'engeri zaabwe ez'Edomu. Era ayogera dda okuvvoola okungi, kubanga ategekebwa olw'ekigendererwa kye eky'amazima.

Kubanga ekizikiza eky'okuzikiriza kijja. Kubanga kiki kye nawandiisa omwesigwa wange Lukka emirembe gyonna: Era bwe muliraba Yerusaalemi nga yeetooloddwa amagye, ne mulyoka mumanya ng'okuzikirizibwa kwayo kuli kumpi. Abo abali mu Buyudaaya ne baddukira ku nsozi; n'abo abali mu kyo ne bavaamu; n'abo abali mu byalo baleme okuyingiramu. Kubanga ezo ze nnaku z'okuwoolera eggwanga, byonna ebyawandiikibwa biryoke bituukirire.

Era bwe kiriba mu kiseera ekituufu, nange ndikuuma abange, kubanga mbatuma ng'abannabbi bange, abasumba, abawonya, n'abaweereza. Kubanga mbayita mu buweereza, okutuukiriza okuyitibwa kwammwe okw'amazima okwaweebwa ng'ekisuubizo eri bajjajjaffe okuva eri Yahweh yennyini, era n'omukono gwange birituukirizibwa. Kubanga Yahweh ateesezza entebe yange ku Ntebe ye, era ngabanye ku ntebe yange n'Omugole wange, abali nange bulijjo. Era bwe ntyo tewali ku nsi ansinga obukulu; tewali n'omu ku bannabbi bammwe ab'obulimba gwe mugeraageranya, kubanga bonna banafu era baavu era batumibwa okubuzaabuza n'okukyamya, kubanga basumba bataliiko mugaso.

Manya, mmwe abantu bange, nti omulabe abateereddewo emitego mingi, kale mugoberere okukulembera kwange era mujja kugyewala gyonna. Kubanga mangu nnyo essanyu lyonna liriggyibwawo ku nsi. Abangi tebalizuula n'akameeme, so tebaliba na mmeeza gye baliirako wabula baliba banoonyi mu nsi olw'okukkiriza kwabwe okuli mu nze.

povemu okukemebwa emmere yaabwe ey'obulimba eneeetaga akabonero k'ekisolo okusobola okuyingira mu ntegeka yaabwe eyonoonese. Kubanga akabonero tekalisonyiyibwa, era bonna abagwa mu mutego guno tebalisumululwa; kubanga kya lubeerera eri bonna abakola okusalawo okwo okusembayo, kubanga baliba bateesezzawo ekyenkomerero kyabwe era ekyo kyenkana nkomerero. Kale temukemebwa ekyambo, oba muliggwa mu kitimba ne mukwatibwa emirembe gyonna okuyitira mu luba lwammwe.

Mbatuma okukuŋŋaanya ekisibo kyange, era ndibakulembera okubatuusa gye bali ne mbaleeta gyemuli, era mulibasumba ku lwange, kubanga nze musumba omukulu. Temujja kwegaana kika kirala oba ggwanga oba ggwanga eddala lyonna, kubanga ekisuubizo kya Yahweh kinywevu, era bonna abakoowoola erinnya lya Mukama balilokolebwa. Temuba na nsingobukakanyavu nga bajjajja mmwe, era mubeere bawombeefu eri eddoboozi lyange. Kubanga bajjajja mmwe banjeema, kale mwatule ebibi bya bajjajja mmwe n'ebyammwe mwennyini mu kwenenya gyendi, nange ndibasonyiwa ne mbafukumulirako okwagala kwange n'ekisa kyange, kubanga ndifuula emigga gy'amazzi amalamu okukulukuta okuva mu lubuto lwammwe. Mbadde mbawa amaanyi olw'ebyamagero ebikulu. Era mbatuma eri abooluganda okubakyusa gyendi, kubanga ensigalira eneeyongera okulokolebwa; era ndirokola Yakobo okuva mu mukono gw'abalabe baabwe, kubanga nkirangiridde era bwe kityo kiribeera.

Nze, ye Yeshua HaMashiach Yahweh gwe yantuma, nze Masiya ow'amazima owa Isirayiri n'ensi yonna. Nange ndituma abajulirwa bange ababiri, abo Yahweh be yaggya ku nsi mu biseera byabwe: olw'ekigendererwa ekyo baatwalibwa, era bwe batyo banaatumibwa. Nina ebintu bingi eby'okubagamba era ebyo birigambibwa, era ndibateekateeka olw'obubaka bwammwe, era mujja kunkolera emirimu eminene egy'amaanyi, n'emirimu gye mutalowoozangako nti gisoboka. Kubanga ebiseera ebibi ennyo bulijjo bivaamu abasajja abakulu aba Yahweh, n'abasajja abatayinza kumenyeka, kubanga mujja kuwummulira mu maanyi gange, era nze siyinza kumenyeka. Kubanga bwe ntyo Yahweh bwe yanfuula, era bwe ntyo bwe ndibafuula. Kale muwulire eddoboozi lyange, kubanga njita era ndituma bamalayika bange okuteeka akabonero ka Yahweh ku 144,000.

Ndibakuuma mu bulamu obutebenkevu okuva ku kisolo n'emizizo gyakyo; ndibakweka, era muliriisibwa okuva mu ggulu ne bwe muba mwetaaga, kale temutya. Temutya ku lw'obulamu bwammwe, kubanga empeera ennene ye yammwe olw'obuweereza n'obwesigwa, era ndibasasula olw'okuyimirirawo kwammwe.

Nze Mpologoma ey'omu kika kya Yuda, era mujja kuwulira okuwuluguma kwange, kubanga mbayita.

Nze oyo aliteeka akabonero ku 12,000 okuva mu buli kika okuggyako Ddaani ne Efulayimu.

Nze oyo alibettenta omwana w'empologoma aliva mu Ddaani.

Nze oyo aliwangula ekisolo ne nnabbi ow'obulimba, era ndibasuula nga balamu mu nnyanja ennene ey'omuliro.

Nze oyo alikulembera abantu bange okutuuka ku malundiro amagimu okuyitira mu basumba bange, kubanga nze musumba omukulu.

Nze oyo alituukiriza ebisuubizo ebyakolebwa eri bajjajjaffe, kubanga bwe ntyo bwe nnatumibwa.

Nze oyo alibettenta omutwe gwa Sitaani, kubanga abalangira b'ensi eno balisasula.

Nze oyo alifuga okuva ku ntebe ya Dawudi, kubanga obwakabaka bwange tebulina nkomerero.

Nze oyo ajja mu bire, era ndijja mu ggwanga lyange nentwala okufuga kwange, so siriziyizibwa.

Nze, ye nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, ntumye ebigambo bino eri abo be njita. Kale muwulire ebigambo byange mu buwulize, era mumanye nti ndisumulula ensi n'ensi yonna ne nfuga mu butuukirivu. Kale muwulire okuyita kwange, kubanga njija gyemuli era ndibayigiriza amakubo gange ne mbakulembera okutuuka mu mazima gonna. Kale temugaana biwandiiko byange, kubanga ebyogererwa bya Katonda biriddizibwa Isirayiri, era ensi erimanya nti waliwo Katonda mu Isirayiri, ye Yahweh, Katonda omukulu ataggwaawo, kubanga tewali mulala, so tewali amufaanana. Shalom! Shalom! Kale mubeere mu mirembe era mubeere bulungi, kubanga ndibawonya mwenna okutuusa ku nkomerero abo abateeka okukkiriza kwabwe mu Yeshua HaMashiach.
