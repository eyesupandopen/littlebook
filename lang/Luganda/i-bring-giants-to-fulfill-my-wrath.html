Ndeeta Abagagga Okutuukiriza Obusungu Bwange!

Laba, bajja! Ndeeta abagagga okutuukiriza obusungu bwange! Wuliriza ebigambo byange, ggwe atuula ku nsi, era ebigambo bya Yakuwa, Katonda omunene ow'emirembe gyonna. Kubanga nga bwe kyali mu nnaku za Nuuwa, bwe kityo bwe kiriba: era waaliwo abagagga mu nsi mu nnaku ezo, okuva mu nnaku za Yeredi okutuuka ku za Nuuwa bwe baakolebwa era ne batambula ku nsi mu bubi obungi. Era nga bwe kyali, bwe kityo bwe kiriba, era abagagga bajja kukomawo. Mu nnaku za Yeredi abalabi baaserengetera ku lusozi Kerumooni, bonna nga bawera ebikumi bibiri mu nnaku za Yeredi bwe baava mu ggulu waggulu. Ne beesiba n'ebikolimo eby'okwerumya, era ekirayiro kyabwe kye beesibako ne kifuuka ekikolimo eky'emirembe n'emirembe eri bonna abakisibwako era kye batayinza kwewonya, era bwe batyo ne basendasenda abantu ne babayimbula mu kibi ekinene.

Abalabi baasendebwasendebwa ba Sitaani, okusinziira ku kwegomba kw'emitima gyabwe, ne bagwa mu kukemebwa okuva mu kitiibwa kyabwe eky'olubereberye, era baalina obulamu obutaggwaawo mu ggulu, kubanga bwe batyo bwe baatondebwa. Naye baagwa, era nga bwe ziri emmunyeenye ezigwa, bwe batyo bwe baagwa, era ne beegatta ku ba Sitaani emirembe n'emirembe. Bwe batyo ba Sitaani ne basendasenda abakazi abeesimbye ne babayigiriza okusendasenda abalabi olw'okwegomba kw'emitima gyabwe, era abakazi abamu ne beewaayo kyeyagalire eri abalabi, era omusango gwabwe baligwetikka. Abalala baatwalibwa ku mpaka kubanga abalabi baajeemesa abakulembeze baabwe ab'ensi abaakulembera, ne bagenda ne batwala abakazi ku mpaka okuva eri babbaabwe n'amaka gaabwe, era bwe batyo abalabi ne batwala bonna be baalonda okuba bakazi baabwe, ne babonyaabonya bangi, era mu kugattibwa okwo okutali kutukuvu ne muzaalibwamu abagagga, era abagagga ab'edda abanene. Kubanga abalabi baayongera okwonoona ensi y'obuntu. Era bwe batyo ne boonoona ensolo, n'ebinyonyi, n'ebyennyanja n'ebika byonna eby'omubiri ne babitabika, era mu butawulira obw'okwesimbye n'obukyayi eri ensalo ze nnateekawo, nka Yakuwa, Katonda Omunene ow'Emirembe gyonna! Okuva ku lubereberye nnatonda ensi nga buli nsigo eri ng'ekika kyayo, era bwe kityo ensalo ne zisukkibwa ezaali teziteekwa kusukkibwa, era okusukka ensalo z'obutonde ezateekebwawo Yakuwa kuleeta eddalu, okubonaabona, n'okufa ku kitonde kya Yakuwa, era bonna abakola batyo balyetikka omusango gwabwe. Obulamu bwateekebwawo ng'olutimbe olw'ebitonde ebyegattira awamu, ng'obulamu bwonna bwegattira mu mirembe n'okutegeeragana, ng'okwagala kufuga omulundi era bonna banoonya obulungi bw'abalala so si bwabwe bokka. Era ekitonde awatali kwegomba n'okwagala okubi okwefaananyiriza okufa n'okuzikiriza, era bwe kityo bwe kiriba emirembe n'emirembe, era ebiseera by'okutuukirizibwa bituuse.

Era bwe batyo bamalayika abakulu abana ab'omu ggulu, Mikayiri, Uryeri, Rafayiri, ne Gaburyeri, ne baleeta omusango ku ebyo banneabwe abaasooka bye baakola ku nsi, era omusango ne gusalibwa era ne gutuukirizibwa mangu ku bonna abaali mu kyo ne ku nsi yennyini, kubanga ensi eyaliwo mu kiseera ekyo yali yonooneddwa ddala. Abaneferimu, era abaana b'abalabi, baalwanyisibwa okutuusa okufa mu ntalo nnyingi ennene ez'amawanga agalwanagana, era ekyo kubanga nziggyawo emirembe mu bo, ne balwana okutuusa okufa era ne battagana nga bakitaabwe (abalabi) bakakibwa okutunuulira okuzikirizibwa kw'ebitonde byabwe eby'omuzizo bye baakolera okuzikiriza abantu. Okuva awo, abalabi baasibibwa mu kubonerezebwa n'omusango era ne bawulibwa okumala emirembe nsanvu. Era mu ngeri y'emu gye nnalina okutunuulira ekitonde kyange kye nnayagala ennyo nga kizikirizibwa eby'omuzizo ebibi, bwe natyo bwe nnaleetera ebitonde ebibi okuttagana mu kubonerezebwa eri bakitaabwe, era bwe natyo bwe nnasasula, kubanga ndi mutuukirivu mu misango gyange bulijjo n'emirembe gyonna, era ndi Katonda ow'emisango emituukirivu egitali gikyuka, so tegiyinza kulemwa kutuukirizibwa.

Era bwe natyo ne ndeeta amataba amanene ku nsi, era amataba gaali ga maanyi mu nnaku za Nuuwa. Ensonvu z'obuziba obunene zaayabizibwa era amazzi ne gasowoka n'amaanyi okuva mu nsi yennyini, n'amadirisa g'eggulu ne gaggulwawo, amazzi agali waggulu w'olubaawo ne gajjula ensi, era ne gasukka ensozi ezisinga obuwanvu, ne gatta ebiramu byonna ku nsi, okuggyako emyoyo munaana egyawonyezebwa mu lyato, kubanga Nuuwa yali mutuukirivu mu maaso gange. Era olw'ensonga eno nnatwala Nuuwa ne mmulagira okukola eryato okusinziira ku ntegeka eyagibwa omukono gwange. Era nnamuleetera ensolo ezaali zigenda okuwona nga ntuuse nazo, era bwe kityo Nuuwa yali mwesigwa okukola byonna bye yalagirwa, era amataba ne gajja nga bwe kyalagulwa era nga bwe kyalangirirwa, kubanga ekyo kye ntegeeza, nka Yakuwa Katonda omunene ow'emirembe gyonna, tewali muntu ayinza kukikyusa wadde okukiziyiza!

Era Enoka, omuwandiisi ow'edda, annyonnyola enkomerero y'abagagga bano bwe giti, kubanga nze, era Yakuwa, bwe nnatyo bwe nnategeeza: Era kaakano, abagagga, abava mu myoyo n'omubiri, baliyitibwa emyoyo emibi ku nsi, era ku nsi gye balibeera. Emyoyo emibi givudde mu mibiri gyabwe; kubanga bazaalibwa mu bantu, okuva mu Balabi abatukuvu gye ntandikwa yaabwe n'ensibuko yaabwe; baliyitibwa emyoyo emibi ku nsi, era emyoyo emibi gye baliyitibwa. Naye emyoyo egy'omu ggulu, mu ggulu mwe munaabanga amaka gaabwe, naye emyoyo egy'oku nsi egyazaalibwa ku nsi, ku nsi gye munaabanga amaka gaabwe. Era emyoyo gy'abagagga gibonyaabonya, gijooja, gizikiriza, girumba, girwana, era gikola okuzikiriza ku nsi, era gireeta emitawaana: tegiriira mmere, naye newankubadde enjala n'ennyonta, era gireeta ensobi. Era emyoyo gino girigolokoka okulwanyisa abaana b'abantu n'abakazi, kubanga givudde mu bo.

Era bwe kityo ku kiseera ekituufu, Nuuwa n'agoba ku Lusozi Alalati, eryato we lyawummulira ng'amazzi gakendedde. Era ne nkola endagaano ne Nuuwa, era mu kiseera abazzukulu ba Nuuwa ne batandika okuva mu ndagaano gye nnakola ne Nuuwa, era olw'ekyo emyoyo emibi ne gitandika okubalumba. Era emyoyo gino, egyali emingi, ne giwangula abaana b'abaana ba Nuuwa, era bwe kityo Nuuwa n'abawolereza, ne nsiba ebitundu ekyenda ku kkumi eby'emyoyo gino emibi mu bunnya, gye gaakuumibwa okuva mu kiseera ekyo okutuusa leero. Era bwe nnatyo bwe nnalagira, era bwe kityo Nuuwa n'ayigirizibwa eddagala lyabwe lyonna, n'asendebwasendebwa n'ayigirizibwa, Nuuwa alyoke abawonye n'emiddo gy'ensi. Era bwe kityo ekimu kya kkumi eky'emyoyo gino ne kikkirizibwa okubeera eddembe, kiryoke kiyimbule mu bubi abaana b'abantu abaali beegomba obulimba bwabwe. Era emyoyo egisigaddewo egisibiddwa okusalirwa omusango mu bunnya girisumululwa mu biseera by'okubonaabona kwa Yakobo, ekikompola bwe kiriggulwawo ddala era ebitundu ekyenda ku kkumi eby'emyoyo egisibiddwa bwe birisumululwa okukola amakubo gaabyo amabi n'amabi ng'ekitundu ky'omusango gw'omuntu ow'obutonde oguli wano, era kirimansuka ku nsi yonna amangu ago n'okuzikiriza okunene!

Era bwe kityo Kanani, omuzzukulu wa Nuuwa Nuuwa gwe yakolimira olw'obubi bwe, n'akola ekibi ekinene (omulundi omulala) ng'akozesa amagezi g'abamalayika abagwa, era mu makubo ga Sitaani mwe yagenda kyeyagalire, mwe yatwalirwa kyeyagalire, era n'akola eby'omuzizo ebirala oluvannyuma lw'amataba ga Nuuwa. Era eby'omuzizo bino ne biyingira ensi za Seemu ezaagabibwa n'akalulu k'obusika, Kanani eyakolimirwa kye yaggyawo n'akibba, ne bayita ensi ye eyabbibwa ensi ya Kanani. Ezzadde lye eddene eringi lyali bitonde ebyakyusibwakyusibwa mu mubiri ebyayingira ensi z'obusuubizo ddala, gye nnatumira Musa okukuŋŋaanya abaana ba Isirayiri okuva mu nsi za Misiri okutwala obusika bwabwe okuva mu bantu ab'omuzizo. Era bwe kityo Yoswa n'alwana entalo ennene n'ebizikiza bino nga bwe kyawandiikibwa mu biwandiiko ebitukuvu, ne batwala okuva mu bizikiza bino obusika bwabwe obutuufu, era bwe kityo bwe kyawandiikibwa ng'ekisiikirize ky'ebigenda okujja. Kubanga mu ngeri y'emu omwana wange, Yeshua HaMashiach, gwe nnafuula Omwami w'abami n'Omukama w'abakama, alijja okutwala obusika bwe okuva mu batamiivu n'abanyazi, kubanga okubeera kwabwe tekuli mu mateeka. Si bakabaka era tebalina bwakabaka, wabula ennyanja ennene eyaka omuliro n'ekibiriiti ye busika bwabwe obw'emirembe n'emirembe.

Naye, Isirayiri teyaali mwesigwa ddala mu kuyitibwa kwe, era ensigalira y'abagagga n'ekkirizibwa okusigalawo. Naye ne mu butawulira bwabwe nnakangavvula eby'omuzizo bino era ne nzikiriza bingi ku byo okuva ku nsi okuva mu biseera ebyo. Era mbagobye bangi ku bo wansi w'ettaka, era bangi ku bo baagenda mu nsi etamanyiddwa emabega w'omuzira abantu gye batatambuliramu ddembe. Tegeera nti omuntu tategeera nsi kw'akolerwa era kw'abeera, kubanga omuntu alya obulimba obutali butuufu obutundibwa ng'amazima, obuddamu okukolebwamu ng'obuyigirize, era bwe kityo omuntu kyeyagalire asigala nga tamanyi nti olw'ekigambo kya Katonda eggulu lyaliwo dda, n'ensi ng'esituse mu mazzi ne mu mazzi: Ensi eyaliwo mu kiseera ekyo, bwe yajjula amazzi, n'ezikirira: Naye eggulu n'ensi, ebiriwo kaakano, olw'ekigambo kye kimu biterekebwa, biterekebwa omuliro okutuusa ku lunaku lw'omusango n'okuzikirira kw'abantu abatali batukuvu.

Era mu kiseera n'aggyawo abagagga ku nsi mu kusaasira kwange n'ekisa kyange n'okwagala kwange okuwonya abantu mu kuzikirira, era ebisigalira byabwe bisaasaanye okwetooloola ensi era biyonoonera ekitonde kyange ng'omujulizi w'okubeerawo kwabwe. N'ebisigalira by'obwakabaka bwabwe eby'edda bikyayimiridde ng'obujulizi nti baali bafuga obwakabaka bw'amawanga ku nsi mu kiseera kyabwe eky'omulembe gwa zzaabu, era mu nnaku za Nuuwa amataba nga teganajja. Ebinyonyi byabyo eby'amayinja amanene bisaasaanye ng'omujulizi okulwanyisa, era okubeerawo kwennyini okw'ebinyonyi bino kuyimiridde ng'omujulizi okulwanyisa obulimba bwa Sitaani yennyini, eyeefuba okukweka amazima alyoke abeere n'okulumba kwe okw'amangu. Era bwe ntyo ntegeeza abo abalina amatu okuwulira n'amaaso okulaba n'omutima okuyiga ebigenda okubaawo nga tebinabaawo, emitima gyabwe gireme okubalemesa mu kwewuunya n'obutakkiriza, wabula bamanye amazima era beetegekeere ebigenda okujja balyoke bawonyezebwe, era bonna abakoowoola erinnya lya Yesu Kristo okutuusa ku nkomerero yaabwe.

Bwe kityo bwe kyategeezebwa era bwe kityo bwe kyatuukirira mu kujjula kw'ebiseera, nti nnasumulula abalabi okuva mu makomera gaabwe gye baasibibwa mu kubonerezebwa n'okubonyaabonyezebwa, nga baawuddwamu buli omu. Era bwe batyo ne basumululwa oluvannyuma lw'ebiseera by'okusibibwa kwabwe bwe byaggwaako: era emirembe nsanvu. Ebyafaayo tebiyinza kukweka ddala kusumululwa kwabwe, era entalo nnyingi n'ebikangabwa n'ebirala ebyabaddewo okwetooloola ensi yonna ne kusumululwa kwabwe, ne beesuula mu buvanjuba, ne beegatta ku bujeemu bwa Sitaani, ddala, omulundi omulala, kubanga bwe batyo bwe beesiba emirembe n'emirembe mu kirayiro kyabwe ekikolimidde, era mu nnyanja y'omuliro ebirayiro byabwe birituukirizibwa emirembe n'emirembe, kubanga basibiddwa ku kubonerezebwa kwa Sitaani n'obusika emirembe n'emirembe.

Era bwe batyo beeteeseteese mu kiseera ekyabaweebwa era mu ngeri ey'okwekuuma okumala emyaka emirala mingi. Mazima ddala bakola eby'omuzizo byabwe omulundi omulala, era mu kyama beeteekateeka era balindirira okuggyibwawo kw'omuziyiza lwe baligezaako omulundi omulala okumenya ensalo ze nnabateerawo, kubanga bajeevu bulijjo. Era newankubadde nga basibiddwa okutuukiriza ebigendererwa bya El Shaddai, kubanga nze Katonda Omuyinza w'ebintu byonna, balinoonya okuddamu okuteekawo omulembe gwabwe ogwa zzaabu n'okutwala ensi bo bennyini, ekitali kyangu; kubanga ensi ya Mukama n'okujjula kwayo, era olupapula lw'obwannannyini olw'ensi luguliddwa omwana wange, Yeshua HaMashiach, era mu mikono gye eminywevu mw'ewereddwa ng'obusika obw'emirembe n'emirembe, era aligabana nabwo ddala n'abeesigwa, abayite, abalonde, n'abanunule.

Era bwe kityo ba Sitaani n'abalabi beeteeseteese, era balindirira n'amaanyi okuggyibwawo kw'omuziyiza, kubanga mu kuggyibwawo okwo obwakabaka bwabwe bumalayo okusumululwa, era ensi eriddamu okuba nga mu nnaku za Nuuwa. Laba! Ndiwa ekiragiro, era mbaleeta: abagagga bajja okutuukiriza obusungu bwange, nga basanyuka mu kiseera kye kimu era nga bavuma, era ndibakkiriza okusumululwa ku nsi omulundi omulala okutuukiriza ebigendererwa byange. Balimenya amangu okuva gye beekwese, era beekwese mu bibuga byabwe wansi w'ettaka, era beekwese wabweru w'omuzira ogulikyusibwa mu bbanga ttono, era mu nsi y'abantu balikkirizibwa okulumba, okunyaga, n'okuwamba, era Ogusota gulibakulembera kubanga bali kitundu kya bwakabaka bwakyo. Naye okumala akaseera katono, era ssabbiiti ya Danyeri ey'omusanvu, ssabbiiti ya myaka musanvu erisumululwa nga buli lunaku luba mwaka, era bwe kityo eriba myaka musanvu. Era birirya bingi, n'omutima gw'abantu gulibagwamu olw'amaaso gaabwe ag'ekittalo n'obunene bwabwe obw'amaanyi bw'ogeraageranya n'omuntu ow'obutonde.

Laba, NZE nze Nze Nze, eyatuma Ebigambo byange ng'abiyisa mu muweereza wange, era okuyita mu muweereza wange ebigambo byange bitumiddwa. Era bwe ntyo nkubuulira ebintu nga tebinabaawo abantu bange balyoke babeere beetegefu era bategeere ebigenda okujja, era babe n'amagezi ng'ebintu bino bigenda kubaawo mangu. Era bonna abakoowoola erinnya lya Yeshua HaMashiach, era Yesu Kristo mu lulimi Olungereza, baliwonyezebwa.

Era bwe kityo obusungu obunene n'omusango omunene nga bwe kyalagulwa edda kaakano biteekeddwawo okujja ku nsi yonna, era ebiseera bituuse, bw'atyo bw'ategeeza Katonda Omunene ow'Emirembe gyonna! Okumala emyaka gyonna egy'ekisa enjiri y'obulokozi n'ekyama ekinene ekya Katonda (ekyabikkulirwa Omutume Pawulo) kijjuzizza ensi era kituuse mu nsonda zonna ez'ensi. Era newankubadde nga bangi nnyo, bangi, bangi baawonyezebwa, era bonna abajja eri Yesu Omunazaaleeti mu kwenenya okutuukiridde, ne bamufuula Omwami waabwe ow'emirembe n'emirembe, ebiseera bituuse okutuukirizibwa kubanga abantu baddamu okugaana omutonzi waabwe. Era nze, El Shaddai, era Katonda Omuyinza w'ebintu byonna, nze eyaziyiza obubi obungi abasika b'obulokozi balyoke bajje mu bulamu obutaggwaawo mu Kristo Yesu, Omwami waabwe eyalinnyisibwa. Era kaakano ntegeeza ebintu bino nga tebinabaawo, kubanga nze Yakuwa, Nze NZE NZE! Kale bagambe nti NZE NZE NZE ategeeza n'obwegendereza nti ku kukuŋŋaana kw'abaana bange abeesigwa eri omwana wange, bwe banaasitulibwa okumusisinkana mu bbanga era ng'omuziyiza aggyiddwawo, obwakabaka bwa Sitaani bwonna bulisumululwa ku nsi era ensi erikyusibwa.

Ntegeeza mu musango ku abo abatuula ku nsi nti ndisumulula obwakabaka bwa Sitaani mu bujjuvu bwabwo, n'eby'omuzizo byabwe byonna birimansuka ku nsi yonna, n'emyoyo gyonna gye nnasiba mu bunnya mu nnaku za Nuuwa girisumululwa ku nsi, kubanga ebiseera by'okwawula okw'enkomerero n'omusango ogw'enkomerero bituuse ku nsi, n'olunaku lw'omusango omunene luteekeddwawo. Era bwe kirityo obwakabaka bwa Sitaani buliweebwa okufuga ensi, kubanga mu butuukirivu bwange, abantu beegombye nnyo okufugibwa ba Sitaani, era ne bakkiririza mu bulimba bwe nti ye katonda omulungi era omulung'amu eyaka, nti kaakano ndiwa abantu ekyo kye beegomba ennyo.

N'abo abaagwa basumuluddwa ddala okusalirwa omusango, era bakola eby'omuzizo byabwe omulundi omulala. Era basibiddwa ku nsi n'ogusota, kubanga bwe kityo bwe kiri ekiseera kyabwe, era balyefaananyiriza ng'abagwira, era ebitonde ebirungi ebiva mu nsi engwira. Naye nze, Yakuwa Katonda Omunene ow'Emirembe gyonna, ndayira erinnya lyange, kubanga siyinza kulayira eri muntu asinga, nti tewali nsi za bulimba ezitambulira mu bbanga eritagambika, wabula waggulu w'ensi waliwo eggulu, era eggulu ly'eggulu erisinga obukulu mu ggulu, Katonda Asinga Obukulu w'atudde mu bufuzi obw'emirembe n'emirembe ku ntebe ye ey'obwakabaka era tewali kiyinza kukwekebwa mu maaso gange.

Laba! Batandise dda okweyanjula, era ebintu byabwe n'ebitangaala byabwe biri kumpi, era ensi ekyuka mangu nnyo kaakano. Era ng'omuntu ow'obutonde bw'agaanye endagaano yange ey'emirembe, era omwana wange omu yekka, Yesu Omunazaaleeti, bwe ntyo kaakano nange ngaanye omuntu ow'obutonde era ndimuleka afugibwe ddala abalabe be, era beegomba okumuzikiriza. Naye, okugaana kwange tekutuukiridde so si kwa lubeerera, kubanga ekibiina kinene kirilokolebwa mu kubonaabona kwa Yakobo, era bonna abakoowoola erinnya lya Mukama, era Yesu Kristo, okutuusa ku nkomerero. Kubanga obulokozi mu kiseera ky'okubonaabona kwa Yakobo tebuyinza kufunibwa mu ngeri ndala yonna. Bwe kityo okwawula okw'enkomerero kulitandika, era bonna be nnalagira okuva mu bulamu obutaggwaawo nga n'emisingi gy'ensi tenateekebwawo balijja mu bulamu obutaggwaawo, era ndibazuukiza mu bulamu obuggya, era balifuna empeera nnene.

Kale wuliriza ebigambo byange era oteeke omutima gwo ku bigambo byange eby'okulabula n'okulangirira, kubanga ebiseera by'Abamawanga kaakano biggwaawo, n'obusungu n'omusango n'okubikkulirwa okunene kaakano biri ku nsi, kubanga nze, era El Shaddai, era Katonda Omuyinza w'ebintu byonna, nze atuula waggulu, era tewali kintu kikwekeddwa mu maaso gange, era kaakano nditambula okuwonya ddala bonna abasika b'obulokozi nga mbawula ku abo abalagiriddwa okuzikirizibwa okw'emirembe n'emirembe. Era bwe kityo eŋŋaano eriyawulibwa ku ngano emirembe n'emirembe, era bonna abali mu bujeemu bwa Sitaani baliweebwa omusango, era ebiseera by'omusango gwabwe biteekeddwawo okumansuka ku nsi yonna.

Wuliriza Ebigambo byange era otegeere obukakali bw'ebigenda okujja, kubanga olunaku lwa Mukama lunaku lwa kizikiza, era ani ayinza okuyimirirawo?

Nze, Yakuwa, Katonda Omunene ow'Emirembe gyonna, ntegeeza ebintu bino nga tebinabaawo, era tewakyali kiseera. Buli ayagala okujja, ajje anywe ku mazzi g'obulamu obutaggwaawo, n'okuyitibwa kwange okujja mu bulamu obutaggwaawo kukyayimiridde, era bonna abakoowoola erinnya lya Mukama okutuusa ku nkomerero baliwonyezebwa.

Nze, Yakuwa, Katonda Omunene ow'Emirembe gyonna, ntumye Ebigambo bino ng'abiyisa mu muweereza wange, era okuyita mu muweereza wange Ebigambo bino bitumiddwa!

Ebiseera bituuse!
