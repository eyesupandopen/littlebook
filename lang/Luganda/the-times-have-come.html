Okubwatuka Okw'okusatu n'Okw'okuna

Obudde Butuuse!

Okuva eri Yeshua HaMashiach:

Laba! Obudde obw'okwawukana busemberedde okukoma, n'okulabika kwange kunaatera okutuuka. Kuli kumpi nnyo, era ne bamalayika n'eggye ly'omu ggulu bali ku kkaala y'ekkondeere. Emikono gyange nagigololera ensi efiridde okumala emyaka mingi egy'emirembe, era bangi bakkirizza endagaano ey'emirembe Kitange, Yahweh, gye yatumira abantu, ye nze, Kristo Yesu, kubanga Nze mirembe gyammwe. Naye ate abasinga obungi bagaanyi ekirabo kyange n'ekisa kya Kitange n'okusaasira kwe okwayiibwa ku nsi.

Abo abateekebwako akabonero era abanunulibwa beebo abaalondebwa okuva ku ntandikwa y'ensi. Kubanga Kitange yabamanya okuva emirembe n'emirembe, era bulijjo mubadde mu mutima gwe, nga nange bwe mbadde mu mutima gwe, kubanga Nze, Yesu Omunazaaleeti, ndi mwana wa Yahweh omu yekka eyazaalibwa. Naye olw'emirimu gyange emituukiridde ng'Omwana gw'Endiga omutuukiridde ow'Okuyitako, n'olw'obuwulize bw'oyo omu yekka eyazaalibwa, abaana ba Katonda bangi nnyo bazaaliddwa omulundi ogw'okubiri okutuusa emirembe n'emirembe, nga baggyiddwa mu baana b'abantu, kubanga Nze ndi Mwana w'Omuntu!

Ebiseera bino eby'ekisa byali kyama ekikwekeddwa mu mutima gwa Yahweh kye yali tatabulirangako muntu okutuusa lwe yakimbikkulira, nange ne nkibikkulira abatume bange abatukuvu n'abannabbi, era n'okkuuma Ekigambo kyange ekitukuvu eri amatu n'emitima gy'abalonde n'abeesigwa. Kubanga ggwe, Omugole wange, oli kitundu ku kyama ekyo ekinene ekya Katonda, n'obulamu bwammwe (obukwekeddwa mu nze) bunaatera okubikkulibwa. Era nja kuwa empeera abeesigwa olw'emirimu gyabwe gyonna egy'obwesigwa gye bankoledde mu kukkiriza okungi, Nze, Yesu Kristo.

Era bwe ntyo ne ndokola ebibiina binene nnyo n'omusaayi gwange ogw'entango, kubanga omusaayi gwange gwali tegwaliiko kibi era n'okutuusa leero sirina kibi, kubanga sijja kusobya. Abo ababeera n'obulamu obw'obwesigwa mu Kristo bajja kufuna empeera nnene, era bajja kumasamasa ng'emmunyeenye ez'omu ggulu, n'ekitiibwa ekinene kiriba kyabwe, kubanga tebeegulumizanga ng'abakulu. Naye bagulumizza Nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, era nja kubaanjula eri Kitange nga tebalina bbala newaakubadde enkanyanya, era ebyambalo byabwe biriba byeru tteree era tebiriddayo kucaafuwazibwa. Era bonna baliba balamu so tebaliddayo kwonoona, oba mu butatya Katonda oba mu malala. Era baligulumizibwa mu Nze.

Kubanga Nze ndi musumba mukulu, era ndongoosezza ekisibo kyange mu malundiro amagimu okutuusa ebiseera by'enkomerero, ebinaatera okujja ku nsi, kubanga tewakyali musenyu gusigaddewo okuyita mu ggalagi. Era nja kuwa empeera bonna, okuva ku muto okutuuka ku mukulu, mu ngeri gye mutasobola kutegeera mu kiseera kino; naye okutegeera okwo kuli ku mulyango gwammwe, kubanga mujja kutwalibwa mu ggulu mangu nnyo. Era bwe kityo Omugole wange alitwalibwa mu kitiibwa, gye nja okusalira emisango emirimu gye n'okununula ddala buli musajja, mukazi, na mwana ow'enkomerero. Nja kubawa empeera nnyingi n'olw'ebintu ebitono ennyo ebikoleddwa mu kunweereza. Kubanga Katonda si mutakiranira, okwerabira omulimu gwammwe n'okutegana kw'okwagala, kwe mwalaga eri Erinnya lye, mu kuweereza kwammwe eri abatukuvu, era nga mukyalaweereza.

p>Tewali n'akatono ke weeraliikirira, ggwe Omugole wange, kubanga Katonda tasala misango nga omuntu bw'asala, kubanga omuntu si mwesigwa era musosoze mu bantu. Omuntu agulumiza abo be ye alowooza nti ba mugaso, n'omuntu ow'obutonde emirundi egisinga obungi anyooma obuwombeefu n'empisa ennungi n'asalawo okugulumiza ab'amalala n'abeetuttumuula. Kubanga omuntu ow'obutonde akaabirira okubalibwa ng'omutuukirivu mu maaso ga Katonda, naye omuntu ow'obutonde tasobola kuwangula kamere ye ey'ekibi era agaana okukkiriza nti ayonooneddwa, n'okutuusa n'okwegaana Mukama wa bakama, eyandimulokodde.

Kale amalala gakulembera okuzikirira, n'omwoyo ogw'ekiruyi gugwa. Mu kifo ky'okukkakkanya emitima gyabwe eri ebigambo byange n'amagezi n'okwagala bye njiyidde ku nsi, omuntu ow'obutonde asalawo okugoberera okwegomba kwe n'ennyonta ye, kubanga enkola z'ensi zategekebwa Sitaani okukema n'okukuma ennyonta ezo, era n'empeera z'obutali butuukirivu. Kale ebiseera by'Abamawanga binaatera okukoma, era kiseera kya nnaku ya Yakobo; kubanga nja kununula Isirayiri, era abalonde bange abeesigwa, abajja okujja gyendi nga benenyezza ebibi byabwe n'ebibi bya bajjajjaabwe abaagaana Nze. Era nga bwe kyawandiikibwa, bwe kityo kikolebwe.

Ggwe omuntu ow'obutonde, lwaki okyalonda ebibi byo n'onyooma ekisa kyange n'obulokozi ng'ekintu ekyangu? Yahweh newaakubadde Nze, Kristo Yesu, tetulina ssanyu lyonna mu misango egijja n'entiisa ey'amaanyi eneetera okusumululwa. Naye Katonda talonze kitundu kino kya ntegeka ye asobole okuba omukambwe eri ekitonde kye: kubanga Katonda kwagala, era Katonda musana, era mu ye temuli kizikiza kyonna. Okubonaabona okujja okw'amaanyi kutegekeddwa okwa abantu omukisa ogw'enkomerero nga tebunnaziba. Ekibiina ekinene kirirokolebwa mu kubonaabona, beebo abakutte erinnya lyange okutuusa ku mukka gwabwe ogw'enkomerero era nga tebaagala bulamu bwabwe okutuusa ku nkomerero.

Ggwe omuntu ow'obutonde, oli mukakanyavu nnyo, era towuliriza, era mu busirusiru ogaana ekisa kyange, era nkyasaba kye kimu: Kitange, basonyiwe, kubanga tebamanyi kye bakola. Ku abo abalisigala mu nnaku ennene, n'abo abagaanye ekirabo kyange eky'emirembe, bangi balizikirira, kubanga bagaanyi Mukama Yesu Kristo. Naye, bangi balirokolebwa, era bonna abalikoowoola erinnya lya Mukama balirokolebwa. Era n'olwekyo okugezesebwa kw'omuliro kunaatera okutandika, kubanga omusango gusemberedde.

Omusango omunene gujja ku bwakabaka bwa Sitaani! Naawe, omuntu ow'obutonde, ne mu butakkiriza bwo obw'obukakanyavu, ogaana n'ekisabibwa ekyangu ennyo. Kubanga mu biseera byange eby'ekisa, obulokozi bubadde bwa bwereere era bwangu, era ekyetaagisa kyokka kwali kwatula nti Yesu ye Mukama n'okukkiriza nti Katonda yanzuukiza mu bafu. Era kati wajja kubaawo enjala y'ebintu byonna ebirungi mu nsi, nga mw'otwalidde n'Ekigambo kyange Ekitukuvu. Ekiseera ekinene ennyo eky'okubonaabona kisemberedde, era Sitaani mu busirusiru yeetegekera okulwana, n'okulowooza mu kiruyi nti asobola okuwangula ye ne bamalayika be babe n'ensi yaabwe, ensi etaliimu Katonda, kubanga amakubo gaabwe makyamu. Mazima, ekyo ekyekyamizza kyokka tekisobola kugololwa, era tewali kusonyiyibwa newaakubadde emirembe eri Sitaani ne bamalayika be, newaakubadde ezzadde lyabwe.

Nze mwana wa Katonda eyajja mu mubiri.

Nze Mpologoma y'Ekika kya Yuda.

Nze ajja okufuga amawanga n'omuggo ogw'ekyuma.

Nze ajja okusumulula obubonero mangu nnyo.

Nze ajja okujja okutwala Omugole wange.

Nze ajja okukusisinkana mu bbanga.

Nze Ndi nga bwe Ndi, era ekkondeere linaatera okuvuga.

Nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, ntumye Ebigambo bino.

Okuva eri Yahweh, Katonda Omukulu ow'Emirembe n'Emirembe:

Kaakano gujja omusango gwa Katonda ow'emirembe n'emirembe, ye Yahweh, kubanga ntuma Omwana wange, era tewakyali kiseera kisigaddewo. Ggwe ataliiko mugaso eyalowooza nti osobola okuwangula Yahweh, ye Katonda Omukulu ow'emirembe n'emirembe! Ggwe ataliiko mugaso eyalowooza mu busirusiru nti osobola okuwangula Omwana wange omu yekka eyazaalibwa, Yeshua HaMashiach. Mwekuŋŋaanyirizza obusungu obw'emirembe n'emirembe, n'obusungu bwange bukambwe ku mmwe, kubanga ndi Katonda wa buggya. Kubanga okuva ku lubereberye mwaziyiza entegeka zange, era mweraliikiriza, ne mujjalabya, ne muyigganya, ne mubonyaabonya, era ne muzikiriza ekitonde kyange. Temulina mirembe.

Mumpulirize era mumpulirize bulungi, kubanga ndangirira ekigenda okubatuukako, era Yahweh ky'alangirira tewali asobola kukikyusa, newaakubadde omulundi gumu, newaakubadde emirembe gyonna. Era n'olwekyo omusango gwammwe gujja. Nja kutuma Omwana wange okukuŋŋaanya abalonde bange n'abeesigwa, era abaana ba Adamu abafuuse kati abaana ba Katonda. Muguddewo, ggwe omusota ogutaliiko mugaso, agwewulula mu bulimba n'obulombolombo. Kubanga temuli bakatonda, era temusobola kwenunula mu mukono gwange. Kubanga nja kusasula!

Nja kubaddiza buli mulimu omubi n'obulumi bwonna n'okubonaabona kwe mwaleetera abataliiko musango, era muliyaka emirembe gyonna mu nnimi z'omuliro ezisinga okwokya ez'ebbulu, n'omukka ogw'obutwa ogukaawa guliba mpewo yammwe, era mu kubonaabona okw'amaanyi gye mulibeera emirembe gyonna. Kubanga mwandeetera okuzikiriza ekitonde kyange, ye ensi nga bwe yali ngaAmataba ga Nuuwa tenajja, olw'ensigo yammwe etabula ey'okuzikiriza abantu; era n'olw'ebintu bino, nja kubazikiriza mu busika bwammwe obw'omuliro.

Era mwagezaako okuzikiriza ensi oluvannyuma lw'amataba ga Nuuwa, nga mukola nate emizizo gyammwe, naye tegigenda kuba na mirandira newaakubadde amatabi; kubanga omuddo gwonna omubi gulikunnulibwa, ne gusibwa, ne gusuulibwa mu muliro. Era bwe ntyo nja kuzikiriza ensi mu musango ogw'omuliro, kubanga nnasuubiza obutaddamu kujjuza nsi yonna na mataba. Naye mu muliro omusango gwange gujja, kubanga ndi muliro oguzikiriza, n'obusungu bwange buyitirira ku babi. Era n'olw'ebintu bino, nja kubazikiriza mu muliro ogusinga okwokya n'ekibiriiti ekiwunya.

Mumpulirize era mumpulirize bulungi, kubanga ndangirira ebigenda okubatuukako, na mmwe Abalinzi abatalina mugaso abaava mu kifo kyammwe ekyasooka. Temulina mirembe, era newaakubadde nga mbasumuludde okuva mu njegere zammwe okumala akaseera katono, nkikoze lwa kutuukiriza bigendererwa byange byokka. Oluvannyuma omusango omunene guli ku mmwe, era temulina mirembe. Kubanga mwandeetera okujjuza ensi n'amataba olw'emizizo gyammwe. Mmwe baani okuzannya obutonzi n'ekitonde kya Yahweh? Mmwe baani okwonoona olusuku lwa Katonda n'enkola zammwe embi? Mwazaala abaana bammwe ab'obwenzi n'abataliiko kkomo nga mwegatta n'abakazi, era nabo baligabana nammwe mu busika bwammwe obw'omuliro. Kuba ki kye nnagamba Malaki: Kubanga, laba, olunaku lujja oluliyaka ng'ekyokero; n'ab'amalala bonna, weewaawo, na bonna abakola ebibi, baliba bisasiro; n'olunaku olujja lulibookya, bw'ayogera Mukama ow'Eggye, ne lutabalekerawo mulandira newaakubadde ettabi. Laba olunaku olwo ddala lusemberedde, era mwenna mulisasulwa okusinziira ku makubo gammwe amabi.

Kubanga mpadde abasajja abakazi baleme okubaako kye betaaga, abakazi ab'okubeera nabo n'okubazaalira abaana. Naye mmwe mwakka ku Lusozi Kerumooni, ne muva mu kifo kyammwe eky'ekitiibwa ekyasooka, ne mwegatta n'abakazi, era temulina mirembe newaakubadde okuwummula. Nga bwe nnalagira Enoka omuwandiisi okubagamba mu kiseera ky'ekibi kyammwe: Temulina mirembe: omusango omukakali gufulumye ku mmwe okubateeka mu njegere: So tolijja kusaasirwa newaakubadde okusaba kwo tekukkirizibwe, olw'obutali butuukirivu bwe wayigiriza, n'olw'emirimu gyonna egy'obutatya Katonda n'obutali butuukirivu n'ekibi bye walaga abantu. Era mu njegere mwe muligenda nga musuuliddwa mu nnyanja y'omuliro.

Mwalumya ekitonde kyange olw'okwegomba kwammwe, nga mutwala n'amaanyi bangi be mwerondera okuba abakazi bammwe, era mulisasula. Mwegasse ku Basitaani, era abalangira abeekungaŋŋaanyizza okulwanyisa Nze, era mwalonze obulimba bw'abaserufi. Kyemuliva mwegatta nabo mu nnyanja ennene ey'emirembe n'emirembe ey'omuliro, era muliyaka emirembe gyonna, so temulifuna kuwummula. Mwalumya ekitonde kyange ne mutta bangi nnyo, era temulina kufaayo ku bulamu bwe nnatonda. Kyenja nneme okubafaako, era nze nabatonda, era kaakano gujja omusango gwammwe n'okuzikirizibwa kwammwe okw'enkomerero.

Ngamba Abasitaani, abalinzi, abakerubi abagwa, abaserufi abagwa, era bonna abeegasse ku bujeemu bwa Sitaani: muliyaka ddala, emirundi n'emirundi okutuusa emirembe n'emirembe; kubanga nja kubasiba ne mbakasukira mu muliro awalina envunyu etabula, era ennoni z'omuliro ezeetegekeddwa ku lwammwe mmwe abataliiko mugaso eziriyaka ennyo ku lwammwe, kubanga mugwanidde. Kubanga nja kubakola kyennyini kye mwakola abalala. Obulumi bwonna, ennaku, okwejjusa, okubonaabona, na buli kikolwa eky'omuzizo biribaddizibwa okumala emirembe gyonna, so temuliwonawo. Nja kubabika mu maaso g'amawanga ne mbakula ku lw'obulimba bwammwe, era baligamba bakatonda abato (abatali bakatonda): Naawe ofuuse munafu nga ffe? Ofuuse okufaanana naffe?

Nja kusasula ddala amakubo gammwe amabi ne ngazza mu bifuba byammwe, era mulisasula emirembe gyonna. Mulisuulibwa nga muli balamu mu nnimi z'omuliro, era muliyaka mu maaso g'Omwana gw'Endiga, ne bamalayika n'abatuukirivu bonna be mwayonoona balifuna okuva gyendi emirundi mingi okusinga bye mwabatwalako. Kubanga bwe mulisasulwa olw'obubi bwammwe, nja kuba nsasudde abalonde bange n'abeesigwa empeera nnene erisinga ekyo kye mwabatwala emirundi mingi n'okusinga ebikolwa byammwe ebitagasa.

Era bwe kiriba bwe kityo. Nja kutonda eggulu eriggya n'ensi empya omulibeera obutuukirivu, n'abaana bange balibeera nange, era nja kuteeka ennyumba yange wakati mu bantu, era bwe kiriba bwe kityo. Naye mmwe muliyaka mu makubo gammwe amabi, na buli kintu ekibi kye mwakola kiribaddizibwa emirembe n'emirembe. Era nja kuteekawo akabonero, era mulyerabirwa, so temulijja mu birowoozo. Era bonna balibeera mu mirembe, so mmwe temuliba na mirembe.

Laba, obudde buggwaawo, era ntuma Omwana wange okujja Omugole we, ye Yesu Kristo Omutuukirivu! Kubanga mu ye nsiimye nnyo! Na bonna b'alokodde n'abawonya mu makubo gammwe agatalina mugaso, n'abo be mwavunaana awatali nsonga, baliwummulira mu nze ne babeera n'emirembe emitukuvu, essanyu, n'okwagala. Kubanga nja kubafuula nga bo ne mbafuula ab'ekitiibwa okusinga bwe mwali, era temulina bwakabaka, kubanga temuli bakabaka.

Zisanze abali ku nsi, kubanga obusungu obungi buli ku mmwe. Kubanga mwagaana omutonzi wammwe era mwagala okufa. Kubanga okunzaagala kwe kwagala okufa, era mu kufa mulifa. Obusungu bwange bukambwe ku mmwe, era nja kuggyawo amasanyu gonna, essanyu lyonna, n'ebintu byammwe eby'obwetaavu tebirifunika. Kubanga bwe nnabakubisa enkuba y'ekisa n'okusaasira, mwasalawo okukozesa ekisa ng'omukisa okwegatta ku batalina mugaso, era obwakabaka bwa Sitaani, so nga mwandiwonye. Era kati bangi balizikirira, amannya gaabwe agaatawandiikibwa mu Kitabo ky'Obulamu okuva ku lubereberye. Omwana wange, Yesu Kristo, asemberedde, era alinyaga omugole we okumutwala mu ggulu, nange nja kuggyawo omuzibi. Laba! Ntuma abazira okutuukiriza obusungu bwange! Mwatwala ekisa kyange n'okusaasira kwange mu buwa, era mubeeredde mu bulamu obw'essanyu ery'ekibi, era nja kusasula.

Kubanga bwe mwandibadde n'emirembe emirembe n'emirembe, era obulamu obw'emirembe obujjudde essanyu n'essanyu, mwerondera okufa kwammwe, era nja kubawa kye mwagala. Mwogala ensi ey'obubi n'ekizikiza; nja kubawa kyennyini kye mwagala, era nja kubasasula n'olw'obulumi bwonna bwe mwaleetera abalala nga muli mu kibi ekinene. Nja kubaggyako essanyu lyonna, era mulirya obucaafu obw'amakubo gammwe. Omusango omunene guli ku nsi, era tewaliba kulwawo.

Laba! Bonna abalikoowoola erinnya lya Mukama balirokolebwa. Kubanga ekibiina ekinene kirirokolebwa mu kubonaabona okujja, era nja kubaaniriza gyendi. Kubanga kyawandiikibwa nti: Bano be bavudde mu kubonaabona okungi, ne boza ebyambalo byabwe, ne babyeruusa mu musaayi gw'Omwana gw'Endiga. Kyebava babeera mu maaso g'entebe ya Katonda, era bamuweereza emisana n'ekiro mu Yeekaalu ye: n'oyo atuula ku Ntebe alibeera mu bo. Tebaliddamu kulumwa njala, newaakubadde ennyonta, newaakubadde enjuba teribookya, newaakubadde ebbugumu lyonna. Kubanga Omwana gw'Endiga ali wakati mu ntebe, alibaliisa, n'abatwala ku nzizi z'amazzi amalamu: ne Katonda alisiimula amaziga gonna mu maaso gaabwe.

Mbasiima mwenna okukoowoola erinnya lya Yesu Kristo, Masiya ow'amazima, era omwana wa Katonda omu yekka eyazaalibwa, kubanga ajja. n'obusungu bw'Omwana gw'Endiga n'obusungu bwa Yahweh binaatera okusumululwa.

Nze Yahweh, Katonda omukulu ow'emirembe n'emirembe.

Nze El Shaddai, Katonda Omuyinza wa byonna.

Nze Jehovah Jireh.

Nze Elohim omukulu, Katonda wa Isirayiri.

Nze eyatuma omwana we omu yekka eyazaalibwa okununula abo abandyagadde okununnulibwa.

Nze eyazuukiza Kristo Yesu mu bafu n'amuwa erinnya erisinga amannya gonna, buli vviivi lifukamirire na buli lulimi lwatule.

NZE NDI nga NZE NDI, n'obusungu bwange bunaatera okutuukirizibwa, so tewali asinga, era njogedde.

Nze, Yahweh, Katonda omukulu ow'emirembe n'emirembe, ntumye ebigambo bye ntumye, era ntumye ebigambo bino. Ntumye omwana wange, Yeshua HaMashiach, naye atumye ebigambo bye eri abeesigwa. Obudde buggwaawo.
