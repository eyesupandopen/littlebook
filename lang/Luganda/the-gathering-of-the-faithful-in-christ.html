Novema 10, 2024

Okukuŋŋaana kw'Abeesigwa mu Kristo!

Laba, Laba! Tegeerera ekyo ekigenda okuteebwa ku nsi.

Laba, Laba! Mazima obusungu bw'Omwana gw'endiga bukutuuseeko ggwe Babulooni ow'ekyama. Era nga nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, bwe nnalabula, bwe kityo bwe kirikolebwa!

Laba! Laba! Olunaku lukutuuseeko, ggwe Babulooni ow'Ekyama, n'olunaku lwo olw'omusango lutuuse, ddala olunaku oluliteebwa olunaku olwo olukulu olwasuubizibwa edda, ddala olunaku lwa Mukama! Zikusanze ggwe atuula ku nsi kubanga obusungu obungi bugenda kuteebwa ku ggwe mu bujjuvu, so tolimalawo, era mazima ddala, sabiiti ya Danyeri ey'amakumi musanvu etuuse.

Noolwekyo mpa amagezi ekkanisa yange, omubiri gwange, era n'omugole wange okwesiba mu butuukirivu bwange n'okweteekateeka okuyimirira nga mugolokofu mu maaso ga Mukama wammwe, ddala mu kisenge eky'entebe ya Yakuwa, gye ndyanjulira ebibala ebisooka, ddala abo bonna be ndikuŋŋaanya abaafa nga bali mu Kristo era nga bawummudde balindirira okuyitibwa kwange, era n'abo bonna abali abalamu ku nsi abali mu Kristo mu bwesigwa mu kiseera ky'okulabika kwange. Era mu ngeri y'emu gye nneeyanjuliramu eri Yakuwa ng'ekibala ekisooka mu bafu, mu ngeri eyo nammwe mufaananyizibwa ne Mukama wammwe Ataggwaawo ow'Obutuukirivu. Kubanga mu ngeri y'emu gye nnatwalibwamu mu ggulu, nammwe bwe mutyo bwe mulitwalibwa mu ggulu, gye ndyanjulira ebibala byange ebisooka eby'amakungula nga tebirina bbala wadde enkunyinyi oba ekintu kyonna ekifaanana bwe kityo.

Era mwenna muliyimirira mu maaso g'entebe yange ey'emisango, ddala entebe eyo enkulu ey'emisango emirimu gyammwe gye girisalirwa omusango, era abamu balisasulwa, n'abamu balifiirwa, n'abamu balifuna byombiriri okufiirwa n'empeera ng'oluku, essubi, n'ebisambu biyokeddwa, kubanga tewali n'omu ku mmwe eyatambulira mu maaso gange mu butuukirivu, naye mu kwagala kwa Yakuwa mulisamaalirira nnyo obunene bw'obusika bwammwe. N'abawanguzi bange, empeera yammwe nnene era mulisikira ebintu byonna nga nze bwe nnakola bwe nnawangula abagwa. Kyokka, bonna abakuŋŋaanyizibwa eri Mukama waabwe Ataggwaawo, ddala Yeshua HaMashiach, balijja mu bulamu obuggya nga batondebwa buto okutuusa emirembe gyonna, so temulivunda newakubadde okukyuka okuva mu butuukirivu obubalindiridde, kubanga Yakuwa bw'atyo bwe yalangirira!

Era mulitwalibwa mangu ddala, n'ensi erimanya nti ndabise, n'omuziyiza aliggibwawo! Era mu kuba nga tewali muziyiza wange, amaanyi gonna n'obuyinza bw'ekibi biriteebwa mu bujjuvu ku nsi. Era nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, ddala Kristo Yesu, alanga Ebigambo bino, era bwe kityo Ebigambo bino birimanyibwa ku nsi, era birizuula amaaso, amatu, n'emitima gy'abo be bitumiddwa.

Kale bwe kityo, era bwe kiriba bwe kityo, naye okumala akaseera akatono ennyo obwakabaka bw'Omuzira buligolokokera mu bujjuvu, kubanga bwe kityo bwe buweereddwa okutuukiriza. Kubanga bwe nnagaanibwa ab'oluganda lwange, ddala Isirayiri gwe ndi omununuzi waabwe ow'oluganda, nnakyukira amawanga amawanga galyoke gaweebwe omukisa, ddala nga Yakuwa bwe yasuubiza edda. N'Ebigambo byange ne bikkirizibwa n'amaanyi, ne mu kuyigganyizibwa okungi n'okugezesebwa kungi, n'emiryango gya magombe tegyawangula Kkanisa yange. Naye, era bwe kityo, Ekkanisa yange bw'eriggibwawo, n'omuziyiza wange n'aggibwawo, ekibi kiriranda mu nsi yonna, so tebalimalawo.

Ddala bonna abasanyukira obutali butuukirivu era abakkiriza okugaana Yesu Omunazaaleesi ne balondawo Balaba, era bwe baligamba nti mirembe na kutebenkera, olwo okuzikirira okw'amangu ddala, so tebalimalawo. Naye Omuzira alyerula, era asibiddwa ku nsi, era okuva mu ggulu agobeddwa emirembe gyonna nga taliddayo, era alina obusungu bungi kubanga amanyi nti obudde bwe butono, naye mu busirusiru alowooza nti asobola okumpakanya nze Yeshua HaMashiach, wadde nga nnamuwangula ddala mu bujjuvu ku Gologoosa mu mirimu gyange egy'okutangirira egy'obulokozi egyaggweerera ku Kiggwaayo!

N'abagwa balowooza nti basobola okwewala omusango gwabwe ogwasuubizibwa edda kubanga batya ebiseera byabwe eby'omu maaso okusinga ekintu kyonna; kubanga bategeera nti mu bukambwe bwabwe bwe baalaga era bwe balaga tebalina kusaasira. Kubanga bamanyi nti tebaalaga kusaasira, n'olwekyo, tewali kusaasira kulibalagibwa. Bamanyi bulungi nti ebikolwa byabwe byonna ebitasaana era eby'omuzizo biribaddizibwa emirembe gyonna emirundi mingi mu nnyanja eyo ennene ey'omuliro ogwaka n'ekibiriiti, ennyiriri z'okubonerezebwa gye zitasobola kuzikizibwa. Ddala ennyiriri ezaatondebwa ezirisasula buli kikolwa ekibi kye baakola mu bifuba byabwe, era balisasula mu bujjuvu era balibeera mu nnaku ez'olubeerera.

Kubanga baalumba Yakuwa ne balumba olusuku lwa Katonda, ddala nga befuula bakatonda n'omusango gwabwe gutuuse. Naye bwe kityo, abagwa beebasizza enkuyanja n'ebikozesebwa byabwe ebya tekinologiya, n'amayengo, n'essanyu ly'ekibi, n'empeera y'obutali butuukirivu. Naye, bwe kityo abantu baagala ekizikiza okusinga omusana, kale kaakano, ndibawa ekizikiza kye beegomba ennyo.

Kubanga essanyu lyonna ligenda kuggibwa ku nsi, n'obulamu obw'ekibi n'obugagga obusukkiridde tebulidibwa kulabika ku nsi mu ngeri eyo, abantu gye beekuŋŋaanyiriza eby'obugagga ne beerabira bannaabwe mu mululu gwabwe n'obutali butuukirivu, nga banyoomoola abali mu bwetaavu nga babalinnyako nga bagenda okwekuŋŋaanyiriza obugagga obusingawo. Omuntu ow'obutonde yeenywezezza nnyo mu ssanyu ly'ekibi n'okutuusa n'olugalo lwe tasobola kuluyimusa kuyamba abali mu bwetaavu, n'olwekyo nange siriyimusaawo lugalo lwange kumuyamba kuwona musango gwe kubanga omuntu ow'obutonde kaakano aweereddwayo mu mikono gy'abalabe be. Era bwe kiriba bwe kityo nti ebintu bino tebirimalawo sabiiti ya Danyeri ey'amakumi musanvu era tebiriddayo kubaawo mu ngeri eyo mu bwakabaka bwange obw'obutuukirivu!

Naye bwe kiriba bwe kityo nti omuziyiza bw'aliggibwawo, ekibi ekiri mu bantu olw'obukujjukujju bw'omusota kaakano tekiriba na muziyiza, n'edalu liriranda ku nsi, ow'oluganda bw'alitta ow'oluganda, n'ab'eggwanga limu balirwana ne bannaabwe, n'akavuyo akasukkiridde n'olutalo biriranda ku nsi. Naye ekikolwa kino kye kuzikirizibwa n'omusango gwa Babulooni ow'ekyama, n'obubonero buteekeddwateekeddwa okusumululwa, n'abeebagadde embalaasi balyebagala!

N'olwekyo okuwaba okunene kubaawo nga bwe kyalagulwa. Kubanga mu kiseera ky'okulabika kwange, abangi bwe bandibadde balindirira nga beeyonja mu Kigambo kyange n'omwoyo, era mu kifo ky'okutuukiriza obulokozi bwabwe mu mikono gyange egy'okwagala, bo ne beerabira Mukama waabwe, ddala Kristo Yesu, ne balondawo Balaba gwe baawa emitima gyabwe. Era nga Pontiyo Piraato, eyali amaliridde okunteebwa okuva mu bulimba bwabwe obw'obufaliisayo obutaliiko nsonga n'okuvunaanibwa, n'awa abantu b'ensi eno omukisa gw'okulonda. N'olwekyo Piraato n'abagamba bwe baali bakuŋŋaanidde wamu nti, Mwagala mbateere ani, Balaba, oba Yesu ayitibwa Kristo? Ne bagamba nti, Balaba. Piraato n'abagamba nti, Kale Yesu ayitibwa Kristo, mmukole ntya? Bonna ne bamugamba nti, Akomererwe.

Era kino kyali kityo kubanga Esawu yakozesa obuyinza bwe obubi ku bantu balyoke beetwalire omussi mu kifo ky'oyo eyatumibwa olw'obulokozi bwabwe, ddala Yesu Omunazaaleesi! Naye ekibonerezo ky'amateeka ga Musa kyandituukiriziddwa kitya okuggyako nga nze nneewaayo mu nnaku enkambwe ng'omwana gw'endiga gwa Katonda, ddala gye nneeweerayo olw'ebibi by'ensi nga kabona asinga obukulu emirembe gyonna ng'engeri ya Merukizeddeeki bwe yali. Era bwe kityo, mu kumanya kwa Yakuwa okw'edda, yamanya mu bujjuvu nti Isirayiri yali agenda kugaana Masiya waabwe ow'amazima n'olwekyo n'akozesa obukyayi bw'omugwa n'obuggya ku bo, kubanga singa baali bamanyi ekyama ekinene ekya Katonda tebandikomereredde Mukama wammwe ow'Ekitiibwa Ataggwaawo!

Naye bwe kityo, Merukizeddeeki Kabaka w'e Salemu n'aleeta omugaati n'omwenge: era yali Kabona wa Katonda Ali Waggulu Ennyo. N'olwekyo Aburamu n'awaayo ebiweebwayo bye nga bwe kyali kyetaagisa okuweesa Yakuwa ekitiibwa, era bwe kityo Merukizeddeeki ye ngeri ey'okukkiriza eyali ekisiikirize ky'ebintu ebyajja. Kubanga nga bwe kyawandiikibwa ku Ibulayimu omwesigwa eyabeerangawo olw'okukkiriza mu Katonda Ali Waggulu Ennyo era okuva ku kyokulabirako kye omutuukirivu alibeerawo olw'okukkiriza emirembe gyonna! Era bwe kityo ab'amawanga baaweebwa omukisa ne bayingizibwa mu bika mu Isirayiri wa Katonda, era benyigira mu bisuubizo ebyakolebwa eri Bakadde mu bujjuvu kubanga baayanirizibwa Yakuwa yennyini ddala n'omukono gwange ogw'amaanyi, kale Katonda ky'alonze ggwe Isirayiri, tokiyita kya bulijjo oba ekitali kirongoofu!

Naye bwe kityo olunaku lwange lutuuse. Era sirigabana kitiibwa kyange na mulala, era Katonda asindise okubuza okw'amaanyi balyoke bakkirize obulimba. Kubanga abo abagoberera Balaba bakikola lwa kwegomba kwabwe kubanga beefuula nti bali mu kiseera kye eky'omulembe gwa zaabu ekyasuubizibwa ekiri ddala nga tekyabateekerwateekerwa, wadde ng'alimbye. Kubanga omulembe gwa zaabu gw'asuubiza gusinziira ku mulembe gwa zaabu ogw'abagwa mu nnaku za Nuuwa, abagwa we baafugira ensi mu butali butuukirivu! N'olwekyo, Balaba bw'ayogera ku mulembe gwa zaabu, ayogera mazima ku kigendererwa ky'emitima gyabwe, wabula nga banoonya okuzikiriza Adamu mu bujjuvu, emizizo gyabwe n'omuddo omubi gulyoke gwande mu nnimiro ya Katonda emirembe gyonna. Era bwe kityo, kaakano otegedde lwaki tewandibaddewo muntu alokoka nga mulamu singa Yakuwa teyakendeeza nnaku.

Naye tewali mulembe gwa zaabu gwe baliba nagwo kubanga nze ndi muzzukulu eyasuubizibwa ow'omukazi alibetenta ddala omutwe gw'omusota emirembe gyonna era ndibetenta mu bujjuvu obwakabaka bwe bwonna so tebuliyimirirawo. Kale mu kifo ky'omulembe gwabwe ogwa zaabu gwe beegomba, balifuna omusango omukakali n'obwakabaka bwabwe bulizikirizibwa mu maaso gaabwe ne butaddayo kugoloka, n'obubonero bulisumululwa, n'amakondeere galifuuyibwa, n'abantu bange abasigaddewo balikusibwa, n'olwo endeku ez'obusungu bwa Katonda obujjuvu ne ziyiibwa, era balibeera mu kubonaabona n'ennaku ennyingi, nga balaba okuwangulwa kwabwe nga kusembera mu bujjuvu.

N'olwekyo, abo abagoberera Balaba mu busirusiru, nga yeefuula nti atumiddwa Yakuwa, ng'amanyi bulungi nti ava eri omuzira, era ng'alondeddwa okukulembera ensi n'ensi yonna n'ensolo eva mu nnyanja. Naye bwe kityo, bangi abawabyemu ne bagwa mu mikono gy'omulimba ono ne beerabira Kristo Yesu kaakano bagenda kuweebwayo mu mikono gye ng'abeesigwa batwaliddwa. Era waliwo abandyavunaanye nti nsuuliridde Ekkanisa yange, so ng'ate abo abatali mu Kristo, abatatuuka ku bulokozi obujjuvu, be bansuuliridde nga beegatta ku Balaba, era nnabalabula nti basaanidde okubeera mu muzabbibu kubanga awatali nze, tewali kye muyinza kukola.

Wadde nga bangi balizikirira abamannya gaabwe gaatawandiikibwa mu Kitabo ky'Obulamu eky'Omwana gw'endiga era balizikirira okuva ku nsi omusango gwabwe bwe gulituuka, era amangu ddala essuubi lyabwe lirisaanyizibwawo mu kaseera katono.

Kubanga tebaalina ssuubi lya butuukirivu!

Kubanga tebaalina ssuubi lya kutuukirizibwa!

Kubanga tebaalina ssuubi lya kutukuzibwa!

Kubanga baafiirwa essuubi lyonna erya Mukama waabwe okubakomyawo nga bwe nnasuubiza mu Ndagaano Yange Empya era buli kisuubizo kya Yakuwa ne Yeshua HaMashiach kirituukirizibwa emirembe gyonna n'emirembe gyonna!

Kubanga ekiseera kyange eky'ekisa n'okusaasira kikomekkerezeddwa, wadde ng'ekisa n'okusaasira biri nange emirembe gyonna, era nga bwe kyawandiikibwa bwe kirikolebwa nti Yakuwa ne Yeshua HaMashiach balisaasira oyo gwe tulisaasira kubanga ani ayinza okuyimirira okuziyiza entebe ya Yakuwa kubanga ye Katonda Ali Waggulu Ennyo era tewali alinga ye! Kale tegeera nti obusungu buteekeddwateekeddwa era bugenda kuteebwa ku nsi nga n'obulumi bwange obw'okuzaala bwe bulangiridde era bwe bulagudde, era olunaku olwo lutuuse!

N'olwekyo ndangirira okulabika kwange, era kiribaawo nga bwe kyawandiikibwa mu byawandiikibwa n'omugole wange alibeera mu ndagaano n'omugole we emirembe gyonna n'abo bonna abalitwalibwa mu ggulu mu kiseera ky'okulabika kwange, k'abe nga mulamu oba mufu, alitondebwa buto emirembe gyonna mu bulamu obuggya obw'olubeerera. N'abo bonna abeeze mu ddala mu bigambo ebirungi n'emboozi ennungi eza Balaba baligwa okuva mu kukuŋŋaana kwange nga n'amakoola agawotose mu kibi. Naye, bangi ku abo abaligwa balijja gye ndi nga bayita mu kugezesebwa okungi n'okubonaabona, era bangi ku bo balikoowoola erinnya lyange okutuusa ku nkomerero n'ekibiina ekinene kiriva mu sabiiti ya Danyeri ey'amakumi asatu okuyingira mu bulamu obutaggwaawo, wadde ng'abasinga obungi balitwala akabonero k'ensolo ne bagwa mu nniriri emirembe gyonna.

N'olwekyo, nze, Kristo Yesu ddala Mukama wammwe eyazuukira n'alinnya, ndagira abeesigwa bange okufuluma mu bujjuvu mu bo n'okwawukana nabo mu bujjuvu. Mwetegeke abaagalwa bange! Temwetaba mu kwekalakaasa kwabwe, mwewale ebibiina byabwe, era ku buli nsimbi mwewale ekibi ekiri mu kwekuŋŋaanya wansi w'omwoyo ogw'obulimba ogw'emirembe kubanga okulumba kwabwe kuli ku mbalama ddala. Mutegeere abaagalwa bange nti mwandibadde mutambulira mu katego kaabwe singa mwandibagattiddeko mu ngeri eyo. Baagalwa bange, ekiseera kituuse okuva ku by'ensi eno, kubanga obulamu bwammwe bukwekeddwa mu nze, Kristo Yesu, era bugenda kubikkulibwa mu bujjuvu mulyoke mumanye nga nammwe bwe mumanyiddwa.

Kale mutegeere obulimba obuli ku mmwe abaagalwa bange mwekweke mulindirire okulabika kwange, kubanga kuli kumpi! Mutegeere obulimba bwabwe, nti omwana gw'endiga alina amayembe abiri aliggya ekiwundu kye eky'oku mutwe, era alirabika ng'azuukidde mu bafu era ng'awonye ekiwundu kye eky'oku mutwe naye byonna by'awaayo ng'okukakasa ebigambo bye eby'obulimba bye bubonero eby'obulimba n'ebyamagero so si Yakuwa Omukulu NZE NZE, wadde nze, Yeshua HaMashiach eyatuma omulimba ono, wadde nga akkiriziddwa okujja mu kifo kye mu kiseera kye ekigereke alyoke afuge n'ensolo eva mu nnyanja n'omuzira ng'omutwe gwabwe kubanga bwe kityo bwe baweereddwa okutuukiriza mu nteekateeka ya Yakuwa ey'olubeerera ey'okununula Adamu!

N'olwekyo mbagamba abaagalwa bange okulindirira n'obugumiikiriza so temukkiriza bulimba bwabwe kubanga tewali muntu wadde ekintu ekiyinza okuziyiza okulabika kwange eri abeesigwa mu Kkanisa yange, wadde nga balinoonya okubikka ku kikolwa kino n'okulumba okw'obulimba okw'abagwira n'olutalo lw'ensi yonna olulisukkuluma entalo zonna ezizze zibaawo ku nsi. Naye, olutalo lwabwe luliba lutono nnyo okugeraageranya n'olutalo lw'olunaku olwo olukulu lwe balizikirizibwa ddala bwe ndijja ku nsi n'eggye ly'omu ggulu, kubanga mu butuukirivu nzijja kusala musango na kulwana!

Nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, ntumye Ebigambo bino ng'omuweereza wange omwesigwa, era nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, akakasa obujulizi buno obuweereddwa mu Njiri Etaggwaawo ebikkuddwa mu kiseera kyayo.

Nze oyo agenda kaakano okukyukira Isirayiri okubaanunula okuva ku nsi ng'omununuzi waabwe ow'oluganda era nkuyita ggwe Isirayiri kubanga ekiseera kituuse okukkiriza Masiya wo ow'amazima, kubanga mu ngeri y'emu omulimba ono gy'alireetera okulyamu olukwe n'okuzikiriza Babulooni ow'ekyama, mu ngeri y'emu alireetera okulyamu olukwe abantu b'ensi eno mu muzizo gwe ogw'okuzikiriza ogusembera ddala.

Kale tegeera nti baliyeekulula ne bagenda mu bifo byabwe eby'okwekweka mu mayinja g'ensi era bangi balifiira omwo. N'olwekyo mbuuza, wa omuntu w'ayinza okwekweka okuwona okutunuulira kwange okw'olubeerera, oba ani ayinza okuwona amaaso ga Yakuwa kubanga byonna birabibwa era bimanyiddwa era kyonna ekibaawo ku nsi kikkirizibwa okubaawo oba mu kusala omusango oba olw'obulokozi bw'abo abalikoowoola erinnya lyange okutuusa ku nkomerero oba abo abanzikiriza nga Masiya waabwe, n'abo bonna abakola ekibi balisasulwa mu bujjuvu.

Era nze oyo alirabikira amangu ddala mu bbanga mu kitiibwa ekingi era balimanya era bangi balitegeera nti basubwa ekikolwa kino kimu kye baali bakakasa nti bali kitundu kyako, naye bagaaniddwa olw'obulamu bwabwe obw'ekibi kubanga ekibi tekiriyingira mu ggulu wadde okuyimirira mu maaso gange.

Laba! Ebiseera bituuse era kaakano nditandika okukuŋŋaanya abeesigwa.

Tunula! Laba! Wuliriza! Mwetegefu abaagalwa bange? Olunaku lubatuuseeko.
