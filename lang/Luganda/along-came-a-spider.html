Abajulirwa Ababiri: Isirayiri
Okukaawa n'Okuwooma
Novemba 25, 2024

Nnabbubi N'ajja

Muwulire, muwulire, Ebigambo byange ggwe Isirayiri era mukotamye emitima gyammwe mu buwulize eri eddoboozi lyange era mwanirize mu bukkakkamu Ebigambo bya Yeshua HaMashiach, nti olw'Ebigambo by'Enjiri etaggwaawo mufune obulokozi era mutuuke ku bulamu obutaggwaawo mu Masiya wammwe ow'amazima ddala abalwanirira mu kwagala. N'olwekyo njija okubalabula, na ddala abo ab'omu nnyumba ya Yakobo n'abo bonna abasoma era abakkiriza ebigambo by'Enjiri etaggwaawo mu kukkiriza okulokola, balyoke babe n'okukkiriza mu Yeshua HaMashiach ye Yesu Kristo, era balyoke babe n'okukkiriza mu Yahweh Omunene NZE NZE, oyo Enjiri etaggwaawo gye yasigala ng'ekwekeddwa mu mutima gwe okutuusa ebiseera byayo eby'okubikkulirwa; era bwatyo Yokaana Omubikkulizi n'asiba amawulire g'okubwatuka omusanvu ge yawulira. N'olwekyo njija okubalabula ku nnabbubi eyaluka olutimba lwayo era n'ekwasa ensi mu kufumbatira kwayo; nga bw'erya ku musaayi gw'abalala, era muleme okugwa mu matimba gaayo amangi muleme okukwatibwa mu kufumbatira kwayo okutta.

N'olwekyo, mukotamye emitima gyammwe mu buwulize era muwulire ebigambo bya Masiya wammwe ow'amazima, ye Yeshua HaMashiach! Kubanga mazima njija okwongera amagezi ku mugezigezi ali mu mmwe, mulyoke mutegeere ebiseera bye mulimu era mulyoke mutegeere abalabe bammwe n'embeera yaabwe n'amakubo gaabwe ag'obulimba; kubanga batambulira mu kyama nnyo n'obukumpanya era balwana na bukuusa. Kubanga bonna befuula be batali, ne nnabbubi etambulira mu kyama nnyo n'obukumpanya okutuusa omuyiggo gwa nnabbubi lwe gugwa mu lutimba lwayo, olutimba nnabbubi lw'akozesa bulijjo mu bigendererwa byayo ebibi.

N'olwekyo, njija okubalabula ku nnabbubi n'okubalaga amakubo gaayo mulyoke mwewale obusagwa bwayo era mwekuume obutagwa mu kufumbatira kwayo okulemaza era okutta. Naye nnabbubi ki eyali yeefudde obwakabaka n'eggwanga? Era nnabbubi ki eyali yeegasse ku lukiiko lw'abaagwa, era n'ekola omulimu gwayo n'essanyu mu bubi obujeemu? Era nnabbubi ki eyali yeefudde abo abakwatiddwa mu lutimba lwayo? Era y'etyo nnabbubi gye njija okubalabulako era mazima ddala, nnabbubi kitundu kimu ku bungi bw'ebitundu by'obwakabaka bw'abalabe era ddala ye nnabbubi ey'obusagwa obw'obutwa.

Kubanga nnabbubi eluka olutimba lwayo mu bukumpanya n'obukuusa. Nnabbubi eluka olutimba lw'obukuusa n'olutimba lw'amabanja, era olutimba lwayo kati luwombese ensi yonna mu kufumbatira kwayo okw'ekyama. Lowooza ku nnabbubi mu butonde bwayo, eluka olutimba lwayo mu bukumpanya mu bifo weesobolera okukwata omuyiggo gwayo, omuyiggo gwayo nga gugwamu terumanyi. Era omuyiggo gwayo bwe gutawaana okwefunza mu lutimba mwe gukwatiddwa nga tegumanyi, ne gweyongera okwesiba, nnabbubi n'etabaaza n'egufumita obusagwa bwayo nga bw'eguwomba mu lutimba lwayo. Oluvannyuma nnabbubi ne yeekuumira omuyiggo gwayo olw'ebigendererwa byayo, n'egulya mpola okutuusa lwe liggwaawo, n'ereka omuyiggo gwayo nga guzikiridde ddala okutuusa lwe guba nga tegukyaliwo.

Naye ago ge makubo ga nnabbubi ow'omu butonde eyatondebwa olw'ebigendererwa bya Yahweh, wabula ani ku mmwe eyali alabye nnabbubi ekolagana n'obwakabaka n'eggwanga lya bannabbubi abalala; naye abaagwa beegasse ne bannabbubi baabwe mu nnaku zino ez'enkomerero. N'olwekyo njija okubalabula ku ggwanga eriva ku Yafeesi, eyazaala Gomeri, ne ku Gomeri ne kuva Asukenaazi, ne ku Asukenaazi ne kuva eggwanga kubanga Asukenaazi ali mu lukalala lw'amawanga mu ntegeka ez'edda, na ddala mu nnaku abantu lwe baali wansi w'endagaano ya Nuuwa, endagaano abantu gye baasuula; naye waaliwo Aburamu omwesigwa eyanoonya amakubo ga Yahweh ensi bwe yajeemera nate Omutonzi waayo. Naye newankubadde, omuntu ow'obutonde yagaana endagaano ya Yahweh; Yahweh mwesigwa eri ebisuubizo bye kyenkanyi. So tekuli kisuubizo na kimu eky'endagaano Yahweh gye yakola ne Nuuwa (eyali omusajja omutuukirivu mu maaso ga Yahweh) ekyagwa wansi nga tekibadde kituukiridde era byonna biriba yee ne amiina emirembe gyonna kubanga Yahweh bulijjo mwesigwa mu bujjuvu!

Ensi bwe yamala okusaanawo n'amataba mu nnaku za Nuuwa, ensi n'egabanyizibwa n'akalulu k'obusika mu batabani ba Nuuwa mu nnaku za Peregi, n'olwekyo Yafeesi n'agenda mu busika bwe mu nsi ez'obukiika kkono. N'abantu baali ba lulimi lumu, n'abaana ba Kamu ne bakuŋŋaanira mu nsi eyafuuka Babulooni. N'abalangira ba Nimuloodi ne bateesa wamu ne Puti, ne Mizulayimu, ne Kuusi, ne Kanaani n'amaka gaabwe okuzimba ekibuga kyabwe ne munda waakyo omuzimbo omuwanvu, na ddala omuzimbo omuwanvu ennyo balyoke basobole okulwanyisa eggulu era beekuŋŋaanye beefunire erinnya mu kujeemera ekiragiro kya Yahweh eky'okuzaala n'okwabya balyoke baddemu okujjuza ensi. Naye ago ge makubo g'omuntu ow'obutonde atalina kukkiriza, mu kwegomba kwe n'amalala aweereza ekibi era n'olwekyo aweereza Kitaawe w'ekibi; ye Setaani, okuyita mu butawulize bw'abantu eri Yahweh olw'ekibi ekiri mu mubiri gw'omuntu ow'obutonde.

Era okuva mu zzadde lya Nimuloodi eryakula mu bubi, kigambibwa nti ku mubi kuva bubi bungi. Era bwatyo Nimuloodi n'ayonoona ensi mu makubo g'abaagwa era okuva mu balogo be n'abafumu be ne muva amasomero g'ekyama ag'edda, abaagwa abalogo mwe baayigiririza ebyama byabwe ebitagasa abo abalisuulibwa mu muliro emirembe gyonna mu bibi bya Basetaani, era balyegatta nabo mu busika bwabwe obutaggwaawo. Ne Nimuloodi n'agolokoka mu bwakabaka obwasooka oluvannyuma lw'amataba ga Nuuwa, na ddala Nimuloodi we yafugira ensi mu butali butuukirivu; na ddala okufuga abaagwa kwe beegomba okuba nakwo mu bujjuvu ku nsi, naye tebaliba na kiseera kya zaabu wabula omusango omukakali.

Awo Yahweh mu musango gwe n'aleetera ensi okwasamuka n'emira ekitundu kimu kya kusatu eky'omuzimbo gwabwe, n'omuliro ne guva mu ggulu ne gwokya ekitundu ekirala kimu kya kusatu eky'omuzimbo, n'ekitundu kimu kya kusatu ne kikkirizibwa okusigalawo ng'ekijjukizo okubalumiriza. N'olwekyo erinnya lyakyo liyitibwa Babbeli, kubanga awo Yahweh we yataataganyiza olulimi lw'ensi yonna: era okuva awo Yahweh n'abasaasaanyiza ku maaso g'ensi yonna. Era amawanga mangi n'abantu baagaana ne beerabira Yahweh eyakola endagaano ne Nuuwa, n'olwekyo ne beerabira endagaano Nuuwa gye yakola ne Yahweh okusinziira ku busika bwabwe n'okusinziira ku nnimi ze baayogera ne beerabira endagaano ya Yahweh ne Nuuwa, ne bakyukira amakubo g'abaagwa kubanga tewali kulondawo kulala. (Naye Aburamu n'afuuka Ibulayimu era abatuukirivu baliba balamu olw'okukkiriza!)

E Babulooni Nimuloodi yakolerayo obubi bungi na ddala bwe yakola ebifaananyi bingi ebisinzibwa n'eyeeyita katonda n'akola obusatu bwe obw'omuvumo, na ddala abasatu abatali batukuvu, nga kigendereddwa okuduulira obwakatonda obw'amazima n'ezzadde erisuubizibwa ery'omukazi n'okubuza abaana b'abantu mu butali butuukirivu. Era bwatyo Nimuloodi n'ateekawo amasomero ge agayitibwa ag'ekyama, okumanya kw'abaagwa bwe kwaddamu okusaasaana n'okubuna ensi yonna n'abantu ne baddamu okuwaanyisa amazima ga Yahweh n'obulimba ne basinza ne baweereza ekitonde okusinga Omutonzi atenderezebwa emirembe gyonna, Amiina!

Era abantu bwe baasaasaanyizibwa okuva e Babulooni n'okumanya n'amakubo amabi ag'abaagwa nabyo ne bisaasaana nabo, na ddala n'emikolo gyabwe gyonna egy'omuzizo era egy'omuvumo, egy'abo abalya ennyama n'omusaayi n'abo abanywa omusaayi gwa Adamu omuli obulamu bw'omubiri; na ddala emikolo egyonooneddwa ennyo era egy'omuzizo Yahweh gy'ayawula omulundi gumu, byonna, n'emirembe gyonna kubanga Adamu yakolebwa mu kifaananyi kya Yahweh n'obulamu bw'omubiri buli mu musaayi. Naye beeyongera mu makubo gaabwe ag'omuzizo era ag'omuvumo okutuusa leero, n'emikolo gyabwe n'empisa zaabwe byakuumibwa mu abo abasinza dayimooni era abavunnamira ebifaananyi ebisiru era gano ge makubo ga nnabbubi, naye nnabbubi yeefuula Yakobo omutuukirivu so nga talina mugabo wadde ekitundu mu ye.

Era bwatyo amakubo g'obubi ne ganywerera mu makubo g'abantu ab'amawanga nate. Ne ku Gomeri ne kuva Asukenaazi ne ku Asukenaazi mu biro ebyaddirira ne kuva eggwanga eddene ery'abantu abaafuuka abatasiima-wamu abaabeeranga era abaazereranga mu ttale lya Yuro-Eziya n'ebitundu ebiriranyeewo na ddala we baateeka obwakabaka bwabwe. Na ddala obwakabaka bwabwe obwakula mu butali butuukirivu ne bugwa eri abalabe baabwe kyenkanyi. Na ddala abalabe baabwe be baabalumba olw'obutali butuukirivu bwabwe era mu kifo ky'okukkiriza Yesu Omunazaaleeti n'okuva mu bubi, bo ne befuula Yakobo, so mu Yakobo temwali kika kya Asukenaazi ekya Yafeesi. Bw'atyo ne bagaana Yesu Omunazaaleeti kubanga ba kitaabwe Setaani era okwegomba kwe be basibiddwa okukola emirembe gyonna.

Era ku bantu ba Asukenaazi ab'amakubo amabi n'ag'obukuusa ne kuva eggwanga, wabula tebaali mu lujja lwa Adamu lulamba, naye baali nnamutta mbi eyasigibwa mu ŋŋaano ya Yahweh abeeyonoonye emirembe gyonna mu butali butuukirivu. Na ddala abo abazaalibwa kitaabwe Setaani kubanga ensigo ya Setaani ekyaliwo, wadde nga nja kugisimbulawo ddala n'okugisuula mu kikoomi nga Yahweh bwe yalangirira. Ne ku Asukenaazi ne kuva nnabbubi naye teyali nnabbubi ya butonde, wabula eggwanga lya bannabbubi ab'omuzizo, na ddala abaakyusibwa okuva mu kifaananyi kya Yahweh Omutonzi waabwe, abasibiddwa emirembe gyonna eri okwegomba kwa Kitaabwe Setaani, okwegomba kwe gye balikola. Era bannabbubi ne basaasaanyizibwa, na ddala bwe baali badduka okuva mu mikono gy'abalabe ab'obutali butuukirivu abaawakanya amakubo ga nnabbubi. N'olwekyo Asukenaazi n'agaana Yeshua HaMashiach, so nga talina mugabo wadde ekitundu mu ye.

N'olwekyo mazima abantu babuzibbwa abaagwa okuva Adamu lwe yagwa okuva mu mbeera ye etalina kibi gye yatondebwamu mu nnimiro y'e Edeni n'okwononeka ne kusaasaana okubuna ensi yonna okuyita mu luliraano lw'omusaayi gwa Kayini. Okwononeka okulala abarinzi abaagwa kwe beeyongera okwonoonera bwe baatuuka ku Lusozi Kerumooni mu nnaku za Yeredi. N'olwekyo, Yahweh n'anaaza okwononeka kwabwe ku maaso g'ensi mu mataba ago amanene mu nnaku za Nuuwa naye abaagwa ne baddamu okuteekawo amakubo gaabwe oluvannyuma lw'amataba kubanga Yahweh bwatyo ye yakkiriza abantu okweronderawo. Kubanga mazima ddala, Yahweh yawadde buli kye yatonda n'eddembe ly'okweronderawo mu kitonde kye eddembe ly'okulonda oba balibeera mu makubo ga Yahweh oba balirondererawo okuweereza Basetaani mu bujeemu bwabwe, obujeemu obuli okumpi n'enkomerero yaabwo. Kubanga nze, ye Yeshua HaMashiach, nze alibétenta omutwe gw'omusota, ye oyo alizikiriza ddala obwakabaka bwe obutaliddamu kugolokoka.

Kale bwe kityo era gano ge mazima ge nnayigiriza agakwata ku bwakabaka bw'eggulu bwe nnajja e Isirayiri ng'Omwana gw'endiga gwa Katonda mu kiseera kyange mu mubiri e Buyudaaya. Kubanga, bwe kityo bwe kyawandiikibwa nti: Obwakabaka bw'eggulu bufaananyizibwa n'omusajja eyasiga ensigo ennungi mu nnimiro ye. Naye abantu bwe baali beebase, omulabe we n'ajja, n'asiga nnamutta mu ŋŋaano, n'agenda. Era ebikoola bwe byamera, ne bibala, ne nnamutta n'alabika. Awo abaddu b'omwami w'ennyumba ne bajja, ne bamugamba nti, Mukama waffe, tewasiga nsigo nnungi mu nnimiro yo? Nnamutta avudde wa? N'abagamba nti, Omusajja ow'obuggya y'akoze ekyo. Awo abaddu ne bamugamba nti, Oyagala tugende tugikuŋŋaanye? Naye n'agamba nti, Nedda, mpozzi nga mugenda okukuŋŋaanya nnamutta, ne musimbulako n'eŋŋaano. Muleke byombi bikulire wamu okutuusa ku makungula, ne mu kiseera ky'amakungula ndigamba abakunguzi nti, Musooke mukuŋŋaanye nnamutta, mumusibe mu binywa okumwokya: naye eŋŋaano mugikuŋŋaanyize mu tterekero lyange.

Bwe kityo kubanga eŋŋaano zikotama mu buwulize ate nnamutta ayimirira butereevu mu kujeema okw'obubi. Seera!

Era nate ekiseera eky’okukung’aanya bwe kituuse, nange nja kukung’aanya eŋŋaano yange mu ggwanika lya Mukama, kubanga obudde bw’okukungula ebibala ebibereberye butuuse. Bwe kityo bwe kiriba nti nja kusimbula omuddo omubi n’emiramwa gyagwo era ndiguziba mu nsimbi, era gulisulibwa mu muliro mu musango gwagwo. Kale tegeera ensi mw’obeerera n’olutalo lwennyini mwe wazaalibwa. Olutalo lw’ensigo lwennyini olwabalukawo okuva mu nnimiro y’e Adeni, Mukama Katonda bwe yalanga olukwe ku musota n’abo abaagwa olw’obubi bwabwe n’olw’okwonoona olusuku lwa Mukama. Olutalo olwo lukomekkerezebwa n’okuwangulwa okw’amaanyi eri abo bonna abaajeema mu ngeri eyo wamu n’abaagwa, era obusika bwabwe obw’okwokebwa n’ekibiriiti bubalindiridde.

Era bwe kityo Mukama n’alangirira nti: Nja kuteeka obulabe wakati wo n’omukazi, na wakati w’ezzadde lyo n’ezzadde lye, naawe olimuluma ekisinziiro, naye alikubetenta omutwe. Era bwe ntyo ekisinziiro kyange ne kirumibwa bwe nnakomererwa, bwe nneewaayo okuba Okuyitako okutuukiridde, ng’Omwana gw’Endiga gwa Katonda, n’okwegomba mu mikono gy’abalabe bange abankambwe. Era nze nze zzadde eryasuubizibwa ery’omukazi ajja okubetenta omutwe gwa Sitaani, era abalabe bange nja kubafuula entebbe y’ebigere byange, kubanga nze Mpologoma y’omu kika kya Yuda, era mu butuukirivu nze ńjija okusala omusango n’okulwana olutalo!

Kale, tegeera nti olutalo lw’ensigo luwanguddwa ddala mu Kristo Yesu, nga bwe nalagira Peetero okuwandiika emyaka emingi egiyise: Nga muzaaliddwa omulundi ogw’okubiri, si lwa nsigo evunda, wabula lwa nsigo etevunda, ku bw’ekigambo kya Katonda, ekiramu, ekibeerera emirembe gyonna. Era nate ne ndagira Yokaana Omubikkulirwa okuwandiika mu bbaluwa ye nti: Buli muntu eyazaalibwa Katonda takola kibi: kubanga ensigo ye ebeera mu ye: so tayinza kwonoona, kubanga yazaalibwa Katonda. Mu kino abaana ba Katonda mwe bamanyikira, n’abaana b’omulyolyomi: buli muntu atakola butuukirivu, si wa Katonda, era n’oyo atayagala muganda we. N’olwekyo, bonna abaliko akabonero ka Kristo tebayinza kwonoonwa mu ngeri eyo, era tebayinza kwonoona awali enkyukakyuka okuva ku kabonero ka Katonda omulamu, ye Mukama Katonda gwe bali ababe. Kyenvudde mbagamba, muleme okutawanyizibwa obusirusiru obwo nti abo abazaaliddwa omulundi ogw’okubiri mu mwoyo gwange omutukuvu ogw’obwana era abaliko akabonero ka Mukama Katonda bayinza okusikulibwa mu mukono gwange ogw’amaanyi mu ngeri yonna. Kale, abo abatuuse ku bulokozi, obulokozi bwammwe bwa lubeerera.

Era n’olwekyo engeri z’ennabbubi nja kuzibikkula, era nja kubikkula ennabbubi mu bujjuvu, newaakubadde ennabbubi zaagala nnyo okutambulira mu nkukutu, naye nja kwolesa engeri zaazo. Kubanga ennabbubi zino ezaakyusibwa ze zireetera abantu okutwalibwa abalala be zitegese okutta. Zino ze nnabbubi ezisanyukira obukambwe obw’amaanyi era ezaagala okubonyaabonya omuyiggo gwazo awatali nsonga ndala yonna okuggyako okumatiza okwegomba kwa Kitaabwe gwe zisibiddwako okuweereza era gwe zaavaamu. Ennabbubi zino ezaakyusibwa ze njogerako ze ziri, ababbi, n’abazzi b’emisango, n’abassi, n’abeefuula (era nga zivunaanibwa ebisingawo) ezitayinza kukkuta era ezegomba okufa kwa Adamu ziryoke zibeere n’emirembe gyazo egya zaabu egy’olubeerera wamu n’abaagwa. Kale ennabbubi zikutte ensi yonna ne ziruka ebitimba byazo mu nkukutu n’obukyama.

N’ekitimba eky’amabanja kubanga ennabbubi esanyuka era yeefudde nzigagga ya zaabu gy’efunye okuyita mu kulya ensimbi ezitali ntuufu. Ennabbubi yeekwese mu bantu ne yeefuula Yakobo era nga yeefudde ekifaananyi ekikkirizisa. Kubanga ennabbubi tesobola kweyoleka ddala ng’ennabbubi embi, ekyusiddwa; kubanga omutegesi w’ennyonyi atega omutego awa ennyonyi w’esobola okulabira? Naye ennabbubi yeefuula era ekyusa ekifaananyi kyayo n’enneeyisa yaayo okugoberera abo b’eyagala okulya. Era ennabbubi bw’etandika okuluka ekitimba kyayo mu abo abagikkirizza mu butamanya okuba n’ekifo eky’okulukira mu ggwanga lyabwe; ennabbubi n’ereeta ennabbubi endala eziza okwegatta nayo mu kuwamba kwayo okubi oba ng’emu ku nnabubi endala eziva mu nsawo y’amagi g’ennabbubi ne zigenda okuluka ebitimba byazo ne zigenda mu maaso n’omuzingo gw’okufa. Bwe kityo Adamu n’asibibwa n’azingibwazingibwa ennabbubi zino embi. Era mpola mpola era n’essanyu eringi, zitega ne ziriira ku Adamu era n’obukambwe n’obunene ennabbubi enoonya ensi yonna ye ne Bakitaabwe ab’okwegomba kwe basibiddwako.

Kubanga mu bulimba bwayo ennabbubi za Asukenazi zeegasse ku Esawu, okuyita mu Talmudi kubanga mu myaka emingi egiyise baafuula Talmudi omukama waabwe era omufuzi waabwe, naye tebaali baawukirivu ba Musa, so tebagoberera kkubo lya Yeshua HaMashiach naye beefuula. Era ku Esawu kwe kwava abassi bange, era ku magezi amakyamu aga Esawu, kwe kugamba, obulombolombo bwabwe obuvvoola Katonda obw’abantu bwe bagulumiza okusinga amakubo ga Mukama Katonda, bayonoonye Yakobo ne batega muganda wa Esawu emirundi mingi. Era bwe ntyo ne mbagamba mu kiseera kyange mu mubiri ng’Omwana gw’Endiga gwa Katonda nti: Mmwe muli ba kitammwe omulyolyomi, n’okwegomba kwa kitammwe kwe mwagala okukola: yali museesi wa musaayi okuva ku lubereberye, so teyanywerera mu mazima, kubanga amazima tegali mu ye. Bw’ayogeranga obulimba, ayogeranga ebibye ye: kubanga mulimba, era kitaawe w’obwo.

Naye ennabbubi teyava mu makubo g’abaagwa era ennabbubi ekyenyigira mu bikolwa byayo ebibi era n’ebisanyukira. Kubanga ennabbubi eyigganya abaana b’ezingizza ng’omuyiggo gwayo era enywa omusaayi gw’abo abato b’ebonyaabonya mu kukwatibwa kwayo. Kubanga ennabbubi etuukiriza okwegomba kwayo okw’omuzizo ku baana abato b’ewamba mu nkukutu mu ggwanga lyaabwe ng’abaana abato batwaliddwa mu kitimba kyayo. Era ku nnabubi ki eyalukako ekitimba naye era n’esima wansi w’ettaka. Kubanga ekitimba ky’ennabbubi kituuka wansi w’ensi era ekitimba kyayo kibuna ekifo kinene era ennabbubi enoonya obwakabaka n’obufuzi obw’ensi yonna. N’olwekyo ennabbubi etambula mu bulimba obungi kubanga mu bulimba bwe balwanira.

Naye abeefuula bano abeegasse ku baagwa emirembe gyonna era be baavaamu ddala; beefuula Yakobo era mu bukyamu ne beewa ettaka eryasuubizibwa, lye batalinaamu mugabo wadde enkumbi naye beefuula. Era mu makubo gaabwe mwe muva omutego ogulitegebwa ensolo eva mu nsi eri Yakobo kubanga okuyita mu Talmudi ya Esawu, Asukenazi yeegasse ne Esawu, era baligattibwa n’omwana gw’endiga gw’ogusota, ye mwana gw’endiga gwabwe ow’obulimba ow’amayembe abiri. Omwana gw’endiga ow’obutonde atali wa bulijjo ng’ejjembe erimu lifuga ennyanja okusinziira ku Noahide ate ejjembe eddala ne lifuga ettaka eryasuubizibwa mu kuvvoola okw’amaanyi n’obulimba kubanga ye jjembe ettono lya Danyeri, era ensi erifugibwa obufuzi bwabwe okumala akaseera katono. Kubanga baligatta amaanyi gonna n’ebibinja by’obubi byonna wansi w’obufuzi bwabwe, era balibakuŋŋaanyiza wamu okulwana ku lunaku olwo olunene, lwe balisuulibwa wansi n’amaanyi emirembe gyonna era mu kubonerezebwa n’omusango ogutaggwaawo mwe baligenda emirembe gyonna.

Naye amakubo g’ennabbubi ge makubo g’okutiisatiisa, n’okunyaga, n’obussi, n’obuli bw’enguzi era amakubo amabi mangi nnyo ennabbubi mw’eyigiriza n’okubeeramu kubanga ennabbubi enoonya bulijjo okufuula abantu ennabbubi endala eziri abaana b’omuliro emirundi ebiri okusinga bo. Era bwe kityo ennabbubi ne zizina ne zeetendereza olw’okutta abantu b’omu nsi, nga bazina nga bakutte emikono ate nga basanyuka olw’okufa n’okuzikirira kw’abantu be batulugunya abasinga obungi nga bakazi na baana na mawere; kale okuzikirira kwabwe tekulwaawo. Naye batutte abakulembeze b’ensi ne batega bakabaka b’ensi yonna mu kukkiriza ne mu kuwamba, era kumpi bawambye ensi yonna mu bitimba byabwe ebingi era mu bitimba byabwe ensi esibiddwa n’eweebwayo mu mikono gy’ogusota olw’omusango n’okuzikirira okumpi okutuuka ddala. Era ennabbubi ekaabira waggulu n’obulumi ng’eriira ku muyiggo gwayo, era nga yeeraga ng’omuntu omwavu atulugunyizibwa, so ng’ate ye nnabubi etulugunya era n’ettirimbula Adamu.

Kyenvudde mbagamba, muwulire eddoboozi lyange, abantu bange, ye, mwenna abasoma ebigambo bino, mweyawule ku nnabubi n’ebitimba byayo, kubanga buli muntu akyuka okuva mu kuzikirira kwe n’akoowoola erinnya lya Mukama, ye Yesu Omunazaaleeti, era ye Yeshua HaMashiach, Masiya wa Isirayiri ow’amazima; mazima ddala, omuntu oyo alirokolebwa.

Mazima ddala, mbalagira mwenna okuvumirira n’okugaana Talmudi yaabwe evvoola Katonda, ekitabo eky’obubi, obugwagwa, n’obulimba; ekitabo ekyawandiikibwa Esawu ne kikkirizibwa Ekkuŋŋaaniro lya Sitaani, ekkuŋŋaaniro eririmu obubi n’obugwagwa bwokka. Kubanga ennabbubi ye Kkuŋŋaaniro lya Sitaani, eyazaalibwa mu kika kya Asukenazi ekya Yafeesi abatalina mugabo wadde enkumbi mu busika bwa Seemu, naddala obwa Yakobo omutuukirivu gwe ndirokosa ensigalira ey’amaanyi.

Kyenvudde mbagamba, muve mu bo, mweyawule, mukyukire Yeshua HaMashiach, mulyoke munyigireko, era mulyoke muyige okubeera mu makubo amatuukirivu ga Mukama Katonda, amakubo ge ndi, era gwe ndi Ekigambo ekyefuula omubiri, kubanga Musa mulamu ddala mu Yeshua HaMashiach, era mu Ndagaano Yange Empya mwe muli ebisuubizo byonna n’endagaano zonna ez’edda, ebisuubizo ebirituukirizibwa mu bujjuvu mu bufuzi bwange obw’emyaka olukumi n’Obwakabaka bwange bwe ndissaawo mu butuukirivu ku nsi.

Nze Yeshua HaMashiach, Masiya ow’amazima owa Isirayiri, era mazima ddala nze Kabaka wammwe, Mukama wammwe, Kabona Asinga Obukulu, ne Masiya wammwe emirembe n’emirembe, era Obwakabaka bwange n’obufuzi bwange tebuliggwaawo.

Nze njija okulabula abantu bange ku bulimba buno obunene n’okuzikirira okubunye ensi yonna mu kitimba kyakwo kubanga ennabbubi kati ziruse ebitimba byazo mu nsi yonna.

Nze Kristo Yesu, era ntumye Ebigambo bino okuyita mu mubaka wange era omuweereza wange, era okuyita mu mubaka wange era omuweereza wange Ebigambo bino biweerezeddwa.

Nze Yesu Kristo, era ye Yeshua HaMashiach abikkudde Ebigambo by’Enjiri ey’olubeerera wamu ne Mukama Katonda yennyini mu biseera by’okubikkulirwa ebiriwo kati. Bye biseera by’okubikkulirwa okubonaabona kwa Yakobo nga tekunnatandika mu bujjuvu, Yakobo alyoke alokolebwe.

Kale, tegeera obuzito bw’Ebigambo byange, so toba atalina kukkiriza, naye kkiriza obujulizi bwange era owulire Ebigambo byange, era ove ku butakkiriza obw’amaka n’obujeemu kubanga kati bye biseera by’okudda eri Mukama Katonda okuyita mu Yeshua HaMashiach, ne mu biseera by’okubonaabona ebituuse.

Era bonna abajja eri Masiya waabwe ow’olubeerera ne banjikiriza n’Endagaano Yange Empya balituuka ku bulokozi buli muntu mu ntegeka ye. Abo abakoowoola erinnya lya Mukama waffe Yesu Kristo mu kitiibwa ky’okufiirira okukkiriza balizuukizibwa mu kuzuukira kw’abatuukirivu, era obuzito bw’empeera yaabwe ey’olubeerera bulisinga nnyo okubonaabona kwabwe n’ebizibu byabwe bye ndibawa amaanyi okuwangula nga Mukama Katonda bwe yampa amaanyi okuwangula.

Era abo ab’oku Yakobo be ndikweka mu ddungu okuva mu biseera by’okubonaabona okusinga byonna; oluvannyuma balitwalibwa nga balamu mu Bwakabaka bwange obw’emyaka olukumi, era balyeyongera okuba abazaazi n’okwongera, era tulibeera mu makubo ga Mukama Katonda kubanga ensi erifugibwa mu butuukirivu, so tewaliba kintu kyonna ekibi ekirigiyingiramu.

Era nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, annyonnyola okutuuka kw’ebiseera ensi okuzimba abo abeebase mu Kristo n’okuwonya abo abalamu era abasigaddewo mu kulabika kwange era abasangibwa nga beesigwa mu Kristo, kubanga mazima ddala, ebiseera bye bino. Seera!

Nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, era nzija okubaggyayo, abange ab’omuwendo, era nze kye ndi NZE NNYINI OMUKULU kye yanfuula okuba.

Kale tegeera okwagala kwe nkulina, era otegeere okwagala Mukama Katonda kw’alina gy’oli kubanga mazima ddala okwagala kwaffe muliro ogwokya gonna, era okwagala kwa Mukama Katonda tekusobola kuwangulwa kubanga Mukama Katonda tasobola kuwangulwa era Mukama Katonda kwagala era Mukama Katonda kitangaala, era mbayita okujja mu bwakabaka bw’okwagala n’ekitangaala n’obutuukirivu; we waliba ekitiibwa ky’abanunulibwa nga kimasamasa emirembe n’emirembe, era ekitonde kirisumululwa mu kusumululwa okw’ekitiibwa kw’abaana abatuukirivu ba Mukama Katonda, be ndikulembera nga Kabaka wa Bakabaka era Mukama wa Bakama.

Era bwe kityo bonna balimanya nti muli bange, era nze ndi wammwe, ku lw’okwagala kwe mundaga mu kugondera amateeka gange, n’oku lw’okwagala kwe mwagalagana nga mukola emirimu gyammwe, ka kube na bizibu ki. Nti mwagale Mukama Katonda wammwe n’omutima gwammwe gwonna, n’emmeeme yammwe yonna, n’amagezi gammwe gonna, n’amaanyi gammwe gonna, era nti mazima ddala mwagale muliraanwa wo nga bwe weeyagala wekka, era ago ge mateeka ga Mukama Katonda.

Weeyongere okugumiikiriza, omugole wange, kubanga essaawa ey’okununulibwa kwo okujjuvu etuuse. Kale nteeka omukono gwange ng’obujulizi bw’Enjiri ey’olubeerera, era kibeere bwe kityo.

Kutereddwako omukono mu kwagala kwa Mukama Katonda ne mu musaayi gwange ogutangirira emirembe n’emirembe!

Mukama Katonda NZE NNYINI OMUKULU, ne Yeshua HaMashiach era ye Yesu Kristo, Amiina.

Seera!
