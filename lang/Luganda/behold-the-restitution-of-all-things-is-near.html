Mujulirwa Omubiri: Isirayiri
Ekikaawa n’Ekiwooma
Julaayi 05, 2024

Laba! Okuzzaawo Ebintu Byonna Kuli Kumpi!

Okuva eri Yeshua HaMashiach:

Ebiro by’okuzza ebintu mu nteeko biriranye ensi, naye okusooka ebiseera n’enkomerero y’omusango omunene biteekwa okutuukirira. Nange, Kristo Yesu, nja kusala omusango gw’ensi kubanga ensi yange n’okujjuza kwayo, era nagigula n’omusaayi gwange ogw’omuwendo ku Kalivaali. Mu mazima ge gatakyuka, nnawaayo buli ttondo ly’omusaayi gwange omutukuvu ogwayiika olw’ebibi by’abantu. Abo abakkiriza okukkiriza kwange okw’emirembe n’obulamu obutaggwaawo waliba emirembe, n’obulamu obutaggwaawo, n’amakubo g’obutuukirivu gajja kufuga. Abo abagaana okukkiriza kwange baba balondewo amakubo g’okufa, omusango, n’ekibonerezo ekitaggwaawo, kubanga ensi eggwaawo so tewali kulondawo kulala. Bwe batyo bajja kwetikka omusango gwabwe na buli kibi, ekigambo ekibi, ekirowoozo ekibi, n’ekikolwa ekibi bijja kwanjululwa balyoke babibabuulire. Naye abo abagaana obulokozi bwange tebajja kusobola kuyimirirawo n’akamu, era mu kubulwa eky’okukola balondawo ennyanja y’omuliro etegekeddwa omulyolyomi n’abamalayika be era ekintu ng’ekyo tekyetaagisa, naye buli omu alina eddembe okweronderawo. Nze ndi oyo ajja okusala omusango n’okulwana, n’amakubo ga Mukama Katonda gajja kufuga ensi mu butuukirivu mu bwakabaka bwange obujja. Ggwe Ekkanisa yange, era n’Omugole wange ow’omuwendo olijja kutwalibwa mu ggulu, era olijja kufuna omugabo gwo, era olijja kutwala ebifo byo mu ntegeka ze nnakutegekera, era n’ekifo kyo mu Yakobo kubanga ggwe Isirayiri wa Katonda.

Kubanga mu nze, Kristo Yesu, temuli Muyudaaya newaakubadde Omunnamawanga wabula ekitonde kipya, era ekitonde ekyo kijja kutambulira wamu ng’omuntu omu omuggya. Laba! Olijja kuba muntu muggya, era olijja kuba kitonde kipya, saakuwa mpeereza ya mwoyo mutuukuvu? Era n’akabonero ka Katonda omulamu, akatasobola kumenyeka? Ani ku mmwe ayinza okuggyawo okuzaalibwa kw’abaana be ab’omubiri? Tosobola kubanga obuzaale bwo buyita mu mirembe n’emirembe era abaana ba lubeerera. Bwe kityo, ensigo ya Mukama Katonda eyakolebwa munda mu baana ba Katonda tesobola kwononeka, kubanga tekiyinzika ggwe okunyagulibwa mu mikono gyange. Temwasomanga? Kubanga mwazaalibwa omulundi ogwokubiri, si lwa nsigo evunda, wabula lwa nsigo etevunda, lwa kigambo kya Katonda, ekiri ekiramu era ekibeerawo emirembe gyonna. Baana bange, ebigambo byange bya kitalo nnyo? Mukkirizza amaloboozi amakyamu okubakyamya okuva mu kulowooza okumu mu Kristo? Tewali kiyinza kuggyawo nsigo ya Mukama Katonda nga yakolebwa munda, kubanga ebirabo n'okuyita kwa Katonda tebyejjusibwa. Kale lwaki obuusabuusa obulokozi bwo? Wadde ng’omubiri gwo gujja kukuleetera okwonoona, nange nja kukusonyiwa era nkuyonje ng’oyatula ebibi byo mu kwenenya, tewali kiyinza kuggyawo baana ba Katonda mu maka ga Katonda. Abo ababeerawo mu butali butuukirivu emirimu gyabwe gijja kwokebwa, era bajja kuyimirira nga bali bwereere era nga baswadde, ate abo emirimu gyabwe egiyimirirawo ng'emituukirivu bajja kufuna empeera ennene, naye abaana ba Katonda bafuna obulokozi obwa lubeerera. Omuntu yenna bw’ataba na mwoyo gwa Kristo, oyo si wuwe.

Abo ababeera mu bulamu obw’obujeemu n’abo abeefuula ababeerera Kristo mu linnya lyokka bajja kugwa ng’Ekkanisa ey’amazima egenda. Kubanga asiga mu mubiri alikungula okuvunda mu mubiri. Era abo abaagezaako okufuula Nze Mukama ow’omutima ogutakkiriza ne beeyongera mu bibi bajja kulekebwa mu kutegana kwa Yakobo olw’okuzikirizibwa kw’omubiri era bajja kwetaaga okukoowoola erinnya lya Mukama okutuusa ku nkomerero, bwe kityo bwe kiriba. Abo abeerya enkwe okutuusa ku mukka gwabwe ogusembayo ne batafiira mu Kristo era nga tebalina nkolagana ya bulokozi nange bajja kwolekagana n’omusango gwabwe. Olowooza nti Mukama Katonda omunoonya emyoyo musirusiru? Olowooza nti nze, Kristo Yesu, sigezesa mitima gya bantu lwa kukkiriza na kwesiga mu nze? Naye bwe kiriba bwe kityo, era nja kugamba bangi abampita Mukama nti: Munveeko, mmwe abakozi b’obutali butuukirivu, saabamanyanga.

Naye eri mmwe Ekkanisa yange, abayite bange era abeesigwa era abalonde: Buli mmeeme efuna obulokozi nayo efuulibwa musika w’ebisuubizo Mukama Katonda bye yasuubiza Bakadde baffe. Kubanga kyawandiikibwa nti Abannamawanga babeere basika bannaffe, era ab’omubiri gumu, era abagabana ekisuubizo kye mu Kristo olw’enjiri, era mbateekereddemu mu Muzeeyituuni ogw’obutonde. N’abo bonna abatonziibwa obuggya mu nze bajja kuzibwa mu bujjuvu mu butuukirivu n’obutuukirivu n’obutukuvu obw’amazima. Ssaabasuubiza byambalo bitereevu, mmwe baana bange abato? Era ntegese omugabo gwammwe mu bika, kubanga mmwe muli Isirayiri wa Katonda gwe nnanunula okuva mu nsi.

Lekera awo okulowooza mu nnono za Adamu, kubanga omusaayi gwa Adamu gwayononebwa mu kibi! Nga bwe kyawandiikibwa nti: omuntu eyasooka Adamu yafuulibwa emmeeme ennamu, ate Adamu ow’oluvannyuma n’afuulibwa omwoyo oguzza obulamu. Temwasomanga? Si bonna abali Isirayiri, abava mu Isirayiri. Kubanga abaana b'ekisuubizo be nsigo ya Ibulayimu olw'okukkiriza, era nga bwe kyawandiikibwa nti Omutuukirivu aliba mulamu lwa kukkiriza. Era mu nze, Kristo Yesu, ebintu byonna bituukirizibwa kubanga Empologoma eriba mu kwegatta okutuukiridde n'Omwana gw'endiga era nammwe mwesanishizza ddala nange, Kristo Yesu Mukama wammwe. Laba! Naye eri mmwe abatya Erinnya lyange, omwana w'obutuukirivu alivaayo! Nze ndi mwana wa Butuukirivu, era omwana omu yekka eyazaalibwa Katonda ali waggulu ennyo, Mukama Katonda, kubanga tewali amufaanana, era nze ndi oyo ali wano okunyakula ekkanisa yange okuva mu kabi, era n'omugole wange okumutwala mu maka ge mu ggulu, era anaabeeranga nange ennaku zonna. Bwe kiriba bwe kityo, nja kubagattako ku nnyumba yammwe ey’amazima kubanga Isirayiri yasaasaanyizibwa mu nsi yonna era mu Isirayiri wa Katonda gye balikung’aanyizibwa, nga tebakyasaasaanyizibwa nate, kubanga mmwe muli Isirayiri wa Katonda!

Bwe ntyo nja kunyakula Ekkanisa yange mu ggulu, mmwe abeesigwa bange, era mujja kubeeranga nange emirembe gyonna. Kubanga mwogerezebwa omwami omu: Yeshua HaMashiach, era olw’omwoyo gwange mbafudde omuwala atamanyi musajja era ne ndongoosa abeesigwa balyoke bayimirire nga batuukirivu mu ssaddaaka yange ey’okutangirira. Bwe mulitwalibwa mujja kuweebwa empeera olw’obwesigwa bwammwe era engule nnyingi ze mujja okutwala, wadde ng’abamu balifuna okufiirwa. Mujja kuweebwa empeera okusinga okulowooza kwammwe okusinga obunene, kubanga eriso teryalabanga newaakubadde okutu tekuwuliranga so tekuyingiranga mu mutima gwa muntu ebyo Mukama Katonda by’ategekedde abamwagala. N’abeesigwa, era n’Omugole wange, banaabeeranga nange emirembe n’emirembe, era balifuga nga bakabaka n’abakabona emirembe n’emirembe, kubanga Obwakabaka bwange tebulina nkomerero.

Bwe kityo malayika wange ajja kulinnya gy’onoobeera nange mu ggulu n’ateeka akabonero ku bantu 12,000 okuva mu buli kika. Temwasomanga Bigambo byange ebyaweebwa Yokaana Omubikkulirwa? Ne ndaba malayika omulala ng’alinnya ng’ava ebuvanjuba, ng’alina akabonero ka Katonda omulamu: n’ayogerera mu ddoboozi ddene eri bamalayika abana, abaweebwa okulumya ensi n’ennyanja, ng’agamba nti, Temulumya nsi, newaakubadde ennyanja newaakubadde emiti, okutuusa lwe tunaatera akabonero abaddu ba Katonda waffe mu byenyi byabwe. Tewaawuliranga kigambo nti alinnye? Bamalayika abatukuvu ab’omu ggulu bava mu ggulu ne bakka ku nsi, kubanga amaka gaabwe gali mu ggulu. Malayika omutukuvu okulinnya, aba alinnya mu ggulu. Bamalayika abagwa be balinnya ku nsi okuva mu bunnya, tomanyi kussa kitiibwa mu Bigambo by’ekigambo kyange ekitukuvu?

Era bajja kuteeka akabonero ku bantu 12,000 okuva mu buli kika erirolondeddwako: Yuda, Lewubeeni, Gaadi, Aseri, Nafutaali, Manaase, Simyoni, Leevi, Isakaali, Zebbulooni, Yusufu, Benyamini. Bwe kityo abantu bange 144,000 bajja kuba n’akabonero ka Katonda omulamu mu byenyi byabwe. Era bajja kuyimirira nange ku lusozi Sayuuni, era bajja kuyimba ng’oluyimba oluggya mu maaso g’entebe y’obwakabaka, ne mu maaso g’ebisolo ebina, n’abakadde: so tewaali muntu eyasobola okuyiga oluyimba olwo wabula abo emitwalo ekkumi n’ena mu enkumi nnya, abaanunulibwa okuva mu nsi. Abo be batayonoonebwa na bakazi; kubanga bali bawala. Bano be bagoberera Omwana gw'endiga wonna w'agenda. Kubanga bano baanunulibwa mu bantu, nga be bibala ebibereberye eri Katonda n’Omwana gw’endiga. Era mu kamwa kaabwe temwaalabika bukuusa: kubanga tebalina nsobi mu maaso g'entebe ya Katonda.

Abantu 144,000 balondebwa Mukama Katonda emisingi gy’ensi nga tennaateekebwawo okuba mu kiseera ng’ekyo. Baweesebwa mu kubonaabona, era bajja kuba n’ebisoboyo eby’enjawulo n’amaanyi ge nja okubambaza mu byambalo byabwe ebipya bye nja okuwa, kubanga ebyambalo byo bitungiddwa ggwe era biribaamu by’otuseeko n’engeri zo. Bajja kutwalibwa mu kukungaanya abeesigwa naye bateereddwawo lwa bigendererwa byange, era n’ebigendererwa bya Mukama Katonda ne Yeshua HaMashiach. Bava mu kiseera ky’okubonaabona okunene okukulembera Isirayiri mu kifo ekirongoofu. Naye basooka kutwalibwa ku mbaga ya Kristo kubanga be Mugole we, abayite, abalonde, abeesigwa, era ab’amazima. Era abo Kristo be yafuula abatukuvu ng’omuwala atamanyi musajja mu musaayi gwe ogw’okutangirira. Era ggwe mugole wange alina okuweereza ebigendererwa bya Mukama Katonda ku kiragiro kyange mu biseera by’obuzibu obusinga obunene.

Kubanga mmwe muli bibala ebibereberye ebikung’aanyizibbwa okuva mu nnimiro z’amakungula, kubanga nze ndi Mukama w’amakungula, era kati kiseera ky’okubakung’aanyiza mu tterekero lyange mulyoke mufune empeera y’emirimu gyammwe. Abo abakung’aanyizza eŋŋaano baliba na bingi, n’abo abakung’aanyizza essubi balifuna okufiirwa ng’ebisusunku byokerwa mu muliro, naye era baliba balokoke. Bwe kityo abakozi bange n’abantu bange bajja kujja mu busika bwabwe mu nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, kubanga nze ndi Mukama wa bakama ne Kabaka wa bakabaka era sijja kusirika so sijja kuziyizibwa.

Tewawandiikibwanga? Bw’ati bw’ayogera Mukama Katonda nti, Laba, ndiggya abaana ba Isirayiri mu mawanga gye baagenda, ne mbakung’aanyiza ku njuyi zonna, ne mbaleeta mu nsi yaabwe: Era ndibafuula eggwanga limu mu nsi ku nsozi za Isirayiri; ne kabaka omu aliba kabaka waabwe bonna: so tebaliba mawanga abiri nate, so tebalyawulwamu bwakabaka bubiri nate n’akamu: So tebalyeyonoonyanga nate n’ebifaananyi byabwe, newaakubadde n’ebintu byabwe eby’omuzizo newaakubadde n’okumenya amateeka gaabwe kwonna: naye ndibalokola mu bifo byabwe byonna gye baayonooneranga, ne mbayonja: bwe batyo baliba bantu bange, nange naabeeranga Katonda waabwe.

Nze Yeshua HaMashiach ntumye Ebigambo bino okuyita mu muweereza wange, era okuyita mu muweereza wange Ebigambo bino biweerezeddwa! Laba! Nze ndi oyo ajja mu bire, kale mweetegeke, mmwe abawala bange ab’amagezi.

Okuva eri Mukama Katonda Omutonzi Ataggwaawo:

Ndaaba okutya kwammwe, mmwe abagwa, kubanga kati gujja omusango gwammwe era n’omusango omunene ogwasuubizibwa edda, n’ebiro by’omusango omunene ebyalangirirwa mu biseera ebyayita nze, Mukama Katonda omutonzi ataggwaawo eyalangirira omusango gwammwe n’okubwatuka musanvu ne biwuluguma amaloboozi gaabyo era bwe kityo eddoboozi lya Katonda omuyinza w’ebintu byonna liribwatuka! N’ebigendererwa byange birigattibwa wamu, era n’entegeka zange ez’emirembe zijja kutuukirizibwa mu mwana wange Yesu Kristo. Kubanga mu bibala ebibereberye mujja empeera z’amakungula eri abakozi abaatumibwa Yeshua HaMashiach era eŋŋaano yange kati ejja kukung’aanyizibwa mu tterekero lyange era okwawukana okusembayo ku nsi kujja kutandika n’okutegana kwa Yakobo kuli wano.

Laba! Nja kwabuluza eggulu waggulu ne nkankanya ensi wansi, era nja kukungaanya abasika abasigaddewo ab’obulokozi okuva mu nsi, era bajja kujja mu bulokozi, era bajja kukoowoola erinnya lya Mukama okutuusa ku nkomerero, era bajja kumpereza mu yeekaalu yange n’ekibiina ekinene era ekinene kirilokolebwa mu wiiki ya Danyeri ey’amakumi musanvu. Nze, era Mukama Katonda, nja kuyuza ensi ne nziggya ensozi mu bifo byazo n’ensi yonna erimanya nti El Shaddai agolose okuva waggulu era omusango omunene kati gujja kubalukawo ku nsi Abaesigwa bange bwe balikung’aanyizibwa kubanga ebyonoono birikoma, era n’okwonoonabwa kwa Yakobo n’ebibi by’ennyumba ya Isirayiri.

Mu kifo kyaabwe mujja okufuga obutuukirivu, era n’okufuga okw’obutuukirivu okw’omwana wange omu yekka gwenzaala, Yeshua HaMashiach, gwe nsiima bulijjo. Ye oyo alikuba amawanga n’omuggo ogw’ekyuma n’okufuga okw’obutuukirivu bwe kiriba, empologoma w’erigalamira n’omwana gw’endiga. Bwe kiriba bwe kityo kabona asinga obukulu emirembe gyonna olw’entegeka ya Merukizeddeeki ne Mukama wa bakama ne Kabaka wa bakabaka bajja kwegatta mu bujjuvu mu kutuukirizibwa okutaggwaawo era okufuga kwe kunaabanga kwa lubeerera, era Yeshua HaMashiach kubanga oyo ye Yesu Kristo!

Mwetegeke mmwe abali ku nsi mu butali butuukirivu kubanga ntuma omwana wange okusasula omusango gwange n’okumenyaamenya okufuga kwonna okw’abagudde, kubanga ebiseera byabwe bituuse era omusango gujja kugwa nnyo ku nsi. Kubanga mweterekera obusungu bungi ku lunaku lw’obusungu era olunaku lw’obusungu lubatuuseeko, mmwe abataliiko mugaso, kubanga mwefuula abataalongoofu mu kwonooneka okw’amaanyi, so temulina mirembe olw’amakubo gammwe amakyafu ag’omuzizo. Naye nga temunnagenda mu busika bwammwe obutaggwaawo gye mujja okubbinkirirwa mu nnimi z’omuliro ezisinga okwokya eza bbulu, mujja kusooka kuweereza ebigendererwa byange, kubanga n’abalabe bange bawulira eddoboozi lyange.

Bwe kityo omusango gujja kutandikira ku nnyumba ya Katonda era nja kutuma omwana wange era Kristo Yesu Masiya wa Isirayiri eyazuukira era omulokozi w’ensi, era ajja kukola byonna bye nnalagira era ne nsuubiza Bakadde baffe n’abaana era byonna biriba yee era amiina ng’okutuukirizibwa kw’ebisuubizo bwe kutuuka.

Kubanga eggulu litegekeddwa Abaana bange, era bonna be nnalagira olw’ebigendererwa byange nga okutondebwa tekunnabaawo. Kubanga nnamanya buli omu ku mmwe mu biseera ebyayita ebitaggwaawo, era nabateekawo ng’ekyama ekikwekeddwa mu mutima gwange kye nabikkula ku kiseera ekituufu mu ntegeka yange ey’obulokozi etaggwaawo. Tewali kiyinza kubaawo nga sikimanyi nga tekinnabaawo, era Nze, Katonda Omuyinza w'ebintu byonna, ayita ebintu ebitaliwo ng'asookedde ddala. O, mmwe abagwa, mujja kwokya ddala mu musango gwammwe ogusemberedde, era mwali basirusiru nnyo okulowooza nti muyinza okutwala Mukama Katonda n’obukujjukujju, naye NZE NDI OYO atwala ab’amalala mu musango, kubanga bali bagezigezi mu kulowooza kwabwe. Kubanga nnabalaga ebyama ebitagasa, kubanga nnali mmanyi nti mujja kulyamu olukwe byonna ebyange. Nga muli basirusiru mmwe abagwa so temuli bagezi. Kyokka kye mwatuukako kwe kubonaabona kwammwe n’okuzikirizibwa okw’olubeerera mu nnimi z’omuliro ezisinga okwokya, era mujja kuwugira mu kibriiti ekyaka, kubanga ennyanja ennene eyaka bw’etyo emirembe gyonna, nange nja kuteekawo akabonero, era mujja kwerabirwa ddala, era temujja kujjukira.

Naye omukisa guno si gwammwe abalonde bange, abeesigwa bange, era bonna abayitibwa erinnya lyange. Ebyammwe eby’omu maaso bya ssanyu etaggwaawo, essanyu etaggwaawo, essanyu etaggwaawo so teribaayo nkomerero ya nnaku so teribaayo lunaku lubi eri abo abayingira mu lusuku lwa Katonda. Laba! Nja kuzzaawo ebintu byonna era n’okubifuula ebirungi okusinga obutuukirivu bwange obw’olubereberye nga okwononeka tekunnagwa, kubanga nja kutonda eggulu eriggya n’ensi empya omubeera obutuukirivu so tewaliba kiyingiramu ekibi newaakubadde ekitatukuvu. Kubanga ebweru waliyo embwa, n’abalogo, n’abenzi, n’abatemu, n’abasinza ebifaananyi, na buli muntu ayagala n’ayimba obulimba, era bonna baliba n’omugabo gwabwe mu nnyanja eyo eyaka omusiri gwaabwe gye gutafiira.

N’olwekyo tewali kya kutya mu biseera byammwe eby’omu maaso mmwe abayite bange, abalonde, n’abeesigwa, era mujja kufugiranga wamu ne Yeshua HaMashiach so tewali nkomerero ya bwakabaka bwe newaakubadde okufuga kwe. Nange Katonda omutonzi ataggwaawo, era Mukama Katonda yennyini ndifuula amaka gange mu bantu era waliba emirembe n’obutuukirivu obuwuliziganya ku bitonde byange byonna era ndisangula amaziga gonna mu maaso gaabwe so tewaliba nate muntu aligolokoka alibanyigiriza n’akatono, kubanga nze, Mukama Katonda, era Katonda omutonzi ataggwaawo, ndayira ebintu bino mu linnya lyange kubanga siyinza kulayira kinene kusingawo. Naye eri mmwe abatya erinnya lyange Omwana w’obutuukirivu alivaayo ng’alina okuwonya mu biwaawaatiro bye; nammwe mulifuluma, ne mukula ng’ennyana ez’omu kiriri.

Nze Mukama Katonda, Omutonzi ataggwaawo, ntumye Ebigambo byange okuyita mu muweereza wange, era okuyita mu muweereza wange Ebigambo byange biweerezeddwa.

NZE NDI NZE NDI omunene era tewali mulala!

Munywegere Omwana aleme okusunguwala, ne muzikirira okuva mu kkubo, obusungu bwe bwe bubuubuuka katono.
