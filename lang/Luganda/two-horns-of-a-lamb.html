Amayembe Abiri ag'Omwana gw'Endiga

Laba, nzijja okubalaga ebyama by'ennaku zino ez'enkomerero ebituuse ku nsi, Yakobo nga tannayingira mu bbanga lye ery'okubonaabona okuzingako ensi yonna. N'olwekyo, mutege amatu gammwe mu bwetoowaze muwulire Ebigambo bya Yeshua HaMashiach, mulyoke mutegeere ebiseera bye mulimu, ebyo ebiseera eby'obulimba obungi ennyo. Era nate, bwe bityo bye biseera bye mulimu era bye biseera by'omusango ogutuuse ku nsi yonna. Laba! Muwulire Ebigambo bya Kristo Yesu abaagalwa bange, era muwulire ebigambo byange bye mbadde mbasindikira okubakubagiza emitima gyammwe n'okubawa okutegeera ebintu ebingi bye mulaba kaakano ebigenda bitya ku nsi, era mulyoke mugobererenga bulijjo etteeka lyange eteriggwaawo ery'obutatya! Olw'amazima ag'emirembe gyonna, emitawaana egituuse ku nsi n'emisango egitegekeddwa abatalina butuukirivu, n'abasekerera, n'abo abagaana endagaano ya Yahweh ey'emirembe tebigendereddwamu bantu butuukirivu na beesigwa naabo abakkirizza endagaano ya Yahweh ey'emirembe kubanga bwe kyawandiikibwa era bwe kiribeera.

N'olwekyo, abaagalwa bange, munkkirize mbakubagize mu biseera by'okunakuwala mulyoke mube n'eky'okuwa eyeetaaga era mulyoke mwagale Yahweh n'omutima gwammwe gwonna, n'obulamu bwammwe bwonna, n'amagezi gammwe gonna n'amaanyi gammwe gonna, era mulyoke mwagale muliraanwa wammwe nga bwe mweyagala mwekka, kubanga mazima ddala, ddala, bonna ababeera mu ngeri eyo balaga amakubo ga Yahweh ge mwayigira ku nze, Mukama wammwe ow'Emirembe gyonna ow'Obutuukirivu, kubanga bwe ntyo bwe ndi! Kale mbajjukiza mukomole emitima gyammwe mu kuwuliza era mukkirize Ebigambo byange eby'amazima era ebiri Ebigambo eby'okukubagiza mulyoke mube n'emirembe mu biseera by'emitawaana, era ekiseera ekyo ebirumi by'okuzaala lwe birikoma ng'abeesigwa mu Kristo batondebwa buto era ne bazaalibwa obulamu obuggya mu ggulu era ne bafaananyizibwa Kristo Yesu ye Adamu ow'oluvannyuma.

Era amazima ag'emirembe gyonna ebiseera bituuse eby'okununulibwa okujjuvu n'okununulibwa okujjuvu n'okulokolebwa kw'abeesigwa mu Kristo kutegekeddwa okubaawo mu kaseera katono n'amangu ddala kati, era olunaku, ekiseera, n'essaawa bimanyiddwa mu ggulu, n'eggye ly'omu ggulu lyesibye lulinda olwogoolo olw'amaanyi, nga balinda okufuuwa kw'ekkondeere erisembayo kubanga ekkondeere liweereddwayo era ebiseera bye bino. Era mu kaseera katono mu kiseera ekyo okununulibwa okwasuubizibwa n'obulokozi obujjuvu biribeerawo mu kubona kw'eriiso mu butuukirivu obw'ekitalo era tekiribaayo kulwawo. Mu kaseera katono mu kiseera ekyo eky'okutuukiririzibwa okujjuvu bonna abeesigwa mu Kristo mu biseera byange eby'ekisa okutandikira ku lunaku lwa Pentekoote emyaka gino gyonna egya Yubile egiwedde, Yahweh lwe yampa omwoyo ogwo omutukuvu ogw'obwana bwe nafukira ku bo nga bonna bali wamu n'omutima gumu mu kifo kimu. Amangu ago ne waba eddoboozi erivudde mu ggulu, ng'empewo ey'amaanyi ekunta, ne lijjula ennyumba yonna mwe baali batudde. Ne walabika ennimi ezaayawukanamu, ng'omuliro, ne gutuula ku buli omu ku bo. Ne bajuluzibwa omwoyo omutukuvu, ne batandika okwogera mu nnimi endala, nga omwoyo bwe yabawa okwogera. Bwe batyo abaana bange abawere mu Kristo abaali baakazaalibwa obulamu obuggya ne baweebwa okwogera okutuukirivu mu nnimi ze baali tebategeera nga boogera n'obuvumu ebikolwa eby'ekitalo ebya Yahweh!

Kale okwo kwe kuzaalibwa kw'Ekkanisa yange era bwe zityo n'ensuubizo ezikikwatako, nti mazima ddala, ddala, emiryango gy'emagombe tegiriwangula Kkanisa yange ey'amazima era tegiriwangula! Newankubadde abangi baliva ku kukkiriza mu butali bwesigwa obwavanga mu mitima gyabwe egy'obutakkiriza bwe baayogeranga nti Yesu ye Mukama mu butakkiriza obw'obutakkiriza, era ne bagaana okukkiriza nti ddala nazuukizibwa mu bafu! Era okwatula nti Yesu ye Mukama, gye bataali beenenyezza kibi wadde okukyusa amakubo gaabwe amabi, naye ne balondawo obuzibe bw'amaaso obwaleetebwa enfumo z'Abayesuyi ezonoonye Ekikristaayo ne zifuuka ebidibyo, era mu mazima ag'emirembe gyonna Kristo tayawukanamu, era Yahweh tasekererwa! N'olwekyo, tegeera nti omwoyo gw'obwana gugimibwa abo abajja eri Yesu Omunazaaleeti nga tebalina bwesigwa era nga tebalina kukkiriza. Kale nno mbuuza nti, okwatula okw'okukkiriza okulokola kuyinza kutya okuva ku mimwa egigattiddwa ku mitima egitalina kukkiriza? Naye, eyo ye nnaku y'ebiseera byammwe omuntu ow'obutonde bw'alina okulondawo okufuna okununulibwa okujjuvu n'okusaasirwa okujjuvu okw'ebibi n'agaana, n'alondawo obubi n'omusango gwe ogw'emirembe gyonna. Era kino kubanga abantu abasinga obungi ab'obutonde, era abantu abatali na mwoyo gwange omutukuvu ogw'obwana, bagaana okukkiriza nti bali mu mbeera y'ekibi era eyonooneddwa, era n'olwekyo bayimirira mu malala ag'obwewaggulu nga beewaana nti ebikolwa byabwe ebitagasa bituukirivu, era ebyo ebyogerwa tebiriyimirira mu misango egigenda okujja, wadde okusiimibwa mu maaso gange kubanga tewali muntu ayinza kujja eri Yahweh, Nze Aliwo Omukulu, okuggyako ng'ayise mu nze, Yesu Omunazaaleeti!

Omusango gugwa! Omusango gugwa! Omusango gugwa! Laba! Omusango gugwa ku Babulooni ow'Ekyama, era nga bwe kyasuubizibwa, era kubanga okulabula kwange okungi kwanyoomwa, era kubanga Ebigambo byange ebyatumibwa ababaka bange n'abannabbi bange okulokola abo kati abalonzeewo omusango n'okuzikirizibwa kwabwe kwennyini mu bujeemu obw'obutawulira byasuulibwa ku bbali ng'ebisasiro ebitagasa. Bwe kityo bwe kyawandiikibwa, era bwe kityo bwe kigenda okutuukirira: Era amayembe ekkumi ge walaba ku nsolo, gano galikyawa omwenzi, era galimufuula matongo, era galimwambula, era galirya ennyama ye, era galimwokya n'omuliro. Kubanga Katonda yakiteeka mu mitima gyabwe okutuukiriza by'ayagala, n'okukkiriza, n'okuwa obwakabaka bwabwe ensolo, okutuusa ebigambo bya Katonda lwe birituukirira.

Bwe kityo bwe kiri nti omusango gugwa ku Babulooni ow'Ekyama, n'eby'essanyu bye biggyibwawo mu musango. Era mu bbanga ttono n'ekala, kirijja okutuukirira ddala nga bwe kyawandiikibwa Yokaana Omubikkulirwa eyawandiika n'obwesigwa Ebigambo ebyaweebwa omukono gwange: Kubanga mu ssaawa emu obugagga obungi bw'otyo bufuuse matongo. N'olwekyo, obugagga n'eby'essanyu biggyibwa ku nsi era tebiridda okutuusa obwakabaka bwange obw'emyaka olukumi lwe bulijja era kino kikolebwa mu musango n'okubonerezebwa ku abo abeegombanga eby'okulya bino nga bagenderera nti bayinza okwenenya bwe bakyukira n'okukkiriza okujjuvu mu mitima gyabwe Yesu Omunazaaleeti nga bakoowoola erinnya lyange ku lw'obulamu bwabwe okufuna obulokozi okuva mu nsi esaliddwa omusango era efa ddala, ddala ejja okuzikirizibwa n'okusalirwa omusango era tewali n'omu alirokoka okuggyako okuyita mu kisa n'okusaasira kwa Yahweh okuweereddwa mu maaso ga Kristo Yesu Kabaka ow'Obutuukirivu eyazuukizibwa n'alinnyira mu ggulu!

Naye, Yahweh mwesigwa kubanga yeetikka endagaano ne Nuuwa nti taliddayo kuzikiriza nsi na mataba, naye nti ensi eriggyibwawo n'omuliro mu musango ogw'enkomerero ogutegekeddwa okugwa ddala okutandikira ku nnyumba ya Katonda. Kubanga ne mu biseera bino eby'omusango ogw'enkomerero, ekisa n'okusaasira n'obulokozi birifunibwa ekibiina ky'ebibiina ebiriraba ensobi y'amakubo gaabyo era ebirikoowoola erinnya lya Yesu okufuna obulokozi bwabyo; ne Isirayiri alikkiriza Yeshua HaMashiach nga Masiya waabwe ow'amazima era abangi baliweebwa okuyingira mu bwakabaka bwange obw'emyaka olukumi omulirabika obutuukirivu bwokka. Kubanga nze Kabaka w'Obutuukirivu era mu butuukirivu nzijja okusala omusango n'okulwana!

N'olwekyo, tegeera nti Yahweh muliro ogwokya byonna, era nze Kristo Yesu atudde n'ekiwujjo mu ngalo nga nkuuma ennyennyi z'omuliro gw'okulongoosebwa eby'obugagga bya Yahweh (be bantu be) birongoosebwe ebisasiro byonna biryoke biyinze okuyimirira mu maaso ga Mukama waabyo ow'emirembe gyonna n'Omulokozi nga tebyambadde bikolwa byabyo eby'obutuukirivu obutaliimu, naye biriyimirira mu maaso gange nga byambadde bafuta enjeru, etaliiko bbala kubanga baakkiriza obutuukirivu obwaweebwa omukono gwange mu Ndagaano Empya gye nnaakozesa okufuga ensi mu butuukirivu, era Endagaano ey'obutuukirivu eteriggwaawo. Era abo abakkiriza obulokozi mu ngeri eno kiribagambibwako nti mazima tebaagala bulamu bwabwe okutuuka ku nkomerero naye balironzeewo okuva mu nsi eno embi mu kitiibwa ky'okufiirira okukkiriza, era mu kitiibwa kye nneeweerayo ng'omwana gw'endiga gwa Katonda n'okutuusa bonna abalonda Yesu Kristo ng'omulokozi waabwe balyoke babeerewo emirembe gyonna mu butuukirivu mu maaso ga Yahweh NZE ALIWO OMUKULU! N'olwekyo, tegeera nti omuweereza tasinga mukama we.

Bwe kityo bwe kiri nti omusango gutandikira ku nnyumba ya Katonda n'okubonaabona kwa Yakobo kutegekeddwa okubuna ensi yonna, amangu ago abeesigwa mu Kristo nga bamaze okukung'aanyizibwa, olwo obubonero bulisumululwa, n'amakondeere galifuuwa n'obusungu bw'Omwana gw'Endiga buligwa ddala ku nsi kubanga Empologoma y'Ekika kya Yuda, omulandira gwa Dawudi, ewangudde okubikkula ekitabo, n'okusumulula obubonero bwakyo omusanvu mu kuwuliza okutuukirivu okw'okwagala n'enteekateeka y'emirembe gyonna ey'obulokozi eyaweebwa Yahweh mu Kyama kya Katonda eky'abo aba Adamu abalikkiriza obulokozi obutuukirivu n'omusango gw'aboonoonyi abaliggibwawo ku maaso g'ensi mu misango egy'obutuukirivu egigenda okujja.

Kubanga amazima ddala singa abalangira b'ensi eno baali bategeera Ekyama Ekikulu ekya Katonda kye nabikkulira omutume Pawulo bwe namuyigiriza, era n'awandiika bulungi olw'okuwuliza obutuukirivu eri bonna abatukuzibwa nakyo: nti abalangira b'ensi eno tebandikomereranga Mukama wa kitiibwa singa baali bategeera Ekyama Ekikulu ekya Katonda. Kubanga bwe nneewaayo mu butaliiko musango mu mikono gyabwe egy'ekyayiro, mu bujeemu bwabwe obw'ekyayiro baawaayo ssaddaaka ey'omwana gw'endiga gwa Katonda ye musaayi ogutalina musango! Bwe batyo ne banunula Adamu ddala eri abo bonna abalondawo okukoowoola erinnya lyange okufuna obulokozi bwabwe, bwe batyo ne bawona okuvunda okw'emirembe gyonna mu musaayi gwa Adamu ogwaleetebwa omusota mu Llusuku Edeni ebbanga lino lyonna. Naye bwe kityo bwe kiri, nti Basetaani mu kwegomba kwabwe okw'ekyayiro n'obutamanya baayamba okuleetawo obulokozi bwa Adamu gwe baagala okuzikiriza okuva ku maaso g'ensi! Okuggyako nti baawangulwa ddala ebikolwa byabwe mu butamanya bwabwe obw'amagezi ga Yahweh ag'emirembe gyonna, era NZE ALIWO OMUKULU, gwe baagala okuwangula n'obukujjukujju, era ekyo ekitaliiko nsa n'ekitayinza kubaawo kubanga Yahweh akwata abakujjukujju mu musango gw'emirembe gyonna gwe bataliyonoonako.

N'olwekyo, tegeera nti nze nkuuma ennyennyi z'omuliro gw'okulongoosa bonna abajja eri Yahweh mu maaso ga Yesu Kristo bayinze okufuna obulokozi ddala era bayinze okujja gyendi okutondebwa buto ddala mu biseera byabwe n'entegeka. Bwe kityo bwe kiri nti ekiseera kituuse okukung'aanya abeesigwa mu Kristo, era ebibala ebisooka okuva mu bafu, era okukungula kwa ssayiri okwo okunene okugenda okubaawo amangu ago kati kubanga ebiseera by'amakungula bituuse mu nnimiro ya Yahweh ey'ekisa omuliyinza okukungulirwa amakungula amangi ag'obutuukirivu mu mawanika ga Yahweh n'ebisusunku birisibwa ne byokebwa emirembe gyonna mu miriro egitazikira egy'okubonerezebwa, bw'atyo bw'agamba Mukama w'Amakungula!

Bwe kityo bwe kiri, mu kiseera eky'okuwandiika kuno okukung'aanyizibwa okunene okw'abeesigwa mu Kristo tekunnabaawo era bangi ku beesigwa bange bafuba obutasuubula ssuubi mu kulabika kwa Mukama waabwe okuli okumpi olw'obutagumiikiriza n'ebikemo ebingi. N'olwekyo, nze, Kristo Yesu, mbawa okutegeera nti okukung'aanyizibwa kw'abeesigwa mu Kristo kutambula dda era tekuyinza kuyimirizibwa wadde okulwazibwa, era n'olwekyo tegeera nti ekyo ekyalangirirwa n'ekyateekebwawo mu ntebe ey'obwakabaka n'amaanyi ga Yahweh tekiyinza kukyusibwa, wadde okukyusibwa mu katono n'akatono era kirijja okutuukirira ddala nga bwe kyalagulwa ne mu kiseera ekituufu ddala ekyalondebwa Yahweh yennyini, kubanga kyonna Yahweh ky'ateekawo okutuukiriza kijja kutuukirira mu butuukirivu obujjuvu kubanga bwe butyo bwe buli ekitiibwa kya Yahweh, NZE ALIWO OMUKULU!

Muve mu bo mweyawule, era mbajjukiza nate kubanga kiseera kya kuva ku by'ensi eno embi kubanga okugenda kwammwe kuli kumpi. Mbajjukiza mubeere mu kiseera kyammwe ekisigaddeyo, ekibuuka, mu butuukirivu mulyoke mube beetegefu eri eddoboozi lya Mukama wammwe ndyoke mbalagire mu kuweereza muliraanwa wammwe gwe mulina okwagala, era mu ngeri y'emu nga bwe mweyagala mwekka mulyoke musige ensigo z'obutuukirivu mu mitima gyabwe era muleete abasembera ku lyato olw'oluggi oluggule olwo olw'omu ggulu nga terunnaggalawo mu musango. Mbajjukiza mubeere mu kiseera ekisigaddeyo mu butuukirivu mulyoke mube beetegefu eri eddoboozi lya Mukama wammwe era mulyoke muwulire olwogoolo olw'amaanyi ekkondeere erisembayo lwe lirifuuwa okukung'aanya abeesigwa mu Kristo. Era okulabika kwange kulirabibwa ne kumanyibwa mu nsi yonna kubanga ng'okumyansa bwe kuva ebuvanjuba, ne kulabikira ebugwanjuba, bwe kutyo n'okujja kw'Omwana w'omuntu bwe kuliba. Naye bwe kityo bwe kiriba bwe nneebikkulira Pawulo ku luguudo olugenda e Damasiko nti abo abalikkirizibwa mu ggulu baliwulira ekkondeere era balitegeera eddoboozi lyalyo era abangi baliraba ekitiibwa kyange nga kibuna mu ggulu. Naye, abasinga obungi tebalisobola kwawula lwogoolo olwo olw'amaanyi era tebalitegeera bigambo ebiweereddwayo, era balisigala.

Kale mbawa okutegeera nti mu kiseera eky'okulabika kwange okumasamasa kw'ekitiibwa kyange kulijjula eggulu n'ekitiibwa kyange kirirabika n'ensi erimanya n'etegeera akaseera nti Kristo Yesu alabise kubanga abeesigwa mu Kristo balitwalibwa mu kaseera katono mu bulinzi obw'omu ggulu gye baliyimirira mu maaso ga Mukama waabwe, era nga batondebwa buto mu butuukirivu obw'emirembe gyonna! N'abo abalisigala ennyuma abaali bakakafu nti balikkirizibwa ku kulabika kwange balikaaba, balikaaba, era baligwa mu nnaku nnyingi ennyingi. Bwe kityo, ng'obuzito bw'embeera yaabwe n'ebiseera byabwe eby'omu maaso bwe bibatuukako. Olwo bangi ku bo balyewaayo ddala okukoowoola erinnya lyange k'ebe ki era n'okukoowoola erinnya lyange okutuusa ku nkomerero, era balizuukizibwa mu kitiibwa ekitaliiko ngeri ya kunnyonnyola kubanga obuzito bw'empeera yaabwe ey'emirembe gyonna tebusaana kugeraageranyizibwa na kubonaabona okufunibwa mu nsi eno embi, bw'atyo bw'agamba Mukama wammwe ow'emirembe gyonna eyeewanika ku muti ku lulwo.

Bwe kityo, ndibateekamu obumalirivu obutayinza kumenyeka okugumiikiriza ebigezo n'okugezesebwa ebiriba bituuse ddala ku nsi, era ebigezo n'okugezesebwa ebikyali mu busibe n'obubonero. Naye, baliko ebikolwa bingi eby'amaanyi eby'okuweereza n'okwagala muntu munnaabwe mu biseera bino era ndibawa eby'amagero bingi eby'amaanyi okufuna obulokozi bwabwe era abangi baligumiikiriza ebigezo n'ekitiibwa ky'okufiirira okukkiriza mu biseera bino, bwe batyo ne bafuna obusika n'empeera ey'omugaso ennyo emirembe gyonna etannayingira ddala mu mitima gy'abantu mu kiseera kino. Bwe ntyo ngamba bonna abalisoma Ebigambo bino eby'Enjiri ey'Emirembe gyonna obutatunula ku kkono wadde ku ddyo naye muteeke essira lyammwe ku Yesu Kristo alibawa amaanyi okugumiikiriza abalala bangi balyoke bajje mu bulokozi okuva mu kyokulabirako kyammwe n'obujulizi bwammwe.

Wabula, bwe kityo bwe kiriba nti ogusota, n’ensolo eva mu nnyanja, n’ensolo eva mu ttaka (oyo era ye nnabbi ow’obulimba) bonna baligolokokera mu kiseera kyabyo, ne bwe biriba bisumuluddwa omugiziiyizi okukola ebyo ebyabiweereddwa okutuukiriza mu kaseera akatono ke birina, era tebiriddamu kugolokokera mu ngeri eyo. Era mu bulimba obw’amaanyi bw’alyerangirira ng’azukkidde mu bafu, kubanga ensolo eva mu ttaka yalirimba nti efudde ekiwundu ku mutwe gwayo era ekikangabwa ekinene kirivaamu, nga bwe kyalagulwa edda Yahweh mu Mwana gw'endiga alina amayembe abiri. Mutegeere nti biryekweka mu ntalo yaabyo ey’akabi gye birwana kati egenda okusumululwa ddala omugiziiyizi bw’aliggibwawo era olutalo lwabyo lulituukirizibwa n’okuzikiriza kwonna olutalo lwabyo lwe lulireeta. Kubanga mu mivuyo egy’olubeerera mulivaamu entegeka yaabyo empya era ensolo eva mu ttaka eririmba n’egamba nti yazuukidde mu bafu olw’okulokola Isiraeri n’okulongoosa ensi okuva mu bubi, era obubi obwo bwennyini bwe birireeta.

Kyokka bwe ntyo mbawa okutegeera obulimba obuleetebwa ensolo zino embi muleme okugwa mu mutego gwabwo. N’olwekyo, mutegeere nti abo abagwa beeyambisa abantu okubazaala nga omulunzi bw’azaala ente. Batabula ne bagatta ne bakola emizizo gyabwe ne beerangirira nti ba katonda olw’okutabula ensigo etateekwa kutabulwa, kubanga Yahweh yatonda ebiramu ng’engeri yaabyo bw’eri era kyali kigendererwa kya Yahweh bulijjo okutonda ensolo ey’oku ttaka okusinziira ku ngeri yaayo, n’ente okusinziira ku ngeri yaazo okusobola okukuuma obulongoofu n’ekitiibwa kya buli kitonde Yahweh ky’atondedde mu kwagala kwe okw’obutuukirivu eri ebitonde bye. Kyokka, abo abagwa boonoona enkula zonna ez’ebitonde bya Yahweh nga banoonya okutabula, okugatta, n’okukoppa ebika n’ebika, bulyo beerimbise nti ba katonda, wabula bonna balimba abeerimbisa.

N’olwekyo, mutegeere nti abo abagwa be banoonya okwetabula n’ezzadde ly’abantu naye tebaligattibwako, nga bwe kitasoboka ekyuma okutabulwa n’ebbumba. Mutegeere nti abo abagwa bakulaakulanyizza tekinologiya n’obukodyo bingi eby’obulimba okufuga n’okulimbalimba abo ababeera ku nsi, era n’abo bonna abasanyukira obutali butuukirivu. Mutegeere nti bakulaakulanyizza emizizo mingi egikolebwa okuzikiriza omuntu ow’obutonde okuva ku nsi. Mutegeere nti abalabi baddamu okukola emizizo gyabwe, era mutegeere nti abo abagwa bayize okukoppa n’okukola ebifaananyi by’obulamu obwafuuweebwa mu nnyindo z’omuntu kubanga omuntu yatondebwa mu kifaananyi kya Yahweh.

N’olwekyo, mutegeere nti omuzizo gwonna ogukoleddwa abagwa tegukoleddwa mu kifaananyi kya Yahweh naye ge gakoppa obulamu, era tegukoleddwa mu bujjuvu okuva mu ngeri ya Adamu, era n’olwekyo, tekiyinza kununulibwa wadde okutuuka ku bulokozi era kigendererwamu okuzikiriza Adamu mu bujjuvu era n’emizizo gyonna egikoleddwa mu ngeri y’emu gisibiddwa okutuukiriza okwegomba kwa Bakitaabwe bye bajja okukola.
