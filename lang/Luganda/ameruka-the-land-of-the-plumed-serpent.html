AmeruKa: Ensi y'Omusota Omuwaawaatiro / Beera Mwetegefu, Nzijja!

Muwulire ebigambo bya Yeshua HaMashiach, Oyo ali era Kristo Yesu, era mutegeere olulimi lwange, mulyoke mutegeere eby'ekyama bya Babulooni ku ntandikwa yakyo ey'ukuri, obuzaale bwakyo, n'ekigendererwa kyakyo, kubanga mazima ddala ye nsi y'omusota omuwaawaatiro, ye AmeruKa, ensi eyeseerera mu bunkenke n'erya abagituulamu n'etta abataliiko musango n'ewaayo kitaawe ne nnyina bombi mu bungi obungi okuva mu biro eby'edda, okutuukira ddala ne mu biro bya kaakano. Ddala ensi ekolimiddwa eyonoona abagituulamu mu bunkenke n'obungi bw'emisango egyonoona era egy'obutali bulongoofu egy'emikolo egisigaddewo egy'omusaayi n'ebikolwa eby'obukambwe ebya ssaddaaka eziziikiddwa mu nsi eno eyonooneddwa mu ntuumo nnyingi ez'ebifaananyi, emikolo, okwokya kwa Tofesi, n'omusaayi ogukulukuta ennyo, omusaayi ogwo ogwanyikizza ettaka lyayo, ettaka we likaalabirira obwenkanya olw'omusaayi ogwayiika, era ogukyayiika mu bungi. Mazima ddala ensi ezo zonna ezonooneddwa zikaabira nnyo era mu ddoboozi eddene eri eggulu okusaba obwenkanya mu kiseera kino, n'ensi bw'etenyiganyiga ennyo ng'omutamiivu anaatera okugwa, nga yeegayirira omusango gw'obutuukirivu ogutayonooneka.

Naawe Babulooni ow'Ekyama, ee, ggwe: Oli lukalu olutali lutukuvu era olwonooneddwa, ddala ensi eyatuulwamu oluvannyuma okuva mu nnaku za Nuuwa, ensi eyawerebwa Heylel Ben Shachar n'amakubo g'omusota, ddala ensi enyikiddwa mu musaayi gw'abataliiko musango olw'emikolo n'ebikolwa by'ezzadde lino eryonoonefu ery'emisota n'aboonoonyi abaagenda okwonoona mu biro eby'edda. Ddala ensi eyakwekebwa mu bunkenke emikono gy'abo abaagwa egitereke, ddala eyaterekebwa n'egendererwamu ng'Atlantis yaabwe empya, ddala eggwanga eryategekebwa n'eliweebwayo okugulumiza ensolo evva mu nnyanja n'ensolo evva mu nsi. Ddala eggwanga eryefuula eriri Bukristaayo, naye mu mazima liri ggwanga lya basitaani ab'ekyama abaateekawo era ne bawummulira mirembe mu bitongole byalyo ebyonooneddwa era ebyonoona okuva ku mutono okutuuka ku mukulu era bwe buno obubinja obw'ekyama obwawerebwa Heylel Ben Shachar (gwe bayita mu bukyamu n'obutamanya Lucifer), nga beeyita mu bukyamu nti baaka era bamulise, ddala mu kulimbibwa kwabwe n'obukuusa bwabwe mwe basibiddwa olubeerera mu birayiro byabwe ebitatukuvu emirembe gyonna, nga balimba, ate nga n'abalala babalimba abo be basibiddwako emirembe gyonna ng'abaana b'ekibi.

Kimanyibwe nti Babulooni w'Ekyama ye United States Of America, eya प्राचीन atera okuyitibwa Ameru-Ca, ddala ensi y'omusota omuwaawaatiro eyeseerera n'ekyusa okuba omwenzi omukulu atuula ku mazzi amangi era liri ggwanga eryakolebwa, ensolo evva mu nnyanja n'ensolo evva ku nsi bisobole okugolokoka okuva mu vvu lyalyo mu kiseera kyabyo eky'okubikkulwa. Ddala eggwanga ery'obwenzi, ddala eggwanga ery'obwamalaaya, eggwanga eriri ddala omwenzi omukulu eyeeyita omugoberezi omutuukirivu owa Yesu Omunazaalaayo. Naye mu mazima liri ggwanga abazimbi baalyo abataalina Lwazi lw'omusingi ogw'amazima, ddala ejjinja eryo essona erikulu abatakkiriza kwe beesittala, ddala Yesu Omunazaalaayo, Oyo ali era Yeshua HaMashiach. Mazima, AmeruKa liri ggwanga eryateekebwawo omukono ogwo omutali mutukuvu era ogw'ekyama, ddala ensi y'ebibiina by'ekyama ebyawerebwa omusota mu bubonero obw'ekyama, era amaanyi ga Rosy Cross bwe gaalumba AmeruKa, n'olwo olububi olw'enkomerero olwa New Atlantis ne luteekebwawo, ddala entegeka ey'edda eyakwekebwa eyandyagasse ebbiina ly'abasota mu nsi yonna, n'ezikiriza era n'ewaayo ssaddaaka bonna ab'omu kibiina kya bakabona ab'ebika ebyo ebyagwa, n'ebika byabyo ebyo byennyini ebyandyagezezzaako n'obuwagazi n'obusirusiru okulemesa omusota gwe gumu gwe baali beewaddeyo mu birayiro ebingi eby'omusaayi okuguweereza ddala, nga tebategeera buzaale bwa kitonde kyennyini kye beesibako.

Kale oli AmeruKa, kubanga njogera n'olukalu oluva mu mambuka okutuuka mu maserengeta n'okuva ebuvanjuba okutuuka ebugwanjuba oluli ddala ensi eyawerebwa omusota ogwo omuwaawaatiro ogw'edda ogwayonoona n'ogusaasaanya obucaafu mu nsi zino, n'obwakabaka bwe bwagulumira ne bugwa munda mu nsalo zaayo, ddala obwakabaka obungi obutawandiikiddwa obw'omusota, ddala eggwanga ly'omusota omuwaawaatiro ogubuuka eryawerebwa abantu abakulemberwa abakyala n'aba demokulasiya, ebibiina ebibi gye byayinza okufuga wansi w'obuyinza bwa Heylel Ben Shachar, n'ezzadde lye ery'emisota eryayonoona ensi za Babulooni ow'ekyama emirembe gyonna, eriri ddala ensi eyatuulwamu abantu bwe baatataganira ku nsi okuva eri eryato gye lyagoba ku lusozi Alalati ebbanga lyonna lino eriyiseewo. Era abantu bwe baalagirwa Yahweh, Nze Aliwo Omukulu, nga basibiddwa wansi w'endagaano ya Nuuwa okwebuna n'okusaasaana n'okuddamu okujjuza ensi mu butuukirivu obw'endagaano; ezzadde lya Haamu mu kifo ky'ekyo ne beekungaanya mu butawulize obunene okwekolera erinnya mu nsi z'e Sinaali, nga beegamba nti: mujje, twetimbire omunaala ogw'amaanyi tusobole okwetaasa, era tusobole okulwana n'eggulu kubanga Nimuloodi yeefuula omuyizzi ow'amaanyi mu maaso ga Mukama, ddala omuyizzi ow'amaanyi ow'abantu be yayigganga olw'okwesanyusa.

Bwe batyo abantu ne bajja mu nsi y'omusota omuwaawaatiro okuva ebuvanjuba nga bayita ku tindo ly'ensi erya Bering nga bwe limanyiddwa mu mulembe guno, ne bajja okuva mu nnyanja okuva ebugwanjuba nga bayita mu makubo ag'enjawulo ag'eby'obusuubuzi n'entambula ne bajja mu nsi zino ezaaterekebwa Heylel Ben Shachar olw'ebigendererwa bye ebibi, ensi ddala abaagwa gye beeteeka mu nsi eyaliwo mu kiseera ekyo, ddala ensi gye baaziikamu ebyama bingi bye balikkirizibwa okuggyayo mu bbanga ttono era mu ngeri etasuubirwa, ddala omulemesa wange bw'aliggibwawo era obufuzi bwabwe obumpi bwe bulikkirizibwa olw'okulokolebwa kwa Yakobo, era nabo basobole okugwa mu musango ogw'omuliro bo bennyini ng'ensonga eno ekkirizibwa okweyongera bo basobole okuzikirizibwa ddala, kubanga ebiseera by'abaagwa bituuse ddala era nze Kristo Yesu, ddala Empologoma ey'omu kika kya Yuda, ey'ajja okubetenta omutwe gw'omusota guno ogw'edda mu nkomerero y'omusango.

N'olwekyo, Kanani n'azuula ekyo abalabi kye baaziika, era n'akulemberwa okusinziira ku kwegomba kw'omutima gwe bwe yalumba n'abba ensi etaali yiye kugituulamu ng'amenya endagaano ya Nuuwa. Naye yeegomba ennaku ez'edda ezaazikirizibwa mu mataba amakulu mu nnaku ez'edda ensi eyaliwo mu kiseera ekyo lwe yazikirira olw'okutetenkanya kwa Setaani abalabi abaagwa okwegatta n'abakazi n'okuzaala ezzadde lyabwe erikolimiddwa, ezzadde eryo eritali kirala wabula abaana b'obwenzi n'aboonoonefu, ddala ekifaananyi ky'obulamu emizizo gyabwe gye gisibiddwa olubeerera okukola okwegomba okw'ekivve n'okuvunda okwa bakitaabwe, n'okwegomba kwa bakitaabwe kwe balina okukola. N'olwekyo, tegeera nti omuddo guno omubi, oguli emizizo emibi okuyita yonna, tegulina mmizi newaakubadde ettabi mu ggulu, ku nsi, newaakubadde wansi w'ensi era gulisiimbulibwawo ddala, olubeerera n'emirembe gyonna, okuva wansi w'enjuba okusinziira ku kiragano eky'endagaano ekya Adonai-YHVH Nze Aliwo Omukulu, era gulisibwa ne gwokebwa mu muliro ogwo omunene ogw'okuwalanirako eggwanga omusota gwagwo gwe gutafa era bonna abasuulibwa mu nnimi z'omuliro balifuna ebibi byabwe emirundi mingi mu bifuba byabwe mu musango gw'omuliro ogw'olubeerera n'ogw'obutuukirivu gwe batayinza kuddukako.

N'olwekyo, Heylel Ben Shachar, ddala omulangira omutono oyo eyawangulwa, ddala omwewulifu omukulu oyo n'omwonoonyi w'ebintu byonna ebirongoofu, n'agenda n'asiga omuddo gwe mu nnimiro yonna eya Yahweh, Nze Aliwo Omukulu, ddala ennimiro y'emu eyasimbwa Elohim bwe yagamba nti wabeewo omusana ne wabaawo omusana. Kubanga ku lubereberye, Elohim yakola eggulu n'ensi, era ensi n'efuuka etaliiko kintu era njerere ng'ekizikiza kijja ku maaso g'obuziba. Era bwe kityo Heylel Ben Shachar ne kimu kya kusatu eky'abamalayika be abaagoberera obujeemu bwe ne basuulibwa ku nsi mu nsonyi, ne mu kuwangulwa, era ye Heylel Ben Shachar, ddala omulangira omutono oyo eyawangulwa eyakaaba mu nsonyi ne mu kuwangulwa bwe yasuulibwa okuva mu ggulu ery'eggulu mu busungu obungi, ddala eyaterekebwa okusalirwa omusango ogw'enkomerero n'ogw'omuliro mu muliro gw'olunaku olwo olunene, ddala omuliro ogwo ogukolebwa n'ennimi ezisinga okwokya n'ezisinga obulongoofu ez'ebbulu, era ne gutegekebwa okwaniriza bonna abeegatta ku bujeemu bwa Setaani okuva ku mutono okutuuka ku mukulu mu bwakabaka bw'abaagwa.

Elohim n'agamba nti wabeewo omusana ne wabaawo omusana, era Elohim n'ateekawo ensi eyaddamu okuteekebwawo ku kiragiro kye kyokka n'ebigambo bye byokka n'okuteesa kwe kwokka okutondebwa kwe kwakolebwa, ne kukolebwa, ne kutondebwa ddala n'ebigambo bye eby'amaanyi n'eby'olubeerera n'amaanyi n'okuteesa kwe kwokka kubanga YHVH teyeetaaga muwi wa magezi. Tewali kitonde kifaanana nga Katonda ow'olubeerera ow'obutuukirivu, wadde nga balimba ne beefuula mu ngeri ey'ekisa. Bwe kityo ensi n'ekolebwa n'ebirimu byonna n'ensi eyo n'eweebwa Adamu, kubanga omuntu yakolerwa ensi, n'ensi n'ekolerwa omuntu, ddala eyatondebwa mu kifaananyi ky'omutonzi we, ddala YHVH - Nze Aliwo Omukulu!

Naye omusota ogwo omukujjukujju ne gulumba obusika bwa Adamu mu nkwe ze, ddala okulumba n'okumenya ebyayeddira ddala ku Kaluvaali, bwe nze, Yesu Omunazaalaayo, ne njogera ebigambo eby'olubeerera nti kiwedde era bwe kityo nagula abantu, ensi, n'ebirimu byonna mu musaayi gwange ogutangirira era ensi ya Mukama n'okujjuza kwayo, era nzijja ng'Empologoma ey'omu kika kya Yuda okusala omusango n'okulwana, kale wulira okuwuluguma kwange, ggwe atuula ku nsi kubanga ebiseera byo bituuse!

Kale abaagwa bagenze okwonoona n'okukyusa okutondebwa okuva ku butuukirivu bwakwo obw'olubereberye mwe kwatondebwa. Bo bennyini tebayinza kutonda kubanga YHVH teyabatonda na maanyi newaakubadde obusobozi obw'engeri eyo. N'olwekyo, tegeera nti abaagwa bano beewulira nti bali bakatonda so nga bonna befuula abeefuula.

N'olwekyo, muwulire ebigambo bya Yeshua HaMashiach, ddala Kristo Yesu bwe njija okubuulira Ekkanisa yange, Omubiri gwange, era n'omugole wange eby'ensi ezonooneddwa era ezisaasaanyiziddwamu obucaafu abangi ku mmwe gye mwazaalibwa era mwe mubeera, bakitammwe bwe baali batambula ku nsi nga babonerezebwa Adonai-YHVH, Nze Aliwo Omukulu, ddala oyo eyagoba ebika eby'omu mambuka mu mawanga ng'alina ebbaluwa y'okwawukana mu ngalo, era eyo, nga basaasaanye mu mawanga, we baakuumirwa era ne bateresebwa okuva olwo ddala n'omuyigiriza waabwe, kubanga baakwata amateeka ga Musa bwe baatambula ne bayingira mu nsi ez'omu mambuka bwe baawangulwa mu butawulize n'emikono gy'Abasuuli, naye ne basaasaanyizibwa Abababulooni, abo nabo ne bawangula ne babonereza abatawulize mu bajjajja bange ab'omu bika bya Yuda ne Benyamini, ddala Obwakabaka bw'obukiikaddyo obwa Yuda mu nnaku ez'edda obwafuuka Yudaaya, Idumea ne Yuda bwe byagattibwa ku buso bw'ekitala.

Bwe batyo endiga ezabula ez'ennyumba ya Isirayiri ne zisaasaanyizibwa mu mawanga mu butawulize bwazo. Obwakabaka obw'obukiikakkono bwasaasaanyizibwa mu mawanga ag'obukiikakkono mu nsi za Gogi ne Magogi, okuyita mu Bulaaya ne mu Amerika gye baagenda. Bwe batyo baganda baabwe n'ab'omu kika kyabwe ab'obwakabaka obw'obukiikaddyo ne basaasaanyizibwa mu nsi zonna mu nnaku za Tito ne Vespasian era nate mu kiseera ky'okwekalakaasa okwakulemberwa Simoni Bar Kokhba era abantu bange bangi ne basaasaanyizibwa mu nsi ez'obukiikaddyo n'ebuvanjuba bwa Yerusaalemi ne bayingira mu bukiikakkono bwa Afirika ne mu Alexandria e Misiri n'ebifo ebirala bingi gye baatambula mu kubonerezebwa kwabwe, naye baakuumibwa Musa, wadde ng'abasinga obungi bali mu busibe bwa Esawu, ne Asikenazi, era abasinga obungi ku bo balimbibwa ne balindirira Mahdi waabwe, mu kifo kya Yeshua HaMashiach, Oyo ali Masiya ow'amazima, era omulokozi wa Isirayiri eyasuubizibwa edda Adonai-YHVH, Seera.

Naye, nga bwe kyawandiikibwa bwe kiri ddala nti abantu bange abasinga obungi tebamanyi era ba muzibe ku kitundu ku by'obuntu bwabwe n'obubalanga. Ddala basimbuliddwa okuva ku Yahweh, Nze Aliwo Omukulu, omuzeyituuni ogw'obutonde gwe gugabana ku masavu g'emizzi, emizzi egyo nga gye Yehovah-YHVH, Nze Aliwo Omukulu.

Naye abantu bange baakyusibwa n'obutamanya okukyawagana mu bulimba n'obukyayi n'obutamanya obuntu bwabwe obw'amazima, ddala nga bayigiriziddwa okukkiriza obulimba bw'ezzadde lino ery'abakabona b'emisota mu ggwanga lyonna mwe beeteeka, ddala ezzadde eryo ery'abakabona b'emisota abaweereza Heylel Ben Shachar, ddala omu seraphim oyo ow'omuliro era eyagwa ategekeddwa okubonerezebwa n'omusango eby'omuliro ogw'olubeerera ogwo omusango ogusinga obukambwe gwe guterekeddwa omusota guno omubi eyeeyonoona mu bujeemu obutaliiko nsonga, ddala eyakwatibwa mu mbeera ey'okuwangulwa n'okugwa gy'ataayinza kuddamu kuvaamu.

N'olwekyo ab'omu nsi yange ne baganda bange bayigiriziddwa okukyawagana n'obulimba obungi obwakolebwa okuyigiriza abantu abangi, naye bwe kityo bwe kyawandiikibwa: Bino omukaaga Mukama by'akyawa: yee, emmeeme ye ekyawa musanvu: amaaso ag'amalala, olulimi olulimba, n'emikono egiyiwa omusaayi ogutaliiko musango, omutima ogulowooza ebikolwa ebibi, ebigere ebyanguwa okuddukira mu bubi, omujulirwa ow'obulimba ayogera obulimba, n'oyo azuukusa enkaayana mu baganda. Bwe kityo abaana b'obwakabaka obw'omu ggulu bayigiriziddwa mu bunkenke, mu bukuusa, ne mu bukyayi bungi nti bo bennyini be basiba baganda baabwe mu busibe mu nnyanja Atalantiki, ddala ekikolwa ekyakolebwa Esawu ne Asikenazi nga baweereza Heylel Ben Shachar, gye bavunaanira abaana b'Obwakabaka obw'omu Ggulu olw'ebibi by'omuddo ogwabba abaana okuva mu nsi n'okuva eri bakitaabwe ne bannyaabwe abaatundibwa mu bukyayi obungi ab'omu kitundu kyabwe ne b'omu kika kyabwe mu mikono gy'abantu ababi mu biro eby'edda. Kale tegeera nti ebikolwa bino ebibi biwandiikiddwa mu butuukirivu mu ggulu era obwenkanya n'okuwalanirako eggwanga birigwa butereevu ku batuuze b'ensi abakyanoonya okuzikiriza Yakobo, wadde nga kwe kuzikirizibwa kwabwe okunaabagwako butereevu n'obutasuubirwa kaakano, Seera.

Naye, abasinga obungi ku mmwe musigala nga temumanyi kubanga langi y'eddiba lyammwe ya njawulo ku y'ab'oluganda lwammwe olw'obukodyo bwa Yahweh n'okutegeka kwe okulungi, naye tewali kitonde kirala ku nsi ekikyawa bwe kityo olw'obulimba bw'omusota mu bitonde byonna ebya Elohim, naye abantu bange beemalira okusibibwa n'obusirusiru obw'engeri eyo n'obulimba obutaliiko nkomerero obw'obusirusiru obutaliiko nkomerero obuweereddwa abaagwa bennyini olw'okuzikiriza abantu bange. Mwenenye ku busirusiru obw'engeri eyo kaakano, kubanga temulina budde!

Naye, abasinga obungi ku mmwe musigala nga temumanyi nti mu Kristo Yesu tewali Muyudaaya newaakubadde Mugeenti, newaakubadde muddu, newaakubadde wa ddembe wabula kitonde kipya mu Kristo, ddala kitonde kipya ebiwandiiko by'obuzzukulu bwa Adamu eby'edda gye biggibwawo olubeerera n'omwoyo ogwo ogw'Obwana, kubanga ebisuubizo ebyakolebwa Ibulayimu omwesigwa birisikizibwa abatuukirivu ababeera n'okukkiriza okumu n'okwa Ibulayimu omwesigwa, kati okuweereddwa ddala mu maaso ga Yesu Kristo mu kuzaalibwa okupya, naye abasinga obungi ku mmwe mulonda butamanya mu makubo gammwe amatanu agatali gange, era ge mulina okwenenya, kubanga nabalagira okwagala Katonda n'omutima gwammwe gwonna n'emmeeme yammwe yonna, n'amagezi gammwe gonna, n'amaanyi gammwe gonna; n'okwagala muliraanwa wo nga bwe weeyagala. Ekiragiro abangi ku mmwe kye mutakkiriza mu bukyayi bwammwe obw'obulango obw'ekika ky'emu ekya Adamu abali n'erangi ey'enjawulo mu bulungi bw'okutegeka kwa Yahweh. Mwenenye!

Era bwe kityo mu nnaku ez'edda, olulyo lwa Kanani ne lweyongera okweyonoona nga lwegatta n'abaagwa, ddala mu nnaku ez'edda, era bwe baali beeyonoona mu kibi ne beewaayo ddala eri abaagwa eyo mu nsi z'e Sinaali ddala mu kisiikirize ky'omunaala ogwo omunene ogwa Babbeli, ddala omunaala YHVH gwe yamenya mu kuwangulwa. Kubanga bwe kityo bwe kyawandiikibwa: Ensi bwe yamala okujjula amazzi mu nnaku za Nuuwa, ensi n'egabanyizibwamu akalulu k'obusika mu baana ba Nuuwa mu nnaku za Pelegi, bwe kityo Yafeesi n'agenda mu busika bwe mu nsi ez'obukiikakkono. Era abantu baali ba lulimi lumu, era abaana ba Haamu ne bakungaana mu nsi eyafuuka Babulooni. Era abalangira ba Nimuloodi ne bateesa wamu ne Puti, Mizulayimu, Kusi, ne Kanani n'amaka gaabwe okuzimba ekibuga kyabwe ne munda mwakyo omunaala ogw'amaanyi ddala omunaala omuwanvu basobole okulwanira ku ggulu era beekungaanye basobole okwekolera erinnya mu bujeemu bw'ekiragiro kya Yahweh eky'okuzaala n'okwala basobole okuddamu okujjuza ensi. Naye bwe zityo engeri z'omuntu ow'obutonde atalina kukkiriza, oyo mu kwegomba kwe n'amalala aweereza ekibi era n'olwekyo aweereza Kitaawe w'ekibi; ddala omulyolyomi, okuyita mu butawulize bw'omuntu eri Yahweh olw'ekibi ekiri mu mubiri gw'omuntu ow'obutonde.

Era okuva mu zzadde lya Nimuloodi, eyakula mu bubi, kigambibwa nti okuva mu mubi muva obubi bungi. Bw’atyo Nimuloodi n’ayonoona ensi mu makubo g’abo abaagwa, era okuva mu balogo be n’abafumu be mwajja amasomero g’ekyama amakadde, abalogo b’abaagwa mwe baayigiririza amakubo gaabwe agatalina mugaso eri abo abalisuulibwa mu muliro emirembe gyonna mu bibi bya Basetaani, era balyegatta nabo mu busika bwabwe obw’emirembe n’emirembe. Nimuloodi n’agolokoka mu bwakabaka obwasooka oluvannyuma lw’amataba ga Nuuwa, era Nimuloodi n’afuga ensi mu butali butuukirivu; okufuga okwo kwe kunoonya abaagwa okuba nakwo ku nsi yonna, naye tebaliba na mulembe gwa zaabu wabula omusango omukakali.

N’olwekyo Yakuwa mu musango gwe, n’aleetera ensi okwasamuka n’emira ekimu kya kusatu eky’omunaala gwabwe, n’omuliro ne guva mu ggulu ne gwokya ekimu kya kusatu ekirala eky’omunaala, era ekimu kya kusatu ne kikkirizibwa okusigalawo ng’ekijjukizo ku bo. N’olwekyo, erinnya lyagwo ne liyitibwa Babbuli, kubanga eyo Yakuwa gye yataataganyiriza olulimi lw’ensi yonna: era okuva eyo Yakuwa n’abasaasaanya ku maaso g’ensi yonna. Era amawanga amangi n’abantu ne beegaana ne beerabira Yakuwa eyakola endagaano ne Nuuwa, ne beerabira endagaano Nuuwa gye yakola ne Yakuwa okusinziira ku busika bwabwe n’ennimi ze baayogera, ne bakyukira amakubo g’abaagwa kubanga tewali kirala kyakukola.
