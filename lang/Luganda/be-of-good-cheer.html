Obujulizi obulongo: Ekkanisa
Mu Biwandiiko bya Peetero
Gatonnya 10, 2024

Mube n’Essanyu!

Bana bange abato, mube n’essanyu; ssinga ssaawangula omubi ne mbakombola n’omusaayi gwange mwennyini? Lwaki mwanaggwaamu amaanyi? Lwaki emitima gyammwe mizito; mulowooza nabalekerera? Nedda, mu kifo ky’okwesiga okutegeera kwammwe, munoonye amagezi gange, agava waggulu; kubanga lwa magezi ga kikatonda lwe mulikulemberwa, n’olwekyo temugasuulanga! Kale amagezi tegeeyongera kulekaanira mu nguudo? N’olwekyo temutyanga era mube n’essanyu, kubanga nawangula era nammwe mujja kuwangula, era n’omusaayi gwange mwennyini. Mukkirize emirembe gyange gibagumye emitima gyammwe era mukkirize ebigambo byange bisanyuse emyoyo gyammwe, kubanga ndi wamu nammwe.

Ani alibawula ku kwagala kwange? Kabi, oba kitala, oba njala biribawula? Ngamba nti nedda, era mutunuulire mumanye nga muli banywevu mu nze. Mumanye nga nabateekako akabonero ka Kitange, era akabonero ako tekasobola kumenyebwa oba okubaggyibwako, n’olwekyo musigale mu nze, Mukama wammwe, amaanyi gammwe, era omununuzi wammwe. Laba! Nabagula n’omusaayi gwange mwennyini, nabayita mannya, nabateekako akabonero okusinziira ku kukkiriza kwammwe mu linnya lyange, era buli yenna akkiririza omwo alirokolebwa olw'okukkiriza, kubanga omutuukirivu aliba mulamu olw'okukkiriza! Mukuumenga essuubi lyammwe nga ddamu era libayongeremu amaanyi, era mwongere okugumiikiriza ku kwagala kwammwe n’okukkiriza, kubanga mwalokolerwa essuubi. Essuubi eddene n’okusuubira okungi bibammwe, kubanga nze mbilangiridde.

Lwaki mwekubagiza era munakuwavu? Laba! Nze nawangula, era mu bbanga ttono mujja kuba nange, era mujja kundaba n’amaaso gammwe, era mujja kumanya nga bwe mumanyiddwa.

Mutunuulire! Bw’atyo bw’ayogera Mukama omukulu, Mukama ow'amaanyi ali Mukama wa bakama. Mundabireko mu kunakuwala kwammwe ne mu bizibu byammwe. Mmanyi emitima gyammwe, era mmanyi bulungi nti munjagala era mwagala nnyo okuba nange nga nze bwe njagala okuba nammwe; kyokka temuzirika. Mumbulire kino, akaseera akatono akasigaddeyo mu kulindirira kwammwe—kaligerageranyizibwa n’emirembe n’emirembe nga tuli wamu, omugole wange? Nedda, okubonaabona kwammwe n'ebibatuukako ku nsi tekigwana kugeraageranyizibwa na kitiibwa kye ŋŋenda okubikkula mu mmwe, kubanga mujja kuba nga bwe ndi, era mujja kundaba nga bwe ndi, n’olwekyo emitima gyammwe tegeeraliikiriranga. Mutegeerenga ebiseera byammwe era mubeerenga beetegefu bulijjo, kubanga okulabika kwange kujja kubaawo mangu ddala, era kujja kusinga okusuubira kwammwe.

Laba, baana bange! Ensi ejja kumanya nti mbayagala era nti muli bange, era Setaani ajja kuleetawo obulimba obunene okulimba abo ababula, naye tebujja kusemberera gye muli. Nedda, omusango si gwammwe, so n’obusungu bwange si bwammwe. Mutegeere, baana bange, nti mu nze, muli batuukirivu, si batuukirivu mu bikolwa byammwe, naye batuukirivu mu butuukirivu bwe nabayambaza. Temwesuulanga byambalo bye nabayambaza, era temuzikirizanga mwoyo gwange. Newankubadde biseera bya nkomerero, ndifuka omwoyo gwange, era batabani bammwe ne bawala bammwe baliragula, n’abakadde bammwe baliroota ebirooto. Gukyaliwo omulimu okukolebwa, era okwolesebwa kwange okw’enkomerero mu biseera bino eby’ekisa kulisinga olunaku lwa Pentekooti bwe nnasooka okufuka ku balonde bange omwoyo gwe nnaweebwa Kitange. Emirimu egy’ekitalo giri ku nkomerero y'engalo zammwe era bangi abakyasigaddeyo okulokoka, n’olwekyo mukole omulimu gwange era muweereze abaana bange era munoonye endiga zange ezabula. Munsabe ndibawa obubaka, kubanga nabasobozesa n’obusobozi obungi era nagaba ebirabo eri abantu. Ani ayinza okubayimirirawo, newankubadde mulina eky’obugagga kino mu bibumbe eby’ebbumba? Lwaki mukyambuusabuusa? Mu bunafu bwammwe mwe mutuukiririzibwa amaanyi gange. N’olwekyo, mwembiikiriremu mu mulimu gwa Mukama, baana bange.

Mwekebejje Ekigambo kyange Ekitukuvu, kubanga nga bwe nnazuukiza abafu, nammwe bwe mutyo musobola okukola. Mweteeke eddagala mu maaso gammwe mulyoke mulabe okuwonya okw’amaanyi okungi kwe nnakola okusumulula abasibe bwe nnajja eri abange; era nammwe bwe mutyo musobola okukola. Ssaagamba nti oyo anzikiririza, emirimu egyo naye aligikola, era emirimu egisingako aligikola kubanga nagenze eri Kitange era Kitammwe, n’eri Katonda wange era Katonda wammwe? Muli bakabaka era bakabona mu maaso gange era mutudde mu bifo eby’omu ggulu, n’olwekyo lwaki mukyabuusabuusa? Mube n’okukkiriza ng’akaweke ka kaladaali, kubanga kasigo katono nnyo akavaamu omuti omunene, era nammwe bwe muliba bwe mutyo bwe muneeyongera okuba abanywevu mu nze.

Lwaki mutabulatabula ebigambo byange ne mubuusa amaaso obunyirivu mu njogera yange? Silimba: musobola okukola ekyo kye nabatuma okutuukiriza bwe munaasigala nga muli beesigwa. Mwogere Ekigambo kyange n’obuyinza obungi n’obuvumu. Mugambe ensozi ezo nti, Musuulibwe mu nnyanja! Ebigambo byange bwa mazima era bwa bulamu, n’olwekyo temukkirizanga bulimba bwa basumba abakyamye abagamba nti temusobola kukola kye mbagamba. Muyige essomo. Mwogera kuwonya balwadde? Mulagire obulwadde buveewo mu linnya lyange, era mwogere ebigambo n’okukkiriza mu nze n’okukkiriza mu bigambo bye ndibawa. Mwogere kibeewo, kubanga obulamu n’okufa biri mu buyinza bw’olulimi; nga ziri n’amaanyi enlimi zammwe bwe mwogera amaanyi ga Katonda ne mulagira ebitasoboka.

Mwogere n’obuvumu. Mwogere n’obwesige. Mwogere mu kukkiriza, nga temubuusabuusa kyonna; kubanga nabatuma era nabawa amaanyi. Lwaki mugoba omuzimu mu linnya lyange nga mulina okutya ku mimwa gyammwe? Mugulagire guveewo mu linnya lyange n’obuvumu bwonna n’obwesige, kubanga oyo ali mu mmwe munene okusinga oyo ali mu nsi. Laba! Emizimu emibi gibatya era giwunya okuva ku bigambo byammwe, n’olwekyo lwaki mutya ebintu ebinafu eby’obwavu ebimaze okuwangulwa era ebirindiridde omuliro? Temutyanga, naye mukkirize kwokka!

Mugende mu linnya lyange mukole emirimu gyange egy’amaanyi. Mujjukire okwagala kwammwe okwasooka era muwummulire mu kuwummula kwe nabawa. Mulekere awo okufuba kwammwe n’emigugu gyammwe, era mwetikke ekikoligo kyange; kubanga ekikoligo kyange kyangu, n’omugugu gwange gwangu. Mukole emirimu gye nabateekereteekera era mugitambuliremu, kubanga bwe mukola mutyo mulirokola bangi okuva mu nnimi z’omuliro, era ebibala biryeyongera ku mweyoyo gwammwe. Ekiseera kituuse, omugole wange, n’enkomerero y’olugendo lwammwe eri kumpi, kumpi okusinga bwe mulowooza. Naye mumpulire, baana bange; mumpulire, omugole wange! Mwetegeereze nga wakyaliwo akadde. Muwonye abalwadde ng’Omwana akyayaka mu bulamu bwammwe, ng'abawa amaanyi n’amaanyi gange.

Munoonye endiga zange ezabula; muziyite, kubanga bangi abakyaliwo okulokoka mu ddakiika zino ez’enkomerero ez’okuzza amaanyi. Kubanga mangu ddala mujja kujja mu busika bwammwe mu nze, era mujja kutegeera obulamu bwammwe obujjuvu mu nze; kubanga obulamu bwammwe bukwekeddwa mu nze, era bwe ndirabika, n’obujjuvu bw’obulamu Kitange bwe yabawa mu nze nabwo bulirabika. Mbayagala nnyo, bana bange abato, n’olwekyo nammwe mwagale abo ababuze mu ngeri y’emu. Temubasalisanga musango wadde okunyooma embeera yaabwe, kubanga baawangulwa Setaani nga nammwe bwe mwali; n’olwekyo mujjukire okununulibwa kwe nabakolera era mukikuwenga abo abakiri mu bwetaavu. Newankubadde bangi balibawakanya, temulekayo okutuusa endiga zange lwe zirikungaanyizibwa, kubanga mangu nnyo eŋŋaano yange erikungaanyizibwa n’obwegendereza mu tterekero lyange. Abo abawakana okulabika kwange banjeemera nange era bawakana ekigambo kyange; n’olwekyo mwetegeereze mu kigambo kyange era muleme kukkiriza babbi, kubanga ababbi era balimba.

Nze nzuuno eyagumiikiriza okutuusa ku mukka gwe ogw’enkomerero, nga ndwanyisa ekibi n’omusaayi gwange ogutaliko kibi.

Nze nzuuno eyazuukizibwa ku lunaku olw’okusatu n’alinnya eri Kitange.

Nze nzuuno eyatambulanga mu bayigirizwa bange n’abatume bange okumala ennaku amakumi ana, era nalabikirako ab’oluganda abasukka mu bitaano omulundi gumu, era nnalabibwa n’okukwatibwako abangi, kubanga omuzimu tegulina mubiri na musaayi.

Nze nzuuno eyatwalibwa waggulu, nga bankebera, mu bire.

Nze nzuuno ow’okumasamasa okusinga okw’enjuba, kubanga njolesa ekitiibwa kya Kitange.

Ndi kumpi n’okubikkula ekitiibwa ekinene mu mmwe.

Ndi wamu nammwe bulijjo, okutuusa enkomerero y’emirembe.
