Laba! Nzijja: Ebbaluwa y'Okwagala ey'Okubudaabuda eri Abesigwa mu Kristo

Muwulire ebigambo bya Yeshua HaMashiach era mukkirize Ebigambo byange eby’okubudaabuda era muwulire ebigambo byange eby’essanyu, kubanga okununulibwa kwammwe okujjuvu kutuuse era kuli kumpi. Mazima, mazima, nze ajja okujja okutwala Ekkanisa yange era ow'ebbanja talyerabira mugole wange, kale munywerere mu kukkiriza okungi era mukkirize Ebigambo byange n'amaanyi n'obuyinza byange bibeere obunywevu bw'ebiro byammwe n'amaanyi g'obulokozi, era okutya Mukama bwe bugagga bwammwe.

N'olwekyo, ebiseera bibatuuseeko, abaagalwa bange, era n’ebiseera by'okwebikkulirwa kwange okujjuvu bituuse. Era nja kwebikkulira buli omu ku kiseera kye. Ndisooka kwebikkulira mu bujjuvu eri abesigwa mu Kristo mu ggulu, mu mangu ddala era mu ngeri etasuubirwa kaakano ku mulanga ogw'amaanyi ogwa Malayika Omukulu, era ekkondeere eryo ery'amaanyi linaavuga kubanga liweereddwayo mu mukono okuvuga ku kiragiro kyange ku kiseera ekyo ekituufu ddala ekyalondebwa Yahweh yennyini mu butayu ayinza! Era mu ntegeka ze ez'emirembe n'emirembe gyonna ez'obulokozi bw'olulyo lwa Adamu n'eddoboozi lyalyo lijja kujjula ensi era Abesigwa mu Kristo bajja kuwulira nti "Mwambuke wano!" mu mangu ddala era mu ngeri etasuubirwa kaakano.

Naye abatali beesigwa n'abakakanyavu balisigala mabega w'abagwa n'abatali bakkiriza okukoowoola erinnya lyange okutuusa ku nkomerero yaabwe, era bonna abanaakikola bajja kutwalibwa mu bulokozi obw'olubeerera n'ekitiibwa, era empeera ey'emirembe n'emirembe, ogw'omugaso n'obuzito mazima tebusaana kugeraageranyizibwa na nnaku na kugezesebwa by'ensi eno, ebitaliiko mugaso era ebitono mu kugeraageranya. Bw'atyo bw'ayogera oyo eyabonaabonera ekibonerezo ekijjuvu eky'ebibi byammwe mu mubiri gwange omutaliiko kibi, nga mfuka n'obulumi bungi omusaayi gwange omutaliiko kibi okutuusa ku ttooke erisembayo, era n'ebyo byonna nsaasira, abaagalwa bange. N’olwekyo mutunuulire nze, Yesu Omunazaaleeti, Omwana gw'endiga gwa Katonda, Omukulu era Omutuukiriza w'okukkiriza kwammwe, oyo olw'essanyu eryateekebwa mu maaso gange eyagumiikiriza omusalaba, ng'anyooma ensonyi, era ntuula ku mukono ogwa ddyo ogw'entebe ya Katonda. Seera!

Bwe mutyo nammwe, abesigwa mu Kristo, mwenna mujja kutwalibwa mu ggulu ery'awaggulu ennyo gye ndibaanjulira Yahweh yennyini! Era mujja kwewuunya ekitiibwa kye mujja okwambazibwa, era mujja kumanya nga bwe mwamanyibwa, era mujja kulaba nga bwe mwalabibwa, era mujja kuwulira nga bwe mwawulirwa Katonda ow'Emirembe n'emirembe Ow'obutuukirivu, ye Yahweh yennyini ajjukira buli Kigambo mu buli ssaala gye mwali mwogedde oba n'eddoboozi mu buvuga bwammwe obw'okwogeramu, oba mu bugenderevu mu mitima gyammwe, kubanga Yahweh ye Mukenneezi w'emitima, era kiki ekisobola okukwekebwa mu maaso ge? Mazima, mazima, kiki ekisobola okukwekebwa mu maaso gange? N’olwekyo, mutegeere nti Kitange nange tuli bumu mu bumu ne mu kigendererwa era byonna Kitange by'alina, yabiwa mu mikono gyange olw'obulokozi bwa Adamu, era mazima obulokozi n'okutukuzibwa kw'ensi yennyini, kubanga ensi yange era mu butuukirivu njija okusala omusango era okulwanyisa abagwa ab'ebiseera byabwe bituuse, n'omusango gwabwe omujjuvu tegulwawo era tebagenda kuguwona.

N’olwekyo, mbawa okutegeera, abaagalwa bange, okunnywerera ku lwange n'okweyongera okukuuma ettaala zammwe nga zijjudde amafuta g'omwoyo gwange ge mbateeseteese era mutambulirenga mu mwoyo gwange Ogw'obutuukirivu nga mutambulira mu kukkiriza kubanga omutuukirivu anaabanga mulamu lwa kukkiriza, Seera! Era muleme kugezesebwa okugenda mu ssaawa eno enkeke mu maanyi gammwe ge muli nago okujjuza ettaala zammwe amafuta agatali malongoofu agasinziira ku bikolwa byammwe, wabula mujjuze ettaala zammwe amafuta amalongoofu ag'omwoyo gwange zisobole okumulisa amakubo gammwe, kubanga mazima ddala, Ekigambo kyange ttaala ku bigere byammwe. Era mbajjuzza mwenna okutuuka ku kigero ekisinga obunene n'omwoyo gwange Ogw'obuzaale bwa Baana era nfuse omwoyo gwange mu nnaku zino ez'enkomerero nga bwe kyasuubizibwa Yoweeri mu biwandiiko Eby'edda ebyaweebwa bakitammwe, abali bakitammwe olw'okuba nti muli baana ba kusuubiza, era baana ba Ibulayimu O mwesigwa, Seera!

Mundeke mbayigirize engeri y'okusala entambi zammwe kubanga mmwe musana gw'ensi ogutasobola kukwekebwa! Okuyita mu Bigambo byange n'omwoyo gwange mwe mufunira ebiragiro mu butuukirivu, era omwoyo gwange ogw'obuzaale bwa baana mwe musinziirira okusalirwa omusango gw'obutuukirivu, nti mazima ddala musobole okubeera mu ngeri entuufu mu bintu byonna. Kubanga omwoyo gwange ogw'obuzaale bwa baana mwe muyita okutuukirizibwa kwange okwa nnamaddala mu mmwe, era mazima ddala mwenna mulina okulondawo gwe mujja okuwuliriza, oba okugondera ekibi okutuusa okufa, oba okugondera okutuusa obutuukirivu okutuusa obulamu obutaggwaawo n'empeera enzijuvu. N'olwekyo, mugondere ebiragiro byange eby'enjawulo mu bulamu bwammwe era mubeere bawombeefu ku ddoboozi lyange n'amaloboozi g'abo be ntuma n'abo abanjogerera, era ku bibala byabwe mwe mujja okubategeererako, ne ku Bigambo byabwe bye mbawa olw'obulokozi bwammwe n'obulungi bwammwe. Era mbalagira okukola ku bulokozi bwammwe mwennyini nga mutya era nga mukankana, era mbalagira obutagabira birungi bwe biba mu buyinza bw'emikono gyammwe okutuukiriza.

N’olwekyo, mutegeere nti mpaye abamu ku mmwe amaanyi era mbateeseteese olw’ebigendererwa byange eby’obulokozi era nti mwandyagalananga. N’olwekyo, temukukuma bintu na nsimbi nungi bye mbawadde, nga mukukuma abalala mu makubo ga Sitaani anoonya okuteeka ebipimo n’ebigera ku bulungi bwa Yahweh, obutapimika mazima kubanga tewali kipimo mu ggulu newankubadde ku nsi ekimala okupima obungi bwa Yahweh bw’ateeseteese mu maaso ga Yesu Kristo eri abo abamwagala.

N’olwekyo, muva mu bo mu bujjuvu era temwenyigira mu bibi byabwe, temwekukumira bungi bwa Yahweh obw'ensimbi n'ebintu ebiweereddwa mu maaso ga Yesu Kristo nga n'amaanyi g'omukono gwammwe mwe mutuukirizza omugabo guno okukwekebwa n'okukukusibwa olw'okwegomba kwammwe. N'olwekyo musobole okubudaabudibwa nti mwerabiridde mu malala n'ekibi, nti munyooma abo abatali na byakutuusa, nga musanyukira n'empeera mu akawunti zammwe n'ensonyi nnyingi.

N'olwekyo, mutegeere era mulabulwe nti bonna abakola ebintu ebyo okuva ku asinga obukulu okutuuka ku asinga obutono, bajja kufiirwa empeera nnyingi era bajja kweddiza ekitiibwa ky'omuntu asinga okuba owa wansi mu bwakabaka bw'eggulu! Era nti olw'omululu gwammwe ebikolwa byammwe bijja kwokya ne biba vvu ku ntebe yange ey'omusango gye mujja okuyimirira mwenna ne mwanjula. N’olwekyo, mugabire abantu abatukuvu ebintu byonna nga mwagalana era temukisa kintu kirungi, kubanga mazima ddala, obugagga bwammwe obw'ebintu n'ensimbi buvudde mu mukono gwange olw'ebigendererwa byange okuva mu mawanika ga Yahweh, n’olwekyo, temukukuma bulungi bwa Mukama wabula tutuukirize ebyo bye ndibalung’amya mu buweereza n’okwagala abatukuvu.

Muve mu bibi byammwe, ggwe ggwanga lyange, muleme kwenyigira mu misango gyabwe, kubanga gano ge makubo ga Babulooni ow'Ekyama era ebiseera by'omusango gwe bituuse, era Adonai Yahweh alina amaanyi amusala omusango n'ekitundu eky'emirundi ebiri, n'olwekyo muve mu makubo ge n'ebibi ebitakkirizibwa era ebibi ennyo mu maaso gange! Seera!

Yee, mu mazima, nsaba buli omu n'abo bonna okweyongera okuva mu bibi byammwe ne mujja mu bujjuvu mu butuukirivu bwange, era mundeke mbeere okumala kwammwe mu bintu byonna, kubanga mazima ddala, Nze ndi.

Nze oyo ajja mu bire olw'abeesigwa mu Kristo mu mangu nnyo era mu ngeri etasuubirwa kaakano, kubanga ensi, mu butali bwesigwa bwayo, tesuubira Kristo Yesu okulabikira mu bire nga bwe kyasuubizibwa mu byawandiikibwa. Naye ekiseera kino tekiija kubatuukako, abaagalwa bange, ng’omubbi ekiro, bwe munaasigala nga muli bawombeefu ku biragiro byange era ne mugoberera ebiragiro byange n’emitima gyammwe gyonna, nga mumanyi nti olw’obunyiikivu bwammwe n’obwesigwa bwammwe, empeera enzijuvu ez’obusika bw’abeesigwa mu Kristo zijja, nti mazima, teziyingirangako ddala mu mitima gy’abantu mu kiseera kino. N’olwekyo, temukkiriza Sitaani kugula empeera zammwe ez’obusika mu ngeri ya Esawu, eyali omuntu omwonoonefu era omwenzi eyatunda obusika bwe olw’ebbenseni y’enva ezaasuulibwa mu kyeeya. Seera!

N’olwekyo, mutegeere okuba okumpi mu kiseera eky’okulabika kwange n’engeri okununulibwa kwammwe okujjuvu gye kuli okumpi, abaagalwa bange. N’olwekyo, mbawa okutegeera nti eggye ly’eggulu litutte ebifo byalyo mu biragiro byalyo ne mu nkola zaalyo, era beeraze okuweereza, era bayimiridde beetegefu. Batutte ebifo byabwe, era beetegefu! N’olwekyo, mutegeere, nti nkwata ekkondeere eryo ery'amaanyi mu mukono, nti ku kiragiro kyange abafu mu Kristo bajja kuzuukizibwa nga tebavunda, era mmwe abali abalamu ne musigalawo mujja kutwalibwa mu mangu okunsisinkana mu bire era bwe mutyo mwenna mujja kufaananyizibwa ne Mukama wammwe emirembe gyonna, era abo abawangudde mu bujjuvu bajja kubeeranga nange bulijjo kubanga ndibatwala gye ndi mu ndagaano nga bange, era mujja kukola ebikolwa bingi eby’amaanyi, kubanga omugole wange ggye lya maanyi erya Bannabbi, Bakabaka, ne Bakabona abajja okuweebwa amaanyi amangi ng’omugole wange omulwanyi, era ani asobola okuyimirira okulwanyisa omugole wange ow’abatalifa abajjuddemu amaanyi abanunuddwa okuva mu nsi mu maanyi amangi n’obutuukirivu, Seera!

N’olwekyo, mutegeere nti ekiseera ekituufu ddala ekyalondebwa Yahweh mu kumanya kwe okw’emabega kyali lunaku lutuufu, kiseera, ne ssaawa, wabula kyakwekebwa mu mutima gwa Yahweh yekka okutuusa ebiseera ebituufu eby’okubikkulirwa kwakyo lwe byatuukirira mu ggulu lyokka, kubanga ekiseera kino tekibikkuliddwa ku nsi, wadde nga Sitaani ayimiridde yeetegefu okumira bonna abasigaddewo n’abasigala ku nsi, n’olwekyo, bonna abakoowoola erinnya lya Yesu okutuusa ku nkomerero yaabwe bajja kulokolebwa. Mutegeere nti ekiseera kino ekituufu ddala tekiweereddwa na Sitaani wabula alina okuyimirira ng’ategeka okutuusa lw’anaasumululwa okufuga kwe okw’ekiseera ekitono oluvannyuma lw’ekyo lw’anaasibibwa n’olujegere olunene mu bunnya obutakoma ng’abonaabona era ng’alumizibwa gye yanditegeeredde mu bujjuvu obusika bwe obugenda okujja.

Bwe kityo nzijja okubawa okutegeera ebiseera bye mubeeramu era nkikola nga mbaddamu Ebigambo byennyini bye nawa Peetero okwogera n’ekibiina ky’abantu ku lunaku olwo olunene olwa Pentekoote bino eby’emirembe mingi ebyayitawo, naye era n’ebigambo bye bikyali bya mazima emirembe n’emirembe mu kutuukirizibwa emirembe n’emirembe. N’olwekyo mbagamba nti kino kye kyayogerebwa nnabbi Yoweeri, nti Kiriba mu nnaku ez’enkomerero, bw’atyo bw’ayogera Katonda, nti Ndifuka omwoyo gwange ku buli muntu, n’abaana bammwe ab’obulenzi, n’abaana bammwe ab’obuwala baliragulanga, n’abavubuka bammwe balirabanga okwolesebwa, n’abakadde bammwe balirootanga ebirooto. Era ku baddu bange, ne ku bawala bange ndifuka ku mwoyo gwange mu nnaku ezo, era baliragulanga. Era ndiraga eby’amagero waggulu mu ggulu, n’obubonero wansi mu nsi, omusaayi, n’omuliro, n’olunyata lw’omukka. Enjuba erifuuka ekizikiza, n’omwezi gufuuke omusaayi, olunaku lwa Mukama olukulu era olw’entiisa nga terunnatuuka. Era aliba nti buli alikoowoola erinnya lya Mukama alirokolebwa.

N’olwekyo, nzijja okulangirira Ebigambo bino nga bituukiridde mu matu gammwe olunaku luno lwennyini, era nti mu mangu nnyo era mu ngeri etasuubirwa kaakano abeesigwa mu Kristo bajja kutwalibwa ne banunulibwa ne bakyusibwa ne bagulumizibwa era n’okuweebwa empeera emirembe n’emirembe. Kale mutegeere okutuukirizibwa okw’amangu okugenda okujja mu mangu nnyo. Temuwuliriza abassaawo ennaku n'Abayudaaya abatategeera bye bayigiriza, newankubadde amazima g'ebiwandiikibwa ge bakyama okwefunira okuzikirira kwabwe.

N’olwekyo, mbawa okutegeera nti okukuŋŋaanya abeesigwa mu Kristo tekusibibwa ku lunaku lwonna olutukuvu, newankubadde ennaku enkulu, newankubadde olunaku lwonna oba ekikolwa ekiweereddwa Isirayiri ow’edda mu mateeka ga Musa, n’amateeka ago gatuukirizibwa mu bujjuvu mu Kristo Yesu Mukama wammwe ow’Emirembe n’emirembe ow’obutuukirivu. Nga bwe nnabagambye edda mbagamba nate abaagalwa bange: N’olwekyo omuntu yenna aleme kubasalira musango mu by’okulya n’okunywa, oba ku bikwata ku lunaku olutukuvu, oba ku mwezi omuggya, oba ku nnaku za ssabbiiti, Ebiri ekisiikirize ky'ebigenda okujja: naye omubiri guli mu Kristo.

Kyokka, mutegeere nti ekintu kino kisobola okubaawo buli sekondi era ku lunaku lwonna era kiyinza nnyo okubaawo ku lunaku olw’amakulu, ekiseera, oba essaawa oba ne mu nnaku enkulu, oba ennaku enkulu, oba ennaku za ssabbiiti, eziri ekisiikirize ky'ebigenda okujja! N’olwekyo, mulindirire era mutunule era musuubire nnyo okulabika kwange kubanga ebiseera byammwe eby’okununulibwa kwammwe okujjuvu bituuse. Era mbawa okutegeera era olw’okugondera kwammwe, nti mulindirire era mutunule buli lunaku (so si biseera byammwe bye mweteekateeka eby’okulinda okwa waggulu) era nti mwongere okussaayo omwoyo ku Bigambo bino nga musuubira mu bujjuvu okutuukirizibwa, kubanga tewakyali kulwawo. Seera!

Nze Kristo Yesu era nze eyasindise Ebigambo bino okuyita mu mubaka wange n'omuweereza wange era okuyita mu mubaka wange n'omuweereza wange Ebigambo bino biweerezeddwa. Okuba okukubiriza Ekkanisa yange, omubiri gwange, n’Omugole wange basobole okubudaabudibwa n’okubudaabudibwa okungi era musobole okutegeera mu bujjuvu okumpi ddala mu kiseera kino, muleme okwewuunya ekikolwa kino ekigenda okubaawo mu mangu nnyo.

Nze Yeshua HaMashiach ajja okusindika abajulirwa bange ababiri n’ebikondo byange eby’ettaala ebibiri, nga bwe kyalangirirwa mu Njiri ey’Emirembe n’emirembe, kubanga Yahweh teyeerabiranga kujulirwa, n’abajulirwa bange ababiri bajja kujulira ebiseera byonna era okuva mu nsi ne mu ggulu, era Enoka Omuwandiisi aleeta ebiwandiiko eby’edda mu nsawo ye n’obujulizi bwabwe bujja kwongerwako ku Njiri ey’Emirembe n’emirembe eneegumanga emirembe n’emirembe awamu n’Ebigambo byonna ebya Yahweh ebijja okuzzibwawo ku nsi mu butuukirivu obutuufu nga bwe byawandiikibwa mu ggulu, n’olwekyo ebikolwa bya sitaani bisobole okusazibwamu ddala n’amakubo n’Ebigambo bya Yahweh bisobole okwanjaala ensi mu butuukirivu emirembe gyonna n’emirembe gyonna! Seera.
