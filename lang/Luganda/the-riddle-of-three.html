Umujulirwa Ow'okubiri: Isirayiri
Ekikaawa Ekirimu Obuwoomu
Ssebutemba 5, 2024

Ekikokyo ky'Essatu!

Muwulire eddoboozi lya Yeshua HaMashiach, abaagalwa bange, mujje gyendi mufune okubudaamirira era muwummulire mu kwagala kwange eri mmwe, mmwe abaana bange abato. Kubanga mubadde beesigwa ku bintu bitono, era kaakano nja kubafuula abafuzi b'ebingi nga muyingira mu ssanyu lya Mukama wammwe. Omusango si gwammwe, mmwe abeesigwa bange; era n'abo bonna abali mu Kristo n'abo bonna abaafa nga bali mu Kristo balizuukizibwa mu bulamu obuggya mu kanyaanyaanya, mu kutebya kw'eriiso. Kubanga mazima ddala, nja kussa okuva mu ggulu n'oluyoogaano, n'eddoboozi lya Malayika Omukulu, era n'ekkondeere lya Katonda: era abaafa nga bali mu Kristo balizuukizibwa mu bulamu obutaggwaawo era mmwe abali abalamu abalisigalawo mulitwalibwa wamu nabo mu bire, era bwe mutyo mulibeeranga ne Mukama wammwe ow'emirembe era nga mufaanana naye. Mulikyusibwa mangu okuva mu mbeera yammwe ey'okugwa ey'omu kiseera kino okufuuka ekitonde ekinunuddwa mu bujjuvu eky'obutaggwaawo, era nga mutondeddwa buppya ng'omuntu omu omuggya, era nga bwendi, nammwe bwe muliba; era nga bwe kyawandiikibwa, bwe kityo bwe kirikolebwa amangu ago.

Lwaki muleka emitima gyammwe okubatyemula, mmwe abaana bange? Lwaki muleka ebigambo by'omuwawaabirizi okubatyemula ne mulowooza nti tewali kunyagulibwa, oba nti temusaana kufuna kisa kyange ekirokolola? Mbakubiriza, mmwe abaana bange, okweyongera okutambulira mu kukkiriza so si mu kulaba; era nze eyabafuula abasaana, era bonna abali mu Kristo era abalina enkolagana ey'obulokozi ne Mukama waabwe omutuukirivu, kale temubuusabuusa! Newankubadde temunandabangako na maaso gammwe ag'omubiri, mumanyi bulungi nti nze Mukama wammwe omulamu era omulokozi, era mu nze mulimu obulamu obujjuvu n'obulokozi. Mazima ddala, ddala, oyo anzikiririzaamu talirumwa njala nate wadde okulumwa ennyonta.

Kubanga nze mugaati gw'obulamu, era bonna abalya tebalirumwa njala nate; era nze oyo avaamu emigga gy'amazzi amalamu mu bungi n'obulamu obuggya, era oyo anywako talirumwa nnyonta nate. Naye mmwe mwenna mulifunira obulamu mu bulamu obulungi ddala, mu mirembe emijjuvu, ne mu kwegatta okutuukiridde ne Yahweh, ne Mukama wammwe omulamu era omulokozi, kubanga nze Kristo Yesu, eyabagula n'omusaayi gwange ogw'etango, era mazima muli ba muwendo gwa waggulu nnyo. Era bwe kityo omuwendo ogutagambika ne guweebwayo bwe nneewaayo nzekka emirembe gyonna ng'Omwana gw'endiga gwa Katonda, era bwe ntyo ne nteeka ssaddaaka yange ku lwammwe nga Kabona Asinga Obukulu emirembe gyonna okusinziira ku mutendera gwa Merukizeddeeki ne nsasula ekinunulo kyammwe n'omusaayi gwange ogutalimu kibi.

Era bwe kityo emirembe n'emirembe tewakyali kwetaaga ssaddaaka ya kibi, kubanga oba ng'omusaayi gw'ennume z'ente n'embuzi, n'evvu ly'ente enduusi, nga limansibwa ku abo abatalongoofu, litukuza okutuusa ku kulongoosa omubiri, Omusaayi gwa Kristo, oyo eyewaayo n'omwoyo ogutaggwaawo awatali kusobya eri Katonda, tegulisinga nnyo kulongoosa omuntu w'omunda wammwe okuva mu bikolwa ebifu, okuba muweereza ba Katonda omulamu? Era ekyo ssaddaaka y'ebisolo kye yali tesobola kukola, omusaayi gwange ogw'etango gwasobola okukikola omulundi gumu emirembe n'emirembe, nga bwe nnatangirira ebibi by'abantu omulundi gumu emirembe n'emirembe, era eri bonna abasalawo okukkiriza obulokozi mu linnya lyange, kubanga tewali linnya eddala wansi w'eggulu erigabiddwa erisobola okulokola abantu. Kale temukkiriza bulimba bwabwe, kubanga ogusota, n'ensolo, n'omunnabbi ow'obulimba baligolokoka, era balinoonya okugatta amadiini gonna ag'ensi ag'obulimba wansi w'ebbendera empya, wabula Yesu Kristo talikkirizibwa mu bo, wadde mu linnya wadde mu kikolwa, era baweereddwa okulwana n'abatukuvu n'okubawangula. Mazima ddala, ddala, sirina mugabo wadde ekifo mu bo, era nze oyo alibazikiriza ddala, kubanga mu butuukirivu nzijja okusala omusango n'okulwana olutalo.

Era kaakano njogera n'abo abalijja gyendi mu kiseera ky'okubonaabona kwa Yakobo, be batukuvu b'omu kiseera ekyo abalifuna empeera enkulu n'obusika obw'emirembe mu Mukama waabwe ow'emirembe era ow'olubeerera ne Masiya, ye Yeshua HaMashiach. Era bwe kityo bwe kyawandiikibwa ng'amazima ag'emirembe, era bwe ntyo njogera emirembe n'emirembe nti muyingirire mu mulyango omufunda: kubanga omulyango mugazi, n'ekkubo ddigazi erituusa ku kuzikirira: era bangi be bali abayita omwo, Kubanga omulyango mufunda, n'ekkubo zziro erituusa ku bulamu, era batono be bali abalirizuula. N'olwekyo mbawa ekiragiro, mmwe abaana bange abato, okusigala ku kkubo eggolokofu era eddfunda era mwewale okuzikirira kw'abasirusiru, be abo amannya gaabwe ge batawandiikibwanga mu Kitabo ky'Obulamu okuva ku ntandikwa y'ensi, kubanga baagala amakubo g'okuzikirira, era baagala obutali butuukirivu, era okulimbibwa okw'amaanyi kwe basinga okwagala okusinga obutuukirivu n'amazima.

Kubanga baliddukira eno n'eri, n'olutalo olutajjudde lukyaliwo ku nsi, n'okuzikirira okutajjudde kulabise era kuli kumpi; akabi, okulabula kw'embiro ewa 911, n'olunaku lusemberera ddala era luli ku mmwe. Era okuva mu vvu ly'olutalo mwe baliva, era okuva mu vvu ly'olutalo mwe balibyetooloolera eri abantu omulundi omulala, era abantu balyewuunya olw'abaggye mu maaso mu kwewuunya okutajjudde olw'obukodyo bwabwe bwe bakozesezza okuloga ababuze okuva ku lubereberye. Baligatta amadiini gaabwe ag'obulimba, era balibagamba obulimba bungi obwogerwa emimwa gy'emyoyo gy'obulimba gya anti-Kristo, kubanga bangi aba anti-Kristo be baagenda mu nsi.

Kiki anti-Kristo, mmwe abaana bange? Anti-Kristo akontana ne Kristo, omulokozi ow'amazima era Masiya, era mu kifo kya Kristo; kubanga nze Yeshua HaMashiach, era tewali mulala. Era bwe kityo obulimba bwabwe bumaze okusaasaanira ensi nga bategeka abantu okuwulira essatu etali ntukuvu, ye ggombolola, ensolo eva mu nnyanja, n'ensolo eva mu nsi, oyo ye nnabbi waabwe ow'obulimba. Naye kiki Yokaana kye yawandiika ku nsonga eno mu bbaluwa ye eyasooka? Sibawandiikirangako, kubanga temumanyi mazima: naye kubanga mugamanyi, era nti tewali bulimba bwa mu mazima. Ani omulimba, wabula oyo eyeegaana nti Yesu ye Kristo? Oyo ye Anti-Kristo, eyeegaana Kitaffe, n'Omwana. Buli eyeegaana Omwana, oyo talina Kitaffe: naye oyo akkiriza Omwana, alina ne Kitaffe.

Era bwe kityo bwe kiri, nti, nze Yesu Omunazaaleeti, nze kkubo, n'amazima, n'obulamu; era tewali n'omu ajja eri Yahweh wabula okuyitira mu nze. Nze mulyango gwokka, era nze kkubo lyokka erituuka eri Yahweh, Katonda omukulu ow'emirembe; kubanga bwe kityo Yahweh bwe yalangirira era bwe kityo Yahweh bwe yategeka era n'asalawo okuva ensi nga tennaabaawo mu kumanya kwe okw'emirembe n'amagezi. Ani ku mmwe asobola okuwa Yahweh amagezi, Katonda omukulu ow'emirembe? Newankubadde emitwalo egiwerako n'emitwalo emitwalo ne gigyetooloola entebe ye, teyeetaaga muwi wa magezi; era ye Yahweh, Katonda omukulu ow'emirembe, akola ebintu byonna ng'okuteesa kw'okwagala kwe bwe kuli nti mubeere ba kutenderezebwa kwa kitiibwa kye mmwe abaasooka okwesiga Kristo.

N'olwekyo mutegeere obulimba obujja obumaze okujja ku nsi. Kubanga abaweereza ba Sitaani boogera nga bawakanya amazima, era boogerera waggulu era boogerera waggulu nti amadiini gonna ag'ensi gatuusa eri katonda omu, ka babe ba ddiini ki, ka babe n'enzikiriza zaabwe, nti amannya amalala gonna agagabiddwa abaggye mu maaso gali ga mazima era gatuusa eri katonda ow'amazima, naye eri ggombolola yokka gye gatuusa. Mazima ddala, ddala mbagamba, temukkiriza bulimba bwabwe, era temubaagaliza wadde omukisa abo abakkiriza obusirusiru obwo. Kubanga omulyango gw'okuzikirira mugazi, ogutuusa ku nnyanja ey'omuliro ennene, era Sitaani anoonya okusindika abangi nga bwe kisoboka mu kkubo lye ery'okuzikirira okw'emirembe, kubanga bwe butyo obusika bwe, era bwe butyo obukyayi bwe eri mmwe mwenna. Temukkiriza bulimba bwabwe obw'okwegatta, era mudduke okwegatta kwabwe okutali kutukuvu, kubanga enkomerero yaakwo be nnimi z'omuliro ezisinga okwokya ez'obululu nga sulufeeti eyeekulukuunya era eyaka emirembe n'emirembe, era tewali n'omu aliwona ku abo abasibiddwa ne basuulibwa mu muliro.

Mutegeere, mmwe abaana bange, nti amannya gaabwe gonna gatuusa eri katonda omu, ye Sitaani, kubanga si bakatonda wabula beeyita abatali. Era bwe batyo balinyeenya amabendera gaabwe ag'enjawulo, era balyogerera waggulu nti amannya mangi gatuusa eri katonda omu. Era ku ludda olumu baliba batuufu kubanga amakubo mangi gatuusa eri katonda w'ensi eno, oyo omusango gwe gwatuuka era n'ekibonerezo kye n'okuzikirira kwe tekulwawo. Ate ku ludda olulala, balimba ensi era banoonya okusendasenda bonna abaligoberera amakubo gaabwe ag'okuzikirira mu kitundu kyabwe ekipya ekiriva mu vvu ly'olutalo ekirigenda okwetaaga akabonero okuyingira.

Temutwala kabonero kaabwe, kubanga bonna abakakola baliba bakolimidde emirembe era bakyusiddwa emirembe n'emirembe. Era balimba nnyo nti amannya gonna gatuusa eri Katonda ow'amazima, ye Yahweh, Katonda ow'emirembe owa Isirayiri, kubanga Yahweh yeerondera Yakobo nti mubeere ggwanga lye ery'enjawulo. Era bwe kityo Sitaani anoonya bulijjo okukoppa amakubo ga Katonda omu ow'amazima, ye El Shaddai, oyo ye Katonda Omuyinza w'ebintu byonna! Era bwe atyo akoze amadiini mangi amakopi agayiiyiddwa okukoppa amakubo ga Yahweh, Katonda ow'emirembe gyonna, era enzikiriza zino zonna ez'obukopi zikoma mu kuzikirira okw'emirembe eri abo abagwamu. Kubanga ani omutezi w'omutego atega omutego mu maaso g'ekinyonyi?

Era bwe batyo bamaze okugaba emikisa eri bonna, era amadiini gonna, era bamaze okwagala okugatta amadiini gonna wansi w'amabendera gaabwe ag'okwegatta, kubanga bwe butyo amakubo gaabwe ag'okukozesa obwongo bwe gatuukirira. Ku ludda olumu baleeta endowooza; ku ludda olulala baleeta endowooza yaabwe ey'okuwakanya; era okuva mu kulwanagana okufugibwa baleetawo okwegatta kwabwe, kubanga banoonya okugatta ebyobuwangwa byonna, amawanga, n'ennimi mu kitundu kyabwe ekya Sitaani basobole okuzikiriza bonna abagoberera amakubo gaabwe ag'okuzikirira. Era n'olwekyo mutegeere, ekyakyama tekisobola kugololwa, n'ebigambo byabwe ebirungi n'enjogera zaabwe ezirungi biyiiyiddwa okukwata ku nneewulira z'abantu n'okwegomba n'okusaasira okw'obulimba kwe balyogerera waggulu ng'obutukuvu. Mutegeere nti omuntu yekka wonna ali mwononefu ddala era tasobola kugolola kkubo lye, era tewali kirungi mu muntu, naye omuntu yayononebwa olw'obutawulira bwa Adamu naddala olw'obulimba n'obukodyo bw'omusota, ogwo omutwe gwe nja kugubetenta ddala, kubanga Yahweh yalangirira okuva ku lubereberye enkomerero y'omusota ogwo omulimba ogulya enfuufu y'ensi.

Bwe kityo bwe kyali, bwe kityo bwe kiri, era bwe kityo bwe kiriba, nti tewali kintu kipya wansi w'enjuba. Era ng'ensi bwe eva ku kisa okudda ku musango, akabi ak'amangu kagenda kugwa, era kiriba mangu ago. N'olwekyo nkubiriza bonna abasoma ebigambo bino okutegeera nti ebigambo bino tebyabasanze lwa butanwa, naye bitumiddwa Yahweh yennyini okuyitira mu mwana we omu yekka, ye Yeshuah HaMashiach, ebyogerwa mu bumu abaweereza bange bannabbi. Era nze, Yesu Omunazaaleeti, ye Yeshua HaMashiach, eyasasula omuwendo ogw'emirembe ogw'obulokozi bwammwe n'obulokozi bw'abo bonna abalijja mu bulamu obutaggwaawo mu kwagala kwange okutakoma era okw'emirembe Yahweh kwe yantonda okutuukiriza. Mbagamba kye kimu nga bwe nagamba okuva ku lubereberye: nti nze, Yesu Kristo, ye Yeshua HaMashiach, nze kkubo, n'amazima, n'obulamu, era tewali muntu ajja eri Kitaffe wabula okuyitira mu nze; era tewali linnya eddala erigabiddwa ku nsi, emirembe gyonna, abantu lye balina okulokokeramu, era amannya amalala gonna ka gabe gasendasenda gatya tegatuusa eri Katonda ow'amazima, ye Kitaffe w'ebitangaala atakyukakyuka wadde okuba n'ekisiikirize eky'okukyuka. N'olwekyo mutegeere, mmwe abaana bange abato, nti mmwe muli bitangaala by'ensi Yahweh bye yazaala, era bye yatonda obuggya mu nze, Kristo Yesu Mukama ow'emirembe omutuukirivu, era nfuusiddwa omwoyo oguwa obulamu.

Laba, mbawa ekikokyo ekirigabira amagezi ab'amagezi era kiriyimiriza abasirusiru n'abatayize okwegatta n'abo abalagiriddwa okuzikirira mu makubo gaabwe. Essatu, ebbiri, emu: era mu madiini amangi ag'ensi kuliko asatu agafuga agalituusa ku limu ery'obulimba eritali tukkirivu, era asatu galabika ng'abalabe era galwanye entalo nnyingi. Naye, ku asatu, abiri gali mikwano mu nkukutu, era limu lirikoze eryokubiri, era gombi gatuusa ku kuzikirira. Kubanga ku asatu, abiri galikyukira limu n'obukambwe, lye limu ettukuvu lye bakyawa naye lye bawagulwa ddala. Era bwe lityo limu ettukuvu lirireeta omusango ku nnyumba ya Katonda okusooka, kubanga omusango gutandikira ku nnyumba ya Katonda era olunaku lw'omusango lutuuse.

Newankubadde limu ettukuvu liwangudde, abiri ku asatu galikyukira okuzikiriza limu, naye okuzikirira okwo tekusoboka, era limu limaze okufuna obuwanguzi obujjuvu. Naye bwe lityo obulimba bwe buliba nti abiri ku asatu galikyuka ne gatta ku limu ne giwaawaabira mu mimwa gy'abajulirwa ab'obulimba abangi, era galilwana n'abatukuvu ne gabawangula. Era n'obulimba obuvvoola obungi balisaasaanya ku nsi, naye limu, lye limu ettukuvu lya Katonda, limaze okubawangula era liribetenta omutwe gwabwe ddala. Naye abiri galirabika ng'awangudde limu lye ganoonya okuzikiriza, era ku abiri galilwana ne limu, wadde ng'okuzikirira kwago okw'enkomerero tekulwawo. Era bwe lityo omutego gulitegebwa ensi, nti okuva mu maanyi amangi n'olutalo balyegatta bokka n'abokka essatu, ebbiri, limu kifuuke ebbiri, limu, essatu kubanga balyegatta mu ddiini emu ey'obulimba ekulemberwa essatu etali ntukuvu, ye ggombolola, ensolo eva mu nnyanja, n'ensolo eva mu nsi, oyo ye nnabbi waabwe ow'obulimba.

Kale nno, mbategeeza ekigambo kyange ekikwafu eky'okusatu. Ebyo ebisatu bye birowoozo by'eddiini essatu ebikulu ebifuga ensi kati; era mu ebyo ebisatu, kimu kyokka kye kya mazima, kye kitukuvu ekya Katonda; kubanga nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, ndi mutukuvu wa Katonda eyawangula omubi era ali Mukama wa bakama ne Kabaka wa bakabaka. Tosobola kungwangula, era mpeereddwa erinnya erisinga amannya gonna, nti mu linnya lya Yesu Kristo, ye Yeshua HaMashiach (kubanga ndi mulokozi w'Abamawanga n'Abayudaaya) buli vviivi lifukamire, mu ggulu ne ku nsi era n'emagombe. Bw'atyo eddiini ebbiri ez'obulimba zirabika nga zirwanagana naye zigezezzaako okwegwanyiza eri emu, era mazima Setaani akoppye ekkanisa yange ey'amazima n'ateekawo omwenzi omulimba agulumiza era asinga malaya w'omu ggulu. Bw'atyo banoonya omukago ogw'obulimba era batabise eby'obulimba bingi mu mazima, kyokka eby'obulimba n'amazima tebitabikako: olina obulimba oba amazima: tewali kirala ky'olonzeewo.

Era ekkanisa yange ey'amazima bw'eneetwalibwa mu ggulu n'omuziyiza n'aggyibwawo, n'ekkanisa ey'ekifaananyi eneeyonoonebwa, era n'eyo emu eneeggyibwawo ddala. Eddiini ebbiri ezisigaddewo zineeyimusa mu buwanguzi obw'obulimba, era zineeba n'emirembe egy'obulimba, era zineenoonya obumu obw'obulimba okugatta ababuze, nga zisuubiza n'obulamu obutaggwaawo, n'obulamu obulungi, n'olunaku oluggya ogusota lwe gunaawa. Era gunaagatta ebbiri okufuuka eddiini emu bakitaabwe gye baali tebamanyi naye eneerabika nga ye nnyini kubanga abantu tebamanyi byafaayo byabwe eby'amazima. Era okuyita mu kutabika okw'okuzikiriza kuno baligenda mu muliro ogutazikira, kubanga bonna abeegatta ku bo be abo abasanyukira obutali butuukirivu, era si ekyo kyokka, naye abo abagala era abakkiriza era abakola era abasinza eby'obulimba kubanga amakubo gaabwe ga bulimba era ga bukyamu era amakubo gaabwe gatwala mu kufa okw'okubiri.

Bw'atyo balikyusa ensi, abalyewuunya olw'ebyo, nga bagamba nti, ani afaanana ensolo? Era ensolo emu eneeweebwa amaanyi g'ensolo endala, era ensolo evva mu nnyanja eneezifuga okusinziira ku kufuga kwayo, era ensolo evva ku nsi enefuga ettaka okusinziira ku kufuga kwayo, era zineefuga ensi mu butali butuukirivu. Era ku zombi ezisigaddewo, zineetabika okufuuka emu. Okuggyako nti, amakubo gaazo kufa, era omwo omutego mwe gutegeddwa, kubanga omuyizzi atega atya omutego mu maaso g'ekinyonyi? Bonna abagoberera amakubo gaabwe ag'okwegatta baligattibwa, mu kusikira kw'abo abaagwa kwokka; era bonna abeegatta okuyita mu kulumwa kw'omusota gwabwe baligabana ku busika bw'oyo eyagwa mu nnyanja ey'omuliro ey'ekkibiriiti omwaka emirembe n'emirembe era omuvunyu gwabwe tegufa. Kale okwegatta kwabwe kujjudde emitego egikwekeddwa mu magezi, kubanga omuyizzi atega atya omutego mu maaso g'ekinyonyi?

Kale mbagabirira mmwe mwenna abasoma ebigambo bino n'abo bonna abalina amaaso okulaba n'amatu okuwulira n'omutima okutegeera, nti obulokozi n'obulamu obutaggwaawo ne Katonda ow'amazima, ye Yahweh yennyini, tebisangibwa mu linnya lirala lyonna okuggyako erinnya ly'Omwana wa Katonda eyazaalibwa omu yekka, ye Yesu Kristo, era ye Yeshua HaMashiach; era ndi mulokozi w'abantu bonna, ye ggwanga lya Adamu omusaayi gwe amawanga gonna g'abantu gwe gagabana. Era mu lulimi lwonna, ne mu ggwanga lyonna, bonna bayinza okujja awatali kusasula ne baweebwa ekirabo kyange eky'obulokozi obutaggwaawo nga bakoowoola erinnya lyange ettukuvu okutuusa ku nkomerero. Kubanga ndi Mukama wammwe era omulokozi wammwe, naye erinnya lyange teriva ku mimwa gyammwe, era ndibalokola ddala.

Temwegatta na kwegatta kwabwe, kubanga tebamanyi kye basinza, era balogeddwa eby'obulimba ebiribatwala mu kuzikirira kwabwe okutaggwaawo. Kale bw'atyo obulokozi tebufunibwa mu linnya lirala lyonna, so tewaweebwa linnya lirala abantu mwe bayinza okulokokerera n'abantu mwe bayinza okusemberera Yahweh, kubanga oyo atalina mwana talina Kitange. Kale nja kuwanirira era nja kunyweza bonna abakoowoola erinnya lyange ettukuvu okutuusa ku nkomerero. Era mulinkolera ebikolwa eby'amaanyi, era mbatuma okwagala Mukama Katonda wammwe n'omutima gwammwe gwonna, n'obulamu bwammwe bwonna, n'amagezi gammwe gonna, n'amaanyi gammwe gonna, n'okwagala muliraanwa wo nga bwe weeyagala wekka n'okuyamba n'okwagalanagana mu biseera ebibi ennyo ebigenda okubuna ensi yonna.

Kale essatu, ebbiri, emu, awali ebbiri ezirirumba emu, era ebbiri ezisigalawo zirifuuka emu era zifugibwe abasatu abatali batukuvu, abalifuga abo amannya gaabwe ge gataawandiikibwa mu Kitabo ky'Obulamu okuva ensi lwe yatandikibwawo. Naye mmwe abatukuvu bange temubeegattako; kubanga kirungi okufiirwa obulamu bwammwe mulyoke mubufune okusinga okwagala obulamu bwammwe n'okufiirwa obulamu bwammwe emirembe n'emirembe mu kuzikirira okutaggwaawo. Laba! Okwawula okw'enkomerero kunaatera okutandika era ebiseera bituuse. Temubatya, mmwe baana bange abato, naye mwesigame ku Mukama wammwe era omulokozi wammwe ow'emirembe n'emirembe. Ndi muyambi ataggwaawo mu biseera eby'okwetaaga, era nja kulokola ekibiina ekinene mu biseera by'okubonaabona kwa Yakobo, era nja kulokola ensigalira ya Isirayiri, so tewali ayinza kubaggya mu mikono gyange.

Mutegeere nti essuubi lyammwe lya lubeerera, era sibaleseewo nga temulina ssuubi, era mbaleseewo ekyokulabirako eky'emirembe n'emirembe. Kubanga kyawandiikibwa ku nze mu Abaebbulaniya: Nga tutunuulira Yesu eyatandika okukkiriza kwaffe n'akutuukiriza, oyo olw'essanyu eryali liteereddwa mu maaso ge, eyagumiikiriza omusalaba, n'anyooma ensonyi, n'atuula ku mukono ogwa ddyo ogw'entebe ya Katonda. Kale mutegeere nti ekyokulabirako kyange eky'obuwulize kyongeramu amaanyi okukkiriza kwammwe mulyoke mugumiikirize okutuusa ku nkomerero, nga nze bwe nnagumiikiriza okutuusa ku nkomerero bwe nnayogera ebigambo ebyo eby'enkomerero ku musalaba: Kiwedde. Bw'atyo Katonda n'anzuukiza mu bafu kubanga tekyasoboka kufa kundemesa, kubanga ogwange gwe musaayi ogutaliiko musango.

Kale mbawadde essuubi eddene, mmwe baana bange abato, mulyoke muteeke ebirowoozo byammwe ku ssanyu eriteereddwa mu maaso gammwe kubanga okubonaabona kw'ekiseera kya Yakobo tekugeraageranyizibwa so tekusaana kwogerwako n'essanyu ery'emirembe eriteereddwa mu maaso gammwe. Laba! Kyawandiikibwa Yokaana Omubikkulirwa: Bano be bava mu kibonyoobonyo ekinene, ne boza engoye zaabwe, ne bazeeza mu musaayi gw'Omwana gw'endiga. Kyebava babeera mu maaso g'entebe ya Katonda, ne bamuweereza emisana n'ekiro mu Yeekaalu ye: n'oyo atuula ku Ntebe alituula mu bo. Tebaliddamu kulumwa njala, so tebalirumwa nnyonta nate, so n'enjuba teribookya, newaakubadde ebbugumu lyonna. Kubanga Omwana gw'endiga, ali wakati mu ntebe, alibaliisa, era alibatuusa ku nzigyi z'amazzi amalamu: ne Katonda alisangula amaziga gonna mu maaso gaabwe. Baliba Bakabona ba Katonda, ne ba Kristo, era balifugira wamu naye emyaka lukumi.

Bw'etyo n'essuubi lyammwe, abaagalwa bange, mulyoke mugumiikirize okutuusa ku nkomerero. Era essuubi lyammwe lya lubeerera kubanga El Shaddai, Katonda Omuyinza w'ebintu byonna, alitonda eggulu eriggya n'ensi empya omutuula obutuukirivu, era muliweebwa okuyingira mu kibuga ekyo ekitukuvu ekyategekebwa era ne kiyondekebwa Yahweh yennyini ng'omugole ategekeddwa omugole, era ku muti gw'obulamu mwe mulirya. Era nga bwe kyawandiikibwa nti: Awangula alisikira byonna, nange ndiba Katonda we, naye aliba mwana wange.

Nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, nze atumye ebigambo byange ng'abiyisa mu muweereza wange, era okuyita mu muweereza wange ebigambo byange bitumiddwa olw'obuwulize bwa bonna abasoma. Era mbikkulidde enjiri ey'emirembe n'emirembe, kubanga ebiseera by'okugibikkula bituuse.

Nze ajja okujja abo abali mu Kristo era ndirokola abeesigwa mu kkanisa yange okuva mu muliro ogulongoosa n'obusungu obugenda okujja.

Nze ayawula eŋŋaano ku ngano, so tewali n'omu ku mmwe ayinza kuggyibwa mu mukono gwange.

Munnyimirirewo, mmwe abatukuvu bange ab'omu kibonyoobonyo, era mujje gye ndi, ggwe Isirayiri, kubanga nze Yeshua HaMashiach, Masiya wammwe ow'amazima, era mbawadde essuubi eddene n'amaanyi amangi. Era bangi ku mmwe balinkolera ebikolwa eby'amaanyi, so tewali n'omu ku mmwe ataliweebwa mpeera, kubanga n'ebintu ebitono ennyo bye mukola mu biseera eby'okubonaabona okungi bireeta empeera ey'emirembe n'emirembe, ekitiibwa kyayo okubonaabona kwammwe tekusaana kwogerwako; kale mukoowoole erinnya lyange ery'amaanyi, mmwe abantu bange! Obulokozi n'empeera ennene biri mu mukono gwange eri abo abawangula, kubanga bw'atyo Yeshua HaMashiach bw'asuubiza abeesigwa.

Laba, ebiseera bituuse!
