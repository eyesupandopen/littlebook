Obujulirwa Obubiri: Ekkanisa
Mu Mirimu gya Yokaana Omutume Omubatiza
Ogokutaano, Gatonnya 2024

Ggwe Omukuumi, Zuukuka!

Ggwe omukuumi, zuukuka, weesibe amazima mu kiwato, era wambale eky'omu kifuba eky'obutuukirivu!

Ggwe omukuumi, zuukuka, weetwalire enkuufiira ey'obulokozi n'ekitala eky'Omwoyo, kye Kigambo kya Katonda!

Ggwe omukuumi, zuukuka, era ebigere byo bibe nga byambadde okweteekateeka kw'Enjiri ey'emirembe!

Mulabire abantu bange era toluma lulimi lwo, nga bwe nsibudde olwo! Lwanirira abantu bange era olabule abalonde bange, kubanga empeera ya nnabbi gy'olifuna bw'onooyogera ebigambo bye nja okukuwa. ZUUKUKA! Ssaagambye bantu bange okulindirira okujja kwange? Ebiro n'ebiseera tebirabika era tebituuse ku mmwe? N'olwekyo, mulindirire okwolesebwa kwange, kubanga kuli kumpi. Mutunuulire obubonero n'ebyamagero mu bbanga, kubanga Kitange yakola entegeka y’emigendo gyabyo okuviira ddala ku lubereberye, era olunaku luteereddwako akabonero. Mulindirire okumasamasa kwange mu bbanga ng'ekkondeere linaatera okuvuga; laba, nkitegeezezza.

Bantu bange, mwekebejje Ekigambo kyange era mutunuulire Ebyawandiikibwa byange Ebitukuvu okutegeera nti nja kuggyawo omugole wange okuva mu musango gw’abakozi b'ebibi. Nja kukuuma omugole wange n'omubiri gwange; kubanga muntu ki eyakyawa omubiri gwe, naye aguliisa era agujjanjaba? Laba, weetegereze, kebera ebiseera, era omanye nti ebiseera bituuse era olunaku luliba lubumbulukuse mangu ddala ng'enjuba enkulu bw'evaayo mangu ku ttale. Siriganyibwa, era siriziyizibwa. Newankubadde omubbi aligezaako, aliwuluguma ng'empologoma; naye ye mpologoma etalina mannyo. Taliyinza kunziyiza, era siriganyibwa ebyange, kubanga nagula ensi naddala abantu bange mu musaayi gwange omutaliiko kibi. N'erinnya lyange liri waggulu wa gonna okuggyako erya Kitange, ansinga obukulu; era siriganyibwa, sirikugirwa, era siriziyizibwa.

Nzija, era sirisirika, n'obusungu bwange bubuguumiridde ku Babulooni Ekyama, yee, Babulooni Ekyama asuubula abaana abato okubatunda mu mikono gy'abasajja ababi, abaliziyizibwa. Kyandibadde kirungi singa tebaazaalibwa okusinga okuzaalibwa ne bakola ebibi ebitali mu mateeka era eby'entiisa ku baana. Kyandibadde kirungi olubengo lwe lusibiddwa mu bulago bwabwe, ne basuulibwa mu nnyanja; naye baterekeddwa olw'omuliro ogutaggwaawo, era obwenkanya bunaatera okukolebwa, kubanga obusungu bw'Omwana gw'Endiga bunaatera okubuna ensi yonna n'okubonaabona kwa Yakobo kutandike ng'omuziyiza aggyiddwawo.

Mwegendereze, bantu bange, era munywerere mu nze n'omutima gumu n'ekigendererwa ekimu. Muleke ebintu by'ensi bye mwegomba, era munteeke nze, Mukama wammwe Yesu Kristo, okuba okwegomba kwammwe; kubanga nze kigo kyammwe eky'amaanyi, olwazi lw'okwekuumirako, n'omukuumi wammwe ow'ennyuma. Ani alibajjira n'ayinza okugumira omukono gwange ogw'amaanyi? Abalonde bange era abeesigwa bali mu mikono gyange egigoloddwa, era tewali ayinza okubaggya mu nze. Muli bange, era muli bage mu nze; n'olwekyo muwummulire mu nze okuva mu mirimu gyammwe, kubanga ebiseera by'okuweebwa empeera biribumbulukuka mangu. Empeera y'omusango ku bakozi b'ebibi etuuse, n'empeera y'abeesigwa bange eri ku balonde bange, kubanga nja kubawa empeera yaabwe ey'obusika bwabwe, kubanga mbafudde abasaana.

Ayi ggwe ggombolola etalina mugaso, etalina mannyo; ggwe omusota ogw'edda: ekiseera kyo kituuse n'omusango guli ku ggwe. Newankubadde olowooza n'amalala nti olijja kuwangula, nja kutwala byonna by'okunze mu mikono gyo egibba, n'abo abakugoberera mu kwegomba kwabwe okululunkanira eby'obugagga baligabana naawe mu busika bwabwe mu nnyanja y'omuliro, awa buli muntu aliweebwa empeera okusinziira ku bikolwa bye emirundi mingi. Obusungu bwa Katonda bulibeera ku ggwe emirembe gyonna, era okuwummula tolikufuna.

Ekibonerezo ekinene kye kiriba omusanyusa wo, n'omukka ogukaawa ogw'ekibiriiti ekyaka gulibeera mu nnyindo zo emirembe gyonna, era olisasula emirembe gyonna. N'omukka gw'okubonyaabonyezebwa kwo gulinyooka emirembe gyonna, n'abo bonna abakugoberera okuva ku muto okutuuka ku mukulu balifuna obusika bwabwe n'empeera yaabwe awamu naawe mu nnimi z'omuliro ez'olubiriiti eza bbulu ezirikutuukirira emirembe gyonna ng'osibiddwa n'osuulibwa mu mpewo yo. Walibaawo okukaaba ennyo n'okuluma amannyo. Era mwalowooza nti muyinza okuyimirira eri Yakuwa ne Kristo we, naye mulireetebwa wansi okutuuka ku kunyoomebwa okutaggwaawo mu kubonyaabonyezebwa kwammwe okutakoma, era emirembe gyonna mulisibibwa. Era toliruma kitonde kyange nate, era olwerabirwa ddala, era tolijja mu birowoozo.

N'olwekyo bantu bange, omugole wange omwesigwa gwe nnjagala ennyo, kiki ekiriwo gye muli okutya? Obusungu bwange n'obusungu bwa Kitange si bwa mmwe era tebulibajjira, era temuliba mu kabi ka kufa kwa kubiri. N'olwekyo temutya nga bwe nja okuwoolera eggwanga ku balabe bammwe, kubanga okuwoolera eggwanga kwange, bw'ayogera Mukama! Temumanyi nti mulisalira bamalayika omusango? Temumanyi nti mulisalira ensi omusango? N'olwekyo mwetukuze mu nze, kubanga omusaayi gwange ogutangirira gwokka gwe gulibafuula abasaana okuyimirira mu maaso gange mu kitiibwa. Musibe ekigambo kyange mu mitima gyammwe, era mukuume ebiwandiiko byange mu byenyi byammwe, era muwummulire mu bigambo byange eby'edda; kubanga bulamu gye muli, n'obulamu bwammwe bukwekeddwa mu nze, era nja kububikkula!

Ayi, Babulooni Ekyama, singa wategeera ekiweebwayo eky'emirembe Kitange ky'akuweerezza, era kaakano ongaanyi nze, Yesu Omunazaaleesi, era kibaddewo okusinziira ku biwandiiko ebitukuvu nti abasajja ababi n'abawenzi balyeyongera okwonooneka. Naye ggwe, ayi Babulooni Ekyama omukulu, oyonoonyeyo omutayimbwa gwa Kitange ogw'amabala amangi gw'eyawa Nuuwa n'abantu bonna ng'ekisuubizo nti taliddamu kuzikiriza nsi yonna na mazzi, era ajja kukwata ekigambo kye. Naye ggwe, ayi Babulooni w'Ekyama, olijja kuggibwawo mu ssaawa emu.

Omuliro omunene gulikujjira; okukankana okunene kulikusaliza; ebigo byo birigwa; ebikolebwa byo biribula mu matongo, n'amayengo amanene galizingiramu ennyanja zo, era nja kukuwa empeera okusinziira ku bikolwa byo, n'omusango omunene gukuli ku ggwe. Kubanga lwali lubaale lw'essuubi n'omusana eri ensi okumala akaseera katono, naye olonze omululu, okwefaako wekka, n'obuli bw'enguzi okuba ababeezi bo, ayi malaaya omukulu! Ani alikulokola okuva mu nze n'obusungu bwange n'ekiruyi kyange ekisemberera ekikuli ku ggwe? Oyimiridde mu njegere, ng'olindirira omusango gwo ogw'enkomerero, ogulirabika ensi yonna erabe, n'okutya okungi kulijja ku bangi.

Laba! Amawanga mangi Kitange g'amenyeewo mu musango, kubanga asitula limu n'assa wansi eddala, kubanga Yakuwa ye afuga era tewali asinga Katonda omukulu ow'emirembe n'emirembe ayimirira n'alokola ababe okuva mu muliro oguterekeddwa ababi. Laba! Amawanga agaali gakola obwakabaka obunene gakyaliwo, naye mu ngeri eyakendeezebwa, naye geeyongeddeyo. Naye ggwe, ayi malaaya omukulu, weeyenze n'abagalwa bangi olw'omululu omubi n'essanyu ly'ekibi. Okuwadde abantu bange okuva mu nze mu nsi yonna olw'obugwagwa bw'owadde amawanga okulya n'okunywa, era olisuulibwa wansi ddala, era tolibaawo nate. Era mu vvu lyo teririvaamu ggwanga ddene, naye abasigaddewo balirokolebwa. Omukono gw'omulabe guli ku nsingo zammwe, era ndeese ab'ebweru mu nsalo zammwe. Balinyaga, balinyaga, era balitwala ebisigaddewo. Ani alikuyamba?

Kubanga ogaanyi muliraanwa wo, era tewali kutya Mukama mu mitima gyammwe. Ogambye nti, Nze siri nnamwandu, naye oli nnamwandu era muzibe w'amaaso era kiggala mu maaso gange. Tewali bulokozi gy'oli olw'obwenzi bwo bw'okoze n'ensi; n'olwekyo, nja kukuzikiriza ddala. Eby'okujja biribaawo mangu n'amangu; olwo amazzi amangi galibikka ebisambi byo. Omuliro gulikwokya ddala era gulirongoosa abakozi b'ebibi okuva mu nsi, n'abasibe bange balisumululwa, era tewali kiyinza kuziyiza ekigenda okubaawo. Nze Mukama Yesu Kristo, era nkubuulira nga tekinnabaawo, era ntegeeza ekigenda okubaawo ng'ekimaze okubaawo, kubanga bigambo bino bya mazima, era tewali kiyinza kubikyusa, kubanga obunnabbi bulituukirira.

Munywerere, bantu bange, nga bwe nja okukwata omugole wange; kubanga nnina entegeka n'ebigendererwa ku ye mu bwakabaka bwange obujja. Alibeera nange bulijjo, era talinvaako. Nkwagala era nkwagala mu kwagala okutuukiridde n'okusaasira. Nja kukufuula ow'amaanyi mu kitiibwa kyange, era olikola ebikolwa eby'amaanyi mu busika bwo obujja, n'olwekyo totya. Manya nti omutima gwange gumenyese, era nnakuwadde olw'abantu bange abateteeseteese. Abalala balisigalawo kubanga katonda w'ensi eno azibye amaaso gaabwe, era bangi tebeeteeseteese. Totya, newankubadde olibonaabona nnyo ku lwange, mmwenna abalina akabonero ka Katonda omulamu mulirokolebwa. Nja kubalabirira bwe mutaligaana linnya lyange, bwe, era ne munkolera mu mulimu gwe nnabawa.

Mulikola ebikolwa eby'amaanyi bwe mulinsaba okubatuma. Mbakooye era mbayagala nnyo, n'abo abakoowoola erinnya lyange ne bagumiikiriza okutuusa ku nkomerero balirokolebwa ne bazuukizibwa mu kitiibwa ekinene ne baweebwa empeera emirembe gyonna olw'okuyimirirawo kwammwe, nga bwe mbayagala era mbikiyogedde mu matu gammwe. Waliwo amakungula ag'enjawulo era buli limu mu kiseera kyalyo, n'olwekyo temutya; kubanga ebiseera ebizibu bulijjo biweebwa abasajja ba Katonda abakulu okuliisa ekisibo, era mbayise. Bamalayika bange baliteeka akabonero ku 144,000, era abamu ku mmwe mulibayamba mu mirimu gyabwe n'obubaka bwe nnabawa. Temutya, kubanga nja kukola obubonero bungi, ebyamagero, n'ebikolwa eby'ewuunyo ng'eyita mu mikono gyammwe, n'olwekyo muwummulire mu kwagala kwange era muwummulire mu maanyi gange; kubanga ndi nammwe era siribaleka era siribasuulirira.

Mulimanya nti nkwase omugole wange, era nja kuba nkunze abeesigwa gye ndi okuleeta okwenenya kw'abangi mu nsi efa. N'olwekyo ensi eyite era temugabana mu kabi kayo n'empewo yaayo kubanga si ya mmwe bwe mulitukuza Mukama mu mitima gyammwe ne mubeerawo nga mwawuddwa ku lwange. Kubanga obubi obunene busemberera, era tebulibangawo mu ngeri eno, era tebuliddamu kubaawo nate, baana bange abato, kubanga nkitegeezezza. Musanyukire mu kkubo lye mbateereddewo, era mukuume ebigere byammwe nga binywevu mu kkubo. Nja kubeera nammwe era nja kubawa emirembe egisinga okutegeerwa kwonna, era muliba n'emirembe wakati mu nsi ey'akavuyo, emirembe egiribakuuma bwe mutakyukira ku kkono newankubadde ku ddyo.

Temutwala kabonero, era temukkiriza bulimba. Balisaba emirembe n'obulamu n'obutebenkevu eri abo abatwala akabonero, era balibagamba nti muyingira mu nsi empya ey'emirembe n'obugagga n'obutebenkevu. Temukkiriza bulimba bwa musota, kubanga kabaka waabwe talikwata kigambo kye. Omusajja oyo ow'ekibi alina omulimu gumu gwokka, era gwe guno: okuzikiriza.

Tewali mazima mu ye, era Setaani yennyini y'alifuga amatemu ge. Tewaliba mirembe okuva mu ye, naye buli kigambo kiriba kya bulimba obusanyusa, obwakolebwa okukulembera ensi mu kuzikirira okutuukiridde. Era singa saafunza nnaku, tewandibaddewo mubiri ogwandirokoddwa okuva mu kibonyoobonyo ky'alireeta ku nsi, kubanga aliweebwa amaanyi okutuukiriza ebigendererwa byange okumala akaseera katono. Olwo alisibibwa mu njegere enzito ez'ekizikiza mu bunnya okusumululibwa akaseera katono okulimba ensi omulundi ogusembayo. Bw'alibakungaanyiza gy'ali, balitabaala ekibuga ekyo ekinene, n'omuliro oguva eri Katonda gulibazikiriza bonna. Olwo omusango ogw'enkomerero, yee, omusango gw'entebe ey'obwakabaka enjeru enene gulibajjira bonna, era tebaliba na ddagala. Mbabuulira bino okubawa essuubi n'okuteekateeka abantu bange, kubanga n'Ekyama Ekinene kya Katonda kirireetebwa mu kutuukirizibwa.

Nze kkubo, n'amazima, n'obulamu.

Nze Kabaka wa Bakabaka era Mukama wa Bakama.

Nze nsaana okusumulula obubonero, era ebiseera bituuse.

Nze ngezesa amatemu g'abalonde bange ndyoke mbafuule abalongoofu era mbalongoose mu musaayi gwange ogutangirira.

Nze Mwana wa Katonda eyajja mu mubiri, n'akomererwa, n'azuukira ku lunaku olw'okusatu, n'alabibwa abangi, n'ayanirizibwa mu ggulu mu maaso gaabwe.

Nze mwana wa Dawudi, era nja kukungaanya abasigaddewo ba Yakobo wansi w'emikono gyange egy'okubudaabuda n'amaanyi.

Nze nja okutuukiriza byonna Kitange by'ayagala.

Nnaatera okufuula abalabe bange entebbe y'ebigere byange, era zisanze abo abanziza, kubanga kinene ekiruyi ky'Omwana gw'Endiga.

Nze nja okugondera ebitonde bya Kitange ne mmuwa byonna mu mukono gwe, Katonda alyoke abeere byonna mu byonna.

Nze oyo obwakabaka bwe buli bwa mirembe n'emirembe, era nja kuwa abalonde bange okulya ku muti gw'obulamu n'okunywa ku migga gy'amazzi amalamu, era temuliddamu kulumwa njala newankubadde ennyonta, era nja kusangula amaziga gonna mu maaso gammwe.

Nze, Mukama, nkyogedde, era nja kukikola. Ekiseera kituuse, n'olwekyo mulindirire okwolesebwa kwange, kubanga tewali kiyinza kuziyiza mukono gwange ogw'amaanyi.
