Gatonnya 01, 2025

Nze Mukama Ow' Obutuukirivu Ataggwaawo!

Laba! Wuliriza ebigambo bya Yesu Kristo, era mukkirize ebirowoozo byammwe, mukomole emyoyo n'amatu gammwe mu kuwuliriza ebigambo bya Yeshua HaMashiach, nange ndibawa amagezi abo abagezigezi, omusajja ow'amagezi mu mmwe alyoke ayige okutya Mukama, era muyige okukankana mu maaso ga Mukama wa Majje, kubanga bw'atyo Yahweh, Nze Aliwo Omukulu bwe yantonda.

Ggwe ani, ggwe omuntu ow'obutonde, okusitula ekisinziiro kyo eri Yahweh, Nze Aliwo Omukulu, ye oyo Omukadde ow'Ennaku, ye oyo Mukama w'Emizimu? Ggwe ani ggwe omuntu ow'obutonde okwegulumiza mu malala g'okwewaana eri Yesu Omunazaaleeti, era ye Kristo Yesu, ye oyo erinnya lye liri waggulu w'amannya gonna, ye oyo Yahweh, Nze Aliwo Omukulu, gwe yagabana naye entebe ye ey'obwakabaka, ye oyo eyagulumizibwa okusinga byonna wabula Yahweh yekka okutuusa abalabe bange lwe balifuulibwa entebbe y'ebigere byange? N'obwakabaka bwange n'okufuga kwange tebikoma, era ndireeta Obwakabaka obugonze eri Kitange w'Ebitangaala, Adonai Yahweh alyoke abeere byonna mu byonna!

Ekitonde kisobola okugamba eyakitonda nti, Lwaki wantonda bwe nti? Ekibumbe eky'ebbumba ekibumbiddwamu eby'ekitiibwa kisobola okugamba omubumbi nti, Lwaki wantonda bwe nti? Lwaki ompadde ekitiibwa eking'ana wano? Nze njagala kubeera kirala, era mu kifo ky'ekyo nja kuba n'ekitiibwa kyabwe! Omubumbi yenna teyandizikirizza kitonde ekibi eking'ana awo ekimujeemera, nga kinaddirira n'okusaanyaawo omubumbi yennyini, awamu n'ebitonde bye ebirala; nga ekintu ekyo bwe kiba kisoboka?

Naye bwe zityo engeri ez'obusirusiru n'obulalu ez'abo abaagwa abaajeemera Omutonzi waabwe, nga bava mu biragiro byabwe n'emirimu gyabwe emitukuvu era egy'ekitiibwa gye baaweebwa okutuukiriza mu butuukirivu, amakubo geenyini agaweebwa Yahweh kennyini ku lunaku lwe yatonda abaana ba Katonda. N'abamalayika abatukuvu Yahweh be yakola nga tannakola nsi, abo abawandiikibwako mu Yobu bwe yakola ensi nti: Emmunyeenye ez'enkya zaayimba wamu, n'abaana ba Katonda bonna ne boogerera waggulu olw'essanyu! Era ddala baayogerera waggulu olw'essanyu ku bwewuunifu n'ebyamagero bye baalaba mu mikono gya Yahweh bwe yatonda ensi. Bwe batyo n'abamalayika abaagwa, n'abaana ba Katonda bwe baatondebwa mu kusooka ne bafuulibwa ab'ekitiibwa n'amaanyi olw'ebigendererwa byabwe, era Yahweh n'abambaza ekitiibwa n'abafuula abasinga mu magezi balyoke baagale Yahweh mu kuwuliriza okutuukiridde eddoboozi lye, era ddala baaweebwa omusingi omw'ekitiibwa gwe baatonderwamu mu kusooka, okutuusa lwe baajeema mu kwegomba kw'emyoyo gyabwe n'obutali butuukirivu ne busangibwa mu bo.

Bwe batyo ne Basitaani ne bajeema ku lubereberye eri Omutonzi waabwe, abo abaali beetoolodde entebe ya Yahweh ey'obwakabaka, ye oyo entebe ye ey'obwakabaka eyetooloddwa kati emirundi 10,000 okukubisaamu 10,000, naye Yahweh akyali Mufuzi era teyeetaaga muwi wa magezi. Naye bwe kityo bwe kyawandiikibwa ku luguudo olumyufu, olulina emitwe omusanvu, n'amayembe kkumi, n'engule omusanvu ku mitwe gyalwo nti omukira gwalyo gwasika eky'okusatu ky'emunyeenye z'eggulu, ne guzigwa ku nsi: bwe batyo ne Basitaani n'eky'okusatu ky'abamalayika abaagoberera obujeemu bwabwe ne basuulibwa ku nsi, mu nnaku ezo obwononefu bw'abamalayika lwe bwagwa okuva mu ggulu mu kibi n'obujeemu, ne bafuuka ekizikiza eky'amaanyi oluvannyuma lw'ebibi byabwe, era bwe batyo bwe bali ne leero.

Bwe ntyo omusota ne gusemberera nnyina w'abalina obulamu bonna mu lusuku Eden emyaka mingi egiyise, ne gusendasenda Kaawa okulya ku kintu Yahweh kye yabalagira obutalira ddala, kubanga ku lunaku lwe balirya ku muti gw'okumanya obulungi n'obubi, ku lunaku lwe lumu balifa ddala. N'olwekyo, Kaawa yakitwala okuva ku musota kubanga yasendasendebwa obulimba bwagwo n'alya, n'awa bbawe ku kibala kino ekigaane era Adamu n'alya kyeyagalire, era mu kufa baafa ddala nga bwe baasuubizibwa, era okuva olwo abantu baafugibwa okufa olw'ekibi ekisangibwa mu musaayi gwe. Kubanga Adamu ye yali omu ku bano aboonoonyeddwa emirembe n'emirembe mu kibi era olw'ekigendererwa kino Omwana w'omuntu yalabisibwa ndyoke nzikirize emirimu gya Sitaani, era ndyoke nkomyewo okunúnula kwa Adamu eyagwa kubanga nze Adamu ow'oluvannyuma Yahweh gw'afuulidde omuzimu oguwa obulamu. Mazima ddala, nditonda obuggya bonna abakkiriza okukkiriza kwange okw'obulokozi okubafuula ekitonde ekiggya era ekituukirivu kubanga Endagaano yange Empya bw'etyo bw'ereeta.

Adamu ne Kaawa baagobebwa mu lusuku Eden, ne bakyusibwa okuva mu butonde obw'ekyamagero obw'okumasamasa mwe baatonderwa mu kitiibwa eky'amaanyi Yahweh yennyini, era wansi w'okufugibwa ekibi n'obubi bwe kireeta baatambulatambulanga ku nsi mu kubonerezebwa kwabwe. Mu nnaku za Yeredi Abalabirizi baaserengesa ku lusozi Kerumooni, abo abaali basibiddwa okwegomba kw'emyoyo gyabwe kubanga baagomba abawala b'abantu, ne beegomba n'okubaako ezzadde okuva mu bo era mu kwegomba kw'emyoyo gyabwe mwe baakemebwa Basitaani ne beesiba mu bikolimo bya wamu, mu kirayiro kyabwe ekitatukuvu ekyakyuka ne kifuuka ekikolimo eky'emirembe n'emirembe ekituukirizibwa mu nnyanja ennene eyo ey'omuliro ogwaka, awali envunyu yaabwe etafa.

Bwe batyo abaana ba Katonda ne bayingira mu bawala b'abantu, ne beetwalira abakyala ku abo bonna be beeyagalira. Abakazi abamu baagenda na bwesige, ne beewaayo ddala mu bibi by'abo abaagwa (abaagenda okusalirwa omusango gwabwe), ate abamu ne batwalibwa ku mpaka ne batulugunyizibwa obutiribiri Abalabirizi mu kwegomba kwabwe okubi. Bwe ntyo ensi eyaliwo edda bwe yali, ensi eyazikirizibwa mu mataba amanene agaaliwo mu nnaku za Nuuwa eyasaanyizibwawo olw'ebizira ebibi ebyakolebwa abalabirizi, abaakolebwa okuzikiriza abantu n'okubalobera okubeera ku nsi gye baagomba era kati gye basibiddwa mu musango.

Kubanga beeyagalira okuzikirizibwa kwabwe n'okubonyaabonyezebwa okw'emirembe n'emirembe mu kifo ky'ebintu bye baagomba ebitabali byabwe okutwala, era ebintu ebyo bye baatwala, era bye bakyanoonya okutwala ku mpaka. Naye, ebikolwa byabwe n'entegeka zaabwe byonna bijja kufuuka bya bwereere, era tebagenda kuddamu kufuna ekyo kye bayita omyaka gwabwe ogwa zzaabu, wabula omusango omukakali, omungi era ogw'emirembe n'emirembe gwe balifuna. Kubanga ku mazima agatakyuka: Ensi ya Mukama n'okujjuza kwayo, era nze Mukama ow'Obutuukirivu ataggwaawo eyagula ensi mu musaayi gwange ogununula, era njija kutwala ku mpaka ekyo kyange eky'obutuukirivu, era ani alisobola okuyimirira okuziyiza amaanyi gange amangi?

Nga bwe kyawandiikibwa ku nze bwe kityo bwe kirikolebwa: Laba, Empologoma y'ekika kya Yuda, omulandira gwa Dawudi, ewangudde okubikkula ekitabo, n'okusumulula obubonero bwakyo omusanvu. N’olwekyo, tegeerera ddala nti nze nsaana okusumulula obubonero kubanga olupapula lw'ensi lulwange! Era nga bwe kyawandiikibwa ku nze bwe kityo bwe kirikolebwa kubanga nsaana okutwala ekitabo, n'okubikkula obubonero bwakyo: kubanga nattibwa, era nnunudde bonna abajja mu bulokozi obw'emirembe n'emirembe eri Yahweh olw'omusaayi gwange ogutalimu kibi, era mbavumuludde mu buli kika, n'olulimi, n'abantu, n'eggwanga: era tewali muntu n'omu ayinza okubakwata mu mikono gyange egy'emirembe n'emirembe! N’olwekyo, ani gw'olina okutya?

Bwe batyo abakazi abaatwalibwa abaana ba Katonda (abalabirizi abaagwa) ne baba embuto ne babazaalira ebikunnumpa ebizira ebyali ebibi okutuuka ku ntobo y'emyoyo gyabwe. Era mu bunene bwabyo n'amaanyi gaabyo mwe beesiga n'okwewaana, naye mu musango gwa Yahweh ku byo olw'obubi bwe baakola abantu yasiba emizimu gyabyo ku nsi ng'ekibonerezo kubanga byakolebwa ku nsi mu ngeri y'omubiri ogw'ekibi era emizimu gyabyo gyali gya lubeerera nga giva eri Bakitaabwe abo okwegomba kwabwe kwe basibiddwa okutuukiriza. Bwe batyo abaNefilimu bano bwe baafa mu misango gyabwe okuva eri Yahweh yennyini, emizimu gyabwe gyaggyibwamu emibiri mu kufa kwabwe, ne beeyongerayo, nga basibiddwa ku nsi ng'ekibonerezo. Kyebava balumwa enjala n’ennyonta naye tebasobola kulya mmere wadde okunywa amazzi. Lwadda lwe baalina emibiri eminene era egy'amaanyi gye baakozesanga okufuga abantu b'oku nsi, baafuulibwa abataliiko maanyi okuggyako nga basobodde okutwala omuntu gw'omulala gwe babeeramu ng'abawuka abalwanyisa endwadde ez'obulimba, kubanga bwe batyo bwe bafuuse. Tegeera, okuva mu nnaku za Nuuwa olw'okukubiriza kwa Nuuwa, ebitundu kyenda ku kkumi ku byo byakuumibwa mu bunnya obutakoma naye biri kumpi okuteebwa ku nsi mu musango.

Naye, bwe gubadde obujeemu bw'abo abaagwa okuva ku lunaku lwennyini obutali butuukirivu lwe bwasangibwa mu mitima gyabwe. Obulalu obwo n’obusirusiru bujjudde mu mitima gy’abo abaagwa mu bujeemu bwabwe; awamu n'omuntu ow'obutonde, eyabeegattako ng'agaana okuleka embeera ye eyagwa era ey'ekibi: nti bonna banoonya okufuuka ekintu ekirala okuggyako ekyo Yahweh ky'agenderera mu kutuukirira okutuukiridde kwonna. Mu kifo ky'okuwuliriza Omutonzi waabwe eyandikakasizza okutuukirizibwa kwabwe okutuukiridde, essanyu, omukisa, n'emirembe; eyandibawadde buli kimu mu ngeri etuukiridde mu kwagala kwe eri ekitonde kye, bo mu kifo ky'ekyo baanoonya ne bagoberera okwegomba kw'emitima gyabwe era beeyagaliza ensi yaabwe etaliimu Yahweh, mwe basobola okubeerera mu kwegomba kw'emitima gyabwe ne beeyagala mu sanyu ly'ekibi ku lwa ekitonde kya Yahweh. N'okwegomba okw'ekibi era okubi kwe batayinza kufuna wadde okutuukako.

Era bwe gutyo omuntu ow'obutonde bwe yeegasse mu bujeemu bwabwe wabula omuntu ow'obutonde azaalibwa mu butamanya ne mu kizikiza eky'ebigendererwa bye eby'amazima n'ekitiibwa kye ekisoboka Yahweh ky'asuubizza abawulize era omuntu ow'obutonde akyeyagalidde agenze mu makubo g'abo abaagwa, newankubadde, abaagwa banyikidde mu kuzikiriza Adamu, Adamu ky'atamanyi mu kwegomba kwe, n'okutuusa lw'asibibwa mu kusaba okw'obusiru ku bulimba bwabwe obungi era obw'obulimba. N'olw'obulimba obwo, bangi balitwalibwa mu nnyanja ey'omuliro ng'ekibonerezo ekyo tekyali kigenderere ku bo.

Naye, obujeemu buno bweyolekedde okuva emyaka n'emyaka kati, obujeemu obuzannyibwa mu lutalo lw'ensigo wakati w'ebitonde ebituukirivu ebya Yahweh n'ebizira ebitatuukirivu ebyakolebwa obukyusabukyusa bw'ababi, era si bakatonda newankubadde beefuula. Bwe zityo n'ennaku z'obeeramu, ggwe omuntu, ajeema mu butawuliriza obw'okukyawa okuva mu makubo g'omutonzi we, n'okwegatta mu butamanya n'abo abanyikidde ddala mu kufa kwo n'okuzikirira okw'emirembe n'emirembe kwe beegomba.

Era n'olwekyo ndangirira olunaku, Nze Yeshua HaMashiach, ndangirira nti olunaku lwa Mukama lutuuse era luli kumpi okusaasaanira ensi yonna mu musango omungi ogw'ekikambwe era ebizibu bingi kati biteekeddwa okuteebwa ku nsi, ebizibu n'emisango ebyalangirirwa edda ebiseera byabyo bye bituuse kati. N'olwekyo, tegeera bulungi ani nze akola ekirangiriro kino mu maanyi n'entebe ya Yahweh yennyini, kubanga nze nsaana okutwala ekitabo n'okukibikkula, n'okusumulula obubonero obukirimu, era nja kukola ekyo ddala, era ani ayinza okuziyiza omukono gwange ogw'amaanyi?!

Tegeera, ggwe omuntu ow'obutonde, nawe agaanira ddala Omutonzi wo, nti ebiseera byo bituuse. Era okwagala kungi n'ekisa n'okusaasira byaweereddwa mu biseera bino eby'omusango, kubanga bonna abakoowoola erinnya lya Yesu Omunazaaleeti okutuusa ku nkomerero yaabwe balimulokola era obuzito bw'empeera yaabwe tebusaana kugeraageranyizibwa na kubonaabona kwe balituuka okuyitamu olw'erinnya lyange. Tegeera, ggwe omuntu ow'obutonde, nti ndikulembera 144,000 bange ab'amaanyi okulokola Yakobo era ndikweka ensigalira ey'amaanyi eya Isirayiri erisigala nga nnamu okuyingira mu Bwakabaka bwange obw'emyaka olukumi mu kifo Yahweh ky'ategekedde mu ddungu, era balyoke baale n'okwaza n'okwesanyusa mu masavu g'ensi mu bwakabaka bwange obw'emyaka olukumi obuli kumpi okujja ku nsi okutuukiriza ekisuubizo, endagaano, n'obujulizi.

Bwe kityo bwe kiriba eri omuntu ow'obutonde, nti balikkiriza omwana gw'endiga okuba omulokozi waabwe oba baliba beeyagalidde omusango gwabwe ogw'emirembe n'emirembe mu nnyanja eyo eyaka emirembe n'emirembe kubanga tewali kulondawo kulala, era tewali kifo kirala gye bayinza kugenda kubanga Yahweh tajja kukkiriza bubi kwesenza na kuzikiriza kitonde kye n'ennaku zaabwe ez'obujeemu obw'amawaggali ziri ku nkomerero.

Nze mwana gw'endiga owa Katonda, eyagabibwa olw'ebibi by'ensi.

Nze Mpologoma y'Ekika kya Yuda, era mu butuukirivu njija okusala omusango n'okulwana.

Nze oyo Omwana gw'endiga n'Empologoma y'Ekika kya Yuda mwe bigattiddwa mu ngeri etuukiridde mu bigendererwa byange eby'emirembe n'emirembe n'okufuga nga Kabaka wa Bakabaka ne Mukama wa Bakama, kale ani asobola okuyimirira okuziyiza Yeshua HaMashiach oyo Omutukuvu wa Adonai, era oyo Yahweh gw'atumye okutuukiriza omusango ogw'emirembe n'emirembe ku abo abaagwa ne bonna abalonda okwegatta mu makubo gaabwe.

N'olwekyo nkubiriza abeesigwa mu Kristo okusigala nga beesigwa ddala, kubanga olunaku lw'okununulibwa kwammwe lutuuse era luli kumpi. Mutunuulire Mukama wammwe ow'Obutuukirivu ataggwaawo yekka, kubanga abagwa bafukiridde ensi n'omujoozi gw'obulimba n'obukuusa ogutegekeddwa okubuzaabuza n'abalonde, singa ekyo kyandisobose. N'olwekyo, mutegeere nti bannabbi ab'obulimba bangi batumiddwa, abalagula mu makubo ga Balamu era abawambiddwa emyoyo gya Yezebeeri era aboogera n'emyoyo gy'omulabe wa Kristo, abayigiriza Yesu omulala, oyo ayimirira mu kifo kya Yesu Kristo, ye Yeshua HaMashiach, mu mitima gy'abo abalimbiddwa. Bonna abagudde mu bubuzaabuza obwo balisalirwa omusango singa bagaana okwenenya okuva mu makubo gaabwe, bwe balisisinkana Mukama waabwe mu mimwa gy'abeesigwa mu Kristo. Mazima ddala, abamu ku bannabbi ab'obulimba baba beesimbu era nga nabo babuusibwabuusibwa era bayinza okutuukirirwa n'amazima agoogererwa ku mimwa egijjudde okwagala, naye amazima gokka ge galibata, era amazima amatuufu agakwata ku bibi byabwe n'obunnabbi bwabwe obw'obulimba ge galisobozesa okwezza obuggya mu kwenenya.

Awo ate wabaawo abo abalagula olw'ensimbi, n'ebintu ebibi, n'okufuna ettutumu n'okwolesebwa era n'okwetaakiriza mu bintu by'ensi eno embi. Newankubadde omuntu alagula obulimba y'ani, mumunenye era mwegaane Ebigambo bye, n'ebigambo ebyategekebwa abagwa okuteega n'abalonde bennyini mu bubuzaabuza singa kisoboka. Lwaki muddukana eno n'eri, mmwe Ekanisa yange eyeesigwa? Sibagambye era sibawadde amagezi era sibawadde maanyi okuba abawanguzi bange? N'abo abawangula buli kitundu ky'obwakabaka bw'omulabe mu ngeri y'emu nga nze bwe nawangula? N'olwekyo, mujje eri Kristo Yesu nange ndikakasa mu mitima gyammwe bannabbi bange ab'amazima, kubanga bannabbi bange ab'amazima boogera mu kwatagana n'omutima gwa Mukama waabwe, ye Kigambo eyafuuka omubiri. N'olwekyo, Kristo Yesu tayinza n'akatono kwesambirira byawandiikibwa, kubanga Nze ndi ekyo!

Abo abalowooza n'amalala nti banyimirirawo ku lwange naye nga bali mu bulimba: Lwaki muddukana eno n'eri, nga munoonya omuntu anaabasiimuula amatu, nga munoonya n'abalogo abavuvung'ana n'abanyenya emitwe abalibagamba ddala kye mwagala okuwulira okusitula okwegomba okuli mu mitima gyammwe emibi, bwe mudduka okuva mu kwenenya okudda mu mikono gy'abalimba abatumiddwa okubuzaabuza. Muddukana eno n'eri kubanga munyooma eddoboozi lya Yeshua HaMashiach, kubanga mugaana amazima agandibatasizza, kubanga mugaana okuyigira ku nze nga mukutte Ekigambo kya Yahweh ng'omutindo gwammwe ogusinga waggulu era ogumu gwokka. N'olwekyo, mpa amagezi bonna abalowooza nti balagulira Kristo Yesu, n'abo bonna abalowooza nti boogerera Yahweh, NZE ALIWO Omukulu, okwekuuma ffe basireke bagwe mu kizikiza eky'olubeerera n'omusango ogutaggwaawo, kubanga bonna abeegatta ku bagwa mu ngeri y'emu ne bagaana okwenenya, amannya gaabwe galisangulwa mu kitabo ky'obulamu singa bagaana omuliro ogulongoosa, gwe balirekerwa singa basigala mu bibi byabwe, kubanga tewakyali kiseera.

N'olwekyo mpa amagezi era ndabula abo be njagala baleme okugwa mu bubuzaabuza obw'amaanyi obutabategekerwa, singa batambulira mu kuwuliza Mukama waabwe; era mpa amagezi era ndabula abo be njagala baleme okutegulwa obulimba obunene obujjudde ensi yonna kaakano. Mpa amagezi bonna abaliwulira Ebigambo bino okuva mu bubi n'okuva mu balogo abavuvung'ana n'abanyenya emitwe, era muggye ebifo byammwe ku mmeeza ya Yezebeeri era mwegaane empeera ya Balamu. Mutegeere nti omuntu yenna alagula ebigambo ebivuganya ebyawandiikibwa aba ayogera bulimba mu bunnanfuusi. Mutegeere nti Yahweh, NZE ALIWO Omukulu, ne Yeshua HaMashiach (era ye Kristo Yesu) tebalisambirira na katono byawandiikibwa. N'olwekyo, abo aboogera okuvuganya ebyawandiikibwa, baba bavuganya Yahweh NZE ALIWO Omukulu, era bavuganya ne Yeshua HaMashiach era ye Kristo Yesu.

Mutegeere ddala Nze ani, era Yahweh bwe yantonda okuba. Nze Kigambo kya Yahweh ekyefuula omubiri ekibeera mu mmwe, baagalwa bange, era siyinza kwesambirira wadde okulimba, kubanga sirina kibi era ndisigala sirina kibi emirembe gyonna. Mutegeere Kitaffe w'ebitangaala, atalina nkyukakyuka wadde ekisiikirize eky'okukyuka; ye oyo gwe mulina okukolagana naye! Mutegeere nti tekiyinzika Adonai Yahweh okwesambirira kubanga tekiyinzika Adonai Yahweh okulimba. Naye, lwaki mu busirusiru obwenkanidde awo muwuliriza abo abayita mu nkukutu mu kiro? Munaayenda mmwe baagalwa bange, so nga mugwanidde okuba abeesigwa era abawulize eri Mukama omu, ye Mulokozi wammwe ow'emirembe n'emirembe, ye oyo ajja okubaggyawo abeesigwa mu Kristo okubatwala mu ggulu ekiseera kyonna okuva kati?

N'olwekyo mutegeere nti enzigi z'e Ggeyeena tezirawangula beesigwa mu Kristo, be Kanisa yange ey'amazima, kubanga bwe kyawandiikibwa era ne kisuubizibwa edda, era ne leero kikyalimu mu byawandiikibwa n'ebisuubizo, kubanga Ebigambo bya Yahweh ne Yeshua HaMashiach bigambo birongoofu ng'effeeza, egezeseddwa mu kikoomi ky'ettaka, erongooseddwa emirundi musanvu. Yee, abagwa tebalina maanyi ku beesigwa mu Kristo, era ndireetera abeesigwa bange okulinnyirira buli kitundu ky'obwakabaka bw'omulabe, nga balinnyirira n'abo abandinoonyezza okuziyiza ekitaziyizika.

Nze Kristo Yesu, era ani ayinza okuyimirira okuwakanya amaanyi n'obuyinza bwange bye nafuna okuva eri Yahweh yennyini!

Mutegeere nti Nze ne Kitange tuli bumu mu kwenyigira n'ekigendererwa, era bulijjo nkola bya Kitange by'ayagala! N'olwekyo, tewali ayinza kunzira, era tewali kintu kyonna mu bagwa ekiyinza okunwangula, oba mu bukujjukujju, oba mu maanyi, oba mu bungi, oba mu tekinologiya waabwe atagasa! Era Nze oyo eyakakasa obutawangulwa bwabwe obw'olubeerera ku Gologooya bwe njogera Ebigambo nti, kiwedde. Ani ku mmwe eyandyekweese ng'omuti mu maka ge? Ani ku mmwe eyandyasirise mu maaso g'obulimba bwabwe obubi? Ani ku mmwe eyandyegondezezza empologoma eyo eterina mannyo erindiridde omusango gwayo? Muwulire okuwuluguma kw'Empologoma y'Ekika kya Yuda eyimiridde okulwanirira mmwe era mutegeere ebigendererwa byange mu nnaku zino ez'enkomerero nga n'okubonaabona kwa Yakobo tekunnatandika! Lwaki mutya n'okukankana abalabe bammwe abawanguddwa? Muntunuulire mu kukkiriza nange ndibawa Ebigambo eby'okwogera okubasiba, n'okubagoba, n'okusirisa abo abandyabanyigirizza mu busiru bwabwe abanoonya okubasiba mu musango gwabwe n'obulimba bwabwe obubakakiddwa bwe banoonya okuteega amayiba gange.

N'olwekyo, mbalagira okuba n'okukkiriza mu Mukama wammwe Ataggwaawo era mukkirize Katonda ow'Obutuukirivu Ataggwaawo okubajjuzamu amaanyi ag'omunda mu maaso ga Mukama wammwe, Kristo Yesu! N'olwekyo, mube n'okukkiriza mu mitima gyammwe era mugambe olusozi luno nti, ggyibwawo! Era lulisuulibwa mu nnyanja n'amaanyi gange amangi.
