Mu Mirimu gya Yokaana Omutume Omubatiza
Maaki 09, 2024

Lwaki Mwegaana Omutonzi Wammwe?

Mukama yampa ebigambo bino. Si byange, era mbigabye nga sirongooseeko wadde okuggyako, nga bwe nsobola.

Lwaki mukyagaana Omutonzi wammwe n'amakubo ge? Tabawadde bulamu butaggwaawo? Tabawadde mugaati gwa bulamu, ku bwereere eri bonna abasaba? Tabawadde ddembe okuva mu kitonde kino ekigudde okuyita mu nze, Mukama ow'ekitiibwa era omwesigwa Yesu Kristo? Nze kkubo, n'amazima, n'obulamu. Nze Kabaka wa bakabaka era Mukama wa bakama, era nawaayo obulamu bwange ku musaalaba ku lwammwe, mulyoke musobole okutambulira mu bwakabaka bwange singa mukkiriza ekirabo eky'obulamu obutaggwaawo. Sibayiseeko mannya? Lwaki mukyeyerowooza nti temusaana kulokoka, so ng’omusaayi gwange ogutangirira gujja kubafuula abasaana singa mukowoola erinnya lyange ne mumfuula Mukama? Nze Mwana wa Katonda, Omwana w'Omuntu, Kristo: Yesu Omunazaaleeti, Mukama omwesigwa, era Mukama ow’ekitiibwa! Tewali mukulu okuggyako Katonda, eyampa ebintu byonna.

Laba! Olunaku olw'akabi luli ku mmwe, olunaku olw'omusango n'okubonaabona. Ebibi bya Amerika biri ng’ebibi bya Sodoma ne Ggomola, era singa tasalira musango malaya omukulu, yandibadde si mwesigwa eri emirembe gye yaggyawo mu kusala omusango. Omwenzi omukulu oyo, eyeegomba omubiri omulala, eyenyigidde ennyo mu kuzimba ensengekera y’ensolo eri omwana w'okuzikirira, ddala, atambulira mu mmwe ng'alimbalimba abalonzi bange n'obudde bwe nga tebunnatuuka. Omusango omunene n'akabi bijja kugwawo mangu ddala. Amerika, osaliddwa omusango okuva ebweru era osaliddwa omusango okuva munda.

Omukono gw'abalabe bo gunaatera okuba ku bulago bwo, era ojja kuggyibwawo n'amaanyi, era amawanga tegakyanywa ku bugwenzi bwo obw'obwenzi bw'olongoosezza nabo bonna. Abalabe bo baweereddwa obuwanguzi ku ggwe, wadde nga weesiga mu buwumu amaanyi go ag'amagye ge nakuggyeko. Oli ggwanga eriri mu njegere, eririndirira okujjukirwa kwo okusembayo mu maaso ga Mukama! Tewali kikulu kyonna ku ggwe, wabula ebibi byo n'obutali butuukirivu byo kivundu mu nnyindo za Kitange.

Oli salirwa omusango okuva munda, kubanga okukankanyizibwa okw'amaanyi, okw'effuttwa, kujja kukumalirawo mangu ddala era awatali kuwonyezebwa. Omwenzi omukulu ajja kusuulibwa wansi ng'okulabula eri bonna era kijja kuba ntandikwa ya ssaawa ey'ekizikiza ennyo. Tajjuddamu kuggyawo mawanga mu butali bwenkanya.

Tajjuddamu kutawaanya nsi n'obutali butuukirivu bwe. Eby'omuzizo bye tebijjuddamu kusendasenda bantu bange. Omwenzi omukulu ayonoonye endagaano ya Kitange, musoke ogwo ow'ekitiibwa gwe yawa Nuuwa ng'obukakafu obw'okutaddamu kujjuza nsi nate. Newankubadde ensi tejjuddamu kujjuzibwa, amayengo g'okuzikirizibwa gajja kugwa ku bangi, era omuliro ogwokya gwonna gusemberedde.

Bantu bange, temubaako na nsa ya kibi kye, ebikolimo, emisango, n'akabi. Nja kukyateekateekera ekifo ku mmeeza yange singa munkyukirira n'omutima gwammwe gwonna.

Mwenenye! Mwatule ebibi byammwe gye ndi, Kristo Yesu! Temusomanga nti abatali batuukirivu tebalisikira Bwakabaka bwa Katonda? Mwandyagadde empeera yammwe okunyagibwa ne giweebwa omulala? Temulowooza nti muliyingira mu maaso gange nga muli mu kugaana okw'omutima; Kitange teyalangirira nti, "Ndi mutukuvu, nammwe mubeere batukuvu?" Muddukire gye ndi era mweggye mu kuwolokoka kwange, kubanga nze Mukama wa bakama era Kabaka wa bakabaka, era ennaku ez'okwegayirira omulembe ogw'ensingo enkalu zigenda ku nkomerero.

Mmaze ebbanga ki nga ngolola omukono gwange ogulokola gye muli, naye mmwe mugaana Katonda wa bajjajjammwe n'ekisa kyange ekilokola ekisobola okubalokola okutuuka ku nkomerero? Mujje gye ndi mwekweke mu kifuba kyange; mazima, nja kubakweka okuva ku lunaku olunene olw'akabi oluli ku mmwe. Temwegatta naabo abaagaana erinnya lyange; Kristo talina kikwatagana na Beryaali, naye abantu bange bakyalogeddwa omusana gwe ogw'obulimba. Mukaabe amaziga era mwenenye olw'engeri zammwe ez'ekibuguumirize. Muddeyo eri okwagala kwammwe okusooka era munoonye amaaso gange ng'obudde bukyaliwo.

Omusaayi gwange ogutangirira gujja kubanaaza singa muva mu bibi byammwe. Nze Mwana w'Omuntu, kabona asinga obukulu emirembe gyonna ng'engeri ya Merukizeddeeki bw'eri. Nze oyo eyeekuuma nga mulongoofu mu maaso g'ebikemo byonna, era n'akemebwa Setaani yennyini naye n'akyawangaala. Nze Mwana omu yekka owa Katonda azaalibwa eyafuuyira omwoyo omutukuvu ku bonna abankyukiridde n'abo bonna abalikyuka gye ndi. Nze, Yesu Kristo, nze bino byonna n'ebisingawo, era mbakangavvula abo be njagala ensonga y'okuyitibwa kwabwe ebeere ntuufu era etuukiridde.

Ggwe omuntu, ebibi byo bingi, naye weerangirira okuba omutukuvu! Ebibi by'obwenzi n'obwa malaya. Ebibi by'eddagala eritta, obulogo, n'eby'obufumu bingi. Ab'omululu, abatasaasira, abeeyagala, abagala eby'essanyu, n'abagala ssente. Kirikufuuka ki okukkiriza kwammwe mu bugagga singa obugagga bwammwe bufuuka obutalina mugaso amangu ago n'obwavu n'okuzikirira okungi ne kubajjira mangu ddalo? Mulivvoola mu maaso g'ekisa? Mulikyasalawo ebibi byammwe? Abo abasalawo ebibi byabwe n'obukakanyavu balizikirira mu byo. Temulowooza nti Katonda asekererwa, kubanga anaatera okufuula abasekerera ekisekererwa; temubeera na nsa nabo.

Essaawa yakedde, era eddakiika ezisembayo zisemberedde era obudde bunaatera okuggwaawo mu mirembe gyange egy'ekisa, era obusungu bujja kutwala ensi ng'Ekkanisa ey'amazima eggibwawo mu maaso gammwe. Sirina ssanyu lya kulaba kitonde kyange nga kizikirizibwa olw'okusobya n'obutali butuukirivu; naye sibawadde kulondawo kubeera mu bwakabaka bwange, naye bangi n'obukakanyavu bagaana? Mufulumemu! Mubuulirire era muleke amakubo gammwe; munjatule, Yesu Kristo, nga Mukama, era muleke okwonoona.

Ssiwadde emirembe mingi eri batabani bange ne bawala bange, abawangudde ebibi byabwe olw'omusaayi gwange gwennyini? Naye bangi bakyagaana obujulizi bwabwe n'okutegeeza kwabwe era bakyagaana okukkiriza, ne basalawo amalala gaabwe okusinga amakubo gange amatoowaze. Amakubo gammwe ge mutego era gajja kubatuusa ku kuzikirira okw'olubeerera, kwe mutayinza kuwonyezebwa. Munkyukirire nga kukyaliwo eddakiika ntono okukikola. Essaawa yakedde, era bonna abakoowoola erinnya lya Mukama balilokolebwa!

Mbakubiriza okugabana mu kitiibwa ky'entebe yange ey'obwakabaka n'okukkiriza obulamu nange. Muve mu makubo gammwe mudde gye ndi, kubanga nze Mukama ataggwaawo era tewali bulokozi mu mulala yenna wabula mu nze! Nze, Yesu Kristo, ndi Mwana wa Katonda era nnajja mu mubiri, nnakomererwa ku musaalaba, nnazuukizibwa ku lunaku olw'okusatu, nnafuulibwa omwoyo oguwa obulamu era ne ntwalibwa mu ggulu okutuusa ebiseera lwe birituukirira. Mazima, ekiro kiyiseeko nnyo n'essaawa yakedde, n'ebiseera eby'okuddizibwawo bisemberedde.

Bantu bange, munywere mu maanyi gange era mubeere mirembe. Muwummulire mu nze era ebbanga ttono erikyaliwo okugulumizibwa kwammwe kujja kujja mangu ddala, era empeera nnene eterekeddwa gye muli mu nnyumba ya Kitange. Ebintu bye mbategekedde tebyayingira na mu mutima gwammwe, mazima, si mu mutima gw'omuntu yenna. Emirembe n'obutebenkevu mulibifuna, era mmwe ababadde abeesigwa ku bintu bitono nja kubasasula nnyo. Muwummulire mu nze era mutunuulire nze. Mweteekerereewo okusiiba era muddemu amaanyi mu ssaawa eno esembayo era mulangirire amakubo gange amatuukirivu n'omusango gwange ogusemberedde, kubanga n'abangi balilokolebwa.

Mbalaze okusaasira kungi, okugumiikiriza kungi, ekisa eky'enkumu, naye temukyagabira balala bino. Mulifiirwa empeera yammwe ne munkakasa okugiwa omulala? Oba mulijagala Katonda n'omutima gwammwe gwonna, n'obulamu bwammwe, n'amagezi gammwe, n'amaanyi gammwe ne muliraanwa wammwe nga bwe mweyagala? Lwaki munyooma muliraanwa wammwe ne mumusalira omusango mu nsobi nti tasaanira bulamu butaggwaawo nga muyimiridde awo mu kasirise? Sibawadde byama byange kubirangirira eri bonna? Munywere mu nze, nange nja kubawa amaanyi okutuuka ku abo omubi b'agezaako okukweka okuva mu mukono gwange ogulokola. Yasamye akamwa ko olangirire ebigambo byonna eby'obulamu buno n'ebigambo eby'okwogera nja kubikuwa. Gabana okwagala kwo okutuusa ku ddakiika esembayo, kubanga eddakiika eyo esemberera n'akatutti nga kagenda okuyita abaana bange okudda eka.

Nze Mukama omwesigwa atalemwa.

Nze musana gw'ensi, era buli akoowoola erinnya lyange ery'amaanyi talizikiririra mu nnimi z'omuliro ezitazikira; ku abo, okufa okw'okubiri tekulina buyinza.

Nze ow'amaanyi okusinga mu bavumu, era ebintu byonna mu ggulu ne ku nsi biweereddwa mu mikono gyange okutuusa okuzzaawo lwe kulituukirira, abalabe bange lwe baliba entebbe y'ebigere byange. Nze kkubo, n'amazima, n'obulamu.

Nze Kabaka wa Bakabaka era Mukama wa bakama.

Nze Mwana wa Katonda, Omwana w'Omuntu, Kristo: Yesu Omunazaaleeti. Ndi mutukuvu, nammwe mubeere batukuvu.

Nze kabona asinga obukulu emirembe gyonna ng'engeri ya Merukizeddeeki bw'eri. Nze oyo eyeekuuma nga mulongoofu mu maaso g'ebikemo byonna.

Nze Mukama ataggwaawo, era tewali bulokozi mu mulala yenna wabula mu nze!
