Omujulirwa Ow'okubiri: Isirayiri
Ekikaawa Ekirimu Obuwoomu
Novembba 20, 2024

Omwana gw'Endiga n'Empologoma Bifuuse Kimu!

Mujje eri Yeshua HaMashiach, mmwe ab'oluganda lwange, mutegeere omulimu gw'Omununuzi wammwe, kubanga nze mununuzi wammwe ow'oluganda. Kubanga nze agenda okununula ensigalira ya Yakobo balyoke babeerewo nga bakyali balamu mu maaso g'Ow'amaanyi NZE NDI, ye YHVH, Katonda omukulu ataggwaawo era ow'emirembe gyonna. Ate okufuga kwange n'obwakabaka bwange tebiribaako nkomerero, kyemunaava muwuliriza Ebigambo bya Mukama wammwe ow'emirembe gyonna ow'Ekisuubizo, ye Yeshua HaMashiach, era ye Yesu Kristo, era ye Kristo Yesu, kubanga bw'atyo Ow'amaanyi NZE NDI bwe yannonda okuba bwe ntyo emirembe n'emirembe.

Era mazima ddala Yahweh yampa erinnya erisinga amannya gonna mu buli kintu, erinnya lya Yesu buli vviivi lyonna lifukaamirire, eby'omu ggulu n'ebyo ku nsi era n'ebyo wansi w'ensi. Kubanga nze oyo Yahweh gwe yawa ebintu byonna mu ggulu ne ku nsi mu mikono gyange egy'amaanyi olw'okutuukiriza ebintu byonna. Kyemunaava muwulira, ggwe Isirayiri, eddoboozi lyange mu kugonda okw'okwagala nga mudda mu kwagala kwe nnabalaga bwe nnafa mu kifo kyammwe mu kubonaabona okw'entiisa okutaali kwange kugumiikiriza. Naye mu kwagala kwange kwe nnina gye muli, nnatwala ekifo kyammwe ndyoke mbakuule mu kibonerezo kya Musa, ekibonerezo ekyo ekizibu ennyo gye muli okwesasulira olw'ekibi ekiri mu mibiri gyammwe. Era mazima ddala nnatuumibwa mu nsi olw'okununula Adamu.

Kubanga Musa mutuukirivu era mutukuvu era wa mazima, era ddala Musa abeera mu Masiya wammwe ow'amazima era Mukama ow'emirembe gyonna ow'Obutuukirivu, kubanga nze bw'entyo. Era nze oyo eyakwata amateeka ga Musa mu bujjuvu olw'okwagala kwange n'okugonda kwange eri Yahweh, eyankulembera mu kwagala kwe okutaggwaawo eri olulyo lwa Adamu olwayonoonwa era ne luwangulwa abagwi, kyekyava kiweebwa ekisa n'okusaasira mu bungi obungi ennyo, okusobola okuziyiza obukambwe bw'omusango n'obusungu mu bujjuvu, okusobola okwawula okw'enkomeredde okubaawo wakati w'eŋŋaano ya Yahweh n'omuddo gw'abagwi, era bwe kityo abo bonna abasikira obulamu obutaggwaawo mu Kristo Yesu balinunulibwa olw'okulonda kwabwe okwegondera Yeshua HaMashiach, ye Kristo Yesu, era ye Yesu Omunazaaleeti.

Era Yahweh yantuma eri amawanga, era nnaweerereza ebika bya Isirayiri ebyabula, kubanga nnagaanibwa ab'oluganda lwange mu butakkiriza obutassaamu kitiibwa, n'okusingira ddala bakabona abakulu ne Sanhedrin abaweereza Gguluddene mu nkukutu, kubanga baava mu Esawu eyeegatta mu bukyayi bwe yalina ku muganda we Yakobo eri Ashikenaazi owa Yafesi, balyoke beekungereza ebintu ebyange era balyoke bafumite Yakobo ekiwundu eky'enkomeredde ku mutwe, Yahweh ky'atajja kukkiriza, era ekikolwa ky'atajja kukkiriza nga kiyita mu mukono gwange ogw'amaanyi era ogugonda bulijjo. Kyemunaava, mmwe ab'oluganda lwange, mwegondere eddoboozi lyange mulyoke mubeerewo nga mukyali balamu mu maaso gange, era mulyoke mubeerewo nga mukyali balamu mu maaso ga Yahweh, omuntu yenna gw'atasobola kusemberera nga talina Yesu Kristo kubanga Yahweh bw'atyo bwe yalonda mu nteekateeka ye ey'emirembe gyonna ey'okununula n'amagezi. Kale mutegeere nti awatali Masiya wammwe ow'amazima, ye Yeshua HaMashiach, musigala bafudde mu byonoono n'ebibi byammwe bye nnawangula bwe nnasasula mu bujjuvu ekibonerezo kya Musa olw'emirembe gyonna era eri bonna abalonda okukkiriza ssaddaaka yange.

Kale bwe kityo Musa abeera mu Yeshua HaMashiach, era nze oyo Musa gwe yalagira okugondera kwammwe okujjuvu, kyemunaava muwulira ebigambo byange era mukomole emitima gyammwe n'amatu gammwe okuwulira bye njogera. Kubanga emitima gyammwe gifuuse migayaavu mu butawulize, era mujjuzizza amatu gammwe n'amagezi ga Esawu era mwonoonese emitima gyammwe n'obulimba bwabwe obw'okuvvoola, obwa Talmud. Mutegeere nti Musa atuukiridde era wa mazima era mutukuvu era omuntu yenna bw'ayita amateeka ga Musa nti gakolimidde, kale omuntu oyo akolimirwe olw'okuvvoola kwe, era ng'asinziira ku mateeka ga Musa ge yakolima. Kyennava mbategeeza nti amateeka gaaweebwa okulumiriza ekibi mu mubiri, kubanga awali amateeka, ekibi tekyogerwako ng'okusinziira ku mateeka, wadde nga ekibi kibeera mu mubiri kye kimu. Kyekiriva, mu bikolwa by'amateeka ga Musa tewali mubiri na gumu gulituukirizibwa mu maaso ga Yahweh: kubanga mu mateeka ga Musa mwe muva okumanya ekibi.

Naye, nga bwe nnawandiisa Pawulo eri Ekkanisa yange mu mirembe gyange egy'Ekisa, nti kaakano obutuukirivu bwa Katonda bubikkuddwa awatali mateeka ga Musa, nga bulina obujulirwa bw'amateeka n'obwa Bannabbi. Mmwe ab'ebyama byange eby'edda abaagwa okuva mu mugabo gwammwe ogwali guweereddwa eggwanga lyammwe eddene ennyo Yahweh yennyini kubanga mwalina okuba abantu ab'enjawulo, abayawuddwa ku mawanga! Naye mwang'aana kye kimu nga bajjajjammwe mu kujeema kwammwe okw'emitima emikakanyavu okulwanyisa ebigendererwa n'enteekateeka eby'emirembe gyonna ebya Katonda Omuyinza wa byonna, ye El Shaddai. Kale bwe kityo olw'okugaana kwammwe Ebigambo byange ebyaweebwa mu njiri y'obulokozi bwe nnabikkulira abatume bange abatukuvu ne bannabbi ekyama kya Katonda ekikulu, mufuuse ng'abeetaaga amata, era temusobola kukuba ku mmere enkalu kubanga mukyali baana bato abatalina kutegeera kujjuvu, wadde nga mu malala mwebala ng'abagezigezi ab'amagezi era eby'okumulisa eby'ekitangaala naye mwamulisibwa bubi abagwi okubuza abantu mu makubo gammwe agatali mayigirize.

Kyemunaava mutegeera, mmwe ab'oluganda lwange, nti omutuukirivu aliba mulamu lwa kukkiriza, era nti mu bikolwa by'amateeka tewali mubiri na gumu gulituukirizibwa! Kyawandiikibwa mu biwandiiko ebitukuvu ebyaweebwa emikono gya Pawulo, eyali ddala Omubenyamini omugonvu, eyali mu kusooka atawulize era mukakanyavu mu kugaana kwe eri Yesu Omunazaaleeti, kye kimu nammwe: Kale abo ab'okukkiriza, baweebwa omukisa wamu ne Ibulayimu omukkiriza. Kubanga bonna ab'ebikolwa by'amateeka, bali wansi w'ekikolimo ky'amateeka: kubanga kyawandiikibwa nti, Akolimidde buli muntu atanywera mu byonna, ebyawandiikibwa mu kitabo ky'Amateeka, okubikola.

Naye, olw'obubi, obukujjukujju, n'amagezi g'omusota ku lunaku Adamu lwe yagwa okuva waggulu; ekibi kifugidde mu mubiri okuva olwo, era ekifaananyi kya Adamu ekyonooneddwa si ye maka gammwe, era temuteekeddwateekeddwa kumala emirembe gyonna mu nnyumba z'ebbumba, ennyumba ezo eziddira mu nfuufu y'ensi gye mwaggyibwa. Kyekyava Yahweh anfumbye omwoyo oguwa obulamu, era nze Adamu ow'oluvannyuma. Era nze oyo eyakolebwa ng'ekifaananyi eky'obulamu obutaggwaawo, mmwe mwenna mulyoke mutondwe buto mu kujjula n'obumu, era ng'omuntu omu omujja. Kubanga tewalibaawo ngeri bbiri za bantu emirembe gyonna, wabula emu yokka, kubanga ekifaananyi kya Adamu kya kuzikirira emirembe n'emirembe awamu n'obuvundu obuli mu mubiri ogwonooneddwa.

Kyemunaava mutegeera nti abagwi bandibakweka amazima mu buzibe bwammwe obw'ekitundu n'amalala, era amazima ago ge gajja okubasumulula mu bujjuvu okuyingira mu ddembe lye njagala okubateekamu. Kale mbategeeza, ggwe Isirayiri, (gwe njagala) nti tewali muntu atuukirizibwa na bikolwa bya mateeka ga Musa mu maaso ga Yahweh, kyeyoleka: kubanga omutuukirivu aliba mulamu lwa kukkiriza. N'amateeka si ga kukkiriza: naye omuntu alikola ebyo, aliba mulamu mu byo. Nange, Yeshua HaMashiach, nnabanunula mu kikolimo ky'amateeka, bwe nnafuuka ekikolimo ku lwammwe, kubanga kyawandiikibwa nti, Akolimidde buli muntu awanikirwa ku muti. Kyemunaava, ggwe Isirayiri, mukkirize ssaddaaka yange, mmwe n'ezzadde lyammwe mulyoke mubeere abalamu, era mulyoke musobole okuyingira mu bwakabaka bwange obw'emyaka olukumi, obuweereddwa ng'okuwummula kwa Ssabbiiti mu nteekateeka za Yahweh ez'emirembe gyonna ez'okununula abantu, kubanga emyaka olukumi giri ng'olunaku lumu n'olunaku lumu ng'emyaka olukumi mu maaso ga Yahweh, oyo gw'olina okukolagana naye, ggwe Isirayiri.

Kyemunaava mwawukane n'abo ababalimba era mweyiseeyo mu kitabo ekyo eky'obulimba eky'okuvvoola ekyatumibwa abagwi okubakema n'okubasiba mu nsobi olw'okuzikirizibwa kwammwe okujjuvu. Mwawukane n'obulimba bw'omusota era muve mu bo, mubeere ba njawulo, ggwe Isirayiri, nange ndibakkiriza gye ndi era ndibakuuma nga muli balamu okuva mu kuzikirizibwa kwa wiiki ya Danyeri ey'amakumi asatu, era okubonaabona kwa Yakobo okugenda okujja okulongoosa ensi okuva mu mikono gy'abakozi b'ebibi mu musango omunene era omutuukirivu.

Kubanga ebiseera by'okwawula bijja kujja mu bujjuvu ku nsi, Yakobo, ogusika gwe guli gw'ekisuubizo eri abaana b'ekisuubizo ababeerawo olw'okukkiriza, w'aliyawulibwa ddala emirembe n'emirembe okuva ku muganda we atawulize, ye Esawu anoonya okubba ekyo kye yatunda mu butakkiriza obutassaamu kitiibwa kubanga teyalina kitiibwa wadde okukkiriza mu bisuubizo eby'obwesigwa ebyakolebwa mu kukkiriza Yahweh eri Ibulayimu omwesigwa, naye n'afaayo nnyo ku bintu by'ensi, ng'omwenzi era omuntu atali mutukuvu eyatunda obusika bwe obw'emirembe gyonna n'obukulu bwe olw'omugaati omutono.

Kale mbategeeza ebiseera eby'omu maaso bye mbayita okwenyigiramu era bye ndibakuumiramu mu kifo eky'obukuumi eky'amaanyi gange singa mwegondera okufuga kwange n'obuyinza bwange, kubanga mu kukola bwe mutyo ndibazzaayo mu bujjuvu eri Yahweh, ye Kitaffe w'Ebitangaala atalina kukyuka wadde ekisiikirize eky'okukyama. Era bwe kityo bwe kyawandiikibwa mu bisuubizo eby'edda era bwe kiriba bwe kityo, mmwe ab'oluganda lwange be ndi omununuzi waabwe ow'oluganda!

Era kiribaawo ku nkomerero y'okubonaabona kwa Yakobo nti nditeekawo obwakabaka bwange mu butuukirivu ku nsi, era tewaliba kintu kibi na kimu kiriyingiramu. Mutegeere nti ndikomya obwakabaka bwa Setaani emirembe gyonna era gguluddene tajja kugolokokera kufuga nate, era ensolo evva mu nnyanja n'ensolo evva mu nsi zirisuulibwa nga nnamu mu nnyanja ey'omuliro ennene ey'ekibiriiti ekiwunya ennyo, ekiwuuma ennyo, ekya bbululu, era mu nnyanja eyo ey'ekibonerezo eky'obugwagwa mwe zirisuulibwa emirembe n'emirembe, omusota gwabyo gye gutafiira, kubanga bwe gityo bwe gisaanira.

Setaani yennyini alikwatibwa n'asibibwa mu lujegere olunene olw'okubonaabona n'ekibonerezo, gy'alindirira ng'atidde era ng'alumwa olw'okuzikirizibwa kwe okusembera, obusika bw'aligabana n'abo bonna abamugoberera. Mazima ddala, ddala, mu bunnya obutakoma mwe mulisigalira ng'asibiddwa era ng'asibidde okumala emyaka olukumi, ku nkomerero yaayo lw'alisumululibwa okukungaanya abo abaliba bajeemye ne beegatta ku makubo ge, era okujeema kwe okw'enkomerero, obubi bulyoke buzikiririzibwe ddala mu musango ogw'enkomeredde. Era ku nkomerero y'ekiseera kye ekitono bw'aliba abakung'aanyizza ng'omusenyu gw'ennyanja okulumba olusiisira lw'abatukuvu ku bugazi bw'ensi, omuliro guligwa okuva eri Yahweh okuva waggulu ne gubookya ddala ne gubamalirawo ddala, era Setaani alisibibwa emirembe n'emirembe n'asuulibwa mu busika bwe obw'emirembe gyonna mu nnimi z'omuliro ezo ezaaka emirembe gyonna ez'ekibonerezo, n'omusango ogw'oku ntebe ey'obwakabaka ennene enjeru gulitandika.

Era mu nnyanja eyo ey'ekibonerezo eyaaka emirembe gyonna, eyaaka ennyo ennyo, era mu kwewumba kwayo okwaaka emirembe gyonna, Setaani alisasulira ddala buli kibi kyonna kye yakola emirundi mingi nnyo, era buli kikolwa ekibi kyawandiikibwa mu bujjuvu mu ggulu era kiriddizibwa mu nnimi z'omuliro ng'obusika bwe obw'emirembe gyonna. Ddala, buli kitonde ekyamugoberera ne kyeewaayo eri ensonga ye kirifuna ekibonerezo kye kimu era tebaliba na kusaasira kubanga tebaalaga kusaasira, kyekiriva tewaliba kusaasira emirembe n'emirembe. Era nga bwe baakola abalala, era nga bwe baayonoona ebitonde bya Yahweh, bwe kityo bwe kirisasulwa ku mitwe gyabwe, nga bwe kyasuubizibwa edda, kubanga tewali kisuubizo kya Yahweh na kimu ekirigwa wansi nga tekituukiridde. Mazima ddala, bonna abagaana eky'okulokolebwa kwange balisuulibwa mu muliro era tewali kusosola mu bantu.

Naye, omugabo gw'abagwi gwe beerondedde ne beekolera mu kujeema kwabwe okubi okulwanyisa Yahweh n'ebitonde bye si gwammwe, mmwe ab'oluganda lwange, singa munkkiriza okuba Masiya wammwe. Nedda, obutafaananako n'ekyo, waliwo empeera nnyingi n'emikisa ebiterekerwa mmwe bye mulizuukusa mu bujjuvu mu bwakabaka bwange bwe ndifuga mu butuukirivu obutaggwaawo. Walibaawo Kabaka omu owa Bakabaka ne Mukama owa Bakama alifuga ensi mu butuukirivu obw'emirembe gyonna, okuva e Yerusaalemi, okuva ku ntebe ya Dawudi gye ndi omusika ow'emirembe gyonna! Era mu mwagalwa wange omwesigwa gwe nditwala gye ndi mu ndagaano ey'emirembe gyonna ndimuteekako engule era ndimwawula eggye eddene ery'Abakabaka n'Abakabona ng'okusinziira ku buwesigwa bwabwe bwe bwabanga, era balifuga mu butuukirivu mu nkolagana ennungi ne Kabaka waabwe owa Bakabaka ne Mukama owa Bakama era tulifuga nga tuli bumu.

N'abantu b'omu nsi n'abantu b'amawanga balifuula ebitala byabwe enkumbi era amawanga tegalirwana nate, n'abantu b'ensi tebaliyiga kulwana mu bwakabaka bwange obutukuvu. Nga bwe kyawandiikibwa bannabbi aba edda: Era n'omusege gulituula wamu n'omwana gw'endiga, n'engo eneegalamira wamu n'omwana gw'embuzi, n'ennyana, n'empologoma, n'ensolo ensava wamu, n'omwana omuto alibakulembera. N'ente n'eddubu biririira wamu: abaana baabyo abato baligalamira wamu: n'empologoma erirya omuddo ng'ente ennume. N'omwana ayonka alizannyira ku kinnya ky'enswera, n'omwana eyalekulira aliteeka omukono gwe ku kinnya ky'empiri. Olwo tewaliba alumya wadde okuzikiriza mu lusozi lwange lwonna olutukuvu: kubanga ensi erijjula okumanya kwa Mukama, ng'amazzi bwe gasaanikira ennyanja.

Era ku lunaku olwo amazzi ag'obulamu galiyiyiika okuva e Yerusaalemi, ekitundu ekimu nga kigenda ku nnyanja ey'Ebuvanjuba, n'ekitundu ekirala ku nnyanja ey'ewala ennyo, era bwe kityo bwe kiriba, mu kiseera eky'omusana ne mu kiseera eky'obutiti. Era Mukama Katonda aliba Kabaka ku nsi yonna: ku lunaku olwo walibaawo Mukama omu, n'Erinnya lye limu. Ensi yonna erifuuka olusenyi okuva e Geba okutuuka e Rimoni, mu Bukiikaddyo bwa Yerusaalemi, era kirigulumizibwa, ne kituulwamu mu kifo kyakyo: okuva ku mulyango gwa Benyamini okutuuka mu kifo ky'omulyango ogwasooka, okutuuka ku mulyango ogw'oku nsonda, n'okuva ku kinaala kya Kananieli, okutuuka ku masito g'omwenge ga Kabaka. N'abantu baligituulamu, so tekiribaawo nate kuzikirizibwa, naye Yerusaalemi kiribeeramu n'emirembe.

N'olwekyo tegeera, ggwe Isirayiri, kye nja okukolera abo bonna abanjagala olw'okugondera n'obwesigwa amateeka gange amagasa ag'obulamu n'emirembe ge mbalagira. N'olwekyo tegeera nti okwagala Yahweh kwe kukwata mateeka ge, era amateeka ga Yahweh galagira okugondera Omwana wa Yahweh, gwe nze: n'olwekyo, munywegere Omwana nneme okusunguwala ne muzikirira okuva mu kkubo.

Kubanga, obwakabaka bwange we bigendererwa byange eby'olubeerera we birituukiririzibwa mu bujjuvu nga Omwana gw'endiga afuuka omu n'empologoma.

Kubanga, nze ndi Mwana gw'endiga gwa Katonda, eyaleetebwa olw'ebibi by'abantu, omusaayi gwe gwe ssaddaaka etangirira emirembe gyonna abatali batuukirivu ne beerusibwa ng'omuzira mu butukuvu obuyengevu.

Kubanga, nze ndi Mpologoma y'Ekika kya Yuda alwanirira ggwe Isirayiri, era alizikiririza ddala abalabe bo nga bwe kyawandiikibwa ku nze; okuwalana eggwanga kwange era nze ndisasula, bw'ayogera Mukama wa bakama era Kabaka wa bakabaka!

N'olwekyo, tegeera ebigendererwa byange n'oyo Yahweh gwe yatumira obulokozi bwammwe era waliwo enjuyi nnyingi mu bigendererwa byange eby'obulokozi. Era nze ndi asinga obukulu, ye, oyo eyawangulira obulokozi bwammwe obw'olubeerera singa musalawo okukkiriza omukono gwange ogw'okwagala n'okukkiriza endagaano yange empya.

Kubanga lwa ndagaano yange empya gye nakakasa mu musaayi gwange gwennyini, mwe muli kati Endagaano Yange Empya ensi kw'eneefugirwa mu butuukirivu! N'okumanya Yahweh kulibuna ensi yonna n'ekitiibwa kyange kirimasamasa okuva e Yerusaalemi amawanga ne geekuŋŋaanya ne gayingira emiryango emitukuvu egy'ekibuga ekyo ekitukuvu basobole okuyiga ebikwata ku Yahweh era babeere mu makubo ge ku nsi.

Mu bwakabaka bwange temulibaawo nate mwana muto, newaakubadde omukadde atatuukirizza nnaku ze: kubanga omwana alifa ng'aweza emyaka kikumi: naye omwonoonyi ng'aweza emyaka kikumi, alikolimirwa. Era kiriba nti oyo aliba omulamu okutuuka ku myaka kikumi aliba ng'akyali luwewere.

N'olwekyo, ensi erizzibwa mu kitiibwa kyayo eky'edda abatemu nga tebannagyonoonona n'emizizo gyabwe bwe baaserengetera ku Lusozi Kerumooni okukola ekirayiro kyabwe ekikolimidde basobole okubeera mu ngeri z'okwegomba kwabwe mu kibi ekinene n'obujeemu eri entegeka z'obulamu mwe baatonderwa mu butuukirivu. Abo tebalibeerawo mu bwakabaka bwange; so n'ensi terifugibwa mu lukwe olw'ekiwaggu awasinga amaanyi y'awangaala, kubanga obusirusiru obw'ekika ekyo bwe bukyuusa amateeka ga Yahweh Setaani kw'ayita bulijjo okukyusa entegeka za Yahweh okuzikiriza kusobole okufuga.

Era nze, Yeshua HaMashiach, alifuga amawanga n'omuggo ogw'ekyuma ng'eyita mu mikono gya Bakabaka ne Bakabona abangi be nditikkira engule ne mbawa obuweereza obwa waggulu ennyo, era be bibala ebibereberye eby'abafu, era tebafa era tebaddamu kufa n'empeera yaabwe tekoma, kubanga baajja gye ndi kyeyagalire mu nnaku ez'ekisa ne mbakuŋŋaanyiza gye ndi mu mubiri gwange ogwaddamu okutondebwa ng'omuntu omu omuggya, k'abeere Muyudaaya oba Mgentile, k'abeere muddu oba wa ddembe. Baliba n'ebirabo bingi n'obusobozi n'engeri eziweereddwa olw'obwesigwa bwabwe kubanga be bawanguzi bange abaawangula ebintu byonna olw'omusaayi gw'omwana gw'endiga.

Kubanga ye Mwana gw'endiga gwa Katonda eyawangula abatemu ku Kalevaale emirembe gyonna era ye Mpologoma y'Ekika kya Yuda eneeteeka ebitonde byonna wansi wa Yahweh mu bujjuvu, n'okutuusa Yahweh lw'aliba byonna mu byonna bwe ndimulaga ebitonde ebimugondera kubanga kinngwanira okufuga okutuusa abalabe bange bonna lwe balifuulibwa entebbe y'ebigere byange, era ye Mpologoma y'Ekika kya Yuda eneerinnyirira buli kikolwa kya Setaani wansi w'ebigere byange eby'amaanyi era eby'olubeerera.

N'olwekyo, nze wuuyo omwana gw'endiga mw'alyegattira mu mirembe n'obumu obujjuvu n'empologoma kubanga ebigendererwa byange eby'olubeerera birikolebwa emirembe gyonna so tewali kintu era tewali muntu ayinza kuyimirira eri Yeshua HaMashiach era ebiseera byange bituuse we nneeyanjulira abo abali ku nsi. Laba, nzija n'ebire, na buli liiso lirindaba: weewaawo, n'abo abaamfummula: n'ebika byonna eby'oku nsi birinkaabirako, weewaawo, Amiina.
