Daroree semmbe, laɓɓinee ko'e mooɗon

Joomiraawo gooŋgaajo on no wi'a suɓaaɓe makko gooŋgaaɓe ɓen: Daroree semmbe, laɓɓinee ko'e mooɗon hara on accii, on njaɓtorii junuubaaji mooɗon. Yaltuɗon mo, onon yimɓe am, tawee on senndii e junuubaaji makko fii wota on tawtore ñaawoore makko e balaa'uuji makko ɗin. Onno ƴama hombo woni kanko on? Wanaa kanko woni Babiilon Mawɗo on, mo ñaawoore mum hewtii? Wanaa kanko woni debbo jeenotooko mawɗo, sigruɗo ƴiiƴam seniiɓe ɓen? Kisiyee e jam mi okkay on, mi ardoyay on haa e hoɗorde ka suudu Baaba am haa saanga on yalta.

Ko miin woni Joomiraawo Iisaa Almasiihu, nodduɗo on e mangu e peewal, wota on welsito e gollegol ko moƴƴi. Mi okkii on daande haa kaalɗanon mi, anndinon ɓadal ardal am. Amma heewɓe nder Eklesiya am majjinii daande mum'en, ɓe wontii ɓe koolaaki e tama'u mo Baaba am waɗani on nder Konngol makko ngol. Wota on wonu ɓe koolaaki, kono goonɗinee tan. Ɗaɓɓee maandeeji ardal am, sabi Baaba am feññinirii ɗi ka kammuuli, ɗi anndaama kadi no feewi. Ko honɗun waɗi haa jooni on newii goonɗingol? Kooloree mi, onon ɓiɓɓe am, sabi ko miin wallata on; aa, mi no saɗta kala catal ngal rimaali e mooɗon. Kooloree Joomirankaaku am e dow mooɗon, tiinnoɗon e kisindam am hara onno gollana kisiyee mooɗon e kulol e diwnaare. Yiiltanee mi e du'aawu, ƴamee mi yo mi holle on junuubaaji mooɗon haa njaɓtoranoɗon mi ɗi, pooliron ɗi kadi ƴiiƴam am. Wota on hulu non, sabi kisiyee mooɗon no ɓadii; aa, junngo ɗiɗaɓo ngoo no waɗtii timmingol wertallo mum cakkitiingo ngoo e jamaanu moƴƴere am.

Yiiltanee mi, ngaree e am sabi mi hebilanii on jooɗorde ka taabal am, mi heblanii on kadi hoɗorde ka suudu Baaba am fii yo on daɗu e tsunami masiibooji garooji ɗin. Ɗi no ɓadoo hoɗuɓe ka leydi, ñaawoore mawnde no waɗtii feyyaade e dow leydi ndin fuf. Wota on accitu innde am! Miɗo anndi on tampii e nder ndee saa'a cakkitiinde, kono fooftoɗon e am, keɓon bawɗe e am, anndinon ɓadal ardal am. Mi okkaali on ñalawma maa saa'a haa reenon, sabi on mahayno nguurndam mooɗon fuf e dow nden kewu. Kono wota on jiiɓo: ñalnde e saa'a no anndaa ka kammuuli e ndee saa'a, malaa'ikaaɓe am no hebilanii, hiɓe ndeenoo wuuferde galanngal ngal, tawi mi mooɓtay yimɓe ɗaaniiɓe ka leydi arano, ɓe nguurata, heddiiɓe ɓen tawtoto ɓe, hawrondiroo e am ka weeyo, mi yahra on e ndonu mooɗon ngun mo Baaba suuɗi e am. Tanaa wota on welsito.

Ɗum laatoto ko e mbelemma yiitere, tawi heewɓe e mooɗon no ka kuuɗe mum'en ñalnde kala, faw faw on daroyoo yeeso am, tawi mi no hollude on Baaba am; menen ɗiɗo fuf en no ŋanaa ñalnde heen no en ŋaniri tawaare mooɗon. Mi waawuno feeñannde mi waajoo heewɓe, hombo waawi wi'ude mi waɗaali ɗum wakkatiiji goɗɗi? On anndintay ko Joomiraawo on waawi waɗude na? Maa on kaɗtay bawɗe am e Joomirankaaku am sabu ŋakkeende goonɗinal mooɗon? Ko huunde nde waawataa laatude! Kono non, mi waɗii on gollidiiɓe am, mi naatinii on e golle ɗe Baaba am halfini mi yo mi timmin, anndee ko teddungal e weltaare nde mi sakkii e dow mooɗon fii kisiyee mooɗon, njoɓdi mooɗon, e cellal mooɗon.

No ndewruɗon woɓɓe e innde am non, miin mi dewaaki on na? Hokkiree wale, wota on suuɗu ganndal ardal am, hay so tawii heewɓe caloto, on limete ko hono jinnaaɓe. Fenanndeeji gaño on ɓolay yeeso konnguɗi mooɗon keewɗi bawɗe ɗin so on haalanii mi tan, on anndinii feeñannde am, on anndinii ñaawoore garoore nden, sabi hay e nder ɗee sekonnaaji cakkitiiɗi, woɓɓe keɓay kisiyee. Heewɓe heblanaaki taw, ɓe caloto konnguɗi mooɗon; njiɗon ɓe non, sabi jamaa mawɗo hisinoyte e nder jarribooji mawɗi e torra, anndee konnguɗi mooɗon mbaaway wonande ɗum'en hono jayngol e tama'u fii jarribooji ɗi ɓe mbaawi hawrude e mum'en ɗin.

Hey onon hoɗuɓe ka leydi, onon caliiɓe noddaango am, limtiiɓe ko'e mooɗon ko on fotaani e dokkal kisiyee am e kala ko addata: Ñaawoore mawnde fawaama e dow leydi ndin, nde waɗtii hewtude on, hay so onno fena hoore mooɗon, hiɗon mbi'a, "Miɗo jogii peeje mawɗe fii hoore am, jaango laatoto ko wano hannde nii!" An ɗoole! A faamaali hay yiilaagol zamaanuuji, tawi kammuuli no anndina teegal jombaajo am e am ka kammuuli, kammuuli no yiiltoo e moƴƴere fayde e ñaawoore yeeso mooɗon. Balaa'u mawɗo aroyay on ko ɓooyaani, heewɓe maayay e nder junuubaaji mum'en. Wanaa anniyee am yo on halku, kono onno caloo sadaka am mo alaa junuuba mo mbaɗanomi on, baawnooɗo hisinde on haa timma.

Aduna mo annduɗon hannde oo majjay yeeso mooɗon, kala welwelooji junuuba ka nguurndam ɗam laatotaako kadi wondiiɓe mooɗon; kono on ndewete, on sonnoya e juuɗe konu gañooɓe mooɗon yirbiiɓe halkaare mooɗon, ŋanɓe jawdi mooɗon. Ndiwnondi mawndi laatoto ley koyɗe mooɗon, leydi seekoo, heewɓe njannoo e nder leydi. Hooreeɓe mooɗon njannotike on, hay so tawii on ɗowtiiɓe ɓe haa jooni e mbeleeŋgu, hiɗon etoo anndude mo cuɓoton. Cuuɗi mooɗon toowɗi ɗin njanay, ponji mooɗon njanay, kuutorɗe mooɗon ɗen majjay yeeso mooɗon, hay huunde waylotaako.

To ndogoton tawi on calike Joomiraawo ceniiɗo on, miin Joomiraawo Iisaa Almasiihu? Hombo hisintat on jooni e wakkati ñaawoore mooɗon? Oh, kono onno mbi'a, mi fotaani e ñaawoore, ko honɗun woni konngol ma? On calaaki tagɗo on tawi o neli mi e mooɗon fii yurmeende makko hisinirnde, yurmeende hisinirnde nde limtuɗon ko'e mooɗon ko on fotaani heɓude? Ko honɗun waɗi on calii mi? Ko honɗun woni aybe mo mbaɗanomi on? Mi hokkii tippudi ƴiiƴam am cakkitiindi e nder torra e muuseendi mawndi fii yo on nguur yeeso am, kono haa jooni on goonɗinaali seedeeɓe am ɓe nelirmi e innde am ɓen.

Hay e nder reentino-gol on balaa'u mooɗon ɓadooɗo on, miin e Baaba am fuf en alaa weltaare e ko waɗtii yaltineede koo. Kono no mbeltiɗon e junuuba non, on moñete jooni e nder nguurndam e battane ɗe mooɓaninoɗon ko'e mooɗon. No njiɗirɗon kaalisi, jawdi, e yettoore kaalisaaji, on waɗaali so wonaa mooɓude tikkere e dow taabal mooɗon, on nyaamay torra e anksiyete. Mi okkay on niɓre nde njiɗuɗon no feewi nden. Niɓre feyyoto e dow leydi mooɗon, nde sudda leydi ndin, niɓre mawnde haa naange caka ñalawma ngoo feeñira wa ɗum ñifaa. Onno mbi'anam, ko honɗum woni junuuba mo njogumi haa ngaddata tikkere ma; men waɗaali golle mawɗe keewɗe e innde ma na?

Gooŋga, on mbaɗii golle keewɗe, kono wonaa golle ɗe okkumi on ɗen; aa'a, on wontii annabiijo fenaannde, hiɗon kaala annabaaku iwngu e ɓalli mooɗon mbonɗi ɗin. Aa'a, onno ngollana ko'e mooɗon fii mangu mooɗon, sabi on mbaɗii reedu mooɗon ko alla mooɗon. Onno mawna konnguɗi moƴƴi e haalaaji belɗi ɗi yimɓe bonɓe ɓen dow Konngol am ceniingol ngol. Miin wanaa mi Konngol ngol waɗaa ɓandu na? On mbaawuno nyaamude e am buuru nguurndam, on daɗuno e halkaare, on welaano kadi abadan. On mbaawuno yarde e canɗi ndiyam nguurndam ilayɗam e am; aa, on mbaawuno yarde e Ruuhu am on njoorataa kadi abadan. Kono e calagol mooɗon mi, on naatay e weelo mawngo e satteende, hay so tawii haa e oo miniti onno miijo haa jooni ɗum hewtataa on e mbelemma yiitere. Faggudu mooɗon yanike, jawdi nde njiɗnoɗon ndin wontay huunde nde alaa nafa faw faw. On mbaɗii goonɗinal mooɗon ko e laamɓe ndee niɓre hannde nden, on njiɗii fenanndeeji Seytaane dow jayngol gooŋga am woodngo saanga fuf.

Ko ɗum waɗi, on ɗaɓɓoyay jooɗorde ka taabal maɓɓe, kono on taway alaa jooɗorde heblanaande on. Ɓe keɓay keewal ngal ɓe mooɓani hoore maɓɓe, on keɓataa so wonaa so on nanii maande dabba kaan. Hay so tawii dabba kaan feññaani taw e wakkati mum, mi rentiniima e mum, mi anndinii ɗum gooŋgaaɓe am fii wota ɓe majjine e konnguɗi mum moƴƴi e haalaaji mum belɗi ɗin, hay so tawii e ndee saa'a o alaa laamu, walla taw. Wota on nanu maande makko, sabi kala baɗɗo ɗum keɓay geɓal mum wondude e dabba kaan e annabiijo fenaannde on e nder weendu yiite, cuurki torra maɓɓe ummoto haa abada.

On ndentinaama, hay so tawii miɗo ɗaɓɓa haa jooni hisinde on e halkaare mooɗon, onno calira e tiiɗere noddaango am haa jooni. Ko ɗum waɗi, mi okkay on ɓiɓɓe leɗɗe gollal mooɗon.

On keɓay weelo mawngo, konu mawngu, halkaare mawnde. Konuuji boɗeeji ɗin ndeway on, torroo on, halka heewɓe e mooɗon; laatoto kadi ko on taartete e boneeji e halkaare. Mo nannaaka kaafaado e torra e maayde sakkitere, o fiyete kaafahi; mo fiyyaaka kaafahi o hawrondiray e kulle ladde, ɗe mbaray heewɓe; mo halkiraaka kulle ladde o maayay e weelo, laabi maayde ɗin keewtay e dow leydi ndin.

Torra mawɗo, balaa'u, halkaare aroyay on faw faw, beeli maayo mawɗi aroyay leydi mooɗon. Leydi mawndi on wontataa kadi abadan, kono heddiiɓe hisinoyte haa jooni. Kala nodduɗo innde Joomiraawo hisinoyte, kono jiɗɗo nguurndam mum haa ka laakara o heɓay geɓal mum e nder weendu yiite, on tawtoto torra e leptungal Seytaane sabi on njogitike aduna makko maayoowo oo haa e pooɗotol mooɗon tiiɗungol cakkitiingol, on halkoyday kadi e oo aduna maayoowo. Yiiltanee mi hara wakkati no woodi haa jooni fii yo mi hisin on onon e ɓesnguuji mooɗon e balaa'u garoowo oo. No jamaa heɓirnoo kisiyee ñalnde Pentekoste duuɓi keewɗi ko ɓenni e minitaaji arani ɗi ndee jamaanu, non kadi jamaa hisinirtee e minitaaji cakkitiiɗi ɗi ndee jamaanu, noddaango am ngoo no darii haa jooni fii wakkati seeɗa goɗɗo.

Bono garoowo oo hormataa rewɓe maa sukaaɓe maa mawɓe maa ñawɓe. Aa'a, oon bono moñay kala ko waawi fuf e nder halkaare e muuseendi mawndi. Doggee e am hara wakkati no woodi haa jooni, sabi montor oo no waɗtii daragol, wakkati laatotaako kadi. Seytaane werlete e leydi ko ɓooyaani, laamu makko yaltinte haa timma e dow aduna mooɗon, omo jogii tikkere mawnde sabi omo anndi o alaa so wonaa wakkati seeɗa. Yiiltanee mi hara on mbaaway haa jooni fii yo on daɗu e bono garoowo oo.

Ko miin woni Joomiraawo Joomiraaɓe e Laamɗo Laamɓe.

Ko miin woni jahroowo hakkunde pitilji jeeɗiɗi ɗin, laamiiɗo malaa'ikaaɓe jeeɗiɗo ɓe eklesiyaaji jeeɗiɗi ɗin.

Ko miin woni laamiiɗo konuuji kammu, sabi Baaba am waɗii laamu maɓɓe e juuɗe am.

Ko miin woni Konngol ngol waɗaa ɓandu, hoɗi hakkunde mooɗon.

Ko miin woni iwdi Yessaa, laamu am alaa timmoode.

Ko miin woni Ɓiɗɗo Alla garɗo e ɓandu fii yo mi yoɓit junuubaaji yimɓe fuf, so tawii fuf njaɓii tan.

Ɓe njaɓii moƴƴere am, ɓe ngoonɗii ɓen laamoto e am e nder laamu am, ɓe tawtora jappeere am, sabi mi waɗii on laamɓe e almaamiiɓe yeeso Alla meeɗen. Njoɓdi mawndi no woodani on, deentanaandi on ka kammuuli; tanaa daroree semmbe, ngoonɗinee minitaaji seeɗa tan goɗɗi.
