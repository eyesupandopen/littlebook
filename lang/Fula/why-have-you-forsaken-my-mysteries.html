Seedeeji ɗiɗi: Eklesiya
E nder Binndanɗe Piyeer
15 Abriil, 2024

Hol ko waɗi on accii sirlam?

Hol ko waɗi on accii sirlam, onon hawtirde Lawdisiya yoornde? Hol ko waɗi on accii sirru mawɗo mo Alla oo? On mbaylitii sirlam ɓurɗam cenidde ɗam Baaba am jogii, hono Almasiihu e mon, koolaalo jom en ngoma, ngam heɓde jawdi tuundi, Alla mon wonti reedu mon. Kono, on mbiyoytoo kam: "Mi alaa ella. Mi dogataa e jawdi tuundi, sabu mi deeƴii yeeso maa." Miin noon, mi jaaboto on: E on ndogataa e linjiila ɓural jawdi fenaande jannginteeka e nder hawtirɗe mon Lawdisiya ɗee? On njeeyii goonga sirlam Alla ɓurɗam cenidde ngam heɓde ngalu meere, on ngalaa kadi koolaalo jom en ngoma mo njiɗ-mi no feewi mbele on paama, kono on mbaylitii koolaalo jom en ngoma wonti koolaalo jawdi aduna ndi njiɗoton mooɓtude e miijooji mon ɗi laaɓaani. Mi noddaaki on e kaalis e jawdi, kono ko e nuunɗal e laaɓal goonga ngal heɓotoo e am.

Ndundee e ellaaji mon, soodanee kam safaare gite, mbele on mbaaway wujtude gite mon haa nji'on goongaaji ɗi ndokku-mi on e nder Binndi ceniiɗi ngam hisnude on. On njaafaama haa njahdon e wallitde laamuuji aduna, on cikkii so on mballitii ɗi, ko Alla suɓiiɓe ɓee mballitoton. Hirsude foti nanngude on yeeso am, sabu e nder majjere mon, on paamaani hol woni Isiraayiil goongaajo Alla, on paamataa kadi sirru mawɗo Alla oo wonde ko Heddiiɓe ɓee poti wonde ronooɓe wondude e maɓɓe, e terɗe ɓanndu wooturu, e tawtoraaɓe fodoore makko e nder Almasiihu, rewrude e Linjiila. Sabu ko miin woni jam mon, miin baɗɗo ɓe fof gooto, miin bonnuɗo mahol hakkundeewol senndunookol on.

On ndokkii yimɓe huutortooɓe innde Seyɗaani fowtere mon, on majjii e konnguɗi annabaaɓe fenaande. E on paamaani wonde ko onon woni Isiraayiil Alla? Kala yimɓe yahruɓe e ndeeɗoo laawol, jam won e maɓɓe, e yurmeende, e dow Isiraayiil Alla. Onon hawtirde yoornde, hol hisnoyoowo on e jeenay mon njuulndiiɗo bonnuɗo on e miijo ngootiri ngo woni e am, hono Almasiihu Iisaa, Joomiraaɗo mon? On mbaawaa jogaade nduu aduna maayoowo, on cikkii so on mbaɗii noon, ko miin ngonɗon e rewde; foti ko on tuubde e ballal mon ngal ndokkaton laamuuji aduna bonɗi ɗii, sabu hol ko hawtini Almasiihu e Ibliisa, kamɓe ɓe ngonaa e rewde am kono ko Seyɗaani. Njehee e Binndi am, njiiloyee goonga mon mo cuuɗu-mi e nder bataakeeji am; sabu on maayii, nguurndam mon cuuɗii e Almasiihu e nder Alla. Kadi maayɗo oo woppitaama e hakke, e sariya Ruuhu baɗoowo nguurndam woppitii on e sariya hakke e maayde.

Ko ɗum waɗi, so neɗɗo ina woni e Almasiihu, ko o tagu kesu: ko wonnoo koo fof rewtii, ndelle, kala huunde wontii kesum. Mi tagii on e am ko on jibinaaɓe kesi, pooftooɓe fofngo mon gadano e duumotooɗo; sabu duumotooɗo fuɗɗanii on gila ñalawma mo njeñtinaa-ɗon e ruuhu am, sabu mi waɗii ɗiɗo ɓee neɗɗo gooto keso, mi addi jam. Eey, neɗɗo gooto keso e am, mo Yahuudiyankooɓe e Heddiiɓe fof kawri e nder hawtirde am Alla. Ɓe noddu-mi e nguurndam kesam e am ɓee ko leñol Alla cuɓaangol, ɓe njeñtinaa e ruuhu am ɓee ko ɓiɓɓe Alla goongaaji, kadi so neɗɗo alaa ruuhu Almasiihu, o jeyanooka e makko.

Hol ko waɗi on njaɓataa feccere e Binndi ceniiɗi, on cikkii ko ɗe laaɓaani sabu miijo mon ngo innirton "miijo moƴƴo"? Hol ko waɗi on peccii Binndi ceniiɗi, on cikkii Deftere Alla ndee fof wonaa ngam janngingol mon, golle mon, e janngugol mon? Miijo moƴƴo fenaande baɗaango e dow goonga ina wumnina on e laabi am e peeje am ɗe peblaninoo-mi on. Eey, foti ko on mbaɗta miijooji mon e ko woni dow koo, kono wotee ninnire feccere e Binndi am fenaande e niɓre sabu on cikkiraani ɗum miijo moƴƴo. On naatii e kuuturu Kerecee’en, on ngoni e huutoraade ganndal maleyka'en toɓɓiiɓe jannginnooɓe aadee en kuuturu e mbilewu e taƴgol leɗɗe gila puɗɗoode.

Ganndal mon ngal maleyka'en toɓɓiiɓe ɓee ko fenaande feewnaande no feewi ngam tiggude yonkiiji mon rewrude e konnguɗi moƴƴi e haalaaji mbelɗi. Anndee wonde worɓe bonɓe heewɓe e nder duuɓi naatinii fenaande e diine Kerecee'en, ina mbiya on so on miijii huunde haa juuti, fotaani so wona ko noon waɗata. Onon majjuɓe! Alla tagiraani on mbele on mbaaway mahde fenaande mon e hoore mon e Kerecee'en mon ballitooji hoore e miijooji wellitaare. On ngoni e rewrude e Iisaa goɗɗo, on accii goonɗinal ngal woni e am, on accii konnguɗi am e sariyaaji am ngam heɓde konnguɗi moƴƴi e haalaaji mbelɗi, kadi on ndenndaama e fowruuji cokliiɗi fenooji on.

Ina penana on, ina kuutoroo sariya Muusaa no fotaani, ina mbiyoya Kerecee’en foti ko ɓe njoɓa zakka ngam wallitde pelle maɓɓe ɗe mahaaka e am, hayre mawnde ndee. Ƴeewtee Binndi ceniiɗi, paamee wonde no neɗɗo feerorii e ɓernde mum nii, yo o rokku, o rokka e weltaare, sabu Alla ina yiɗi rokkoowo e weltaare. Kadi oo ko sariya mawɗo e nder Aadi Keso ngo sinndaa e ƴiiƴam am. Zakka oo ko ngam wallitde limanɓe leñol Lewi, kamɓe noon ina mballita mawɗo limanɓe oo e sadak toownaaɗo. Ɗum fof momtaama e am, sabu ko miin woni mawɗo limanɓe haa abada e mbaadi Melkisedek, mi waɗii on limanɓe e laamɓe yeeso am. Kono onon, onon hawtirde yoornde, on wumnaama e yi'annde laamuuji e mbaawkaaji fenaande, mbaawkaaji ɗi keɓotaako e Almasiihu.

On mbaawaa fewjude hay huunde e miijooji mon tan, on wumnii e am e peeje am ɗe peblan-mi on. On uddii noppi mon haa on njaɓataa nande janngingol moƴƴol ngam heɓde janngingol fenaande ngo welaa noppi mon. Wonaa ɗuum tan, kono on njippii e miñiraaɓe wonɓe ayɓe mon sabu ina mbiyana on goonga e ɗee geɗe, on njaɓaani miijo yimɓe Bereya ngam heɓde duɗal ngal innirton Kerecee'en fenaande. Paamee ɗum, onon ardiiɓe majjuɓe: on njoɓete e ñaawoore sabu majjin'de on baali am, kadi yoɓo ko yam jeyi, kadi mi yoɓtoto.

Paamee ɗum, onon ɓiɓɓe am: on mbaawaa fewjude hay huunde e miijooji mon moƴƴi, on ngonaa kadi ɓural kaalis sabu miijooji mon mbelɗi. On njaafaama, on majjinaama e jaafotooɓe heewɓe. Ko ɓuri ɗum, on mbaawaa waɗde hay huunde so wonaa e am no miin mi waawataa waɗde hay huunde so wonaa e Baaba am; sabu ko Alla semmbinta nuunɗuɓe ɓee, ko Baaba tagɗo kala huunde, kadi kala huunde ko ngam mawninde innde Yahweh nuunɗunde dow kala huunde!

Ngurtee e cokli mon, ndacce yiɗde kaalis, sabu yiɗde kaalis ko iwdi kala bone.

So on uddii gite mon e linjiila am fof, on cuɓii ko on cikkii ko "moƴƴo," on ngoni e nder ngaska e ardiiɓe wumɓe ardinooɓe on haa njottoɗon toon. Mi noddaaki on mbele on naata e ngaska, onon ɓiɓɓe am, kono mi noddii on mbele on nguura yeeso am e nuunɗal e laaɓal goonga, njahdee siisoo gila e loope ɗee. Mi noddii on mbele on ndacca hakkeeji mon, on tuubta e laabi mon ɗi nuunɗaani. Ko e ƴiiƴam am laɓɓinoojam tan on mbaawi laɓɓineede e hakkeeji mon; miijooji mon moƴƴi ngalaa golle nuunɗuɗe, hay so tawii on mbaɗii ɗi tooruuji ɓerɗe mon.

Hol ko waɗi on njaɓii miñiraaɓe fenaande e Yahuudiyankooɓe naatde e mon ngam humpitaade ndimaagu ngu njogii-ɗon e am, Joomiraaɗo nuunɗuɗo oo? So on paamnooma sirru mawɗo Alla oo, on paamnooma wonde Almasiihu ko timmoode Sariya ngam nuunɗal kala goonɗinɗo, Sabu Muusaa ina siftina nuunɗal ngal ummii e Sariya, wonde neɗɗo baɗoowo ɗeen geɗe maa wuuru e majji. Sabu mi seediima kadi kala gorko taadnaaɗo, wonde o ñamaama yo o waɗu sariya oo fof. Hol ko woni e sariya Muusaa ndeen?

Mi timminii ko sariya oo naamndii, mi nuunɗinii neɗɗo e goonɗinal e am, sabu mi foolii, mi timminii sariya oo, mi rokki hoore am ko miin woni Juloore timmunde laawol gootol haa abada; ko ɗum waɗi, nuunɗuɗo oo maa wuuru e goonɗinal. E on njanngaani wonde mi ardaani ngam bonnude sariya walla annabaaɓe, kono ko ngam timminde sariya oo e rokkude worɓe nuunɗal rewrude e nanondiral maɓɓe e am, Joomiraaɗo maɓɓe Iisaa Almasiihu.

Ko ɗum mbiymi ndeen, njahdee e ruuhu, on timmintaa yiɗde ɓanndu, kadi alaa sariya haɗoowo ɓiɓɓe ruuhu. Ko ɗum waɗi, on ubbondirii e am e lootgol e nder maayde am, mbele no Almasiihu umminiraa e maayɓe e dow teddungal Baaba: noon ne onon kadi foti yahdude e nguurndam kesam. Ko e golle am hisninooje tan, ko miin woni Jawgel Alla ngel alaa hakke, neɗɗo fof waawi foolde hakke, o woppitee e kuddi sariya; sabu mi waɗaama kuddi ngam mon, sabu neɗɗo fof fawaaɗo e lekki ina kuddaa.

Muusaa siftinii nuunɗal ngal ummii e sariya, wonde neɗɗo baɗoowo ɗeen geɗe maa wuuru e majji. Sabu e nder Almasiihu Iisaa, taadgol alaa nafoore, kadi taadaaka alaa nafoore, kono ko tagu kesu. Almasiihu ko timmoode sariya ngam kala goonɗinɗo, kono heewɓe e mon maa njaɓu ɗee konnguɗi sabu on njiɗi konnguɗi limanɓe mon, waajotooɓe mon, e jannginooɓe mon ɓuri konnguɗi Joomiraaɗo mon e hisnoowo mon, Almasiihu Iisaa. E on paamaani wonde on mahaama e dow fonndasiyoŋ nelaaɓe e annabaaɓe, Iisaa Almasiihu e hoore mum woni hayre mawnde ndee? Sabu alaa fonndasiyoŋ goɗɗo mo neɗɗo waawi fawde, so wonaa ko fawaa koo, hono Iisaa Almasiihu; ko ɗum waɗi, on ngonaa e les sariya kono ko e yurmeende.

Sabu goonɗinɓe ɓee mbaawi naatde e fooftere am, sabu naatɗo e fooftere am oo kadi woppii golle mum, no Alla woppiri golle mum ñalnde jeeɗiɗaɓere. E on anndaa wonde sariya oo ko jannginoowo mon ngam addude on e am, Almasiihu Iisaa? Kono heewɓe njaɓii kam e yurmeende am e moƴƴere am ngam heɓde golle mum en nuunɗuɗe ɗe ngalaa nafoore. Wumre e feccere arii e Isiraayiil haa timmal Heddiiɓe ɓee timma. Onon catal oliwa ladde, wotee mawnin hoore mon e dow lekki oliwa ki njippinaa-ɗon e mum, wotee ndewon e wumre maɓɓe; sabu mi waawi hisnude Yaaquuba, kadi mi waɗan ɗum no heddiiɓe hisnirtee.

Woto neɗɗo ñaawee on e ñamri, walla e njaram, walla e ñalngu ceniiɗo, walla e lewru hesru, walla e ñalɗi Asabar. Hol ko waɗi on mbaɗtata e ɓiɓɓe am doole, on mbiyoyta ɓe foti ko ɓe njetta innde am e ɗemngal ceniiɗo? Miɗo faama ɗemɗe fof, miɗo ndaara ɓernde, mi anndi so ɓiɓɓe am noddii innde am e kala ɗemngal ɓe noddiri, mi siiforii ɓe e siifol Baaba am no haanirta nii, mi naatna ɓe e Hawtirde Alla. Wanaa baabiraaɓe ɓee, wanaa onon mbaawi rewde sariya oo, kono sariya oo timminii e mon e nder nuunɗal am; sabu sariya oo ina fawi e sariyaaji ɗiɗi mawɗi: Yiɗ Joomiraaɗo Alla maa e ɓernde maa fof, e yonki maa fof, e hakkilantaagal maa fof, e semmbe maa fof, kadi yiɗ ɓadondiraaɗo maa no hoore maa.

Nuunɗal ummotaako e sariya kono ko e goonɗinal e am, Joomiraaɗo Iisaa Almasiihu. Sabu ko miin woni mawɗo limanɓe haa abada e mbaadi Melkisedek, kadi e nder ƴiiƴam am hisninoojam on nuunɗinaama, sabu ko miin woni Juloore timmunde, kadi ko miin woni jawgel Alla. Woto neɗɗo majjin on e ɗee geɗe. Sariya oo waɗaaka ngam neɗɗo nuunɗuɗo, kono ko ngam bonɓe e ɗoftaani. Ndelle! Mi momtii binndol sariyaaji, ngol wonnoo e mon, ngol luutndii on, mi itti ngol e laawol, mi fawi ngol e leggal am. Kono, on njiɗi haa jooni wonde maccuɓe e geɗe loowɗe e meereeje. Hol ko Binndi ceniiɗi mbiyi: Njaawninee maccuɗo debbo e ɓiyiiko, sabu en ngonaa ɓiɓɓe maccuɗo debbo kono ko ndimaagu.

E mi haalaani no laaɓiri: Ndelle! Mi hollii on sirru, en fof en njaŋanooka, kono en fof en mbayloto, sabu mi jippoto gila e asamaan e sawta, e sawta maleyka mawɗo e buunndu Alla, maayɓe e Almasiihu ummoto adii, ndeen onon wuurnooɓe e heddiiɓe ɓee, on tikkete e duule ngam hawrude e am, Almasiihu Iisaa e weeyo, ko noon njogor-ɗon wonde e am haa abada. Onon hawtirde Lawdisiya, on njaɓii hay konnguɗi am ɓurɗi laaɓde ɗi ndokku-mi on. Ko ɗum waɗi, anndee wonde mi jaɓan on e taabal am, so miɗo yiɗi no feewi waɗande on nokku e nder asamaan.

On cikkii ko huunde famɗunde njaɓde konnguɗi am e majjinde woɗɓe e ellaaji mon. Ko ɗum waɗi, gila on njiɗi ñalawma Joomiraaɗo, mi accan on muñde ñalawma Joomiraaɗo, miɗo yi'a on cikkii no feewi on mbaawi daraade. On njaɓii feeñde am, on mawnini konnguɗi annabaaɓe fenaande ɓuri janngingol moƴƴol ngol ndokku-mi e am. E on njanngaani nate sukaaɓe rewɓe sappo ɓee? Miɗo wasiyoo on yo on ngoonɗin konnguɗi am, wotee njaɓe kam, mbele mi hoto mi jaɓe on. Hol heen ngon-ɗon, onon ɓiɓɓe am? E on ngonoyto e sukaaɓe rewɓe rewɓe hakkilooɓe, feewnuɓe fitilaaji mum en e nebam e nder lampaaji mum en? Walla on njahoyto e sukaaɓe rewɓe rewɓe majjuɓe, ina mbiya, Joomiraaɗo, Joomiraaɗo udditan min; miin noon mi mbiyata ɓe, mi anndaa on!

E on njanngaani? Ko ɗum waɗi gorko accata baaba mum e neene mum, o resnda e debbo mum, ɓe ɗiɗo fof ɓe ngonta ɓanndu wooturu. Oo ko sirru mawɗo: kono miɗo haala ko fayti e Almasiihu e Hawtirde. E on paamaani konnguɗi gorko resɗo, onon rewɓe am? Foti ko on njogo am ko miin woni hooreejo mon no debbo jogorii gorko mum ko kanko woni hooreejo mum, sabu Debbo oo ko Hawtirde am e ɓanndu am, kadi hol gorko asɗo ɓanndu mum? Kono, on ndokkii ardiiɓe majjuɓe ɓee majjinde on e ndee geɗe, kadi heewɓe e mon ina cemmbina konnguɗi bonɗi ummoriiɗi e annabaaɓe fenaande bonɓe ɓuri Konngol am ceniiɗo.

On mbaɗii goonɗinal mon e worɓe bonɓe, worɓe ɓe neldaani; kono ina njaafa on e konnguɗi moƴƴi e haalaaji mbelɗi ɗi ndokkaani ɓe. On ngoni e rewrude e maɓɓe no ko miin ngonɗon e rewrude. Tuubee! Heewɓe e mon ina ndewa e ardiiɓe fenaande feeñooɓe no ardiiɓe nuunɗuɓe nii. Kono, hay huunde, sabu Seyɗaani ina wayla hoore mum wonti maleyka annoore, ko ɗum waɗi fotaani haawnude so ardiiɓe mum mbaɗii noon. Heewɓe e mon ina wumnaa e annoore fenaande, ummoriiɓe e ardiiɓe fenaande ɓe neldaani, kadi ina kaala konnguɗi mawɗi luutndiiɗi janngingol moƴƴol ngol woni e am, Joomiraaɗo Iisaa Almasiihu.

Ndaranee hoore mon, onon Kerecee’en ɓe ngalaa semmbe, njaltinee e mum, njahdee e Sirru Babiloon. Miɗo yiɗi no feewi so on nguli walla on keewi, on ngonaani yoorɓe, mi alaa yiɗde tuutde on e hunuko am. Ko ɗum waɗi, ngartee e am, onon ɓiɓɓe am, ngartee e konnguɗi am. Njogo-ɗee kam ko mi hooreejo mon, sabu ko miin woni Joomiraaɗo maayɗo ngam mon, on ummii nde ummii-mi. Hol ko waɗi on mballitorii annabaaɓe fenaande? On njiɗaa hay neɗɗo gooto janngina on, kono ko teddungal ngal keɓ-ɗon e am ngal woni e mon, ngal janngina on kala huunde, onon ɓe goonɗinal mon famɗi! Ngartanee kam, ngartee e laawol famɗungol. Mboɗee, wullee, tuubee, onon ɓiɓɓe am! Kala mo njiɗ-mi, miɗo felana ɗum, mbele noddaango mon e cuɓagol mon laaɓa.

Anndee wonde miɗo yiɗi on no feewi, miɗo yiɗi ɓernde mon timmunde ɓuri golle mon. Ngaree e am e ɓernde udditiinde, ndokkee kam kala ko njogii-ɗon, no mi rokkiranii on kala ko njogii-mi. Woto kulon, woto ñaawee hoore mon, sabu alaa ñaawoore e ɓe ngoni e Almasiihu Iisaa. Ngartanee kam tan, njaɓee hakkeeji mon, mi yaafoo on, mi artir on e peeje e dokke ɗe ndokku-mi on. Ndaree no semmbiɗiri, sabu mi feeñoyto e wakkati seeɗa, aduna oo annda wonde mi hippii on, sabu ndee huunde waɗataa e cuuɗaari. Wakkati oo famɗii, onon ɓiɓɓe am, kadi ɓooyaani wakkati oo timma.

Mi waawi hisnude Yaaquuba, kadi mi waɗan ɗum no heddiiɓe hisnirtee.

Ko miin woni mawɗo limanɓe haa abada e mbaadi Melkisedek.

Ko miin woni Juloore timmunde.

Ko miin woni jawgel Alla.

Ko miin woni Joomiraaɗo maayɗo ngam mon, hono Yeshua HaMashiach.
