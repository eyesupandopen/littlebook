Seedamaku Ɗiɗo: Isra'iila
Ko Welɗi e ko Hawɗi
8 Oktoobar 2024

Arde Timmuɗo to Joomiraawo maa Duumotooɗo!

Nanndee, nanndee! Nanndee daande Joomiraawo mon, nanndee konnguɗi Yeesu Almasiihu, ɗowtanee ɓerɗe mon e anniyee Joomiraawo mon, accitee konnguɗi am ɗi'i mbelna ruuhuuji mon. Miɗo yama on naamnal. E on nani? So Ɓii Neɗɗo oo ari, mi tawanan goonɗinal e leydi ndii? Goonga, goonga, mi tawanan goonɗinal e nder goonɗinɓe am ɓe njahratmi e maɓɓe to kisal asamaanuuji ngam goonɗinal maɓɓe, kono ko ɓuri heewde e yimɓe wonɓe e leydi ndii, ngalaa goonɗinal. Ko ɗuum waɗi miɗo jokki e rentinde on, yiɗɓe am, mbele on ndentotoo e bakkaatuuji bakkaatunooɓe, njaltaton e laabi maɓɓe, ceertoton, taa on keɓda e kiitaaji maɓɓe.

Kono non, yimɓe heewɓe ina ɓattoo kam e tuuɗe mum en tan, tawi ɓerɗe mum en ina ngoɗɗi kam. Ñalnde heen, heewɓe maa mbi'am, Joomiraawo, Joomiraawo, mi wi'a ɓe: ngoɗɗee kam, mi anndaa on! Faamee wonde yettaade kam Joomiraawo e ɓernde nde alaa goonɗinal yettaaki, kono goonɗinɓe kam Joomiraawo tigi-rigi ko ɓeen tuubantooɓe bakkaatuuji mum en, accita ɗi, ndaara no mbaawirta wuurdude e nuunɗal yeeso am. On anndaa wonde miin Yeesu Nasaraatnaajo, miɗo ɓura yiɗde ɓernde mon ndee fof e yiɗde e koolaare, di ko sadakeeji tuuɗe mon ɗee? Ee, miɗo yiɗi yurmeende, wonaa sadak, sibu ko e yurmeende am, hono deƴƴere e tikkere fotnde waɗeede, yiɗde am ndee feeñirta, hay yiɗde Yaweh Alla mawɗo duumotooɗo oo! Kono konnguɗi am rentinooji ɗii ina nananee e juuɗe yiɗɓe kam, tuubiiɓe ngam yiɗde am nde feññinmi hoore am e maɓɓe ngam kisal maɓɓe. Ko noon yiɗde am ndee jaɓortee hakkunde ɓeen ɓe ɓerɗe mum en njaɓi daande am, e goonga, hakkunde yiɗɓe kam fof e ɓeen ɓe njannginmi janngude e am, hay ɓeen ɓe ndoowatmi e laabi am kisal.

Hol ko waɗi on accita ɓerɗe mon ina njuurna, onon ɓiɓɓe am tokosɓe? Tawa nelaaɓe am e annabaaɓe am rentinaaki e ndee ñalaande gila jamaanu fuɗɗii? Tawa annabaaɓe adinooɓe kabraaki yontere cappanɗe jeeɗiɗiire Daniyel ndee ina ara e kiita mawɗo e dow leydi ndii? Sabu ɗuum, faamee, oɗon nguurdi e balɗe garooje hade caɗeele Yaaquuba udditaade timmuɗe e dow leydi ndii fof, e nder ɗeen balɗe, masiibaaji keewɗi, maandeeji, kaawisaaji, kaayeefiiji, e kiitaaji kadi, kabranooma, jooni kadi udditaama, hol ko waɗi oɗon kaawi? Ɗii ko rentinordi cakkitiindi e mbaadi tokosiri e ko arata e dow leydi ndii fof so goonɗinɓe am ɓee njaltinaama to am to asamaanuuji gila e leydi ndii so mi mooftii ɓe wano ga'i caalle, ɓe keɓa kisal timmungal e timmal so ɓe ndarii e sahaa maɓɓe gadano jibineede kadi haa abada, ɗo ɓe maayataa, ɓe torraataa, ɓe ñawataa, kono ɓe keɓa weltaare timmunde e deeƴre timmunde e Yaweh, e goonga, e am, Yeesu Almasiihu, mi rokkata ɓe njeenaari mawndi e ndonu, sibu miɗo jogii njeenaari maɓɓe e juuɗe am baawɗe ɗee.

Heewɓe e goonɗinɓe am mbaɗaama lebenaaɓe e jannginooje fenaande ummoriiɗe e linjiila jawdi ka waajetee e laawol bonngol e juuɗe annabaaɓe fenaande e jannginooɓe fenooji yimɓe am, mbi'a ɓe masiiba sooyataa memde ɓe so tawii miijooji maɓɓe ina mbelɗi. Ɗiin ɓulli ɗi ngalaa ndiyam ina mbaaji Yeesu goɗɗo, ɓe anndaa kam, Yeesu Almasiihu, mi neldaani ɓe kadi. Pirotee saanga fof konnguɗi ɓeen wiyooɓe ina kaalda e innde am e binndi laaɓtuɗi, ɗo ɓe kaññi e binndi laaɓtuɗi, ko ɗoon ɓe kaaldata e ɓerɗe maɓɓe ngam nafoore maɓɓe njoorndi.

Goonga, goonga, won waajotooɓe fenaande heewɓe, aawɓe fenaande e nder jamaaji am, linjiila jawdi fenaande ina addana laawol yaajngol hallude ɗo dame jahannama udditoytoo e sara ɓeen rewɓe ɗum haa e maayo yiite ngo wulngo e ɗemɗe wulɗe no sinnotoo koppi bulooji, peewnanaaɗe tooñooɓe e rewɓe ɓe, woto on nane fenaande maɓɓe! Mi haalataa fii annabaaɓe am goonga, potɓe woofde konngol e innde am, walla ɓeen waɗooɓe majjere laaɓtunde, pooccitooɓe. Faamee wonde hay gooto e annabaaɓe am annabaaki timmuɗo, kono annabaaɓe am fof ina annabi feccere, ɓe kaññataa e binndi laaɓtuɗi so ɓe kaaldii e innde am tigi-rigi, Almasiihu Yeesu, sabu ko miin woni Konngol Alla tagngol ɓalliwal, hoɗngol e mon, hay so tawii e wiyde ɓalndu mi annditaake kadi.

Onon ɓiɓɓe am tokosɓe, woto on njiiloyee sabu saanga fof e nduu aduna ina woodi torra e satteende, haa teeŋti noon e joofnirde laamu Seytaane nde tooñooɓe ɓee ngari e kiita, nde ɓe nani ina njanngina golle ɗe ɓe njaɓɓaama waɗde e kiita maɓɓe sabu hay añɓe Yaweh ina njaɓa daande mum. On njanngaani e nder Roomnaaɓe ɗo njannginmi Pool e laabi am? Moy seerndata en e yiɗde Almasiihu? Woni torra, walla satteende, walla tooñannge, walla heege, walla ɓoornaare, walla kulol, walla kaafa? (Hono no winndiraa nii, sabu maa min mbaretee ñalawma fof, min limtee ko wano baali njahrooji to hirseteeri.) Alaa, e nder ɗii geɗe fof, min ɓuri foolde, e juuɗe giɗɗo min oo. Sibu miɗo goongɗini, wonde hay maayde, hay nguurndam, hay malaa'ikaaji, hay laamuuji, hay baawɗe, hay geɗe jooni, hay geɗe garooje, hay tooweeki, hay luggeendam, hay tagoore wootere, waawaa seerndude en e yiɗde Alla, ndeen wonnde e Almasiihu Yeesu Joomiraawo men.

Ko noon suɓaaɓe am ɓee ndañrunoo saanga fof torraaji keewɗi sabu innde am, woniino yimɓe heewɓe seedtooɓe e jamaanu am moƴƴere, rokkuɓe nguurndam mum en e golle ngam yaajnude linjiila kisal, hay linjiila ngoo hisinɗo ruuhuuji mon e goonɗinal e Yeesu Nasaraatnaajo sabu feccere ko e golle seedtooɓe am linjiila kisal ngoo heɓi e leydi ndii. Kono, heewɓe e mon mbaɗaama lebenaaɓe e lebenaaɗe jannginooje fenaande keewɗe “innde mum e wiyde mum” ɗe keɓi e Egliis am Laodikiyanke.

Ɗee jannginooje bonɗe ngonaa e am, miijooji mon keɓataa hay huunde e yaasi mon so wonaa so on kaaldii konnguɗi am e tuuɗe goonɗinal so mi rokkii maandeeji, kaawisaaji, e kaayeefiiji mbaɗee e juuɗe mon. O waawaa miijaade hoore mon haa njalton e caɗeele e tooñannge, kadi noddaaɓe am, suɓaaɓe e goonɗinɓe ngonaa ndimaagu e masiiba. Hol to e Konngol Alla wi'etee wonde kala goonɗinɗo kam sooyataa muusde torra, walla satteende, walla tooñannge, walla heege, walla ɓoornaare, walla kulol, walla kaafa? Ko luutndii ɗum, yoga walla hay fof e ɗii geɗe ina laaɓti e kala wuurdiiɗo e goonɗinal ngam am, ko ɗuum waɗi miɗo wasiyoo on mbele on njaɓataa konnguɗi maɓɓe sabu konnguɗi maɓɓe ko daari peewnaaɗi no feewi, nelaaɗi e juuɗe tooñooɓe ngam liɓde goonɗinal suɓaaɓe so tawii ɗuum ina waawi. Kono, faamee wonde goonɗinɓe am fof rewɓe kam e ɓernde mum en fof ina kisnaa e tikkere Jibraa'il e tikkere Alla, ɓe njaltintee to kisal e jooɗorde laamu Yaweh hade kiita oo fof waɗde e leydi ndii.

Ko noon woniri, ɓiɓɓe am, wonde heewɓe e mon maa njuurto e masiibaaji e tooñanngeeli keewɗi baɗaaɗi e juuɗe tooñooɓe, heewɓe maa njippoo jawdi aduna oo e ndee joofnirde hade am mooftude goonɗinɓe am. E kala saniye rewtungo, feeñde am ndee ina ɓeydoo ɓadtaade. Ko ɗuum waɗi miɗo semmbina on, yiɗɓe am, mbele on ndarotoo ngam am, njogitoo yiɗde am hono semmbe mon duumotooɗo ngam muñde ko heddii ngam addande majjuɓe kisal so on njiilani ɓe haa e laana kaa hade masiiba oo waɗde.

Faamee ko ndokmi Pool winndude e Kolosnaaɓe: Jooni miɗo weltii e torraaji am ɗi njuurtoomi sabu mon, mi timmina ko heddii e muuseeji Almasiihu e nder ɓalndu am sabu ɓalli mum, woni jamaa, mo mbaɗaami waajotooɗo e fawaade e yamiroore Alla, ndeen yamiroore rokkaami on ngam timminde konngol Alla. Ko noon woniri, yiɗɓe am, wonde so on njuurtiima sabu innde am, on njokkata e on keɓdata e torraaji am ɗi njuurtoomi to Kalvaari. Hay so tawii mi accaani hay huunde e kisal mon, mi woni Paska timmuɗo e laaɓɗo haa abada, mi noddii kala e mon ngam muñde geɗe keewɗe sabu innde am, ndañee ɗum weltaare fof! Anndee, ɓiɓɓe am, wonde yitere yiyaani, nofru nanaani, hay naataani e ɓerɗe yimɓe geɗe ɗe Alla feewnanii yiɗɓe mo. Dogee, dogee mbele on keɓa! Sabu Alla wonaa tooñoowo, haa o yejjita golle mon, e golle yiɗde, ɗe kolluɗon e Innde mum, nde njaɓɓiɗon Seniiɓe, oɗon njaɓɓii haa jooni.

Heewɓe e mon jiiɓnaama e miijooji mon haa on ngannditaaki wonde laabi keewɗi noddaaɓe am, suɓaaɓe, e goonɗinɓe ina njaɓa muñde caɗeele, hoyre, majjere, e ko ɓuri ɗum heewde, battane muñal maɓɓe e torraaji Almasiihu ko kisal majjuɓe e njeenaari mawndi e njoɓdi ngam ɓeen sadakooɓe hoore mum en e noon. On njanngaani e Golleeji ɗo Pool e Silas njuurti e caɗeele keewɗe to Masedoni, ɓe pawaa no feewi, ɓe njuɓɓinaa e kasoo e laawol bonngol, ɓe mbaɗaa e kaɓɓirɗe to Filipi?

Kono mi neli leydi yiiwri ngam hisnude ɓe e kaɓɓirɗe maɓɓe, ko noon gardiiɗo kasoo Filipi oo e galle mum keɓiri kisal e muñal Pool e Silas. E heewɓe e rewɓe kam, ɓe njuurtoytoo e caɗeele e tooñannge e torra. Kono e nder ɗii geɗe fof oɗon ɓuri foolde, miɗo haɓana on, ɓiɓɓe am, so tawii oɗon keddii e ɗooftaade daande am, oɗon rewi e anniyee am e ɓernde mon fof, mi waɗa on foolde geɗe fof so tawii oɗon keddii e goonɗinal e daande am, oɗon acca mi ardoo koyɗe mon, sabu kisal mon ina woni e ɗooftaade kisnoowo mon duumotooɗo.

Ko ɗuum waɗi miɗo wasiyoo on, yiɗɓe am, mbele on ndarotoo ngam am, ndokka yiɗde am aduna maayɗo, kiitaaɗo, dariiɗo e jolɗe so kiita oo ina foti waɗde timmuɗo. On njaɓay mi wallude on foolde mette mbele on njogitotoo yaakaare mon e hakkillaaji mon? On njettii konnguɗi am, yiɗɓe am? Jooni ina heddii goonɗinal, yaakaare e yiɗde, kono ko ɓuri mawnude e ɗii ko yiɗde. Ko ɗuum waɗi miɗo nela on yiɗde majjuɓe, sabu ko moƴƴere Alla addanta yimɓe tuubde laabi keewɗi.

Ko ɗuum waɗi, ngollee ngam am e nder renndooji mon, nduwanee ɓeen majjuɓe fof sabu so tawii ina poofta haa jooni, ina waawi wonde majjere aduna oo ina addana ɓe heɓde duumiyaare fof. Waajee bakkaatunooɓe, ndaddanee majjuɓe kisal, ciftoree on ko nelaaɓe am, yiɗɓe am, timminee noddaango mon e am, keddoɗon e tiiɗnaare e ndee joofnirde jamaanuuji. Miɗo nela on yiɗde hoɗdiiɓe mon, mballon e wallude ɓeen wonɓe e haaju. Hay so ko yarde ndiyam walla taasa nyamri, ndokkee kala ko mbaawɗon ngam wallude ɓeen njuurtooɓe e sikke hay huunde. So tawii kadi on njuurtiima e sara majjuɓe, nden ndañee ɓe yiɗde timmunde, kollon seedamaku am e baawɗe am, kaaldee e innde am e tuuɗe goongɗinɗe ummoriiɗe e ɓernde heewnde goonɗinal, yaakaare, e yiɗde!

Miɗo yama on, on kuli wallude bakkaatunooɓe? On faamaani wonde nde nelanoomi so wonaa e baali majjuɗi galle Isra'iila, mi waɗi waktu am e jaɓooɓe njoɓdi e bakkaatunooɓe? On njettii wonde onon kadi on ngonnoo ko e nguurndam bakkaatuuji duumotooɗi? On njettii wonde ko miin tan woni neɗɗo mo alaa bakkaatu, hay jibraa'il Alla? Accitee nden hisnaaɓe Joomiraawo ɓee mbi'a noon, ɓeen ɓe njoɓnoomi e juuɗe añɓe. Yiɗde, yiɗde, yiɗde haa e poofngol mon cakkitiingol, sibu ɓuri moƴƴude ko maayde e golle yiɗde Almasiihu di ko hulde e golle pamare geɗe bonɗe, ɗe njoginoomi fof, ndokki foolde kala waɗɗo kam Joomiraawo mum duumotooɗo. Woto on njiɗ nguurndam mon haa e joofnirde, rewee e yeru am golle ɗe ngalaa kulol yeeso bonannde e maayde, foolaaɗe timmuɗe e yiɗde duumotoonde Alla mawɗo duumotooɗo, hay El Shaddai!

Kulee wota, sabu miɗo wonndi e mon: wota on kule, sabu ko miin woni Alla mon: mi semmbinat on, mi walla on, mi jogorotoo on e junngo am nyaamo ngo peewal. Ngaree e am, onon sukaaɓe am, fooftoɗon e giɗal am ngal njoganii-mi on, mi deeƴna ɓerɗe mon, mi sellina gaañiiɗe mon, mi semmbinat on e mberlere mon ngam mbaawon hokkude wonɓe e haaju mon ɓee, sabu ko ngam ɗum on nelaa. Sabu ko onon woni nelaaɓe am, ɓe cuɓaaka tan, ɓe noddaaka tan e teddungal Laamu am, kono kadi ngam on muusana innde am.

Gooto, gooto, onon ko on geɗdiiɓe e muuseeji am; tokkee tan yeru am, wota on yoofe e waktu mon caɗeele. Sabu miɗo wonndi e mon e kala taaɓal laawol, ko miin, Joomiraawo Iisaa Almasiihu, laɓɓinoowo ruuhuuji mon e yiite am laɓɓinoowo, hay so tawii ko on leɗɗe leydi e juuɗe badoowo. Yiite laɓɓinoowo ngoo waɗat yeesooji mon mbaɗa no kaaƴe caɗɗe nii, haa on mbaasa heltaade e doole am, kono taw on heddii e lo'ere ɓalli mon. Kono noon, ko e nder lo'ere ɓalli bonɗi giɗal am timmirtee e mon nde mi ɓattini on e am, mi hebilana on ko ɓuri ɗum ngam anniyaaji mon poomayankeeji. Ko ɗum waɗi, ko peewal wonannde hisnaaɓe ɓee muusande innde am, sabu mi hokkii hoore am fof e sadaka muusɗo, mbele mbaawon wuurde yeeso Yahwe, kanko Alla tedduɗo, baawɗo fof. Faamee wonde e nder jarribooji e muuseeji mon, on ngaddataa tan yurmeende am hisinoore to wonɓe e haaju, kono kadi onon e ko'e mon, on ndon gollana hisnugol mon e kulol e diwnaare, mbele mi addana on njoɓdi mon timmundi, ko noon kadi yaakaare mon ɓadorii timmude!

Maa taw on nanuno muñal Ayuuba? Hol no Ayuuba wonnoo neɗɗo koolaaɗo, tawɗo caɗeele, mo Seytaane tooñi tawa alaa ko waɗi. Maa taw on nanuno battane Ayuuba, sabu Ayuuba wonaano neɗɗo bonɗo mo bone fooli, kono ko neɗɗo peewɗo, keɓɗo yurmeende, foolɗo bone e moƴƴere, sabu o jokki konnguɗi Yahwe, o heddii e peewal hay so tawii omo e nder tooñanngeeji bonɗi. Ko noon Yahwe artiri Ayuuba laabi ɗiɗi kala ko heɓtanoo e makko e dow tooñannge, Yahwe yoɓi Ayuuba caɗeele makko e tooñannge makko. Ndaaree, min njettii muñɓe ɓee. On nanuno muñal Ayuuba, on ngannduno battane ɗe Joomiraawo waɗi. Sabu Joomiraawo ko keewɗo yurmeende e muñɗo.

Ko noon ngoongɗingol, e yaakaare, e giɗal keddii, ɓurngal mawnude e ɗiin fof ko giɗal. Nde faamee, giɗaaɓe am, wonannde koolaaɓe, yaakaare ɓadake timmude no feewi nde njaltinteeɗon haa e kisal kammuuli, ngam daɗde e ñaawoore aroore. Nanee daande am, faamee giɗal am ngal njoganii-mi on, onon noddaaɓe am, koolaaɓe am, cuɓaaɓe am, hay e ɓanndu am, hay e jommbaajo am! Nde fooftoɗon e giɗal am, sabu hay e nder caɗeele ɓurɗe mawnude, giɗal am heddoto ko semmbe, ngo alaa baawɗo foolde ɗum, sabu miin e Baaba am ko min gootum e anniya. Ko giɗal Yahwe duumingal ngal mi hokkata kala ronooɓe nguurndam duumingam e kala arooɓe to Iisaa Nasarankeejo ngam hisnude.

Faamee, hay gooto waawaa arde to Yahwe so wonaa e am, Almasiihu Iisaa. Ko noon kadi giɗal Yahwe alaa baawɗo foolde ɗum, sabu Yahwe alaa baawɗo foolde ɗum, ko ɗum waɗi miin kadi alaa baawɗo foolde am, sabu mi foolii. Ko miin woni Joomiraawo hisnugol duumingal, kanko mo Yahwe waɗi Joomiraawo Joomiraaɓe e Laamɗo Laamɓe, o rokki mi innde ɓurnde kala innde mawnude, mbele e innde Iisaa Almasiihu kala koofu sujjida, e dow kammu e dow leydi e les leydi. Faamee wonde yanɓe ɓee kulii on, giɗaaɓe am, kulol mawngol nanngii ɓerɗe maɓɓe sabu ñaawoore maɓɓe ɓadoriinde, nde neeɓataa.

Miɗo yamira on, miɗo semmbina on, onon sukaaɓe am, yo on njahdu e giɗal am e aduna oo ñawɗo, maayoowo, ñaawoowo, e oo waktu cakkitiɗo hade musiiba oo fof yani. Miɗo wi'a on, cellinee ñawɓe, ummitinee maayɓe, laɓɓinee ngañamseeɓe, riiwee jinnaaji, sabu kala goongɗinɗo kam golle ɗe ngollu-mi e dow leydi, kanko ne o golloyan ɗum, o golloyan kadi golle ɓurɗe ɗum mawnude, sabu mi yahi to Baaba men. Njaree e giɗal mawngal e neɗɗaaku, kawron e wonɓe e haaju e doole Almasiihu ɗe njippinan-mi e mon e semmbe. Sabu ko e nder mon, onon leɗɗe am leydinkeeje, doole teddungal Alla e nder Almasiihu hoɗi. Kaalduɗon e caɗeele mon e ngoongɗingol. Ngoongɗinee Yahwe, ngoongɗinee Yeesu HaMasiihu, mbi'ee kaa hooseere, ITTU, NJIPPOY E MAAYO! So on mbaɗii ɗum e koolgol, mi hokkay maandeeli keewɗi, kaawniiɗi, e kattanɗe waɗireede e juuɗe koolaaɓe.

Cuusɗon, ngaree semmbe, sabu ko miin woni Mbaroodi leñol Yahuuda, haɓananoowo on. Miɗo yetta on, miɗo haɗa on e giɗal Alla ngal woni e Almasiihu Iisaa Joomiraawo mon, yo on muñan innde am e dow yiɗde, ngartiron majjuɓe to Baaba men, hay e oo waktu cakkitiɗo. Miɗo yetta on, onon cuɓaaɓe am, mbaɗee yeru, sabu ko onon woni koolaaɓe am cuɓaaɓe ɓe njalbin-mi e nder torra, mbele e nder lo'ere e mberlere ɓalli mon, on kollira doole Alla mawɗo duumiiɗo, kanko Yahwe e hoore mum, nde njahaton e gollal e laamu mawngu to ɓe neldu-mi on ngam gollanaade ɓe.

Miɗo wonndi e mon e kala taaɓal laawol.

Miin e Baaba am ko min gootum e anniya.

Ko miin woni Joomiraawo hisnugol duumingal.

Ko miin woni mo Yahwe waɗi Joomiraawo Joomiraaɓe e Laamɗo Laamɓe.

Ko miin woni Mbaroodi leñol Yahuuda, haɓananoowo on.

Miin, Yeesu HaMasiihu, neldirii ɗii konnguɗi e maccuɗo am, ko e maccuɗo am konnguɗi am nelaa ngam ɗoofagol e semmbin'gol kala ɓe ɗii konnguɗi keɓata. Konnguɗi am keɓay gite, e noppi, e ɓerɗe ɓe nelanooɓe ɓee. Ñiiɓee, giɗaaɓe am, sabu njoɓdi mon ina e dammbude mon, wota on yoofe tan. Fooftoɗon e giɗal am, fooftoɗon e golle am, nde ngoppoton golle mon, jooni ko waktu hisnugol mon, jooni ko waktu hisnugol mon timmungal, miɗo ara e mon, giɗaaɓe am. Nanee kam, nanee konnguɗi am, mi accataa on, mi welsindataa on, onon koolaaɓe am e nder Almasiihu, hay e kala ɗoftiiɓe daande am. Ndaaree kammuuli, sabu feeñde am ɓadake yani e kammuuli e waktu e saa'a mo cikkaano. Miɗo ara e mon, koolaaɓe am, on njettaama?
