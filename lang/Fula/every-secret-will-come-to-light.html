Seedeeɓe ɗiɗo: Eklesiya
E nder Golle Piyeer
12 Abriil, 2024

Kala Gundo Feeñete!

On njanngaali naa? Kala gundo feeñete. On njanngaali naa? Kala ko suuɗaa yaltinte. On njanngaali naa? Kala anniya ɓernde yaltata yeeso am. Kala ɗemngal anndinte kam, wiyi Joomiraaɗo Poondinoowo e Laamɗo Poondinoowo, laamotooɗo e poondagol. Hey, yimɓe am, ko waɗi on suɓii ngeñdi Aadama e Hawaa, ɓe gila puɗɗagol etinoo suuɗde hersa maɓɓe e golle maɓɓe, ɓe ɲooti kaakooli ɓokko ɓe mbaɗani hoore maɓɓe comci. Kono, Alla yi'i ko waɗi, o anndi ɓe njaltayno hade makko tagde ɓe. Ko Baabaajo oo waɗani ɓe? O WAƊANI ɓe comci guri o ɓorni ɓe, ko wonaa golle maɓɓe.

Ko waɗi on cikki on mbaawan suuɗde mi bakkaatuuji mon, ɓiɓɓe am? Miɗo yi'a on fuu, miɗo anndi on ko ɓuri no anndirton hoore mon. Accitee heɗaade duwooɓe woofaɓe, waajotooɓe Iisaa goɗɗo; goonga e nder weenduuji mbusɗi ɓe ndokkata ndiyam ɓuuɓɗam to eklesiya ɓuuɓɗo, mooftaaɗo e ngoonga mboda e miijooji diɗɗi mbiyateeɗi. ɓe mboofana on e Kerecee'enku wallitoogu hoore mum, ɓe kaalana on goongaaji baɗɗi no mbaɗiri hono Alla ina yiɗi on no ngonir-ɗon. Hay so tawii giɗli Baabaajo oo ko duumotooɗi, njoorɗi no feewi ngam ɓiɓɓe makko, Kerecee'enku goonga ina waajoo tuubugol e yaafagol bakkaatuuji, bakkaatuuji anndinaaɗi e goonga e ɓernde laaɓnde, ɗi njahdata e hisde e timmal. Kono, heewɓe e nder Eklesiya am ina cemmbina, ina pooɗana bakkaatuuji maɓɓe. ɓe cemmbina e filosofiiji maɓɓe, ɓe njiɗi jooɗorde fenaande, ɓe ndaara banndiraaɓe maɓɓe e mawnikinaare nde ɓe mooftata bonanndeeji maɓɓe e pooɗgol fenaande maɓɓe. Ndee wonaa ganndal Seytaane nde o hokki Aadama e Hawaa ɓiɓɓe leɗɗe harminaaɗe ɗe? Ndee wonaa golle ɓanndu naa?

Miɗo naamno on naamnal; on fuu on anndi jaabawol ngol. Ko woni njoɓdi bakkaat? Ko Deftere Seniinde ndee wi'i njoɓdi ndii yo won? Wonaa maayde naa? Kono, heewɓe e mooɗon ina cikki bakkaatuuji maɓɓe ina poti, ina jeyaa e maɓɓe, ko ɗum waɗi ɓe keertiiɓe, nde njagganoton moƴƴere am. E maayde, on maayan. Hay so tawii on njogii nguurndam duumotooɗam e am, mi yaafoto on fuu, mi woppataa on so on keddii e am, e maayde on maayan.

Gila e fuɗɗoode mon, on mbonata sabu tagoodi bakkaat wonndi e mooɗon.

So on njokkii e bakkaat, on aawii e ɓanndu, on soottan bonannde kala sahaa, sabu e maayde on maayan. Bakkaat en haa ragare maaynan kala fannu e nguurndam mon, haa e ɓanndu mon haa e fooftere mon sakkitiinde so on njokkii. Wonaa ɗum tan, kono on keɓataa njoɓdi mon, on ndarotoo e hersa e nder asamaanuuji e jooɗorde ñaawoore am nde leɗɗe, huɗo, e cobbal ɗi ndesunoo-ɗon ɗii njalbi haa laaɓi yeeso mon, on keɓan hasara nde mi ittata njoɓdi mon mi rokka goɗɗo, sabu ko miin woni ñaawoowo poondinoowo. Ko waɗi on ngarata e am e tuubugol, nden on nduttoo e wuttaare mon? Ko mbaɗoton? On paamaani on mbaawataa gollande mi e Seytaane e sahaa gooto? So on mbaɗii bakkaat, on wonan maccuɗo bakkaat haa on laaɓa e am, kala ɗemngal anndinte! Ko waɗi on njiɗi ruttude e geɗe lo'uɗe e miskineeje? Ko ɓe mbaɗata fof ko yiɗde warude on nde ɓe njahri on no coftal.

Anndee wonde jinneeji bonɗi ina mawnina hoore mum en e dow ɓiɓɓe am ɓe mbaawi nanngeede. Ko waɗi on suɓii addande innde mon hersa, mbaɗa hoore mon jaleede? On njippii giɗli mon gadani, heewɓe e mooɗon ina njeyaa e Kerecee'enku mbilewu e Kerecee'enku ballitoowu hoore mum, yeru diine mo mi alaa heen, sabu mi naatataa e eklesiya Laodise, kono miɗo darii e dammugal miɗo fiyata. Miɗo wi'a on laaɓɗum, miijo mon diɗɗungo ngoo laamotoo hay huunde, on njeeyaama.

Weltaare bakkaat ngam sahaaji seeɗa ina njogii nafoore e mooɗon haa hono noon? On paamaani miɗo yoɓoya on no feewi e geɗe asamaan ngam caɗeele mon e jarribooji mon? Mi yoɓan on ko njaɓanoton ngam am e innde am. Ruttoo e giɗli mon gadani, ɓiɓɓe am, ngaree e am, njaltee e Babilon. Sabu balɗe debbo mawɗo oo limtaama, alaa safaara mum; ko ɗum waɗi, njaltee e makko, yimɓe am, keerondiree woto on ndenndu e masiibooji makko. Ceertee e jiɗɗe bonɗe e golle ɓanndu. Sabu kala ko woni e aduna, jiɗɗe ɓanndu, e jiɗɗe gite, e mawnikinaare nguurndam, ummaaki e Baabaajo oo, kono ummii ko e aduna, aduna oo ina rewi.

Doggee jeeno, woto on ndenndu e hersa maɓɓe e batte mum. Ɓiɓɓe am, ko hono no ko on mbaɗata golle ɗee e koye mon nde keɓoton weltaare e bonannde e mbon-di. Accitee laabi mon ɗi ngalaa keeri, sabu miɗo jogori wonde e dow yimɓe ɓe keɓata jawdi ngam koye mum en, ɓooyataa kaalis mon wontan meere. Naatee e ruuhu am, sabu ruuhu am ko nguurndam e jam. Ceertee e bonannde, woto mbaɗee bakkaat! Sooɗee ɓiɓɓe ruuhu e am, sabu mi semmbinii on e dow bakkaatuuji mon so on laaɓii e am. On cikki on mbaawan suuɗde mi hay huunde? Mi semmbinaama ngam yi'de, miin e Baaba am ko en gootum e anniya e faandaare. Waawaa suuɗaade, waawaa suuɗde mi bakkaatuuji mon. Heewɓe e mooɗon nganndaa no mba'ir-ɗon mboniri nde pooɗoton hoore mon no wonaa on njogii bakkaat, kono gite mon ina njula e jiɗɗe bonɗe, ɗe njoɓata ɓanndu mon.

Paamee wonde gujjo oo ina huutoroo kuuje keewɗe e peeje keewɗe e dow mooɗon. So on njahdii e anniya am ngam haɓde e ruttaade e am, ko mi ballal duumotoongal e sahaa muñal, mi reentoto on, mi laɓɓinan on, mi waɗan on laawol ngam daɗde e jarribooji keewɗi ɗi njiidoton, kono haaɗa haɓee e kala ko njogii-ɗon. Alaa ko firti nde mbi'oton on nimsitii, on nduttoo e wuttaare mon, kono on hersayno yi'de rawaandu ina waɗa noon, kono e mawnikinaare mon on cikki on ceertuɓe, mbele woto mbaɗee hono rawaandu ladde.

Bakkaat ko bakkaat. Laawol gootol ngam foolde bakkaat ko e ƴiiƴam am cafroojam e fooftaade e am. Ruuhu mo njillu-mi e dow mooɗon oo semmbinan on. Mi tagii tagoodi kesiri e mooɗon, so on naamndii tan, mi tagon ɓernde laaɓnde e mooɗon.

Mbele ndarnee hunukooji mon ɓutti, udditee gite mon jiɗɗuɗe e duwaawu, ceertee e bonannde, mi laɓɓinan on. Eey, mi laɓɓinan on laaɓɗum no neesaa keso nii nde anndinton, kadi on mbaɗtii, on nduttii e laabi mon ɓutti. No keewi ni yiɗ-mi, miɗo fela; no keewi ni yiɗ-mi, miɗo hirsina. Hol baaba mo hirsintaa ɓiɗɗo mum? Hay so tawii heewɓe ina mbaɗa noon, ɗee wonaa laabi am, ɗee wonaa laabi Baaba am. Nde tawnoo Alla ina jokkondiri e mooɗon no ɓiɓɓe nii, o hirsinto on kadi no ɓiɓɓe nii; sabu so o hirsinaaki on, ndeen on wonaa ɓiɓɓe makko kono ko on leñɓe.

Kono onon ko on ɓiɓɓe makko, mbele ndokkee noppi mon e konnguɗi am, muñnee hirsingol, ndeenoo e jeertinirɗe, sabu nguurndam e weltaare ina ndaarii. Alaa hirsingol ngol yiyotoo weltaare e sahaa mum, kono ngo addan ɓiɓɓe jam e poondagol. Ngaree e am, mi laɓɓinan on e laabi mon ɓooyɗi, mi jannginan on laabi am ngam mbaawon yahde e poondagol e jam. Wallitee banndiraaɓe mon, laatoo yeru tuubugol, sabu e darnde mon on njippan jolokooje keewɗe e kaɓirɗe keewɗe, haa e kaɓirɗe ɗe njogii-ɗon e juuɗe mon, ɗe njogii-ɗon no feewi. Miɗo wasiyoo on yo on accit ɗe fuu. Bakkaat ina addana maccungaagu, ina yoɓa maayde; ko ɗum waɗi, njaltee e makko, woto on ndenndu e ñaawoore makko.

Miin ko mi laaɓɗo, mbele onon kadi laaɓee.

Miin ko mi jaɓnoowo ɓiɓɓe am.

Miin ko mi laɓɓinoowo ɓiɓɓe am.

Miin ko mi waɗoowo ɓiɓɓe am poondiiɓe e am.

Miin ko mi hisnoowo ɓiɓɓe am e moƴƴere am e yurmeende am.

Miin ko mi lootirteeɗo on e ƴiiƴam am ɗam alaa bakkaat.

Miin ko mi ñaawoowo poondinoowo.

Wakkati juutaa, ɓiɓɓe am, laabi mon ɓooyataa timma. Woto cikki ko bonannde naatata e laamu am, walla on ndarotoo yeeso am e bakkaatuuji mon on keɓa njoɓdi. Ko foolɓe tan ndarotoo e am. On hisete, kono ko waɗi on njiɗi hasaraade mawngu nguu ndesinan-mi on, sabu on mbaawataa hay faamde ko njaɓanoton ngam jiɗɗe mon.

Ngaree e am, sabu miɗo heewi yurmeende.

Ngaree e am, sabu moƴƴere am wallan on.

Ngaree e am, sabu ko miin woni jahoowo e nder lampaaji jeeɗiɗi ɗii, ko miin hisnata njoɓdi mon so on ngarii e am e lesɗinaare, on accitii mawnikinaare mon meere. Miɗo yiɗi on, ɓiɓɓe am, ɓooyataa on ngonndanta e am. Ndaaree! Miɗo darii e dammugal miɗo fiyata haa sahaaji seeɗa goɗɗi. Miɗo haalana on geɗe ɗee, wonaa ngam ñaawoode on, kono ngam mbaawon heɓde nebam e nder lampaaji mon, mbaawon heɓanaade mi so mi arii. Miɗo ara e sahaa mo on cikkaaki, miɗo ara ko ɓuri yaawde e ko mbaawon miijaade. Wakkati ina ɓadii timmude.
