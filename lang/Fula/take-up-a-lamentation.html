Seedamaku Ɗiɗo: Eklesiya
E nder Golleeji Yuhanna Baptista
13 Mars, 2024

Heɓtee Woyde

Konnguɗi ɗi Joomiraawo hokki mi, mi anndinan ɗi e udditere: Nanee Konngol Joomiraawo!

Heɓtee woyde; goonga, heɓtee woyde sabu ɓe njaŋkotooɓe e ndee aduna bonndi. Sabu kamɓe ɓe mbi'i e nder ɓerɗe maɓɓe, "Ko waawi heɓde mi? No wonirnoo gila adan, noon wonirta haa abada; miin e yimɓe am min njokkay wuurdude e weltaare, min mooftanay hoore amen moƴƴere ndee aduna, min kebbina jawdi, nde hisnata en goonga e wakkati baasi.” On mbi'i e nder ɓerɗe mon, "Min ngoni annduɓe, min ngoni alɗuɓe; masiibo heɓataa en, no heɓirtaa en nii, sabu Alla amen accataa leydi amen yani; sabu min ngoni annduɓe e semmbe.” Kono on wonaa annduɓe, sey annduɓe e miijooji mon tan; mi hokkii añɓe mon poolgu dow mon.

Hey, neɗɗo, hol hisnata ma e waktu ñaawoore maa nde ɓadotoo aduna oo fof? Ardiiɓe maa hisnay ma? Goonga, mi wi'ataa, nde ɓiɗɗo halkaare oo ari e mon, omo yaha hakkunde mon e udditere, omo nanna heewɓe e konnguɗi moƴƴi e haalaaji lobbi. Ceede mon hisnay on? Aa'a, jawle mon fuu keɓay nafoore e nder waktu gooto, ngeenaari golleeji nguurndam mon ɗi mooɓtunoɗon e muuyɗe heɓataake. Eh, si on accaano laabi Joomiraawo, on njiɗnoo koree mon, on mballitnoo miskineeɓe e keewal mon ngal.

Kono aa'a, on mooftii keewal ngal Alla hokki on ngam weltinde ruuhu mon. Goonga, mi hokkii heewɓe e mon ko ɓuri haaje mon e ngam barkinde koree mon; kono aa'a, on mbi'i, mi reñanay hoore am ngal keewal mawngal sabu Alla goonga barkinii kam sabu moƴƴere e semmbe am. Ndaaree, mi anndinii on huunde hade mayre waɗde, ngam e nder waktu masiibo mon on ciftora daande am e konnguɗi hunduko am ɗi mi wuurnini e annabaaɓe am. Sabu mi nelaali ɓe ngam haalde e ko'e maɓɓe, kono mi nelii ɓe ngam wonde reentinoore e mon, ngam on mbaawa hisnude ruuhu mon e waylitagol e am, Joomiraawo Iisa Almasiihu, baawɗo hisnude on haa timmi. Kono on mbi'i e nder ɓernde mon, Kisndam? Mi alaa haaje hisneede, sabu miɗo weli no mi jibiniraa nii. Kono haram mon ko urdi bonndi e kine Baaba am, leydi ndii bonii sabu bonaneeji mon. On anndaa on jibinaama maayɓe e nder calɗi e haram, ɗum bonnii jikku mon?

Hay so tawii miɗo yiɗi hokkude on jikku keso, laaɓɗo, on njogitii sadaka am ko meere, on njaŋkotoo e ɓanndu mon bonndu ndu.

Heɓtee woyde sabu yimɓe leydi mon mawndi adan ndi salii daande am. Sabu sunaare mawnde e torra ari e mon e tampere. Wonay dillere mawnde, konu am semmbinngu to dow nguu toɓanay mbonnaari mawndi dow debbo jeenu mawɗo oo, o ummataako kadi, hay so tawii heddiiɓe hisnay. Hay e nder masiibo mon e halkaare mon nde ɓadotoo goonga, mi nelanay on annabaaɓe am. On anndiray ɓe e ɓiɓɓe leɗɗe maɓɓe, konnguɗi, e cellal; cellal keewngal e kaayɗe mi waɗay e juuɗe maɓɓe. Kono, haa jooni heewɓe halkay, maayay e nder haram maɓɓe, kono newiiɓe ndonay leydi ndii; sabu ndonu am ina wondi e am, ina heɓanii rokkude goonɗinɓe ɓe. Ko honno mi fawirta junngo am e yimɓe salotooɓe ɓe ndarii e am e laabi am, ɓe e nder nannannoo maɓɓe mbi'i ko kamɓe ngoni fonnditiiɓe yeeso Alla e comci maɓɓe tuunɗi?

Tuubee! Woyee, uumee sabu haram mon, woyee e am ngam mi waawa hisnude on. Heewɓe e eklesiyaaji mon naatii e mbarooɗi njoorɗi, ɗi waajotoo on konnguɗi ardotooɗi on haa e timmoode mon, haa jooni on njahdataa e maɓɓe, on ngartaa e am e yeeso e ɓernde udditiinde, no mi hisnirta on e torraaji mon, mi reena sukaaɓe mon e konuuji bonɗo oo, ɓe njaahata e laabi mon e udditere, ɓe puɗɗiima wujjude on sanne. Muuyɗe maɓɓe keɓataa haa ɓe keɓa kala huunde e mon, ɓe mbonna heewɓe. Kono ɓe noddooɓe innde am hisnay so ɓe muñii haa timmoode.

Hol ko waɗi on njoornini ɓerɗe mon e Joomiraawo Konuuji? Hol ko waɗi on njokkii fenaade ɓerɗe mon, on mbi'a, "Alaa masiibo; no wonirnoo keeñol, noon wonirta hannde, janngo adduroyta ko nanndi?” Onon jeegooɓe on keɓay ngeenaari laabi mon ɗi ngalaa goonɗinal, ɓe calii mi ɓe keɓay halkaare timmunde e torra mawɗo. Nanee konnguɗi am, hey, jiilol jeenungal e bonngol. Nanee konnguɗi am, toɗɗee noppi mon e majji ngam nanude kala alkulal ngal mi haalata. Dillere mawnde bonnay leydi mon nde njogitinoɗon sanne, e nder dillere mawnde ariroyta mbonnaari mawndi. Mi bonnay gure mon mawɗe adan, mi addoyta ndiyam ngam suuɗde daande maayo mon haram, sabu on njiɗaa Joomiraawo kono on njiɗɓe weltaare ko ɓuri yiɗde Alla.

Mi alaa weltaare e ñaawoore mon, sabu moƴƴere am e yurmeende am keewii e mon gila duuɓi. Heewɓe e ɓe mbonnii alkulal Nuuhu kamɓe e ko'e maɓɓe suuɗete e ndiyam sabu bonnude qaasi laaɓɗo oo mo kalaaji keewɗi mo Baaba am hokki ngam wonde alkulal. Yiite toɓay e mon e juuɗe añɓe mon, heewɓe halkay e nder yiite e tooke cakkitiiɗe ɗee. Leydi mon seekete, masiibo mawɗo wonay hiraande mon, sabu on caliima Joomiraawo Joomiraaɓe e Laamɗo Laamɓe.

Bonɗo oo naatinii Konu mum Boɗeewu e nder gure mon, gure tokosooje, e gure. Ɓe njahata hakkunde mon, ɓe njiɗi halkude on; ɓe ndarii e yamirooje maɓɓe e yahdu reenoowo am, mo mi accata ɓooyataa. Ɓe laɓɓinay, ɓe halkay, ɓe njaɓɓoytoo ko heddii koo. Wonaa ɗum tan, kono ɓe njogitoyto ko kañum en jeyi, ɓe nawroy heewɓe e mon haa e kampuuji maɓɓe ɗi ɓe mbaɗiino. Heewɓe taƴete ko'e sabu innde am, mi yoɓay ɓe sanne so ɓe keddii goonɗinɓe haa timmoode. Wanaa annoora; wonay ponji yani, laabi bonɗi. Hol rokkay on ñamri e ndiyam nde leydi mon bonni, ummataako kadi? Kono mi hisnay heddiiɓe, mi hisnay heddiiɓe, sabu mi anndinii ɗum!

Woto kulee, yimɓe am e goonɗinɓe am. Mi nanngay on hakkunde majji e nder lemsal gite haa e suudu Baaba am, ɗo mi waɗanii on nokkuure e taabal ngam en cippoo, sukaaɓe am. Miɗo wuuri yiide mon, ɓooyataa on njiyay yeeso am e gite mon, on keɓay ngeenaari goonɗinal mon e am sabu on ngalaa goonɗinal e nder ɓernde mon nde on nji'aani hay e gite mon teddungal am, walla teddungal Baaba am, mo ɓuri mi mawnude. Mi noddii on, mi suɓii on ngam wonde jeyaaɓe am, on ceertataa e am haa abada, sabu on wonay e Joomiraawo mon e hisnoowo mon haa abada. Anndee mi yoɓay on sanne, mi toɓanay on barkeeji ɗi on mbaawaa faamde e oo waktu. Keddinee goonɗinɓe haa timmoode, sabu waktu seeɗa ɗee geɗe ngarday; goonga, waktu oo leelii, jemma oo yaniima e leydi ndii.

Miin woni Laamɗo Laamɓe e Joomiraawo Joomiraaɓe, innde hokkanoo mi dow kala innde, ngam e innde Iisa Almasiihu kala ko woni e dow e leydi fof ɗoofoo.

Miin woni Laamɗo Jam, yaasi am alaa jam.

Miin woni on hokkuɗo on fooftere, ngam on fooftoo e am, on keɓa reedu e nder moƴƴere am.

Miin woni on jahowo hakkunde lampaaji jeɗɗi ɗii, on yamirɗo malaa'ikaaji Baaba am, kala huunde e dow e leydi ina woni e juuɗe am, no Baaba am hokkirnoo mi e nder poolgu am dow bonɗo oo haa añɓe am mbaɗee teɓteɓel koyɗe am.

Miin woni Joomiraawo mon e hisnoowo mon, miin woni gaynaako moƴƴo oo mo laaminoo e kala teddungal, miɗo jogii nguurndam haa abada e juuɗe am ngam ɓe njaɓɓotooɓe hoore maɓɓe ɓe njiɗi.

Anndinee mi Joomiraawo! Woto kulee, sukaaɓe am tokosooɓe, sabu ko wakkatiiji cakitiiɗi, yimɓe heewɓe hisnay e nder torra garoowo oo. Kala noddoowo innde Iisa Almasiihu hisnay, mi sellinay ɓe, mi yoɓa ɓe sabu goonɗinal maɓɓe. Goonga, miɗo yaha hakkunde mon, ko honno huunde mawnde Joomiraawo waɗi nde o anndinaani annabaaɓe makko hade mayre waɗde? Mi wi'ii on e laaɓal, nanee daande am, sabu waktu seeɗa heddii hade sawta buutol cakitiiingol. Hay malaa'ikaaji ina ndarii e dow to dow, ina ndarii sawta buutol! Kisndam mon timmungam ina woni e damal mon, woto kulee, njaŋkotoo e am, sabu nguurndam mon ina suuɗii e am, ina ɓadii feeñde.
