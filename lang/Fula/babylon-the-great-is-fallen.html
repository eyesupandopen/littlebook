Seedeeɓe ɗiɗo: Isra'iila
Ko Welɗi e ko Haɗi
Ut 18, 2024

Baabila Mawɗo oo Yani, Yani!

Njeertinee ɓerɗe mon e konnguɗi am, nanee haala am, nguuree konnguɗi am naata nder ɓerɗe mon: sabi hay goonɗinɓe e nder Eklesiya am e Yettanaaɗo am oo eɓe njogii jarribooji wallitde e nuunɗaare. Nanee kam no feewi, njeeɗtinee noppi mon faa paamon jamaanu mo ngonɗon oo, sabi hay gooto e mon meeɗaay yiyde kewuuji jooni ɗii no ɗi njuɓɓirtoo e dow aduna ngam ñaawoore. Sabi ñaawoore e musiiba ngarii, ɗi puɗɗiima kadi, kono ɗii ko geɗe fuɗɗoode dee, ɗe ndaɗataa jooni, ɗe mbaɗiraa ko ngam rentinde ɓe nguurdata e muñal. Kono, hay e nder cuɓaaɓe am e goonɗinɓe am, woodi ɓe cuɓotoo e anniya mum en mbumdam e nokku goonga, ɓe cuɓotoo kadi e anniya mum en nande sawtu mo laaɓaani e nokku laaɓɗo e jam ɗo Konnguɗi Joomiraaɗo addata.

Sabi miin, Yesuwaa HaMasiyaak, woni Iisaa Almasiihu e ɗemngal Pulaar, ko miin neldi ɗii konnguɗi rewrude e maccuɗo am mo ummini-mi, mi toɗɗii, mi suuɗii e nder faho am ngam jamaanu hono oo. Alaa ko haawnii e ɗuum, sabi noon ko laabi am, sabi miin Almasiihu Iisaa, ko miin waawi umminde nelaaɓe, annabaaɓe, waajuɓe linjiila, durooɓe, e jannginooɓe ummoraade e yimɓe ɓe ngalaa seedanteeje aduna oo yaniiɗo, ɓe ngonaa kadi e ɓe kawritii e nanondiral ekumenik e jaagorɗe Seytaane ɗe nanndi e jaagorɗe nuunɗal. Alaa ko haawnii e ɗuum, sabi Seytaane e hoore mum waylotoo ko malaa'ika annoora, ɗuum noon wonaa huunde mawnde so jaagorɗe mum wayliima no mba'i wa jaagorɗe nuunɗal, sabi goonga ko ɓe njiɗi ko hawrude denndaangal yimɓe e les laawol maɓɓe gootol adunaaru.

Hono no soobaajo am Yuhanna Jaatigi haalirnoo duuɓi keewɗi jooni nde o fonndunoo laabi ɗi njahratnoo-mi ɗii: Woto on ciftor e nder ko'e mon, Min njogii Ibraahiima baaba amen: sabi miɗo haalana on, Alla waawan umminde e ɗii kaaƴe ɓiɓɓe Ibraahiima. Jooni noon kadi jammbere ndee waɗaama e daaɗe leɗɗe: ɗuum noon, kala lekki ngo rimaani ɓiɓɓe leɗɗe moƴƴe, maa taƴe, werlee e yiite.

Njanngee, paamon, toroo hakkilantaagal, onon sukaaɓe am tokosɓe, kuutoree ɗii konnguɗi e jamaanuuji mon. Sabi miɗo haalana on laaɓɗum, mi nelaani mawɓe aduna oo hono nelaaɓe am e annabaaɓe am, mi naamnaaki kadi seedanteeje aduna, hono Doktoor oo e Reverend oo, hay tiitooɗe ɗe yimɓe ndokkata koye mum en ngam nanndude e Alla. Sabi neɗɗo yaniiɗo ina yiɗi safooɓe teddinde ɓanndu mum e golle aduna, ina yiɗi hay dariimaade e nuunɗal yeeso Alla e hoore mum e nder ɓanndu bonndu ndu hay neɗɗo waawaa laɓɓinde, so wonaa e am Iisaa Nasaraatuujo. Jooni noon, miɗo naamnoo on seedanteeje aduna ɗe njoginoo-mi e nder golle am e Yahuuda, nde mi nelaaka so wonaa e baali majjuɗi galle Isra'iila? Wonaa ɗum wonnoo fattu yeeso goonɗinɓe e nder leñol am nde ɓe naamndinoo hol to ngol hakkilantaagal e ngol golle mawɗe ngari e oo neɗɗo? Wonaa oo ko ɓiy sawruɗo oo?

Hono no winndiraa, noon kadi timminirtee: Kono Alla suɓiima geɗe aduna ɗe ngalaa hakkille ngam haawnaade annduɓe; Alla suɓiima kadi geɗe aduna ɗe ngalaa doole ngam haawnaade geɗe doole; Geɗe aduna leslese, e geɗe calɗaaɗe, ɗe Alla suɓii, ee, e geɗe ɗe ngalaa, ngam bonnude geɗe gonɗe: Fawaade e ɗuum, hay ɓanndu woto mawnin e yeeso makko. Nanee konnguɗi am goonɗinal ɗi kaaletee e tonngooɗe goonɗinɓe am, mi haaliino ɗum ko adii, miɗo haala kadi jooni wonde annabaaɓe am, ɓe keewi, ɓe luurataa e binndi laaɓtuɗi, ɓe luurataa kadi e am, Almasiihu Iisaa, Joomiraaɗo Jowitiiɗo oo!

Paamee, onon yo goonɗinal mon leefngal, wonde annabaaɓe am ina kaalda e daande wootere, ɓe kaalata ko konnguɗi ɗi ndokku-mi ɓe. On anndaa daande Joomiraaɗo mon? Konnguɗi am ko jananɗe e mon, onon sukaaɓe am tokosɓe? On kulii goonga? Sabi ko miin woni Konngol Alla ngol wonti ɓanndu, ngol hoɗdi e mon, ko miin rokkii on ruuhu am doole oo mo njuurnu-mi e dow denndaangal ɓanndu e ɗii jamaanuuji cakkitiiɗi hono no fodaaka. Sabi winndaama: ɓiɓɓe mon worɓe e rewɓe maa annaba, sukaaɓe mon worɓe maa nji'a yi'annde, mawɓe mon worɓe maa koyɗa koyɗi: E dow maccuɓe am worɓe e rewɓe, miɗo juurna e ɗeen ñalɗi Ruuhu am; ɓe annaboto: Miɗo hollira kaawisaaji e dow asama, e maale e les leydi; ƴiiƴam, e yiite, e cuurki: Naange maa wayle niɓre, lewru maa wayle ƴiiƴam, hade ñalawma mawɗo Joomiraaɗo oo arde.

Nanee konnguɗi am, njaɓee Konnguɗi am sabi ko goonga, denndaangal ɗi maa timme, hay yod gooto e hay gaasa yod gooto maa timme sabi kala Konngol Alla rokkiraama ko ngam ɗooftaade, kala Konngol Alla maa timme timmunde tawa alaa ko ŋakki, sabi laabi Yahweh ko timmuɗi, kala ko o waɗi juuɗe makko ngam timminde ina adda timmal kaawningal. On anndaa laabi Baaba mon, onon sukaaɓe am tokosɓe?

Nanee daande am, onon cuɓaaɓe am goonɗinɓe, Eklesiya am e Yettanaaɗo am kadi! On njokkat e muñde konnguɗi am, on nduttoo e daande am ngam yiɗde fenaande aduna? On njokkat daande yaniiɓe ɓee nde on nguurndam mon fof on njiylotoo e ekran? Miɗo ñaawoo on, nanee daande am, miɗo haalana on, hol ko waɗi ɓerɗe mon ina njirbina on? On paamaani wonde ko miin jooɗiiɗo e juuɗe am, miɗo wuppa yiite laɓɓinirde ngam ittude mbonndu e kaŋŋe tedduɗe e nder jawdi Yahweh keewndi ndii?

Noon kadi golle juuɗe am ina mbaɗa e nder ɓerɗe mon e nguurndam mon, yiɗɓe am: sabi ko miin ittoowo goonɗinal, ko miin ittoowo laabi mon goonɗinɗi, ko miin laɓɓinoowo on haa laaɓa e kala sifaa bonannde ɓanndu e bonnde nde ngaraton e am, njaɓon aybeeji mon e ellaaji mon ngam mi laɓɓina on timmunde e nder ƴiiƴam am ndimɗinɗam ngam mbaawon dariimaade tawa on ngalaa aybe yeeso jappeere Yahweh nde mi hollitata on timmunde e Baaba am e hoore mon e nder ko arata ko ɓooyaani.

Kono, heewɓe e mon ina njiɗi ruttaade e nguurndam mon ɓooyɗam nde mi timminaani anniya Yahweh fawaade e waktuuji mon e peeje mon. Sabi noon, hay almuɓɓe am e nelaaɓe am ɓurɓe ɓadaade ruttiima e liɗɗi caggal nde mi fawaa e leggal, mi ummitinaa, kono mi waɗi ɓe liɗɗi yimɓe. Jooni noon, miɗo ara ngam mooɓtude on kadi e sawtu daande am, on paamaani goonga kulhuli ɗi ngondi e mon e nder oo aduna bonɗo jooni oo. Jooni noon, miɗo nodda on e hoore am e ɗii jamaanuuji cakkitiiɗi ngam ngar-ɗon timmunde e am, ngaree timmunde e aybe e goonɗinal, sabi ko miin noddi on e moƴƴere e goonga, hay so tawii heewɓe e mon ina njiɗi ruttaade e Misra.

Jooni noon, miɗo wasiyoo on yo on njogoo jannde nde winndaa gila ko adii, ndokka rento e hakkilantaagal annduɓe. Sabi ɓe kaalani Muusaa: Sabi alaa yanaande e Misra, ko ɗuum waɗi ngaddan-ɗon en maayde e ladde? Hol no mbaɗir-ɗon e amen noon, njaltinon en e Misra? Hol ko waɗi on njogii haa jooni yiɗde ñalɗi ɓooyɗi? On njogii haa jooni yiɗde nguurndam e wellitaare aybe? Hol ko waɗi on njiɗi heddaade e rewɓe ɓe ngalaa hakkille nde mi darii e dow mon, miɗo rokka on juuɗe ngam ummaade, sabi on yanii tawa on anndaa, on ngondi e mboorndi leydi, sabi mboorndi maa won ñamri mboros. Ummoo onon yo ɗaaniiɓe, njufon mboorndi leydi, ummoo e teddungal asama, ngoonɗinee konnguɗi am goonɗinal ɗi goonɗinɓe kaalata e tonngooɗe goonɗinal!

Jooni noon, paamee ko woni e waɗeede jooni, paamee jamaanu mo ngonɗon oo, sabi heewɓe njaɓii ɗaanaade, ɓe ngoonɗini konnguɗi Seytaane ɗi ngalaa goonɗinal nde o wuppatnoo fenaande welnde nannde, ndaartooɗo hay cuɓaaɓe so ɓe cuɓiima goonɗinde fenaande makko. Paamee wonde Seytaane, hay Iblis janfotooɗo aduna oo fof, werlaama jooni ummoraade e asama, haɓɓaama e leydi ngam ñaawoore ɓadtiinde ndaɗataa.

Jooni noon, woodi darnde nde yaniiɓe ɓee njaɓata waɗde geɗe mum en e dow laawol aduna, e darnde nde ruuhuuji cakkitiiɗi ɗii njaɓata jooni e laana ndimaagu duumingal e nder am Almasiihu Iisaa, Joomiraaɗo maɓɓe ummitiiɗo e kisɗo. Kono, heewɓe e mon, e miijo mon ngo alaa goonɗinal, njaɓii konnguɗi am ko fenaande, ɓe njaɓii e majjere wonde on njogii waktu ngam ruttaade e nguurndam mon ɓooyɗam aybe e wellitaare. So wonaa, e nder jaawngal, goonɗinɓe ɓee maa njahde e jappeere Yahweh e hoore mum e njoɓdi mawndi, e ndonu duumingal, goonɗinɓe ɓee fof maa wayle haa abada.

On njokkondiray e goonɗinɓe? On njogii neɗɗaagu ngam anndude goonɗinal mon, tuubde ngam naatde e fedde goonɗinɓe? So on cuɓiima yiyde, miin Almasiihu Iisaa, mi hollirta on. Hol ko waɗi on tampi e golle moƴƴe? On pellitii wonde ɓe ngaddan-mi on e ɓe ngaddan-mi on, ɓe potaani nguurndam duumingal? Jooni noon, miɗo wasiyoo on yo on kollu yurmeende e yurmeende banndiraaɓe mon hono no mi hollirnoo on e nder ndee darnde sakkitiinde hade bonannde arde. E waɗde noon, on kisnday heewɓe, sabi on ngoni haa jooni nelaaɓe am. Udditee hunuko mon e goonɗinal, tawa on sikkaani hay huunde, miɗo wuppa e nder mon konnguɗi kaaloton.

Jooni noon, sukaaɓe am tokosɓe e hay goonɗinɓe am ina ngondi e muñal, hay e nder ndee darnde sakkitiinde hade Eklesiya am e Yettanaaɗo am yahde asama. Kono, hono no wonnoo e ladde nde Muusaa hawri e Yahweh e dow Sinai, ɓiɓɓe ɓee ummii ngam ñamde, yarde, fijde, ɓe mbaɗani koye maɓɓe ga'al kaŋŋe ngam rewrude e nokku Alla guurɗo oo, mo ɓe njaɓaani daranaade, kono ɓe ndarii sara, ɓe nji'i teddungal wonde Alla mawɗo duumiiɗo oo e dow Sinai! Hono no Muusaa ñaanirnoo ɓiɓɓe ɓee, noon kadi miɗo ñaananoo denndaangal ronooɓe ndimaagu duumingal, ngam denndaangal noddaaɓe mbaawa arde timmunde e ndimaagu e tuubugol ummoraade e laabi maɓɓe bonɗi e goonɗinɗi. Ruttanee daande am, keddoɗon e nokkuuji ɗi ndokku-mi on, mi reentoyta on e ñaawoore ndiyam ndiyam ndiyam. Mi haalanaani on yo on njaltu e maɓɓe, on ceerta? Ɗoftee daande am!

Jooni noon, ñaawoore yanii e leydi, ñaawoore feewnaama gila ko adii ngam goonɗinɓe e tiiɗɓe, hay ɓe e anniya mum en walla e majjere njogii e murto Seytaane. Sabi heewɓe, heewɓe, heewɓe yimɓe maa cuɓoo e anniya mum en yiite ndiyam ndiyam ndiyam ndiyam ngam Iblis e malaa'ikaaji mum e nokku ummaade e aybe, arde e nguurndam duumingal rewrude e noddude Iisaa Nasaraatuujo ngam ndimaagu maɓɓe. Noon kadi maa won, sabi El Shaddai, hay Alla Baawɗo oo, rokki neɗɗo yiɗde ngam neɗɗo waawa suɓaade nguurndam e nuunɗal e jam yeeso Yahweh, walla suɓaade bonannde duumingal e nder ndiyam mawɗam ndiyam ndiyam ndiyam ngam yaniiɓe ɓee ɗo liɗɗi maɓɓe maayataa. Waktuuji ñaawoore ngarii.

Jooni noon, miɗo haalana denndaangal ɓe njanngata kewuuji ɗi mbaɗata hade maɓɓe waɗde. Ɗee kewuuji ngonaa sirlu, kono annabaaɓe am e maccuɓe am kaali ɗum gila duuɓi keewɗi jooni, hay denndaangal ɓe neldu-mi to Baabila sirlu. Sabi waktuuji ñaawoore ndeen jeenoowo mawɗo oo ngarii, colli ɗi o ronndii ɗii maa bonnu mo timmunde. Sabi Alla waɗii e ɓerɗe luuɓe sappo ɓee timminde anniya makko, ɓe njiɗaa jeenoowo oo, ɓe njokkat e waɗde mo moƴƴere e ɓoornaade, ɓe ñamrat ɓanndu makko, ɓe njokkat e wulde mo e yiite. Noon kadi Baabila sirlu maa waɗe moƴƴere timmunde, waɗee e semteende ngam denndaangal yiyoo.

Gure makko mawɗe ɗe nganndiranoo ko noone golle tiiɗɗe e peewnugol e heewde El Sadday jooni ina ngonndi e mbonanndeeji, iwdi makko ina heɓtinaa e dorog e wuurde e nder tuundi e laabi makko ñabbuɗi. O waɗaama e mbaadi col, sibu ndeenka makko ina ittaa haa woɗɗi, ngalu makko e semmbe makko ina ndokkee e juuɗe añɓe makko Edomiyankooɓe, ɓe mbaɗata ko no wondiiɓe nii. Debbo jeenayanke mawɗo oon, mo haɓdunoo e leƴƴi kala ɗi o nelanoo ngam heɓtude, jooni o haɓɓaama, o alaa ko o reenata, o heɓorii haa o heɓtoree. Kala tergal ina maaya e mette, kala nanngaaɗo ina woppiree, ɓeen baawɓe huuwande daaba oon ina njahra e maccungaagu, heddiiɓe ɓee fof ina mbaree, hay so tawii heddiiɓe ina wuura. Omo wula, yiite ina heewi e keeri makko, e leyɗeele hoɗdiiɓe makko to bannge worgo, ɓe ndenndi e ñawuuji makko. Kono ɗii ko tan ko mettuɗi jibinannde, wonaa ñaawooje cakkitiiɗe, kono ko jeertinaango wonande kala bonannde maɓɓe nde arata. Sabu ɗum waɗirta ko e jaawngal, ɗum waɗirta ko e jaawngal. Ɗum waɗirta ko tan e no mi nannfirta goonɗinɓe am e leydi ngam hisnude ɓe, mi renndina alkamaari Yahweh e nder ngesa mum duumotooɗa, sabu waktuuji ñamri ngari. Kono wonaa ñamri cakkitiiɗi, kono ko gadani e keewɗi, ko noon ñaawoore fuɗɗorii e galle Alla.

Babiil Ceniiɗo, waktuuji ma ngarii, ñalɗi ma timmii. Miin, Iisaa Nasarankeejo, mi woyii gite keewɗe sabu bonannde maa nde arata, mi nelii maande to dow, e kaawniiɗe e nder asamaanuuji, e maande e leydi, haa e kulhuli keewɗi ina mbaɗa e sahaa gooto e hakkunde makko, kono Babiil Ceniiɗo ina fadda noppi mum e mbonannde mum, jooni noon omo ñaama haa o heewa laabi mum mbonanndeeji. Mbaɗe non, no mi renndinirta goonɗinɓe e kisal e njoɓdi, noon kadi bonannde jaawnde fawotoo e Babiil sirlu oon so gardiiɗo oon ittaama no haanirta nii. Nanee konnguɗi am, njanngon daande am, sabu gardiiɗo oon ko kañum tan reeni giɗli bonɗi ɗi liɓaaɓe ɓee, haa o reena haa o reena, o ittee e hakkunde.

Ndeen bonannde jaawnde fawotoo e aduna oo fof. Laabi konu ɗii njuɓɓinaama, ɓe njuɓɓinii wolde maɓɓe tataɓere, haa e wolde maɓɓe ngam waylude laabi ɓooyɗi e kuule ɓooyɗe. Nii woni, yiite fawotoo e Babiil Ceniiɗo e jaawngal, gure mum mbontinee. Yiite fawotoo e jaawngal so misilaaji ɗii njippii e leyɗeele mum, acca ɗe ina mbula, ina coola. Kono gure mum keewɗe e daande maayo mum bonɗe ina njalta e ndiyam ngam arde e jaawngal, ina njippoo e daande maayo mum, ina njippoo e nder leydi fotde kilomeeteeruuji keewɗi, ɗum waɗirta ko to fuɗnaange e to hirnaange. Leydi ndii ina dimbitoo e jaawngal, bonannde mawnde fawotoo e leyɗeele Babiil ceniiɗo, leyɗeele ɗee mbaylotoo, mbaylotoo. Laabi mum ina njalta, laabi mum ina keɓtoree, ladde e jeereende ina keddoo. Uurngol maayde ina sudda leyɗeele mum, ñawu e ñawuuji ina kebbina leyɗeele mum, omo wontude mo alaa ballal sam.

So ndee hareeji cakkitiiɗi mbaɗii, konuuji konu boɗeejo ɗii fof ina njaltinee e hoɗɓe heddiiɓe e leydi ndii. Ɓe mbara kala saliiɗo, ɓe mbara heewɓe e ɓe nsalataa. Sabu yimɓe Babiil ceniiɗo ɓee njoɓaama e maccungaagu sabu ñamaale mawɗe ɗe o waɗi, sabu ko e ñamaande ɓe liɓaaɓe ɓee ngollirta ngam bonnude yimɓe heewɓe. Hono no yimɓe mum njaɓiri, njoɓiri nguurndam mum en ngam heɓde jawdi e weltaare bonannde, noon kadi ɓeen ɓe cuɓii ngam maccinde ɓe, mbaɗata ɓe golle doole, maande ndee ina fawanee kala wuuriɗo, kono woto keɓee maande ndee.

Nii woni aduna oo jooni, ina waylee e jaawngal e mbonanndeeji e heddiiɓe e renndo ngo wuurnoo. Nii woni heege fawotoo e leyɗeele ɗee, sabu Babiil Ceniiɗo waɗii heege e leyɗeele keewɗe, jooni heege wontoyta ko hoɗo mum ñalnde wootere, sabu ko noon o rokkiri, ko noon kadi o heɓirta laabi ɗiɗi. Ndiyam wontoyta ko huunde ɗaɓɓirteende, ndiyam keewɗam ina kaɓɓa sabu bonannde e yiite konu e ñawuuji ɓe ubbitaaka ɗi mbonnata leydi ndii. Heewɓe ina njogotoo, ina maaya e ñaawoore sabu bonanndeeji mum en, so tawii noon ɗum wonaa ko haani e maɓɓe, kono ɓeen heddiiɓe e ñaawooɓe ɓee ina ñaawee, kono kala nodduɗo innde Joomiraawo mum haa timma ina hisnee, kono ina foti muñde haa ɓe puɗɗoo. Sabu koolondiral haa timma ina wonnoo ko haani wonande goonɗinɓe, haa e sukaaɓe Ibraahiima ɓe woni sukaaɓe alkawal, sabu peewal ina wuura e goonɗinal.

Nii woni Babiil ceniiɗo feccitirta haa laaɓa, ndiyam ina doga ɗo leydi yoorndi wonnoo, haa e leyɗeele hakkundeeje ina kebbina hakkunde. Nii woni yurmeende e moƴƴere Alla ina ittee e leydi ndii so gardiiɗo oon ittaama e jaawngal, bonannde ina resnda bonannde, sabu ɗii ko waktuuji bonannde ɗo laamu Seytaane ñaawee, bonnee haa laaɓa, kono gadano ɓe ngollanta laabi Yahweh e nder bonnugol Babiil sirlu oon, nii woni o wulirta, o bonnira e yiite keewɗe ɗe mbontata, puɗɗoo laɓɓinde leydi ndii. Nii woni bonannde Babiil Ceniiɗo waɗata ko e waktu gooto, sabu no winndiraa nii, noon waɗirta, o heɓata ko ñaawoore laabi ɗiɗi. No winndiraa nii, noon waɗirta.

Sabu winndaama gila e Yuhanna Gaynitiiɗo: Mi yi'i Malaa'ika goɗɗo ina jippo e asamaan, ina jogii baawɗe mawɗe, haa leydi ndii laaɓi e teddungal mum, O woyii e daande toownde, o wiyi, Nde yani, nde yani, Babiil wuro mawngo ngoo, ngo wonti hoɗorde jinneeji, e nokku kala ruuhu bonɗo, e kaɓirgal kala colli bonɗi e añam. Sabu leƴƴi kala njarrii e njaram tikkere jeenay makko, Laamɓe leydi ndii njaɓii jeenay e makko, njulaaɓe leydi ndii ngoni alɗuɓe e heewde weltaare makko.

Mi nani daande woɗnde ummoraade e asamaan, mi wiyi, Yaltu e makko, yimɓe am, woto on mbaɗdu e bonanndeeji makko, woto on keɓu e ñawuuji makko. Sabu bonanndeeji makko ngarii haa asamaan, Alla siftorii bonanndeeji makko. Njoɓdee mo, hono no o njoɓdiri on nii, ndokkee mo laabi ɗiɗi e golle makko: e nder koppi ɗi o hebbini on, kebbinee koppi makko laabi ɗiɗi. Hono no o teddiniri hoore makko, o wuuri e weltaare, noon ndokkee mo mette e sunaare: sabu o wiyi e nder ɓernde makko, Mi jooɗii miin laamɗo, mi wonaa jom suudu, mi yi'ataa sunaare. Ko ɗum waɗi, ñawuuji makko ngarata ko ñalnde wootere, maayde, e sunaare, e heege, o wulirta ko yiite: sabu Alla ñaawoowo mo, ko Joomiraawo semmbe!

Nii woni ndee leñol mawngol dowlaaji e gure keewɗe ina njippoo e jaawngal, malaa'ika bonannde oo ina rewi e wuro mum ɓurngo mawnude e nder ndiyam fuɗnaange, teddungal mum e weltaare mum e añam aduna ina bonnee haa abada. Nii woni kadi bonannde makko ina renndinee e kala naatɗo e makko, sabu ko kanko woni debbo jeenayanke mawɗo oon jooɗiiɗo e ndiyam keewɗam, woni yimɓe e yimɓe heewɓe e leƴƴi e ɗemɗe.

Sabu ñaawoore maɓɓe ina yahra yeeso, laamɓe leydi ndii fof naatii e luuɗe sappo ɗee e nder hareeji maɓɓe ɗi njiɗaa. Sabu ardiiɓe maɓɓe ina ngolloo e yiɗde ɓe liɓaaɓe ɓee, sabu ko noon ardiiɓe aduna fof ngoni. Alaa moƴƴo, alaa hay gooto. Ardiiɓe ɓee ngollii duuɓi keewɗi ngam addude ndee hareeji, ɓe njoɓii leƴƴi maɓɓe ɗi ɓe nelanoo ngam janfude. Yimɓe ɓe njaɓi rewrude e maɓɓe e weltaare ina njalta e yiite wolde, ñawu, maayde e bonannde. Kadi ɗee geɗe ina njuɓɓinee ngam yaltineede e jaawngal e leydi ndii. Nii woni leƴƴi Kerecee'en fof ina njalta e kaafaahi so ñaawoore fuɗɗii e galle Alla. Hono no wonnoo e ñalɗi ɓooyɗi ɗo Isra'iil acci Baaba annoore, o golli e allaaji goɗɗi, ɗi ngalaa allaaji, noon kadi ɓee ndokketee e juuɗe añɓe mum en, haa e leƴƴi sappo e ɗiɗi ɗii feccitaama, keɓtoraama gila e ɓooyma sabu mbonanndeeji maɓɓe e alkawal ngal waɗi sabu ñaawoore sariya wonande ɓe ngalaa ɗooftaare e ɓernde tiiɗnde.

Gila e mbonanndeeji wolde e bonannde timmunde, njuɓɓudi daaba cakkitiiɗo ndii ummotoo, sabu jawngol ina njuɓɓina njuɓɓudi daaba ngam ummaade, haa e ɓeen naatɓe e laamɓe sappo ɓe ndokkata baawɗe mum en fof e daaba ummoraade e maayo e daaba ummoraade e leydi, woni annabi mum en fenaande e Moshiach mum en fenaande. Daaba ummoraade e maayo ina ummoo no Mahdi mum en nii, omo rokkaa baawɗe haɓde e seniiɓe e heɓtude ɓe. Nii woni daaba ummoraade e leydi ina renndi baawɗe e daaba ummoraade e maayo, ko kanko woni Luggere Nde Daniyel annabi gila e ɓooyma.

Nii woni ɗii daabaaji ɗiɗi ina keɓa baawɗe timmuɗe e jawngol boɗewol mawngol ngol, ina ummoo haa laaɓa, ina keɓtora, ina yahra yeeso ngam heɓtude, ina mbiya: Hol ko nanndi e daaba? Hol baawɗo haɓde e daaba? Nii woni kala ina foti heɓde maande mum en, ɓe njaɓata naatde e renndo maɓɓe Seytaanankeewo. Jinnaaɗo ina sudda leydi ndii fof, sabu gardiiɗo oon wontataa toon ngam ñaawoyde neɗɗo e bonannde e reende giɗli mum e giɗli bonɗi. Nii woni bonannde timmunde, nde alaa ko reeni, ina foti yaltineede e leydi ndii, lugge ɗee ina udditee haa laaɓa, sabu hoɗɓe e leydi ndii njaɓii Yahweh, sabu ɓe njaɓii miin, Iisaa Nasarankeejo, neldaaɗo ngam hisnude ɓe. Kono ɓe njaɓii hoore maɓɓe ko ɓe ngalaa nafoore e nguurndam duumotooɗam, ɓe njaɓii Yahweh so Yahweh arii e maɓɓe e yiɗde, e neɗɗaagu, e yurmeende, e yurmeende, o rokkii neɗɗo nanondiral jam duumotoongal. Nanondiral jam ngal ɓe njaɓii jooni. Kono, kala nodduɗo innde Joomiraawo haa timma ina hisnee.

Hay so tawii waktuuji ñaawoore, bonannde mawnde, wolde, heege, ñawu e ko nanndi heen ina njaltinee e leydi ndii, ndee yontere cakkitere, haa e yontere cappanɗe jeeɗiɗi Daniyel, ina ndokkee ngam hisnude Yakuba, miin, Yeshua HaMashiach, mi hisnata heddiiɓe e Isra'iil ɓe njaɓata mi ko mi Masiya maɓɓe goonga, sabu ko noon mi tagiraa, mi waɗiraa, mi neldaa e Alla Baawɗo fof ngam teddinde alkawal e alkawal ngal waɗanaa Baabiraaɓe maɓɓe.

Sabu Yahweh ko Alla mawɗo duumotooɗo jooɗiiɗo to dow, jooni omo yaha ngam hollitde hoore makko e tagoore makko, miin, Almasiihu Iisaa, ko mi Joomiraawo Joomiraaɓe duumotooɗo e Laamɗo Laamɓe mo laamu mum alaa timmal. Mi nelata annabi'en am ɗiɗo, haa e fitirlaaji am ɗiɗi ɗi nganndinta, mi nelata 144,000 am ngam hisnude ɓeen ɓe neldaa e ladde e nokku mo Yahweh suɓii, e Yahweh ɓe nyaamata, ɓe ndeenetee. Kadi Alla duumotooɗo o wiyi ɗum gila e ɓooyma. Sabu ko Yahweh haalata geɗe ɗe ngalaa haa jooni, no mbaɗirnoo nii, sabu ko noon laabi Alla duumotooɗo, mo rokki mi Linjiila duumotooɗo ngam holliteede e leydi ndii, ngam hisnude duumotoonde ari e kala nodduɗo innde am mawnde, duumotoonde.

Miin Yeshua HaMashiach, haa e Almasiihu Iisaa, mi nelii ɗii konnguɗi e maccuɗo am, e maccuɗo am ɗii konnguɗi neliraa.

Miin woni Konngol Alla ngol wonti tergal, ngo hoɗi e mon.

Miin woni jooɗiiɗo e juuɗe am ina wula yiite laɓɓinirde.

Miin woni ittoowo ngoongɗinaani.

Miin woni ittoowo laabi mon ɗi ngalaa goonɗinal.

Miin woni laɓɓinoowo on haa laaɓa e kala sifaa mbonannde tergal.

Miin woni nodduɗo on e moƴƴere e goonga.

Mi darii e dow mon, mi rokkii on juuɗe ngam artude e koyɗe mon. On njaɓan rokkude am?

Nganndee wonde ko miin woni mo Alla mawɗo duumotooɗo, mo alaa ko foti e mum, rokki mi kala huunde e juuɗe am semmbe, o rokki mi innde dow kala innde, ngam e innde Iisaa kala ɓoggol ina foti ɓoggude e asamaan, e leydi, e les leydi. Miin woni Mbaroodi leñol Yahuuda, e peewal mi arata ngam ñaawoyde, ngam haɓde, ngam waɗde ñaawoore e liɓaaɓe, waktu ñaawoore oo arii. Nii woni mi wasiyoo hoɗɓe e leydi ndii yo njanngu konnguɗi Yahweh, njanngu Ɓiɗɗo, woto mi tikkanee, on maaya e laawol, so tikkere am wulii seeɗa.

Barkinaaɓe ngoni kala kooliiɗo mi, haa e Yeshua HaMashiach, e kala nodduɗo innde am haa timma ina hisnee, haa e kala ronooɓe kisal gila aduna oo sosaaka.
