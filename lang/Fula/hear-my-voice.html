Seedeeɓe ɗiɗo: Eklesiya
E nder Golle Yahya Baftistaajo
14 mars 2024

Nanee Daande Am!

Konngol Joomiraaɗo ngol ari e am. Nanee daande am, onon sukaaɓe am tokosooɓe, ngaree e juuɗe am peññaaɗe. Anndee wonde yiɗde am e mooɗon ndee fusaataa, tee heɓataake foolde, sabu ko yiɗde Baaba am e nder ɗuum, sibu o hokkii ɓiɓɓe makko e jikku makko, tee e nder am timmal ngal fof hoɗi. Mi innirii on inɗe mooɗon, mi noddii on ngam ndarooɗon yeeso am. Baali am ina nana daande am, no njanngirta daande gaynaako mum’en. On nanaali taw daande am? On udditii noppi mooɗon e daande am? Mi woyii e daande toownde e laabi e nder culɗi; mi yilloyii kadi kasooji mooɗon, mi addanii tooñaaɓe e yejjitaaɓe ɓee kisiyee.

Yo neɗɗo, hol ko waɗi a acci Joomiraaɗo maa? Hol ko waɗi a ɗuurnii laabi am? Kono, a wiyata, mi ɗuurnaaki laabi maa; ko miin woni ka Joomiraaɗo noddi. Goonga, mi noddii, on njaabii fotde sahaaji seeɗa, sahaaji sukaaɓe e weltaare mawnde. Woɓɓe e mooɗon nawaama to mboɗeeji ɗii koɗi, heewɓe e mooɗon kadi njuumtinaama e konnguɗi moƴƴi, haalaaji laaɓtuɗi, e demɗe fenaaje ɗe ɓooyataa dartineede.

Ciftoree yiɗde mooɗon adannde ndee, tuubee; tuubanee kam junuubaaji mooɗon, sibu miɗo heɓanii sahaa fof laɓɓinde junuubaaji mooɗon e nder ƴiiƴam am kiɓɓinoojam.

Nanee daande am, onon sukaaɓe am, sibu daandeji keewɗi ina nanndi, kadi Almasiihuuji fenaaɓe keewɗi ina etoo nanndinde daande am. Goonga, miɗo toppitii e miɗo ayna eklesiya am e junngo am, ngam mbaaw-mi hollirde on Alla on laaɓɓe no debbo laaɓɗo nii, on ɓoorniiɗo comci boɗeeji laaɓtuɗi, e tinuuji junuubaaji mooɗon njaafee, ittaa, yejjitaa, sibu mi werlii ɗi e nder lugge ɓurɗe luggiɗde, ngam ɗi ummitaaki abadan. Kono, heewɓe e mooɗon ina njuumtini kadi ina mbeltini aduna oo bonɗo. Ɓe njanii e bilkiiji mum en rewrude e caangu mboddi, kono faamee wonde hay so mboddi caangii on, hay lorra woota heɓataa on, sabu maande Baaba am ndee fusaataa. Tabbintinee hakkillaaji mooɗon e am, Ɓiɗɗo makko gooto jibinaaɗo, mo o hokki e yiɗde makko ngam kiɓɓinande junuubaaji mooɗon ngam en mbaawa hawtude, en ceertataa kadi abadan. Accam mi cellina on; accam mi cellina on haa timma, mi itta baɗule mooɗon ngam on mbaawa wonde celluɓe e timmuɓe yeeso am. Rewee laabi am, rewee Konngol am, sibu:

Ko miin woni Konngol ngol wonti ɓanndu, ngoɗoon hoɗi hakkunde mooɗon.

Ko miin woni mbuuru nguurndam, kala ka nyaami heen, weelataa kadi abadan.

Ko miin woni ka laamu mum wonaa e aduna oo, njoɓdi am ina wondi e am ngam koolaaɓe am e cuɓaaɓe am.

Ko miin woni ka yaha hakkunde lampaaji jeeɗiɗi ɗii, ka yamirta malaa'ikaaji jeeɗiɗi Eklesiya ɗii.

Ko miin woni Ceniiɗo Alla.

Ko miin woni daraniido mooɗon.

Ko miin woni ka fista ɓerɗe yimɓe.

Miɗo heɓanii sahaa fof laɓɓinde junuubaaji mooɗon e nder ƴiiƴam am kiɓɓinoojam.

Ko miin woni Ñaawoowo Nuunɗuɗo.

Ko miin woni Almasiihu ka anndinaa gila e fuɗɗoode.

Ko miin woni Ɓiɗɗo Alla ngarɗo e ɓanndu.

Ko miin woni ka hokki ƴiiƴam mum ɗam alaa junuuba ngam junuubankooɓe, ngam ɓe ndona nguurndam duumotooɗam.

Ko miin woni Ɓiɗɗo Alla ngarɗo e ɓanndu.

Ko miin woni Gaynaako Moƴƴo.

Ko miin woni ka ari e yimɓe mum, ka salaa e yimɓe mum.

Ko miin woni Almasiihu ka, ka Baaba am suɓii gila e cosgol aduna.

Ko miin woni Ka mi woni.

Sukaaɓe am, miɗo haalana on goonga wonde mi peññanii junngo am aduna maayoowo gila e Yubile keewɗe jooni, junngo am ngool peññataako sahaa fof e yurmeende e moƴƴere, hay so tawii yurmeende e moƴƴere ina ngondi e am sahaa fof. Mi noddii heewɓe, heewɓe kadi calii. Kono, ɓeen ɓe nodduno-mi, ɓe cuɓii-mi, ɓe ceni-mi, ɓe loot-mi, ɓe laɓɓin-mi, ɓe cellin-mi, ɓeen kamɓe ndaroto yeeso jooɗorgal ñaawoore am, ɓe njaɓɓoytoo njoɓdi mawndi ngam koolaaɗal mum en.

Hay so miɗo joganii on golle keewɗe ko timminde, hay so on mbaɗanii kam e innde am ko heewi, miɗo yiɗi ɓernde mooɗon timmunde ɓura golle mooɗon. Miɗo yiɗi ko ɗuurnorgol am e accugol junuubaaji mooɗon. Ngaree e am laaɓɓe, sibu Joomiraaɗo mooɗon ina heɓanii e junngo mum cellinoowo peññaango ngam mbaaw-mi laɓɓinde on no mbuneeje laaɓɗe nii. Hay so juuɗe am piɗaama, ƴiiƴam am kiɓɓinoojam njuurii e majje e ɓanndu am haa e toɓɓere sakkitiinde. Mi hokkii fof ngam on mbaawa wuurdude yeeso Baaba men haa abada.

Goonga, mi kiɓɓinaaki junuuba gooto tan; goonga, mi kiɓɓinaaki junuubaaji seeɗa tan; goonga, mi kiɓɓinaaki junuubaaji keewɗi, kono mi kiɓɓinanii junuubaaji fof e sahaa fof so on njaaboto kam tan nde njiɗ-mi ɓerɗe mooɗon rewrude e Ruuhu am baawɗo. On faamaani wonde mi alaa yiɗde ñaawoore on? Ina woodi gooto jogiiɗo ooɗoo yiɗde, omo yiɗi halkaare mooɗon, omo tuuma on jemma e ñalawma yeeso Baaba am, hay so tawii ko miin woni daraniiɗo on. O werlaama, o foolaama; kono, hol ko waɗi on mawnini gujjo oo e nokku toowɗo e nder ɓerɗe mooɗon so tawii miɗo jogii e dow makko e laamu makko ndamngu timmungu, laamu ngu ɓooyataa wontude meere? Deeƴee, deeƴee, ngaree e am e ɓernde lesɗunde. On anndaa wonde so on ñaawoyii koye mooɗon, on ñaaweetaake? Ňaagee kam mi holla on nguurndam mooɗon, sibu mi feeñananta on geɗe keewɗe ngam on mbaawa tuubande aybeeji mooɗon, on mbaɗee timmuɓe.

Woto kulee, onon sukaaɓe am tokosooɓe, sibu wonaa miin ñaawoowo on. Nanee kumpa mooɗon, woto njaɓee daande am e daliiluuji meere. Ooɗoo aduna ina foti werleede les, nde ooɗoo aduna waylii haa abada, onon on ɓamtete to kammuuli toowɗi, ngam mbaaw-mi hollirde on Baaba, ngam on mbaawa anndude ndonu mooɗon, keɓon njoɓdiiji koolaaɗal mooɗon. Miɗo yiɗi ñalawma mo njiidirten e kammuuli, ngam on mbaawa heɓde nguurndam mooɗon goonga, nde njaltu-ɗon ooɗoo nguurndam aduna bonɗam, junuubaaji, e bonanndeeji. Ko miin woni ka fista ɓerɗe yimɓe, wonaa ngam mi ñaawoo on, kono ko rewrude e yiite laɓɓinoowo, on mbaawa laɓɓineede e laabi mooɗon kiɗɗi, on annda laabi am yiɗde, annoore, weltaare, jam, muñal, e newaare.

Nanee feloore am, njaɓee jannginol am, sabu Baaba mooɗon ina hiisodoo e mooɗon no sukaaɓe nii, hol e sukaaɓe ɓe Baaba yiɗi ɓe felaaka, ɓe jannginaaka e nder ɗuum. Jannginol am ina addana on ɓiɓɓe jam nuunɗal, sibu jannginol am ko ngam laɓɓinde on timmal e junuubaaji mooɗon so on nanii tan konnguɗi am, on rewi daande am. Njaree, ndaree, kaalee konnguɗi nguurndam ɗii fof. Woɓɓe ina ngaroyoo e kisiyee e nder ooɗoo waktu sakkitiɗo, tee ko e innde Iisaa Almasiihu tan, innde am mawnde ɗo kisiyee woni, sabu alaa e innde woɗnde kisiyee hokkiraa. Innde am mawnde ndee ina darii tiiɗndi, ina fadi ngam on ngaddana kam ɓe, ngam mbaaw-mi hollirde tuubiiɗo oo Baaba no jibinannde hesere jibinaande e nguurndam duumotooɗam nii. Miɗo jogii yiɗde mawnde e mooɗon, tee no Baaba hokkiri kam nii, mi hokkorta koolaaɓe am e cuɓaaɓe am.

Ko miin woni Ɓiɗɗo Alla ngarɗo e ɓanndu, alaa junuuba, laaɓɗo haa abada. Hono noon, ko miin woni ka hokki ƴiiƴam mum ɗam alaa junuuba ngam junuubankooɓe ngam ɓe ndona nguurndam duumotooɗam. Onon, koolaaɓe am e cuɓaaɓe am, on jeyaaka e ronooɓe teddungal duumotoongal? Hol ko waɗi on ndarii e deƴƴere haa jooni no ko on njaakorii konnguɗi potɗi? Kaalee! Udditee kuɗɗe mooɗon, kaalee, sibu mi ardoto haala mooɗon ngam waajoraade bone ka noddaaɓe am njoofi haa jooni, heewɓe kadi njaaboto haa jooni. Naatdee e am e nder golle am e nder ooɗoo waktu sakkitiɗo hade am dimmbude lesɗe aduna, mi seeka ɗe e nokkuuji keewɗi, ñaawoore mawnde ina sudda leydi. Mi artiranta on ndee leydi e nder Laamu am garoowu, kono gadano laamu bonɓe nguu halkete, nawaa e ñaawoore ngam haɓɓeede e lugge, ɓe anndi kadi wonde weendu yiite welndu nde ɓe njooɗoto haa abada nduu ina fadi ɓe.

Mi halkirta jooɗorɗe laamu mum en fof; mi halkirta laamuuji mum en fof; mi wulirta ɗi haa laaɓa e yiite ngu nyifataa, no miin, Joomiraaɗo koolaaɗo, kaalanirno-mi on hade mum waɗde. E nder galle Baaba am, ina woodi cuuɗi keewɗi, teddungal e mawnikinaare mawnde ina fadi on. Ko ɗuum waɗi, woto hay gooto fena on e njoɓdi mooɗon, kooloree kam. Tabbintinee hakkillaaji mooɗon e am tan, Joomiraaɗo duumotooɗo, kadi Joomiraaɗo teddungal. Ko miin woni gaynaako moƴƴo; ko miin woni Almasiihu ka Baaba am suɓii gila e cosgol aduna. Ko miin woni ka ari e yimɓe mum, ka salaa e yimɓe mum, hay so tawii rewrude e jarribooji mawɗi, Yakuuba hisnirtee e junngo am baawngo. Gasgol geɗe fof ina ɓadii, timmal laamɗo ooɗoo aduna ina ɓadii. Hay so tawii o yaltineete fotde sahaaji seeɗa, ñaawoore makko duumotoonde ndee ina rewi, nde o waawataa hisde abadan, tee alaa safaara. Ngartanee kam, kaaldiree e am, gollanee kam, sibu Baaba am hokkii kam geɗe fof, njoɓdi am ngam mooɗon ina moƴƴinaa e kammuuli ngam koolaaɓe am e cuɓaaɓe am, tee ina goongɗi no maande Baaba am nde mi maandiniri on ndee nii. Heddii ko minitaaji seeɗa tan e yonta moƴƴere, ñaawoore am ndee arii e tampere no tikkere Jibinaaɗo ndee arii e leydi nii. Pree ɓerɗe mooɗon, pree koye mooɗon, sibu waktu oo arii.
