Seedeeɓe ɗiɗo: Isra'iila
Ko weli e ko haaɗi
3 Bowte, 2024

Ƴiiƴam Jeynanka Yaahwe

Ngar-ɗon to Joomiraawo mon duumotooɗo, nanee Konnguɗi am, onon yimɓe am, cujjonon e nder ɓerɗe mon e dow ɗooftaare Konnguɗi Yeesu Almasiihu. Sabu miin, Yeesu Almasiihu, mi waɗan neɗɗo hakkilante e nder mon, onon Isra’iila, o ɓura hakkilude so on ɗooftike yamirooje am, mbele on keɓa heen hakkil e ganndal ɓeydaangal, sabu hakkil e ganndal wonan en semmbe e jam e jamaanuuji mon, kadi wonan en doole kisal; kulol Joomiraawo ko ngalu mon.

Ko ɗum waɗi, paamon jamaanu mo ngon-ɗon oo, mbele njahdiron e peeje laaɓtuɗe, mbele peeje am kisinooje mbaawa timmude e mooɗon, onon noddaaɓe e innde am, hay onon Isra'iila, hay so tawii hannde on anndaa kam, on nguuraani kadi e laabi am, sabu Iisaawu e Askenaas laamiima Isra'iila, tawi ko winndaa laaɓtii wonde mawɗo oo rewan minñiiko, kadi Iisaawu e Askenaas fof alaa hakke e ndonu Yaaquuba. Kono tan, Yaaquuba salii Yeesu Almasiihu, mi waylitii to leƴƴi goɗɗi e nder dumunna gooto ngam timminde ko Isra'iila meeɗaa ɗooftaade haa timma, woni gila e Isra'iila leƴƴi goɗɗi barkintee, e nder leƴƴi majjuɗi ɗi, heddiiɓe kisiniree.

Kono non, so on mbaylitii ɓerɗe mon e nder ɗooftaare e daande Konnguɗi yamirooje Almasiihu mon goongaajo oo, on ngartiree leñol keeriiɗi to Yaahwe, hay so tawii baabiraaɓe mon mbaylitii e Yaahwe gila ko ɓooyi. Eey, ɓe mbaylitii e Yaahwe nde ɓe calii kam, Yeesu mo Naasaret, woni kadi Yeesu Almasiihu, hay e jamaanu mo Pontiyos Pilaat laaminoo Yahuuda, nde hooreeɓe maɓɓe diine e Sanhedrin njaltini kam e juuɗe worɓe bonɓe ngam waɗde geɗe ɗe sariya haɗnoo ɓe laaɓtii, kono e nder añde ɓe kam e kala geɗe jowitiiɗe e Yaahwe, ɓe mbaɗi ɗum e weltaare e añde mawnde e ñaawoore. Ko noon kisal Aadama timminirtee e juuɗe añɓe mum, haa kala goongɗinɗo, noddoowo innde Yeesu mo Naasaret, woni kadi Yeesu Almasiihu, kisintee haa laaɓa.

Ko noon woniri sabu ko miin woni jeynanka Yaahwe, hol no kafaraandu ngam boneeji aadee en mbaawirta waɗeede so wonaa nde ƴiiƴam am laaɓɗam ɗam ruttee e sadaka ngam yoɓde coggu ngal goonga alaa njoɓdi. Faamee wonde, hay so tawii neɗɗo aduna oo ina manti e mawnikinaare wonde ko kanko tagi hoore makko, o hebbanii hoore makko e kala fannu, neɗɗo aduna oo yanii e ngonka bonka e jikku bonɗo, mo fotaani, mo heɓi ko foti heɓde.

Kono, e nder yurmeende am e moƴƴere am e sadaka am e hoore am to Golgota, nde mi hollitii hoore am to Yaahwe ko mi jeynanka Alla, nde mi hoki hoore am e nder mette mawɗe haa toɓɓere am cakkitiinde ƴiiƴam ɗam yani e Golgota, haa e fofngo am cakkitiingo nde mi haali konnguɗi "timmiima". Mi ubbanooma, mi ummii ñalnde tataɓere hono no binndi ɗi mbiyri; hono no Yaahwe fodinoo o waɗata, sabu maayde waawaano jogaade ƴiiƴam laaɓɗam. Yaahwe immini kam, ɗo, seeɗa caggal ɗum, mi naati e nokku ɓurɗo laaɓde e kala nokku laaɓɗo e nder kammuuji, ɗo mi hollitii hoore am ko mi mawɗo almuudo haa abada to Yaahwe, Jom Baawɗe on, sadaka mo o jaɓi haa abada. Ndeke, so on nganndii on njogii mawɗo almuudo mawɗo hono oo, naatɗo kammu, hono Yeesu, ɓiy Yaahwe, njogo-ɗen seedantaagal men. Sabu on alaa mawɗo almuudo, onon Isra'iila, mo yurmeende mon waawaa memde, kono o jarribaa e kala fannu no hono noon, kono o bonnaani.

Ndeen mi naamndii, onon Isra'iila, ko woni ko timmi? Mi jaabotoo no laaɓiri: Aadi am keso oo, woni aadi duumotooɗo, kadi Alkawal am Keso! Sabu Alkawal ngal tabitintee so worɓe maayii: sabu ngal alaa doole so tawii neɗɗo waɗɗo ngal oo ina wuuri; kadi fotde kala huunde e dow sariya laɓɓinirtee ko ƴiiƴam, so ƴiiƴam ruttaaka, alaa yaafuya bone. No Alkawal kiingal waynoo nii, nde Muusaa haalannoo yimɓe ɓe kala yamiroore, e fawaade e Sariya, o ƴetti ƴiiƴam ƴe'i e mbeewa, e ndiyam e wutte boɗeejo e hisopo, o fisti deftere ndee e yimɓe ɓe fof, o wiyi, "Ndee ko ƴiiƴam alkawal ngal Alla yamiri on."

Kono, alkawal kiingal, hay alkawal Muusaa kiingal, meeɗaano wonde joofnirde peeje Yaahwe kisinooje aadee, Isra'iila meeɗaano kadi hawritde e mawnikinaare, taga pelle ceertuɗe e ceerndagol keewngol ngol haanataa, ngol warooɓe. Kono e nokku mum, Isra'iila fotaani wonde maande kisal to jentil en, mbele leƴƴi ɗi njiya wonde Alla ina woodi e Isra'iila, mbele leƴƴi ɗi naata e Isra'iila ngam janngude ko fayti e Yaahwe hono no winndiranoo e jamaanuuji ɓooyɗi.

Ko ɗum waɗi, paamon sifaa wakkatiijo Muusaa no Isra'iila gollirnoo ɗum e jamaanuuji hade Alkawal am Keso, paamon kadi wonde Muusaa ina wuuri haa timmi e nder Almasiihu mon goongaajo, hono laaɓɗo Alla oo, guurɗo no haanirta nii e fawaade e Muusaa, rokkuɗo nguurndam mum laawol gootol, fof, e haa abada ngam yoɓde njoɓdi Muusaa e dow mooɗon. No winndiraa nii, ko ɗum waɗi, paamon Alkawal am Keso, onon Isra'iila: Sabu Sariya oo ina jogii natal geɗe moƴƴe garooje, wonaa natal tigi-rigi geɗe ɗe, waawaa hay gooto e sadakaaji ɗi ɓe ndokkata hitaande kala, laɓɓinde garooɓe ɓe. Sabu so wonaano ɗum, ɓe accatno haa abada waɗeede, sabu garooɓe ɓe, so laɓɓinaama laawol gootol, ɓe mbaɗataa kadi ɓernde bone? Kono e nder ɗeen sadakaaji, ina woodi ciftorgol boneeji hitaande kala.

Sabu waawaa wonde ƴiiƴam ƴe'i e mbeewa itta boneeji. Ko noon woniri, yimɓe leydi am, Muusaa waawnoo tan ko laɓɓinde boneeji e wakkati, kono boneeji keewɗi e les Muusaa ina keddii, ɗo waɗooɓe ɗum en njoopitortee e yimɓe ɓe haa abada sabu boneeji maɓɓe. Ko ɗum waɗi, Muusaa ko natal geɗe garooje, geɗe ɗe timminii haa timmi e nder sadaka am e hoore am, sabu ƴiiƴam am laaɓɗam ɗam ina laɓɓina boneeji fof, laawol gootol, fof e haa abada, haa kala mo iwdi e Aadama waawa heɓde kisal heen. Kono wonande jaɓooɓe maande dabba oo, yaafuya meeɗaa heɓde ɓe.

Ko noon winndiraa e Yeesu Almasiihu, wonde e nguurndam am e ɗooftaare am Aadama waawaa artireede to Elohim, tagɗo mo duumotooɗo e laaɓɗo. Ko ɗum waɗi, nde o ari e aduna, o wiyi, sadaka e dokkal a yiɗaano: kono ɓanndu a waɗanii kam. E sadakaaji ɗi mbelnataa, e sadakaaji bone, a weltinaaka. Ndeen mi wiyi, Ndaa mi ari (E fuɗɗoode deftere ndee ko winndaa e am) ngam waɗde yiɗde maa, an Alla. To dow, nde o wiyi, Sadaka e dokkal, e sadakaaji ɗi mbelnataa, e sadakaaji bone a yiɗaano, a weltinaaka heen (ɗi ndokketee e dow Sariya) Ndeen o wiyi, Ndaa mi ari ngam waɗde yiɗde maa, an Alla, o itti gadano oo, ngam o tabitina ɗiɗaɓo oo. E dow yiɗde nde, en laɓɓinaama, kadi e dokkal ɓanndu Yeesu Almasiihu laawol gootol. Kala Almuudo Levi ina darii ñalnde kala ina gollina, ina heewi waɗde sadaka gooto, mo meeɗaa ittude boneeji: Kono oo neɗɗo caggal nde o waɗi sadaka gooto ngam boneeji, o jooɗii haa abada to nyaamo Alla, Gila ndeen o ina heddii, haa añɓe makko mbaɗee leydi koyɗe makko. Sabu e sadaka gooto o laɓɓinii haa abada laɓɓinaaɓe ɓe.

Sabu Yaahwe kadi ina seedanii en: sabu caggal nde o wiyi hade mum, Ndee ko Alkawal ngal mi waɗanta ɓe caggal ɗeen ñalɗi, wiyi Elohim, mi waɗan sariyaaji am e nder ɓerɗe maɓɓe, e nder hakkillaaji maɓɓe mi winnda ɗum en. Boneeji maɓɓe e bonanndeeji maɓɓe mi siftorataa kadi. Jooni, ɗo yaafuya geɗe ɗee woni, alaa kadi sadaka ngam bone. On ndarii kadi e jamaanu majjere ngenndi, ɗo baabiraaɓe mon gila ko ɓooyi calii on mo woni Jeynanka Paska laaɓɗo Yaahwe e hoore mum, hay ɗo Yaahwe yiɗiri aduna haa o rokki ɓiyiiko gooto taa, haa kala goongɗinɗo innde Yeesu Almasiihu, hay Yeesu Almasiihu oo, oo neɗɗo kisintee haa laaɓa. Ko ɗum waɗi mi noddi on yo on mbaylito e majjere mon e wumre mon feccere, onon Isra'iila, hay so tawii ko e salaade e salagol haa timmi hakkil bonɗo Iisaawu wonɗo e Talmud mum loʻɗɗo, ngarton to Konnguɗi laaɓɗi Yaahwe, hay konnguɗi ɗi ndesaa e nder diidi Binndi Bizantiin, hay Textus Receptus no ndesiraa e binndol Stephens nii.

Ko ɗum waɗi, paamon ko salaade Yeesu Almasiihu firtata e mon haa abada, onon Isra'iila, mbele paamon tiiɗnaare ndee salaare. Sabu so on mbonnii e yiɗde caggal nde keɓ-ɗon, nganndu-ɗon ndee goonga, sadaka ngam boneeji heddotaako, kono ko ɗaminaare kulniinde ñaawoore, e yiite tiiɗngo, ngo halkata añɓe. Neɗɗo ñiŋɗo Sariya Muusaa, maayii tawa yurmeende alaa e les seedeeɓe ɗiɗo, walla tato: Njoɓdi ɓurndi tiiɗde no feewi, hol no miijor-ɗon on fotata, onon njiiɓooɓe Ɓiy Alla, on njogorii ƴiiƴam Alkawal Keso ko huunde nde laaɓaani. Ko ɗum waɗi, njaɓee sadaka am, tuubanee kam boneeji mon mbele mi yaafoo on, sabu miin ko mi koolaaɗo, mi laaɓɗo ngam yaafaade on boneeji mon, mbele laɓɓinee e nder ƴiiƴam am laɓɓinoojam, tuubanee goɗɗo e goɗɗo.

Ko ɗum waɗi, mi ari e mooɗon e yurmeende am e moƴƴere am ngam ardude on e wumre mon feccere, onon Isra'iila, hay mbele kisal heɓee e dow ɗooftaare mon e daande am, mbele njaltaton e salaare baabiraaɓe mon nde woni e Talmud mon loʻɗɗo haa abada, e diine mon loʻɗɗo mo Iisaawu waɗani on ngam halkude on, kono mo njogorii-ɗon wa si ko hakkil makko ko Yaahwe e hoore mum hokki, wonaa Seytaane. Onon Isra'iila, nanee daande am kisinoore, njaɓee yiɗde am e mooɗon, ngaree e am e ɓerɗe mon udditiiɗe, keɓɗe ngam keɓde, mbele kebbinee kala teddungal e moƴƴere.

Mi rokkii on faamde yaafuya timmungo e artirgol ngol ƴiiƴam am laɓɓinoojam addata e kala jaɓɗo kam ko mi Joomiraawo mum kisinoowo, kadi ko mi Almasiihu mum goongaajo, sabu ko noon mi woniri. Hono no Aadama tagiraa, tagiraa, waɗiraa tawa bonnaani, e timmal haa ñalnde o bonni e yiɗde, noon kadi yoɓde bone Aadama naamnii ƴiiƴam laaɓɗam, hay naamnal ngal waawaa wonde ngam kala jooɗiiɗo e leydi e nder bonannde. E nder yiɗde Yaahwe o rokki ndee timmal e ƴiiƴam laaɓɗam e nder ɓanndu ɓiyiiko gooto taa, rokkaaɗo e sadaka ngam boneeji Aadama, ko ɗum waɗi sadaka ngam bone heddotaako.

Ko miin rokki hoore am haa timmi ngam boneeji Aadama yaniiɗo.

Ko miin rokki hoore am haa timmi e kafaraandu duumotoondu haa kala goongɗinɗo kisal duumotoongal laɓɓinee heen.

Ko miin woni Mawɗo Mon Almuudo haa abada e fawaade e Laamu Malkisada, hay on rokkuɗo hoore mum ko Paska timmuɗo laawol gootol, fof, e haa abada.

Ko miin Yeesu Almasiihu, hay on woni Almasiihu mon goongaajo, laamu am alaa joofnirde.

Ko miin Almasiihu Yeesu, mi neldii Konnguɗi am e juuɗe nelaaɗo am e golloowo am, e juuɗe nelaaɗo am e golloowo am Konnguɗi am nelaama.

Ko ɗum waɗi, paamon timmal kala huunde arii, onon Isra'iila, mi noddii on e am e laawol Linjiila Duumotooɗo, rokkaaɗo e jamaanuuji peeñɗi, e jamaanuuji peeñɗi ko jooni. Sabu gila e fuɗɗoode Yaahwe haalani mbaroodi ndii e nder ngesa e jamaanuuji ɗo bone naati e aduna, mi waɗan añde hakkunde maa e debbo oo, e hakkunde jibinannde maa e jibinannde mum, a fiyay teppere makko, kanko o fiyay hoore maa.

Ko noon e nder wakkati, Daawuuda, nde o wonnoo suka gaynaako, laamɗo Isra'iila hade laamu nguu feccude; o woni kadi neɗɗo e fawaade e ɓernde Yaahwe, o reeni Isra'iila, o wari oo mawɗo Filistiin, hono Goliyaat mo Gat, mo wonnoo taar iwdi e Refayim! Ko noon Daawuuda suka gaynaako oo yahi haɓde e Goliyaat mo Gat hono gaynaako, e sawru, e mbaggu e fiyrude, o yahata haɓde e mawɗo konu, kono Daawuuda yahi haɓde e innde Yaahwe, mo Filistiin en njippini, Daawuuda darii ngam haɓde e añɓe Isra'iila mbele konu Elohim heɓa poolgu.

No kaaƴe ummoraade e fiyrude Daawuuda ɗee njaniri e oo mawɗo bonɗo oo haa o yani e leydi ina yirla, Daawuuda fusi kaafaahi oo mawɗo, o taƴi hoore mum. Hono no winndiraa e jamaanu ɓooyɗo: Daawuuda ƴetti hoore Filistiin oo, addi ɗum to Yerusalaam, kono o waɗi kaɓirɗe makko e nder layma makko. Ko noon Daawuuda ubbiri hoore Goliyaat mo Gat yaasi maadi wuro Yerusalaam. Kono, e nder sadaka am e hoore am ko mi jeynanka Alla, mi yaltinaama yaasi wuro ngo, toon to Golgota mi fasaama, hay nokku hoore oo. Nde mi fasaa, ɓe ndiiwnii kam e ndee lekki tiiɗki, ɗo ɓe ndiiwnii teppere am e diiwnorɗe maɓɓe nde ɓe ndiiwnii kam e ndee lekki tiiɗki. Ko noon poolgu heɓaa toon ngam Aadama nde teppere am fiyee toon to Kalvaari, ɗo hoore Seytaane kadi fiyee no ƴiiƴam am njilliri nii, addi poolgu e dow iwdi mbaroodi ndii haa abada. Ko ɗum waɗi, no suka gaynaako fooliri iwdi Seytaane e hoore mum, noon kadi mawɗo gaynaako oo fooliri Seytaane haa abada e sadaka am yiɗde mo mbaɗmi ngam boneeji Aadama.

Kono, no mi maayrata e mette mawɗe hono bonɗo (ɗo mi laaɓi e bonannde), yaasi damal wuro ngo laaɓngo ngoo, ƴiiƴam am laaɓɗam ɗam njalti e ɓanndu am fof, holliri neɗɗo bonɗo leydi boɗeeri arii e kisal timmungal. Nde ƴiiƴam am looti leydi boɗeeri to nokku hoore to kalvaari, hoore mbaroodi ndii fiyee haa abada, e nder timmal kala huunde, oo mbaroodi mawɗo e hoore mum haɓɓete, werlee haa abada e yiite njoɓdi, ɗo liɗɗi maɓɓe maayataa, sabu ko ɗum woni ndonu makko.

Ko miin garoowo ngam halkude laamu añɓe haa laaɓa, mbele mbaroodi ndii haɓɓee, uddiree e nder luggeere duuɓi ujunere, mbele laamu am laaɓngu e leydi tabitinee. E joofnirde ɗum, o yaltintee e dumunna makko tokooso, ɗo o mooɓtata murto makko cakkitiingo. Nde ɓe njippii e nokku laaɓɓe, yiite jippotoo e Yaahwe e hoore mum, halka ɓe haa laaɓa, e oon sahaa Seytaane werletee e yiite duumotooɗe, ina wula haa abada e ɓuuɓri loʻndi.

Ko ɗum waɗi, mi rokkii on faamde, onon Isra'iila, njoɓdi Muusaa yoɓaama haa timmi e sadaka Yeesu Almasiihu, woni Yeesu Almasiihu e ɗemngal Engele, hay mbele paamon timmal sadaka am e timmal ngal heɓaa e ƴiiƴam am laɓɓinoojam duumotooɗam, ɗam ruttaa, ndokkaama ngam boneeji Aadama. Faamee haa timmi, onon Isra'iila, so tawii yanɓe ɓe paamnoo sirlu Yaahwe, ɓe meeɗaano faddaade Joomiraawo mon teddungal! Sabu e faddaade am ɗo mbaroodi ndii fiyi teppere am e wakkati, hoore mbaroodi ndii fiyii haa abada, hay fiyngo ngo meeɗaa cellude.

Ko ɗum waɗi, onon Isra'iila, nanee Konnguɗi am, paamon yiɗde am e mooɗon, e wonde mi noddii on e ndee wakkati cakkitiindi. Ndeen paamon haala am sabu ko winndaa: Alla jam, mo immini e maayɓe Yeesu Almasiihu, mawɗo gaynaako baali, e laawol ƴiiƴam alkawal duumotoongal, waɗa on timmuɓe e kala golle moƴƴe, ngam waɗde yiɗde mum, ina gollina e mooɗon ko weli e yeeso mum e laawol Yeesu Almasiihu, mo teddungal woni haa abada e abadin, Aamiin.
