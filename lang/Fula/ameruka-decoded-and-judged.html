Nanee Konnguɗi Yeshua HaMashiach, mo woni kadi Almasiihu Iisaa, paamon konngol am ngam paamon fuɗɗoode goonga, e jikku, e anniya Babilon Sirri. Hay ƴiiƴam ɗam loowi leydi makko ɗo leydi ndii woyata ngam adalankaagal ngam ƴiiƴam ilɗam, ɗam ilata haa jooni e heewde. Hay leyɗeele ɗeeɗoo mbonɗe ɗe woyata no feewi e asamaan ngam adalankaagal e ndeeɗoo sahaa, hay so leydi ndii ina diwna no feewi no neɗɗo sakuɗo cokli faɗde, hay so ina ɗaɓɓa ñaawoore nuunɗunde ndi ustata hay seeɗa.

An, Babilon Sirri, hay an: Aɗa woni duunde nde alaa laaɓal, nde tunni, hay leydi ndi yimɓe hoɗi e mum gila e balɗe Nuuhu, leydi ndi halfinaa Heylel Ben Shachar e laabi mboodi, leydi ndi loowi ƴiiƴam ɓe alaa aybe e laabi e golle ɓiɓɓe leƴƴi mbonɗi e tunnooɓe yahnooɓe tunnaade e jamaanuuji ɓooyɗi. Hay leydi ndi reenaa e cuccu e juuɗe suuɗiiɗe ɓe yani, hay leydi ndi reenaa, ndi anniyanaa wonde Atlantis maɓɓe keso, hay ngenndi ndi tagiraa, ndi halfinaa ngam ummital mbeewa ummoraade e maayo e mbeewa ummoraade e leydi. Hay ngenndi ndi waɗata hoore mum ko Kerecee'en, kono e goonga ko ngenndi setanisteeɓe sirri ɓe cosi, ɓe njooɗii e wellitaare e nder juɓɓule mum tunnduɗe e tunnooje (gila e ɓurɗe famɗude haa e ɓurɗe mawnude) e ko ɗeeɗoo juɓɓule laaminooɗe e pelle sirri ɗe koye mum en halfinaa Heylel Ben Shachar (mo ɓe innirta e majjere e majjere Lusifer), hay ɓe cikkata e majjere koye mum en ko jalbooje e annduɓe, hay e nder majjere maɓɓe e fenaande ɗo ɓe koye mum en haɓɓaa e alkule maɓɓe ɗe ngalaa laaɓal haa abada, ina njiiloo, kono e nder ɗuum fof ina njiiloo ɓe ɓe haɓɓaa e mum en haa abada no ɓiɓɓe aybeeji ɓe yani haa abada.

Yo anndee, Babilon Sirri ko Dental Dowlaaji Amerik, hay so ina anndiranoo Ameru-Ka, Leydi Mboodi Duuɗi, ndi rewi, ndi waylii hoore mum e ndeen debbo jeenoowo mawɗo jooɗiiɗo e ndiyam keewɗam, ko ngenndi ndi waɗiraa, ngam mbeewa ummoraade e maayo e mbeewa ummoraade e leydi ummoo e dottaaje mum e jamaanuuji mum en feeñde. Hay ngenndi jeenoori, hay ngenndi jeenoori, ngenndi ndi woni goonga jeenoowo mawɗo ina wiya hoore mum ko rewoowo nuunɗuɗo Iisaa Nazaareen; kono e goonga ko ngenndi ndi mahooɓe mum en ngalaa e Hayre Fondasiyoŋ goonga, hay ndeen hayre mawnde ndi ɓe ngalaa goonɗinal ɓe ŋakkata e mum, hay Iisaa Nazaareen mo woni kadi Yeshua HaMashiach.

Goonɗin, AmeruKa laatiima ndee Dental Dowlaaji jooni, ko ngenndi ndi cosiraa e juuɗe maɓɓe ɗe ngalaa laaɓal, ɗe cuuɗii, hay leydi pelle sirri halfinaaɗe mboodi e nder geɗe cuuɗiiɗe; e no doole Rosy Cross naatiri AmeruKa, noon kadi laabi maɓɓe cakkitiiɗi ɗi Atlantis Keso ɗii cosi. Nii woni aɗa woni AmeruKa: Sabu miɗo haalda e duunde ndi gila e worgo haa e funnaange, gila e fuɗnaange haa e hirnaange, ko leydi ndi halfinaa ndeen mboodi duuɗi ɓooyndi ndi tunni, ndi tunni ɗeeɗoo leyɗeele ngam anniyaaji mum, hay so laamuuji ummii, yani e keeri mum, hay laamuuji keewɗi ɗi mboodi ɗi mbinndaaka, hay ngenndi mboodi duuɗi, diwndi, ndi halfinaa pelle matriarkal e demokaraasi ɗo pelle bonɗe mbaawi laamaade e les njiimaandi Heylel Ben Shachar. Yanti heen, leñol mum mbonngol tunni leyɗeele Babilon sirri no feewi, ɗum woni leydi ndi yimɓe hoɗi e mum no neɗɗo yahrunoo e leydi ndii gila ɗo Laana ndii darii e dow hooseere Ararat gila e oon sahaa. Nii worɓe ngari e leydi mboodi duuɗi ummoraade e hirnaange, ina njaha fuɗnaange rewrude e pont leydi Bering no anndiraa hannde, ɓe ngari ummoraade e maayo gila fuɗnaange haa hirnaange rewrude e Atlantik, rewrude e laabi keewɗi ceertuɗi ɗi njulaagu e yah-ngartaa ɓe ngari e ɗeeɗoo leyɗeele ɗe Heylel Ben Shachar reeni ngam anniyaaji mum bonɗi. Hay leydi ndi ɓe yani ɓe ndokki timmunde e winndere ndee oon sahaa, hay leydi ndi ɓe ubbii sirluuji keewɗi ɗi ɓe njoɓetee no feewi, ɗi njoɓetaake ngam heɓtude, hay so o haɗoowo am umminaama, hay so laamu maɓɓe juutngu ina accaa ngam hisnude Yaaquuba, kadi ngam ɓe mbaawa faɗde e ñaawoore yiite koye mum en no ndeeɗoo haala jokkiri gila e balɗe Nuuhu, ngam ɓe mbaawa halkireede no feewi, sabu jamaanuuji ɓe yani ɗii ngari timmoode mum en, ko miin Almasiihu Iisaa, hay Muudo Leñol Yahuuda, mo firtata hoore ndeeɗoo mboodi ɓooyndi e ñaawoore cakkitiiɗo.

Nii woni Kanan tawi ko reenooɓe ɓee ubbunoo, o ardiraa e yiɗde ɓernde makko nde o naati, o wujji leydi ndi wonaano makko ngam hoɗde e mum e bonnude alkule Nuuhu. Kono o yiɗnoo balɗe ɓooyɗe ɗe halkiranoo e ndeen ilam mawɗam ɗo winndere ndee oon sahaa halkiri sabu Setan en ina cemmbina reenooɓe yaniiɓe ɓee ngam hawrude e rewɓe ngam jibinde iwdi maɓɓe laanaandi. Hay iwdi ndi wonaa goɗɗum so wonaa ɓiɓɓe jeenooji e bonɓe, hay nanndingol nguurndam ɗo bonanndeeji maɓɓe ina kaɓɓaa haa abada ngam waɗde yiɗde bonɗe e bonɗe baabiraaɓe maɓɓe, e waɗde yiɗde baabiraaɓe maɓɓe ɓe kaɓɓaa haa abada. Ko ɗum waɗi, paamon wonde ɗeeɗoo geɗe bonɗe, ɗe ngoni bonɗe, bonɗe haa laaɓi, ngalaa ɗaɗol, ngalaa calɗi e asamaan, e leydi, hay e les leydi, ɗe ndoƴetee no feewi, haa abada, e les naange e fawaade e alkule alkule Adonai-YHVH Miin Mawɗo! Ɓe kaɓɓetee, ɓe wulee e ɗeen yiiteeji mawɗi ɗi njoɓdi ɗo ñorndi maɓɓe maayataa, kala werlaaɗo e yiiteeji ɗii ina heɓa aybeeji mum en laabi keewɗi e nder ɓerɗe mum en e ñaawoore yiite welnde haa abada, ndi ɓe ndogataa hay abada.

Nii woni, Heylel Ben Shachar, hay oon laamɗo tokooso foolaaɗo, hay oon mawninoowo mawɗo e tunnoowo kala ko laaɓi, yahi aawde geɗe mum e nder gese Yahweh, Miin Mawɗo, hay oon gese gooto ɗe aawaa nde Elohim wiyi yo annoore won, annoore woni. Sabu e fuɗɗoode, Elohim waɗi asamaan e leydi, leydi ndii wonti alaa mbaadi, ndi woni meere nde niɓre ari e yeeso lugge. Ndeen niɓre ko Heylel Ben Shachar e tataɓal malaa'ikaaɓe mum ɓe rewi e murto makko, ɓe werlaa e leydi e semteende mawnde, e foolde. Ko Heylel Ben Shachar, hay oon laamɗo tokooso foolaaɗo mo wulli e semteende e foolde nde o werlaa e leydi ummoraade e asamaan asamaan'en e tikkere mawnde, hay o reenaa ngam ñaawoore cakkitiiɗo e yiite e nder yiiteeji ñalawma mawɗo oo, hay yiiteeji ɗi ngoni ɓurɗi wulde e laaɓde e yiiteeji bulooji ɗi keblaa ngam jaɓɓaade kala naatɗo e murto Setan en gila e ɓurɗo famɗude haa e ɓurɗo mawnude e nder laamu ɓe yani.

Elohim wiyi "Yo annoore won, annoore woni!" Ndeen Elohim tabitini, leydi tabitinaandi e yamiraare makko tan, e Konnguɗi makko tan, e wasiya makko tan tagngo ngoo tagiraa, waɗiraa, tagaa. Nii leydi ndii waɗiraa e kala ko woni e mum, ndeen leydi ndokkaa Aadama, sabu neɗɗo waɗiraa ngam leydi, leydi ndii waɗiraa ngam neɗɗo, mo goonga tagaa e natal tagɗo mum, hay YHVH - Miin Mawɗo!

Kono ndeen mboodi annduɗi ndii yani e ndonu Aadama e peeje mum, hay yani e bonannde ndi timminaa timmunde e Kalvaari, nde miin, Iisaa Nazaareen, mbiyi konnguɗi duumotooɗi, "Timmii!"; nii woni, mi soodi neɗɗaagu, leydi, e kala ko woni e mum e nder ƴiiƴam am hisnoojam. E fawaade e ɗuum, leydi ndii ko Joomiraawo e timmal mum, miɗo ara no Muudo Leñol Yahuuda ngam ñaawoore e haɓde, nanee wullaango am, onon hoɗɓe e leydi, sabu jamaanuuji mon ngari!
