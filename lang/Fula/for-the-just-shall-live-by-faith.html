Seedeeji Ɗiɗi: Eklesiya
E nder Golle Poola Nelaaɗo
27 abriil 2024

Sabu dartiiɗo oo e iimanaaku wuuri!

Sabu dartiiɗo oo e iimanaaku wuuri! Fin, finndin miññiraaɓe mon, onon eklesiya am Lawdisiya mo yurmeende mum uumi, mo yejjiti sirruuji am; sabu waktu oo feewii, sahaaji ɗii bo ɓadake timmude. Ngaron e am, onon ɓiɓɓe am, e juuɗe udditiiɗe e ɓernde udditiinde (sabu juuɗe am e ɓernde am ina udditanii on); ngaron e am e ɓernde newiinde, heblaniinde jaɓde konnguɗi am. Sabu hol ko miñi am Yaaquuba wi'i? Ko ɗuum waɗi, ngoppee kala tuundi e kala ko ɓuri heewde e bonannde, njaɓiree e newaare konngol aawngol ngol, ngol baawngol hisnude yonkiiji mon. Onon ɓiɓɓe am, laaree mi, sabu miɗo newii, miɗo joltii e ɓernde, njaɓiree e newaare Konngol aawngol ngol, ngol mbaɗ-mi e hakkunde mon, mbele mbaawee yiyrude gite e nanirde noppi. Ngartiree ɓerɗe mon e am, sabu ko miin woni laawol, goonga, e nguurndam, hay gooto araani e Baaba so wonaa e am, sabu ko noon yiɗde Baaba am feeñiri e laaɓal, o waɗi ɗum noon.

Ko waɗi ngoppaton ɓerɗe mon ina njonka, onon ɓiɓɓe am? Ko waɗi haa jooni ngoppaton konnguɗi am, njowon heɗɓe e majjere mon? Sabu onon, eklesiya am Lawdisiya, on njejjitii hol ko ngonɗon e am, Almasiihu Yeesu, on njuumtii sirruuji am, hay so tawii ko miin halfini on ɗi. Sabu ko miin woni Joomiraawo soodirɗo on yiiƴam am; ko miin woni Joomiraawo Joomiraaɓe e Laamɗo Laamɓe ronɗo leydi ndii e Baaba am, mi renndat kadi ndonu am e gooŋɗinɓe e suɓaaɓe ɓee; mi renndat ndonu am e onon, Ceyngu am! Woto kulee non, sabu hay so tawii on mbaasɓe, on alɗuɓe e am, sabu Baaba oo hokkii mi kala huunde e juuɗe am. Yoo, eklesiya Lawdisiya, a wontii debbo mo alaa iimaana, mo yuumtude.

Alaa, on njiɗaa ko teewu konngol am, on njokaani kadi mi e ɓernde mon fof; mbele on njanngaani Balndol ngol? Faamde moƴƴere ina heɓna moƴƴere, kono laawol ɓe ngalaa iimaana ɓee ina saɗi. Laabi yuumtude ɗi cuɓii-ɗon ɗii ina saɗi; laabi mon ɗi ngalaa iimaana ɗii ummaaki e am, on ndartiima yahde laawol ngol ndokku-mi on ngol, koyngal gootal ina e laawol, goɗngal ngal ina e luggere, kono haa jooni on mbaɗii wakkilaare tuumde mi, Yeshua HaMashiach, so on nanii e luggere no ndowraa-ɗon nii. Sabu njillu mon ina heewi nanngude, yaakaare am e mon haa jooni ko nanngude mon nanndude e nanngude dartiiɗo: Sabu dartiiɗo ina naana laabi jeeɗiɗi, ummoo kadi: kono bonɓe ɓee naanat e bone.

Mi noddaaki on nanngude e bone, onon ɓiɓɓe am, hay onon eklesiya am Lawdisiya, sabu mi accaani on haa jooni e sahaaji jarribooji ɗii. Sabu ndaaree! Miɗo darii haa jooni e damal, miɗo fiyata sabu mi renndataa hay dara e laabi mon ɗi yurmeende mum en uumi, ɗi njuumtata ɗii, kono miɗo nodda on e nuunɗal. Miɗo nodda on daranaade nuunɗal, sabu durooɓe majjuɓe ɓee njaɓii on, ndowi on e laabi Balaam; heewɓe e mon ina njanngina annabaagal fenaande e ruuhuuji Yezebel, ɓe njiɗnoo ustude konnguɗi annabaaɓe am goonga, njiɗi mbarde annabaaɓe ɓe laɓɓin-mi e am, sabu ko miin rokkii worɓe dokke. Sabu mi winndanii Galatiya nde ɓe mbattii no mbattir-ɗon nii: Miɗo haawnii, no on ngoppiri mo noddi on e neema Almasiihu nii, haa njahdon e Linjiila goɗɗo: Mo wonaa goɗɗo. Eey, njanngee ɗuum kadi, sabu alaa Linjiila goɗɗo, alaa kadi innde woɗnde rokkaande nde worɓe hisirtee so wonaa am, Yeesu Almasiihu. Ko waɗi on njowetee e am, onon ɓiɓɓe am tokosɓe? Mbele mi rokkataa, mi toppitotaako on, sabu ko miin woni duroowo moƴƴo rokkuɗo nguurndam mum ngam baali? Kono konnguɗi ɗi kaala-mi ɗii, ɗi njiɗ-ɗon e ɗi njokkaton ɗii, ina njuurnoo noppi mon; sabu ñiiɓal ngal wontii yiɗde mon adannde e laabi mon mawnikinaare; sabu on ndesndii fenaande e koye mon no goonga nii, on njuumtii kala ceertugol.

Mbele on njejjitii daartol eklesiya gadanol ngol? Mbele on ngañiima no feewi haa on njanngataa? Mbele ndee toɓɓere njuurnotaako noppi mon haa mbaawee nande konnguɗi am, ngoonɗinon ɗi? Sabu neɗɗo wuuraani e mburu tan, kono e kala konngol ummotoongol e hunduko Laamɗo! Eklesiya am jibinaama e ñalawma Pentekosta, winndaama e Golle ngam on njiylawto. Sabu eklesiya am fuɗɗii ko e Isiraayiil, e Yahuudiya, hay so tawii ɓe paamaani konnguɗi ɗi kaal-mi e Piyeer e oo ñalawma tedduɗo nde njillu-mi ruuhu laaɓɗo e suɓaaɓe am, nde ɓe kaaldi e ɗemɗe goɗɗe no ruuhu oo hokkiri ɓe haalde nii! Sabu hol ko Piyeer wi'i jamaa oo to Yerusalem ñalnde o annabii ngam am? Sabu fodoore ndee ko onon, e ɓiɓɓe mon, e kala wonɓe woɗɗude, hay no Joomiraawo Laamɗo men noddirta nii. Onon ɓiɓɓe am, ko onon wonɓe woɗɗude, sabu heewɓe e mon ummaaki e Isiraayiil e fawaade e ɓanndu, kono mi ittii on e leƴƴi e neema am mbele ndarooɗon e yurmeende am, sabu on noddaama.

Mbele on njanngaani? Kono jooni e Almasiihu Yeesu, onon wonnooɓe woɗɗude, on ngaddaama ɓadaade e yiiƴam Almasiihu. Kono e nder cuuɗi mon dewal njuumtuɗi ɗi ndokkaton e innde am e majjere (hay so tawii mi naatataa heen kono miɗo darii e damal miɗo fiyata), on cikkat ko on keɓii sariya Muusaa e am, e mawnikinaare mon on paamaani wonde Muusaa ko Isiraayiilnaajo, ko Isiraayiil rokkaa Tawreeta ngam rewde, ko huunde nde ɓe mbaawaani; sabu hay gooto waawaa rewde sariya Muusaa sabu bonannde nde mboddi ndii waɗi e ɓalli mon, sabu ganndal mum ina bonna sahaa kala, njoɓdi greti ko maayde. Sabu Korintiyankooɓe ina ceedtoo konnguɗi am: Mo waɗi en kadi jaagoriiɓe waawɓe Alkawal Kesal, wonaa e alkulal, kono e ruuhu: sabu alkulal ina wara, kono ruuhu ina rokka nguurndam. On noddaama yahde e ruuhu, hay so tawii heewɓe e mon ina njiɗi alkulal sariya ngal e majjere, ngal wonaa ngam mon, kono ngal timminii e am.

Njaabee mi ɗoo: Mbele ruuhu oo arii e mon e timmingol mon Tawreeta? Walla o arii ko e timmingol am nuunɗungol Tawreeta no Paska mon timmuɗo nii? Sabu hol ko seedantaagal Galatiya wullata? Ko ɗuum tan njiɗ-mi anndude e mon, mbele on keɓii ruuhu oo, e golle Sariya, walla e nande iimaana? Kadi Poola ina wiyana Galatiyankooɓe: Mi bonnataa neema Laamɗo: sabu so nuunɗal arii e Sariya, ndeen Almasiihu maayii e meere. Mbele on paamaani e fuɗɗoode jamaanu oo, wonnoo ko eklesiya Yahuudiyankeejo e eklesiya Jentilnaajo? Hay so tawii eklesiya am jibinaama e Yahuudiya, Poola wi'i Galatiyankooɓe, mi reeni ɗum toon: Sabu o golliɗo no feewi e Piyeer e Nelaaɗo sunna oo, kañum ne ina wawi e am feewde e Jentilnaaɓe. Sabu gadano oo, mi neldi Piyeer to Jentilnaaɓe, winndaama e Golle nde mbiynoo-mi Piyeer: Ko Laamɗo laɓɓini, woto noddu ɗum ko boni. Sabu nde nelnoo-mi Piyeer to Korneliyus, seedantaagal ngal rewi heen ngal winndaama: E ɓe sunna gooŋɗinɓe ɓee, ɓe kaawnii, no heewɓe e arɓe e Piyeer nii, sabu e Jentilnaaɓe kadi, dokkal ruuhu laaɓɗo ngoo yuppaama. Miɗo naamnoo on ɗuum, onon Lawdisiyankooɓe, mbele Korneliyus heɓii hisnude e rewde Tawreeta (sabu ko o neɗɗo dewooɗo)? Walla o heɓii fodoore ndee e iimaana? Korintiyankooɓe ina njogii daande e haala kaa nde Poola winndanno ɓe: E ɗuum kadi on hisni, so on ndesndii ko waajii-mi on, so wonaa on ngoonɗinii e meere. Hisnude arii ko e waaju iimaana, wonaa e alkulal sariya, sabu nawde hoore mum e les sariya Muusaa, hay iimaana mon ko meere.

Nii woni Roomnaaɓe ina mbiya: Kono jooni nuunɗal Laamɗo tawa Sariya alaa feeñii, seedtinaama e Sariya e Annabaaɓe. Hay nuunɗal Laamɗo, ngal iimanaaku Yeesu Almasiihu rokkata kala, e kala gooŋɗinɓe: sabu alaa ceertugol: Sabu kala en njuumii, njottaaki teddungal Laamɗo. Sabu mi wi'i eklesiya am to Galatiya ɗo Poola winndi e hoolaare: Sabu e Yeesu Almasiihu, sunna alaa nafoore, sunna alaa kadi, kono ko iimaana golliroowo e yiɗde. Kadi to Galatiya Poola winndi: Sabu e Almasiihu Yeesu sunna alaa nafoore, sunna alaa kadi, kono ko tagu kesu. Hisnude ndee rokketaake e golle sariya, nguurndam mon e hisnude mon kadi nguurtaake e golle sariya. Sabu winndanaama Roomnaaɓe: Ndaaree, mi waɗii e Siyoona kaaƴe mbattudi, e kaaƴe bonɗe: kala gooŋɗinɗo mo, semtataa. Onon ɓiɓɓe am, ko miin woni kaaƴe mbattudi e kaaƴe bonɗe, sabu ko miin Yeesu Nasarankaajo, jawgel Laamɗo, mi timminii sariya.

Yo, onon yimɓe Lawdisiya, mbele on cikkat ko onon tan mbatti e ndee haala? Mi wiyataa. Sabu on mbaawi renndinde ɓataakeeji am e pecce ɗiɗi kuuɓtidinɗe, so wonaa ɗiɗo fof mbinndanaama neɗɗo gooto keso e Almasiihu Yeesu. Sabu ɓataakeeji am mbinndanaama eklesiyaaji Jentilnaaɓe, yoga heen mbinndanaama eklesiyaaji Yahuudiyankooɓe, ko ɗuum waɗi Piyeer winndi e feeñninande Sirru nguu ndeen mi feeñnani Poola: No kadi e nder kala Epistolaaji makko, omo haala e majji ko fayti e ɗee geɗe, ɗo won heen geɗe saɗtuɗe faamde, ɗe ɓe nganndaa ɓe, ɓe ngonaa deeƴɓe ɓee, mbirlata, no ɓe mbaɗirta e binndi goɗɗi ɗii nii, haa njahat e halkere maɓɓe.

Nii woni caɗeele mon ngonaa kese, sabu alaa jarribo ngo nanngi on so wonaa no heewi e neɗɗo nii: kono Laamɗo ina koolnii, mo accataa on jarribeede ko ɓuri baawɗe mon; kono e jarribo ngoo kadi o waɗanat laawol daɗirgol, mbele mbaawee muñde ɗum. Mbele on njaɓat laawol daɗirgol ngol Laamɗo rokkii on e neema am e yurmeende am ngam daɗde e ɓe njiɗat wujjude on njoɓdi mon e waɗde on nanngude e neema? Mbele on caloto konnguɗi am ngam wallitde Yahuudiyankooɓe ɓe e majjere mbiyata ina foti rewde Tawreeta ngam hisnude mon? Mbele on mbaasii haa mbaawa sikde wonde, nde puɗɗii-ɗon sariya nguurndam e ruuhu Yeesu Almasiihu, jooni on rokkitaama sariya greti e maayde? Sabu hol ko sariya? Onon ɓiɓɓe am, mi haalan on. Woto njuɓɓinee e majjere wonde ko mi daande fenaande ndowat on e majjere, sabu heewɓe e mon nganndaa daande am, nganndaa kadi mi, hay so tawii miɗo nodda on! Woto cikkee mi arii ko ngam bonnude sariya walla annabaaɓe. Mi ardaaki bonnude, kono ko ngam timminde. Sabu goonga miɗo wiya on, Haa kammu e leydi paasa, yod gooto walla gaawal yod, soorataa e sariya, haa kala timma.

Ko miin timminɗo sariya Muusaa, yamirooje mum ina ndewee e hoolaare konnguɗi am neema e yurmeende e nder epistolaaji am e iimanaaku Yeesu Almasiihu. On cikkat e majjere sabu ko miin woni timmoode sariya, miɗo wasiyoo hay gooto yo bonnu sariyaaji Laamɗo, tawa ko sariya Muusaa walla sariyaaji dewal Laamɗo. Sabu hol ko mbiyno-mi hoɗɓe Korinta ɓee? Eklesiya am Lawdisiya, mo njiɗ-mi, wonaa onon adii haɓde e ndee jarribo, ko ɗuum waɗi mi waɗi Poola winndude: Mo kadi waɗi en jaagoriiɓe baawɓe Alkawal Kesal, wonaa e alkulal, kono e ruuhu: sabu alkulal ina wara, kono ruuhu ina rokka nguurndam. Kono so tawii ministeer maayde binndaaɗo, ñeeñaaɗo e kaaƴe, ina teddini, haa ɓiɓɓe Isiraayiil mbaawaano ndaarde yeeso Muusaa e deeƴre, sabu teddungal yeeso mum, teddungal ngal foti ko itteede: Hol no ministeer ruuhu oo ɓurat teddude? Woto njuumee, onon ɓiɓɓe am, alkulal ina wara sabu greti wonɗo e ɓanndu mon, mo alkulal ngoo fof hollata, hay sariya Muusaa: Nii woni sariya oo ina laaɓi, yamiroore ndee ina laaɓi, ina nuunɗi, ina moƴƴi. Mbele ko moƴƴi ndeen, wontanii mi maayde? Laamɗo haɗa. Kono greti, mbele o feeña greti, omo golla maayde e am e ko moƴƴi: mbele greti e Yamiroore ndee wontana bonannde mawnde. Ko ɗuum waɗi mi waɗi Poola wiyde Galatiyankooɓe: Mi bonnataa neema Laamɗo: sabu so nuunɗal arii e Sariya, ndeen Almasiihu maayii e meere. Onon ɓiɓɓe am, on ndowaama e majjere, mi wi'ii on kadi inaboojam kesam ina foti waɗeede e ɓulli kesi, ndaree non e neema mo miin, Almasiihu Yeesu, rimɗini on.

Onon ɓiɓɓe am, on mbaawaa bonnude sariyaaji Laamɗo so on njahii e ruuhu, sabu ɗum waawaa wonde. On mahiraama e dow fonndasiyoŋ nelaaɓe e annabaaɓe, Yeesu Almasiihu e hoore mum woni kaaƴe mawɗe ɗee. To Hebree en, wiyanooma: Sabu mi yurminat bonanndeeji maɓɓe, mi siftorataa kadi gretiiji maɓɓe e bonanndeeji maɓɓe. E nder ko o wiyi, alkawal kesal, o waɗii gadanal ngal woni kiɗngal. Jooni ko bonnata, ko naawata ngal, ina heddii maayde. Onon ɓiɓɓe am, nuunɗal arataa e rewde sariya, sabu ko dokkal keɓotoongal e iimaana e am, Almasiihu Yeesu. E sabu on laɓɓinaama e am, on rimɗinaama e sariya greti e maayde.

Sabo ko kufru woni miijo wonde yahde e ruuhu nguurndam ko noone e hakke, sabo kala goonɗinɗo ɗum ina jogii sadak am ngam hakkeeji yimɓe ko huunde nde laaɓaani. Sabo mi mawni Muusaa, Muusaa e hoore mum wiyi: Joomiraaɗo Alla maa umminir on Annabiijo gila e hakkunde mooɗon, e banndiraaɓe mooɗon, nannduɗo e am, kanko njooɗoton. Nanee daande am, onon ɓiɓɓe, njooɗee konngol am, sabo mi waɗaani on bonɓe, sabo ɓiɓɓe Seyɗaani ko bonɓe, wonaa ɓiɓɓe Alla! Sabo e nder Roomnaaɓe, Poolus winndii no laaɓiri: Ndeen noon, alaa ñaawoore jooni e dow ɓe ngonɗi e Almasiihu Yeesu, ɓe njahdataa e ko ɓalli mum en njiɗi, kono e ko Ruuhu wi'i. Sabo sariya ruuhu nguurndam, e nder Almasiihu Yeesu, rimɗinii kam e sariya hakke e maayde.

Sabo ko sariya oo waawaano waɗde, e nder baasal mum e nder ɓanndu, Alla neldi Ɓiɗɗo mum, e nanndugol e ɓanndu hakkeejo, e ngam hakke O ñaawoyi hakke e nder ɓanndu: Ngam peewal sariya oo timminiree e meeɗen, menen ɓe njahdataa e ko ɓalli mum en njiɗi, kono e ko Ruuhu wi'i. Kadi Roomnaaɓe ina kaala: Ngam hollude, mi wiyi, e oo sahaa peewal makko: ngam o laatoo peewɗo, e peewnoowo neɗɗo goonɗinɗo Yeesu. To mawninkinaare ndee woni, ndeen? Nde ittaama. E hol sariya? E golle? Alaa: kono e sariya goonɗinal. Ndeen noon, en pelliti, wonde neɗɗo ina feewnee e goonɗinal, tawa wonaa e golle sariya. Sabo ko mbii-mi: Sabo e nder maggal peewal Alla ina feeñnee gila e goonɗinal haa e goonɗinal; no winndiraa nii, Peewɗo maa wuuran e goonɗinal.

Kono heewɓe e mooɗon ina njooɗondira teddeendi ndi fotaani e dow goɗɗo e goɗɗo sabo on ngoonɗinii annabaaɓe Seyɗaani ɓe neldaa ngam tamminde on e reende Tawreeta e fenaande ngam wonde sarɗi kisal e kadi ngam wuuraade kisal; yoga e mooɗon ina kufrude, ina mbiya wonde mi timminii sariya oo e sadak am ngam on mbaawa jooni reende Tawreeta. E wootere, on poti, sabo yahde e sariya ruuhu nguurndam ko reende Tawreeta timmunde e annditidiral mooɗon e am; sabo eklesiya am ina mahiraa e peewal e laaɓal goonga e dow am, Almasiihu Yeesu, kaaƴel mawngel. On reentii Tawreeta e ɗooftaare goonɗinal ngal woni e am. Ko waɗi on suɓii daande yahuudiyankooɓe dow daande am laaɓunde? Ko waɗi on njooɗii teddeendi ndi fotaani e dow banndiraaɓe mooɗon, walla on njanngaani? Sabo mi anndiniiino Kolosenaaɓe: Woto neɗɗo ñaawo on e nguura, walla e njaram, walla e ñalngu laaɓngu, walla e lewru hesru, walla e ñalɗi asawe, ɗi ngoni ɗowdi geɗe garooje, kono ɓanndu ndu ko e Almasiihu.

Onon ɓiɓɓe am, mi ñaawoyi sariya oo; miɗo hollita tan ko laaɓi, wonde sabo hakke e nder ɓalli mooɗon on mbaawaa reende Tawreeta so wonaa e goonɗinal mooɗon e am, sabo peewɗo maa wuuran e goonɗinal! Hol ko woni goonɗinal e am, on mbaawi naamndaade? Goonɗinal e am ina mahiraa e hoolaare konnguɗi am e faamde wonde on annditidiraama e am e kala tolno, sabo on ngoni eklesiya am mo mi laɓɓiniri e ƴiiƴam am ɗam alaa hakke; sabo yaafagol hakkeeji waawaa heɓeede e sadak daabaaji. Ngoonɗinee kam, sabo e nder goonɗinal e am Tawreeta ina reenee, e nder goonɗinal e am sariya ina timminiree e mooɗon, on ngoni peewɓe, peewnaaɓe, e laaɓnaaɓe! Nde mi ummitinaa e nguurndam kesam, onon kadi on ummitinaama; e nde mi ƴeftaa to nyaamo Baaba, onon kadi on njooɗinaama e nokkuuje toowɗe. Sabo Efesenaaɓe ina seedtoo: Kono Alla keewɗo yurmeende, ngam yiɗde makko mawnde nde o yiɗiri en, Hay so tawii en maaynoo e hakkeeji, o wuurnidii en e Almasiihu (e moƴƴere on kisiraama), O ummidinii en kadi, o jooɗinidii en e nokkuuje toowɗe e nder Almasiihu Yeesu. Hoto on faamde wonde sariya Muusaa wonaa alkawal mooɗon, sabo mooɗon ko alkawal kesal ngal tabbintiniraa e ƴiiƴam am, ngal yoɓiraa e torra am.

Ndeen noon, njoofto-ɗee goonɗinal, onon yimɓe am, woto on laato yoorɓe kadi. Suɓee nguurndam e am, Yeesu Almasiihu, sabo ko noon on noddiraa. Ngollee golle mawɗe keewɗe ɗe mi rokkii on, hay e oo minit cakkitiɗo. Miɗo yiɗi on faamde wonde tawa mi alaa, on mbaawaa waɗde hay huunde, e on reentii kala fannu Tawreeta e goonɗinal e am. Yanti heen, yahuudiyankooɓe inɗe laaɓɗe ɓee kebbinii eklesiya am, ina njooɗii teddeendi ndi fotaani e dow ɓe ɗaɓɓata kisal e ɓe ngoni ɓe am. Baaba am rokki kam innde dow kala innde, ngam e innde Yeesu Almasiihu kala koppi fof ŋoftoo.
