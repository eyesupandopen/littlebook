Seedeeɓe ɗiɗo: Isra'iila
Ko Welɗi e ko Welataa
7 lewru suwee, 2024

No Winndiraa, Nii waɗirtee!

Kawtoree, onon ɓiɓɓe am, nanee konnguɗi Joomiraaɗo mon, hono Yesuwa Almasiihu, sabu ko miin woni Joomiraaɗo Joomiraaɓe e Laamɗo Laamɓe, miɗo ara e nuunɗal ngam ñaawoyaade e haɓde. Nde tawnoo noon, paamee jamaanu mo nguɗon e ko wonata, tinee e daande am, nanee konnguɗi Joomiraaɗo mon duumiiɗo haa abada, hono Almasiihu Iisaa mo ummitinaa, mo teddinaa! Jooni mi haalan ko arata e nder yontere capanɗe jeeɗiɗiire Daniyel, mi weeɓnan on laabi mon, mi jaajnan on moƴƴere hisinirnde am rewrude e juuɗe 144 000 am ɓee. Sabu kamɓe ɓe arat ngam hisnude yimɓe am e halkere, ɓe arat ngam suuɗude yimɓe am e tikkere, hono tikkere Allah ndeen feewnanaande welsindiiɓe ɓee e ɓeen fof jokkondirɓe e maɓɓe haa abada e laamu maɓɓe, ɓe njaɓi maande maɓɓe, hono ŋatere ngaan mboddi mbeestaandi ndi alaa safaara.

Kono non mi fawoto yummiraawo am to asamaanuuji, mi maandinan 144 000 am ɓee ummoriiɓe e leƴƴi garooji ɗii, hono 12 000 ummoriiɓe e gooto e leƴƴi ɗii fof: Yahuuda, Ruuben, Gaad, Aser, Neftali, Manase, Simiyona, Lewi, Isakaar, Sabulon, Yuusuf, e Benyamin. Nde tawnoo noon, nanee daande am!

Kamɓe ɓe laatoto semmbe mo alaa fof haɗata, semmbe mo alaa fof feƴƴata. Sabu maɓɓe nguurndam duumiiɗam, ɗam bonnere alaa ko bonnata heen. Kamɓe ɓe keɓan ɓalli ɗi alaa ko halkata, sabu halkere alaa ko waawi waɗde sobbinaaɓe ɓee; sabu foolde maayde e halkere ko maɓɓe haa abada. Ɓe tagaakiree ko ɓiɓɓe annoora, ɓe ɓoornee teddungal duumiiɗo, ɓe keɓa baawɗe e dokke keertiiɗe binndaaɗe e wuurɗere maɓɓe, ɗe kollirta golle maɓɓe, ɗe njiimata baawɗe ɗe mi tagata e maɓɓe. Sabu miin Almasiihu Iisaa, ko miin laɓɓinoowo jooɗiiɗo e juuɗe mum ina jogii pufirgel, miɗo huɓɓa yiite laɓɓinirgel, ngam mi taga on fof kesum e am, Almasiihu Iisaa Joomiraaɗo mon e Kisinoowo mon mo fawaa; kala fof foolɗo, o ronat kala huunde.

Miɗo rokka on faamde ko arata ɓooyataa, yoga e majji ina tokkondiri, yoga kadi ina keddondiri, mi limtan ɗum en haa paamon jamaanu mo nguɗon, mbele onon seniiɓe am torra wonta on horiiɓe e majjere mawnde ndee udditiriinde aduna oo, nde heewɓe majjinirtee, njahra e ñaawoore. Sabu welsindiiɓe ɓee, ɓe peeñat, ɓe fenan laabi keewɗi, ɓe ndokan nguurndam kono ɓe ngalaa baawɗe rokkude nguurndam, kadi ɓe mbaawaa rokkude nguurndam; kono kala konngol ngol ɓe kaalata ina jiiɓii e fenaande yahroore e maayde ɗiɗmere, ko halkere duumiinde tan tawaa e juuɗe rewɓe laawol welsindiiɓe ɓee e fof jaɓɓe fenaande maɓɓe bonnde ndee ko goonga. Kono ko ɗum woni ñaawoore ariinde e aduna, wonde yimɓe ina njiɗi niɓre ko ɓuri annoora, yimɓe ina njiɗi fenaande ko ɓuri goonga, sabu neɗɗaagu salii miin, Yesuwa Almasiihu, hono Iisaa Almasiihu, ngam weltanaade hisinoowo fenaande; kono goonga ina woni e Almasiihu tan, ko heddii koo fof ko fenuɓe e njaafaaɓe.

Ko arata koo ko nii woni, yoga e majji ina keddondiri, yoga kadi ina tokkondiri, ina poti waɗde ngam yoga waɗa. Heewde e majji limtaaka kono arat e sahaaji mum en. Ko yoga innirta fawgol, walla dental kala wonɓe e Almasiihu e Joomiraaɗo mum en, ko ngol woni go'o ko firti kala goɗɗi e nder torra Yaakuuba, anndiraango kadi yontere capanɗe jeeɗiɗiire Daniyel, sabu yontere duuɓi jeeɗiɗi wonata, ñalawma kala ko hitaande wootere. Nde tawnoo noon, gomɗinɓe Eklesiya am, woni Ɓanndu am e Yummiraawo am, ɓe nawat so miin Almasiihu Iisaa, mi jippii ummoraade asamaanuuji e wullaango, e daande maleyka mawɗo e alaadu Allah. Sabu alaadu sakkitiindu nduu fiyatee, e oon sahaa maayɓe e nder Almasiihu ummitintoo e nguurndam kesam; ndeen onon wuurnooɓe heddiiɓe ɓee, on nawat e duule wondude e maɓɓe, ɗum waɗirta no winndiraa nii, e no limtiraa nii ɗum waɗirta e nder ŋalɗungal gite.

Nde tawnoo noon paamee, onon gomɗinɓe am, onon ɓe njiɗir-mi yiɗde duumiinde haa abada, wonde on njiytitoyto e nder laamorgo Yawhe e hoore mum, toon mi hollirta on tawa alaa on peccel, alaa on nyorol walla hay huunde hono noon e Baaba annooraaji, onon woni annooraaji aduna. Oon sahaa on nganndoyto no on nganndiranoo nii, on njiyoyto miin Yesuwa Almasiihu, on njiyoyto Yawhe, Allah Mawɗo Duumiiɗo, e gite mon e hoore mon laawol gadanol, on haayɗintoo sanne e ko njiyoton e teddungal ngal ɓoorneteeɗon. Haa abada on ngonoytoo e dental duumiiɗo e Baaba, e Ɓiɗɗo, haa abada en ngonoytoo.

E sahaaji mooɓtugol seniiɓe ɓee, kaɗoowo oo ittetee, ñaawoore yanana suudu Allah timmunde. Ɓeen jaɓɓe mi laɓɓina ɗum en e yiite laɓɓinirgel ngel darotoo sabu ɓe laɓɓinaama e ƴiiƴam Jawgel ngel, ɓe ndaroo e nuunɗal ngal mi rokkii ɓe, sabu miin ko mi Joomiraaɗo Nuunɗuɗo, ruuhu rokkoowo nguurndam. Ɓeen saliiɓe daande am, keddiiɓe e mbaadi mum en saliiɓe yiɗde am, saliiɓe yaltude e greka naata e nuunɗal, ɓe accetee ngam ɓeydaade laɓɓineede e sahaaji torra Yaakuuba, yimɓe heewɓe keɓat hisde duumiinde, njoɓdi, e teddungal, ɓe nguura haa abada, ɓe keɓa teddungal mawngal haa abada. Kadi eklesiyaaji fenaande keddiiɗi ɗii halketee no mboddi ndii, mbeestaandi ummoriindi e maayo, e mbeestaandi ummoriindi e leydi ndii, njaawirta haɓde e seniiɓe keddiiɓe ɓee, ɓe poola ɓe. Ko nii wonirta.

So kaɗoowo oo ittaama, Baabila suuɗiiɗo oo halketee no winndiraa nii. E nder waktu gooto, joofnirde mum laaɓta, musiiba mawɗo e halkere njaltata e leydi, diƴƴere mawnde waɗata, ɗum laaɓan aduna oo wonde ñalawma Joomiraaɗo arii, hay so tawii welsindiiɓe ɓee fenat, peeña ko no tageefooji moƴƴi, ɓe kaɓat ngam suuɗde peeñgol am e gomɗinɓe ɓee e hare e wolde fenaande e tageefooji. Nde tawnoo noon woto ngoonɗinee fenaande maɓɓe, onon ɓiɓɓe am, sabu fenaande maɓɓe ndee ko feewnanaande yahrunde on e halkere duumiinde ndeen reervanaande welsindiiɓe ɓee, woto njokkondiree e maɓɓe.

Maandeele ɗee udditoytee, pucci nayi ɗii njoñoytee e aduna, yimɓe heewɓe maayat sabu ɗum. Laamu Baabila suuɗiiɗo jooɗiiɗo e ndiyam keewɗam, hay ɗiin halketee, moɓtee, jawdi ndii huutoree laamu mbeestaandi garoowo oo, joofnirɗo oo; ko joofnirɗo sabu miin Almasiihu Iisaa, mi halkoyat laamu Seytaane fof, o ummitaaki kadi. Aduna oo haayɗintoo e mbeestaandi ndii, heewɓe naatoytoo e laamu maɓɓe seytaanankeewu. Tagoodi bonndi e neɗɗaagu, jibinaandi e greka Aadama nde Hawaa hooyi e mboddi ndii, ñaami, wontoytaa kaɗaaɗo sam, neɗɗaagu heɓoytaa añam-añam timmuɗo e ɓerɗe mum en, heewɓe njokkondirtoytoo e mbeestaandi ndii haa abada, ɓe njaɓa, ɓe keɓa maande mbeestaandi ndii. Woto keɓee maande mum, sabu kala baɗɗo ɗum, o werletee haa abada e nder weendu yiite e kubriit, huɓɓoore yiite ɓurnde wulde e bulo.

E nder mbulmbuldi wolde ndee laamu maɓɓe joofnirngu ummotoo, hay e mbulmbuldi njuɓɓudi ɓooyndi ndii, sabu e nder jiiɓru ndu ɓe ngaddata nduu ummotoo njuɓɓudi maɓɓe kesiri, hono njuɓɓudi mboddi ndii. Ɓe njiɗoyta hawrude aduna oo fof e les lefol maɓɓe, ɓe kawritoyta diineeji aduna oo fof, ɓe njaɓataa miin, Yesuwa Almasiihu, hono Iisaa Almasiihu, mo jaɓaaka e maɓɓe. Goonga, goonga, mi meeɗaa jogaade heen feccere walla geɗal e maɓɓe. Ɓe njaawroyta e yaawnde mawnde e bonannde mawnde e nder wolde maɓɓe e seniiɓe ɓee, aduna oo waylitoyta fof e seniiɓe torra ɓee, kala foolɗo e noddude innde am haa joofnirde, o teddinetee haa abada e njoɓdi duumiindi e teddungal duumiingal, njoɓdi maɓɓe ina mawni, ɓeen foolɓe ndonat kala huunde.

No renndo maɓɓe keso ummirtoo nii, ko non kadi leɗɗe am oliif ɗiɗi ɗee ummortoo, hono fiiltorɗe am ɗiɗi ndariiɗe yeeso Allah leydi ndii. Baawɗe mawɗe ndokketee ɓe, ɓe kaaloyta annabaaku lebbi 42 ɓooriiɓe comci booro; so neɗɗo ina yiɗi lorlaade ɓe, yiite yaltat e kunndule maɓɓe, ñama añɓe maɓɓe: so neɗɗo ina yiɗi lorlaade ɓe, o foti warde noon. Ɓeen keɓoyta baawɗe uddude asamaan, haa toɓo toɓataa e balɗe annabaaku maɓɓe: ɓe keɓoyta baawɗe e dow ndiyam ngam waylude ɗam ƴiiƴam, e fiide leydi e kala belaaji, no keewi no ɓe njiɗiri fof. Yiɗde aduna oo e maɓɓe ina mawni. E daranaade leɗɗe am oliif ɗiɗi ɗee, ko mbeestaandi ndii e annabi fenaande oo, gooto hollirta goɗɗo oo, ɓe teddinoyta koye maɓɓe, ɓe kaaloyta konnguɗi keewɗi jancooɗi, ɓe mbaɗoyta maandeele e kaawniiɗe fenaande keewɗe yeeso yimɓe.

Mbeestaandiiji ɗii mbaɗoyta kaawniiɗe mawɗe ngam majjinde hoɗɓe e leydi ndii, sabu o accetee waɗde maandeele e kaawniiɗe fenaande keewɗe, e baawɗe nannduɗe e ɗeen ɓe laamoytoo. Mbeestaandi ummoriindi e leydi ndii waɗoyta yiite jippoo e leydi yeeso yimɓe, o ardoyta yimɓe fof ngam haayɗinde ɓe e maandeele e kaawniiɗe fenaande keewɗe. O waɗoyta natal mbeestaandi ndii, o waɗa yimɓe fof ngam rewrude e natal ngal o waɗata. O accetee rokkude ruuhu natal mbeestaandi ndii haa natal mbeestaandi ndii haala, o waɗa kala mo rewaani natal mbeestaandi ndii waraa. O waɗoyta fof, alɗuɓe e miskineeɓe, rimɓe e maccuɓe, keɓa maande mum e junngo mum en nyaamo walla e tiinde mum en.

SO NEƊƊO REWII MBESTAANDI NDII E NATAL MUM, O HEƁI MAANDE MUM E TIINDE MUM, WALLA E JUNNGO MUM, OON YAROYTA E WIIƁERE TIKKERE ALLAH, NDE LOOWETEE TAWAA JIIƁAAKA E KOP INDIGNASIYOŊ MUM, O TORRETEE E YIITE E KUBRIIT, YEESO MALEYKAAƁE SENIIƁE, E YEESO JAWGEL NGEL: CURKI TORRA MAƁƁE ƁEYDOTO HAA ABADA. ƁE NGALA A FOOFTUDE ÑALAWMA WALLA JEMMA, ƁEEN REWƁE MBESTAANDI NDII E NATAL MUM, E KALA HEƁƊO MAANDE INNDE MUM.

E nder oon sahaa temple maɓɓe mahoytee, hay so tawii sadaka daabaaji wonaani haaju sabu ko miin woni Jawgel Allah, mi sadakii hoore am ngam grekaaji neɗɗaagu ko mi mawɗo sariya haa abada e mbaadi Melkisedek. Kafara waɗaama ngam grekaaji neɗɗaagu laawol gootol, fof, e haa abada e nder kafara am, mo alaa greka, ƴiiƴam am. Nde tawnoo noon, sadaka goɗɗo ngam greka alaa.

So leɗɗe am oliif ɗiɗi ɗee timminii seedanfaagu mum en e joofnirde lebbi 42, mbeestaandi ummotoondi e lugge ɓuuɓɗe ndee haɓoyta e maɓɓe, wara fiiltorɗe am ɗiɗi ɗee, sabu ko nii rokkiraa ɗum waɗde. Ɓalli maɓɓe leloytoo maayɗi balɗe tati e feccere e laabi, no aduna oo weltortoo, rokkindirta dokke e maayde maɓɓe. Daande mawnde ummotoo e asamaanuuji, wiyoyta, Ngaree ɗoo! Ɓe ummitintoo e nguurndam kesam, ɓe njaɓɓoytee e asamaanuuji yeeso añɓe maɓɓe.

Ɓe timminoyta temple maɓɓe, piccal ngal waɗoytee, sabu hol fowru waɗata piccal yeeso colli? Sabu mbeestaandi ummoriindi e leydi ndii ko kañum waɗata piccal e leydi, o luretoyta heewɓe e nder lowre makko fenaande, o janfoyta yimɓe leydi ndii. Oo neɗɗo greka oo peeñoyta, hono ɓiy halkere, woni gaño, o mawnintoo hoore mum e kala ko noddirtee Allah, walla ko rewetee: haa o jooɗoo no Allah nii e nder Temple Allah, o hollira hoore makko ko o Allah. Temple maɓɓe timmoyta, bonnere halkere ndee timminirtee e juulde maɓɓe fenaande. O tabintinoyta alkawal e heewɓe yontere wootere: e caka yontere ndee o waɗoyta sadaka e dokkal ngal haaɗa, e ngam saaktude bonnereeji; o waɗoyta ɗum majji, haa timmal ngal laaɓtiniraa loowee e dow majji.

Torra ɓurɗo mawnude waɗoyta, hay torra mo meeɗaa waɗde e leydi, goonga, meeɗataa waɗde kadi. Ndaaree! Miin, hay Yesuwa Almasiihu, mi accataa yimɓe am e halkere nde alaa keɓal, mi hisinoyta Isra'iila, hay oo heddiiɓe semmbe ɓeen jaɓɓotooɓe mi ko mi Almasiihu goonga, sabu ko nii Yawhe tagiri mi. 144 000 am ɓee ngartoyta ballal ɓeen halkunooɓe e nodduɓe innde am. Mi ardoyta ɓe seeɗa seeɗa, 144 000 am ɓee mbaɗanoyta mi golle mawɗe keewɗe.

Sabu ɓeen ko ɓe nawtaa e dental seniiɓe ɓee to am, Almasiihu Iisaa, e weeyo e daande alaadu sakkitiindu nduu, alaadu nduu ina fotnoo fiyeede. Ɓeen ko gomɗinɓe am, hay ɓeen ɓe alaa fenaande tawaa e mum en, sabu ɓe laɓɓinii koye maɓɓe no debbo laaɓɗo nii e ƴiiƴam Jawgel ngel, ngel mi rokkii haa abada ko mi mawɗo sariya maɓɓe, sabu miin ko mi mawɗo sariya haa abada e mbaadi Melkisedek. Ɓeen ko Yummiraawo am, ɓe ndewoyta mi kala ɗo njah-mi. Ndaaree! Mi waɗoyta ɓe semmbe, hay huunde meeɗaa yiyeede e leydi nanndunde e baawɗe ɗe mi ɓooroyta ɓe. Ɓe ɓoornetee comci laaɓɗi, daneeji, ɗi alaa peccel, baawɗe maɓɓe kollirta golle maɓɓe, ɓe keɓa baawɗe mawɗe; sabu ko ɓe gomɗinɓe, noddaaɓe, cuɓaaɓe, hay gila hade aduna oo sosde e juuɗe Yawhe ngam ɗiin dalillaaji.

144 000 am ɓee njippoyto e dow tulde Sion, mi ardoyta ɓe. Ɓe nawat ɓeen ɓe mi ardii ɓe to ladde ngam suuɗeede e niɓre timmunde e torra laamu añɓe. Ɓe nawat e kisal ummoraade e belaaji jeeɗiɗi ɗii, sabu e nder ɗiin ñaawooje ko ñaawoore tikkere Allah loowetee e jooɗorde mbeestaandi ndii, e torraade laamu mboddi ndii, sabu lepte maɓɓe ina mawni. 144 000 ɓee nawat ɓe e kisal; ɓe cellintoyta ɓe e gañanɗe maɓɓe; ɓe balloyta ɓe, ɓe ndokka ɓe yaakaare mawnde ngam noddude innde Joomiraaɗo, hay haa joofnirde so haaju. Sabu so mi famɗinaano balɗe ɗee, hay ɓanndu wootere heddotaako wuurnoonde. Ɓe timminoyta ko mi ardii ɓe.

Nde ɓe tato bonɓe ɓen ummotoo mbonna ɓe njaɓɓii kam wonde Almasiihu maɓɓe, non kadi tikkere Alla timmunde nden fof hebboytoo dow ko'e maɓɓe e dow laamu maɓɓe ngun fop; goonga-goonga, koo'e jeeɗiɗi ɗe tikkere Alla heewi ɗen; ñaawoore mawnde e bone mawɗo ina ngoodi nder koo'e maɓɓe, ɗum laatotoo no winndiraa non:

Malaa'ikaajo arano on wippitay ko'el mum dow leydi, ɓe njaɓɓi maandeeji maɓɓe bonɗi ɗin fof, ɓe nanngaay ñabbuuli nyidduɗi, bonɗi sabu ko'el ngel malaa'ikaajo arano on wippitata.

Malaa'ikaajo ɗiɗaɓo on wippitay ko'el mum, nden maaje ɗen laatoo no ƴiiƴam neɗɗo maayɗo nii, tagooje geec živeeje ɗen fof maayay.

Malaa'ikaajo tataɓo on wippitay ko'el mum, waɗa maaje e caaɗli laatoo ƴiiƴam, sabi ɓe liɓii ƴiiƴam seniiɓe e annabaaɓe, ngam maajum ina foti ɓe ndokkaa ƴiiƴam ɓe njara.

Goonga-goonga, malaa'ikaajo nayaɓo on wippitay ko'el mum dow naange, o rokke ngam o torra yimɓe e wulnde yiite, yimɓe ɓen wuloyto nguleeki, ɓe kaala haalaaji mawɗi, ɓe njena Joomiraaɗo asama.

Malaa'ikaajo jowaɓo on wippitay ko'el mum dow jooɗorgal dabba ka, laamu makka ngun laatoo niɓɓere, e semmbe mawɗo, ɓe ɲaatay ɗemɗe maɓɓe ngam mettam-alla.

Malaa'ikaajo jeego'o on wippitay ko'el mum dow maayo mawngo Yufuratees, ndiyam maggo ngoon yoora, ngam laawol laamɓe Fuɗnaange ngol hebla.

Malaa'ikaajo jeeɗiɗaɓo on wippitay ko'el mum nder henndu: nden daande toownde yaltay e suudu dewal asama, iwde e jooɗorgal, wiyde, Gasii. Nden wooday daaɗe, e diƴƴe, e maƴƴe, e dimmbungal mawngal waɗay, ngal meeɗaay waɗde gila yimɓe ngoni dow leydi, hay dimmbungal mawngal hono ngal. Nden wuro mawngo ngoon feccoyto pecce tati, gure leƴƴi ɗen njannoyto. Nii kadi geɗe ɗee ngonirta.

Ngam maajum, paamee, ɓiɓɓe am, wonde Yahweh suuday yimɓe mum hay e nder fitina Yaakuuba, hay e nder fitina mawka oon, heddiiɓe hisinay ngeedam, ɓe keddoo e kisal nder ladde, hay nokku mo Alla e hoore mum heblani, ngam ɓe nyaamna ɗon balɗe ujunere e teemedde ɗiɗi e capanɗe jeegom. Hay ɓanndu wooturu heddaano e ngeedam so tawii balɗe ɗen njuutnaaka. Nden yankiniiɓe ɓen mooɓondiray ngam haɓde e Laamɗo laamɓe e Joom Joomiraaɓe, non winndiraa, non kadi waɗirtee.

Mi yi'i asama udditii, nden njiimi-mi puccu danewu, jooɗiiɗo e maggu on ina wiyee Koolaaɗo e Goongɗinɗo, e nder peewal omo ñaawee omo haɓa. Gite makko ina nandi e yiite wulngo, dow hoore makko ina woodi laamɗaaje keewɗe, omo jogii innde winndaande, nde hay gooto anndaa so wonaa kanko e hoore makko. O ɓoornaama comcol muusungal nder ƴiiƴam, innde makko ina wiyee, Konngol Alla. Nden konuuji gonɗi nder asama ɗin njokki mo e dow pucci daneeji, ɓoornaaɗi liiliwol laaɓngol, danewol, laaɓngol. Nden e hunduko makko ina yalta kaafa nebbam, ngam e maggal o fiyira leƴƴi: o laamoto ɗi e sawru njamndi: omo yanca nokku njaram tikkere e mettere Alla Joomiraaɗo Baawɗo fof. Omo jogii e comcol makko, e dow bowngol makko innde winndaande, Laamɗo Laamɓe, E Joom Joomiraaɓe.

Nii kadi malaa'ikaajo am jippotoo iwde e asamaaji, omo jogii coktirgal ngaska luggu ka e jolokere mawnde nde o haɓɓirta Seyɗaane nder ngaska luggu ka duuɓi ujunere, o waɗa maande ngam o fena leƴƴi kadi duuɓi ujunere. To laamu am duuɓi ujunere timmi, Seyɗaane yaltine e kasoo mum seeɗa.

Ndaaree! No Yuuhanna Wahayuujo winndiri non, non kadi laatortoo: Mi yi'i jooɗorɗe laamu, ɓe njooɗii e majje, ñaawoore rokkaama ɓe: mi yi'i yonkiiji ɓe ta'aa ko'e ngam seedee Iisaa, e ngam konngol Alla, e ɓe ndewaano dabba ka, wanaa natal magga, wanaa ɓe njaɓɓi maandee magga dow tiiɗe maɓɓe, koo e juuɗe maɓɓe; ɓe nguuri ɓe laamodii e Almasiihu duuɓi ujunere. Kono heddiiɓe e maayɓe ɓen nguuraano haa duuɓi ujunere ɗin timmi. Ndee woni immital aranal. Barkinaaɗo e seniiɗo woni jogiiɗo geɗal e immital aranal: e dow hono ɓeen maayde ɗiɗaɓere nden alaa baawɗe, kono ɓe laatoto Liman'en Alla, e Almasiihu, ɓe laamodoo e makko duuɓi ujunere.

To laamu am duuɓi ujunere timmi, Seyɗaane yaltine e kasoo mum seeɗa ngam o mooɓa ɓe hono njaareendi geec. O yaltay ngam o fena leƴƴi gonɗi e kaɓirɗe nayi leydi ndin, Gog e Magog, ngam o mooɓa ɓe ngam haɓde: limoore maɓɓe ina wayi no njaareendi geec nii. Ɓe ƴeɓoyto dow yaajeende leydi ndin, ɓe taarii kampu seniiɓe ɓen, e wuro yidaango ngoon: nden yiite jippotoo iwde e Alla e asama, ngoon halka ɓe. Nden ibliisa fenuɗo ɓe on werlee nder weendu yiite e jammoori, ɗo dabba ka e annabiijo fenaande on ngonata, ɓe torree ñalawma e jemma haa abada.

Nii kadi immital bonɓe ngal aroyta, sabi non Yuuhanna Wahayuujo winndiri: Mi yi'i jooɗorgal laamu danewal mawngal, e jooɗiiɗo e maggal, mo yeeso mum leydi e asama ndoggi; nokku heɓaaka ngam maɓɓe. Mi yi'i maayɓe, tokosɓe e mawɓe, ndarii yeeso Alla; defte ɗen udditaama: deftere woɗnde udditaama, nde woni Deftere Nguurndam: maayɓe ɓen ñaawe e geɗe binndaaɗe e defte ɗen, fawaade e golle maɓɓe. Nden geec yaltini maayɓe wonɓe e magge; maayde e jahannama yaltini maayɓe wonɓe e majje: ɓe ñaawee kala neɗɗo fawaade e golle mum. Maayde e jahannama werlaama nder weendu yiite. Ndee woni maayde ɗiɗaɓere. Kala mo innde mum heɓaaka winndaande e Deftere Nguurndam, werlaama nder weendu yiite.

Nii winndiraa, nii kadi waɗirtee, wonde wooday mooɓondiral ɗo maayɓe e nder Almasiihu immintintee, ɓe nguurɗi keddiiɓe ɓen nanngondiray e maɓɓe e nder duule, ɗum waɗay hade fitina Yaakuuba fuɗɗaade. Nden to fitina Yaakuuba timmi, immital peewɓe ngal waɗay, ngal woni seniiɓe fitina on e ɓe Alkawal Kiiɗngal maayɓe e gooŋɗinal, ɓe nguuri fawaade e foddeeji ɗin. No winndanaa Daniyel non, non laatortoo: Kono yah laawol maa haa ragare: sabi a fooftoto, a dara e ndonu maa to ñalɗi ɗin timmi. Nii kadi ragare ñalɗi ɗin woni timmoode asaweere cappanɗe jeeɗiɗiire Daniyel e fuɗɗoode laamu am duuɓi ujunere.

To laamu am duuɓi ujunere timmi, caggal nde Seyɗaane werlee e ndonu mum cakkitiingu e nder weendu yiite wulndu nduun, immital bonɓe ngal waɗay, hay ñaawoore jooɗorgal danewal mawngal nden waɗay, kala mo innde mum heɓaaka winndaande e Deftere Nguurndam werlee nder weendu yiite.

Nii kadi min, hay Iisaa Naasirankeejo, hay Jawgel Alla, hay Almasiihu Iisaa, mo woni kadi Muudo leñol Yahuuda, mi aray ngam mi ñaawee, mi haɓa e Liwiyatan, laamu makka ngun fof fusa sanne, ngam meeɗaa ummaade kadi. Nden dabba ka e annabiijo fenaande on werlee e wuurɓe nder weendu yiite, Seyɗaane haɓɓee jolokere mawnde nder ngaska luggu ka duuɓi ujunere, nii kadi laamu am duuɓi ujunere fuɗɗotoo, e nder peewal laamu am ngun wonirta. Ndaaree! Mi addii resraaɗo am e mooɓere laamɓe e liman'en ɓe mi laamodotoo leydi ndin e nder peewal, mi laamoto leƴƴi e sawru njamndi. Ngam maajum, njogoree yaakaare mawnde, ɓiɓɓe am, sabi timmoode kala huunde ina ɓadii, e duuɓi jeeɗiɗi fitina mawka ɗii, hay fitina Yaakuuba, ɗi njokkirtee laamu am peewngu, Yahweh laatotoo fof e nder fof.

Nii kadi mi rokkii on yaakaare mawnde, tokosɓe am, hay ngam on mbaawa nanngude hakkillaaji mon e weltaare wonnde yeeso mon sabi torraaji fitina Yaakuuba ɗin potndiraaka, wanaa ɗi poti siftoreede so yerondiraama e weltaare duumotoonde wonnde yeeso mon nden. Ndaaree! Yuuhanna Wahayuujo winndii: Ɓee ko yaltuɓe e fitina mawka, ɓe lootii comci maɓɓe, ɓe laɓɓini ɗi e nder ƴiiƴam Jawgel ngel. Ngam maajum, ɓe ngonii yeeso jooɗorgal Alla, ɓe ndewda mo ñalawma e jemma e nder suudu dewal makko: jooɗiiɗo e jooɗorgal on hoɗdoytoo e maɓɓe. Ɓe keɓataa heege kadi, wanaa ɓe njoorataa kadi, wanaa naange wulata ɓe, wanaa nguleeki goɗɗum. Sabi Jawgel ngel, wonngel e hakkunde jooɗorgal ngel, nyaamnoyta ɓe, ardoo ɓe haa e punaaje ndiyam nguurndam: Alla ittay go'ooji fof e gite maɓɓe. Ɓe laatoto Liman'en Alla, e Almasiihu, ɓe laamodoo e makko duuɓi ujunere.

Nii woni yaakaare mon, yiɗɓe am, ngam on mbaawa muñde haa ragare, yaakaare mon ko yaakaare duumotoonde, sabi El Shaddai, Alla Joomiraaɗo Baawɗo fof, tagay asama keso e leydi heseri ɗo peewal hoɗi, on ndokkee naatde e wuro seniiwo ngo Yahweh e hoore mum heblani, o pini hono no resraaɗo heblaniraa jom suudu nii, e lekki nguurndam ngin on ɲaamat. No winndiraa non: Foolɗo, ronay kala huunde, mi laatoto Alla makko, o laatotoo ɓiɗɗo am.

Ndaaree! Ko miin woni Yeshua HaMashiach, mi neldirii konnguɗi am e maccuɗo am, e maccuɗo am konnguɗi am ɗin neliraa.

Ndaaree! Linjiila duumotooɗo on feññinaama, neliraama e sahaaji mum potɗi, konnguɗi ɗii reenee, ɗi keɓay gite, e noppi, e ɓerɗe fof ɓe ɗi neliraa ɓen.

Ndaaree! Ndeenoo, woto accude nokkuuji mon, yimɓe am, sabi jamaanuuji ɗin njottake.
