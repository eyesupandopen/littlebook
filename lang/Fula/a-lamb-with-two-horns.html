Gila e YAHWEH: Ko Miin Wonɗo Mawɗo On!

Ndaaree! Njiilam mawɗam ummiima e Baabila Asiri, nannam aduna oo e nanngere mum waroore! Arde faamoya njiilam mawɗam, onon hoɗɓe e leydi, paamon kadi jamaanu mo ngonɗon, sabu njiilam mawɗam ina woni ɗoo, ngam aduna oo ina weltoo heen, sabu aduna maɓɓe ko fijirde, ardiiɓe maɓɓe ko fiyooɓe, hay e fenaaɓe. Kadi miin mi feññinan jooni goonga dow fijirde maɓɓe nde ɓe kuutorotoo ngam hoɗnude yimɓe heewɓe.

Keeɗee! Njiilam mawɗam arii e mon e maale e kaawisaaji fenaande keewɗi, njiilam mawɗam ngam halkude Yaquuba no feewi, hay e anniyaaji ɗi Isaa suuɗinoo e nder duuɓi, kono jooni ina feññinee, ina weeɓtee ngam kala yiyde. No ɓiɗɗo am, Iisaa Nasariŋke on, wiyrunoo baabiraaɓe maɓɓe e Yahuuda e nder juulɗeeje ɗee: Onon on ko e baaba mon Seytaane ummorii, kadi ko yiɗde baaba mon mbaɗoton. Kadi mi arii e mon, onon Isra'iila, ngam woto on nannge e njiilam mawɗam ngam, sabu ko onon woniɓe e nokku (hay so tawii aduna oo ina dilloo) ngam hol fowru waɗata pitamol mum yeeso colli?

Ko miin, Yahwe hay e Miin Wonɗo Mawɗo On, ngar-mi ngam rentinde yimɓe am e ɗee konnguɗi ɗi mi feññini, ɗi mi neli e nelal am e maccuɗo am mo mi halfini ngam ɗee golle. Nii woni, mi arii e ɓeen jeyaaɓe e leƴƴi ɗii, tawa ina ɓadii walla ina woɗɗi, tawa ina ngondi e leydi ndii walla ina caakii, mi mooɓtoyan kadi heddiiɓe am fof e Masiya maɓɓe e Kalfinaaɗo mawɗo, Yesuwaa Almasiihu.

Sabu ko kanko ardinta Isra'iila haa e kisal e juuɗe 144,000 am doole, sabu Iisaa Nasariŋke on mi waɗii Laamɗo laamɓe e Joomiraaɗo joomiraaɓe! Ko kanko kadi laamotoo aduna oo e nuunɗal e nder laamu mum duuɓi ujunere gila e jooɗorde Daawuuda to Isra'iila nde o ronani. Sabu ko noon mi fodannoo baabiraaɓe mon, kadi ko miin timminan kala fodoore nde mbaɗ-mi laabi keewɗi e nuunɗal am duumingal, ngam kala ko woni fof wonta eey e aamiin, kadi ko Miin Wonɗo Alla Nuunɗal Duumingal mo tampataa!

Kono noon, liingu mawngu ngun ummoto no feewi, daaba ka yaltunooka e maayo ngoo suuɗiima ngam ummaade no feewi e nder ɓeen ɓe ardotoo, o ummotoo e ndoondi e waktu mum toɗɗaaɗo. O heɓtii, o yuɓɓinii halkere Baabila Asiri haa laaɓi, o golliima kadi e daaba ka ummoriiɗo e leydi ngam yuɓɓinde e waɗde halkere maɓɓe, kadi mi accii ɓe mbaɗa ɗum. Aduna oo ina fija e debbo jeenoowo mawɗo oo, o waawaa miijaade aduna mo alaa e mum tooñannge debbo mawɗo jeenoowo oo, mo o tooñi yimɓe aduna oo, o bonni tagooje am e bonanndeeji jeenoode mum, hay e ko rokkiraa aduna oo e ndiyam kaŋŋe bonɗam wonɗam e juuɗe makko.

Nii woni, mi arii ngam rentinde on, onon Isra'iila, e njiilam mawɗam ngootaagu ngam naatnude aduna oo e wolde e caɗeele, hay e njiilam ngam ɓe njanngii e kuutoragol karallaagal maɓɓe, sabu ɓe fof ko fenaaɓe. Annabi maɓɓe fenaande naatata e leydi ndii e njiilam maɓɓe, hay no Masiya fenaande Isaa.

Isaa ina maska, ina suuɗoo e nder Isra'iila, hay ina wiyee ko kamɓe ngoni Isra'iila no ɓe njiɗiri jillondirde e Isra'iila, hay ina cuuɗa tigi-rigi maɓɓe e nder ɓeen ɓe njiɗi. (Ko ɓe alkama e nder maaro, Joomiraaɗo Mooɓoowo oo ittan ɓe haa laaɓa.) Nii woni, liingu mawngu ngun ummoto sabu ko waktu mum, Almasiihu Iisaa ittan goonɗinɓe e Almasiihu e leydi ndii (woniɓe e nguurndam e maayɓe), ndeen ñaawoore ari no feewi e Baabila Asiri, hay e debbo boɗeejo mawɗo oo mo Yuhanna Feññinoowo oo anndini duuɓi keewɗi ko adii.

Ko miin Wonɗo Duuɓi Mawɗi, hay e Joomiraaɗo Ruuhuuji, mi seediima, ngam kala faama haala am: Ko waɗnoo kadi waɗat: ko waɗaa, kadi waɗetee, alaa ko hesɗi les naange. Nii woni, ɓe ndartiiɓe ɓee ngolliima ngam halkude Aadama e leydi ndii gila e fuɗɗoode ngam heɓtude ɗum, hay e gese am nguurndam ɗo mi toppitotoo, mi reena yonkiiji yimɓe ngam mi hisna ɓe e bonannde e Almasiihu Iisaa mo mi tagi no Aadama gadiiɗo. Ko e Masiya mon, onon Isra'iila, Isra'iila Alla mooɓtindirtee, hisnee no neɗɗo gooto keso siforaa no Joomiraaɗo mon e hisnoowo mon, mo soodi hisnugol mon e ƴiiƴam mum kisnoojam.

Paamon onon Isra'iila wonde Iisaa Nasariŋke on ko Yesuwaa Almasiihu, mo woni kadi Almasiihu Iisaa (paamon tiiɗnaare inɗe makko sabu kisal mon timmii), nanee konnguɗi makko e ɗooftaare ngam hisnude yonkiiji mon, sabu on alaa waktu ngam haalde e tagɗo mon, kadi on alaa waktu ngam salaade Masiya mon goonga.

Ina winndaa no mi wiyri, kadi mi waɗi Yesuwaa Almasiihu feññini nelal mum Poolu dow makko: Ko ɗum waɗi, Elohim kadi toowni mo no feewi, o rokki mo innde ɓurnde kala innde, Ngam e innde Iisaa kala koppi fof ɓoftoo, ko woni e asamaan, e ko woni e leydi, e ko woni les leydi, Kadi kala ɗemngal seedoo wonde Iisaa Almasiihu ko Joomiraaɗo, ngam teddungal Alla Baaba. Nii woni, miin YAHWEH, Miin Wonɗo Mawɗo On, mi seediima kadi goonga ɗee konnguɗi ngam goonɗinal mon wonta ngo alaa ko dillini.

Njiilam mawɗam ina woni ɗoo, heewɓe nanngetee heen, heewɓe naatata e yiite, hay e ɓeen ɓe inɗe mum en mbinndaaka e Deftere Nguurndam Bawnel ngel gila e fuɗɗoode. Sabu, miin El Shaddai, ko miin annduɗo fuɗɗoode e sakkitoode, hay so mi rokkii kala yonki fartaŋŋe wuurdude nguurndam nuunɗuɗam yeeso am, mi anndi ɓe cuɓotooɓe hebbinde aybeeji mum en haa timma e ɓeen ngarooɓe e Almasiihu ngam ɓe tagaa e nuunɗal, kadi ñaawooje am ina laaɓi e nuunɗal am.

Kono onon Isra'iila, on alaa nuunɗal sabu on caliima Alla Nuunɗal Duumingal, sabu on caliima ɓiɗɗo am gooto mo mi neli ngam yaafagol aybeeji no bawnel Alla, ko ɗum waɗi aybeeji mon ina keddii. Nii woni, mi wasiyii on, Isra'iila, nanee konnguɗi Miin Wonɗo Mawɗo On e ndee waktu sakkitiindu, njaɓee sadak am!

Eey, Isra'iila, mi waɗanii on sadak laaɓɗo sabu Aadama bonnii e laaɓal, nguurndam ɓanndu ina woni e ƴiiƴam. Nii woni, aybe ina rewa e ƴiiƴam, woni nguurndam ɓanndu, gila e baaba haa e iwdi mum, tawa ko gorko walla debbo, ɓe ndona bonannde sabu Aadama bonnii haa abada. E yurmeende am duumingal e yiɗde am, mi waɗii ko waawaa waɗeede e kala tagofeere woɗnde: mi tagii, mi rokki ɓiɗɗo am gooto, mo Muusaa yamiri on ɗooftaade, ngam aybeeji yimɓe fof, tawa ko Yahuuda walla ko alaa e sago, tawa ko maccuɗo walla ko rimɗuɗo.

Kono noon, Isra'iila, añɓe mon ina njiɗi halkude on sabu ɓe njiɗi heɓtude jamaanu maɓɓe kaŋŋe, hay e jamaanu kaŋŋe ngo ɓe keɓtataa kadi, sabu e nokku jamaanu maɓɓe kaŋŋe mo ɓe cuɓii waɗde aybe, ɗo bonanndeeji kala sifaa njillunoo, ɓe keɓan ñaawoore tiiɗnde e lepte no laamu maɓɓe halkirtee haa laaɓi. Ina ɓadii waɗde daaba ka e annabi fenaande oo werlee e maayo yiite ngootaagu, Seytaane haɓɓee duuɓi ujunere e torra e kulol mawngol e ndonu mum ngarowu e yiite duumingal lepte ɗo liɗɗi mum en maayataa.

Liingu mawngu ngun ina haɓɓaa e leydi ngam woto ɓe naat e asamaan haa abada, kono ɓe ndesndaa les asamaan haaɓaa e leydi ngam ñaawoore nde ɓe njiɗnoo, liingu mawngu ngun ina jogii tikkere mawnde e foolde mum, sabu o foolaama no feewi e juuɗe Yesuwaa Almasiihu, mo woni aawdi fodaandi yumma kala guurɗo, sabu ko noon mi fodannoo Aadama e Hawaa e yiɗde am e maɓɓe. Nii woni, o acciraa tan timminde anniyaaji am duumingal hade makko neldeede e yiite, mi waɗan maande, ɓe ciftortaake, ɓe limtetaake, sabu ko noon woni ko arata e mon e fodoore ɗo on rimɗintee e tooñooɓe mon haa abada, kadi e jam duumingal e laaɓal ngonɗon so on ɓoftii koppi mon e Masiya mon goonga, hay e Yesuwaa Almasiihu, sabu alaa laawol goɗngol!

Nii woni, miin mi rokkata on faamde ko ɓadii waɗde, kadi pitamol mo fowru waɗi ina foti feññineede, hay e pitamol mo liingu mawngu ngun waɗi ngam nanngude colli am tedduɗi, hay e yimɓe am keeriiɗi ɓe mi reeni ngam am. Ko miin waɗi colli Nuuhu ngarti e kaakol oliwi e hunuko mum, kadi no noon, ko miin neli nanondiral am jam duumingal e yeeso Iisaa Almasiihu, Masiya mon ummitiiɗo, onon Isra'iila!

Nii woni, paamon jamaanu mo ngonɗon. Juulirde Seytaane ina dilla e maale e kaawisaaji fenaande keewɗi, e njiilam ɓe ngollirta. Ɓe mbaɗata aadaaji maɓɓe e yeeso yimɓe, ina cuuɗii seeɗa no welwelo e kabaruuji e mooɓondire politik. Ɓe timminii laawol cuɓagol maɓɓe, ɗo Isaa cuɓii jooni bawnel mum e nder juulirde Seytaane ngam njiilam maɓɓe bonɗam ngo ɓe njiɗi nanngude. Kono daaba ka ummoriiɗo e leydi ngoo wonaa bawnel Alla ngo ɓe njiɗiri, ngo ɓe njiɗiri, kono ko annabi maɓɓe fenaande mo ɓe cuɓii jooni, jogiiɗo galaaɗe ɗiɗi no bawnel, kono haalata no liingu mawngu.

Nii woni, mi waɗi ɓiɗɗo am, Iisaa Almasiihu Nuunɗuɗo, feññini e "Cuɓal ko Mon" (janngoyee ngam anndude) nde Barabbas heɓi gaññe e hoore mum no maayde nii, kadi ndeen kewu ina joginoo maanaa keewɗo mo o feññinaani e oon sahaa, kono ko e ndee mooɓondiral, hay e aada cuɓagol makko ɗo ɓe mbiyata, wonde Yahwe e hoore mum wuddi nofru makko, o waɗi mo maccuɗo am diine. Nii woni, mi woondii e innde am, hay e YAHWEH Miin Wonɗo Mawɗo On, wonde mi waɗaani mo maccuɗo am, kadi mi rokkataa Masiya goɗɗo so wonaa ɓiɗɗo am gooto, hay e Yesuwaa Almasiihu, mo woni bawnel Alla gooto mo nelan-mi. Nii woni, etaade warde mo kuutoraama no gooto e maale e kaawisaaji fenaande keewɗi ɗo oo fenaaɗo fena. Kadi oo fenaaɗo wonaa e sifaa am, wonaa e yimɓe am, kadi ko añam.

Kono, heewɓe e Baabila Asiri ɓe mbiyata Iisaa Almasiihu ko Joomiraaɗo maɓɓe, accii jooni Joomiraaɗo maɓɓe potɗo wonde ngam Barabbas, haaloowo no liingu mawngu, kono ko bawnel jogiingel galaaɗe ɗiɗi. Heewɓe e ɓeen waɗɓe Barabbas Joomiraaɗo maɓɓe accetee e yiite laɓɓinoowo sabu kisal maɓɓe timmaani, sabu ɓe ngaraani e Almasiihu no feewi e ɓerɗe goonɗinɗe e tuubooje, kono e yiɗde nde linjiila jawdi waɗata. Sabu juulirde Seytaane bonnii binndi maɓɓe ceniiɗi e jaɓgol maɓɓe, Siyonist en ɓe ngonti jooni. Kono heewɓe e ɗee yonkiiji, ndokketee teddungal maayde ngam hisnude so ɓe cuɓii jaɓde kisal e noddude innde Joomiraaɗo maɓɓe Iisaa Almasiihu haa e sakkitoode, mi jaɓan ɓe haa abada, kadi torraaji maɓɓe nandaani e teddungal ngo feññintee e maɓɓe.

Nii woni, mesiyaajo fenaaŋkeejo oo ina waɗa yah-ngartaa, ina waajoo, ina haalda no mboddi nii, ina waɗa hoore mum, ina haala geɗe mawɗe, ceeneendiije, hade mum ummitaade timmunde, tee o wiyata o ummoto. Yimɓe am, paamee wonde annabiijo fenaaŋkeejo oo, woni kadi kuullel leydi ngel, gollanngel juulirde Seytaane, suɓaama jooni ngam wonde jawgel maɓɓe. Kadi maa won ko hoore makko wayata no barminaande haa heddii maayde, kono barminol mum maayngol ngol sellinte, aduna oo fof maa haaɓa, rewa e kuullel ngel. Nii woni, oo jillinoowo maa ummo.

So o ummiima, o wiyata o sadakiniima hoore makko no jawgel Alla nii, o addanii Isra'iila jam no bammbal ngal addanta ɓe kaakol oliwiye! Wonaa Isra'iila tan, kono leydi ndi e hoore mum, o feeñirta no artuɗo ngam addude jam, kono o anndaa laabi jam, tee nde Baabila sirriijo oo ñaawee, hareeji mawɗi ngartat. Sabu ruuhu fenaaŋkeejo jam ummoriiɗo e mboddi ndi jooɗiima e leydi, min kadi mi neldii jiiɓru mawndu haa kala jiɗɓe bonannde ɓee ngoongɗina fenaande, ngam yo kala jiɗɓe bonannde ɓee njane. Sabu nde ɓe mbiyata "jam e kisal", e oon sahaa halkere tampere arat, ɓe ndifatataa.

Heewɓe e Baabila sirriijo oo, hay e gooŋɗinɓe fenaande makko, rewɓe konnguɗi makko moƴƴi e haala makko mbelka, njaltinaama, njaggaama e jam fenaaŋkeejo ɗo ɓe cikkata wonde nguurndam maɓɓe mbelɗam, nuunɗaaki ɗam, jokkoto, ɗo hannde wonata no hanki nii, janngo kadi wonata no hannde nii. Heewɓe maa coow, ngulla, njoole e nder woytaare wonde koolol maɓɓe ngol ittaama e maɓɓe. Ɓe kaɓdat e leñol maɓɓe haa maayde, e nder ɗuum fof, optere maɓɓe mawnde ndee yaltinee timmunde, hono no tabitinoowo am oo ittirtee e leydi nii. Baabila sirriijo oo maa yani e ñaawoore ɗiɗmere e nder waktu gooto, nde balaa'uuji keewɗi njani.

Juulirde Seytaane ndee maa heɓ hare mum ndee, ndee nde mbarata yimɓe potɓe e nder njiyaagu mum ngam wuurnude, rewɓe kuube maɓɓe ɓaleeje ɗee maa tampu e warngo, warngo ngo addiraa e anniya e njiyaagu mawngu, ɓe ummotoo e dow mum. Yooɓiiɓe ɓee maa peeñnina hoore mum en timmunde, ɓe ndokka yimɓe ballal ngal wayata no moƴƴal, kono laabi maɓɓe e karallaagal maɓɓe ngal ko ngam nanngude kala ɓe mbaawi e ndonu maɓɓe e nder yiite lepte, ngal ɓe njaltataa hay abada, ɗo ɓe keɓata laabi keewɗi e kala greki gooto ɓe mbaɗi, ñaawoore maa waɗee. Kono hol fettannde piilol tawa colli ina njiya?

Nii woni, miɗo rokkude on paamgol, wonde ñaawoore Baabila sirriijo ndee heblanaama, ɓe ndariima e heblaare. Kono arano, jawgel fenaaŋke ngel, jogiingel galleeje ɗiɗi, maa waɗ hoore mum no peewɗo, tawa ko ngaynaako bonɗo, gooto e galleeje ɗiɗi laamotooɗe, hay galaangel tokosel ngel Daniyel haali ngel. Maa o feeñir no o etinoo hisnude Baabila sirriijo oo e laɓɓinde bonannde mum, kono goonga ko kanko woni bonnoowo mawɗo, nannguɗo heewɓe ɓe o laɓɓinta jooni, o tuuma bonannde makko ndee, ndee nde o doolniri heewɓe e nder muñal. Jooni noon, kala ko yuumtanaaki anniyaaji maɓɓe, walla baawɗo seedtaade bonannde maɓɓe e hoore mum, maa itte e juuɗe oo ngaynaako bonɗo, tuumee grekiiji ɗi ɓe njokki e waɗde, ɗi ɓe ardii heen woɗɓe, ɓe ndifatataa.

Paamee kadi, wonde heewɓe e tawtoraaɓe bonanndeeji maɓɓe, wonɓe no ngaynaaɓe bonɓe nii, maa naat e nder buŋkeruuji mum en, cuuɗoo e nder ɓernde leydi ngam daɗde e tikkere aroore ndee. Ɓe mbonnat kala seedam nguurndam ngam ɗam ɓe mbaɗi e Baabila sirriijo e nder njiyaagu mawngu, ɓe ndifatataa e tikkere aroore ndee! Kono nii woni, grekiiji maɓɓe ɗii maa peeñninee e naafikaagal e juuɗe oo annabiijo fenaaŋkeejo, o feeñira no nuunɗuɗo, dariiɗo, hay neɗɗo neldaaɗo ummoraade e Alla ngam hisnude Isra'iila. Nii woni, pitamol ngol heblaniima waɗteede. Kono, kala gooto e golle maɓɓe ɗee mi winndiima e defte dow kammuuji, miɗo siftora e hoore am kala detal, miɗo anndi kala bonannde maɓɓe, mi peeñninat ɗe, ɓe njoɓat haa abada grekiiji maɓɓe bonɗi ɗii.

Nii woni, kuullel maayo ngel ina heblanoo hoore mum ngam ummaade no Mahdi maɓɓe nii, kuullel leydi ngel kadi ina heblanoo hoore mum ngam ummaade no jawgel fenaaŋke. Hol jawgel jogiingel galleeje ɗiɗi, haaldata no mboddi nii? Kono Esawu heblaniima jaɓɓaade mo e leydi ndi o yiɗi wujjude ndii, tee maa ɓe nduppitee haa laaɓa e juuɗe doole Yasuhuwaa HaMasiyaak mo mi semmbini oo, hay miɗo renndi e makko jappeere laamu am e Ɓiɗɗo am giɗaaɗo oo, o ronkat hoore mboddi ndii haa laaɓa, kala añɓe makko mbaɗee tuggorde koyɗe makko. Sabu ko yooɓiiɓe ɓee ngoni saliiɓe sujjude e yamiroore am e nder murtal maɓɓe, ko kamɓe njani e tagooje am e peeje e bonannde, tee maa ɓe njoɓ haa abada.

Nii woni, miɗo wondi e innde am kadi laawol gootol, wonde miin, YAHWEH Mawɗo On Mi Woni, miɗo wondi wonde Mesiyaajo gooto mo neldimi to Isra'iila, mo neldatmi goonga, ko ɓiɗɗo am jibinaaɗo gooto oo, Yasuhuwaa HaMasiyaak, mo baabiraaɓe mon njaɓaani, mo on njaɓataa sabu fenaande maɓɓe ndee, ndee nde ndonuno-ɗon e ɓerɗe maɓɓe tiiɗɗe, rokkaande on e nder Talmud mon ceeneendiijo oo. Nii woni, miɗo arde e mon e yiɗde am e mon, onon Isra'iila, ngam mi peeñnina on timmunde pitamol ngol Esawu, mo woni e yiɗde halkude Yakuuba, waɗani on.

Kuullel leydi ngel mesmeri on no annabiijo mon fenaaŋkeejo, tee maa o ummo, arda e mon no jawgel mon sadakinaangel, sadakiniingel hoore mum ngam Isra'iila, o waɗa hoore mum no Mesiyaajo mon goongaajo nii. Tawa goonga ko o polliraaɗo e Esawu, alaa Mesiyaajo ummotooɗo hay abada e Dan. Goonga, o aranii halkere Yakuuba, kono pitamol ngol o waɗata ngol, nanngat ko yiɗɓe rewrude e makko ɓee, ɓe njaltinee haa abada e fenaande makko, ɓe njaɓa maande makko ndee, ndee nde o yiɗata doolnude e aduna ngam maccinude ɗum e halkude ɗum.

Nii woni, miɗo wiya on, onon Isra'iila, cujjee e nder ɗooftaare e Mesiyaajo mon goongaajo, gartuɗo e mon no jawgel Alla nii, mo baabiraaɓe mon njaɓaani sabu Esawu. Cujjee e Joomiraaɗo mon Joomiraaɓe, Laamɗo Laamɓe, njaɓee kisal am, ngam mi soottitoo on e kuddi sariya, sabu alaa neɗɗo nuunɗuɗo garɗo e am rewrude e Muusaa, so wonaa neɗɗo biyeteeɗo Almasiihu Iisaa, mo mbaylu-mi haa abada, tee o anndaaka kadi e ɓanndu. Sabu mi teddinirii mo no Firawna teddiniri Yuusuf, renndi e makko jappeere laamu, so wonaa e jappeere laamu ndee o ɓuri Yuusuf mawnude, o reeni laamu makko.

Sabu:

Miin woni on gonɗo gila e fuɗɗoode haa e abada, alaa ɓurɗo Mawɗo On Mi Woni mawnude!

Miin woni on nelɗo ɗee Konnguɗi e yimɓe am giɗaaɓe, ngam mi arda e mon, mi itta wumre mon seeɗa ndee, nde ɓerɗe mon ngartani Mesiyaajo mon timmunde! Hay Mesiyaajo mon goongaajo, Almasiihu Iisaa, Joomiraaɗo ummitinaaɗo, teddinaaɗo oo!

Miin woni Alla Duumiiɗo Nuunɗal, mo tampataa, tee miɗo naamnoo, woodi ko saɗtani junngo am dooleengo ngoo waɗde?

Nii woni, miɗo ñaagoo on, onon Isra'iila, yo on ɗofto daande YAHWEH, hay Elohim mon, gonnooɗo ngaynaako baabiraaɓe mon e juuɗe maccuɗo am Muusaa, mo hisnu-mi nde o ittaa e ndiyam Nil haa o heɓi kisal e heblo laamɗo e nder galle Firawna, mo o fooli e nder ɗooftaare YAHWEH!

Miin woni kanko, hay YAHWEH Mawɗo On Mi Woni, wondiɗo e innde am, seedtinɗo timmunde e goonga duumiiɗo, wonde Yasuhuwaa HaMasiyaak timminii sariya Muusaa no Paska timmuɗo ngam kala wakkati, mo mi rokki timmunde e junngo am, alaa haaju kadi e sadak.

Miin woni YAHWEH, Mawɗo On Mi Woni, miɗo wondi e innde am wonde ko miin ummitini Iisaa Nasaraanjo oo e maayde, caggal nde o sadakini hoore makko no jawgel Alla nii, hay sadak mo njaɓ-mi ngam kala wakkati e haa abada ngam sottugol Aadama no neɗɗo keso e nder Almasiihu Iisaa, woni Yasuhuwaa HaMasiyaak, Mesiyaajo mon goongaajo!

Miin kadi woni on seedtinɗo goonga ɗee Konnguɗi ɗe neldimi e Linjiila Duumiika, peeñninaaka e am e hoore am, e Yasuhuwaa HaMasiyaak, e nelal amen dooleewal, heblanaangal e Yasuhuwaa HaMasiyaak e nder lepte keewɗe e laɓɓingol ngam anniyaaji am duumiiɗi. O artirii e deeƴre e Yasuhuwaa HaMasiyaak, o artiraama!

Nii woni, miɗo nodda on yimɓe am, hay Isra'iila mo njiɗ-mi e yiɗde duumiinde, yo on naat e Isra'iila Alla, ngare e Yahweh, hay Alla Ibraahiima, e Isaaqa, e Yakuuba rewrude e laawol Yasuhuwaa HaMasiyaak, ngam on mooɓondiree haa abada e Isra'iila Alla, ngartanee kam rewrude e Mesiyaajo mon goongaajo, hay Yasuhuwaa HaMasiyaak. Mi jaɓɓotoo on e hoore am, mi artira on e ndonu mon e njoɓdi mon wonndi e junngo dooleengo Almasiihu Iisaa.

Alaa innde woɗnde rokkaande nde yimɓe mbaawi ɓadtaade YAHWEH, Mawɗo On Mi Woni, sabu mi haaliima noon, mbufee ɓiɗɗo oo woto o tikka, on halka e laawol.

Ndaaree! Wakkatiiji ɗii ngari jooni, teeyol gadanol ngol ari, tee maa ɓe mooɓondiree e Joomiraaɗo maɓɓe, ɓe teddinee ngam anniyaaji am duumiiɗi.

Miin woni On Mi Woni, miin woni YAHWEH, ɗofto-ɗee daande am duumiinde, ngaree e Baaba Annoore, mo heblani on laawol mon.
