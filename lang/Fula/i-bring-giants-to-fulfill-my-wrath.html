Seedeeji ɗiɗi: Isra’iila
Ko Welɗi e ko Haaɗi
Setemburu 03, 2024

Mi addan Jaambaareeji ngam Timmingol Tikkere am!

Ndaaree, hiɓe ngara! Mi addan jaambaareeji ngam timmingol tikkere am! Nanee konnguɗi am, onon hoɗɓe e leydi, konnguɗi Yahweh, Alla mawɗo duumiiɗo on. Sabu no waynoo e jamaanu Nuuhu, non kadi wayrata: Jaambaareeji ina ngonnoo e leydi e nder balɗe ɗeen, gila e jamaanu Yareedi haa e jamaanu Nuuhu, ɗi taganoo, ɗi njahii e leydi ndii e nder bonannde mawnde. No wonirnoo nii, non kadi wonirta, jaambaareeji ɗii ngartan. E jamaanu Yareedi, reenooɓe ɓee njiippii e dow tulde Hermon, ɓe keewi teemedde ɗiɗi fof e jamaanu Yareedi ɓe njiippii gila e dow kammuuli. Ɓe kaɓɓondiri e kunondiral, hay hunayeere maɓɓe nde ɓe kaɓɓondiri ndee, nde wonti kuddi duumiiɗi wonande kala kaɓɓaaɗo heen, nde ɓe njaltataa hay abada, ko noon ɓe njooriri, ɓe majjini neɗɗaagu e nder grebuli mawɗi.

Reenooɓe ɓee, Seyɗaaneeji kuli ɓe, no ɓerɗe maɓɓe cuurii nii, ɓe naati e jarribo gila e darnde maɓɓe teddunde adannde ndee, nguurndam duumiiɗam ina njoginoo e nder kammuuli, sabu ko noon ɓe tagiraa. Kono ɓe njippii, hono no koode ndiamooje nii njippudi maɓɓe waɗiri, ɓe naati e Seyɗaaneeji haa abada. Nii woni Seyɗaaneeji ɗii kuli rewɓe jaɓɓe, ɓe njanngini ɓe no ɓe kulirta reenooɓe ɓee no ɓerɗe maɓɓe cuurii nii, yoga e rewɓe ɓee ndokkii reenooɓe ɓee hoore mum en e dow yiɗde, ñaawoore maɓɓe ndee ɓe njogoto. Woɓɓe nanngaama e doole sabu reenooɓe ɓee njippiima ardiiɓe maɓɓe adunaaru nduu adanɓe, ɓe njaahi ɓe nanngi rewɓe e doole gila e worɓe mum en e ɓesnguuji mum en, ko noon reenooɓe ɓee nanngiri kala ko ɓe cuɓii hono rewɓe maɓɓe, ɓe bonni heewɓe, e ndee dental haramneewal jibinaa jaambaareeji, hay jaambaareeji mawɗi adanɗi. Sabu reenooɓe ɓee kadi bonnii tagooje neɗɗaagu. Ko noon ɓe mbaɗiri grebuli e kulle ladde, e colli, e liɗɗi, e kala sifaa tagooje ɓalliije ɓe njillondiri, hay e nder muñal e wanyde kuulal ngal mi Yamiri, hay miin Yahweh, Alla Mawɗo Duumiiɗo oo! Gila e fuɗɗoode mi tagii leydi ndii e kala aawdi e sifaa mum, ko noon keeri ɗii njalti ɗi potnoo hay abada salteede, saltaade keeri tagooje ɗe Yahweh Yamiri ko addude fijirde, torra, e maayde e dow tagu Yahweh, kala baɗɗo ɗum maa jogoo ñaawoore mum. Nguurndam feewnaama no leggal jokkondirngal nii, ɗo nguurndam fof jokkondiri e jam e deeƴre, ɗo yiɗde laamii ñalawma, ɗo kala yimɓe njiɗata moƴƴere woɓɓe, wonaa koye mum en tan. Hay tagu ngu alaa heen cuuno e yiɗde bonnde ngam feeñde e maayde e bonannde, ko noon wonirta haa abada, saa'iiji timmal ngal ngarii.

Ko noon malaa'ika'en mawɓe nayo kammuuli ɓee, hono Mika'iil, Uriyel, Rafa'iil e Jibriil, ngaddi ñaawoore e dow ko wondiiɓe maɓɓe adanɓe mbaɗnoo e leydi ndii, ñaawoore ndee woppitaa, waɗaa e dow yaawre e dow kala tawaaɗo heen e dow leydi ndii e hoore mum, sabu aduna mo ngonnoo oon bonnii haa laaɓi. Nefilim en, hay sukaaɓe reenooɓe ɓee, mbaɗaama haɓde e goɗɗo e goɗɗo haa maayde e nder hareeji mawɗi keewɗi hakkunde leƴƴi gañondirɗi, ɗum ko sabu mi ittii jam hakkunde maɓɓe, ɓe kaɓi haa maayde, ɓe mbari goɗɗo e goɗɗo tawa baabiraaɓe maɓɓe (reenooɓe ɓee) ina ndaara bonannde tagooje maɓɓe ɓaleeje ɗe ɓe mbaɗannoo bonannde neɗɗaagu. Gila toon, reenooɓe ɓee kaɓɓaa e nder torra e ñaawoore, ɓe ceertinaa goɗɗo e goɗɗo duuɓi capanɗe jeeɗiɗi. No mi yiirunoo tagu am ngun mi yiɗnoo no feewi bonnirtee e bonanndeeji ɓaleeji, ko noon kadi mi waɗiri tagooje bonɗe ɗee mbara goɗɗo e goɗɗo ngam torra baabiraaɓe maɓɓe, ko noon mi yoɓiri, sabu miin ko mi nuunɗuɗo e ñaawooje am haa abada, miin ko mi Alla ñaawooje nuunɗuɗe, ñaawooje am njippataa, njahataa e timmal.

Ko noon mi addiri ilam mawɗam e dow leydi ndii, ilam ngam ina metti e jamaanu Nuuhu. Buruuji luggiɗɗi ɗii peccii, ndiyam yaltii e leydi ndii e doole, daaɗe kammuuli udditaama, ndiyam dow asamaan ngam ilnaade leydi ndii, hay dow tule ɓurɗe toowde, ngoɗɗini nguurndam fof e leydi ndii, so wonaa yimɓe njeetato hisɓe e laana ndiyam, sabu Nuuhu tawaama ko nuunɗuɗo yeeso am. Ko ɗum waɗi mi ƴetti Nuuhu, mi Yamiri ɗum waɗde laana ndiyam no mi Yamiriri nii e juuɗe am. Mi addani ɗum kadi kulle ɗe hisata ɗee, ko noon Nuuhu waɗiri muñal e waɗde kala ko o Yamiraa, ilam ngam ari no holliraa, no wiyraa nii, sabu ko miin Yahweh, Alla Mawɗo Duumiiɗo, mi wiyi, hay gooto waawaa waylude, waawaa haɗde hay seeɗa!

Ko noon kadi Enok, binndoowo adanɗo oo, siftorii maayde ɗeen jaambaareeji, sabu miin Yahweh, mi wiyi: Jooni noon, jaambaareeji ɗi ngoni e ruuhuuji e ɓalli, maa noddiree ruuhuuji bonɗi e dow leydi, e dow leydi wonata hoɗorde maɓɓe. Ruuhuuji bonɗi njaltii e ɓalli maɓɓe; sabu ɓe jibinaama e worɓe, e Reenooɓe ceniiɗi woni fuɗɗoode maɓɓe e iwdi maɓɓe adanndi; ɓe ngonaa ruuhuuji bonɗi e leydi, ruuhuuji bonɗi ɓe noddirtee. So tawii ko ruuhuuji kammu, e kammu wonata hoɗorde maɓɓe, kono so tawii ko ruuhuuji leydi ɗi jibinaa e leydi, e leydi wonata hoɗorde maɓɓe. Ruuhuuji jaambaareeji ɗii ina torra, ina tooña, ina mbonna, ina kaɓa, ina mbaɗa hareeji, ina mbaɗa bonannde e leydi, ina ngadda caɗeele: ɓe njaɓataa ñamri, kono ina njoomta, ina njoomta, ina ngadda bonanndeeji. Ɗiin ruuhuuji maa ummo e dow sukaaɓe worɓe e dow rewɓe, sabu ɓe njaltii e maɓɓe.

Ko noon kadi, e sahaa potɗo, Nuuhu jippii e dow tulde Ararat, ɗo laana ndiyam ndee darii nde ndiyam ngam ustii. Mi naati e nanondiral e Nuuhu, e nder sahaaji taaniraaɓe Nuuhu puɗɗii yaltude e nanondiral ngal mi waɗnoo e Nuuhu, ko ɗum waɗi ruuhuuji bonɗi puɗɗii haɓde e maɓɓe. Ɗiin ruuhuuji, ɗi keewi, njippii sukaaɓe ɓiɓɓe Nuuhu, ko noon Nuuhu ñaaŋii ɓe, mi kaɓɓi jeenayi e sappo e ɗiin ruuhuuji bonɗi e nder luggeere, ɗo ɓe ndesndaa gila e oon sahaa haa hannde. Ko noon mi Yamiri, ko noon Nuuhu jannginaa safaarooji maɓɓe fof, e njooram jannginaa, ngam Nuuhu waawa safraade ɓe e leɗɗe leydi. Ko noon wootere e sappo e ɗiin ruuhuuji acciraa yaltude, ngam ɓe mbaawa majjinde e bonannde ɓiɓɓe worɓe ɓe njiɗata fenaande maɓɓe. Ko noon ruuhuuji heddiiɓe ɗi kaɓɓaa ngam ñaawoore e nder luggeere ɗii maa njaltine e jamaanu torra Yaakuuba, nde luggeere ndee udditoto haa laaɓi, jeenayi e sappo e maɓɓe ɓe kaɓɓaa ɓee maa njaltine ngam waɗde laabi maɓɓe bonɗi e bonɗi hono feccere e ñaawoore neɗɗo tagɗo ngo woni ɗoo, ngo yaltata e dow leydi ndii e dow ɓuuɓri, e bonannde mawnde!

Ko noon kadi Kanaan, taaniraawo Nuuhu mo Nuuhu huɗi sabu bonannde mum, waɗi grebuli mawɗi (kadi) e ganndal malaa'ika'en njippiiɓe, e laabi Seyɗaane o naati e yiɗde, o ardinaama e yiɗde, o waɗi kadi bonanndeeji caggal ilam Nuuhu. Ɗeen bonanndeeji njippii leyɗeele Shem ɗe ndokkaa e dow ndonu, ɗe Kanaan huɗaaɗo oo nanngi, o wujji hoore mum, ɓe noddiri leydi mum wujjaandi ndii leydi Kanaan. Heewɓe e iwdi mum bonndi ndii ko tagooje genetik waylaaɗe, ɗe njippii haa laaɓi leyɗeele alkawal, ɗe mi neldi Muusaa ngam mooɓtude sukaaɓe Isra'iila gila e leyɗeele Misra ngam ƴettude ndonu mum en e bonɓe ɓee. Ko noon kadi Yuusuf waɗiri hareeji mawɗi e ɗeen bonanndeeji no winndiraa e binndi ceniiɗi nii, ɓe ƴetti e ɗeen bonanndeeji ndonu mum en potngu, ko noon winndiraa hono nate ko arata. Sabu no noon kadi ɓiyam, Yeshua HaMashiach, mo mi waɗi Laamɗo laamɓe e Laamɗo laamɓe, arata ngam ƴettude ndonu mum e juuɗe jooɗiiɓe e wujjooɓe, sabu jooɗorde maɓɓe ko haram. Ɓe ngonaa laamɓe, ɓe ngalaa laamu, kono maayo mawngo ngo ina wula e yiite e sulufara woni ndonu maɓɓe duumiiɗo.

Kono, Isra'iila muñnaaki haa laaɓi e noddaango mum, heddiiɓe e jaambaareeji ɗii acciraama jooɗaade. Kono hay e nder muñal maɓɓe, mi haɗii ɗeen bonanndeeji, mi bonnii heewɓe e maɓɓe e leydi ndii gila e oon sahaa. Mi riiwi heewɓe e maɓɓe les leydi, heewɓe e maɓɓe naati e leyɗeele ɗe anndaaka caggal galaas ɗo neɗɗaagu yahataa no haanirta nii. Faamdee wonde neɗɗaagu faamataa leydi ndi o tagiraa e ndi o hoɗi ndii, sabu neɗɗaagu ina ñamma fenaande ndi soodaa e goonga, ndi feewnaama hono jaŋde, ko noon neɗɗaagu woniri e yiɗde majjere wonde e konngol Alla kammuuli ko adanɗi, leydi ndii ina darii e ndiyam e nder ndiyam: Ko ɗum waɗi aduna mo ngonnoo oon, ilnaaɗo e ndiyam, bonnii: Kono kammuuli e leydi, ɗi ngoni jooni ɗii, e konngol gootol ina ndesndaa, ina ndesndaa ngam yiite haa ñalawma ñaawoore e bonannde worɓe bonɓe.

Ko noon kadi, e nder sahaaji, mi ittii jaambaareeji e leydi ndii e yurmeende am e moƴƴere am e yiɗde am hisnude neɗɗaagu e bonannde, heddiiɓe e maɓɓe ina njaajii e dow leydi ndii, ina mbonna tagu am hono seedeeje nguurndam maɓɓe. Hay heddiiɓe e siwilisaasiyoŋaaji maɓɓe adanɗi ina ndarii hono seedeeje wonde ɓe laaminoo laamu leƴƴi e leydi ndii e jamaanu maɓɓe biyeteeɗo jamaanu kaŋŋe, hay e jamaanu Nuuhu hade ilam. Mahdiiji maɓɓe mawɗi ina njaajii hono seedeeje e dow maɓɓe, nguurndam ɗeen mahdiiji ina ndarii hono seedeeje e dow fenaande Seyɗaane e hoore mum, mo golli no feewi ngam suuɗde goonga ngam o heɓa haa laaɓi hareeji mum ɗi o haalataa. Ko noon mi haalanirta ɓe noppi ngam nanaade, gite ngam yiyde, e ɓernde ngam janngude ko arata hade mum waɗde, ngam ɓerɗe maɓɓe njahataa e ɓuuɓri e keefeere, kono ngam ɓe ngannda goonga, ɓe mbaawa heblude ko arata ngam ɓe hisa, hay kala nodduɗo innde Iisaa Almasiihu haa e cakkital maɓɓe.

Nii woni ko yamiraa, nii kadi wontiri e timmal wakkatiiji, wonde mi yaltinii reenooɓe ɓe e kasooji maɓɓe ɗo ɓe kaɓɓanoo e nder torra e lepte, gooto heen fof kaɓɓanaa ko seerndi e yummiraaɗo mum. Nii woni ɓe yaltinaa caggal nde wakkatiiji kaɓɓital maɓɓe timmi: haa e duuɓi cappanɗe jeeɗiɗi. Taariika waawataa suuɗde yaltugol maɓɓe haa laaɓa, kono kadi keewɗi, musiibaaji, e kewuuji goɗɗi mbaɗii e aduna hee fof e yaltugol maɓɓe, ɓe njani e fuɗnaange, ɓe naati e murto Sayɗaane, timmal, laawol goɗngol kadi, sabu nii ɓe kaɓɓiri koye maɓɓe haa abada e nder hunayeere maɓɓe laananaande ndee, tee e nder weendu yiite ndu hunayeere maɓɓe ndee timminirtee haa abada, sabu ɓe kaɓɓaama e lepte Sayɗaane e ndonu mum haa pooma.

Nii woni ɓe njuɓɓinii koye maɓɓe e wakkati dokkaandi ndii, e mbaadi deeƴndi gila e duuɓi keewɗi jooni. Tigi rigi, ɓe ngadditii kadi aybeeji maɓɓe, tee e gunndoo ɓe njuɓɓinta koye maɓɓe, ɓe ndarii ittugol daaƴoowo oo, nde ɓe ngardata kadi e feere ngam taƴde keeri ɗi mi waɗani ɓe ɗii, sabu ko ɓe murtooɓe tum. Hay so tawii ɓe kaɓɓaama ngam timminde anniyaaji El Shaddai, sabu ko miin woni Alla Jom Baawɗe oo, ɓe njiɗat moƴƴitinde yonta kaŋŋe maɓɓe oo, ɓe keɓa leydi ndii ngam koye maɓɓe, ko ɗum woni ko waawataa wonde; sabu leydi ndii ko Joomiraaɗo jeyi ɗum e timmal mum, tee tiitoonde leydi ndii ko ɓiy am, Yesua HaMasiyaak, soodi ɗum, tee e juuɗe mum mawɗe o rokkaama ɗum ndonu duumotoongu, omo feññinana ɗum timmal e goonɗinɓe, noddaaɓe, cuɓaaɓe, e daɗndaaɓe.

Nii woni kadi Sayɗaaneeɓe ɓee e reenooɓe ɓee njuɓɓiniima koye maɓɓe, tee ɓe njarliima no feewi ittugol daaƴoowo oo, sabu e nder ngool ittugol laamu maɓɓe nguu yaltintee timmal, leydi ndii wonta kadi no ñalɗi Nuuhu nii. Ndaaree! Miɗo yamira, miɗo adda ɓe: yimɓe mawɓe ina ngara ngam timminde tikkere am, ina njesa, ina njanfa kadi, tee mi accat ɓe njoñeede e leydi ndii laawol goɗngol kadi ngam timminde anniyaaji am. Ɓe njaltat e dow muusu-muusuuji ɗo ɓe cuuɗinoo, ɓe cuuɗiima e gure maɓɓe les leydi, ɓe cuuɗiima kadi caggal galaas mo ɓooyataa wayleede oo, tee e aduna yimɓe ɓe accete ngam naatde, wujjude, e foolde, tee Liwre ndee ardotoyoo ɓe sabu ko ɓe geɗal laamu mum. Kono tan ko e dumunna seeɗa, hay e yontere cappanɗe jeeɗiɗaɓere Daniyel, yontere duuɓi jeeɗiɗi nde ñalawma heen fof wontata hitaande, nii woni wontata duuɓi jeeɗiɗi. Tee ɓe njoñat no feewi, tee ɓerɗe yimɓe ina keɓa kulol sabu gite maɓɓe kulɓiniiɗe ɗee e mawnude maɓɓe so yerondiraama e neɗɗo tagoore.

Ndaaree, ko miin woni MIIN MAWƊO oo, nelɗo Konnguɗi am rewrude e maccuɗo am, tee rewrude e maccuɗo am Konnguɗi am neliraama. Nii woni miɗo haalana on geɗe hade majje waɗde ngam yimɓe am mbaawa heblude, ɓe paama ko arata, ɓe keɓa kadi hakkilantaagal sabu ɗee geɗe ɓooyataa waɗde. Tee kala noddoowo innde Yesua HaMasiyaak, woni Iisaa Almasiihu e ɗemngal Pulaar, hisay.

Nii woni kadi tikkere mawnde ndee e ñaawoore mawnde ndee no fodaaka gila ko ɓooyi nii, jooni ko ɗum fawii ngam waɗde e leydi ndii timmal, tee wakkatiiji ɗii ko jooni, ko nii Alla Mawɗo Duumotooɗo oo yamiri! E nder duuɓi yurmeende timmuɗi, linjiila kisal e gunndoo mawka Alla (ka feññinanoo Nelaaɗo Pool) heewii leydi ndii, yettii haa e pelle leydi ɓurɗe woɗɗude. Hay so tawii heewɓe no feewi kisaama, hay e kala gardo e Iisaa Nasarankeejo oo e tuubugol timmungol, waɗi mo Joomiraaɗo mum duumotooɗo, wakkatiiji ɗii njettii ngam timmal sabu aadee kadi salike tagɗo ɗum oo. Tee miin, El Shaddai, woni Alla Jom Baawɗe oo, ko miin daaƴnooɗo bonannde heewnde ngam ronooɓe kisal ɓee mbaawa heɓde nguurndam duumotooɗam e nder Almasiihu Iisaa, Joomiraaɗo maɓɓe ɓamteteeɗo oo. Nii woni jooni miɗo yamira ɗee geɗe hade majje waɗde, sabu ko miin woni Yahwe, MIIN MAWƊO oo! Nii mbi’on ɓe MIIN MAWƊO oo hunorii wonde e mooɓondiral sukaaɓe am goonɗinɓe e ɓiy am, nde ɓe ɓamtete ngam hawrude e mum e henndu, e nde daaƴoowo oo ittaa, laamu Sayɗaane nguu fof yaltintee e leydi ndii, leydi ndii wayloyete.

Miɗo yamira e nder ñaawoore e dow hoɗɓe e leydi ndii wonde mi yaltinay laamu Sayɗaane nguu timmal, tee aybeeji maɓɓe fof njaltay e leydi ndii, tee ruuhuuji ɗi mi haɓɓunoo e lugge ɗee kala e ñalɗi Nuuhu ɗii, yaltintee e leydi ndii, sabu wakkatiiji ceerungal sakkitiingal e ñaawoore sakkitiinde njettii e leydi ndii, tee ñalawma ñaawoore mawnde ndee fawaama. Nii woni kadi laamu Sayɗaane nguu rokketee laamu e dow leydi ndii, sabu e nder nuunɗal am, aadee yiɗii no feewi yo laamte ɗum e juuɗe Sayɗaaneeɓe ɓee, hay e goonɗinde fenaande mum wonde ko o alla moƴƴo, kadi ko o annoorɗo anndinoowo, wonde mi rokkata jooni aadee ko o yiɗi no feewi koo.

Tee yaniiɓe ɓee njoñaa timmal ngam ñaawoore maɓɓe, ɓe mbaɗii kadi aybeeji maɓɓe. Tee ɓe kaɓɓaama e leydi ndii e liwre ndee, sabu nii woni wakkati maɓɓe, tee ɓe mbaɗoytoo koye maɓɓe ko no aljanna en nii, hay e no tagoore moƴƴere ummoriiɓe e adunaaji janani. Kono miin, Yahwe Alla Mawɗo Duumotooɗo oo, mi hunorii innde am, sabu mi waawataa hunoraade ko ɓuri ɗum toowde, wonde alaa adunaaji fenaande pirlooje e nder weeyo ngo alaa ko woni heen, kono dow leydi ko kammuuji, hay e kammu kammuuji woni kammu ɓurngu toowde, ɗo Alla ɓurɗo toowde oo jooɗii e laamu mum duumotoongu e jappeere mum, tee alaa ko suuɗotoo yitere am.

Ndaaree! Ɓe puɗɗiima anndinde koye maɓɓe, tee geɗe maɓɓe e kuule maɓɓe ina taari kala nokku, tee leydi ndii ina wayloo no feewi jooni. Tee no neɗɗo tagoore salorii nanondiral am jam nii, woni ɓiy am bajjo, Iisaa Nasarankeejo oo, nii kadi mi salorii neɗɗo tagoore, mi accat ɗum laameede timmal e juuɗe añɓe mum, tee ɓe njiɗi halkude ɗum. Kono, salagol am ngol wonaa timmungol, wonaa kadi sakkitiingol, sabu jamaa mawɗo hisay e nder torra Yaakuuba, hay e kala noddoowo innde Joomiraaɗo, woni Iisaa Almasiihu, haa timmal. Sabu kisal e nder torra Yaakuuba waawataa heɓeede e laawol goɗngol. Nii woni ceerungal sakkitiingal ngal fuɗɗotoo, tee kala mo mi yamiri nguurndam duumotooɗam gila hade fonndaasiyoŋaaji leydi mbaɗeede, heɓay nguurndam duumotooɗam, tee mi umminay ɓe e nguurndam kesam, tee njoɓdi mawndi ɓe keɓay.

Nii nanee konnguɗi am, ndartinee ɓerɗe mon e konnguɗi am anniya e bayyinaango, sabu wakkatiiji Jentil en ɗii timmii jooni, tee tikkere e ñaawoore e feññinagol mawngol ina ngondi e leydi ndii jooni, sabu miin, hay e El Shaddai, hay e Alla Jom Baawɗe oo, ko miin woni jooɗiiɗo toowɗo oo, tee alaa ko suuɗii yitere am, tee mi yahay jooni ngam hisnude timmal kala ronooɓe kisal, tawa miɗo seernda ɓe e leyɗinaaɓe e halkere duumotoonde. Nii woni alkamaari ndii seerndete e conndi ndii haa abada, tee kala jeyaaɗo e murto Sayɗaane rokketee ñaawoore, tee wakkatiiji ñaawoore maɓɓe ɗii fawaama ngam yaltude e leydi ndii.

Nanee Konnguɗi am, paamon caɗeele ko arata koo, sabu ñalawma Joomiraaɗo ko ñalawma niɓɓere, tee holi waawi daraade?

Miin, Yahwe, Alla Mawɗo Duumotooɗo oo, miɗo yamira ɗee geɗe hade majje waɗde, tee wakkati alaa kadi. Kala yiɗɗo arde, yo ar yo yar e ndiyam nguurndam duumotooɗam, tee noddaango am e nguurndam duumotooɗam ina darii, tee kala noddoowo innde Joomiraaɗo haa timmal hisay.

Miin, Yahwe, Alla Mawɗo Duumotooɗo oo, mi neldii ɗee Konnguɗi rewrude e maccuɗo am, tee rewrude e maccuɗo am ɗee Konnguɗi neliraama!

Wakkatiiji ɗii ko jooni!
