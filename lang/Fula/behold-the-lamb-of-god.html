Seedeeji ɗiɗi: Israyiila
Ko Welɗi e ko Haɗɗi
Noowammbar 16, 2024

Ndaaree! Jawgel Alla!

Kettinee Konguɗi Yeshua HaMashiach, weeɗitanee konnguɗi am, sunninee ɓerɗe mon ngam jaɓde Mesiya mon goongaajo, onon Israyiila, faamee haala am mbele mbaawon heɓde ñeeñal e laabi mon, mbele kadi njahdon e am mi fotinana on koyɗe mon, mi wayla laawol mon gila e laawol halaguyee ngol rewdunooɗon, fayde e laawol ciiɓungol hisndam ngol woni laabi am e ko mi woni. Sabu ko miin woni on mo Yahweh neldi ngam hisndam mon, miin kadi ko mi iwdi rewɓe ndi fiydata hoore mboddi ndii, miɗo nodda on e am onon musiɓɓe am, yoo, leñol am ngol mi wonani soottorɗo. Mi yamirii on sunninee ɓerɗe mon, accoon gilli mon ɗi ngalaa nafoore, mbele mbaawon rimeede e nder Mesiya mon goongaajo. Kettinee siisiiɗe am ɗe ndokkaaɗon ngam ngoonɗinon ɗe, mbele mbaawon wuurdude yeeso Yahweh mawɗo oo, miin woni oo, so on njaɓii miin woni Mesiya mon duumiiɗo, goonga goonga: ko miin woni.

Hono no Yahweh neldirnoo sariya Muusaa e baabiraaɓe mon (sariya wonnooɗo jannginoowo mon fayde e Almasiihu), noon kadi Yahweh neldirimmi hono no fodaano gila adan, ɗo o fodinoo hisndam baabiraaɓe ɓe njeyaaka e sukaaɓe ɓe kaaldataa mi hannde ɓee, hay sukaaɓe Israyiila ɓe njiɗir-mi yiɗde nde meeɗaa fuusde. Kono e nder majjere e keeferaaku, baabiraaɓe mon njaɓaani mi, ndokki mi e juuɗe worɓe bonɓe mbele mbaɗana mi tanaa e konnaagu mawngu, geɗe ɗe sariya haɗnoo ɓe. Kadi mbele peeñira fotaare yeeso yimɓe leydi ndii nde ɓe mbari Jawgel Alla ngel e dow yamiroore baabiraaɓe mon, bonannde nde heewɓe e mon haa jooni njaɓiri e majjere.

Kono, faamee Israyiila, wonaa doole añɓe am e añɓe mon nanngiri mi. Faamee, mi nanngaaka e doole, mi nanngaaka e feere, alaa kaɓirgal mbaɗaangal e am mbaawngal dañde. Sabu noon Yahweh haaliri, ko Yahweh haali, hay gooto waawaa waylude! Goonga, Israyiila, ko miin woni on kaalannooɗo Yahuuda Iskariyoos nde o yeeyannoomi coggu kaalisi capanɗe tati, "ko mbaɗataa koo, yaawno waɗde!" Sabu coggu ngorgu nguu jom maccuɗo yoɓetee so ñagaari mum wartii maccuɗo mum, ko nguurndam am yeeyaama e juuɗe ɓe tuppimmi ɓee, rokkaama hay e juuɗe mawɓe Imaam en e Sanhedrin ɓe kaɓdirnoomi sabu bonanndeeji maɓɓe e naafikaaku maɓɓe. Nii woni Yahuuda yeeyannoomi e juuɗe Esawu, on mo jogitii leydi fodanoondi ndii e doole, hay so tawii Esawu yeeyinoo ndii ko e dow buuru. Kono miɗo jaɓi e yiɗde am yo añɓe am nanngam, tawi miɗo waawi dogde seeɗa, goonga goonga, so mi torike Yahweh, o rokkaynoo kam ardungal konuuji malaa'ikaaji ko ɓuri sappo e ɗiɗi, kono no Binndi ɗii mbaawirta timmude? Nii woni timmi.

Kono noon woniri, paandaale am e laabi am paamaaka e leñol am e yimɓe ngenndi am ɓe njiɗir-mi yiɗde duumiinde, luggunde, wulnde, nde waawaa ñifde, nde waawaa foolde. Sabu ko miin woni Hayre mon Cosal, nde aldaa e mum, hay mahoowo gooto waawaa mahde, nde keefeero en nduppatnoo. Sabu ko e yiɗde am on, e Aadama fof mo foolaa e laamɓe tooñooɓe ɓee, mi rokki hoore am e juuɗe añɓe am ngam soottude on e kuddi sariya.

Nii woni yamirde am duumiinde ndee, yo on njiɗondir, hono no njiɗir-mi on. Alaa yiɗde mawnde ɓurnde ndee, so neɗɗo rokki nguurndam mum sadak ngam sehilaaɓe mum, ko ɗum waɗ-mi e yiɗde am, miɗo anndi so mi ɗoftaaki yiɗde Baaba am, aadee fof waawanno riiweede haa abada. Kono ko miin woni hisnoowo Aadama, on sootti aadee e ngonka mum tooñiika, so tawii neɗɗo arii e am e dow koppi mum, seedii mi woni Joomiraaɗo mum. Goonga, ko miin woni Mesiya mon goongaajo, onon Israyiila.

Faamee musiɓɓe am, mi haalaaki hay huunde e hoore am nde Yahweh winndaani e dow alluhaaje kaaƴe, nde o haalniraani e daande maccuɓe makko annabaaɓe, mbele winndee ngam goonɗinal e ɗooftaare ronooɓe fodal, ɓe on njeyaa e mum en, onon Israyiila, so on calminaaki ɓerɗe mon hono no baabiraaɓe mon mbaɗirnoo e jeereende nde ɓe njuumtunoo Yahweh. Nguurndam maɓɓe keeferaaku timmiri e nder jeereende e nder meere, ɓe ndonaani fodal ngal, kono ko sukaaɓe goonɗinal ɓee!

Kono e nder wumre mon, on ngoonɗinii fenaande Esawu, wonde ko mi fenoowo. Tigi rigi, Esawu jaɓaani mi sabu omo yiɗi jogitaade ndonu ngu o yeeyannoo miniraaɗo makko, alaa laawol tuubgol Esawu, hay so o ɗaɓɓiri ngol e gonɗi. Baabiraaɓe mon paamaani paandaale am goongaaje sabu ɓe njiɗnoo ko hooreejo adunaajo ngam liɓde ɓe ɓe ndokki hoore maɓɓe e ley njiimaandi maɓɓe, mbele ko mbaawi jogaade darndeeji maɓɓe e nder yimɓe. Kono mi neldaama ngam hisnude aadee e juuɗe tooñooɓe ɓe Aadama naatunoo e majjere mum e nder ɗooftaaki yamirde Yahweh sabu peeje mboddi ndii, nde fenni mo e yumma wuuruɓe fof nde ɓe mbaɗi junuuba e Eden gila puɗɗoode. Hay so tawii ngenndiiji keewɗi ummiima, njibiima, laamuuji keewɗi ummiima, njibiima gila e sahaaji sadak am, Israyiila ina heddii e njiimaandi añɓe mum haa hannde, ina gollande paandaale Esawu, mo yiɗi halkude ɓe haa laaɓa.

Eey, Israyiila heddiima e njiimaandi sabu ngenndi mon ittaama e mon ngam fawde on sabu warde ƴiiƴam laaɓɗam, hay warde e yiɗde e konnaagu neɗɗo laaɓɗo mo Yahweh neldi ngam sottorgol mon. Ko miin, Yeshua HaMashiach, nde waktu timmungu yettii, Alla neldi Ɓiɗɗo mum, jibinaaɗo e debbo, jibinaaɗo e ley sariya, mbele o sootta ɓe ngoni e ley sariya ɓee. E nder nguurndam am laaɓɗam e ɗooftaare, mi holliri hoore am ko mi Jawgel Alla e nder sadak mettungo, bonngo, nde mi rokki hoore am Imaam mawɗo haa abada e dow darnde Melchizedek.

Golle ɗee timmii, mi jooɗiima e nyaamo Baaba am, hono Yahweh, mawɗo oo, MIIN WONΙ. Mi ronii kala huunde, e juuɗe am doole ɗee, kala huunde rokkiraama e nder asamaanu e leydi haa añɓe am laatoo teɓteɓel koyɗe am, kono laamu am ngu alaa battane! Laamu ngu laamɗo mon laamoriima nguu, ina arda e timmoode torra Yaaquuba, ɗo mi halkata laamu añɓe, mi adda ñaawoore mawnde e batte e dow tooñooɓe ɓee, ɓe naata e lepte sabu bonanndeeji maɓɓe keewɗi, ñaawoore maɓɓe duumiinde nde alaa fowtere hay seɗɗa. Yoga e maɓɓe naatat e weendu yiite wulndu nduu e jayngol e nder artugol am ɗiɗaɓol ngam laamoraade aduna, nde daaba ka e annabi fenaande oo njaɓɓetee e mayru ɓe wuuri. Seytaane en kaɓɓete e callalal mawngal ngal ɓe kaɓɓirtee e nder torra e kulol mawngol, nde ɓe kaɓɓaa e hulɓere e ko woni yeeso maɓɓe e nder ndonu maɓɓe duumiingu lepte haa abada. Hay ñaawoore hulɓiniinde nde Seytaane waawaa daɗde, ɗo o kaɓɓaa, o werlee e yiiteeji ɓurɗi wulde haa abada e timmoode waktu makko seeɗa oo.

Sabu ko miin woni on rokkiraaɗo hisndam Yaaquuba, hay iwdi fodanoondi rewɓe ndii nde Aadama goonɗini e yaakaare, o umminoyte e nguurndam kesam haa abada. Sabu ko miin woni on neldaaɗo ngam sottorgol Aadama, mi rokki ɓatton am cakkitiingol ngol yoorii, ngol leydi boɗeeri ndii yari e Calvary, hay ɗo mi haalunoo konnguɗi duumiiɗi ɗii: "Timmii". Nii woni, ko miin woni on e nder yiɗde am Yahweh e yiɗde am yimɓe banndam, rokki hoore am e sadak e nder mettam-badee, hersa, e torra, mi foolii junuuba e maayde, ñalawma ina arda ɗo kala huunde bonnde fuusata.

Mi waɗii ɗum e yiɗde am, e yiɗde am so mi salii, ngam teddungal ngal wonnoo yeeso am, mi waɗii ɗum miɗo yawa hersa fof mo wonaano am. Kono e yiɗde am on, mi jogitii hersa mon e lepte mon e dow ɓaawo am, hay nde ɓe ceerti ɓalli am e yi'e am e boɗi maɓɓe. Eey, mi jogitii hersa mon e lepte mon e nder tooñannge e konnaagu worɓe bonɓe ɓe tooñooɓe njiimi, nde ɓe mbari mi e nokku mon e majjere, ɓe paamaani e laabi maɓɓe ɗi ngalaa hakkil, wonde so ɓe mbarii Jawgel Alla, ɓe ciiforii ko hoore maɓɓe yiite haa abada, sabu maayde waawaa jogoraade mi, sabu mi meeɗaa waɗde junuuba, hay laawol gootol, hay laawol gootol, goonga mi waɗataa hay abada!

Eey, Israyiila, mi lomtii on e nokku lepte, mi lomtii baabiraaɓe mon ɓe njaɓaani mi e majjere e dow yamiroore Esawu! Kono no mbaawirno-ɗon sottoreede so wonaa e fenaande maɓɓe keeferaaku? Sabu ngam sottude Aadama, ko ƴiiƴam laaɓɗam tan mbaawi timminde ko sariyaaji duumiiɗi, potɗi ɗii ɗaɓɓi, sabu so ƴiiƴam yoorii, alaa yaafuya junuuba. Nde tawnoo Aadama tagaama ko laaɓɗo, timmuɗo e ñalawma nde o tagaa ndee, noon kadi miin, Yeshua HaMashiach (Jawgel Alla), mi tagaama ko mi laaɓɗo, timmuɗo ngam sottorgol aadee timmungol. Sabu so ƴiiƴam ga'i e mbordi, e coondi nagge, nde fofirtee e dow tuunɗuɓe, ina laɓɓina ko fayti e laɓɓingol ɓalndu, haa teeŋti noon ƴiiƴam Almasiihu, mo rokki hoore mum e Ruuhu duumiiɗo, mo alaa gaññeeje e Alla, ina laɓɓina hakkillaaji mon e golle maayɗe, ngam rewde Alla guurɗo oo?

Nii woni, miɗo rokka on faamde wonde mi lomtii on e nokku fawaade e lepte, mi waɗii ɗum e yiɗde am, tawa hay gooto doolaani mi; hay so tawii mi torike e nder gese Getsemane so tawii woodii laawol ngoɗngol, kono wonaa yiɗde am, kono ko yiɗde Yahweh waɗee. Nii woni e nder hersa am, mi yaltinaama e damuɗe wuro mawngo ngoo, (hono Yerusalaam, wuro waroowo annabaaɓe) ɗo mi neldaa e leñol am, toon comci am peccitaa, ɓe mbaɗi kural! Eey, toon e Calvary, caggal nde mi muñii waktuuji keewɗi lepte ɗe ngalaa battane, ɗe mi alaa heen, toon mi fenaa e juuɗe worɓe bonɓe, ɓe mi alaa heen junuuba hay seɗɗa. Kono e yiɗde am, mi haɓɓaa, mi pikaa e juuɗe am e koyɗe am ngam mi fawaade e dow lekki nguu e nokku mon, lepte ɗe wonaano am. Hol nde rokkaa laaɓɗo waɗde e yiɗde mum, e hersa, lekki njurum e nder hersa e mettam-badee mawngo ngam hisndam musiɓɓe mum tooñooɓe, ngam o saftaade e nokku maɓɓe, kono e yiɗde am on mi waɗii noon. Faamee, kala wakkati miɗo waawi dartinde ɓe so mi naamndii Baaba am, o rokkaynoo kam ko ɓuri konuuji malaa'ikaaji sappo e ɗiɗi e dow yamiroore am, kono mi naamndaaki, mi suɓii ko muñde timmude e yiɗde am lepte mon ɗe wonaano am. Nii woni e yiɗde am mi rokki hoore am ngam mon, mi waɗtaynoo ɗum kadi, hay so tawii noon wonaa dole, Yahweh jaɓii sadak am laawol gootol, fof, haa abada, ɗum noon alaa sadak goɗɗo heddii ngam junuuba.

Ko ɗum waɗi, Israyiila, faamee yiɗde, muñal, yurmeende e moƴƴere nde mi hollirta on e ngonka mon hannde ka jippagol e salagol Mesiya mon goongaajo, miɗo ara e mon e ndee sahaa cakkitiinde e yiɗde am on nde meeɗaa fuusde, nde meeɗaa foolde, so tawii tan on njaɓii Mesiya mon goongaajo. Sabu ko miin woni on jaɓɗo nokku mon e nder fawaade, lepte, e warde junuubankooɓe, sabu njoɓdi junuuba ko maayde, kono miin ko mi laaɓɗo haa abada, miɗo waɗa yiɗde Baaba am e kala sahaa, hay yeru mo njaɓataa e nder ɗooftaaki mon Yahweh e yiɗde mon, e nder salagol mon Mesiya mon goongaajo.

Ko waɗi mi salaa? Haa Pontius Pilat mo laaminoo Yahuuda e oon sahaa oo, fellitnoo yo o woppam, o haali goonga e dow fenaanɓe am ɓee, haa e ɓiɓɓe Esawu ɓe njaɓɓunoo Baabiraaɓe mon ɓee yo ɓe ewno mo, o woppana ɓe Barabas! Haa ɓe ɗon ewna kadi e daande wootere “Mbarde mo!”, ɓe njaɓaani Pilat yo o woppana ɓe Mesiya maɓɓe goongaajo oo e ƴiiƴam laaɓɗam ɗam ɓe calii ɗam. Sabu o annduno no feewi wonde ko e dow añamɓe ɓe njowiri mi. Kadi, nde o jooɗii e jooɗorgal ñaawoore ngal, debbo makko neldi e makko, wiyi, “Woto naat e geɗe neɗɗo oo potɗo: sabu mi tampii no feewi hannde e koyɗol sabu makko.” Ko noon wonnoo, ko noon kadi winndaa e binndanɗe laaɓtuɗe ɗee so on keɓii goonɗinal ngal on ngoonɗinta seedanfaagu am nguu.

Fof ko sabu Baabiraaɓe mon njiɗnoo geɗe aduna oo, ɓe njiɗi nokkuuji maɓɓe hakkunde yimɓe ɓee, ɗo ɓe mbaawi wuuraade e dow zakkaaji e sadakaaji yimɓe ɓee, ɓe ndara no annabaaɓe fenaaɓe potɓe, ɗo ɓe njanngiti hakkilantaagal Esawu e noppi yimɓe ɓee: hakkilantaagal fenaande ngal mawɓe maɓɓe teddinnoo sanne ɓurngal daande e hakkilantaagal Yahwe, MIIN MAWƊO oo, mo ɓe calii nde ɓe calii, ɓe mbardi e hoore maɓɓe Iisaa, gorko Nasara.

Ko noon woniri, e salaare mon ndee, haa e am, Iisaa gorko Nasara, mi fayi e jeyaaɓe, ɓe nani daande am fotde saha, heewɓe, heewɓe ɓe ngonnoo alaa e hoolaare, jooni keɓii kisal e nder Joomiraawo maɓɓe, haa e jamaa mawɗo mo mi ummintinta e wuurɓe ngam hollitde Yahwe e mooɓondiral mawngal seniiɓe ngal feerii waɗde. Heewɓe ummoriiɓe Yahuuda e heewɓe ɓe njanfaama ɓee, maantiniraama ngam kisal timmungal. E musiɗɓe mon ɓee, haa e ɓe laamu worgo ɓe maantiniraa ngam kisal duumingal, heewɓe e maɓɓe jooni maantiniraama haa abada, ɓe ummintee e nguurndam kesam. Goonga, goonga, ɓeeɗoo fof accii ma e misaalu tuubugol yo a jokku, so a ɗoftiima daande Mesiya goongaajo gooto mo Yahwe meeɗaa nelde so wonaa Iisaa gorko Nasara, Mesiya mon goongaajo!

Sabu ko e dow bonanndeeji maɓɓe leƴƴi worgo ɗii njanfaama hakkunde leƴƴi ɗii, ɗo ɓe ndesaa, ɓe ndeeniraa ngam kisal, haa e juuɗe cemmbinɗe Yahwe. Kadi ko noon winndiraa e juuɗe Nelaaɗo am Pol nde o haaldunoo e Roomanaaɓe duuɓi heewɗi ɓennuɗi: “Min nganndi geɗe fof na cooftana moƴƴere yimɓe yiɗɓe Alla ɓee, haa e ɓe noddaa e faandaare makko ndee. Sabu ɓe o anndunoo gila adan, o fellitiino kadi yo ɓe nanndine e natal Ɓiɗɗo makko, ngam o wona ardiɗo e musiɗɓe heewɓe. Kadi, ɓe o fellitii ɓee, ɓe o noddi kadi, ɓe o noddi ɓee, ɓe o laaɓini kadi, ɓe o laaɓini ɓee, ɓe o teddini kadi.” Ko noon e nder Isiraayiil Alla mooɓondiral leƴƴi majjuɗi ɗii timminirtee, Isiraayiilnaaɓe heewɓe keɓii kisal e annditande mi Joomiraawo maɓɓe, haa e am, Iisaa gorko Nasara, e jamaanu am mo yurmeende e kisal ɓe keɓi oo.

Hay so tawii jooni on njogiima golle am e nder mbonki, haa e golle am ɗe kisal ɗe mi timminii e leƴƴi ɗii e yamiroore Yahwe e jamaanu am mo yurmeende oo, mo gasii jooni; anndee ko Yahwe laɓɓini e laawol ƴiiƴam Yesuwa HaMasiyah, woto inniree ko heewi walla ko tuundi! Miin, Yesuwa HaMasiyah, mi ñaagiima on yo on njokku laabi musiɗɓe mon, woni ɓe koɗdiiɓe walla woɗɗuɓe, ɓe ngari e am e tuubugol timmungol ngam mi jaɓɓoro on, mi artira on e Yahwe, sabu jom Ɓiɗɗo oo ina jogii Baaba oo kadi, kono jom alaa Ɓiɗɗo oo alaa Baaba oo kadi, ko noon Yahwe holliri!

Ko noon mi fayi e yummiraaɓe am kadi no mi fawtorii leƴƴi ɗii e ngol waktu cakkitiingu ngol, ceerungal cakkitiingal maa waɗ jooni e nder yontere cappanɗe jeeɗiɗiire Danyel ndee, ngal ari ngam laɓɓinde aduna oo e yiite am laɓɓinoowo, Esawu maa seertine e Yaaquuba haa abada. Sabu Esawu yeeyii ndonu mum, Yahwe jaɓii suɓngo makko haa abada ngam yo ngo won suɓngo duumingol, sabu ɓiɓɓe fodoore ɓee ko ronooɓe foddeeji ɗii, wonaa ɓiɓɓe ɓanndu. So on njeyaaɓe Almasiihu, ndeen onon ko on iwdi Ibraahiima, on ronooɓe e fodoore. Ko noon foddeeji ndonu ɗe Yahwe e hoore mum waɗi ɗee, mbaɗiraa e goonɗinal e ɓe mbaɗanaa ɓee, sabu potɗo oo maa wuuru e goonɗinal! Nde foddeeji goonga ɗii ndokkaa, ahdiiji mbaɗaa, maande fodoore ndokkaa, ahdiiji ngonti wasiyaaji e ƴiiƴam wullaam, ahdiiji ɓooyɗi ɗii fof e foddeeji Yahwe fof, gila e ɓurɗi famɗude haa e ɓurɗi mawnude, naatii e nder Wasuyee am Keso mo fodoore, ɗo tagoore ndee waɗetee potnde haa abada e nder artirde duumingal e Baaba Annoora, mo alaa e mum mbayliigu, alaa kadi e ɗowdi pirlitagol.

Mi arii e yummiraaɓe am e yiɗde e deeƴre mawnde, mi arii e mon e yiɗde am e mon, mi yiɗaa hay gooto e mon yo halku e nder ngoongɗingol. Goonga, goonga, heddiiɓe maa ndesee e wuurɓe ummoriiɓe e Yaaquuba, haa e nder tampere Yaaquuba, woni yontere cappanɗe jeeɗiɗiire Danyel. Ko ɗum waɗi, mi ñaagiima on musiɗɓe am, nanee daande am, sabu ko ngol woni waktu cakkitiingu, ngol woni ceerungal cakkitiingal hade tikkere woppiteede e timmal, hade am arde ngam halkude laamu gaño oo fof, sabu e potal mi arii no Raawaandu leñol Yahuuda, mi arii ngam ñaaawde e haɓde!

Kala garɗo e am e oon sahaa, an yo Isiraayiil, haa e kala mo mi yamiri jibinaaɗo e Baabiraaɓe ɓooyɓe yo nana daande am, e kala noddoowo innde am cemmbinnde haa timma ummoraade e leƴƴi ɗii, maa hisu, ɓe hisinee e tolnooji maɓɓe. Leƴƴi ɗii maa keɓ teddungal nanndungal e am nde mi rokkii hoore am ngam bonanndeeji aadee, ɓe mbaree no seedeeɓe nde ɓe ndokki innde am kisnoore haa timma. Ɓe naata e teddungal mawngal haa abada, njoɓdi maɓɓe nduumoo ngam darnde maɓɓe, e ɗooftaare maɓɓe ɓe foolirta laabi maɓɓe ɗi ngoongɗingol e ɗi nguli ɗii, haa e yiite.

E Isiraayiil, musiɗɓe am, miɗo ara ngam hisinde on, mi arana on ballal e 144 000 am, haa e ɓe laɓɓinnoo hoore maɓɓe fof no foolɓe am e nder ƴiiƴam am laɓɓinoojam e hisnoojam e ñalawma maɓɓe kisal, ɓe mooɓondiree e am e mooɓondiral mawngal ngal feerii waɗde e leydi. Sabu mi ardoto 144 000 am ɓee ngam jippaade e dow tulde Siyon, ɓe njokkondira e am taƴre e taƴre no ɓe ardori heddiiɓe am Yaaquuba feewde e nokku kisal e nder ladde, ɗo Yahwe heblani ɓe nokku kisal, ɓe ñammintee, ɓe ndeenetee, ndaaba-ngaawla, mbeewa, e annabi fenaaŋke oo meemataa ɓe, tawaataa ɓe kadi, e heddiiɓe ɓe onon ngonɗon ɓee, an yo Isiraayiil. Kono Barabas maa addan tuundi mbonndi ndii, lekki keewki maa laɓɓine e leydi ndii, mi suuɗana heddiiɓe am e wolde ñalawma mawɗo oo, ñalawma garol am ɗiɗaɓol ngol Yahwe maantiniino gila e duuɓi duumingal, ngol tabitinaa e limlebbi makko kisal, ñalawma oo ina ɓadii no feewi, sabu ko noon winndiraa, ko noon kadi waɗetee.

Ko ɗum waɗi, yummiraaɓe am ɓe njiɗmi haa abada, miɗo jogorii teddinde hakke mon mo Alla rokki on ngam suɓaade laawol ngol njahaton. Ko ɗum waɗi miɗo naamnoo on, e nder laawol yaajngol feewde e halkaare, ngol feewde e yiite ɗe ñifataa, ɗe mbaɗanaama mbonɓe ɓee, on nduumoto? Woni, on ɗoftoto daande Mesiya mon goongaajo oo, haa e makko mo rokkunoo ɓulde kala e ƴiiƴam mum laaɓɗam e nder torra e mette mawɗe ngam on mbaawa wuuraade yeeso Alla mon, haa e Yahwe MIIN MAWƊO oo? Ee, Yahwe mo wullini gonɗi keewɗi e mette mum ngam baali majjuɗi galle Isiraayiil, haa e kala saliiɗo daande makko e daande am. On njokkondirta e am e ɗooftaare mon e Mesiya mon goongaajo? Woni on njokkondirta e Barabas mo Esawu neldi ngam halkude on?

Nanee Konnguɗi am an yo Isiraayiil, faamee suɓngo ngo njogiiɗon ngoo, faamee miɗo doŋngude on feewde e am, mi neldan seedeeɓe am ɗiɗo cemmbinɓe, mi yamiro on yo on nane ɓe ngam on keɓa seedanfaagu maɓɓe, sabu ɓe ceedoto geɗe suuɗiiɗe keewɗe ɗe cuuɗanoo ɓerɗe yimɓe, kono ɗe ɓe peeññinta, seedanfaagu maɓɓe nguu dara haa abada. Ko ɗum waɗi, nanee daande ɓiɗngel baali goongaajo ngel, an yo Isiraayiil, sabu kisal e rimɗineede alaa e innde woɗnde meeɗnoonde rokkireede, goonga, goonga, hay innde woɗnde rokkirtaa ngam ndee faandaare, so wonaa Yesuwa HaMasiyah, mo woni kadi Iisaa gorko Nasara, hay so tawii e nder ɓanndu mi anndaaka kadi, miin ko mi Almasiihu Iisaa, haa e makko Joomiraawo Joomiraaɓe e Laamɗo Laamɓe.

Miin woni Raawaandu leñol Yahuuda, e potal mi arii ngam ñaaawde e haɓde.

Miin woni nelɗo ɗee Konnguɗi e maccuɗo am, e maccuɗo am ɗee Konnguɗi neliraa, miɗo seedtoo, miɗo seedtina wonde Konnguɗi Linjiila Duumingal ɗi peeññinaama e juuɗe am, e juuɗe Yahwe e hoore mum, ko goonga, ko duumingal, maccuɗo am haalii e goonga Konnguɗi ɗi peeññinaama e mum e waktu mum, ɗi ndeenetee e nder masiibaaji garooji ɗii, ɗi tawa gite, noppi, e ɓerɗe ɓe neliraa ɓee.

Miin woni Mesiya mon duumingal e duumingal, jiɗɗo kisal mon e rimɗineede mon, Isiraayiil, goonga, goonga miɗo heɓanii hoore am kala jaabiiɗo daande am, on naata e Laamu Duuɓi Ujunnaaje, on ngona leñol keeriiɗo, on nguura nguurndam duumingam, on tawa huɗo duumingol hakkunde Isiraayiil Alla, meeɗa on seertineede e laamu potngu Laamɗo mon, Joomiraawo, Limaam Mawɗo, e Mesiya, haa abada on ardoree e jam e potal, on mawna no ga'i e nder damal.

Miin woni Yesuwa HaMasiyah, miin woni ko MIIN MAWƊO oo waɗi mi, miin woni ɓiɗngel baali Alla ngel rokkaama no juulde mon timmunde, laaɓtunde haa abada.

Hol ɓiɗngel baali ngel cuɓoton, an yo Isiraayiil?
