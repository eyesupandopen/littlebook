Seedeeji Ɗiɗi: Eklesiya
E nder Golle Yuhanna Baptista
1 Abriil, 2024

E Mi laaɓinaali on?

Deƴƴee, ndaaree e deeƴre, nganndon ko miin woni Joomiraaɗo nodduɗo on ngam ndaron yeeso am e laaɓal e nder kala laabi mon. E mi laaɓinaali on? E mi waɗaali on fonnditiiɓe e am, Joomiraaɗo Iisaa Almasiihu? E on nanndinaaka e am e nder torraaji am e e nder teddungal am? Ko miin woni Joomiraaɗo Joomiraaɓe e Laamɗo Laamɓe, mi fenataa. E Binndi Ceniɗi ɗii bayyinaali ɗum? Ñiiɓee e hoolaare e fonnditaare, ndaaree e deeƴre e nder ɓerɗe mon, nganndon ko miin, Joomiraaɗo Iisaa Almasiihu, mi rokki on yaafuya timmuka e deeƴre yeeso am so tawii on nanii konnguɗi am, njaɓon kadi jannde am.

Hakkilantaagal ko huunde mawnde; ko ɗum waɗi, ndaaree hakkilantaagal — wonaa hakkilantaagal aduna ngal wontata meere, kono ko hakkilantaagal Joomiraaɗo. Nduttoɗee les juuɗe am baawɗe. Sabu hulde Joomiraaɗo ko woppude bone e yahrude e fonnditaare yeeso am, wiyi Joomiraaɗo baawɗo oo, eey, Joomiraaɗo Iisaa Almasiihu. Nanoyee daande am! Nanoyee daande am, wata on nanoy goɗɗo! Daandeji keewɗi ina kaɓa e Konngol am, ina fenana on, nde tawnoo ina ɗaɓɓa laamu e dow pittaali mon ngam waɗde on coftal so on njokkii ɗi. Wata on njiɗ aduna, ɓiɓɓe am, wata kadi on njiɗ geɗe wonɗe e aduna. Ndee aduna bonka ina yaha e joofnirde, miin Joomiraaɗo, mi haalii ɗum, mi deƴƴintaa sabu keeferaaku mon, mi deƴƴintaa kadi sabu ŋakkeende goonɗinal mon. Alaa, mi wulliran dow sanne ngam mbaawon anndude goonga konnguɗi ɗi kaal-mi ɗii. Nanndinee ɗi e Binndi Ceniɗi. E wonaa miin woni Konngol ngol wonti ɓanndu, hoɗi e mon? E mi rokkiraali on neema e Ballo haa ngar-mi?

Anndee, ɓiɓɓe am, ko mi soodiri on ƴiiƴam am, leydi ndii ko yam jeyi, ɓooyataa fonnditaare laamoo e leydi. Mi haalii ɗum. Mi rokki on nguurndam e am; tan wata on njañu laabi am. Mi rokki on anniya; tan wata on njañu nguurndam ngam noddi-mi on. Mi rokki on noddaango, eey, noddaango toowngo ngo woni duumingol, ngo alaa joofnirde so on njuurii e am. Njuuree e am e yeeso udditiingo e ɓernde, njaɓon mi laɓɓina on e hakkeeji mon e bonanndeeji mon e nder ƴiiƴam am kafaruuji. Kala mo njiɗ-mi, mi felat, ngam mbaawon daraade yeeso am e fonnditaare e nder comci daneeji laaɓɗi ɗi ndokkat-mi on ko ɓooyaani.

Kala comci mon ko paɗe tuunɗuɗe ɗe cuɓidaniɗon koye mon ngam suuɗaade mi. Kono alaa nokku ɗo neɗɗo fof waawi suuɗaade gite am, miɗo yi'a on no ngonirɗon tigi-rigi, e no tagir-mi on keso e am. Njoɓdi mawndi ina resanaa ɓiɓɓe am koolniiɓe; kono wonande jañooɓe mi, mi jañay on. Hol ko waɗi hiɗon kaɓɓii e ndee aduna maayoowo e caɗeele mum e muuseeki mum? Hiɗon njogii cuɓagol e am, cuɓagol ngo yontaaji goɗɗi njogaaki hade sadak am kafaruujo. Ko ɗum waɗi, kala jañoowo mi e sahaaji kesɗitingol, ina jogii hakke ɓurɗo mawnude. Yiɗde am e mon ko duumingol, waawaa helleede. Baaba am e am ko gootum, anniyaaji amen e peeje amen ngenndataa. Baaba on rokki mi kala huunde ngam mi hisna yoga.

Wata on jooɗo e nokku keeferɓe, no ndokkir-mi on dalillaaji keewɗi ngam goonɗinal mawngal. Goonɗinal nanndungal e gabbel mutardiir ko kañum tan cokliɗon, tan hoolaare seeɗa. Ɓoorno-ɗee comci ɗi ndokku-mi on. E mi wippaali ruuhu am e mon, onon sikkitiiɓe am? Wata on won keeferɓe, kono tan ngoonɗinee, nde tawnoo sahaaji e jamaanuuji ngarii e mon, alaa ruttagol. Aduna mo anndunoo-ɗon on meeɗataa artude, aduna mo anndu-ɗon hannde on ko aduna njiyaagu mawngu. Hay so tawii geɗe ina nanndi haa jooni e mon, kala ko joginoo-ɗon nafoore ko bonɗo oo nanngi; sabu omo jogii waktu toɗɗaaɗo, waktu mo so mi ustoraano, hay ɓanndu wuurtaano. Hol ko waɗi hiɗon njiɗi ñalawma Joomiraaɗo garoowo e aduna ngam etaade ɓerɗe yimɓe? Ko ñalawma niɓre ɗo hay gooto waawaa daraade. Ko ñalawma kiita e batte bonɗe, hay e ñalɗi ɓurɗi niɓɓude, kala noddoowo innde Joomiraaɗo haa laaɓi, hisan.

Sahaaji e jamaanuuji ina mbayla yeeso gite mon, kono haa jooni on ngoonɗinaa gite mon. Hay so miɗo waɗa maandeeji e kaawniiɗi e mon e hakkunde mon, hiɗon udda gite mon, hiɗon faɗɗa noppi mon e miijooji keeferaaku. Moƴƴere am e yurmeende am ko duumiiɗe gila e yonta haa e yonta, kono sahaaji ina mbayla, kiita e musiiba ina ɓadtoo aduna mo njiɗ-ɗon no feewi oo, geɗe ɗe njogii-ɗon nafoore ɗee ina njiiloo e juuɗe mon no njaareendi nii. Ɓooyataa aduna oo ronkata noddaango moƴƴere e yurmeende, kono hisooɓe ɓee fotaani muñde haa poofɗol mum en gadiingol ngam hisde e am. Njuuree e am, njaɓon noddaango am, sabu wonde e Almasiihu ko jogaade jokkondiral hisinngal e am, Joomiraaɗo Joomiraaɓe e Laamɗo Laamɓe. Ko on tan mbaawi haɗde hisnugol mon so on ngoppii mi e keeferaaku, on njañii noddaango am. Miijito-ɗee Esawu, yeeyɗo ndonu mum sabu mburu tokooso werlaaɗo e yooro, rokkuɗo miñiiko ndonu mum e keeferaaku ŋakkuɗo goonɗinal. Faaleede konnguɗi am e yahde e niɓre ko hakke, ko ɗum waɗi wata on waɗ hakke, ndaron yeeso am.

Hol ko waɗi hiɗon njiyra koye mon ko on fotanaa moƴƴere am, tawa ko jaɓde tan foti waɗde? Jaɓee golle am kafaruuje e nguurndam mon, njaɓon mi naata ngam wallude on e sahaaji mon cokli, so tawii tan ɓerɗe mon keefeerje ɗee njaɓii mi. Suɓo-ɗee nguurndam, sabu hannde ko saha suɓagol; nanoyee daande am, wata on tiiɗin ɓerɗe mon e ladde no baabiraaɓe ɓee mbaɗiri, njippii e sara laawol. Wata on njaltu e am; wata on njokku mbonka e laabi bonɗi keeferaaku ŋakkuɗo goonɗinal, kono ndaaree e am, koolo-ɗee daande am. Eey, baali am ina nana daande am, ko ɗum waɗi njokee mi haa kisal. Wata on cikki ɓernde keefernde waawaa daraade laaɓtinaande yeeso am, sabu keeferaaku ko jañugol goonɗinal ngal ndokku-mi on e am.

Ceninee mi, Joomiraaɗo Iisaa Almasiihu, e nder ɓerɗe mon, wata on kule, sabu feeñde am ina ɓadii, ina ɓadii sanne. Koolo-ɗee daande am, njahon e batteeji am, sabu laawol ngol mbeli-mi on ngol ina yahra e nguurndam kesam e nder wonnde Alla. Sabu ko miin tan woni laawol fayde e Alla; ko miin woni laawol, goonga, e nguurndam. Hay so bonɗo oo fodi geɗe keewɗe, o waawaa rokkude hay huunde, o waawaa kadi hisnude hoore makko e juuɗe am. Alaa, tikkere mawnde ina resanaa mo, Seytaani ina jogii ndonu garoowu e suɓagol mum e nder ndiyam yiite ngoo. Kala suɓiiɗo mo, kala nannguɗo maande mum, kala dewoowo kullel ngel e nate mum, jokkay mo e ndonu mum tikkere Alla ndu alaa joofnirde.

E Baaba am fonnditaaki e reende tagu mum? E o fonnditaaki e kiitaaji makko e laabi makko ɗi o weerti yeeso mon? Ko ɗum waɗi, suɓo-ɗee nguurndam, suɓo-ɗee kadi ndonu nguu ndokku-mi on, sabu miɗo foda asamaan keso e leydi kesiri ɗo fonnditaare hoɗi. Miɗo foda on nguurndam duumingol, nguurndam keewngal, deeƴre, e weltaare. Kono, e nder ŋakkeende goonɗinal mon, hiɗon kaɓa haa jooni. Hol ko mbaɗ-mi on, mi mettini on haa on njañii jokkondiral e am, ɓiy Alla, e Baaba teddungal? Suɓaade ndee aduna e laabi mum ko suɓaade maayde, haa ragare suɓagol kala neɗɗo wonan joofnirde, nde tawnoo sahaaji senndindiral ngarii; ɓe suɓaaki ɓee, pellital waɗanee ɓe.

Alaa weltaare e mbonka garoowa, mo ɓooyataa heɓtude aduna mon. Musiibaaji tago ina keewi, leydi ndii ina dimmba, kure mawɗe ina njana e Babilon Sirri; sabu leydi ndii sekete e nokkuuji keewɗi. Yiite mawngo heɓtan leydi ndii, yeeyooɓe ɓee ndaran woɗɗude sabu kulol torra makko. Mbonka mawka ina woni e debbo jeenoowo oo, Babilon mawɗo, mo njarini aduna oo e jeene mum e bonanndeeji mum, sabu omo joginoo giɗiraaɓe heewɓe. Ɓe ngenndataa e makko ɓee, ina keɓda e kiita makko, ko ɗum waɗi hol no mbaawir-ɗon suɓaade maayde e dow nguurndam ngam ndokku-mi on, soodiraaɗam ƴiiƴam am ceniiɗam? Hare ina ɓadii arde timmunde e leydi mon e ngenndi mon, hay so tawii ina jokki haa jooni — eey, hare nde bayyinaaka nde haɓata e mon — ɓooyataa nde yaaja e aduna oo fof so maandeeli ɗii udditaama.

Hare mawnde e torra mawɗo ina ara e leydi so ɓe etii halkude kala huunde jeyaaɗo e Alla e Almasiihu mum so ɓe bayyinii hare ceniiɗe e mon. ɓe nanngan heewɓe, heewɓe nanngete, hisooɓe ɓee ina njogii cuɓagol gootol tan: muñde haa laaɓi, wata ɓe njiɗ nguurndam mum en haa laaɓi, ɓe noddoo innde am haa laaɓi. Hooreeɓe mon njaafii on, ɓe ndokkan heewɓe e bonɗo oo e nder hareeji ɗi ɓooyataa fuɗɗaade. Ngenndi mon ndii ina haɓee gila e nder mum haa e yaasi mum. Konuuji boɗeeji ngarii haa jooni, ina ndenti yamiroore mum en; sabu ina peeja feere fitina, heewɓe nanngete. Konuuji bonɗo oo ina nannga kala huunde e leydi ndii ngam koye mum en e jañooɓe mi. ɓe njankan leyɗe mon, galleeji mon, iwdi mon; nguurndam keewɗam majjan e keeferaaku. Hare ina ummoo yaasi sabu ngenndiiji keewɗi kawritii e mon; ina njogii jaɓgol hooreeɓe mon, hooreeɓe mon ina ngollana maayde mon. Ina njiɗi aduna mum en, aduna sadqinaaɗo Seytaani ɗo mbaawi jokkirde timmunde boneeji hoɗɗi e mum en, hay huunde haɗataa anniyaaji mum en bonɗi, sabu haɗoowo oo yahan.

ɓe njulan Babilon Sirri oo yiite timmunde, kala mo yiite ngoo heɓaani, heege heɓan mo; kala mo heege heɓaani, ñawu heɓan mo; kala mo ñawu heɓaani, kaafaaje heɓan mo, neldee e fenaande sabu innde am, heewɓe taƴete ko'e no Konngol am Ceniingol ngol bayyiniri nii. Ɓe mbaraaɓe sabu innde am ɓee, ina njogii njoɓdi mawndi, mi momtan ɓe kala gonɗi e gite mum en.

Anndee, ɓiɓɓe am, kala welwelooji e welwelooji hakke ɗi worɓe bonɓe resanta koye mum en, majjiran tan e henndu, torra e anksiyete ndaran e nokkuuji mum en. Niɓre suddan leydi ndii, Konngol am wontan seeɗa, daande jom suudu e daande jom suudu meeɗataa naneede e mum kadi.

Hay e oon sahaa, mi nelan annabaaɓe am e mon, no maa mi maandin limoore e leƴƴi keddiiɗi ɗii ngam wallude ɓeen, kaawniiɗi mawɗi ɓe mbaɗata. Maandeeji keewɗi e kaawniiɗi ndokketee e nder ɗii ñalɗi niɓre, ɓe noddooɓe innde Joomiraaɗo haa laaɓi ɓee, hisan. Cuuɗi mon toowɗi ɗii njanan. Ponte mon njanan. Heege fuɗɗan e leydi ndii, wonan sariya alaa, ɓe ndaɗndan ɓe ndariiɓe ngam am, heewɓe mbarete; kono njoɓdi mawndi ina heblanaa ɓe ndariiɓe haa laaɓi. Hol ko waɗi hiɗon njiɗi ñalawma Joomiraaɗo? Sabu o halkatno kala ɓanndu e leydi so mi ustoraano ñalɗi ɗii. Ndeen aduna oo yi'an mi ina ummoo e asamaanuuji e konuuji wonɗi e asamaan, sabu leydi ndii ko Joomiraaɗo jeyi e keewal mum.

Eey, miɗo haalana on ɗee geɗe hade mum en waɗde, wonaa ngam ndaron toon e keeferaaku, kono ngam njogo-ɗon tan goonɗinal nanndungal e gabbel mutardiir, sabu ngo mawnan ɓura leɗɗe goɗɗe ɗee, ngo wontan ngo dimmbataa e am so on njaɓii mi laɓɓina on hakkeeji mon, so on ngarii e am tan ngam cellal e kesɗitingol. Noddaango buunel ngel ina ɓadii sanne, ina foti arde e mon, mi faɓɓan jom suudu am e hakkunde. Miɗo heblana haa jooni nokku e taabal am kala jaɓɗo mi Joomiraaɗo e jaabaade dame mum en; sabu ndaaree, miɗo darii miɗo fiyta!

Ko miin woni Ɓiy Daawuuda, mi laamoto leydi ndii gila e jooɗorde makko.

Ko miin woni ɗaɗol Yese; nanoyee daande am!

Ko miin woni Ɓiy Aadama; wata on kule, ndaaree e am.

Ko miin woni Ɓiy Alla, eey, jibinaaɗo gooto e Baaba on.

Ko miin woni Ñaawoowo fonnditiiɗo, ñaawoowo e cenneral e fonnditaare.

Ko miin woni jawgel Alla cuɓaangel e nder durngol, ngel alaa ella, ngel alaa blemish.

Ko miin woni damal baali, hay mborosaaji nyaamooɓe mbaawaa naatde.

Ko miin woni mburu nguurndam; kala nyaamɗo ɗum, meeɗataa heegeede.

Ko miin woni jawgel Alla fotngel udditde maandeeli kaɓɓirɗi deftere ndee.
