Seedeeɓe ɗiɗo: Eklesiya
E nder Golle Pool Nelaaɗo
10 Mee, 2024

Ee onon junuubankeeɓe! Ngaree e am e nder oo sahaa sakkitorɗo!

Nanee Konnguɗi am, hay Konnguɗi Iisaa Nasaraankaajo, sabu miɗo ara e mooɗon e ruuhu giɗli, yurmeende, toowre, moƴƴere, teeŋti noon e yurmeende. Sabu ɓooyataa moƴƴere am e yurmeende am lomtiniree fitina mawka, hay fitina Yakuuba, ndee duuɓi jeeɗiɗi fof maa laato ko leydi ndii meeɗaani yiyde so wonaa gila puɗɗagol tagoore. Maa fuɗɗoroy e halkere e torra ñiirnde, ɗi njokkata tan e tiiɗde. Ko ɗuum waɗi, miɗo ara oo sahaa sakkitorɗo e junuubankeeɓe ɓe tuubaani tawo e nder moƴƴere am e yurmeende am. Sabu heddii tan seeɗa ngam on tuuba so on ngarii e am, Iisaa Almasiihu, on njaɓa ko mi Joomiraaɗo mooɗon, on koola e nder ɓerɗe mooɗon wonde Alla imminii kam e maayɓe. Mi fontinii juuɗe am e nder moƴƴere e yurmeende feewde e aduna bonɗo fotde duuɓi moƴƴere timmuɗi jooni, heewɓe kadi mbaɗii kam Joomiraaɗo mum en, hay no heewi hono no Joomiraaɗo Alla men noddiri, suɓii gila aduna fuɗɗaaka ngam ɓe peeñtine e am, Iisaa Almasiihu Joomiraaɗo baawɗo: Sabu ko miin woni Joomiraaɗo Joomiraaɓe e Laamɗo Laamɓe.

Mi noddii suɓaaɓe ɓee, e kala laatiiɗo goongɗinɗo e kisal am. Heewɓe kisaama! On naamndoo, kisaama e hol ko? Kisaama e ñaawoore e halkere nde ardata e dow oo aduna, e allaaɗi mum, e laamɓe mum, e laamuuji mum, e kala jokkuɗo Seytaane, sabu ko kañum woni alla oo aduna, ñaawoore mum arii. Hay kisaama e ñaawoore jooɗorgal mawngal daneewal ngal e weendu yiite ndu senaanaama Iblis e maleykaaji mum. Ñaawoore ndee waɗaama e laamɓe oo niɓeere hanndeere, ɓooyataa nde timma, nde laaɓta, sabu leydi ndii laamorte e juuɗe am e nuunɗal, sabu wakkati am arii ngam mi peeñtine, leydi ndii ko miin jogii e timmal mum, hay miin, Yesuwaa Hammasiih!

Ñaawoore ndee arii, hay so tawii woodii kono wonaa timmunde tawo. Heewɓe e mooɗon cikkii ina njogii wakkati ngam weltinde junuubaaji mooɗon, e yiɗde heɓde weltaare e annduure mooɗon bonɗe, ummoriiɗe e terɗe mooɗon bonɗe. Paamee wonde alaa e am yiɗde ñaawoyde walla daɗndude hay gooto e mooɗon, sabu mi hokkii nguurndam am e nder giɗli am e mooɗon, mi yoɓii junuubaaji mooɗon fof e mette e torra mawka e dow leggal gaafaangal! Sabu alaa junuuba gooto mo yoɓaaka timmi e sadak am moƴƴinɗo, hono Paska timmuɗo. Ko miin woni Jawgel Alla ittoowengel junuubaaji aduna oo. Mi araani ngam mi daɗnda on, kono miɗo ɗoo ngam mi hisna on. Aduna oo no nganndir-ɗon ɗum nii, maa waylo no feewi haa mbaawaa anndude ko ummotoo e doodi ndii, sabu geɗal mawngal e aduna oo maa yani e yiite, maa halka e laabi keewɗi e nder ñaawoore Yahwe nuunɗunde. Kadi kulol mawngol maa yaltu e ndee doodi.

Sabu laamɗo oo aduna ñaawooma, ñaawoore mum maa timmine, maa laaɓtine timmi. Sabu Seytaane waɗii bone keewɗo, laabi mum ina keeɗɗi, o arata tan ko ngam wujjude, warde, e halkude. O bonnii aadee fof e laabi keewɗi, omo yiyloo halkude kala ko Alla yiɗi. Sabu Alla yiɗii aduna oo no feewi haa hokki ɓiyiiko gooto bajjo ngam kala goongɗinɗo kam wota halku, kono heɓa nguurndam duumotooɗam. Ndeen noon, paamee timmi wonde ko miin woni jawgel Paska sakkitoroongel ngam wakkatiiji fof (alaano goɗngel), mi yoɓii junuubaaji fof. Seytaane bonnii tagooje aadee gila e ngesa Eden, nde o jarribi Aadama e Hawaa ngam njaɓa ɓiɓɓe makko pooftuɗe, koola ganndal makko meere, sabu ganndal makko ina addana maayde kala ñamɗo heen. Ko sabu ɗuum Alla Jom Baawɗe oo, waɗi nanondiral jam e tagooje mum, hay miin, Iisaa Almasiihu.

Sabu hol ko wiyi daande yimɓe Efesus, nde Pool winndannoo ɓe ko fayti e golle am timmuɗe (sabu mi wiyi e foondu am sakkitorndu e dow leggal gaafaangal, “Gasii”)? Kono jooni, e nder Almasiihu Iisaa, onon wonnooɓe woɗɗuɓe ko ɓooyaani koo, ɓaditiniraama e ƴiiƴam Almasiihu.

Sabu ko kanko woni jam men, o waɗii ɗiɗo fof gootum, o udditii maadi ndi wonnoo ko faddaare hakkunde men. Nanee daande am. Ko miin woni jam mooɗon, sabu sariya oo daɗndii junuuba e nder terɗe; hay terɗe mooɗon e ƴiiƴam mooɗon mbonniima e junuuba, hay luutnaama gila Eden. Miin, Iisaa Almasiihu, e nder sadak am moƴƴinɗo e dow Kalvaari, mi udditii maadi hakkundeere senngo hakkunde Yahuudankeeɓe e Geno'en, mi waɗi ɓe ɗiɗo fof neɗɗo gooto keso, hay mi tagitii ɓe e nuunɗal e laaɓal goonga ngam kala jaɓɗo jam e anndinde ko mi Joomiraaɗo mum en umminaaɗo!

Kono woodi ko ɓuri ɗum ummoraade e yimɓe Efesus (hono no ɗum woniri ko haawni), Efesus ina jokki: o ittii kadi e terɗe makko sabaabu konnaagu (ko woni, sariya yamirɗe gonɗo e sariya binndaaɗo) ngam waɗde ɗiɗo neɗɗo gooto keso e hoore makko, o waɗi jam: e ngam o nanndina ɗiɗo fof e Alla e nder tergal gootal e dow leggal makko gaafaangal, o wari konnaagu heen. Nanee kam, an neɗɗo saliiɗo noddaango am: miɗo nodda on jooni e nder ɗii leƴƴanɗe sakkitorɗe, wakkati oo fotii timmude! Jaɓee kam ko mi sadak mooɗon jam, sabu miɗo rokka on kisal e ndimaagu e terɗe mooɗon bonɗe, hay nguurndam duumotooɗam e am, Almasiihu Iisaa. Woto njejjitee: Kala dokkal moƴƴal, e kala dokkal timmungal, ummorii ko to dow, ummorii ko e Baaba annoore, mo alaa baylagol, alaa kadi ɗowdi waylo.

Nanee daande am, paamee wonde kala ko moƴƴi e nguurndam mooɗon, kala ko woodi nafoore e moƴƴere, ko e Baaba ummorii, rokkaama e Baaba Annoore ngam on keɓa timmal nguurndam, on ngara e am, Joomiraaɗo Iisaa, ko mi hisnoowo mooɗon keeriiɗo. E ngam on annda woodii nguurndam duumotooɗam ngam mooɗon so on njaɓii tan. Hino on njiɗiri hoore mooɗon haa on njaɓa sadkude kala ko on njiɗi e nguurndam, ko rokkaama e Baaba, ngam weltinde annduure mooɗon bencil? Hino junuubaaji mooɗon ina njogii nafoore haa on suɓoo daɗndagol duumotoongol e Seytaane dow moƴƴere Alla e kesam-hesaagu nguurndam e am, Almasiihu Iisaa? Hino junuubaaji mooɗon ina njogii nafoore haa hono noon?

Miɗo nodda on, an neɗɗo tago, miɗo nodda on e jam e nguurndam duumotooɗam, nguurndam laaɓɗam e ñawu e junuuba, hay nguurndam laaɓɗam e rafiiji e ɓerɗe bonɗe, nguurndam laaɓɗam e torra ɗo on njiyataa kadi ɓe on njiɗi ɓee ina torroo; sabu geɗe adanɗe ɗee maa njahdu ɓooyataa. Wakkati alaa kadi ngam weltinde junuubaaji mooɗon. Sabu heewɓe e mooɗon njaɓii moƴƴere Alla meere, koolii fenaande wujjo, jiɗɗo wujjude nguurndam mooɗon ngam on naatda e mum e nder lepte mum duumotooɗe. Woto njejjitee. Salagol am, Iisaa Nasaraankaajo (hay kanko hokkuɗo hoore mum ngam junuubaaji mooɗon e kisal mooɗon), ko salagol nguurndam e suɓagol maayde ɗiɗaɓere duumotoonde nde Seytaane e maleykaaji mum ndaɗndanaa: Sabu ɓe ngalaa yaafuya, ɓe keɓataa deeƴre, ɓe ngalaa jam. Weendu yiite nduu senaanaama Iblis e maleykaaji mum.

Salagol sadak am ko salagol nguurndam duumotooɗam e salagol Alla timmi. Ko ɗuum waɗi, alaano yurmeende, moƴƴere, giɗli, annoore, walla jam so wonaa hersa, niɓeere, e torra ɗo on cottata ɓiɓɓe terɗe mooɗon pooftuɗe duumoo. On njiyataa no ɗum woniri mbonɗe? Mi yoɓii junuubaaji mooɗon e ƴiiƴam am moƴƴinɗam! Kono on calii laabi am, hay so tawii miɗo nodda on jooni, wonande heewɓe ko sahaa sakkitorɗo. On calii giɗli am, hay so tawii ɓernde am ina taƴana on e galleeji mooɗon, miɗo hisna on haa timma so on tuubii tan, on acca junuubaaji mooɗon. On paamataa wonde mi torraama hono junuubanke ɓurɗo lesde e nder sadak am moƴƴinɗo ngam mi hisna on haa e Alla e nder kisal? Sabu mi ɓentinaama e dow leggal gaafaangal ngam mi hisna yonkiiji mooɗon, mi nanndina on e Baaba, mi meeɗaani waɗde junuuba, njaɓee ƴiiƴam am laaɓɗam!

Sabu hol ko wiyi Deftere? Sabu goonga on nanii wonde Alla yiɗii aduna oo no feewi haa hokki Ɓiyiiko gooto bajjo: ngam kala goongɗinɗo mo wota halku, kono heɓa nguurndam duumotooɗam. Miɗo anndi ɗee konnguɗi ina keewi e mooɗon, sabu hay weltinooɓe mooɗon jom en jaggirde on e maccungaagu Seytaane, njippinii konnguɗi am e laawol bonngol. Kono so on njokkii janngude ko ɓuri moƴƴude, njaɓee nanee daande am! Sabu Alla neldaani Ɓiyiiko e aduna ngam ñaawoyde aduna oo: kono ngam aduna oo hisna e makko. Goongɗinɗo mo, ñaawootaako: kono mo goongɗinaani oo, ñaawooma tawo, sabu o goongɗinaani Innde Ɓiɗɗo Alla gooto bajjo oo.

Haala am laaɓaani no feewi? Konnguɗi am paamotaako no weeɓi? So on calii kam, Joomiraaɗo Iisaa, Jawgel Alla, on naatdat e gaño am duumoo, hay ɓeen jogiiɓe darnde e ruttingol ƴiiƴam laaɓɗam: Sabu salagol am ko naatde e murto Seytaane, sabu Iblis waɗii junuuba gila puɗɗagol. Ndee woni daɗndagol, wonde annoore arii e aduna, yimɓe njiɗi niɓeere ɓuri annoore, sabu golle mum en mbonii. Ko noon woniri goonga e mooɗon, an neɗɗo tago, hay onon salotooɓe ruuhu am nguurndam, e dow ɗuum fof miɗo nodda on, hay so tawii ko ɗum noddaango sakkitoroongo e timmungo hade ñaawoore fuɗɗaade. Sabu wakkati alaa kadi. Hino junuubaaji mooɗon ina njogii nafoore haa on mbaasa duumagol e niɓeere yaajnde e lepte e nder ndeen weendu yiite mawndu senaanandu Iblis e maleykaaji mum? Sabu Alla yiɗaani on oo lepte, o nelii kam ko mi sadak makko jam ngam o nanndina on e mum e ƴiiƴam am moƴƴinɗam.

Nanee konnguɗi am, njaɓee jam am. Kala nanɗo ɗee konnguɗi, anndee wonde miin, Iisaa Nasaraankaajo, miɗo nodda on; mi sikitaani ko honɗi junuubaaji kaɓɓi on sabu mi yoɓii ɗi fof. Paamee wonde jaɓde maande kullel ngel, yaafotaako, miɗo etaade hisnude on e ndee jarribaaki (hay so tawii maande ndee wonaani ɗoo tawo); sabu heewɓe maa majjina nguurndam mum en e torra ngam salagol. Kono paamee ko rewi heen: On mbaawaa jippaade les haa mi waawaa yettaade on; kono woto njaɓee maande ndee. Hay mawnikinaaɓe mbaawaa ɓentinde hoore mum en e junuuba haa mi waawaa jippinde ɓe e jam feewde e kisal, mi tappa ɓe ruuhu am ngam mi ɓentina ɓe! Sabu mi yoɓii junuubaaji aadee fof ngam wakkatiiji fof, mi accaani hay huunde. Junuubaaji ina keewi, kono mi noddiran yoga e mooɗon inɗe bonɗe mooɗon. Anndee mi yaafoto on junuubaaji mooɗon fof, wonaa ɗee tan, mi laɓɓinan on timmi e ƴiiƴam am e junuubaaji mooɗon fof.

Miɗo wiya jarnoowo e kuutorɗo dorog: Ngartiree e am jooni e nder wakkati laaɓal duumotooɗo, nanee konnguɗi am, sabu so on nanii e goongɗingol, konnguɗi am maa ngaddan on annoore teddungal kisal. Konnguɗi am maa kaɓɓa jinnaaji baɗooji on yara, kuutora dorog, hay so tawii on mbaɗii ɗi sehilaaɓe mooɗon. Miɗo rokka on laaɓal kaawningal ngam on mbaawa suɓaade ko on njogoytoo duumoo: Nguurndam duumotooɗam e am, Iisaa Almasiihu, e ngootaagu timmungu e Alla e hoore mum? Waa daɗndagol duumotoongol e yiite ngu meeɗataa ñifde? Hino junuuba mo njogiiɗon e juuɗe mooɗon, hay yarnooje mooɗon e dorog, ina jogii nafoore haa on suɓoo halkude, hay so tawii on mbaɗii ɗum e ɓe on njiɗi, ɓe njokkata on e nokku am?

On njippaaki haa mi waawataa ɓamtude on, e miɗo yiɗi yaafude on no feewi, kono dole on jaaboo daande am, on njalta e laabi mon e tuubu. Liɓee njaram mon mbuurnooɗam, accitee tooknude ko'e mon, ngaree e am e tuubu, mi hisna on. Wakkati walaa ko majjintee; woto on calo mi, on njirla e mbundi, on kejjita haala am, sabu haala mon salagol winndoyte e dow kammuuji ngam seedaade on ñalnde ñaawoore, on ndawataa!

E mbardoowo on, miɗo wi'a: Accu bonannde ma warhoore nde warataa ɓeen tagiraaɓe e sifa Laamɗo, hay si ko yimɓe ma, sabu ɗum ko hakke mawɗo. Goonga, e goonga mo waylotaako, on mbarii ko'e mon e kala yonki ki oɗon keɓi, so wonaa miin, alaa safaara lepte mon garooje. Kono hay warngooji mon njoɓaama timmi e sadaka am, sabu mi accaali hay huunde nde mi timminaani. Mi hollii moƴƴere am e nguurndam Nelaaɗo mawɗo Pool ngam wonande on seedam semmbe, onon warooɓe.

Sabu hay Sawul majjunooma, o fa'unoo ko e ñaawoore mawnde e laawol Damas, ɗo mi hisni mo e hoore makko. Pool torri, naawi, jaɓi, waɗi warngo e dow Eklesiya am laaɓuɗo! Sabu omo jaɓi ɗum e golle Nelaaɓe: Mi torrii ndee laawol haa maayde, miɗo haɓɓa, miɗo naatna e kaso worɓe e rewɓe fof. Kono o jaɓii noddaango am nguurndam duumotooɗam, o waɗi miin, Iisaa Almasiihu, Joomiraaɗo makko, o golli kam e dewal ngam wonde misaalu tuubu wonande fof, mi ɓamti mo no feewi sabu ɗooftaare makko. Mi yoɓanii on laabi mon warhooɗi so on tuubii, on ngarii e am e tuubu goonga, sabu so on mbaɗii ɗum jooni, mi jaɓɓoroytoo on e moƴƴere e yurmeende. Woto on majjiniree; wakkati walaa ko majjintee.

E ɓurɓe leefde fof, miɗo wi'a: Mi waɗanii on hay laawol ngam yaltude e majjere mon e caɗeele mon, sabu mi accii on e cuɗaariiji mon bonɗi no deftere Room wi'iri: Ko ɗuum waɗi Laamɗo acci ɓe e cuɗaariiji bonɗi: sabu hay rewɓe maɓɓe mbayli kuutoragol mum en tagoore, mbaɗi ko feewnaani tagoore: Noon ne worɓe, acci kuutoragol debbo ngol tagoore, ɓe mbuldi e cuɗaariiji mum en, worɓe e worɓe ina mbaɗa ko boni, ina keɓa e ko'e mum en njoɓdi majjere mum en fotannde.

Njaltee e tunnere mon, njaltee e mawnikinaare mon meere, accitee tuunude e maande ndokkaande Nuuhu e kala tagoore wuurnoonde, hay si ko tiivliire yi'eteende e duule toɓo. Tiivliire ndee wonannde aduna ngam anndude wonde Laamɗo halkirataa aduna ndiyam kadi, hay so tawii ñaawoore yiite ina arda. Ngaree e am, tuubee, mbaylee laabi mon, noddee innde am, ceedee wonde mi Joomiraaɗo gila e ɓernde heewnde goonɗinal ummital am, mi jaɓɓoroytoo on. Ngaree law! Sabu on ngalaa wakkati miijaade, sabu ñaawoore ina foti fuɗɗaade. Wakkati walaa ko majjintee.

Nanee haala am, ngaree e am, sabu mi welataa e ñaawoore e halkere garoore, Yahweh ne, alaa, hay Laamɗo Jom Baawɗe fof! Sabu Aadam tagiraama e giɗli Baabiraawo e timmal e peewal e laaɓal goonga. Laamɗo rokki mo nguurndam timmuɗam, alaa ko haasi. Ndeen noon Seytaane yani e tagooje, bonni ɓalli mon e jikkuuji mon haa on mbaɗi e yumma, sabu hakke ina woni e ƴiiƴam mon: Nguurndam ɓalndu ina woni e ƴiiƴam. Kono Laamɗo, e yurmeende makko mawnde, sabu yurmeende makko duumoto haa abada, hay Laamɗo Jom Baawɗe fof rokki miin, ɓiyiiko gooto, ngam wonde sadaka timmuɗo hisnoowo on haa laaɓa so on ngarii e am e tuubu e hakkeeji mon. Mi bonnitay golle Seytaane e nguurndam mon so on ngarii e am. Min ndaarii on, ngaree, sabu juuɗe am ɓooyataa ina njaajii!

Wakkati heddiiɗo oo ina famɗi no feewi, torra Yaaquuba ina foti fuɗɗaade e aduna hee fof, ɓerɗe yimɓe njirloytoo sabu tiiɗere ko arata. Seytaane heɓtii bonanndeeji keewɗi ɗi o yaltinta, sabu salaade noddaango am ko suɓaade laamu maayde e halkere Seytaane; mi accoytoo on e cuɓal mon, mi rokka on niɓe e mette ɗe njiɗɗon no feewi, on ñaama ɓiɓɓe laabi mon so on tuubaaki. Sabu ko ɗuum woni, ko ɗum woni noddaango cakkitiingo hade ñaawoore arde law. Hay maccuɓe am e gollooɓe am ina nodda haa e keeri aduna e ndeeɗoo sahaa, so wonaa ɓe noddoyta ngam hisnude on haa ɓe njettoyee e am e duule nde mi arata ngam jom suudu am to dow, ɗum waɗata ko e fotaake, hay so tawii miɗo haalana on laaɓɗum hade mum waɗde. Ndeen noddaango am cakkitiingo moƴƴere e yurmeende gasat. Nde mi hisni jom suudu am, halkere yaawnde fuɗɗotoo e aduna, heewɓe e mon nanɓe ɗee konnguɗi ndawataa. Won e mon e majjere ina cikka ina mbaawi jokkude e bonnude moƴƴere am, nde jokkoyta haa abada, kono goonga mettungo woni moƴƴere ndee waylitoyto ñaawoore, nde waɗata ko e fotaake, heewɓe ndawataa.

Sabu ɓeen ɓe ndee halkere yaawnde waɗata e Babilon Misal waraani, taway ko'e mum en e aduna mo ɓe miijaaki, aduna mo maayde e halkere duumotoo, sabu Seytaane ittiray kala ko jokkondiri e am, Iisaa Almasiihu, o waɗirta ɗum ko e yurmeende. Torra ina woodi e leydi ndii fof e bonannde, arde e am e oo sahaa ñaawoore e jarribo wonata ko ɓuri saɗtude; kono kala nodduɗo innde Joomiraaɗo hisat, dole ɓe muñña haa e sakkito, heewɓe taƴoytee koye, heewɓe maayoyta e torra mawɗo.

Kono hay e oon sahaa, Laamɗo momtat gonɗi mum en fof, sabu Yuuhanna Wahayu wi'iino e dow maɓɓe: Ko ɓeen ngoni yaltuɓe e torra mawɗo, ɓe looti comci mum en, ɓe laɓɓini ɗi e ƴiiƴam Jibinaaɗo on. Ko ɗuum waɗi ɓe ngoniri yeeso jappeere laamu Laamɗo, ɓe ndewata mo jamma e ñalawma e suudu dewal makko: o jooɗiiɗo e jappeere laamu ndee hoɗdoyta e maɓɓe. Ɓe keefataa kadi, ɓe ɗomɗataa kadi, naange huɓɓantaa ɓe, hay nguleeki. Sabu Jibinaaɗo on, wonɗo e hakkunde jappeere laamu ndee, ñamminta ɓe, ardoo ɓe haa e ilam nguurndam: Laamɗo momtat gonɗi mum en fof.

Miin, hay miin, Almasiihu Iisaa, Joomiraaɗo ummitiiɗo, hay Almasiihu goonga: miɗo nodda on, miijo am e yaakaare am ko jaɓde sadaka am jam, ngaree e hisnugol am ngam nguurdondirde e am haa abada e nder ɓernde Baabiraawo! Woto tan calo noddaango am, sabu mi noddataa ɓooyde, mi noddirataa e moƴƴere e yurmeende e ngol laawol kadi, sabu wakkati gasii (hay so tawii moƴƴere e yurmeende ina njokki e am haa abada). Min ndaarii on, ngaree e am ngam mi hisna on e ɗeeɗoo sahaaji cakkitiiji moƴƴere. Sabu on ceertaani e ɓeen goonɗinɓe gila e fuɗɗoode duuɓi am moƴƴere, hisnugol am e njoɓdi am wonata ko mo mon so on ɗoftii daande am jooni. Jooni woni wakkati hisnugol! Tuubee, ngaree e am e lesɗinaare. Mbaɗee mi Joomiraaɗo mon, anndee wonde miɗo wuuri haa abada, sabu Laamɗo ummitinii mi e maayɓe ngam mi maayataa kadi. So on jaɓii noddaango am, on foolii, on keɓoyta njoɓdi foolooɓe:

Ndaa, miɗo darii e damal, miɗo fiyata: so neɗɗo nanii daande am, o udditii damal, mi naatoyta e makko, mi ñaamdondira e makko, kanko e am. Mo fooli, mi hokkoyta mo jooɗaade e am e jappeere am laamu, no min ne mi fooliri, mi jooɗii e Baaba am e jappeere makko laamu. Njanngee ɗum laabi keewɗi, sabu miɗo rokka on fartaŋŋe cakkitiinde hisnugol e moƴƴere. Woto tan calo noddaango am, sabu wakkati alaa e ko wayi noon. E heewɓe e mon, salaade mi wonata ko sakkitiingo e duumotoongo, on keɓataa kadi, on ndawataa ñaawoore garoore, sabu halkere yaawnde ina woni e mon. Ruuhu e Jom suudu ina mbi'a, "ngaree." Kalla nanɗo o wi'a, "ngaree." Kalla ɗomɗuɗo, yo ar. Kalla yiɗɗo, yo hooy ndiyam nguurndam e meere.

E sakkitii: Mo boni, yo won bonɗo haa jooni: mo tunni, yo won tunno haa jooni: mo peewi, yo won peewɗo haa jooni: mo laaɓi, yo won laaɓɗo haa jooni. Ndaa, miɗo arde law, njoɓdi am ina wondi e am, ngam rokkude kala neɗɗo no golle mum mbaɗiri. Yo ɗum waɗ.

Miɗo nodda hakkeejo'en e ruuhu giɗli am.

Ko miin woni Joomiraaɗo Joomiraaɓe e Laamɗo Laamɓe.

Ko miin woni Jibinaaɗo Laamɗo ittoowo hakkeeji aduna.

Miɗo ɗoo ngam hisnude on.

Ko miin woni jibinaaɗo Paska cakkitiido.

Ko miin woni sadaka mon jam.

Miɗo nodda on jooni e oo wakkati cakkitiido.

Miɗo rokka on hisnugol e rimɗineede.

Miɗo nodda on jooni e tuubu e yaafagol hakkeeji.

Miɗo nodda on e jam e nguurndam duumotooɗam.

Miɗo jokki e noddude on.

Miɗo wuuri haa abada. On naatat e am?
