E nder Golle Yuhanna Wahyudanke
16 lewru Duujal, 2024
Jime 3 e 4

Wakkatiiji ɗii njottake!

Gila e Yeshua HaMashiach:

Ndaaree! Wakkatiiji ceergal ɗii ɓadtii timmude, ummaade am kadi ɓadtake jooni. No feewi ɓadaaki, kadi malaa'ika'en e mooliiɓe asama'en ngoni e noddaango buututal. Mi fawii juuɗe am feewde e aduna maayoowo gila e duuɓi keewɗi jooni, teemedere heewɓe njaɓii nanondiral jam ngal Baaba am, Yahweh, neldi neɗɗaagu, hay so tawii ko miin, Almasiihu Iisaa, sabu ko miin woni jam mon. Kono heewɓe goɗɗi njaɓaani dokkal am e yurmeende e moƴƴere Baaba am ɗe o loowi e dow leydi.

Ɓeen tiɗɗaaɓe e soodtaaɓe ko ɓeen cuɓaaɓe gila e fuɗɗoode aduna. Sabu Baaba am anndinoo on gila e fuɗɗoode haa e abada, on ngonnoo ko e hakkunde ɓernde makko, no miin kadi ngonirnoo e hakkunde ɓernde makko, sabu miin, Iisaa Nasaraatu, ko mi ɓiɗɗo Yahweh gooto joomum. Kono e golle am timmuɗe no mi woniri Paska timmuɗo e dow toowgol ɓiɗɗo gooto joomum, ɓiɓɓe Alla heewɓe, heewɓe njibinaama kadi e nder abada, hay so tawii ɓe njibinaama e nder ɓiɓɓe yimɓe, sabu ko miin woni Ɓiɗɗo Neɗɗo!

Wakkatiiji yurmeende ɗii ko sirlu suuɗaaɗo e nder ɓernde Yahweh, mo o haalanaani hay gooto haa o feeñani mi, miin kadi mi feeñanii nelaaɓe am laaɓɓe e annabaaɓe am, mi reeni Konngol am laaɓngol ngam noppi e ɓerɗe cuɓaaɓe e gooŋɗinɓe. Sabu onon, Jommbaajo am, on jeyaa ko e sirlu mawɗo Alla, nguurndam mon (ngol suuɗii e nder am) ngol ɓadtiima feeñeede. Miin kadi mi yoɓan gooŋɗinɓe ɓee golle maɓɓe gooŋɗinɗe ɗe ɓe mbaɗi e nder goongɗingol mawngol feewde e am, Iisaa Almasiihu.

Mi hisnii mooliiɓe heewɓe e nder ƴiiƴam am cafroojam, sabu ƴiiƴam am alaa bakkaat, haa hannde mi alaa bakkaat, sabu mi meeɗataa waɗde bakkaat. Ɓeen nguurnooɓe e nder Almasiihu e gooŋɗinal keɓan njoɓdi mawndi, ɓe njalban no koode e nder asama, teddungal mawngal woodani ɓe, sabu ɓe teddinaani hoore maɓɓe no ɓe mawɓe nii. Kono ɓe teddinii miin, Yeshua HaMashiach, miin kadi mi hollan ɓe Baaba am tawa alaa wempeere walla ñoodi, comci maɓɓe maa laaɓ no mbuneeje nii, meeɗataa tuutde. Ɓe nguurdan fof, meeɗataa kadi waɗde bakkaat, woni e bonannde walla e mawnikinaare. Ɓe teddiniree e nder am.

Sabu ko miin woni gaynaako mawɗo, mi ardii gese am haa e laddeeji heewɗi haa wakkatiiji timmoode, ɗi ɓadtii ari e dow leydi, sabu alaa kadi njaareendi heddiindi e nder werlaas. Mi yoɓan fof, gila e famɗo haa e mawɗo, e laawol ngol mbaawaa faamde e oo sahaa; kono faamde ndee ina woni e damal mon, sabu on nanngete e nder asama'en ɓooyataa. Jommbaajo am kadi maa nanngete e nder teddungal, ɗo mi ñaawoyan golle mum, mi soodta kala gorko, debbo, e cukalel haa laaɓa. Mi yoɓan ɓe no feewi hay e geɗe pamare ɗe ɓe mbaɗi e golle am. Sabu Alla wonaa bonɗo, haa o yejjita golle mon e golle giɗli mon, ɗe on kolliri feewde e Innde makko, e nder nde njokkirɗon Seniiɓe ɓee, on njokki.

Kulee, Jommbaajo am, sabu Alla ñaaawataa no neɗɗo ñaaawirta, sabu neɗɗo wonaa nuunɗuɗo, omo horsina yimɓe. Neɗɗo ina teddina ɓeen ɓe neɗɗo jogori himmude, neɗɗo tagoowo kadi ina heewi welsindaade deeƴre e moƴƴere ngam weltinde mawnikinaaɓe e heɓɓitiiɓe hoore mum'en. Sabu neɗɗo tagoowo ina wulla yo o hiiseede nuunɗuɗo yeeso Alla, kono neɗɗo tagoowo waawaa foolde jikku mum bakkaat, o salii goongɗinde wonde o bonɗo, o welsindii hay Joomiraawo Joomiraaɓe, mo hisnannoo mo.

Mawnikinaare ina adii halkaare, hakkilantaagal mawnikinaare kadi ina adii fiyande. E nokku ɗo ɓe tuubata ɓerɗe maɓɓe feewde e konnguɗi am e hakkilantaagal e giɗli ɗi mi loowi e dow leydi, neɗɗo tagoowo ina suɓoo rewde e muuyɗe mum e yiɗde mum, sabu laabi aduna ɗii ko Seytaane feewni ngam jarribde e udditde ɗeen yiɗde, hay njoɓdi bonannde. Wakkatiiji jentilɓe ɗii ngarii e timmoode, wakkati caɗeele Yakuuba njottike; sabu mi soodtan Isra'iila, hay so tawii ko cuɓaaɓe am gooŋɗinɓe, ɓe ngaran e am e tuubgol ngam bakkaatuuji maɓɓe e bakkaatuuji maamiraaɓe maɓɓe ɓe njaɓaani mi. No winndiraa nii, yo waɗe.

Hey, neɗɗo tagoowo, hol ko waɗi aɗa suɓoo haa jooni bakkaatuuji maa, aɗa welsindoo yurmeende am e kisal am no ko huunde famɗunde nii? Hay Yahweh, hay miin, Almasiihu Iisaa, min keɓaani weltaare e ñaawooje garooje e kulol mawngol ngol ɓooyataa feeñeede. Kono Alla suɓaani ndee feccere e peeje mum ngam o wona bonɗo e tagooje mum: sabu Alla ko giɗli, Alla ko annoora, alaa niɓre e nder makko sam. Caɗeele mawɗe garooje ɗee ko ngam hokkude neɗɗaagu fartaŋŋe cakkitiiɗo hade mum laataade laaɓɗum. Mooliiɓe heewɓe maa hisne e nder jarribo ngoo, hay so tawii ko ɓeen jogiiɓe innde am haa e foondu maɓɓe cakkitiindu, ɓe njiɗaa nguurndam maɓɓe haa e timmoode.

Neɗɗo tagoowo, aɗa tiiɗi no feewi, a nanataa, aɗa welsindoo yurmeende am e nder majjere, miɗo juula haa jooni: Baaba, yaafo ɓe, sabu ɓe nganndaa ko ɓe mbaɗata. E nder ɓeen accaaɓe e caɗeele mawɗe, hay so tawii ko ɓeen njaɓaani dokkal am jam, heewɓe maa majje e halkaare, sabu ɓe njaɓaani Joomiraawo Iisaa Almasiihu. Kono, heewɓe maa hisne, hay so tawii ko kala noddoowo innde Joomiraawo maa hisne. Kadi jarribooji yiite ɗii ngoni ko e fuɗɗaade, sabu ñaawoore ina ɓadii.

Ñaawoore mawnde ina ari e dow laamu Seytaane! Onon kadi, neɗɗo tagoowo, hay so tawii on ngalaa goongɗinal tiiɗngal, on njaɓaani hay ɗaɓɓaande ɓurnde weeɓde. Sabu e nder wakkatiiji am yurmeende, kisal ina wonnoo e meere, weeɓi, fof ko neɗɗo foti waɗde ko seedaade Iisaa ko Joomiraawo, goongɗinde Alla umminii mi e maayɓe. Jooni kadi maa wood weelo e kala geɗe moƴƴe e nder leydi, hay so tawii ko Konngol am laaɓngol. Wakkati caɗeele mawɗe, mawɗe ina ɓadii, Seytaane kadi ina feewna e nder majjere ngam daranaade hoore mum, hay so tawii omo sikki omo waawi foolde ngam kanko e malaa'ika'en makko ɓe keɓa aduna maɓɓe, aduna mo alaa Alla, sabu laabi maɓɓe ko bonɗi. Goonga, ko woni ko waɗi hoore mum ooñtude waawaa fonnditeede, alaa yaafuya, alaa jam e Seytaane e malaa'ika'en mum, walla e iwdi maɓɓe.

Ko miin woni ɓiɗɗo Alla garɗo e nder ɓanndu.

Ko miin woni Mbaroodi leñol Yahuuda.

Ko miin woni laamotooɗo leƴƴi ɗii e sawru njamndi.

Ko miin woni uddittooɗo tiɗɗanɗe ɗee ɓooyataa.

Ko miin woni ardoowo Jommbaajo am.

Ko miin woni hawroowo e mon e nder weeyo.

Ko miin woni miin, buututal ngal ɓadtiima fiyde.

Miin, Yeshua HaMashiach, mi neldi ɗee Konnguɗi.

Gila e Yahweh, Alla Mawɗo Duumotooɗo:

Jooni ari ñaawoore Alla duumotooɗo, hay so tawii ko Yahweh, sabu miɗo nela Ɓiɗɗo am, alaa kadi wakkati heddiiɗo. Hey, onon meereeji, sikkuɓe no mbaawirta foolde Yahweh, hay so tawii ko Alla mawɗo duumotooɗo! Hey, onon meereeji, sikkuɓe e nder majjere no mbaawirta foolde Ɓiɗɗo am gooto joomum, Yeshua HaMashiach. On mooɓondirii e mon tikkere duumotoonde, tikkere am ina tiiɗi e mon, sabu ko mi Alla jiibinaaɗo. Sabu gila e fuɗɗoode on ndarii e peeje am, on mettinii, on torrii, on torrii, on torrii, on halki tagooje am. On ngalaa jam.

Nanndee mi, nanndee mi no moƴƴi, sabu miɗo anndina ko laatoto e mon, ko Yahweh anndini kadi hay gooto waawaa waylude, hay laawol gootol, hay laawol gootol. Ñaawoore mon ari. Mi nelan Ɓiɗɗo am ngam mooɓde cuɓaaɓe am e gooŋɗinɓe, hay so tawii ko ɓiɓɓe Aadama ɓe ngonti jooni ɓiɓɓe Alla. On poolaama, hey, onon mboddi meere, njahdoowo e nder fenaande e fenaande. Sabu on ngonaa allaaji, on mbaawaa hisnude hoore mon e juuɗe am. Sabu mi yoɓan!

Mi artiran e mon kala golle bonɗe e kala mette e torra ɗe ngaddan-ɗon e dow aldaaɓe e aybeeji, on njulan haa abada e nder yiite ɓurɗe wulde bulo, cuurli tooke bonɗi maa ngona henndu mon, e nder torra mawɗo on ngonata haa abada. Sabu on mbaɗii mi halka tagooje am, hay so tawii ko leydi no wonirnoo hade ilam Nuuhu, hay so tawii ko sabu aawdi mon meere ndii nde mbaɗan-ɗon ngam halkaare neɗɗaagu; e sabu ɗee geɗe, mi halkiran on e nder ndonu mon yiite.

On njarnii halkude aduna caggal ilam Nuuhu, hay so tawii on mbaɗii kadi bonanndeeji mon, kono ɗi ngalaa ɗaɗol, ngalaa caɓal; sabu kala huɗo bonngo maa loote, haɓɓee, werlee e yiite. Mi halkiran kadi leydi e ñaawoore yiite, sabu mi fodaniino mi meeɗataa ilnude leydi ndii fof kadi. Kono e nder yiite ñaawoore am ari, sabu ko mi yiite jom fof, tikkere am ina wulla e dow bonɓe. E sabu ɗee geɗe, mi halkiran on e nder yiite ɓurnde wulde e yiite wulnde.

Nanndee mi, nanndee mi no moƴƴi, sabu miɗo anndina ko laatoto e mon, hay so tawii ko onon reenooɓe meereeji ɓe acci darnde mon adannde. On ngalaa jam, hay so tawii mi yaltinii on e kaɓirɗe mon ngam wakkati seeɗa, mi waɗii ɗum tan ngam on mbaawa gollanaade anniyaaji am. Ndeen ñaawoore mawnde ina woni e mon, on ngalaa jam. Sabu on mbaɗii mi ilna leydi sabu bonanndeeji mon. Hol onon mbaɗooɓe tagooje e tagooje Yahweh? Hol onon tuutnooɓe gese Alla e laabi mon laaɓtuɗi? On mbaɗii ɓiɓɓe mon bonɓe e bonɓe e nder bonnude hoore mon e rewɓe, ɓe ndenndan e ndonu mon yiite. Sabu hol ko kaal-mi Malaki: Sabu ndaar, ñalawma ari o wulan no foondu, kala mawnikinaaɓe, eey, e kala bonɓe, maa ngona huɗo, ñalawma garoowo oo, maa wulɓe, wiyi Joomiraawo Jaagorɗe, o accataa ɓe hay ɗaɗol, hay caɓal. Ndaar oo ñalawma ina ɓadii, on fof on njoɓan e laabi mon bonɗi.

Sabu mi hokkii worɓe rewɓe ngam ɓe njoɓaaka hay huunde, rewɓe ngam wonde wondiiɓe e jogaade ɓiɓɓe. Kono on njiippii e dow tulde Hermon, hay so tawii on accii darnde mon adannde teddungal, on mbonnii hoore mon e rewɓe, on ngalaa jam, on ngalaa fooftere. No mi waɗiri Henok binndoowo oo haalana on e yonta bonannde mon: On ngalaa jam: ñaawoore tiiɗnde yaltii e mon ngam waɗde on e kaɓirɗe: On keɓataa muñal, on keɓataa ɗaɓɓaande, sabu bonannde nde njahdu-ɗon, e sabu kala golle bonannde e bonannde e bakkaat ɗe kollu-ɗon yimɓe. E nder kaɓirɗe on njahan so on werlaama e maayo yiite.

On mettinii tagooje am ngam muuyɗe mon, hay so tawii on nannii heewɓe ɓe cuɓii-ɗon ngam wonde rewɓe mon, on njoɓan. On naatii e Seytaaneeji, hay so tawii ko laamɓe dariiɓe e am, on cuɓii fenaande serafim'en. Ko ɗum waɗi, on naatdan e maɓɓe e nder maayo yiite mawngo duumotoongo, on njulan haa abada, on meeɗataa fooftaade. On mettinii tagooje am, on mbarii heewɓe, on njogoraaki nguurndam ɗam mi tagi. Ko ɗum waɗi, mi jogoraaki on, mi tagii on, jooni ari ñaawoore mon e halkaare mon cakkitiiɗo.

Miɗo wiya Seytaaneeji, reenooɓe, kerubin'en yaniɓe, serafim'en yaniɓe, hay so tawii ko kala naatɗo e murto Seytaaneeji: on njulan fof, laabi keewɗi haa abada; sabu mi haɓɓan on, mi werloo on e yiite ɗo liɗɗi mon maayataa, hay so tawii ko yiite ndeniteende ngam mon meereeji ɗi njulan yiite ɓurɗe wulde tan ngam mon, sabu on poti. Sabu mi waɗan on ko poti e ko mbaɗan-ɗon woɗɓe. Kala mette, suna, ñimsitaare, torra, hay kala golle bonɗe maa njoɓe e mon haa abada, on ndoggataa. Mi fawan on e yeeso leƴƴi, mi feeñina on e fenaande mon, ɓe mbiya allaaji pamari (ɓe ngonaa allaaji): A laatiima kadi lo'uɗo no men nii? A laatiima no men nii?

Mi yoɓan fof laabi mon bonɗi e nder ɓerɗe mon, on njoɓan haa abada. On werleede e yiite tawa on nguurɗi, on njulan e yeeso Jibinaaɗo, malaa'ika'en e kala nuunɗuɓe ɓe bonnu-ɗon maa keɓ e am teemedere ko ɓuri ko njaɓan-ɗon e maɓɓe. Sabu so on njoɓaama bonannde mon, mi yoɓan cuɓaaɓe am e gooŋɗinɓe am njoɓdi mawndi ndi ɓuran ko njaɓan-ɗon teemedere ko ɓuri golle mon meereeje.

Nii wonirta. Mi tagan asama keso e leydi kesiri ɗo nuunɗal hoɗi, ɓiɓɓe am nguurdan e am, mi waɗan hoɗorde am e nder yimɓe, nii wonirta. Kono on njulan e laabi mon bonɗi, kala huunde bonnde nde mbaɗ-ɗon maa artire e mon haa abada. Mi waɗan tiɗɗannde, on yejjiteede, on ngarataa e hakkilantaagal. Fof maa hoɗ e jam, on meeɗataa heɓde jam.

Ndaaree, wakkati timmii, miɗo nela Ɓiɗɗo am ngam Jommbaajo mum, hay so tawii ko Iisaa Almasiihu Nuunɗuɗo! Sabu e makko mi weltii no feewi! Fof ko o hisni, o sellini e laabi mon meereeji, hay so tawii ko ɓeen ɓe tuum-ɗon e bonannde, maa foofto e am, keɓa jam timmuɗo, weltaare, e giɗli. Sabu mi waɗan on no maɓɓe nii, mi waɗa ɓe tedduɓe ko ɓuri ko ngonno-ɗon, on ngalaa laamu, sabu on ngonaa laamɗo.

Bone wonande hoɗɓe e leydi, sabu tikkere mawnde ina woni e mon. Sabu on njaɓaani tagɗo on, on njiɗi maayde. Sabu jaɓde mi ko yiɗde maayde, e nder maayde on maayan. Tikkere am ina wuli e mon, mi ittan kala weltaare, kala weltaare, hay so tawii ko haajuuji mon ɓurɗi himmude maa njoɓe. Sabu so mi loowii on e yurmeende e yurmeende, on suɓii e nokku mum huutoraade yurmeende ngam naatde e meereeji, hay so tawii ko laamu Seytaane, so on mbaawnoo dogde. Jooni heewɓe maa halke, ɓe inɗe mum'en mbinndaaka e Deftere Nguurndam gila e fuɗɗoode. Ɓiɗɗo am, Iisaa Almasiihu, ina ɓadii, o nannan jommbaajo mum e nder asama'en, mi ittan ndeenooɗo. Ndaaree! Miɗo nela mawɓe ngam timminde tikkere am! On njaɓii yurmeende am e yurmeende am e meere, on nguurnii nguurndam weltaare bakkaat, mi yoɓan.

Sabu so on mbaawnoo heɓde jam haa abada, hay so tawii ko nguurndam duumotooɗam weltaare e weltaare timmunde, on cuɓii maayde mon, mi hokkan on ko njiɗ-ɗon. On njiɗi aduna bonannde e niɓre; mi hokkan on ko poti e ko njiɗ-ɗon, mi yoɓan on kadi e kala mette ɗe ngaddan-ɗon e woɗɓe so on nguurnoo e bakkaat mawɗo. Mi ittan e mon kala weltaare, on ñaman tuundi laabi mon. Ñaawoore mawnde ina woni e dow leydi, alaa kadi leeltugol.

Ndaaree! Kala noddoowo innde Joomiraawo maa hisne. Sabu mooliiɓe heewɓe maa hisne e jarribo garoowo ngoo, mi jaɓan ɓe e hoore am. Sabu ina winndaa: Ɓee ko ɓe ummii e jarribo mawngo, ɓe looftii comci maɓɓe, ɓe mbaɗi ɗi laaɓtuɗi e nder ƴiiƴam Jibinaaɗo. Ko ɗum waɗi ɓe ngonii yeeso jappeere Alla, ɓe ngollanii mo ñalawma e jamma e nder Suudu makko: jooɗiiɗo e dow Jappeere ndee maa hoɗ e maɓɓe. Ɓe meeɗataa weelaade kadi, ɓe meeɗataa ɗomɗude kadi, naange meeɗataa fawaade e maɓɓe, hay wulnde. Sabu Jibinaaɗo wonɗo e hakkunde jappeere ndee, maa ñammin ɓe, o ardoo ɓe haa e ɓulli ndiyam nguurnam: Alla maa momta kala gonɗi e gite maɓɓe.

Miɗo wasiyoo on fof yo on noddu innde Iisaa Almasiihu, Masiya goongaajo, hay so tawii ko ɓiɗɗo Alla gooto joomum, sabu omo ari. Tikkere Jibinaaɗo e tikkere Yahweh ɓadtii feeñeede.

Ko miin woni Yahweh, Alla mawɗo duumotooɗo.

Ko miin woni El Shaddai, Alla Jom Baawɗe.

Ko miin woni Jehovah Jireh.

Ko miin woni Elohim mawɗo, Alla Isra'iila.

Ko miin neldi ɓiɗɗo am gooto joomum ngam soodtaade ɓeen soodtaateeɓe.

Ko miin umminɗo Almasiihu Iisaa e maayɓe, mi hokki mo innde ɓurnde kala innde, ngam kala hoore fof yo sujjid, kala ɗemngal fof yo seedo.

Ko MIIN woni MIIN MAWƊO, tikkere am ɓadtiima timmude, alaa ɓurɗo mawnude, mi haalii.

Miin, Yahweh, Alla mawɗo duumotooɗo, mi neldi konnguɗi ɗi nel-mi, mi neldi ɗee konnguɗi. Mi neldi ɓiɗɗo am, Yeshua HaMashiach, kanko o neldi konnguɗi makko e gooŋɗinɓe. Wakkati timmii.
