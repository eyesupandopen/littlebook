Seedeeji ɗiɗo: Isra'iila
Ko Welɗi e ko Haaɗi
Jolay 05, 2024

Hol ko waɗi on nattii muñde?

Ngaree, cujjee ɓerɗe mon e noppi mon, nanee Konnguɗi Joomi mon, woni Eklesiya am e Cendum am. Hol ko waɗi on ittaama e yaakaare mon e feeñnde am e law, wano mi arataa? Wano bayyinaaɗi Yahuwah ina mbaawi wayleede? Malla Alla mawɗo duumiiɗo oo, Yahuwah, yejjitii ahduuji mum? Malla mi yejjitii konnguɗi am e ahduuji am? Goonga, goonga, miɗo wi'a on, alaa noon! Kala goɗɗo fof tuumɗo Yahuwah, Alla mawɗo duumiiɗo oo, e miin, Yeesu Almasiihu, Mawɗo Joomiraaɓe e Laamɗo Laamɓe, wonde o waɗii majjere, yo tuubu jooni jooni. Ƴamee yaafuya, ɓiɓɓe am, njokkee e heblanaade sabu mi feeñan e ɗooftaare, maa on haawnito no kewu mawngu garoowo oo e feere hisnugol neɗɗaŋke ndi Yahuwah waɗi ndii yaawirta waɗde, sabu ɗum waɗan ko e ɗooftaare, ko anndaaka, no ɗum bayyinaa. Sabu maa waɗu mi ummina maayɓe e nder Almasiihu ɓen adii, onon wuurnooɓe heddiiɓe ɓee, on njettoyee ngam hawritde e am e dow duule, ɗum waɗirta ko e nder mbelemma yitere.

Hol ko waɗi on nattii muñde? Hol ko waɗi yoga e mooɗon mbaɗtii wa'de no huunde fof artii no wonnoo? Ina laaɓi wonaa artugol e laabi ɓooyɗi ɗii, sabu aduna hannde oo maa hawru e musiiba mawɗo, o wayloto haa pooma, o wayloto kadi no feewi e saanga torra Yaaquuba, leydi ndii wonira no ñalɗi Nuuhu nii. Nde haɗoowo oo ittetaake, alaa ko haɗata galleeji bonɗi ɗii, hay konuuji malaa'ikaaji nattuɗi ɗii, ɗi njantotoo e kala ko jeyaa e am, sabu Yahuwah hokkii liingu nguu baawɗe haɓde e seniiɓe ɓee, foola ɓe. Heewɓe ngaran e am sabu halkere ɓalli maɓɓe, hay ɓalndu ndu ɓe njiɗi, ndu haɓɓi ɓe haa jooni nduu. Sabu kala noddirɗo innde Joomiraawo hisan, jiyaaɗo ɓuraa joom mum.

Hay so tawii mi hollirii laawol daɗndogol e halkere garaynde ndee wonande gooŋɗinɓe, heewɓe ngeraaka, kono maa ɓe kis, ɓe ngolla golle mawɗe e Laamu am garowu nguu, sabu mi laamoranoyto e jooɗorgal Daawuuda, miɗo rennda e Baaba am jooɗorgal mum, onon kadi maa on renndu e jooɗorgal am, onon gooŋɗinɓe am. Wonande ɓe ngeraaka ɓee, maa ɓe torro no feewi sabu innde am, maa ɓe torro no miin, Yeesu Nasaraat, torrirnoo ngam heɓtinde neɗɗaŋke hisnugol, rewrude e mbaroodi am e dow leggal palaangewal, mi accii yeru ɗooftaare haa e mayde, ngam kala fof rewa heen. Rewrude e torra mawɗo, jamaaji keewɗi maa kis, keɓa njeenaari mawndi e nder am, miin Almasiihu Yeesu, haa e gollanaade Yahuwah e nder suudu dewal makko. Njeenaari mawndi ina heddii kala nodduɗo innde am, ɓeen noddooɓe innde am haa ka laakara maa kis, hay e ɓeen ɓe njiɗaa nguurndam mum en haa ka laakara. Sabu aduna oo ina fadi halkere, o halkete, o lettiree haa laaɓa hade Laamu am nuunɗungu nguu tabitineede e dow leydi.

Woto kadi ngoƴee, ɓiɓɓe am, sabu ahduuji am ko ii e aamiin, mi janngan cendam am haa e dow asamaanuuji no mi ahdirnoo, hay neɗɗo gooto mo Yahuwah toɗɗii ngam hisnugol duumingol hade lowre aduna tagneede, ittaataa e juuɗe am duumiiɗe ɗee, hisnugol mon ngol ina tabbunoo e nder am, miin Almasiihu Yeesu, tan njokkee e gooŋɗinde, keddoo e nder Almasiihu haa ka laakara. Ndee ko sarɗi Yahuwah gila e fuɗɗoode, wonde neɗɗo tawaama ko gooŋɗinɗo e laabi mum. Miin, Yeesu Almasiihu, miɗo adda laabi Yahuwah, ɓeen jannguɓe e am ina njannga laabi Yahuwah, sabu miin e Baaba am ko min gootum e miijo e faandaare. Ɓooyataa, on ngonta gootum e amen, woto winndaaka wonde on mbaɗaama geɗal e Allaahu duumiiɗo oo? Sabu ko nii woni ndonu mon, ko nii kadi woni njeenaari am wonande ɓiɓɓe Alla gooŋɗinɓe, tawaaɓe ina ngooŋɗini e nder am. Sabu e nder am, miin Almasiihu Yeesu, ko ton timmal Allaahu ngal fof hoɗi e nder ɓalndu, onon kadi on renndoyto e ndonu am sabu gooŋɗinal mon, on renndoyto e jooɗorgal am ko on Laamɓe e Ceerno'en, Laamu am nguu alaa ka ɗum timmata.

Nii woni, ndaree tiiɗɗi ngam am e nder ɗee sekoŋɗe cakkitiiɗe jamaanu, faamee wonde wakkati timmii. Eeey, wakkati timmii ngam Baabila Sirri oo tuuba. Wakkatiiji am yurmeende e moƴƴere timmii, hay so tawii yurmeende e moƴƴere ina ngondi e am haa pooma. Jooni noon, kiita mawka arii e dow leydi, e leƴƴi, hay e leydi ndii. Kono kiita oo warataa haa halka kala ko wuuri, kono maa toɓɓo, ɓeydoo. Kiita e dow kiita, maande e dow maande, buututal e dow buututal, mbuudu e dow mbuudu, bone e dow bone, bela e dow bela. Sabu ɗuum, yimɓe heewɓe sanne maa keɓ hisnugol e nder am, miin Yeesu Nasaraat, sabu ko miin woni Jippagol Paska timmungol ngam jamaanuuji fof, alaa kadi haaju e sadak goɗɗo. Kono onon, Cendum am gooŋɗinɗum, wakkatiiji ndeenka mon timmii.

Woto nelaata, woto kadi ndeen, on keɓan njeenaari mon timmundi e ndonu mon e nder am. Hay so tawii yoga maa njo'u, yitere yiyaani, nofru nanaani, hay e nder ɓernde neɗɗo naataani, geɗe ɗe Yahuwah heblani ɓeen yiɗɓe mo. Nii woni, onon yiɗɓe am gooŋɗinɓe, on kollirii giɗli mon e am, miin Almasiihu Yeesu, e giɗli mon e Yahuwah sabu on ɗoftike yamiraaɗe am rewrude e goonɗinal e nder ndee ñalawma bonɗo. Hay so tawii on njiyaani mi e gite mon, walla on njiyaani teddungal Yahuwah duumingal ngal e gite mon, on keddiima e gooŋɗinde am haa ka laakara, ko ɗuum waɗi njeenaari mawndi sirlanaama on e dow asamaan, woto ngoƴee!

Jokkee e muñal, ɓiɓɓe am, sabu miɗo ɓadoo. Woto njeeɗtee yimɓe jalkitooɓe on ɓee, sabu jalngo maɓɓe ngoo ɓooyataa waylitoo e sunaare mawnde, heewɓe e jalkitooɓe ɓee njontoyta e deƴƴere nde nguurndam maɓɓe timmitoo. Sabu ko nii kiitaaji garooji ɗii ngoni. Wonande ɓeen saliiɓe juuɗe am udditiiɗe ɗee, ɓe cikki ƴiiƴam am jamfaaɗam ɗam ko huunde hersiniinde, ɓe njogaaki koye maɓɓe ko hewɓe nguurndam duumingam e Laamu am garowu nguu, kamɓe ne maa ɓe ndonu niɓre e maayde ɗe ɓe njiɗnoo ɗe, ɗe ɓe njiɗnoo sanne, heewɓe kadi maa njiɗa naatde e maayde ɗiɗaɓere ndee.

Sabu e giɗli mawɗi Baaba am, hono Yahuwah Alla duumiiɗo, sabu alaa nannduɗo e mum, o doolataa hay tagu gooto e tagooje makko rewde e mum, yiɗde mo. Kala fof hokkiraama suɓaade, kala fof keɓan njeenaari suɓngo mum. Sabu ko nii sariyaaji Yahuwah ngoni. Ɓeen yiɗɓe bonannde, yiɗɓe weltaare e grek, yiɗɓe bonannde grekaaji maɓɓe mbaɗata e woɗɓe torrotooɓe e dow mum en sabu anniyaaji maɓɓe e laabi maɓɓe bonɗi, maa ɓe ndonu laabi maɓɓe bonɗi ɗii artirta e gite maɓɓe, ko nii lettugal maɓɓe wonirta, kala grek fof lettiretee haa pooma. Kono ɓeen jaɓɓe nanondiral jam Yahuwah, hono miin Yeesu Nasaraat, maa ɓe ndonu nguurndam duumingam e jam e njeenaari e giɗli e weltaare duumiinde e yeeso am e yeeso Yahuwah e hoore mum, sabu ɓooyataa hoɗorde Yahuwah wonoyta hakkunde yimɓe no winndiraa nii. Miɗo ɓadoo ngam janngude on haa e jooɗorgal Yahuwah e hoore mum, ɗo njooɗino-ɗon haa jooni, e kaafaaje mon ina ndeenndi on.

Woto ngoonɗinee fenaande maɓɓe, ɓiɓɓe am, miɗo ara e mooɗon, tan keddoo e tiiɗnaare sekoŋ gooto walla ɗiɗi, sabu feeñnde am ɓadii, ina waawi waɗde e kala sekoŋ jooni, mi ɓuraynoo yaakaare mon. Heblee ko'e mon, laɓɓinee ko'e mon e nder ƴiiƴam am jamfaaɗam. Jokkee e nguurndam mon tuubugol, njokkee e farde laabi mon ɓalli, sabu ɗi ngonaa e am, hay huunde bonnde naatataa e Laamu am, hay huunde bonnde dartotaako e yeeso Yahuwah. Ko miin woni sadak mon duumiiɗo, mi waɗii on ɓe alaa peccol, ɓe alaa nyorol, alaa kadi tuundi e dow gooŋɗinɓe am sabu ƴiiƴam am ina laɓɓina on tawa wontaa so on njaɓii grekaaji mon e tuubugol, hay so tawii ɓalli mon ina mbonnii no feewi.

Ngaree e am e ɓernde udditiinde, mi holliran on e yurmeende ŋakkeende mon ngam mbaawon jaɓde grekaaji mon e am, e waɗde noon, mi laɓɓinan on, mi jokkan e timminde on no on tagu kesu e nder am nii, miin Almasiihu Yeesu. Goonga, goonga, kala fof siiliraaɗo e Ruuhu am ceniiɗo ahdi oo, tawa ko Yahuudiyanke walla Jentil (tawa ko maccuɗo walla dimo) maa o tagite timmuɗo no neɗɗo keso gooto e nder am, miin Almasiihu Yeesu, o dara e ndimaagu teddinaangu haa pooma! Sabu ko miin woni ruuhu rokkowo nguurndam, no Baaba am waɗirimi nii. Miin ko miin ko Mawɗo MIIN WONƊO oo waɗiri mi haa pooma. Jooni kadi on mbaɗete no am nii e nder ɓalndu teddinndu, ɓalndu mbaɗiraandu njeenaari, ndi hollirta golle mon e darnde mon ngam am e nder ndee ñalawma bonɗo. Hay gooto e tiiɗnaare mon yejjitetaake: Sabu Alla wonaa nuunɗaaro, haa o yejjita golle mon, e golle giɗli mon, ɗe kollir-ɗon e Innde makko, nde njogino-ɗon seniiɓe ɓee, nde njogii-ɗon haa jooni.

Goongaaji mon ina cuuɗii e nder am, hono hisnoowo mon e Almasiihu mon. Haa pooma on ngondoyan e am, on ngonta miijo gooto e ɓernde wootere e am, miin Yeesu Almasiihu, hay e Yahuwah, sabu Mawɗo El Shaddai, hono Alla Baawɗo oo, wonoyta fof e fof ngam faandaare mum timmina e mooɗon, onon noddaaɓe am, cuɓaaɓe, gooŋɗinɓe. Miɗo ara e mooɗon, Cendum am, on ngerii? On njogii nebam e lampaaji mon? On lootii ko'e mon timmuɗe e nder ƴiiƴam am jamfaaɗam? Malla on ngoppii lootude koyɗe goɗɗo e goɗɗo?

Ndeenee! Njeeɓee! Nanee! Ndeenee feeñnde am, kumpitiree sabu Seytaane suuɗaynoo feeñnde am so tawii ɗum ina waawi. Njeeɓee e am, binndoowo e timminnoowo goonɗinal mon, sabu e nder mbelemma yitere on mbaɗoytee, on mbaɗoytee no am nii haa pooma. Nanee Konnguɗi am, keblee feeñnde am, ko ɓuri heen fof, ɗoftee sabu Giɗli e Yahuwah e am ngam rewde Konngol am ceniiŋgol e laaɓal ɓernde, anndee wonde hisnugol mon ɓadaaki, kono arii.

Miin e Baaba am ko min gootum e miijo e faandaare, ɓooyataa, on ngonta gootum e amen.

Ko miin woni Jippagol Paska timmungol ngam jamaanuuji fof.

Miɗo ɓadoo. Woto njeeɗtee yimɓe jalkitooɓe on ɓee.

Miɗo ara e mooɗon, tan keddoo e tiiɗnaare sekoŋ gooto walla ɗiɗi.

Ko miin woni sadak mon duumiiɗo.

Ko miin woni ruuhu rokkowo nguurndam, no Baaba am waɗirimi nii.

Miin ko miin ko Mawɗo MIIN WONƊO oo waɗiri mi haa pooma.

Miɗo ara e mooɗon, Cendum am, on ngerii?
