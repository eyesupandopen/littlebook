Seedeeji ɗiɗi: Eklesiya
E nder Golleeji Yuhanna Jaltinoowo
Juko 04, 2024

Ndaaree! Ñaawoore ina ara, jamaanuuji ɗii ko jooni!

Jamaanuuji ɗii njettiima ngam timminde, onon sukaaɓe am tokosɓe, haa teeŋti e timminde kala ko yowitii e yontere cappanɗe jeeɗiɗaɓere Daniyel. Sabu yontere cappanɗe jeeɗiɗaɓere Daniyel ko yontere duuɓi nde ñalawma kala woni hitaande. Yontere cappanɗe jeeɗiɗaɓere Daniyel ina foti timminde, nde fuɗɗotoo nde gomɗinɓe e Eklesiya am ɓee njaɓɓetee ngam hawrude e am e nder duule. Buutol cakkitol ngol fiyee, e daande mawnde maleyka mawɗo, maayɓe e Almasiihu ɓee ummotoo ko adii, onon wuurnooɓe heddiiɓe ɓee, on njaɓɓoytoo e nder duule ɗee wondude e maɓɓe, ko noon njogorɗon wonirde e am haa abada, miin Almasiihu Iisaa, sabu ko miin woni Nasarankeejo ummitinɗo, maayɗo e greka laawol gootol, mo maayataa kadi. Sabu laamu am ko gila e fuɗɗoode haa e timmoode, laamu am nguu alaa ɗo timmata, sabu Yahweh hollitii ɗum.

So gomɗinɓe am ɓee njaɓɓaama e nder kammuuji, ko noon kadi dartinoowo oo ittetee, ko noon kadi yontere cappanɗe jeeɗiɗaɓere Daniyel fuɗɗortoo. Heewɓe e mon ina njeertina miijo wonde miin, Yeesu HaMasiihu, mi jaɓɓatnoo gomɗinɓe am ngam daɗndude ɓe e ñaawoore nde wonaa maɓɓe, kono mi addanay on faamde, so on ngarii e am e deentaare suka tokoso, haa teeŋti e laaɓal e yiɗde anndude suka tokoso. Sabu onon ko on sukaaɓe am tokosɓe, haa teeŋti e sukaaɓe am tokosɓe yiyooɓe e gite laaɓtuɗe, annabaaɓe feccere. Ko ɗum waɗi miɗo wi'a on, deentee, tuubee e mawnikinaare mon ndi alaa nafoore, sabu ko greka, ngaree e am ngam janngude e jaɓde binndi laaɓtuɗi. Salagol feccere kala e binndi ko greka, sabu kala Konngol Alla ko laaɓngol, ina foti jaɓireede e horma ngam ɗooftaade, miin Iisaa Nasarankeejo, ko mi konngol ngol waɗi ɓanndu.

Hawrondirde seniiɓe am e am e nder duule ko sirlu suuɗaaɗo e nder sirlu mawɗo Alla. Ko ɗum waɗi ko sirlu e nder sirlu, ko ɗum waɗi ɗum feññinaaka haa nde ɗum feññinanaa Nelaaɗo Pool e nder ɓataakeeji mum. O feññinanaa sirlu mawɗo Alla, sabu Pool kadi ko o deenoowo sirluuji Alla, sabu ko noon mi yamiriri mo. Ko ɗum waɗi faamee wonde salagol binndi e ndee hiirde ko salagol am, ko jeyeede e sukaaɓe rewɓe rewɓe ɓe ngalaa hakkille.

Ko ɗum waɗi mi faamnirat on geɗe keewɗe ɗe ngonaa keddiiɗe, haa teeŋti e jokkude golle ɗe mi rokki Yuhanna Jaltinoowo, sabu seedantaagal feññinde am ngal winndaa ko ngam suuɗde no ngal feññinirta. Ko ɗum waɗi jooni mi haaldata on geɗe ko adii nde ɗe mbaɗata ngam on mbaawa heblude ñalɗi garooji. Sabu miin, Almasiihu Iisaa, mi addanay yimɓe am daranaade golle ɗo mi neli ɓe ngam on mbaawa timminde haa laaɓa golle keewɗe doole ɗe mi joganii on ngam timminde.

So hawrunde ndee waɗii, dartinoowo oo ittetee, halkaare mawnde nanngata leydi ndii, e jaawle e nder waktu gooto. Sabu so dartinoowo oo ittaama, alaa ko heddotoo ngam haɗde bone e nder aduna e yiɗde e giɗli bonɗi neɗɗaagu. Hare mawnde fuɗɗotoo e leydi ndii fof, miñi mum ummotoo kadi ngam warde miñi mum, halkaare maɓɓe ndee ina mawni. Leƴƴi keewɗi njandotoo no Babiloon Sirru, haa teeŋti e debbo mawɗo jooɗiiɗo e ndiyamji keewɗi, o laatoo mo alaa ko woni e waktu gooto.

Ndeen wuro mawngo, haa teeŋti e leñol Dowlaaji Dentuɗi keewɗi, halkete haa laaɓa; sabu o heɓii feccere ñaawoore nde o heɓi, o foti. Sabu o wuuri ko e weltaare e nder greka mawɗo; o jooɗii ko e mawnikinaare mawnde, omo sikki o alaa e sago o yani, geɗe ɗee njokkondirta, hannde no keñii nii, subaka ina ɓeydoo nanndude, alaa ko waylata. Debbo mawɗo oo bonnii leydi ndii e kaŋŋe e juuɗe mum, heewnde ko boni e ko boni e jeenay mum. Ko ɗum waɗi ñaawoore makko ndee jippotoo e jaawle. Yiite mawngo nanngata gure mum so misilaaji njanii; dillere mawnde waɗata, leñol ngol feccotoo hakkunde. Daande maayo mum ina heewi ndiyam, o ummataa kadi, sabu grekaaji makko ina mawni, ñaawoore makko ina mawni.

So hare fuɗɗiima e leydi ndii, puccuuji ɗii njaltina ñaawooje mum en e leydi ndii, bone e halkaare mawnde njaltata e aduna. Aduna gonɗo jooni oo wontataa kadi; so dartinoowo oo ittaama, kala ko waylata. Maayde mawnde, ñawu, heege mawnde, e tooñannge mawngo ina woodi; kono 144,000 am ɓee ndeenndete e tikkere e ñaawoore, sabu ɓe njogoriino maande Alla Guurɗo, haa teeŋti e Yahweh, e tiinde maɓɓe; sabu ko noon ɓe njogorirta maande, miɗo nela maleykaaji am ngam maande maɓɓe. Ko ɗum waɗi caɗeele mawɗe e añam-laawol ina laamoo ñalawma oo. Lebbi 42 gadani ɗii ko sahaaji tooñannge mawngo e caɗeele mawɗe, kono torra mawɗo oo waɗata ko e lebbi 42 ɗiɗaɓere: sabu lebbi 84 ina ngoodi e balɗe jeeɗiɗi duuɓi caɗeele Yaaquuba. Ko ɗum waɗi feccere ɗiɗmere ndee ko torra mawɗo, kono torra e tooñannge e leydi ndii ina boni e hoɗɓe e leydi ndii e nder dumunna oo fof.

Sabu alaa miin, Almasiihu Iisaa Joomiraawo Jippiiɗo, alaa Yahweh, Alla mawɗo duumotooɗo (sabu alaa ko nanndi e makko), alaa weltaare e ñaawooje e caɗeele garooje. Baaba am tagaani neɗɗaagu e yiɗde ngam ñaawoode e halkude ɓe. Kono Iblis bonnii gese moƴƴere Alla, jooni bonannde ndee laɓɓintee ngam nuunɗal laamoo. Ko ɗum waɗi alaa laawol goɗngol ngam hisnude ɓeen saliiɓe noddaango am hisnude e nder duuɓi moƴƴere. Yimɓe ina poti ummaade e ɗoyngol mum en e weltaare greka, sabu alaa waktu kadi, jooni ko waktu suɓaade haa abada, walla suɓngo ngoo waɗete e ñaawoore ngam ɓeen saliiɓe am e salagol suɓaade. Caɗeele keewɗe e ñaawooje garooje ɗee umminat heewɓe haa hisnude duumotooɗo, haa teeŋti e jaɓɓude ɓe e yiite duumotooɗo; sabu heewɓe ina njara e weltaare greka, ko ɗum waɗi weltaare ittetee. Ɓe keɓataa weltaare e ɗeen ñalɗi, daɗde ko huunde minit kala sahaa e sahaa fof, kulhuli keewɗi ina keewi.

Ko noon wonirta, gomɗinɓe am e suɓaaɓe e Eklesiya am ina suuɗee e sahaaji caɗeele Yaaquuba e nder kammuuji. Golle maɓɓe ñaaweete e yiite, kala golle mum en keddiiɗe ina keɓa njoɓdi, kala golle mum en mbuulaama ina ñamliree, kono kam en e koye mum en ina hisnee, haa jooni e yiite. Ɓe njogoriino jooɗaade e nokkuuji ɗi ɓe njiɗi e nder kammuuji. Sabu miin, Yeesu HaMasiihu, mi foti jaɓde deftere ndee, uddita maande ndee, so maande ndee udditaama, puccuuji ɗii njaltinee. Ɓe njaltata e leydi ndii ngam foolde, ngam haɓde, ngam addude ñawu e maayde mawnde. Buubaaji jeeɗiɗi ɗii ina pooftoo, bone ina ara, buubaaji jeeɗiɗi tikkere Alla ndii ina njaltinee e leydi ndii fof.

Hare adunaare garoore ndee juutataa duuɓi, sabu Iblis ina anndi o alaa so wonaa waktu seeɗa, kono hare ina woodi e nder ɗeen sahaaji caɗeele. E nder bonanndeeji e kulhuli hare, daaba ummotoo e maayo, e daaba ummotoo e leydi, mo kadi woni annabi fenaande. Ɓeen ɗiɗo ina ngollondira, no ɓe ngollondirta e sirlu ngam halkude Babiloon Sirru. E kalaaɗe sappo ɗee, ɗee ina njiɗi debbo oo, ɓe mbaɗa ɗum mo alaa ko woni, ɓe mbaɗa ɗum mo alaa ko woni, ɓe njaɓa ɓanndu mum, ɓe mbuula ɗum e yiite. Sabu Alla waɗii e ɓerɗe maɓɓe ngam timminde yiɗde mum, e jaɓde, e rokde laamu mum daaba, haa konnguɗi Alla timma. (Ko ɗum waɗi puccuuji ɗii njahata.)

Ɓe ummotoo ko e jam, e kisal, e dewal fenaande. Ɓe kaɗata kala ko yowitii e am, Iisaa Almasiihu, e nder aduna fof, ɓe kaɓat e seniiɓe ɓe foola ɓe, sabu ɗum rokkiraama e juuɗe maɓɓe ngam timminde ɗum. Aliens maɓɓe mbiyata ɓe ina kollita koye mum en, ina ndokka yimɓe ballal mum en, kono ɓee ko maleykaaji jippiiɗi ngaddata fenaande mawnde. Ndaaree, miɗo adda mawɓe ngam timminde tikkere am, ko noon Yahweh hollitiri. Hono no ñalɗi Nuuhu nii, ko noon wonirta e maleykaaji jippiiɗi ɓooyɗi; haa teeŋti e laamɓe ɓooyɗi mbiyateeɓe (ɓe ngonaa laamɓe) ina njiɗi mahde duuɓi kaŋŋe mum en e leydi ndii, ɓe njaltinaa ngam ñaawoore, kono alaa duuɓi kaŋŋe ɗi ɓe keɓata. Bonanndeeji maɓɓe njaltinee, sabu dartinoowo oo yahii, ittaama haa laaɓi, alaa ko haɗata ɓe. Ɓeen yiɗɓe nguurndam mum en ina njippina ɗum haa abada, sabu ɓe njiɗata maande ngam tawtoreede renndo mum en keso, haa teeŋti e laawol mum en kesol. Kala jaɓɗo maande daaba oo ina jogii feccere mum e maayo yiite.

144,000 am ɓee neldaaka ngam hoɗde e gure mum en, ɗo ɓe poti jaɓde maande ngam wuurdude. Kono ɓe neldatee ɗoo e ɗoo, ɓe ngartirta heewɓe e maɓɓe, ɓe ndeennda heewɓe, ɓe mballa ngam addude yimɓe heewɓe e hisnude e am, Yeesu HaMasiihu (e ɗemngal kala): kala nodduɗo innde am haa timma ina hisnee. Alla laɓɓinat gite maɓɓe fof; maayde alaa kadi, alaa mette, alaa wullaango, alaa kadi metti, sabu geɗe ɓooyɗe ɗee njalti. Hono no ñalɗi Nuuhu nii, ɓeen wonɓe e dewal ina nguuri yaasi renndo mum en bonngo, sabu renndo mum en ko renndo greka ngaddoowo ñaawoore duumotoonde e yiite ɗe mbaawaa ñifde; so woto naatde e mum en sabu suɓngo ngoo ko duumotoongo. Ko ɗum waɗi 144,000 am ɓee ngaddata hisnude e ballal e heewɓe.

Ɓe mbaɗata maandeeji doole, kaawniiɗi, e kaawniiɗi, mi ardotoo ɓe laawol e laawol, mi ardotoo laabi maɓɓe haa e ɓeen ɓe mi neli ɓe. Mi hawrundirat baali am e maɓɓe ngam ɓe mbaawa ardoreede e kisal. Kala mo yiɗaa nguurndam mum haa timma ina hisnee. Ɓeen wonɓe e hakkille ina njalta no laaɓal asamaan nii; ɓeen ngartirta heewɓe e nuunɗal no koode nii haa abada.

Daaba ummotooɗo e maayo oo ummotoo, o golla ngam yiɗde bonnde ɓeen laamɓe leydi alkule e nder greka mawɗo, haa teeŋti e juulirde Iblis. O laamotoo leƴƴi, o haalata ko heewi e tooñanngeeji, o waɗata maandeeji e kaawniiɗi fenaande, ɓe ndewa daaba ka rokki daaba ka doole, ɓe ndewa daaba ka, ɓe mbi'a, Hol ko nanndi e daaba ka? Hol baawɗo haɓde e makko? Juulirde Iblis waɗat kala ko mbaawi ngam halkude kala nodduɗo innde am, daaba e annabi fenaande ina doolnira Nuwa e leƴƴi, ina nanndi e ko ɓe laɓɓinta bone e leydi ndii. Kono ɓe kaɓat e seniiɓe, ɓe foola ɓe, ɗum rokkiraama daaba ummotooɗo e maayo ngam jokkude lebbi 42.

Daaba ummotooɗo e leydi ndii kadi ummotoo e nder ɓuuɓri. O jogotoo doole daaba gadano fof, o waɗata maandeeji e kaawniiɗi fenaande keewɗi yeeso daaba gadano, o waɗata natal daaba, ruuhu ina jaɓee ngam addude natal daaba ka haalde, o wiyde kala mo sujidantaa, mo dewaani natal daaba gadano oo ina foti warde.

Sabu daaba ummotooɗo e leydi ndii, ɓe laamɗo leydi alkule, o wontata Mosiiya maɓɓe dugoowo, o mahata suudu dewal maɓɓe (hay so tawii Yahweh yamiraani mo), ko noon wonirta. Ɓe njaɓɓotoo mo no laamɗo mawɗo, moƴƴo, annabi, e laamɗo nii. Daaba ummotooɗo e leydi ndii (haa teeŋti e annabi fenaande mum) ina addana yimɓe fof, tokosɓe e mawɓe, alɗuɓe e miskineeɓe, rimɓe e maccuɓe, jaɓde maande e juuɗe maɓɓe nano walla e tiinde maɓɓe, So tawii alaa neɗɗo soodata walla soodde, so wonaa neɗɗo jogiiɗo maande, walla innde daaba, walla limre innde mum. Ɓe mbaɗii leydi ndii jaɓde maande, system oo laɓɓinaama e nder ñawu nguu mbiyata.

(Windaa ko e Wahyu: Malaa’ika tataɓo oo rewi e maɓɓe, omo wulla e daande toownde, omo wi’a, So neɗɗo goɗɗo sujjdanii daaba mawka ka e natal mum, jaɓi maande mum e tiinde mum, walla e junngo mum, Oon neɗɗo maa yar e inaboojam tikkere Laamɗo, ɗam o hebbinaa tawa alaa ko jillondiraa e heen, e nder mbuudu tikkere Makko; o torree e yiite e mbarnaawe yeeso malaa’ikaaɓe laaɓɓe ɓe, e yeeso Jibinaaɗo oo: Cuurki torra maɓɓe ina ɓeydoo haa poomaa e poomaa: ɓe keɓataa fooftere jemma e nyalawma, ɓeen sujjidiiɓe daaba mawka ka e natal mum, e kala jaɓɗo maande innde mum.)

E nder lebbi 42 gadani ɗi, mi hokkan seedeeɓe am ɗiɗo ɓe doole, ɓe kaɓɓoo comci booro, ɓe nganndina ko feewti e Laamɗo balɗe ujunere e teemedde ɗiɗi e cappanɗe jeegom. Ko ɓee ɗoo ngoni leɗɗe oliiwe ɗiɗi ɗe, e lampaaji ɗiɗi dariiɗi yeeso Laamɗo leydi ndii. So neɗɗo goɗɗo yiɗii waɗde ɓe bone, yiite yaltat e kunndule maɓɓe, ngoon yiite yoola ayɓe maɓɓe: so neɗɗo goɗɗo yiɗii waɗde ɓe bone, ko noon o foti wardeede. Ɓee ɗoo ina njogii baawɗe uddude kammu, mbele toɓo toɓataa e balɗe annabaagal maɓɓe: ɓe njogii kadi doole e dow ndiyam, ɓe mbaawa waylude ngam laatoo ƴiiƴam, ɓe mbaawa kadi fiyde leydi ndii e kala masiibooji, kala nde ɓe njiɗi. Nde ɓe timmini seedanfaagu maɓɓe, daaba mawka ummotoongo e ngaska lugguɗo ka, haɓan e maɓɓe, foola ɓe, wara ɓe.

Daaba mawka ka ummoraade e leydi, kanko woni annabiijo maɓɓe fenaaninkeejo oo, maa addan bonannde mbonnoojel. O addan sadaka ñalnde kala oo dartoo, o lomtinira ngol bonannde mbonnoojel. Ko noon mbii-mi, ko noon kadi Matta winndi: Nde njiy-ɗon bonannde mbonnoojel, nde annabiijo Daniyaal haali ndee, ina darii e nokku laaɓɗo oo (kala jiŋoowo, yo faam), Ndeen, yo ɓeen wonɓe e Yahuuda ndogoy e pelle: Oon wonɗo e dow suudu, woto jippo ngam hooyde hay huunde e nder galle mum: Oon wonɗo e ngesa kadi, woto yiltu ngam hooyde comci mum. Bone woodanii rewɓe reeduɓe ɓe, e ɓeen musnooɓe e ɗeen balɗe! Kono toroɗee mbele dogdu mon wota laato e ndunngu, wota laato kadi e ñalawma aset: Sabu ndeen, maa wood bone mawɗo, mo meeɗaa woodde gila puɗɗal aduna haa hannde, tee meeɗataa woodde kadi.

(Mawninaaɓe ngoni maayɓe e nder Joomiraaɗo gila jooni: Ee, wiyi Ruuhu, mbele ɓe pooftoo e golle maɓɓe; tee golle maɓɓe ina njokki e maɓɓe.)

Ko noon mi reenirta heewɓe, mi suuɗa heewɓe e nder ladde ngam daɗndude ɓe e bone mawɗo oo, sabu so tawno balɗe ɗe ustotaakeno, hay neɗɗo gooto daɗataano. Ndeen, daaba mawka ka e annabiijo fenaaninkeejo oo mooɓtindinan laamɓe leydi ndii e kala dariiɓe e maɓɓe ummoriiɓe e laamu nareeji ɗii ngam ñalawma wolde mawnde ndee. Ko noon kadi mbaɗir-mi Yuhanna Wahyu winnda: Nji’ii-mi kammu ina udditii, nji’ii-mi kadi puccu danewu; jooɗiiɗo e mum oo ina wiyee Koolaaɗo e Goonganteejo, omo ñaawee, omo haɓira nuunɗal. Gite makko ko hono yiite jeyngol, e dow hoore makko ina woodi laamuuji keewɗi; omo jogii innde winndaande, nde hay gooto anndaa, so wonaa kanko e hoore makko. O ɓoorii comci cuurnaaɗi e ƴiiƴam: innde makko ko Konngol Laamɗo. Konuuji wonnooɗi e kammu ina njokki e makko e dow pucci daneeji, ɓe ɓoorii liiliwol laaɓngol, danewol, ceniiɗam. E kunngel makko ina yalta kaafaala nela, mbele omo fiya leƴƴi ɗii e maggal: o laamoto ɓe e sawru njamndi: omo naawa e nder nawaango inaboojam tikkere e mettere Laamɗo Jom Baawɗe oo.

Daaba mawka ka e annabiijo fenaaninkeejo oo fof nanngetee ina nguurdi, cawee e weendu yiite wulndu e mbarnaawe. Heediiɓe ɓe fof mbaree kaafaala jooɗiiɗo e dow puccu oo, kaafaala yaltuka e kunngel makko: colli ɗii fof kebbinaa e teewuuji maɓɓe. Nji’ii-mi jooɗorɗe laamu, ɓe njooɗii e majje, ñaawoore hokkaa ɓe: nji’ii-mi kadi yonkiiji ɓeen taƴanooɓe hoore ngam seedanfaagu Iisaa, e ngam konngol Laamɗo, e ɓeen ɓe sujjidaaki e daaba mawka ka, walla e natal mum, ɓe njaɓaani kadi maande mum e tiiɗe maɓɓe, walla e juuɗe maɓɓe; ɓe nguuri, ɓe laamodii e Almasiihu duuɓi ujunere.

Ko noon malaa’ika am jipportoo e kammuuli, omo jogii cottirgal ngaska lugguɗo ka e sasal mawngal, ngal o haɓɓirta Ibliisa e nder ngaska lugguɗo ka duuɓi ujunere, o waɗa maande mbele o yejjintaa leƴƴi ɗii kadi duuɓi ujunere. E joofnirde laamu am duuɓi ujunere nguu, Ibliisa yaltinee e ngaska lugguɗo ka heddii seeɗa.

O yaltan ngam yejjinde leƴƴi e nder geɗe nay aduna oo, Yajuuja e Majuuja, ngam mooɓtindinde ɓe hono njaareendi maayo, o atakan nokkuure seniiɓe, o huubitoo wuro ceniiɗo ngoo, yiite jippotoo ummoraade e Yahweh, ngoon yiite yoola ɓe haa laaɓa. Seytaane yejjinnooɗo ɓe oo haɓɓetee, werlee ina wuuri e nder weendu yiite mawndu ndu, ndu wulirta mbarnaawe e nder ɗemɗe yiite bulo ɓurɗe wulde. Ndeen, maa wood njoɓdi bonannde mawndi, nuunɗal mawngal waɗee e hoore Seytaane e hoore mum, e malaa’ikaaɓe mum halkaaɓe, e kala naatɓe e murtal maɓɓe; ɓe ummataako kadi abadan, ɓe ngejjite haa laaɓa; ɓe ciftoretaako kadi abadan.

Ndeen Yahweh waɗan kammu keso e leydi heseri, o waɗa hoɗorde makko e nder yimɓe, e jam e deeƴre wonata haa abada, saa’a caɗeele woodataa kadi, ɓe mbaɗataa goɗngol kadi, wanaa e mawnikinaare, wanaa e yeddugol Laamɗo, balɗe maɓɓe fof mawninee.

Ko miin woni Naasaraajo umminaaɗo oo, maayɗo e goɗngol laawol gootol, mi maayataa kadi.

Ko miin woni Yasuwa, kanko woni Mesiya mon goongaajo oo, mo Yahweh neldi ngam timminde alkule baɗanooɗe e Baabiraaɓe ɓe.

Ko miin woni mo gite mum ngoni hono yiite jeyngol, e dow hoore am ina woodi laamuuji keewɗi.

Ko miin woni Joomiraawo Joomiraaɓe e Laamɗo Laamɓe, miɗo ara e nuunɗal ngam ñaawoyde e haɓde.

Ko miin woni gardotooɗo, ardotooɗo 144,000 am ɓe, ɓe njimoyan jimol kesol ngol hay neɗɗo waawaa janngude.

Ko miin woni fooloowo laamu Seytaane, mi foonan hoore mum.

Ko miin woni mo Yahweh neldi ngam hisnude kala goonɗinɓe, saa’a am arii. Ndaaree! Ñaawoore ina ara, jooni woni wakkatiiji ɗii.
