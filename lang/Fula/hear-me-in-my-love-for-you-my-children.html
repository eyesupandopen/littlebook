Seedeeji Ɗiɗi: Ekklesiya
Nder Kuuɗe Paul Nelaaɗo
21 Abriil, 2024

Nanee Lan Nder Yiɗde Am Faa Mo'on Ɓiɓɓe Am!

(E nder konteks nelal annabaaku, “Edomiŋke” na siftina ɓe njaɓi laabi Kananankeeɓe ɗi waylude tagu, mbilewu, sadakaaji ɓiɓɓe, yimɓe-nyaamooɓe, e ko nanndi heen. Esawu goonga oo resi rewɓe ɗiɗo Hittiŋkooɓe, ɓe Kananankeeɓe, o resi kadi debbo tataɓo diga galle Ismaa'iil. Resgol Esawu rewɓe Kananankeeɓe na holla ɗurngol e jiiɓru e mbaawka mbonnere tagu, sabu Kanan bonnino tagu no feewi. Innde Edomiŋke na huutoree nder ɗoo ngam annditinde ɓe tokki huutoraade ɗee aadaaji seytaanankeeji, ndee innde waaway heɓde leñol walla ngenndi fuu nde nanndi e maɓɓe. Laamɗo Herodus laati Edomiŋke, wano non kadi Farisiŋkooɓe heewɓe, Iisaa noddi ɓe ɓiɓɓe Seytaane, ɗum na holla iwrude. Kono, anndee, konngol Edomiŋke no huutoree nder ndee nelal alaa ko jokkondiri e neɗɗo fuu sabu leñol mum walla ngenndi mum. Ko maande huutoraande ngam noddude ɓe cuuɗii na huutora mbilewu Kananankeeɓe walla ɓe iwi e ɗeen lenyonyi ɓe njaajii e nder leyɗeele.)

— William Brooks


Nanee konguɗi am, Ekklesiya am, coŋŋinee noppi mooɗon e hikma e goonga, sabu ndaaree: Konguɗi am ko hikma e goonga. Hikma na eewoo e daande toownde e laabi, faamu na ɗaɓɓita haaldude e mooɗon; jooni on kokkayam noppi mooɗon e nder ɗoftaare yiɗde? Sabu min, Yesuwaa Almasiihu, miɗo wulla e daande toownde ngam mi naniree e jommbaajo am, koo moy woni e nder ɓanndu Almasiihu. Nanee konguɗi am, wiyi Joomiraawo mawɗo, koolniiɗo, on mo laamu mum wonaa e adunaaru, sabu laamu am ko laamu kammu. Wano non e laamu am, wano non e kammu, non kadi laatotoo e dow leydi; sabu mi addan feewal e dow leydi, hay huunde bonnde fuu naatataa e dame am, sabu laamu am ko laamu feewal ɗo rawanndu waaldata e mbeelu e nder jam e kisal. Heewɓe e mooɗon, jommbaajo am, on mawninkinii e nder ɗii balɗe ganndal ɓeydaangal, ɗum kadi wonaa ko haaynii, sabu hol ko konngol am wiyi: Sabu ganndal na fuufna! Kono yiɗde na mahata. Jooni nanee lan e nder yiɗde am faa mooɗon, ɓiɓɓe am!

Nanee konguɗi am, sabu mi haalan dow Edomiŋkooɓe wonɓe hakkunde mooɗon, sabu heewɓe e mooɗon anndaa ko hombo mi siftinta. Mi haalan dow ɓe ɓoornii comci baali kono ko uuruuji nyaamɗi, sabu hiɓe cuuɗoo hiɓe njaha hakkunde mooɗon. Mi haalan dow suudu dewal Seytaane e fow ɓe naati e tagu Baaba am, ɓe Seytaane neldi, sabu kamɓe woni iwrude mboddi ndii. Hey, taa udditee noppi mooɗon, taa muumee gite mooɗon sabu on njaɓaay konguɗi am, onon tokosɓe am muumɓe e muuɗɓe e yiɗde mooɗon, kono jiccee hoore mooɗon e nder tooke, coŋŋinee noppi mooɗon e konguɗi am, sabu mawninkinaare na ardaade halkaare, e ruuhu mawninkinaaru na ardaade fiyugol.

Anndee, tokosɓe am, wonnde konguɗi nder Konngol am, Deftere Laamɗo, ko goonga, laaɓɗi, koolniiɗi: koo nde on paamaay ɗee konguɗi, on na etoo firtaade goonga mum tigi-tigi, on na mbi'ana hoore mooɗon: Ah, ɗum wonaa tigi-tigi kono ko misaalu. Ah, ɗum winndaaka faa am kono ko faa goɗɗo. Ah, ɗee ko taali e kuutorɗe binndol tan. Ah, ɗee ko haalaaji wakkatiiji ɓooyɗi tan, fotaa nanngireede tigi-tigi. An woonoowo! A accay durooɓe majjuɓe mbi'a wonnde Konngol Laamɗo e hoore mum, konnguɗi ɗi kisinam mooɗon iwi, konnguɗi Yahuwah e hoore mum ngonaa goonga kono mbaaway firteede e nder yeddugol? Hey, ngoɗɗitee laabi mooɗon yedduɗi, yimɓe am, ngaree e am e ɓernde udditiinde e hakkilantaagal ɓoornaangal tooke.

On njanngaay naa? Hol ko Jibinannde wiyi e haala kaa nde Yahuwah haɓdi e mboddi ndii sabu fitina mum kalluka ka waɗi e nder Aljanna nder duuɓi keewɗi jooni ɓennuɗi? Mi waɗan konnaagu hakkunde maa e debbo on, e hakkunde iwrude maa e iwrude makko: ngoon taƴan hoore maa, an a taƴan teppere makko. On miilii ɗee konguɗi ko pijirlooji e misaaluuji, ɓiɓɓe am? On ngoonɗinii konnguɗi yedduɗi zamanu anndal ka wumnii hakkillaaji mooɗon e konguɗi am gila ndeen haa jooni? Fenaande njaɓaande e nder gannde mooɗon, sabu ɗe mbonniraama fenaande maɓɓe. Fenaande njaɓaande e nder anndal mooɗon dow tagu aduna, sabu ɗe mbonniraama fenaande maɓɓe. Fenaande njaɓaande e nder taariika mooɗon, sabu on njijjii ko on kon, tokosɓe am, on taƴaama e taariika mooɗon goonga, on na nguura e fenaande gaño on e nder majjere timmunde dow aduna goonga mo nguuroton.

Wano non tigi-tigi ko min woni iwrude uddaande ndee iwrude e debbo, ee, oon debbo mannduɗo nder deftere (mo yoga e mooɗon na ndewa e nder hakke) ka wiyi, yo laato e am wano yiɗde maa ni, mo mi iwi ngam mi arde e nder adunaaru ndu. Mi jibinaama e Mariyama e Betleem Yahuuda, mi mawni ko mi ɓiy sehil, mi mawni e juuɗe Yuusuf mo iwdi Daawuuda, nde o yiɗaano waɗde mo jeewte yeeso yimɓe (sabu o yiɗi mo no feewi), sabu o tawaama omo reedu e Ruuhu Ceniiɗo. Wano non ko miin woni iwrude uddaande ndee nde Laamɗo neldi, non kadi Seytaane ina jogii iwrude. Wanaa sabu omo waawi tagde huunde fuu e hoore mum, sabu ko o jomdo e gujjo, kono o waɗii kire ngam halkaare mooɗon.

Hol no ɗum saɗtiri faa mooɗon ngoonɗinon? E heɗaade fenaande firtaande ko goonga, on njaɓaali geɗe ɓurɗe weeɓde, sabu mi haalanii on geɗe leydi, on ngoonɗinaay: no mbaawirton goonɗinde so mi haalanii on geɗe kammu? On njanngaay naa konguɗi Yahuwah to maccuɗo mum koolniiɗo Job? To ngonno-ɗaa nde mi waɗnoo pongɗe leydi? Anndin, so aɗa jogii faamu. Moy waɗi ɓetirɗe magge, so aɗa anndi? Kaa moy ɓetti gaarawol e dow magge? E dow hol ko pongɗe magge mbaɗaa? Kaa moy waɗi kaaƴel koññol magge? Nde koode subaka njimdi, e ɓiɓɓe Laamɗo fow ngullii e weltaare. Njanngee ndee feccere cakkitiinde kadi, ɓiɓɓe am,

"ɓiɓɓe Laamɗo ngullii e weltaare" — ee, malaa'ika'en ngonnoo toon e tagngo, ɓe na anndi heewde e cuuɗaariiji kammu ɗi fotaani yimɓe anndude, sabu yimɓe njiilodiima duuɓi e nder njurumji mum'en ngam heɓde baawɗe e teddungal.

Ɗum ko ganndal malaa'ika'en yanɓe, ɓiɓɓe am, on njanngaay naa? Kaa on tokki goonɗinde fenaande Edomiŋkooɓe nde ɓe aawi hakkunde mooɗon? Kono hol ko Jibinannde wiyi e haala kaa? Wonnoo jamfaaɓe e dow leydi e nder ɗeen balɗe: e kadi caggal ɗum, nde ɓiɓɓe Laamɗo naati e ɓiɓɓe rewɓe yimɓe, ɓe ndimi ɓiɓɓe; ɓeen laatii worɓe semmbe, ɓe wakkatiiji ɓooyɗi, worɓe mannduɓe. Heewɓe e mooɗon na tampina muñal am e goonɗinde taali bonɗi ɗi ɓiɓɓe Laamɗo iwi e Set, ɓe resi ɓiɓɓe rewɓe bonɓe yimɓe, ɓe ndimi jamfaaɓe e nder bonannde. Yimɓe laatiima bonɓe gila e yannde, hay so no yimɓe bonɓe keewi jibinde fuu, jibinannde maɓɓe laatotaako jamfaaɓe mawɓe sanne. Alaa. Ndeen ko taalol feere ngam majjinde, sabu yoga e nasaraaku Seytaane iwi ko e cuuɗaari mum bonndi, o foolaama, ndeen nasaraaku mum wakkati seeɗa joofiri ko e foolaare timmunde, o daɗataa e weendu mum. Taa hulee mo, kono paamee konguɗi Konngol am e nder konteks mum dewal, taa laateeɓe woonaaɓe e majjuɓe goonga.

Ɓiɓɓe Laamɗo ko aynooɓe nder ɗoo, malaa'ika'en ɓe ndokkaa golle wallude yimɓe kono ɓe acci darnde maɓɓe adannde, sabu ɓe njuurnii ɓiɓɓe rewɓe yimɓe. Sabu hol ko mi wiynoo Yuuda yo anndin e innde am? E Malaa'ika'en ɓe ndeenndaay darnde maɓɓe adannde, kono ɓe acci hoɗorde maɓɓe, o reeni ɓe e nder jolokooje duumiiɗe e les niɓere, haa ñalawma kiita mawɗo. Njanngee ɗum kadi, taa laateeɓe yedduɓe, kono tan ngoonɗinee. Ɓiɓɓe Laamɗo ko malaa'ika'en yanɓe ɓe tuunini hoore mum'en e rewɓe; heewɓe e ɓee rewɓe nanngaama, torraama, koo nde yoga e maɓɓe mbaɗi ɗum e yiɗde mum'en, ɓe ndoƴƴan kiita maɓɓe.

Jibinannde nde ɓe keɓi e nder tuunol maɓɓe, ee, ndee dental haramka jibini jamfaaɓe, taguuji bonɗi baɗaaɗi ngam halkaare mooɗon. On na bonnana hoore mooɗon no feewi e fawaade tan e defte mooɗon 66. Ina woodi defte keewɗe, defte mooɗon Protestan en 66 ɗee ngalaa fow annabaakuuji baɗaaɗi kono ina njogii fow geɗe jowitiiɗe e nguurndam e dewal e nder am Almasiihu Iisaa. Njanngee defte 1 Enok e Yasar e Jubiliiji. Ngonaa defte ceniiɗe faa mooɗon kono ko jannde moƴƴere wasiyaande, siftinaande e nder defte ceniiɗe ɗe njiɗɗon ɗee. Taa laateeɓe laamaaɓe e miijo wonnde tan defte jaɓaaɗe e nder defte goote ko ɗe fow annabaakuuji baɗaaɗi. Njanngee e am.

Kono, Laamɗo halki adunaaru wonnoondu ndeen, sabu hol ko giɗo am Piyeer wiyi: Sabu ɗum ɓe njiɗi majjude, wonnde e konngol Laamɗo kammuuji ngonnoo gila e adan, e leydi ndii na darii e ndiyam, e nder ndiyam, E ɗum adunaaru wonnoondu ndeen, nde ilam yooli, halkaama. E nder balɗe Nuuhu, Baaba am neldi ilam mawɗam ka momti fow kire maɓɓe bonɗe, sabu ɓe mbaɗi kire keewɗe ɗe ngaddi halkaare mawnde e dow leydi. Ɓe njiilii yuɓɓo tagu nguurndam, ɓe ngaddi caɗeele e nder DNA yimɓe, neɗɗo jiiɓaama e dabba, dabba jiiɓaama e liingu, e mbaydi dabba go'o jiiɓaama e mbaydi dabba goɗɗo haa kire yiɗaaɗe ɗee keɓaa. Nden kire ɗee kalki yimɓe. Nden Baaba am halki ɓe, o hisni yimɓe njeetato wuurnooɓe. Nuuhu heɓi moƴƴere yeeso Laamɗo, Nuuhu e saare mum ko kamɓe tan ngonaano bonnuɓe e dow leydi e nder waylude tagu maɓɓe, sabu Nuuhu laatiino gorko feewuɗo.

Njanngee konguɗi am, paamee aduna mooɗon, sabu heewɓe e mooɗon na ngoonɗini fenaande zamanu anndal bonɗo ka salii huunde fuu jowitiinde e ruuhu, ka golli ngam woofinde yimɓe heewɓe e fenaande maɓɓe yeddunde wonnde geɗe leydi tan ngoni ko heewi nafoore. On paamaay naa, ɓe njaɓaali Laamɗo e nder haalaaji maɓɓe yedduɗi? Ɓe njiilanii on e anniya e janngirde luurondirnde: njanngee ngam paamon ndee dogiriiwal Yesuwit en.

Kono hol ko Jibinannde wiyi e haala kaa? Wonnoo Jamfaaɓe e dow leydi e nder ɗeen balɗe: e kadi caggal ɗum.

Caggal holɗee balɗe, on na naamnoo? Caggal balɗe Nuuhu. Sabu caggal ilam leydi ndii fecciraama e ngeɗu ndonu, e nder balɗe Peleg leydi ndii fecciraama e ndonu hakkunde ɓiɓɓe Nuuhu, Sem, Ham e Yafet. Ngam faandaare meeɗen, men tokkan lenyol Ham, sabu e Ham iwi lenyonyi kuddaaɗi, koo Nimrod iwrude e Kush e Misra walla Mizrayim, e Kananankeeɓe bonɓe iwɓe e Kanan bonɗo. Sabu ko Kanan Nuuhu huddi. Sabu Kanan acci ndonu mum, o sosi wuro Tyre e dow ko laati daande maayo Fenisiya, o ardii haa Sidon ɗo Yezebel iwdi. O waɗi ɗum ngam o laatoo ɓadiiɗo e Hooseere Hermon, ɗo aynooɓe njippinoo, e ɗo mi waylaa.

Kanan tawi ko aynooɓe acci, o huutori ganndal maɓɓe malaa'ika'en yanɓe e nder mbilewuuji mawɗi, e aadaaji keewɗi bonɗi mbaɗaama e juuɗe Kanananke on, sabu ɓe sadakii koo ɓiɓɓe maɓɓe. No ɓe bonirnoo ni, no ɓe njaajirnoo ni haa o dañi ñawndude leydi Kanan e kire makko keso baɗaaɗe, ɗo Seytaane golli sabu bonanndeeji makko. Nde Baaba am yaltini Ibraniŋkooɓe e maccungaaku Misra, o yamiri ɓe yo ɓe njaɓtu ndonu maɓɓe e juuɗe ɗee leƴƴi kire jamfaaɓe. (On tokki saldeede goonɗinde Konguɗi am naa?) On njanngaay naa haala bonka ka sappoo e nder njillooɓe sappo e ɗiɗo ɓe Muusaa nelnoo e leydi Kanan ngam njilloya? Sabu tan Yosuwa e Kaleb kaaldi konguɗi goonɗinal, kono haala bonka ka hokkaa ka laati goonga tigi-tigi, sey wonde ko haala hakkeejo, yedduɗo, ka sali konguɗi Baaba am ngam ko ɓe njiiri e gite maɓɓe, sabu Laamɗo na ɓuri mawde gite neɗɗo fuu, tokosɓe am. Njanngee haala kaa e nder Deftere Sariyaaji ɗiɗaɓere: Kono worɓe yahdiiɓe e makko mbiyi, Men mbaawaa yaltude ngam haɓde e yimɓe ɓee, sabu ɓe na ɓuri en semmbe. Ɓe ngaddi haala bonka dow leydi ndi ɓe njiiloynoo, to ɓiɓɓe Isra'iila, ɓe mbi'i, Leydi ndi men njahinoo, ngam njiiloya, ko leydi nyaamoyndi hoɗɓe e mum, e yimɓe fow ɓe njiinoo men e mum ko worɓe mawɓe sanne. E toon men njiinoo jamfaaɓe ɓee, ɓiɓɓe Anak, iwɓe e jamfaaɓe: e men laati e gite amen wano mbabbaaji ni, e non men ngonirnoo e gite maɓɓe.

Nii woni, Yosuwa laɓɓini leydi ndin e ɗee bonanndeeji anndindeeji, ɓe ngonnoo ko en mbaawi wi'ude yimɓe; ɓe ngonnoo ko mboreeji jillondirɗi, mbaɗaaɗi ngam halkude leñol Almasiihu, no Seyɗaani yiɗiri haɗude garal am gadanal ngal. Kono, ɓiɓɓe Isra'iila ɓe mbaɗaani e hoolaare golle ɗe Baaba am halfini ɓe ɗee, ɗe keɓtingol ndonu maɓɓe e juuɗe nannguɓe e doole. Heewɓe e ɗee tagooje ko nyaamooɓe yimɓe; on njanngaano na ɗo leydi ndin nyaamunoo hoɗɓe e mayri ɗoo? Hol ko deftere Ñaawooɓe wi'ata e ngoo haala: Joomiraawo wonidii e Yahuuda, o riiwi hoɗɓe e dow hooseere ndee, kono o waawaano riiwude hoɗɓe e nder falnde ndee, sabu ɓe njoginoo ko sareeteeji njamndi.

Deftere Ñaawooɓe ndee ina wi'a kadi: Manasse kadi riiwaani hoɗɓe e Betse'an e gure mum, walla Taanak e gure mum, walla hoɗɓe e Dor e gure mum, walla hoɗɓe e Ibleyam e gure mum, walla hoɗɓe e Megiddo e gure mum: kono Kananankooɓe ɓee njaɓi hoɗude e ndeen leydi. Nde Isra'iila semmbidi, ɓe njoɓini Kananankooɓe ɓee njoɓdi, ɓe riiwaani ɓe haa laaɓi. Hol ko deftere Limgal wi'ata e ɗuum: Kono so on riiwaani hoɗɓe e leydi ndin yeeso mon, maa heddooɓe e maɓɓe ɓee ngona gi'e e nder gite mon, e gi'e e nder bangeeji mon, ɓe torra on e nder leydi ndi hoɗu-ɗon ndin.

Nii woni, binndi ɗii timmi, leƴƴi Kananankooɓe ɓee ina njokki torrude ɓiɓɓe Alla haa hannde. Ɓe jillondirii e tagu Alla, ɓe cuuɗii e yeeso yimɓe, haa ɓe nanngi pinal e jikkuuji leƴƴi ɗi ɓe koɗdii ɗii. Kono joofnirde ndee ko wootere sahaa kala: Ɓe kalkata leƴƴi ɗi ɓe koɗdii ɗii e anniya (Alla waɗii ɓe dogooɓe e jahooɓe e dow leydi). Ɓe kaalii fenaande mawnde, ɓe yeeyiri ndeen fenaande goonga. Ɓe keɓtii leyɗeele mon, on njiyataa ɓe kadi, sabu gite mon ina mbumni e laabi maɓɓe, sibu heewɓe e mon mbaɗdii e maɓɓe nanondiral, haa e juulirde Seyɗaani ndee.

Ngartiree e Konngol am laaɓungol ngol, accitee konnguɗi e laabi Edominkooɓe ɓee. Accitee mawninde konnguɗi bonɗi dow Konngol Baaba am ɓurngol senieede ngol. Sabu heewɓe e ɗii Kananankooɓe ngonti Idumiyankooɓe e nder yontaaji, ɓe koɗdi e Yahuuda. Nde John Hyrcanus heɓtini Idumiya, o naatni Idumiyankooɓe ɓee e diine Yahuudiyanke, Yahuudiya jibinaa e jillondiral Yahuuda e Idumiya. Nii woni, mi darii e maɓɓe e nder Isra'iila duuɓi 2000 jooni, darnde am ndee winndaama e nder Linjiilaaji ɗii, mbele on mbaawa yahrude no njahruno-mi nii. Konnguɗi maɓɓe bonnii Tawreeta, aadaaji maɓɓe kaaleteɗi e golle maɓɓe cuuɗiiɗe ummoriiɗe Babilon cuuɗinoo e nder leydi am, sabu leydi am nanngaama e juuɗe fena-hooreeɓe, woni Juulirde Seyɗaani ndee.

Gila e Jibinannde 3:15, wolde iwdi wonii hakkunde Alla e Seyɗaani, iwdi Seyɗaani ndii ina haɓa ngam heɓde dowla, ngu o heɓataa. Mi heɓii poolgu, onon ɓiɓɓe am, mi semmbinii on, onon ɓiɓɓe am, e kala sifaa bonannde, hay huunde waawaa haɗude on; sabu miin, Iisaa Almasiihu, mi foolii laamu gaño on. Ndaree e maɓɓe, accitee saaktude laabi maɓɓe bonɗi ɗii. Ngurtee e maɓɓe, sabu ɓe mbaɗtii nanndude e mooɗon. On ngoonɗinaani na ngeedan am alkama e ayre? O waɗani ɓe kadi ngeedan goɗɗan, o wiyi: Laamu kammu ina nanndi e neɗɗo ciiwɗo aawdi moƴƴiri e nder ngesa mum: Kono nde yimɓe ɓee njaanii, gaño makko ari, o aawi ayre e nder alkama kan, o yahi. Ngurtee e maɓɓe, ceertonee e juulirde Seyɗaani, ɓeen wi'ooɓe ko ɓe Yahuudiyankooɓe kono ina fena, ɓe ngonaa. Sabu Isra'iila saaktaama e nder leƴƴi goɗɗi ɗii, mi nelaaka so wonaa e baali majjuɗi ɗi galle Isra'iila; nii woni, jamaanu leƴƴi goɗɗi ɗii ina jogori timmude, mi waɗii e ɗiɗo ɓee neɗɗo gooto keso e nder am, mi addi jam.

Onon ɓiɓɓe am, mi hokkii on tinndinoore daartol mon, sabu daartol neɗɗaagu ina rewi gila e Nuuhu haa e Aadama, Alla waɗii e ƴiiƴam gootam leƴƴi kala. Onon ɓiɓɓe am, waktu oo ina jogori timmude, kono ɗuum firti ko golle mon timmii. Miɗo jokki noddude ɓiɓɓe am ngam ndara e am, ngam ngollana kam. Sabu mi waɗii on gollooɓe baawɓe e Alkawal Kesal ngal e nder ƴiiƴam am. On njanngaano na ɗo mbii-mi almuɓɓe am so ɓe mbiyii ndee hooseere ittu, werlee e maayo, so ɓe cikkaani, maa ɓe keɓ ko ɓe njiɗi koo? Mi wasiyii on yo on ndutto seeɗa, sabu haala ka ina fuɗɗoo e ‘koolaade Alla’ (sabu e Alla, hay huunde waawaa waasde waɗeede), on mbaawaa waɗde hay huunde so wonaa e am, onon tokosɓe am.

Heewɓe e mon njannginaama fenaande Edominkooɓe, ndartinoonde on e laabi maɓɓe. Kono so on koolii Alla, on njamirat ko waawaa waɗeede, tee ko ɗuum pot-ɗon waɗde e golle ɗe nel-mi on ɗee. Miɗo nelda on, onon ɓiɓɓe am, ngam mooftude baali heddiiɗi ɗii, sabu heewɓe maa ngar e am e ɗee minitaaji cakkitiiɗi, mi salotaako ɓe, kono miɗo ɗaɓɓitiri ɓe haa jooni e yurmeende. Udditee kunuɗe mon e hoolaare, ndaree e am, ngollanee ɓiɓɓe am.

Mi haalanii on no kuutorirton baawɗe am, kono on calii miijo am, mi haalan kadi yejjitooɓe e mon ɓee. Hol ko waɗi on ina ɗaɓɓa e kulol yo ruuhu yaltu e neɗɗo e demɗe didɗo, on kuutora innde am, Iisaa Almasiihu, e laawol ngol alaa hoolaare? Woto kuutoree innde am e meere! Njamiree ruuhuuji ɗii e innde am e hoolaare mawnde, kooloree konnguɗi ɗi mi rokkata on ɗii, ruuhuuji bonɗi ɗii njaltirat e baawɗe am rewrude e mon!

Hol ko waɗi on ina ɗaɓɓa e kulhuli yo ñawu yaltu, tawi oɗon poti haalde e ñawu nguu e hoolaare e innde am, on njamira ko waawaa waɗeede e innde am, on cellina ñawɓe. Mbiyee ndeen hooseere: ittu, werlee e maayo; cellinee ñawɓe, ndiiwee ruuhuuji, umminee maayɓe, njamiree ko waawaa waɗeede, sabu ko noon mbaawinir-mi on.

On njanngaano na wonde adiiɓe laatooɓe sakkitiiɓe, e sakkitiiɓe laatooɓe adiiɓe? Anndee, onon ɓiɓɓe am, miɗo yaha e baawɗe mawɗe ɓurɗe ɗe mbaɗ-mi e teemedannde adannde ndee, mi wuddii Ruuhu am e njuuteendi timmundi e dow leydi hee. Hol e mon jogiiɗo cuusal daranaade kam? Haalande kam konnguɗi ɗi mi rokkata on ɗii? Hol e mon jogiiɗo hoolaare haa hulataa humpito bonngo, kono haɓata e bonannde ndee e hoore mum? Sabu ɓurɗo mawnude ko wonɗo e mon on, omo ɓuri wonɗo e aduna on. Woto on laato maccuɓe Kanananke, sabu ɓe njuumaama yo ɓe laato maccuɓe mon. Yanti heen, woto on njahdondir e iwdiiji leƴƴi, sabu mi nelaama e leƴƴi kala mbele mi hisna yoga e maɓɓe, e waɗde ɗum, miɗo hisna Isra'iila goongaajo, sabu o riiwaama e nder leƴƴi, onon kala on ngoni Isra'iila goongaajo Alla!

Ndaree e am, kaalanee kam, sabu waktu ina jogori timmude. Ma on laato na gooto e yimɓe arɓe e jappeere am ñaawoore, tawi ina njogii leɗɗe, huɗo, e boore, ngam ñalnde mawnde araynde ndee? Walla ma on ndokkam kaŋŋe, kaalis, e kaaƴe tedduɗe, sabu yimɓe am ko jawdi am. Ndimeen jeyaaɓe ɓee, ngollidee e am e ɗee minitaaji cakkitiiɗi. Tawa alaa jeddi, mawɗo ko sirlu dewal: Alla feeñii e ɓanndu, feewnaa e Ruuhu, yiyraa e malaa'ika'en, waajnaama e leƴƴi goɗɗi, goonɗinaa e aduna, jaɓɓaama haa e teddungal.

Onon ɓiɓɓe am, ko miin woni Konngol ngol laatii ɓanndu, miin e Baaba am ko en gootum. E noon sifaa, ngonee gootum e amen e ɗooftaare mon e am, sabu ɓooyataa on nanndat e am, on njaɓɓoyee e teddungal, sabu ko miin woni ɓiɓɓe adanɗe ummoriiɗe e maayɓe, mi hollan on eklesiya tedduɗo, mo alaa toɓɓere, walla nyanyere, walla hay huunde hono noon; kono yo o laato ceniiɗo, mo alaa ella. Waktu oo ina jogori timmude, woto on njuumtu. Ngollidee e am e ɗee minitaaji cakkitiiɗi, woto on njuumtu udditde kunuɗe mon. Ma mi addan on ɓe, ngollee golle am, accitee golle mon. Ma mi waɗ heewɓe golle doole e juuɗe mon, tan woto on cikku!

Ko miin woni aawdi fodaandi ndii ummoraade e debbo.

Ko miin woni aawdi fodaandi ndii, nelaandi e Alla.

Miɗo jokki noddude ɓiɓɓe am yo ndaru e am, yo ngollanam. Miɗo nelda on, onon ɓiɓɓe am, ngam mooftude baali heddiiɗi ɗii.

Miɗo hisna Isra'iila goongaajo, sabu o riiwaama e nder leƴƴi, onon kala on ngoni Isra'iila goongaajo Alla!

Mi nelaama e leƴƴi kala mbele mi hisna yoga e maɓɓe.

Ko miin woni Konngol ngol laatii ɓanndu, miin e Baaba am ko en gootum.

Ko miin woni ɓiɓɓe adanɗe ummoriiɗe e maayɓe.
