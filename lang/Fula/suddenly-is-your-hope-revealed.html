Ko weli e ko haaɗi
Ut 09, 2024

Yaakaare Mo'on Feññinaama e Tummude!

Kawree, nanee daande Joomiraaɗo mo'on, daande Iisaa Nasaraajo, sabu ko miin woni on yeɗinooɗo hoore am laawol gootol, fof, haa abada, mi laatii Jawgel Paska timmungel to Yaahwe ngam junuubaaji mo'on, mi laatii Alla mawɗo duumiiɗo on, mawɗo sadakaaji mo'on duumiiɗo e laawol Melkisedek, fii yo on keɓe yaafuya junuubaaji. Ngaree e am, ngaddon noppi mo'on e ɓerɗe mo'on ngam heɗaade e goonɗinal timmungal konnguɗi am goonɗinɗi! Ee, miɗo ñaagii on yo on nane, wonaa tan heɗaade sawtu konnguɗi am, kono yo on nane e ɓerɗe mo'on haa paamon anniya am, keɓon deeƴere e yiɗde am e mo'on, kadi yo on njaɓu e newaare konngol ngol aawetee e mo'on, ngol waawi hisnude pittaali mo'on.

Sabu miɗo haalda e goonɗinɓe e nder Eklesiya am, hono ɓanndu am, hono jommbaajo am, hono ɓeen fof ɓe ɓooyataa mi mooɓta e hoore am e kisal to asamaanuuji, e nder jooɗorde laamu Yaahwe. Sabu ko miin deenoowo pittaali mo'on, kadi ko miin toppitiiɗo ɓeydagol mo'on duumingol, haa mawniron no lekki dartiiɗi yeeso am, sabu ko miin Yaahwe hokki golloowo e deenoowo ngesa mum ceniika sanne, hono ngesa pittaali rimɗinaaɓe. Sabu onon woni luulu'uure teddunde, e nder jawdiiji Yaahwe keewɗi, onon jeyaa ko e ko alaa coggu, ko hay haayre wootere accaaka e hisnugol mo'on, hay golle goote mbaɗaaka ngam seernude on e wallude on e hollude on to Yaahwe, hono Baaba mo'on Jom-bawɗe fof, no suka debbo laaɓɗo mo alaa toɓɓere, alaa nyorsol, alaa hay huunde hono noon. Ko ɗum waɗi, wote kulee, wote ngeɗee sikkitaare hay seeda e Konnguɗi am!

Jooni miɗo naamnii on, yiɗɓe am, hol ko addani ɓerɗe mo'on torraade? Hol ko waɗi hiɗon ndima ko'e mo'on no ko'e mo'on njogii semteende? Mbele on ngeɗii Seyɗaneeji naatii e ɓerɗe mo'on, ina njuurnitoo konnguɗi deeƴre fenaande ɗi njaltinta tan ko keeferaaku, nde ɗi njiɗi seeltude e bonnude kala ko laaɓi? Mbele on njaɓan ngol mboddi kiingol e mbaawka mum fenaande duumingol e njiyaagu mum ɓeydotoongu, ngol naatata e noppi mo'on konnguɗi fenaande sanne ɗi liɓata goonɗinal mo'on, no waɗiri e heewɓe ngeɗunooɓe hakkillaaji mum'en bonnee e hakkilanaade miin tan, Almasiihu Iisaa Joomiraaɗo mo'on?

Sabu alaa goɗɗo deenotooɗo on, woɓɓe ɓee fof ko fenooɓe tan. Alaa Iisaa goɗɗo so wonaa nanndinɗe puyɗe e penuuje meereeje peewnaaɗe ngam njiyaade. Ko ɗum waɗi miɗo ñaagii on wote puyɗinee ndee anndal keeringal ngal miɗo rokka on, haa njokkon laataade no fitirla jalboowo hay e nder ɗee balɗe cakkitiiɗe hade aduna mo ngannduno-ɗon oo momtude e wayleede haa abada, gila e bonannde mum hannde ndee haa e ñaawoore duumiinde ndi jooni yani e bonɓe njiɗɓe jogitaade ndonu am. Sabu leydi ndii ko am, e timmal mayri fof, kadi ko miin woni Joomiraaɗo mawɗo duumiiɗo, Laamɗo laamɓe, hono Joomiraaɗo mo'on peewɗo, kadi ko miin heɓtoowo ndonu am e juuɗe jooɗiiɓe ɓe ngalaa hakke, ɓe ngonaa laamɓe, ɓe ngonaa allaaji, ɓe ngonaa yimɓe ummoriiɓe e adunaaji goɗɗi, kono ko yaniɓe fenooɓe, sabu fof ko fenooɓe. Ñalɗi maɓɓe ma njoofir e joofnirde duumiinde e ndonu maɓɓe duumiingu e nder yiite bulo ɓurnge wulde.

Sabu muuseeji jibingol ɗii ndartaa jooni, ina woodi maandeji keewɗi to dow e maandeji keewɗi e leydi, ɗi peeñata, ɗi ndartaa. Ɗeeɗoo ko ngam anndinde e haa keɓon yaakaare, ko ɗum waɗi wote njaare e miijitaade ɗe no jalnooɓe ɓee nii! Golle juuɗe Yaahwe baɗaaɗe e juuɗe maleyka'en mum tedduɓe, kadi ko e hoore mum Alla fonndiri ɗe e sahaa goɗɗo, e dow yamiroore mum duumiinde, ina kollita kewuuji asamaan ɓattooji, sabu ñalawma oo yettima, o ari e mo'on yiɗɓe am, ko ɗum waɗi wote kulee! Wakkatiiji ngam mooftude goonɗinɓe am ngam hawrude e am e duule nde miɗo jippoo e asamaan e daande maleyka mawɗo e callalol teddungol Alla, ko ɗum waɗi ngoonɗinee Konnguɗi am, sabu ko ɗi eeey e aamiin!

Hol e hol ko foti torraade on, ɓiɓɓe am? Mbele hiɗon kuli jommbaajo oo araani, o nanga junngo jommbaajo makko e ahdi duumiindi no fodaaka gila ko ɓooyi? Mbele Konngol Alla ngol wontii meere? Alla haɗi! No winndiraa e no anndiniraa, noone nii maa laatoo, nde e tummude mi nanngitoytoo on e tikkere haa e kisal asamaanuuji ɗo njahnoomi mi heblana on nokku, sabu e nder galle Baaba am ina woodi cuuɗi keewɗi. Noone nii on ngonndidat e am haa abada, on laatoo laamɓe e sadakaaji yeeso Yaahwe, Alla mawɗo duumiiɗo, sabu ko ɗum o anniyii e o heblanii e nguurndam mo'on, hol baawɗo haɗde anniyaaji e heblande Alla duumiiɗo oo?

Miɗo haalana on goonga, alaa hay huunde, alaa hay gooto baawɗo haɗde hay seeɗa anniyaaji El Shaddai, hay gooto waawaa daraade (hay e seede salaare) tawa o deƴƴinaaka e hulde ngam semmbe timmuɗo, manngu e dimbande nde Yaahwe hay so o dimmbii seeɗa ngam ummaade e jooɗorde laamu mum duumiinde, asamaan e leydi fof ndimban e woodgol makko teddungol. Sabu hay so tawii Yaahwe suuɗiima hoore mum e newaare, e yiɗde, e yurmeende gila e balɗe keewɗe jooni (sabu ñalawma gooto ko no duuɓi ujunere nii yeeso Alla duumiiɗo), jooni ko wakkati mo Yaahwe suɓii mo o feññinanta tagooje makko, nde ɓe fof nganndata wonde Alla ina woodi to asamaan, kadi maa ɓe paam no feewi wonde no woniri to dow, noon maa wonir to leydi, sabu Yaahwe ko Alla asamaan e leydi fof, o laamii dow tagooje makko, alaa goɗɗo gaagaa makko e semmbe, e manngu, e baawɗe. (Yaniɓe ɓee nanngaama e kulol duumingol) Ko ɗum waɗi wote kulee, Ɓiɓɓe Toowɗo oo, sabu Alla mawɗo duumiiɗo, hono Yaahwe e hoore mum, ko kañum haɓananta on, kañum hisnata on, rimɗina on, wayla nguurndam mo'on wonta manngu duumiiŋgu e nder leplel gite.

Ko ɗum waɗi hiɗon muƴataa? Mbele hiɗon njiyri no Joomiraaɗo mo'on duumiiɗo e jommbaajo mo'on yejjitii on, jommbaajo am? Mbele hisnoowo mo'on duumiiɗo woppii ɓanndu mum, o acci nde alaa hooreejo? Alla haɗi hay miijo noon! Hol huunde nde Yaahwe anndini tagooje mum ina arta, nde waɗaani caggal mum? Mbele Konnguɗi Nuuhu njani e leydi yooriindi no fodoore meere nde o wonnoo waajotooɗo peewal duuɓi teemedere e noogas e aduna mo wonnoo oon? Alaa! Kono e tummude e doole mawɗe ndiyam yaltinaa e nder lepte, o ɓuri toowde dow ko'e kaaƴe toowɗe e leydi e balɗe garooje ɗee.

Ko ɗum waɗi miɗo wasiyii on wote naatite e limre ɓeen majjuɓe e anniya. Ɓe cuɓii majjere e anniya ngam welsindaade wonde e konngol Alla asamaanuuji ngonnoo gila ko ɓooyi, e leydi ndii dariiɗo e ndiyam e nder ndiyam: Ko ɗum waɗi aduna oon, nde yoolnaa e ndiyam, halkii: Kono asamaanuuji e leydi, ɗi ngoodi jooni, e konngol gootol ngol, ndesaa ngam yiite haa ñalawma ñaawoore e halkaare worɓe bonɓe. Jooni miɗo anndina kadi wonde ñalawma yiite e ñaawoore e halkaare worɓe bonɓe yettima, no fodaaka gila ko ɓooyi, noone nii maa laatoo e tummude.

Jooni miɗo naamnii on kadi, sukaaɓe am tokosɓe, Mbele Alla ko neɗɗo haa o fena? Mbele miin, Iisaa Almasiihu peewɗo, mi koolnataako haa mi yejjita on, mi rokka on yaakaare fenaande? Mbele mi yejjitii on, mi yahi miɗo liɗa? Miɗo naamnii on, yiɗɓe am, mbele mi hokkii toɓɓere am cakkitiinde e nder muuseendi e dow gaafaangal haa mi woppa on, nde mi fodanii on fodoore mawnde sanne e teddunde, hay fodoore rokkaande e hoore mum Yaahwe e sirlu mum duumiiɗo, hono sirlu mawɗo Alla?

Alla haɗi hay miijo noon, ko ɗum waɗi annditee miijooji bonɗi ɗi Seytaane werlii e mo'on, njaɓee, ceernee ɗi, lomtinon miijooji meereeji e bonɗi ɗiin e binndi ceniɗi, hono fodoore Yaahwe ndi woni eeey e aamiin. Kono e kala wakkati, haa teeŋti noon e wakkatiiji hono ɗii, ina winndaa, ko ɗum waɗi Pool winndani Korintinaaɓe e innde am: Kono miɗo huli, woto no mboddi ndii njiyaari Hawa e njiyaagu mum, noon kadi hakkillaaji mo'on mbonniree e ngootaagu hakkilantaagal ngo woni e Almasiihu. Ko ɗum waɗi miɗo wasiyii on, ɓiɓɓe am, hay yiɗɓe am sanne e ɓe njiɗmi, yo on ndeenan hakkilantaagal mo'on ngootaagal e am, e konnguɗi am, sabu ko miin woni Konngol Alla ngol laatii neɗɗo, kala Konngol Alla ma laatoo, hay gootol e Konnguɗi am ɓurɗi famɗude ma taƴataa, ɗi njuurnitaa e ɗi mballitaa e Ruuhu Ceniiɗo mawɗo duumiiɗo oo, hono Yaahwe, Alla mawɗo duumiiɗo!

Ko ɗum waɗi miɗo wasiyii on, ɓiɓɓe am, hay yiɗɓe am sanne e ɓe njiɗmi, hay onon weltaare am e laamu am: poofteree e am, poofteree e golle am, ceeeree e golle mo'on. Sabu ina winndaa: Woodanii yimɓe Alla pooftol. Sabu on naatɗo e pooftol mum, o dartiima e golle makko, no Alla waɗiri e mum. Waɗee golle ngam naatde e pooftol am, woto hay gooto yanu e yeru gooto keeferaaku. Ɓiɓɓe am, gollee ngam dartaade e golle mo'on, mbaɗon golle ɗe mi rokki on ngam timminde, wote mbeɗee e paamgol mo'on, kono e laabi mo'on fof njaɓee miin, Almasiihu Iisaa Joomiraaɗo mo'on ɓamtiiɗo, maa on keɓ pooftol e nder gese koreeje e kisal, haa teeŋti noon e les deenal junngo am semmbe, sabu ko miin woni Yesuwa HaMasiyak, kadi ko miin woni gaynaako mo'on mawɗo mo ɓooyataa feññude!

Jooni miɗo naamnii on, yiɗɓe am, mbele on ngartan nguurndude e nder yimɓe majjuɓe jalnooɓe, ɓe njiɗi faddaade on e laabi maɓɓe keeferaaku? Mbele on puyɗinay konnguɗi am e hay maandeji keewɗi, kaawniiɗi e kaawisaaji ɗi nduurnii on? Mbele on majjii haa on miijii hiɗon mbaawi jokkude nguurndam no majjuɓe e musiiba e ñaawoore ɓattoore? Mbele on ngartan nguurndude e nder ñaaweeɓe yahooɓe, saliiɓe tuubde, cuɓiiɓe ñaawoore tiiɗnde e lepte? Miɗo haalana on laaɓɗum, so on etii, on tawan ko'e mo'on no meeɗenɓe ɓe njahata gaagaa jeertine am, ɓe leptetee ko alaa kaanga, ko ɗum waɗi on jeertinaama.

Paamee wonde aduna mo ngon-ɗon jooni oo nanndaani e no wonnoo hay e yontereeji seeɗa jooni, omo jokki e waylaade e yaawnde ɓeydotoonde. Paamee wonde hay gooto e mo'on wuuraani e aduna mo woodi jooni oo, sabu aduna mo ngon-ɗon jooni oo, o sokeede e ñaawoore, kono o accaama o feññina anndinooje tedduɗe dewgal garoowol to asamaan ɗo miin Almasiihu Iisaa mi resata goonɗinɓe am haa abada, noone nii ɓe ngonndidirta e am. Nde jommbaajo am e Eklesiya am nanngitaama e tummude, ndeen halkaare tummude yani e jalnooɓe fof e worɓe bonɓe jalnooɓe on, ina mbi'a: Jaŋngo maa nanndu e hannde, hannde nanndi e keenya, mi yi'ataa musiiba, mi wonaa wuddu, kono ko mi laamɗo laamiiɗo dow kala huunde! So wonaa e tummude halkaare maɓɓe ari e maɓɓe no debbo jibinoowo, ɓe ndawataa.

Ko ɗum waɗi njaltee e maɓɓe, ceertee e KALA huunde e laabi mo'on fof. On mbaawaa jogaade feccere walla geɗal e maɓɓe, so wonaa on ndenndat e ñawanɗe maɓɓe. Paamee wonde kewuuji puɗɗiima, hay kewuuji ɗi memata junuubankeeɓe, kono miɗo rokkata goonɗinɓe am kisal haa ɗee ñaawoore bonnataa on, ɓiɓɓe am. Sabu ɗee muuseeji jibingol ɗi ndartaa ɗii, ko ngam finndinde ɓeen cakkitiiɓe ɗaaniiɓe e ngam seertude ɓeen cakkitiiɓe naatɓe e Laana-ndiyam hade musiiba yannde e tummude, wakkati timma. On mbaawaa kadi wuurdude nguurndam meere, oɗon poti yahdude e hakkilantaagal mawngal, ko ɗum waɗi nanee jeertine am, paamee wonde mi rokki on hakkilantaagal mawngal e paamgol haa mbaawon anndude wakkatiiji ɗi ngon-ɗon, hay e nder ɓeen ɗaaniiɓe sanne, ɓe nganndaa musiiba ɓattoowo, ɓe muusata sabu mum.

Ko ɗum waɗi paamee wonde wakkati heddiiɗo oo alaa nafoore, o woni tan ko e anniyaaji Yaahwe hade ñaawoore yannde e tummude. Ee, gaño oo rokkaama fartaŋŋe heblanaade njanngu mum e dow Babilon sirlu, sabu ko noon Yaahwe hokkiri mo ngam timminde. Kono paamee no feewi wonde Seytaane ko gaño foolaaɗo, o sokaama e leydi ngam ñaawoore, o accaama tan o jokkina anniyaaji Yaahwe, o jooɗoto o deƴƴii, o heɗo haɗoowo oo ittee, o waawaa hay huunde waɗde so wonaa ko o accaama, ko ɗum waɗi wote kulee wullaango ngol mbaroodi alaa nyiƴe, sabu laamɗo tokooso oo sokaama e foolde! Sabu goonga, yaniɓe ɓee ina kulii on, ɓe paamii, ɓe anndi wonde ɓeen ɓe torlunoo ko ɓeen ngaddata ñaawoore e lepte peewɗe e nder ɓerɗe maɓɓe haa abada. Holɗum? Mbele on nganndaa on ñaawoyay maleyka'en?

Nii woni, miɗo yamo on, giɗaaɓe am, so on njaɓataa jooni yurmeende e moƴƴere am nde njahran-mi majjuɓe ɓee, hay so tawi ñaawoore e musiibaaji ɗii fof mbaɗaama ngam haɓɓitaade e dow mum en e wakkati gooto? Mbele on njejjitii duuɓi keewɗi ɗi yurmeende e moƴƴere nde mi hokki on tawa on heɓaani ɗum, ngam on kebbina junuubaaji mon hade mon ruttaade e peewal? Mbele on calotooɓe ɓe njiɗata naatde e laana ndiyam (Arka) ngam en kawra ko adii seeɗa? Miɗo jogii hoolaare e mon, ɓiɓɓe am, ko ɗum waɗi miɗo ñaagoo on muñal e njentaare, hay hade musiibaaji ɗii fof haɓɓitaade e wakkati gooto.

So tawii woodii e mon goɗɗo sikkuɗo ina jogii daliilu ngam ñaawoore Iblisa e laamu mum, miin miɗo jogii daliilu ɓurɗo kala ko naati e ɓerɗe mon. Hol e mon haɓantooɗo Iblisa haa toɓɓere mum cakkitiinde nde o daasii e dow leggal ngal e nder muuseeki mawki? Sabu miin mi ronndii e dow yiɗde am leggal am ngal, mi rokkii hoore am e nasru duumotooɗo nde mi tooñaa e juuɗe bonɓe, nde mi lomtii on e ñaawoore e nder muuseeki mawki caggal damal wuro ngo e nder ɓurɓe bonde e junuubankooɓe. Ƴiiƴam am laaɓnoo, alaa e sago bonannde fof ina fawaa e am. Kono sabu giɗli am e Baaba am e giɗli am e mon, mi rokkii nguurndam am e dow yiɗde am, mi waɗtayno ɗum kadi e nder wakkati gooto, hay so tawii ɗum haanaani. Ko ɗum waɗi, hay e nder caɗeele mon jooni ɗee, njokkee yeru am muñal ngal, anndee wonde e wakkati gooto on naatata e hisde duumotoonde!

Ko ɗum waɗi, miɗo yamo on nde njokkoton nanngude haa to bannge cakkitiingal, hay so tawi ko to bannge ɗo nanngeteeɗon e wakkati gooto e nder leplel gite haa to asamaanuuji ngam heɓde njoɓdi duumotoondi e anniya duumotooɗo, kono maayɓe e Almasiihu ɓee ummotoo ko adii, on naatda e maɓɓe e wakkati gooto e nder mawninngol duumotoongol. Sabu anniyaaji duumotooɗi ɗi noddaaɗon, cuɓaaɗon, maandeede, reeneede ɗii, kebbinta anniyaaji duumotooɗi Jomiraawo ɓurɗo toowde, teeru fof ina heddii feeñde e mon, ɓiɓɓe am, ko ɗum waɗi njaaynanon am hono fitillaaji am jooni e nder ndee darnde cakkitiinde hade musiibaaji bonɗi haɓɓitaade e leydi. Mi addan on ɓe ngam on njaha e maɓɓe to am, sabu on ngoni haa jooni nelaaɓe am haa nde nanngeteeɗon e wakkati gooto haa to asamaanuuji. Kadi e wakkati gooto musiiba mawɗo maa yani.

Faamee wonde wakkati oo timmii, alaa ko waawi ruttaade e kewuuji garooji ɗii, ɗi njahdata e hoɗɓe e leydi ndii, sabu mawngu woni ñaawoore garoore ndee e dow junuubankooɓe. Ko ɗum waɗi, miɗo yamo on, miɗo doolnira on e yurmeende, e moƴƴere, e yurmeende nde Yahweh holliri on e yeeso Iisaa Almasiihu ngam hisde mon. Mbele on mbaaway jogaade goonga haa to bannge cakkitiingal? Mbele on njaɓay naatde e fooftere am duumotoonde tawa on accaani e wakkati cakkitiingo? Mbele on mbaaway rokkude ɓerɗe mon fof e hakkillaaji mon fof konnguɗi am, on faama wonde e wakkati gooto ɗum wayloto haa abada, teeru wakkati gooto oo ko jooni.

Alaa wakkati heddii ngam Babilon Suurɗo oo tuuba, alaa kadi wakkati heddii e yonta am mo yurmeende e moƴƴere sabu wakkati oo timmii. E kala sahaa, woodi wakkati e anniya, e timmal wakkatiiji, hisde wakkati gooto maa yani e hisnaaɓe, ko ɗum waɗi yo hisnaaɓe leydi ndii mbiy so, ɓe o daɓɓiti e juuɗe gaño. Woto njaɓee gujjo oo wujja weltaare mon, sabu hol ko woni jombaajo sunaare ñalawma dewgal mum, hay so tawi ko ñalawma dewgal tedduɗo, laaɓɗo, mo hay gooto waawaa bonnude walla torraade.

Ko ɗum waɗi, keɓee deeƴre e Konnguɗi am deeƴre, accitee wuurdude hono ɓe njuurnaa e semteende e aybeeji. Sabu onon jombaajo am, on ngoni ɓe mbaɗ-mi laaɓɓe tawa alaa gaɗa e yeeso jappeere laamu Yahweh, on ngoni ɓiɓɓe leɗɗe gadane ummoraade e maayɓe. Hay so tawii onon wuuruɓe, heddiiɓe ɓee, meeɗataa maayde, on ndañaama on maayɓe e nder bonanndeeji e junuubaaji. On njawtay tigi-rigi gila e maayde (nde so on maayii, on maayatnoo), haa e nguurndam duumotooɗam ɗam on nguuri ko adii, hay e maande mawnde Yahweh nde maandeede ngam ñalawma hisde wakkati gooto oo. Sabu on ndañaama kadi e aawdi nde bonnataa, on ngoni ɓiɓɓe Jomiraawo ɓurɗo toowde, hay so tawi ko ɓiɓɓe Jomiraawo Baawɗo!

Ko ɗum waɗi, keɓee deeƴre, fooftoo, njahdanee e am e ndee dogdu nasru duuɓi. Sabu meeɗaa woodde e leydi wakkati hono oo, meeɗataa kadi woodde wakkati hono oo. Hay so tawi ko wakkati ɗo ɓiɓɓe Jomiraawo duumotooɓe ɓee, e anndal mum en, ndaardii fooftere mum en adannde e nguurndam duumotooɗam hono sukaaɓe duumotooɓe haa e yeeso Jomiraawo duumotooɗo, ɗo ɓe resata Joomiraawo maɓɓe duumotooɗo e alkawal duumotoongal, e... wakkati gooto, maa ɗum won!

Ko miin woni on rokkunooɗo hoore am laawol gootol, fof, haa abada, hono Jawgel Paska timmungel.

Miɗo heddii mooɓtude goonɗinɓe ɓee to am e nder deeƴre asamaanuuji e nder suudu jappeere laamu Yahweh.

Ko miin woni on toppitiiɗo ruuhuuji mon, on toppitiiɗo ɓeydagol mon ngol njokkoton ngam on mawnira hono lekki peewal.

Ko miin woni on mo Yahweh rokki hono demoowo e toppitiiɗo gese mum ɓurɗe laaɓde, gese ruuhuuji hisnaaɓe.

Ko miin woni Joomiraawo mawɗo, duumotooɗo, Laamɗo Laamɓe, hay so tawi ko Joomiraawo mon peewɗo.

Ko miin woni on nanngowo ndonu am e juuɗe jooɗiiɓe ɓe ngalaa hakke.

Ko miin woni Konngol Jomiraawo ngol waɗaa ɓanndu, kala Konngol Jomiraawo maa hebbine.

Ko miin woni Yeesuha Ha'Masiya, kanko woni kadi gaynaako mon mawɗo, omo heddii feeñde!

Miin, Yeesuha Ha'Masiya, mi neldirii ɗee Konnguɗi e maccuɗo am, e maccuɗo am ɗee Konnguɗi neldaa!

Ko ɗum waɗi, miɗo hirjina on, giɗaaɓe am, woto on tampu, njokkee nanngude haa to bannge cakkitiingal wakkati gooto ngal timminta haa abada wakkati mon e leydi e ngonka mon jooni. E wakkati gooto, e nder leplel gite, on njiytoyta on dariiɗo e suudu jappeere laamu Yahweh e nder weltaare duumotoonde, on faamta, on annda tigi-rigi hono no on faamirtee, on anndirtee tigi-rigi. Nii woni, alkawal e timmal kawray e weltaare duumotoonde e teddungal duumotoongal ngal ndaardii on, teeru fof maa hebbine e mon e nder leplel gite, teeru e wakkati gooto maa won. Heedee e am, ɓiɓɓe am, sabu miɗo yiɗi on no feewi, mi semtaani noddude on miñiraaɓe am.
