Seedeeji ɗiɗi: Isra'iila
Ko weli e ko haaɗi
31 Juko, 2024

Bone wonandeɓe noddata niwre annoora, annoora niwre!

Nanee konnguɗi Joomiraaɗo mon e kisinoowo mon, Ekklessiya am, kadi e Jombam, sabu ko miin woni dokkoowo ñeeñal ñeeñɓe, ngam ñeeñɓe wonɓe e mon ɓeydoo ñeeñal. Ko miin kadi woni baɗoowo yiite laɓɓinirde, ndeke nanee konnguɗi am on fuu, njokkee e duwaade ngam heɓde nguurndam duumotooɗam, njiɗee ñeeñal sabu ñeeñal ko huunde mawnde, ngam maajum njiɗee ñeeñal. Ngaroyee to am e ɓernde laaɓunde e juuɗe udditiiɗe, njokkee e duwaade, njokka e waɗde miin, Iisaa Nasarankeejo, Joomiraaɗo mon duumotooɗo, sabu miɗo jokki e noddude fuu wonɓe ronooɓe nguurndam duumotooɗam, hay so tawii woɓɓe maɓɓe ina nani seeɗa.

Kono nduñee ñeeñal adunaaru ngal alaa nafa, sabu ñeeñal adunaaru ko iwde e laamɓe adunaaru, ɓe fuu ɓe njaɓɓotoo ko ɓooyaani duuɓi ujunere e nder luggeendi torra e lepte ngam laabi maɓɓe. Sabi hay e nder callaali teeŋtuɗi ɗi ɓe njaɓɓirtee ko ɓooyaani ɗii, ɓe paamoyay ko arata e maɓɓe, ɓe njiikoyay, ɓe njiikoyay duuɓi ujunere tawa kadi nguurndam maɓɓe duumotooɗam e nder maayo ngootaako ngo wulngo sulfure ina taaroo, ina wula e yiite ɓurɗe wulde e nder bulo kobalt, sabu noon Yahweh feewniri, ndeke ndonu maɓɓe ina fadi ɗum en.

Sabu noon woniri yiɗde ɓernde maɓɓe e sukaaɓe Alla guurɗo oo, ndeke Yahweh ruttitintoo yiɗde ɓernde maɓɓe e nder ɓerɗe maɓɓe, ɓe nguloyay haa abada abadin. Hono noon haa teeŋti e malaa'ika'en tooñooɓe ɓe keɓoyay golle maɓɓe sabu ko kamɓe ngoni baabiraaɓe greka, tooñannge, bonannde e salaare sariya, e golle maɓɓe bonɗe ɗee ruttitoytee e maɓɓe haa abada. Ɓe nganndoyay, ɓe njoɓoyay haa laaɓa kala greka gooto mo ɓe mbaɗi laabi keewɗi, lepte maɓɓe keewoyay, sabu ko ɗum tawaa e nder ɓerɗe maɓɓe e golle maɓɓe ko bonɗe haa abada abadin. Sabu noon laabi Yahweh ngoriri, no neɗɗo ñaawoirta goɗɗo, noon kadi o ñaawoortee, ko noon woni battane e lepte fuu suɓiiɓe mbonniindi maɓɓe e nokku nguurndam duumotooɗam e nder am miin Iisaa Nasarankeejo, sabu ko miin woni Jippoore Alla.

Kala baasɗo hakkilankaagal haa o salii njuɓɓudi tagoore, hay njuɓɓudiiji Yahweh baɗɗo nguurndam no huunde jokkondirnde nii, o tawtortoo lepte maɓɓe. Salagol njuɓɓudiiji nguurndam ko addude maayde e mbonniindi e woɓɓe, e bone mawɗo e lorra mawka ina waɗee e juuɗe fuu saliiɓe noon, rewɓe e neɗɗaagu maɓɓe haa bonna woɓɓe. Weltaade e salaare tooñooɓe ko tawtoreede laabi maɓɓe, ndeke fuu waɗooɓe ɗum, ɓe tawtortoo ndonu maɓɓe e nder yiite duumotooɗam, alaa kadi suɓaare woɗnde.

Sabu gila ganni, gila e fuɗɗoode, neɗɗaŋke rokkaama hoolaare, Aadama yirbinaa e Hawaa, o heɓi e mborosa ka o yirbini mo e fenaande mum. Ndeke Aadama e Hawaa cuɓii maayde ngam battane greka, sabu yimɓe tagaa ko no malaa'ika'en nii, ngam ɓe njokke e laaɓal e peewal, e maayde, ngoon mbonnoowo kala huunde, waawaano nanngude ɓe.

Ndeke ganndal moƴƴere e bone bonnii fuu nyaamɓe gila ndeen. E wolde iwdi ndii ina jokki, ina wuuri gila e Jeneesi e iwdi debbo ahdaandi ndii, e mbonniindi mawndi, e nder ndee wolde alaa ko heddii e battane mum. Haa jooni, tagoore ndee fuu ina ŋiiwa, ina woya e mette, sabu tagoore ndee kadi hisoyay e maccungaagu bonannde, naata e ndimaagu teddungal sukaaɓe Alla, e kisal ngal yahataa. Kono paamee sukaaɓe am, haa jooni, wolde iwdi ndii foolaama e nder am, Iisaa Almasiihu, sabu on ndimaama e iwdi ndi bonnataa, e nder Jippoore Alla, njuldam waɗaama ngam grekaaji neɗɗaŋke laawol gootol haa abada. E baawɗe Yahweh keewɗe, maayde foolaama haa abada e nder ummital am e maayɓe, sabu waawaa maayde jogaade mi, sabu alaa e am greka, e njoɓdi greka ko maayde.

Kono mi yoɓii coggu keewngu ngam hisde mon e ndimaagu mon, e sukaaɓe Alla guurɗo oo, e seese, ɓe tawat hoore maɓɓe ina ndarii e jooɗorde laamu Alla, ɓe njuumtaama e nguurndam duumotooɗam. Ko noon woniri, ko noon wonirta, kala nodduɗo innde Joomiraaɗo hisoyay haa abada, so tawii o jokkii e noddude innde am haa e battane. Sabu tooñooɓe ko añɓe foolaaɓe, jooni ngari sahaaji mbonniindi laamu maɓɓe e lepte timmuɗe, tawa tikka Jippoore e tikka Alla ina njuurtinee e balɗe garooje. Sabu ñalawma Joomiraaɗo arii, ndeke nanee daande am hade damal Laana ndiyam udditeede.

Nanee konnguɗi am, paamee haala am. Sabu miɗo haala geɗe jooniɗe, e kadi fenaande mawnde baawnde yirbude hay suɓaaɓe so ɓe cuɓii uddude noppi maɓɓe e daande am. Jooni mi haaloyan ko fayti e nguruuji ladde ɗi njiɗi feeñde no tawa wonaa e nder pelle ɗe jogaaɗo am joginoo ɗi. Hay seppo e njuɓɓudiiji nguruuji ɗi mbonnata ñalawma e ñalawma, ɗi ngaddata adunaaru ko ɓuri ɓadaade wolde timmunde, e musiiba timmuɗo, sabu noon rokkaa ɗi ngam waɗde ɗum, Alla waɗii e nder ɓerɗe maɓɓe ngam timminde yiɗde mum, sabu hay añɓe Yahweh ina ɗooftoo daande mum.

Nguruuji ɗii ina njaɓɓondira e tuundi maɓɓe, ina njuula e nder loope, meeɗaa haarde, kono ina njuppa kala ko mbaawi, tawa ina njuppa e kine maɓɓe haa e leydi, sabu ko e leydi iwri, ina ngumɗi so wonaa e yiɗde reedu maɓɓe e neɗɗaagu baaba maɓɓe ngol ɓe mbaɗata. Sabu noon ardiiɓe leydi ndii ngoniri jooni, ina mboya e mette tawa ina njani e luutndiiɓe maɓɓe. Sabu alaa ko waawi haarnude nguruuji ɗii, ina toskaaɗi haa laaɓi gila e nder mum en sabu ko ɓe nyaami, sabu fuu ko ɓe tuunɗuɓe, ko ɓe leɗɗe bonɗe, ko noon sifaa maɓɓe woniri.

Beestooje bonɗe ɗee ko leɗɗe bonɗe, ɗe alaa ɓiɓɓe leɗɗe, ɗe maayii laabi ɗiɗi, ɗe njoɓaa e daaɗe. Meeɗataa heɓde nguurndam duumotooɗam, laabi maɓɓe ko maayde duumotoonde e mbonniindi, hay mbonniindi duumotoondi e nder ndonu Baabiraaɓe maɓɓe e nder ngoon maayo mawngo yiite bulo. Ko ɓe njaayndiyankooɓe, ina njuppa e fuɗɗoode ina njiɗi hoɗorde tawa ina mboya luutndiiɓe, hay so tawii ina tuuma woɓɓe e konnaagu nguu ɓe mbaɗata e leyɗe. Ina mboya e mette tawa ina njani e ɓe njiɗi halkude ngam feeñde e laabi maɓɓe bonɗi.

Sabu beestooje bonɗe ɗee mbaawaa yurmeede, laabi maɓɓe ko mbonniindi, ɓe mbaɗoyay neɗɗaagu Baaba maɓɓe Seytaane, sabu neɗɗaagu mum ko weltaare maɓɓe, ko noon ɓe timminirtee. Bone wonande ɓe ngeltotoo e bone, ɓe noddata annoora niwre, niwre annoora. Sabu laabi maɓɓe ko mbonniindi, e yiite duumotooɗe ɗee meeɗataa daɗde. Paamee sukaaɓe am, e seese, nguruuji ɗii ndimaama e pelle ɗe jogaaɗo am joginoo ɗi. Sabu so jogaaɗo oo ittaama, nguruuji ɗii, ɗi nanndi e ko moƴƴi, ɗi peeñata no suɓaaɓe Alla nii, ngartoyay ladde so ɓe ndimaama e nanngere jogaaɗo oo tiiɗnde, sabu ko noon sifaa maɓɓe goonga woniri.

Ɓe naatoyay leyɗe keewɗe, ɓe njuppoyay fuu ko ɓe tawi. Ɓe nyaamoyay maayde e mbonniindi woɓɓe, sabu ko noon sifaa maɓɓe woniri. Ina njahaa gila e nokku haa e nokku, ina njogii njuɓɓudiiji mum en e renndooji mum en, sabu Yahweh ina rokka hay nguruuji hakkilankaagal e njuɓɓudi, sabu laabi Yahweh ko nuunɗuɗi e peewɗi. Nguruuji ɗii ngalaa yurmeende, ngalaa yarlitaare, ngalaa caɗeele e nyaamde maayɓe ngam haarnude neɗɗaagu maɓɓe, hay so tawii ina nyaama guurɓe so ɗum nafi ɗi. Ndaaree sahaaji ɗii, ɗo nguruuji ɗii ngalaa ko haɗata ɗi, so ɗum waɗii, ɓe nyaamoyay leydi, ɓe njuppoyay neema leydi, ɓe mbonnoyay jam e leydi, ɓe nyaamoyay, ɓe njaɓɓondiroo e nder ƴiiƴam wolde timmunde. Sabu hay yimooɓe mon bonɓe ina njima e laabi maɓɓe, laabi maɓɓe ngalaa sirlu.

Nguruuji ladde ɗii mbaɗaama ngam mbonniindi e nyaamde tuundi leydi ngam laɓɓinde leydi ngam faandaare Yahweh, ndeen ɓe njaɓɓoyee, ɓe werlee e yiite duumotooɗe haa abada. Sabu grekaaji maɓɓe ko mawɗi, grekaaji maɓɓe ko keewɗi. Kono paamee wonde nguruuji ɗii njogaama e pelle mum en haa sahaaji potɗi, heewɓe ina peeñata no kuutorɗe laaɓɗe ngam laamu maɓɓe ummotoongu. Jooni ko sahaaji ndimaagu maɓɓe, ɓe kollitoyay jikku maɓɓe goonga oo, mo ɓe cuuɗinoo e comci diine, e kuubol Saturna ɓaleejo ngoo ina woni e hakkunde koode maɓɓe, hay e dow gite. Ina njiɗi seerndude hoore maɓɓe sabu ɓe cuɓiima hoore maɓɓe wonde yimɓe Alla. Kono alaa garoowo to Yahweh so wonaa e dow am, miin Iisaa Nasarankeejo, mo Baabiraaɓe maɓɓe nduñi, e ɓe mbaɗi mi Joomiraaɗo ko kamɓe ngoni Isra'iila goongaajo Alla, ɓe laatoytoo e nder am no neɗɗo keso gooto, tawa ko Yahuudiyanke walla Jentil, tawa ko maccuɗo walla dimo. Lulluɓe Edom ɓee peeñoyay no fenaande nii, ɓe ittiree e leydi ndii e adunaaru fuu e ñaawoore mawnde e lepte mawɗe.

Kono hade maɓɓe itteede, ɓe accoyee yiɗde maɓɓe e ƴiiƴam tawa sifaa maɓɓe goonga oo ina feeñanee fuu yiyde, sabu jooni ina njuuɗii e comci diine. Ina nyaama tuundi, ina nyaama maayɓe, ina njani, ina njuppa guurɓe sabu yiɗde maɓɓe e ƴiiƴam ina mawni, waawaa haarde! E nder ɗuum fuu, ina peeñata no laaɓɓe nii, ina peeñata no yimɓe leydi ndii nii, no ronooɓe nii, ina peeñata no tawa wonaa njaayndiyankooɓe nii, sabu hoɗorde maɓɓe ko ladde, wonaa leydi ahdaandi. Ko noon sifaa nguru woniri, so ɓe ngartii ladde tan, sifaa maɓɓe goonga oo ina feeña haa laaɓa. Kono, ina peeñata no njiɗi foolde nii, ɓe njoɓoyee e leydi ndii, ngalaa daaɗe, ngalaa calɗi, sabu ko noon Yahweh ahdi. Ndeke paamee nguruuji ɗii ina nanndi e ardiiɓe moƴƴuɓe leydi ahdaandi ndii, tawa ko ɓe beestooje njuppooje, njiɗɗe warngooji, njiɗɗe halkude kala huunde ummoraade e am, Iisaa Almasiihu, hay so tawii ina njuppa ɓe e nder leydi ndii, ɓe rewata e yamiroore maɓɓe Talmuudik haa e mbonniindi, kono heddiiɓe e leydi ndii ngaroyay to am, Yeshua HaMashiach, sabu mi hisinoyay Yakuba!

Bone wonande ɓe noddata niwre annoora, annoora niwre! Hay ɓe njani e Yahweh, e Yeshua HaMashiach, tawa ina njani e sukaaɓe Alla guurɗo oo. Sabu e nder ɗuum, ko ɓe mbaɗani ɓurɗo famɗude e miñiraaɓe am gooto, ko miin ɓe mbaɗani, e yoɓre ko e am woni, wiyi Joomiraaɗo duumotooɗo, mi yoɓoyay. Ndeke udditee gite mon, sukaaɓe am, nduñee ɓe njuumtata e koode Remfan e dow bandera maɓɓe! Sabu tawtoreede ɓe ko wuurde e nder suudu Moloch e duwaade Baphomet maɓɓe! Wonaa mi wiyi on limoode limoore beestoo ndee, e oon limoore ina feeñi e teddungal e nder sifa ka ɓe mbaɗani hoore maɓɓe ngam deweede koode maɓɓe, sabu e hakkunde koode maɓɓe ko kuubol maɓɓe woni! Paamee ndee misal, sabu toɓɓe jeegom ina ngadda toɓɓe jeegom baɗɗe sifa jeegom, e nder mum ina heɓa kuubol maɓɓe, ndeke limoode limoore beestoo ndee, njoppee hoolaade fenaande Edom en, sabu ina ndewa tooñooɓe, e alla maɓɓe ko dragon.

Gila e balɗe Nuuhu, hay caggal ilam mawɗam, sappo e jeenayi e ruuhuuji Nephilim ɗi ngalaa ɓalli njogaama e nder luggeendi, sabu Nuuhu duwaama ngam taaniraaɓe mum ɓe ɓe njani e mum en, ɓe fooli ɗum en. Ndeke Yahweh teddinii naamnal Nuuhu, o haɓɓi ɓurɓe bonde e ɓurɓe tuunɗude, e sappo e jeenayi e limoore maɓɓe njogaama e nder luggeendi gila ndeen. Nanee mi, nanee mi no feewi, sukaaɓe am, paamee daande Joomiraaɗo mon. E mooɓondiral gooŋɗinɓe e nder Ekklessiya am, hay e Jombam to am e nder duule, jogaaɗo oo ittoyte, e luggeendi ndii udditoyte haa laaɓa.

Koonde bonde ndee heertinaataake kadi, sabu Jawmiraawo o heertinii timmoode laamu Ibliisa mbele ronooɓe ndimaagu duumingol ɓee ndima, hay e ɓeen waɗɓe kam Joomiraawo mum en e jamaanuuji am yurmeende e moƴƴere. Nde tawnoo neɗɗaagu ngoo yeddiima kam jooni, bonannde ndee heertinaataake e neɗɗaagu. Faamee wonde kala, hay e mbeela mawka e ɗiin jinneeji, fof njoppite, hay e limoore mawnde sanne nde hebbinta leydi ndii fof e bone keertinaaka, mo haalataake. Sabu Anuhu winndiyanke oo winndii e dow ɗiin : « Jinneeji semmbeeji ɗii ina nawa, ina kaɗa, ina mbonna, ina kaɓa, ina ngolloo mbonnaari e dow leydi, ina ngadda caɗeele : ɓe nyaamataa, kono kadi ɓe ngeeɗataa, ɓe ndomɗataa, ɓe ngaddata bonanndeeji. Ɗiin jinneeji kadi ina ummoo e dow ɓiɓɓe Aadama e rewɓe, sabu ɗiin ummorii ko e mum en. »

Mbele on njanngaani e konngol am laaɓngol ngol nokku mo njahrumi e leydi Gadaren en? Sabu mi riiwii jinneeji e ɗiɗo jeyaaɓe heen, mi acci ɗiin jinneeji bonɗi naata e ngalu nguluuje saraaji mum en. Faamee wonde timmoode laamu Pooliigu nguu ina foti yaltineede seeɗa, mbele ina addanaɓe ndimaagu duumingol ɓee e juuɗe am ndimaagu! Sabu ɗiin jinneeji kasooji ɗii maa njoppite ngam naatde e laamu Ibliisa timmungu e yeeso leydi. Tee maa ɓe naat kadi e nguluuje ɗee. Sabu ɓe kaɓatnoo e worɓe, ɓe keɓatnoo worɓe sabu yiɗde maɓɓe meeɗaa hebbineede, ɗiin ngoni jinneeji baawɗi rokkude nguluuje ɗee doole, gollodooɓe e nguluuje ɗee, tee ɗeen nguluuje ladde ko kaɓirɗe maɓɓe.

Wonaa kamɓe tan kono kala jaɓɓuɓe maande maɓɓe mbeeyu ndee kadi maa ngontu kaɓirɗe yiɗde ngam ɗiin jinneeji, ɗi kaɓata e neɗɗaagu kadi. Sabu maa ɓe ndartu kadi neɗɗo tagoore e ɗiiɗoo balɗe cakkitiiɗe, sabu noon yamiraama waɗde e ɗiiɗoo balɗe cakkitiiɗe. Tee joofirde ndee fuɗɗiima, ina arda timmude no feewi nde caɗeele Yakuuba peeñata e leydi ndii. Riwɗe ɗee ina ɓadii haa jooni, ɗe ndartaaki, Jawmiraawo o waɗii maandeeli keewɗi e leydi, e maandeeli keewɗi e kaayɗe e dow asamaan e dow asamaan e leydi, kono heewɓe ina keddii e yeddugo ɗiin jeertinaaɗe e miijooji ŋakkere muñal.

Ndaaree! E nokku ɗo keɓata tuububal, yimɓe ɓee ina njiylondira e mbuubiiji keewɗi, ɗi keɓi heewɓe e leyɗeele keewɗe e dow leydi. Ina njala, ina njaɓɓa jeertinaaɗe ɗe Jawmiraawo rokki leydi ndii, sabu Alla duumotooɗo oo maa ummo e hoɗorde mum, hollita hoore mum, tee tooñooɓe ɓee ina njiɗi sabu wakkatiiji mum en njettiima, tee ɓe njiɗi ko e kulol duumingol. Sabu onon tooñooɓe ɓee, on miijinoo on mbaawata heɓde Alla duumotooɗo oo e dow peeje, nde tawnoo ko huunde waawaa waɗeede. Sabu hol waktu mo leeɗe bonɗe ummotoo ngam haɓde e heɓde potɗo oo e peeje? Kono ko noon woni fenaande e mawnikinaare tooñooɓe ɓee, tee ɓe ndoggataa e yiite mum en duumingol.

Ndeen noon, faamee wakkatiiji ɗi ngonɗon ɗii, keddo-ɗon e am, Eklesiya am, hay e Jom suudu am! Sabu ndimaagu mon timmungu e ndonu mon timmungu ina jokki, on accataake e nder nguluuje ɗee sabu on ngoongɗinii innde am e ɗiiɗoo balɗe yurmeende e moƴƴere, tee noon yurmeende e moƴƴere duumingol maa keɓon haa abada. Tee banndiraaɓe mon maa naatdu e mon e duumingol e e laamu am garoowu nguu, laamu nuunɗal, hay e kala ɓe ngonaa pellital e peeñgol am.

Sabu ko mi laɓɓinoowo mo liɓata yiite laɓɓingol ngol mbele mi laɓɓina jawdi Jawmiraawo, itta ɓuuɓri ndii, tee ko miin jooɗiiɗo no cehoowo jogiiɗo juuɗe liɓoowo kaaye e juuɗe, mo liɓata e mo liɓata njamndiiji tedduɗi Jawmiraawo ngam waɗde semmbe duumotooɗo, mo alaa keerol. Mbeela mawka, hay e mbeela mbeelaaji, maa ngar e am rewrude e caɗeele Yakuuba, tee maa ɓe keɓ heen njoɓdi mawndi, tee ko ronooɓe ndimaagu duumingol, tee ko e weltaare duumingol ɓe naatata, tee Israayiil maa hisnu! Ndeen noon, kulee ɓiɓɓe am, wullito-ɗee ngam yiɗɓe mon, ngoongɗinee kam Almasiihu Iisaa, sabu miɗo waawi hisnude haa timma kala noddooɓe innde am, tee hay gooto e yamiraaɓe nguurndam duumingol waawaa woppeede e juuɗe am, tee mi hisnan heewɓe e yiite duumingol ngol, hay e yaltinde ɓe e yiite ngol.

Miin Yeesu HaMasiiya, mi neldii ɗee konnguɗi rewrude e maccuɗo am, tee rewrude e maccuɗo am ɗee konnguɗi neldaa!

Ko miin rokkoowo hakkilantaagal hakkilanteeɓe, mbele neɗɗo hakkilanteeɗo oo ɓeydoo hakkilantaagal.

Ko miin woni Joomiraawo Alla garoowo no Mbaroodi leñol Yahuuda nii.

Mi alaa bakkaat, kono mi yoɓii njoɓdi mawndi ngam ndimaagu mon e ndimaagu mon.

Ko miin jooɗiiɗo no cehoowo liɓoowo, forjoowo njamndiiji tedduɗi jawdi Jawmiraawo.

Miɗo waawi hisnude haa timma kala noddooɓe innde am, tee hay gooto e yamiraaɓe nguurndam duumingol waawaa woppeede e juuɗe am.

Ndeenee peeñgol am, sabu wakkatiiji ɗii njettiima. Wakkati heddiiɗo oo ko ngam gollandeede Jawmiraawo tan, ɓiɓɓe am, njaɓee hoore mon deyyineede haa kadi, hay e woppude konnguɗi am hakkilantaagal e jeertinaaɗe keewɗe.

Gorko hakkilanteeɗo ina yiyata ñawu nguu, ina suuɗtoo, kono fenaaɓe ɓee ina njokki, ina nawa bone. Mbele mi nodaaki on e hakkilantaagal Jawmiraawo duumingol ngol?

Tee ko miin suuɗata on ɗo on mbaawaa suuɗde hoore mon, sabu mi suuɗat on e jappeere laamu Jawmiraawo, hay e asamaan asamaan ɗo naatoton e weltaare Joomiraawo mon, tee cuuɗi ñamri asamaan maa mbeltoo e mon fof, sabu ko ɓooyaani koo, on nji'an gite mon teddungal duumingol, ngal on ndaartoo e ndonu mon. Jokkee e yiɗde am, sabu on njiɗaa no feewi e Baabiraawo am, tee ko on ɓiɗɗo gite am, mo mi semtataa noddude on banndiraaɓe am.

Miin Yeesu HaMasiiya mi haaldii, mi neldii ɗee konnguɗi, tee noon ɗe mbinndaama, Linjiila duumingol ngol feeñi e wakkati mum.
