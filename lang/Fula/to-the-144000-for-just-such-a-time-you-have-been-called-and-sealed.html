E nder Golle Yuhanna Wahayanke
Yarkoma 01, 2024

Tilay 144,000: Ngam Wakkati Bana Ni'i On Noddaama On Maandinaama

Daree ngam am, kadi jokkee yeru am, sabi ko Iburaniyankooɓe mbiyata dow darnde am ngam Yahwe? On mo ngam welwelo ngo wonnoo yeeso makko, muñi leggal maayde, o yefi hersa, o jooɗinaa e nyaamo jappeere laamu Allah. Nii kadi e nder giɗli am e Baabiraawo, ɗi taƴataa, e nder ɗowtanaare am sabu giɗli, e giɗli am e aade, e yiɗde am sakkanagol hoore am ngam hisnude on, mi muñii tampere mawnde, mette, e torra, mi waɗi ɗum e yiɗde, e welwelo e nder ɓernde am. Mi annduno wonde e sadaka am e maayde am, heewɓe maa laatoo feewuɓe, keɓa nguurndam e baawɗe ummital am, sabi Yahwe e hoore mum ummini yam e maayɓe, sabi waawaaka maayde jogoo yam. E oon baawɗe kadi, onon maa on ummine ngam nguurndam kesam, sabi mi yoɓii coggu ngam hokkude ƴiiƴam am ceniiɗam ngam hisnude kala goonɗinɗo innde am, kala nodduɗo innde Joomiraawo maa hise, ɓe njiɗaa nguurndam mum'en haa laakara!

Jooni miɗo naamnoo on, goonɗinɓe am suɓaaɓe, hay leñol am, yo on ndaro ngam am, woto on ndillo. Sabi mi anndinii no laaɓi e nder mbindol am to Iburaniyankooɓe: Jooni feewuɗo maa wuure e goonɗinal: ammaa si goɗɗo dillii, yonki am heɓataa welwelo e makko.

Ndee konngol naatoo on, njanngon ɗum kadi, woto on ndillo e Tawreeta, sabi Tawreeta ina rewee e goonɗinal e am; ngam maajum, feewuɗo maa wuure e goonɗinal. On nanndinaama e am, miin woni feewnintunooɗo on e golle am kafaraaku e ƴiiƴam am ceniiɗam.

Heewɓe e mooɗon ina piyo e miijo wonde mi ƴetti nokku mooɗon e bonnugol dow leggal ngam hakkeeji ɗi mi waɗaani, mi waɗi ɗum e yiɗde e welwelo ngam addude kisndam to neɗɗo tagoowo bonɗo, ngam mi soottora ɓe to Yahwe, Allah mooɗon e Baaba mooɗon, hay Baaba Annooraaji mo alaa waylaare walla ɗowdi pirlitagol. Sabi Yahwe waylataako; kanko woni hande, hannde, e haa pooma!

Kanko woni Allah mo maamiraaɓe mooɗon nanondiri e alkawal, hay to Duunde Sinai nde Muusa heɓi sariya ummaade e Yahwe e hoore mum. Hay so tawii sariya ina senii, e yamiroore ina senii, ina feewi, ina moƴƴi, aade meeɗaa waawde rewde tolnooji mum sabi sariya rokkiraama ngam hollude hakke e nder terɗe, onon fof on ndimaama maayɓe e nder bonanndeeji e hakkeeji sabi hakke ina woni e terɗe mooɗon. Tawreeta rokkiraaka ngam feewuɓe kono ngam bonɓe, ngam hakke feeña e terɗe. Tawreeta rokkiraama no jannginoowo mooɗon ngam addude on to am, hay Almasiihu Iisaa, Joomiraawo Joomiraaɓe e Laamɗo Laamɓe ummiiɗo!

Nii kadi mi ƴetti e yiɗde am batte hakke dow am, sabi noon mi jibinaa ngam soottoragol aade e kuddi sariya. Sabi sariya hudaaka; ee, yo sariya daro, kala neɗɗo hude sariya Muusa yo hude, sabi mi araani ngam momtude sariya, kono ngam tabitinde sariya e hay ɓurde sariya Muusa. On mbiyi ko kufru?

Seedantaagal Iburaniyankooɓe ina darii hakkunde men: Sabi si ƴiiƴam ga'i e mbordi, e coondi ngaari laɓɓinoori ɓe tuunɗunoo, ina laɓɓina terɗe: Haala njiimaandi ƴiiƴam Almasiihu, mo e Ruuhu duumotooɗo sakkanii hoore mum tawa alaa toɓɓe to Allah, ina ɓura laɓɓinde hakkillaaji mooɗon e golle maayɗe ngam rewde Allah guurɗo?

Gila on njiɗi Muusa (no min nii), miɗo wasiyoo on yo on ɗowto e giɗli yamirooje Muusa; sabi o wiyi: Yahwe Elohim mooɗon maa umminane on Annabi ummaade e hakkunde mooɗon, e banndiraaɓe mooɗon, nannduɗo e am; kanko maa on keɗo. Nii kadi nanee daande Yesuwaa HaMasiiyak! On laɓɓinaama e hakke e kala hakkeeji ɗi mbaɗaton e ƴiiƴam am ceniiɗam kafaraaku ɗi mi hokki Yahwe ngam hisnude nguurndam mooɗon, mi soottorii on hay e juuɗe gaño. Woto on mbaɗ hakke! Si goɗɗo waɗii hakke, anndinee hakkeeji mooɗon to am, sabi miin woni laɓɓinoowo on e nder ƴiiƴam am kafaraaku.

Mi waɗaani kafaraaku ngam hakkeeji mooɗon e batte mum'en e mbordi mbordi, mi hokkii kadi mbaalndi hitaande arandeere; sabi miin woni Mbaalndi Allah hitaande arandeere, miin woni hokkunooɗo hoore am tawa alaa toɓɓe e alaa mbonndi, hay seniiɗo, laaɓɗo, ceniiɗo to Yahwe e hoore mum (hay Baaba am) ngam hakkeeji mooɗon laawol gootol e haa abada, hay ngam wakkati fof. Mi waɗii kafaraaku ngam hakkeeji mooɗon e ƴiiƴam am ceniiɗam; ngam maajum, sadaka goɗɗo ngam hakke heddaaki. Salagol am, Yesuwaa HaMasiiyak, hay nanondiral jam timmungal Yahwe to aade, ko salagol Yahwe e naatde e murto Seyɗaani, sabi Seyɗaani ina murti e yamiroore Allah gila puɗɗam.

Sabi mi wiyi Iburaniyankooɓe: Sabi si en mbaɗii hakke e yiɗde caggal nde en keɓi anndal goonga, sadaka goɗɗo ngam hakkeeji heddaaki. Nii kadi waɗde hakke e yiɗde e salagol ƴiiƴam am kafaraaku, e salagol nanondiral jam ngal Yahwe neldi, ko bone mawɗo sanne, sadaka goɗɗo alaa. Sabi ina winndaa e Iburaniyankooɓe: On mo yefi sariya Muusa maayi tawa yurmeende alaa e seedeeji ɗiɗo walla tato: E torra no heewi siforii on, ina sikkaa o foti, on mo njaɓɓi Ɓiɗɗo Allah e koyɗe, o hiisii ƴiiƴam alkawal, ɗam o laɓɓiniraa, ko huunde nde laaɓaani, o waɗii bonannde e Ruuhu moƴƴere?

Nii kadi miɗo hirjina on fof yo on njaɓɓu sadaka am, on faama wonde sadakaaji goɗɗi potaani, Almasiihu goonga gooto tan woodi, hay miin, Yesuwaa HaMasiiyak. Ina winndaa dow am: Ammaa oo neɗɗo, caggal nde o waɗi sadaka gooto ngam hakkeeji haa abada, o jooɗii e nyaamo Allah; Gila jooni omo heddii haa gañɓe makko laatoo teɓteɓel koyɗe makko. Sabi e sadaka gooto o timminii haa abada ɓe laɓɓinaa.

Nii kadi faamee wonde mi laɓɓinii on e kala fannuuji e fannuuji jowitiiɗi e hakke e batte mum. On laɓɓinaama e am; on feewnitaama e am; on laataama feewal Allah e am; mi soottorii on fof to am, hay miin, Yesuwaa HaMasiiyak, mo Yahwe neldi no Almasiihu mooɗon. Nii kadi faamee wonde Ceerno'en Mawɓe Lewi heddaaki, Ceerno'en Haruuna ngarii, njahi, sabi ko ɗowdi geɗe garooje. Sabi miin woni ceerno mooɗon mawɗo haa abada e mbaadi Melkisedek.

Sabi sariya timminaani hay huunde: kono ko naatgol yaakaare ɓurnde moƴƴude, yaakaare nde en ɓadortoo Yahwe. Kadi: Ceerno Mawɗo hono oo fotani en, seniiɗo, mo alaa bonannde, mo laaɓi, seerndaaɗo e hakke'en, o waɗaa toowɗo e dow asamaaji. Kadi: Sabi sariya ina waɗa yimɓe ceerno'en, ɓe ngalaa semmbe: kono konngol hunayeere ngol ari caggal sariya, ina waɗa ɓiɗɗo ceerno, mo timmi haa pooma. On njanngaani ko mbindi ceniidi mbiyata dow am? Yahwe hunii, mettataa: A woni Ceerno haa abada e mbaadi Melkisedek.

Faamee wonde hay huunde ŋakkaani e sadaka am, on ndesndaama e am, Almasiihu Iisaa. Tawreeta ina rewee e goonɗinal e am, Yesuwaa HaMasiiyak, waawaaka on luutoo hay sariya gooto e sariyaaji Allah so on njahii e am, Almasiihu Iisaa. Sabi on njahaton e sariya Ruuhu nguurndam, mi rimɗini on e sariya hakke e maayde. Nii kadi ngoonɗinee e am, sabi feewuɗo maa wuure e goonɗinal. Miɗo jogii golle mawɗe keewɗe ɗe mi noddi on, on mballoytoo e addude kisndam to heewɓe. Yaakaare mawnde e njoɓdi ina ndesndanaa on e asamaaji ngam darnde mooɗon e goonga mooɗon.

Sabi miɗo nelda on, on maandinaama e tiiɗe mooɗon e maande Yahwe e hoore mum, e nder aduna mo añata on no feewi, hay huunde haaynii, sabi aduna añii yam arande, golloowo ɓuraa joom mum. Onon woni seedeeji am e annabaaɓe am e cellinooɓe am e rokkooɓe am. Miɗo nelda on e nder niɓere ɓurnde niɓɓude nde meeɗaa suddude tagoore Yahwe. Ngam wakkati hono oo on noddaama, on suɓaama, on maandinaama, ɗii wakkatiiji maa ngona ɓurɗi bonde e bonde ɗi aduna meeɗaa yiyde, hay ɓurɗi ñalɗi Nuuhu. E nder maɓɓe miɗo nelda on, miɗo wiya on woto kulon! Woto kulon kulhuli maɓɓe; woto kulon ko neɗɗo waawi waɗde on; woto kulon geece maɓɓe; woto kulon ruuhuuji baawɗi ɓe; woto kulon kulle e annabi fenaande; woto kulon Seyɗaani e hoore mum: Hay ndee mboddi mawndi. Sabi on njaɓɓoyto kala fannu laamu Seyɗaani, hay huunde waawaa haɗde on so on keddii e goonga e daande am, on acci mi ardita on no feewi. Sabi goonga, onon ko gollooɓe e am e nder oo wakkati torra Yakuuba, mi hokkii on baawɗe.

Onon ko semmbinɓe e baawɗe, miɗo nelda on e deƴƴere e neɗɗaagu. Miɗo nelda on ngam rewrude sariya e goonɗinal mooɗon e am, ngam noon njiɗon Yahwe e ɓernde mooɗon fof, e yonki mooɗon, e hakkille mooɗon, e semmbe mooɗon, njiɗon kadi koɗo mooɗon no hoore mooɗon. Hol koɗo mooɗon? Kala ɓe ngoni e haaju e ballal mooɗon ɓe mi neldata on, mi haɗiraani on keerol leydi. Njaree ndurnee yimɓe am, sabi miin woni Durɗo Mawɗo jogiiɗo njoɓdi mooɗon e juuɗe am ngam goonga. Njaree ndurnee yimɓe am, ndurnee mborosaaji maɓɓe, ñamminee ɓe, cellinee ɓe, ardoo ɓe, e dow fof ngaddee ɓe to am; sabi e ko'e mooɗon on ngalaa hay huunde, ammaa e am, onon ko worɓe am baawɗe, semmbinɓe Allah. Jannginee ɓe noddude innde am haa laakara, sabi kala baɗɗo ɗum maa hise.

Njogitee gite mooɗon e njoɓdi e welwelo ngo woni yeeso mooɗon, ndaaree yeeso ngam njoɓdi noddaango toowngo Allah e nder Almasiihu Iisaa. Sabi njoɓdi mooɗon maa won mawndi, on maa kaaynito e ko ndesndanaa on e asamaaji. Njoɓdi am wonaa wakkati, kono ko duumotoondi, njoɓdi mooɗon majjataa kono maa won ndonu duumotoongu ngam timminde alkawal ngal Yahwe waɗani maamiraaɓe; kala yod walla ŋoral yod maa timmine. On faamataa mawngu golle mooɗon haa ñalnde njoɓdi ndee, kono mbindi ceniidi ina laaɓi dow lewlewndu golle mooɗon.

Sabi Yuhanna Wahayanke winndii e goonga ko mi hollunoo ɗum: Caggal ɗii geɗe mi yiyi, e yoo jamaa mawɗo, mo hay gooto waawaa limde, ummaade e kala leƴƴi e leñol, e yimɓe, e ɗemɗe, ina darii yeeso jappeere laamu, e yeeso Mbaalndi, ina ɓoornii comci daneeji juutɗi, e caɓɓe e juuɗe mum'en. Ɓe mbulli e daande toownde, ina mbiya, Kisndam ummorii ko e Allah men, jooɗiiɗo e jappeere laamu, e Mbaalndi. Sabi jamaa mawɗo maa on mballu hisnude e gollanaade, njoɓdi mooɗon goonga maa won mawndi.

On njokkay am kala ɗo njahumi, onon woni lewlewndu arandeere soodataandu e yimɓe, on njimoyta jimol kesol yeeso jappeere laamu ngol onon tan mbaawi janngude. On laamodoyto e am e nder Laamu am duuɓi ujunere, on ngonndoyto e am haa abada e nder duumo. Sabi mooɗon ko welwelo duumotoongo, sabi on ngonndoyto e am e nder Laamu am e dow leydi nde mi laamoto leydi e feewal ummaade e jappeere laamu Daawuuda, Laamu am alaa timmoode. Hay huunde bonnde naatataa, torra goɗɗo woodataa, am ko laamu feewal ɗo mbaalndi e rawaandu ndaɗndotoo e jam. E timmoode laamu am duuɓi ujunere, Seyɗaani maa rimɗine wakkati seeɗa ngam hawrude e hare mum sakkitiinde, yiite maa jippo ummaade e Yahwe, halka ɓe fof. Seyɗaani maa werle e weendu yiite, meeɗaa ummaade kadi, Yahwe maa tago asamaan keso e leydi heso, o waɗa hoɗorde makko hakkunde yimɓe, onon kadi on koɗoyto e Yerusalem keso, hay ndee wuro ceniingo ngo Yahwe e hoore mum tagi. Yahwe e hoore mum wiyi: On mo fooli maa ron fof; mi laatoo Allah makko, kanko laatoo ɓiɗɗo am.
