Hol An Wonduɗon?

Konngol Joomiraawo arii e am.

Joomiraawo Iisa Almasiihu wiyi: Hol an wonduɗon mbele on ndogga tikkere am? On kooloto worɓe bonɓe neldaaɓe mbele so waawii ɓe njoofa hay cuɓaaɓe ɓen? On kooloto kullel Baabila, debbo jeenotoodo mawɗo o? Mbele on njoofaama faa miijoton waawde suɓaade laamiiɗo mon e woote? Mbele on faamaani wonde Seytaane ko laamɗo aduna oo, omo joofna aduna oo fof? On kooloto pelle ɗe neɗɗo waɗi e cuuɗi annabaaɓe fenaaɓe neldaaɓe mbele ɓe njoofa hay cuɓaaɓe am ɓen? Hol an wonduɗon? Toy njahoton so tawii hay huunde waawaa suuɗaade gite am?

On nji'ii worɓe bonɓe ɓen ina njananora suuɗaade e lowe ɗe ɓe njasani hoore maɓɓe e nder leydi ngam dogde kam, kono ɓe njasanii meere. Tikkere am ina arana, hay neɗɗo gooto waawaa daraade. Noddee innde am so tawii wakkati ina woodi haa jooni. Baabaajo am hokkii kam kala huunde e juuɗe am, e dow kammu e dow leydi fof, mi timminan peeje makko. Hay huunde e hay neɗɗo gooto waawaa daɗndude kam, sabu Baabaajo am semmbinii kam, mi waawaa haɗeede. Tikkere Jawarel ngel arii haa to damal mon, hay so tawii miɗo darii haa to damal miɗo fiyana. Miɗo heewi yiɗde naatde mi ñaamdida e mon so tawii on njaaboto damal mon tan.

Yimɓe am finnee!! Finnee!! FINNEE!! Finnee e peewal woto ngoondu e bakkaat. Mi nanii duwaawuuji mon, mi yiyii mettere mon e ruuhu e cooyngal mon. Ngaranee kam e ɓernde mon fof, ngoppee bakkaat. Ngoppee bakkaatuuji Baabila e ardiiɓe mum; miɗo wi'a on laaɓtuɗum ngoppee mo fof, hay e ekklessiyaaji mum murtuɗi. Sabu kiita Baabila ina arana, o aranan mo e sahaa gooto, o acca mo o meeree e waktu gooto. Sabu bakkaatuuji mum ina keewi; ko ɗuum waɗi kiita mum kadi ina mawnude.

Dogdu mon tan ko e kisal junngo am, hay gooto waawaa itude on e juuɗe am. Woto ngonon wela-wula, sabu jamaanuuji seerndude ɗii puɗɗiima deja. To yimɓe ɓen: toy nduttoton so mi mooɓii debbo am e nder mon? Mbele on njanngaani? Mbele on nanaani?

Miɗo heewi yiɗde naatde mi ñaamdida e mon so tawii on njaaboto damal mon tan.


Aaye mo mi heɓi semmbe mi sennda:

Wahyu 18

Annduural pitilol anndataako kadi e maa abada: daande jom-gorel e jom-debbo kadi nanataako e maa abada: sabu njulaaɓe maa ko worɓe mawɓe aduna ɓen: sabu ko e piltuuji maa leyɗeele fof njoofnaa.

Daande jom-gorel e jom-debbo nanataako kadi sabu mi mooɓan debbo am, mi suuɗa mo e cuuɗi ɗi mi janananii wakkati garoowo o, sabu e nder galle baaba am ina woodi cuuɗi keewɗi.
