Seedeeji Ɗiɗi: Eklesiya
E nder Golleeji Poola Nelaaɗo
15 Mee, 2024

Ndaaree, Njoɓdi Am ina Wondi e Am!

Haa e Giɗaaɗo am, jom suudu am koolaaɗo, giɗɗo am e ɓernde mum fof. Aan woni ɓiɗɗo yiitere am, ɓooyataa miɗo ara e maa, ɓuri ko miijotooɗaa. Sabu koolaaɗankaagal maa, njoɓdi mawndi wonannde ma e dow kammuuji ndi heblanaama ma. Ndaaree! Yiitere yiyaani, noppi nanaani, kadi naataani e ɓernde neɗɗo, geɗe ɗe Alla heblani giɗɓe Mo. Ko noon kadi woniri goongaaji kaawniiɗi jowitiiɗi e maa, giɗo am, sabu aɗa jogoo geɗal e ndonu am no koolaaɗankaagal maa seedotoo e jooɗorde ñaawoore am, hay e ndeen Bema mawnde, nde mi ñaawoyata golleeji kala e noddaaɓe am e suɓaaɓe e koolaaɓe.

Sabu ɓeen ɓe golleeji mum en njoldi, ɓe keɓat maayde, kono kamɓe e ko'e maɓɓe ɓe hisnat no haa jooni e yiite; e ɓeen ɓe golleeji mum en nduurii, ɓe keɓat jawdi keewndi, sabu ñalawma oo ɓooyataa hollita ɗum. Sabu ɗum wonaa miijo ngo anndaaka e maa, kono ko miijo ngo janngu-ɗaa e seedeeji Korentiyankooɓe e nder ɓataake ɗiɗaɓo Poola: Sabu en fof en poti yaltude yeeso jooɗorde ñaawoore Almasiihu, ngam kala neɗɗo heɓa geɗe baɗaaɗe e ɓanndu mum, no o waɗiri nii, tawa ko moƴƴe walla ko bonɗe. Nde tawnoo en anndi kulol Joomiraawo, en mbeltinirii yimɓe; kono en mbaɗaama laaɓɓe e Alla, miɗo hoolii kadi, en mbaɗaama laaɓɓe e kersaaji mon.

Sabu kersa maa seedondirat e konnguɗi maa nde ɓooyataa a rokkata hoore maa ñaawoore yeeso Ñaawoowo Nuunɗuɗo, hay ko miin, Iisaa Almasiihu. Woɓɓe ndarat yeeso am, ɓe kollita golleeji maɓɓe ɗi yiite laɓɓinoowo oo wulata, ɗum addana en hersa e maayde. Woɓɓe ndarat yeeso am dariima, ɓe laɓɓinii hoore maɓɓe e ƴiiƴam am cafroojam, ɗo njippintoton mi golleeji ɗi mi rokki on mbaɗon. Sabu naatde e deeƴre am ko deeƴde e golleeji maa. Ko noon kadi, ɓeen deeƴɓe e golleeji am ina njogii njoɓdi mawndi sabu koolaaɓe. Kono on fof on hisnat, hay gooto e mon yahaani laawol timmungol yeeso am, ko noon ñalawma oo hollirta ɗum. Wota kule, mi njoɓat on haa timma, sabu golleeji tedduɗi ɗi mbaɗanomi e gunndoo fof maa peeñe, ko noon ñalawma oo hollirta ɗum.

Hol ko waɗi ɓerɗe mon ina njiɗi e ɗee konnguɗi? Mbele on njanngaani e ɓataake gadano Yuhanna, ɗo mi waɗi mo o winndi? Sabu so ɓernde men felii en, Alla ɓuri ɓernde men mawnude, o anndi kala huunde. Giɗaaɓe, so ɓernde men felaani en, ndeen en njogii hoolaare e Alla. Kadi kadi: So en mbiyi en alaa bakkaat, en njippiima ko'e men, goonga alaa e men. So en njaɓii bakkaatuuji men, o Koolaaɗo, o Nuunɗuɗo ngam yaafoyaade en bakkaatuuji men, e laɓɓinde en e kala bonannde. Ko noon, njaɓee bakkaatuuji mon e am ñalnde kala e no njiɗir-ɗon fof, laɓɓinee ko'e mon e ƴiiƴam am cafroojam.

Sabu miin woni daraniiɗo on, on coodaama e sadak am ko mi Juulde timmunde ngam haɗde bakkaatuuji, e waɗde nanondiral sabu bonannde, e addude nuunɗal duumingal. Ndaaree, Giɗo am! Waktuuji artirde ngari, sabu Piyeer annabiima e nguu sahaa nde o sellini gorko to Damal Suudu Alla Belngo: O neldat Iisaa Almasiihu, mo waajaama on ko adii. Mo kammuuji poti jaɓɓaade, haa waktuuji artirde kala huunde ngara. Jooni noon, ngari waktuuji artirde, Giɗo am belɗo, sabu a laaɓɗo yeeso am, a laɓɓinaama e kala bonannde e nder ƴiiƴam Jawgel ngel, sabu miin woni Jawgel Alla! Ko ɗum waɗi, njaɓee bakkaatuuji mon e koolaaɗankaagal.

Ko noon kadi ñaawoore, artirde, maayde, e njoɓdi mawndi fof puɗɗotoo ko e galle Alla. Sabu ɓooyataa, on mooɓondirat e duule ngam hawritde e am e dow weeyo, sabu mi jippoto e kammuuji e ewnagol, e daande maleyka mawɗo, e buuteteendu sakkitiindu maa foonde! Jokkee e ndaarde kammuuji e ndaarde, sabu Alla waɗii maandeeli tedduɗi keewɗi e dow kammuuji, hay e anndinde dewgal jom suudu e giɗo mum, hay e alkule duumingol hakkunde am e giɗo am, ɗe ɓooyataa waɗde. Ko noon, njaɓee bakkaatuuji mon, ñaagoo-mi mi holla on bakkaatuuji ɗi njejjit-ɗon ngam njaɓon ɗi kadi; e dow kala huunde, ndaree e giɗli Alla, sabu giɗli Alla ina sudda bakkaatuuji keewɗi. Ko noon, wota kule, sabu waawaa njaɓde ɗi fof.

Ko faati e ñaawoore: sabu on njaltii e maayde haa e nguurndam e nder am, Joomiraawo mon ummitiiɗo, Iisaa Almasiihu. Sabu mi rokki nguurndam am; hay gooto nanngaani ɗum. Mi rokki ɗum e yiɗde am, mi waɗatno ɗum kadi so ina fotnoo. Kono alaa haaju e hono ɗuum, sabu hol ko mbii-mi Iburaniyankooɓe: Sabu waawaa wonde nde ƴiiƴam ƴe'i e mbeewa, itta bakkaatuuji. Kono oo gorko, caggal nde o waɗi sadak gooto sabu bakkaatuuji haa abada, o jooɗii e junngo nano Alla. Giɗo am, mi ñaaawataa on no bakkaatuuji mon nii; sabu kala wonɓe e Almasiihu ina nuunɗinaama, eey laɓɓinaama, hay e waɗde nuunɗuɓe e nder am, Almasiihu Iisaa, guurɗo, laamotooɗo haa abada, sabu laamu am alaa joofnirde. Ko noon on njoofaama e bakkaatuuji mon; ko ɗum waɗi, on ngonataa e ñaawoore bakkaatuuji kono ko e ñaawoore nuunɗuɓe, sabu ñaawoore fuɗɗotoo ko e galle Alla.

Ko faati e artirde: sabu miɗo yoɓa, wiyi Joomiraawo. Ko heewɓe e mon accii cuuɗi, leyɗeele, ɓesnguuji, ɓe ndokki geɗe keewɗe, keewɗe; heewɓe e giɗo am ndokki nguurndam mum en, ko kamɓe ngoni Seedeeji am. Ko noon artirde geɗe sadakaaɗe e hay majjuɗe waɗata. Sabu hol ko woni joofnirde Yob: Joomiraawo wayli maccungaagu Yob, nde o juuli e sehilaaɓe makko: kadi Joomiraawo rokki Yob laabi ɗiɗi ko o joginoo ko adii. Daande Yakuba miñi am kadi wiyi: Ndaaree, en limtii ɓe ko weltaare muñɓe. On nanndii e muñal Yob, on njiyii joofnirde Joomiraawo: wonde Joomiraawo ina yurmi, ina yurmi no feewi. Ko noon kadi Baaba mon, ko noon kadi miɗo woniri, sabu miin ko mi jeyaaɗo e MIIN MAWƊO!

Ko faati e maayde: sabu heewɓe maa keɓ maayde, sabu miɗo haali ko fayti e jooɗorde ñaawoore am to Korentiyankooɓe: Sabu en fof en poti yaltude yeeso jooɗorde ñaawoore Almasiihu, ngam kala neɗɗo heɓa geɗe baɗaaɗe e ɓanndu mum, no o waɗiri nii, tawa ko moƴƴe walla ko bonɗe. Kadi kadi Korentiyankooɓe ina ceedoo: Golle kala neɗɗo maa peeñe. Sabu ñalawma oo hollitata ɗum, sabu maa feeñir e yiite, yiite ngoo ñawndat golle kala neɗɗo ko honno ngoniri. So golle neɗɗo njoldii, o heɓat maayde: kono kanko e hoore makko o hisnat: kono, no haa jooni, e yiite.

Ko faati e njoɓdi mawndi: sabu Korentiyankooɓe mbiyi kadi: Yiitere yiyaani, noppi nanaani, kadi naataani e ɓernde neɗɗo, geɗe ɗe Alla heblani giɗɓe Mo. Koolaaɓe am, cuɓaaɓe, on paamataa tan ñalɗi mawɗi e ñalɗi ɗi Baaba am heblani on e dow kammuuji; sabu on ngarata ko e ñalɗi, jawdi mawndi ina ndesana on e dow kammuuji. (Sabu on ngartata ko galle to am, Almasiihu Iisaa, e Baaba men, Yahwe, Alla duumiiɗo.) Kadi kadi: so golle neɗɗo nduurii, ɗe o mahiri e dow mum, o heɓat njoɓdi. Miɗo jogii njoɓdiiji keewɗi ngam rokkude, ndonu am ina wondi e am; ko noon, wota kule, sabu on keɓat geɗal e ndonu am; e ɗo ngon-mi, ko noon kadi on ngonirta, sabu waktuuji ceerndam ngasi.

Kufuneeje njoɓdi e laamu ina ndaarii on. Mi limtii woɓɓe e nder binndi: kufune muusataaɗo, kufune welwelo, kufune nuunɗal, kufune teddungal, e kufune nguurndam.

Sabu ɓeen dogɓe e dogdu, hay e laabi golle ɗi mi waɗani ɓe, maa keɓ kufune muusataaɗo: sabu on njogoriino hoore mon e les ruuhu am, on njannginiino hoore mon no kala fijoowo fof nii. Sabu ɓeen keɓɓe dogdu e sariya ko kufune muusataaɗo.

Ina woodi kufune welwelo. Sabu hol ko woni hoolaare men, walla welwelo, walla kufune welwelo? Mbele wonaa hay on e yeeso Joomiraawo men Iisaa Almasiihu e arde makko? Sabu, onon ngoni teddungal men e welwelo men. Ko noon kadi en keɓat welwelo mawngo, on fof on ngonat e teddungal, on teddiniree e am! Sabu ɓeen golliɓe e koolaaɗankaagal ngam waylude e mawninde banndiraaɓe mon ko kufune welwelo.

Ina woodi kufune nuunɗal ngam ɓeen timminɓe laabi mum en e ɓeen yiɗɓe peeñgol am. Ko noon, jokkee e ndaarde dow, sabu miɗo ɓadii, miɗo jogii hoolaare mawnde e peeñgol am. Sabu hoolaare mawnde e peeñgol am kadi ina adda njoɓdi mawndi. Sabu ɓeen jogiiɓe hoolaare mawnde e peeñgol am ko kufune nuunɗal.

Ina woodi kufune teddungal ngam ɓeen nyaamnɓe jawdi Alla. Sabu Piyeer winndi: nyaamnee jawdi Alla wonndi e mon, ndaaree ɗum, wonaa e doole, kono ko e yiɗde: wonaa sabu jawdi bonndi, kono ko e ɓernde laaɓnde. Sabu ɓeen ɓe njippintaaki hoore mum en e dow ndonu Alla, ɓe ngonaani e caɗeele jawdi bonndi, ɓe ngollanii mi e koolaaɗankaagal e hoyɗude e ɓernde giɗli potndi ko kufune teddungal.

Ina woodi kufune nguurndam mo mi rokkata seedeeji am, sabu ɓeen muñɓe torra e tooñannge sabu innde am e ɓeen muñɓe e muñal peeje e peeje ibliis. Sabu ɓeen keɓɓe e koolaaɗankaagal e ɗee geɗe ko kufune nguurndam.

On mbaɗete kadi no am nii, Aadama sakkitiɗo, on ngonat no am nii, on njiyat mi no ngon-mi nii, ko noon kadi on ngonirta. Ndaaree! Miɗo tagat on ɓalli kesi ɗi nanndaani e hay huunde nde mbaaw-ɗon miijaade. Ndaaree binndanɗe e nder binndi ɗo mi feeñani koolaaɓe am hade am yaltineede e dow kammuuji. Miɗo taƴanat on ndee ɓanndu no feewi, nde jogoto baawɗe mon, keɓe mon, baɗe mon; on kaawnat sanne.

Mbele on paamataa ko mi waɗi Poola o winndani Korentiyankooɓe? Ina woodi kadi ɓalli kammuuji, e ɓalli leydi: Kono teddungal kammuuji ko gootum, teddungal leydi ko goɗɗum. Ina woodi teddungal naange gootal, goɗɗum ko lewru, goɗɗum ko koode: sabu hoodere wootere ina seerti e hoodere wootere e teddungal. Ko noon kadi ummital maayɓe: ina aawaa e bonannde, ina ummitinee e laaɓal. Sukaaɓe am, on keɓat baawɗe keewɗe ɗe meeɗaani miijaade. Hay so tawii kala e mon ina seerti e goɗɗo, on fof on njahat e laawol timmungol e nder am, sabu mi waɗat on laamɓe e almuɓɓe yeeso Alla men!

Heewde geɗe ɗe ngonataa e mon teddeendi. Alaa ñawu kadi, alaa bakkaat kadi e mon, alaa maayde kadi e mon, alaa kulol, alaa mettere; sabu on fof on tagaama e laawol timmungol e nder am, Almasiihu Iisaa Joomiraawo. Ko noon mi wi'ata on, Giɗo am, won keɓoowo, sabu njoɓdiiji keɓoowo mi rokkata, ɗi ɓuri miijooji mon e koyɗi mon. Sabu lekki nguurndam, ngu rimi ɓiɓɓe leɗɗe sappo e ɗiɗi, ngu rokkii ɓiɓɓe mum lewru kala: haakooji lekki ngii ko ngam sellinde leñol. Alaa kadi kuddi, kono jooɗorde Alla, e Jawgel ngel wonat e mum, maccuɓe mum ngollanat mo. Ɓe njiyoyat yeeso mum, innde mum wonat e tiiɗe maɓɓe. Alaa kadi jemma toon, ɓe njiɗaa lampa, hay annoore naange, sabu Joomiraawo Alla ina rokka ɓe annoore, ɓe laamotoo haa abada. Ko noon, wota kule, Giɗo am koolaaɗo, njoɓdi mawndi ina ɓadii heɓeede e mon. Sabu Alla wonaa nuunɗuɗo, ngam yejjitde golle mon e golle giɗli mon, ɗe kollir-ɗon e Innde makko, e nder golle ɗe mbaɗ-ɗon e Seniiɓe, e ɗe mbaɗaton.

Miɗo ara e maa, ɓuri ko miijotooɗaa.

Miin woni daraniiɗo on.

Miɗo woniri, ko noon kadi on ngonirta, sabu on njiyat mi no ngon-mi nii.

Miɗo ara, njoɓdi am ina wondi e am ngam koolaaɓe.

Miin woni rokkoowo on ndonu mon timmungu, sabu ko noon rokkaami.

Miin woni rokkoowo on ɓalli kesi ngam lomtaade ɓanndu bakkaat.

Miin woni, sabu miin ko mi jeyaaɗo e MIIN MAWƊO!

Ndaaree peeñgol am e duule, no maandeeli ɗii anndinirta mi hade am jalbugol am yani e dow asamaan. Miin, Yesuwa HaMasiyak, mi nelii ɗee Konnguɗi.
