Noddaango Sakkitiiŋo Awa Nguun Nguurndam Timma e dow Ñaawooɓe

Ndaaree! Ko miin woni Yahweh, mawɗo miin woni, mi arii ngam nelde konnguɗi am yiɗde e noddaango sakkitiiŋo feewde eɓe njogorii timminde ñaawoore so ɓe calii noddaango am sakkitiingo. Hay so tawii, kala noddoowo innde Iisaa haa timma, ina heɓa kisal mawngal, duumingal, e njoɓdi mawndi, worɓe heewɓe maa njippoo e ñaawoore sabu tooñanngeeji mum en e tiiɗnaare ɓerɗe mum en. Sabu, ko miin woni ñaawoowo tooñannooɓe ngam tuubde, ɓeen noon caliiɓe tuubde, miɗo jokki e ñaawoore haa ɓerɗe mum en tiiɗna no feewi, ɓe njippoo e ñaawoore.

Ko ɗum waɗi, miɗo ari ngam waɗde ndeeɗoo mesaas e yiɗde am e mon, miɗo nelda moƴƴere am nde njogaaki e aduna ñaawoowo oo kadi e golle am ngam ustude ɓerɗe mon ɗe njiɗaa tuubde ngam jaɓde kisal; walla noon, e yiɗde mon e murto mon, mon accaa e nokku calagol timmungal, mon ɓennda, mon mbaɗa ñaawoore timmunde. Faamee, alaa waktu goɗɗo e waktuuji am yurmeende e yurmeende am ngam nguurndam tuubgol sabu peeje am kisal ngam neɗɗaagu kala garoowo e kisal timmungal e waktuuji mum en e yamirooje mum en ngam ɓeen jaɓɓotooɓe yiɗde am e mum en. Ko ɗum waɗi, waktuuji mon ko jooni, noddaango sakkitiiŋo ina neldee jooni. Selah.

Nanee, O nanee daande Alla mawɗo duumotooɗo nuunɗal, hay Yahweh, mawɗo miin woni, mo woni kadi Alla duumotooɗo nuunɗal, kadi faamde maanaa haala am, kadi anndude daande am ina nanndi e daande ndiyam keewɗam ina doga, ina wuuri, kadi faamde daande am ina diwta no daande diwɗi keewɗi nii, (hay diwɗi diwɗi) e nder tiknaraare am nuunɗunde e hoɗɓe e leydi ndii, hoɗɓe heen e nder mawnude e mawnude e tooñanngeeji e tooñanngeeji.

Ndaaree! Ko miin woni miin woni, hay mawɗo miin woni, mi tagii asamaanuuji e leydi e timmal heen kala, e nder diisnondiral am nuunɗungal, mi waɗii asamaanuuji e leydi timmunde e laabi am timmal, hay so tawii mi tagii ɗi e nder nanondiral e timmal nuunɗungal ɗo tagoore am fof foti wuurdude e timmal nanondiral e yiɗde e golle e nder yiɗde gooto e goɗɗo. E nder yiɗde maɓɓe e Baaba maɓɓe e tagɗo, ɓe ndokka daande am e yamirooje ngam nguurndam maɓɓe ngam goonga ɓe mbaawa faamde koye maɓɓe no ɓe tagiraa nii, kadi ɓe mbaawa faamde laabi am. Kono, tooñanngeeji tawaama e Heylel, hay so tawii o yani e timmal teddungal, hay so tawii o tagiraama e mawnude e mawnude, tan o yani e leydi, o ustaama e tooñanngeeji e niɓɓere ngam hoɗde e mbaydi mboddi ndi huɗaa sabu mawnude Serafim en. Selah.

Sabu goonga duumingal, laabi am ina ɓuri laabi neɗɗo, ko miin woni taariiɗo jappeere am laamu ujunaaje sappo laabi sappo, hay so tawii limoore malaa'ikaaji ɗi limoore mum en alaa, ɓurɓe hakkilantaagal, doole, e semmbe, ɓe njiɗi Yahweh, ɓe njiɗi janngude laabi Yahweh, mawɗo miin woni e nder mawnude e mawnude e dewal duumingal: Selah.

Goonga, e goonga mawɗo, duumingal, mi tagii ɓe doole e teddungal, mi ɓoorii ɓe jalbugol jalbungol, e nuunɗal duumingal, e teddungal duumingal. Goonga, e goonga mawɗo, duumingal, ɓe meeɗaa waasde yiɗde walla yiɗde hay so tawii ko e geɗe ɓurɗe famɗude abada; ɓe ngollanta mi e tagoore am timmunde e ɓerɗe maɓɓe yiɗde Baaba maɓɓe e tagɗo, hay Elohim, e dewal maɓɓe e Almasiihu Iisaa.

(Hay so tawii ko Kanko, Yahweh, mawɗo e duumotooɗo miin woni, o waɗii geɗe kala e asamaan e leydi e juuɗe makko doole e duumotooɗe e dewal.)

Ko ɗum waɗi anndude goonga duumingal wonde Almasiihu Iisaa ko Joomiraawo Joomiraaɓe e Laamɗo Laamɓe, kanko woni garoowo jam e asamaanuuji kam e leydi, kanko woni gartoowo e juuɗe am tedduɗe (e yiɗde makko e Baaba makko e Tagɗo, Elohim, hay mawɗo miin woni) Laamu gonngu e les njiimaandi makko so geɗe kala timmii so juuɗe makko doole timminii geɗe kala binndaaɗe, ndewnaaɗe ko fayti e Joomiraawo nuunɗal duumingal! Hay so tawii ɓiy neɗɗo oo timminii geɗe kala e doole mawɗe, e yiɗde mawnde. Hay so tawii e oon sahaa, mi timminii e doole am e juuɗe makko doole e sadak geɗe kala dokkaaɗe e peeje am duumingal e sirluuji am ngam o timmina.

Ko ɗum waɗi, miɗo rokka on faamde, wonde ko miin woni baawɗo e baawɗe kala e njiimaandi kala, miɗo faama, miɗo timmina, miɗo annda no feewi, miɗo toppitii kala detal jowitiingal e kala fannu tagoore am, hay so tawii no mawniri walla no anndiraa doole e detal ɓurngal famɗude, alaa ko suuɗii e gite am! Ko miin woni baawɗo timminde geɗe kala e sahaa gooto e doole am e doole am duumingal. Kono, ko miin kadi gollortoo geɗe kala caggal wasiyaaji yiɗde am, mi alaa wasiya, ko ɗum waɗi, peeje am e sirluuji am maa timmin e nder ɗiiɗoo waktuuji timminirɗi ɗii, hay so tawii ko waktuuji JOONI.

Kono, e yiɗde am e tagoore am e yiɗde am e sukaaɓe e galle, miɗo taga tagoore e yimɓe (e yiɗde wootere) ngam dalillaaji am duumingal dokkaaɗi e dewal mawngal e teddungal, miɗo rokka ɓe doole e mbaadiiji mum en. Ko ɗum waɗi, miɗo wasiyoo on yo on njanngu laabi am e kisal walla on cuɓoo yiite mbonka haa abada. Kono anndee, aan neɗɗo jippiiɗo, aɗa jogii cuɓagol ngol waylotoo e sahaa gooto haa abada, aɗa joginoo cuɓagol, kadi kala cuɓiiɗo bonannde e timmal so timmal arii, meeɗaa daɗde e ñaawoore mum en haa abada, haa abada. Selah.

Hol an, aan neɗɗo jippiiɗo, hay an naatɗo e murto ɓe ngalaa hakkille, ɓe ngalaa hakkille, ɓe ngalaa hakkille e ɓeen jippiiɓe e tooñanngeeji kala, e yiɗde, e yiɗde, e mbonka ngam mawninde koye mon e mawnikinaare e Alla mawɗo, duumotooɗo, tagɗo on ngam dalillaaji am e welo-welo am?

Hol an, aan neɗɗo jippiiɗo, e nder majjere maa e yiɗde maa, aɗa waawi ɓamtude koyngal maa e Alla mawɗo, duumotooɗo asamaan asamaanuuji (hay O rokkirɗo ma nguurndam) e laabi Seytaane en? Ko ɗum waɗi, faamde dalillaaji am e anniya am sabu miɗo nelda ɗeeɗoo Konnguɗi e MON ko noddaango am sakkitiiŋo e heewɓe e mon, ɓe cikkata e majjere wonde ina njogii waktu mo njogii, miɗo rokka on hay konnguɗi jeertinaango e yiɗde am nde alaa maayde e mon, miɗo siftina on kadi e bayyinaango ñaawoore e musiibaaji dokkaaɗi ɗi njiɗɗon timmude, ɗi Almasiihu Iisaa waɗi e jappeere laamu e doole Yahweh, kadi ɗi Yahweh e hoore mum bayyini, hay mawɗo miin woni!

(Mbele on paamaani bayyinaango am meeɗaa wayleede?)

Kono, on njiɗi hay haala diwɗi jeeɗiɗi ɗi mi holliri, ɗi mi waɗi Yuhanna hollitoowo oo momti e sahaa mum haa waktuuji laaɓtuɗi hollitde ɗi waktuuji ɗii ngoni jooni. (O jokkata haa seedantaagal seedeeɓe am ɗiɗo timma e ñaawoore timmunde, ko adii nde musiiba ɓurɗo mawnude oo yani e tooñannooɓe, Selah.) Hay diwɗi ɗi Yeshua HaMashiach e Yahweh, mawɗo miin woni, njaltini e jappeere am doole bayyinaango e ñaawoore, ɗi jooni dokkaa e juuɗe nelaaɗo am doole, mo mi neldata ɗeeɗoo Konnguɗi yiɗde e jeertinaango tiiɗngo, tiiɗngo, hay so tawii kala baawɗo arde fof ina foti arde, hade ñaawoore yani timmude, waktu alaa!

Ko ɗum waɗi, so on njiɗii daɗde e ñaawoore mon e mbonka mon duumingal, miɗo yamira on yo on mbaɗ Iisaa joomiraawo mon timmuɗo, tawa alaa ko cikkaton. Sabu ñaawoore am ina jippoo, ina jippoo e kala mo neldaa, hay ñaawoore kuulal ngo duumotoo e timmal mum haa abada, haa abada. Ko ɗum waɗi, miɗo wasiyoo kala mo anndaani Iisaa Almasiihu ko Joomiraawo ummoraade e ɓernde timmunde e iimaanu ngam waɗde ɗum jooni, miɗo anndi ko miin ummini mo e maayɓe e doole mawɗe sanne ngam maayde kadi e jokkude e ndonu mum haa abada, haa abada. Kono, ko ɓuri heewde e yimɓe ina njalta e ñaawoore mawnde, duumingal no binndiraa e binndi ceniɗi ɓooyɗi e binndi ceniɗi jooni nii.

Kono miɗo ari ngam waɗde jeertinaango am, hay so tawii bayyinaango ñaawoore mon timmunde bayyinaama, ina jokki. Sabu laabi am ko laabi yurmeende e yurmeende e yiɗde e goonga duumingal, miɗo yiɗi so wonaa gooto maayde, kono worɓe fof ina keɓa kisal duumingal e mbayliigu, hay so tawii ko ɓuri heewde e maɓɓe ina caloo dokkal am, ina cuɓoo torra mum en duumingal e ñaawoore mum en. Hay so tawii mi arii ngam jeertinde worɓe, alaa jeertinaango ngo waylata ñaawoore am, walla waktuuji mum en, walla batte mum en, walla peeje am duumingal ngam hisnude ɓiɓɓe am adanɓe, hay so tawii miɗo ƴetta ɓe ngam timminde ndonu mum en duumingal hade musiiba yani. Selah.

Miɗo nelda ndeeɗoo mesaas yiɗde e jeertinaango e yiɗde am e neɗɗo bonɗo, hay e yiɗde wootere nde neldatmi mesaas yiɗde e jeertinaango e sukaaɓe am ɓe njaɓi Iisaa Almasiihu e dewal; sabu miɗo addana ɗum toɓo e dow nuunɗuɓe e tooñannooɓe fof. Sabu yiɗde am e mon, miɗo ari ngam waɗde noddaango am sakkitiiŋo hade musiiba ari ummoraade e jappeere am laamu doole duumingal, ñaawoore, e bayyinaango ɗo daande am diwnde ndee woni haa jooni ina diwta e tikkere mawnde, nuunɗunde. Hay so tawii, sabu on CALII nande mi hay so tawii mi jeertinii, mi jeertinii duuɓi keewɗi, keewɗi jooni! Nanee mi, nanee mi no feewi sabu waktuuji mon mbonka duumingal maa neldee ngam haɓde e ñaawoore kala yiɗɗo waɗde fenaande, mo njiɗi tooñanngeeji, kadi so on calii mi e ɗiiɗoo waktuuji yurmeende, on njaltata e ñaawoore duumingal sabu alaa cuɓagol goɗngol.

Kono, e nder mawnikinaare mon wumnde, tiiɗnde, on njaɓataa neɗɗo gooto mo neldimi ngam kisal mon timmungal, hay Iisaa Nasaraatu, hay ɓiy oo, golloowo Yahweh, mo mbaɗɗirmi ruuhu hokkoowo nguurndam, hay mo mbaɗɗirmi seeɗa seeɗa les Yahweh e hoore mum e ummital am e maayɓe e teddungal Almasiihu Iisaa! Sabu ko miin woni mawɗo e laamɗo dow tagoore am fof, e goonga duumingal, ngu waylotaako, ko miin neldata toɓo e dow nuunɗuɓe e tooñannooɓe fof e yiɗde am e tagoore am, e nder laabi am alaa ko woni teddungal yimɓe e ñaawoore am nuunɗunde e tagoore am.

Ko ɗum waɗi, faamde wonde neɗɗo teddinɗo ko gooto e laabi keewɗi pollitɗi ɗi jippiiɓe ɓee, ko ɗum waɗi miɗo ari ngam jeertinde hay tooñannooɓe ɓurɓe bonde e hoɗɓe e leydi ndii hade ñaawoore mum en yani, hay so tawii ɓe mbaawaa daɗde e seeɗa seeɗa e yurmeende hade musiiba yani. Miɗo wasiyoo kala nanoowo walla janngoowo ɗeeɗoo Konnguɗi yo yiltu Iisaa Nasaraatu jooni, tawa alaa ko sikkata sabu yimɓe heewɓe keɓataa fartaŋŋe goɗɗo, sabu e nder tooñanngeeji mum en bonɗi e yiɗde mum en kala ko ceni, ɓe njaltata e ñaawoore duumingal, hay so tawii ɓe ndesndaa e torra ɗo ɓe ndoongata ñaawoore jappeere laamu boɗeejo mawnde, ɗo ɓe njahataa, kono ɓe kaɓɓetee, ɓe ndokkee yiite kuuge ɗo tooñanngeeji maɓɓe ndokketee yiite ngam njoɓdi timmundi nuunɗal haa abada.

Kono, an, aan neɗɗo jippiiɗo, aɗa jooɗii e laabi maa tooñanngeeji, hay so tawii a foolaama no feewi e jippiiɓe ɓee e nder majjere maa e wumɗere maa sabu fenaande maɓɓe, hay fenaande nde njiɗɗaa sabu aɗa yiɗi laabi fenaande maa. Ko ɗum waɗi, mi neldi leydi ndii delko mawngo, tiiɗngo, mbele on fof mon kaɓɓee, ɓe njiɗi tooñanngeeji, ɓe calii Iisaa Nasaraatu, hay Yeshua HaMashiach. Nde on njippii e majjere e laabi mon wumɗi, tiiɗɗi, nde on njooɗii e niɓɓere e nder apathy e tooñannge mawngo, nde on njahata e nder futility ina ndesndoo tikkere mawnde e batte duumingal e nder mawnude mon, miɗo ɓamta daande am haa jooni, hay so tawii miɗo nelda ɗeeɗoo Konnguɗi ko seedantaagal e ɓeen caliiɓe, hay so tawii miɗo yaakorii on njippoo e Almasiihu Iisaa, mbele mi hisna on e ñaawoore jippiiɓe ɓee: Selah.

Aan neɗɗo tago, hay on fof sukaaɓe Adam ɓe ngalaa hisnude, ɓe ngalaa tuubde, hay on fof ɓe njokkondirmi e leydi ndii e yiɗde am e mon! Kono on ngoongɗinii fenaande ɓurnde laaɓde nde ndokkirɗon ko janngirde e nder jaŋde mon e jippiiɓe ɓee. Hay janngirde maayde dokkaande e nder juɓɓule mon bonɗe, bonɗe maayde ɗo laabi sodom en laamii jooni, ɗe njiɗi, njiɗi sukaaɓe mon mbele ɓe mbaawa ñamminde ɓe ngalaa tooñanngeeji e yiɗde maɓɓe bonnde. Kono, on njiɗii no feewi welo-welo tooñanngeeji, on njiɗii no feewi yiɗde mon ngam heɓde jawdi ɓurndi heewde, haa on njaɓi waɗde sadak sukaaɓe mon e laabi Baal ngam heɓde mburu pollitiiɗo. Ko ɗum waɗi, mi jaɓii jippiiɓe ɓee toonya nguura mon, mi rokki ɓe timmal mon timmal, hay so tawii ko mboros, daaba ladde ummoriiɗo e maayo, e daaba ladde ummoriiɗo e leydi mbaawa timminde dalillaaji am, sabu hay añɓe am ina ndokka daande am.

Sabu jaŋde mon mbiyateeɗo ko fenaande, ko fenaande e diisnondiral, ɗum noon ko sabu on njaɓaani ganndal goonga e laabi Elohim tagɗo on, on cuɓii ko yo on mbaɗ hypnotisé e gite mon ɓaleeje ɗe njiɗɗon e dewal so on njahii e ekran e ekran e nder niɓɓere ɓurnde niɓɓude. Kono on foolaama no feewi, on wumɗinaama e welo-welo jirlotooɗo, jirlotooɗo so hakkillaaji mon mbaɗtaama e kemikal e diisnondiral e laawol kuutorɗe ɗe kaɓɓirton koye mon e tooñanngeeji. Gandal fenaande noon ina addana mawnikinaare, mawnikinaare ina adii mbonka mawka. Faamde, aan neɗɗo, ganndal maa waawaa hisnude ma e ñaawoore am, ñaawoore jippiiɓe ndee ina foti jippaade jooni. Kono, an, aan neɗɗo tago, aɗa majji no feewi haa a faamaani wonde jippiiɓe ɓee njaɓii ma e fenaande mum en, hay so tawii ɓe mbaɗatno worɓe heewɓe no mbaawirta nii ngam naatde e maɓɓe e ndonu mum en wulngo, bonngo, diwngo. Hay kuuge tiiɗɗe baɗaaɗe ngam seytaane e malaa'ikaaji mum, wonaa Adam. Selah.

Kono, e jaɓgol mon jaɓgol, on njiɗi mawninde koye mon e golle mon adunaaruuje ɗe ngalaa nafoore, ɗo njiɗɗon mooftude kaayitaaji mon fenaande ɗi ngalaa nafoore e limoore mon fenaande nde ngalaa nafoore e konte fenaande ɗe ngalaa nafoore, ɗe njippotoo e mon e sahaa gooto e ñaawoore jooni, hay so tawii ko sabu ɗee ko laabi Esawu yeeyɗo ndonu mum ngam heɓde mburu. Kono, e nder jaɓgol mon jaɓgol, on njaɓii jippiiɓe ɓee ƴettude kaalis e mon e hulɓinaare doole ngam yoɓde jaŋde sukaaɓe mon e laabi maayde. Hay so tawii kala jannguɗo e majjere e wumɗere hono ndee ina jaɓi gollaade jippiiɓe ɓee tawa ina sikka ina waɗana Alla e hoore mum moƴƴere mawnde e tooñanngeeji mum en, no Yahweh foti yaltude e tagoore mum e njiimaandi mum sabu mawnikinaare mum en nde alaa nafoore, nde tooñanngeeji mum en. Hay ko waawaa waɗde, meeɗaa waɗde, sabu ko miin woni.

E nder laabi mon tiiɗɗi, tooñanngeeji, on majjinii hay noppi mon, on ndarii e muusde, e muusde, e wumɗe e laabi mon tooñanngeeji ɗi usti on e maccungaagu ko maccuɗo tooñannge ngam weltinde yiɗde jippiiɓe ɓee, ɓe ngollorton e laabi mon ɗi ngalaa iimaanu. Kono, golloowo oo heddotaako e galle haa abada so o jaɓii naatde e damal gootal tan feewde e asamaanuuji ɗi meeɗataa udditde neɗɗaagu: Iisaa Almasiihu mo woni kadi, Iisaa Masiya, mo innde mum woni kadi Yeshua HaMashiach, mo woni damal mawngal, udditiingal kisal ngal meeɗataa udditeede e juuɗe am ngam nuunɗuɓe dewal e nder Almasiihu Iisaa. Selah!

Ko miin woni, mi neldi ɗeeɗoo Konnguɗi yiɗde e ñaawooɓe ɓee e juuɗe nelaaɗo am e golloowo am, e juuɗe nelaaɗo am e golloowo am mi neldi ɗeeɗoo Konnguɗi e ɓeen ɓe ñaawoowo seeɗa.

Faamde kala nanoowo ɗeeɗoo Konnguɗi, wonde ɗum wonaa bayyinaango ñaawoore timmunde, ngo addanta neɗɗo tago timmal. E ko wonaa ɗuum, ndeeɗoo mesaas ina gollina ngam jeertinde ɓe njiɗaa dewal e tooñannooɓe Babilon sirlu e hay kala mo ɗeeɗoo Konnguɗi neldaa, (woɓɓe maa ngar e kisal e nder ɗeeɗoo leƴƴanɗe cakkitiiɗe) kono kadi ko seedantaagal jeertinaango sakkitiiŋo dokkaaɗo hade ñaawoore yani timmude e Babilon sirlu.

Faamde, Konnguɗi am maa tawa gite, noppi, e ɓerɗe ɓeen ɓe neldaa, alaa golle ummoriiɗe e jippiiɓe ɓee, walla bonanndeeji mum en, walla karallaagal mum en ngal alaa nafoore, ngal majjere waawaa haɗde ko mawɗo miin woni yiɗi timminde.

Miɗo siyna ɓataake am yiɗde e ñaawooɓe ɓee e yaakaare wonde e dewal e Konnguɗi am neldaaɗi e yiɗde am e maɓɓe, ɓe ndaɗatno e ñaawoore maɓɓe hade juuɗe cakkitiiɗe ɗee yani e leƴƴannde cakkitiiɗo ndee.

Miɗo siyna ɓataake am yiɗde e yiɗde mawnde e Laamu duumingal!

Ko miin woni miin woni, ko miin woni Yahweh, mawɗo e duumotooɗo miin woni.
