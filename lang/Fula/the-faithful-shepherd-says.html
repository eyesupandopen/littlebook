Seedeeɓe ɗiɗo: Eklesiya
E nder Golle Yuhanna Baptista
12 Mars, 2024

Duɗo Goongaajo O Wi'i

Duɗo goongaajo o wi'i goonga'en: Goonga, goonga, mi wi'i on, ngoɗɗitee bonannde, wota on waɗ hakke. Sabu njoɓdi am ina wondi e am ngam mi hokkude kala neɗɗo no golle mum njiirunoo. Mi noddirii on e yiɗde Baaba am mbele keɓon nguurndam, kadi keɓon ngam haa abada. Mi noddirii on ngam njahon e Laamu am, ngam ngollanon mi, kadi ngam njooɗoɗon e dow leydi e nder peewal. Mi hokkii on moƴƴere am mbele mawniron e nder am, miin Joomiraawo mon Iisaa Almasiihu. Wota on tennito e laɓɓinol am, sabu kala mo mi yiɗi, mi felan ɗum, mbele moƴƴere am kisinoore ndee ina ɓesda e nder luggeende yonki mon, haa mbaɗon golle mawɗe, golle barke, golle cellal, kadi mbaron ɓiɓɓe keewɗe e nder am, yimɓe am.

Sabu Baaba am ko Alla giɗo, kadi yiɗde Baaba am fotaa fooldeede. E nder am, miin Joomiraawo peewɗo o, ina woodi heewende timmunde nde Baaba am hokki mi. O hokkii mi kala huunde e juuɗe am, no Firawna mawnirnoo Yuusuf, o hokki mo Misra fof e juuɗe makko, non Baaba am mawniri mi, o hokki kala huunde wonnde e asamaan e dow leydi e juuɗe am. O hokkii mi innde ɓurnde kala inɗe, mbele e innde Iisaa kala koppi fof ina fowta, woni e asamaan e dow leydi, kadi kala ɗemngal fof ina seedtoo joomankaagal am, woni e asamaan e dow leydi.

Mi hokkii on nguurndam haa abada, mi waɗanii on laawol mbele ndogaton e ladde, ndogaton e ñalaande mawnde tikkere aroore ndee, mbele wota on tennito! Wota on tampu e waɗde ko moƴƴi, kono ndaron e yeeso am, mi jokkan e diwtingol on e tawaare am. On jooɗinaama e nokkuuje toowɗe, kadi on keɓan geɗal e jappeere am no mi heɓiri geɗal e jappeere Baaba am. Miɗo jogii kuruneeje keewɗe e barkeeji keewɗi ɗi ndesanaɗon, yimɓe am goonga'en. Ndaron e yeeso am, laatoɗon timmuɓe e nder am. Ngoɗɗitee hakkeeji mon, accitee sahaaaji coytuɗi ɗii pooɗa mbuunndi ndii e nguurndam mon. E nder am nguurndam haa abada ina woodi, e nder am kadi tagoore hesere ndee ina woodi, neɗɗo keso oo, hono Almasiihu e nder mon, jikke teddungal! Ngoɗɗitee bonannde, ngoɗɗitee hakke. Jokkee e seedeede mi e nder heeferɓe ɓee, sabu haa jooni ina woodi yimɓe ɓe kisintee e nder waktu cakkitiiɗo oo. Jokkee e seedeede innde am, sabu alaa innde woɗnde les asamaan nde yimɓe mbaawi hisirde. Keɓee deeƴere e nder fooftere nde mi hokki on ndee, njoɓnee doŋɗe mon e koyɗe am; sabu doŋgal am ina newii, kadi jolokowal am ina hoyi.

Goonga'en (ɓeen foolɓe hakke e caɗeele rewrude e ƴiiƴam am jarnaangam), mi wi'i goonga'en: Miɗo jogii njoɓdi mawndi nde ndesanaɗon e nder galle Baaba am. Gite nji'aani, noppi nanaani, kadi naataani e ɓerɗe yimɓe geɗe ɗe peblanaa ɓe ɗee. Mi njoɓtan on no feewi kuruneeje keewɗe, mbele ndaron ngam am, laatoɗon goonga'en e daande am e konnguɗi am; sabu e nder konnguɗi ɗii on njiytan nguurndam. Wanaa miin woni Konngol ngol laatii terɗe, jooɗiingol e nder mon?

Miin woni Ñaawoowo peewɗo o.

Miin woni Duɗo moƴƴo duɗo gurndan ɗan.

Miin woni cellal mon, mi sellinan on haa timma.

Mi jibinaama e debbo, mi laatii terɗe, hay so tawii alaa hakke e nder am.

Mi fawaama e leggal ngam hakkeeji mon, goonga, hay gooto ƴettude nguurndam am, kono miin e hoore am mi rokkiri ngam yiɗde nde njoganii mi ɓiɓɓe worɓe e rewɓe Baaba am.

Mi umminaama e maayde ñalnde tataɓere ndee, sabu waawaano maayde jogoo mi. Baaba am heli kolɗe maayde ɗee e baawɗe ummital am.

Miin woni ɓiɓɓe adanɗe maayɓe ɓee.

Ɓooyataa on kawran e am e dow weeyo, kadi mette maayde ɗee njoppitan ɓeen ɗaaniiɓe, ndeen onon wuurɓe heddiiɓe ɓee on njaɓɓondiran e dow, kadi haa abada on wondon e am. Miin woni Ɓiɗɗo Alla, e nder junngo am njoɓdi am ina wonani goonga'en am. Ndaree tiiɗɗi, ɓiɓɓe am, wota on tennito. Koolon Joomiraawo e baawɗe doole am, sabu ko Baaba am tan ɓuri mi mawnude. Mi nanii duwaawuuji mon, mi jaɓii on no njeyaaɓe am.

Muñee, njokkee e gollanaade mi. Sabu ɓooyataa on ngartan galle e am, kadi haa abada en njokkan e golle Baaba men. Koolon, njooɗee e welwelo. Mi anndi heewɓe e mon ina njiya fitina e musiibaaji ɗii ina ɓadoo, kadi heewɓe e mon nji'ii konuuji bonɗo oo ina taƴa keeri mon. Wota kulon ɗi! Wota kulon ɗi, hay so tawii ina njiɗnoo halkude on so ɗi mbaawnoo, kono ɗi ngaraani ngam goonga'en am, sabu dogdu mawndu nduu e dental mawngal ngal ina heddii ina hisina goonga'en am e waktu jarribo e jarribo ngo fotnoo wonde e winndere ndee fof. Fooftoɗon e yiɗde am, anndon mi accataa on, mi woppataa on, no Baaba am acciraani mi, woppiraani mi e waktu sadak am. Sabu hisnaare mon ina deeƴi e nder am; ko ɗum waɗi, tiiɗnoɗon e am, sabu mi semmbinan on haa ɓura no mbaɗirnoo mi. Mawni Sirri dewal!
