Laawol Jaɓɓugol Annabaaku: No Mi Jaɓɓirta Batakeeji Annabaaku

Ñalnde 2 lewru Mbooy hitaande 2024, Joomiraawo men Iisaa Almasiihu fuɗɗii hokkal am batakeeji annabaaku ngam mi yeggina yimɓe fii feeñtere makko nde ardata e nder jamaa makko e fii geɗe goɗɗe. Annabaakuuji ɗii arii e am e mbaadi siforaandi e nder Deftere Alla no "juuɗe Joomiraawo" fawii e dow neɗɗo nii. E dow am, ko teddeendi tiiɗndi e nder ruuhu am, nde yaltataa haa mi timmina bataku ngun. Almasiihu ina naatna konnguɗi seeɗa walla haalaaji e nder hakkil am ɗi mbaawataa accude, ɗi njiɗɗataa. Wakkatiiji keewɗi, miɗo nela hoore am haala ka ngam mi siftora. So mi jooɗiima mi fuɗɗii winndude, Joomiraawo ina holla mi Konnguɗi ɗin tigi-rigi e nder ruuhu am so miɗo winnda: Eey, wakkatiiji goɗɗi miɗo heɓa yi'annde konnguɗi ɗin, wakkatiiji keewɗi kadi miɗo nana konnguɗi ɗin so miɗo winnda, wonaa no nanirtee e noppi kono ko e nder ruuhu am, tee ko e laawol ngol alaa e sago.

So mi waɗii juumre, mi winndii miijooji am, konnguɗi ɗin ina ndarii haa mi momta ko mi ɓeydi kon. So mi sikkitiima hoore am, mi momtii haala ka Almasiihu yiɗunoo heddaade, konnguɗi ɗin ina ndarii haa mi artira haala ka no wonirnoo nii, tee haala ka yaltataa e hakkil am haa mi waɗa ɗum. Gila jooni, Joomiraawo ina hokkam batakeeji diina luggiɗɗi sanne, o yahrii kam e aayeeje keertiiɗe. O fawata haala e nder ruuhu am, mi winnda ɗum; ndeen mi yiiloo haala ka e nder Deftere am Alla, mi tawa haala ka ina woni e feccere winndannde. E oon sahaa, o hollirta mi aayeeje ɗe pot-mi limtude, mi naatna ɗe e nder daartol ngol, ndeen konnguɗi ɗin njokkondira ɗoon e ɗoon. Haa jooni miɗo haawnii no Konnguɗi ɗi Joomiraawo hokkatam ɗin kawrirta e aayeeje ɗe o hokkatam ngam mi limta, e no aayeeje ɗe limtumi ɗen njahdirta e daartol ngol e laawol ngol mbaawataa heɓde e hoore am. Mi meeɗaa waɗde hono ɗuum e nguurndam am fof, haa jooni. Heewi wonde, mi anndaa bataku ngun fof haa mi artii mi laaɓndini binndol e maantore. Ndeen, mi janngitay ɗum, Joomiraawo waɗa mi waylude geɗe seeɗa, ma taw o ɓeyda haala ngam laaɓndude, ma taw o momta konngol o lomtina ngol e goɗngol, e ko nanndi heen. Ndeen teddeendi ndin yalta, mi annda mi timminii.

Hollitaama wonde e nder kala ɗeen batakeeji annabaaku, mi meeɗaa waylude hay huunde e tiitoonde, hay laawol gootol; tee meeɗaa wonde waylo mawngo e ko woni e nder mum, hay laawol gootol. Annabaakuuji juutɗi ɗin heewi ko nanngude waktu gooto walla ko ɓura seeɗa ngam winndeede, laaɓndingol ngol ina waawi ɓeydude waktu gooto walla ɗiɗi goɗɗi. Miɗo laaɓndina kala huunde e winndannde ngam mi hooloo mi alaa ko mi bonni e faamde am neɗɗaagu. Haa jooni mi waɗaani, kono ngam laaɓndude: Heewi e ɗee Konnguɗi ina ɓuraa faamde am so miɗo winnda ɗe, tee ina foti mi yaha e winndannde ngam mi jannga ko ɓuri ɗum tuugnaade e ko mi heɓi kon. Hay laawol gootol batakeeji ɗin meeɗaa luurdude e winndannde, hay so tawii ina caɗtina faamde am e winndannde, tee kam kadi mi janngoowo. Jooni, mi waajotooɗo, mi jogii ko ina wona duuɓi 30 e nder winndannde, mi waɗii waajuuji, janngirɗe ekn. Woto mi wiy ɗum e dow humpito am duuɓi: Ko huunde nde mbaawataa mi waɗde, winndude huunde e ndee tolno e nder oon waktu seeɗa e doole am e faamde am. Ina nannga balɗe keewɗe, wakkatiiji goɗɗi yontere walla ko ɓuri ɗum, ngam mi heblude waaju juutɗo, tee ɗum nanndaa, hay seeɗa. Ɗum ummaaki e am, ɗum ɓuri faamde am. Ruuhu annabaaku yanii e dow am, sabu Seedee Iisaa ko ruuhu annabaaku.
