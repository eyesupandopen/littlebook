Seedeeji ɗiɗi: Eklesiya
E nder Golle Poola Nelaaɗo
1 mee 2024
Duule 7

Nanaam, nanaam booɗɗum!

Nanaam, nanaam booɗɗum, onon annabaaɓe fenaaɓe bonnooɓe Konngol am laaɓungol, sabu on ngonaa e am, on alaa Ruuhu am laaɓɗo, on njippii e Jom suudu am, Eklesiya am mo noddu-mi! Nanaaɗon, mbaajorooɗon konngol am, onon leñol ngol alaa goonɗinal, ngol wonki, ngol bonni! Sabu on njaaboto kala golle mon bonɗe! Nanaaɗon konngol am jeertinngol, onon wonnooɓe Eklesiya am, sabu tikkere am ina wuli e mooɗon, sabu miin woni Joomiraawo Alla! Sabu heewɓe e mooɗon ina nduumotoo e lowe e kooseeje. On njaŋan kooseeje e kaaƴe, “Tippanee min, nduume min e yeeso jooɗiiɗo e jappeere laamu, e tikkere Joomiraawo”: Sabu ñalawma mawɗo tikkere makko arii, holi ardo fotde daranaade ɗum?

Sabu aduna oo fof maa yiy feeñere am, dimbande mawnde maa ari e kala neɗɗo, halkaare nde aletaake maa heɓta aduna oo so Jom suudu am sottinaama e kisal. Sabu mi ittay haɗoowo oo, bonannde fof maa won e weytaare timmunde, ñaawoore mawnde maa iwe e dow kammu, no winndiraa e deftere mawnde feeñere am, deftere Wahyu! Nii mi ñaawoyi on, onon fof bonnooɓe Eklesiya am: balɗe mon limtaama, ɓooyataa ɓerɗe mon maa njoobu so tikkere am feeñii. Onon annabaaɓe fenaaɓe ɓe ngalaa nafoore, ɓe naati e Eklesiya am, on njannginii diineeji cakkanɗi e Konngol am laaɓungol e laabi am laaɓɗi, on njoɓay.

On njaddinii sukaaɓe am tokosɓe luuttaade e naatde e caɗeele mawɗe, ɗe mi artiranta on laabi teemedere e ñaawoore am e dow mon. On mbayli maanaa konnguɗi e binndi am laaɓɗi, on mbonnii binndi am laaɓɗi e winnditde ɗi e waylude konnguɗi Alla Duumotooɗo, konnguɗi Yahwe, on njoɓay haa abada. On naatinii binndi bonɗi e Eklesiya am no maamiraaɓe mon ɓe ngalaa nafoore mbontiniri ɓulli e nyaamdu neɗɗaagu, on njoɓay haa abada. Kala konngol ngol kaalɗon, kala ceede bonɗe ɗe ndesondirɗon e hoore mon, kala miijo bonngo ngo miijiiɗon, kala anniya bonɗo mo ndewɗon, fof maa holliree on, on njoɓay haa ko ɓuri famɗude; on mbaawataa hay dara daraade e yeeso am, on keɓay nokku mon e yiite ngol Baaba am heblani Seyɗaane e malaa’ikaaji mum.

Kono paamee wonde on ngonaa kaawis, alaa ko haawnii e mooɗon, sabu onon ko on nelaaɓe fenaaɓe, gollooɓe jammfa, mbaɗtooɓe hoore mon nelaaɓe Almasiihu. Kadi alaa ko haawnii, sabu Seyɗaane e hoore mum ina mbaɗtoo hoore mum malaa’ika annoore. Ko ɗum waɗi, wonaa huunde mawnde so gollooɓe makko kadi mbaɗtii hoore mum en gollooɓe nuunɗal, ɓe joofnirta ko e golle mum en. Sabu ko nii mi winndani Korint!

On njokkay golle mon, on kebbina luutti mon haa keewi, on kebbina abada oo e torra mon, sabu tikkere am ina heewi e mooɗon, on naatday e baabiraaɓe mon e yiite. Sabu onon ko on cukkuri e nder alkama, mi fukkitay on haa laaɓa; sabu Baaba am, Yahwe, wiyi on keɓataa hay ɗaɗol walla ndonu, sabu iwdi mon ko e bonɗo iwri, on fof ko on warooɓe e fenooɓe. Hol ko mbiy-mi baabiraaɓe mon to Yahuuda duuɓi keewɗi hannde? Onon ko on e baaba mon Seyɗaane iwri, ko yiɗde baaba mon mbaɗoton: ko o waroowo gila e fuɗɗoode, o daraaki e goonga, sabu alaa goonga e makko. So o fenii, ko e hoore makko o haalata: sabu ko o fenoowo, kanko woni baaba mum.

Anndee ɗum, onon fof jibinaaɓe e calagol, calagol Yahwe no baabiraaɓe mon ɓe ngalaa nafoore njiɗiri waylude sifa Alla, mo Aadamu tagiraa, wonti ko bonni ngam halkaare neɗɗaagu, balɗe mon timmiima. Hol e annabaaɓe ɓe baabiraaɓe mon torraaki? Ɓe mbari ɓeen kollitooɓe ardeede Nuunɗuɗo on; mo onon hande ngonɗon jammfa e warooɓe. On ndoggataa, hay so on naatii e nder leydi ngam suuɗaade tikkere am; tikkere am maa tawa on, sabu alaa ko suuɗotoo e yitere am, leydi ndii ko yam jeyi e ko heewi e mum, sabu mi soodirii ɗum ƴiiƴam am ɗam maamiraaɓe mon njuulnoo e calagol, ɗam tabbintini nokku mon e yiite. Miin, Almasiihu Iisa, jippiiɗo e teddinaaɗo, mi arii e mooɗon, on ndoggataa.

Nanaam, onon tippiiɓe, onon fof ɓoornuɓe am, sabu on mbiyi hoore mon, min foolii Yahwe, min mbari ɓiyum mo o neldi e amen ngam jogaade leydi, min poolii haa abada, sabu Yeesu maayii, on nsewi e bewre! Onon bewɓe ɓe ngalaa faamu! On paamaaki wonde Alla umminayno am, o woppi mette maayde: sabu waawaano mi jogaade e mum. On njuurnii tan teppere am, on ngalaa baawɗe e am, on paamaaki wonde toowɗo on, tagɗo on, Yahwe, holluno on tan ko sirluuji ɓe ngalaa nafoore!

Sabu o annduno on nduñantoo mo e tagoore makko, on njiɗayno heɓde ko o heblani yam, Joomiraawo Iisa Almasiihu. Joomiraaɓe am ñiɓɓuɓe maa ndona leydi, sabu leydi ndii ko yam jeyi e ko heewi e mum. Sabu on njannginii neɗɗaagu ganndal haɗaangal; on njannginii wolde; on njoɓini rewɓe e mbaydiiji gite ngam timminde yiɗde mon e maɓɓe; on mbonnii gese Alla e aawde cukkuri e nder alkama, kala cukuri mo aawɗon haa abada maa fukkitee, haɓɓee, werlee e yiite e mooɗon! Sabu on poolaama nde mi rokki hoore am ngam hisnude neɗɗaagu, Alla ummini am e maayɓe ngam mi maayataa kadi!

Kono on ngalaa hakkille haa on paama mi rokkayno hoore am ngam yiɗde Alla nde o tagi e am, sabu miin woni hisnoowo aduna oo! Yiɗde Yahwe waawaa foolde, sabu Yahwe waawaa foolde, alaa e tagoore fof tagɗo nannduɗo e makko, sabu ko kanko woni toowɗo, annoore makko e mawngu makko ina heewi doole gila e fuɗɗoode haa abada! Sabu mi wiyi on, “ɗum ɗo mbaɗata ɗo, mbaɗaa ɗum law!” On mbaɗii ɗum e bewre e mawnikinaare, on paamaaki mi accuno on nanngude yam e juuɗe gollooɓe mon ɓe ngalaa nafoore, ɓe mbaɗi golle bonɗe ɗe mbari ƴiiƴam laaɓɗam! E nder bewre mon, on paamaaki mi jogiino konuuji malaa’ikaaji sappo e ɗiɗi e yamiroore am, sabu kala saha mi waawno haɗde on. Kono no anniya Yahwe waɗirnoo so wonaa e yiɗde am e ɗooftaade Baaba am? Sabu yiɗde Yahwe alaa baawɗo foolde! Alaa, wonaa e kammu, wonaa e leydi, alaa e laawol gootol, alaa abada!

Onon tippiiɓe, onon laamɓe aduna tippiiɗo oo, onon Seyɗaaneeji, on fof on njoolay e yiite ngo ñifataa ngam konnaagu mon ngo alaa laaɓal e laabi mon bonɗi ɗi njuɓɓinaaka e bonannde. No mbaɗirɗon gooto e ɓeen tokosɓe, miñiraaɓe am, ko miin mbaɗirɗon. Onon jeertooɓe tippiiɓe, onon malaa’ikaaji njuɓɓinɓe ɓe ngoppi nokku maɓɓe gadano, on ngalaa jam! On njoolay e yiite ngo on ngoppataa, on njoolay haa laaɓa. Kala cukkuri mon bonɗi ɗi aawɗon e feewnude keeri Yahwe e nder mbayliigu mon hakkunde neɗɗo e malaa’ika, hakkunde neɗɗo e dabba, hakkunde neɗɗo e tagooje maayo, hakkunde dabbaaji ceertuɗi, kala huunde bonnde nde mbaɗɗon, maa werlee e yiite e mooɗon, sabu on ngalaa jam, on ngalaa yaafuya!

On ndewii Seyɗaaneeji ɗi njiɗnoo halkude tagoore Yahwe, haa e warde am, Iisa Almasiihu, nde mi rokki hoore am e juuɗe makko no Paska laaɓɗo ngam aduna oo, mi tabbintini hisnugol neɗɗaagu, mi tabbintini nokku mon e yiite mawngo ngo ñifataa! On njoɓay luutti mon fof e bonanndeeji mon, kala golle mon bonɗe maa ngartiree on laabi teemedere, sabu kala neɗɗo maa ñaawee no golle mum njiirri. Laabi mon bonɗi fof maa ngartiree e ko’e mon laabi keewɗi, sabu on miijinoo on mbaaway foolde am, on njokki e miijaade on mbaaway foolde so on poolaama.

Nanaaɗon konngol am, aan mboddi mawndi, sabu ñaawoore maa arii, mi yiyii kulol maa, sabu aɗa huli ko arata ko ɓuri kala huunde, aan mbaroodi ngo alaa ñiiƴe! Joftoore ko yam jeyi, mi joftoto, waktu mon arii. Tikkere Alla e tikkere Joomiraawo ina ardi, mi udditay maande jeeɗiɗi, malaa’ikaaji jeeɗiɗi neldee ngam wuurde buubaaji ñaawoore, malaa’ikaaji jeeɗiɗi neldee jogaaɗi ñaawooje jeeɗiɗi cakkitiije, ɗe njaltinee, sabu e majje tikkere Alla heewi. Caɗeele keewɗe maa keɓta on fof, so mi halkii laamu mon. Laamɗo mon mawɗo mi foolay, annabi mon fenaaɗo mi foolay, ɓe njooloyee e wuurde e maayo yiite ngo wulirta kaayeefi; ɗoon ɓe ndaaran on naatde e maɓɓe, ɗoon on njooloyee e yiite e kaayeefi, ñaawoore mon fuɗɗiima!

Jom suudu am, woto hule! Konnguɗi am ñaawoore ngonaa ngam mon, kono ngam wellitaare mon, sabu mi hisnii on e tikkere ardoonde, mi suuɗay on e kisal e njoɓdi mawndi, on ngonnday e am haa abada. Sabu mi suɓiima on, mi noddiima on, onon ko on jawdi am ɓurndi teddude, onon ko on jawdi Yahwe ɓurndi teddude; onon ko on perle tedduɗe, sabu mi rokki on fof. Mi wasiyii Jom suudu am yo ɗaɓɓu faamu, yo ar e am ngam heɓde hakkille, sabu mi jannginay on. Sabu yoga e mooɗon ndewii yoga e laabi annabaaɓe fenaaɓe ɓee; ngartee tan e tuubugol e Joomiraawo mon, mi jaɓɓotoo on.

Sabu ɓe njiɗnoo on e diine Almasiihu fenaaɗo, on ngoonɗinii fenaande keewnde nde yeeyanoo on, nde mbiyanoo on ko goonga, kono ko fenaande bonnde. Ɓulli ɗi ngalaa ndiyam ɗii njiɗnoo seerndude on e am, ɗum waawaa waɗde, woto hulee, sabu mi salotoo! Mi jokkay rimɗinde on haa on rimɗa goonga, mi deƴƴataa! Mi haɓanay on e laabi keewɗi ɗi on anndaa. Sabu on fof on maantinanoo hade mon jibineede, sabu Baaba oo annduno on gila e nder reedu yumma mon, o reeni kala e mooɗon waktu mo on mbaawataa faamde, on ngonnduno e nder ɓernde makko. On maantinanoo gila e jibinannde, malaa’ikaaji am ndeenii on e yamiroore am, ɓe ndeenii on, ɓe ndeenii on, sabu ko nii ɓe nelaa. Ɗum ina haawnii on? On njaɓay ko Iburanko’en mbiynoo? Kono hol e malaa’ikaaji ɓe o wiyi e sahaa gooto, jooɗo e nyaamo am, haa mi waɗa gaño maa ko ɓuuɓri koyɗe maa? Wonaa ɓe fof ko ruuhuuji gollotooɗi, neldaaɗi ngam gollanaade ɓeen ɓe ndonata hisnugol?

Jom suudu am, onon ko on ndonooɓe hisnugol e am, Iisa Almasiihu. On noddaama e am; on ndeenama e am, e juuɗe am; on keɓii fartaŋŋe nande waaju goonɗinal jowitiingu e am ngam mbaɗam Joomiraawo mon, ko ɗum waɗi mi maantinii on e ruuhu laaɓɗo alkawal. Anndee maande ndee waawaa feewnude, on ardiima e hisnude timmunde haa abada, sabu on anndaa timmal golle tago ɗe ngardii-mi timminde e mooɗon, Jom suudu am! Anndee tippiiɓe ɓee e cukkuri maɓɓe ina njiɗi on, ɓe njiɗaa kala ko Yahwe yiɗi, mi joftotoo on e ko’e maɓɓe, sabu ko miin woni barɗo hoore mboddi mawndi ndii, sabu ɗum wiyanooma, ko Yahwe wiyi waawaa wayleede.

Alaa baawɗo seerndude on e am; alaa bonannde baawnde feewnude maande ndee; onon ko on am, onon ko on Baaba, sabu kala ko Baaba rokki am, mi artiranay Alla ngam en fof ngonnda gootum! O rokki am kala huunde e kammu e leydi, so wonaa e jappeere makko o ɓuri yam mawnude!

Miin, Almasiihu Iisa, mi haalanii on konnguɗi ɗii, ko miin, Iisa Almasiihu, ñaawoowo gañooji mon, mi timminay ɗum haa ko ɓuri famɗude. Jom suudu am, yiɗde am e mooɗon waawaa ñifde, mi heblanii on nokku, sabu e galle Baaba am ina woodi cuuɗi keewɗi! Hono non, ɓooyataa on resndete e am haa abada, on ngonnday e am, on paamay mawngu anniya mon e am mo tagaaɗon ngam timminde!

Sabu e am, Yahwe rokki on ko arata e anniya, anniya duuɓi ina ardi e feeñde e mooɗon, sabu mi arii! Mi salotoo. Ndaaree feeñere am. Ndaaree annoore ardeede am, sabu ɓooyataa nde feeñay e dow leydi so mi feeñii e duule e wullaango mawngo, e daande malaa’ika mawɗo, e buuba Alla, sabu mi haalanii on goonga wonde hay malaa’ikaaji ina ndaara buuba ngo ardi e wuurde, ɓe nheblaniima! Heblooɗee yam, ndaaree, njokkee, no mbaɗirnoo; sabu ɓooyataa on njiyay am e teddungal am ngal Baaba rokkii am, sabu ko nii mi rokkirta on, sabu on nannday e am, on njiyay am no mi wirri.

Miin woni Joomiraawo Alla!

Mi arii ngam ñaawoyde laamu Seyɗaane fof, ɓe ndoggataa.

Miin woni hisnoowo aduna oo!

Miin woni Almasiihu Iisa, joftoore ko yam jeyi, mi joftoto!

Miin woni barɗo hoore mboddi, sabu Alla Jom doole fof wiyi ɗum! Mi ardiima e feeñde e duule ngam Jom suudu am, mo mi sottinta.

Miin woni garoowo e puccu boɗeewu.

Miin woni noddiraaɗo Goongaajo e Nuunɗuɗo, e nuunɗal mi ñaawoyta, mi haɓa.

Mi arii!
