Tilimɓe 144,000 e Seniiɓe Nguurndam Muusɗam e Jiiɓru Yaquuba

E tilimɓe 144,000 e seniiɓe nguurndam muusɗam e jiiɓru Yaquuba: Ndaaree! Miɗo haalan on geɗe keewɗe hade majje waɗde, ngam neɗɗo hakkilante e mon, nanoowo daande am, ɓeydoo hakkilantaagal, tee on annda goonga konnguɗi am. Sabu Seyɗaani maa eto suuɗtude yahdu gooŋɗinɓe e Eklesiya am (sabu mi nanngatɓe e yahdu maɓɓe gila e leydi haa e dow kammuuli) e hare e feeñninde fenaande hono tageefooji guurɗi feere ɗi ngonaa goɗɗum so wonaa malaa'ika'en rammuɓe, feeñninanɓe hoore mum'en e leydi e nder bonanndeeji jokkondirɗi, ɗi fenaandeeji maɓɓe taarii. Sabu kamɓe koɓe fenaande.

Ɓe fenaama e ko wiyetee winndere ndee; sabu alaa winndere yaajotoonde e mbaadi mbuurnam, kono dow leydi ko kammu kammuuli, hay kammu (kammu toowɗo oo) ɗo Yahweh, Alla Toowɗo oo, jooɗii e jooɗorgal mum, kono o waɗat hoɗorde makko e yimɓe. Kadi omo woni kala nokku, omo woni kala huunde e nder Ɓanndu Almasiihu. E leydi ndii hoɗi perel makko teddungel, hay worɓe suɓaaɓe gila e duuɓi duuɓi e nder ɓernde Yahweh e hoore mum. Nii woni, leydi ndii e ko heewi e mum fof ko jeyaa e Joomiraawo; sabu miin, Iisaa Nasaraat, mi soodirii leydi ndii ƴiiƴam am ki sottinirteeɗam.

Anndee tigi-rigi taggol aduna mon (janngon haa anndon) e ngooŋɗinon siftorde e konnguɗi e nder Deftere Sennde jowitiiɗe e tagngo ngoo, sabu ko no Yahweh siforii e Deftere Sennde nii, tee Yahweh waawaa fende. So mi haalanii on geɗe leydi, on ngooŋɗinaani, hol no ngooŋɗinirton so mi haalanii on geɗe kammuuli? Alaa, alaa tageefooji guurɗi feere no fenaandeeji maɓɓe ciforii nii, kono ko malaa'ika'en rammuɓe, yimɓe murɓe, hay Seyɗaani e laamu mum, ɓe feni, ɓe njiilanii leydi ndii haa kala neɗɗo welɗo e bonannde halka.

Ɓe mbaɗii daari fenaande keewɗe ɗe ɓe peewniri, ɓe njiimi heen banngeeji ɗiɗi fof gila e puɗɗoode haa ɓe njaltini peeje maɓɓe. Sabu kamɓe ɓe njogii ko ɓe njiɗi, ɓe njogii ko luutndii ɗum, ɓe njaɓɓondiri golle maɓɓe bonɗe seeɗa seeɗa, tee fenaande maɓɓe ina mawnude! Kono annoora Deftere Sennde ina feeñnina fitinaaji ɗii, kono neɗɗo ina sala ballal Alla haa ñaawoore waɗa, musiiba yani. Ndeen neɗɗaagal ina yiɗi tuubde nde laaɓi, caggal nde ñaawoore waɗaa, tee worɓe wuurdude e ndeen mbaadi mbaawaa daɗde e ñaawoore mum'en. Sabu kala ko Yahweh holliti, hay gooto waawaa waylude ɗum!

Heewɓe maa njano e majjere garoore, ndi ummortoo e wolde winndere e musiibaaji jogorɗi arde e leydi ndii. Sabu kamɓe ɓe feeñninat hoore mum'en e neɗɗaagal no tageefooji moƴƴi ummoriiɓe e adunaaji ɓamtaare ngam renndude ganndal maɓɓe moƴƴal. Kono ko ganndal moƴƴere e bonannde tan woni ko ɓe mbaɗi ngam heɓde haajuuji maɓɓe. Ɓe njogoto karallaagal toowngal, ɓe nanndat e ɓamtaare mawnde, ɓurnde neɗɗo tagɗo. Kono ɓamtaare maɓɓe tan ko e bonannde, tee ɓe ngonaa leydiyankooɓe, kono ko Yahweh tagi ɓe gila e puɗɗoode tagngo e kammuuli. Ɓe ngonnoo ɗoon nde Yahweh tagi leydi ndii. Baaba oo e ganndal mum ko adii, o annduno ɓe njanfoto, o suuɗi sirru ɓernde mum teddunde e worɓe ɓe o anndi maa ngaddan tagngo mum bonannde bonnde.

Miɗo jokki noddude neɗɗo tago ngam nuunɗal, kono neɗɗo tago ina ɓuri yiɗde ñawu, bakkaat e niɓe; ko ɗum waɗi mi rokkata neɗɗaagal ko o yiɗi sanne. Nde tawnoo neɗɗo tago ina golla bonannde, ina yiɗi niɓe, mi rokkata ɗum niɓe ɗe ngalaa keeri.

Sabu hannde, neɗɗo tago ina darii e mawnikinaare e nder yurmeende am, ina huutoroo yurmeende ngam waɗde bakkaat dow bakkaat, wonaa jaɓde nanondiral jam Yahweh, hay miin, Yesuwa Almasiihu, rokkuɗo ƴiiƴam am ɗam alaa bakkaat, e nder ɗooftaare Yahweh, ngam yoɓde bakkaatuuji neɗɗaagal fof haa abadan. Woto on njiiloo: ko miin woni Almasiihu goonga, tee kala goɗɗo fof ko mbaydiiji bardi ɗi ngalaa nafoore.

Seyɗaani murti ko juuti caggal puɗɗoode tagngo ngoo, o bonni tagngo Baaba oo e mbaadi hersa, bonnde, ngayngu e mbonniindi; kono ɗum waɗiraa ko e mbaydi mawndi e maale fenaande e kaawniiɗe, hay karallaagal ɓamtaare ngo hulɓinii gite. Kadi ɓe njiɗi dewal e ɗooftaare tiiɗnde e laabi maɓɓe, kono laabi maɓɓe ina addana neɗɗaagal maayde e bonannde, sabu ko noon ɓe mbaɗiraa.

Alaa ko hesɗi e les naange, sukaaɓe am. Ko wiyetee allaaji daartol ɓooyngol e iwdi maɓɓe bonndi ndi wiyetee allaaji feccere maa ngartu, tee no ñalɗi Nuuhu nii, nii wonirta. Sabu ɓe rimɗinaama e jolɗe maɓɓe e torra maɓɓe haa ɓe keɓa ñaawoore maɓɓe timmunde, tee mi yoɓtotoo, wiyi Joomiraawo Iisaa Almasiihu, tee Yahweh maa yoɓtitoo ɓe haa timma.

Seyɗaani e rammuɓe mum njaɓaani sujjande laamu Yahweh, kono ɓe etii wujjude leydi e neɗɗaagal e fenaande mawnde. Seyɗaani hay etii wujjude ndonu am e Baaba Annoora! Kono hay gooto waawaa heɓtude hay gooto e mon e juuɗe am, tee hay gooto waawaa wujjude ndonu am; sabu ko Yahweh e hoore mum rokki ɗum, tee kala neɗɗo salii sujjande am, Iisaa Almasiihu, ina murti e laamu Yahweh, ina naata e rammuɓe haa abadan.

Ko ɗum waɗi mi haalan on, woto on ngooŋɗin fenaandeeji Copernicus, wonnooɗo Yesuwit e Freemason, golloowo Seyɗaani. O janngini tan konnguɗi annduɓe filosofi Gerek paganeeɓe ɓooyɓe. Nii woni, taggol aduna mon jaɓaangol ko dewooɓe naange e paganeeɓe mbaɗi ɗum e dow fenaandeeji Seyɗaani, hay Yesuwit'en laaɓtuɓe bonninɓe njuɓɓudiiji jaŋde mon e jaŋde luulndiinde jaŋde, e anniya timmuɗo ngam winndere maɓɓe fenaande ndee winndee e hakkillaaji yimɓe e fenaandeeji gonɗi e jaŋde. Kono noon, janngingol ngol alaa hakkille waɗii, tee aduna no woniri tigi-rigi nii, worɓe hoɗɓe e leydi ndii paamataa ɗum.

Jooni dingiral ngal ina heɓii majjere tiiɗnde sanne, tee fenaandeeji ɗii mahaama, sabu paɗe mum ko bonanndeeji e fenaande mawnde. Ɗeen fenaandeeji ina njogii ɗaɗi e nder jaŋde mon Yesuwit. Sabu aduna oo fof ina majji e fenaandeeji mum'en; hay suɓaaɓe ɓee maa majjine so tawii ina waawi. Nii woni, fellere ndee ina heɓii fiyde, tee huunde tan rewnude nde ko gardiiɗo oo, mo ittetee e yaawre e ɓooyaani. Ndeen hare mawnde maa yani e leydi ndii nde gooŋɗinɓe am mooɓondirata e am e kammuuli. Ndeen e nder ɗoondi ndii, dabba e annabi fenaande maa ummoo, ɓe ngona hakkunde fenaande garoore ndee.

Ko ɗum waɗi woto on ngooŋɗin fenaandeeji maɓɓe, semmbinɓe am, sabu on ko on yimɓe keeriiɗi, mi noddi on, mi maantiniri on maande Alla Guurɗo oo, ngam tikkere Alla woto mem on. Mi neldoyat on ko on semmbinɓe am. On ngonat daaɗe am keewɗe, noddooje e ladde kala ɗo neldaɗon, tee miɗo nelda on ngam mooɓtude baali am majjuɗi keddiiɗi ɗii.

Sabu mooɓere mawnde maa yaltu e jiiɓru Yaquuba, on ngollat golle mawɗe, on njamirot ko waawaa waɗeede, sabu mi rokkata maale mawɗe keewɗe, kaawniiɗe e kaawniiɗe ngam waɗeede e juuɗe mon. On ngollat kaawniiɗe mawɗe ɓooyɗe, binndaaɗe e Deftere Sennde, hay e doole Eliya, so wonaa golle ɓurɗe mawnude e doole ɗe ngolloton ɓurɗe ko binndaa e makko gila e jamaanu.

Sabu Deftere Sennde wiyi kala neɗɗo goonɗinɗo am, Yesuwa Almasiihu, wonaa tan golle ɗe ngollunoomi e leydi e nder golle am to Yahuuda, hono gorobba Alla, kono golle ɓurɗe mawnude e ɗe ngollunoomi ɗee, on ngollat, sabu mi rokkata maale mawɗe, kaawniiɗe e kaawniiɗe e juuɗe mon nde njamiroton ko waawaa waɗeede e goonɗinal e am. Nii woni, ndaaree am sahaa fof. Kadi njogitee gite mon e am, sabu mi ardotò on seeɗa seeɗa. Yahdu mon wonta yahdu hakkilantaagal, on njogoto ɗemngal hakkilanteeɓe, tee mi wonan on semmbe e kisal, sabu e am, Almasiihu Iisaa, ko toon timmal fof hoɗi.

Nii woni, miɗo wasiyoo on onon fof, ɓooyaani sanne kaalis wonta alaa nafoore, tee kaalis gooto jaɓaado oo maa ɗaɓɓu maande ngam heɓde jawdi aduna. Woto on njaɓ maande ndee, sabu maayde e nguurndam ɗam (yiɗaa nguurndam mon haa laakara) ina ɓuri nafoore e jaɓde maande ndee e werleede e maayo yiite duumotoongo, ngam Seyɗaani e malaa'ika'en mum, sabu waktuuji maɓɓe njettiima; woto on naatde e maɓɓe tan. Sabu ɓe njananat alkule mawɗe tedduɗe, kono konnguɗi maɓɓe ko fenaande, tooke ina ngoni e konnguɗi maɓɓe, tee alkule maɓɓe ko fenaande, keewɗe geɗe laaɓtuɗe fof. So tawii ɓe mbaawaa timminde alkule maɓɓe, sabu ñaawoore maɓɓe ina ara; ɓe ngalaa jam, tee jam maa itte e leydi ndii haa laaɓa.

Bonannde neɗɗaagal maa won duunde; tee heewɓe, so gardiiɗo oo ittaama, maa ndokku hoore mum'en haa abadan e majjere e bonannde. So wonaa neɗɗo wuurdude e kaafaahi maa wara e kaafaahi, tee maa ɓe njanu e nano e nyaamo e limoore mawnde, kono ɗum arataa e mon nde njahaton e am; woto on njiɗ nguurndam mon haa laakara, njoftoɗon yeru am e golle e sadak ngal alaa neɗɗo. Sabu njeenaari mawndi maa wona mon ngam ɗooftaare e gooŋɗinal.

Nii woni, yiɗde neɗɗaagal e bonannde maa won duunde, tee Seyɗaani e malaa'ika'en mum maa won duunde, tee kala bonanndeeji maɓɓe maa won duunde, tee ɓe ngarat no wallooɓe ɓooyɓe nii. Fenaande maɓɓe ina luggi, ina jogii ɗaɗi luggiɗi, ɗi ɓooyaani maa ƴette haa laaɓa. Ɓe ngonaa allaaji, ɓe ngonaa kadi worɓe ɓamtaare moƴƴi, kono ko fenooji, tee ɓe njuɓɓini hoore mum'en e laabi kala. Nii woni, ɓe ngonat duunde, tee timmal laamu Seyɗaani maa yaltine e luggere ndee, sabu maa nde uddite.

Ɗum waɗirtee ko ngam hollude neɗɗo tago ko o suɓii e rokkude fartaŋŋe cakkitiiɗo ngam tuubde; sabu bonannde suuɗataako, rewnataako kadi, tee kala sirru maa yaltu e annoora. Hay fenaandeeji fenaande maɓɓe mawnde e majjere tiiɗnde maa peeñne ngam kala neɗɗo yiyde, sabu Yahweh hollitii ɗum e Deftere Sennde, tee ko Yahweh holliti, hay gooto waawaa waylude ɗum.

Nii woni, feccere cakkitere maa waɗu, tee heewɓe e worɓe welɓe ɓe njiɗmi ɓee maa accane e jarribooji e jarribooji garooji, haa ɓe hisna. Sabu alaa waktu, tee yurmeende am e yurmeende am ndokketee kadi e mbaadi wootiri, tee alaa waktu ngam laabi welɗi. Kala neɗɗo maa suɓoo; tee kulooɓe e ngooŋɗinɓe ɓee maa cuɓoo Seyɗaani e laamu mum nde mbaawnoo suɓaade am, hay Iisaa Almasiihu, ɓiy Alla garɗo e ɓanndu, rokkuɗo hoore am hono Paasika timmuɗo ngam bakkaatuuji neɗɗaagal fof haa abadan. Heewɓe maa njokku e salaade junngo am ballal e waktu haaju mawɗo, sabu ballal am ko ballal duumotoongal, teddungal, yurmeende, yurmeende e yiɗde.

Kono, ko ɓuri heewde e aduna oo maa ngooŋɗin fenaandeeji ɗii, ɓe njaɓɓondiri e dabba oo, ɓe mbiyi, holi nanndi e dabba oo? Ɓe njaɓat e hoolaare ñawsude e ñawsugol mboɗeeri, ndi tooke mum mbaawaa safreede, sabu ko duumotoonde. Neɗɗo suɓaade bonannde e waktuuji suɓngo wonta suɓngo duumotoongo, tee worɓe waɗooɓe ndeen suɓngo maa ndarii e ñaawoore mawnde.

Worɓe suɓotooɓe bonannde e hoolaare maa ndokku konngol ngam kala miijo bonngo, kala konngol bonngol e kala golle bonɗe ɗe ɓe mbaɗi e leydi, sabu defte ɗee maa uddite, alaa yurmeende. Tee hay gooto waawaa daraade e nuunɗal mum, sabu neɗɗo alaa nuunɗal mum; sabu mboɗeeri ndii bonnii ɓe gila e puɗɗoode, tee ñalɗi bonannde mum ina njotti haa laaɓa. Ko ɗum waɗi Yahweh neldi am, ɓiy mum gooto, hono nanondiral jam e neɗɗo tago, tee neɗɗo tago saliima jam duumotooɗo sabu neɗɗo tago ina yiɗi bonannde.

Ñaawoore mawnde ina jogori yande e laamu Seyɗaani. Ndee ñaawoore maa ɓeydoo bonde, tee kewuuji baɗaaɗi e ñaawoore maa ɓeydoo bonde. Bone maa ɓeydee e bone, torra maa ɓeydee e torra ngam kala neɗɗo saliiɗo tuubde e golle mum bonɗe. Sabu heewɓe tuubataa e golle juuɗe mum'en, haa ɓe ndewataa seyɗaneeji e sanamuuji kaŋŋe e kaalis e mbuuɗu e kaaƴe e leɗɗe: ɗi mbaawaa yiyde, mbaawaa nande, mbaawaa yahde: Kadi ɓe tuubataa e warngooji maɓɓe, walla e kuuɗe maɓɓe, walla e jeenade maɓɓe, walla e wuyɓe maɓɓe.

Sabu ɓe nani ɓen wonaa laamɓe, kono ko fenooɓe pewuɗi, ñemmbintooɓe, fijoowooji, e majjinooɓe. Ɓe ngaddataa so wonaa maayde e halkere e rewɓe ɓe, e kala mo majjinaa fof, maa o heɓ mettittoode mawnde, sabu tama'uuji maɓɓe maa laatoo comci e nder ndiyam yiite mawɗam ɗam alaa yaakaare. E miin, Almasiihu Iisaa, mi bonniran laamu Seyɗaani ngun e ñaawoore mawnde sanne, sabu ñaawoore mawnde maa hisna e maɓɓe fof, tee miin woni Muusu Kabiila Yahuuda.

Nii woni kala maande maa ɓeydu ñaawoore dow ñaawoore, e kala buutaan maa ɓeydu ñaawoore dow ñaawoore, kala bone maa ɓeydu bone dow bone, e kala loonde maa arda e tikkere Alla timmunde, hay tikkere dow tikkere. Laamu Seyɗaani maa tampu sanne, e laamu makko maa halkire haa laaɓa, maa bonnire haa laaɓa.

Sabu ɓe mooɓondiran, ɓe ndarnoo ngam haɓde e garol Joomiraawo, sabu miɗo ara tigi-rigi. Gila e fuɗɗoode tampere Yaaquuba, maa wood lebbi jeeɗiɗi duuɓi, sabu yontere cappanɗe jeeɗiɗaɓere Daaniyel maa timminire ngam halkude bonannde e ngam hisnude Israa'iila. Sabu mi laɓɓinan Israa'iila e bone nannguɗo leydi ndin.

Sabu wonaa Yahweh, wonaa kadi miin e hoore am, Yeshua HaMashiach, neli walla toɗɗi ardiiɓe maɓɓe, sabu ɓe laamoto e bonannde, e warhoore maɓɓe ko huunde soɓunde, tee yimɓe am ina njoɓa coggu hareeji maɓɓe. Kono heewɓe e Eklesiya am ina ngoongɗini fenaande maɓɓe Siyonist en, ngam noon, paamee wonde mi wonaa Siyonist, sabu ko ɓe mawɓe yahuud en, tee ko ɓe gawƴi, aawdi Seyɗaani tigi-rigi; tee mi ittiran ɓe e leydi ndin, sabu ɓe njogii ndonu am, tee miɗo ara ngam heɓtude ko woni ko am. Sabu miin, Yeshua HaMashiach, hay miin, miɗo jogii seedanfaagu leydi; sabu mi soodiri ɗum e ƴiiƴam am laaɓɗam, tee miɗo jogii coktirɗe maayde e Jahannama. Tee mi yoɓii bakkaatuuji neɗɗaŋke, e kala nodduɗo innde Joomiraawo maa hise.

Nii woni, leydi ko yam e ko heewi e mayri, tee mi laɓɓinan leydi ndin e bonannde maɓɓe, e juulirde Seyɗaani maa halkire haa laaɓa, e nii mi addan alkawal kesal e leydi, mi laamoro e nuunɗal. Sabu no leydi ndin ɓurirta niɓɓude e bonde e kala leƴƴannde, nii garol am ɗiɗaɓol ɓurirta ɓadtaade e kala leƴƴannde.

Njogoree yaakaare mawnde e garol am; sabu mi aray, mi yaltay e kammuuli e konuuli kammu, hay konu ngal alaa yeru, sabu konu hono ngal am ngal meeɗaa woodde, konu ngu foolataa, ngo yimɓe wuurooɓe nguurndam duumotooɗam, ɓe mbaawaa wareede walla foolde. Sabu mi heɓii baawɗe e juuɗe Yahweh ngam haalande hono ndee, tee mi semmbinay, mi njoɓtan ɓe sabu dewal maɓɓe e yiɗde maɓɓe e am e innde am, hay Iisaa Almasiihu! Sabu kala yiɗɗo, rewoowo Yeshua HaMashiach ina yiɗi, ina rewa kadi Yahweh, sabu alaa ko ɓuri Alla mawɗo duumotooɗo Israa'iila! Kala yedduɗo Ɓiɗɗo on, o alaa Baaba; tee miin woni Iisaa Almasiihu, jibinaaɗo gooto e Baaba, rokkuɗo hoore mum ngam bakkaatuuji yimɓe fof, tee miin woni Limaam mawɗo haa abada e mbaadi Melkisedek.

E mi foolan tigi-rigi mboddi ndin, daaba ka, e annabiijo fenaannde on. Sabu daaba ka e annabiijo fenaannde on maa njuute e wuurde e nder ndiyam yiite nguleeki ɓurngo welde e bulooji ɗi mbaawaa ñifde, ɗo fowtere hay leƴƴannde wootere alaa. Kono golle maɓɓe bonɗe fof maa nduttane ɓe haa abada. Tampere nde ɓe ngaddunoo fof maa yoɓtore haa abada, sabu Yahweh maa yoɓtu kala golle bonɗe haa e dinngere sakkitiinde e nder ɓerɗe maɓɓe bonɗe, tee ɓe njoɓan haa abada bonanndeeji maɓɓe.

E nii, Seyɗaani maa haɓɓe e nder luggeendam duuɓi ujunere, sabu ko nii Yahweh feññini. Miin, Yeshua HaMashiach, mi laamoran leydi ndin e nuunɗal ñalawma gooto. Sabu to Yahweh ñalawma gooto ko hono duuɓi ujunere, e duuɓi ujunere ko hono ñalawma gooto. Tee mi laamoran e jooɗorgal Daawuuda, e nder Israa'iila laamu am wonata ngam timminde alkule ɗe Yahweh waɗani Baabiraaɓe ɓen, tee ñalawma am ina foti arde.

Kono adan, ñaawoore foti arde e leydi, e leydi ndin maa laɓɓinire kala huunde bonnde. Sabu alaa huunde bonnde naatata e laamu am, ɗo jam e kisal wonata hono no Yahweh e hoore mum alkuli. E muusu maa fukkoro e mbalu. Miin, hay miin, Yeshua HaMashiach, miɗo haala konnguɗi ɗin hade majji waɗde, mbele anndon wonde Alla Israa'iila arii ngam mooɓtude baali mum majjuɗi e galle Israa'iila haa e am, Gaynaako Mawɗo on, tee mi fiyan leyɗeele ɗen e sawru njamndi, tee mi lelnan tigi-rigi tagu Baaba e nuunɗal e laaɓal goonga. Wakkati timmii.

Miin woni Yeshua HaMashiach, Almasiihu goongaajo yimɓe am Israa'iila.

Miin woni on laamotooɗo leydi ndin e nuunɗal gila e jooɗorgal Daawuuda.

Miin woni on feeñatnooɗo e duule e konuuli kammu, sabu mi ardoto ɗi.

Miin woni on mooɓtoowo heddiiɓe am hono jamaa mawɗo gila e fitinaaji garooji ɗin.

Miin woni on jarriboowo ɓerɗe yimɓe, tee mi seertinan baali e be'i.

Miin woni Joomiraawo weeyo, e wakkatiiji weeyo njottii.

Miin woni Joomiraawo Joomiraaɓe e Laamɗo Laamɓe, e holi waawɗo daranaade?

Ndaaree, ñalawma Joomiraawo ina foti fuɗde e leydi ndin e musiiba e ñaawoore. Sabu ñalawma mawɗo tikkere makko arii, e holi waawɗo daranaade?
