Seedeeji ɗiɗo: Israa’iila
Ko Welɗi e ko Haɗɗi
5 Sulet, 2024

Haala Sirri nda Tati!

Heɗee daande Yeesu Almasiihu, yiɗɓe am, ngaree e am ngam heɓde deeƴre e fowtere e nder giɗli am e mon, onon cukalel am. Sabu on kooliima e geɗe seeɗa, jooni mi waɗan on laamiiɓe e dow geɗe keewɗe nde naatoton e weltaare Joomiraaɗo mon. Ñaawoore wonaa ngam mon, onon kooliiɓe am; hay maayɓe fof e nder Almasiihu e fof ɓe ɗaaniiɓe e nder Almasiihu, ɓe umminoyte e nguurndam kesam e nder ɗanle gooto, e nder leppaare yitere. Sabu goonga, mi jippoto ummaade e dow kammu e ewnagol, e daande Malaa'ika mawɗo, e buutngol Allah: maayɓe e nder Almasiihu ummoto e nguurndam ngu walaa bonannde, onon wuurnooɓe heddiiɓe ɓee, on nanngete wondude e maɓɓe e nder duule, ko noon nganndunirton haa abada wondude e Joomiraaɗo mon duumiiɗo. On mbaɗtirteete ɗoon e ɗoon ummaade e ngonka mon bonka haa e tagoore laaɓtunde timmunde, waɗiraande kesum no neɗɗo gooto keso nii, no mi woniri nii, ko noon on ngonirta; no winndiraa nii, ko noon waɗirtee e jaawngal.

Hol ko waɗi ɓerɗe mon ina njirrondira, onon sukaaɓe am? Hol ko waɗi konnguɗi tuumoowo oo ina njirrondira on haa miijitoɗon wonde alaa nanngugol, walla on potaani e yurmeende am hisinoore? Miɗo wasiyoo on, onon sukaaɓe am, yo on jokku yahde e goongɗingol, wonaa e yiitere; ko miin waɗi on fotɓe, hay fof e nder Almasiihu, jogiiɓe jokkondiral hisinngal e Joomiraaɗo mum en nuunɗuɗo, woto on cikku! Hay so tawii on njiyaani mi e gite mon ɓanndu, on nganndi no feewi wonde ko miin woni Joomiraaɗo mon wuurnooɗo e hisinoowo, e nder am ko nguurndam timmuɗam e hisnugol. Goonga, goonga, goongɗinɗo mi oo meeɗataa rafi walla meeɗataa ɗomɗude kadi.

Sabu ko miin woni mburu nguurndam, kala ñamɗo meeɗataa rafi kadi; ko miin kadi ilnata maaje ndiyam guurɗam e nder heewde e kesam-hesaagu nguurndam, kala yardo heen meeɗataa ɗomɗude kadi. Kono on fof on nguurinte e cellal timmungal, e jam timmuɗo, e dental timmungal e Yahuwah, e Joomiraaɗo mon wuurnooɗo e hisinoowo, sabu ko miin woni Almasiihu Yeesu, soodunooɗo on e ƴiiƴam am laɓɓinoojam, on njogaaki coggu goonga. Ko noon yoɓre nde alaa coggu ndee yoɓiraa, nde mi rokki hoore am haa abada hono Jawgel Allah, mi holliti sadaka am e dow mon no hooreejo liman'en haa abada e mbaadi Melkisedek, mi yoɓi njoɓdi mon e ƴiiƴam am ɗam alaa ella.

Ko noon woniri haa abada, wonaa haaje kadi e sadaka ngam bakkaat, sabu so ƴiiƴam ga'i e mbordi, e ndoondi nagge, ina loote ɓe tuunɗuɓe, ina laɓɓina ko fayti e laɓɓingol ɓanndu, haa teeŋti noon e ƴiiƴam Almasiihu, mo e nder Ruuhu duumiiɗo rokki hoore mum tawa alaa ella e Allah, ina laɓɓina hakkillaaji mon e golle maayɗe, ngam dewon Allah guurɗo oo? Ko sadakaaji daabaaji mbaawaani timminde, ƴiiƴam am laɓɓinoojam mbaawii timminde ɗum laawol gootol haa abada, no mi laɓɓiniri bakkaatuuji neɗɗaagu laawol gootol haa abada nii, hay ngam fof suɓiiɓe jaɓde hisnugol e innde am, sabu alaa innde woɗnde rokkaande les kammu nde yimɓe mbaawi hisde. Woto on ngoongɗin fenannde maɓɓe, sabu liingu mawngu, daaba ladde, e annabiijo fenaannde ummoto, ɓe ndartoo hawrude e diineeji aduna fof ɗi ngonaa goonga ɗii e les caltal kesal, so wonaa Yeesu Almasiihu jaɓetaake e maɓɓe, wonaa e innde, wonaa e golle, rokkaama ɓe haɓde e seniiɓe e foolde ɓe. Goonga, goonga, mi alaa heen geɗal, ko miin halkata ɓe gila e les, sabu e nuunɗal mi arata ngam ñaaawde e haɓde.

Jooni miɗo haalda e ɓeen garooɓe e am e sahaa caɗeele Yakuuba, hay seniiɓe sahaa ñalɗi bonɗi ɓeen, ɓe keɓata njeenaari mawndi e ndonu duumiingu e nder Joomiraaɗo mum en duumiiɗo e Almasiihu, hono Yeesu Almasiihu. Ko noon winndiraa no goonga duumiiɗo nii, ko noon mi haalirta haa abada, yo on naat e damal famɗungal: sabu ko damal yaajngal, laawol yaajngol ngol yahdata e halkere: heewɓe naatata heen, sabu damal ngal ina famɗi, laawol ngol ina famɗi yahde e nguurndam, seeɗaɓe tawi ngol. Miɗo yamira on, onon sukaaɓe am, yo on keddo e laawol toowngol famɗungol, nduttoɗon halkere majjuɓe, hay ɓeen ɓe inɗe mum en meeɗaani winndeede e Deftere Nguurndam gila e sosngo aduna, sabu ɓe njiɗi laabi halkere, ɓe njiɗi bonannde, ɓe cuɓii majjere mawnde e dow nuunɗal e goonga.

Sabu ɓe ndogata haa e ɗoo, konngol mawngol ina ara e dow leydi, halkere mawnde ina yalti, ina ɓadii; bone, alerte emergency 911, ñalawma oo ina ɓaditoo, ina heɓi on. E nder ndoondi konu ɓe ummoto, e nder ndoondi konu ɓe peeñanoyta e neɗɗaagu kadi, neɗɗaagu hantoto e ɓeen yaniiɓe e kaawnital timmungal e mbaydiiji maɓɓe ɗi ɓe njippini majjuɓe gila e fuɗɗoode. Ɓe kawrita diineeji maɓɓe ɗi ngonaa goonga, ɓe kaalana on fenannde keewɗe ummoriiɗe e hunukooji ruuhuuji fenaannde luutndiiɓe Almasiihu, sabu luutndiiɓe Almasiihu heewɓe naatii e aduna.

Hol ko woni luutndiiɗo Almasiihu, onon sukaaɓe am? Luutndiiɗo Almasiihu ko luutndiiɗo Almasiihu, hisinoowo goonga e Almasiihu, kadi e nokku Almasiihu; sabu ko miin woni Yeesu Almasiihu, alaa goɗɗo. Fenannde maɓɓe ndee yaajii e aduna no ɓe njannginiri yimɓe ɓee yo ɓe ɗofto tati ɓe ngonaa seniiɓe ɓee, hono liingu mawngu, daaba ladde ummoriingu e maayo, e daaba ladde ummoriingu e leydi, woni kadi annabiijo maɓɓe fenaannde. Kono hol ko Yahanes winndi e ndee toɓɓere e nder ɓataake mum gadano? Mi winndaani on, sabu on nganndaa goonga: kono sabu on nganndi ɗum, kadi alaa fenannde ummortoo e goonga. Hol fenoowo, so wonaa saliiɗo wonde Yeesu ko Almasiihu? Ko kanko woni luutndiiɗo Almasiihu, saliiɗo Baaba, e Ɓiɗɗo. Kala saliiɗo Ɓiɗɗo, o alaa Baaba: kono jaɓɗo Ɓiɗɗo, ina jogii Baaba kadi.

Ko noon woniri, miin, Yeesu Naasaret, ko mi laawol, goonga, e nguurndam; hay gooto arataa e Yahuwah so wonaa e am. Ko miin woni damal gootal, ko miin woni laawol gootal yahde e Yahuwah, Allah mawɗo duumiiɗo; sabu ko noon Yahuwah hollitiri, ko noon Yahuwah yuɓɓini, felliti gila ko adii sosngo leydi e nder anndal mum duumiingal e hakkilantaagal mum. Hol e mon waawɗo wasiyaade Yahuwah, Allah mawɗo duumiiɗo? Hay so tawii ujunnaaji sappo laabi ujunnaaji sappo ina taarii jappeere mum laamu, o alaa haaje e wasiyanke; ko Yahuwah, Allah mawɗo duumiiɗo, golliroowo kala huunde e mbaadi yiɗde mum, yo on laato mawninɓe teddungal mum, onon ɓeen goongɗinɓe Almasiihu gila e fuɗɗoode.

Njiɗ-mi on faamde fenaande garoore ndee, ndeen yaltii e aduna. Sabu gollooɓe Seytaane ina kaala ko luutndii goonga, ɓe njuumatnoo haa yeeso, ɓe njuumatnoo no feewi, wonde diineeji aduna fof ina njahra e alla gooto, hay so tawii ko e diine goɗɗo, hay so tawii ko e goongɗingol maɓɓe, wonde inɗe goɗɗe fof ɗe ɓeen yaniiɓe ndokki ɗee ko goonga, ina njahra e alla goonga, kono ko e liingu mawngu tan njahri. Goonga, goonga miɗo haalana on, woto on ngoongɗin fenannde maɓɓe, woto on njiɗan hay gooto waasde goongɗinde majjere hono ndee. Sabu damal halkere ina yaaji, yahde e maayo yiite wulngo ngoo, Seytaane ina yiɗi surde heewɓe no waawirta nii e laawol mum halkere duumiinde, sabu ko noon woni ndonu mum, ko noon woni konne am e mon fof. Woto on ngoongɗin fenannde maɓɓe kawritoore, ndogee e dental maɓɓe ngal wonaa seniiɗo, sabu battane mum ko yiite bulo ɓurngo wulde, so duƴƴe wulɗe ina taaroo, ina wula haa abada, hay gooto daɗataa e ɓeen haɓɓaaɓe, werlaaɓe e yiite.

Faamee, onon sukaaɓe am, wonde inɗe maɓɓe fof ina njahra e alla gooto, hono Seytaane, sabu ɓe ngonaa allaaji kono ko fenaaɓe feññinɓe. Ɓe njuɓɓinta calɗi maɓɓe ceertuɗi, ɓe mbiyata wonde inɗe keewɗe ina njahra e alla gooto. E bannge gooto, ɓe poti, sabu laabi keewɗi ina njahra e alla aduna oo, mo ñaawoore mum ari, mo torra mum e halkere mum natti. E bannge goɗɗo, ɓe fenata aduna, ɓe njiɗi faddaade kala rewooɓe laabi maɓɓe halkooje e nder renndo maɓɓe keso ngo ummotoo e ndoondi konu ngo haajata maande ngam naatde.

Woto on njaɓu maande maɓɓe, sabu kala jaɓɗo ndee maande, o laanete haa abada, o waylite haa abada. Ɓe fenata no feewi wonde inɗe fof ina njahra e Allah goonga, hono Yahuwah, woni Allah duumiiɗo Israa'iila, sabu Yahuwah suɓii Yakuuba ngam hoore mum yo on laato yimɓe keeriiɓe e mum. Ko noon Seytaane yiɗiri reftinde laabi Allah gooto goonga, hono El Shaddai, woni Allah Jom Baawɗe! O waɗii diineeji keewɗi ɗi o reftini, ɗi peewniraa ngam reftinde laabi Yahuwah, Allah duumiiɗo duuniyaaru fof, ɗii diineeji fof ina timmina e halkere duumiinde ngam ɓeen nanngaaɓe heen. Sabu hol fowruɗo waɗata piccal e yiitere colli?

Ɓe ndokkaama barkeeji ngam fof, hay diineeji fof, ɓe njiɗi hawrude e diineeji fof e les calɗi maɓɓe kawritooji, sabu ko noon laabi maɓɓe caɗtuɗi ɗii timminirtee. E bannge gooto ɓe ndokka miijo; e bannge goɗɗo ɓe ndokka luural mum; e nder hare laɓɓinaande ɓe ngaddata dental maɓɓe, sabu ɓe njiɗi hawrude e pinalle fof, leƴƴi fof, e yimɓe e ɗemɗe e nder renndo maɓɓe seytaane ngam halkude fof rewooɓe laabi maɓɓe halkooje. Njiɗ-mi on faamde, ko woni ko firtii waawaa fonndeede, konnguɗi maɓɓe moƴƴi e haalaaji maɓɓe moƴƴi peewniraa ngam welde hakkillaaji neɗɗo e yiɗde e yurmeende fenaande nde ɓe mbiyata senuye. Faamee wonde neɗɗo e hoore mum ina boni no feewi, waawaa fonndude laawol mum, alaa moƴƴere e neɗɗo, kono neɗɗo bonnii sabu ɗoftaaki Aadama, haa teeŋti noon e fenannde e mbaydiiji mboddi, nde hoore mum mi heɓata ɗum no feewi, sabu Yahuwah hollitii gila e fuɗɗoode battane ndeen mboddi fenaande nyaamoore mbuldi leydi.

Ko noon wonnoo, ko noon woni, ko noon wonirta, wonaa huunde hesere les naange. Nde aduna oo yalti e yurmeende yahde e ñaawoore, musiiba jaawɗo ina foti yande, jaawɗo o waɗata. Miɗo wasiyoo fof janngooɓe ɗii konnguɗi yo faam wonde ɗii konnguɗi tawaani on e laawol, kono ko Yahuwah e hoore mum neldi ɗi e laawol ɓiyum gooto jibinaaɗo, hono Yeesu Almasiihu, kaalaaɗi e dental e juuɗe gollooɓe am annabaaɓe. Ko miin, Yeesu Naasaret, hono Yeesu Almasiihu, yoɓi njoɓdi duumiindi ngam hisnugol mon e hisnugol fof garooɓe e nguurndam duumiingam e nder giɗli am ɗi walaa keerol e duumiingam, ɗi Yahuwah sosi mi ngam timminde. Miɗo haalana on ko nanndi e ko kaal-mi gila e fuɗɗoode: wonde miin, Yeesu Almasiihu, hono Yeesu Almasiihu, ko mi laawol, goonga, e nguurndam, hay gooto arataa e Baaba so wonaa e am; alaa innde woɗnde rokkaande e aduna, hay abada, nde yimɓe poti hisde, inɗe goɗɗe fof hay so tawii ko no mbaydiiji ɗii njiylirtee nii, njahdataa e Allah goonga, hono Baaba annabaaɓe mo alaa waylo-waylo walla niɓɓere waylo. Njiɗ-mi on faamde, onon sukaaɓe am, wonde onon ko on annabaaɓe aduna ɗi Yahuwah jibini, hay sosaa kesum e nder am, Almasiihu Yeesu, Joomiraaɗo nuunɗuɗo duumiiɗo, mi waɗaama ruuhu hokkoowo nguurndam.

Ndaaree, miɗo hollita on haala sirri ka hokkata hakkilantaagal annduɓe, ka reena majjuɓe e anndaaɓe e hawtaade e ɓeen haɓɓaaɓe ngam halkere e laabi maɓɓe. Tati, ɗiɗi, gooto: e nder diineeji aduna keewɗi, tati ina njogii doole, ɗi njahdata e gooto fenaannde, tati ɓee ina nanndi e añɓe, kono konuuji keewɗi ɓe kaɓii. Kono, e nder tati ɓee, ɗiɗo ko sehilɓe e wirnii, gooto huutortoo goɗɗo oo, ɓe ɗiɗo fof njahra e halkere. Sabu e nder tati ɓee, ɗiɗo mbeltata e gooto oo no feewi, hay seniiɗo oo mo ɓe koni kono mo ɓe pooli no feewi. Ko noon seniiɗo oo addanta ñaawoore e galle Allah adan, sabu ñaawoore fuɗɗotoo ko e galle Allah, ñalawma ñaawoore oo ari.

Hay so tawii seniiɗo oo foolii, ɗiɗo e nder tati ɓee mbeltata ngam halkude gooto oo, kono ndeen halkere waawaa waɗde, gooto oo heɓii poolgu timmungu. Kono noon fenaande ndee wonirta, wonde ɗiɗo e nder tati ɓee mbeltata e gooto oo, ɓe mbaɗa tuume fenaannde e hunukooji seedeeji fenaannde heewɓe, ɓe kaɓa e seniiɓe, ɓe poola ɓe. E fenannde keewɗe ɗe ɓe njahri e aduna, kono gooto oo, hay seniiɗo Allah oo, foolii ɓe, o halkata hoore maɓɓe no feewi. Kono ɗiɗo ɓee peeñanoyta no pooliiɓe e dow gooto oo mo ɓe njiɗi halkude, e nder ɗiɗo ɓee ɓe kaɓata e gooto oo, hay so tawii halkere maɓɓe timmunde natti. Ko noon piccal ngal waɗirtee ngam aduna, wonde e nder fitina mawɗo e konu, ɓe kawrita hoore maɓɓe haa tati, ɗiɗi, gooto laatoo ɗiɗi, gooto, tati, sabu ɓe kawrita hoore maɓɓe e diine gooto fenaannde laaminooɗo e tati ɓe ngonaa seniiɓe ɓee, hono liingu mawngu, daaba ladde ummoriingu e maayo, e daaba ladde ummoriingu e leydi, woni kadi annabiijo maɓɓe fenaannde.

Ko noon mi hollirta on jaabawol haala am sirri tati. Tati ɓee ko diineeji tati mawɗi laamiiɗi aduna oo jooni; e nder tati ɓee, gooto tan ko goonga, hay seniiɗo Allah oo; sabu miin, Yeesu Almasiihu, ko mi seniiɗo Allah foolɗo bonɗo oo, woni Joomiraaɗo Joomiraaɓe e Laamɗo Laamɓe. Mi waawaa fooldeede, mi rokkaama innde dow kala innde, wonde e innde Yeesu Almasiihu, hono Yeesu Almasiihu (sabu ko mi hisinoowo Gentile e Judean fof) yo kala koppi sujjida, e dow kammu e e dow leydi, hay les leydi. Ko noon diineeji fenaannde ɗiɗi peeñiri e luural kono njiɗi weltinde hoore mum en e gooto oo, goonga Seytaane reftinii eklesiya am goonga e debbo fenaannde mawninoowo e dewoowo laamɗo kammu. Ko noon ɓe njiylitiri dental fenaannde, ɓe njillondiri fenannde keewɗe e goonga, so wonaa fenannde e goonga njillondirtaa: aɗa jogii fenannde walla aɗa jogii goonga: alaa suɓngo ngoɗngo.

Nde jama'aare am goonga'en nanngete to dow kammu, nden mo haɗata oo ittete, non kadi jama'aare fewreere nden halkete, nden noon go'o oo ittetee haa laaɓa. Diinaaji ɗiɗi keddiiɗi ɗin ummoto nder poolgu fewreewu, ɓe keɓan jam fewreeru, ɓe ɗaɓɓan kadi ngootaagu fewreewu ngam hawtindude majjuɓe ɓen, haa ma ɓe ndokka nguurndam duumotooɗam, cellal timmungal, nden nyannde hesere ka jawlal ngal hokkan. O hawtindinan ɗiɗi ɗin faa laatoo diina ka baabiraaɓe maɓɓe nganndaano, kono ngo nanndan e goonga ngam yimɓe ɓen mbaawataa anndude ko laatiino e maɓɓe. E nder ndee hawtindinoore bonannde, ɓe njahan to yiite duumotooɗe, ngam kala hawtuɓe e maɓɓe ko ɓe keɓooɓe welwelo e nder nuunɗaare, wanaa ɗum tan, kono kadi ko yiɗɓe e goonɗinɓe e waɗooɓe e dewooɓe fenaande, ngam laabi maɓɓe ko laabi fewre e jonyeeje, ɗi njahdata e maayde ɗiɗaɓere.

Non ɓe majjinirta aduna, ka heɓtintoo caggal maɓɓe, ina wiyoo, hol no nanndi e dabba ka? Nden dabba gooto rokketee baawɗe dabba goɗɗo oon, nden dabba ummiika e maayo ngon laamoto ɗum en e laamu mum, nden dabba ummiika e leydi ngon laamoto leydi ndin e laamu mum, ɓe laamoto leydi ndin e nder nuunɗaare. Nden e nder ɗiɗi keddiiɗi ɗin, ɓe kawtan faa laatoo go'o. Kono, laabi maɓɓe ko maayde, toon pitfall oon woni, ngam holi fowru tefotooru ɗaɓɓere e yeeso colli? Kala tokkuɓe laabi maɓɓe kawtindinooji ɗin fof kawtindete, tan e nder ndonu malaa'ika'en iliiɓe ɓen; nden kala kawtindinɓe ko'e mum'en e nder ñawbu mboddi maɓɓe ndin, keɓidan e ndonu iliiɗo oon e nder maayo mawngo yiite suluf e yiite ngo ɓe ngulata haa abada, nden liɗɗi maɓɓe maayataa. Non kadi, laabi maɓɓe kawtindinooji ɗin heewi pittirlaaji cuuɗiiɗi no feewi, ngam holi fowru tefotooru ɗaɓɓere e yeeso colli?

Mi wasiyoo kala janngoowo konnguɗi ɗii, e kala jogiiɗo gite ngam yiyde, e noppi ngam nanude, e ɓernde ngam faamde, wonde hisnde e nguurndam duumotooɗam e Alla goongaajo, kanko Yahweh, heɓotaako e innde woɗnde so wonaa e innde ɓiɗɗo Alla gooto bajjo, kanko Iisa Almasiihu, mo kadi woni Yeshua HaMashiach; min woni kisinoowo yimɓe fof, haa e leñol Aadama ngo leñol yimɓe fof renndata e mum'en ƴiiƴam. Nden e kala ɗemngal, e kala leñol, fof ina waawi arde e dow wellitaare, keɓa dokkal am kisal duumotoongal e noddude innde am seninaande haa ragare. Ngam miin woni Joomiraawo mon e kisinoowo mon, wata on accude innde am yaltude e toni mon, mi hisinan on haa timma.

Wata on kawtu e laabi maɓɓe kawtindinooji ɗin, ngam ɓe nganndaa ko ɓe ndewata, ɓe mbaree e fenaandeiji ɗi njahdata e maɓɓe to halkaare maɓɓe duumotoonde. Non kadi, kisal heɓotaako e innde woɗnde, alaa innde woɗnde rokkaande nde yimɓe mbaawi hisde, nde yimɓe mbaawi ɓadtaade Yahweh, ngam on mo alaa Ɓiɗɗo oon, alaa Baabiraawo. Non mi semmbinan, mi tiiɗtinan kala noddooɗo innde am seninaande haa ragare. On mbaɗanan am golle mawɗe, mi nelii on ngam yiɗde Joomiraawo Alla mon e ɓernde mon fof, e ruuhu mon fof, e hakkilantaagal mon fof, e doole mon fof, e yiɗde hoɗdiiɓe mon no on njiɗdiri hoore mon, e wallude e yiɗde gooto e goɗɗo e nder sahaaji ɓurɗi saɗtude ɗi ngoni e arde e dow leydi.

Non, tati, ɗiɗi, go'o, ɗo ɗiɗo ummotoo e go'o oon, nden ɗiɗi keddiiɗi ɗin kawtan faa laatoo go'o, ɗi laamotoo e juuɗe tato ɓe ngalaa senaare ɓen, ɓe laamotoo ɓe inɗe mum'en mbinndaaka e Deftere Nguurndam gila aduna fuɗɗaaka. Kono wata on kawtu e maɓɓe, onon seniiɓe am; ngam ɓuri moƴƴude majjinde nguurndam mon ngam keɓtude ɗam, wonaa yiɗde nguurndam mon ngam majjinde ɗam haa abada e nder halkaare duumotoonde. Ndaaree! Serrondiral gadiingal ngal ina foti fuɗɗaade, nden sahaaji ɗin ko jooni. Wata on kulu ɓe, onon cukalel am, kono kooloree e Joomiraawo mon duumotooɗo, kisinoowo mon. Miin ko ballal duumotoongal e sahaaji caɗeele, mi hisinan haa jooni jama'aare mawnde e nder caɗeele Yakuuba, mi hisinan haa jooni kadi heddiiɓe e Isra'iila, alaa baawɗo ittude on e juuɗe am.

Faamee wonde yaakaare mon ko yaakaare duumotoonde, mi accaali on e nder yaakaare, mi accanii on yeru duumotooɗo. Ngam ina winndaa e am e nder deftere Hebreeɓe: Hoolnaade Iisa, fuɗɗoode e timmoode goonɗinal meeɗen, mo ngam weltaare ndaneede yeeso mum, muñani leggal palaangal, o yefani hersa, o jooɗinaama e nyaamo jappeere laamu Alla. Non faamee wonde yeru am muñal ina wona yeru ngam semmbinde goonɗinal mon ngam on mbaawa muñde haa ragare, no mi muñiri haa ragare nde mi wiyi konnguɗi gadiiɗi ɗin e dow leggal palaangal: Timmii. Non Alla ummini kam e maayɓe ngam maayde waawaano nanngude am, ngam am ko ƴiiƴam laaɓɗam.

Non mi rokkii on yaakaare mawnde, onon cukalel am, haa mbaawon semmbinde hakkillaaji mon e weltaare ndaneede yeeso mon, ngam torraaji caɗeele Yakuuba ɗi potaaka, ɗi potaaka siftoreede e weltaare duumotoonde ndaneede yeeso mon. Ndaaree! Ina winndaa e juuɗe Yuhanna Feeñinoowo: Ɓee ko ummiiɓe e caɗeele mawɗe, ɓe looftii comci maɓɓe, ɓe laɓɓini ɗi e ƴiiƴam Jibinannde. Ko ɗum waɗi ɓe ngonndi yeeso jappeere laamu Alla, ɓe ndewata ɗum jemma e nyalawma e nder suudu dewal mum: nden jooɗiiɗo e jappeere laamu oon hoɗdan e maɓɓe. Ɓe njaawataa kadi, ɓe njoomataa kadi, naange waɗataa e maɓɓe, wulaango kadi waɗataa e maɓɓe. Ngam Jibinannde, ngo woni hakkunde jappeere laamu ngon, nyaamnan ɗum en, ardoo ɗum en to ɓulli ndiyam nguurndam: nden Alla ittan goƴɗi fof e gite maɓɓe. Ɓe laatoo Limaam'en Alla, e Almasiihu, ɓe laamotoo e makko duuɓi ujunere.

Non woni yaakaare mon, onon yiɗɓe am, ngam on mbaawa muñde haa ragare. Ndon ko yaakaare duumotoonde ngam El Shaddai, Alla Baawɗo fof, tagan kammu kesu e leydi kesiri ɗo nuunɗal hoɗata, nden on ndokketee naatde e wuro senngo ngoo, ngo Yahweh e hoore mum feewni, no gelooɗo feewniraaɗo ngam gelooɗo, nden e lekki nguurndam ngon on nyaaman. Nden no winndiraa: Mo heɓi poolgu, ronay kala huunde, mi laatoo mo Alla mum, o laatoo mo ɓiɗɗo am.

Miin, Yeshua HaMashiach, ko miin neldi konnguɗi am e juuɗe maccuɗo am, nden e juuɗe maccuɗo am konnguɗi am nelaama ngam ɗoftaare kala janngoowo. Mi feññinii linjiila duumotooɗo, ngam sahaaji feññinde ɗum ko jooni.

Miin ko miin aranooɓe wonɓe e nder Almasiihu, mi hisinan goonɗinɓe e nder jama'aare am e yiite laɓɓinirde e tikkere aroore.

Miin ko miin serndooɗo alkama e tawa, alaa e mon gooto baawɗo ittude e juuɗe am.

Daranee kam, onon seniiɓe am torraaji, ngaree e am, onon Isra'iila, ngam miin ko Yeshua HaMashiach, Almasiihu mon goongaajo, mi rokkii on yaakaare mawnde e doole mawɗe. Nden heewɓe e mon mbaɗanan am golle mawɗe, alaa e mon gooto mo njoɓdi mum yahataa, ngam haa e huunde ɓurnde famɗude nde mbaɗoton e sahaaji caɗeele mawɗe ina addana on njoɓdi duumotoondi, nde teddungal mum torraaji mon potaaka siftoreede; non noddee innde am teddunde, onon yimɓe am! Hisnde e njoɓdi mawndi ina woni e juuɗe am ngam ɓe keɓɓe poolgu, ngam noon Yeshua HaMashiach fodi ngam goonɗinɓe.

Ndaaree, sahaaji ɗin ko jooni!
