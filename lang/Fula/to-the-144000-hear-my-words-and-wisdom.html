Tilimmaaji 144,000: Nanee Konnguɗi Am e Ñeeñal Am!

Nanee konnguɗi am, ndence noppi mon e ñeeñal am, sabu miin, Joomiraawo Iisa Almasiihu, mi haaldata e mooɗon so on nanii konnguɗi am. Sabu mi maantinay 144,000 am; on njanngaali naa? Mi yii maleyka goɗɗo ina ummoo fuɗnaange, ina jogii maande Alla guurɗo: o ewnii e daande toownde maleykaaji nayo ɓe ndokkaa doole ngam lorlude leydi ndii e maayo ngoo, o wiyi, wata on lorlu leydi ndii, wata kadi on lorlu maayo ngoo malla leɗɗe ɗee, haa min mbaɗa maande e tiiɗe maccuɓe Alla amen. Nii woni mi nelat ɓe mbaɗdam maande, onon kadi on mbaɗanay kam golle mawɗe, on njahrat e baawɗe mawɗe. Kono ɗum laatortaako no e jamaanu moƴƴere, ɗo waajuɓe e minbarji njannginnoo e binndanɗe ceniiɗe ɗee. (Woɓɓe kaalii fenaande, woɓɓe kadi mbaɗanii kam golle mawɗe e nder eklesiyaaji mum en.) Jamaanu eklesiya oo ina jogori timmude, jamaanu moƴƴere am oo kadi ina ɓadii timmude, ko ɗum waɗi jamaanuuji jarribooji ɗii maa puɗɗo, haa e torra Yaakuuba.

Nii woni mi nelat on ngam ndeenon durngol am ngol ɓeydotoo saaktaade e jarribeede e musiibaaji jokkondirɗi. Sabu balɗe ɗee laatoto ko bonɗe, onon kadi on jalbirtay no fitillaaji, on ndarotoo no doose hakkunde saaktiɓe ɓee. Nii woni mi nelat on ngam yiɗde e toppitaade hoɗdiiɓe mooɗon. Sabu onon, wonɓe goongɗinɓe e noddaango am, on nguurtay e sariya mo ndokku-mi, wonaa sariya Muusaa. Sabu miin, Yeesuwa Almasiihu, mi timminii sariya Muusaa, Tawreeta kadi ina reenee e goongɗinde kam, sabu peewɗo o wuurirta ko goongɗinal.

Nii woni maa laato: Yiɗee Joomiraawo Alla mooɗon e ɓernde mooɗon fof, e ruuhu mooɗon fof, e hakkilantaagal mooɗon fof, e semmbe mooɗon fof, kadi yiɗee hoɗdiiɓe mooɗon no hoore mooɗon nii. Sabu e waɗde ɗeen geɗe, on kisintay e goonga ruuhu mooɗon e halkaare, kam e ɓe nel-mi on e mum en. Ɗii ngonaa jamaanuuji nguura juutka e alɗude, sabu ɗiin balɗe njaltii e leydi haa mi wara ngam laamaade leydi e nuunɗal. On yiɗaaɗo nguurndam mum haa timmal, o hisat, o wuura haa abada.

On nguuri ko e jamaanuuji konu, e tikkere mawnde, e wahyu mawɗo e geɗe fof. Sabu Alla mawɗo duumiiɗo oo, hono Yahwe, suuɗortaako kadi e leydi; sabu o yaltay e hoɗorde makko, o nelat kam, hono Yeesuwa Almasiihu, ngam timminde tikkere makko e timminde fodaariiji makko ɗi o fodannoo Baabiraaɓe ɓee. Kono gadano, ñaawoore maa yani, tikkere mawnde maa yani e aduna, on mbaawataa kadi tawtoreede renndo maɓɓe. Sabu tawtoreede renndo maɓɓe ngo ummortoo e duƴƴe ɗee, maa ɗaɓɓu e kala neɗɗo yo jaɓɓu maande maɓɓe. So on naatdii e maɓɓe, on tawtodoto e maɓɓe e jukkungo maɓɓe duumiiŋgo e nder weendu yiite mawndu nduu, ndeen weendu ko Alla waɗani Iblisa e maleykaaji mum, wata on naatdu e maɓɓe.

On keɓataa jam e aduna maɓɓe ummotooɗo oo, so wonaa bone mo alaa keerol, sabu mi ittay haɗoowo oo. So on jeyaaka e hakkilantaagal maɓɓe gootal ngal, ndeen on jeydaaki e maɓɓe, sabu ɓe mbartay kala saliiɗo. Sabu maande ndee maa waylu haa abada ɓe njaɓɓi ɓee e waylude DNA maɓɓe, sabu neɗɗo waɗaama e sifa Yahwe, hay so tawii Seyɗaane ina yiɗi waylude e tooñannge ngal sifa e laawol mo alaa firo rewrude e safaareeji mum ɗi feññinirtee e fenaande mawnde. Ɓe fodanat on nguurndam juutɗam, jam, e alɗude, kono ɗeen fenaande ko nyiɓre tan. Sabu so maande ndee nanii, hay huunde waylataa bonannde ndee, sabu Yahwe hollitii ɗum!

Sabu maccuɗo ɓuraa joom mum, miɗo nodda on e golle ɗo njahdirammi e mooɗon seeɗa-seeɗa, no ñalɗi Nuuhu nii, nii maa laatoroo. Nii woni miin, Yeesuwa Almasiihu, mi timminii paandaale am hono Jawgel Alla, sabu mi hokkii Yahwe iyi'am ɗam alaa ella e yiɗde am e hoore am e sabu yiɗde timmunde ndee, ndiyamɗo e ɓernde am.

Nii woni sabu weltaare ndee, nde ndaaraami, mi muñii torra mawka, masiibo, e mette, (hay so tawii ko e tooñannge) sabu mi jogitii batte kala bonannde aadee fof e hoore am. Mi yoɓii kala bonannde, haa laaɓi, mi waɗii ɗum e yiɗde am. Nde ndaaree kam, Iisa, puɗɗoowo e timminoowo goongɗinal mooɗon, on sabu weltaare ndee, ndaaraami, muñii leggal dasal, mi yeddii hersa, mi jooɗii e ñaamo jooɗorde Alla. Nii woni njoñee yeru am haa e nasaraaku mawɗo, sabu hay e ladde mi laaɓinay laawol mooɗon.

Nde faamee, jam alaa, miɗo nela on ngam ngollinee e jamaanu konu e torra mawka. Sabu liingu mawngu nguun haɓdata e ceniiɓe ɓee, ngu foola ɓe, sabu ko ɗuum ngu hokkiraa baawɗe ngam timminde. Kadi ɓe noddooɓe innde Joomiraawo maa hise, sabu ɓe yiɗaaɓe nguurndam mum en haa timmal maa keɓ nguurndam mum en haa abada e nder am, Almasiihu Iisa. Sabu mi kafanii bonanndeeji aadee e nder iyam am ɗam alaa ella, mi woni hooreejo liman'en haa abada e mbaadi Melkisedek, on hokkunooɗo Yahwe iyam am ɗam alaa ella laawol gootol haa abada. Ko ɗuum waɗi, wata on ngartu e laabi ɓooyɗi, malla aadaaji maɓɓe, malla Talmud maɓɓe, malla Tawreeta; sabu Tawreeta ina reenee haa laaɓi e goongɗinde kam, Iisa Almasiihu.

Nii woni rewrude e torraaji mawɗi, masiibooji mawɗi, hareeji mawɗi, ceniiɓe am maa njalbu, njeenaari maɓɓe maa mawnoy, sabu njeenaari am ina mawni. Sabu hol ko fodoore ndee wiyi: Ko ɓee ngoni yaltuɓe e torra mawka, ɓe lootii comci maɓɓe, ɓe mburtinii ɗi e nder iyam Jawgel ngel. Ko ɗuum waɗi, ɓe ngonii yeeso jooɗorde Alla, ɓe ndewata mo jamma e nyalawma e nder suudu dewal makko: on jooɗiiɗo e jooɗorde ndee kadi maa hoɗdu e maɓɓe. Ɓe njuumtataa kadi, ɓe nɗomɗataa kadi, naange waɗataa e maɓɓe, malla nguleeki ngal fof. Sabu Jawgel ngel, ngo woni e hakkunde jooɗorde ndee, maa ñammin ɓe, maa ardina ɓe haa e ilamji nguurndam: Alla kadi maa momtu goƴɗi fof e gite maɓɓe.

Nii woni mi nelat ɓe noddu-mi ɓee. Miɗo nodda on ngam yiɗde Alla e ɓernde mooɗon fof, e ruuhu mooɗon fof, e hakkilantaagal mooɗon fof, e semmbe mooɗon fof, miɗo nela on ngam yiɗde hoɗdiiɓe mooɗon no hoore mooɗon nii. Sabu hisde maa ummoo e konnguɗi hokkooji nguurndam ɗi Baaba am hokki ngam ɗoftaare fof. Nii woni yiɗee hoɗdiiɓe mooɗon, toppitondiree, mballondiree, ndimondiree; sabu yimɓe am maa saaktoo. Ɓe mooɓondirtay e renndooji e ladde, hay so tawii ladde ndee wonataa kisal, mi waɗanay yimɓe am reedu, mi waɗanay ɓe ko ɓe kaajata.

Nii woni mi nelat ɓe noddu-mi ɓee. Miɗo nela on ngam yamirde ko waawaaka so on njahii e yiɗde e baawɗe mawɗe, hay baawɗe ɗe mbaɗnata mi on. Sabu on njahanay kam seeɗa-seeɗa laabi keewɗi e ɗoftaare, so on fawii e am, Almasiihu Iisa, on ardintooɗo e ruuhu am; toɓɓere ndee, ndee jogiiɗon ndee, maa waɗ on njaha e nasaraaku. Kono maa laato no binndi ɗii mbiiri: Barkinaaɓe ko maayɓe e nder Joomiraawo, gila jooni, ee, wiyi Ruuhu, ngam ɓe pooftoo e golle maɓɓe, golle maɓɓe kadi ina njokka ɓe.

Seeɗa-seeɗa on njahrat, mi ardintoo on e oon laawol. Sabu miin woni reedu mooɗon e tata mooɗon, hay Almasiihu mooɗon goongaajo mo koolotooɗon. Hay so tawii ujunere ina yani e bannge mooɗon, sappo ujunere e bannge mooɗon ñaamo: kono ɗum ɓadataako on. Sabu on mbaɗaama maande ngam timminde paandaale am, kadi njeenaari mawndi e fodoore mawnde ndokku-mi on ngam wallude on e semmbinaade on, sabu miɗo nela on e golle ɗe Baaba am yamiri kam, nii woni mi nelat on ko on ministeeruuji am e annabaaɓe am.

Nii woni so on njahii ngam yamirde ko waawaaka, anndee on yamirat ko heewi e ruuhu jibinaaɗo e nder mooɗon oo. Nii woni yamiree ko'e ɗee ittee, njaɓɓee e maayo, so on ngoongɗinii Yahwe. Nii woni yamiree jinneeji ɗii njalta so on kaalii konnguɗi mawɗi e goongɗinal mawngal e Yahwe. Nii woni yamiree ñawuuji fof e rafiiji fof njalta so on kaalii konnguɗi ɗi poti ɗoftaade (hay e geɗe fof) so on kaalii konnguɗi baawɗi mawɗi e goongɗinal mawngal e Yahwe. Sabu ko e peeje e paandaale Yahwe e hoore mum neliraa-mi, e nder ɗeen peeje e paandaale miɗo nela on, on mbaɗanay kam golle mawɗe. Sabu Yahwe ko fof e fof e ruuhu am, mo mbaɗdam maande cuɓaaɓe ɓee.

Sabu e nder am, Yeesuwa Almasiihu, timmal fof ina hoɗi, sabu Baaba oo hokkii kam geɗe fof e asamaan e leydi, ngam mi timmina fof. Nii woni Yahwe ko fof e fof, on keɓii naatde e Baaba oo e e Ɓiɗɗo mum, sabu ko nii Muusaa janngini: Joomiraawo Alla mooɗon maa ummin e mooɗon Annabi ummoriiɗo e hakkunde mooɗon, e banndiraaɓe mooɗon, nannduɗo e am, kanko on ndentotoo. Miin woni on annabi mo Muusaa haali oo (hay umminaama e leñol Yahuuda), mi ɓuri ɗum no feewi; sabu Baaba oo fellitii wonde miin, Yeesuwa Almasiihu, miɗo jogii kala teddungal, e nder am timmal fof ina foti hoɗde. Miin woni on annabi mo Muusaa haali oo, hay umminaama e maayɓe ngam oon paandaale.

Nii woni mi waɗanay on e ɓe nel-mi on e mum en. Sabu on laatoto ko fitilla jalboowo, hay so tawii ko suuɗiiɗo e yeeso aduna niɓɓo e mo alaa deeƴre. On mbaajotoo geɗe fof jowitiiɗe e am, Joomiraawo mooɗon e hisnoowo, sabu miin woni jawgel Alla, hay Paska timmuɗo. Kisal alaa e innde woɗnde so wonaa e innde am, hay Iisa Almasiihu Nuunɗuɗo.

Woɓɓe e mooɗon mi nawata nokku haa nokku, on laatoto ko haalooɓe am, annabaaɓe, e ministeeruuji. On kaajotoo jarribaade jinneeji ɓe kawrton ɓee, on njaha e ñeeñal mawngal; sabu aduna mo anndu-ɗon oo maa lomtine aduna mo alaa sariya, mo laddeeji keewɗi, woɓɓe e mooɗon maa nele ngam ngollinee ɓe ngonɓe e laddeeji ɗii, on paamatay yamiraaji am, on mbaɗat golle mawɗe.

Nii woni paamee wonde caggal konu e mbonniigu nguu, nguu foti waɗde caggal nde mi mooɓta goongɗinɓe am (mi itta haɗoowo oo), maa wood bonannde e mbonniigu keewngu. Yimɓe ndogata ina ndilloo, heewɓe maa halke e nder goongɗinaani, ɓe ndaɗata, ɓe nanga, ɓe mbara heewɓe. Kadi heewɓe, daande adannde nde ɓe nanata ndee wonataa daande am, hay so tawii ndeen daande maa fodan kisal fenaande. Alaa, hay kullel ngel, ngel ummortoo e baawɗe mawɗe ummoraade e liingu mawngu nguun, maa noddu heewɓe e laawol fenaande haa e timmal maɓɓe duumiiŋgal ngo alaa yaakaare.

Ɓe fof ɓe mbaɗat no feewi, sabu ko ɓe pewjooɓe mawɓe, ɓe mbaɗat no ko kamɓe ngoni Almasiihu goongaajo, yimɓe maɓɓe kadi maa mbi'a ko kamɓe ngoni lomtiiɓe Almasiihu goongaajo e Alla goongaajo; kono mi nelanooka ɓe, wata on njoñu fitillaaji maɓɓe fenaande ɗii. Sabu ɓe podanat naatde e renndo maɓɓe keso ngam mahde aduna maɓɓe keso; kono ngam naatde, neɗɗo foti jaɓde maande ndee. Kadi e ɓe njaɓɓi maande ndee, naatgol maa rokkire e nder lewlewndu yiite bulo e nder ndeen weendu yiite mawndu ndu wulirta e kibiri. Sabu kala jaɓɓiiɗo maande ndee maa tawtore e yiite ngu nyifataa nguu, o waylitii nguurndam duumiiɗam e tamre mburu e laabi Esawu.

On kaajotoo yejjitde kala daande so wonaa daande am, on ceerta e kala saliiɗo kam. Maa ɗaɓɓu ñeeñal, karallaagal, e pewje ngam hisde, mi hokkat on ɗemngal ñeeñal ngam on annda no njaabortoo kala neɗɗo. Sabu mi laaɓinay daande am e mooɗon, hay so tawii ko daande famarde, deeƴnde. On anndat daande am, sabu mi tabitintay daande am e mooɗon laabi keewɗi, wata on ngontu goongɗinaaɓe kono ngoodo-ɗon goongɗinal mawngal e konnguɗi am fodoore.

Mi jannginat on geɗe fof, on anndat no mbaɗirton golle ɗe nel-mi on ngam timminde. Daande am ko daande timmunde, hay daande wootere ewnaande e ladde, miɗo haalda e daande wootere ewnaande e ladde; sabu ko nii ɓe neliraa, hay annabaaɓe am e ministeeruuji am ɓe njahnoo yeeso ngam laaɓinde laabi mooɗon e laabi mooɗon. Mi yamirii on golle mawɗe keewɗe, on mbaɗat no feewi.

Ko ɗuum waɗi, anndee leƴƴi am, musiɓɓe, e ɓiɓɓe fodoore: remuru nduu ko mawndu e goonga, kono gollotooɓe ɓee ko seeɗa; toɗɗee ndeen Joomiraawo remuru nduu, ngam o nela gollotooɓe e remuru makko. Njaree laabi mooɗon: Ndaaree, miɗo nela on no jawɗi e hakkunde luuɗi. Sabu miin woni Joomiraawo Remuru, hay so tawii gollotooɓe ɓee ko seeɗa koppi, ina woodi njeenaari mawndi e nafoore duumiinde e teddungal ngam golle ɗe mbaɗaton. Sabu on keɓat njeenaari e ɓiɓɓe leɗɗe keewɗe e nder am; tan wata on tampu e torraaji mooɗon e masiibooji. Ndee ko ceerungal cakkitiingal, kala yamiraaɗo nguurndam duumiiɗam gila aduna fuɗɗaaka maa tawaa, ɓe majjataa abada, sabu hay gooto waawaa fotde ɓe e juuɗe am.

Nii woni golle mooɗon maa njokku haa e duumiinde, e nder golle mooɗon keewɗe on teddintay Yahwe, on nelɗo kam, Yeesuwa Almasiihu, hono Almasiihu mooɗon goongaajo. Kadi e golle mooɗon keewɗe mawɗe e konnguɗi goonga lamɗuɗi keewɗi ɗi kaalaton, on kumpitay artugol am ɓadiingol, on njaha e golle Yuhanna Baftista ngam heblanaade garol Joomiraawo: nii woni kumpitee artugol am, sabu mi timminay e goonga konnguɗi am fodoore. Sabu e goonga e timmal torra Yaakuuba, hay e timmal yontere cakkitiinde duuɓi, hay yontere cappanɗe jeeɗiɗi Daniyel, mi artay e konuuli asamaan ngam tabitinde Laamu am e Leydi, hay Laamu Alla; laamu am duuɓi ujunere maa fuɗɗoo, hay laamu nuunɗal, ceniingal, e jam no rawaandu nduu fawortoo e Jawgel ngel!

Miɗo nodda on e golle ɗo njahdirammi e mooɗon seeɗa-seeɗa.

Miɗo nodda on ngam yiɗde Alla e ɓernde mooɗon fof, e ruuhu mooɗon fof, e hakkilantaagal mooɗon fof, e semmbe mooɗon fof, e yiɗde hoɗdiiɓe mooɗon no hoore mooɗon nii.

Miɗo nela on ngam yamirde ko waawaaka so on njahii e yiɗde mawnde e baawɗe mawɗe.

Miin woni reedu mooɗon e tata mooɗon, hay Almasiihu mooɗon goongaajo mo koolotooɗon.

Miɗo nela on e golle ɗe Baaba am yamiri kam, nii woni mi yamirii on ko on ministeeruuji am e annabaaɓe am.

Miɗo nela on, on mbaɗanay kam golle mawɗe.

Miin woni on annabi mo Muusaa haali oo, hay umminaama e Yahuuda ngam oon paandaale.

Mi ɓuri ɗum no feewi, sabu e nder am, Yeesuwa Almasiihu, timmal fof ina hoɗi.

Miin woni jawgel Alla, hay Paska timmuɗo.
