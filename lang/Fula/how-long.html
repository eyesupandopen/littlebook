Seedeeji ɗiɗi : Jama'aare nden
E nder golle Yuhanna Takkunooɗo
Mbooye 02, 2024

Haade tuma foti?

Konngol Joomiraawo ngol Iisaa Almasiihu hokki.

Haade tuma foti, haade tuma foti, yimɓe am njaŋtanto noddaango am? Haade tuma foti ɓe takkoo hakkeeji maɓɓe? Haade tuma foti ɓe nguura e nder weltaare hakke? Tuubee. Tuubee. Tuubee! Ngartiree kam e ɓerɗe mooɗon fuu. Ngartiree Joomiraawo Alla mooɗon e juuɗe am poftaaɗe!

Fii teeyam am: Mi hokkii on nguurndam, kono nguurndam mooɗon suuɗiima e nder am; tuma nde njaltumi, onon duu on njaltan e nder nguurndam ka mi hokki on. On njaɓii e pawa, kono ɗum yoɓanii kam coggu mawngu fii soktoragol mooɗon. E on naftoray moƴƴere am wa saa'i ɗaanaaki? Kaa on naftoray moƴƴere am wa saa'i laɓɓingol ko'e mooɗon sabu ƴiiƴam am ɗam mi wujji fii mooɗon to Kalvaari? E on njaɓay noddaango am fii waajaade aduna maayɗo e hisnude heewɓe e musiɓɓe mooɗon? Kaa on njaŋtanto noddaango am, on cuɓoo weltaare hakke fii saa'i timmoowo?

Fii mooɗon fuu, mi wi'i: Ngartiree kam e ɓernde mooɗon timmunde; tuubee e wonki mooɗon fuu! Ɗaɓɓee yeeso am, njaɓee yaafaare am, sabu Ñalawma Joomiraawo ɓadake, hono no mi rentiniri yimɓe am. Mi deƴƴataa, mi ɗon hirsinira on udditugol kunɗe mooɗon fii hisnude ɓe ngiɗɗon e ɓe ngaddan-mi on. Ciftoree giɗli mooɗon arandeeji, ngaree e am hade luwal wifaneede.
