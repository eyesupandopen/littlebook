Ut 03, 2024

Peccee Wullaango sabu Babilon Sirri!

Nanee konnguɗi am, nanee daande wullotooɗo e nder ladde! Miɗo nodda, miɗo nodda, on nani kam? Ñiiɓnee ɗemɗe mon keewɗe hakkeeji e nder deƴƴere, sujjanon e tuubal, nanee daande am ndu alaa maayde nde fota mon woodi tawo, sabu giɗal am e mon ina mawnude, ko miin woni ilnoowo gonɗi keewɗi e nder ɓernde mettunde sabu ko heɓata ɓeen saliiɓe daande am. Sabu hay e nder ndee waktu cakkitiindu, kala jaabiiɗo ina noddaama e nguurndam duumotooɗam. Ndeen, njaɓee mi ko mi Joomiraawo mon, sabu ko miin woni Iisaa mo Naasaret, ko miin kadi Alla duumotooɗo oo neli ngam hisnude on hade masiibo e ñaawoore ari. Hol ko waɗi on njiyrata ko'e mon ko on potaani nguurndam duumotooɗam? Mbele on cuɓoto yiite malkisal dow gese deeƴɗe keewɗe jawdi ɗo keɓoton na'i duumotooɗi?

Sabu no giɗo bonannde kaasnooɗo e bannge am to Kalvaari wonirta e am to aljanna, noon ne mi hisnirta on haa laaɓa so on mbaɗii mi Joomiraawo mon jooni! WAKTU ALAA KO FOYDATA. Sabu mi maayii yaasi damal Urusaliima e hakkeeɓe nde njoginoo mi nokku mon e nder ñaawoore, on hisay so on tuubanii mi jooni. Miɗo wi'a on goonga, goonga: ɗii konnguɗi njiyraani noppi mon e dow waɗde, ɓooyataa masiibo mawnungo ari, heewɓe nguurataa, salagol maɓɓe ngoon duumoto e nder yiite duumotooɗe mbaɗanaama kala cuɓiiɗo jogaade hakkeeji mum, heddoo e dental e tooñooɓe.

Naatde e murto Seytaane, hay so tawii ko e majjere laaɓtunde, ko yiɗde renndude e hakkeeji maɓɓe, ɗum noon kala baɗɗo ɗum duumoto e ñaawoore maɓɓe. Mbele on njaɓan noddaango am, anndude Alla imminii mi e maayɓe ngam mi maayataa kadi, anndude ko miin woni Joomiraawo Joomiraaɓe e Laamɗo Laamɓe? Tuubee nde fota mon woodi tawo, sabu Yahweh ina hebiloo uddude damal laana kaa, waktu alaa, e kala saha mi nanngan gomɗinɓe mi nawta e kammu, hay so tawii ko ɓeen hisnaaɓe e tikkere e nder sadak am kisnoowo, duumotooɗo. Ndeen, mbi'ee miin, Iisaa mo Naasaret, ko mi Joomiraawo mon, anndee miɗo wuuri haa abada, sabu Alla imminii mi e maayɓe ngam mi maayataa kadi.

Sukaaɓe am, nanee, jokkee sujjude e tuubal jokkungal, nanee daande Joomiraawo mon Iisaa Almasiihu, kañum Yahweh neli no Jawgel Alla. Nanee Konnguɗi am, Eklesiya am, hay so tawii ko Resam am, Sabu no mi woniri Liman mawɗo mon duumotooɗo e laawol Melkisedek, mi sakkorii hoore am no mi woniri Paasika mon laaɓɗo, mi hokkii hay ƴiiƴam am ɗam alaa hakke haa toɓɓere cakkitiinde ngam hisnude on haa laaɓa e ngonka mon tooñuka, hisnugol mon timmungal ngol arii, ɗum noon ceyoree!

Heewɓe njaɓɓiiɓe mi, njaɓɓii innde am duumotoonde no Joomiraawo maɓɓe, keɓan nguurndam duumotooɗam. Sabu ko miin woni Iisaa mo Naasaret, Joomiraawo mon duumotooɗo, mi waɗii kala jaɓɗo innde am e nder ɗii jamaanuuji yurmeende e moƴƴere, ɓiɓɓe Alla duumotooɓe. Ndaaree, hol giɗal baaba on hokki, haa noddiree ɓiɓɓe Alla: sabu ngal dalil aduna oo anndaa on, sabu anndaa mi, Almasiihu Iisaa, Joomiraawo Joomiraaɓe e Laamɗo Laamɓe. Giɗaaɓe am, jooni ko on ɓiɓɓe Alla, kono haa jooni feeñaani ko ngonoton: onon ne on anndi so mi feeñii, on nannday e am: sabu on njiyran mi no mi wayi.

Sukaaɓe am tokosooɓe, paamee mi feeñay e kala saha jooni, sabu feeñde am e nder duule ina ɓadii. Sabu miin, Almasiihu Iisaa, mi jippoto e kammu e noddaango, e daande Malaa'ika mawɗo, e buutol Alla: maayɓe e nder Almasiihu ummoto adii. Ndeen onon wuuruɓe, heddiiɓe, on njanngante e maɓɓe e nder duule, ngam hawrude e Joomiraawo mon duumotooɗo e nder weeyo: noon on ngonndirta e Joomiraawo mon haa abada. Mbele on njaɓan ɗii konnguɗi kofna on, sukaaɓe am? Mbele on njaɓii Seytaane waɗi on njaŋkii ɗii konnguɗi laaɓtuɗi mbinndaaɗi ngam kala yiyde? Mbele on cuɓoto heddaade e ñaaweeɓe e nder murto tiiɗngo? Salagol ndee hiirde mbinndaande e nder binndi laaɓtuɗi e juuɗe Nelaaɗo am Pool ko salagol am, sabu miɗo ara ngam hisnude gomɗinɓe e tikkere, sabu ko noon fodoore Yahweh feeñiri e nder sirlu mawɗo Alla mo Pool winndi duuɓi keewɗi jooni.

Miɗo wasiyoo on: woto on caalo am, naatee e laana kaa hade mum laataade. Sabu kala cuɓiiɗo heddaade e ñaawoore aroore, arat e am e noddude innde am haa laaɓa, heewɓe torroto haa ɓe poofda cakkitiingo. Kono kamɓe ne ɓe ummoto e nguurndam duumotooɗam, e njoɓdi mawndi, e weltaare duumotoonde! Ndeen kala noddaaɗo e nguurndam duumotooɗam arat e ndonu mum duumotoongu, sabu hay gooto waawaa fooɗtude hay ɓurɗo famɗude e mon e juuɗe am duumotooɗe. Ndeen nanee konnguɗi am, keddee e miijo ngootaagu ngo woni e am, Almasiihu Iisaa, sabu ko miin woni Konngol Alla ngol wonti neɗɗo, miɗo haala goonga heddii. Ngaccitee heɗaade daande keewɗe ɗe ngalaa goonɗinal ɗe caloto konnguɗi am, walla on njiyran hoore mon e maɓɓe nde ɗum wonaa ko foti.

Sabu onon hisnaaɓe am, hay so tawii ko jooni on ngoni Sukaaɓe Alla Wuuruɗo oo, hay so tawii ko ɓiɓɓe Yahweh e laawol e kaawis jibinannde hesere on hisnaama haa abada, on jibinaama kadi e aawdi ndu bonnataa. Kono, on koɓɓe e jippiiɓe e leyɗeele janane, heewɓe e mon ina ngoni e keeri ndeen debbo jeenoowo mawɗo, hay leyɗeele wonɗe e jolokooje ina ndaara ñaawoore mum timmunde e malkisal: Sabu ñaawoore ɗiɗmere hollitaama e Babilon Sirri, noon ne wonirta. Sabu hol waktu Yahweh hisnaani fonnditiiɓe e Ñaawoore, sabu hay gooto waawaa reftinde majjere e Yahweh no fotiri, seedanfaagu Luut ina seedtoo haa hannde. Hay e nder leyɗeele ñidduɗe Sodom e Gomorra, Yahweh neli Malaa'ikaaji mum ngam hisnude oo neɗɗo fonnditiiɗo gooto jooɗinooɗo e dame wuro gaño.

Noon ne mi hisnirta Eklesiya am gomɗinɗo, hay ɓeen ɓe njuurnii mi e ruuhu ceniiɗo fodoore gila ñalawma Pentekoste e jibinannde Eklesiya am mbinndaande e seedanfaagu golle! (Mbele on njanngaani?) Kala maayɗo e goonɗinal e nder Almasiihu gila puɗɗagol jamaanu Eklesiya am, e kala wuuruɗo heddiiɗo, mooɓondirtee e nder lewlewngo yiitere, haa abada ɓe ngonndata e am! Sabu ko miin woni garoowo ngam gomɗinɓe am, noddaaɓe, cuɓaaɓe, hay ɓanndu am e Eklesiya am, hay Resam am. Mi soottan gomɗinɓe, hay ɓeen njahruɓe e laawol ruuhu am, e kala tuubiiɗo e hakkeeji mum, waɗi mi Joomiraawo e ɓernde keewnde goonɗinal, sabu ko kamɓe ngoni Isra'iila Alla! Ndee hiirde aroore mawnay, diwgol ngol mawnay, e maandeeji keewɗi garol am anndinaama, ina jokki e hollireede e dow kammuuji e maandeeji to dow e maandeeji e leydi to les. Noon ne mooftugol ɓiɓɓe leɗɗe adanɓe wonirta ngam kala yiyde e haawee!

Noon ne daande am ina jokki e wullude e nder ndee aduna tooñuka, ina yiyla gite, noppi, e ɓerɗe kala ɓe neldaa. Mbele on nanii daande Yesuwa HaMasiyak, mbele on annditii daande am? Mbele on njaɓan konnguɗi am? Mbele on nanii miɗo haalda e ɓernde mon? Sabu no winndiraa: ɓerɗe ndee yimɓe mbarii e ɓeydagol nyaamdu, noppi maɓɓe nfaɗɗii e nanngo, gite maɓɓe ɓe muumii, woto e kala saha ɓe njiya e gite maɓɓe, ɓe nana e noppi maɓɓe, ɓe paama e ɓernde maɓɓe, ɓe mbaala, mi sellina ɓe. Heewɓe e ɓeen ɓe njoñaa haa ɓe naniima e konnguɗi ñidduɗi kono belɗi ɗi Iisaa goɗɗo, kono kooloree alaa goɗɗo.

Sabu e kala ɗemngal innde am noddirtee, miin Iisaa mo Naasaret, hay Yesuwa HaMasiyak, mi nanan, mi hisnan haa laaɓa kala nodduɗo innde am haa laaɓa. Sabu konnguɗi am hakkilantaagal, hay linjiila duumotooɗo, feeñiima, ina wulla e nder leydi. Konnguɗi am ina njiyla ɓe neldaa, sabu ko noon Konnguɗi Alla Toowɗo, hay Yahweh, ngoni, sabu hay Konngol Alla gootol wuurtaaki meere, ɗi timminan ko ɗi niyetee, gite mon mbarjinaama, sabu ɗe nji'ii: noppi mon ne, sabu ɗe nanii, ɓerɗe mon keewi goonɗinal, mi liiɓii giɗal Alla e nder ɓerɗe mon, haa oɗon nodda Abba Baaba! Onon ko ɓiɓɓe Alla, feeñaani ko ngonoton, kono so mi feeñii on njiyran timmude, nguurndam mon suuɗiima e am, Almasiihu Iisaa, nguurndam mon ina ɓadii feeñde haa laaɓa, jooni ko waktu feeñnude.

Ndaaree! Babilon Sirri ina jooɗii e jolokooje nde galleeji sappo ɗii timmini lebbindanɗe mum en ngam malkisal mum timmungal. Goonga, goonga, to Babilon Sirri e duuɓi keewɗi jooni mi neli nelaaɓe am, mi neli annabaaɓe am, mi neli linjila'en am, mi neli durooɓe am, mi neli jannginooɓe am, mi neli almuɓɓe am gomɗinɓe e miñiraaɓe am, mi neli hay rewɓe jom en galle'en e nder wootere maɓɓe, mi liiɓii ruuhu am haa laaɓa e nder ɗii ñalɗi cakkitiɗi yurmeende e moƴƴere!

Kono e nokku ɗo leñol ngol jooɗii e comci boɗeeji e ndoondi, ɓe nweltorii maandeeji e jeertinirɗe ñaawoore aroore, ɓe nfoto, ɓe mbaɗa wideyoo ngam weltinde hoore maɓɓe. Ɓe calii nelaaɓe am addunooɓe annoore linjiila am kisal, ɓe njaɓɓii, ɓe njaltini annabaaɓe am, ɓe njippii linjila'en am, ɓe mbonnii durooɓe am, ɓe torrii, ɓe njuɓɓini jannginooɓe am.

Konuuji mboddi mawndi boɗeeri ndii ina nduurnii on, Babilon sirri, ina ngoni haa laaɓa e nder keeri mon. Sabu hay ardiiɓe mon Siyonist en janfiiɓe on njoɓii on e mburu tokoso to ganyo'en mon, kadi ngam ɓamtude peeje maɓɓe seytaane. El Shaddai jaɓii ɗii jinnaaɓe njokku, hay so tawii ko o waɗi ɗum e ɓerɗe maɓɓe ngam timminde anniyaaji makko. El Shaddai doole mum ina ñaawoo on, aan debbo jeenoowo boɗeejo jooɗiiɗo e mawnikinaare e weltaare hay so tawii on njoɓaama teddungal kala, on njoɓaama delicatessen kala, on njoɓaama hay galleeji mon sabu mi hokkii galleeji mon e leyɗeele mon e juuɗe ganyo'en mon. Hay e juuɗe kuullewal e annabiijo fenaande, sabu ɓe ngollodoo e juuɗe e juuɗe ngam malkisal mon timmungal caggal yeeso nayeejo. Sabu kuullewal ngal ina ɓadii liɓde debbo jeenoowo mawɗo oo mo kuullewal ngal heɓi haa heƴi, anniyaaji mum timmiima. Ko ɗum waɗi, o wuliree haa laaɓa, o halkee haa laaɓa e nder waktu gooto, malkisal makko ngal leeltataa.

Sabu linenji maɓɓe konu, hay linenji maɓɓe boɗeeji dokkaaɗi e fenaande ngam woto taƴee, ndarii, taƴaama laabi keewɗi e anniya. Woto ngoonɗinee fenaande maɓɓe, walla hujjaaji maɓɓe fenaande, sabu ɓe njiɗi feeñde no fonnditiiɓe e konu maɓɓe aroowu, kuullewal e annabiijo fenaande ɓe ummaaki tawo haa laaɓa, ummoto haa laaɓa e ndoondi, mbi'a ñaawoore Alla yani ngam wallude anniyaaji maɓɓe, ɗum noon woto ngoonɗinee hay gooto e fenaande maɓɓe ɓurnde famɗude, ñaawoore maɓɓe cakkitiinde ndee leeltataa.

Sabu ɓe njokkoytoo, ɓe mbaɗa hakkeeji ɓurɗi bonde e Babilon Sirri, ɓe njogi hakke e ardude Babilon Sirri haa laaɓa e majjere e malkisal mum timmungal, sabu Alla waɗii e ɓerɗe maɓɓe ngam timminde anniya mum. Ɓe ngollodii, kala feeñɗo no gaño gollodii e sirlu nde ɓe pijata e laawol binndol maɓɓe, ɓe pijata geɗe maɓɓe e dow dingiral aduna, ɓe mbarto kala e nder kuule maɓɓe cuɓiiɗo woto janngu, waɗa geɗe mum no haanirta ngam timminde anniyaaji mboddi ndii. Sabu mboddi ndii ina hokkata kuule doole mum e jooɗorde mum, e laamu mawngu, Galleeji sappo ɗii njaɓata laamu no laamɓe waktu gooto e kuullewal ngal, ɗiin ina njogi miijo ngootaagu, ɓe njokkata doole maɓɓe e semmbe maɓɓe e kuullewal ngal, sabu ko noon Yahweh hokkiri ɓe ngam ɓe timmina. Toɗɗee hakkillaaji mon sukaaɓe am, jannge e am, njahdee e laabi am, sabu laabi am ko laabi Yahweh, Alla mawɗo duumotooɗo!

Paamee, toɗɗee hakkillaaji mon, sabu ɗii kuule janfiiɗe e laamɓe bonɓe ko e kuule Mboddi ngoni, laaminooɓe Babilon Sirri gila e pelle maɓɓe sirri gila e puɗɗagol mum. Neɗɗo fawotooɗo, fenatooɗo, hay neɗɗo bonɗo, ina yaha e hunduko pervers. Eey, ɓe mbiyata ko ɓe woɗɓe, sabu fof ko fenatooɓe, ɓe suuɗii e nder leñol kala, taariiji ɗii njaafamaama. Ɓe mbaɗata maandeeji e gite maɓɓe: ɓe mbaɗata maandeeji e koyɗe maɓɓe: ɓe njahata e junngo maɓɓe. Geɗe bonɗe ina ngoni e ɓerɗe maɓɓe: ɓe miijoto bonannde e kala saha, ɓe ngaddata kaaldigal. Ko ɗum waɗi malkisal maɓɓe arata yaawde: ɓe halkee e wakkati gooto tawa alaa ko fawata. Ɗee geɗe jeegom Joomiraawo ina wondi: eey, ruuhu mum ina wondi e jeeɗiɗi: Gite mawɗe, ɗemngal fenaande, e juuɗe liiɓɗe ƴiiƴam alaa hakke, ɓernde miijotoonde peeje bonɗe, koyɗe yaawɗe dogde e bonannde, Seedeejo fenaande haaloowo fenaande, e gaddoowo kaaldigal e miñiraaɓe.

E on mbunɗii? E on njaɓataa annditde maale maɓɓe ngam on njogoɗon e majjere mon nde on njiɗi, wonde ardiiɓe mon ɓee ko e yimɓe ronkinaaɓe ɓe njeyaa? E on paamataa wonde fijooɓe mon ɓee ko e ronkinaaɓe ɓe njeyaa? E on paamataa wonde ɓe ngalaa jikku gooto e mon, sabu heewɓe e maɓɓe mbayliima haa abadaa e nder jinnaare maɓɓe, yoga e maɓɓe ko gawri bonndi gila e jibinannde, so Iblisa woni baaba maɓɓe? Kono on suɓiima jooɗaade e niɓre e yiɗde mon, on mbeltoo e nder bakkaatuuji mon, on taccinoo e nder loope e kine ɗe ardii on haa e dame jahannama, ɗo heewɓe e majjere mawnintoo e mbonnateeri e mawninkinaare. Ko ɗum waɗi mi wiyi e nder ɓataake am feewde e Efesus mo mi hokki Pool: Finu aan ɗaaniido, ummo e maayde, miin, Almasiihu Iisaa, mi hokkat ma annoora! Ndeentoɗee non no njahdirton: wonaa no wuyɓe nii, kono no yimɓe hakkilɓe nii. Ko noon mi feññinirta ardiiɓe bonɓe Babilon Siriijo ɓee e noddaaɓe am suɓaaɓe e gooŋɗinɓe, ngam woto on naatu e fenaande maɓɓe, mbele on njaltii e mum fof, on ceertana e laabi mon fof, woto on tawtore ñawuuji mum.

Nanee konnguɗi am, paamon ko woni waɗde jooni, sabu jolngo maɓɓe ngam haawninde ngoo ina rewi e laawol. Miɗo haalda e Babilon Siriijo jooni, hay so tawii ko o muumo, o wumo, o bumɗo, omo haɓɓaa e jolokooje ngam ñaawoore makko. No ɓe njiilirnoo jolngo maɓɓe haawninoowo oo duuɓi keewɗi jooni, ɓe ndantini mbonniigu Babilon Siriijo rewrude e riba, e peeje fenaande goɗɗe, sabu hay kaalisiiji mum ko fodoore meere tan e ñamaale baɗaaɗe e juuɗe ardiiɓe mon gollantooɓe waylooɓe kaalis, Babilon Siriijo yeeytaama e juuɗe añɓe mum en e ɗee peeje bonɗe. E on paamataa banke kisal feccere e kaalis fiat? Janngon ngam anndude.

Wanaa mi hokkii on misaalu nde njippimi waylooɓe kaalis ɓee e suudu dewal? Kono, e nokku riiwde ɓe, on noddii ɓe ngam on hirana e galleeji mon, on ndokkii sukaaɓe mon e taaniraaɓe mon ngalu ngam timminde yiɗde mon bonnde, ko noon iwdi mon yeeyiraa ngam tabitinde mbonniigu mon mbele on keɓa wuurde e weltaare bakkaatu. Aan, Babilon Siriijo, aɗa wara ɓiɓɓe maa ɓe njibinaaka e weltaare mawnde e mawninkinaare, e warooɓe ɓe ngalaa aybe ina njogii ñaawoore mum en teeŋtinaande, ñaawoore maa ndee jonyotaako. Hay so tawii heddiiɓe seeɗa ina njaha e dow laana ko adii El Shaddai udda damal ngal, ko ɓuri heewde tawat ko ɓe accaa e ñaawoore sabu ɓe cuɓotoo ko heddaade yaasi ina njaɓɓa, ina nguura e weltaare.

Tigirigi, e nder waktu gooto mbonniigu mum yani e mum, ɓe ndacatataa. Nde tawnoo o hokki hoore makko golle e añɓe makko duumotooɓe, jooni ɓe ngaddan mo mbonniigu mum duumotoongu, sabu Babilon Siriijo ummataako kadi haa abadaa. Ee Babilon Siriijo, nde tawnoo a yeeyii sukaaɓe maa ngalu ngam ñamaale maa mbele aɗa jokki e weltaare bakkaatu (sabu hay kaalis maa ko riba), nde tawnoo a warii ɓe ngalaa aybe, a waɗii ko ɓuri ɗum heewde. Jooni mi hokkan ma fof e juuɗe maɓɓe, kadi Yahwe o feññinii ñaawoore laabi ɗiɗi e dow maa, aan Babilon Siriijo. Heewɓe e sukaaɓe maa neldatee e kampuuji maɓɓe golle e janngingol, heewɓe njahrat e leyɗeele janane sabu naree ndee yamiraama jogaade leyɗeele e yimɓe saliiɓe mi, miin Yeshua HaMasiihu, ngam suɓaade Barabbas, Almasiihu maɓɓe fenaande oo. Heewɓe njahrat e maande daaba maɓɓe, ɓe ngeeyii wuurndeeje maɓɓe e iwdi maɓɓe e lepte duumotooɗe ummoraade e Alla duumotooɗo, ngam weltaare bakkaatu e waktu juutɗo.

Puccitee yimɓe Babilon Siriijo, hay onon jooɗiiɓe e niɓre e majjere huuɓtidinnde e mbonniigu mon arayngu nguu. Yeeso gite mon, e jaɓgol woote mon, e laawol weybitaare mon, leñol mon fof yanii yeeso gite mon. On ngalaa kadi baawɗe walla jawdi ngam haɓde e wolde, sabu miin, hay Almasiihu Iisaa, mi ittii doole mon konu, mi hokkii ɗum e juuɗe añɓe mon. Gure mon mawɗe ɗe ngannduno-ɗon jooni ina njooɗii e mbonniigu bonannde e baasal, ɗo fijooɓe mon e onon fof mawnintoo e mawninkinaare. Edomiyankooɓe ɓee ina njaltini gila ko ɓooyi gollorɗe mum en e leyɗeele mon, on ngalaa baawɗe gollirde ɗe njogino-ɗon, hay e geɗe gollirteeɗe ngam waɗde ko kaanɗon ngam haɓde e koode mon ɗe njoɓataa.

Sabu on njahata ko e wolde haa e wolde, on mbarii leyɗeele ɗee fof, ko e janane e nder leydi. Sabu e mon, Babilon Siriijo, yaltii ñawu konuuji boɗeeji, hay naatni e winndere ndee fof liwliwal boɗewal naree boɗeere mawnde ndee, sabu on ngollaniima ronkinaaɓe ɓee e sirlu gila e fuɗɗoode, on njokki haa hannde. Babilon Siriijo, on ndokkii e yiɗde mon jaɓgol laamɓe mon Seytaaneeɓe ɓee, ɓe nganndi no feewi peeje maɓɓe, ɓe nganndi no feewi wonde ina ngoni e laawol mbonnude leñol mon mawngol ngol ngannduno-ɗon e onon. Sabu on njogii aybe yiiyam seniiɓe, yiiyam şehidiiɓe ina wulla e leydi ngam ñaawoore mon, so tawii Yahwe ñaaawataa on, o foti tuubde e leƴƴi ɗi o ñaaawi e yontaaji ɓennuɗi, hay e ɓeen gollotooɓe e reentinoore ñaawoore mon araynde nde on calii no feewi.

E mon, Babilon Siriijo, ummorii mbonniigu addaangu e leyɗeele janane e juuɗe konuuji boɗeeji, nde Edomiyankooɓe ɓee mbeltini e gollotooɓe ɓee e liwliwal maɓɓe tookeewal, hay e bayyinaango maɓɓe bonngo ngo kaalis mum ko e juuɗe Gaara Boɗeejo yalti. Sabu wuyɓe yontaaji ɓennuɗi, hay ardiiɓe gollorɗe mon e yontaaji ɓennuɗi, (hay e ɓeen ɓe ngeeynoo-ɗon iwdi mon) ko kamɓe ngoni sabaabu ñawu konuuji boɗeeji. Babilon Siriijo e caɗeele mum ñamaale ina kaalisna, ina wallita konuuji boɗeeji e golle mum en gila e fuɗɗoode, Babilon Siriijo ina jeyaa e caɗeele mum en gila e fuɗɗoode nde Gaara Boɗeejo oo tabitini hoore mum e winndere ndee. Hay henndu yiite naree ndee ummorii ko e Gaara Boɗeejo, sabu ko kamɓe ngoni ronkinaaɓe yahdooɓe e mon.

E nder sirlu Babilon, ardiiɓe maɓɓe "waylooji" ɓee njannginaama e sirlu, sabu omo jooɗii e ndiyam keewɗam, ɓe neldaa e sirlu e leyɗeele janane, hay e Gog e Magog, waylooji maɓɓe ina mawni, ina njogii doole, ina njogii bonannde, jooni ina njuɓɓini leydi ndii fof. Ɓe njaajni liwliwal maɓɓe Seytaaneewal e leyɗeele fuɗnaange, ɓe njoofniri fof e laabi maɓɓe. Ɓe njalti e Babilon Siriijo, ɓe ngonti ñawu e leydi ndii, e nder ɗum fof sirlu Babilon ina fena, debbo jeenay mawɗo oo ina wiyoo ina haalda e innde am, ina wiyee Kiristaan. Ndee liɓtugol bonngol laamu ɓooyngu nguu yahii e winndere ndee fof, jooni ina yaha e tolno mum cakkitiinde, ɓe njaɓɓotoo laamu maɓɓe, kono ko ngam gollanaade anniyaaji duumotooɗi Yahwe tan, sabu goonga ronkinaaɓe ɓee ina ndilla, kulol ina nanngi ɓerɗe maɓɓe bonɗe sabu ɓe nganndi ñaawoore maɓɓe ɓooynde ndee ina yettii ɓe. Sabu aan Babilon Siriijo, e nder jeenay e jeenay maa, aɗa ɗaana e añam.

Ndaa! Debbo jeenay mawɗo oo, hay Babilon Siriijo leñol dawlaaji keewɗi, ko debbo oo holliraa Yuhanna Feññinoowo, hay debbo oo siwɗo yiiyam Seniiɓe, e yiiyam şehidiiɓe am, ñaawoore maa ina yettii ma. Sabu no ngon-ɗaa e wuurde e weltaare, tawi ardiiɓe maa ina mbonna leƴƴi goɗɗi e jaɓgol maa e kaalis maa, aɗa jogii aybe no gawri Edomiyankeeri ndii nii, nde ngeeynoo-ɗaa iwdi maa ngam weltaare bakkaatu. A kaalisnii, a wallitii, a sirlanii liɓtugol leƴƴi keewɗi! Leƴƴi fof ɗi konuuji boɗeeji liɓti e ballal Gaara Boɗeejo, njoofaama, njoofaama, njoofaama, njoofaama, njoofaama e laawol ɓurngol bonde. Seniiɓe wonnooɓe e ɗee leyɗeele, a waɗii ɓe şehidiiɓe, hay miliyoŋaaji keewɗi. E nder ɗum fof aɗa yama, aɗa huutoroo safaarooji Seytaane e mawninde, aɗa sikka so tawii ɗum yettaaki ma, hay safaarooji dokkaaɗi ma ngam maayde maa. Babilon Siriijo, a sadakii yonta sukaaɓe timmuɗo, jooni ko worɓe fotɓe haɓde, ɓe mbaawnoo daɗndude ma, kono jooni ina nguura e laabi gure maa nyidduɗe, ina njuutii e ɗee safaarooji, ɓe ngonti zombiiji maayɓe yahooɓe, nde ɗee safaarooji ina ñola ɓalli maɓɓe tawi ina nguuri.

Jooni ñaawoore maa timmunde ari, Babilon Siriijo, a wontii yitere wonande fof, kono aɗa jokki e majjere e bumɗo, aɗa wiyoo e ɓerɗe maa so tawii musiiba yettaaki ma, hay so tawii aɗa weltanoo nde ardiiɓe maa ngaddi musiiba e daande maayo leƴƴi keewɗi e nder koode maɓɓe bonɗe, ɗe njiɗnoo-ɗaa e nder tele. Ko noon on pecciraa e hakkunde añɓe mon, ɓe njiɗii jawdi wolde ko adii misilaaji yani. Sabu foolde Babilon Siriijo ko tabitinaande, ko Yahwe e hoore mum rokki, hay huunde dartintaa mbonniigu mum timmungu, o ummataako kadi haa abadaa.

Ko miin Iisaa Nasaraat.

Ko miin woni on mo Alla duumotooɗo neldi ngam daɗndude on ko adii musiiba e ñaawoore yani.

Ko miin woni Joomiraawo mawɗo Joomiraaɓe e Laamɗo Laamɓe!

Miɗo ara ngam hisnude gooŋɗinɓe e tikkere, sabu ko noon fodoore Yahwe woniri e nanondiral am jam.

Ko miin woni on arayɗo e gooŋɗinɓe am, noddaaɓe, e suɓaaɓe, hay Ɓanndu am e Eklesiya am, hay e jommbaajo am.

Miin, Yeshua HaMasiihu, mi neldirii ɗee konnguɗi e maccuɗo am, e maccuɗo am ɗee konnguɗi neldaa.

Nanee daande am sukaaɓe am, njaɓee noddaango am, njanngon e am, njaɓee hakkilantaagal am. Woto on keɓee, sabu mettineeje jibinannde ina njokki jooni, kono ndee ñaawoore wonaa ngam mon. Mi ɓuri ɓadaade on henndu mon arooru, feeñde am ina ɗoo. Tigirigi on tawat ko on ngoni e jooɗorde laamu Yahwe, ɗo on mbaɗtee haa timma e Joomiraawo mon, Almasiihu Iisaa, on nanndat e am haa abadaa, on ngonndat e am haa abadaa, miɗo yiɗi on no feewi, noddaaɓe am, suɓaaɓe, e gooŋɗinɓe.
