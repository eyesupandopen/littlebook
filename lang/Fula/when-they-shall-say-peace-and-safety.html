Ko Welɗi e ko Haɗɗi

Nde Ɓe Njaynotoo Jam e Kisal!

Gila e Yeshua HaMashiach, on woni kadi Almasiihu Iisaa:

Nanɗee Konnguɗi Yeshua HaMashiach, on mo Yahweh neldi wonande on Almasiihu mon, Laamɗo mon, Joomiraawo mon, e Mawɗo Limaam en mon haa poomaa e Laawol Melkisedek, ɓe ndewtonoo ɓerɗe mon e ɗoftaare Konnguɗi am sabu miɗo ari ngam rentinde goonɗinɓe e nder Eklesiya am, e kadi Resndaaɗo am, (e kala jiɗɗo nana, woni o ɓadiiɗo maa o woɗɗuɗo) kadi laawol gootol e majjere mawnde jooni gollotoonde no feewi e aduna oo fof, nde bonnata tan so haɗoowo oo ittaama ɓooyataa, e dow tampere! Malla mi woyaani no puɗoowo werlortoo dabre mum yeeso colli? Yoo onon ɓe keewaa goonɗinal!

Nii woni, miɗo anndina on kadi ko njanngetoomi yummiraaɓe am nde ngarmi Isra'iila miin Jawgel Alla ngel, ɗum non nanɗee Konnguɗi am! Sabu ummoto Almasiihu'en fenaanɗeeɓe, e annabaaɓe fenaanɗeeɓe, ɓe kolliroytoo maale mawɗe e kaawniiɗe, haa so tawii ina waɗi, ɓe njuurnitoo hay suɓaaɓe ɓee. Ndaaree, mi anndinino on gila ko adii. Ko ɗum waɗi so ɓe mbari on, 'Ndaaree, omo woni e ladde,' woto njaltuɗon: 'Ndaaree, omo woni e nokkuuji cuuɗiiɗi,' woto ngoonɗine. Sabu no maayel yaltirta funnaange, yiyee haa hirnaange, non kadi garol Ɓii Neɗɗo oo wonirta! Miɗo anndina on noon wonde feeññere am ndee ɓadiima, nde arat e tampere e doole, mi salotaako jeyi am mo njeernoomi e jinngol am, woni resndaaɗo am goonɗinɗo e terɗe am, woni eklesiya am goonɗinɗo mo njiɗirmi yiɗde nduumiinde! Onon ko jeyi am!

Nii woni, giɗaaɓe am, Almasiihu'en fenaanɗeeɓe ummiima, ɓe keewi no feewi. Kono jooni, wakkatiiji gummbal ngal njettiima, torra Yaaquuba yettiima, kala keddiiɗo e ɓiɓɓe Aadama e dow leydi ina foti suɓaade suɓngo duumingol. Sabu gummbal ngal hokkiraama laamu e dow leydi wondude e gaynaaɓe mum ɗiɗo bonɓe ɓee, woni kullel ngel yaltata e maayo e kullel ngel yaltata e leydi, on woni kadi annabiijo maɓɓe fenaanɗeejo oo. Malla on ndaɗndoto hoore mon e majjere mawnde ndee e dow ɗoftagol daande am? Malla on etoto rewrude e aduna oo e majjere mum e fenaande mum haa e halkaare timmunde e dow salaare daande am, hay so mi arii mi rentinii on e majjere mawnde jooni nanngunde aduna oo? Malla on accii ɓerɗe mon e goonɗinal mon njiimtee e majjere mawnde ndee ngam hoolaare fenaande?

Nii woni, hay huunde waawaa waylude bayyinaale kiita baɗaaɗe e Yahweh e hoore mum e dow Babilon Cuuɗiiɗo, kiita ka hokkiraama e juuɗe am doole ngam udditde so goonɗinɓe ɓee mooftaama, haɗoowo oo ittaama, ɗum waɗat tampere no fodaaka nii! Hay kiitaaji ka anndinaama e rentinnde yimɓe Babilon Cuuɗiiɗo, rentinnde nde ɓe calii haa timmi, ɓe njaɓɓi e dow ŋakkeende goonɗinal. Hay rentinɗe am keewɗe, keewɗe, baɗaaɗe e kunndule annabaaɓe am heewɓe ɓe mbayyini Konnguɗi rentinnde ɗii no feewi gila duuɓi keewɗi jooni. Rentinɗe ɗe njoofmi, e nokku maɓɓe bayyinaale njooɗiima jooni, hay bayyinaale Yahweh, e Yeshua HaMashiach, bayyinaale ɗe hay gooto waawaa haɗde walla waylude. Sabu ko bayyinaa e doole e jooɗorde Yahweh, hay gooto waawaa waylude, sabu noon laabi Alla Duumiiɗo Nuunɗal ngal nii, on baɗoowo kala huunde e laawol yiɗde mum.

Nii woni, miɗo wi'a on goonɗinɓe am, no mbi'irumi Sim'uunu Biirusa e waktu sadak am nii: Sim'uunu, Sim'uunu, ndaar, Seytaane yiɗi ma, ngam seertude ma no gawri nii. Kono mi du'aniima ma, ngam goonɗinal maa woto taasu: ko ɗum waɗi so a tuubii, semmbin banndiraaɓe maa. Nii woni, miɗo ara e mon e ɗee balɗe cakkitiiɗe (sabu waktu oo ɓuri timmude) ngam rentinde on no nanndi nii ngam mbaawon jogaade hoore mon e laawol, miɗo yamira on onon jalbooji no jayngol Aduna nii ngam semmbinde banndiraaɓe mon e semmbindirde hakkunde mon e ɗee Konnguɗi e yiɗde goonɗinal e ɗoftaare ngam on fof mbaawon jogaade e hisnude hoore mon e laawol, ko ɗum waɗi ɗoftoɗon daande am, njahdon e maggo.

Ndaaree! Ina winndaa: Ngam ɗuum, Alla neldat e maɓɓe majjere mawnde, ngam ɓe ngoonɗina fenaande: ngam ɓe fof njahdaneeɓe ɓe ngoonɗinaali goonga, kono ɓe njiɗi ko boni. Malla on njaɓan ndee majjere nde wonaa mon, giɗaaɓe am? Goonga, goonga, miɗo wi'a eklesiya am, malla on ndoggan haa e juuɗe ɗii fenaanɗeeɓe? Hay ɓeen neldaaɓe ngam halkaare mon e gummbal ngal e hoore mum? Ko ɗum waɗi, giɗaaɓe am, njokkee yaltude e hakkunde maɓɓe, ceertuɗon! Woto naatnon e golle maɓɓe bonɗe e ko ɓe njiɗi sabu heewɓe njaɓɓiino bilki maɓɓe e kuule maɓɓe, on mo ɓe cuɓii jooni oo ina haala no gummbal nii, omo foda fodoore mawnde, omo waɗa konnguɗi mawɗi e hoore mum, heewɓe kadi ndewat e laabi makko bonɗi, heewɓe njuurnitoyee, nanngee, ko ɗum waɗi ngartiree e daande am, nanɗee konnguɗi am, ndoggee to am giɗaaɓe am, woto nguurtee nano, walla nyaamo, mi hisnan on e newe am duumiiɗe ɗe hay gooto waawaa ittude on e nanngere am doole ndee.

Ko waɗi ndeen on accata ɓerɗe mon torree on, onon goonɗinɓe am goonga, hay ɓeen ɓe njiɗirmi yiɗde nduumiinde, hay ɓeen ɓe gummbal ngal yiɗi nanngude e majjere e kiita ka wonaa mon. Ko ɗum waɗi, woto naatnon e majjere maɓɓe, njaltuɗon e hakkunde maɓɓe, ceertuɗon! Onon ɓe nguurti e majjere mawnde ndee, miɗo yamira on woto ngartiree hay seeɗa. Ndaaree! Hay Yahweh, Alla Mawɗo Duumiiɗo oo, hay Yeshua HaMashiach nelataa walla nelii hay feere politik walla laamɓe aduna ngam safrude caɗeele aduna oo e oo sahaa. Hay caɗeele garooje e kiita e dow yimɓe leydi ndii gila e Yahweh e hoore mum, kiita ka hay gooto waawaa daɗde, so wonaa goonɗinɓe e Almasiihu Iisaa. Faamee no feewi ko mbi'irmi Pool winnda e Efeesiyaaɓe e nder ɓataake mo mbi'irmi o winnda e innde am feewde e eklesiya am giɗaaɗo oo:

Ragare, banndiraaɓe am, semmbiniree e Joomiraawo, e doole doole makko. Ɓoornee kaɓirɗe Alla fof, ngam mbaawon daraade e njiimaandi ibliis. Sabu en kaɓataa e terɗe e jinngol, kono e laamuuji, e doole, e laamɓe aduna, laamɓe niɓɓere aduna oo, e ruuhuuji bonɗi wonɗi e nokkuuji toowɗi. Faamee noon giɗaaɓe am wonde konnguɗi am ngalaa waylude, ko waɗi ndeen miin, Almasiihu Iisaa Joomiraawo mon ummitinaaɗo oo, mi neldat on laamɗo leydi, walla laamɗo walla laamɗo kaɗoowo kiitaaji garooji ɗii? No Yahweh, mo yiɗde mum timminirmi haa laaɓi, mo waawaa fenaade, waawirta luutndirde e hoore mum e peeje mum duumiiɗe ɗe hokkiraami haa timmi ngam timminde?

Sabu laamɓe aduna fof njaɓii, njoɓi doole maɓɓe e gummbal ngal, sabu noon hokkiraama ɓe ngam timminde sabu hay añɓe Yahweh ina ɗoftoo daande mum, ɓe timminat yiɗde mum ndee nde hokkiraa ɓe e nder ndee yontere juutnde no feewi, nde woni e arde e dow leydi e tikkere mawnde! Sabu wakkatiiji tikkere Jawgel ngel e tikkere Alla Baawɗo oo njettiima, alaa ko waawi haɗde, walla lettude hay seeɗa ko woni e udditeede ɓooyataa caggal nde mooftumi goonɗinɓe am e dow kammuuli. Nii woni, miɗo wulla! Hol baawɗo daraade e yiɗde Yahweh duumiinde, on woni MIIN MAWƊO?!

Kono, faamee e nder duuɓi am moƴƴere mi jannginii Eklesiya am yo du'ano laamɓe maɓɓe aduna, miɗo anndi laamɓe maɓɓe aduna ko bonɓe e jikku, ngam e du'aawuuji seniiɓe bonannde ndee haɗee (haɗoowo am oo ina haɗa haa jooni), ɗii laamɓe mbaawi suɓaade ko gollata ngam moƴƴere e cellal yimɓe njiɗɓe Alla, noon kadi duuɓi am moƴƴere ngonnoo, hay so tawii torraaji ina keewi. Hay so tawii, du'aawu nuunɗuɗo ina heewi nafoore, faamee wonde alaa laamɓe aduna hokkiraaɓe ngam hisnude leydi ndii haa mi ara e konu mawngu laamɓe e Limaam en duumiiɓe laamiiɓe leydi ndii e nuunɗal e dow yamiroore am sabu miin ko mi Joomiraawo Joomiraaɓe, miin ko mi Laamɗo Laamɓe, miin ko mi Mawɗo Limaam en haa poomaa e Laawol Melkisedek.

Faamee no feewi, tawa alaa ko luutndii, alaa laamɓe aduna mbaawɓe addude jam e leydi. Alaa laamɓe aduna mbaawɓe addude kisal e leydi. Alaa laamɓe aduna mbaawɓe artirde jawdi e leydi. Hay so tawii, ɓe podat jam e kisal e jawdi artinaandi, faamee wonde ɓe ngalaa doole ngam hokkude ko ɓe podi koo, hay so tawii ɓe njokkat jawdi aduna ndi alaa nafoore ndii e ɓeen ɓe njaɓata maande kullel ngel, hay njoɓdi bonannde ndii, nde ittetee e maɓɓe haa timmi e kiita! Ko ɗum waɗi woto ndewee e maɓɓe sabu kala dewɗo e maɓɓe fof, e joofnirde fof, ina foti jaɓde maande kullel ngel, ɓeen baɗooɓe ɗum kadi, njahrat e yiite duumiingol, ngu nyifataa, ngu woni lepte, aduna oo e jawdi mum ndi alaa nafoore ndii ina majja. Gummbal ngal ummoto haa timmi e kulle mum, kullel ngel yaltata e leydi ngel heɓtii tooke mum baɗeteeɗe e ñawre mboɗi ngam sowde tooke mum, njamndi jillondirat e loope, kono ɓe nanngondirtaa.

Nii woni, ina winndaa, ina winndaa no feewi e seedanfaagu feeññere am, hay Feeññere Iisaa Almasiihu ngam kala jiɗɗo yiyde e nanaade, e ɓernde lewlewnde ngam faamde, noon kadi Yuhanna winndi: Malaa'ikaajo oo wiyi kam: Ko waɗi a haawnii? Mi anndin ma sirlu debbo oo, e kullel ngel roondii mo ngel, ngel jogii ko'ɗe jeeɗiɗi e kalle sappo. Kullel ngel njiynooɗa ngel, woodnoo, woodaani; ngel ummotoo e lugge nde alaa les, ngel yaha e halkaare: yimɓe hoɗɓe e leydi ɓee, ɓe inɗe mum en mbinndaaka e deftere nguurndam gila e sosngo aduna, njaɓɓoto so ɓe njiyii kullel ngel woodnoongel, woodaani, kono ina woodi.

Ko ɗum waɗi, sukaaɓe am, njaɓee janngude, miɗo anndina on hay so tawii laamu gummbal ngal waawaa ummaade haa timmi (sabu haɗoowo oo haɗat haa haɗoowo oo haɗataa, hay so goonɗinɓe e Almasiihu njaɓɓaama e dow kammuuli) tooñooɓe ɓee njaɓaama yo mbaɗtu ngam tampere maɓɓe, Alla kadi ina nelda yimɓe hoɗɓe e leydi e nder salaare majjere mawnde. Kono ɗee geɗe ngonaa mon onon goonɗinɓe am cuɓaaɓe, miin, Yeshua HaMashiach, miɗo bayyina, miɗo seedtoo e ngoo goonga e ɗee konnguɗi ɗi neldirmi e nelal am e maccuɗo am ngam janngeede ngam deeƴre e ɗoftaare e kala mo ɗee konnguɗi poti. Nii woni, miɗo naamnoo onon ɓe njiɗi ndee majjere e dow goonga am duumiiɗo oo, ko waɗi on kaawnii? Malla on cuɓoto fenaande gaynaaɓe bonɓe ɓee e dow goonga e deeƴre nde ruuhu annabaagal addata?

Jooni ɓe cuɓiima hoore maɓɓe Masiya maɓɓe fenaanndeejo, kanko woni Barabbas, o mahante laamu maɓɓe, ngayka ummoriika e maayo ngoo ina jokki e fiyde fijo mum e oo mawɗo mum puuyɗo, tee maa ɓe dañ heen nafoore, ɓe ngaddana Babilon sirlu oo halkaare timmunde, sabu ko e fenaande ɓe ngollirta hare, ko noon kadi jikku maɓɓe woniri.

Leydi ndii maa dimmbita no feewi, maa nde feccoy e hakkunde, ɗo wuro ngoo wonnoo, ndiyam maa heddo, ngo fecca ɗum ɗiɗi, maa ngo yani haa laaɓi e ñaawoore mum ɗiɗmere ndee. Sabu ɓeen baawnooɓe rewrude e Iisaa Almasiihu no Joomiraawo maɓɓe nii, jooni ɓe naatii e juuɗe fenaannde, sibu ɓe njiɗi ko wuurde e miijooji maɓɓe, ɓe cikki wonde no woniri hannde nii, ko noon wonnoo ñannde heñun, tee goonga oo laamɗo mawɗo oo maa waɗam mawɗo kadi! Kono, aan Babilon sirlu, a waɗataa mawɗo kadi, tee aybeeji maa ina keddii, a daɗataa ñaawoore maa nde woni e maa ndee.

Ko min, Yesuwa HaMasiyaak, kanko woni Laamɗo laamɓe e Joomiraawo joomiraaɓe, mi rokkaani laamɗo gooto e leydi ndii e oo sahaa, sibu alaa laamu goɗngu ngu Yaawe sosi, so wonaa Laamu Asamaan, nguu min, Yesuwa HaMasiyaak, ko mi Laamɗo laamɓe e Joomiraawo joomiraaɓe! Paamee noon, kala neɗɗo rokkuɗo hoore mum oo fenaannde, acci Joomiraawo mum e kisnoowo mum Iisaa Almasiihu, maa acciree yiite laɓɓinoowo, sibu on ngonii e juuɗe Almasiihu fenaanndeejo, kanko kadi woni Masiya maɓɓe fenaanndeejo. Ko ɗuum waɗi ceergal cakkitiingal ngal ari e jamaanu am moƴƴere, keewɗi fenaama, ngayka ummoriika e leydi ndii nawii ɗum en no nanngaaɓe nii. Hay so tawii keewɗi maa njaltu e fenaande mum, naata e nguurndam haa abada, maa naamnoo Yaquuba torra ngam itta ɗum en e miijooji bonɗi so ɓe caliima noddaango am ngam tuubde ɗoon e ɗoon e nder ɗii seppooji cakkitiɗi.

Miɗo anndina on noon, wonde Yaawe hokkii buunel cakkitiingel ngel ina fiyee, hay buunel am cakkitiingel ngel e jamaanu am moƴƴere, tee buunel ngel maa fiyee. Sibu waktu oo arii, o woni e mon, yiɗɓe am, ɗo njillotoomi iwde e asamaan e sawtawol, e daande maleyka mawɗo e buunel Alla, maayɓe e nder Almasiihu maa ummo arta, onon wonɓe e Almasiihu maa njahdondir e maɓɓe e dow weeyo, ko noon kadi maa ngoniron e am haa abada, yiɗɓe am, mi accataa gooŋɗinɓe am e yiite laɓɓinoowo, sibu laɓɓingol maɓɓe ngol timminiri mi e yiɗde am e maɓɓe, sibu ɓe ngarii e am e yiɗde maɓɓe hay so mi feeñnaama e maɓɓe.

Paamee noon, yiɗɓe am, waktu oo yettima ngam jawdi ndii ummoo no feewi, ngam hare e ñaawoore njaha no feewi, ngam ngayka ummoriika e maayo ngoo e ngayka ummoriika e leydi ndii ummoo no feewi e jawdi mum en. So mi ŋakkintaano balɗe ɗee, hay neɗɗo gooto wuuraano, ko ɗuum waɗi njangu maɓɓe mbonngu nguu maa ari e aduna oo fof, ɓe kalka Babilon sirlu oo no feewi. Woto ngon e miijooji maɓɓe e fenaandeeji maɓɓe, nde ɓe ndonki yettude hoore maɓɓe, ɓe mbiya e yeeso ñaawooje Yaawe e tikkere Jibinaaɗo oo, “jam e kisal” “jam e kisal”! Sabu ko mi Yesuwa HaMasiyaak, mo ittan jam e kisal e leydi ndii haa timma yontere capanɗe jeeɗiɗi Daneyel, nde sositam laamu am no feewi e leydi ndii. Ko ɗuum waɗi, woto ngon e miijooji maɓɓe.

Ko miin neldata ɗee Konnguɗi e juuɗe nelaaɗo am e maccuɗo am, tee e juuɗe nelaaɗo am e maccuɗo am ɗee Konnguɗi neldaa.

Ndaaree! Waktu oo yettima ngam Joomiraawo mon jalbina teddungal mum e mawnikinaagal mum e dow asamaan ngam yimɓe fof njiyta, mi mooɓtan gooŋɗinɓe am fof e nder Almasiihu hay e nder leyɗeele ɗee kala, nde peeñatmi e dow weeyo ngam mooɓtude gooŋɗinɓe e nder Almasiihu, tee feeñde am ndee maa yiye e asamaan no maayel yaltirta e fuɗnaange, yiyee e hirnaange nii, tee mi jaɓataa!

Ko miin woni Yeesu Almasiihu, garoowo ñaawoyde e haɓde e dow leydi, laamu am nguu alanaa ɗum joofnirde.

Ko miin woni on mo MIIN MAWƊO oo waɗi, sahaaji ɗii njottike.

Ko miin woni Muusu Kabiila Yahuuda, sahaaji am ɗii njottike.

Kala huunde fuu heddoto haa pooma, Eeey e Aamiin!

Gila e Yahweh, Alla mawɗo duumotooɗo oo:

Nanee, nanee konnguɗi Alla duumotooɗo, jom Battedeeŋ! Sahaaji ñaawoore Yaaquuba njottike e dow leydi, sahaaji coññal ngal njottike. Ndeen e law, tawa hay gooto fawaaki heen hakkille, ɓiyam, Almasiihu Yeesu, woni Yeesu Almasiihu, feeñoyan e dow duule, haa maayɓe e wuuruɓe ɓee e nder Almasiihu ummoo e leydi ndii e nguurndam duumotooɗam, tawaɓe nattataa, ngam daraade e tagaale kesle haa abada, hay so tawii ko e nder jooɗorde laamu am ndee e hoore mum, woni sahaa mo njiɗnoomi, mo ciftormi gila e duuɓi duuɓi ɓennuɗi ɗii.

Ko ɗum waɗi, njaɓee Yeesu Almasiihu sabu miin mi halfinii yamiroore ndee, sahaa o kam e hoore mum ina arta, ñalawma e waktu goonga oo anndaama jooni e nder kammuuli, hay so tawii ɗum feññinaaka no haanirta nii e dow leydi. Ko noon kadi sahaaji buutol cakkitiingol ngol njottiri, ɓiɓɓe leɗɗe arandeeji ɗii mooɓte, jom galle keso oo naatondira e jom suudu mum ñiiɓɗo oo e alkawal duumotoongal, ɓe timmina peeje am duumotooɗe ɗee, Alkawal Kesal Yeesu Almasiihu ngal tabitinte no haanirta nii haa abada, sabu alkule am ɗee fof ko Eeey e Aamiin!

Ñiiɓɓe e nder Almasiihu ɓee ndaroto yeeso jooɗorde ñaawoore Joomiraaɗo mum en, njoɓee golle mum en, tawa ko moƴƴe walla ko bonɗe, kono kamɓe fof ɓe keɓii nguurndam duumotooɗam. Heewɓe njaltata e njoɓdi mawndi ndi meeɗaa naatde e ɓernde neɗɗo no haanirta nii, hono geɗe ɗe mbaɗanannoomi ɓe. Sabu Almasiihu Yeesu waɗat ɓe Laamɓe e Liman'en yeeso jooɗorde laamu am, ɓe ndona no haanirta nii jooɗorɗe laamu maɓɓe e baawɗe maɓɓe gila e Yeesu Almasiihu e nder ndonu ngun mo ndonirmi mo, ngam o rennda ɗum e ñiiɓɓe ɓee, e noddaaɓe ɓee, e teskaaɓe ɓee, e goongɗuɓe ɓee.

Ko noon mbiyrumi hoɗɓe e leydi ndii yo nɗoofto, njaɓa konngol Yeesu Almasiihu no haanirta nii sabu omo ara ñaawoyde e haɓde, seerndude cakkitiinde ndee ina arta. Kono gadano, sahaaji yontere capanɗe jeeɗiɗiire Danyel ɗee timminte, Yeesu Almasiihu oo, woni Yeesu Almasiihu, ina ara e leydi ngam hisnude heddiiɓe e iwdi Yaaquuba sabu MIIN woni MIIN, hay YHVH Alla Mawɗo duumotooɗo oo, holi wonɗo nannduɗo e am? Fotondirɗo e am alaa, kadi goonga alaa fof so tawii omo wooda.

Ko MIIN woni on rokkunooɗo peeje am duumotooɗe ɗe cuuɗoomi e nder ɓernde am haa ɗe peeñananoo ɓiyam, Almasiihu Yeesu, Gundo Mawngo Alla ngoo peeñtinaama, omo ɓadii timmineede. Mi rokkii kadi Linjiila duumotooɗo oo ɓiyam Almasiihu Yeesu, mi peeñtini ɗum e makko e nelaaɗo am cuɓaaɗo oo, gartirɗo e hoolaare e noddaango mum duumotoongo ngoo, omo timmina peeje am duumotooɗe ɗee e les njiimaandi Laamɗo Laamɓe e Joom Joomiraaɓe, mo o jeyaa fof.

Ko MIIN woni MIIN MAWƊO oo, mbiyee ɓe ko MIIN MAWƊO oo neli ɗeeɗoo Konnguɗi, sahaaji ɗii ko jooni.

Ndaaree! Ndaaree, ndaaree sabu waktu oo wonii e mon, nanee, udditee noppi mon haa mbaawon nande sawta buutol ngol, sabu buutol cakkitiingol ngol yamiraama yo wutte, waktu wuttugol magol wonii e mon, woto tan njaatnon e nder mbeelu mawngu.

On njiɗii? Yo waɗee noon e waktu toɗɗaaɗo mo maandiniimi e nder ɓernde am hade leydi ndii tageteede.

MIIN woni MIIN, Almasiihu Yeesu omo hebilanii waɗde peeñgol mum hay e oon waktu mo cuɓinoomi e wasiya am e duuɓi duuɓi ɓennuɗi, hay waktu timmuɗo rokkaaɗo ngam sottugol timmungol ɓiɓɓe leɗɗe arandeeji peewal ngal! Ndaaree! Sahaaji ɗii ko jooni!

Ko noon Alla mawɗo duumotooɗo oo wiyri, Eeey e Aamiin!
