Seedeeji ɗiɗi: Eklesiya
E nder Golleeji Yuhanna Wahyudanke
Juko 02, 2024

Tilimɓe 144 000: Ngannditee Fenanndeeji Maɓɓe

Toɗɗee noppi mon e daande am, nanee ganndal am, sabu ganndal ina ewnoo e daande toownde e laabi mawɗi: on nani daande am? Ko miin woni Yeshua HaMashiach, ɓii Yahwe, garɗo e ɓanndu, miin woni Jawgel Alla ittoowengel aybeeji aduna. Faamee wonde aduna mo wuurdonton oo wonaa natal fenaande ngal jannginooɓe mon Yesuit en pentiri ngam majjinde on. Heewɓe e mon mbaɗaama e fenanndeeji Yesuit en, haa nganndon ko feewti e janngude. Ndee hare e goonga feewnaama ngam mbaylaade hakkillaaji yimɓe mbaɗa ɗe ɗoftooɗe Seyɗaani, tee ina anniyii ittude e anndude doole neɗɗo e suɓaade ko yiɗi, ngam neɗɗo rewa e ko jannginaa koo tawa alaa miijo.

Taariika goonga oo waawaa faamireede e fenanndeeji filosof en Yesuit en, sabu kamɓe ko fenooɓe, ina njeeyda fenaande e nder njoofka mawka. Deftere Alla ina laaɓti e haala kaa, tee ɗerewol Roomnaaɓe ina hollita goonga oo no laaɓiri: Sabu tikare Alla ina yalta gila e dow, feewde e kala bonannde e tooñannge yimɓe, jaggitooɓe goonga oo e nder tooñannge.

Mbela ɗee konnguɗi naatii e ɓerɗe mon? Yimɓe ina njaggitoo goonga oo e anniya e nder bonannde e tooñannge, tee tikare Yahwe ina woni e dow waɗooɓe ɗum ɓee. Ko ɗum waɗi jaŋde mon ndee laatii meere sabu fenanndeeji maɓɓe.

Kamɓe ɓe mbaɗii ɗum ngam heblanaade laawol njoofka mawka garoowa kaa, e ngam laamu Seyɗaani njoɓtine ngam laamaade e njoofka e fenanndeeji mawɗi. Fenanndeeji ɗi ɓe kaalani on e jaŋde ɗii ina njogii paandaale ngam wumnude yimɓe heewɓe ɓee e goonga oo: Alaa aliyen en, so wonaa malaa'ikaaji njippiiɗi, feewde e weendu yiite ngam haɓɓeede e wuleede haa abada. Woto ngoonɗinee fenanndeeji maɓɓe ɗii, ndogee faade e am, miin Almasiihu Iisaa, sabu e nder am ina woodi goonga timmuɗo, baawɗo rimɗinde on. Mbele miɗo haalana on ɗee geɗe ngam woto on njoofnee e wondiiɓe e Seyɗaani, jaɓooɓe maande mbeewa garoowo oo.

Taariika ina faamiree so rewraama e goongaaji tabitinaaɗi e nder Deftere Alla e defte goɗɗe baɗɗe no 1 Enok, Yasar, Yubile, ekn., gila e fuɗɗoode. Sabu ɓee yimɓe bonɓe e fenanndeeji ɗi ɓe kuutorotoo ngam suuɗde goonga oo ko geɓal tan e hare mawnde ndee, woni hare aawdi hakkunde Yahwe e Seyɗaani, ndeen hare ina jokki haa e oo minita, kono ndee heɓaama haa abada sabu golle am kisinooje. Sabu Yahwe habrii hare e ndee mboddi ɓooyndi sabu muurtal mum, gila e fuɗɗoode tagngo neɗɗaŋke, nde Seyɗaani etinoo halkude Aadama e Hawa. Yahwe wiyi mboddi ndii e nder haala mum: Mi waɗan ngayngu hakkunde maa e debbo oo, e hakkunde aawdi maa e aawdi makko: ngoon fiyan hoore maa, a nanngan teppere makko. Gila ndeen, hare aawdi ndee ina jokki.

E nder Aljanna Adnin, Seyɗaani dañii bonnude aawdi Aadama, waɗi Aadama e les doole e bonannde greti. (Ko e nder ɗuum ɓiy Alla yalti, ngam o bonna golle Ibliis.) Sabu Seyɗaani huutorii ganndal mum bonngal ko fayti e moƴƴere e bonannde ngam bonnude neɗɗaŋke fof, tee kamɓe nanooɓe daande am, ndeen bonannde ittaama e nder am, miin Almasiihu Iisaa, mo nganndu-ɗaa on ngoni tagngo heso ngo waawaa bonneede hay gooto. Aawdi am waawaa bonneede, sabu Yahwe waɗii ndi ndi bonnataa, tee e laawol jibinannde hesere ndee, mi maandinirii suɓaaɓe am e koolniiɓe am haa e oo Ruuhu Ceniiɗo ahdi!

Yuhanna e nder ɗerewol mum gadanol ina hollita goonga duumotooɗo oo: Kala jibinaaɗo e Alla waɗataa greti: sabu aawdi makko ina heddii e mum, tee o waawaa waɗde greti, sabu o jibinaama e Alla.

Caggal seeɗa nde Adnin yani, ɗo Aadama waɗi greti, o yani e timmal haa e bonannde, aynooɓe ɓee njippii e dow tulde Hermon e jamaanu Yared. Kamɓe fof ɓe kaɓɓondiri e alkule bonɗe, haa e ngoon hunayeere maɓɓe ceniiɗo, nde ɓe kaɓɓiri koye maɓɓe haa abada e greti e lepte duumotooɗe. Ɓe mbaɗiri ɗum noon ngam kala e maɓɓe heɓa rewɓe e kala ko ɓe cuɓii, ɓe mbayla noon sifaa maɓɓe.

Ɓe ngari e neɗɗaŋke ina ndokkira ɗum en ganndal maɓɓe bonngal, ganndal malaa'ikaaji njippiiɗi, sabu ɓe naatii e muurtal Seyɗaani.

Ɓe njanngini yimɓe waɗde kaafaaje, e laɓɓe, e waleeji, e comci hare, ɓe nganndini ɗum en njamndiiji leydi e karallaagal gollaade ɗi, e juuɗe, e cuɗaari, e kuutoragol antimoine, e moƴƴinde gite, e kala sifaa kaaƴe tedduɗe, e kala noon pentiirɗi. Ɓe njanngini bolle, e taƴde ɗaɗi, e feewnude bolle, e ganndal koode, e koode, e ganndal duule, e maale leydi, e maale naange, e doggol lewru.

Ko noon aynooɓe njippiiɓe ɓee njogorii rewɓe mum en, ɓe mbaɗdi e maɓɓe njeenu, ɓe reedi, ɓe njibini ɓe yimɓe mawɓe sanne. Nde waktu yetti, ndee aawdi bonndi ndii ñami kala ko wonnoo e dow leydi, e kala ko rokkaama neɗɗaŋke, haa ragare mum ñami neɗɗaŋke no keewiri fof. Sabu kamɓe ko yimɓe yardooɓe ƴiiƴam, ñamooɓe yimɓe, ɓe mbaɗi bonannde mawnde e yeeso Yahwe, sabu nguurndam ɓanndu ina woni e nder ƴiiƴam, tee neɗɗaŋke tagiraama e nanndugol Yahwe. Ɓe mawni, ɓe keewi, haa ɓe ndarni laamuuji maɓɓe e dow leydi ndii fof. Gila! Heedduɓe mahdiiji maɓɓe mawɗi ina keddii e dow leydi ndii, ko seedeeji e dow maɓɓe. Ɓe laamiima leydi ndii e oon sahaa, tee ɓe mbaɗaama ngam halkude neɗɗaŋke. Ko noon kala sifaa bonanndeeji mbaɗiraa, sabu binndi ceniiɗi ɗii kollitii no ɗum waɗiraa.

Hono no neɗɗaŋke yiɗiri ɓalli keeɗɗi, waɗdi njeenu e malaa'ikaaji njippiiɗi, jibini ɓiɓɓe mawɓe, sabu aynooɓe ɓee, kamɓe fof ɓe mbaɗi greti e colli, e daabeeji, e kulle ladde, e liɗɗi. Gila e fuɗɗoode tagngo, Yahwe yamiri kala tagngo sifaa mum. Kono aynooɓe bonɓe ɓee njippiima e tagngo, haa ɓe kawriti tagooje goɗɗe e tagooje nannduɗe e mum en ngam naatinde caɗeele e mbayliigu e tagngo adanngo ngo Yahwe waɗnoo e deeƴre timmunde. Ndee golle ina jokki, ɓe kawriti tagooje nannduɗe e goɗɗe haa ɓe keɓi ko ɓe njiɗi. Ko noon aduna oon bonniri e kala sifaa bonanndeeji bonɗi, baɗɗi greti mawɗi e dow Yahwe, neɗɗaŋke, e tagngo ngoo fof. Faamee, njippiiɓe ɓee ina njiɗi aduna mum en, aduna niɓɓere, seerndiindi fof e Yahwe, tee ko ɗuum ɓe ndonatat e nder weendu yiite nde Yahwe e hoore mum heblani ɓe.

Ko noon ñalɗi Nuuhu ngari, aduna oon, dariiɗo e ndiyam e nder ndiyam, moolnaama e ndiyam, halkaama. Sabu buruuji lugge mawɗe ɗii mbelaama, waɗi fitina mawɗo nde leydi ndii feccitii e doole ndiyam bonɗam, tee daaɗe kammu udditaama, waɗi ndiyam ummoraade dow kammu (e les leydi) ina suuɗa e halkude aduna oo fof.

Ko noon Enok binndoowo oo siftori batte ɓee mawɓe, sabu Yahwe wiyi noon: Jooni, ɓee mawɓe, jibinaaɓe e ruuhuuji e ɓalli, ina noddiree jinneeji bonɗi e dow leydi, tee e dow leydi wonata hoɗorde maɓɓe. Jinneeji bonɗi njaltii e ɓalli maɓɓe; sabu ɓe jibinaama e yimɓe, e aynooɓe ceniiɓe woni fuɗɗoode maɓɓe e iwdi maɓɓe adanndi; ɓe ngoni jinneeji bonɗi e dow leydi, tee jinneeji bonɗi ɓe noddirtee. Ko fayti e ruuhuuji kammu, e kammu wonata hoɗorde maɓɓe, kono ko fayti e ruuhuuji leydi jibinaaɗi e dow leydi, e dow leydi wonata hoɗorde maɓɓe. Tee ruuhuuji mawɓe ɓee ina torra, ina tooña, ina halka, ina haɓa, ina waɗa hare, ina mbaɗa bonannde e dow leydi, ina ngadda caɗeele: ɓe ñamataa, kono ina njoomta, ina ndomɗa, ina ngadda aybeeji. Tee ɗii ruuhuuji ina ummoo e dow ɓiɓɓe Aadama e rewɓe, sabu ɓe njaltii e maɓɓe. Faamee wonde jeenayi e sappo e ɗii jinneeji bonɗi ina ngoni e nder lugge sabu duwaawu Nuuhu, tee neɗɗaŋke ina haɓa tan e go'o e sappo e maɓɓe gila ndeen. Ɗuum ina foti wayleede so lugge ɗee udditaama, fof njoɓtinee.

Ko noon leydi ndii, e kala bonanndeeji aynooɓe ɓee, halkiraa. Aynooɓe ɓee e koye mum en kaɓɓaama e nder ñaawooje mum en ceertuɗe duuɓi cappanɗe jeeɗiɗi, ngam njoɓtineede ngam ñaaweede e sahaa ñaawoore mawnde ndee. (Sabu ñaawoore mawnde ndee ina foti fuɗɗaade.) Kono, Nuuhu heɓi yurum e yeeso Yahwe sabu ko o neɗɗo peewɗo, e saare makko Yahwe hisini nguurndam mum en, woni yimɓe njeetato fof, e wondude e daabeeji keewɗi goɗɗi e nder laana ndema. Nuuhu reeni hoore mum e saare mum e mbayliigu iwdi hade ilam. Kono ɓiyum Ham e iwdi mum bonndi ndii njahdi haa njaɓaani Nuuhu e Yahwe e hoore mum (tagɗo ɓe, hisinɗo ɓe), ɓe naati e muurtal Seyɗaani.

Konne Jibbirila 6 timminiraama jurumɗe iwdi Nuuhu, haa e iwdi Haama. Mawɓe tedduɓe ngonnoo e leydi ndii e ɗeen ñalɗi: e kadi caggal ɗuum. "Ɗeen ñalɗi" ko ñalɗi gila e Yaareedi haa e Nuuhu, "mawɓe tedduɓe e leydi ndii" ko iwdi aynooɓe ɓee e ɓiɓɓe rewɓe yimɓe ɓee hade ilam mawɗam naam. "Caggal ɗuum" ko caggal ñalɗi Nuuhu, nde taariika genetik aadee bonnaa kadi e ganndal malaa'ika'en yibɓe. Miɗo ñaagii on yo on accit goongɗingol fenaande teyori Sethite bonɗo oo, on njoona konne binndi laaɓtuɗi ɗii, sabu konne ɗee ko goonga.

Nde leydi ndii feccaa e paɗe hakkunde ɓiɓɓe Nuuhu (Seem, Haama, e Yafeta), paɗal Haama ko yo o ron leydiiji to funnaange, ɗe ngoni kadi ɗe makko. Kono, Kanaana yeli feccere e ndonu Seem, o yahi o hoɗi e leyɗeele ɗe ngonaano ɗe makko. Sabu ko Kanaana Nuuhu huɗi. Sabu Kanaana yalti ndonu mum, o sosi wuro Tyre e nokku biyeteeɗo Phoenicia, ngol addi haa Sidon, ɗo Yezebel iwi. O waɗi ɗum ngam o wona ɓadimaa hoore-wuro Hermon, ɗo aynooɓe ɓee njippii e ɗo mi waylaa.

Kanaana tawi ko aynooɓe ɓee acci hade ilam Nuuhu naam, sabu ɓe njiɗnoo ko hisnude murto maɓɓe hay caggal ilam ngam, sabu ɓe mbinndi ko heewi e kaaƴe e hayre. Kanaana huutoriima ganndal maɓɓe malaa'ika'en yibɓe ngal e kuule mawɗe, e golle keewɗe bonɗe mbaɗaa e juuɗe Kanaani'en, sabu ɓe kirsatnoo hay ɓiɓɓe maɓɓe. No ɓe bonirnoo nii e no ɗum yaajirnoo nii haa o dañi bonnude leydi Kanaana ndii e golle makko kese bonɗe, ɗo Seytaane golli sabu bonanndeeji makko.

Yosuwa naati e leydi fodanoondi ndii ngam heɓtude ko Seem rokkaa e paɗal, e leydi ndi Isra'iila heɓi e fodoore Yaahwe e hoore mum. Kono ɓiɓɓe Isra'iila ɓee timminaani no haanirta nii yamirooje Yaahwe ɗe accataa hay gooto e gawje ɗee ina wuuri, sabu ɓe ngonnoo ko bonɓe, ɓe ngonaano yimɓe, golle maɓɓe ina boni, ɓe mbaɗi ko heewi e bonanndeeji yeeso Yaahwe, sabu ko nii sifaa maɓɓe woniri. Ko ɗum waɗi Yaahwe wiyi Isra'iila hono no winndiraa e binndi ɗii: Kono so on ngoppii riiwde jooɗiiɓe leydi ndii yeeso mon, maa arta e mon wonɓe e maɓɓe ɓe njoppoton ɓee, maa ɓe ngonan on giɗe e gite mon, e giɗe e daaɗe mon, ɓe torra on e leydi ndi koɗoton ndii. Kadi ko non waɗiri.

Taariika ina holla seeɗa no ɗee golle bonɗe njahdiri e no njahdiri, haa e gawje ɗe Seytaane e hoore mum aawi, no ɓe nguurnodiri e alkama gila e jamaanuuji ɓooyɗi. ɓe cosi siwilisaasiyoŋaaji keewɗi ina kuutoroo karallaagal maɓɓe e ganndal malaa'ika'en yibɓe; ko maɓɓooɓe mawɓe, ko ɓe seernaaɓe, heewɓe e maɓɓe ina njogii baawɗe keewɗe, ɓe njokkondiri e baaba maɓɓe Seytaane, haa ɓe ndenndi sifaa mum e yiɗde mum. Ɗee gawje naatii e kala fannu e renndo, wonaa kamɓe fof ngoni mawɓe. Heewɓe ina nanndi e yimɓe, so wonaa wonde ko ɓe tagooje jiiɓruɗe waylaaɗe haa abada e peeje tagoore Yaahwe, ɓe njoɓetaake. Woto hulɓe, sabu mawɗo wonɗo e mon oo ɓuri mawɗo wonɗo e aduna oo.

Ɗee gawje bonɗe ina njiɗi heɓtude ko jeyi ndonu e fodoore ɓiɓɓe Alla. Nanondiral alaa e maɓɓe, sabu ko baaba maɓɓe Seytaane jeyi ɓe, e yiɗde baaba maɓɓe ndee ɓe mbaɗata. Ko o waroowo gila e fuɗɗoode, so o haalii fenaande o haalata ko e hoore makko, sabu ko o fenoowo, ko kanko woni baaba mum. Ɓe mbeltata, ɓe mbonnata, ɓe mbeltata, ɓe mbara, ɓe njana, ɓe njana, ɓe mbaɗa ko ɓuri bonde e kala ko Alla jogii ko ɓe njiɗi. Jam alaa e maɓɓe, sifaa maɓɓe ina boni haa laaɓi. Ɓe mbonnii aduna, ɓe cosi laamu maɓɓe waktu gooto no liingu nguu jogorii laamaade e leydi ndii. Kono ɓe njiɗi ko artirde jamaanu maɓɓe kaŋŋe, kono alaa jamaanu kaŋŋe ngu ɓe keɓata so wonaa ñaawoore tiiɗnde, ɓe ndifataa.

Ɗee gawje bonɗe mbonnii ganndal, jaŋde, taariika, diine, ganndal, ekn. Ɓe mbonnii. Ɓe njaltinii karallaagal malaa'ika'en yibɓe, alaa ko hesɗi e les naange. Nde waktuuji ɗii timmi, ɓe njuɓɓini leydi ndii fof e fenaande, aduna oo fof ina lelii e bonannde. E dow laaɓal maɓɓe, gawje Jesuit en mbonnii renndo ngoo fof, nawi aadee e maayo fenaande, ɗo daɗndude tan ko e tuubde e woppude jurumɗe e fenaande fof. (Sabu Room waɗii Lislaam kadi; janngu ngam anndude.) Ganndal maɓɓe e fenaande maɓɓe ina luurdi e daartol binndol, ina ndokka taali puuyɗi ɗi mumnata hakkillaaji e ɓerɗe yimɓe. Ko ɗum waɗi, njalte e maɓɓe, ceerte.

Aduna hannde oo ko heewɓe, heewɓe ina njogii yejjitoore renndinde sabu jaŋde Jesuit en. Heewɓe e mon njiytiima wonaa tan alkule e laabi Yaahwe, kono kadi njiytiima hol ko woni on e hol ko woni maamiraaɓe mon goonga. Ɗum waɗiraa ko e anniya e wolde, e fenaande, e golle ngam artirde yimɓe e nder duuɓi ɗi njaajni heewɓe haa e nokkuuji nayi leydi ndii. E ɗuum ɓe njiɗi yejjitde wonde ina saɗi suuɗde hay huunde e gite Yaahwe; e miin, Yesuwaa HaMashiya, miɗo anndi kam e ɓe noddatmi e kisal.

Yoga e ɓeen warɓe ɓee ko alkiyankooɓe, ɓe njippinaa e ɓesnguuji maɓɓe walla e wolde walla e nanngugol. Ɗee ruuhuuji mawnirii e daartol fenaande, haa fenaande e miijooji giɗliiji jowitiiɗi e daartol fenaande, ko ɗum waɗi fenaande ndee ina juutɗi e ɓerɗe yimɓe, heewɓe ina mumnaa haa abada e fenaande jaɓaande no goonga nii. Kono wonande ɓeen artuɓe e am, Yesuwaa HaMashiya, maa on nganndu goonga, goonga oo maa rimɗin on. Renndo mon hannde ngoo mahiraa ko e dow mborosaaji e ndoondi siwilisaasiyoŋaaji ɓooyɗi, e golle mawɗe mbaɗaama ngam suuɗde goonga. Ko wonnoo koo maa won kadi, alaa ko hesɗi e les naange.

So tawii alaa sadak am kafara e sadak am kisal, on fof on majjatnoo. Ndeke, ngaree e am, Yesuwaa HaMashiya, mi ardoo on e goonga fof, mi janngina on daartol mon goonga. Miɗo jokkondirta on e leñol mon goonga, sabu heewɓe e mon ko e leydi ndii iwi, hay so tawii kisal ko ngam leyɗeele fof. Mi janngina on laabi laaɓtuɗi, on nguura e nuunɗal e goongɗingol am, Iisaa Almasiihu, on nguura haa abada. Alaa ko suuɗii ko yaltataa e laaɓal, fof maa feeñde haa laaɓi, ngam goonga anndeede e yimɓe fof.

Ndeke accit ñamminde fenaande no ndenndinirton e weltinde hoore mon, sabu waktuuji weltinde hoore mon ngasii. Waktuuji feeñninde ngarii. Sabu ndaaree! No ñalɗi Nuuhu nii, ko noon wonirta. Seytaane huutoriima limre mum fenaande ngam waɗde dingiral ngam fenaande mum mawnde sakkitiinde, heewɓe e aduna oo maa njokku fenaande mum. Sabu laamu Seytaane fof maa rimɗine e lugge, mawɓe tedduɓe maa nguuran kadi e hakkunde yimɓe, sabu ɓe ngari ko ngam timminde tikkere Yaahwe.

Seytaane ina haala fenaande mawnde, ɓeen yibɓe maa mbiyi ko e adunaaji goɗɗi iwi, ɓe ɓuri aadee duuɓi ujunnaaje keewɗe. Ɓe ngarata ko ina ndokka ganndal yooŋiiɗo, ɓe kuɓɓoo e moƴƴere fenaande. Kono ɗiin ko laamu Seytaane, mbele woto on naata e fenaande ndee. Woto njokke laabi aduna oo, sabu aduna oo maa haawnito laamu Seytaane, haa wiyee, holi nannduɗo e kullel ngel?

Mi suuɗa on, mi reenan on, mi semmbina on, mi ardoo on seeɗa seeɗa, mi itta haa laaɓi wutte fenaande kufniiɗo aduna oo e fenaande. Miin, hay Yesuwaa HaMashiya, maa mi semmbina on, mi waɗa on waɗde kaawniiɗe keewɗe mawɗe e nder oo sahaa torra Yaaquuba. Ko ɗum waɗi miɗo haalana on ɗee geɗe, ngam goonga oo rimɗina on, ngam on tigi-rigi rimɗude, on naatataa e fenaande maɓɓe mawnde ndee. Sabu fonndaasiyoŋaaji mum ko fenaande, e fenaande, e fenaande nde iwdi gawje liingu nguu.

Ko miin woni laawol, goonga, e nguurndam; hay gooto arataa to Baaba so wonaa e am.

Ndaaree! Mi haalanirii on ɗee geɗe, wonaa ngam jannginde on daartol timmungol, kono ko ngam ɓeydude on e laawol goonga. So waktuuji njottii, kala huunde feeñoyay, laamu ngun mboddi maa annde, sabu miin, Yeesu Almasiihu, mbiɗo haali ɗum, te ko miin on mo Yahwe neli.
