Hwɛ! Nsɛm a Wɔaka afa Atemmuo a Ɛda Adi wɔ Babilon Ahintasɛm so Ansa na Atemmuo no Aba Dwoodwoo

Atemmubuo a edidi soɔ yi firi Adiyisɛm Nwoma Ketewa no Ti 10 mu, a ɛno nso ne Asomdwoeɛ a Ɛwɔ Hɔ Daa no. Wɔada atemmubuo a ɛwɔ Babilon Ahintasɛm so adi mfeɛ pii ne mfeɛ pii a atwam. Nea edidi soɔ yi yɛ Atemmuo a wɔada no adi a wɔayi afiri Nwoma Ketewa no mu a ɛbɛba mu ampa ara mpofirim na wɔanhwɛ kwan seesei. Wɔ saa dibea yi mu no, meyɛ ɔtemmufoɔ bɔfoɔ a wɔasoma me sɛ menkɔda atemmubuo a ɛfiri Yahweh ahengua no so adi, a wɔde ama wɔ Yesu Kristo anim, a mesom no no.

Momma wɔnhunu sɛ, ɛnnɛ mpo Ayɛwohomumɔ 16, 2025 wɔ Gregorian Nnipa Nsɛm mu (mpo kalenda koro no ara a Babilon Ahintasɛm de di dwuma) sɛ me Wura Kristo Yesu a Wɔmaa no soɔ no, mpo Yeshua HaMashiach hyɛ me sɛ menkenkan atemmubuo yinom wɔ Babilon Ahintasɛm so, mpo saa United States of America yi, sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a obiara bɛtumi asakra berɛ a berɛ tiawa bi da so wɔ hɔ, na wɔada atemmubuo adi ansa na ogya atɔ. Selah!

Saa nkaebɔ yi nyɛ nea ɛdi mu koraa ɛfiri sɛ yɛnyi atemmubuo foforɔ nanso wɔayi no adi koraa firi Oforisuo 2, 2024, de kɔsi Oforisuo 20, 2025. Yei mu dodoɔ no ara firi Yeshua HaMashiach hɔ, na faako firi Yahweh, ME KƐSE no hɔ. Afei mɛkɔ so ada adi.
