Deɛ Ɛyɛ Nwono na Ɛyɛ Dɛ
Ayɛwohomumɔ 31, 2024

Nnue Mmɔborɔ Ma Wɔn A Wɔfrɛ Esum Kanea Na Kanea Nso Esum No!

Tie wo Wura ne wo gyefoɔ nsɛm, m’Asɔre, aane, m’ayeforɔ, ɛfiri sɛ mene deɛ ɔma anyansafoɔ nyansa, sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a anyansafoɔ a wɔwɔ mo mu no bɛnya nyansa kɛseɛ mpo. Na mene ɔtomfoɔ a ɔsɔ ogya a ɛhohoro nnipa ho no mu, enti tie me nsɛm, mo nyinaa, na monkotow m’akyɛdeɛ a ɛde nkwa a ɛnni awieeɛ bɛma mo no anim na monhwehwɛ nyansa, ɛfiri sɛ nyansa ne adeɛ a ɛhia paa, enti monnya nyansa. Enti momfa akoma a ɛbue ne nsa a ɛbue mmra me nkyɛn na monkotow na monkɔ so nyɛ me, Yesu Nasareni no, mo Wura a ɔwɔ hɔ daa, ɛfiri sɛ meda so ara frɛ wɔn a wɔyɛ nkwa a ɛnni awieeɛ no adedifoɔ nyinaa, ɛwom sɛ ebinom aso yɛ den deɛ.

Nanso mompo wiase yi mu nyansa a ɛbɛba sɛ hwee no, ɛfiri sɛ wiase nyansa firi wiase yi mu ahotee a wɔn nyinaa, ɛrenkyɛ koraa, wɔde wɔn bɛto amena donkudonku no mu mfeɛ apem wɔ amanehunu ne asotwe mu ɛnam wɔn akwan so nti. Na mpo wɔ nkɔnsɔnkɔnsɔn duruduru a wɔde bɛkyekyere wɔn ɛberɛ tiawa bi mu no, wɔbɛte wɔn daakye ase, na wɔn ho bɛpopo na wɔawoso mfeɛ apem berɛ a wɔn daa asotwe a wɔahyɛ ato hɔ wɔ ogya tadeɛ a ɛhyehyeɛ a sufre a ɛporɔ wom no mu no redɛre na ɛrehyehyeɛ wɔ ogya a ɛyɛ bruu kɔkɔɔ a ɛyɛ hye paa no mu, ɛfiri sɛ saa na Yahweh asiesie na saa na wɔn agyapadeɛ retwɛn wɔn.

Na sɛdeɛ wɔn akoma pɛ ma Onyankopɔn teasefoɔ no mma no, saa ara na Yahweh bɛdane wɔn akoma mu apɛdeɛ no asan aba wɔn ara wɔn koko so, na saa ara na wɔbɛhyehyeɛ daa daa kɔsi nna a ɛnni awieeɛ no mu. Na titire no, wɔn a wɔahwe aseɛ no bɛnya wɔn akwan so akatua ɛfiri sɛ wɔne bɔne, mmarato, amumuyɛ, ne mmara a wɔanni so no agyanom na wɔn nneyɛɛ a ɛyɛ abofono no, wɔde bɛsan ama wɔn daa. Na wɔbɛhunu na wɔatua bɔne biara a wɔayɛ no so ka yie mmɔho pii, ɛfiri sɛ saa na wɔahunu wɔ wɔn akoma mu na wɔn nneyɛɛ yɛ akyiwadeɛ koraa daa daa. Na saa na Yahweh akwan teɛ, sɛdeɛ onipa bu ne yɔnko atɛn no, saa ara na wɔbɛbu no atɛn, na ɛno ne wɔn a wɔpaw wɔn ara wɔn sɛeɛ sene nkwa a ɛnni awieeɛ a ɛwɔ me Yesu Nasareni no mu no nyinaa nkranteɛ ne wɔn asotwe, ɛfiri sɛ mene Onyankopɔn Odwan.

Na obiara a ɔyɛ kwasea a ɔsɔre tia abɔdeɛ nhyehyɛeɛ, mpo Yahweh abɔdeɛ mu mmara a ɔhyehyɛɛ nkwa sɛ ntoma a wɔanwen abom no, ɔbɛnya wɔn asotwe no mu bi. Sɛ wosɔre tia nkwa nhyehyɛeɛ a, ɛde owuo ne ɔsɛeɛ brɛ afoforɔ, na wɔn a wɔsɔre tia saa kwan yi so na wɔdi wɔn ara wɔn akɔnnɔ akyi de sɛe afoforɔ no yɛ bɔne kɛseɛ na wɔde ɔhaw kɛseɛ ba. Sɛ wodi ahurisie wɔ deɛ wahwe aseɛ no atuateɛ mu a, na wo ne wɔn ayɛ baako wɔ wɔn akwan so na wɔn a wɔyɛ saa nyinaa bɛnya wɔn agyapadeɛ no bi daa wɔ ogya a ɛnnum da mu, na ɔkwan foforɔ biara nni hɔ.

Na ɛfiri teteete ne mfitiaseɛ no, wɔmaa onipa hokwan sɛ ɔmpaw deɛ ɔpɛ na Hawa yɛɛ Adam adɔɔdɔɔ, na ɔno nso gyee firii ɔwɔ a ɔde ne nnaadaa daadaa no no nkyɛn. Enti Adam ne Hawa paw owuo sɛ bɔne so akatua, ɛfiri sɛ wɔbɔɔ nnipa sɛ abɔfoɔ pɛpɛɛpɛ, a wɔn adwene ne sɛ wɔbɛkɔ so ayɛ kronkron na wɔatene, na owuo a ɛsɛe biribiara no, anka ɛrentumi wɔn so.

Na saa nti, papa ne bɔne ho nimdeɛ asɛe wɔn a wɔadidie firi saa berɛ no nyinaa. Na aba no mu ko no abɔ na ɛsoɛ firi Genesis so ne ɔbaa no aba a wɔhyɛɛ ho bɔ no, wɔ ɔsɛeɛ kɛseɛ mu, na wɔ saa ɔko yi mu no, biribiara nni hɔ a ɛnnyaa ne so nsunsuansoɔ. Kɔsi nnɛ da yi, abɔdeɛ nyinaa repene na ɛsi apini wɔ ɛyaw mu mpo seesei ara ɛfiri sɛ abɔdeɛ no nso, wɔbɛgye no afiri ɔporɔ nkoasom mu akɔ Onyankopɔn mma anuonyam ahofadie no mu, na nkwagyeɛ ntwentwɛn ne nan ase! Nanso monte ase, me mma, sɛ kɔsi nnɛ da yi, aba no mu ko no, wɔadi so nkonim wie koraa wɔ me, Kristo Yesu mu, na wɔawo mo foforɔ firi aba a ɛmporɔ mu na wɔde Onyankopɔn Odwan no ayɛ mpata ama adesamma bɔne prɛko pɛ daa. Na ɛnam Yahweh tumi a wontumi nsusuw so, wɔdii owuo so nkonim korakora wɔ me wusɔreɛ a mesɔre firii awufoɔ mu no mu, ɛfiri sɛ ɛntumi nyɛ yie sɛ owuo bɛkura me mu ɛfiri sɛ meyɛ deɛ bɔne nni me mu na bɔne akatua ne owuo.

Nanso, metuaa boɔ a ɛso a wontumi nsusuwɛ mmaa mo nkwagyeɛ ne mo ahofadie, na Onyankopɔn teasefoɔ no mma no, ɛberɛ biara, wɔbɛhunu wɔn ho sɛ wɔgyina Onyankopɔn ahennwa dan mu a wɔafura nkwa a ɛnni awieeɛ daa. Saa na ɛteɛ na saa na ɛbɛyɛ, sɛ obiara a ɔbɔ Wura no din no, wɔbɛgye no nkwa daa daa, sɛ ɔkɔ so de nokorɛdie bɔ me din kɔsi awieeɛ a. Na wɔn a wɔahwe aseɛ no yɛ atamfo a wɔadi wɔn so nkonim, na seesei berɛ a wɔn ahennie bɛsɛe koraa na wɔanya asotwe a ɛdi mu no aduru, berɛ a wɔregu Odwan no abufuo ne Onyankopɔn abufuo no agu wɔ nna a ɛreba no mu. Na Awurade da no adu, enti tie me nne ansa na wɔato Adaka no pono no mu.

Tie me nsɛm na te me kasa ase. Na mereka nneɛma a ɛrekɔ so seesei ara ho asɛm ne nnaadaa kɛseɛ bi a anka ɛbɛdaadaa wɔn a wɔapaw wɔn no mpo sɛ wɔpaw sɛ wɔbɛsi wɔn aso wɔ me nne so a. Afei mɛkasa afa mprako a wɔwɔ wuram a wɔpɛ sɛ wɔyɛ wɔn ho sɛ wɔnni mprako dan a deɛ ɔsi ano kwan no de wɔn agu mu no mu. Mpo mprako nnipadɔm ne wɔn nhyehyɛeɛ a ɛde ɔsɛeɛ ba daa daa no de asase no rebɛn ɔko kɛseɛ ne amanehunu kɛseɛ ho, ɛfiri sɛ saa na wɔama wɔn kwan sɛ wɔnyɛ na Onyankopɔn de ahyɛ wɔn akoma mu sɛ wɔnyɛ n’apɛdeɛ, na mpo Yahweh atamfoɔ tie ne nne.

Mprako no pirim wɔn ho wɔ wɔn ara wɔn fi mu, wɔtutututu dɔteɛ mu, na wɔn ani nsɔ da, na biribiara a wɔbɛtumi anya no, wɔbɛmene berɛ a wɔde wɔn hwene retututu asase mu no, ɛfiri sɛ wɔfiri asase so, na wɔn ani afira nneɛma foforɔ nyinaa so gye wɔn yafunu mu apɛdeɛ ne wɔn agya akɔnnɔ a wɔbɛyɛ. Na saa na asase no so mpanimfoɔ a wɔwɔ hɔ seesei no teɛ, na wɔteam wɔ ɛyaw mu berɛ a wɔrebɔ wɔn a wɔsɛe wɔn no. Na biribiara ntumi mma saa mprako yi ani nsɔ koraa, na deɛ wɔnom no ama wɔn mu ayɛ wɔn awuduro koraa, ɛfiri sɛ wɔn nyinaa ho nte na wɔyɛ nwura ankasa na saa na wɔn suban teɛ.

Saa mmoa bɔne yi yɛ nnua a aporɔ na aba nni so, awu mprenu, na wɔatu wɔn ase. Wɔrennya nkwa a ɛnni awieeɛ da, na wɔn akwan yɛ owuo ne ɔsɛeɛ a ɛwɔ hɔ daa, mpo ɔsɛeɛ a ɛwɔ hɔ daa wɔ wɔn Agyanom agyapadeɛ mu wɔ ogya tadeɛ kɛseɛ a ɛyɛ bruu no mu. Wɔn suban ne sɛ wɔba bɛsɛe adeɛ, na wɔde wɔn ho fra afoforɔ, mfitiaseɛ no wɔhwehwɛ tenabea berɛ a wɔresu sɛ wɔn na wɔredi bem no, na mpo wɔbɔ afoforɔ soboɔ wɔ ɔtan a wɔde ba amanaman so no ho. Wɔteam wɔ ɛyaw mu berɛ a wɔrebɔ wɔn a wɔpɛ sɛ wɔsɛe wɔn no sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a wɔbɛhunu sɛ wɔn amumuyɛ no yɛ deɛ ɛfata.

Na saa mmoa hunu yi, wontumi nhu wɔn mmɔbɔ ɛfiri sɛ wɔn akwan yɛ ɔsɛeɛ deɛ na yeinom bɛyɛ wɔn Agya ɔbonsam akɔnnɔ, ɛfiri sɛ n’akɔnnɔ yɛ wɔn anigyeɛ na saa na wɔn ani sɔ. Nnue mmɔborɔ ma wɔn a wɔdi bɔne ho ahurisie na wɔfrɛ kanea sɛ esum na esum nso sɛ kanea. Na wɔn akwan yɛ ɔsɛeɛ deɛ, na ogya a ɛwɔ hɔ daa no mu, wɔrennya kwan nguan da. Monte ase, me mma, sɛ ɛberɛ biara seesei ara, wɔbɛyi mprako no afiri adan a deɛ ɔsi ano kwan no de wɔn agu mu no mu. Na sɛ wɔyi deɛ ɔsi ano kwan no a, saa mprako a ɛyɛ sɛ wɔyɛ mmoa a wɔdɔ na asɛm nni wɔn ho a wɔyɛ wɔn ho sɛ wɔyɛ Onyankopɔn nkurɔfoɔ a wɔapaw wɔn no bɛdane mmoa a wɔyɛ hu ampa ara pɛ a wɔbɛyi wɔn afiri deɛ ɔsi ano kwan no nsa a wontumi mmu mu no mu, ɛfiri sɛ saa na wɔn suban ankasa teɛ.

Wɔbɛto ahyɛ nsase pii so na wɔamene biribiara a wɔbɛhunu. Wɔbɛdidie wɔ afoforɔ owuo ne ɔsɛeɛ so, ɛfiri sɛ saa na wɔn suban teɛ. Wɔatutu akɔ mmeaemmeae, na wɔwɔ wɔn mmara ne wɔn nnipa mu nhyehyɛeɛ, ɛfiri sɛ Yahweh mpo ma mprako no nyansa ne nhyehyɛeɛ bi, ɛfiri sɛ Yahweh akwan yɛ deɛ ɛtene na ɛyɛ kronkron. Saa mprako yi nni adwen-nusɛeɛ biara, wɔnni atenka biara, na wɔnni ɔhaw biara sɛ wɔbɛdi awufoɔ de ama wɔn akɔnnɔ adwo, na mpo wɔbɛdi ateasefoɔ sɛ ɛbɛboa wɔn atirimpɔ a. Hwɛ, dɔnhwere no adu seesei a wɔremfa mprako no nsie bio na sɛ ɛba saa a, wɔbɛdi asase no, wɔbɛmene asase no so sradeɛ, wɔbɛsɛe asomdwoeɛ koraa afiri asase so na wɔadidi na wɔapirim wɔn ho wɔ ɔko kɛseɛ mogya a ɛbɛhwie agu mu. Na mpo mo anigyedeɛfoɔ bɔnefoɔ no to dwom fa wɔn akwan ho, na wɔn akwan nyɛ ahintasɛm.

Saa mprako a wɔyɛ hu yi, wɔayɛ wɔn ama ɔsɛeɛ ne sɛ wɔbɛmene asase no so fi de ahoro asase no ama Yahweh atirimpɔ, afei wɔbɛkyekyere wɔn na wɔato wɔn agu ogya a ɛwɔ hɔ daa no mu daa. Na wɔn bɔne so, na wɔn bɔne dɔɔso. Nanso monte ase sɛ wɔde saa mprako yi asie wɔn dan mu ama ɛberɛ a wɔahyɛ, na pii yɛ wɔn ho sɛ wɔapaw wɔn sɛ nkuku kronkron ama wɔn ahennie a ɛresɔre no. Na seesei ne berɛ a wɔbɛyi wɔn, na afei wɔbɛda wɔn suban ankasa a wɔde asie wɔ ntadeɛ a ɛyɛ sɛ ɛyɛ nyamesom deɛ mu no adi, na Saturn ahina tuntum no wɔ wɔn nsoromma no mfimfini na ɛwɔ wɔn ani so mpo. Wɔpɛ sɛ wɔtete wɔn ho mu ɛfiri sɛ wɔapaw wɔn ho sɛ wɔyɛ Onyankopɔn dea. Nanso onipa biara mma Yahweh nkyɛn gye sɛ ɔnam me, Yesu Nasareni, a wɔn Agyanom poo no no so, na wɔn a wɔayɛ me Wura no ne Onyankopɔn Israel ankasa na wɔbɛbɔ wɔn foforɔ koraa wɔ me mu sɛ onipa foforɔ baako, sɛ ɔyɛ Yudani anaa ɔyɛ Amanaman muni, sɛ ɔyɛ akoa anaa ɔdehyeɛ. Saa Edomfoɔ nwura yi, wɔbɛda wɔn adi koraa sɛ nnaadaafoɔ na wɔbɛyi wɔn afiri asase no so ne asase nyinaa so koraa wɔ atemmuo kɛseɛ ne asotwe kɛseɛ mu.

Nanso ansa na wɔbɛyi wɔn no, wɔbɛma wɔn kwan ma wɔanya wɔn mogya ho akɔnnɔ berɛ a wɔreda wɔn suban ankasa adi ama obiara ahunu, ɛfiri sɛ seesei deɛ wɔde wɔn ho ahinta nyamesom nneɛma a ɛyɛ sɛ ɛyɛ nokorɛ mu. Wɔdidi sumina so, wɔdidi awufoɔ so, wɔto hyɛ ateasefoɔ so na wɔmene wɔn ɛfiri sɛ wɔn mogya ho akɔnnɔ so na wontumi mma ɛnna! Na ɛberɛ korɔ no ara mu no, wɔyɛ wɔn ho sɛ wɔyɛ kronkron, wɔyɛ wɔn ho sɛ wɔfiri asase no so na wɔyɛ adedifoɔ a ɛfata, na wɔyɛ wɔn ho sɛ wɔnyɛ nnipa a wɔba bɛsɛe adeɛ ɛfiri sɛ wɔn tenabea ankasa ne serɛ so na ɛnyɛ bɔhyɛ asase no so. Na saa na prakɔ suban teɛ, ɛfiri sɛ pɛ a wɔbɛsan de wɔn akɔ wuram no, wɔn suban ankasa a ɛyɛ hu no da adi koraa. Nanso, wɔyɛ wɔn ho sɛ wɔte ase sɛ wɔn a wɔpɛ sɛ wɔdi wɔn so nkonim no na wɔbɛtu wɔn ase koraa afiri asase no so na wɔnni ntini anaa baa, ɛfiri sɛ saa na Yahweh ahyɛ ho bɔ. Enti monte ase sɛ saa mprako yi yɛ wɔn ho sɛ wɔyɛ bɔhyɛ asase no so mpanimfoɔ a wɔdɔ nnipa berɛ a nokorɛ mu no, wɔyɛ mmoa a wɔpɛ sɛ wɔsɛe nnipa na wɔbɛpɛ sɛ wɔsɛe biribiara a ɛfa me, Kristo Yesu ho, na mpo wɔbɛyi wɔn a wɔwɔ Asase no so a wɔdi wɔn Talmud mmara akyi akɔ ɔsɛeɛ mu no ama, nanso asase no sofoɔ nkaeɛ bi bɛba me, Yeshua HaMashiach nkyɛn, ɛfiri sɛ mɛgye Yakob nkwa!

Nnue mmɔborɔ ma wɔn a wɔfrɛ esum kanea na kanea nso esum! Mpo wɔn a wɔto hyɛ Yahweh, ne Yeshua HaMashiach so denam Onyankopɔn Teasefoɔ no mma so a wɔto hyɛ wɔn so no. Na ɛfiri sɛ wɔayɛ ama me nuanom a wɔsua koraa no mu baako no, wɔayɛ ama me, na aweretɔ yɛ me dea, sɛdeɛ Wura a ɔwɔ hɔ daa no seɛ, na mɛtua ka. Enti mommue mo ani, me mma, na mompo wɔn a wɔde Remfan nsoromma frafra wɔn frankaa so no! Efirisɛ sɛ woka wɔn ho a, na wote Molok ntomadan mu na wokotow wɔn Baphomet anim! Menka nkyerɛɛ mo sɛ monkan aboa no nɔma na saa nɔma no, wɔde anuonyam ahyɛ agyiraeɛ wɔ saa ahoni a wɔyɛ maa wɔn ho sɛ wɔnsom wɔn nsoromma nyame no so, na wɔn nsoromma no mfimfini ne wɔn ahina no! Monte saa abɛbuo yi ase, na nkontimma nsia na ɛkɔ nsa-nsa nsia a ɛyɛ nsia-nsia na emu na wɔn ahina no fata, enti monkan aboa no nɔma na monnyae Edomfoɔ nnaadaa no gyidie ɛfiri sɛ wɔsom wɔn a wɔahwe aseɛ no na wɔn nyame ne ɔwɔ kɛseɛ no.

Na firi Noa nna no so, mpo nsuyire no akyi no, wɔde Nephilim no honhom a wonni nipadua no mu nkyɛmu du mu nkron asie amena donkudonku no mu, ɛfiri sɛ Noa bɔɔ mpaeɛ maa ne nananom a wɔtow hyɛɛ wɔn so na wɔdii wɔn so nkonim no. Na saa nti, Yahweh dii Noa abisadeɛ so na ɔkyekyeree wɔn a wɔyɛ bɔne na wɔyɛ atantanneɛ paa no, na wɔde wɔn dodow mu nkyɛmu du mu nkron asie amena donkudonku no mu firi saa berɛ no. Tie me na tie me yie, me Mma, na te wo Wura nne ase. Berɛ a wɔboaboa anokwafoɔ ano wɔ m’Asɔre mu ne mpo m’Ayeforɔ ma me wɔ mununkum no mu no, wɔbɛyi deɛ ɔsi ano kwan no, na wɔabue amena donkudonku no mu koraa.

Wɔrensi bɔne kwan bio, ɛfiri sɛ Yahweh asi Satan ahennie no nyinaa kwan bio sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a wɔbɛgye wɔn a wɔyɛ nkwa a ɛnni awieeɛ adedifoɔ no nkwa, mpo wɔn a wɔayɛ me wɔn Wura wɔ m’adom ne me mmɔborɔhunu berɛ mu. Esiane sɛ nnipa apo me seesei nti, wɔrensi bɔne kwan mma nnipa bio. Monte ase sɛ wɔbɛyi saa ahonhom yi nyinaa, mpo dɔm kɛseɛ bi, dodoɔ kɛseɛ pa a wɔbɛboro asase so koraa de bɔne a wɔnsi ano kwan na wontumi nka ho asɛm aba. Na Henok ɔtwerɛfoɔ no twerɛɛ faa yeinom ho sɛ: Na akɛseɛ no ahonhom haw, hyɛ so, sɛe, to hyɛ so, ko, na wɔyɛ ɔsɛeɛ wɔ asase so, na wɔde ɔhaw ba: wɔnnidi, nanso ɛkɔm ne sukɔm de wɔn, na wɔma afoforɔ hwe ase. Na saa ahonhom yi bɛsɔre atia nnipa mma ne mmaa, ɛfiri sɛ wɔfiri wɔn mu na wɔbaeɛ.

Monkenkanee wɔ me nsɛm kronkron no mu baabi a metwaa Gadarenfoɔ asase so? Na metuu ahonhom firii nnipa baanu a wɔwɔ wɔn mu, na saa nti memaa saa ahonhom bɔne yi kwan sɛ wɔnkɔ mprakokuo bi a ɛbɛn hɔ no mu. Monte ase sɛ wɔrebɛyi Ɔwɔ kɛseɛ no ahennie no nyinaa adi berɛ tiawa bi, sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a wɔbɛka wɔn a wɔahyɛ wɔn ama nkwa a ɛnni awieeɛ no aba me nkwagyeɛ nsam! Na wɔbɛyi saa ahonhom a wɔde wɔn agu afiase yi adi na wɔakɔka Satan ahennie no ho koraa wɔ asase so. Na wɔbɛkɔ mprako no mu bio. Na wɔne nnipa ko na wɔpɛ sɛ wɔdi nnipa so nkonim ɛfiri sɛ wɔn akɔnnɔ ntumi nna da na yeinom ne ahonhom a wɔma mprako no tumi na wɔne mprako no yɛ adwuma bom na saa mprako a wɔyɛ hu yi ne wɔn teaseɛnam.

Ɛnyɛ wɔn nko ara na mmom wɔn a wɔgye wɔn aboa no agyiraeɛhyɛdeɛ no nso bɛyɛ teaseɛnam a wɔpɛ ama saa ahonhom a wɔbɛko atia adesamma bio no. Na wɔbɛmene onipa a ɔwɔ abɔdeɛ mu no bio wɔ saa nna a ɛdi akyire yi mu, ɛfiri sɛ saa na wɔahyɛ sɛ ɛnsi wɔ saa awieeɛ mmerɛ yi mu. Na awieeɛ no afi ase dedaw na ɛreba ntɛm paa sɛdeɛ Yakob haw no bɛpae wɔ asase no so. Awoɔ yaw no abɛn paa seesei ara a ɛnnyae da na Yahweh ayɛ nsɛnkyerɛnneɛ pii wɔ asase so ne nsɛnkyerɛnneɛ ne anwanwadeɛ pii wɔ wim ne ɔsoro ne asase so, nanso pii da so ara de gyidie a enni mu nteaseɛ yi saa kɔkɔbɔ yi to nkyɛn.

Hwɛ! Sɛ anka wɔbɛba adwensakra mu no, nnipadɔm no ani gye mpo wɔ mmoa nketenkete akuokuo a wɔakata nnipa pii so wɔ nsase pii so wɔ asase so no ho. Wɔresere na wɔreseresere kɔkɔbɔ a Yahweh de ma asase no, na Onyankopɔn a ɔwɔ hɔ daa no bɛsɔre afiri ne tenabea na wayi ne ho adi na wɔn a wɔahwe aseɛ no ho popo ɛfiri sɛ wɔn berɛ adu, na ehu a ɛwɔ hɔ daa ahyɛ wɔn ma. Na mo a moahwe aseɛ no, modwenee sɛ mobɛtumi de nnaadaa afa Onyankopɔn a ɔwɔ hɔ daa no, berɛ a biribi a ɛte saa ntumi nyɛ yie. Na ɛberɛ bɛn na nkuku a ɛnsom bo asɔre sɛ wɔrebɛko na wɔde nnaadaa afa ɔnwemfoɔ no? Nanso saa na wɔn a wɔahwe aseɛ no dadwene ne wɔn ahomasoɔ teɛ, na wɔrennya kwan nguan mfiri wɔn fononoo a ɛredɛre daa no mu.

Enti monte mmerɛ a mote mu no ase na montena me mu, m’Asɔre, mpo m’Ayeforɔ! Na mo nkwagyeɛ a ɛdi mu ne mo agyapadeɛ a ɛdi mu no ntwentwɛn ne nan ase, na wɔrennyaw mo wɔ mprako no mu ɛfiri sɛ mogyee me din dii wɔ saa adom ne mmɔborɔhunu nna yi mu, na saa nti, mobɛnya adom ne mmɔborɔhunu a ɛwɔ hɔ daa. Na mo nuanom bɛka mo ho wɔ daa nkwa mu ne m’ahennie a ɛreba a ɛyɛ tenenee no mu, mpo wɔn a wɔnsiesiee wɔn ho mmaa me mmaeɛ no nyinaa.

Na mene deɛ ɔhohoro nnipa ho a ɔsɔ ogya a ɛhohoro nnipa ho no mu sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a mɛhohoro Yahweh agyapadeɛ no ho, na mayi dadeɛ fĩ no, na mene deɛ ɔte sɛ ɔtomfoɔ a ɔkura mframa-hyɛn mu a ɔresɔ gya no mu na ɔdane na ɔbɔ Yahweh sika kɔkɔɔ a ɛsom bo no ma ɛnya ahoɔden a ɛwɔ hɔ daa na enni awieeɛ. Nnipakuo kɛseɛ, mpo nnipakuo pii, bɛba me nkyɛn afa Yakob haw no mu, na wɔbɛtwa nnɔbaeɛ kɛseɛ, na wɔyɛ nkwa a ɛnni awieeɛ adedifoɔ na wɔbɛkɔ anigyeɛ a ɛwɔ hɔ daa mu na wɔbɛgye Israel nkwa! Enti monnsuro, me mma, na munnwennwen mo adɔfoɔ ho na monnya gyidie wɔ me Kristo Yesu mu, na metumi agye wɔn a wɔbɔ me din nyinaa nkwa koraa, na obiara a wɔahyɛ no ama nkwa a ɛnni awieeɛ no, wontumi ntu no mfiri me nsam, na mɛgye pii afiri ogya a ɛwɔ hɔ daa no mu, mpo matwe wɔn afiri ogyaframa no mu.

Me Yeshua HaMashiach na mede me somfoɔ so asoma saa nsɛm yi, na me somfoɔ so na wɔde saa nsɛm yi asoma!

Mene deɛ ɔma anyansafoɔ nyansa, sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a onyansafoɔ bɛnya nyansa kɛseɛ mpo.

Mene Onyankopɔn Odwan a mereba sɛ Yuda Abusuakuo no mu Gyata.

Meyɛ deɛ bɔne nni me mu, nanso metuaa boɔ a ɛso a wontumi nsusuwɛ mmaa mo nkwagyeɛ ne mo ahofadie.

Mene deɛ ɔte sɛ ɔtomfoɔ a ɔdane na ɔbɔ Yahweh agyapadeɛ a ɛsom bo no.

Metumi agye wɔn a wɔbɔ me din nyinaa nkwa koraa, na obiara a wɔahyɛ no ama nkwa a ɛnni awieeɛ no, wontumi ntu no mfiri me nsam.

Monhwɛ me mmaeɛ kwan, na mmerɛ no adu. Berɛ a aka no, wɔde ama sɛ wɔnsom Yahweh atirimpɔ nko ara, me mma, enti mma mo ho nnto mo sɛ mobɛda bio, na mpo monyi me nyansa nsɛm ne me kɔkɔbɔ pii no nni.

Onyansafoɔ hunu ɔyaredɔm no na ɔde ne ho hinta, na nkwaseafoɔ deɛ, wɔkɔ so ara, na wɔhunu amane. Memfrɛɛ mo amma Yahweh nyansa a ɛwɔ hɔ daa no anaa?

Na mene deɛ ɔbɛhinta mo wɔ baabi a anka morentumi nhinta mo ho da, na mɛhinta mo wɔ Yahweh ahennwa dan mu mpo wɔ ɔsoro soro baabi a mobɛkɔ mo Wura anigyeɛ mu na ɔsoro apontoɔ adan no bɛdi mo nyinaa ho ahurisie, na ntɛm a morensusuwɛ no, mobɛde mo ara mo ani ahunu anuonyam kɛseɛ a ɛwɔ hɔ daa a ɛretwɛn mo wɔ mo agyapadeɛ mu no. Monkɔ so ntena me dɔ mu, na m’Agya dɔ mo yie pa ara na moyɛ m’ani so sika, a mefɛre sɛ mɛfrɛ mo me nuanom.

Me Yeshua HaMashiach na makasa na masoma saa nsɛm yi na saa nti wɔatwerɛ na wɔada Asɛmpa a Ɛwɔ Hɔ Daa no adi wɔ ne berɛ mu.
