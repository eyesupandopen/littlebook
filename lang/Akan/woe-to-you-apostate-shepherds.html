Adansefoɔ Mmienu: Asɔre no
Wɔ Ɔsomafoɔ Paulo Nnwoma mu
Kɔtɔnimaa 04, 2024

Nnue ma Mo, Ahunahunafoɔ Aguanhwɛfoɔ!

Nnue ma mo, ahunahunafoɔ aguanhwɛfoɔ, ɛfiri sɛ moadua mo asɔre a ɛnyɛ nokorɛ no wɔ nsase nyinaa so, na monkasa mma me, nso memmaa mo tumi wɔ nnwuma a mode faa mo ho no mu. Na moayɛ sika mo nyame, ɛfiri sɛ mode mo ho to sika fi so, na moakyerɛkyerɛ me nkurɔfoɔ sɛ ahonyadeɛ yɛ onyamesom. Nnue ma mo, ahunahunafoɔ aguanhwɛfoɔ, ɛfiri sɛ Yeremia nsɛm no ka mo ho asɛm pɛpɛɛpɛ: Afunumu mfunumu a ɔwɔ serɛ so, a ɔtwe mframa berɛ a ɔpɛ; ɛberɛ a ɔpɛ no, hwan na ɔbɛtumi adane no? Efisɛ mo awarebɔ ne mo adwamammɔɔ yɛ bɔne, ɛfiri sɛ moayi Satan sɛ mo nyame, na saa ahemfo a wɔnni mfasoɔ no nyɛ anyame. Moyɛ asomfoɔ a wɔnyɛ nokorɛ a wɔyɛ wɔn ho sɛ tenenee asomfoɔ, na ɛnyɛ nwanwa, ɛfiri sɛ Satan yɛ ne ho sɛ hann bɔfoɔ.

Nnue ma mo, ahunahunafoɔ aguanhwɛfoɔ, ɛfiri sɛ moadua asɔre a wɔayɛ me nkwa nsuo dɛdɛ no sɛ nwɔnwɔno a ɛyɛ nwunu, a mode ma wɔn a mafrɛ wɔn no, nsuo a ɛyɛ hye a ɛyɛ fi a ɛyɛ nwunu ma yafunu. Mo nsuo a ɛyɛ nwunu mpo no, mode gu nsuo ahyɛn a abubuo mu, na monni tumi, ɛwom sɛ mohuahoa mo ho wɔ tumi kɛseɛ ho deɛ. Moagu nsase no fi wɔ atoro nkyerɛkyerɛ so na moasi mo asɔre a ɛnyɛ nokorɛ no sɛ honhom mu afiase a mo aban asɛe no, ɛfiri sɛ moatɔn me nguan no ahyɛ ɔtamfo no nsa; enti, monfire ne mu seesei, me nkurɔfoɔ! Mo asɔre a ɛnni mfasoɔ a ɛyɛ ahunahuna no, wɔde yɛ adwuma sɛ mfinimfini a mobɛfa so de owuo ne ɔsɛeɛ abrɛ wɔn a wɔaka akyire no, na mobɛtua ka.

Moayɛ Remfan mo Nyame. Mosom Baal. Mobɔ mmofra afɔdeɛ ma Molok. Moasɛe koraa. Moyɛ nsubura a nsuo nni mu, nneɛma a ɛda hɔ a ɛfata ɔsɛeɛ, ɛfiri sɛ mo agyapadeɛ yɛ sufre a ɛredɛre, na mobɛkɔ ɔtare kɛseɛ no mu aka mo agya, ɔbonsam ho. Moyɛ asomafoɔ a wɔnyɛ nokorɛ, adiyifoɔ a wɔnyɛ nokorɛ, asɛmpakafoɔ a wɔnyɛ nokorɛ, asɔfoɔ a wɔnyɛ nokorɛ, ne akyerɛkyerɛfoɔ a wɔnyɛ nokorɛ; na wɔn a modane wɔn ma wɔkɔ mo akwan a ɛyɛ hu no, wɔyɛ ɔbonsam ba mmɔho mmienu sene mo, ɛfiri sɛ moyɛ ɔsɛeɛ a ɛsɛe koraa. Efisɛ Stefano dii kan kaa mo akwan ho asɛm berɛ a ɔkaa mo nananom ho asɛm, nwura a ɛhaw Israel no mpo, ɛfiri sɛ ɔkaa sɛ: Aane, mofaa Molok Ntomadan ne mo Nyame Remfan nsoromma, nsusuiɛ a moyɛeɛ sɛ mobɛsom wɔn no. Mo ahunahunafoɔ aguanhwɛfoɔ no mpo, mode aniwuo hyɛ Remfan nsoromma a mode nnim de bata Dawid, me nana ho; ɛfiri sɛ me, Yesu Kristo, na mefiri hɔ, na mɛdi hene afiri Dawid ahennwa so wɔ tenenee mu. Efisɛ mene onipa a wɔama no saa tumi no, ɛfiri sɛ mɛdi asase so hene wɔ tenenee mu!

Nnue ma mo, ahunahunafoɔ aguanhwɛfoɔ, mɛtua mo ka koraa wɔ mo nsɛm bɔne nyinaa ho, na mo akwan bɔne nyinaa bɛba mo ara mo ti so daa. Efisɛ mobɛka ɔbonsam ne n'abɔfoɔ ho wɔ wɔn agyapadeɛ a ɛfiri Onyankopɔn a ɔkorɔn no hɔ; Yahweh mpo asiesie ogya tare a ɛnnume da mma mo. Moapo nkwagyeɛ amma wɔn a wɔhwehwɛɛ no, nanso monim sɛ mɛgye wɔn na mobɛtua ka. Moapo me honhom amma wɔn a anka wɔbɛka me din no denam nsɛm a ɛnyɛ nokorɛ a mode ma wɔn sɛ wɔnka ne mpaebɔ a ɛyɛ hu, a ɛnni mfasoɔ na ɛsan so yɛ mpɛn pii a ɛnnye nkwa no so. Montie me, me nkurɔfoɔ: nkwagyeɛ nam Yesu a wobɛpae mu aka sɛ ɔyɛ wo Wura ne gyidie a wogye di sɛ Onyankopɔn nyanee me firii awufoɔ mu no so na ɛba, na ɛnam me ne wo ntam abusuabɔ a ɛgye nkwa no nkutoo so na wobɛtumi anya nkwagyeɛ; ɛfiri sɛ mede Onyankopɔn nsɔano no sie wɔn a wɔka me din firi akoma a gyidie nni mu mu, ɛfiri sɛ mehwehwɛ akoma mu na mesɔ nsɔano no hwɛ.

Mode ahomasoɔ susuw sɛ ɛfiri sɛ mofrɛ me Awurade wɔ mo asafo a moagye no nsam no anim nti na menim mo? Mɛka akyerɛ mo saa da no sɛ, monfire me so, mo amumuyɔfoɔ, ɛfiri sɛ mennim mo da! Mokyerɛkyerɛ atorɔsɛm sɛ nokorɛ, na modane wɔn a wɔrehwehwɛ me, Awurade Yesu Kristo no, kɔ asɛmpa a asɛe, a ɛyɛ nkwaseasɛm mu, ɛfiri sɛ asɛmpa foforɔ biara nni hɔ: Efisɛ asɛmpa wɔ me mu, Kristo Yesu! Mo nsɛm a ɛnni mfasoɔ no rebɛba awieeɛ, na m'abufuo ayɛ hye wɔ mo so, na morennya kwan nguan. Mehunu mo abususɛm biara a moyɛ; mete abususɛm biara a mokaeɛ wɔ me din mu, ɛfiri sɛ moyɛ ɔtorofoɔ. Moyɛ mo ho sɛ mokaeɛ me nsɛm, sɛdeɛ moyɛ mo ho sɛ mesomaa mo ne mo anansesɛm a ɛnyɛ nokorɛ ne mo anwanwadeɛ a ɛnyɛ nokorɛ ne mo ayaresa a ɛnyɛ nokorɛ a moayɛ no sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a ɛbɛyɛ sɛ ɛyɛ nokorɛ, ɛfiri sɛ moyɛ ɔtorofoɔ!

Nnue ma mo, ahunahunafoɔ aguanhwɛfoɔ, ɛfiri sɛ mowia firi wɔn a anka wɔbɛba me nkyɛn no hɔ, na modi atorɔ kyerɛ wɔn sɛ Onyankopɔn ahyɛ wɔn sɛ wɔmma mo sika. Mode nnim twitwiw me Kyerɛwsɛm Kronkron no mu, na moka kyerɛ wɔn a moadaadaa wɔn no sɛ ɛsɛ sɛ wɔtɔ ntotosoɔ ma mo. Efisɛ ɛdeɛn na meka kyerɛɛ Paulo berɛ a ɔkyerɛw kɔmaa wɔn a wɔwɔ Korinto no: Nanso yei na meka, deɛ ɔdua adeɛ ketewaa bi no, ɔbɛtwa ketewaa bi: na deɛ ɔdua adeɛ bebree no, ɔbɛtwa bebree. Onipa biara sɛdeɛ ɔsi pɛ wɔ ne komam no, saa ara na ɔmma; ɛnyɛ anibereɛ so, anaa ɛhia sɛ ɔyɛ saa: ɛfiri sɛ Onyankopɔn pɛ ɔmafoɔ a n'ani gye. Nanso daabi, mokyerɛkyerɛ atorɔsɛm sɛ nokorɛ na mowia firi me nkurɔfoɔ hɔ, na sika a mogyigye no mpo, mode som Satan atirimpɔ, na mobɛtua ka, ɛfiri sɛ m'abufuo ayɛ kɔkɔɔ wɔ mo so ɛfiri sɛ modane nkurɔfoɔ a wɔhwehwɛ me, Yesu Kristo no, firi nkwagyeɛ so kɔ ɔsɛeɛ mu. Nanso monim yei, ahunahunafoɔ aguanhwɛfoɔ, morentumi ntutu yeinom mu baako mpo mfiri me nsam, ɛfiri sɛ wɔn a Agya no de wɔn ama me nyinaa mu no, meyeraa baako pɛ, ɔsɛeɛ ba no, Yuda mpo, ɛfiri sɛ ɔyii n'ankasa ɔsɛeɛ sene nkwa a ɛwɔ me mu! Mo nnwuma ne mo adwumaden yɛ kwa, o ahunahunafoɔ aguanhwɛfoɔ; moaboaboa atemmuo ne abufuo kɛseɛ ano ama mo ho. Efisɛ mekaa mo nananom akwan ho asɛm, na moyɛ wɔn a wɔdi akunafoɔ afie na wɔde animguaseɛ bɔ mpaeɛ tenten: Mobɛnya ɔdane kɛseɛ!

Nnue ma mo, ahunahunafoɔ aguanhwɛfoɔ, ɛfiri sɛ moakyerɛ me nkurɔfoɔ kwan ma wɔatena wiase aban a ɛnyɛ me dea akyi, ɛwom sɛ asase yɛ me dea deɛ. O, mo ahunahunafoɔ aguanhwɛfoɔ, mokyerɛ me nkurɔfoɔ kwan kɔ wiase akannifoɔ a wɔfiri ɔbonsam hɔ, na moka kyerɛ wɔn sɛ wɔfiri Onyankopɔn hɔ. Modane nkurɔfoɔ kɔ adwenem naayɛ mu sɛ merenhyira wɔn gye sɛ wɔhyira wɔn a wɔkum nnipa a wɔnni fɔ no. Nanso ɛdeɛn na maka wɔ kyerɛwsɛm mu fa mo ho, ɛfiri sɛ yei nyɛ foforɔ, na nnipa pii dii kan baa mo anim wɔ nnaadaa mu, ɛwom sɛ moyɛ nkwasea sɛ mobɛdwene sɛ moyɛ mfitiaseɛ deɛ. Montie me, me mma, ɛfiri sɛ mereyi saa bɔne yi adi ama mo nti. Efisɛ me kyerɛwsɛm ka sɛ: Mo mma ketewa, mofiri Onyankopɔn mu, na moadi wɔn so nkonim: ɛfiri sɛ deɛ ɔwɔ mo mu no so sene deɛ ɔwɔ wiase no. Wɔfiri wiase: enti wɔka wiase ho asɛm, na wiase tie wɔn. Mo wiase asɛnka, mo wiase nneyɛeɛ, mo wiase apam a mo ne Satan ayɛ no, ne nyinaa bɛba awieeɛ prɛko pɛ, na morensɔre bio. Nanso wo, m'ayeforɔ, woyɛ me dea, na saa nsɛm yi nka wo, ɛfiri sɛ wobɛkɔ wo Wura, me, Kristo Yesu anigyeɛ mu.

Merekasa akyerɛ ahunahunafoɔ asɔre a ahunahunafoɔ aguanhwɛfoɔ di anim no asafo no, wo mpo, me Laodikea asafo no. Menim wo nnwuma, sɛ wonyɛ nwunu nso wonyɛ hye; mepɛ sɛ anka woyɛ nwunu anaa hye. Efisɛ Paulo kyerɛw kɔmaa Timoteo faa wo ne afoforɔ a wɔte sɛ wo ho: Efisɛ ɛberɛ bɛba a wɔrentumi nnye nkyerɛkyerɛ pa, na mmom wɔn ankasa akɔnnɔ akyi no, wɔbɛboaboa akyerɛkyerɛfoɔ ano ama wɔn ho, a wɔn aso ayɛ wɔn krakra: Na wɔbɛdane wɔn aso afiri nokorɛ no ho, na wɔadan akɔ anansesɛm so. Mo mu pii kɔ saa ahunahunafoɔ asɔre yi ase wɔ nnipa ntam nhyɛsoɔ ase ne sɛ mobɛyɛ mo ho sɛ morebɔ mmɔden sɛ morehwehwɛ me, Kristo Yesu, a nokorɛ mu deɛ, monhwehwɛ.

Mopɛ sɛ wɔma mo ani gye, ɛfiri sɛ anigyeɛ ne mo akɔnnɔ, na anigyeɛ abɛyɛ mo abosom! Na morentie me, Yesu Kristo, na morentie m'asomafoɔ no mu biara gye sɛ wɔkiti mo aso, nanso me nsɛm nkiti aso! Mo ahunahunafoɔ asafo: Montie me na montie me yie, ɛfiri sɛ mekaa wɔ Adiyisɛm nwoma kɛseɛ no mu sɛ: Na metee nne foforɔ firi soro sɛ, Monfire ne mu, me nkurɔfoɔ, na moanyɛ ne bɔne no mu kyɛfa, na moannya ne ɔhaw no bi: Efisɛ ne bɔne adu soro, na Onyankopɔn akae ne amumuyɔ. Monfire ne mu, anyɛ saa a mobɛnya ne ɔhaw ne n'atemmuo no mu kyɛfa, ɛfiri sɛ ɛberɛ adu. Ma me Laodikeafoɔ: Monfiri mo ahunahunafoɔ asɔre no mu seesei, ɛfiri sɛ ɛberɛ no abɛn, na merebɔ mo kɔkɔ, ɛwom sɛ mo mu pii yɛ nkwadwofoɔ dodo sɛ mobɛyɛ adwuma wɔ me nsɛm so.

Monntwentwɛn mo nan ase wɔ me kɔkɔbɔ no ho, ɛfiri sɛ ɛdeɛn na memaa Yohane sɛ ɔnkyerɛw wɔ ne krataa a ɛtɔ so mmienu no mu? Obiara a ɔto mmara na ɔntena Kristo nkyerɛkyerɛ mu no, ɔnni Onyankopɔn: deɛ ɔtena Kristo nkyerɛkyerɛ mu no, ɔwɔ Agya ne ɔba no nyinaa. Sɛ obi ba mo nkyɛn, na ɔmfa saa nkyerɛkyerɛ yi mmra a, monnye no wɔ mo fie, na monnkyia no mpo. Efisɛ deɛ ɔkyia no no, ɔyɛ ne nnwuma bɔne no mu kyɛfa. Nanso mo Laodikeafoɔ, moyɛ asoɔdenfoɔ araa ma motwe mo ho firi saa nsɛm yi ho na mmom sɛ mobɛpɛ wɔn yie, a ɛno mpo yɛ bɔne no, mokɔ bɔne mu kɔ akyiri na mofrɛ wɔn ba mo afie mu na moma wɔnya mo mma so kwan, na moadi fɔ koraa wɔ m'anim, enti MONNU MO HO!

Efisɛ nokorɛ mu, mobuee mo apono maa wiase ne n'akwan; momaa mpataku baa mu, mpo wɔn a wɔwɔ fam koraa, sɛ wɔmmɛdi me mma so, na gye sɛ monu mo ho, mobɛtua ka. Monu mo ho anyɛ saa a mɛba mo so, ɛfiri sɛ moyɛ wɔn nnwuma bɔne no mu kyɛfa, na moyɛ asoɔden ma me, Kristo Yesu, ɔno a ɔbɛgye mo akatua afiri mo nsam na ɔde ama obi foforɔ sɛ mokɔ so bu mo ani gu me so a. Efisɛ m'adom ne me mmɔborɔhunu rebɛba awieeɛ, na abufuo bɛtena ananmu, ɛwom sɛ adom ne mmɔborɔhunu ka me ho daa, na obiara a ɔbɔ Awurade din kɔsi awieeɛ no, wɔbɛgye no nkwa. Efisɛ morentumi ne sum akwan a ɛnso aba no nni ayɔnkofa, na mmom ɛsɛ sɛ mokasa tia.

Nanso moyɛ mmerɛ dodo sɛ mobɛdwene sɛ ɛfiri sɛ obi di atorɔ na ɔde abususɛm de me din kronkron no gu n'adwuma a ɛnyɛ kronkron so nti na me na mesomaa no na ɔka me nsɛm. Montie me nsɛm, ɛfiri sɛ wɔn aba so na mobɛhunu wɔn! Monsan nkɔ me Onyankopɔn Asɛm a ɛho te no so na monsua me ho adeɛ, ɛfiri sɛ monnim me na me nne yɛ ahɔhoɔ ma mo, na monni Agya no ɛfiri sɛ monni ɔba no. Monnane mmra me nkyɛn wɔ adwensakra mu, na mɛgye mo. Montena ntoma sakyee ne nsõ mu wɔ mo akoma mu na mommrɛ mo ho ase na matumi ama mo so! Mefrɛɛ mo sɛ monni nkonim, na seesei ne mo hokwan a ɛtwa toɔ sɛ mobɛdi saa ahunahunafoɔ aguanhwɛfoɔ yi ne wɔn akwan bɔne no so nkonim; ɛfiri sɛ mereba, na mede abufuo kɛseɛ reba Ahintasɛm Babilon so.

Ɛberɛ yɛ adeɛ a ɛsom bo a monni bio. Morentumi nyɛ nkwadwofoɔ bio, anyɛ saa a mobɛhunu mo ho wɔ Yakob ɔhaw mu, berɛ a anka mɛsiesie beaeɛ ama mo wɔ me pon so na maware mo sɛ m'ayeforɔ, nanso mode nokorɛ di foforɔ, Satan mpo nsam! Enti mommra me nkyɛn na matumi asan de mo akatua ama mo. Ɛnsɛ sɛ mode abufuo firi hɔ kɛkɛ; ɛsɛ sɛ monu mo ho wɔ saa amumuyɔ yi ho na mosakra mo akwan na moka saa bɔne yi kyerɛ me, na mɛhohoro mo wɔ me mogya a ɛsɔre no mu na mayi nkekaeɛ no afiri mo so wɔ me kronkronyɛ a ɛdi mu no mu, ɛfiri sɛ me ho te na m'atadeɛ yɛ fitaa sɛ sukyerɛmma foforɔ. Monsan mmra me nkyɛn na daa mobɛtena me nkyɛn; momfiri adi na mommra me nsa a matene mu no mu, ɛfiri sɛ mewɔ ha sɛ mɛka akyerɛ mo sɛ monni ɛberɛ a mobɛsɛe.

Ma m'Ayeforɔ nokwafoɔ, a moatie me nne na moadi m'akwan akyi no: Mo deɛ, meka sɛ, monkɔ so nni nokorɛ ma me, na sɛ mo mu biara de ne nan baako ahyɛ Laodikea asɔre no pono mu a, mehyɛ mo sɛ monyi mfiri ɔpono no ano na momma ɔpono no nto mu! Efisɛ morentumi nyɛ deɛ mo Wura renyɛ! Monkenkanee me krataa a mekɔmaa Laodikea asɔre no wɔ Adiyisɛm mu no anaa? Hwɛ, megyina ɔpono no ano, na merebɔ mu: sɛ onipa bi te me nne, na ɔbue ɔpono no a, mɛkɔ ne nkyɛn, na me ne no adidi, na ɔne me adidi. Monhwɛ yie, manka sɛ mɛkɔ Laodikea Asɔre no mu, na mmom sɛ onipa biara a ɔwɔ Laodikea asɔre no mu te me nne na ɔbue ɔpono no a, mɛkɔ akɔdidi ne no, ɛnyɛ n'asɔre a na ɛyɛ ahunahuna no, ɛwom sɛ mefrɛ wɔn nso sɛ wɔbɛtie nko ara. M'Ayeforɔ, akoa nso sene ne wura, enti monni m'akwan akyi.

M'Ayeforɔ, ɛkaa kakra bi na mafa mo akɔ soro, enti monkɔ so nsiesie mo ho na monkɔ so nka me mmaeɛ no ho asɛm. Efisɛ masoma mo mu pii wɔ Yohane Suboni adwuma mu sɛ wɔnka me mmaeɛ no ho asɛm na wɔnfrɛ nnipa sɛ wɔnsakra wɔn adwene. Masoma mo mu pii wɔ Petro adwuma mu sɛ wɔnhwɛ nguan no na wɔmma wɔn aduane. Masoma mo mu binom sɛ Onyankopɔn ahintasɛm kɛseɛ no so ahwɛfoɔ, wɔ Paulo ɔsomafoɔ (ne ahintasɛm no so ahwɛfoɔ foforɔ) adwuma mu. Masoma mo mu pii wɔ Filipo ɔsɛmpakafoɔ no adwuma mu, enti monka asɛm no; monyɛ ntɛm wɔ ɛberɛ pa ne ɛberɛ bɔne mu; monka anim, monka anim, monhyɛ nkuran wɔ abodwokyɛre ne nkyerɛkyerɛ nyinaa mu. Masoma mo mu binom wɔ ɔsomafoɔ Yohane adwuma mu, a na ɔsan yɛ ɔsɔfoɔ na ɔhwɛɛ nguan no so wɔ ɔdɔ, ayamhyehyeɛ, ne mmɔborɔhunu mu, na ɔsaa apirakuru a ɔbɔnefoɔ no de baa me nguan so no mpo. Monnnye ho akyinnyeɛ, ɛfiri sɛ masoma mo wɔ wɔn nnwuma mu; ɛfiri sɛ ɛberɛ adu, ɛfiri sɛ merebɛware m'Ayeforɔ, ɛfiri sɛ mereba abɛfa mo, me dɔfoɔ! Monwie mo nnwuma, ɛfiri sɛ mabɛn. Ɛberɛ asa.

Mene Yesu Nasareni no.

Mene deɛ ɔbɛdi hene afiri Dawid ahennwa so, ɛfiri sɛ saa na mefiri.

Mereyi saa bɔne yi adi, ɛwom sɛ Satan pɛ sɛ ɛtena ahintasɛm mu.

Merebɔ mo kɔkɔ, ɛwom sɛ mo mu pii yɛ nkwadwofoɔ dodo sɛ mobɛyɛ adwuma wɔ me nsɛm so.

Mereba, na mede abufuo kɛseɛ reba Ahintasɛm Babilon so.

Me ho te na m'atadeɛ yɛ fitaa sɛ sukyerɛmma foforɔ.

Mewɔ ha sɛ mɛka akyerɛ mo sɛ monni ɛberɛ a mobɛsɛe.

Merefrɛ, titire wo, me Laodikea Asɔre.

Ɛkaa kakra bi na mafa mo akɔ soro.

Merebɛware m'Ayeforɔ.

Mereba abɛfa mo, me dɔfoɔ!

Mabɛn na medɔ mo yie; mowɔ me koma mfinimfini.

Mene mo anidasoɔ ne nkwagyeɛ, Awuradefoɔ Awurade ne Ahemfo Hene mpo.

Mene Yeshua HaMashiach.
