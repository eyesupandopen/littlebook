Adanseɛ Mmienu: Asɔre no
Wɔ Ɔsomafoɔ Paulo Nnwoma mu
Oforisuo 30, 2024

Onyankopɔn yɛ ɔdɔ!

Me mma, monhunu mo su ankasa, ɛfiri sɛ mo deɛ yɛ su korɔ no ara a Onyankopɔn de maeɛ wɔ me, Kristo Yesu mu, ɛfiri sɛ Onyankopɔn yɛ ɔdɔ! Nanso, Onyankopɔn dɔ nte sɛ onipa dɔ sɛdeɛ Onyankopɔn akwan nso nte sɛ onipa akwan no; nanso mosua Onyankopɔn akwan wɔ me mu, ɛfiri sɛ meyɛ mo kyerɛkyerɛfoɔ ne mo hwɛfoɔ. Sɛdeɛ Onyankopɔn dɔɔ wiase ara ni, sɛ ɔde ne Ba a ɔwoo no koro no maeɛ, na obiara a ɔgye no die no anyera, na wanya nkwa a ɛnni awieeɛ. Na wɔ Onyankopɔn dwumadie yi a ɔde me, Yesu Kristo, ne ba a ɔwoo no koro no maeɛ ɛnam nnipa bɔne nti no, ne dɔ da adi wɔ pɛyɛ mu wɔ me mu. Efiri sɛ mefiri Agya no nkyɛn na meyɛ su korɔ ne honhom korɔ no ara, ɛfiri sɛ Onyankopɔn yɛ Honhom, na wɔn a wɔsom no no, ɛwɔ sɛ wɔsom no wɔ honhom ne nokorɛ mu, ɛfiri sɛ me ne m’Agya yɛ baako wɔ honhom, baakoyɛ, ne botaeɛ mu.

Monhunu sɛ Onyankopɔn dɔ nte sɛ onipa dɔ, ɛfiri sɛ onipa dɔ taa gyina biribi so, na onipa a wahwe aseɛ no taa da ne dɔ adi wɔ adom a ɔde ma na ɔgyeɛ mu, na onipa dɔ dane kɔ ne ho so de boa onipa a ɔdɔ no, ɛfiri sɛ saa na onipa dɔ teɛ. Mpɛn pii no, Onyankopɔn dɔ da adi wɔ biribi a wɔde ma kwa de boa obi foforɔ, ne wo yɔnko nipa a wosom no a wunhwehwɛ akatua ɛtɔ da, ne akunafoɔ a wokɔsra wɔn, ayarefoɔ a wosa wɔn yareɛ, ne Kristo Nipadua no a wosi no pi. Enti mebisa mo, me mma, sɛ monsua biribi mfiri me Kristo Yesu hɔ, ɛfiri sɛ deɛ wahunu me no ahunu Agya no, na ɛwom sɛ monhunuu me wɔ mo ankasa ani so deɛ, nanso ɛrenkyɛ koraa mobɛhunu me.

Na nhyira ne deɛ ɔnhunuiɛ nanso ɔgye diiɛ, na moahunu me wɔ honhom ne nokorɛ mu, na moahunu me wɔ m’akwan so ne ɔdɔ a mesoma no so ne adwuma a m’asomfoɔ yɛ ma me ɛnam mo nti no so. Enti monim me na moahunu me, na sɛ monantew Kristo honhom a wɔawo no wɔ mo mu no mu a, nokorɛm monantew ɔdɔ mu, ɛfiri sɛ ɛno ne me su a mede ama mo, na saa nso na m’Agya a ɔde ne su ama me no teɛ: Hwɛ, ɔdɔ korɔ bɛn na Agya no de adom yɛn, sɛ wɔbɛfrɛ yɛn Onyankopɔn mma: ɛno nti na wiase nnim yɛn, ɛfiri sɛ ɛannim no. Na bio firi Yohane nwoma a ɛdi kan no mu: Ɔdɔ wɔ yei mu, ɛnyɛ sɛ yɛn na yɛdɔɔ Onyankopɔn, na mmom ɔno na ɔdɔɔ yɛn, na ɔsomaa ne Ba no sɛ ɔmmɛyɛ mpata mma yɛn bɔne. Adɔfoɔ, sɛ Onyankopɔn dɔɔ yɛn saa a, ɛsɛ sɛ yɛn nso yɛdodɔ yɛn ho.

Me mma nketewa, moyɛ Onyankopɔn teasefoɔ, Yahweh mpo, mma, ɛfiri sɛ sɛdeɛ Yahweh duaa turo wɔ apueeɛ fam wɔ Eden no, saa ara na waduadua ɔba su honhom no aba wɔ mo nipasu ankasa mu, ɛfiri sɛ meyɛ ne turo so hwɛfoɔ a ɔhwɛ mo akra so. Efiri sɛ wɔasan abɔ mo wɔ me mu, na wɔabɔ mo wɔ ɔdɔ mu, na Onyankopɔn dɔ wɔ mo nipasu ankasa mu. Monhunu sɛ mo gyeɛ a ɛdi mu no abɛn anaponoeɛ a mobɛhunu sɛdeɛ wɔhunu mo no. M’ayeforɔ, mewɔ pii a mɛkyerɛkyerɛ mo na makyerɛ mo na mayi adi akyerɛ mo fa mo ho ne atirimpɔ a wɔate mo ho ama sɛ monni ho dwuma, ɛfiri sɛ ayɛ ahintasɛm wɔ Agya no akoma mu firi abɔdeɛ mfiaseɛ, na ɛrenkyɛ koraa mobɛte aseɛ.

Enti hwan na ɔbɛtete mo afiri Onyankopɔn dɔ no ho? Ahokyere, anaa amanehunu, anaa ɔtaa, anaa ɛkɔm, anaa adagya, anaa asiane, anaa nkrantɛ? Daabi, moyɛ nkonimdifoɔ a mosen saa wɔ me, Kristo Yesu mu, na me dɔ reba ne nwieeɛ pɛyɛ mu wɔ mo, m’ayeforɔ mu, ɛfiri sɛ mmerɛ no adu na ɛrenkyɛ koraa mobɛhunu. M’Agya dɔ yɛ deɛ wontumi nni so nkonim, ɛfiri sɛ ɔyɛ deɛ wontumi nni ne so nkonim, ɛfiri sɛ Onyankopɔn yɛ ɔdɔ, na ɔdɔ ne Yahweh nipasu ankasa ɛfiri sɛ Onyankopɔn yɛ ɔdɔ. Satan nnaadaa pii a ɔde di dwuma de di mo amim no mu baako ne sɛ ɔda ɔdɔ adi sɛ deɛ ɛyɛ mmerɛ, enni anisoadeɛ, ɛpɛ ne yieyɛ na ɛnni mfasoɔ: Efiri sɛ yeinom ne onipa abɔdeɛ mu akwan.

Nokorɛm, Satan apa, ɛfiri sɛ ɔno na wɔama no ayɛ ɔfasu, na wontumi nni m’Agya dɔ so nkonim, ɛfiri sɛ obiara nni hɔ a ɔte sɛ ɔno! Wɔ me mu na ne dɔ yɛɛ pɛ wɔ ɔhonam mu, ɛfiri sɛ ɛnam ɔdɔ a mewɔ ma m’Agya ne mo, m’ayeforɔ nti na mede me ho maeɛ sɛ agyedeɛ a bɔne nni ho; ɛfiri sɛ wɔdɔ mo yie. Na monhunu sɛ moyɛ Yahweh ani so sradeɛ, na mo, m’ayeforɔ, moyɛ m’ani so sradeɛ, na ɔdɔ mu na megyee mo firii ɔtamfo no nsam pɛpɛɛpɛ, a hwee anka, wɔ me mogya a ɛtɔɔ mpata no mu. Ma Awurade agyefoɔ no nka saa, wɔn a wagye wɔn afiri ɔtamfo no nsam!

Me mma, esiane sɛ Onyankopɔn dɔ mo saa nti, ɛyɛ mo asɛdeɛ sɛ mogye ne dɔ no tom na mogye ne nsɛm a ɛfa mo ho ne ogye kɛseɛ a Agya no nam m’afɔrebɔ so yɛeɛ sɛ mo Twam a ɛyɛ pɛ no tom. Mo mu pii nte abɔdeɛ a mede mo ayɛ wɔ me, Kristo Yesu mu, ne honhom a mabɔ wɔ mo mu no nwieeɛ no ase, ɛfiri sɛ saa honhom no wɔ tumi, na ɛyɛ ɔdɔ; ɛfiri sɛ ɛyɛ Agya no nipasu ankasa, ɛfiri sɛ Yahweh yɛ ɔdɔ. Hwɛ, ɔdɔ kɛseɛ biara nni hɔ a ɛsen yei, sɛ onipa de ne kra bɛto hɔ ama ne nnamfonom. Na mede me kra too hɔ maa me nnamfonom, na ɛnam so mayɛ mo me nuanom na mayɛ mo Onyankopɔn mma: Hwɛ, ɔdɔ bɛn na Agya no de ama yɛn, sɛ wɔbɛfrɛ yɛn Onyankopɔn mma: ɛno nti na wiase yi nnim mo, ɛfiri sɛ ɛnnim no.

Montie me Nsɛm a mede maa Yohane wɔ ne nwoma a ɛdi kan no mu, na montie no yie. Yahweh de adom a ɛkorɔn paa ama mo atia ɔkasatiafoɔ no ne wɔn a wɔbu mo fɔ no, na wama wɔn nsɛmmɔne no ayɛ hunu atia mo, ɛfiri sɛ wɔde me mogya a bɔne nni ho atɔ mo, na wɔabu mo bem afiri nsɛmmɔne nyinaa ho wɔ me mpata adwuma no mu. Monkenkanee deɛ meka kyerɛɛ Romafoɔ no mfeɛ pii a atwam ni? Hwan na ɔbɛbɔ Onyankopɔn apawfoɔ no soboɔ? Onyankopɔn na ɔbu bem: Hwan na ɔbu fɔ? Kristo na ɔwuiɛ, aane, mmom a wasɔre bio, ɔno na ɔte Onyankopɔn nifa so, na ɔsan srɛ ma yɛn. Monte Hebrifoɔ adanseɛ no, baabi a wɔn nne di me ɔsɔfodie a ɛnsesa no ho adanseɛ? Ɛno nti ɔtumi nso gye wɔn a wɔnam ne so ba Onyankopɔn nkyɛn no nkwa koraa, esiane sɛ ɔte ase daa de srɛ ma wɔn.

Montie nsɛm yinom, na momma ɛnkɔ mo nipasu mu ankasa, na montie nne a mede maa Roma asɔre no: Efiri sɛ ɔno a ɔnnim bɔne no, ɔyɛɛ no bɔne maa yɛn, na yɛnam ne so abɛyɛ Onyankopɔn tenenee. Nsɛm yinom yɛ anwanwasɛm dodo ma mo anaa? Mobɛma mo ho kwan ate aseɛ, anaa mobɛsi mo aso wɔ mo amanneɛ hunu ne adanseɛ hunu a mokura mu a atoro adiyifoɔ hunu de ama mo de adi mo amim no mu? Ɛyɛ den dodo sɛ mobɛgye atom sɛ wɔayɛ mo Onyankopɔn tenenee wɔ me, Kristo Yesu mu?

Mehia sɛ moyɛ dinn koraa wɔ mo adwene ne mo akoma mu na momma me nne nka mo honhom asɛm na montie me nsɛm. Momrɛ mo akoma ase na monnwiriw mo ahomasoɔ ngu; ɛfiri sɛ mewɔ biribi a mɛka akyerɛ mo a ɛbɛgye mo afiri nkoasom mu sɛ mobɛgye nsɛm pɔtee a merebɛsan de ama mo bio yi adi. Efiri sɛ Romafoɔ nne kasa kyerɛ mo berɛ a morekenkan no, enti monkenkan na monte aseɛ; na wɔ nteaseɛ mu no, monnye nokorɛ a ɛfa mo gyinabea wɔ me, Kristo Yesu mu no ntom. Na me nsɛm ne yeinom: Enti afei, afɔbuo biara nni hɔ mma wɔn a wɔwɔ Kristo Yesu mu, wɔn a wɔnnantew ɔhonam akwan so, na mmom honhom no akwan so. Efiri sɛ nkwa honhom no mmara, a ɛwɔ Kristo Yesu mu no, agye me afiri bɔne ne owuo mmara no mu. Mma mo ani nntra nsɛm no so kɛkɛ, na mmom monkenkan emu biara brɛoo ne gyidie mu, gyidie wɔ me mu, ɛfiri sɛ maka nsɛm yinom agu mo so, me mma; na me, Kristo Yesu, magye mo afiri afɔbuo nyinaa ho wɔ me mu. Monte aseɛ sɛ wɔayɛ mo Onyankopɔn tenenee anaa? Monte m’ase na montie me nne na monhwɛ me nsɛm yie: Hwan na ɔbu mo fɔ? Kristo na ɔwuiɛ, aane, mmom a wasɔre bio, ɔno na ɔte Onyankopɔn nifa so, na ɔsan srɛ ma yɛn.

Me mma, mamfrɛ mo amma me ho wɔ tenenee ne kronkronyɛ nokorɛ mu sɛ mɛbu mo fɔ, na ɛwɔ sɛ mote nokorɛ no ase: Monni ho kwan sɛ mobu mo ho fɔ, na mo mu pii yɛ saa, na mo asetena yɛ amanehunu wɔ ɔtan a motan mo ho a ɔtamfo no de agu mo so no ho. Momma memmu ɔtamfo no nkɔnsɔnkɔnsɔn atoro yinom, na montie me nsɛm na monnye nni, ɛfiri sɛ ɛyɛ nokorɛ, na wɔatwerɛ ama mo sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a mobɛnya ahofadie ankasa wɔ me mu. Efiri sɛ mayi mo afiri afɔbuo nyinaa mu, na wɔatu ɔtamfo ne anuanom soboɔfoɔ no agu fam: Afei na nkwagyeɛ, ne ahoɔden, ne yɛn Nyankopɔn ahennie, ne ne Kristo tumi no aba: ɛfiri sɛ wɔatu yɛn nuanom soboɔfoɔ no agu fam, deɛ ɔbɔɔ wɔn soboɔ wɔ yɛn Nyankopɔn anim awia ne anadwo no.

Monte aseɛ, me mma, sɛ moyɛ ateneneefoɔ, na sɛ mobu mo ho fɔ a, ɛkyerɛ sɛ mopoo me nsɛm a Agya no de ama mo no; enti monnyɛ gyidie-nni-foɔ, na mmom monnya me nsɛm mu ahotosoɔ kɛseɛ, ɛfiri sɛ ɛyɛ nokorɛ, na wɔmmaa adanseɛ a ɛyɛ nokorɛ sen saa da. Monhunu nsonsonoeɛ a ɛda ɔfɔ a mobu mo ho ne me honhom a ɛyɛ adwuma wɔ mo mu de twe mo adwene si bɔne so no ntam; ɛfiri sɛ ɛdeɛn na meka kyerɛɛ Yohane wɔ ne nwoma a ɛdi kan no mu: Sɛ yɛn akoma bu yɛn fɔ a, Onyankopɔn so sen yɛn akoma, na ɔnim nnoɔma nyinaa. Adɔfoɔ, sɛ yɛn akoma mmu yɛn fɔ a, ɛnneɛ yɛwɔ Onyankopɔn anim akokoɔduru. Monte aseɛ sɛ me honhom a mabɔ wɔ mo mu biara, m’ayeforɔ, no, nokorɛm twe mo adwene si mo bɔne so mpo berɛ a wɔawo mo foforɔ wɔ me, Kristo Yesu, mo Awurade mu akyi?

Monkenkanee me adanseɛ a wɔatwerɛ wɔ Yohane asɛmpa no mu a ɛfa ɔwerɛkyekyefoɔ no ho anaa? Na sɛ ɔba a, ɔbɛtwe wiase adwene asi bɔne, ne tenenee, ne atemmuo so. Bɔne ho, ɛfiri sɛ wɔannye me anni. Tenenee ho, ɛfiri sɛ mekɔ m’Agya nkyɛn, na monhunu me bio. Atemmuo ho, ɛfiri sɛ wɔabu wiase yi hene fɔ. Me mma, ɔwerɛkyekyefoɔ no twee mo adwene sii bɔne so, na mo adane aba me nkyɛn, na wɔ awoɔ foforɔ ne honhom a mabɔ wɔ mo mu no tumi mu, honhom a ɛyɛ ɔba su no, Kristo a ɔwɔ mo mu a ɛyɛ animuonyam anidasoɔ no, mayɛ mo Onyankopɔn tenenee wɔ me mu. Enti, wɔntwe mo adwene nsi bɔne so bio, na mmom wɔtwe mo adwene si tenenee so sɛ moyɛ bɔne a, mpo sɛ modane kɔ mo akwan dada so a, ɛfiri sɛ ɔwɔ no pɛ sɛ ɔdi me nkurɔfoɔ amim na afei ɔde soboɔ ba m’Agya ne me anim wɔ ne nnwuma bɔne ho, na meyɛ mo ntamgyinafoɔ.

Na bio, Yohane adanseɛ a ɛwɔ ne nwoma a ɛdi kan no mu no kasa fa asɛm no ho: Me mma nketewa, nnoɔma yinom na metwerɛ ma mo, sɛ monnyɛ bɔne. Na sɛ obi yɛ bɔne a, yɛwɔ ɔgyinafoɔ wɔ Agya no nkyɛn, Yesu Kristo ɔteneneeni no: Na ɔno ne yɛn bɔne ho mpata: na ɛnyɛ yɛn deɛ nko, na mmom wiase nyinaa bɔne nso. Me mma, meyɛ mo gyinafoɔ, na moyɛ ateneneefoɔ wɔ me mu, na moyɛ ateneneefoɔ daa daa, ɛfiri sɛ wɔawo tenenee wɔ mo nipasu ankasa mu. Enti monya akoma brɛbrɛ mma me, na mɛkyerɛ mo mo bɔne ne mo mfomsoɔ, mpo mo su bɔne ne mo akwan bɔne a atwam a ɛtɔ da a wɔsɔ mo hwɛ sɛ mobɛsane akɔ mu no. Efiri sɛ sɛ mo akoma twe mo adwene si so a, monnane mmra me nkyɛn na monka mo bɔne, ɛfiri sɛ ɛdeɛn na kyerɛwsɛm no ka: Na sɛ yɛnantew hann no mu, sɛdeɛ ɔno wɔ hann no mu no a, yɛne yɛn ho yɛn ho wɔ ayɔnkofa, na Yesu Kristo ne Ba no mogya no te yɛn ho firi bɔne nyinaa ho. Sɛ yɛka sɛ yɛnni bɔne a, yɛdaadaa yɛn ho, na nokorɛ no nni yɛn mu. Sɛ yɛka yɛn bɔne a, ɔyɛ nokwafoɔ, na ɔteɛ sɛ ɔde yɛn bɔne bɛkyɛ yɛn, na wate yɛn ho afiri deɛ ɛntene nyinaa ho.

Me mma, mabu mo ateneneefoɔ wɔ me mu, na Yahweh abu mo bem afiri nsɛmmɔne nyinaa ho wɔ me mu; adɛn nti na moremma mo ho kwan nnye me nsɛm a ɛyɛ adom na ɛyɛ ɔdɔ no nni? Efiri sɛ Onyankopɔn dɔ da adi wɔ emu ne ɔdɔ a mewɔ ma m’ayeforɔ ne wiase nyinaa mpo, ɛfiri sɛ obiara a ɔpɛ no bɛtumi aba, monbra ansa na wɔato ɔpono no mu ɛfiri sɛ berɛ asa. Monsane mmra me nkyɛn da biara da na monhohoro mo ho wɔ me mogya a ɛtɔɔ mpata no mu, ɛfiri sɛ m’afɔrebɔ no yɛ pɛ na ɛyɛ prɛko pɛ ma daa: Efiri sɛ Hebrifoɔ no ka fa me ho pefee sɛ: Ɔno a ɔyɛ animuonyam no hann, ne ne nipasu mfonini pɛpɛɛpɛ, na ɔnam n’asɛm a tumi wom so soa nnoɔma nyinaa, ɔnam ne ho so ate yɛn bɔne ho, na ɔte Otumfoɔ no nifa so wɔ mmeaeɛ a ɛkorɔn. Me mma, monhunu sɛ ɔwerɛkyekyefoɔ no asan atwe wiase adwene asi atemmuo so, ɛfiri sɛ wɔabu wiase yi hene fɔ, na ne sentanseɛ fata no. Nanso de bɛsi simma yi ara no, ɔhwehwɛ sɛ ɔbɛtwe mo akɔ ne fɔbuo a ɛfata no no mu, enti ɔba sɛ ɔrebɛbu mo fɔ; enti monntie ne nsɛm na mompo n’atorɔsɛm.

Me nketewa, monnantew Onyankopɔn dɔ mu wɔ me, Kristo Yesu mo awurade mu. Monnɔ Agya no na monnɔ me denam me mmara nsɛm a mobɛdi so, ɛfiri sɛ ɛdeɛn na Yohane a ɔdi kan no ka? Deɛ ɔka sɛ, Menim no, na ɔnni ne mmara nsɛm so no, ɔyɛ ɔtorofoɔ, na nokorɛ no nni ne mu. Na deɛ ɔdi n’asɛm so no, nokorɛm Onyankopɔn dɔ ayɛ pɛ wɔ ne mu: yei so na yɛhunu sɛ yɛwɔ ne mu. Deɛ ɔka sɛ ɔte ne mu no, ɛsɛ sɛ ɔno nso nantew sɛdeɛ ɔnanteweeɛ no. Mehyɛ mo, m’ayeforɔ, sɛ monkɔ so nnantew sɛdeɛ menanteweeɛ na monni me mmara nsɛm so, ɛfiri sɛ meyɛɛ m’Agya apɛdeɛ daa, na saa ara na ɛsɛ sɛ mobɔ mmɔden sɛ mobɛyɛ m’apɛdeɛ daa, ɛfiri sɛ me deɛ yɛ Yahweh apɛdeɛ. Sɛ modi me mmara nsɛm so a, ɛbɛhia sɛ modane kɔ anuanom no nkyɛn, ɛfiri sɛ ɛdeɛn na Yohane a ɔdi kan no ka? Yei so na yɛhunu sɛ yɛdɔ Onyankopɔn mma, sɛ yɛdɔ Onyankopɔn na yɛdi ne mmara nsɛm so. Efiri sɛ yei ne Onyankopɔn dɔ, sɛ yɛdi ne mmara nsɛm so, na ne mmara nsɛm nyɛ den. Montie me nne na monni me nsɛm akyi kɔsi osetie awieeɛ, na monkɔ so nnɔ mo nuanom na monsom wɔ ɔdɔ mu, ɛnyɛ anifereɛ nti; ɛfiri sɛ mo akatua wɔ me nsam, ɛnyɛ wɔn a mosom wɔn no nsam.

Mommma biribiara nnyɛ akasakasa anaa ahantan mu; na mmom adwenebrɛaseɛ mu no, momma obiara mmu ne yɔnko sɛ ɔsen ɔno ankasa.

Meyɛ mo kyerɛkyerɛfoɔ ne mo hwɛfoɔ.

Mefiri Agya no nkyɛn na meyɛ su korɔ ne honhom korɔ no ara.

Meyɛ Onyankopɔn tenenee, na mayɛ mo Onyankopɔn tenenee.

Meyɛ deɛ wɔabu no bem wɔ n’akwan so.

Minni bɔne, na mabu mo bem wɔ me, Kristo Yesu mu.

Meyɛ deɛ wɔate ne ho daa ne daa daa, ɛfiri sɛ me na Ahennie no yɛ me dea, na mate mo ho denam ɔba su honhom no so.

Meyɛ deɛ ɔde ne mogya a ɛtwa toɔ no maeɛ wɔ asɛnnua no so sɛdeɛ ɛbɛyɛ a mɛte mo ho afiri bɔne nyinaa ho, ɛfiri sɛ bɔne biara nni ɔba su honhom a mede ama mo no mu.

Meyɛ mo ntamgyinafoɔ.

Meyɛ mo gyinafoɔ.

Monnyina hɔ pɛpɛɛpɛ, m’ayeforɔ, ɛfiri sɛ nsiesie mmerɛ no abɛn, na me pue a mɛpue no abɛn sene sɛdeɛ mosusu.
